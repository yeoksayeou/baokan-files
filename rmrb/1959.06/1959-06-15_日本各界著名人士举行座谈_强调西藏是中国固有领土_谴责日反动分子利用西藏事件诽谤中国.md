Title: Prominent Figures from All Walks of Life in Japan Hold Symposium, Emphasizing That Tibet Is An Inherent Part of China's Territory, Condemning Japanese Reactionaries for Using the Tibet Incident to Vilify China
Author:
Date: 1959-06-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 13th dispatch from Tokyo: Japanese renowned scholars of Chinese history, Shiro Nohara, and two other figures from the cultural sphere, unanimously emphasized at a symposium today that Tibet is an inherent part of China's territory. They also condemned a segment of Japanese reactionary elements for spreading fallacies to defame China by exploiting the Tibetan rebellion incident.

The Japan-China Friendship Association, the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, the Japan-China Buddhist Exchange Round-Table Conference, the China Research Institute, the Japan Peace Committee, and the Japan-Asia-Africa Solidarity Committee jointly held a symposium in Tokyo today regarding the Tibet issue. More than fifty prominent figures from peace and friendship organizations, labor unions, cultural circles, religious circles, and trade circles attended the symposium.

Shiro Nohara, Professor Shigetada Sakamoto of Tokyo University of Foreign Studies, and Yoshiro Rosan, former East Asia Department Director of Kyodo News, each delivered a report on the Tibetan rebellion incident. In their reports, they also pointed out the crimes of Tibet's backward social system and the fact that imperialists were using Tibet's backwardness to plot conspiracies.

All attendees applauded enthusiastically and made speeches, expressing their agreement with the opinions.

### 日本各界著名人士举行座谈  强调西藏是中国固有领土  谴责日反动分子利用西藏事件诽谤中国

1959-06-15
第4版()
专栏：

    日本各界著名人士举行座谈  强调西藏是中国固有领土
    谴责日反动分子利用西藏事件诽谤中国
    新华社13日讯　东京消息：日本著名的研究中国历史的学者野原四郎等三位文化界人士，今天在一个座谈会上一致强调西藏是中国固有的领土，并且谴责日本一部分反动分子利用西藏叛乱事件散布诽谤中国的谬论。
    日中友好协会、日中文化交流协会、日中佛教交流恳谈会、中国研究所、日本和平委员会和日本亚非团结委员会今天在东京联合举行了关于西藏问题的座谈会。和平、友好团体和工会、文化界、宗教界以及贸易界著名人士五十多人出席了座谈会。
    野原四郎和东京外国语大学教授坂本是忠、共同社前东亚部部长蜡山芳郎分别就西藏叛乱事件做了报告。他们在报告中还指出了西藏的落后的社会制度的罪恶和帝国主义者利用西藏的落后状态策划阴谋的事实。
    全体与会者热烈鼓掌并发言，对他们的意见表示赞同。
