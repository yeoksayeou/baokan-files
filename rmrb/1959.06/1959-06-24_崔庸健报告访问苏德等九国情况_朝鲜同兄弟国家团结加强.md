Title: Choe Yong-gon Reports on Visits to Nine Countries Including the Soviet Union and Germany: Solidarity Between Korea and Fraternal Countries Strengthened
Author:
Date: 1959-06-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 23rd: Pyongyang newspapers reported today that the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, and the Standing Committee of the Cabinet held a joint meeting on the 22nd. The meeting heard a report by Choe Yong-gon, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, on his visits to nine fraternal countries, including the Soviet Union, the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, Albania, and Mongolia.

The meeting was attended by members and alternate members of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Vice-Premiers of the Cabinet, and the Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, Vice-Chairmen, and members of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly.

Chairman Choe Yong-gon gave a detailed account of the achievements of the people of the fraternal countries in socialist construction, the further strengthening of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and the warm friendly feelings expressed by the party and government leaders and people of the fraternal countries towards the Korean people.

Chairman Choe Yong-gon pointed out that the visits to the fraternal countries headed by the Soviet Union had contributed to strengthening and developing the friendship and unity between the Korean people and the people of the fraternal countries and to safeguarding the cause of peace.

The meeting expressed its gratitude to the party and government leaders and people of the fraternal countries for the warm welcome and reception given to Chairman Choe Yong-gon, and expressed its gratitude for their love and friendly feelings towards the Korean people, as well as their strong support for the Korean people's struggle to achieve the peaceful reunification of their motherland and build socialism.

### 崔庸健报告访问苏德等九国情况  朝鲜同兄弟国家团结加强

1959-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    崔庸健报告访问苏德等九国情况
    朝鲜同兄弟国家团结加强
    新华社平壤23日电　平壤报纸今天报道，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会常务委员会、朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会和内阁常务会议在22日举行了联席会议。会议听取了朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健所作的关于访问苏联、德意志民主共和国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、阿尔巴尼亚和蒙古等九个兄弟国家的报告。
    参加会议的有朝鲜劳动党中央委员会常务委员会委员和候补委员，内阁副首相，最高人民会议议长、副议长、常任委员会副委员长和委员。
    崔庸健委员长详细介绍了兄弟国家人民在社会主义建设中所取得的成就，以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的进一步壮大以及兄弟国家的党政领导人和人民对朝鲜人民所表示的热烈的友好感情。
    崔庸健委员长指出，访问以苏联为首的兄弟国家，对加强和发展朝鲜人民同兄弟国家人民之间的友谊团结，对维护和平事业作出了贡献。
    会议表示感谢兄弟国家党政领导人和人民给予崔庸健委员长的热烈欢迎和接待，并且表示感谢他们对朝鲜人民表达的热爱和友好感情以及对朝鲜人民为实现祖国的和平统一和建设社会主义的斗争的大力声援和支持。
