Title: Bratsk Hydroelectric Station: Generating Capacity Increased to 4.5 Million Kilowatts
Author:
Date: 1959-06-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow (TASS dispatch): According to a plan recently approved by the Soviet Ministry of Power Station Construction, the generating capacity of the Bratsk Hydroelectric Station, the world's largest currently under construction on the Angara River in Siberia, will be increased from the originally planned 3.6 million kilowatts to 4.5 million kilowatts.

According to the Ministry of Power Station Construction's plan, the increase of 900,000 kilowatts in generating capacity will be achieved by increasing the generating capacity of each turbine and by increasing the number of turbines from eighteen to twenty.

The designers of the Bratsk Hydroelectric Station's turbines have successfully increased the generating capacity of the turbines from the originally planned 200,000 kilowatts to 225,000 kilowatts without changing the size of the turbines. These largest turbines in the world will be manufactured in Leningrad.

During the period of 1959-1965, fourteen turbines at the Bratsk Hydroelectric Station (with a total generating capacity of 3.6 million kilowatts) will begin generating electricity. The start-up time for the remaining turbines will be determined based on the growth of electricity consumption in the regions of Siberia that use the electricity from this power station. ### 布拉次克水电站  发电能力增至450万瓩

1959-06-19
第5版()
专栏：

    布拉次克水电站
    发电能力增至450万瓩
    新华社讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联电站建造部最近通过的方案规定，正在西伯利亚安加拉河上建设的世界最大的布拉次克水电站的发电能力，将从由原定的三百六十万瓩增加到四百五十万瓩。
    根据电站建造部的方案，增加九十万瓩发电能力，一方面是靠提高每台涡轮机的发电能力，另一方面是把涡轮机从十八个增加到二十个。
    布拉次克水电站涡轮机的设计者们已经成功地把涡轮机的发电能力，由原定的二十万瓩增加到了二十二万五千瓩，而不需要改变涡轮机的体积。这些世界上最大的涡轮机将在列宁格勒制造。
    在1959——1965年期间，布拉次克水电站将要有十四台涡轮机（总发电能力为三百六十万瓩）开始发电。其余涡轮机的发电时间，将根据西伯利亚那些使用这个电站的电力的地区电力消费量的增长情况来决定。（附图片）
