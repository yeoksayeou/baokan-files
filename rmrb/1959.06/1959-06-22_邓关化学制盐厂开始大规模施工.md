Title: Large-Scale Construction Begins at Dengguan Chemical Salt Factory
Author: Hai Que
Date: 1959-06-22
Page: 3

    Large-Scale Construction Begins at Dengguan Chemical Salt Factory
    A large salt factory is being built in the Southwest – the Zigong Dengguan Chemical Salt Factory has recently begun large-scale construction.

    After completion this year, this salt factory can produce 250,000 tons of edible salt annually.

    The Dengguan area is very rich in underground resources such as brine and natural gas. Currently, the lighting, cooking, and other needs of the thousands of workers on the construction site are all met using natural gas. Drilling only a few hundred meters deep brings a huge flow of gas surging up, demonstrating how abundant the natural gas is.
                  (Hai Que)

### 邓关化学制盐厂开始大规模施工
海鹊
1959-06-22
第3版()
专栏：

    邓关化学制盐厂开始大规模施工
    西南兴建一座大盐厂——自贡邓关化学制盐厂已于最近开始大规模施工。
    这座制盐厂今年完工后，可以年产食盐二十五万吨。
    邓关地区卤水和天然气等地下资源非常丰富，目前工地上几千职工的照明、烧饭等都全是利用天然气。钻井钻到几百公尺就被地下巨大的气流顶上来了，可见，天然气是多么丰富。
                  （海鹊）
