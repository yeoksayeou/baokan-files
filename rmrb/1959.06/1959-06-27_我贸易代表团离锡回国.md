Title: Our Trade Delegation Departs Colombo for Home

Author:

Date: 1959-06-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, June 25th: The Chinese trade delegation headed by Lei Renmin left Colombo by plane this afternoon to return home.
