Title: Communist Youth League Construction Project
Author:
Date: 1959-06-28
Page: 3

    Communist Youth League Construction Project
    The Soviet Communist Youth League actively participates in the construction of the Seven-Year Plan. Of the total production capacity to be put into operation within the planned seven years, industrial enterprises built with the assistance of the Communist Youth League will produce more than 50% of pig iron, more than 40% of steel, about 70% of steel products, about 40% of cement, more than 60% of electricity, and more than 20% of coal, and so on.
    In terms of the ferrous metallurgical industry, twenty-three construction projects, including the West Siberian and Karaganda Iron and Steel Plants, will be constructed under the responsibility of Communist Youth League members. The Communist Youth League will help build one hundred large-scale enterprises in the metallurgical, coal, mining, chemical, petroleum, and gas industries, construct power stations, and actively participate in the reconstruction and expansion of existing enterprises. The youth will complete the electrification of more than 20,000 kilometers of railways and the construction of 14,000 kilometers of new railway lines with their own labor. (Hai)

### 共青团建设工程

1959-06-28
第3版()
专栏：

    共青团建设工程
    苏联共青团积极参加七年计划的建设事业。在预定七年内投入的生产能力的总数中，共青团帮助建设的工业企业，将生产50％以上的生铁，40％以上的钢，70％左右的钢材，40％左右的水泥，60％以上的电力和20％以上的煤等等。
    在黑色冶金工业方面，包括西西伯利亚、卡拉干达钢铁厂等二十三项建设工程将由共青团员负责建设。共青团帮助修建冶金、煤炭、矿山、化学、石油和煤气工业的一百个大型企业，建设发电站，并将积极参加改建和扩建现有的企业。青年们将以自己的劳动，完成两万多公里铁路的电气化工程以及修建一万四千公里的新铁路线。　　（海）
