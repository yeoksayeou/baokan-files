Title: Chairman Mao and Chairman Liu Receive Former Vice President of Chile
Author:
Date: 1959-06-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 18th: Chairman Mao Zedong and Chairman Liu Shaoqi jointly received Guillermo del Pedregal, former Vice President of the Republic of Chile, and his wife this afternoon.

Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, was present at the reception. (Attached picture)

Chairman Mao and Chairman Liu with Former Vice President of Chile, Betregal, and his wife.

### 毛主席刘主席接见智利前副总统

1959-06-19
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席刘主席接见智利前副总统
    新华社18日讯　毛泽东主席和刘少奇主席今天下午一起接见了智利共和国前副总统吉勒摩·德尔·贝特雷加尔和他的夫人。
    接见时中国人民外交学会会长张奚若在座。（附图片）
    毛主席刘主席同智利前副总统贝特雷加尔和他的夫人合影。
            新华社记者　侯波摄
