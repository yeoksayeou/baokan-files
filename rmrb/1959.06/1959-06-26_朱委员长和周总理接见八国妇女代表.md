Title: Chairman Zhu and Premier Zhou Receive Women Representatives from Eight Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-06-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th: Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai received this noon women representatives and women's delegations from eight countries—Chile, Denmark, Finland, Iraq, Norway, Sweden, Uruguay, and Venezuela—who are currently paying a friendly visit to China.

Present at the reception were Cai Chang, President of the All-China Women's Federation; Kang Keqing, Vice-President; and Cao Mengjun, member of the Presidium.

### 朱委员长和周总理接见八国妇女代表

1959-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    朱委员长和周总理接见八国妇女代表
    新华社25日讯　朱德委员长和周恩来总理今天中午接见了正在我国进行友好访问的智利、丹麦、芬兰、伊拉克、挪威、瑞典、乌拉圭和委内瑞拉八个国家的妇女代表团和妇女代表。
    接见时在座的有全国妇联主席蔡畅、副主席康克清、主席团委员曹孟君等。
