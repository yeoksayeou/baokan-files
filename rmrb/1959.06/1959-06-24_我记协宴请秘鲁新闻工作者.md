Title: ACJA Hosts Banquet for Peruvian Journalists
Author: 
Date: 1959-06-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd. The Vice-Presidents of the All-China Journalists Association, Mei Yi and Wang Yunsheng, hosted a banquet tonight to entertain the journalists in the Peruvian parliamentary delegation visiting our country.

### 我记协宴请秘鲁新闻工作者

1959-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    我记协宴请秘鲁新闻工作者
    新华社23日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长梅益、王芸生今晚举行宴会，招待来我国访问的秘鲁议员团中的新闻工作者。
