Title: All Bridge Piers of Chongqing Yangtze River Bridge Completed
Author:
Date: 1959-06-12
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 10th June: All the bridge piers of the Chongqing Baishatuo Yangtze River Bridge have been completed one month ahead of schedule. Late at night on June 6th, after the concrete of the last pier was poured, the workers immediately began the work of pre-assembling the plate girders. Now, seven of the twelve plate girders of the entire bridge have been assembled, and three have been riveted. The steel beams will soon be erected.
　　
The Chongqing Baishatuo Yangtze River Bridge is 820 meters long and is scheduled to be completed this year. In recent days, the bridge beams manufactured by the Shanhai Pass Bridge Factory have been transported to the construction site. Workers are rushing to repair the bridgehead lines in an effort to officially erect the beams in late June.

### 重庆长江大桥桥墩全部建好

1959-06-12
第2版()
专栏：

    重庆长江大桥桥墩全部建好
    新华社重庆10日电　重庆白沙沱长江大桥全部桥墩已提前一个月建成。6月6日深夜，当最后一个桥墩的混凝土灌注完毕之后，工人们马上投入了钣梁预拼工作。现在，全桥十二孔钣梁已拼好七孔，铆合三孔，不久就可以开始架钢梁了。
    重庆白沙沱长江大桥全长八百二十公尺，计划在今年建成。这几天，由山海关桥梁工厂承制的桥梁已经运到工地，工人们正在抓紧抢修桥头线路，争取在本月下旬正式架梁。
