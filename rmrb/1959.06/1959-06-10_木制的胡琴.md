Title: Wooden Huqin
Author:
Date: 1959-06-10
Page: 2

    Wooden Huqin
    Hong Guangyuan and Shi Shanpeng, veteran artisans at the Beijing First Musical Instrument Cooperative, have successfully crafted a huqin using wood instead of purple bamboo. Due to meticulous craftsmanship, it is difficult to distinguish it from a purple bamboo huqin in appearance, and its volume also meets standard requirements. This wooden huqin has recently been evaluated by over a dozen renowned qin masters, including Xu Lanyuan, Yang Baozhong, and Li Gongliang, who unanimously agreed that it is a great success.
    The success of this innovation opens up a vast source of materials for the mass production of huqin. (Attached image)

### 木制的胡琴

1959-06-10
第2版()
专栏：

    木制的胡琴
    北京第一乐器社老艺人洪广源和史善朋，用木头代替紫竹制成了胡琴。由于加工精细，从外表上很难看出它和紫竹制的有什么不同，而且音量也能达到标准要求。这种木制的胡琴，最近已由全国著名的徐兰沅、杨宝忠、李恭良等十多位琴师鉴定，一致认为很成功。
    这一创造的成功，为大量生产胡琴开辟了广大的材料来源。（附图片）
