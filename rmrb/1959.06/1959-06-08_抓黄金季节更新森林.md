Title: Seize the Golden Season to Renew Forests
Author:
Date: 1959-06-08
Page: 3

    Seize the Golden Season to Renew Forests
    The vast numbers of workers and masses in the Greater Khingan Range Forest Area are seizing the current golden season for forest renewal, vigorously carrying out forest renewal work while ensuring the completion of logging tasks. From late April to late May, sowing and tree planting have reached more than 50,000 mu, completing one-third of the annual artificial renewal plan, and nursery cultivation has been completed on more than 1,000 mu.

### 抓黄金季节更新森林

1959-06-08
第3版()
专栏：简明新闻

    抓黄金季节更新森林
    大兴安岭林区广大职工和群众抓紧当前森林更新的黄金季节，在保证完成采伐任务的同时，大力展开了更新森林工作。从四月下旬到五月下旬，播种和植树造林已达五万多亩，完成了全年人工更新计划的三分之一，苗圃育苗已完成一千多亩。
