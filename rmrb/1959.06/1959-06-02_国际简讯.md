International Briefs

    South Vietnamese Authorities Intensify "Sweeps" Against Patriotic People
    Hanoi Dispatch: According to reports in local newspapers on the 31st, the Ngo Dinh Diem authorities are preparing to further expand their "sweeps" against local residents in the eastern and western regions of South Vietnam. According to Saigon Radio broadcasts, on May 26, 27, and 28, Ngo Dinh Diem personally "inspected the state of tranquility" in the eastern provinces of South Vietnam, and convened a meeting of the provincial governors. The commander of the South Vietnamese "First Military Region" and the commanders of the security forces participated in this meeting. At the same time, the Ngo Dinh Diem authorities are also dispatching regular troops to the western region to strengthen the suppression of the local people.

    Burma Permits American Company to Explore for Oil in Burma
    Yangon Dispatch: According to the "Nation" newspaper, the Burmese government has issued an exploration license to the American California General Exploration Company, allowing it to explore for oil and natural gas in Burma.

### 国际简讯

第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    南越当局加紧“扫荡”爱国人民
    河内讯：据此间报纸31日报道：吴庭艳当局正在准备进一步扩大在南越东部和西部地区对当地居民的“扫荡”。据西贡电台广播，5月26、27和28日，吴庭艳亲临南越东部各省“视察安宁状况”，召集了各省省长会议。南越“第一军区”的司令员和保安团的指挥员参加了这个会议。吴庭艳当局同时还向西部地区增派正规军队以加强对当地人民的镇压。
    缅甸允许美公司在缅勘探石油
    仰光讯：据“民族报”报道，缅甸政府把勘探许可证发给了美国加利福尼亚通用勘探公司，允许它勘探缅甸的石油和天然煤气。
