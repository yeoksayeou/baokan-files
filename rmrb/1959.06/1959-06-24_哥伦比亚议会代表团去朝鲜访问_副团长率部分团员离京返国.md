Title: Colombian Congressional Delegation Visits North Korea; Deputy Head Leads Some Members Home
Author:
Date: 1959-06-24
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd. Rodriguez, the head of the Colombian Congressional Delegation, led part of the Colombian Congressional Delegation and left Beijing by plane this morning to visit the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. Franco, the deputy head of the delegation, and other members also left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

### 哥伦比亚议会代表团去朝鲜访问  副团长率部分团员离京返国

1959-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    哥伦比亚议会代表团去朝鲜访问
    副团长率部分团员离京返国
    新华社23日讯　哥伦比亚议会代表团团长罗德里格斯率领哥伦比亚议会代表团部分团员，今天上午乘飞机离开北京，前往朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访问。代表团副团长佛朗哥和其他团员也在今天上午乘飞机离开北京返国。
