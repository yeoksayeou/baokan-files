Title: U.S. Aircraft Intrudes into the Airspace Over Our Xisha Islands Four Times; We Issue the Fiftieth Serious Warning
Author: 
Date: 1959-06-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th: On June 23rd, at 9:05 AM and 12:40 PM, a U.S. Navy patrol plane twice intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. On the same day, at 10:03 AM and 11:53 AM, another U.S. Navy reconnaissance plane also twice intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the fiftieth serious warning.

### 美机四次侵入我西沙群岛上空  我提出第五十次严重警告

1959-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    美机四次侵入我西沙群岛上空
    我提出第五十次严重警告
    新华社25日讯　6月23日九时零五分和十二时四十分，美国海军巡逻机一架，两次侵入我国广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛上空。同日十时零三分和十一时五十三分，又一架美国海军侦察机，也两次侵入我国广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第五十次严重警告。
