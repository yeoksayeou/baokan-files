Title: Bolivian Medical Delegation Leaves Beijing for Visit to Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1959-06-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 12th. The six-member Bolivian medical delegation, led by Dr. H. Torres Goitia, left Beijing by train tonight for a visit to Northeast China.

The Bolivian guests, including five doctors and one professor, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 8th at the invitation of the Chinese Medical Association. Fu Lianzhang, President of the Chinese Medical Association, met with all members of the delegation on the 9th and hosted a banquet to welcome them.

After arriving in Beijing, the guests visited and toured the Tongren Hospital, the Academy of Medical Sciences, and the Beijing Medical College.

### 玻利维亚医学代表团离京赴东北访问

1959-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    玻利维亚医学代表团离京赴东北访问
    新华社12日讯　由哈·托雷斯戈伊蒂亚医生率领的玻利维亚医学代表团一行六人，今天晚上乘火车离开北京去东北访问。
    包括五位医生、一位教授的玻利维亚客人，是应中华医学会的邀请在8日下午到达北京的。中华医学会会长傅连暲9日接见了代表团全体成员，并设宴欢迎他们。
    客人们到达北京后，曾到同仁医院、医学科学院、北京医学院进行了参观、访问。
