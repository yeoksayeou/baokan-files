Title: Nicaraguan Anti-Dictatorial Leader Points Out: U.S. Ambassador is an Agent of Tyranny
Author:
Date: 1959-06-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, San José, 5th: The leader of the Nicaraguan anti-dictatorial armed forces, Enrique Lacayo Farfán, condemned on the 2nd that the U.S. ambassador to Nicaragua is an agent of the Nicaraguan dictatorial regime.

Farfán said that the U.S. ambassador to Nicaragua, Thomas Whelan, "does not represent American democracy," and "in my opinion, he has done many bad things to my country, causing resentment among our people towards the U.S. State Department."

It is reported that on the same day Farfán condemned the U.S. ambassador, the U.S. Congress discussed the situation in Nicaragua. U.S. Congressman Keogh publicly stated at the meeting that the United States would obstruct the Latin American people from overthrowing the dictatorships in their countries. He threatened that the U.S. government "cannot turn a blind eye" to the situation in Nicaragua, and "the United States should be prepared to use force if necessary to show its attitude."

### 尼加拉瓜反独裁领袖指出  美国大使是暴政的代理人

1959-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    尼加拉瓜反独裁领袖指出
    美国大使是暴政的代理人
    新华社5日讯圣约瑟消息：尼加拉瓜反独裁武装部队领袖恩里克·拉卡约·法尔方2日谴责美国驻尼加拉瓜大使是尼加拉瓜独裁政权的代理人。
    法尔方说，美国驻尼加拉瓜大使托马斯·惠伦“不代表美国的民主”，“在我看来他对我的国家做了许多坏事，引起了我国人民对美国国务院的愤慨。”
    据报道，在法尔方谴责美国大使的同一天，美国国会曾讨论了尼加拉瓜的局势。美国国会议员凯普哈特在会上公然表示，美国将阻挠拉丁美洲人民推翻他们国家的独裁政权。他威胁说，美国政府对尼加拉瓜的局势“不能熟视无睹”，“美国应该准备在必要时用武力来表明它的态度”。
