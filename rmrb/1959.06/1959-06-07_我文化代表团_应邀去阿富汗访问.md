Title: My Cultural Delegation Invited to Visit Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1959-06-07
Page: 3
    My Cultural Delegation
    Invited to Visit Afghanistan
    Xinhua News Agency, June 6th. At the invitation of the government of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, the six-member Chinese cultural delegation to the Kingdom of Afghanistan departed from Beijing by plane this morning. The head of the delegation is Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, and the members include Lin Lin, Wu Bolun, He Qijun, and Li Zisheng.

### 我文化代表团  应邀去阿富汗访问

1959-06-07
第3版()
专栏：

    我文化代表团
    应邀去阿富汗访问
    新华社6日讯　应阿富汗王国政府邀请，前往阿富汗王国访问的中国文化代表团一行六人，今天早上自北京乘飞机启程。代表团团长是对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武，团员有林林、武伯伦、何启君、李梓盛。
