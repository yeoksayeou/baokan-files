Title: Further Enhance the Friendship and Unity Between China and Mongolia: Our Military Delegation Arrives in Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1959-06-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, June 6th: The Chinese military goodwill delegation, headed by Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Marshal Peng Dehuai, arrived in Ulaanbaatar by special plane from Moscow this morning. The delegation is visiting here for five days at the invitation of the Mongolian Ministry of Military and Public Security.

Those who greeted the delegation at the airport included: Tsende, member of the Political Bureau of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Central Committee; Ramsuren, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers; Lieutenant General Dorj, Minister of Military and Public Security; Shagdaruren, Minister of Foreign Affairs; Shalab, President of the Mongolia-China Friendship Association, and others. Xie Fusheng, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, and diplomatic envoys from fraternal countries to Mongolia also greeted the delegation at the airport.

Marshal Peng Dehuai, accompanied by Minister Dorj, reviewed the guard of honor. Minister Dorj and Marshal Peng Dehuai spoke at the airport successively.

In his speech, Minister Dorj said that the Chinese army is a strong fortress defending world peace and security, and the Chinese people are making unremitting efforts to consolidate peace in Asia and the world and to ease international tensions. He also stated that the Mongolian people and army fully support the just struggle of the Chinese people to liberate their inalienable territory of Taiwan and the coastal islands.

Marshal Peng Dehuai said in his speech that China and Mongolia are neighbors, brothers, and comrades-in-arms. There has been a profound friendship between the people and armies of our two countries for a long time. We believe that the visit of the Chinese military goodwill delegation will further enhance our friendship and unity. We will also learn from the advanced experience of our Mongolian comrades-in-arms during this visit.

This afternoon, Zedanbal, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, received all members of the Chinese military goodwill delegation headed by Marshal Peng Dehuai.

### 进一步增强中蒙两国的友谊和团结  我军事代表团到达乌兰巴托

1959-06-07
第3版()
专栏：

    进一步增强中蒙两国的友谊和团结
    我军事代表团到达乌兰巴托
    新华社乌兰巴托6日电　以副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅为首的中国军事友好代表团，今天上午乘专机由莫斯科到达乌兰巴托。代表团应蒙古军事公安部的邀请，将在这里访问五天。
    到机场迎接的有：蒙古人民革命党中央政治局委员曾德、部长会议副主席拉姆苏伦、军事公安部长道尔吉中将、外交部长沙格达尔苏伦、蒙中友好协会会长沙拉布等人。中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生和各兄弟国家驻蒙古的外交使节也到机场迎接。
    彭德怀元帅在道尔吉部长陪同下，检阅了仪仗队。道尔吉部长和彭德怀元帅先后在机场上讲了话。
    道尔吉部长在讲话中说，中国军队是保卫世界和平和安全的坚强堡垒，中国人民为巩固亚洲和世界和平、为缓和国际紧张局势进行着不懈的斗争。他还表示，蒙古人民和军队完全支持中国人民解放自己不可分割的领土台湾和沿海岛屿的正义斗争。
    彭德怀元帅讲话说，中、蒙两国是邻居，是兄弟，又是战友。我们两国人民和军队之间，长期以来就存在着深厚的友谊。我们相信，这次中国军事友好代表团的访问，将会进一步增强我们的友谊和团结，我们还要在这次访问中，学习蒙古战友们的先进经验。
    今天下午，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记、部长会议主席泽登巴尔，接见了以彭德怀元帅为首的中国军事友好代表团全体成员。
