Title: Vietnamese Railway Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-06-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 28th. The Vietnamese Democratic Republic railway delegation, a group of ten people led by Tran Quang Son, Director of the Engineering Bureau of the General Railway Administration of the Vietnamese Ministry of Transportation and Post, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon.

Welcoming them at the station were Chen Tan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Railways, and Xiao Hongqi, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union.

### 越南铁路考察团到京

1959-06-29
第4版()
专栏：

    越南铁路考察团到京
    新华社28日讯　越南人民共和国铁路考察团一行十人，在团长、越南交通邮电部铁路总局工程局局长陈光山率领下，今天下午乘火车到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有铁道部办公厅主任陈坦、中国铁路工会全国委员会副主席萧洪启等人。
