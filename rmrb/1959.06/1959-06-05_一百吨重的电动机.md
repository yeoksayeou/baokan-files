Title: A Hundred-Ton Electric Motor
Author:
Date: 1959-06-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 4th - China's first large-scale 2,800 kW DC motor has been successfully trial-produced at the Harbin Electric Machinery Plant. This electric motor weighs a total of one hundred tons. Driven by it, a 700 mm rolling mill can roll 500,000 to 700,000 tons of steel for the country annually.

In the process of manufacturing this large-scale DC motor, the Harbin Electric Machinery Plant received enthusiastic assistance from Soviet experts.

### 一百吨重的电动机

1959-06-05
第2版()
专栏：

    一百吨重的电动机
    新华社哈尔滨4日电　我国第一台两千八百瓩大型直流电动机，已在哈尔滨电机厂试制成功。这台电动机的总重量达一百吨，用它带动七百公厘的轧钢机，每年可以为国家轧制五十万到七十万吨钢材。
    哈尔滨电机厂在制造这台大型直流电动机的过程中，得到了苏联专家的热心帮助。
