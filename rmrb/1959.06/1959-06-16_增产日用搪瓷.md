Increase Production of Daily Use Enamelware

Guangzhou Yifeng Enamel Factory is actively increasing the production of daily-use enamel products. The output in the second quarter is expected to increase by about 48% compared to the first quarter. In order to increase the variety of designs and colors, the art design team has already designed more than two hundred new varieties. (Picture attached)

### 增产日用搪瓷

1959-06-16
第2版()
专栏：

    增产日用搪瓷
    广州益丰搪瓷厂积极增产搪瓷日用品。第二季度的产量要比第一季度增长48％左右。为了增加花色品种，美术设计小组已经设计好二百多种新的品种。（附图片）
