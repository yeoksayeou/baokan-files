Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Meeting Approving Sino-Hungarian Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1959-06-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th June. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its fourth meeting this morning. The meeting decided to approve the Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the Hungarian People's Republic. This treaty was signed in Beijing on May 6 this year.

The meeting discussed and adopted resolutions approving the State Council's abolition of the Legislative Bureau of the State Council, with its functions to be managed by the General Office of the State Council; the abolition of the Personnel Bureau of the State Council, with its functions to be managed by the Ministry of Internal Affairs; the abolition of the Central Confidential Communications Bureau; and the abolition of the State Council's Bureau of Experts, with its functions to be managed by the Science and Technology Commission.

The State Council still has the following fourteen directly affiliated institutions: the State Statistical Bureau, the National Bureau of Surveying and Mapping, the People's Bank of China, the Central Meteorological Bureau, the Central Administration for Industry and Commerce, the Xinhua News Agency, the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau, the Chinese Language Reform Committee, the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council, the National Archives Bureau, the Counselor's Office of the State Council, the Bureau of Foreign Experts of the State Council, the Government Offices Administration of the State Council, and the Premier's Office of the State Council.

### 人大常务委员会举行会议  批准中匈友好合作条约

1959-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

    人大常务委员会举行会议
    批准中匈友好合作条约
    新华社20日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在今天上午举行第四次会议。会议决定批准中华人民共和国和匈牙利人民共和国友好合作条约。这个条约是今年5月6日在北京签订的。
    会议讨论和通过决议，批准国务院撤销国务院法制局，其业务改由国务院秘书厅管理；撤销国务院人事局，其业务改由内务部管理；撤销中央机要交通局；撤销国务院专家局，其业务改由科学技术委员会管理。
    国务院现在仍设置有下列十四个直属机构：国家统计局、国家测绘总局、中国人民银行、中央气象局、中央工商行政管理局、新华通讯社、广播事业局、中国文字改革委员会、国务院宗教事务局、国家档案局、国务院参事室、国务院外国专家局、国务院机关事务管理局、国务院总理办公室。
