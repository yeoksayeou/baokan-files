Title: Picture
Author: Wen Shan, Wu Ran
Date: 1959-06-29
Page: 8
Column: Poetic Sentiment

    A winding path circles the fence, leading through the forest and trees to my home.
    On a moonlit night, let's go to the riverbank, to watch the falling blossoms in the shadows of the pear trees.
      Poem by Wen Shan, woodcut by Wu Ran
