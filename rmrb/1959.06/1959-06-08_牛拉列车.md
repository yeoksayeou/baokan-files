Title: Ox-Drawn Train
Author: Chen Gaoshi, Xu Guishi
Date: 1959-06-08
Page: 2

    Ox-Drawn Train
    The Lianjiang Transportation Company of Fujian has successfully trial-produced a ball-bearing "ox-drawn train" which can pull sixteen carts at a time, with a load capacity of four tons. It can replace the labor of four flatbed carts and twenty people. The wheels of this ox-drawn train are equipped with ball bearings, and the carts are connected by hooks and cotter pins, making it easy to attach and detach, and flexible for turning. In short-distance transportation, it can complete a daily cargo volume of twenty-four tons.
                        (Chen Gaoshi, Xu Guishi) (Attached picture)

### 牛拉列车
陈高使  许桂使
1959-06-08
第2版()
专栏：四通八达

    牛拉列车
    福建连江运输公司试制成功滚珠化“牛拉列车”，一次能拉十六个车斗，载重四吨，可代替四架板车、二十个人的劳动量。这种牛拉列车，车轮装有滚珠，车斗与车斗的连接，采取挂钩插肖的办法，因此挂、托方便，拐弯灵活，在短途运输中，每日能完成二十四吨的货运量。
                        （陈高使、许桂使）（附图片）
