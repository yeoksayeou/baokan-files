Title: Hello, Bucharest
Author: Chen Canyun
Date: 1959-06-29
Page: 8

Hello, Bucharest!
The kind shepherd Bucur, settled on this wilderness, a full five hundred years ago; five hundred years,
	Unusual storms,
	Unusual rain,
	Unusual flames, have not destroyed you!
Hello, Bucharest!
Five hundred years, not a short time, you shed tears of oppression, and yet in unyielding resistance:
	Saw the destruction of the Turkish rulers,
	Saw the destruction of the Austro-Hungarian Empire,
	Saw the destruction of the Hitler Group,
	Saw the destruction of the Groza Dynasty!
Hello, Bucharest!
When Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej and his comrades,
	Walked out of the dark prison;
When the red flag of the Romanian Communist Party,
	Rose above the grey palace;
When the passionately cheering people,
	Embraced the invincible Soviet Red Army;
You then became a glorious symbol,
Hello, Bucharest!
From that time on, you put on beautiful clothes,
You wear bright red flowers,
You sing songs of youth,
You laugh unrestrainedly!
Seventeen million hearts of different nationalities,
Jump with you.
Hello, Bucharest!
From that time on,
The warm wind blows from the Black Sea,
The withered poplars sprout green buds;
The flowing water on the Danube,
Emits a peaceful and light sound;
A golden sunny road spreads out before you,
You open your chest and move forward!
Hello, Bucharest!
Only fifteen years have passed,
You have become a happy garden.
Four hundred and eighty-five years of trauma,
Has also turned into a force to inspire you forward.
The great-grandchildren of the robbers who ravaged you,
Are dying in your laughter!
But you are striding forward, continuing to advance!
Hello, Bucharest!
Advance, advance with a mighty army!
The sound of one million three hundred thousand tons of steel,
The sound of Europe's second-largest oil field,
The sound of a powerful chemical industry,
The sound of the collective farm winning victory after victory,
Like a majestic symphony,
	Advance under the direction of the Workers' Party!
Hello, Bucharest!
Good, you and Moscow, Beijing,
And the hearts of all brothers,
Jump together, and march in unison,
Towards the great garden of communism!
Good, the shepherd Bucur is about to be resurrected,
He will smile and walk with everyone towards
A happier and happier tomorrow!
Hello, Bucharest!!

1959, 6, 15, in Bucharest

### 布加勒斯特，你好
陈残云
1959-06-29
第8版()
专栏：

    布加勒斯特，你好
    陈残云善良的牧羊人布加勒，从这草野上定居开始，整整五百年啦；五百年，
  不平常的暴风，
  不平常的暴雨，
  不平常的火焰，没有把你摧毁！布加勒斯特，你好！五百年，日子并不短，你流着被压迫的眼泪，而又在不屈的反抗中：
  看见土耳其统治者的毁灭，
  看见奥匈帝国的毁灭，
  看见希特勒集团的毁灭，
  看见葛洛尔王朝的毁灭！布加勒斯特，你好！当乔治乌—德治和他的战友，
  从黑暗的监狱中走出；当罗马尼亚共产党的红旗，
  在灰色的王宫上升起；当激情地欢呼着的人们，
  和无敌的苏联红军拥抱；你便变成了光荣的标志，布加勒斯特，你好！从那时候起，你穿上了美丽的衣裳，你戴上了鲜艳的红花，你唱着青春的赞歌，你奔放地欢笑！一千七百万颗不同民族的心，跟着你一同跳跃。布加勒斯特，你好！从那时候起，黑海吹来温暖的风，枯毁了的杨树抽出绿芽；多瑙河上的流水，发出和平轻快的声响；你的面前铺开金色的阳光大
  道，你敞开胸怀往前走！布加勒斯特，你好！到如今，才不过十五年，你变成幸福的花园。四百八十五年的创伤，也变作激励你前进的力量。摧残过你的强盗的徒孙们，在你的笑声下奄奄一息了！你却迈着大步，继续前进！布加勒斯特，你好！前进，千军万马地前进！一百三十万吨钢铁的声音，欧洲第二的石油的声音，强大的化学工业的声音，集体农庄胜利再胜利的声音，像一支雄壮的交响曲，
  在工人党的指挥下前进！布加勒斯特，你好！好呀，你和莫斯科，北京，
  和所有兄弟们的心脏，共同跳跃，又合着齐一的步伐，向共产主义的大花园前进！好呀，牧羊人布加勒要复活了，他将笑着，和大家一起走向
  更幸福更幸福的明天！布加勒斯特，你好！！
  1959，6，15，于布加勒斯特
