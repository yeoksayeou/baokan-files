Title: Gromyko Meets with Lai Yali

Author:

Date: 1959-06-03
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 2nd - TASS reports: Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko met today with Lai Yali, the Chinese Chargé d'affaires ad interim in Switzerland. Also present was Zemskov, Secretary-General of the Soviet delegation to the Geneva Conference.

### 葛罗米柯同赖亚力会谈

1959-06-03
第5版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯同赖亚力会谈
    新华社2日讯　塔斯社日内瓦讯：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯今天同中国驻瑞士临时代办赖亚力进行了会谈。在座的还有苏联参加日内瓦会议的代表团秘书长泽姆斯科夫。
