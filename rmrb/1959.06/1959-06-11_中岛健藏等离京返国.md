Title: Nakajima Kenzo and Others Leave Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1959-06-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 10th - The delegation of four members from the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association and the Japan-China Friendship Association, headed by Nakajima Kenzo, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

### 中岛健藏等离京返国

1959-06-11
第5版()
专栏：

    中岛健藏等离京返国
    新华社10日讯　以中岛健藏为首的日中文化交流协会、日中友好协会代表一行四人，今天上午乘飞机离开北京返国。
