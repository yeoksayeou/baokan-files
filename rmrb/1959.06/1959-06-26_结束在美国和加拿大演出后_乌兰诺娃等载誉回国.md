Title: Ulanova and Others Return Home with Honors After Performances in the United States and Canada
Author:
Date: 1959-06-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow Dispatch: According to TASS, the famous actors of the Soviet Bolshoi Theatre Ballet, Ulanova and Plisetskaya, etc., returned to Moscow on the evening of the 22nd after completing their tour performances in the United States and Canada.

The Bolshoi Theatre Ballet concluded its performances in the United States and Canada on the 21st. During the approximately two and a half month tour, they performed sixty-two shows, with an audience of nearly 400,000 people. Everywhere they went, they caused a great sensation.

After arriving in Canada, the Bolshoi Theatre performed the ballets "Swan Lake" and "Giselle" in the two major cities of Toronto and Montreal, and also held two concerts. More than 80,000 spectators enjoyed the performances. The grand occasion during the performances was unprecedented in the history of Canadian theater.

### 结束在美国和加拿大演出后  乌兰诺娃等载誉回国

1959-06-26
第5版()
专栏：

    结束在美国和加拿大演出后
    乌兰诺娃等载誉回国
    新华社讯　据塔斯社莫斯科消息：苏联大剧院芭蕾舞蹈团著名演员乌兰诺娃、普列谢茨卡娅等在结束了赴美国和加拿大的巡回演出后已于22日晚回到莫斯科。
    大剧院芭蕾舞蹈团是21日结束在美国和加拿大的演出的。在约莫两个半月的访问演出中，他们共演出六十二场，观众近四十万人。到处都引起很大的轰动。
    大剧院到了加拿大后，在多伦多和蒙特利尔两个大城市演出了舞剧“天鹅湖”和“吉赛尔”，并且举行了两个音乐会。有八万余名观众欣赏了演出。演出时的盛况是加拿大剧院历史上从来没有过的。
