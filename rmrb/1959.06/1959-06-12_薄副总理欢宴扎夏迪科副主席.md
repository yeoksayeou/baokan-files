Title: Vice Premier Bo Hosts Banquet in Honor of Vice Chairman Zasiadko
Author: 
Date: 1959-06-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 11th - Vice Premier of the State Council Bo Yibo hosted a banquet this evening to warmly welcome the Vice Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Zasiadko, and other accompanying Soviet dignitaries: Deputy Chairman of the Soviet State Scientific and Technical Committee Kolomytsev, Deputy Chairman of the State Construction Committee Korobov, Assistant to the Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers Tkachenko, and Director of the Economic Research Institute of the Coal Research Institute of the Academy of Sciences Khalchinko.

    Soviet Embassy in China Counselor Sudarikov and others were invited to attend the banquet.

    During the banquet, the hosts and guests had cordial conversations and raised their glasses to wish the great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people would be forever consolidated and forever developed.

    Among those present to accompany the guests were heads of relevant departments, including Zhang Linzhi, Zhao Erlu, Liu Xiangsan, Yang Zuocai, and Ji Pengfei.

    Before the banquet, Vice Premier Bo Yibo met with Vice Chairman Zasiadko and other Soviet dignitaries.
    Xinhua News Agency, June 11th - Vice Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers Zasiadko and other accompanying Soviet dignitaries, accompanied by Vice Minister of the Coal Industry Liu Xiangsan, left Beijing by train this evening to visit Tangshan.

    Vice Chairman Zasiadko and other Soviet dignitaries visited the National Coal Industry Exhibition and the Palace Museum in Beijing today.

### 薄副总理欢宴扎夏迪科副主席

1959-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

    薄副总理欢宴扎夏迪科副主席
    新华社11日讯　国务院副总理薄一波今天晚间举行宴会，热烈欢迎苏联部长会议副主席扎夏迪科和随行的其他苏联贵宾：苏联国家科学技术委员会副主席科洛米采夫，国家建设委员会副主席科罗鲍夫，部长会议副主席助理特卡钦科，科学院煤炭研究院经济研究所所长哈尔钦科。
    苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫等应邀参加了宴会。
    宴会上，宾主亲切交谈，举杯祝中苏两国人民的伟大友谊永远巩固，永远发展。
    出席作陪的，有各有关部门的负责人张霖之、赵尔陆、刘向三、杨作材、姬鹏飞等。
    宴会之前，薄一波副总理会见了扎夏迪科副主席和其他苏联贵宾。
    新华社11日讯　苏联部长会议副主席扎夏迪科和随行的其他苏联贵宾，由煤炭工业部副部长刘向三陪同，在今晚乘火车离开北京，前往唐山参观访问。
    扎夏迪科副主席和其他苏联贵宾今天在北京参观了全国煤炭工业展览会，游览了故宫。
