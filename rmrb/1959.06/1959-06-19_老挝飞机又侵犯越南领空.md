Title: Lao Aircraft Again Violate Vietnamese Airspace
Author:
Date: 1959-06-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 18th: According to a report by the Vietnam News Agency today, at 11:00 AM on June 16th, a Lao Kingdom military aircraft intruded into the airspace over the Meng Sheng southwestern region of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

This is the fourth time in two weeks that Lao Kingdom military aircraft have violated Vietnamese airspace.

### 老挝飞机又侵犯越南领空

1959-06-19
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝飞机又侵犯越南领空
    新华社河内18日电　据越南通讯社今天报道，6月16日十一时，老挝王国军用飞机一架侵入越南民主共和国孟生西南地区的领空。
    这是两星期来老挝王国军用飞机第四次侵犯越南领空。
