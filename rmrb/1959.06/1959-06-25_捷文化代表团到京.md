Title: Czechoslovak Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-06-25
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, June 24th: The Czechoslovak Cultural Delegation of five people, led by head Zdislav Briewal, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to China according to the 1959 executive plan of the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 捷文化代表团到京

1959-06-25
第5版()
专栏：

    捷文化代表团到京
    新华社24日讯　根据中捷文化合作协定1959年执行计划来我国访问的捷克斯洛伐克文化代表团一行五人，在团长兹吉斯拉夫·布瑞瓦尔率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
