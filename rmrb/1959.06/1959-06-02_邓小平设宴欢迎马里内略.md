Title: Deng Xiaoping Hosts Banquet to Welcome Marinello
Author:
Date: 1959-06-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st June. Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, hosted a banquet this afternoon to welcome Juan Marinello, Chairman of the Popular Socialist Party of Cuba. Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Ningyi, member of the CPC Central Committee, and Wu Xiuquan, among others, attended the banquet.

### 邓小平设宴欢迎马里内略

1959-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    邓小平设宴欢迎马里内略
    新华社1日讯　中共中央总书记邓小平今天下午设宴欢迎古巴人民社会党主席胡安·马里内略。参加宴会的有中共中央书记处书记王稼祥、候补书记杨尚昆，中共中央委员刘宁一和伍修权等。
