Title: Holding High the Banner of Defending Sino-Indian Friendship: Calcutta Masses Hold Unprecedented Demonstration, Condemning Those Who Convened the "All-India Tibet Conference" as Imperialist Agents
Author:
Date: 1959-06-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 31st: Calcutta dispatch: While some reactionaries were holding the so-called "All-India Tibet Conference" on the evening of the 30th, interfering in China's internal affairs, the people of Calcutta held an unprecedentedly grand demonstration to defend Sino-Indian friendship. This demonstration was jointly initiated by the Communist Party of India, the Marxist Forward Bloc, and the United Socialist Organization.

　　Tens of thousands of participants in the parade held high banners and shouted slogans: "Tibet is an indivisible part of China!" "Indians and Chinese are brothers!" The marching masses condemned those who convened the "All-India Tibet Conference" as "enemies of Panchsheel" and "agents of imperialists."

　　Leading the two-mile-long procession were: Indian Communist Party leaders Bhupesh Gupta and Muzaffar Ahmed, Marxist Forward Bloc leader Amar Basu, United Socialist Organization leader Mukherjee, and women's leader Manikuntala Sen. Among the marchers were many left-wing members of the State Legislative Assembly, members of Parliament, trade union leaders, workers, writers, artists, professors, students, farmers, and large numbers of women.

　　The parade was led by a red banner with the golden characters "Long Live Sino-Indian Friendship." When the procession reached the main streets of the city center, tens of thousands of citizens gathered on both sides of the street. The red flags of the left-wing parties flew alongside the flags of the West Bengal Peace Committee, decorated with doves of peace.

　　The parade lasted two and a half hours, passing through four miles of streets. Until night, loud slogans still echoed in the streets: "Down with the enemies of Sino-Indian friendship!" "Stop the slander against China!" "Smash the US-engineered conspiracy against China!" "Long live Sino-Indian and Indo-Soviet friendship!" "Down with the US-Pakistan military agreement!" "Down with imperialism!"

### 高举保卫印中友好的旗帜  加尔各答群众举行空前大示威  谴责召开“全印西藏会议”的人是帝国主义代理人

1959-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

    高举保卫印中友好的旗帜
    加尔各答群众举行空前大示威
    谴责召开“全印西藏会议”的人是帝国主义代理人
    新华社新德里31日电　加尔各答消息：当在30日晚，一些反动分子正在举行干涉中国内政的所谓“全印西藏会议”的时候，加尔各答人民举行了一次规模空前盛大的大游行来保卫印中友好。这次游行是由印度共产党、马克思主义者前进同盟和统一社会主义组织联合发起的。
    参加游行的成千上万的人高举着横幅标语，他们高呼口号：“西藏是中国不可分割的一部分！”“印度人和中国人是兄弟！”游行群众谴责那些召开“全印西藏会议”的人是“潘查希拉的敌人”和“帝国主义者的代理人”。
    走在长达两英里的游行队伍前面的有：印度共产党的领袖布佩希·古普塔、穆扎法尔·艾哈默德，马克思主义者前进同盟袖领阿马尔·巴苏，统一社会主义组织领袖穆克吉，妇女领袖马尼孔塔拉·森。在游行队伍中，有邦立法会议的许多左翼议员，国会议员、工会领袖、工人、作家、艺术家、教授、学生、农民以及大批妇女。
    游行队伍以写着“印中友好万岁”的金色大字的红色横幅标语为前导，当队伍到达市中心的主要街道时，街道两旁聚集着成千上万的市民。左翼政党的红旗同西孟加拉邦和平委员会的装饰着和平鸽的旗帜一起飘扬。
    这次游行历时二小时半，通过了四英里长的街道。直到夜间，响亮的口号声还在街上回响：“打倒印中友好的敌人！”“制止对中国的诽谤！”“粉碎美国策划的针对中国的阴谋！”“印中、印苏友好万岁！”“打倒美国—巴基斯坦军事协定！”“打倒帝国主义！”
