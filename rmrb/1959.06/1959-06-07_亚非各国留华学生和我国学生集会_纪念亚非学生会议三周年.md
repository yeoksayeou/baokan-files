Title: African and Asian Students in China and Chinese Students Gather to Commemorate the Third Anniversary of the Asian-African Students Conference

Author:
Date: 1959-06-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, June 6th. Asian and African students studying in China and Chinese students held a gathering at the Indonesian Embassy in China this evening to commemorate the third anniversary of the Asian-African Students Conference. European, American, and Australian students in Beijing were also invited to participate in the commemoration. Indonesian student Sazono, representing the preparatory committee of the Asian and African Students in China and Chinese Students Commemoration of the Third Anniversary of the Asian-African Students Conference, spoke first, elaborating on the significance of commemorating the third anniversary of the Asian-African Students Conference. Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, also spoke. Indonesian Embassy in China Cultural Counselor Indrosugondo delivered a speech on behalf of the Embassy's Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, congratulating the strengthening of friendship and cooperation between students from Asian and African countries.

### 亚非各国留华学生和我国学生集会  纪念亚非学生会议三周年

1959-06-07
第3版()
专栏：

    亚非各国留华学生和我国学生集会
    纪念亚非学生会议三周年
    新华社6日讯　亚非各国在华留学生和我国学生，今晚在印度尼西亚驻我国大使馆举行了纪念亚非学生会议三周年大会。在北京的欧洲、美洲和澳洲留学生也应邀参加了纪念会。印度尼西亚留学生沙佐诺代表亚非各国在华留学生与中国同学纪念亚非学生会议三周年大会筹备委员会首先讲话，阐述了纪念亚非学生会议三周年的意义。中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立也讲了话。印度尼西亚驻我国大使馆文化参赞英德罗苏贡多代表大使馆临时代办致词，祝贺亚非各国学生友好和合作的加强。
