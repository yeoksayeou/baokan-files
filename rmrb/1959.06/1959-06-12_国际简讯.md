International Briefs

    Malinovsky Visits Vienna
    Vienna News: Soviet Defense Minister Marshal Malinovsky and his entourage arrived in Vienna on the 10th for a friendly visit at the invitation of Austrian Defense Minister Graf.

    GDR and Morocco Sign Trade Agreement
    Berlin News: The German Democratic Republic and Morocco signed a trade agreement in Berlin on the 8th. The agreement stipulates that the trade volume between the two parties before March 31, 1960 will be $3,755,000 (equivalent to 8,261,000 marks) each, an increase of 41% compared to the trade volume between the two countries last year.

    Nixon Prepares to Go to Moscow
    Washington News: The White House Press Secretary announced on the 9th that U.S. Vice President Nixon will be accompanied by U.S. President's brother Milton Eisenhower to Moscow to preside over the opening ceremony of the American Exhibition there. Milton Eisenhower is a member of the U.S. Exhibition Advisory Committee.

    GDR Sculptural Art Exhibition Opens in Cairo
    Cairo News: An exhibition of sculptural art by famous sculptors from the German Democratic Republic opened in Cairo on the 8th. The exhibition features forty-nine sculptures and one hundred and twenty carvings.

    Soviet Union Increases Loan to Indonesia
    Jakarta News: The Soviet government has accepted the Indonesian government's request to increase the $100 million loan to Indonesia stipulated in the agreement signed on September 15, 1956, by an additional $17.5 million.

### 国际简讯

1959-06-12
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    马利诺夫斯基到维也纳访问
    维也纳讯：苏联国防部长马利诺夫斯基元帅及其随行人员应奥地利国防部长格拉夫的邀请，在10日到达维也纳进行友好的访问。
    德摩签订贸易协定
    柏林讯：德意志民主共和国和摩洛哥8日在柏林签订一项贸易协定。协定规定，在1960年3月31日前双方贸易额各为三百七十五万五千美元（相当于八百二十六万一千马克），比去年两国的贸易额增加了41％。
    尼克松准备去莫斯科
    华盛顿讯：白宫新闻秘书9日宣布，美国副总统尼克松将由美国总统的兄弟密尔顿·艾森豪威尔陪同前往莫斯科，主持在那里举行的美国展览会的开幕式。密尔顿·艾森豪威尔是美国展览会顾问委员会的委员。
    德意志民主共和国造型
    艺术展览会在开罗开幕
    开罗讯　德意志民主共和国著名雕塑家的造型艺术展览会8日在开罗开幕。展览会展出了四十九件雕塑品和一百二十件雕刻品。
    苏联增加对印度尼西亚的贷款
    雅加达讯　苏联政府已经接受印度尼西亚政府的请求，把1956年9月15日签订的协定所规定的给印度尼西亚的一亿美元贷款再增加一千七百五十万美元。
