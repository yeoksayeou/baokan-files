Title: China and Vietnam Sign Protocol on Surveying the Beibu Gulf

Author:

Date: 1959-06-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 27th: China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam signed a protocol here today in Hanoi regarding cooperation between the two countries in conducting a comprehensive marine survey of the Beibu Gulf.

Lu Wei, head of the delegation of the State Scientific and Technological Commission of China, and Le Duy Trinh, head of the delegation of the State Scientific Committee of Vietnam, signed the protocol on behalf of their respective governments.

Vietnamese Deputy Prime Minister and Chairman of the State Scientific Committee of Vietnam, Truong Chinh, attended the signing ceremony. Also present at the signing ceremony were Le Loc, Director of the Asian and African Department of the Vietnamese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Mo Yanzhong, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam.

### 中越签订调查北部湾海洋议定书

1959-06-28
第3版()
专栏：

    中越签订调查北部湾海洋议定书
    新华社河内27日电　中国和越南民主共和国今天在河内签订了两国合作进行北部湾海洋综合调查的议定书。
    中国国家科学技术委员会代表团团长律巍和越南国家科学委员会代表团团长黎维贞分别代表两国政府在议定书上签了字。
    越南副总理、越南国家科学委员会主任长征出席了签字仪式。出席签字仪式的还有越南外交部亚洲和非洲司司长黎禄和中国驻越南大使馆临时代办莫燕忠。
