Title: 400,000 Romanians Visit Chinese Economic Construction Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1959-06-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 15th - The Chinese Economic Construction Exhibition, which began here on May 15th, closed yesterday. Approximately 400,000 people visited the exhibition. The exhibition was originally scheduled to last for twenty-one days, but was extended for nine days at the request of the audience. Romanian Party and government leaders Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chivu Stoica, and others, as well as the Chinese military goodwill delegation headed by Marshal Peng Dehuai, visited the exhibition. Chinese films shown during the exhibition were also warmly welcomed, with nearly 40,000 viewers.

### 罗马尼亚四十万人参观我经济建设展览会

1959-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚四十万人参观我经济建设展览会
    新华社布加勒斯特15日电　自5月15日起在这里举行的中国经济建设展览会昨天闭幕。参观这次展览会的观众约有四十万人。展览会原订展出二十一天，后经观众要求又延长了九天。罗马尼亚党和政府领导人乔治乌—德治、基伏·斯托伊卡等和以彭德怀元帅为首的中国军事友好代表团都参观了展览会。在展览会举行期间放映的中国电影也受到热烈欢迎，观众将近四万人。
