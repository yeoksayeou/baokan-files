Title: Albanian People Vigorously Green Their Motherland: Four Months to Complete the Annual Tree Planting Plan
Author:
Date: 1959-06-02
Page: 5

    The Albanian People Vigorously Green Their Motherland
    Four Months to Complete the Annual Tree Planting Plan

    (Reported from Tirana on June 1) Albania has overfulfilled its annual tree planting plan in the first four months of this year.
    According to statistics from the Albanian Ministry of Agriculture, during this spring tree planting season, the country has planted more than 7,454,000 various fruit trees and olive trees, exceeding the original annual tree planting plan by more than 1,580,000 trees. The plan to open up 4,300 hectares of new vineyards this year has also been completed by 68% so far, with 5,740,000 grape vines planted.
    Albania is one of the countries with the most forests in Europe, with 46% of its land covered by forests. It is also a country rich in various fruits.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民大力绿化祖国   四个月完成全年植树计划

1959-06-02
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民大力绿化祖国
    四个月完成全年植树计划
    本报地拉那1日电　阿尔巴尼亚今年以头四个月的时间超额完成了全年的植树计划。
    根据阿尔巴尼亚农业部的统计，在今年春季植树季节内，全国已种植了各种果树和橄榄树七百四十五万四千多株，比原订的全年植树计划还多种植了一百五十八万多株。今年将新开辟四千三百公顷葡萄园的计划，到目前为止也已完成了68％，而栽种的葡萄藤已有五百七十四万株。
    阿尔巴尼亚是欧洲森林最多的国家之一，全国有46％的土地覆盖着森林。它也是盛产各种水果的国家。
