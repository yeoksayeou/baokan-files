Author:

    Great Construction Achievements
    ——At the Exhibition of Achievements of the National Economy of the Soviet Union
    The picture shows a powerful 140-horsepower tracked tractor for dragging and hoisting heavy materials. It is a powerful tool for the logging industry. The picture shows an industrial television, which can inspect the production process from a distance.
    The picture shows the Georgian Soviet Socialist Republic Pavilion. Teapot-shaped shelves are filled with various teas produced in Georgia.
    The picture shows the exterior of the newly built Radio and Electronics Pavilion at the Exhibition of Achievements of the National Economy of the Soviet Union.

### 伟大的建设成就——在苏联国民经济成就展览会上（图片）

1959-06-21
第4版()
专栏：

    伟大的建设成就
    ——在苏联国民经济成就展览会上
    图为一百四十匹马力强大履带式拖拉机，供拖曳和吊挂笨重物资时使用。它是伐木工业的有力工具。图为工业用电视机，可以在远距离内检视生产过程。
    图为在格鲁吉亚苏维埃社会主义共和国馆里。茶壶形的架子上摆满了格鲁吉亚出产的各种茶叶。
    图为在苏联国民经济成就展览会上新建立的无线电和电子学馆的外貌。　　塔斯社稿（新华社发）
