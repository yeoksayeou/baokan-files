Title: Premier Zhou Enlai's "Government Work Report" at the First Session of the Second National People's Congress
Author:
Date: 1959-06-12
Page: 6

    In our country, not only are all nationalities equal politically, but the more advanced nationalities, which are in the majority, have a responsibility to help the more backward nationalities, which are in the minority, in economic, political, and cultural aspects, so that everyone can progress together and develop together.
    --Premier Zhou Enlai in the "Government Work Report" at the First Session of the Second National People's Congress

### 周恩来总理在第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的“政府工作报告”

1959-06-12
第6版()
专栏：

    在我们的国家里，各个民族不但在政治上是平等的，而且在经济、政治、文化各方面，比较先进的、居于多数地位的民族有责任帮助比较落后的、居于少数地位的民族，使大家一起进步，共同发展。
    ——周恩来总理在第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的“政府工作报告”
