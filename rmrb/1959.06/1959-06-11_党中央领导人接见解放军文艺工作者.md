Title: Central Party Leaders Receive PLA Literary and Art Workers
Author:
Date: 1959-06-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th—Central Party leaders Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Peng Zhen, Kang Sheng, and Huang Kecheng received all the literary and art workers attending the Second Literary and Art Performance Conference of the Chinese People's Liberation Army this afternoon.
    Guo Moruo and Zhou Yang were present at the reception.
    Also present at the reception were General Gan Siqi, Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Navy Lieutenant General Luo Shunchu, and Air Force Major General Lin Jiebiao.
    Party Central Committee leaders Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, and others, received all the literary and art workers participating in the Second Literary and Art Performance of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on June 10. The picture shows the reception. Xinhua News Agency reporter Huang Huaide photo

### 党中央领导人接见解放军文艺工作者

1959-06-11
第1版()
专栏：

    党中央领导人接见解放军文艺工作者
    新华社10日讯　中共中央领导人毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来、彭真、康生、黄克诚，今天下午接见了参加中国人民解放军第二届文艺会演大会的全体文艺工作者。
    接见时在座的有郭沫若、周扬。
    接见时在座的还有甘泗淇上将、刘志坚中将、萧向荣中将、罗舜初海军中将、林接彪空军少将。
    党中央领导人毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来等，6月10日接见了参加中国人民解放军第二届文艺会演的全体文艺工作者。图为接见时情形。新华社记者　黄怀德摄
