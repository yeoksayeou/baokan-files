Title: CPSU Central Committee Plenary Session Continues
Author:
Date: 1959-06-28
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 27th: According to TASS, the Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union continued on the 26th and 27th in the Grand Kremlin Palace. The meeting unfolded discussions on various reports.
    At the meeting on the 26th, twenty comrades spoke, including Ukrainian automation device expert Melnik, State Construction Committee Chairman Kucherenko, Perm National Economic Council Chairman Soldatov of the Russian Federation, and Sobolev, director of the Glukhov Cotton Textile Combine of the Russian Federation.
    At the meeting on the 27th, speakers included Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Kaharov, Chairman of the State Planning Committee of the Tajik Republic, Pavlov, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, Aristov, Member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee, Beshev, Soviet Minister of Communications, and Kostousov, Chairman of the State Committee for Automation and Machine Building of the Soviet Council of Ministers, among others.
    The next meeting of the CPSU Central Committee Plenary Session is scheduled to be held at 10:00 AM on June 29th.

### 苏共中央全会继续举行

1959-06-28
第3版()
专栏：

    苏共中央全会继续举行
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会全体会议26日和27日继续在大克里姆林宫举行。会议对各项报告展开了讨论。
    在26日的会议上发言的有乌克兰自动化装置专家麦尔尼克、国家建设委员会主席库切连科、俄罗斯联邦的白尔姆国民经济委员会主席索尔达托夫、俄罗斯联邦的格卢霍夫棉纺联合工厂厂长索波列夫等二十位同志。
    在27日会议上发言的有苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫、塔吉克共和国国家计划委员会主席卡哈罗夫、全苏列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会第一书记巴夫洛夫、苏共中央主席团委员、苏共中央书记阿里斯托夫、苏联邮电部长别谢夫、苏联部长会议自动化和机器制造国家委员会主席科斯托乌索夫等人。
    苏共中央全会下次会议定于6月29日上午十时举行。
