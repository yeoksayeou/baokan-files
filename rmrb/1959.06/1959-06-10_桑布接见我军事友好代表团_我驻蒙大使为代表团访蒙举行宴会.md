Title: Sangbu Receives Our Military Friendship Delegation; Our Ambassador to Mongolia Hosts Banquet for the Delegation's Visit to Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1959-06-10
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, June 9th: Sangbu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of Mongolia, received the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation headed by Marshal Peng Dehuai today.
    Present at the reception were Yangjima, Damdinsuren, Hashganbay, Secretary Sharab, Minister of Military and Public Security, Lieutenant General Dorj, and Foreign Minister Shagdar Surun, all members of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of Mongolia.
    Also present at the reception was Xie Fusheng, Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia.
    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, June 9th: Xie Fusheng, Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, hosted a banquet tonight for the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation headed by Marshal Peng Dehuai's visit to Mongolia.
    Among those present at the banquet were Mongolian Party and government leaders Tsendenbal, Sangbu, Tsend, Baljinnyam, Tumur-Ochir, Regvalal, and Minister of Military and Public Security of Mongolia, Lieutenant General Dorj, and ministers of various departments.
    Diplomatic envoys from various brotherly countries to Mongolia also attended the banquet.
    In his speech at the banquet, Marshal Peng Dehuai expressed his heartfelt gratitude for the warm welcome and reception given to the delegation by the Mongolian people and the People's Army. He praised the remarkableachievements made by the Mongolian people in socialist construction under the leadership of the Mongolian People's RevolutionaryParty, and by the Mongolian People's Army in military training and construction labor.
    Marshal Peng Dehuai said that the Chinese people have received strong support from the Mongolian people in the struggle to build socialism, oppose imperialist aggression, and defend peace. The friendly cooperation between our two countries in political, economic, cultural and other fields, based on the principle of proletarian internationalism, has not only further enhanced unity and accelerated the socialist construction of the two countries, but also strengthened the unity and strength of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.
    Tsendenbal, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, spoke at the banquet. He said that the visit of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation headed by Comrade Peng Dehuai to Mongolia is a clear sign of the further consolidation of friendly cooperation between the Mongolian and Chinese peoples. The Chinese People's Liberation Army has made significant contributions in the struggle against domestic and foreign enemies. The Mongolian people regard the great achievements made by the Chinese people under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party in socialist construction as their own achievements and are happy about it. He said that the fraternal assistance given to Mongolia by the Soviet and Chinese peoples is of great significance to the development of Mongolia's economy and culture.
    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, June 8th: Accompanied by Lieutenant General Dorj, Minister of Military and Public Security of Mongolia, and Xie Fusheng, Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation headed by Marshal Peng Dehuai today visited the Zargaltangtu State Farm in the Central Province of Mongolia.
    Workers of the farm and Chinese workers who are helping to build the farm held a rally to welcome the delegation. Marshal Peng Dehuai spoke at the meeting. He thanked the farm for its welcome, wished the farm prosperous production, and encouraged the Chinese employees and Mongolian comrades to unite better and contribute to Mongolia's socialist construction.

### 桑布接见我军事友好代表团  我驻蒙大使为代表团访蒙举行宴会

1959-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    桑布接见我军事友好代表团
    我驻蒙大使为代表团访蒙举行宴会
    新华社乌兰巴托9日电　蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布今天接见了以彭德怀元帅为首的中国军事友好代表团。
    接见时在座的有蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团委员扬日玛、达木丁苏伦、哈什甘巴伊、秘书长沙拉布、军事公安部长道尔吉中将、外交部长沙格达尔苏伦。
    接见时在座的还有中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生。
    新华社乌兰巴托9日电　中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生今天晚上为以彭德怀元帅为首的中国军事友好代表团访问蒙古举行了宴会。
    出席宴会的有蒙古党和政府的领导人泽登巴尔、桑布、曾德、巴尔吉尼亚姆、图穆尔—奥奇尔、热格瓦拉尔以及蒙古军事公安部长道尔吉中将、各部部长等。
    各兄弟国家驻蒙古的外交使节也出席了宴会。
    彭德怀元帅在宴会上讲话时对蒙古人民和人民军给予代表团的热烈欢迎和接待表示了衷心的感谢。他赞扬了蒙古人民在蒙古人民革命党的领导下，在社会主义建设中以及蒙古人民军在军事训练和建设劳动中所取得的卓越成就。
    彭德怀元帅说，中国人民在建设社会主义、反对帝国主义侵略和保卫和平的斗争中得到了蒙古人民的有力支持。我们两国以无产阶级国际主义原则为基础，在政治、经济、文化等各方面的友好合作，不仅进一步增进了团结，加速了两国的社会主义建设，而且加强了以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结和力量。
    蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记、部长会议主席泽登巴尔在宴会上讲了话。他说，以彭德怀同志为首的中国军事友好代表团到蒙古访问，这是蒙中两国人民友好合作进一步巩固的鲜明标志。中国人民解放军在反对国内外敌人的斗争中作出了重大的贡献。蒙古人民把中国人民在中国共产党领导下，在社会主义建设中取得的伟大成就看作是自己的成就，并为此感到高兴。他说，苏联人民和中国人民给予蒙古的兄弟援助，对蒙古的经济文化的发展有很大的意义。
    新华社乌兰巴托8日电　以彭德怀元帅为首的中国军事友好代表团在蒙古军事公安部长道尔吉中将和中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生的陪同下，今天参观了蒙古中央省的扎尔格朗图国营农牧场。
    农场的职工和在这里帮助建设的中国工人举行集会，欢迎代表团。彭德怀元帅在会上讲了话。他感谢农牧场的欢迎，预祝农牧场的生产兴旺，并且勉励中国员工和蒙古同志更好地团结，为蒙古的社会主义建设做出贡献。
