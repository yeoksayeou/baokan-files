Title: Korean Industrial Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-06-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 31st - The Korean Industrial Delegation, led by the Vice Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Jung Il-ryong, arrived in Beijing by train this morning.

Vice Premier Jung Il-ryong and the members of the delegation were warmly welcomed at the train station by Vice Premier of the State Council Bo Yibo and other responsible persons.

The Korean Industrial Delegation consists of thirty-two people, including Minister of Machine Industry Choe Jae-woo, Minister of Coal Industry Kim Tae-geun, Director of the Light Industry Department of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party Lim Kye-cheol, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Metal Industry Heo Young-il, and directors of relevant professional bureaus, factory directors, engineers, workshop directors, etc.

Those who came to the station to welcome them included Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Deputy Director of the State Economic Commission Xue Muqiao, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building Wang Daohan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Coal Industry Liu Xiangsan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Light Industry Deng Jie, as well as responsible persons from the Ministry of Water Resources and Electric Power, the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry, the Ministry of Textile Industry, and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee.

Korean Ambassador to China, Ri Young-ho, and embassy staff members greeted them at the station. (Attached picture)

### 朝鲜工业参观团到北京

1959-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜工业参观团到北京
    新华社31日讯　由朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相郑一龙率领的朝鲜工业参观团，今天上午乘火车到达北京。
    郑一龙副首相和参观团团员们在火车站受到国务院副总理薄一波等负责人的热烈欢迎。
    朝鲜工业参观团由三十二人组成，其中有机械工业相崔载羽、煤炭工业相金泰根、劳动党中央委员会轻工业部长林桂哲、金属工业省副相许荣一和各有关专业局局长、工厂厂长、工程师、车间主任等。
    到车站欢迎的，有外交部副部长姬鹏飞、国家经济委员会副主任薛暮桥、第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵、煤炭工业部副部长刘向三、轻工业部副部长邓洁以及水利电力部、冶金工业部、纺织工业部和北京市人民委员会的有关负责人。
    朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐和大使馆人员到车站迎接。（附图片）
    朝鲜工业参观团5月31日乘火车到达北京。郑一龙副首相（左）在薄一波副总理陪同下步出车站。
