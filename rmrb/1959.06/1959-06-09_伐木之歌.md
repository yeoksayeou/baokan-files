Title: Song of the Lumberjack
Author: 
Date: 1959-06-09
Page: 2

    Song of the Lumberjack
    Blue sky above, mountains underfoot,
    Fleecy white clouds wrapped around the waist.
    With a swing of the axe, the sea of trees stirs,
    Felling great trees to compose poems.
    (Worker from the Yichun Forestry Area)

### 伐木之歌

1959-06-09
第2版()
专栏：

    伐木之歌
    头顶蓝天脚踏山
    绵绵白云腰中缠
    大斧一抡树海动
    伐倒大树成诗篇
    （伊春林区工人）
