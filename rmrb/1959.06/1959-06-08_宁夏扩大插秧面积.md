Title: Ningxia Expands Rice Transplanting Area
Author:
Date: 1959-06-08
Page: 3

Brief News

    Ningxia Expands Rice Transplanting Area
    The more than 800,000 mu of rice planned to be planted in the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region this year have generally begun sowing and transplanting.
    This year, the biggest feature of rice planting in the autonomous region is the greatly increased area of transplanting. In the past, rice planting here has always used the broadcast sowing method, and the yield was very low. Last year, more than 50,000 mu of rice in the region adopted the transplanting method, and all achieved bumper harvests. Therefore, this year, all people's communes planting rice are taking increasing the transplanting area as a major measure to increase rice production.

### 宁夏扩大插秧面积

1959-06-08
第3版()
专栏：简明新闻

    宁夏扩大插秧面积
    宁夏回族自治区今年计划种植的八十多万亩水稻普遍开始播种和插秧。
    今年，自治区水稻种植的最大特点是插秧面积大大增加。过去，这里种水稻一直沿用撒播的方法，产量很低。去年，全区有五万多亩水稻采取插秧的方法，都获得丰收。因此今年各个种植水稻的人民公社，都把增加插秧面积作为提高水稻产量的重大措施。
