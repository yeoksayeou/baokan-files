Title: Premier Zhou Meets Betregal
Author:
Date: 1959-06-16
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 15th: Premier Zhou Enlai and his wife met with Guillermo del Pedregal, former Vice President of the Republic of Chile, and his wife at noon today.

    Present at the meeting were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Ji Chao Ding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Lu Xuzhang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; and Shen Jian, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

    After the meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai and his wife had lunch with Guillermo del Pedregal and his wife. (Attached picture)

    Premier Zhou and his wife had a cordial conversation with Mr. and Mrs. Betregal.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dongao

### 周总理会见贝特雷加尔

1959-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理会见贝特雷加尔
    新华社15日讯　周恩来总理和夫人今天中午会见了智利共和国前副总统吉勒摩·德尔·贝特雷加尔和夫人。
    会见时在座的，有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎，对外贸易部副部长卢绪章，外交部美澳司司长申健。
    会见以后，周恩来总理和夫人同吉勒摩·德尔·贝特雷加尔和夫人共进午餐。（附图片）
    周总理和夫人和贝特雷加尔夫妇亲切交谈。
    新华社记者  刘东鳌摄
