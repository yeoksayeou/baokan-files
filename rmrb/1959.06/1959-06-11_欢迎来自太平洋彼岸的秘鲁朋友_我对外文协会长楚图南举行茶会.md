    Making a Friendly Visit to Our Country
    Indonesian Navy Training Ship Arrives in Guangzhou
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 10th -- The Indonesian Republic Navy training ship "Dewaruci," led by its commander Major Folez Suak, arrived in Guangzhou this morning for a friendly visit.
    Leading officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou, including Vice Admiral Zhao Qimin, Rear Admiral Zeng Sheng, Major General Huang Yiping, Air Force Major General Zhang Xisan, as well as Vice Mayor of Guangzhou Sun Leyi and 1,500 others, warmly welcomed the guests at Huangpu Military Port. Heads of the Polish, Vietnamese Democratic Republic, and Czechoslovakian consulates in Guangzhou also went to the dock to welcome the guests.

### 欢迎来自太平洋彼岸的秘鲁朋友  我对外文协会长楚图南举行茶会

1959-06-11
第5版()
专栏：

    来我国作友好访问
    印度尼西亚海军练习舰到广州
    新华社广州10日电　前来广州作友好访问的印度尼西亚共和国海军“德哇鲁芝”号练习舰，在该舰司令官福列兹·苏阿克少校率领下，今天上午到达广州。
    中国人民解放军驻广州陆、海、空军首长赵启民海军中将、曾生海军少将、黄一平少将、张西三空军少将及广州市副市长孙乐宜等一千五百人，到黄埔军港码头热烈欢迎客人。到码头欢迎客人的还有波兰、越南民主共和国、捷克斯洛伐克驻广州领事馆负责人。
