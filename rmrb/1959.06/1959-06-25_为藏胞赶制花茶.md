Title: Rushing to Make Scented Tea for Tibetan Compatriots
Author:
Date: 1959-06-25
Page: 2

    Rushing to Make Scented Tea for Tibetan Compatriots
    The Fuzhou Tea Factory in Fujian Province is currently rushing to produce a batch of the famous "Jasmine Tea" to meet the needs of the people in Tibet. This is the first time that Fuzhou scented tea has been shipped to Tibet. After accepting this task, the workers of the Fuzhou Tea Factory were all excited and striving to produce it ahead of schedule for shipment to Tibet.

### 为藏胞赶制花茶

1959-06-25
第2版()
专栏：

    为藏胞赶制花茶
    福建省福州茶厂最近正在赶制一批著名的“茉莉花茶”，供应西藏人民需要。福州花茶调运西藏还是第一次。福州茶厂接受这一任务后，职工们都以兴奋的心情，力争提前制成，调往西藏。
