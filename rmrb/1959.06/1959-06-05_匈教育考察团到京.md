Title: Hungarian Education Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-06-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. A four-member Hungarian education delegation, led by Professor Gelendás István, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a study tour of China, in accordance with the 1959 executive plan of the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Cui Zhongyuan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Education, and responsible persons from the Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences welcomed them at the airport.

### 匈教育考察团到京

1959-06-05
第5版()
专栏：

    匈教育考察团到京
    新华社4日讯  根据中匈文化合作协定1959年执行计划来我国考察的匈牙利教育工作者考察团一行四人，在团长格连达什·伊什特万教授率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。教育部办公厅主任崔仲远和中国农业科学院有关负责人到机场欢迎。
