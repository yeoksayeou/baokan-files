    An air force fighter unit shot down an American-made Chiang Kai-shek plane near Enping, Guangdong Province, on the evening of May 29th. The wreckage of the enemy plane fell in the Naguan area south of Enping. The picture shows the tail of the enemy plane wreckage on Naguan Mountain. Photo by Ren Mingfu (Xinhua News Agency draft fax photo)
    Top right corner: Lieutenant Jiang Zhelun of a certain fighter unit that shot down the enemy plane. Xinhua News Agency draft
