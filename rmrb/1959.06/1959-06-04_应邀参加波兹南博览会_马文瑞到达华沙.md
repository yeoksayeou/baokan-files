Title: Ma Wenrui Arrives in Warsaw to Attend Poznań Fair
Author:
Date: 1959-06-04
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 2nd: At the invitation of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland, Ma Wenrui, Minister of Labor of China, arrived in Warsaw by plane today.
    Ma Wenrui will lead the Chinese government delegation to attend the opening ceremony of the 28th Poznań International Fair. Members of the Chinese government delegation include: Wang Bingnan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, and Tian Lichun, Curator of the Chinese Pavilion at the Poznań International Fair.
    Welcoming Ma Wenrui at the airport were: Polish Minister of Foreign Trade Trampczynski, Minister of Labor and Social Welfare Zawadzki, and others. Ambassador Wang Bingnan also greeted him at the airport.
    Minister Ma Wenrui will stay in Poland for twelve days. In addition to attending the opening ceremony of the fair, he will also visit some cities in Poland.

### 应邀参加波兹南博览会  马文瑞到达华沙

1959-06-04
第5版()
专栏：

    应邀参加波兹南博览会
    马文瑞到达华沙
    新华社华沙2日电　我国劳动部长马文瑞应波兰部长会议主席的邀请，今天乘飞机到达华沙。
    马文瑞将率领中国政府代表团参加第二十八届波兹南国际博览会开幕式。中国政府代表团团员有：中国驻波兰大使王炳南和波兹南国际博览会中国展览馆馆长田立春。
    到机场迎接马文瑞的有：波兰对外贸易部长特隆普钦斯基、劳动和社会福利部长萨瓦茨基等人。王炳南大使也到机场迎接。
    马文瑞部长将在波兰逗留十二天。他除参加博览会开幕式外，还将访问波兰的一些城市。
