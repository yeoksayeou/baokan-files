Title: Mexican Guests End Visit to China, Leave Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1959-06-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 16th—Mexican guests, Dr. Rozaya and his wife, the director of the Mexican Military Medical Hospital, and Mr. and Mrs. Agenlela, road construction engineers, concluded their visit to China and left Beijing by plane today to return home.

These four Mexican guests arrived in Beijing on June 8th. During their stay in Beijing, Li Dequan, Minister of Public Health, met with them.

### 结束在我国的访问  墨西哥客人离京回国

1959-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    结束在我国的访问
    墨西哥客人离京回国
    新华社16日讯　墨西哥军医医院院长罗扎亚夫妇和筑路工程师阿根来拉夫妇，结束了在我国的访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    这四位墨西哥客人是在6月8日到北京的。在北京期间，卫生部部长李德全曾接见了他们。
