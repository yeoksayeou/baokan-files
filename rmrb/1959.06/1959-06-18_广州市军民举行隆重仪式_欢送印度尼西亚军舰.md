Title: Guangzhou Military and Civilians Hold Grand Ceremony to See Off Indonesian Warship
Author:
Date: 1959-06-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, June 17th: Today, 1,500 people in Guangzhou warmly saw off the Indonesian Navy training ship "Dewaruci," which had come to Guangzhou for a friendly visit.

    In the morning, a grand farewell ceremony was held at Huangpu New Port Wharf. Sun Leyi, Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou, said at the meeting that this friendly visit by the guests has further strengthened the friendship between the two countries' armies and peoples. Commander Suak, Major of the "Dewaruci" training ship, said that during our visit to Guangzhou, we discovered that our two peoples have a solid friendship. He praised the Chinese people for building their motherland with hard work. He enthusiastically shouted: Long live our common cause of striving for national independence and anti-colonialism, long live the friendship between our two countries.

    Zeng Sheng, Rear Admiral of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou, Gui Shaobin, Rear Admiral of the Navy, and Liu Xinglong, Major General, as well as Xie Zhiguang, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the CPPCC, and heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations came to see them off. Amidst the band playing farewell music and enthusiastic applause, the hosts and guests said goodbye reluctantly.

    Also present to see them off were: Lieutenant Colonel Ratumahirra, Acting Military Attaché of the Indonesian Embassy in China, and his wife, and Le Chung Thuy, Acting Consul General of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Guangzhou.

    During their visit to Guangzhou, the guests visited the site of the Peasant Movement Training Institute, which was hosted by Comrade Mao Zedong, and the Guangzhou Uprising Martyrs Cemetery, as well as visited the People's Commune in the suburbs. Major Suak praised the free and happy labor and life of the commune members, saying that everything in the commune is progressing.

### 广州市军民举行隆重仪式  欢送印度尼西亚军舰

1959-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    广州市军民举行隆重仪式
    欢送印度尼西亚军舰
    新华社广州17日电　今天，广州市一千五百多人热烈欢送前来广州市进行友好访问的印度尼西亚共和国海军“德哇鲁芝”号练习舰。
    上午，黄埔新港码头举行了隆重的欢送仪式。广州市副市长孙乐宜在会上说，这次客人的友好访问，使两国军队和人民的友谊更进一步加强了。“德哇鲁芝”号练习舰司令官苏阿克少校说，我们这次访问广州，发现我们两国人民有巩固的友谊。他赞扬中国人民正在用辛勤的劳动建设自己的祖国。他热情地高呼：我们争取祖国独立和反殖民主义的共同事业万岁，我们两国的友谊万岁。
    中国人民解放军驻广州陆海军首长曾生海军少将、桂绍彬海军少将和刘兴隆少将及政协广州市委员会副主席谢志光、各民主党派和人民团体负责人等都前来送行。在乐队高奏欢送曲和热烈掌声中，主宾依依告别。
    前来送行的还有：印度尼西亚驻华代理武官拉图玛希拉中校和夫人，越南民主共和国驻广州代总领事黎终始。
    客人在访问广州期间，曾先后参观游览了毛泽东同志主办的农民运动讲习所旧址和广州起义烈士陵园，以及参观访问了市郊的人民公社。苏阿克少校赞扬了公社社员们自由愉快的劳动和生活，公社的一切都在前进中。
