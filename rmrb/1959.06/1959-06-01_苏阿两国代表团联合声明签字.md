Title: Signing of Joint Statement by Soviet and Albanian Delegations
Author:
Date: 1959-06-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, May 30th. The joint statement of the Soviet and Albanian Party and Government delegations was signed in Tirana this afternoon.

Signing the joint statement on the Soviet side was Nikita Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union; on the Albanian side were First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Hoxha, and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Shehu.

Attending the signing ceremony were all members of the Soviet Party and Government delegation and other Albanian Party and Government leaders such as Baluku, Nushi, and Leshi.

In the evening, the Albanian Labor Party and the Council of Ministers jointly held a grand banquet to welcome the Soviet Party and Government delegation headed by Khrushchev.

The Chinese military friendship delegation led by Marshal Peng Dehuai was also invited to attend the banquet.

### 苏阿两国代表团联合声明签字

1959-06-01
第5版()
专栏：

    苏阿两国代表团联合声明签字
    新华社地拉那30日电　苏联和阿尔巴尼亚两国党政代表团联合声明，今天下午在地拉那签字。
    在联合声明上签字的，苏联方面是苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记，苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫；阿尔巴尼亚方面是阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查和部长会议主席谢胡。
    参加签字仪式的有：苏联党政代表团全体团员和阿尔巴尼亚其他党政领导人巴卢库、努什和列希等。
    晚上，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和部长会议联合举行盛大宴会，欢迎以赫鲁晓夫为首的苏联党政代表团。
    由彭德怀元帅率领的中国军事友好代表团也应邀出席了宴会。
