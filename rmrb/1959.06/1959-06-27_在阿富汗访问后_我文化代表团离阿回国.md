Title: Our Cultural Delegation Leaves Afghanistan for Home After Visit
Author:
Date: 1959-06-27
Page: 4

    Following a Visit to Afghanistan
    Our Cultural Delegation Leaves Afghanistan for Home
    Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, June 26th: The Chinese cultural delegation headed by Qu Wu, left Kabul on the 25th to return home after a two-week visit to Afghanistan.
    Among those at the airport to see off the delegation were the Afghan Deputy Minister of Education, Dr. Anas, and other officials.

### 在阿富汗访问后  我文化代表团离阿回国

1959-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    在阿富汗访问后
    我文化代表团离阿回国
    新华社26日讯　喀布尔消息：以屈武为首的中国文化代表团在阿富汗作了二星期的访问后，25日离喀布尔回国。
    到机场欢送代表团的有阿富汗王国教育副大臣阿纳斯博士和其他官员。
