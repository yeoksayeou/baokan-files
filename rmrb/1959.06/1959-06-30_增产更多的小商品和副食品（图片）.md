Title: Produce More Small Commodities and Non-staple Foods (Photos)
Author: Zhang Qingyun, Jin Xueqi, Yang Putao, Zhang Ping
Date: 1959-06-30
Page: 1

Increase the production of more small commodities and non-staple foods

From left: 1. A large quantity of high-quality soap produced by the by-product workshop of the Zhengzhou Oil and Grease Chemical Plant in Henan Province is being packed and prepared for shipment. 2. The bamboo chopsticks produced in Chengdu, Sichuan Province are famous throughout the country. This is a worker at the Chengdu Bamboo Ware Factory manufacturing bamboo chopsticks. 3. The workers of the Shanghai Instrument Factory have raised more than 170 pigs, the larger ones already weighing more than 150 jin. Xinhua News Agency reporters Zhang Qingyun, Jin Xueqi, Yang Putao, Zhang Ping photographed.

### 增产更多的小商品和副食品（图片）
张青云  金学琪  杨溥涛  张萍
1959-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    增产更多的小商品和副食品
    左起：1、河南郑州市油脂化工厂副产品车间生产的大批优质肥皂正在装箱准备外运。2、四川成都市生产的竹筷驰名全国。这是成都竹器制造厂的工人在制造竹筷。3、上海仪表厂职工喂养了一百七十多头猪，大的已经长到一百五十多斤重了。　新华社记者　张青云、金学琪、杨溥涛、张萍摄
