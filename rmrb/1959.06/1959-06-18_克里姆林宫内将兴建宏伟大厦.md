Title: Magnificent Building to be Constructed in the Kremlin

Author:

Date: 1959-06-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 16th (TASS): A grand building for holding congresses and other mass activities will be constructed in the area of the Moscow Kremlin. This building will be completed before October 15, 1960.

    The design work for the building has already been started by some architects in Moscow. The building will have modern television, radio, telephone, and film equipment. Large-scale preparatory work will begin this year.

### 克里姆林宫内将兴建宏伟大厦

1959-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    克里姆林宫内将兴建宏伟大厦
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科16日讯：莫斯科克里姆林宫地区内将兴建一所规模宏伟的举行代表大会和进行其他群众性活动的大厦。这座大厦将在1960年10月15日以前落成。
    大厦设计工作已由莫斯科一些建筑师开始设计，大厦将有现代化的电视、无线电、电话和电影等设备。今年将开始大规模的准备工作。
