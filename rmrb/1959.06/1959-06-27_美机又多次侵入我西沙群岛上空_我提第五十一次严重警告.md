Title: U.S. Planes Repeatedly Invade Airspace Over Xisha Islands; We Issue 51st Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1959-06-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 26th: At 9:45 AM and 11:00 AM on June 25th, a U.S. Navy reconnaissance plane twice intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island in China's Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. At 10:24 AM on the same day, a U.S. Navy patrol plane also intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island, Beidao Island, and Nandao Island in China's Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, a spokesman for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 51st serious warning.

### 美机又多次侵入我西沙群岛上空  我提第五十一次严重警告

1959-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

    美机又多次侵入我西沙群岛上空
    我提第五十一次严重警告
    新华社26日讯　6月25日九时四十五分和十一时零分，美国海军侦察机一架，两次侵入我国广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛上空。同日十时二十四分，美国海军巡逻机一架，又侵入我国广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、北岛、南岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第五十一次严重警告。
