Title: Poznań International Fair Opens; Cyrankiewicz and Others Visit Chinese Pavilion
Author:
Date: 1959-06-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Poznań, 7th June: The 28th Poznań International Fair grandly opened here at 10:00 this morning.

Polish party and government leaders Cyrankiewicz, Jędrychowski, Loga-Sowiński, Ochab, and thousands of representatives from Polish factories, enterprises, and other sectors attended the opening ceremony of the fair. Also attending the opening ceremony were government delegations from eleven countries, including the Soviet Union, China, Albania, Bulgaria, Hungary, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the German Democratic Republic, Korea, Romania, Czechoslovakia, and Iraq, invited by the Polish government to participate in the Poznań Fair.

Xinhua News Agency, Poznań, 7th June: After the opening of the 28th Poznań International Fair today, Polish Chairman of the Council of Ministers Cyrankiewicz, Polish United Workers' Party Political Bureau member Loga-Sowiński, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers Nowak, and Minister of Foreign Trade Trampczyński visited the Chinese Pavilion.

Comrade Cyrankiewicz visited the pavilion and left a message in the guest book. He wrote: "I am very pleased that our Chinese friends are exhibiting more and more rich goods and machines to the Polish public year by year. These products are produced at lightning speed by the great and flourishing Chinese industry. I wish the cooperation between Poland and China to develop and expand day by day. Long live the friendship between Poland and China."

### 波兹南国际博览会开幕  西伦凯维兹等参观中国馆

1959-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    波兹南国际博览会开幕
    西伦凯维兹等参观中国馆
    新华社波兹南7日电　第二十八届波兹南国际博览会今天上午十时在此间隆重开幕。
    波兰党政领导人西伦凯维兹、英德里霍夫斯基、洛加—索文斯基、奥哈布以及波兰工厂、企业等各界代表数千人参加了博览会的开幕式。参加开幕式的还有应波兰政府邀请前来参加波兹南博览会的苏联、中国、阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、匈牙利、越南民主共和国、德意志民主共和国、朝鲜、罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克和伊拉克等十一个国家的政府代表团。
    新华社波兹南7日电　今天在第二十八届波兹南国际博览会开幕以后，波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹、波兰统一工人党政治局委员洛加—索文斯基、部长会议副主席诺瓦克和对外贸易部部长特隆姆普钦斯基参观了中国馆。
    西伦凯维兹同志参观后并且在留言簿上留言。他写道：“我们的中国朋友一年比一年更多地向波兰公众展出丰富的商品和机器，这些产品都是伟大的中国的蓬勃发展的工业，以闪电般的速度生产出来的，这使我感到非常高兴。祝波中两国的合作日益发展和扩大。波中两国的友谊万岁”。
