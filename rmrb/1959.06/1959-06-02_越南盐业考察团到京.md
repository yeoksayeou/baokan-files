Title: Vietnamese Salt Industry Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-06-02
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, June 1st. The Vietnamese Salt Industry Delegation, a group of eleven led by Zhang Guangfan, Deputy Director of the Salt Affairs Bureau of the Vietnamese Ministry of Finance, arrived in Beijing by train today.
    Liang Hongzhai, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Light Industry, Jiang Shiyan, Director of the Salt Industry Bureau, and others were at the station to welcome them.

### 越南盐业考察团到京

1959-06-02
第5版()
专栏：

    越南盐业考察团到京
    新华社1日讯　越南盐业考察团一行十一人，在越南财政部盐务总局副局长张光翻率领下，今天乘火车到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有轻工业部办公厅主任梁鸿斋、制盐工业局局长姜时彦等人。
