Title: Panchen Erdini Returns to Tibet from Xining; Vice Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, Wang Feng, Travels on the Same Flight
Author:
Date: 1959-06-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Xining, 6th - Panchen Erdini Qoigyijabu, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Acting Director of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, returned to Tibet from Xining by special plane on the 5th. Wang Feng, Vice Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, also traveled to Tibet on the same flight.

Jan Tung Jigme, a representative of the National People's Congress, Ngawang Jinba, a member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and other accompanying officials also left Xining by plane.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Xie Hechou, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, who accompanied Panchen Erdini to Xining, Huang Zhengqing, Vice Governor of Gansu Province, as well as Gao Feng, First Secretary of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the CPC, Yuan Renyuan, Governor of Qinghai Province, Colonel Qi Weichuan of the Qinghai Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, Ye Xipeng, Secretary of the Xining Municipal Committee of the CPC and Mayor of Xining, Chuanba Pengcuo, Acting Director of the Panchen Erdini Office in Xining, and other responsible party and government officials and representatives from all ethnic groups in Qinghai Province, totaling more than 300 people.

Before his departure, the Qinghai Provincial People's Committee held a banquet to bid farewell to Panchen Erdini and his accompanying officials. Panchen Erdini also held a banquet on the 4th to bid farewell to the leading cadres of Qinghai Province and representatives from all ethnic groups.

On the 3rd, Panchen Erdini and his accompanying officials went to Huangzhong County to pay homage to the birthplace of Tsongkhapa, the founder of the Yellow Sect of Tibetan Buddhism – Ta'er Monastery. The lamas of the entire monastery greeted Panchen Erdini with the most solemn religious ceremony. Panchen Erdini offered "Dönchog" (one thousand butter lamps) to the statue of Tsongkhapa and performed solemn prostration rituals and presented a Hada (ceremonial scarf) to the statue of Tsongkhapa. Then, at the invitation of the monks of Ta'er Monastery, he lectured on the "Gadarajama" scripture written by Tsongkhapa to all the monks in the Great Sutra Hall. After the lecture, Jiasui, the Abbot of Ta'er Monastery, on behalf of all the monks, presented Panchen Erdini with forty-three Tibetan scriptures written by Tsongkhapa, Kezhiji, and Jiacaiji, as well as three statues of the Buddha of Longevity and other gifts. Panchen Erdini also paid homage to the statues of the previous Panchen Lamas and the statue of Sakyamuni Buddha and participated in the "Buddha Feast" held by the monks of Ta'er Monastery.

During his stay in Qinghai, Panchen Erdini also visited Qinghai Lake, the largest inland lake in China, and visited the Riyue Mountain Brigade of the Daotang River People's Commune in Hainan Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture, where he learned in detail about the production development and the lives of the commune members.

Panchen Erdini and his entourage, accompanied by Xie Hechou, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, left Beijing by train on May 25 and arrived in Lanzhou on the 27th. During his stay in Lanzhou, Panchen Erdini visited the Lanzhou Refinery, the Lanzhou Chemical Plant, the Lanzhou Petrochemical Machinery Plant, and the Northwest Nationalities Institute, where he was warmly welcomed by the masses. The Gansu Provincial People's Committee held a farewell banquet for Panchen Erdini on May 30. On May 31, Panchen Erdini, accompanied by Xie Hechou, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, Huang Zhengqing, Vice Governor of Gansu Province, and Zaxi Wangxu, Vice Governor of Qinghai Province who went to Lanzhou to greet him, arrived in Xining by car from Lanzhou.

### 班禅额尔德尼离西宁回西藏  中共中央统战部副部长汪锋同机前往

1959-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    班禅额尔德尼离西宁回西藏
    中共中央统战部副部长汪锋同机前往
    新华社西宁6日电　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，5日从西宁乘专机回西藏。同机前往西藏的还有中共中央统一战线工作部副部长、民族事务委员会副主任汪锋。
    全国人民代表大会代表詹东·计晋美，政协全国委员会委员纳旺金巴以及其他随行官员，也一同乘飞机离开西宁。
    前往机场欢送的有陪同班禅额尔德尼来西宁的民族事务委员会副主任谢鹤筹、甘肃省副省长黄正清，以及中共青海省委第一书记高峰、青海省省长袁任远，人民解放军青海军区齐渭川大校，中共西宁市委书记、西宁市市长叶锡彭，班禅驻西宁办事处代理处长川巴彭措和青海省其他党政负责人、各族各界代表共三百多人。
    行前，青海省人民委员会举行宴会，为班禅额尔德尼和他的随行官员饯行。班禅额尔德尼也曾在4日举行宴会，向青海省的领导干部和各族各界代表告别。
    3日，班禅额尔德尼和他的随行官员曾到湟中县朝拜了黄教创始人宗喀巴诞生地——塔尔寺。全寺喇嘛用最隆重的宗教仪式迎接了班禅额尔德尼。班禅额尔德尼向宗喀巴像献了“顿却”（一千盏酥油灯），并且向宗喀巴塑像行了隆重的叩头礼和献了哈达；接着，应塔尔寺僧众的邀请，在大经堂向全寺僧众讲解了宗喀巴著的“尕达拉加玛”经。讲经结束后，塔尔寺法台嘉随代表全体僧众向班禅额尔德尼献了宗喀巴、客智机、嘉才机等著的四十三部藏经，还献了三尊长寿佛像等礼物。班禅额尔德尼还朝拜了前世班禅佛像和释迦牟尼佛像，参加了塔尔寺僧众举行的“佛宴”。
    班禅额尔德尼在青海期间，还游览了我国最大的内陆湖——青海湖，访问了海南藏族自治州倒淌河人民公社日月山大队，详细地了解了这个大队的生产发展和社员生活等情况。
    班禅额尔德尼等一行由民族事务委员会副主任谢鹤筹陪同，5月25日乘火车离开北京后，在27日到达兰州。在兰州期间，班禅额尔德尼先后参观了兰州炼油厂、兰州化工厂、兰州石油化工机械厂和西北民族学院等单位，受到了群众的热烈欢迎。甘肃省人民委员会曾在5月30日为班禅额尔德尼举行欢送宴会。5月31日，班禅额尔德尼由民族事务委员会副主任谢鹤筹、甘肃省副省长黄正清和前往兰州迎接的青海省副省长札喜旺徐等陪同，乘汽车从兰州到达西宁。
