Title: Albanian Government Agrees to Convene Balkan Conference to Make Balkans a Region of Peace and Friendship
Author:
Date: 1959-06-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, June 16th: According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency, the Government of the People's Republic of Albania issued a statement on the 15th.

The statement said that it unreservedly supports the Romanian government's proposal to convene a meeting of the heads of government of the Balkan countries in order to reach a common agreement to transform the Balkan region into a zone free of atomic weapons and rocket weapons, and expressed its willingness to participate in such a meeting.

The statement said that the Albanian government believes that, due to the development of the international situation, especially the situation in the Balkan and Adriatic regions, the convening of such a meeting has become urgently necessary.

The statement emphasized that the victory of socialism in some Balkan countries and the new balance of world forces favorable to peace and progress have ended the era of the Balkans as a powder keg. The Albanian people will never allow imperialist countries to turn the Balkans into a breeding ground for atomic war.

The statement concluded that the Government of the People's Republic of Albania hopes that all Balkan countries will treat the proposal of the Romanian People's Republic government with a positive attitude, and unite and work together to reach an agreement to make the Balkans a region of peace and friendship, and a powerful factor in maintaining security and international mutual understanding.

### 阿尔巴尼亚政府赞同召开巴尔干会议  让巴尔干成为和平友好地区

1959-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚政府赞同召开巴尔干会议
    让巴尔干成为和平友好地区
    新华社地拉那16日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府15日发表声明。
    声明说，它毫无保留地支持罗马尼亚政府关于召开巴尔干地区各国政府首脑会议的建议，以便达成共同协议，把巴尔干地区变成为一个无原子武器和无火箭武器的地区，并表示它准备参加这样的会议。
    声明说，阿尔巴尼亚政府认为，由于国际局势的发展，特别是由于巴尔干和亚得里亚海地区的局势，使这种会议的召开成为迫切必须的。
    声明强调指出，社会主义在一些巴尔干国家的胜利和有利于和平和进步的世界力量的新对比，结束了巴尔干做为火药库的时代。阿尔巴尼亚人民绝不允许帝国主义国家把巴尔干变成为原子战争的策源地。
    声明最后说，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府希望全体巴尔干地区国家以积极的态度来对待罗马尼亚人民共和国政府的建议，并且联合起来，共同努力，达成协议，以使巴尔干变成为一个和平和友好的地区，变成一个维护安全及国际互相谅解的强大因素。
