Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Japanese Small and Medium-Sized Enterprise Workers Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1959-06-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 21st – Premier Zhou Enlai received the Japanese Small and Medium-Sized Enterprise Workers Delegation, headed by Ishikawa Shigeru, Director of the Dispute Countermeasures Department of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, this afternoon. Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Liu Changsheng, and Chen Yu.

(Attached picture)
Premier Zhou is in cordial conversation with Ishikawa Shigeru (second from right) and others. Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao.

### 周恩来总理接见日本中小企业工人代表团

1959-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见日本中小企业工人代表团
    新华社21日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了以日本工会总评议会争议对策部部长石川茂为首的日本中小企业工人代表团。接见时在座的有廖承志、刘长胜和陈宇等。

    周总理和石川茂（右二）等亲切交谈。　新华社记者　孟庆彪摄
