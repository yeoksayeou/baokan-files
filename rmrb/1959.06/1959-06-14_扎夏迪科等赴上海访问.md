Title: Zasyadko and Others Visit Shanghai

Author: 
Date: 1959-06-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 13th. Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Zasyadko and other Soviet guests accompanying him, accompanied by Vice Minister of the Coal Industry Liu Xiangsan, left Beijing by special plane this morning to visit Shanghai.

On the 12th, the distinguished guests visited in detail the entire production process of China's first hydraulic coal mining well in the Tangjiazhuang Mine of the Kailuan Coal Mine near Tangshan, and also visited the Tangshan Steel Plant and the Tangshan Iron Plant, receiving a warm welcome from the workers of these factories and mines. The distinguished guests returned to Beijing by train that evening.

### 扎夏迪科等赴上海访问

1959-06-14
第3版()
专栏：

    扎夏迪科等赴上海访问
    新华社13日讯　苏联部长会议副主席扎夏迪科和随行的其他苏联贵宾，由煤炭工业部副部长刘向三陪同，在今天上午乘专机离开北京，前往上海参观访问。
    贵宾们12日在唐山附近开滦煤矿唐家庄矿井详细地参观了我国第一座水力采煤矿井的全部生产过程，还参观了唐山钢厂和唐山铁厂，受到这些厂矿职工的热烈欢迎。贵宾们当晚乘火车回到北京。
