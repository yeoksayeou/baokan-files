Title: Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Hosts a Banquet in Honor of Former Chilean Vice President Betregal
Author:
Date: 1959-06-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 9th: Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet tonight in honor of Guillermo del Pedregal, former Vice President of the Republic of Chile, and his wife, who are currently visiting China.

　　Zhang Xiruo delivered a speech at the banquet. He extended a warm welcome on behalf of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to the Chilean guests who had come from afar. He said that what is most needed in the international arena now is mutual understanding and friendship between the people of all countries. He believes that the visit of Mr. and Mrs. Betregal to China will help to further develop the friendship between the Chinese and Chilean peoples.

　　In his speech, Betregal expressed his heartfelt gratitude for the friendly reception of the Chinese people. He said that the purpose of his visit to China was to understand the situation of China's economic construction. He believes that Chile and China can have a large degree of mutual supplementation in their economies. He said, "Therefore, my visit to China is in the hope of realizing as soon as possible the establishment of trade relations between the two countries, which is very important for both of us."

　　Betregal said that although he has not been in China for a long time, he has already seen the spirit of international friendship of the Chinese people and the desire of the Chinese people not only to build their own country well, but also to hope that other countries will be built well.

　　During the banquet, the hosts and guests had a cordial conversation and mutually wished for the increasing understanding and friendship between the Chinese and Chilean peoples.

　　Among those present were Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Zhou Gengsheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Ji Chaoding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; and Le Songsheng, Vice Mayor of Beijing. Mr. and Mrs. Jose Venturelli, Chilean peace activists in Beijing, were also invited to attend the banquet.

### 我国人民外交学会会长张奚若  设宴欢迎智利前副总统贝特雷加尔

1959-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    我国人民外交学会会长张奚若
    设宴欢迎智利前副总统贝特雷加尔
    新华社9日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今晚设宴欢迎正在我国访问的智利共和国前副总统吉勒摩·德尔·贝特雷加尔夫妇。
    张奚若在宴会上首先致词。他代表中国人民外交学会热诚欢迎远道而来的智利客人们。他说，现在国际上最需要的是各国人民间的相互了解和友谊。他相信贝特雷加尔夫妇这次到中国来访问，将有助于中智两国人民之间的友谊进一步发展。
    贝特雷加尔在讲话中对中国人民的友好接待表示衷心感谢。他说，到中国来是要了解中国经济建设方面的情况。他认为智利和中国在经济上可以有很大程度的互相补充。他说：“因此，我到中国来访问是希望尽快实现在两国之间建立对我们两国都非常重要的贸易关系。”
    贝特雷加尔说，他来到中国的时间虽不长，但已经看到了中国人民的国际友爱的精神和中国人民不仅要把自己的国家建设好，而且也希望其他国家建设好的愿望。
    席间，宾主亲切交谈，互祝中智两国人民之间相互了解和友谊的日益增长。
    出席作陪的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，中国人民外交学会副会长周鲠生，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎，北京市副市长乐松生等。在北京的智利和平人士何塞·万徒勒里夫妇也应邀出席了宴会。
