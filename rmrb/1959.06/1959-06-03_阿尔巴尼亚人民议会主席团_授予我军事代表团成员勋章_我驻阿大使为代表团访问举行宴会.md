Title: Albanian People's Assembly Presidium Awards Medals to Members of Chinese Military Delegation; Chinese Ambassador to Albania Hosts Banquet for Delegation's Visit
Author:
Date: 1959-06-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, June 1st. The Presidium of the Albanian People's Assembly held a grand award ceremony on the afternoon of June 1st, awarding two types of medals to all members of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation.

At the award ceremony, Haxhi Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the Albanian People's Assembly, awarded the First Class "Skanderbeg" Medal to Marshal Peng Dehuai, head of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense. He awarded the First Class "Partisan" Medal to Grand General Wang Shusheng, Senior General Zhang Zongxun, Senior General Xiao Hua, and Senior General Yang Dezhi. He awarded the Second Class "Skanderbeg" Medal to Senior General Chen Bojun, Air Force Major General Chen Xi, and Navy Major General Zhang Xuesi. He awarded the Second Class "Partisan" Medal to Senior Colonel Lu Yang, Senior Colonel Zhu Kaiyin, Colonel Feng Zheng, and Colonel Zheng Wenhan.

Attending the award ceremony were Enver Baluku, First Deputy Chairman of the Albanian Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Kellezi, and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Shtulla, among others.

Also present at the award ceremony was Luo Shigao, Chinese Ambassador to Albania.

In his speech at the award ceremony, Lleshi said that the Albanian People's Assembly's awarding of medals to the members of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation is a manifestation of the Albanian people's love for the fraternal Chinese people. He said that the Chinese People's Liberation Army was built under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party headed by Comrade Mao Zedong. Today, the Chinese People's Liberation Army is an important factor in safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.

Marshal Peng Dehuai, on behalf of all the comrades who received the medals, said that we feel very honored to be awarded the medals by the Presidium of the Albanian People's Assembly. This is a manifestation of the profound friendship of the Albanian people towards the Chinese people and the People's Liberation Army.

On the same afternoon, Enver Baluku, First Deputy Chairman of the Albanian Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense, held a banquet to bid farewell to the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation headed by Marshal Peng Dehuai.

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, June 1st. Luo Shigao, Chinese Ambassador to Albania, hosted a banquet on the evening of June 1st for the visit of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to Albania. Attending the banquet were Albanian Party and government leaders Kambo, Nushi, Belyushova, Muftiu, Baluku, Pechajuvani, Kellezi, and Feodosi, among others.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团  授予我军事代表团成员勋章  我驻阿大使为代表团访问举行宴会

1959-06-03
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团
    授予我军事代表团成员勋章
    我驻阿大使为代表团访问举行宴会
    新华社地拉那1日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团1日下午举行隆重的授勋仪式，把两种勋章分别授予中国军事友好代表团全体成员。
    在授勋仪式上，阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席列希把一级“斯坎德培”勋章授予中国军事友好代表团团长、国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅，把一级“游击队”勋章授予王树声大将、张宗逊上将、萧华上将、杨得志上将，把二级“斯坎德培”勋章授予陈伯钧上将、陈熙空军少将、张学思海军少将，把二级“游击队”勋章授予路扬大校、朱开印大校、冯征上校和郑文翰上校。
    参加授勋仪式的有阿尔巴尼亚部长会议第一副主席兼国防部长巴卢库、部长会议副主席凯列奇和外交部部长什图拉等。
    我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高也出席了授勋仪式。
    列希在授勋仪式上致词说，阿尔巴尼亚人民议会把勋章授予中国军事友好代表团的成员是阿尔巴尼亚人民对兄弟的中国人民热爱的表现。他说，中国人民解放军是在以毛泽东同志为首的中国共产党的英明领导下建立起来的。今天中国人民解放军是保卫亚洲和世界和平的重要因素。
    彭德怀元帅代表全体接受勋章的同志致词说，阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团授予我们的勋章使我们感到十分光荣，这是阿尔巴尼亚人民对中国人民和解放军的深厚友谊的表现。
    同日下午，阿尔巴尼亚部长会议第一副主席兼国防部长巴卢库举行了宴会，欢送以彭德怀元帅为首的中国军事友好代表团。
    新华社地拉那1日电　我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高1日晚为中国军事友好代表团访问阿尔巴尼亚举行了宴会。出席宴会的有阿尔巴尼亚党和政府领导人卡博、努西、别利绍娃、穆夫蒂乌、巴卢库、佩查久瓦尼、凯列兹、芾奥多西等。
