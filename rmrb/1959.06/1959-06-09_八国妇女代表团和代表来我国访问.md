Title: Women's Delegations and Representatives from Eight Countries Visit China
Author:
Date: 1959-06-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 8th:  Recently, thirty-five women delegates and representatives from eight countries, at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China, came to our country for a visit.

    Arriving in Beijing today was the Iraqi women's delegation, including Salima Fakhri and six others.  Prior to this, those who had arrived in Beijing included Finnish women's representative Irma Toivi and two others, the Venezuelan women's delegation led by Ajalia Raya and three others, the Chilean women's delegation led by Elena Pedraza and six others, the Uruguayan women's delegation led by Julia Arevalo de Roche and six others, the Danish women's representative Ester Brink and one other, and the Norwegian women's representative Siri Sverdrup Lundon and one other.

    Three Swedish women's representatives will also arrive in Beijing in the coming days.

### 八国妇女代表团和代表来我国访问

1959-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

    八国妇女代表团和代表来我国访问
    新华社8日讯　最近有八个国家的妇女代表团和妇女代表共三十五人，应中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会的邀请，前来我国参观访问。
    今天到北京的是伊拉克妇女代表团萨利玛·法赫里等七人。在这以前到北京的，有芬兰的妇女代表伊尔玛·托维等三人，委内瑞拉妇女代表团阿赫利亚·拉亚等四人，智利妇女代表团埃伦娜·佩德拉萨等七人，乌拉圭妇女代表团胡利娅·阿雷瓦洛·德罗切等七人，丹麦妇女代表埃斯特·布林克等二人，挪威妇女代表西丽·斯维德鲁普·隆敦等二人。
    瑞典妇女代表三人也将在日内到达北京。
