Title: Soviet-German Party and Government Leaders Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1959-06-11
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, June 10th, Moscow Dispatch: TASS Moscow reported that members of the German Democratic Republic party and government delegation, headed by Ulbricht and Grotewohl, held talks with Soviet party and government leaders in the Kremlin on the 9th.

The Soviet side participating in the talks included Kirichenko, Kozlov, Mikoyan, Khrushchev, Pervukhin, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Kuznetsov, and others.

Xinhua News Agency, June 10th, Riga Dispatch: According to TASS, the German Democratic Republic party and government delegation headed by Ulbricht and Grotewohl, accompanied by Khrushchev, arrived in Riga, the capital of the Latvian Republic, by plane on the evening of the 9th for a visit from Moscow.

### 苏德党政领导人会谈

1959-06-11
第5版()
专栏：

    苏德党政领导人会谈
    新华社10日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：以乌布利希和格罗提渥为首的德意志民主共和国党政代表团成员9日在克里姆林宫同苏联党政领导人举行了会谈。
    苏联方面参加会谈的有基里钦科、科兹洛夫、米高扬、赫鲁晓夫、别尔乌辛、苏联外交部第一副部长库兹?佐夫等人。
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社里加讯：以乌布利希和格罗提渥为首的德意志民主共和国党和政府代表团在赫鲁晓夫陪同下，9日晚间乘飞机自莫斯科到达拉脱维亚共和国首都里加进行访问。
