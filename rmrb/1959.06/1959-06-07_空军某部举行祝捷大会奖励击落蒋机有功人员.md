Title: Air Force Unit Holds Victory Rally to Reward Personnel Who Shot Down Chiang Kai-shek's Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1959-06-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, June 6th. An air force fighter unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held a victory award ceremony on the 5th to reward personnel who shot down a Chiang Kai-shek aircraft on May 29th.

　　This battle took place at night under complex weather conditions, shooting down a Chiang Kai-shek aircraft attempting to sneak into the Guangdong area to harass the region by taking advantage of the adverse weather. All twelve people on board the Chiang Kai-shek aircraft were killed. In addition to the officers and soldiers of this unit, generals from the Guangzhou area troops and the party and government leaders of Guangzhou City, Guangdong Province, attended the meeting.

　　Pilot Jiang Zhelun, and guidance navigator Tan Liuguang, and radar technician Wang Xizhang, who demonstrated bravery, tenacity, resourcefulness, and flexibility in this battle, were awarded promotions in rank and grade ahead of schedule. Other meritorious personnel and units were also awarded at the same time. The award recipients stated at the meeting that to thank the party and the state for their care, they will continue to work hard and better accomplish the glorious task of defending the socialist construction of the motherland.

### 空军某部举行祝捷大会奖励击落蒋机有功人员

1959-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    空军某部举行祝捷大会奖励击落蒋机有功人员
    新华社广州6日电　中国人民解放军空军某歼击机部队，5日举行祝捷授奖大会，奖励5月29日击落蒋机一架的有功人员。
    这次战斗是在夜间复杂的气象条件下，击落了一架企图利用恶劣气象窜入广东地区骚扰的蒋机，蒋机十二人全部毙命。出席大会的除这支部队的官兵以外，还有广州地区部队的将领和广东省广州市的党政负责人。
    在这次战斗中表现英勇顽强机智灵活的飞行员蒋哲伦，和引导领航员谭流光，雷达技师王希章，分别受到提前晋衔、晋级的奖励。其他有功人员和单位也同时受奖。受奖人员在会上表示，为感谢党和国家的关怀，他们将继续努力，更好地完成保卫祖国社会主义建设的光荣任务。
