Title: Romanian Handicraft Delegation Head Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-06-06
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency:

    The head of the Romanian Handicraft Cooperative delegation, Comrade Gheorghe Vasilichi, an alternate member of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, who visited our country at our invitation, left Beijing by plane on the 4th to return to his country.

### 罗工艺代表团团长回国

1959-06-06
第5版()
专栏：

    罗工艺代表团团长回国
    新华社讯　应邀来我国访问的罗马尼亚工艺合作社代表团团长、罗马尼亚工人党中央候补委员乔治·瓦西里克，4日乘飞机离开北京返国。
