Title: Coconut Trees
Author:
Date: 1959-06-21
Page: 8

    ·Coconut Trees·
    July in Hainan is like a steamer,
    September in Hainan brings typhoons;
    We are the sky-supporting coconut trees,
    Afraid neither of the heat nor the wind.
    (Su Zhizu, Hainan Garrison)

### 椰子树

1959-06-21
第8版()
专栏：

    ·椰子树·
    七月海南像蒸笼，
    九月海南刮台风；
    我们是撑天椰子树，
    不怕热来不怕风。
    （海南驻军苏志祖）
