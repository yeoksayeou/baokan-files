Title: The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission Convenes Sports Work Conference; Decides to Successfully Hold the First National Games

Author: 
Date: 1959-06-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 10th: The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission convened a national sports work conference in Beijing from June 1st to 8th. The conference determined that sports work in 1959 should better proceed from the overall situation, further strengthen the unified leadership of the Party, closely coordinate with industrial and agricultural production and various work tasks, continue to carry out mass sports activities, improve the level of sports technology, and successfully hold the First National Games. The conference also made specific arrangements for this year's sports work and related issues of the First National Games.

After discussion, the representatives unanimously agreed that in 1958, China's people's sports activities had developed widely and rapidly. Chinese athletes broke national records in various sports more than 800 times and some world records, which played a positive role in supporting the completion of production, work, and study, enlivening people's cultural life, and promoting international cultural exchanges. These achievements were due to the correct implementation of the Party's sports work policy under the strong leadership of the Party. During the discussion, the representatives also pointed out some shortcomings and noteworthy issues in last year's sports work.

The conference believed that experience and lessons should be drawn from last year's work to make future sports work develop more healthily. The current sports work should follow the important principle of improving on the basis of popularization and popularizing under the guidance of improvement. Developing mass sports activities should start from the needs, voluntariness, and practical possibilities of the masses to enhance people's physique and make them better benefit production, work, and study. Therefore, it is necessary to emphasize the development of diverse sports activities; on the basis of having already created some excellent results, further summarize and improve the training methods of athletes, and continue to improve the technical level of various sports, so as to better promote the development of mass sports and create excellent results at the First National Games.

The conference emphasized that sports departments at all levels should carry out their work under the unified leadership of the Party committee, adhere to putting politics in command, strengthen ideological education, and earnestly implement the spirit of diligence and thrift in running sports.

Wang Wei, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Li Shubin, Minister of the Sports Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, spoke at the meeting. At the end of the meeting, Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, made a summary speech.

The conference was attended by the heads of sports commissions of various provinces, cities, and autonomous regions; representatives attending the symposium of sports cadres of the Communist Youth League from four provinces and ten cities convened by the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League; and representatives of relevant units such as the General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army, the Ministry of Education, the Ministry of Health, and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 国家体委召开体育工作会议  确定要开好第一届全国运动会

1959-06-12
第6版()
专栏：

    国家体委召开体育工作会议
    确定要开好第一届全国运动会
    新华社10日讯　国家体委6月1日到8日在北京召开了全国体育工作会议。会上确定：1959年的体育工作应该更好地从全局出发，进一步加强党的统一领导，密切配合工农业生产和各项工作任务，继续开展群众性体育活动，提高运动技术水平，开好第一届全国运动会。会议并对今年的体育工作和第一届全国运动会的有关问题，作了具体的安排。
    会议经过讨论，代表们一致认为：1958年我国人民的体育运动，得到了广泛迅速的发展，我国运动员打破了八百多次各项运动的全国纪录和一些世界纪录，这对于配合生产、工作、学习的完成，以及活跃人民的文化生活，促进国际文化交流，都起了良好的作用。这些成绩的取得，是由于在党的坚强领导下，正确贯彻党的体育工作方针的结果。代表们在讨论中，同时指出在去年的体育工作中，也存在一些缺点和值得注意的问题。
    会议认为，应从去年的工作中，吸取经验教训，使今后体育工作更健康地发展。当前体育工作，应遵循在普及基础上提高和在提高的指导下普及这一重要原则。发展群众性的体育活动，从群众的需要、自愿和实际的可能出发，以增强人民体质，使之更好地有益于生产、工作和学习。因此应该强调发展多种多样的体育活动；在已经创造了一些优良成绩的基础上，进一步总结和改进运动员的训练方法，继续提高各项运动技术水平，以便更好地促进群众体育运动的发展，在第一届全国运动会上创造优良成绩。
    会议强调指出：各级体育部门应在党委统一领导下进行工作，坚持政治挂帅，加强思想教育，认真贯彻勤俭办体育的精神。
    会上，共青团中央书记处书记王伟，全国总工会体育部部长栗树彬作了发言。会议结束时，国家体委副主任荣高棠作了总结发言。
    出席这次会议的，有各省、市、自治区体育运动委员会的负责人；出席团中央召集的四省十市共青团体育干部座谈会的代表，以及解放军总政治部、教育部、卫生部和全国总工会等有关单位的代表，也参加了会议。
