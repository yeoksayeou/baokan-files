Title: Romanian Cultural Circles Commemorate Poet Eminescu
Author:
Date: 1959-06-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 15th: Romanian cultural circles in the capital have held activities in the past two days to commemorate the 70th anniversary of the death of the great Romanian poet Mihai Eminescu (1850-1889).

Bucharest newspapers all published articles commemorating Eminescu on the 14th and published his poems. The Romanian Writers' Association and the "Caragiale" National Theater of the capital jointly held a memorial evening on the 15th. The First Secretary of the Writers' Association, Beniuc, spoke at the meeting. In addition, the Chairman of the Writers' Association, Sadoveanu, and others introduced Eminescu's creative activities on the radio and recited his works.

### 罗马尼亚文化界  纪念诗人艾米尼斯库

1959-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚文化界
    纪念诗人艾米尼斯库
    新华社布加勒斯特15日电　罗马尼亚人民共和国首都近两天来举行了纪念罗马尼亚伟大诗人艾米尼斯库（1850—1889）逝世七十周年的活动。
    布加勒斯特各报在14日都发表了纪念艾米尼斯库的文章，并且刊登了他的诗作，罗马尼亚作家协会和首都“卡拉吉阿尔”民族剧院15日晚联合举行了纪念晚会，作协第一书记贝纽克在会上讲了话，另外，作协主席萨多维亚努等还在电台介绍了艾米尼斯库的创作活动，并朗诵了他的作品。
