Title: Rats Run Wild in Slums: Over Two Hundred Children Injured in Five Months
Author:
Date: 1959-06-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, June 27th: According to TASS, the "New York Post" reported that in the slums of New York, "a four-month-old infant was bitten to death by a rat" on the 25th. The newspaper quoted a Department of Health spokesperson as saying that as of June 1st this year alone, there have been two hundred and sixty-two incidents of rats injuring children in New York slums.

### 贫民窟里老鼠逞凶  五个月伤孩童二百多人

1959-06-30
第5版()
专栏：

    贫民窟里老鼠逞凶
    五个月伤孩童二百多人
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社纽约讯：“纽约邮报”报道，在纽约的贫民窟里，25日有“一个刚生四个月的婴孩被老鼠咬死”。这家报纸引用卫生部发言人的话说，仅到今年6月1日为止，在纽约贫民窟中老鼠伤害小孩的事件就有二百六十二起。
