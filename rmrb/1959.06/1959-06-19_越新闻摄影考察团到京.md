Title: Vietnamese News Photography Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-06-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, June 18th. The Vietnamese News Photography Delegation, consisting of four members led by Hoang Tu Xai, Director of the Vietnam Central Photography Bureau, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon to study news photography work in China according to the 1959 executive plan of the Sino-Vietnamese Cultural Agreement. Deng Gang, Deputy Director of the Xinhua News Agency, and heads of the Photography Department of the Xinhua News Agency and the China Photographic Society were at the station to welcome them.

Pham Binh, Counselor of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, and Le Phu Hao, a journalist from the Vietnam News Agency stationed in Beijing, were also at the station to welcome them.

### 越新闻摄影考察团到京

1959-06-19
第5版()
专栏：

    越新闻摄影考察团到京
    新华社18日讯　根据中越文化协定1959年执行计划来我国考察新闻摄影工作的越南新闻摄影考察团一行四人，在团长、越南中央摄影局局长黄思斋率领下，今天下午乘火车到达北京。到车站欢迎的有新华通讯社副社长邓岗，新华通讯社摄影部和中国摄影学会的负责人。
    越南驻华大使馆参赞范平和越南通讯社驻北京记者黎富豪等也到车站欢迎。
