Title: Delegation of the Vietnam Labor Youth League Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-06-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, May 31st. The delegation of the Vietnam Labor Youth League, consisting of six members led by Wu Guang, Secretary of the Central Executive Committee of the Vietnam Labor Youth League, arrived in Beijing by train today for a visit to our country at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League.

Those who greeted the delegation at the station included Wang Wei, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Wang Jiamiao, Deputy Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, among others.

### 越南劳动青年团代表团到京

1959-06-01
第5版()
专栏：

    越南劳动青年团代表团到京
    新华社31日讯　应中国共青团中央邀请来我国访问的越南劳动青年团代表团一行六人，在越南劳动青年团中央执行委员会书记武光率领下，今天乘火车到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有共青团中央书记处书记王伟、共青团北京市委副书记汪家缪等人。
