Title: Mass rallies to be held in major cities across China to support the Korean people's demand for the withdrawal of US troops from South Korea
Author:
Date: 1959-06-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Chinese people are warmly responding to the call of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Council to hold a "U.S. Troops Out of South Korea Day." Today, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, the China-Korea Friendship Association, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Women's Federation, and the All-China Youth Federation jointly held a meeting and decided to hold a mass rally of people from all walks of life in the capital on June 24 to firmly support the Korean people's struggle to demand the withdrawal of US troops from South Korea and the peaceful reunification of their motherland. On the 24th and 25th, cities such as Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenyang, Andong, Changchun, and Yanji will also hold mass rallies to fully support the just struggle of the Korean people.

### 我国各大城市将举行群众大会  支持朝鲜人民要求美军撤出南朝鲜

1959-06-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我国各大城市将举行群众大会
    支持朝鲜人民要求美军撤出南朝鲜
    新华社18日讯　中国人民热烈响应亚非人民团结理事会关于举行美军撤出南朝鲜日的号召。今天，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国亚非团结委员会、中国朝鲜友好协会、中华全国总工会、中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会、中华全国青年联合会联合举行会议，决定在6月24日举行首都各界群众大会，坚决支持朝鲜人民要求美军撤出南朝鲜、实现祖国和平统一的斗争。在24日和25日，上海、广州、沈阳、安东、长春、延吉等城市，也将分别举行群众集会，全力支持朝鲜人民的正义斗争。
