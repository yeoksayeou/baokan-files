Title: Chairman Liu Receives Burmese Ambassador Maung Maung Gyaw Win
Author:
Date: 1959-06-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd — Chairman Liu Shaoqi received the Burmese Ambassador to China, Maung Maung Gyaw Win, this evening. Present at the reception were Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu and Director of the Protocol Department Yu Peiwen.

### 刘主席接见缅甸大使貌貌叫温

1959-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见缅甸大使貌貌叫温
    新华社3日讯　刘少奇主席今晚接见了缅甸驻华大使貌貌叫温。接见时在座的有外交部副部长章汉夫、礼宾司司长俞沛文。
