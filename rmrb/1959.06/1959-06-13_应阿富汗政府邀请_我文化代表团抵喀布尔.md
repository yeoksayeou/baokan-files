Title: Chinese Cultural Delegation Arrives in Kabul at the Invitation of the Afghan Government
Author: 
Date: 1959-06-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, 10th—A Chinese cultural delegation headed by Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Kabul, the capital of Afghanistan, on the 9th. The delegation is visiting at the invitation of the Government of the Kingdom of Afghanistan.
    Dr. Mohammad Anas, Deputy Minister of Education of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, and other officials welcomed the delegation at the airport.

### 应阿富汗政府邀请  我文化代表团抵喀布尔

1959-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    应阿富汗政府邀请
    我文化代表团抵喀布尔
    新华社10日讯　喀布尔消息：以对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武为首的中国文化代表团在9日到达阿富汗首都喀布尔。代表团是应阿富汗王国政府的邀请，前往访问的。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有阿富汗王国政府教育副大臣穆罕默德·阿纳斯博士和其他官员。
