Title: Ho Chi Minh and Sukarno Issue Joint Statement: Divided Countries Should Achieve Peaceful Reunification; Sukarno Ends Visit to Vietnam and Returns to Jakarta
Author:
Date: 1959-06-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 29th: A joint statement issued here today by Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Sukarno, President of the Republic of Indonesia, stated that, reaffirming the spirit of the joint statement issued by President Ho Chi Minh and President Sukarno at the conclusion of President Ho Chi Minh's visit to the Republic of Indonesia on March 8th of this year, President Ho Chi Minh once again affirmed that the Democratic Republic of Vietnam supports the Indonesian people's struggle to restore the Republic of Indonesia's sovereignty over West Irian; President Sukarno supports the Vietnamese people's efforts to achieve the reunification of their country and believes that the provisions of the 1954 Geneva Accords must be implemented to resolve the issue of Vietnam's reunification.

The joint statement said that during President Sukarno's visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, he exchanged views with President Ho Chi Minh on issues of common interest to Vietnam and Indonesia and on the international situation. The talks were conducted in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

The joint statement said that in exchanging views on issues related to the international situation, both sides reiterated their view that the fact that some countries in the world remain divided is one of the causes of tensions and international disputes; therefore, countries currently divided must achieve reunification in a peaceful and democratic manner in order to safeguard peace and security in the regions concerned.

The joint statement said that in this spirit, both sides expressed their earnest hope that the foreign ministers currently participating in the Geneva Conference will not abandon their efforts but should continue their discussions to make the conference a success.

The joint statement said that both sides believe that, in the current international situation, the holding of a summit meeting will help to ease tensions and safeguard world peace and security.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 29th: After a five-day friendly visit to Vietnam, Indonesian President Sukarno left Hanoi by plane this morning to return home.

President Sukarno delivered a speech at the airport, expressing his gratitude to the government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for their warm reception. He said: The people of our two countries are forever linked together in spirit and feeling. This spirit and feeling will help us to approach each other across the sea.

President Sukarno said, "Let us move forward together and achieve our common goals together, which are national unity, prosperity, complete independence, and world peace."

President Ho Chi Minh delivered a speech at the airport, bidding farewell to President Sukarno. He asked President Sukarno to convey the warmest wishes of the people and government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Indonesian people and government. He said that the friendship between the people of our two countries binds us closely together.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 29th: Indonesian President Sukarno returned to Jakarta tonight from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 胡志明和苏加诺发表联合声明  被分裂的国家应实现和平统一  苏加诺结束在越访问回到雅加达

1959-06-30
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明和苏加诺发表联合声明
    被分裂的国家应实现和平统一
    苏加诺结束在越访问回到雅加达
    新华社河内29日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明和印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺今天在这里发表的联合声明说，在再次肯定胡志明主席今年3月8日结束在印度尼西亚共和国的访问时同苏加诺总统发表的联合声明的精神的基础上，胡志明主席再次确认越南民主共和国支持印度尼西亚人民为恢复印度尼西亚共和国对西伊里安的主权而进行的斗争；苏加诺总统支持越南人民为争取实现祖国的统一而进行的努力，并且认为1954年的日内瓦协议的各项条款必须得到履行，以解决越南的统一问题。
    联合声明说，在越南民主共和国进行访问期间，苏加诺总统同胡志明主席就有关越南和印度尼西亚的共同利益和国际局势等问题交换了意见。会谈是在亲切友好的气氛中进行的。
    联合声明说，在就有关国际局势的问题交换意见的时候，双方重申了他们的观点：世界上的一些国家仍然处于分裂状态是造成紧张局势和引起国际争端的原因之一；因此，目前被分裂的国家必须按照和平和民主的方式实现统一，以维护有关地区的和平和安全。
    联合声明说，在这种精神的基础上，双方表示殷切希望目前参加日内瓦会议的外交部长们不要放弃自己的努力，而应当继续进行讨论，使会议获得成功。
    联合声明说，双方认为，在当前的国际局势下，最高级会议的召开将有助于缓和紧张局势和维护世界的和平和安全。
    新华社河内29日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺在越南进行了五天的友好访问之后，今天上午坐飞机离开河内回国。
    苏加诺总统在机场致词，表示感谢越南民主共和国政府和人民对他的热情接待。他说：我们两国人民永远在精神上、感情上连结在一起。这种精神和感情将帮助我们越过大海而互相接近。
    苏加诺总统说，“让我们共同前进，一起达到共同的目的，这就是国家的统一、繁荣、完全独立和世界和平。”
    胡志明主席在机场致词，欢送苏加诺总统。他请苏加诺总统向印度尼西亚人民和政府转达越南民主共和国人民和政府的最友好的祝愿。他说，我们两国人民之间的友谊把我们紧紧地连结在一起。
    新华社雅加达29日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺今晚从越南民主共和国回到雅加达。
