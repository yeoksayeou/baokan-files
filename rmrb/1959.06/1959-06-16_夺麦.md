Title: Seizing the Wheat
Author: Xu Hanru
Date: 1959-06-16
Page: 8

   Seizing the Wheat
   Xu Hanru
   Wind-whipped yellow sand strikes the face,
   A burst of rain pours overhead,
   The battle drum for seizing the wheat beats loudly!
   Red flags flutter in the wheat sea.
   Cutting wheat in front, gleaning behind,
   You bundle, I carry on my shoulder,
   Mules, horses, and cars all join the battle,
   Fully loaded, rushing, running together.
   The wheat is winnowed, clean, and stored away,
   Ten thousand people sing, ten thousand people laugh,
   In the autumn, we'll harvest golden grains again,
   To build a golden mountain reaching the clouds.

### 夺麦
许翰如
1959-06-16
第8版()
专栏：

   夺麦
   许翰如
   风卷黄沙扑面来，
   阵阵暴雨头上浇，
   夺麦战鼓擂得响呀！
   麦海红旗空中飘。
   前边割麦后边拣，
   你打捆来我肩挑，
   骡马汽车同上阵呀，
   满装快拉齐飞跑。
   小麦扬净归了仓，
   万人欢唱万人笑，
   秋后再打黄金粒呀，
   垒座金山上云霄。
