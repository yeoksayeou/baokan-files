Title: Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Zasyadko Visits Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1959-06-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, June 14th. Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, A. F. Zasyadko, accompanied by Deputy Minister of the Coal Industry Liu Xiangsan and other Soviet dignitaries, arrived in Shanghai yesterday from Beijing by special plane. Soviet Embassy in China Counselor Safonov, Fumin, and Soviet Consul General in Shanghai Yelisavigin also accompanied them.

Those who greeted them at the airport included Cao Diqiu, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, and responsible persons from relevant departments. Soviet Deputy Consul in Shanghai Yushin and others also welcomed them at the airport.

After arriving in Shanghai, the distinguished guests drove around the city and visited the Shanghai No. 3 Steel Plant, where they were warmly welcomed by the workers.

Last night, Shanghai Mayor Ke Qingshi hosted a banquet to welcome the distinguished guests.

### 苏联部长会议副主席扎夏迪科到上海访问

1959-06-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联部长会议副主席扎夏迪科到上海访问
    新华社上海14日电　苏联部长会议副主席扎夏迪科和随行的其他苏联贵宾，昨天在煤炭工业部副部长刘向三陪同下，由北京乘专机到达上海。苏联驻华大使馆参赞萨福隆、符明和苏联驻上海总领事叶里沙维金也陪同前来。
    到机场欢迎的有上海市副市长曹荻秋及有关部门负责人。苏联驻上海副领事尤申等也到机场欢迎。
    贵宾们到上海后，驱车游览了市容，参观了上钢三厂，受到职工们的热烈欢迎。
    昨天晚上，上海市市长柯庆施设宴欢迎贵宾。
