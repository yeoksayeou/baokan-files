Title: Kozlov Departs for New York to Attend Opening Ceremony of Soviet Exhibition

Author: 
Date: 1959-06-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 28th, Moscow dispatch: Kozlov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, left Moscow by plane on the morning of the 28th to attend the opening ceremony of the Soviet Exhibition in New York, according to TASS. He was accompanied by Nasredinova, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Uzbek Republic, and Novikov, Chairman of the State Planning Committee of the Russian Federal Republic, among others.

The Soviet delegation traveled on the new "Tu-114" turboprop airliner designed by Academician Tupolev. This 220-seat scheduled passenger plane made its first non-stop flight from Moscow to New York.

The "Tu-114" aircraft is scheduled to complete the journey from Moscow to New York in eleven and a half hours. The aircraft will remain in New York until July 14.

Aircraft designers Tupolev and Arkhangelsky, along with a group of Soviet actors and journalists, also traveled to New York on this aircraft.

Soviet Party and government leaders Mikoyan, Kosygin, and Polyansky were among those who saw Kozlov and his entourage off at Vnukovo Airport.

U.S. Ambassador to the Soviet Union Thompson was also at the airport to see them off.

### 参加苏联展览会开幕式  科兹洛夫启程前往纽约

1959-06-29
第4版()
专栏：

    参加苏联展览会开幕式
    科兹洛夫启程前往纽约
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席科兹洛夫28日清晨从莫斯科乘飞机前往纽约参加苏联展览会的开幕式。和他同行的有乌兹别克共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席纳斯里金诺娃和俄罗斯联邦共和国国家计划委员会主席诺维科夫等人。
    苏联代表团是乘图波列夫院士设计的“图——一一四”新型涡轮螺旋桨飞机前往的。这架有二百二十个座位的定期航行的客机第一次从莫斯科到纽约作不着陆飞行。
    “图——一一四”飞机要在十一个半钟头内完成从莫斯科到达纽约的航程。这架飞机将在纽约逗留到7月14日。
    飞机设计师图波列夫和阿尔汉格尔斯基等人以及一批苏联演员和新闻工作者也乘这架飞机前往纽约。
    到伏努科夫机场欢送科兹洛夫和他的随行人员的有苏联共产党和政府领导人米高扬、柯西金和波利扬斯基等人。
    美国驻苏联大使汤普森也到机场送行。
