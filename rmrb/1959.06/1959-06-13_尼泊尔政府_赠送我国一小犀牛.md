Title: The Nepalese Government Presents a Baby Rhinoceros to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1959-06-13
Page: 4

    Nepalese Government
    Presents a Baby Rhinoceros to Our Country
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 12th - The Nepalese government has presented a small male rhinoceros to the Chinese government. The presentation ceremony was held on June 10 in Birgunj, a city near the Indian border.
    Officials from the Chinese Embassy in India accepted the gift at this friendly ceremony. The male rhinoceros will be transported to Calcutta on June 13 and then shipped to China.
    The Nepalese government presented a small female rhinoceros to China last year.

### 尼泊尔政府  赠送我国一小犀牛

1959-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔政府
    赠送我国一小犀牛
    新华社新德里12日电　尼泊尔政府赠给中国政府一头小的公犀牛。赠送仪式已经于6月10日在靠近印度边境的伯甘吉城举行。
    中国驻印度大使馆的官员在这个友好的仪式上接受了这份礼物。这头公犀牛将在6月13日运到加尔各答然后转运到中国去。
    尼泊尔政府曾经在去年赠给中国一头小的母犀牛。
