Title: Professor Tirto Diningrat Leaves Beijing to Visit Various Places

Author:

Date: 1959-06-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Professor Zohri Tirto Diningrat, President of Hasanuddin University of Indonesia, who came to China at the invitation of Peking University, left Beijing today. He will visit Shenyang, Changchun, Wuhan, Hangzhou, Guangzhou and other places before returning home.

### 第尔多迪宁格勒教授离京赴各地参观访问

1959-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    第尔多迪宁格勒教授离京赴各地参观访问
    新华社16日讯　应北京大学邀请来我国访问的印度尼西亚国立哈山奴汀大学校长佐·第尔多迪宁格勒教授，今天离开北京。他将到沈阳、长春、武汉、杭州、广州等地参观访问后回国。
