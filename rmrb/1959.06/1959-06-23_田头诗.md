Title: Fieldside Poem
Author:
Date: 1959-06-23
Page: 3

    Fieldside Poem
    The garden is full of green vegetables,
    The soldiers' hearts are full of joy;
    Sweat exchanges for fruitful results,
    Supporting the Great Leap Forward for the Motherland.

### 田头诗

1959-06-23
第3版()
专栏：

    田头诗
    满园蔬菜青又青，
    战士心里好欢欣；
    汗水换来丰收果，
    支援祖国大跃进。
