Title: Cameroonian Writer Matip Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-06-20
Page: 4

    Cameroonian Writer Matip Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 19th - Cameroonian writer Benjamin Matip, who was invited by the Chinese Writers Association to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

### 喀麦隆作家马蒂普到京

1959-06-20
第4版()
专栏：

    喀麦隆作家马蒂普到京
    新华社19日讯　应中国作家协会邀请来我国访问的喀麦隆作家本杰曼·马蒂普，今天乘飞机到达北京。
