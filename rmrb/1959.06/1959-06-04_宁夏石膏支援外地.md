Title: Ningxia Gypsum Supports Other Regions

Author:

Date: 1959-06-04
Page: 2

    Ningxia Gypsum Supports Other Regions
    The Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region is actively mining gypsum and transporting it in large quantities to support the production and construction of various provinces and autonomous regions.
    The Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region has abundant reserves of high-quality gypsum. According to surveys, gypsum mines exist in thirteen of the region's nineteen counties and cities.

### 宁夏石膏支援外地

1959-06-04
第2版()
专栏：

    宁夏石膏支援外地
    宁夏回族自治区正在积极开采石膏并大量外运，支援各省、自治区的生产建设。
    宁夏回族自治区石膏储量丰富、质量好。据勘察，全区十九个县市中有十三个县有石膏矿。
