Title: Bulgaria Abounds in Granulated Sugar; Sugar Production Per Capita Exceeds that of the United States
Author:
Date: 1959-06-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia – Bulgaria will produce 183,000 tons of granulated sugar this year, an increase of about one-quarter compared to 1958, and 6.3 times that of 1939 before liberation.

In 1958, Bulgaria's sugar production averaged 19.2 kilograms per person, while its neighbor Yugoslavia produced 9.2 kilograms per person.

In 1957, Bulgaria's sugar production per capita exceeded that of the United States, the United Kingdom, and Yugoslavia. Bulgaria averaged 15.3 kilograms per person, while the United States was 13.6 kilograms, the United Kingdom was 14.8 kilograms, and Yugoslavia was only 8.3 kilograms.

Since liberation, Bulgaria's granulated sugar production has developed rapidly. In 1939, Bulgaria had only two sugar factories with an annual output of 25,100 tons of granulated sugar. In 1958, Bulgaria had five sugar factories with modern equipment, producing 149,000 tons of granulated sugar annually.

### 保加利亚盛产沙糖  糖产量按人口计超过美国

1959-06-05
第5版()
专栏：

    保加利亚盛产沙糖
    糖产量按人口计超过美国
    新华社索非亚讯  保加利亚今年将生产沙糖十八万三千吨，即比1958年增加约四分之一，而为解放前1939年的六点三倍。
    1958年保加利亚的糖产量按人口平均计算是每人十九点二公斤，而它的邻国南斯拉夫则为九点二公斤。
    在1957年，保加利亚的糖产量按人口平均计算超过了美国、英国和南斯拉夫。保加利亚平均每人为十五点三公斤，而美国为十三点六公斤，英国为十四点八公斤，而南斯拉夫只有八点三公斤。
    解放以来，保加利亚的沙糖生产得到了迅速的发展。在1939年，保加利亚只有两个制糖厂，年产沙糖二万五千一百吨。1958年，保加利亚已有五个具有现代化设备的制糖工厂，年产沙糖十四万九千吨。
