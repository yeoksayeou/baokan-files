Title: 阿联和匈牙利签订海运协定 (United Arab Republic and Hungary Sign Maritime Transport Agreement)
Author:
Date: 1959-06-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo. On June 13, the United Arab Republic and the Hungarian People's Republic signed a maritime transport agreement in Cairo.

    It is reported that this agreement stipulates the establishment of sea routes between the two countries and stipulates that the total cargo volume between the two countries shall be equally shared by the maritime transport companies of both countries.

### 阿联和匈牙利签订海运协定

1959-06-15
第4版()
专栏：

    阿联和匈牙利签订海运协定
    据新华社开罗讯　阿拉伯联合共和国和匈牙利人民共和国13日在开罗签订了一项海上运输协定。
    据报道，这项协定规定在两国之间建立海上航线，并且规定两国之间的总的载货量由两国海运公司平均分担。
