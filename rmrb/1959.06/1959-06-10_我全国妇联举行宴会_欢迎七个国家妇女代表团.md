Title: All-China Women's Federation Holds Banquet to Welcome Women's Delegations from Seven Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-06-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th June: The All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China held a banquet this evening to welcome women's delegations from seven countries who have come to China for a friendly visit.
    At this banquet, filled with friendship and joy, women representatives from Chile, Denmark, Finland, Iraq, Norway, Uruguay, and Venezuela engaged in lively conversations with their Chinese hosts. Cai Chang, President of the All-China Women's Federation, warmly welcomed the guests from afar, thanking them for the friendship and encouragement they brought to the Chinese people and women.
    During the banquet, the guests presented Cai Chang with many gifts with national characteristics, expressing the deep friendship of the women of various countries towards Chinese women.

### 我全国妇联举行宴会  欢迎七个国家妇女代表团

1959-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    我全国妇联举行宴会
    欢迎七个国家妇女代表团
    新华社9日讯　中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会今天晚间举行宴会欢迎前来我国进行友好访问的七个国家的妇女代表团。
    在这洋溢着友谊和欢乐的宴会上，来自智利、丹麦、芬兰、伊拉克、挪威、乌拉圭、委内瑞拉的妇女代表和中国主人尽情交谈。全国妇联主席蔡畅热忱地向远道而来的客人们表示热烈的欢迎，感谢客人们给中国人民和妇女带来的友谊和鼓舞。
    宴会上，客人们分别向蔡畅赠送了许多具有民族特色的礼品，表达了各国妇女对中国妇女的深厚友情。
