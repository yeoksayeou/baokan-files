Title: Kerala Chief Minister Speaks: State Government Has Maintained Control of Situation in State

Author:

Date: 1959-06-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 17th. Kerala Chief Minister Namboodiripad said at a press conference on the 16th that, up to now, the state government has maintained control of the situation within the state. He hoped that no more shooting incidents would occur. He said that the demand for the state government to resign "cannot be agreed to."

News published in Delhi newspapers today indicates that the 16th was a "relatively peaceful day" in Kerala. According to "The Statesman," apart from occasional stone-throwing incidents, there was no news of unfortunate events occurring.

On the evening of the 16th, a mass rally was held in the state capital, Trivandrum, to celebrate the passage of the Land Relations Bill by the Kerala Legislative Assembly. State Chief Minister Namboodiripad said at the meeting that the vast majority of the people oppose incitement and will defeat such incitement.

### 喀拉拉邦首席部长发表谈话  邦政府一直控制邦内局势

1959-06-18
第5版()
专栏：

    喀拉拉邦首席部长发表谈话
    邦政府一直控制邦内局势
    新华社新德里17日电　喀拉拉邦首席部长南布迪里巴德16日在记者招待会上说，到目前为止，邦政府一直控制住邦内的局势。他希望不再发生开枪事件。他说，要求邦政府辞职“是不能同意的”。
    今天德里报纸刊载的消息表明，16日是喀拉拉邦“比较平静的一天”。据“政治家报”报道，除了偶然发生的掷石头的事件之外，没有关于发生不幸事件的消息。
    16日晚上在邦首府特里凡得琅举行了一次群众大会，庆祝喀拉拉邦立法会议通过了土地关系法案。邦首席部长南布迪里巴德在会上说，绝大多数的人民反对煽动，并且将击败这种煽动行动。
