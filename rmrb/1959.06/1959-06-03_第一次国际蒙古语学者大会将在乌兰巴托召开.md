Title: First International Congress of Mongolian Language Scholars to be Held in Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1959-06-03
Page: 7

Academic Trends

    First International Congress of Mongolian Language Scholars
    To Be Held in Ulaanbaatar

    According to an article answering readers' questions published by Professor D. Tsebegmid of the National University of the Mongolian People's Republic in the 120th issue of the Mongolian People's Republic's "Literary Gazette," the First International Congress of Mongolian Language Scholars is being actively prepared. This congress will be held from September 1st to 7th this year in Ulaanbaatar, the capital of the Mongolian People's Republic. The congress preparatory committee has sent invitations to thirty-four prominent scholars from the Soviet Union, China, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Poland, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the German Democratic Republic, the United States, India, Japan, West Germany, the United Kingdom, France, Finland, Turkey, and Afghanistan. Fourteen scholars from the Mongolian People's Republic, including Ts. Damdinsüren, will participate. They and scholars from other countries attending the conference will give academic reports. The reports they plan to present include topics on Mongolian literary heritage (Damdinsüren), Mongolian phonetics (Lubsang Wangdan), the research tasks of the Mongolian Language Department of the Mongolian National University (Pagba), Mongolian-Tibetan dictionaries written by Mongolians (Dorji), and Mongolian spoken and written language (Rinchen), among others. To coincide with the conference, the Mongolian People's Republic will publish a series of research collections on Mongolian language works, such as the "Mongolian Studies" series (approximately fifty printer's sheets), the "Oral Literature Studies" series (over forty printer's sheets), and the "Mongolian Inscriptions" series (two hundred and seventy printer's sheets). After the conference, scholars from various countries will visit historical sites and use libraries for their work. The article states that this conference will be beneficial to the development of Mongolian language science and the expansion of the exchange of research results among Mongolian language scholars.

### 第一次国际蒙古语学者大会将在乌兰巴托召开

1959-06-03
第7版()
专栏：学术动态

    第一次国际蒙古语学者大会
    将在乌兰巴托召开
    根据蒙古人民共和国国立大学教授德·策伯格密德在蒙古人民共和国“文艺报”第一百二十期发表的答读者问文章表明，第一次国际蒙古语学者大会正在积极筹备中，这次大会将于今年9月1日到7日在蒙古人民共和国首都乌兰巴托召开。大会筹备委员会已向苏联、中国、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、波兰、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国，德意志民主共和国、美国、印度、日本、西德、英国、法国、芬兰、土耳其和阿富汗等国的三十四位著名学者发出了请柬。蒙古人民共和国将有策·达木丁苏伦等十四位学者参加。他们和出席大会的其他各国学者均将在大会作学术报告，他们准备提出的报告有关于蒙古文学遗产问题（达木丁苏伦）、关于蒙古语音问题（洛布桑旺丹）、关于蒙古国立大学蒙古语文研究任务问题（帕格瓦）、关于蒙古人所著蒙藏词典问题（多尔济）、关于蒙古口语和书面语问题（仁亲）等。为了配合这次大会的召开，蒙古人民共和国将出版一系列有关蒙古语言著作方面的研究丛书，例如，“蒙古研究”丛书（约五十印张）、“口头文学研究”丛书（四十余印张）、“蒙古文字碑铭”丛书（二百七十印张）等。会后各国学者将参观历史遗迹和利用图书馆进行工作。文章说，这次大会将有利于发展蒙古语言科学和扩大蒙古语学者研究成果的交流。
