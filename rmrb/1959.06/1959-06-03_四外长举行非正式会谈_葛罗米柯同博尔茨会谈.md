Title: Four Foreign Ministers Hold Informal Talks; Gromyko Meets with Bolz
Author:
Date: 1959-06-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 2nd. According to TASS: The foreign ministers of the Soviet Union, the United States, Britain, and France held another meeting on June 1st, informally exchanging views on the same issues they discussed at the meeting on May 30th. The foreign ministers agreed to hold a formal meeting of the East-West Foreign Ministers' Conference at 15:30 on June 2nd and an informal meeting in the evening.

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 2nd. According to TASS: Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko met with the Foreign Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Bolz, on the evening of June 1st. Participants in the meeting included Soviet delegation members Zorin and Biryuzov, advisor Ilyichev, and German Democratic Republic delegation members Wentzel, Toeplitz, Florin, and Hoffmann. The meeting continued to discuss the future progress of the Foreign Ministers' Conference.

### 四外长举行非正式会谈  葛罗米柯同博尔茨会谈

1959-06-03
第5版()
专栏：

    四外长举行非正式会谈
    葛罗米柯同博尔茨会谈
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社日内瓦讯：苏美英法四国外长1日又举行了会谈，非正式地就他们在5月30日会谈时所讨论的同样问题交换了意见。外长们商妥6月2日十五时三十分举行东西方外长会议的正式会议，晚间举行非正式会议。
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社日内瓦讯：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯1日晚间同德意志民主共和国外交部长博尔茨会谈。参加会谈的有苏联代表团成员佐林、别尔乌辛，顾问伊利切夫，德意志民主共和国代表团成员文策尔、特普利茨、弗洛林、霍夫曼等人。会谈时继续商讨了外长会议今后的进程。
