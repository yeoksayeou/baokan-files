International Briefs

Sudan to Try Generals Shannan and Saad

Cairo dispatch, "Al-Ahram" reported on the 16th that the Sudanese authorities have filed public charges against General Shannan and General Saad, former members of the Supreme Council of the Sudanese Armed Forces and cabinet ministers.

The indictment accuses them of three counts related to instigating rebellion. They were arrested about two weeks ago.

Belgian Communist Party Calls on Workers to Oppose Mine Closures

Brussels dispatch, The Belgian Communist Party's newspaper "Red Flag" published a resolution of the Belgian Communist Party's Political Bureau on the 15th, calling on Belgian miners to unite closely and oppose the Belgian government and the "European Coal and Steel Community" organization's decision to close more mines this year.

Kuwaiti Authorities Expel More Than 300 Communists

Cairo dispatch, A "News" reporter reported from Kuwait on the 15th that Kuwaiti authorities had expelled 330 communists, including people from Jordan, Lebanon, Iraq, Iran, and the United Arab Emirates.

U.S. University Tuition Continues to Rise

This newspaper reports: The latest issue of the American "Time" magazine quoted figures published by the U.S. Department of Education saying that in the past four years, U.S. university tuition has increased by about 33%, and by 9.5% in 1958 alone. "Time" magazine said it is estimated that in the next four years, it will continue to increase as it has in the past four years.

### 国际简讯

1959-06-18
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    苏丹将审讯沙南和萨德准将
    开罗讯　“金字塔报”16日报道，苏丹当局已经对原苏丹武装部队最高委员会委员和内阁部长沙南准将和萨德准将提起公诉。
    起诉书指控他们犯有三项有关制造叛变的罪状。大约两星期前他们被逮捕。
    比共号召工人反对关闭矿井
    布鲁塞尔讯　比利时共产党机关报“红旗报”15日发表了比利时共产党政治局的一个决议，号召比利时矿工紧密团结，反对比利时政府和“欧洲煤钢联营”组织关于在今年内关闭更多的矿井的决定。
    科威特当局把三百多共产党人驱逐出境
    开罗讯　“消息报”记者15日从科威特报道说，科威特当局已驱逐三百三十名共产党人出境，这些人当中有约旦、黎巴嫩、伊拉克、伊朗和阿联等国的人。
    美国大学学费不断上涨
    本报讯　最新一期的美国“时代”周刊引用美国教育局发表的数字说，在过去四年中，美国大学的学费约增加了33％，而仅仅是1958年就增加了9.5％。“时代”周刊说估计在今后四年内，还要同过去四年一样地继续增加。
