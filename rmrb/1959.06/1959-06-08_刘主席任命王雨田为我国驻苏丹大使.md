Title: Liu Shaoqi Appoints Wang Yutian as China's Ambassador to Sudan
Author:
Date: 1959-06-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th—Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, has appointed Wang Yutian as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Sudan.

### 刘主席任命王雨田为我国驻苏丹大使

1959-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席任命王雨田为我国驻苏丹大使
    新华社7日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇任命王雨田为中华人民共和国驻苏丹共和国特命全权大使。
