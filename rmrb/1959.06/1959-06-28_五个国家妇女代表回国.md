Title: Women Representatives from Five Countries Return Home
Author:
Date: 1959-06-28
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, June 27th – Women's delegations and women representatives from Denmark, Finland, Norway, Uruguay, and Venezuela, who were invited by the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China to visit our country, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home. They visited Shanghai, Hangzhou, Dalian, Shenyang, Anshan, and other places.

### 五个国家妇女代表回国

1959-06-28
第3版()
专栏：

    五个国家妇女代表回国
    新华社27日讯　应中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会邀请前来我国访问的丹麦、芬兰、挪威、乌拉圭和委内瑞拉的妇女代表团和妇女代表，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。她们曾分别在上海、杭州、大连、沈阳、鞍山等地进行了参观访问。
