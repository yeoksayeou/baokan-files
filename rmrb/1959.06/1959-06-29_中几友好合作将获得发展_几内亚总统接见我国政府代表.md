Title: Sino-Guinean Friendly Cooperation to be Developed; Guinean President Receives Chinese Government Representative
Author:
Date: 1959-06-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, June 27th: Guinean President Sékou Touré received Bai Ren, the Chinese government representative, today.

Bai Ren, on behalf of the Chinese government and people, extended greetings to the Guinean government and people, and expressed his hope that the friendship and cooperation between China and Guinea would be further developed.

Sékou Touré, on behalf of the Guinean government and people, welcomed the visit of the Chinese government representative. He believed that the friendship and cooperation between Guinea and China would be further strengthened.

Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, June 27th: Bai Ren, the Chinese government representative, met with Béavogui, the Guinean Minister of Economic Affairs and Planning, today.

### 中几友好合作将获得发展  几内亚总统接见我国政府代表

1959-06-29
第4版()
专栏：

    中几友好合作将获得发展
    几内亚总统接见我国政府代表
    新华社科纳克里27日电　几内亚总统塞古·杜尔今天接见了中国政府代表白认。
    白认代表中国政府和人民向几内亚政府和人民致意，并且表示希望中国和几内亚两国之间的友谊和合作将进一步得到发展。
    塞古·杜尔代表几内亚政府和人民对中国政府代表的来访表示欢迎。他相信，几内亚和中国之间的友谊和合作将进一步得到加强。
    新华社科纳克里27日电　中国政府代表白认今天会见了几内亚共和国经济事务和计划部长贝阿沃吉。
