Title: Japanese Small and Medium Enterprise Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-06-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 13th -- The Japanese Small and Medium Enterprise Workers Delegation, led by Ishikawa Shigeru, the Dispute Countermeasures Director of the Japanese Trade Union General Council, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. The delegation consists of five people.

### 日本中小企业工人代表团到京

1959-06-14
第3版()
专栏：

    日本中小企业工人代表团到京
    新华社13日讯　以日本工会总评议会争议对策部长石川茂为首的日本中小企业工人代表团一行五人，应中华全国总工会邀请，在今天乘飞机到达北京。
