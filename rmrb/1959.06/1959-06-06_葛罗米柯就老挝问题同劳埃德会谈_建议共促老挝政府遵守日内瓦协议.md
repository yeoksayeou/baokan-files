Title: Gromyko Discusses Laotian Issue with Lloyd, Suggests Joint Promotion of Laotian Government's Compliance with Geneva Accords
Author:
Date: 1959-06-06
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 5th June: According to TASS, Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko, as one of the chairmen of the Geneva Conference on Indochina, met with British Foreign Secretary Lloyd, the other chairman of the conference, on the 4th and held talks with him on the situation in Laos.
    It is reported that, considering the current situation in Laos caused by the Laotian government's violation of the Geneva Accords and the serious danger this situation poses to peace and security in Indochina, Gromyko, in the course of exchanging views, suggested to the British side that a letter be written as soon as possible in the name of the two chairmen to the Laotian government, requesting it to comply with the Geneva Accords and resume cooperation with the International Commission for Laos, so that the commission can continue its work as soon as possible.
    On the 5th, the two chairmen will continue to exchange views on this issue.

### 葛罗米柯就老挝问题同劳埃德会谈  建议共促老挝政府遵守日内瓦协议

1959-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯就老挝问题同劳埃德会谈
    建议共促老挝政府遵守日内瓦协议
    新华社5日讯  据塔斯社日内瓦4日讯：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯，作为关于印度支那问题的日内瓦会议的主席之一，4日会见了作为这一会议的另一位主席的英国外交大臣劳埃德，并就老挝局势问题同他举行了会谈。
    据悉，考虑到由于老挝政府破坏日内瓦协议而造成的目前老挝局势以及这一局势对印度支那和平和安全孕育着严重的危险，葛罗米柯在交换意见过程中向英国方面建议尽快以两个主席的名义写信给老挝政府，要求它遵守日内瓦协议和恢复同老挝国际委员会的合作，以便使委员会尽快能够继续工作。
    5日，两个主席将就这一问题继续交换意见。
