    Extracting Tannin from Wild Plants

    The Yulin Special District of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region has established many small tannin factories to extract tannin from wild plants.

    Tannin is an important raw material for industries such as leather, fishing gear, rubber shoes, oilcloth, and raincoats. The Yulin Special District has discovered twenty-five kinds of raw materials that can be used to extract tannin.

### 用野生植物炼栲胶

1959-06-10
第2版()
专栏：

    用野生植物炼栲胶
    广西僮族自治区玉林专区建立了许多小型栲胶厂，利用野生植物炼制栲胶。
    栲胶是皮革、渔具、胶鞋、油布、雨衣等工业的重要原料之一。玉林专区已经发现可以提炼栲胶的原料二十五种。
