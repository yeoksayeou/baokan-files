Title: Two Indonesian University Presidents Invited to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1959-06-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, June 4th: Professor Iwa Koesoema Soemantri, President of the Indonesian National Padjadjaran University, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese Ministry of Education.

Those welcoming him at the airport included Cui Zhongyuan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Education, Sun Xiaocun, President of Beijing Agricultural University, Zhang Wei, Vice President of Tsinghua University, and Xiao Ying, Vice President of Beijing Institute of Geology Exploration.

Indonesian Embassy in China Cultural Counsellor Indrosugondo and others also greeted him at the airport.

That evening, Liu Aifeng, Vice Minister of Education, hosted a banquet to welcome the guest.

Xinhua News Agency, June 4th: Professor Z. A. Tirtodiningrat, President of the Indonesian National Hasanuddin University, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon at the invitation of Peking University for a visit to China.

### 印度尼西亚两位大学校长  应邀到我国访问

1959-06-05
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚两位大学校长
    应邀到我国访问
    新华社4日讯  印度尼西亚国立巴查查兰大学校长伊娃·库苏马·苏曼特里教授今天乘飞机到达北京。苏曼特里教授是应我国教育部邀请前来我国访问的。
    到机场欢迎的有教育部办公厅主任崔仲远、北京农业大学校长孙晓村、清华大学副校长张维和北京地质勘探学院副院长萧英等。
    印度尼西亚驻华大使馆文化参赞英德罗苏贡多等也到机场迎接。
    当天晚上，教育部副部长刘皑风设宴欢迎客人。
    新华社4日讯  印度尼西亚国立哈山奴汀大学校长佐·第尔多迪宁格勒教授应北京大学邀请来我国访问，在今天下午乘火车到达北京。
