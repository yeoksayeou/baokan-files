Title: U.S. Plane Twice Intruding into Airspace Over Xisha Islands; We Issue 52nd Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1959-06-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 27th — On June 26th at 10:00 AM, a U.S. Navy patrol plane intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island, Beidao Island, and Shudao Island of China's Xisha Islands in Guangdong Province. On June 27th at 12:37 PM, another U.S. Navy patrol plane intruded into the airspace over Dongdao Island of China's Xisha Islands in Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 52nd serious warning.

### 美机两次侵入我西沙群岛上空  我提第五十二次严重警告

1959-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    美机两次侵入我西沙群岛上空
    我提第五十二次严重警告
    新华社27日讯　6月26日十时美国海军巡逻机一架，侵入我国广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、北岛、树岛上空。6月27日十二时三十七分，又有美国海军巡逻机一架，侵入我国广东省西沙群岛的东岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅行为，我外交部发言人奉命提出第五十二次严重警告。
