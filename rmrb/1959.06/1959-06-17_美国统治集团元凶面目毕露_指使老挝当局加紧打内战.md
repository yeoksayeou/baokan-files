Title: The Ugly Face of the U.S. Ruling Clique Fully Exposed: Instigating the Lao Authorities to Intensify the Civil War
Author:
Date: 1959-06-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 16th dispatch from Washington: Recent statements by U.S. officials and monopoly capitalist newspapers regarding the situation in Laos demonstrate the U.S. government’s sinister intentions to encourage and support the Lao Kingdom authorities in launching a civil war and undermining peace in Indochina.

    A U.S. government spokesman, when discussing the U.S. "aid" program to Laos on the 14th, declared that the United States was "satisfied" with the current civil war situation in Laos. He said that the "primary purpose" of U.S. "aid" to Laos was "to help Laos avoid Communist rule." He said, "Today, Laos is firmly leaning towards the free world," and "Laos's current government is the best."

    At the same time, diplomatic figures in Washington are attempting to create public opinion for the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization to interfere in the Laotian situation and pull Laos into the group. United Press International quoted these diplomatic figures on June 5th as saying that if the former Pathet Lao forces pose a so-called "threat" to the Laotian government, a serious international crisis may occur, and the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization may directly intervene.

### 美国统治集团元凶面目毕露  指使老挝当局加紧打内战

1959-06-17
第5版()
专栏：

    美国统治集团元凶面目毕露
    指使老挝当局加紧打内战
    据新华社16日讯　华盛顿消息：美国官方和垄断资本集团报刊最近关于老挝局势的言论，说明了美国政府鼓励和支持老挝王国当局发动内战、破坏印度支那和平的罪恶用意。
    美国政府发言人14日在谈到美国对老挝的
  “援助”计划时扬言美国对老挝当前的内战局面是“满意的”。他说，美国“援助”老挝的“首要目的是帮助老挝避免共产党的统治”。他说，“今天老挝是坚决倒向自由世界的”，
  “老挝今天的政府是最好的”。
    在这同时，华盛顿的外交人士正企图为东南亚条约集团插手老挝局势和把老挝拉进这个集团制造舆论。合众国际社6月5日引这些外交人士的话说，如果前寮国战斗部队对老挝政府造成所谓“威胁”的话，可能会发生严重的国际危机，东南亚条约组织可能直接干预。
