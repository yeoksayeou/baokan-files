Title: Czech Film Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-06-12
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency Despatch: A Czechoslovak film workers delegation of four people, led by Mr. F. Šolc, Deputy Director of the Czechoslovak Film General Administration, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 10th for a visit to China in accordance with the 1959 executive plan of the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 捷电影代表团到京

1959-06-12
第5版()
专栏：

    捷电影代表团到京
    新华社讯　根据中捷文化合作协定1959年执行计划来我国访问的捷克斯洛伐克电影工作者代表团一行四人，在团长、捷克斯洛伐克电影总管理局副局长弗·索尔茨率领下，10日乘飞机到达北京。
