Title: The Cicada Sings
Author: 
Date: 1959-06-28
Page: 8

    The cicada chirps, the grain ears turn golden;
    The cicada chirps, the new grain is about to arrive on the scene;
    The cicada chirps, the head of the commune is flustered;
    So much grain is harvested, the granary is not big enough to store it.

### 蝉儿叫

1959-06-28
第8版()
专栏：

    蝉儿叫
    蝉儿吱吱叫，谷穗澄澄黄；
    蝉儿吱吱叫，新谷要登场；
    蝉儿吱吱叫，社长着了慌；
    谷子打的多，仓房不够装。
