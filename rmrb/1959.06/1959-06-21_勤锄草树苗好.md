Title: Diligently Weeding is Good for Tree Seedlings
Author:
Date: 1959-06-21
Page: 7

    Diligent weeding is good for tree seedlings.
    One weeding makes the trees look fresh,
    Ten weedings make the trees taller than a person.
                (Farmers' proverb)

### 勤锄草树苗好

1959-06-21
第7版()
专栏：

    勤锄草树苗好
    种树勤锄草，树苗长得好。
    一锄树色新，十锄树过人。
                （农谚）
