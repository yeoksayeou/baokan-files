Title: Vice Premier Zheng Yilong Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-06-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. Vice Premier Zheng Yilong of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and some members of the Korean Industrial Delegation he led, accompanied by Yong Longgui, Director of the World Economic Bureau of the State Planning Commission of our country, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon from Nanjing. The Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yongho, also returned to Beijing on the same flight.

Vice Premier Zheng Yilong and other Korean guests arrived in Nanjing on the 14th from Shanghai. The guests visited factories in Nanjing and also paid their respects at the Yuhuatai Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery, where they laid a wreath.

### 郑一龙副首相回京

1959-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    郑一龙副首相回京
    新华社15日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相郑一龙和由他率领的朝鲜工业参观团的部分团员，在我国国家计划委员会世界经济局局长勇龙桂等陪同下，今天下午乘飞机从南京回到北京。陪同的朝鲜驻我国大使李永镐也同机回到北京。
    郑一龙副首相和其他朝鲜贵宾是在14日从上海到达南京的。贵宾们在南京曾参观了工厂，还凭吊了雨花台革命烈士墓，在墓前献了花圈。
