Title: Soviet Union Successfully Designs World's Largest Blast Furnace
Author:
Date: 1959-06-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 3rd (TASS) - The Soviet Union has successfully designed a blast furnace with a volume of 2,700 cubic meters.

Currently, the world's largest blast furnace has a volume of 1,810 cubic meters, built in 1955 by a factory of the "Great Lakes Steel Corporation" (Detroit) in the United States.

According to the head of the Soviet State Institute for the Design of Steel Plants, this large blast furnace will be able to smelt 4,500 to 5,500 tons of pig iron per day, depending on the quality of the raw materials. In the Soviet Union, the highest output achieved by a blast furnace is 2,400 tons of pig iron per day, while in the United States it is about 2,000 tons, slightly less.

According to the design, this highly efficient blast furnace will use natural gas and oxygen-enriched blast during the smelting process. Weighing and feeding will be fully automated for the first time. The measurement of temperature and humidity will also be automatic. The design also proposes methods for mechanized tapping of iron and slag.

In the next seven years (1959-1965), the Soviet Union plans to build several large blast furnaces.

### 世界最大高炉苏联设计成功

1959-06-05
第5版()
专栏：

    世界最大高炉苏联设计成功
    新华社4日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科3日讯：苏联已经设计成功了一座容积为二千七百立方公尺的高炉。
    目前世界上最大的高炉的容积是一千八百一十立方公尺，它是1955年由美国的“大胡钢铁公司”（底特律）的一个工厂建造的。
    据苏联国家炼钢厂设计研究所的负责人对塔斯社记者说，这种大型高炉每昼夜将能根据原料的质量情况冶炼四千五百吨到五千五百吨生铁。在苏联，已经达到的高炉最高产量是每昼夜二千四百吨生铁，而在美国大约是二千吨少一些。
    根据设计，这座效力强大的高炉在冶炼过程中将使用天然瓦斯和富氧鼓风。称量和供料等将第一次实现完全自动化。温度和湿度的测量也将是自动的。设计还拟定了机械化放铁和放渣的方法。
    在今后七年（1959年—1965年）中，苏联计划要建成好几座大型高炉。
