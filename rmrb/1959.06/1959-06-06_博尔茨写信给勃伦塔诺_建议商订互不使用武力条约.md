Title: Bolz Writes to Brentano Suggesting Negotiation of a Treaty on the Non-Use of Force
Author:
Date: 1959-06-06
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 5th. German News Agency reports from Geneva that the Foreign Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Bolz, on the 4th, through the Director of Protocol of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, delivered a letter to the West German Foreign Minister Brentano. In the letter, Bolz informed him that the Government of the German Democratic Republic has authorized him to begin negotiations on the conclusion of a treaty on the non-use of force between the two Germanies. Bolz suggested that Brentano determine the location and time of the first meeting.
    The proposal to conclude a treaty on the non-use of force between the two Germanies was made by the Deputy Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Ulbricht, in a letter to Adenauer not long ago.

### 博尔茨写信给勃伦塔诺  建议商订互不使用武力条约

1959-06-06
第5版()
专栏：

    博尔茨写信给勃伦塔诺
    建议商订互不使用武力条约
    新华社5日讯　据德意志通讯社自日内瓦报道，德意志民主共和国外交部长博尔茨4日通过德意志民主共和国外交部礼宾司司长把一封信转交给西德外交部长勃伦塔诺。博尔茨在信中通知说，德意志民主共和国政府已授权他就缔结两个德国之间不使用武力的条约开始谈判。博尔茨建议勃伦塔诺确定首次会见的地点和时间。
    关于缔结两个德国之间不使用武力条约的建议，是德意志民主共和国副总理乌布利希不久前在给阿登纳的一封信中提出的。
