Title: Chairman Zhu De Receives Peruvian Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-06-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, 9th. Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received the Peruvian parliamentary delegation in Lüda city this morning.

Those present at the reception included Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Zhang Su, a member of the Standing Committee, Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Hu Ming, Mayor of Lüda City. The Peruvian parliamentary delegation arrived in Lüda on the 8th. (Attached photo)

Chairman Zhu De receives members of the Peruvian parliamentary delegation. The photo shows a group photo after the meeting. The fifth person from the left in the front row is the head of the Peruvian parliamentary delegation, Ernesto More, a member of the Peruvian House of Representatives and a member of the World Peace Council. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong, Miao Ming photographed (facsimile photo)

### 朱德委员长接见秘鲁议员团

1959-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德委员长接见秘鲁议员团
    新华社旅大9日电　全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德，今天上午在旅大市接见了秘鲁议员团。
    接见时在座的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林枫、常务委员张苏，中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之和旅大市市长胡明。秘鲁议员团是8日到达旅大的。（附图片）
    朱德委员长接见秘鲁议员团团员。图为接见后的合影。前排左起第五人是秘鲁议员团团长、秘鲁众议员、世界和平理事会理事埃尔内斯托·莫雷。　新华社记者　邹健东、苗明摄（传真照片）
