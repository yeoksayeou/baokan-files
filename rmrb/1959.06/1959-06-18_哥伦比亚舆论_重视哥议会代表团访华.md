Title: Colombian Public Opinion Emphasizes Visit of Colombian Parliamentary Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1959-06-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 17th dispatch from Bogotá: Colombian public opinion attaches great importance to the visit of the Colombian parliamentary delegation to China.
    On the 16th, all newspapers in the Colombian capital published news of the Colombian parliamentary delegation's arrival in Beijing on the 15th and the warm welcome it received at the airport. General public opinion in Bogotá believes that the visit of the Colombian parliamentary delegation to China will contribute to peace and to the development of friendly relations between the two countries.

### 哥伦比亚舆论  重视哥议会代表团访华

1959-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    哥伦比亚舆论
    重视哥议会代表团访华
    新华社17日讯　波哥大消息：哥伦比亚舆论十分重视哥伦比亚议会代表团对中国的访问。
    16日哥伦比亚首都各报都刊登了哥伦比亚议会代表团15日到达北京、以及在机场上受到欢迎的消息。波哥大一般舆论认为，哥伦比亚议会代表团对中国的访问将对和平、对发展两国间的友好关系作出贡献。
