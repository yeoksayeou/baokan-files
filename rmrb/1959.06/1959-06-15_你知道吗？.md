Title: Did You Know?
Author: Lü Yin
Date: 1959-06-15
Page: 8

    Did you know?
    ＊The world's deepest fishing area is near the Kuril Islands of the Soviet Union, where deep-sea trawls are lowered to a depth of 1,200 meters to catch fish.
    ＊The largest hailstorm in the world occurred in Potter, USA, in 1928, with some hailstones weighing 600 grams.
            Lü Yin

### 你知道吗？
吕音
1959-06-15
第8版()
专栏：

    你知道吗？
    ＊世界上最深的捕鱼区是在苏联千岛群岛附近，人们是把深海曳网放到一千二百公尺的深海里去捕捉的。
    ＊世界上最大的一次冰雹是1928年降落在美国波特城里，其中有些冰雹重量有六百克。
            吕音
