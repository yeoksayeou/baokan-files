Title: Increase Irrigated Area to 20 Million Hectares: Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Discusses Specific Measures
Author:
Date: 1959-06-11
Page: 5

    Increase Irrigated Area to 20 Million Hectares
    Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Discusses Specific Measures
    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, June 9th. The Bulgarian Communist Party held a plenary session of its Central Committee on June 8th. The meeting, presided over by First Secretary Zhivkov, discussed water conservancy construction, soil improvement, and measures to increase the irrigated area to 20 million hectares by 1965.
    In addition to the members of the Central Committee, the meeting was attended by the first secretaries of the party committees of various administrative and economic states (formerly translated as districts), experts from relevant departments, and responsible personnel.
    Minister of Agriculture and Forestry, Primov, delivered a report at the meeting on "Measures to Increase the Irrigated Area to 20 Million Hectares by 1965." (Attached picture)

### 把灌溉面积增到二千万公顷  保共中央全会讨论具体措施

1959-06-11
第5版()
专栏：

    把灌溉面积增到二千万公顷
    保共中央全会讨论具体措施
    新华社索非亚9日电　保加利亚共产党在6月8日举行了中央委员会全体会议，会议在第一书记日夫科夫的主持下，讨论了水利建设、土壤改良和到1965年增加灌溉面积到二千万公顷的措施问题。
    参加会议的除中央委员会的委员外，还有各行政经济州（前译为区）州党委员会的第一书记、各有关部门的专家和负责人员。
    农林部长普里莫夫在会议上作了“关于到1965年把灌溉面积增加到二千万公顷的措施”的报告。（附图片）
