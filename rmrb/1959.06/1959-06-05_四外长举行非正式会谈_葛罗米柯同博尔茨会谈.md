Title: Four Foreign Ministers Hold Informal Talks; Gromyko Meets with Bolz
Author:
Date: 1959-06-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, June 4th, Geneva dispatch: The foreign ministers of the Soviet Union, the United States, Britain, and France held another informal meeting at the residence of U.S. Secretary of State Herter on the afternoon of the 3rd. The meeting lasted for two hours and twenty-five minutes. The foreign ministers agreed to hold another informal meeting at the French delegation's residence at 15:30 on the 4th. The foreign ministers also decided to hold a formal meeting of the East-West Foreign Ministers Conference at the Palais des Nations at 15:30 on June 5th.

Xinhua News Agency, June 4th, Geneva dispatch: Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko visited Dr. Bolz, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, on the evening of the 3rd and held talks with him. Taking part in the talks were members of the Soviet delegation, Belousov and advisor Ilyichev, and members of the German Democratic Republic delegation, Winzer, Toeplitz, and Florin. During the talks, they continued consultations on issues related to the Geneva Conference.

### 四外长举行非正式会谈  葛罗米柯同博尔茨会谈

1959-06-05
第5版()
专栏：

    四外长举行非正式会谈
    葛罗米柯同博尔茨会谈
    新华社4日讯  据塔斯社日内瓦讯：苏联、美国、英国和法国四国外长3日下午在美国国务卿赫脱的寓所举行又一次非正式会谈，会谈进行了二小时二十五分。外长们商定，4日十五时三十分在法国代表团寓所再举行非正式会谈。外长们还决定6月5日十五时三十分在国联大厦举行东西方外长会议的正式会议。
    新华社4日讯  据塔斯社日内瓦讯：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯3日晚上拜访了德意志民主共和国外交部长博尔茨博士，并同他进行了会谈。参加会谈的有苏联代表团成员别尔乌辛、顾问伊利切夫和德意志民主共和国代表团成员文策尔、特普利茨和弗洛林。他们在会谈时，就有关日内瓦会议的问题继续进行了磋商。
