Title: Mongolian Ambassador to China Recalled Home

Author:

Date: 1959-06-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, June 13th: Mongolian People's Republic Ambassador to China, Rubsan, was recalled home, and left Beijing by train this afternoon. Those who went to the station to see him off included Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chen Shuliang, Director of the Second Asian Department, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China.

### 蒙古驻华大使奉调回国

1959-06-14
第3版()
专栏：

    蒙古驻华大使奉调回国
    新华社13日讯　蒙古人民共和国驻华大使鲁布桑奉调回国，在今天下午乘火车离开北京。前往车站送行的有外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文、第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮和各国驻华外交官员。
