Title: Yang Xiufeng Receives Latin American Student Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-06-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, June 16th: Minister of Education Yang Xiufeng received a student delegation from eight Latin American countries, including Argentina, Brazil, Chile, Colombia, Cuba, Venezuela, Uruguay, and Peru, this afternoon.

Present at the meeting were Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, and Liu Ziyu, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Education. The student delegation of eleven members from the eight Latin American countries arrived in Beijing in two groups on June 6th and 10th.

### 杨秀峰接见拉丁美洲学生代表团

1959-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    杨秀峰接见拉丁美洲学生代表团
    新华社16日讯　教育部部长杨秀峰今天下午接见了阿根廷、巴西、智利、哥伦比亚、古巴、委内瑞拉、乌拉圭、秘鲁等拉丁美洲八国学生代表团。
    接见时在座的有中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立、教育部办公厅主任刘子余等。拉丁美洲八国学生代表团一行十一人，是在6月6日、10日分两批到达北京的。
