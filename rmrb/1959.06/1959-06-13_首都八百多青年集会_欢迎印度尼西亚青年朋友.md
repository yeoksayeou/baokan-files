Title: More Than 800 Young People Gather in the Capital to Welcome Indonesian Youth Friends
Author:
Date: 1959-06-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 12th: More than 800 young people in the capital gathered this evening to warmly welcome the Indonesian youth delegation from the friendly neighboring country. Yang Jingren, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, expressed his respect to the Indonesian youth friends on behalf of the Chinese youth at the meeting. Yang Jingren said that the youth of our country are watching with joy the valuable contributions made by the people and youth of Indonesia in promoting Asian-African solidarity. He congratulated the friendship between the people and youth of China and Indonesia on its growing development and consolidation in the common struggle to defend peace and oppose colonialism.

The head of the Indonesian youth delegation, Ailiande Danjie, a member of the Central Council of the Indonesian Youth Front and a member of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Christian Youth Federation, repeatedly thanked the Chinese youth for their deep friendship in his speech, and enthusiastically praised the great role played by the Chinese youth in the socialist construction of the motherland, as well as the outstanding achievements made by Chinese students in combining learning with practice. He expressed the hope that the youth of China and Indonesia will strengthen contact and unity in promoting the "Bandung Spirit," safeguarding world peace, and maintaining Asian-African solidarity.

### 首都八百多青年集会  欢迎印度尼西亚青年朋友

1959-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    首都八百多青年集会
    欢迎印度尼西亚青年朋友
    新华社12日讯　首都青年八百多人今天晚间集会，热情欢迎来自友好邻邦的印度尼西亚青年代表团。全国青联副主席杨静仁在会上代表中国青年向印度尼西亚青年朋友表示敬意。杨静仁说，我国青年怀着高兴的心情注视着印度尼西亚人民和青年在增进亚非团结方面所作的有价值的贡献。他祝贺中国和印度尼西亚两国人民和青年的友谊在保卫和平、反对殖民主义的共同斗争中日益发展和巩固。
    印度尼西亚青年代表团团长、印度尼西亚青年阵线中央理事会理事、印度尼西亚基督教青年联合会中央委员艾里安德·丹杰在致词中一再感谢中国青年的深厚友谊，并且热情地赞扬中国青年在祖国社会主义建设中所发挥的巨大作用，以及中国青年学生在学习和实际相结合方面所取得的出色成绩。他希望中国和印度尼西亚青年，在发扬“万隆精神”、保卫世界和平和维护亚非团结中加强联系和团结。
