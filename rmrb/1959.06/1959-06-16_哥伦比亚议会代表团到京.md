Title: Colombian Parliamentary Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-06-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 15th—The Colombian Parliamentary Delegation, consisting of sixteen members, led by Dr. Horacio Rodriguez Plata, the head of the delegation, and Dr. Joaquin Franco Burgos, the deputy head, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the China Peace Committee.

At the airport, the delegation was warmly welcomed by Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, and Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman. A group of Young Pioneers presented flowers to the guests.

Among those welcoming the delegation at the airport were Lian Guan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Mei Gongbin, Deputy Secretary-General of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Ji Chao Ding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Ouyang Jingrong, Deputy Secretary-General of the China Political Science and Law Society; Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Shen Zijiu, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation; and Zhao Feng and other people who had visited Latin America.

Mr. and Mrs. Jose Venturelli, Chilean peace activists in Beijing, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation. (Attached picture)
    Guo Moruo (third from left) accompanying the head of the delegation Horacio Rodriguez Plata (second from left), the deputy head of the delegation Joaquin Franco Burgos (first from left) and others walking out of the airport.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Tang Likui.

### 哥伦比亚议会代表团到京

1959-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

    哥伦比亚议会代表团到京
    新华社15日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问的哥伦比亚议会代表团一行十六人，在团长奥拉西奥·罗德里格斯·普拉塔博士，副团长华金·佛朗哥·布尔戈斯博士率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团在机场上，受到了中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，副主席廖承志的热烈欢迎。一群少先队员向来宾们献了鲜花。
    到机场欢迎的，还有人大常委会副秘书长连贯，政协全国委员会副秘书长梅龚彬，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎，中国政治法律学会副秘书长欧阳景荣，中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪，全国妇联主席团委员沈兹九，以及曾经访问过拉丁美洲的人士赵沨等。
    在北京的智利和平人士何塞·万徒勒里夫妇也到机场欢迎。（附图片）
    郭沫若（左三）陪同奥拉西奥·罗德里格斯·普拉塔团长（左二）、华金·佛朗哥·布尔戈斯副团长（左一）等步出机场。
  新华社记者　唐理奎摄
