Title: Two Pieces of Good News
Author:
Date: 1959-06-24
Page: 6

    Two Pieces of Good News
    I. At the Chinese Academy of Sciences' conference on mechanical, power, and electrical engineering held in Changchun, it was clarified that the work of these three disciplines should henceforth focus on studying major problems in local production and construction, combined with the development of new technologies and basic scientific research.
    II. Scientific research institutions, universities, and production units in Shanghai are broadly cooperating, adhering to the spirit of "the nation first, the locality second; others first, oneself second," and are first concentrating their main efforts to ensure the completion of key research projects.

### 两件喜事

1959-06-24
第6版()
专栏：

    两件喜事
    一、中国科学院在长春召开的机械、动力、电工专业会议上，明确了这三门科学工作，今后应以研究当地生产建设中的重大问题为主，结合开展新技术和基础科学的研究。
    二、上海科学研究机关、高等学校和生产单位广泛协作，本着先国家后地方、先人后己的精神，首先集中主要力量，保证完成重点研究项目。
