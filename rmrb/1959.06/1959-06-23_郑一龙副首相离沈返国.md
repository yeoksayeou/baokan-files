Title: Vice Premier Jeong Il-ryong Departs from Shenyang to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1959-06-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, June 22nd: Vice Premier Jeong Il-ryong of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and some members of the Korean Industrial Delegation he led, concluded their visit to Northeast China and departed from Shenyang by train tonight to return home.

	Yong Longgui, Director of the World Economy Bureau of China's State Planning Commission, accompanied the distinguished guests to the border.

	Those who saw them off at the station included Qiu Youwen, Vice Governor of Liaoning Province, Xu Zhi, Vice Mayor of Shenyang, and others.

	Before their departure, leaders of the Liaoning Province and Shenyang City Party and government held a banquet to bid farewell to the distinguished guests.

	From the 18th to the 22nd, Vice Premier Jeong Il-ryong and the other distinguished guests of the Korean Industrial Delegation visited factories and mines in Anshan, Harbin, Fushun, Shenyang, and other places, and were warmly welcomed by the workers.

### 郑一龙副首相离沈返国

1959-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

    郑一龙副首相离沈返国
    新华社沈阳22日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相郑一龙和他率领的朝鲜工业参观团的部分团员，结束在我国东北地区的参观访问后，今天晚上从沈阳乘火车回国。
    我国国家计划委员会世界经济局局长勇龙桂陪送贵宾到国境。
    到车站欢送的有辽宁省副省长仇友文，沈阳市副市长徐志等人。
    临行前，辽宁省和沈阳市党和政府的领导人设宴欢送贵宾。
    郑一龙副首相和朝鲜工业参观团的其他贵宾从18日到22日先后在鞍山、哈尔滨、抚顺、沈阳等地参观访问了厂矿企业，到处受到职工群众的热烈欢迎。
