Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into Waters off Pingtan and Matsu Islands, Provoking: Chinese Foreign Ministry Issues 49th Serious Warning

Author: 
Date: 1959-06-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 5th: At 21:49 on June 4th, a U.S. warship intruded into the waters off the Pingtan and Matsu Islands of Fujian Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, the Spokesperson of the Chinese Foreign Ministry was instructed to issue the 49th serious warning.

### 美舰侵入我平潭、马祖地区海域挑衅  我外交部奉命提出第49次严重警告

1959-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

    美舰侵入我平潭、马祖地区海域挑衅
    我外交部奉命提出第49次严重警告
    新华社5日讯  6月4日二十一时四十九分，美舰一艘侵入我国福建省平潭、马祖地区海域。对美舰这种军事挑衅行为，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四十九次严重警告。
