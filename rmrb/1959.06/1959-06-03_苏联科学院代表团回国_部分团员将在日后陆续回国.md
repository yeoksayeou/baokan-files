Title: Soviet Academy of Sciences Delegation Returns Home; Some Members Will Return Later

Author:

Date: 1959-06-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd.  The head of the Soviet Academy of Sciences delegation, A.N. Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and some members of the delegation, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home after completing their visit to China. The remaining members in Beijing will return home successively in the coming days.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Zhang Jingfu and Wu Youxun, Vice Presidents; Fan Changjiang, Deputy Director of the Science and Technology Commission; Pei Lisheng, Secretary-General of the Academy of Sciences; Du Runsheng and Xie Xinhe, Deputy Secretaries-General; as well as responsible persons of relevant departments and institutes of the Academy of Sciences, and well-known scientists such as Qian Xuesen, Hua Luogeng, and Yang Shixian. Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin and Counselor Antonov, Totmakov of the embassy, also saw them off at the airport.

### 苏联科学院代表团回国  部分团员将在日后陆续回国

1959-06-03
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联科学院代表团回国
    部分团员将在日后陆续回国
    新华社2日讯苏联科学院代表团团长、苏联科学院院长?斯米扬诺夫和代表团部分团员，结束在中国的访问以后，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。留在北京的部分团员将在日后陆续回国。
    到机场送行的，有中国科学院院长郭沫若、副院长张劲夫、吴有训，科学技术委员会副主任范长江，科学院秘书长裴丽生、副秘书长杜润生、谢鑫鹤，以及科学院有关学部、研究所的负责人和著名科学家钱学森、华罗庚、杨石先等多人。苏联驻华大使尤金和大使馆参赞安东诺夫、托特马科夫，也到机场送行。
