Title: Polish Party Second Plenary Session Opens, Gomulka Reports on Agricultural Issues
Author:
Date: 1959-06-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, June 22nd: The Third Second Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party opened in Warsaw this morning. The meeting will discuss agricultural development issues for 1959-1965. The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Gomulka, delivered a report on this issue at the meeting.

### 波兰党二中全会开幕  哥穆尔卡报告农业问题

1959-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰党二中全会开幕
    哥穆尔卡报告农业问题
    新华社华沙22日电　波兰统一工人党中央委员会三届二中全会今天上午在华沙开幕。会议将讨论1959—1965年农业发展问题。波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记哥穆尔卡在会议上作了关于这个问题的报告。
