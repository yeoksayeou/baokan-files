Title: Iraqi Minister of Economy Discusses Relations Between Iraq and East Germany
Author: 
Date: 1959-06-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, June 15th: The Iraqi Minister of Economy, Kuba, who is currently visiting here, told reporters from the German News Agency that Iraq and the German Democratic Republic have reached an agreement on the issue of the German Democratic Republic stationing a representative in Iraq. He pointed out that the conclusion of this agreement is the beginning of the development of trade and diplomatic relations between the two countries.

### 伊拉克经济部长谈伊德两国关系

1959-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克经济部长谈伊德两国关系
    新华社柏林15日电　正在此间访问的伊拉克经济部长库巴对德意志通讯社的记者说，伊拉克和德意志民主共和国已经就德意志民主共和国在伊拉克派驻代表的问题达成了协议。他指出，缔结这项协议是发展两国贸易和外交关系的开端。
