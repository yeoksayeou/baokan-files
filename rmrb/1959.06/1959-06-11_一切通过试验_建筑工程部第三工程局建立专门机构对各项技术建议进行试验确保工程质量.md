Title: Everything Through Experiment: The Third Engineering Bureau of the Ministry of Construction Establishes a Special Organization to Experiment with Various Technical Suggestions to Ensure Project Quality
Author:
Date: 1959-06-11
Page: 2

　　 本报兰州10日电　建筑工程部第三工程局抽调技术力量、建立专门机构，对一切代用材料的采用和原料材料的节约，进行了严格的试验，保证了工程的质量。

　　不久以前，这个局的工程技术人员设计成“预应力芯力棒小梁砖拱楼板”，邀请了各工地的工人和技术人员来参观，在现场进行实地试验。按照设计要求，平常的钢筋混凝土楼板每平方公尺如能承受二百五十公斤压力，就算合格；当时在这种以砖拱代替钢筋混凝土的楼板上，每平方公尺压力增加到四百公斤仍没有断裂，证明质量很高，当场受到参观者的赞赏，他们回去以后就在西固化工学校等工地推广，这样就比原设计用钢筋混凝土做楼板节省了90％的钢筋、80％的木材和一半的水泥。第三工程局还集中职工提出的数十种代用材料，在工地建起了一座三层试验楼，各工地职工参观以后，承认这种楼房可以作为今后推广代用材料的“标兵”。

　　在试验以后，对于不能保证质量的建议，就坚决不推行。如在用玻璃丝代替钢筋的试验中，发现玻璃丝的抗剪力较小，所以只在吃力较小的建筑物上推广应用，同时还配上四分之一的钢筋。各工地自制的土水泥，经过试验，证明强度小、质量不稳定，因此只准用在次要工程中，同时还要掺上少量的“洋水泥”。

　　有些合理化建议经过试验，证明可以节省材料，但是不能保证质量，也决不能轻率采用。如兰州化工厂的混凝土基础，按照设计先要刷上一层用汽油调制的“冷底子”，再涂上防酸用的沥青，因为汽油供应不足，工地职工建议取消“冷底子”，直接在混凝土基础上涂沥青。这个建议经过对比试验，证明先刷“冷底子”的基础，沥青和混凝土粘合得很牢固；不用“冷底子”直接在混凝土上涂沥青的基础，表面看来也很好，但是用刀片一刮就能剥离，不能保证质量。

　　外地的先进经验在采用以前，也都经过试验。用预应力钢筋混凝土代替钢板建设油罐，在沿海地区已经试验成功，但是兰州的气候条件同沿海不一样，这里又是地震区，所以现在正在结合本地条件进行试验，等到有了结果以后，再作决定。

　　这个局成立了科学研究所，所属各工程公司也建立了试验室，在各工区与工段的现场设立试验站。专门从事这项工作的人员共一百多人，另外还聘请了一批职工做兼职试验员。

　　今年1至5月，这个工程局共推行了二千七百多件合理化建议、修改设计和节约原材料的新技术，节约了一千多吨钢材、八千多吨水泥、四千多立方公尺的木材。因为贯彻了“一切通过试验”的原则，节约原料材料都有可靠的科学根据，所以，工程质量都得到保证。
Lanzhou Dispatch, June 10th. The Third Engineering Bureau of the Ministry of Construction has extracted technical personnel and established a specialized organization to conduct rigorous testing on the adoption of all substitute materials and the conservation of raw materials, ensuring the quality of projects.

Not long ago, the engineering and technical personnel of this bureau designed a "prestressed core force rod small beam brick arch floor slab," inviting workers and technicians from various construction sites to visit and conduct on-site practical experiments. According to design requirements, ordinary reinforced concrete floor slabs are considered qualified if they can withstand 250 kilograms of pressure per square meter. At that time, on this type of floor slab, which used brick arches instead of reinforced concrete, the pressure increased to 400 kilograms per square meter without any fracture, proving that the quality was very high. The visitors praised it on the spot. After returning, they promoted it at construction sites such as the Xigu Chemical Industry School. This saved 90% of the steel, 80% of the wood, and half of the cement compared to using reinforced concrete for the floor slab in the original design. The Third Engineering Bureau also concentrated dozens of substitute materials proposed by employees and built a three-story experimental building on the construction site. After visiting, the employees of various construction sites admitted that this building could serve as a "model soldier" for promoting substitute materials in the future.

After the experiments, suggestions that could not guarantee quality were resolutely not implemented. For example, in the experiment of using glass fiber to replace steel bars, it was found that the shear strength of glass fiber was small, so it was only promoted and applied to buildings with less stress, and a quarter of the steel bars were also added. The soil cement made by each construction site, after testing, proved to have low strength and unstable quality. Therefore, it was only allowed to be used in minor projects, and a small amount of "foreign cement" had to be added.

Some rationalization proposals have been tested and proved to save materials, but if they cannot guarantee quality, they must not be adopted rashly. For example, for the concrete foundation of the Lanzhou Chemical Plant, according to the design, a layer of "cold primer" mixed with gasoline should be brushed first, and then acid-resistant asphalt should be applied. Because the supply of gasoline was insufficient, the construction site workers suggested canceling the "cold primer" and directly applying asphalt to the concrete foundation. This suggestion was subjected to a comparative experiment. The experiment proved that the foundation with the "cold primer" brushed first had very strong adhesion between the asphalt and the concrete. The foundation that applied asphalt directly to the concrete without the "cold primer" also looked good on the surface, but could be peeled off with a knife, which could not guarantee quality.

The advanced experience of other places has also been tested before adoption. Using prestressed reinforced concrete instead of steel plates to build oil tanks has been successfully tested in coastal areas, but the climatic conditions in Lanzhou are different from those in the coastal areas, and it is also a seismic zone. Therefore, experiments are now being conducted in conjunction with local conditions, and a decision will be made after the results are available.

The bureau has established a scientific research institute, and its affiliated engineering companies have also established laboratories, and experimental stations have been set up on the sites of various work areas and sections. There are more than 100 people specializing in this work, and a group of employees are also hired as part-time experimenters.

From January to May this year, the engineering bureau promoted more than 2,700 rationalization proposals, design modifications, and new technologies for saving raw materials, saving more than 1,000 tons of steel, more than 8,000 tons of cement, and more than 4,000 cubic meters of wood. Because the principle of "everything through experiment" has been implemented, the saving of raw materials has a reliable scientific basis, so the quality of the project is guaranteed.
