Title: Mongolia Establishes Embassy in Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1959-06-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 13th: According to today's "People's Daily" report: The Mongolian People's Republic has recently officially established an embassy in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    The Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the embassy, ​​Buchaichin Tserenchoidor, arrived in Hanoi on June 9.
    Vietnamese Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Ung Van Khiem received Buchaichin Tserenchoidor on the 11th.

### 蒙古设立驻越南大使馆

1959-06-15
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古设立驻越南大使馆
    新华社河内13日电　据“人民报”今天报道：蒙古人民共和国最近正式设立驻越南民主共和国大使馆。
    大使馆临时代办布察钦·策仁超德尔已经在6月9日抵达河内。
    越南外交部副部长雍文谦11日接见了布察钦·策仁超德尔。
