Title: State Council Appointment and Dismissal List
Author:
Date: 1959-06-27
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 25th — The State Council appointed and dismissed a number of personnel from state organs, embassies abroad, and institutions of higher learning on April 16 and June 19 respectively.

The list of appointments and dismissals on April 16 is as follows:

    Appointed Wu Jinshi as Consul General of the People's Republic of China in Geneva;
    Li Shi as Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Republic of Sudan;
    Li Peizhi as Vice President of Renmin University of China;
    Zou Lufeng as Vice President of Peking University;
    Xue Xun and Ma Jianmin as Vice Presidents of Beijing Normal University;
    Jia Jimin as Vice President of Xi'an Highway Institute;
    Lin Qichen as Vice President of Nanjing Institute of Posts and Telecommunications.

    Removed Li Jinde and Chen Zhao from their positions as Deputy Directors of the General Office of the State Council;
    Liang Longtai from his position as Deputy Director of the Bureau of Government Offices Administration of the State Council;
    Gan Dezhou from his position as Deputy Director of the Central Meteorological Bureau;
    Yang Shaoqiao, Bei Zhongxuan, Li Yuang, and Ai Chunan from their positions as Assistant Ministers of the Ministry of Finance;
    Wang Xiaoci from his position as President of Beijing Railway Institute;
    Zou Lufeng from his position as Vice President of Renmin University of China;
    He Bingzhang from his position as Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Coal Industry;
    Xue Xun and Huang Kai from their positions as Assistant Ministers of the Ministry of Petroleum Industry;
    Shen Ping from his position as Consul General of the People's Republic of China in Geneva.

The list of appointments and dismissals on June 19 is as follows:

    Appointed Zhang Xiongfei as Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics;
    Li Yingji as Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the United Arab Republic;
    Ye Jinghao as Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Kingdom of Cambodia;
    Zhang Tingyu as Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Kingdom of Morocco;
    Zhang Kezeng as Counselor of the State Council;
    Zhou Longnan as Vice President of Changchun Geological Institute;
    Tang Nanping as President of Central South Institute of Mining and Metallurgy;
    Deng Yizhen as Vice President of Beijing Institute of Physical Education.

    Removed Li Tao from his position as Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics;
    Li Kefu from his position as Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Republic of Finland;
    Li Yingji from his position as Representative of the Commercial Representative Office of the People's Republic of China in the Egyptian Region of the United Arab Republic, and Zhang Huazeng from his position as Representative of the Commercial Representative Office in the Syrian Region;
    Chang Lifu from his positions as Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council and Director of the General Office of the State Council;
    Wang Gonggui from his position as Deputy Director of the Central Meteorological Bureau;
    Li Fangyuan and Ouyang Jingrong from their positions as Assistant Ministers of the Ministry of Internal Affairs;
    Sun Wenshu from her position as Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Education;
    Tang Nanping from his position as Vice President of Central South Institute of Mining and Metallurgy.

### 国务院任免名单

1959-06-27
第6版()
专栏：

    国务院任免名单
    新华社25日讯　国务院在4月16日和6月19日分别任免了一批国家机关、驻外使馆和高等学校的工作人员。
    4月16日任免的名单如下：
    任命吴晋石为中华人民共和国驻日内瓦总领事；
    李石为中华人民共和国驻苏丹共和国大使馆参赞；
    李培之为中国人民大学副校长；
    邹鲁风为北京大学副校长；
    薛迅、马健民为北京师范大学副校长；

    免去李金德、陈昭的国务院秘书厅副主任职务；

    甘德洲的中央气象局副局长职务；

    常黎夫的国务院副秘书长职务、国务院秘书厅主任职务；
    王功贵的中央气象局副局长职务；
