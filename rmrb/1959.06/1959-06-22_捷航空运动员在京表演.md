Title: Czechoslovakian Aviation Athletes Perform in Beijing
Author: Wang Yuanjing
Date: 1959-06-22
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, June 21st. Our reporter Wang Yuanjing reports: The Czechoslovakian aviation sports team, which possesses world-class skills, gave its first performance in China in Beijing today. The guest team's athletes performed various thrilling airplane and glider aerobatics, special parachute jumps, and radio-controlled and manipulated model aircraft flights, which were warmly welcomed and praised by more than 40,000 spectators. Xie Juezai, President of the Supreme People's Court, Zhang Dingcheng, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, Li Da, Director of the Chinese People's National Defense Sports Association, and Wu Faxian, Political Commissar of the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, met with all members of the guest team on the airport runway.

### 捷航空运动员在京表演
王元敬
1959-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

    捷航空运动员在京表演
    据新华社21日讯　本社记者王元敬报道：具有世界水平的捷克斯洛伐克航空运动队，今天在北京举行了访华第一次表演。客队运动员表演的各种惊险的飞机和滑翔机特技飞行，特技跳伞，以及无线电和操纵模型飞机飞行等节目，受到了四万多名观众的热烈欢迎和称赞。参观表演的最高人民法院院长谢觉哉，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，中国人民国防体育协会主任李达和中国人民解放军空军政治委员吴法宪等人，在机场跑道上接见了客队全体人员。
