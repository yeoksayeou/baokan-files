Title: At the Invitation of the CPSU Central Committee and the Council of Ministers, German Party and Government Delegation Will Visit the Soviet Union
Author: 
Date: 1959-06-06
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, June 5th: The Press Office of the Premier of the German Democratic Republic announced today that at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers, a party and government delegation from the German Democratic Republic will depart for the Soviet Union on June 8th for a visit.

The delegation members include: First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and First Deputy Premier of the German Democratic Republic, Walter Ulbricht; Member of the Central Politburo and Premier, Otto Grotewohl; Member of the Central Politburo, Deputy Premier, and Chairman of the State Planning Commission, Bruno Leuschner; Member of the Central Politburo and Mayor of Greater Berlin, Ebert; Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber and Chairman of the Christian Democratic Union, August Bach; Deputy Premier and Chairman of the Liberal Democratic Party, Dr. Hans Loch; Deputy Premier and Vice Chairman of the Democratic Farmers' Party, Paul Scholz; Chairman of the National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany, Professor E. Correns; Vice Chairman of the People's Chamber and Vice Chairman of the National Democratic Party, Heinrich Homann; and the Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to the Soviet Union, König, totaling eighteen people.

### 应苏共中央和部长会议邀请  德党政代表团将访苏

1959-06-06
第5版()
专栏：

    应苏共中央和部长会议邀请
    德党政代表团将访苏
    新华社柏林5日电　德意志民主共和国总理新闻处今天宣布，应苏联共产党中央委员会和部长会议的邀请，德意志民主共和国党和政府代表团将在6月8日启程，前往苏联访问。
    代表团的成员有：德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记、德意志民主共和国第一副总理乌布利希，中央政治局委员、总理格罗提渥，中央政治局委员、副总理兼国家计划委员会主席布鲁诺·洛伊施纳，中央政治局委员、大柏林市长艾伯特，人民议院主席团副主席、基督教民主联盟主席奥古斯特·巴赫，副总理、自由民主党主席汉斯·洛赫博士，副总理、民主农民党副主席保罗·绍尔茨，民主德国全国阵线全国委员会主席埃·柯伦斯教授，人民议院副主席、国家民主党副主席海因里希·霍曼以及德意志民主共和国驻苏联大使柯尼希等十八人。
