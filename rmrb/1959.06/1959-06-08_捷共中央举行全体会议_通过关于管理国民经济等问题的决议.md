Title: Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session, Passes Resolution on Issues Such as Managing the National Economy
Author:
Date: 1959-06-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 6th. The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party held a plenary session on June 5th and 6th.

At the meeting, Viliam Široký, member of the Politburo of the Party Central Committee and Prime Minister of the Republic, Ludmila Jankovcová, alternate member of the Politburo and Deputy Prime Minister, and Pastyřík, member of the Central Committee, delivered reports on issues such as the management of the national economy, the new system of agricultural product procurement and procurement prices, and the organizational situation of the Party.

The meeting discussed these reports and unanimously passed corresponding resolutions on the issues discussed.

### 捷共中央举行全体会议  通过关于管理国民经济等问题的决议

1959-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    捷共中央举行全体会议
    通过关于管理国民经济等问题的决议
    新华社布拉格6日电　捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会在6月5日和6日举行全体会议。
    会上，党中央政治局委员、共和国总理西罗基、政治局候补委员、副总理扬科夫卓娃和中央委员帕斯提济克分别就管理国民经济、新的农产品收购制度和收购价格以及党的组织状况等问题作了报告。
    会议讨论了这些报告，并且就所讨论的问题一致通过了相应的决议。
