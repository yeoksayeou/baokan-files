Title: Ending a Friendly Visit to Mongolia, Our Military Delegation Departs Mongolia for Home
Author:
Date: 1959-06-12
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 11th—The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation led by Marshal Peng Dehuai, after a five-day friendly visit to Mongolia, departed Ulaanbaatar by special train today for home.
    Those who saw the delegation off at the station included Tsende, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Ramsuren, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Lieutenant General Dorj, Minister of Military and Public Security, Shagdar Surun, Minister of Foreign Affairs, Sharab, Chairman of the Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Association, and others.
    Xie Fusheng, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Mongolia also saw the delegation off at the station.
    Marshal Peng Dehuai, accompanied by Lieutenant General Dorj, reviewed the guard of honor.
    Following this, Lieutenant General Dorj delivered a farewell speech. He asked the delegation to convey the wishes of the Mongolian people and army to the Chinese people and army, and wished the Chinese people and army greater achievements in socialist construction. Marshal Peng Dehuai said in his farewell speech that we will definitely bring everything we have seen in your country, and your deep friendship for the Chinese people and army, to our people and army. The cooperative relationship between our two peoples and armies will be further strengthened, and our unity and friendship will last forever.

### 结束在蒙古的友好访问  我军事代表团离蒙回国

1959-06-12
第5版()
专栏：

    结束在蒙古的友好访问
    我军事代表团离蒙回国
    新华社乌兰巴托11日电　由彭德怀元帅率领的中国军事友好代表团在蒙古进行了五天友好访问之后，今天从乌兰巴托乘专车回国。
    到车站欢送的有蒙古人民革命党中央政治局委员曾德、部长会议副主席拉姆苏伦、军事公安部长道尔吉中将、外交部长沙格达尔苏伦、蒙中友好协会主席沙拉布等人。
    中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生和各国驻蒙古的外交使节也到车站送行。
    彭德怀元帅在道尔吉中将的陪同下检阅了仪仗队。
    接着道尔吉中将致欢送词。他请代表团向中国人民和军队转达蒙古人民和军队的祝愿，并且祝中国人民和军队在社会主义建设中取得更大成就。彭德怀元帅致告别辞说，我们一定要把在你们国家所看到的一切，把你们对中国人民和军队的深情厚谊带给我国人民和军队。我们两国人民和军队间的合作关系必将进一步加强，我们的团结和友谊将万世长存。
