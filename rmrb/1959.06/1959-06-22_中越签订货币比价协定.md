Title: China and Vietnam Sign Currency Exchange Rate Agreement
Author:
Date: 1959-06-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 21st: A currency exchange rate and non-trade payment agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam was signed in Hanoi on the 20th.

Cao Juru, head of the Bank of China delegation and Director-General of the People's Bank of China, and Le Thiet Luong, the Vietnamese government representative and Director-General of the National Bank of Vietnam, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments. The Vietnamese side present at the signing ceremony included Nguyen Duc Duong, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Le Loc, Director of the Asian and African Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. The Chinese side included Mo Yanzhong, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam, and Cheng Enshu, Deputy Representative of the Economic Liaison Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade in Vietnam.

### 中越签订货币比价协定

1959-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

    中越签订货币比价协定
    新华社河内21日电　中华人民共和国和越南民主共和国之间的货币比价和非贸易支付协定20日在河内签字。
    中国银行代表团团长、中国人民银行总行行长曹菊如和越南政府代表、越南国家银行总行行长黎曰亮代表两国政府分别在协定上签了字。参加签字仪式的，越南方面有：外交部副部长阮德扬、外交部亚非司司长黎禄；中国方面有：中国驻越南大使馆临时代办莫燕忠和中国对外贸易部经济联络局驻越南代表处副代表成恩树。
