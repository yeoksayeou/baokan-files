Title: New Edition of "History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union" Published
Author:
Date: 1959-06-14
Page: 3

    New Edition of "History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union" Published

    The new edition of the textbook "History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union" has been published in Moscow.

    The publication of the new edition of the "History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union" textbook is of great political significance. The broad masses of working people will learn from this book the path to revolutionary victory of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the knowledge of the laws of building socialism and communism, and further arm themselves with Marxist-Leninist theory.

    The new edition of the textbook is divided into eighteen chapters. It begins with the start of the Russian workers' movement in 1883 and the spread of Marxism in Russia, and continues until the period when the Soviet Union entered the stage of comprehensively unfolding the construction of a communist society, the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

    The new edition of the "History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union" textbook was compiled jointly by many historians, economists, and philosophers under the leadership of Ponomarev, Corresponding Member of the Academy of Sciences of the Soviet Union. (Attached picture)

### 新版“苏共党史”出版

1959-06-14
第3版()
专栏：

    新版“苏共党史”出版
    新版“苏联共产党历史”教科书已在莫斯科出版。
    新版“苏联共产党历史”教科书的出版具有重大的政治意义。广大的劳动人民将从这本书中学习到苏联共产党革命胜利的道路和建设社会主义与共产主义的规律的知识，并进一步用马克思列宁主义理论来武装自己。
    新版教科书共分十八章。从1883年俄国工人运动的开始和马克思主义在俄国的传播起一直写到苏联进入全面展开共产主义社会建设时期，苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会。
    新版“苏联共产党历史”教科书是在苏联科学院通讯院士波诺马烈夫的领导下，由许多历史学家、经济学家和哲学家共同编写的。（附图片）
