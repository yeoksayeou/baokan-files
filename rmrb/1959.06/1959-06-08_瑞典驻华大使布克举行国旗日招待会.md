Title: Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Booker, Holds National Flag Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1959-06-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 6th: The Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Booker, held a reception on the afternoon of June 6th to celebrate the Kingdom of Sweden's National Flag Day. Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China attended the reception.

### 瑞典驻华大使布克举行国旗日招待会

1959-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞典驻华大使布克举行国旗日招待会
    新华社6日讯　瑞典驻中国大使布克6日下午举行招待会，庆祝瑞典王国国旗日。国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，外交部副部长罗贵波，以及各国驻我国使节应邀出席了招待会。
