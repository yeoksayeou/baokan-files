Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Continue

Author: 
Date: 1959-06-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, June 19th: The 90th meeting of the Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks was held in Warsaw this afternoon. The meeting lasted one hour and forty minutes.

After the meeting, both sides announced that the next meeting would be held on July 28th.

### 中美大使会议继续举行

1959-06-20
第4版()
专栏：

    中美大使会议继续举行
    新华社华沙19日电　中美两国大使级会谈第九十次会议，于今天下午在华沙举行。会议历时一小时四十分。
    会后双方宣布，下次会议将在7月28日举行。
