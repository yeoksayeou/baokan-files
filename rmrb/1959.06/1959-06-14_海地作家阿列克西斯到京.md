Title: Haitian Writer Alexis Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-06-14
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 13th June: Haitian writer Jacques Stephen Alexis arrived in Beijing by plane this morning. He is visiting China at the invitation of Mao Dun, Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association, after attending the Third Congress of Soviet Writers.

### 海地作家阿列克西斯到京

1959-06-14
第3版()
专栏：

    海地作家阿列克西斯到京
    新华社13日讯　海地作家雅克·斯蒂芬·阿列克西斯在今天上午乘飞机到达北京。他是在出席苏联第三次作家代表大会后，应中国作家协会主席茅盾的邀请来我国访问的。
