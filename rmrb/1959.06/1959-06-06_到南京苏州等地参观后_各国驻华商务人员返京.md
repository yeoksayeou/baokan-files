Title: Commercial Representatives of Various Countries Stationed in China Return to Beijing After Visiting Nanjing, Suzhou and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1959-06-06
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 4th. The tour group of commercial representatives of various countries stationed in China, organized by the Ministry of Foreign Trade, returned to Beijing on the 2nd after a two-week visit to Jiangsu Province. Commercial representatives from nineteen countries participated in the tour group.

### 到南京苏州等地参观后  各国驻华商务人员返京

1959-06-06
第5版()
专栏：

    到南京苏州等地参观后
    各国驻华商务人员返京
    新华社4日讯　对外贸易部组织的各国驻华商务人员参观旅行团在江苏省访问两周后，已经在2日返回北京。参加旅行团的共有十九个国家的驻华商务代表。
