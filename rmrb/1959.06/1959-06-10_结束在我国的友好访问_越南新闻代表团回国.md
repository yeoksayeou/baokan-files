Title: Vietnamese News Delegation Ends Friendly Visit to China and Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-06-10
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, 9th. The Vietnamese news delegation of nine people, led by He Wenlu, member of the editorial board of the "People's Daily," the central organ of the Vietnamese Labor Party, left Nanning by train this morning to return home. The delegation was invited by the All-China Journalists Association and visited China to implement the Sino-Vietnamese Cultural Cooperation Agreement. During their visits to Beijing, Hefei, Shanghai, Wuhan, Guangzhou, and other places, they extensively exchanged work experience with Chinese journalists.

### 结束在我国的友好访问  越南新闻代表团回国

1959-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    结束在我国的友好访问
    越南新闻代表团回国
    新华社南宁9日电　由越南劳动党中央机关报“人民报”编委何文禄率领的越南新闻工作者代表团一行九人，今天上午乘火车离开南宁回国。代表团是应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请和执行中越文化合作协定来我国访问的。他们在北京、合肥、上海、武汉、广州等地参观访问期间，同我国新闻界广泛交流了工作经验。
