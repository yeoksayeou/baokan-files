Title: Cui Yongjian Returns to Pyongyang After Ending Visit to Brotherly Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-06-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 19th: Cui Yongjian, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, returned to Pyongyang by jet plane at 11:30 this morning after visiting nine brotherly countries, including the Soviet Union, at their invitation.

### 结束在兄弟国家访问  崔庸健回到平壤

1959-06-20
第4版()
专栏：

    结束在兄弟国家访问
    崔庸健回到平壤
    新华社平壤19日电　朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健，应邀访问苏联等九个兄弟国家后，在今天上午十一时半乘喷气式客机回到平壤。
