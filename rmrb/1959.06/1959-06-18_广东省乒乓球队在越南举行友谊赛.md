Title: Guangdong Provincial Ping Pong Team Holds Friendly Matches in Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1959-06-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi: The Guangdong Provincial Ping Pong Team competed with the Vietnam Central Sports School Team in Hanoi on the evening of the 16th, playing nine matches (six men's singles, two women's singles, and one men's doubles), winning eight and losing one. Vietnamese party, government, and military leaders, foreign guests, and over a thousand Hanoi ping pong enthusiasts watched the intense competition.

On the evening of the 15th, the Guangdong Provincial Ping Pong Team achieved victory in their first match in Vietnam, winning six out of nine matches. This match was held against the Hanoi City Representative Team. In the eight singles matches, the Guangdong team's women's team won two out of three matches, and the men's team won three out of five matches. The Guangdong team won the doubles match.

### 广东省乒乓球队在越南举行友谊赛

1959-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    广东省乒乓球队在越南举行友谊赛
    据新华社河内讯　广东省乒乓球队16日晚间在河内与越南中央体校队比赛了九盘（男子单打六盘、女子单打两盘、男子双打一盘），八胜一败。越南党政军领导人、各国外宾和河内乒乓球爱好者一千多人观看了这场激烈的比赛。
    15日晚，广东省乒乓球队以九盘六胜取得了访越第一次比赛的胜利。这次比赛是与河内市代表队进行的。在八盘单打中，广东队的女子组三盘二胜，男子组五盘三胜。一盘双打广东队获胜。
