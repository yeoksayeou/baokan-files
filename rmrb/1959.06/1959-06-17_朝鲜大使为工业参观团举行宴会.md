Title: Korean Ambassador Holds Banquet for Industrial Delegation

Author:

Date: 1959-06-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 16th: Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yongho, held a banquet this evening for the Korean industrial delegation, led by Deputy Premier Jung Il-yong, visiting China. Vice Premier of the State Council, Bo Yibo, attended the banquet.

    Ambassador Li Yongho said in his speech at the banquet: The warm reception given by the Chinese people to the Korean industrial delegation demonstrates that the unbreakable unity and friendship between the Korean and Chinese people, forged in blood, is being further consolidated and strengthened.

    Deputy Premier Jung Il-yong and Vice Premier Bo Yibo had cordial conversations at the banquet, and proposed toasts to the growing friendship between the two peoples and to the increasing prosperity and strength of both countries.

    Also attending the banquet were Zhao Erlu, Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building; Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Xue Muqiao, Deputy Director of the State Economic Commission; Chai Shufan, Deputy Director of the State Capital Construction Commission; Xu Daben, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Coal Industry; Cheng Mingsheng, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Water Resources and Electric Power; Wang Xinyuan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Light Industry, and others.

### 朝鲜大使为工业参观团举行宴会

1959-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜大使为工业参观团举行宴会
    新华社16日讯　朝鲜驻华大使李永镐今天晚间为以郑一龙副首相为首的朝鲜工业参观团在中国访问举行宴会。国务院副总理薄一波出席了宴会。
    李永镐大使在宴会上致词说：中国人民对朝鲜工业参观团的热情款待，标明了朝中两国人民以鲜血结成的牢不可破的团结和友谊在进一步巩固和加强。
    郑一龙副首相和薄一波副总理在宴会上亲切交谈，并且互相提议为两国人民间日益增长的友谊、为两国日益繁荣富强干杯。
    参加宴会的还有第一机械工业部部长赵尔陆、外交部副部长姬鹏飞、国家经济委员会副主任薛暮桥、国家基本建设委员会副主任柴树藩、煤炭工业部副部长徐达本、水利电力部副部长程明升、轻工业部副部长王新元等。
