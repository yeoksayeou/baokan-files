Title: Harvest Joy
Author: Mao Qi
Date: 1959-06-13
Page: 8

　　Harvest Joy
　　Mao Qi
　　Silvery moonlight shines on the wheat stacks,
　　Young men winnowing, girls sifting,
　　Young men love to sing while working,
　　Calling out to the girls, "Let's have one!";
　　One comes, one responds,
　　The threshing ground is filled with "Harvest Joy,"
　　Stars twinkle as the east turns white,
　　Songs and grain fill ten thousand baskets.

### 丰收乐
毛锜
1959-06-13
第8版()
专栏：

    丰收乐
    毛锜
    银白的月光照麦垛，
    小伙子扬场姑娘簸，
    小伙子干活爱唱歌，
    吆喝姑娘们“来一个”；
    来一个，应一个，
    麦场上一片“丰收乐”，
    星星眨眼东方白，
    歌和粮食装万箩。
