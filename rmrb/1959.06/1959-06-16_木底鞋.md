Title: Wooden Clogs
Author:
Date: 1959-06-16
Page: 2

    Wooden Clogs
  　The Qixinggang Wood Sole Shoe Factory in Chongqing uses wood instead of leather for the soles of its shoes, producing large quantities of inexpensive and practical sandals. Because the workers pay attention to improving technology, the wooden clogs they make are better each time. (Attached picture)
    The picture above shows workers studying the style of the shoes. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Shilin photo

### 木底鞋

1959-06-16
第2版()
专栏：

    木底鞋
  　重庆七星岗木板鞋厂用木料代替皮革作鞋底，生产出大批、价廉适用的凉鞋。由于职工注意改进技术，做出的木底鞋一次比一次好。（附图片）
    上图是工人正在研究鞋的式样。新华社记者　刘诗临摄
