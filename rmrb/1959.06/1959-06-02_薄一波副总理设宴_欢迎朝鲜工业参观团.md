Title: Vice Premier Bo Yibo Hosts Banquet to Welcome Korean Industrial Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-06-02
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, June 1st — Vice Premier Bo Yibo hosted a banquet this evening to welcome Korean Deputy Premier Jeong Il-yong and the entire members of the Korean Industrial Delegation led by him. At the banquet, the hosts and guests engaged in cordial conversations, raising their glasses to toast the eternal development of Sino-Korean friendship.

    Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yong-ho, was invited to attend the banquet.

    Among those present were: Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Deputy Directors of the State Economic Commission Xue Muqiao and Guo Hongtao, Deputy Director of the State Construction Commission Chai Shufan, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building Wang Daohan, Vice Minister of the Coal Industry Liu Xiangsan, Vice Minister of Water Conservancy and Electric Power Cheng Mingsheng, as well as responsible individuals from the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry, the Ministry of Light Industry, and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee.

    Prior to the banquet, Vice Premier Bo Yibo had a cordial meeting with Deputy Premier Jeong Il-yong and members of the delegation.

### 薄一波副总理设宴  欢迎朝鲜工业参观团

1959-06-02
第5版()
专栏：

    薄一波副总理设宴
    欢迎朝鲜工业参观团
    新华社1日讯　薄一波副总理今天晚间设宴欢迎朝鲜内阁副首相郑一龙和由他率领的朝鲜工业参观团全体团员。宴会上，宾主亲切交谈，举杯祝贺中朝友谊永远发展。
    朝鲜驻华大使李永镐应邀出席宴会。
    出席作陪的有：外交部副部长姬鹏飞，国家经济委员会副主任薛暮桥、郭洪涛，国家基本建设委员会副主任柴树藩，第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，煤炭工业部副部长刘向三，水利电力部副部长程明升，以及冶金工业部、轻工业部和北京市人民委员会的负责人。
    宴会以前，薄一波副总理同郑一龙副首相以及参观团团员们进行了亲切的会见。
