Title: China-Ceylon Trade Negotiations Joint Communiqué; Ceylon Prime Minister Hosts Banquet for Chinese Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-06-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 13th - This dispatch was released here today, it is the joint communiqué between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of Ceylon regarding the 1959 trade negotiations. The full text of the joint communiqué is as follows:
The representatives of both the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of Ceylon, in accordance with the provisions of the "Trade and Payments Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of Ceylon" concluded on September 19, 1957, signed the 1959 China-Ceylon Barter Protocol, the contract for the Government of the People's Republic of China to purchase 30,000 metric tons of rubber from the Government of Ceylon, and the contract for the Government of Ceylon to purchase 230,000 metric tons of rice from the Government of the People's Republic of China after negotiations in Colombo.
The 1959 barter protocol between the two countries indicates that the total amount of China's exports to Ceylon reached 124,600,000 rupees, and Ceylon's exports to China reached the same amount.
The negotiations were conducted in a friendly and sincere atmosphere, and the agreements reached by both sides demonstrated the further consolidation and development of trade and friendly relations between China and Ceylon.

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 13th - The signing ceremony for the 1959 barter protocol and rubber and rice trade contracts between the Government of Ceylon and the Government of the People's Republic of China was held here this morning.
Ceylon's Minister of Commerce and Trade, Mr. Senanayake, and the head of the Chinese trade delegation, Mr. Lei Renmin, signed the protocol and contracts on behalf of their respective governments.
Ceylon's Prime Minister, Mr. Bandaranaike, said at the ceremony that although the negotiations had taken a considerable amount of time, both sides had reached very gratifying and satisfactory conclusions in an atmosphere of mutual understanding and friendship between the two countries. He said, "(The China-Ceylon Trade and Payments) Agreement will further help strengthen the friendship and understanding between our two countries in the coming year. I hope that our friendship will be further strengthened in the future."
Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Trade, said at the ceremony that with the growing friendly relations between China and Ceylon, economic and trade relations between China and Ceylon have made good progress. The economic and trade relations between the two countries are based on equality and mutual benefit and according to the needs and possibilities of both sides. This relationship is fully in line with the spirit of the Bandung Conference. He said that China and Ceylon are engaged in economic construction, and the further development of economic and trade relations between the two countries will help the economic construction and development of both countries. He expressed his belief that both sides will fulfill the obligations stipulated in the protocol and contract in the spirit of cooperation that permeated the talks.
Earlier, Mr. Senanayake said that he was very pleased to see Ceylon and China sign the seventh year of the barter protocol. Reviewing the signing of the agreement in 1952, the Minister said that at that time, due to the drop in rubber prices, the Ceylon government was planning to abandon the rubber industry. The agreement signed with China not only helped Ceylon stabilize prices, but also restored Ceylon's rubber industry.

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 13th - Ceylon's Prime Minister, Mr. Bandaranaike, hosted a luncheon today in honor of the Chinese trade delegation led by Mr. Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Trade. The banquet was attended by the Governor-General of Ceylon, Sir Oliver Goonetilleke, as well as some ministers and other senior officials of the Ceylon government. Mr. Zhang Canming, the Chinese Ambassador to Ceylon, also attended the banquet.
Yesterday, Ceylon's Governor-General Goonetilleke and Prime Minister Bandaranaike respectively received Mr. Lei Renmin. On the same day, Mr. Lei Renmin also met with Ceylon's Minister of Commerce and Trade, Mr. Senanayake.
Lei Renmin, head of the Chinese government trade delegation and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, arrived in Colombo on the afternoon of the 11th.

### 中锡贸易谈判联合公报  锡兰总理设宴招待我贸易代表团

1959-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中锡贸易谈判联合公报
    锡兰总理设宴招待我贸易代表团
    新华社13日讯　此间今天发表了中华人民共和国政府和锡兰政府关于1959年贸易谈判的联合公报。联合公报全文如下：
    中华人民共和国政府和锡兰政府双方的代表根据1957年9月19日两国政府所缔结的“中华人民共和国政府、锡兰政府贸易和支付协定”的规定，在科伦坡进行谈判后，签订了1959年中国和锡兰换货议定书、中华人民共和国政府向锡兰政府购买三万公吨橡胶的合同以及锡兰政府向中华人民共和国政府购买二十三万公吨大米的合同。
    1959年两国间的换货议定书表明，中国向锡兰出口的总金额达一二四，六○○，○○○卢比，锡兰向中国的出口达到同等金额。
    谈判是在友好和诚恳的气氛中进行的，双方所达成的各项协议显示了中锡两国间的贸易和友好关系的进一步巩固和发展。
    新华社科伦坡13日电　锡兰政府和中华人民共和国政府1959年的换货议定书和橡胶、大米贸易合同的签字仪式今天上午在这里举行。
    锡兰商业和贸易部长森纳那亚克和中国贸易代表团团长雷任民分别代表两国政府在议定书和合同上签了字。
    锡兰总理班达拉奈克在仪式上说，虽然谈判进行了相当长的时间，但是双方已经在两国互相谅解和友谊的气氛中得出十分令人高兴和满意的结论。他说，“（锡中贸易和支付）协定到来年将进一步帮助加强我们两国之间的友谊和谅解。我希望，我们的友谊将来会进一步加强。”
    中国对外贸易部副部长雷任民在仪式上说，随着中锡两国间的友好关系日益增长，中国和锡兰的经济和贸易关系已经取得了良好的进展。两国的经济和贸易关系是以平等互利和根据双方的需要和可能为基础的。这种关系是同万隆会议的精神完全符合的。他说，中锡两国正在从事经济建设，两国之间的经济和贸易关系的进一步发展，将有助于两国的经济建设和发展。他表示相信，双方将以在会谈中充满的那种合作精神来履行议定书和合同所规定的义务。
    森纳那亚克早些时候说，他非常高兴地看到锡兰和中国签订第七年的换货议定书。这位部长在回顾1952年签订协定的时候说，当时由于橡胶价格下跌，锡兰政府正打算放弃橡胶工业。同中国签订的协定不仅帮助锡兰稳定了价格，而且还恢复了锡兰的橡胶工业。
    据新华社科伦坡13日电　锡兰总理班达拉奈克今天午宴招待以中国对外贸易部副部长雷任民为首的中国贸易代表团。出席宴会的有锡兰总督奥利弗·古涅狄莱克以及锡兰政府的一些部长和其他高级官员。中国驻锡兰大使张灿明也出席了宴会。
    昨天，锡兰总督古涅狄莱克、总理班达拉奈克分别接见了雷任民。同一天，雷任民还会见了锡兰商业和贸易部长森纳那亚克。
    中国政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长雷任民是在11日下午到达科伦坡的。
