Title: Korean Industrial Delegation Leaves Beijing for Northeast China Visit
Author:
Date: 1959-06-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 17th. The Vice-Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Jung Il-yong, and some members of the Korean Industrial Delegation he leads, accompanied by Yong Longgui, Director of the World Economy Bureau of the State Planning Commission of our country, left Beijing by train tonight to visit Northeast China.
    Vice Premier Bo Yibo went to the station to see them off. Those who went to the station to see them off also included Xue Muqiao, Ji Pengfei and other responsible persons from relevant departments.
    Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yong-ho, and embassy staff went to the station to see them off.

### 朝鲜工业参观团离京去东北访问

1959-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜工业参观团离京去东北访问
    新华社17日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相郑一龙和他率领的朝鲜工业参观团部分团员，由我国国家计划委员会世界经济局局长勇龙桂陪同，今天晚上乘火车离开北京前往东北各地参观访问。
    薄一波副总理前往车站欢送。到车站欢送的还有薛暮桥、姬鹏飞等有关部门负责人。
    朝鲜驻我国大使李永镐和使馆人员到车站送行。
