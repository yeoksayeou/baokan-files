Title: Castro Says at Mass Rally in Santa Clara: Cuban Land Reform Will Never Be Abandoned Halfway; Announces That Anyone Taking Up Arms in Rebellion Against Land Reform Will Be Executed

Author:
Date: 1959-06-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 22nd - Yesterday afternoon, a mass rally of 80,000 people was held in Santa Clara City to defend land reform and the Cuban revolution. Cuban Prime Minister Castro attended the rally and addressed the masses.

Castro reaffirmed the determination of the Cuban government and people to implement land reform. He stated: Land reform must be carried through to the end, and if necessary, he is determined to carry out land reform while fighting. Castro said, "Land reform is a continuation of the struggle for national independence and the struggle between the revolution and the enemies of the revolution, and it must not be abandoned halfway."

Castro said, "War criminals, colluding with Trujillo, are buying planes and weapons in Mexico, Miami (in the United States), and the Dominican Republic, preparing for invasion." Castro pointed out that if the invaders dare to set foot on Cuban soil, they must fight the entire people, "They must fight for every street, every house, every hilltop, every river, every trench, and even face resistance from children."

Castro said that the Cuban government has already passed a law on the 20th stipulating that anyone who launches an armed rebellion against the revolution and land reform, anyone who lands from the sea or air with weapons from abroad, will be executed. Castro asked the audience, "Those who agree with this, please raise your hands." At this moment, everyone attending the meeting raised their hands together.

Castro explained the Land Reform Law and the prospects it opens up for farmers.

The General Secretary of the Cuban Workers' Confederation, Salvador, announced at this mass rally that the National Leadership Committee of the "July 26 Movement" called on the people to donate their income from July 26th to the National Land Reform Committee.

### 卡斯特罗在圣克拉腊城群众大会上说  古巴土地改革决不半途而废  宣布凡武装叛乱反对土改者一律处死

1959-06-24
第5版()
专栏：

    卡斯特罗在圣克拉腊城群众大会上说
    古巴土地改革决不半途而废
    宣布凡武装叛乱反对土改者一律处死
    新华社哈瓦那22日电　昨天下午在圣克拉腊城举行了有八万人参加的保卫土地改革和保卫古巴革命的群众大会。古巴总理卡斯特罗参加了这次大会并向群众讲了话。
    卡斯特罗重申了古巴政府和人民实行土地改革的决心。他表示：土地改革必须进行到底，如果有必要的话，决心一面进行土地改革一面战斗。卡斯特罗说，“土地改革是民族独立斗争以及革命和革命敌人之间斗争的继续，不许半途而废。”
    卡斯特罗说，“战犯们同特鲁希略勾结在一起，正在墨西哥、（美国的）迈阿密和多米尼加购买飞机和武器，准备入侵。”卡斯特罗指出，如果入侵者敢于踏上古巴的土地，他们就必须和全体人民交战，“他们必须一条街挨着一条街，一家挨着一家，一个山头挨着一个山头，一条河接着一条河，一个战壕连着一个战壕地争夺，甚至还要遭到小孩子的抵抗。”
    卡斯特罗说，古巴政府已经在20日通过一项法律，这项法律规定凡是举行武装叛乱反对革命和土地改革，凡是从外国带着武器从海上或从空中登陆的，一概处以死刑。卡斯特罗问到会群众，“同意这点的请举手”。这时参加会议的人都一齐举起了手。
    卡斯特罗向农民解释了土地改革法和它给农民打开的前景。
    古巴工人联合会总书记萨尔瓦多在这个群众集会上宣布，“7月26日运动”的全国领导委员会号召人民把7月26日那天的收入捐献给土地改革全国委员会。
