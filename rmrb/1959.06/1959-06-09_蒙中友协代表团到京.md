Title: Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-06-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 8th: The delegation of the Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Association, led by Natsukdorj, Vice-Chairman of the Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Association, arrived in Beijing by train today at the invitation of the Chinese-Mongolian Friendship Association for a visit to our country. Zhang Zhixiang, President of the Chinese-Mongolian Friendship Association, and others went to the station to welcome them.

### 蒙中友协代表团到京

1959-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙中友协代表团到京
    新华社8日讯　应中蒙友好协会邀请前来我国访问的蒙中友好协会代表团一行三人，在蒙中友好协会副主席那楚克道尔吉率领下，今天乘火车到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有中蒙友好协会会长张致祥等。
