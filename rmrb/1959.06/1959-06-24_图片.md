Title: Left Sea, Painting by Li Xiongcai
Author: Li Xiongcai
Date: 1959-06-24
Page: 8

Column: Poetic Sentiment and Pictorial Meaning

	The scenery of Fujian is joyfully renewed, a true depiction of Antai Bridge.
	Most beautiful are the banyan flowers that bloom without end,
	Their fragrance fills the city, delighting visitors.
		Poem on Left Sea, Painting by Li Xiongcai
