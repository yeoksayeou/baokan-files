Title: Czechoslovakian Union of the Disabled Delegation Returns Home After Visiting Various Parts of Our Country
Author:
Date: 1959-06-12
Page: 5

　　(Our report) The Czechoslovakian Union of the Disabled delegation, led by Dr. Rudolf Dierl, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovakian Union of the Disabled, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 11th to return home. During their visits to Beijing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Wuhan, and Shenyang, they extensively exchanged work experiences with workers for the blind and deaf-mute in our country.

### 捷残缺人联盟代表团在我国各地访问后回国

1959-06-12
第5版()
专栏：

    捷残缺人联盟代表团在我国各地访问后回国
    本报讯　由捷克斯洛伐克残缺人联盟中央委员会主席罗道尔夫·迪尔博士率领的捷克斯洛伐克残缺人联盟代表团，11日上午乘飞机离开北京回国。他们在北京、上海、杭州、武汉、沈阳等地参观访问期间，同我国盲人、聋哑人工作者广泛交流了工作经验。
