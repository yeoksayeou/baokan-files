Title: My Education Delegation Visits North Korea
Author:
Date: 1959-06-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 16th: The Chinese education delegation, led by Shi Weisan, Deputy Director of the General Education Department of the Chinese Ministry of Education, arrived in Pyongyang by train today. They are visiting North Korea for one month according to the 1959 Sino-Korean cultural cooperation plan.

Li Leyan, Vice Minister of Education and Culture of North Korea, Korean educators, and staff from the Chinese Embassy in North Korea welcomed the delegation at the station.

### 我教育代表团访问朝鲜

1959-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    我教育代表团访问朝鲜
    新华社平壤16日电　由中国教育部普通教育司副司长师唯三率领的中国教育代表团一行三人今天坐火车到达平壤。他们是根据中朝1959年文化合作计划来朝鲜进行为期一个月的访问的。
    到车站欢迎代表团的有朝鲜教育文化省副相李乐彦和朝鲜的教育工作者以及中国驻朝使馆人员。
