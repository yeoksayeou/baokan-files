Title: New Russian Edition of "History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union" to Be Distributed in Major and Medium Cities Nationwide
Author:
Date: 1959-06-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 26th: The new Russian edition of the textbook "History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union" was released in Beijing on June 26th. Foreign language bookstores and foreign language departments of Xinhua bookstores in major and medium cities nationwide will also distribute it successively.

The new edition of the "History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union" textbook has eighteen chapters, starting from the beginning of the Russian workers' movement in 1883 and the spread of Marxism in Russia, and narrating up to the 21st Congress of the CPSU in 1959. The chapters on the Soviet Union's struggle to achieve victory in the Great Patriotic War and to restore and rapidly improve the national economy after the war, and on the realization of the historic resolutions of the 20th and 21st Congresses of the CPSU, have never been introduced in previously published books on the history of the CPSU.

The publication of the new edition of the "History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union" textbook has attracted widespread attention among the Soviet working people.

The Bureau for the Translation of Marx, Engels, Lenin and Stalin's Works of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and related units are translating the new edition of the "History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union" textbook. It is scheduled to be published before National Day.

### 新版“苏共党史”俄文版  将在全国大中城市发行

1959-06-27
第6版()
专栏：

    新版“苏共党史”俄文版
    将在全国大中城市发行
    新华社26日讯　俄文新版“苏联共产党历史”教科书26日已在北京发行。全国大中城市外文书店、新华书店外文部也将先后发行。
    新版“苏联共产党历史”教科书共十八章，从1883年俄国工人运动的开始和马克思主义在俄国传播起，一直叙述到1959年苏共第二十一次代表大会止。书中关于苏联在伟大卫国战争中为取得胜利和战后为恢复和迅速提高国民经济所作的斗争，关于苏共第二十次和第二十一次代表大会的历史性决议的实现等章，是过去出版的有关苏共历史书籍中从来没有介绍过的。
    新版“苏联共产党历史”教科书出版后，在苏联劳动人民中间引起了广泛的注意。
    中共中央马恩列斯著作编译局和有关单位正在翻译新版“苏联共产党历史”教科书。预定在国庆节前出书。
