Title: Korean Industrial Delegation Leaves for Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1959-06-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, June 14th: The Vice Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Jung Il-yong, and some members of the Korean Industrial Delegation he is leading, accompanied by Yong Long-gui, Director of the World Economy Bureau of the State Planning Commission, left Shanghai by train last night for a visit to Nanjing. The Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yong-ho, also accompanied them.

Those who saw them off at the station included Cao Diqiu, Vice Mayor of Shanghai, and Qi Ming, Deputy Director of the Shanghai Municipal Economic Planning Commission.

### 朝工业参观团赴南京

1959-06-15
第4版()
专栏：

    朝工业参观团赴南京
    新华社上海14日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相郑一龙和他率领的朝鲜工业参观团的部分团员，昨天晚上在国家计划委员会世界经济局局长勇龙桂陪同下，乘火车离开上海前往南京访问。朝鲜驻华大使李永镐也陪同前往。
    到车站欢送的有上海市副市长曹荻秋、上海市经济计划委员会副主任戚铭等。
