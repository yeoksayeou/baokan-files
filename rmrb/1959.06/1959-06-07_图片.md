In the nursery of Xingcheng County, Liaoning Province, eight million saplings of black pine, larch, locust, mulberry, poplar, and other trees have been cultivated. This is a scene of nursery workers irrigating black pine seedlings. (Photo by Hu Yongquan, Xinhua News Agency)
