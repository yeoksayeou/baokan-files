    Quotes from Bulgarian Communists
    Only revolutionary fervor is not enough; we must know how to master the weapons of revolutionary theory.
    Understanding theory alone is not enough; we must also temper ourselves into strong individuals with Bolshevik tenacity.
    Knowing what should be done is not enough; we must also have the courage to do what should be done.
    We must be prepared at any time to devote ourselves, without any sacrifice, to work that truly benefits the working class.
    We must be able to subordinate our entire personal lives to the interests of the proletariat.
    ——Excerpt from Dimitrov's preface to "Thälmann's Biography"
    ☆☆☆
    Do not pity me, but strive together with the heroes of the world to eliminate the executioners who kill us. As I calmly face execution, I have already tasted the sweetness of the victory to come. Farewell, do not cry for me. Seek comfort in the ranks of the fighters!
    Be more courageous, more vigorous, and filled with greater confidence in victory.
    ——Anton Ivanov's last words to his family before his execution
    ☆☆☆
    This battle is arduous and merciless.
    This battle, as people say, is epic.
    I fall. Another person takes my place—
    Why is it necessary to mention one person's name in particular?
    After the execution—maggots.
    It is such simple logic.
    But in the storm we will be with you,
    My people, because we love you so much.
    ——Vaptsarov's last poem before his martyrdom
    ☆☆☆
    My last words are: Love the Party, it is our mother; love the team, it is our strength.
    Comrade guerrillas, raise high the banner of liberation..., dedicate your strength and your lives to this great cause....
    ——Guerrilla fighter Zahari Georgiev

### 保加利亚共产党人语录

1959-06-05
第5版()
专栏：

    保加利亚共产党人语录
    只有革命的气质是不够的，我们必须懂得怎样掌握革命理论的武器。
    只懂得理论是不够的，我们还必须把自己锻炼成为一个具有布尔什维克的坚韧性的坚强人物。
    只懂应该做什么是不够的，我们还必须具有把应该做的事做出来的勇气。
    我们必须随时准备不惜任何牺牲从事于真正有利于工人阶级的工作。
    我们必须能够把我们个人的整个生命从属于无产阶级的利益。
    ——摘自季米特洛夫给“台尔曼传”写的序言
    ☆☆☆
    不要怜惜我，要同全世界的英雄们一起奋力去消灭那些杀害我们的刽子手。我在从容就义的时候已经尝到即将到来的胜利的甜味。永别了，不要为我而哭泣。在战士的行列中寻找安慰吧！
    要更加勇敢，更加奋发，对胜利充满更大的信心。
    ——安东·伊凡诺夫就义前给家属的遗嘱
    ☆☆☆
    这战斗是艰苦而无情的。
    这战斗，正如人们所说的，是史诗。
    我倒下。另一个人接替了我的位置——
    何必要特别提到一个人的名字？
    在执行枪决之后——蛆虫。
    就是这样简单的逻辑。
    但是在暴风雨中我们将和你在一起，
    我的人民呀，因为我们是如此的爱你。
    ——诗人瓦普察洛夫殉难前的最后一首诗
    ☆☆☆
    我的遗言是：要爱党，它是我们的母亲，要爱队，它是我们的力量。
    游击队员同志们，高高举起解放的旗帜……，把自己的力量和自己的生命献给这个美好的事业……。
    ——游击队员扎哈里·格奥尔基耶夫
