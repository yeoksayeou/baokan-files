Title: Korean News Delegation Goes to Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1959-06-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th - The Korean news delegation headed by Hyun Joon-geuk left Beijing by plane this morning to visit Shenyang.

Yesterday evening, Mei Yi, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, hosted a banquet to bid farewell to all members of the delegation.

### 朝鲜新闻代表团去沈阳

1959-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜新闻代表团去沈阳
    新华社6日讯　以玄峻极为首的朝鲜新闻工作者代表团，今天上午乘飞机离开北京去沈阳访问。
    昨天晚上，中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长梅益曾设宴欢送代表团全体成员。
