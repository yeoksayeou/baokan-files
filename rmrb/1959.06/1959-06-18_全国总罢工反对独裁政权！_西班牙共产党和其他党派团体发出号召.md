Title: National General Strike Against the Dictatorial Regime! Spanish Communist Party and Other Parties Issue Call
Author:
Date: 1959-06-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Madrid, June 17th: The Spanish Communist Party, the left-wing Christian Democratic Party, and other parties and groups in Spain have issued a call to all the Spanish people, urging them to hold a twenty-four-hour national peaceful general strike starting from zero o'clock on June 18th.

The full text of the call is as follows:

To all the Spanish People, June 18th is the day to hold a twenty-four-hour national peaceful general strike. The Spanish Communist Party, the Left-wing Christian Democratic Party, the Democratic Action Party, the People's Liberation Front, the Unified Socialist Party of Catalonia, the General Union of Workers, the Socialist Movement of Catalonia, the Catholic Social Movement, the Joint Action Committee of Madrid University Students (composed of students belonging to the Communist Party, the Socialist Organization of University Students, the Left-wing Christian Democratic Party, the Liberal Party, and the People's Liberation Front), the Republican Left of Catalonia, the National Front of Catalonia, the National Union of Students of Catalonia, the New Republican faction, the National Confederation of Labor of Catalonia, the Socialist Organization of Spanish University Students, etc., call on all the Spanish people to participate in the national peaceful general strike to express:

    National indignation at the extremely corrupt dictatorial regime and reactionary economic policies;
    Protest against the high cost of living and demand a general increase in wages; Unanimously demand amnesty for political prisoners and political exiles, restoration of political freedoms, and the resignation of Franco.

Workers, all of Spain expects you to unite as one and strike on this day!

Peasants, on June 18th, the Spanish countryside should be completely paralyzed!

Employees, poor peasants, middle peasants, and rich peasants, unite with each other, support each other, and take action against the dictatorship!

Clerks, civil servants, do not go to work on this day; intellectuals, artists, and freelancers, stop your activities and unite with the people in this peaceful protest!

Students, in the national strike, support the workers and the people, let your protests be manifested in universities and colleges; young people, use your enthusiasm to set an example for everyone on June 18th!

Women, stand at the forefront of the national strike and encourage your husbands, brothers, and sons to fulfill their responsibilities.

Believers, on the morning of June 18th, go to the parish churches to pray for the freedom and peace of the Spanish people!

Soldiers, armed police, civil guards, and police, the national strike is not against you, but against the dictatorship. You, like all the people, suffer from the dictatorship. On June 18th, treat the people with a brotherly attitude, do not arrest people, and do not carry out suppression! We are all Spaniards and we all hope that the situation in Spain will change.

Spanish people, on the 18th, let us all unite and let us express our will for change fervently. Do not be intimidated by the threats and repression of the tottering dictatorial government, and do not wait for your neighbors to mobilize you to participate in this action. If we unite, we will be much stronger than the dictatorship!

June 18th, from zero o'clock to twenty-four o'clock, national general strike!

Give the Spanish people peace, unity, freedom, and justice!

Long live the twenty-four-hour national peaceful general strike!

### 全国总罢工反对独裁政权！  西班牙共产党和其他党派团体发出号召

1959-06-18
第5版()
专栏：

    全国总罢工反对独裁政权！
    西班牙共产党和其他党派团体发出号召
    新华社17日讯　马德里消息：西班牙共产党，左翼天主教民主党以及西班牙其他党派和团体向全体西班牙人民发出了号召书，号召西班牙人民从6月18日零点起举行二十四小时全国和平总罢工。
    号召书全文如下：
    全体西班牙人民，6月18日是举行全国二十四小时和平总罢工的日子。西班牙共产党，左翼天主教民主党，民主行动党，人民解放阵线，加泰罗尼亚统一社会党，工人总联合会，加泰罗尼亚社会主义运动，天主教社会运动，马德里大学生联合行动委员会（由属于共产党，大学生社会主义组织，左翼天主教民主党，自由党，人民解放阵线的学生组成），加泰罗尼亚左翼共和人士，加泰罗尼亚民族阵线，加泰罗尼亚全国学生联盟，新共和国派，加泰罗尼亚全国劳工联合会，西班牙大学生社会主义组织等号召全体西班牙人民参加全国和平总罢工以表示：
    全国对极端腐朽的独裁政权和反动的经济政策的愤懑；
    抗议生活费用的昂贵和要求普遍提高工资；一致要求大赦政治犯和政治流放犯以及要求恢复政治自由和要求佛朗哥下台。
    工人们，整个西班牙期望你们在这一天团结得如同一个人一样地进行罢工！
    农民们，在6月18日，西班牙农村应该完全瘫痪！
    雇工们、贫农、中农和富农们，互相团结，互相支援，行动起来反对独裁统治！
    职员们、公务员们，这一天不要去上班；知识分子、艺术家、自由职业者们，停止你们的活动，在这次和平的抗议中，同人民团结在一起！
    学生们，在全国罢工中，支持工人和人民，让你们的抗议表现在大、专学校里，青年们，用你们的热情，在6月18日这一天，为大家作出榜样！
    妇女们，站到全国罢工的最前列来，鼓舞你们的丈夫，兄弟和儿子完成他们的责任，
    教徒们，6月18日上午，到区教堂去为西班牙人民的自由和和平祈祷！
    军人们、武装警察、宪警和警察，全国罢工不是反对你们的，是反对独裁统治的。你们和全体人民一样，都因独裁统治而受苦。在6月18日这一天，你们要用兄弟态度对待人民，不要逮捕人，也不要进行镇压！我们都是西班牙人，都希望西班牙局面改变。
    西班牙人民，在18日这一天，让我们所有的人团结一致，让我们要求变革的意志热烈地表现出来。不要被摇摇欲坠的独裁政府的威胁和镇压吓倒，不要等邻居们来动员你才参加到这一行动中来。大家团结起来，我们就比独裁统治要强大得多！
    6月18日，从零点到二十四点，全国大罢工！
    给西班牙人以和平、团结、自由和正义！
    二十四小时全国和平总罢工万岁！
