Title: Our Muslim Hajj Delegation Departs for Mecca
Author:
Date: 1959-06-07
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, June 6th. A Chinese Muslim Hajj delegation of seventeen people, led by Head of Delegation Ma Zhuozhou and Deputy Heads of Delegation Ma Motuo and Yu Zhanlin, departed from Beijing on the 4th for Mecca, the holy city of Islam. The Hajj delegation arrived in Cairo on the evening of the 5th and will stay there for a few days before proceeding to Mecca.

### 我穆斯林朝觐团赴麦加

1959-06-07
第3版()
专栏：

    我穆斯林朝觐团赴麦加
    新华社6日讯　中国穆斯林朝觐团一行十七人，在团长马卓洲和副团长马摩托夫、禺占林率领下，已经在4日由北京启程前往伊斯兰教圣地麦加。朝觐团于5日晚间到达开罗，在那里停留几天后即前往麦加。
