Author: Qian Junrui
Date: 1959-06-27
Page: 8

Wheat Harvest
    Qian Junrui
    The scorching sun holds up the sky, white clouds drift away, bending and straightening knees, sweat drips.
    The wind blows across thousands of miles, turning golden waves, exerting full strength, pulling the wheat.
    Clusters forming mountains, grains full, yes, yes, whipping the horse, cart wheels sinking.
    Just watch the joyful celebration of the harvest day, wearing multiple layers, leaping into the Great Leap Forward gate.

### 麦收
钱俊瑞
1959-06-27
第8版()
专栏：

    麦收
    钱俊瑞
    烈日擎天逝白云，弯躬挺膝汗淋淋。
    风吹千里翻金浪，劲鼓十分拔麦人。
    簇簇成山颗粒饱，唯唯策马车轮沉。
    且看欢庆丰收日，穿戴几重跃进门。
