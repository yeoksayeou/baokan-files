Title: Bulgarian National Football Second Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-06-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency News: The Bulgarian National Football Second Team, led by team leader Maslev, with a total of 22 members, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 24th in response to an invitation to visit our country and conduct friendly matches. They were warmly welcomed by Chinese football players.

### 保国家足球第二队到京

1959-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    保国家足球第二队到京
    新华社讯　应邀来我国访问和进行友谊比赛的、由领队马斯列夫率领的保加利亚国家足球队第二队全体人员二十二人，24日乘飞机到达北京时，受到了我国足球运动员们的热烈欢迎。
