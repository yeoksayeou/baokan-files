Title: Valvanne Appointed Finnish Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1959-06-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 31st. The President of the Republic of Finland appointed Hugo Valvanne as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of China, effective August 1, 1959.

### 瓦尔瓦尼任芬驻华大使

1959-06-01
第5版()
专栏：

    瓦尔瓦尼任芬驻华大使
    新华社31日讯　芬兰共和国总统已于22日任命雨果·瓦尔瓦尼自1959年8月1日起为驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使。
