Title: Korean Industrial Delegation Returns to Pyongyang After Concluding Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1959-06-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 23rd. The Korean industrial delegation, led by Korean Deputy Premier Zheng Yilong, returned to Pyongyang this afternoon by international train after concluding its visit to China.

### 结束在我国的访问  朝工业参观团回到平壤

1959-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    结束在我国的访问
    朝工业参观团回到平壤
    新华社平壤23日电　以朝鲜内阁副首相郑一龙为首的朝鲜工业参观团结束了在中国的访问以后，在今天下午乘国际列车回到平壤。
