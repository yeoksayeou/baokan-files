Title: Uruguayan Council of State Member Receives Chinese News Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-06-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, 8th: Uruguayan Council of State member and Vice Chairman of the National Party, Aido, received the Chinese News Workers Delegation this afternoon and held friendly talks with the members. The Chinese News Workers Delegation visited Uruguay's largest modern ranch on the 7th.

### 乌拉圭国务会议委员接见我新闻代表团

1959-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    乌拉圭国务会议委员接见我新闻代表团
    新华社蒙得维的亚8日电　乌拉圭国务会议委员、民族党副主席艾多今天下午接见了中国新闻工作者代表团，同团员们进行了友好交谈。中国新闻工作者代表团7日曾参观乌拉圭最大的一个现代化牧场。
