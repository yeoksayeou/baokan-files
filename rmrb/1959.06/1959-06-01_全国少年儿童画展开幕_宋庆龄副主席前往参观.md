Title: National Children's Art Exhibition Opens, Vice Chairman Soong Ching-ling Visits

Author:
Date: 1959-06-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 31st - The National Children's Art Exhibition opened in Beijing this afternoon. This exhibition is jointly sponsored by the Ministry of Education, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, the National Committee for the Defence of Children, and the Chinese Artists Association. The exhibits include more than 360 outstanding works of art by children selected from twenty-two provinces (autonomous regions) and cities across the country.

This morning, Soong Ching-ling, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China and Chairman of the National Committee for the Defence of Children, visited the exhibition accompanied by Wu Zuoren and Ye Qianyu, Vice Chairmen of the Chinese Artists Association, and Li Lilian, Deputy Secretary-General of the National Committee for the Defence of Children. Vice Chairman Soong Ching-ling expressed great satisfaction after seeing the exhibition. In her inscription, she said, "From these pictures, we can clearly see with what affectionate care our Party and Chairman Mao are educating our new generation, enabling their hearts to be filled with ideals of peace and happiness through practical labor. We hope that our educators, artists, and children's workers will pay more attention to the education and cultivation of children in the future, so that these artistic flowers will grow vigorously into red artists with a high degree of communist consciousness and artistic level, so as to better serve socialism and communism."

### 全国少年儿童画展开幕  宋庆龄副主席前往参观

1959-06-01
第6版()
专栏：

    全国少年儿童画展开幕
    宋庆龄副主席前往参观
    新华社31日讯　全国少年儿童画展今天下午在北京开幕。这个展览会是由教育部、共青团中央、保卫儿童全国委员会和中国美术家协会联合主办的。展品包括全国二十二个省（区）、市选出的三百六十多幅优秀的少年儿童美术作品。
    今天上午，中华人民共和国副主席、中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会主席宋庆龄，在中国美术家协会副主席吴作人、叶浅予，保卫儿童全国委员会副秘书长李丽莲等人陪同下，参观了这个展览会。宋庆龄副主席看了这个展览会以后表示很满意，她在题词中说，“从这些图画中可以清楚地看到我们的党和毛主席是怀着怎样爱抚的心情来教育我们的新生的一代，通过劳动实践，使他们的心灵洋溢着和平和快乐的理想。我们希望我们的教育家、艺术家和儿童工作者，今后要更加重视对孩子们的教育和培养，让这些艺术的花朵茁壮地成长为具有高度的共产主义觉悟和艺术水平的红色艺术家，以便更好地为社会主义和共产主义服务。”
