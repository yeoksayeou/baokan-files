Title: Dissatisfied with the Rule of Chiang Kai-shek's Army, Determined to Return to the Embrace of the Motherland: Jinmen Chiang Kai-shek's Army Sergeant Squad Leader He Yechen Crosses Over to Our Army

Author:
Date: 1959-06-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, June 12th: On the morning of the 11th, He Yechen, a sergeant squad leader of the 1st Company, 1st Battalion, 79th Regiment, 27th Division of the Chiang Kai-shek army, carrying a U.S.-made pistol, swam from Greater Jinmen Island back to the mainland and crossed over to the People's Liberation Army.

He Yechen is from Beihejia Village, Renhe Township, former Muping County, Shandong Province, and is thirty-four years old this year. He felt that there was no future in Chiang Kai-shek's army and was determined to risk his life to return to the embrace of the motherland. He was warmly welcomed by the People's Liberation Army on the Fujian Front.

### 不满蒋军统治　决心回到祖国怀抱  金门蒋军中士班长贺业臣向我军投诚

1959-06-13
第6版()
专栏：

    不满蒋军统治　决心回到祖国怀抱
    金门蒋军中士班长贺业臣向我军投诚
    新华社福建前线12日电　11日早上，蒋军二十七师七十九团一营一连中士班长贺业臣携带美制手枪一枝，从大金门岛泅水回到大陆，向人民解放军投诚。
    贺业臣是山东省原牟平县仁和乡北贺家村人，今年三十四岁。他感到在蒋军中没有出路，决心冒死回到祖国怀抱。他受到福建前线人民解放军的热情欢迎。
