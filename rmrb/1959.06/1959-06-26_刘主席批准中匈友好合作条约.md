Title: Chairman Liu Approves Sino-Hungarian Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1959-06-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 25th: According to the decision of the Fourth Session of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on June 20, 1959, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, approved the Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the Hungarian People's Republic, signed in Beijing on May 6, 1959, on June 25, 1959.

### 刘主席批准中匈友好合作条约

1959-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席批准中匈友好合作条约
    新华社25日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会1959年6月20日第四次会议的决定，于1959年6月25日批准了1959年5月6日在北京签订的中华人民共和国和匈牙利人民共和国友好合作条约。
