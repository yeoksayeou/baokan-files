Title: Hungarian People's Republic Presidium Holds Grand Reception for Choi Yong-kun; Khrushchev and Kabo Invited to Attend
Author:
Date: 1959-06-06
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, June 4th: The Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic held a grand reception at the Parliament Building on the evening of June 4th to welcome Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly, and his entourage, who are currently visiting Hungary.

Nikita Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and Marshal Rodion Malinovsky, Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, who arrived here from Tirana on the same day, were also invited to attend the reception.

Also invited were Albanian Labor Party Central Politburo member Kabo and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Defense Baluku, who accompanied the Soviet guests to Budapest.

Hungarian party and state leaders Dögei, Kádár, and Münnich, as well as some government ministers, heads of social organizations, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Hungary, were present as hosts.

Chairman Dögei of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic and Choi Yong-kun spoke at the reception.

The reception was held in a very warm and friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, June 5th: Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, and his entourage left Budapest by plane today to visit Albania.

Present at the airport to see them off were Dögei, Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, Kish and Ronai, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, Foreign Minister Sik, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Hungary.

### 匈牙利人民共和国主席团  举行盛会招待崔庸健  赫鲁晓夫和卡博等应邀出席

1959-06-06
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利人民共和国主席团
    举行盛会招待崔庸健
    赫鲁晓夫和卡博等应邀出席
    新华社布达佩斯4日电　匈牙利人民共和国主席团4日晚在国会大厦举行盛大招待会，招待正在这里访问的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健和他的随行人员。
    当天由地拉那到达这里访问的苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫和苏联国防部长马利诺夫斯基元帅，也应邀出席了招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有陪同苏联客人一起到达布达佩斯的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员卡博和部长会议第一副主席兼国防部长巴卢库。
    出席作陪的有：匈牙利党和国家领导人道比、卡达尔、明尼赫，以及政府的一些部长、社会团体的负责人和各国驻匈牙利的外交使节等。
    匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比和崔庸健在招待会上讲了话。
    招待会是在非常热烈友好的气氛中进行的。
    新华社布达佩斯5日电　朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健及其随行人员今天乘飞机离开布达佩斯，前往阿尔巴尼亚访问。
    到机场欢送的有匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比、匈牙利社会主义工人党中央政治局委员基什和罗奈伊、外交部部长西克以及各国驻匈牙利的外交使节。
