Title: The Indomitable Fighting Spirit of Africans in Durban Port, South African Ruling Authorities Conduct Searches Everywhere
Author:
Date: 1959-06-23
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd, Durban Dispatch: Following the large-scale demonstration on the 18th, African residents in Durban Port, South Africa, are bravely engaging in various forms of struggle, defying the white authorities' brutal suppression, to resist discrimination and mistreatment by the whites.

　　On the 22nd, a boycott of municipal buses was launched again in the Claremont African residential area on the outskirts of Durban. Hundreds of African men and women, determined not to ride the municipal authorities' buses, walked three miles from the residential area to take privately-owned buses.

　　Despite the South African white authorities' propaganda that the situation in some other African residential areas of Durban has calmed down, police patrols are still active everywhere and continue to conduct searches. Some armored vehicles dispatched from Transvaal after the large-scale African demonstration in Durban arrived in Durban on the 21st.

　　On the night of the 21st, searchlights continued to shine randomly in the Cato Manor area (an African slum) where the large-scale demonstration erupted.

　　At the same time, all African-owned beer shops in the city have been closed.

### 德班港非洲人斗志不屈  南非统治当局到处进行搜捕

1959-06-23
第5版()
专栏：

    德班港非洲人斗志不屈
    南非统治当局到处进行搜捕
    新华社22日讯　德班消息：南非联邦德班港的非洲居民在18日的大示威以后，不顾白人当局的残酷镇压，仍在英勇地进行着各种形式的斗争，反抗白人对他们的歧视和虐待。
    22日，在德班市郊克莱尔蒙特非洲人居住区，又展开了抵制市政府公共汽车的运动。数百名非洲男女为了坚决不坐市政当局办的公共汽车，从居住区一直走了三英里，去坐私人开的公共汽车。
    尽管南非白人当局宣传在德班其它一些非洲人居住区局势已经平静，但是警察巡逻队仍在到处活动，并且继续进行搜捕。白人当局在德班非洲人大示威后从德兰斯瓦尔赶派来的一些装甲车已在21日到达德班。
    21日晚，探照灯光仍在爆发大示威的卡托马诺尔区（非洲人贫民区）乱照。
    与此同时，在这座城市里，所有非洲人开的啤酒店都已给关闭了。
