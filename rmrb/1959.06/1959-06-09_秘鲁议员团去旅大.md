Title: Peruvian Parliamentary Delegation Goes to Lüda

Author:
Date: 1959-06-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 8th — The Peruvian parliamentary delegation, which is visiting our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, left Beijing by plane today to visit Lüda. The delegation arrived in Beijing on the 4th of this month. In Beijing, the members of the delegation held discussions with relevant Chinese parties on the situation of economic construction and agricultural production, and visited scenic spots and historical sites.

### 秘鲁议员团去旅大

1959-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

    秘鲁议员团去旅大
    新华社8日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请正在我国访问的秘鲁议员团今天乘飞机离开北京前往旅大市访问。议员团是本月4日到达北京的。团员们在北京曾分别和中国有关方面座谈了经济建设和农业生产等情况，并且参观了名胜古迹。
