Title: German Democratic Republic Red Cross Donates a Set of First-Aid Station Equipment to China

Author:

Date: 1959-06-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The Red Cross Society of the German Democratic Republic donated a set of first-aid station equipment to the Red Cross Society of China. The Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Wang Daire, held a reception this afternoon to hand over the set of first-aid station equipment to the Red Cross Society of China. Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, accepted this valuable gift.

In his speech at the reception, Li Dequan expressed his gratitude to the German people and the German Red Cross for their concern for the Red Cross Society of China, and stated on behalf of the Red Cross Society of China that it would make full use of this equipment. Ambassador Wang Daire expressed his gratitude at the reception for the support given by the Chinese people to the German Democratic Republic.

Among those attending the reception were Wu Yunfu, Vice President of the Red Cross Society of China, Lin Shixiao and Peng Yan, Deputy Secretaries-General, and Wang Kangjiu, Director-General of the Beijing Red Cross Society.

### 德意志民主共和国红十字会  赠我国一套急救站设备

1959-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国红十字会
    赠我国一套急救站设备
    新华社16日讯　德意志民主共和国红十字会将一套急救站装备赠送给中国红十字会。德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔今天下午为此举行酒会，将这套急救站装备交给中国红十字会。中国红十字会会长李德全接受了这一珍贵的礼品。
    李德全在酒会上讲话，对德国人民和德国红十字会对中国红十字会的关怀表示感谢，并代表中国红十字会表示将充分利用这套装备。汪戴尔大使在酒会上对中国人民给予德意志民主共和国的支持表示感谢。
    参加酒会的有中国红十字会副会长伍云甫，副秘书长林士笑、彭炎，北京市红十字会总干事王康久等。
