Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1959-06-06
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, June 5th: Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council today received Nakajima Kenzo, Iwamura Michio, Koshi Hisao, Yamamoto Hisao, and Yokoi Yuichi, representatives of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association and the Japan-China Friendship Association.
    Present at the reception were Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee.
    Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, was also present.
    The conversation took place in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

### 陈毅副总理接见日本客人

1959-06-06
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见日本客人
    新华社5日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天接见了日中文化交流协会、日中友好协会代表中岛健藏、岩村三千夫、越寿雄、山本悠夫、横井雄一等人。
    接见时，在座的有中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙、中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。
    谈话在亲切、友好的气氛中进行。
