Title: New Masterpieces of Abstract Art
Author: Le Shan (Lè Shān)
Date: 1959-06-22
Page: 5
Such a Free World

    New Masterpieces of Abstract Art

    On the Rue de Fourcroy in Paris, there is a strange attic. In this simple little room, nine bizarre machines are on display. Nine turntables hang from the ceiling. Each turntable is equipped with a variety of brushes and paint applicators. When the motor is turned on, the turntables rotate rapidly, and the brushes continuously come into contact with the drawing paper placed on the belt rack. By controlling the speed of the turntables and changing the paint applicators, chaotic lines and colors appear on the drawing paper.

    This is a report on the latest developments in abstract art by Art Buchwald, a columnist for the American "New York Herald Tribune" on June 3rd.

    Buchwald and an art critic named Watt made a special visit to Tingley, the thirty-four-year-old inventor of the abstract painting machine on Rue de Fourcroy.

    Tingley told the visitors: "This is an anti-mechanization machine, because the paintings it produces will never be exactly the same. At the same time, it is also an anti-abstract machine, because it proves that anyone, even a machine, can produce abstract paintings."

    "Everyone has a sense of art. But many people lack the courage to express it. My machine will revolutionize the art world."

    The nine machines can produce "works" of various sizes and styles, and can also produce pencil drawings, charcoal drawings, ink paintings...

    Buchwald also reported on the "market" for new masterpieces of abstract art:

    "For just one U.S. dollar, you can create your own abstract work, and its quality is on par with works popular on the market. No one can criticize it." "If you have an hour or two and are willing to spend fifteen U.S. dollars, then you can produce enough abstract artwork to open an exhibition." (Le Shan)

### 抽象派艺术的新杰作
乐山
1959-06-22
第5版()
专栏：如此自由世界

    抽象派艺术的新杰作
    巴黎福希拉尔街有一间奇怪的阁楼。在这所简陋的小房间里，陈列着九架稀奇古怪的机器。天花板上悬吊着九个转盘。每一个转盘上都插着各色各样的毛刷和颜色笔。当马达开动以后，转盘迅速转动，毛刷不断地接触着摊放在皮带架上的绘画纸。随着对转盘速度的控制与颜色笔的调换，绘画纸上出现了乱七八糟的线条与色彩。
    这是6月3日美国“纽约先驱论坛报”专栏作家布希瓦尔德，关于抽象派艺术最近动态的报道。
    布希瓦尔德与一位名叫瓦特的艺术评论家，专程拜访了福希拉尔街抽象派绘画制造机发明者、三十四岁的丁格里。
    丁格里向来访者说：“这是一部反机械化的机械，因为它生产出来的绘画，绝不会有两张完全相同的。同时，它也是一部反抽象的机器，因为它证明无论是谁、甚至于连一部机器都可以产生出抽象派绘画。”
    “每一个人都有艺术感。但是许多人缺乏表达的勇气。我的机器将要使艺术世界发生一次革命。”
    九部机器可以产生出各种大小不同、样式不同的“作品”，也可以产生铅笔画、炭画、墨画……。
    布希瓦尔德也报道了抽象派艺术新杰作的“行情”：
    “只花一元美金，你就可以绘出你自己的抽象派作品，它的质量与市场上流行的作品并驾齐驱。决没有人能够对它进行指摘。”“如果你有一两小时的时间，又肯花十五块美金，那么你就可以生产足够开一个展览会的抽象派艺术作品。”（乐山）
