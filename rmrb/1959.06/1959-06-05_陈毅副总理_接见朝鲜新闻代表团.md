Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Korean News Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-06-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, June 4th: Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received this afternoon the Korean news delegation headed by Hyun Joon-keuk, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the Korean "Rodong Sinmun," and the delegation's deputy head, Park Kyung-joon, Director of the Domestic Reporting Editorial Bureau of the Korean Central News Agency, along with all members of the delegation.

Present at the reception were Mei Yi, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association; Kang Maozhao, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Lu Minggu, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Journalists Association; and Tian Zhongluo, Deputy Director of the Literary and Art Department of the People's Daily.

Also present at the reception were Ma Dongshan, Counselor of the Korean Embassy in China, and Kim Seok-chun, Third Secretary.

### 陈毅副总理  接见朝鲜新闻代表团

1959-06-05
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理
    接见朝鲜新闻代表团
    新华社4日讯国务院副总理陈毅今天下午接见了朝鲜新闻工作者代表团团长、朝鲜“劳动新闻”副总编辑玄峻极，代表团副团长、朝鲜中央通讯社国内报道编辑局局长朴景浚和代表团全体团员。
    接见时在座的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长梅益、外交部新闻司副司长康茅召、中华全国新闻工作者协会副秘书长卢鸣谷、人民日报文艺部副主任田钟洛。
    接见时，朝鲜驻中国大使馆参赞马东山和三等秘书金锡春也在座。
