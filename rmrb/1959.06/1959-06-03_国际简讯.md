International Briefs

    Iraqi People's Delegation Visits Arab Countries
    Baghdad dispatch: The Iraqi People's Delegation, headed by Aziz Sharif, Secretary-General of the National Council of Iraqi Peace Partisans, left here on June 1st for Morocco. The delegation will also visit other Arab countries.

    Sukarno Leaves Mexico for the United States
    Mexico City dispatch: Indonesian President Sukarno departed for Los Angeles in the United States on June 1st after a six-day friendly visit to Mexico.

    Defendant in Assassination Attempt on President Sukarno Sentenced to Death
    Jakarta dispatch: The Jakarta Military Court on June 1st sentenced Wahab Pena, the fifth defendant in the Cikini case of the attempted assassination of President Sukarno in November 1957, to death. Prior to this, the Jakarta Military Court executed the three main criminals in the case, Yusuf Ismail, Satun bin Muhammad, and Tarif bin Hussein, in August 1958.

    Venezuela Holds Farmers' Congress
    Caracas dispatch: The First Venezuelan Farmers' Congress was held on May 31st in the Venezuelan capital to discuss a draft land reform law formulated by a special government committee. On the same day as the Farmers' Congress, 4,500 Venezuelan small farmers and farm workers held a march in downtown Caracas. The marchers chanted slogans of "Land to the Tillers" along the way.

### 国际简讯

1959-06-03
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    伊人民代表团访问阿拉伯各国
    巴格达讯：以伊拉克和平战士全国委员会秘书长阿齐兹·谢里夫为首的伊拉克人民代表团1日离开这里前往摩洛哥。代表团还将访问其他阿拉伯国家。
    苏加诺离墨西哥赴美
    墨西哥城讯：印度尼西亚总统苏加诺在墨西哥进行了六天友好访问以后在1日动身赴美国洛杉矶。
    谋杀苏加诺总统案被告判死刑
    雅加达讯：雅加达军事法庭1日判处1957年11月间图谋暗杀苏加诺总统的芝基尼案件中的第五名被告瓦哈布·佩纳死刑。在此以前，雅加达军事法庭曾经在1958年8月处决了这个案件中的三个主要罪犯优素福·伊斯梅尔，萨顿·本·穆罕默德和塔里夫·本·侯赛尼。
    委内瑞拉开农民代表大会
    加拉加斯讯：第一届委内瑞拉农民代表大会5月31日在委内瑞拉首都举行会议，讨论了政府一个特别委员会制订的土地改革草案。在举行农民代表大会的同一天，有四千五百名委内瑞拉小农和农场工人在加拉加斯闹区举行了游行。游行者沿途高呼“耕者有其田”的口号。
