Title: Over Forty Million Young People Wear Red Scarves
Author:
Date: 1959-06-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 31st - According to statistics from the Youth Department of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, there are currently 44 million Young Pioneers in the country.

    1958 was a year of great development for Young Pioneer organizations in various regions. Throughout the year, more than nine million children joined the Young Pioneers. The number of Young Pioneers in Zhejiang Province doubled. In Sichuan Province, one million children joined the Young Pioneers last year, and there are now a total of 4.5 million members in the province. In most minority areas, Young Pioneer organizations have also been widely established. There are 650,000 Young Pioneers in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and 200,000 in the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region. In Tibet, Lhasa alone has 700 Young Pioneers. Under the guidance of the Young Pioneer organizations, these Young Pioneers are working hard to study, actively participating in various social and political activities and productive labor, and striving to cultivate themselves into successors to the cause of communist construction.

### 四千多万少年系上红领巾

1959-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    四千多万少年系上红领巾
    新华社31日讯　据中国共产主义青年团中央委员会少年部统计，目前全国已经有四千四百万名少年先锋队员。
    1958年是各地少先队组织大发展的一年，全年共有九百万以上的少年儿童加入少先队。浙江省的少先队员增加了一倍。四川省去年有一百万少年儿童入队，目前全省共有队员四百五十万。在大多数少数民族地区，少先队组织也已普遍建立，内蒙古自治区有少先队员六十五万，新疆维吾尔自治区有少先队员二十万。在西藏，仅拉萨市就有七百名少先队员。这些少先队员在少先队组织的引导下，正在努力读书，积极参加各种社会政治活动和生产劳动，努力把自己培养成为共产主义建设的接班人。
