Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Deputy Chairman Zasyadko and Deputy Prime Minister Zheng Yilong
Author:
Date: 1959-06-18
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 17th: Premier Zhou Enlai met this afternoon with Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Zasyadko, and the four Soviet guests accompanying him. Present at the meeting were Vice Premier Bo Yibo, and Zhang Linzhi, Zhao Erlu, Ji Pengfei, and others. Soviet Embassy Counselor to China, Sudarikov, was also present.
    Xinhua News Agency, June 17th: Premier Zhou Enlai met this afternoon with Korean Deputy Prime Minister Zheng Yilong and some members of the Korean Industrial Delegation he led. Present at the meeting were Vice Premier Bo Yibo, and Xue Muqiao, Ji Pengfei, and others. Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yongho, was also present. (Attached pictures)
    Above: Premier Zhou Enlai and Deputy Chairman Zasyadko in cordial conversation.
    Right: Premier Zhou Enlai and Deputy Prime Minister Zheng Yilong shake hands warmly. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Huaxue.

### 周总理会见扎夏迪科副主席郑一龙副首相

1959-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理会见扎夏迪科副主席郑一龙副首相
    新华社17日讯　周恩来总理今天下午会见了苏联部长会议副主席扎夏迪科和随行的四位苏联贵宾。会见时在座的，有薄一波副总理和张霖之、赵尔陆、姬鹏飞等。会见时，苏联驻中国大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫也在座。
    新华社17日讯  周恩来总理今天下午会见了朝鲜内阁副首相郑一龙和由他率领的朝鲜工业参观团的部分团员。会见时在座的，有薄一波副总理和薛暮桥、姬鹏飞等。会见时，朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐也在座。（附图片）
    上：周恩来总理和扎夏迪科副主席亲切交谈。
    右：周恩来总理和郑一龙副首相亲切握手。 新华社记者 吴化学摄
