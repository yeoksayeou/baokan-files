Title: Uruguay-China Cultural Association Established
Author:
Date: 1959-06-06
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, June 4th: The Uruguay-China Cultural Association was officially established in Montevideo, the capital of Uruguay, on the evening of June 2nd. People from cultural, educational, and scientific circles in Montevideo attended the inaugural meeting. The meeting discussed the association's tasks and elected the board of directors. The Chinese journalists delegation, which is currently visiting Montevideo, attended the meeting.

### 乌中文化协会成立

1959-06-06
第5版()
专栏：

    乌中文化协会成立
    新华社4日讯　蒙得维的亚消息：乌拉圭—中国文化协会2日晚在乌拉圭首都蒙得维的亚正式成立。参加成立大会的有蒙得维的亚的文化、教育和科学界人士。会上讨论了协会的任务，并选举了理事会。正在蒙得维的亚进行访问的中国新闻工作者代表团出席了这次大会。
