Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Indonesian Professor Sumantri
Author:
Date: 1959-06-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. Vice Premier Chen Yi received Professor Iwa Kusuma Sumantri, President of the National Padjadjaran University of Indonesia, and his secretary, Professor Muhammad Chatib Basri, this afternoon.

Present at the reception was Liu Aifeng, Vice Minister of Education, and others.

### 陈毅副总理  接见印度尼西亚苏曼特里教授

1959-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理
    接见印度尼西亚苏曼特里教授
    新华社8日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了印度尼西亚国立巴查查兰大学校长伊娃·库苏马·苏曼特里教授和他的秘书穆罕默德·柴纳尔·阿比汀教授。
    接见时在座的有教育部副部长刘皑风等。
