Title: Shijingshan Steel Celebrates Completion of Three Major Projects
Author:
Date: 1959-06-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: Shijingshan Iron and Steel Company held a grand meeting on the 7th to celebrate the successful completion of three major projects: No. 3 Blast Furnace, No. 3 Coke Oven, and the Sintering Plant.

The No. 3 Blast Furnace and No. 3 Coke Oven, which went into production about two weeks ago, are operating normally. The No. 3 Blast Furnace has already produced over 7,000 tons of high-quality pig iron, and the No. 3 Coke Oven has produced over 6,800 tons of coke. On the 7th, the No. 3 Blast Furnace and No. 3 Coke Oven were decorated with colorful banners, and the entire company was filled with joy.

At the celebration meeting, Zhou Guanwu, Manager of Shijingshan Iron and Steel Company, Wang Guang, Head of the No. 3 Blast Furnace, and worker representative Wang Jinshan delivered speeches. While celebrating the successful completion of the three major projects, they expressed their determination to guard against arrogance and rashness, strive to improve technology in production, and endeavor to quickly reach the advanced level in the country. They also pledged to continue their efforts in the second batch of construction projects, which have now been fully launched, and make even greater achievements. In their speeches, they also expressed deep gratitude to the workers from all over the country and the people of the capital for their support in the construction of the three major projects.

Wan Li, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Mayor of Beijing, Yuan Baohua, Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry, Jia Tingsan, Standing Committee Member of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Minister of Industry, and Vice Minister Lu Yu, among others, attended today's meeting. Wan Li and Yuan Baohua delivered speeches at the meeting. They encouraged the workers of Shijingshan Steel to work hard to learn and improve their technical level, to muster greater enthusiasm, and to complete this year's production and capital construction tasks in a faster, better, and more economical way.

### 石钢庆祝三大工程竣工

1959-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    石钢庆祝三大工程竣工
    新华社7日讯　石景山钢铁公司7日举行三大工程——三号高炉、三号焦炉和烧结厂的胜利竣工大会。
    两周左右以前投入生产的三号高炉和三号焦炉，生产情况正常。三号高炉已生产了七千多吨优质生铁。三号焦炉生产了六千八百多吨焦炭。7日，三号高炉和三号焦炉被职工们打扮得五彩缤纷，全公司喜气洋洋。
    在庆祝大会上，石景山钢铁公司经理周冠五，三号高炉炉长王广，工人代表王近山在会上讲了话。他们在欢庆三大工程胜利竣工的同时，表示要戒骄戒躁，在生产上要努力提高技术，力争迅速达到国内的先进水平；在现在已经全面展开的第二批建设工程中，要再接再厉，作出更大的成绩。他们在讲话中，还对支援三大工程建设的全国各地职工和首都人民表示深切的感谢。
    中共北京市委书记处书记、北京市副市长万里，冶金工业部部长助理袁宝华，中共北京市委常委、工业部长贾庭三，副部长陆禹等人，参加了今天的大会。万里和袁宝华在会上讲了话。他们勉励石钢职工努力学习和提高技术水平，鼓起更大的干劲，多快好省地完成今年的生产和基本建设任务。
