Title: UAR Ambassador Ragab Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-06-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, June 6th: Ragab, the Ambassador of the United Arab Republic to China, left Beijing by train today to return to his country at the end of his term.

Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, He Gongkai, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department, and Zhang Jie, Vice President of the Chinese-UAR Friendship Association, were at the station to see him off.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China and diplomats from the UAR Embassy in China were also at the station to see him off.

### 阿联大使拉加卜回国

1959-06-07
第3版()
专栏：

    阿联大使拉加卜回国
    新华社6日讯　阿拉伯联合共和国驻华大使拉加卜离任回国，在今天乘火车离开北京。
    到车站送行的有外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文，西亚非洲司副司长何功楷和中国阿联友好协会副会长张杰。
    各国驻华使节和阿联驻华大使馆的外交官也到车站送行。
