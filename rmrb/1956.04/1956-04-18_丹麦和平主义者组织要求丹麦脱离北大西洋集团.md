Title: Danish Pacifist Organization Demands Denmark Withdraw from North Atlantic Group
Author:
Date: 1956-04-18
Page: 4

    Danish Pacifist Organization
    Demands Denmark Withdraw from North Atlantic Group
    Xinhua News Agency, April 17th, According to TASS, Copenhagen, April 16th: The pacifist organization "No More War" held its annual meeting in Copenhagen from April 14th to 15th. According to "Land og Folk," the meeting passed a resolution demanding that Denmark withdraw from the North Atlantic Group, withdraw Danish troops from Germany, and, like Iceland, demand the withdrawal of foreign troops from Danish territory.

### 丹麦和平主义者组织要求丹麦脱离北大西洋集团

1956-04-18
第4版()
专栏：

    丹麦和平主义者组织
    要求丹麦脱离北大西洋集团
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社哥本哈根十六日讯：和平主义者的组织“再也不要战争”四月十四日到十五日在哥本哈根举行年会。据“国土与人民报”报道，会上通过一项决议，要求丹麦脱离北大西洋集团，从德国撤回丹麦的军队，并且同冰岛一样要求外国军队撤出丹麦的领土。
