Title: The CPSU Central Committee Issues Slogans for Celebrating "May Day"

Author:

Date: 1956-04-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 18th, (TASS dispatch): The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union has issued slogans for celebrating the 1956 "May Day" International Labor Day.

The slogans first hail: Long live May Day, the festival of international proletarian solidarity of the working people, the festival of fraternity of workers all over the world!

The slogans say: Raise the banner of proletarian internationalism higher! Workers of all countries, unite!

A fraternal salute to all peoples fighting for peace, democracy, and socialism!

Working people of all countries! War can and must be prevented! Raise the banner against the danger of war and for international peace and cooperation higher! Strive for the reduction of armaments and armed forces! Demand the prohibition of atomic weapons, hydrogen weapons, and other weapons of mass destruction! Make atomic energy serve the peaceful progress of mankind!

May the cooperation between Communists, Socialists, and all progressive forces be increasingly consolidated, in the struggle for peace, democracy, and independence of all nations!

Workers of the Soviet Union and people's democratic countries! Tirelessly strengthen the great cooperation of socialist countries, and develop and consolidate the fraternal friendship between our peoples!

A fraternal salute to the great Chinese people who are victoriously carrying out socialist transformation in their country! Long live the People's Republic of China! May the unbreakable friendship and cooperation between the Soviet and Chinese peoples, a powerful factor for world peace, flourish!

A fraternal salute to the workers of the people's democratic countries who are waging selfless struggles for the further development of the national economy and culture, for the continuous improvement of the welfare of the people, and for peace and the building of socialism!

A fraternal salute to the heroic peoples of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam who are struggling for the restoration and further development of the national economy, for peace, and for the reunification of the country on a democratic basis!

Long live the German Democratic Republic, the bastion of all peace forces in Germany, fighting against the revival of militarism, for the rapprochement of the two Germanys, and for the reunification of the fatherland on the basis of peace and democracy! For the sake of peace in Europe, may the friendship and cooperation between the German and Soviet peoples be increasingly consolidated and developed!

A fraternal salute to the people of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia who are building socialism!

A fraternal salute to the great Indian people who are fighting for the progress of their motherland and for peace in Asia and the world!

Long live the friendship and cooperation between the peoples of the United Kingdom, the United States, France, and the Soviet Union in the struggle for easing international tension and peaceful coexistence among nations, for ensuring collective security in Europe and lasting world peace!

The slogans then wish the friendship and cooperation between the Soviet and Italian peoples, and the Austrian people to be increasingly consolidated, and wish the friendly relations between the Soviet Union and Finland, Sweden, Norway, Denmark, and Iceland to be increasingly developed.

The slogans also salute the Japanese people who are fighting against the revival of militarism, for national independence, and for the democratic development of their motherland! A warm salute to the peoples of Eastern countries who have freed themselves from the shackles of colonial oppression and are fighting for peace, freedom, and the consolidation of their national independence! A salute to the people of colonies and dependent countries who are fighting against imperialist oppression and for their freedom and national independence!

The slogans say: Long live Lenin's policy of peaceful coexistence of different social systems, the general line of Soviet foreign policy, the policy of defending and consolidating peace, and developing economic and cultural ties with all countries!

The slogans call on the soldiers of the Soviet Army and Navy to assiduously increase their military and political knowledge, improve their combat skills, and master modern combat technology and weapons!

The slogans call on the working people of the Soviet Union, the Soviet trade unions, women, youth, Communist Party members, and Communist Youth League members to unite more closely around the Communist Party and the Soviet government, to implement the historic resolutions of the 20th Congress of the CPSU, to engage in the great cause of building a communist society, to strive for the victorious realization of the Sixth Five-Year Plan, for the further development of the national economy and culture, for technological progress and increased labor productivity, and for the improvement of the welfare of the working people.

### 苏共中央发布庆祝“五一”节的口号

1956-04-20
第4版()
专栏：

    苏共中央发布庆祝“五一”节的口号
    新华社19日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科十八日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会发布了庆祝1956年“五一”国际劳动节的口号。
    口号首先欢呼：劳动人民的国际无产阶级团结的节日，全世界工人友爱的节日——“五一”节万岁！
    口号说：更高地举起无产阶级国际主义的旗帜！全世界无产者联合起来！
    向一切为和平、民主、社会主义而奋斗的人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    各国的劳动人民！战争是可以而且必须防止的！更高地举起反对战争危险、争取国际和平和合作的旗帜！争取裁减军备和武装部队！要求禁止原子武器、氢武器和其他大规模毁灭性武器！要使原子能为人类的和平进步服务！
    祝共产党人、社会党人和一切进步力量之间的合作日益巩固，为争取各国人民的和平、民主和独立而奋斗！
    苏联和人民民主国家的劳动者们！不倦地加强社会主义国家的伟大合作，发展和巩固我们各国人民之间的兄弟友谊！
    向在自己的国家内胜利地进行着社会主义改造的伟大的中国人民致兄弟的敬礼！中华人民共和国万岁！祝世界和平的强大因素——苏中两国人民的牢不可破的友谊和合作蓬勃发展！
    向在为进一步发展国民经济和文化，为不断提高人民群众的福利，为和平和建成社会主义而进行忘我斗争的人民民主国家的劳动者致兄弟的敬礼！
    向在为恢复和进一步发展国民经济、为和平和在民主的基础上统一国家而进行斗争的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和越南民主共和国的英勇的人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    为反对军国主义复活、为两个德国的接近、为在和平和民主的基础上统一祖国而斗争的全德国和平力量的堡垒——德意志民主共和国万岁！为了欧洲的和平，祝德苏两国人民的友谊和合作日益巩固和发展！
    向正在建设社会主义的南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    向为祖国的进步、为亚洲和全世界和平而斗争的伟大的印度人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    英、美、法和苏联四国人民在争取缓和国际紧张局势和各国和平共处、争取保障欧洲集体安全和世界持久和平的斗争中的友谊和合作万岁！
    口号接着祝苏联人民和意大利人民、奥地利人民之间的友谊和合作日益巩固，祝苏联和芬兰、瑞典、挪威、丹麦、冰岛之间的友好关系日益发展。
    口号又向为反对军国主义复活、争取民族独立、争取祖国民主发展而斗争的日本人民致敬！向摆脱了殖民压迫锁链，为和平、自由和巩固本国民族独立而斗争的东方各国人民致热烈的敬礼！向为反对帝国主义压迫、争取自己的自由和民族独立而斗争的殖民地和附属国的人民致敬！
    口号说：列宁的关于不同社会制度和平共处的政策，苏联外交政策的总路线、保卫和巩固和平、发展同一切国家的经济和文化联系的政策万岁！
    口号号召苏联陆海军军人刻苦地增进自己的军事和政治知识，改进作战技能，掌握现代化作战技术和武器！
    口号号召苏联的劳动人民，苏联的工会、妇女、青年、共产党员和共青团员等更加紧密地团结在共产党和苏联政府的周围，实现苏共第二十次代表大会的历史性决议，从事建成共产主义社会的伟大事业，为胜利实现第六个五年计划，为进一步发展国民经济和文化，为争取技术进步和提高劳动生产率，为增进劳动人民的福利而奋斗。
