Date: 1956-04-29
Page: 2

    Giving Play to the Role of Irrigation Projects
    The Beiguan Han River Diversion Irrigation Project, completed last year in Chao'an County, Guangdong Province, ensured irrigation for more than 68,900 mu of farmland in the surrounding area during this year's spring drought. This is a scene of members of the Second Agricultural Production Cooperative of Fengxi Township, Chao'an County, cultivating in the fields after irrigation. The canal on the right is a branch canal of the Beiguan Han River Diversion Irrigation Project. Xinhua News Agency Draft

### 发挥灌溉工程的作用（图片）

1956-04-29
第2版()
专栏：

    发挥灌溉工程的作用
    广东省潮安县去年建成的北关引韩灌溉工程，在今年春旱的情况下，保证了周围六万八千九百多亩农田灌溉。这是潮安县枫溪乡第二农业生产合作社的社员在灌溉后的田里进行耕作。右边的渠道是北关引韩灌溉工程的一条支渠。　　　　　　　　　　新华社稿
