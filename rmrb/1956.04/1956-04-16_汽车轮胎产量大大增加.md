Title: Automobile Tire Production Greatly Increased
Author:
Date: 1956-04-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch

    Automobile Tire Production Greatly Increased
    The output, variety, and specifications of automobile tires produced in our country can now basically meet the needs. According to statistics, the output of automobile tires in our country in 1955 increased more than twenty-one times compared to 1949, and the variety increased from seven to more than fifty. This year, in addition to trial-producing eight types of large heavy-duty truck tires, various rubber factories will also trial-produce sixteen new products such as tires for tractors. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 汽车轮胎产量大大增加

1956-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    汽车轮胎产量大大增加
    我国生产的汽车轮胎的产量、品种、规格等方面现在都基本上可以满足需要。据统计，我国汽车轮胎的产量1955年比1949年增加了二十一倍多，品种由七种增加到五十多种。今年各橡胶工厂除试制八种大型载重汽车轮胎以外，还要试制拖拉机使用的轮胎等新产品十六种。（据新华社讯）
