Title: Soviet Ambassador Yudin Holds Reception for Visit of Soviet Government Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1956-04-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Soviet Ambassador to China, Yudin, held a grand reception on the evening of the 7th for the visit of the Soviet Government Delegation headed by Soviet Minister Council First Deputy Chairman Mikoyan to China.

Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai were invited to attend the reception. At 7:30, they entered the hall with Mikoyan and others amidst music. At this moment, a long and warm applause resounded through the hall.

Ambassador Yudin, Vice Chairman Zhu De, and Deputy Chairman Mikoyan delivered speeches at the banquet successively.

During their speeches, the whole audience burst into storms of applause continuously.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently raised their glasses and toasted to the great close unity and friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people, and to the ever-growing strength and development of the world peace and socialist forces.

Also attending the banquet were Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, Deng Zihui, He Long, Ulanhu, Li Fuchun, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Marshal Ye Jianying, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Wang Jiaxiang, Ji Pengfei, as well as heads of various departments of state organs, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, responsible personnel of various departments of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and responsible personnel of various democratic parties and people's organizations, deputy mayors of Beijing, and people from all walks of life in Beijing.

Also attending the banquet were the ambassadors of Burma, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Romania, Albania, Hungary

### 为苏联政府代表团访问中国  苏联大使尤金举行招待会

1956-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

    为苏联政府代表团访问中国
    苏联大使尤金举行招待会
    新华社7日讯  苏联驻华大使尤金为以苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬为首的苏联政府代表团访问中国，在七日晚举行盛大招待会。
      朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理应邀出席了招待会。七时半，他们同米高扬等在乐声中进入大厅。这时候，大厅里响起了长久的热烈的掌声。
    尤金大使、朱德副主席和米高扬副主席在宴会上先后致词。
    他们在讲话的时候，全场不断响起暴风雨般的掌声。
    席间，宾主频频举杯，为中苏两国人民伟大的亲密团结和友谊，为世界和平社会主义力量的日益强大和发展而干杯。
    出席今天宴会的，有国务院副总理陈云、邓小平、邓子恢、贺龙、乌兰夫、李富春，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒、郭沫若，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真，国防委员会副主席叶剑英元帅，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，外交部副部长张闻天、王稼祥、姬鹏飞，以及国家机关各部门负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领，中共中央各部门负责工作人员和各民主党派、各人民团体负责人，北京市副市长们，北京各界人士。
    出席宴会的，还有缅甸、越南民主共和国、罗马尼亚、阿尔巴尼亚、匈牙利、芬兰、捷克斯洛伐克、巴基斯坦、南斯拉夫、挪威、阿富汗的大使，丹麦和瑞士的公使，荷兰的代办，瑞典、印度尼西亚、波兰、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、印度、德意志民主共和国、保加利亚、蒙古的临时代办，英国代办处的临时代办。
