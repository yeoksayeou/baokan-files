Title: CPPCC National Committee Standing Committee Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-04-28
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency

The Standing Committee of The Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held its twenty-first meeting on the morning of the 27th.

The meeting heard a report from Cao Guan群, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, on the situation of the National Congress of Family Members of Industrialists and Businessmen and Female Industrialists and Businessmen, and a supplementary explanation from 项叔翔, Standing Committee Member of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, on the situation of this representative meeting.

The meeting approved Jin Baoshan, Zhao Shuping, and Lin Sixin as deputy heads of the Medical and Health Group; Pan Guangdan, Gan Chunlei, and Zai Tao as deputy heads of the Ethnic Group; Shen Zijiu, Du Junhui, Wu Ping, and Guan Ruiwu as deputy heads of the Women's Group; and Li Zheng as deputy head of the Education Group, and approved Yang Xinrong's resignation from the post of deputy head of the Overseas Chinese Group. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 政协全国委员会常务委员会开会

1956-04-28
第3版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会常务委员会开会
    中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会常务委员会在二十七日上午举行第二十一次会议。
    会议听了中华全国民主妇女联合会书记处书记曹冠群关于全国工商业者家属和女工商业者代表会议情况的报告和中华全国工商业联合会常务委员项叔翔关于这个代表会议情况的补充说明。
    会议通过了以金宝善、赵树屏、林斯馨为医药卫生组副组长，潘光旦、甘春雷、载涛为民族组副组长，沈兹九、杜君慧、吴平、关瑞梧为妇女组副组长，李蒸为教育组副组长，并批准杨新容辞去华侨组副组长职务。（新华社）
