Title: Voroshilov Congratulates Tunisia on its Independence
Author:
Date: 1956-04-07
Page: 4

    Voroshilov Congratulates Tunisia on its Independence
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 5th: Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, sent a telegram to King Muhammad al-Amin of Tunisia, congratulating him on Tunisia's independence. The telegram stated: The Soviet people wish the Tunisian people every success in promoting the prosperity of an independent Tunisia, and hope that friendly relations will develop between the Soviet Union and Tunisia.
    Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, also sent a telegram to French Prime Minister Mollet, welcoming the French government's move to resolve the Tunisian issue in the spirit of peace and friendship between the French and Tunisian peoples.

### 伏罗希洛夫祝贺突尼斯独立

1956-04-07
第4版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫祝贺突尼斯独立
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科5日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫打电报给突尼斯国王穆罕默德·阿明，祝贺突尼斯独立。电报说：苏联人民祝突尼斯人民在促进独立的突尼斯繁荣事业中获得各种成就，并且希望苏联和突尼斯之间将开展友好关系。
    苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁也打电报给法国总理摩勒，表示欢迎法国政府本着和平和法突两国人民友好精神解决突尼斯问题的举动。
