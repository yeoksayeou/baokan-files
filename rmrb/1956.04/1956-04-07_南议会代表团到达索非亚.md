Title: South Parliament Delegation Arrives in Sofia
Author:
Date: 1956-04-07
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch,

    South Parliament Delegation Arrives in Sofia

    According to Xinhua News Agency, the Yugoslav Parliament delegation, headed by the Chairman of the Federal People's Assembly, Pijade, arrived in Sofia on the afternoon of the 5th at the invitation of the Bulgarian National Assembly.

    The Yugoslav Parliament delegation will stay in Sofia for a few days and then visit several important economic and cultural centers throughout Bulgaria.

### 南议会代表团到达索非亚

1956-04-07
第4版()
专栏：

    南议会代表团到达索非亚
    据新华社讯　以联邦国民议会主席皮雅杰为首的南斯拉夫议会代表团，应保加利亚国民议会的邀请，在五日下午到达索非亚。
    南斯拉夫议会代表团将在索非亚逗留几天，然后去保加利亚各地访问几个重要的经济和文化中心。
