Title: West German Government Uneasy About Mollet's Remarks
Author:
Date: 1956-04-07
Page: 4

    West German Government Uneasy About Mollet's Remarks

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 6th. From Bonn: French Prime Minister Mollet's interview with a reporter from "U.S. News & World Report" has caused great unease to the West German government. The West German Foreign Ministry issued a statement on April 5th, saying that the West German government "strongly disagrees" with Mollet's proposition of dealing with disarmament before resolving European security and German unification issues.

    It is reported that the West German Ambassador to France, Maltzan, already visited Mollet on the 5th, requesting Mollet to "clarify" his remarks.

### 西德政府对摩勒谈话感到不安

1956-04-07
第4版()
专栏：

    西德政府对摩勒谈话感到不安
    据新华社柏林6日电　波恩消息：法国总理摩勒对“美国新闻与世界报道”记者的谈话引起了西德政府极大的不安。西德外交部在四月五日发表声明说，西德政府“坚决不同意”摩勒的关于在解决欧洲安全和德国统一问题前先处理裁军问题的主张。
    据报道，西德政府驻法国大使马尔藏已经在五日访问摩勒，要求摩勒对他的谈话加以“澄清”。
