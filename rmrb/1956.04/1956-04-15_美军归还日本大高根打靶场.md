Title: US Military Returns Otakane Firing Range to Japan
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo: According to an announcement on April 13 by the Procurement Agency of the Japanese government, the U.S. military will return the entire Otakane firing range in Yamagata Prefecture, Japan, to Japan. The official date of the return will be decided by the Japan-U.S. Joint Committee.

### 美军归还日本大高根打靶场

1956-04-15
第4版()
专栏：

    美军归还日本大高根打靶场
    据新华社讯　东京消息：据日本政府调达厅四月十三日宣布，美国驻军在日本山形县大高根的打靶场将全部归还给日本。正式归还的日期将由日美联合委员会决定。
