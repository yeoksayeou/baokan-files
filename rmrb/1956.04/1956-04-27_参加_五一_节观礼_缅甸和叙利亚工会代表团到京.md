Title: Burmese and Syrian Trade Union Delegations Arrive in Beijing to Attend "May Day" Observances
Author:
Date: 1956-04-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 26th -- At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Trade Union Congress (Burma) delegation headed by U Than Tun and the Syrian Trade Union delegation headed by Subhi Jattiyeh, who are visiting China to attend the "May Day" celebrations, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

### 参加“五一”节观礼  缅甸和叙利亚工会代表团到京

1956-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

    参加“五一”节观礼
    缅甸和叙利亚工会代表团到京
    据新华社26日讯  应中华全国总工会邀请来中国参加“五一”节观礼的以吴丹吞为首的工会大会（缅甸）代表团和以索比·加蒂贝为首的叙利亚工会代表团，今天乘飞机到达北京。
