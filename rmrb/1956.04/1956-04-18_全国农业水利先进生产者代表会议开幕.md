Title: National Conference of Advanced Agricultural Water Conservancy Producers Opens
Author:
Date: 1956-04-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 17th: The National Conference of Advanced Agricultural Water Conservancy Producers opened in Beijing today.

Attending today's meeting were 585 representatives of advanced producers, advanced workers, and advanced units selected from state-owned mechanical farms, ranches, agricultural machinery and tractor stations, water conservancy construction, and surveying and design departments.

He Yingcai, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Agricultural Water Conservancy Trade Union, delivered the opening speech. He pointed out that the main tasks of this conference are to summarize, exchange, and promote advanced experience, further organize the vast number of workers in state-owned agricultural enterprises and water conservancy departments to carry out the advanced producer movement, push the socialist competition to a new climax, and better improve production, improve technology and labor productivity, and ensure the early and overfulfillment of the First Five-Year Plan.

Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan and Vice Minister of Water Conservancy Zhou Junming made reports at the meeting. Zhang Xiuzhu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Zhang Shuguang, representative of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, spoke at the meeting, congratulating the advanced producers present.

Soviet Socialist Labor Hero, tractor team leader of the agricultural machinery and tractor station, M.A. Aksyonov, who was invited to participate in the meeting, will introduce the advanced experience of the Soviet Union in agricultural mechanization to the meeting.

### 全国农业水利先进生产者代表会议开幕

1956-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    全国农业水利先进生产者代表会议开幕
    据新华社17日讯　全国农业水利先进生产者代表会议今天在北京开幕。
    出席今天会议的有从国营机械农场、牧场、农业机器拖拉机站、水利建设的施工和勘察设计等部门评选出来的先进生产者、先进工作者和先进单位的代表五百八十五人。
    中国农业水利工会筹备委员会主任何英才向大会致开幕词。他指出，这次会议的主要任务，是总结、交流和推广先进经验，进一步组织国营农业企业和水利部门的广大职工开展先进生产者运动，把社会主义竞赛推向新的高潮，更好地提高生产，提高技术和劳动生产率，保证提前和超额完成第一个五年计划。
    农业部长廖鲁言、水利部副部长周骏鸣都在会上作了报告。中华全国总工会书记处书记张修竹、青年团中央委员会代表张曙光都在会上讲了话，向到会的先进生产者表示祝贺。
    应邀参加会议的苏联社会主义劳动英雄、农业机器拖拉机站的拖拉机队队长米·阿·阿克谢诺夫，将向会议介绍苏联农业机耕方面的先进经验。
