Title: Head of Yugoslav Parliamentary Delegation Issues Statement: Inter-Parliamentary Union Council's Decision to Not Admit China is Illegal
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-10
Page: 4
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, April 9th, Dubrovnik dispatch: The Head of the Yugoslav Parliamentary Delegation, Mr. Simic, stated to the press on the 8th that the Inter-Parliamentary Union Council's decision to postpone the admission of the People's Republic of China is illegal and legally invalid. He emphasized that the Inter-Parliamentary Union should be universal and pointed out that the People's Republic of China meets the conditions for membership stipulated in the Inter-Parliamentary Union's charter.
    The Yugoslav News Agency stated that the Yugoslav delegation reserves the right to raise the issue of admitting China again at the Inter-Parliamentary Union Bangkok Conference in November of this year.

### 南议会代表团主席发表声明说  各国议会联盟理事会不接纳中国的决定是不合法的

1956-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

    南议会代表团主席发表声明说
    各国议会联盟理事会不接纳中国的决定是不合法的
    新华社9日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社杜布罗夫尼克消息：南斯拉夫议会代表团主席西米奇在八日向报界发表声明说，各国议会联盟理事会延期接纳中华人民共和国的决定是不合法的，而且在法律上是无效的。他强调各国议会联盟应该具有普遍性，并且指出中华人民共和国具备各国议会联盟章程所规定的接纳会员的条件。
    南斯拉夫通讯社说，南斯拉夫代表团保留权利，要在今年十一月各国议会联盟曼谷大会上再次提出接纳中国的问题。
