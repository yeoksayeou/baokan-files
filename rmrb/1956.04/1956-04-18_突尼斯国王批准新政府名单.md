Title: Tunisian King Approves New Government List
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tunis - It was reported that the King of Tunisia approved on the 14th the list of ministers of the new Tunisian government submitted by Prime Minister Bourguiba. The new government has fifteen ministers, eight of whom are from Bourguiba's New Constitutional Party, four belong to the Tunisian Workers' Union, and two belong to the Shopkeepers' Alliance. Bourguiba concurrently serves as Foreign Minister and Defense Minister.

### 突尼斯国王批准新政府名单

1956-04-18
第4版()
专栏：

    突尼斯国王批准新政府名单
    据新华社讯　突尼斯消息：突尼斯国王十四日批准了首相布尔吉巴提出的突尼斯新政府大臣的名单。新政府有十五个大臣，其中八个大臣是布尔吉巴的新宪政党人，四个属于突尼斯工人工会，两个属于店主联盟。布尔吉巴兼任外交大臣和国防大臣。
