Title: Ten Brother Newspapers Publish "On the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat"
Author:
Date: 1956-04-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Korean "Nodong Sinmun," "Minju Choson," "Korean People's Army Daily," the Vietnamese "Nhan Dan," the Polish "People's Tribune," the Hungarian "Szabad Nép" ("Free People"), "Népszava" ("People's Voice"), "Magyar Nemzet" ("Hungarian Nation"), and the Albanian "Zëri i Popullit" ("Voice of the People") all published the article "On the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat," written by the editorial department of the Beijing "People's Daily," on April 8th and 7th respectively. The "Neues Deutschland" ("New Germany") published an abstract of "On the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat" on April 6th, under the title "The Chinese Communist Party and the Soviet Communist Party are United."

### 十种兄弟报纸刊登“关于无产阶级专政的历史经验”

1956-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    十种兄弟报纸刊登“关于无产阶级专政的历史经验”
    据新华社讯　朝鲜“劳动新闻”、“民主朝鲜报”、“朝鲜人民军报”，越南“人民报”，波兰“人民论坛报”，匈牙利“自由人民报”、“人民之声报”、“匈牙利民族报”和阿尔巴尼亚“人民之声报”，分别在四月八日和七日刊登了北京“人民日报”编辑部写的“关于无产阶级专政的历史经验”的文章。“新德意志报”四月六日刊登了“关于无产阶级专政的历史经验”一文的摘要，标题是“中国共产党和苏联共产党团结一致”。
