Title: Chinese Chargé d'Affaires in Burma Holds Reception to Celebrate Opening of China-Burma Air Route
Author:
Date: 1956-04-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 12th. Cheng Zhiping, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, held a reception at the Chinese Embassy on the evening of April 11 to celebrate the direct opening of the China-Burma air route.

Those attending the reception included U Tun Maung, Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of Burma, U Hla Maung, Burmese Ambassador to China, Ba Swe, Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, U Shwe Gyi, Secretary of the Ministry of Transport and Communications, Bo Setkya, Member of the Advisory Committee of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, U Chit, Chairman of the Management Committee of Union of Burma Airways, and U Toe, General Manager of Union of Burma Airways, and more than 200 others. (Attached photo)

The opening ceremony of the China-Burma air route held at Kunming Airport on April 11. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao

### 中国驻缅甸临时代办举行招待会庆祝中缅开航

1956-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中国驻缅甸临时代办举行招待会庆祝中缅开航
    据新华社仰光12日电　中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办程之平四月十一日晚间在中国大使馆举行招待会，庆祝中缅航空线直接开航。
    出席招待会的有缅甸最高法院首席法官吴登貌、缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂、外交部常务秘书巴林顿、交通和运输部秘书吴瑞咯、外交部谘询委员会委员波塞恰、缅甸联邦航空公司管理委员会主席吴七和缅甸联邦航空公司总经理吴铎等二百多人。（附图片）
    四月十一日在昆明机场上举行的
    中缅航空线开航典礼。　新华社记者　刘东鳌摄
