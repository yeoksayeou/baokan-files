Title: Japanese Diet Members' League for Promoting Sino-Japanese Trade Decides to Extend Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-04-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 24th: The Japanese Diet Members' League for Promoting Sino-Japanese Trade held a meeting of its executive board in Tokyo on the 24th, formally deciding to extend the Third Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement, which is due to expire in May of this year, for one year. This decision will be communicated to the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade for consultation. This decision was reached after repeated consultations in April between the Japanese Diet Members' League for Promoting Sino-Japanese Trade, the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, and the Japan-China Import and Export Association.

The board also decided to continue to work hard to resolve the various obstacles and difficulties that have not been resolved in the year since the Third Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement was signed last May. Furthermore, it will discuss with the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade the issue of expanding the volume of trade between China and Japan.

### 日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟  决定延长日中贸易协定期限

1956-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

    日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟
    决定延长日中贸易协定期限
    新华社24日讯  东京消息：日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟二十四日在东京召开常务理事会，正式决定要把将在今年五月满期的第三次日中贸易协定延长一年；并且把这个决定通知中国国际贸易促进委员会，以便进行协商。这个决定是经过日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟、日本国际贸易促进协会和日中输出入协会，在四月间多次洽商后产生出来的。
    理事会还决定，继续努力解决在第三次日中贸易协定从去年五月签订以来这一年中未能解决的种种障碍和困难。并且就扩大中日两国之间的贸易数量问题，同中国国际贸易促进委员会进行商谈。
