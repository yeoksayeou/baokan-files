Title: Secretary-General of the Indonesian Labour Party Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 26th: Mr. Asrarudin, Secretary-General of the Indonesian Labour Party, and his wife, arrived in Beijing on the 26th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 印度尼西亚工党秘书长到京

1956-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚工党秘书长到京
    据新华社26日讯  印度尼西亚工党秘书长阿斯拉鲁丁及夫人，应中国人民外交学会邀请，在二十六日到达北京。
