Title: The Dalai Lama's Speech
Author:
Date: 1956-04-18
Page: 1

Dalai Lama's Speech

Vice Premier Chen, all comrades of the Central Delegation:

On the occasion of the imminent establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Central People's Government, and Chairman Mao have sent a Central Delegation composed of representatives of fraternal ethnic groups from various regions. This is a great honor and concern for the Tibetan people, and I express my heartfelt gratitude and respect for this.

Vice Premier Chen, leading the Central Delegation, has traveled tirelessly over thousands of miles and arrived safely and smoothly in Lhasa today. I express my warm welcome to your arrival with boundless excitement.

Because of your arrival, the successful convening of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, the close relationship between the motherland and Tibet, the strengthening of the unity among all fraternal ethnic groups and within Tibet, and the future construction of Tibet in all aspects will receive intimate guidance and assistance. Therefore, let me once again express my sincere gratitude to you.

Finally, I wish you good health.

Long live the great unity of the people of all ethnic groups in China!

Long live the People's Republic of China!

Long live the Communist Party of China!

Long live our beloved great leader Chairman Mao!

### 达赖喇嘛的讲话

1956-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛的讲话陈副总理、中央代表团的全体同志们：
    值此西藏自治区筹备委员会行将成立之际，中国共产党中央委员会、中央人民政府和毛主席派来了各地区兄弟民族代表参加的中央代表团，这是给予西藏人民的极大荣誉和关怀，我对此表示衷心的感谢和敬意。
    陈副总理领导的中央代表团，不辞辛苦，万里跋涉，今天平安的顺利的到达了拉萨，让我以无限兴奋的心情对你们的来临表示热烈欢迎。
    由于你们的来临，对我们开好西藏自治区筹备委员会，密切祖国与西藏地方的关系，加强各兄弟民族的团结和西藏内部的团结，以及对西藏今后各方面的建设都将得到亲切的指导和帮助。因此，让我再一次向你们表示由衷的感谢。
    最后，敬祝你们身体健康。
    中国各族人民大团结万岁！
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    中国共产党万岁！
    敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席万岁！
