Title: Supply of Sodium Bicarbonate for Farmers to Soak Seeds
Author:
Date: 1956-04-16
Page: 2

The Shanghai Chemical Industrial Raw Materials Company recently supplied 75 tons of sodium bicarbonate to farmers in the Songjiang Special District of Jiangsu Province for soaking seeds during spring plowing.

In the past, farmers in the Songjiang Special District generally used salt water to soak seeds. Now, they have adopted the advanced Soviet experience of using sodium bicarbonate to soak seeds. Soaking seeds with sodium bicarbonate can make rice seeds germinate earlier, enhance their cold resistance, and promote strong plant development. It is estimated that using sodium bicarbonate to soak seeds can increase yield by 6%.
(Our correspondent in Shanghai)

### 供应农民浸种使用的小苏打

1956-04-16
第2版()
专栏：

    供应农民浸种使用的小苏打
    上海市化学工业原料公司最近以七十五吨小苏打，供应江苏省松江专区农民在春耕生产中浸种时使用。
    松江专区农民过去一般都用盐水浸种，现在纷纷采用了苏联用小苏打浸种的先进经验。用小苏打浸种，可以使稻种提前发芽，增强稻种的抗寒能力，促使稻棵发育强壮。据估计，用小苏打浸种，可以增加产量6％。
              （本报驻上海记者）
