Title: Soviet Union Establishes Institute of World Economy and International Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-04-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 23rd: According to TASS, the Soviet Academy of Sciences has established a new institute—the Institute of World Economy and International Relations. Its task is to study the laws of development of modern capitalism, the economies and politics of various countries, the interrelationships between the two world economic systems, the new role of Asian countries in world economy and politics, and other issues. This institute will publish a monthly magazine, "World Economy and International Relations."

### 苏联成立世界经济和国际关系研究所

1956-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联成立世界经济和国际关系研究所
    新华社24日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科二十三日讯：苏联科学院成立了一个新的研究所——世界经济和国际关系研究所。它的任务是研究现代资本主义发展的规律，各国的经济和政治，两种世界经济体系的相互关系，亚洲国家在世界经济和政治中的新作用等问题。这个研究所将出版“世界经济和国际关系”月刊。
