Author: Shen Tongheng
Date: 1956-04-25
Page: 2

Ancient Poem Explained
    Shen Tongheng
    Some factories produce daily-use industrial products of very poor quality.

    Spring slumber unaware of dawn,
    Everywhere one hears the singing of birds.
    Last night the sound of wind and rain,
    Who knows how many flowers have fallen.

    古诗今释
