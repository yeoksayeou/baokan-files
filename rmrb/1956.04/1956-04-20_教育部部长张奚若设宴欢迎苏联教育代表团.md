Title: Education Minister Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet to Welcome Soviet Education Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 19th: Minister of Education Zhang Xiruo hosted a banquet at the Beijing Hotel tonight to welcome the Soviet Education Delegation, which is visiting China at his invitation.

　　Zhang Xiruo, the head of the Soviet Education Delegation and Deputy Director of the Academy of Educational Sciences of the Russian Federation, Markushevich, and the Deputy Mayor of Beijing, Wu Han, spoke at the banquet.

　　The Soviet Education Delegation is composed of educational science research experts, heads of educational administrative departments, teaching equipment supply experts, and principals and teachers of general education schools and normal schools. The delegation arrived in Beijing by plane on the 18th.

### 教育部部长张奚若设宴欢迎苏联教育代表团

1956-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    教育部部长张奚若设宴欢迎苏联教育代表团
    据新华社19日讯　教育部部长张奚若今天晚上在北京饭店设宴招待应邀来中国访问的苏联教育代表团。
    张奚若和苏联教育代表团团长、俄罗斯联邦共和国教育科学院副院长马尔库舍维奇，以及北京市副市长吴晗等都在宴会上讲了话。
    苏联教育代表团由教育科学研究专家、教育行政部门负责人、教学仪器供应专家以及普通教育学校和师范学校校长、教师等人组成。代表团是在十八日乘飞机到北京的。
