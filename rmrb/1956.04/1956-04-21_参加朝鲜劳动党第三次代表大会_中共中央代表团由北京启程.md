Title: CCP Central Committee Delegation Departs from Beijing to Attend the Third Congress of the Workers' Party of Korea
Author:
Date: 1956-04-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 20th. At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China dispatched a delegation consisting of Nie Rongzhen, member of the CCP Central Committee, Wang Congwu, alternate member of the CCP Central Committee, and Qiao Xiaoguang, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of China to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (already in Korea), to attend the upcoming Third Congress of the Workers' Party of Korea. The delegation members left Beijing by train on the morning of the 20th.

Those who went to the station to see them off included Wang Jiaxiang, Yang Shangkun, Liu Xiwu, Xiao Xiangrong, and others.

Kim Gui-nam, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, and embassy staff also went to the station to see them off.

### 参加朝鲜劳动党第三次代表大会  中共中央代表团由北京启程

1956-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    参加朝鲜劳动党第三次代表大会
    中共中央代表团由北京启程
    新华社20日讯  中国共产党中央委员会应朝鲜劳动党中央委员会的邀请，派遣由中共中央委员聂荣臻、中共中央候补委员王从吾和中国驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国特命全权大使乔晓光（已在朝鲜）组成的代表团，前往参加即将举行的朝鲜劳动党第三次代表大会。代表团人员已在二十日上午乘火车离开北京。
    前往车站送行的有王稼祥、杨尚昆、刘锡五、萧向荣等人。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办金贵南和使馆人员也到车站送行。
