Title: Drama Workers' Garden Party (Photo)
Author: Wang Zhiyuan
Date: 1956-04-07
Page: 3

Drama Workers' Garden Party
    After the conclusion of the First National Drama Festival, drama workers from all over the country held a garden party in the Summer Palace. This photo shows the actresses from the South China Drama Troupe, the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Drama Troupe, and the Jilin Province Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture Drama Troupe participating in the garden party. (Our newspaper reporter Wang Zhiyuan)

### 戏剧工作者游园联欢（图片）
王志渊
1956-04-07
第3版()
专栏：

    戏剧工作者游园联欢
    第一届全国话剧观摩演出会演出结束，来自祖国各地的戏剧工作者在颐和园举行了游园联欢。这是参加游园的华南话剧团、新疆维吾尔自治区话剧团和吉林省延边朝鲜族自治州话剧团的女演员们。　本报　王志渊摄
