Title: Establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1956-04-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, April 22nd. The Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region was officially established today.

Today, people from all walks of life in Lhasa were excited by this great joyous event. Many families hung up national flags and painted auspicious patterns with white powder, and many people lit pine smoke on the banks of the Lhasa River.

The inaugural meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region was held in the newly built auditorium in front of the Potala Palace. In the center of the rostrum was the national flag and a portrait of Chairman Mao, with portraits of the Dalai Lama and Bainqen Erdini on either side of Chairman Mao's portrait. On both sides of the venue were inscriptions by Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai. Chairman Mao's inscription: "Congratulations on the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region!"; Vice Chairman Zhu's inscription: "Promote the spirit of patriotism and consolidate national defense!"; Chairman Liu's inscription: "Strengthen the unity within the Tibetan people. Strengthen the unity between all ethnic groups in China. Train cadres and strive to build a prosperous and happy Tibet Autonomous Region!"; Premier Zhou's inscription: "Work hard to strengthen unity, train cadres, develop economic and cultural construction, and realize regional autonomy for the Tibetan people."

The members of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, Living Buddhas and Khenpos from the three major monasteries of Lhasa and Tashilhunpo Monastery, representatives from all regions, strata, and sects in Tibet, and observers, as well as representatives and staff of the People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Tibet, arrived here one after another in the morning to participate in this unprecedented gathering of great unity in Tibetan history. A total of one thousand and eighty-three people attended the meeting.

At 1:00 PM, the Dalai Lama announced the opening of the inaugural meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region and delivered an opening speech.

Then, Vice Premier Chen Yi read out the State Council's order and, on behalf of the State Council, presented the seal of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region to the Dalai Lama. At this moment, the whole audience stood up, and the band played the song of praise to the motherland.

After the presentation of the seal, Vice Premier Chen Yi delivered a speech. At this time, thousands of lamas, citizens, and farmers and herdsmen gathered outside the venue in front of the loudspeakers, listening to the live broadcast of the meeting.

Deputy Head of the Central Delegation Zhang Jingwu and Wang Feng also spoke at today's meeting.

When the congratulatory messages from Chairman Mao, Chairman Liu, and Premier Zhou were read out at the meeting, the whole audience stood up, and stormy applause lasted for a long time.

The Central Delegation presented gifts to the meeting, including a porcelain statue of Chairman Mao, palace lanterns, flower pots, and various fruit tree seedlings, various tree seeds, flower seeds, etc.

The Dalai Lama, Bainqen Erdini, and Zhang Guohua also accepted congratulatory banners presented to the inaugural meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region by Chairman Mao Zedong, the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress, the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China, many people's organizations, and the people's committees of provinces and cities, as well as many ethnic autonomous regions and autonomous prefectures. When the Tibetan representatives from various Tibetan autonomous prefectures presented their congratulatory banners, they were also blessed by the Dalai Lama and Bainqen Erdini.

The Tibet Working Committee of the CPC, the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Tibetan local government, the Bainqen Kanbu Conference Hall Committee, the People's Liberation Committee of the Qamdo Region, many temples in Tibet, and mass organizations and institutions in Lhasa, Xigaze, and other places all presented congratulatory banners to the meeting.

Today, the people of Lhasa warmly celebrated their joyous day. Groups of people dressed in festive spring clothes, carrying tableware, went to the Linka to drink and sing. In Lhasa's Jokhang Temple and Ramoche Temple, thousands upon thousands of Buddha lamps were lit today, and more than a thousand lamas from the three major monasteries of Lhasa recited auspicious prayers in these temples, celebrating the great joyous day of the Tibetan people.

### 西藏自治区筹备委员会成立

1956-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    西藏自治区筹备委员会成立
    据新华社拉萨22日电  西藏自治区筹备委员会今天宣告正式成立。
    今天，整个拉萨市各阶层人民都被这件大喜事所激动。许多人家挂上了国旗和用白粉画了吉祥图案，许多人在拉萨河岸燃起了松烟。
    西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会在布达拉宫前面的新建大礼堂内举行。会场的主席台正中高挂着国旗和毛主席像，毛主席像的两旁是达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼的画像。会场两旁挂着毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理的题词。毛主席的题词：“祝贺西藏自治区筹备委员会的成立！”，朱副主席的题词：“发扬爱国主义精神，巩固国防！”，刘委员长的题词：“加强藏族内部的团结。加强中国各民族之间的团结。培养干部，为建设繁荣幸福的西藏自治区而奋斗！”，周总理的题词：“为加强团结，培养干部，发展经济文化建设事业，实现西藏民族的区域自治而努力。”
    西藏自治区筹备委员会的委员、拉萨三大寺和扎什伦布寺的活佛、堪布，来自西藏各地区、各阶层、各教派的代表和旁听人员，以及在西藏的人民解放军部队代表和工作人员，上午就陆续来到这里参加这个西藏历史上空前大团结的聚会。参加大会的共有一千零八十三人。
    下午一时，达赖喇嘛宣布西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会开幕，并且致开幕词。
    接着，陈毅副总理宣读国务院命令，并且代表国务院把西藏自治区筹备委员会的印鉴授予达赖喇嘛，这时候，全场起立，乐队奏起了歌唱祖国的歌曲。
    授印后，陈毅副总理讲了话。这时候，几千名喇嘛、市民和农牧民围在会场外面的扩音器前，收听大会的实况广播。
    中央代表团副团长张经武和汪锋也在今天的大会上讲了话。
    大会在宣读毛主席、刘委员长和周总理的贺电时，全场起立，暴风雨般的掌声经久不息。
    中央代表团向大会赠送了礼品。礼品中有毛主席瓷像一面，宫灯、花盆和各种果树苗、各种树籽、花籽等。
    达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼和张国华还接受了毛泽东主席、全国人民代表大会常务委员会、全国人民代表大会民族委员会、中华人民共和国民族事务委员会、许多人民团体和省、市人民委员会以及许多民族自治区、自治州赠给西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会的贺幛。各藏族自治州的藏族代表在赠贺幛时，还受到了达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼的摸顶。
    中共西藏工作委员会、中国人民解放军西藏军区、西藏地方政府、班禅堪布会议厅委员会、昌都地区人民解放委员会、西藏的许多寺庙和拉萨、日喀则等地的群众团体、机关都向大会献了贺幛。
    今天，拉萨市人民热烈地欢庆自己的喜日。成群结队的人群穿起节日的春装，提着餐具到林卡里饮酒高歌。在拉萨的大昭寺和小昭寺里，今天点起成千上万的佛灯，拉萨三大寺的一千多名喇嘛在这些寺院念了吉祥的祈祷经文，庆祝西藏人民的大喜日。
