Title: Nasser Says Belief that the Soviet Union Will Not Invade Is One of the Fundamental Principles of Egyptian Foreign Policy
Author:
Date: 1956-04-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th, London dispatch: Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser told a reporter from the British "Daily Telegraph" that suspicion of British imperialism and the conviction that the Soviet Union would not commit aggression are two fundamental principles of Egyptian foreign policy. The "Daily Telegraph" published Nasser's conversation on April 3rd.

Nasser accused the British government of encouraging Sudanese people to oppose Egypt's proposal to share the waters of the Nile. He believes that the threat of imperialist monopoly is the real threat. He said that the relationship between Egypt and socialist countries is purely commercial.

### 纳赛尔说确信苏联不会侵略是埃及外交政策根本原则之一

1956-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔说确信苏联不会侵略是埃及外交政策根本原则之一
    新华社4日讯　伦敦消息：埃及总理纳赛尔对英国“每日电讯报”的记者说，对英国帝国主义心存疑虑和确信苏联不会进行侵略是埃及外交政策的两个根本原则。“每日电讯报”四月三日刊登了纳赛尔的谈话。
    纳赛尔指责英国政府怂恿苏丹人反对埃及共用尼罗河水的建议。他认为，帝国主义独霸的威胁是真正的威胁。他说，埃及和社会主义阵营国家的关系纯粹是商业性质的。
