Title: Parisian Police Arrest Algerians
Author:
Date: 1956-04-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 1st. French police conducted a large-scale search of Algerians residing in France in various districts of Paris on March 31st. According to police authorities, a total of approximately three thousand Algerians were arrested in one day.
    The report stated that the French police authorities conducted this search in order to disrupt a planned demonstration by Algerians demanding recognition of the Algerian people's aspirations.
    As a prelude to this planned large-scale demonstration, on March 30th, approximately one thousand Algerian workers in Longwy, eastern France, went on strike. The police suppressed the strikers and arrested one hundred and fifty people.

### 巴黎警察逮捕阿尔及利亚人

1956-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    巴黎警察逮捕阿尔及利亚人
    新华社1日讯　巴黎消息：法国警察三月三十一日在巴黎各区大举搜查住在法国的阿尔及利亚人。据警察当局宣布，一天内总共有三千左右阿尔及利亚人被逮捕。
    消息说，法国警察当局进行这次搜捕，是为了破坏阿尔及利亚人预定举行的要求承认阿尔及利亚民族愿望的示威。
    作为这次预定的大示威的前奏，三月三十日，在法国东部的隆威有一千左右阿尔及利亚工人举行罢工。警察对罢工者进行镇压，逮捕了一百五十人。
