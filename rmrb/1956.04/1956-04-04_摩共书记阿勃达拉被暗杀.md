Title: Moroccan Communist Party Secretary Abdallah Assassinated
Author:
Date: 1956-04-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 2nd (TASS dispatch): According to "l'Humanité", Abd el-Krim ben Abdallah, Secretary of the Moroccan Communist Party, was assassinated in Casablanca on March 31st. "l'Humanité" stated that the assassination was carried out by terrorist gangs directed by colonialists. The assassination of Abd el-Krim ben Abdallah has aroused great anger among the Moroccan democratic public.

### 摩共书记阿勃达拉被暗杀

1956-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    摩共书记阿勃达拉被暗杀
    新华社3日讯　塔斯社巴黎二日讯：据“人道报”报道，三月三十一日，摩洛哥共产党书记阿勃德·埃尔·克里姆·本·阿勃达拉在卡萨布兰卡被暗杀。“人道报”说，这次暗杀是殖民主义者指挥下的恐怖分子匪帮干的事。暗杀阿勃德·埃尔·克里姆·本·阿勃达拉的行为引起了摩洛哥民主公众极大的愤怒。
