Title: Friendly Exchanges on the Eve of "May Day": Trade Union Representatives from Ceylon and Other Countries Arrive in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 4

Friendly Exchanges on the Eve of "May Day"
	Trade Union Representatives from Ceylon and Other Countries Arrive in Beijing
	At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the "May Day" celebrations in China, the Ceylon Trade Union delegation headed by Samarawickrama and Ceylon Democratic Workers Congress representative Subiah, the Japanese Workers' delegation headed by Jiro Konno, the Japanese newspaper delegation headed by Toshio Sakurai, the Moroccan Labor Federation delegation headed by Kizzioua, the United Workers' Union of Chile delegation headed by Castillo, the British grassroots trade union delegation headed by Goode, and the first group of three representatives from the Indian Trade Union delegation, the first group of two representatives from the Pakistan Trade Union delegation, one representative from the Australian Trade Union, one representative from the Syrian Trade Union, and one representative from the Mexican Trade Union arrived in Beijing by train or plane on the 29th.

### “五一”前夕的友好往来  锡兰等国工会代表到京

1956-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    “五一”前夕的友好往来
    锡兰等国工会代表到京
    应中华全国总工会邀请来中国参加“五一”节观礼的以萨马拉韦克拉马为首的锡兰工会代表团和锡兰民主工人大会代表苏比亚，以今野治郎为首的日本工人代表团，以樱井俊夫为首的日本机关报代表团，以基齐渥阿为首的摩洛哥劳工联合会代表团，以卡斯蒂略为首的智利工人统一工会代表团，以古德为首的英国基层工会代表团和印度工会代表团首批代表三人，巴基斯坦工会代表团首批代表二人，澳大利亚工会代表一人，叙利亚工会代表一人，墨西哥工会代表一人，在二十九日分别乘火车或飞机到达北京。
