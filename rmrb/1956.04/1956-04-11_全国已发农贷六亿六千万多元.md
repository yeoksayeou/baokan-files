Title: National Agricultural Loans Issued Total Over 660 Million Yuan
Author:
Date: 1956-04-11
Page: 2

National agricultural loans issued total over 660 million yuan

Xinhua News Agency: As of March 20th, various agricultural loans totaling 666.88 million yuan had been issued nationwide, accounting for 33.36% of this year's new agricultural loan plan. Among these, agricultural capital construction loans and general production expense loans issued to agricultural production cooperatives and collective farms amounted to 266.55 million yuan, poverty alleviation cooperative fund loans amounted to 346.57 million yuan, and loans to state-owned farms, rural credit cooperatives, mutual aid groups, and individual farmers amounted to 53.76 million yuan.

### 全国已发农贷六亿六千万多元

1956-04-11
第2版()
专栏：

    全国已发农贷六亿六千万多元
    全国各地到三月二十日为止，共已发出各种农业贷款66，688万元，占今年农贷新投放计划的33.36％。其中对农业生产合作社、集体农庄发放的农业基本建设贷款和一般生产费用贷款有26，655万元，贫农合作基金贷款34，657万元，对国营农场、农村信用合作社、互助组和个体农民等的贷款有5，376万元。　（新华社）
