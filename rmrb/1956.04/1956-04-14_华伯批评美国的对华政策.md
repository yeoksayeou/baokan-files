Title: Warburg Criticizes U.S. China Policy
Author:
Date: 1956-04-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 13th, TASS, New York, April 12th: "The Denver Post" published a commentary by the well-known banker and writer James Warburg, criticizing the U.S. policy towards China.

	Warburg said: First, the Beijing government, "whether we like it or not, has already firmly established itself as the government of a country with nearly a quarter of the world's population."
	Second, "there is no way to solve the problems of Korea, Indochina, or Taiwan except through war or negotiation with Beijing."
	Third, "we cannot negotiate with a government whose existence we refuse to recognize."
	Warburg said: "The American people want neither war nor to live forever on the 'brink of war'. They want peace—real peace, not an unreliable, terrible equilibrium—if real peace can be achieved honorably. This being the case, the American people will hope that their government will take the only path that can lead to real peace, if they have the opportunity to understand the facts rather than being incited to take an uncompromising attitude..."
	Warburg emphasized: "The ostrich-like refusal to face the unpleasant, but unchangeable facts of the Far East will not only certainly place the United States on the brink of war with China, but will also quickly lead to the complete isolation of the United States. Our China policy is rapidly alienating us from the people of countries that have not joined either side and from most of our allies, with the exception of Chiang Kai-shek and Syngman Rhee, both of whom openly hope to drag the United States into war."

### 华伯批评美国的对华政策

1956-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    华伯批评美国的对华政策
    新华社13日讯　塔斯社纽约12日讯：“丹佛邮报”登载了著名银行家兼作家詹姆斯·华伯的一篇评论，批评美国的对华政策。
    华伯说：首先，北京政府，“不论我们是不是喜欢它，已经作为一个拥有差不多全世界四分之一的人口的国家的政府而巩固地屹立在那里了”。
    第二，“除了通过战争或者同北京谈判以外，是没有其他办法能够解决朝鲜、印度支那或者台湾问题的”。
    第三，“我们不能同一个我们拒绝承认它的存在的政府进行谈判”。
    华伯说：“美国人民既不要战争，也不希望永远生活在‘战争边缘’上。他们要的是和平——真正的和平，不是一种靠不住的可怕的均衡——如果真正的和平能够光荣地达到的话。情况就是这样，美国人民将希望他们的政府采取唯一可以导致真正的和平的道路，如果他们有机会了解事实而不是被煽动采取不妥协态度的话……”。
    华伯着重指出：“鸵鸟式地拒绝正视远东的虽然是不愉快、但是却是不可改变的事实的做法，不但会肯定使美国置身于对华战争的边缘，并且很快就会导致美国的完全孤立。我们的对华政策正在迅速地使那些未参加任何一方的国家的人民和我们的大多数盟友同我们疏远，例外的只有蒋介石和李承晚，他们两人是公然希望把美国拖入战争的。”
