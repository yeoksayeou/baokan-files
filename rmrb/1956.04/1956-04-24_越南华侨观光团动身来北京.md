Title: Vietnamese Overseas Chinese Visiting Group Departs for Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 23rd. A fifteen-member Vietnamese Overseas Chinese Visiting Group, returning to China to participate in the "May Day" celebrations, left Hanoi by train on April 23rd for Beijing. The visiting group is composed of representatives from all circles of overseas Chinese in Hanoi, Haiphong, and Hong Gai, and is headed by Xian Yitang.

### 越南华侨观光团动身来北京

1956-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    越南华侨观光团动身来北京
    新华社河内23日电  回中国参加“五一”节纪念的越南华侨归国观光团一行十五人四月二十三日乘火车离开河内前往北京。观光团是由河内、海防和鸿广区等地的各界华侨代表组成的，团长是冼亦唐。
