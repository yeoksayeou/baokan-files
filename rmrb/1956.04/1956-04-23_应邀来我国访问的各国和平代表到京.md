Title: Peace Representatives from Various Countries Arrive in Beijing at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1956-04-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd.  A delegation of peace representatives from various countries, invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, arrived in Beijing on the 21st. They are: from Burma, Tekin Lay Maung; from Egypt, Hossein Kamal El-Din Zaki; from Iceland, Alexdottir-Thorvaldsen, Ranveig Tomasdottir; from Jordan, Abdul Qader Al-Saleh, Issa Madanat; from the Union of South Africa, Ronald Edwin Press; and from Sudan, Sayed Yacoub Hamid Bakier, Lutfi Muhammad Ibrahim, Osman Abdul Wakiara.

### 应邀来我国访问的各国和平代表到京

1956-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国访问的各国和平代表到京
    新华社22日讯  应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来中国访问的各国和平代表一行十人，在二十一日到达北京。他们是：缅甸的德钦礼貌，埃及的侯赛尼·卡马尔·埃丁·扎基，冰岛的艾里克斯多蒂尔—索尔瓦尔森、兰维格·托马斯迪，约旦的阿卜杜勒·卡德尔·萨利、伊萨·马达纳特，南非联邦的罗纳德·埃德温·普勒斯和苏丹的赛义德·雅各布·哈密德·巴基克尔、卢特斐·穆罕默德·易卜拉欣、欧斯曼·阿卜杜勒·瓦基亚拉。
