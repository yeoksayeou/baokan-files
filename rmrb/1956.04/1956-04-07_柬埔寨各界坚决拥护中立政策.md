Title: All Circles in Cambodia Firmly Support the Neutral Policy
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 6th: According to Agence France-Presse, Phnom Penh: Several thousand Cambodian government officials and farmers held a demonstration in Phnom Penh on April 5th, supporting Cambodia's neutral policy.

After marching and demonstrating in front of the Prime Minister's Office and the National Assembly Building, the demonstrators submitted a petition to King Suramarit, requesting his son, Prince Sihanouk, to continue serving as Prime Minister.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 6th: Phnom Penh dispatch: According to the Cambodian "Democracy Newspaper," the Central Committee of the Cambodian Democratic Party passed a statement at its meeting on March 29th, expressing support for the neutral policy of the Kingdom of Cambodia's government. The Democratic Party believes that the neutral policy is in line with the national aspirations of Cambodia. It calls on the Cambodian people to unite closely, support the neutral policy that is related to the survival of the Cambodian nation, and calls on the people to be ideologically prepared to sacrifice everything to defend the neutral policy and oppose all blatant intentions and actions that infringe on Cambodia's neutral status.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 4th: According to a broadcast by Phnom Penh Radio today: The Committee for Supporting the Neutral Policy of the Kingdom of Cambodia issued a statement on the 2nd, saying that Cambodia's neutral policy is being hindered, therefore it is necessary to strengthen unity to defend Cambodia's independence, peace and sovereignty, firmly support Prince Sihanouk's neutral policy, and be prepared to make all sacrifices to defend the neutral policy.

The committee stated that the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence are very necessary, and Cambodia also respects the resolutions of the Bandung Conference.

According to news from Phnom Penh, the masses in the provincial capital of Banam Province, southeast of Phnom Penh, held a demonstration on April 1st, expressing support for the neutral policy and expressing regret over Sihanouk's resignation.

### 柬埔寨各界坚决拥护中立政策

1956-04-07
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨各界坚决拥护中立政策
    新华社河内6日电　据法新社金边消息：好几千柬埔寨政府官员和农民四月五日在金边举行示威游行，赞同柬埔寨奉行中立政策。
    示威者在首相办公处和国民会议大厦前面游行示威之后，交给苏拉玛里特国王一项请愿书，要求他的儿子西哈努克亲王继续担任首相。
    新华社河内6日电　金边消息：据柬埔寨“民主报”报道，柬埔寨民主党中央委员会在三月二十九日的会议上通过了一项声明，表示拥护柬埔寨王国政府的中立政策。民主党认为，中立政策是符合于柬埔寨的民族愿望的。它号召柬埔寨人民紧密地携起手来，拥护关系着柬埔寨国家生存的中立政策，并且号召人民在思想上作好牺牲一切的准备，以保卫中立政策和反对一切蛮横的侵犯柬埔寨的中立地位的意图和行动。
    新华社河内4日电　据金边电台今天广播：柬埔寨王国的拥护中立政策委员会在二日发表声明说，柬埔寨的中立政策正在遭到阻碍，因此必须加强团结以保卫柬埔寨的独立、和平和主权，坚决拥护西哈努克亲王的中立政策，并且准备不惜一切牺牲来保卫中立政策。
    这个委员会在声明中认为和平共处的五项原则是非常必要的，柬埔寨并且还尊重万隆会议的决议。
    又根据金边来的消息说，金边东南的波萝勉省会的群众曾在四月一日举行了示威游行，表示拥护中立政策，并且对西哈努克的辞职表示惋惜。
