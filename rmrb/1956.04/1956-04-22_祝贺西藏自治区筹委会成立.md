Title: Congratulations on the Establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1956-04-22
Page: 1

Mao Zedong's Congratulatory Telegram

To the Dalai Lama, the Panchen Erdeni, and all members of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region:

    I am pleased to congratulate you on the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and I sincerely hope that under your guidance, the people of all strata in Tibet will unite and progress even further, and achieve greater success in developing Tibet's political, economic, and cultural undertakings.
                        Mao Zedong, April 20, 1956

Liu Shaoqi's Congratulatory Telegram

To the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region:

    The establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region is a great happy event for the people of Tibet and also a great happy event for the people of all ethnic groups in our country. I believe that the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region will certainly be able to further strengthen the unity within the Tibetan ethnic group and the unity among all ethnic groups in the motherland, and encourage the people of all strata in Tibet to make greater contributions to the cause of building a prosperous and happy Tibet.
                          Liu Shaoqi, April 20, 1956

Zhou Enlai's Congratulatory Telegram

To the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region:

    On the occasion of the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to you and all the monks and laypeople of Tibet. The establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region is another great victory for the ethnic policies of the Chinese Communist Party and the Central People's Government; it is the result of the close unity and joint efforts of all the monks and laypeople of Tibet under the leadership of the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni. I am deeply convinced that after the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, it will make greater achievements in realizing regional autonomy for the Tibetan ethnic group, strengthening ethnic unity, training cadres, and developing economic and cultural undertakings.
                          Zhou Enlai, April 20, 1956

Congratulatory Telegram from the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress

To the Dalai Lama, the Panchen Erdeni, and the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region:

    We warmly congratulate the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region. This is an important development in the mutual unity of all ethnic groups in our country and the internal unity of the Tibet region. We believe that the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region will certainly be able to further unite the monks and laypeople of Tibet to work together to achieve new successes in the unity of the Tibet region and the country as a whole and in various construction undertakings, and to improve the lives of the Tibetan people day by day.
              Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, April 20, 1956

Congratulatory Telegram from the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress

To the Dalai Lama, the Panchen Erdeni, and the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region:

    The establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region is a sign of greater unity within the Tibetan ethnic group and also a sign of greater unity among all ethnic groups in our great motherland. We would like to extend our warm congratulations to you and all the monks and laypeople of Tibet, and wish you new and greater achievements in building Tibet and participating in the various construction undertakings of the motherland.
              Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress, April 20, 1956

Congratulatory Telegram from the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China

To the Inaugural Meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region:

    The establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region is a good start for the implementation of ethnic regional autonomy in the Tibet region, and it is also a major event in the political life of the people of all ethnic groups in our country. With joyful hearts, we extend our warm congratulations to you. We believe that after the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, under the leadership and assistance of the Chinese Communist Party and the Central People's Government, the cause of unity, progress, and development in the Tibet region will achieve greater success. We wish the conference success.
            Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China, April 20, 1956

### 祝贺西藏自治区筹委会成立

1956-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺西藏自治区筹委会成立
      毛泽东主席的贺电达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼和西藏自治区筹备委员会各位委员：
    我愉快地祝贺西藏自治区筹备委员会的成立，热忱地希望西藏各阶层人民在你们指导之下更加团结和进步，在发展西藏政治、经济和文化事业上获得更大的成就。
                        毛泽东  1956年4月20日
      刘少奇委员长的贺电西藏自治区筹备委员会：
    西藏自治区筹备委员会的成立，是西藏人民的大喜事，也是我国各民族人民的大喜事。我相信西藏自治区筹备委员会的成立，一定能够更进一步地加强西藏民族内部的团结和祖国各民族间的团结，鼓舞西藏各阶层人民在建设繁荣幸福的西藏的事业上作出更大的贡献。
                          刘少奇  1956年4月20日
      周恩来总理的贺电西藏自治区筹备委员会：
    在西藏自治区筹备委员会成立的时候，我谨向你们和西藏全体僧俗人民致以热烈的祝贺。西藏自治区筹备委员会的成立，是中国共产党和中央人民政府的民族政策的又一伟大胜利；是西藏全体僧俗人民在达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼领导下，亲密团结和共同努力的结果。我深信，西藏自治区筹备委员会成立以后，在实现西藏民族的区域自治，加强民族团结、培养干部和发展经济、文化事业上都将做出更大的成绩。
                          周恩来  1956年4月20日
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会的贺电达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼并西藏自治区筹备委员会：
    我们热烈地祝贺西藏自治区筹备委员会的成立。这是我国各民族相互团结和西藏地方内部团结的一个重要发展。我们相信，西藏自治区筹备委员会一定能够进一步团结西藏僧俗人民共同努力，使西藏地方和全国的团结和各项建设事业获得新的成就，使西藏人民的生活日益改善。
              全国人民代表大会常务委员会  1956年4月20日
    全国人民代表大会民族委员会的贺电达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼并西藏自治区筹备委员会：
    西藏自治区筹备委员会的成立，是西藏民族内部更加团结的标志，也是我们伟大祖国各民族更加团结的标志。我们特向你们和西藏全体僧俗人民致以热烈的祝贺，并祝你们在建设西藏和参加祖国各项建设事业中获得新的更大的成就。
              全国人民代表大会民族委员会  1956年4月20日
    中华人民共和国民族事务委员会的贺电西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会：
    西藏自治区筹备委员会的成立，是西藏地方实行民族区域自治的良好开端，也是我国各民族人民政治生活中的一件大事。我们怀着欢欣的心情，向你们致热烈的祝贺。相信在西藏自治区筹备委员会成立以后，在中国共产党和中央人民政府的领导和帮助下，西藏地方团结、进步和发展的事业，将获得更大的成就。敬祝大会成功。
            中华人民共和国民族事务委员会  1956年4月20日
