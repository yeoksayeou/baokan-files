Title: Our Trade Delegation Returns to Cairo from Sudan
Author:
Date: 1956-04-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 14th: The Chinese trade delegation headed by Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of China, has concluded its visit to Sudan and returned to Cairo by plane this morning from Sudan.
    When the delegation left Khartoum, Sudanese Minister of Trade, Industry and Supply, Ibrahim Muffti, and other Sudanese government officials went to the airport to see them off.

### 我贸易访问团从苏丹回到开罗

1956-04-15
第4版()
专栏：

    我贸易访问团从苏丹回到开罗
    新华社开罗14日电　以中国对外贸易部长叶季壮为首的中国贸易访问团，已经结束了在苏丹的访问，并且在今天上午从苏丹乘飞机回到开罗。
    访问团在离开喀土穆的时候，苏丹贸易、工业和供应部长易卜拉欣·穆弗蒂和其他苏丹政府官员曾经到机场送行。
