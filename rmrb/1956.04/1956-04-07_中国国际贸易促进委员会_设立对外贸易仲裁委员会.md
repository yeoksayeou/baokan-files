Title: China Council for the Promotion of International Trade Establishes Foreign Trade Arbitration Commission
Author:
Date: 1956-04-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Foreign Trade Arbitration Commission of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade has been formally established. The first session of the commission's first committee was held on April 2nd, and Ji Chaoding was elected as chairman of the commission, with Zhou Gengsheng and Dai Xiuzan as vice-chairmen.

According to the person in charge of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, the establishment of the Foreign Trade Arbitration Commission is to meet practical needs and to better and more quickly and reasonably resolve any disputes that may arise in foreign trade through arbitration, especially disputes between foreign firms, companies or other economic organizations and Chinese firms, companies or other economic organizations.

The China Council for the Promotion of International Trade held a meeting of its members on March 31st. In accordance with the decision of the 215th meeting of the Government Administration Council regarding the establishment of the Foreign Trade Arbitration Commission within the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, the members of the first committee of the Foreign Trade Arbitration Commission were elected and the Provisional Rules of Arbitration Procedure were formulated. The first committee consists of twenty-one members: Wang Guangdou, Liu Jinsheng, Xing Yimin, Bei Zhongxuan, Li Zhuchen, Zhou Gengsheng, Fan Yunzhong, Chen Hansheng, Nan Hanchen, Ma Naishu, Ma Yinchu, Xi Wenguang, Huang Changshui, Xu Dixing, Jia Zhenzhi, Deng Chenxi, Cai Wuji, Ji Chaoding, Lu Xuzhang, Dai Xiuzan, and Gong Yinbing.

### 中国国际贸易促进委员会  设立对外贸易仲裁委员会

1956-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中国国际贸易促进委员会
    设立对外贸易仲裁委员会
    新华社6日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会对外贸易仲裁委员会正式成立。这个委员会的第一届委员会在四月二日举行第一次会议，推选冀朝鼎为委员会主席，周鲠生、戴修瓒为副主席。
    据中国国际贸易促进委员会负责人谈，成立对外贸易仲裁委员会是为了适应实际需要，更好地和迅速合理地用仲裁方式解决对外贸易中一切可能发生的争议，特别是外国商号、公司或其他经济组织同中国商号、公司或其他经济组织间的争议。
    中国国际贸易促进委员会曾经在三月三十一日召开委员会议，根据前政务院第二百一十五次政务会议通过的关于在中国国际贸易促进委员会内设立对外贸易仲裁委员会的决定，选出了对外贸易仲裁委员会第一届委员会委员，并且制定了仲裁程序暂行规则。第一届委员会委员共二十一人，他们是：王光斗、刘今生、邢亦民、贝仲选、李烛尘、周鲠生、范允中、陈翰笙、南汉宸、马乃庶、马寅初、席文光、黄长水、许涤新、贾振之、邓辰西、蔡无忌、冀朝鼎、卢绪章、戴修瓒、龚饮冰。
