Title: China and Cambodia Issue Joint Communique on Economic and Trade Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: The Ministry of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China and the Cambodian Royal Economic Delegation to China conducted discussions in Beijing from April 10th to 24th regarding the development of economic and trade relations between the two countries. As a result of the discussions, representatives of both sides signed the Trade Agreement and Payment Agreement between China and Cambodia in Beijing on the 24th and issued a joint communique by the representatives of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Kingdom of Cambodia on economic and trade negotiations.
    The full text of the communique is as follows:
    The representatives of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the representatives of the Government of the Kingdom of Cambodia conducted discussions in Beijing from April 10, 1956, to April 24, 1956, regarding the development of economic and trade relations between the two countries. As a result of the discussions, representatives of both sides signed the Trade Agreement and Payment Agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Cambodia in Beijing on April 24, 1956.
    The Trade Agreement stipulates the principle of balanced trade in imports and exports and the principle of granting the most favorable treatment possible in issuing import and export licenses. The agreement also includes lists of goods for export by each side, each totaling five million pounds.
    The Payment Agreement stipulates that the People's Bank of China and the National Bank of Cambodia shall establish direct clearing relations.
    The negotiations were conducted in an extremely friendly and sincere atmosphere, and an agreement was reached in a very short period of time. The rapid conclusion of the agreement demonstrates the full understanding of the delegations of both sides and their desire to consolidate and develop economic relations between China and Cambodia.
    In addition, the representatives of both sides also discussed the issue of economic assistance in friendly talks. The representatives of both sides are prepared to submit this issue to their respective governments for consideration.

### 中柬两国代表发表经济贸易谈判公报

1956-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    中柬两国代表发表经济贸易谈判公报
    新华社29日讯  中华人民共和国对外贸易部同柬埔寨王国访华经济代表团，于四月十日至二十四日期间，在北京进行了有关发展两国经济贸易关系的商谈。根据商谈的结果，双方代表于二十四日在北京签订了中柬两国贸易协定和支付协定，并且发表了中华人民共和国政府代表和柬埔寨王国政府代表关于经济贸易谈判的联合公报。
    公报的全文如下：
    中华人民共和国政府代表同柬埔寨王国政府代表于1956年4月10日至1956年4月24日的期间内在北京进行了有关发展两国经济贸易关系的商谈。根据商谈的结果，双方代表于1956年4月24日在北京签订了中华人民共和国和柬埔寨王国之间的贸易协定和支付协定。
    在贸易协定中，规定了贸易进出口平衡的原则和在发给进出口许可证方面给予尽可能优惠待遇的原则。协定并且附有双方各出口五百万英镑的货单。
    支付协定规定由中国人民银行和柬埔寨国民银行建立直接清算关系。
    谈判是在极友好诚恳的空气中进行的，并且在很短的时间内达成了协议。谈判的迅速达成协议，显示了双方代表团的充分谅解以及巩固和发展中柬两国经济关系的愿望。
    此外，双方代表在友好的会谈中曾经谈到经济援助的问题。双方代表准备将这个问题提交各自政府加以考虑。
