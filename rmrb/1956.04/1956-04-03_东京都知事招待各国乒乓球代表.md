Title: Tokyo Governor Hosts Table Tennis Representatives from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-04-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, April 1st: The Governor of Tokyo, Seiichiro Yasui, held a tea party on the afternoon of April 1st to host representatives from various countries participating in the 23rd World Table Tennis Championships.

    Rong Gaotang, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, and the Chinese table tennis team were invited to attend the tea party.

    Also attending the tea party were Montague, Chairman of the International Table Tennis Federation, and representatives from various national table tennis teams.

    On the Japanese side, those attending the tea party included Prince and Princess Takamatsu, the Emperor's second younger brother, Masashi Adachi, President of the Japan Table Tennis Association, representatives of sports organizations, government representatives, and members of the Diet, totaling about three hundred people.

    At the tea party, wonderful programs such as Japanese folk dances were performed. Representatives from various countries engaged in friendly conversations at the tea party.

### 东京都知事招待各国乒乓球代表

1956-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    东京都知事招待各国乒乓球代表
    新华社1日讯　东京消息：日本东京都知事安井诚一郎四月一日下午举行茶会，招待参加第二十三届世界乒乓球锦标赛的各国代表。
    中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠和中国乒乓球代表队应邀参加了茶会。
    参加茶会的还有国际乒乓球联合会主席蒙塔古和各国乒乓球队的代表。
    日本方面参加茶会的有日本天皇的第二个弟弟高松宫夫妇、日本乒乓球协会会长足立正、体育团体代表、政府代表和国会议员等约三百人。
    在茶会上表演了日本民间舞蹈等精采节目。各国代表在茶会上进行了亲密的交谈。
