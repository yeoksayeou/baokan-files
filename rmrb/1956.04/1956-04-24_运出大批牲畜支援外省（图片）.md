Author: Sun Jing
Date: 1956-04-24

　　Transporting a Large Number of Livestock to Support Other Provinces
　　Henan Province plans to transfer 60,000 head of draft animals this year to support agricultural production in Hebei, Shanxi, Gansu and other provinces. This is a portion of the draft animals gathered in Luohe City awaiting transport.

### 运出大批牲畜支援外省（图片）

    运出大批牲畜支援外省
    河南省今年计划调出六万头耕畜支援河北、山西、甘肃等省的农业生产。这是集中在漯河市等待外运的一部分耕畜。
