Title: Dutt Emphasizes Cooperation of British Workers' Parties at CPGB Congress
Author:
Date: 1956-04-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, April 1st (TASS dispatch): The Communist Party of Great Britain's Congress continued to discuss the Executive Committee's political summary report on March 31st.

    The question of unity in the workers' movement was the focus of attention at the meeting. The Vice-Chairman of the Communist Party Executive Committee, Palme Dutt, stressed that divisions in the workers' movement brought losses to both the Labour Party and the Communist Party.

    Dutt said that both parties, whether at the local or national level, should take new steps to openly and amicably discuss cooperation issues.

    Other speakers in the discussion talked about the tasks of the workers' movement and the need to unite and fight together to defend the interests of the working people.

### 杜德在英共代表大会上强调英国工人政党的合作

1956-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    杜德在英共代表大会上强调英国工人政党的合作
    新华社1日讯　塔斯社伦敦讯：英国共产党代表大会三月三十一日继续讨论执行委员会的政治总结报告。
    工人运动的团结问题成为会议的注意中心。共产党执行委员会副主席帕姆·杜德强调指出，工人运动的分歧给工党和共产党带来同样的损失。
    杜德说，这两个党无论在地方或者在全国范围内都应当采取新的步骤来公开地和友好地讨论合作问题。
    其他发言人在讨论中都谈到工人运动的任务和必须团结一致共同为了保卫劳动人民的利益而斗争。
