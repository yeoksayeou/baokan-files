Title: Another Anti-Communist Atrocity in Morocco
Author:
Date: 1956-04-11
Page: 4
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Casablanca, 10th -- Three members of the Executive Committee of the Moroccan Communist Party: Bourkia, El Maleh, and Layani were shot while driving in Casablanca on the 9th. Their driver was seriously injured. This is another assassination following the assassination of Moroccan Communist Party Secretary Abdallah.

### 在摩洛哥的又一次反共暴行

1956-04-11
第4版()
专栏：

    在摩洛哥的又一次反共暴行
    新华社10日讯　卡萨布兰卡消息：摩洛哥共产党执行委员会的三个委员：布尔基亚、埃尔·马累和拉雅奈九日乘汽车在卡萨布兰卡驶行途中遭到枪击，他们的司机受重伤。这是摩洛哥共产党书记阿勃达拉被暗杀以后的又一次谋杀事件。
