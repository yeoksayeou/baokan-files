Title: Nearly Two Million Trees Planted Around Erhai Lake and Cangshan Mountain
Author:
Date: 1956-04-02
Page: 1

More than 100,000 youths of various ethnic groups in the Erhai minority region of Yunnan Province have planted nearly two million trees around the famous scenic spot of Erhai Lake and in Cangshan Mountain beside Erhai Lake. In Dali County, youths of various ethnic groups planted ornamental trees in scenic spots and historical sites such as the Qianxun Pagoda, which has a history of more than 1,000 years, and the tomb of Du Wenxiu, the Hui leader of the Hui uprising.

By April, 32 agricultural producers' cooperatives at the foot of Cangshan Mountain will plant tens of millions of economic forests, such as tea trees, on the mountainside of Cangshan Mountain, which stretches for more than 100 li. (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 洱海和苍山植树近二百万株

1956-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

    洱海和苍山植树近二百万株
    云南省洱海民家族地区的十万多名青少年在著名的风景区洱海周围和洱海旁的苍山等地种植了将近两百万株树。大理县的各族青少年在有一千多年历史的千寻塔等名胜古迹地区和回民起义的回族领袖杜文秀坟地种植了风景林木。
    到四月份，苍山脚下的三十二个农业生产合作社将在绵亘一百多里长的苍山山腰种植上千万株茶等经济林。　（据新华社讯）
