Title: Six New Department Stores Open in Succession in Newly Built Areas of the Capital
Author:
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 2
Six comprehensive department stores in the newly built areas of Beijing have opened successively from April 12th to 28th. These six department stores are distributed in Zanan Chu, Ganjiakou, Gongzhufen, Yongding Road in the western suburbs, Muxiyuan in the southern suburbs, and Jiuxianqiao in the eastern suburbs. These areas are all newly built areas with government offices, factories, schools, and family residential areas.

### 首都新建区六大商场先后开幕

1956-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    首都新建区六大商场先后开幕
    北京市新建区六个综合性商场先后在四月十二日到二十八日开幕。这六个商场分布在西郊的暂安处、甘家口、公主坟、永定路，南郊的木樨园，东郊的酒仙桥等地。这些地区都是新建起来的机关、工厂、学校和家属住宅区。
