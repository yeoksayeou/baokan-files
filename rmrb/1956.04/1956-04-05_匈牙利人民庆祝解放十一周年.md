Title: Hungarian People Celebrate the Eleventh Anniversary of Liberation
Author:
Date: 1956-04-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest (via TASS) – The Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, the Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic, and the National Council of the Hungarian Patriotic People's Front held a celebration rally in Budapest on the evening of April 3rd to celebrate the eleventh anniversary of Hungary's liberation.

    The President of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, I. Dobi, first delivered the opening speech.

    Then, Sándor Nógrádi, member of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, delivered a report on the eleventh anniversary of Hungary's liberation. Nógrádi said that on the anniversary of the liberation, we must first express our gratitude to the Soviet people and the invincible Soviet army. The great achievements made by the Hungarian people in the period after liberation demonstrate that the worker-peasant regime is a tremendous source of people's power.

    Nógrádi's speech was met with enthusiastic and prolonged applause from all those present.

### 匈牙利人民庆祝解放十一周年

1956-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利人民庆祝解放十一周年
    新华社据塔斯社布达佩斯讯　匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会、匈牙利人民共和国部长会议和匈牙利爱国人民阵线全国委员会四月三日晚间在布达佩斯举行庆祝大会，庆祝匈牙利解放十一周年。
    匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席伊·道比首先致开会辞。
    接着，匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员桑·诺格拉迪作了关于匈牙利解放十一周年的报告。诺格拉迪说，在解放纪念日，我们首先要向苏联人民和战无不胜的苏军表示感谢。匈牙利人民在解放后的时期内所取得的巨大成就说明，工农政权是人民力量的巨大泉源。
    诺格拉迪的讲话受到全体到会者热烈的长时间的鼓掌。
