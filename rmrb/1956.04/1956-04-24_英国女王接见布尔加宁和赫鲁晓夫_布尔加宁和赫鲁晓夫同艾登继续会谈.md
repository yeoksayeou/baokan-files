Title: Queen of England Receives Bulganin and Khrushchev; Bulganin and Khrushchev Continue Talks with Eden
Author:
Date: 1956-04-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, April 23rd, via TASS: Queen Elizabeth II of England received the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Bulganin, and the member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Khrushchev, at Windsor Castle on April 22nd. Prime Minister Eden introduced Bulganin and Khrushchev to the Queen. Also present at the meeting were the Queen's husband, the Duke of Edinburgh, and the Queen's children.

Bulganin presented Queen Elizabeth II with a letter from Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR. The conversation lasted thirty minutes.

Bulganin and Khrushchev went to Windsor Castle after continuing talks with Eden at Chequers, Eden's country residence. As they drove through the towns and villages of Buckinghamshire and Berkshire, thousands of local residents and Londoners spending a sunny spring Sunday in the countryside waited for the Soviet leaders to pass by on the streets and at intersections of residential areas. They greeted the guests with "Ura" and cheers.

On Windsor Street, leading to Windsor Castle, the Soviet leaders were warmly welcomed by thousands of Britons crowded on the sidewalks and pavements, as well as many foreign travelers. They raised their hands, applauded, and shouted welcoming slogans to greet the guests. Bulganin and Khrushchev warmly responded to the cheers of the crowd.

After leaving Windsor, Bulganin and Khrushchev returned to London. That evening, the Soviet leaders attended a reception hosted by the London County Council.

Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd, via TASS, London, April 22nd: Bulganin and Khrushchev, who spent the night at the British Prime Minister's country residence, Chequers, continued their talks with Eden there on April 22nd. Lloyd and Butler also participated in the talks.

Prime Minister Eden held a luncheon on April 22nd to entertain Bulganin and Khrushchev. Those attending the luncheon included Gromyko, Malik, Lloyd, Butler, Kirkpatrick, Hayter, Gaitskell, Robens, and Griffiths.

After the talks at Chequers, the Soviet guests traveled by car to Windsor, where they were received by Queen Elizabeth II of England.

In Windsor, thousands of people welcomed the Soviet leaders as they drove to Windsor Castle.

According to a report from the British United Press, the British Under-Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs, Nutting, and the Soviet Minister of Culture, Mikhailov, held talks on April 21st. They discussed issues related to cultural relations between Britain and the Soviet Union.

### 英国女王接见布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫  布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫同艾登继续会谈

1956-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

    英国女王接见布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫
    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫同艾登继续会谈
    新华社23日讯  据塔斯社伦敦讯：英国女王伊丽莎白二世四月二十二日在温莎堡接见了苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫。艾登首相向英国女王介绍了布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫。接见时在座的还有女王的丈夫爱丁堡公爵和女王的子女。
    布尔加宁向女王伊丽莎白二世递交了苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫的信件。谈话进行了三十分钟。
    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫是在同艾登在艾登的乡间别墅契克斯继续举行会谈后到温莎堡去的，他们驱车经过布金罕姆郡和培克郡的城市和乡村时，数以千计的当地居民和到乡间度晴朗的春季星期天的伦敦人在居住区的街道上和十字路口等待着苏联领导人经过。他们以乌拉和欢呼声欢迎客人们。
    在通向温莎堡的温莎街道上，苏联领导人受到了数以千计的拥挤在人行道和便道上的英国人以及许多外国旅行者的热烈欢迎。他们举起他们的手，鼓着掌，喊着欢迎的口号欢迎客人们。布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫热情地答谢人群的欢呼。
    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫在离开温莎后回到伦敦。同日晚上，苏联领袖们出席了伦敦郡议会的招待会。
    新华社23日电  塔斯社伦敦二十二日讯：在英国首相的乡间别墅契克斯度夜的布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫在四月二十二日继续同艾登在那里举行会谈。参加会谈的还有劳埃德和巴特勒。
    艾登首相四月二十二日举行午宴，招待布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫。参加午宴的有葛罗米柯、马立克、劳埃德、巴特勒、柯克帕特里克、海特尔、盖次克尔、罗本斯和葛莱菲斯。
    在契克斯举行会谈以后，苏联客人乘汽车到温莎去，他们在那里受到英国女王伊丽莎白二世的接见。
    在温莎，当他们乘车到温莎堡去的时候，有几千人在欢迎苏联领袖。
    根据英国报联社的一个消息说，英国外交副大臣纳丁和苏联文化部长米哈伊洛夫在四月二十一日举行会谈。他们讨论了英国和苏联之间的文化关系问题。
