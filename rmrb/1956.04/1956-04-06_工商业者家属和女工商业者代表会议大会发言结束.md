Title: End of Speeches at the Conference of Representatives of Industrialists' and Businessmen's Families and Female Industrialists and Businesswomen
Author:
Date: 1956-04-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The National Conference of Representatives of Industrialists' and Businessmen's Families and Female Industrialists and Businesswomen concluded its general session speeches this afternoon. As of today, 79 representatives have spoken at the general session, and 121 representatives have submitted written statements.

    Li Huiying, He Lizhen, and Liu Shuixiong, from the Hong Kong and Macau Women's Visiting Group attending the conference as observers, also spoke at the meeting today. They all mentioned the warm welcome and care they received along the way from Guangzhou to Beijing. They stated that upon their return, they would certainly convey the achievements of the motherland's construction in all aspects that they had seen, as well as everything they had heard and experienced at the conference, to compatriots in Hong Kong and Macau.

    Tonight, the female industrialists and businesswomen representatives attending the conference also held a symposium to exchange experiences in enterprise operations.

### 工商业者家属和女工商业者代表会议大会发言结束

1956-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    工商业者家属和女工商业者代表会议大会发言结束
    据新华社5日讯　全国工商业者家属和女工商业者代表会议今天下午结束大会发言。到今天止，先后在大会上发言的代表有七十九人，提出书面发言的代表有121人。
    列席这次大会的香港和澳门妇女观光团的李惠英、何丽贞和刘帨雄也在今天会上发言。她们都谈到从广州到北京来，沿途受到欢迎和照顾。她们表示回去后一定把所看到的祖国各方面建设的成就，以及在大会上听到和体会到的一切，转告给香港和澳门同胞。
    今天晚上，出席大会的女工商业者代表还举行了座谈会，交流了有关企业业务方面的经验。
