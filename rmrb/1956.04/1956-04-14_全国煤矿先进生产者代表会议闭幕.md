Title: National Coal Mine Advanced Producers Representative Conference Concludes

Author: 
Date: 1956-04-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 13th: The National Coal Mine Advanced Producers Representative Conference concluded in Beijing today.

    The more than 1,200 advanced producers attending the meeting today passed a resolution. The resolution stated that we must universally promote the various advanced experiences exchanged at the conference, fully implement the 55 proposals put forward at the conference, assist administrations and trade unions in formulating plans and specific measures to promote advanced experiences based on the specific circumstances of each unit, unite all employees to further carry out socialist emulation and the advanced producer movement, and strive to complete the first Five-Year Plan for the coal industry ahead of schedule, as a gift to the Eighth National Congress of the Party.
    At today's closing ceremony, Chen Yu, Minister of the Coal Industry, presented certificates of merit to the advanced units whose representatives attended the conference. The presidium of the conference presented souvenirs to the advanced producers and representatives of advanced units attending the conference, and presented silk banners to 113 advanced units that had made outstanding achievements in their work.
    The conference selected 293 of the most outstanding advanced producers and advanced units as representatives of the national coal mine system to attend the National Advanced Producers Representative Conference.

### 全国煤矿先进生产者代表会议闭幕

1956-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    全国煤矿先进生产者代表会议闭幕
    新华社13日讯　全国煤矿先进生产者代表会议今天在北京闭幕。
    出席会议的一千二百多名先进生产者今天通过了一项决议。决议说，我们要普遍推广会议上交流的各项先进经验，全面实现会议上所提出的五十五件倡议，根据各个单位的具体情况，协助行政和工会制定推广先进经验的计划和具体措施，团结全体职工进一步开展社会主义竞赛和先进生产者运动，争取提前完成煤炭工业的第一个五年计划，作为对党的第八次代表大会的献礼。
    在今天的闭幕会上，煤炭工业部部长陈郁向有代表参加这次会议的先进单位颁发了奖状。大会主席团向到会的先进生产者和先进单位代表赠送了纪念品，并且向其中在工作上有突出成绩的113个先进单位赠送了锦旗。
    会议选出了293个最出色的先进生产者和先进单位，作为全国煤矿系统出席全国先进生产者代表会议的代表。
