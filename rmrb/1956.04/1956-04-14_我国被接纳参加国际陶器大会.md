Title: China Accepted into the International Academy of Ceramics

Author:

Date: 1956-04-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 12th (TASS): The People's Republic of China has been accepted into the International Academy of Ceramics. Representatives of the Chinese Artists Association will officially participate in the International Academy of Ceramics Council meeting to be held in Geneva in May.

    Representatives of the People's Republic of China will participate in the conference's discussions and its exhibitions.

### 我国被接纳参加国际陶器大会

1956-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    我国被接纳参加国际陶器大会
    新华社13日讯　塔斯社日内瓦十二日讯：中华人民共和国已经被接纳参加国际陶器大会。中国美术家协会的代表将正式参加五月份在日内瓦举行的国际陶器大会理事会会议。
    中华人民共和国的代表将参加大会的讨论和它所举办的展览会。
