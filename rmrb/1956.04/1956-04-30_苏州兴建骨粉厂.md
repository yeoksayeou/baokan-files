Title: Suzhou Builds Bone Meal Factory
Author:
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 2

Suzhou Builds Bone Meal Factory

    The local state-owned Suzhou Bone Meal Factory officially started construction on April 21st. This bone meal factory will be put into production on October 1st.

    After this bone meal factory is put into production, it can produce 7,000 to 8,000 tons of bone meal annually, which is sufficient to meet the annual fertilization needs of more than 600,000 mu of paddy fields. This bone meal contains 3%-5% nitrogen fertilizer and 20%-30% phosphate fertilizer, and is suitable for all crops, especially for paddy fields. (Our correspondent in Nanjing)

### 苏州兴建骨粉厂

1956-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    苏州兴建骨粉厂
    地方国营苏州骨粉厂在四月二十一日正式开工兴建。这座骨粉厂将在十月一日投入生产。
    这座骨粉厂投入生产以后，全年可以产骨粉七千到八千吨，足供六十多万亩稻田的全年施肥需要。这种骨粉含有3％—5％的氮肥和20％—30％的磷肥，适用于一切农作物，特别适用于水稻田。（本报驻南京记者）
