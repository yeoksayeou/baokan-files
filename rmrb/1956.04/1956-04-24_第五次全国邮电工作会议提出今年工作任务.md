Title: The Fifth National Postal and Telecommunications Work Conference Proposes This Year's Tasks
Author:
Date: 1956-04-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd:

    The Fifth National Postal and Telecommunications Work Conference has concluded.
    Based on summarizing the postal and telecommunications work in 1955, this conference studied and proposed the tasks for the postal and telecommunications work in 1956 and the measures that should be taken.
    The conference believes that, in accordance with the basic tasks of postal and telecommunications enterprises during the transitional period of the state and the requirements of the state's First Five-Year Plan, postal and telecommunications departments must further transform enterprises, improve enterprise management, and, under the general requirements of improving communication quality, improving equipment utilization, improving and expanding postal and telecommunications services, and ensuring smooth communication, extensively and deeply carry out socialist competitions and advanced producer movements, fully utilize the enthusiasm and creativity of the vast number of employees, and expand the ranks of advanced producers.
    The conference proposed that the work to be carried out in 1956 in terms of improving enterprise management should mainly include rectifying labor organizations and implementing staffing standards for administrative and production personnel; reforming the wage system on the basis of increasing wages; adjusting postal and telecommunications rates based on the principle of uniformity and rationality; continuing to formulate and implement business systems and strengthen business and technical leadership; and strengthening scientific research and cadre training to lay a preliminary foundation for gradually carrying out technical transformation.

### 第五次全国邮电工作会议提出今年工作任务

1956-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    第五次全国邮电工作会议提出今年工作任务
    据新华社23日讯  第五次全国邮电工作会议已经闭幕。
    这次会议在总结1955年邮电工作的基础上，研究和提出了1956年的邮电工作任务和应该采取的措施。
    会议认为，根据邮电企业在国家过渡时期的基本任务和国家第一个五年计划的要求，邮电部门必须进一步改造企业，改善企业管理，并且要在提高通信质量，提高设备利用率，改善和扩大邮电服务，保证通信顺畅的总要求下，广泛深入地开展社会主义竞赛和先进生产者运动，充分发挥广大职工的积极性和创造性，扩大先进生产者队伍。
    会议提出，在改善企业管理方面，1956年应当进行的工作主要是整顿劳动组织，实行行政和生产人员的定员标准；在增加工资的基础上改革工资制度；以统一合理的原则，调整邮电资费；继续制定和贯彻业务制度，加强业务和技术领导；加强科学研究和干部培养，为逐步进行技术改造打下初步基础。
