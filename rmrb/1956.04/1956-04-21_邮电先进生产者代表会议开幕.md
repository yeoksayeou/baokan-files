Title: Postal and Telecommunications Advanced Producers Representative Conference Opens
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 20th - The National Postal and Telecommunications Advanced Producers Representative Conference opened in Beijing today.

　　Attending the conference were more than 470 advanced producer representatives from postal and telecommunications enterprises across the country and 45 non-voting delegates; eighteen people including postmen from agricultural producers' cooperatives and heads of newspaper and magazine distribution stations were also invited to participate.

　　Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications, and Xiang Minghua, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union, delivered reports at the meeting. Zhang Xiuzhu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, also spoke at the meeting.

　　The head of the Soviet Postal and Telecommunications Advanced Producers Delegation, V.S. Sorokin, also spoke at the meeting and presented gifts to the conference.

### 邮电先进生产者代表会议开幕

1956-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    邮电先进生产者代表会议开幕
    据新华社20日讯  全国邮电先进生产者代表会议今天在北京开幕。
    参加会议的有来自全国各地邮电企业的四百七十多个先进生产者代表和四十五个列席代表；会议还邀请了农业生产合作社的邮递员、报刊发行站的站长等十八人参加。
    邮电部部长朱学范、中国邮电工会全国委员会副主席向明华都在会上作了报告。在会上讲话的还有中华全国总工会书记处书记张修竹等。
    苏联邮电先进生产者代表团团长弗·斯·索罗金也在会上讲了话，他并且向大会赠送了礼物。
