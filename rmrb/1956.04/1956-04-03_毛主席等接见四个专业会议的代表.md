Title: Chairman Mao and Others Receive Representatives of Four Professional Conferences
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd—Chairman Mao Zedong and members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Peng Zhen, and Deng Xiaoping, received all the representatives of the Civil Aviation Advanced Producers Representative Conference, all the representatives of the Third National Procuratorial Work Conference, all the representatives of the National Wage Conference, and all the representatives of the Xinhua News Agency Domestic Branch Society Conference this afternoon.

### 毛主席等接见四个专业会议的代表

1956-04-03
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席等接见四个专业会议的代表
    新华社2日讯　毛泽东主席和中共中央政治局委员刘少奇、周恩来、彭真、邓小平，在今天下午分别接见了民用航空先进生产者代表会议的全体代表，第三届全国检察工作会议的全体代表，全国工资会议的全体代表和新华通讯社国内分社会议的全体代表。
