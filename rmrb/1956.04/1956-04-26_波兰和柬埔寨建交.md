Title: Poland and Cambodia Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, April 25th - According to the Polish Press Agency, the Polish government and the Royal Government of Cambodia, in order to strengthen friendly relations between the two countries and promote further normalization of future international relations, have decided to establish diplomatic relations and exchange ministers.

### 波兰和柬埔寨建交

1956-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰和柬埔寨建交
    新华社华沙25日电  据波兰通讯社报道：波兰政府和柬埔寨王国政府为加强两国间的友好关系和促进今后国际关系的进一步正常化，已经决定建立外交关系和互派公使。
