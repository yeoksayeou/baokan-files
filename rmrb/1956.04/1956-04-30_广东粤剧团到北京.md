Title: Guangdong Cantonese Opera Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 3

    Guangdong Cantonese Opera Troupe Arrives in Beijing

    The Guangdong Cantonese Opera Troupe, which came to Beijing to perform, arrived in Beijing on the 29th.

    Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan and Chairman of the Chinese Dramatists Association Tian Han went to the station to greet them. They warmly shook hands with the main actors of the troupe, including Ma Shizeng, Hong Xiannu, Xinzhu, and Li Cuifang. Ma Yanxiang, Zhou Weizhi, and other figures from the literary and art circles, as well as representatives from many Beijing theater groups, also came to the station to welcome them. Zhu Guang, the Mayor of Guangzhou, who is currently in Beijing, also came to the station to welcome them.

    One of the main actors of the troupe, Bai Juyi, has already arrived in Beijing because he was selected as a representative to attend the National Congress of Advanced Producers and will also participate in the performance. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 广东粤剧团到北京

1956-04-30
第3版()
专栏：

    广东粤剧团到北京
    前来北京公演的广东粤剧团二十九日到北京。
    文化部副部长夏衍、中国戏剧家协会主席田汉都到车站迎接，他们和剧团的主要演员马师曾、红线女、新珠、李翠芳等亲切握手。到车站欢迎的还有马彦祥、周巍峙等文艺界人士和北京许多戏剧团体的代表。正在北京的广州市市长朱光也到车站欢迎。
    剧团的主要演员之一白驹荣，因为被选为出席全国先进生产者代表会议的代表，已经先来北京，也将参加演出。（新华社）
