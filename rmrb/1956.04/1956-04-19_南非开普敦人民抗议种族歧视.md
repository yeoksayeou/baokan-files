Title: People of Cape Town, South Africa, Protest Against Racial Discrimination
Author:
Date: 1956-04-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, London, April 17th, TASS dispatch: According to a Reuters dispatch from Cape Town (Union of South Africa), Cape Town has begun implementing an order to reserve special seats for "white" passengers on buses, prohibiting "colored" passengers from sitting in them. This racist measure has aroused great indignation among local residents. A mass protest rally was held in Cape Town, attended by several thousand people. Speakers at the meeting condemned the authorities' racist measure. The meeting passed a resolution boycotting the city transportation that is implementing the new rules.

### 南非开普敦人民抗议种族歧视

1956-04-19
第4版()
专栏：

    南非开普敦人民抗议种族歧视
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社伦敦十七日讯：据路透社开普敦（南非联邦）消息，开普敦开始实施一项命令：在公共汽车中给“白种”乘客保留专用座位，这种座位禁止“有色人种”乘客坐。这项种族主义的措施引起当地居民极大的愤慨。在开普敦举行了群众抗议大会，参加大会的有好几千人。在会上发言的人谴责当局这项种族主义的措施。大会通过决议，抵制推行新规则的市内交通。
