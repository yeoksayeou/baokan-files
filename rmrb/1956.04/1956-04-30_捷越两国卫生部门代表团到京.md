Title: Czechoslovak and Vietnamese Health Delegations Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 4

　　Czechoslovak delegation of seven people headed by Health Minister Plohar and the delegation of five people from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam headed by Minister of Medical Economy Hoang Tich Tri arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 29th at the invitation of the Ministry of Health of the People's Republic of China. The two delegations will participate in the "May Day" celebrations.

### 捷越两国卫生部门代表团到京

1956-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    捷越两国卫生部门代表团到京
    捷克斯洛伐克以卫生部部长普洛伊哈为首的代表团七人和越南民主共和国以医济部部长黄积智为首的代表团五人，应中华人民共和国卫生部的邀请，在二十九日下午乘车到达北京。这两个代表团将参加“五一”节观礼。
