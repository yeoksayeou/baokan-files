Title: Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway: Surveying Completed from Yumen to Hami
Author:
Date: 1956-04-23
Page: 2

    Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway: Surveying Completed from Yumen to Hami
    The detailed survey work for the Yumen to Hami section of the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway has been completed. Staff from the various alignment survey teams have recently returned from the field to Hami to prepare design documents.
    Employees of the Yumen-Hami section survey and design team completed the field survey work more than half a month ahead of schedule. Surveyors now guarantee to submit all design documents in June. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 兰新铁路玉门到哈密完成定测

1956-04-23
第2版()
专栏：

    兰新铁路玉门到哈密完成定测
    兰新铁路玉门到哈密段的定测工作已经完成。各个选线分队的工作人员最近已经从野外回到哈密编制设计文件。
    玉（门）哈（密）段勘测设计总队职工提前半个多月完成了野外定测工作。现在勘测人员保证在六月交出全部设计文件。（据新华社讯）
