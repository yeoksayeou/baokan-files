Title: Premier Phạm Văn Đồng's Reply Telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1956-04-26
Page: 1

To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of the opening of the civil aviation route between the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, I would like to express my heartfelt gratitude on behalf of the government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for your congratulations.

    On this occasion, the government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam extend warm congratulations to you and to the Chinese government and people.

    I am convinced that the opening of our two countries' civil aviation route will further strengthen the friendly cooperation between our two countries in cultural exchange and economic ties.

                Phạm Văn Đồng, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
                                April 24, 1956, Hanoi

### 范文同总理给周总理的复电

1956-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    范文同总理给周总理的复电中华人民共和国总理兼外交部长周恩来同志：
    我谨代表越南民主共和国政府和人民对你在中华人民共和国和越南民主共和国之间的民用航空线开航的时候的祝贺表示衷心的感谢。
    借此机会，越南民主共和国政府和人民谨向你，并且向中国政府和人民致热烈的祝贺。
    我深信，我们两国民用航空线的开航，必将进一步加强我们两国在文化交流和经济联系中的友好合作。
                越南民主共和国总理兼外交部长  范文同
                                1956年4月24日于河内
