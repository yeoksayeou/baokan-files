Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1956-04-27
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, April 26th: The 45th meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks was held at the Palais des Nations at 10:00 a.m. on April 26th. The meeting adjourned at 11:45 a.m. The next meeting is scheduled for May 5th.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1956-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦26日电  中美两国大使级会谈四月二十六日上午十时在国联大厦举行第四十五次会议。会议在十一时四十五分休会。下次会议定五月五日举行。
