Title: Hainan Island to Build Mechanized Sea Salt Farm
Author:
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 2

Hainan Island is preparing to build a state-owned Yinggehai Salt Farm with an area of more than 50,000 mu. The equipment of this salt farm is fully mechanized. After it is put into production in the second half of next year, the annual output of first-class salt will be more than the total annual output of all salt farms in Guangdong Province combined. (Southern Daily Editorial Department)

### 海南岛筹建机械化海盐场

1956-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    海南岛筹建机械化海盐场
    海南岛正在筹建一个面积五万多亩的国营莺歌海盐场。这个盐场的设备是全部机械化的。明年下半年投入生产以后，每年所产的一等盐，将比现在广东全省各盐场年产量的总和还要多。（南方日报编辑部）
