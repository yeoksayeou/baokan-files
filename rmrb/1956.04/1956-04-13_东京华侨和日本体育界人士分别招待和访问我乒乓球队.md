Title: Tokyo Overseas Chinese and Japanese Sports Figures Host and Visit Our Table Tennis Team
Author:
Date: 1956-04-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 12th: The Tokyo Overseas Chinese Association hosted a banquet on the evening of the 12th to welcome Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, Chen Xian, leader of the Chinese Table Tennis Team, and all the players.

Table tennis players from Hong Kong, Singapore, and Macau were also invited to attend the banquet.

Also invited to attend the banquet were Kanzo Uchiyama, Vice Chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Junichi Nakahara, representative of the Japan Peace Supporters Committee, Han Tok-su, representative of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan, and Vietnamese overseas Chinese in Japan.

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 12th: Joji Tsuji, representative of the Japan Skating Federation, visited the Chinese Table Tennis Team on the evening of April 11th to discuss the issue of the Japanese ice hockey team visiting China next year.

On the same day, Japanese sports figures Toshio Yasui, professor at Waseda University, and Mitsuo Mizutani of Meiji University visited Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation. Takeshi Kobayashi, Chairman of the Japan Teachers Union, visited the Chinese Table Tennis Team to express his gratitude for the warm welcome the Japan Teachers Union Education Delegation received during their visit to China last year.

### 东京华侨和日本体育界人士分别招待和访问我乒乓球队

1956-04-13
第4版()
专栏：

    东京华侨和日本体育界人士
    分别招待和访问我乒乓球队
    据新华社12日讯　东京消息：东京华侨总会十二日晚上设宴欢迎中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠、中国乒乓球代表队领队陈先和全体选手。
    香港、新加坡、澳门的乒乓球选手也应邀出席了宴会。
    应邀出席宴会的还有日中友好协会副会长内山完造、日本拥护和平委员会代表中原淳吉、在日朝鲜人总联合会代表韩德珠和旅日越南侨民。
    新华社12日讯　东京消息：日本滑冰协会代表辻?造四月十一日晚上访问了中国乒乓球代表队，对日本冰球队明年访问中国的问题进行了商谈。
    同一天，日本体育界人士早稻田大学教授安井俊雄、明治大学的水谷光壮访问了中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠。日本教职员工会主席小林武访问了中国乒乓球代表队，对日本教职员工会教育考察团去年访问中国时受到热烈欢迎表示感谢。
