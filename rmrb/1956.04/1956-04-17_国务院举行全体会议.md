Title: State Council Holds Plenary Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-04-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The 27th plenary meeting of the State Council of the People's Republic of China was held on the 13th.

    The meeting first listened to explanations by Minister of Labor Ma Wenrui regarding wage reform plans for the industrial, capital construction, and transportation sectors, and explanations by Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun on several issues in the wage reform plan for state organ personnel, before discussing the national wage reform issue.

    After listening to the explanation by Vice Minister of Finance Jin Ming, the meeting discussed the issue of controlling the 1956 national budget revenue and expenditure figures.

    The meeting approved the "Organizational Regulations (Draft) of the People's Congress of the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture" and the "Organizational Regulations (Draft) of the People's Committee of the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture," and decided to submit them to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for approval.

    The meeting approved the "Decision of the State Council on the Establishment of Seda, Galuo, Wagang, and Hongxi Counties in Sichuan Province" and the "Decision of the State Council on the Establishment of the Qian Dongnan Miao and Dong Autonomous Prefecture and the Qiannan Buyi and Miao Autonomous Prefecture in Guizhou."

    Finally, the meeting also approved several appointment and removal items.

### 国务院举行全体会议

1956-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行全体会议
    新华社16日讯　中华人民共和国国务院全体会议第二十七次会议在十三日举行。
    会议首先在听了劳动部部长马文瑞关于工业、基本建设、交通运输部门工资改革方案的说明，国务院秘书长习仲勋关于国家机关工作人员工资改革方案中几个问题的说明以后就全国工资改革问题进行了讨论。
    会议在听了财政部副部长金明的说明以后，就1956年国家预算收支控制数字问题进行了讨论。
    会议通过了“湘西苗族自治州人民代表大会组织条例草案”和“湘西苗族自治州人民委员会组织条例草案”，并且决定提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会批准。
    会议通过了“国务院关于设置四川省色达、呷洛、瓦岗、洪溪等四个县的决定”和“国务院关于设置贵州黔东南苗族、侗族自治州和黔南布依族、苗族自治州的决定”。
    会议最后还通过了任免事项多起。
