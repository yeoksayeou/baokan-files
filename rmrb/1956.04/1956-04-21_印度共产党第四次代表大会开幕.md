Title: Fourth Congress of the Communist Party of India Opens
Author:
Date: 1956-04-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 20th. The Fourth Congress of the Communist Party of India opened on April 19th in Palghat, on the Malabar Coast of southern India. The meeting will last for ten days.

    According to the Press Trust of India, the congress, in its first day of sessions, elected a seven-member presidium, a nine-member general affairs committee, and a credentials committee. The meeting also discussed and decided on the agenda for the congress.

    The congress received congratulatory telegrams from the Communist and Workers' Parties of many countries, including the Soviet Union and China.

    In its first day of sessions, the congress passed two resolutions mourning the martyrs who sacrificed themselves for the patriotic and democratic cause. The resolution commemorating the heroic freedom fighters who died under Portuguese bullets in the struggle for the liberation of Goa stated that the Communist Party of India will never let the sacrifices of these martyred national heroes be in vain, and will continue this great patriotic cause until victory.

### 印度共产党第四次代表大会开幕

1956-04-21
第4版()
专栏：

    印度共产党第四次代表大会开幕
    新华社新德里20日电  印度共产党第四次代表大会四月十九日在印度南部马拉巴海岸的帕尔加特开幕。会议将举行十天。
    据印度报业托辣斯报道，大会在第一天的会议上选举了由七人组成的大会主席团、九人组成的总务委员会和一个证书委员会。会议也讨论了并且决定了大会的议程。
    大会收到了苏联、中国等许多国家的共产党和工人党的贺电。
    大会在第一天的会议上通过了两个悼念为爱国和民主事业而牺牲的烈士的决议。在追悼在为解放果阿而进行的斗争中死于葡萄牙的枪弹下的英勇的自由战士的决议中说，印度共产党将永远不会让这些殉难的民族英雄白白牺牲，并且将继续进行这一伟大的爱国事业直到胜利。
