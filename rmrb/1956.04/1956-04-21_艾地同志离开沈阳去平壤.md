Title: Comrade Aidit Leaves Shenyang for Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1956-04-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 20th — After visiting three industrial cities in Liaoning Province, Indonesian Communist Party General Secretary Aidit left Shenyang for Pyongyang at 2:59 AM on the 20th.
    Aidit arrived in Shenyang on the 15th and visited the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Factory, the Shenyang Pneumatic Tool Factory, and the Shenyang Wire Factory. He also attended a film evening and a song and dance evening jointly held by the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Shenyang Municipal Committee. On the 18th, Aidit went to Fushun to visit the open-pit mine and the No. 1 Petroleum Factory. On the 19th, he visited the three major projects and the No. 2 Thin Plate Factory in Anshan. Aidit visited Changchun from Shenyang on the evening of the 15th, where he visited the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant and the Changchun Film Studio, and toured the city.

### 艾地同志离开沈阳去平壤

1956-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    艾地同志离开沈阳去平壤
    新华社沈阳20日电  印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地在访问辽宁省三个工业城市后，在二十日早上两点五十九分离开沈阳赴平壤。
    艾地是十五日到达沈阳的，在沈阳参观了沈阳第一机床厂、沈阳风动工具厂和沈阳电线厂，并出席了中共辽宁省委和沈阳市委联合举行的电影晚会和歌舞晚会。十八日，艾地赴抚顺参观了露天矿和石油一厂，十九日又到鞍山参观了三大工程和第二薄板厂。艾地曾经在十五日晚由沈阳去长春访问，在长春参观了第一汽车制造厂和长春电影制片厂，并游览了市容。
