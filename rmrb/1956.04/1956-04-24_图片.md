Ning Pinglan, a young fine yarn spinning worker at the state-owned Zhengzhou No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory, will sign contracts with fifteen workers with lower technical skills this year to teach and guide them, promoting her experience. The image shows Ning Pinglan (left) explaining the method of "joining broken ends" to worker Ma Jianzhen.
