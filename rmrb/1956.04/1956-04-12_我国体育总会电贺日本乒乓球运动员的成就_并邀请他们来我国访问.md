Title: All-China Sports Federation Cables Congratulations to Japanese Table Tennis Players for Their Achievements and Invites Them to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1956-04-12
Page: 4

    All-China Sports Federation Cables Congratulations to Japanese Table Tennis Players for Their Achievements
    and Invites Them to Visit China
    Xinhua News Agency, 11th -- The All-China Sports Federation today sent a telegram to the Japan Table Tennis Association, congratulating the Japanese table tennis players on their great achievements in the 23rd World Table Tennis Championships.
    Xinhua News Agency, 11th -- The All-China Sports Federation has invited the Japanese men's and women's table tennis teams to visit China. This was proposed by the All-China Sports Federation in a telegram sent today to Rong Gaotang, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, who is currently in Japan. It also requested Rong Gaotang to propose to the Japan Table Tennis Association that they invite the Japanese men's and women's table tennis teams to visit China.

### 我国体育总会电贺日本乒乓球运动员的成就  并邀请他们来我国访问

1956-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

    我国体育总会电贺日本乒乓球运动员的成就
    并邀请他们来我国访问
    据新华社11日讯　中华全国体育总会今天打电报给日本乒乓球协会，祝贺日本乒乓球运动员在第二十三届世界乒乓球锦标赛中所获得的巨大成就。
    据新华社11日讯　中华全国体育总会邀请日本男、女乒乓球队来中国访问。这是中华全国体育总会今天给正在日本的中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠的电报中提出的。并且请荣高棠向日本乒乓球协会提出，邀请日本男、女乒乓球队来中国访问。
