Title: Representatives from Various Regions of Tibet Arrive in Lhasa to Attend the Inaugural Meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1956-04-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 16th. Representatives from various regions of Tibet who are to attend the inaugural meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region have begun to register with the Secretariat of the inaugural meeting. As of yesterday, most of the members of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region from the Tibetan local government, the Panchen Kampotse Conference Hall Committee, and the Qamdo (Changdu) People's Liberation Committee, as well as observers to the meeting, have completed their registration. Among those who have already registered are also Living Buddhas and Khenpos from major monasteries in Lhasa, Xigaze (Rikaze), Qamdo and other regions. They have all been warmly received by the Tibetan and Han staff of the Secretariat of the meeting.

In order to participate in this historically significant gathering of the Tibetan people, many Tibetan representatives of various dzongs (districts), shikas (manorial estates), patriotic organizations and various religious sects in Tibet have come to Lhasa for the first time.

### 参加自治区筹委会成立大会  西藏各地代表到拉萨

1956-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    参加自治区筹委会成立大会
    西藏各地代表到拉萨
    据新华社拉萨16日电　前来拉萨参加西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会的西藏各地代表，已经开始向成立大会秘书处报到。到昨天为止，西藏地方政府、班禅堪布会议厅委员会、昌都地区人民解放委员会参加西藏自治区筹备委员会的大部分委员和大会的列席人员，都已经完成报到手续。在已经报到的人员中，还有拉萨、日喀则、昌都等地区各大寺院的活佛和堪布。他们都受到了大会秘书处的藏、汉族工作人员的热情接待。
    为了参加西藏人民这个有历史意义的集会，许多藏族人士代表各地的宗、溪卡、爱国团体和西藏的各个教派第一次来到拉萨。
