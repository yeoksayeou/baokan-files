Title: All-China Association for Science and Technology Holds Symposium to Welcome Soviet Academician Oparin
Author:
Date: 1956-04-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th: The All-China Association for Science and Technology held a symposium in Beijing this afternoon to welcome Academician Oparin, Chairman of the All-Union Society for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge of the Soviet Union, who is in China to attend the World Federation of Scientific Workers meeting.

茅以升, Vice Chairman of the All-China Association for Science and Technology, read a welcome address at the symposium from 梁希, Chairman of the All-China Association for Science and Technology, to Oparin. Liang Xi was unable to attend the symposium due to illness.

Academician Oparin then conveyed the greetings of the All-Union Society for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge to their Chinese counterparts present at the meeting. This veteran scientist, enthusiastic about popularizing political and scientific knowledge, introduced the work of disseminating political and scientific knowledge in the Soviet Union.

袁翰青, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Association for Science and Technology, introduced the organizational structure and leadership of the All-China Association for Science and Technology to Academician Oparin. He raised some questions about improving the work of popularizing science with Oparin.

Finally, Oparin answered many questions raised by Yuan Hanqing and other science popularization workers at the meeting, including the promotion of agricultural mechanization knowledge and medical knowledge.

### 全国科普协会举行座谈会欢迎苏联奥巴林院士

1956-04-05
第2版()
专栏：

    全国科普协会举行座谈会欢迎苏联奥巴林院士
    据新华社4日讯　中华全国科学技术普及协会今天下午在北京举行座谈会，欢迎来中国参加世界科协会议的全苏政治与科学知识普及协会主席奥巴林院士。
    全国科学技术普及协会副主席茅以升在座谈会上宣读了全国科学技术普及协会主席梁希对奥巴林的欢迎辞。梁希因为身体不好不能出席这次座谈会。
    奥巴林院士接着向到会的中国科学工作者转达了全苏政治与科学知识普及协会对他们的中国同行们的致意。这位热心普及政治和科学知识的老科学家介绍了苏联政治与科学知识普及工作的情况。
    全国科学技术普及协会副秘书长袁翰青向奥巴林院士介绍了全国科学技术普及协会的组织机构和组织领导的情况。他向奥巴林提出了一些关于改进科学普及工作的问题。
    最后，奥巴林解答了袁翰青以及会上的其他科学普及工作者提出的许多问题，包括农业机械化知识和医学知识的宣传等。
