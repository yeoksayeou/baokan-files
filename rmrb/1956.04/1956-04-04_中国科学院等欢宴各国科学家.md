Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences and Others Host Banquet for Scientists from Various Countries
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd: The Chinese Academy of Sciences, the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, and the All-China Science and Technology Popularization Association jointly hosted a banquet in Beijing tonight to welcome World Federation of Scientific Workers Vice-Presidents Bernal and Oparin, Secretary-General Biquard, and more than twenty council members and other distinguished guests from fourteen countries. Among the hosts attending the banquet were Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Li Siguang, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Scientific Societies, Liang Xi, Chairman of the All-China Science Popularization Association, and other prominent Chinese scientists in Beijing.

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd: World Federation of Scientific Workers Honorary Treasurer Wooster and Honorary Secretary Málek have been in extensive contact with Chinese scientists in recent days.

British Dr. Wooster delivered eight academic lectures on different topics at the Institute of Applied Physics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Peking University, respectively. The content of these lectures focused on his research insights and the latest achievements in crystallography.

Czechoslovakian Academician Málek gave two lectures to Chinese scientists, comprehensively introducing Czechoslovakia's new achievements in microbiology.

### 中国科学院等欢宴各国科学家

1956-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中国科学院等欢宴各国科学家
    新华社2日讯　中国科学院、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会和中华全国科学技术普及协会，今晚在北京联合设宴欢迎世界科学工作者协会副主席鲍威尔、奥巴林，秘书长比加，以及理事等二十多位来自十四个国家的贵宾。出席宴会的主人有中国科学院院长郭沫若、全国科联主席李四光、全国科普协会主席梁希以及在北京的中国著名科学家等。
    据新华社2日讯　世界科协名誉会计伍斯特和名誉秘书马列克连日来同中国科学界进行了广泛的接触。
    英国伍斯特博士在中国科学院应用物理研究所和北京大学分别作了八次不同题目的学术讲演，内容都是他的研究心得和结晶学的最新成就。
    捷克斯洛伐克马列克院士向中国科学家作了两次讲演，全面介绍了捷克斯洛伐克微生物学方面的新成就。
