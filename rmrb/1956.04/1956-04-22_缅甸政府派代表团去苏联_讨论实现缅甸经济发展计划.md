Title: The Burmese Government Sends a Delegation to the Soviet Union to Discuss Implementing Burma's Economic Development Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-04-22
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 19th, TASS reported: According to an official announcement here, the Burmese government has appointed a seven-member delegation scheduled to depart for the Soviet Union on April 24th to discuss with the Soviet government certain draft plans for Burma's agricultural, industrial, and housing construction development.

### 缅甸政府派代表团去苏联  讨论实现缅甸经济发展计划

1956-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸政府派代表团去苏联
    讨论实现缅甸经济发展计划
    新华社21日讯  塔斯社仰光十九日讯：据这里官方宣布，缅甸政府已经指派一个七人代表团，预定在四月二十四日动身去苏联同苏联政府讨论实现缅甸发展农业、工业和住宅建设的某些计划草案。
