Title: Albanian Labor Party Central Committee Passes Draft Directives for New Five-Year Plan and Draft Party Constitution
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-12
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 11th: Tirana dispatch: Albanian newspapers reported on April 10th that the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party held a plenary session from April 5th to 7th. The plenary session discussed and passed the draft directives of the Albanian Labor Party's Third Congress regarding the Albanian second five-year plan for the development of the national economy from 1956 to 1960, as well as the draft constitution of the Albanian Labor Party.
    The draft directives and the draft party constitution will be submitted to the Third Party Congress to be held in May this year for approval.
    The plenary session also decided to submit the draft directives of the second five-year plan and the draft constitution of the Albanian Labor Party to the party and the masses for discussion.

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会  通过新五年计划指示草案和党章草案

1956-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会
    通过新五年计划指示草案和党章草案
    据新华社布拉格11日电　地拉那消息：阿尔巴尼亚报纸四月十日报道，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会在四月五日到七日举行了全体会议。全体会议讨论并且通过了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第三次代表大会关于1956年到1960年的阿尔巴尼亚第二个发展国民经济五年计划的指示草案，以及阿尔巴尼亚劳动党党章草案。
    指示草案和党章草案将提交在今年五月间举行的第三次党代表大会批准。
    全体会议还决定把第二个五年计划的指示草案和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党党章草案交由党和群众讨论。
