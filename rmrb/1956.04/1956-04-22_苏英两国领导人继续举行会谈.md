Title: Soviet and British Leaders Continue Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-04-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, April 21st, TASS dispatch: The British Foreign Office issued a joint communiqué on April 20th regarding the talks between Soviet Premier Nikolai Bulganin and member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Nikita Khrushchev with British Prime Minister Anthony Eden and other British leaders. The communiqué stated that the leaders of Britain and the Soviet Union held three meetings at 10 Downing Street on April 19th and April 20th. The two sides exchanged opinions frankly and beneficially in a cordial atmosphere.

The first meeting, held on the afternoon of April 19th, discussed issues related to Anglo-Soviet relations. In particular, the issue of cultural and other contacts between the two countries was discussed. In this regard, representatives from both sides were instructed to consider and put forward various suggestions for further study. In addition, a preliminary exchange of views on the European situation took place.

The second meeting was held on the morning of April 20th. This meeting was devoted to discussing the Middle East issue. It was unanimously agreed that representatives from both sides should prepare appropriate recommendations based on this discussion for further consideration.

The third meeting was held on the afternoon of April 20th. During this meeting, views were exchanged on the issue of disarmament.

### 苏英两国领导人继续举行会谈

1956-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    苏英两国领导人继续举行会谈
    据新华社21日讯  据塔斯社伦敦二十一日讯：英国外交部四月二十日就苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫同英国首相艾登和其他英国领导人员的会谈发表了一个双方一致同意的公报。公报说，在四月十九日和四月二十日，英国和苏联两国的领导人员在唐宁街十号举行了三次会谈。双方在融洽的气氛中坦率地和有益地交换了意见。
    四月十九日下午举行的第一次会谈讨论了有关英苏关系的问题。特别讨论了两国之间的文化方面和其他方面的接触问题。在这方面，双方代表奉命考虑和提出各种建议以供进一步研究。此外还就欧洲局势初步交换了意见。
    第二次会谈在四月二十日上午举行。这次会谈专门讨论中东问题。会上一致同意双方代表应当根据这次讨论准备适当的建议以供进一步的考虑。
    第三次会谈在四月二十日下午举行。在这次会谈中就裁军问题交换了意见。
