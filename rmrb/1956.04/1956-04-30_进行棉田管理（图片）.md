Cotton Field Management
    In order to increase cotton production, the Qianfeng Agricultural Production Cooperative in Shehong County, Sichuan Province, created an advanced "square grid seedling raising" method, which advanced the maturity of cotton and ensured the quality and yield of cotton. This is the work of the co-op members managing the seedlings in the field.

### 进行棉田管理（图片）
甘洪广
1956-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    进行棉田管理
    四川省射洪县前锋农业生产合作社，为了增产更多的棉花，创造了“方格育苗”的先进方法，提早了棉花的成熟期，保证了棉花的质量和产量。这是社员们正在田间作育苗的管理工作。
                                  甘洪广摄（四川日报社稿）
