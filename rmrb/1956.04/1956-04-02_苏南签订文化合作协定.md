Title: Soviet-Yugoslavia Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-04-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, March 31st: According to TASS, the negotiations between the delegation of the Government of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia and the delegation of the Soviet Government in Belgrade on drafting a cultural cooperation agreement between the two countries have ended.

The negotiations took place in an atmosphere of friendship and mutual understanding. As a result of the negotiations, the two sides agreed on the agreement and held a provisional signing ceremony on the 30th. The official signing ceremony will be held in Moscow soon.

The agreement stipulates the development of cultural ties and mutual contact between workers in science, culture, education, and sports between the two countries to promote mutual understanding and exchange of experience. The agreement also stipulates the mutual sending of university students, experts, and scientific workers to each other's countries to study, research, and conduct scientific research.

### 苏南签订文化合作协定

1956-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    苏南签订文化合作协定
    新华社31日讯　据塔斯社贝尔格莱德三十日讯：南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国政府代表团和苏联政府代表团在贝尔格莱德就拟订两国文化合作协定举行的谈判，已经结束。
    谈判是在友好和互相谅解的气氛中进行的。谈判结果，双方商定了协定，并且在三十日举行了协定的临时签字仪式。协定的正式签字仪式不久将在莫斯科举行。
    协定规定，在两国之间发展文化联系和科学、文化、教育、体育工作者的互相接触，以促进相互了解和交流经验。协定还规定互派大学生、专家和科学工作人员到对方国家学习、研究专业和进行科学研究工作。
