Title: Delegation of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 4

The seventeen-member cultural delegation of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 29th. The delegation consists of prominent figures from all walks of life in India. The head of the delegation, P. V. Bapat, is a professor of Pali and Buddhism at the University of Pune, and the deputy head, B. S. Guha, is a professor of applied chemistry at the University of Calcutta and a member of the National Council of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association. Members include scientists, writers, educators, lawyers, doctors, and social welfare workers. Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, hosted a banquet for the delegation that evening.

### 印中友协文化代表团到京

1956-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    印中友协文化代表团到京
    印中友好协会文化代表团一行十七人在二十九日下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团由印度各界著名人士组成，团长普·维·巴帕特是浦那大学的巴里文和佛学教授，副团长博·斯·古哈是加尔各答大学应用化学教授、印中友好协会全国理事会理事。团员中有科学家、文学家、教育家、律师、医生和社会福利工作者。中印友好协会会长丁西林当晚欢宴代表团。
