Title: National Advanced Producers' Representatives Conference Ready
Author:
Date: 1956-04-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: Preparations for the National Advanced Producers' Representatives Conference have been fully completed. Dong Xin, Secretary-General of the conference's preparatory committee, announced the above situation at the second meeting of the preparatory committee this morning. He also reported to the preparatory committee members present on the selection of advanced producer representatives in various regions and industries, and explained the conference's agenda, the publicity work during the conference, and matters needing attention during the conference.

Li Fuchun, Chairman of the National Advanced Producers' Representatives Conference Preparatory Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, spoke at today's meeting. He said that the National Advanced Producers' Representatives Conference is a national conference, and also a grand conference that has never been held in Chinese history. In order to make this conference achieve the goal of mobilizing the working class nationwide, outstanding figures from all fields and all nationalities, driving the backward, further promoting the high tide of socialist competition, and striving to complete the first five-year plan ahead of schedule, all units participating in this conference and all staff members must make the greatest efforts to ensure that the conference is successful.

### 全国先进生产者代表会议准备就绪

1956-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    全国先进生产者代表会议准备就绪
    新华社28日讯  全国先进生产者代表会议的各项准备工作已经全部完成。这个会议的筹备委员会秘书长董昕，在今天上午举行的筹备委员会第二次会议上宣布了上述的情况后，还向到会的筹备委员们报告了各个地区、各产业评选先进生产者代表的情形，同时说明了会议的日程、大会期间的宣传工作以及会议中应该注意的事项。
    全国先进生产者代表会议筹备委员会主任委员，国务院副总理李富春，在今天的会议上讲了话。他说，全国先进生产者代表会议，是一个全国性的会议，也是中国历史上从来没有开过的一次盛大的会议。为了使这次会议达到动员全国工人阶级、各方面和各民族的优秀人物，带动落后，进一步推进社会主义竞赛的高涨，争取提前完成第一个五年计划，所有参加这次会议的单位和全体工作人员，都必须尽最大的努力，保证把会议开好。
