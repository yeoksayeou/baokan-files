Title: King of Jordan Visits Syria
Author:
Date: 1956-04-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, April 10th - Damascus dispatch: King Hussein of Jordan arrived in Damascus on April 9th at the invitation of Syrian President Kuwatly for a three-day official visit.

It is reported that King Hussein and President Kuwatly began talks on April 9th on the following issues: the issue of Arab countries providing financial assistance to Jordan, the issue of convening a summit of Arab countries, the issue of concluding a military agreement between Syria and Jordan, the issue of sending Syrian military experts to Jordan to replace British officers, and other issues. Syrian Prime Minister Ghazi also participated in the talks.

### 约旦国王访问叙利亚

1956-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

    约旦国王访问叙利亚
    据新华社11日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特十日讯：大马士革消息：约旦国王侯赛尼应叙利亚总统库阿特利的邀请在四月九日到达大马士革，进行三天的正式访问。
    有消息说，侯赛尼国王和库阿特利总统在四月九日开始就下列问题举行会谈：阿拉伯国家将给予约旦以财政援助的问题，召开阿拉伯国家首脑会议的问题，叙利亚和约旦之间缔结一项军事协定的问题，派遣叙利亚军事专家前往约旦代替英国军官的问题以及其他一些问题。叙利亚总理加齐也参加了这次会谈。
