Title: Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee Plenary Session Passes Resolution on Resolutions of the CPSU Congress and Other Issues
Author:
Date: 1956-04-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 1st—According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency: The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, at its plenary session held from March 29th to March 30th, discussed the report by the First Secretary of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, Novotny, regarding the 20th Congress of the CPSU and what conclusions can be drawn from this Congress for the work of the Czechoslovak Communist Party. The meeting unanimously adopted a corresponding resolution on the above report.

Subsequently, the Central Committee discussed the report by the Minister of Domestic Trade, Krajcir, on measures to further improve the living standards of the working people, and adopted the draft resolution proposed by the Party Central Committee and the government on reducing retail prices of consumer goods and adjusting the wages and salaries of some employees.

In order to implement a wage policy based on the quantity, quality, and social importance of work, the Party Central Committee and the government also decided to adjust the wages of some workers and staff in the second and third quarters of this year.

### 捷共中央全会就苏共代表大会决议等问题通过决议

1956-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    捷共中央全会就苏共代表大会决议等问题通过决议
    据新华社布拉格1日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道：捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会在三月二十九日到三月三十日举行的全体会议上，讨论了捷共中央第一书记诺沃提尼关于苏共第二十次代表大会以及从这次代表大会对捷克斯洛伐克共产党的工作可以得出些什么结论的报告。会议一致就上述报告通过了相应的决议。
    接着，中央委员会讨论了国内贸易部部长克拉伊希尔的关于进一步提高劳动人民生活水平的措施的报告，并且通过了党中央委员会和政府所提出的关于降低消费品零售价格和调整某些职工的工资和薪金的决议草案。
    为了实行以工作的数量、质量和社会重要性为标准的工资政策，党中央委员会和政府还决定在今年第二季和第三季内调整一些工人和职员的工资。
