Title: Bulganin and Khrushchev Leave Kaliningrad for England
Author:
Date: 1956-04-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaliningrad, April 16th: According to TASS, Bulganin and Khrushchev arrived in Kaliningrad by special train at a little after 2:00 PM on April 15th, and departed for England at 5:00 PM the same afternoon aboard the cruiser "Ordzhonikidze" for a visit.

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫离开加里宁格勒前往英国

1956-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫离开加里宁格勒前往英国
    据新华社16日讯　据塔斯社加里宁格勒讯：布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫在四月十五日下午两点多钟乘专车到达加里宁格勒，并且在当天下午五点钟乘“奥尔忠尼启则号”巡洋舰赴英国访问。
