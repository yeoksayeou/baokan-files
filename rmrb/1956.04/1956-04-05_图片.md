Author:

Date: 1956-04-05
Page: 1

The people building the First Tractor Manufacturing Plant have completed the first quarter's construction plan for three large-scale construction projects 22 days ahead of schedule. Now, they are actively adopting the Soviet advanced "integrated hoisting method" for construction, and the project progress has been accelerated by about 30%. This is the workshop building of the forging workshop under construction.
