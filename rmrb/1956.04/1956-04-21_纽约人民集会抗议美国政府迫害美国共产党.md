Title: New York Residents Rally to Protest US Government Persecution of the Communist Party USA
Author:
Date: 1956-04-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th — According to a report in the New York "Worker," more than two thousand residents of New York recently held a rally, under the auspices of the Emergency Civil Liberties Committee, to protest the U.S. government's violation of people's rights. Speakers at the meeting included the well-known black historian Du Bois, writer Lamont, and economist Walsh. They all condemned the U.S. government's persecution of the Communist Party USA and demanded the defense of democratic freedom. The meeting decided to launch a signature petition campaign demanding the repeal of the Smith Act, which the U.S. authorities specifically use to trample on civil rights.

### 纽约人民集会抗议美国政府迫害美国共产党

1956-04-21
第4版()
专栏：

    纽约人民集会抗议美国政府迫害美国共产党
    新华社20日讯  据纽约“工人日报”报道，在公民自由紧急委员会的主持下，纽约两千多居民最近举行了一次集会，抗议美国政府对人民权利的侵犯。在这个会议上发言的有著名黑人历史学家杜波依斯、作家拉蒙特和经济学家华尔希等人。他们都指责美国政府对美国共产党进行迫害，并且要求维护民主自由。会议决定展开一个签名请愿运动，要求废除美国当局专门利用来蹂躏民权的史密斯法。
