Title: Philippine House Foreign Affairs Committee Chairman Opposes US Military Bases in the Philippines
Author:
Date: 1956-04-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 6th: According to a France-Presse report on the 6th, the Chairman of the Philippine House Foreign Affairs Committee, Cuenco, said that US bases in the Philippines cannot provide real protection, but are military targets and will invite nuclear attacks.

He also said, "The Philippines does not have its own problems with Russia and Communist China that would make a war between the Philippines and either of these countries necessary." Cuenco pointed out that the Japanese government had opposed the United States storing atomic and hydrogen bombs in Japan. He called on the Philippine government to emulate this "wise example."

Agence France-Presse reported that Cuenco also advocated taking back all currently unused US bases in the Philippines and shortening the 99-year lease term for the bases.

### 菲众议院外交委员会主席反对美国在菲设立军事基地

1956-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    菲众议院外交委员会主席反对美国在菲设立军事基地
    新华社6日讯　据法新社六日报道：菲律宾众议院外交委员会主席库恩科说，美国在菲律宾的基地不能提供真正的保护，反而是军事目标，并且将招致核子攻击。
    他又说，“菲律宾同俄国和共产党中国之间并没有它们自己的问题，可以使得菲律宾同其中任何一国之间的战争成为必要。”库恩科指出，日本政府曾经反对美国在日本储存原子弹和氢弹。他要求菲律宾政府仿效这个“明智的榜样”。
    法新社报道，库恩科还主张收回美国在菲律宾的所有目前没有使用的基地，并且主张把九十九年的基地租借期限加以缩短。
