Title: Moroccan Prime Minister Bekkay's Reply to Premier Zhou

Author: 
Date: 1956-04-19
Page: 1

Moroccan Prime Minister Bekkay's Reply to Premier Zhou
To Mr. Zhou Enlai, Premier of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

I am deeply moved by the noble sentiments you have expressed towards the people of our country. I warmly express my gratitude to you and hope that independence will help us to support all just causes for the realization of the lofty human ideals of peace, fraternity and progress, and for the establishment of fruitful relations between our two countries.
                                 Bekkay, Prime Minister of Morocco
                               Rabat, April 17th

### 摩洛哥首相贝凯给周总理的复电

1956-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥首相贝凯给周总理的复电北京中华人民共和国总理周恩来先生：
    您对我国人民所表示的高贵情谊使我深为感动。我热烈地向您表示感谢，并希望独立将有助于我们支持一切为了实现和平、博爱与进步这一人类崇高理想和建立我们两国间富有成果的关系的正义事业。
                                 摩洛哥首相　贝　凯
                               四月十七日于拉巴特
