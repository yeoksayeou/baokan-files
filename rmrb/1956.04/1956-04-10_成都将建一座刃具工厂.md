Title: Chengdu to Build a Cutting Tool Factory
Author:
Date: 1956-04-10
Page: 2

Chengdu to Build a Cutting Tool Factory

    A cutting tool factory will soon begin construction in Chengdu. Currently, the site selection work has been basically completed. Construction preparations are actively underway.

    This factory is the second modern tool manufacturing factory in China after the Harbin Measuring & Cutting Tool Plant. The products of this factory include 49 types of cutting tools alone, with 2,086 specifications. The general precision of the measuring tools is equivalent to one one-hundred-and-fortieth of the thickness of a hair; the cutting tools include the smallest drill bits, equivalent to three and a half hairs thick.

    The basic construction project of this factory will largely reuse the complete set of Soviet designs from the Harbin Measuring & Cutting Tool Plant. At the same time, it will draw on the construction experience of the Harbin Measuring & Cutting Tool Plant, so the construction progress will be very rapid.
            (Sichuan Daily Editorial Department)

### 成都将建一座刃具工厂

1956-04-10
第2版()
专栏：

    成都将建一座刃具工厂
    一座刃具工厂即将在成都动工兴建。目前，选厂址工作已经基本结束。施工准备工作正在积极进行。
    这座工厂是继哈尔滨量具刃具厂之后的我国第二座现代化工具制造工厂。这座工厂的产品仅刃具部分就有四十九种，2，086种规格。量具的一般精密度相当于一根头发粗细的一百四十分之一；刃具中有相当于三根半头发粗的最小的钻头。
    这座工厂的基本建设工程，绝大部分是重复使用哈尔滨量具刃具厂的全套苏联设计，同时要吸取哈尔滨量具刃具厂的建厂经验，因此建设进度将很迅速。
            （四川日报编辑部）
