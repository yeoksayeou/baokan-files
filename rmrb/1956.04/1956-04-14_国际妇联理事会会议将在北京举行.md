Title: International Women's Federation Council Meeting to be Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 12th. The Secretariat of the Women's International Democratic Federation (WIDF) issued a communiqué stating that the WIDF Council meeting will be held in Beijing from April 24th to 28th.

The communiqué said that the national organizations of the WIDF and the organizations cooperating with it are making final preparations to give the meeting worldwide representation. Council members of the WIDF from all over the world and prominent guests will attend the meeting, and the participation of representatives from Asian countries will be of particular importance.

The meeting agenda will include: an opening address by Eugénie Cotton, President of the WIDF, which will discuss the WIDF's contributions to the establishment and solidarity of the women's movement on the occasion of its tenth anniversary. Secretary-General Angela Minella will deliver the main report, which will address the tasks of the WIDF in launching more vigorous actions by women in the struggle for peace, for their rights, and for the happiness of children.

An Indian representative will give a special report on the aspirations and actions of women in Asia and Africa. The All-China Women's Federation has invited the council members to visit China, and the guests will have the opportunity to study the achievements of Chinese women and their contributions to the country's political, economic, and social life in a position of full equality.

### 国际妇联理事会会议将在北京举行

1956-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    国际妇联理事会会议将在北京举行
    新华社柏林12日电　国际民主妇女联合会书记处发表公报说，国际民主妇女联合会理事会会议将在四月二十四日到二十八日在北京举行。
    公报说，国际民主妇女联合会的各国组织和同它合作的各组织正在作最后的准备来使会议具有世界范围的代表性。出席会议的将有从世界各地来的国际民主妇女联合会理事会理事和著名的来宾，亚洲国家代表的参加将具有特别重要的意义。
    会议议程将包括：由国际民主妇女联合会主席欧仁妮·戈登致开会辞，开会辞将谈到国际民主妇女联合会在成立十周年的时候对建立和团结妇女运动方面的贡献。总书记安乔拉·米奈拉将作主要报告，报告将谈到国际民主妇女联合会在展开妇女们在争取和平、争取她们的权利和争取儿童的幸福方面的更有力的行动的任务。
    一位印度代表将作关于亚洲和非洲的妇女的愿望和行动的专门报告。中华全国民主妇女联合会已经邀请理事会理事们在中国访问，客人们将有机会研究中国妇女的成就和她们在完全平等的地位上对于国家政治、经济和社会生活所作的贡献。
