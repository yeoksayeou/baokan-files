Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd: Vice Chairman Zhu De of the People's Republic of China returned to Beijing by train from Ulaanbaatar this evening.

Those who welcomed him at the station included Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council; Shen Junru and Peng Zhen, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Peng Dehuai and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Nie Rongzhen and Long Yun, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Luo Ruiqing, Minister of Public Security; Teng Daiyuan, Minister of Railways; Zhang Wentian and Ji Pengfei, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; Huang Kecheng and Tan Zheng, Vice Ministers of National Defense; senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Chen Geng, General Xu Guangda, General Li Kenong, General Gan Siqi, Air Force General Liu Yalou, General Song Renqiong, General Yang Chengwu, Navy General Wang Hongkun, General Hong Xuezhi, members and alternate members of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and heads of various departments Tan Zhenlin, Wang Jiaxiang, Ma Mingfang, Liu Lantao, Yang Shangkun, Li Xuefeng, Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Hu Yaobang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League; Zeng Xianzhi, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Feng Jiping, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; Liu Ren, Second Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, as well as more than 1,000 people from all walks of life in the capital.

Also welcoming him at the station were Romanian Ambassador Guo Zuowen, Soviet Ambassador Yudin, Hungarian Ambassador Szkla'dan, Czechoslovak Ambassador Gregor, Polish Chargé d'affaires Maiman, German Democratic Republic Chargé d'affaires Everhartz, and Mongolian People's Republic Chargé d'affaires Khosbayar.

After Vice Chairman Zhu De stepped off the train, he accepted flowers presented by the children of Beijing, and the station was filled with sustained applause and cheers. (Image attached)

### 朱德副主席回到北京

1956-04-03
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席回到北京
    新华社2日讯　中华人民共和国副主席朱德在今天晚间由乌兰巴托乘火车回到北京。
    到车站欢迎的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒、彭真，国务院副总理彭德怀、邓小平，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，国防委员会副主席聂荣臻、龙云，国务院秘书长习仲勋，公安部长罗瑞卿，铁道部长滕代远，外交部副部长张闻天、姬鹏飞，国防部副部长黄克诚、谭政，中国人民解放军高级将领陈赓大将、许光达大将、李克农上将、甘泗琪上将、刘亚楼空军上将、宋任穷上将、杨成武上将、王宏坤海军上将、洪学智上将，中共中央委员和候补中央委员及各部门负责人谭震林、王稼祥、马明方、刘澜涛、杨尚昆、李雪峰，中华全国总工会主席赖若愚，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦，中华全国民主妇女联合会副秘书长曾宪植，北京市副市长冯基平，中共北京市委第二书记刘仁，以及首都各界人士一千多人。
    到车站欢迎的，还有罗马尼亚大使郭佐文，苏联大使尤金，匈牙利大使斯克拉丹，捷克斯洛伐克大使格里哥尔，波兰临时代办马伊曼，德意志民主共和国临时代办埃弗哈而茨和蒙古人民共和国临时代办浩斯巴雅尔。
    朱德副主席走下火车后，接受了北京市少年儿童的献花，车站上响起了经久不息的掌声和欢呼声。（附图片）
