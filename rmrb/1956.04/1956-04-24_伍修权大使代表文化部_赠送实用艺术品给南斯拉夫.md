Title: Ambassador Wu Xiuquan, on Behalf of the Ministry of Culture, Presents Practical Artworks to Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1956-04-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 23rd: Belgrade Dispatch: Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia, Wu Xiuquan, visited Yugoslav Federal Executive Council Vice-Chairman Kardelj on the afternoon of April 21st and informed him that the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China had presented the Chinese practical artworks recently exhibited in Belgrade to the Yugoslav government as a gift. Ambassador Wu Xiuquan expressed his firm belief that this gift will become a permanent memento of the Chinese people's friendly sentiments towards the Yugoslav people, and that it will promote understanding between the two peoples, especially in their cultural cooperation.

Kardelj, on behalf of the Federal Executive Council, expressed gratitude to the Chinese government, saying that this action will further promote the development of friendly relations and comprehensive cooperation between the Yugoslav and Chinese peoples.

### 伍修权大使代表文化部  赠送实用艺术品给南斯拉夫

1956-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    伍修权大使代表文化部
    赠送实用艺术品给南斯拉夫
    新华社布拉格23日电  贝尔格莱德消息：中国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权，四月二十一日下午访问了南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席卡德尔，并且告诉他说，中华人民共和国文化部已经把最近在贝尔格莱德展览的中国实用艺术品，作为礼物送给南斯拉夫政府。伍修权大使表示深信，这份礼物将成为中国人民对南斯拉夫人民的友好感情的永远纪念品，而且它将促进两国人民之间的谅解，特别是他们在文化方面的合作。
    卡德尔代表联邦执行委员会对中国政府表示感谢，他说，这个行动将进一步促进南斯拉夫和中国人民之间的友好关系和全面合作的发展。
