Title: Ceylon Parliamentary Election Second Vote Results Announced; People's United Front Wins Total of Forty-Two Seats
Author:
Date: 1956-04-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 9th: Colombo News: The results of the second vote in the election for members of the House of Representatives of the Ceylon Parliament, announced on the evening of April 8th, show that the number of seats won by the opposition People's United Front has increased to forty-two. The ruling United National Party still only holds eight seats. In addition, the New Lanka Sama Samaja Party won eleven seats, the Communist Party two seats, and independent candidates two seats.

In these two votes, the total number of seats won by the various opposition parties—the People's United Front, the New Lanka Sama Samaja Party, and the Communist Party—has reached fifty-five, exceeding half of the total of one hundred and one seats in the House of Representatives. The United National Party has lost its majority party status.

### 锡兰国会选举第二次投票揭晓  人民联合阵线共获四十二席

1956-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰国会选举第二次投票揭晓
    人民联合阵线共获四十二席
    据新华社新德里9日电　科伦坡消息：四月八日晚间揭晓的锡兰国会众议院议员选举第二次投票的结果表明，反对党人民联合阵线所获得的席位已经增加到四十二席。执政党统一国民党的席数仍然只是八席。除此以外，新平等社会党获得了十一席，共产党两席，无党派人士两席。
    在这两次投票中，各反对党——人民联合阵线、新平等社会党和共产党所取得的议席总数已达五十五席，超过了众议院全部一百零一席的半数。统一国民党已经丧失了多数党的地位。
