Title: Premier Zhou's Congratulatory Telegram to Ceylonese Prime Minister Bandaranaike
Author:
Date: 1956-04-17
Page: 1

    Premier Zhou's Congratulatory Telegram to Ceylonese Prime Minister Bandaranaike
    Colombo, Ceylon
    To His Excellency Prime Minister Bandaranaike:

    I am honored to extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people on your assumption of the esteemed office of Prime Minister of Ceylon.

    There has always been a traditional friendship between the people of China and the people of Ceylon. Our two countries have established and developed good trade relations on the basis of equality and mutual benefit. I believe that with our joint efforts, the friendly relations between our two countries will be further developed.

    I sincerely hope that the people of Ceylon will achieve success in their cause of maintaining independence and building a happy life. I wish your country growing prosperity.

          Zhou Enlai
          Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
          April 14, 1956, Beijing

### 周总理给锡兰总理班达拉奈克的贺电

1956-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理给锡兰总理班达拉奈克的贺电锡兰科伦坡尊敬的班达拉奈克总理阁下：
    当阁下荣任锡兰政府总理的时候，我荣幸地代表中华人民共和国政府和中国人民向阁下致热烈的祝贺。
    中国人民和锡兰人民之间，一向存在着传统的友谊。我们两国已在平等互利的基础上，建立和发展了良好的贸易关系。我相信在我们的共同努力下，我们两国的友好关系将得到进一步的发展。
    我衷心希望锡兰人民在维护独立和建设幸福生活的事业中获得成就。祝你们的国家日益繁荣。
          中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来
            　1956年4月14日于北京
