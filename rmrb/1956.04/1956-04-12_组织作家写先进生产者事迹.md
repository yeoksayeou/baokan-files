Title: Organizing Writers to Write About the Deeds of Advanced Producers
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-12
Page: 3

    Organizing Writers to Write About the Deeds of Advanced Producers
    The All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Chinese Writers Association have jointly organized 106 writers and young literary creators to participate in the publicity work for the upcoming National Conference of Advanced Producers Representatives. They will use features, communications, and other literary forms to introduce the deeds of advanced producers to the people of the whole country.
    Among the young literary creators participating in this publicity work, some are of worker origin.
    Currently, some writers have already begun interviews, and many young literary creators have gone to factories in various places to collect materials. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 组织作家写先进生产者事迹

1956-04-12
第3版()
专栏：

    组织作家写先进生产者事迹
    中华全国总工会和中国作家协会共同组织了106个作家和青年文学创作者参加即将举行的全国先进生产者代表会议的宣传工作，他们将用特写、通讯等文艺形式，把先进生产者的事迹介绍给全国人民。
    这次参加宣传工作的青年文学创作者中，有些人是工人出身的。
    目前，有的作家已经开始采访活动，许多青年文学创作者分别到各地工厂搜集材料。　　　（新华社）
