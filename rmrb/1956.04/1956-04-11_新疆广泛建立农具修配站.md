Title: Xinjiang Widely Establishes Farm Tool Repair Stations
Author:
Date: 1956-04-11
Page: 2
Xinjiang is widely establishing farm tool repair stations

    Fifty counties in Xinjiang's agricultural areas are establishing one hundred and one farm tool repair stations. The staff of these repair stations are all members of local handicraft production cooperatives (groups) who are skilled in repair work. These repair stations will adopt the method of using fixed stations and dispatching mobile repair teams for巡回services to help farmers repair various farm tools in a timely manner.
        (Xinjiang Daily Editorial Department)

### 新疆广泛建立农具修配站

1956-04-11
第2版()
专栏：

    新疆广泛建立农具修配站
    新疆农业区的五十个县正在建立一百零一个农具修配站。这些修配站的工作人员，都是当地手工业生产合作社（组）中长于修配工作的社员或组员。这些修配站将采用固定站、址派出流动修配小组巡回服务的方法，帮助农民及时修理各种农具。
              （新疆日报编辑部）
