Title: Burmese Government Delegation Departs for the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-04-27
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 25th: A seven-member delegation appointed by the Burmese government, headed by Financial Commissioner U Ba Swe, departed for the Soviet Union on April 24th. This delegation will discuss with the Soviet government issues related to the implementation of certain draft plans for developing agriculture, industry, and housing construction in Burma.

　　The projects to be discussed will also include the construction of a hotel for receiving foreign guests, a conference hall, and a stadium in Rangoon.

### 缅甸政府代表团启程赴苏

1956-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸政府代表团启程赴苏
    新华社仰光25日电  一个由缅甸政府委任的以财政专员吴巴泰为首的七人代表团，四月二十四日启程前往苏联。这个代表团将同苏联政府讨论有关实现缅甸发展农业、工业和住宅建设的某些计划草案的问题。
    在讨论的项目中还将包括在仰光建筑一个招待外宾用的旅馆、一个会议厅和一个运动场的问题。
