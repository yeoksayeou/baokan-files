Title: Premier Zhou Makes a Report to Young Literary Creators and Others
Author:
Date: 1956-04-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 31st: Premier Zhou Enlai gave a report this afternoon to all the representatives attending the National Youth Literary Creators Conference and the First National Drama Festival on cultivating and expanding the new forces in literary and art circles.

### 周总理向青年文学创作者等作报告

1956-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理向青年文学创作者等作报告
    新华社31日讯　周恩来总理今天下午向出席全国青年文学创作者会议和第一届全国话剧观摩演出会的全体代表，作了关于培养和扩大文艺界的新生力量的报告。
