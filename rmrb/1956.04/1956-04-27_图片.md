Title: Lhasa Launches Greening Campaign
Author:
Date: 1956-04-27
Page: 1

A greening campaign has been launched in Lhasa. Large tracts of wasteland along the Lhasa River are filled with people planting trees and making forests, hoping to make this ancient city on the frontier of the motherland even more beautiful. Staff members of the organizations directly under the CPC Tibet Work Committee are digging ditches and planting trees.
