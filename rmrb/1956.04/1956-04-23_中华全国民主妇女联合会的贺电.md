Title: Congratulatory Telegram from the All-China Democratic Women's Federation

Author: 
Date: 1956-04-23
Page: 3

Congratulatory Telegram from the All-China Democratic Women's Federation to the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region:

    On the occasion of the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, we extend our warm congratulations to you. We believe that under the leadership of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, in the united family of all nationalities of the motherland, the unity of the Tibetan people will be further strengthened, the construction undertakings of Tibet will be further developed, and the Tibetan people and women will obtain a happier life.
                            All-China Democratic Women's Federation
                                        April 22, 1956

### 中华全国民主妇女联合会的贺电

1956-04-23
第3版()
专栏：

    中华全国民主妇女联合会的贺电西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会：
    值此西藏自治区筹备委员会成立之际，谨向你们致以热烈的祝贺。我们相信在西藏自治区筹备委员会的领导下，在祖国各民族团结的大家庭里，西藏人民的团结将会进一步加强，西藏的建设事业将会进一步发展，西藏人民和妇女将会获得更加幸福的生活。
                            中华全国民主妇女联合会
                                        1956年4月22日
