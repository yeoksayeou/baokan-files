Title: Observation Performance by Advanced Producers (Picture)
Author: Yuan Ling
Date: 1956-04-19
Page: 2

Observation Performance by Advanced Producers

Some representatives attending the National Textile Industry Advanced Producers Representative Meeting conducted an observation performance at the Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory. The picture shows cotton planting expert Qu Yaoli and national textile industry advanced producers visiting the performance in the spinning workshop. Front row, from left to right: Bushare (Uyghur, spinning worker from Xinjiang July 1st Cotton Textile Factory), Qu Yaoli, Gao Shuzhen (spinning worker from State-owned Tianjin No. 3 Cotton Textile Factory), Ma Guizhen (spinning worker from Tianjin Beiyang Yarn Factory).
        Xinhua News Agency reporter Yuan Ling photographed

### 先进生产者的观摩表演（图片）
袁苓
1956-04-19
第2版()
专栏：

    先进生产者的观摩表演
    参加全国纺织工业先进生产者代表会议的部分代表，在北京第二棉纺织厂进行了观摩表演。图为在细纱车间参观表演的植棉能手曲耀离和全国纺织工业先进生产者们。前排自左至右：布沙热（维吾尔族，新疆七一棉纺织厂细纱工人）、曲耀离、高淑珍（国营天津第三棉纺织厂细纱工人）、马桂珍（天津北洋纱厂细纱工人）。
                新华社记者　袁苓摄
