Title: Central Delegation Visits Famous Monasteries in Lhasa, CCP Tibet Work Committee and Tibet Military Region Hold Banquet

Author: 
Date: 1956-04-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, April 27th: The renowned Drepung Monastery, Sera Monastery, and Upper and Lower Tantric Colleges of Lhasa today welcomed the central delegation with the most solemn religious ceremonies. When members of the central delegation arrived separately at these monasteries, monks held aloft Buddhist banners, incense, and flowers, lining up outside the monasteries to welcome them. When the delegation members entered the main scripture halls, the Buddhist bands played large and small horns in unison. Monks from each monastery then gathered inside and outside the scripture halls, chanting the "Jue De Ba" Buddhist scriptures in loud voices, wishing the delegation members auspiciousness and peace.

Today, the delegation distributed alms to over 10,000 lamas in these monasteries. Members of the delegation also visited the scripture halls and Buddha halls of each monastery, and the members who believed in Buddhism performed worship according to religious rituals.

The members of the delegation held cordial conversations with the Khenpos and Living Buddhas of each monastery.

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, April 27th: The CCP Tibet Work Committee and the Chinese People's Liberation Army Tibet Military Region jointly held a banquet today to welcome the central delegation and all members of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, as well as representatives from various parts of Tibet.

Zhang Guohua, Acting Secretary of the CCP Tibet Work Committee, and Vice Premier Chen Yi both spoke at the banquet.

### 中央代表团访问拉萨著名寺院  中共西藏工委和西藏军区举行欢宴会

1956-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中央代表团访问拉萨著名寺院
    中共西藏工委和西藏军区举行欢宴会
    据新华社拉萨27日电  拉萨市著名的哲蚌寺、色拉寺和上、下密院今天以最隆重的宗教仪式欢迎中央代表团去访问。当中央代表团团员分头到达这些寺院的时候，寺院的僧众举着佛幡、佛香和鲜花，列队在寺院外欢迎。代表团人员进入大经堂的时候，佛乐队齐奏大、小号。各寺的僧众随即聚集在经堂内外，高声诵念“觉得巴”佛经，祝代表团人员吉祥平安。
    代表团今天向这些寺院的一万多个喇嘛放了布施。代表团的团员们，还参观了各寺的经堂和佛殿，信仰佛教的团员都按照宗教仪式进行朝拜。
    代表团的团员们同各寺院的堪布、活佛进行了亲切的交谈。
    据新华社拉萨27日电  中共西藏工委和中国人民解放军西藏军区今天联合举行宴会，欢宴中央代表团和西藏自治区筹备委员会全体委员以及西藏各地代表。
    中共西藏工委代书记张国华和陈毅副总理都在宴会上讲了话。
