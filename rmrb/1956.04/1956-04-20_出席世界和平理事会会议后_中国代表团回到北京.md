Title: Chinese Delegation Returns to Beijing After Attending World Peace Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-04-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th – The Chinese delegation, headed by Chen Shutong, deputy heads Liu Ningyi, and members Cheng Qian, Shao Lizi, and Luo Longji, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon after attending the special meeting of the World Peace Council. The Vietnamese peace delegation, including Le Dinh Tham and Nguyen Xuan Thuy, also arrived in Beijing on the same plane.

### 出席世界和平理事会会议后  中国代表团回到北京

1956-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    出席世界和平理事会会议后
    中国代表团回到北京
    新华社19日讯　出席世界和平理事会特别会议的中国代表团团长陈叔通、副团长刘宁一、团员程潜、邵力子、罗隆基等在今日下午乘飞机回到北京。越南和平代表团黎廷探、阮春水等也同机到北京。
