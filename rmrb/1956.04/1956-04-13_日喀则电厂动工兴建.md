Title: Construction of Shigatse Power Plant Begins
Author:
Date: 1956-04-13
Page: 1

Construction of Shigatse Power Plant Begins

Construction of the Shigatse thermal power plant began on the 4th. The power plant's factory building, repair shop, material room and other buildings will be completed by the end of May, and the machinery will be installed in June. Most of the various materials needed to build the power plant have been transported to the construction site. The Shigatse thermal power plant is one of the projects decided by the State Council to help Tibet with its construction. After the power plant is completed, it can solve the problem of lighting electricity in Shigatse City. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 日喀则电厂动工兴建

1956-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    日喀则电厂动工兴建
    日喀则火力发电厂在四日动工兴建。电厂的厂房、修配间、材料间等建筑物将在五月底完工，六月份机器就要安装完毕。建筑电厂所需要的各种器材，大部分已经运到工地。日喀则火力发电厂是国务院决定帮助西藏建设的工程之一。电厂建成后可解决日喀则市的照明用电。（据新华社讯）
