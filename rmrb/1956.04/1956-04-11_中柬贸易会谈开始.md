Title: China-Cambodia Trade Talks Begin
Author:
Date: 1956-04-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 10th—The trade talks between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Royal Government of Cambodia began in Beijing on the 10th.

    The Chinese side participating in the talks included Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, heads of relevant bureaus, and representatives from the People's Bank of China.

    The Cambodian side participating in the talks included the head of the Cambodian Royal Economic Delegation to China, Minister of Engineering, Posts and Telecommunications, and Planning, Hu Sen A, and all members of the delegation.

### 中柬贸易会谈开始

1956-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    中柬贸易会谈开始
    新华社10日讯　中华人民共和国政府和柬埔寨王国政府的贸易会谈，十日在北京开始举行。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有对外贸易部副部长雷任民和各有关局的负责人以及人民银行的代表。
    柬埔寨方面参加会谈的，有柬埔寨王国访华经济代表团团长、工程邮电和计划部长胡森阿及代表团全体人员。
