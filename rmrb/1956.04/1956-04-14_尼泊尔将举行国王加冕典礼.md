Title: Nepal to Hold King's Coronation Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1956-04-14
Page: 4
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 13th: Kathmandu dispatch: Nepal will officially hold the King's coronation ceremony on May 2nd. King Mahendra Bir Bikram Shah Deva of Nepal will officially ascend the throne on this day.
　　The late King Tribhuvan Bir Bikram of Nepal passed away on March 12th of last year. Mahendra Bikram announced his succession to the throne on March 14th of the same year.

### 尼泊尔将举行国王加冕典礼

1956-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔将举行国王加冕典礼
    新华社新德里13日电　加德满都消息：尼泊尔将在五月二日正式举行国王加冕典礼。尼泊尔国王马亨德拉·比尔·比克拉姆·沙阿·德瓦将在这一天正式登位。
    故尼泊尔国王特里布文纳·比克拉姆是在去年三月十二日逝世的。马亨德拉·比克拉姆在同年三月十四日宣布继承王位。
