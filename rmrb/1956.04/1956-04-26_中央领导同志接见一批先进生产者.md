Title: Central Leaders Receive a Group of Advanced Producers
Author:
Date: 1956-04-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 25th—Chairman Mao Zedong and members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Peng Zhen, Dong Biwu, Peng Dehuai, Kang Sheng, and Deng Xiaoping received the representatives attending the First National Advanced Producers Representative Conference of the Second Ministry of Machine Building Industry, the Advanced Producers Representative Conference of the Ministry of Geology, the National Advanced Producers Representative Conference of the Electric Power Industry, and the National Advanced Producers Representative Conference of the Postal and Telecommunications Industry this afternoon.

### 中央领导同志接见一批先进生产者

1956-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中央领导同志接见一批先进生产者
    新华社25日讯  毛泽东主席和中共中央政治局委员刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、彭真、董必武、彭德怀、康生、邓小平，今天下午接见了出席全国第二机械工业第一次先进生产者代表会议、地质部先进生产者代表会议、全国电业先进生产者代表会议、全国邮电先进生产者代表会议的代表们。
