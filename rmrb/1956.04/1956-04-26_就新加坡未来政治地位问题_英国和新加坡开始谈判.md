Title: UK and Singapore Begin Negotiations on the Future Political Status of Singapore
Author:
Date: 1956-04-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, April 24th: British Colonial Secretary Lennox-Boyd and a delegation of representatives from all parties in the Singapore Legislative Assembly began negotiations in London on April 23rd regarding the future political status of Singapore. The Singapore delegation is demanding that Singapore achieve independence within the British Commonwealth by April 1st of next year. Singapore is currently a directly-administered British colony.

The communique issued after the meeting stated that the meeting considered a memorandum and a draft agreement project put forward by Singapore Chief Minister Marshall and discussed the various issues arising therefrom.

According to Western news agencies, Marshall warned Britain at the negotiation meeting not to permanently maintain the colonial system and the current constitution. He said that doing so "will intensify the deep-rooted hostility that subordinate people will naturally have." Marshall also stated that if Britain agrees to grant Singapore independence, it will allow Britain to continue to control Singapore's foreign defense and foreign affairs.

Speaking at the meeting, Lennox-Boyd particularly emphasized that Singapore is a commercial center in Southeast Asia and a "bulwark in the Western defense system." He declared that Britain's naval and air force bases in Singapore must be guaranteed. He also stressed that Britain needs to obtain affirmative guarantees that its bases will continue to be guaranteed even if the current Marshall coalition government falls.

According to Reuters, quoting people who attended the meeting, the negotiation meeting "immediately ran into difficulties on the crucial issue of control over Singapore's internal security."

### 就新加坡未来政治地位问题  英国和新加坡开始谈判

1956-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    就新加坡未来政治地位问题
    英国和新加坡开始谈判
    据新华社24日讯  伦敦消息：英国殖民大臣伦诺克斯—波伊德和新加坡立法议会各党派代表团四月二十三日在伦敦开始就新加坡的未来政治地位举行谈判。新加坡代表团要求在明年四月一日以前实行新加坡在英联邦范围内的独立。新加坡现在是英国直辖殖民地。
    会后发表的公报说，会议考虑了新加坡首席部长马歇尔提出的一项备忘录和协议项目草案，并且讨论了由此而产生的各项问题。
    据西方通讯社报道，马歇尔在谈判会议上警告英国不要把殖民制度和现行宪法永久保持下去。他说：这样“将使得附属国人民自然会有的根深蒂固的敌对情绪更加强烈”。马歇尔同时也表示，如果英国答应给予新加坡独立，将让英国继续掌握对新加坡的对外防御和外交事务的控制权。
    伦诺克斯—波伊德在会上讲话时，特别强调新加坡是东南亚的一个商业中心，并且是西方国家“防御体系中的一个堡垒”。他声明，英国在新加坡的海空军基地必须获得保障。他还强调，英国需要获得肯定的保证，保证万一目前的马歇尔联合政府下台后，英国的基地仍将获得保障。
    据路透社援引参加会议的人士的话说，在这个谈判会议上，立刻在关于新加坡“内部安全的控制权的关键问题上陷入困难”。
