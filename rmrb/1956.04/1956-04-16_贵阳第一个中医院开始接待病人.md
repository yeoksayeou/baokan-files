Title: Guiyang's First Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital Begins Accepting Patients
Author:
Date: 1956-04-16
Page: 3

    Guiyang's First Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital Begins Accepting Patients
    Guiyang's first Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) Hospital began receiving its first patients on the 14th.
    The hospital's construction began in January of this year. The entire hospital is divided into inpatient and outpatient sections, with more than 50 medical personnel. The hospital has 50 beds. The outpatient department can accommodate approximately 400 patients per day. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 贵阳第一个中医院开始接待病人

1956-04-16
第3版()
专栏：

    贵阳第一个中医院开始接待病人
    贵阳市的第一个中医医院十四日开始接待第一批病人。
    这座医院是从今年一月开始筹建的。全院分住院和门诊两部分，有医务人员五十多人。院内设有病床五十张。门诊部每天大约能容纳四百个病人看病。　（新华社）
