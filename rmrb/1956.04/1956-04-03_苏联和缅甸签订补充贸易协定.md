Title: Supplementary Trade Agreement Signed Between Soviet Union and Burma
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-03
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 2nd April - A communiqué on Soviet-Burmese trade relations was published here on April 1st.

　　The communiqué states that as a result of Soviet-Burmese negotiations, a supplementary trade agreement, a long-term protocol on the mutual provision of goods and services, and a number of documents determining the settlement methods between the two governments were signed on April 1st, with the participation of Mikoyan, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and U Nu, Prime Minister of the Union of Burma.

　　The communiqué states that the supplementary trade agreement extends the validity of the Soviet-Burmese trade agreement signed on July 1, 1955, to five years, in order to comprehensively develop and consolidate trade relations between the two countries on the basis of equality and mutual benefit. According to the protocol, Burma will export 400,000 tons of rice to the Soviet Union annually for four years, while the Soviet Union will provide the Union of Burma with equipment, machinery and other goods, and provide the Union of Burma with technical and other assistance.

　　The communiqué also states that if the Government of the Union of Burma so wishes, the Soviet Government will enable the national institutions of Burma and their agents and companies to send people to the Soviet Union to familiarize themselves with the machinery, equipment and other goods that the Union of Burma will purchase.

　　The communiqué states that the signing of the Soviet-Burmese supplementary trade agreement and the long-term protocol will help to further consolidate and expand trade and economic ties between the two countries on the basis of equality and mutual benefit.

### 苏联和缅甸签订补充贸易协定

1956-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和缅甸签订补充贸易协定
    新华社仰光2日讯　四月一日在这里发表了苏联和缅甸关于苏缅两国贸易关系的公报。
    公报说，由于苏缅谈判的结果，1956年4月1日，在苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬和缅甸联邦总理吴努的参加下，签订了补充贸易协定和关于双方相互提供商品和劳务的长期议定书，以及确定两国政府之间的结算办法的一些文件。
    公报说，补充贸易协定把1955年7月1日签订的苏缅贸易协定的有效期限延长到五年，以便在平等互利的基础上来全面发展和巩固两国之间的贸易关系。根据议定书，缅甸在四年内，每年将向苏联输出四十万吨大米，同时苏联将向缅甸联邦提供装备、机器和其他商品并且给予缅甸联邦以技术和其他援助。
    公报又说，如果缅甸联邦政府愿意，苏联政府将使缅甸的国家机构和它们的代理机构以及公司有可能派人到苏联熟悉缅甸联邦所将购买的机器、装备和其他商品。
    公报说，苏缅补充贸易协定和长期议定书的签订，将有助于在平等互利的基础上，进一步巩固和扩大两国之间的贸易经济联系。
