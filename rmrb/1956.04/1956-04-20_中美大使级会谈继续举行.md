Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1956-04-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, April 19th. The Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks held their forty-fourth meeting at the Palais des Nations at 10:00 AM on April 19th. The meeting adjourned at 11:40 AM. The next meeting will be held on April 26th.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1956-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦19日电　中美两国大使级会谈四月十九日上午十时在国联大厦举行第四十四次会议。会议在十一时四十分休会。下次会议将在四月二十六日举行。
