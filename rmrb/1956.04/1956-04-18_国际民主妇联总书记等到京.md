Title: General Secretary of the Women's International Democratic Federation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 17th: The General Secretary of the Secretariat of the Women's International Democratic Federation, Angiola Minella, and Deputy General Secretary Simone Bertrand, arrived in Beijing by special plane this afternoon. Also arriving on the same plane were: the resident secretaries from the United Kingdom and the United States, as well as resident representatives and staff from Bulgaria, Costa Rica, France, Romania, Sweden, and other countries, totaling eighteen people. The Chinese resident secretary also arrived on the same plane.

They are here to attend the Council Meeting of the Women's International Democratic Federation, which will be held in Beijing soon.

Greeting them at the airport today were Shi Liang, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and secretaries of the Secretariat, among others. (Attached picture)

Angiola Minella (center) and Shi Liang (right), who came to welcome her, walk out of the airport.

### 国际民主妇联总书记等到京

1956-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    国际民主妇联总书记等到京
    新华社17日讯　国际民主妇女联合会书记处总书记安吉奥拉·米涅拉、副总书记西蒙·贝特朗在今天下午乘专机到达北京。同机到达的有：英国和美国的驻会书记及保加利亚、哥斯达黎加、法国、罗马尼亚、瑞典等国驻会代表和工作人员共十八人。中国驻会书记也同机到达。
    她们是来参加即将在北京举行的国际民主妇联理事会议的。
    今天到机场欢迎的有全国民主妇联副主席史良和书记处书记等人。（附图片）
    安吉奥拉·米涅拉（中）和前往欢迎的史良（右）走出机场。
