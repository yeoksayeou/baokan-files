Title: The Ministry of Culture to Hold "Czechoslovak Republic Film Week"
Author:
Date: 1956-04-18
Page: 3

    The Ministry of Culture to Hold "Czechoslovak Republic Film Week"
    In order to further develop and consolidate the friendship between China and Czechoslovakia and strengthen cultural exchanges between the two countries, the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China has decided to hold the "Czechoslovak Republic Film Week" from May 9 to 15, 1956, in ten cities: Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Shenyang, Harbin, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chongqing, Xi'an, and Urumqi. Two new films, "Two Fathers and Sons in Steel City" and "Dangerous Love," will participate in this film week. In addition, four excellent Czechoslovakian re-release films have been selected to participate in the film week. These four films are: "Children of Nature," "Fight Before Dawn," "The Proud Princess," and "Two Sisters."
    During the film week, a delegation of Czechoslovak film workers will be invited to visit China and participate in the activities of the film week. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 文化部将举办“捷克斯洛伐克共和国电影周”

1956-04-18
第3版()
专栏：

    文化部将举办“捷克斯洛伐克共和国电影周”
    为进一步发展和巩固中国与捷克斯洛伐克之间的友谊，加强中捷两国文化交流，中华人民共和国文化部决定自1956年5月9日起到15日止，在北京、天津、上海、沈阳、哈尔滨、武汉、广州、重庆、西安、乌鲁木齐十个城市举办“捷克斯洛伐克共和国电影周”。参加这次电影周的有“钢城两父子”和“危险的恋爱”两部新片。此外，还选择四部优秀的捷克斯洛伐克复映片参加电影周，这四部影片是：“自然之子”、“黎明前的战斗”、“骄傲的公主”、“姊妹俩”。
    电影周期间，将邀请捷克斯洛伐克电影工作者代表团来中国参观访问，并参加电影周的活动。（新华社）
