Title: Comrade Aidit Returns to Beijing After Visiting Shanghai and Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1956-04-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 12th. After visiting the cities of Shanghai and Hangzhou, Indonesian Communist Party General Secretary Aidit returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon.

Aidit arrived in Shanghai on the 2nd. In Shanghai, he visited the site of the First National Congress of the Chinese Communist Party in 1921, and visited the Workers' Cultural Palace, museums, factories, children's palaces, and kindergartens, and visited workers' residential areas. When visiting the Yong'an Third Cotton Textile Factory, a joint state-private enterprise, Aidit held a discussion with Guo Dihuo, the capitalist representative of the factory and the General Manager of the Yong'an Cotton Textile Printing and Dyeing Factory, a joint state-private enterprise.

On the evening of the 6th, Aidit met with Song Qingling, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, who was in Shanghai, and had a cordial conversation with her.

While in Hangzhou, Aidit visited scenic spots and historical sites in West Lake, such as Three Pools Mirroring the Moon, Lingyin Temple, and Yuquan, cruised on West Lake by boat, and visited the Hulin Silk Factory and the Hangzhou Silk Factory.

### 艾地同志访问上海杭州后回到北京

1956-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    艾地同志访问上海杭州后回到北京
    新华社12日讯　印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地在访问了上海和杭州两个城市以后，今天下午乘飞机回到北京。
    艾地是二日到上海的。他在上海曾参观了1921年中国共产党第一次全国代表大会的会址，参观了工人文化宫、博物馆、工厂和少年宫、幼儿园，访问了工人住宅区。在参观公私合营永安第三棉纺织厂的时候，艾地和这个厂的资方、公私合营永安棉纺织印染厂总管理处总经理郭棣活进行了座谈。
    六日晚，艾地会见了正在上海的全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄，和她进行了亲切的交谈。
    艾地在杭州时，曾游览了三潭印月、灵隐、玉泉等西湖名胜古迹，泛舟漫游了西湖，并且参观了虎林丝厂和杭州丝厂。
