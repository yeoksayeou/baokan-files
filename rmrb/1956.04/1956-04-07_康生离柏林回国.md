Title: Kang Sheng Leaves Berlin for Home

Author: 
Date: 1956-04-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 5th - Kang Sheng, representative of the Chinese Communist Party and member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, who attended the Third Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, left Berlin by plane for home on April 5 after a short visit to the German Democratic Republic. Qi Li, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, candidate member of the Central Committee Florin, and Zeng Yongquan, Chinese Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, and others saw him off at the airport.

### 康生离柏林回国

1956-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    康生离柏林回国
    新华社柏林5日电　出席德国统一社会党第三次代表会议的中国共产党代表、中共中央政治局委员康生在民主德国作短期参观后，四月五日乘飞机离柏林回国。德国统一社会党中央书记处书记齐勒，候补中央委员弗洛林以及中国驻德意志民主共和国大使曾涌泉等人到机场送行。
