Title: Yugoslavian Newspapers Reprint "On the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat"
Author:
Date: 1956-04-18
Page: 1

Yugoslavian newspapers reprint "On the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat"
    This dispatch: The Yugoslavian newspapers "Borba" and "Politika" excerpted and reprinted the People's Daily editorial "On the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat" on April 6th.

### 南斯拉夫报纸转载“关于无产阶级专政的历史经验”

1956-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫报纸转载“关于无产阶级专政的历史经验”
