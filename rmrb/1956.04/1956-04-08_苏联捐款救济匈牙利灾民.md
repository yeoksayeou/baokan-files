Title: Soviet Union Donates Funds to Aid Hungarian Flood Victims
Author:
Date: 1956-04-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 6th (TASS dispatch): Soviet Ambassador to Hungary, Andropov, expressed his deep sympathy to the Hungarian government regarding the Danube River flooding disaster. He also announced that the Executive Committee of the Union of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies of the Soviet Union had allocated 100,000 rubles to the Hungarian Red Cross to aid Hungarian residents affected by the flood.

### 苏联捐款救济匈牙利灾民

1956-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联捐款救济匈牙利灾民
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯六日讯：苏联驻匈牙利大使安德罗波夫就多瑙河泛滥成灾一事向匈牙利政府表示深切同情，并且通知说，苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会执行委员会拨款十万卢布给匈牙利红十字会，救济匈牙利遭受水灾的居民。
