Title: Wujin County Holds Literacy Examination

Author: 
Date: 1956-04-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

    Wujin County Holds Literacy Examination
    Recently, Wujin County, Jiangsu Province, held a literacy examination. More than 20,000 members of agricultural production cooperatives throughout the county have reached the standard for literacy graduation.
    The examination method was uniformly formulated by the Wujin County Literacy Committee, and the exam papers were printed and distributed by the county literacy committee, with agricultural production cooperatives or townships as the units for the examination.
    A total of 160,000 people from the entire county participated in this examination, all of whom were members of agricultural production cooperatives who participated in literacy studies last winter and this spring.
                        (Xinhua News Agency)

### 武进县举行扫盲大会考

1956-04-12
第3版()
专栏：

    武进县举行扫盲大会考
    江苏省武进县最近举行了扫盲大会考，全县农业生产合作社中有两万多个社员已经达到了扫盲毕业标准。
    会考办法是由武进县扫盲委员会统一制定的，并且由县扫盲委员会印发考卷，以农业生产合作社或乡为单位进行会考。
    全县参加这次会考的共有十六万人，他们都是去年冬天和今年春天参加扫盲学习的农业生产合作社社员。
                        （新华社）
