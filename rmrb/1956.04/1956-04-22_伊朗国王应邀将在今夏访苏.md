Title: Iranian King Invited to Visit Soviet Union This Summer
Author:
Date: 1956-04-22
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th (TASS dispatch): As previously reported, His Majesty Mohammad Reza Pahlavi, the King of Iran, and the Queen, upon the invitation of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and its chairman, Voroshilov, will visit the Soviet Union this summer. Recently, Moscow and Tehran exchanged views on the timing of the Iranian King's visit to the Soviet Union. Both sides have confirmed that he will arrive in the Soviet Union on June 25, 1956, with his wife and entourage for a two-week visit.

### 伊朗国王应邀将在今夏访苏

1956-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    伊朗国王应邀将在今夏访苏
    新华社21日讯  塔斯社莫斯科20日讯：前已报道，伊朗国王穆罕默德·礼萨·巴列维陛下和皇后应苏联最高苏维埃主席团和苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫个人的邀请将于今年夏季访问苏联。最近，莫斯科和德黑兰就伊朗国王访问苏联的时间问题交换了意见。双方确定，他将在1956年6月25日同夫人和随行人员前来苏联访问，他们将在苏联访问两个星期。
