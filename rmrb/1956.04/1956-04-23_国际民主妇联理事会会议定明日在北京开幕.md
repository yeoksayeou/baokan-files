Title: International Democratic Women's Federation Council Meeting Scheduled to Open Tomorrow in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: The International Democratic Women's Federation Council announced in Beijing on April 21st that the International Democratic Women's Federation Council meeting will open at 3:00 PM on April 24th in the auditorium of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beijing.

The President of the International Democratic Women's Federation, Eugénie Cotton of France, will deliver the opening speech at the opening meeting of the Council. The General Secretary of the International Democratic Women's Federation, Angiola Minella of Italy, will give the main report of the Secretariat of the International Democratic Women's Federation. Mrs. Safia, a member of the Executive Committee of the All-India Women's Conference and a council member of the International Democratic Women's Federation from Bombay, will give a special report on the living conditions and aspirations of women in Asia and Africa.

Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: Today, thirty-two council members and specially invited representatives of the International Democratic Women's Federation arrived in Beijing by plane and train, respectively. They will attend the International Democratic Women's Federation Council meeting held in Beijing.

Among them are five council members from Albania, Canada, India, Romania, and the Soviet Union, as well as twenty-seven specially invited representatives and staff members from Albania, Finland, France, India, Lebanon, Mongolia, Romania, the United Kingdom, and the Soviet Union.

Besides those who arrived with Mrs. Cotton on the same plane, those who arrived in Beijing yesterday included four council members from Indonesia, Iraq, Mongolia, and Poland, as well as nine specially invited representatives, resident representatives, and staff members from Indonesia, Jordan, Mongolia, Poland, and Spain.

### 国际民主妇联理事会会议定明日在北京开幕

1956-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    国际民主妇联理事会会议定明日在北京开幕
    新华社22日讯  据国际民主妇女联合会理事会四月二十一日在北京宣布：国际民主妇女联合会理事会会议将要在四月二十四日下午三时在北京中国人民政治协商会议大礼堂开幕。
    国际民主妇联主席、法国的欧仁妮·戈登将要在理事会开幕会议上致开幕词。国际民主妇联总书记、意大利的安吉奥拉·米涅拉将作国际民主妇联书记处的主要报告。国际民主妇联理事会理事、印度全国妇女联合会执行委员会委员、孟买的萨菲亚夫人将要作关于亚非两洲妇女的生活情况和愿望的专题报告。
    新华社22日讯  今天有三十二名国际民主妇女联合会的理事和特邀代表分别乘飞机和火车到达北京。她们将出席在北京召开的国际民主妇女联合会理事会会议。
    她们中有阿尔巴尼亚、加拿大、印度、罗马尼亚和苏联等国的理事共五人，还有阿尔巴尼亚、芬兰、法国、印度、黎巴嫩、蒙古、罗马尼亚、英国和苏联等国的特邀代表和工作人员共二十七人。
    昨天到达北京的，除了同戈登夫人同机到达的人员外，还有印度尼西亚、伊拉克、蒙古、波兰等国的理事共四人，以及印度尼西亚、约旦、蒙古、波兰、西班牙等国的特邀代表、驻会代表和工作人员共九人。
