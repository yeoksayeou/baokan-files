Title: Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike's Reply to Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1956-04-19
Page: 1

To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

I thank the Government and people of the People's Republic of China and yourself for your congratulations on my assumption of the office of Prime Minister of Ceylon and for your expression of the desire to further strengthen the friendly relations between our two countries, which I warmly share.

Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike
Colombo, April 17, 1956

### 锡兰总理班达拉奈克给周总理的复电

1956-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

    锡兰总理班达拉奈克给周总理的复电北京中华人民共和国总理周恩来阁下：
    我感谢中华人民共和国政府和中国人民以及你本人对我就任锡兰总理职务的祝贺和你所表示的关于进一步加强贵我两国间友好关系的愿望，对此，我热切地抱有同感。
                               锡兰总理　班达拉奈克
                                       1956年4月17日科伦坡
