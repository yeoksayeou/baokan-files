Title: Hefei Builds a Joint State-Private Cotton Mill
Author:
Date: 1956-04-22
Page: 2

Hefei, Anhui Province, is stepping up the construction of a joint state-private cotton mill with 50,000 spindles. This factory is a joint venture between the state and Shanghai capitalists, including Rong Yiren. The main factory building has already begun construction. Trains transporting materials directly to the construction site. Thousands of workers are engaged in concrete engineering. It is expected that this factory will be put into production in the fourth quarter of this year. (Reporter from this newspaper stationed in Hefei)

### 合肥兴建一公私合营棉纺织厂

1956-04-22
第2版()
专栏：

    合肥兴建一公私合营棉纺织厂
    安徽省合肥市有一个拥有五万枚纱锭的公私合营棉纺织厂，正在加紧建设中。这是国家和上海资本家荣毅仁等合营的一个厂。这个厂现在已经开始建筑主厂房。通往这里的火车把大批材料直送工地。成千的工人在进行混凝土工程。预计今年第四季度，这个厂就可以投入生产。（本报驻合肥记者）
