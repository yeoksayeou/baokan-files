Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Australia
Author:
Date: 1956-04-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sydney, 25th: A Chinese trade union delegation arrived in Sydney, Australia on April 24th.

The head of the delegation is Zhao Guoqiang, chairman of the Chinese Heavy Industry Trade Union. The members include Zhang Tianmin, director of the preparatory committee of the Chinese Construction Trade Union, and Liang Zhengping, chairman of the Shanxi Provincial Coal Miners' Trade Union. They were invited by the Australian Sheet Metal Workers' Union, the Australian Building Workers' Industrial Union, and the Australian Miners' Federation, respectively, to participate in the Australian working people's "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations.

This is the first Chinese trade union delegation to visit Australia. They will visit various parts of Australia.

### 中国工会代表团到达澳大利亚

1956-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

    中国工会代表团到达澳大利亚
    新华社25日讯  悉尼消息：中国工会代表团在四月二十四日到达澳大利亚的悉尼。
    代表团团长是中国重工业工会主席赵国强，团员有中国建筑工会筹备委员会主任张天民和山西省煤矿工会主席梁正平。他们是分别应澳大利亚薄金属板工人工会、澳大利亚建筑工人产业工会和澳大利亚矿工联合会的邀请，前往参加澳大利亚劳动人民的“五一”国际劳动节的。
    这是到澳大利亚访问的第一个中国工会代表团。他们将到澳大利亚全国各地访问。
