Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences to Build Botanical Garden in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

    The Institute of Botany of the Chinese Academy of Sciences is preparing to build the Beijing Botanical Garden in the area of the Temple of the Reclining Buddha in the Western Hills of Beijing.
    When completed, this botanical garden will cover an area of more than 8,000 mu, including a systematic arboretum, an experimental orchard, a large tropical greenhouse, various experimental areas, and exhibition areas. Here, you can appreciate various famous plants from all over the world. A Michurin Memorial Hall and a Youth Science Palace will also be established here.
    The person in charge of the preparation for the Beijing Botanical Garden said that this botanical garden is an institution for the study of plant cultivation. Its task is to introduce and domesticate plants of economic value from around the world, increase the types of cultivated plants in our country, expand the area of plant cultivation, enable plants from all over the world to grow in China, and promote scientific teaching and urban greening.
    After a long period of preparation, this botanical garden has now established ten greenhouses and cold frames, including geographical, succulent, economic, begonia, and citrus greenhouses, and has cultivated more than 2,100 species of plants collected from various places and exchanged from various countries, including the metasequoia, a fossil plant discovered in western China, the world-famous redwood, the tropical fruits longan and lychee, and the famous Soviet flower gloxinia, totaling more than 2,100 species. There are also grape experimental orchards, rootstock gardens, and original material nurseries on the experimental grounds.

### 中国科学院在北京筹建植物园

1956-04-09
第3版()
专栏：

    中国科学院在北京筹建植物园
    中国科学院植物研究所正在北京西山卧佛寺一带筹建北京植物园。
    这个植物园建成后有八千多亩地的面积，包括系统树木园、试验果园、大型热带温室、各种试验区和展览区。在这里，可以欣赏全世界各种有名的植物。这里还将建立米丘林纪念馆和青年科学宫等。
    筹建北京植物园的负责人说，这个植物园是研究植物栽培的机构，它的任务是引种驯化世界上富有经济价值的植物，增加我国栽培植物的种类和扩大植物栽培的面积，使世界各地的植物都能在中国生长，并推进科学教学工作和都市绿化工作。
    这个植物园经过长期准备，现在已经建立了地理、多浆、经济、秋海棠类、柑桔等十个温室和冷室，还有从各地搜集和各国交换来的种子培植成的二千一百多种植物，其中有在中国西部发现的化石植物——水杉，世界著名的大树美洲红木，热带水果龙眼、荔枝和苏联赠送的有名花卉大岩桐等二千一百多种。试验地上还有葡萄试验果园、砧木园、原始材料圃。
                      （新华社）
