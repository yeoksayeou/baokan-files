Title: Closing of the Textile Industry Advanced Producers Representative Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-04-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th — The National Textile Industry Advanced Producers Representative Conference closed this afternoon.
    The more than 800 representatives attending the conference unanimously adopted a resolution today, calling on all workers in the national cotton, wool, linen, silk, knitting, printing and dyeing, textile machinery manufacturing, and textile basic construction industries to fully utilize their enthusiasm and creativity, launch the advanced producer movement, and strive to complete the first Five-Year Plan for the textile industry ahead of schedule and in an all-round manner.
    At the closing ceremony, the signing ceremony of the 1956 inter-factory competition contract for the national textile industry was also held. A total of 275 textile factories and textile machinery manufacturing factories signed the inter-factory competition contract.
    The conference elected 294 representatives of advanced producers and advanced units to attend the National Advanced Producers Representative Conference. Jiang Guangnai, Minister of the Ministry of Textile Industry, presented prizes and award banners to all the representatives who attended the conference at the closing ceremony.

### 纺织工业先进生产者代表会议闭幕

1956-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    纺织工业先进生产者代表会议闭幕
    据新华社20日讯  全国纺织工业先进生产者代表会议今天下午闭幕。
    参加会议的八百多名代表，今天一致通过决议，号召全国棉、毛、麻、丝绸、针织、印染、纺织机械制造、纺织基本建设等各行业的全体职工，充分发挥积极性和创造性，开展先进生产者运动，争取提前全面超额完成纺织工业第一个五年计划。
    在闭幕会上，还举行了全国纺织工业1956年厂际竞赛合同签字仪式。在厂际竞赛合同上签字的纺织厂和纺织机械制造厂单位，共有275个。
    会议选出了二百九十四个先进生产者和先进单位的代表出席全国先进生产者代表会议。纺织工业部部长蒋光鼐，在闭幕会上向参加这次会议的全体代表发了奖品和奖旗。
