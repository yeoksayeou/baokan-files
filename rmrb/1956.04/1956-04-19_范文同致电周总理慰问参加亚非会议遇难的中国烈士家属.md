Title: Pham Van Dong Sends Telegram to Premier Zhou Expressing Condolences to Families of Chinese Martyrs Who Perished Attending the Asian-African Conference

Author:

Date: 1956-04-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. Pham Van Dong, Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai on the 11th, extending condolences to the families of the Chinese martyrs who unfortunately perished while attending the Asian-African Conference. The telegram reads as follows:

To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the Government of the People's Republic of China:

On the occasion of the first anniversary of the unfortunate passing of Chinese and Vietnamese comrades who were participating in the Asian-African Conference, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam extends its sincere condolences to the families of the Chinese martyrs.

Pham Van Dong
Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
April 11, 1956

### 范文同致电周总理慰问参加亚非会议遇难的中国烈士家属

1956-04-19
第4版()
专栏：

    范文同致电周总理慰问参加亚非会议遇难的中国烈士家属
    新华社18日讯　越南民主共和国总理范文同十一日致电周恩来总理，向参加亚非会议不幸遇难的中国烈士的家属表示问候。电文如下：
    中华人民共和国政府总理周恩来同志：
    当中国和越南参加亚非会议的一些同志不幸遇难一周年的时候，越南民主共和国谨向中国烈士们的家属致亲切的问候。
    越南民主共和国总理　范文同
                    1956年4月11日
