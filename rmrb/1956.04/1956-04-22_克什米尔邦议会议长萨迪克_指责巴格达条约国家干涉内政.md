Title: Kashmir State Assembly Speaker Sadiq Accuses Baghdad Pact Countries of Interfering in Internal Affairs
Author:
Date: 1956-04-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 21st - According to the Indian Press Trust of Srinagar, Kashmir State Assembly Speaker Sadiq said on April 20th that any opinion expressed by the Baghdad Pact countries on the Kashmir issue constitutes "unjustified interference" in India's internal affairs.

He said: "This issue is of the utmost importance to us, and I believe that no Indian would treat this issue indifferently."

Sadiq made these remarks when commenting on the Baghdad Pact Council's communique issued in Tehran on April 19th, which mentioned Kashmir.

He reiterated that the people of Kashmir have finally and irrevocably decided to continue to be an integral part of India. He said: "Threats from relevant foreign countries cannot prevent them from following the path they have chosen and love."

### 克什米尔邦议会议长萨迪克  指责巴格达条约国家干涉内政

1956-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    克什米尔邦议会议长萨迪克
    指责巴格达条约国家干涉内政
    新华社新德里21日电  据印度报业托辣斯斯利那加消息，克什米尔邦议会议长萨迪克四月二十日说，巴格达条约国家就克什米尔问题发表任何意见都是对印度内政的“不正当的干涉”。
    他说：“这个问题对我们说来具有最重大的意义，我相信任何印度人都不会若无其事地对待这个问题。”
    萨迪克是在对巴格达条约理事会德黑兰会议四月十九日发表的公报中提到克什米尔一事发表评论的时候说这番话的。
    他重申克什米尔人民已经最后而且不可更改地决定继续成为印度不可分割的一部分。他说：“有关外国的威胁决不能阻止他们走他们自己所选择和喜爱的道路。”
