　　Sports News
　　△ Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, and Chen Xian, the leader of the Chinese delegation participating in the 23rd World Table Tennis Championships, visited Mr. Montagu, Chairman of the International Table Tennis Federation, and Mr. Tadashi Adachi, President of the Japan Table Tennis Association, and Mr. Daisuke Omon, Executive Director, on March 30th. On the morning of March 31st, Chinese team members Sun Meiying, Qiu Zhonghui, and Zhuang Jiafu held a joint discussion with Japanese team members.
　　△ The Chinese ice hockey team, after a two-week visit to Czechoslovakia at the invitation of the Czech government, left Prague by car on the 30th and arrived in Berlin.

### 体育消息

1956-04-02
第4版()
专栏：体育消息

    体育消息
    △中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠和参加第二十三届世界乒乓球锦标赛的中国代表队领队陈先，在三月三十日先后拜访了国际乒乓球联合会主席蒙塔古和日本乒乓球协会会长足立正、专务理事大门大亮。三月三十一日上午，中国代表队队员孙梅英、丘锺惠、庄家富等曾同日本代表队员举行联欢座谈。
    △中国冰球队应邀在捷克斯洛伐克作了两个星期的访问以后，在三十日乘车离布拉格到达柏林。
