Title: Tractor Factory Engine Workshop Completes Concrete Pouring and Other Projects Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1956-04-07
Page: 1

Tractor Factory Engine Workshop
    Completes Concrete Pouring and Other Projects Ahead of Schedule

    (Our Newspaper) News – The employees building the engine workshop of the First Tractor Manufacturing Plant completed the first phase of earthwork and all concrete work on April 4th, two working days ahead of the original schedule. The quality of the project is good.
    The engine workshop is one of the largest construction projects of the First Tractor Manufacturing Plant. Construction on this workshop began in early March. In the socialist competition, employees actively sought ways to speed up the project while ensuring quality. Workers Wang Yonglou, Lao Yingui, and others improved their mastery of the tamping method, increasing work efficiency by an average of more than 90%. Members of the Youth Engineering Team adopted the "middle-convex layered and segmented pouring method," and the average workload per person greatly exceeded the quota.

### 拖拉机厂发动机工场  提前完成浇灌混凝土等工程

1956-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    拖拉机厂发动机工场
    提前完成浇灌混凝土等工程
    本报讯　修建第一拖拉机制造厂发动机工场的职工，在四月四日比原定计划提前两个工作日完成了第一期土方工程和全部混凝土工程。工程质量良好。
    发动机工场是第一拖拉机厂最大的工程项目之一。这个工场从三月上旬开工兴建，职工们在社会主义竞赛中积极设法在保证质量情况下加快工程进度。工人王永楼、劳银贵等改进掌握木夯的方法，平均提高工作效率90％以上。青年工程队的队员们采用“中凸分层分段浇灌法”，每人平均的工作量大大超过了定额。
