Dedicated to the Builders of the Jining-Ulaanbaatar Railway

Editor's Note: Vi. Lapshin (pen name Vi. Galkin) from Ukhta, Komi Autonomous Republic, Soviet Union, sent the lyrics of "Road of Friendship" to the people who built the Jining-Ulaanbaatar Railway - workers, peasants and intellectuals from China, the People's Republic of Mongolia, and the Soviet Union. We are now publishing these lyrics below, hoping that Chinese composers will set them to music.

Road of Friendship (Lyrics)

Lyrics by Vi. Galkin (Soviet Union), Translated by Qiu Qin (China)

Not afraid of the scorching heat,
Not afraid of the howling wind,
We will surely complete the task assigned by the motherland:
We will learn through labor,
And build friendship,
Erecting a steel transportation line in the Gobi Desert.

It is like a clear stream in the desert,
Our common labor is rewarded today:
A railway connects Mongolia,
Connects Moscow,
We extend a hand of friendship to Europe.

People affectionately call it the "Road of Friendship."
It is a great symbol of our profound friendship.
Beijing and Moscow will be closer,
People all over the world warmly welcome us.

It is like a huge steel bond,
Connecting our cities and villages.
We love and respect our dear friends,
When we shake hands, joy flows through our whole body.

Chorus:
Hearts are beating, wheels are rumbling:
Tuguo, Tuguo, Tuguo…
Our train speeds along the Road of Friendship.
Friends often meet on this road,
My good friend is in Beijing, your good friend is in Moscow.

Chorus:
Hearts are beating, wheels are rumbling:
Tuguo, Tuguo, Tuguo…
Our train speeds along the Road of Friendship.
Friends often meet on this road,
My good friend is in Beijing, your good friend is in Moscow.

(Note: When composers set the music, they should set "Tuguo, Tuguo..." to the sound of the wheels hitting the rails as the train moves.)

### 献给集宁—乌兰巴托铁路建设者

1956-04-09
第7版()
专栏：

    献给集宁—乌兰巴托铁路建设者
    编者按：苏联科米自治共和国乌赫塔城维·拉普申（笔名是维·加尔金）寄来“友谊之路”的歌词，献给修建集宁—乌兰巴托铁路的人们——中国、蒙古人民共和国和苏联三国的工人、农民和知识分子。现在把这首歌词发表在下面，希望中国作曲家谱曲。
    友谊之路 （歌词）
    苏联　维·加尔金作词  中国　丘琴译词不怕炎热的蒸烤，不怕寒风的吹卷，祖国交给的任务我们一定实现：我们要在劳动中学习，建立起友谊，在戈壁滩修起一条钢铁的运输线。它好像是沙漠中的一道清流，我们共同的劳动今天得到报酬：一条铁路连接起蒙古，连接起莫斯科，我们向欧洲伸出了友谊的手。人们亲切地把它叫做“友谊之路”。它是我们深厚友情的伟大象征。北京城和莫斯科将更加亲近，全世界人民都热烈欢迎我们。它好像是一条巨大的钢铁纽带它把我们的城市乡村连接起来。我们和那亲爱的朋友互相敬爱，当我们握起手来，欢乐便流遍了全身。副歌：心儿在跳动，车轮在震响：突戈、突戈、突戈……
    友谊之路上飞驶着我们的列车。
    朋友们在这条路上常常见面，
    我的好朋友在北京，你的好朋友在莫斯科。副歌：心儿在跳动，车轮在震响：突戈、突戈、突戈……
    友谊之路上飞驶着我们的列车。
    朋友们在这条路上常常见面，
    我的好朋友在北京，你的好朋友在莫斯科。
        （附注：作曲家谱曲时，应把“突戈、突戈……”谱为列
        车行进时轮轨碰击的声音。）
