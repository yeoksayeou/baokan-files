Title: Air Battle Between Israel and Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-04-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 13th. According to the Associated Press: An Egyptian military spokesman announced on the evening of April 12th that the Egyptian Air Force shot down an Israeli aircraft in an air battle that occurred on the morning of the 12th over the northeast of the Auja demilitarized zone.

The news said that the Israelis reported that Israel also shot down an Egyptian aircraft in the same air battle.

### 以色列和埃及发生空战

1956-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    以色列和埃及发生空战
    新华社13日讯　据美联社报道：埃及军方一位发言人四月十二日晚上宣布，埃及空军在12日上午在奥贾非军事区东北上空发生的一次空战中击落了一架以色列飞机。
    消息说，以色列人报告在同一次空战中以色列也击落了一架埃及飞机。
