Title: Central Delegation Arrives Near Lhasa
Author:
Date: 1956-04-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 16th -- The central delegation that is going to Lhasa to congratulate the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region arrived this afternoon at Donggar Dzong Wuzhubuding Village, which is more than 20 li west of Lhasa. Representatives from the Tibetan local government, the Bainqen Kanpo Lija Conference Committee, and the Qamdo People's Liberation Committee went to greet them that afternoon. The representatives sent by the Tibetan local government to greet the delegation included Acting Jikhyab Khenpo Lobsang Sandan, Kalon Surkhang Wangchen Gelek, Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Assistant Kalon Shasu Gyume Tsering Dorje, and Acting Kalon Gadzhang Lobsang Rinchen. The representatives sent by the Bainqen Kanpo Lija Conference Committee to greet the delegation included Bainqen Erdini's father, Yaksie Gonpo Tsetan, Zhan Dong Ji Jinmei, Director of the Bainqen Kanpo Lija Conference Committee, and Lhamin Yexi Chuchen. The representative sent by the Qamdo People's Liberation Committee to greet the delegation was Vice Chairman Bangda Doje. Also present to greet the delegation were Zhang Guohua, Acting Secretary of the CPC Tibet Work Committee and Commander of the PLA Tibet Military Region, Fan Ming, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Tibet Work Committee and Deputy Political Commissar of the PLA Tibet Military Region, and others.

Lobsang Sandan and Yaksie Gonpo Tsetan presented scarves (Hada) to Chen Yi, the head of the delegation, and Zhang Jingwu and Wang Feng, the deputy heads of the delegation, on behalf of the Dalai Lama and the Bainqen Erdini respectively. The officials who came to welcome them also presented scarves to the head and deputy heads of the delegation to express their greetings. Chen Yi and the deputy heads of the delegation had cordial conversations with the people who came to greet them.

Xinhua News Agency, Heihe, 16th -- The central delegation entered Tibetan territory and began its visit.

When the central delegation arrived in Heihe, a key town in northern Tibet, on the 14th, they were greeted by nearly two thousand people lining the streets. Among the welcoming crowd were Dorga Pengcuo Raojie, Kalon of the Tibetan local government, sent by the Dalai Lama to Heihe to greet the delegation, and Lobsang Chuni, the representative of the Bainqen Erdini.

After arriving in Heihe, the central delegation participated in a mass rally of welcome held locally. Chen Yi, the head of the central delegation and Vice Premier of the State Council, accepted a "Qianma" (a wooden box divided into two compartments, one containing highland barley and ginseng fruit, and the other containing tsampa and highland barley wine) presented by three young Tibetan men and women at the welcoming rally. Tudan Jiangqu, Jiangji (chief administrator) of the Tibetan local government in Heihe, and Chen Yi delivered speeches at the meeting.

### 中央代表团到达拉萨附近

1956-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中央代表团到达拉萨附近
    新华社拉萨16日电　前往拉萨祝贺西藏自治区筹备委员会成立的中央代表团，今天下午到达了离拉萨以西二十多里的东噶宗吴珠不顶村。西藏地方政府、班禅堪布会议厅委员会和昌都地区人民解放委员会，当天下午派代表前往迎接。西藏地方政府派往迎接代表团的有代理机巧堪布洛桑三旦、噶伦索康·旺清格来、阿沛·阿旺晋美，助理噶伦夏苏·举墨策仁多吉，代理噶伦噶章·罗桑仁增。班禅堪布会议厅委员会派往迎接代表团的有，班禅额尔德尼的父亲尧西·贡保才旦、班禅堪布会议厅委员会主任詹东·计晋美、拉敏·益喜楚臣。昌都地区人民解放委员会派往迎接代表团的有副主任委员邦达多吉。前往迎接代表团的还有中共西藏工作委员会代书记、中国人民解放军西藏军区司令员张国华，中共西藏工作委员会副书记、中国人民解放军西藏军区副政治委员范明等人。
    洛桑三旦和尧西·贡保才旦分别代表达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼向代表团团长陈毅和代表团副团长张经武、汪锋献了哈达。前来欢迎的官员们又向代表团团长和副团长献哈达问好。陈毅和代表团副团长同前来迎接的人员进行了亲切的交谈。
    据新华社黑河16日电　中央代表团进入西藏境内，开始访问活动。
    中央代表团十四日到达西藏北部重镇黑河的时候，受到了近两千人的夹道欢迎。欢迎的人群中有达赖喇嘛派往黑河迎接代表团的代表西藏地方政府噶伦朵噶·彭错饶杰，班禅额尔德尼的代表罗桑楚尼等。
    中央代表团到达黑河后，就参加了当地举行的群众欢迎大会。中央代表团团长、国务院副总理陈毅在欢迎大会上接受了三个藏族男女青年献给的“千吗”（这是一个分成两格的木质盒子，在盒子的一格盛着青稞和人生果，另一格盛着糌粑和青稞酒）。西藏地方政府绛机（黑河总管）土旦江曲和陈毅在会上讲了话。
