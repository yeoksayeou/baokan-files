Title: Two Paper Mills Improve Production Levels
Author:
Date: 1956-04-16
Page: 1

Two paper mills, the state-owned Shixian Paper Mill and the state-owned Jilin Paper Mill, reached their 1957 production levels ahead of schedule in late March. The newsprint, Daolin paper, coated printing paper, cement bag paper, and high-grade book paper produced by these two paper mills have met the needs of various regions.

    This year, the workers of the state-owned Shixian Paper Mill submitted 2,548 rationalization proposals, of which 89% have been adopted and implemented. The labor productivity of the state-owned Jilin Paper Mill in the first quarter also increased by 32.5% compared to the same period last year. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 两个纸厂生产水平提高

1956-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    两个纸厂生产水平提高
    国营石岘造纸厂和国营吉林造纸厂，在三月下旬提前达到1957年的生产水平。这两个造纸厂生产的新闻纸、道林纸、胶板印刷纸、水泥袋纸和高级书籍用纸等，供应了各地的需要。
    国营石岘造纸厂的职工，今年提出了2，548件合理化建议，其中有89％已经被采纳实现了。国营吉林造纸厂第一季度的劳动生产率，也比去年同期提高了32.5％。（据新华社讯）
