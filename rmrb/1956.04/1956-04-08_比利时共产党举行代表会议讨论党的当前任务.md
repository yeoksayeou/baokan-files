Title: Belgian Communist Party Holds Delegates Meeting to Discuss Current Tasks of the Party
Author:
Date: 1956-04-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 5th, Brussels dispatch: According to TASS, the Belgian Communist Party held a delegates meeting in Brussels from March 31st to April 2nd to listen to and discuss the report by the Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party, Burnelle, regarding the tasks of the party in the most recent period.

According to the "Red Flag Daily," Burnelle made an analysis of the economic situation in Belgium in his report, and also spoke about the struggles of the working people to improve their living conditions and to strive for increased wages.

When Burnelle spoke about the current international situation, he pointed out the significant role played by the Soviet Union and other countries in the socialist camp in easing international tensions. He said that the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union was an extremely valuable contribution to the cause of defending peace for the working class and the people of all countries. Burnelle demanded that Belgium implement a foreign policy that could promote the easing of international tensions. He called on Communist Party members to fight for the unity of the Belgian working class.

### 比利时共产党举行代表会议讨论党的当前任务

1956-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    比利时共产党举行代表会议讨论党的当前任务
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社布鲁塞尔四日讯：比利时共产党从三月三十一日到四月二日在布鲁塞尔举行了代表会议，听取和讨论了共产党中央委员会书记伯内尔关于党在最近一个时期中的任务的报告。
    据“红旗报”报道，伯内尔在报告中对比利时的经济情况作了分析，并且讲到劳动人民争取改善生活条件和争取增加工资的斗争。
    伯内尔谈到当前的国际局势的时候，指出苏联和社会主义阵营其他国家在缓和国际紧张局势方面所起的重大作用。他说，苏联共产党第二十次代表大会是对各国工人阶级和人民的保卫和平的事业的一个极宝贵的贡献。伯内尔要求比利时执行能够促使国际紧张局势缓和的外交政策。他号召共产党员为争取比利时工人阶级团结一致而斗争。
