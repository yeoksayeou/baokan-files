Title: Mikoyan Leaves Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1956-04-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 2nd. After a three-day visit to Burma, Mikoyan, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and his entourage left Rangoon by plane on April 2 for Hanoi, the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    Among those who saw them off at the airport were Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, Minister of Trade U Hla Myint, Minister of Supply Bo Min Gaung, Soviet Ambassador to Burma Syzganov, Chinese Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to Burma Cheng Zhiping, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Burma.
    Before leaving Rangoon, Mikoyan and others visited Shan State, a component of the Union of Burma, on April 1. They were warmly welcomed by the local residents there.

### 米高扬离开仰光

1956-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    米高扬离开仰光
    据新华社仰光2日讯　苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬和他的随行人员在缅甸访问三天以后，在四月二日乘飞机离仰光前往越南民主共和国首都河内。
    到机场送行的有缅甸联邦总理吴努、贸易部长吴拉实、物资供应部长波敏贡、苏联驻缅甸大使希鲍林、中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办程之平和各国驻缅甸的外交使节等人。
    米高扬等离开仰光前曾于四月一日访问了掸邦——缅甸联邦的一个组成部分。他们在那里受到当地居民的热烈欢迎。
