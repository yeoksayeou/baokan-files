Title: Tea Picking Season Arrives in Hangzhou

Author: 
Date: 1956-04-20
Page: 1

The tea trees in Longjing and Meijiawu in the West Lake District of Hangzhou have sprouted verdant buds, and the busy tea-picking season has arrived again. Members of the tea production cooperatives are happily picking tea.
