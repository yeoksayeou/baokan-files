Title: Chinese Weightlifting Team in Mongolia Breaks Multiple National Records
Author:
Date: 1956-04-29
Page: 3

The Chinese weightlifting team returned to Beijing by train on the 28th, after concluding its visit to the Mongolian People's Republic.

In the Mongolian People's Republic, the Chinese weightlifting team held three friendly matches with the Mongolian weightlifting team. In the competition, the Chinese weightlifting team won with a total score of 26 to 20, and four people broke the national records for the total score in two weight classes and five individual weightlifting events.

Lightweight weightlifters Chen Jingkai and Chen Yongbo both broke the national record of 295 kg for the total score in the featherweight class, previously held by Yin Guohua; Light heavyweight weightlifter Liu Yunfu broke his own national record of 355 kg for the total score in the light heavyweight class.

In terms of individual event scores, those who broke the national records were Chen Yongbo in the featherweight class for the two-hand press, Chen Jingkai in the featherweight class for the two-hand clean and jerk, Huang Qianghui in the lightweight class for the two-hand clean and jerk, and Liu Yunfu in the light heavyweight class for the two-hand press and the two-hand snatch.

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 中国举重队在蒙古  打破多项全国最高纪录

1956-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

    中国举重队在蒙古
    打破多项全国最高纪录
    中国举重队结束了在蒙古人民共和国的访问以后，二十八日乘火车回到北京。
    中国举重队在蒙古人民共和国，先后同蒙古举重队举行了三场友谊比赛。在比赛中，中国举重队以总分二十六比二十获胜，并且有四个人打破了两级举重总成绩和五个举重单项成绩的全国最高纪录。
    次轻量级举重运动员陈镜开和陈永博的总成绩，都打破了殷国华保持的次轻量级举重总成绩295公斤的全国最高纪录；轻重量级举重运动员刘云福打破了他自己保持的轻重量级举重总成绩355公斤的全国最高纪录。
    在单项成绩方面打破全国最高纪录的，有陈永博次轻量级的双手推举、陈镜开次轻量级的双手挺举、黄强辉轻量级的双手挺举和刘云福轻重量级的双手推举和双手抓举。
                  （据新华社讯）
