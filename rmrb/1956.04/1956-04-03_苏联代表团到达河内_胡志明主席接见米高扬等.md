Title: Soviet Delegation Arrives in Hanoi, Chairman Ho Chi Minh Receives Mikoyan and Others
Author:
Date: 1956-04-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 2nd. The Soviet government delegation headed by Soviet Council of Ministers First Deputy Chairman Mikoyan arrived in Hanoi, the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, by plane at 2:50 PM today.

Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Pham Van Dong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Vietnamese National Assembly Ton Duc Thang, General Commander of the Vietnamese People's Army General Vo Nguyen Giap, and General Secretary of the Vietnamese Labor Party Truong Chinh all went to the airport to welcome them.

After disembarking, Mikoyan and others shook hands with Prime Minister Pham Van Dong and others, and accepted flowers presented by children from Hanoi. Accompanied by Pham Van Dong and Vo Nguyen Giap, Mikoyan reviewed the guard of honor and warmly shook hands with the welcoming crowd. Mikoyan also accepted flowers and garlands presented to him by representatives of all circles of Vietnamese people and Chinese and Indian overseas Chinese.

Pham Van Dong delivered a welcoming speech at the airport. He expressed a warm welcome to the Soviet government delegation's visit. Pham Van Dong said that the Vietnamese people believe that the Soviet government delegation's visit to Vietnam is of great significance, as it will further strengthen the friendship between the peoples of Vietnam and the Soviet Union, and make the Vietnamese people stronger in the political struggle to build a peaceful, unified, independent, democratic and prosperous Vietnam.

In his speech, Mikoyan said, "I am honored to pay tribute to the heroic Vietnamese people on the territory of free Vietnam. In the liberation war and the struggle for peace, the Vietnamese people have done their best and endured many hardships and sacrifices for many years. Today, the free Vietnamese people are engaged in creative labor in peace. The Vietnamese people face major tasks: to heal the wounds of war, ensure economic development, improve the material and cultural living standards of the people, and achieve the peaceful reunification of the country through free and democratic general elections on the basis of the Geneva Accords." Mikoyan concluded by congratulating the Vietnamese people on their victory in achieving the above tasks on behalf of the Soviet people and government.

Accompanied by Pham Van Dong, the entire Soviet government delegation traveled to the Presidential Palace by car. President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam received them at the Presidential Palace.

On the way to the Presidential Palace, more than 100,000 Hanoi residents waved flowers and red flags along the five-kilometer-long streets, warmly cheered the distinguished guests, and threw flowers at their vehicles.

Also present at the airport today to welcome the Soviet government delegation were: Ministers of the various ministries of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam government, leaders and representatives of the Vietnamese Fatherland Front, various democratic parties and mass organizations, diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries to Vietnam, representatives of the Vietnam International Commission, the General Representative of the French government to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam government, and representatives of various countries' expert delegations currently in Vietnam, totaling more than 4,000 people.

### 苏联代表团到达河内  胡志明主席接见米高扬等

1956-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联代表团到达河内
    胡志明主席接见米高扬等
    新华社河内2日电　以苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬为首的苏联政府代表团，今天下午两点五十分乘飞机到达越南民主共和国首都河内。
    越南民主共和国总理兼外交部长范文同、越南国民大会常务委员会委员长孙德胜、越南人民军总司令武元甲大将和越南劳动党总书记长征都到飞机场欢迎。
    米高扬等下飞机后，和范文同总理等一一握手，并且接受了河内市少年儿童的献花。米高扬在范文同和武元甲陪同下检阅了仪仗队，并且和欢迎的人们热烈握手。米高扬还接受了越南各界人民的代表、中国和印度侨民献给他的鲜花和花环。
    范文同在飞机场上致欢迎词。他对苏联政府代表团的访问表示热烈的欢迎。范文同说，越南人民相信，苏联政府代表团访问越南有着重大的意义，它将更加强越苏两国人民的友谊，使越南人民在建设一个和平、统一、独立、民主和富强的越南的政治斗争中更加坚强。
    米高扬在致辞时说，在自由越南的领土上，我荣幸地向英勇的越南人民致敬。在解放战争和争取和平的斗争中，越南人民尽了自己的力量，并且在许多年头中忍受了许多艰苦和牺牲。今天，自由越南人民在和平中投入创造性的劳动；在越南人民面前摆着重大任务，就是医治战争创伤， 保证经济的发展，提高人民的物质文化水平，并且在日内瓦协定的基础上通过自由民主普选达到国家的和平统一。米高扬最后代表苏联人民和政府，祝贺越南人民在实现上述任务中取得胜利。
    苏联政府代表团全体人员在范文同的陪同下，乘坐汽车赴主席府。越南民主共和国主席胡志明在主席府接见了他们。
    在赴主席府途中，有十万以上的河内居民在长达五公里的街道两旁，挥舞着鲜花和红旗，热烈地向贵宾欢呼，并且向贵宾的座车投掷鲜花。
    今天到飞机场欢迎苏联政府代表团的还有：越南民主共和国政府各部部长，越南祖国战线、各民主党派和群众团体的负责人和代表，各国驻越南的外交使节和外交官员，越南国际委员会的代表，法国政府驻越南民主共和国政府的总代表，现在越南的各国专家代表团的代表等共四千多人。
