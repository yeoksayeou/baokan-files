Title: Activities of Soviet Leaders' Visit to Britain
Author:
Date: 1956-04-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, April 24th (TASS dispatch) - After holding talks with British leaders on the morning of the 24th, Bulganin and Khrushchev left Downing Street at 12:30 PM and went to the nearby Houses of Parliament - the Palace of Westminster.

The Speaker of the House of Commons, Morrison, received the Soviet leaders at the Palace of Westminster. He also hosted a banquet for Bulganin and Khrushchev.

After the banquet, Bulganin and Khrushchev went to the House of Commons and listened to the debate between government representatives and members of parliament. Eden, Chancellor of the Exchequer Macmillan, and others answered various questions from the members of parliament. For example, members asked Eden whether he intended to issue a statement on the talks with the Soviet leaders regarding the situation in the Middle East. Eden replied that he did not intend to issue a statement before the talks were over.

After an hour of answering questions, Bulganin and Khrushchev left the House of Commons and went to the House of Lords.

Xinhua News Agency, London, April 24th (TASS dispatch) - On the afternoon of the 24th, a jointly agreed communiqué was issued in London regarding the talks between Bulganin and Khrushchev and Eden and other British leaders. The communiqué stated that issues concerning Anglo-Soviet trade were discussed during the talks, and discussions on cultural exchanges and disarmament continued.

Xinhua News Agency, London, April 24th (TASS dispatch) - On the evening of April 24th, the Soviet Ambassador to Britain, Malik, held a reception at the "Claridge's" Hotel for Bulganin and Khrushchev's visit to Britain.

More than 1,500 people attended the reception. The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 苏联领导人在英国的访问活动

1956-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联领导人在英国的访问活动
    新华社据塔斯社伦敦24日讯  布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫在二十四日上午同英国领导人举行会谈以后，在十二时三十分离开唐宁街，前往附近的议会大厦——威斯特敏斯特宫。
    下院议长摩里逊在威斯特敏斯特宫接见了苏联领导人。他还设宴招待布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫。
    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫在宴会之后来到下院，旁听政府代表和议员们的辩论。艾登、财政大臣麦克米伦等人回答了议员们的各种质询。例如，议员们问艾登是否打算发表一项关于同苏联领导人就中东局势进行会谈的情况的声明。艾登回答说，在会谈还没有结束的时候，他不打算发表声明。
    在一个小时的答辩结束后，布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫离开下院前往上院。
    新华社据塔斯社伦敦24日讯  二十四日下午，在伦敦发表了关于布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫同艾登和英国其他领导人之间的会谈的双方同意的公报。公报说，会谈期间讨论了关于英苏贸易的问题，并且继续讨论了关于文化交流的问题和裁军问题。
    新华社据塔斯社伦敦24日讯  四月二十四日晚间，苏联驻英国大使马立克在“克莱里吉”饭店为布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫的访问英国举行招待会。
    出席招待会的共有一千五百多人。招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
