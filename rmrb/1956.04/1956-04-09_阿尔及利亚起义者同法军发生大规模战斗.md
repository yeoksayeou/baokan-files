Title: Algerian Insurgents Engage in Large-Scale Battles with French Troops
Author:
Date: 1956-04-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 8th, TASS, Paris, April 8th: According to reports in Parisian newspapers, French troops have engaged in new large-scale armed conflicts with Algerian insurgents. French artillery and air force participated in the conflict. The fighting took place in the residential area of Djelfa (near the Tunisian border) and the Arlan province, resulting in heavy casualties on both sides.

### 阿尔及利亚起义者同法军发生大规模战斗

1956-04-09
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚起义者
    同法军发生大规模战斗
    新华社8日讯　塔斯社巴黎八日讯：据巴黎各报报道，法国军队同阿尔及利亚起义者的队伍发生了新的大规模的武装冲突。法国方面的炮兵和空军都参加了这次冲突。战斗发生在居民区德热尔弗（突尼斯边界附近）和阿兰省地区，结果双方都有很大伤亡。
