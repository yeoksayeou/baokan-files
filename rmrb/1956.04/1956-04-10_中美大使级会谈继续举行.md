Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1956-04-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, April 9th: The Sino-US ambassadorial talks held their forty-third meeting here at 10:00 a.m. this morning. The meeting adjourned at 11:10 a.m. The next meeting is scheduled for April 19th.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1956-04-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦9日电　中美两国大使级会谈九日上午十时在这里举行第四十三次会议，会议在十一时十分休会。下次会议定在四月十九日举行。
