Title: Famous Americans Issue Statement

Author:

Date: 1956-04-03
Page: 4

    Defend the "Daily Worker" in the United States
    Famous Americans Issue Statement
    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. According to a report in the New York "Daily Worker" on March 29th, ten prominent Americans have issued a statement announcing the establishment of an emergency committee to defend freedom of the press, supporting the New York "Daily Worker's" struggle to continue publication. The statement calls on the American people to donate to the committee as a fund to fight for democracy, the Bill of Rights, and freedom of the press.
    The statement said: Because we believe that the action of sealing up the "Daily Worker's" offices and property is a despotic and illegal action that endangers the privileges and rights of all newspapers in the United States, we believe that defending the "Daily Worker" is of paramount importance to all Americans. We believe that defending the right to freedom of the press is a primary responsibility of all patriotic Americans.
    Signatories to the statement include John McManus, Howard Fast, Angus Cameron, Helen Alfred, Dr. Du Bois, Dr. Doxey Wilkerson, and Robert Dunn, among others.

### 美国著名人士发表声明

1956-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    保卫美国“工人日报”
    美国著名人士发表声明
    新华社2日讯　据纽约“工人日报”三月二十九日报道，美国十个著名人士发表了一个声明，宣布成立维护新闻自由非常委员会，支持纽约“工人日报”为继续出版而进行的斗争。声明号召美国人民捐款给这个委员会，作为用来为民主、权利法案及新闻自由而斗争的基金。
    声明中说：由于我们认为查封“工人日报”办事处和财产的行动是危及美国一切报纸的特权和权利的一种专制和非法的行动，因此我们认为保卫“工人日报”对所有美国人都具有首要的意义。我们认为，保卫新闻自由权利是全体爱国的美国人的首要责任。
    在声明上签名的有约翰·麦克曼纳斯，霍华德·法斯特，安格斯·凯麦隆，海伦·艾尔弗雷德，杜波依斯博士，多克西·威尔克逊博士和罗伯特·邓恩等人。
