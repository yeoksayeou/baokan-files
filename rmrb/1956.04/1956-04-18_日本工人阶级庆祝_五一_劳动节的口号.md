Title: Slogans of the Japanese Working Class Celebrating "May Day" Labor Day
Author:
Date: 1956-04-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 17th: The Central "May Day" Labor Day Unified Implementation Committee, composed of national trade unions such as the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, today announced the slogans of the Japanese working class for celebrating this year's "May Day" Labor Day.

These slogans are: Rely on the unity of workers to increase wages, formulate minimum wage laws, and shorten working hours; stop the malicious modification of the social insurance system, oppose layoffs and heavy taxes, and safeguard the basic rights of workers.

The slogans also call on workers to: stop revising the constitution, crush the small electoral district system bill, oppose the use of atomic and hydrogen bombs by any country, and promote the peaceful use of atomic energy.

The Unified Implementation Committee plans to mobilize 500,000 to 600,000 people to participate in the Tokyo "May Day" Labor Day celebration this year. Approximately 4 million people will be mobilized nationwide to participate in the celebrations.

### 日本工人阶级庆祝“五一”劳动节的口号

1956-04-18
第4版()
专栏：

    日本工人阶级庆祝“五一”劳动节的口号
    新华社17日讯　东京消息：日本工会总评议会等全国性工会组成的中央“五一”劳动节统一实行委员会，在今天宣布了日本工人阶级庆祝今年“五一”劳动节的口号。
    这些口号是：依靠工人的团结来提高工资，制定最低限度的工资法，缩短工作时间；制止恶劣地修改社会保险制度，反对解雇和重税，维护工人的基本权利。
    口号还号召工人：制止修改宪法，粉碎小选举区制度法案，反对任何国家使用原子弹和氢弹，促进原子能的和平利用。
    统一实行委员会预定今年要动员五十万到六十万人参加东京的庆祝“五一”劳动节大会。在全国要动员约四百万人进行庆祝活动。
