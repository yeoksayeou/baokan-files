Title: New Zealand Cultural Figures Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 4

    New Zealand Cultural Figures Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    The New Zealand Cultural Figures Delegation, headed by Ormond Wilson and James Bertram, a group of ten people, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 28th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. They will visit China and conduct friendly activities with Chinese cultural figures.

### 新西兰文化界人士访华团到京

1956-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    新西兰文化界人士访华团到京
    以奥蒙德·威尔逊和詹姆斯·伯特来姆为首的新西兰文化界人士访华团一行十人，应中国人民对外文化协会的邀请，已经在二十八日下午到达北京。他们将在中国参观访问并和中国文化界人士进行友好活动。
