Title: Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission and other Units Host Banquet for Returned Overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macao Compatriots
Author:
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: The Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China, the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, and the General Administration Office of the Bank of China, tonight hosted a banquet to welcome overseas Chinese from Vietnam, Indonesia and other places who have returned to the country for sightseeing, compatriots from Hong Kong and Macao who have come to Beijing for sightseeing, and representatives of overseas Chinese relatives from Guangdong Province.
    He Xiangning, Chairman of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China, spoke at the banquet, extending a warm welcome to the returned overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macao compatriots.
    Vice Premier of the State Council He Long then spoke, saying that the motherland's government and people are always warmly concerned about the more than one million compatriots in Hong Kong and Macao and the thirteen million overseas Chinese far away abroad. He hoped that overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macao compatriots would further promote the fine tradition of loving the motherland and expand the patriotic unity of overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macao compatriots themselves.
    Xian Yitang, head of the Vietnamese Overseas Chinese Visiting Group, Huang Yanqing, head of the Hong Kong and Macao Representatives Visiting Group to Beijing, and Chen Shutong, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, also spoke at the meeting.

### 华侨事务委员会等单位欢宴归国华侨和港澳同胞

1956-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    华侨事务委员会等单位欢宴归国华侨和港澳同胞
    据新华社29日讯  中华人民共和国华侨事务委员会、中国文学艺术界联合会、中华全国工商业联合会、中国银行总管理处，今晚设宴欢迎越南、印度尼西亚等地回国观光的华侨，香港澳门到北京观光的同胞和广东省的侨眷代表。
    中华人民共和国华侨事务委员会主任何香凝在宴会上讲话，她对回国侨胞和港澳同胞表示热烈欢迎。
    国务院副总理贺龙接着讲话说，祖国政府和人民时刻都在热烈地关怀着香港澳门的一百多万同胞和远处国外的一千三百万侨胞。他希望海外华侨和港澳同胞更加发挥热爱祖国的优良传统，扩大国外侨胞和港澳同胞本身的爱国团结。
    越南华侨回国观光团的团长冼亦唐，香港澳门各界同胞赴京观光团团长黄燕清和中华全国工商业联合会主任委员陈叔通等都在会上讲了话。
