Title: New Chinese Ambassador to India Presents Credentials to President Prasad
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 17th: Pan Zili, the newly appointed Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of India, accompanied by Counselor Fu Hao and other officials of the Embassy, presented his credentials to Dr. Rajendra Prasad, President of India, on the morning of April 17th.

    When presenting his credentials, Pan Zili delivered a eulogy to President Prasad. Pan Zili said that the Chinese government and the Chinese people greatly cherish the growing great friendship between China and India. Under the initiative of India and other Colombo countries, and with the active participation of twenty-nine Asian and African countries, the historic Bandung Conference has achieved great success and is having an increasingly profound impact internationally.

    Pan Zili said: The Chinese government and the Chinese people are willing to continue to work hard to strengthen the great friendship and cooperative relations between China and India.

    President Prasad delivered a reply. He said that since ancient times, there has been complete mutual goodwill between our two countries. As Your Excellency said, the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence elucidated by our two countries for world peace are being recognized by other countries day by day. I hope that our efforts to establish world peace will achieve even greater success in the years to come.

### 我新任驻印度大使向普拉沙德总统递交国书

1956-04-18
第4版()
专栏：

    我新任驻印度大使向普拉沙德总统递交国书
    据新华社新德里17日电　中华人民共和国新任驻印度共和国大使潘自力在四月十七日上午偕大使馆参赞符浩和其他官员向印度总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德博士递交国书。
    潘自力在递交国书时，向普拉沙德总统致颂词。潘自力说，中国政府和中国人民极为珍视中印两国之间日益发展的伟大友谊。在印度同其他科伦坡国家的倡议和二十九个亚非国家的积极参与下，历史性的万隆会议获得了巨大的成就，并且在国际间正产生着越来越深远的影响。
    潘自力说：中国政府和中国人民愿意继续努力加强中国同印度之间的伟大的友谊和合作关系。
    普拉沙德总统致答词。他说，自从古时以来，在我们两国之间一直存在着圆满的相互之间的善意。正如阁下所说的，我们两个国家为了世界和平而阐明的五项原则正在一天天地获得其他国家的承认。我希望，我们为建立世界和平而作出的努力在将来的年代里将取得更多的成就。
