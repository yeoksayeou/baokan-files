Title: Delegation of Polish Filmmakers Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency report: A delegation of Polish filmmakers, invited by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing on the 3rd, led by Radkiewiczowa, a member of the Presidium of the Polish Central Film Bureau and director of the Polish Documentary Film Studio. The delegation members include screenwriter and director Konwicki, Krulle, director of the Production Organization Department of the Central Film Bureau, and Kulesza, director of the Łódź Province Cinema Administration Bureau. The delegation will exchange experiences in film work with Chinese filmmakers and will also conduct visits and tours.

### 波兰电影工作者代表团到京

1956-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰电影工作者代表团到京
    据新华社讯　应中华人民共和国文化部邀请来中国访问的波兰电影工作者代表团，由波兰中央电影局主席团委员、波兰纪录电影制片厂厂长拉德凯维超娃率领，三日到达北京。代表团团员有编剧和导演孔维茨基，中央电影局生产组织部主任克鲁勒，罗兹省电影院管理局局长库莱沙。代表团将和中国电影工作者交流电影工作经验，并且进行参观和访问。
