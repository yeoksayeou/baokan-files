Title: Ye Jizhuang and Others Depart for Khartoum at the Invitation of the Sudanese Government
Author:
Date: 1956-04-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 4th: Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, and his party of three, left Cairo by plane on the morning of April 4th for Khartoum. They are visiting Sudan formally at the invitation of the government of the Republic of Sudan. Babikir Muhammad Dib, Chargé d'Affaires of the Republic of Sudan in Egypt, saw them off at the airport.

The remaining members of the Chinese trade delegation who were originally in Egypt are currently staying in Cairo to continue negotiations with Egyptian industrialists on trade issues between China and Egypt.

### 叶季壮等应苏丹政府邀请前往喀土穆

1956-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    叶季壮等应苏丹政府邀请前往喀土穆
    新华社开罗4日电　中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮等一行三人在四月四日上午乘飞机离开开罗前往喀土穆。他们是应苏丹共和国政府的邀请前往苏丹进行正式访问。苏丹共和国驻埃及代办巴比克尔·穆罕默德·迪勃到机场上送行。
    原来在埃及的中国贸易访问团的其他人员目前仍留在开罗，继续同埃及工业界人士就中埃两国之间的贸易问题进行谈判。
