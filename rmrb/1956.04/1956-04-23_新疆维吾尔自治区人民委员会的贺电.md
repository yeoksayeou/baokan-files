Title: Telegram of Congratulations from the People's Committee of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1956-04-23
Page: 3

To the Dalai Lama Tenzin Gyatso and the Panchen Erdeni Chokyi Gyaltsen:

    In the past few years since the peaceful liberation of Tibet, under the leadership of the great leader of the Chinese Communist Party, the Central People's Government, and the people of all ethnic groups, Chairman Mao, and with the concerted efforts of all monastic and secular officials and people of all strata in the Tibetan region headed by you, the Tibetan region has achieved tremendous accomplishments in political, economic, and cultural undertakings. On the occasion of the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, the People's Committee of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region and the people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang extend our warm congratulations to you and, through you, to all monastic and secular officials and people of all strata in the Tibetan region on the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region. We congratulate you on the tremendous achievements made in various aspects of work in the Tibetan region in recent years, and we wish you even greater achievements in your future work in all aspects.
                        The People's Committee of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region
                                          April 21, 1956

### 新疆维吾尔自治区人民委员会的贺电

1956-04-23
第3版()
专栏：

    新疆维吾尔自治区人民委员会的贺电达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞：
    西藏和平解放几年来，在中国共产党、中央人民政府和各民族人民的伟大领袖毛主席的领导下，在以你们为首的西藏地区的全体僧俗官员和各阶层人民的一致努力下，西藏地区在政治、经济和文化事业等方面都获得了巨大的成就。当着西藏自治区筹备委员会成立的时候，我们新疆维吾尔自治区人民委员会和新疆各民族人民谨向您们并通过您们向西藏地区的全体僧俗官员和各阶层人民，热烈祝贺西藏自治区筹备委员会的成立，祝贺西藏地区几年来各个方面工作获得的巨大成就，并预祝你们今后在各个方面的工作获得更大的成就。
                        新疆维吾尔自治区人民委员会
                                          1956年4月21日
