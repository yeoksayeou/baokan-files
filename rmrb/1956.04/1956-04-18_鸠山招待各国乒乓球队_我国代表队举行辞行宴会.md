Title:鸠山 Hosts Various National Table Tennis Teams; Our Delegation Holds Farewell Banquet
Author:
Date: 1956-04-18
Page: 4

    HATOYAMA Hosts Various National Table Tennis Teams
    Our Delegation Holds Farewell Banquet
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 17th -- Japanese Prime Minister Ichiro Hatoyama held a garden party at the Prime Minister's official residence today to entertain representatives from various countries and regions participating in the 23rd World Table Tennis Championships.
    Rong Gaotang, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, and the Chinese Table Tennis Delegation were also invited to attend the garden party.
    More than one hundred prominent figures from the political and sports circles of Japan, including Minister of Agriculture and Forestry Ichiro Kono, were invited to attend the garden party.
    Prime Minister Ichiro Hatoyama and his wife talked with Rong Gaotang and the Chinese table tennis players at the garden party and took pictures together. Finally, all attendees watched a documentary film of the Table Tennis Championships.
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 17th -- The Chinese Table Tennis Delegation participating in the 23rd World Table Tennis Championships held a farewell banquet in Tokyo on the 16th.
    Invited to the banquet were Tetsuji Katayama, Chairman of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association; Kenzo Nakajima, Director-General; Takeo Ito, Secretary-General of the Japan-China Friendship Association; Kesaichi Ito, Managing Director of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association; Yatsuo Yamaya, Director-General of the Tokyo Metropolitan Assembly Members' League for the Promotion of International Trade; Tamotsu Ida, Chairman of the All-Japan Youth and Women's Conference; representatives of Korean residents in Japan; and representatives of Overseas Chinese in Japan.
    The banquet proceeded in a friendly and cordial atmosphere. Guests and hosts toasted to promoting sports, cultural and economic exchange between China and Japan, and to enhancing the friendly relations between the two countries.

### 鸠山招待各国乒乓球队  我国代表队举行辞行宴会

1956-04-18
第4版()
专栏：

    鸠山招待各国乒乓球队
    我国代表队举行辞行宴会
    据新华社17日讯　东京消息：日本首相鸠山一郎今天在首相官邸举行游园会，招待参加第二十三届世界乒乓球锦标赛的各国和地区的代表。
    中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠、中国乒乓球代表队也应邀出席了游园会。
    日本方面应邀参加游园会的有农林相河野一郎等政界和体育界著名人士一百多人。
    鸠山一郎首相夫妇在游园会上同荣高棠和中国乒乓球选手们进行了交谈，并且在一起照了像。最后全体与会者观看了乒乓球锦标赛的纪录电影。
    新华社17日讯　东京消息：参加第二十三届世界乒乓球锦标赛的中国乒乓球代表队，十六日在东京举行辞行宴会。
    应邀出席宴会的有日本中国文化交流协会会长片山哲、理事长中岛健藏，日中友好协会事务总长伊藤武雄，日本国际贸易促进协会常务理事伊藤今朝市，东京都议员促进国际贸易联盟理事长山屋八万雄，全日本青年妇女会议议长井田保以及旅日朝鲜侨民代表，旅日华侨代表等。
    宴会在友好亲切的空气中进行。宾主纷纷为促进中日两国体育、文化和经济的交流以及增进中日两国的友好关系而干杯。
