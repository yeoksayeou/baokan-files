Title: Burmese President Hosts Banquet in Honor of Mikoyan
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, March 31st: The President of the Union of Burma, Ba U, hosted a banquet on March 30th to welcome the First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Mikoyan. The Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of Uzbekistan, Rashidov, their accompanying personnel, and the Soviet Ambassador to Burma, S.P. Suzdalev, attended the banquet.

That same afternoon, Mikoyan laid a wreath at the tomb of Aung San, a leader in the Burmese people's struggle for national independence.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, March 31st: A garden party was held at the Presidential Palace in Burma on the afternoon of March 31st to welcome the First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Mikoyan.

Mikoyan attended the garden party wearing Burmese national clothing. Approximately six hundred guests enthusiastically welcomed Mikoyan. Mikoyan, accompanied by the President of Burma, Ba U, and Prime Minister U Nu, enjoyed Burmese songs and dances.

On that morning, Mikoyan and his entourage visited factories near Rangoon. At nine o'clock in the morning, they arrived at a textile factory in Thamaing. This is the largest textile factory in Burma, with 20,000 spindles. Currently, with the technical assistance of Chinese technicians, the factory is being expanded. After visiting the workshops, Mikoyan wrote in the guest book: I am very happy to visit the first textile factory in Burma, which is undergoing industrialization. I sincerely wish you to establish a highly developed industry on your land to eliminate the abominable vestiges of colonial rule—your backwardness.

Mikoyan called on Prime Minister U Nu at noon and had lunch with him.

### 缅甸总统设宴欢迎米高扬

1956-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸总统设宴欢迎米高扬
    新华社据塔斯社仰光31日讯　缅甸联邦总统巴宇在三月三十日设宴欢迎苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬。参加宴会的有乌兹别克最高苏维埃主席团主席拉希多夫，他们的随行人员和苏联驻缅甸大使希鲍林。
    同一天下午，米高扬在缅甸人民争取民族独立斗争中的领袖昂山的墓前献了花圈。
    据新华社仰光31日电　缅甸总统府在三月三十一日下午举行了游园会，欢迎苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬。
    米高扬穿着缅甸民族服装参加游园会。到会的大约六百名客人都热情地欢迎米高扬。米高扬在缅甸总统巴宇和总理吴努的陪同下欣赏了缅甸的歌舞。
    在这一天上午，米高扬和随行人员参观了仰光市附近的工厂。他们在上午九时到了达棉的一个纺织厂。这是缅甸最大的一个纺织厂，有两万个纱锭，现在，这个工厂在中国技术人员的技术帮助下正在扩充。在参观了各车间以后米高扬在来宾留言簿上写道：我参观了正在进行工业化的缅甸的纺织业的第一家工厂，感到非常高兴。我衷心祝你们在你们的土地上建立起高度发展的工业，以消除殖民统治的可恶的遗迹——你们的落后状态。
    米高扬在中午拜会了吴努总理，并且同吴努同进午餐。
