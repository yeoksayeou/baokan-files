Title: Bourhan and Others Visit Sudanese Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1956-04-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Khartoum, April 25th: Bourhan, head of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, and deputy heads Ma Yuhuai and Ma Hanbing, as well as Zhang Yue, the delegation's advisor and Deputy Commercial Representative of China to Egypt, visited Sudanese Foreign Minister and Minister of Justice Mubarak Zarouk at noon on April 24th.

During the conversation, both sides expressed the hope of developing friendly cooperation between China and Sudan by realizing the spirit of the Bandung Conference.

Prior to this, Bourhan had visited Sudanese Minister of Education Ali Abdel Rahman.

### 鲍尔汉等访问苏丹外交部长

1956-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    鲍尔汉等访问苏丹外交部长
    新华社喀土穆25日电  中国文化艺术代表团团长鲍尔汉，副团长马玉槐和马寒冰，以及代表团的顾问、中国驻埃及副商务代表张越，在四月二十四日中午访问了苏丹外交部长兼司法部长穆巴拉克·查鲁克。
    在谈话时，双方都希望通过实现万隆会议精神来发展中国和苏丹的友好合作。
    在这以前，鲍尔汉曾经访问了苏丹教育部长阿里·阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼。
