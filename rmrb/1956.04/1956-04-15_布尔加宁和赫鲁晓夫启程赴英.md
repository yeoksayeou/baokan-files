    Bulganin and Khrushchev Depart for England
    (Our Moscow dispatch, April 14th) Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, and Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, along with their entourage, left Moscow this afternoon for a visit to England. They will first travel by train and then reach England via the Baltic Sea. The Belorussky railway station in Moscow was decorated with Soviet flags. Seeing them off at the station were Soviet party and government leaders, diplomatic envoys, and Soviet and foreign journalists. At 2:15 p.m., Bulganin and Khrushchev bid farewell to everyone, and the special train slowly departed from the station.

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫启程赴英

1956-04-15
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫启程赴英
    本报莫斯科14日专电　苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫及随行人员今天下午离开莫斯科赴英国访问。他们将首先乘坐火车，然后经波罗的海到达英国。莫斯科的白俄罗斯车站上挂着苏联国旗。到车站送行的有苏联党政领导人员、外交使节和苏联与外国记者。下午二时十五分，布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫向大家告别，专车缓缓开离车站。
