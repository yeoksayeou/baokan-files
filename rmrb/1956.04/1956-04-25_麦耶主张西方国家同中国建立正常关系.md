Title: Mayer Advocates for Western Countries to Establish Normal Relations with China
Author:
Date: 1956-04-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th, Dateline Paris, TASS, April 23rd: According to newspaper reports, the Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the French National Assembly, Daniel Mayer (Socialist), delivered a speech in Angers, advocating that Western countries establish normal relations with the People's Republic of China.

### 麦耶主张西方国家同中国建立正常关系

1956-04-25
第4版()
专栏：

    麦耶主张西方国家同中国建立正常关系
    新华社24日讯  塔斯社巴黎二十三日讯：据报纸报道，法国国民议会外交委员会主席丹尼埃·麦耶（社会党人）在翁热城发表演说，主张西方国家同中华人民共和国建立正常的关系。
