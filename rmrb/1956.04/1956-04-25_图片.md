Title: The Spring Salt Production Has Begun at the Sandaowan Salt Field in Liaoning Province
Author:
Date: 1956-04-25
Page: 1

The spring salt production has begun at the Sandaowan Salt Field in Liaoning Province. Salt workers have expanded the salt evaporation area and have proposed to complete the production targets of the first Five-Year Plan by May of next year. The picture shows the salt workers collecting salt in the salt field.
