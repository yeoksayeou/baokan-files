Title: The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and the Government Decide to Lower Consumer Goods Prices for the Fifth Time
Author:
Date: 1956-04-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 31st. According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency, the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic have decided to once again lower state retail prices for food and industrial products, effective April 1st of this year. This is the fifth price reduction since the currency reform in Czechoslovakia in 1953. Residents will benefit by 2.1 billion crowns (Czechoslovak currency) from this price reduction.

### 捷共中央和政府决定第五次降低消费品价格

1956-04-01
第4版()
专栏：

    捷共中央和政府决定第五次降低消费品价格
    新华社布拉格31日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会和捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府，决定从今年四月一日起再次降低食品和工业品的国家零售价格。这是1953年捷克斯洛伐克实行币制改革以来的第五次降低物价，从这次降低物价中，居民将可以获得二十一亿克朗（捷币名）的利益。
