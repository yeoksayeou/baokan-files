Title: Third Congress of the Workers' Party of Korea Continues
Author:
Date: 1956-04-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 24th. The Third Congress of the Workers' Party of Korea continued today. Today's meeting began discussing the summary report on the work of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea made by Chairman Kim Il-sung on April 23rd, and the summary report on the work of the Party's Inspection Committee made by Li Zhouyuan, Chairman of the Central Inspection Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea.

Comrade Brezhnev, head of the delegation of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and Comrade Nie Rongzhen, head of the delegation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, delivered congratulatory speeches to the congress this morning and afternoon, respectively. All the representatives applauded warmly and for a long time to express their welcome.

The speakers at today's meeting discussing the reports included: Kim Du-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, Kim Kwang-hyup, Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, as well as representatives of the Party organizations in Pyongyang, South Hwanghae Province, North Hwanghae Province, South Pyongan Province, North Pyongan Province, and other local areas and factories and mines.

### 朝鲜劳动党第三次代表大会继续举行

1956-04-25
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜劳动党第三次代表大会继续举行
    新华社平壤24日电  朝鲜劳动党第三次代表大会今天继续举行。今天的会议开始讨论四月二十三日金日成委员长所作的关于朝鲜劳动党中央委员会工作总结报告和朝鲜劳动党中央检查委员会委员长李周渊所作的关于党的检查工作总结报告。
    苏联共产党代表团团长勃列日涅夫和中国共产党中央委员会代表团团长聂荣臻分别在今天上午和下午向大会致祝词，全场代表长久地热烈鼓掌，表示欢迎。
    在今天讨论报告的会上发言的有：朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉，朝鲜人民军总参谋长金光侠以及平壤市、黄海南道、黄海北道、平安南道、平安北道等地方和厂矿的党组织的代表。
