Title: Amir Appointed Commander-in-Chief of Joint Armed Forces of Egypt, Saudi Arabia, and Yemen
Author:
Date: 1956-04-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 23rd (TASS): According to newspaper reports, with the common consent of the heads of state of Egypt, Saudi Arabia, and Yemen, General Abdul Hakim Amir, Commander-in-Chief of the Egyptian Armed Forces and Minister of the Army and Navy, has been appointed Commander-in-Chief of the armed forces of these three countries, which have been placed under a joint command.
　　
It is known that, according to the bilateral defense treaty between Egypt and Syria, Amir has already been appointed Commander-in-Chief of the joint armed forces of the two countries. "Al-Gomhuria" pointed out that, therefore, General Amir is now the Commander-in-Chief of the armed forces of four Arab countries: Egypt, Syria, Saudi Arabia, and Yemen.

### 阿密尔被任命为埃、沙、也三国联合武装部队总司令

1956-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

    阿密尔被任命为埃、沙、也三国联合武装部队总司令
    新华社24日讯  塔斯社开罗二十三日讯：据报纸报道，经过埃及、沙特阿拉伯和也门三国首脑的共同同意，埃及武装部队总司令、陆海军部长阿卜杜勒·哈基姆·阿密尔将军已经被任命为这三国拨归一个联合司令部指挥的武装部队的总司令。
    大家知道，根据埃及和叙利亚之间的双边防御条约，阿密尔已经被任命为这两国的联合武装部队的总司令。“共和国报”指出，因此，阿密尔将军现在是埃及、叙利亚、沙特阿拉伯和也门四个阿拉伯国家的武装部队的总司令。
