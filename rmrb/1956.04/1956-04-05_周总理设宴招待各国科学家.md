Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Banquet for Scientists from Various Countries
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China hosted a banquet tonight to entertain the executive council members, observers from various countries, and staff attending the 16th Council Meeting and 10th Anniversary Celebration of the World Federation of Scientific Workers.

Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a speech at the beginning of the banquet. He said that it was an honor for the Chinese people to have the World Federation of Scientific Workers hold its meeting in China. China is an economically and culturally backward country. The new China has not been established for long and is beginning its construction. It needs to gradually approach and catch up with the levels of many advanced countries in terms of economy and culture, and it needs the help of scientists from many advanced countries. Premier Zhou Enlai hoped that scientists from various countries could give lectures to the Chinese scientific and educational communities during their travels in China. He also said that China's door is open to righteous scientists from around the world. Chinese scientists are also willing to visit countries around the world that are willing to receive us. He hoped that righteous scientists would advocate peaceful coexistence and promote academic exchanges. He said that the Chinese government and Chinese scientists are willing to contribute their efforts to this cause that is beneficial to human progress.

Professor Powell of Britain, Vice-President of the World Federation of Scientific Workers and Chairman of the Executive Council, spoke at the meeting. He said that their visit to China was one of the most memorable moments of their lives; what impressed him most was the excellent character of the Chinese people. He said that the Chinese people have already demonstrated their strength in creatively mastering social sciences, and there is every reason to believe that China will soon catch up with the advanced countries of the world in various scientific fields. Professor Powell also stated in his speech that the World Federation of Scientific Workers will do its utmost to promote cooperation and mutual visits between scientists from various countries.

The executive council members and observers from various countries attending today's banquet were: Professor Powell (Britain), Academician Oparin (Soviet Union), Professor Li Siguang (China), Dr. Wooster (Britain), Academician Malek (Czechoslovakia), Dr. Edwards (Britain), Professor Zhou Peiyuan (China), Berringer (Britain), Dr. Válko (Hungary), Dr. Nørregaard (Denmark), Professor Hua Luogeng (China), Professor Bratanov (Bulgaria), Professor Tuge Hideomi (Japan), Dhawan (India), Professor Butschilowski (German Democratic Republic), Jakubowski (Poland), Sutskov (Soviet Union), Bija (France), Dr. Bashir Ahmed (Pakistan), Candidate Dr. Misk (Mongolia), Petre Dudevice (Albania), Professor Oliver Russo (Romania), Li Jaekun (North Korea), Rais Ahmed (India), Chen Kangbai (China), Tu Changwang (China), Shen Qiyi (China), as well as staff members Burghope (Britain), ?Mejz (Czechoslovakia), Ms. Remell (Britain), Ms. Hendry (Britain), Mrs. Emisia Allen Young (Britain), Zabrogin.

Also present at the banquet were Vice-Premier of the State Council Li Fuchun, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Guo Moruo, heads of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, and other people's organizations, heads of research departments directly under the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Chinese scientists.

At the banquet, Chinese scientists had cordial conversations with scientists from various countries. The banquet was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 周总理设宴招待各国科学家

1956-04-05
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理设宴招待各国科学家
    新华社4日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天晚上举行宴会，招待参加世界科学工作者协会第十六届理事会和成立十周年纪念会的世界科协第十六届执行理事会理事、各国观察员及工作人员。
    周恩来总理在宴会开始的时候致词。他说，世界科协在中国开会，使中国人民得以享受光荣。中国是一个在经济上、文化上落后的国家。新中国成立不久，正在开始建设，需要在经济上、文化上逐步地接近和赶上许多先进国家的水平，需要得到许多先进国家科学家的帮助。周恩来总理希望各国科学家在中国旅行期间，能为中国的科学界、教育界讲学。他还说，中国的大门是为世界正义的科学家们敞开着的。中国科学界人士也愿意到世界上愿意让我们去的国家访问。他希望正义的科学家们倡导和平共处，提倡学术交流。他说，中国政府和中国的科学家愿在这个有利于人类进步的事业上贡献自己的力量。
    世界科协副主席和执行理事会主席、英国鲍威尔教授在会上讲了话。他说，在中国的访问是他们一生中最值得记忆的时刻；给他印象最深的是中国人民的优良品德。他说，中国人民已经在创造性地掌握社会科学上表现了自己的力量，完全有理由相信，中国能够在各个科学领域上很快赶上世界上先进国家的水平。鲍威尔教授在讲话中还表示，世界科协将尽它的一切力量来推动各国科学家之间的合作和互相访问。
    出席今天宴会的世界科协第十六届执行理事会理事和各国观察员是：鲍威尔教授（英国）、奥巴林院士（苏联）、李四光教授（中国）、伍斯特博士（英国）、马列克院士（捷克）、爱德华博士（英国）、周培源教授（中国）、柏林格尔（英国）、瓦尔可博士（匈牙利）、诺瑞加德博士（丹麦）、华罗庚教授（中国）、勃拉泰诺夫教授（保加利亚）、柘植秀臣教授（日本）、达万（印度）、布特希拉乌斯基教授（德意志民主共和国）、杰库博夫斯基（波兰）、苏特斯科夫（苏联）、比加博士（法国）、贝书尔·阿梅特博士（巴基斯坦）、米斯克候补博士（蒙古）、彼德雷杜维次嘉（阿尔巴尼亚）、奥利佛·卢素教授（罗马尼亚）、李载坤（朝鲜）、雷士·阿梅特（印度）、陈康白（中国）、涂长望（中国）、沈其益（中国）以及工作人员柏贺浦（英国）、?麦茨（捷克）、瑞米尔女士（英国）、亨德黎女士（英国）、爱米西亚·爱伦·杨夫人（英国）、扎布罗金。
    出席作陪的，有国务院副总理李富春、秘书长习仲勋，北京市市长彭真，中国科学院院长郭沫若，中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、中华全国科学技术普及协会以及其他各人民团体的负责人、中国科学院直属研究部门的负责人和中国的科学家们。
    宴会上，中国科学家同各国科学家亲切地交谈。宴会充满了热烈友好的气氛。
