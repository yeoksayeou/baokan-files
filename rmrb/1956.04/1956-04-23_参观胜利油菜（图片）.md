Title: Visiting the "Victory" Rapeseed
Author: Zhang Qicai
Date: 1956-04-23
Page: 2

    Visiting the "Victory" Rapeseed
    The visiting delegation to Sichuan Province to study rapeseed planting techniques was organized by the Ministry of Agriculture and included more than 100 agricultural labor models, representatives of agricultural producers' cooperatives, and agronomists from ten provinces, including Hunan and Anhui. The picture shows representatives from various provinces visiting the large-scale high-yield field of the "Victory" rapeseed variety at the Second Agricultural Production Cooperative in Gongping Township, Wenjiang County. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Qicai

    参观胜利油菜
    到四川省学习油菜种植技术的参观团，是由农业部组织的，包括湖南、安徽等十个省的农业劳动模范、农业生产合作社的代表、农学专家等一百多人。图为各省的代表们在温江县公平乡第二农业生产合作社参观“胜利油菜”良种大面积丰产田。新华社记者  张齐才摄
