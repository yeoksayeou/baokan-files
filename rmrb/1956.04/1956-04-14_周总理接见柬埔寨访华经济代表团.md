Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Cambodian Economic Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1956-04-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 13th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, received this evening the head of the Cambodian Economic Delegation to China, Minister of Public Works, Telecommunications, and Planning, Hou Youn, as well as delegation members Director of the National Postal and Telecommunications Bureau, former Minister Ly Loume, Member of Parliament, former Minister Mau Say, and delegation secretary Ly Kimhinh. Present at the reception were Acting Minister of Foreign Trade Li Zheren, Vice Minister Lei Renmin, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Jiakang, and Director of the Fourth Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade Yang Haolu.

### 周总理接见柬埔寨访华经济代表团

1956-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见柬埔寨访华经济代表团
    新华社13日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天晚间接见了柬埔寨王国访华经济代表团团长、工程邮电和计划部长胡森阿，代表团团员、全国邮电局局长、前部长李洛穆，议员、前部长毛赛，以及代表团秘书李金兴。接见时在座的，有对外贸易部代理部长李哲人、副部长雷任民，外交部部长助理陈家康，对外贸易部第四局局长杨浩庐。
