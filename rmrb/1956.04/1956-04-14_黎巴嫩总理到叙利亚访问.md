Title: Lebanese Prime Minister Visits Syria
Author:
Date: 1956-04-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, April 13th: According to an official announcement, Lebanese Prime Minister Abdullah Yafi and a delegation of ministers arrived in Damascus on April 12th for a four-day visit. Yafi will hold talks with Syrian President Kuwatli.

### 黎巴嫩总理到叙利亚访问

1956-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩总理到叙利亚访问
    新华社13日讯　大马士革消息：据官方发表的公报说，黎巴嫩总理阿卜杜拉·雅非和一个由部长组成的代表团在四月十二日到达大马士革，作为时四天的访问。雅非将同叙利亚总统库阿特利举行会谈。
