Title: Inauguration of "Tibet Daily"
Author:
Date: 1956-04-23
Page: 3

The "Tibet Daily," the official newspaper of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, was inaugurated on the 22nd.

This newspaper will be published daily in both Tibetan and Chinese languages.

The inaugural editorial stated: "Under the leadership of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, the Dalai Lama, the Panchen Erdeni, and the Tibet Working Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Tibet Daily will propagate the Chinese Communist Party, the Central People's Government, and Chairman Mao Zedong's policies of national unity and equality, freedom of religious belief, national regional autonomy, and the principles of unity, progress, and greater development; promote patriotic ideology, educate cadres and people of all strata, and raise political consciousness; clarify the guidelines, policies, and measures of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region in its various tasks, and guide the development and implementation of various tasks in Tibet; reflect national unity and internal unity in Tibet, as well as the production, construction, and living conditions of the Tibetan people in the common construction of the great family of the motherland; exchange and disseminate various work experiences; introduce the great achievements of socialist construction in the motherland and the development of the world peace and democracy movement; and introduce the history, culture, and art of Tibet, as well as modern scientific knowledge."

The "Tibet Daily" was formerly the "Lhasa News Briefs." The "Lhasa News Briefs" was first published on November 1, 1952. The Tibetan and Chinese editions of the "Lhasa News Briefs" were discontinued on the 21st.

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### “西藏日报”创刊

1956-04-23
第3版()
专栏：

    “西藏日报”创刊
    西藏自治区筹备委员会机关报“西藏日报”在二十二日创刊。
    这个报纸将每天用藏、汉两种文字出版。
    创刊号的发刊词说：“西藏日报将在西藏自治区筹备委员会、达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼和中共西藏工委的领导下，宣传中国共产党、中央人民政府和毛泽东主席的民族团结平等政策、宗教信仰自由政策、民族区域自治政策和团结、进步、更加发展的方针；宣传爱国主义思想，教育干部和各阶层人民，提高政治觉悟；阐明西藏自治区筹备委员会各项工作的方针、政策和措施，指导西藏各项工作的开展和进行；反映民族团结和西藏内部的团结，以及西藏人民在共同建设祖国大家庭中的生产建设和生活情况，交流与传播各种工作经验；介绍祖国社会主义建设的各项伟大成就和世界和平民主运动的发展情况；介绍西藏的历史、文化和艺术，以及现代的科学知识。”
    “西藏日报”的前身即“拉萨新闻简讯”。“拉萨新闻简讯”是1952年11月1日创刊的。“拉萨新闻简讯”的藏汉文版，都已经在二十一日终刊。
            （据新华社讯）
