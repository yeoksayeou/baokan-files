Title: Argentine Medical Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 19th: The Argentine medical delegation of fifteen people, led by Professor Argens, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese Medical Association. The delegation is composed of well-known figures in the Argentine medical field, including doctors in internal medicine, surgery, pediatrics, and obstetrics and gynecology.

### 阿根廷医学代表团到京

1956-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    阿根廷医学代表团到京
    据新华社19日讯　应中华医学会邀请来中国访问的阿根廷医学代表团一行十五人，在团长阿尔根斯教授率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。代表团是由阿根廷医学界著名人士组成的，其中包括内科、外科、小儿科、妇产科医生等。
