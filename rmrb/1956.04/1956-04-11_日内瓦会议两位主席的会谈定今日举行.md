Title: Geneva Conference Co-Chairmen's Meeting Scheduled for Today
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-11
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 9th: TASS reported that regarding the Soviet Foreign Ministry's note on the meeting of the two chairmen of the Geneva Conference on Indochina, published in Soviet newspapers on April 6th, the British government has notified that it accepts the Soviet government's proposal to hold this meeting soon. The British government has authorized the Under-Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs, Lord Reading, to participate in the talks as the representative of the British Foreign Secretary, one of the chairmen of the Geneva Conference.
    The first meeting between Gromyko and Lord Reading is scheduled to take place on April 11th.

### 日内瓦会议两位主席的会谈定今日举行

1956-04-11
第4版()
专栏：

    日内瓦会议两位主席的会谈定今日举行
    新华社10日讯　塔斯社莫斯科九日讯：关于四月六日苏联报纸上发表的苏联外交部关于印度支那问题的日内瓦会议的两位主席会晤问题的照会，英国政府通知说，它接受苏联政府提出的关于最近举行这一会晤的建议。英国政府授权外交副大臣里丁勋爵作为日内瓦会议主席之一的英国外交大臣的代表参加会谈。
    葛罗米柯和里丁勋爵的第一次会晤定于四月十一日举行。
