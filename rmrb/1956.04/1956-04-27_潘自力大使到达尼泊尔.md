Title: Ambassador Pan Zili Arrives in Nepal
Author:
Date: 1956-04-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, 26th: Chinese Ambassador Pan Zili arrived in Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal, by plane from India on the afternoon of April 25th. Among those who welcomed him at the airport were Lieutenant General Rana, the Military Secretary to the King of Nepal, Deputy Secretary Singh of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, other senior officials of the Nepalese government, and representatives of the Nepal-China Friendship Association and other mass organizations.

    Ambassador Pan Zili will be here to attend the coronation ceremony of King Mahendra of Nepal.
    Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, 26th: The newly appointed Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, Pan Zili, called on Foreign Minister Sharma at the Nepalese Ministry of Foreign Affairs at noon on April 26th to discuss matters concerning the presentation of his credentials. Then, Pan Zili met with Finance, Industry and Commerce Minister Gunja Man Singh. In the afternoon of the same day, Sharma paid a return visit to the Chinese Ambassador's residence.

### 潘自力大使到达尼泊尔

1956-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    潘自力大使到达尼泊尔
    据新华社加德满都26日电  中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力在四月二十五日下午从印度乘飞机到达尼泊尔首都加德满都。到机场欢迎他的有尼泊尔国王军事秘书拉纳中将、外交部副秘书辛格、尼泊尔政府其他高级官员以及尼中友好协会和其他群众团体的代表等。
    潘自力大使将在这里参加尼泊尔国王马亨德拉的加冕典礼。
    新华社加德满都26日电  新任命的中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力四月二十六日中午到尼泊尔外交部拜会外交大臣夏尔马，商谈递交国书事宜。然后，潘自力又同财政、工业和商业大臣贡杰曼·辛格会见。当天下午，夏尔马到中国大使的寓所作了回拜。
