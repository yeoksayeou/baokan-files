Title: Mikoyan and Rakhimov Leave Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 8th—After concluding their visit to China, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Mikoyan, and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Uzbek Republic, Rakhimov, departed Beijing by plane this morning.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing, Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, Nie Rongzhen, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Wentian, Deputy Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Tan Zhenlin, Vice Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, Cai Tingkai, Vice Chairman of the China Democratic League, Zhang Bojun, Deputy Directors of the China Democratic National Construction Association, Zhang Naiqi, Li Zhuchen, as well as heads of various government departments, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, responsible personnel of various departments of the CPC Central Committee, heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and cadres from the capital's agencies.

Soviet Ambassador to China, Yudin, embassy staff, and Soviet experts in Beijing also went to the airport to see them off.

Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries who went to the airport to see them off included: the ambassadors of Burma, Romania, Albania, Hungary, Finland, Czechoslovakia, Pakistan, Yugoslavia, Afghanistan, the minister of Switzerland, the chargés d'affaires ad interim of Indonesia, Poland, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, India, the German Democratic Republic, Bulgaria, Mongolia, the chargé d'affaires ad interim of the British Representative Office, the Deputy Representative of the Egyptian Commercial Representative Office, and officials from the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Legation of Denmark.

Before boarding the plane, Deputy Chairman Mikoyan shook hands with Premier Zhou Enlai and others one by one, and bid farewell to all who had come to see them off. As the plane took off, a burst of applause resounded throughout the airport. (Attached picture)

### 米高扬和拉希多夫离开北京

1956-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    米高扬和拉希多夫离开北京
    新华社8日讯　苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬和乌兹别克共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席拉希多夫结束了在中国的访问后，已在今天早晨乘飞机离开北京。
    到机场欢送的，有中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，副总理陈云、彭德怀、邓小平、李富春、李先念，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒、郭沫若，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真，国防委员会副主席聂荣臻，外交部副部长张闻天，中共中央副秘书长谭震林，中国国民党革命委员会副主席蔡廷锴，中国民主同盟副主席章伯钧，中国民主建国会副主任委员章乃器、李烛尘，以及政府各部门的负责人，中国人民解放军的高级将领，中共中央各部门负责工作人员，各民主党派、人民团体的负责人和首都机关干部。
    苏联驻中国大使尤金和使馆人员以及在京的苏联专家也到机场欢送。
    到机场欢送的各国使节和外交官员有：缅甸、罗马尼亚、阿尔巴尼亚、匈牙利、芬兰、捷克斯洛伐克、巴基斯坦、南斯拉夫、阿富汗的大使，瑞士的公使，印度尼西亚、波兰、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、印度、德意志民主共和国、保加利亚、蒙古的临时代办，英国代办处的临时代办和埃及商务代表处副代表，越南民主共和国大使馆和丹麦公使馆的官员。
    米高扬副主席在登上飞机前，同周恩来总理等一一握手，并向所有前来欢送的人们告别。当飞机起飞的时候，机场上响彻着一片掌声。（附图片）
