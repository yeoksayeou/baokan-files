Title: Cotton Sowing in沟赵 Agricultural Cooperative, Zhengzhou

Author: 
Date: 1956-04-11
Page: 1

The沟赵 Agricultural Production Cooperative in the suburbs of Zhengzhou City, Henan Province, plans to sow 21,000 mu of cotton this year. The members are using wooden rakes with adjusted row spacing to sow cotton, implementing rational close planting, and striving for a greater harvest.
