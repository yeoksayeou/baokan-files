Title: American Women Demand Cancellation of Hydrogen Bomb Tests
Author:
Date: 1956-04-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 23rd: The Women's International League for Peace and Freedom of the United States wrote to Eisenhower on the 21st, demanding the cancellation of a series of hydrogen bomb tests scheduled to take place in the Pacific next month. The letter was signed by fifty-one people, including Nobel Prize winner Emily Greene Balch, Roger Baldwin of the New York Civil Liberties Union, and writer Sterling F. Bar.
    Prior to this, the League also wrote a letter to Congress, requesting a halt to nuclear weapons testing and striving to reach an agreement on disarmament.

### 美国妇女要求取消氢弹试验

1956-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

    美国妇女要求取消氢弹试验
    新华社23日讯  华盛顿消息：美国妇女争取和平和自由国际联盟二十一日写信给艾森豪威尔，要求取消定于下月在太平洋举行的一系列氢弹试验。在这封信上签名的有诺贝尔奖金获得者埃米利·格林·贝尔奇、纽约争取人权联盟的罗杰·鲍德温和作家斯特林番罗·巴尔等五十一人。
    在这以前，联盟还写了一封信给国会，要求停止核子武器试验，争取就裁军问题达成协议。
