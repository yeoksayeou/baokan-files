Title: Chiang Kai-shek Troops Shell Fujian Coastal Villages
Author:
Date: 1956-04-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, March 31st. From 9:43 PM on March 28th to 3:30 PM on March 29th, Chiang Kai-shek's artillery on Greater and Lesser Kinmen Islands shelled our areas of Xiamen Island, Dadeng Island, Jiaoyu Island, and Lianhe, etc. They fired more than 1,140 shells, destroying 16 civilian houses, killing 6 residents, and wounding 4; 5 head of cattle were killed and 3 wounded.

The artillery units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army retaliated with fierce artillery fire.

At 3:00 PM on March 29th, Chiang Kai-shek troops entrenched on Wuchiu Island hijacked two fishing boats from Hui'an County and nine fishermen in the sea north of Wuchiu Island.

### 蒋军炮击福建沿海村庄

1956-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋军炮击福建沿海村庄
    新华社福建前线31日电　三月二十八日晚九时四十三分到二十九日下午三时三十分，大、小金门岛蒋军炮兵先后向我厦门岛、大嶝岛、角屿和莲河等地区炮击。发射炮弹一千一百四十多发，击毁民房十六间，打死居民六人，伤四人；打死耕牛五头，伤三头。
    中国人民解放军炮兵部队以猛烈炮火给予还击。
    三月二十九日下午三时，盘踞乌丘屿的蒋军在乌丘屿以北海面劫去惠安县渔船两只和渔民九人。
