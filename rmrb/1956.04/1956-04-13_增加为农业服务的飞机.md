Title: Increase Aircraft Serving Agriculture

Author:

Date: 1956-04-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch:

    The number of aircraft used for agricultural production in China this year will more than double compared to last year. These aircraft will primarily be used to control locusts, the most damaging pest to crops. Some will also be used by farmers to control late-season cotton bollworms, wheat midges, rice pests, armyworms, and rice blast. In addition, they will help farmers apply fertilizer to wheat.

    A small aircraft is used to perform special flight missions such as eliminating locusts and applying fertilizer. An An-2 type aircraft can spray pesticide powder over 40,000 to 50,000 mu of locust-infested farmland in a single day, equivalent to the work of 40,000 people in one day.

### 增加为农业服务的飞机

1956-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    增加为农业服务的飞机
    今年中国用来为农业生产服务的飞机，将比去年增加一倍多。这些飞机主要是用作防治危害农作物最大的害虫——蝗虫；还有一部分，要为农民防治后期棉虫、小麦吸浆虫、水稻害虫、粘虫和稻热病。另外，还要帮助农民为小麦追肥。
    担负消灭蝗虫、施肥等特殊飞行任务的是一种小飞机。一架安二型的飞机一天可以把药粉喷洒到四万到五万亩有蝗虫的农田上，抵得上四万个人一天的工作。　（据新华社讯）
