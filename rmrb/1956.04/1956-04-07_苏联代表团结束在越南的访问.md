Title: Soviet Delegation Concludes Visit to Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1956-04-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 6th. The Soviet government delegation, headed by Soviet First Deputy Premier of the Council of Ministers Anastas Mikoyan, concluded its visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and left Hanoi by plane this morning for a visit to China.

Those who saw them off at the airport included: Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Pham Van Dong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Vietnamese National Assembly and Chairman of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Fatherland Front Ton Duc Thang, General Secretary of the Vietnamese Labor Party Truong Chinh, and more than 5,000 representatives of the people from Hanoi and other places.

Prime Minister Pham Van Dong spoke at the airport. He said that the visit of the Soviet government delegation has further inspired the Vietnamese people and made them more confident in the great strength and possibilities of the Vietnamese nation in its just struggle to restore the economy, develop culture, consolidate the North, consolidate peace, and achieve national reunification on the basis of the Geneva Accords. Pham Van Dong asked the delegation to convey the fraternal friendship of the Vietnamese people to the Soviet people.

Mikoyan warmly bid farewell to the Vietnamese people, saying that the Soviet government delegation's visit to Vietnam and the talks between the leaders of the two countries will further develop and consolidate the cooperation and friendship between the peoples of the Soviet Union and Vietnam.

### 苏联代表团结束在越南的访问

1956-04-07
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联代表团结束在越南的访问
    据新华社河内6日电　以苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬为首的苏联政府代表团结束了在越南民主共和国的访问，在今天早晨乘飞机离开河内，前往中国访问。
    到飞机场欢送的有：越南民主共和国总理兼外交部长范文同，越南国民大会常务委员会委员长、越南祖国战线中央委员会主席孙德胜，越南劳动党总书记长征，河内和其他地方的人民代表共五千多人。
    范文同总理在飞机场上讲话。他说，苏联政府代表团的访问使越南人民更加振奋，更加相信越南民族在恢复经济、发展文化、巩固北方、巩固和平和在日内瓦协议的基础上实现国家统一的正义斗争中的巨大力量和可能。范文同请代表团把越南人民兄弟般的友谊带给苏联人民。
    米高扬热情地向越南人民告别说，苏联政府代表团的这次访问越南以及两国领导人间的会谈将进一步发展和巩固苏联和越南两国人民之间的合作和友谊。
