Title: Sino-Vietnamese Air Route Trial Flight Successful, To Be Officially Opened Soon; Premier Zhou Sends Telegram to Premier Pham Van Dong to Congratulate
Author:
Date: 1956-04-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 23rd: The air route between the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, from Guangzhou via Nanning to Hanoi, has successfully completed its trial flights yesterday and today by the Civil Aviation Administration of China.

	The entire route is 852 kilometers long, with a total flight time of three hours and five minutes. It will be officially opened to traffic in the coming days.

Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd: Premier Zhou Enlai sent a congratulatory telegram to Premier Pham Van Dong of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam regarding the opening of Sino-Vietnamese air service:

	To Comrade Pham Van Dong, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam:

	On the eve of the opening of the civil aviation route between the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the People's Republic of China, I would like to extend warm congratulations to you, and to the government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China.

	I am deeply convinced that the opening of civil aviation between China and Vietnam will further strengthen the friendly cooperation, economic ties, and cultural exchanges between our two brotherly countries.

		Zhou Enlai
		Premier of the State Council and
		Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
		April 23, 1956, in Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd: Kuang Rinnong, Director of the Civil Aviation Administration of China, sent a telegram to Deng Xing, Director of the Civil Aviation Administration of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, on the 23rd, congratulating the upcoming official opening of scheduled civil aviation between China and Vietnam.
	The telegram reads as follows:

	On the occasion of the opening of the Sino-Vietnamese air route, I, on behalf of all the staff of the Civil Aviation Administration of China and in my own name, would like to extend warm congratulations to you and, through you, to all the working comrades of the Civil Aviation Administration of Vietnam.

	The opening of the Sino-Vietnamese air route provides more rapid and convenient transportation conditions between China and Vietnam. We are deeply convinced that it will make the fraternal friendship and ties between the people of the two countries closer and more consolidated.

	I wish the civil aviation undertakings of China and Vietnam development and prosperity.

### 中越航线试航成功即将正式开航  周总理致电范文同总理祝贺

1956-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    中越航线试航成功即将正式开航
    周总理致电范文同总理祝贺
    新华社广州23日电  从广州经南宁到河内的中华人民共和国和越南民主共和国的航空运输线，已经在昨天和今天由中国民用航空局试航成功。
    这条航线全程八百五十二公里，飞行时间共三小时零五十分，日内即将正式开航。
    新华社23日讯  周恩来总理给越南民主共和国范文同总理关于中越通航的贺电：越南民主共和国总理兼外交部长范文同同志：
    在越南民主共和国同中华人民共和国之间的民用航空线开航的前夕，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向你，并且向越南民主共和国政府和人民致热烈的祝贺。
    我深信，中越两国民用航空的通航，必将进一步加强我们两个兄弟国家间的友好合作、经济联系和文化交流。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理
      兼外交部长  周恩来
      1956年4月23日于北京
    新华社23日讯  中国民用航空局局长邝任农在二十三日致电越南民主共和国民用航空局局长邓性，祝贺中越两国间定期民用航空即将正式开航，
  电文如下：
    值此中越开航前夕，我代表中国民用航空局全体人员和以我个人名义向您并通过您向越南民航局全体工作同志表示热烈的祝贺。
    中越航线的开辟，为中越两国间提供了更为迅速便利的交通条件。我们深信它将使两国人民的兄弟友谊和联系更为密切和巩固。
    祝中越两国民航事业发展和繁荣。
