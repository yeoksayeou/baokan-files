Title: "New France" Weekly Publishes "On the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship"
Author:
Date: 1956-04-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 20th. Paris dispatch: The "New France" weekly, published by the French Communist Party, printed the full text of the People's Daily editorial "On the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship" in its April 14th issue.

### “新法兰西”周刊刊载“关于无产阶级专政的历史经验”

1956-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    “新法兰西”周刊刊载“关于无产阶级专政的历史经验”
    新华社布拉格20日电  巴黎消息：法国共产党主办的“新法兰西”周刊在四月十四日出版的一期里，全文刊载了人民日报编辑部所写的文章“关于无产阶级专政的历史经验”。
