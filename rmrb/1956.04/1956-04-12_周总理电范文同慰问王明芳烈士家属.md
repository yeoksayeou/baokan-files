Title: Premier Zhou Sends a Telegram to Premier Phạm Văn Đồng, Expressing Condolences to the Family of Martyr Wang Mingfang
Author:
Date: 1956-04-12
Page: 1

To Comrade Phạm Văn Đồng, Premier of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam:

    On the first anniversary of the death of the martyrs who perished while attending the Asian-African Conference, please convey the Chinese government's deep sympathy and condolences to the family of Martyr Wang Mingfang, a staff member of the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Asian-African Conference, who unfortunately died.

    Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                            April 11, 1956

### 周总理电范文同慰问王明芳烈士家属

1956-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电范文同慰问王明芳烈士家属越南民主共和国总理范文同同志：
    当参加亚非会议死难烈士遇难一周年的时候，谨请转达中国政府对不幸遇难的越南民主共和国参加亚非会议代表团工作人员王明芳烈士家属的深切同情和慰问。
  中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来
                    1956年4月11日
