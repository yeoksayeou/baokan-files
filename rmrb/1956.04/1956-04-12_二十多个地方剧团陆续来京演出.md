Title: More than Twenty Local Opera Troupes Arrive in Beijing for Performances
Author:
Date: 1956-04-12
Page: 3

The Zhejiang Kunqu and Su Opera Troupe, performing in Beijing for the first time from Hangzhou, premiered the newly revised Kunqu traditional play "Fifteen Strings of Cash" on the evening of the 10th.

This year, Beijing audiences will successively enjoy operas and song and dance dramas with unique artistic styles performed by more than twenty local opera and song and dance troupes. Before April, the Inner Mongolia Song and Dance Troupe, the Baotou Jin Opera Troupe, and the Nanjing Yue Opera Troupe had already met with Beijing audiences. In May and June, the Guangdong Cantonese Opera Troupe, the Shanghai Hu Opera Troupe, the Hunan Xiang Opera Troupe, the Zhejiang Yue Opera Troupe, the Fujian Quanzhou Puppet Experimental Troupe, and the Yunnan Dian Opera Troupe, among others, will successively come to Beijing to perform. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 二十多个地方剧团陆续来京演出

1956-04-12
第3版()
专栏：

    二十多个地方剧团陆续来京演出
    从杭州初次来北京演出的浙江昆苏剧团，十日晚第一次演出了重新整理的昆剧传统剧目“十五贯”。
    今年，北京的观众将陆续观赏到二十多个地方戏曲、歌舞剧团演出的具有独特艺术风格的戏曲和歌舞。在四月份以前，已有内蒙古歌舞团、包头市晋剧一团、南京市越剧团和北京观众见过面。在五、六月份，将陆续来北京演出的，有广东的粤剧团、上海的沪剧团、湖南的湘剧团、浙江的越剧团、福建的泉州木偶实验剧团和云南的滇剧团等。　　（新华社）
