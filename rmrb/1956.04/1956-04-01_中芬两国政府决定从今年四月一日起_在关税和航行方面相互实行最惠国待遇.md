Title: China and Finland Decide to Grant Each Other Most-Favored-Nation Treatment in Customs Duties and Navigation from April 1 This Year

Author:

Date: 1956-04-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 31st—The governments of the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Finland, in order to further consolidate and develop the existing friendly trade relations between the two governments, after discussions between representatives of both sides, today exchanged letters in Beijing on the issue of granting each other most-favored-nation treatment in customs duties and navigation. During the exchange, both sides unanimously confirmed that from April 1, 1956, the most-favored-nation treatment will be granted to each other with regard to tariffs and all other taxes and fees on import and export goods between the two countries, as well as ships and their crews and goods traveling between the two countries.

    The exchange of letters was signed by the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Finland to China, Mr. Sundström, and the Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, Mr. Lei Renmin, respectively.

### 中芬两国政府决定从今年四月一日起  在关税和航行方面相互实行最惠国待遇

1956-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中芬两国政府决定从今年四月一日起
    在关税和航行方面相互实行最惠国待遇
    新华社31日讯　中华人民共和国政府和芬兰共和国政府为了进一步巩固和发展两国政府间已有的友好贸易关系，经过双方代表商谈，今天在北京就两国关于相互实行关税和航行方面最惠国待遇问题交换了信件。在交换中，双方一致确认自1956年4月1日起，对于两国间进出口货物的关税，以及其他一切税费和来往于两国间的船只及其船员和货物等彼此实行最惠国待遇。
    换文是由芬兰共和国驻华特命全权大使孙士敦和中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长雷任民分别签署的。
