Title: Ambassador He Ying Holds Banquet in Mongolia for Vice Chairman Zhu De's Visit

Author: 
Date: 1956-04-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, March 31st: Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, He Ying, held a banquet on the evening of March 30th to celebrate Vice Chairman Zhu De's visit to Mongolia.

The Mongolian side present at the banquet included Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural; Tsedenbal, Prime Minister; Shirindib, First Deputy Prime Minister; Lubsan, Maidar, and Tsende, Deputy Prime Ministers; Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, and other members of the Politburo; Ministers of various government departments, the Mongolian Ambassador to China and Korea, senior military generals, and heads of people's organizations.

The Soviet and Korean Ambassadors to Mongolia and diplomatic officials from various embassies, personnel from the Chinese Embassy in Mongolia and the Chinese Economic Delegation, representatives of overseas Chinese, and representatives of students also attended the banquet.

A total of more than 280 people attended the banquet.

Vice Chairman Zhu De spoke at the banquet. He first expressed his gratitude to the government and people of the Mongolian People's Republic for their warm welcome and hospitality. He said that although our visit to Mongolia was short, we gained an extremely deep impression. We have seen that under the leadership of the glorious Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and with the help of the great Soviet Union, the heroic Mongolian people have carried out a series of significant social and economic reforms in a short historical period, fundamentally changing the face of Mongolia. He said that because the Mongolian People's Republic has made significant efforts and contributions in the struggle against wars of aggression and in defense of peace in the Far East and the world, Mongolia's international influence and international status have been further expanded and enhanced.

Chairman Sambu delivered a speech saying that Comrade Zhu De's visit to Mongolia is of great significance for the further consolidation of the friendship and comprehensive cooperation between the peoples of China and Mongolia. The Mongolian people are constantly watching with joy the great achievements made by the great fraternal Chinese people in a very short historical period in the cause of building a strong socialist country and in the struggle to defend peace and security in the Far East and among the peoples of the world.

Prime Minister Tsedenbal said in his speech that Vice Chairman Zhu De's visit to Mongolia is of great historical significance, and this visit will surely further enhance the friendship between the peoples of China and Mongolia.

During the banquet, the guests repeatedly toasted to the friendship between the two countries, to the development of the socialist construction cause of the two countries, to the health of the national leaders of the two countries, and to the strengthening of the forces of the peace, democracy, and socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

### 为朱德副主席访问蒙古  我驻蒙古大使何英举行宴会

1956-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    为朱德副主席访问蒙古
    我驻蒙古大使何英举行宴会
    新华社31日讯　乌兰巴托消息：中国驻蒙古大使何英在三月三十日晚上为朱德副主席访问蒙古举行了宴会。
    蒙古方面出席宴会的有大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布，总理泽登巴尔，第一副总理锡林迪布，副总理鲁布桑、迈达尔、曾德，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴和其它政治局委员，政府各部部长、蒙古驻中国和朝鲜大使以及军队高级将领、人民团体负责人等。
    苏联和朝鲜驻蒙古大使和各使馆的外交官员、中国驻蒙古大使馆人员和中国经济代表团、华侨代表、留学生代表也出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的共有二百八十多人。
    朱德副主席在宴会上讲了话。他首先对蒙古人民共和国政府和人民给他的热烈欢迎和盛情招待表示感谢。他说，我们在蒙古访问的时间虽然很短，但是却获得了极其深刻的印象。我们看到英勇的蒙古人民在光荣的蒙古人民革命党的领导下，在伟大苏联的帮助下，在短短的一个历史时期内进行了一系列巨大的社会、经济改革，使蒙古的面貌发生了根本变化。他说，由于蒙古人民共和国在反对侵略战争、保卫远东和世界和平的斗争中作了重大努力和贡献，因此蒙古的国际影响和国际地位已经有了进一步的扩大和提高。
    桑布主席致辞说，朱德同志来蒙古访问，对中蒙两国人民之间的友谊和全面合作的进一步巩固具有巨大意义。蒙古人民不断地注视着伟大的兄弟般的中国人民在很短的历史时期内，在从事强大的社会主义国家的建设事业中、在保卫远东和世界各国人民之间的和平与安全的斗争中获得的巨大成就，并且为此而高兴。
    泽登巴尔总理致辞说，朱德副主席的访问蒙古是有伟大历史意义的，这次访问必将进一步增强中蒙两国人民之间的友谊。
    在宴会进行中，宾主一再为两国友谊、为两国社会主义建设事业的发展、为两国国家领导人的健康和为以苏联为首的和平民主社会主义阵营力量的增强干杯。
