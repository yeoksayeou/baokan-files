Title: Playwright Song Zhi's Dies in Beijing

Author:

Date: 1956-04-18
Page: 3

Playwright Song Zhi's passed away in Beijing at 2:27 PM on the 17th, at the age of 42. He suffered from cirrhosis of the liver in March and passed away despite treatment by famous Chinese and Western doctors.

Song Zhi's was a member of the Chinese Communist Party and a colonel in the Chinese People's Liberation Army. In 1932, he joined the League of Left-Wing Dramatists led by the Chinese Communist Party in Beijing and began his revolutionary activities. Over the past two decades, he has created many excellent works. His representative works include the reportage "Spring in Taiyuan in 1936," and the plays "Wu Zetian," "Foggy Chongqing," "Early Spring," and "Apes." After joining the Chinese People's Liberation Army in 1948, he made great contributions to building the literary and artistic work of the troops and cultivating young writers in the troops. His new work "Defending Peace," which reflects the struggle to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea, was well received at the First National Drama Festival.

The Song Zhi's Funeral Committee was established on the 17th, and a memorial service is scheduled for the morning of the 19th.
                      (Xinhua News Agency)

### 剧作家宋之的在北京病逝

1956-04-18
第3版()
专栏：

    剧作家宋之的在北京病逝
    剧作家宋之的十七日下午二时二十七分在北京病逝，享年四十二岁。他在三月间患肝脏硬化症，经中、西名医治疗无效。
    宋之的是中国共产党员、中国人民解放军上校。1932年在北京参加中国共产党领导的左翼戏剧家联盟，开始从事革命活动。二十多年来曾经创作了许多优秀的作品。他的代表作有报告文学“一九三六年春在太原”，剧本“武则天”、“雾重庆”、“春寒”、“群猴”等。他在1948年参加中国人民解放军后，对建设部队文艺工作、培养部队青年作家等方面有很大贡献。他的反映抗美援朝斗争的新作“保卫和平”，在第一届全国话剧观摩演出会上得到好评。
    宋之的治丧委员会已经在十七日成立，定十九日上午举行追悼会。
                      （新华社）
