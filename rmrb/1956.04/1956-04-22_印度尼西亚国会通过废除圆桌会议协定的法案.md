Title: Indonesian Parliament Passes Law to Abolish Round Table Conference Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-04-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 21st: According to Antara News Agency, the Indonesian Parliament today unanimously passed a law to completely abolish the Round Table Conference Agreement signed with the Netherlands in 1949. According to this law, the Dutch charter transferring sovereignty to Indonesia was also declared invalid. A clause in this charter stipulated that the status of West Irian would be decided later.

Antara News Agency said that the purpose of abolishing this charter is to eliminate the basis for the Netherlands to continue to control West Irian. The Republic of Indonesia will demand sovereignty over the West Irian region based on the 1945 Declaration of Independence. The Declaration of Independence stipulates that Indonesian territory includes the entirety of the former Netherlands East Indies.

The new law also stipulates that Dutch capital in Indonesia will be given the same status as other foreign capital, in accordance with Indonesia's provisional constitution and current laws and regulations.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 20th: The Indonesian Parliament unanimously expressed confidence in the Ali Sastroamidjojo government this morning, deciding to "give the government an opportunity to implement its program."

The parliament made this decision after hearing the government's response to the second stage of the debate on the government's policy statement.

Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo thanked the parliament for expressing its great goodwill. He believes that this goodwill is a valuable help for the new government to carry out its important tasks.

Parliament Speaker Sardono expressed his hope that the new cabinet will be able to complete its tasks for the benefit of the Indonesian people.

### 印度尼西亚国会通过废除圆桌会议协定的法案

1956-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国会通过废除圆桌会议协定的法案
    新华社雅加达21日电  据安塔拉通讯社报道，印度尼西亚国会今天一致通过了全部废除1949年同荷兰签订的圆桌会议协定的法案。根据这一法案，荷兰对印度尼西亚移交主权的敕书也宣布无效了，这个敕书有一条条文规定暂缓决定西伊里安的地位。
    安塔拉通讯社说，废除这个敕书的目的是在于消除荷兰继续控制西伊里安的根据，印度尼西亚共和国将根据1945年的独立宣言来要求对西伊里安地区的主权。独立宣言规定，印度尼西亚国土包括前荷属东印度的全部。
    新的法案还规定，对于荷兰在印度尼西亚的资本，将按照印度尼西亚的临时宪法和现行的法律和条例给予同其他外国资本同等的地位。
    新华社雅加达20日电  印度尼西亚国会在今天上午一致对阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约政府表示信任，决定“给予政府机会来实行它的纲领”。
    国会是在听取了政府提出的关于政府政策声明的第二阶段的辩论的答复之后，作出这个决定的。
    沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理感谢国会所表示的巨大的善意。他认为这种善意对于新政府执行它的重大任务是一个宝贵的帮助。
    国会议长沙多诺表示，为了印度尼西亚人民的利益，希望新内阁将能够完成它的任务。
