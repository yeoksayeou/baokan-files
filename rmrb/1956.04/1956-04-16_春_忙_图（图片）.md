Title: Spring "Busy" Picture
Author: Han Yu
Date: 1956-04-16
Page: 6

Spring "Busy" Picture
　　Han Yu
Some agricultural production cooperative workers love pomp and circumstance and like to put on a big show. Shortly after the agricultural cooperatives were established, they were busy building clubs, repairing ball fields, and engaging in other non-productive construction, wasting money extravagantly, abusing civilian labor, and seriously affecting agricultural production.

### 春“忙”图（图片）
韩羽
1956-04-16
第6版()
专栏：

    春“忙”图
  　韩羽
    有些农业生产合作社的工作人员，爱排场，好摆大摊子。农业社成立不久，他们就忙于盖俱乐部、修球场等非生产性建设，铺张浪费，滥用民力，严重地影响了农业生产。
