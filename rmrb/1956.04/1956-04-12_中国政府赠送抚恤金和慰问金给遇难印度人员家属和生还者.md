Title: Chinese Government Presents Condolences and Sympathy Money to Families of Deceased Indian Personnel and Survivors

Author:

Date: 1956-04-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 11th: The Government of the People's Republic of China has presented 43,000 yuan (equivalent to over 83,000 rupees) in condolences and sympathy money to the families of the Indian aircrew who died in the "Kashmir Princess" plane crash and to the surviving aircrew. This money has already been entrusted by the Chinese Embassy in India to the Indian Ministry of External Affairs on April 10 for transfer.

### 中国政府赠送抚恤金和慰问金给遇难印度人员家属和生还者

1956-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

    中国政府赠送抚恤金和慰问金给遇难印度人员家属和生还者
    新华社新德里11日电　中华人民共和国政府以为数四万三千元人民币（合八万三千多卢比）的抚恤金和慰问金赠给印度“克什米尔公主”号飞机失事时遇难的印度机务人员的家属和生还的机务人员。这笔抚恤金和慰问金已经由中国驻印度大使馆在四月十日请印度外交部代为转交。
