Title: Sino-Philippine Football Preliminary Competition to be Held in India
Author:
Date: 1956-04-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th — The preliminary competition between the Chinese and Philippine football teams for the 16th Olympic Games has been confirmed to be held in Calcutta, India, in mid-May.

Two preliminary matches will be held. The winning football team in the preliminary competition will be eligible to participate in the football competition of the 16th Olympic Games, which will begin in Melbourne, Australia in late November this year.

### 中菲足球预选赛将在印度举行

1956-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

    中菲足球预选赛将在印度举行
    据新华社11日讯　中国足球队同菲律宾足球队参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会以前的预选赛，已经确定五月中旬在印度的加尔各答举行。
    预选赛将举行两场。在预选赛中获得优胜的足球队，将有资格参加今年十一月下旬开始在澳大利亚的墨尔本举行的第十六届奥林匹克运动会中的足球比赛。
