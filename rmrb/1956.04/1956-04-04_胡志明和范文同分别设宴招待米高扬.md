Title: Ho Chi Minh and Pham Van Dong Host Banquets for Mikoyan Separately
Author:
Date: 1956-04-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 3rd: President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held a banquet on the evening of April 2nd to entertain Soviet First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Mikoyan.

Also present at the banquet were Rashidov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Uzbek Republic, Soviet Ambassador to Vietnam Zimyanin, and Counselor Sokolov of the Soviet Embassy in Hanoi.

On the Vietnamese side were Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong, General Secretary of the Vietnam Workers' Party Truong Chinh, Deputy Prime Ministers Vo Nguyen Giap and Phan Ke Toai, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly Ton Duc Thang, members of the Politburo and Central Committee of the Labor Party, ministers of various ministries, leaders of various democratic parties, members of the Central Committee of the Fatherland Front, representatives of the National Assembly, and representatives of various mass organizations.

Also present at the banquet were Vietnamese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Nguyen Luong Bang and Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam Luo Guibo.

President Ho Chi Minh and Mikoyan both spoke at the banquet.

The banquet was held in an extremely cordial and friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 3rd: Prime Minister Pham Van Dong of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held a grand reception banquet at 7:30 PM on April 3rd to welcome the Soviet government delegation headed by Mikoyan.

Present at the banquet were: President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Interior Phan Ke Toai, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Defense Vo Nguyen Giap, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of Vietnam and Chairman of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front Ton Duc Thang, General Secretary of the Vietnam Workers' Party Truong Chinh and Politburo member Hoang Quoc Viet, ministers and deputy ministers of various government ministries, senior generals of the Vietnamese People's Army, leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations, as well as figures from religious and cultural circles.

Also present at the banquet were: Soviet Ambassador to Vietnam Zimyanin and diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries to Vietnam, representatives of the Vietnam International Commission, and Soviet experts in Vietnam, totaling more than 600 people.

Prime Minister Pham Van Dong and Soviet First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Mikoyan both spoke at the banquet.

After the banquet, the Vietnam Central People's Art Troupe gave a brilliant artistic performance.

### 胡志明和范文同分别设宴招待米高扬

1956-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明和范文同分别设宴招待米高扬
    新华社河内3日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明在四月二日晚上举行宴会，招待苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬。
    出席宴会的还有乌兹别克共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席拉希多夫，苏联驻越南大使吉米亚宁和苏联驻河内大使馆参赞索科洛夫。
    越南方面出席的有总理兼外交部长范文同，越南劳动党总书记长征，副总理武元甲、潘继遂，国民大会常务委员会委员长孙德胜，劳动党政治局和中央委员会的委员们，各部部长，各民主党派领导人，祖国战线中央委员会委员，国民大会代表，各群众团体的代表。
    出席宴会的还有越南驻苏联大使阮良朋和中国驻越南大使罗贵波。
    胡志明主席和米高扬都在宴会上讲了话。
    宴会在极其亲切和友好的气氛中进行。
    新华社河内3日电　越南民主共和国总理范文同在四月三日下午七时三十分举行盛大的招待宴会，欢迎以米高扬为首的苏联政府代表团。
    出席宴会的有：越南民主共和国主席胡志明，副总理兼内务部长潘继遂，副总理兼国防部长武元甲，越南国民大会常务委员会委员长、越南祖国战线中央委员会主席孙德胜，越南劳动党总书记长征和政治局委员黄国越，政府各部部长和副部长，越南人民军高级将领，各民主党派和各人民团体的负责人以及各宗教界、文化界人士。
    出席宴会的还有：苏联驻越南大使吉米亚宁和各国驻越南的使节和外交官员，越南国际委员会代表以及苏联在越南的专家等共六百多人。
    范文同总理和苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬都在宴会上讲了话。
    宴会后，越南中央人民文工团作了精采的文艺表演。
