Title: French Government Decides to Increase Troops in Algeria
Author:
Date: 1956-04-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 12th -- The French cabinet decided on the 11th to conscript 70,000 reservists to strengthen the military suppression of the Algerian people. The first batch of reservists will arrive in Algeria on May 15th.
    The measure of conscripting reservists has been opposed by the French people. According to a report in "l'Humanité" on the 10th, the French Republican Youth League issued a declaration opposing the government's plan.

### 法国政府决定增兵阿尔及利亚

1956-04-13
第4版()
专栏：

    法国政府决定增兵阿尔及利亚
    新华社12日讯　巴黎消息：法国内阁在十一日决定征召七万名后备兵，用来加强对阿尔及利亚人民的军事镇压行动。后备兵中的第一批将在五月十五日到达阿尔及利亚。
    征召后备兵的措施遭到法国人民的反对。据“人道报”十日报道，法兰西共和青年联盟曾经发表宣言，反对政府的这项计划。
