Title: Machine that produces two tons of granular fertilizer per hour

Author:

Date: 1956-04-17
Page: 2

A granular fertilizer machine has been successfully trial-manufactured by the Sichuan Provincial Local State-owned Zigong Iron Factory. This granular fertilizer machine is composed of thirteen machines and can produce two tons of granular fertilizer per hour, even in rainy weather.

This machine has already been put into production at the Chengdu Fertilizer Plant. Fertilizer plants in Jiangxi, Zhejiang, Guizhou and other places have already placed orders for this fertilizer machine with the Zigong Iron Factory. (Our correspondent in Chengdu)

### 每小时产两吨颗粒肥料的机器

1956-04-17
第2版()
专栏：

    每小时产两吨颗粒肥料的机器
    四川省地方国营自贡铁工厂试制成功一部颗粒肥料机。这部颗粒肥料机是由十三台机器联合组成的，每小时可以生产二吨颗粒肥料，在雨天也可以照常生产。
    这部机器已经在成都肥料厂投入生产。江西、浙江、贵州等地的肥料厂已经向自贡铁工厂订购这种肥料机。　　　　（本报驻成都记者）
