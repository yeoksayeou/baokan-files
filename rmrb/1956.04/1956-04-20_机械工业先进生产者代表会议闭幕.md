Title: Closing of the National Mechanical Industry Advanced Producers Representative Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-04-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 19th: The National Mechanical Industry Advanced Producers Representative Conference, lasting ten days, closed today. The conference adopted a resolution on promoting advanced experiences and carrying out the advanced producer movement, calling on workers, engineers, technicians, and enterprise managers in the mechanical industry to actively learn and promote advanced experiences, widely participate in the advanced producer movement, and strive to build China's mechanical industry into a strong industrial sector capable of independently supplying the national economy with various technical equipment as soon as possible.

Kang Yonghe, Chairman of the National Committee of the First National Machinery Industry Trade Union of China, said at today's meeting that this conference has gained a lot in exchanging advanced experiences. During the conference, each representative learned one to more than ten advanced technical experiences through discussions, reports, and technical demonstrations. The promotion of these advanced experiences will be of great significance for the development of China's mechanical industry and ensuring the early completion of the first five-year plan.

At today's closing meeting, Wang Daohan, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, and Han Chunde, Vice Minister of the Third Ministry of Machine Building, presented certificates of merit, medals, and bonuses to the representatives of advanced producers and advanced collective units attending the meeting, and also presented banners to advanced collective units with outstanding achievements. The conference elected 391 advanced producers to attend the National Advanced Producers Representative Conference.

During the conference, the representatives put forward more than 30 proposals on carrying out competitions in the same type of work and business and promoting advanced experience, and more than 130 representatives put forward guarantees for improving production.

### 机械工业先进生产者代表会议闭幕

1956-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    机械工业先进生产者代表会议闭幕
    据新华社19日讯　历时十天的全国机械工业先进生产者代表会议今天闭幕。会议通过了关于推广先进经验和开展先进生产者运动的决议，号召机械工业的工人、工程技术人员和企业管理人员，积极学习和推广先进经验、广泛参加先进生产者运动，争取尽快地把中国机械工业建设成为一个强大的、能够独立供应国民经济各种技术装备的工业部门。
    中国第一机械工会全国委员会主席康永和在今天的会上说，这次会议在交流先进经验方面有很大收获。会议期间，通过座谈、报告和技术表演，每个代表都学到了一种到十多种先进技术经验。这些先进经验推广以后，对于发展中国机械工业、保证提前完成第一个五年计划有很大意义。
    在今天的闭幕会上，第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵、第三机械工业部副部长韩纯德，向出席会议的先进生产者和先进集体单位的代表颁发了奖状、奖章和奖金，并且向有突出成绩的先进集体单位颁发了锦旗。会议选出了391名先进生产者出席全国先进生产者代表会议。
    会议期间，代表们提出了三十多件关于开展同工种同业务竞赛、推广先进经验的倡议，并且有一百三十多名代表提出了改进生产的保证书。
