Title: Albanian Labor Party Issues Draft Directives for the Second Five-Year Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-04-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 16th: Tirana, April 16th – Albanian newspapers published the draft directives of the Third Congress of the Albanian Labor Party regarding the Second Five-Year Plan of the People's Republic of Albania for 1956-1960.

The draft directives point out that the main economic task of the First Five-Year Plan (1951-1955) – to transform Albania from a backward agricultural country into an industrial-agricultural country – has been basically completed. In 1955, the industrial output value increased by 179% compared to 1950 (calculated at constant prices), which was 10.5 times that of 1938. During the First Five-Year Plan, the output of means of production increased by 156%.

The tasks of the First Five-Year Plan regarding the development of transportation, expansion of trade turnover, improvement of labor productivity, and improvement of the material welfare of the working people have been successfully completed. Illiteracy among adults under the age of forty has been eliminated.

The draft directives stipulate that the total industrial output value should increase by 91% during the Second Five-Year Plan. Compared with 1955, the mining of petroleum in 1960 will increase by 56%, chromium ore by 146%, coal by 49%, and electricity production by 86%. The output value of light industry will double, and the food industry will increase by 94%.

The task put forward in the draft directives in the agricultural sector is to greatly expand the production of agricultural and livestock products during the Second Five-Year Plan. The grain harvest in 1960 should increase by 46% compared to 1955, cotton by 87%, vegetables by 43%, meat by 53%, milk by 40%, and wool by 36%. The national sown area will expand by 19% in five years, reaching 445,000 hectares by 1960. The sown area of cooperatives will account for 70% of the total national sown area by 1960.

The total investment during the five-year plan will reach 21.9 billion lek, an increase of 27% compared to the First Five-Year Plan. Of this, 35% will be used for industry, 21.5% for the development of agriculture, and 19% for cultural and social welfare, etc.

National income will increase by 53% in five years. The real wages of workers and staff will increase by 23%. The area of housing built during the Second Five-Year Plan will reach 663,000 square meters, which is 47% more than during the First Five-Year Plan.

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动党发表第二个五年计划的指示草案

1956-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党发表第二个五年计划的指示草案
    新华社布拉格16日电　地拉那十六日讯：阿尔巴尼亚报纸刊载了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第三次代表大会关于阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国1956—1960年第二个五年计划的指示草案。
    指示草案指出，第一个五年计划（1951—1955年）的主要经济任务——把阿尔巴尼亚从落后的农业国变成一个工业一农业国——基本上已经完成了。在1955年，工业产值比1950年增加了179％（按不变价格计算），为1938年的十点五倍。在第一个五年计划期间，生产资料的产量增加了156％。
    第一个五年计划关于发展运输、扩大贸易流转额、提高劳动生产率和改善劳动人民物质福利的任务，已经胜利地完成了。四十岁以下的成年文盲已经扫除了。
    指示草案规定在第二个五年计划期间工业总产值增加91％。同1955年相比，1960年的石油开采量将增加56％，铬矿石将增加146％，煤将增加49％，电力生产将增加86％。轻工业产值将增加一倍，食品工业将增加94％。
    指示草案在农业方面提出的任务是，在第二个五年计划期间大大扩大农产品和畜产品的产量。1960年的谷物收获量应比1955年增加46％，棉花增加87％，蔬菜43％，肉类53％，奶类40％，羊毛36％。全国的播种面积在五年中将扩大19％，到1960年将达到四十四万五千公顷。合作社的播种面积到1960年将占全国播种总面积的70％。
    五年计划期间的投资总额将达到二百一十九亿里克，即比第一个五年计划增加27％。其中将有35％用于工业，21.5％用于发展农业，19％用于文化社会福利等等。
    国民收入在五年中将提高53％。工人和职员的实际工资将提高23％。在第二个五年计划期间建筑的住宅的面积，将达到六十六万三千平方公尺，即比第一个五年计划期间多47％。
