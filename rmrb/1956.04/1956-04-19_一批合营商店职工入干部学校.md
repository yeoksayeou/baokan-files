Date: 1956-04-19
Page: 2

A Batch of Jointly-Operated Store Employees Enter Cadre School

The Shanghai First and Second Commercial Bureaus recently selected more than 1,500 employees from public-private joint venture stores to participate in studies at the First Commercial Bureau Cadre School. Through learning, their policy and ideological levels and work abilities will be further improved, so as to better complete the reform and transformation work of public-private joint ventures.

The selected employees include group leaders, section chiefs, and department store directors from the public-private joint venture stores. (Our correspondent in Shanghai)

### 一批合营商店职工入干部学校

1956-04-19
第2版()
专栏：

    一批合营商店职工入干部学校
    上海市第一、第二商业局最近抽调了一千五百多名公私合营商店的职工，参加第一商业局干部学校学习。通过学习，将进一步提高他们的政策思想水平和工作能力，以便更好地完成公私合营企业的改革改造工作。
    这批被抽调的职工，包括公私合营商店的组长、股长、门市部主任等。　（本报驻上海记者）
