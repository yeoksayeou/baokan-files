Title: A Major Event in the History of the German Workers' Movement: Representatives of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and the Social Democratic Party Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-04-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, April 4th: According to today's "Neues Deutschland," during the Third Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, the Political Bureau of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany invited representatives of the Social Democratic Party attending the meeting to discuss issues related to the interests of the German working class. The talks were conducted in a spirit filled with understanding and comradeship.

The Socialist Unity Party of Germany's Political Bureau was represented by Ulbricht, Grotewohl, Matern, Stoph, and Ebert, among others. The Social Democratic Party was represented by seventy-five party cadres from various districts of the Federal Republic of Germany and West Berlin. The First Secretary of the German Communist Party, Reimann, and several secretaries and cadres from the Central Secretariat of the German Communist Party also attended the talks.

Those participating in the talks unanimously agreed that the struggle for peace is the primary task of the German working class, and this struggle requires understanding between the German workers' parties and organizations. The current differences of opinion within the German workers' movement can be clarified at the negotiating table, and all the conditions for reaching understanding exist, if all parties are sincere.

"Neues Deutschland" commented when announcing this news that the talks between the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, the Social Democratic Party, and the German Communist Party are a major event in the history of the German workers' movement and a start to implementing the resolutions of the Third Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, April 5th: According to the German News Agency, representatives of the German Communist Party attending the Third Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany issued a statement expressing their commitment to strive for the unity and solidarity of the German working class. The statement said: "We will do everything possible to improve our relations with Social Democratic comrades in factories and trade unions, and to this end, we will put all our common opinions in the first place and put the differences of opinion in a secondary place." The statement also expressed the need to mobilize the working class and the broad masses of West Germany to defend democratic rights and freedoms, to defend the legal existence of the German Communist Party and the free activities of all democratic organizations, and to strengthen the solidarity movement demanding the release of all imprisoned peace fighters.

### 德国工人运动史上的重大事件  德国统一社会党同社会民主党代表举行会谈

1956-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    德国工人运动史上的重大事件
    德国统一社会党同社会民主党代表举行会谈
    新华社柏林4日电　据“新德意志报”今天报道，在德国统一社会党第三次代表会议期间，德国统一社会党政治局曾经邀请出席会议的社会民主党代表就有关德国工人阶级利益的问题进行了会谈。这次会谈是在充满了谅解和同志之谊的精神中进行的。
    德国统一社会党政治局方面出席会谈的有乌布利希、格罗提渥、马特恩、斯多夫和艾伯特等人；社会民主党方面出席的有德意志联邦共和国各区和西柏林的党的干部七十五人。德国共产党第一书记雷曼、德国共产党中央书记处书记和党的干部多人也出席了会谈。
    参加会谈的人一致认为：争取和平的斗争是德国工人阶级的主要任务，而这个斗争要求德国工人阶级政党和工人组织取得谅解。德国工人运动中现在的意见分歧是可以在谈判桌上解说清楚的，为求得谅解的一切条件是存在的，如果所有方面都具有诚意的话。
      “新德意志报”在公布这个消息的时候评论说，德国统一社会党、社会民主党和德国共产党的会谈是德国工人运动史上一个重大事件，它是实现德国统一社会党第三次代表会议决议的一个开端。
    据新华社柏林5日电　据德意志通讯社报道，出席德国统一社会党第三次代表会议的德国共产党代表发表声明，表示要尽力为争取德国工人阶级的统一和团结而奋斗。声明说：“我们将尽一切力量来改善我们在工厂和工会中同社会民主党同志的关系，为此我们将把我们的一切共同意见放在第一位，把分歧的意见放在次要的地位。”声明还表示要动员工人阶级和西德的广大群众来保卫民主权利和自由，保卫德国共产党的合法存在和所有民主组织的自由活动，并且加强要求释放一切被囚禁的和平战士的声援运动。
