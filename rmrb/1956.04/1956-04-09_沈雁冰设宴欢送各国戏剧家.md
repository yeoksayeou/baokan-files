Title: Shen Yanbing Hosts Banquet to See Off Foreign Dramatists
Author:
Date: 1956-04-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing hosted a banquet today to see off dramatists from twelve countries who came to China to observe the Drama Festival.

Shen Yanbing delivered a speech at the banquet. He thanked the dramatists from various countries for their enthusiastic help to Chinese drama work during the festival, and hoped that they would maintain regular contact with Chinese dramatists in the future.

Soviet dramatist N.A. Streltsov and German Democratic Republic writer H. Hauser spoke at the banquet. N.A. Streltsov said that they were pleased to see the socialist realism of Chinese drama, from which they saw the new life of the Chinese people. He said that Chinese drama is on the right path, and he firmly believes that it will advance by leaps and bounds along with the development of socialist construction.

At the banquet, dramatists from various countries received gifts from the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China.

Before returning to their countries, dramatists from various countries will also make short visits to Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou and other places.

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: N.A. Streltsov, head of the Soviet dramatists delegation who came to China to participate in the First National Drama Festival, presented a batch of gifts to Chinese drama workers at the Ministry of Culture this afternoon.

Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing accepted the gifts on behalf of Chinese drama workers.

Among the gifts are recordings of world-famous plays by Gorky, Shakespeare, Gogol, Schiller, Shaw and others, performed by outstanding Soviet actors, as well as recordings of "Stage Sound Effects", stills, and the latest published Soviet drama art books and periodicals. Part of these gifts were presented to the Chinese Dramatists Association by the All-Russian Theater Society.

### 沈雁冰设宴欢送各国戏剧家

1956-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    沈雁冰设宴欢送各国戏剧家
    据新华社7日讯　文化部部长沈雁冰今天设宴欢送来中国观摩话剧会演的十二个国家的戏剧家。
    沈雁冰在宴会上致词。他感谢各国戏剧家在会演中给予中国话剧工作的热情帮助，并且希望他们今后同中国的戏剧家保持经常的联系。
    苏联戏剧家尼·阿·斯特列里佐夫和德意志民主共和国作家哈·豪塞在宴会上讲了话。尼·阿·斯特列里佐夫说，他们高兴地看到了社会主义现实主义的中国话剧，从这里看到了中国人民的新生活。他说，中国的话剧是走在一条正确的道路上，深信它将随着社会主义建设事业的发展而一日千里地前进。
    在宴会上，各国戏剧家们接受了中华人民共和国文化部赠送给他们的礼品。
    各国戏剧家在回国以前，还将到上海、杭州、广州等地作短期的参观访问。
    新华社7日讯　来中国参加第一届全国话剧观摩演出会的苏联戏剧家代表团团长尼·阿·斯特列里佐夫，今天下午在文化部把一批礼品赠送给中国戏剧工作者。
    文化部部长沈雁冰代表中国戏剧工作者接受了礼品。
    这批礼品中，有苏联卓越的演员演出高尔基、莎士比亚、果戈里、席勒、萧伯纳等所著的世界名剧的录音唱片，还有“舞台音响”的唱片和剧照以及最新出版的苏联戏剧艺术书刊等。这批礼品的一部分是全俄罗斯戏剧家协会赠送给中国戏剧家协会的。
