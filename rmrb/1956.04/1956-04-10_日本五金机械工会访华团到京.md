Title: Japanese Metal and Machinery Workers' Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 9th. The Japanese Metal and Machinery Workers' Union Delegation, invited by the National Committee of the China First Machine Workers' Union to visit China, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon. The delegation is led by the head of the delegation, Tsuyoshi Matsuo, Deputy Secretary-General of the National Metal Workers' Union, and the deputy head of the delegation, Mitsuji Mizoguchi, Chairman of the All-Japan Shipbuilding Workers' Union.

The Japanese Metal and Machinery Workers' Union Delegation consists of twenty members from seven national organizations of metal and machinery workers' unions in Japan.

### 日本五金机械工会访华团到京

1956-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

    日本五金机械工会访华团到京
    据新华社9日讯　应中国第一机械工会全国委员会的邀请前来中国访问的日本五金机械工会访华代表团，由团长、全国金属工会副总书记松尾乔和副团长、全日本造船工会主席沟口光治率领，今天下午乘火车到达北京。
    日本五金机械工会访华代表团是由日本七个五金机械工会全国组织的人员组成的，共二十人。                                 2
