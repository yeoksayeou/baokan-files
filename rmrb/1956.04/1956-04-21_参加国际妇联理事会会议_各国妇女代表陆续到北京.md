Title: Delegates from Various Countries Arrive in Beijing to Attend the International Women's Federation Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-04-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 20th. A total of sixty-three International Democratic Women's Federation (IDWF) Vice-Presidents, Council members, special representatives and staff members arrived in Beijing today and yesterday to attend the IDWF Council meeting.

    Those who arrived in Beijing today included IDWF Vice President Blanca Fiallo (Brazil), and eleven council members from Argentina, Chile, Cuba, the German Democratic Republic, the Federal Republic of Germany, India, Italy, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Mexico, Spain, and Switzerland.

    Also arriving today were eight specially-invited representatives from Chile, the German Democratic Republic, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    Eight resident secretaries and representatives from various countries of the IDWF, as well as staff members, arrived in Beijing today and yesterday.

    Li Dequan, Shi Liang, Vice-Presidents of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and secretaries of the Secretariat, went to the airport and railway station today to welcome them.

### 参加国际妇联理事会会议  各国妇女代表陆续到北京

1956-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    参加国际妇联理事会会议
    各国妇女代表陆续到北京
    新华社20日讯  今天和昨天到达北京出席国际民主妇女联合会理事会会议的国际民主妇联副主席、理事、特邀代表及工作人员共有六十三人。
    今天到达北京的有国际民主妇联副主席布朗卡·费雅洛（巴西）、有阿根廷、智利、古巴、德意志民主共和国、德意志联邦共和国、印度、意大利、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、墨西哥、西班牙、瑞士等国理事十一人。
    今天到达的还有智利、德意志民主共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、越南民主共和国等国的特邀代表八人。
    今天和昨天到达北京的有国际民主妇联各国驻会书记和代表共八人，还有工作人员等。
    今天前往飞机场和火车站欢迎的有中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全、史良和书记处书记等人。
