Title: India and Pakistan Hold Talks on Border Demarcation
Author:
Date: 1956-04-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 12th: Government survey officials from India and Pakistan began negotiations in New Delhi on April 11th regarding the demarcation of the border between India and West Pakistan.

These negotiations are based on the border rulings made during the partition of India and Pakistan in 1947. The negotiations will involve the issue of demarcating the 800-mile border in the western region of India and Pakistan (excluding the border between Jammu and Kashmir and West Pakistan) with boundary markers.

### 印巴就划定边界问题举行谈判

1956-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    印巴就划定边界问题举行谈判
    新华社新德里12日电　印度和巴基斯坦的政府测量官员就划定印度同西巴基斯坦之间的边界问题四月十一日在新德里开始举行谈判。
    这次谈判是以1947年印度和巴基斯坦分治时关于边境的裁决为基础的。谈判将涉及关于用标柱来划分印巴西部地区（查谟和克什米尔邦同西巴基斯坦的边境除外）中的八百英里长的边境问题。
