Title: Xinjiang Changji Hui Autonomous Prefecture is Building a Large New City
Author:
Date: 1956-04-29
Page: 3

Xinjiang Changji Hui Autonomous Prefecture
    Is Building a Large New City
    The Changji Hui Autonomous Prefecture in the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region is building a new city in the southern suburbs of Changji County. Currently, seventeen buildings including the Autonomous Prefecture People's Committee, the CPC Changji Local Committee, the Autonomous Prefecture People's Hospital, and a middle school have been completed. Greenification work in the urban area has also begun.
    This year, sixteen more projects will continue to be built in this new city. The entire city construction project will be completed in 1962, with an urban area of two million square meters, and a construction scale equivalent to the current city of Urumqi. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 新疆昌吉回族自治州  正建设一个大的新城市

1956-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

    新疆昌吉回族自治州
    正建设一个大的新城市
    新疆维吾尔自治区昌吉回族自治州，在昌吉县城南郊正建设一个新的城市。现在，已经完成了自治州人民委员会、中共昌吉地方委员会、自治州人民医院和中学等十七项建筑物。市区的绿化工作也已开始。
    今年，这个新城市里将继续修建十六项工程。整个城市的建设工程将在1962年完成，市区面积可达两百万平方公尺，建筑规模相当于目前的乌鲁木齐市。　　　（据新华社讯）
