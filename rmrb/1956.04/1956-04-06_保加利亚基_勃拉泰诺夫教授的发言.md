Title: Speech by Bulgarian Professor Kiril Bratanov
Author:
Date: 1956-04-06
Page: 3

Esteemed friends and comrades:

On behalf of the Bulgarian Union of Scientific Workers, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to the grand assembly celebrating the tenth anniversary of our beloved Association.

To celebrate the tenth anniversary of our Association in Beijing, the capital of the great People's Republic of China, is a symbolic manifestation of the greatness and power of the ideas that inspire our Association. This anniversary proves that our Association has its own base in almost every corner of the globe, with its own supporters who are fighting with great enthusiasm to achieve the lofty humanitarian goals of the Association.

A mere glance at the circumstances in 1946 when a group of enthusiastic scientific workers established our Association proves that the heroic past decade has been a decade of persistent struggle to achieve international cooperation and to make science serve the cause of peace and progress, and proves the continuous organizational consolidation and growth of our Association. Therefore, it can be stated with satisfaction today that, thanks to these efforts, the great ideas for which our Association has striven have united millions upon millions of scientists around the world, and the World Federation of Scientific Workers is already an important factor in international cooperation and the consolidation of world peace.

The Bulgarian Union of Scientific Workers, which has more than ten years of history and has been a member of the Association since 1948, greets this truly great festival with heartfelt joy. This festival is also a festival for our country's scientific workers, who are clearly aware of the social responsibilities of scientific workers, and who work with the utmost perseverance and high enthusiasm to establish a true science that serves the people and benefits the people, to consolidate cooperation among scientific workers worldwide, and to realize the great ideals and goals of science.

Bulgarian scientists contribute their knowledge, strength, and peaceful labor with unparalleled enthusiasm to improve agriculture, build huge reservoirs, new factories and roads, exploit underground treasures, and improve our country's economic and cultural level.

The scientific workers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria wish the World Federation of Scientific Workers further success in consolidating the organization, in the selfless struggle for international cooperation, and in using science for peaceful purposes for the benefit of all mankind.

May the World Federation of Scientific Workers prosper and live long!

### 保加利亚基·勃拉泰诺夫教授的发言

1956-04-06
第3版()
专栏：

    保加利亚基·勃拉泰诺夫教授的发言敬爱的朋友们和同志们：
    我代表保加利亚科学工作者联合会向隆重庆祝我们亲爱的协会成立十周年纪念的大会致以衷心的祝贺。
    在伟大的中华人民共和国的首都——北京来庆祝我们协会成立的十周年，这是鼓舞着我们协会的思想的伟大和威力的象征性的表现。这个纪念日证明：我们协会几乎在地球的每个角落都有自己的基地，有自己的拥护者，他们以极大的热情为实现协会崇高的人道主义的目标而奋斗。
    只要回顾一下1946年一批热情的科学工作者建立我们协会时的情形，就证明了过去英雄的十年是为实现国际合作、为使科学服务于和平与进步的事业而进行顽强斗争的十年，证明了我们的协会在组织上不断巩固和成长。因此，今天可以满意地指出，由于这些努力，我们的协会为之奋斗的伟大思想已经团结了世界上千千万万的科学家，世界科学工作者协会已经是国际合作和巩固世界和平的重要因素。
    有了十年以上的历史并且从1948年就已成为协会会员的保加利亚科学工作者联合会以衷心喜悦的心情来迎接这个真正的伟大节日。这个节日也是我国科学工作者的节日，他们清楚地意识到科学工作者的社会职责是什么，并为了建立为人民服务的真正的有益于人民的科学、为了巩固全世界科学工作者间的合作、为了实现科学的伟大理想和目的而以极其顽强的精神和高度热情工作着。
    保加利亚科学工作者们以无比的热情贡献出自己的知识、力量和和平的劳动用于改善农业、建设巨大的水库、新的工厂和道路、开采地下宝藏和提高我国经济和文化水平。
    保加利亚人民共和国的科学工作者预祝世界科学工作者协会在巩固组织，在为争取国际合作和使科学用于为全人类造福的和平目的的忘我斗争中取得进一步的成就。
    祝世界科学工作者协会繁荣和万岁！
