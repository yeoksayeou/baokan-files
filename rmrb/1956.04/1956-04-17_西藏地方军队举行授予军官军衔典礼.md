Title: Tibetan Local Army Holds Ceremony for Conferring Military Ranks
Author:
Date: 1956-04-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 16th. A ceremony for conferring the ranks of General, Colonel, and Lieutenant to officers of the Tibetan local army and the Panchen Guard Battalion was held in Norbulingka, Lhasa on the 15th.

The conferment of military ranks to officers of the Tibetan local army and the Panchen Guard Battalion was requested by the Dalai Lama Tenzin Gyatso and the Panchen Erdini Choekyi Gyaltsen to the State Council and approved by the State Council.

Attending the ceremony were the Dalai Lama, Lieutenant General Zhang Guohua of the Tibet Military Region, Lieutenant General Apao Awang Jinmei, Major General Chen Mingyi, and Major General Jin Shaoshan. Also invited were Fan Ming, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Tibet Working Committee, responsible cadres of organizations under the Tibet Working Committee, colonels of the Tibet Military Region in Lhasa, and principal officials of the Tibetan local government and the Panchen Kampus Assembly Hall.

The troops of the Tibetan local army and the Panchen Guard Battalion stationed in Lhasa participated in the rank conferring ceremony.

After the ceremony began, Lieutenant General Apao Awang Jinmei respectively announced the order of Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, conferring the ranks of Colonel and Lieutenant. Dalai Lama Tenzin Gyatso, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, conferred the rank of Major General to Maji Keimo Soan Wangdui of the Tibetan local army on behalf of Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council. Then, Lieutenant General Zhang Guohua, on behalf of Minister of National Defense Peng Dehuai, conferred the ranks of Colonel and Lieutenant.

### 西藏地方军队举行授予军官军衔典礼

1956-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    西藏地方军队举行授予军官军衔典礼
    新华社拉萨16日电　西藏地方军队和班禅警卫营十五日在拉萨罗布林卡举行授予将军、校、尉官军衔典礼。
    这次授予西藏地方军队和班禅警卫营军官军衔，是达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措和班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞向国务院呈请并经国务院批准授予的。
    参加典礼的有达赖喇嘛、西藏军区张国华中将、阿沛·阿旺晋美中将、陈明义少将、金绍山少将。应邀参加的有中共西藏工委副书记范明，还有西藏工委所属机关的负责干部，西藏军区在拉萨的校级军官，西藏地方政府和班禅堪布会议厅的主要官员。
    西藏地方军队和班禅警卫营在拉萨的部队，参加了授衔典礼。
    典礼开始后，阿沛·阿旺晋美中将分别宣读了中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀授予校官、尉官的命令。全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措代表国务院周恩来总理授予西藏地方军队马基凯墨·索安旺堆少将军衔。接着，由张国华中将代表国防部长彭德怀授予校官、尉官军衔。
