Title: Central Leaders Receive a Group of Advanced Producers
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-25
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: Chairman Mao Zedong and members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Peng Zhen, Dong Biwu, Kang Sheng, and Deng Xiaoping, received representatives from the National Light Industry Advanced Producers Conference, the National Agricultural Water Conservancy Advanced Producers Conference, the National Cultural Advanced Workers Conference, and the National Transportation Advanced Producers Conference this afternoon.

### 中央领导同志接见一批先进生产者

1956-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中央领导同志接见一批先进生产者
    新华社24日讯  毛泽东主席和中共中央政治局委员刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、彭真、董必武、康生、邓小平，今天下午接见了出席全国轻工业先进生产者代表会议、全国农业水利先进生产者代表会议、全国文化先进工作者会议和全国交通先进生产者代表会议的代表们。
