Title: Peng Zhen Receives Five Legal Workers from Britain and France
Author:
Date: 1956-04-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received British legal workers Jones, Gower, and Milner, and French legal workers Sarot and Lask today morning. Wu Kejian, Wu Defeng, and Wang Lifu were present at the reception.

### 彭真接见英法五位法律工作者

1956-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真接见英法五位法律工作者
    新华社25日讯  全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真今天上午接见了英国法律工作者琼斯、高厄、米尔纳，法国法律工作者萨罗特、拉斯克。接见时在座的有吴克坚、吴德峰、王黎夫。
