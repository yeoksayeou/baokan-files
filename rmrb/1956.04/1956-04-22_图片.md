Cadmium sulfide photoresistors have been successfully trial-produced by Zhou Dingxin (left) and Huang Daorong, technicians at the state-owned Shanghai Precision Medical Instrument Factory. This is a scene of them studying the performance of cadmium sulfide semiconductors in the laboratory.
