Title: Inner Mongolia Establishes an Amateur Marxist-Leninist University
Author:
Date: 1956-04-16
Page: 3

Inner Mongolia establishes an amateur Marxist-Leninist university
    The Inner Mongolia Amateur Marxist-Leninist University opened on the afternoon of the 14th. It is jointly run by the Inner Mongolia College of Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Medicine and the Inner Mongolia Normal College. Assistant teachers, lecturers, and professors from both colleges have already enrolled to study. There are currently 218 students. The students will complete courses such as Dialectical and Historical Materialism and Political Economics within six years. They will study for four hours per week. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 内蒙古举办一所马克思列宁主义业余大学

1956-04-16
第3版()
专栏：

    内蒙古举办一所马克思列宁主义业余大学
    内蒙古马克思列宁主义业余大学十四日下午开学。这是由内蒙古畜牧兽医学院和内蒙古师范学院合办的，两个学院的助教、讲师和教授都已经报名参加学习，现有学员218名。学员们将在六年内分别学完辩证唯物主义和历史唯物主义、政治经济学等课程。每周学习四小时。　（新华社）
