Title: Ceylon People's United Front Leader Bandaranaike Becomes Prime Minister
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 13th. Colombo dispatch: Bandaranaike, leader of the People's United Front, which won a majority of seats in the Ceylon House of Representatives elections, was sworn in as Prime Minister of Ceylon at noon on April 12th.

    Bandaranaike announced the list of twelve other cabinet members of the new cabinet, and he himself concurrently serves as Minister of Foreign Affairs and Minister of Defense.

    Bandaranaike delivered a radio address to the nation on the evening of the 12th. He asked all friends and supporters of his new government, as well as his political opponents, to give him support and cooperation.

    The Lanka Sama Samaja Party, which won fourteen seats in the election, issued a statement on the same day, announcing that the party will fully support the new government in completing the various reforms it promised to implement during the election campaign.

### 锡兰人民联合阵线领袖班达拉奈克就任总理

1956-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰人民联合阵线领袖班达拉奈克就任总理
    新华社新德里13日电　科伦坡消息：在锡兰国会众议院议员选举中赢得多数议席的人民联合阵线的领袖班达拉奈克已经在四月十二日中午宣誓就任锡兰总理的职务。
    班达拉奈克宣布了新内阁其他十二个阁员的名单，他本人兼任外交部长和国防部长。
    班达拉奈克在十二日晚上向全国人民发表了广播演说。他要求他的新政府的一切朋友和支持者以及他的政治对手给他以支持和合作。
    在选举中获得十四席的新平等社会党在同一天发表声明，宣布这个党将充分支持新政府完成它在竞选期间所保证要实行的各种改革。
