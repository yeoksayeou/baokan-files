Title: Trade Union Delegations from Various Countries Arrive in Beijing to Attend "May Day" Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1956-04-24
Page: 4
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd. Trade union delegations invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the "May Day" celebrations in China, including the Mongolian trade union delegation headed by Lubusan Sangbu, the Korean trade union delegation headed by Li Hengkui, and the Romanian trade union delegation headed by Liuba Kisinyevsky, arrived in Beijing on the 22nd and 23rd respectively.

### 参加“五一”节观礼  各国工会代表团陆续到北京

1956-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

    参加“五一”节观礼
    各国工会代表团陆续到北京
    新华社23日讯  应中华全国总工会邀请来中国参加“五一”节观礼的以鲁布山桑布为首的蒙古工会代表团，以李亨奎为首的朝鲜工会代表团，以柳巴·基辛涅夫斯基为首的罗马尼亚工会代表团，分别在二十二日和二十三日到达北京。
