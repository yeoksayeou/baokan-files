Title: The United States Prepares for Armed Intervention in the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1956-04-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington: Dulles said at an emergency meeting with leaders of both parties in the U.S. Congress on the 10th regarding the Middle East situation, that if the situation in the Middle East becomes serious, the government may request the use of American troops in that region. An Associated Press dispatch stated: This meeting was held a few hours after President Eisenhower issued a policy statement on the Middle East situation. "The meeting discussed what actions Congress might take on the Middle East issue."

### 美国准备武装干涉中东

1956-04-13
第4版()
专栏：

    美国准备武装干涉中东
    据新华社讯　华盛顿消息：杜勒斯在十日同美国国会两党领袖就中东局势举行的紧急会议上说：如果中东局势严重起来的话，政府可能要求在那个地区使用美国部队。合众社一条消息说：这次会议是在艾森豪威尔总统就中东局势发表政策声明之后几小时举行的。“会上讨论了国会在中东问题上可能采取什么行动的问题”。
