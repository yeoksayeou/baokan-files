Title: National Women's Federation and other organizations jointly hold a reception to host representatives of the International Democratic Women's Federation Council

Author:
Date: 1956-04-25
Page: 3

The All-China Women's Federation and other organizations jointly hold a reception
To host representatives of the International Democratic Women's Federation Council

　　The All-China Women’s Federation, the Chinese People’s National Committee for the Defence of Children, the Chinese Women’s Federation Association, the National Association of the Young Women’s Christian Association of China, and the National Association of the Chinese Women’s Temperance Union jointly held a grand reception at the Beijing Hotel at 8 pm on the 24th to host friends from various countries attending the Beijing meeting of the International Democratic Women's Federation Council.

　　Eugénie Cotton, President of the International Democratic Women’s Federation, the Vice-Presidents of the International Democratic Women’s Federation, the Secretary-General, Deputy Secretary-General, and the Council members, specially invited representatives, and staff from various countries attended the reception. Cai Chang, President of the All-China Women’s Federation, Vice-Presidents Shi Liang and Xu Guangping, Kang Keqing, Secretary-General of the Chinese People’s National Committee for the Defence of Children, Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Women’s Federation Association, Deng Yuzhi, General Secretary of the National Association of the Young Women’s Christian Association of China, and Liu Wang Liming, President of the National Association of the Chinese Women’s Temperance Union, warmly received friends from all over the world.

　　Cai Chang announced the start of the reception. She warmly welcomed Mrs. Cotton and friends from various countries attending the Beijing meeting of the International Democratic Women’s Federation, and wished everyone good health. The guests and hosts began warm and friendly conversations, and the venue was very lively. Cai Chang raised her glass and circled the venue, toasting friends at each table, for world peace, and for the unity, friendship, and cooperation between women of all countries.

　　He Xiangning, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission and Honorary President of the All-China Women’s Federation, also raised her glass to toast everyone.

　　Mrs. Cotton raised her glass amid warm applause from the audience and said: Someone told me that I am the mother of a large family. When I entered this hall tonight and saw so many daughters, I felt that I am indeed such a mother. I thank the daughters of Asia, especially the daughters of China, for such a good reception. I toast to everyone's health and achievements in their work.

　　After the reception, a wonderful artistic program was performed.

　　Female representatives of the National People's Congress, female members of the Political Consultative Conference, female ministers of the Central People's Government, heads of various people's organizations, women representatives of various ethnic groups, and women from all walks of life in the capital attended the reception on the 24th.

　　More than 700 guests and hosts attended the reception on the 24th. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国妇联等团体联合举行酒会  招待国际民主妇联理事会代表

1956-04-25
第3版()
专栏：

    全国妇联等团体联合举行酒会
    招待国际民主妇联理事会代表
    中华全国民主妇女联合会、中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会、中国妇女联谊会、中华基督教女青年会全国协会、中华妇女节制会全国总会二十四日晚间八时联合在北京饭店举行盛大酒会，招待参加国际民主妇女联合会理事会北京会议的各国朋友们。
    国际民主妇女联合会主席欧仁妮·戈登和国际民主妇女联合会的副主席们，总书记、副总书记以及各国理事、特邀代表和工作人员等参加了酒会。中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅、副主席史良、许广平，中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会秘书长康克清，中国妇女联谊会主席李德全，中华基督教女青年会全国协会总干事邓裕志，中华妇女节制会全国总会会长刘王立明热情地接待来自世界各地的各国朋友们。
    蔡畅宣布酒会开始。她热烈欢迎参加国际民主妇女联合会北京会议的戈登夫人和各国朋友们，并祝大家身体健康。宾主们开始了热烈友好的谈话，会场里显得十分活跃。蔡畅举杯环绕会场，到每个桌子前向朋友们祝酒，为世界和平，为各国妇女之间的团结、友谊、合作而干杯。
    华侨事务委员会主任、中华全国民主妇女联合会名誉主席何香凝，也举杯向大家祝酒。
    戈登夫人在全场热烈鼓掌声中举杯说：有人告诉我说我是一个大家庭的母亲，当我今天晚上进入这个大厅，看到这么多的女儿时，我感到我确实是这样一个母亲。我感谢亚洲的女儿们特别是中国的女儿们这样好的招待我们。我为大家的身体健康和工作上的成就而干杯。
    酒会后表演了精采的文艺节目。
    二十四日出席作陪的有全国人民代表大会的女代表，政治协商会议的女委员，中央人民政府的女部长以及各人民团体负责人、各民族的妇女代表和首都各界妇女等。
    参加二十四日酒会的宾主们共七百多人。（新华社）
