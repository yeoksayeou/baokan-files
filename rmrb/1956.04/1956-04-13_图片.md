Title: Our country's first domestically produced Soviet-style 6AM 12-tank flotation machine successfully trial-produced
Author:
Date: 1956-04-13
Page: 1

Our country's first domestically produced Soviet-style 6AM 12-tank flotation machine has been successfully trial-produced at the Shenyang Mining Machinery Plant. This tank flotation machine, mostly made of materials supplied by Anshan Iron and Steel, is now directly supporting the production of the Anshan Iron and Steel Concentrator.
