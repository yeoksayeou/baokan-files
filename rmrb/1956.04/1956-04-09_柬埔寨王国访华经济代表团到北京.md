Title: Economic Delegation from the Kingdom of Cambodia Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 8th: The economic delegation from the Kingdom of Cambodia arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

This four-person delegation is headed by Hu Sen'a, Minister of Engineering, Posts and Telecommunications, and Planning. The members include Li Luomu, Director of the National Postal and Telecommunications Bureau and former Minister, and Mao Sai, Member of Parliament and former Minister. The delegation's secretary is Li Jinxing.

The delegation was warmly welcomed upon arrival in Beijing by Li Zheren, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister, and others.

### 柬埔寨王国访华经济代表团到北京

1956-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨王国访华经济代表团到北京
    新华社8日讯　柬埔寨王国访华经济代表团，在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    这个由四人组成的代表团，团长是工程、邮电和计划部长胡森阿，团员有全国邮电局局长、前部长李洛穆和议员、前部长毛赛，代表团秘书是李金兴。
    代表团到达北京时，受到了对外贸易部代理部长李哲人、副部长雷任民等人的热情欢迎。
