Title: Yemen's King Temporarily Signs Military Agreement Between Yemen and Saudi Arabia
Author:
Date: 1956-04-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hodeida, Yemen, April 11th: According to Agence France-Presse, Yemen's King Imam Ahmad temporarily signed a military agreement between Yemen and Saudi Arabia on April 10th. This agreement will link Yemen to the military treaty system already established between Saudi Arabia, Syria, and Egypt.

The report says that Yemen's Crown Prince Emir Badr will go to Jeddah to deliver a personal letter from the King of Yemen to the King of Saudi Arabia, as well as the full text of the approved draft agreement. Yemen's Deputy Foreign Minister, Muhammad al-Amari, will meet with Emir Badr in Jeddah. They will complete negotiations on the new agreement.

Agence France-Presse pointed out that the Saudi Arabia-Yemen treaty will strengthen the position of both countries in their disputes with Britain. Saudi Arabia and Britain have a dispute over the Buraimi Oasis, and Yemen and Britain have a dispute over the tribal states bordering Yemen and the Aden Protectorate.

### 也门国王临时签署也门和沙特阿拉伯军事协定

1956-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

    也门国王临时签署也门和沙特阿拉伯军事协定
    新华社11日讯　据法新社也门荷得达消息：也门国王伊马姆·艾哈迈德四月十日临时签署了也门和沙特阿拉伯之间的一项军事协定，这项协定将把也门同沙特阿拉伯、叙利亚和埃及已经缔结的军事条约体系联结起来。
    消息说，也门太子埃米尔·巴德尔将去吉达向沙特阿拉伯国王递交也门国王的亲笔信和已经批准的协定草案的全文。也门外交副大臣穆罕默德·艾马里将在吉达同埃米尔·巴德尔会合。他们将完成关于新协定的谈判。
    法新社指出，沙特阿拉伯—也门条约将加强两国在它们同英国的争执中的地位。沙特阿拉伯同英国间存在着关于布赖米绿洲的争执，也门同英国间存在着关于也门同亚丁保护地接壤的土邦的争执。
