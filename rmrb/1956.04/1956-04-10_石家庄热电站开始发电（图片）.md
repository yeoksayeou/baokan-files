Title: Shijiazhuang Thermal Power Plant Starts Generating Electricity (Photo)
Author: Li Yu
Date: 1956-04-10
Page: 2

    Shijiazhuang Thermal Power Plant Starts Generating Electricity

    The first turbine generator unit of the Shijiazhuang Thermal Power Plant was completed five months ahead of schedule and has begun generating electricity. The electricity generated by the power plant's turbine is equivalent to 65% of the existing power equipment capacity of Shijiazhuang City. In a corner of the central control room of the power plant, the on-duty worker is adjusting the load of the generator.
                                        Photo by Li Yu (Manuscript from Hebei Daily)

### 石家庄热电站开始发电（图片）
李棫
1956-04-10
第2版()
专栏：

    石家庄热电站开始发电
    石家庄热电站第一号汽轮发电机组比原计划提前五个月建成，并开始发电。这个热电站的汽轮机发出的电力，相当于石家庄市现有电力设备能力的65％。在热电站中心控制室的一角，值班工人正在调整发电机的负荷。
                                        李棫摄（河北日报社稿）
