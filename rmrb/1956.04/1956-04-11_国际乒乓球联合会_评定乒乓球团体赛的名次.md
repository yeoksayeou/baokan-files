Title: International Table Tennis Federation Ranks Teams in Table Tennis Team Championships
Author:
Date: 1956-04-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 10th. The International Table Tennis Federation (ITTF) at its annual meeting in Tokyo decided today, at a plenary session, on the ranking of the results of this year's World Table Tennis Team Championships.

According to the ITTF's decision, the Chinese men's team was ranked sixth in the first division, and the Chinese women's team was ranked eleventh in the first division. This assessment by the ITTF indicates that China's table tennis technical level has been recognized internationally as having significantly improved in the last two years. In 1954, the Chinese men's team was ranked tenth in the first division, and the women's team was ranked seventh in the second division.

The ITTF's ranking of men's and women's teams is as follows:

    Men's Teams – First Division: 1. Japan; 2. Czechoslovakia; 3. Romania; 4. Hungary; 5. Great Britain; 6. China; 7. Germany (The table tennis organizations of East Germany and West Germany are a single member of the ITTF); 8. South Vietnam; 9. Yugoslavia; 10. Hong Kong; 11. Sweden; 12. India; 13. United States; 14. France. Other men's teams were ranked in the second or third divisions.

    Women's Teams – First Division: 1. Romania; 2. Great Britain; 3. Japan; 4. Hungary; 5. Austria; 6. Wales; 7. France; 8. Czechoslovakia; 9. United States; 10. South Korea; 11. China. Other women's teams were ranked in the second or third divisions.

The ITTF also decided at today's plenary session that the 1959 World Table Tennis Championships will be held in Germany (location to be determined).

The ITTF plenary session approved Ecuador and Syria as members. This increases the number of ITTF members to seventy.

The plenary session also re-elected the ITTF President, Montagu, and all other leaders to their current positions.

### 国际乒乓球联合会  评定乒乓球团体赛的名次

1956-04-11
第4版()
专栏：

    国际乒乓球联合会
    评定乒乓球团体赛的名次
    新华社10日讯　东京消息：国际乒乓球联合会年会在今天的全体会议上，决定了今年度世界乒乓球团体赛成绩的名次。
    根据联合会的决定，中国男子团体被评为第一级第六名，中国女子团体被评为第一级第十一名。联合会的这次评定表明，中国乒乓球运动的技术水平在国际上被认为在最近两年来有了显著的提高。在1954年度，中国男子团体曾被评为第一级第十名，女子团体曾被评为第二级第七名。
    联合会评定的男、女团体的名次如下：
    男子团体——第一级：1、日本；2、捷克斯洛伐克；3、罗马尼亚；4、匈牙利；5、英国；6、中国；7、德国（东德和西德的乒乓球组织是国际乒乓球协会的单一会员）；8、南越；9、南斯拉夫；10、香港；11、瑞典；12、印度；13、美国；14、法国。其他男子团体分别被评为第二级或第三级。
    女子团体——第一级：1、罗马尼亚；2、英国；3、日本；4、匈牙利；5、奥地利；6、威尔士；7、法国；8、捷克斯洛伐克；9、美国；10、南朝鲜；11、中国。其他女子团体分别被评为第二级或第三级。
    国际乒乓球联合会在今天的全体会议上，还决定1959年的世界乒乓球锦标赛将在德国（地点未定）举行。
    联合会的全体会议批准了厄瓜多尔和叙利亚两国为会员。这就使得国际乒乓球联合会的会员增加到七十个。
    全体会议并且选举了联合会主席蒙塔古等全体领导人员连任现在的职务。
