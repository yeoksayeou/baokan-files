Title: The Expansion Project of Baotou Power Plant
Author:
Date: 1956-04-14
Page: 1

The expansion project of the Baotou Power Plant completed 75% of all installation work one month ahead of schedule at the end of March. A new generator, originally planned to be put into production on May 1st, has also started generating electricity ahead of schedule on April 10th. This is an exterior view of the Baotou Power Plant under expansion.
