Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Banquet for International Democratic Women's Federation Council Members

Author:
Date: 1956-04-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, hosted a grand banquet at the Beijing Hotel on the evening of the 27th to entertain all council members, special representatives, and staff attending the Beijing meeting of the International Democratic Women's Federation Council.

At the banquet, Premier Zhou Enlai spoke first, extending a warm welcome on behalf of the Chinese government and the Chinese people to friends from various countries attending this meeting of the International Democratic Women's Federation Council. He said, "This meeting will help promote mutual understanding and solidarity and cooperation among women from various countries, and will certainly be conducive to realizing the common aspirations of women from all countries." Premier Zhou Enlai raised his glass for the solidarity, friendship, and cooperation among people from all countries, especially among women from all countries, for world peace, and for the health of Madame Eugénie Cotton, President of the International Democratic Women's Federation, and friends from various countries attending this meeting. At this moment, the entire audience stood up, and the applause lasted for a long time.

Guo Moruo, representing the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Chen Shutong, representing the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Peng Zhen, Mayor of Beijing, representing all the people of Beijing, successively raised their glasses to express a warm welcome to the foreign guests and to congratulate the meeting on its success.

A group of female advanced producers who attended the National Conference of Advanced Producers' Representatives came to the head table, and they raised their glasses to wish Madame Cotton and the leaders of the women's movements of various countries good health.

The banquet was repeatedly interrupted by joyful performances of folk dances and songs from various countries. The guests and hosts participating in the banquet enthusiastically danced the Romanian folk dance - the Handkerchief Dance, and they danced lightly around each other with handkerchiefs. A Korean friend invited Mr. Mei Lanfang to dance the Korean folk dance "Chailang and the Village Girl" with her. Friends from Nigeria, Egypt, India, Nepal, Côte d'Ivoire, Gold Coast, Senegal, Italy, and other countries sang their national folk songs. The Chinese Yangko dance aroused great interest among foreign friends. People held hands and danced around the entire hall for a long time. Everyone laughed loudly and embraced each other affectionately as they danced.

Madame Cotton gave a speech amidst warm applause from the audience. She said that the kind sisterly feelings and the noble human emotions seen at the banquet were very moving to those who attended the banquet. The Chinese people have already touched the hearts of women all over the world.

Madame Cotton said that we live in a very great era, an era of people's liberation and independence. It is understandable that in this era, women's liberation and the attainment of human status is possible.

Madame Cotton pointed out that women are proud of the achievements of the peace movement because women have played a part in it.

Madame Cotton said that the International Democratic Women's Federation has been working among the most distressed women since its establishment, helping them to unite. We are happy that the International Democratic Women's Federation family is growing day by day.

Finally, Madame Cotton said that the overflowing enthusiasm of the Chinese people sets an example for women, and women should strengthen cooperation and strive to defend peace and the happiness of children.

Those present today to accompany the guests included Shen Junru, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Chen Shutong, He Xiangning, and Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, and Vice Chairmen Deng Yingchao, Li Dequan, and Shi Liang, ministers and vice ministers of various ministries of the Central People's Government, heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and representatives from all walks of life and fraternal nationalities in the capital. Some female representatives from Beijing who came to attend the National Advanced Producers' Representatives Conference were also invited to attend tonight's banquet.

Before the end of the banquet, Premier Zhou Enlai raised his glass and went around the hall to toast the guests one by one. Finally, Deng Yingchao raised her glass to wish all the friends a pleasant stay in China and good health.

### 周总理宴请国际妇联理事等

1956-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理宴请国际妇联理事等
    据新华社27日讯  中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来二十七日晚八时在北京饭店举行盛大宴会，招待参加国际民主妇联理事会北京会议的全体理事、特邀代表和工作人员。
    宴会上，周恩来总理首先讲话，他代表中国政府和中国人民向参加这次国际民主妇联理事会会议的各国朋友们表示热烈的欢迎。他说：“这次会议将有助于促进各国妇女之间的相互了解和团结合作，而且也一定会有利于争取各国妇女共同愿望的实现”。周恩来总理举杯为各国人民之间，特别是各国妇女之间的团结、友好和合作，为世界和平，为国际民主妇联主席欧仁妮·戈登夫人和参加这次会议的各国朋友们的健康干杯。这时全场起立，掌声经久不息。
    郭沫若代表中国人民保卫世界和平委员会，陈叔通代表中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会，北京市市长彭真代表北京市全体人民，先后举杯向外宾表示热烈欢迎，并祝贺会议成功。
    一批出席全国先进生产者代表会议的女先进生产者来到了主宾席，她们举杯祝贺戈登夫人和各国妇女运动的领导人身体健康。
    宴会多次为欢乐的各国民间舞蹈和歌唱的表演所中断。参加宴会的宾主们热情地跳着罗马尼亚的民间舞蹈——手绢舞，她们互相用手绢围着对方跳着轻松的舞步。一位朝鲜的朋友请梅兰芳先生和她一起跳了朝鲜的民间舞蹈“柴郎和村女”。尼日利亚、埃及、印度、尼泊尔、象牙海岸、黄金海岸、塞内加尔、意大利等国家的朋友演唱了她们国家的民间歌曲。中国的秧歌舞引起了外国朋友们极大的兴趣，人们手拉手的围着整个大厅跳了很长的时间，大家忘情地高声欢笑着，互相亲热地拥抱着，舞跳着。
    戈登夫人在全场热烈鼓掌声中致词，他说，在宴会上看到的亲切的姊妹的感情，人类崇高的感情，使参加宴会的人非常感动，中国人民已经打动了全世界妇女的心。
    戈登夫人说，我们生活在非常伟大的时代，人民解放和独立的时代。就在这时代，妇女的解放，获得作为人的地位，这是可以理解的。
    戈登夫人指出，妇女们对和平运动的成就感到自豪。因为这中间有妇女的一份作用。
    戈登夫人说，国际民主妇联从成立的时候起，就在最困苦的妇女中工作，帮助她们团结起来，我们因国际民主妇联大家庭人员的一天天增长而高兴。
    戈登夫人最后表示，中国人民洋溢的热情为妇女们作出榜样，妇女们要加强合作，为保卫和平和儿童幸福而努力。
    今天出席作陪的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通、何香凝、章伯钧，中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅，副主席邓颖超、李德全、史良，中央人民政府各部部长，副部长，各民主党派、各人民团体负责人和首都各界人士和兄弟民族代表。来北京参加全国先进生产者代表会议的部分女代表也应邀出席了今晚的宴会。
    散席之前，周恩来总理举杯绕场一周，向客人们一一敬酒。最后，邓颖超举杯祝各位朋友们在中国期间精神愉快，身体健康。
