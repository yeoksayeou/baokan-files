Today is the 60th anniversary of the founding of Jiaotong University. This university is gradually developing into an advanced multi-disciplinary higher industrial school for training talents for heavy industry construction. The university has seven departments and twenty-three specialties, with a total of 6,050 students. This is an exterior view of Jiaotong University.
