Title: Italian Communist Party Issues Appeal to Voters for Municipal Elections
Author:
Date: 1956-04-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, 9th: Italy will hold municipal council elections in 7,443 districts nationwide on May 27th. According to a report in "Unità", the Italian Communist Party has issued an appeal to Italian voters for this election.

The appeal to voters analyzes the international situation and the internal situation in Italy on the eve of the election. It states that the Communist Party "calls on everyone to unite around its ranks, calls on workers, all working people, and all good people to cooperate and unite to move forward on the path of peace, democracy, and progress."

### 意共为市政选举发表告选民书

1956-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

    意共为市政选举发表告选民书
    新华社9日讯　罗马消息：意大利将在五月二十七日举行全国七千四百四十三个市区的市政委员会的选举。据“团结报”报道，意大利共产党为这次选举发表了告意大利选民书。
    告选民书对选举前夕的国际局势和意大利内部情况作了分析，它说，共产党“号召大家团结在它的队伍的周围，号召工人、全体劳动人民和全体善良的人们进行合作，团结起来，在和平、民主和进步的道路上向前迈进。”
