Title: Twenty-Five Student Organizations from Different Countries Condemn Colonialism for Hindering Cultural Development
Author:
Date: 1956-04-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 21st: Thirty-six representatives from twenty-five student organizations from various countries held a conference in Prague from April 14th to 19th to discuss issues related to culture and education in colonial countries.

    The discussion unanimously adopted a statement condemning colonialism for hindering the development of national cultures of various peoples, and concluded that the primary conditions for revitalizing and developing the national cultures of various peoples, for making school education serve the people, are the elimination of the colonial system and the achievement of national independence.

    The representatives of various countries participating in the discussion exchanged experiences of student life under colonial rule, and also expressed the hope that such discussions will become a regular activity of the international student movement.

### 不同国家的二十五个学生组织  谴责殖民主义阻碍文化的发展

1956-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    不同国家的二十五个学生组织
    谴责殖民主义阻碍文化的发展
    新华社布拉格21日电  各个不同国家的二十五个学生组织的三十六位代表自四月十四日到十九日在布拉格举行了一个会议，讨论了殖民地国家文化教育的问题。
    讨论会一致通过声明，谴责殖民主义阻碍各国人民的民族文化的发展，并且作出结论：要振兴和发展各国人民的民族文化，要使学校教育为人民服务，消灭殖民制度和取得民族独立是首要条件。
    参加讨论会的各国代表们在会上交流了殖民主义统治下的学生生活经验，并且还表示希望这种讨论会将成为国际学生运动的经常活动之一。
