Title: Bo Yibo Presents Gift on Behalf of Premier Zhou to Sudanese Prime Minister Azhari; Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation Holds Final Performance in Sudan
Author:
Date: 1956-04-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Khartoum, 26th April: On April 25th, Bo Yibo, head of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, presented a gift on behalf of Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, to Sudanese Prime Minister Azhari. Ibrahim Mufti, the Sudanese Acting Prime Minister and Minister of Industry, Commerce and Supply, accepted the gift on behalf of Azhari, who is currently visiting Cairo.

After presenting the gift, Bo Yibo expressed gratitude to the Sudanese government and people for their welcome and reception of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation to Mufti.

Mufti expressed his gratitude for the visit and performance of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation to Sudan.

During the conversation, both sides expressed their belief that this visit by the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation would help strengthen the friendly relations between China and Sudan.

Prior to this, Sudanese Minister of Social Affairs Ziada Ababu hosted a banquet on the evening of April 24th to entertain the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, Khartoum, 26th April: The Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation held its final performance in Khartoum on the evening of April 23rd.

Like the previous performances, which required the addition of temporary seats, on the evening of April 23rd, in addition to all 1,200 seats being filled, there were also 600 audience members standing to watch. At the same time, several hundred more audience members stood outside the theater, listening to Chinese songs and music. They also applauded and cheered from time to time, unwilling to leave.

Many people waited at the entrance of the theater to see off the Chinese artists, requesting them to sign autographs and chanting "Long live peace" in Chinese.

The performance of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation has become the center of discussion in the Sudanese capital. The Chinese artists were warmly welcomed everywhere when they walked on the streets, people shook hands with them, and expressed their gratitude for the opportunity to see the excellent art of China.

### 鲍尔汉代表周总理向苏丹总理阿扎里赠送礼物  我国文化艺术代表团在苏丹举行最后一次访问演出

1956-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

    鲍尔汉代表周总理向苏丹总理阿扎里赠送礼物
    我国文化艺术代表团在苏丹举行最后一次访问演出
    新华社喀土穆26日电  四月二十五日，中国文化艺术代表团团长鲍尔汉代表中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来向苏丹总理阿扎里赠送了礼物。礼物由苏丹代理总理、工商供应部长易卜拉欣·穆弗蒂代为接受。阿扎里总理目前在开罗访问。
    鲍尔汉在赠送礼物以后，向穆弗蒂表示感谢苏丹政府和人民对于中国文化艺术代表团的欢迎和接待。
    穆弗蒂对于中国文化艺术代表团到苏丹访问和演出表示感谢。
    在谈话时，双方都表示相信中国文化艺术代表团的这次访问将有助于加强中国和苏丹的友好关系。
    在这以前，苏丹社会事务部长齐亚达·阿巴布曾在四月二十四日晚间设宴招待中国文化艺术代表团。
    据新华社喀土穆26日电  中国文化艺术代表团四月二十三日晚上在喀土穆举行了最后一次访问演出。
    像过去的每次演出都需要临时增添座位一样，在四月二十三日晚上演出时，除全部一千二百个座位都坐满以外，还有六百名观众站着看。同时还有好几百位观众站在戏院外面，倾听中国的歌曲和音乐。他们也时时鼓掌叫好，不愿意离开。
    许多人在戏院门口等着欢送中国艺术家们，请求他们签名留念，并且用中国话高喊“和平万岁”。
    中国文化艺术代表团的演出已经成为苏丹首都谈论的中心。中国艺术家们在街上行走的时候到处受到人们的热情欢迎，人们同他们握手，对于自己有机会能够看到中国的优秀艺术表示感谢。
