Title: PLA Officers and Soldiers Over-Subscribe to Government Bonds
Author:
Date: 1956-04-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The officers and soldiers of the entire Chinese People's Liberation Army have completed their subscription to the 1956 National Economic Construction Bonds, exceeding the planned total subscription amount by 35%. The total amount of government bonds planned to be subscribed by the entire army this year has doubled compared to last year.

### 解放军官兵认购公债超过计划

1956-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军官兵认购公债超过计划
    新华社5日讯　中国人民解放军全军官兵超过预定认购总额35％完成了1956年国家经济建设公债的认购工作。今年解放军全军预定认购公债的总额比去年增加一倍。
