Title: Belgian Foreign Trade Minister Says Embargo Against China is Absurd

Author: 
Date: 1956-04-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 17th - According to a Reuters report, Belgian Foreign Trade Minister Larock made a speech to the members of the Mons Chamber of Commerce in Mons, Belgium on the 14th, saying that the trade embargo against China is an absurd thing.

Larock said that the West will sooner or later have to abandon this policy of prohibiting trade. He advocated that the West adopt a more "flexible policy" in trade with countries of the socialist system.

### 比利时对外贸易大臣说  对中国实行禁运是荒谬的事

1956-04-18
第4版()
专栏：

    比利时对外贸易大臣说
    对中国实行禁运是荒谬的事
    新华社17日讯　据路透社报道，比利时对外贸易大臣拉罗克十四日在比利时的蒙斯对蒙斯市商会会员发表演说，他说，对中国实行的贸易禁运是一件荒谬的事。
    拉罗克说，西方迟早将不得不放弃这种禁止贸易的政策。他主张西方在同社会主义体系各国进行贸易方面采取比较“灵活的政策”。
