Title: Romanian, Bulgarian Newspapers Publish "On the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat"
Author:
Date: 1956-04-29
Page: 1

    Romanian, Bulgarian Newspapers Publish "On the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat"
    (Xinhua News Agency) The Romanian Workers' Party's central organ, "Scînteia," and the Bulgarian Communist Party's central organ, "Rabotnichesko Delo," published the People's Daily editorial "On the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat" on April 10th and April 8th respectively. The articles were slightly abridged during publication.

### 罗、保报纸刊载“关于无产阶级专政的历史经验”

1956-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    罗、保报纸刊载“关于无产阶级专政的历史经验”
    本报讯  罗马尼亚工人党中央机关报“火花报”和保加利亚共产党中央机关报“工人事业报”在四月十日和四月八日分别刊登了人民日报编辑部所写的“关于无产阶级专政的历史经验”一文。在刊登时对文章略有删节。
