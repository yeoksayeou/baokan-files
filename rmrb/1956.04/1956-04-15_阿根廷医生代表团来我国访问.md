Title: Argentine Medical Delegation Visits China
Author:
Date: 1956-04-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Buenos Aires, April 14th: A fifteen-member Argentine medical delegation, at the invitation of the Chinese Medical Association of the People's Republic of China, departed on the 11th to visit China.

During their visit to China, the delegation will study the state of China's health undertakings, the implementation of infectious disease prevention measures, and particularly the prevention of children's diseases.

### 阿根廷医生代表团来我国访问

1956-04-15
第4版()
专栏：

    阿根廷医生代表团来我国访问
    新华社14日讯　布宜诺斯艾利斯消息：由十五人组成的阿根廷医生代表团应中华人民共和国中华医学会的邀请，在十一日动身前来中国访问。
    代表团访问中国期间将研究中国卫生事业状况，传染病预防措施执行情况，特别是小儿疾病预防情况。
