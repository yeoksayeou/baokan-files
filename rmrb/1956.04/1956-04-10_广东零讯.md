	Guangdong Snippets
	The Guangdong Provincial State-owned Shunde Sugar Factory produced 958 tons more sugar than the state plan in the first quarter of this year. The factory's boiling recovery rate in March reached 95.02%, and the total recovery rate reached 90.46%, breaking its national record set in February.
	Guangdong Province has had a bumper harvest of spring tea this year. Preliminary estimates indicate an increase of about 30% compared to the same period last year. Tea farmers in various regions generally started picking twenty days earlier than last year, and various new teas have been successively launched on the market.

### 广东零讯

1956-04-10
第2版()
专栏：广东零讯

    广东零讯
    广东省国营顺德糖厂今年第一季度生产的砂糖，比国家计划多958吨。这个厂三月份的煮炼收回率达到95.02％，总收回率达到90.46％，突破了它在二月份创造的全国最高纪录。
    广东省今年春茶大丰收，初步估计，比去年同期增产30％左右。今年各地茶农一般都比去年提早二十天开始采摘，目前各种新茶已经陆续上市。
