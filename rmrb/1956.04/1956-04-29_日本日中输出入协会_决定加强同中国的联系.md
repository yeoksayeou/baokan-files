Title: Japan-China Import and Export Association Decides to Strengthen Ties with China
Author:
Date: 1956-04-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 28th: The Japan-China Import and Export Association's Board of Directors decided on the 27th to strengthen communication with Chinese trade organizations and invite Chinese trade representatives to visit Japan.

    The association's business plan for this year, as decided by the Board of Directors, also includes: strengthening communication with relevant organizations and negotiations with the Japanese government, promoting the signing of the fourth Japan-China trade agreement, and striving to achieve the association's target of a total trade volume of 43 million pounds per year for each of Japan and China. At the same time, the association will establish a committee to assist the Japanese commodity exhibition to be held in China. The association will also fully assist the Japan International Trade Promotion Association in carrying out its work. The Board of Directors decided to send its chairman, Saburo Nango, to visit China.

### 日本日中输出入协会  决定加强同中国的联系

1956-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

    日本日中输出入协会
    决定加强同中国的联系
    据新华社28日讯  东京消息：日本日中输出入协会理事会在二十七日决定，要加强同中国贸易机关的连络，并且邀请中国贸易代表访问日本。
    理事会所决定的协会今年度的事业计划中还包括：加强同有关团体的连络和同日本政府的交涉，促进签订第四次日中贸易协定，努力达成协会所规定的日中双方每方一年各四千三百万英镑贸易总额的目标。同时，协会将设立一个委员会，协助将在中国举行的日本商品展览会。协会还要全面帮助日本国际贸易促进协会顺利进行工作。理事会决定派遣理事长南乡三郎访问中国。
