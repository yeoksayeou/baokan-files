    Chen Songling (left), a student at the Central Conservatory of Music, East China Branch, Secondary Music School, was originally a fifth-grade technician in the high-voltage equipment workshop of the Shanghai Huatong Switch Factory. After being admitted to the music school and studying for more than three years, he is now able to compose music. Here, he is playing his composition, "Fantasia." Photo by Wang Chaozhen, submitted by the Labor Daily.
