Title: Premier Zhou Sends Telegram to Cyrankiewicz to Express Condolences to the Family of Martyr Starzec
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-12
Page: 1
Premier Zhou Sends Telegram to Cyrankiewicz to Express Condolences to the Family of Martyr Starzec

To Comrade Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Poland:
    On the first anniversary of the death of the martyrs who perished while attending the Asian-African Conference, please convey the Chinese government's deep sympathy and condolences to the families of the unfortunate Martyr Starzec.
  
Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
April 11, 1956

### 周总理电西伦凯维兹慰问斯塔列茨烈士家属

1956-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电西伦凯维兹慰问斯塔列茨烈士家属波兰人民共和国部长会议主席约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹同志：
    当参加亚非会议死难烈士遇难一周年的时候，谨请转达中国政府对不幸遇难的斯塔列茨烈士家属的深切同情和慰问。
  中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来
                      1956年4月11日
