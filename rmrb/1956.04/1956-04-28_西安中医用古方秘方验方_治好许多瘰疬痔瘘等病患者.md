Title: Xi'an Traditional Chinese Medicine Doctors Use Ancient, Secret, and Proven Formulas to Cure Many Patients with Scrofula, Hemorrhoids, and Fistulas
Author:
Date: 1956-04-28
Page: 3

(Shaanxi People's Broadcasting Station)

Traditional Chinese medicine doctors in Xi'an City have used ancient, secret, and proven formulas from Chinese medicine to cure many patients suffering from diseases such as scrofula, hemorrhoids and fistulas, and cirrhosis with ascites. Wang Xifeng, a traditional Chinese medicine doctor at Xi'an People's Hospital in Shaanxi Province, has treated more than 520 scrofula patients in the past year using corrosive therapy and acupuncture based on ancestral secret formulas. More than 510 patients have been cured, and there have been no recurrences. Wang Qinglin, a traditional Chinese medicine doctor at Xi'an Central Hospital, uses ancestral secret formulas to treat hemorrhoids and fistulas. First, he performs a simple surgical excision of the fistula, and then applies medicines such as "corrosion-resolving and flesh-generating powder," which can cure the condition in about 20 days. For patients with hemorrhoids, the method of injecting carbolic acid and glycerin into the hemorrhoids is used, and the condition can be cured in about ten days. Cirrhosis with ascites is also a difficult disease to treat. Shen Fanbai, of Xi'an Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital, has used ancient and proven formulas such as "Shi Zao Tang" (Ten Jujube Decoction), "Xiao Zhang Wan" (Bloating Relief Pill), and "Xiao Shui Dan" (Water Elimination Pill) to treat 39 patients in the past five months. Among them, nine have been completely cured, and the condition of the remaining patients has also been greatly alleviated.

### 西安中医用古方秘方验方  治好许多瘰疬痔瘘等病患者

1956-04-28
第3版()
专栏：

    西安中医用古方秘方验方
    治好许多瘰疬痔瘘等病患者
    西安市的中医用我国医学上的古方、秘方和验方治好了许多患瘰疬、痔瘘和肝硬变腹水等病的病人。陕西省西安人民医院中医师王锡俸最近一年来根据祖传秘方，采用腐蚀疗法和针灸疗法治疗的五百二十多名瘰疬患者中，已经有五百一十多名痊愈，而且再没有复发现象。西安市中心医院中医师王庆林用祖传秘方治疗痔瘘时，首先进行简单的切除瘘管手术，再敷上化腐生肌散等药物，约二十天就可以治好。对痔核病患者，用石炭酸和甘油注射在痔核内的办法治疗，十多天就可以治好。肝硬变腹水也是一种很难治的病，西安市中医医院沈反白在最近五个月中用十枣汤、消胀丸和消水丹等古方和验方治疗了三十九个病人，其中有九个人已经完全好了，其余的患者病情也大大减轻。
        （陕西人民广播电台）
