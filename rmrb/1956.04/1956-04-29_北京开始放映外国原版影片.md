Title: Beijing Begins Screening Original Foreign Films
Author:
Date: 1956-04-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency:

    The Soviet Exhibition Center Cinema and the China Youth Art Theater in Beijing began screening the original Soviet films "The Gadfly" and "Loyalty Test" on the 28th, in order to enrich the cultural life of international friends in Beijing and to allow Beijing audiences to appreciate foreign films sooner.
    Original foreign films will be screened successively every Saturday and Sunday from now on. The films to be screened soon include the Soviet film "Twelfth Night", the British film "Hamlet", the French film "No Address Left", the Italian film "Peace Under the Olive Trees", the German Democratic Republic film "Devil's Circle", and the Federal Republic of Germany film "Heart of the World".

### 北京开始放映外国原版影片

1956-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

    北京开始放映外国原版影片
    北京的苏联展览馆电影馆和中国青年艺术剧院二十八日开始分别放映苏联原版影片“牛虻”和“忠诚的考验”，以便丰富在北京的国际友人的文艺生活，同时使北京的观众更早地欣赏到外国的影片。
    今后每星期六和星期日将陆续放映外国的原版影片。最近放映的将有苏联影片“第十二夜”，英国影片“哈姆雷特”，法国影片“没有留下地址”，意大利影片“橄榄树下无和平”，德意志民主共和国影片“魔鬼集团”，德意志联邦共和国影片“世界之心”等。
                          （新华社）
