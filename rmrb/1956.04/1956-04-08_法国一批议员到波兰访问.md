Title: French Delegation of Parliamentarians Visits Poland
Author:
Date: 1956-04-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: A delegation of French Senators arrived in Warsaw on the 5th. The members of the delegation are all former participants in the Resistance Movement, and they are visiting Poland at the invitation of the Polish Alliance of Fighters for Freedom and Democracy. The head of the delegation, the famous writer Jacques Debû-Bridel, told reporters at the airport: "We are here to promote the cause of consolidating the traditional friendship between the French and Polish people."

### 法国一批议员到波兰访问

1956-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    法国一批议员到波兰访问
    据新华社讯　法国参议院议员代表团五日到达华沙。代表团成员都是前抵抗运动的参加者，他们是应波兰争取自由与民主战士联盟的邀请到波兰来访问的。代表团团长、著名作家雅克·德布—布里德在机场上告诉记者们说：“我们到这里来是为了促进巩固法国和波兰人民之间的传统友谊的事业的。”
