Title: Closing of the Workers' Amateur Quyi Performance and Competition

Author: 
Date: 1956-04-22
Page: 3

The National Workers' Amateur Quyi Performance and Competition closed in Beijing on the afternoon of the 21st. Representatives of the judging committee announced the results at the closing ceremony: 104 authors received first, second, and third prizes for creation respectively; 196 performing units received first, second, and third prizes for programs respectively; and a total of 480 worker actors and accompanists received first, second, and third prizes for actors and accompanists respectively. Almost all the worker actors and accompanists who participated in this performance and competition received awards.

This performance and competition began on April 5th. Over the past half month, worker amateur actors performed more than 190 quyi, music, dance, and other programs, of which 103 were created or adapted by the workers themselves, and 180 were based on real-life themes. These programs included eighty-two different art forms.

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 职工业余曲艺观摩演出会闭幕

1956-04-22
第3版()
专栏：

    职工业余曲艺观摩演出会闭幕
    全国职工业余曲艺观摩演出会二十一日下午在北京闭幕。评奖委员会的代表在闭幕式上宣布了评奖结果：分别得到一、二、三等创作奖的有104个作者，分别得到一、二、三等节目奖的有196个演出单位，分别得到一、二、三等演员奖和伴奏奖的共有480名职工演员和伴奏人员。几乎所有参加这次会演的职工演员和伴奏人员都受到了奖励。
    这次会演是从四月五日开始举行的，半个月以来，职工业余演员们表演了一百九十多个曲艺、音乐、舞蹈等节目，其中有103个是职工自己创作或改编的，有180个以现实生活为题材。在这些节目中包括了八十二种不同的文艺形式。
            （据新华社讯）
