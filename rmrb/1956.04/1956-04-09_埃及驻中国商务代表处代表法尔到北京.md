Title: Egyptian Commercial Representative to China, Farr, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 8th. Mr. Farr, the representative of the Egyptian Commercial Representative Office in China, and his wife arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the eighth.

Those who went to the station to greet them included Lin Haiyun, Assistant Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, Yang Haolu, Director of the Fourth Bureau, Tian Suixiang, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, and Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Meng Dawei, Deputy Representative of the Egyptian Commercial Representative Office in China, and his wife, as well as personnel from foreign embassies in China, also went to greet them.

### 埃及驻中国商务代表处代表法尔到北京

1956-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    埃及驻中国商务代表处代表法尔到北京
    新华社8日讯　埃及驻中国商务代表处代表法尔及其夫人在八日下午乘火车到北京。
    前往车站迎接的，有中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长助理林海云、第四局局长杨浩卢、交际处副处长田绥祥和外交部礼宾司副司长王拓。
    埃及驻中国商务代表处副代表孟大维及其夫人和外国驻华使馆人员也前往迎接。
