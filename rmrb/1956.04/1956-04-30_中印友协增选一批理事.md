Title: China-India Friendship Association Adds New Council Members
Author:
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 3

The Council of the China-India Friendship Association recently added a total of eighteen council members from Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenyang, Xi'an, Wuhan, and Tibet. The new council members are Wu Han, Yan Wenjing, Chen Shouyi, Zhou Erfu, Zhou Bizhen, Li Jiefu, Wang Pi-yi, Wu Bolun, Zhang Fengbo, Xiao Junying, Ke Lin, Hu Yichuan, Zhang Shunhui, Lhalu, Ngagchang Lobsang Rinchen, Demo, Taktser Tsering Rabten Dorje Yougyal, and Chakyeling Wangdan Chenlei Gyal.

The Council of the China-India Friendship Association held a meeting on the 28th, at which President Ding Xilin reported on the recent development of the association's work, and Wu Han reported on his visit to India. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中印友协增选一批理事

1956-04-30
第3版()
专栏：

    中印友协增选一批理事
    中印友好协会理事会在最近增选了北京、上海、广州、沈阳、西安、武汉、西藏各地的理事共十八人。新理事是吴晗、严文井、陈守一、周而复、周碧珍、李劫夫、王丕一、武伯伦、张锋伯、萧隽英、柯麟、胡一川、张舜徽、拉鲁、噶章·洛桑日增、德穆、台吉·德来热不旦·多杰友甲、才觉林·玩丹陈列弓等。
    中印友好协会理事会在二十八日举行会议，会上由会长丁西林报告了最近会务发展的情况，吴晗报告了访印情况。（新华社）
