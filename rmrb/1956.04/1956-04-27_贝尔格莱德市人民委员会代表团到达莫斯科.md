Title: Belgrade City People's Committee Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-04-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 26 (TASS) - The Belgrade City People's Committee delegation, headed by Mi. Minić, Chairman of the Belgrade City People's Committee, arrived in Moscow by plane on April 25 at the invitation of the Executive Committee of the Moscow City Soviet of Working People's Deputies.

### 贝尔格莱德市人民委员会代表团到达莫斯科

1956-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

    贝尔格莱德市人民委员会代表团到达莫斯科
    新华社据塔斯社莫斯科讯  以贝尔格莱德市人民委员会主席米·米尼奇为首的贝尔格莱德市人民委员会代表团，应莫斯科市劳动人民代表苏维埃执行委员会的邀请，在四月二十五日乘飞机到达莫斯科。
