    The 16th Executive Council Meeting of the World Federation of Scientific Workers was held in Beijing on April 1st. The left picture shows a panoramic view of the venue. The right picture shows Professor Powell of the United Kingdom, Vice-President of the World Federation of Scientific Workers and Chairman of the Executive Council, speaking at the meeting (from right to left: Academician Oparin of the Soviet Union, Vice-President of the World Federation of Scientific Workers; Professor Li Siguang of China; Powell; Dr. Bigard, Secretary-General of France).　Photo by our reporter Cang Shi
