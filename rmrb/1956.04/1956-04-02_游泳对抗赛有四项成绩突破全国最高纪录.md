Title: Swimming Competition Sees Four National Records Broken
Author:
Date: 1956-04-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The seven-unit swimming competition began on the 1st in the swimming pool of the Beijing Gymnasium. Four athletes broke four national records today.

The athletes who broke the national records are: Lin Jinzhu, from the Chinese People's Liberation Army, in the men's 100-meter freestyle; Mu Xiangxiong, from Tianjin, in the men's 100-meter breaststroke; Wang Qiangli, from Beijing, in the men's 200-meter butterfly; and Dai Lihua, from Shanghai, in the women's 200-meter breaststroke.

In the men's 800-meter freestyle relay final held today, the Chinese People's Liberation Army relay team took first place. The first place winner of the women's 100-meter freestyle final was Fu Cuimei, an athlete from Fujian.

Today's swimming events also included: boys' 100-meter freestyle, boys' 100-meter breaststroke, girls' 100-meter freestyle, and girls' 200-meter breaststroke. Two water polo matches were also held today.

The swimming competition will continue on the 2nd.

### 游泳对抗赛有四项成绩突破全国最高纪录

1956-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

    游泳对抗赛有四项成绩突破全国最高纪录
    据新华社讯　1956年七单位游泳对抗赛一日开始在北京体育馆的游泳馆中举行。有四名运动员今天打破四项全国最高纪录。
    打破全国最高纪录的有：中国人民解放军运动员林锦珠的男子一百公尺自由泳；天津市运动员穆祥雄的男子一百公尺蛙泳；北京市运动员王强立的男子二百公尺蝶泳和上海市运动员戴丽华的女子二百公尺蛙泳。
    在今天举行的男子八百公尺自由泳接力决赛中，中国人民解放军接力队得了第一名。获得女子一百公尺自由泳决赛第一名的是福建省的运动员傅翠美。
    今天举行的游泳比赛项目还有：少年男子一百公尺自由泳，少年男子一百公尺蛙泳，少年女子一百公尺自由泳，少年女子二百公尺蛙泳。今天还举行了两场水球比赛。
    游泳对抗赛将在二日继续举行。
