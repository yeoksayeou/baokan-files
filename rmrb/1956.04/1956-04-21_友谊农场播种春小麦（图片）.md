Title: Spring Wheat Sowing at Friendship Farm (Picture)
Author: Zhang Wenli
Date: 1956-04-21
Page: 2

    Friendship Farm Sows Spring Wheat
    Spring sowing has begun at the state-owned Friendship Farm. This year, more than 15,000 hectares of wheat will be sown on newly cultivated land, with the goal of achieving a yield of more than 3,000 jin per hectare. The picture shows the seed drill of the Fourth Production Team of the Second Branch Farm sowing spring wheat.
                                Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zhang Wenli Photo

### 友谊农场播种春小麦（图片）
张文礼
1956-04-21
第2版()
专栏：

    友谊农场播种春小麦
    国营友谊农场的春播开始了，今年将要在新开垦的土地上播种小麦一万五千多公顷，每公顷的产量要争取达到三千多斤。图为第二分场第四生产队的播种机在播种春小麦。
                                新华社记者  张文礼摄
