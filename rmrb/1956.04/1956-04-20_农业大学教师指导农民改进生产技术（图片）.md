Title: Agricultural University Teachers Guide Farmers in Improving Production Techniques (Photo)
Author: Shi Panqi
Date: 1956-04-20
Page: 2

    Agricultural University Teachers Guide Farmers in Improving Production Techniques
    More than seventy teachers from Beijing Agricultural University have established long-term connections with agricultural production cooperatives in the suburbs of Beijing to guide farmers in adopting advanced scientific methods to improve production techniques. The picture shows Associate Professor Yin Xinyun (right) of the Plant Protection Department, guiding Rong Shuzhen (left), a technician from the Heping Agricultural Cooperative, in using "antibiotic cake soil" to prevent cotton seedling diseases. Xinhua News Agency reporter Shi Panqi.

### 农业大学教师指导农民改进生产技术（图片）
时盘棋
1956-04-20
第2版()
专栏：

    农业大学教师指导农民改进生产技术
    北京农业大学有七十多位教师同京郊的农业生产合作社建立了长期联系，指导农民采用先进的科学方法改进生产技术。图为植物保护系尹莘耘副教授（右），在指导和平农业合作社技术４荣淑珍（左）使用“抗生菌饼土”防止棉花苗期病害。　新华社记者　时盘棋摄
