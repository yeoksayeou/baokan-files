Title: Spring Harrow (Picture)
Author: Gao Liang
Date: 1956-04-28
Page: 2

    Spring Harrow
    The first agricultural machinery station established in Jin County, Liaoning Province, has cultivated 52,795 mu of land for thirteen agricultural production cooperatives and one farm in the area since its establishment on April 5th. This is a tractor from the agricultural machinery station carrying out spring harrowing. Our reporter Gao Liang took this photo.

### 春耙（图片）
高粮
1956-04-28
第2版()
专栏：

    春耙
    辽宁省金县新建的第一座农业机器站，从四月五日成立以来，已经为这个地区的十三个农业生产合作社和一个农场耕作52795亩土地。这是农业机器站的拖拉机正在进行春耙。  本报记者  高粮摄
