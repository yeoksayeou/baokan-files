Title: Songhua River Thaws and Opens to Navigation
Author:
Date: 1956-04-24
Page: 2

    The Songhua River, which had been frozen for more than five months, thawed and opened to navigation on the 22nd. The first batch of four passenger and cargo ships and two tugboats sailed upstream.
        (Our correspondent in Harbin)

### 松花江解冻开航

1956-04-24
第2版()
专栏：

    松花江解冻开航
    冰封了五个多月的松花江，已于二十二日解冻开航，首批客货轮四艘、拖轮二艘往上游驶去。
        （本报驻哈尔滨记者）
