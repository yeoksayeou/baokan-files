Title: Irène Joliot-Curie, Qi Baishi and Others Awarded International Peace Prize

Author:

Date: 1956-04-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th, Vienna. According to TASS, Vienna, April 28th: The Secretariat of the World Peace Council announced on the 27th that it would award the 1955 International Peace Prize to a group of outstanding peace fighters.

The honorary Peace Prize was posthumously awarded to Irène Joliot-Curie, a member of the World Peace Council who recently passed away, a famous French scientist and an enthusiastic fighter for peace and progress. The Peace Prize was also awarded to Nikos Kazantzakis, a famous progressive writer from Greece, Qi Baishi, a famous Chinese painter, and Reverend William Howard Melish of the United States.

### 约里奥—居里夫人和齐白石等  荣获国际和平奖金

1956-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

    约里奥—居里夫人和齐白石等
    荣获国际和平奖金
    新华社28日讯  据塔斯社维也纳二十八日讯：世界和平理事会书记处在二十七日宣布把1955年国际和平奖金授给一批杰出的和平战士。
    荣誉和平奖金被追授给最近逝世的世界和平理事会理事、法国著名的科学家和争取和平和进步的热情战士伊伦·约里奥—居里。被授给和平奖金的还有希腊的著名进步作家尼科斯·卡桑特查基斯、中国的名画家齐白石、美国的威廉·霍华德·麦利希牧师。
