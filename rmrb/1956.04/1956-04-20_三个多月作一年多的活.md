Title: Doing More Than a Year's Work in Three Months
Author:
Date: 1956-04-20
Page: 2

Having worked for three months and ten days, technician Zhang Wenwei completed a workload equivalent to one year and three months.
Zhang Wenwei is a fitter at the state-owned Guiyang Mining Machinery Factory. Since January, he has put forward a total of twelve rationalization proposals and continuously improved tools twelve times. He has already begun working on the second quarter of 1957. Under his leadership, other workers in the group also improved eight tools. The entire group completed seven months and ten days of work in the first quarter.
(Our correspondent in Guiyang)

### 三个多月作一年多的活

1956-04-20
第2版()
专栏：

    三个多月作一年多的活
    技工张文蔚在三个月零十天的时间内，完成了一年零三个月的工作量。
    张文蔚是国营贵阳矿山机器厂的钳工。从一月份以来，他共提出了十二件合理化建议，连续改进工具十二次。他现在已经开始做1957年第二季度的工作。在他的带动下，组里的其他工人也改进了八件工具。全组第一季度完成了七个月零十天的工作。
              （本报驻贵阳记者）
