Title: Cambodian King Appoints Khim Tit as Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1956-04-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 3rd -- Phnom Penh Dispatch: Cambodian King Suramarit appointed Khim Tit, the former Deputy Prime Minister of the Sihanouk cabinet, as the new government's Prime Minister on April 2nd.

According to Agence France-Presse, only one or two members of the former cabinet have changed. Cambodian Ambassador to Washington, Long Kim Nhy, has been appointed Minister of Foreign Affairs.

### 柬埔寨国王任命钦·迪为首相

1956-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨国王任命钦·迪为首相
    新华社河内3日电　金边消息：柬埔寨国王苏拉玛里特在四月二日任命前西哈努克内阁的副首相钦·迪为新政府的首相。
    据法新社报道，前内阁中的成员只有一两个人变动。柬埔寨驻华盛顿大使龙金尼出任外交大臣。
