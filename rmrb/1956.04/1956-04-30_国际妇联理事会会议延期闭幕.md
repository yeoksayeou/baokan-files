Title: International Federation of Women's Council Meeting Postponed Closing

Author:

Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 29th: According to the announcement of the International Democratic Women's Federation Council, the council meeting has been postponed to close on April 30th because the subcommittees will hold meetings successively on April 29th.

### 国际妇联理事会会议延期闭幕

1956-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　国际妇联理事会会议延期闭幕
    新华社29日讯  据国际民主妇女联合会理事会公布，由于小组委员会要在四月二十九日陆续举行会议，理事会会议延期至四月三十日闭幕。
