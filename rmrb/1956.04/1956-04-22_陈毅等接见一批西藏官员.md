Title: Chen Yi and Others Receive a Group of Tibetan Officials
Author:
Date: 1956-04-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, April 21st: Chen Yi, head of the Central Delegation, and the deputy heads received the monastic and secular officials of the Tibetan local government, the Panchen Kanpo Lija Committee, and the Qamdo Regional People's Liberation Committee, who were of the fourth rank or higher.
　　Delegation head Chen Yi and deputy head Zhang Jingwu expressed their regards on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai, and presented them with commemorative badges.

### 陈毅等接见一批西藏官员

1956-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅等接见一批西藏官员
    新华社拉萨21日电  中央代表团团长陈毅和各副团长今天下午在拉萨接见了西藏地方政府、班禅堪布会议厅委员会和昌都地区人民解放委员会四品以上的僧俗官员。
    代表团团长陈毅、副团长张经武代表中共中央和毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理向他们表示慰问，并且向他们赠送了纪念章。
