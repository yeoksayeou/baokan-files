Title: The Soon-to-Be-Completed Dujiatai Flood Diversion Project (Picture)
Author: Liu Xinning
Date: 1956-04-26
Page: 2

The Dujiatai Flood Diversion Project on the lower reaches of the Han River will be basically completed by the end of April. This project includes a modern large flood diversion gate and an artificial flood diversion channel connecting the flood diversion gate and the flood diversion area. After the completion of the project, it can eliminate the frequent flood disasters in the lower reaches of the Han River, ensure the safety of the lives and property of the people in the vast plains on both sides, and reduce the threat of extraordinarily large floods.

This is the flood diversion gate that is about to be completed, with a gate length of more than 410 meters, a height of 14 meters, and 30 openings. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Xinning photo

### 即将完工的杜家台分洪工程（图片）
刘馨宁
1956-04-26
第2版()
专栏：

    即将完工的杜家台分洪工程
    汉江下游杜家台分洪工程，在四月底就要基本完工了。这个工程包括一座现代化的大分洪闸、一条连接分洪闸和分洪区的人工分洪道。工程建成后，可以解除汉水下游经常性的洪水灾害，保障两岸广大平原人民生命财产的安全，并且可以减轻特大洪水的威胁。
    这是即将完工的分洪闸，闸长四百一十多公尺，高十四公尺，三十孔。            新华社记者  刘馨宁摄
