Title: Ambassador U Ohn Maung Holds Reception in Kunming to Celebrate Sino-Burmese Air Service

Author: 
Date: 1956-04-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, April 20th. To celebrate the official opening of the Sino-Burmese regular air transport service on the 11th, the Ambassador of the Union of Burma to China, U Ohn Maung, held a grand reception in Kunming tonight.

Among those invited to the reception were Ye Ji Zhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, Guo Yingqiu, Governor of Yunnan Province, the Vice-Governors, and people from all walks of life in Yunnan Province and Kunming City. U Ba Maw, Consul General of Burma in Kunming, also attended the reception. Gao Hongling, Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming, was also invited to attend the reception.

Ambassador U Ohn Maung and Guo Yingqiu delivered speeches at the reception.

### 吴拉茂大使在昆明举行招待会庆祝中缅通航

1956-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    吴拉茂大使在昆明举行招待会庆祝中缅通航
    据新华社昆明20日电  缅甸联邦驻中国大使吴拉茂为庆祝在十一日正式开航的中缅两国间定期航空运输，今晚在昆明举行盛大招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的有中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮，云南省省长郭影秋，副省长以及云南省和昆明市的各界人士。出席招待会的还有缅甸驻昆明总领事吴巴貌。越南民主共和国驻昆明领事高鸿岭也应邀出席了招待会。
    吴拉茂大使和郭影秋在招待会上先后致词。
