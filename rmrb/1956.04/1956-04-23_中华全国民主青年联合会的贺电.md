Title: Telegram of Congratulations from the All-China Youth Federation

Author: 
Date: 1956-04-23
Page: 3

Telegram of Congratulations from the All-China Youth Federation
To the Inaugural Meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region:

    On the occasion of the opening of the inaugural meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, we extend our warm congratulations!
    The establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region is a great joyous event for the people of all ethnic groups and the youth of all ethnic groups in Tibet. We believe that the hardworking and brave people of all ethnic groups in Tibet will unite even more closely in the future and push forward the construction of Tibet to a new stage.

                            All-China Youth Federation
                                        April 22, 1956

### 中华全国民主青年联合会的贺电

1956-04-23
第3版()
专栏：

    中华全国民主青年联合会的贺电西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会：
    值此西藏自治区筹备委员会成立大会开幕的时候，我们谨致热烈的祝贺！
    西藏自治区筹备委员会成立，是西藏各族人民和各族青年的一件大喜事。我们相信，勤劳、勇敢的西藏各族人民，今后将会更加亲密地团结起来，把西藏地方的建设事业，推向一个新的阶段。
                            中华全国民主青年联合会
                                        1956年4月22日
