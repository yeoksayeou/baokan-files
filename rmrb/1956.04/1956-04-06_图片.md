Title: 200,000 Young People in Zhengzhou, Henan Province, Plant Over a Million Trees on "April 1st" Afforestation Day

Author:
Date: 1956-04-06
Page: 1

On "April 1st" Afforestation Day, 200,000 young people in Zhengzhou City, Henan Province, planted more than one million trees. This is a grand scene of women creating a "Women's Forest" on both banks of the Jin Yong River in Zhengzhou City.
