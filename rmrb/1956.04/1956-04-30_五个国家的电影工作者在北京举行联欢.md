Title: Film Workers from Five Countries Hold Joint Party in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 3

Film workers from five countries held a joyous and grand get-together in Beijing on the evening of the 29th.

This party was held by the Preparatory Committee of the China Film Workers Association to welcome the Polish film workers delegation headed by Radkiewicz, the Burmese film delegation headed by Wu Yang Na, the Czechoslovak film workers delegation headed by Ili Malik, and the famous Soviet director Chrisky, as well as other film workers visiting China.

Amidst the music of "The People of the World are of One Heart," and the cheers of more than 1,000 Chinese film workers and artists, these film envoys from foreign countries arrived at the venue. When the beautiful and lively dance music started, Wang Lanxi, Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, invited Radkiewicz, the head of the Polish film workers delegation, to dance with him. Subsequently, Chinese and foreign film artists also danced happily.

The famous Burmese actress Wu Min Min Qing and the famous Burmese actor Wu Miao Qi sang beautiful Burmese folk songs and classical love songs at the meeting.

During the party, Ma Shizeng and Hong Xiannv, famous Cantonese opera actors who came to Beijing from Guangzhou, and Yuan Yang'an, Kuang Shanxiao, Yuan Meiyun, etc., who came to Beijing from Hong Kong for sightseeing, arrived at the venue. At this time, the audience burst into thunderous applause to express their warm welcome.

At the meeting, Beijing artists performed more than 20 song and dance programs.

The party continued until after 11 o'clock in the evening. Wang Yang, director of the Beijing Film Studio, delivered a speech on behalf of the Preparatory Committee of the China Film Workers Association, saying: This is a friendly and unforgettable happy night.
(Xinhua News Agency)

### 五个国家的电影工作者在北京举行联欢

1956-04-30
第3版()
专栏：

    五个国家的电影工作者在北京举行联欢
    五个国家的电影工作者二十九日晚在北京举行了一个欢乐的盛大的联欢晚会。
    这个晚会是中国电影工作者联谊会筹备委员会为欢迎以拉德凯维超娃为首的波兰电影工作者代表团、以吴仰拿为首的缅甸电影代表团、以伊里·马里克为首的捷克斯洛伐克电影工作者代表团和苏联著名导演克里斯基以及其他电影工作者来中国访问而举行的。
    在“全世界人民心一条”的乐曲中，在一千多名中国电影工作者和艺术家们夹道欢呼声中，这些来自国外的电影使者们来到了会场。当优美轻快的舞曲奏起来的时候，文化部电影事业管理局局长王阑西请波兰电影工作者代表团团长拉德凯维超娃同他跳舞。随后，中外电影艺术家们也都愉快地翩翩起舞。
    缅甸著名女电影演员吴敏敏庆和缅甸著名演员吴谬七先后在会上唱了优美的缅甸民歌和古典情歌。
    在联欢进行中，从广州来京的著名粤剧演员马师曾、红线女，从香港来京观光的袁仰安、邝山笑、袁美云等，来到了会场。这时全场响起了暴风雨般的掌声，向他们表示热烈欢迎。
    在会上，北京艺术工作者们先后演出了二十多个歌舞节目。
    晚会一直进行到深夜十一点多钟。北京电影制片厂厂长汪洋在会上代表中国电影工作者联谊会筹备委员会致词说：这是一个友谊的令人难忘的快乐之夜。
                （新华社）
