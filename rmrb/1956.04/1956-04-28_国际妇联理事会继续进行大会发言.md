Title: International Federation of Women's Councils Continues General Assembly Speeches
Author:
Date: 1956-04-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: The International Democratic Women's Federation Council meeting continued at 9:30 this morning. The invited representative, former President of the All India Women's Conference and current Vice Chairman of the Bombay Social Education Committee, Mrs. Sayani, and Ms. Lucienne Mazéland, member of the Executive Bureau of the French Women's Union, served as executive chairpersons of the meeting successively.

Today, those who spoke at the meeting included Mrs. Madelano, Secretary General of the Democratic Women's Union of Mexico; Nguyen Thi Thap, member of the Standing Committee of the Vietnam Women's Union; Deng Yingchao, Vice President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; the invited representative, Ms. Wilmott, Secretary of the Gold Coast Women's Federation; Mrs. Hajela, Secretary of the All India Women's Federation, and representatives from France, Korea, and Poland.

The Indian women's representatives presented gifts to the conference today.

The conference read out a congratulatory telegram from the Vice President of the International Democratic Women's Federation, the famous social activist Dr. Andre Andreen. Mrs. Roimann of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, who was invited to attend the meeting, gave a congratulatory speech to the conference. (Attached picture)

International Democratic Women's Federation President, Mrs. Gordon, delivered a speech at the banquet. Photographer: Cang Shi, Wang Zhiyuan (of this newspaper).

### 国际妇联理事会继续进行大会发言

1956-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    国际妇联理事会继续进行大会发言
    据新华社27日讯  国际民主妇女联合会理事会会议今天上午九时半继续举行。特邀代表、曾任全印妇女大会主席，现任孟买社会教育委员会副主席的赛亚尼夫人和法兰西妇女联盟执行局委员莉西恩·马泽兰先后担任会议的执行主席。
    今天在会上发言的有墨西哥民主妇女联盟总书记麦德兰诺夫人，越南妇女联合会常务委员会委员阮氏十，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超，特邀代表、黄金海岸妇女联合会书记威尔莫特，印度全国妇女联合会书记哈杰拉夫人和法国、朝鲜、波兰的代表。
    印度妇女代表今天向大会献了礼物。
    大会宣读了国际民主妇联副主席、著名的社会活动家安德烈·安德琳博士的贺电。被邀参加会议的国际民主法律工作者协会的若伊曼夫人向大会致了贺词。（附图片）
