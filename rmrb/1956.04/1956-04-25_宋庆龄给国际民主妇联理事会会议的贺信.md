Date: 1956-04-25
Page: 3

    Soong Ching Ling's Congratulatory Letter to the International Democratic Women's Federation Council Meeting
    I extend the warmest welcome to the representatives from more than seventy countries who have come to Beijing to attend the Council Meeting of the International Democratic Women's Federation. Among you are old friends whom we have met before, and people we are welcoming for the first time. Welcome to all of you! This Council meeting is being held at a time when the sentiment and activities of demanding peace around the world have reached a climax. This is also a time when the unprecedented movement for national liberation and revival continues to surge among the people of Asia and Africa. Proceeding simultaneously with these two great movements is the unremitting struggle for the rights of women and children, and the work of the International Democratic Women's Federation is a very brilliant symbol of this struggle.
    Now the conditions are ripe for achieving new victories in these things we cherish most. Please believe that this Council meeting will further prove this fact. I wish your discussions the greatest success. I wish each of you good health.
    So

### 宋庆龄给国际民主妇联理事会会议的贺信

1956-04-25
第3版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄给国际民主妇联理事会会议的贺信
    谨向从七十多个国家来到北京参加国际民主妇女联合会理事会会议的代表们致最热烈的欢迎。你们中间有我们以前见过的老朋友，也有我们第一次欢迎的人们。欢迎你们大家！这次理事会会议正好在世界各地要求和平的情绪和活动达到高潮的时候举行。这个时候也是前所未有的争取民族自由和复兴的运动在亚非人民中继续汹涌澎湃的时候。和这两个伟大的运动同时并进的还有争取妇女和儿童权利的不懈的斗争，国际民主妇联的工作就是这个斗争的非常光辉的象征。
    现在已经具备了在我们所最珍视的这些事情中取得新的胜利的条件。请相信这次理事会会议将进一步证明这是一个事实。谨祝你们的讨论取得最大的成功。谨祝你们每一个人身体健康。
    中华人民共和国全国人民代
    表大会常务委员会副委员长
    中华全国民主妇女联合会名
    誉主席宋庆龄
              1956年4月24日
