Title: Moroccan Communist Party General Secretary Cables Moroccan Prime Minister, Demanding Punishment for Abdallah's Assassins
Author:
Date: 1956-04-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 3rd (TASS dispatch) “L'Humanité” reports that the General Secretary of the Moroccan Communist Party, Ali Yata, sent a telegram to the Moroccan Prime Minister, Bekkai. In the telegram, he emphasized that the heinous act of assassinating Abd-el-Krim Ben Abdallah, a prominent activist of the Moroccan Communist Party, is clearly part of an overall plan, "the purpose of this plan is to intimidate staunch patriots, strengthen the state of chaos, and hinder the final victory of Morocco's national independence." Ali Yata demanded the arrest and punishment of the participants in this crime.
The Moroccan student organizations in Paris also sent a telegram to the Sultan of Morocco, demanding the trial and severe punishment of the murderers.

### 摩共总书记致电摩洛哥首相  要求惩办暗杀阿勃达拉的凶手

1956-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    摩共总书记致电摩洛哥首相
    要求惩办暗杀阿勃达拉的凶手
    新华社据塔斯社巴黎3日讯　“人道报”报道，摩洛哥共产党总书记阿里·雅塔向摩洛哥首相贝凯发出一封电报。他在电报中强调指出，暗杀摩洛哥共产党著名活动家阿勃德·埃尔·克里姆·本·阿勃达拉的凶恶行为，很显然是一个总计划里的一部分，“这个计划的目的在于恫吓坚强的爱国者，加强混乱状况，阻碍摩洛哥取得民族独立的最后胜利”。阿里·雅塔要求逮捕和惩办这一罪行的参加者。
    巴黎的摩洛哥学生组织也向摩洛哥苏丹发出了电报，要求审讯和严惩凶手。
