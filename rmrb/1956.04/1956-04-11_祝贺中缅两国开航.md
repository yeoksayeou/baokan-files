Title: Congratulations on the Inauguration of Air Routes Between China and Burma
Author:
Date: 1956-04-11
Page: 1

Premier Zhou Enlai Sends a Telegram to Prime Minister U Nu
To His Excellency U Nu, Prime Minister of the Union of Burma:

    On the occasion of the inauguration of the air route between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Burma, I extend warm congratulations to Your Excellency, the Burmese government, and the Burmese people on behalf of the Chinese government and people, and in my own name.
    I wish in advance that the economic and cultural exchanges and friendly interactions between China and Burma will be further strengthened with the opening of this air route.

   　Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                     April 9, 1956, Beijing

The Director of the Civil Aviation Administration of China Sends a Telegram to the Director of the Civil Aviation Administration of Burma, etc.
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 10th - Kuang Rennong, Director of the Civil Aviation Administration of China, today sent telegrams to U Kyaw Tun, Director of the Civil Aviation Bureau of the Union of Burma, U Chit, Chairman of the Management Committee of the Union of Burma Airways, and U Toe, Manager, congratulating them on the inauguration of air routes between China and Burma. The telegram stated:

    "On the eve of the inauguration of air routes between China and Burma, please allow me to express my warm congratulations to you on behalf of the employees of Civil Aviation of China.
    "The opening of the air route between China and Burma has greatly shortened the geographical distance between the two countries. We firmly believe that it will play a positive role in enhancing the profound friendship between China and Burma and strengthening the economic and cultural ties between the two countries.
    "I wish the friendly cooperation and development of the civil aviation industries of China and Burma to prosper."

Guo Yingqiu, Governor of Yunnan Province, Holds Celebration Banquet

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 10th - Guo Yingqiu, Governor of Yunnan Province, held a banquet tonight to celebrate the inauguration of the Kunming-Mandalay-Yangon air route between China and Burma.

U Hla Maung, the Burmese Ambassador to China, and his wife, and U Ba Maw, the Burmese Consul General in Kunming, were invited to attend the banquet.

At the banquet, Guo Yingqiu and U Hla Maung delivered speeches successively, warmly congratulating the opening of the China-Burma air route. In his speech, U Hla Maung particularly emphasized the increasingly developing friendly and cooperative relations between China and Burma, and expressed his gratitude for China's assistance to Burma during its economic difficulties.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently stood up to toast the opening of the China-Burma air route, the continuous growth of friendly cooperation between the peoples of China and Burma, and the ever-growing economic exchanges and cultural exchanges between the two countries.

Also present at the banquet today were Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Kuang Rennong, Director of the Civil Aviation Administration of China, and others. Cao Hongling, Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming, was also invited to attend the banquet.

### 祝贺中缅两国开航

1956-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺中缅两国开航
    周恩来总理致吴努总理电缅甸联邦总理吴努阁下：
    当中华人民共和国和缅甸联邦之间的航空线开航的时候，我谨代表中国政府和人民，并且以我个人名义向阁下、缅甸政府和人民表示热烈的祝贺。　　我预祝中缅两国间的经济、文化交流和友好往来将随着这一航线的开辟而进一步加强。
  　中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                     1956年4月9日北京
      中国民航局局长致电缅甸民航局局长等
    新华社昆明10日电　中国民用航空局局长邝任农今天分别致电缅甸联邦民用航空局局长吴叫呑、缅甸联邦航空公司管理委员会主席吴七和经理吴铎，祝贺中缅开航。电文内称：
    “值此中缅开航前夕，请允许我代表中国民用航空的从业人员向您表示热切的祝贺。　　“中缅航线的开辟，使中缅两国间的地理距离大为缩短，我们深信它对增进中缅两国的深厚友谊和加强两国的经济和文化联系都将会起到良好的作用。
    “祝中缅两国民用航空事业的友好合作发展繁荣。”云南省省长郭影秋举行庆祝宴会
    据新华社昆明10日电　云南省省长郭影秋今晚举行宴会，庆祝中缅两国的昆明—曼德勒—仰光航空线开航。
    缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂和夫人，缅甸驻昆明总领事吴巴貌应邀出席了宴会。
    在宴会上，郭影秋和吴拉茂先后致词，热烈祝贺中缅航空线的开辟。吴拉茂在致词中，特别强调中缅两国友好合作关系日益发展的情况，他并对中国在缅甸经济困难时给予的帮助表示感谢。
    席间，宾主频频起立，互为中缅航空线的开辟，为中缅两国人民的友好合作不断增长，为两国经济往来和文化交流的日益发展而干杯。
    今天出席宴会的，还有中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明、中国民用航空局局长邝任农等。越南民主共和国驻昆明领事高鸿岭也应邀出席了宴会。
