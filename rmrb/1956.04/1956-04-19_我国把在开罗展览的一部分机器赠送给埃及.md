Title: China Donates Part of Machinery from Cairo Exhibition to Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-04-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 18th: The Government of the People's Republic of China has donated part of the machinery exhibited at the Chinese Commodity Exhibition in Cairo to the Egyptian government, to express the friendship of the Chinese people towards the Egyptian people. This machinery includes machine tools, textile machines, knitting machines, electric motors, and agricultural machinery.

    The donation ceremony was held on April 16th. Ye Jizhuang, the representative of the Chinese government and Minister of Foreign Trade, delivered a speech at the donation ceremony. He congratulated the growing development and consolidation of the friendship between the Chinese and Egyptian peoples. The Egyptian Minister of Industry and Commerce, Nusseir, expressed his gratitude to the Government of the People's Republic of China.

### 我国把在开罗展览的一部分机器赠送给埃及

1956-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我国把在开罗展览的一部分机器赠送给埃及
    据新华社开罗18日电　中华人民共和国政府把在开罗举行的中国商品展览会上展出的一部分机器赠送给埃及政府，以表示中国人民对埃及人民的友谊。这些机器包括工作母机、纺织机、针织机、电动机和农业机械等。
    赠送仪式是在四月十六日举行的。中国政府的代表中国对外贸易部部长叶季壮在赠送仪式上致辞。他祝贺中埃两国人民的友谊日益发展和巩固。埃及工商部部长努赛尔对中华人民共和国政府表示感谢。
