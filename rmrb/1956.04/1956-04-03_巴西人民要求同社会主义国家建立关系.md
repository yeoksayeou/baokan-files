Title: Brazilian People Demand Establishment of Relations with Socialist Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-04-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Rio de Janeiro, 2nd -- Brazilian people from all walks of life are launching a movement to demand the establishment of relations with socialist countries and the implementation of an independent foreign policy in order to resist the cruel exploitation of American monopoly capital and strive to improve their economic situation.

    Brazilian people from all walks of life are launching a broad movement demanding the establishment of relations with the Soviet Union, China, and other socialist countries in order to get rid of American monopoly. According to the "Workers' Voice," representatives of all strata of residents are participating in the movement to demand the restoration of diplomatic relations with the Soviet Union -- from the largest suppliers of agricultural products, industrialists, and businessmen to democratic organizations and people's groups. The newspaper also said that at least seven of Brazil's twenty states -- Paraná, São Paulo, Mato Grosso, Rio Grande do Sul, Pernambuco, Paraíba, and Rio de Janeiro -- have legislative assemblies advocating the immediate restoration of relations. In the Senate, the proposal to restore relations is supported by 38 of the 63 senators, including leaders of all parties in the Senate. In the House of Representatives, 80 representatives hold a positive attitude toward this issue.

### 巴西人民要求同社会主义国家建立关系

1956-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    巴西人民要求同社会主义国家建立关系
    新华社2日讯　里约热内卢消息：巴西各阶层人士为了反抗美国垄断资本的残酷压榨，争取改善经济境况，正在展开要求同社会主义阵营国家建立关系、执行独立外交政策的运动。
    巴西各阶层人士为了摆脱美国的垄断，正在展开一个广泛的要求同苏联、中国以及其他社会主义阵营国家建立关系的运动。据“工人呼声报”报道，参加要求同苏联恢复外交关系运动的，有各阶层居民的代表——从最大的农产品供售者、工业家和商人直到民主组织和人民团体。这家报纸还说：在巴西全国二十个州中，至少已经有七个州，即巴拉那、圣保罗、马托格罗索、里约格朗德苏尔、伯南布哥、帕拉伊巴、里约热内卢诸州的立法议会主张立即恢复关系。在参议院总数六十三个参议员中，恢复关系的建议得到三十八个参议员的支持，其中包括参议院一切党派的领袖。而在众议院中，有八十名众议员对这个问题抱肯定态度。
