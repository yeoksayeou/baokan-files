Title: Cambodian Parliament Expresses Confidence in New Cabinet
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 4th: Phnom Penh dispatch: The Cambodian National Assembly on April 3rd passed a vote of confidence in the new cabinet headed by Prime Minister Khim Tit.

Prime Minister Khim Tit, in a conversation with reporters, said that his government will pursue the same neutral policy as its predecessor.

### 柬埔寨议会对新内阁表示信任

1956-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨议会对新内阁表示信任
    新华社河内4日电　金边消息：柬埔寨国民议会四月三日对以钦·迪为首相的新内阁投了信任票。
    钦·迪首相在对记者谈话中说，他的政府将奉行同它的前任所采取的同样的中立政策。
