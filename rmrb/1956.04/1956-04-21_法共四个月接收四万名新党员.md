Title: French Communist Party Receives 40,000 New Members in Four Months
Author:
Date: 1956-04-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 20th, TASS, Paris, April 19th: According to "l'Humanité," the French Communist Party has received 40,000 new members in the past four months. The provinces with the largest number of new members are Bouches-du-Rhône, Seine-et-Oise, and Pas-de-Calais. Nine hundred and thirty-four new party branches have been established in industrial enterprises and in rural areas.

### 法共四个月接收四万名新党员

1956-04-21
第4版()
专栏：

    法共四个月接收四万名新党员
    新华社20日讯  塔斯社巴黎十九日讯：据“人道报”报道，最近四个月内，法国共产党接收了四万名新党员。接收党员人数最多的地方是罗尼河口省、塞纳—瓦斯省和加来海峡省。在工业企业中和农村里成立了九百三十四个新的党支部。
