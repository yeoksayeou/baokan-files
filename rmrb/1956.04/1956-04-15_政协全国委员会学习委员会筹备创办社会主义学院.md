Title: CPPCC National Committee Study Committee Prepares to Establish Socialist Academy
Author:
Date: 1956-04-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 14th: The Study Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held its first meeting on the 14th, discussed and approved the "Implementation Plan for Establishing a Socialist Academy."

    The Socialist Academy will be established in Beijing. It will enroll democrats whose positions or ranks are roughly equivalent to those of senior cadres at the Central Party School in batches. Each term will last one year, with approximately 500 students enrolled each term. The first term will enroll approximately 200 students and is scheduled to begin on September 1st of this year. The curriculum will include Dialectical Materialism and Historical Materialism, Political Economy, and the History of the Chinese Revolution. At the same time, the academy will also organize the study of important current events and policies.

    In order to actively carry out the preparatory work for establishing the Socialist Academy, the meeting decided to form a preparatory committee.

    The meeting also decided to establish two offices under the Study Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, which will be responsible for handling the study matters of democrats and industrialists and businessmen from all walks of life, respectively.

### 政协全国委员会学习委员会筹备创办社会主义学院

1956-04-15
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会学习委员会
    筹备创办社会主义学院
    据新华社14日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会学习委员会在十四日举行了第一次会议，讨论并通过了“创办社会主义学院的实施方案”。
    社会主义学院设在北京。它吸收和中共高级党校学员的职务或者级别大体相当的民主人士分批入学。每期学习一年，每期招收学员五百名左右，第一期招收学员二百名左右，定于今年九月一日开学。学习课程是辩证唯物主义与历史唯物主义、政治经济学、中国革命史。同时，还要组织一些重要的时事、政策的学习。
    为积极进行创办社会主义学院的筹备工作，会议决定组成筹备委员会。
    这次会议还决定，在中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会学习委员会下设立两个办公室，分别负责办理各界民主人士和工商业者的学习事项。
