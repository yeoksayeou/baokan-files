Title: List of Winners of First-Class Performance Awards, Directing Awards, and Acting Awards at the National Drama Festival
Author:
Date: 1956-04-14
Page: 3

The National Drama Festival has recently concluded its awards. A total of fifty plays performed by forty-one drama troupes, directors of forty-one plays, and two hundred and fifty-nine actors received first, second, and third-class performance awards, directing awards, and acting awards, respectively; stage artists of forty-seven plays received first, second, and third-class stage design awards, stage production management awards, and stage technology innovation awards, respectively. The list of winners of first-class performance awards, directing awards, and acting awards is published below:
