Title: The First Seven-Foot Grand Piano is Born
Author:
Date: 1956-04-29
Page: 2

    The First Seven-Foot Grand Piano is Born
    Veteran workers He Sanyuan and Wang Pinlin, from the Shanghai Joint State-Private Owned Mingfeng Piano Shop, successfully produced China's first seven-foot grand piano on the 26th. This piano has a large volume and carries sound far, making it suitable for use in concert performances. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 第一架七尺三角式大钢琴诞生

1956-04-29
第2版()
专栏：

    第一架七尺三角式大钢琴诞生
    上海公私合营鸣凤琴行老工人贺三元和王品林在二十六日制成我国第一架七尺的三角式大钢琴。这架钢琴的音量大，声音传播得远，适合在演奏会上使用。　　　（据新华社讯）
