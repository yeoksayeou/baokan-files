    Beijing Holds Weekly Concerts
    Beijing's first weekly concert was held on the evening of March 31st at the Beijing Theater. The audience enjoyed performances by the Central Song and Dance Troupe Orchestra of many famous Chinese and foreign orchestral pieces. These included Xian Xinghai's "Man Jiang Hong Suite" (first movement), the multi-movement orchestral "Chinese Folk Song Suite" depicting the happy life of the people, and the "Suona Concerto" expressing the joyful scenes of the people after liberation. The audience also enjoyed "In the Steppes of Central Asia" by the Russian musician Borodin, and selections from the opera "Carmen," a representative work of the 19th-century French composer Bizet. Li Xuequan, the first-prize winner of the solo competition at the Fourth World Festival of Youth and Students for Peace and Friendship, performed Mozart's D major flute concerto, by the Austrian composer who is one of the world cultural figures being commemorated this year.
    This regular concert is sponsored by the Central Song and Dance Troupe. Such weekly concerts have been tentatively scheduled until the end of June, and the audience will enjoy new musical works each time. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 北京举行星期音乐会

1956-04-02
第3版()
专栏：

    北京举行星期音乐会
    北京的第一次星期音乐会三月三十一日晚上在北京剧场举行。人们在音乐会上欣赏了中央歌舞团管弦乐队演奏的我国和外国许多著名管弦乐曲。其中有冼星海的“满江红组曲”（第一乐章），描述人民幸福生活的多乐章的管弦乐“中国民歌组曲”和表现解放后人民狂欢情景的“唢呐协奏曲”。观众们还欣赏了俄罗斯音乐家鲍罗亭的“在中亚细亚草原上”和法国十九世纪作曲家比泽的代表作品歌剧“卡门”的选曲。第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节独奏比赛一等奖获得者李学全，演奏了今年纪念的世界文化名人之一的奥地利作曲家莫扎特的D调长笛协奏曲。
    这个定期性的音乐会是由中央歌舞团主办的。这样的星期音乐会，已经初步安排到六月底，观众在每次将欣赏到新的音乐作品。　（据新华社讯）
