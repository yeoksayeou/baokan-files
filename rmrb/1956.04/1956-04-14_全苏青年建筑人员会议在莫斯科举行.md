Title: All-Union Conference of Young Builders Held in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-04-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, TASS dispatch: The All-Union Conference of Young Builders opened in Moscow on April 10th and closed on April 11th.

    Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Kuchelenko, gave a report at the meeting on the 10th regarding the tasks of young builders in the Sixth Five-Year Plan. He said that there are more than two million young workers engaged in construction in the Soviet Union. In many large construction projects, young workers account for 60% to 70% of the total number of workers. More than 100,000 young workers have participated in the construction work in the reclamation areas. During the Sixth Five-Year Plan, the scale of capital construction in the eastern regions of the Soviet Union will be greatly expanded. Kuchelenko devoted a large part of his report to issues such as the industrialization of construction, improving the labor productivity of construction workers, promoting advanced working methods, and strengthening the allocation of skilled construction cadres.
    Leaders of the Communist Party and the Government of the Soviet Union attended the meeting on the 11th. First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, delivered a lengthy speech at the meeting. He put forward significant tasks to the Soviet Communist Youth League and all Soviet youth, and highly praised the work of Soviet youth. His speech was warmly welcomed by those present.
    The meeting unanimously adopted a letter to all young builders in the Soviet Union. The letter called on them to actively launch socialist emulation, widely adopt advanced technologies in the construction industry, improve construction quality, reduce construction costs, and strive to successfully complete the Sixth Five-Year Plan.

### 全苏青年建筑人员会议在莫斯科举行

1956-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    全苏青年建筑人员会议在莫斯科举行
    新华社据塔斯社莫斯科讯：全苏青年建筑人员会议于四月十日在莫斯科开幕，十一日闭幕。
    苏联部长会议副主席库切连科在十日会议上就青年建筑人员在第六个五年计划中的任务的问题作了报告。他说，在苏联，从事建筑工作的共有二百多万青年工人。在许多大建筑工程中，青年工人占工人总数的60％到70％。十多万青年工人参加了垦荒地区的建设工作。在第六个五年计划期间，苏联东部地区的基本建设规模将要大大扩大。库切连科在报告中以很大一部分谈到了关于建筑工业化、提高建筑工人的劳动生产率、推广先进工作法和加强建筑工程的熟练干部的配备等问题。
    苏联共产党和政府的领导人出席了十一日的会议。苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫在会上发表了长篇演说。他向苏联共青团和全体苏联青年提出了重大的任务，并且对苏联青年的工作给予了很高的评价。他的演说受到了到会者的热烈欢迎。
    会议最后一致通过了告苏联全体青年建筑人员书。书中号召他们积极展开社会主义竞赛，在建筑业中广泛地采用先进技术，提高建筑质量，降低建筑成本，为争取胜利完成第六个五年计划而奋斗。
