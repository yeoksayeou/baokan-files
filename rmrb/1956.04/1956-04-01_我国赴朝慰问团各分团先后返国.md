Title: Our Country's Delegation to Korea Returns Home in Successive Groups
Author:
Date: 1956-04-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 31st: The various sub-delegations of the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea have concluded their慰问 (consolation) activities and returned home in successive groups. The fifth sub-delegation of the慰问团 (consolation delegation), which was the last to leave Korea, arrived in Andong on the evening of the 30th.

When the various sub-delegations of the慰问团 (consolation delegation) left the stations of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army they were慰问 (consoling), all units held farewell meetings.

The Chinese People's Delegation to Korea received a large number of letters and gifts from the Korean people, the Korean People's Army, and the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. The fifth sub-delegation of the慰问团 (consolation delegation) alone received more than 3,200 letters written by officers and soldiers of the Volunteer Army, "addressed to relatives in the motherland." In these letters, the officers and soldiers of the Volunteer Army reported to the people of the motherland on the achievements they had made in various fields since the Korean armistice, and expressed their determination to defend peace and support the socialist construction of the motherland.

Various artistic troupes that went to Korea to perform慰问 (consolation) performances continue to tour and perform in the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army.

### 我国赴朝慰问团各分团先后返国

1956-04-01
第4版()
专栏：

    我国赴朝慰问团各分团先后返国
    据新华社平壤31日电　中国人民赴朝慰问团各分团已结束慰问活动，先后返国。最后离开朝鲜的慰问团第五分团，已经在三十日晚到达安东。
    慰问团各分团离开他们所慰问的中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军各部队驻地的时候，各部队都举行了送别大会。
    中国人民赴朝慰问团收到了朝鲜人民、朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军官兵大批信件和礼物。单是慰问团第五分团，就收到了三千二百多封志愿军官兵写的“寄给祖国亲人”的信件。在这些信上，志愿军官兵向祖国人民汇报了他们在朝鲜停战以来各方面所取得的成绩，并且表示了他们对保卫和平、支援祖国社会主义建设的决心。
    到朝鲜进行慰问演出的各个艺术团体，继续在朝鲜人民军、中国人民志愿军各部队巡回演出。
