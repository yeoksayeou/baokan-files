Title: Over 3,000 Key Industrial Enterprises Nationwide Exceed First Quarter Gross Output Value Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-04-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: According to statistics from the National Bureau of Statistics on 3,501 key industrial enterprises under state-owned, locally state-owned, and joint state-private ownership, enterprises of all economic types and all industrial ministries completed or overfulfilled their gross output value plans and most product plans in the first quarter of this year, with significant growth compared to the same period last year. In terms of gross output value, these enterprises collectively completed 105.3% of the plan, a 24.7% increase compared to the same period last year. Among them, state-owned enterprises completed 105.2% of the plan, a 28% increase compared to the same period last year; locally state-owned enterprises completed 105.6% of the plan, a 22.1% increase compared to the same period last year; centrally administered joint state-private enterprises completed 103% of the plan, a 19.8% increase compared to the same period last year; and locally administered joint state-private enterprises completed 107% of the plan, an 18.1% increase compared to the same period last year.

The seven industrial ministries collectively completed 104.1% of the gross output value plan, a 29% increase compared to the same period last year. Among them, the Ministry of Electric Power Industry completed 102.3% of the plan, an 11.9% increase compared to the same period last year; the Ministry of Coal Industry completed 103.2% of the plan, a 25.6% increase compared to the same period last year; the Ministry of Petroleum Industry completed 104.5% of the plan, a 55.2% increase compared to the same period last year; the Ministry of Heavy Industry completed 100.2% of the plan, a 26.1% increase compared to the same period last year; the First Ministry of Machine Building completed 115.5% of the plan, a 77.7% increase compared to the same period last year; the Ministry of Textile Industry completed 102.3% of the plan, a 16.5% increase compared to the same period last year; and the Ministry of Light Industry completed 105.1% of the plan, a 23.7% increase compared to the same period last year.

Among the twenty-nine important industrial products of key enterprises nationwide, twenty-one products, including electricity, raw coal, gasoline, pig iron, copper, nitric acid, caustic soda, soda ash, ammonium nitrate, cement, electric motors, metal-cutting machine tools, double-wheeled double-shared plows, automobile tires, rubber shoes, paper, flour, cigarettes, cotton yarn, cotton cloth, and麻袋, completed or overfulfilled their plans; crude oil, steel, rolled steel, sulfuric acid, ammonium sulfate, generators, edible vegetable oil, and sugar did not complete their plans. All of these products showed varying degrees of growth compared to the same period last year. Among them, generators, electric motors, and metal-cutting machine tools of the First Ministry of Machine Building increased by about 100% compared to the same period last year, and double-wheeled double-shared plows increased by more than five and a half times.

In the first quarter of this year, socialist emulation campaigns were widely launched in various industrial sectors, greatly improving production efficiency. According to statistics from six industrial ministries (excluding the Ministry of Textile Industry), the total labor productivity of workers in the first quarter increased by 30.7% compared to the same period last year.

### 全国三千多个重点工业企业  超额完成第一季度总产值计划

1956-04-15
第1版()
专栏：

    全国三千多个重点工业企业
    超额完成第一季度总产值计划
    据新华社讯  根据国家统计局对国营、地方国营及公私合营3，501个重点工业企业的统计，今年第一季度各种经济类型企业和各工业部都完成或超额完成了总产值计划和大部分产品计划，并且比去年同期有很大的增长。在总产值方面，这些企业合计完成计划105.3％，比去年同期增长了24.7％。其中国营企业完成计划105.2％，比去年同期增长28％；地方国营企业完成计划105.6％，比去年同期增长22.1％；中央公私合营企业完成计划103％，比去年同期增长19.8％；地方公私合营企业完成计划107％，比去年同期增长18.1％。
    七个工业部合计完成总产值计划104.1％，比去年同期增长29％。其中电力工业部完成计划102.3％，比去年同期增长11.9％；煤炭工业部完成计划103.2％，比去年同期增长25.6％；石油工业部完成计划104.5％，比去年同期增长55.2％；重工业部完成计划100.2％，比去年同期增长26.1％；第一机械工业部完成计划115.5％，比去年同期增长77.7％；纺织工业部完成计划102.3％，比去年同期增长16.5％；轻工业部完成计划105.1％，比去年同期增长23.7％。
    全国重点企业二十九种重要工业产品中，有电力、原煤、汽油、生铁、铜、硝酸、烧碱、纯碱、硝铵、水泥、电动机、金属切削机床、双轮双铧犁、汽车外胎、胶鞋、纸、面粉、卷烟、棉纱、棉布、麻袋等二十一种产品完成或超额完成了计划；原油、钢、钢材、硫酸、硫铵、发电机、食用植物油、糖等产品没有完成计划。所有这些产品比去年同期都有不同程度的增长。其中如第一机械工业部的发电机、电动机、金属切削机床比去年同期增长了一倍左右，双轮双铧犁增长了五倍半以上。
    在今年第一季度内，各工业部门普遍开展了社会主义竞赛，大大提高了生产效率。根据六个工业部（缺纺织工业部）的统计，第一季度工人劳动生产率合计比去年同期增长了30.7％。
