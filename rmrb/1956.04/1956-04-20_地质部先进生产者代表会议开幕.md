Title: Geology Ministry Holds Advanced Producers Representative Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: The Geology Ministry's Advanced Producers Representative Meeting opened in Beijing on the 18th.

There were 182 official representatives and 60 specially invited representatives attending this meeting, along with more than 400 people including observers. These representatives have all made contributions to the exploration of mineral resources in our country, in different regions and at different positions.

Li Siguang, the Minister of Geology, delivered the opening speech at the meeting. He said that the main purpose of this meeting is to further mobilize the broad masses of workers and staff, launch a more widespread and in-depth advanced producer movement, and strive to complete the First Five-Year Plan more quickly and comprehensively.

Zhang Weizhen, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, representatives of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and representatives from the geological education field attended the meeting to congratulate the advanced producers of the Geology Ministry.

### 地质部先进生产者代表会议开幕

1956-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    地质部先进生产者代表会议开幕
    据新华社18日讯　地质部先进生产者代表会议十八日在北京开幕。
    出席这次会议的有182名正式代表，六十名特邀代表，连同列席代表在内，共有四百多人。这些代表对于我国矿产资源的勘探工作，都在不同地区和不同岗位上作出了贡献。
    地质部部长李四光在会上致开幕词。他说，这次会议的主要目的，就是进一步动员广大职工，掀起更广泛、更深入的先进生产者运动，争取更加迅速地全面完成第一个五年计划。
    到会向地质部先进生产者祝贺的，有中华全国总工会书记处书记张维桢、青年团中央委员会代表和地质教育界的代表。
