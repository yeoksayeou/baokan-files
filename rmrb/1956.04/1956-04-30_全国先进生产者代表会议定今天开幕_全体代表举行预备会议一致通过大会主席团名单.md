Title: National Advanced Producers' Representative Conference Scheduled to Open Today; All Representatives Hold Preparatory Meeting and Unanimously Approve the List of the Presidium of the Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. The National Advanced Producers' Representative Conference is scheduled to open at the Beijing Gymnasium on the afternoon of the 30th.

This afternoon, all the representatives attending held a preparatory meeting. At the preparatory meeting, the representatives listened to Lai Ruoyu, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the National Advanced Producers' Representative Conference and Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, regarding the explanation of the registration of representatives, and unanimously approved the list of the Presidium of the Conference.

As of this evening, a total of six thousand one hundred and fifty-six official and specially invited representatives have registered. These representatives are the most outstanding advanced figures from all industries, regions, and fields across the country.

Two hundred and fifty trade union representatives from thirty-seven countries who have arrived in Beijing will also attend the opening ceremony of the conference on the 30th.

This newspaper reports: The more than six thousand representatives attending the National Advanced Producers' Representative Conference have settled in seventeen residences. To facilitate the representatives, temporary postal and telecommunications, book and periodical service departments have also been set up at the Xiyuan Hotel and other places. The staple and non-staple foods for the representatives during the conference have been fully prepared, and special canteens have been prepared for representatives of various ethnic minorities. In addition to the allocation of some vehicles from various departments in Beijing, more than 40 large sedans have been drawn from various units in Tianjin to support the conference's transportation needs. The drivers have carefully washed and repaired the vehicles to ensure the comfort and safety of the representatives during their ride. The Petroleum Corporation has also set up a special gas station for the conference to ensure smooth traffic. (Image attached)

### 全国先进生产者代表会议定今天开幕  全体代表举行预备会议一致通过大会主席团名单

1956-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    全国先进生产者代表会议定今天开幕
    全体代表举行预备会议一致通过大会主席团名单
    新华社29日讯  全国先进生产者代表会议，定三十日下午在北京体育馆开幕。
    今天下午，到会的全体代表举行了预备会议。在预备会议上，代表们听取了全国先进生产者代表会议筹备委员会副主任委员、中华全国总工会主席赖若愚关于代表报到情况的说明，并且一致通过了大会主席团名单。
    到今天晚上为止，已经报到的正式代表和特邀代表共有六千一百五十六人。这些代表都是全国各产业、各地区和各方面的最优秀的先进人物。
    已经到达北京的三十七个国家的二百五十位工会代表，也将出席三十日的会议开幕式。
    本报讯  出席全国先进生产者代表会议的六千多个代表，已分别在十七处住所中安住下来。为了便利代表，西苑大旅社等处还设立临时的邮电、书刊等服务部。代表们开会期间的主副食品已准备齐全，并为各少数民族的代表准备了专门食堂。会议期间所需用的车辆，除北京各部门调拨一部分外，天津各单位又抽出四十多辆大轿车来支援。司机人员对车辆进行了认真的洗刷、检修，保证代表们乘坐时舒适安全。石油公司并为大会专设了加油站，保证交通畅达。（附图片）
