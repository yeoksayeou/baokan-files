Title: Sino-Mongolian Weightlifting Friendship Competition
Author:
Date: 1956-04-27
Page: 4

    Sino-Mongolian Weightlifting Friendship Competition
    Xinhua News Agency, April 26th, Ulaanbaatar, TASS, April 25th: Weightlifters from Mongolia and China held a competition in Ulaanbaatar. This is the first international competition held in Mongolia, and it has attracted great interest from the audience.
    During the competition, Chinese weightlifters set eight Chinese national records, some of which approached international records. Mongolian weightlifters also set some Mongolian national records.

### 中蒙举重运动员友谊赛

1956-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

    中蒙举重运动员友谊赛
    新华社26日讯  据塔斯社乌兰巴托二十五日讯：蒙古和中国的举重运动员在乌兰巴托举行比赛。这是在蒙古举行的第一次国际比赛，它引起了观众极大的兴趣。
    在比赛过程中，中国的举重运动员创造了八项中国的全国纪录，其中有些接近国际纪录。蒙古的举重运动员也创造了一些蒙古的全国纪录。
