Title: Ulanhu Leads Chinese Delegation from Beijing to Attend the Coronation Ceremony of King Mahendra of Nepal
Author:
Date: 1956-04-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 25th. The People's Republic of China delegation, led by Ulanhu, Special Envoy of the People's Republic of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, left Beijing by plane this morning for Nepal to attend the coronation ceremony of King Mahendra of Nepal.

The delegation members are: Pan Zili, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Nepal, Dunwang Jianzan Zhaba, representative of the Dalai Lama, Luosang Zhaxi, representative of the Panchen Erdeni, and Bi Shuowang, Commissioner of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China. Ambassador Pan Zili is currently in India and will travel directly to Nepal from India.

Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included: He Long, Vice Premier of the State Council, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, Liu Chun, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, and representatives from the Dalai Lama's office in Beijing and the Panchen Erdeni's office in Beijing.

### 参加尼泊尔国王马亨德拉加冕典礼  乌兰夫率领我国代表团由京启程

1956-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    参加尼泊尔国王马亨德拉加冕典礼
    乌兰夫率领我国代表团由京启程
    新华社25日讯  由中华人民共和国特使、国务院副总理乌兰夫率领的中华人民共和国代表团，今天早上乘飞机离开北京前往尼泊尔，参加尼泊尔国王马亨德拉的加冕典礼。
    代表团团员有：中华人民共和国驻尼泊尔特命全权大使潘自力，达赖喇嘛代表顿旺·坚赞札巴，班禅额尔德尼代表罗桑札西和中华人民共和国外交部亚洲司专员毕朔望。潘自力大使现在在印度，他将从印度直接前往尼泊尔。
    到飞机场送行的有，国务院副总理贺龙，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长陈叔通，国务院秘书长习仲勋，民族事务委员会副主任刘春，外交部部长助理陈家康以及达赖驻京办事处和班禅驻京办事处的代表等。
