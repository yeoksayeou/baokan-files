Title: Protesting American Hydrogen Bomb Tests
Author:
Date: 1956-04-16
Page: 4

    Protesting American Hydrogen Bomb Tests
    All-India Seamen's Union Issues Statement
    According to a Press Trust of India dispatch from Calcutta, the All-India Seamen's Union on April 12th called upon its 80,000 Indian seamen to demand a halt to the upcoming American hydrogen bomb tests in the Pacific. The union's statement said that the hydrogen bomb tests have aroused "anger and unease among seamen."
    The All-India Seamen's Union supports the call of the All-Japan Seamen's Union, demanding protection for personnel on all ships traveling between Australia, the Philippines, India, Burma, and Indonesia from the dangers of American hydrogen bomb tests. The statement said that nearly 15,000 Indian sailors on 175 ships sailing in the South Pacific may be in the area affected by the hydrogen bomb tests.

    Thai Socialist Party Lodges Protest
    The Executive Committee of the Thai Socialist Party, at a recent ad hoc meeting, decided to send telegrams to the United Nations and U.S. President Eisenhower respectively, protesting against U.S. nuclear weapons tests in the Pacific region.
    Socialist Party Secretary Nai Wang Nabothong told reporters on April 9th that the Socialist Party Executive Committee's decision was made after accepting the request of the fourteen youth branches it leads. The sixty representatives of these fourteen youth branches believe that the results of nuclear weapons tests may endanger human life, including threatening the safety of the Thai people. (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 抗议美国试验氢弹

1956-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

    抗议美国试验氢弹
    全印海员工会发表声明
    据印度报业托辣斯加尔各答消息，全印海员工会四月十二日号召它的八万印度海员要求制止美国即将在太平洋举行的氢弹试验，工会发出的声明说，氢弹试验已经引起“海员们的愤怒和不安”。
    全印海员工会支持全日本海员工会的呼吁，要求保护所有来往于澳大利亚、菲律宾、印度、缅甸和印度尼西亚之间的船只上的人员，以免受到美国氢弹试验的危害。声明说，在南太平洋航行的一百七十五艘船只的近一万五千印度船员，都可能处在受氢弹试验影响的地区内。泰国社会主义党提出抗议
    泰国社会主义党执行委员会在最近召开的临时会议上，决定分别打电报给联合国和美国总统艾森豪威尔，抗议美国在太平洋地区试验核子武器。
    社会主义党书记乃旺·纳博他隆在四月九日向报界发表谈话说，社会主义党执行委员会的这个决议，是接受它所领导的十四个青年支部的要求后作出的。这十四个青年支部的六十位代表认为，核子武器试验的结果可能危害人类的生命安全，包括威胁泰国人民的安全。　（据新华社讯）
