Title: Guangdong Vice Governors He Ximing and Qiu Zhe Host Banquet to Celebrate Sino-Vietnamese Air Route Inauguration
Author:
Date: 1956-04-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 23rd: Guangdong Vice Governors He Ximing and Qiu Zhe held a banquet at the Yingbin Hotel tonight to celebrate the inauguration of the Sino-Vietnamese air route from Guangzhou via Nanning to Hanoi. Zhou Liang, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, attended the banquet.

　　At the banquet, He Ximing and Zhou Liang spoke successively, warmly congratulating the inauguration of the Sino-Vietnamese air route. He Ximing said that China and Vietnam will officially open air routes tomorrow, which is a great event worthy of warm congratulations from the people of our two countries. The opening of Sino-Vietnamese air routes is of great significance in promoting economic cooperation and cultural exchanges between the two countries.

　　At the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently stood up and toasted each other to promote the common and increasing prosperity of the economies of the two countries, the increasing development of cultural exchanges, and the further growth of the friendship between the two peoples.

### 广东省副省长贺希明、丘哲设宴庆祝中越通航

1956-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    广东省副省长贺希明、丘哲设宴庆祝中越通航
    据新华社广州23日电  广东省副省长贺希明、丘哲今晚在迎宾馆举行宴会，庆祝由广州经南宁到河内的中越航空线开航。越南民主共和国驻华大使馆临时代办周亮出席了宴会。
    在宴会上，贺希明和周亮先后讲话，热烈祝贺中越通航。贺希明说，中越将在明天正式通航，这是值得我们两国人民热烈祝贺的一件大喜事。中越通航对于促进两国的经济合作和文化交流具有极重大的意义。
    宴会上，宾主频频起立，互相为促进两国经济的共同日益繁荣和文化交流的日益开展以及两国人民的友谊更加增长而干杯。
