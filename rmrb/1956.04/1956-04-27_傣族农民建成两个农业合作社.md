Title: Two Agricultural Cooperatives Established by Dai Farmers

Author: 
Date: 1956-04-27
Page: 2

    Dai farmers in the Xishuangbanna Dai Autonomous Prefecture of Yunnan Province have established agricultural production cooperatives for the first time. In Mancangzai Township, Jinghong, Xishuangbanna, the "Jiangliang" (Brightness) and "Susheng" (Happiness) agricultural production cooperatives were officially established on the 23rd. All sixty-five member households are Dai farmers.
            (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 傣族农民建成两个农业合作社

1956-04-27
第2版()
专栏：

    傣族农民建成两个农业合作社
    云南省西双版纳傣族自治州的傣族农民，第一次建成了农业生产合作社。在版纳景洪的曼藏宰乡，二十三日正式成立“疆亮”（光明）和“苏生”（幸福）两个农业生产合作社，六十五户社员全部是傣族农民。
            （据新华社讯）
