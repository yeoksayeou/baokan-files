Title: "Pravda", "Akahata" and other newspapers publish "On the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship"
Author:
Date: 1956-04-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 7th: "Pravda" published on April 7th the article written by the Editorial Department of China's "People's Daily" entitled "On the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship."
Xinhua News Agency, April 7th, Tokyo dispatch: The Central Organ of the Japanese Communist Party, "Akahata," published in full on April 7th the article "On the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship" written by the Editorial Department of China's "People's Daily."
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, April 7th: London's "Daily Worker" published on the first page of its April 6th issue an abstract of the article "On the Historical Experience of the Proletarian Dictatorship" written by the Editorial Department of China's "People's Daily," under the title "China's View on Stalin."

### 真理报赤旗报等刊登“关于无产阶级专政的历史经验”

1956-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

    真理报赤旗报等刊登“关于无产阶级专政的历史经验”
    新华社莫斯科7日电　“真理报”四月七日刊载了中国“人民日报”编辑部所写的题为“关于无产阶级专政的历史经验”的文章。
    新华社7日讯　东京消息：日本共产党中央机关报“赤旗报”在四月七日全文刊登了中国“人民日报”编辑部所写的“关于无产阶级专政的历史经验”。
    新华社柏林7日电　伦敦“工人日报”四月六日在第一版刊载了中国“人民日报”编辑部写的“关于无产阶级专政的历史经验”一文的摘要，标题是“中国对斯大林的看法”。
