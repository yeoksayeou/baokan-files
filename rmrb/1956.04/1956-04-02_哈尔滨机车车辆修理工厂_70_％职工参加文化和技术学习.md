Author:
Date: 1956-04-02
Page: 3

Harbin Rolling Stock Repair Factory: 70% of Employees Participate in Cultural and Technical Studies
　　About 70% of the employees of the Harbin Rolling Stock Repair Factory have participated in cultural and technical studies. Cultural studies are organized into classes ranging from literacy classes to university, according to the employees' educational levels. In addition, there are various technical studies tailored to the different production roles of the employees. (Our correspondent in Harbin)

### 哈尔滨机车车辆修理工厂70％职工参加文化和技术学习

1956-04-02
第3版()
专栏：

    哈尔滨机车车辆修理工厂70％职工参加文化和技术学习
    哈尔滨机车车辆修理工厂约有70％的职工参加了文化和技术的学习。文化学习是按照职工的文化程度从识字班到大学分别编班。此外，根据职工生产的不同，还有各种技术学习。　（本报驻哈尔滨记者）
