Title: People's Air Force Shoots Down Chiang Kai-shek Aircraft in Fujian
Author:
Date: 1956-04-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, April 14th: At 9:18 AM on the 14th, four Chiang Kai-shek F-84 fighter jets intruded into the coastal areas of Fujian. The Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force immediately dispatched aircraft to pursue them. At 9:20 AM, the People's Air Force intercepted the Chiang Kai-shek aircraft in the airspace above Shacheng area, Fuding County, Fujian Province. Our aircraft, with accurate and fierce artillery fire, immediately shot down one of the Chiang Kai-shek aircraft. The downed Chiang Kai-shek aircraft crashed into the sea east of Shacheng.

### 人民空军在福建击落蒋机一架

1956-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    人民空军在福建击落蒋机一架
    新华社福建前线14日电　十四日九时十八分，蒋军F—84型战斗机四架窜扰福建沿海地区，中国人民解放军空军机群立即起飞追击。九时二十分，人民空军机群在福建省福鼎县沙埕地区上空截击蒋机，我机以准确、猛烈的炮火立刻击落蒋机一架。被击落的蒋机坠毁在沙埕以东的海里。
