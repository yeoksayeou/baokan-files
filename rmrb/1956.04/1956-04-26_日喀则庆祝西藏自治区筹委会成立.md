Title: Shigatse Celebrates the Establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1956-04-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, April 25th: Over 3,000 people from all walks of life in Shigatse City held a meeting on the 23rd to celebrate the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region.

At the celebration meeting, Wang Ting, Deputy Secretary of the Shigatse Branch of the Chinese Communist Party, Zhongyi Qinbo Qekang of the Panchen Kanpo Conference Hall Committee, the Shigatse Dzongpon of the Tibetan local government, as well as representatives from various mass organizations, commercial circles, handicraftsmen, and farmers in Shigatse, all delivered speeches. They unanimously expressed their determination to further strengthen unity and create conditions for the establishment of a unified Tibet Autonomous Region.

The celebration meeting also adopted a congratulatory telegram to the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region.

People from all walks of life in Shigatse City continuously held three grand celebration evening parties on the 23rd, 24th, and 25th.

Celebration meetings were also held in more than a dozen dzongs and xiqia, including Kangba, Jinlong, and Lazhi, in the Himalayan Mountains and on both banks of the Yarlung Tsangpo River.

### 日喀则庆祝西藏自治区筹委会成立

1956-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    日喀则庆祝西藏自治区筹委会成立
    据新华社拉萨25日电  日喀则市各界三千多人在二十三日举行大会，庆祝西藏自治区筹备委员会成立。
    庆祝大会上，中共日喀则分工委副书记王挺、班禅堪布会议厅委员会仲译钦波却康、西藏地方政府日喀则宗本以及日喀则各群众团体、商业界、手工业者和农民代表都讲了话。他们一致表示要进一步加强团结，为成立统一的西藏自治区创造条件。
    庆祝大会还通过了给西藏自治区筹备委员会的贺电。
    日喀则市各界人民在二十三、二十四、二十五日三天内连续举行了三个盛大的庆祝晚会。
    在喜马拉雅山区和雅鲁藏布江两岸的康巴、金龙、拉孜等十几个宗和溪卡，也都举行了庆祝会。
