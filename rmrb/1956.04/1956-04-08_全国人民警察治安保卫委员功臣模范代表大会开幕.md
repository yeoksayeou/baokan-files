Title: National People's Police and Public Security Committee Heroes and Models Representative Conference Opens
Author:
Date: 1956-04-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The National People's Police and Public Security Committee Heroes and Models Representative Conference opened in Beijing on the 6th.

Among the 906 representatives attending this conference, there were 532 hero and model representatives of the People's Police and 374 hero and model representatives of the Public Security Committee, including a 71-year-old man and a 20-year-old girl. The representatives consisted of seventeen ethnic groups.

Wang Zhao, Deputy Minister of Public Security, delivered the opening speech. He encouraged all heroes and models to connect with the masses, raise awareness, strengthen learning, and strive to eliminate all counter-revolutionaries and criminals, and safeguard the great socialist construction cause of the motherland.

Huang Huoxing, Deputy Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, and Min Ganghou, Deputy Minister of Justice, were invited to speak at the conference.

At the conference on the afternoon of the 6th and the morning of the 7th, fourteen model figures from various fronts reported on their deeds. Among the representatives who spoke was Zhang Yinghong, director of the Public Security Committee of Huluxiang Township, Huaning County, Yunnan Province, who had repeatedly assisted the government in arresting counter-revolutionaries who had fled to the mountains; Yang Wanyin, deputy director of the Huaihe River Water Public Security Committee and a fisherman from Anhui Province, who successively uncovered two counter-revolutionary groups who had fled to the water in July and October 1951; Wang Jinhe, a water people's policeman from Wuhan City, who rescued sixty people in rapid currents and huge waves during the flood prevention struggle in July 1954; and Wang Mingfu, an armed people's policeman from Bishan County, Sichuan Province, who pursued and arrested thirteen counter-revolutionaries from 1953 to 1955. When pursuing the counter-revolutionary Deng Fangxun, he traveled more than 1,300 li through barren mountains and ridges in severe winter, taking more than a month to finally complete the mission.

### 全国人民警察治安保卫委员功臣模范代表大会开幕

1956-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民警察治安保卫委员功臣模范代表大会开幕
    据新华社讯　全国人民警察、治安保卫委员功臣模范代表大会六日在北京开幕。
    出席这次大会的906名代表中，人民警察的功臣模范代表有532人，治安保卫委员功臣模范代表有374人，其中有七十一岁的老大爷和二十岁的姑娘，全体代表共有十七个民族成分。
    公安部副部长王昭致开幕词。他勉励全体功臣模范要联系群众、提高觉悟、加强学习，为肃清一切反革命分子和各种犯罪分子、保卫祖国的伟大的社会主义建设事业而奋斗。
    最高人民检察院副检察长黄火星、司法部副部长闵刚侯应邀在会上讲了话。
    六日下午和七日上午的大会上，有各个战线上的十四位模范人物报告了自己的事迹。发言的代表中，有云南省华宁县葫芦乡治安保卫委员会主任张应红，他曾多次协助政府逮捕过逃亡到山区的反革命分子；有安徽省淮河水上治安保卫委员会副主任、渔民杨万银，他在1951年7月和10月，先后查获了两个逃亡水上的反革命集团；有武汉市水上人民警察王金和，他在1954年7月的防汛斗争中曾先后在急流大浪中抢救了六十个人的生命；有四川省璧山县的武装人民警察汪明福，他在1953到1955年中，曾先后追捕了反革命分子十三人。在追捕反革命分子邓仿勋的时候，曾在严冬穿过荒山野岭步行一千三百多里，前后经过一个多月，终于完成了任务。
