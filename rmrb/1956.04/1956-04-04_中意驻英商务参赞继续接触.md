Title: Chinese and Italian Commercial Counselors Stationed in the UK Continue Contact
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, London, 2nd—On March 28th, the Commercial Counselor of the Office of the Chargé d'Affaires of the People's Republic of China in the United Kingdom held a fourth contact with the Commercial Counselor of the Italian Embassy in the United Kingdom, and exchanged views again on how to conduct trade negotiations.

### 中意驻英商务参赞继续接触

1956-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中意驻英商务参赞继续接触
    新华社2日讯　伦敦消息：三月二十八日，中华人民共和国驻英代办处商务参赞同意大利驻英大使馆商务参赞进行了第四次接触，就如何进行贸易谈判问题再度交换了意见。
