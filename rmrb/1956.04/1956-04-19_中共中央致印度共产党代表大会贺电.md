Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Indian Communist Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1956-04-19
Page: 1

    CPC Central Committee Congratulatory Telegram to Indian Communist Party Congress
    To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of India and to all the delegates of the Fourth Congress of the Communist Party of India, comrades:
    Dear comrades, on the occasion of the Fourth Congress of the Communist Party of India, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends its warm congratulations to you. We wish the Congress success! We congratulate you on achieving further successes in uniting the people of the whole country for the cause of consolidating India's national independence, safeguarding peace in Asia and the world, and defending the interests of the Indian people.
                    Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, April 16, 1956

### 中共中央致印度共产党代表大会贺电

1956-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央致印度共产党代表大会贺电印度共产党中央委员会并转印度共产党第四次代表大会全体代表同志们：
    亲爱的同志们，值此印度共产党举行第四次代表大会之际，中国共产党中央委员会谨向您们致以热烈的祝贺。祝贺大会成功！祝贺您们在团结全国人民为巩固印度的民族独立、为维护亚洲和世界的和平和保卫印度人民利益的事业中，获得进一步的成就。
                    中国共产党中央委员会　1956年4月16日
