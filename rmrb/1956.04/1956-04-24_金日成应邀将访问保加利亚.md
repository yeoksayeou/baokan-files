Title: Kim Il-sung to Visit Bulgaria at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1956-04-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 23rd: The Korean Central News Agency reported on the 23rd that the government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, in order to further consolidate and develop the friendly and cooperative relations between it and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, has invited a government delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Premier Kim Il-sung, to pay a friendly visit to the People's Republic of Bulgaria.
	Premier Kim Il-sung has accepted this invitation with satisfaction.

### 金日成应邀将访问保加利亚

1956-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

    金日成应邀将访问保加利亚
    新华社平壤23日电  朝鲜中央通讯社二十三日讯：保加利亚人民共和国政府为了进一步巩固和发展它同朝鲜民主主义人民共和国之间的友好合作关系，邀请以金日成首相为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团到保加利亚人民共和国进行友好访问。
    金日成首相已经满意地接受了这一邀请。
