Title: Cyrankiewicz's Reply Telegram to Premier Zhou

Author:

Date: 1956-04-22
Page: 4

Cyrankiewicz's Reply Telegram to Premier Zhou

Beijing, Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:

Thank you warmly, Comrade Premier, for your condolences on the anniversary of the death of Comrade Starets. The Polish people deeply mourn the heroic Chinese comrades, Comrade Starets, and other individuals who unfortunately perished while performing their duties in the fight for peace and friendship among peoples and who were victims of this monstrous crime.

Józef Cyrankiewicz
Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic
Warsaw, April 19, 1956

### 西伦凯维兹给周总理的复电

1956-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    西伦凯维兹给周总理的复电
    北京  中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志：
    斯塔列茨编辑遇难一周年时，承致悼念，总理同志，谨向您表示热烈的感谢。在争取和平和各国人民间友谊的斗争中，几位英勇的中国同志、斯塔列茨同志和牺牲在这一滔天罪行的其它人士，在执行自己的职务时不幸遇难，波兰人民对他们深表哀悼。
    波兰人民共和国部长会议主席
            约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹
          1956年4月19日于华沙
