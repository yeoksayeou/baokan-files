Title: China Theatre Association Strengthens Leadership

Author:
Date: 1956-04-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency dispatch:
    The China Theatre Association held its Fourth (Enlarged) Meeting of the Standing Council in Beijing on the 18th and 19th.

    In order to strengthen the leadership of the association, a Presidium of the Council of the China Theatre Association was established at the meeting, and a Working Committee, a Drama Creation Committee, and a Drama Art Committee were also established. The meeting decided to establish branches in eight cities, including Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Shenyang, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chongqing, and Xi'an, as well as in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region this year; and to develop a number of new members in the near future.

    The meeting heard a report by Tian Han, Chairman of the China Theatre Association. Zhou Yang, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, also attended the meeting and gave instructions. He requested the association to vigorously promote creation, recommend excellent plays, and encourage the creation of performing arts; and to strengthen ideological leadership, so as to achieve greater unity and better serve socialist construction.

### 中国戏剧家协会加强领导力量

1956-04-21
第3版()
专栏：

    中国戏剧家协会加强领导力量
    十八、十九两日，中国戏剧家协会在北京举行常务理事会第四次（扩大）会议。
    为了加强协会的领导力量，会上成立了中国戏剧家协会理事会主席团，并且设立了工作委员会、戏剧创作委员会和戏剧艺术委员会。会议决定今年内在北京、天津、上海、沈阳、武汉、广州、重庆、西安八个城市，以及内蒙古自治区、新疆维吾尔自治区等地成立分会；在最近发展一批会员。
    会议听取了中国戏剧家协会主席田汉的报告。中共中央宣传部副部长周扬也到会作了指示。他要求协会大力繁荣创作，推荐优秀剧目，鼓励表演艺术的创作；并且加强思想领导，达到更加团结，更好地为社会主义建设服务的目的。（据新华社讯）
