Title: Changchun Holds City Ring Race and Bicycle Race
Author:
Date: 1956-04-16
Page: 3

    Changchun Holds City Ring Race and Bicycle Race
    Nearly two thousand male and female athletes participated in the Changchun City 1956 Winners Cup City Ring Race and Women's Bicycle City Ring Race on the 15th.
    Both the race and the bicycle race were 13,000 meters long. After starting from People's Square, the athletes passed through the bustling city area and were cheered by crowds lining the streets along the way. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 长春举行环城赛跑和自行车比赛

1956-04-16
第3版()
专栏：

    长春举行环城赛跑和自行车比赛
    近二千名男、女运动员，十五日参加了长春市1956年优胜杯环城赛跑和女子自行车环城比赛。
    赛跑和自行车比赛全程都是一万三千公尺。运动员们从人民广场出发后，通过繁华的市区，沿途受到夹道欢呼。　（据新华社讯）
