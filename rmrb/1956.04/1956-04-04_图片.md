Young female worker Zhang Jinmei of the State-owned Shijiazhuang No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory recently set a new national record of 5.04 warp axes manually threaded per shift, fully meeting quality standards. She has recently been selected to attend the National Textile Industry Advanced Producers Representative Conference.
