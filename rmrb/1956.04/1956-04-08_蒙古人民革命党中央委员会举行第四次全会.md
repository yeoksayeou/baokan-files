Title: Fourth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Held
Author:
Date: 1956-04-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, 7th. TASS reports from Ulan Bator on the 7th: The Fourth Plenary Session (expanded) of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party concluded on April 6th after being held for five days. The summary of the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the organizational tasks of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party were the focus of attention for the participants of the expanded meeting.
    Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, delivered a report on the summary of the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. He said that the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union was an event of world historical significance, and its resolutions would have an extremely significant impact on all progressive mankind.
    When discussing the directives on the Sixth Five-Year Plan adopted by the Congress, Damba said that this plan would not only ensure an unprecedented increase in the economy and culture of the Soviet Union, and improve the material living standards of the Soviet people, but also ensure the further strengthening of the camp of peace, democracy, and socialism.
    Damba pointed out that the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union once again proved that the Soviet Union considers the maintenance and consolidation of peace as its most important task. He said that the Mongolian people and their government warmly support the Soviet Union's consistent policy of peaceful coexistence between countries with different social systems, and support its proposals aimed at easing international tensions.
    After describing the achievements of the Mongolian People's Republic in industry, agriculture, and culture, Damba spoke about the tasks facing the country this year. He said that the total number of livestock should increase by 3.2% in one year, reaching 23.8 million head. When talking about agricultural cooperation, Damba said that there are now a total of 439 collective farms in the country, which is more than double the number in 1954.
    Damba said that industrial production is planned to increase by 12.2% in one year. Some measures should also be implemented in the development of trade, transportation, postal and telecommunications, and healthcare.
    Finally, Damba also talked about improving the work of the Party.

### 蒙古人民革命党中央委员会举行第四次全会

1956-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民革命党中央委员会举行第四次全会
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社乌兰巴托7日讯：蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第四次全体会议（扩大）在举行五天之后，已经在四月六日闭幕。关于苏共第二十次代表大会的总结和蒙古人民革命党组织的任务的问题，是扩大会议参加者的注意中心。
    蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴作了关于苏共第二十次代表大会的总结报告。他说，苏共第二十次代表大会是有世界历史意义的事件，它的决议将对全体进步人类产生极其重大的影响。
    达姆巴在谈到代表大会所通过的对第六个五年计划的指示的时候说，这一计划不仅会保证空前地提高苏联的经济和文化、提高苏联人民的物质生活水平，而且会保证进一步加强和平、民主和社会主义阵营。
    达姆巴指出，苏共第二十次代表大会再一次证明，苏联是以维护和巩固和平为最主要的任务。他说，蒙古人民和他们的政府热烈拥护苏联一贯执行的不同社会制度的国家和平共处的政策，拥护它提出的旨在缓和国际紧张局势的建议。
    达姆巴叙述了蒙古人民共和国在工业、农业和文化方面的成就之后谈到国家在今年所面临的任务。他说，牲畜总头数在一年中应增加3.2％，即达到二千三百八十万头。达姆巴在谈到农业合作化的时候说，现在全国共有四百三十九个集体农庄，也就是说，比1954年多一倍以上。
    达姆巴说，工业产品产量在一年中计划增加12.2％。在发展贸易、运输、邮电和保健事业方面也都要实行一些措施。
    达姆巴最后还曾谈到改进党的工作的问题。
