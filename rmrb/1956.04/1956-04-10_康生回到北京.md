Title: Kang Sheng Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-10
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 9th. Kang Sheng, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and representative of the Chinese Communist Party attending the Third Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 康生回到北京

1956-04-10
第1版()
专栏：

    康生回到北京
    新华社9日讯　出席德国统一社会党第三次代表会议的中国共产党代表、中共中央政治局委员康生，今天下午乘飞机回到北京。
