Title: Renovation of Fushun Coal Mine's West Open-Pit Mine Begins
Author:
Date: 1956-04-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency report:

    The renovation project of the Fushun Coal Mine's West Open-Pit Mine commenced on the 3rd. The West Open-Pit Mine has the highest coal production in the Fushun Coal Mine. After the completion of the renovation project, the mining volume of coal and oil shale in the West Open-Pit Mine will be approximately doubled compared to the present, while the number of production personnel can be reduced by more than half, and the production cost will be greatly reduced.

### 抚顺煤矿西露天矿开始改建

1956-04-09
第2版()
专栏：

    抚顺煤矿西露天矿开始改建
    抚顺煤矿西露天矿改建工程在三日动工。西露天矿是抚顺煤矿煤产量最高的矿。改建工程完工以后，西露天矿的煤和油母页岩开采量将比现在提高一倍左右，而生产人员却可以减少一半以上，生产成本将大大降低。
                  （据新华社讯）
