Title: List of All Members of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1956-04-23
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency

The list of all members of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region is as follows:

    Chairman: Dalai Lama Danzeng Gyatso
    First Vice Chairman: Bainqen Erdini Qoiji Gyaincain
    Second Vice Chairman: Zhang Guohua
    Members: Chijiang Luosang Yixi, Suokang Wangqing Gele, Duoga Pengcuo Raojie, Apo Awang Jinmei, Xianka Jumai Dorje, Gazhang Luosang Rizeng, Dala Luosang Sandan, Liuxia Tubo Tabba, Kaimo Sonam Wangdui, Yutuo Zhaxi Dunzhu, Sangpo Caiwang Renzeng, Ronglangse Tubo Nuosang, Pala Tubo Weideng, Ouxie Tubo Sangque, Langlin Banzhujiumei, Minlin Jiayang Jianzan, Yaoxi Gongbao Caidan, Zhandong Ji Jinmei, Laming Yixi Chuchen, Nuozhang Luosang Jianzan, Nawang Jinba, Derao Duoji Youjia, Duodan Luosang Jinba, Cidanjianzan, Luosang Chenlie, Tuankang Sonam Daojie, Pabala, Chaya Luodeng Xirao, Bangda Duoji, Pingcuo Wangjie, Gesang Wangdui, Silang Jiangcun, Caiwang Duoji, Pangqiu, Weisa Jianzan, Aqu, Suolang, Tubo Cairen, Lunzhu Taokai, Tubo Qeqi Jianzan, Dingji Living Buddha, Sagya Awang Tuqin Banba, Chupu Garma Ba Beiri Duoji, Molin Awang Qezha, Langdun Gongga Wangjiu, Xuekang Tubo Nima, Danzeng Gyatso, Yaoxi Zeren Zhuoma (The above are Tibetan) Tan Guansan, Fan Ming, Wang Qimei, Mu Shengzhong.
                (Xinhua News Agency)
