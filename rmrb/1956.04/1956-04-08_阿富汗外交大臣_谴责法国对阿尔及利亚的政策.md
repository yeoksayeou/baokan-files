Title: Afghan Foreign Minister Condemns French Policy Towards Algeria
Author:
Date: 1956-04-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul: Afghan Foreign Minister Naim Khan, in response to a question from a Bakhtar News Agency reporter regarding the situation in Algeria, said: "The pressure exerted by France on the Algerian people and the prohibition of their exercising their rights is one of the most regrettable events of our time." He added: "France's statement has aroused strong opposition and astonishment throughout the East and the Islamic world, as well as among people of all walks of life in countries around the world who love humanity." Naim stated that the Afghan government and people express their sincere sympathy for the misfortune of the Algerian people and believe that France's attitude towards Algeria is a noteworthy warning to the people of Asia, Africa, and the world who love freedom.

### 阿富汗外交大臣  谴责法国对阿尔及利亚的政策

1956-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    阿富汗外交大臣
    谴责法国对阿尔及利亚的政策
    据新华社讯　喀布尔消息：阿富汗外交大臣纳伊姆·汗就阿尔及利亚局势答复巴赫塔通讯社记者的询问说：“法国对阿尔及利亚人民施加的压力和禁止他们行使自己权利的作法，是当代一件最遗憾的事。”他又说：“法国的声明已在整个东方和伊斯兰教世界以及世界各国爱好人道的各阶层人民中引起了强烈的反对和惊愕。”纳伊姆声明说，阿富汗政府和人民对阿尔及利亚人民的不幸表示衷心的同情，并且认为法国对阿尔及利亚的态度是对于亚洲、非洲以及全世界爱好自由的人民一个值得注意的警告。
