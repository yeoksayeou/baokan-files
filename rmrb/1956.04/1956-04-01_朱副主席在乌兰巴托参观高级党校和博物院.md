Title: Vice Chairman Zhu Visits Advanced Party School and Museum in Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1956-04-01
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 31st: Vice Chairman Zhu De and his entourage visited the Sukhe-Bator Advanced Party School and the Sukhe-Bator and Choibalsan Museum on the morning of March 31st.
    Accompanying them on the visit were: Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia; Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party; Sirindibu, First Deputy Prime Minister; Rabdan, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs; Ochirbat, Mongolian Ambassador to China; and He Ying, Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia.
    During the visit to the Advanced Party School, Vice Chairman Zhu De was warmly welcomed by all the teachers and students of the school. Principal Dorjbat introduced the school's situation.
    After the visit, Vice Chairman Zhu De wrote the following inscription in the commemorative album: "I wish the Advanced Party School of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party to train more cadres with both ability and integrity for the country."
    During the visit to the museum, Curator Ser-Otzab introduced the situation of the museum.
    Vice Chairman Zhu De's inscription in the museum's commemorative album was: "Here we have seen the facts of the heroic struggle and diligent construction of the Mongolian people under the leadership of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party."

### 朱副主席在乌兰巴托参观高级党校和博物院

1956-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    朱副主席在乌兰巴托参观高级党校和博物院
    新华社31日讯　乌兰巴托消息：朱德副主席和他的随行人员三月三十一日上午参观了苏赫—巴托尔高级党校和苏赫—巴托尔、乔巴山博物院。
    陪同参观的有：蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布、蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴、第一副总理锡林迪布、代理外交部长拉布丹、蒙古驻中国大使奥其尔巴特和中国驻蒙古大使何英等。
    参观高级党校时，朱德副主席受到了党校全体师生的热烈欢迎。校长多尔日巴特介绍了党校的情况。
    参观完毕后，朱德副主席在纪念册上写了下面的题词：祝蒙古人民革命党高级党校给国家培养更多的才德兼备的干部。
    参观博物院时，院长赛尔·奥特扎布介绍了博物院的情况。
    朱德副主席在博物院的纪念册上的题词是：在这里我们看到了蒙古人民在蒙古人民革命党的领导下英勇斗争和勤劳建设的事实。
