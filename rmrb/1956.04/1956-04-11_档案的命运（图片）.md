Title: The Fate of Archives (Picture)
Author: Yingtāo
Date: 1956-04-11
Page: 3

    The Fate of Archives
    Yingtāo
    Haphazardly sent in,
    Laying traps for one's own use.
    Simply burn them in a fire,
    Wandering and scattered to the east and west.
    Exposed to wind and rain, no one cares,
    Empty nine days out of ten.

    档案的命运
    英韬

    干脆烧它一把火
