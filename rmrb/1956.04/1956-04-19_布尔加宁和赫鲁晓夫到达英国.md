Title: Bulganin and Khrushchev Arrive in Britain
Author:
Date: 1956-04-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Portsmouth, April 18th, TASS dispatch: At the invitation of the British government, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, and Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, arrived in Britain on April 18th. The cruiser "Ordzhonikidze," carrying Bulganin, Khrushchev, and their entourage, along with the escorting destroyers "Perfect" and "Lookout," arrived together in Portsmouth.

Xinhua News Agency, Portsmouth, April 18th, TASS dispatch: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, and Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, arrived in Portsmouth on April 18th. Welcoming Bulganin and Khrushchev in Portsmouth were: the British First Lord of the Admiralty, Lord Cilcennin, the Joint Under-Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs, Lord Reading, the Soviet Ambassador to Britain, Malik, the British Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Sir William Hayter, and British Admiral of the Fleet, Sir George Creasy, among others. Bulganin and Khrushchev reviewed the guard of honor, and then traveled to London by special car.

Xinhua News Agency, London, April 17th, TASS dispatch: British Prime Minister Eden said in the House of Commons on the 17th, in response to questions, that the main purpose of the talks to be held between the leaders of the Soviet Union and Britain is "to discuss the many issues that currently divide the world." Eden said: "The negotiations will be private, and I cannot say in advance what issues will be included on the agenda."

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫到达英国

1956-04-19
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫到达英国
    新华社18日讯　塔斯社朴次茅斯十八日讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫应英国政府的邀请在四月十八日抵达英国。布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫以及他们的随行人员所乘的巡洋舰“奥尔忠尼启则号”以及护航的“完美号”驱逐舰和“了望号”驱逐舰已经一同到达了朴次茅斯。
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社朴次茅斯十八日讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫四月十八日抵达朴次茅斯。在朴次茅斯欢迎布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫的有：英国海军大臣基尔肯宁勋爵、外交副大臣里丁勋爵、苏联驻英国大使马立克、英国驻苏联大使威廉·海特尔爵士、英国海军元帅乔治·克里西爵士等人。布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫检阅了仪仗队，随后乘专车赴伦敦。
    据新华社18日讯　据塔斯社伦敦十七日讯：英国首相艾登十七日在下院答复质询时说，苏联和英国两国领导人将要进行的谈判的主要目的是“讨论目前使世界分歧的许多问题”。艾登说：“谈判将是不公开的，议程里将包括些什么问题，我不能事先讲出。”
