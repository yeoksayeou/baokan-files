Title: End of Talks Between Soviet and British Leaders
Author:
Date: 1956-04-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, London, April 26th, via TASS: On the morning of April 25th, Bulganin and Khrushchev continued their talks with British leaders at 10 Downing Street. The talks took place from 10:30 AM to 12:30 PM local time. Following the talks, Bulganin and Khrushchev attended a banquet hosted by British Foreign Secretary Lloyd at his official residence.

　　After the banquet, Bulganin and Khrushchev returned to the Claridge's Hotel, and from there went back to 10 Downing Street to continue their talks with British leaders, which lasted from 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM. After this meeting, a joint communiqué was issued on the conclusion of the talks.

　　The communiqué stated: "The final stage of the Anglo-Soviet talks concluded this afternoon (25th).

　　Tomorrow evening (26th), after the Soviet leaders return from their visit to Scotland, the results of the talks will be announced.”

Xinhua News Agency, London, via TASS: On the evening of April 25th, Bulganin and Khrushchev attended a ballet performance at the Royal Opera House. They sat in the Royal Box with Prime Minister Eden. The audience applauded the guests.

　　The performance was a great success. After the performance, Bulganin and Khrushchev went backstage to thank the ballerina Margot Fonteyn and the other performers for their outstanding performances, and presented them with flowers.

### 苏联和英国领导人的会谈结束

1956-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和英国领导人的会谈结束
    新华社26日讯  据塔斯社伦敦二十五日讯：四月二十五日上午，布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫在唐宁街十号继续同英国领导人进行会谈。会谈从当地时间十时三十分举行到十二时三十分。在会谈结束后，布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫出席了英国外交大臣劳埃德在官邸为他们举行的宴会。
    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫在宴会后回到 “克莱里吉”饭店，从那里再去唐宁街十号，同英国领导人继续会谈，会谈从下午四时开始，直到六时结束。在这次会谈后，发表了双方同意的关于会谈结束的公报。
    公报说：“英苏会谈的最后阶段在今天（二十五日）下午结束了。
    明天（二十六日）晚间，在苏联领导人访问苏格兰回来之后，将宣布会谈结果。”
    新华社据塔斯社伦敦讯  布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫四月二十五日晚上到皇家歌剧院看巴蕾舞剧。他们同艾登首相一起坐在皇室包厢里。观众鼓掌欢迎客人们。
    演出获得很大成功。在演出结束后，布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫到后台感谢芭蕾舞女演员玛格特·方特英和其他演员的出色表演，并且向演员们献了花。
