Title: Yugov Elected Chairman of the Council of Ministers by Bulgarian National Assembly

Author:

Date: 1956-04-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 18th - According to the Bulgarian Telegraph Agency, Sofia: The Fifth Extraordinary Session of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Bulgaria began in Sofia on April 16th.

Present at the opening session on April 16th were deputies of the National Assembly, ministers of various ministries of the Bulgarian government, members of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, members of the National Committee of the Fatherland Front, and representatives of other social organizations.

The Yugoslav parliamentary delegation, currently visiting Bulgaria, was also invited to attend the opening ceremony. The head of the delegation, Pijade, also spoke at the opening session, pointing out that there are various conditions for developing friendly relations and cooperation between the two countries, South [Yugoslavia] and Bulgaria.

At the meeting on April 17th, Atanasov, Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly, who was presiding over the meeting, announced that the Presidium had received a request from Vulko Chervenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, to be relieved of his duties. In his application, Chervenkov stated that due to the incorrect working methods he had adopted while serving as Chairman of the Council of Ministers, the work of the country had suffered certain losses. He therefore requested the National Assembly to relieve him of his duties as Chairman of the Council of Ministers.

The Presidium proposed that the National Assembly accept Vulko Chervenkov's application and relieve him of his duties as Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria.

The Presidium's proposal was unanimously adopted by the meeting.

Subsequently, National Assembly member Zhivkov, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Fatherland Front, the Bulgarian Communist Party parliamentary group, and the Bulgarian Agrarian People's Union parliamentary group, proposed the election of Comrade Anton Yugov as Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria.

All deputies unanimously elected Anton Yugov as Chairman of the Council of Ministers.

### 保加利亚国民议会选举于哥夫为部长会议主席

1956-04-19
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚国民议会选举于哥夫为部长会议主席
    新华社布拉格18日电　据保加利亚通讯社索非亚讯：保加利亚人民共和国国民议会第五次特别会议四月十六日起在索非亚举行。
    出席四月十六日开幕会议的有，国民议会的议员、保加利亚政府各部部长、保加利亚共产党中央委员、祖国阵线全国委员会委员和其他社会团体的代表。
    目前正在保加利亚访问的南斯拉夫议会代表团也应邀参加了会议的开幕式，代表团团长皮雅杰还在开幕会议上讲了话。他指出南保两国之间有发展友好关系和合作的各种条件。
    在四月十七日会议上，主持会议的保加利亚国民议会主席团副主席阿塔纳索夫宣布说，主席团收到了部长会议主席伏尔科·契尔文科夫请求解除他所担任的职务的一份申请书。契尔文科夫在申请书中说，由于他在担任部长会议主席的职务中采用的不正确的工作方法给国家工作带来了一定的损失，所以请求国民议会解除他所担任的部长会议主席的职务。
    主席团建议国民议会接受伏尔科·契尔文科夫的申请，并且解除他所担任的保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席的职务。
    主席团的建议被会议一致通过。
    接着，国民议会议员日夫科夫代表保加利亚共产党中央委员会、祖国阵线全国委员会常务委员会和保加利亚共产党议会党团、保加利亚农民联盟议会党团提议选举安东·于哥夫同志为保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席。
    全体议员一致选举了安东·于哥夫为部长会议主席。
