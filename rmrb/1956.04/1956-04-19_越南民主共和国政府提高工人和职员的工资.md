Title: The Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Raises Wages for Workers and Staff
Author:
Date: 1956-04-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 18th: According to the Vietnam News Agency: The government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam recently promulgated measures to increase wages for workers and staff. The purpose of this measure is to initially improve the living standards of workers and government employees, and to encourage them to improve technology in order to complete the national plan for economic recovery and cultural development.

According to this new measure, the average wage increase for workers and government employees will be 20% compared to the current level. In addition, because the new wage standards took effect in July of last year, they will also be paid supplementary wages.

### 越南民主共和国政府提高工人和职员的工资

1956-04-19
第4版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国政府提高工人和职员的工资
    据新华社河内18日电　据越南通讯社讯：越南民主共和国政府最近颁布了关于提高工人和职员工资的措施。这一措施的目的在于初步提高工人和政府机关工作人员的生活水平，鼓励他们改进技术，以便完成恢复经济和发展文化的国家计划。
    根据这一新的措施，工人和政府机关工作人员的工资平均比目前增加20％。此外，由于新的工资标准从去年七月生效，因此还要发给他们追加的工资。
