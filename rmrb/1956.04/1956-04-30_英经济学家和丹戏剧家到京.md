Title: British Economist and Danish Playwright Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 4

British economist Nicholas Kaldor and his wife, and Danish playwright Kjeld Abell and his wife, arrived in Beijing on the 28th, having been invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China.

### 英经济学家和丹戏剧家到京

1956-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    英经济学家和丹戏剧家到京
    应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国参观访问的英国经济学家尼古拉斯·卡尔德和他的夫人，丹麦戏剧家基耶尔德·阿贝尔和他的夫人已经在二十八日先后到达北京。
