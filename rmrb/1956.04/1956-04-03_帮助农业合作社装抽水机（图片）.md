Title: Helping Agricultural Cooperatives Install Water Pumps
Author: Sun Baopu
Date: 1956-04-03
Page: 2
Helping Agricultural Cooperatives Install Water Pumps

    After supplying new agricultural implements to agricultural producers' cooperatives, the Gongxiao (supply and marketing) Cooperative of Gaoqing County, Shandong Province, also sends technicians to help them install the equipment. This is a technician from the Gongxiao Cooperative helping the Hongqi Agricultural Production Cooperative of Tianzhen install a water pump. Sun Baopu (Draft from Dazhong Daily)

### 帮助农业合作社装抽水机（图片）
孙保朴
1956-04-03
第2版()
专栏：

    帮助农业合作社装抽水机
    山东省高青县供销合作社把新式农具供应给农业生产合作社后，并派技术人员帮助他们安装。这是供销合作社的技术员正在帮助田镇红旗农业生产合作社安装抽水机。　孙保朴（大众日报社稿）
