Title: President of the International Federation of Women, Mrs. Gordon, Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 21st: Mrs. Eugénie Cotton, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, arrived in Beijing by plane at 5:00 PM today. Accompanying her were: Mrs. Sisa Nabaraoui (Egypt), Vice-President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, and Zoya Ivanova (Soviet Union), Deputy Secretary-General. Also arriving on the same flight were two council members from the Soviet Union and Bulgaria, as well as special representatives and staff members from Czechoslovakia, Israel, Norway, Switzerland, the United Kingdom, the Soviet Union, and other countries, totaling sixteen people. They are all here to attend the Women's International Democratic Federation Council meeting scheduled to be held in Beijing on April 24th.

Welcoming them at the airport were Simone Bertrand, Deputy Secretary-General of the Women's International Democratic Federation, who had already arrived in Beijing, as well as Cai Chang, President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and Vice-President Li Dequan, among others. ### 国际妇联主席戈登夫人等到北京

1956-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    国际妇联主席戈登夫人等到北京
    据新华社21日讯  国际民主妇女联合会主席欧仁妮·戈登夫人今天下午五时乘飞机到达北京。和她一起到达的有：国际民主妇联副主席西萨·纳巴拉奥维（埃及）、副总书记卓娅·伊凡诺娃（苏联）。同机到达的还有苏联和保加利亚的理事共二人，捷克斯洛伐克、以色列、挪威、瑞士、英国、苏联等国的特邀代表和工作人员等十六人。她们都是来参加预定在四月二十四日在北京举行的国际民主妇联理事会会议的。
    到机场欢迎的有已经来到北京的国际民主妇联副总书记西蒙·贝特朗，中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅和副主席李德全等。（附图片）
