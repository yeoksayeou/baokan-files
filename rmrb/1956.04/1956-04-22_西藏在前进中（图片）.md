Tibet Advances

A panoramic view of the beautiful "City of Sunlight" - the Potala Palace in Lhasa.
			Xinhua News Agency reporter Ren Yongzhao

Chairman Mao Zedong with the Dalai Lama Danzeng Gyatso (right) and the Panchen Erdeni Qoiji Gyaltsen (left).
                              Hou Bo (Xinhua News Agency)

In order to further assist the Tibetan people in construction, the Tibet Engineering Survey Team has basically completed the survey and partial preliminary design work for the establishment of the Lhasa Hydropower Station, the Shigatse Thermal Power Station, and the construction of river embankments and dams on the Lhasa River and Nyang Chu River. This is a member of the survey team's water conservancy group measuring the Lhasa River.  Xinhua News Agency reporter Ren Yongzhao

A meteorological station built on the "Roof of the World."
          Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio

Since the Kangzang, Qingzang, and Rijiang highways opened to traffic, the exchange of goods between the inland and Tibet has become more active. Tibetan people are selecting daily necessities transported from the inland at the Gyangze Trading Company office.	    Fan Shilin (Xinhua News Agency)

The magnificent paintings, exquisite sculptures, and grand architectural art created by the Tibetan working people throughout the generations are an important part of our national heritage. The old Tibetan painter, Suolang Renji, who has been engaged in painting for thirty-seven years, is happily creating because he has been encouraged by the People's Government.	  Xinhua News Agency reporter Ren Yongzhao

The Lhasa "July 1st" Agricultural Experiment Farm promotes various crop varieties in the Tibetan region, which is warmly welcomed by the Tibetan people. The Tibetan farmer, Sha Menggudeng, is introducing the growth of the Wugong 774 spring wheat he planted to the deputy director of the experimental farm (center).
                        Xinhua News Agency reporter Ren Yongzhao

New buildings such as the post office, People's Bank, and trading company, and state-owned stores in Changdu.
                                Xinhua News Agency reporter Ren Yongzhao

People's Hospitals in various parts of Tibet provide free prevention and treatment of diseases for the Tibetan people.	  Xinhua News Agency reporter Ren Yongzhao

Tibetan young women League members are learning to use new-style seeders.	  	Cheng Minggui (Nationalities Pictorial)

Tibetan primary school students are reviewing their lessons in the warm sunshine.	      Su Liangzhi (PLA Pictorial)

In addition to providing free breeding services for the Tibetan people's livestock, the Qianning Animal Husbandry Experiment Station also regularly helps the Tibetan people check the growth of sheep.	Cheng Minggui (Nationalities Pictorial)

### 西藏在前进中（图片）

1956-04-22
第5版()
专栏：

    西藏在前进中
    美丽的“日光城”——拉萨布达拉宫全景。			新华社记者  任用昭摄
    毛泽东主席同达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措（右）、班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞（左）在一起。
                              侯  波摄（新华社稿）
    为了进一步帮助西藏人民进行建设，西藏工程勘察队已经基本完成了建立拉萨水力发电站、日喀则火力发电站和修建拉萨河、年楚河河堤及水坝等工程的勘测和部分初步设计工作。这是勘察队水利组员在测量拉萨河。  新华社记者    任用昭摄
    在“世界屋脊”上修建的气象站。
          中央新闻纪录电影制片厂
    稿康藏、青藏、日江等公路通车以后，内地和西藏地区的物资交流更加活跃。藏民正在江孜贸易公司办事处选购内地运来的日用品。	    范士林摄	    （新华社稿）
    藏族劳动人民历代所创造的瑰丽的绘画、精美的雕刻以及宏伟的建筑艺术，是我国民族遗产的重要组成部分。这位从事三十七年绘画工作的藏族老画师索朗仁吉，由于得到人民政府的鼓励，正在愉快地进行创作。	  新华社记者	    任用昭摄
    拉萨“七一”农业试验场在西藏地区推广各种农作物的品种，受到藏民的热烈欢迎。藏族农民沙蒙古登正向试验场副场长（中）介绍他种植的武功774号春小麦生长的情况。
                        新华社记者  任用昭摄
    昌都新修建的邮局、人民银行、贸易公司等机关和国营商店的大楼。
                                新华社记者  任用昭摄
    西藏各地人民医院，免费为藏民防治疾病。	  新华社记者	    任用昭摄
    藏族女青年团员在学习使用新式播种机。	  	程明贵摄（民族画报社稿）
    藏族小学生在温暖的阳光下温习功课。	      苏良质摄	    （解放军画报社稿）
    乾宁农牧试验站除了免费为藏民的牲畜配种以外，还经常帮助藏民检查羊的成长情况。	程明贵摄（民族画报社稿）
