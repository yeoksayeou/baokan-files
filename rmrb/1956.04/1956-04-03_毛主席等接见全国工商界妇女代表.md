Title: Chairman Mao and Others Receive Representatives of the National Industrial and Commercial Women's Circles
Author:
Date: 1956-04-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Chairman Mao Zedong, along with members of the Politburo of the CPC Central Committee Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Peng Zhen, Deng Xiaoping, and leaders of democratic parties He Xiangning, Zhang Bojun, Huang Yanpei, and Li Zhuchen, received this afternoon all the representatives of the National Conference of Industrialists and Businessmen's Families and Female Industrialists and Businessmen, as well as all members of the Hong Kong and Macao Industrial and Commercial Women's Sightseeing Group, and took a group photo together. Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and Vice-Chairpersons Deng Yingchao and Xu Guangping also participated in the reception. (Attached photo)

Front row from left to right: Deng Yingchao, Cai Chang, Peng Zhen, Li Zhuchen, Zhou Enlai, Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Huang Yanpei, Deng Xiaoping, Zhang Bojun, He Xiangning. ### 毛主席等接见全国工商界妇女代表

1956-04-03
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席等接见全国工商界妇女代表
    新华社2日讯　毛泽东主席和中共中央政治局委员刘少奇、周恩来、彭真、邓小平以及民主党派的负责人何香凝、章伯钧、黄炎培、李烛尘，今天下午接见了全国工商业者家属和女工商业者代表会议的全体代表，以及港澳工商界妇女观光团全体人员，并且在一起照了像。中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅、副主席邓颖超、许广平都参加了接见。（附图片）
    毛主席和中共中央政治局委员、各民主党派负责人等和参加全国工商业者家属和女工商业者代表会议的代表及港澳工商界妇女观光团全体人员合影。前排由左至右：邓颖超、蔡畅、彭真、李烛尘、周恩来、毛泽东、刘少奇、黄炎培、邓小平、章伯钧、何香凝。　新华社记者　齐观山摄
