Title: Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation Visits Ethiopia at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Addis Ababa, April 29th: After visiting Sudan, the Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation arrived in Addis Ababa, the capital of Ethiopia, by plane on April 28th at the invitation of the Government of the Kingdom of Ethiopia for a visit and performance.

At the airport, the delegation was welcomed by Zewde Gabre-Meher, Director of the School Administration Department of the Ministry of Education, who was specially appointed by the Emperor to receive the delegation, and Abebe Sium, Acting Director-General of the Ministry of Education. The Soviet and Czechoslovak Ambassadors to Ethiopia and personnel from the embassies of the two countries also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

Baurhan, head of the Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation, said in a written statement at the airport: The Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation's visit to Ethiopia not only brings classical and modern Chinese art programs, but more importantly, it brings the friendship of the Government and people of the People's Republic of China to the Government and people of Ethiopia. We will feel extremely honored and happy if our visit can promote understanding and cultural exchange between our two countries.

### 我国文化艺术代表团应邀到埃塞俄比亚访问

1956-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    我国文化艺术代表团应邀到埃塞俄比亚访问
    新华社亚的斯亚贝巴29日电  中国文化艺术代表团在访问苏丹以后，应埃塞俄比亚王国政府的邀请在四月二十八日从喀土穆乘飞机到达埃塞俄比亚首都亚的斯亚贝巴进行访问和演出。
    在机场上欢迎代表团的有受皇帝特派来接待代表团的教育部学校行政司司长泽乌迪·加布雷·梅尔赫姆，教育部代理总监阿贝贝·西乌姆。苏联和捷克斯洛伐克驻埃塞俄比亚的公使和两国公使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
    中国文化艺术代表团团长鲍尔汉在机场发表书面谈话说：中国文化艺术代表团访问埃塞俄比亚，不仅带来了中国古典和现代的艺术节目，而更重要的是带来了中华人民共和国政府和人民对埃塞俄比亚政府和人民的友情。如果我们的访问能促进我们两国间的谅解和文化交流，我们将感到极其光荣和愉快。
