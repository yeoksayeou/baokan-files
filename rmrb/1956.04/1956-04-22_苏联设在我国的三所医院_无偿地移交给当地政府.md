Title: The Three Hospitals Established in China by the Soviet Union Were Transferred to Local Governments Free of Charge
Author:
Date: 1956-04-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 21st: The three hospitals established in Dalian-Lüda, Urumqi, and Yining by the Union of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies of the Soviet Union have all been transferred to local governments free of charge.

    The transfer ceremony of the Dalian Soviet Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies Hospital in Dalian-Lüda City was held today. Che Xiangchen, Deputy Governor of Liaoning Province, announced that the hospital would be renamed the Dalian-Lüda Sino-Soviet Friendship Hospital. He also presented the Sino-Soviet Friendship Medal to the Soviet medical staff on behalf of the State Council of the People's Republic of China.

    The two hospitals in Urumqi and Yining were also transferred to local governments on January 28th and February 14th, respectively.

    The Dalian Soviet Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies Hospital in Dalian-Lüda City has 200 beds and an outpatient department that can accommodate more than 1,000 patients. The hospitals in Urumqi and Yining each have 50 beds.

### 苏联设在我国的三所医院  无偿地移交给当地政府

1956-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联设在我国的三所医院
    无偿地移交给当地政府
    据新华社21日讯  苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会设在旅大、乌鲁木齐和伊宁的三所医院，已经全部无偿地移交给当地政府。
    设在旅大市的大连苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会医院的移交仪式在今天举行。辽宁省副省长车向忱宣布这个医院改名为旅大市中苏友谊医院。他还代表中华人民共和国国务院把中苏友谊章分别赠给苏联医务工作人员。
    设在乌鲁木齐市和伊宁市的两所医院也已经分别在一月二十八日和二月十四日移交给当地政府。
    旅大市的大连苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会医院，有二百张病床和一个可以容纳一千多名患者的门诊部。设在乌鲁木齐和伊宁的医院各有病床五十张。
