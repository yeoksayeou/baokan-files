Title: Indonesia Opposes Atomic Weapons Testing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 22nd. The Indonesian Parliament unanimously passed a resolution opposing atomic weapons testing on April 21st.

The resolution stated that the use of atomic bombs would only bring disaster to human life. The Indonesian Parliament has decided to stop any testing or use of atomic bombs or other nuclear weapons not for peaceful purposes, especially those carried out on the Asian and African continents and in places close to Indonesian territory. The resolution pointed out that the upcoming atomic bomb test on Christmas Island may cause damage to the Indonesian islands.

The resolution emphasized that atomic energy should be used for peaceful purposes.

This resolution will be submitted to the United Nations for consideration and will be distributed to Asian and African countries and peoples, as well as to all countries in the world. Twenty-eight members of parliament, representing all parliamentary groups, signed the proposal.

At this meeting, the Indonesian Parliament also unanimously passed a resolution protesting the French government's policies in Algeria.

### 印度尼西亚反对原子武器试验

1956-04-23
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚反对原子武器试验
    新华社雅加达22日电  印度尼西亚国会在四月二十一日一致通过了反对原子武器试验的决议。
    决议说，原子弹的使用只会给人类生命带来灾难。印度尼西亚国会已经决定制止不是为了和平目的的原子弹或其他核子武器的任何试验或使用，特别是在亚非两洲大陆和在靠近印度尼西亚领土的地方进行这些试验。决议指出，即将在圣诞岛进行的原子弹试验可能对印度尼西亚岛屿造成损害。
    决议强调指出，应当把原子能用于和平用途。
    这个决议将提交联合国考虑，并且将分发给亚非各国和人民以及全世界所有国家。代表所有国会党团的二十八个国会议员在提案上签了字。
    印度尼西亚国会在这次会议上还一致通过了抗议法国政府在阿尔及利亚的政策的决议。
