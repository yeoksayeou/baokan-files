Title: “Doubt and Confusion” Replace Determination: Radford Discusses Atmosphere Among U.S. Allies
Author:
Date: 1956-04-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 18th Dispatch from Washington: According to a United Press report, Chairman of the U.S. Joint Chiefs of Staff Radford, while testifying on the "foreign aid" plan at a closed-door meeting of the House Foreign Affairs Committee on April 17th, said that "doubt and confusion" had arisen among some U.S. allies. He said: "I have recently visited various parts of the world and discovered some serious problems. In the year since my last visit to those countries, there has been a subtle but noticeable change in the atmosphere in some of them." "In some cases, doubt and confusion have replaced firm determination." Radford requested that Congress pass the government's proposed $4.9 billion "foreign aid" plan to "maintain" the "collective strength" of the Western world.

### “怀疑和混乱”代替了决心  雷德福谈美国盟国中的气氛

1956-04-19
第4版()
专栏：

    “怀疑和混乱”代替了决心
    雷德福谈美国盟国中的气氛
    新华社18日讯　据合众社华盛顿消息：美国参谋长联席会议主席雷德福四月十七日在众议院外交委员会秘密会议上就
  “援外”计划作证时说，美国一些盟国已经发生了“怀疑和混乱”。他说：“我最近曾访问了世界各地，发现了一些严重的问题。在我上次访问过那些国家之后的一年中，在其中一些国家里在气氛方面已经有了微妙的但是看得出来的变化。”“在一些情况下，怀疑和混乱已经代替了坚定的决心。”雷德福要求国会通过政府提出的四十九亿美元“援外”计划，来“维持”西方世界的
  “集体力量”。
