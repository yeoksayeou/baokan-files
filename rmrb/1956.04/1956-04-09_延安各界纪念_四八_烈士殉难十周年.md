Title: Yan'an Circles Commemorate the Tenth Anniversary of the Martyrdom of the "April 8th" Martyrs
Author:
Date: 1956-04-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 8th. Today, more than 500 representatives from all walks of life in Yan'an held a memorial meeting at the "April 8th" Martyrs' Cemetery to commemorate the tenth anniversary of the martyrdom of Comrades Wang Ruofei, Qin Bangxian, Ye Ting, Deng Fa, and Mr. Huang Qisheng.

The martyrs' tombs were covered with flowers. The cypresses planted by the representatives of the Youth Afforestation Conference of Five Provinces (Regions) around the martyrs' tombs are already a vibrant green. At the beginning of the memorial ceremony, representatives from the Party and government organs, people's organizations, troops, and schools in the Yan'an area and Yan'an County, as well as representatives from the "Shan'guang" Advanced Agricultural Production Cooperative near the Martyrs' Cemetery, held a memorial service. Subsequently, Xu Ziyou, Deputy Secretary of the Yan'an Prefectural Committee of the Communist Party of China, delivered a speech.

### 延安各界纪念“四八”烈士殉难十周年

1956-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    延安各界纪念“四八”烈士殉难十周年
    新华社西安8日电　延安各界代表五百多人今天在“四八”烈士陵园举行纪念会，纪念王若飞、秦邦宪、叶挺、邓发等同志和黄齐生先生殉难十周年。
    烈士墓前献满了鲜花。五省（区）青年造林大会的代表们在烈士墓周围植的柏树已是一片新绿。纪念仪式开始，由延安专区和延安县的党政机关、人民团体以及部队和学校的代表，烈士陵园附近的“陕光”高级农业生产合作社的代表举行祭奠。随后，中共延安地委副书记徐子猷讲了话。
