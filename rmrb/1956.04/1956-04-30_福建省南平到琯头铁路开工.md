Title: Railway Construction Begins from Nanping to Guantou in Fujian Province
Author:
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 2

Railway construction has begun on the Nanping-Guantou Railway in Fujian Province.

This railway, which is over 211 kilometers long, will connect with the Yingtan-Xiamen Railway, which is scheduled to be completed ahead of schedule by the end of this year. It runs from Nanping through mountains and across the Min River, passing through Fuzhou City and reaching Guantou Town in Lianjiang County. This railway is being constructed by the Railway Corps of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.
            (Xinhua News Agency)

### 福建省南平到琯头铁路开工

1956-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    福建省南平到琯头铁路开工
    福建省境内南平—琯头铁路已经开工。
    这条铁路和将在今年年底提前修通的鹰潭—厦门铁路连接在一起，全长211公里多。它从南平穿过丛山峻岭，跨过闽江，经过福州市直达连江县的琯头镇。这条铁路由中国人民解放军铁道兵部队施工。
            （据新华社讯）
