One of the construction projects of China's first Five-Year Plan—the construction period for the Xiejiaji No. 2 modern large-scale vertical shaft of the Huainan Coal Mine—will be shortened from five years to two years and three months. The entire project can be completed by the end of May this year. This is the appearance of the completed ground project of the vertical shaft.
