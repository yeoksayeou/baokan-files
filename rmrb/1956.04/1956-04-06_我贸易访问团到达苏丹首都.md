Title: Our Trade Delegation Arrives in Sudanese Capital
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 5th: Khartoum dispatch: The Chinese trade delegation headed by Ye Jizhuang, Minister of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Trade, arrived in Khartoum, the capital of Sudan, at 2:00 p.m. on April 4th at the invitation of the Sudanese government. Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included the representative of Sudanese Prime Minister Azhari, the Director and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Sudanese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the State Secretary of the Ministry of Finance, the Director of the Ministry of Industry and Commerce, as well as the Hungarian diplomatic representative in Sudan, and the commercial representatives of Czechoslovakia and the German Democratic Republic.

### 我贸易访问团到达苏丹首都

1956-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    我贸易访问团到达苏丹首都
    新华社开罗5日电　喀土穆消息：以中国对外贸易部部长叶季壮为首的中国贸易访问团，应苏丹政府的邀请在四月四日下午二时到达苏丹首都喀土穆。到机场欢迎的有苏丹总理阿扎里的代表、苏丹外交部礼宾司正副司长、财政部国务秘书、工商部司长以及驻苏丹的匈牙利外交代表、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国的商务代表等。
