Title: Closing of the Eighth Session of the Polish Parliament
Author:
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, April 29th. The Eighth Session of the Polish People's Republic Parliament closed on April 28th.

    The session heard and discussed the report on the government's domestic and foreign policies made by the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland, Cyrankiewicz. The parliamentarians approved measures aimed at further democratizing social and political life, strengthening the socialist legal system, and enhancing the role of local organs in state management. The session adopted the 1956 national budget and approved the report of the Council of Ministers on the implementation of the 1954 national budget. The session also approved the reorganization of the Council of State and the Council of Ministers, passed an amnesty law and other laws.

    At the April 28th meeting, Cyrankiewicz also responded to some of the inquiries raised by the parliamentarians.
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, April 28th. The Eighth Session of the Polish People's Republic Parliament continued on the 27th. The session heard and discussed the relevant reports of the committees and passed some bills and the following appointments and removals: In accordance with his request, Józef Winiewicz was relieved of his post as Minister of Foreign Affairs and Adam Rapacki was appointed Minister of Foreign Affairs, relieving him of his post as Minister of Higher Education; Marian Jurewicz was appointed Minister of Higher Education, and Zofia Wasilkowska was appointed Minister of Justice. The parliament also approved the decision of the Council of State to relieve Feliks Widy-Wirski of his post as Secretary of the Council of State and appoint him Prosecutor General, and unanimously elected Józef Winiewicz as Secretary of the Council of State.

### 波兰议会第八次会议闭幕

1956-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰议会第八次会议闭幕
    新华社华沙29日电  波兰人民共和国议会第八次会议在四月二十八日闭幕。
    这次会议听取和讨论了波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹所做的关于政府内政和外交政策的报告。议员们批准了旨在使社会和政治生活进一步民主化、加强社会主义法制、提高地方机关在国家管理工作中的作用的措施。这次会议通过了1956年国家预算，并且批准了部长会议关于1954年国家预算的执行情况的报告，这次会议还批准了国务委员会和部长会议的改组，通过了大赦法以及其他一些法律。
    在四月二十八日会议上，西伦凯维兹还就议员们所提出的一些质询，作了答复。
    新华社华沙28日电  波兰人民共和国议会第八次会议在二十七日继续开会。会议听取和讨论了各委员会的有关报告，并且通过了一些法案和以下的任免事项：根据本人的请求，解除斯克热歇夫斯基的外交部长职务，任命拉帕茨基为外交部长并解除他的高等教育部长职务，任命尤尔克夫斯基为高等教育部长、瓦西尔科夫斯卡为司法部长。议会还批准了国务委员会关于解除雷比茨基国务委员会秘书职务、任命他为检察长的决定，同时一致选举斯克热歇夫斯基为国务委员会秘书。
