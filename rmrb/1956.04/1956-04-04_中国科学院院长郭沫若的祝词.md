Title: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Delivers Congratulatory Speech
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-04
Page: 3

The Chinese Academy of Sciences, on behalf of all Chinese scientists, extends its congratulations on the tenth anniversary of the World Federation of Scientific Workers, held in Beijing.

	Modern science is a lofty crystallization of human wisdom.

	The construction undertakings of all countries, the lasting peace of the whole world, and the happy life of all mankind must rely on the continuous development and proper application of science.

	We have now entered the age of atomic energy. The call for the peaceful utilization of atomic energy is resounding throughout the world.

	For the past ten years, the World Federation of Scientific Workers has united outstanding scientists from various countries to strive for the development of science and the welfare of scientists, and to strive for science to serve peaceful construction and human happiness, making many contributions.

	In particular, the association has made its best efforts in striving for the comprehensive peaceful utilization of atomic energy.

	The Chinese Academy of Sciences, on behalf of all Chinese scientists, is extremely willing to work together with scientists from around the world, and sincerely wishes the work of the World Federation of Scientific Workers a smoother development, and the material and cultural life of mankind a more comprehensive and rapid step-by-step improvement relying on the power of science.

	Glory forever belongs to the seekers of truth who seek happiness for all mankind!

### 中国科学院院长郭沫若的祝词

1956-04-04
第3版()
专栏：

    中国科学院院长郭沫若的祝词
    世界科学工作者协会十周年纪念大会在北京举行，中国科学院谨代表全体中国科学工作者表示庆贺。
    近代科学是人类智慧崇高的结晶。
    世界各国的建设事业、全世界的持久和平、全人类的幸福生活，都要依靠科学的不断发展与妥当应用。
    目前已经到了原子能的时代。原子能和平利用的呼声遍于全世界。
    世界科学工作者协会，十年来团结世界各国优秀的科学家，为争取科学的发展与科学家的福利，为争取科学为和平建设与人类幸福服务，作出了不少的贡献。
    尤其在争取原子能全面的和平利用方面，协会是尽了至善的努力的。
    中国科学院代表全体中国科学工作者极愿与世界科学家一起共同努力，谨祝世界科学工作者协会的工作更顺利地开展，人类的物质生活与文化生活依靠科学的力量更全面、更迅速地逐步愈益提高。
    光荣永远属于全人类谋幸福的真理探求者！
