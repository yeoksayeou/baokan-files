Title: Representatives of All Ethnic Groups in the Capital Celebrate the Establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1956-04-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: Today, more than 1,100 representatives of all ethnic groups in the capital and Tibetans in Beijing gathered at the auditorium of the Central Institute for Nationalities to warmly celebrate the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region.

    Ulanhu, Vice Premier of the State Council and Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, congratulated the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region at the meeting.

    Ping Jiesan, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, also spoke at the meeting. He said that the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region gives the Tibetan people an organ with political power to uniformly prepare for and implement national regional autonomy. It will greatly enhance the confidence and enthusiasm of the Tibetan people in building a new Tibet and building the motherland. Undoubtedly, the work of the Preparatory Committee for the Autonomous Region will receive the heartfelt support and assistance of people from all walks of life in Tibet and all ethnic groups in the motherland.

    Xie Fumin, Deputy Chairman of the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress, said that the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region shows that the unity of all ethnic groups in our country and the internal unity of the Tibetan nationality are increasingly strengthened.

    The speech by Xi Rao Jia Cuo (Tibetan), President of the Buddhist Association of China, was constantly interrupted by applause. Xi Rao Jia Cuo said that the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region marks that the Tibetan people have embarked on the dawn stage of establishing their own autonomous regime as masters of their own affairs. All ethnic groups in the country hope and deeply believe that after the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, it will further promote the unity between the motherland and the Tibetan region and within Tibet under the leadership of the Communist Party of China, the Central People's Government, and Chairman Mao, under the close unity of the Dalai Lama and Bainqen Erdini, and with the help of people of all ethnic groups and staff sent to Tibet and the People's Liberation Army stationed in Tibet. It will promote the further development of the unity of the motherland and the Tibetan region and within Tibet, and enable the Buddhist and construction undertakings in Tibet to advance at the speed of a Peng bird's flight. At the end of his speech, Xi Rao Jia Cuo, as an old Tibetan Shamen, remotely wished the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region success in its work and the early birth of the Tibetan local autonomous regime.

    Dunwang Jianzan Zhaba, Director of the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing, and Lian Huizhu, Acting Director of the Bainqen's Office in Beijing, both spoke at the celebration meeting. Dunwang Jianzan Zhaba said that every Tibetan people must sincerely welcome the cordial help of the Han brothers, and always contribute all their strength under the leadership of the Communist Party of China to build a better home, strengthen national unity, and consolidate national defense. Lian Huizhu said that the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region shows that the Tibetan people can only more closely unite around the Communist Party of China to fully realize regional autonomy and further promote the political, economic, and cultural development of the Tibetan region.

    Nanka Jia Cai, a Tibetan student from the Central Institute for Nationalities, represented all Tibetan students at the meeting, expressing his determination to study hard and work hard to build a new Tibet.

    Also attending the celebration meeting today were Liu Chun and Sa Kongliao, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, Da Puson, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association, and Ma Songting, Deputy Director of the China Hui Nationality Culture Advancement Association, and others. A banquet and evening party were also held after the meeting.

    At the banquet, representatives of students from all ethnic groups offered toasts to Vice Premier Ulanhu, Dunwang Jianzan Zhaba, Director of the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing, Lian Huizhu, Acting Director of the Bainqen's Office in Beijing, and others, congratulating the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region.

### 首都各民族代表庆祝西藏自治区筹委会成立

1956-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各民族代表庆祝西藏自治区筹委会成立
    据新华社22日讯  今天，首都各民族代表和在北京的全体藏族人士共一千一百多人，欢聚在中央民族学院大礼堂，热烈庆祝西藏自治区筹备委员会成立。
    国务院副总理、民族事务委员会主任乌兰夫在大会上祝贺西藏自治区筹委会的成立。
    中共中央统一战线工作部副部长平杰三也在会上讲话。他说，西藏自治区筹备委员会的成立，使西藏人民有了统一的筹备实施民族区域自治的带政权性质的机关，它将大大地增强藏族人民建设新西藏和建设祖国的信心和积极性。无疑地，在自治区筹备委员会工作的进行中，将会得到西藏各阶层人民和祖国各民族的衷心的支持和帮助。
    全国人民代表大会民族委员会副主任委员谢扶民讲话说，西藏自治区筹备委员会的成立，显示着我国各族人民的团结以及西藏民族内部的团结日益加强。
    中国佛教协会会长喜饶嘉错（藏族）的讲话，不断地被掌声打断。喜饶嘉错说，西藏自治区筹备委员会的成立，标志着西藏人民走上了建立自己当家作主的自治政权的黎明阶段。全国各族人民都希望并深深相信西藏自治区筹备委员会成立后，一定会进一步在中国共产党、中央人民政府和毛主席的领导下，在达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼的亲密团结下，在各族人民和派赴西藏工作人员、驻藏人民解放军的帮助下，促进祖国与西藏地方和西藏内部的团结获得进一步的发展，并使西藏的佛教和各项建设事业以鹏鸟的飞行速度前进。喜饶嘉错结束讲话时，他以藏族老沙门的身份，遥祝西藏自治区筹备委员会工作胜利，西藏地方自治政权早日诞生。
    达赖喇嘛驻京办事处处长顿旺·坚赞扎巴和班禅驻京办事处代处长联慧珠都在庆祝大会上讲了话。顿旺·坚赞扎巴说，我们每一个藏族人民要诚心诚意地欢迎汉族兄弟亲切的帮助，永远在中国共产党领导下，为建设美好的家园，为加强民族团结、巩固国防而贡献一切力量。联慧珠说，西藏自治区筹备委员会成立了，表现西藏人民只有更加团结在中国共产党的周围，才可以完满地实现区域自治和更进一步促进西藏地方政治、经济和文化向前发展。
    中央民族学院藏族学生南卡加采在会上代表全体藏族学生，表示一定努力学习、为建设新西藏而努力。
    今天参加这个庆祝大会的，还有民族事务委员会副主任刘春、萨空了，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生，中国回民文化协进会副主任马松亭等。会后还举行了宴会和晚会。
    宴会上，各民族学生代表纷纷向乌兰夫副总理和达赖喇嘛驻京办事处处长顿旺·坚赞扎巴、班禅驻京办事处代处长联慧珠等敬酒，祝贺西藏自治区筹备委员会成立。
