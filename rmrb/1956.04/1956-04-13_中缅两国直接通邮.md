Title: Direct Postal Service Between China and Burma

Author:

Date: 1956-04-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 12th. Direct postal service has been established between China and Burma. This afternoon, a plane returning to Kunming from Rangoon brought the first batch of mail from Burma to China. The first batch of mail from China to Burma was transported from Kunming to Rangoon yesterday.

### 中缅两国直接通邮

1956-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中缅两国直接通邮
    据新华社昆明12日电　中缅两国已经直接通邮。今天下午从仰光飞回昆明的客机带来了缅甸寄到中国的第一批邮件。由中国寄往缅甸的第一批邮件已在昨天由昆明运到仰光。
