Title: Singapore Legislative Assembly Passes Resolution Demanding Immediate Independence
Author:
Date: 1956-04-07
Page: 4

    Singapore Legislative Assembly
    Passes Resolution Demanding Immediate Independence
    Xinhua News Agency, Singapore, 6th – Singapore news: The Singapore Legislative Assembly unanimously passed a resolution on April 5th, demanding that the delegation going to Britain to negotiate Singapore's future status secure Singapore's immediate independence.
    The resolution expressed support for the proposal put forward by Singapore's Chief Minister Marshall, requesting Singapore to obtain independence within the Commonwealth.
    During the debate, People's Action Party leader Lee Kuan Yew supported Marshall's position against adopting a "go slow" attitude on the issue of independence. Lee Kuan Yew also advocated that the process of replacing important positions in the Singapore government with Malayan personnel should be accelerated.

### 新加坡立法议会  通过决议要求立刻独立

1956-04-07
第4版()
专栏：

    新加坡立法议会
    通过决议要求立刻独立
    新华社6日讯　新加坡消息：新加坡立法议会在四月五日一致通过决议，要求即将前往英国谈判新加坡未来地位的代表团要取得新加坡的即刻独立。
    决议表示支持新加坡首席部长马歇尔所提出的要求新加坡取得在英联邦内的独立的提案。
    在进行辩论的时候，人民行动党领袖李光耀支持马歇尔反对在独立问题上采取
  “慢慢来”的态度的立场。李光耀还主张，新加坡政府中的重要职位改由马来亚人担任的过程应当加快实行。
