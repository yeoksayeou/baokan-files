Title: Czechoslovak Film Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 4

    Czechoslovak Film Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    A six-member Czechoslovak film workers delegation, led by head of the delegation Jiří Mařík, arrived in Beijing on the 29th. They are visiting China at the invitation of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China to participate in the "May Day" celebrations and the "Czechoslovak Republic Film Week" activities held in China.

### 捷电影工作者代表团到京

1956-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    捷电影工作者代表团到京
    捷克斯洛伐克电影工作者代表团一行六人，在团长伊里·马里克率领下，在二十九日到达北京。他们是应中华人民共和国文化部邀请前来中国访问，并且参加 “五一”节观礼和在中国举行的“捷克斯洛伐克共和国电影周”活动的。
