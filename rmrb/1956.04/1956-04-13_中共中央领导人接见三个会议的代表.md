Title: Central Committee Leaders of the Chinese Communist Party Meet with Representatives of Three Conferences
Author:
Date: 1956-04-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Chairman Mao Zedong and members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Peng Zhen, Kang Sheng, and Deng Xiaoping, successively received all the representatives attending the National Coal Mine Advanced Producers Representative Conference, the National Textile Industry Advanced Producers Representative Conference, and the National Mechanical Industry Advanced Producers Representative Conference on the afternoon of the 12th.

### 中共中央领导人接见三个会议的代表

1956-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见三个会议的代表
    新华社讯　毛泽东主席和中共中央政治局委员刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、陈云、彭真、康生、邓小平，在十二日下午先后接见了出席全国煤矿先进生产者代表会议、全国纺织工业先进生产者代表会议和全国机械工业先进生产者代表会议的全体代表。
