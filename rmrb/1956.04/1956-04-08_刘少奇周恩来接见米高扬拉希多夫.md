Title: Liu Shaoqi and Zhou Enlai Receive Mikoyan and Rashidov
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th - Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council, received Mikoyan, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist of the Soviet Union, and Rashidov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Uzbek Republic, this afternoon.

### 刘少奇周恩来接见米高扬拉希多夫

1956-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇周恩来接见米高扬拉希多夫
    新华社7日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇和国务院总理周恩来，在今天下午接见了苏联部长会议第一副主席、苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员米高扬和乌兹别克共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席拉希多夫。
