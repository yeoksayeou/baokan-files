Title: Egypt and Israel Agree to Unconditional Ceasefire
Author:
Date: 1956-04-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 20th: According to a Reuters report, United Nations Secretary-General Dag Hammarskjöld announced on April 19th that Egypt and Israel had agreed to an unconditional ceasefire on their borders, effective from 6:00 PM on April 18th.

Hammarskjöld's statement was issued through the headquarters of the United Nations Palestine Truce Commission and simultaneously released in Cairo, Jerusalem, and at the United Nations headquarters in New York.

Prior to this, Hammarskjöld had held talks with government leaders from Egypt, Lebanon, and Israel. Hammarskjöld visited various Middle Eastern countries based on a recent resolution passed by the United Nations Security Council. This resolution charged him with conducting investigations in the Palestinian region, exploring measures to ease tensions, and reporting to the Security Council.

### 埃及和以色列无条件停火

1956-04-21
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及和以色列无条件停火
    据新华社20日讯  据路透社报道，联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德四月十九日宣布，埃及和以色列已经同意从四月十八日下午六时起，在它们的边界上无条件停火。
    哈马舍尔德的声明是通过联合国巴勒斯坦停战委员会总部发表的，同时在开罗、耶路撒冷和纽约联合国总部发表。
    在这以前，哈马舍尔德曾经先后同埃及、黎巴嫩和以色列的政府领导人举行了会谈。哈马舍尔德是根据联合国安全理事会最近通过的决议访问中东各国的。这个决议责成他前往巴勒斯坦地区进行调查，探讨可能缓和紧张局势的措施，向安全理事会提出报告。
