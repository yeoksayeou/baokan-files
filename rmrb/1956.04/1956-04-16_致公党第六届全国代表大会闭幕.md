Title: Zhigong Party Sixth National Congress Concludes

Author:
Date: 1956-04-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 15th: The Sixth National Congress of the China Zhi Gong Party was held in Beijing from the 5th to the 14th.

The delegates reviewed the work of the past three years since the Fifth National Congress of the China Zhi Gong Party in November 1952, discussed the work report of the Central Committee, and adopted a resolution on future guidelines and tasks based on this report. After discussing the explanation of the amendments to the constitution of the China Zhi Gong Party, the meeting adopted the new constitution.

At the last meeting of the congress, the delegates elected 34 central committee members and 5 alternate central committee members to form the new central committee.

The delegates studied and discussed Premier Zhou Enlai's political report at the Second Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and other documents. Fang Fang, Deputy Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, attended the meeting on the afternoon of the 5th and made a report to the delegates.

The delegates unanimously adopted a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao, pledging to forge ahead on the people's democratic front led by the Communist Party of China and strive for the socialist construction of our country.

The United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee held a reception on the 9th to welcome all the delegates attending the congress.

The Sixth Central Committee of the China Zhi Gong Party held its first plenary session on the 15th, electing Chen Qiyou as Chairman of the Central Committee, Guan Wensen as Vice Chairman, and appointing Zheng Tianbao as Secretary-General and Yan Xichun as Deputy Secretary-General.

### 致公党第六届全国代表大会闭幕

1956-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    致公党第六届全国代表大会闭幕
    新华社15日讯　中国致公党第六届全国代表大会从五日到十四日在北京举行。
    代表们检查了自1952年11月致公党第五届全国代表大会后三年来的工作，讨论了中央委员会的党务报告，并且根据这个报告通过了关于今后方针任务的决议。会议在讨论了修改致公党的章程的说明后，通过新的章程。
    在大会的最后一次会议上，代表们选出了中央委员三十四人，候补中央委员五人组成新的中央委员会。
    代表大会学习和讨论了周恩来总理在中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第二次全体会议上的政治报告等文件。华侨事务委员会副主任方方在五日下午到会向代表们作了报告。
    代表们一致通过了向毛主席致敬电，保证要在以中国共产党为领导的人民民主战线上勇往直前，为我国社会主义建设事业努力奋斗。
    中共中央统一战线工作部在九日举行酒会，欢迎参加这次大会的全体代表。
    中国致公党第六届中央委员会在十五日举行了第一次全体会议，选出陈其尤为中央委员会主席，官文森为副主席，并且推举郑天保为秘书长，严希纯为副秘书长。
