    “Strive for Lasting Peace, Strive for People's Democracy!” Publishes "On the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat"
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 14th -- Bucharest Dispatch: The "Strive for Lasting Peace, Strive for People's Democracy!" published on April 13th carried the article "On the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat" written by the editorial department of the Chinese "People's Daily."
