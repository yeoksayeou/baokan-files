Title: World Federation of Scientific Workers Executive Council Begins Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-04-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 1st. The 16th Executive Council meeting of the World Federation of Scientific Workers began in Beijing at 9:00 am today.
