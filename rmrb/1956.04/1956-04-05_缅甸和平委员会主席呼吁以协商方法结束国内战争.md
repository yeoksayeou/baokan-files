Title: Chairman of the Burmese Peace Committee Calls for Negotiated End to Civil War
Author:
Date: 1956-04-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 4th: According to the "People's Daily" of Mandalay on April 4th, Thakin Kodaw Hmaing, Chairman of the Burmese World Peace Defense Committee, issued an appeal calling for a negotiated end to the domestic war.

    This appeal, addressed to the country's senior monks, religious leaders, political parties, mass organizations, social celebrities, and all peace-loving people of the country, calls on the people of the whole country to redouble their efforts to strive for domestic peace, and calls on the Burmese government and underground armed forces to promptly conduct peace negotiations to satisfy the people's desire for peace.

    The "Yangon Daily" also published an article on April 2nd calling for the realization of domestic peace through talks, so that Burma can develop economic construction under good conditions.

### 缅甸和平委员会主席呼吁以协商方法结束国内战争

1956-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸和平委员会主席呼吁以协商方法结束国内战争
    新华社仰光4日电　据曼德勒“人民报”四月四日报道，缅甸保卫世界和平委员会主席德钦哥都迈发表了一项呼吁书，要求以协商的方法结束国内的战争。
    这项向全国高僧、宗教界领袖、各政党、各群众团体、社会名流和全国爱好和平的人民发出的呼吁书要求全国人民加倍努力争取国内的和平，并且呼吁缅甸政府和地下武装力量迅速进行和平谈判，以满足人民要求和平的愿望。
    “仰光日报”也在四月二日发表文章要求通过会谈来实现国内的和平，以使缅甸在良好的条件下发展经济建设。
