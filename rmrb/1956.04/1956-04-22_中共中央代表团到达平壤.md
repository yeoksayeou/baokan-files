Title: CPC Central Committee Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1956-04-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 21st. Nie Rongzhen, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China (CPC) and head of the CPC Central Committee Delegation, and Wang Congwu, alternate member of the CPC Central Committee and member of the delegation, arrived in Pyongyang by train on the evening of April 21st to attend the Third Congress of the Workers' Party of Korea.

Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, Vice-Chairmen Park Jeong-ae and Park Kim-cheol, Central Committee members Park Chang-ok and Choi Chang-ik, and Vice-Premier of the Cabinet Park Ui-wan were among those who welcomed the delegation at the station.

Qiao Xiaoguang, member of the CPC Central Committee Delegation and Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, also welcomed the delegation at the station.

### 中共中央代表团到达平壤

1956-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央代表团到达平壤
    新华社平壤21日电  前来参加朝鲜劳动党第三次代表大会的中国共产党中央委员会代表团团长、中国共产党中央委员会委员聂荣臻和代表团团员、中国共产党中央委员会候补委员王从吾，四月二十一日晚间乘车抵达平壤。
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长金日成，副委员长朴正爱、朴金喆，中央委员朴昌玉、崔昌益，内阁副首相朴义琓等到车站欢迎。
    中共中央代表团团员、中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光也到车站欢迎。
