Title: The 24th Congress of the Communist Party of Great Britain Concludes

Author: 
Date: 1956-04-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, April 3rd: The 24th Congress of the Communist Party of Great Britain concluded on April 2nd, according to TASS.

    On April 1st, the Congress heard and discussed a report by George Matthews, Deputy General Secretary of the Communist Party of Great Britain, on "The Communist Party and Youth." The report put forward the following demands: immediately shorten the term of compulsory military service, stop colonial wars and strengthen friendship between young people of all countries, increase wages and improve the working conditions of young workers, improve the education system and ensure more opportunities for exercise and rest.

    At a closed meeting held on this day, the Congress heard a report by the General Secretary of the Party, Harry Pollitt, on the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. The closed meeting of the Congress approved this report and expressed complete trust in the Soviet Union, the Soviet people, and the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. The Congress expressed its belief that the great prospect of advancing towards communism, reflected in the entire policy of the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, will be fully realized.

    The closed meeting of the Congress also heard a report from the Central Auditing Commission and elected a new Executive Committee by secret ballot.

    At the closing meeting on April 2nd, the Congress unanimously approved a general political resolution clarifying the party's policies and demands. This resolution included the basic content of the political report made by the General Secretary of the Party, Harry Pollitt, and took into account the revisions and supplements to the draft resolution made by local party organizations. Pollitt's political report was also unanimously approved by the Congress.

    The Congress successively adopted many proposals put forward by party organizations in various regions, which called for consolidating the unity of the workers' movement and improving the situation in housing construction and agriculture. The Congress also adopted a resolution calling for actively launching a campaign to shorten the term of compulsory military service. The Congress unanimously passed a resolution expressing support for the residents of Cyprus who are fighting for the right to self-determination.

    Campbell, the chief writer of the "Daily Worker," delivered the closing speech to the Congress. He emphasized that after the conclusion of the Congress, the Communist Party of Great Britain will be more consolidated, and the members of the Communist Party of Great Britain will continue to contribute their strength to the cause of the working class in the future.

    When the Congress closed, everyone sang "The Internationale."

### 英国共产党第二十四次代表大会闭幕

1956-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    英国共产党第二十四次代表大会闭幕
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社伦敦讯：英国共产党第二十四次代表大会在四月二日闭幕。
    大会在四月一日听取和讨论了英国共产党副总书记乔治·马修斯关于“共产党和青年”的报告。在报告中提出了下列的要求：立刻缩短义务兵役期限，停止殖民战争和加强各国青年之间的友谊，增加工资和改善劳动青年的劳动条件，改善教育制度和保证有更多的运动和休息机会。
    代表大会在这一天举行的秘密会议上听取了党的总书记波立特关于苏联共产党第二十次代表大会的报告。代表大会的秘密会议批准了这个报告，并且对苏联、苏联人民和苏联共产党表示完全信任。代表大会表示相信，在苏联共产党第二十次代表大会的整个政策中所反映的向共产主义前进的伟大远景将会完全实现。
    大会的秘密会议还听取了中央监察委员会的报告，并且以秘密投票的方式选出了新的执行委员会。
    代表大会在四月二日的闭幕会议上一致赞同阐明党的政策和要求的总的政治决议。这个决议包括党的总书记波立特在政治报告中所提出的基本内容，并且考虑了各地方党组织对决议草案的修正和补充。波立特的政治报告也得到大会一致赞同。
    代表大会先后通过了各地区党组织所提出的许多提案，这些提案要求巩固工人运动的团结，要求改善住房建筑和农业方面的情况。大会还通过了号召积极展开争取缩短义务兵役期限的运动的决议。代表大会一致通过决议，表示支持为争取自决权而斗争的塞浦路斯居民。
      “工人日报”主笔坎贝尔向代表大会致闭幕词。他强调说，在代表大会结束以后，英国共产党将更加巩固，英国共产党党员今后仍然要把自己的力量贡献给工人阶级的事业。
    代表大会闭幕时，全体高唱“国际歌”。
