Title: The State Council Appoints Yuan Zhongxian as Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs
Author:
Date: 1956-04-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The State Council of the People's Republic of China has appointed Yuan Zhongxian as Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs.

    This appointment was passed at the 22nd plenary session of the State Council held on January 23rd.

### 国务院任命袁仲贤为外交部副部长

1956-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院任命袁仲贤为外交部副部长
    新华社8日讯　中华人民共和国国务院任命袁仲贤为外交部副部长。
    这项任命是在一月二十三日举行的国务院第二十二次全体会议上通过的。
