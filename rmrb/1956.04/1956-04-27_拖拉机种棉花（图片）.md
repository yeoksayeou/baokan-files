Title: Tractor Planting Cotton (Picture)
Author: Zhang Ruihua
Date: 1956-04-27
Page: 2

    Tractor Planting Cotton
    The Xizhanggeng Agricultural Machine Tractor Station in Jieyu County, Shanxi Province, responded to the initiative of the four tractor stations in Beijing, Changzhi, Shenyang, and Huachuan regarding the socialist labor competition to improve operation quality, save funds, and ensure increased production. During the spring plowing season, they launched a labor competition between teams. This is the first team of the Xizhanggeng Agricultural Machine Tractor Station sowing cotton for the Xinghuo Agricultural Cooperative.
        Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zhang Ruihua

### 拖拉机种棉花（图片）
张瑞华
1956-04-27
第2版()
专栏：

    拖拉机种棉花
    山西省解虞县西张耿农业机器拖拉机站响应北京、长治、沈阳、桦川等四个拖拉机站关于提高作业质量、节约资金、保证增产的社会主义劳动竞赛的倡议，在春耕季节里，展开了队和队间的劳动竞赛。这是西张耿农业机器拖拉机站第一队正在给星火农业合作社播种棉花。
        新华社记者  张瑞华摄
