Title: My Newly Appointed Ambassador to North Korea Presents Credentials to Chairman Kim Tu-bong
Author:
Date: 1956-04-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 17th: China's newly appointed ambassador to North Korea, Qiao Xiaoguang, accompanied by all diplomatic officials of the embassy, presented his credentials to Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea, at 12:00 noon on April 16th.

    During the presentation of credentials, Ambassador Qiao Xiaoguang delivered a congratulatory speech, saying that the great victory of the Korean armistice and the significant efforts made by the Democratic People's Republic of Korea over the past two years to maintain peace in the Korean region have had a significant impact on easing tensions in Asia and the world.

    Qiao Xiaoguang said that the Chinese people sincerely hope that the Korean people will achieve new and great victories in their great cause of realizing the peaceful reunification of their motherland and laying the socialist foundation of the country.

    Chairman Kim Tu-bong said in his reply that the historical friendly relations between the peoples of North Korea and China have been further strengthened in the common struggle against foreign imperialist aggressors, and have been further consolidated and developed due to the agreement on economic and cultural cooperation concluded between our two countries.

    Kim Tu-bong said that the Korean people fully support the People's Republic of China's peaceful diplomatic policy for the easing of international tensions and the security of all peoples, and the Chinese people's just struggle for the liberation of Taiwan. I wish the fraternal Chinese people brilliant achievements in the struggle to complete and overfulfill the first five-year plan for the socialist construction of their motherland.

### 我新任驻朝鲜大使向金枓奉委员长递交国书

1956-04-18
第4版()
专栏：

    我新任驻朝鲜大使向金枓奉委员长递交国书
    据新华社平壤17日电　中国新任驻朝鲜大使乔晓光四月十六日中午十二时偕大使馆全体外交官员，向朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉递交国书。
    在递交国书时，乔晓光大使致颂词说，朝鲜停战的伟大胜利和两年多来朝鲜民主主义人民共和国为维护朝鲜地区的和平所作的重大努力，对亚洲和世界紧张局势的缓和有着重大的作用。
    乔晓光说，中国人民衷心地希望朝鲜人民在为实现祖国的和平统一、为奠定国家的社会主义基础的伟大事业中获得新的、巨大的胜利。
    金枓奉委员长在答词中说，朝中两国人民之间所结成的历史性的友好关系在反对外国帝国主义侵略者的共同斗争中进一步加强了，并且由于我们两国缔结了关于经济及文化合作的协定，得到进一步的巩固和发展。
    金枓奉说，朝鲜人民完全支持中华人民共和国为了国际紧张局势的和缓和各国人民的安全的和平外交政策和中国人民为解放台湾而进行的正义斗争。谨祝兄弟般的中国人民在为完成和超额完成自己祖国的社会主义建设的第一个五年计划的斗争中取得光辉的成就。
