Title: World Peace Council Special Meeting Concludes

Author:

Date: 1956-04-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, April 10th. TASS reports: The Special Session of the World Peace Council has concluded. The closing meeting began on April 9th at 23:00 local time, chaired by Iturraspe (Argentina).

    At the beginning of the meeting, on the recommendation of World Peace Council Secretary Jones, the new list of World Peace Council members was approved. Subsequently, the meeting participants unanimously adopted the following documents: a telegram to the United Nations Disarmament Commission Subcommittee, an appeal to world public opinion, the World Peace Council Special Meeting's declaration on cooperation with all peace forces, a declaration on economic, social, and cultural issues, a declaration on atomic energy issues, and a recommendation to national peace movements. In addition, a joint statement by the Greek and British representatives participating in the special meeting was adopted, calling for support for the Cypriot residents' demands for self-determination. The World Peace Council called on men and women of all countries to support this demand, thereby promoting the easing of international tensions.

    Iturraspe pointed out at the close of the meeting that the spirit of cooperation among all peace-loving people has strengthened recently, and their views have converged. He said that methods must be sought to further expand this cooperation. We have expressed our desire to avoid the scourge of war and the arms race, and we believe that in close cooperation with other peace-loving forces we can achieve our goals. We have announced the beginning of a new stage in our movement, a movement that will lead to the establishment of peace and the happiness of all peoples.

### 世界和平理事会特别会议闭幕

1956-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会特别会议闭幕
    新华社10日讯　塔斯社斯德哥尔摩讯：世界和平理事会特别会议已经闭幕。闭幕会议在四月九日当地时间二十三时开始，由伊图拉斯佩（阿根廷）担任主席。
    在会议开始的时候，根据世界和平理事会书记琼斯的建议，批准了世界和平理事会新的理事名单。随后，会议参加者一致通过了下列文件：致联合国裁军委员会小组委员会的电报、告世界舆论书、世界和平理事会特别会议关于同一切和平力量合作的宣言、关于经济、社会和文化问题的宣言、关于原子能问题的宣言以及致各国和平运动建议书。此外，还通过了参加这次特别会议的希腊和英国代表的联合声明，号召支持塞浦路斯居民对自决权的要求。世界和平理事会号召世界各国男女支持这个要求，从而促进国际紧张局势的缓和。
    伊图拉斯佩在会议闭幕的时候指出，最近一切爱好和平的人民之间的合作精神加强了，他们的观点有了接近。他说，必须要寻求出方法，以便进一步扩大这种合作。我们表示了要避免战争灾祸和军备竞赛的愿望，我们相信，在同其他爱好和平力量的密切合作中我们能够达到自己的目标。我们宣布了我们运动的新阶段的开始，我们的运动将会导致建立和平和各国人民的幸福。
