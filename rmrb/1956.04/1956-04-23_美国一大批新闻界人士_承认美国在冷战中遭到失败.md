Title: A Large Group of American Journalists Admit the United States Has Failed in the Cold War
Author:
Date: 1956-04-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd dispatch from Washington: A large group of American journalists are deeply convinced that the United States is failing in the Cold War. At the meeting of the American Society of Newspaper Editors and the alumni associations of American journalism schools held on the 21st, participants voted by a two-to-one margin that they agreed with the view that the United States is failing in the Cold War. At the same time, these American reporters and editors also admitted that the Soviet Union's current policies have reduced the possibility of war.

### 美国一大批新闻界人士  承认美国在冷战中遭到失败

1956-04-23
第4版()
专栏：今日美国

    美国一大批新闻界人士
    承认美国在冷战中遭到失败
    新华社22日讯  据法新社华盛顿消息：一大批美国新闻界人士深信美国正在冷战中遭到失败。在二十一日举行的美国报纸编辑协会和美国各新闻学校校友会会议上，参加的人以二比一的票数表示同意美国正在冷战中遭到失败的看法。同时，这些美国记者和编辑还承认苏联目前的政策减少了战争的可能性。
