Title: Arab League Council Actively Supports the Struggle Against Colonialism
Author:
Date: 1956-04-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 13th: The Arab League Council meeting was held in Cairo from March 29th to April 12th. The League's Political Committee and other standing specialized committees also held meetings at the same time.

    Delegations from all nine member states of the League participated in the work of the League Council and its committees.

    The Arab League Council passed resolutions recognizing the independence of Tunisia and Morocco. Members of the Council expressed their readiness to provide these countries with assistance so that they could fully realize their national aspirations.

    Newspapers reported that the Political Committee condemned the actions of the French authorities in Algeria and expressed support for the Algerian people's struggle for liberation.

    The Political Committee also discussed the issue of UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld's mission to the Near East.

    The Political Committee discussed the British seizure of the Imamate of Oman and decided to send an Arab League delegation to the Imamate of Oman and Muscat. The Council approved this decision.

    Newspapers reported that the League's Political Committee passed a resolution stating that the Arab League countries should recognize the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    The Council recommended commemorating the first anniversary of the Asian-African Conference on April 18th, treating this day as a day of peace and cooperation among the people of all countries, a day for the struggle for freedom and the right to self-determination of all peoples, and a symbol of universal peace.

### 阿拉伯联盟理事会议积极支持反殖民主义的斗争

1956-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯联盟理事会议积极支持反殖民主义的斗争
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社开罗十三日讯：阿拉伯联盟理事会会议从三月二十九日到四月十二日在开罗举行。联盟的政治委员会和其他常设专门委员会也同时举行会议。
    参加联盟理事会和各委员会工作的有联盟所有九个成员国的代表团。
    阿拉伯联盟理事会通过了关于承认突尼斯和摩洛哥独立的决议。理事会的成员表示准备给这些国家以援助，使它们完全实现自己的民族愿望。
    报纸报道，政治委员会谴责法国当局在阿尔及利亚的行动，并且表示支持阿尔及利亚人民的解放斗争。
    政治委员会也讨论了关于联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德来中近东的使命的问题。
    政治委员会讨论了英国强夺阿曼酋长国的问题，并且决定派阿拉伯联盟代表团到阿曼和穆斯喀特两个酋长国去。理事会赞同这个决定。
    报纸报道，联盟的政治委员会通过决议，认为阿拉伯联盟各国应当承认越南民主共和国。
    理事会建议在四月十八日纪念亚非会议一周年，把这一天当作各国人民和平和合作的、为自由和各国人民的自决权而斗争的日子和普遍和平的象征。
