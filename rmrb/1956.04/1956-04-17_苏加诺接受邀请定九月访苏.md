Title: Sukarno Accepts Invitation to Visit Soviet Union in September
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 15th: TASS reported that Soviet Ambassador to Indonesia, Mr. Zhukov, on behalf of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and Mr. Voroshilov personally, invited the President of the Republic of Indonesia, Mr. Sukarno, to pay an official visit to the Soviet Union a few days ago.

President Sukarno accepted the invitation and expressed his readiness to depart at the end of August this year and visit the Soviet Union in the first half of September.

### 苏加诺接受邀请定九月访苏

1956-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

    苏加诺接受邀请定九月访苏
    新华社16日讯　塔斯社莫斯科十五日讯：苏联驻印度尼西亚大使茹科夫前几天代表苏联最高苏维埃主席团和伏罗希洛夫本人，邀请印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺先生正式访问苏联。
    苏加诺总统接受了这个邀请，并且表示准备在今年八月底动身，九月上半月在苏联进行访问。
