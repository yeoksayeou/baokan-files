Title: U.S. Seventh Fleet Exercises in Waters off Okinawa

Author: 
Date: 1956-04-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 18th: The U.S. Seventh Fleet began a three-day task force exercise in the waters near Okinawa on the 17th. The mission of the U.S. Seventh Fleet is to carry out armed intervention in the Chinese people's liberation of Taiwan. According to a communique issued by the Seventh Fleet, the purpose of the exercise is to "maintain a high degree of readiness at all times" for the Seventh Fleet.

### 美第七舰队在冲绳岛海面演习

1956-04-19
第4版()
专栏：

    美第七舰队在冲绳岛海面演习
    据新华社18日讯　美国第七舰队从十七日开始在冲绳岛附近海面举行三天特遣舰队演习。美国第七舰队所担负的任务是武装干涉中国人民解放台湾。据第七舰队发表的公报说，演习的目的是所谓使第七舰队“经常保持高度的准备”。
