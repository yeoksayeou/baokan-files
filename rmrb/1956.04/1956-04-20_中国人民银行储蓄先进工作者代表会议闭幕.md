Title: Closing of the Chinese People's Bank Savings Advanced Workers Representative Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-04-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report: The Chinese People's Bank Savings Advanced Workers Representative Conference closed on the 18th.

    The representatives attending the conference issued a letter of proposal to savings workers across the country. They proposed: extensively launch advanced worker movements; implement the four principles of "in-depth publicity, voluntary deposit and withdrawal, convenience, enthusiastic service to depositors, and prompt, accurate, and orderly handling of transactions" in savings work; and strive to complete the task of adding 5,000 savings offices and 15,000 savings agency offices ahead of schedule this year.

    At the conference, the President of the People's Bank of China, Cao Juru, and Vice President, Hu Jingyun, both gave reports.

    The conference began on the 5th. During the conference, the representatives extensively exchanged experiences and comprehensively summarized the experience of six aspects of work: savings management, investigation and research, publicity, savings offices, agency offices, and accounting and cashier. The representatives of advanced savings workers and advanced units attending the conference were rewarded at the meeting.

### 中国人民银行储蓄先进工作者代表会议闭幕

1956-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民银行储蓄先进工作者代表会议闭幕
    据新华社讯　中国人民银行储蓄先进工作者代表会议在十八日闭幕。
    出席这次会议的代表向全国储蓄工作者提出了一份倡议书。他们倡议：广泛开展先进工作者运动；在储蓄工作中贯彻执行“深入宣传，存款取款自愿、方便，热心为储户服务，办事迅速，不错不乱”等四项原则；争取提前完成今年增设五千个储蓄所、一万五千个储蓄代办所的任务等。
    在会议上，中国人民银行行长曹菊如、副行长胡景澐都作了报告。
    这次会议从五日开始。会议期间，代表们广泛交流了经验，全面地总结了储蓄管理、调查研究、宣传、储蓄所、代办所、会计出纳等六项工作经验。出席会议的储蓄先进工作者和先进单位代表，会上都受到了奖励。
