Title: Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Holds Meeting and Approves Decision on Handling Japanese War Criminals
Author:
Date: 1956-04-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 25th — The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 34th session on the morning of the 25th.

　　The meeting, after hearing a report by Zhang Dingcheng, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, on the investigation of war criminals in custody from the Japanese war of aggression against China, approved the decision on the handling of war criminals in custody from the Japanese war of aggression against China.

　　The meeting also adopted and approved several appointment matters.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议  通过处理日本战犯的决定

1956-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议
    通过处理日本战犯的决定
    新华社25日讯  全国人民代表大会常务委员会在二十五日上午举行第三十四次会议。
    会议在听了最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞关于侦查在押的日本侵略中国战争中的战争犯罪分子的情况的报告后，通过了关于处理在押日本侵略中国战争中战争犯罪分子的决定。
    会议还通过和批准了任命事项多起。
