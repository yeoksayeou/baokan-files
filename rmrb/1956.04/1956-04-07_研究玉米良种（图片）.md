    Studying Improved Corn Varieties
    The high-yield crop experiment group of the Youth Production Team of the Wo Niu No. 1 Agricultural Production Cooperative, Longshan Township, Yuzhong County, Gansu Province, is studying and comparing improved corn varieties, preparing to trial-plant them in the high-yield crop experimental area this year.
