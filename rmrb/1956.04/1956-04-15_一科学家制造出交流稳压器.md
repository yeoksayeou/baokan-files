Title: A Scientist Creates AC Voltage Stabilizer
Author:
Date: 1956-04-15
Page: 3

    A Scientist Creates AC Voltage Stabilizer
    Huang Wuhan, a researcher at the Institute of Physics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, has designed and manufactured an AC voltage stabilizer.

    The AC voltage stabilizer is specifically used to stabilize AC voltage. It can be widely used in automatic control equipment in modern industry, communication equipment in telecommunications departments, broadcasting equipment in rural broadcasting networks, and various precision electronic instruments in laboratories.

    Huang Wuhan demonstrated the use of this instrument to reporters. Regardless of any voltage fluctuations in the input power supply ranging from 130 volts to 280 volts, the voltage can be automatically fixed at around 220 volts after being stabilized by this instrument.
　　　(Xinhua News Agency)

### 一科学家制造出交流稳压器

1956-04-15
第3版()
专栏：

    一科学家制造出交流稳压器
    中国科学院物理研究所研究员黄武汉设计、制造了一架交流稳压器。
    交流稳压器是专门用来稳定交流电电压的。它可以广泛地在近代工业的自动控制设备、电讯部门通讯设备、农村广播网的广播设备以及实验室的各种精密电子学仪器上应用。
    黄武汉为记者表演了这种仪器的使用情况，不论输入的电源从130伏特到280伏特范围内的任何电压变动情况下，经过这种仪器来稳定电压，就能使电源自动地固定在220伏特左右。
　　　（据新华社讯）
