Title: China and Cambodia Sign Trade Agreement and Payment Agreement; Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Cambodian Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-04-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 24th—After the arrival of the Economic Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia in Beijing, it held friendly talks with the Ministry of Foreign Trade of our country. The two parties signed the Trade Agreement and Payment Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Kingdom of Cambodia in Beijing on the afternoon of April 24.
    Representing the Chinese side in signing the agreement was Ye Ji Zhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China. Representing the Cambodian side in signing the agreement was Hu Sen’a, head of the Economic Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia and Minister of Engineering, Posts and Telecommunications and Planning.
    Participating in the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were Li Zheren and Lei Renmin, Vice Ministers of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China; Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Qiao Peixin, Vice President of the People's Bank of China; and relevant directors and bureau chiefs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Ministry of Foreign Trade.
    Participating in the signing ceremony on the Cambodian side were members of the Economic Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia: Li Luomu, Director of the National Postal and Telecommunications Bureau and former minister; Mao Sai, member of parliament and former minister; and Li Jinxing, secretary of the delegation.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 24th—Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China hosted a banquet this evening in honor of Hu Sen’a, head of the Economic Delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia, and members Li Luomu, Mao Sai, and Secretary Li Jinxing.
    Present as guests were Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Ye Ji Zhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade; Li Zheren and Lei Renmin, Vice Ministers; Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications; Xue Muqiao, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission; Qiao Peixin, Vice President of the People's Bank of China, and others.

### 中柬两国签订贸易协定和支付协定  周总理招待柬埔寨经济代表团

1956-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中柬两国签订贸易协定和支付协定
    周总理招待柬埔寨经济代表团
    新华社24日讯  柬埔寨王国访华经济代表团到达北京后，同我国对外贸易部进行了友好的商谈，双方于四月二十四日下午在北京签订了中华人民共和国和柬埔寨王国两国政府贸易协定和支付协定。
    代表中国方面在协定上签字的是中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮，代表柬埔寨方面在协定上签字的是柬埔寨王国访华经济代表团团长、工程、邮电和计划部部长胡森阿。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，有中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长李哲人、雷任民，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中国人民银行副行长乔培新及外交部、对外贸易部有关司长、局长等。
    柬埔寨方面参加签字仪式的有：柬埔寨王国访华经济代表团团员：全国邮电局局长、前部长李洛穆，议员、前部长毛赛，以及代表团秘书李金兴。
    新华社24日讯  中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天晚间举行宴会，招待柬埔寨王国访华经济代表团团长胡森阿，团员李洛穆、毛赛，秘书李金兴。
    出席作陪的，有国务院秘书长习仲勋，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，副部长李哲人、雷任民，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，邮电部部长朱学范，国家计划委员会副主任薛暮桥，中国人民银行副行长乔培新等人。
