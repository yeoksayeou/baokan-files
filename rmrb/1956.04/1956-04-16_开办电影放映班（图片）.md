Opening a Movie Projection Class
In order to invigorate rural cultural life, the Liaoning Provincial Cultural Cadre Training Class has opened a movie projection class. The picture shows teacher Wang Mingluan (right) teaching students how to operate a projector. Photo by Liu Baocheng (Draft from Liaoning Daily)
