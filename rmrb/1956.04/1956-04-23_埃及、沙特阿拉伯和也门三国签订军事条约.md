Title: Egypt, Saudi Arabia, and Yemen Sign Military Treaty
Author:
Date: 1956-04-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 22nd: According to Cairo Radio, Egypt, Saudi Arabia, and Yemen signed a military treaty in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia, on April 21st.

The broadcast stated that representing the three countries in signing the military treaty were Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser, Saudi Arabian King Saud, and Yemeni King Ahmad.

The broadcast said that the leaders of these three Arab nations subsequently held an important meeting.

The broadcast said that a communique announcing the signing of the military treaty stated that the heads of state of the three countries also exchanged views on plans to guarantee the development of economic, cultural, and technological cooperation between the three countries. They discussed consolidating cooperation between them for the sake of the Arab cause and the prosperity of the Arab world.

Prior to this, Egypt had already signed defense treaties with Syria and Egypt with Saudi Arabia respectively.

### 埃及、沙特阿拉伯和也门三国签订军事条约

1956-04-23
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及、沙特阿拉伯和也门三国签订军事条约
    新华社22日讯  开罗消息：据开罗电台广播，埃及、沙特阿拉伯和也门四月二十一日在沙特阿拉伯的吉达签订了一项军事条约。
    广播说，代表三国在军事条约上签字的是埃及总理纳赛尔、沙特阿拉伯国王沙特和也门国王艾哈迈德。
    广播说，这三个阿拉伯国家的领袖随后还举行了一个重要的会议。
    广播说，宣布签订军事条约的一项公报说，这三国首脑还曾经就保证发展三国之间的经济、文化和技术合作的计划交换了意见。他们讨论了为了阿拉伯事业和阿拉伯世界的繁荣而巩固他们之间的合作问题。
    在这以前，埃及同叙利亚、埃及同沙特阿拉伯已经分别签订了防御条约。
