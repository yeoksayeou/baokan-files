Title: Indian and Finnish Newspapers Publish Historical Experiences of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat
Author:
Date: 1956-04-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 13th: The latest issue of the Indian Communist Party's organ "New Age" weekly published in full the editorial by the "People's Daily" of China entitled "On the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat."
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 13th: Helsinki News: On April 12th, the Finnish Communist Party's organ "People's Paper" and Helsinki's largest newspaper "Helsinki Sanomat" published in full the article "On the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat" written by the editorial department of the Chinese "People's Daily." "Free Press" published a summary of this article on the same day.

### 印度和芬兰报刊登载关于无产阶级专政的历史经验

1956-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    印度和芬兰报刊登载关于无产阶级专政的历史经验
    新华社新德里13日电　最近一期的印度共产党机关刊“新世纪”周报全文刊载了中国“人民日报”编辑部所写的“关于无产阶级专政的历史经验”。
    新华社布拉格13日电　赫尔辛基消息：四月十二日，芬兰共产党机关报“劳动人民报”和赫尔辛基最大的报纸“赫尔辛基新闻”全文刊载了中国“人民日报”编辑部所写的“关于无产阶级专政的历史经验”一文。“自由新闻报”在同一天刊载了这篇文章的摘要。
