Title: Tunisian Prime Minister Cables Premier Zhou Enlai to Thank Him for Congratulations on Tunisian Independence
Author:
Date: 1956-04-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 8th: Tunisian Prime Minister Tahar Ammar sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai on the 6th, expressing his gratitude for Premier Zhou Enlai's congratulations on Tunisian independence. The full text of the reply is as follows:

To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:

Your Excellency's warm congratulations to our government and people on Tunisia's restoration of independence deeply moved me. On behalf of the Tunisian government and people, I would like to express my most sincere gratitude to Your Excellency and the Chinese government and people. The Chinese people's profound sympathy for our long struggle for independence fills us with the deepest gratitude and the highest admiration. The Tunisian people are determined to dedicate themselves to establishing a democratic system based on the happiness and freedom of the people, as this is the most reliable way to safeguard Tunisian's national independence. We look forward to the development of friendly relations between our two countries. On Behalf of the Tunisian government and people I gladly offer my sincere wishes for the lasting happiness and prosperity of the Chinese People.

Tahar Ammar
Prime Minister of Tunisia
Tunis, April 6, 1956

### 感谢对突尼斯独立的祝贺  突尼斯首相复电周恩来总理

1956-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    感谢对突尼斯独立的祝贺
    突尼斯首相复电周恩来总理
    新华社8日讯　突尼斯首相塔哈尔·阿玛尔六日打电报给周恩来总理，对周恩来总理对突尼斯独立的祝贺表示感谢。复电全文如下：中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来阁下：
    阁下为了突尼斯恢复独立而给我国政府和人民的热烈祝贺使我深深感动。我谨代表突尼斯政府和人民向阁下以及中国政府和人民表示最诚挚的感谢。中国人民对我们争取独立的长期斗争的深厚同情，使我们感到最深切的感激和最崇高的钦佩。突尼斯人民决心致力于建立以人民的幸福和自由为基础的民主制度，因为这是维护突尼斯民族独立的最可靠的方法。我们期望我们两国之间的友好关系获得发展。我代表突尼斯政府和人民愉快地谨祝中国人民长久幸福和兴旺。
                                 突尼斯首相　塔哈尔·阿玛尔
                                          1956年4月6日突尼斯
