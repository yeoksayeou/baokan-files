Title: Kim Il-sung to Visit Romania at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1956-04-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 9th - According to the Korean Central News Agency: To further consolidate and develop the friendly and cooperative relations between Korea and Romania, the Government of the Romanian People's Republic has invited a government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Marshal Kim Il-sung, to pay a friendly visit to Romania.
    Marshal Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, has accepted this invitation with satisfaction.

### 金日成将应邀访问罗马尼亚

1956-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

    金日成将应邀访问罗马尼亚
    新华社平壤9日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：罗马尼亚人民共和国政府为了进一步巩固和发展朝鲜同罗马尼亚之间的友好合作关系，邀请以金日成元帅为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团到罗马尼亚进行友好访问。
    朝鲜内阁首相金日成元帅满意地接受了这一邀请。
