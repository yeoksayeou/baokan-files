Title: Indonesian and Other Trade Union Delegations Arrive in Beijing to Attend the "May Day" Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1956-04-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency report: At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Indonesian trade union delegation of thirteen people including Munir, and the Brazilian trade union delegation headed by Jovini, arrived in Beijing on the 20th and 21st respectively to attend the "May Day" celebrations and pay a visit to our country.

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations in China, the Tunisian trade union delegation headed by Sherif, the Black African trade union delegation headed by Yoke Gom, the Swiss trade union delegation headed by Stoller, and Iraqi trade union representative Amit Alwan arrived in Beijing by plane today.

### 参加“五一”节观礼  印度尼西亚等工会代表团到京

1956-04-28
第4版()
专栏：

    参加“五一”节观礼
    印度尼西亚等工会代表团到京
    据新华社讯  应中华全国总工会的邀请，来我国参加“五一”节观礼和访问的印度尼西亚工会代表穆尼尔等十三人，以约维尼为首的巴西工会代表团，分别在二十日和二十一日到达北京。
    据新华社27日讯  应中华全国总工会邀请来中国参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼的以歇里夫为首的突尼斯工会代表团，以约克·高姆为首的黑非工会代表团，以斯托勒为首的瑞士工会代表团和伊拉克工会代表阿密特·阿尔万在今天乘飞机到达北京。
