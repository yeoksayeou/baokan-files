Title: U.S. Military Personnel Arrive in South Vietnam to Help Expand the Army
Author:
Date: 1956-04-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 14th: News from South Vietnam says that recently, many U.S. military personnel have arrived in South Vietnam to help the Ngo Dinh Diem regime increase its armed forces. These U.S. military personnel include Major General Berquist.

　　Berquist holds an important position in the U.S. military delegation stationed in Saigon. According to South Vietnam's "Vietnam News Agency" on April 4, Berquist has gone to Long Xuyen Province in southwestern South Vietnam to directly supervise the Ngo Dinh Diem army, which is currently fighting against opposition factions and the Hoa Hao religious army.

　　By the end of 1955, the U.S. military delegation stationed in Saigon numbered 1,200. This number has now greatly increased.

### 美国军事人员到南越帮助扩军

1956-04-15
第4版()
专栏：

    美国军事人员到南越帮助扩军
    据新华社河内14日电　据越南通讯社十四日讯：从南越传来的消息说，最近许多美国军事人员到达南越帮助吴庭艳政权增加它的武装部队，这些美国军事人员中包括伯奎斯特少将。
    伯奎斯特在驻西贡的美国军事代表团中担任重要职位。据南越的“越南新闻社”四月四日报道，伯奎斯特已经到南越西南部的龙川省去直接监督现在正在对反对派和好教军队作战的吴庭艳军队。
    到1955年年底为止，驻西贡的美国军事代表团有一千二百人。这个数字现在已经大大增加。
