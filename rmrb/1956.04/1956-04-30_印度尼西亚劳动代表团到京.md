Title: Indonesian Labor Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 4

    Indonesian Labor Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    The Indonesian Labor Delegation, a group of nine people led by the head of the delegation, Sanyono, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 29th. They were invited by the Ministry of Labor of the People's Republic of China to visit our country and to participate in the May Day celebrations.

### 印度尼西亚劳动代表团到京

1956-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚劳动代表团到京
    印度尼西亚劳动代表团一行九人，由团长桑约诺率领，在二十九日下午到达北京。他们是应中华人民共和国劳动部邀请来我国访问，并且参加“五一”节观礼的。
