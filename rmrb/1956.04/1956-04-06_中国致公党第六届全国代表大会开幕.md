Title: China Zhi Gong Party Sixth National Congress Opens
Author:
Date: 1956-04-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 5th—The Sixth National Congress of the China Zhi Gong Party opened in Beijing on the 5th.
    The task of this congress is to summarize past experiences, determine the guiding principles and tasks for future work, revise the party's constitution, and elect new central leading bodies.
    Chen Qiyou, Chairman of the Central Committee of the China Zhi Gong Party, delivered the opening speech at the meeting. He said that the task of the China Zhi Gong Party is to contribute its strength to the cause of expanding and developing the patriotic unity of overseas Chinese, to help overseas Chinese raise their political awareness and patriotic enthusiasm, and to lead them to contribute to the motherland's construction cause. Now is the time when our country's socialist construction is advancing by leaps and bounds, and the people of the whole country, in addition to actively preparing to liberate Taiwan by war if necessary, are also striving to liberate Taiwan by peaceful means. The China Zhi Gong Party should strengthen patriotic propaganda and education work among overseas Chinese, which is our current urgent task.
    Representatives of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, and various democratic parties also attended the meeting. Zhang Bojun, Xiong Kewu, Gao Chongmin, and Li Zhuchen spoke at the meeting and expressed warm congratulations to the congress.

### 中国致公党第六届全国代表大会开幕

1956-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中国致公党第六届全国代表大会开幕
    据新华社5日讯　中国致公党第六届全国代表大会五日在北京开幕。
    这次大会的任务是总结过去经验，决定今后工作的方针任务，修改党的章程，选举新的中央领导机关。
    中国致公党中央委员会主席陈其尤在会上致开幕词。他说，中国致公党的任务，是要在扩大和发展华侨爱国团结的事业中，贡献自己的力量，帮助华侨提高政治觉悟和爱国热情，带动他们为祖国建设事业贡献力量。现在正是我国社会主义建设事业突飞猛进，全国人民除了积极准备在必要的时候用战争方式解放台湾以外，还努力争取用和平方式解放台湾的时候，中国致公党应该加强向华侨进行爱国主义的宣传教育工作，这更是我们当前迫切的任务。
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会、中共中央统一战线工作部和各民主党派的代表也参加了会议。章伯钧、熊克武、高崇民和李烛尘在会上讲话，向大会表示热烈祝贺。
