Title: Sino-British Symphony Orchestra Returns to Hong Kong After Performance in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1956-04-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 24th: The Sino-British Symphony Orchestra of the Sino-British Society Music Group of Hong Kong, at the invitation of the South China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, arrived in Guangzhou on the 20th and held two performances at the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall on the 21st and 22nd, with an audience of more than 10,000 people. Each program of the performance was warmly welcomed by the audience.
　　At the beginning of the first performance, Ouyang Shan, Chairman of the South China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, delivered a speech, expressing his welcome and gratitude to the Sino-British Symphony Orchestra for coming to Guangzhou to perform, and hoped to be able to appreciate more performances from Hong Kong art troupes in the near future. Ms. Tan Baolian, the head of the Sino-British Symphony Orchestra, said that the members of the Sino-British Symphony Orchestra were very happy to be able to come to Guangzhou to perform this time.
　　After the Sino-British Symphony Orchestra finished its performance in Guangzhou, except for a few personnel who stayed in Guangzhou to continue their visit, they have left Guangzhou in two batches by train to return to Hong Kong.

### 中英交响乐团在广州演出后回香港

1956-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中英交响乐团在广州演出后回香港
    据新华社广州24日电  香港中英学会音乐组中英交响乐团，应华南文学艺术界联合会的邀请，在二十日到达广州市，二十一和二十二日在中山纪念堂举行了两场演出，观众有一万多人。演出的每个节目都受到观众的热烈欢迎。
    在第一场演出开始时，华南文学艺术界联合会主席欧阳山致词，他对中英交响乐团来广州演出表示欢迎和感谢，并且希望不久以后能够欣赏到更多的来自香港的艺术团的演出。中英交响乐团团长谭保莲女士表示，中英交响乐团的人员对这次能够来到广州演出感到非常愉快。
    中英交响乐团在广州演出完毕后，除个别人员留在广州继续访问外已经分两批乘火车离广州回香港。
