Title: Nepal Government and Communist Party Negotiation Results: Decision to Lift Ban on Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1956-04-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 16th -- The "Indian Press Trust" reported from Kathmandu that the Nepalese government decided on April 15th to lift the ban on the Communist Party.

This decision is the result of negotiations between the Prime Minister of Nepal and representatives of the Communist Party of Nepal.

The Communist Party of Nepal was declared illegal in 1952.

### 尼泊尔政府和共产党谈判结果  决取消对共产党的禁令

1956-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔政府和共产党谈判结果
    决取消对共产党的禁令
    新华社新德里16日电　“印度报业托辣斯”从加德满都发来的消息说，尼泊尔政府在四月十五日决定取消对共产党的禁令。
    这个决定是尼泊尔首相和尼泊尔共产党代表之间谈判的结果。
    尼泊尔共产党是在1952年被宣布为非法的。
