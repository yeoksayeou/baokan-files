Title: Traffic Advanced Producers Representative Conference Opens
Author:
Date: 1956-04-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th: The National Traffic Advanced Producers Representative Conference opened in Beijing today.

　　Attending the conference were 1,056 representatives from national highway transportation lines, new highway construction projects, maritime and inland river shipping, navigation projects, dockworkers from various ports, and automobile and ship repair factories.

　　Minister of Communications Zhang Bojun delivered the opening speech at the conference.

　　The head of the Soviet Maritime and Inland River Transport Advanced Producers Delegation, Burlakov, Boris Ivanovich, said in his speech that he hoped that the friendship and cooperation between China and the Soviet Union would be further strengthened through the exchange of advanced experiences. He said that the crew of the Soviet ship "Krasnoyarsk" had already used the segmented stoking method created by Chinese crew members, and the effect was very good. On behalf of the Soviet sailors, he presented a model of a new type of internal combustion engine passenger ship and books recording the advanced experience of Soviet sailors to the Chinese sailor representatives.

　　The head of the Soviet Highway Transport Advanced Producers Delegation, Rushakov, Alexei Ivanovich, also spoke at the meeting today.

　　Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, General Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Li Dequan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and representatives of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League all attended the meeting today to offer their congratulations.

### 交通先进生产者代表会议开幕

1956-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    交通先进生产者代表会议开幕
    据新华社20日讯  全国交通先进生产者代表会议今天在北京开幕。
    参加会议的有全国各公路运输线、新建公路工程、海上和内河航运、航务工程、各港口装卸工人、汽车和轮船修理工厂代表一千零五十六人。
    交通部部长章伯钧在会上致开幕词。
    苏联海上和内河运输先进生产者代表团团长布尔拉科夫·鲍里斯·伊凡诺维奇在致词中说，他希望中苏两国的友谊和合作通过交流先进经验更加巩固。他说，苏联的“克拉斯诺雅尔斯克号”船员已经运用了中国船员创造的分段烧火法，效果很好。他代表苏联海员把一艘新型的内燃机客轮模型和记载着苏联海员先进经验的书籍赠送给中国海员代表。
    苏联公路运输先进生产者代表团团长鲁沙克夫·阿列克塞·伊凡诺维奇今天也在会上讲了话。
    中华全国总工会主席赖若愚、中国人民解放军总政治部副主任甘泗淇上将、中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会代表今天都到会祝贺。
