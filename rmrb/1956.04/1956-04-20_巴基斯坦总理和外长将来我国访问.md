Title: Pakistani Prime Minister and Foreign Minister to Visit Our Country
Author:
Date: 1956-04-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 20th. At the invitation of the Chinese government, the Prime Minister of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, Muhammad Ali, and the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Hamidul Huq Choudhury, along with an entourage of twenty people, will depart from Karachi on May 14th or 15th for a friendly visit to China. Prime Minister Muhammad Ali and his entourage will visit China for approximately two weeks.

### 巴基斯坦总理和外长将来我国访问

1956-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦总理和外长将来我国访问
    新华社20日讯　应中国政府的邀请，巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总理穆罕默德·阿里和外交部长哈米杜尔·哈克·乔德里以及包括二十人的随行人员将在五月十四日或十五日自喀喇蚩动身前来中国作友好访问。穆罕默德·阿里总理和他的随行人员将在中国访问两星期左右。
