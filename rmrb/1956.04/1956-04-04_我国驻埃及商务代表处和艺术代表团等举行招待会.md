Title: Chinese Commercial Representative Office in Egypt, Art Delegation, etc. Hold Reception
Author:
Date: 1956-04-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 2nd. The Commercial Representative Office of the People's Republic of China in Egypt, the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, and the Chinese Trade Delegation held a reception in Cairo on April 1st.

Invited to the reception were Egyptian Minister of Industry and Commerce Nuseir, Minister of National Guidance Radwan, Governor of Liberation Province Hassani, Mayor of Cairo Bindari, President of Cairo University Mursi, as well as other senior Egyptian government officials, people from Egyptian business and cultural circles, and journalists.

Also invited to the reception were diplomatic envoys and commercial representatives from the Soviet Union, Poland, Hungary, Yugoslavia, India, Burma, Pakistan, Afghanistan, Thailand, Yemen, the Netherlands, and other countries stationed in Egypt.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 3rd. Egyptian Minister of Industry and Commerce Nuseir said at a press conference on the 2nd that the first phase of Sino-Egyptian negotiations had been successfully concluded. Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, will travel to Sudan on April 4th.

Nuseir hoped that the current negotiations would lead to an increase in the volume of trade between Egypt and China.

### 我国驻埃及商务代表处和艺术代表团等举行招待会

1956-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    我国驻埃及商务代表处和艺术代表团等举行招待会
    新华社开罗2日电　中华人民共和国驻埃及商务代表处、中国文化艺术代表团和中国贸易访问团四月一日在开罗举行了招待会。
    应邀参加招待会的有埃及工商部部长努赛尔、国家指导部部长拉德温、解放省省长哈萨尼、开罗市长宾达里、开罗大学校长穆尔西以及埃及政府其他高级官员、埃及工商界和文化界人士以及新闻记者。
    应邀参加招待会的还有苏联、波兰、匈牙利、南斯拉夫、印度、缅甸、巴基斯坦、阿富汗、泰国、也门、荷兰等国驻埃及的外交使节和商务代表。
    新华社开罗3日电　埃及工商部长努赛尔二日在记者招待会上说，中埃谈判的第一阶段已经圆满结束。中华人民共和国对外贸易部长叶季壮将于四月四日前往苏丹。
    努赛尔希望，目前的谈判将导致埃中两国贸易量的增加。
