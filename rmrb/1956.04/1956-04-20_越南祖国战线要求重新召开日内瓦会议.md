Title: Vietnam Fatherland Front Demands Reconvening of Geneva Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-04-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 18th: The "People's Daily" of Vietnam today published a statement issued by the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front. The statement said that the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, representing all the Vietnamese people, expressed complete agreement and support for the letter sent by the Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the two chairmen of the Geneva Conference on April 9th.
    The statement believes that the French government and the South Vietnamese regime have unavoidable responsibilities for fulfilling the Geneva Accords, and condemns the conspiracies of the United States and Ngo Dinh Diem to sabotage the Geneva Accords.
    The statement stated that the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front and the Vietnamese people will resolutely fight for the demand to reconvene the Geneva Conference. The statement also expressed the hope that the talks between the representatives of the two chairmen of the Geneva Conference can achieve good results, leading to the reconvening of the Geneva Conference.
    The National Congress of the Vietnam Socialist Party also passed a resolution, expressing full support for Pham Van Dong's letter to the two chairmen of the Geneva Conference, and expressing the hope that the Geneva Conference will be reconvened as soon as possible to find measures to ensure the implementation of the Geneva Accords.

### 越南祖国战线要求重新召开日内瓦会议

1956-04-20
第4版()
专栏：

    越南祖国战线要求重新召开日内瓦会议
    新华社河内18日电　越南“人民报”今天刊载了越南祖国战线中央委员会发表的一项声明，声明说，越南祖国战线中央委员会代表全体越南人民，表示完全赞成和拥护越南民主共和国总理兼外交部长在四月九日给日内瓦会议两位主席的信。
    声明认为法国政府和南越政权对于履行日内瓦协定都有不可逃避的责任，并且谴责了美国和吴庭艳破坏日内瓦协定的阴谋。
    声明表示，越南祖国战线中央委员会和越南全国人民将为要求重新召开日内瓦会议而坚决斗争。声明并且希望日内瓦会议两位主席的代表的会谈能够获得良好的结果，导致日内瓦会议的重新召开。
    越南社会党全国代表大会也通过决议，表示完全拥护范文同给日内瓦会议两位主席的信，并且希望尽早地重新召开日内瓦会议，以寻找措施保证日内瓦协定的履行。
