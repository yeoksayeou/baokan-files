Title: Chiang Kai-shek Army Officer Peng Yi Returns to the Mainland
Author:
Date: 1956-04-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 11th: Peng Yi, a lieutenant and deputy company commander of the 1st Motor Transport Battalion of the Chiang Kai-shek United Service Headquarters, dissatisfied with the dark rule of the US-Chiang regime in Taiwan, defected from the Chiang army in Japan while en route back to Taiwan from training in the United States this January. He returned to the motherland on March 20th and arrived in Beijing on April 8th, where he was warmly received by relevant departments.

Peng Yi is from Changsha, Hunan, is twenty-seven years old this year, and joined the Chiang army in 1949. In September 1955, he was sent by the Chiang Kai-shek United Logistics Headquarters to the United States Army Transportation School for training.

### 蒋军军官彭毅回到大陆

1956-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋军军官彭毅回到大陆
    新华社11日讯　蒋军联合勤务总司令部汽车运输第一营上尉副连长彭毅，因为对美蒋在台湾的黑暗统治不满，今年一月在美国受训完毕乘船返回台湾途经日本时脱离蒋军。他在三月二十日回到祖国大陆，四月八日到达北京，受到有关部门的热情接待。
    彭毅是湖南长沙人，今年二十七岁，1949年参加蒋军。1955年9月，他被蒋军联勤总部派到美国陆军运输学校受训。
