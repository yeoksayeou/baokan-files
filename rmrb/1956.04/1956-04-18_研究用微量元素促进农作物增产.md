    Research on Using Trace Elements to Promote Increased Crop Yields
    Professor Wu Lü, Director of the Biology Teaching and Research Section of Dalian Medical College, is writing a paper on the use of trace elements to promote increased crop yields.
    The results of field experiments conducted under the direction of Wu Lü have proven that cotton, soybean, and peanut seeds soaked in solutions of four trace elements—copper sulfate, manganese sulfate, potassium iodide, and boric acid—can yield 2% to 33.9% more than ordinary seeds.
    Since beginning this research work in 1954, Wu Lü has experimented with dozens of trace elements. He soaked cotton, soybean, and peanut seeds in solutions of various trace elements at different concentrations, observing and recording the germination and growth of the seeds. Sowing on four mu of land at the Jin County Demonstration Breeding Farm, the cotton, soybean, and peanut seeds soaked in potassium iodide, zinc sulfate, manganese sulfate, and boric acid yielded bumper harvests for two consecutive years. Soybean seeds treated with zinc sulfate yielded 33.9% more than ordinary seeds, and cotton seeds treated with manganese sulfate increased pre-frost cotton production by 20%. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 研究用微量元素促进农作物增产

1956-04-18
第3版()
专栏：

    研究用微量元素促进农作物增产
    大连医学院生物教研室主任伍律教授正在写作微量元素促进农作物增产问题的论文。
    伍律主持的田间试验的结果证明，由硫酸铜、硫酸锰、?化钾和硼酸等四种微量元素溶液分别浸泡过的棉花、大豆和花生的种籽，收获量能比一般种籽的收获量高出2％到33.9％。
    伍律从1954年开始这项研究工作以来，曾经先后对几十种微量元素做了试验。他把棉花、大豆和花生的种籽分别浸在不同浓度的各种微量元素的溶液里，观察和纪录种籽发芽生长的情况。在金县示范繁殖农场的四亩土地上播种结果，用?化钾、硫酸锌、硫酸锰和硼酸浸泡过的棉花、大豆和花生的种籽，连续二年获得丰收。用硫酸锌处理过的大豆种籽，收获量比一般种籽的收获量高出33·9％，用硫酸锰处理过的棉花种籽，霜前花增产20％。　（新华社）
