Title: Shenyang Agricultural College Holds Short-Term Training Class
Author:
Date: 1956-04-21
Page: 1

Shenyang Agricultural College is holding a short-term training class to train agricultural technical personnel for agricultural technology guidance stations in Liaoning Province. This is a scene of a college lecturer explaining crop diseases and insect pests to the students in the laboratory.
