Title: World Federation of Scientific Workers Executive Council Concludes

Author: 
Date: 1956-04-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th. The 16th Executive Council meeting of the World Federation of Scientific Workers concluded in Beijing at 2:00 PM today.

    The meeting discussed the issue of preparing for an international scientific conference to explore the dangers of atomic and thermonuclear weapons, and issued a special statement on the dangers posed by nuclear weapons.

    The meeting also discussed the establishment of an international organization for the exchange of scientific information; promoting mutual contact between scientific communities of various countries; publishing publications such as "Science and Humanity"; investigating and researching the living and working conditions of scientific workers in various countries in order to improve them; proposing amendments to the constitution to be submitted to the next general assembly for discussion; and expanding the global scientific influence, among other issues.

    At this meeting, scientific organizations from Romania, Albania, and Mongolia were accepted as members of the World Federation of Scientific Workers.

### 世界科学工作者协会执行理事会闭幕

1956-04-05
第2版()
专栏：

    世界科学工作者协会执行理事会闭幕
    新华社4日讯　世界科学工作者协会第十六届执行理事会，今天下午二时在北京闭幕。
    这次会议讨论了关于准备召开一次探讨原子及热核子武器危害性的国际科学会议的问题，并且就核子武器所带来的危险发表了特别声明。
    会议还讨论了建立国际交换科学资料的机构；关于促进各国科学界互相接触；出版“科学与人类”等刊物，调查研究各国科学工作者的生活和工作条件以便改善；提出修改会章的意见，准备提请下届大会讨论；以及扩大世界科学影响等问题。
    在这次会议上，接收了罗马尼亚、阿尔巴尼亚、蒙古三个国家的科学团体为世界科学工作者协会会员。
