Title: Our Army in Fujian Shoots Down and Damages Two Chiang Kai-shek Planes
Author:
Date: 1956-04-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 13th. At 10:19 on the 12th, four Chiang Kai-shek F-84 fighter planes intruded into the airspace over Fuyu Island, Fujian Province. The People's Liberation Army's anti-aircraft artillery units intercepted them with fierce artillery fire, shooting down and damaging one Chiang Kai-shek plane each. The downed Chiang Kai-shek plane crashed into the sea ten kilometers northeast of the Daishan Islands.

### 福建我军击落击伤蒋机二架

1956-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    福建我军击落击伤蒋机二架
    新华社福建前线13日电　十二日十时十九分，蒋军F—84型战斗机四架窜扰福建省福瑶岛上空，中国人民解放军高射炮兵部队以猛烈炮火截击，击落、击伤蒋机各一架。被击落的蒋机坠入台山列岛东北十公里的海里。
