Title: Chinese Trade Delegation Leaves Cairo
Author:
Date: 1956-04-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 17th: The Chinese trade delegation, led by Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of China, has concluded its visit to Egypt and left Cairo by plane on the morning of April 17th.

　　Egyptian Minister of Commerce and Industry, Nuseir, and other senior Egyptian government officials saw them off at the airport.

### 我国贸易访问团离开开罗

1956-04-18
第4版()
专栏：

    我国贸易访问团离开开罗
    新华社开罗17日电　由中国对外贸易部长叶季壮率领的中国贸易访问团已经结束它在埃及的访问，在四月十七日早晨乘飞机离开开罗。
    埃及工商部长努赛尔和埃及政府的其他高级官员到机场欢送。
