Title: Indonesian Cabinet Holds Meeting; Approves Bill to Abolish Round Table Conference Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-04-06
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 4th: Antara News Agency reported today that the Indonesian Cabinet, at its second meeting held on April 3rd, passed a bill to abolish all Round Table Conference agreements concluded with the Netherlands in 1949. This bill will soon be submitted to the parliament.
    Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo wrote to the parliament on April 3rd, stating that the government will explain its political program at the parliamentary session on April 9th. The letter also requested the parliament to prioritize the discussion of the bill on abolishing the Round Table Conference agreements.

### 印度尼西亚内阁举行会议  通过废除圆桌会议协定法案

1956-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚内阁举行会议
    通过废除圆桌会议协定法案
    据新华社雅加达4日电　安塔拉通讯社今天报道，印度尼西亚内阁在四月三日举行的第二次会议上通过了废除1949年同荷兰缔结的全部圆桌会议协定的法案。这个法案即将向国会提出。
    沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理在四月三日写信给国会说，政府将在四月九日的国会会议上解释它的政纲。这封信还要求国会优先讨论关于废除圆桌会议协定的法案。
