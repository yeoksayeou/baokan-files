Title: AFL-CIO Vice President Reuther Expresses Support for the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence
Author:
Date: 1956-04-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 8th. Walter Reuther, Vice President of the American Federation of Labor and Congress of Industrial Organizations, stated his support for the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence at a press conference in New Delhi on April 6th. Reuther arrived in India on April 4th for a ten-day visit.

    In response to a question, Reuther said, "I agree with these principles. I don't understand how anyone could disagree with such principles. It's like asking me if I agree with sin. I disagree with sin."

    Reuther stated his opposition to military treaties in Asia, believing that attempting to transplant Europe's North Atlantic Treaty Organization to Asia, with its different economic, political, cultural, and historical backgrounds, is a mistake. He said that upon returning home, he would ask the United States to re-evaluate its policy towards Asia.

    Reuther said he favored disarmament and hoped that the London Disarmament Conference could reach some feasible plan.

### 美国劳联和产联副主席路德  表示支持和平共处五项原则

1956-04-09
第4版()
专栏：

    美国劳联和产联副主席路德
    表示支持和平共处五项原则
    新华社新德里8日电　美国劳工联合会和产业工会联合会副主席华尔特·路德四月六日在新德里举行的一次记者招待会上发表谈话，表示支持和平共处的五项原则。路德是在四月四日来到印度的，他将进行十天访问。
    路德在回答问题的时候说：“我同意这样的原则。我不明白怎么会有人能够不同意这样的原则。这就像是问我是否同意罪恶一样。我不同意罪恶。”
    路德表示他反对在亚洲的军事条约，他认为，企图把欧洲的北大西洋公约组织硬搬到具有不同的经济、政治、文化和历史背景的亚洲，这种作法是错误的。他说，他回国后将要求美国重新估价它对亚洲的政策。
    路德说，他赞成裁军，并且希望伦敦裁军会议能够达成某种可行的方案。
