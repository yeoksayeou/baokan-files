Title: National Cotton Procurement Plan Completed
Author:
Date: 1956-04-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 5th: The national cotton procurement plan for the 1955 crop year (September 1955 – August 1956) was completed ahead of schedule in mid-March. By mid-March, the quantity of cotton purchased had reached 100.04% of the original plan. Twelve provinces and cities with higher cotton production, including Hebei, Jiangsu, Shandong, Shanghai, and Beijing, all exceeded their procurement plans. At present, various regions are striving to purchase even more cotton.

### 全国棉花采购计划完成

1956-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    全国棉花采购计划完成
    据新华社5日讯　1955年度（1955年9月—1956年8月）的全国棉花采购计划已经在三月中旬提前完成了。到三月中旬，棉花收购数量已经达到原订计划的100.04％。产棉较多的河北、江苏、山东、上海、北京等十二个省市都超额完成了采购计划。目前，各地正争取收购更多的棉花。
