Title: Women From All Circles in the Capital Hold Garden Party to Congratulate the Success of the International Democratic Women's Federation Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: Tonight, over ten thousand people in the capital – female advanced producers, young men and women, children, and women from all walks of life – joined foreign friends attending the Beijing meeting of the International Democratic Women's Federation Council to celebrate the success of the Council meeting in Zhongshan Park.

At 7:30 PM, Madam Eugénie Cotton, President of the International Democratic Women's Federation, who tirelessly fights for peace, women's, and children's rights, and all the foreign guests attending the meeting arrived at Zhongshan Park successively. Accompanied by Cai Chang, President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Lin Qiaozhi, Vice President of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, and others, they entered the park. They were warmly welcomed by the crowd.

At 8:00 PM, Xu Guangping, Vice President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation and Chairman of the conference, announced the opening of the garden party. This was followed by a speech by Madam Cotton. She said, "We will never forget that at the imminent closing of the International Democratic Women's Federation Council meeting, such a beautiful evening has been prepared for us in such a beautiful place." Madam Cotton expressed her gratitude to the Chinese women.

After Madam Cotton's speech, applause and music erupted, and people immediately formed circles and danced, filling the entire park with joy. Foreign friends, accompanied by their Chinese hosts, joined the various joyful dance circles in groups of three or five. The foreign guests watched acrobatics, classical opera, folk songs and dances, puppet shows, and other programs.

The garden party ended at 10:00 PM.

### 首都各界妇女同外宾举行游园会  祝贺国际民主妇联理事会会议成功

1956-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　首都各界妇女同外宾举行游园会
    祝贺国际民主妇联理事会会议成功
    据新华社29日讯  今天晚上，在中山公园里，首都一万余人——女先进生产者、男女青年、少年儿童和各界妇女——同参加国际民主妇联理事会北京会议的外国朋友们在一起，祝贺理事会会议的成功。
    下午七点半钟，为保卫和平、妇女和儿童权利而不疲倦地奋斗的国际民主妇联主席戈登夫人和参加会议的全体外宾们陆续来到中山公园，在中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅、北京市民主妇女联合会副主席林巧稚等陪同下，步入公园。她们受到人群的热烈欢迎。
    八点钟，大会主席中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平宣布游园大会开始。接着由戈登夫人讲话。她说：“我们永远也不会忘记在国际民主妇联理事会即将闭幕的时候，在这样美丽的地方给我们准备了一个这样美丽的晚会”。戈登夫人向中国妇女表示谢意。
    戈登夫人讲话后，掌声和乐声四起，人们马上围成圈圈翩翩起舞，全园一片欢腾。外国朋友们在中国主人陪同下，三五成群，分别参加到各个欢乐的舞圈里。外宾们分别观看了杂技、古典剧、民间歌舞、木偶戏等节目。
    游园会在十点钟结束。
