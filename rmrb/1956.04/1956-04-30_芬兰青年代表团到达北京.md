Title: Finnish Youth Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 4

A Finnish youth delegation, invited by the All-China Youth Federation, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 28th to visit China. The delegation is jointly composed of representatives from various Finnish youth organizations and will participate in the "May Day" celebrations in Beijing. The head of the delegation is Yu. Huasama, the third chairman of the Finnish Youth Organization Representative Council and the general secretary of the Finnish Youth League.

### 芬兰青年代表团到达北京

1956-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    芬兰青年代表团到达北京
    应中华全国民主青年联合会邀请来中国访问的芬兰青年代表团，二十八日乘飞机到达北京。代表团是由芬兰各方面青年组织推派代表联合组成的，在北京将参加 “五一”节观礼。代表团团长是芬兰青年组织代表理事会第三主席、芬兰青年联盟总书记于·华沙玛。
