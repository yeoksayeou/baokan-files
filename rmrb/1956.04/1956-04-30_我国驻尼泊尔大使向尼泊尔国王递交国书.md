Title: Chinese Ambassador to Nepal Presents Credentials to the King of Nepal
Author: 
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, April 29th: Pan Zili, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Nepal, presented his credentials to King Mahendra Bir Bikram Shah Deva of the Kingdom of Nepal on April 29.

Ambassador Pan Zili said in his eulogy: Since the establishment of formal diplomatic relations between China and Nepal and the determination of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence as the basic principles guiding the relations between the two countries, the friendly relations between the two countries have been further developed. There are broad prospects for the development of friendly and cooperative relations between our two countries. The Chinese government and people are willing to continue to strengthen economic and cultural cooperation with Nepal. This will promote the growing friendship between the people of China and Nepal and help maintain peace in Asia and the world.

The King of Nepal said in his reply: The establishment of formal diplomatic relations between China and Nepal provides full possibilities for the further development of the long-standing friendship and goodwill between our two countries. Since our relationship is based on the affirmation of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, my government will be ready at any time to cooperate with your government to promote and consolidate world peace.

### 我国驻尼泊尔大使向尼泊尔国王递交国书

1956-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    我国驻尼泊尔大使向尼泊尔国王递交国书
    据新华社加德满都29日电  中华人民共和国驻尼泊尔王国大使潘自力四月二十九日向尼泊尔王国国王马亨德拉·比尔·比克拉姆·沙阿·德瓦递交了国书。
    潘自力大使致颂词说：自从中国和尼泊尔两国建立正式外交关系和确定以和平共处五项原则为指导两国关系的基本原则以来，两国的友好关系已经获得进一步的发展。我们两国间的友好合作关系有着广阔的发展前途。中国政府和人民愿意继续加强同尼泊尔之间的经济和文化合作。这将使中尼两国人民的友谊日益发展，并且有助于维护亚洲和世界和平。
    尼泊尔国王致答词说：中国和尼泊尔之间正式外交关系的建立给我们两国间的长期友谊和善意的进一步发展提供了充分的可能性。由于我们的关系是以肯定和平共处的五项原则为基础的，我国政府将随时准备同贵国政府合作以促进和巩固世界和平。
