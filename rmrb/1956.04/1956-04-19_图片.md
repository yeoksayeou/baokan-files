    Representative Liu Jie, a young policewoman attending the National People's Police and Public Security Committee Heroes and Model Representatives Conference, is highly vigilant politically in her work and resolute in her struggle against the enemy. Since starting her job, she has discovered more than 200 leads on counter-revolutionaries, apprehended two hidden counter-revolutionary elements, and solved a criminal case. The picture shows her reporting her work to leading comrades. Photo by Ding Fengling (Xinhua News Agency draft).
