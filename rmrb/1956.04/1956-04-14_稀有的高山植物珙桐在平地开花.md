Title: Rare Dove Tree Blooms on Flat Land

Author: 
Date: 1956-04-14
Page: 3

    Rare high mountain plant, the dove tree (Davidia involucrata), is now blooming on flat land in Sichuan Province.

    This dove tree is planted in the Chengdu People's Park. Its petals resemble two green leaves, and its flower heart looks like a small purple-red pompon. When the dove tree blooms in full, the petals turn white, like butterflies resting on the branches.

    This world-rare high mountain plant grows on Mount Emei in Sichuan Province. In 1953, the Chengdu People's Park sent people to collect a batch of dove trees from Mount Emei and transplanted them in the park's shady and humid area. This spring, they bloomed for the first time, attracting many people to come and admire them.
                        (Xinhua News Agency)

### 稀有的高山植物珙桐在平地开花

1956-04-14
第3版()
专栏：

    稀有的高山植物珙桐在平地开花
    世界上稀有的高山植物珙桐，现在在四川省平地上开花了。
    这株珙桐栽植在成都市人民公园里。它的花瓣像两片绿色的树叶，花心像个紫红色的小绒球。当珙桐花盛开的时候，花瓣转白，好像一只只粉蝶停留在树枝上。
    四川省峨眉山上生长着这种世界稀有的高山植物。1953年，成都市人民公园派人从峨眉山上采集了一批珙桐，移植在园内阴湿地带，今年春天，第一次开了花，许多人前去观赏。
                        （新华社）
