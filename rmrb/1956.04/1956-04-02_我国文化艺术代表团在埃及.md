Title: Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation in Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-04-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 31st: Egyptian Minister of Education, Hussein, held a grand banquet on the evening of March 29th to entertain the Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation.

The entire Chinese Trade Delegation headed by Ye Jizhuang, which is currently visiting Egypt, and Zhang Yue, the Chinese Deputy Commercial Representative in Egypt, were also invited to attend the banquet.

More than one hundred prominent educators, artists, and journalists from Cairo attended on the Egyptian side.

Hussein spoke at the banquet, expressing his welcome to the Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation. He said, "I pay my respects to you, and through you, to the great Chinese people. I hope that the two great peoples who have discarded the old era and gained liberation will cooperate closely for world peace." Hussein expressed his confidence in the happy future of the two peoples. He said that the wheel of history will continue to move forward, and the evil forces of imperialism will never be able to stop the advance of the wheel of history.

Bao Erhan, the head of the Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation, delivered a reply, expressing his sincere gratitude for the warm hospitality of the hosts and his hope that the friendship between the Chinese and Egyptian people would last forever.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 1st: The Egyptian Ministry of Education and the National Guidance Department jointly held a grand Egyptian music and dance evening on the evening of March 31st to welcome the entire Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation headed by Bao Erhan.

The Egyptian Minister of Education, Minister of National Guidance, Minister of Finance, Minister of Agriculture, and Minister of Health attended the evening. More than 700 people, including famous Egyptian artists and prominent figures from all walks of life in Cairo, also attended the evening.

Egyptian artists performed wonderful programs at the evening, including Chinese folk songs. The Chinese artists warmly welcomed these wonderful performances.

### 我国文化艺术代表团在埃及

1956-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    我国文化艺术代表团在埃及
    新华社开罗31日电　埃及教育部长侯赛尼在三月二十九日晚间举行盛大宴会招待中国文化艺术代表团。
    正在埃及访问的以叶季壮为首的中国贸易访问团全体人员和中国驻埃及商务副代表张越也应邀出席了宴会。
    埃及方面出席的有一百多名开罗的著名教育家、艺术家和新闻记者。
    侯赛尼在宴会上讲话，表示欢迎中国文化艺术代表团。他说，我向你们致敬，并且通过你们，向伟大的中国人民致敬，希望已经摒弃了旧时代的获得解放的两国伟大人民将为世界和平而密切合作。侯赛尼对两国人民的幸福前途表示具有信心。他说，历史车轮将继续前进，帝国主义恶势力决不能制止历史车轮的前进。
    中国文化艺术代表团团长鲍尔汉致答词，他表示诚恳感谢主人的热烈招待，希望中埃两国人民之间的友谊万世永恒。
    据新华社开罗1日电　埃及教育部和国家指导部三月三十一日晚上联合举行一次盛大的埃及音乐歌舞晚会，欢迎以鲍尔汉为首的中国文化艺术代表团全体人员。
    埃及教育部长，国家指导部长，财政部长，农业部长和卫生部长出席了晚会。出席晚会的还有埃及的著名艺术家和开罗各界的著名人士等共七百多人。
    埃及艺术家们在晚会上演出了精采的节目，其中有中国的民歌。中国艺术家们热烈欢迎这些精采的表演。
