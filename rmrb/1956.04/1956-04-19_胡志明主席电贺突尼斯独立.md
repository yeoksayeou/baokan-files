Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Congratulates Tunisia on Independence
Author:
Date: 1956-04-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 18th—According to Vietnam News Agency, President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam sent a congratulatory telegram to King Sidi Mohammed al-Amin of Tunisia on the occasion of Tunisia's declaration of independence.

In his congratulatory telegram, Chairman Ho Chi Minh wished the King of Tunisia and the government and people of Tunisia the greatest success in consolidating national independence and building a strong and prosperous country.

In his reply telegram, the King of Tunisia thanked Chairman Ho Chi Minh, the government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for their congratulations. The reply telegram said that the Tunisian people will use all their strength to establish a democratic regime and build a prosperous country, and are very willing to cooperate with all free countries to ensure world peace and good understanding.

### 胡志明主席电贺突尼斯独立

1956-04-19
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席电贺突尼斯独立
    新华社河内18日电　据越南通讯社讯：越南民主共和国主席胡志明在突尼斯宣布独立的时候向突尼斯国王西迪·穆罕默德·阿明发出了贺电。
    胡志明主席在贺电中祝贺突尼斯国王以及突尼斯政府和人民在巩固民族独立和建立强大和繁荣的国家方面取得最大的成就。
    突尼斯国王在他的复电中感谢胡志明主席、越南民主共和国政府和人民的祝贺。复电说，突尼斯人民将以全部力量来建立民主政权，建立繁荣的国家，并且很希望和所有自由国家进行合作以保障世界和平和良好的谅解。
