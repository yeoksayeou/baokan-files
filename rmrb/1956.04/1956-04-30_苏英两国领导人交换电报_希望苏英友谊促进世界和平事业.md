Title: Exchange of Telegrams Between Soviet and British Leaders: Hoping that Soviet-British Friendship Will Promote the Cause of World Peace
Author:
Date: 1956-04-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th (TASS dispatch): While returning home on the cruiser "Ordzhonikidze," Bulganin and Khrushchev sent a telegram from the ship to Eden on the 27th, again thanking the British side for their friendly and warm hospitality. The telegram stated that they sincerely hoped that mutual understanding, cooperation, and friendship between the Soviet Union and Britain would continue to develop and strengthen for the benefit of the two peoples and for the benefit of world peace.

In his reply telegram to Bulganin and Khrushchev, Eden said that he was greatly pleased by their friendly and kind telegram. He believed that their visit and their talks would help eliminate suspicion. He hoped that this would be the beginning of growing trust between the two countries. He also warmly hoped, like them, that their friendship and joint work would further advance the common cause of world peace and good relations.

### 苏英两国领导人交换电报  希望苏英友谊促进世界和平事业

1956-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    苏英两国领导人交换电报
    希望苏英友谊促进世界和平事业
    新华社29日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科二十八日消息：布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫在乘“奥尔忠尼启则号”巡洋舰回国途中，二十七日从船上打电报给艾登，再次感谢英国方面对他们的友好、殷勤的招待。电文说，我们真诚地希望，苏联和英国的相互了解、合作和友谊将为两国人民的利益，为世界和平的利益不断发展和加强。
    艾登在给布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫的复电里说，你们的友好、亲切的电报使我感到莫大的愉快。我相信，你们的访问和我们的会谈有助于消除猜疑。我希望，这将是我们两国之间日益增长的信任的开端。我也像你们一样热烈地希望，我们的友谊和共同工作将使世界和平和良好关系的共同事业进一步发展。
