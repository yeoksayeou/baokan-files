Title: Academician Kurchatov of the Soviet Academy of Sciences Reports on Atomic Energy Issues to British Scientists
Author:
Date: 1956-04-27
Page: 4

    Academician Kurchatov of the Soviet Academy of Sciences
    Reports on Atomic Energy Issues to British Scientists
    Xinhua News Agency, London, April 25th: TASS, London, April 25th—Academician Kurchatov of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, who accompanied Bulganin and Khrushchev on their visit to Britain, gave a report on the achievements of Soviet scientists in atomic energy research at the Harwell Atomic Energy Research Establishment on April 25th. More than three hundred British scientists listened to the report.
    This newspaper reports: According to a Reuters dispatch from London: Academician Kurchatov of the Soviet Academy of Sciences gave a report at the Harwell Atomic Energy Research Establishment that "greatly surprised British scientists." The Reuters dispatch said that "Reporters who questioned the scientists after the meeting said that British experts considered the speech to be 'sensational'." The message also stated that Kurchatov's report indicated that the Soviet Union "has far surpassed Britain, and probably the United States as well, in the race to harness hydrogen power and put it to industrial use."

### 苏联科学院院士库尔查托夫  向英科学家报告原子能问题

1956-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联科学院院士库尔查托夫
    向英科学家报告原子能问题
    新华社26日讯  塔斯社伦敦二十五日讯：随同布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫访问英国的苏联科学院院士库尔查托夫四月二十五日在哈威尔原子中心作了关于苏联科学家在研究原子能方面的成就的报告。三百多个英国科学家听了这个报告。
    本报讯  据路透社伦敦消息：苏联科学院院士库尔查托夫在哈威尔原子中心作报告，“使英国科学家们大吃一惊”。路透社消息说，“会后询问了科学家们的记者说，英国专家们认为这次演讲是‘引起轰动的’”。消息还说，库尔查托夫的报告表示，苏联“在驾御氢动力把它用于工业的竞赛中，已经远远地超过英国，大概也超过了美国”。
