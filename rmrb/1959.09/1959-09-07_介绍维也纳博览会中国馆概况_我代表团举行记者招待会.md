Title: Introducing the Overview of the Chinese Pavilion at the Vienna Exposition; Our Delegation Holds Press Conference
Author:
Date: 1959-09-07
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, September 6th: Ji Chao-ding, head of the Chinese delegation to the Vienna Exposition, held a press conference at the Chinese Pavilion on the morning of the 5th (the day before the opening of the exposition). Reporters from Austrian newspapers attended the press conference.
    Ji Chao-ding introduced the overview of the Chinese Pavilion to the reporters, and used some exhibits as examples to illustrate the great achievements of China's economic construction. The reporters listened to his introduction with great interest. After the meeting, the reporters visited the Chinese Pavilion.
    The Chinese Pavilion covers an area of 1,500 square meters and has more than 4,200 exhibits, including heavy industrial products, light industrial products, agricultural products and handicrafts, which are exhibited in four sections.

### 介绍维也纳博览会中国馆概况  我代表团举行记者招待会

1959-09-07
第6版()
专栏：

    介绍维也纳博览会中国馆概况
    我代表团举行记者招待会
    新华社6日讯　维也纳消息：参加维也纳博览会的中国代表团团长冀朝鼎，5日（博览会开幕前一天）上午在中国馆举行记者招待会。奥地利各报记者出席了招待会。
    冀朝鼎向记者们介绍了中国馆的概况，并且用一些展品为例说明了中国经济建设的巨大成就，记者们怀着极大的兴趣听取了他的介绍。会后，记者们参观了中国馆。
    中国馆占地一千五百平方米，有四千二百多件展品，包括重工业产品、轻工业产品、农产品和手工艺品，分四个部分展出。
