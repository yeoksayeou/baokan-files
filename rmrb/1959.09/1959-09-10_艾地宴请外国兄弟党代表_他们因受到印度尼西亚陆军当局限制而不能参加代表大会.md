Title: Aidit Hosts Banquet for Foreign Brother Party Representatives Who Were Unable to Attend the Congress Due to Restrictions Imposed by the Indonesian Army Authorities
Author:
Date: 1959-09-10
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 9th. According to a report in the Indonesian "People's Daily" today, Aidit, General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 8th at the Indonesian Communist Party Central Committee office to entertain representatives of foreign brother parties who were invited to attend the Sixth Congress of the Indonesian Communist Party.

Due to the temporary announcement by the Indonesian Army authorities regarding restrictions on attending the Indonesian Communist Party Congress, these brother party representatives, although they had already arrived in Jakarta, were unable to attend the Congress.

Representatives from the Communist and Workers' Parties of Bulgaria, Poland, the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, Australia, Italy, and Cuba attended the banquet.

Also present at the banquet were Indonesian Communist Party First Deputy General Secretary Lukman, Second Deputy General Secretary Njoto, and Political Bureau member Sakirman.

### 艾地宴请外国兄弟党代表  他们因受到印度尼西亚陆军当局限制而不能参加代表大会

1959-09-10
第5版()
专栏：

    艾地宴请外国兄弟党代表
    他们因受到印度尼西亚陆军当局限制而不能参加代表大会
    新华社雅加达9日电　据印度尼西亚“人民日报”今天报道，印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地8日晚上在印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会办事处设宴招待应邀参加印度尼西亚共产党第六次代表大会的外国兄弟党代表。
    由于印度尼西亚陆军当局临时宣布了关于限制参加印度尼西亚共产党代表大会的规定，这些兄弟党代表虽然已经到达雅加达，但是没有能够出席代表大会。
    出席宴会的有保加利亚、波兰、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利、澳大利亚、意大利和古巴的共产党和工人党的代表。
    出席宴会的还有印度尼西亚共产党第一副总书记鲁克曼、第二副总书记约多和政治局委员萨基尔曼。
