Title: Argentine Women's Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: The Argentine women's delegation of five people, headed by Rosa Inga Linelia, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning. They are visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation to participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the country.

### 阿根廷妇女代表团到京

1959-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    阿根廷妇女代表团到京
    新华社26日讯　以罗莎·因加利内利亚为首的阿根廷妇女代表团一行五人，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。她们是应全国妇联邀请前来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
