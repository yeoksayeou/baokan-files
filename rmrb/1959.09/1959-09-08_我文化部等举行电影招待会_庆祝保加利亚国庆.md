Title: Ministry of Culture and Others Hold Film Reception to Celebrate Bulgarian National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-09-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 7th - The Ministry of Culture, the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and the Sino-Bulgarian Friendship Association jointly held the premiere reception of two Bulgarian films at the Capital Cinema tonight, celebrating the 15th anniversary of the Bulgarian Socialist Revolution. The two films are the documentary "The Seventh Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party" and the feature film "On the Eve of Taking Office."

Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan and Bulgarian Ambassador to China Panchevski spoke at the reception, warmly congratulating the continuous development of cultural exchanges and friendship between the two countries of China and Bulgaria.

### 我文化部等举行电影招待会  庆祝保加利亚国庆

1959-09-08
第6版()
专栏：

    我文化部等举行电影招待会
    庆祝保加利亚国庆
    据新华社7日讯　文化部、对外文化联络委员会和中保友好协会今晚在首都电影院联合举行保加利亚两部电影片的首映招待会，庆祝保加利亚社会主义革命十五周年。这两部电影是纪录片“保加利亚共产党第七次代表大会”和故事片“上任前夕”。
    文化部副部长夏衍和保加利亚驻中国大使潘切夫斯基在招待会上讲话，热烈祝贺中保两国文化交流和人民友谊的不断发展。
