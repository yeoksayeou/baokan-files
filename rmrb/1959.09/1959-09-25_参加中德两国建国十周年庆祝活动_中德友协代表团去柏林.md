Title: Chinese-German Friendship Association Delegation Goes to Berlin to Participate in Celebrations of the Tenth Anniversary of the Founding of China and Germany
Author:
Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th. A delegation of five members from the Sino-German Friendship Association of the People's Republic of China, led by Gao Kelin, Vice President of the Association, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Vice President of the Supreme People's Court, left Beijing by plane today for the German Democratic Republic.

They are visiting the German Democratic Republic at the invitation of the National Committee of the National Front of Democratic Germany and the Association for Foreign Cultural Relations to participate in the celebrations of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic.

### 参加中德两国建国十周年庆祝活动  中德友协代表团去柏林

1959-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    参加中德两国建国十周年庆祝活动
    中德友协代表团去柏林
    新华社24日讯　中华人民共和国德意志民主共和国友好协会代表团一行五人，在友协副会长、中共中央候补委员、最高人民法院副院长高克林率领下，今天乘飞机离开北京去德意志民主共和国。
    他们是应民主德国全国阵线全国委员会和对外文化联络协会的邀请，去德意志民主共和国参加中华人民共和国和德意志民主共和国成立十周年庆祝活动的。
