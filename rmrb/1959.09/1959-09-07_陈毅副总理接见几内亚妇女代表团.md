Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Guinean Women's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-09-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 6th. Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received the Guinean women's delegation headed by Mariama Mataide Camara this evening. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China. Present at the reception was Cao Mengjun, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China.

### 陈毅副总理接见几内亚妇女代表团

1959-09-07
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见几内亚妇女代表团
    新华社6日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天晚间接见了以玛丽亚姆·马太德·卡马拉为首的几内亚妇女代表团。这个代表团是应中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会邀请来我国访问的。接见时在座的有中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会主席团委员曹孟君。
