Title: Ode to the Signal
Author: Guo Huang
Date: 1959-09-03
Page: 8

    Ode to the Signal
    Guo Huang
    Young postman,
    What makes your face blush with a smile?
    Wheels spin outside the door,
    Your voice bursts into my ears!
    Ah! Resolution of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee!
    Every word shines like gold,
    Every sentence illuminates the heart,
    I excitedly spread out the newspaper,
    A blueprint of happiness unfolded before me.
    I see steel mills spewing fiery waterfalls,
    And evergreen springs in the communes…
    Rows of words are like blue waves,
    The motherland surges forward in the resolution!
    Ah, all the high mountains and great rivers of the motherland,
    Please listen carefully to this resolution,
    Remember this signal for a vigorous advance:
    Increase production and practice thrift,
    March towards a more beautiful tomorrow!
    Young postman,
    See how fast your vehicle is, like an arrow,
    I join you in shouting: Motherland
    Ah! Great Leap Forward!
    Let every word of the resolution become a golden whip on a swift horse!

### 信号颂
郭煌
1959-09-03
第8版()
专栏：

    信号颂
    郭煌
    年轻的邮递员呵，
    是什么笑红了你的脸？
    车轮儿在门外旋转，
    你的嗓音就冲到了我的耳边！
    啊！八届八中全会决议！
    字字起金光，
    句句亮心间，
    我兴奋地把报纸摊开，
    幸福的蓝图摊在面前。
    我看见钢厂在喷射火的瀑布，
    公社里有常绿的春天……
    一排排字迹像蓝色的波涛，
    祖国在决议里拨浪而前！
    啊，祖国所有的高山大川，
    请都把这决议细听一遍，
    记住这奋战进军的信号：
    增产节约，
    奔向更美的明天！
    年轻的邮递员啊，
    看你的车快得多么像箭，
    我同你一起去呼喊：祖国
    啊！跃进！
    让决议的每个字都变成快马的金鞭！
