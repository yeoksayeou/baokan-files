Title: At Deputy Prime Minister Naim's Farewell Banquet, Deputy Prime Minister Naim and Premier Zhou Warmly Congratulate the Further Development of Sino-Afghan Friendly Relations; Asian and African Countries Should Hold High the Banner of the Bandung Conference; Adhering to the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence Will Enable Mutual Friendship Without External Interference

Author:
Date: 1959-09-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th - Afghan Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Sardar Mohammad Naim held a farewell banquet at the Beijing Hotel this evening. Prince Naim and his entourage will leave Beijing tomorrow to visit various parts of our country.

Deputy Prime Minister Naim and Premier Zhou Enlai spoke at the banquet, warmly congratulating the further development of the friendship between the people of China and Afghanistan and the friendly relations between the two countries.

In his speech, Deputy Prime Minister Naim said that everything we have seen in China in the past few days is forever worth remembering with pleasure. The warm welcome and cordial reception I and my friends have received fully demonstrate the deep understanding and friendship between our two countries. I hope that the understanding and friendly feelings between our two countries can develop forever.

Deputy Prime Minister Naim said, we firmly believe that there is no other path for the future of mankind, but only one path, which is to enhance understanding and live together in mutual friendship.

Premier Zhou Enlai said in his speech that he was very happy to attend such a harmonious and friendly banquet today. Premier Zhou said, we believe that as long as our Asian and African countries hold high the banner of the Bandung Conference and adhere to the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, we will be able to be friendly to each other without being subject to external interference.

Premier Zhou said, I agree with what Prince Naim said the day before yesterday, that the friendly coexistence of the Kingdom of Afghanistan and the People's Republic of China is a model of friendly coexistence between Asian and African countries, and I believe that this model of friendly relations will continue to develop.

Invited to the banquet were Vice Premier He Long and Chen Yi, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Shen Junru, and Cheng Qian, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, our Ambassador to Afghanistan Hao Ting, heads of various departments of our government, people's organizations, democratic parties, senior generals of the People's Liberation Army, and people from all walks of life in the capital. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China were invited to attend the banquet.

Xinhua News Agency, 8th - When visiting the Central Institute for Nationalities this afternoon, Afghan Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Sardar Mohammad Naim said to the head of the institute: "In education, you have preserved the characteristics of all ethnic groups and their different religious beliefs, which will surely make your great country a happy family for all ethnic groups."

At noon today, Deputy Prime Minister Naim and other Afghan dignitaries, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and Deputy Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, visited the Central Institute for Nationalities. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by Xiong Shouqi, Zong Qun, the vice presidents of the Nationalities Institute, and students from various ethnic groups.

After listening to Xiong Shouqi's introduction to the Nationalities Institute, Deputy Prime Minister Naim said: "I am very happy to see that this institute not only spreads knowledge to ethnic minorities, but also creates an atmosphere of mutual respect and unity in your country."

The distinguished guests then visited student dormitories, the halal cafeteria, and the reading room.

As the distinguished guests were leaving, Deputy Prime Minister Naim shook hands with everyone who came to see them off and said: "I wish you greater achievements and wish you to cultivate more ethnic minority students. Here, I would also like to, in my own name and on behalf of the people of all ethnic groups in Afghanistan, greet the Chinese students of all ethnic groups and the Chinese people of all ethnic groups."

In the morning, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and Deputy Mayor Wu Han, visited the Tiananmen Square construction project, and in the afternoon they visited the Summer Palace. (Picture attached)

Top: Deputy Prime Minister Naim, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Vice Premier Chen Yi at the banquet.

### 在纳伊姆副首相告别宴会上  纳伊姆副首相和周总理热烈祝贺中阿友好关系进一步发展  亚非各国要高举万隆会议旗帜  坚持和平共处五项原则，就能够互相友好，而不受外来干涉的影响

1959-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    在纳伊姆副首相告别宴会上
    纳伊姆副首相和周总理热烈祝贺中阿友好关系进一步发展
    亚非各国要高举万隆会议旗帜
    坚持和平共处五项原则，就能够互相友好，而不受外来干涉的影响
    新华社8日讯　阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆亲王今天晚间在北京饭店举行告别宴会。纳伊姆亲王和他的随行人员明天将离开北京到我国各地参观访问。
    纳伊姆副首相和周恩来总理在宴会上先后讲话，热烈祝贺中阿两国人民友谊和两国友好关系的进一步发展。
    纳伊姆副首相在讲话中说，我们这几天在中国看到的一切，永远值得我们愉快地怀念。我和我的朋友们受到的热烈欢迎和亲切接待，充分表明我们两国有着深切的了解和友谊。我希望我们两国之间的了解和亲善的情谊能够永远地发展下去。
    纳伊姆副首相说，我们深信，人类的将来没有其他道路可以选择，而只有一条道路，这就是增进了解和相互友好地相处。
    周恩来总理在讲话中首先表示，他很高兴参加今天这样和谐的、充满友谊的宴会。周总理说，我们相信，我们亚非各国只要高举万隆会议的旗帜，坚持和平共处五项原则，我们就能够互相友好，而不受外来干涉的影响。
    周总理说，我同意纳伊姆亲王前天说过的话，阿富汗王国和中华人民共和国的友好相处是亚非各国友好相处的模范，并且相信这种模范的友好关系会继续发展下去。
    应邀出席宴会的，有国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅，全国人民代表大会常委会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、程潜，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长章汉夫，我国驻阿富汗大使郝汀，我国政府各部门、人民团体、民主党派的负责人、人民解放军高级将领以及首都各界人士。各国驻中国使节应邀出席了宴会。
    新华社8日讯　阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆亲王，今天中午在参观中央民族学院的时候，向这个学院的负责人说：“在教育当中，你们保存了各民族的特点和他们的不同的宗教信仰，这一定使你们这个伟大的国家成为各民族欢乐的大家庭。”
    今天中午，纳伊姆副首相和其他阿富汗贵宾在陈毅副总理和北京市副市长吴晗陪同下，参观了中央民族学院。贵宾们受到民族学院副院长熊寿祺、宗群和各民族学生的热烈欢迎。
    纳伊姆副首相在听了熊寿祺对民族学院的介绍后说：“我很高兴地看到，这个学院不仅把知识传播到少数民族当中去，而且更在你们的国家中造成了相互尊重、相互团结的气氛。”
    贵宾们接着参观了学生宿舍、清真食堂和阅览室。
    贵宾们在离开的时候，纳伊姆副首相同送行的人们一一握手，并且说：“我祝你们取得更大的成就，祝你们培养出更多的少数民族学生。在这里，我还要以个人名义并且代表阿富汗的各族人民，问候中国各民族学生和中国各族人民。”
    贵宾们在陈毅副总理和吴晗副市长陪同下，上午参观了天安门广场建筑工程，下午游览了颐和园。（附图片）
