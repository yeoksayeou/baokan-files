Title: China's Journalism Circles Jointly Hold Reception to Welcome Journalists from Various Countries
Author: 
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: China's All-China Journalists Association, People's Daily, Xinhua News Agency, Broadcasting Administration, and the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs jointly held a reception on the eve of National Day to welcome journalist delegations and journalists from various countries who are visiting China to participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the country.

Invited to attend the reception were the Iraqi journalist delegation headed by Abdullah Abbas Ali, President and Editor-in-Chief of the Iraqi "Al-Jumhuriyah" newspaper; the Chilean journalist delegation headed by Juan Emilio Pacull, President of the Chilean Journalists Association; the Sudanese journalist delegation headed by Salih Orabi, Editor-in-Chief of the Sudanese "Al-Telegraph Weekly"; Samuel Wainer, founder of the Brazilian "Ultima Hora"; Uruguayan journalists Carlos Borche and Hugo Rodriguez Vela; and journalists from the cultural delegations of Mexico, Venezuela, Italy, Belgium, France, Switzerland, Brazil, and other countries who were invited to China by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, as well as journalists stationed in Beijing from various countries, news officials from embassies of various countries in China, and foreign experts working in Chinese news organizations.

Wu Lengxi, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, in his speech at the beginning of the reception, extended a warm welcome on behalf of Beijing's news organizations to the journalists from various countries who are visiting China. He expressed the hope that journalists from all countries will cooperate more closely and strengthen friendly exchanges in the future, and work together to safeguard the cause of world peace.

Mei Yi and Wang Yunsheng, Vice Presidents of the All-China Journalists Association, Gong Peng, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and heads of various news organizations in Beijing attended the reception.

### 我国新闻界联合举行招待会  欢迎各国新闻工作者

1959-09-30
第3版()
专栏：

    我国新闻界联合举行招待会
    欢迎各国新闻工作者
    新华社29日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会、人民日报、新华通讯社、广播事业局和外交部新闻司，在国庆节前夕联合举行招待会，欢迎来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的各国新闻工作者代表团和新闻工作者。
    应邀出席招待会的有以伊拉克
  “国民报”社长兼主编阿卜杜拉·阿巴斯·阿里为首的伊拉克新闻工作者代表团，以智利记者协会主席胡安·埃米略·帕库耳为首的智利新闻工作者代表团，以苏丹“电讯周报”主编萨利赫奥拉比为首的苏丹新闻工作者代表团，巴西“最后一点钟报”创办人萨穆埃耳·韦纳尔，乌拉圭新闻工作者卡洛斯·博尔切和乌果·罗德里格塞·贝拉，以及应对外文协邀请来我国的墨西哥、委内瑞拉、意大利、比利时、法国、瑞士、巴西等国文化代表团中的新闻工作者，以及各国驻京记者、各国驻华使馆的新闻官员和在我国新闻机构中工作的外国专家。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长吴冷西在招待会开始时的致词中，代表北京各新闻单位对来我国访问的各国新闻工作者表示热烈欢迎。他希望今后各国新闻工作者更密切合作和加强友好往来，共同为维护世界和平事业而努力。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长梅益、王芸生，外交部新闻司司长龚澎，以及北京各新闻单位的负责人都出席了这一招待会。
