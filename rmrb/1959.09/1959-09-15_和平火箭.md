Title: Peace Rocket
Author: Guo Moruo
Date: 1959-09-15
Page: 5

    Peace Rocket
    Guo Moruo's aim is accurate, truly accurate! One arrow hits the center of the moon. It seems that human travel to the moon will not be too far off. Since the moon can be hit so accurately, no place on earth will be missed. Therefore, this red rocket that hit the moon can also shatter the dreams of war maniacs. No one in the world wants war, and everyone can see the superiority of socialism. Cold War policies can be put back in the basket; let everyone seriously consider world peace. Let everyone compete peacefully in science, ensuring the future happiness of all mankind. Banning nuclear weapons, dismantling military bases, no need to delay any longer, should be accelerated. The peace rocket cheers in the sky: humanity's destiny will be determined by itself, and the people of the world will unite and no longer be freely manipulated by war maniacs! World peace is the ocean of human progress, and the people of all countries will move in one direction. If war maniacs are stubbornly ignorant, they are like dead pufferfish, thrown onto the shore.
          September 14, 1959

### 和平火箭
郭沫若
1959-09-15
第5版()
专栏：

    和平火箭
    郭沫若射得准呵，真是射得准，一箭便射中了月球的中心。看来人要到月球中去旅行，已经不会是太长远的事情。月球既然可以如意地命中，地上任何地点都将不会落空。因此射中月球的这只红色火箭，也可射破了战争狂人的迷梦。世界人类谁都不要战争，谁都看得到社会主义的优越性。冷战政策可以收进网篮里了，让大家认真地考虑到世界和平。让大家在科学上和平竞赛，保证全人类的幸福的将来。禁止核武器，撤消军事基地，用不着再作拖延，应该加快。和平的火箭在空中欢呼：人类的命运将由自己作主，全世界人民一致团结起来，不再听战争狂人的自由摆布！世界和平是人类进展的海洋，各国人民都会走上一个方向。战争狂人如果是死不觉悟，就像是死河豚，把它抛到岸上。
          一九五九年九月十四日
