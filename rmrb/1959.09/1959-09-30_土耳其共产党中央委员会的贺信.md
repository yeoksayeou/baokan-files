Title: Letter of Greetings from the Central Committee of the Turkish Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 9

Comrades!
The Turkish Communist Party and its Central Committee, in the name of the Turkish workers and the Turkish people, pay tribute to the tenth anniversary of the People's Republic of China.

In these ten years, the great Chinese people, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and its outstanding leaders, have made tremendous achievements in economic, social and political fields, and have become a great force for maintaining world peace.

At present, reactionary forces and the Cold War are becoming increasingly unpopular. The leading country of the socialist camp, the Soviet Union, is moving towards communism at an accelerating pace. The rapid development of technology, science and economy fully proves this fact. Soviet scientists launched rockets to the moon for the first time, and Comrade Khrushchev's successful visit to the United States disrupted the plans of the Cold War planners.

The colonial people are supported by the People's Republic of China and all socialist countries in their struggle against the colonialists for freedom and independence. The Turkish people's struggle for national liberation draws strength from these supports.

The Chinese people drove away the colonialists of the United States and other countries, and the Turkish people have once again begun the struggle to expel these people. In order to achieve their goals, the Turkish people have established a National Independence Front. Restoring diplomatic and friendly relations with People's China is also one of the many goals of the Turkish people and the Communist Party.

I wish the Chinese people great new achievements on the road to building socialism.

Long live the People's Republic of China!

Long live the glorious Chinese Communist Party!

Long live the friendship between the Turkish and Chinese people!
September 28, 1959

### 土耳其共产党中央委员会的贺信

1959-09-30
第9版()
专栏：

    土耳其共产党中央委员会的贺信同志们！
    土耳其共产党及其中央委员会以土耳其劳动者及土耳其人民的名义，向中华人民共和国十周年致敬。
    在此十年中，伟大的中国人民在中国共产党及其杰出的领导人的领导下，在经济、社会和政治各方面取得了巨大的成就，成为一支巨大的维护世界和平的力量。
    目前反动势力和冷战越来越不得人心。社会主义阵营的领导国家，苏联正在以日益加快的速度向共产主义迈进。技术、科学和经济的飞速发展充分地证明了这一事实。苏联科学家第一次将火箭放射到月球，赫鲁晓夫同志访问美国的成功，打乱了冷战策划者的计划。
    殖民地人民在反对殖民者、争取自由和独立的斗争中受到人民中国以及所有社会主义国家的支持。土耳其人民争取民族解放的斗争从这些支持中汲取到力量。
    中国人民赶走了美国和其他国家的殖民主义者，土耳其人民也已经再次开始了驱逐这些人的斗争。为了实现自己的目标，土耳其人民建立了民族独立阵线。恢复与人民中国的外交和友好关系也是土耳其人民和共产党的多项目标之一。
    祝中国人民在建设社会主义的道路上取得伟大的新成就。
    人民中国万岁！
    光荣的中国共产党万岁！
    土耳其和中国人民之间的友谊万岁！
                                              1959年9月28日
