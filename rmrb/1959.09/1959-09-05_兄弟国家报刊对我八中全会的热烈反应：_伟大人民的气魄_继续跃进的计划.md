Fraternal Countries' Newspapers' Enthusiastic Reactions to the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee:
    The Spirit of a Great People, Plans for Continued Leap Forward

    Soviet Newspapers: Chinese Working People's High Enthusiasm
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 4th: Soviet newspapers prominently featured the documents of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Premier Zhou Enlai's report at the plenary session of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress. They also reported on the Chinese people's enthusiastic support for the resolutions of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the CPC.
    On August 28th, "Pravda," under the general title "On Increasing Production and Practicing Economy", published the full text of the Resolution of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party on launching a campaign to increase production and practice economy, the main points of Premier Zhou Enlai's report at the plenary session of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and the communique of the Chinese State Statistical Bureau on revising the 1958 agricultural statistics, all in a full-page spread. "Izvestia," "Trud," "Sovetskaya Rossiya," "Komsomolskaya Pravda," "Krasnaya Zvezda," and "Gudok" also published prominent excerpts of Premier Zhou Enlai's report on the same day. "Trud's" headline was "The Spirit of a Great People," and "Komsomolskaya Pravda's" headline was "Magnificent Plans."
    The previous day, August 27th, "Pravda," "Izvestia," "Trud," "Krasnaya Zvezda," and "Sovetskaya Rossiya" all published the full text of the communique of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the CPC. "Pravda" occupied about half of its third page to publish the full text of the communique. "Izvestia" added the large-character headline "The Great Tasks of the Chinese People" when publishing the communique. "Trud's" headline was "Socialism – The General Line."
    In recent days, some Moscow newspapers have also successively published news from TASS and Xinhua News Agency reporting on the Chinese people's enthusiastic support for the resolutions of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the CPC. On August 30th, "Pravda" reported the news of the Chinese people's support for the resolutions of the Central Committee of the CPC under the headline "The Chinese People Enthusiastically Support the Resolutions of the Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC." On August 29th, "Izvestia" used the headline "Responding to the Call of the Chinese Communist Party" when reporting the above news, "Komsomolskaya Pravda's" headline was "The Unity of the Party and the People," "Sovetskaya Rossiya's" headline was "The High Enthusiasm of the Chinese Working People," and "Air Force Daily's" headline was "The Entire Chinese People Support the Resolutions of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC."

    Korean Newspapers: Completing the Five-Year Plan in Two Years is the Pride of the Times
    Imperialists Trembled Before the Great Victory of the Chinese People
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 4th: Korean newspapers have been publishing the documents of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the CPC in prominent positions for days, and have published editorials and commentaries, enthusiastically hailing the great victories achieved by the Chinese people in the Great Leap Forward.
    On August 30th, "Rodong Sinmun" and "Minju Choson" published the full text of Premier Zhou Enlai's "Report on Adjusting the Major Targets of the 1959 National Economic Plan and Further Developing the Campaign to Increase and Practice Economy." "Rodong Sinmun" also published the full text of the "People's Daily" editorial published on the 27th, "Oppose Right Deviation, Boost Enthusiasm, and Struggle to Complete the Main Targets of the Second Five-Year Plan This Year." North Korea's "Democratic Youth Daily" published a commentary entitled "The Great Victory of the Chinese People in the Leap Forward."
    Prior to this, on August 28th, "Rodong Sinmun" published the full text of the communique of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the CPC under the banner headline "The Chinese People Continue to Leap Forward in Socialist Construction," and simultaneously published a commentary entitled "The Brilliant Victory of the General Line of the Chinese Communist Party." The commentary pointed out: The general line of the Chinese Communist Party for building socialism united hundreds of millions of people and continued to bring forth great material forces like the ocean. To increase industry and agricultural products by tens of percentage points in one year, and to complete the five-year plan in two years, is indeed the pride of the times, and cannot be said to be anything less than a miracle. The commentary said that despite the slander of imperialists and their lackeys, the Chinese people, illuminated by the Party's general line, achieved an unprecedented Great Leap Forward last year, and are continuing to leap forward this year, and the people's communes in various places have also been consolidated.
    On the same day, "Minju Choson," "Pyongyang Sinmun," "Korean People's Army Daily" published in Pyongyang, and "Kaesong Sinmun," "Kangwon Ilbo," "Jagang Daily," "Hwangnam Daily" published in local areas, all published the full text of the communique of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the CPC.
    "Rodong Sinmun" and "Korean People's Army Daily" published the full text of the Resolution of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the CPC on further developing the campaign to increase production and practice economy on August 29th. "Rodong Sinmun" also published an editorial entitled "The Great Victory of the Chinese People" on the front page in the top position. The editorial said that the imperialists trembled before the great victory of the Chinese people, and "the Chinese people are continuing to advance while smashing the enemy's slander, defamation, and sabotage activities." The commentary published by "Minju Choson" was titled "The Fraternal Chinese People are Advancing Victoriously." The commentary said that the new and important achievements made by the Chinese people in various sectors of the national economy in the first half of this year proved the complete correctness of the general line of the Chinese Communist Party and once again demonstrated to the world the victorious advance of the Chinese people in the Great Leap Forward.
    On September 1st, "Rodong Sinmun" published the full text of the "People's Daily" editorial published on August 29th, entitled "Long Live the People's Communes."
    From August 30th to September 2nd, Korean newspapers published news such as "The Chinese People Warmly Welcome the Communique and Resolutions of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the CPC" and "The First Anniversary of the Chinese People's Commune Movement," and successively published talks by Ri Hyo-sun, Chairman of the Central Committee of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea, Park Yong-guk, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Democratic Youth League of Korea, Kim Young-soo, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Women's League, and Labor Hero Choi Seong-soo on the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the CPC.

    Vietnamese Newspapers: China's Line of Building Socialism is Very Correct
    The People's Communes are a Very Good Organizational Form, and the Mass Steelmaking Movement has Opened Up the Road for the Future Development of the Steel Industry
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 4th: The Vietnamese newspapers "Nhan Dan," "Thoi Dai Moi," and "Thu Do Hanoi" have all prominently published the main contents of Premier Zhou Enlai's "Report of Adjusting the Major Targets of the 1959 National Economic Plan and Further Developing the Campaign of Increasing Production and Practicing Economy" in recent days.
    "Nhan Dan's" headline was: "The Chinese People Will Surely Achieve Even More Brilliant Victories on the Road of Building Socialism." "Thu Do Hanoi's" headline was: "Premier Zhou Enlai Emphasized the Superiority of the People's Communes and the Important Role of the Mass Steelmaking Movement."
    "Thoi Dai Moi's" headline was: "China's Industry and Agriculture Have Achieved Unprecedentedly Great Achievements. The People's Communes are a Very Good Organizational Form. The Mass Steelmaking Movement has Opened Up the Road for the Future Development of the Steel Industry."
    Since the publication of the communique of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the CPC, Hanoi's "Nhan Dan," "Thoi Dai Moi," and "Thu Do Hanoi" have all successively published this important document in full on the front page in prominent positions. "Thoi Dai Moi" also published the resolution of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the CPC. The newspapers also successively published news of the enlarged meeting of the Standing Committee of the Second National People's Congress and the resolutions to launch a campaign to increase production and practice economy, as well as news of the Chinese people's enthusiastic response to the call of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the CPC.
    Vietnamese newspapers and readers attach great importance to the various documents and news of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the CPC.
    "Nhan Dan," the organ of the Vietnamese Labor Party, published an editorial on the 28th entitled "The Chinese People Continue to Leap Forward on the Road of Building Socialism," pointing out: "The fact that the Chinese people will complete the Five-Year Plan three years ahead of schedule brilliantly demonstrates the pace of the great Chinese people's progress under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao. Compared with the declining and stagnant production of imperialist countries, the current continuous and great progress of China's economy is demonstrating the superiority of the socialist system over the capitalist system." The article pointed out that the imperialists' attempts to deny China's achievements are bound to fail.
    "Thoi Dai Moi" published an editorial on the 29th entitled "The New Victories of the Chinese People Gladden the Vietnamese People," pointing out: China's tremendous achievements in building socialism "eloquently prove that the Chinese Communist Party's line of building socialism is very correct. The wisdom and strength of the Chinese people are inexhaustible."
    The newspapers used eye-catching headlines when publishing the documents and news of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the CPC. "Nhan Dan's" headline was: "The Chinese People Continue to Leap Forward and Will Complete the Main Targets of the Second Five-Year Plan Ahead of Schedule This Year."

    Mongolian Newspapers: The Diligent and Brave Chinese People's Strength is Inexhaustible
    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, September 4th: The Mongolian newspaper "Unen" published the full text of the resolution of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC on launching the campaign to increase production and practice economy on September 3rd.
    On September 2nd, this newspaper published a commentary entitled "The New Achievements of the Chinese People's Leap Forward Encourage Us." The commentary said that the communique and resolutions of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC are great events in the new life construction of the great Chinese people. The outstanding achievements made by the Chinese Communist Party in the socialist construction of industry and agriculture make the people of socialist countries and all progressive mankind feel infinitely happy, and encourage them to move towards new victories. The commentary pointed out: The Chinese people are continuing the leap forward in various sectors of the national economy that began last year and in the first half of this year, and have launched a struggle to complete the great task of the Second Five-Year Plan this year. This is a great victory for the 600 million Chinese people, and at the same time, it shows the correctness of the political line followed by the Chinese Communist Party in socialist construction. The great achievements of China's socialist construction mark the inexhaustible strength of the diligent and brave Chinese people.
    On August 29th, "Unen" published the full text of the communique of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC.
    On August 28th, the Mongolian National Radio broadcast the full text of the communique of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC.

    Czechoslovak Newspapers: People's Communes Promote the Comprehensive Development of the Rural Economy
    The Famous Great Leap Forward Shocked the World, and Even the Enemies Who are Incompatible with the Chinese People Cannot Deny It
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 4th: Czechoslovak newspapers have recently been publishing documents from the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC and publishing commentaries hailing the Great Leap Forward of the Chinese people.
    "Rude Pravo," "Prace," and "Zemedelske Noviny" published summaries of Premier Zhou Enlai's report at the plenary session of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on August 29th under the headlines "China's Industrial Production Increased by 65% Compared to the First Half of Last Year," "Industrial Products Increased by 20%-100%," and "China's Economic Strength is Rising Sharply." On August 30th, "Rude Pravo," "Svobodne Slovo," and "Zemedelske Noviny" published summaries of this report under the headlines "People's Communes are a Very Good Thing," "People's Communes Have Been Tested in China," and "People's Communes Have Shown Their Strength."
    "Rude Pravo" published a detailed summary of the communique of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC and the resolutions of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee on August 27th. "Lidova Armada," "Zemedelske Noviny," "Svobodne Slovo," "Lidova Demokracie," "Pravda (Bratislava)," "Mlada Fronta," and "Prace" all published communiques, summaries of resolutions, or news about the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC.
    "Prace" published a commentary entitled "People's China is Moving Forward Unstoppably" on August 28th.
    "Svobodne Slovo" also published a commentary on August 29th. The headline was: "The Great Leap Forward Continues." "Rude Pravo" published a commentary entitled "The Great Leap Forward Continues" on August 30th. "Zemedelske Noviny" published a commentary entitled "The Great Leap Forward" on September 3rd.
    "Rude Pravo" emphasized in its commentary that the main achievement of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party proved the correctness of the Party's general line. It said that China's movement to establish and develop people's communes last year, the movement to rapidly increase steel production, and the extraordinarily good harvests in agriculture are undoubtedly the most glorious page in China's history. The commentary emphasized that this is an unprecedented revolution in the world. "Today in China, what is decisive is not adjusting the plan," but "completing the Five-Year Plan three years ahead of schedule and catching up with Britain in ten years."
    "Prace's" commentary pointed out that completing the main tasks originally scheduled for 1962 this year is the result of thoroughly implementing the general line formulated by the Central Committee of the CPC, and is the result of creatively applying the principles of Marxism-Leninism to China's specific conditions. The newspaper said that the famous Great Leap Forward shocked the world, and even the enemies who are incompatible with the Chinese people cannot deny it. Due to the continued consolidation of the people's communes, they are promoting the rapid and comprehensive progress of all aspects of rural residents' lives.
    On the evening of August 26th, Czechoslovak Radio broadcast the news of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC and the main contents of the plenary session's communique several times.

    Newspapers of the German Democratic Republic: China is Building Socialism with an Irresistible Force
    Western Newspapers, Facing the Great Changes in Socialist China, Bury Their Heads in the Sand Like Ostriches
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 4th: The German Democratic Republic's "Wirtschaftswoche" published an article on September 2nd entitled "China Continues to Leap Forward," praising the communique and resolutions of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC. The article pointed out that the final figures for China's production last year greatly exceeded those of 1957, which is a particularly gratifying increase.
    Prior to this, on August 27th, "Neues Deutschland" published a detailed summary of the communique of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee on the front page of its international section. The large-character headline was: "On the Eve of the Tenth Anniversary of the National Day of the Asian Socialist Power, People's China Completes the Main Tasks of the Five-Year Plan in Two Years." The newspaper also published a boxed news item on the front page entitled "The Great Leap Forward of People's China," briefly writing that China's industrial production in the first half of this year increased by 65% compared with the same period last year, and that it would catch up with Britain in ten years. On the same day, "Berliner Zeitung," "Junge Welt," and "National-Zeitung" also published news about the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC. ADN issued a detailed summary of the plenary session's communique and news about the meetings of the State Council and the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.
    On August 28th, "Neues Deutschland" published an article on the seventh page by ADN's Beijing correspondent, Hans Hick, entitled: "China Continues to Leap Forward." This article introduced the leaping speed of the Chinese people's socialist construction. The author wrote: "The people of all countries respect and praise the 650 million Chinese people who are firmly and irresistibly building socialism in their powerful country." On the same day, "Berliner Zeitung" published a commentary under the title "China is Advancing," saying: "China will catch up with Britain in ten years instead of fifteen years as originally planned for some important industrial products. The Central Committee of the CPC made this historically significant decision at the Eighth Plenary Session." The commentary pointed out that Western newspapers, facing this great change in socialist China, bury their heads in the sand like ostriches. This tactic of keeping silent about achievements and distorting the truth has been repeatedly defeated throughout history. "Junge Welt" and "Neue Zeit" also published summaries of the plenary session's communique on this day.
    "Volksarmee," which also published a commentary on the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC, believed that the new targets announced by China for 1959 were truly great targets.

    Polish Newspapers: China's Economic Plan This Year is a Program for the Great Leap Forward
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, September 4th: Poland's "Trybuna Ludu," "Zycie Warszawy," "Dziennik Ludowy," "Glos Pracy," "Sztandar Mlodych," and most Polish provincial newspapers (including the provincial committee organs of the United Workers' Party and the Democratic Party) as well as "Express Wieczorny" in Warsaw, all published news about the Chinese Communist Party holding the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee and the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, as well as summaries of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee's communique, on August 27th.
    "Trybuna Ludu" published news about the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee and the meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on the front page under the title "The Central Committee Plenary Session of the CPC on the Issue of Further Development of the National Economy," and published a detailed summary of the communique of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee on the second page. The headlines of the other newspapers were: "Zycie Warszawy": "China Adjusts the 1959 National Economic Plan, Which is Still a Broad Program for the Great Leap Forward"; "Glos Pracy": "Important Resolutions of the Eighth Plenary Session of the CPC and the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Adjusting the Economic Plan and Continuing to Adhere to the Principles of the Great Leap Forward"; "Sztandar Mlodych": "The Five-Year Plan Will Be Completed in Two Years, and China Will Catch Up with Britain in Ten Years."
    Some provincial newspapers used the following headlines when publishing summaries of the communique of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC and news of the meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress: "The Chinese People Have Overtaken the Calendar by Three Years" (Lodz Provincial Newspaper), "People's China Will Reach the Production Level Originally Scheduled for 1962 This Year" (Krakow Provincial Newspaper), "Communique of the Eighth Plenary Session of the CPC Central Committee: It is Entirely Possible to Complete the Main Product Targets of Industrial and Agricultural Production Originally Scheduled for the Second Five-Year Plan This Year" (Bialystok Provincial Newspaper), "Eighth Plenary Session of the CPC Central Committee: The Adjusted Economic Tasks for 1959 are Still a Plan for the Great Leap Forward, China Satisfiedly Welcomes the Visit of Soviet and American Leaders to Each Other" (Szczecin Provincial Newspaper), "Communique of the Eighth Plenary Session of the CPC Central Committee: China Continues to Leap Forward" (Poznan Provincial Newspaper).

    Hungarian Newspapers: Important Decisions of China to Develop the National Economy
    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, September 4th: On August 28th, the Hungarian newspapers "Nepszabadsag" and "Nepakarat" published summaries of Premier Zhou Enlai's report at the plenary session of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress. On August 29th, "Magyar Nemzet" also published a summary of this report.
    On August 28th, "Nepszabadsag" and "Nepakarat" also published summaries of the communique and resolutions of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC.
    "Magyar Nemzet" published a summary of Premier Zhou's report on the 29th, and published news of the Eighth Plenary Session of the CPC Central Committee on the first page on the 28th under the title "Important Decisions of China on the Development of the National Economy," and published a comprehensive summary of the communique and resolutions of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee on the second page under the title "China's Decisions," with small subheadings in the text such as: "The Five-Year Plan May Be Completed Ahead of Schedule," "Steel Growth 50%, Coal Growth 24%," "Favorable International Environment." The newspaper also published the resolutions of the Supreme State Council and the plenary session of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress. Budapest's "Esti Hirlap" published news of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee on the front page on the 28th, titled: "Important Resolutions of the Central Committee Meeting of the Chinese Communist Party."
    On the evening of August 27th and the morning of August 28th, Kossuth Radio broadcast the resolutions and communique of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee.

    Bulgarian Newspapers: Achievements of Creatively Applying Marxism-Leninism
    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, September 4th: Bulgaria's "Rabotnichesko Delo," "Otechestven Front," "Zemedelsko Zname," "Narodna Mladezh," "Trud," and "Narodna Armiya" all published detailed summaries of the communique of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC in prominent positions on the 28th.
    "Rabotnichesko Delo" and "Otechestven Front" published this document on the first page, and the other newspapers published it on the third and fourth pages. "Rabotnichesko Delo's" headline was: "Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party," "Otechestven Front" - "More, Faster, Better, Cheaper," "Zemedelsko Zname" - "Continued Great Leap Forward in China's National Economy," "Narodna Mladezh" - "The Fighting Tasks of the Chinese People," "Trud" - "The Great Tasks of a Great People."
    On August 29th, "Trud" published a detailed summary of the resolutions of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC, titled: "The Great Determination and Great Wisdom of 650 Million Diligent and Brave Chinese People."
    "Otechestven Front," "Zemedelsko Zname," "Narodna Armiya," and "Narodna Mladezh" all published brief summaries of this resolution on the third and fourth pages. The headlines were: "China's Achievements are Great, the Future is Bright," "Achievements of Creatively Applying Marxism-Leninism," "Striving for Greater Victories This Year," "Our Achievements are Great, Our Future is Bright."
    On August 30th, "Rabotnichesko Delo," "Zemedelsko Zname," and "Narodna Armiya" prominently published news of the Chinese people's warm support for the resolutions of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee on the fourth page. "Rabotnichesko Delo's" headline was: "The Great Leap Forward Continues - The Entire Chinese People Support the Resolutions of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC." The common headline of the other two newspapers was: "The Entire Chinese People Support the Resolutions of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC."
    Prior to this, on August 27th, the central newspapers of Bulgaria all reported the news of the Central Committee of the CPC holding the Eighth Plenary Session under the title "Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party."

    Albanian Newspapers: China's Economic Achievements are Great
    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, September 4th: "The 650 Million Chinese People Express Their Determination to Realize the Party's General Line of Socialist Construction," under this headline, Albania's "Luftëtari" published news of the Chinese laboring people in various regions warmly supporting the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee.
    Albanian newspapers prominently featured news about the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC. "Zeri i Popullit" published news on the front page of the international section on August 30th about the achievements of the first half of the year in Premier Zhou Enlai's report at the meeting of the Standing Committee of the Second National People's Congress, titled: "The New Achievements of the Chinese People." On the same day, "Bashkimi" also published the aforementioned news in a prominent position on the international section, and indicated the production figures of major industrial products in the first half of the year in boldface, with the main title: "China's Economic Situation is Good, the Achievements are Great, the General Trend is Continuing to Leap Forward," and the subtitle: "The Achievements of the Chinese People's Economic Leap Forward." Tirana Radio quoted some figures from Premier Zhou Enlai's report in its broadcast on the 28th.
    "Zeri i Popullit" published a detailed summary of the communique of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC on the third page on the 28th, occupying nearly half a page. On the same day, "Bashkimi" also published a summary of the plenary session's communique.

    Romanian Newspapers: The Chinese People Warmly Support the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee
    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, September 4th: The newspapers of the Romanian capital have all published Premier Zhou Enlai's report at the plenary session of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress in a relatively prominent position, and have successively published news reporting on the widespread support of the Chinese people for the resolutions of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee.
    "Scinteia," "Romania Libera," and others published summaries of Premier Zhou Enlai's report in a relatively prominent position on the fourth page on August 28th. The radio also broadcast this news.
    The capital's newspapers and radio stations had previously published news of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC and published summaries of the resolutions of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPC on August 27th and August 29th, respectively. The excerpted content of the resolutions included verified production of coal, steel, grain, and cotton in 1958, production in the first half of 1959, suggestions to the State Council to revise this year's main production targets, catching up with Britain in the output of major industrial products within ten years, and launching a competition to increase production and practice economy, etc. The newspapers also published summaries of the evaluations of the general line, the Great Leap Forward, and the People's Communes in the resolutions, as well as the refutation of domestic and foreign reactionaries.

### 兄弟国家报刊对我八中全会的热烈反应：  伟大人民的气魄　继续跃进的计划

1959-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    兄弟国家报刊对我八中全会的热烈反应：
    伟大人民的气魄　继续跃进的计划
    苏联报刊：中国劳动人民热情高涨
    新华社莫斯科4日电　苏联各报以显著地位刊载了中国共产党八届八中全会的文件以及周恩来总理在全国人民代表大会常务委员会全体会议上的报告，并且报道了关于中国人民热烈拥护中共八届八中全会的决议的消息。
    8月28日，“真理报”在“关于增产节约”的总标题下，以整版篇幅刊载了中国共产党八届八中全会关于开展增产节约运动的决议全文、国务院总理周恩来在全国人民代表大会常务委员会全体会议上的报告要点以及中国国家统计局关于修订1958年农业统计数字的公报。“消息报”、“劳动报”、“苏维埃俄罗斯报”、“共青团真理报”、“红星报”和“汽笛报”在同一天也以显著地位刊载了周恩来总理的报告摘要；“劳动报”的标题是“伟大人民的气魄”，“共青团真理报”的标题是“宏伟的计划”。
    在前一天，8月27日，“真理报”、“消息报”、“劳动报”、“红星报”和“苏维埃俄罗斯报”都全文发表了中共八届八中全会的公报。“真理报”在第三版以约一半的篇幅刊载了公报的全文。“消息报”在刊登公报时加上大字标题：“中国人民的伟大任务”。“劳动报”的标题是“社会主义—总路线”。
    几天来，莫斯科一些报纸还陆续发表了塔斯社和新华社报道中国人民热烈拥护中共八届八中全会的决议的新闻。8月30日，“真理报”以“中国人民热烈拥护中共中央全会决议”为标题报道了中国人民拥护中共中央全会的决议的消息。8月29日，“消息报”在报道上述消息时用“响应中国共产党的号召”作标题，“共青团真理报”的标题是“党和人民的团结”，“苏维埃俄罗斯报”的标题是：“中国劳动人民热情高涨”，“空军报”的标题是“全中国人民支持中共八中全会决议”。
    朝鲜报刊：两年完成五年计划是时代的骄傲
    帝国主义者在中国人民的伟大胜利面前发抖了
    据新华社平壤4日电　朝鲜各报连日来用显著地位刊登了中共八届八中全会的文件，并且发表社论和评论，热烈欢呼中国人民在大跃进中取得的伟大胜利。
    8月30日，“劳动新闻”和“民主朝鲜报”全文刊登了周恩来总理的“关于调整1959年国民经济计划主要指标和进一步开展增产节约运动的报告”。“劳动新闻”还全文刊登了“人民日报”在27日发表的“反右倾，鼓干劲，为在今年完成第二个五年计划的主要指标而斗争”的社论。朝鲜“民主青年报”发表了“跃进中的中国人民的伟大胜利”的评论。
    在这以前，“劳动新闻”8月28日在“中国人民在社会主义建设中继续大跃进”的通栏大标题下，刊登了中共八届八中全会的公报全文，同时发表了题为“中国共产党总路线的光辉胜利”的评论。评论指出：中国共产党的建设社会主义的总路线团结了千百万群众，继续涌现出了海洋般的伟大物质力量。一年内使工业和农产品增长百分之几十，以两年的时间完成五年计划的事情，确实是时代的骄傲，不能不说是个奇迹。评论说，帝国主义者及其走狗尽管进行中伤，但中国人民在党的总路线照耀下去年取得了空前的大跃进，而今年还在继续跃进，各地人民公社也已经巩固了。
    同一天，平壤出版的“民主朝鲜报”“平壤新闻”“朝鲜人民军报”和地方出版的“开城新闻”“江原日报”“慈江日报”“黄南日报”都全文刊登了中共八届八中全会的公报。
    “劳动新闻”和“朝鲜人民军报”在8月29日刊登了中共八届八中全会关于进一步开展增产节约运动的决议全文。“劳动新闻”还在第一版头条位置发表了题为“中国人民的伟大胜利”的社论。社论说，帝国主义者在中国人民的伟大胜利面前发抖了，“中国人民正在粉碎敌人的中伤、诽谤和破坏活动而继续前进”。“民主朝鲜报”发表的评论题目是“兄弟般的中国人民胜利前进”。评论说，今年上半年中国人民在国民经济各部门取得的新的重要成就证明了中国共产党的总路线的完全正确，向全世界再一次显示了中国人民在大跃进中胜利前进。
    9月1日，“劳动新闻”全文刊登了“人民日报”在8月29日发表的题为“人民公社万岁”的社论。
    从8月30日到9月2日，朝鲜报纸刊登了“中国人民热烈欢迎中共八届八中全会公报和决议”、“中国人民公社运动一周年”等消息，陆续发表了朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会委员长李孝淳、朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会委员长朴容国、朝鲜女性同盟中央委员会副委员长金英秀和劳动英雄崔圣寿等对中共八届八中全会的谈话。

    罗马尼亚报刊：中国人民热烈拥护八中全会
    新华社布加勒斯特4日电　罗马尼亚首都各报都以较显著的地位登载了周恩来总理在全国人民代表大会常务委员会全体会议上的报告并纷纷发表消息，报道中国广大人民热烈拥护八中全会决议的情况。
    “火花报”“自由罗马尼亚报”等8月28日都在第四版较显著地位摘要刊载了周恩来总理的报告。电台也广播了这个消息。
    首都各报和电台在8月27日和8月29日都曾分别发表了中共八中全会消息和摘要发表了中共八中全会的决议。摘发的决议内容包括1958年煤、钢、粮、棉核实产量，1959年上半年生产情况，建议国务院修改今年主要生产指标，十年内在主要工业产品产量方面赶上英国，开展增产节约竞赛运动等。各报还摘要刊载了决议中关于总路线、大跃进和人民公社的评价，以及对国内外反动派的驳斥部分。
