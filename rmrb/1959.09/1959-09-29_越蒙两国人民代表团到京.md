Title: Vietnamese and Mongolian People's Delegations Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. The delegation of Vietnamese Democratic Republic Labor People, led by Bui Ky, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front and President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon. They were invited by the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Communist Youth League, the All-China Women's Federation, the All-China Youth Federation, the All-China Students' Federation, and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.
    Sun Xiaocun, Xu Zhizhen, Hu Naiqiu, Wang Wei, Mo Yichang and other heads of the inviting organizations went to the station to welcome them.
    Pham Binh, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, also went to the railway station to meet them.
    Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. The Mongolian Labor People's Delegation, led by Genden, Chairman of the Central Supervisory Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon. They were invited by the China-Mongolia Friendship Association, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Communist Youth League, the All-China Women's Federation, the All-China Youth Federation, the All-China Students' Federation, and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.
    Zhang Zhixiang, Li Zaiwen, Hu Naiqiu, Yang Jingren, Qian Dawei, Cheng Jimin, Liu Ziyu, Mo Yichang and other heads of the inviting organizations went to the station to welcome them.
    Dangersuren and Doorji, counselors of the Embassy of the Mongolian People's Republic in China, also went to the railway station to meet them.

### 越蒙两国人民代表团到京

1959-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

    越蒙两国人民代表团到京
    新华社28日讯　由越南祖国战线中央委员会主席团委员、越中友好协会会长裴杞率领的越南民主共和国劳动人民代表团一行二十人，今天下午乘火车到达北京。他们是应中越友协、全总、和大、共青团、全国妇联、青联、学联、对外文协邀请，来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
    前往车站欢迎的有各邀请团体的负责人孙晓村、许之桢、胡耐秋、王伟、莫艺昌等。
    越南民主共和国驻华大使馆参赞范平也到火车站迎接。
    新华社28日讯　由蒙古人民革命党中央监察委员会主席根登率领的蒙古劳动人民代表团一行十五人，今天下午乘火车到达北京。他们是应中蒙友协、全总、和大、共青团、全国妇联、全国青联、全国学联、对外文协邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
    前往车站欢迎的有各邀请团体的负责人张致祥、栗再温、胡耐秋、杨静仁、钱大卫、程极明、刘子余、莫艺昌等。
    蒙古人民共和国驻华大使馆参赞丹格苏伦和道尔吉也到火车站迎接。
