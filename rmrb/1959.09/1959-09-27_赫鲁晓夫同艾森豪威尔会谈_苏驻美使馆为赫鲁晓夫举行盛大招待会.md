Title: Khrushchev Meets with Eisenhower; Soviet Embassy in the U.S. Holds Grand Reception for Khrushchev
Author:
Date: 1959-09-27
Page: 9
Xinhua News Agency, September 26th, Washington dispatch: TASS Washington dispatch: The press corps accompanying the Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers released the following communiqué on Khrushchev's visit to the United States on the 25th:

	On the afternoon of the 24th, Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers Khrushchev and his entourage arrived in Washington, the capital of the United States, from Pittsburgh.

	Tens of thousands of Washington residents warmly welcomed the head of the Soviet government. In the evening, a reception was held at the Soviet Embassy to welcome Khrushchev. Among those attending the reception were U.S. Vice President Nixon, Lodge, White House Press Secretary Hagerty, prominent American industrialist and social figure Eaton, as well as national leaders, social celebrities, and cultural figures. The reception was conducted in a warm and friendly atmosphere. Later, American business figures hosted a banquet in honor of Khrushchev. During the banquet, both sides engaged in a warm exchange of views on issues related to Soviet-U.S. relations.

	On the 25th, U.S. Secretary of State Herter hosted a banquet in honor of Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers Khrushchev.

	After the banquet, Khrushchev, accompanied by Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko, went to U.S. President Eisenhower's villa, Camp David, to hold talks.

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th dispatch: According to TASS Washington dispatch: The Soviet Embassy in the United States held a grand reception on the evening of the 24th for Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers Khrushchev.

	The Soviet side present at the reception included Khrushchev and his entourage visiting the United States.

	The American side present included: U.S. Vice President Nixon, U.S. President Eisenhower's special representative Lodge, U.S. Secretary of State Herter, U.S. Secretary of Commerce Mueller, and other officials, members of Congress, and representatives from the business, media, and intellectual circles of the U.S. capital.

	Diplomatic envoys from various countries were also present.

	Approximately five hundred guests attended the reception, which was conducted in a very warm atmosphere.

	After the reception, the publisher of "Commercial Daily," Ridder, held a grand banquet at one of Washington's largest hotels, the "Sheraton-Carlton Hotel," in honor of Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers Khrushchev.

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th dispatch: According to TASS Washington, September 25th dispatch: Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers Khrushchev and U.S. President Eisenhower arrived at the Camp David villa, 100 kilometers from Washington, by helicopter on the afternoon of the 25th.

	Khrushchev and Eisenhower will stay at Camp David until the morning of the 27th (local time). They will hold talks here on issues of mutual concern. The Soviet side participating in the talks will include: Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko, Soviet Ambassador to the United States Menshikov, Director of the American Countries Department of the Soviet Foreign Ministry Soldatov, and advisor Troyanovsky.

	The American side participating in the talks will include: U.S. Secretary of State Herter, U.S. Ambassador to the Soviet Union Thompson, and U.S. Permanent Representative to the United Nations Lodge.

	Before going to Camp David, Khrushchev received the famous American pianist Cliburn at his residence in Washington.

### 赫鲁晓夫同艾森豪威尔会谈  苏驻美使馆为赫鲁晓夫举行盛大招待会

1959-09-27
第9版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫同艾森豪威尔会谈
    苏驻美使馆为赫鲁晓夫举行盛大招待会
    新华社26日讯　塔斯社华盛顿讯：苏联部长会议主席随行记者组25日发表赫鲁晓夫访美行踪的公报如下：
    24日下午，苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫及其随行人员从匹兹堡到达美国首都华盛顿。
    成千上万的华盛顿居民热情地欢迎苏联政府首脑。晚上，在苏联大使馆举行招待会欢迎赫鲁晓夫。出席招待会的有美国副总统尼克松，洛奇，白宫新闻秘书哈格蒂，美国著名工业家、社会名流伊顿以及国家领导人、社会名流和文化界人士。招待会是在热烈友好的气氛中进行的。后来，美国工商界人士设宴招待赫鲁晓夫。席间，双方就苏美关系问题热烈地交换了意见。
    25日，美国国务卿赫脱设宴招待苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫。
    宴会后，赫鲁晓夫在苏联外交部长葛罗米柯陪同下到美国总统的别墅戴维营同艾森豪威尔总统举行会谈。
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社华盛顿讯：苏联驻美国大使馆24日晚上为苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫举行了盛大招待会。
    苏联方面出席招待会的有赫鲁晓夫及其访问美国的随行人员。
    美国方面出席的有：美国副总统尼克松、美国总统艾森豪威尔的特派代表洛奇、美国国务卿赫脱、美国商务部长谬勒及其他官方人士、国会议员、以及美国首都的实业界、舆论界和知识界的代表。
    出席的还有各国外交使节。
    大约有五百个客人出席了招待会，招待会是在非常热烈的气氛中进行的。
    招待会之后，“商业日报”的发行人李德尔在华盛顿最大的饭店之一“谢勒顿—卡尔敦饭店”举行盛大的宴会招待苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫。
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社华盛顿25日讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫和美国总统艾森豪威尔25日下午乘直升飞机到达离华盛顿一百公里的戴维营别墅。
    赫鲁晓夫和艾森豪威尔在戴维营将停留到27日的上午（当地时间）。他们将在这里就双方有关的一些问题进行会谈。苏联方面参加会谈的将有：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯、苏联驻美国大使缅希科夫、苏联外交部美洲司司长索尔达托夫和顾问特罗扬诺夫斯基。
    美国方面参加会谈的将有：美国国务卿赫脱、美国驻苏联大使汤普森和美国常驻联合国代表洛奇。
    在到戴维营之前，赫鲁晓夫在华盛顿的住所接见了美国著名的钢琴家克利伯恩。
