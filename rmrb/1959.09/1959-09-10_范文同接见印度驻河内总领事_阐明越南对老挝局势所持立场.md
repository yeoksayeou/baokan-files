Title: Pham Van Dong Receives Indian Consul General in Hanoi, Explains Vietnam's Position on the Situation in Laos
Author:
Date: 1959-09-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 9th: According to the Vietnam News Agency today, Pham Van Dong, Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, received Mynesse, Consul General of the Republic of India in Hanoi this morning. Prime Minister Pham Van Dong explained to Mynesse the position of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on the current extremely serious situation in Laos, and requested the Government of the Republic of India to take appropriate actions to prevent further deterioration of the situation in Laos.

### 范文同接见印度驻河内总领事  阐明越南对老挝局势所持立场

1959-09-10
第5版()
专栏：

    范文同接见印度驻河内总领事
    阐明越南对老挝局势所持立场
    新华社河内9日电　据越南通讯社今天报道：越南民主共和国总理兼外交部部长范文同今天上午接见了印度共和国驻河内总领事麦尼西斯。范文同总理向麦尼西斯阐明了越南民主共和国政府对老挝当前极其严重的局势所持的立场，并且要求印度共和国政府采取适当的行动，来防止老挝局势进一步恶化。
