Title: Indonesian-China Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, the 14-member delegation of the Indonesian-China Friendship Association, led by Chairman Langgar Rasuna Said and Deputy Chairman Heng Andong, arrived in Beijing on the 25th and 27th respectively to visit China and participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 印度尼西亚中国友协代表团到京

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚中国友协代表团到京
    新华社27日讯　应中国印度尼西亚友好协会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的印度尼西亚中国友协代表团一行十四人，在团长朗加尔·拉苏娜·赛依德、副团长亨·岸东率领下，已分别于25日和27日到达北京。
