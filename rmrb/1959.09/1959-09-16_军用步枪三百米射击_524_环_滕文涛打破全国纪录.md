Title: Military Rifle 300-Meter Shooting: Teng Wentao Breaks National Record with 524 Rings
Author:
Date: 1959-09-16
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 15th: The National Games shooting competition entered its second day on the 15th. In the completed free pistol 50-meter slow-fire competition, the People's Liberation Army (PLA) won the single event team championship, and Han Guobin won the individual championship. In the competition on the 15th, Teng Wentao broke the national record for military rifle 300-meter sixty-shot prone, kneeling, and standing with 524 rings.

In the free pistol 50-meter sixty-shot slow-fire two-day competition, Han Guobin of the PLA set a new record, leading with 538 rings. The five shooters from the PLA team were of similar skill, averaging over 535 rings. As a result, they won the single event team championship with a total score of 2677 rings. The runner-up was the Beijing team (2546 rings), and the third place was the Guangxi team (2537 rings). The following rankings are: Liaoning, Guangdong, Sichuan, Shandong, Jilin, Gansu, Yunnan, Hunan, Shanxi.

In the other three events held on the 15th, Teng Wentao, a 21-year-old squad leader from the PLA team, achieved an excellent score of 524 rings in the military rifle 300-meter sixty-shot prone, kneeling, and standing, breaking the national record of 521 rings set by Zhao Shanjun in Leipzig in September 1958.

### 军用步枪三百米射击524环  滕文涛打破全国纪录

1959-09-16
第7版()
专栏：

    军用步枪三百米射击524环
    滕文涛打破全国纪录
    新华社15日讯　全国运动会射击比赛15日进入第二天。已结束的自选小口径手枪五十米六十发慢射竞赛解放军队获单项团体冠军，韩国斌获得个人冠军。在15日的比赛中，滕文涛以五二四环打破军用步枪三百米六十发卧跪立射的全国纪录。
    自选小口径手枪五十米六十发慢射两天比赛中，解放军队韩国斌首开纪录，以五三八环领先。解放军代表队参加比赛的五名射手，水平相差不多，平均在五三五环以上，结果，他们以二六七七环的总成绩取得单项团体冠军，亚军是北京队（二五四六环），第三名是广西队（二五三七环）。以下的名次是：辽宁、广东、四川、山东、吉林、甘肃、云南、湖南、山西。
    在15日进行比赛的另外三个项目中，军用步枪三百米六十发卧跪立射，解放军代表队二十一岁的班长滕文涛获得五二四环的优秀成绩，打破了赵善俊于1958年9月在来比锡创造的五二一环的全国最高纪录。
