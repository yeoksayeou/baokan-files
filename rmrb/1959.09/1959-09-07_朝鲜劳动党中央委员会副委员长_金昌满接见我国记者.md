Title: Kim Chang-man, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, Receives Chinese Journalists
Author:
Date: 1959-09-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 6th. Kim Chang-man, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, received Li Wei, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of Nanjing Xinhua Daily, and Yu Shiliang, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of Chongqing Daily, Chinese journalists who are visiting Korea according to the 1959 executive plan of the Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Agreement, on the afternoon of the 5th. Wang Yuzhang, reporter of the Pyongyang branch of Xinhua News Agency, was also received. Present from the Korean side was Hyun Pil-hoon, Editor-in-Chief of "Rodong Sinmun" and Chairman of the Central Committee of the Journalists' Union.

### 朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长  金昌满接见我国记者

1959-09-07
第6版()
专栏：

    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长
    金昌满接见我国记者
    新华社平壤6日电　朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长金昌满在5日下午接见了根据中朝文化合作协定1959年执行计划正在朝鲜访问的中国记者、南京新华日报副总编辑李维和重庆日报副总编辑余时亮。新华社平壤分社记者王玉章同时被接见。朝鲜方面在座的有“劳动新闻”总编辑、记者同盟中央委员会委员长玄弼勋。
