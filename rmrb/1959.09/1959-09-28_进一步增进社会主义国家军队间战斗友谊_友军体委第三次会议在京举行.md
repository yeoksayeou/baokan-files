Title: Further Enhancing the Combat Friendship Between Armies of Socialist Countries: The Third Meeting of the Friendly Armies Sports Committee Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: The Third Plenary Meeting of the International Friendly Armies Sports Committee, held in Beijing, concluded on the 27th after lasting four days.

Attending this meeting were military sports delegations from twelve countries including the Soviet Union, Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, North Korea, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Mongolia, the German Democratic Republic, Albania, Vietnam and the People's Republic of China. The meeting, presided over by Major General P.I. Venkov, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Friendly Armies Sports Committee, heard reports from the Soviet military delegation on sports training in the Soviet army in 1959, from the Polish military delegation on the organization of sports activities in the Polish army, and from the Chinese People's Liberation Army delegation on the advanced experiences of an excellent PLA company in organizing sports training and mass sports activities. It also discussed issues such as the events, timing, location, and participating members of the first Friendly Armies Winter Games, and exchanged preliminary views on the issue of the Second Friendly Armies Summer Games. Finally, a resolution of the Third Plenary Meeting of the Friendly Armies Sports Committee was made. Through this meeting, experiences in military sports work of socialist countries were exchanged, and the combat friendship between the armies of socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union was further enhanced.

At the opening ceremony, General Tan Zheng, Deputy Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, delivered a speech on behalf of the Ministry of National Defense, extending a warm welcome to the military sports delegations of the fraternal countries attending the meeting.

### 进一步增进社会主义国家军队间战斗友谊  友军体委第三次会议在京举行

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    进一步增进社会主义国家军队间战斗友谊
    友军体委第三次会议在京举行
    新华社27日讯　在北京召开的国际友军体育运动委员会第三次全体会议，历时四天，于27日闭幕。
    出席这次会议的有苏联、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、朝鲜、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、蒙古、德意志民主共和国、阿尔巴尼亚、越南和中华人民共和国等十二个国家的军队体育代表团。会议在友军体委常委会主席彼·?文科少将主持下，听取了苏军代表团关于苏军1959年体育训练的报告、波军代表团关于波军体育运动组织的报告和中国人民解放军代表团关于解放军一个优秀连队组织体育训练和群众性体育活动的先进经验的报告，并讨论了举行友军第一届冬季运动会的项目、时间、地点和参加成员等问题，对举行第二届友军夏季运动会的问题也交换了初步意见，最后，作出了友军体委第三次全体会议决议。通过这次会议，交流了社会主义国家军队体育工作的经验，进一步增进了以苏联为首的社会主义各国军队间的战斗友谊。
    在开幕式上，中华人民共和国国防部副部长谭政大将代表国防部向大会致词，对到会的各兄弟国家军队体育代表团表示了热诚的欢迎。
