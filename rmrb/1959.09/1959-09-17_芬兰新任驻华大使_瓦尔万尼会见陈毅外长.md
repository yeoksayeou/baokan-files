Title: Finnish New Ambassador to China, Mr. Vahervuori, Meets with Foreign Minister Chen Yi
Author:
Date: 1959-09-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 16th: The new Ambassador of the Republic of Finland to China, Mr. Vahervuori, met with Foreign Minister Chen Yi this morning to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Present at the meeting were Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Song Zhiguang, Deputy Director of the Department of West European Affairs.

### 芬兰新任驻华大使  瓦尔万尼会见陈毅外长

1959-09-17
第5版()
专栏：

    芬兰新任驻华大使
    瓦尔万尼会见陈毅外长
　　新华社16日讯　芬兰共和国新任驻华大使瓦尔万尼今天上午会见我外交部长陈毅，商谈递交国书事宜。会见时在座的有外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文、西欧司副司长宋之光。
