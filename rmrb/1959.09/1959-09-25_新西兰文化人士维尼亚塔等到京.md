Title: New Zealand Cultural Figures Viniata and Others Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, September 24th. Maharajah Viniata and Edward George Beardsley, two New Zealand cultural figures who were invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China and participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 新西兰文化人士维尼亚塔等到京

1959-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    新西兰文化人士维尼亚塔等到京
    新华社24日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的新西兰文化人士马哈拉亚·维尼亚塔、爱德华·乔治·比尔兹利二人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
