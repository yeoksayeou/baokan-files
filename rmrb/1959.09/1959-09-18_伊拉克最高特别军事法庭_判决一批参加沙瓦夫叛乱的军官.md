Title: Iraqi Supreme Special Military Tribunal Sentences a Group of Officers Who Participated in the Shawwaf Rebellion
Author:
Date: 1959-09-18
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, September 16th: The Iraqi Supreme Special Military Tribunal sentenced four officers who participated in the Shawwaf rebellion to death tonight, and another four officers to life imprisonment.

Among the four officers sentenced to death were Brigadier General Nazim Tabaqchali, former commander of the Second Division, and Siri, the former head of military intelligence.

The Supreme Special Military Tribunal announced the acquittal of another five officers.

The interrogation of these officers began in August.

### 伊拉克最高特别军事法庭  判决一批参加沙瓦夫叛乱的军官

1959-09-18
第6版()
专栏：

    伊拉克最高特别军事法庭
    判决一批参加沙瓦夫叛乱的军官
    新华社巴格达16日电　伊拉克最高特别军事法庭今晚判决参加沙瓦夫叛乱事件的四名军官以死刑，另外四名军官以无期徒刑。
    在被判决死刑的四名军官中有前第二师师长纳齐姆·塔巴克沙利准将和前军事情报的负责人西里。
    最高特别军事法庭宣布无罪释放另外五名军官。
    对这些军官的审讯工作是从8月开始的。
