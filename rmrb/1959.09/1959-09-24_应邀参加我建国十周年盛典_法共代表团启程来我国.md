Title: French Communist Party Delegation Departs for China to Attend Our Tenth National Day Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1959-09-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, September 22nd. At 2:30 this afternoon, the French Communist Party delegation, invited to attend the celebrations for the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, departed from Paris aboard a TU-104 jet airliner, heading to Beijing via Moscow.

The delegation is led by Waldeck Rochet, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party. Members of the delegation include Marcel Servin, Secretary of the Central Committee and member of the Political Bureau; Bernard Jodoin, member of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Paris Provincial Committee; Juliette Dubois, member of the Central Committee; and Georges Maranne, Chairman of the Central Financial Supervisory Committee.

### 应邀参加我建国十周年盛典  法共代表团启程来我国

1959-09-24
第6版()
专栏：

    应邀参加我建国十周年盛典
    法共代表团启程来我国
    新华社巴黎22日电　应邀参加庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年盛典的法国共产党代表团今天下午二时三十分乘图—一○四喷气式客机离开巴黎取道莫斯科前往北京。
    代表团是由法共中央政治局委员、中央委员会书记瓦德克·罗歇率领的。团员包括中央委员会书记、政治局委员马赛尔·赛尔文，中央委员、巴黎省委会书记贝纳尔·儒杜伊，中央委员朱丽埃特·杜布瓦和中央财政监察委员会主席乔治·马兰。
