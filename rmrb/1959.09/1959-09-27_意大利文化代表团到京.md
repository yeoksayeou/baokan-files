Title: Italian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 26th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Italian Cultural Delegation, led by Italian writer Carlo Levi, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning to visit our country and participate in the celebrations for the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 意大利文化代表团到京

1959-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    意大利文化代表团到京
    新华社26日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会的邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼，由意大利作家加尔罗·勒维率领的意大利文化代表团一行五人，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
