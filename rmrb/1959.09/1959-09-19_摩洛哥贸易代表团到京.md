Title: Moroccan Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Moroccan government trade delegation, led by Mr. Laraki, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

### 摩洛哥贸易代表团到京

1959-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥贸易代表团到京
    新华社18日讯　摩洛哥政府贸易代表团在团长拉腊基先生率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
