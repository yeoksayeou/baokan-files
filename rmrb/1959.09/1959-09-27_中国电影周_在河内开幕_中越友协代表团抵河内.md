Title: "Chinese Film Week" Opens in Hanoi; Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1959-09-27
Page: 9

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 25th: To celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the "Chinese Film Week" organized by the Vietnamese Ministry of Culture opened tonight at the "August" Cinema in Hanoi.

Prime Minister Pham Van Dong and other party and government leaders, heads of mass organizations, Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam He Wei, diplomatic envoys from various countries to Vietnam, and the Chinese film workers delegation invited to visit Vietnam attended the opening ceremony.

Vietnamese Deputy Minister of Culture Do Duc Dục delivered a speech saying that the purpose of holding the Chinese Film Week is to introduce the achievements of China's Great Leap Forward and strengthen the friendship between the Chinese and Vietnamese people. He outlined the great development of China's film industry over the past decade. After the opening ceremony, the Chinese color film "Daughter of the Party" with Vietnamese dialogue was screened.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 25th: The delegation of the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association, invited by the Vietnam-China Friendship Association to pay a friendly visit to Vietnam, arrived in Hanoi by plane this afternoon. The delegation is led by Yang Xiufeng, President of the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Minister of Education.

The delegation was warmly welcomed at the airport by Nguyen Xuan Thuy, member of Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party and Vice President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association; Ton Quang Phiet, Secretary-General of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association and other executive members of the association; Nguyen Van Huyen, Minister of Education; and more than two hundred representatives from all walks of life and various organizations. Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam He Wei was also present at the airport to welcome them.

Nguyen Xuan Thuy delivered a welcome speech at the airport, warmly welcoming the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association delegation to visit Vietnam during the grand celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### “中国电影周”在河内开幕  中越友协代表团抵河内

1959-09-27
第9版()
专栏：

    “中国电影周”在河内开幕
    中越友协代表团抵河内
    据新华社河内25日电为庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年，越南文化部举办的“中国电影周”今天晚上在河内“八月”电影院举行开幕式。
    范文同总理和其他党政领导人、各群众团体负责人、中国驻越南大使何伟、各国驻越南使节和应邀到越南访问的中国电影工作者代表团出席了开幕式。
    越南文化部副部长杜德育致词说，举办中国电影周的目的，是为了介绍中国大跃进取得的成就，加强中越人民之间的友谊。他概述了中国十年来电影事业的巨大发展。开幕式结束后，放映了越语对白的中国彩色影片“党的女儿”。
    据新华社河内25日电  应越中友好协会邀请前来越南进行友好访问的中越友好协会代表团今天下午乘飞机到达河内。代表团是由中越友好协会会长、中国共产党中央委员会委员、教育部长杨秀峰率领的。
    代表团在机场受到越南劳动党中央委员会委员、越中友好协会副会长阮春水，越中友协秘书长孙光阀和协会的其他执行委员，教育部长阮文煊，以及各界和各机关代表二百多人的热烈欢迎。到机场欢迎的还有中国驻越南大使何伟。
    阮春水在机场致欢迎词时表示热烈欢迎中越友协代表团在越南隆重庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年的时候来到越南访问。
