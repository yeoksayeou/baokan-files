Title: Our Sichuan Opera Troupe Visits Czechoslovakia for Performances
Author:
Date: 1959-09-02
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 31st. The Chinese Sichuan Opera Troupe, led by its head Ming Lang, arrived in Ostrava, Czechoslovakia, on the afternoon of August 31st. On September 2nd, the Sichuan Opera Troupe will give its first performance at the Opatrava in Ostrava. The Chinese Sichuan Opera Troupe is visiting Czechoslovakia for performances according to the 1959 executive plan of the Sino-Czech Cultural Agreement. It will stay in Czechoslovakia for about four weeks.

### 我川剧团到捷作访问演出

1959-09-02
第5版()
专栏：

    我川剧团到捷作访问演出
    新华社布拉格31日电　中国川剧团在团长明朗的率领下，8月31日下午到达捷克斯洛伐克的俄斯特拉发。9月2日，川剧团将在俄斯特拉发的俄巴发首次演出。中国川剧团是根据中捷文化协定1959年执行计划到捷克斯洛伐克作访问演出的。它将在捷克斯洛伐克停留约四个星期。
