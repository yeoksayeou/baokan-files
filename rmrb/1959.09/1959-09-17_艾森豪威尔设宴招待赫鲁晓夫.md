Title: Eisenhower Hosts Khrushchev at Banquet
Author:
Date: 1959-09-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, September 16th, TASS Washington, September 15th: U.S. President Dwight D. Eisenhower hosted a banquet for Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Nikita Khrushchev at his official residence, the White House, on the evening of September 15th. (Attached picture)

　　Khrushchev (right) and Eisenhower (left) at the White House. In Eisenhower's hand is a replica of the small sphere from the Soviet Union's second cosmic rocket, a gift from Khrushchev.

　　TASS Telephoto (Xinhua News Agency photo)

### 艾森豪威尔设宴招待赫鲁晓夫

1959-09-17
第5版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔设宴招待赫鲁晓夫
    新华社16日讯　塔斯社华盛顿15日讯：美国总统艾森豪威尔15日晚间在他的官邸白宫设宴招待苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫。（附图片）
    赫鲁晓夫（右）和艾森豪威尔（左）在白宫。艾森豪威尔手中拿的是赫鲁晓夫送给他的苏联第二个宇宙火箭上小圆球的复制品。
                           塔斯社传真照片（新华社发）
