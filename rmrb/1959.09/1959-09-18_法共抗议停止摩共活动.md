Title: French Communist Party Protests the Ban on Moroccan Communist Party Activities
Author: 
Date: 1959-09-18
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 16th. Jacques Duclos, Secretary of the French Communist Party, in the name of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, sent a telegram yesterday to the Central Committee of the Moroccan Communist Party, expressing his indignation at the Moroccan government's ban on the activities of the Moroccan Communist Party.
    The telegram expressed the fraternal solidarity of the French Communist Party with the Moroccan Communist Party.
    Yesterday afternoon, a delegation composed of Member of Parliament Robert Ballanger and Senator Camille Vallin went to the Moroccan Embassy in Paris to express the protest and indignation of the French communists and working people at the Moroccan government's decision.

### 法共抗议停止摩共活动

1959-09-18
第6版()
专栏：

    法共抗议停止摩共活动
    据新华社巴黎16日电　法国共产党书记雅克·杜克洛以法国共产党中央委员会的名义，昨天致电摩洛哥共产党中央委员会，对摩洛哥政府停止摩洛哥共产党活动表示愤慨。
    电报向摩洛哥共产党表示了法国共产党的兄弟般的声援。
    昨天下午，由议员罗伯·巴朗歇和参议员卡米耶·瓦兰组成的一个代表团前往摩洛哥驻巴黎大使馆，表达法国共产党人和劳动人民对摩洛哥政府这一决定的抗议和愤慨。
