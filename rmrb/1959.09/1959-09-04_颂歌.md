Author: You Siłowacki
Date: 1959-09-04
Page: 8

Ode
[Poland] Juliusz Słowacki

Holy Mother! Virgin Mother!
Mother of God, listen to our singing,
—It is the song of our ancestors.
The light of freedom is shining brilliantly,
The bells of freedom have resounded in all directions,
The trees of freedom are growing strong.
Holy Mother!
Let the song of free people
Fly high to the throne of God in the clouds.
Knights, sing loudly,
So that the song of freedom
Will reach the walls of Moscow!
I will use the ode of freedom
To destroy the solid ice on the Neva River.
There are many people there,
Who also have noble souls.
Night has come…
A two-headed eagle
Sleeps on the roof of the palace.
In its claws it tightly grips heavy chains.
Listen! The alarm bell has rung,
The alarm bell has rung, this cursed eagle
Soars above the cross on the church roof.
It seems that it no longer has the courage
To look at the people fighting for freedom,
It is dazzled by the light of freedom,
It wants to find a sheltering shadow… so in the hazy night it flies north.
Oh, shame! Shame on the Lithuanian people,
If in the ancient city of Gediminas,
The treetops are to let this eagle perch and hide…
The people will never forgive such a sin,
There, until today,
They still pay tribute to the crown stained with blood.
You kowtow before foreigners,
But we believe in our own strength;
We want to live in our own land,
We also want to sleep in our own graves.
Brothers, take up arms!
We are ready!
The day of the people's revival has come,
Just like a new phoenix reborn from the ashes,
The people have raised their heads from the abyss of oppression.
God, have mercy!
Let our majestic ode sing even louder!
Holy Mother! Virgin Mother!
Mother of God, listen to our singing,
—It is the song of our ancestors.
The light of freedom is shining brilliantly,
The bells of freedom have resounded in all directions,
In the angry battle blood flowed like a river.
Holy Mother!
Let the song of free people fly high to the throne of God in the clouds.
(Translated by Ge Baoquan)

Neva River is in Petersburg, here referring to the autocratic rule of Tsarist Russia.
Possibly referring to the Decembrists who revolted in Petersburg in 1825.
The two-headed eagle is the emblem of the Russian Empire.
The ancient city of Gediminas refers to Vilnius, the capital of Lithuania.
In the spring of 1831, Lithuania also broke out a revolutionary uprising against the rule of Tsarist Russia under the influence of the Warsaw Uprising.

### 颂歌
尤·斯洛伐茨基
1959-09-04
第8版()
专栏：

    颂歌
    〔波兰〕尤·斯洛伐茨基
    圣母呀！圣处女呀！
    上帝的母亲，倾听我们歌唱吧，——
    这是我们祖先的歌声。
    自由的光芒正在辉煌照耀，
    自由的钟声已经响彻四方，
    自由的树木正在坚固成长。
    圣母呀！
    让自由的人民的歌声，
    高飞到云霄中的上帝的宝座旁。
    骑士们，高声歌唱吧，
    好让自由的歌声，
    一直传到莫斯科的城墙！
    我要用自由的颂歌，
    把?瓦河上的坚冰摧毁。
    ?在那儿有许多人，
    他们也有着崇高的灵魂。
    ?黑夜来到了……
    一只双头的老鹰?
    沉睡在皇宫的屋脊上。
    它的利爪里紧握着的是沉
    重的锁链。
    倾听吧！警钟已经敲响，
    警钟已经敲响，这只该诅咒的老鹰
    在教堂屋顶的十字架上面翱翔。
    看起来，它再没有勇气
    注视着为自由而斗争的人民，
    它被自由的光辉照耀得头晕目眩，
    它想寻找掩蔽的阴影……于是就在夜色朦胧中飞向北方。
    哦，可耻呀！可耻呀，立陶宛人民，
    假如在格德明的古城中，?
    树梢要让这只老鹰栖息隐藏……
    人民将永远不会宽恕这样的罪恶，
    在那儿直到如今，
    还对被鲜血染红了的皇冠致敬。?
    你们在外国人的面前卑躬屈膝，
    但我们却相信自己的力量；
    我们要生活在自己的国土上，
    我们也要在自己的坟墓里长眠。
    弟兄们，拿起武器来！
    我们准备好啦！
    人民复兴的日子已经来临，
    正像新的凤凰从灰烬中重新再生，
    人民已从压迫的深渊中抬起头来。
    上帝呀，开开恩吧！
    让我们的威严的颂歌唱得更外响亮！
    圣母呀！圣处女呀！
    上帝的母亲，倾听我们歌唱吧，——
    这是我们祖先的歌声。
    自由的光芒正在辉煌照耀，
    自由的钟声已经响彻四方，
    在愤怒的激战中血流成河。
    圣母呀！
    让自由的人民的歌声高飞到云霄中的上帝的宝座旁。
              （戈宝权译）
     ??瓦河在彼得堡，此处系指沙皇俄国的专制统治。
     ?可能系指1825年在彼得堡起义的十二月党人而言。
     ?双头鹰是俄罗斯帝国的国徽。
     ?格德明古城系指立陶宛的首都维尔诺。
     ?1831年春天，立陶宛在华沙起义的影响之下也爆发了反对沙皇俄国统治的革命起义。
