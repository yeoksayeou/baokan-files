Title: The Flower of Friendship
Author: Wen Jie
Date: 1959-09-07
Page: 6

    The Flower of Friendship
    Wen Jie

    I walk through the seething workshop,
    Motors roar, composing a symphony of steel,
    The rotating lathe flashes a cyan halo,
    Molten iron flows in a river from the furnace.
    You raise your bright eyes,
    Smiling, nodding, welcoming Chinese guests,
    Then wipe away the sweat flowing from your foreheads,
    And bury your heads in tense operations again.
    How fervent this scene of labor,
    I can't help but smile and ask: "What are you manufacturing?"
    A young lad answers loudly:
    "We are working for China!"
    This answer provokes hearty laughter,
    An old worker explains to me with gestures—
    It turns out the water pumps being manufactured for us,
    The first batch has already been shipped to China...
    Brothers! Please reach out and touch,
    Touch how my heart is jumping with excitement;
    I seem to see the Bulgarian water pumps,
    Raising white waves and emerald ripples in China.
    Perhaps it is installed in the mountains of the southeast,
    Drawing water from the Yangtze River to irrigate fertile fields;
    Perhaps it is subduing the torrents of the Yellow River,
    Nourishing our arid northwest...
    I stand intently in the workshop,
    But my thoughts gallop towards my motherland;
    Thank you—Bulgarian brothers,
    Thank you for supporting China's construction!
    At parting, the workers warmly ask me:
    "What do you irrigate with so many water pumps you ordered?"
    I thank everyone with a voice rushing from my heart:
    "We irrigate the flowers of friendship."
          Written on August 8, 1959, on the banks of the Danube in Vidin

### 友谊的花朵
闻捷
1959-09-07
第6版()
专栏：

    友谊的花朵
    闻捷
    我从沸腾的车间走过，
    马达轰鸣，合奏着钢铁交响乐，
    旋转的车床闪出青色的光圈，
    熔炉里流出铁水的长河。
    你们抬起明亮的眼睛，
    含笑、点头、迎接中国的来客，
    然后拭去额角上流淌的汗水，
    又埋下头紧张地操作。
    这劳动的景象多么热烈，
    我不禁笑问：“你们在制造什么？”
    一个年轻小伙子响亮地回答：
    “我们在为中国而工作！”
    这回答引起爽朗的笑声，
    一个老工人又比着手势向我解说——
    原来为我们制造的水泵，
    第一批已经运往中国……
    兄弟呵！请伸出手来摸摸，
    摸摸我的心是怎么激动地跳着；
    我仿佛看见保加利亚的水泵，
    在中国扬起了白浪碧波。
    它也许安装在东南山区，
    引来扬子江水灌溉肥沃的田野；
    它也许正擒住黄河的激流，
    滋润着我们干旱的西北……
    我在车间里凝神站着，
    我的思想却驰向我的祖国；
    谢谢你们——保加利亚的兄弟，
    谢谢你们支援中国建设！
    临别时，工人们热情问我：
    “你们订制这样多水泵灌溉什么？”
    我用胸中奔流的声音答谢大家：
    “我们灌溉友谊的花朵。”
          1959年8月8日写于多瑙河畔之维定
