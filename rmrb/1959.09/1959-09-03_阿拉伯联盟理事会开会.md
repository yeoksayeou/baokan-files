Title: Arab League Council Meets
Author:
Date: 1959-09-03
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Casablanca – The thirty-second session of the Arab League Council opened in Casablanca on September 1. Delegations from eight member states—Jordan, Lebanon, Libya, Morocco, Saudi Arabia, Sudan, the United Arab Republic, and Yemen—as well as representatives from Algeria and Palestine, are participating in the meeting. Iraq and Tunisia, the other two member states, have not sent delegations to the meeting.

The twelve items on the agenda include "Discussion of the Palestine Question from All Angles," "Preparation for Participating in the United Nations Debate on the Algerian Question," and France's planned nuclear testing in the Sahara Desert.

The meeting will consider a report submitted by the recent Arab Experts Conference held in Lebanon, which outlines the position of the Arab states on United Nations Secretary-General Dag Hammarskjöld's plan regarding Palestinian refugees.

### 阿拉伯联盟理事会开会

1959-09-03
第6版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯联盟理事会开会
    据新华社卡萨布兰卡讯　阿拉伯联盟理事会第三十二次会议1日在卡萨布兰卡开幕。参加会议的有约旦、黎巴嫩、利比亚、摩洛哥、沙特阿拉伯、苏丹、阿联和也门八个成员国家的代表团以及阿尔及利亚和巴勒斯坦的代表。另外两个成员国伊拉克和突尼斯没有派代表团参加会议。
    会议预定的十二项议程包括“从各个角度讨论巴勒斯坦问题”、“为参加联合国关于阿尔及利亚问题的辩论作准备”和法国计划在撒哈拉沙漠试验原子弹等。
    这次会议将考虑最近在黎巴嫩召开的阿拉伯专家会议提交的一份报告，这个报告表明阿拉伯国家对联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德关于巴勒斯坦难民问题的计划的立场。
