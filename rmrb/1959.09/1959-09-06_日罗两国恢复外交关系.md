Title: Japan and Romania Restore Diplomatic Relations
Author: 
Date: 1959-09-06
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw. Representatives of the Japanese and Romanian governments exchanged notes here on the 1st regarding the restoration of diplomatic relations between the two countries. The two countries will soon send ministers to each other.

### 日罗两国恢复外交关系

1959-09-06
第5版()
专栏：

    日罗两国恢复外交关系
    新华社华沙电　日本和罗马尼亚两国政府的代表1日在这里互相交换了关于恢复两国外交关系的照会。两国不久将互派公使。
