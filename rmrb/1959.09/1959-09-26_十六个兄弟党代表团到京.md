Title: Sixteen fraternal party delegations arrive in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-09-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th.

The French Communist Party Delegation

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th. The French Communist Party delegation, invited by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, led by Waldeck Rochette, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party and Secretary of the Central Committee; delegation members: Marcel Servin, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party and Secretary of the Central Committee, Bernard Jodoin, member of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Paris Provincial Committee, Juliette Dubois (female), member of the Central Committee, and Georges Marlin, Chairman of the Central Financial Supervisory Committee, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

Welcoming them at the airport were Peng Zhen and Tan Zhenlin, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretaries of the Secretariat, and Wang Jiaxiang and Yang Shangkun, Secretaries of the Secretariat and Alternate Secretaries of the Secretariat, respectively.

    新华社25日讯　应中国共产党中央委员会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的法国共产党代表团团长、法国共产党中央政治局委员、中央委员会书记瓦德克·罗歇，代表团团员：法国共产党中央政治局委员、中央委员会书记马赛尔·赛尔文，中央委员、巴黎省委书记贝纳尔·儒杜伊，中央委员朱丽埃特·杜布瓦（女），中央财政监察委员会主席乔治·马兰，于今日下午乘飞机到达北京。

    在机场欢迎的，有中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记彭真、谭震林，中央书记处书记王稼祥、候补书记杨尚昆。
