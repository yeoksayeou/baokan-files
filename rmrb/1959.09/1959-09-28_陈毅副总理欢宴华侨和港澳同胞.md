Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Hosts Banquet for Overseas Chinese and Compatriots from Hong Kong and Macao
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi hosted a grand banquet at the Beijing Hotel tonight to welcome over 900 overseas Chinese and compatriots from Hong Kong and Macao who came to Beijing to participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. At the banquet, Vice Premier Chen Yi, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chairman Mao, Chairman Liu, and the State Council, extended cordial greetings to the reunited overseas Chinese and compatriots from Hong Kong and Macao and delivered a speech.

He Xiangning, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Chen Jiageng, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, attended the banquet.

Also present at the banquet were Liao Chengzhi, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, and Deputy Directors Fang Fang, Zhuang Xiquan, Huang Changshui, and Zhuang Mingli, members Shao Lizi, Chen Qiyuan, Cai Tingkai, Ping Jiesan, Deputy Director of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, Xue Zizheng, and other responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations.

He Xiangning, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Liao Chengzhi, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, spoke at the banquet. Situ Zan, Chairman of the Indonesian Chinese Association, also spoke at the banquet on behalf of the overseas Chinese attending the banquet. (Attached picture)

### 陈毅副总理欢宴华侨和港澳同胞

1959-09-28
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理欢宴华侨和港澳同胞
    新华社27日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今晚在北京饭店举行盛大宴会，欢迎到北京参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的海外华侨和港澳同胞九百多人。宴会上，陈毅副总理代表中共中央、毛主席、刘主席、国务院对团聚的侨胞和港澳同胞们，致以亲切的慰问并讲了话。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长何香凝、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈嘉庚出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有华侨事务委员会主任廖承志，副主任方方、庄希泉、黄长水、庄明理，委员邵力子、陈其瑗、蔡廷锴，中共中央统一战线工作部副部长平杰三、薛子正等，以及各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人等。
    在宴会上讲话的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长何香凝、华侨事务委员会主任廖承志。印度尼西亚中华侨团总会主席司徒赞代表出席宴会的华侨也在宴会上讲了话。（附图片）
