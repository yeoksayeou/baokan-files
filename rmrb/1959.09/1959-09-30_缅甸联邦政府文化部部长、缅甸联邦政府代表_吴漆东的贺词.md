Title: Congratulatory Message from U Chit Thoung, Minister of Culture of the Union of Burma, Representative of the Government of the Union of Burma
Author:
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 9

Esteemed Leaders, Dear Friends:

I am deeply honored to lead the Burmese Cultural Friendship Delegation to participate in the celebrations for the 10th Anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. At the same time, I am very grateful for your invitation.

China is our neighbor, so we, the people of Burma, constantly wish the People's Republic of China progress and development in all areas.

Burma and China have had close ties for many years. Up to now, the people of our two countries have maintained and developed this relationship.

We Burmese call Chinese people "Pauk Phaw," and "Pauk Phaw" means brothers. Therefore, we, the people of Burma, consider the Chinese and Burmese people to be close brothers.

Finally, let us congratulate the leaders and people of China for their health and happiness, and wish you even greater achievements for China.

Now, I will read the congratulatory telegram from the President of the Union of Burma, U Win Maung, to Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and the congratulatory telegram from the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, General Ne Win, to Premier Zhou Enlai.

	Congratulatory telegram from U Win Maung, President of the Union of Burma
	To His Excellency Mr. Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:

On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I am very pleased to extend to Your Excellency, and through you to the Chinese people, my warm congratulations and those of the people of the Union of Burma. On this auspicious day, the Chinese people can look back with justifiable satisfaction and pride on the achievements they have made in the past decade in building a new China, while at the same time gaining new inspiration and strength to face the tasks ahead.

The people of the Union of Burma join me in wishing Your Excellency good health and the Chinese people success and happiness in their continued great endeavors; and express with full confidence the hope that the friendly ties formed between our two peoples in the past decade will not only be maintained but further enhanced and strengthened in the years to come.
	　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　President of the Union of Burma U Win Maung
	　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　September 22, 1959, at the Presidential Palace in Rangoon

		Congratulatory telegram from Ne Win, Prime Minister of the Union of Burma
		To His Excellency Mr. Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:

Mr. U Chit Thoung, formally representing Burma in Beijing to attend the celebrations for the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, brings with him the sincere congratulations and all good wishes of the government and people of the Union of Burma.

On this day, the Chinese people proudly celebrate the achievements they have made in the first decade of the People's Republic. Ten years is only a short period in the life of a nation, but the progress China has made in so many areas in such a short time is truly remarkable. Please accept our heartfelt congratulations to the Chinese people and their government, and our best wishes for their future welfare, happiness and prosperity.

Mr. U Chit Thoung's presence in Beijing as a guest of the government of the People's Republic of China is a symbol of the pleasant friendship existing between our two countries. The people and government of the Union of Burma also earnestly hope to further enhance and consolidate this friendship, so that the people of our two countries can live together forever in peace and harmonious neighborly understanding.
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Prime Minister of the Union of Burma　Ne Win
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　September 22, 1959, in Rangoon

### 缅甸联邦政府文化部部长、缅甸联邦政府代表  吴漆东的贺词

1959-09-30
第9版()
专栏：

    缅甸联邦政府文化部部长、缅甸联邦政府代表
    吴漆东的贺词各位领袖、亲爱的朋友们：
    我非常荣幸地能率领缅甸文化友好代表团来参加中华人民共和国建国十周年国庆的庆祝典礼。同时，十分感谢你们的邀请。
    中国是我们的邻国，所以，我们缅甸人民时常祝愿中华人民共和国在各方面的进步和发展。
    缅甸和中国在很多年以前就有着亲密的联系。一直到现在我们两国人民仍保持和发展着这种联系。
    我们缅甸人称中国人为“胞波”，“胞波”的意思是兄弟。所以，我们缅甸人民认为中国人和缅甸人是亲密的弟兄。
    最后，让我们祝贺中国的领袖和中国的人民健康、愉快，祝你们为中国做出更大的成就。
    现在，我宣读缅甸联邦共和国总统吴温貌给刘少奇主席的贺电和缅甸联邦共和国总理奈温将军给周恩来总理的贺电。
        缅甸联邦总统吴温貌的贺电中华人民共和国主席刘少奇先生阁下：
    值此中华人民共和国成立十周年之际，我非常愉快地向阁下，并通过您向中国人民致以我的和缅甸联邦人民的热烈祝贺。在这个吉祥的日子，中国人民可以怀着正当的满意和自豪的心情回顾他们在过去十年中在建设新中国方面取得的成就，同时得到新的鼓舞和力量来面对今后的任务。
    缅甸联邦的人民和我一起祝阁下健康，祝中国人民在继续进行巨大的努力的过程中获得成功和幸福；并满怀信心地表示希望我们两国人民在过去十年中形成的友好联系在未来的年代中不但会保持下去，而且会得到进一步的增进和加强。
                                      缅甸联邦总统　吴温貌
                                  1959年9月22日于仰光总统府
          缅甸联邦总理奈温的贺电中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来先生阁下：
    正式代表缅甸到北京参加中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼的吴漆东先生带去了缅甸联邦政府和人民的诚挚的祝贺和一切良好的祝愿。
    在这一天，中国人民自豪地庆祝他们在人民共和国成立头十年中所取得的成就。十年在一个国家的生命中只不过是短短的一段时间，但是中国在这么短的时间内在这么多的方面所取得的进展的确是引人注目的。谨向中国人民和他们的政府致以衷心的祝贺，并对他们的未来的福利、幸福和繁荣致以最好的祝愿。
    吴漆东在北京作中华人民共和国政府的客人，这是可喜地存在于我们两国间的友谊的象征。缅甸联邦的人民和政府还殷切地希望进一步增进和巩固这种友谊，而使我们两国人民可以永远一起生活在和平和睦邻的谅解中。
  　缅甸联邦总理　奈温
                                        1959年9月22日于仰光（附图片）
