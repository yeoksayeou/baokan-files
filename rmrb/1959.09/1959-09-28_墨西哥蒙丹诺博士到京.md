Title: Mexican Dr. Montano Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 27th. Dr. Guillermo Montano, a member of the Mexican cultural friendship delegation invited by the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries to visit our country and participate in the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China celebrations, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 墨西哥蒙丹诺博士到京

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    墨西哥蒙丹诺博士到京
    新华社27日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的墨西哥文化友好代表团团员吉莫·蒙丹诺博士，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
