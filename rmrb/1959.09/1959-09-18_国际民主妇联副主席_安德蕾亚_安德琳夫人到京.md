Title: Vice President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, Mrs. Andrea Andreen, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Mrs. Andrea Andreen, Vice President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, member of the World Peace Council, Swedish medical doctor, and her daughter, Miss Hillevi Svedberg, editor and architect of "Swedish Architectural Review" and "Swedish Architecture Review," arrived in Beijing by plane this morning at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation to attend the National Day tenth anniversary celebrations in our country.

### 国际民主妇联副主席  安德蕾亚·安德琳夫人到京

1959-09-18
第5版()
专栏：

    国际民主妇联副主席
    安德蕾亚·安德琳夫人到京
    新华社17日讯　应全国妇女联合会邀请来我国参加国庆十周年观礼的国际民主妇女联合会副主席、世界和平理事会理事、瑞典医学博士安德蕾亚·安德琳夫人，和她的女儿、“瑞典建筑学评论”及“瑞典建筑评论”编辑、建筑师希勒维·斯维德贝格，在今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
