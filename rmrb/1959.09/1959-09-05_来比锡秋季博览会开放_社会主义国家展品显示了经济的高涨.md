Title: Leipzig Autumn Fair Opens, Socialist Countries' Exhibits Display Economic Upsurge
Author:
Date: 1959-09-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Leipzig Telegram, August 30th: The 1959 Leipzig International Autumn Fair officially opened. German Democratic Republic Deputy Prime Ministers Heinrich Rau and Bruno Leuschner and other government leaders, as well as foreign government delegations and diplomatic envoys, were among the first distinguished guests to visit the fair's foreign collective exhibits.

This fair focuses on the consumer goods industry. In Hall 1 of the fair's technical exhibition area, the collective exhibitions of the four socialist countries—the Soviet Union, China, Romania, and North Korea—fully demonstrated the common upsurge of the socialist countries' economies and the further improvement of people's lives.

### 来比锡秋季博览会开放  社会主义国家展品显示了经济的高涨

1959-09-05
第6版()
专栏：

    来比锡秋季博览会开放
    社会主义国家展品显示了经济的高涨
    据新华社来比锡电　1959年来比锡国际秋季博览会8月30日正式开放。德意志民主共和国副总理海因里希·劳·布鲁诺·洛伊施纳和政府其他领导人，以及外国政府代表团和外交使节们作为第一批贵宾参观了博览会的外国集体展出。
    这次博览会以消费品工业为展览中心。在博览会技术展览场的一号大厅中，苏联、中国、罗马尼亚和朝鲜四个社会主义国家的集体展出，充分显示了社会主义国家经济的共同高涨和人民生活的进一步提高。
