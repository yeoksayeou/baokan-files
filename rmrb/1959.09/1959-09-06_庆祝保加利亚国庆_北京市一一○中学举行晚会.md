Title: Beijing No. 110 Middle School Holds Evening Party to Celebrate Bulgarian National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-09-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 5th: More than seven hundred teachers and students of Beijing No. 110 Middle School held an evening party tonight to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the Bulgarian socialist revolution.

When Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Panchevski, embassy staff, and Bulgarian children wearing red scarves arrived at the school, one hundred Young Pioneers lined up at the school gate to welcome them. Principal Lin Ke led the Bulgarian guests to visit the photo exhibition introducing Bulgaria's achievements, which was held by the students to commemorate the festival.

Ambassador Panchevski and Principal Lin Ke spoke at the celebration.

At the evening party, students from No. 110 Middle School performed Chinese and Bulgarian song and dance programs and screened the Bulgarian film "Alarm Bell".

### 庆祝保加利亚国庆  北京市一一○中学举行晚会

1959-09-06
第2版()
专栏：

    庆祝保加利亚国庆
    北京市一一○中学举行晚会
    新华社5日讯　北京市一一○中学的七百多师生今晚举行晚会，庆祝保加利亚社会主义革命十五周年。
    当保加利亚驻华大使潘切夫斯基、使馆工作人员和带着红领巾的保加利亚儿童到达这个学校时，一百名少先队员在校门口列队欢迎他们。校长林克引导保加利亚客人们参观了学生们为纪念这个节日而举办的介绍保加利亚成就的图片展览。
    潘切夫斯基大使和林克校长在庆祝会上讲了话。
    晚会上，由一一○中学的学生们表演了中国和保加利亚的歌舞节目，放映了保加利亚电影“警钟”。
