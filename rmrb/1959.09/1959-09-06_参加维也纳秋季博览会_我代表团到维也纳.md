Title: Delegation to Vienna to Participate in the Vienna Autumn Fair
Author:
Date: 1959-09-06
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, 4th: The Chinese delegation to the 1959 Vienna Autumn Fair, led by Ji Chao-ding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, arrived in the Austrian capital on the evening of the 3rd.
	Welcoming the delegation were Marek, the person in charge of the Fair, and Meier-Meinhof, Vice Chairman of the Austrian Industrial Association.
	The Fair is scheduled to officially open on September 6.

### 参加维也纳秋季博览会  我代表团到维也纳

1959-09-06
第2版()
专栏：

    参加维也纳秋季博览会
    我代表团到维也纳
    新华社4日讯　维也纳消息：参加1959年维也纳秋季博览会的中国代表团，由中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎率领，于3日晚抵达奥地利首都。
    欢迎代表团的有：博览会负责人马雷克和奥地利工业家协会副主席迈尔—梅恩霍夫。
    博览会预定在9月6日正式开幕。
