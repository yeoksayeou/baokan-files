Title: Typhoon Alert
Author:
Date: 1959-09-03
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a typhoon warning at 7 p.m. on the 2nd: The typhoon over the Pacific Ocean was centered approximately 300 kilometers east of the Philippines at 4 p.m. today, around 18 degrees north latitude and 125 degrees east longitude. The typhoon center is currently moving northwest at a speed of 11 to 13 knots per hour, with a maximum wind force of level 12 near the center. In the next 36 hours, the wind force in the Taiwan Strait and the eastern part of the South China Sea will increase to level 8 to 10, and the wind force along the coast of Fujian, the eastern coast of Guangdong, and the southern part of the East China Sea will increase to level 6 to 8.

### 台风警报

1959-09-03
第4版()
专栏：

    台风警报
    新华社2日讯　中央气象科学研究所2日下午七时发布台风警报：太平洋上的台风，今天下午四时中心位置在菲律宾以东三百公里左右的洋面上，就是北纬十八度，东经一百二十五度附近。目前台风中心，正以每小时十一至十三浬的速度向西北方向移动，中心附近最大风力有十二级。未来三十六小时内，台湾海峡和南海东部海面的风力，将加大到八级至十级，福建沿海、广东东部沿海和东海南部海面的风力将加大到六级到八级。
