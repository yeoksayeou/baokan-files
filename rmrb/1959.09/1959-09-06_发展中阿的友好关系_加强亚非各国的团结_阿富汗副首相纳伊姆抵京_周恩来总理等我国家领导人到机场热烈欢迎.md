Title: Developing Friendly Relations Between China and Afghanistan, Strengthening Solidarity Among Asian and African Countries: Afghan Deputy Prime Minister Naim Arrives in Beijing; Premier Zhou Enlai and Other Chinese Leaders Warmly Welcome Him at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1959-09-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 5th. At the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai, the Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Sardar Mohammad Naim, and his entourage arrived in Beijing by special plane from Guangzhou this afternoon for a friendly visit to our country. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the Capital Airport by Premier Zhou Enlai and other Chinese leaders.

In his speech (full text on page two), Premier Zhou Enlai welcomed the distinguished Afghan guests on behalf of the Chinese government and the Chinese people. In his speech, he said that China and Afghanistan have a traditional friendship of more than 2,000 years. Especially after the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Afghanistan, the cultural and economic exchanges between the two countries and the mutual visits of national leaders have greatly strengthened the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries. Premier Zhou Enlai said that Deputy Prime Minister Naim's visit "will not only further consolidate and develop the existing friendly relations between China and Afghanistan, but will also help strengthen the solidarity among Asian and African countries and the cause of world peace."

In his speech (full text on page two), Deputy Prime Minister Naim said: "Our people sincerely hope to develop and consolidate our historically long-standing friendship with your people." "I hope my visit will help strengthen the friendly relations that already exist between our two countries."

The personnel accompanying Deputy Prime Minister Naim included Minister of Commerce Ghulam Mohammad Sherzad, Director of the Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Department of the Ministry of Agriculture Dud Mohammad, Director of the Economic Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Sardar Charman Mahmood Ghazi, Director of the Plant Protection Department of the Ministry of Agriculture Mohammad Yassim Mail, Acting President of the Rural Industrial and Agricultural Bank Noor Ali, Private Secretary to Deputy Prime Minister Naim Mohammad Shah Suliman, and Editor-in-Chief of the "Friendship Daily" Mohammad Ibrahim Abbasi.

Today, the national flags of China and the Kingdom of Afghanistan fluttered in the sky above the Capital Airport. At 3:50 p.m., when Deputy Prime Minister Naim appeared at the door of the cabin with a smile, warm applause of welcome resounded throughout the airport. At this time, Premier Zhou Enlai and other national leaders went to the side of the plane to welcome the distinguished Afghan guests who had traveled from afar to visit our capital.

After the military band played the national anthems of Afghanistan and China, Deputy Prime Minister Naim, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, reviewed the guard of honor. A group of Young Pioneers presented flowers to Deputy Prime Minister Naim and other distinguished Afghan guests.

Among those who welcomed them at the airport today were Vice Premiers of the State Council He Long and Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan Hao Ting, and responsible persons from various departments of the Chinese government, people's organizations, democratic parties, and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee. Diplomatic officials from the Afghan Embassy in China and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China also welcomed them at the airport.

Afghan Ambassador to China Samad and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs Ma Zhenwu, who went to Guangzhou to greet them, accompanied Deputy Prime Minister Naim to Beijing today.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 5th. The Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Sardar Mohammad Naim, and his entourage left Guangzhou by plane at 8:10 this morning for Beijing.

The responsible persons who saw the distinguished guests off at the airport included Governor of Guangdong Province Chen Yu and Vice Governor Lin Qiangyun. Consuls of Poland, Czechoslovakia, and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Guangzhou also saw them off at the airport.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, September 5th. The Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Sardar Mohammad Naim, and his entourage passed through Wuhan today on a special plane from Guangzhou to Beijing.

After having lunch at Wuhan Airport, Prince Naim and his entourage set off for Beijing.

Those who welcomed and saw off the distinguished guests at the airport included Governor of Hubei Province Zhang Tixue and others. (Attached picture)

### 发展中阿的友好关系　加强亚非各国的团结  阿富汗副首相纳伊姆抵京  周恩来总理等我国家领导人到机场热烈欢迎

1959-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    发展中阿的友好关系　加强亚非各国的团结
    阿富汗副首相纳伊姆抵京
    周恩来总理等我国家领导人到机场热烈欢迎
    据新华社5日讯　应周恩来总理邀请前来我国进行友好访问的阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆亲王和他的随行人员，今天下午乘专机由广州到达北京。贵宾们在首都机场受到周恩来总理等我国国家领导人的热烈欢迎。
    周恩来总理在致词（全文见第二版）时，代表中国政府和中国人民欢迎阿富汗贵宾们。他在致词中说，中阿两国有着两千多年的传统友谊，特别是在中阿两国建交以后，两国间的文化、经济交流和国家领导人的互相访问大大加强了两国间的友好合作关系。周恩来总理说，纳伊姆副首相的访问，“将不仅使中阿两国已有的友好关系获得进一步的巩固和发展，而且也必将有助于加强亚非各国间的团结和世界的和平事业。”
    纳伊姆副首相在讲话（全文见第二版）中说：“我国人民诚恳地希望同贵国人民发展并且巩固我们之间的具有悠久历史的友谊。”“我希望我的访问将有助于加强我们两国之间业已存在的友好关系”。
    随同纳伊姆副首相来访的人员中，有商业大臣吴拉姆·穆罕默德·舍尔扎德，农业部畜牧兽医司司长杜德·穆罕默德，外交部经济处处长萨达尔·查尔曼·马哈茂德·加齐，农业部植物保养司司长穆罕默德·亚西姆·迈埃勒，乡村工农业银行代行长努尔·阿里，纳伊姆副首相私人秘书穆罕默德·沙阿·苏里曼，“友谊报”主编穆罕默德·易卜拉欣·阿巴西等。
    今天，中国和阿富汗王国的国旗在首都机场上空迎风飘扬。三时五十分，当纳伊姆副首相含笑出现在机舱门口时，机场上响起一片热烈的欢迎掌声。这时，周恩来总理等国家领导人，都走到机旁，迎接不远千里而来的阿富汗贵宾们光临我国首都。
    军乐队高奏阿富汗和中国国歌后，纳伊姆副首相由周恩来总理陪同检阅了仪仗队。一群少年先锋队员向纳伊姆副首相和其他阿富汗贵宾献了鲜花。
    今天到机场欢迎的，有国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅，全国人民代表大会常委会副委员长、政协全国委员会副主席李济深，国防委员会副主席张治中，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长章汉夫，我国驻阿富汗大使郝汀，我国政府各部门、人民团体、民主党派和北京市人民委员会的负责人。阿富汗驻华大使馆外交官员和各国驻华使节也到机场欢迎。
    前往广州迎接的阿富汗驻华大使萨马德和我国外交部礼宾司副司长马振武，今天陪同纳伊姆副首相到达北京。
    据新华社广州5日电　阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆亲王和随行人员，今晨八时十分乘飞机离广州前往北京。
    到机场欢送贵宾的，有广东省省长陈郁、副省长林锵云等负责人。波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、越南民主共和国驻广州领事也到机场送行。
    据新华社武汉5日电　阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆亲王和他的随行人员，今天上午乘专机从广州前往北京途中，路过武汉。
    纳伊姆亲王和他的随行人员在武汉机场用过午餐后，启程前往北京。
    到机场欢迎欢送贵宾的，有湖北省省长张体学等人。（附图片）
