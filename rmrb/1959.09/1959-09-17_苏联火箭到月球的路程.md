Title: The Journey of the Soviet Rocket to the Moon
Author:
Date: 1959-09-17
Page: 12

Little Common Knowledge

    The Soviet Union's second cosmic rocket launched from Earth on September 12th and reached the Moon at 00:02:24 (Moscow time) on the 14th. In just over thirty hours, it completed the journey of more than 379,000 kilometers from Earth to the Moon. It would take a person or various modes of transportation the following amount of time to complete the same journey (assuming constant speed and a straight-line route):
    Mountaineer, at 1,550 meters per day, approximately 680 years; Pedestrian, walking at 5 kilometers per hour, approximately 8 years and 238 days; Bicycle, traveling at 30 kilometers per hour, approximately 1 year and 161 days; Car, traveling at 100 kilometers per hour, approximately 158 days; Passenger plane, flying at 800 kilometers per hour, approximately 20 days.

### 苏联火箭到月球的路程

1959-09-17
第12版()
专栏：小常识

    苏联火箭到月球的路程
    苏联第二个宇宙火箭9月12日从地球发射后，14日零时二分二十四秒（莫斯科时间）到达月球。它在三十多小时里，走完了从地球到月球之间的三十七万九千多公里的路程。人和各种交通工具走完这段路程需要以下的时间（以不变速度和直线路程计算）：
    登山运动员，每日一五五○米，约需680年；行人，每小时步行五公里，约需八年零二三八天；自行车，每小时行走三十公里，约需一年零一六一天；汽车，每小时行驶一○○公里，约需一五八天；客运飞机，每小时飞八○○公里，约需二十天。
