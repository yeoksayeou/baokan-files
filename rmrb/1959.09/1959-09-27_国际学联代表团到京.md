Title: IUS Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-27
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, September 26th. A six-member delegation from the International Student Union (IUS), led by IUS President Jiri Pelikan, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning. They are visiting China and attending the 10th Anniversary Celebration of the founding of the People's Republic of China at the invitation of the All-China Students' Federation.
    Members of the delegation arriving in Beijing today included: IUS Vice President Mamadou Balde, President of the General Union of Students of the Republic of Iraq Mahdi Abdul Karim, President of the National Union of Students of Brazil Conrado, former President of the Student Union of Khartoum University in Sudan Ahmadi, and representative of the General Union of West African Students Diani.
    Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, and others were at the airport to greet them.

### 国际学联代表团到京

1959-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    国际学联代表团到京
    新华社26日讯　以国际学生联合会主席尤里·贝利康为首的国际学生联合会代表团一行六人，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中华全国学生联合会邀请前来我国参观访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
    今天到京的代表团团员有：国际学联副主席马马杜·巴里，伊拉克共和国学生总联合会主席麦赫迪·阿卜杜勒·卡里姆，巴西全国学生联合会主席康拉多，苏丹喀土穆大学学生会前主席艾哈迈迪，西非学生总联合会代表第亚尼。
    前往机场迎接的有全国学联主席胡启立等。
