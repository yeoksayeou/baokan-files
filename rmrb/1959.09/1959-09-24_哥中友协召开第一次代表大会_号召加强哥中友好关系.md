Title: Colombia-China Friendship Association Holds First Congress, Calls for Strengthening Colombia-China Friendship
Author:
Date: 1959-09-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bogota, September 22nd: The Colombia-China Friendship Association held its first congress. More than two hundred people attended, including Colombian political figures, student and labor union leaders, members of Congress, professionals, artists, and other social figures. Horacio Rodríguez Plata, Chairman of the Colombia-China Friendship Association and Senator, explained the association's purpose at the meeting and called on all Colombians to strengthen cultural, sports, and friendly relations with the People's Republic of China. He said that this relationship is the foundation for establishing future trade and diplomatic relations.

Alfonso Romero, Secretary-General of the Colombia-China Friendship Association, reported on the activities of the Provisional Leadership Committee of the Colombia-China Friendship Association. He presented to the congress a plan for carrying out activities, including screening Chinese films, establishing a record room and library at the Friendship Association's office, holding Chinese art exhibitions, and inviting a Chinese cultural delegation to visit Colombia early next year.

### 哥中友协召开第一次代表大会  号召加强哥中友好关系

1959-09-24
第6版()
专栏：

    哥中友协召开第一次代表大会
    号召加强哥中友好关系
    据新华社波哥大22日电　哥伦比亚—中国友好协会举行了第一次代表大会，参加大会的有哥伦比亚政界人士、学生和工会的领袖、国会议员、自由职业者和艺术家及其他社会名流共二百多人。哥中友好协会主席、参议员奥拉西奥·罗德里格斯·普拉塔在会上说明了友好协会的宗旨，并且号召全体哥伦比亚人民加强同中华人民共和国的文化、体育和友好关系。他说，这种关系是将来建立贸易和外交关系的基础。
    哥中友好协会秘书长阿方索·罗梅罗作了关于哥中友好协会临时领导委员会活动的报告。他向大会提出一项展开活动的计划，其中包括放映中国电影，在友好协会办公处设立唱片室和图书馆，举办中国艺术展览会以及邀请中国派文化代表团于明年初访问哥伦比亚。
