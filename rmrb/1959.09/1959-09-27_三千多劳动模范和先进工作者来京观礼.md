Title: More Than Three Thousand Labor Models and Advanced Workers Arrive in Beijing to Attend National Day Celebrations

Author:

Date: 1959-09-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: More than three thousand labor models and advanced workers from the national industrial and agricultural production fronts and other positions have arrived in Beijing one after another. They are here to participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and various celebratory activities.

Up to the 26th, twenty-four of the twenty-eight National Day observation delegations of labor models and advanced workers had arrived in Beijing, and the remaining delegations will all arrive on the 27th. The representatives participating in the observation include labor models and advanced workers from all fronts of the country, including industry, agriculture, transportation, capital construction, finance and trade, state farms, forestry, meteorology, hydropower, education, culture, health, science, and public security. They are active executors and supporters of our country's General Line, Great Leap Forward, and People's Commune movement. They have played a good leading role, backbone role, and bridging role in last year's Great Leap Forward and this year's continued leap forward.

### 三千多劳动模范和先进工作者来京观礼

1959-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    三千多劳动模范和先进工作者来京观礼
    新华社26日讯　三千多名来自全国工农业生产战线和其他岗位的劳动模范及先进工作者，已经陆续到达北京。他们是前来参加建国十周年庆祝典礼和各项庆祝活动的。
    到26日止，全国二十八个劳动模范和先进工作者国庆观礼代表团，已经有二十四个代表团到达北京，其余的代表团将在27日全部到达。参加观礼的代表包括全国工业、农业、交通运输、基本建设、财政贸易、农垦、林业、气象、水电、教育、文化、卫生、科学、公安等各条战线上的劳动模范和先进工作者，他们是我国总路线、大跃进、人民公社运动的积极执行者和支持者，他们在去年的大跃进和今年的继续跃进中，很好地发挥了带头作用、骨干作用和桥梁作用。
