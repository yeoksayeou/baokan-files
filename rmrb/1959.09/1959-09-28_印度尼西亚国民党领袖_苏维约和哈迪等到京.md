Title: Indonesian Nationalist Party Leaders Suwirjo and Hardi Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th. Indonesian Nationalist Party Chairman Suwirjo and his wife, and member of the Central Standing Committee of the Nationalist Party, former First Deputy Prime Minister Hardi and his wife, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

They were invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People’s Republic of China.

Those who went to the airport to greet them included Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and his wife; Burhan Shahidi Vice Chairman, of The National Committe of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association; Cai Tingkai, Vice Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang and Standing Committee Member of the National Committee of the CPPCC; and Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

Indonesian Ambassador to China Wijopranoto also welcomed them at the airport.

### 印度尼西亚国民党领袖  苏维约和哈迪等到京

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国民党领袖
    苏维约和哈迪等到京
    新华社27日讯　印度尼西亚国民党总主席苏维约和夫人，国民党中央常务委员会委员、前第一副总理哈迪和夫人今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    他们是应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问和参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
    前往机场迎接的有中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之和夫人，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席、中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长包尔汉，中国国民党革命委员会副主席、政协全国委员会常务委员蔡廷锴和中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪等。
    印度尼西亚驻中国大使维约普拉诺托也到机场欢迎。
