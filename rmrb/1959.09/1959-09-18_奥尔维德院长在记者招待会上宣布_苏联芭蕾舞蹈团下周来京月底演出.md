Title: President Olvid Announced at Press Conference: Soviet Ballet Company to Visit Beijing Next Week for Performances at the End of the Month
Author: 
Date: 1959-09-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 17th: The Soviet Bolshoi Theatre Ballet Company and Orchestra, totaling 195 people, will arrive in Beijing by plane on September 25th for a 40-day visit and performances to participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. This was announced by Olvid, Director of the Bolshoi Theatre, at a press conference held on the afternoon of the 16th.

Olvid said: The Soviet people will celebrate the great festival of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China together with all the Chinese people. The Bolshoi Theatre Ballet Company's visit to China will be a cordial and warm congratulation from all Soviet artists to the 600 million Chinese people. The Bolshoi Theatre's visit to China is also another proof of the true and increasingly consolidated friendship between the Soviet and Chinese people.

The Bolshoi Theatre Ballet Company has rehearsed many wonderful programs for this visit to China. The opening performance will be held at the Capital Theatre in Beijing on September 29th.

### 奥尔维德院长在记者招待会上宣布  苏联芭蕾舞蹈团下周来京月底演出

1959-09-18
第5版()
专栏：

    奥尔维德院长在记者招待会上宣布
    苏联芭蕾舞蹈团下周来京月底演出
    据新华社莫斯科17日电　为参加中华人民共和国十周年国庆，苏联大剧院芭蕾舞蹈团和管弦乐队一行一百九十五人，将在9月25日乘飞机到北京进行为期四十多天的访问演出。这是大剧院院长奥尔维德在16日下午举行的记者招待会上宣布的。
    奥尔维德说：苏联人民将和全中国人民一起庆祝中华人民共和国建国十周年这一伟大的节日。苏联大剧院芭蕾舞蹈团的访华演出，将是全苏联艺术工作者对六亿中国人民的亲切、热烈的祝贺。大剧院的访华演出也是苏中两国人民真正的、日益巩固发展的友谊的又一明证。
    大剧院芭蕾舞蹈团已为这次访华演出排练了许多精彩的节目。演出开幕式将于9月29日在北京的首都剧场举行。
