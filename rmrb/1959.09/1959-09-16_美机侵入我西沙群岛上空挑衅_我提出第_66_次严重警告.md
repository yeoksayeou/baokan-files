Title: U.S. Military Plane Intrudes into Airspace Over China's Xisha Islands, Provoking; I Issue the 66th Serious Warning

Author:
Date: 1959-09-16
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 15th. At 12:46 on September 15th, a U.S. Navy patrol plane intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island in China's Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. military aircraft, a spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 66th serious warning.

### 美机侵入我西沙群岛上空挑衅  我提出第66次严重警告

1959-09-16
第4版()
专栏：

    美机侵入我西沙群岛上空挑衅
    我提出第66次严重警告
    新华社15日讯　9月15日十二时四十六分，美国海军巡逻机一架侵入我国广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第六十六次严重警告。
