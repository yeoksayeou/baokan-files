Title: Swedish Natural Scientist Gustafsson Arrives in Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1959-09-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 26th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Swedish natural scientist Gustafsson and renowned poets Blomberg and Kalleby Andersson arrived in Beijing by plane this morning to visit China and attend the National Day celebrations.

### 瑞典自然科学家古斯塔夫逊等到京

1959-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典自然科学家古斯塔夫逊等到京
    新华社26日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会的邀请，来我国访问并参加国庆典礼的瑞典自然科学家古斯塔夫逊、著名诗人布隆拜、卡列布·安德逊等三人今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
