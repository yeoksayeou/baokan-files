Title: Another World Record Broken: Women's Daytime 1000-Meter Group Precision Skydiving Competition – Mei Yan, Guo Xin'e, Zhang Jingwen 5.11 Meters from the Bullseye
Author:
Date: 1959-09-19
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: Good news from the Liangxiang competition zone of the National Games: On the morning of the 18th, Shaanxi team female skydivers Mei Yan, Guo Xin'e, and Zhang Jingwen broke the world record of 8.77 meters set by Soviet female athletes in April 1959 for women's daytime 1000-meter group precision skydiving, with an average distance of 5.11 meters from the center of the circle. These three female athletes are all twenty-year-old young women. Mei Yan and Guo Xin'e only started participating in aircraft skydiving at the end of last year, and Zhang Jingwen only started participating in aircraft skydiving in March of this year. Today, their individual scores are: Mei Yan 2.19 meters, Guo Xin'e 5.84 meters, and Zhang Jingwen 7.30 meters.

This is the second time that Chinese athletes have broken the world record of 8.77 meters for women's daytime 1000-meter group precision skydiving. The first time was broken by Chinese female skydivers He Jianhua, Li Shuhui, and Li Guizhen in July of this year, with a score of 2.213 meters. These two records of our athletes are currently the highest records in the world. (Attached picture)

### 又破一项世界纪录  女子日间一千米集体定点跳伞比赛  梅严郭新娥张景文距靶心5.11米

1959-09-19
第7版()
专栏：首届全运会

    又破一项世界纪录
    女子日间一千米集体定点跳伞比赛
    梅严郭新娥张景文距靶心5.11米
    新华社18日讯　全国运动会良乡竞赛区传来捷报：18日上午，陕西队女跳伞运动员梅严、郭新娥、张景文三人，以平均距圆心五点一一米的成绩，打破了苏联女运动员1959年4月所创造的女子日间一千米集体定点跳伞八点七七米的世界纪录。这三个女运动员都是二十岁的年轻姑娘，梅严和郭新娥是从去年年底才参加飞机跳伞运动，张景文是今年3月才参加飞机跳伞运动的。今天她们三人的成绩分别是：梅严二点一九米、郭新娥五点八四米、张景文七点三零米。
    这是我国运动员第二次突破女子日间一千米集体定点跳伞的八点七七米的世界纪录。第一次是我国女跳伞运动员赫建华、李淑慧、李桂珍三人今年7月突破的，成绩是二点二一三米。我国运动员的这两个纪录都是目前世界上最高纪录。（附图片）
