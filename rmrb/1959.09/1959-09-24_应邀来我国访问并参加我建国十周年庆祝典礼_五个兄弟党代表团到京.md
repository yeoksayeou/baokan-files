Title: Five fraternal party delegations arrive in Beijing at invitation to visit our country and attend our National Day 10th Anniversary celebrations
Author:
Date: 1959-09-24
Page: 1

    Australian Communist Party Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd. At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the head of the Australian Communist Party delegation and General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Australian Communist Party, Lance Louis Sharkey, and Mrs. Sharkey, members of the delegation, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.
    Welcoming them at the airport were Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat, Yang Shangkun, alternate secretary of the Secretariat, and others.
    The other two members of the delegation, Sam Aarons, member of the Central Committee of the Australian Communist Party, and Mrs. Aarons, will arrive in Beijing soon.
  New Zealand Communist Party Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd. At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the head of the New Zealand Communist Party delegation and General Secretary of the National Committee of the New Zealand Communist Party, Vic Wilcox, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.
    Welcoming them at the airport were Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat, Yang Shangkun, alternate secretary of the Secretariat, and others.
    The two members of the delegation, Alec Ostler, member of the Political Committee of the National Committee of the New Zealand Communist Party, and Rita Smith (female), member of the National Committee of the New Zealand Communist Party, will arrive in Beijing soon.
    Danish Communist Party Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd. At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the head of the Danish Communist Party delegation and member of the Central Political Bureau of the Danish Communist Party, Robert Sartov, and members of the delegation: Petra Petersen (female), member of the Central Political Bureau of the Danish Communist Party and member of parliament, and Gottfried Appel, head of the "Era" Publishing House of the Danish Communist Party, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.
    Welcoming them at the airport were Dong Biwu, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Lantao, alternate secretary of the Secretariat, Lin Feng, Liu Ningyi, and others.
  Colombian Communist Party Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd. At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the head of the Colombian Communist Party delegation, member of the Central Executive Committee of the Colombian Communist Party, and National Secretary of Agricultural Work, Victor H. Merchan, and members of the delegation: Gustavo Castro, member of the Central Committee of the Colombian Communist Party and Secretary of the Bogota Regional Organization, and Carlos H. Gomez, alternate member of the Central Committee of the Colombian Communist Party and Political Secretary of the Quindio Region, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.
    Welcoming them at the airport were Dong Biwu, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Lantao, alternate secretary of the Secretariat, Lin Feng, Liu Ningyi, and others.
  Argentine Communist Party Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd. At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the head of the Argentine Communist Party delegation, Secretary of the Central Secretariat of the Argentine Communist Party, Victorio Codovilla, and members of the delegation: Rodolfo Ghioldi, member of the Central Executive Committee of the Argentine Communist Party, and Miguel Contreras, member of the Central Committee of the Argentine Communist Party and Secretary of the Provincial Committee of Cordoba, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.
    Welcoming them at the airport were Dong Biwu, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Lantao, alternate secretary of the Secretariat, Lin Feng, Liu Ningyi, and others.

### 应邀来我国访问并参加我建国十周年庆祝典礼  五个兄弟党代表团到京

1959-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国访问并参加我建国十周年庆祝典礼
    五个兄弟党代表团到京
    澳大利亚共产党代表团
    新华社23日讯　应中国共产党中央委员会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的澳大利亚共产党代表团团长、澳大利亚共产党中央委员会总书记兰斯·路易斯·夏基，代表团团员夏基夫人，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记彭真，中央书记处候补书记杨尚昆等。
    代表团的另外两位团员：澳大利亚共产党中央委员萨姆·阿隆斯和阿隆斯夫人将在最近到达北京。
  新西兰共产党代表团
    新华社23日讯　应中国共产党中央委员会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的新西兰共产党代表团团长、新西兰共产党全国委员会总书记维克·威耳科克斯，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    到飞机场欢迎的，有中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记彭真，中央书记处候补书记杨尚昆等。
    代表团的两位团员：新西兰共产党全国委员会政治委员会委员阿利克·奥斯特勒，新西兰共产党全国委员会委员丽塔·史密斯（女）将在最近到达北京。
    丹麦共产党代表团
    新华社23日讯　应中国共产党中央委员会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的丹麦共产党代表团团长、丹麦共产党中央政治局委员罗伯特·萨尔陶里，代表团团员：丹麦共产党中央政治局委员、国会议员佩特拉·彼得森（女），丹麦共产党“时代”出版社负责人戈特弗雷德·阿佩耳，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到飞机场欢迎的，有中共中央政治局委员董必武，中央书记处候补书记刘澜涛，中央委员林枫、刘宁一等。
  哥伦比亚共产党代表团
    新华社23日讯　应中国共产党中央委员会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的哥伦比亚共产党代表团团长、哥伦比亚共产党中央执行委员会委员、全国农业工作书记维克托·赫·梅尔昌，代表团团员：哥伦比亚共产党中央委员、波哥大地区组织书记古斯塔沃·卡斯特罗，哥伦比亚共产党中央候补委员、金地奥地区政治书记卡洛斯·赫·戈麦斯，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中共中央政治局委员董必武，中央书记处候补书记刘澜涛，中央委员林枫、刘宁一等。
  阿根廷共产党代表团
    新华社23日讯　应中国共产党中央委员会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的阿根廷共产党代表团团长、阿根廷共产党中央书记处书记维克托里奥·柯都维亚，代表团团员：阿根廷共产党中央执行委员会委员罗道夫·基奥迪，阿根廷共产党中央委员、科尔多伐省省委书记米格尔·孔特拉尔斯，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中共中央政治局委员董必武，中央书记处候补书记刘澜涛，中央委员林枫、刘宁一等。
