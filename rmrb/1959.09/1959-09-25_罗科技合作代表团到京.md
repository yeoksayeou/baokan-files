Title: Romanian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th. A two-member Romanian scientific and technological cooperation delegation, led by Anton Constantinescu, Chairman of the Romanian Group of the China-Romania Scientific and Technological Cooperation Joint Committee and Deputy Minister of the Petrochemical Industry, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 23rd to attend the sixth session of the China-Romania Scientific and Technological Cooperation Joint Committee and participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of our country's founding. Zhou Wenlong, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Committee and Vice Minister of the Petroleum Industry, Xiao Guichang, Member of the Committee and Vice Minister of the Chemical Industry, Wang Dongnian, Director of the International Cooperation Bureau of the Science and Technology Commission, and others welcomed them at the airport. The Romanian Ambassador to China, B. Zaharescu, and others also welcomed them at the airport.
Other members of the delegation will arrive in Beijing successively.

### 罗科技合作代表团到京

1959-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    罗科技合作代表团到京
    新华社24日讯　来我国参加中国、罗马尼亚科学技术合作联合委员会第六届会议并参加我国建国十周年庆祝典礼的罗马尼亚科学技术合作代表团一行两人，在委员会罗马尼亚组主席、石油化工部副部长安东·康斯坦丁聂斯库率领下，23日乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有委员会中国组主席、石油工业部副部长周文龙，委员、化学工业部副部长萧桂昌，科学技术委员会国际合作局局长王东年等人。罗马尼亚驻华大使巴·查哈勒斯库等人也到机场欢迎。
    代表团其他团员将陆续来北京。
