Title: Reading the Communiqué Boosts Enthusiasm
Author: Su Jing
Date: 1959-09-10
Page: 8

Reading the communiqué brightens the heart,
Day and night, water pumping becomes even more vigorous,
Silver dragons of water roll into the fields,
Pumping out waves of golden harvest across ten thousand acres.

Reading the communiqué boosts enthusiasm,
Treating all rivers and seas as water ladles,
Watering the cotton until it resembles small trees,
Growing silver peaches galore on every branch.

Reading the communiqué brings joyful singing,
Singing praises of the Party's good policies,
Singing until the drought demon surrenders,
And grain and cotton joyfully top the high-yield charts.

### 看了公报干劲高
苏靖
1959-09-10
第8版()
专栏：

    看了公报干劲高
        苏靖
    看了公报心头亮，
    日夜车水劲更强，
    车的银龙滚进田，
    车出万顷黄金浪。
    看了公报干劲高，
    五湖四海当水瓢，
    浇的棉花像小树，
    浇出满枝银桃桃。
    看了公报齐欢唱，
    歌唱党的好主张，
    唱的旱魔喊投降，
    粮、棉笑登丰产榜。
