Title: Chilean Press Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: A Chilean journalist delegation of four, headed by Juan Emilio Pacull, president of the Chilean Journalists Association, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They are visiting China and attending the National Day 10th anniversary celebration at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association. The delegation includes Genaro Medina Vera, first vice-president of the Chilean Journalists Association and president of "Please See" magazine; Adriana Zearrale (female), deputy secretary-general of the Journalists Association; and Max Nolff, president of "Economic Situation" magazine.

### 智利新闻代表团到京

1959-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    智利新闻代表团到京
    新华社24日讯　以智利记者协会主席胡安·埃米略·帕库耳为首的智利新闻工作者代表团一行四人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请，来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。代表团包括有智利记者协会第一副主席、“请看”杂志社社长赫纳罗·梅迪纳·贝拉，记者协会副秘书长阿德里亚娜·塞亚尔莱（女），“经济情况”杂志社社长马克斯·诺耳夫。
