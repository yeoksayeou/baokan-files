Title: Brazilian Congressman De Vallesco Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 26th. Brazilian Congressman and member of the Presidium of the World Peace Council, De Vallesco, and his wife, arrived in Beijing by plane yesterday. They are visiting China and attending the 10th anniversary celebration of the founding of the country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 巴西众议员德瓦拉斯科到京

1959-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    巴西众议员德瓦拉斯科到京
    新华社26日讯　巴西众议员、世界和平理事会主席团委员德瓦拉斯科和他的夫人，昨天乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中国人民外交学会邀请前来我国访问和参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
