Title: Typhoon Enters Taiwan Strait
Author:
Date: 1959-09-06
Page: 3

    Typhoon Enters Taiwan Strait
    Xinhua News Agency, September 5th - The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a typhoon message at 7:00 AM on the 5th:

    The typhoon in the Pacific Ocean crossed Taiwan and entered the Taiwan Strait in the morning of the 4th, and its intensity has greatly weakened. On the 4th, there were winds of level 10 to 12 along the central and northern coasts of Fujian. On the night of the 4th, the center of the typhoon landed near Fuding, Fujian Province, and there were rainstorms along the coast of Zhejiang and the central and northern coasts of Fujian. In the future, the typhoon will move in a north-north-easterly direction and continue to weaken and tend to disappear. Except for the northern East China Sea, the coast of Zhejiang, and the southern coast of Jiangsu, which still have winds of level 6 to 7 today, the wind force in other areas has weakened.

### 台风进入台湾海峡

1959-09-06
第3版()
专栏：

    台风进入台湾海峡
    新华社5日讯　中央气象科学研究所5日早晨七时发布台风消息：
    太平洋上的台风，已在4日早晨穿过台湾进入台湾海峡，强度已经大大减弱。4日白天，福建中部和北部沿海曾有十级至十二级的大风。4日夜间，台风中心已在福建省福鼎附近登陆，浙江沿海和福建中部、北部沿海曾有暴雨。未来台风将向偏北方向移动，并继续减弱，趋于消失。除东海北部、浙江沿海、江苏南部沿海今天尚有六级至七级大风外，其余地区的风力已经减弱。
