Title: Afghan Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Sardar Mohammed Naim

Author: 
Date: 1959-09-05
Page: 2

    Afghan Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister
    Sardar Mohammed Naim
    At the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai of our country, Afghan Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Sardar Mohammed Naim has come to our country for a friendly visit.
    Naim was born in 1910 in Kabul, the capital of Afghanistan. He received his primary and higher education at Habibia School and Independence College in Kabul.
    In 1930, Naim joined the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and subsequently served as Director of the Political Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. In 1932, he served as the Afghan Envoy to Italy and was subsequently appointed First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs. In 1937, he served as Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Education. In 1946, he was sent as Envoy to the United Kingdom. In 1948, he was transferred to the United States as Ambassador.
    After returning to China in November 1949, Naim successively served as Minister of Public Works, Deputy Prime Minister, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Minister of Mines. After Daud became Prime Minister in 1953, he served as Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister until now.
    Deputy Prime Minister Naim is the younger brother of Afghan Prime Minister Daud and a close aide in Prime Minister Daud's leadership of the country's work. Deputy Prime Minister Naim supports and implements Afghanistan's current policy of peaceful neutrality. He said in April this year: Afghanistan's foreign policy is based on neutrality and free judgment, peace, and seeking friendship and cooperation with the people of all countries in the world. Deputy Prime Minister Naim cherishes friendly feelings towards the Chinese people. His friendly visit to our country will contribute to the further development of friendly relations between China and Afghanistan. (Xinhua News Agency) (with picture)

### 阿富汗副首相兼外交大臣  萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆

1959-09-05
第2版()
专栏：

    阿富汗副首相兼外交大臣
    萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆
    阿富 汗副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆应我国周恩来总理的邀请，前来我国进行友好访问。
    纳伊姆1910年生于阿富汗首都喀布尔。在喀布尔的哈比比亚学校和独立学院受过初等教育和高等教育。
    1930年，纳伊姆参加外交部工作，随后任外交部政治司司长。1932年，任阿富汗驻意大利公使，随后，又被任命为外交部第一副大臣。1937年，出任副首相兼教育大臣。1946年，被派为驻英国公使。1948年，调任驻美国大使。
    纳伊姆在1949年11月回国后，历任公共工程大臣、副首相、副外交大臣和矿务大臣等职。1953年达乌德任首相后，他出任副首相兼外交大臣，直到现在。
    纳伊姆副首相是阿富汗首相达乌德的胞弟，也是达乌德首相领导国家工作中的亲密助手。纳伊姆副首相支持和执行阿富汗现行的和平中立政策。他在今年4月间曾说：阿富汗的外交政策是以中立和自由判断、和平以及谋求同全世界各国人民的友谊和合作为基础的。纳伊姆副首相对中国人民怀抱着友好的感情。他对我国的友好访问，将对中阿两国友好关系的进一步发展作出贡献。　（新华社）（附图片）
