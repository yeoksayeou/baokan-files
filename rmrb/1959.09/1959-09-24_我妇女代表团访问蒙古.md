Title: Chinese Women's Delegation Visits Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1959-09-24
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: A women's delegation of three people, led by Guo Mingqiu, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China, left Beijing by train today to visit the Mongolian People's Republic at the invitation of the Mongolian Women's Committee.

### 我妇女代表团访问蒙古

1959-09-24
第6版()
专栏：

    我妇女代表团访问蒙古
    新华社23日讯　由中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会主席团委员郭明秋率领的妇女代表团一行三人，应蒙古妇女委员会邀请，今天乘火车离开北京去蒙古人民共和国访问。
