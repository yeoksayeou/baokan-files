Title: Radio Telegraphy Competition Day One: 25 Participants Break National Records, National Men's and Women's Telegraphy Records All Broken
Author:
Date: 1959-09-16
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 15th: On the first day of the National Games' radio telegraphy competition, 25 participants broke national records. All six national records for men's and women's long code sending, short code sending, and character code sending from 1958 were broken.

The results of the first round of telegraphy held on the 14th saw seven women break the national record of 88 codes per minute for women's long code sending, set by Wang Hefang in 1958. They were: Wang Shiming (Beijing, score 98), Zhu Wanqin (PLA, score 94.4), Zhong Yuefang (Guangxi, score 94), Zhou Luo (Shanghai, score 90.8), Yang Fengzhi (Beijing, score 90.6), Feng Shuhua (Heilongjiang, score 89.8), and Chang Suzhen (Shandong, score 88.6). Seven women also broke Cao Qingyun's 1957 national record of 114.4 codes per minute for women's short code sending: Wang Shiming (Beijing, 124.8), Zhou Luo (Shanghai, 120.4), Zhong Yuefang (Guangxi, 118.4), Chang Suzhen (Shandong, 117.2), Zhu Wanqin (PLA, 116.6), Feng Shuhua (Heilongjiang, 116.4), and Yang Fengzhi (Beijing, 116). Zhang Jinhua of the PLA broke Sun Shuzhi's 1956 national record of 128 codes per minute for women's character code sending with a score of 135.5 codes per minute.

In the men's group, Wu Liqing of the PLA broke his own 1958 national record of 105.1 codes per minute for long code sending with a score of 140.8 codes per minute. Sun Hongcai (Shandong) and Tao Shian (Guangxi) also broke the national record with scores of 108.6 codes per minute and 107.6 codes per minute respectively. Three people broke Xiao Bingyan's 1958 record of 135.2 codes per minute for short code sending: Wu Liqing (PLA, 172.8), Sun Hongcai (Shandong, 139.8), and Du Xiaofu (Beijing, 136.3). Wang Xicheng's 1958 record of 138 codes per minute for character code sending was also broken by Wang Shulin (Heilongjiang, 145.6), Sun Hongcai (Shandong, 144.2), Wu Liqing (PLA, 140.4), and Zhu Xuegang (Jilin, 138.4).

The second round of telegraphy competitions began in the afternoon of the 15th. Male and female athletes from each delegation competed fiercely, striving to create better results in the second competition in order to select the highest score from the two telegraphy competitions as the final score.

### 无线电收发报竞赛第一天，二十五人次破全国纪录  全国男女发报纪录全部刷新

1959-09-16
第7版()
专栏：

    无线电收发报竞赛第一天，二十五人次破全国纪录
    全国男女发报纪录全部刷新
    新华社15日讯　全国运动会无线电收发报竞赛第一天，就有二十五人次破全国纪录，1958年男子、女子长码发报、短码发报和字码发报六项全国纪录全部被刷新。
    14日举行第一次发报结果，女子组有七人破王和芳在1958年创造的每分钟八十八码女子长码发报全国纪录。她们是：王世明（北京、成绩九十八）、朱婉琴（解放军、成绩九十四点四）、钟月芳（广西、成绩九十四）、周洛（上海、成绩九十点八）杨凤芝（北京、成绩九十点六）、冯书华（黑龙江、成绩八十九点八）、常素珍（山东、成绩八十八点六）。破曹庆云在1957年创造的每分钟一一四点四码女子短码发报全国纪录的也有七人：王世明（北京、一二四点八）、周洛（上海、一二○点四）、钟月芳（广西、一一八点四）、常素珍（山东、一一七点二）、朱婉琴（解放军、一一六点六）、冯书华（黑龙江、一一六点四）、杨凤芝（北京、一一六）。解放军队张锦华以每分钟一三五点五码的成绩，破孙淑芝在1956年创造的每分钟一二八码女子字码发报全国纪录。
    男子组解放军队吴立清以每分钟一四○点八码破他自己在1958年创造的长码发报一○五点一码的全国纪录。孙洪才（山东）、陶世安（广西）也分别以每分钟一○八点六码、一○七点六码破全国纪录。破萧炳炎在1958年创造的每分钟一三五点二码短码发报纪录的有三人：吴立清（解放军、一七二点八）、孙洪才（山东、一三九点八）、杜效甫（北京、一三六点三）。汪锡澄在1958年创造的每分钟一三八码字码发报纪录也为王树林（黑龙江、一四五点六）、孙洪才（山东、一四四点二）、吴立清（解放军、一四○点四）、朱学刚（吉林、一三八点四）所打破。
    15日下午第二次发报开始竞赛，各代表队的男、女选手们争夺激烈，人人争取第二次竞赛中创造出更好的成绩，以便挑选两次发报竞赛中的最高成绩作为最后成绩。
