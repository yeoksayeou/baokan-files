Title: Lion
Author: Tian Jian
Date: 1959-09-09
Page: 5

    Lion	Tian Jian
    Roses bloom on alpine ridges,
    The valley transforms into an art gallery;
    The earth is like a colorful painting,
    The sky is brighter than gold.
    Oh, lion of the Balkans,
    Long liberated from the yoke; (Note)
    The roar of the lion,
    Can summon waves from a thousand miles away.
    Lion, oh Bulgaria,
    The Party has given you strength;
    Beside the cliffs of Hemus,
    A new paradise of red flowers blooms.
    The furnaces of the metallurgical plant,
    Sparkle day and night;
    Trains pass through the mountains,
    Whistles resound across the land.
    More than three thousand years before our era,
    This was a landscape of broken rocks;
    This was but a stone cave,
    Where humans dwelled.
    Now there are power stations,
    And a nationwide power grid;
    With just one female worker,
    Twelve machine tools can be managed.
    I traveled throughout Bulgaria,
    Once singing to the lion;
    Every one of your valleys,
    Is carved like an art gallery.
    To the men and women workers of the new world,
    I offer my congratulations again—
    Behold the homeland of heroes,
    The lion flies in freedom!

    (Note) The Bulgarian poet Vazov wrote a novel called "Under the Yoke," which profoundly narrates the people's struggle for liberation. In his short poem, he calls Bulgaria "the lion of the Balkans, he calls this lion to rise and fly!"

### 雄狮
田间
1959-09-09
第5版()
专栏：

    雄狮	田间
    玫瑰开在高山岗，
    山谷变成了画廊；
    大地像一幅彩画，
    天空比金子还亮。
    呵，巴尔干的狮子，
    早已从轭下解放；（注）
    雄狮的一声呼唤，
    能召来千里波浪。
    雄狮呵保加利亚，
    党使你有了力量；
    赫姆斯的山崖之旁，
    新的乐园红花开放。
    冶金工厂的火炉，
    火花日夜在照耀；
    火车从群山穿过，
    汽笛在地上鸣响。
    纪元前三千多年，
    这里是残岩断壁；
    这里不过是石洞，
    人类在洞里栖息。
    现在有了发电站，
    全国有了电力网；
    只要用一名女工，
    就能管理十二台机床。
    我走遍保加利亚，
    曾经对雄狮歌唱；
    你的每一条山谷，
    雕得像画廊一样。
    新世界的男女工人，
    我再来向你们祝贺——
    请看英雄的家乡，
    雄狮在自由飞翔！
    （注）保加利亚诗人伐佐夫，他写的一本小说，叫作“轭下”，书里深刻地叙述人民的解放斗争。在他的短诗里，他称保加利亚是“巴尔干的狮子，他呼唤这狮子起来飞翔！”
