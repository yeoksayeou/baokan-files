Title: Vietnam-China Friendship Association Establishes Preparatory Committee to Celebrate the 10th Anniversary of Our National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-09-16
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, - The Vietnam-China Friendship Association held a meeting on the morning of the 13th and established a preparatory committee to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. This preparatory committee includes thirty-six members from various Vietnamese political parties, people's organizations, and people from all walks of life.
    The meeting elected Hoang Van Hoan, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and former Vietnamese Ambassador to China, as Chairman of the Preparatory Committee; Bui Ky, Le Dinh Tham, Nguyen Xuan Thuy, Nguyen Chan, and Nghiem Xuan Yem as Vice Chairpersons; Nguyen Xuan Thuy as Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the Preparatory Committee; and Son Quang Phiet, Hoang Minh Giam, Le Quang Dao, and Vu Quoc Uy as Standing Committee members. Members include Truong Chinh, Phan Ke Toai, To Huu, and others.
    The meeting also discussed and approved the plan formulated by the Vietnam-China Friendship Association to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The main activities include celebration meetings, report meetings, publication of books, publication of celebratory articles in newspapers and periodicals, film screenings, exhibitions, get-togethers, cultural and sports activities, broadcasts, and the issuance of commemorative stamps. In addition, local preparatory committees are also being established in various regions.
    The Vietnam-China Friendship Association will also send a delegation to China to participate in the National Day celebrations.

### 庆祝我国国庆十周年  越中友协成立筹委会

1959-09-16
第7版()
专栏：

    庆祝我国国庆十周年
    越中友协成立筹委会
    据新华社河内电　越中友好协会在13日上午举行会议，成立了庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年筹备委员会。这个筹备委员会包括越南各政党、各人民团体和各阶层人民的三十六名委员。
    会议选出越南劳动党中央委员会政治局委员、越南民主共和国国会常务委员会副委员长、前越南驻中国大使黄文欢为筹备委员会主席；裴?、黎廷探、阮春水、阮阐、严春庵为副主席；阮春水为筹备委员会常务委员会秘书长，孙光阀、黄明鉴、黎光道、武国威为常务委员。委员中有长征、潘继遂、素友等。
    会议还讨论并通过了越中友好协会制订的庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年的计划。主要活动有庆祝会、报告会、出版书籍、报刊发表庆祝文章、放映电影、举办展览会、联欢会、文体活动、广播、发行纪念邮票等。此外，各地方也成立地方筹备委员会。
    越中友好协会还派出代表团到中国参加中国国庆观礼。
