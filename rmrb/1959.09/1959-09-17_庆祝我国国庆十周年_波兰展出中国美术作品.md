Title: Poland Exhibits Chinese Fine Art Works to Celebrate the 10th Anniversary of National Day

Author: 
Date: 1959-09-17
Page: 5

To celebrate the 10th anniversary of our National Day, Poland Exhibits Chinese Fine Art Works.

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw Electric Dispatch: The Chinese Arts and Crafts Exhibition and the Chinese Photo Exhibition simultaneously opened on the 14th at the Palace of Culture and Science in Warsaw. These two exhibitions were organized by the Polish Ministry of Culture and Art to celebrate the 10th anniversary of China's National Day.

Polish Minister of Culture and Art, Garinski, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Nashikovski, Secretary-General of the Presidium of the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association, Burkin, and others, as well as Yao Guang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Poland, attended the opening ceremony. Stazynski, Director of the Polish National Institute of Art, spoke at the opening ceremony. (Photo attached)

Left picture: Czechoslovak propaganda poster celebrating our National Day. The slogans in the picture read: Above: "Wishing the friendship between socialist countries grows and consolidates day by day." Below: "The 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China."　　　
Czechoslovak News Agency Manuscript (Xinhua News Agency issued)

### 庆祝我国国庆十周年  波兰展出中国美术作品

1959-09-17
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝我国国庆十周年
    波兰展出中国美术作品
    据新华社华沙电　中国工艺美术展览会和中国图片展览会，14日在华沙文化科学宫同时开幕。这两个展览会是波兰文化艺术部为庆祝中国国庆十周年而举办的。
    波兰文化艺术部部长加林斯基、外交部副部长纳希科夫斯基、波中友好协会总会主席团总书记布尔金等人以及中国驻波兰大使馆临时代办姚广参加了开幕式。波兰国立艺术研究所所长斯塔津斯基在开幕式上讲了话。（附图片）
    左图：捷克斯洛伐克庆祝我国国庆宣传画。画内的标语，上面：“祝社会主义国家之间的友谊日益增长和巩固”下面：“中华人民共和国建国十周年”　　　捷克斯洛伐克通讯社稿（新华社发）
