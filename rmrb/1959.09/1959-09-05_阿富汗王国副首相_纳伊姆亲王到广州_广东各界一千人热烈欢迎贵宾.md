Title: Afghan Kingdom's Deputy Prime Minister Prince Naim Arrives in Guangzhou, Greeted Warmly by 1,000 People from All Walks of Life in Guangdong

Author: 
Date: 1959-09-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 4th.  Sardar Mohammad Naim, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, and his entourage of eleven, arrived in Guangzhou by special train from Hong Kong at 1:30 PM today at the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai for a friendly visit to our country. They were warmly welcomed by Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province, and 1,000 people from all walks of life in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City. Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Yang Kezhong, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee, went to Shenzhen to greet them and accompanied the distinguished guests to Guangzhou.

Also accompanying Deputy Prime Minister Naim was Abdul Samad, Ambassador of the Kingdom of Afghanistan to China.

The national flags of China and Afghanistan were hung on the platform of Guangzhou Railway Station today. At 1:30 PM, the train slowly pulled into the station. Prince Naim and others stepped out of the carriages amidst applause and enthusiastic cheers from the crowd. Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province, Jiao Linyi, Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou, and Lieutenant General Zhan Caifang, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou, stepped forward to shake hands warmly with the distinguished guests. A group of Young Pioneers presented flowers to Deputy Prime Minister Naim and others. As the distinguished guests, accompanied by Governor Chen Yu and others, walked out of the station, the welcoming crowds lined up on both sides, holding flowers and applauding warmly to express their welcome. In front of the group of more than sixty Islamic figures, Deputy Prime Minister Naim also received flowers from Ma Fengda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Guangzhou Islamic Association.

Also present at the station to welcome the distinguished guests were: Lin Qiangyun, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, Zhang Ruiquan, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou, Major General Huang Yiping, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou, Sha Manbi, Director of the Guangzhou Islamic Association, and responsible persons of democratic parties and people's organizations of the province and city.

Kopek, the Czechoslovak Consul in Guangzhou, Wygoski, the Polish Consul in Guangzhou, and Le Chung Thi, the Acting Consul General of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Guangzhou, also went to the station to welcome the envoys of friendship from Afghanistan.

### 阿富汗王国副首相  纳伊姆亲王到广州  广东各界一千人热烈欢迎贵宾

1959-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    阿富汗王国副首相
    纳伊姆亲王到广州
    广东各界一千人热烈欢迎贵宾
    新华社广州4日电　应周恩来总理邀请来我国进行友好访问的阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆亲王和随行人员共十一人，于今天下午一时三十分从香港乘专车到达广州，受到广东省省长陈郁和广东省、广州市各界人民一千人的热烈欢迎。外交部礼宾司副司长马振武、广东省人民委员会外事处副处长杨可忠等前往深圳迎接，并陪同贵宾前来广州。
    陪同纳伊姆副首相前来的还有阿富汗王国驻我国大使阿卜杜勒·萨马德。
    广州车站的站台上今天悬挂着中、阿两国国旗。下午一时三十分，列车徐徐驶进车站，纳伊姆亲王等在群众的掌声和热烈欢呼声中走出车厢，广东省省长陈郁、广州市副市长焦林义和中国人民解放军驻广州部队首长詹才芳中将上前和贵宾热烈握手。一群少先队员向纳伊姆副首相等献花。贵宾们由陈郁省长等陪同走出车站的时候，手持鲜花排列在两旁的欢迎群众热烈鼓掌，表示欢迎。在六十多位伊斯兰教界人士的队伍前面，纳伊姆副首相又接受了广州伊斯兰教协会副秘书长马逢达的献花。
    到车站欢迎贵宾的还有：广东省副省长林锵云、广州市副市长张瑞权、中国人民解放军驻广州部队首长黄一平少将、广州伊斯兰教协会主任沙蔓弼，以及省、市各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻广州领事考贝克、波兰驻广州领事维高斯基、越南民主共和国驻广州代总领事黎终始也到车站欢迎来自阿富汗的友谊使者。
