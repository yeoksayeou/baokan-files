Title: Tunisian-Chinese Association Representative Hache Makes Report
Author: 
Date: 1959-09-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. The Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries invited Abdel Aziz Hache, a member of the Tunisian-Chinese Association delegation, tonight to make a report on the economic and cultural situation in Tunisia.

After the report meeting, a Tunisian film was shown.

### 突中协会代表哈希歇作报告

1959-09-20
第6版()
专栏：

    突中协会代表哈希歇作报告
    新华社19日讯　中国人民对外文化协会今晚邀请突尼斯—中国协会代表团团员阿卜杜勒·阿齐兹·哈希歇作关于突尼斯经济、文化情况的报告。
    报告会以后，放映了突尼斯电影。
