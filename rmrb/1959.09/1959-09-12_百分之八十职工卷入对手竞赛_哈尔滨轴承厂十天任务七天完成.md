Title: Eighty Percent of Employees Involved in Emulation Campaigns; Harbin Bearing Factory Completes Ten-Day Task in Seven Days
Author:
Date: 1959-09-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, September 11th: Workers at the Harbin Bearing Factory, through technological innovation and the promotion of advanced experience, overfulfilled the new production target for the first ten days of September by 5%, three days ahead of schedule. All employees of the factory have proposed increasing the output of bearings by 530,000 sets in September as a gift for the 10th anniversary of National Day.

In the first ten days of September, a new upsurge of large-scale competition was launched in this factory, with more than 80% of the employees involved in opponent emulation. The factory Party committee guided the workers' enthusiasm towards technological innovation and the promotion of advanced experience. The factory's office staff and workshop cadres also went deep into key shifts to work alongside the workers, while strengthening ideological education and work such as planning and scheduling, and statistical analysis. In the first seven days of September, the entire factory achieved more than 120 technological innovations and promoted 68 advanced experiences, increasing production efficiency by 10% to 11.5 times.

### 百分之八十职工卷入对手竞赛  哈尔滨轴承厂十天任务七天完成

1959-09-12
第5版()
专栏：

    百分之八十职工卷入对手竞赛
    哈尔滨轴承厂十天任务七天完成
    据新华社哈尔滨11日电　哈尔滨轴承厂职工，经过革新技术，推广先进经验，提前三天超额5％完成了9月上旬的新的生产任务。全厂职工已提出：在9月份增加五十三万套轴承，向国庆十周年献礼。
    9月上旬，这个厂掀起了大比大赛的新高潮，80％以上的职工卷入对手竞赛。工厂党委把职工的干劲引向技术革新和推广先进经验方面。厂里科室人员和车间干部也深入重点跟班劳动，同时加强思想教育和计划调度、统计分析等工作。在9月头七天，全厂就实现了一百二十多项技术革新，推广了六十八项先进经验，使生产效率提高10％到十一倍半。
