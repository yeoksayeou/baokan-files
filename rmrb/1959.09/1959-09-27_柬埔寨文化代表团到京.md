Title: Cambodian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: The Cambodian Cultural Delegation, led by its head, Phithien Lai, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, to visit China and attend our National Day celebrations for the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 柬埔寨文化代表团到京

1959-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨文化代表团到京
    新华社26日讯　应对外文化联络委员会邀请，来我国访问并参加我国建国十周年庆祝典礼的柬埔寨文化代表团一行三人，在团长披天来率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
