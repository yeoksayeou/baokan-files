Title: Greek Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Greek cultural delegation of four people, led by head of delegation Kostopoulos, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon to visit our country and participate in the celebrations for the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 希腊文化代表团到京

1959-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    希腊文化代表团到京
    新华社24日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的希腊文化代表团一行四人，在团长科斯托普洛斯率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
