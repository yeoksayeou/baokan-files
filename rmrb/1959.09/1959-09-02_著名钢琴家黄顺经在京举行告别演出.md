Title: Famous Pianist Huang Shunjing Holds Farewell Performance in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-02
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, September 1st. Famous pianist and professor at the Luxembourg Conservatory of Music, Huang Shunjing, held a farewell performance in Beijing tonight. Each program she performed won extremely enthusiastic applause.
    After the performance, the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries held a reception to congratulate Huang Shunjing on the success of her performance. Among those present at tonight's concert and reception were Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and famous musicians from the capital.

### 著名钢琴家黄顺经在京举行告别演出

1959-09-02
第5版()
专栏：

    著名钢琴家黄顺经在京举行告别演出
    新华社1日讯　著名钢琴家、卢森堡音乐学院教授黄顺经今晚在北京举行告别演出。她演出的每一个节目，都博得了极其热烈的掌声。
    演出结束后，中国人民对外文化协会举行酒会祝贺黄顺经演出成功。出席今晚音乐会和酒会的，有中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥，北京市副市长吴晗和首都著名音乐家。
