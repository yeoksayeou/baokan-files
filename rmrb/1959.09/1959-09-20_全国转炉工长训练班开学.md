Title: National Converter Shop Foreman Training Class Opens
Author:
Date: 1959-09-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Qingdao, 18th. The national small converter shop foreman training class, organized by the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry in Qingdao, officially opened on the 16th of this month. The one hundred and eleven participants in the training class are all shop foremen from various parts of the country, working in 0.5 to 1.5-ton small converter steelmaking units, in the cupola, converter, ingot casting, and furnace lining trades.

The training class will adopt a method combining theory and practice, studying the important documents issued at the National Converter Steelmaking Quality Improvement Experience Exchange Conference, and conducting on-site operational practice.

The overall requirement for the students is to basically master the operation of small converters within two to three weeks, so that they can quickly bring the greater power of small converters into play after returning to their units, and make greater contributions to completing and over fulfilling this year's steel production target of 12 million tons.

### 全国转炉工长训练班开学

1959-09-20
第3版()
专栏：

    全国转炉工长训练班开学
    新华社青岛18日电冶金工业部在青岛举办的全国小转炉工长训练班本月16日正式开学。参加训练班学习的一百十一人，都是来自全国各地零点五吨至一点五吨小转炉炼钢单位的化铁炉、转炉、铸锭和炉衬等工种的工长。
    这个训练班将采取理论与实践相结合的方法进行，一面学习全国转炉炼钢提高质量经验交流会所印发的重要文件，一面到现场操作实习。
    对学员的总的要求是，在二到三周的时间内，基本上掌握小转炉的操作，以便回去后能迅速使小转炉发挥更大威力，为完成和超额完成今年产钢一千二百万吨的任务作出更大贡献。
