Title: Premier Zhou at the Farewell Reception for Tanzan Ishibashi Said: May the Chinese and Japanese People Live Together in Peace and Friendship; Tanzan Ishibashi Expressed Complete Satisfaction with This Visit
Author:
Date: 1959-09-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 22nd: Premier Zhou Enlai said at the farewell reception held by former Japanese Prime Minister Tanzan Ishibashi this evening: "The current relationship between China and Japan is not satisfactory. Although there are many difficulties in improving this relationship, these difficulties are not insurmountable as long as the people of both countries work hard with the desire to live together in peace and friendship."

The reception was held at the Beijing Hotel. Vice Premier Chen Yi also attended today's reception.

Tanzan Ishibashi said at the beginning of the reception: This visit will be the beginning of opening up the relationship between China and Japan. He said, "I am returning from this visit to China with complete satisfaction." He expressed his gratitude for the warm hospitality he received during the visit and raised his glass to wish Premier Zhou Enlai good health and to congratulate China on its tenth anniversary of founding.

Premier Zhou Enlai said in his speech that he hoped Mr. Ishibashi would convey to the Japanese people the Chinese people's wish to live in peace and friendship with the Japanese people.

He said: "Following Mr. Tanzan Ishibashi's visit, Mr. Kenzo Matsumura will also visit. We welcome him."

The Premier said: "As stated in the communique, mutual visits between politicians of the two countries are conducive to improving the existing relations between China and Japan and promoting friendship between the two peoples." He finally proposed a toast to the friendship between the Chinese and Japanese peoples, and to the health of Mr. Tanzan Ishibashi and his wife and entourage.

Also invited to the reception were Guo Moruo, Liao Chengzhi, Li Dequan, Liu Changsheng, Zhang Hanfu, Lei Renmin, Hu Yuzhi, Cai Tingkai, Li Zhuchen, Xu Guangping, Yang Hansheng, Mei Lanfang, Ouyang Yuqian, Hou Debang, Zhao Puchu, Xie Bingxin, Zhao Anbo, Xie Nanguang, Ding Tuo and other people from all walks of life in the capital.

Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji and his wife, who are in Beijing, also attended the reception.

The reception was held in a very friendly atmosphere throughout. (Attached picture)

### 周总理在石桥湛山告别酒会上说  愿中日人民和平友好相处  石桥湛山表示对这次访问完全满意

1959-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理在石桥湛山告别酒会上说
    愿中日人民和平友好相处
    石桥湛山表示对这次访问完全满意
    新华社22日讯　周恩来总理今晚在应邀出席日本前首相石桥湛山举行的告别酒会上说：“中日两国之间现存的关系是不能令人满意的，要改善这种关系虽有很多困难，但是只要两国人民本着和平友好相处的心愿加以努力，这种困难不是不能克服的。”
    酒会在北京饭店举行。陈毅副总理也应邀出席了今天的酒会。
    石桥湛山在酒会开始时说：通过这次访问，将是打开中日两国之间关系的一个开端。他说：“这次访问中国，我是怀着完全满意的心情回去的。”他对在访问期间受到的亲切招待表示感谢，并举杯祝周恩来总理身体健康，祝贺中国建国十周年。
    周恩来总理在致词中表示希望石桥先生把中国人民愿和日本人民和平相处、友好往来的心愿转达给日本人民。
    他说：“继石桥湛山先生来访之后，松村谦三先生也要来访，我们表示欢迎。”
    总理说：“照公报所说，两国政治家彼此访问，有利于改善中日两国之间现存的关系，促进两国人民友好。”他最后提议为中日两国人民友好，为石桥湛山先生和他的夫人以及随行人员的健康干杯。
    应邀出席酒会的还有郭沫若、廖承志、李德全、刘长胜、章汉夫、雷任民、胡愈之、蔡廷锴、李烛尘、许广平、阳翰笙、梅兰芳、欧阳予倩、侯德榜、赵朴初、谢冰心、赵安博、谢南光、丁拓等首都各界人士。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一和他的夫人也出席了酒会。
    酒会一直在非常友好的气氛中举行。（附图片）
