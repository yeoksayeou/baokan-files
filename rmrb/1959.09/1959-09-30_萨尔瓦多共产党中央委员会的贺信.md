Title: Letter of Greetings from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of El Salvador
Author:
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 9

Comrades:
    The Communist Party of El Salvador and the people of El Salvador extend our heartfelt congratulations to the Chinese government, the Chinese Communist Party, and the Chinese people. Your extraordinary achievements in industrial development demonstrate your ability to become a first-class industrial power in a very short period of time.
    We know that the liberation of China is an event of enormous significance because it greatly strengthens the world socialist front; we know that the victory of the Chinese people is a great defeat for the world imperialist front, making the imperialist countries paper tigers; we also know that this victory of the Chinese people is an invaluable contribution to the people of colonial and dependent countries in their struggle for liberation.
    Our congratulations signify, on the one hand, our admiration for the great victories achieved by the Chinese people in economic development, and on the other hand, the support of the Salvadoran people for the struggle of the world anti-imperialist front, the aim of which is to have true China recognized in the United Nations as a great world power, because the participation of liberated China in this organization would be the best contribution to the struggle for peace and peaceful coexistence among the peoples of the world.
    We know that U.S. imperialism is using the Chiang Kai-shek puppet to divide the Chinese people and open the door to imperialist intervention, and therefore they oppose the People's Republic of China being admitted to the United Nations, but it is the responsibility of the peoples of the world to condemn the deceitful and evil policy of the main enemy of national independence – U.S. imperialism.
    Fight for the representation of the People's Republic of China in the United Nations!
    Fight for the liberation of Taiwan!
    Fight for the continuous Great Leap Forward in the socialist development of the Chinese people!
    Fight for peace and peaceful coexistence among all countries in the world!
    Long live the great Chinese people!
    Long live the Chinese Communist Party!
    Long live Comrade Mao Zedong!

### 萨尔瓦多共产党中央委员会的贺信

1959-09-30
第9版()
专栏：

    萨尔瓦多共产党中央委员会的贺信同志们：
    萨尔瓦多共产党和萨尔瓦多人民向中国政府、中国共产党和中国人民表示衷心的祝贺，你们在发展工业方面的非凡的成就表明了你们能够在很短时期内成为第一流的工业强国。
    我们知道，中国的解放是一个有巨大影响的事件，因为它大大加强了世界社会主义战线；我们知道，中国人民的胜利是世界帝国主义战线的巨大失败，它使帝国主义国家成为纸老虎；我们也知道，中国人民的这个胜利对于为争取自身解放的殖民地和附属国人民是一个极宝贵的贡献。
    我们的祝贺一方面意味着我们对于中国人民在发展经济方面所取得的巨大胜利的钦佩，同时也意味着萨尔瓦多人民对世界反帝战线所进行的斗争的支援，其目的在于使真正的中国作为伟大的世界强国的意义在联合国中获得承认，因为解放了的中国参加这一组织就将是对争取和平以及世界各国人民和平共处的斗争最好的贡献。
    我们知道，美帝国主义利用蒋介石傀儡是为了分裂中国人民，使大门为帝国主义干涉敞开着，因此他们反对中华人民共和国被接纳入联合国，但是谴责这个各国人民的独立的主要敌人——美帝国主义的欺骗的罪恶政策是世界各国人民的责任。
    为中华人民共和国在联合国中的代表权而奋斗！
    为解放台湾而奋斗！
    为中国人民的社会主义发展取得不断大跃进而奋斗！
    为和平和世界各国和平共处而奋斗！
    伟大的中国人民万岁！
    中国共产党万岁！
    毛泽东同志万岁！
