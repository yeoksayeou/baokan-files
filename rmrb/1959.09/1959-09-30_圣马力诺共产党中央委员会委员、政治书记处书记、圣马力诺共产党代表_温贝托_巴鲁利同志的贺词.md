Title: Comrade Umberto Barulli, Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of San Marino, Secretary of the Political Secretariat, Representative of the Communist Party of San Marino, Sends Greetings

Author: 
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 8

Comrade Umberto Barulli, Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of San Marino, Secretary of the Political Secretariat, Representative of the Communist Party of San Marino, Sends Greetings

Dear Comrades:

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of San Marino extends its warmest respects and deepest gratitude to the glorious Communist Party of China and all the Chinese people for allowing a representative of our party to participate in the great celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Ten years ago, in your vast country, the Chinese people, under the correct leadership of your great Party and its wise leader Mao Zedong, after a difficult, cruel, and protracted struggle, gained freedom and independence and began to build a socialist society.

For the struggle of the proletariat of all countries of the world under the leadership of the Communist and Workers' Parties to overthrow capitalism and colonialism, your victory in the people's democratic revolution in 1949 was a historical event of great significance following the Great October Socialist Revolution of 1917.

The celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China is held at a time when the Communist and Workers' Parties of all countries are closely united in the spirit of proletarian internationalism. The unity of socialist countries, the fraternal cooperation embodied in the teachings of Marxism-Leninism, and the consistent policy of peace, the struggle of the people of all countries to ease tensions, and the great achievements of Soviet science have laid the foundation for preventing the outbreak of new wars and peacefully resolving all international disputes.

In the ten years since the People's Government led by your Party came to power, the Chinese people have made tremendous achievements spiritually and materially; the experience you have gained in advancing along the road of building a socialist society is immense, enriching the international communist movement with new factors and experiences.

Before your Party lies the great task of socialist construction. You will deal with and solve these tasks with the spirit and political talent that have made your Party one of the most capable and thorough parties.

The people of the world's oldest republic—the people of San Marino, who in the autumn of 1957 became victims of a coup by capitalists to overthrow the Socialist and Communist government—admire with deep feeling the great achievements your country has made in such a short period of time, and sincerely wish you new victories and new achievements.

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of San Marino once again expresses its warm gratitude, and at the same time wishes you, the Communists, and all the Chinese people new and greater achievements in guiding this great and attractive country towards the road of building a communist society.

Long live the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China!

Long live the great and glorious Communist Party of China!

Long live proletarian internationalism!

Long live the friendship of the people of all countries!

Long live peace! Long live communism! (Attached picture)

### 圣马力诺共产党中央委员会委员、政治书记处书记、圣马力诺共产党代表  温贝托·巴鲁利同志的贺词

1959-09-30
第8版()
专栏：

   圣马力诺共产党中央委员会委员、政治书记处书记、圣马力诺共产党代表
    温贝托·巴鲁利同志的贺词亲爱的同志们：
    承你们让我们党的代表有机会来参加中华人民共和国成立十周年的伟大庆祝典礼，圣马力诺共产党中央委员会谨向光荣的中国共产党和全体中国人民表示最热烈的敬意和最深切的感谢。
    十年前，在你们辽阔的国家中，中国人民在你们伟大的党和它的英明领袖毛泽东的正确领导下，经过艰巨的残酷的长期斗争获得了自由和独立，开始建设社会主义社会。
    对于世界各国无产阶级在共产党和工人党的领导下所进行的为推翻资本主义和殖民主义的斗争来说，你们在1949年获得的人民民主革命的胜利，是继1917年伟大十月社会主义革命之后的一个具有极大意义的历史事件。
    中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼，是在各国共产党和工人党本着无产阶级国际主义精神紧密地团结一致的时候举行的。社会主义国家的团结，以马克思列宁主义的教导为依据而具体化了的兄弟般的合作，和始终如一的和平政策，各国人民争取缓和紧张局势的斗争以及苏联科学所取得的巨大成就，已经为防止新战争爆发以及和平解决一切国际争端奠定了基础。
    由你们党领导的人民政府执政十年来，中国人民在精神上和物质上已取得了巨大的成就；你们在沿着建设社会主义社会道路前进中所取得的经验是巨大的，它以新的因素和经验丰富了国际共产主义运动。
    在你们党面前正摆着宏伟的社会主义建设的任务。你们将用使你们党成为最有能力和最彻底的党之一的精神和政治才干来处理和解决这些任务。
    世界最古老的共和国的人民——圣马力诺的人民在1957年秋曾经成为资本家推翻社会党和共产党政府的政变的牺牲者，他们怀着深切的心情钦佩你们的国家在这样短促的时间内所取得的巨大成就，并衷心地祝你们取得新的胜利和新的成就。
    圣马力诺共产党中央委员会再次表示热烈的感谢，同时祝你们共产党人和全体中国人民在引导这个伟大的和富于吸引力的国家走向建设共产主义社会的道路上取得新的更巨大的成就。
    中华人民共和国成立十周年万岁！
    伟大和光荣的中国共产党万岁！
    无产阶级国际主义万岁！
    各国人民的友谊万岁！
    和平万岁！共产主义万岁！（附图片）
