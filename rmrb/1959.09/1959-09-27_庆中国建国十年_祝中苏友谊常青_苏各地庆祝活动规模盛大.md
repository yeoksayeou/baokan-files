Title: Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of the Founding of China, Wishing Everlasting Sino-Soviet Friendship, Large-Scale Celebrations Held in Various Parts of the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1959-09-27
Page: 9
Countries Celebrate China's National Day

    Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of the Founding of China, Wishing Everlasting Sino-Soviet Friendship
    Large-Scale Celebrations Held in Various Parts of the Soviet Union
    Xinhua News Agency, September 26th dispatch. TASS, Moscow, September 25th dispatch: Novgorodsky, Secretary-General of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, told a TASS reporter that the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association and its local branches are preparing to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, a day of great significance. From September 25th to October 3rd, tens of thousands of gatherings will be held to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of China.
    He added that the staff of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Council have been unprecedentedly busy recently. Every morning, they receive bundles of letters from working people all over the country, in which they request to convey their good wishes to the Chinese people and request to send them pictures, books and materials related to the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.
    Novgorodsky pointed out that celebrations have already begun across the country. In the cities and villages of Ukraine, report meetings have been held on the great achievements of the Chinese people in socialist construction and the unbreakable friendship between the people of the two major countries, the Soviet Union and China.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 25th: People from all walks of life in Moscow held a grand gathering on the evening of the 25th to warmly celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.
    This celebration was jointly organized by the Soviet Association for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge and the Institute of Chinese Studies of the Soviet Academy of Sciences.
    Dubrovina, Vice Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, Academician Viksinsky of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Doctor of Medicine Bogolepova, Sculptor Keber, Painter Kravchenko, Deputy Doctor of Philosophy Edlin and others spoke at the meeting.
    The celebration was conducted in an extremely warm and friendly atmosphere. The speakers all praised the great achievements of the Chinese people. From time to time, people stood up and shouted slogans such as "Long live the heroic and hard-working Chinese people," "Long live the Chinese Communist Party," "Long live Comrade Mao Zedong," "Long live Sino-Soviet friendship," and "Glory to the great Chinese people."

### 庆中国建国十年　祝中苏友谊常青  苏各地庆祝活动规模盛大

1959-09-27
第9版()
专栏：

    各国人民庆祝我国国庆
    庆中国建国十年　祝中苏友谊常青
    苏各地庆祝活动规模盛大
    据新华社26日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科25日讯：苏中友好协会理事会秘书长诺夫戈罗德斯基对塔斯社记者说，苏中友协和它的各地分会正在准备庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年这个具有重大意义的日子。在9月25日到10月3日期间，将要举行几万个庆祝中国建国十周年的集会。
    他又说，最近苏中友协理事会的工作人员空前地忙碌，这里每天早晨都收到全国各地的劳动人民寄来的一束束信件，他们在这些信件中请求转达他们对中国人民的良好祝愿，并请求寄给他们有关中华人民共和国成立十周年的图片、书籍和材料。
    诺夫戈罗德斯基指出，目前庆祝活动在全国各地已经展开。在乌克兰的城市和农村已经举行了有关中国人民在社会主义建设中的巨大成就、世界两大国苏联和中国人民牢不可破友谊的报告会。
    据新华社莫斯科25日电  莫斯科各界人民25日晚间举行隆重集会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年。
    这个庆祝会是由苏联政治和科学知识普及协会和苏联科学院汉学研究所联合举办的。
    苏中友好协会理事会副主席杜伯洛维娜、苏联科学院院士维克辛斯基、医学博士鲍戈列波娃、雕刻家克贝尔、画家克拉夫钦科、哲学副博士埃德林等都在会上讲了话。
    庆祝会是在极其热烈友好的气氛中进行的。讲话的人都盛赞中国人民的伟大成就。会场上不时有人起立高呼：“英勇勤劳的中国人民万岁”、“中国共产党万岁”、“毛泽东同志万岁”、“苏中友谊万岁”、“光荣归于伟大的中国人民”等口号。
