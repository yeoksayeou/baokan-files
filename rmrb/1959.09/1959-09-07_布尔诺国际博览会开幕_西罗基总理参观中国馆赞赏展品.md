Title: Brno International Fair Opens; Premier Široký Visits Chinese Pavilion and Praises Exhibits
Author: 
Date: 1959-09-07
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Brno, September 5th: The first Brno International Fair, marking the comprehensive development achievements of machinery manufacturing in various countries around the world, especially in the socialist camp, held a grand opening ceremony here this afternoon.

Czechoslovak Premier Viliam Široký delivered a speech at the opening ceremony.

In discussing the important significance of the fair, the Premier said that it is an important new tool for further developing cooperation among socialist countries, and it once again proves that developing fraternal cooperation is beneficial to all socialist countries in every aspect.

Široký welcomed the representatives of some independent countries in Asia, Africa, and Latin America who participated in the fair. He emphasized that Czechoslovakia attaches great importance to economic and trade cooperation with countries whose economies are backward due to the colonial rule of imperialism.

In discussing the international situation, he expressed his firm belief that the results of Khrushchev's visit to the United States will be an important step towards easing international tensions, resolving pressing issues, and realizing a summit conference.

He said that it is time to end the "Cold War." He also emphasized that it is absolutely necessary to conclude a German peace treaty and eliminate the abnormal situation in West Berlin.

Attending the opening ceremony were Premier Široký and other Czechoslovak party and government leaders, the Soviet government delegation headed by Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Ustinov, the Chinese government delegation headed by Vice Premier of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, government delegations from other socialist countries, as well as the heads of exhibition delegations from the thirty countries participating in the fair, and foreign diplomatic envoys, totaling approximately seven hundred people.

After the ceremony, guests invited to the ceremony visited various exhibition halls in batches.

The fair covers an area of ​​52 hectares, with a visiting route of 60 kilometers, and is equipped with various modern facilities. It is one of the largest and best fairs in the world.

The 300,000 products exhibited separately by various Czechoslovak foreign trade specialized companies were widely praised. The richness and ingenuity of the exhibits from the Soviet Union, China, the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Hungary, Bulgaria, Romania, and other countries attracted great attention. It demonstrates the power of the socialist peaceful economy with irrefutable facts.

Xinhua News Agency, Brno, September 5th: Czechoslovak Premier Viliam Široký, Deputy Premier Šimůnek, and other government officials visited the Chinese Pavilion at the first Brno International Fair this afternoon.

After the visit, he pointed out to Gao Shirong, the head of the Chinese exhibition delegation, that everyone can see the achievements of China's Great Leap Forward here.

Premier Široký spent a total of fifteen minutes visiting. He viewed most of the machine tools and machinery exhibits in the Chinese Pavilion. During the visit, he frequently nodded, expressed his appreciation for the exhibits, and repeatedly said: "Very good."

The Chinese Pavilion covers an area of ​​more than 3,000 square meters and exhibits more than 2,000 products. Among them, machine tools, textile machinery, and electrical products are the main exhibits, and railway electrical control equipment, metallurgical sections, medical equipment, tools, instruments, and ball bearings are also exhibited.

### 布尔诺国际博览会开幕  西罗基总理参观中国馆赞赏展品

1959-09-07
第6版()
专栏：

    布尔诺国际博览会开幕
    西罗基总理参观中国馆赞赏展品
    新华社布尔诺5日电　标志着世界各国、特别是社会主义阵营各国机器制造业全面发展成就的第一届布尔诺国际博览会，今天下午在这里举行了隆重的开幕典礼。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理西罗基在开幕典礼上致了词。
    总理在谈到博览会的重要意义时说，这是进一步发展社会主义国家之间合作的一个重要的新工具，它再一次证明，对一切社会主义国家来说，发展兄弟般的合作在各个方面来看都是有利的。
    西罗基对参加这次博览会的亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲一些独立国家的代表表示欢迎，他强调说，捷克斯洛伐克非常重视同那些因帝国主义的殖民统治而经济落后的国家进行经济和贸易合作。
    在谈到国际局势时，他表示坚信，赫鲁晓夫访问美国的结果将是走向缓和国际紧张局势、解决迫切问题和实现最高级会议的一个重要步骤。
    他说，现在是结束“冷战”的时候了。他还强调说，完全有必要缔结对德和约和消除西柏林的不正常状态。
    参加开幕典礼的有西罗基总理和捷克斯洛伐克其他党政领导人，以部长会议副主席乌斯季诺夫为首的苏联政府代表团，以国务院副总理习仲勋为首的中国政府代表团，其他社会主义国家的政府代表团，以及参加博览会展出的三十个国家的展览代表团团长、各国外交使节等共约七百人。
    典礼结束后，应邀参加典礼的客人们分批参观了各个展览馆。
    这个博览会占地五十二公顷，参观路程达六十公里，博览会并备有各种现代化设备。它是世界上最大、最好的博览会之一。
    由捷克斯洛伐克各个对外贸易专业公司分头展出的三十万件产品，受到大家普遍的赞扬。苏联、中国、德意志民主共和国、波兰、匈牙利、保加利亚、罗马尼亚等国展品的丰富和精巧，引起了人们极大注意。它以无可辩驳的事实显示了社会主义和平经济的威力。
    新华社布尔诺5日电　捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基、副总理西穆?克以及其他政府负责人，今天下午参观了第一届布尔诺国际博览会的中国馆。
    他在参观以后对中国展览团团长高仕融指出，每个人都从这里看到了中国大跃进的成绩。
    西罗基总理共参观了十五分钟。他观看了中国馆的大部分机床和机械展品。参观时，他频频点头，对展品表示赞赏，连声说：“非常好”。
    中国馆的面积是三千多平方米，展品有二千多种。其中以机床、纺织机器和电机产品为主，并配合展出铁道电气控制设备、冶金切片、医疗器械、工具仪表和滚珠轴承等。
