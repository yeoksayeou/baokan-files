Title: Anniversary of Victory Against Japan and 14th Anniversary of Soviet Army Helping Liberate Northeast China Marked with Memorial Activities in Liaoning and Heilongjiang

Author: 
Date: 1959-09-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, September 3rd: Representatives from all walks of life in Liaoning Province and Shenyang City held a memorial ceremony at the Soviet Army Martyrs' Monument today to commemorate the victory of the War of Resistance Against Japan and the 14th anniversary of the Soviet Union's dispatch of troops to help China liberate Northeast China.

Nearly a thousand people participated in the monument ceremony, including leaders of the Liaoning Province and Shenyang City Party and government, as well as leaders of local troops, various democratic parties, people's organizations, and representatives from all walks of life.

Soviet experts in Shenyang and Kim Yong-bin, the Consul General of North Korea in Shenyang, also attended the monument ceremony.

After the ceremony began, representatives from all walks of life in the province and city presented wreaths to the Soviet Army martyrs who heroically sacrificed themselves for the liberation of the people of Northeast China to pay their respects. Soviet experts in Shenyang and personnel from the North Korean Consulate General in Shenyang also presented wreaths.

Shenyang Vice Mayor Song Guang, on behalf of the people, thanked the Soviet army for its internationalist action in liberating the people of Northeast China. Soviet expert representative Tokarev also spoke.

Tonight, the Liaoning Province and Shenyang City branches of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will also hold a "September 3rd" celebration, inviting Soviet experts who are helping China's construction in Shenyang to celebrate with representatives from all walks of life in Shenyang.

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, September 3rd: Today is the 14th anniversary of the victory of the "September 3rd" War of Resistance Against Japan. Since the morning, workers, students, cadres, and children in Harbin have gone to the Soviet Army Anti-Japanese War Victory Monument, the Soviet Army Martyrs' Memorial Tower, and the Soviet Army Cemetery to lay flowers.

The Heilongjiang Province and Harbin City branches of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association jointly held a report meeting tonight with the theme of "Looking at the Great Development of Sino-Soviet Friendship from Commemorating 'September 3rd'."

### 抗战胜利和苏军帮助解放东北十四周年  辽宁和黑龙江举行纪念活动

1959-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    抗战胜利和苏军帮助解放东北十四周年
    辽宁和黑龙江举行纪念活动
    据新华社沈阳3日电　辽宁省、沈阳市各界人民代表今天在苏军烈士纪念碑前举行祭碑仪式，纪念抗日战争胜利和苏联出兵帮助我国解放东北十四周年。
    参加祭碑仪式的有，辽宁省、沈阳市党和政府负责人以及当地驻军、各民主党派、各人民团体负责人、各界人民代表近千人。
    在沈阳的苏联专家和朝鲜驻沈阳总领事金龙彬也参加了祭碑仪式。
    祭碑仪式开始后，省、市各界人民向为解放东北人民而英勇牺牲的苏军烈士献花圈致敬。在沈阳的苏联专家和朝鲜驻沈阳总领事馆人员也分别献了花圈。
    沈阳市副市长宋光代表各界人民感谢苏联军队解放东北人民的国际主义行动。苏联专家代表托卡列夫也讲了话。
    今晚，辽宁省和沈阳市中苏友好协会还举行了纪念“九三”联欢晚会，邀请在沈阳帮助我国建设的苏联专家同沈阳市各界人民代表一起联欢。
    新华社哈尔滨3日电　今天是“九三”抗日战争胜利十四周年纪念日。从早晨起，哈尔滨市工人、学生、干部、儿童纷纷前往苏军抗日战争胜利纪念碑、苏军烈士纪念塔和苏军墓地献花。
    黑龙江省和哈尔滨市中苏友好协会今晚联合举行了以“从纪念‘九三’看中苏友谊的巨大发展”为主题的报告会。
