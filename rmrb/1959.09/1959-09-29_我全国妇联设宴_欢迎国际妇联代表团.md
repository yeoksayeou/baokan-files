Title: All-China Women's Federation Hosts Banquet to Welcome International Women's Federation Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-09-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet at noon today to welcome the delegation of the International Democratic Women's Federation, headed by Marie-Claude Vaillant-Couturier, Vice-President of the International Democratic Women's Federation. Also invited to the banquet were Andrea Andreen, Vice-President of the International Democratic Women's Federation, and Mrs. Mary Prit.

Among those attending the banquet were Li Dequan, Xu Guangping, Shi Liang, Liu Qingyang, and Kang Keqing, Vice-Presidents of the All-China Women's Federation; Cao Mengjun, Shen Zijiu, Zhang Xiaomei, Cao Guanqun, and Zeng Xianzhi, members of the Presidium; and secretaries of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation.

### 我全国妇联设宴  欢迎国际妇联代表团

1959-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

    我全国妇联设宴
    欢迎国际妇联代表团
    新华社28日讯　中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会主席蔡畅，今天中午设宴欢迎以国际民主妇女联合会副主席玛丽—克洛德·瓦扬—古久里夫人为首的国际民主妇联代表团，同时被邀请参加宴会的有国际民主妇联副主席安德蕾亚·安德琳夫人，以及玛利·普里特夫人。
    出席宴会的有全国妇联副主席李德全、许广平、史良、刘清扬、康克清，主席团委员曹孟君、沈兹九、张晓梅、曹冠群、曾宪植和全国妇联书记处书记等人。
