Title: China and Afghanistan Issue Joint Communiqué: Unanimously Agree to Strengthen and Develop Economic and Cultural Ties and Expand the Scope of Technical Cooperation; Both Sides Hope that Asian and African Countries Should Strictly Abide by the Bandung Conference Spirit and the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence
Author:
Date: 1959-09-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 14th. Joint Communiqué of the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Afghanistan

At the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Sardar Mohammad Naim, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, arrived in Beijing on September 5, 1959, accompanied by Sardar Ghulam Mohammad Sherzad, Minister of Commerce of Afghanistan, and other officials of the Government of the Kingdom of Afghanistan.

During Deputy Prime Minister Sardar Mohammad Naim's visit to Beijing, he was received by Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and President Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China.

Deputy Prime Minister Sardar Mohammad Naim held sincere and friendly talks with Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi of the People's Republic of China. During the talks, the two sides exchanged views on issues directly related to China and Afghanistan, major issues in Asia, and important issues of general concern to the people of the world.

Both sides expressed satisfaction with the development of friendly relations between the two countries. Both sides unanimously agreed to continue to strengthen and develop economic and cultural ties between the two countries and expand the scope of technical cooperation. Both sides also expressed their willingness to further strengthen the existing good-neighborly relations between China and Afghanistan.

Both sides reaffirmed their firm belief in the principles formulated and unanimously agreed upon at the Bandung Asian-African Conference. Both sides expressed their determination to work to safeguard harmonious relations among Asian and African countries based on peaceful cooperation, mutual respect, and non-interference in internal affairs. Both sides hope that Asian and African countries should strictly abide by the spirit of the Bandung Conference and the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence to strengthen friendly relations. They also believe that all disputes between Asian and African countries should be resolved reasonably through peaceful consultation so that friendly relations between Asian and African countries will not be harmed.

On international issues of universal significance, both sides welcomed the upcoming visits of the leaders of the Soviet Union and the United States to each other's countries, as they believe that this will be beneficial to further easing international tensions and safeguarding the cause of world peace.

Both sides believe that the beliefs expressed by both sides in this meeting are fully in line with the policies of the two governments, the basic principles of the United Nations Charter, and the spirit of the Bandung Asian-African Conference.

Deputy Prime Minister Sardar Mohammad Naim invited Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi of the State Council of the People's Republic of China to visit Afghanistan. This invitation has been accepted, and the visit will take place at an appropriate time next year.

Beijing, September 9, 1959.

Chen Yi （signed）
Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the State Council of the People's Republic of China

Sardar Mohammad Naim（signed）
Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Afghanistan

### 中国和阿富汗发表联合公报  一致同意加强和发展两国经济文化联系和扩大技术合作范围  双方希望亚非国家应严格遵守万隆会议精神与和平共处原则

1959-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中国和阿富汗发表联合公报
    一致同意加强和发展两国经济文化联系和扩大技术合作范围
    双方希望亚非国家应严格遵守万隆会议精神与和平共处原则
    新华社14日讯　中华人民共和国和阿富汗王国联合公报
    应中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来的邀请，阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆于1959年9月5日抵达北京，同行的有阿富汗商业大臣萨达尔·吴拉姆·穆罕默德·舍尔扎德先生和阿富汗王国政府的其他官员。
    在萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆副首相在北京访问的期间，中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东阁下和中华人民共和国主席刘少奇阁下接见了他。
    萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆副首相同中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来和副总理兼外交部长陈毅进行了诚恳友好的会谈，会谈中双方就中阿两国直接有关的问题、亚洲的重大问题和世界人民普遍关心的重要问题交换了意见。
    双方对两国友好关系的发展表示满意。双方一致同意继续加强和发展两国间的经济、文化联系和扩大技术合作的范围。双方并且表示愿意进一步加强中国和阿富汗之间现存的睦邻关系。
    双方重申对万隆亚非会议所制定和一致同意的原则的坚定信念。双方表示决心致力于维护亚非各国之间建立在和平合作、互相尊重和互不干涉内政的基础上的和睦关系。双方希望亚非国家应该严格遵守万隆会议的精神和和平共处的原则，加强友好关系；并且认为，亚非各国之间的一切争端都应该通过和平协商来求得合理解决，使亚非国家之间的友好关系，不致于受到损害。
    在具有普遍意义的国际问题中，双方表示欢迎苏联和美国的领导人对彼此的国家即将进行的访问，因为双方相信，这对于进一步和缓国际紧张局势和维护世界和平事业是有益的。
    双方相信，在这次会谈中双方所表示的信念完全符合两国政府的政策，也符合联合国宪章的基本原则和万隆亚非会议的精神。
    萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆副首相邀请了中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅访问阿富汗。这一邀请已被接受，访问将在明年适当的时候进行。
    1959年9月9日于北京。
    中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长  陈毅 （签字）
    阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣  萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆（签字）
