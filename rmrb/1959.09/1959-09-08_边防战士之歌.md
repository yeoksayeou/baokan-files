Title: Songs of Border Guards
Author: Sun Dewei, Li Zhilin, Yu Youxin
Date: 1959-09-08
Page: 8

    Border Village Smoke
    Border village smoke,
    Pond ducks and geese call,
    Fat pigs in the pen are noisy,
    Red flowers bloom before the house,
    White sheep run along the cliff.
    See off the moon on the mountain,
    Awake the birds in the forest,
    Return from patrol at dawn,
    Border village smoke drifts.
    (Sun Dewei)
    Border Guard on Night Sentry Duty
    Border guard on night sentry duty,
    Looks up at the moon, the moon smiles,
    Moon, moon, what are you smiling at?
    It must be a bumper harvest back home!
    (Sergeant Li Zhilin)
    Border Guard and the Stars
    Little stars, little stars,
    You and I have deep feelings,
    Night after night you accompany me,
    Loyally guarding the border.
    (Sergeant Yu Youxin)

### 边防战士之歌
孙德维  李芝林  余又新
1959-09-08
第8版()
专栏：

    边防战士之歌
    边寨炊烟
    池塘鸭鹅叫，
    圈里肥猪闹，
    舍前红花开，
    崖边白羊跑。
    送走山上月，
    唤醒林中鸟，
    天明巡逻归，
    边寨炊烟飘。
    （孙德维）
    边防战士夜放哨
    边防战士夜放哨，
    抬头望月月儿笑，
    月儿月儿你笑什么？
    一定是家乡丰收了！
  （中士　李芝林）
    边防军和星星
    小星星呀小星星，
    我和你呀有深情，
    夜夜你来陪伴我，
    忠心耿耿守边境。
    （中士　余又新）
