Title: Women Representatives from Guinea and Other Countries Leave Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 7th. The Guinean women's delegation, which visited China at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China, left Beijing by plane this morning to return to their country.

Prior to this, the Algerian women's delegation and the Tunisian women's delegation had gone to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for a visit. Mrs. Shirin Fudar, the Singaporean women's representative, has left Beijing to return home, and another representative, Mrs. Lee George, will participate in the National Day celebrations in China.

### 几内亚等国妇女代表离京

1959-09-10
第2版()
专栏：

    几内亚等国妇女代表离京
    新华社7日讯　应中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会邀请来我国访问的几内亚妇女代表团今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    在这以前，阿尔及利亚妇女代表团和突尼斯妇女代表已去越南民主共和国访问。新加坡妇女代表希林·福达尔夫人已离京回国，另一代表李乔治夫人将在我国参加国庆十周年观礼。
