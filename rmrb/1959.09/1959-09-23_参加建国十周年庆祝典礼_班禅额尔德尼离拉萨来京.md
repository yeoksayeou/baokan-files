Title: Panchen Erdeni Leaves Lhasa for Beijing to Attend the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the Nation Celebration

Author:
Date: 1959-09-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 22nd - Panchen Erdeni Qoiji Gyaltsen, Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, set off this afternoon for Beijing to attend the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the Nation celebration. Accompanying him to Beijing for the celebration were Pabala Gelleg Namgyal, Vice Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and Ngabo Ngawang Jigme, Vice Chairman and Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee. More than 600 people from all ethnic groups and sectors of Lhasa gathered in front of the Drepung Monastery in the western suburbs to see them off.

Zhou Renshan, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Tibet Working Committee, accompanied Panchen Erdeni to Beijing.

Zhang Jingwu, Representative of the Central People's Government in Tibet and Secretary of the CPC Tibet Working Committee, and Zhang Guohua, Commander of the People's Liberation Army Tibet Military Region and Vice Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Autonomous Region, were both present at Drepung Monastery to see them off.

Sangpo Tenzin Dhondup, Jangdong Jigme, Lhalu Tsewang Dorje, Ngawang Jinba, Standing Committee Members of the Preparatory Committee for the Autonomous Region, and Panchen Erdeni's relatives, also set off this afternoon for Beijing to attend the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the Nation celebration.

### 参加建国十周年庆祝典礼  班禅额尔德尼离拉萨来京

1959-09-23
第3版()
专栏：

    参加建国十周年庆祝典礼
    班禅额尔德尼离拉萨来京
    新华社拉萨22日电　西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞今天下午启程去北京参加建国十周年庆祝典礼。和他一道去北京参加庆祝典礼的还有自治区筹备委员会副主任委员帕巴拉·卓列朗杰活佛，副主任委员兼秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美。拉萨市各族各界人士六百多人在西郊哲蚌寺前为他们送行。
    中共西藏工委副书记周仁山陪同班禅额尔德尼去北京。
    中央人民政府驻西藏代表、中共西藏工委书记张经武，人民解放军西藏军区司令员、自治区筹备委员会副主任委员张国华都到哲蚌寺前欢送。
    自治区筹备委员会常务委员桑颇·才旺仁增、詹东·计晋美、拉敏·益喜楚臣、纳旺金巴和班禅额尔德尼的亲属，也在今天下午启程去北京参加建国十周年庆祝典礼。
