Title: Albanian Ambassador to China, Pāllivti, Meets with Foreign Minister Chen

Author: 
Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Albania to China, Pāllivti, met with Foreign Minister Chen Yi this morning to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials.

Present at the meeting were Li Huichuan, Acting Director of the Department of Soviet and East European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

### 阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使帕里夫蒂会见陈外长

1959-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使帕里夫蒂会见陈外长
    新华社24日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国新任驻华特命全权大使帕里夫蒂今日上午会见了我外交部长陈毅，商谈递交国书事宜。
    会见时在座的有外交部苏欧司代司长李汇川，礼宾司副司长马振武。
