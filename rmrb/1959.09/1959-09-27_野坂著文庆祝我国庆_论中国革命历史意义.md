Title: Nosaka Publishes Article Celebrating Our National Day, Discussing the Historical Significance of the Chinese Revolution
Author: 
Date: 1959-09-27
Page: 9

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 26th: Sanzo Nosaka, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, published an article entitled "The Chinese Revolution and the Struggle of the Japanese People" in the October issue of the magazine "Zenei" to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. In this article, Nosaka discusses three issues: 1. The world historical significance of the Chinese Revolution; 2. The Chinese Revolution and the Japanese people; 3. The experiences and lessons of the Chinese Revolution.

### 野坂著文庆祝我国庆  论中国革命历史意义

1959-09-27
第9版()
专栏：

    野坂著文庆祝我国庆
    论中国革命历史意义
    新华社26日讯　东京消息：日本共产党中央委员会主席野坂参三在“前卫”杂志10月号上发表题为“中国革命和日本人民的斗争”的文章，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年。野坂在这篇文章中论述了三个问题：一、中国革命的世界历史意义；二、中国革命和日本人民；三、中国革命的经验教训。
