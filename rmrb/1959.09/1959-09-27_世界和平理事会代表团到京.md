Title: World Peace Council Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: A delegation from the World Peace Council arrived in Beijing by plane today.

This delegation, composed of world-renowned peace advocates, was invited by the China Peace Committee to participate in the celebrations for the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

The head of the delegation is John Desmond Bernal, winner of the International Lenin Peace Prize, Executive Chairman of the Presidium of the World Peace Council, and British physicist. He will arrive in Beijing in the coming days. The members of the delegation who arrived in Beijing today are: Mrs. Isabelle Blume, winner of the International Lenin Peace Prize, member of the Presidium of the World Peace Council, and Belgian peace activist; James Gareth Endicott, winner of the International Lenin Peace Prize, member of the Presidium of the World Peace Council, Chairman of the Canadian Peace Congress, and his wife; Mr. Sediati, member of the Presidium of the World Peace Council, Vice-Chairman of the Indonesian Peace Committee; Mrs. Yves Farge, winner of the International Lenin Peace Prize, member of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, member of the Presidium of the French National Peace Council; and Oliverio, winner of the International Lenin Peace Prize, member of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, and Argentine writer. Nikolai Tikhonov, member of the delegation, winner of the International Lenin Peace Prize, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee, and Permanent Committee Member of the World Peace Council, arrived in Beijing on the same plane today, leading a delegation from the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport today included Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman, as well as Liu Changsheng, Chu Tunan, Hu Yuzhi, Ji Chaoding, Qu Tangliang, Liang Sicheng, Zhao Fusan, Wu Maosun, Wang Mingyuan, and others.

Peace activists in Beijing, including Kinkazu Saionji (Japan), Manuel Viturriaga (Chile), and Ker (Sudan), also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

Zheng Senyu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the World Peace Council, accompanied the guests to Beijing on the same plane.

### 世界和平理事会代表团到京

1959-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会代表团到京
    新华社26日讯　世界和平理事会代表团今天乘飞机到达北京。
    这个由世界著名和平人士组成的代表团是应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请来中国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
    代表团团长是列宁国际和平奖金获得者、世界和平理事会主席团执行主席、英国物理学家约翰·德斯豪德·贝尔纳，他将在日内到达北京。今天到达北京的代表团团员有：列宁国际和平奖金获得者、世界和平理事会主席团委员、比利时和平人士伊莎贝丽·布伦姆夫人，列宁国际和平奖金获得者、世界和平理事会主席团委员、加拿大和平大会主席文幼章和他的夫人，世界和平理事会主席团委员、印度尼西亚和平委员会副主席塞第亚狄，列宁国际和平奖金获得者、世界和平理事会常务委员、法国全国和平理事会主席团委员伊夫·法奇夫人，列宁国际和平奖金获得者、世界和平理事会常务委员、阿根廷作家奥利弗。团员、列宁国际和平奖金获得者、世界和平理事会常务委员、苏联保卫和平委员会主席吉洪诺夫另率苏中友协代表团于今天同机到达北京。
    今天到机场欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若、副主席刘宁一，以及刘长胜、楚图南、胡愈之、冀朝鼎、区棠亮、梁思成、赵复三、吴茂荪、王明远等。
    在京和平人士西园寺公一（日本）、万徒勒里（智利）、凯尔（苏丹）等也到机场欢迎。
    世界和平理事会书记处书记郑森禹同机陪同客人到京。
