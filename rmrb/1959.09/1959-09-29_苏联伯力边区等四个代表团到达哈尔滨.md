Title: Soviet Khabarovsk Krai and Four Other Delegations Arrive in Harbin
Author:
Date: 1959-09-29
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 28th -- At the invitation of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Heilongjiang Provincial People's Committee, the Soviet Khabarovsk Krai, Amur Oblast, Primorsky Krai, and Sakhalin Oblast delegations, who are visiting Heilongjiang Province to participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of our country, arrived in Harbin City by train this morning, led by Azhibkov, Secretary of the Khabarovsk Krai Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Skoblikov, Secretary of the Amur Oblast Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Vorobyov, Deputy Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Primorsky Krai Soviet, and Neyodkevich, Member of the Sakhalin Oblast Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and First Secretary of the South Sakhalinsk City Committee.

### 苏联伯力边区等四个代表团到达哈尔滨

1959-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联伯力边区等四个代表团到达哈尔滨
    新华社哈尔滨28日电　应中共黑龙江省委、黑龙江省人民委员会邀请，来黑龙江省参加我国建国十周年庆祝典礼的苏联伯力边区、阿穆尔州、滨海边疆区、库页岛（萨哈林）州四个代表团，分别由苏共伯力边区委员会书记阿日奇勃科夫，苏共阿穆尔州委员会书记斯科勃利科夫，滨海边疆区苏维埃执行委员会副主席沃罗比尧夫，苏共库页岛州委员会委员、南库页岛市委第一书记涅约德克维奇率领，今天上午乘火车到达哈尔滨市。
