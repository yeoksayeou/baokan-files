Title: Labor People's Delegations from North Korea, Czechoslovakia, and Bulgaria Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: A fifteen-member labor people's delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Choi Ki-chul, Vice Chairman of the Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries of Korea and Vice Chairman of the Korean National Peace Committee, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon.

　　The delegation was invited by the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Communist Youth League, the All-China Women's Federation, the All-China Youth Federation, the All-China Students' Federation, and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People’s Republic of China.

　　Li Dequan, Li Jiebo, Chu Tunan, Lao She, Shen Zijiu, Cui Yueli, and Wang Wei, heads of the inviting organizations, went to the railway station to welcome them.

　　Officials from the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China also went to the railway station to greet them.

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: A fifteen-member Czechoslovak labor people's delegation, led by Bedřich Kozelka, Secretary of the Central Council of Czechoslovak Trade Unions, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They were invited by the Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship Association, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Communist Youth League, the All-China Women's Federation, the All-China Youth Federation, the All-China Students' Federation, and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People’s Republic of China.

　　Liu Changsheng, Xia Yan, Xu Guangping, Ou Tangliang, Liang Buting, Hu Qili, Wu Huazhi, and other heads of the inviting organizations went to the airport to welcome them.

　　Ševčík, Commercial Counselor of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, also went to the airport to welcome them.

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: A fifteen member Bulgarian labor people's delegation, led by Gocho Grozev, member of the Central Commitee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and member of the Presidium of the National Assembly, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They were invited by the Sino-Bulgarian Friendship Association, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Communist Youth League, the All-China Women's Federation, the All-China Youth Federation, the All-China Students' Federation, and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People’s Republic of China.

　　Liao Chengzhi, Li Jiebo, Liu Ruilong, Kang Keqing, Liang Buting, Wang Chuanbin, Zhao Feng, and other heads of the inviting organizations went to the airport to welcome them.

　　Panchevski, the Bulgarian Ambassador to China, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 朝、捷、保三国劳动人民代表团到京

1959-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

    朝、捷、保三国劳动人民代表团到京
    新华社27日讯　由朝鲜对外文化联络委员会副委员长、朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会副委员长崔基哲率领的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国劳动人民代表团一行十五人，今天下午乘火车到达北京。
    代表团是应中朝友协、全总、和大、共青团、全国妇联、全国青联、全国学联、对外文协八个团体邀请前来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
    前往火车站欢迎的，有各邀请团体的负责人李德全、李颉伯、楚图南、老舍、沈兹九、崔月犁、王伟。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使馆官员也前往火车站迎接。
    新华社27日讯　由捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会书记贝德里赫·科泽尔卡率领的捷克斯洛伐克劳动人民代表团一行十五人，今天乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中捷友协、全总、和大、共青团、全国妇联、全国青联、全国学联、对外文协邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
    前往机场迎接的有各邀请团体的负责人刘长胜、夏衍、许广平、区棠亮、梁步庭、胡启立、吴化之等。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻中国商务参赞舍伏契克也到机场欢迎。
    新华社27日讯　由保加利亚共产党中央委员会委员、国民议会主席团委员戈乔·格罗节夫率领的保加利亚劳动人民代表团一行十五人，今天乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中保友协、全总、和大、共青团、全国妇联、全国青联、全国学联、对外文协邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
    前往机场迎接的有各邀请团体负责人廖承志、李颉伯、刘瑞龙、康克清、梁步庭、王传斌、赵沨等。
    保加利亚驻华大使潘切夫斯基也到机场欢迎。
