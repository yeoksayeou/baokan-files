Title: Congratulatory Message from Comrade Enrique Rodriguez, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Uruguay and Head of the Delegation of the Communist Party of Uruguay
Author: 
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 6

Dear Comrades and Friends:

It goes without saying with what joy people all over the world exclaim the short sentence: "Today is the tenth anniversary of the birth of People's China!"

It is no exaggeration to say that the joy felt by people of all countries for the achievements made by the Chinese People's regime in just ten years stands in stark contrast to the hatred and fury of certain "politicians" and imperialist military blocs. These people are determined to ignore China's ten years of socialist construction, and even determined to ignore the existence of a powerful country with 650 million people steadily moving towards the future. Just as they did when the Soviet Union—the first glorious announcer of the moment of socialism and communism on the clock of humanity—appeared. Today, the class sentenced to death by history is trying to carry out its "conspiracy of silence" in the face of this glorious achievement. This glorious achievement is hailing its first decade with the enormous power that foretells a bright future.

We will not allow this absurd conspiracy to succeed. Nor will another conspiracy to alienate People's China from the Soviet Union and other socialist countries succeed. The presence here today of the delegation of the Soviet Union, which has made tremendous achievements in communist construction and in the struggle for peaceful coexistence, demonstrates the rock-solid unity of the socialist camp, which, in this world-portending moment, is the guarantee of world peace.

We, the Communists of Uruguay, standing at the forefront of other progressive forces, have waged a great workers' and people's struggle to smash the absurd policy of discrimination against your great friendly country of all peace-loving people, for the great People's China to occupy its place in the United Nations, and for all countries of the world to maintain friendly, diplomatic, and trade relations with it; we will continue this struggle, convinced that in doing so, we embody the will of the vast majority of our people and better defend the sacred cause of world peace.

Our small Uruguay, a member of the great family of Latin American peoples, currently faces a dual great historical task: to oppose the aggressive policies of the United States in all forms of oppression, and to pave the way for land reform. These two points are indispensable preconditions for avoiding national bankruptcy and the deepening poverty of the working masses in urban and rural areas.

The achievements of rural China that we are now witnessing and the recent example of land reform already implemented in our close brother Cuba correctly indicate that the aforementioned two tasks are not only necessary today, but also possible. We will continue to work tirelessly to advance our beloved country on the path of progress, peace, and national independence.

We wholeheartedly congratulate the heroic Chinese people on their achievements and wish them even greater achievements in their glorious socialist construction.

Long live world peace!

Long live the friendship between People's China and Uruguay! (Attached picture)

### 乌拉圭共产党中央委员会书记处书记、乌拉圭共产党代表团团长  恩里克·罗德里格斯同志的贺词

1959-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

    乌拉圭共产党中央委员会书记处书记、乌拉圭共产党代表团团长
    恩里克·罗德里格斯同志的贺词亲爱的同志们和朋友们：
    用不着说全世界各国人民是以何等喜悦的心情呼出“今天是人民中国诞生十周年！”这样短短几个字的。
    如果我们说，各国人民为中国人民政权在仅仅十年内所取得的成就感到的喜悦正和某些
  “政治家”和帝国主义军事集团的仇恨和狂怒形成鲜明的对照，这并不是夸张。这些人决心无视中国的十年社会主义建设，甚至决心无视拥有稳步地向着未来前进的六亿五千万人民的强大国家的存在。正如同当苏联——在人类的时钟上社会主义和共产主义时刻到来的第一个光荣的宣告者——出现时他们所做的那样。今天被历史宣判了死刑的阶级在这辉煌的成就面前力图施展其“沉默的阴谋”。这种辉煌的成就正用预示着前程万里的巨大力量来欢呼它自己初生的十年。
    我们不会让这种荒谬的阴谋得逞。另一种企图使人民中国和苏联及其他社会主义国家对立的阴谋也决不会得逞。在共产主义建设和为争取和平共处的斗争中取得巨大成就的苏联的代表团今天在这儿出席说明了社会主义阵营磐石般的团结，在这世界预兆性的时刻，这团结是维护世界和平的保证。
    我们乌拉圭共产党人，站在其他进步力量的前列，为了粉碎对你们这个伟大的所有和平人民的友好国家的荒谬的歧视政策，为了巨大的人民中国在联合国占有它的地位，为了世界各国和它保持友好的、外交的、贸易的关系，进行了一个伟大的工人和人民的斗争；我们将继续进行这个斗争，深信这样做，我们体现了我国绝大多数人民的意志并且更好地保卫世界和平的神圣事业。
    我们不大的乌拉圭，这个拉丁美洲各国人民大家庭的成员目前面临着一个双重的伟大的历史任务：反对美国采取各种压迫形式的侵略政策，并为土地改革打开道路。这两点是为了避免民族破产和城乡劳动群众日益加深的贫困所不可缺少的前提。
    我们现在亲眼看到的中国农村的成就和在我们亲密的兄弟的古巴所已经实行的土地改革的近例正确的指明前述两个任务在今天来说不但是必需的，而且是可能的。我们将继续不懈地努力使我们亲爱的国家在进步、和平和民族独立的道路上前进。
    我们衷心地祝贺英勇的中国人民所取得的成就，并祝他们在辉煌的社会主义建设中取得更大的成就。
    世界和平万岁！
    人民中国和乌拉圭之间友谊万岁！（附图片）
