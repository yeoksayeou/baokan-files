Title: Vice-Chairman Soong Ching Ling Hosts a Banquet for President Ho Chi Minh
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: Vice-Chairman Soong Ching Ling hosted a banquet at her home this evening in honor of President Ho Chi Minh. Vice-Premier Vo Nguyen Giap was also invited to attend the banquet.

Vice-Chairman Dong Biwu, Vice-Chairman Lin Boqu, and Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council Qi Yanming, among others, were also present at the banquet. (Attached photo)

The picture shows Vice-Chairman Soong Ching Ling and President Ho Chi Minh (left) having a cordial conversation before the banquet.

### 宋庆龄副主席宴请胡志明主席

1959-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄副主席宴请胡志明主席
    新华社27日讯　宋庆龄副主席今晚在她的家里设宴招待胡志明主席。武元甲副总理也应邀出席宴会。
    宴会上作陪的有董必武副主席，林伯渠副委员长，以及国务院副秘书长齐燕铭等人。（附图片）
    图为在宴会前宋庆龄副主席和胡志明主席（左）亲切交谈。
