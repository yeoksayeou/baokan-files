Title: President of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, Pritt, Invited to Beijing to Attend China's National Day Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1959-09-08
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Dan N. Pritt, head of the delegation of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, President of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, and a renowned peace activist, and his wife, arrived in Beijing this afternoon from the Soviet Union on a Tu-104 aircraft at the invitation of the Chinese Political Science and Law Association to attend the 10th Anniversary National Day celebrations in China. Wu Defeng, Vice President and First Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Political Science and Law Association, Yao Zhongming, Secretary, Cao Mengjun, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, and heads of organizations such as the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace were at the airport to greet them.

### 国际民主法律工作者协会主席  普里特应邀到京参加我国庆典礼

1959-09-08
第6版()
专栏：

    国际民主法律工作者协会主席
    普里特应邀到京参加我国庆典礼
    新华社7日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国参加国庆十周年观礼的国际民主法律工作者协会代表团团长、国际民主法律工作者协会主席、著名和平人士丹·诺·普里特和他的夫人，今天下午乘图—104飞机从苏联到达北京。前往机场迎接的有中国政治法律学会副会长兼书记处第一书记吴德峰、书记姚仲明，中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会主席团委员曹孟君和中国人民保卫世界和平委员会等团体的负责人。
