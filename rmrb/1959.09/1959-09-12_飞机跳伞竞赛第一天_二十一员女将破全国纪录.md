Title: First Day of Airplane Parachuting Competition: Twenty-One Female Athletes Break National Record
Author:
Date: 1959-09-12
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 11th: Great news has arrived from the China People's Aviation Club in Liangxiang, Beijing: During the first day of competition at the First National Games airplane parachuting event today, twenty-one female athletes broke the 1958 national record of 13.02 meters for the women's daytime 600-meter individual precision parachute jump. The best performance was by 19-year-old Mu Guangqin from the Heilongjiang team, with a score of 2.795 meters (the average distance from the center of the ground circle in two jumps).

A total of sixty-eight female athletes from sixteen teams participated in today's competition. Among the twenty-one athletes who broke the national record held by Cui Xiuying, seventeen were novices who had only recently started participating in airplane parachuting. The champion, Mu Guangqin, is a student at the Harbin Turbine Manufacturing School. She only first experienced the thrill of airplane parachuting on January 19th of this year. Yet today, she defeated all the veterans, including He Jianhua and Geng Guifang. Her distance from the center of the circle on her first jump today was 5.26 meters, and even closer on her second, at 0.33 meters.

The other seven athletes who achieved excellent rankings in today's competition were: Li Shuhua (PLA), Li Guizhen (PLA), Bu Guilan (Shanxi), Zhang Minlan (Liaoning), Li Cuiyun (Heilongjiang), Yan Baozhen (Henan), and Geng Guifang (PLA).

The other thirteen athletes who broke the 1958 national record today were: Wang Suzhen (Sichuan), Wei Xiuling (Beijing), Zhao Chengying (Sichuan), Liang Yi (Jiangsu), Yang Lianzhen (Shanxi), Li Huiping (Hebei), He Jianhua (PLA), Wang Naiting (Beijing), Hou Yide (Jiangxi), Gu Liangzhen (Heilongjiang), Guo Xin'e (Shaanxi), Zhao Huihua (Heilongjiang), and Hua Shaolin (Beijing).

The men's 600-meter individual precision parachute jump will be held on September 12th.

### 飞机跳伞竞赛第一天  二十一员女将破全国纪录

1959-09-12
第5版()
专栏：体育常识

    飞机跳伞竞赛第一天
    二十一员女将破全国纪录
    新华社11日讯　从北京良乡中国人民航空俱乐部传出了捷报：第一届全国运动会飞机跳伞竞赛今天在举行第一天的比赛时，就有二十一名女运动员打破了1958年女子日间六百米单人定点跳伞十三点○二米的全国纪录，其中成绩最好的是黑龙江队十九岁的牟广琴，成绩是二点七九五米（两次试跳距地面圆心的平均距离）。
    参加今天这项比赛的共有十六个代表队的六十八名女运动员。在二十一名打破崔秀英保持的全国纪录的运动员中，有十七人是不久前才开始参加飞机跳伞运动的新手。获得冠军的牟广琴是哈尔滨汽轮机制造学校的学生，她今年1月19日才开始第一次尝试飞机跳伞的滋味。但今天就战胜了赫建华、耿桂芳等所有的老手。她今天第一次试跳距圆心的距离是五点二六米，第二次的更近，是零点三三米。
    在今天的比赛中获得优胜名次的其他七名选手是：李淑花（解放军）、李桂珍（解放军）、卜桂兰（山西）、张敏兰（辽宁）、李翠云（黑龙江）、阎宝珍（河南）、耿桂芳（解放军）。
    今天打破1958年全国纪录的另外十三名选手是：王素珍（四川），魏秀玲（北京）、赵成英（四川）、梁漪（江苏）、杨莲珍（山西）、李惠平（河北）、赫建华（解放军）、王乃亭（北京）、侯仪德（江西）、顾良珍（黑龙江）、郭新娥（陕西）、赵慧华（黑龙江）、华绍林（北京）。
    飞机跳伞竞赛12日将举行的项目是男子六百米单人定点跳伞。
