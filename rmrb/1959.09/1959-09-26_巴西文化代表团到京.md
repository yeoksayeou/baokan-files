Title: Brazilian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th. The Brazilian Cultural Delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China and participate in the celebrations for the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The members of the delegation are Mr. and Mrs. Etmundo Vasconcelos, Mr. and Mrs. Vosvaldo Gavarganti, and Mr. and Mrs. Jose Sheraudo Vieira.

### 巴西文化代表团到京

1959-09-26
第2版()
专栏：

    巴西文化代表团到京
    新华社25日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的巴西文化代表团，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团成员有埃特蒙多·瓦斯冈塞约斯夫妇、沃斯伐尔多·加瓦尔冈蒂夫妇和约塞·谢劳多·维埃依拉夫妇。
