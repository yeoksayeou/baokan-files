Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi Receive Guests from Three Latin American Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-09-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi together received guests from three Latin American countries this afternoon who are currently visiting our country. Among these guests were five Venezuelan individuals: Fabricio Ojeda, member of the National Leadership Committee of the Venezuelan "Democratic Republican Union" and member of Congress, and his wife; Antonio Jose Estevez, renowned conductor and composer of the Venezuelan Symphony Orchestra; Professor Santiago Betancourt Infante, Secretary of the Democratic Action Party of Carabobo State; Hector Rodriguez Bauza, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Venezuelan Communist Party, Chairman of the Venezuelan Communist Youth League, and member of Congress. Also present were the Cuban youth delegation headed by Baldomero A. Rios, and the Argentine student representative Fermin Luis Melendez.

Present at the reception were Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Shen Jian, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Chao, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, and others. ### 周恩来总理和陈毅副总理接见三个拉丁美洲国家的来宾

1959-09-12
第3版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理和陈毅副总理
    接见三个拉丁美洲国家的来宾
    新华社11日讯　周恩来总理和陈毅副总理今天下午一起接见了正在我国访问的三个拉丁美洲国家的来宾。这些来宾中有五位委内瑞拉人士：委内瑞拉“共和民主联盟”全国领导委员会委员、国会议员法夫里西奥·奥赫达和他的夫人，委内瑞拉交响乐队功勋指挥、作曲家安东尼奥·何塞·埃斯特维斯，卡拉博博州民主行动党书记圣地亚哥·贝坦科尔特·因方特教授，委内瑞拉共产党中央政治局委员、委内瑞拉共青团主席、国会议员埃克托·罗德里格斯·包萨。有以巴尔多麦罗·阿·里欧斯为首的古巴青年代表团，以及阿根廷学生代表费尔明·路易士·梅连德斯。
    接见时在座的，有中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之、外交部美澳司司长申健，中华全国青年联合会副主席张超等。（附图片）
