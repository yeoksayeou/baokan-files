Title: More Than 800 Overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macau Compatriots Arrive in Beijing to Participate in the National Day Celebrations

Author:
Date: 1959-09-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th. More than 870 Overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macau compatriots participating in the National Day celebrations have all arrived in Beijing. The returning Overseas Chinese come from more than 40 countries and regions across Asia, Europe, Africa, the Americas, and Australia, including Indonesia, Cambodia, India, Japan, the Netherlands, New Zealand, and faraway Cuba.

These Overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macau compatriots have received warm reception and welcome from the people of the motherland. The newly completed Overseas Chinese Mansion held a grand opening ceremony yesterday, and the Overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macau compatriots who just returned to Beijing became its first guests. Relevant reception units also organized Overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macau compatriots who arrived earlier to visit many factories, enterprises, and buildings in South China, Central South China, Southwest China, and Northeast China, and to tour scenic spots and historical sites.

He Xiangning, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, recently met with a group of Overseas Chinese women who arrived in Beijing earlier.

### 参加祖国建国十周年盛典  华侨和港澳同胞八百多人到京

1959-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    参加祖国建国十周年盛典
    华侨和港澳同胞八百多人到京
    新华社26日讯　参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的华侨和港澳同胞八百七十多人，已全部到达北京。回国的华侨来自亚洲、欧洲、非洲、美洲、澳洲五大洲的印度尼西亚、柬埔寨、印度、日本、荷兰、新西兰，以及遥远的古巴等四十多个侨居国家和地区。
    这些侨胞和港澳同胞受到祖国人民的热情接待和欢迎。新近落成的华侨大厦已在昨天隆重举行了落成典礼，刚刚回到北京的华侨和港澳同胞成了它的第一批客人。有关接待单位还组织先期到达的侨胞和港澳同胞在华南、中南、西南、东北等地参观了许多工厂、企业、建筑物，并且游览了名胜古迹。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长何香凝最近接见了一部分先期到达北京的华侨妇女。
