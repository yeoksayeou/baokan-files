Title: Three Brazilian Guests Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 14th - At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries to visit China and attend the National Day celebrations, members of the Brazilian Cultural Friendship Delegation: playwright Pedro Bloch and his wife, and Luci Bloch, president of the magazine "Peneira," a total of three people, arrived in Beijing this afternoon.

### 三位巴西客人到京

1959-09-15
第5版()
专栏：

    三位巴西客人到京
    新华社14日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问并参加国庆观礼的巴西文化友好代表团部分团员：剧作家彼得罗·波罗奇夫妇和“珠筛”杂志社社长路茜·波罗奇共三人，今天下午到达北京。
