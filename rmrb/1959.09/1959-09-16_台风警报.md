Title: Typhoon Alert
Author:
Date: 1959-09-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th: The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a typhoon warning at 7:00 PM on the 15th:

    The typhoon in the Pacific Ocean was centered approximately 400 kilometers east of Taiwan at 2:00 PM today, located at 24.1 degrees North latitude and 125.9 degrees East longitude, with maximum winds near the center exceeding force 12. The typhoon center is currently moving northwest at a speed of 9 to 13 knots per hour. In the next 24 hours, the typhoon will gradually approach the Zhejiang coast and turn in a north-by-west direction. In the next 48 hours, the typhoon will shift from the northwestern East China Sea to a north-by-east direction, moving towards the Sea of Japan. It is expected that in the next 48 hours, the Taiwan Strait and the northern Fujian coast will experience winds of force 7 to 9, the southern Yellow Sea and the East China Sea will experience winds of force 10 to 12, the Zhejiang coast, southern Jiangsu coast, and central Yellow Sea will experience winds of force 8 to 11, the central Jiangsu coast, southern Jiangsu, and eastern Zhejiang will experience winds of force 6 to 8, and eastern Zhejiang, central Jiangsu, and southern Jiangsu will experience heavy to torrential rainfall.

### 台风警报

1959-09-16
第4版()
专栏：

    台风警报
    新华社15日讯　中央气象科学研究所15日下午7时发布台风警报：
    太平洋上的台风，今天下午二时中心位置在台湾以东四百公里左右的洋面上，就是北纬二十四点一度、东经一百二十五点九度的地方，中心附近最大风力在十二级以上。目前台风中心正以每小时九至十三浬的速度向西北方向移动。未来二十四小时内，台风将逐渐接近浙江沿海，并转向偏北方向。未来四十八小时内，台风将从东海西北部转为北偏东方向，移向日本海。预计未来四十八小时内，台湾海峡、福建北部沿海，风力将有七至九级，黄海南部、东海，风力有十级至十二级，浙江沿海、苏南沿海、黄海中部风力有八级至十一级，江苏中部沿海、江苏南部、浙江东部风力有六级至八级，浙江东部、江苏中部、南部将有大雨至暴雨。
