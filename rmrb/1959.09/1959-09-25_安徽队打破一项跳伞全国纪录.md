Title: Anhui Team Breaks National Skydiving Record
Author:
Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, 24th dispatch: In the airplane skydiving performance held in Liangxiang on the 23rd, Anhui team's Guan Ziheng, Shen Shide, and Zou Peian broke the national record of 4.8 meters, set by PLA athletes Liu Haijin, Han Jintang, and Zhang Deyong in Kaifeng in February this year, with a score of 3.44 meters from the center in the men's 1000-meter daytime team combined skydiving.

### 安徽队打破一项跳伞全国纪录

1959-09-25
第8版()
专栏：

    安徽队打破一项跳伞全国纪录
    新华社24日讯　23日在良乡举行的飞机跳伞表演赛中，安徽队的管自恒、沈士德、邹培安三人在男子一千米日间集体综合跳伞中，以距圆心三米四四的成绩，打破了解放军运动员刘海金、韩金堂、张德永今年2月在开封创造的四米八的全国纪录。
