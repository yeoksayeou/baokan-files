Title: Handball Finals Begin Today
Author:
Date: 1959-09-15
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, September 14th. The fierce battles of the First National Games handball finals will fully unfold in Beijing starting from the 15th. A total of sixteen men's and women's teams are participating in the finals. The men's group consists of eight teams: the People's Liberation Army, Beijing, Guangdong, Anhui, Hubei, Shaanxi, Yunnan, and Heilongjiang. The women's group consists of eight teams: Beijing, Guangdong, Hubei, Yunnan, Guizhou, Hebei, Liaoning, and Shanxi.

### 手球决赛今天开始

1959-09-15
第7版()
专栏：

    手球决赛今天开始
    新华社14日讯　第一届全国运动会手球决赛的激战从15日起在北京全面展开。参加决赛的共有十六个男、女代表队。男子组是解放军、北京、广东、安徽、湖北、陕西、云南、黑龙江八队。女子组是北京、广东、湖北、云南、贵州、河北、辽宁、山西八队。
