Title: On Drive
Author: Tian Yu, Chen Xiao
Date: 1959-09-09
Page: 8

    Creating everything through labor is glorious.
    And what can add even more brilliance to labor is drive.
                 *
    Drive is a shining giant hand! Steel, grain, happiness, harvest, the honor of labor, proud victory, colorful fantasies, a bright future, and everything most precious in the world, all are in this shining hand.
                    *
    Drive is also a raging fire! Whether it is thunder, lightning, wind, rain, floods and fierce beasts, mountain demons and earth spirits, the five plagues and ten famines, a thousand hardships and ten thousand difficulties, the stubborn rocks of right-leaning conservatism, the dregs of negativity and fear of difficulty... all will be burned by this fire!
                              *
    A person without drive is like a river without water; like a mountain without mineral deposits; like a field without crops.
    A person without drive is like a zither without strings; like a knife without steel; like a gun without ammunition.

### 干劲篇
田雨　陈宵
1959-09-09
第8版()
专栏：

    干劲篇
    田雨　陈宵
    创造一切的劳动，是光荣的。
    而能给劳动增加更大光彩的，却是干劲。
                 ＊
    干劲是一支光辉的巨手！钢铁，粮食，幸福，丰收，劳动的荣誉，骄傲的胜利，彩色的幻想，灿烂的未来，以及世界上一切最珍贵的东西，统统在这支光辉的手里。
                    ＊
    干劲又是一团熊熊的烈火！不论是雷，是电，是风，是雨，是洪水猛兽，是山精地魃，是五灾十荒，是千艰万难，是右倾保守的顽石，是消极畏难的渣滓，……都将被这团烈火烧毁！
                              ＊
    没有干劲的人，就像一条河没有水；就像一座山没有矿藏；就像一块地没有庄稼。
    没有干劲的人，就像一把琴没有弦；就像一口刀没有钢；就像一支枪没有弹药。
