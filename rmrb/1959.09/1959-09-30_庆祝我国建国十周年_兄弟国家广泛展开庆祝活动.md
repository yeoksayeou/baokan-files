Title: Brotherly Countries Widely Launch Celebratory Activities to Celebrate the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of Our Country
Author:
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Brotherly countries continue to send news of celebrating the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

On the evening of the 29th, Lubsan, Chairman of the Mongolia-China Friendship Association, held a reception to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, and entertained Chinese employees and experts in Mongolia. Mongolian party and government leaders Tumur-Ochir, Baljinnyam, Molomjamts, and Jagvaral attended the reception. Xie Fusheng, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, as well as representatives of Chinese employees and experts, a total of more than 300 people, attended the reception.

Lubsan spoke at the reception, congratulating our country on the 10th anniversary of its founding. He said that under the wise leadership of the great Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, the Chinese people have built their motherland into a great power with modern industry, agriculture, and prosperous science and culture in a very short period of time. The great achievements of the Chinese people have strengthened and consolidated the strength of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union and made great contributions to world peace.

On the evening of the 28th, the Bulgarian Committee for Foreign Friendship and Cultural Relations, the Film Bureau, and the Film Workers Association jointly held a Chinese film reception in Sofia to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of our country. The Secretary-General of the Bulgarian Film Workers Association spoke at the meeting, introducing the great development of the Chinese film industry in the past ten years. At the reception, the Chinese color feature film "The Daughter of the Party" was screened.

On the same day, Sofia cinemas also screened Chinese feature films such as "Nurse's Diary" and "Song Jingshi."

In Albania, in recent days, agricultural production cooperatives named "Mao Zedong" and "Albania-China Friendship" in various places have successively held celebrations to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of our country. The members of these cooperatives, like welcoming their own festivals, celebrated the Chinese people's festival with ethnic-style dinners and ethnic songs and dances.

### 庆祝我国建国十周年  兄弟国家广泛展开庆祝活动

1959-09-30
第3版()
专栏：

    庆祝我国建国十周年
    兄弟国家广泛展开庆祝活动
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：兄弟国家继续传来欢庆中华人民共和国成立十周年的消息。
    29日晚，蒙中友好协会主席鲁布桑为庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年举行招待会，招待在蒙古的中国员工和专家。出席招待会的有蒙古党政负责人图穆尔—奥奇尔、巴尔吉尼雅姆、莫洛姆扎木茨、扎格瓦尔拉等人。中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生以及中国员工、专家代表共三百多人出席了招待会。
    鲁布桑在招待会上讲了话，祝贺我国建国十周年。他说，中国人民在伟大的中国共产党和毛泽东主席英明领导下，在很短时期内把自己的祖国建设成有现代化工业、农业和繁荣发展的科学文化的伟大强国了。中国人民的伟大成就加强和巩固了以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的力量，对世界和平作出很大贡献。
    保加利亚对外友好和文化联络委员会、电影局和电影工作者协会28日晚在索非亚联合举行中国电影招待会，庆祝我国建国十周年。保加利亚电影工作者协会总书记在会上讲话，介绍中国电影事业十年来的巨大发展。在招待会上，放映了中国五彩故事片“党的女儿”。
    同日，索非亚各电影院还上映了“护士日记”、“宋景诗”等中国故事片。
    在阿尔巴尼亚，这几天来，各地以“毛泽东”和“阿中友好”命名的农业生产合作社，都先后分别举行了庆祝大会，庆祝我国建国十周年。这些合作社社员们像迎接自己的节日一样，以民族形式的聚餐和民族歌舞来欢庆中国人民的节日。
