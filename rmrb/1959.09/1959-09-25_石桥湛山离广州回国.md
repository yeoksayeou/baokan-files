Title: Ishibashi Tanzan Departs Guangzhou to Return Home

Author:

Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 24th: Former Japanese Prime Minister Ishibashi Tanzan, his wife, and his entourage left Guangzhou by train this morning to return home. Sun Pinghua, Director of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, accompanied them to Shenzhen.

Those who went to the station to see them off included Zhu Guang, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province and Mayor of Guangzhou; Sun Leyi, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou; Du Guoxiang, Deputy to the National People's Congress and Director of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Luo Peiyuan, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the CPPCC; as well as representatives to the People's Congress of Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, members of the CPPCC, and directors of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs in Guangzhou.

### 石桥湛山离广州回国

1959-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    石桥湛山离广州回国
    新华社广州24日电　日本前首相石桥湛山和夫人以及他的随行人员，今天早晨乘火车离开广州回国。中国人民外交学会理事孙平化陪送他们到深圳。
    前往车站送行的有广东省副省长兼广州市市长朱光，广州市副市长孙乐宜，全国人民代表大会代表、中国人民外交学会理事杜国庠，政协广州市委员会副主席罗培元以及广东省、广州市人民代表、政协委员和中国人民外交学会在广州的理事等。
