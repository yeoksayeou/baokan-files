Title: Ishibashi Tanzan Departs Beijing for Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1959-09-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: Former Japanese Prime Minister Ishibashi Tanzan, his wife, and his entourage, concluded their two-week visit to Beijing and departed Beijing by special plane this morning, heading to Guangzhou on their way home.

Those seeing them off at the airport included Guo Moruo, Li Dequan, Cai Tingkai, Xu Guangping, Hu Yuzhi, Wang Kunlun, Hou Debang, Liu Xiyuan, Yang Hansheng, Xiao Fangzhou, Xie Nanguang, Chen Yu, and Ding Tuo.

Japanese peace activists in Beijing, Kinkazu Saionji and his wife, and Mrs. Kanzo Uchiyama, also saw them off at the airport today.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 23rd: Former Japanese Prime Minister Ishibashi Tanzan and his wife arrived in Guangzhou this afternoon by special plane from Beijing. Ishibashi Tanzan's entourage also arrived on the same plane.

In the evening, Zhu Guang, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, hosted a banquet for the guests. At the meeting, Zhu Guang congratulated Mr. Ishibashi Tanzan on the success of his visit to China. In his speech, Ishibashi Tanzan stated that upon returning to Japan, he would do his utmost to overcome various difficulties and work to promote the friendship between the people of China and Japan.

### 石桥湛山离京抵广州

1959-09-24
第6版()
专栏：

    石桥湛山离京抵广州
    新华社23日讯　日本前首相石桥湛山和夫人及其随行人员，结束了在北京两星期的访问，今天上午乘专机离开北京取道广州回国。
    到机场送行的，有郭沫若、李德全、蔡廷锴、许广平、胡愈之、王昆仑、侯德榜、刘西元、阳翰笙、萧方洲、谢南光、陈宇、丁拓等。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一夫妇和内山完造夫人今天也到机场送行。
    新华社广州23日电　日本前首相石桥湛山和他的夫人今天下午由北京乘专机到达广州。同机前来的还有石桥湛山的随行人员。
    晚上，广东省副省长朱光设宴招待客人。朱光在会上祝贺石桥湛山先生访问中国成功。石桥湛山在讲话中表示回到日本以后，将尽力克服种种困难，为促进中日两国人民的友谊而努力。
