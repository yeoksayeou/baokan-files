Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into Waters off My Fujian's Baiquan and Dongyin Islands; I Issue 68th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1959-09-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th September. At various times between 10:39 and 15:42 on September 19th, one U.S. warship intruded into the waters off the Baiquan and Dongyin Islands in Fujian Province. With regard to this military provocation by the U.S. warship, a spokesman for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 68th serious warning.

### 美舰侵入我福建白犬东引地区海域  我提出第68次严重警告

1959-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    美舰侵入我福建白犬东引地区海域
    我提出第68次严重警告
    新华社19日讯　9月19日十时三十九分至十五时四十二分，美国军舰一艘侵入我国福建省白犬、东引地区海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第六十八次严重警告。
