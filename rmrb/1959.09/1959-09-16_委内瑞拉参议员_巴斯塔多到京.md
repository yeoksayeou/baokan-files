Title: Venezuelan Senator Bastardo Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-09-16
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 15th: José Luis Salcedo Bastardo, President of Santa Maria University of Venezuela and Senator, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit our country and participate in the National Day celebrations.

### 委内瑞拉参议员  巴斯塔多到京

1959-09-16
第7版()
专栏：

    委内瑞拉参议员
    巴斯塔多到京
    新华社15日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问并参加国庆观礼的委内瑞拉圣玛丽亚大学校长、参议员何塞·路易斯·萨尔塞多·巴斯塔多，在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
