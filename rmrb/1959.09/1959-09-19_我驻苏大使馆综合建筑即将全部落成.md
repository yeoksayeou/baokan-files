Author: 
Date: 1959-09-19
Page: 5

Our Embassy Complex in the Soviet Union Nears Completion

    A large complex is nearing completion in the southwestern district of Moscow, the capital of the Soviet Union. This complex is the new site of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Soviet Union. To celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of our country, Soviet workers are determined to complete all construction work before October 1st. (Attached photo)
    The above picture shows the exterior of our newly built embassy.
                  TASS telephoto (Xinhua News Agency)

### 我驻苏大使馆综合建筑即将全部落成

1959-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻苏大使馆综合建筑即将全部落成
    在苏联的首都——莫斯科西南区，一座庞大的综合建筑即将落成。这座综合建筑是中华人民共和国驻苏联大使馆的新馆址。苏联工人们为了庆祝我国建国十周年，决心在10月1日以前结束全部建筑工程。（附图片）
    上图是新建的我国大使馆外貌。
                  塔斯社传真照片（新华社发）
