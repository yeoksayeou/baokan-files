Title: Czechoslovakian Embassy Commercial Section in China Holds Brno Trade Fair Sample Exhibition in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-09-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 3rd: In view of the upcoming opening of the first Brno International Trade Fair in Czechoslovakia, the Commercial Section of the Czechoslovakian Embassy in China held an opening exhibition of sample products at the Sample Exhibition Hall in Beijing on the morning of September 3rd. Those present included responsible persons and representatives from the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Trade, relevant Chinese import and export corporations, and the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

### 捷驻华使馆商务处  在京举办布尔诺博览会样品展览

1959-09-04
第5版()
专栏：

    捷驻华使馆商务处
    在京举办布尔诺博览会样品展览
    据新华社3日讯　鉴于捷克斯洛伐克举办的第一届布尔诺国际博览会即将开幕，捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使馆商务处9月3日上午在北京的样品展览所举行样本展览会开幕展览。到场的有中国对外贸易部、中国有关的进出口公司和中国国际贸易促进委员会的负责人和代表等。
