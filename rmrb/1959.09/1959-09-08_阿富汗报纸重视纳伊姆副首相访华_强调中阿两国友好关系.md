Title: Afghan Newspapers Value Deputy Prime Minister Naim's Visit to China, Emphasizing Friendly Relations Between China and Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1959-09-08
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, September 6th: Afghan newspapers are paying close attention to the visit of Afghan Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Mohammed Naim to the People's Republic of China. Many newspapers pointed out that the distinguished Afghan national leader received a warm welcome in China. Newspapers also emphasized the importance of establishing contact and mutual visits between national leaders.

    "Islah" (Reform) newspaper pointed out in an editorial on September 6th regarding Naim's visit to China that China and Afghanistan have maintained good relations in recent years. The newspaper also mentioned that Afghanistan has consistently advocated for the restoration of China's legitimate seat in the United Nations.

    The newspaper also pointed out that the two countries have maintained contact for many centuries. The newspaper wrote that Afghanistan was one of the first countries to recognize the new Chinese government after the founding of the People's Republic of China.

    The newspaper gave a positive evaluation of the mutual visits of Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai and Afghan Prime Minister Mohammed Daoud in 1957 and hoped that the Afghan Foreign Minister's current visit to China will be a new step in strengthening the existing good relations between the two countries. The article also pointed out that cultural ties between the two countries are also expanding.

### 阿富汗报纸重视纳伊姆副首相访华  强调中阿两国友好关系

1959-09-08
第5版()
专栏：

    阿富汗报纸重视纳伊姆副首相访华
    强调中阿两国友好关系
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社喀布尔6日讯：阿富汗报纸十分重视阿富汗副首相兼外交大臣穆罕默德·纳伊姆对中华人民共和国的访问。许多报纸指出，这位著名的阿富汗国家领导人在中国受到了热情的接待。报纸还强调指出国家领导人之间建立接触和互相访问的重要意义。
    “革新报”6日在一篇关于纳伊姆访问中国的社论中指出，近几年来中国和阿富汗两国间一直保持着良好的关系。报纸还提到，阿富汗一贯主张恢复中国在联合国中的合法地位。
    报纸还指出，两国之间许多世纪以来一直保持着联系。报纸写道，在中华人民共和国成立以后，阿富汗是最先承认中国新政权的国家之一。
    报纸对中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来和阿富汗首相穆罕默德·达乌德在1957年的相互访问给予良好的评价，并且希望阿富汗外交大臣这次访问中国将成为加强两国间现有的良好关系的新步骤。文章中还指出，两国文化联系也正在不断扩大。
