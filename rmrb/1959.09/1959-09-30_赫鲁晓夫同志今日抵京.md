Title: Comrade Khrushchev Arrives in Beijing Today
Author:
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Comrade N.S. Khrushchev, head of the Soviet Party and Government Delegation and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, who is invited to participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, will arrive in Beijing on September 30th by special plane from Moscow.

### 赫鲁晓夫同志今日抵京

1959-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫同志今日抵京
    新华社29日讯　应邀参加中华人民共和国建国十周年庆祝典礼的苏联党政代表团团长、苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联部长会议主席尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫同志，将于9月30日乘专机自莫斯科飞抵北京。
