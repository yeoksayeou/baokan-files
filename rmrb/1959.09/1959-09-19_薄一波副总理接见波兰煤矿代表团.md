Title: Vice Premier Bo Yibo Receives Polish Coal Mine Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-09-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. Vice Premier Bo Yibo received the Polish Coal Mine Delegation headed by Jan Mitrega, Minister of Coal Mining and Power Industry of Poland, this morning.

Present at the reception were Zhang Linzhi, Minister of the Coal Industry; Gao Tieying, Deputy Director of the Technology Department; and Fularato, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, and Szymanski, Commercial Counselor.

### 薄一波副总理接见波兰煤矿代表团

1959-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    薄一波副总理
    接见波兰煤矿代表团
    新华社18日讯　薄一波副总理今天上午接见了以波兰采煤和动力工业部部长扬·米特伦加为首的波兰煤矿代表团。
    接见时在座的，有煤炭工业部部长张霖之，技术司副司长高铁英，以及波兰驻华大使馆临时代办傅拉托、商务参赞席曼斯基。
