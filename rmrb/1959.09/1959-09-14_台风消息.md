Title: Typhoon News
Author:
Date: 1959-09-14
Page: 5

　　Typhoon News
　　Xinhua News Agency, September 13th—The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute released a typhoon bulletin at 7:00 PM on the 13th:
　　A typhoon has appeared in the Pacific Ocean. At 4:00 PM today, the center was located in the ocean about 1,300 kilometers southeast of Taiwan, around 17.5 degrees north latitude and 133.7 degrees east longitude. In the future, the typhoon center will move in a northwesterly direction at a speed of 14 to 17 knots per hour. In the next 48 hours, the wind force in the eastern part of the East China Sea will increase to level 8 to 12, and the wind force in the Taiwan Strait, the western part of the East China Sea, and the southeastern part of the Yellow Sea will increase to level 6 to 8.

### 台风消息

1959-09-14
第5版()
专栏：

    台风消息
    新华社13日讯　中央气象科学研究所13日下午七时发布台风消息：
    太平洋上有台风出现，今天下午四时中心位置在台湾东南方一千三百公里左右的洋面上，就是北纬十七点五度、东经一百三十三点七度附近。未来台风中心将以每小时十四至十七浬的速度向西北方向移动。未来四十八小时内，东海东部风力将加大到八级至十二级，台湾海峡、东海西部、黄海东南部的风力将加大到六级至八级。
