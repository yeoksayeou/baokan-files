Title: Germany Holds "Chinese Film Week"
Author:
Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 23rd: A Chinese film delegation arrived in Berlin today to participate in the "Chinese Film Week" held by the German Democratic Republic to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of our country. This film week will begin on September 25th.

The delegation, led by Zhang Wang, deputy director of the Changchun Film Studio, also includes delegation members Zhao Dan and Yu Yanfu.

### 德国举行“中国电影周”

1959-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    德国举行“中国电影周”
    新华社柏林23日电　中国电影代表团今天抵达柏林，前来参加德意志民主共和国为庆祝我国建国十周年而举行的“中国电影周”。这个电影周将从9月25日开始。
    由长春电影制片厂副厂长张望率领的代表团还包括团员赵丹和于彦夫。
