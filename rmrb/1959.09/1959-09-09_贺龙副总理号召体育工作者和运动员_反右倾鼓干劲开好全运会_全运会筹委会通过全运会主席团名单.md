Title: Vice Premier He Long Calls on Sports Workers and Athletes to Oppose Rightism, Exert Efforts, and Hold a Successful National Games; National Games Organizing Committee Passes List of National Games Presidium

Author:

Date: 1959-09-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 8th: The Preparatory Committee for the First National Games held its second meeting on the afternoon of the 7th. The meeting listened to and unanimously approved the "Report on the Preparatory Work for the First National Games" by Huang Zhong, Secretary-General of the National Games Preparatory Committee. The meeting discussed and approved the list of the Presidium of the First National Games of the People's Republic of China (attached below). Thus, the work of the First National Games Preparatory Committee, established in September of last year, has been successfully completed.

Vice Premier of the State Council and Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, He Long, made an important speech at the meeting. He said: The scale of the First National Games is very large, and the selection work in various regions has been done very well. Vice Premier He Long called on all sports workers and athletes to study the spirit of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Party, oppose rightism, exert efforts, and make this National Games a success.

Xinhua News Agency, 8th: The first meeting of the Presidium of the First National Games of the People's Republic of China was held on the afternoon of the 7th. The meeting discussed and approved the list of the General Command and heads of various departments of the First National Games of the People's Republic of China; and approved the program for the opening and closing ceremonies of the First National Games.

  The General Command of the First National Games is headed by Rong Gaotang as the Commander-in-Chief, and Li Da, Huang Zhong, Zhang Qingji, and Li Menghua as the Deputy Commanders-in-Chief. The General Command has an office and departments such as the Competition Department, the Propaganda Department, the Administrative Management Department, the Mass Gymnastics Performance Department, and the Foreign Guests Reception Department.

### 贺龙副总理号召体育工作者和运动员  反右倾鼓干劲开好全运会  全运会筹委会通过全运会主席团名单

1959-09-09
第3版()
专栏：

    贺龙副总理号召体育工作者和运动员
    反右倾鼓干劲开好全运会
    全运会筹委会通过全运会主席团名单
    新华社8日讯　第一届全国运动会筹备委员会7日下午举行第二次会议。会议听取和一致通过了全运会筹备委员会秘书长黄中关于“第一届全运会筹备工作报告”。会议讨论和通过了中华人民共和国第一届运动会主席团名单（附后），至此，去年9月成立的第一届全国运动会筹备委员会的工作，已经完满地结束。
    国务院副总理兼国家体育运动委员会主任贺龙在这次会议上作了重要讲话。他说：第一届全国运动会的规模很大，各地选拔工作做得很好。贺龙副总理号召全体体育工作者和运动员学习党的八届八中全会决议精神，反右倾、鼓干劲，把这次全国运动会开好。
