Title: Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant Builds Sintering Plant
Author:
Date: 1959-09-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency: The newly built sintering plant of Shijingshan Iron and Steel Company has recently been officially put into production. The entire set of equipment in this sintering plant, from raw material crushing and batching to the production of high-quality sinter with a high iron content, is operated using chain-locked control. The commissioning of this plant provides favorable conditions for Shijingshan Iron and Steel Company to increase pig iron production capacity and complete this year's pig iron production tasks.

The workers who built this project were full of energy from beginning to end. They overcame the shortage of materials and equipment, as well as the difficulties brought by the hot summer and severe winter to the construction, ensuring the progress of the project. During the equipment installation stage, the Party committee secretaries and managers of the contracting units went to the front line one after another, focusing on equipment installation and organizing forces to rush to complete the remaining projects, solving problems in a timely manner, which further inspired the workers' enthusiasm. Workers in the first engineering district of the First Engineering Company completed the earthwork and concrete pouring of the fan foundation in the pouring rain. The fitters and riggers of the third production team of the Third Engineering Company worked together with one heart and one mind, becoming more and more enthusiastic, and completed the installation of the main body of the fan fourteen hours ahead of schedule.

### 石钢建成烧结厂

1959-09-21
第3版()
专栏：

    石钢建成烧结厂
    新华社讯　石景山钢铁公司新建的烧结厂最近正式投入生产。这座烧结厂，从原料破碎、配料，直到制成含铁量丰富的优质烧结矿的全套设备，都是采用链锁控制操作的。这个厂的投入生产，为石景山钢铁公司提高生铁生产能力和完成今年的生铁生产任务提供了有利的条件。
    建设这项工程的职工，自始至终干劲十足。他们克服了材料设备不足，以及炎夏隆冬给施工带来的一些困难，保证了工程进度。到了设备安装阶段，各承建单位的党委书记、经理纷纷走上第一线，一手抓设备安装，一手组织力量突击结尾工程，发现问题及时解决，更加鼓舞了工人的干劲。第一工程公司一工区的工人冒着暴雨完成了抽风机基础的土方和混凝土浇灌工程。第三工程公司第三生产队的钳工和起重工们同心同德，越干越起劲，提前十四小时完成了抽风机主体的安装工程。
