Title: The Communist Youth League, All-China Youth Federation, and All-China Students' Federation Host Banquet for World Federation of Democratic Youth and International Student Union Delegations
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 27th. The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, the All-China Youth Federation, and the All-China Students' Federation jointly hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation led by World Federation of Democratic Youth Secretary-General, Eshar, and the International Student Union delegation led by International Student Union President, Jiri Pelikan. The guests are visiting our country at our invitation and participating in the 10th Anniversary Celebration of the founding of the nation.

Among those attending the banquet were Hu Yaobang, Liu Xiyuan, Hu Keshi, Wang Wei, Liang Buting, Wang Zhaohua, Hu Qili, and others.

### 我共青团、青联和学联  宴请世界青联和国际学联代表团

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    我共青团、青联和学联
    宴请世界青联和国际学联代表团
    新华社27日讯　中国共产主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国青年联合会和中华全国学生联合会今晚联合设宴，欢迎以世界民主青年联盟总书记埃夏尔为首的世界民主青年联盟代表团和以国际学生联合会主席优利·贝利康为首的国际学联代表团，客人们是应邀前来我国访问并参加建国十周年典礼的。
    出席宴会的，有胡耀邦、刘西元、胡克实、王伟、梁步庭、王照华、胡启立等。
