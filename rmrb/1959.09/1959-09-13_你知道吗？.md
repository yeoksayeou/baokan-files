    Did you know?
    China's first national games were the First National School District Team Sports Alliance Meeting held in 1911 (the third year of the reign of Emperor Xuantong). This games was held during the Nanjing Industrial Exposition. It lasted five days and was attended by 140 people.

### 你知道吗？

1959-09-13
第8版()
专栏：

    你知道吗？
    我国第一次全国性的运动会是在1911年（宣统三年）举办的全国学校区分队第一次体育同盟会。这次运动会是在南京南洋劝业会召开的，会期共五天，参加人数为一百四十人。
