Title: Zhang Qishan Breaks National Long Jump Record
Author:
Date: 1959-09-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 5th -- Zhang Qishan, a male long jump athlete from the Chinese People's Liberation Army, broke the national record of 7.39 meters for the men's long jump, which had been maintained for nearly two years, with an excellent result of 7.49 meters in Beijing today. So far, twenty of the twenty-eight national men's track and field records in our country up to 1958 have been broken.

Zhang Qishan achieved this highest national score today during a track and field test competition held for track and field athletes from all over the country participating in the First National Games. His first jump today was 7.19 meters, and the second jump reached 10 centimeters further than the national record.

In today's test competition, other good results included: Zhou Lianli from Beijing in the men's 110-meter hurdles, 14.4 seconds (breaking the national record of 14.6 seconds); Li Henian from Hebei in the men's 1,500-meter race, 3 minutes 58.3 seconds (breaking the national record of 3 minutes 59.8 seconds); Yin Zhengde and Li Bihua from Hubei in the men's 100-meter race, both with a score of 10.7 seconds, reaching the master athlete standard; and Qu Jiaxi from Guangdong, whose men's long jump result broke the 7-meter mark, reaching 7.02 meters. ### 张启山打破跳远全国纪录

1959-09-06
第6版()
专栏：

    张启山打破跳远全国纪录
    新华社5日讯　中国人民解放军的男子跳远选手张启山，今天在北京以七米四十九的优异成绩，打破了已经保持近两年的男子跳远七米三十九的全国纪录。至此，我国到1958年为止的全部二十八项男子田径全国纪录，已经被打破了二十项。
    张启山今天是在各地参加第一届全国运动会的田径选手举行田径测验比赛时，创造这一全国最高成绩的。他今天第一次试跳的成绩是七米十九，第二次就跳到比全国纪录远十厘米的地方。
    在今天的测验比赛中，成绩较好的还有：北京选手周连立的男子一百一十米高栏，十四秒四
  （破十四秒六的全国纪录）；河北选手李贺年的男子一千五百米赛跑，三分五十八秒三（破三分五十九秒八的全国纪录）；湖北选手殷正德和李必华的男子一百米赛跑，成绩都是十秒七，达到运动健将标准，广东选手区嘉锡男子跳远成绩突破了七米“大关”，达到了七米零二。（附图片）
