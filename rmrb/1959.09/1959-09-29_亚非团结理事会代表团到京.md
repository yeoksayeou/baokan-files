Title: Afro-Asian Solidarity Council Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-29
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. The Afro-Asian Solidarity Council delegation, invited by the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee to visit China and attend the celebrations of the tenth anniversary of the founding of our country, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon, led by Youssef El-Sebai, the head of the delegation and Secretary-General of the Permanent Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Solidarity Council.

    The delegation members include Mazmout Diop, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the African Independence Party and representative of the Afro-Asian Solidarity Council in West Africa; Mosha Camara Sangiiana, member of the National Assembly of Guinea; Abubakar Hamud Sokolo, Chairman of the Somali National League Party; Hash Deo Malaviya, Indian Secretary of the Permanent Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Solidarity Council; and Masao Kitazawa, Japanese Secretary of the Permanent Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Solidarity Council. Mussi Sad-Din, technical secretary of the delegation, also arrived on the same flight. Abdurasidov Gafur, member of the delegation and Soviet Secretary of the Permanent Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Solidarity Council, will arrive in Beijing tomorrow.

    Hu Yuzhi, Wu Maosun, Wang Mingyuan, Zhang Jie, Zhang Tiesheng, Chen Yifan and other leaders of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee went to the airport to welcome them.

### 亚非团结理事会代表团到京

1959-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

    亚非团结理事会代表团到京
    新华社28日讯　应中国亚非团结委员会邀请来我国访问并参加我国建国十周年庆祝典礼的亚非团结理事会代表团，在团长、亚非团结理事会常设书记处总书记优素夫·西巴伊率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团团员有非洲独立党中央委员会第一书记、亚非团结理事会驻西非代表马兹穆特·迪奥普，几内亚国民议会议员莫沙·卡马拉·桑几亚那，索马利兰民族联盟党主席阿布巴喀·哈穆得·索科洛，亚非团结理事会常设书记处印度书记哈什·迪奥·马拉维亚，亚非团结理事会常设书记处日本书记北泽正雄。同机前来的还有代表团技术秘书穆西·萨得丁。团员、亚非团结理事会常设书记处苏联书记阿布杜拉西多夫·加富尔将于明天到京。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国人民外交学会和中国亚非团结委员会等团体的负责人胡愈之、吴茂荪、王明远、张杰、张铁生、陈依范等。
