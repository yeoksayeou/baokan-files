Title: Ceylon Prime Minister Assassinated and Seriously Wounded
Author:
Date: 1959-09-26
Page: 9

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, September 25th. Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike was assassinated and seriously wounded at his official residence this morning.

The assassin was an unidentified person dressed in monastic robes.

Bandaranaike was immediately sent to the hospital for emergency surgery and is still in critical condition.

Today at noon, the Governor-General of Ceylon declared a state of emergency nationwide two hours after the assassination of the Prime Minister.

The Prime Minister was originally scheduled to leave Colombo for London, New York, and Bonn for visits within three days.

### 锡兰总理遇刺受重伤

1959-09-26
第9版()
专栏：

    锡兰总理遇刺受重伤
    新华社科伦坡25日电　锡兰总理班达拉奈克今天上午在他的官邸遇刺受重伤。
    凶手是一个身着僧侣服装，身份不明的人。
    班达拉奈克立即被送进医院，施行紧急手术，目前还未脱离危期。
    今天中午锡兰总督在暗杀总理案件发生后两小时宣布全国戒严。
    总理原定三天内离科伦坡前往伦敦、纽约和波恩访问。
