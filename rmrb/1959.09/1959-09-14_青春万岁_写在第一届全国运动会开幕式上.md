Title: Long Live Youth – Written on the Opening Ceremony of the First National Games
Author: Zou Difan
Date: 1959-09-14
Page: 12

In Beijing's eastern suburbs, new chimneys sing to the sky, new willows stand in rows, and a sun-shaped stadium rises. The National Games open here, bright as the rising sun in the east. The audience encircles like mountains, like constellations shining in the heavens. Play on, joyful band! Red flags flutter, athletes enter, the footsteps of youth resounding with the music. Oh, the demonstration of youth! Oh, the power of unity! See them stand tall, marching from all fronts, from the Yangtze, the Yellow River, the borderlands, from the Tianshan Mountains, Inner Mongolia, Tibet… You offer flowers to our beloved leaders, orchids from the south, crabapples from the north… Conveying our heartfelt devotion. Salutations, cheers, we all applaud our leaders, ten million swallows soar, every beak blooming red. The Young Pioneers' collective dance expresses our beautiful hopes, white shirts, blue pants, red scarves, little girls in red dresses; you collectively skip rope, arms linked on team days, nurturing a green seedling in every small hand, singing team songs in labor. Young girls move in dance steps, a hundred flowers bloom in your hands, blooming with the plum blossoms of peace, releasing the fragrance of labor. Suddenly, the girls harvest wheat, ah, a golden ocean, the wind blows, the wheat waves surge, the people's commune is just riding the waves. Suddenly, young workers appear, you shout in unison, showing earth-shaking power, wheels are turning, motors are roaring, molten iron flows from the steel furnace… You show us the example of labor. Suddenly, the flower of people is in full bloom on the circular lawn, blooming with "Long Live the Motherland!" Great and glorious motherland, this year is your tenth National Day, you are striding forward, like these youthful and strong footsteps. Ah, athletes of our era, stride forward! Extend your arms! Swim, strive upstream! Run, on the general line! With a leap, break the record! With a throw, hurl the javelin! Shoot the ball, towards our goal! Draw the bow, if the enemy blocks us! Ah, athletes of our era, youth belongs to you! Belongs to our great leader! Belongs to our invincible Party! Long live youth! Long live youth!

### 青春万岁——写在第一届全国运动会开幕式上
邹荻帆
1959-09-14
第12版()
专栏：

    青春万岁
    ——写在第一届全国运动会开幕式上
    邹荻帆在北京的东郊，新烟囱满天吹唱，新柳树排列成行，浮起了一座太阳形体育场。全运会在这儿开幕，光明啊，日出东方。观众环列成山，像星座在天体发光。吹奏吧，欢乐的乐队！红旗猎猎飘扬，运动员出场，青春的脚步踏着音乐响。哦，青春的示威！哦，团结的力量！看他们挺起胸膛，从各个战线走来，从长江、黄河、塞上，从天山、内蒙、西藏……你们把鲜花献给我们敬爱的领导人，南方的兰草，北地的海棠……代我们把赤心献上。致敬啊，欢呼啊，我们全体向领导人鼓掌，千万只海燕在飞翔，每张嘴都红花开放。少先队的集体舞表达我们美好的希望，白衣蓝裤红领巾，小小姑娘红衣裳；你们集体跳红绳，队日里挽着臂膀，小手上都培养一枝绿苗，劳动中把队歌齐唱。少女们移动舞步，百花在你们手上开放，开的是和平之梅，放的是劳动芬芳。忽然间少女们手采麦穗，?，一片金色的海洋，大风吹起麦浪滔滔，人民公社正好乘风破浪。忽然间青年工人们出场，你们齐声呼喊啊，显出地动山摇的力量，车轮在动，马达在响，炼铁炉铁水奔流……你们给我们看劳动的榜样。忽然间圆形的草地人的花朵怒放，开的是“祖国万岁”！伟大光荣的祖国啊，今年是你十年国庆，你正迈步前进，像这青春健壮的脚步一样。?，我们时代的运动员们，迈开你们的脚步吧！伸张你们的臂膀吧！游泳吧，力争上游！赛跑吧，在总路线上！一跃身，打破记录！一撒手，投出标枪！射球吧，朝我们的目的！弯弓吧，如果敌人把我们阻挡！?，我们时代的运动员们，青春属于你们！属于我们伟大的领袖！属于我们战无不胜的党！青春万岁！青春万岁！
