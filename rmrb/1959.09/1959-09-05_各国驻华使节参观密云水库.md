Title: Diplomatic Envoys from Various Countries Visit Miyun Reservoir
Author: 
Date: 1959-09-05
Page: 2
　
Xinhua News Agency, 4th - Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China and foreign journalists in Beijing today visited the Miyun Reservoir, the largest comprehensive water conservancy project in North China. They were received by the headquarters of the construction site.
　　
The diplomatic envoys from various countries and foreign journalists visited the Baihe Dam and Chaohe Dam of the reservoir, and took a boat trip to view the scenery of the reservoir.

### 各国驻华使节参观密云水库

1959-09-05
第2版()
专栏：

    各国驻华使节参观密云水库
    新华社4日讯　各国驻华使节和在北京的外国记者，今天参观了华北最大的综合性水利工程——密云水库。他们受到了工地指挥部的接待。
    各国驻华使节和外国记者参观了水库的白河大坝、潮河大坝，并且乘船观光了水库的景色。
