Title: To Cooperate with the Study of Documents from the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the CPC, Standing Committee of the CPPCC Holds Enlarged Meeting to Hear Special Reports
Author:
Date: 1959-09-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. Beijing. To respond to the call of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the CPC to oppose right-leaning tendencies, boost morale, and launch a production increase and economy campaign, and to cooperate with CPPCC members and members of democratic parties in studying the Communiqué and Resolutions of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the CPC, the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held an enlarged meeting this afternoon at the CPPCC Auditorium in Beijing. Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Education, delivered a work report at the meeting entitled "The Great Development and Great Revolution of Education in Our Country and Current Work Tasks."

The meeting will continue with various special reports.

Participating in this enlarged meeting were members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the CPPCC, CPPCC members in Beijing, members of the central committees of various democratic parties, and members of the Beijing Municipal CPPCC.

### 为配合中共八届八中全会文件的学习  政协常委会扩大会议听取专题报告

1959-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    为配合中共八届八中全会文件的学习
    政协常委会扩大会议听取专题报告
    新华社23日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会今天下午在北京政协礼堂举行扩大会议。教育部部长杨秀峰在会上以“我国教育大发展、大革命及当前工作任务”为题，做了工作报告。
    扩大会议是为了响应中共八届八中全会关于反右倾、鼓干劲、开展增产节约运动的号召，为配合政协委员、各民主党派成员学习中共八届八中全会公报和决议而举行的。会议将继续进行各项专题报告。
    参加这次扩大会议的有政协全国委员会常务委员会委员，政协在京委员，各民主党派中央委员和北京市政协委员等。
