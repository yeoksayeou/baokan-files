Title: Algerian National Liberation Army Kills or Wounds Over Six Hundred French Soldiers in Five Days
Author:
Date: 1959-09-14
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 12th: The Algerian National Liberation Army's High Command announced today in a war bulletin that from September 5th to 9th, Algerian nationalists, in battles against the French invading army, killed 405 French invading soldiers and wounded 287. They shot down four French military aircraft in Boussada and Biskra and destroyed twenty-two military vehicles.

    Algerian nationalists captured twenty-four weapons and a large amount of ammunition. Forty-two French soldiers defected with military equipment to join the Algerian National Liberation Army.

    The bulletin said that the Algerian army carried out twelve ambushes against enemy military convoys. Nationalists and French forces engaged in thirty-two encounters. They attacked six French military positions.

    The bulletin also said that Algerian commando teams launched several lightning attacks on French police positions. Algerian nationalist sabotage teams destroyed many telecommunications facilities, utility poles, and telephone poles throughout Algeria.

### 阿尔及利亚民族解放军  五天毙伤法军六百余人

1959-09-14
第8版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚民族解放军
    五天毙伤法军六百余人
    新华社开罗12日电　阿尔及利亚民族解放军最高司令部今天发表的战报说，从9月5日到9日，阿尔及利亚民族主义者在同法国侵略军进行的战斗中，打死法国侵略军四百零五人，打伤二百八十七人。他们在布萨达和比塞卡击落了四架法国军用飞机，并且击毁了二十二辆军用车辆。
    阿尔及利亚民族主义者缴获了二十四件武器和大批弹药。四十二名法军带着军事装备投诚加入了阿尔及利亚民族解放军。
    公报说，阿尔及利亚军队对敌人的军用车队进行了十二次伏击战。民族主义者和法军进行了三十二次遭遇战。他们袭击了六个法军阵地。
    公报又说，阿尔及利亚突击队对法国警察阵地发动了多次闪电进攻。阿尔及利亚民族主义者的破坏队在阿尔及利亚各地破坏了许多电讯机构、电线杆和电话杆。
