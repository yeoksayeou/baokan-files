Title: Jin Hu Song – Ode to Miyun Reservoir
Author: Liu Lanshan
Date: 1959-09-02
Page: 8

Jin Hu Song
——Ode to Miyun Reservoir
	Liu Lanshan

	The wild geese have not yet returned south for the second time,
	A golden lake appears in the Yanshan Mountains;
	Sparkling waves hold up the rising sun,
	Low-lying Miyun unfolds its smiling face.
	Fields of Jiangnan are moved to the foot of the Great Wall,
	Frontier communes settle beside West Lake,
	If not for the relics of the Great Wall,
	The wild geese would surely no longer fly south.

	This golden lake is not very far away,
	You can stand on tiptoe and look east,
	Follow the long river and keep walking forward,
	On the green fields where the rising sun appears.
	This is not a golden fairy tale,
	It is an ocean created by the Great Leap Forward!
	A granary of gold and silver in North China,
	Flow on, sing on, socialist red light!

### 金湖曲——密云水库之歌
刘岚山
1959-09-02
第8版()
专栏：

    金湖曲
    ——密云水库之歌
        刘岚山
    大雁还没有第二次南归，
    一座金湖在燕山中出现；
    粼粼的水波上托起红日，
    低低的密云张开了笑脸。
    江南水田搬到长城脚下，
    塞上公社迁居西子湖边，
    若不是万里长城遗迹在，
    大雁一定会不再往南飞。
    这个金湖并不很远很远，
    你踮起脚尖向东望一望，
    沿着长河一直向前走去，
    在旭日升起的绿野边上。
    这不是一篇金色的童话，
    它是大跃进造出的海洋！
    藏金银的华北大仓库呵，
    流吧唱吧社会主义红光！
