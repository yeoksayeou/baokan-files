Title: Weightlifting Competition Concludes with Seven National Records Broken; Li Baiyu's Middleweight Total Reaches 437.5 kg
Author:
Date: 1959-09-20
Page: 7

    Weightlifting Competition Concludes with Seven National Records Broken
    Li Baiyu's Middleweight Total Reaches 437.5 kg

    Xinhua News Agency, September 19th: The First National Games weightlifting competition, after six days of contests, concluded this evening. In this competition, a total of seven 1958 national records were broken, five of which are now the highest national scores.

    In the seven weight classes, the People's Liberation Army team won first place in the overall team score (82 points), Shanghai team came in second (64 points), Beijing team was third (62 points), Guangdong team fourth (60 points), Hebei team fifth (52 points), Anhui team sixth (34 points), Jiangsu team seventh (30 points), and Heilongjiang team eighth (22 points).

    In the heavyweight competition held this evening, veteran Chang Guanqun from Shanghai broke the 1958 national snatch record of 135 kg with a score of 137.5 kg, and won the championship in this class with a total score of 395 kg.

    Xinhua News Agency, September 19th: Li Baiyu, a famous weightlifter of our country from a peasant background, refreshed three national weightlifting records this morning in the middleweight weightlifting competition of the First National Games and won the championship in this class.

    In the snatch, Li Baiyu snatched 132.5 kg, which is 2 kg more than the national highest score he created in April of this year. In the clean and jerk, he lifted 170.5 kg on his second attempt, breaking his own national record of 170 kg. At this point, his total score was 437.5 kg, exceeding his own national record by as much as 5 kg.

### 举重赛结束共破七项全国纪录  李白玉次重量级总成绩达到437.5公斤

1959-09-20
第7版()
专栏：

    举重赛结束共破七项全国纪录
    李白玉次重量级总成绩达到437.5公斤
    新华社19日讯　第一届全国运动会举重赛经过六天的比武已经在今天晚上结束。在这一次比赛中，一共打破了七项1958年的全国最高纪录，其中有五项是目前全国的最高成绩。
    解放军代表队在七个级的比赛中获得团体总分第一名（八十二分），上海队获得第二名（六十四分），北京队获得第三名（六十二分），广东队第四名（六十分），河北队第五（五十二分），安徽队第六（三十四分），江苏队第七（三十分），黑龙江队第八（二十二分）。
    在今天晚上举行的重量级比赛中，上海的老将常冠群以一三七点五公斤成绩打破一三五公斤的1958年全国推举最高纪录，并以三九五公斤的总成绩获得这个级的冠军。
    据新华社19日讯　农民出身的我国著名举重运动员李白玉今天上午在第一届全运会次重量级举重比赛中，一人刷新了三项全国举重最高纪录，并获得这个级的冠军。
    李白玉在抓举中，抓起了一百三十二点五公斤，这个成绩比他今年4月间创造的全国最高成绩多了二公斤。挺举中，他在第二次试举时就挺起一百七十点五公斤的重量，打破他本人所保持的一百七十公斤全国最高纪录。这时他的总成绩为四百三十七点五公斤，比他本人保持的全国最高纪录提高了五公斤之多。
