	International Briefs

	Pakistani Official Says Steel Plant Will Not Be Built in Five Years
	Karachi dispatch: According to the "Today" newspaper on the 14th, Dr. Nurbi, industrial advisor to the Pakistan Planning Commission, said that "considering the limitations of technology and natural resources," it would be impossible to build a steel plant during the second five-year plan.

	West Germany Threatens to Break Off Relations with the United Arab Republic
	Cairo dispatch: Discord has arisen between the United Arab Republic and West Germany because the German Democratic Republic has established a consulate general in Cairo, and West Germany has threatened to break off diplomatic relations with the United Arab Republic. According to newspapers, Sabri, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the United Arab Republic, explained Cairo's views when he met with Weber, the West German Ambassador to Cairo, on the 14th.

### 国际简讯

1959-09-19
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    巴一官员说五年内将不建钢厂
    卡拉奇讯　据“今天报”14日报道，巴基斯坦计划委员会工业顾问努尔比博士说，“考虑到技术和天然资源有限”，要在第二个五年计划期间建立炼钢厂是不可能的。
    西德威胁要同阿联绝交
    开罗讯　由于德意志民主共和国在开罗设立总领事馆，西德扬言要同阿联断绝外交关系，因而阿联和西德之间发生了龃龉。据报纸透露，阿联外交部副部长萨布里在14日同西德驻开罗大使韦伯会晤时说明了开罗方面的看法。
