Title: Vice Premier Lin Biao Receives Military Attaches from Various Countries

Author:
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense, Marshal Lin Biao, received military attaches from various countries stationed in China on the 27th.

During the reception, the military attaches of various countries, in their collective capacity, presented Marshal Lin Biao with a letter of congratulations. The letter warmly congratulated the People's Republic of China on the brilliant achievements it has made in economic and cultural construction and in consolidating its national defense forces over the past ten years since its founding. They also expressed their deep belief that the friendship between their countries and the People's Republic of China will further develop and be conducive to promoting world peace.

Present at the reception were General Luo Ruiqing, Vice Minister of National Defense and Chief of the General Staff; General Tan Zheng, Vice Minister of National Defense and Director of the General Political Department; General Xu Guangda, Vice Minister of National Defense; and Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense.

### 林彪副总理接见各国驻华武官

1959-09-30
第3版()
专栏：

    林彪副总理接见各国驻华武官
    新华社29日讯　副总理兼国防部长林彪元帅27日接见了各国驻华武官。
    接见时，各国驻华武官以集体名义交给林彪元帅一封祝贺信，热烈祝贺中华人民共和国成立十年来在经济、文化建设和巩固国防力量各方面取得的辉煌成就，并深信他们的国家和中华人民共和国之间的友谊，将进一步发展和有利于促进世界和平。
    接见时，在座的有国防部副部长兼总参谋长罗瑞卿大将，国防部副部长兼总政治部主任谭政大将，国防部副部长许光达大将和国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将。
