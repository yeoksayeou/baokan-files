Title: Afghan Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. The government delegation of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, led by Ghulam Muhammad Sherzad, Minister of Commerce of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation is visiting Beijing at the invitation of our government to attend the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of our country.

The delegation members are Sardar Chariman Mahmoud Ghazi, Acting Director of the Economic Department of the Afghan Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, welcomed the distinguished guests at the airport.

After the military band played the national anthems of China and Afghanistan, Ghulam Muhammad Sherzad, the head of the delegation, reviewed the guard of honor in the company of Minister Ye Jizhuang. The distinguished guests received flowers from children in the capital.

Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation were Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Deng Jie, Vice Minister of Light Industry, as well as heads of various government ministries, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations.

Diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China also welcomed the delegation at the airport. (Attached picture)

### 阿富汗政府代表团到京

1959-09-30
第3版()
专栏：

    阿富汗政府代表团到京
    新华社29日讯　由阿富汗王国商业大臣吴拉姆·穆罕默德·舍尔扎德率领的阿富汗王国政府代表团，在今天下午乘飞机达到北京。代表团是应我国政府邀请来北京参加我国建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
    代表团团员是阿富汗王国外交部经济司代司长萨达尔·查尔曼·马哈茂德·加齐。
    对外贸易部部长叶季壮到机场欢迎贵宾。
    军乐队奏中国和阿富汗两国国歌后，代表团团长吴拉姆·穆罕默德·舍尔扎德在叶季壮部长陪同下检阅了仪仗队。贵宾们接受了首都儿童的献花。
    今天到机场欢迎的还有文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长章汉夫，轻工业部副部长邓洁以及政府各部负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领，各民主党派和人民团体负责人。
    到机场欢迎的还有各国驻华使馆官员。（附图片）
