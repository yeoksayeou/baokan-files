Title: French-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-26
Page: 2
    Xinhua News Agency, September 25th. The delegation of the French-Chinese Friendship Association, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China and attend the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the country, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon, led by the head of the delegation, Pierre Abraham.

### 法中友协代表团到京

1959-09-26
第2版()
专栏：

    法中友协代表团到京
    新华社25日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的法中友协代表团一行三人，在团长皮埃尔·阿勃拉汗率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
