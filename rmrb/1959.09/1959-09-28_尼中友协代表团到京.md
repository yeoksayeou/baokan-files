Title: Nepal-China Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th. The delegation of the Nepal-China Friendship Association, invited to China to participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation was led by Purna Bahadur, President of the Nepal-China Friendship Association.

### 尼中友协代表团到京

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    尼中友协代表团到京
    新华社27日讯　应邀来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的尼泊尔中国友好协会代表团一行三人，在团长、尼泊尔中国友好协会主席普尔纳·巴哈杜尔率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
