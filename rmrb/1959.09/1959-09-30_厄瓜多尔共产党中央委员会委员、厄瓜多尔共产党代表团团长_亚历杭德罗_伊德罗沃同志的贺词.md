Title: Greetings from Comrade Alejandro Ydrovo, Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ecuador and Head of the Delegation of the Communist Party of Ecuador

Author:
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 7

Comrades and friends: I am very happy to speak to you, expressing our love for peace, our infinite love for the People's Republic of China, and the conviction that socialism will soon triumph throughout the world. (Reading the message of greetings from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ecuador)

To the Glorious Central Committee of the Communist Party of China,

We, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ecuador, extend our congratulations to you.

We congratulate you on the tenth anniversary of the victory of the Chinese Revolution. The Chinese Revolution has pointed out that the people of feudal and semi-colonial countries can and should achieve liberation.

We congratulate you on the development of socialist industry, on the contribution of your people's communes, and on the development of your people's cultural and educational undertakings.

We congratulate you on your continuous contributions to peace and friendship between the peoples of all countries, and on your tireless support and solidarity with the oppressed nations struggling for their national independence.

We pay tribute to our beloved Comrade Mao Zedong, to all the comrades of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and to all the heroic party members who have tirelessly fought great battles from guerrilla warfare to the construction of socialism.

We express to you through our representatives our heartfelt great joy and our confidence in the future generated by seeing the vigorous development of your country. In your country, the flowers of joy are blooming, and a new world is growing. Together with our beloved Soviet Union, you are the pioneers in creating a socialist world and building a communist society on the ruins of imperialism and feudalism.

                                  Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ecuador (Attached image)

### 厄瓜多尔共产党中央委员会委员、厄瓜多尔共产党代表团团长  亚历杭德罗·伊德罗沃同志的贺词

1959-09-30
第7版()
专栏：

    厄瓜多尔共产党中央委员会委员、厄瓜多尔共产党代表团团长
    亚历杭德罗·伊德罗沃同志的贺词
    同志们，朋友们：我感到很幸福来向你们致词，表达我们对和平的热爱，对中华人民共和国无限的挚爱，并确信社会主义不久将在全世界取得胜利。（宣读厄瓜多尔共产党中央委员会的贺词）
    致光荣的中国共产党中央委员会
    我们代表厄瓜多尔共产党中央委员会向你们表示祝贺。
    我们祝贺中国革命胜利十周年。中国革命指出了封建主义和半殖民地国家的人民能够而且应该取得解放。
    我们祝贺你们社会主义工业的发展，祝贺你们人民公社的贡献，祝贺你们人民文化教育事业的发展。
    我们祝贺你们对和平和各国人民间的友谊不断作出的贡献，祝贺你们对为争取自己民族独立而斗争的被压迫各民族的不倦支持和团结精神。
    我们向敬爱的毛泽东同志、向中国共产党中央委员会全体同志、向全体英勇的党员表示敬意，他们从打游击起到建设社会主义，不倦地进行了巨大的战斗。
    我们通过我们的代表向你们表达我们出自内心的极大喜悦，以及由于看到你们国家蓬勃发展而产生的对我们未来的信心。在你们的国家里，欢乐的花朵盛开，新的世界正在成长。和我们敬爱的苏联一起，你们是在帝国主义和封建主义的废墟上缔造社会主义世界和建立共产主义社会的先锋。
                              厄瓜多尔共产党中央委员会（附图片）
