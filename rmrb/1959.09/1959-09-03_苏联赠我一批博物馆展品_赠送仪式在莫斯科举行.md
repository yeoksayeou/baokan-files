Title: The Soviet Union Presents a Batch of Museum Exhibits to China; Presentation Ceremony Held in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1959-09-03
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 1st. The Soviet Union presented 751 exhibits for museum display to China. The presentation ceremony for these exhibits was held today at the Soviet State Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in Moscow.
    These exhibits were collected and presented to China by the Institute of Marxism-Leninism under the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Internal Affairs, the Ministry of Defense, the Ministry of Culture, the Soviet State Committee for Foreign Economic Relations, the Institute of Chinese Studies of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, the Lenin State Library, the Central Archives Administration of the Soviet Union, and other organizations. Among these exhibits are authentic documents and pictures reflecting the impact of the Great October Socialist Revolution on the Chinese people's national liberation movement. Many exhibits reflect the Soviet people's assistance to China during the War of Resistance Against Japan and in socialist construction. Among them are a model of a gunboat used by the Soviet army in the liberation of Northeast China while fighting the Japanese invaders, a model of a rolling mill manufactured by the Ural Machinery Plant for the Anshan Iron and Steel Company, a model of the first artificial satellite, as well as many paintings and sculptures.
    At the presentation ceremony, G. Zhukov, Chairman of the Soviet State Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, spoke. Zhang Weilie, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, accepted the gifts and expressed his gratitude to the Soviet Union for donating these valuable exhibits.

### 苏联赠我一批博物馆展品  赠送仪式在莫斯科举行

1959-09-03
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联赠我一批博物馆展品
    赠送仪式在莫斯科举行
    新华社莫斯科1日电　苏联赠送给中国七百五十一件供博物馆展览的展品。今天在莫斯科苏联国家对外文化委员会举行了这些展品的赠送仪式。
    这些展览品是苏联共产党中央委员会马克思列宁主义研究院、苏联外交部、内务部、国防部、文化部、苏联国家对外经济关系委员会、苏联科学院中国问题研究所、国立列宁图书馆、苏联档案总局等机关搜集并赠送给中国的。在这些展览品中，有反映伟大十月社会主义革命对中国人民民族解放运动的影响的真实文件和图片。有许多展览品反映了苏联人民在抗日战争中和在社会主义建设中对中国的帮助。其中，有苏联军队参加解放中国东北时跟日本侵略者作战时用的炮舰的模型，有乌拉尔机器工厂为鞍山钢铁公司制造的轧钢机模型，有第一颗人造卫星的模型以及许多图画和雕刻。
    在赠送仪式上，苏联国家对外文化委员会主席格·茹科夫讲了话。中国驻苏联大使馆临时代办张伟烈接受了礼品，他对苏联赠送这些有价值的展览品表示感谢。
