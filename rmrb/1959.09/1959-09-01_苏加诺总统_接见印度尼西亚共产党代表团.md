Title: President Sukarno Receives Indonesian Communist Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-09-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 30th – President Sukarno received a delegation from the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party (PKI) consisting of D.N. Aidit, Njoto, and Sakirman at his Bogor villa this morning. The meeting lasted for one hour. A press release issued by the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the PKI stated that the delegation conveyed to President Sukarno the resolutions made by the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the PKI regarding the political situation after the restoration of the 1945 Constitution, and invited President Sukarno to deliver a speech at the Sixth National Congress of the PKI. President Sukarno accepted the delegation's invitation and agreed to speak at the reception scheduled for the evening of September 16 to celebrate the closing of the National Congress of the PKI.

### 苏加诺总统  接见印度尼西亚共产党代表团

1959-09-01
第6版()
专栏：

    苏加诺总统
    接见印度尼西亚共产党代表团
    据新华社雅加达30日电　苏加诺总统今天上午在他的茂物别墅接见了由艾地、约多、萨基尔曼组成的印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会代表团。接见时间为一小时。印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会书记处发表的新闻公报说，代表团向苏加诺总统转达了印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会第八次全体会议对在恢复了1945年宪法以后的政治形势所作的决议，并且邀请苏加诺总统在印度尼西亚共产党第六次全国代表大会上发表演说。苏加诺总统接受了代表团的邀请，并且同意在预定在9月16日晚上为庆祝印度尼西亚共产党全国代表大会闭幕而举行的招待会上讲话。
