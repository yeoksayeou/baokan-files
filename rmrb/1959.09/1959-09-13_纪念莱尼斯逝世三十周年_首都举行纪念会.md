Title: Capital Holds Commemorative Meeting on the 30th Anniversary of the Death of Rainis
Author:
Date: 1959-09-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th – The China-Soviet Friendship Association, the Beijing China-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Beijing Library jointly held a commemorative meeting today marking the 30th anniversary of the death of the great Latvian revolutionary poet Jānis Rainis.

	Go Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the China-Soviet Friendship Association, delivered a report at the meeting on the poet's life and works. He introduced the poet's activities during the 1905 Revolution and pointed out that the poet's creative career was closely linked to the people's revolutionary movement. He also said that Rainis had deep feelings for the Chinese people. In his works, he warmly praised the struggle of the Chinese people. After the meeting, the film "The Poet Rainis" was screened.

	More than 700 literary and art enthusiasts from the capital attended the memorial meeting.

	Shan Fielov, First Secretary of the Soviet Embassy in China, was also invited to attend the memorial meeting.

### 纪念莱尼斯逝世三十周年  首都举行纪念会

1959-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念莱尼斯逝世三十周年
    首都举行纪念会
    新华社12日讯  中苏友好协会总会、北京市中苏友好协会和北京图书馆今日联合举行拉脱维亚伟大革命诗人扬·莱尼斯逝世三十周年纪念会。
    中苏友好协会总会副秘书长戈宝权在会上做了关于诗人的生平及创作的报告。他介绍了诗人在1905年革命中的活动，并且指出，诗人的创作事业是和人民革命运动紧密连系在一起的。他还说，莱尼斯对于中国人民也怀有深刻感情。他在作品中，对于中国人民的斗争给予了热情的歌颂。会后，放映了“诗人莱尼斯”影片。
    出席纪念会的，有首都文艺爱好者七百余人。
    苏联驻华大使馆一等秘书善菲罗夫也应邀出席了纪念会。
