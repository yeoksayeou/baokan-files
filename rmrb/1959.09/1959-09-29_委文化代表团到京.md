Title: Venezuelan Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th – The Venezuelan Cultural Friendship Delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China and participate in the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the country, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon, led by the head of the delegation, Humberto Cuenca.

### 委文化代表团到京

1959-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

    委文化代表团到京
    新华社28日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的委内瑞拉文化友好代表团一行五人，在团长温伯托·昆卡率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
