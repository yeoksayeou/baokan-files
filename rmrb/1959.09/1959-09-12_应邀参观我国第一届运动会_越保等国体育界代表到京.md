Title: Sports Representatives from Vietnam, Bulgaria, and Other Countries Arrive in Beijing to Attend China's First National Games

Author: 
Date: 1959-09-12
Page: 3
　　
Xinhua News Agency, 11th September: The sports delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, invited to visit the First National Games of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 10th. The sports delegations of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, the People's Republic of Romania, the People's Republic of Hungary, the Czechoslovak Republic, and the German Democratic Republic arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 11th. The Vietnamese sports delegation consists of three members, led by Tran Chi Hien, member of the Standing Committee of the Vietnam Sports and Gymnastics Commission. The Romanian sports delegation consists of three members, led by Manoli Podnarachi, Chairman of the Romanian Sports Federation. The Hungarian sports delegation consists of four members, led by Gyula Hegyi, Chairman of the Hungarian Sports and Games Committee. The Czechoslovak sports delegation consists of two members, led by František Fiedler, Chairman of the Czechoslovak Sports Federation. The sports delegation of the German Democratic Republic is led by Rudi Reichert, Chairman of the German Democratic Republic Sports Federation and Sports Association. The head of the Bulgarian sports delegation, Zhe. Kolev, Chairman of the Bulgarian Sports Federation, will arrive in Beijing slightly later.

Xinhua News Agency, 11th September: Abdul Razak Azami, Director of Student Recreation Activities at the Sports Department of the Iraqi Ministry of Education, and Youssef Bazi, Physical Education Teacher at Baghdad Engineering College and Chairman of the Iraqi Basketball Association, sports representatives of the Republic of Iraq invited to visit the First National Games of the People's Republic of China; Rene Napoleonni, Secretary General of the French Labour Sports Federation, Maurice Bak, Committee Member of the Physical Education Office of the French Labour Sports Federation and Deputy Provost of the National Sports Institute, and Atos Bambi, Chairman of the Blue Coast Committee of the French Labour Sports Federation, a delegation of the French Labour Sports Federation comprising three people; and Abdul Rahim Shaddad, Honorary Secretary of the Sudanese Football Association and Honorary Treasurer of the Sudanese Olympic Committee, a representative of the Sudanese sports community, arrived in Beijing on the 10th and 11th.

### 应邀参观我国第一届运动会  越保等国体育界代表到京

1959-09-12
第3版()
专栏：

    应邀参观我国第一届运动会
    越保等国体育界代表到京
    新华社11日讯　应邀来我国参观中华人民共和国第一届运动会的越南民主共和国体育代表团已于10日下午抵达北京。保加利亚人民共和国体育代表团、罗马尼亚人民共和国体育代表团、匈牙利人民共和国体育代表团、捷克斯洛伐克共和国体育代表团、德意志民主共和国体育代表团于11日下午抵达北京。越南体育代表团一行三人，由越南体育体操委员会常务委员会委员陈志贤率领。罗马尼亚体育代表团一行三人由罗马尼亚体育总会主席马诺利·波德纳拉希率领。匈牙利体育代表团一行四人由匈牙利体育运动委员会主席纠拉·赫基率领。捷克斯洛伐克体育代表团一行二人，由捷克斯洛伐克体育联合会主席弗朗齐歇·富德斯伦率领。德意志民主共和国体育代表团由德意志民主共和国体育总会和运动协会主席鲁迪·赖歇耳特率领。保加利亚体育代表团团长、保加利亚体育联合会主席热·科列夫稍缓抵京。
    新华社11日讯　应邀来我国参观中华人民共和国第一届运动会的伊拉克共和国体育界代表、伊拉克教育部体育司学生文娱活动处处长阿卜杜勒·拉扎克·阿札米和巴格达工程学院体育教员、伊拉克篮球协会主席优素福·巴齐、法国劳动体育联合会代表团，法国劳工体育运动联合会秘书长雷内·拿破伦尼、法国劳工体育运动联合会育体办公室委员、国立体育学院副教务长莫里斯·巴克和法国劳工体育运动联合会蓝色海岸委员会主席阿托斯·班比等三人及苏丹体育界代表、苏丹足球协会名誉秘书、苏丹奥委会名誉会计阿卜杜勒·拉希姆·沙达德，于10日和11日抵京。
