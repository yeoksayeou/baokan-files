Title: Mu Xiangxiong Breaks World Record Again in 100m Breaststroke, Improving it by 0.2 Seconds with a Time of 1 Minute 11.1 Seconds
Author: 
Date: 1959-09-18
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: Exciting news came from the Beijing Gymnasium swimming pool. China's famous breaststroke swimmer Mu Xiangxiong (Hebei), at 7:47 PM tonight in the men's 100-meter breaststroke final of the National Games, once again broke the world record he set for the second time on August 30th of this year, with an excellent time of 1 minute 11.1 seconds. This is the first world record broken since the opening of the National Games.

Mu Xiangxiong has broken the world record three times after the breaststroke rule prohibiting underwater swimming was implemented, with less than nine months between each record. The second and third times were only eighteen days apart. The first time was on December 20, 1958, with a time of 1 minute 11.4 seconds. Such an instance of breaking one's own high record three times in a short period is rare in the history of world swimming. As soon as this news was released, the swimming pool immediately erupted with thunderous applause, which lasted for a full fifteen minutes.

In the competition, breaststroke athlete Mo Guoxiong (Guangdong) won second place with a time of 1 minute 13.5 seconds. Former world record holder Qi Lieyun won third place with a time of 1 minute 13.6 seconds. The following rankings are: Fourth place He Man (PLA) 1 minute 14.5 seconds; Fifth place Zeng Yingyi (Guangxi) 1 minute 14.7 seconds; Sixth place Yang Zhaozhong (PLA) 1 minute 16.8 seconds; Seventh place Wei Qilun (Hubei) 1 minute 16.8 seconds; Eighth place Yang Shufu (Guangdong) 1 minute 17.7 seconds. ### 穆祥雄再创百米蛙泳世界纪录  以一分十一秒一的成绩把世界纪录缩短零点二秒

1959-09-18
第7版()
专栏：

    穆祥雄再创百米蛙泳世界纪录
    以一分十一秒一的成绩把世界纪录缩短零点二秒
    新华社17日讯　北京体育馆游泳馆传出了振奋人心的消息。我国著名的蛙泳选手穆祥雄（河北），今天晚上七时四十七分在全国运动会男子一百米蛙泳决赛中，以一分十一秒一的优异成绩，再次打破了自己在今年8月30日第二次创造的男子百米蛙泳一分十一秒三的世界纪录。这是全国运动会开幕以来第一次打破世界纪录。
    穆祥雄三次创造蛙泳规则中禁用潜水以后的世界纪录，相隔时间不到九个月，第二次和第三次仅隔十八天。第一次是在1958年12月20日，成绩是一分十一秒四。这种在短时间内三次打破自己创造的高纪录的事例，在世界游泳运动史上是罕见的。这项消息一发布，游泳馆立即响起了暴风雨般的掌声，掌声延续达一刻钟之久。
    比赛中，蛙泳健将莫国雄（广东）获第二名，成绩一分十三秒五。前世界纪录保持者戚烈云获第三名，成绩一分十三秒六。以下的名次是：第四名何满（解放军）一分十四秒五；第五名曾英逸（广西）一分十四秒七；第六名杨兆忠（解放军）一分十六秒八；第七名韦起伦（湖北）一分十六秒八；第八名杨树福（广东）一分十七秒七。（附图片）
    9月17日，在百米蛙泳决赛中，我国优秀运动员穆祥雄以一分十一秒一的优异成绩，打破了世界最高纪录。
                    雪印　李虎臣摄（新华社稿）
