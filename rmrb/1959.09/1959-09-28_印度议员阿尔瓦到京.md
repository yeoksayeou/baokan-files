Title: Indian MP Alva Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7

　　Xinhua News Agency, 27th - Indian Member of Parliament Joachim Alva arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. He is visiting our country and attending the 10th anniversary celebrations at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 印度议员阿尔瓦到京

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    印度议员阿尔瓦到京
    新华社27日讯　印度国会议员乔奇姆·阿尔瓦今天下午乘飞机到达北京。他是应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问和参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
