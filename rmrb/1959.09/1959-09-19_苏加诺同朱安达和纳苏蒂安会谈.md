Title: Sukarno Meets with Djuanda and Nasution
Author:
Date: 1959-09-19
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 17th: President Sukarno held talks this morning with Djuanda, First Minister of the Indonesian Working Cabinet, and Nasution, Minister of Defense and Security.

Sukarno told reporters waiting outside after the meeting that they had discussed certain issues facing Indonesia at the moment, which were expected to be resolved within a few days.

It is widely believed here that these three important figures in Indonesia are studying ways to resolve the case of Nasution's detention of Acting Attorney General Tarunami Hadjaria and other related issues. The Tarunami Hadjaria case has become the focus of the current situation in Indonesia.

### 苏加诺同朱安达和纳苏蒂安会谈

1959-09-19
第6版()
专栏：

    苏加诺同朱安达和纳苏蒂安会谈
    新华社雅加达17日电　苏加诺总统今天上午同印度尼西亚工作内阁第一部长朱安达、国防和安全部长纳苏蒂安举行会谈。
    苏加诺在会后对在外面等候的记者说，他们讨论了印度尼西亚目前面临的某些问题，这些问题预料将在几天之内获得解决。
    这里普遍认为，印度尼西亚这三位重要人物正在研究解决纳苏蒂安拘留代理最高检察总长塔鲁纳米哈加的案件和其它一些有关的问题。塔鲁纳米哈加案件已经成为印度尼西亚当前局势的焦点。
