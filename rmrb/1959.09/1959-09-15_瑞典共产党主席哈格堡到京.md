Title: Chairman Hagberg of the Communist Party of Sweden Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 14th: Hilding Hagberg, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Sweden, and his wife, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China for a visit to our country.

Those who went to the airport to greet them included Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee. (Attached picture)

Below: Chairman Hilding Hagberg (second from left) and his wife (first from left) are at the airport with Peng Zhen (second from right), member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and Wang Jiaxiang (first from right), Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, who came to welcome them. (Photo by Liu Changzhong, Xinhua News Agency)

### 瑞典共产党主席哈格堡到京

1959-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞典共产党主席哈格堡到京
    新华社14日讯　应中国共产党中央委员会邀请前来我国访问的瑞典共产党中央委员会主席希尔丁·哈格堡和他的夫人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的，有中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记彭真，中共中央书记处书记王稼祥等。（附图片）
    下图：希尔丁·哈格堡主席（左第二人）和他的夫人（左第一人）和前来欢迎的中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记彭真（右第二人）、中共中央书记处书记王稼祥（右第一人）在机场上。　　新华社记者　刘长忠摄
