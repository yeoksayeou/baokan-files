Title: Swedish Upper House Member Georg Branting Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th—Georg Branting, a member of the Swedish Upper House, and another person, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 瑞典上院议员乔治·布朗亭到北京

1959-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典上院议员乔治·布朗亭到北京
    新华社11日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请前来我国访问的瑞典上院议员乔治·布朗亭等二人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
