Title: Premier Zhou Sends a Telegram to the Governor-General of Ceylon to Express Condolences on the Death of Bandaranaike; Premier Zhou and Vice Premier Chen Visit the Ceylon Embassy to Express Condolences
Author:
Date: 1959-09-27
Page: 9

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th. Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to the Governor-General of Ceylon, Sir Oliver Goonetilleke, to express condolences on the death of Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To His Excellency Sir Oliver Goonetilleke, Governor-General of Ceylon:

I was shocked to learn of the unfortunate death of His Excellency Mr. Bandaranaike, Prime Minister of your country. On behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China, I would like to express my condolences to Your Excellency and the Government of Ceylon, and please convey my deepest condolences to the family of Mr. Bandaranaike.

        Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, September 26, 1959

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th. Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi visited the Ceylon Embassy in China this evening to express condolences on the death of Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike.

Accompanying Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi were Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, Director of the First Asian Department of the Foreign Ministry Zhang Wenjin, and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department Ma Zhenwu.

Premier Zhou and others, accompanied by Ambassador Gooneratne, observed a moment of silence before the portrait of Prime Minister Bandaranaike and presented a wreath.

### 周总理致电锡兰总督  吊唁班达拉奈克逝世  周总理和陈副总理到锡兰大使馆致哀

1959-09-27
第9版()
专栏：

    周总理致电锡兰总督
    吊唁班达拉奈克逝世
    周总理和陈副总理到锡兰大使馆致哀
    新华社26日讯　周恩来总理致电锡兰总督古涅狄莱克，哀悼锡兰总理班达拉奈克的逝世。电文如下：
    锡兰总督古涅狄莱克爵士阁下：
    惊悉贵国政府总理班达拉奈克先生阁下不幸遇害逝世，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府向阁下和锡兰政府致唁，并请向班达拉奈克先生阁下的家属转达我的深切哀悼。
            中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来　1959年9月26日
    新华社26日讯　周恩来总理、陈毅副总理兼外交部长今晚前往锡兰驻中国大使馆，吊唁锡兰总理班达拉奈克的逝世。
    随同周恩来总理和陈毅副总理兼外交部长前往吊唁的，有外交部副部长章汉夫，外交部第一亚洲司司长章文晋、礼宾司副司长马振武。
    周总理等由高伯拉瓦大使陪同，在班达拉奈克总理遗像前静默致哀，并献了花圈。
