Title: Pakistani Socialite Ispahani and Wife Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th. Pakistani socialite Mr. Ispahani and his wife, invited by the Chinese-Pakistani Friendship Association to attend the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of our country, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

### 巴基斯坦社会名流  伊斯巴哈尼夫妇到京

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦社会名流
    伊斯巴哈尼夫妇到京
    新华社27日讯　应中国巴基斯坦友好协会邀请前来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的巴基斯坦社会名流伊斯巴哈尼夫妇，今天乘飞机到达北京。
