Title: Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia Presents Awards to Mongolian Film Workers on Behalf of the Ministry of Culture
Author:
Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, September 23rd: Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, Xie Fusheng, presented the "Excellent Worker" medals and certificates of the Ministry of Culture to five Mongolian film workers, including Tsendra, tonight on behalf of the Chinese Ministry of Culture, in recognition of their outstanding achievements in co-producing the large-scale documentary "Ulaanbaatar - Beijing" with Chinese film workers.

    The award ceremony was held at the Chinese Embassy in Mongolia. Ambassador Xie Fusheng spoke at the award ceremony. Mongolian film director Tsendra spoke on behalf of the award recipients to express his gratitude.

### 我驻蒙大使代表文化部向蒙古电影工作者授奖

1959-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    我驻蒙大使代表文化部向蒙古电影工作者授奖
    据新华社乌兰巴托23日电　中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生今天晚上代表中国文化部把文化部“优秀工作者”奖章和奖状授给蒙古电影工作者藏德拉等五人，以表彰他们在和中国电影工作者合拍大型纪录片“乌兰巴托——北京”的工作中作出的优秀成绩。
    授奖仪式在中国驻蒙古大使馆举行。谢甫生大使在授奖仪式上讲了话。蒙古电影编导藏德拉在讲话时代表受奖者表示感谢。
