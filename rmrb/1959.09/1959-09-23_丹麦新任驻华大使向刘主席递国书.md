Title: New Danish Ambassador to China Presents Credentials to Chairman Liu
Author:
Date: 1959-09-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd: The new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Denmark to China, Mr. Bartholdy, accompanied by all the diplomatic officers of the Embassy, presented his credentials to Chairman Liu Shaoqi this afternoon.

Present at the occasion were: Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Xu Dashen, Director of the Department of West European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

### 丹麦新任驻华大使向刘主席递国书

1959-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    丹麦新任驻华大使向刘主席递国书
    新华社22日讯　丹麦王国新任驻中国特命全权大使巴特森偕大使馆全体外交官员，今天下午向刘少奇主席递交国书。
    在场的有：外交部副部长罗贵波，外交部西欧司司长徐达深、礼宾司副司长马振武。
