Title: Sri Lanka's New Prime Minister Announces Continued Pursuit of Former Prime Minister's Policies
Author: 
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, September 26th: Sri Lanka's new Prime Minister, Wijayananda Dahanayake, announced on national radio tonight that the new government is prepared to work according to the policies and principles of the late Prime Minister Bandaranaike during the remaining eighteen months of the Bandaranaike government's term. In his brief speech, Dahanayake called on all ethnic groups to fully support the new government and help it implement government policies.

### 锡兰新总理宣布  继续奉行前总理的政策

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    锡兰新总理宣布
    继续奉行前总理的政策
    新华社科伦坡26日电　新任锡兰总理伍·达哈纳亚克今天夜间在国家广播电台宣布，新政府准备在班达拉奈克政府今后十八个月的任期内，根据已故总理班达拉奈克的政策和原则来进行工作。达哈纳亚克在简短的讲话中，要求各族人民完全支持新政府，帮助它执行政府的政策。
