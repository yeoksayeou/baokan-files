Title: Haitian Writer Fouché Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. Haitian writer Frank Fouché, who was invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China and participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 海地作家富希到京

1959-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

    海地作家富希到京
    新华社28日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的海地作家弗兰克·富希，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
