Title: Commune Nursery Rhymes
Author: Li Zuohua
Date: 1959-09-07
Page: 8

    ·Commune Nursery Rhymes·
      Li Zuohua
      Rice Planting
    The water pump,
    Screeches,
    The river water smiles as it goes up the mountain;
    The rice planting song,
    Floats on the water,
    The uncles are planting rice in the clouds;
    White clouds,
    Circle the mountains,
    In a blink, the slopes are covered in green waves.
      My Home
    The loofah vine,
    Climbs the fence,
    Blooms with golden flowers,
    Grows big gourds,
    The gourds grow as big as clubs,
    Inside the fence is my home.

### 公社儿歌
李作华
1959-09-07
第8版()
专栏：

    ·公社儿歌·
      李作华
      插秧
    抽水机，
    嘎嘎叫，
    河水笑着上山腰；
    插秧歌，
    水上飘，
    叔叔们插秧在云霄；
    白云朵，
    绕山腰，
    眨眼满坡绿滔滔。
      我的家
    丝瓜秧，
    爬篱笆，
    开金花，
    结大瓜，
    瓜儿结得棒锤大，
    篱笆里面是我家。
