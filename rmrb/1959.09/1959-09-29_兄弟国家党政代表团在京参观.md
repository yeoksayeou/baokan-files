Title: Delegations from Fraternal Countries' Parties and Governments Visit Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-29
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: Delegations from the fraternal countries of the Soviet Union, Vietnam, Czechoslovakia, North Korea, Mongolia, Poland, Hungary, Bulgaria, Albania, the German Democratic Republic, and Romania, who are here to participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of our country's founding, visited Beijing yesterday and this morning, accompanied by responsible comrades from our Party and government.

The Soviet Party and Government Delegation, led by Comrade Suslov, toured Beijing city and visited the Summer Palace this morning.

The Vietnamese Party and Government Delegation, headed by President Ho Chi Minh, visited the expanded Tiananmen Square and the newly built Beijing Railway Station yesterday morning.

The Czechoslovak Republic Party and Government Delegation, led by President Novotny, visited the Beijing Railway Station this morning and then went to the Chinese History Museum, another recently completed large building in the capital.

Premier Kim Il-sung, head of the Korean Party and Government Delegation, and some members of the delegation, visited the Dingling Mausoleum Museum and the Ming Tombs Reservoir yesterday, and toured the North China Radio Equipment United Factory.

All members of the Mongolian Party and Government Delegation, led by Chairman Tsedenbal, visited the Palace Museum this morning to appreciate China's ancient architectural art and cultural relics. Some members toured the city and the Temple of Heaven Park yesterday.

The Hungarian Party and Government Delegation, led by Chairman Dobi, visited Beijing city and the Beijing Railway Station yesterday and today. Some members of the Party and Government Delegations from fraternal countries such as Poland, Bulgaria, Albania, and Romania also took a tour of the city by car yesterday.

Members of the German Democratic Republic Party and Government Delegation, led by First Deputy Chairman Hermann Matern, visited the Beijing Railway Station and the Dingling Mausoleum Museum yesterday. Some members also visited the Beijing Synthetic Fiber Experimental Factory this morning, which was built with the help of the German Democratic Republic.

The National Industrial and Transportation Exhibition, which opened yesterday, received Chairman Tsedenbal and all members of the Mongolian Party and Government Delegation, Chairman Zawadzki and all members of the Polish Party and Government Delegation, Chairman Ganev and all members of the Bulgarian Party and Government Delegation, Chairman Shehu and all members of the Albanian Party and Government Delegation, Vice-Chairman Bodnaras and all members of the Romanian Party and Government Delegation, as well as some members of the Vietnamese and Korean delegations. The distinguished guests warmly congratulated China on its great achievements in industry and transportation over the past ten years during their visits.

### 兄弟国家党政代表团在京参观

1959-09-29
第2版()
专栏：

    兄弟国家党政代表团在京参观
    新华社28日讯　来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的苏联、越南、捷克斯洛伐克、朝鲜、蒙古、波兰、匈牙利、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、德意志民主共和国、罗马尼亚等十一个兄弟国家党政代表团，昨天和今天上午分别在我国党和政府负责同志的陪同下，在北京进行了参观访问。
    由苏斯洛夫同志率领的苏联党政代表团在今天上午参观了北京市容，游览了颐和园。
    以胡志明主席为首的越南党政代表团在昨天上午参观了扩建后的天安门广场和新建的北京车站。
    由诺沃提尼总统率领的捷克斯洛伐克共和国党政代表团，今天上午在参观了北京车站后，到首都另一个最近建成的巨大建筑物——中国历史博物馆参观。
    朝鲜党政代表团团长金日成首相和部分团员昨天游览了定陵博物馆和十三陵水库，参观了华北无线电器材联合厂。
    由泽登巴尔主席率领的蒙古党政代表团全体团员今天上午到故宫博物院欣赏我国古代建筑艺术和文物，部分团员昨天观看了市容和天坛公园。
    由道比主席率领的匈牙利党政代表团在昨天和今天先后参观了北京市容和北京火车站。波兰、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、罗马尼亚等兄弟国家党政代表团部分团员，昨天也乘车观看了市容。
    由赫尔曼·马特恩第一副主席率领的德意志民主共和国党政代表团团员昨天参观了北京车站和定陵博物馆，部分团员在今天上午还访问了北京合成纤维实验厂，这个厂是由民主德国帮助我国建设的。
    昨天开幕的全国工业交通展览会今天先后接待了蒙古党政代表团团长泽登巴尔主席和全体团员，波兰党政代表团团长萨瓦茨基主席和全体团员，保加利亚党政代表团团长加涅夫主席和全体团员，阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团团长谢胡主席和全体团员，罗马尼亚党政代表团团长波德纳拉希副主席和全体团员，以及越南和朝鲜两个代表团的部分团员。贵宾们参观时对中国工业交通在十年中的伟大成就表示热烈祝贺。
