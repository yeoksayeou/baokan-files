Title: Comrade Harry Pollitt's Greetings, Chairman of the Central Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Great Britain, Head of the Delegation of the Communist Party of Great Britain
Author:
Date: 1959-09-29
Page: 8

Dear Comrades:
　　On this historic occasion of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I extend fraternal congratulations on behalf of the Communist Party of Great Britain and all progressive and democratic people in Great Britain.
　　We have been watching with great admiration the tremendous progress your country has made in achieving the goals of socialism over the past decade. We are well aware of the terrible legacy that imperialism, especially British imperialism, left in your country. Despite having to face all these obstacles, you have finally overcome them, and thanks to the efforts of the Chinese Communist Party, hundreds of millions of Chinese people today have embarked on the road of transforming China into a great progressive socialist country in the world.
　　Imperialists have never given up their attempts to discredit the achievements of the People's China. Just recently, they even attempted to stir up international anti-China sentiment on the Tibet issue. By doing so, they were able to once again refuse to restore the People's Republic of China's rightful seat in the United Nations General Assembly.
　　But the imperialists' conspiracies are bound to fail. Compared to China's recent Great Leap Forward, what China will do in the coming years will be immeasurably greater.
　　We wish you complete success in your great endeavors. We know that you will achieve victory, and that you, together with all other socialist countries in the world, will become increasingly powerful, because you know that your people love you and are willing to contribute everything in order to build a new world of peace, democracy, and socialism in our time.
　　Long live peace and friendship between the British and Chinese people!
　　Long live the People's China!
　　Long live Mao Zedong!
　　Long live the Chinese Revolution!
　　Long live peace among all nations! (Attached picture)

### 英国共产党中央执行委员会主席、英国共产党代表团团长  哈里·波立特同志的贺词

1959-09-29
第8版()
专栏：

    英国共产党中央执行委员会主席、英国共产党代表团团长
    哈里·波立特同志的贺词亲爱的同志们：
    在中华人民共和国成立十周年这一历史性的节日，我代表英国共产党以及英国全体进步和民主的人士向你们致以兄弟般的祝贺。
    我们一直怀着十分钦佩的心情，注视你们国家过去十年在实现社会主义的目标方面取得的巨大进展。我们十分清楚地知道帝国主义特别是英帝国主义在你们国家留下来的可怕的遗产。尽管你们不得不面对这一切障碍，你们终于克服了这些障碍，并且由于中国共产党的努力，今天几亿中国人民已经走上了把中国改变成为世界上的一个伟大的进步的社会主义国家的道路。
    帝国主义者从来没有放弃他们的诋毁人民中国的成就的勾当。就在最近，他们还企图在西藏问题上煽起国际反华情绪。由于这样做，他们才能在联合国大会上再一次拒绝恢复人民中国在联合国组织中应有的地位。
    但是帝国主义者的阴谋诡计是一定要失败的。同中国最近的大跃进比起来，中国在今后若干年要做的事情将会大得不可比拟。
    我们祝你们所作的巨大努力完全获得成功。我们知道你们会得到胜利，知道你们同全世界所有其他社会主义国家一道将越来越有力量，因为你们知道你们的人民是热爱你们的，他们愿意贡献一切，以便在我们的时代能够建立起一个和平、民主和社会主义的新世界。
    和平和英中两国人民的友谊万岁！
    人民中国万岁！
    毛泽东万岁！
    中国革命万岁！
    世界各国之间的和平万岁！（附图片）
