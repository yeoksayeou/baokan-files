Title: Premier Zhou Reiterates China's Welcome to Mutual Visits Between Soviet and American Leaders, and Points Out That Asian and African Countries Should Be Vigilant Against Imperialist Schemes to Create Tension and Instigate Conflicts in the Far East
Author:
Date: 1959-09-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Premier Zhou Enlai tonight reiterated that the Chinese people welcome the mutual visits between the leaders of the Soviet Union and the United States, which are conducive to further easing international tensions and safeguarding world peace. At the same time, he pointed out that the governments and peoples of all Asian and African countries should be seriously vigilant against the imperialists who are intensifying their efforts to create tension in the Far East and instigate relations between Asian and African countries.

Premier Zhou spoke about the current international situation at a banquet tonight welcoming the Deputy Prime Minister of Afghanistan. In his speech, he said: "We are pleased to see that, thanks to the joint efforts of the Soviet Union and all peace-loving countries and peoples of the world, the current international situation is developing in a direction conducive to peace. The Chinese people welcome the upcoming mutual visits between the leaders of the Soviet Union and the United States, as this is beneficial to further easing international tensions and safeguarding world peace."

Premier Zhou then pointed out: "However, we must also note that the imperialists are intensifying their efforts to create tension in the Far East and instigate relations between Asian and African countries. This is something that the governments and peoples of all Asian and African countries should be seriously vigilant against."

### 周总理重申我国欢迎苏美首脑互相访问  并指出亚非国家应警惕帝国主义在远东制造紧张局势和挑拨阴谋

1959-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理重申我国欢迎苏美首脑互相访问
    并指出亚非国家应警惕帝国主义在远东制造紧张局势和挑拨阴谋
    新华社6日讯　周恩来总理今晚重申中国人民对有益于进一步和缓国际紧张局势和维护世界和平事业的苏美两国首脑互相访问表示欢迎，同时指出一切亚非国家的政府和人民应该严重警惕帝国主义者正在远东加紧制造紧张局势和挑拨亚非各国的关系。
    周总理今晚在欢迎阿富汗副首相的宴会上谈到了目前的国际形势。他在讲话中说：“我们高兴地看到，由于苏联和全世界爱好和平的国家和人民的共同努力，目前国际形势正朝着有利于和平的方向发展。中国人民对苏美两国首脑即将进行互相访问表示欢迎，因为这对于进一步和缓国际紧张局势和维护世界和平事业是有益的。”
    周总理接着指出：“但是，我们也不能不注意到，帝国主义者正在远东加紧制造紧张局势，挑拨亚非各国的关系，这是值得一切亚非国家的政府和人民严重警惕的。”
