Title: Basiah Karam leaves Beijing for Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1959-09-06
Page: 2

    Basiah Karam
    Leaves Beijing for Indonesia
    Xinhua News Agency, September 5th: Basiah Karam, Director of the Women and Children's Department of the Permanent Secretariat of the Asian-African People's Solidarity Council, concluded her visit to China and left Beijing by plane this morning for a visit to Indonesia.

### 巴西亚·卡拉姆  离北京去印度尼西亚

1959-09-06
第2版()
专栏：

    巴西亚·卡拉姆
    离北京去印度尼西亚
    据新华社5日讯　亚非人民团结理事会常设书记处妇女儿童部主任巴西亚·卡拉姆结束了在我国的访问，今天上午乘飞机离开北京前往印度尼西亚访问。
