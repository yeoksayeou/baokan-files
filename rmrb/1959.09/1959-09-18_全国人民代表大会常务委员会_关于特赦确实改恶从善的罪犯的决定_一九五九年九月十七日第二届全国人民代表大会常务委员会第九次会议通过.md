Title: Decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on Granting Special Amnesty to Criminals Who Have Truly Turned Over a New Leaf
Author:
Date: 1959-09-18
Page: 1

    Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
    Decision on Granting Special Amnesty to Criminals Who Have Truly Turned Over a New Leaf
    Adopted at the Ninth Session of the Second National People's Congress Standing Committee on September 17, 1959
    The Ninth Session of the Second National People's Congress Standing Committee discussed the proposal of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China submitted by Chairman Mao Zedong regarding granting special amnesty to war criminals, counter-revolutionary criminals, and ordinary criminal offenders of the Chiang Kai-shek Group and the Puppet Manchurian Regime who have truly turned over a new leaf after a certain period of labor reform. The meeting unanimously agreed with this proposal. In accordance with Article 31, Paragraph 15 of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China, it is decided that on the occasion of celebrating the tenth anniversary of the founding of the great People's Republic of China, special amnesty shall be granted to war criminals, counter-revolutionary criminals, and ordinary criminal offenders of the Chiang Kai-shek Group and the Puppet Manchurian Regime who have undergone a certain period of labor reform and have truly turned over a new leaf.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会  关于特赦确实改恶从善的罪犯的决定  一九五九年九月十七日第二届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第九次会议通过

1959-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常务委员会
    关于特赦确实改恶从善的罪犯的决定
    一九五九年九月十七日第二届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第九次会议通过
    第二届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第九次会议，讨论了毛泽东主席所提出的中国共产党中央委员会关于特赦确实已经改恶从善的战争罪犯、反革命罪犯和普通刑事罪犯的建议。会议一致同意这个建议。根据中华人民共和国宪法第三十一条第十五项的规定，决定：在庆祝伟大的中华人民共和国成立十周年的时候，对于经过一定期间的劳动改造、确实改恶从善的蒋介石集团和伪满洲国的战争罪犯、反革命罪犯和普通刑事罪犯，实行特赦。
