Title: Madame Mudidiyo and Other Members of the International Federation of Women Delegation Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-24
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd – At the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China to attend the National Day celebrations, members of the delegation of the Women's International Democratic Federation: Madame Mudidiyo, member of the Council of the Women's International Democratic Federation, Member of the Communist Party Parliamentary Group of the Indonesian Parliament, Vice Chairman of the Indonesian Women's Movement Association, and Takada Kotoko, Director of the Fukushima Mothers' Conference in Japan, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.
    Delegation members Noreen Hewitt, National President of the Australian Women's Federation, Helen Keaysian, Executive Committee Member of the Canadian Women's Congress, and Grace Ayensu, Councilor of Sekondi-Takoradi City in Ghana, arrived in Beijing on the 21st and 22nd respectively.

### 国际妇联代表团团员穆迪迪约夫人等到京

1959-09-24
第6版()
专栏：

    国际妇联代表团团员穆迪迪约夫人等到京
    据新华社23日讯　应中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会邀请来我国参加国庆观礼的国际民主妇女联合会代表团团员：国际民主妇女联合会理事、印度尼西亚议会共产党议会党团议员、印度尼西亚妇女运动协会副主席穆迪迪约夫人和日本福岛母亲会议事务局长高田琴子，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团团员：澳大利亚妇女联合会全国主席诺琳·休伊特，加拿大妇女大会执行委员海伦·凯西安和加纳的塞孔迪—塔科腊迪市议会议员格雷斯·阿延苏等，已经分别于21日和22日到达北京。
