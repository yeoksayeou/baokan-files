Title: Female Athletes Reach One-Third
Author:
Date: 1959-09-13
Page: 6

Although there are no women's events in twelve sports such as soccer, water polo, polo, baseball, and wrestling, the total number of female athletes participating in the National Games is still 3,670, accounting for one-third of all athletes. This number reflects the increasing number of women participating in sports in our country. Among the 29 delegations, Guizhou has the most female athletes, with a total of 163. Followed by Guangxi with 152, Shanghai with 151, and Sichuan and Yunnan with 150 each.

### 女选手达三分之一

1959-09-13
第6版()
专栏：

    女选手达三分之一
    足球、水球、马球、棒球、摔跤等十二个项目是没有女子比赛的。但是参加全运会的女选手总计仍然有三千六百七十人，占到全部选手人数的三分之一。这个数字反映了我国女子参加体育运动的人数已越来越多。二十九个代表团中，贵州的女选手最多，共有一百六十三人。其次是广西，共有一百五十二人，上海有一百五十一人，四川和云南都有一百五十人。
