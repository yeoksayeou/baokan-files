Title: Burmese Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, September 27th. U Chit Thoung, the Burmese government representative and Minister of Industry, Mines, Culture and Labour of Burma, arrived in Beijing by plane today, leading the Burmese Cultural Delegation, to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.
    Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Directors of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Zhixiang and Ding Xilin were at the airport to welcome them.
    Burmese Ambassador to China Kyaw Winn also went to the airport to greet them.

### 缅甸文化代表团到京

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    缅甸文化代表团到京
    新华社27日讯　缅甸政府代表、缅甸工业、矿务、文化和劳工部长吴漆东，今天率领缅甸文化代表团乘飞机到达北京，前来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼。
    到机场欢迎的，有陈毅副总理兼外交部长，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥、丁西林等人。
    缅甸驻华大使叫温也到机场迎接。
