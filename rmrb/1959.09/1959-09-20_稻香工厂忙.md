Title: Busy Rice Fragrance Factory
Author: Huang Jixiang
Date: 1959-09-20
Page: 12

　　Rice Fragrance Factory Busy
　　Huang Jixiang
　　
　　Golden waves roll in the fields,
　　The fragrance of rice blossoms travels far,
　　A bumper harvest beckons,
　　Busy agriculture, busy industry too.
　　On the mountaintop north of the city,
　　The farm tool processing factory,
　　Rushes to make "golden sickles,"
　　Labor is very intense.
　　The bellows puff and call,
　　The red furnace flashes with rosy light,
　　Iron hammers dance like dragons,
　　A clang echoes all around.
　　Not afraid of aching arms,
　　Not afraid of the sweltering heat,
　　With one heart for the autumn harvest,
　　Resolutely fight for victory.
　　Fight hard in August and September,
　　Guarantee quality.
　　Workers are like soldiers,
　　The workshop is like a battlefield.
　　Each hammer, one sickle,
　　Each hammer, ten thousand catties of grain.
　　Workers and peasants unite tightly,
　　Every year the granaries are full.

### 稻香工厂忙
黄及翔
1959-09-20
第12版()
专栏：

    稻香工厂忙
    黄及翔田垅翻金浪，稻花十里香，丰收在招手，农忙工亦忙。城北山顶上，农具加工厂，赶制“摆金镰”，劳动多紧张。风箱噗噗叫，红炉闪霞光，铁锤如龙舞，一片响叮当。不怕胳膊疼，不怕热难当，一心为三秋，坚决打胜仗。大战八九月，保证合质量。工人像战士，车间如战场。一锤一把镰，一锤万斤粮。工农团结紧，年年谷满仓。
