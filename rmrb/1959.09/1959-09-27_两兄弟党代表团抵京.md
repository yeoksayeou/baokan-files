Title: Delegations from Two Fraternal Parties Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-27
Page: 1

    Iraqi Communist Party Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, September 26th. The Iraqi Communist Party delegation, led by Comrade Mohammed Hussein Abu Iss, which was invited by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.
    Welcoming them at the airport were Tan Zhenlin, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat, and Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee.
    German Communist Party Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, September 26th. The German Communist Party delegation, headed by Karl, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the German Communist Party, and two members of the Central Committee, who were invited by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.
    Welcoming them at the airport were Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat, and Nie Rongzhen and Wu Xiuquan, members of the Central Committee.

### 两兄弟党代表团抵京

1959-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    两兄弟党代表团抵京
    伊拉克共产党代表团
    新华社26日讯　应中国共产党中央委员会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的伊拉克共产党代表团代表穆罕默德·侯赛因·阿布·伊斯等同志，于今日上午乘飞机到达北京。
    在机场欢迎的，有中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记谭震林、中央委员伍修权。
    德国共产党代表团
    新华社26日讯　应中国共产党中央委员会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的德国共产党代表团团长、德国共产党中央政治局委员卡尔，和代表团团员：两位中央委员，于今日上午乘飞机到达北京。
    在飞机场欢迎的，有中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记彭真，中央委员聂荣臻、伍修权。
