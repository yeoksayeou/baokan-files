Title: Japanese-China Friendship Association Vice President Kanzo Uchiyama and his Wife Arrive in Beijing to Attend China's National Day Ceremony

Author:
Date: 1959-09-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 19th - Kanzo Uchiyama, Vice President of the Japanese-China Friendship Association, and his wife, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China and attend the National Day celebrations, arrived in Beijing today.

### 应邀参加我国庆观礼  日中友协副会长内山完造夫妇到京

1959-09-20
第6版()
专栏：

    应邀参加我国庆观礼
    日中友协副会长内山完造夫妇到京
    新华社19日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问并参加国庆观礼的日中友好协会副会长内山完造夫妇，已在今天到达北京。
