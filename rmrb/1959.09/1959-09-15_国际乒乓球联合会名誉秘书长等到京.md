Title: ITTF Honorary Secretary-General Arrives in Beijing

Author:

Date: 1959-09-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 14th – Invited to visit the First National Games of the People’s Republic of China, the Honorary Secretary-General of the International Table Tennis Federation (ITTF) Roy Evans, Mrs. Roy Evans, consultant to the ITTF, the Honorary Treasurer of the ITTF, the Chairman of the English Table Tennis Association, Mr. Wint, and Mrs. Wint arrived in Beijing on the 14th.

### 国际乒乓球联合会名誉秘书长等到京

1959-09-15
第5版()
专栏：

    国际乒乓球联合会名誉秘书长等到京
    新华社14日讯　邀请来我国参观中华人民共和国第一届运动会的国际乒乓球联合会名誉秘书长罗依·伊凡斯、国际乒乓球联合会咨询委员罗伊·伊凡斯夫人、国际乒乓球联合会名誉会计英格兰乒乓球协会主席文特及文特夫人于14日到达北京。
