Title: Czechoslovak Celebrations Enter Climax
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 25th: The grand commemorative activities in Czechoslovakia celebrating the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China have entered a climax starting today. Various celebration gatherings are being widely held across the country.

The Central Committee of the Slovak National Front held a celebration rally in Bratislava on the evening of the 25th. Bačilek, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Slovakia, David, member of the Political Bureau of the CPCz Central Committee, and Štrechaj, alternate member of the Political Bureau and Chairman of the Slovak Administrative Council, attended the meeting. David also gave a report on the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China at the meeting.

In his report, David pointed out that the Great Leap Forward launched in China last year had achieved outstanding results. Thanks to the success of the people's communes, a real Great Leap Forward has been achieved in rural China. He expressed his belief that the Chinese people would realize the slogan of catching up with and surpassing Britain in the production of major industrial products ahead of schedule.

Ge Buhai, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia, was invited to attend the celebration and spoke at the meeting.

In Tábor, a cultural city in Czechoslovakia, the opening ceremony of the Tábor County "Czechoslovak-Chinese Friendship Week" was held on the evening of the 25th. Gao Renrong, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia, was invited to attend the opening ceremony and spoke.

As part of the "Czechoslovak-Chinese Friendship Week," Tábor County also held an exhibition of Chinese books, publications, pictures, and commodities, and will screen the Chinese film "Naval Battle."

In Karlovy Vary, a health resort and glass manufacturing center in Czechoslovakia, a celebration party was also held on the evening of the 25th. The head of the Karlovy Vary State Committee of the CPCz and representatives of the Chinese Embassy spoke at the meeting.

### 捷庆祝活动进入高潮

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    捷庆祝活动进入高潮
    新华社布拉格25日电　捷克斯洛伐克各地庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年的盛大纪念活动，从今天起进入高潮。各种庆祝集会开始在各地广泛举行。
    斯洛伐克民族阵线中央委员会25日晚上在布拉迪斯拉发举行了庆祝集会。捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央政治局委员、斯洛伐克共产党中央第一书记巴契列克，捷共中央政治局委员戴维，政治局候补委员、斯洛伐克行政委员会主席斯特雷哈伊等出席了会议。戴维并在会上作了关于庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年的报告。
    戴维在报告中指出，中国去年掀起的大跃进取得了出色的成就。由于人民公社的成功，中国农村中实现了真正的大跃进。他表示相信，中国人民将提前实现在主要工业产品生产方面赶上和超过英国的口号。
    中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使馆参赞葛步海应邀出席了庆祝会，并在会上讲了话。
    在捷克斯洛伐克文化古城塔布尔，25日晚上举行了塔布尔县“捷中友好周”的开幕式。中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使馆商务参赞高仁融应邀出席开幕式并讲了话。
    作为“捷中友好周”的部分内容，塔布尔县还举办了中国书刊、图片和商品展览会，并将上映中国电影“海魂”等。
    在捷克斯洛伐克休养胜地、玻璃制造工业中心卡罗维发利，25日晚上也举行了庆祝晚会。捷共卡罗维发利州委员会负责人和中国大使馆代表都在会上讲了话。
