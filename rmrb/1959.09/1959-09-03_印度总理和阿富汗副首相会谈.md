Title: Indian Prime Minister Meets with Afghan Deputy Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1959-09-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, September 1st. According to today's "Times of India" report, Indian Prime Minister Nehru and Afghan Deputy Prime Minister Sardar Mohammed Naim Khan held an hour-long meeting at the Indian Prime Minister's residence on the afternoon of August 31. They discussed the international situation and issues of common interest to both countries.

### 印度总理和阿富汗副首相会谈

1959-09-03
第6版()
专栏：

    印度总理和阿富汗副首相会谈
    新华社新德里1日电　据“印度时报”今天报道，印度总理尼赫鲁和阿富汗副首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆汗8月31日下午在印度总理官邸举行了一小时的会谈。他们讨论了国际局势和有关两国共同利益的问题。
