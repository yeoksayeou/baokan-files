Title: New Finnish Ambassador to China, Hugo Valvanne, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 10th—Hugo Valvanne, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Finland to China, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

Zhang Shijie, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was at the airport to welcome him.

Nevalainen, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Finnish Embassy in China, and embassy officials were also at the airport to welcome him.

### 芬兰新任驻华大使  雨果·瓦尔万尼到京

1959-09-11
第3版()
专栏：

    芬兰新任驻华大使
    雨果·瓦尔万尼到京
    新华社10日讯　芬兰共和国新任驻华特命全权大使雨果·瓦尔万尼在今天乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有外交部礼宾司副司长张世杰。
    芬兰驻华大使馆临时代办耐瓦莱宁和大使馆官员也前往机场欢迎。
