Title: Afghan Deputy Prime Minister Naim Sets Off for Friendly Visit to Our Country at Our Invitation
Author:
Date: 1959-09-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, August 31st: At the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Mohammad Naim, and his entourage left Kabul by plane this morning for a friendly visit to China.
    Those who saw him off at the airport included: Afghan Prime Minister Daud, First Deputy Prime Minister Ali Mohammad, ministers and the Chief of Staff.
    Li Yuchi, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Afghanistan, and embassy staff also saw him off at the airport.
    Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Afghanistan also saw him off at the airport.

### 应邀来我国进行友好访问  阿富汗副首相纳伊姆启程

1959-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国进行友好访问
    阿富汗副首相纳伊姆启程
    新华社31日讯　喀布尔消息：应中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来的邀请，阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣穆罕默德·纳伊姆和他的随行人员今天上午乘飞机离开喀布尔，前往中国进行友好访问。
    到机场送行的有：阿富汗首相达乌德、第一副首相阿里·穆罕默德、各部部长和参谋总长。
    中国驻阿富汗大使馆临时代办李玉池和使馆人员也到机场送行。
    到机场送行的还有各国驻阿富汗的外交使节。
