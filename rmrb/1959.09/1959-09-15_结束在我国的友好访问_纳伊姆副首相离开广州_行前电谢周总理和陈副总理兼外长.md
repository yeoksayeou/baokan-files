Title: Deputy Prime Minister Naim Leaves Guangzhou After Concluding Friendly Visit to Our Country, Sends Telegram of Thanks to Premier Zhou and Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Before Departure
Author:
Date: 1959-09-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 14th: Sardar Mohammad Naim, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, and his entourage, concluded their friendly visit to our country and left Guangzhou by special train at 11:30 this morning.

Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of our country, Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province, and more than 1,000 people from all walks of life in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou Municipality went to the station to see off the distinguished guests.

Today, the Guangzhou Railway Station was decorated with the national flags of China and Afghanistan, as well as colorful flags. At 11:20, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Ji Pengfei, Chen Yu and others, arrived at the station. Beside the carriages, Vice Minister Ji Pengfei, Governor Chen Yu and others warmly shook hands with the distinguished guests, asking them to convey the sincere friendship of the Chinese people to the Afghan people.

Also at the station to see off the distinguished guests today were Hao Ting, our country's ambassador to the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Lin Qiangyun, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, Jiao Linyi, Sun Leyi, and Zhang Ruiquan, Vice Mayors of Guangzhou Municipality, Lieutenant General Zhan Caifang, head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou, and heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations.

The heads of the consulates in Guangzhou of Czechoslovakia, Poland, and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam also went to the station to see them off today.

Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of our country, and Yang Kezhong, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the People's Committee of Guangdong Province, accompanied the distinguished guests to Shenzhen. Abdul Samad, Ambassador of the Kingdom of Afghanistan to our country, accompanied Prince Naim and others to Hong Kong.

The distinguished guests arrived in Guangzhou by plane at 11:00 this morning from Wuhan, accompanied by Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of our country, Hao Ting, our country's ambassador to Afghanistan, and Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, September 14th: Sardar Mohammad Naim, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, and his entourage, left Wuhan by special plane this morning for Guangzhou.

Zhang Tixue, Governor of Hubei Province, Chen Li, Vice Governor, Liu Huinong, Mayor of Wuhan, Hua Yuqing, Vice Mayor, Zhang Guangcai, Major General, head of the Wuhan Garrison, and others went to the airport to see off the distinguished guests.

Xinhua News Agency, September 14th: Premier Zhou Enlai received a telegram from Deputy Prime Minister Naim of the Kingdom of Afghanistan upon his departure from our country. In the telegram, Deputy Prime Minister Naim expressed his gratitude for the welcome and reception he received in various parts of China. Deputy Prime Minister Naim's telegram is as follows: To His Excellency Premier Zhou Enlai:

    As I am about to leave your great country, I vividly recall the warm welcome and cordial reception we received in various parts of China. This friendly expression is very commensurate with the long-standing friendship between Afghanistan and China. I thank you again, bid you farewell, and wish your great country prosperity.
          Mohammad Naim
          September 14, 1959, Bao'an

Xinhua News Agency, September 14th: Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi received a telegram today from Naim, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of Afghanistan, upon his departure from our country. The text of the telegram is as follows: To His Excellency Marshal Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs:

    On this last day of my stay in China, I fondly recall the various scenes during our visit to your great country. The warm reception and enthusiastic sympathy we received throughout our visit to this friendly country fully illustrate the friendship between our two peoples. Please accept my renewed thanks. I look forward to our next meeting in Kabul.
          Mohammad Naim
          September 14, 1959, Bao'an

### 结束在我国的友好访问  纳伊姆副首相离开广州  行前电谢周总理和陈副总理兼外长

1959-09-15
第5版()
专栏：

    结束在我国的友好访问
    纳伊姆副首相离开广州
    行前电谢周总理和陈副总理兼外长
    新华社广州14日电　阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆亲王和他的随行人员，结束了在我国的友好访问，在今天上午十一时三十分乘专车离开广州。
    我国外交部副部长姬鹏飞、广东省省长陈郁和广东省、广州市各界人民一千多人到车站欢送贵宾。
    今天，广州车站上悬挂着中国和阿富汗两国国旗，及五色缤纷的彩旗。十一时二十分，贵宾们在姬鹏飞、陈郁等人陪同下来到车站。在车厢旁，姬鹏飞副部长和陈郁省长等同贵宾们亲切握手告别，请贵宾们把中国人民的诚挚友谊带给阿富汗人民。
    今天到车站欢送贵宾的，还有我国驻阿富汗王国大使郝汀，广东省副省长林锵云，广州市副市长焦林义、孙乐宜、张瑞权，中国人民解放军驻广州部队首长詹才芳中将以及各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人等。
    捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、越南民主共和国驻广州领事馆的负责人今天也到车站送行。
    我国外交部礼宾司副司长马振武、广东省人民委员会外事处副处长杨可忠陪同贵宾到深圳。阿富汗王国驻我国大使阿卜杜勒·萨马德陪同纳伊姆亲王等到香港。
    贵宾们是在我国外交部副部长姬鹏飞、我国驻阿富汗大使郝汀、外交部礼宾司副司长马振武等陪同下，在今天上午十一时由武汉乘飞机到达广州的。
    据新华社武汉14日电　阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆亲王和他的随行人员，今天早晨乘专机离开武汉前往广州。
    到机场欢送贵宾的有湖北省省长张体学、副省长陈离，武汉市市长刘惠农、副市长华煜卿，武汉驻军首长张广才少将等。
    新华社14日讯　周恩来总理收到了阿富汗王国纳伊姆副首相在离开我国的时候发来的一份电报。纳伊姆副首相在电报中对他在中国各地受到的欢迎和招待表示感谢。纳伊姆副首相的来电如下：周恩来总理阁下：
    在即将离开你们伟大的国家的时候，我生动地回忆起我们在中国各地所受到的热烈欢迎和亲切招待。这种友好的表示同阿富汗和中国之间的悠久的友谊是十分相称的。我再一次向你致谢，告别，并且祝你们伟大的国家繁荣。
          穆罕默德·纳伊姆
          1959年9月14日于宝安
    新华社14日讯　陈毅副总理兼外交部长今天收到了阿富汗副首相兼外交大臣纳伊姆在离开我国时发来的一份电报。电文如下：外交部长陈毅元帅阁下：
    当这在中国逗留的最后一天，我留恋地回忆起我们在你们伟大的国家访问期间的各种情景。我们在这个友好的国家的整个访问期间所受到的殷切的招待和热烈的同情，充分地说明了我们两国人民之间的友谊。请接受我再一次的感谢。我期待着我们下一次在喀布尔的会晤。
          穆罕默德·纳伊姆
          1959年9月14日于宝安
