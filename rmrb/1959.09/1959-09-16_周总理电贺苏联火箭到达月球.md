Title: Premier Zhou Sends Telegram Congratulating the Soviet Rocket on Reaching the Moon
Author:
Date: 1959-09-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 15th — Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Nikita Khrushchev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, warmly congratulating the Soviet Union on the successful arrival of its second cosmic rocket to the moon. The telegram is as follows:

To Comrade N.S. Khrushchev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Moscow
Dear Comrade Khrushchev,

Delighted to learn that the second cosmic rocket launched by the Soviet Union has successfully reached the moon, I, on behalf of the Chinese people and the Chinese government, extend the warmest congratulations to you and, through you, to the great Soviet people and the Soviet government.

The Soviet cosmic rocket's arrival on the moon marks the beginning of mankind's ascent from Earth to other celestial bodies. This has made an outstanding contribution to the development of mankind's latest science and technology. The great achievements of Soviet science have once again demonstrated to the world the powerful vitality and unparalleled superiority of the socialist system.

This major victory of the Soviet people has greatly strengthened the power of the socialist camp headed by the great Soviet Union, and will further inspire the confidence of all peace-loving countries and peoples around the world in their struggle for world peace.

Zhou Enlai
Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
September 15, 1959, in Beijing

### 周总理电贺苏联火箭到达月球

1959-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺苏联火箭到达月球
    新华社15日讯　国务院总理周恩来致电苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫，热烈祝贺苏联发射的第二个宇宙火箭成功地到达月球。贺电如下：莫斯科苏联部长会议主席尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫同志亲爱的赫鲁晓夫同志：
    欣悉苏联发射的第二个宇宙火箭成功地到达了月球，我代表中国人民和中国政府向你，并且通过你向伟大的苏联人民和苏联政府表示最热烈的祝贺。
    苏联宇宙火箭到达月球，标志着人类从地球攀登其他天体的开端。这对人类最新科学技术的发展作出了卓越贡献。苏联科学的巨大成就，又一次向全世界显示了社会主义制度的强大生命力和无比优越性。
    苏联人民的这一重大胜利，大大地加强了以伟大苏联为首的社会主义阵营的力量，从而必将进一步鼓舞全世界一切爱好和平的国家和人民为争取世界和平而斗争的信心。
      中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                  1959年9月15日于北京
