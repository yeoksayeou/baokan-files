Title: Ambassador Liu Xiao, on behalf of the Chinese Government, Presents Friendship Medals to Soviet Experts
Author:
Date: 1959-09-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 24th. Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Liu Xiao, on behalf of the Chinese government, presented Sino-Soviet Friendship Medals on September 24th to a group of Soviet experts who had assisted in construction in China.
    More than seventy Soviet experts who had worked in China attended the ceremony, along with Kapitsa, Deputy Director of the Far East Department of the Soviet Foreign Ministry, and officials from the Soviet State Committee for Foreign Economic Relations.

### 刘晓大使代表我国政府  赠给苏联专家友谊纪念章

1959-09-26
第2版()
专栏：

    刘晓大使代表我国政府
    赠给苏联专家友谊纪念章
    据新华社莫斯科24日电　中国驻苏联大使刘晓24日代表中国政府将中苏友谊纪念章赠给一批曾在中国帮助建设的苏联专家。
    参加仪式的有七十多名曾在中国工作过的苏联专家，苏联外交部远东司副司长卡皮察和苏联国家对外经济关系委员会的负责人。
