Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into Territorial Waters off Pingtan, Fujian Province; We Issue 69th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1959-09-23
Page: 3

    U.S. Warship Intrudes into Territorial Waters off Pingtan, Fujian Province
    We Issue 69th Serious Warning
    Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd: At 11:20 to 16:00 on September 22nd, a U.S. warship intruded into the waters off Pingtan, Fujian Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 69th serious warning.

### 美舰侵入我福建平潭地区领海  我提出第69次严重警告

1959-09-23
第3版()
专栏：

    美舰侵入我福建平潭地区领海
    我提出第69次严重警告
    新华社22日讯　9月22日十一时二十分至十六时，美国军舰一艘侵入我国福建省平潭地区海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第六十九次严重警告。
