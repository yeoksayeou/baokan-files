Title: Mid-Autumn New Moon
Author: Yuan Shuipai
Date: 1959-09-17
Page: 12

    Mid-Autumn New Moon
    Yuan Shuipai
    The moon is especially bright at Mid-Autumn,
    This year's moon is even more spirited,
    A divine arrow pierces the clouds for thirty thousand miles,
    The red flag flutters in the heart of the moon!
    The people's power is great,
    Turning earth into gold with a touch,
    Pointing to the sky to open roads,
    Knocking on the moon, and the moon opens its doors.
    For thousands of years, the desolate moon has only shone on human misfortune,
    Today, the round moon is a joyful sight of universal celebration,
    Celebrating the Soviet Union for building another remarkable feat for humanity,
    Pretty Chang'e, holding the national emblem, circles the heavens.
    Marxism-Leninism is good,
    Victorious in every battle,
    Protect world peace!
    March towards the universe!
                        September 15th (with picture)
    Good scenery for this year's Mid-Autumn Festival Jiang Fan

### 中秋新月
袁水拍
1959-09-17
第12版()
专栏：

    中秋新月
    袁水拍
    月到中秋分外明，
    今年月色更精神，
    神箭穿云卅万里，
    红旗飘飘月中心！
    人民力量大，
    点土土成金，
    指天天辟路，
    叩月月开门。
    几千年凄凉月尽照人间不幸，
    今日里月团圝喜看普天同庆，
    庆苏联为人类再建奇功，
    俏嫦娥捧国徽周天绕行。
    马列主义好，
    百战无不胜，
    保世界和平！
    向宇宙进军！

    今年中秋好景色　　江帆
