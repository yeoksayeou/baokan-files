Title: Soviet, Hungarian, and Argentine Astronomers See Signs of Red Rocket Reaching the Moon
Author:
Date: 1959-09-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 17th: When the Soviet Union's second cosmic rocket reached the moon at the predicted time, astronomers in the Soviet Union, Hungary, Argentina, and other countries observed traces of the rocket landing at the rocket's predicted landing site. According to a report in the Soviet "Labor Daily" on the 16th, the Kharkov Observatory in the Soviet Union saw signs of light in the area between the "Sea of ​​Clarity," "Sea of ​​Tranquility," and "Sea of ​​Vapors" when the rocket reached the moon. Scientists are currently studying this material.

Scientists at the Astronomical Observatory of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences in Budapest saw dust rise from the moon when the rocket hit the moon. They said that after the radio signals from the rocket stopped due to the rocket colliding with the moon, a "black circle" could be seen on the moon for more than an hour. Scientists estimate that this is a new crater caused by the rocket hitting the soft surface of the moon's mud, sand, and rocks. The Urania Observatory in Budapest is now using a twenty-centimeter telescope to search for this trace.

Observers at the San Miguel Observatory in Argentina saw the rocket collide with the lunar surface. The director of the observatory, Juan Bussolini, said that when he was observing the moon with a Zeiss equatorial instrument with a diameter of 200 mm and a magnification of 500 times, he saw a semi-bright and semi-dark shadow in the area where the rocket landing was later announced, and then saw an annular black shadow constantly expanding. Another observer confirmed Bussolini's observations.

### 苏联匈牙利和阿根廷天文学家  看到红色火箭到达月球迹象

1959-09-19
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联匈牙利和阿根廷天文学家
    看到红色火箭到达月球迹象
    新华社17日讯　当苏联第二个宇宙火箭按预定的时间达到月球的时候，苏联、匈牙利和阿根廷等国的天文学家在火箭预定的降落地点观测到火箭着陆的痕迹。据苏联“劳动报”十六日报道：苏联哈尔科夫天文台在火箭达到月球时，在火箭预定降落的“澄海”、“静海”和“汽海”之间的区域内，看到了光亮的迹象。目前，科学家正在对这一材料进行研究。
    布达佩斯的匈牙利科学院天文台的科学家在火箭碰到月球时，看到月球上升起尘土。他们说，当火箭上的无线电讯号因火箭同月球相碰而停止以后，在一个多小时以内可以看到月球上有一个“黑圈”。据科学家估计，这是由于火箭撞在月球的泥沙和石块的松软表面上而造成的新的洞坑。现在，布达佩斯乌拉尼亚天文台正在用一架二十厘米口径的望远镜寻找这个痕迹。
    阿根廷圣米格尔天文台的观测人员看到了火箭与月面相撞。这个天文台的台长胡安·布索利尼说，他在用口径二百毫米和放大五百倍的蔡斯赤道仪观察月球的时候，在后来宣布火箭降落的那个地区，看到了一个半明半暗的影子，后来看到了一个环状黑影不断扩大。另一位观测员证实了布索利尼的观测结果。
