Title: Typhoon Emergency Alert
Author:
Date: 1959-09-11
Page: 4

    Typhoon Emergency Alert
    Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a weak typhoon emergency alert at 8:00 AM on the 10th.

    A weak typhoon has appeared in the South China Sea. At 2:00 AM this morning, the center was located about 200 kilometers south of Hong Kong, around 20.4 degrees north latitude and 114.5 degrees east longitude. The maximum wind force near the center is estimated to be level 8 to 9. The typhoon will move in a north-northwesterly direction. It is expected that the typhoon will make landfall this afternoon in the area between Bao'an and Yangjiang in Guangdong Province. Coastal areas in this region and nearby waters of the South China Sea will experience winds of level 8 to 9, with heavy rain to torrential rain.

### 台风紧急警报

1959-09-11
第4版()
专栏：

    台风紧急警报
    新华社10讯　中央气象科学研究所10日上午8时发布弱台风紧急警报。
    南海上有弱台风出现，今天早上二时中心位置在香港以南约二百公里左右的海面上，就是北纬二十点四度、东经一百一十四点五度附近。中心附近最大风力估计有八级至九级。未来台风将向北偏西方向移动。预计今天下午台风将在广东省宝安到阳江之间的地区登陆，这一带沿海和附近南海海面将有八级至九级大风，并有大雨到暴雨。
