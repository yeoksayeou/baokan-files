Title: Nepalese Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: The Nepalese government delegation led by Dr. Tulsi Giri, Minister of Village Development of the Kingdom of Nepal, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation is visiting Beijing at the invitation of the Chinese government to attend the celebrations for the 10th anniversary of the founding of our country.

Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi welcomed the distinguished guests at the airport.

Members of the delegation include Nara Pratap Thapa, Foreign Affairs Secretary of the Nepalese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Lieutenant General Daman Shamsher Jung Bahadur Rana, Nepalese Ambassador to China. The delegation's secretary is G.M. Singh.

After the band played the national anthems of Nepal and China, Dr. Tulsi Giri, head of the Nepalese government delegation, inspected the army honor guard in the company of Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi.

The distinguished guests accepted flowers from the Young Pioneers of the capital.

Dr. Tulsi Giri, head of the delegation, spoke at the airport. He said, "I bring the friendly and peaceful message of the government and people of Nepal to the government and people of China. The friendship between Nepal and China is long-standing and close, and has developed greatly, especially in the last ten years. I believe that the current friendly relations between our two countries should be a model of close friendship between neighbors. I hope and believe that close contacts like this friendly visit will further strengthen the friendly relations between our two countries." He also said that every country in Asia is deeply interested in China's development and progress, and China's development and progress is a source of pride for every country in Asia.

Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation today were Vice Foreign Ministers Zhang Hanfu and Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Agriculture Liu Ruilong, ministers and vice ministers of other government departments, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, heads of democratic parties and people's organizations, and people from all walks of life in the capital.

Diplomatic envoys of various countries to China also went to the airport to welcome the delegation. (Attached photo)

### 尼泊尔政府代表团到京

1959-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔政府代表团到京
    新华社27日讯　由尼泊尔王国乡村发展大臣图尔西·吉里博士率领的尼泊尔王国政府代表团今天下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团是应我国政府的邀请，前来北京参加我国建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
    贵宾们在机场受到了陈毅副总理兼外交部长的欢迎。
    代表团团员有尼泊尔外交部外事秘书纳腊·普腊塔普·塔帕和尼泊尔驻华大使达曼·沙姆谢尔·忠格·巴哈杜尔·拉纳中将。代表团的秘书是格·姆·辛格。
    乐队奏尼泊尔和中国两国国歌后，尼泊尔王国政府代表团团长图尔西·吉里博士在陈毅副总理兼外交部长陪同下检阅了陆军仪仗队。
    贵宾们接受了首都少年先锋队队员们的献花。
    代表团团长图尔西·吉里博士在机场讲了话。他说，我把尼泊尔政府和人民的友好、和平的讯息带给中国政府和人民。尼泊尔和中国两国之间的友谊是悠久的、亲密的，特别是最近十年来有了巨大的发展。我认为，我们两国之间目前的友好关系应当成为邻国亲密友谊的一个范例。我希望并且相信，像我这次友好访问那样的亲密接触，将会进一步加强我们两国之间的友好关系。他又说，亚洲每一个国家对于中国的发展和进步都深感兴趣，中国的发展和进步，对亚洲每个国家说来都是可以引以为光荣的。
    今天，在机场欢迎的还有外交部副部长章汉夫、罗贵波，农业部副部长刘瑞龙和政府其他各部部长、副部长，中国人民解放军高级将领，各民主党派、人民团体负责人以及首都各界人士。
    各国驻华使节也到机场欢迎。（附图片）
