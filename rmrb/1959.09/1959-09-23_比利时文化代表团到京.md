Title: Belgian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd: A four-member Belgian cultural delegation, led by head of the delegation Jean Absil, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation is visiting our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and will participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 比利时文化代表团到京

1959-09-23
第6版()
专栏：

    比利时文化代表团到京
    新华社22日讯　比利时文化代表团一行四人，在团长让·阿布希率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。代表团是应中国人民对外文化协会邀请，来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
