Title: Gromyko Demands Soviet Disarmament Proposals Be Included on Agenda
Author:
Date: 1959-09-23
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 21st (TASS dispatch): Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko has suggested that the issue of "complete and thorough disarmament" be included on the agenda of the 14th session of the United Nations General Assembly as an important and urgent item.

In a letter to the United Nations Secretariat and an accompanying memorandum, Gromyko pointed out that the reason it is now urgently necessary to consider the issue of complete and thorough disarmament is that the arms race poses a greater threat to peace now than at any time in the past.

The letter and memorandum have been distributed by the United Nations Secretariat.

### 葛罗米柯要求把苏联裁军建议列入议程

1959-09-23
第7版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯要求把苏联裁军建议列入议程
    新华社22日讯　据塔斯社纽约21日讯：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯已经建议把“全面和彻底裁军”问题作为一个重要和迫切的项目列入联合国大会第十四届会议的议程。
    葛罗米柯在一封给联合国秘书处的信件和随函附上的备忘录中指出，目前所以迫切需要考虑全面和彻底裁军的问题，是因为军备竞赛对和平的危害现在比过去任何时候都要大。
    这封信和备忘录已由联合国秘书处分发。
