Title: Vice Prime Minister Naim Holds Talks with Premier Zhou; Distinguished Guests Visit Miyun Reservoir; Ministry of Culture Holds Song and Dance Welcome Soiree
Author:
Date: 1959-09-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: This morning, the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Sardar Mohammad Naim, and Premier Zhou Enlai held talks.

The Afghan side participating in the talks included Minister of Commerce Ghulam Mohammad Sherzad, Afghan Ambassador to China Abdul Samad, Director of the Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Department of the Ministry of Agriculture Doud Mohammad, Director of the Economic Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Sardar Chariman Mahmoud Ghazi, Director of the Plant Protection Department of the Ministry of Agriculture Mohammad Yassim Myel, and Acting Governor of the Rural Industrial and Agricultural Bank, Noor Ali.

The Chinese side participating in the talks included Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Land Reclamation Wang Zhen, Vice Minister of Agriculture Liu Ruilong, and Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan Hao Ting, among others.

The talks proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Sardar Mohammad Naim, and the accompanying distinguished Afghan guests, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, and our country's Ambassador to Afghanistan Hao Ting, visited the largest comprehensive water conservancy project in North China this afternoon—the Miyun Reservoir.

When Deputy Prime Minister Naim and other distinguished Afghan guests arrived at the reservoir construction site, they were warmly welcomed by Qian Zhengying, Vice Minister of Water Resources and Electric Power, Wang Xian, the General Commander of the reservoir construction site, and other responsible persons.

During the visit, Deputy Prime Minister Naim listened to Wang Xian's introduction to the reservoir project. Accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi, Deputy Prime Minister Naim ascended the Baihe Dam of the reservoir to look out. Afterwards, he took a boat tour in the reservoir. The distinguished guests visited the Miyun Reservoir for more than four hours.

During the visit, Deputy Prime Minister Naim wrote an inscription for the reservoir headquarters. In his inscription, he wrote enthusiastically: "The spirit of voluntary cooperation of the people is a major factor in achieving this success. The tremendous speed of completing this project clearly demonstrates the unwavering determination of the Chinese people to achieve the grand national ideals. I would like to especially thank His Excellency Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China for his graciousness in giving me the opportunity to see firsthand a part of the great construction projects underway in China."

Afghan Ambassador to China Samad accompanied Deputy Prime Minister Naim on his visit today.

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China held a song and dance soiree tonight to welcome the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Sardar Mohammad Naim, and all the distinguished Afghan guests who came to visit our country.

Vice Premier Chen Yi accompanied the distinguished Afghan guests to watch tonight's song and dance performance. Afghan Ambassador to China Abdul Samad was invited to attend the soiree.

After the performance, Deputy Prime Minister Naim, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Ding Xilin, and our country's Ambassador to Afghanistan Hao Ting, stepped onto the stage amidst warm applause, shook hands cordially with the performers, and presented them with flower baskets.

### 纳伊姆副首相和周总理会谈  贵宾们参观密云水库　文化部举行歌舞欢迎晚会

1959-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    纳伊姆副首相和周总理会谈
    贵宾们参观密云水库　文化部举行歌舞欢迎晚会
    新华社7日讯　今天上午，阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆亲王和周恩来总理举行了会谈。
    阿富汗方面参加会谈的，有商业大臣吴拉姆·穆罕默德·舍尔扎德，阿富汗驻中国大使阿卜杜勒·萨马德，农业部畜牧兽医司司长杜德·穆罕默德，外交部经济处处长萨达尔·查尔曼·马哈茂德·加齐，农业部植物保养司司长穆罕默德·亚西姆·迈埃勒，乡村工农业银行代行长努尔·阿里。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有国务院副总理陈毅，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，农垦部部长王震，农业部副部长刘瑞龙，中国驻阿富汗大使郝汀等。
    会谈在友好的气氛中进行。
    新华社7日讯　阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆亲王和随同他来访的阿富汗贵宾，在周恩来总理、陈毅副总理、北京市副市长吴晗和我国驻阿富汗大使郝汀陪同下，今天下午参观了华北最大的综合水利工程——密云水库。
    纳伊姆副首相和其他阿富汗贵宾到达水库工地的时候，受到水利电力部副部长钱正英，水库工地总指挥王宪等负责人的热烈欢迎。
    纳伊姆副首相在参观中听取了王宪对水库工程的介绍。在周恩来总理和陈毅副总理等陪同下，纳伊姆副首相登上水库白河大坝眺望。以后，乘船在水库内游览。贵宾们在密云水库参观游览了四个多小时。
    纳伊姆副首相在参观中为水库指挥部题词。他在题词中热情地写道：“人民的自动合作精神是取得这个成就的重大因素。完成这个工程的巨大速度，清楚地显示了中国人民为要达到宏伟的全民理想的不可动摇的决心，我愿特别感谢中华人民共和国周恩来总理阁下，他的盛情使我有机会亲眼看到在中国进行着的巨大建设工程的一部分。”
    阿富汗驻华大使萨马德今天陪同纳伊姆副首相参观。
    新华社7日讯　中华人民共和国文化部今晚举行歌舞晚会，欢迎阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆亲王和随同他来我国访问的全体阿富汗贵宾。
    陈毅副总理陪同阿富汗贵宾观看了今晚的歌舞表演。阿富汗驻中国大使阿卜杜勒·萨马德应邀出席了晚会。
    演出结束后，纳伊姆副首相由陈毅副总理、文化部部长沈雁冰、对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林、我国驻阿富汗大使郝汀等陪同，在热烈的掌声中登上舞台，同演员们亲切握手，并且向他们赠送了花篮。纳伊姆副首相和周恩来总理会谈　新华社记者　邹健东摄
