Title: Albanian New Ambassador Parifiti Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd. The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Albania to China, Parifiti, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane today. Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was at the airport to welcome them.

All the diplomats of the Albanian Embassy in China were also at the airport to welcome them.

### 阿尔巴尼亚新任大使帕里夫蒂到京

1959-09-24
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚新任大使帕里夫蒂到京
    新华社23日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国新任驻华特命全权大使帕里夫蒂和他的夫人今天乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有外交部礼宾司副司长马振武。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻华使馆全体外交官也到机场欢迎。
