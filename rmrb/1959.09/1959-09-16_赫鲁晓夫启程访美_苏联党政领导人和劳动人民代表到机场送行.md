Title: Khrushchev Departs for the United States; Soviet Party and Government Leaders and Representatives of the Working People See Him Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1959-09-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 15th: Soviet Premier Nikita Khrushchev and his family left Moscow today aboard a "Tu-114" special plane for Washington for an official visit at the invitation of U.S. President Dwight D. Eisenhower.

Accompanying Khrushchev on his visit to the United States were: Soviet Foreign Minister Andrei Gromyko, Soviet Minister of Higher and Secondary Specialized Education Yelyutin, Chairman of the Soviet State Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhukov, Director of the Main Administration for the Utilization of Atomic Energy under the Soviet Council of Ministers Yemelyanov, writer Mikhail Sholokhov, Chairman of the Dnepropetrovsk National Economic Council of the Ukrainian Republic Tikhonov, Professor Markov of the Collegium of the Soviet Ministry of Health, as well as Ilyichev, Satyukov, Adzhubey, Shusky, Lebedev, Troyanovsky, Shevchenko, Soldatov, Grubyakov, Tupolev, Kharlamov, Molochkov, Lonkov, Kovalev, and Vinogradov.

During his visit to the United States, Khrushchev will hold talks with U.S. President Eisenhower. Khrushchev will visit the cities of Washington, New York, Los Angeles, San Francisco, Des Moines, and Pittsburgh. He will meet with representatives of U.S. political and business circles and social figures, visit some U.S. industrial and agricultural enterprises and scientific research institutions, and learn about the life of the American people.

During his stay in New York, Khrushchev will deliver a speech at the 14th session of the United Nations General Assembly at the invitation of UN Secretary-General Dag Hammarskjöld.

Today, Vnukovo Airport was decorated with many Soviet flags, and many representatives of the working people of Moscow came to see Khrushchev off.

Among those who saw him off at the airport were Aristov, Belyayev, Brezhnev, Ignatov, Kirichenko, Mukhitdinov, Suslov, Korotchenko, Kalnberzin, Kirilenko, Kosygin, Mazurov, Mzhavanadze, Podgorny, Polyansky, Deputy Chairmen of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Paleckis and Rakhmatov, Deputy Chairmen of the Soviet Council of Ministers Zasyadko and Ustinov, Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Lobanov, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Georgadze, as well as ministers of various ministries of the Soviet government, members and alternate members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, chairmen of various state committees of the Soviet Union, marshals of the Soviet Union, etc.

Also present at the airport to see him off were diplomatic envoys of various countries to the Soviet Union, U.S. Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to the Soviet Union Freers, as well as many Soviet and foreign journalists.

Those seeing him off warmly congratulated Khrushchev and his entourage and wished them a safe journey.

### 赫鲁晓夫启程访美  苏联党政领导人和劳动人民代表到机场送行

1959-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫启程访美
    苏联党政领导人和劳动人民代表到机场送行
    新华社莫斯科15日电　苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫和他的家属应美国总统艾森豪威尔的邀请，今天乘“图—114”专机离开莫斯科前往华盛顿作正式访问。
    陪同赫鲁晓夫访问美国的有：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯，苏联高等和中等专业教育部部长叶留金，苏联国家对外文化委员会主席茹科夫，苏联部长会议原子能利用总管理局局长叶麦利扬诺夫，作家萧洛霍夫，乌克兰共和国第聂伯罗彼得罗夫斯克国民经济委员会主席吉洪诺夫，苏联卫生部部务委员会委员马尔科夫教授，以及伊利切夫、萨丘科夫、阿朱别伊、舒斯基、列别杰夫、特罗扬诺夫斯基、谢夫钦科、索尔达托夫、格鲁比雅科夫、图波列夫、哈尔拉莫夫、莫洛奇科夫、隆科夫、科瓦廖夫和维诺格拉多夫。
    在美国访问期间，赫鲁晓夫将同美国总统艾森豪威尔会谈。赫鲁晓夫将访问华盛顿、纽约、洛杉矶、旧金山、得梅因、匹兹堡等城市，将会见美国政界、实业界代表和社会名流，参观美国的一些工业企业、农业企业和科学研究机关，了解美国人民的生活。
    在纽约期间，赫鲁晓夫应联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德的邀请，将在联合国第十四届大会上发表演说。
    今天，伏努科夫机场上装饰着许多苏联国旗，莫斯科劳动人民的许多代表都来欢送赫鲁晓夫。
    到机场送行的有阿里斯托夫、别利亚耶夫、勃列日?夫、伊格纳托夫、基里钦科、穆希金诺夫、苏斯洛夫、科罗特钦科、卡恩别尔津、基里连科、柯西金、马祖罗夫、姆日阿瓦纳泽、波德戈尔内、波利扬斯基，苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席帕列茨基斯、拉赫马托夫，苏联部长会议副主席扎夏迪科和乌斯季诺夫，苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席洛巴诺夫，苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书格奥尔加泽，以及苏联政府各部部长、苏联共产党中央委员会委员和候补委员、苏联各国家委员会主席、苏联元帅等。
    到机场送行的还有各国驻苏联的外交使节、美国驻苏联临时代办弗里尔斯，以及苏联和外国的许多记者。
    送行的人热烈欢送赫鲁晓夫和他的随行人员，并祝他们一路平安。
