Title: Algerian Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th. The delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, headed by Ben Youssef Ben Khedda, Minister of Social Welfare of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation is in Beijing at the invitation of our government to attend the celebrations of the tenth anniversary of the founding of our country.

Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi greeted the distinguished guests at the airport.

The Deputy Head of the delegation is Ahmed Tewfik El Madani, Minister of Cultural Affairs of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria. Members of the delegation include: Ahmed Abdel Hafid, Government Counselor, Hadj Hilal, Director of the Office of the Minister of Social Welfare, and Rida Malik, Editor-in-Chief of the "Combat" magazine, Special Envoy.

Minister Ben Khedda spoke at the airport on behalf of himself and the Deputy Head of the delegation. He said that the delegation felt very fortunate to be able to come to China to participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the country. The Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria has sent a delegation to visit China for the second time, which shows the great friendship between the people and governments of China and Algeria. He warmly congratulated the People's Republic of China on its freedom, peace, and prosperity.

Speaking about the situation in Algeria, Minister Ben Khedda said that for five years, the Algerian people have been fighting for their freedom and independence. The Algerian people want peace, but not the colonialist "peace" desired by imperialism, but a peace of honor, justice, and national independence.

Before the speech, the band played the national anthems of Algeria and China. Minister Ben Khedda, accompanied by Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, reviewed the guard of honor of the army.

All members of the delegation received flowers from the Young Pioneers.

Those who arrived at the airport today to welcome the delegation included: Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Ji Zhuang, Minister of Internal Affairs Qian Ying, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Zeng Yongquan, ministers and vice-ministers of various ministries, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and people from all walks of life in the capital.

Heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials from various countries in China also came to the airport to welcome the distinguished guests. (Attached photo)

### 阿尔及利亚政府代表团到京

1959-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚政府代表团到京
    新华社27日讯　以阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府社会福利部部长本·优素福·本·赫达为首的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团应我国政府的邀请，来北京参加我国建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
    陈毅副总理兼外交部部长到机场迎接贵宾。
    代表团副团长是阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府文化事务部部长艾哈迈德·陶菲克·迈达尼，团员有：政府参事艾哈迈德·阿卜杜勒·哈菲德，社会福利部部长办公室主任哈吉·希尔沙利，战斗杂志主编、特派员里扎·马利克。
    本·赫达部长以他自己和副团长的名义在机场讲话。他说，代表团对这次能够来中国参加建国十周年的庆祝活动感到非常幸运。阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府已是第二次派代表团来中国访问，这说明中阿两国人民与政府之间的伟大友谊。他热烈祝贺中华人民共和国自由、和平和繁荣。
    本·赫达部长在谈到阿尔及利亚局势时说，五年以来，阿尔及利亚人民为自己的自由、独立进行了斗争。阿尔及利亚人民希望和平，但是这不是帝国主义所要的殖民主义的“和平”，而是一个荣誉的、正义的、民族独立的和平。
    在讲话以前，乐队奏了阿尔及利亚和中国国歌。本·赫达部长由陈毅副总理兼外交部部长陪同，检阅了陆军仪仗队。
    代表团全体团员接受了少年先锋队队员们的献花。
    今天到机场欢迎的有对外贸易部部长叶季壮、内务部部长钱瑛、外交部副部长章汉夫、曾涌泉以及各部部长、副部长，中国人民解放军高级将领，各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人和首都各界人士。
    各国驻华使节和外交官员也到机场迎接贵宾。（附图片）
