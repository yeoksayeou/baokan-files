Title: Soviet Disarmament Proposal Included in UN General Assembly Agenda
Author:
Date: 1959-09-24
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd, New York (TASS) - The Soviet Union's proposal for "complete and general disarmament" has been included in the agenda of the 14th session of the United Nations General Assembly.

At the plenary meeting of the United Nations General Assembly held on the 22nd, the General Committee's proposal to include the Soviet proposal on the UN General Assembly agenda was discussed. The plenary meeting unanimously agreed to include the Soviet proposal as a separate agenda item and refer it to the General Assembly's Political Committee for discussion.

The plenary meeting also decided that the current General Assembly will close on December 5th.

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd, New York (TASS) - The General Committee of the United Nations General Assembly recommended on the 22nd that the General Assembly include the proposal by the Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Khrushchev, on "complete and general disarmament" on the agenda of the current session of the United Nations General Assembly.

### 苏联裁军建议列入联大议程

1959-09-24
第7版()
专栏：

    苏联裁军建议列入联大议程
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社纽约23日讯：苏联提出的“全面和彻底裁军”的建议已经列入联合国大会第十四届会议的议程。
    在22日举行的联合国大会全体会议上，讨论了总务委员会提出的把苏联这个建议列入联大议程的建议，全体会议一致同意把苏联这项建议列为单项议程，并且交给大会政治委员会去讨论。
    全体会议还决定，本届联大在12月5日闭幕。
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社纽约讯：联合国大会总务委员会22日建议大会把苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫提出的关于“全面和彻底裁军”的建议列入联合国大会本届会议的议程。
