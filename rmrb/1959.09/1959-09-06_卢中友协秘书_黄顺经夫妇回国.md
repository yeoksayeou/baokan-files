Title: Secretary of the Luxembourg-China Friendship Association, Mr. and Mrs. Huang Shunjing, Return to Their Country

Author:
Date: 1959-09-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 5th. Secretary of the Luxembourg-China Friendship Association and pianist, Huang Shunjing, and her husband, Malgre, concluded their visit to China and left Beijing by plane this morning to return to their country.

Mr. and Mrs. Huang Shunjing arrived in China on July 24th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries. They visited cities such as Beijing, Wuhan, and Shanghai. Huang Shunjing also held piano concerts in Beijing and Shanghai.

### 卢中友协秘书  黄顺经夫妇回国

1959-09-06
第2版()
专栏：

    卢中友协秘书
    黄顺经夫妇回国
    新华社5日讯　卢森堡中国友好协会秘书、钢琴家黄顺经和她的丈夫马尔格，结束了在我国的访问，在今天早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。
    黄顺经夫妇是应中国人民对外文化协会邀请在7月24日到我国访问的。他们曾访问了北京、武汉、上海等城市；黄顺经并在北京、上海举行了钢琴演奏会。
