Title: Premier Zhou Again Meets with Ishibashi
Author:
Date: 1959-09-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi met with former Japanese Prime Minister Tanzan Ishibashi today for a two-hour meeting.

Liao Chengzhi was present.

### 周总理再接见石桥

1959-09-18
第5版()
专栏：

    周总理再接见石桥
    新华社17日讯　周恩来总理和陈毅副总理今天接见了日本前任首相石桥湛山，进行了两小时的会谈。
    在座的有廖承志。
