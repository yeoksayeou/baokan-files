Title: Our Volleyball Team Arrives in Paris to Participate in the Three Continents Volleyball Championship
Author:
Date: 1959-09-10
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 7th. The Chinese men's volleyball team, participating in the European, Asian, and American Volleyball Championships, arrived here on the evening of the 6th to prepare for the Three Continents competition to be held from the 10th to the 19th.
    When the Chinese volleyball team arrived here, they were welcomed at the airport by officials from the French Volleyball Federation and the French General Confederation of Labour.

### 参加三大洲排球锦标赛  我排球队到达巴黎

1959-09-10
第2版()
专栏：

    参加三大洲排球锦标赛
    我排球队到达巴黎
    新华社巴黎7日电　参加欧洲、亚洲、美洲排球锦标赛的中国男子排球队6日晚到达这里，准备参加10日至19日举行的三大洲比赛。
    中国排球队到达这里时，在机场受到法国排球联合会和法国劳联负责人的欢迎。
