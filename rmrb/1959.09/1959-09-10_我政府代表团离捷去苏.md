Title: Chinese Government Delegation Leaves Czechoslovakia for Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1959-09-10
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 8th: The Chinese Government Delegation led by Vice Premier of the State Council, Xi Zhongxun, left here by plane this afternoon for Moscow. Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included Czechoslovak Minister of General Mechanical Engineering Industry, Polacek, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Hajek, and Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, Cao Ying, and others.

The delegation arrived here on September 4 to attend the opening ceremony of the Brno International Trade Fair. The delegation has visited factories, cooperatives, and some places in the Brno region.

Czechoslovak Deputy Prime Minister Šimůnek received members of the delegation this morning and had friendly talks with them.

### 我政府代表团离捷去苏

1959-09-10
第2版()
专栏：

    我政府代表团离捷去苏
    新华社布拉格8日电　由国务院副总理习仲勋率领的中国政府代表团今天下午乘飞机离开这里前往莫斯科。在机场为代表团送行的有捷克斯洛伐克通用机械工业部部长波拉契克，外交部副部长哈耶克，中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛等。
    代表团是在9月4日到达这里参加布尔诺国际博览会开幕典礼的。代表团曾经访问了工厂、合作社和布尔诺州的一些地方。
    捷克斯洛伐克副总理西穆涅克今天上午接见了代表团人员，并和他们进行了友好的谈话。
