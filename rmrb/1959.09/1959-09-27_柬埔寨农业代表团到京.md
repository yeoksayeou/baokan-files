Title: Cambodian Agricultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th — The Cambodian agricultural delegation, consisting of three members and led by delegation head Xuan Qiang, arrived in Beijing this afternoon at the invitation of the Ministry of Agriculture to visit China and attend the celebrations for the 10th anniversary of the founding of our country.

### 柬埔寨农业代表团到京

1959-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨农业代表团到京
    新华社26日讯　应农业部邀请来我国访问和参加我国建国十周年庆祝典礼的柬埔寨农业代表团一行三人，在团长轩强率领下，今天下午到达北京。
