Title: Foreigners Plot to Assassinate King and Queen: Bomb Explosion in Cambodian Palace
Author:
Date: 1959-09-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, September 1st: A bomb explosion occurred in the Cambodian Royal Palace on August 31st, and the King and Queen of Cambodia narrowly escaped assassination. Prince Norodom Vakrivan and a palace attendant were killed in the explosion, and two attendants were injured.

According to a communique issued by the Privy Council this morning, Norodom Vakrivan, the acting Master of Ceremonies, received a large package delivered by a foreigner on the morning of August 31st. The package contained a "gift" for the Queen from a friend in Hong Kong. Vakrivan took the package to the King to present it to the Queen. After opening the package, the King and Queen left the room to receive Foreign Minister Song San and the Cambodian delegation to the United Nations. After the King and Queen left, a large bomb hidden in the package exploded. Vakrivan was killed, and two attendants were injured. The communique stated that an immediate investigation has been launched to arrest the perpetrators. The communique said, "Undoubtedly, this crime was prepared from outside."

### 外国人图谋暗杀国王和王后  柬埔寨宫廷发生炸弹爆炸

1959-09-03
第6版()
专栏：

    外国人图谋暗杀国王和王后
    柬埔寨宫廷发生炸弹爆炸
    新华社金边1日电　柬埔寨宫廷在8月31日发生炸弹爆炸案，柬埔寨国王和王后险遭杀害。诺罗敦·瓦克里万亲王和一个宫廷侍从被炸死，两个侍从受伤。
    据枢密院今天上午发表的公报说，代理宫廷典礼官诺罗敦·瓦克里万在8月31日上午接到一个外国人送来的一个大包裹，在这个包裹里装着一位香港朋友送给王后的“礼物”。瓦克里万到国王那里把包裹送交王后。在拆开包裹以后，国王和王后就离开那间屋子去接见外交大臣松山和派往联合国的柬埔寨代表团。国王和王后离去后，藏在包裹里的大炸弹就爆炸了。瓦克里万被炸死，两位侍从受伤。公报说，已经立即进行调查以便逮捕罪犯。公报说，“无疑这个罪行是从外部准备的”。
