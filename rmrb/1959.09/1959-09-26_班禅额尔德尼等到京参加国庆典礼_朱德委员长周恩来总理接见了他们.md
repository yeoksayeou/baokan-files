Title: Panchen Erdene Arrives in Beijing to Attend National Day Celebrations; Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai Receive Them
Author:
Date: 1959-09-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th. Panchen Erdene Choskyi Gyaltsen, Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and Vice Chairmen Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal and Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, who came to the capital to attend the 10th anniversary celebration of the founding of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing by special plane this morning.

Vice Premiers of the State Council Ulanhu and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Weihan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Chen Shutong, and more than 400 people from all walks of life in the capital warmly welcomed them at the airport.

Zhou Renshan, Deputy Secretary of the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Party of China, accompanied Panchen Erdene to Beijing.

Standing Committee members of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, Jamyang Gyamtso, Lhalu Tsewang Dorje, Sampo Tenzin Dondup, Ngawang Jinba, as well as Panchen Erdene's family members and entourage, also arrived in Beijing today.

After Panchen Erdene, Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal, Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, and others stepped off the plane amidst warm applause, they presented Hada to Vice Premiers Ulanhu and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairman Li Weihan, and Vice Chairman Chen Shutong. Vice Premier Ulanhu and others returned the Hada, and children from the capital presented flowers to Panchen Erdene and others. Then, accompanied by Xie Fumin, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress, and Liu Chun, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, Panchen Erdene and others took a car to the guesthouse.

Among those who welcomed them at the Capital Airport today were Wang Weizhou, Lu Han, Deng Chumin, Shi Liang, Chen Qiyu, Zhang Su, Zhang Zhizhong, Shao Lizi, Zhao Shoushan, Xu Guangping, Yang Mingxuan, Li Dequan, and other members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and ministers and vice ministers of various ministries of the government, Yang Chengwu, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Wang Feng, Sa Kongliao, Yang Jingren, Xie Hechou, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, and He Chengxiang, Director of the Bureau of Religious Affairs.

Tan Guansan, Deputy Secretary of the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Party of China, also welcomed them at the airport. Cheng Hongyi, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, Xi Rao-Jia-Cuo, President of the Buddhist Association of China, and Da Piao-Sheng, Deputy Director of the Islamic Association of China, all came to the airport to welcome them today.

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th. Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai received Panchen Erdene Choskyi Gyaltsen, Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and Vice Chairmen Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal and Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme this evening.

During the reception, Panchen Erdene, Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal, and Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme presented Hada to Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai. Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai returned the Hada.

Also received were Jamyang Gyamtso, Lhalu Tsewang Dorje, Sampo Tenzin Dondup, Ngawang Jinba, Standing Committee members of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, as well as Panchen Erdene's family members and entourage.

Present were Vice Premiers of the State Council He Long, Ulanhu, and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Weihan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission Wang Feng and Liu Chun, and Deputy Secretaries of the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Party of China Tan Guansan and Zhou Renshan.

### 班禅额尔德尼等到京参加国庆典礼  朱德委员长周恩来总理接见了他们

1959-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    班禅额尔德尼等到京参加国庆典礼
    朱德委员长周恩来总理接见了他们
    新华社25日讯　来首都参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞和副主任委员帕巴拉·卓列朗杰活佛、副主任委员兼秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美，今天上午乘专机到达北京。
    国务院副总理乌兰夫、习仲勋，人大常委会副委员长李维汉，政协全国委员会副主席陈叔通和首都各界人士四百多人到机场热烈欢迎。
    中共西藏工委副书记周仁山陪同班禅额尔德尼来京。
    西藏自治区筹备委员会常务委员詹东·计晋美、拉敏·益喜楚臣、桑颇·才旺仁增、纳旺金巴和班禅额尔德尼的家属、随行官员，也在今天同时到达北京。
    班禅额尔德尼和帕巴拉·卓列朗杰活佛、阿沛·阿旺晋美等在热烈的掌声中走下飞机后，向乌兰夫、习仲勋副总理、李维汉副委员长、陈叔通副主席献了哈达，乌兰夫副总理等回敬了哈达，首都少年儿童向班禅额尔德尼等献了花。然后，由人大民族委员会副主任委员谢扶民、民族事务委员会副主任刘春陪同班禅额尔德尼等乘车前往宾馆。
    今天到首都机场欢迎的，有人大常委会委员和政府各部部长、副部长王维舟、卢汉、邓初民、史良、陈其瑗、张苏、张治中、邵力子、赵寿山、许广平、杨明轩、李德全等，中国人民解放军副总参谋长杨成武，民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、萨空了、杨静仁、谢鹤筹，宗教事务局局长何成湘。
    到机场欢迎的，还有中共西藏工委副书记谭冠三。北京市副市长程宏毅，中国佛教协会会长喜饶嘉错、中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生等人，今天都到机场欢迎。
    新华社25日讯　朱德委员长、周恩来总理今晚接见了西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞和副主任委员帕巴拉·卓列朗杰活佛、副主任委员兼秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美。
    在接见时，班禅额尔德尼和帕巴拉·卓列朗杰、阿沛·阿旺晋美向朱德委员长和周恩来总理献了哈达，朱德委员长、周恩来总理回敬了哈达。
    同时受到接见的还有，西藏自治区筹备委员会常务委员詹东·计晋美、拉敏·益喜楚臣、桑颇·才旺仁增、纳旺金巴以及班禅额尔德尼的家属和随行人员。
    在座的有，国务院副总理贺龙、乌兰夫、习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李维汉，政协全国委员会副主席陈叔通，国防委员会副主席张治中，民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、刘春，中共西藏工委副书记谭冠三、周仁山。
