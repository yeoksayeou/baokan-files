Title: Beijing, Shandong, Henan, Jilin, Shanxi, Hebei, Yunnan: Steel and Coal Experience a Great Harvest in September
Author:
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 11

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: Workers on the capital's steel front have completed their September production plans for steel and steel materials three and six days ahead of schedule, respectively, as a gift to the great 10th anniversary of the founding of the nation. The average daily output of these two products increased by 31.4% and 38.1% respectively compared to August. The quality of steel and steel materials has also significantly improved.

On the 28th, the Industrial Production Committee of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China held a meeting of负责人 for all steel enterprises in the city, requiring all factories to prepare for production and ensure a "red opening" in October.

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, September 29th: Shandong Province has exceeded its steel production plan for the third quarter five days ahead of schedule. As of September 25th, the province produced a total of 353,464 tons of iron, exceeding the plan by 3,464 tons; 53,637 tons of steel, exceeding the plan by 877 tons.

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, September 29th: As of September 20th, Shandong Province had produced 76,326 tons of steel materials, exceeding the annual plan by 5.5% ahead of schedule. The yield rate reached 70%, and the qualification rate was over 95%.

Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, September 28th: Workers on the steel front in Henan Province have completed their September steel production plan four days ahead of schedule. The average daily output of steel this month increased by 132% compared to August, and the average daily output of pig iron increased by 71.68% compared to August. As of September 26th, the province had completed 88.5% of its annual pig iron production plan, and it is expected to complete 90% by the end of the month.

Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, September 27th: Jilin Province has comprehensively overfulfilled its steel production plan for the third quarter. As of September 22nd, the province produced a total of 10,111 tons of pig iron, exceeding the national plan by 28.9%; 9,162 tons of steel, exceeding the national plan by 25.85%; and 10,187 tons of steel materials, exceeding the national plan by 100.37%. During this period, the qualification rate of pig iron reached over 90%, and the quality of steel and steel materials was also very good.

Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, September 28th: Coal mine workers in Shanxi Province are presenting a great gift to the 10th anniversary of the founding of the nation with the tremendous achievement of overfulfilling the third-quarter raw coal production plan four days ahead of schedule. As of September 26th, state-owned and local coal mines in the province produced a total of 9,946,000 tons of coal, exceeding the state-stipulated third-quarter plan by 86,000 tons.

This newspaper reports: Hebei Province has overfulfilled its third-quarter and September raw coal production plans two and a half to three and a half days ahead of schedule, respectively.

Coal mine workers in various parts of Hebei Province responded to the call of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Party, went all out, and widely launched competitions. Most leading cadres went deep into the mines, participating in production, inspecting production, and directing production, solving many key problems together with the workers. Kailuan Coal Mine completed its September production task two and a half days ahead of schedule and also overfulfilled its third-quarter production plan by more than 2,500 tons.

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, September 29th: Yunnan Province has overfulfilled its third-quarter raw coal production plan by 7% ten days ahead of schedule. Cadres of the Tuobai Coal Mine of the Mile Mining Bureau went deep into the site and worked alongside the workers, and the daily output of raw coal quickly increased from 84 tons to 112 tons. The Dali Bai Autonomous Prefecture Coal Mine completed 5% over the third-quarter raw coal production plan by September 10th.

### 北京山东河南吉林山西河北云南  钢煤九月大丰收

1959-09-30
第11版()
专栏：

    北京山东河南吉林山西河北云南
    钢煤九月大丰收
    据新华社29日讯　首都钢铁战线上的职工已分别提前三天和六天完成了9月份钢和钢材的生产计划，向伟大的建国十周年献礼。这两项产品的平均日产量比8月份分别提高31.4％和38.1％。钢和钢材的质量也有显著提高。
    28日，中共北京市委工业生产委员会举行了全市各钢铁企业负责人会议，要求各厂作好生产准备工作，保证实现10月开门红。
    据新华社济南29日电　山东省已提前五天超额完成了第三季度钢铁生产计划。到9月25日止，全季共产铁三十五万三千四百六十四吨，比计划超产三千四百六十四吨；产钢五万三千六百三十七吨，比计划超产八百七十七吨。
    据新华社济南29日电　山东省到9月20日止已产钢材七万六千三百二十六吨，提前超额5.5％完成了全年计划。成材率达到70％，合格率在95％以上。
    据新华社郑州28日电　河南省钢铁战线上的职工，已经提前四天完成了9月份的钢铁生产计划。这个月钢的平均日产量比8月份增长132％，生铁平均日产量比8月份增长71.68％。到9月26日，全省生铁年产量计划已完成88.5％，预计到月底可以完成90％。
    据新华社长春27日电　吉林省第三季度钢铁生产计划已经全面超额完成。到9月22日止，全省共产生铁一万零一百一十一吨，超过国家计划28.9％；产钢九千一百六十二吨，超过国家计划25.85％，产钢材一万零一百八十七吨，超过国家计划100.37％。在这期间，生铁合格率达到90％以上，钢和钢材的质量也很好。
    据新华社太原28日电　山西省的煤矿职工，以提前四天超额完成第三季度原煤生产计划的巨大成绩，向建国十周年献礼。到9月26日止，全省国营和地方煤矿共产煤九百九十四万六千多吨，超过国家规定的第三季度计划八万六千吨。
    本报讯　河北省已提前两天半到三天半分别超额完成第三季度和9月份的原煤生产计划。
    河北省各地煤矿职工响应党的八届八中全会号召，鼓足干劲，广泛开展竞赛。大部分领导干部都深入井下，边参加生产，边检查生产，边指挥生产，同工人一起解决了许多关键问题。开滦煤矿提前两天半完成了9月份生产任务，同时超额二千五百多吨完成了第三季度生产计划。
    据新华社昆明29日电　云南省已提前十天超额7％完成了第三季度的原煤生产计划。弥勒矿务局拖白煤矿的干部深入现场同工人一道劳动，原煤的日产量很快由八十四吨提高到一百一十二吨。大理白族自治州煤矿在9月10日就超额5％完成了第三季度的原煤生产计划。
