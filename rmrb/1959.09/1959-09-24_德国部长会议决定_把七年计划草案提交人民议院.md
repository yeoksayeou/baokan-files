Title: German Council of Ministers Decides to Submit Seven-Year Plan Draft to People's Chamber
Author:
Date: 1959-09-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin. According to a communique issued by the German Democratic Republic News Agency, the Council of Ministers met on the 21st to discuss the draft of the German Democratic Republic's National Economic Development Plan (1959-1965) and the guidelines for the socialist transformation of industry, as proposed by the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party, and unanimously agreed to submit the seven-year plan draft to the People's Chamber.

The Council of Ministers also passed a draft law amending the law on the national emblem and flag of the German Democratic Republic today. The draft stipulates that the black, red, and gold tricolor flag of the German Democratic Republic will henceforth bear the national emblem of the German Democratic Republic in the center, in order to distinguish it from the flag of West Germany, and to demonstrate the peaceful and democratic development of the German Democratic Republic.

### 德国部长会议决定  把七年计划草案提交人民议院

1959-09-24
第6版()
专栏：

    德国部长会议决定
    把七年计划草案提交人民议院
    据新华社柏林电　据德意志民主共和国新闻局发表的公报说，部长会议21日开会讨论了统一社会党中央委员会政治局提出的关于德意志民主共和国发展国民经济计划（1959—1965）的草案和工业的社会主义改造方针，并一致同意将七年计划草案提交人民议院。
    部长会议今天还通过一项修改德意志民主共和国国徽和国旗法草案。这个草案规定，德意志民主共和国的黑、红、黄三色国旗今后将在中间带有德意志民主共和国的国徽，以区别于西德的国旗，以表明德意志民主共和国的和平民主的发展。
