Title: Uruguayan Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: The Uruguayan Cultural Friendship Delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries to visit China and participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation was led by its head, Sarandy Cabrera.

### 乌拉圭文化代表团到京

1959-09-26
第2版()
专栏：

    乌拉圭文化代表团到京
    新华社25日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的乌拉圭文化友好代表团一行六人，在团长沙朗第·加勃莱拉率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
