Title: Polish Coal Mine Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 4th: A four-member Polish coal mine delegation, led by Jan Mitrega, Minister of the Polish Mining and Power Industry, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The guests were invited by the Ministry of Coal Industry to attend the transfer-to-production ceremony of the Zhuzhou Coal Preparation Plant and to visit China.

### 波兰煤矿代表团到京

1959-09-05
第2版()
专栏：

    波兰煤矿代表团到京
    据新华社4日讯　波兰煤矿代表团一行四人，在团长、波兰采煤和动力工业部部长扬·米特伦加率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。客人们是应煤炭工业部邀请，来我国参加株洲选煤厂移交生产典礼和参观访问的。
