Title: Iraqi Chargé d'Affaires in China, Jalal Jaf, Meets with Foreign Minister Chen

Author:
Date: 1959-09-27
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: Jalal Jaf, Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy of the Republic of Iraq in China, met with Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, this morning. Gong Dafei, Deputy Director of the West Asian and African Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was present at the meeting.

### 伊拉克驻华大使馆临时代办  贾拉尔·贾夫会见陈外长

1959-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克驻华大使馆临时代办
    贾拉尔·贾夫会见陈外长
    新华社26日讯　伊拉克共和国驻华大使馆临时代办贾拉尔·贾夫今天上午会见我外交部长陈毅和副部长章汉夫。会见时在座的有外交部西亚非洲司副司长宫达非。
