Title: Afghan King Receives Head of Chinese Acrobatic Troupe
Author:
Date: 1959-09-05
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, September 4: Afghan King Mohammed Zahir Shah received Sun Chuan Si, head of the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe, which is visiting Kabul, at the Royal Palace on the evening of the 3rd.

King Zahir expressed his pleasure that the acrobatic troupe's visit had enhanced the friendship between China and Afghanistan.

The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe arrived in Kabul on August 17 at the invitation of the Afghan government to participate in the celebrations of Afghanistan's Independence Day.

The acrobatic troupe performed a total of sixteen shows during the Afghan Independence Day celebrations, with an audience of more than 30,000 people. The Afghan Prime Minister and Deputy Prime Minister, the King's uncle, members of the royal family, cabinet ministers, and senior generals all watched the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe's performances with great interest. The acrobatic troupe also performed specifically for Afghan army officers, students, and women.

The commander of the Afghan Central Corps awarded the "Independence" Medal to Xia Juhua, a female performer of the acrobatic troupe, for her outstanding performance of "Plate Spinning" during the performance for Afghan army officers.

### 阿富汗国王接见我杂技艺术团团长

1959-09-05
第2版()
专栏：

    阿富汗国王接见我杂技艺术团团长
    新华社4日讯　喀布尔消息：阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿3日晚在王宫接见了在喀布尔访问的中国杂技艺术团团长孙川四。
    查希尔国王对杂技艺术团的访问增进了中国和阿富汗之间的友谊表示高兴。
    中国杂技艺术团是应阿富汗政府邀请，在8月17日到达喀布尔，参加阿富汗独立日的庆祝活动的。
    杂技艺术团在阿富汗独立日庆祝期间共演出了十六场，观众达三万多人。阿富汗的首相和副首相、国王的叔父、王室成员、内阁大臣和高级将领等都怀着很大兴趣观看了中国杂技艺术团的演出。杂技艺术团还专门分别为阿富汗的陆军军官、学生和妇女作了演出。
    阿富汗中央军团司令为了杂技艺术团女演员夏菊花在给阿富汗陆军军官演出时出色地表演的“顶碗”，特别授于“独立”奖章。
