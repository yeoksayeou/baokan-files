Title: Soviet State Grand Theatre Ballet Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-09-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: The Soviet State Grand Theatre Ballet arrived in Beijing this afternoon. Bringing with them the world-renowned art of ballet and the sincere congratulations of the Soviet people, they have come to our country especially to participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and to pay a visit and give performances.

    The dance troupe is led by Deputy Minister of Culture of the Soviet Union, Danilov, and includes Soviet People's Artist and conductor Fayer, outstanding Soviet ballet artists Ulanova, Lepeshinskaya, Plisetskaya, Sergeyev, and ballet director Semyonova, who all arrived on the same plane. They were warmly welcomed at the airport by Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, famous artists Mei Lanfang, Hong Xiannv, Chen Bohua, and heads of the Chinese Dramatists Association, Dance Art Research Association, and Musicians Association. More than 100 young Chinese ballet dancers, including Bai Shuxiang, presented flowers to all the Soviet ballet artists. Antonov, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, also went to the airport today to greet them.

    The Soviet State Grand Theatre is the largest theatre in the Soviet Union with a long history, having been established for more than 180 years. The ballet troupe of the Grand Theatre has visited and performed in many countries around the world and enjoys a high international reputation. This time, at the invitation of the Chinese Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, they have come to China for a visit and performance, with a total of more than 190 people, making it the largest overseas performance of the Grand Theatre to date. In addition to performing famous classical ballets such as "Swan Lake," "Giselle," and "The Stone Flower" in China, they have also specially rehearsed the modern ballet "The Path of Thunder," which is themed on the anti-colonialist struggle, for this visit to China.

    The orchestra of the Soviet State Grand Theatre, consisting of 75 outstanding musicians, is visiting China for the first time to perform abroad. The orchestra will be conducted by Soviet People's Artist Fayer and Honored Artist Svetlanov. In addition to providing accompaniment for the ballet performances, they will also hold large-scale concerts, performing works by Shostakovich, selections from the opera "Faust," and other musical excerpts from ballets of different styles.

    After performing in Beijing, the Soviet State Grand Theatre Ballet will also visit Shanghai, Guangzhou, Wuhan and other places.

### 苏联国家大剧院芭蕾舞团到京

1959-09-26
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联国家大剧院芭蕾舞团到京
    新华社25日讯　苏联国家大剧院芭蕾舞团，今天下午到达北京。它带着享有世界盛誉的芭蕾舞艺术和苏联人民亲切祝贺的心意，专程前来我国参加建国十周年庆祝活动并进行访问演出。
    舞蹈团由苏联文化部副部长达尼洛夫率领，苏联人民演员、乐队指挥法耶尔，苏联杰出的芭蕾舞表演艺术家乌兰诺娃和列皮辛斯卡娅、普利谢茨卡娅、谢尔盖耶夫，以及芭蕾舞导演谢苗诺娃等都同机到达。他们在机场上受到我国对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥、文化部副部长夏衍、中苏友好协会总会副秘书长戈宝权、著名的文艺界人士梅兰芳、红线女、陈伯华以及中国戏剧家协会、舞蹈艺术研究会、音乐家协会等负责人的热烈欢迎。我国年轻的芭蕾舞演员白淑相等一百多人向所有苏联芭蕾舞艺术家献了鲜花。苏联驻华大使馆临时代办安东诺夫今天也去机场迎接。
    苏联国家大剧院是苏联规模最大的具有悠久历史的剧院，它成立到现在已有一百八十多年。大剧院的芭蕾舞团曾经先后到世界上很多国家访问演出，享有很高的国际声誉。这次他们应我国对外文化联络委员会的邀请前来中国访问演出，全团共一百九十多人，是大剧院历来出国演出规模最大的一次。这次他们除了将在中国演出“天鹅湖”、“吉赛尔”、“宝石花”等著名的古典芭蕾舞剧外，还专为这次访华演出排练了以反殖民主义斗争为主题的现代芭蕾舞剧“雷电的道路”。
    由七十五名优秀音乐家组成的苏联国家大剧院管弦乐队，这次是第一次来中国作出国演出。乐队指挥由苏联人民演员法耶尔和功勋演员斯维特拉诺夫担任。他们除了给芭蕾舞剧伴奏外，还将单独举行大型音乐会，演奏肖斯塔科维奇的作品、歌剧“浮士德”选曲和其他不同风格的舞剧音乐片断。
    苏联国家大剧院芭蕾舞团在北京演出后，还将去上海、广州、武汉等地访问。
