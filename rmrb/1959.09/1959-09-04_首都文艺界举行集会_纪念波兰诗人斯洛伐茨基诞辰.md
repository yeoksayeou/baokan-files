Title: Capital Literary and Art Circles Hold Gathering to Commemorate the Birth of Polish Poet Słowacki
Author:
Date: 1959-09-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 3rd. The capital's literary and art circles held a gathering tonight to commemorate the 150th anniversary of the birth of Polish poet Juliusz Słowacki.

The commemorative evening was jointly organized by the Sino-Polish Friendship Association, the Chinese Writers Association, and the Beijing Library. He Chengxiang, Vice President of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association, said in his opening remarks that Juliusz Słowacki was a great Polish poet who fought for the freedom and independence of his motherland with his poems and poetic dramas. The literary works he left behind are not only treasures of the Polish people but are also valued by the Chinese people.

Ge Baoquan, a director of the Chinese Writers Association, gave a report at the meeting, reporting on Słowacki's life and works to hundreds of literary and art figures and enthusiasts in the capital.  Franciszek Pluta, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, and other embassy officials were invited to attend the commemorative evening.

### 首都文艺界举行集会  纪念波兰诗人斯洛伐茨基诞辰

1959-09-04
第5版()
专栏：

    首都文艺界举行集会
    纪念波兰诗人斯洛伐茨基诞辰
    新华社3日讯　首都文艺界今晚举行集会，纪念波兰诗人尤·斯洛伐茨基诞生一百五十周年。
    纪念晚会是由中波友好协会、中国作家协会和北京图书馆联合举办的。中波友好协会副会长何成湘在开会词中说，尤·斯洛伐茨基是波兰伟大的诗人，他曾经以自己的诗歌和诗剧为祖国的自由和独立而战斗。他遗留下来的文学著作不仅是波兰人民的珍宝，而且为中国人民所重视。
    中国作家协会理事戈宝权在会上作报告，向几百名首都文艺界人士和文艺爱好者报告了斯洛伐茨基的生平和创作。波兰驻中国大使馆临时代办傅拉托和其他使馆官员应邀出席了纪念晚会。
