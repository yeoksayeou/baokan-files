Title: Afghan Deputy Prime Minister and Entourage Visit the Palace Museum and the Tuancheng Fortress

Author:
Date: 1959-09-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 6th: Afghan Deputy Prime Minister Prince Naim and his entourage, accompanied by Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Hao Ding, our Ambassador to Afghanistan, visited the Palace Museum and the Tuancheng Fortress this morning in the rain.

When Deputy Prime Minister Naim arrived at the Palace Museum and the Tuancheng Fortress, he was warmly welcomed by Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, Wu Zhongchao, Director of the Palace Museum, Bi Pingfei, Deputy Director of the Arts and Crafts Bureau of the Ministry of Light Industry, and other responsible persons.

After admiring the various arts and crafts displayed at the Tuancheng Fortress, Deputy Prime Minister Naim wrote an inscription for the exhibition hall. In his inscription, he expressed his intention to bring the very pleasant impression he gained during the visit back to Afghanistan.

### 阿富汗副首相和随行人员  游览故宫和团城

1959-09-07
第5版()
专栏：

    阿富汗副首相和随行人员
    游览故宫和团城
    新华社6日讯　阿富汗王国副首相纳伊姆亲王和他的随行人员，在外交部礼宾司副司长马振武和我国驻阿富汗大使郝汀陪同下，今天上午冒雨参观游览故宫和团城。
    纳伊姆副首相来到故宫和团城的时候，受到文化部部长沈雁冰，故宫博物院院长吴仲超，轻工业部工艺美术局副局长毕平非等负责人的热烈欢迎。
    纳伊姆副首相在团城欣赏了陈列在那里的各种工艺美术品以后，曾为陈列馆题词。在题词中，他表示要把他在参观中获得的十分愉快的印象，带回阿富汗去。
