Title: Cuban Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 27th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, members of the Cuban Cultural Friendship Delegation, who are visiting our country and participating in the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China celebration, writer Enrique Labrador Ruiz, painter Mariano Rodríguez, actor Vicente Revuelta, and director of the National Theatre, Isabel Monal, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The head of the delegation, poet Nicolás Guillén, had arrived in Beijing earlier.

### 古巴文化代表团到京

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    古巴文化代表团到京
    新华社27日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的古巴文化友好代表团团员、作家恩利盖·拉勃拉多尔·罗易斯，画家马利阿诺·罗德里盖斯，演员维逊坦·兰乌凡尔塔斯和国家剧院院长伊莎贝尔·蒙娜尔今天乘飞机到达北京。团长、诗人尼古拉·纪廉已先期到达北京。
