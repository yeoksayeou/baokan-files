Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes Into Territorial Waters Off Pingtan, Fujian Again; I Issue the 70th Serious Warning

Author:

Date: 1959-09-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: From 8:04 AM to 11:08 AM on September 23rd, a U.S. warship intruded into the sea area off Pingtan, Fujian Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 70th serious warning.

### 美舰又侵入我福建平潭地区领海  我提出第70次严重警告

1959-09-24
第5版()
专栏：

    美舰又侵入我福建平潭地区领海
    我提出第70次严重警告
    新华社23日讯　9月23日八时零四分至十一时零八分，美国军舰一艘侵入我国福建省平潭地区海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第七十次严重警告。
