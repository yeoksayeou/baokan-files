Title: More than Four Thousand Six Hundred Matches

Author: 
Date: 1959-09-13
Page: 6

The various competitions of the First National Games are very enthusiastic. Not counting the zonal preliminaries for the six items of football, basketball, volleyball, handball, baseball, and the Class A gliding competition, the Games will hold a total of 4,635 matches. Table tennis has the most matches, totaling 675; Chinese wrestling has 674 matches, and fencing has 461 matches. The basketball game lasts the longest, continuing for eighteen days. The most lively competitions are on the 14th, 15th, and 16th of September. On the 14th alone, there will be twenty-seven events with 622 matches, on the 15th there will be twenty-eight events with 599 matches, and on the 16th there will be twenty-seven events with 541 matches.

### 四千六百多场次比赛

1959-09-13
第6版()
专栏：

    四千六百多场次比赛
    第一届全运会的各项竞赛是十分热烈的。不计算足球、篮球、排球、手球、棒球六项分区预赛和甲级滑翔赛，运动会总共要进行四千六百三十五场次的比赛。乒乓球赛的场次最多，共达六百七十五场次；中国式摔跤六百七十四场，击剑四百六十一场。比赛时间最久的是篮球赛，前后延续达十八天。比赛最热闹的是9月14、15、16三天。14日一天就举行二十七项六百二十二场次，15日举行二十八项五百九十九场次，16日举行二十七项五百四十一场次。
