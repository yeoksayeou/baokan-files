Title: Peruvian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, the Peruvian Cultural Friendship Delegation arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The head of the delegation is Asunción Caballero Méndez, and the members are César Guardia Mayorga and Carlos Núñez Anavitarte.

### 秘鲁文化代表团到京

1959-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    秘鲁文化代表团到京
    新华社24日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的秘鲁文化友好代表团，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团团长是阿顺松·卡瓦列罗·门德斯，团员有塞萨·瓜迪亚·马约尔加、卡洛斯·努涅斯·阿纳维塔特。
