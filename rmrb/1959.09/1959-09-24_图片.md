    Inspired by the communique and resolutions of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Party, cadres and members of the Pohu People's Commune in Changge County, Henan Province, are working with greater enthusiasm to actively prepare for wheat planting.
    Left picture: Workers at the Pohu Commune Iron Factory are rushing to produce a large number of small farm tools for the "Three Autumns" [autumn harvesting, plowing, and sowing].
    Right picture: Members of the Mengpai Production Team are carefully selecting improved wheat varieties.
                                   Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zhang Qingyun Photo
