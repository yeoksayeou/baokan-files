Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and All-China Women's Federation Send Congratulatory Messages on the 15th Anniversary of Bulgaria's National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-09-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 8th - The All-China Federation of Trade Unions Chairman Liu Ningyi, the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, and the All-China Women's Federation, respectively sent telegrams to the Chairman of the Central Council of Bulgarian Trade Unions, Prahov, the Central Committee of the Dimitrov Communist Youth League of Bulgaria, and the Bulgarian Women's Committee, warmly congratulating them on the 15th anniversary of Bulgaria's National Day.

The congratulatory messages pointed out that under the leadership of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the government, the Bulgarian people have made great achievements in all aspects of socialist construction and the defense of world peace. The congratulatory messages expressed deep confidence that, under the correct leadership of the Party, the Bulgarian people will surely achieve the great goal of striving to complete the Third Five-Year Plan in three or four years.

The congratulatory messages wished that the fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Bulgarian people would develop day by day.

### 我全总、团中央和妇联  电贺保加利亚国庆十五周年

1959-09-09
第5版()
专栏：

    我全总、团中央和妇联
    电贺保加利亚国庆十五周年
    新华社8日讯　中华全国总工会主席刘宁一、中国共产主义青年团中央委员会、中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会，分别致电保加利亚工会中央理事会主席普拉霍夫、保加利亚季米特洛夫共产主义青年团中央委员会、保加利亚妇女委员会，热烈祝贺保加利亚国庆十五周年。
    贺电指出，保加利亚人民在保加利亚共产党和政府的领导下，在社会主义建设和保卫世界和平的各个方面都取得了巨大的成就。贺电表示深信，保加利亚人民在党的正确领导下，一定能够实现争取在三、四年内完成第三个五年计划的伟大目标。
    贺电祝中保两国人民的兄弟友谊日益发展。
