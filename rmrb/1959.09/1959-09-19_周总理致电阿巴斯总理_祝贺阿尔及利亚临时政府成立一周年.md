Title: Premier Zhou Sends a Cable to Premier Abbas to Congratulate the First Anniversary of the Establishment of the Provisional Government of Algeria
Author:
Date: 1959-09-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 18th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China today sent a telegram to Ferhat Abbas, Premier of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, to congratulate the first anniversary of the establishment of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria. The text of the telegram is as follows: To His Excellency Ferhat Abbas, Premier of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, Cairo:

    On the occasion of the first anniversary of the establishment of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the heroic Algerian people on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people.

    In the past year, the Algerian people have continuously achieved new victories in their struggle for national independence, which has encouraged the people of Asian and African countries to oppose colonialism and strive for national independence, and has won the warm support and praise of the Chinese people and peace-loving people all over the world.

    We are pleased to see that with the development of friendly relations between China and Algeria and the friendly visits to our country by the delegation and military delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, the friendship and cooperation between the Chinese and Algerian people have greatly developed. We firmly believe that the sincere friendship between the Chinese and Algerian people will be developed and consolidated in the common struggle against imperialism. The heroic Algerian people will surely achieve the final victory of national liberation.

    Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, September 18, 1959

### 周总理致电阿巴斯总理  祝贺阿尔及利亚临时政府成立一周年

1959-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理致电阿巴斯总理
    祝贺阿尔及利亚临时政府成立一周年
    新华社18日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理费尔哈特·阿巴斯祝贺阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府成立一周年。电文如下：开罗阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理费尔哈特·阿巴斯阁下：
    欣逢阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府成立一周年，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和中国人民向阁下和英雄的阿尔及利亚人民表示热烈的祝贺。
    一年来，阿尔及利亚人民，在争取民族独立的斗争中，不断地取得了新的胜利，鼓舞了亚非各国人民反对殖民主义、争取民族独立的斗志，赢得了中国人民和全世界爱好和平人民的热烈支持和赞扬。
    我们高兴地看到：随着中、阿两国友好关系的发展和阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团和军事代表团到我国进行的友好访问，中、阿两国人民之间的友谊和合作有了很大的发展。我们坚信：中、阿两国人民诚挚的友谊在反对帝国主义的共同斗争中必将日益发展和巩固。英雄的阿尔及利亚人民一定能够取得民族解放的最后胜利。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来　一九五九年九月十八日
