Title: Soviet Science and Technology Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: The Soviet science and technology cooperation delegation, headed by N.M. Siruyanov, Chairman of the Soviet side of the Sino-Soviet Science and Technology Cooperation Committee, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They will attend the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of our country and attend the tenth session of the Sino-Soviet Science and Technology Cooperation Committee.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Han Guang, Chairman of the Chinese side of the Sino-Soviet Science and Technology Cooperation Committee and Vice-Chairman of the Science and Technology Commission; Wu Heng, Vice-Chairman of the Science and Technology Commission; Yan Jici, member of the Chinese side and Director of the Department of Technical Sciences of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Qian Xinzhong, member and Vice-Minister of the Ministry of Health, and others.

### 苏联科技代表团到北京

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    苏联科技代表团到北京
    新华社27日讯　以中苏科学技术合作委员会苏联组主席尼·米·西鲁扬诺夫为首的苏联科学技术合作代表团，今天乘飞机到达北京。他们将参加我国建国十周年庆祝典礼和出席中苏科学技术合作委员会第十届会议。
    前往机场欢迎的有中苏科学技术合作委员会中国组主席、科学技术委员会副主任韩光，科学技术委员会副主任武衡，中国组委员、中国科学院技术科学部主任严济慈，委员、卫生部副部长钱信忠等。
