Title: Colombian Cultural Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: The five-member Colombian Cultural Friendship Delegation, led by the head of the delegation, Enrique Gomez Hurtado, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit our country and participate in the celebrations for the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 哥伦比亚文化友好代表团到京

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    哥伦比亚文化友好代表团到京
    新华社27日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的哥伦比亚文化友好代表团一行五人，在团长安利盖·戈麦兹·乌尔达多率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
