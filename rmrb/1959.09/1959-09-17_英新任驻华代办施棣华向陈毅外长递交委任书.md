Title: New British Chargé d'Affaires to China, Shidi Hua, Presents Credentials to Foreign Minister Chen Yi
Author:
Date: 1959-09-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The new British Chargé d'Affaires to China, Shidi Hua, presented his credentials to Foreign Minister Chen Yi this morning. Present were Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Song Zhiguang, Deputy Director of the Department of West European Affairs.

### 英新任驻华代办施棣华向陈毅外长递交委任书

1959-09-17
第5版()
专栏：

    英新任驻华代办施棣华向陈毅外长递交委任书
    新华社16日讯　英国新任驻我国代办施棣华今天上午向我外交部长陈毅递交委任书。在座的有外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文、西欧司副司长宋之光。
