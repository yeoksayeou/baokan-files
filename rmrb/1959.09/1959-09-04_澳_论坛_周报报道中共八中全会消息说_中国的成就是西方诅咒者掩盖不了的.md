Title: Australian "Tribune" Weekly Reports on the CCP's Eighth Plenary Session: China's Achievements Cannot Be Concealed by Western Detractors
Author:
Date: 1959-09-04
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Sydney, September 3rd: The latest issue of the Australian Communist Party's organ, the "Tribune" weekly, published a message on the front page entitled "Newspapers Lie, Attempting to Conceal China's Amazing Achievements" regarding the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party. The message reported in detail that China will fulfill the main targets of the 1962 plan three years ahead of schedule this year.

The "Tribune" weekly pointed out, "The capitalist world is shouting and cursing that China is heading for disaster, that the plan will fail, and that the people's communes will collapse. But this message is China's silencing response to them." The newspaper quoted China's planned economic development targets, saying that compared with the economic development speed of capitalist countries, this speed is astonishing to them.

### 澳“论坛”周报报道中共八中全会消息说  中国的成就是西方诅咒者掩盖不了的

1959-09-04
第5版()
专栏：

    澳“论坛”周报报道中共八中全会消息说
    中国的成就是西方诅咒者掩盖不了的
    新华社3日讯　悉尼消息：最近一期的澳大利亚共产党机关报“论坛”周报在第一版以“报刊撒谎，企图掩盖中国惊人的成就”为题发表关于中国共产党八届八中全会的消息。消息详细地报道了中国今年将提前三年完成1962年的主要计划的指标。
    “论坛”周报指出，“资本主义世界在大叫大嚷，诅咒中国将有灾难临头、计划将失败、人民公社将垮台。但这个消息是中国使他们哑口无言的回答。”这家报纸援引中国发展经济的计划指标说，同资本主义国家的经济发展速度比较起来，这个速度是使他们吃惊的。
