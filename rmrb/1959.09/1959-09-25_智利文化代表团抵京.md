Title: Chilean Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th—The Chilean cultural delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries to participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of our country, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon, led by the head of the delegation, Francisco Cuevas Mackenna. Members of the delegation include: Jorge Errázuriz, Mario Saez Lagos, Luis Armando Sepúlveda Morales, and Paulina Pinto de Cuevas, wife of the head of the delegation, Francisco Cuevas Mackenna.

### 智利文化代表团抵京

1959-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    智利文化代表团抵京
    新华社24日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的智利文化代表团，在团长弗朗西斯科·奎瓦斯—马肯纳率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团团员有：豪尔赫·埃拉苏里斯、马里奥·赛斯·拉戈斯、路易斯·阿曼多·塞普耳维达—莫拉莱斯和团长弗朗西斯科·奎瓦斯—马肯纳的夫人保林娜·平托·德·奎瓦斯。
