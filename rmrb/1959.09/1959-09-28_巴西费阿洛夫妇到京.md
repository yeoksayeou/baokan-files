Title: Brazilian Fialho Couple Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: Mr. and Mrs. Fialho, members of the Brazilian cultural delegation invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China and participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 巴西费阿洛夫妇到京

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    巴西费阿洛夫妇到京
    新华社27日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的巴西文化代表团团员费阿洛夫妇，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
