Title: Soviet Children Celebrate China's National Day; Moscow-Beijing Friendship Relay Enters Our Territory; Harbin Thousand Young Pioneers Hold Welcoming Rally
Author:
Date: 1959-09-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 30th - A thousand Young Pioneers from Harbin held a rally at the Harbin Railway Cultural Palace on the 30th to welcome the arrival of the "Moscow-Beijing" friendship relay baton in Harbin. The ten Young Pioneers from Heilongjiang Province who represented the Chinese Young Pioneers in receiving the relay baton from Bikin City, Khabarovsk Krai, Soviet Union, attended the rally.

The "Moscow-Beijing Friendship Relay Race" was organized by the Soviet Young Pioneer "Counselor" magazine to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of our country. The "relay baton" is a specially made commemorative album, which records the most meaningful activities held by Young Pioneers from all over the Soviet Union to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of our country.

### 苏联儿童庆祝我国庆  莫斯科—北京友谊接力棒进入我国境  哈尔滨千名少先队队员举行欢迎大会

1959-09-01
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联儿童庆祝我国庆
    莫斯科—北京友谊接力棒进入我国境
    哈尔滨千名少先队队员举行欢迎大会
    据新华社哈尔滨30日电　千名哈尔滨市的少先队员30日在哈尔滨铁路文化宫举行大会，欢迎“莫斯科—北京”友谊接力棒到达哈尔滨。代表中国少年先锋队前往苏联伯力边区比金市接来接力棒的十名黑龙江省的少先队员，出席了大会。
    “莫斯科—北京友谊接力赛”是由苏联少先队“辅导员”杂志社为庆祝我国国庆十周年而举行的。“接力棒”是一个特制的纪念册，其中记载苏联各地少先队为庆祝我国建国十周年而举行的最有意义的活动。
