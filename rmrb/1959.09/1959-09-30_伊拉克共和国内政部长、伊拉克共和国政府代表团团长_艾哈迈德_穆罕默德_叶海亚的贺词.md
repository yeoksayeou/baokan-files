Title: Congratulatory Message from Ahmed Mohammed Yehia, Minister of Interior of the Republic of Iraq and Head of the Government Delegation of the Republic of Iraq
Author:
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 9

Your Excellency Chairman, Honorable Leaders, Ladies and Gentlemen:
    We, the delegation of the Republic of Iraq, feel deeply honored to represent our country in this grand celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the friendly People's Republic of China, and to take this historic opportunity to let the voice of a free Iraq be heard in this great country on behalf of my fellow delegates. I am pleased to convey the highest respect of the Iraqi people and their sole leader, Abdul Karim Kassem, to the friendly Chinese people and their great leaders, and to wish them progress and continuous achievements.
    The Iraqi people will never forget the close cooperation, genuine support, and unbreakable friendship you showed in the early days of Iraq's immortal July 14th Revolution. The Iraqi Revolution eliminated oppression, autocracy, and foreign forces, thereby beginning to restore the traditional friendship between our two peoples on a closer and stronger foundation.
    We believe that the powerful Chinese people, under the leadership of their great leaders, are building a bright future for themselves, because their goal is continuous and organized labor, striving for complete freedom and strengthening the friendship and peaceful ties between all peoples. There is no doubt that we will fully benefit from your experience, from your steps, and from the experience of freedom-loving and friendly people, in order to build a bright and splendid future for ourselves and establish a foundation of freedom and progress for our people.
    We have seen the tremendous work and great achievements that your country has accomplished in just ten years—a very short time in history—which would have been impossible without the conviction that "with one heart, we can move mountains" and without your firm belief in the slogan of "more, faster, better, and more economical production."
    We are very grateful for the hospitality extended to the representatives of our Republic.
    Long live the eternal friendship between Iraq and China!
    Long live the great Chinese people! (Attached picture)

### 伊拉克共和国内政部长、伊拉克共和国政府代表团团长  艾哈迈德·穆罕默德·叶海亚的贺词

1959-09-30
第9版()
专栏：

    伊拉克共和国内政部长、伊拉克共和国政府代表团团长
    艾哈迈德·穆罕默德·叶海亚的贺词主席阁下，各位首长，各位女士，各位先生：
    我们伊拉克共和国代表团能够代表我们国家参加庆祝友好的中华人民共和国建国十周年的这一盛会，我能够乘这个具有历史意义的机会代表我的团员兄弟们，使人们在这个伟大的国家里听到自由伊拉克的声音，我们感到十分荣幸。我能够把伊拉克人民及其唯一领袖阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·卡塞姆的最高的敬意带给友好的中国人民及其伟大的领袖们，并祝他们进步和不断地取得成就，我感到高兴。
    伊拉克人民永远不会忘记你们在伊拉克不朽的七月十四日革命初期所表示的紧密合作真正的支持和牢不可破的友谊。伊拉克革命消灭了压迫、专制和外国势力，从而在更亲密和更牢固的基础上开始恢复我们两国人民之间的传统友谊。
    我们相信强大的中国人民在他们伟大领袖们的领导下，正在为自己建设美好的未来，因为他们的目标是进行不断的有组织的劳动，力图取得彻底的自由并加强各国人民的友谊和和平的联系。毫无疑问，我们将从你们的经验中，从你们的步骤中并从爱好自由的友好人民的经验中全面地取得好处，以便使我们自己建设一个光辉灿烂的未来，为我国人民建立自由的进步的基础。
    我们已经看到你们国家在仅仅十年中间——这在整个历史上是一个很短的时间——已经进行了巨大的工作，而且取得了伟大的成就，如果不是众志可以移山，如果不是你们坚信你们“多快好省地进行生产”的口号，这些工作和成就简直是不可能的。
    我们十分感激我们共和国的代表们所受到的盛意款待。
    伊拉克——中国万古长青的友谊万岁！
    伟大的中国人民万岁！（附图片）
