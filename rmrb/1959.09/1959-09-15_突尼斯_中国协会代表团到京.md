Title: Tunisian-Chinese Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-15
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 13th. The Tunisian-Chinese Association delegation of five, led by Head of Delegation Rachid Beller Ajouza, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries to attend the National Day celebrations in our country.

### 突尼斯—中国协会代表团到京

1959-09-15
第5版()
专栏：

    突尼斯—中国协会代表团到京
    新华社13日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来参加我国国庆节观礼的突尼斯—中国协会代表团一行五人，在团长拉希德·贝勒·阿朱扎率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
