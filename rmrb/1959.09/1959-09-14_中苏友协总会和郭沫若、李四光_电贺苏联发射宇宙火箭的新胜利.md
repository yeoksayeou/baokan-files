Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Guo Moruo, and Li Siguang Send Congratulatory Telegrams on the New Victory of the Soviet Union's Launch of a Cosmic Rocket
Author:
Date: 1959-09-14
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 13th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions today sent a telegram to Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, and Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, congratulating the Soviet Union on the successful launch of its second cosmic rocket. The telegram stated that when the news of the Soviet Union's first successful launch of a giant cosmic rocket towards the moon reached China at the beginning of this year, the Chinese people, like the people of all countries in the world, were overjoyed by this exciting news. Only eight months have passed, and as the Chinese people are preparing to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the country, the Soviet Union has successfully launched its second cosmic rocket. This is another major event in the history of human science and the glory and pride of the entire socialist camp. To this end, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Beijing Municipal Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association jointly held a symposium on September 13. The symposium was presided over by Li Dequan, Vice President of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. Invited to attend were the leaders of China’s democratic parties and people’s organizations, representatives of workers, peasants, youth, women, and the People’s Liberation Army, as well as well-known scientists and figures from the literary and art circles. The participants warmly hailed the great and brilliant achievements made by the Soviet people and expressed their warm congratulations to the Soviet people!

The telegram stated that inspired by the resolution of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Chinese people are enthusiastically responding to the call of the Party, closely uniting around the Chinese Communist Party and the great leader Comrade Mao Zedong, and, under the brilliant guidance of the General Line, are working with full confidence and vigor to complete the main targets of the Second Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule and in excess in 1959. At the same time, we are also very pleased to see that the Soviet people are making remarkable achievements in realizing the magnificent cause of communist construction.

The telegram stated that the participants unanimously expressed that the Chinese people should learn from the Soviet people even better and continuously strive to further strengthen the unbreakable great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples!

Xinhua News Agency, September 13th. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, sent a telegram today to Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, congratulating the Soviet Union on the successful launch of its second cosmic rocket. The telegram said, "Please allow me, on behalf of Chinese scientific workers and myself personally, to extend my warm congratulations and high respect to you and through you to the Soviet scientific workers."

The telegram stated that the successful launch of the Soviet Union's second cosmic rocket is a new and important stage in mankind's research and conquest of the universe. It expands the prospects for international cooperation in conquering outer space, which will help further ease international tensions and consolidate the cause of peace. The launch of this rocket once again eloquently demonstrates the peaceful aspirations of Soviet science. We have every reason to call the Soviet Union's cosmic rocket a rocket of peace. It further demonstrates the strength of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union and further encourages the confidence of peace-loving people all over the world to fight for the cause of peace.

The telegram stated, we wish the Soviet Union's scientific research undertakings further development and greater creative achievements.

Xinhua News Agency, September 13th. Li Siguang, Chairman of the Science and Technology Association of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to V. Siruyanov, Chairman of the All-Union Scientific and Technical Association, warmly congratulating the Soviet Union on the successful launch of its second cosmic rocket. The telegram said: "On the occasion of learning that the Soviet Union successfully launched its second cosmic rocket to the moon on September 12, 1959, please allow me, on behalf of the Science and Technology Association of the People's Republic of China, to extend my warm congratulations to you and through you to the Soviet scientists, engineers, and all the Soviet people!"

The telegram stated that the success of this cosmic rocket launched by the Soviet Union to study the cosmic space on the road to the moon shows that advanced Soviet science has taken another new and giant step forward on the road to conquering the universe. We believe that Soviet scientists and the Soviet people will surely make greater contributions to the scientific cause of all mankind, the peaceful and happy life, and the progress of mankind.

### 中苏友协总会和郭沫若、李四光  电贺苏联发射宇宙火箭的新胜利

1959-09-14
第7版()
专栏：

    中苏友协总会和郭沫若、李四光
    电贺苏联发射宇宙火箭的新胜利
    据新华社13日讯　中苏友好协会总会今天致电苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫并苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫，祝贺苏联成功地发射第二个宇宙火箭。电报说，当今年年初，苏联第一次向月球方向发射巨型宇宙火箭的胜利消息传到中国后，我国人民同全世界各国人民一样，都为这个振奋人心的喜讯欢欣鼓舞。时间只过了八个多月，当中国人民正准备欢庆建国十周年时，苏联又成功地发射了第二个宇宙火箭，这是人类科学历史上的又一次重大事件，是整个社会主义阵营的光荣和骄傲。为此，中苏友好协会总会和北京市中苏友好协会在9月13日联合举行了座谈会。座谈会是由中苏友好协会总会李德全副会长主持的。应邀出席参加的，有我国各民主党派和各人民团体的负责人，工人、农民、青年、妇女和人民解放军的代表，以及著名科学家和文学艺术界人士。与会者热烈欢呼苏联人民所取得的又一次伟大的辉煌成就，并向苏联人民表示热烈的祝贺！
    电报说，为中共八届八中全会的决议所鼓舞的中国人民，正在热烈响应党的号召，紧密地团结在中国共产党和伟大的领袖毛泽东同志的周围，在总路线的光辉照耀之下，信心百倍，干劲冲天地为在1959年提前和超额完成第二个五年计划的主要指标而努力奋斗。与此同时，我们也十分高兴地看到，苏联人民在实现宏伟的共产主义建设事业中正在取得日新月异的成就。
    电报说，与会者一致表示，中国人民要更好地向苏联人民学习，要为进一步加强中苏两国人民的牢不可破的伟大友谊而不断地努力！
    新华社13日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若今日打电报给苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫，祝贺苏联发射第二个宇宙火箭成功。电报中说，“请允许我代表中国科学工作者和我个人，向您并通过您向苏联科学工作者致以热烈的祝贺和崇高的敬意。”
    电报说，苏联第二个宇宙火箭发射成功是人类研究和征服宇宙的新的重要阶段。它扩大了在征服宇宙空间方面国际合作的前景，这将有助于进一步缓和国际紧张局势和巩固和平事业。这个火箭的发射，再一次雄辩地证明了苏联科学的和平愿望。我们完全有理由称苏联的宇宙火箭为和平的火箭。它更加显示了以苏联为首的社会主义阵营力量的强大，进一步鼓舞了全世界爱好和平的人民为和平事业而斗争的信心。
    电报说，我们预祝苏联科学研究事业进一步的发展和获得更伟大的创造性的成就。
    新华社13日讯　中华人民共和国科学技术协会主席李四光致电全苏科学技术协会主席弗·西鲁扬诺夫，热烈祝贺苏联发射第二个宇宙火箭成功。电报说，欣悉苏联于1959年9月12日成功地发射了飞向月球的第二个宇宙火箭之际，请允许我代表中华人民共和国科学技术协会向您并通过您向苏联科学家、工程师和全体苏联人民致以热烈的祝贺！
    电报说，这次苏联为研究飞向月球道路上的宇宙空间而发射的宇宙火箭的成功，表明了先进的苏联科学在征服宇宙的道路上又向前迈进了新的巨大的一步。我们相信苏联科学家和苏联人民对于全人类的科学事业、和平幸福生活和人类的进步必将作出更大的贡献。
