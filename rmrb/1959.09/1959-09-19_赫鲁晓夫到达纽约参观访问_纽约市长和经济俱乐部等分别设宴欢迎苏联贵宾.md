Title: Khrushchev Arrives in New York for a Visit; New York's Mayor and Economic Club Host Banquets to Welcome Soviet Guests
Author:
Date: 1959-09-19
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, September 18th -- According to TASS, Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev and his entourage left Washington by train on the morning of the 17th for New York, beginning their eight-day visit across the United States.

Accompanying Khrushchev on his visits to various locations on behalf of the U.S. government are: U.S. Permanent Representative to the United Nations Lodge, U.S. Ambassador to the Soviet Union Thompson, Chief of Protocol of the U.S. Department of State Buchanan, and Acting Assistant Secretary of State for European Affairs Kohler.

Also accompanying Khrushchev and his entourage is the Soviet Ambassador to the United States, Menshikov.

Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev and his entourage arrived in New York at 11:54 a.m. local time on the 17th.

The Mayor of New York, Wagner, held a luncheon on the 17th to entertain Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev. More than two thousand people attended the banquet, including prominent New York politicians and social activists, figures from the business community, and leaders of the municipal authorities.

People from all walks of life in New York welcomed Khrushchev with warm and sustained applause. U.S. Permanent Representative to the United Nations Lodge and New York Mayor Wagner spoke at the banquet, expressing their welcome for Khrushchev's visit to New York.

Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev delivered a reply. Khrushchev said that the Soviet Union hopes to live in peace and friendship with all countries, regardless of their systems. He emphasized that peaceful coexistence between countries with different social and political systems is very important.

Xinhua News Agency, September 18th -- According to TASS: The New York Economic Club hosted a banquet on the 17th to entertain Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev. Nearly two thousand people attended the banquet. Among them were managers of major New York banks, commercial firms, and industrial companies, as well as prominent economic figures.

When Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev and his entourage appeared in the hall, the attendees greeted them with sustained applause. The director of the club, Woodman, introduced Khrushchev and his entourage to the attendees.

Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev delivered a long speech at the banquet. Khrushchev's speech was warmly welcomed by the participants.

Finally, Khrushchev answered a series of questions raised by people in the business community.

The attendees listened very carefully to Khrushchev's speech and his answers, his speech being frequently interrupted by applause. Former U.S. Ambassador to the Soviet Union, the great industrialist Harriman, held a reception on the evening of September 17th to welcome Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev.

Among those attending the reception were: Soviet Ambassador to the United States Menshikov, U.S. Permanent Representative to the United Nations Lodge, as well as managers of many large American companies, figures from the business community, and people from other sectors.

### 赫鲁晓夫到达纽约参观访问  纽约市长和经济俱乐部等分别设宴欢迎苏联贵宾

1959-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫到达纽约参观访问
    纽约市长和经济俱乐部等分别设宴欢迎苏联贵宾
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫和他的随行人员17日晨乘火车离华盛顿赴纽约，开始他们横贯美国为期八天的访问。
    美国政府方面陪同赫鲁晓夫到各地访问的有：美国驻联合国常任代表洛奇，美国驻苏联大使汤普森，美国国务院典礼局局长布坎南，美国代理欧洲事务助理国务卿科勒。
    陪同赫鲁晓夫和他的随行人员一起访问的还有苏联驻美国大使缅希科夫。
    苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫和他的随行人员在17日当地时间十一点五十四分抵达纽约。
    纽约市长华格纳17日举行午宴招待苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫。出席宴会的有纽约著名政治家和社会活动家，实业界人士，市政当局领导人等共两千多人。
    纽约各界人士用经久不息的热烈掌声欢迎赫鲁晓夫。美国驻联合国常任代表洛奇和纽约市长华格纳在宴会上讲话，表示欢迎赫鲁晓夫访问纽约。
    苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫致答词。赫鲁晓夫说，苏联希望同一切国家和平、友好地相处，不管这些国家的制度如何。他着重指出，不同社会和政治制度的国家和平共处是非常重要的。
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社讯：纽约经济俱乐部17日设宴招待苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫。参加宴会的将近两千人。这里有纽约的大银行、大商行和大工业公司的经理以及著名的经济活动家等。
    当苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫及其随行人员出现在大厅时，到会者报以经久不息的掌声。俱乐部主任伍德曼向到会者介绍了赫鲁晓夫及其随行人员。
    苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫在宴会上发表了长篇演说。赫鲁晓夫的演说受到与会者的热烈欢迎。
    最后，赫鲁晓夫回答了实业界人士提出的一系列问题。
    到会者十分注意地倾听了赫鲁晓夫的演说和他的答问，他的讲话不时被掌声打断。前美国驻苏大使、大工业家哈里曼9月17日晚间举行招待会，欢迎苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫。
    出席招待会的有：苏联驻美国大使缅希科夫、美国常驻联合国代表洛奇，以及美国许多大公司的经理、实业界人士和其他各界人士。
