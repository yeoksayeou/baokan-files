Title: Nigerian Trade Union Congress Chairman and Two Uruguayan Journalists Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-24
Page: 6
　　
Xinhua News Agency, 23rd—Michael Imoudu, Chairman of the Nigerian Trade Union Congress and Chairman of the Nigerian Railway and Port Workers' Union, and Uruguayan journalists Carlos Borche and Hugo Rodriguez Vera, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.
　　The guests are invited to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 尼日利亚工会大会主席和乌拉圭两位记者到京

1959-09-24
第6版()
专栏：

    尼日利亚工会大会主席和乌拉圭两位记者到京
    新华社23日讯　尼日利亚工会大会主席、尼日利亚铁路港口工会主席迈克尔·伊穆杜，乌拉圭新闻工作者卡洛斯·博尔切和乌果·罗德里格塞·贝拉，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    客人们是应邀来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
