Title: Vietnam Holds Chinese Book Distribution Week
Author:
Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 23rd. To celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Vietnam Central Book and Periodical Distribution Office and the Vietnam Book and Periodical Import and Export Office will hold a "Chinese Book Distribution Week" from September 26th to October 5th.

During the "Distribution Week," Chinese-published books, collections of paintings, albums, picture cards, posters, records, and stamps in Chinese, Vietnamese, English, and French will be distributed in Hanoi, Haiphong, Nam Dinh, Thanh Hoa, Nghe An, Hon Gai, Hai Ninh, Lang Son, Cao Bang, Lao Cai, and Thai Nguyen. The books and periodicals include 200 titles, including important documents of the Chinese Communist Party and the government, and works by Chairman Mao Zedong and Chairman Liu Shaoqi. In addition, there are many philosophical works and famous literary works by Lu Xun, Mao Dun, Zhao Shuli, and others.

### 越南举行中国图书发行旬

1959-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    越南举行中国图书发行旬
    新华社河内23日电　为庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年，越南中央书刊发行所和越南书刊进出口所将从9月26日到10月5日举行“中国图书发行旬”。
    “发行旬”期间，在河内、海防、南定、清化、宜安、鸡广、海宁、谅山、高平、老街、太原等地将发行中国出版的中、越、英、法等文书籍、画集、画册、画片、招贴画、唱片、邮票等。书刊包括二百种，其中包括中国共产党和政府的重要文件，毛泽东主席和刘少奇主席的著作等。此外，还有许多哲学著作和鲁迅、茅盾、赵树理等人著名的文学作品。
