Title: British Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-08
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th – The British Cultural Delegation, consisting of five members led by Sir Herbert Read, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries to visit China and attend the National Day celebrations.

### 英国文化代表团到达北京

1959-09-08
第6版()
专栏：

    英国文化代表团到达北京
    据新华社7日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问并参加国庆节观礼的英国文化代表团一行五人，在团长赫伯特·瑞德爵士率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
