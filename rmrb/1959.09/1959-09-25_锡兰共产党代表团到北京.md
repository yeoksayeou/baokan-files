Title: Ceylon Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th. The Ceylon Communist Party delegation, invited by the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the nation, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation leader is K.P. de Silva, member of the Central Politburo of the Ceylon Communist Party, and the delegation members are P. Kumarasiri and A. Apphami, members of the Central Politburo of the Ceylon Communist Party.

Welcoming them at the airport were Li Fuchun, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the CPC and Secretary of the Secretariat, Li Weihan, member of the Central Committee, and Wu Xiuquan, among others.

### 锡兰共产党代表团到北京

1959-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    锡兰共产党代表团到北京
  新华社24日讯　应中国共产党中央委员会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的锡兰共产党代表团团长、锡兰共产党中央政治局委员克·普·德·席尔瓦，代表团团员：锡兰共产党中央政治局委员普·库马拉西里和阿·阿普哈米，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记李富春，中央委员李维汉、伍修权等。
