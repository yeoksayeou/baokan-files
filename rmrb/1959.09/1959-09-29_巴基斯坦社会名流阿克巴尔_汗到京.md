Title: Pakistani Social Figure Akbar Khan Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-09-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. Mr. Akbar Khan, a Pakistani social figure, and his wife and daughter, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the China-Pakistan Friendship Association to visit China and attend the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 巴基斯坦社会名流阿克巴尔·汗到京

1959-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦社会名流阿克巴尔·汗到京
    新华社28日讯　应中国巴基斯坦友好协会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的巴基斯坦社会名流阿克巴尔·汗先生和他的夫人、女儿，今天乘飞机到达北京。
