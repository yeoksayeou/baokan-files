Title: Party and State Leaders Receive Returned Students
Author: 
Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, September 24th - Party and state leaders Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Peng Zhen, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, Lu Dingyi, Kang Sheng, Bo Yibo, Nie Rongzhen, Guo Moruo, Lin Feng and other comrades, this afternoon received students who have returned from studying in the Soviet Union and other brotherly countries. Yang Xiufeng, Zhang Jichun, An Ziwen, Zhou Yang, Fan Changjiang, Zeng Yongquan and other comrades also attended the reception. (Attached picture)
    Party and state leaders Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De and other comrades received the students who returned from studying in the Soviet Union and other brotherly countries on September 24. The picture shows the scene of the reception. Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao

### 党和国家领导人接见回国留学生

1959-09-25
第3版()
专栏：

    党和国家领导人接见回国留学生
    新华社24日电　党和国家的领导人刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、彭真、陈毅、李富春、陆定一、康生、薄一波、聂荣臻、郭沫若、林枫等同志，今天下午接见了从苏联和其他兄弟国家学习回国的留学生。参加这次接见的还有杨秀峰、张际春、安子文、周扬、范长江、曾涌泉等同志。（附图片）
    党和国家的领导人刘少奇、周恩来、朱德同志等9月24日接见了从苏联和其他兄弟国家学习回国的留学生。图为接见时情形新华社记者　孟庆彪摄
