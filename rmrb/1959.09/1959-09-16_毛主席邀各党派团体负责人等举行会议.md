Title: Chairman Mao Invites Leaders of Various Parties and Organizations to a Meeting
Author:
Date: 1959-09-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 15th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, on September 15th, invited leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations, prominent non-party democratic人士 (ren shi - people) and prominent figures from cultural and educational circles to a meeting. At the meeting, discussions were held on the issues of opposing right-leaning tendencies, boosting morale, adhering to the general line of socialist construction, granting amnesty to various criminals who have truly turned over a new leaf during the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, and removing the "rightist" label from rightists who have indeed shown improvement.

Chairman Mao Zedong delivered an important speech at the symposium.

Those who attended today's meeting included:

Vice Chairmen of the CPC Central Committee Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, and Zhu De; Members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, Peng Zhen, and Chen Yi; Alternate Members Lu Dingyi, Chen Boda, and Kang Sheng; Members of the CPC Central Committee Li Weihan, Hu Qiaomu, and Xi Zhongxun; Alternate Members of the CPC Central Committee Liu Ren, Xu Bing, and Zhang Su; Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily Wu Lengxi;

Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China Soong Ching Ling;

Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Li Jishen, Vice Chairmen He Xiangning, Cheng Qian, Cai Tingkai, Zhang Zhizhong, and Xiong Kewu, Standing Committee Members Lu Han, Shao Lizi, and Mei Gongbin;

Chairman of the China Democratic League Shen Junru, Vice Chairmen Yang Mingxuan, Shi Liang, Gao Chongmin, Hu Yuzhi, Deng Chumin, Wu Han, and Chu Tunan, Standing Committee Member Min Ganghou;

Chairman of the China Democratic National Construction Association Huang Yanpei, Vice Chairmen Li Zhuchen, Shi Fuliang, Hu Ziang, and Sun Qimeng, Standing Committee Members Rong Yiren and Sun Xiaocun;

Vice Chairmen of the China Association for Promoting Democracy Wang Shao'ao, Xu Guangping, and Yang Dongchun, Standing Committee Member Xu Boxin;

Chairman of the Presidium of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party Ji Fang, Presidium Member Guo Zechen;

Chairman of the China Zhi Gong Party Chen Qiyou;

Chairman of the Jiusan Society Xu Deheng, Vice Chairmen Zhou Peiyuan and Pan Shu, Standing Committee Member Sun Chengpei;

Secretary-General of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League Xu Mengshan;

Minister of Food Sha Qianli, Minister of Textile Industry Jiang Guangnai, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Forestry Liu Wenhui, Minister of Water Resources and Electric Power Fu Zuoyi, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Xiruo;

Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation Cai Chang, Vice Chairman Li Dequan, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China Hu Yaobang, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Chairman of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Mao Dun, Presidium Member Lao She, Chairman of the China Association for Science and Technology Li Siguang, Vice Chairman Zhu Kezhen, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Chen Shutong, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese Chen Jiageng, Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association Wang Yunsheng;

President of Peking University Ma Yinchu, Vice President Lu Ping, President of Tsinghua University Jiang Nanxiang, President of Beijing Normal University Chen Yuan. (Attached Photo)

### 毛主席邀各党派团体负责人等举行会议

1959-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席邀各党派团体负责人等举行会议
    毛主席作了重要讲话。会议座谈了关于反右倾、鼓干劲、坚持社会主义建设总路线的问题，关于在中华人民共和国成立十周年期间对确已改恶从善的各种罪犯实行特赦，以及对确实表现改好了的右派分子摘掉右派帽子的问题。
    新华社15日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志，9月15日邀集各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人，著名无党派民主人士和著名文化教育界人士举行会议。在会议上就关于反右倾、鼓干劲、坚持社会主义建设总路线的问题，关于在中华人民共和国成立十周年期间对确已改恶从善的各种罪犯实行特赦，以及对确实表现改好了的右派分子摘掉右派帽子的问题，进行了座谈。
    毛泽东同志在座谈会上作了重要的讲话。
    参加今天的会议的有：
    中共中央副主席刘少奇、周恩来、朱德，中共中央政治局委员林伯渠、董必武、彭真、陈毅，候补委员陆定一、陈伯达、康生，中共中央委员李维汉、胡乔木、习仲勋，中共中央候补委员刘仁、徐冰、张苏，人民日报总编辑吴冷西；
