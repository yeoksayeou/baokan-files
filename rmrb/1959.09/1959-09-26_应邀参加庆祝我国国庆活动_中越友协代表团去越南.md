Title: Chinese-Vietnamese Friendship Association Delegation to Vietnam to Attend Celebrations of China's National Day

Author: 
Date: 1959-09-26
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 25th. At the invitation of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association to participate in the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the founding of our country, the Chinese-Vietnamese Friendship Association delegation of four people, led by Yang Xiufeng, President of the Chinese-Vietnamese Friendship Association, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Minister of Education, took a plane from Beijing to Vietnam today.
    Those who saw them off at the airport included Sun Xiaocun, Vice President of the Chinese-Vietnamese Friendship Association, Ding Xilin, Director of the Council of the Chinese-Vietnamese Friendship Association and Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, and Dong Chuncai, Vice Minister of Education. Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Tran Tu Binh, also saw them off at the airport.

### 应邀参加庆祝我国国庆活动  中越友协代表团去越南

1959-09-26
第2版()
专栏：

    应邀参加庆祝我国国庆活动
    中越友协代表团去越南
    新华社25日讯　应越中友好协会邀请到越南民主共和国参加庆祝我国建国十周年活动的中越友好协会代表团一行四人，在中越友好协会会长、中共中央委员、教育部部长杨秀峰率领下，今天乘飞机由北京启程去越南。
    到机场送行的，有中越友好协会副会长孙晓村，中越友协理事、对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林，教育部副部长董纯才等。越南驻华大使陈子平等也到机场送行。
