Title: New Danish Ambassador to China Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-09-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Denmark to China, Mr. Bartholdy, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 18th. Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was at the airport to welcome him.
    Mr. Andersen, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Danish Embassy in China, and embassy officials were also at the airport to welcome him.

### 丹麦新任驻华大使到京

1959-09-20
第6版()
专栏：

    丹麦新任驻华大使到京
    新华社19日讯　丹麦王国新任驻华特命全权大使巴特森18日乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有外交部礼宾司副司长马振武。
    丹麦驻华大使馆临时代办安徒生和大使馆官员也前往机场欢迎。
