Title: Delegation of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association to North Korea to Participate in Activities for the Month of Korea-China Friendship
Author:
Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 24th - A four-member delegation of the China-Korea Friendship Association, led by Wei Chuantong, Vice President of the Association, left Beijing by train today for the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

　　The delegation was invited by the Korea-China Friendship Association to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to participate in activities for the Month of Korea-China Friendship.

### 参加朝中友好月活动  中朝友协代表团赴朝鲜

1959-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    参加朝中友好月活动
    中朝友协代表团赴朝鲜
    新华社24日讯　中朝友好协会代表团一行四人，在友协副会长魏传统率领下，今天乘火车离开北京去朝鲜民主主义人民共和国。
    代表团是应朝中友好协会邀请，到朝鲜民主主义人民共和国参加朝中友好月活动的。
