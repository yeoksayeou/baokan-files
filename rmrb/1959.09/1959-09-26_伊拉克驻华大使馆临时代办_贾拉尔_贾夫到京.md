Title: Iraqi Embassy in China Chargé d'affaires Jalal Jaf Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: The Chargé d'affaires of the Iraqi Republic's Embassy in China, Jalal Jaf, arrived in Beijing by plane today. Zhao Shi, a specialist from the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was at the airport to welcome him.

### 伊拉克驻华大使馆临时代办  贾拉尔·贾夫到京

1959-09-26
第2版()
专栏：

    伊拉克驻华大使馆临时代办
    贾拉尔·贾夫到京
    新华社25日讯　伊拉克共和国驻华大使馆临时代办贾拉尔·贾夫今天乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有外交部礼宾司专员赵实。
