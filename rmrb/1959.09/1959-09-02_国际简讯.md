International Briefs

    President Sukarno Visits North Sumatra
    Jakarta Dispatch: Indonesian President Sukarno left Jakarta on the morning of September 1st to visit Aceh and Kalimantan in North Sumatra.
    In addition, ten ambassadors to Indonesia from other countries were also invited to visit these places with Sukarno.
    Aceh is a place where "Islamic Army" rebels are rampant. Some of the "Islamic Army" have recently been negotiating surrender with the government, while the rest of the "Islamic Army" are still carrying out harassment.
    Kassem Receives British Ambassador to Iraq
    Baghdad Dispatch: According to Baghdad Radio, Iraqi Prime Minister Kassem received the British Ambassador to Iraq, Sir Humphrey Trevelyan, on the afternoon of August 31st. The Iraqi Minister of Finance, Hadid, was also present. According to the broadcast, the two sides discussed the establishment of friendly relations between the two countries.

### 国际简讯

1959-09-02
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    苏加诺总统赴北苏门答腊访问
    雅加达讯　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺于1日上午离开雅加达前往北苏门答腊的亚齐和加里曼丹访问。
    另外还有十个国家的驻印度尼西亚大使也被邀请同苏加诺一起去访问这些地方。
    亚齐是“伊斯兰教军”叛乱分子猖獗活动的地方。那里的部分“伊斯兰教军”最近一直在同政府谈判投降的问题，而其余的“伊斯兰教军”仍然在进行骚扰。
    卡塞姆接见英国驻伊拉克大使
    巴格达讯　据巴格达电台广播，伊拉克总理卡塞姆在8月31日下午接见了英国驻伊拉克大使杜维廉，在座的有伊拉克财政部长哈迪德。据广播，双方讨论了建立两国之间的友好关系的问题。
