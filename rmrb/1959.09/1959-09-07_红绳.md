Title: Red Rope
Author: Yán Zhèn
Date: 1959-09-07
Page: 8

    Red Rope
    Yan Zhen
    Outside the village, by the stream, stands an ancient willow,
    Its branches bending and drooping low,
    Dense leaves facing the summer breeze,
    Gently bubbling the ceaseless stream.
    Beautiful red ropes hang from the willow branches,
    Children swing on them, making a swing set there,
    An old woman stands beneath the tree,
    She is the new nursery worker of the commune.
    The beautiful red rope sways gently,
    Reminding her of the past:
    The red rope suddenly turns into a red waistband,
    From which hangs a poor peasant girl…
    A burst of laughter interrupts her thoughts,
    The red rope reappears before her eyes:
    The children are like a flock of spring butterflies,
    Spinning and twirling with the red rope.
    The beautiful red rope sways gently,
    Again, it stirs up endless reflections:
    The red rope suddenly turns into a blood-stained hemp rope,
    Hanging from it, the arm of a Red Army soldier…
    Another burst of laughter interrupts her thoughts,
    The red rope reappears before her eyes:
    The children are like red lotuses blooming by the stream,
    Joyful shadows flashing in the water.
    The beautiful red rope is still swaying,
    Bringing to her mind another scene:
    Beneath the willow, a neighing warhorse is tied,
    The red rope is its swinging rein…
    A bell rings like rolling pearls in the distance,
    The children gather and run into the classroom,
    The old woman also leaves the ancient willow with a smile,
    Satisfied, she takes away a new impression.
    Outside the village, by the stream, stands an ancient willow,
    Its branches bending and drooping low,
    It knows the true weight of happiness,
    Because it has borne the blood and tears of the past…

### 红绳
严阵
1959-09-07
第8版()
专栏：

    红绳
    严阵
    村外的小溪边有一棵古柳，
    它的树枝弯曲地下垂，
    浓密的叶子迎着夏风，
    轻沸着回流不息的溪水。
    柳枝上挂下了美丽的红绳，
    孩子们在那儿荡起了秋千，
    一个老妈妈站在树下，
    她是公社的新保育员。
    美丽的红绳悠悠地飘荡，
    使她想起了那往日的时光：
    红绳顿时变成一条红色的腰带，
    上面悬着一个贫农的姑娘……
    一阵笑声把她的思绪打断，
    那条红绳重又出现在她的眼前：
    孩子们像一群春天的蝴蝶，
    正随着红绳不停地飞旋。
    美丽的红绳悠悠地飘荡，
    又引起了她那不尽的回想：
    红绳忽然变成一条血染的麻绳，
    上面吊着一只红军的臂膀……
    又一阵笑声把她的思绪打断，
    那条红绳重又在她的眼前出现：
    孩子们像溪边盛开的红莲，
    欢乐的影子闪动在溪水中间。
    美丽的红绳呵还在飘荡，
    使她想起了另一种景象：
    柳枝下拴着一匹嘶鸣的战马，
    红绳就是那摆动的马缰……
    滚珠似的铃声在远处响了，
    孩子们簇拥着跑进课堂，
    老妈妈也微笑着离开了古柳，
    她满意的带走了新的印象。
    村外的小溪边有一棵古柳，
    它的树枝弯曲地下垂，
    它知道幸福的真正分量，
    因为它承担过往日的血泪……
