Title: The Capital Dons Festive Attire
Author:
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. On the eve of the great tenth anniversary of the founding of the nation, the motherland’s capital – Beijing – appears exceptionally solemn and beautiful. Red flags flutter throughout the city, colorful arches stand tall, and the people of the city have made all preparations for the celebratory parade, joyfully welcoming the arrival of the festival.

    Tiananmen Square and East and West Chang'an Avenue are filled with a strong festive atmosphere. The huge portrait of Chairman Mao Zedong has been hung in the center of the red wall of Tiananmen, palace lanterns are hung high on the city tower, and red flags are flying. The towering golden slogans on the slogan towers on both sides of the square read:
    "Go all out, aim high, and build socialism with greater, faster, better, and more economical results!" "Long live the People's Commune!" "Long live the Great Leap Forward!" "Develop the Increase Production and Practice Economy Movement in a Comprehensive and Deep-going Way!" "Long live Marxism-Leninism!" "Long live the People's Republic of China!" "Long live the Chinese Communist Party!" "Long live Chairman Mao!"
    At night, countless huge lamp posts fitted with milky white bud-shaped lamp clusters illuminate Tiananmen Square as if it were daytime. The Tiananmen Gate tower and the Great Hall of the People, museums, and other buildings on both sides of the square, decorated with tens of thousands of electric lights, also shine brightly, dazzling everywhere, like a crystal world, attracting tens of thousands of citizens and foreign guests every night to come here to admire the beautiful festive night scene. The bouquets, charts, and models prepared by various agencies, factories, and schools throughout the city for the festival parade have all been prepared. Colorful theatrical floats and huge models of houses, machine tools, etc., fill the entire Dongdan Square, waiting to be reviewed by the party and state leaders during the festival.
    More and more good news is coming from workers across the city to celebrate the festival. As of today, 419 factories in the city have completed their third quarter production plans ahead of schedule, and 577 factories have completed their September production plans ahead of schedule. ### 首都披上节日盛装

1959-09-30
第3版()
专栏：

    首都披上节日盛装
    新华社29日讯　在伟大建国十周年的前夕，祖国的首都——北京显得分外庄严和美丽。全城红旗飘扬，彩牌林立，全市人民已做好了庆祝游行的一切准备工作，正喜气洋洋地迎接着节日的到来。
    天安门广场和东西长安街充满了浓重的节日气氛。毛泽东主席的巨幅画像已悬挂在天安门红墙的正中，城楼上宫灯高挂，红旗飘扬。矗立在广场两侧的标语塔上写着耀眼的金色大字标语：
    “鼓足干劲，力争上游，多快好省地建设社会主义！”“人民公社万岁！”“大跃进万岁！”“全面深入地开展增产节约运动！”“马克思列宁主义万岁！”“中华人民共和国万岁！”“中国共产党万岁！”“毛主席万岁！”
    夜晚，无数装着乳白色花蕾形灯簇的巨大灯柱，把天安门广场照耀得如同白昼，被成万盏电灯装饰起来的天安门城楼和广场两侧的人民大会堂、博物馆及其他建筑物，也齐放光明，到处灿烂夺目，如同水晶世界，每夜吸引着成千上万的市民和内外宾客，来到这里欣赏美丽的节日夜景。全市各机关、工厂、学校供节日游行用的花束、图表、模型已经全部准备好。五彩缤纷的戏剧彩车、房屋、机床等巨大的模型，摆满了整个东单广场，等待着在节日接受党和国家领导人的检阅。
    全市工人向节日献礼的捷报越来越多，到今天止，全市已有四百一十九个工厂提前完成了第三季度生产计划，有五百七十七个工厂提前完成了9月份生产计划。（附图片）
