Title: Polish People Enthusiastically Launch Celebrations

Author:

Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, September 25th: News continues to arrive from all parts of Poland that people from all walks of life are enthusiastically launching various activities to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

The Polish capital, Warsaw, is preparing to screen Chinese films, hold celebrations, and host exhibitions to widely celebrate the historic festival of the Chinese people. The Warsaw Classical Theatre is rehearsing the famous Chinese writer Cao Yu's play "Thunderstorm," preparing to perform it in October. This will be the first Chinese drama performed in a Polish theater.

Polish Radio has launched a radio lecture series entitled "Changes in the People's Republic of China in the Last Ten Years." Polish Television is working with the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association to prepare an interesting program: a "What Do You Know About China?" quiz night to increase the Polish people's understanding of China.

The Polish postal and telecommunications departments are preparing to issue commemorative stamps in October to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Currently, postal and telecommunications workers are working on exquisite designs for the commemorative stamps.

Provinces and cities across Poland are also preparing grand celebrations for the 10th anniversary of the founding of our country. Many provinces have established special preparatory committees composed of local Party and government leaders. The members of the Poznan Province Preparatory Committee include Krasko, First Secretary of the Provincial Committee of the United Workers' Party. The Koszalin Province Preparatory Committee is chaired by Tomar, Chairman of the Provincial People's Committee. Factories and enterprises throughout Poland are also holding activities to promote Sino-Polish friendship and celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The employees of the Gdańsk Shipyard recently named an about-to-be-launched ten-thousand-ton ship the "Beijing" to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and to express the friendship between the Polish and Chinese people. This ship will sail on the Far East route.

### 波人民热烈展开庆祝活动

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    波人民热烈展开庆祝活动
    据新华社华沙25日电　从波兰各地继续传来各界人民热烈展开各种活动，庆祝中华人民共和国建国十周年的消息。
    波兰首都华沙正在筹备放映中国电影，举行庆祝会和举办展览会，以广泛庆祝中国人民的历史性节日。华沙古典剧院正在排练中国作家曹禺的名剧“雷雨”，准备在10月间演出，这将是波兰剧院演出的第一个中国戏剧。
    波兰电台举办了题为“中华人民共和国十年来的变化”的广播讲座。波兰电视台正在和波中友好协会合作，准备一项有趣的节目：“关于中国你知道些什么？”问答晚会，以增加波兰人民对中国的了解。
    波兰的邮电部门准备在10月间发行庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年的纪念邮票。现在，邮电工人们正在为纪念邮票制作精美的图案。
    波兰各省市也都准备隆重庆祝我国建国十周年。不少省成立了专门的筹备委员会，由当地党政领导人员组成。波兹南省的筹备委员会委员中包括统一工人党省委第一书记克腊希科等人。柯萨林省的筹备委员会由省人民委员会主席托马尔担任主席。在波兰各地的工厂企业中，也都在举行宣传波中友好、庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年的活动。革但斯克造船厂的职工最近将一艘即将下水的万吨轮船命名为“北京”号，以庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年和表示波中人民的友谊。这只轮船将在远东航线上航行。
