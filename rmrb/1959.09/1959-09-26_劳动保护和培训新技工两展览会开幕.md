Title: Labor Protection and New Technician Training Exhibitions Open

Author:

Date: 1959-09-26
Page: 10

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: The National Labor Protection Exhibition, reflecting the achievements of China's labor protection work over the past ten years, opened in Beijing on the 26th.

This exhibition is jointly sponsored by the Ministry of Labor, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and relevant central departments. It has a comprehensive hall and thirteen industrial halls for metallurgy, coal, machinery, construction, water conservancy and electricity, agriculture and forestry, geology, petroleum, railway, transportation, chemical industry, light industry, and textiles. There is also a Soviet pavilion dedicated to introducing the Soviet Union's assistance to China's labor protection cause, as well as the great achievements and experiences of the Soviet labor protection cause.

The comprehensive hall displays important documents, laws, and various charts and photos related to labor protection promulgated by the Party and the government. They vividly illustrate the Party and government's infinite concern for the working people.

Each industrial hall systematically introduces the labor protection measures and experiences of various industries with a large number of objects and models.

This exhibition is located in the newly built Labor Protection Exhibition Hall in Beijing. During the exhibition, in addition to receiving visitors, relevant units will be organized to exchange experiences.

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: The National New Technician Training Exhibition is scheduled to officially open on the 26th at the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace.

This exhibition focuses on reflecting the tremendous achievements made in China in training technical workers over the past decade. In the past ten years, China has trained a total of 8.44 million new technical workers, of which more than 90% were trained through apprenticeships, and more than 200,000 were trained through technical schools.

The exhibition vividly reflects the changes in the lives of apprentices before and after liberation, introduces the rich experience of various industries in training technicians, and the achievements made by various technical schools in implementing the policy of combining education with productive labor.

This exhibition is jointly sponsored by the Ministry of Labor, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and the relevant central industrial and transportation departments. It is scheduled to last for about one month.

### 劳动保护和培训新技工两展览会开幕

1959-09-26
第10版()
专栏：

    劳动保护和培训新技工两展览会开幕
    据新华社25日讯　反映我国建国十年来劳动保护工作成就的全国劳动保护展览会26日在北京开幕。
    这个展览会是由劳动部、全国总工会和中央各有关部门共同举办的。它设有综合馆和冶金、煤炭、机械、建筑、水利电力、农林、地质、石油、铁道、交通、化工、轻工、纺织十三个产业馆。还设有苏联馆，专门介绍苏联对我国劳动保护事业的帮助，以及苏联劳动保护事业的伟大成就和经验。
    综合馆里陈列着党和政府颁布的有关劳动保护的重要文件、法令及各种图表照片。它们生动地说明了党和政府对劳动人民的无限关怀。
    各个产业馆用大量的实物和模型，系统地介绍了各行各业的劳动保护措施和经验。
    这个展览会设在北京新落成的劳动保护展览馆内。展出期间，除接待参观外，还要组织有关单位交流经验。
    据新华社25日讯　全国新技工培养训练展览会，定26日在北京市劳动人民文化宫正式开幕。
    这个展览会集中反映了我国建国十年来在培养训练技术工人方面的巨大成就。十年来，我国共计培养训练了八百四十四万名新技术工人，其中90％以上是通过学徒方式培养的，有二十多万人是通过技术学校培养的。
    展览会生动地反映了解放前后我国学徒生活的变化，介绍了各行各业在培养技工方面的丰富经验，以及各技工学校在贯彻教育与生产劳动相结合方针中所取得的成就。
    这个展览会是由劳动部、全国总工会、共青团中央和中央各有关工业交通部门联合举办的，预定展出一个月左右。
