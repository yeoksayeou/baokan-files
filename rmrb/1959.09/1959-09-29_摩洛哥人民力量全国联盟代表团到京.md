Title: Moroccan People's National Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: A delegation from the Moroccan People's National Union, headed by Mahdi Ben Barka, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Barka's wife accompanied him. They were invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit China and participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the country.

The deputy head of the delegation is Abd al-Rahman Zniber. Members include: Abdallahai Shami, Abd al-Rahman Sayah, Abu Teslam Jebli, and Ahmed Ben Clilou.

Greeting them at the airport were Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Zhang Tiesheng, head of the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe to the Middle East and Africa, and Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 摩洛哥人民力量全国联盟代表团到京

1959-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥人民力量全国联盟代表团到京
    新华社28日讯　以迈赫迪·本·巴尔卡为首的摩洛哥人民力量全国联盟代表团一行七人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。同来的有巴尔卡的夫人。他们是应中国人民外交学会的邀请来我国访问和参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
    代表团副团长是阿卜杜拉赫曼·兹尼巴尔。团员有：阿卜杜拉哈依·沙米，阿卜杜拉赫曼·赛雅，阿布台斯拉姆·杰布利，艾哈迈德·本·克利卢。
    前往机场迎接的有中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，前赴中东、非洲中国杂技艺术团团长张铁生和中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪。
