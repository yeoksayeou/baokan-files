Title: Our Party and State Leaders Receive Distinguished Guests from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-09-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: Party and state leaders Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Soong Ching Ling, Dong Biwu, Zhu De, Zhou Enlai, Lin Biao, Deng Xiaoping, and Peng Zhen, prior to the start of the celebrations for the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China this afternoon, received all members of the party and government delegations from the Soviet Union and other socialist countries, all members of the delegations from communist and workers' parties from various countries, the heads of the military delegations from Vietnam and Korea, the heads of government delegations and government representatives from Asian and African countries, as well as the heads of delegations from six international organizations. The reception took place in a very warm and friendly atmosphere.

1959-09-29
