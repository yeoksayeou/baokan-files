Title: Bolivian Cultural Representative Ricardo Anaya Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th. Dr. Ricardo Anaya, the Bolivian cultural representative who was invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China and attend the 10th Anniversary Celebration of the founding of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 玻利维亚文化代表  利卡尔多·阿纳亚到京

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    玻利维亚文化代表
    利卡尔多·阿纳亚到京
    新华社27日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的玻利维亚文化代表利卡尔多·阿纳亚博士，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
