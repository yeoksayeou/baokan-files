Title: Agricultural Machinery Manufactured in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1959-09-04
Page: 6

Korean Central News Agency dispatch (Xinhua News Agency dispatch):

Pyongyang's machine manufacturing plant responded to the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party's call to further promote agricultural mechanization, and rushed to produce various agricultural machines for the countryside. The picture on the right shows a batch of newly manufactured agricultural machines being loaded onto vehicles, ready to be transported to various rural areas in South Hwanghae Province.
