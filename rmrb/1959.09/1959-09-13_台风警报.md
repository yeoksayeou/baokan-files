Title: Typhoon Alert
Author:
Date: 1959-09-13
Page: 5

    Typhoon Alert
    Xinhua News Agency, 12th. The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a typhoon message at 12:00 noon on the 12th: The typhoon that landed in Guangdong from the South China Sea and entered Fujian has weakened. At 8:00 a.m. today, the center was located in northeastern Fujian. In the future, it will turn into a weak temperate cyclone and enter the sea via Zhejiang in the northeast. Except for the coastal areas of Zhejiang, the southern coastal areas of Jiangsu, and the nearby East China Sea, which will still have winds of about level 6 tonight, the wind force in other areas will continue to weaken. Tomorrow, the wind force in the southern coastal areas of Jiangsu, the coastal areas of Zhejiang, and the East China Sea will also weaken. In the next twenty-four hours, there will still be heavy rain to torrential rain in eastern Zhejiang and southeastern Jiangsu.

### 台风警报

1959-09-13
第5版()
专栏：

    台风警报
    新华社12日讯　中央气象科学研究所12日中午十二时发布台风消息：自南海登陆广东进入福建的台风，已经减弱，今天上午八时中心位置在福建东北部，未来将变为弱的温带气旋向东北方经浙江入海，除今天夜间浙江沿海、江苏南部沿海、及附近东海海面仍将有六级左右的大风外，其他地区的风力将继续减弱。明天江苏南部沿海、浙江沿海和东海海面的风力也将减弱。未来二十四小时内，浙江东部和江苏东南部仍有大雨到暴雨。
