Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Tanzan Ishibashi
Author:
Date: 1959-09-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi today received former Japanese Prime Minister Tanzan Ishibashi and held a two-hour meeting.

Liao Chengzhi was present at the meeting.

### 周总理接见石桥湛山

1959-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见石桥湛山
    新华社16日讯　周恩来总理和陈毅副总理今天接见了日本前任首相石桥湛山，进行了两小时的会谈。
    接见时在座的有廖承志。
