Title: Cyrankiewicz Speaks at Reception: Polish-Soviet Friendship is Being Increasingly Consolidated
Author:
Date: 1959-09-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 5th dispatch from Moscow: The Polish Industrial Exhibition opened on the afternoon of September 4th in Moscow's Central Cultural Rest and Recreation Park. This exhibition is the largest one Poland has held both domestically and internationally. The exhibition features more than 12,000 items, reflecting Poland's significant achievements in economic development.
    First Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPSU and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Khrushchev, and other leaders of the Soviet Party and government attended the opening ceremony.
    Khrushchev and Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz, spoke at the opening ceremony.
    Xinhua News Agency, September 5th dispatch from Moscow: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Khrushchev, held a breakfast banquet on September 5th to entertain Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Planning Commission of the Council of Ministers, Jędrychowski, and Polish Foreign Minister Rapacki, who are currently in Moscow.
    Also present at the banquet were Aristov, Brezhnev, Kirichenko, Mukhitdinov, Suslov, Kosygin, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Zasyadko, and Foreign Minister Gromyko, among others.
    The banquet proceeded in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

### 西伦凯维兹在招待会上讲话  波苏两国的友谊正日益巩固

1959-09-06
第5版()
专栏：

   西伦凯维兹在招待会上讲话  波苏两国的友谊正日益巩固
   据新华社5日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：波兰工业展览会4日下午在莫斯科中央文化休息公园开幕。这个展览会是波兰在国内外举办过的展览会中最大的一次展览会，展览品有一万二千多件，它们反映了波兰在经济发展方面所取得的重大成就。
    苏共中央第一书记、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫及苏联党和政府的其他领导人出席了展览会开幕式。
    赫鲁晓夫和波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹在开幕典礼上讲了话。
    据新华社5日电　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫5日举行早宴，招待目前正在莫斯科的波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹、部长会议计划委员会主席英德里霍夫斯基、波兰外交部长腊帕茨基等人。
    出席宴会的还有阿里斯托夫、勃列日涅夫、基里钦科、穆希金诺夫、苏斯洛夫、柯西金、苏联部长会议副主席扎夏迪科、外交部长葛罗米柯等人。
    宴会在亲切友好的气氛中进行。
