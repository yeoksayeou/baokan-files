Title: Vietnam to Hold Chinese Film Week, Chinese Film Workers Delegation Arrives in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1959-09-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 22nd: According to the 1959 implementation plan of the Sino-Vietnamese Cultural Cooperation Agreement, the three-member Chinese film workers delegation, headed by Zheng Junli, arrived in Hanoi by plane this afternoon for a visit to Vietnam. The delegation members are Su Li and Wang Danfeng.

The delegation was warmly welcomed by Vietnamese film and literary figures at the airport.

The delegation will participate in the Chinese Film Week held to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

The Film Week will open on September 25th.

### 越南将举行中国电影旬  我电影工作者代表团到达河内

1959-09-24
第6版()
专栏：

    越南将举行中国电影旬
    我电影工作者代表团到达河内
    据新华社河内22电　根据中越文化合作协定1959年执行计划前来越南访问的中国电影工作者代表团一行三人，在团长郑君里率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达河内。团员有苏里和王丹凤。
    代表团在机场受到越南电影和文艺界人士的热烈欢迎。
    代表团将参加为庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年举行的中国电影旬。
    电影旬将在9月25日开幕。
