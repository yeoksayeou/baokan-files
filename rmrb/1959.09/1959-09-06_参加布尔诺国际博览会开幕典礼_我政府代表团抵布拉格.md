Title: Chinese Government Delegation Arrives in Prague to Attend Opening Ceremony of Brno International Fair
Author:
Date: 1959-09-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 4th. At the invitation of the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic, the Chinese Government Delegation, led by Xi Zhongxun, Vice Premier of the State Council, arrived here today by plane from Moscow to attend the opening ceremony of the Brno International Fair.

Those welcoming at the airport included Dvorjanski, First Deputy Prime Minister of the Czechoslovak Republic, as well as Krajcir, Minister of Foreign Trade, Reitmayer, Minister of Heavy Machinery Industry, and Grigor, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs.

Cao Ying, Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia (member of the delegation), also greeted them at the airport.

### 参加布尔诺国际博览会开幕典礼  我政府代表团抵布拉格

1959-09-06
第2版()
专栏：

    参加布尔诺国际博览会开幕典礼
    我政府代表团抵布拉格
    新华社布拉格4日电　应捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府邀请，参加布尔诺国际博览会开幕典礼的中国政府代表团，在国务院副总理习仲勋率领下，今天由莫斯科乘飞机到达这里。
    在机场欢迎的有：捷克斯洛伐克共和国第一副总理杜兰斯基，以及对外贸易部长克拉伊契尔、重型机器工业部长雷特马耶尔和外交部第一副部长格里哥尔。
    中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使（代表团团员）曹瑛也到机场迎接。
