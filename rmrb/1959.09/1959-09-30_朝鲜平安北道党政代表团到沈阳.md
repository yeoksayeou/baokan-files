Title: North Pyongan Province Delegation from North Korea Arrives in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 28th. At the invitation of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Liaoning Provincial People's Committee, the seven-member North Pyongan Province Party and Government Delegation from North Korea, led by Kim Il-ryong, Chairman of the North Pyongan Provincial Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, and Vice-Chairman Jang Kuk-chan of the North Pyongan Provincial People's Committee, arrived in Shenyang by train this evening to visit Liaoning Province and participate in our country's tenth anniversary celebrations.

### 朝鲜平安北道党政代表团到沈阳

1959-09-30
第3版()
专栏：

    朝鲜平安北道党政代表团到沈阳
    新华社沈阳28日电　应中共辽宁省委员会、辽宁省人民委员会邀请，前来辽宁省访问并参加我国建国十周年庆祝活动的朝鲜平安北道党政代表团一行七人，在团长、朝鲜劳动党平安北道委员会委员长金曰龙、副团长、朝鲜平安北道人民委员会副委员长张国赞率领下，今晚乘火车到达沈阳。
