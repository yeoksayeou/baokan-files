Title: NPC Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Mongolia; Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling Welcomes Them at the Station; Delegation Head Lin Boqu Cables Thanks to Chairman Jalkhasaikhan
Author:
Date: 1959-09-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 5th. The National People's Congress delegation, led by head Lin Boqu and deputy head Huang Yanpei, returned to Beijing by special train this morning after concluding its friendly visit to the Mongolian People's Republic.

Vice Chairpersons Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong were among those who greeted them at the station.

Also at the station to welcome them were members of the Standing Committee of the NPC, Wang Weizhou, Shi Liang, Chen Shaoxian, Wu Xinyu, Ji Fang, Hu Ziang, Hu Yuzhi, Xie Fumin; Deputy Secretaries-General Yu Xinqing, Sun Qimeng, Lian Guan, Chen Cisheng; Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council Qi Yanming; Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei; Deputy Secretary-General of the CPPCC National Committee Shen Bocun; leaders of democratic parties and people's organizations Mei Gongbin, Min Ganghou, Sun Xiaocun, Yang Bin; and President of the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association Zhang Zhixiang.

D. Tserensodnom, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian Embassy in China, was also at the station to welcome them.

Xinhua News Agency, September 5th. Lin Boqu, head of the delegation of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to Jalkhasaikhan, Chairman of the Great Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, when leaving the territory of the Mongolian People's Republic, thanking them for their care and reception of the delegation. The telegram reads as follows:

To Comrade Jalkhasaikhan, Respected Chairman of the Great Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic:

We have brought the sincere friendship of the Chinese people to the Mongolian people. After a few days of friendly visits in the Mongolian People's Republic, we are returning to China with the profound friendship of the Mongolian people. As we leave the territory of the Mongolian People's Republic, I, on behalf of all the comrades of the delegation of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, would like to express our sincere gratitude to you and, through you, to the Party, government, Great Hural, and people of Mongolia once again, for your meticulous care and warm reception.

We will convey to the Chinese people the great achievements of the Mongolian people in building socialism and the deep friendship of the Mongolian people towards the Chinese people. I firmly believe that through this friendly visit, the fraternal friendship between our two countries will be further strengthened.

I wish the Mongolian People's Republic prosperity and strength. I wish the heroic Mongolian people continued new achievements in the cause of building socialism and defending world peace.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Lin Boqu, Head of the Delegation of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
                                            September 4, 1959

### 人大代表团访问蒙古后返京  宋庆龄副主席等到车站迎接  林伯渠团长电谢贾尔卡赛汗主席

1959-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    人大代表团访问蒙古后返京
    宋庆龄副主席等到车站迎接
    林伯渠团长电谢贾尔卡赛汗主席
    新华社5日讯　全国人民代表大会访问蒙古人民共和国代表团结束了在蒙古的友好访问以后，在团长林伯渠和副团长黄炎培率领下，今天上午乘专车回到北京。
    宋庆龄副主席，李济深、彭真、陈叔通副委员长等到车站迎接。
    到车站迎接的，还有人大常委会委员王维舟、史良、陈劭先、武新宇、季方、胡子昂、胡愈之、谢扶民，副秘书长余心清、孙起孟、连贯、陈此生，国务院副秘书长齐燕铭，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，政协全国委员会副秘书长申伯纯，民主党派和人民团体的负责人梅龚彬、闵刚侯、孙晓村、杨彬，中蒙友好协会会长张致祥等。
    蒙古驻中国大使馆临时代办丹格苏伦也到车站欢迎。
    新华社5日讯　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会代表团团长林伯渠，在离开蒙古人民共和国国土的时候，致电蒙古人民共和国大呼拉尔主席贾尔卡赛汗，感谢他们对代表团的关怀和接待。电文如下：蒙古人民共和国大呼拉尔主席敬爱的贾尔卡赛汗同志：
    我们把中国人民诚挚的友情带给了蒙古人民，在蒙古人民共和国进行友好访问几天之后，我们带着蒙古人民的深情厚谊返回中国。当我们离开蒙古人民共和国国土的时候，我代表中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会代表团全体同志谨向您并通过您向蒙古的党、政府、大呼拉尔和蒙古人民，再一次表示诚恳的谢意，感谢你们对我们无微不至的关怀和热烈的接待。
    我们将把蒙古人民在建设社会主义事业中获得的巨大成就和蒙古人民对中国人民的深厚友情转告中国人民。我深信通过这次友好访问，我们两国兄弟般的友谊将会进一步增强。
    祝蒙古人民共和国繁荣富强。祝英雄的蒙古人民在建设社会主义和捍卫世界和平事业中，不断取得新的成就。
            中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会代表团团长　林伯渠
                                            1959年9月4日（附图片）
