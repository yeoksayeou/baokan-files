Title: Active Preparations for Celebrations in Various Parts of Poland

Author: 
Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, September 24th: According to the Polish Press Agency, various activities are being carried out in various parts of Poland to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of our country.

    The Polish-Chinese Friendship Association is currently preparing various celebration activities in provinces and cities to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of our country. Chinese evenings will be held in large enterprises. Those enterprises that manufacture products for China are making special and grand preparations to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Schools in various places will hold meetings to discuss various issues related to China.

    In order to welcome the 10th anniversary of the founding of our country, the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party organized a lecture entitled "The Significance of the People's Republic of China for the Development of the Liberation Movement" for activists in various provinces and cities, as well as activists in large enterprises.

    In the hall of the Palace of Culture and Science in Warsaw, the exhibition "Ten Years of Achievements of the People's Republic of China" has opened. The Polish-Chinese Friendship Association exhibited a set of Chinese pottery in the National Museum of Krakow and is preparing to hold exhibitions introducing China in various provinces and cities.

### 波兰各地积极筹备庆祝活动

1959-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    波兰各地积极筹备庆祝活动
    新华社华沙24日电  据波兰通讯社报道：波兰各地正在开展各种活动，庆祝我国建国十周年。
    波中友好协会目前正在各省市筹备举行庆祝我国建国十周年的各种庆祝活动。在大企业中将举行中国晚会。那些为中国制造产品的企业正在特别隆重地准备庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年。各地的学校将开会讨论有关中国的各种问题。
    为了迎接我国建国十周年，波兰统一工人党中央委员会宣传鼓动部为各省市的积极分子以及大企业的积极分子组织了题为“中华人民共和国对解放运动发展的意义”的讲演。
    在华沙文化科学宫的大厅里，“中华人民共和国十年成就”展览会已经开幕。波中友好协会在克拉科夫国家博物馆展出了一套中国陶器，并准备在各省市举行介绍中国的展览会。
