Title: Decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on Allowing Municipalities Directly Under the Central Government and Larger Cities to Lead Counties and Autonomous Counties
Author:
Date: 1959-09-18
Page: 1

    Decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
    on Allowing Municipalities Directly Under the Central Government and Larger Cities to Lead Counties and Autonomous Counties
    Adopted at the Ninth Meeting of the Second National People's Congress Standing Committee on September 17, 1959
    In order to adapt to the rapid development of our country's socialist construction, especially the great leap forward in industrial and agricultural production and the rural people's communes since last year, to closely connect the economic relationship between urban and rural areas, to promote mutual support between industry and agriculture, and to facilitate the allocation of labor, it is decided that municipalities directly under the Central Government and larger cities may lead counties and autonomous counties.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会  关于直辖市和较大的市可以领导县、自治县的决定

1959-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常务委员会
    关于直辖市和较大的市可以领导县、自治县的决定
    1959年9月17日第二届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第九次会议通过
    为了适应我国社会主义建设事业的迅速发展，特别是去年以来工农业生产的大跃进和农村的人民公社化，密切城市和农村的经济关系，促进工农业的相互支援，便利劳动力的调配，决定：直辖市和较大的市可以领导县、自治县。
