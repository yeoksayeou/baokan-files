Title: Typhoon to Land in Fujian and Zhejiang

Author: 
Date: 1959-09-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. September - The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued an urgent typhoon warning at 8 PM this evening: At 4 PM today, the center of the typhoon was located approximately 100 kilometers southeast of Hualien Port in Taiwan, near 22.9 degrees north latitude and 122.4 degrees east longitude, with a maximum wind force of level 12 near the center. Currently, the typhoon center is moving towards the northwest by north at a speed of 12 to 14 knots per hour. It is expected that the typhoon center will pass through northern Taiwan and make landfall in the area between Fuzhou and Wenzhou around noon on the 4th. The wind force near the landing site will be level 12. After landfall, the typhoon will rapidly weaken and turn to move in a northerly direction. In the next 36 hours, the wind force in the central and northern coastal areas of Fujian, the southern part of Zhejiang and the coastal areas of Zhejiang, the Taiwan Strait, and the East China Sea will increase to level 8 to level 11. The wind force in the northern part of Zhejiang, the southern part of Jiangsu, and the southern part of the Yellow Sea will increase to level 6 to level 9. There will be heavy rain to torrential rain in northern Fujian and eastern Zhejiang.

### 台风将在闽浙登陆

1959-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    台风将在闽浙登陆
    新华社3日讯　中央气象科学研究所3日下午八时发布台风紧急警报：今天下午四时，台风中心位置在台湾的花莲港的东南一百公里左右的洋面上，就是北纬二二·九度，东经一二二·四度附近，中心附近最大风力有十二级。目前台风中心正以每小时十二至十四浬的速度，向西北偏北方向移动。预计未来台风中心，将穿过台湾北部，在4日中午前后在福州到温州之间的地区登陆，登陆地点附近的风力有十二级，台风登陆以后，将迅速减弱，并转向偏北方向移动。未来三十六小时内，福建中部和北部沿海、浙江南部和浙江沿海、台湾海峡、东海的风力将加大到八级至十一级，浙江北部、江苏南部、黄海南部的风力将加大到六级至九级，福建北部、浙江东部将有大雨到暴雨。
