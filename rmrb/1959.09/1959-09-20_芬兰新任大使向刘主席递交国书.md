Title: New Finnish Ambassador Presents Credentials to Chairman Liu
Author:
Date: 1959-09-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th—Finland’s new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Valvanne, accompanied by all the diplomatic staff of the embassy, presented his credentials to Chairman Liu Shaoqi this afternoon.

Present were: Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yu Peiwen, and Deputy Director of the Department of West European Affairs Song Zhiguang.

### 芬兰新任大使向刘主席递交国书

1959-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    芬兰新任大使向刘主席递交国书
    新华社19日讯　芬兰共和国新任驻中国特命全权大使瓦尔瓦尼偕大使馆全体外交官员，今天下午向刘少奇主席递交国书。
    在场的有：外交部副部长罗贵波，外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文、西欧司副司长宋之光。
