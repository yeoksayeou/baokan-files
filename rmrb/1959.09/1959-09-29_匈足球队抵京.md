Title: Hungarian Soccer Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. The Hungarian soccer team, invited to China to participate in the international friendly soccer matches, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

　　The soccer tournament will be participated in by the soccer teams of the Soviet Union, Hungary, and China. The matches will begin after National Day.

### 匈足球队抵京

1959-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

    匈足球队抵京
    新华社28日讯　应邀来我国参加国际足球友谊赛的匈牙利足球队在今天下午乘飞机抵达北京。
    这次足球赛将有苏联、匈牙利和中国的足球队参加。比赛将在国庆日以后开始。
