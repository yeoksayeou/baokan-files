Title: Algerian National Liberation Army Achieves New Victories: Completely Destroys French Colonialists' Challe Line
Author:
Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 23rd: The Supreme Command of the Algerian National Liberation Army announced in a military communique today that Algerian armed forces launched an attack on the French invading army's Challe Line on September 11th.

The communique stated that detachments of the Algerian National Liberation Army used automatic rifles, rocket launchers, and heavy weapons to launch the attack, completely destroying the Challe Line. The French army abandoned fifty French soldiers' bodies, fifteen destroyed armored vehicles, and eleven destroyed tanks, and was forced to retreat from the Challe Line. The Algerian National Liberation Army seized a large number of weapons.

The Supreme Command of the Algerian National Liberation Army said in a communique issued on the 22nd that between September 13th and 16th, the Algerian National Liberation Army attacked seven French military strongholds.

### 阿尔及利亚解放军又奏捷  完全摧毁了法国殖民者夏耳防线

1959-09-25
第7版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚解放军又奏捷
    完全摧毁了法国殖民者夏耳防线
    据新华社开罗23日电　阿尔及利亚民族解放军最高司令部今天发表的一项军事公报说，阿尔及利亚的武装力量在9月11日对法国侵略军的夏耳防线发动了进攻。
    公报说，阿尔及利亚民族解放军的分遣队用自动步枪、火箭炮和重武器发动进攻，完全摧毁了夏耳防线。法国军队遗弃了五十具法军尸体，十五辆被击毁的装甲车和十一辆被炸毁的坦克，被迫从夏耳防线撤退。阿尔及利亚民族解放军缴获了大量武器。
    阿尔及利亚民族解放军最高司令部在22日发表的公报说，在从9月13日到16日期间，阿尔及利亚民族解放军攻击了七个法军的据点。
