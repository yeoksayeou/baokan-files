Title: German Labor People's Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 26th. The Labor People's Delegation from the German Democratic Republic, headed by Adolf Dädel, Secretary of the Committee for the Struggle for German Unification, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.

The delegation was invited by eight organizations, namely the Sino-German Friendship Association, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Communist Youth League, the All-China Women's Federation, the All-China Youth Federation, the All-China Students' Federation, and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the country.

Among those who went to the airport to welcome them were the heads of the inviting organizations, Liu Changsheng, Liu Aifeng, Xu Zhizhen, Kang Keqing, Hu Keshi, Qu Tangliang, and Zhu Boshen.

The Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Wang Guans, also went to the airport to greet them.

### 德劳动人民代表团到京

1959-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    德劳动人民代表团到京
    新华社26日讯　以争取德国统一委员会书记阿道尔夫·戴德尔为首的德意志民主共和国劳动人民代表团一行十四人，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团是应中德友协、全总、和大、共青团、全国妇联、全国青联、全国学联、对外文协八个团体邀请前来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
    前往机场欢迎的，有邀请团体的负责人刘长胜、刘皑风、许之桢、康克清、胡克实、区棠亮、朱伯深等。
    德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔也前往机场迎接。
