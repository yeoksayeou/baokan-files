Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Premier Kim Il Sung Send Telegrams Congratulating the Soviet Union on the Lunar Landing of its Space Rocket
Author:
Date: 1959-09-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 14th: Chairman Ho Chi Minh sent a telegram today to Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, congratulating the Soviet Union on the successful arrival of its space rocket on the moon.

The telegram stated, "The Vietnamese people are extremely delighted by the successful arrival of the Soviet rocket on the moon. This is a new victory for Soviet science. We would like to extend our warmest fraternal congratulations to you, and please convey our sincere congratulations to the Soviet scientists, engineers, workers, and the Soviet people."

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 14th: Premier Kim Il Sung of the Cabinet of North Korea sent a telegram today to Khrushchev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, warmly congratulating the Soviet Union on the great achievement of the successful landing of its second space rocket on the surface of the moon.

The telegram stated, "The successful launch of the Soviet Union's second space rocket, marking a new stage in human research and conquest of outer space, demonstrates the superiority and power of the Soviet socialist system, makes a great contribution to the cause of world peace, and becomes a great symbol of the victory of communism."

The telegram stated, "This historic victory achieved by Soviet science makes the Korean people, who are advancing together with the Soviet people in the big family of the socialist camp, feel a high degree of pride, and further strengthens their confidence in victory in the struggle for socialist construction and the peaceful reunification of the fatherland."

### 胡志明主席和金日成首相  电贺苏联宇宙火箭抵月球

1959-09-15
第5版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席和金日成首相
    电贺苏联宇宙火箭抵月球
    新华社河内14日电　胡志明主席今天打电报给苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫和苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫，祝贺苏联宇宙火箭成功到达月球。
    电报说，“越南人民对苏联火箭成功到达月球感到十分高兴。这是苏联科学的新的胜利。我们谨向你们致最热烈的兄弟般的祝贺，并请你们把我们的真诚的祝贺转达给苏联的科学家、工程师、工人和苏联人民。”
    新华社平壤14日电　朝鲜内阁首相金日成今天打电报给苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫，热烈祝贺苏联发射的第二个宇宙火箭到达月球表面的伟大成就。
    电报说：“标志着人类研究和征服宇宙空间的一个新阶段的苏联第二个宇宙火箭的发射成功，显示了苏联社会主义制度的优越性和它的威力，对世界和平事业作出了巨大的贡献，并且成为共产主义胜利的伟大象征”。
    电报说：“苏联科学取得的这一历史性胜利，使在社会主义阵营的大家庭中同苏联人民一起前进的朝鲜人民感到高度的自豪。并进一步坚定了他们在为社会主义建设和争取和平统一祖国的斗争中的胜利信心。”
