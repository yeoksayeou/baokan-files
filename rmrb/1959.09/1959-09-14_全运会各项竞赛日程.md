Title: Schedule of Events for the National Games

Author: 
Date: 1959-09-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 13th – The First National Games officially opened today at the Beijing Workers' Stadium. Apart from some events (Group A Gliding, Rowing, etc.) which were held in advance, the vast majority of competitions will be held successively from the 14th to the 27th. The scheduled start and end dates and locations of the various competitions are as follows:

    Track and Field: September 13-22 (Beijing Workers' Stadium)
    Acrobatic Gymnastics: September 14-16 (Beijing Shichahai Gymnasium)
    Fencing Exhibition Match: September 14-17 (Beijing Institute of Physical Education)
    Group B Gliding: September 14-18 (Beijing Liangxiang Airport)
    Weightlifting: September 14-19 (Beijing Gymnasium)
    Swimming: September 14-20 (Beijing Gymnasium)
    Archery: September 14-21 (Beijing Guanyuan Stadium)
    Chinese Wrestling: September 14-21 (Beijing Beihai Stadium)
    International Freestyle Wrestling Exhibition Match: September 14-22 (Beijing Institute of Physical Education)
    International Greco-Roman Wrestling Exhibition Match: September 14-22 (Beijing Institute of Physical Education)
    Nautical Modeling: September 14-23 (Beijing Longtan Lake, National Sports Commission Auditorium)
    Chinese Chess: September 14-25 (Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace)
    Radio Telegraphy and Receiving: September 14-25 (Beijing Temple of Heaven Radio Club)
    Shooting: September 14-25 (Beijing Western Suburbs Shooting Range)
    Weiqi (Go): September 14-26 (Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace)
    International Chess: September 14-26 (Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace)
    Tennis: September 14-26 (Beijing Gymnasium)
    Baseball: September 14-26 (Beijing Institute of Physical Education)
    Volleyball: September 14-27 (Beijing East Chang'an Street Stadium, Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace, Tianjin Gymnasium)
    Table Tennis: September 14-27 (Beijing Gymnasium)
    Water Polo: September 14-24 (Beijing Taoranting Swimming Pool)
    Badminton: September 14-27 (Beijing Institute of Physical Education)
    Handball: September 15-25 (Beijing Workers' Stadium, Beijing Xianongtan Stadium)
    Women's Softball: September 15-27 (Beijing Institute of Physical Education)
    Diving: September 16-20 (Beijing Taoranting Swimming Pool)
    Aeronautical Modeling: September 16-24 (Beijing Liangxiang Airport)
    Parachute Tower Jumping: September 17-25 (Beijing Parachute Tower Club)
    Motorcycle Ring Road: September 19-20 (Beijing Western Suburbs Laosan)
    Gymnastics: September 20-27 (Beijing Shichahai Gymnasium)
    Wushu (Martial Arts): September 22-25 (Beijing Beihai Stadium)
    Road Cycling: September 23-26 (Beijing-Tianjin Highway)
    Velodrome Cycling Exhibition Match: September 24-25 (Beijing Velodrome)
    Motorcycle Cross-country: September 25-26 (Beijing Western Suburbs Laosan)

The football and basketball finals, which started simultaneously in Beijing and Tianjin on September 1st and 4th respectively, will conclude simultaneously on September 27th.

Polo, horse racing, and steeplechase horse racing, which began on August 27th and September 1st respectively in Hohhot, will end in mid-September.

Nautical multi-event competition and water motorboat exhibition matches began on September 8th in Qingdao and will end on September 17th.

Aircraft parachute jumping competition began on September 11th at Beijing Liangxiang Airport and will end on September 19th.
