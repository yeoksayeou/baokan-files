Title: New British Chargé d'Affaires to China, Mr. C.T. Crowe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 14th - The new British Chargé d'Affaires to China, Mr. C.T. Crowe, arrived in Beijing by train today.

Han Xu, a specialist from the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was at the station to welcome him.

Officials from the British Chargé d'Affaires office in China were also at the station to welcome him.

### 英国新任驻华代办  施棣华抵京

1959-09-15
第5版()
专栏：

    英国新任驻华代办
    施棣华抵京
    新华社14日讯　英国新任驻我国代办施棣华先生今天乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有外交部礼宾司专员韩叙。
    英国驻华代办处官员也前往车站欢迎。
