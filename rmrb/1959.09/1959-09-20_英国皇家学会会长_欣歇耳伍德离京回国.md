Title: President of the British Royal Society, Hinshelwood, Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1959-09-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 19th: Sir Cyril Norman Hinshelwood, President of the British Royal Society, left Beijing by plane this morning to return to his country. He had been invited by Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, for a four-week visit and lecture tour in China. Guo Moruo received Sir Hinshelwood on the afternoon of the 18th and held a farewell banquet for him.

### 英国皇家学会会长  欣歇耳伍德离京回国

1959-09-20
第6版()
专栏：

    英国皇家学会会长
    欣歇耳伍德离京回国
    据新华社19日讯　英国皇家学会会长欣歇耳伍德爵士今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。他应中国科学院院长郭沫若的邀请，在中国作了为期四周的访问和讲学。郭沫若在18日下午接见了欣歇耳伍德爵士，并为他举行了欢送宴会。
