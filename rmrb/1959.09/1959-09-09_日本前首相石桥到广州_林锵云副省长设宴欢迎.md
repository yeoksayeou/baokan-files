Title: Former Japanese Prime Minister Ishibashi Arrives in Guangzhou; Vice Governor Lin Qiangyun Hosts Banquet to Welcome Him
Author:
Date: 1959-09-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 8th: Former Japanese Prime Minister Tanzan Ishibashi, his wife, and his entourage of twelve people, arrived in Guangzhou by train from Hong Kong at 2:17 p.m. today, at the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai for a friendly visit to our country. Sun Pinghua, a member of the Council of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, went to Shenzhen to greet them and accompanied the distinguished guests to Guangzhou.

Those who welcomed the distinguished guests at the station included Lin Qiangyun, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province; Jiao Linyi, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou; Du Guoxiang, Deputy to the National People's Congress and member of the Council of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Luo Peiyuan, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and member of the Council of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, as well as many representatives of the people and members of the CPPCC from Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, and members of the Council of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in Guangzhou.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 8th: Lin Qiangyun, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome former Japanese Prime Minister Tanzan Ishibashi, his wife, and his entourage.

Those present included Jiao Linyi, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou; Du Guoxiang, Deputy to the National People's Congress and member of the Council of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Luo Peiyuan, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the CPPCC; Feng Naichao, member of the Council of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; and many representatives of the people and members of the CPPCC from the province and city.

### 日本前首相石桥到广州  林锵云副省长设宴欢迎

1959-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    日本前首相石桥到广州
    林锵云副省长设宴欢迎
    新华社广州8日电　应周恩来总理邀请前来我国进行友好访问的日本前首相石桥湛山和他的夫人以及他的随行人员一行十二人，今天下午二时十七分由香港乘火车到达广州。中国人民外交学会理事孙平化前往深圳迎接并陪同贵宾前来广州。
    到车站欢迎贵宾的有广东省副省长林锵云，广州市副市长焦林义，全国人民代表大会代表、中国人民对外文化协会理事杜国庠，广州市政协副主席、中国人民外交学会理事罗培元以及广东省、广州市的人民代表、政协委员、中国人民对外文化协会在广州理事等多人。
    新华社广州8日电　广东省副省长林锵云今晚设宴欢迎日本前首相石桥湛山和他的夫人以及他的随行人员。
    出席作陪的有广州市副市长焦林义，全国人民代表大会代表、中国人民外交学会理事杜国庠，政协广州市委员会副主席罗培元，中国人民外交学会理事冯乃超，以及省、市的人民代表和政协委员等多人。
