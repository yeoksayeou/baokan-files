Title: Indonesian Joesoefianto Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, September 27th—Mr. and Mrs. Hadji Moealiff Joesoefianto from Indonesia, invited by the China-Indonesia Friendship Association to visit our country and attend the National Day tenth anniversary celebrations, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 印度尼西亚约苏蒂安到京

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚约苏蒂安到京
    新华社27日讯　应中国印度尼西亚友好协会邀请前来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的哈夷·穆阿力夫·约苏蒂安夫妇，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
