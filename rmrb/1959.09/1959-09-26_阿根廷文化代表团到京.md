Title: Argentine Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 25th -- The Argentine Cultural Friendship Delegation, led by Miguel Carlos Lombardi, and two members, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit our country and participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the country.

### 阿根廷文化代表团到京

1959-09-26
第2版()
专栏：

    阿根廷文化代表团到京
    新华社25日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的阿根廷文化友好代表团团长米盖·卡洛斯·龙巴尔第和团员二人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
