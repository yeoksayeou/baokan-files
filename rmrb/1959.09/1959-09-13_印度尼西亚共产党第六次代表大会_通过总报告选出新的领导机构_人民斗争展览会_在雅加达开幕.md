Title: Sixth Congress of the Indonesian Communist Party Passes General Report and Elects New Leadership; "People's Struggle Exhibition" Opens in Jakarta
Author:
Date: 1959-09-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 11th: A press communique issued today by the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party stated that the Sixth Congress of the Indonesian Communist Party unanimously adopted, on its third day (September 9th), the general report titled "Striving for Democracy, Striving for a Cooperation Cabinet" delivered by the General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, Aidit, to the Congress, and the report on the revision of the Party Program and Constitution delivered by the Second Deputy General Secretary, Njoto, to the Congress.

The communique stated that the Congress, on its fourth day (September 10th), elected the Party's new Central Committee and Supervisory Committee; the Congress unanimously elected the candidates nominated by the election committee. The new Central Committee consists of thirty-five members and eleven alternate members; the Supervisory Committee consists of eleven members.

The communique announced that Aidit, Lukman, Njoto, Sudisman, Sakirman, and Jono, among others, were elected as members of the new Central Committee.

For the past few days, the Indonesian "People's Daily" has continuously reported news about the people of Indonesia presenting gifts to the Congress and holding various activities. The newspaper has also published, over the past few days, congratulatory letters to the Congress from some ministers of the Indonesian government, including Foreign Minister Subandrio and Deputy Minister of Defense Hidayat, as well as congratulatory telegrams and letters sent to the Congress by people's organizations and individuals from all over Indonesia.

The "People's Daily" has published congratulatory telegrams and letters to the Congress from the Communist and Workers' Parties of the Soviet Union, China, Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, Albania, Mongolia, Hungary, the Netherlands, Australia, Italy, France, Chile, Japan, Malaya, Iran, and India, as well as the African Independence Party.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 12th: According to a report in today's Indonesian "People's Daily," the "People's Struggle Exhibition" organized by the Indonesian Communist Party opened here on the 11th. The General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, Aidit, on behalf of the newly elected Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party by the Sixth Congress, presided over the opening ceremony. The delegates attending the Congress participated in the opening ceremony.

The exhibits at the exhibition reflect the struggles of the Indonesian Communist Party and the Indonesian people from the period of Dutch colonial rule and Japanese militarist rule to the period of the Indonesian Republic.

Speaking at the opening ceremony, Aidit said that the history of the Indonesian Communist Party is closely linked to the history of the Indonesian people. He said that the history of the Indonesian Communist Party is a history full of dedication in the heroic and patriotic struggles for the people, the motherland, and humanity. It is an integral part of the history of the working people of the world.

Aidit said, "In the movement within the Party to oppose subjectivism, 'our responsibility is to study well the history of our Party, to learn from its failures and successes. Only with a full understanding of the history of the Party can we make fewer mistakes.'"

The exhibition is scheduled to close on September 17th. According to the restrictions announced by the military authorities, the exhibition is only allowed to be open to a limited number of people.

### 印度尼西亚共产党第六次代表大会  通过总报告选出新的领导机构  “人民斗争展览会”在雅加达开幕

1959-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚共产党第六次代表大会
    通过总报告选出新的领导机构
    “人民斗争展览会”在雅加达开幕
    新华社雅加达11日电　印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会书记处今天发表的新闻公报说，印度尼西亚共产党第六次代表大会在第三天（9日）的会议上全体一致通过了印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地向大会作的题为“争取民主，争取合作内阁”的总报告，和第二副总书记约多向大会作的关于修改党纲党章的报告。
    公报说，代表大会在第四天（10日）的会议上选举了党的新的中央委员会和监察委员会；代表大会一致选举了由选举委员会提出的候选人。新的中央委员会包括三十五名委员和十一名候补委员；监察委员会由十一人组成。
    公报宣布，艾地、鲁克曼、约多、苏蒂斯曼、萨基尔曼和约诺等都当选为新的中央委员会委员。
    印度尼西亚“人民日报”几天来连续报道了关于印度尼西亚各地人民向代表大会献礼和举行各种活动的消息。这家报纸过去几天来还刊登了印度尼西亚政府一些部长包括外交部长苏班德里约和国防部副部长希达雅特给代表大会的贺信，以及印度尼西亚各地人民团体和个人寄给代表大会的贺电和贺信。
    “人民日报”曾经发表了苏联、中国、保加利亚、捷克斯洛伐克、阿尔巴尼亚、蒙古、匈牙利、荷兰、澳大利亚、意大利、法国、智利、日本、马来亚、伊朗和印度的共产党和工人党以及非洲独立党致代表大会的贺电和贺信。
    新华社雅加达12日电　据印度尼西亚“人民日报”今天报道，印度尼西亚共产党举办的“人民斗争展览会”11日在这里开幕。印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地代表第六次代表大会新选出的印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会主持了开幕仪式。参加开幕仪式的有出席代表大会的代表们。
    展览会上的展品反映了印度尼西亚共产党和印度尼西亚人民从荷兰殖民统治时期和日本军国主义统治时期到印度尼西亚共和国时期所进行的斗争。
    艾地在开幕仪式上讲话时说，印度尼西亚共产党的历史是同印度尼西亚人民的历史密切联系在一起的。他说，印度尼西亚共产党的历史是一部在为人民、祖国和人类而进行的英勇和爱国斗争中充满着献身精神的历史。它是世界劳动人民的历史的不可分割的一部分。
    艾地说，在党内展开反对主观主义的运动中，“我们的责任是很好地学习我们党的历史，学习它的失败和成功的经验。只有对党的历史有充分的认识，我们才能少犯错误。”
    这个展览会预定在9月17日闭幕。根据军事当局公布的限制条例，这个展览会只准许向一部分人士开放。
