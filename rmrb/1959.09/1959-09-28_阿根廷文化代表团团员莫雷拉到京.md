Title: Member of the Argentine Cultural Delegation, Moreira, Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: Moreira, a member of the Argentine Cultural Delegation invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China and participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 26th.

### 阿根廷文化代表团团员莫雷拉到京

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    阿根廷文化代表团团员莫雷拉到京
    新华社27日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的阿根廷文化代表团团员莫雷拉，于26日下午乘飞机到达北京。
