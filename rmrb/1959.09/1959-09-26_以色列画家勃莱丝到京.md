Title: Israeli Painter Blais Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: Israeli printmaker Charlotte Blais arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries to visit China and participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the country.

Original: 


### 以色列画家勃莱丝到京

1959-09-26
第2版()
专栏：

    以色列画家勃莱丝到京
    新华社25日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的以色列版画家夏洛蒂·勃莱丝女士，在今天乘飞机到达北京。
