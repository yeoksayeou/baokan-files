Title: Another Flower of Sino-Czechoslovak Friendship: New Beijing Stamp Factory Completed; Each Machine Can Produce 500,000 Stamps Per Hour
Author:
Date: 1959-09-27
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th. The newly built Beijing Stamp Factory held its completion and opening ceremony today and was officially put into use.

Attending the opening ceremony were Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications, Vice Ministers Shen Guang, Zhao Zhigang, and Gu Chunfan; Cheng Tan, Deputy Minister of the Communications Work Department of the CPC Central Committee; Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Bušiňák, Commercial Counselor Ševčík, and experts from Czechoslovakia who came to help guide production at the Beijing Stamp Factory were also invited to attend the conference. Representatives from the Industrial Department of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC, the Xuanwu District Committee, and factory construction departments also attended the conference.

At 2:30 p.m., the completion and opening ceremony was held in the newly built conference hall inside the factory. Minister of Posts and Telecommunications Zhu Xuefan, on behalf of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, extended warm congratulations to all the staff of the stamp factory. He said that the newly built stamp factory has the latest machinery and equipment, and each machine can produce 500,000 stamps per hour. It has created favorable conditions for the further development of China's stamp issuance work.

Minister Zhu Xuefan also expressed his gratitude on behalf of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications to the Czechoslovakian Party and government, as well as to the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Czechoslovak Republic and the experts who helped with our country's construction, and presented banners and commemorative albums to the Czech Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications.

The Czechoslovak Ambassador and Commercial Counselor pointed out in their speeches that the completion of the stamp factory marks the friendly unity and unbreakable friendship between Czechoslovakia and China. Zhao Xingmin, Director of the Beijing Stamp Factory, Karel Motl, the representative of the Czech experts, and representatives of the employees, all spoke at the conference.

Amidst firecrackers and drums, Minister Zhu Xuefan cut the ribbon, and the stamp factory officially announced the start of production.

### 又一朵中捷友谊之花  新建北京邮票厂落成  每部机器每小时能生产五十万枚邮票

1959-09-27
第2版()
专栏：

    又一朵中捷友谊之花
    新建北京邮票厂落成
    每部机器每小时能生产五十万枚邮票
    新华社25日讯　新建的北京邮票厂今天举行落成开工典礼，并且正式交付使用。
    参加开工典礼的有邮电部部长朱学范，副部长申光、赵志刚、谷春帆，中共中央交通工作部副部长程坦；捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使布希尼亚克、商务参赞舍伏契克，以及捷克斯洛伐克派来帮助北京邮票厂指导生产的专家们也应邀参加了大会。参加大会的还有中共北京市委工业部、宣武区区委，以及工厂施工等部门的代表。
    下午二时半，落成开工典礼在工厂内新建的会场里举行。邮电部朱学范部长代表邮电部向邮票厂的全体职工表示热烈的祝贺。他说，新建的邮票厂拥有最新式的机器设备，每部机器每小时能生产五十万枚邮票。它为我国邮票发行工作的进一步发展，创造了有利条件。
    朱学范部长还代表邮电部向捷克斯洛伐克党和政府，以及捷克斯洛伐克共和国邮电部和帮助我国建设的专家们表示感谢，并且向捷邮电部赠送了锦旗和纪念册。
    捷克斯洛伐克大使和商务参赞在讲话中一致指出，邮票厂建成标志着捷中两国友好团结和牢不可破的友谊。北京邮票厂厂长赵醒民，捷专家代表卡莱尔莫特耳，以及职工代表等，都在大会上讲了话。
    在鞭炮和锣鼓声中，朱学范部长剪了采，邮票厂宣告正式开工生产。
