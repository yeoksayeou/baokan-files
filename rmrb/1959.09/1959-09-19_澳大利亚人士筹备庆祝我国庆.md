Title: Australian Personages Prepare to Celebrate Our National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-09-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sydney, September 17th: Australian personages are preparing to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The Australia-China Association plans to hold film evenings, banquets, and dances to celebrate. The Australian Communist Party will organize celebration rallies in major cities.

### 澳大利亚人士筹备庆祝我国庆

1959-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    澳大利亚人士
    筹备庆祝我国庆
    新华社17日讯　悉尼消息：澳大利亚人士正在筹备庆祝中华人民共和国建国十周年。澳中协会打算举行电影晚会、宴会和舞会表示庆祝。澳大利亚共产党将在各大城市组织庆祝集会。
