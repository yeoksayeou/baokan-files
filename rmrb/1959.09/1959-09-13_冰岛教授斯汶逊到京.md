Title: Icelandic Professor Sveinsson Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - Icelandic University Professor Sveinsson and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China and attend the National Day celebrations.

### 冰岛教授斯汶逊到京

1959-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    冰岛教授斯汶逊到京
    新华社11日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问并参加我国国庆观礼的冰岛大学教授斯汶逊夫妇今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
