Title: Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Second Secretary Tsende said that the Chinese People are Advancing from Victory to Victory
Author: 
Date: 1959-09-06
Page: 2

    Ulan Bator Dispatch, September 5th. On the afternoon of September 5th, a mass rally was held in Ulan Bator to celebrate the commissioning of the pulp and paper mill and prefabricated wooden structure housing plant built with Chinese assistance in Mongolia. Tsende , Second Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, said at meeting that the Chinese people are leaping forward from victory to victory in order to build a strong socialist country. Not long ago, the Eighth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party made an important resolution to further accelerate development and further consolidate the people's communes on the basis of discussing and summarizing the country's leap forward work. The Chinese people warmly accepted the Party's call for increasing production and practicing economy and working actively. Therefore, China will be able to catch up with Britain not in fifteen years, but in ten years. The Mongolian people extend their heartfelt congratulations to the Chinese people and the Chinese Communist Party who are consolidating the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union and world peace, are completing grand plans and have achieved remarkable achievements.

### 蒙古人民革命党第二书记曾德说  中国人民正从胜利走向胜利

1959-09-06
第2版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民革命党第二书记曾德说
    中国人民正从胜利走向胜利
    本报苏赫巴托尔5日电　5日下午苏赫巴托尔市人民举行群众大会，庆祝中国帮助蒙古建造的制浆造纸厂和木结构房屋预制厂投入生产。蒙古人民革命党中央第二书记曾德在会上说，中国人民正在为建成强大的社会主义国家从胜利走向胜利地跃进着。不久前中国共产党八中全会在讨论和总结国家跃进工作的基础上作出了进一步加快发展和更加巩固人民公社的重要决议。中国人民热烈接受党的增产节约的号召和积极劳动，因此，中国赶上英国的时间不是十五年，而是十年就能完成了。蒙古人民向巩固以苏联为首的社会主义阵营和世界和平，正在完成宏伟的计划并已取得显著成就的中国人民和中国共产党致以衷心的祝贺。
