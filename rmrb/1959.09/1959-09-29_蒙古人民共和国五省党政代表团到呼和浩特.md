Title: Mongolian People's Republic Five Province Party and Government Delegation Arrives in Hohhot
Author:
Date: 1959-09-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, September 28th. At the invitation of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Committee, the twelve-member Party and Government Delegation from the five provinces of Sukhe-Bator, East Gobi, South Gobi, Bayankhongor, and Choibalsan of the Mongolian People's Republic arrived in Hohhot by train today to participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The delegation was led by Da. Batehurel, First Secretary of the Sukhe-Bator Provincial Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party.

### 蒙古人民共和国五省党政代表团到呼和浩特

1959-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民共和国五省党政代表团到呼和浩特
    新华社呼和浩特28日电　应中共内蒙古自治区委员会、内蒙古自治区人民委员会邀请，来内蒙古自治区参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的蒙古人民共和国苏赫巴托尔、东戈壁、南戈壁、巴彦洪果尔、乔巴山五省党政代表团一行十二人，在团长、蒙古人民革命党苏赫巴托尔省委第一书记达·巴特胡热勒率领下，今天乘火车到达呼和浩特。
