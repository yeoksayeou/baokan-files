Title: U.S. Military Plane Intrudes into Airspace Over Xisha Islands for Provocation; I Issue 65th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1959-09-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th September: At 11:40 AM on September 9th, a U.S. Navy patrol plane intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by a U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 65th serious warning.

### 美机侵入我西沙群岛上空挑衅  我提出第65次严重警告

1959-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    美机侵入我西沙群岛上空挑衅
    我提出第65次严重警告
    新华社9日讯　9月9日十一时四十分，美国海军巡逻机一架侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第六十五次严重警告。
