Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Afghan Dignitaries
Author:
Date: 1959-09-06
Page: 1
    
Xinhua News Agency, 5th September: Premier Zhou Enlai met this afternoon with the Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Sardar Mohammed Naim Khan, and other Afghan dignitaries who accompanied him on his visit. Present at the meeting were Vice Premier Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and China's Ambassador to Afghanistan Hao Ting. The Afghan Ambassador to China, Samad, was also present at the meeting. After the meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai hosted a luncheon to entertain the Afghan dignitaries.

### 周总理会见阿富汗贵宾

1959-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理会见阿富汗贵宾
    新华社5日讯　周恩来总理今天下午会见了阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆亲王和随同他来访的其他阿富汗贵宾们。会见时在座的，有陈毅副总理，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长章汉夫，我国驻阿富汗大使郝汀等。会见时，阿富汗驻华大使萨马德也在座。会见以后，周恩来总理设便宴招待阿富汗贵宾们。
