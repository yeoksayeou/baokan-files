Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into Territorial Waters of Pingtan Area, Fujian Province; I Issue 71st Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 3

    U.S. Warship Intrudes into Territorial Waters of Pingtan Area, Fujian Province
    I Issue 71st Serious Warning
    Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: From 10:00 to 12:40 on September 24th, a U.S. warship intruded into the sea area of ​​Pingtan area, Fujian Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, a spokesman for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 71st serious warning.

### 美国军舰侵入我福建省平潭地区领海  我提出第71次严重警告

1959-09-25
第3版()
专栏：

    美国军舰侵入我福建省平潭地区领海
    我提出第71次严重警告
    新华社24日讯　9月24日十时至十二时四十分，美国军舰一艘侵入我福建省平潭地区海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第七十一次严重警告。
