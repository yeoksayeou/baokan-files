Miyun Reservoir Locks the Dragon

　　On September 1st, the 200,000-strong army building the Miyun Reservoir held a grand gathering in front of the reservoir dam to celebrate the reservoir's successful flood control and the first anniversary of construction. Xinhua News Agency reporter Gu Dehua.

　　Commune members participating in the construction of the reservoir created a "pulley hoist," which is both labor-saving and improves work efficiency. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yu Huiru.
