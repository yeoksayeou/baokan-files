Title: Khrushchev Receives Menon
Author:
Date: 1959-09-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 12th: According to TASS, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, received the Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Menon, in Moscow on the 12th at Menon's request, and spoke with him.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见梅农

1959-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见梅农
    新华社12日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫应印度驻苏联大使梅农的请求，12日在莫斯科接见了他，并和他谈了话。
