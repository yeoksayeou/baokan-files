Title: Ceylon-China Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the four-member delegation of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association, led by Chairman Martin Wickramasinghe, arrived in Beijing by plane on the morning of the 26th to visit our country and attend the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 锡中友协代表团到北京

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    锡中友协代表团到北京
    新华社27日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的锡中友协代表团一行四人，在团长马丁·魏克拉马沁率领下，于26日上午乘飞机到达北京。
