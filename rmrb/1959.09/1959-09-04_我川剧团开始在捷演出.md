Title: Our Sichuan Opera Troupe Begins Performances in Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1959-09-04
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 2nd: The Chinese Sichuan Opera Troupe gave its first performance in Czechoslovakia tonight in the Okres (county) of Orlová in the Ostrava Region, and was warmly welcomed by the local audience.

### 我川剧团开始在捷演出

1959-09-04
第5版()
专栏：

    我川剧团开始在捷演出
    据新华社布拉格2日电　中国川剧团今晚在俄斯特拉发州的俄巴发县举行在捷克斯洛伐克的首次访问演出，受到当地观众的热烈欢迎。
