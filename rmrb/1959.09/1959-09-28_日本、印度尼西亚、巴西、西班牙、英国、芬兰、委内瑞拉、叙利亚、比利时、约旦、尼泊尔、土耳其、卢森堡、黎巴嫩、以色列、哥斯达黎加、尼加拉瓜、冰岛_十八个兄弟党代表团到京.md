Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: The delegation of the Japanese Communist Party, led by Nozaka Sanzo, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, and including members of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, including Ritsumi Kutsuda, Korehito Kurahara, Kazuyoshi Sunaama, and Masao Iwama, as well as the Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the "Akahata" newspaper, Chikara Hoshino, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to visit China and attend the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.
