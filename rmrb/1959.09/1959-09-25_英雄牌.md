Title: Hero Brand
Author: Cao Mingde
Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 12

    Hero Brand
    Cao Mingde
      "Thought is 'Liberation Brand', drive is 'Forever Brand', determination is 'Invincible Brand'." – This is the heroic declaration shouted by the Shanghai workers.
    This slogan fully embodies the revolutionary firmness of the working class. A person's thought, drive, and determination cannot be without this or that brand: Thought, either it is "Liberation Brand" or "Conservative Brand," sticking to old ways; drive, either it is "Forever Brand" or "Lightning Brand," fleeting; determination, either it is "Invincible Brand" or "Bubble Brand," easily shattered. Without the iron will of the working class, thought cannot be "Liberation Brand," drive cannot be "Forever Brand," and determination cannot be "Invincible Brand." So, I think, if the working class has a general brand, it should be called: "Hero Brand."
    Victory always belongs to the steadfast. With "Liberation Brand" thought, "Forever Brand" drive, and "Invincible Brand" determination, we will advance courageously, overcome all obstacles on the road forward, and win victory.

### 英雄牌
曹明德
1959-09-25
第12版()
专栏：大家谈

    英雄牌
    曹明德
      “思想是‘解放牌’，干劲是‘永久牌’，决心是‘无敌牌’。”——这是英雄的上海工人喊出来的豪言壮语。
    这个口号充分的体现了工人阶级的革命坚定性。一个人的思想、干劲和决心，不能没有这样或那样的牌子：思想，要么就是“解放牌”，要么就是“保守牌”，因循守旧；干劲，要么就是“永久牌”，要么就是“闪电牌”，瞬息即失；决心，要么就是“无敌牌”，要么就是“泡沫牌”，一触即破。没有工人阶级的铁硬骨头，思想便不可能是“解放牌”的，干劲便不可能是“永久牌”的，决心便不可能是“无敌牌”的。于是，我想，如果工人阶级有一个总的牌子，应该叫做：“英雄牌”。
    胜利永远是属于坚定的人的。有了“解放牌”的思想，“永久牌”的干劲，“无敌牌”的决心，我们就会一鼓作气，所向披靡，胜利的克服前进道路上的一切困难。
