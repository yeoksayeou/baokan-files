Title: Commander Danilov Hosts Journalists, Introduces the Performance Plan of the Soviet Ballet Troupe
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 27th. Danilov, head of the Soviet State Grand Theatre Ballet Troupe and Deputy Minister of Culture of the Soviet Union, held a press conference at 6 p.m. today regarding the ballet troupe's performance tour in China. Outstanding Soviet ballet performing artists Ulanova, Lepeshinskaya, and Sergeyev attended.

	At the meeting, Commander Danilov introduced the history of the Soviet State Grand Theatre and the plan for this performance tour in China. He said:
	"We hope that through this performance tour of the ballet troupe in China, we can contribute to strengthening the unbreakable friendship between the people of the Soviet Union and China, as well as to our common cause—the struggle to defend world peace." Commander Danilov also stated at the meeting that during the performance tour in China, the ballet troupe will extensively contact Chinese artists and cultural workers to exchange experiences and learn from each other.
	The Soviet State Grand Theatre Ballet Troupe has decided to hold its first performance in China in Beijing on the 29th.

### 达尼洛夫团长招待记者  介绍苏芭蕾舞团演出计划

1959-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

    达尼洛夫团长招待记者
    介绍苏芭蕾舞团演出计划
    据新华社27日讯　苏联国家大剧院芭蕾舞团团长、苏联文化部副部长达尼洛夫，今天下午六时为芭蕾舞团访华演出举行记者招待会。苏联杰出的芭蕾舞表演艺术家乌兰诺娃和列皮辛斯卡娅、谢尔盖耶夫等都出席参加。
    达尼洛夫团长在会上向大家介绍了苏联国家大剧院的历史和这次访华演出的计划。他说：
  “我们希望通过这次芭蕾舞团的访华演出，能对加强苏中两国人民牢不可破的友谊，以及对我们的共同事业——为保卫世界和平的斗争作出贡献”。达尼洛夫团长在会上还表示芭蕾舞团在访华演出期间，将同中国艺术家和文化工作者广泛联系，交流经验，互相学习。
    苏联国家大剧院芭蕾舞团已决定29日在北京举行访华第一场演出。
