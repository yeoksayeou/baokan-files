Title: Heilongjiang Men's Basketball Team Defeats Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1959-09-22
Page: 8

Heilongjiang Men's Basketball Team Defeats Beijing
New developments emerged in today's basketball finals, with the so-called "weak teams" of Heilongjiang men's team and Guizhou women's team defeating the strong Beijing men's team and Hebei women's team, respectively. The Heilongjiang team won by two points with a score of seventy-two to seventy, and the Guizhou women's team won by six points with a score of sixty-five to fifty-nine.

### 黑龙江男篮胜北京

1959-09-22
第8版()
专栏：

    黑龙江男篮胜北京
    篮球决赛今天出现了新的变化，所谓“弱队”的黑龙江男队和贵州女子队分别打败了实力雄厚的北京男队和河北女队。黑龙江队以七十二比七十胜了二分，贵州女队以六十五比五十九胜了六分。
