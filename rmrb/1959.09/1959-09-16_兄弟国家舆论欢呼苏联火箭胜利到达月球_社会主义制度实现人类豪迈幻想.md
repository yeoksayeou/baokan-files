Title: Fraternal Countries' Public Opinion Cheers Soviet Rocket's Successful Arrival on the Moon; Socialist System Realizes Humanity's Grand Fantasy
Author:
Date: 1959-09-16
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: People and public opinion in socialist countries warmly celebrate the Soviet cosmic rocket reaching the moon.

    On the evening of the 14th, people from all walks of life in Pyongyang held a mass rally at the Moranbong Theater to congratulate the great achievement of Soviet science. Among those attending the rally were Choe Won-taek, Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, Li Gi-ru, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, and Dorbinikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in Korea.

    Li Seung-gi, Academician of the Korean Academy of Sciences and a renowned scientist, delivered a report at the meeting. He said: The successful launch of the Soviet second cosmic rocket has infinitely inspired the Korean people, the socialist camp, and all peace-loving people around the world, greatly struck a blow to the imperialist bloc headed by US imperialism, and made a great contribution to easing international tensions.

    The "Rodong Sinmun" of Korea published an editorial entitled "The Communist Flag is Flying on the Moon." The editorial said that the Soviet cosmic rocket reaching the moon is an epoch-making event in the development of human history.

    Vietnam's "Nhan Dan" published an editorial on the 15th, congratulating the Soviet Union on the successful launch of the second cosmic rocket to the moon. The editorial said: "This is the first creation in human history to bring an object from one celestial body to another according to one's own will," and it brings "immense glory to us and progressive mankind." The editorial pointed out: "When Chairman Khrushchev visits the United States to improve Soviet-American relations, stop the Cold War, and consolidate world peace, the Soviet Union's successful launch of a cosmic rocket to the moon has great political significance. This event shows the United States that the Soviet Union surpasses the United States in many of the most important scientific aspects, but the Soviet Union wants peace, not war. Those who attempt to launch an anti-Soviet war can only bring destruction upon themselves."

    The "Neues Deutschland" published an editorial on the 14th, stating that the Soviet cosmic rocket reaching the moon is an event of world historical significance. The conquest of the moon will serve the cause of mutual understanding among the people of all countries. Peace is even more necessary for conquering outer space.

    Professor Kollenz, Director of the Synthetic Fiber Research Institute of the Academy of Sciences of the German Democratic Republic and Chairman of the National Council of the National Front, said: "The Soviet cosmic rocket is a messenger of peace and a pioneer of the lofty spirit of mankind, because its purpose is to enable mankind to make progress. This great achievement of the Soviet people is of great importance to Khrushchev's visit to the United States, because the higher the prestige of the Soviet Union, the stronger the Soviet Union's peace force."

    Professor Paczkowski, Chairman of the Polish Interplanetary Aviation Association, told a reporter from the Polish Press Agency about the Soviet Union's successful launch of the second cosmic rocket: The fact that Soviet scientists can use relevant instruments to control the distance of the rocket's flight in outer space is of particularly great significance from a technical point of view. The professor said that, after this experiment, there is no doubt that Soviet scientists will be able to obtain many new and valuable materials about outer space and the moon. I think what is particularly interesting is the possibility of obtaining materials about the moon's magnetic field and determining the characteristics of this magnetic field. Because, until now, the scientific community has not determined whether the moon has a magnetic field. If it is determined that the moon has a magnetic field, then this is of particularly great significance for the further development of interplanetary flight. This magnetic field will facilitate the landing of spacecraft taking off from Earth on the moon in the future.

    Paczkowski continued, we congratulate the Soviet scientific community on this outstanding achievement with great excitement. This achievement has opened up new and broad prospects for mankind's conquest of outer space.

    The Polish "Trybuna Ludu" published an editorial on the 14th, stating: The Soviet Union's victorious launch of the lunar rocket and the rocket's arrival on the moon surpass all previous scientific achievements in this area. The editorial said that mankind's ideal of exploring the secrets of the universe is gradually being realized.

    The Hungarian "Népszabadság" published a special issue on the 14th, warmly celebrating the Soviet rocket reaching the moon. The newspaper pointed out in an editorial entitled "The Red Flag Waves on the Moon" that the day the Soviet cosmic rocket reached the moon has become a day of world historical significance. The editorial pointed out that this great victory of the Soviet Union once again powerfully proves the possibility of the socialist system enabling science and technology to achieve tremendous development. The socialist and communist systems are realizing mankind's most courageous fantasies.

### 兄弟国家舆论欢呼苏联火箭胜利到达月球  社会主义制度实现人类豪迈幻想

1959-09-16
第7版()
专栏：

    兄弟国家舆论欢呼苏联火箭胜利到达月球
    社会主义制度实现人类豪迈幻想
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：社会主义国家人民和舆论热烈欢呼苏联宇宙火箭抵达月球。
    14日晚上，平壤市各界人民在牡丹峰剧场举行群众集会，祝贺苏联科学的这一伟大成就。参加集会的有：朝鲜最高人民会议议长崔元泽、最高人民会议常任委员会副委员长李克鲁和苏联驻朝鲜大使馆参赞道尔宾可夫等。
    朝鲜科学院院士、著名的科学家李升基在会上作了报告。他说：苏联第二个宇宙火箭的发射成功无限鼓舞了朝鲜人民、社会主义阵营和全世界爱好和平的人民，大大地打击了以美帝国主义为首的帝国主义集团，对缓和国际紧张局势有很大的贡献。
    朝鲜“劳动新闻”发表了题为“共产主义的旗帜在月球上飘扬”的社论。社论说，苏联宇宙火箭到达月球是在人类历史发展中具有划时代意义的事件。
    越南“人民报”15日发表社论，祝贺苏联发射的第二个宇宙火箭到达月球。社论说：“这是人类第一次按照自己的意图把一个物体从这个天体带到另一个天体去的创举”，它给“我们和进步人类带来了莫大的光荣”。社论指出：“当赫鲁晓夫主席为了改善苏美关系、停止冷战、巩固世界和平而到美国访问的时候，苏联成功地向月球发射宇宙火箭这件事具有重大的政治意义。这件事向美国表明，苏联在许多最重要的科学方面超过美国，但是，苏联要求和平，不要战争。妄图发动反苏战争的人只能自取灭亡。”
    “新德意志报”14日发表社论说，苏联宇宙火箭到达月球，这是具有世界历史意义的事件。对月球的征服将为各国人民的互相谅解的事业服务。为了征服宇宙空间，更加需要和平。
    德意志民主共和国科学院合成纤维研究所主任、全国阵线全国委员会主席柯伦斯教授说：“苏联宇宙火箭是和平的使者，是人类崇高精神的先锋，因为它的目的是使人类取得进步。苏联人民的这一伟大的成就对于赫鲁晓夫美国之行具有巨大的重要意义，因为苏联的威信愈高，苏联的和平力量就愈强大。”
    波兰星际航空协会主席帕奇科夫斯基教授对波兰通讯社记者谈苏联成功地发射第二个宇宙火箭说：苏联科学家利用有关仪器能够控制火箭在宇宙空间中的飞行距离，这从技术观点来看，具有特别重大的意义。这位教授说，经过这一试验，毫无疑问，苏联科学家就能获得许多关于宇宙空间和月球的新的宝贵材料。我认为，特别使人感到兴趣的是有可能获得关于月球磁场的材料和确定这一磁场的特性。因为，到现在为止，科学界还没有断定月球是否有磁场。如果确定月球有磁场，那末，这对星际飞行的进一步发展具有特别大的意义。这种磁场会便于将来从地球起飞的宇宙飞机在月球降落。
    帕奇科夫斯基接着说，我们十分兴奋地祝贺苏联科学界这一杰出的成就。这一功绩为人类征服宇宙空间方面打开了新的广阔的前景。
    波兰“人民论坛报”14日发表社论说：苏联胜利地发射了月球火箭和火箭到达月球的成就超过了迄今为止在这方面的一切科学成就。社论说，人类探索宇宙秘密的理想在逐渐实现。
    匈牙利“人民自由报”14日出版专号，热烈庆祝苏联火箭到达月球。报纸在题为“红旗在月球上招展”的社论指出，苏联宇宙火箭到达月球的日子已成为一个具有世界历史意义的日子。社论指出，苏联这一伟大胜利又一次有力地证明了社会主义制度使科学和技术获得巨大发展的可能。社会主义和共产主义制度实现着人类最勇敢的幻想。
