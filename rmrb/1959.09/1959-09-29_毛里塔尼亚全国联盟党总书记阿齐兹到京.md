Title: Mauritanian National Union Party Secretary-General Aziz Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-29
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 28th: Ba Abdou Aziz, Secretary-General of the Mauritanian National Union Party and member of the Legislative Assembly, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese Committee for Asian-African Solidarity to visit China and attend the celebrations for the tenth anniversary of our country's founding.
    Hu Yuzhi, Wang Mingyuan and other responsible persons from the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and the Chinese Committee for Asian-African Solidarity were at the airport to greet him.

### 毛里塔尼亚全国联盟党总书记阿齐兹到京

1959-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

    毛里塔尼亚全国联盟党总书记阿齐兹到京
    新华社28日讯　应中国亚非团结委员会邀请前来我国访问并参加我国建国十周年庆祝典礼的毛里塔尼亚全国联盟党总书记、立法议员巴·阿卜杜·阿齐兹今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的有中国人民外交学会和中国亚非团结委员会负责人胡愈之、王明远等。
