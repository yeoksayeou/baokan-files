A Grand Reunion of Athletes from All Ethnic Groups

The First National Games brought together a total of 10,658 outstanding male and female athletes from across the country. This is a grand reunion of athletes from all ethnic groups in China. The athletes include over twenty ethnic groups, such as Han, Mongolian, Hui, Tibetan, Uyghur, Manchu, Miao, Korean, Zhuang, Yi, Gaoshan, Tu, Dai, Daur, Kazakh, Russian, Uzbek, Xibe, Kirgiz, Lisu, Buyi, and Dong.

The athletes are also widely representative. There are workers, farmers, officers, soldiers, students, cadres, nurses; there are advanced producers and advanced workers, combat heroes, "three good" students, and activists in socialist construction; there are elderly people in their sixties, and there are also children as young as nine years old.

### 各民族运动健儿大团聚

1959-09-13
第6版()
专栏：

    各民族运动健儿大团聚
    第一届全国运动会共集中了一万零六百五十八名全国优秀男、女运动员。这是我国各民族运动健儿的一次大团聚。运动员中包括汉、蒙、回、藏、维吾尔、满、苗、朝鲜、僮、彝、高山、土、傣、达斡尔、哈萨克、俄罗斯、乌兹别克、锡伯、柯尔克孜、傈僳、布依、侗等二十多个民族。
    运动员的代表性也很广泛。有工人、农民、军官、战士、学生、干部、护士；有先进生产者和先进工作者、战斗英雄、三好学生、社会主义建设积极分子；有六十多岁高龄的老人，也有九岁的少年儿童。
