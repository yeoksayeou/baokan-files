Title: Telegram of Greetings from the Executive Committee of the Irish Workers' League
Author:
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 9

To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China,
Dear Comrades!
    On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, we extend our warmest fraternal congratulations to the Chinese people.
    We have been constantly amazed by the enormous and rapid material and spiritual changes that have taken place in People's China under the brilliant leadership of the Communist Party of China headed by Comrade Mao Zedong.
    The brilliant achievements made through the creative application of Marxism in the specific conditions of China have greatly enriched the doctrine of Marxism-Leninism and inspired the people of all countries around the world who are fighting for peace, national independence and socialism.
    Long live the People's Republic of China!
    Long live peace and friendship between the peoples of all countries!
    Long live the glorious Communist Party of China!
                        Executive Committee of the Irish Workers' League
                              Chairman　Seán Nolan
                            Secretary　Michael O'Riordan (signature)
                                          September 19, 1959

### 爱尔兰工人联盟执行委员会的贺电

1959-09-30
第9版()
专栏：

    爱尔兰工人联盟执行委员会的贺电中国共产党中央委员会亲爱的同志们！
    值此中华人民共和国建国十周年之际，我们谨向中国人民致最热烈的兄弟的祝贺。
    在以毛泽东同志为首的中国共产党的光辉领导下，人民中国国内发生的物质和精神变化之巨大和迅速，一直使我们惊叹不置。
    通过在中国的具体条件下创造性地运用马克思主义而取得的光辉成就大大丰富了马克思列宁主义的学说，并鼓舞着为争取和平、民族独立和社会主义而斗争的全世界各国人民。
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    和平和各国人民之间的友谊万岁！
    光荣的中国共产党万岁！
                        爱尔兰工人联盟执行委员会
                              主席　塞安·诺兰
                            书记　迈克尔·奥赖尔登（签名）
                                          1959年9月19日
