Title: Soviet Embassy in China Holds Friendship Soiree to Celebrate the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of Our Country
Author:
Date: 1959-09-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: The Soviet Embassy in China held a friendship soiree tonight to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of China. Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi was invited to attend the soiree.

    Antononov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, delivered a speech at the meeting, extending warm congratulations on the 10th anniversary of the founding of China on behalf of the Soviet Embassy in China, the Commercial Representative Office, all staff of other Soviet institutions in China, and Soviet experts working in China. He said that the outstanding achievements made by the Chinese people in economic and cultural construction under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the government over the past ten years are sufficient proof of the great vitality of China's new socialist system. The high speed of development of Chinese industry, agriculture, transportation, science, and culture has shocked the world and aroused the sincere admiration and joy of friends in all countries. Antononov said that the foreign policy of the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and other countries in the socialist camp, based on striving for peace and friendship among all peoples, is breaking the imperialist conspiracies aimed at launching new wars and is increasingly consolidating our international position.

    Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi then delivered a speech at the meeting. He said that the great achievements China has made are inseparable from the help of the Soviet government, the Soviet Communist Party, and Soviet experts. He expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Soviet Union for its great assistance on behalf of the Chinese government and the Chinese Communist Party. Vice Premier Chen Yi said that the Soviet Union's great Seven-Year Plan is progressing well, and China's great Great Leap Forward is also very good. It can be predicted that in another ten years, the world will definitely undergo a new and greater change.

    Those invited to attend today's friendship soiree included: Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Zeng Yongquan, Vice Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Li Dequan and Shao Lizi, Vice President of the All-China Women's Federation Shi Liang, and heads of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Sports Commission, the Chinese Communist Youth League, and other units.

    The soiree was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere. Guests and hosts frequently toasted to the eternal and unbreakable friendship between China and the Soviet Union, and to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong and Chairman Khrushchev. Wonderful cultural performances were staged at the meeting.

### 庆祝我国建国十周年  苏联驻华大使馆举行友谊晚会

1959-09-26
第2版()
专栏：

    庆祝我国建国十周年
    苏联驻华大使馆举行友谊晚会
    据新华社25日讯　苏联驻中国大使馆今晚举行友谊晚会，庆祝中国建国十周年。陈毅副总理兼外交部长应邀出席了晚会。
    苏联驻我国大使馆临时代办安东诺夫首先在会上致词，他代表苏联驻我国大使馆、商务代表处、苏联驻中国的其他机关全体工作人员和在华工作的苏联专家，热烈祝贺我国建国十周年。他说，中国人民在中国共产党和政府领导下，十年里在经济和文化建设上所达到的优越成绩，足以证明中国新的社会主义制度的巨大生命力。中国工业、农业、运输业、科学文化发展的高速度震动世界，并且引起所有各国朋友们的衷心佩服和喜悦。安东诺夫说，苏联、中华人民共和国和社会主义阵营其他各国的外交政策，以争取和平和各国人民之间的友谊为基础，正在打破旨在发动新战争的帝国主义的阴谋，而且越来越巩固我们的国际地位。
    陈毅副总理兼外交部长接着在会上致词。他说，中国伟大成就的取得，是和苏联政府、苏联共产党和苏联专家的帮助分不开的。他代表中国政府、中国共产党对苏联的巨大援助表示衷心的感谢。陈毅副总理说，苏联伟大的七年计划进行得很好，中国伟大的大跃进也很好。可以预言：再过一个十年，世界肯定将会发生一个新的更加伟大的变化。
    应邀出席今天友谊晚会的有：对外贸易部部长叶季壮，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长章汉夫、曾涌泉，中苏友好协会总会副会长李德全、邵力子，全国妇联副主席史良，以及对外文化联络委员会、体育运动委员会、中国共产主义青年团等单位的负责人。
    晚会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。宾主频频为中苏两国永恒的、牢不可破的友谊，为毛泽东主席和赫鲁晓夫主席的健康干杯。会上表演了精彩的文娱节目。
