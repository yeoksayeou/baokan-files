Title: Typhoon Weakens After Landfall, Heavy to Torrential Rain Expected in Shandong, Jiangsu, Anhui, and Zhejiang
Author:
Date: 1959-09-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 31st - The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a typhoon warning at 7:00 AM this morning:

　　At 1:00 PM yesterday, the center of the typhoon in the Pacific Ocean made landfall in the coastal area of Putian County, Fujian. At 5:00 AM this morning, the typhoon center had moved to the mountainous area at the junction of northwestern Fujian and Jiangxi. Its intensity has greatly weakened, with winds near the center estimated at force 6 to 8. In the next thirty-six hours, the typhoon will turn to move in a northerly direction, and then continue to turn northeast, re-entering the sea from northern Jiangsu. Winds in Jiangsu, Anhui, eastern Hubei, southeastern Henan, eastern and southern Shandong, Bohai Sea, and northern Yellow Sea will continue to increase to force 6 to 8. Winds in the central and southern Yellow Sea will increase to force 8 to 11. Winds along the southeastern coast of Shandong and the coast of Jiangsu will increase to force 7 to 9. Shandong, Jiangsu, Anhui, and Zhejiang will experience heavy to torrential rain. After thirty-six hours, the typhoon will continue to move northeast and enter the Sea of Japan.

### 台风登陆后强度减弱  山东江苏安徽浙江一带将有大雨到暴雨

1959-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    台风登陆后强度减弱
    山东江苏安徽浙江一带将有大雨到暴雨
    新华社31日讯　中央气象科学研究所31日上午7时发布台风警报：
    昨天下午一点钟，太平洋上的台风中心已在福建莆田县沿海地区登陆，今天早晨五点钟，台风中心已移到了福建西北部与江西交界一带的山地地区，强度已大大减弱，中心附近风力估计有六到八级。未来三十六小时内，台风将转向偏北方向移动，并再继续转向东北方，由江苏北部重新入海。江苏、安徽、湖北东部、河南东南部、山东东部南部、渤海和黄海北部一带的风力，将继续加大到六至八级。黄海中部、南部的风力将加大到八至十一级。山东东南部沿海和江苏沿海的风力将加大到七至九级。山东、江苏、安徽、浙江一带将有大雨到暴雨。三十六小时以后，台风将继续向东北方移动进入日本海。
