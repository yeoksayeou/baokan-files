Title: ACFTU Chairman Liu Ningyi Holds Reception for World Federation of Trade Unions, Nigerian Trade Union Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions (ACFTU), held a reception this evening to welcome the World Federation of Trade Unions (WFTU) delegation led by WFTU Secretary Souiri and Michael Imoudu, Chairman of the Nigerian Trade Union Congress and Chairman of the Nigerian Railway and Ports Workers' Union. The guests are visiting our country at the invitation of the ACFTU and to participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Liu Changsheng and Li Jiebo, Vice-Chairmen of the ACFTU, Kang Yonghe, Secretary of the Secretariat, and Ma Chungu, Secretary of the ACFTU Secretariat stationed at the WFTU, also attended the reception.

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: The World Federation of Trade Unions delegation, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of our country, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon, led by Souiri, Secretary of the Secretariat of the WFTU. Members of the delegation included Vannote, General Secretary of the International Federation of Chemical, Oil and Allied Workers' Unions, and representatives of the International Federation of Commercial Workers' Unions, Siro and Bekhovsky (Soviet Union), Mahendra Sen (India), and Lola Schier (Austria). Ma Chungu, Secretary of the ACFTU Secretariat stationed at the WFTU, also arrived in Beijing on the same plane. Liu Changsheng, Vice-Chairman of the ACFTU, and Kang Yonghe, Secretary of the Secretariat, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 我全总主席刘宁一举行酒会  招待世界工联、尼日利亚工会代表团

1959-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    我全总主席刘宁一举行酒会
    招待世界工联、尼日利亚工会代表团
    新华社24日讯　中华全国总工会主席刘宁一今晚举行酒会，欢迎以世界工会联合会书记处书记苏吉里为首的世界工联代表团和尼日利亚工会大会主席、尼日利亚铁路港口工会主席迈克尔·伊穆杜。客人们是应邀前来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
    中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜、李颉伯，书记处书记康永和，中华全国总工会驻世界工联书记处书记马纯古等人也出席了酒会。
    新华社24日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请前来参加我国建国十周年庆祝典礼的世界工会联合会代表团，在世界工联书记处书记苏吉里率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团员有化学、石油与有关工人工会国际总书记万诺特，商业工作者工会国际代表西罗和贝霍夫斯基（苏联）、马亨德拉·森（印度）、洛拉·歇尔（奥地利）。中华全国总工会驻世界工联书记处书记马纯古也同机抵京。前往机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜、书记处书记康永和等。
