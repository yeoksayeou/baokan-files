Title: Mongolian Government Delegation Leaves Kunming for India; Tsedenbal Cables Premier Zhou to Express Gratitude Before Departure
Author:
Date: 1959-09-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, September 9th. The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Tsedenbal, and the Mongolian People's Republic government delegation he led, left Kunming by special plane at 9:00 AM today for a visit to India.

Before his departure, Chairman Tsedenbal, accompanied by Yan Hongyan, First Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Yu Yichuan, Governor of Yunnan Province, reviewed the guard of honor and accepted flowers from young women of all ethnic groups in Kunming.

Those who saw the distinguished guests off at the airport today also included Zhou Chiping, Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Liu Piyun, Vice Governor, Zhao Zengyi, First Secretary of the Kunming Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Yu Futing, Vice Mayor, Hu Ronggui, Major General of the Kunming Garrison, and others.

Chen Shuliang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, also saw them off at the airport.

Personnel from the Consulate of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming also went to the airport to see them off today.

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, September 9th. Before leaving Kunming this morning, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Tsedenbal, sent a cable to Premier Zhou Enlai. The text of the cable is as follows:
Dear Comrade Zhou Enlai:

As we leave the People's Republic of China today, I, in my own name and on behalf of my fellow comrades, extend our most sincere gratitude to you and the government of the People's Republic of China for the great care and brotherly assistance you have given us during our transit through China.

Dear Premier, please accept our best wishes for your good health and for greater victories in your activities to further the prosperity of the People's Republic of China.

May the brotherly and unbreakable friendship between the Mongolian and Chinese peoples last forever.

Tsedenbal
Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic
September 9, 1959, in Kunming

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, September 8th. The government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, led by Chairman Tsedenbal, today toured the scenic spots of Kunming and visited the Yunnan Printing and Dyeing Factory and the Yunnan Nationalities Institute.

This morning, Chairman Tsedenbal and all members of the delegation, accompanied by Liu Piyun, Vice Governor of Yunnan Province, Yu Futing, Vice Mayor of Kunming, and Chen Shuliang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, toured the scenic Western Hills of Kunming. Afterward, the distinguished guests drove to the Yunnan Printing and Dyeing Factory for a visit, where they were warmly welcomed by the workers. After visiting the printing workshop, Chairman Tsedenbal praised the factory for printing fabrics especially for various fraternal ethnic groups. He also inquired in detail about the factory's production management.

In the afternoon, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Lubsan, and other distinguished guests visited the Yunnan Nationalities Institute, which has students from more than 20 ethnic groups, and were welcomed by teachers and students of all ethnic groups lining the streets.

Yesterday evening, Yan Hongyan, First Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, hosted a banquet in honor of Chairman Tsedenbal and all members of the delegation.

### 蒙古政府代表团离昆明赴印  泽登巴尔行前致电周总理表示感谢

1959-09-10
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古政府代表团离昆明赴印
    泽登巴尔行前致电周总理表示感谢
    新华社昆明9日电　蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔和他率领的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团，今天上午九时乘专机离开昆明前往印度访问。
    泽登巴尔主席临行前，由中共云南省委第一书记阎红彦和云南省省长于一川陪同检阅了仪仗队，并接受了昆明市各族女青年的献花。
    今天到机场欢送贵宾的，还有中共云南省委书记周赤萍、副省长刘披云、中共昆明市委第一书记赵增益、副市长于馥庭、昆明驻军首长胡荣贵少将等多人。
    到机场上欢送的还有外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮。
    越南民主共和国驻昆明领事馆人员今天也到机场欢送。
    新华社昆明9日电　蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔今天上午离开昆明前致电周恩来总理，电文如下：亲爱的周恩来同志：
    当我们今天离开中华人民共和国的时候，我以个人的名义并代表我同行的同志们，向您和中华人民共和国政府表示最衷心的感谢，感谢您在我们从中国过境期间所给予的巨大关怀和兄弟般的帮助。
    亲爱的总理同志，请接受我们最良好的祝愿，祝您身体健康，并祝您在为进一步繁荣中华人民共和国的活动中取得更大的胜利。
    蒙中两国人民之间的兄弟般的、牢不可破的友谊万古常青。
    蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔
                    1959年9月9日于昆明
    新华社昆明8日电　由泽登巴尔主席率领的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团，今天游览了昆明的风景区，参观访问了云南印染厂和云南民族学院。
    今天上午，泽登巴尔主席和全体团员由云南省副省长刘披云、昆明市副市长于馥庭和外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮等陪同，游览了昆明的风景区西山。接着，贵宾们驱车前往云南印染厂进行参观访问，受到工人们的热烈欢迎。泽登巴尔主席参观印花车间后，赞扬了这个厂专为各兄弟民族印染的花布。他还详细询问了工厂的生产管理情况。
    下午，部长会议副主席鲁布桑和其他贵宾们参观了有二十多种民族学生的云南民族学院，受到了各民族师生的夹道欢迎。
    昨天晚间，中共云南省委第一书记阎红彦曾设宴招待泽登巴尔主席和代表团全体团员。
