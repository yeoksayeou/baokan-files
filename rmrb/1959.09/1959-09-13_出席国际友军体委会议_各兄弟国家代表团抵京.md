Title: Delegations from Brotherly Countries Arrive in Beijing to Attend the International Military Sports Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1959-09-13
Page: 4

    To Attend the International Military Sports Council Meeting
    Delegations from Brotherly Countries Arrive in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 12th September: The Third Plenary Meeting of the International Military Sports Council is about to be held in Beijing. Military sports delegations from eleven brotherly countries—the Soviet Union, Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, North Korea, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Mongolia, the German Democratic Republic, Albania, and Vietnam—arrived in Beijing successively from the 7th to the 12th to attend this meeting.
    The military sports delegations from brotherly countries will also be invited to visit the First National Games of the People's Republic of China.

### 出席国际友军体委会议  各兄弟国家代表团抵京

1959-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    出席国际友军体委会议
    各兄弟国家代表团抵京
    新华社12日讯　国际友军体育运动委员会第三次全体会议即将在北京召开。出席这次会议的苏联、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、朝鲜、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、蒙古、德意志民主共和国、阿尔巴尼亚、越南等十一个兄弟国家的军队体育代表团于7日至12日先后到达北京。
    各兄弟国家军队体育代表团还将应邀参观中华人民共和国第一届运动会。
