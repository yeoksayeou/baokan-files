Title: Successful Trial Production of High Hot Blast Pipe for Small Blast Furnaces
Author: 
Date: 1959-09-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 26th - The Kunming Metallurgical Ceramics Research Institute of the Chinese Academy of Sciences has successfully trial-produced a simple, heat-resistant cast iron hot blast pipe using ordinary pig iron, creating an important technical condition for increasing the blast temperature of small blast furnaces.

At present, the pig iron cast hot blast pipes on general small blast furnaces do not have strong oxidation resistance at high temperatures, and can only reach 500 to 600 degrees Celsius. If they reach 800 degrees Celsius, the hot blast pipes begin to oxidize and peel off layer by layer like a snake shedding its skin. However, the simple heat-resistant iron-aluminum alloy cast iron hot blast pipe will not generate iron oxide scale even at a high temperature of 1,000 to 1,100 degrees Celsius. The use of this type of hot blast pipe can not only increase the blast temperature and reduce the coke ratio, but also save materials. If the smooth tube type pig iron cast hot blast pipe on a small blast furnace with a volume of 30 cubic meters is replaced with a needle tube type simple heat-resistant iron-aluminum alloy cast iron hot blast pipe, the amount of metal used can be reduced by four-fifths. This iron-aluminum cast iron hot blast pipe has good casting performance and can be machined.

The Kunming Metallurgical Ceramics Research Institute and the Yunnan Provincial Department of Mechanical Industry have jointly held an on-site meeting and decided to promote this new technology in the province's iron and steel industry.

### 小高炉高热风管试制成功

1959-09-27
第3版()
专栏：

    小高炉高热风管试制成功
    新华社昆明26日电　中国科学院昆明冶金陶瓷研究所，利用普通生铁，试制简易耐热的铸铁热风管成功，为提高小高炉风温创造了一项重要的技术条件。
    目前，一般小高炉上的生铁浇铸热风管，在高温下的抗氧化性不强，只能到摄氏五百度至六百度，如果到了八百度，热风管就开始氧化，像蛇脱皮似的一层一层往下掉，而简易耐热铁铝合金的铸铁热风管，在摄氏一千度至一千一百度的高温下，仍不会生成氧化铁皮。采用这种热风管，不仅能提高风温，降低焦比，而且节约材料。一座容积三十立方米的小高炉，上面的平滑管式生铁浇铸热风管，如果改用针状管式简易耐热铁铝合金铸铁热风管，使用的金属就可以减少六分之五。这种铁铝铸铁热风管，浇铸性能良好，能切削加工。
    昆明冶金陶瓷研究所和云南省机械工业厅已经联合召开现场会议，决定在全省炼铁工业中推广这项新的技术。
