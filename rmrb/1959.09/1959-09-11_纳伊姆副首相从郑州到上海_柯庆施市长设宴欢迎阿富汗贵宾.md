Title: Vice Premier Naim Arrives in Shanghai from Zhengzhou; Mayor Ke Qingshi Hosts Banquet Welcoming Afghan Guests
Author:
Date: 1959-09-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 10th September: Afghan Kingdom's Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Sardar Mohammad Naim Khan and other Afghan dignitaries accompanying him, arrived in Shanghai this morning from Zhengzhou on a special plane, accompanied by Vice Foreign Minister Ji Pengfei, Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan Hao Ting, and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Ma Zhenwu, and were warmly welcomed by Shanghai Mayor Ke Qingshi and people from all walks of life.

    Also present at the airport to welcome the distinguished guests were Major General Xiong Yingtang, Deputy Commander of the Shanghai Garrison, Hu Juewen, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Zeng Tao, Secretary-General of the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, and others.

    Also present at the airport to welcome them were the consuls-general and consuls of various countries stationed in Shanghai.

    Afghan Ambassador to China, Samad, accompanied Prince Naim to Shanghai today.

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 10th September: Shanghai Mayor Ke Qingshi held a banquet this evening to welcome Afghan Kingdom's Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Naim Khan and other Afghan dignitaries who came to visit Shanghai.

    Attending the banquet were Vice Foreign Minister Ji Pengfei, Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan Hao Ting, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Ma Zhenwu, Vice Mayors of Shanghai Niu Shucai, Zhao Zukang, Sheng Pihua, and Secretary-General of the Municipal People's Committee Zeng Tao, who accompanied the distinguished guests to Shanghai.

    Afghan Ambassador to China, Samad, also attended the banquet.

    Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 10th September: Afghan Kingdom's Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Sardar Mohammad Naim Khan and his entourage, after a friendly visit in Zhengzhou on the 9th, left for Shanghai by special plane at 9:00 a.m. today.

    Present at the airport to see them off were Wu Zhipu, Governor of Henan Province, Zhao Wenfu, Vice Governor, Du Mengmo, Vice Chairman of the Henan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Wang Junzhi, Mayor of Zhengzhou, and Hu Xuanchang, Deputy Director of the Henan Provincial Ethnic Affairs Commission, and others.

### 纳伊姆副首相从郑州到上海  柯庆施市长设宴欢迎阿富汗贵宾

1959-09-11
第3版()
专栏：

    纳伊姆副首相从郑州到上海
    柯庆施市长设宴欢迎阿富汗贵宾
    据新华社上海10日电　阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆亲王和随他前来的其他阿富汗贵宾，今天上午在外交部副部长姬鹏飞、中国驻阿富汗大使郝汀和外交部礼宾司副司长马振武陪同下，从郑州乘专机到达上海，受到上海市市长柯庆施和各界人民的热烈欢迎。
    今天到机场欢迎贵宾的还有上海警备区副司令员熊应堂少将，政协上海市委员会副主席胡厥文，上海市人民委员会秘书长曾涛等。
    到机场欢迎的还有各国驻上海的总领事、领事等。
    阿富汗驻华大使萨马德今天陪同纳伊姆亲王到达上海。
    新华社上海10日电　上海市市长柯庆施今天晚上举行宴会，欢迎前来上海访问的阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣纳伊姆亲王和其他阿富汗贵宾。
    出席宴会的，有陪同贵宾来上海的我国外交部副部长姬鹏飞、我国驻阿富汗大使郝汀、外交部礼宾司副司长马振武，上海市副市长牛树才、赵祖康、盛丕华，市人民委员会秘书长曾涛等。
    阿富汗驻华大使萨马德也出席了宴会。
    新华社郑州10日电　阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆亲王和他的随行人员9日在郑州进行了友好访问后，于今天上午九时乘专机去上海。
    到机场欢送的有河南省省长吴芝圃、副省长赵文甫，河南省政协副主席杜孟模，郑州市市长王均智，河南省民族事务委员会副主任虎轩昌等。
