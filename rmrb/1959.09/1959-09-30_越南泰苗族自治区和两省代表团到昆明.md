Title: Delegation from Vietnam's Thai-Meo Autonomous Region and Two Provinces Arrives in Kunming
Author:
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, September 29th. At the invitation of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee, a delegation of thirty people from the Thai-Meo Autonomous Region, Ha Giang Province, and Lao Cai Province of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Hoang Xuan Thai, member of the Thai-Meo Autonomous Region Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, arrived in Kunming by train on the evening of the 28th to visit Yunnan and participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 越南泰苗族自治区和两省代表团到昆明

1959-09-30
第3版()
专栏：

    越南泰苗族自治区和两省代表团到昆明
    新华社昆明29日电　应中共云南省委、云南省人民委员会邀请来云南访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的越南民主共和国泰族—苗族自治区、河江省、老街省党政人民代表团一行三十人，在团长、越南劳动党泰族—苗族自治区委员会委员黄春泰率领下，于28日晚间乘火车到达昆明。
