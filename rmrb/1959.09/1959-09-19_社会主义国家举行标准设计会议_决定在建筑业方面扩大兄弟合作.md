Title: Socialist Countries Hold Standard Design Conference, Decide to Expand Brotherly Cooperation in Construction

Author: 
Date: 1959-09-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 17th: The communique of the Second International Conference on Standard Design of Socialist Countries has been published here.

The communique states that, in accordance with the resolution adopted at the First International Conference on Standard Design held in Berlin in May 1957, the Second International Conference on Standard Design was held in Leningrad from September 9 to 14, 1959.

The conference was attended by delegations from the member countries of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance: Albania, Bulgaria, Hungary, the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Romania, the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, and countries with observers in the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance: the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, China, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and Mongolia.

Representatives of the Permanent Committee on Construction Cooperation of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance also participated in the conference.

The communique states that the conference discussed the following issues: the tasks of standard design arising from the expansion of international cooperation among socialist countries in the construction industry, the basic principles of a unified international modular system, the determination of unified terminology to be used in standard design, the preparation of a unified international standard plan for architectural design, the determination of a unified method for evaluating the technical and economic indicators of the design of houses and buildings, and the unified classification of agricultural buildings.

The communique states that the views of the socialist countries on the further development of standard design were consistent at the conference. The communique points out that standard design is of utmost importance for the successful completion of the grand designs of all sectors of the national economy.

The basic regulations of the unified international modular system adopted by the conference are of great significance for the standardization of the specifications of house and building designs and structural treatments.

The communique states that the conference adopted the basic terminology to be used in design and made suggestions for compiling a unified international glossary of basic technical terms and concepts used in construction.

The communique states that the resolution adopted by the conference on the formulation of unified international engineering design standards, as well as a series of specific plans in international documents on standard issues jointly drafted in recent years, are of great significance in the development of international cooperation.

The communique concludes by stating that the results of the conference prove that the conference has successfully completed its tasks and reinforces the conviction that the fraternal cooperation among socialist countries in construction will be further expanded.

### 社会主义国家举行标准设计会议  决定在建筑业方面扩大兄弟合作

1959-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    社会主义国家举行标准设计会议
    决定在建筑业方面扩大兄弟合作
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科17日讯：此间发表了社会主义国家标准设计第二次国际会议的会议和决议公报。
    公报说，根据1957年5月在柏林举行的标准设计第一次国际会议所通过的决议，1959年9月9日至14日在列宁格勒举行了标准设计第二次国际会议。
    出席会议的有经济互助委员会成员国的代表团：阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、匈牙利、德意志民主共和国、波兰、罗马尼亚、苏联、捷克斯洛伐克和在经济互助委员会中派有观察员的国家：越南民主共和国、中国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和蒙古。
    参加会议的还有经济互助委员会建筑合作常设委员会的代表。
    公报说，会议讨论了下列问题：由于社会主义国家在建筑业方面的国际合作的扩大而产生的标准设计的任务问题、统一国际模数制的基本原则问题、确定在标准设计中采用的统一术语问题、编制建筑设计统一国际标准计划问题、确定估价房屋和建筑物的设计的技术经济指标的统一方法问题和农业建筑物统一分类问题。
    公报说，各社会主义国家在进一步发展标准设计这个问题上的观点，在会议上取得了一致。公报指出，标准设计对顺利完成国民经济各部门的宏伟设计具有极重要的意义。
    会议通过的统一的国际模数制的基本条例对房屋和建筑物的设计和结构处理的规格化有极重大的意义。
    公报说，会议通过了在设计中采用的基本术语，并且就编制在建设中所应用的基本技术术语和概念的统一国际词汇一事提出了建议。
    公报说，这次会议所通过的关于制定统一的国际工程设计标准的决议，以及在最近几年联合拟定的一系列关于标准问题的国际文献中的具体计划，在发展国际合作方面具有重大意义。
    公报最后指出，会议结果证明，会议已经胜利地完成了它的任务，并使人坚信社会主义国家在建筑方面的兄弟般的合作将进一步扩大。
