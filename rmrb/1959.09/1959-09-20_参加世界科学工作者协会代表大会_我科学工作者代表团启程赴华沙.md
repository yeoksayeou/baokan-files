Title: Chinese Scientific Workers Delegation Departs for Warsaw to Attend the World Federation of Scientific Workers Congress
Author:
Date: 1959-09-20
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, September 19th: The Chinese scientific workers delegation left Beijing by plane this morning for Warsaw to attend the Sixth Congress and thematic discussions of the World Federation of Scientific Workers, to be held in the Polish capital from September 24th to 28th. The head of the delegation is Zhu Kezhen, the deputy head is Ding Ying, the secretary-general is Zhou Peiyuan, and the delegates include well-known scientists such as Huang Jiasi, Shen Qiyi, Ma Xingyuan, Nie Chunrong, Xia Kangnong, and advisor Zhang Youxuan.
    Traveling on the same plane were scientists Nguyen Khanh Toan and Ho Dac Di from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, scientists Hu Bentze and Asa Munanda from the Republic of Indonesia, and scientists Babushti, Weiruikun, and Koti Goda from Ceylon, who were transiting through China.
    Fan Changjiang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Association for Science and Technology, and more than twenty people from scientific circles, including Yang Xiandong and Pei Wenzhong, saw them off at the airport.

### 参加世界科学工作者协会代表大会  我科学工作者代表团启程赴华沙

1959-09-20
第6版()
专栏：

    参加世界科学工作者协会代表大会
    我科学工作者代表团启程赴华沙
    新华社19日讯　中国科学工作者代表团今日上午由北京乘飞机前往华沙，参加9月24日到28日在波兰首都举行的世界科学工作者协会第六届代表大会及专题讨论会。代表团团长是竺可桢，副团长丁颖，秘书长周培源，代表有黄家驷、沈其益、马杏垣、聂春荣、夏康农和顾问张有萱等著名的科学家。
    同机前往的，有路经中国的越南民主共和国科学家阮庆全、胡得怡，印度尼西亚共和国科学家胡本智、阿萨·慕南达，锡兰科学家巴布斯梯、魏瑞昆、柯提哥达。
    中华全国科学技术协会副主席范长江和科学界人士杨显东、裴文中等二十多人到机场送行。
