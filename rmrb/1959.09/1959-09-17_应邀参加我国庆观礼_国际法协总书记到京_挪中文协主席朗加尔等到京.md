Title: Invited to Attend China's National Day Ceremony: Secretary-General of the International Association of Jurists Arrives in Beijing; Chairman Langar of the Norwegian-Chinese Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-17
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, 16th: At the invitation of the China Political Science and Law Society to visit China and attend the 10th anniversary National Day celebrations, members of the delegation of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, including Norman, the Secretary-General of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon after visiting the Mongolian People's Republic.
    Xinhua News Agency, 16th: At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China and attend the National Day celebrations, Langar, Chairman of the Norwegian-Chinese Cultural Association, and Dr. Gulvin, a music professor at the University of Oslo, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 应邀参加我国庆观礼  国际法协总书记到京  挪中文协主席朗加尔等到京

1959-09-17
第5版()
专栏：

    应邀参加我国庆观礼
    国际法协总书记到京
    挪中文协主席朗加尔等到京
    据新华社16日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请到我国访问并参加国庆十周年观礼的国际民主法律工作者协会代表团成员、国际民主法律工作者协会总书记诺尔曼等三人，在访问蒙古人民共和国以后，已于今天下午乘飞机到北京。
    新华社16日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问并参加国庆观礼的挪威中国文化协会主席朗加尔和奥斯陆大学音乐教授古尔温博士，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
