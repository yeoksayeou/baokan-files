Title: Greetings from Comrade Cao Bo Yangnason, Member of the Central Committee of the Icelandic Socialist Unity Party and Head of the Delegation of the Icelandic Socialist Unity Party

Author:
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 7

Dear Comrades:
	The Icelandic delegation is greatly honored to represent the Socialist Unity Party in extending our heartfelt congratulations to the Chinese Communist Party and the People's Republic of China on this glorious tenth anniversary of the People's Republic of China.
	The people of the Icelandic Socialist Unity Party and the people of Iceland have long paid close attention to the magnificent struggles of the Chinese people who seized power ten years ago.
	We recognize that when the Chinese people broke free from the shackles of foreign rule and domestic exploiting classes, an event of world significance occurred in the great process of humanity moving from capitalism to socialism; the Chinese people, having achieved victory, set about the task that the Russians began forty years ago.
	For ten years, we Icelandic Socialist Unity Party members have paid close attention to the extremely rapid changes in Chinese society in the political, economic, and cultural fields. We have seen how hundreds of millions of ordinary people, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, have built socialism and enabled their country to achieve ever-greater prosperity and cultural achievements.
	But there are even more ties that bind our hearts together.
	Our two countries—one the largest in the world and the other the smallest—are still engaged in the struggle against our common enemy, American imperialism. Ignoring the will of China and Iceland, American imperialism has stationed its troops on the lands of both countries. We are convinced that these two countries, the largest and the smallest in the world, will achieve complete victory in this struggle; the day when the last imperialist soldier leaves the land of our ancestors will be a day of joy for everyone.
	The entire Icelandic people are also engaged in a fierce struggle with British imperialism. British imperialism is trying to force the Icelanders to abandon their twelve-nautical-mile fishing limit by force, and thus to abandon their means of survival.
	We are confident that the Icelandic people will also win the final and complete victory in this struggle.
	The Icelandic delegation would like to take this opportunity to express its sincere gratitude to the Chinese Communist Party, the Chinese people, and their government for their support of the vital interests of the Icelandic people in this matter.
	As we humbly express our hope to further strengthen the ties of friendship between the Icelandic people and the Chinese people, we wish the Chinese people and the Chinese Communist Party all the best in their struggle to build a socialist society and to achieve world peace.

### 冰岛统一社会党中央委员会委员、冰岛统一社会党代表团团长  曹勃扬纳逊同志的贺词

1959-09-30
第7版()
专栏：

    冰岛统一社会党中央委员会委员、冰岛统一社会党代表团团长
    曹勃扬纳逊同志的贺词亲爱的同志们：
    冰岛代表团能够在中华人民共和国的这个辉煌的十周年纪念日代表统一社会党向中国共产党和中华人民共和国致以衷心的祝贺，感到非常荣幸。
    冰岛统一社会党人和冰岛人民长期以来一直非常注意在十年前取得了政权的中国人民的波澜壮阔的斗争。
    我们认识到：当中国人民摆脱外国统治和国内剥削阶级的锁链的时候，人类从资本主义走向社会主义的伟大进程中的一件具有世界重要意义的事件发生了；中国人民由于取得了胜利，就着手进行俄国人在四十年前开始的任务。
    我们冰岛的统一社会党人十年来一直非常注意中国社会在政治、经济和文化方面的极其迅速的变革。我们看到，几亿普通人民在中国共产党的领导下怎样在建设社会主义的基础上使他们的国家得到越来越大的繁荣和文化成就。
    但是，还有更多的纽带把我们的心连在一起。
    我们两国——一个是世界上最大的国家，一个是世界上最小的国家——仍然在进行反对共同的敌人美帝国主义的斗争。美帝国主义不顾中国和冰岛的意志，在两国的土地上都驻扎了它的军队。我们确信：这两个世界上最大和最小的国家都会在这一斗争中取得完全的胜利；当最后一个帝国主义的士兵离开我们祖先的土地的时候，那将是一个人人感到欢欣鼓舞的日子。
    全体冰岛人民还在同英帝国主义展开激烈的斗争。英帝国主义试图用武力迫使冰岛人放弃他们的十二浬的捕鱼界限，因而也就是要他们放弃自己的生存手段。
    我们确信，冰岛人民在这场斗争中也将赢得最后的完全的胜利。
    冰岛代表团希望利用这个机会对中国共产党、中国人民和他们的政府表示衷心的谢意，感谢他们在这件事情上对冰岛人民的切身利益给予的支持。
    在我们不揣冒昧地表示希望进一步加强冰岛人民和中国人民之间的友谊的联系的时候，我们祝中国人民和中国共产党在争取建成社会主义社会和争取实现世界和平的斗争中一切顺利。
