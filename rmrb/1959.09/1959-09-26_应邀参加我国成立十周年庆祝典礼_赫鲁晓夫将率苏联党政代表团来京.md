Title: Khrushchev to Lead Soviet Party and Government Delegation to Beijing to Attend China's Tenth Anniversary Celebration at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1959-09-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 25th, TASS dispatch from Moscow on the 24th: At the invitation of Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Comrade Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Soviet Union, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union have decided to dispatch a Soviet party and government delegation to attend the celebrations of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The members of the Soviet party and government delegation are: Comrade Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union (Head of the Delegation), Comrade Suslov, Member of the Presidium and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Comrade Gromyko, Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Minister of Foreign affairs of the Soviet Union, Comrade Tashenev, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union , Comrade Shelepin, Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and the Chairman of the State Security Committee of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Comrade Chervonenko, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ukraine, Comrade Nikolayeva, Secretary of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, Comrade Arkhipov, Vice Chairman of the State Committee of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union for Foreign Economic Relations, and Comrade Andropov, Minister of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.
	The delegation will leave Moscow for Beijing on September 27. As Comrade Khrushchev and Comrade Gromyko are still in the United States, they will travel to Beijing later. During this time, the delegation will be led by Comrade Suslov.

### 应邀参加我国成立十周年庆祝典礼  赫鲁晓夫将率苏联党政代表团来京

1959-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加我国成立十周年庆祝典礼
    赫鲁晓夫将率苏联党政代表团来京
    新华社25日讯　塔斯社莫斯科24日电：应中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志、中华人民共和国主席刘少奇同志、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德同志和中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志的邀请，苏联共产党中央委员会主席团、苏联最高苏维埃主席团和苏联部长会议决定派遣苏联党和政府代表团前往参加中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝典礼。苏联党和政府代表团的成员有：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫同志（代表团团长），苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员、苏联共产党中央委员会书记苏斯洛夫同志，苏联共产党中央委员会委员、苏联外交部部长葛罗米柯同志，苏联共产党中央委员会候补委员、苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席塔谢涅夫同志，苏联共产党中央委员会委员、苏联部长会议国家安全委员会主席谢列平同志，乌克兰共产党中央委员会书记契尔沃年科同志，全苏工会中央理事会书记尼古拉耶娃同志，苏联部长会议国家对外经济关系委员会副主席阿希波夫同志和苏联共产党中央委员会部长安德罗波夫同志。
    代表团将于9月27日离开莫斯科前往北京。由于赫鲁晓夫同志和葛罗米柯同志还在美国，他们将晚一些时候前往北京。在这段时间里，代表团将由苏斯洛夫同志率领。
