Title: Finnish Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 28th -- The Finnish Cultural Delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China and participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the country, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation was led by its head, Ilmari Halki.

### 芬兰文化代表团到京

1959-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

    芬兰文化代表团到京
    新华社28日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的芬兰文化代表团一行四人，在团长伊尔马里·哈尔基率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
