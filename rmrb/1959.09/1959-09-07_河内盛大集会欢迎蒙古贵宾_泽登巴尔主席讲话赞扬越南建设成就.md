Title: Grand Rally in Hanoi Welcomes Mongolian Guests; Chairman Tsedenbal's Speech Praises Vietnam's Construction Achievements
Author:
Date: 1959-09-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 6th. Over 10,000 representatives from all walks of life in Hanoi held a grand rally at the Hanoi People's Theater on the evening of the 5th to warmly welcome the Mongolian Party and Government Delegation led by Tsedenbal, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic.

When Chairman Tsedenbal, Vietnamese Democratic Republic Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, and National Assembly Standing Committee Chairman Ton Duc Thang stepped onto the rostrum, enthusiastic cheers and applause resounded throughout the venue. Also attending the conference were Vietnamese Democratic Republic Deputy Prime Minister Phan Ke Toai, ministers of various government departments, and foreign diplomatic envoys stationed in Vietnam.

Chairman Tsedenbal delivered a speech amid cheers from the entire audience. He warmly praised the brilliant construction achievements made by the Vietnamese people after winning the anti-colonial war. He said, "We are elated by the brilliant achievements made by the Vietnamese people in economic and cultural construction. These achievements are a solid foundation for the cause of Vietnamese national unification."

Chairman Tsedenbal expressed congratulations on the continuous consolidation and development of friendship between Mongolia and Vietnam. He said that the joint communique signed by the party and government delegations of the two countries will help further consolidate and develop the friendship between the two countries and help consolidate the unity of the socialist camp and safeguard peace in Asia and the world.

In his speech, Chairman Tsedenbal expressed his belief that under the leadership of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the Vietnamese people will surely crush all the conspiracies of imperialism, will surely unify their motherland, and will make greater contributions to the cause of defending world peace.

Chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee Tran Duy Hung delivered a welcoming speech at the beginning of the meeting, warmly welcoming the distinguished Mongolian guests. In his speech, he stated that the Vietnamese Democratic Republic supports the Mongolian People's Republic's accession to the United Nations and thanked the Mongolian people for their support to Vietnam's construction cause.

At the meeting, Chairman Tsedenbal presented a banner and a Mongolian horse-head fiddle to the Vietnamese people. He said: "It is hoped that this horse-head fiddle will play songs praising the friendship between the people of the two countries and socialism." Chairman Tran Duy Hung presented a banner from the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee to the Mongolian people.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 6th. This morning, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Tsedenbal, accompanied by Prime Minister of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic, Pham Van Dong, and Chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee, Tran Duy Hung, visited the Phu Thuong Advanced Agricultural Cooperative in the suburbs of Hanoi.

More than a thousand members of the agricultural cooperative lined the streets to welcome the distinguished Mongolian guests.

Chairman Tsedenbal and his entourage cordially talked with the cooperative members in the rice fields about the autumn agricultural production this year. The distinguished guests praised the good growth of the cooperative's rice.

Chairman Tsedenbal attended a welcome meeting attended by more than a thousand cooperative members and spoke to the members. Chairman Tsedenbal congratulated them on their bumper harvest in summer rice production and conveyed the friendship of Mongolian herdsmen to Vietnamese farmers. He finally shouted in Vietnamese: "Long live unified Vietnam!"

The director of the agricultural cooperative and the person in charge of the Phu Thuong Township branch of the Vietnam Workers' Party presented the distinguished guests with agricultural products such as fragrant bananas and lemons that the agricultural cooperative had produced in abundance. Chairman Tsedenbal presented a banner to the agricultural cooperative.

### 河内盛大集会欢迎蒙古贵宾  泽登巴尔主席讲话赞扬越南建设成就

1959-09-07
第6版()
专栏：

    河内盛大集会欢迎蒙古贵宾
    泽登巴尔主席讲话赞扬越南建设成就
    新华社河内6日电　河内各界人民的代表一万多人5日晚上在河内人民剧场举行盛大集会，热烈欢迎以蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记、蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔为首的蒙古党政代表团。
    当泽登巴尔主席和越南民主共和国总理范文同、国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜等走上主席台时，会场上响起热烈的欢呼和掌声。参加大会的还有越南民主共和国副总理潘继遂、政府各部部长和外国驻越南的外交使节。
    泽登巴尔主席在全场欢呼声中发表讲话。他热烈赞扬越南人民在取得反殖民主义战争的胜利之后，接着取得辉煌的建设成就。他说，“我们为越南人民在经济和文化建设方面取得的辉煌成就而欢欣鼓舞。这些成就是越南国家统一事业的稳固基础。”
    泽登巴尔主席表示祝贺蒙越两国之间的友谊不断巩固和发展。他说，两国党政代表团签署的联合公报将有助于进一步巩固和发展两国之间的友谊，并且有助于巩固社会主义阵营的团结一致和维护亚洲和全世界的和平。
    泽登巴尔主席在讲话中表示相信，越南人民在越南劳动党的领导下，必将粉碎帝国主义的一切阴谋，必将统一他们的祖国，并且对保卫世界和平的事业作出更大的贡献。
    河内市行政委员会主席陈维兴在大会开始时致欢迎词，热烈欢迎蒙古贵宾。他在讲话中表示，越南民主共和国支持蒙古人民共和国加入联合国，并且感谢蒙古人民对越南建设事业所给予的支援。
    在大会上，泽登巴尔主席把一面锦旗和一只蒙古马头琴送给越南人民。他说：“希望这只马头琴将奏出颂赞两国人民友谊和社会主义的歌声。”陈维兴主席把河内市行政委员会的一面锦旗送给蒙古人民。
    新华社河内6日电　蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔今天上午在越南民主共和国总理范文同和河内市行政委员会主席陈维兴的陪同下，访问了河内郊区富上乡高级农业合作社。
    蒙古贵宾受到一千多名农业社社员的夹道欢迎。
    泽登巴尔主席和他的随行人员在合作社的稻田里同社员们亲切地谈到今年秋季农业生产的情况。贵宾们称赞合作社的稻子长得很好。
    泽登巴尔主席出席了有一千多名社员参加的欢迎大会，并且对社员们讲了话。泽登巴尔主席庆贺他们在夏稻生产中获得丰收，并且向他们转达了蒙古农牧民对越南农民的友谊。他最后用越语高呼：“越南统一万岁！”
    农业合作社主任和越南劳动党富上乡支部负责人把农业社丰产的香焦、柠檬等农产品送给贵宾。泽登巴尔主席送给农业合作社一面锦旗。
