Title: Albanian Physical Culture and Sports Commission Member Karaguni Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, September 21st. Ki Karaguni, member of the Albanian Physical Culture and Sports Commission, arrived in Beijing on the 21st to visit China's First National Games.

### 阿尔巴尼亚体委委员卡拉古尼抵京

1959-09-23
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚体委委员卡拉古尼抵京
    新华社21日讯　阿尔巴尼亚体育运动委员会委员基·卡拉古尼于21日到达北京参观我国第一届全国运动会。
