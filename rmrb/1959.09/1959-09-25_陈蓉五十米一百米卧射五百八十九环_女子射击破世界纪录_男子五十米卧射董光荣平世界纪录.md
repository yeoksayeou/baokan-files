Title: Chen Rong scores 589 rings in 50-meter and 100-meter prone shooting, women's shooting breaks world record, Dong Guangrong equals world record in men's 50-meter prone shooting
Author:
Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, 24th: More good news from the National Games shooting competition: a female athlete broke a world record, and a male athlete equaled a world record.

This morning, thirty-one female shooters participated in the women's free-choice small-bore rifle 50-meter and 100-meter, 30-shot prone shooting record-breaking competition. Chen Rong, a 24-year-old female shooter from the People's Liberation Army—a nurse at the Guangzhou Military Region clinic and a mother of one child—scored 294 rings in the 50-meter distance shooting and 295 rings in the 100-meter distance shooting. Her total score of 589 rings broke the world record of 588 rings set by Soviet female athlete Donskaya at the 37th World Shooting Championships held in Moscow in 1958.

The men's free-choice small-bore rifle 50-meter 40-shot prone shooting competition, with 140 participants from 28 delegations, saw 28 athletes complete their shooting today. Dong Guangrong, a 22-year-old private from the People's Liberation Army with a farming background, achieved the highest score of 396 rings. This score equals the world record of 396 rings set at the 37th World Shooting Championships held in Moscow in 1958. (Attached photo)

### 陈蓉五十米一百米卧射五百八十九环  女子射击破世界纪录  男子五十米卧射董光荣平世界纪录

1959-09-25
第8版()
专栏：

    陈蓉五十米一百米卧射五百八十九环
    女子射击破世界纪录
    男子五十米卧射董光荣平世界纪录
    新华社24日讯　全国运动会射击竞赛再传捷报：一名女运动员破世界最高纪录，一名男运动员平世界最高纪录。
    今天上午有三十一名女射手参加女子自选小口径步枪五十米和一百米各三十发卧射创纪录赛。解放军队二十四岁的女射手陈蓉——广州部队机关门诊部的护士、一个孩子的妈妈，在五十米距离射击中命中二百九十四环，一百米距离射击中命中二百九十五环，以五百八十九环的总成绩打破了苏联女运动员顿斯卡娅于1958年在莫斯科举行的第三十七届世界射击冠军赛创造的五百八十八环世界最高纪录。
    有二十八个代表队共一百四十人参加的男子自选小口径步枪五十米四十发卧射竞赛，今天有二十八名选手射击完毕，解放军队选手、二十二岁农民出身的上等兵董光荣创三百九十六环最高成绩，这一成绩平1958年在莫斯科举行的第三十七届世界射击冠军赛三百九十六环的世界最高纪录。（附图片）
