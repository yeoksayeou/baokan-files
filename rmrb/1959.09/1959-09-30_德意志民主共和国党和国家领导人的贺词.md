Title: Greetings from Party and State Leaders of the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 8

Greetings from the Party and State Leaders of the German Democratic Republic
To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Chinese Communist Party
To Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
To Comrade Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
Dear friends and comrades:

    On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, please allow us to extend to you, and through you to all the fraternal Chinese people, our most heartfelt congratulations and fraternal combat salute.

    The victory of the Chinese people against feudal and imperialist exploiters and the founding of the People's Republic of China are a decisive turning point in the history of the Chinese people, rich in revolutionary and cultural traditions. After shaking off oppression, the Chinese people, under the leadership of the glorious Communist Party, have resolutely embarked on the path of socialism. The founding of the People's Republic of China is the victory of the scientific doctrine of Marxism-Leninism under the specific historical conditions of China, and at the same time, it is another heavy defeat for the world's imperialist forces. The victory of the Chinese people has become a new and enormous source of strength for the forces of peace and socialism. The People's Republic of China has set a brilliant example for the people of Asian and African countries in their struggle for national liberation.

    The first ten years of the powerful People's Republic of China mark the historic achievements of the Chinese people, under the leadership of the battle-tested Chinese Communist Party, in overcoming centuries of backwardness and poverty in all aspects of political, economic, and cultural life and striving for the victory of socialism. The Great Leap Forward demonstrates the high level of political consciousness, diligence, and creativity of the Chinese working people, who firmly support the government and the Communist Party headed by Comrade Mao Zedong.

    All attempts by reactionary forces to obstruct this development and undermine the great construction cause of the Chinese people have failed due to the unity of the Chinese people under the leadership of the Communist Party.

    The People's Republic of China is a powerful fortress in the socialist camp led by the Soviet Union and firmly stands on the internationalist position of socialism. The People's Republic of China has made outstanding contributions in the continuous deepening of fraternal relations between socialist countries and the increasing strength of the socialist camp in the worldwide struggle to defend peace and strive for the victory of socialism. The People's Republic of China, like all socialist countries, is providing new and convincing proof of the superiority of socialism over capitalism.

    Whenever there is a need to restrain imperialist war provocations, the People's Republic of China always powerfully voices its call. Today, no problem in world politics can be solved without the participation of the People's Republic of China. In the ten years of the existence of the People's Republic of China, despite all kinds of sabotage by imperialism, its international prestige has continuously and significantly increased.

    U.S. imperialism is still occupying Taiwan and provocatively infringing upon the sovereignty of the People's Republic of China's territorial waters and airspace, thereby interfering in the internal affairs of the People's Republic of China, and refusing to grant the People's Republic of China—the sole legitimate representative of the Chinese people—its rightful seat in the United Nations. In the struggle against the conspiracies of U.S. imperialism, the progressive forces of the world, including the German Democratic Republic, faithfully stand by the Chinese people.

    Since the founding of the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic, a new type of relationship has formed between our two fraternal countries. This relationship is guided by the ideas of proletarian internationalism, peace, and friendly equal cooperation. This new fraternal relationship between our two countries is prominently reflected in the Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation between the German Democratic Republic and the People's Republic of China, concluded on December 25, 1955. Our two countries provide each other with all possible assistance in the struggle to defend peace and build socialism. We would like to take this opportunity to express our deep gratitude to the fraternal Chinese people for the enormous support they have given to the people of the German Democratic Republic in building socialism and solving the vital national problems of Germany. We firmly believe that the fraternal relations between our two socialist countries, as well as the eternal friendship between the fraternal Chinese people and the people of the German Democratic Republic, will continue to develop and consolidate in the future.

    We wish you and all the Chinese people new achievements in the struggle to rapidly build socialism and safeguard world peace.

      Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic
      Walter Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany
      Otto Grotewohl, Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic
      Dr. Johannes Dieckmann, Chairman of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic

### 德意志民主共和国党和国家领导人的贺词

1959-09-30
第8版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国党和国家领导人的贺词中国共产党主席毛泽东同志中华人民共和国主席刘少奇同志中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德同志中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志亲爱的朋友们和同志们：
    在中华人民共和国成立十周年的时候，请允许我们向你们并通过你们向全体兄弟的中国人民致以最衷心的祝贺和兄弟般的战斗敬礼。
    中国人民反对封建和帝国主义剥削者的胜利和中华人民共和国的成立是富有革命和文化传统的中国人民历史上一个决定性的转折点。中国人民摆脱了压迫之后，在光荣的共产党的领导下坚定地走上了社会主义的道路。中华人民共和国的成立是马克思列宁主义的科学学说在中国具体历史条件下的胜利，同时也是世界帝国主义势力的又一次惨重的失败。中国人民的胜利成为和平和社会主义力量的一个新的巨大的力量源泉。中华人民共和国为亚非各国人民进行自己的民族解放斗争树立了一个光辉的榜样。
    强大的中华人民共和国成立的头十年标志着中国人民在经过战斗考验的中国共产党的领导下，在政治、经济和文化生活各个方面克服千百年的落后和贫困状态而争取社会主义胜利的斗争所取得的历史性成就。大跃进表明了中国劳动人民的高度的政治觉悟、勤劳和创造性，他们坚定地拥护政府和以毛泽东同志为首的共产党。
    反动势力阻挡这种发展和破坏中国人民的伟大建设事业的一切企图，由于中国人民在共产党领导下的团结一致而失败了。
    中华人民共和国是在苏联领导下的社会主义阵营的一个强大堡垒，并且坚定地站在社会主义的国际主义立场上。在维护和平和争取社会主义胜利的世界范围的斗争中，社会主义国家间的兄弟关系不断加深，社会主义阵营日益强大，中华人民共和国在这方面作出了卓越的贡献。中华人民共和国同所有社会主义国家一样，为社会主义对资本主义的优越性日益提供着新的、令人信服的明证。
    每当需要约制帝国主义战争挑衅的时候，中华人民共和国总是强有力地发出它的呼声。今天在世界政治中没有一个问题可以在没有中华人民共和国参加的情况下得到解决。在中华人民共和国存在的十年中，尽管帝国主义进行种种破坏，它的国际威信仍然不断地并且十分显著地得到了提高。
    美帝国主义仍然占领着台湾和挑衅地侵犯中华人民共和国领海、领空的主权，从而干涉着中华人民共和国的内政，并且拒绝给予中华人民共和国——中国人民唯一合法的代表——在联合国中的应有席位。在反对美帝国主义阴谋的斗争中，全世界的进步力量，包括德意志民主共和国在内，忠实地站在中国人民的一边。
    中华人民共和国和德意志民主共和国成立以来，我们两个兄弟国家之间形成了崭新的关系。这种关系是由无产阶级国际主义思想，和平和友好平等合作的思想所指导的。我们两国之间这种新的兄弟关系突出地表现在1955年12月25日缔结的德意志民主共和国和中华人民共和国友好合作条约中。我们两国在维护和平和建设社会主义的斗争中相互给予一切可能的援助。我们愿利用这个机会向兄弟的中国人民表示深切的谢意，感谢他们对德意志民主共和国人民建设社会主义和解决德国民族切身问题所给予的巨大支持。我们坚信，我们两个社会主义国家间的兄弟关系，以及兄弟的中国人民同德意志民主共和国人民之间的永恒友谊，今后将继续得到发展和巩固。
    我们祝你们和全体中国人民在迅速建设社会主义和保障世界和平的斗争中获得新的成就。
      德意志民主共和国总统　威廉·皮克
      德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记　瓦尔德·乌布利希
      德意志民主共和国总理　奥托·格罗提渥
      德意志民主共和国人民议院主席　约翰尼斯·狄克曼博士
