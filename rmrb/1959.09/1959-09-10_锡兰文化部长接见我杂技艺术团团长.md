Title: Sri Lankan Minister of Culture Receives Head of Chinese Acrobatic Troupe
Author:
Date: 1959-09-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 8th. I.R.P. Karugala, the Sri Lankan Minister of Culture and Social Services, received Sun Chuansi, the head of the Chinese Acrobatic Arts Troupe, in his office today and had a sincere conversation with him.

Present at the reception were Le. Si. Bu. Perera, Permanent Secretary of the Sri Lankan Ministry of Culture and Social Services, and Mu. Jie. Perera, Acting Director of the Bureau of Cultural Affairs. Zhang Canming, the Chinese Ambassador to Sri Lanka, was also present.

### 锡兰文化部长接见我杂技艺术团团长

1959-09-10
第2版()
专栏：

    锡兰文化部长接见我杂技艺术团团长
    新华社科伦坡8日电　锡兰文化和社会服务部部长伊·鲁·普·格·卡卢加拉今天在他的办公室接见中国杂技艺术团团长孙川四，并且同他进行了诚挚的交谈。
    接见时在座的，有锡兰文化和社会服务部部务秘书勒·斯·布·佩雷拉，文化事业局代理局长姆·杰·佩雷拉。中国驻锡兰大使张灿明也在座。
