Title: Deputy Prime Minister Naim Visits Hangzhou After Visiting Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1959-09-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, September 12th: After a two-day visit to Shanghai, Prince Sardar Mohammad Naim, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, and his entourage, accompanied by Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Hao Ting, China's Ambassador to Afghanistan, and Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, left Shanghai by special plane for Hangzhou this morning. Afghan Ambassador to China, Samad, also accompanied them.

Those who welcomed and saw off the distinguished guests at the airport included Ke Qingshi, Mayor of Shanghai, Vice Mayors Niu Shucai, Zhao Zukang, and Sheng Pihua, Major General Xiong Yingtang, Deputy Commander of the Shanghai Garrison, Hu Juewen, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Zeng Tao, Secretary-General of the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee.

Consuls-General and Consuls of various countries in Shanghai also went to the airport to see them off.

Yesterday, the distinguished guests visited the Shanghai Steam Turbine Factory, the Shanghai Electric Motor Factory, the Golden Star Pen Factory, and the Shanghai No. 3 Woolen Textile Factory. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by workers lining the streets at the entrances of these factories.

On the afternoon of the 10th, the distinguished guests visited the state-owned Shanghai No. 1 Printing and Dyeing Factory and the Children's Palace, and toured the city by car. They also overlooked the entire city of Shanghai from the rooftop of the seventeen-story Shanghai Mansion.

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, September 12th: Prince Sardar Mohammad Naim, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, and his entourage arrived in Hangzhou by special plane from Shanghai at 9:30 this morning. Accompanying the distinguished guests to Hangzhou were Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Hao Ting, China's Ambassador to Afghanistan, and Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Abdul Samad, the Afghan Ambassador to China, also arrived on the same plane.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Zhou Jianren, Governor of Zhejiang Province, Wu Xian, Vice Governor and Mayor of Hangzhou, Lieutenant General Qian Jun, Deputy Commander of the Zhejiang Military Region, Yu Jiyi, Vice Chairman of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Gu Chunlin, Deputy Mayor of Hangzhou.

Accompanied by Wu Xian, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province, Gu Chunlin, Deputy Mayor of Hangzhou, and others, the distinguished guests visited the Hangzhou Du Jinsheng Silk Weaving Factory, where they were warmly welcomed by the staff. In the afternoon, the distinguished guests also visited scenic spots and historical sites such as Lingyin Temple, Yuquan, Liuhe Pagoda, and Huagang Park to View Fish, and boated on West Lake, enjoying the beautiful scenery.

In the evening, Zhou Jianren, Governor of Zhejiang Province, hosted a banquet in honor of the distinguished guests.

### 在上海参观访问后  纳伊姆副首相到杭州

1959-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    在上海参观访问后
    纳伊姆副首相到杭州
    据新华社上海12日电　阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆亲王和他的随行人员，在上海参观访问了两天后，今天早晨由外交部副部长姬鹏飞、我国驻阿富汗大使郝汀和外交部礼宾司副司长马振武陪同，乘专机离开上海前往杭州。阿富汗驻华大使萨马德也陪同前往。
    到机场欢送贵宾的有上海市市长柯庆施，副市长牛树才、赵祖康、盛丕华，上海警备区副司令员熊应堂少将，政协上海市委员会副主席胡厥文，上海市人民委员会秘书长曾涛等。
    各国驻上海的总领事、领事也到机场欢送。
    昨天，贵宾们参观了上海汽轮机厂、上海电机厂、金星金笔厂和上海第三毛纺织厂。贵宾们在这些工厂的门口受到了工人们的夹道欢迎。
    10日下午，贵宾们参观了国营上海第一印染厂和少年宫，并驱车游览了市区，还在十七层高的上海大厦屋顶上俯瞰了上海全景。
    新华社杭州12日电　阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆亲王和他的随行人员，今天上午九时三十分乘专机由上海到达杭州。陪同贵宾前来杭州的有外交部副部长姬鹏飞，我国驻阿富汗大使郝汀和外交部礼宾司副司长马振武。阿富汗驻中国大使阿卜杜勒·萨马德也同机前来。
    到机场欢迎的有浙江省省长周建人，副省长、杭州市市长吴宪，浙江军区副司令员钱钧中将，浙江省政协副主席余纪一，杭州市副市长顾春林等。
    贵宾们在浙江省副省长吴宪，杭州市副市长顾春林等陪同下，参观了杭州市都锦生丝织厂，受到了职工们的热烈欢迎。下午，贵宾们还游览了灵隐寺、玉泉、六和塔、花港观鱼等名胜古迹，并泛舟西湖，欣赏了湖光山色。
    晚上，浙江省省长周建人设宴招待贵宾。
