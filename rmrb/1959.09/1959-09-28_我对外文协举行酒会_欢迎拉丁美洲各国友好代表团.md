Title: Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Holds Reception Welcoming Friendship Delegations from Latin American Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 27th. Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Yang Hansheng, Vice President, held a reception tonight to welcome the cultural friendship delegations from Latin American countries who came to China to participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Scholars, poets, writers, artists, journalists, and well-known figures from all walks of life from eleven countries including Cuba, Brazil, Chile, Colombia, Bolivia, Argentina, Uruguay, Mexico, Venezuela, Paraguay, and Peru, as well as well-known figures from the cultural and artistic circles of the Chinese capital, gathered together. Nearly two hundred people attended today's reception.

Chu Tunan spoke at the reception. On behalf of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, he extended a warm welcome to the Latin American friends who had traveled far to China to participate in the celebration. He said that the Chinese people and the people of Latin American countries have a deep friendship. We believe that through the friendly unity and joint efforts of the Chinese people and the people of Latin American countries, we will not only enhance mutual understanding and friendship, and strengthen cultural ties, which will help each other's cultural prosperity and progress, but also make important contributions to anti-colonialism, opposition to wars of aggression, and the defense of world peace.

Chu Tunan described the great achievements made by our country in the past ten years under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, and thanked the Latin American people for their sympathy and support for the Chinese revolution and construction.

He said that the Chinese people have always expressed their high respect and great sympathy and support for the national democratic movements in Latin American countries. May the cultural exchanges and friendship between the Chinese people and the people of Latin American countries continue to develop and strengthen in our common struggle for a bright future.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently toasted the cultural exchanges and friendship between the Chinese people and the people of Latin American countries, and to the health of the friends present. Finally, famous Chinese artists performed wonderful programs.

Jose Venturelli, a Chilean peace activist, and his wife, who are in Beijing, were also invited to attend today's reception.

### 我对外文协举行酒会  欢迎拉丁美洲各国友好代表团

1959-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

    我对外文协举行酒会
    欢迎拉丁美洲各国友好代表团
    新华社27日讯　中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、副会长阳翰笙今晚举行酒会，欢迎来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的拉丁美洲各国文化友好代表团。
    来自古巴、巴西、智利、哥伦比亚、玻利维亚、阿根廷、乌拉圭、墨西哥、委内瑞拉、巴拉圭、秘鲁等十一个国家的学者、诗人、作家、艺术家、记者以及社会各界著名人士，和中国首都著名的文化艺术界人士欢聚一堂。参加今天酒会的近二百人。
    楚图南在酒会上讲话。他代表中国人民对外文化协会对远道来我国参加庆祝典礼的拉丁美洲朋友们表示热烈欢迎。他说，中国人民和拉丁美洲各国人民有着深厚的友谊。我们相信，通过中国人民和拉丁美洲各国人民的友好团结和共同努力，不仅会增进相互的了解和友谊，密切文化联系，有助于彼此的文化繁荣和进步，而且对于反殖民主义，反对侵略战争和保卫世界和平，也将作出重要的贡献。
    楚图南叙述了我国建国十年来在中国共产党和毛泽东主席领导下所获得的伟大成就，感谢拉丁美洲人民对中国革命和建设事业的同情和支持。
    他说，中国人民对于拉丁美洲各国的民族民主运动，也一贯表示了崇高的敬意和极大的同情与支持。愿中国人民和拉丁美洲各国人民的文化交流和友谊，在我们为了光明的未来的共同斗争中，不断发展，不断增强。
    席间，宾主双方频频为中国人民和拉丁美洲各国人民的文化交流和友谊干杯，为在座朋友们的健康干杯。最后，中国著名艺术家表演了精采节目。
    在京的智利和平人士何塞·万徒勒里和他的夫人也应邀参加了今天的酒会。
