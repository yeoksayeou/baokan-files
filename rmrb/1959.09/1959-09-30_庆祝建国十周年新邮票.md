Title: New Stamps Commemorating the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 12

The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications has issued five sets of commemorative stamps for the "10th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China," totaling nineteen stamps. The following set of three stamps is printed in two-color photogravure, with a red ribbon in the center of each stamp bearing the inscription "10th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China." (Image attached)

	First image: Features Chairman Mao and Tiananmen Square as its theme, representing the strength, unity, and growing prosperity of the People's Republic of China under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao.

	Second image: Features portraits of Marx and Lenin, the Kremlin, and twelve red flags as its theme. It represents Long Live Marxism-Leninism, Long Live the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union: The twelve red flags symbolize the twelve socialist countries.

	Third image: Features a dove of peace, the Earth, and giant hands of black, yellow, and white races, representing the great unity of the people of the world and the defense of world peace.

### 庆祝建国十周年新邮票

1959-09-30
第12版()
专栏：

    庆祝建国十周年新邮票
    邮电部发行了“中华人民共和国成立十周年”纪念邮票五组。计十九枚。下面这一组三枚邮票，是采用两色影写版套印，每枚图案中间均有一红色采带，上写“中华人民共和国成立十周年”。（附图片）
    第一图：以毛主席和天安门为主题，表示在中国共产党和毛主席英明领导下的中华人民共和国强大、统一和日益繁荣。
    第二图：以马克思、列宁像及克里姆林宫和十二面红旗为主题。表示马克思列宁主义万岁，以苏联为首的社会主义阵营万岁：十二面红旗象征十二个社会主义国家。
    第三图：为和平鸽、地球、黑、黄、白三种肤色的巨手，表示全世界人民大团结，保卫世界和平。
