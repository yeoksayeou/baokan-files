Title: Ministry of Metallurgical Industry and Heavy Industry Trade Union Issue Call: National Steel Workers Respond to Anshan Iron and Steel's Initiative
Author:
Date: 1959-09-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd: The Ministry of Metallurgical Industry and the National Committee of the China Heavy Industry Trade Union jointly issued a notice on September 1st, calling on steel enterprise workers nationwide to actively respond to the initiative of Anshan Iron and Steel workers, launch a socialist labor competition, and strive to complete and overfulfill the steel production targets of the Second Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule this year.

The notice said: Recently, inspired and encouraged by the great call of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Party, all the workers of Anshan Iron and Steel Company have been in high spirits and full of energy, with frequent reports of success, and steel production has been rising day by day. In August, five large fixed open-hearth furnaces and thirteen rolling mills all completed and overfulfilled their production plans ahead of schedule. However, they are not satisfied with the achievements they have already made. They have also issued a fighting call to steel plants across the country, resolutely guaranteeing: to complete the annual steel production plan ten days ahead of schedule, and at the same time overfulfill and complete the production tasks of pig iron, ore, coke, and refractory materials ahead of schedule, and guarantee the completion of quality, variety and cost indicators.

This initiative expresses the determination and will of all Anshan Iron and Steel workers to respond to the call of the Party. The Ministry of Metallurgical Industry and the National Committee of the China Heavy Industry Trade Union warmly congratulate the great achievements made by all the steel fighters of Anshan Iron and Steel, resolutely support this heroic initiative of the Anshan Iron and Steel workers, and hope that workers in steel enterprises across the country, especially the eighteen key enterprises, will summon up their energy and actively respond to their initiative, immediately launching a vigorous mass movement to increase production and practice economy on the steel front, and guarantee the completion and overfulfillment of steel production plans ahead of schedule.

### 冶金工业部和重工业工会发出号召  全国钢铁职工响应鞍钢倡议

1959-09-03
第2版()
专栏：

    冶金工业部和重工业工会发出号召
    全国钢铁职工响应鞍钢倡议
    新华社2日讯　冶金工业部同中国重工业工会全国委员会1日联合发出通知，号召全国钢铁企业职工积极响应鞍钢职工的倡议，开展社会主义劳动竞赛，为在今年内提前完成和超额完成第二个五年计划的钢铁生产指标而奋勇前进。
    通知说：最近，鞍山钢铁公司全体职工在党的八届八中全会的伟大号召和鼓舞下，斗志昂扬，干劲十足，捷报频传，钢铁生产日日上升。8月份，五座大型固定式平炉和十三个轧钢厂都提前和超额完成生产计划。但是，他们并不满足于已经取得的成就，他们又向全国各钢铁厂提出了一个充满干劲的战书，坚决保证：提前十天完成全年钢产计划，同时相应地超额和提前完成生铁、矿石、焦炭、耐火材料等生产任务，并且保证完成质量、品种和成本等指标。
    这个倡议表达了鞍钢全体职工响应党的号召的决心和意志。冶金工业部和中国重工业工会全国委员会热烈祝贺鞍钢全体钢铁战士所取得的伟大成就，坚决支持鞍钢职工这一豪迈的倡议，并且希望全国各个钢铁企业，特别是十八个重点企业的职工，鼓足干劲，积极响应他们的倡议，在钢铁战线上立即掀起一个轰轰烈烈的增产节约运动大高潮，保证提前和超额完成钢铁生产计划。
