Title: Two Italian Friends Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7
　
Xinhua News Agency, September 27th—Mr. and Mrs. Giffis, Vice Chairman of the Italian State-owned Carbon Cyanide Company, and Mr. Latti, Director of the Market Research Institute of this company, arrived in Beijing today. They are visiting our country and participating in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

### 意大利两位友人到京

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    意大利两位友人到京
    新华社27日讯　意大利国营碳化氰公司副董事长吉菲斯夫妇和这个公司的市场研究所主任拉蒂，今天到达北京。他们是应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请，来我国访问和参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
