    Frost Forecast
    Xinhua News Agency, 20th — The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a frost forecast at 8:00 AM on the 20th, stating:
    A cold high pressure area is strengthening in Siberia and northern Mongolia, and it will move southeastward in the future. It is expected that on the morning of the 22nd, there will be heavy frost in the entire Inner Mongolia region, northern Shanxi, and northwestern Hebei, and light frost in the mountainous areas of northeastern Hebei. On the morning of the 23rd or 24th, there will be frost in Heilongjiang and Jilin.

### 霜冻预报

1959-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    霜冻预报
    新华社20日讯　中央气象科学研究所20日上午八时发布霜冻预报说：
    在西伯利亚和蒙古北部一带有冷高气压加强，未来将向东南方向移动。预计22日早晨，内蒙古全区、山西北部、河北西北部，将有较重霜冻，河北东北部山地将有轻霜。23日或24日早晨，黑龙江、吉林将有霜冻。
