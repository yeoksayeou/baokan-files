Title: Letting Compatriots in Kinmen and Nearby Islands Celebrate National Day Together: Our Army Announces Ceasefire for Two Days
Author:
Date: 1959-09-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 28th. To celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Fujian Front Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army has decided to cease shelling on September 29th and October 1st. The announcement was broadcast on the front lines on the morning of September 28th. The full text of the broadcast is as follows:
  Compatriots in Greater Kinmen, Lesser Kinmen, Greater Tan, and Lesser Tan, and officers and soldiers of the Kuomintang: October 1st this year marks the great tenth anniversary of the founding of our nation. Our army has decided to cease shelling on September 29th and October 1st to allow you to celebrate this great festival together with the people of the whole country.

### 让金门和附近岛屿同胞共同欢度国庆  我军宣布停止炮击两日

1959-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

    让金门和附近岛屿同胞共同欢度国庆
    我军宣布停止炮击两日
    新华社福建前线28日电　为了欢度建国十周年，中国人民解放军福建前线司令部决定在9月29日和10月1日停止炮击，并且已经在9月28日上午在前线作了广播。广播词的全文如下：
  大金门、小金门、大担、二担的人民同胞们，国民党军官兵们：今年10月1日是我国伟大的建国十周年，本军决定在9月29日、10月1日停止炮击，让你们与全国人民一道共同欢度这一伟大的节日。
