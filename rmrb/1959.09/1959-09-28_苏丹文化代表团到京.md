Title: Sudanese Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 27th - At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the three-member Sudanese Cultural Delegation, led by its head, Gubara, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon to visit China and attend the National Day celebrations.

### 苏丹文化代表团到京

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    苏丹文化代表团到京
    新华社27日讯　应中华人民共和国对外文化联络委员会邀请来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的苏丹文化代表团一行三人，在团长古巴拉率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
