Title: China's Tenth Anniversary Celebration Concludes Successfully

Author: 
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: The celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China concluded victoriously at 6:10 PM today. Before the conclusion of the meeting, thirty-two distinguished guests delivered enthusiastic congratulatory speeches. Chairman Liu Shaoqi delivered the closing address.

The conference began at 2:40 PM today. Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and other Party and state leaders including Soong Ching Ling, Dong Biwu, Zhu De, Zhou Enlai, Lin Biao, Peng Zhen, and others, along with leading comrades from the Soviet Union and other socialist countries such as Suslov, Ho Chi Minh, Novotny, Kim Il-sung, Tsedenbal, Zawadzki, Dobi, Ghanev, Shehu, Matern, and Podnarasi, all members of the party and government delegations from the Soviet Union and other socialist countries, and the heads of fraternal party delegations from various countries, continued to participate in today's conference. When they ascended the rostrum together, the entire audience rose and applauded warmly for a long time.

The government delegation of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, which arrived in Beijing this afternoon, also participated in today's conference.

During the two-day celebration conference, international friends from eighty-three countries around the world celebrated the great festival of the Chinese people together with representatives of all ethnic groups in China. Sixty distinguished guests delivered speeches at the conference, warmly congratulating the Chinese people on their glorious achievements over the past decade.

Chairman Liu Shaoqi delivered the closing address at the end of the conference, expressing heartfelt gratitude on behalf of the entire Chinese people for the warm congratulations from the distinguished guests from various countries.

Chairman Liu Shaoqi said: "We are very grateful that leading comrades of the party and government from the Soviet Union and other fraternal socialist countries, leading comrades of communist parties from various countries, government representatives from friendly countries in Asia and Africa, representatives from international people's mass organizations, and friends from eighty-three countries in Asia, Europe, Africa, South America, North America, and Australia, have accepted our invitation to participate in our conference. The fact that so many international friends have come to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China with us is a tremendous support for us.

"At our conference, many respected guests have given us speeches, expressing warm encouragement and earnest expectations. These speeches will surely have a tremendous encouraging effect among our people. Let me, here, on behalf of the Chinese people, express my heartfelt gratitude to our respected guests.

"We have also received many congratulatory messages, telegrams, and other gifts from international friends. We would also like to express our thanks here.

"The Chinese people will certainly not disappoint the support and expectations of our friends from all countries. I believe that the comrades from all parts of the country, from all ethnic groups, and from all walks of life who are participating in this conference, as well as the representatives of patriotic overseas Chinese who have returned from abroad, will all have this feeling: what lies before us is the greatest undertaking in the history of China. We will still encounter many difficulties, but under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Comrade Mao Zedong, relying on the great unity of our people and the great unity between us and the people of the world, we will certainly be able to overcome any difficulties and achieve our great goals. In a not too long period of time, we will build China into a great socialist country with modern industry, modern agriculture, and modern science and culture."

Chairman Liu Shaoqi's closing remarks repeatedly aroused stormy and enthusiastic applause.

At today's conference, the heads and representatives of the fraternal parties from twenty-one countries, the heads and government representatives of friendly countries in Asia and Africa from four countries, and the heads of seven international organizations delivered congratulatory speeches. (The congratulatory speeches can be found on pages five to ten of today's newspaper.)

The friendly congratulatory speeches of the distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by the entire audience, and waves of applause echoed through the venue.

During the conference, it was announced that as of September 29, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China had received congratulatory letters and telegrams from the central committees of the following sixteen fraternal communist parties: the Communist Party of the United States, the Communist Party of Germany, the Communist Party of Burma, the Communist Party of Malaya, the Communist Party of Norway, the Communist Party of Portugal, the Communist Party of Thailand, the Communist Party of Paraguay, the Communist Party of Turkey, the Communist Party of Sudan, the Communist Party of El Salvador, the Progressive Party of the Workers of Cyprus, the Nicaraguan Socialist Party, the People's Party of Panama, the Communist Party of Pakistan, and the Irish Workers' League.

The meeting also announced that the delegation of the Communist Party of India headed by Comrade Ghosh, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of India, and the delegation of the Communist Party of Norway headed by Comrade Jorgen Vogt, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Norway, as well as the government delegation of Guinea, had all set out to China to participate in the celebrations for the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and were currently en route.

After Chairman Liu Shaoqi delivered the closing address, the band played music, and more than 300 Young Pioneers, holding bright bouquets of flowers, entered the venue in six groups, surged onto the rostrum, and presented flowers to Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and other Party and state leaders, to the distinguished guests from various countries, and to people from all walks of life in China on the rostrum. At this time, the entire audience stood up, the rostrum was filled with flowers, and the entire venue was like a sea of joy, with rhythmic applause lasting for more than ten minutes.

At 6:10 PM, the band played the solemn Internationale. Chairman Liu Shaoqi finally announced the end of the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. (Attached image)

### 我国建国十周年庆祝大会胜利闭幕

1959-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我国建国十周年庆祝大会胜利闭幕
    八十三国的朋友参加大会，六十位贵宾在会上致词，还有许多朋友送来贺词
    刘少奇主席致闭幕词。
    他代表我国人民感谢来宾们的鼓励和期望，并指出：摆在我们面前的是中国有史以来最伟大的事业，在中国共产党和毛泽东同志领导下，依靠我们全国人民的伟大团结，依靠我们和世界人民的伟大团结，一定能够在一个不太长的时间内，把中国建成为一个具有现代工业、现代农业、现代科学文化的伟大的社会主义国家。
    据新华社29日讯　中华人民共和国成立十周年庆祝大会今天下午六时十分胜利闭幕。在大会结束之前有三十二位贵宾在会上致热烈的祝词。刘少奇主席最后致闭幕词。
    今天的大会在下午二时四十分开始。毛泽东主席、刘少奇主席以及党和国家的其他领导人宋庆龄、董必武、朱德、周恩来、林彪、彭真等，同苏联和其他社会主义各国的领导同志苏斯洛夫、胡志明、诺沃提尼、金日成、泽登巴尔、萨瓦茨基、道比、加涅夫、谢胡、马特恩、波德纳拉希，苏联和其他社会主义各国党政代表团全体团员和各国兄弟党代表团团长等贵宾继续参加了今天的大会，当他们一起登上主席台的时候，全场起立，长时间地热烈鼓掌。
    今天下午到达北京的阿富汗王国政府代表团也参加了今天的大会。
    在两天的庆祝大会上，有来自全世界八十三个国家的国际朋友同中国各族人民的代表一起欢庆中国人民的伟大节日，有六十位贵宾在会上致词热烈祝贺中国人民十年来的光辉成就。
    刘少奇主席在大会最后致闭幕词，他代表全中国人民对各国贵宾的热情祝贺表示衷心感谢。
    刘少奇主席说：“我们非常感谢，苏联和其他兄弟社会主义国家的党政领导同志们，各国共产党的领导同志们，亚洲、非洲友好国家的政府代表们，国际性人民群众组织的代表们，以及亚洲、欧洲、非洲、南美洲、北美洲、澳洲的八十三个国家的朋友们，应邀参加了我们的大会。这么多的国际朋友来同我们一起庆祝中华人民共和国成立十周年，这是对于我们极大的支持。
    “在我们大会上，许多尊敬的来宾们给我们讲了话，对我们表示了热情的鼓励和殷切的期望。这些讲话，一定会在我们全国人民中产生巨大的鼓舞作用。让我在这里，以中国人民的名义，向我们尊敬的来宾们，表示衷心的谢意。
    “我们还收到了许多国际朋友送来的贺词、贺电和其他礼物。我们也在这里一并致谢。
    “中国人民一定不辜负各国朋友们对我们的支持和期望。我相信，参加我们这个大会的来自全国各地、各民族的各界同志们，以及从海外回国的爱国侨胞的代表们，一定都会有这样的感觉：摆在我们面前的是中国有史以来最伟大的事业。我们还会遇到许多困难，但是，在中国共产党和毛泽东同志的领导下，依靠我们全国人民的伟大团结，依靠我们和世界人民的伟大团结，我们一定能够克服任何困难，达到我们伟大的目标。在一个不太长的时间内，把中国建设成为一个具有现代工业、现代农业、现代科学文化的伟大的社会主义国家。”
    刘少奇主席的闭幕词，一再激起了暴风雨般的热烈掌声。
    在今天大会上致贺词的有二十一个国家兄弟党的代表团团长和代表，有四位亚非友好国家政府代表团的团长和政府代表，有七位国际组织的负责人。（贺词见今天本报第五至十版）
