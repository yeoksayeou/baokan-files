Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into Territorial Waters of Fujian Region; 67th Serious Warning Issued

Author: 
Date: 1959-09-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. On September 17, from 13:00 to 15:45, a U.S. warship intruded into the sea area of the Baiquan area of ​​Fujian Province, China. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, a spokesman for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 67th serious warning.

### 美国军舰侵入我福建地区领海  我提出第67次严重警告

1959-09-18
第2版()
专栏：

    美国军舰侵入我福建地区领海
    我提出第67次严重警告
    新华社17日讯　9月17日十三时至十五时四十五分，美国军舰一艘侵入我国福建省白犬地区海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第六十七次严重警告。
