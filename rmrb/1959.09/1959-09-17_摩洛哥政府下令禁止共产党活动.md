Title: Moroccan Government Orders Ban on Communist Activities
Author:
Date: 1959-09-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, September 14th: A Moroccan Prime Minister's spokesperson announced at a press conference tonight that in the latest issue of the government gazette, the Prime Minister has ordered the Moroccan Communist Party to cease activities from September 10, 1959. He said that the Moroccan Prime Minister took this action based on Article 19 of the King's decree of November 1958.

### 摩洛哥政府下令禁止共产党活动

1959-09-17
第6版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥政府
    下令禁止共产党活动
    据新华社拉巴特14日电　摩洛哥首相发言人今晚在记者招待会上宣布，在最近一期的政府公报中，首相已经命令摩洛哥共产党从1959年9月10日起停止活动。他说，摩洛哥首相是根据1958年11月的国王法令第十九条采取这个行动的。
