Title: Chinese Acrobatic Troupe Performs in Colombo; Bandaranaike Welcomes Sino-Sri Lankan Artistic Exchange
Author:
Date: 1959-09-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Colombo Dispatch: The Chinese Acrobatic Art Troupe held its premiere performance at the Royal College Auditorium in Colombo, the capital of Ceylon, on the evening of the 10th.

Present to watch were the Governor-General of Ceylon, Goonetilleke, Prime Minister Bandaranaike, government ministers, members of parliament, foreign diplomatic envoys, and other prominent figures.

Prime Minister Bandaranaike spoke before the performance, welcoming the Chinese Acrobatic Art Troupe's visit to Ceylon. He said that in order to promote international understanding, understanding each other's art is as important as cooperation in political, economic, and social aspects. He hoped that this visit by the Chinese Acrobatic Art Troupe would help further promote mutual understanding and understanding of each other's way of life between the two countries. Prime Minister Bandaranaike thanked the Chinese government for sending the acrobatic art troupe to visit Ceylon, and hoped that the friendship between China and Ceylon would develop, which is not only in the interests of the two countries, but also in the interests of peace.

Chinese Ambassador to Ceylon, Zhang Canming, expressed his hope in his speech that the Chinese Acrobatic Art Troupe's visit to Ceylon would further strengthen mutual understanding and friendly relations between the two countries.

Sun Chuansi, head of the Chinese Acrobatic Art Troupe, pointed out in his speech that they came to Ceylon not only to perform, but also to learn something from Ceylon's culture and art. He believed that this kind of cultural exchange is one of the best ways to promote mutual understanding between the people of the two countries.

During the intermission, the Governor-General and the Prime Minister of Ceylon received the head of the art troupe, Sun Chuansi, and the main actors in the reception room of the auditorium. At the end of the performance, Prime Minister Bandaranaike went on stage to present garlands to the head of the acrobatic art troupe and some of the actors.

### 我杂技团在科伦坡演出  班达拉奈克欢迎中锡艺术交流

1959-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    我杂技团在科伦坡演出
    班达拉奈克欢迎中锡艺术交流
    据新华社科伦坡电　中国杂技艺术团10日晚在锡兰首都科伦坡皇家学院礼堂举行首次演出。
    出席观看的有锡兰总督古涅狄莱克、总理班达拉奈克、政府部长、议员、各国外交使节和其他知名人士。
    班达拉奈克总理在演出开始前讲话，表示欢迎中国杂技艺术团访问锡兰。他说，为了促进国际谅解，了解彼此的艺术是同在政治、经济和社会等方面进行合作同样重要。他希望，中国杂技艺术团的这次访问将有助于进一步促进两国之间的相互谅解和对两国的生活方式的了解。班达拉奈克总理感谢中国政府派遣杂技艺术团访问锡兰，并且希望，中国和锡兰之间的友谊将得到发展，这不仅符合两国的利益，而且也符合和平的利益。
    中国驻锡兰大使张灿明在讲话中表示希望，中国杂技艺术团的访问锡兰将进一步加强两国之间的相互了解和友好关系。
    中国杂技艺术团团长孙川四在讲话时指出，他们到锡兰来不仅是为了演出，而且也是为了向锡兰的文化艺术学习一些东西。他相信，这种文化交流是促进两国人民相互了解的最好方式之一。
    在演出休息的时候，锡兰总督和总理在礼堂会客室接见了艺术团团长孙川四和主要演员。在演出结束时，班达拉奈克总理登上舞台为杂技艺术团团长和一些演员戴上花环。
