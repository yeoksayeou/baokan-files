Title: US Rushes Arms to Lao Government from Japan and Pacific Bases
Author:
Date: 1959-09-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Honolulu, 11th. A US official spokesman announced on the 10th that the United States is using airplanes to supply military materials to the Lao Sananikone government from its bases in Japan and other areas under the Pacific Command.

A spokesman for US Pacific Commander Felt said that some supplies are being shipped from Japan, while some supplies sent from other Pacific bases are bypassing Japan entirely and being flown directly to Laos. (Attached picture)

### 美国从日本和太平洋基地  赶运军火供应老挝政府

1959-09-12
第3版()
专栏：

    美国从日本和太平洋基地
    赶运军火供应老挝政府
    新华社11日讯　火奴鲁鲁消息：美国官方发言人10日宣布，美国正用飞机从它在日本以及太平洋司令部所属其他地区的基地向老挝萨纳尼空政府供应军事物资。
    美国太平洋司令费耳特的一个发言人说，有些供应正从日本运去，而从其他太平洋基地送出的一些供应是完全绕过日本直接用飞机运到老挝去的。（附图片）
