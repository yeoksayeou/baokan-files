Title: Czechoslovak Men's Basketball Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. The Czechoslovakian men's basketball team, invited to China to participate in the International Basketball Friendship Tournament, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

　　The basketball tournament will be attended by the Czechoslovak, Bulgarian and Chinese basketball teams. The competition will begin after National Day.

### 捷男子篮球队到京

1959-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

    捷男子篮球队到京
    新华社28日讯　应邀来我国参加国际篮球友谊赛的捷克斯洛伐克男子篮球队在今天下午乘飞机抵达北京。
    这次篮球赛将有捷克斯洛伐克、保加利亚和中国的篮球队参加。比赛将在国庆日以后开始。
