Title: Iraqi News Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: The Iraqi news delegation, invited by the All-China Journalists Association to visit China and participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of our country's founding, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.

The head of the delegation is Abdullah Abbas Ali, president and editor-in-chief of "Al-Jumhuriya," and the deputy head is Mohammed Helmi Ali Sharif, representative of "Al-Kifah." The secretary-general is Inam Abdul Hamid Abaji, editor of "Ittihad al-Sha'ab." Members of the delegation include: Salma Mahdi Douglah, editor of "Sawt al-Sha'ab," Mohammed Hassan Suri, editor-in-chief of the weekly "Al-Tamaddun," Farid Youssef Ufi, secretary-general of "Al-Akhbar," and Mufeed Jazari, reporter for "Al-Umma."

### 伊拉克新闻代表团到京

1959-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克新闻代表团到京
    新华社26日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问并参加我国建国十周年庆祝典礼的伊拉克新闻工作者代表团一行七人，今天早晨乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团团长是“国民报”社长兼主编阿卜杜拉·阿巴斯·阿里，副团长是“斗争报”代表穆罕默德·赫勒米·阿里·谢里夫，秘书长是“人民团结报”编辑伊娜姆·阿卜杜勒·哈米德·阿巴吉。代表团团员有：“人民之声报”编辑萨勒马·马赫迪·杜格拉、“文明”周刊主编默罕默德·哈桑·苏里、“消息报”秘书长法里德·尤素福·乌菲、“国家报”记者穆费德·贾扎里。
