Title: Labor People's Delegations from Poland, Romania, Hungary, and Albania Arrive in Beijing to Attend Our National Day Tenth Anniversary Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1959-09-26
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 25th - The Polish Labor People's Delegation, led by Stefan Żółkiewski, member of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and director of the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association, a group of thirteen people, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They were invited by the Sino-Polish Friendship Association, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Communist Youth League, the All-China Women's Federation, the All-China Youth Federation, the All-China Students' Federation, and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China and participate in the tenth anniversary celebrations.
　　Those who went to the airport to greet them included Shen Yanbing, Zhou Yang, Zhang Xiuzhu, Qu Tangliang, Liu Xiyuan, Hu Naiqiu, Hu Qili, Zhou Erfu, and others, who are responsible for the inviting organizations.
　　Polish Embassy in China Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, Frątczak, also went to the airport to welcome them.
　　Xinhua News Agency, September 25th - The Romanian Labor People's Delegation, headed by Mihai Marin, Secretary of the Central Council of Romanian Trade Unions, a group of thirteen people, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They were invited by the Sino-Romanian Friendship Association, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Communist Youth League, the All-China Women's Federation, the All-China Youth Federation, the All-China Students' Federation, and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China and participate in the tenth anniversary celebrations.
　　Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Li Jiebo, Shi Liang, Ding Xilin, Lu Ping, Wu Maosun, Liang Buting, Hu Qili, Wang Chuanbin, and others, who are responsible for the inviting organizations.
　　Romanian Ambassador to China, Barbu Zaharescu, also went to the airport to welcome them.
　　Xinhua News Agency, September 25th - The Hungarian Labor People's Delegation, led by Dr. Molnár Erik, Chairman of the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association, a group of fifteen people, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They were invited by the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Association, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Communist Youth League, the All-China Women's Federation, the All-China Youth Federation, the All-China Students' Federation, and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to come to China to participate in the tenth anniversary celebrations.
　　Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Qu Wu, Liu Changsheng, Luo Yi, Wu Maosun, Hu Naiqiu, Hu Qili, Wang Chuanbin, and others, who are responsible for the inviting organizations.
　　Hungarian Ambassador to China, Nogradi, also went to the airport to welcome them.
　　Xinhua News Agency, September 25th - The Albanian Labor People's Delegation, led by Koço Tashko, Chairman of the Central Control Commission of the Albanian Labor Party, a group of fifteen people, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They were invited by the Sino-Albanian Friendship Association, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Communist Youth League, the All-China Women's Federation, the All-China Youth Federation, the All-China Students' Federation, and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China and participate in the tenth anniversary celebrations.
　　Those who went to the airport to greet them included Jiang Nanxiang, Zhang Xiuzhu, Qu Tangliang, Liu Xiyuan, Hu Naiqiu, Sa Kongliao, Hu Qili, and others, who are responsible for the inviting organizations.
　　Albanian Embassy in China Second Secretary, Muhamet, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 应邀参加我国建国十周年盛典  波罗匈阿劳动人民代表团到京

1959-09-26
第2版()
专栏：

    应邀参加我国建国十周年盛典
    波罗匈阿劳动人民代表团到京
    新华社25日讯　由波兰统一工人党中央委员、波兰中国友好协会理事斯特凡·祖凯夫斯基率领的波兰劳动人民代表团一行十三人，今天乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中波友协、全总、和大、共青团、全国妇联、全国青联、全国学联、对外文协的邀请，来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
    前往机场迎接的有各邀请团体的负责人沈雁冰、周扬、张修竹、区棠亮、刘西元、胡耐秋、胡启立、周而复等。
    波兰驻华大使馆临时代办傅拉托也到机场欢迎。
    新华社25日讯　以罗马尼亚工会中央理事会书记米海依·马林为首的罗马尼亚劳动人民代表团一行十三人今天下午乘飞机到达北京。他们应中罗友协、全总、和大、共青团、全国妇联、全国青联、全国学联、对外文协的邀请，前来我国访问并参加建国十周年的庆祝典礼。
    前往机场欢迎的有各邀请团体的负责人李颉伯、史良、丁西林、陆平、吴茂荪、梁步庭、胡启立、王传斌等。
    罗马尼亚驻华大使巴尔布·查哈勒斯库也到机场欢迎。
    新华社25日讯　由匈牙利—中国友好协会主席莫尔纳尔·埃里克博士率领的匈牙利劳动人民代表团一行十五人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中匈友协、全总、和大、共青团、全国妇联、全国青联、全国学联、对外文协的邀请，前来我国参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
    前往机场欢迎的有各邀请团体的负责人屈武、刘长胜、罗毅、吴茂荪、胡耐秋、胡启立、王传斌等。
    匈牙利驻华大使诺格拉第也到机场欢迎。
    新华社25日讯　由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央检查委员会主席科乔·塔什科率领的阿尔巴尼亚劳动人民代表团一行十五人，今天乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中阿友协、全总、和大、共青团、全国妇联、全国青联、全国学联、对外文协的邀请，来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的。
    前往机场迎接的有各邀请团体的负责人蒋南翔、张修竹、区棠亮、刘西元、胡耐秋、萨空了、胡启立等。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使馆二等秘书穆罕默德也到机场欢迎。
