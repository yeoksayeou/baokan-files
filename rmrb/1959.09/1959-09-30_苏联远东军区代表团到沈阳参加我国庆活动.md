Title: Soviet Far Eastern Military District Delegation Arrives in Shenyang to Participate in China's National Day Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 29th. At the invitation of the leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Shenyang, a delegation from the Soviet Far Eastern Military District, consisting of five members led by Lieutenant General Aleksey Ivanovich Shmilev, Military Commissar of the Soviet Far Eastern Military District, arrived in Shenyang by plane on the afternoon of the 28th to visit Shenyang and participate in the celebrations for the tenth anniversary of the founding of our country.

### 苏联远东军区代表团到沈阳参加我国庆活动

1959-09-30
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联远东军区代表团到沈阳参加我国庆活动
    新华社沈阳29日电　应中国人民解放军沈阳驻军首长邀请，前来沈阳访问并参加我国建国十周年庆祝活动的苏联远东军区代表团一行五人，在团长苏联远东军区军事委员阿列克谢·伊凡诺维奇·什米廖夫中将率领下，28日下午乘飞机到达沈阳。
