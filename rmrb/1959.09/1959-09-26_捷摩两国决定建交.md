Title: Czechoslovakia and Morocco Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1959-09-26
Page: 9

    Czechoslovakia and Morocco Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations
    Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, September 24th: Moroccan radio announced this afternoon that the Moroccan government has decided to establish diplomatic relations with Czechoslovakia, and the two countries will send diplomatic representatives at the ambassadorial level to each other.
    Denek Pravda, the First Secretary of the soon-to-be-established Czechoslovakian Embassy in Morocco, has arrived in Rabat to prepare for the establishment of the embassy.

### 捷摩两国决定建交

1959-09-26
第9版()
专栏：

    捷摩两国决定建交
    新华社拉巴特24日电　摩洛哥电台今天下午宣布，摩洛哥政府已经决定同捷克斯洛伐克建立外交关系，两国将互派大使级的外交代表。
    即将设立的捷克斯洛伐克驻摩洛哥大使馆一等秘书德内克·普拉夫达已经到达拉巴特筹建大使馆。
