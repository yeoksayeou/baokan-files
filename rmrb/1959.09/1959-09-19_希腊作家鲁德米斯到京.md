Title: Greek Writer Roudemis Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: Greek writer Menelaos Roudemis arrived in Beijing by train at 5 p.m. on the 17th, at invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Chinese Writers Association.
    Yang Shuo, member of the Council of the Chinese Writers Association and deputy director of the Foreign Literature Committee, and others welcomed him at the station.

### 希腊作家鲁德米斯到京

1959-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    希腊作家鲁德米斯到京
    新华社18日讯　应我国对外文化协会、作家协会邀请来华访问的希腊作家梅内劳斯·鲁德米斯在17日下午五时乘火车抵北京。
    到车站欢迎的有中国作家协会理事、外国文学委员会副主任杨朔等。
