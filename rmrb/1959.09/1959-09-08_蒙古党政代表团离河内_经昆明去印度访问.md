Title: Mongolian Party and Government Delegation Leaves Hanoi to Visit India via Kunming
Author:
Date: 1959-09-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 7th - After a week-long friendly visit to Vietnam, the Mongolian Party and Government Delegation, headed by Chairman Tsedenbal, left Hanoi by plane this morning to visit India via Kunming.

Pham Van Dong, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labor Party and Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, other Vietnamese party and government leaders, and tens of thousands of Hanoi residents saw off the distinguished Mongolian guests at Gia Lam Airport.

Those who saw off the distinguished guests at the airport today included Truong Chinh, Vo Nguyen Giap, Phan Ke Toai, and Pham Hung, Deputy Prime Ministers of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly; Hoang Van Hoan, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labor Party; and government ministers. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Vietnam and Indian Consul-General to Vietnam, Maini Sis, also went to the airport to see off the delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 7th - Tsedenbal, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, and the government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic he led, arrived in Kunming by special plane from Hanoi this afternoon on their way to visit India.

### 蒙古党政代表团离河内  经昆明去印度访问

1959-09-08
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙古党政代表团离河内
    经昆明去印度访问
    据新华社河内7日电　以泽登巴尔主席为首的蒙古党政代表团在越南作了为期一周的友好访问后，今天上午乘飞机离开河内，取道昆明前往印度访问。
    越南劳动党中央政治局委员、越南民主共和国总理范文同和越南其他党政领导人以及成千上万的河内群众在嘉林机场欢送蒙古贵宾。
    今天到机场欢送贵宾的还有越南民主共和国副总理长征、武元甲、潘继遂、范雄，国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜，越南劳动党中央政治局委员黄文欢，政府部长们。各国驻越南的外交使节和印度驻越南总领事麦尼西斯也到机场欢送代表团。
    据新华社昆明7日电　蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔和他率领的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团，今天下午在前往印度访问途中，由河内乘专机到达昆明。
