Title: Mongolian "Truth News" and British Communist "World News" Publish Documents of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the CPC
Author:
Date: 1959-09-04
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 3rd. The Mongolian "Truth News" today published the full text of the "Resolution on Launching the Production Increase and Thrift Movement" of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on the fourth page.

Xinhua News Agency, London, 2nd. The British Communist Party's "World News" weekly published the full text of the communique of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China in its latest issue.

### 蒙古“真理报”和英共“世界新闻”  刊载中共八届八中全会文件

1959-09-04
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古“真理报”和英共“世界新闻”
    刊载中共八届八中全会文件
    新华社乌兰巴托3日电　蒙古“真理报”今天在第四版全文刊登了中国共产党八届八中全会关于开展增产节约运动的决议。
    新华社伦敦2日电　英国共产党的“世界新闻”周刊在最近出版的一期上，刊载了中国共产党八届八中全会的公报全文。
