Title: Premier Zhou Holds Reception to Welcome Ishibashi

Author: 
Date: 1959-09-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Premier Zhou Enlai held a reception this evening to welcome former Japanese Prime Minister Tanzan Ishibashi, his wife, and his entourage, who are visiting China.

In his speech, Premier Zhou Enlai said, "We know that Mr. Tanzan Ishibashi has the desire to promote friendship between the Chinese and Japanese people, improve relations between China and Japan, and maintain world peace, especially peace in the Far East. This desire is not only the desire of the Japanese people but also the desire of the Chinese people and the Chinese government. Therefore, we are very pleased to invite Mr. Tanzan Ishibashi, his wife, and his entourage to visit China, and we would like to take this opportunity to express our welcome."

Premier Zhou Enlai said, "I believe that during Mr. Ishibashi's stay in Beijing, we will have the opportunity to hold sincere and frank discussions to promote the realization of the wishes of the people of China and Japan."

In his speech, Mr. Tanzan Ishibashi said, "My entourage and I are visiting China to strengthen the friendship between the peoples of Japan and China and to make our contribution to Japan-China friendship and peace." He also expressed his hope to discuss ways to promote friendly relations between Japan and China during his stay in China.

Among those present at the reception today were Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, as well as Guo Moruo, Liao Chengzhi, Li Dequan, Liu Ningyi, Zhang Hanfu, Lei Renmin, Wang Zhen, Hu Yuzhi, Cai Tingkai, Xu Guangping, Wang Kunlun, Yang Hansheng, Li Zhuchen, Ouyang Yuqian, Hou Debang, Zhao Puchu, Xie Bingxin, Xie Nanguang, Zhao Anbo, and Wu Maosun, and people from various fields.

Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji and his wife, who are in Beijing, were invited to attend the reception. (Photo attached)

### 周总理举行酒会招待石桥

1959-09-11
第3版()
专栏：

    周总理举行酒会招待石桥
    新华社10日讯　周恩来总理今天晚上举行酒会，招待前来我国访问的日本前首相石桥湛山和他的夫人以及随行人员。
    周恩来总理在致词中说：“我们知道，石桥湛山先生具有促进中日两国人民友好、改善中日两国关系和维护世界和平特别是远东和平的愿望。这样的愿望，不仅是日本人民的愿望，而且也是中国人民和中国政府的愿望。因此，我们很高兴邀请石桥湛山先生和他的夫人以及随行人员来中国访问，我们愿借此机会表示欢迎。”
    周恩来总理说：“我相信，当石桥先生在北京逗留期间，我们将有机会进行恳切而坦率的商谈，来促进中日两国人民的愿望的实现。”
    石桥湛山先生在致词中说：“我和我的随行人员是为了加强日中两国人民的友谊，为了使我们对日中友好和和平有所贡献而来中国访问的。”他还表示希望在中国逗留期间商谈关于促进日中两国友好关系的方法。
    出席今天酒会的，有国务院副总理陈毅，以及郭沫若、廖承志、李德全、刘宁一、章汉夫、雷任民、王震、胡愈之、蔡廷锴、许广平、王昆仑、阳翰笙、李烛尘、欧阳予倩、侯德榜、赵朴初、谢冰心、谢南光、赵安博、吴茂荪等各方面的人士。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一和他的夫人应邀出席了酒会。（附图片）
