Title: Guangdong Cantonese Opera Troupe Leaves Pyongyang for Home

Author:

Date: 1959-09-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang. The Guangdong Cantonese Opera Troupe, which was invited to visit Korea under the 1959 Sino-Korean cultural cooperation plan, left Pyongyang by train on the evening of the 16th to return home after a month-long visit and performances in various parts of Korea.

The Korean Minister of Education and Culture, Li Il-kyung, held a banquet on the evening of the 15th to bid farewell to the Cantonese Opera Troupe. The Cantonese Opera Troupe will also pay a two-day visit and performance in Sinuiju on its way home.

The Guangdong Cantonese Opera Troupe, led by famous Cantonese opera actors Ma Shizeng and Hong Xiannv, visited Pyongyang, Hamhung, Hungnam, Wonsan, Kaesong and other places during its stay in Korea, performing a total of twenty-eight shows. The repertoire they performed included "Guan Hanqing", "Searching the Academy", "Zhao Jun's Departure from the塞", and "Baishuitan".

### 广东粤剧院离平壤回国

1959-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    广东粤剧院离平壤回国
    据新华社平壤电　根据1959年中朝文化合作计划应邀来朝鲜访问的广东粤剧院，在朝鲜各地进行一个多月的访问和演出以后，16日晚乘火车离平壤回国。
    朝鲜教育文化相李一卿15日晚上曾举行宴会为粤剧院饯行。粤剧院在回国途中还将在新义州进行两天的访问演出。
    由著名粤剧演员马师曾和红线女率领的广东粤剧院在朝鲜期间，先后在平壤、咸兴、兴南、元山、开城等地访问，共演出了二十八场。他们演出的剧目“关汉卿”、“搜书院”、“昭君出塞”、“白水滩”等。
