Title: Telegram of Greetings from the Nicaraguan Socialist Party
Author:
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 9

Telegram of Greetings from the Nicaraguan Socialist Party

To the Comrades of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China:

    For the oppressed people of Latin America, including the Nicaraguan people, the anti-colonial struggle waged by the Chinese people under the leadership of the Communist Party has both educational and inspiring significance. The victory of the Chinese people over anti-national forces at home and abroad vividly demonstrates that ultimate victory always belongs to those who fight for the cause of the majority. Just as in China in the past, the clutches of those anti-national forces continue to grip the people of Latin American countries.

    Currently, Nicaragua is living under the terror of the most anti-popular regimes, regimes established by American imperialism in these regions to serve its interests. The Nicaraguan Socialist Party, as an inspirer of the united front, fights against a political group that sells out national interests and barbarously deprives the most basic human rights with the support of warlords. Thus, because the proletariat, freedom, and world peace have only one enemy, the Nicaraguan people and the Chinese people are united and celebrate the victory of the Chinese people as if celebrating the victory of the oppressed class.

    On the tenth anniversary of China's liberation, the Nicaraguan Socialist Party extends warm congratulations to the fraternal Communist Party of China, which it has always regarded as a model of efficiency and decisive action in political line.

    The proletariat of Nicaragua congratulates the fraternal Chinese people and, taking the opportunity to celebrate the tenth anniversary of their victory, wishes them smooth sailing in the great task of building a new China.

    Long live the Communist Party of China!
    Long live the People's Republic of China!
    Long live the cause of socialism!

### 尼加拉瓜社会主义党的贺信

1959-09-30
第9版()
专栏：

    尼加拉瓜社会主义党的贺信中国共产党中央委员会的同志们：
    对于拉丁美洲被压迫人民，其中也包括尼加拉瓜人民，中国人民在共产党领导下所进行的反殖民斗争具有教育和鼓舞的双重意义。中国人民对国内外反民族的力量的胜利生动地表明最终胜利总是属于为大多数人的事业而斗争的人。正和过去在中国一样，那些反民族力量的魔爪现在还继续攫住拉丁美洲各国人民。
    目前，尼加拉瓜生活在最反人民的政权的恐怖统治下，这些最反人民的政权是美帝国主义在这些地区为它的利益服务而建立起来的。尼加拉瓜社会主义党，作为一个统一战线的鼓舞者，为反对一个在军阀的支持下出卖民族利益，野蛮地剥夺最基本的人权的政治集团而斗争。这样，由于无产阶级、自由和世界和平的敌人只有一个，尼加拉瓜人民和中国人民是一致的，并且庆祝中国人民的胜利就像庆祝被压迫阶级的胜利一样。
    在中国取得解放十周年的时候，尼加拉瓜社会主义党向兄弟的中国共产党表示热烈的祝贺。它一直把中国共产党看作组成政治路线上的效率，果断的行动的榜样。
    尼加拉瓜的无产阶级向兄弟的中国人民祝贺，并借庆祝他们胜利十周年的机会，预祝他们建设新中国伟大的任务一帆风顺。
    中国共产党万岁！
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    社会主义事业万岁！
