Title: Telegram of Greetings from the Central Committee of the Portuguese Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1959-09-30
Page: 8

To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China,
Dear Comrades!

    On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Central Committee of the Portuguese Communist Party extends warm congratulations to you and, through you, to all the Chinese Communists and working people.

    The victory of the Chinese People's Revolution, achieved under the firm leadership of the heroic Communist Party of China headed by our dear Comrade Mao Zedong, is a huge and heavy blow to the world's reactionaries and imperialist forces, and supports the struggle of the peoples of all countries still under oppression for freedom, independence, peace and socialism.

    For us Portuguese Communists, the victories achieved by the Chinese Communists and the Chinese people in building a new life are a new inspiration to our thirty-three years of arduous struggle against fascism and for democracy under extremely difficult underground conditions.

    The consolidation of the socialist camp headed by the great Soviet Union, and the unity of thought and action of the international communist movement, are guarantees for the working class and all working people in their struggle for better living conditions, for democracy, peace and socialism to achieve new victories.

    Dear Comrades!

    The festival of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China is also our festival, and it is also the festival of all people who pursue progress and love peace.

    The Portuguese Communists, together with the vast majority of the Portuguese people, respectfully wish you new and greater achievements in fulfilling the great task of China's socialist construction.

    Long live the People's Republic of China!

    Long live the great and hardworking Chinese people!

    Long live the Communist Party of China!

    Long live Comrade Mao Zedong!

    Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Portuguese people!

    Long live peace!

                The Central Committee of the Portuguese Communist Party, September 1959, in Portugal

### 葡萄牙共产党中央委员会的贺信

1959-09-30
第8版()
专栏：

    葡萄牙共产党中央委员会的贺信致中国共产党中央委员会亲爱的同志们！
    值此中华人民共和国成立十周年之际，葡萄牙共产党中央委员会向你们并通过你们向全体中国共产党人和劳动人民致以热烈的祝贺。
    在以亲爱的毛泽东同志为首的英勇的中国共产党坚定的领导下取得的中国人民革命的胜利，是对世界反动势力和帝国主义势力的一个巨大而沉重的打击，支援了仍然受压迫的各国人民的争取自由、独立、和平和社会主义的斗争。
    对于我们葡萄牙共产党人来说，中国共产党人和中国人民在建设新生活中所取得的胜利，是对我们三十三年来在极恶劣的地下活动条件下所进行的反法西斯和争取民主的艰苦斗争的新的鼓舞。
    以伟大的苏联为首的社会主义阵营的巩固，国际共产主义运动的思想和行动的统一，是争取更好的生活条件，争取民主、和平和社会主义的工人阶级以及一切劳动人民取得新的胜利的保证。
    亲爱的同志们！
    中华人民共和国成立十周年的节日也是我们的节日，也是一切追求进步和爱好和平的人们的节日。
    葡萄牙共产党人，和他们一起的还有绝大多数的葡萄牙人民，敬祝你们在实现中国社会主义建设的伟大任务中获得新的更大的成就。
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    伟大而勤劳的中国人民万岁！
    中国共产党万岁！
    毛泽东同志万寿无疆！
    中葡人民的友谊万岁！
    和平万岁！
                葡萄牙共产党中央委员会　1959年9月于葡萄牙
