Title: Soviet Ballet Troupe's Trip Scheduled to Participate in China's National Day Celebrations; Strong Cast of Performers; Famous Orchestra to Accompany

Author:
Date: 1959-09-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 4th: TASS reports that the Bolshoi Theatre Ballet, headed by the famous actress Ulanova, will depart for China on the 22nd of this month to participate in the celebrations of the great tenth anniversary of China's National Day. The cast for this performance tour will be even stronger than any previous foreign tour by this group.

Among those coming to China for this performance tour are the famous ballet dancers Ulanova, Plisetskaya, Lepeshinskaya, Struchkova, Koren, Levashov, Fadeyechev, Riabinkina, and Sergeyev.

In addition to the unprecedentedly strong cast of performers, the Bolshoi Theatre's orchestra will also be coming, with the famous conductors Fayer, Rozhdestvensky, and Svetlanov taking turns conducting.

After arriving in China, the Bolshoi Theatre Ballet and Orchestra will perform in Beijing, Shanghai, Wuhan, and Guangzhou for a fifty-day tour.

### 参加我国庆庆祝活动  苏联芭蕾舞蹈团行期已定  演员阵容强大　著名乐队同来

1959-09-05
第2版()
专栏：

    参加我国庆庆祝活动
    苏联芭蕾舞蹈团行期已定
    演员阵容强大　著名乐队同来
    据新华社4日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科消息：以著名演员乌兰诺娃为首的苏联大剧院芭蕾舞蹈团将于本月22日启程来我国访问，参加中国伟大的国庆十周年的庆祝活动。这次访问演出的演员阵容将比这个团体任何一次国外演出的阵容都要强大。
    这次前来我国访问的有，著名芭蕾舞演员乌兰诺娃、普利谢茨卡娅、列皮辛斯卡娅、斯特鲁奇科娃、科列尼、列瓦绍夫、法捷耶契夫、里亚宾基娜和谢尔盖耶夫等。
    这次访问演出的特点，除了演员阵容空前强大之外，大剧院的乐队也将同时前来，指挥由著名指挥家法耶尔、罗日杰斯特文斯基和斯维特拉诺夫担任。
    大剧院芭蕾舞蹈团和乐队来到中国后，将在北京、上海、武汉、广州作为期五十天的访问演出。
