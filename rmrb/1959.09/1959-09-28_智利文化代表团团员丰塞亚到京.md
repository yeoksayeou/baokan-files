Title: Chilean Cultural Delegation Member Fonseca Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: Pedro Fonseca, a member of the Chilean Cultural Delegation who was invited by the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries to visit China and attend the celebrations for the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 26th.

### 智利文化代表团团员丰塞亚到京

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    智利文化代表团团员丰塞亚到京
    新华社27日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问并参加建国十周年庆祝典礼的智利文化代表团团员佩德罗·丰塞亚，于26日下午乘飞机到达北京。
