Title: Ambassador Nogradi, on behalf of Premier Münnich, presents a truck to the Huangtugang Commune
Author:
Date: 1959-09-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: Ambassador Nogradi of Hungary presented a truck to the Huangtugang People's Commune today on behalf of Premier Münnich of the Hungarian Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government.

At the presentation ceremony, when Ambassador Nogradi handed the truck license to Yin Weichen, the director of the commune, they warmly embraced for the great friendship between the Chinese and Hungarian people. Ambassador Nogradi said in his speech that the People's Commune has become the pillar of socialist construction in the Chinese countryside, and this is a powerful force of the entire socialist camp. He wished the People's Commune further development and consolidation, and wished the friendship between the Chinese and Hungarian people would last forever.

Ambassador Nogradi also visited the commune's public canteen, kindergarten and flower production team. Accompanying him on the visit were Jia Tingsan, Standing Committee Member of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 诺格拉第大使代表明尼赫总理  赠送黄土岗公社一辆载重汽车

1959-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    诺格拉第大使代表明尼赫总理
    赠送黄土岗公社一辆载重汽车
    新华社24日讯　匈牙利驻中国大使诺格拉第今天代表匈牙利工农革命政府明尼赫总理赠送给黄土岗人民公社一辆载重汽车。
    在赠礼仪式上，当诺格拉第大使把汽车执照交给社主任殷维臣的时候，他们为中匈人民的伟大友谊热情地拥抱。诺格拉第大使致词说，人民公社已经成为中国农村社会主义建设的支柱，这是整个社会主义阵营的一个强大的力量。他祝人民公社进一步发展和巩固，祝中匈人民的友谊万古常青。
    诺格拉第大使还参观了公社的公共食堂、幼儿园和鲜花生产队。陪同他参观的有中共北京市委常委、北京市副市长贾庭三和外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清等。
