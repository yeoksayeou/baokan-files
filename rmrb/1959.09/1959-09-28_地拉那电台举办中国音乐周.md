Title: Tirana Radio to Hold Chinese Music Week
Author:
Date: 1959-09-28
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, September 26th. To celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of our country, Tirana Radio will hold a Chinese Music Week from the 25th to October 1st. During the Music Week, Chinese music will be systematically introduced, and folk music and choruses will be broadcast.

    At the opening ceremony held on the evening of the 25th, Tirana Radio specially invited Gala Avkazi, leader of the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe who had visited China, to make a report on the achievements of Chinese music in the past ten years. During the Music Week, Tirana Radio will also organize special programs to introduce the achievements China has made in socialist construction over the past ten years, and will invite Luo Shishang, the Chinese Ambassador to Albania, to give a radio speech to the radio audience about China.

### 地拉那电台举办中国音乐周

1959-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    地拉那电台举办中国音乐周
    新华社地拉那26日电　为庆祝我国建国十周年，地拉那广播电台从25日到10月1日举办中国音乐周。音乐周期间将系统地介绍中国的音乐，播送民间音乐和大合唱等。
    在25日晚举行的开幕式上，地拉那广播电台特别邀请了曾经访问过中国的阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团领导人加拉·阿夫卡齐作了十年来中国音乐成就的报告。在音乐周期间，地拉那广播电台还将组织特别节目，介绍中国十年来在社会主义建设中所取得的成就，并将邀请中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士商向电台听众作关于介绍中国的广播演说。
