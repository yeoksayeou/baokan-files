Title: Beijing Botanical Garden Gifts Plants to Pyongyang Botanical Garden
Author:
Date: 1959-09-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 3rd. Mao Dafeng, the cultural attache of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea, today represented the Beijing Botanical Garden in presenting 73 kinds of plants to the Pyongyang Botanical Garden in Pyongyang. Han Shijin, Vice Chairman of the Pyongyang Municipal People's Committee, accepted the plants on behalf of the Korean side.

The plants donated by China totaled more than 120 pots, including Chinese tea trees, spice plants, Malus spectabilis, and Codiaeum variegatum. In addition, there were seeds of 90 kinds of plants.

### 北京植物园赠送平壤植物园一批植物

1959-09-05
第2版()
专栏：

    北京植物园赠送平壤植物园一批植物
    新华社平壤3日电　中国驻朝鲜大使馆文化专员毛大凤今天在平壤代表北京植物园把七十三种植物赠送给平壤植物园。平壤市人民委员会副委员长韩石镇代表朝鲜方面接受了这批植物。
    中国赠送的植物共有一百二十多盆，其中包括中国茶树、香料植物、金丝海棠、电叶木等。另外，还有九十种植物的种子。
