Title: Athletes from All Nationalities Gather in the Nation's Capital; First National Games to Open Today
Author:
Date: 1959-09-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 12th: The grand opening of the First National Games of the People's Republic of China will be held on the afternoon of the 13th at the newly built Beijing Workers' Stadium, which can accommodate 80,000 spectators. This is the largest review of the development of sports in our country in the ten years since the founding of the nation.

More than 10,600 athletes from all nationalities will participate in 42 competitions and exhibition matches at this National Games, with over 4,000 matches and exhibitions. Before the opening of the National Games, some events had already ended or started early in Wuhan, Qingdao, Hohhot, Tianjin, Beijing, and other places, but most events will take place after the opening and will continue until September 27th.

The capital is the central hub of this National Games. It has gathered 7,700 athletes from all over the country and the People's Liberation Army, including workers, farmers, herdsmen, students, teachers, cadres, medical workers, and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army, representing more than 20 nationalities. Among the athletes are 640 master athletes and more than 2,700 first-class athletes.

The National Games will also receive sports delegations from socialist countries such as the Soviet Union, North Korea, and Vietnam, as well as sports figures from countries such as France, Iraq, and Sudan. Delegations and sports figures from these countries will participate in the opening ceremony on the 13th. (Detailed report on page 6)

### 各民族男女运动健儿云集祖国首都  首届全国运动会今日开幕

1959-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    各民族男女运动健儿云集祖国首都
    首届全国运动会今日开幕
    据新华社12日讯　规模盛大的中华人民共和国第一届运动会，13日下午将在新近落成的、可容八万观众的北京工人体育场正式开幕。这是我国建国十年来发展体育运动事业的最大一次检阅。
    一万零六百多名各民族运动员在这次运动会上进行四十二项竞赛和表演赛，比赛和表演赛将达四千多场次。在全运会开幕以前，部分项目已分别在武汉、青岛、呼和浩特、天津、北京等地提前结束或提前开始，但是绝大部分项目都将在开幕以后进行。一直要进行到9月27日。
    首都是这次全运会的中枢所在。这里汇集了来自祖国各地和人民解放军的男女运动员共七千七百多人，有工人、农牧民、学生、教师、干部、医务工作者和人民解放军战士，共包括二十多个民族。运动员中有六百四十名运动健将和两千七百多名一级运动员。
    全运会还将接待来自苏联、朝鲜、越南等社会主义国家的体育代表团和法国、伊拉克、苏丹等国的体育界人士。这些国家的代表团和体育界人士将参加13日的开幕式。（详细报道见第六版）
