Title: Sino-Afghan Joint Communiqué Signing Ceremony Held in Beijing; Deputy Prime Minister Naim Visits Wuhan After Visiting Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1959-09-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 14th. The signing ceremony of the Joint Communiqué between the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Afghanistan was held in Beijing on the morning of September 9th. Premier Zhou Enlai attended the signing ceremony.

Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi and Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Naim signed the joint communiqué on behalf of their respective governments. After the signing, the hosts and guests warmly shook hands and congratulated each other.

On the Chinese side, those who attended the signing ceremony included Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Ji Zhuang, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Ji Pengfei, and Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan Hao Ting.

On the Afghan side, those who attended the signing ceremony included Minister of Commerce Ghulam Mohammad Sherzad, Afghan Ambassador to China Abdul Samad, and Director of the Economic Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Sardar Charman Mahmoud Ghazi.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, September 13th. Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Sardar Mohammad Naim, and his entourage, visited Wuhan today.

This afternoon, accompanied by Zhang Ti Xue, Governor of Hubei Province, and Chen Li, Deputy Governor, the distinguished guests drove to the Wuhan Heavy Machine Tool Plant, where they were warmly welcomed by Sun Yi, Deputy Director of the plant, and others. The distinguished guests visited the First Machining Workshop, the Foundry Workshop, the Second Machining Workshop, and the Forging Workshop. After the visit, Prince Naim attributed the early start of production at the plant to the result of high speed and high efficiency in his inscription. Then the distinguished guests visited the coking plant of the Wuhan Iron and Steel Corporation, blast furnaces No. 1 and No. 2, and the open-hearth furnace construction project, which is under intense construction. The distinguished guests asked many questions about the construction speed and technical issues, to which Li Yiqing, the general manager of the company, answered one by one. The Prince also wrote an inscription for the Wuhan Iron and Steel Corporation.

Some of the distinguished guests also visited the animal husbandry farm of the Central China Agricultural College this afternoon to learn about the experience of livestock breeding and prevention and treatment of animal diseases.

In the evening, the distinguished guests drove to the Yangtze River Bridge to view the night scenery of the bridge and the three towns of Wuhan connected by it.

Tonight, Zhang Ti Xue, Governor of Hubei Province, hosted a banquet to entertain Prince Naim and all the distinguished Afghan guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, September 13th. Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Sardar Mohammad Naim, and his entourage, arrived in Wuhan this morning from Hangzhou by special plane. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan Hao Ting, and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Ma Zhenwu accompanied the distinguished guests.

Afghan Ambassador to China Samad also accompanied Prince Naim to Wuhan.

The national flags of China and Afghanistan were flying high at Wuhan Airport today. When the distinguished Afghan guests stepped off the plane, they were warmly welcomed by Zhang Ti Xue, Governor of Hubei Province, Chen Li, Deputy Governor, Liu Hui Nong, Mayor of Wuhan, Hua Yuqing, Deputy Mayor, Cai Shubin, Vice Chairman of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the CPPCC, and Zhang Guangcai, Major General of the Wuhan Garrison. ### 中国阿富汗联合公报签字仪式在京举行  纳伊姆副首相从杭州到武汉参观访问

1959-09-14
第2版()
专栏：

    中国阿富汗联合公报签字仪式在京举行
    纳伊姆副首相从杭州到武汉参观访问
    新华社14日讯　中华人民共和国和阿富汗王国联合公报的签字仪式，9月9日上午在北京举行。周恩来总理参加了签字仪式。
    陈毅副总理兼外交部长和纳伊姆副首相兼外交大臣分别代表两国政府在联合公报上签字。签字以后，宾主热烈握手，互相祝贺。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，有对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长章汉夫、姬鹏飞，中国驻阿富汗大使郝汀等。
    阿富汗方面参加签字仪式的，有商业大臣吴拉姆·穆罕默德·舍尔扎德，阿富汗驻中国大使阿卜杜勒·萨马德，外交部经济处处长萨达尔·查尔曼·马哈茂德·加齐等。
    新华社武汉13日电　阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆亲王和他的随行人员，今天在武汉参观访问。
    下午，贵宾们在湖北省省长张体学、副省长陈离陪同下，驱车到武汉重型机床厂参观时，受到这个厂副厂长孙毅等的热情欢迎。贵宾们参观了第一加工车间、铸造车间、第二加工车间和锻造车间。纳伊姆亲王参观以后在题词中，把这个厂提前投入生产归之于高速度、高效率的结果。接着贵宾们到武钢参观了焦化厂，一号、二号高炉和正在紧张施工的平炉基建工程。贵宾们询问了很多有关建设速度及技术问题，公司总经理李一清一一作了答复。亲王并为武钢题了词。
    部分贵宾今天下午还参观访问了华中农学院的畜牧场，了解了畜类饲养、兽病防治等经验。
    晚上，贵宾们驱车到长江大桥，观赏这座桥梁和被它连接起来的武汉三镇的夜景。
    今晚湖北省省长张体学设宴招待纳伊姆亲王和阿富汗的全体贵宾。
    新华社武汉13日电　阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆亲王和他的随行人员，今天上午由杭州乘专机到达武汉。我外交部副部长姬鹏飞、中国驻阿富汗大使郝汀和外交部礼宾司副司长马振武陪同贵宾前来。
    阿富汗王国驻华大使萨马德也陪同纳伊姆亲王前来武汉。
    武汉机场今天高悬中阿两国国旗。阿富汗贵宾走下飞机时，受到湖北省省长张体学、副省长陈离，武汉市市长刘惠农、副市长华煜卿，政协湖北省委员会副主席蔡书彬及武汉驻军首长张广才少将等人的热烈欢迎。（附图片）
