Title: In Lai Châu and Điện Biên Phủ Areas, Vietnamese People's Army Annihilates Over a Thousand Enemy Troops
Author:
Date: 1953-12-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd: A communiqué released by the Vietnam News Agency on the 21st stated that in the eight days from the liberation of Lai Châu Province's capital on December 11th to December 18th, the Vietnamese People's Army annihilated six enemy companies and routed another five companies in the Lai Châu and Điện Biên Phủ areas, totaling the annihilation and routing of 1,300 enemy troops.
    Another communiqué released on the 21st stated that the enemy army dispatched two battalions from Điện Biên Phủ to rescue the remnant enemies fleeing from Lai Châu. These reinforcements were intercepted by the Vietnamese People's Army on December 14th and driven back. Two enemy companies belonging to these reinforcements were annihilated on the Phú Xa hill, 14 kilometers north of Điện Biên Phủ. Simultaneously, in Cham Nua, north of Lai Châu Province's capital, and in Mong Tong to the south, two platoons of enemy troops ran out of their hiding places on December 15th to surrender to the People's Army, handing over all their weapons.

### 在莱州和奠边府地区  越南人民军歼敌千余

1953-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

    在莱州和奠边府地区
    越南人民军歼敌千余
    【新华社二十二日讯】据越南通讯社讯：二十一日发表的公报说，从十二月十一日莱州省省会解放到十二月十八日这八天中，越南人民军在莱州和奠边府地区共歼灭敌军六个连，击溃另外五个连，共计歼灭和击溃敌军一千三百名。
    二十一日发表的另一公报说，敌军从奠边府派了两个营来援救自莱州逃跑的残敌。这些援军已在十二月十四日受到越南人民军的截击，并被赶了回去。属于这支援军的两连敌军在奠边府以北十四公里的富山高地被歼灭；同时，在莱州省会以北的占诺和以南的孟董，又有两排敌军于十二月十五日跑出掩蔽处所向人民军投降，并交出全部武器。
