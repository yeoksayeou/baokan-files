Title: Inaugural National Conference of the India-China Friendship Association Grandly Held
Author:
Date: 1953-12-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 15th: The first national conference of the India-China Friendship Association was held from the 11th to the 13th. The opening ceremony took place on the afternoon of the 11th at the Constitution Club Hall in New Delhi, in a warm, solemn, and grand atmosphere.

Approximately one thousand people attended the opening ceremony. As the delegation of the China-India Friendship Association walked through the central aisle of the hall, all attendees stood up and welcomed the delegation with prolonged cheers and applause, shouting "Long Live India-China Friendship!"

Mrs. Gunawardene, Chairwoman of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association, came specially from Ceylon to attend the conference and also received a warm welcome.

About 154 delegates from all parts of India attended the conference. In addition, prominent figures who attended the opening ceremony included: Mr. Ananthasayanam Ayyangar, Deputy Speaker of the People's House of the Indian Parliament; Mr. Gurmukh Nihal Singh, Speaker of the Delhi State Legislative Assembly; Chaudhry Brahm Prakash, Chief Minister of the Delhi State Government; and Mr. Shah Nawaz Khan, Indian Member of Parliament and Deputy Minister of Railways and Transport of the Indian Government. Personnel from the Chinese Embassy in India and representatives from the Soviet, Czechoslovakian, and Hungarian embassies in India also attended the opening ceremony.

Dr. Gyan Chand, convener of the preparatory committee for the India-China Friendship Association National Conference, announced the list of the presidium, which included: Pandit Sundarlal, former leader of the Indian goodwill delegation to China; Dr. Saifuddin Kitchlew, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council; Major General Sahib Singh Sokhey, Indian Member of Parliament; and Mrs. Hannah Sen, President of the All-India Women's Conference.

Dr. Gyan Chand also invited the delegation of the China-India Friendship Association, led by Ding Xilin, and the Chinese Ambassador to India, Yuan Zhongxian, to take seats on the podium.

Mrs. Hannah Sen, Chairwoman of the conference, declared the conference open and welcomed the delegation of the China-India Friendship Association led by Ding Xilin. Mrs. Sen said that the deep and traditional cultural understanding between India and China is a major characteristic shared by both countries. She said that the people of India and China have good reason to be rightly proud of this history.

Mr. K. Khalilkar, Member of the Council of States of the Indian Parliament, in his opening address, called for further strengthening the cultural ties between India and China.

Chinese Ambassador Yuan Zhongxian was also invited to address the conference. He said, "The development of friendship between the Chinese and Indian people is conducive to peace in Asia and the world. The Chinese people, like the Indian people, have always attached great importance to and cherished efforts in this direction."

At the meeting, the chairman read out congratulatory telegrams to the conference from Soong Ching Ling and others. The conference also received a congratulatory message from Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan, Vice President of the Republic of India.

Subsequently, Pandit Sundarlal, Mr. R.K. Karanjia, President of the Bombay India-China Friendship Association and editor of the "Blitz" Weekly, Dr. Syed Mahmud, Member of the Bihar Provincial Assembly of the Indian Parliament, and Dr. Prafulla Ghosh, former Chief Minister of Bengal Province before the partition of India and Pakistan, all spoke at the meeting. They congratulated and welcomed the representatives of the China-India Friendship Association. All speakers enthusiastically spoke of the long-standing friendship between the two countries and called for further strengthening and developing this friendship.

Amidst thunderous applause lasting for three minutes, Dr. Ding Xilin, head of the delegation of the China-India Friendship Association, rose to address the meeting, conveying the congratulations and respect of the China-India Friendship Association. In his address, Ding Xilin narrated the traditional friendship between the Chinese and Indian peoples. He then pointed out: Today, China-India friendship has emerged before us in a new form. One of the best proofs is the successful convening of the National Conference of the India-China Friendship Association.

After his address, Ding Xilin, on behalf of the China-India Friendship Association, presented a banner to the conference, with the inscription: "Strengthen Friendly Unity, Promote Cultural Exchange." Mrs. Hannah Sen accepted the banner on behalf of the conference.

At the meeting, the chairman also announced the gifts presented by the delegation of the China-India Friendship Association and other Chinese people's organizations to the National Conference of the India-China Friendship Association, the Indian Progressive Writers' Association, and the Indian People's Theatre Association.

### 印中友协首次全国会议隆重举行

1953-12-16
第4版()
专栏：

    印中友协首次全国会议隆重举行
    【新华社新德里十五日电】印中友好协会第一次全国会议于十一日到十三日举行。会议的开幕式是于十一日下午在新德里宪法俱乐部大厅举行的，气氛热烈、严肃和隆重。
    参加会议开幕式的约有一千人。当中印友好协会代表团走过大厅中央过道时，全体到会者起立，以历久不息的欢呼声和掌声欢迎代表团代表，并高呼“印中友谊万岁！”
    锡（兰）中友好协会主席古纳瓦地尼夫人特自锡兰前来出席会议，也受到热烈的欢迎。
    参加会议的有印度各地派来的代表约一百五十四人。此外，参加会议开幕式的著名人士有：印度国会人民院副议长阿南塔萨雅纳姆·阿阳加尔，德里邦立法会议议长古鲁穆克·尼哈尔·辛格，德里邦政府首席部长乔度里·德拉姆·布拉加希，印度国会议员、印度政府铁道与运输部驻印度国会次官沙·纳瓦兹·康恩。中国驻印度大使馆人员以及苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利等国驻印度使馆代表也出席了会议开幕式。
    印中友协全国会议筹备委员会召集人吉安·昌德博士宣布主席团名单，其中包括：前印度亲善访华团团长潘迪特·森德拉尔，全印和平理事会主席赛福丁·克其鲁博士，印度国会议员萨希布·辛格·索克少将，全印妇女大会主席汉纳·塞恩夫人等。
    吉安·昌德博士并邀请以丁西林为首的中印友协代表团和中国驻印度大使袁仲贤至主席台就座。
    会议主席汉纳·塞恩夫人宣布会议开幕，并对由丁西林率领的中印友协代表团表示欢迎。塞恩夫人说，印中两国之间的深刻的、传统的文化了解是两国所共有的主要特色。她说，对于这个历史，印中两国人民有理由感到正当的骄傲。
    印度国会联邦院议员卡·卡里尔卡在致开幕词时，要求进一步加强印中两国之间的文化联系。
    中国大使袁仲贤也应邀向会议致词。他说：“中印人民友好的发展有助于亚洲与世界和平，凡为此之努力，中国人民正如印度人民一样，一向十分重视与珍贵。”
    会上，主席宣读了宋庆龄等致会议的贺电。会议也收到了印度共和国副总统萨·拉德哈克里希南博士的贺词。
    接着，潘迪特·森德拉尔，孟买印中友好协会会长、“闪电”周报主编鲁·克·卡朗吉亚，印度国会比哈尔省议员赛义德·马茂德博士，印巴分治前孟加拉省首席部长普拉福拉·高希博士等都在会上发言。他们庆贺并欢迎中印友好协会的代表。所有发言人都热烈地谈到两国之间的历史悠久的友谊，并要求进一步加强和发展这种友谊。
    在长达三分钟之久的雷动的掌声中，中印友好协会代表团团长丁西林博士起立致词，他向大会转达了中印友好协会的祝贺和敬意。丁西林在致词中叙述了中印两国人民的传统友谊。接着他指出：今天，中印友谊更以新的姿态在我们眼前出现了。最好的证明之一就是印中友协全国会议的胜利召开。
  丁西林在致词后，代表中印友好协会向会议献旗，旗上写着：“加强友好团结，促进文化交流”。汉纳·塞恩夫人代表会议受旗。
    会上，主席还宣布了中印友好协会代表团和其他中国人民团体向印中友好协会全国会议、印度进步作家协会和印度人民戏剧协会献礼。
