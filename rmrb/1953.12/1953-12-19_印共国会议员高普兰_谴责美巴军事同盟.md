Title: Indian Communist Member of Parliament Gopalan Condemns US-Pakistan Military Alliance
Author: 
Date: 1953-12-19
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 16th. Indian Communist Member of Parliament leader Gopalan spoke at a rally in Quilon, Travancore-Cochin State, attended by more than 100,000 people on the 13th, opposing the proposed US-Pakistan military alliance. He said that American imperialism is interfering in the internal affairs of Asian countries. The United States is desperately trying to establish bases in Asian countries to protect its interests and endanger Asian peace. He pointed out that American imperialism is now the real threat to Asian countries, and it promises to give these countries economic and military "aid" so that it can establish military bases in these countries.

### 印共国会议员高普兰  谴责美巴军事同盟

1953-12-19
第4版()
专栏：

    印共国会议员高普兰
    谴责美巴军事同盟
    【新华社新德里十六日电】印度国会共产党议员领袖高普兰十三日在特拉凡哥尔—柯钦邦奎隆地方有十多万群众参加的集会上发表演说，反对拟订中的美巴军事同盟。他说：美帝国主义在干涉亚洲国家的内政。美国拚命地想在亚洲国家内建立基地来保护它的利益，危害亚洲和平。他指出：美帝国主义现在是对亚洲国家的真正威胁，它答应给这些国家以经济和军事“援助”，以使它能在这些国家建立军事基地。
