Title: New Danish Envoy Grueggesen Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-12-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 5th—The newly appointed Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Denmark to China, Mr. Grueggesen, accompanied by the Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Danish Legation in China, Mr. Kristensen, arrived in Beijing by train on the 5th.

Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, was present at the station to greet them.

Also present at the station to greet them were foreign diplomatic envoys and embassy staff stationed in China.

### 丹麦新任公使格瑞杰生到京

1953-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    丹麦新任公使格瑞杰生到京
    【新华社五日讯】丹麦王国新任驻我国公使格瑞杰生，由该国驻华公使馆临时代办克里斯顿生陪同于五日乘车抵京。
    前往车站迎接的有中央人民政府外交部交际处处长王倬如。
    前往车站迎接的还有外国驻华使节和使馆人员。
