    Our Great Motherland
    Severe winter has descended upon the grasslands of Inner Mongolia. Herdsmen have already prepared for winter settling and grazing. Gala Sandorji, a herdsman from Temuhuzhu Baga (village), Xini River Sumu (district), Solon Banner (county), Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and his family have settled in this newly built yurt to spend the winter.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-12-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    严冬降临到内蒙古的草原上了。牧民们都已准备好冬季定居放牧。内蒙古自治区索伦旗（县）锡尼河苏木（区）特木胡珠巴嘎（村）的牧民嘎拉桑多尔吉，全家已定居在这新盖的蒙古包中过冬。
