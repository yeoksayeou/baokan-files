Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Banquet to See Off Ambassador Kuznetsov
Author:
Date: 1953-12-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd:

Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, held a reception at 6:00 PM on the 2nd to bid farewell to Kuznetsov, the outgoing Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, who is returning to his country.

Ambassador Kuznetsov and all the diplomatic personnel of the Soviet Embassy in China were invited to attend.

Among those present were: Gao Gang, Chairman of the State Planning Commission of the Central People's Government; Dong Biwu and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Zhang Zhiran, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court; Huang Kecheng, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Xiao Hua and Gan Siqi, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department; Li Jishen and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; heads of various departments of the Central People's Government; heads of various people's organizations; and figures from cultural, educational, scientific, and artistic circles, totaling more than 200 people.

At the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a speech expressing his regret at Ambassador Kuznetsov's departure and admiration and respect for the significant tasks completed by Ambassador Kuznetsov during his tenure in further promoting diplomatic relations between China and the Soviet Union. Subsequently, Ambassador Kuznetsov delivered a speech in reply, thanking the hosts. He spoke of his impressions of New China and the great achievements of the Chinese people in national construction and defending world peace, as well as the profound friendship of the Chinese people towards the Soviet people. He concluded by expressing his continued efforts to further promote the friendship and cooperation between China and the Soviet Union.

The reception took place in an atmosphere of cordiality and friendship. The hosts and guests toasted the great and unbreakable friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union, the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, and the health of the leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and the Soviet government headed by Chairman Malenkov.

### 周总理设宴欢送库兹涅佐夫大使

1953-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理设宴欢送库兹涅佐夫大使
    【新华社三日讯】苏联驻我国特命全权大使库兹涅佐夫将离任返国。我中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，特于二日下午六时举行酒会欢送。
    库兹涅佐夫大使及苏联驻我国大使馆全体外交人员都应邀出席。
    出席作陪的有：中央人民政府国家计划委员会主席高岗，政务院副总理董必武、邓小平，最高人民法院副院长张志让，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长黄克诚、总政治部副主任萧华、甘泗淇，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席李济深、陈叔通，中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章，北京市人民政府副市长张友渔，中央人民政府各部门首长，各人民团体负责人，文化、教育、科学、艺术界人士等共二百多人。
    会上，周恩来总理致词，对库兹涅佐夫大使的离任表示惜别，并对库兹涅佐夫大使在任期间在进一步促进中苏两国邦交方面所完成的重大任务表示钦佩和敬意。接着，库兹涅佐夫大使致词答谢。他谈到了他对于新中国的印象和中国人民在国家建设与保卫世界和平方面的巨大成就，以及中国人民对苏联人民的深厚友谊。他最后表示将继续为进一步促进中苏两国的友谊合作而努力。
    酒会在亲切友好的空气中进行。宾主并为中苏两国人民伟大的、牢不可破的友谊，为毛泽东主席的健康，为以马林科夫主席为首的苏联共产党和苏联政府领袖们的健康干杯。
