Author:
Date: 1953-12-10
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The rapid development of the Yangtze River shipping industry enables Tung oil, a famous local product of Sichuan Province, to be transported to various places in time to meet the needs of industrial construction. This is a picture of iron barges loaded with Tung oil, heading towards the lower reaches of the Yangtze River.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    长江航运事业的迅速发展，使四川省有名的土产——桐油，能够及时运往各地供工业建设的需要。这是满载桐油的铁驳船，正驶向长江下游的情形。
