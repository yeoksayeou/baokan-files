Title: Li Xiangchao Submits Memorandum from Our Country to Switzerland to Timayya and Asks Him to Distribute it to All Members of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission
Author:
Date: 1953-12-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 17th. Lieutenant General Li Xiangchao, Senior Member of the Korean-Chinese side of the Military Armistice Commission, wrote to General Timayya, Chairman of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission, on the 17th, submitting to him the memorandum from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China in reply to the Swiss Federal Government, and requested him to distribute it to all members of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission.

### 李相朝把我国复瑞士的备忘录送交蒂迈雅  并请他分发给中立国遣返委员会各委员

1953-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    李相朝把我国复瑞士的备忘录送交蒂迈雅
    并请他分发给中立国遣返委员会各委员
    【新华社开城十七日电】军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员李相朝中将在十七日写信给中立国遣返委员会主席蒂迈雅将军，把中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部答复瑞士联邦政府的备忘录送交他，并请他分发给中立国遣返委员会各委员。
