Title: The General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Issues Instructions, Calling on All Officers of the People's Liberation Army to Actively Subscribe to National Construction Bonds
Author:
Date: 1953-12-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 25th: The General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government issued instructions to all units of the People's Liberation Army on the 21st, calling on all officers to promote the spirit of patriotism, actively subscribe to the 1954 National Economic Construction Bonds, and support national economic construction. The instructions, after explaining the great significance of the Central People's Government's issuance of the 1954 National Economic Construction Bonds, pointed out: All units should immediately convene cadre meetings, and in accordance with the spirit of the State Council of the Central People's Government's "Instructions on the Issuance of the 1954 National Economic Construction Bonds" and the People's Daily's December 10th editorial "Welcoming the Issuance of the 1954 National Economic Construction Bonds," conduct in-depth mobilization, and call on the officers of the People's Liberation Army to take the lead in responding and play a leading role. The instructions pointed out: All units must strengthen their leadership of this work, conduct full publicity and mobilization in the newspapers of all units, so that all officers of the army, with a high degree of patriotism and enthusiasm for supporting the country's general line and general tasks during the transition period, will actively and voluntarily subscribe to the National Economic Construction Bonds, and strive to overfulfill the task.

### 人民革命军事委员会总政治部发出指示  号召解放军全军干部踊跃认购建设公债

1953-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    人民革命军事委员会总政治部发出指示
    号召解放军全军干部踊跃认购建设公债
    【新华社二十五日讯】中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部于二十一日向人民解放军各部队发出指示，号召全体干部发扬爱国主义精神，踊跃认购一九五四年国家经济建设公债，支援国家经济建设。指示在说明了中央人民政府发行一九五四年国家经济建设公债的重大意义后指出：各部队应即召开干部会议，根据中央人民政府政务院“关于发行一九五四年国家经济建设公债的指示”、人民日报十二月十日“迎接一九五四年国家经济建设公债的发行”的社论的精神，进行深入的动员，并号召人民解放军干部率先响应并起带头作用。指示指出：各单位必须加强对这一工作的领导，在各部队报纸上进行充分的宣传动员，使全军干部以高度的爱国主义精神和拥护国家在过渡时期的总路线、总任务的热情，踊跃地、自愿地认购国家经济建设公债，并争取超额完成任务。
