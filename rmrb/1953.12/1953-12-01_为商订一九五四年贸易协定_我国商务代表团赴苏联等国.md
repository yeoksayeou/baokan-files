Title: Our Country's Business Delegation Leaves for the Soviet Union and Other Countries to Negotiate the 1954 Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1953-12-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: To negotiate the 1954 trade agreement, the seven business delegations sent by our country to the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Romania, Hungary, and Bulgaria have departed from Beijing for the capitals of the respective countries in recent days.

### 为商订一九五四年贸易协定  我国商务代表团赴苏联等国

1953-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    为商订一九五四年贸易协定
    我国商务代表团赴苏联等国
    据新华社讯：为商订一九五四年贸易协定，我国派往苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、罗马尼亚、匈牙利和保加利亚的七个商务代表团，已于日前先后自北京启程前往各国首都。
