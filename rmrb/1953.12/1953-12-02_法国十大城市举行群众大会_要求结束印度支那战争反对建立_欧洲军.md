Title: Mass Meetings Held in Ten French Cities Demand an End to the Indochina War and Oppose the Establishment of a "European Army"
Author:
Date: 1953-12-02
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, December 1st: TASS reports: On November 29th, mass meetings were held in ten major French cities under the auspices of the provincial peace councils. The central themes of these meetings were: opposition to the militarization of West Germany, opposition to the ratification of the Bonn and Paris treaties, and striving for an end to the Indochina War.

    "L'Humanité" reports: On November 29th, the National Conference of Disabled Veterans of the Resistance Movement, the Vaucluse Department Radical Socialist Party Congress, and the National Congress of the Young Republicans pledged to oppose the establishment of the "European Defense Community."

### 法国十大城市举行群众大会  要求结束印度支那战争反对建立“欧洲军”

1953-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

    法国十大城市举行群众大会
    要求结束印度支那战争反对建立“欧洲军”
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社巴黎讯：十一月二十九日，法国十个大城市在各省和平理事会主持下，举行了群众大会。这些大会的中心思想是：反对西德军国主义化，反对批准波恩和巴黎条约，争取结束印度支那战争。
    “人道报”报道：十一月二十九日，抵抗运动残废者全国会议、瓦克卢斯省激进社会党代表大会及青年共和党全国代表大会保证反对建立“欧洲防务集团”。
