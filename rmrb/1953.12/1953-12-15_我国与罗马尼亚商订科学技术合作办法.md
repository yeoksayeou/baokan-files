Title: China and Romania Agree on Scientific and Technological Cooperation Measures
Author:
Date: 1953-12-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 14th: The first session of the Sino-Romanian Joint Committee on Scientific and Technical Cooperation was recently held in Bucharest. According to the signed protocol, both parties should help each other in the development of science and technology by exchanging experiences, exchanging technical materials and specifications.

	The People's Republic of China will provide the People's Republic of Romania with technical data and specifications for the manufacture of hard alloys, penicillin, black ink, silkworm rearing and raw silk processing, and will provide the People's Republic of Romania with agricultural technical assistance in the planting of sorghum, dry rice cultivation, and the planting of certain fruit tree varieties.

	The People's Republic of Romania will provide the People's Republic of China with technical data and specifications for the design and manufacture of oil extraction and refining equipment.

### 我国与罗马尼亚商订科学技术合作办法

1953-12-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我国与罗马尼亚商订科学技术合作办法
    【新华社十四日讯】布加勒斯特讯：中罗科学与技术合作联合委员会第一届会议最近在布加勒斯特举行。根据签订的议定书，双方应通过交流经验、交换技术资料和说明书，以便在发展科学和技术方面互相帮助。
    中华人民共和国将供给罗马尼亚人民共和国以制造硬合金、盘尼西林、黑墨、养蚕及生丝加工方面的技术资料和说明书，并在种植高粱、栽培旱稻及种植若干果树品种方面给罗马尼亚人民共和国以农业技术的帮助。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国将供给中华人民共和国以设计和制造采油和炼油设备的技术资料和说明书。
