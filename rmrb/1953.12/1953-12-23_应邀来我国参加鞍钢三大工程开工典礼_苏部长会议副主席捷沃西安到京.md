Title: Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Zhevoronian Arrives in Beijing to Attend the Groundbreaking Ceremony of Anshan Iron and Steel's Three Major Projects
Author:
Date: 1953-12-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd: At the invitation of the Central People's Government of China, I.F. Tevosian, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of the Metallurgical Industry of the Soviet Union, Bobylev, Director of the Planning Administration of the Ministry of Transportation and Heavy Machinery Manufacturing Industry of the Soviet Union, Busikin, Director of the Second Export Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and Oblomsky, a responsible staff member of the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry, accompanied by Li Qiang, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 22nd to attend the groundbreaking ceremony of the Anshan Iron and Steel's large-scale rolling mill, seamless steel tube plant, and No. 7 blast furnace.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Li Fuchun, Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission of the Central People's Government; Wang Heshou, Minister of Heavy Industry; Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Li Zheren, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade; Lai Jifa, Deputy Minister of Heavy Industry; Wang Daohan, Deputy Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building Industry; Lai Zulie, Director of the Experts Affairs Bureau of the Government Administration Council; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Wang Tuo, Deputy Director.

Dr. Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, as well as Counselor Arkhipov and Deputy Commercial Representative Navicki of the Embassy, also welcomed them at the airport.

On the evening of the 22nd, Gao Gang, Chairman of the State Planning Commission of the Central People's Government, hosted a banquet to welcome the distinguished guests.

When I.F. Tevosian and his party left Moscow for China on the morning of the 21st, Ge Baoquan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, and Commercial Counselors Yi Qintang and Xu Dashen, etc., went to the airport to see them off.

### 应邀来我国参加鞍钢三大工程开工典礼  苏部长会议副主席捷沃西安到京

1953-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国参加鞍钢三大工程开工典礼
    苏部长会议副主席捷沃西安到京
    【新华社二十二日讯】应我中央人民政府邀请前来我国参加鞍山大型轧钢厂、无缝钢管厂和第七号炼铁炉开工典礼的苏联部长会议副主席兼冶金工业部部长伊·费·捷沃西安、苏联运输和重型机器制造工业部计划管理局局长鲍贝列夫、对外贸易部第二出口管理局局长布西金和冶金工业部负责工作人员奥布洛姆斯基，由我国驻苏联大使馆商务参赞李强陪同，在二十二日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有：我中央人民政府国家计划委员会副主席李富春、重工业部部长王鹤寿、外交部副部长伍修权、对外贸易部副部长李哲人、重工业部副部长赖际发、第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵、政务院专家事务管理局局长赖祖烈、外交部交际处处长王倬如、副处长王拓。
    苏联驻我国大使尤金博士、大使馆参赞阿希波夫、商务副代表那维琪等也到机场欢迎。
    二十二日晚，我中央人民政府国家计划委员会高岗主席设宴为贵宾洗尘。
    伊·费·捷沃西安等于二十一日上午离莫斯科前来我国时，我国驻苏大使馆临时代办戈宝权和商务参赞衣钦堂、徐达深等曾到机场欢送。
