Title: General Secretary of the Communist Party of Great Britain, Pollitt, Arrives in Bombay to Attend the Third Congress of the Communist Party of India
Author:
Date: 1953-12-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 16th. Harry Pollitt, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Great Britain, arrived in Bombay on the 12th. He will attend the Third Congress of the Communist Party of India, which will be held in Madurai on December 27th, as a representative of the Communist Party of Great Britain.
    On the 14th, Pollitt gave a speech at a public gathering welcoming him. Pollitt said: The British people today are fighting to get rid of American control and for our national independence. The British people increasingly feel that they should get rid of their dependence on the United States. He said that the foreign policy of the Communist Party of Great Britain advocates eliminating obstacles that hinder trade between Great Britain and the Soviet Union, Eastern European countries, and China.

### 参加印共第三届代表大会  英共总书记波立特到达孟买

1953-12-17
第4版()
专栏：

    参加印共第三届代表大会
    英共总书记波立特到达孟买
     【新华社新德里十六日电】英国共产党总书记哈里·波立特于十二日抵达孟买。他将以英国共产党代表的身份参加十二月二十七日在马都拉举行的印度共产党第三届代表大会。
    十四日，波立特在欢迎他的公共集会上发表谈话。波立特说：英国人民今天正在为摆脱美国控制、为我们的民族独立而斗争。英国人民越来越觉得应该摆脱对美国的依赖。他说，英国共产党的外交政策主张消除阻碍英国与苏联、东欧国家和中国之间进行贸易的障碍。
