Title: Students of Major Universities and Middle Schools in the Capital Commemorate the "December 9th" Movement
Author:
Date: 1953-12-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency Report:

    Students of major universities and middle schools in the capital launched various activities to commemorate the 18th anniversary of the "December 9th" Student Movement. Some schools held report meetings and symposiums. Some schools invited people who participated in the "December 9th" Movement to give reports. Students from Peking University and Tsinghua University jointly held a commemorative meeting and invited Wu Yunduo to give a report. Zou Lufeng, Vice President of Renmin University of China, who participated in the "December 9th" Student Movement, gave a report at the commemorative meeting of Renmin University students. Many schools also held picture exhibitions, big-character posters, and blackboard newspapers commemorating the "December 9th" Movement, introducing the course of the "December 9th" Movement.

### 首都各大、中学校学生纪念“一二·九”运动

1953-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各大、中学校学生纪念“一二·九”运动
    据新华社讯：首都各大、中学校学生展开各种活动，纪念“一二·九”学生运动十八周年。有些学校举行了报告会、座谈会。有些学校请了当年参加过“一二·九”运动的人作报告。北京大学和清华大学的学生共同举行了纪念会，并请了吴运铎作了报告。曾参加“一二·九”学生运动的中国人民大学副校长邹鲁风，在人民大学同学的纪念会上作了报告。许多学校并举办了纪念“一二·九”的图片展览和大字报、黑板报等，介绍“一二·九”运动的经过。
