Title: Japanese Worker and Farmer Representatives Return Home
Author:
Date: 1953-12-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 16th: The Japanese trade union delegation of twelve people and one Japanese farmer representative, who were invited to visit our country to attend the Third World Trade Union Congress and the International Conference of Agricultural and Forestry Workers, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 12th to return home, led by the head of the Japanese trade union delegation, Mitsuo Nakamura.
    Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Li Jiebo, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Fang Ming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department, and Chen Yu, Secretary-General of the Asia-Australia Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions.
    During their visit to Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other cities, the Japanese trade union delegation and the farmer representative were warmly received by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and trade union organizations in various cities, and visited factories, rural areas, schools, water conservancy projects, welfare facilities, and scenic spots.

### 日本工人和农民代表返国

1953-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

    日本工人和农民代表返国
    【新华社十六日讯】应邀来我国访问的出席世界工会第三次代表大会及国际农林工人会议的日本工会代表团十二人和日本农民代表一人，在日本工会代表团团长中村满夫率领下，已于十二日晨乘飞机离北京返国。
    前往机场欢送的有：中华全国总工会书记李颉伯、国际联络部副部长方明和世界工会联合会亚澳联络局秘书长陈宇等人。
    日本工会代表团和农民代表在访问北京、南京、上海、杭州等城市时，曾受到中华全国总工会及各城市工会组织的热诚招待，并参观了工厂、农村、学校、水利工程、福利设施和名胜古迹等。
