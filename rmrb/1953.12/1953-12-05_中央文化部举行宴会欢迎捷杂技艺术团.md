Title: Ministry of Culture Hosts Banquet to Welcome Czech Acrobatics Troupe
Author:
Date: 1953-12-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 4th: The Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government held a banquet at the Beijing Peace Hotel on the evening of the 4th to welcome the Czechoslovak Acrobatics Troupe, which was invited to visit and perform in China.

    The banquet was hosted by Tian Han, Director of the Bureau of Arts Affairs of the Ministry of Culture.

    Miloslav Brauhej, head of the Czechoslovak Acrobatics Troupe, and all the troupe members who had arrived in Beijing attended the banquet. Also present were Šmíd, Counselor of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, and Hedlicka, Cultural Attaché, and others.

    Among those present as hosts were Zhao Feng, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture, Zhou Weizhi, Head of the Central Song and Dance Troupe, Wang Dizi, Head of the China Acrobatics Troupe, Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Zou Difan, Director of the Liaison Department of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, and cultural and artistic figures Li Wei, Ma Hanbing, Wang Songsheng, Zhang Menggeng, as well as Jin Yeqin and Yin Yuhong, actors from the Chinese Acrobatics Troupe who had visited and performed in Czechoslovakia, and many others.

    The banquet proceeded in a pleasant and friendly atmosphere. The hosts and guests repeatedly toasted to the brotherly friendship between the peoples of China and Czechoslovakia, and to the further exchange of culture and art between the two countries. After the banquet, a social dance was held.

### 中央文化部举行宴会欢迎捷杂技艺术团

1953-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中央文化部举行宴会欢迎捷杂技艺术团
    【新华社四日讯】中央人民政府文化部四日晚在北京和平宾馆举行宴会，欢迎应邀来我国访问演出的捷克斯洛伐克杂技艺术团。
    宴会由文化部艺术事业管理局局长田汉主持。
    捷克斯洛伐克杂技艺术团团长米洛斯勒夫·布劳海和已经到北京的全体团员都出席了宴会。出席宴会的还有捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使馆参赞石密茨，文化专员赫德利奇卡等。
    出席作陪的有文化部办公厅主任赵沨，中央歌舞团团长周巍峙，中国杂技团团长王地子，外交部交际处副处长王拓，对外文化联络事务局联络处处长邹荻帆，和文化艺术界人士李伟、马寒冰、王松声、张梦庚，以及曾赴捷克斯洛伐克作访问演出的中国杂技团演员金业勤、尹钰宏等多人。
    宴会在愉快友好的空气中进行。宾主双方曾一再为中捷两国人民兄弟般的友谊，为两国文化艺术进一步的交流干杯。宴会后并举行了联欢舞会。
