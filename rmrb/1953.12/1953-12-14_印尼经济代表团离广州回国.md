Title: Indonesian Economic Delegation Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author: 
Date: 1953-12-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 11th: The seven-member economic delegation of the Government of the Republic of Indonesia, headed by Mr. Asmaun, left Guangzhou on the 10th to return home via Hong Kong. Those who saw them off at the station included Qiu Guoguang, Deputy Special Commissioner of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government in South China, Cao Ruoming, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangzhou Municipal People's Government, and Ye Hezhong, Deputy Manager of the Guangzhou Branch of the China National Import and Export Corporation.
    During their stay in our country, Mr. Asmaun and his party visited Nanjing and Shanghai.

### 印尼经济代表团离广州回国

1953-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    印尼经济代表团离广州回国
    【新华社广州十一日电】印度尼西亚共和国政府经济代表团团长阿斯玛温等一行七人，十日离广州取道香港回国。到车站欢送的有中央人民政府对外贸易部华南副特派员邱国光，广州市人民政府外事处处长曹若茗，中国进出口公司广州分公司副经理叶和中等人。
    阿斯玛温等在我国逗留期间，曾到南京和上海游览参观。
