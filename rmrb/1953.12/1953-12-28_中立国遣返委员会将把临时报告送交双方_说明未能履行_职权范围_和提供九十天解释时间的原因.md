Title: Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission Will Submit Interim Report to Both Sides Explaining Failure to Fulfill "Terms of Reference" and Reasons for Providing Ninety Days for Explanation

Author:
Date: 1953-12-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, December 27th: The Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission will tomorrow submit its interim report to the Korean-Chinese side and the American side, explaining the reasons for its failure to fulfill the "Terms of Reference of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission" and for providing non-directly repatriated prisoners of war with ninety days to provide explanations. According to the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission's communique, the report was adopted by the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission on December 24th by a vote of three to zero, with the Swedish and Swiss members abstaining. The separate reports of the abstaining members will also be attached to the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission's report. The full text of the report will be released to the press at 1:00 PM Korean time.

### 中立国遣返委员会将把临时报告送交双方  说明未能履行“职权范围”和提供九十天解释时间的原因

1953-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

    中立国遣返委员会将把临时报告送交双方
    说明未能履行“职权范围”和提供九十天解释时间的原因
    【新华社开城二十七日电】中立国遣返委员会明天将把它的临时报告送交朝中方面和美方，说明未能履行“中立国遣返委员会的职权范围”和向不直接遣返的战俘提供九十天时间进行解释的原因。据中立国遣返委员会的公报说，这份报告已经中立国遣返委员会于十二月二十四日以三票对零票通过，瑞典和瑞士委员弃权。弃权的委员的单独报告也将附在中立国遣返委员会的这份报告的后面。这份报告将在朝鲜时间下午一时向报界全文公布。
