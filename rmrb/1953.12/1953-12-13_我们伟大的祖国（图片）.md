    Our Great Motherland
    The Tanggu New Port has overfulfilled the annual national harbor operation plan ahead of schedule, further ensuring the safety of ten-thousand-ton ships entering the port. This is the ship lock of Tanggu New Port. A tide mark is set beside the lock to report the rise and fall of the water level.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-12-13

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    塘沽新港已提前超额完成全年国家港湾作业计划，进一步保证了万吨轮船进港的安全。这是塘沽新港的船闸。闸旁设有潮水标志牌报告水位的升降。
