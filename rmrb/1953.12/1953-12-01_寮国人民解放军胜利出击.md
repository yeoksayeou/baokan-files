Title: Lao People's Liberation Army Victorious Offensive
Author: 
Date: 1953-12-01
Page: 4

　　(Xinhua News Agency, dated 29th) A recent communique issued by the Lao People's Liberation Army Command, as reported by the Vietnam News Agency on the 28th, stated that from July to September, the Lao People's Liberation Army and guerrillas conducted a total of forty-two battles in southern Laos. In the battle to remove one enemy stronghold alone, seventy enemy soldiers were annihilated. During this period, the Lao People's Liberation Army seized a large number of enemy machine guns and rifles, a large quantity of military supplies, and destroyed five trucks and two generators.

### 寮国人民解放军胜利出击

1953-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    寮国人民解放军胜利出击
    【新华社二十九日讯】据越南通讯社二十八日消息引寮国人民解放军司令部最近发表的公报说：从七月到九月，寮国人民解放军和游击队在寮国南部共进行了四十二次战斗，仅在拔除一个敌军据点的战斗中就歼灭了敌军七十名。在这个期间，寮国人民解放军缴获敌军大批机枪和步枪及大量军用物资，并击毁卡车五辆和发电机两部。
