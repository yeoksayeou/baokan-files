Title: Premier Zhou Holds Reception to Welcome Ambassador Yudin
Author:
Date: 1953-12-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 21st—Zhou Enlai, Premier and concurrently Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, held a reception on the 21st to welcome Dr. Yudin, the newly appointed Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China. All diplomatic personnel of the Soviet Embassy in China were invited to attend.

Those present to accompany Premier Zhou included: Gao Gang, Chairman of the State Planning Commission of the Central People's Government; Dong Biwu, Huang Yanpei, and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council; He Long, Liu Bocheng, Chen Yi, and Ye Jianying, members of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of the General Staff; Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff; Fu Zhong and Gan Siqi, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department; Zhang Zhirang, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court; Gao Kelin, Deputy Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate; Li Jishen and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Zhang Youyu, Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; heads of various departments of the Central People's Government; heads of various people's organizations; and people from cultural, educational, scientific, and artistic circles, totaling more than 200 people.

At the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a speech, extending a warm welcome and respect to Ambassador Yudin, and wishing Ambassador Yudin new achievements in consolidating and developing the friendly alliance and comprehensive cooperation between the people of China and the Soviet Union.

Ambassador Yudin delivered a speech in reply and expressed his thanks. He congratulated the Chinese people on the achievements they have made in political, economic, and cultural construction, and pointed out the great significance of the unbreakable friendly alliance between the people of China and the Soviet Union in defending world peace.

The reception proceeded in a cordial and friendly atmosphere. The hosts and guests toasted to the unbreakable, fraternal friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union, to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, and to the health of the leaders of the Communist Party and Government of the Soviet Union headed by Comrade Malenkov.

### 周总理举行酒会欢迎尤金大使

1953-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理举行酒会欢迎尤金大使
    【新华社二十一日讯】我中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，二十一日举行酒会，欢迎苏联新任驻我国特命全权大使尤金博士。苏联驻我国大使馆全体外交人员都应邀出席。
    出席作陪的有：中央人民政府国家计划委员会主席高岗，政务院副总理董必武、黄炎培、邓小平，人民革命军事委员会委员贺龙、刘伯承、陈毅、叶剑英，代总参谋长聂荣臻，副总参谋长张宗逊，总政治部副主任傅钟、甘泗淇，最高人民法院副院长张志让，最高人民检察署副检察长高克林，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席李济深、陈叔通，中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章，北京市人民政府副市长张友渔，中央人民政府各部门首长，各人民团体负责人及文化、教育、科学、艺术界人士共二百多人。
    会上，周恩来总理致词，对尤金大使表示热烈的欢迎和敬意，并祝尤金大使在巩固和发展中苏两国人民之间的友好同盟和全面合作上获得新的成就。
    尤金大使致词答谢。他祝贺中国人民在政治、经济、文化建设各方面所获得的成就，并指出中苏两国人民牢不可破的友好同盟在保卫世界和平中的伟大意义。
    酒会在亲切友好的空气中进行。宾主并为中苏两国人民牢不可破的、兄弟般的友谊，为毛泽东主席的健康，为以马林科夫同志为首的苏联共产党和政府的领袖们的健康干杯。
