Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author: 
Date: 1953-12-26
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The machinery and equipment of the first automated thermal power plant in Xinjiang—the Dihua Power Plant—has been installed two months ahead of schedule. After partial trial runs, the quality fully meets Soviet technical standards. It is now actively preparing for formal power transmission. This is the central control room of the Dihua Power Plant. Soviet electrical experts are inspecting the distribution board.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新疆第一座自动化的火力发电厂——迪化电厂的机器设备已提前两个月安装完毕，经局部试车，质量完全合乎苏联技术标准。现正积极准备正式送电。这是迪化电厂的总操作室。苏联电气专家正在检查配电盘。
