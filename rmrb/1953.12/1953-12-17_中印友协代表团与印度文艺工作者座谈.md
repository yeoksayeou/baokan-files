Title: Sino-Indian Friendship Association Delegation Holds Talks with Indian Artists and Writers
Author:
Date: 1953-12-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 16th: The All-India Peace Council invited the delegation of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, headed by Ding Xilin, to hold a symposium with Indian artists and writers on the 12th. Mulk Raj Anand, Vice-Chairman of the All-India Peace Council and Chairman of the Indian Progressive Writers Association, Gu Baiqike, Vice-Chairman of the All-India Peace Council and a Punjabi writer, Sardar Jafri, an Urdu poet, and more than eighty well-known Indian literary critics, musicians, painters, and young artists and writers attended the symposium.

In his speech, Anand requested the establishment of regular contacts between artists and writers of India and China, and the exchange of works between the two countries. He said: Chairman Mao Zedong's "Talks at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art" has had a wide impact in Indian literary circles.

At the meeting, the artists and writers of the two countries discussed issues related to literature and drama. Indian artists and writers also inquired about the creative work and activities of Chinese writers. Xia Yan, the deputy head of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association delegation, and delegation member Yuan Shuipai, explained the relevant issues separately.

### 中印友协代表团与印度文艺工作者座谈

1953-12-17
第4版()
专栏：

    中印友协代表团与印度文艺工作者座谈
    【新华社新德里十六日电】全印和平理事会于十二日邀请以丁西林为首的中印友好协会代表团与印度文艺工作者举行座谈会。全印和平理事会副主席、印度进步作家协会主席穆尔克·拉志·阿南德，全印和平理事会副主席、旁遮普文作家郭白克，乌尔都文诗人萨达尔·贾弗里以及印度著名的文学批评家、音乐家、画家和青年文艺工作者八十余人出席了座谈会。
    阿南德在致词时要求在印中两国文艺工作者之间建立经常的联系，交流两国的作品。他说：毛泽东主席“在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话”在印度文艺界中有着广泛的影响。
    会上，两国文艺工作者谈论了有关文学和戏剧等问题。印度文艺工作者并询问了中国作家的创作和活动情况。中印友协代表团副团长夏衍和团员袁水拍对有关问题分别作了说明。
