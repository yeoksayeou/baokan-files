Title: First Group of Members of the Czechoslovak Acrobatic Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-12-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: According to the 1953 implementation plan of the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement, the first group of sixteen members of the Czechoslovak Acrobatic Troupe, invited by the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government to visit and perform in China, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 1st, led by the head of the troupe, Miloslav Brouhal. Tian Han, Director of the Art Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, and others went to the airport to welcome them. Mr. Hrdlička, Cultural Counsellor of the Embassy of the Czechoslovak Republic in China, also went to the airport to welcome them.

The Czechoslovak Acrobatic Troupe, which has come to China this time, consists of thirty artists and musicians, who will visit and perform in cities such as Beijing.

### 捷杂技艺术团首批团员到京

1953-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

    捷杂技艺术团首批团员到京
    据新华社讯：根据中捷文化合作协定一九五三年执行计划，应中央人民政府文化部邀请来我国作访问演出的捷克斯洛伐克杂技艺术团首批团员十六人在团长米洛斯勒夫·布劳海率领下，一日乘飞机抵达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中央人民政府文化部艺术事业管理局局长田汉等。捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻我国大使馆文化专员赫德利奇卡也到机场欢迎。
    这次来我国的捷克斯洛伐克杂技艺术团共有三十位艺术家和音乐家，他们将在北京等城市访问和演出。
