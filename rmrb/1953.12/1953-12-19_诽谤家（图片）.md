Title: The Slanderer (Image)
Author: Kukryniksy; Marshak
Date: 1953-12-19
Page: 6

    The Slanderer

    The slanderer arrives at the United Nations General Assembly.
    It uses an innocent and sorrowful mask,
    To conceal its fierce beastly face.
    Out of that unspeakable, raging bestiality,
    It accuses North Korea of "atrocities" on the podium.
    While weeping profusely,
    It produces a piece of false evidence,
    That originally came from the Li Chengwan grocery store,
    A product of notorious rumor-mongers.
    The beast accuses others of "bestial acts"!
    This green-faced, fanged fellow is not to be trusted.
    It pieced together this "atrocity" report
    In the tiger's den,
    Intending to sweep away the traces of blood
    Along its path.

    (Originally published on December 7th in "Pravda")
    Soviet Union Kukryniksy painting, Marshak poem

### 诽谤家（图片）
库克雷尼克塞  马尔夏克
1953-12-19
第6版()
专栏：

    诽谤家
    诽谤家来到联合国大会中。
    它用无辜而又哀怨的假面具，
    掩盖起凶恶的野兽面孔。
    出于那不可告人的狂暴的兽性，
    它在讲台上控诉北朝鲜的“暴行”。
    它一面哭得涕泪纵横，
    一面拿出一件伪证，
    那原是李承晚杂货店里
    造谣名家的出品。
    野兽在控诉“兽行”！
    这青面獠牙的家伙，不能相信。
    它在老虎窝里
    把这份“暴行”报告凑成，
    是打算在它走过的路上
    把那斑斑的血迹扫净。
    （原载十二月七日“真理报”）
    苏联  库克雷尼克塞画  马尔夏克诗
