    Our Great Motherland
    Wang Chonglun, special-class labor model of Anshan City and planer of the tool workshop of Anshan General Machinery Factory, completed three and a half years of production tasks on December 9th this year and decided to complete the work for 1956 by the end of this year.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    鞍山市特等劳动模范、鞍山机械总厂工具车间的刨工王崇伦，在今年十二月九日完成了三年半的生产任务，并决定到今年年底做完一九五六年的工作。
