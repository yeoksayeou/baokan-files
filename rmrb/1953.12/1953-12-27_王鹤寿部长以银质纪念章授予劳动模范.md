Title: Minister Wang Heshou Awards Silver Medals to Labor Models
Author:
Date: 1953-12-27
Page: 2

    Minister Wang Heshou Awards Silver Medals to Labor Models
    At the commencement ceremony for the large-scale rolling mill, seamless steel tube plant, and No. 7 blast furnace of Anshan Iron and Steel Company, Wang Heshou, Minister of Heavy Industry of the Central People's Government, awarded silver medals to over a dozen labor models who made special contributions to the construction of the three major projects. The recipients included: Wang Jinzhong, Huang Demao, Hu Zhaosen, Zhong Zhenqing, Jin Changming, Lü Baofang, He Shanshu, Zhuang Jiqing, Han Qingchen, Xu Chunqi, Su Menglan, Huo Weizhou, Liu Zhenju, Sun Rixiang, Sun Xiang, Zhang Yongbin, Liu Maolin, Tong Zhankui, Chen Jiyuan, and others.
    In addition, the Ministry of Heavy Industry of the Central People's Government will award silver medals to Liu Chunde and 46 others. In the construction of the motherland's first powerful iron and steel base, hundreds of workers, engineering technicians, and veteran cadres have also made significant contributions, and Anshan Iron and Steel Company will also award them medals. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 王鹤寿部长以银质纪念章授予劳动模范

1953-12-27
第2版()
专栏：

    王鹤寿部长以银质纪念章授予劳动模范
    在鞍山钢铁公司大型轧钢厂、无缝钢管厂和第七号炼铁炉开工典礼大会上，中央人民政府重工业部部长王鹤寿授予在建设三大工程中有特殊贡献的十几位劳动模范以银质纪念章。受奖的有：王进忠、黄德茂、胡兆森、钟振庆、金长明、吕宝芳、贺善述、庄吉庆、韩庆臣、徐春棋、苏孟兰、霍维周、刘振举、孙日祥、孙祥、张永斌、刘茂林、佟占奎、陈吉元等。
    此外，中央人民政府重工业部将授予刘纯德等四十六人以银质纪念章。在建设祖国第一个强大的钢铁基地中，还有数百个工人、工程技术人员和老干部有不少贡献，鞍山钢铁公司也将授予他们纪念章。（新华社）
