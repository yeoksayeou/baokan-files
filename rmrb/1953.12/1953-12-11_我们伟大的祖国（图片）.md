Our Great Motherland
Members of the Gaokan Agricultural Producers' Cooperative in Gaokan Village, a suburb of Shenyang, enthusiastically sold their surplus grain to the state after hearing the propaganda about the general line for the transition period. This is a scene of the cooperative members concentrating the grain to be sold to the state for weighing.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    沈阳市郊区高坎村高坎农业生产合作社的社员，在听了国家过渡时期总路线的宣传以后，踊跃地把余粮卖给国家。这是社员们将卖给国家的粮食集中起来过秤的情形。
