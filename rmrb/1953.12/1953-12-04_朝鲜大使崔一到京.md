Title: DPRK Ambassador Choe Il Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1953-12-04
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd: Choe Il, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 3rd. Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and Yang Chengwu, Deputy Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command, went to the station to welcome him. Diplomatic envoys and diplomatic personnel from various countries to China were also present to welcome him. Xu Zhe, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, and all members of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China were also present to welcome him.

### 朝鲜大使崔一到京

1953-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜大使崔一到京
    【新华社三日讯】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华特命全权大使崔一，在三日下午到达北京。赴车站欢迎的有中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫、亚洲司司长陈家康、交际处处长王倬如，北京市人民政府副市长张友渔，京津卫戍司令部副司令员杨成武。到站欢迎的还有各国驻华外交使节和外交人员。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办徐哲和全体馆员也前往欢迎。
