Title: Premier Zhou Receives Indonesian Government Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1953-12-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 30th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government's State Council received Asmaun, head of the Indonesian Republic Government Economic Delegation, and members Fei Jie·Shalin, Batila Rajawani, Sunazo, Hasuno, and Da Wulanni, in the afternoon of the 30th. Also present at the reception was Mononutu, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Indonesia to China.

    Present at the reception were: Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Lu Xuzhang, Director of the Third Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade.

### 周总理接见印尼政府经济代表团

1953-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见印尼政府经济代表团
    【新华社三十日讯】我中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理在三十日下午接见了印度尼西亚共和国政府经济代表团团长阿斯玛温，团员：费节·沙林、巴蒂拉渣哇尼、苏纳佐、哈苏诺、达务兰尼。参加这一接见的还有印度尼西亚共和国驻我国特命全权大使莫诺努图。
      接见时在座的有：中央人民政府对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，外交部亚洲司司长陈家康，对外贸易部第三局局长卢绪章。
