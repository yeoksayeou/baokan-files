Title: Molotov Receives Former German Weimar Republic Chancellor Wirth
Author:
Date: 1953-12-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 19th: TASS Moscow reported: V.M. Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Foreign Affairs, received Dr. Joseph Wirth, former Chancellor of the German Weimar Republic, on the 18th. Dr. Wirth is currently visiting Moscow at the invitation of the Soviet Peace Committee.

### 莫洛托夫接见德国前魏玛共和国总理维尔特

1953-12-20
第4版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫接见德国前魏玛共和国总理维尔特
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫十八日接见了德国前魏玛共和国总理约瑟夫·维尔特博士。维尔特博士应苏联拥护和平委员会的邀请，目前正在莫斯科访问。
