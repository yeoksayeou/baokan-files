Title: Government Administration Council Holds 197th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-12-14
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 11th—The 197th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 10th. The meeting heard a report by Xie Juezai, Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government, on the Second National Civil Affairs Conference, and approved the resolution of the Second National Civil Affairs Conference. The meeting also approved various lists of appointments and removals.

### 政务院举行第一九七次会议

1953-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一九七次会议
    【新华社十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百九十七次政务会议在十日下午举行。会议听了中央人民政府内务部谢觉哉部长所作的关于第二次全国民政会议的报告，批准了第二次全国民政会议决议。会议并通过了各项任免名单。
