Title: Vyshinsky Awarded the Order of Lenin
Author:
Date: 1953-12-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 9th: TASS reported from Moscow on the 9th that on the occasion of the 70th birthday of Andrei Yanuaryevich Vyshinsky, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has awarded him the Order of Lenin in recognition of his outstanding contributions to the Soviet state.

### 维辛斯基荣获列宁勋章

1953-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

    维辛斯基荣获列宁勋章
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社莫斯科九日讯：在苏联外交部第一副部长安·扬·维辛斯基七十寿辰的时候，苏联最高苏维埃主席团特授予他列宁勋章，以表扬他对苏维埃国家的杰出贡献。
