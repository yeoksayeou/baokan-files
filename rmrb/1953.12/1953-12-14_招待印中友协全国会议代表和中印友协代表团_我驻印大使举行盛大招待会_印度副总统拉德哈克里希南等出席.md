Title: Ambassador to India Hosts Grand Reception for Delegates to All-India Conference of India-China Friendship Association and Delegation of Sino-Indian Friendship Association; Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan and Others Attend
Author:
Date: 1953-12-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 13th - Chinese Ambassador to India Yuan Zhongxian hosted a grand reception on the evening of the 11th to entertain delegates to the All-India Conference of the India-China Friendship Association and the delegation of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association invited to attend the conference. Four hundred and fifty guests attended.

Among the guests were Indian Vice President Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan, Indian Ambassador to China, Mr. R.K. Nehru and his wife, Secretary-General of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, N.R. Pillai, Foreign Secretary of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, R.K. Nehru, Chief Minister of the Delhi State Government, Dharam Bhaga, President of the All-India Peace Council, Dr. Saifuddin Kitchlew, former head of the Indian Goodwill Mission to China, Pandit Sundarlal, Indian government officials, members of the Indian Parliament, writers, artists, journalists, and representatives of youth, women, and business circles.

The reception proceeded in a very warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 招待印中友协全国会议代表和中印友协代表团  我驻印大使举行盛大招待会  印度副总统拉德哈克里希南等出席

1953-12-14
第4版()
专栏：

    招待印中友协全国会议代表和中印友协代表团
    我驻印大使举行盛大招待会
    印度副总统拉德哈克里希南等出席
    【新华社新德里十三日电】中国驻印度大使袁仲贤十一日晚举行盛大的招待会，招待印中友好协会全国会议代表和应邀参加会议的中印友好协会代表团。到会的来宾有四百五十位。
    到会的来宾中有印度副总统萨瓦帕利·拉德哈克里希南博士，印度驻华大使赖嘉文及其夫人，印度政府外交部秘书长纳·鲁·皮莱、印度政府外交部外事秘书鲁·克·尼赫鲁、德里邦政府首席部长德拉姆·布拉加希，全印和平理事会主席赛福丁·克其鲁博士，前印度亲善访华团团长潘迪特·森德拉尔，印度政府官员、印度国会议员、作家、艺术家、记者以及青年、妇女和商界代表。
    招待会在十分热烈和友好的气氛中进行。
