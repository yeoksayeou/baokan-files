Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Message to CPI on Holding its Congress
Author:
Date: 1953-12-27
Page: 4
To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of India and to all delegates of the Third Congress of the Communist Party of India, Comrades:

    On the occasion of the Third Congress of the Communist Party of India, we extend our brotherly congratulations to the Congress. We congratulate you on the success of the Congress and on your further achievements in the great cause of uniting all the Indian people and striving for India's complete independence, democracy, peace, and prosperity.

    Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    December 15, 1953

### 中共中央电贺印共举行代表大会

1953-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    中共中央电贺印共举行代表大会印度共产党中央委员会并转印度共产党第三次代表大会全体代表同志们：
    兹值印度共产党举行第三次代表大会之际，我们谨向大会致兄弟般的祝贺。祝贺大会的成功，祝贺你们在团结全印度人民，争取印度的完全独立、民主、和平与繁荣的伟大事业中获得更进一步的成就。
    中国共产党中央委员会
    一九五三年十二月十五日
