Title: Order of the State Council of the Central People's Government

Author:
Date: 1953-12-05
Page: 2

The "Measures of the State Council of the Central People's Government Regarding Land Requisition for National Construction", passed at the 192nd Executive Meeting of this Council on November 5, 1953, has been submitted to the Chairman of the Central People's Government for approval, and is hereby promulgated and shall be implemented from the date of promulgation.

Premier Zhou Enlai
December 5, 1953

### 中央人民政府政务院命令

1953-12-05
第2版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府政务院命令
    一九五三年十一月五日本院第一百九十二次政务会议通过的“中央人民政府政务院关于国家建设征用土地办法”，业经报请中央人民政府主席批准，特令公布，并自公布之日起实行。
    总理  周恩来
    一九五三年十二月五日
