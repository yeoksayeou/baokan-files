Title: German Democratic Republic Art Troupe Holds Performance; Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, and Zhou Enlai Receive Troupe Leader Before Viewing

Author:

Date: 1953-12-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 27th: The German Democratic Republic National People's Art Troupe held a song and dance performance at Huairentang in Beijing on the evening of the 27th. The performance was hosted by the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government.

Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government, Liu Shaoqi, and Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council, attended the evening's performance and, before the start of the program, received Professor Hans Pischner, head of the art troupe, cultural and political representative Uskoreit, artistic director Hans Müller, and dance team captain Mrs. Tia Maas. The Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Koenig, participated in the reception.

Also present were Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government, and Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations.

Also attending the evening were: Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government, Dong Biwu, Huang Yanpei, and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premiers of the State Council, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, as well as heads of various departments of the Central People's Government and well-known writers, playwrights, musicians, dancers, artists, and actors from the capital.

The German Democratic Republic National People's Art Troupe performed many songs and dances with rich national characteristics and folk styles at the evening. These programs demonstrated the excellent artistic traditions of the German people and their firm will to strive for national unity and world peace, and were warmly welcomed by the attendees during the performance.

### 民主德国艺术歌舞团举行表演会  朱德、刘少奇、周恩来观看前接见歌舞团长

1953-12-28
第1版()
专栏：

    民主德国艺术歌舞团举行表演会
    朱德、刘少奇、周恩来观看前接见歌舞团长
    【新华社二十七日讯】德意志民主共和国国家人民艺术歌舞团二十七日晚在北京怀仁堂举行歌舞表演晚会，主持晚会的是中央人民政府文化部。
    中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇，政务院总理周恩来出席了晚会，并在节目开始前接见了艺术歌舞团团长汉斯·皮什纳教授，文化政治代表乌斯科雷特，艺术总指导汉斯·缪勒，乐队指挥罗尔夫·吉列克，舞蹈队队长蒂阿·马斯夫人等。德意志民主共和国驻中国大使柯尼希参加了这一接见。
    在座的有中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰，对外文化联络事务局局长洪深。
    出席晚会的还有：中央人民政府副主席李济深，政务院副总理董必武、黄炎培、邓小平，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通，以及中央人民政府各部门首长和首都著名作家、戏剧家、音乐家、舞蹈家、美术家、演员等。
    德意志民主共和国国家人民艺术歌舞团在晚会上演出了许多富有民族色彩和民间风格的歌咏舞蹈。这些节目表现了德国人民的优秀的艺术传统，和他们争取祖国统一与世界和平的坚强意志，在演出时受到了与会者的热烈欢迎。
