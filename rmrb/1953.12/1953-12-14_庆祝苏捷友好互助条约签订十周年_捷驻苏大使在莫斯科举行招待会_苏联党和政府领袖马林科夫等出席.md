Title: Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of the Signing of the Soviet-Czech Treaty of Friendship, Mutual Assistance and Post-War Cooperation, Czechoslovak Ambassador to the Soviet Union Holds Reception in Moscow, Soviet Party and Government Leaders Malenkov and Others Attend

Author:

Date: 1953-12-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 13th: According to TASS, Czechoslovak Ambassador to the Soviet Union, J. Vosaglik, held a reception in Moscow on the 12th to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Soviet-Czechoslovak Treaty of Friendship, Mutual Assistance and Post-War Cooperation. Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, G.M. Malenkov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, V.M. Molotov, and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, N.S. Khrushchev, attended the reception and offered toasts. Also attending the reception were heads of various departments of the Soviet government, senior generals of the Soviet army, heads of various people's organizations, figures from cultural and scientific circles, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union.

### 庆祝苏捷友好互助条约签订十周年  捷驻苏大使在莫斯科举行招待会  苏联党和政府领袖马林科夫等出席

1953-12-14
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝苏捷友好互助条约签订十周年
    捷驻苏大使在莫斯科举行招待会
    苏联党和政府领袖马林科夫等出席
    【新华社十三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：捷克斯洛伐克驻苏联大使雅·沃萨格里克，十二日在莫斯科举行招待会，庆祝苏捷友好互助与战后合作条约签订十周年。苏联部长会议主席格·马·马林科夫，苏联部长会议第一副主席、苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫，苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫，均出席招待会并致祝词。出席招待会的还有苏联政府各部门首长、苏军高级将领、各人民团体负责人、文化科学界人士和各国驻苏外交使节。
