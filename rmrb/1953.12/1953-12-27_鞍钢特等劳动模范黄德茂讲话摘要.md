Title: Summary of Speech by Huang Demao, Special-Class Labor Model of Anshan Iron and Steel

Author: 
Date: 1953-12-27
Page: 2

Summary of Speech by Huang Demao, Special-Class Labor Model of Anshan Iron and Steel

    Huang Demao, Special-Class Labor Model of Anshan Iron and Steel, spoke at the inauguration ceremony of the Large Rolling Mill, Seamless Steel Tube Plant, and No. 7 Blast Furnace. He said: "In the first year of our country's first Five-Year Plan, we have victoriously built the Large Rolling Mill, Seamless Steel Tube Plant, and No. 7 Blast Furnace. This is a great victory in the process of our country's gradual realization of socialist industrialization. This achievement was made under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, with the assistance of the Soviet government and the Soviet people, and under the personal guidance of Soviet experts, with the strong support of the people of the whole country, and through the hard work of our working class, learning from Soviet advanced experience, and overcoming all difficulties."
    He said: "We must, in accordance with the instructions of Chairman Mao's letter, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, strive to improve our political and cultural level, learn advanced Soviet science and technology, and use all the strength of our employees to meet the new tasks assigned to us by the Party and the people, build more automated large factories, and establish a strong material foundation for the socialist industrialization of our motherland. We must cherish the machinery supplied to us by the Soviet Union as we cherish our own eyes, learn to operate the machinery skillfully, better utilize the equipment's capabilities, increase production, increase income, accumulate funds, build our motherland, and strive for the gradual realization of the country's socialist industrialization and transition to a socialist society!" (Xinhua News Agency)

### 鞍钢特等劳动模范黄德茂讲话摘要

1953-12-27
第2版()
专栏：

    鞍钢特等劳动模范黄德茂讲话摘要
    鞍钢特等劳动模范黄德茂在大型轧钢厂、无缝钢管厂和七号炼铁炉开工典礼大会上讲话。他说：“当我们祖国开始第一个五年建设计划第一年里就胜利地建设起大型轧钢厂、无缝钢管厂、七号炼铁炉，这是我们国家在逐步实现社会主义工业化进程中的一个巨大胜利。这个成就是在毛主席和中国共产党英明领导下，在苏联政府、苏联人民的援助下和苏联专家的亲自指导下，在全国人民大力支援下，和我们工人阶级努力劳动、学习苏联先进经验、克服了一切困难所取得的。”
    他说：“我们一定要按照毛主席来信的指示，在中国共产党的领导下，努力提高政治文化水平，学习苏联先进科学技术，以我们职工的全部力量迎接党和人民交给我们的新任务，建设更多的自动化的大工厂，给祖国社会主义工业化建立强大的物质基础。我们要爱护苏联供给我们的机械，像爱护我们的眼睛一样，学会熟练地操纵机械，更好地发挥设备能力，增加生产，增加收入，积累资金，建设我们的祖国，为逐步实现国家的社会主义工业化、过渡到社会主义社会而奋斗！”（新华社）
