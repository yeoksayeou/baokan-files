Title: Active Sino-Indian Friendship Activities Unfold Across India
Author:
Date: 1953-12-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi Dispatch: On the eve of the All-India Conference of the India-China Friendship Association, the India-China Friendship Associations in various provinces (states) of India are actively carrying out Sino-Indian friendship activities.

    The All-State Conference of the Central India State - Bhopal State India-China Friendship Association was held in Deogarh from November 14th to 16th. About 100 representatives from Bhopal, Indore, and other places attended the meeting. The meeting was presided over by Mr. Kanghaiya Lal Vaidya, a member of the Indian Parliament. Kang Maozhao, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in India, was invited to attend the meeting and conveyed congratulations on behalf of the Chinese Embassy in India.

    The meeting passed a resolution expressing deep concern about the international situation and calling for the maintenance of peace at all costs. The meeting also passed a resolution paying tribute to Chairman Mao Zedong.

    On the evening of November 16th, people from all walks of life in Deogarh held a mass meeting under the auspices of the India-China Friendship Association to welcome Kang Maozhao, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in India. About 10,000 people from all walks of life attended the welcome meeting.

    In Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, the Chinese Art Exhibition organized by the India-China Friendship Association opened at Aligarh University on November 14th. Professor Muhammad Habib presided over the opening ceremony.

    The exhibition displayed pictures about the life of women and children in New China, handicrafts, embroidery, Chinese paintings, and other exhibits.

    The exhibition was moved to Aligarh City from November 17th to 18th, and attracted a large number of visitors. Muslim women wearing veils also came to visit, and they showed great interest in the lives of women and children in New China.

    In West Bengal, more than 30 Chinese New Year picture exhibitions were held in various places throughout the month of October.

### 印度各地积极展开印中友好活动

1953-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    印度各地积极展开印中友好活动
    据新华社新德里电：在印中友好协会全国会议召开的前夕，印度各省（邦）印中友好协会正积极展开印中友好活动。
    中央印度邦—波保尔邦印中友好协会全邦会议已于十一月十四日到十六日在由金召开。参加会议的有波保尔、印度尔和其他地方的代表约一百人。会议由印度国会议员康海雅莱·德·瓦伊迪雅先生主持。中国驻印大使馆文化参赞康矛召应邀参加会议，他代表中国驻印大使馆向会议致贺。
    会议通过决议，对于国际形势表示深切的关怀，并呼吁以一切代价维护和平。会议并通过决议向毛泽东主席致敬。
    十一月十六日晚，由金市各界在印中友好协会主持下，举行群众大会欢迎中国驻印度大使馆文化参赞康矛召。参加欢迎大会的各界人民共约一万人。
    在北方省阿里加，由印中友好协会组织的中国艺术展览会于十一月十四日在阿里加大学开幕。穆罕默德·哈比伯教授主持开幕典礼。
    展览会中陈列了有关新中国妇女和儿童的生活图片、手工艺品、刺绣品、中国绘画及其他展览品。
    展览会于十一月十七日到十八日移至阿里加城举行，观众颇为踊跃。带着面纱的回教妇女也来参观，她们对新中国妇女和儿童的生活表示很大的兴趣。
    西孟加拉省各地在十月份一个月中共举行了中国年画展览会三十余次。
