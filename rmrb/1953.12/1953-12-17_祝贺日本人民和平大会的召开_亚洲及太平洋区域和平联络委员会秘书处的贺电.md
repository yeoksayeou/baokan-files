Title: Congratulations on the Opening of the Japanese People's Peace Conference - Telegram from the Secretariat of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee
Author:
Date: 1953-12-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 16th: The Secretariat of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee sent a telegram congratulating the Japanese People's Peace Conference. The telegram reads: To the Japanese Peace Supporters Committee, forwarded to the Japanese People's Peace Conference:

    On the occasion of the opening of the Japanese People's Peace Conference, please accept our heartfelt congratulations. We believe that the conference will make important contributions to defending peace in Asia, the Pacific region, and the world, and to mobilizing the Japanese people to fight for independence and democracy.
                   Secretariat of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee
                                   December 14th
    Telegram from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace

Xinhua News Agency, December 16th: The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace sent a telegram on December 14th congratulating the Japanese People's Peace Conference, which was held in Tokyo from the 14th to the 15th. The telegram reads: To the Japanese Peace Supporters Committee, forwarded to the Japanese People's Peace Conference:

    On behalf of the Chinese people, we extend our heartfelt congratulations to your conference. We believe that the convening of your conference will further unite the Japanese people in the great cause of defending peace and striving for independence, democracy, and freedom, and striving to resolve international disputes through consultation. There is no doubt that the friendship between the Chinese and Japanese people will also grow in the common struggle to defend world peace.
                               Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
                                    December 14th

### 祝贺日本人民和平大会的召开  亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书处的贺电

1953-12-17
第4版()
专栏：

    祝贺日本人民和平大会的召开
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书处的贺电
    【新华社十六日讯】亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书处电贺日本人民和平大会，电文如下：日本拥护和平委员会转日本人民和平大会：
    值此日本人民和平大会召开之际，谨致衷心的祝贺。相信大会将对保卫亚洲、太平洋区域与世界和平以及动员日本人民争取独立、民主的斗争，作出重要的贡献。
                   亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书处
                                   十二月十四日
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的贺电
    【新华社十六日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会曾于十四日电贺于十四日到十五日在东京召开的日本人民和平大会，电文如下：日本拥护和平委员会转日本人民和平大会：
    我们谨代表中国人民，对贵大会致以衷心的祝贺。我们相信贵大会的召开将使日本人民在保卫和平、争取独立、民主与自由的伟大事业中更进一步团结起来，为争取用协商方法来解决国际争端而奋斗。毫无疑义的，中日两国人民的友谊也将在共同保卫世界和平的斗争中日益增长。
                               中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
                                    十二月十四日
