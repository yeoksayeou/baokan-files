Title: Minister Li Dequan Visits Calcutta
Author:
Date: 1953-12-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi: At the invitation of the Indian Minister of Health, Kaur, Li Dequan, Minister of Health of the Central People's Government of China, who is currently visiting various parts of India, arrived in Calcutta by plane from Madras at noon on the 26th, accompanied by Shen Jian, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in India.

More than 300 people welcomed her at the airport, including officials from the West Bengal Provincial Government, Liu Yufeng, Chinese Consul General in Calcutta, and representatives of overseas Chinese.

In the afternoon of the same day, Minister Li Dequan, accompanied by Consul General Liu Yufeng, called on the Governor of West Bengal Province, H. K. Mukherjee. In the evening, Consul General Liu Yufeng held a grand reception to entertain Minister Li Dequan and introduce Li Dequan to the overseas Chinese.

### 李德全部长访问加尔各答

1953-12-30
第4版()
专栏：

    李德全部长访问加尔各答
    据新华社新德里电：应印度政府卫生部部长考尔的邀请现在印度各地访问的我国中央人民政府卫生部部长李德全，由我国驻印度大使馆申健参赞陪同，在二十六日中午乘飞机由马德拉斯抵达加尔各答。
    在机场欢迎的有西孟加拉省政府官员，我国驻加尔各答总领事柳雨峰和华侨代表等共三百余人。
    当天午后李德全部长由柳雨峰总领事陪同拜会西孟加拉省省长赫·克·慕克吉。当晚，柳雨峰总领事举行盛大酒会招待李德全部长，并介绍李德全与侨胞见面。
