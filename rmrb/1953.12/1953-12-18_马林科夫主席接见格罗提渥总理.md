Title: Chairman Malenkov Receives Prime Minister Grotewohl
Author:
Date: 1953-12-18
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 17th: TASS Moscow reported: On the 16th, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, G.M. Malenkov, received the Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Otto Grotewohl.
　　The meeting took place in an atmosphere of harmony and friendship. Taking part in the meeting were: First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Foreign Affairs, V.M. Molotov, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, N.S. Khrushchev, and Soviet High Commissioner to Germany, V.S. Semyonov.

### 马林科夫主席接见格罗提渥总理

1953-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    马林科夫主席接见格罗提渥总理
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：十六日，苏联部长会议主席格·马·马林科夫接见了德意志民主共和国总理奥托·格罗提渥。
    这次会谈是在融洽与友好的气氛中进行的。参加会谈的有：苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫、苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫和苏联驻德高级专员弗·斯·谢麦诺夫。
