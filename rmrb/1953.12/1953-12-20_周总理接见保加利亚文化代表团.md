Title: Premier Zhou Receives Bulgarian Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1953-12-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 19th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government received Ma. Dozev, head of the Bulgarian Cultural Delegation visiting China, and delegation members E. Mannov, A. Hadzhiolov, O. Vassilev, and N. Lankov on the 19th. Petkov, the Bulgarian Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, also attended the reception.

Present at the reception were: Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government, Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Fan Changjiang, Deputy Secretary-General of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the State Council.

During the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai expressed his deep gratitude to the representatives for the valuable experience they had introduced to China's cultural, scientific, and artistic circles during their visit to China.

The reception took place in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

### 周总理接见保加利亚文化代表团

1953-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见保加利亚文化代表团
    【新华社十九日讯】我中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理十九日接见访问我国的保加利亚文化代表团团长马·多泽夫和团员埃·曼诺夫、阿·哈吉奥洛夫、奥·瓦西列夫、尼·朗科夫。参加这一接见的还有保加利亚驻华特命全权大使彼得科夫。
    接见时在座的有：中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰、外交部副部长伍修权、政务院文化教育委员会副秘书长范长江。
    接见中，周恩来总理对代表们在访问我国期间向我国文化科学艺术界所介绍的各种宝贵经验，深致感谢。
    接见在亲切友好的气氛中进行。
