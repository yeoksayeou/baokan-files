Title: Indian Health Minister Hosts Reception Welcoming Minister Li Dequan; Indian President, Vice President, Prime Minister, and Others Attend Reception
Author: 
Date: 1953-12-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 21st: The Minister of Health of the Republic of India, Rajkumari Amrit Kaur, held a reception on the 17th at the Presidential Palace of the Republic of India to welcome Li Dequan, Minister of Health of the Central People's Government of China, who was invited to visit India.
    Those present at the reception included: President of the Republic of India Prasad, Vice President Radhakrishnan, Prime Minister of the Indian government Nehru and ministers of various ministries, diplomatic envoys and diplomatic personnel from various countries stationed in India, as well as well-known doctors, health workers, and professors from Delhi. Yuan Zhongxian, the Chinese Ambassador to India, was also invited to attend the reception.

### 印度卫生部长举行招待会欢迎李德全部长  印度总统、副总统和总理等出席招待会

1953-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

    印度卫生部长举行招待会欢迎李德全部长
    印度总统、副总统和总理等出席招待会
      【新华社新德里二十一日电】印度共和国政府卫生部部长拉志库马里·阿姆里特·考尔于十七日在印度共和国总统府举行招待会，欢迎应邀到印度访问的我国中央人民政府卫生部部长李德全。
    出席招待会的有：印度共和国总统普拉沙德、副总统拉德哈克里希南、印度政府总理尼赫鲁和各部部长，各国驻印度使节和外交人员，以及德里的著名医生、卫生工作者和教授等。我国驻印度大使袁仲贤也应邀出席了招待会。
