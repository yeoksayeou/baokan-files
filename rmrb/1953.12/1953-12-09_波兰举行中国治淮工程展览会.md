Title: China's Huai River Project Exhibition Held in Poland
Author:
Date: 1953-12-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, December 7th: According to the Polish Press Agency, a Chinese Huai River Project Exhibition opened in Warsaw on the 5th. Representatives from the Polish Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Ministry of Culture and Art, as well as representatives of the Warsaw working people, attended the opening ceremony. Zeng Yongquan, the Chinese Ambassador to Poland, and members of the embassy staff also attended the opening ceremony. The exhibition displayed many charts and photographs about the Huai River Project.

On the same day, an exhibition of works by modern Chinese artists and Dunhuang murals also opened in Warsaw.

### 波兰举行中国治淮工程展览会

1953-12-09
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰举行中国治淮工程展览会
    【新华社八日讯】据波兰通讯社华沙七日讯：中国治淮工程展览会于五日在华沙开幕。参加开幕典礼的有波兰外交部和文化艺术部的代表以及华沙劳动人民的代表。中国驻波兰大使曾涌泉和大使馆馆员也参加了开幕典礼。展览会展出了许多关于治淮工程的图表和照片。
    同日，现代中国艺术家作品和敦煌壁画展览会也在华沙开幕。
