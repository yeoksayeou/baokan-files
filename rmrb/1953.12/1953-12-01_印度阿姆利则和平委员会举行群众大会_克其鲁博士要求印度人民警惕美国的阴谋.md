Title: Amritsar Peace Committee in India Holds Mass Meeting; Dr. Kitchlew Calls on Indian People to Be Vigilant Against American Conspiracies
Author:
Date: 1953-12-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 30th—Dr. Saifuddin Kitchlew, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, has concluded his trip to East Punjab. He gained an in-depth understanding of the peace movement's progress in various regions and called on more Indian people to participate in the peace movement. Dr. Kitchlew received a warm welcome from the people in East Punjab and his hometown, Amritsar.

　　On November 22, the Amritsar Peace Committee organized a mass meeting. In his speech, Dr. Kitchlew urged the people to remain vigilant against American conspiracies. He said: The peace movement advocates for the complete independence of all countries and all nations. The people of Asia should unanimously demand that "imperialists get out of Asia."

　　Referring to the U.S.-Pakistan military alliance, he said: The people of India and Pakistan are wary of the conspiracies of U.S. imperialism. He pointed out that the establishment of military bases by the United States in Pakistan will not only endanger the peace and security of India, but also the peace and security of Asia and the world.

　　Dr. Kitchlew's visit to various parts of East Punjab strengthened the activities of peace advocates in the province. Many peace conferences discussed the program of the peace movement and the attitude to be taken on various international issues. The East Punjab Provincial Peace Committee also organized special meetings to discuss the Korean armistice issue, the issue of the People's Republic of China joining the United Nations, the Kashmir issue, the consultation movement, and other issues, while also establishing new units of the Peace Committee.

### 印度阿姆利则和平委员会举行群众大会  克其鲁博士要求印度人民警惕美国的阴谋

1953-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    印度阿姆利则和平委员会举行群众大会
    克其鲁博士要求印度人民警惕美国的阴谋
    【新华社新德里三十日电】全印和平理事会主席赛福丁·克其鲁博士已经结束了他的东旁遮普之行。他在各地深入了解了和平运动开展的情况，并号召更多的印度人民参加和平运动。克其鲁博士在东旁遮普各地和他的故乡——阿姆利则受到广大人民的热烈欢迎。
    十一月二十二日，阿姆利则和平委员会组织了一个群众大会。克其鲁博士在他的演说中要求人民继续警惕美国的阴谋。他说：和平运动主张一切国家、一切民族的完全独立。亚洲人民应该一致要求“帝国主义者滚出亚洲去”。
    他提到美国和巴基斯坦的军事同盟时说：印度和巴基斯坦人民提防着美帝国主义者的阴谋。他指出，美国在巴基斯坦建立军事基地不仅将危害印度的和平和安全，并且将危害亚洲和世界的和平和安全。
    克其鲁博士在东旁遮普各地的访问加强了该省和平拥护者的活动。许多和平会议都讨论了和平运动的纲领和对各种国际问题所采取的态度。东旁遮普省和平委员会还组织了特别的会议来讨论朝鲜停战问题、人民中国参加联合国组织问题、克什米尔问题、协商运动以及其他各种问题，同时并建立了和平委员会的新单位。
