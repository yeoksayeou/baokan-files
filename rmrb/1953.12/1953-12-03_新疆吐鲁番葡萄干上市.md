Title: Xinjiang Turpan Raisins Hit the Market
Author:
Date: 1953-12-03
Page: 2

Xinjiang Turpan raisins have hit the market. Turpan, Shanshan and other counties in Xinjiang Province are famous raisin producing areas, and the raisins produced in recent years sell well throughout the country. This year, in addition to supplying large quantities to various parts of the country, 100 tons of high-quality seedless green raisins will be processed to supply the Chinese People's Volunteers in Korea, and 150 tons will be processed for export to Czechoslovakia. These raisins are now being shipped out successively. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 新疆吐鲁番葡萄干上市

1953-12-03
第2版()
专栏：

    新疆吐鲁番葡萄干上市
    新疆省吐鲁番葡萄干已上市。吐鲁番、鄯善等县，是新疆省著名的葡萄干产区，近年来所产葡萄干畅销全国。今年除大量供应国内各地外，还将加工一百吨上等无核绿色葡萄干，供给在朝鲜的中国人民志愿军，加工一百五十吨出口到捷克斯洛伐克。这些葡萄干现正陆续外运。（新华社）
