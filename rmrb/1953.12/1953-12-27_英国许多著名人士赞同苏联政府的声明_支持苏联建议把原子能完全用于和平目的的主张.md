Title: Many Prominent Figures in Britain Endorse the Soviet Government's Statement Supporting the Soviet Proposal to Use Atomic Energy Entirely for Peaceful Purposes
Author: 
Date: 1953-12-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, December 24th: "The Daily Worker" published endorsements from many prominent British figures regarding the Soviet government's statement on U.S. President Eisenhower's speech. Dr. Donald Soper, President of the British Methodist Church, said, "I haven't heard such good news in a long time. As I see it, accepting the Russian proposal is in the interest of peace." Dr. Hewlett Johnson, the Dean of Canterbury, said, "I think Russia is acting very wisely. Russia firmly believes that atomic war will not happen, and the large sums of money they are allocating are not used to develop atomic energy for military purposes, but to develop their agriculture." Labor MP John Baird said, "The Soviet government's response to Eisenhower's speech is constructive and beneficial." Professor John Bernal of the University of London said that the Soviet government's response to Eisenhower's proposal is a very pleasant thing. The Soviet government has shown its willingness to discuss the peaceful use of atomic energy on an international scale. The Soviet government's proposal is very reasonable: not just to use some parts of atomic materials for peaceful purposes, but to use all of it entirely for peaceful purposes, and to call for a formal rejection of the use of atomic weapons.

Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, December 24th: According to the Press Trust of India, Indian newspapers welcomed the news of the Soviet government accepting U.S. President Eisenhower's proposal for secret or diplomatic talks on the issue of atomic energy. "The Times of India" wrote: The Soviet Union has shown its willingness "to solve the atomic energy issue in a spirit of cooperation and joint effort."

### 英国许多著名人士赞同苏联政府的声明  支持苏联建议把原子能完全用于和平目的的主张

1953-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    英国许多著名人士赞同苏联政府的声明
    支持苏联建议把原子能完全用于和平目的的主张
     【新华社二十五日讯】据塔斯社伦敦二十四日讯：“工人日报”刊载了英国许多著名人士赞同苏联政府就美国总统艾森豪威尔的演说发表的声明。英国美以美教会教长唐纳德·索佩尔说：“我好久没有听到好消息了。照我看来，接受俄国的建议是符合和平的利益的。”坎特伯雷教长休勒特·约翰逊博士说：“我认为，俄国作得十分明智。俄国坚信原子战争是不会发生的，他们拨出的大宗款项并不是用来发展以军事为目的的原子能，而是发展自己的农业。”工党议员约翰·比尔德说：“苏联政府对艾森豪威尔演说的答复是建设性的和有益的。”伦敦大学教授约翰·贝尔纳说，苏联政府对艾森豪威尔建议的答复是一个十分令人愉快的事。苏联政府表明自己愿意在国际范围内讨论关于和平使用原子能的问题。苏联政府的主张十分合理：不仅仅是把原子物资的一些部分用于和平目的，而应把全部完全用在和平目的上，并号召正式拒绝使用原子武器。
    【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社德里二十四日讯：据印度新闻处报道，印度报纸欢迎苏联政府接受美国总统艾森豪威尔就原子能问题进行秘密的或是外交的会谈的建议的消息。“印度时报”写道：苏联表明愿意“以合作和共同努力的精神来解决原子能问题”。
