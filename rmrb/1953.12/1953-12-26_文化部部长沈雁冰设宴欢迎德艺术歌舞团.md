Title: Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing Hosts Banquet to Welcome German Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1953-12-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency:

    Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing held a banquet on the evening of the 25th to welcome the State Folk Art Ensemble of the German Democratic Republic.

    Professor Hans Pischner, head of the State Folk Art Ensemble of the German Democratic Republic, and all 230 members of the troupe attended the banquet.

    Also present at the banquet were Johannes König, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, and embassy staff.

    Invited to accompany them were Fan Changjiang, Deputy Secretary-General of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government, Liu Zhiming, Vice Minister of Culture, Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and more than 70 writers, playwrights, musicians, dancers, and famous actors from the capital.

    Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing said in his speech at the banquet: "This visit of the art troupe to our country will have a great impact on cultural exchanges between China and Germany; it will also promote the fraternal friendship between the people of China and Germany, as well as the close unity of the socialist peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union."

    The hosts and guests repeatedly toasted at the banquet for the friendship and cultural exchange between the people of China and Germany, for the health of Mao Zedong, Wilhelm Pieck, and the Soviet government leaders headed by Malenkov, and for the common cause of defending world peace. After the banquet, a social dance was held.

### 文化部部长沈雁冰设宴欢迎德艺术歌舞团

1953-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    文化部部长沈雁冰设宴欢迎德艺术歌舞团
    据新华社讯：中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰二十五日晚举行宴会，欢迎德意志民主共和国国家人民艺术歌舞团。
    德意志民主共和国国家人民艺术歌舞团团长汉斯·皮什纳教授和全团二百三十多人出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有德意志民主共和国驻我国大使柯尼希和使馆人员。
    应邀作陪的有中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会副秘书长范长江，文化部副部长刘芝明，外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航和首都的作家、戏剧家、音乐家、舞蹈家、著名演员共七十多人。
    文化部部长沈雁冰在宴会上致词说：艺术歌舞团这次来我国访问，对于中德两国的文化交流，将发生很大的作用；也将促进中德两国人民的兄弟般的友谊，以及以苏联为首的社会主义和平民主阵营的亲密团结。
    宾主双方在宴会上曾一再为中德两国人民的友谊与文化交流，为毛泽东、威廉·皮克和以马林科夫为首的苏联政府领袖们的健康，为保卫世界和平的共同事业而干杯。宴会后，还举行了联欢舞会。
