Title: Romanian Local People's Congress Elections Concluded; Candidates of the People's Democratic Front Led by the Workers' Party All Elected
Author:
Date: 1953-12-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, December 25th: Romanian newspapers published the results of the elections to local people's congresses at all levels held on the 20th. The candidates of the People's Democratic Front led by the Romanian Workers' Party have all been elected. According to the materials provided by the election results, a total of 9,418,087 voters participated in the voting for the people's congresses of all states, of which 98.68 percent voted for the candidates of the People's Democratic Front. A total of 8,163,505 people participated in the voting for the people's congresses of all districts, of which 98.52 percent of the voters voted for the candidates of the People's Democratic Front. A total of 3,110,133 people participated in the voting for the people's congresses of all cities (including the city of Bucharest), of which more than 98 percent of the voters voted for the candidates of the People's Democratic Front. A total of 7,172,832 people participated in the voting for the people's congresses of all villages, of which 98.12 percent of the voters voted for the candidates of the People's Democratic Front. A total of 135,220 representatives were elected to the people's congresses at all levels.
	On the day of the election, the entire urban and rural areas of Romania presented a festive scene. In the early morning, voters from all over the country went to the polling stations to vote with excitement and joy. In the constituencies where the leaders of the Romanian Workers' Party and the government were nominated as candidates, the voters were even more enthusiastic. For example, in the Moni Shiti constituency where the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Romania, Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, was nominated as a candidate, the voters completed their voting by 4:00 p.m. that day.

### 罗马尼亚地方人民会议选举结束  工人党领导的人民民主阵线候选人全部当选

1953-12-26
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚地方人民会议选举结束
    工人党领导的人民民主阵线候选人全部当选
    【新华社二十五日讯】据罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚各报二十三日发表了在二十日举行的各级地方人民会议选举的结果。罗马尼亚工人党领导的人民民主阵线候选人已全部当选。根据选举结果所提供的材料，参加各州人民会议投票的选民共有九百四十一万八千零八十七人，其中有百分之九十八点六八的选民投了人民民主阵线候选人的票。参加各区人民会议投票的共有八百一十六万三千五百零五人，其中百分之九十八点五二的选民投票选举人民民主阵线候选人。参加各市（布加勒斯特市在内）人民会议投票的共有三百一十一万零一百三十三人，其中百分之九十八以上的选民投票选举人民民主阵线的候选人。参加各乡人民会议投票的共有七百一十七万二千八百三十二人，其中百分之九十八点一二的选民投票选举人民民主阵线的候选人。被选入各级人民会议的代表共有十三万五千二百二十人。
    在选举的那一天，罗马尼亚全国城乡各地呈现一片节日景象。清晨，各地选民都怀着兴奋愉快的心情到选举站投票。在罗马尼亚工人党和政府领袖们被提名为候选人的选区中，选民们更是热情高涨。例如，在罗马尼亚部长会议主席乔治乌—德治被提名为候选人的摩尼希蒂选区，选民们在当天下午四时就投完了票。
