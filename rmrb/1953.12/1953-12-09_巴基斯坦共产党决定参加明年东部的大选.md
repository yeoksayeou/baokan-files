Title: Communist Party of Pakistan Decides to Participate in Next Year's General Election in the East
Author:
Date: 1953-12-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 7th: The Communist Party of Pakistan recently decided to participate in the general election to be held in East Pakistan in February next year and decided to work towards establishing a democratic united front.

    The East Bengal Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of Pakistan convened a conference of representatives from various counties in Dhaka from November 10th to 12th. The meeting decided to actively participate in the upcoming general election and adopted an election manifesto. A committee was established at the meeting to take steps to establish a united front of all democratic parties and individuals to conduct election activities.

    Other opposition parties in East Pakistan, including the People's Muslim League, the Peasants and Workers Party, and the Democratic Party, also held provincial representative conferences and decided to establish a united front.

### 巴基斯坦共产党决定参加明年东部的大选

1953-12-09
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦共产党决定参加明年东部的大选
    【新华社新德里七日电】巴基斯坦共产党最近决定参加明年二月间在东巴基斯坦举行的大选，并决定为建立民主联合阵线而努力。
    巴基斯坦共产党东孟加拉省委员会于十一月十日到十二日在达卡召开了各县代表会议。会议决定积极参加将要到来的大选，并通过竞选宣言。会上成立了一个委员会，负责采取步骤建立一个所有民主党派与个人的联合阵线，进行选举活动。
    东巴基斯坦的其他反对党派，包括人民回教联盟、农工党和民主党，也举行了省的代表会议，决定成立联合阵线。
