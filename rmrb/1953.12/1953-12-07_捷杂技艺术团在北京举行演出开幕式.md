Title: Czechoslovak Acrobatic Troupe Holds Opening Ceremony in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1953-12-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 5th: The Czechoslovak Acrobatic Troupe, invited by the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government to visit and perform in China, held its acrobatic performance opening ceremony at the Capital Theater in Beijing on the evening of the 5th. The opening ceremony was presided over by Zhou Yang, Deputy Minister of Culture.

Those attending the opening ceremony included leading members of various departments of the Central People's Government, including Hu Yuzhi, Zhang Xiruo, Wu Xiuquan, Qian Junrui, Yang Hansheng, Fan Changjiang, Zeng Zhaolun, Wu Youxun, as well as over one thousand people from literary and art circles.

Also attending the opening ceremony were the Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Kousal, and embassy staff.

Deputy Minister of Culture Zhou Yang, Czechoslovak Ambassador to China Mr. Kousal, and the head of the Czechoslovak Acrobatic Troupe, Miroslav Drouha, spoke at the opening ceremony. They unanimously congratulated the acrobatic troupe on its performances in China, believing that it would further promote cultural exchanges between China and Czechoslovakia and consolidate the fraternal friendship between the peoples of the two countries in the common cause of striving for world peace.

After receiving flowers presented by young Beijing literary and art workers, the members of the acrobatic troupe began their performance. They performed more than ten wonderful programs, including martial arts, high platform stationary cycling, accordion solo, ladder act, ground balancing exercises, comedy rocking board, ring tossing, tightrope walking, trick cycling, and magic. The Yala father and sons presented beautiful performances of various balancing exercises. The Melber couple performed stick twirling and ring tossing, able to toss eight rings simultaneously. Klarka's tightrope walking was technically thrilling and wonderful. Bertini and three others performed trick cycling, able to perform various solo and group performances on unicycles of different heights. The acrobatic performances were interspersed with very interesting magic and comedic acts. These performances were warmly welcomed by the audience.

After the performance, young Chinese literary and art workers once again presented flowers to the members of the Czechoslovak Acrobatic Troupe to express their gratitude.

### 捷杂技艺术团在北京举行演出开幕式

1953-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    捷杂技艺术团在北京举行演出开幕式
    【新华社五日讯】应中央人民政府文化部邀请来我国访问演出的捷克斯洛伐克杂技艺术团五日晚在首都北京剧场举行杂技艺术演出开幕式。开幕式由文化部副部长周扬主持。
    出席开幕式的有中央人民政府各部门首长胡愈之、张奚若、伍修权、钱俊瑞、阳翰笙、范长江、曾昭抡、吴有训以及文学艺术界人士一千多人。
    出席开幕式的还有捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使康萨拉和使馆人员。
    文化部副部长周扬，捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使康萨拉，捷克斯洛伐克杂技艺术团团长米洛斯勒夫·德劳海都在开幕式上讲了话。他们一致祝贺杂技艺术团在中国的演出，认为它将进一步促进中捷两国的文化交流，巩固两国人民在争取世界和平的共同事业中的兄弟般的友谊。
    杂技艺术团团员们在接受了北京青年文艺工作者的献花后，就开始了表演。他们共表演了武术、高台定车、手风琴独奏、衔梯、地上平衡运动、滑稽晃板、掷圈、走绳索、车技和魔术等十多个精彩节目。亚拉父子作了各种平衡运动的优美表演。梅尔伯夫妇表演的耍棍棒和掷环圈，能同时掷八个环圈。克拉尔卡表演的走绳索，技术惊险绝妙。贝尔悌尼等四人的车技，能在高低不同的独轮车上作各种单人和集体的表演。在杂技表演中，还穿插了非常有趣的魔术和滑稽表演。这些表演都受到观众的热烈欢迎。
    表演结束后，我国青年文艺工作者再一次向捷克斯洛伐克杂技艺术团人员们献花表示谢意。
