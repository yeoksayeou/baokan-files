Title: Vice Chairman Gao Gang Presents Certificates of Appreciation and Medals to Soviet Experts at the Inauguration and Production Ceremony of the Three Major Projects in Anshan
Author:
Date: 1953-12-27
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, December 26th: In order to thank the Soviet government and the Soviet people for their great and selfless assistance to the construction of Anshan Iron and Steel Company, and to thank the Soviet experts for their outstanding contributions to the construction of Anshan Iron and Steel, Gao Gang, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government and Chairman of the State Planning Commission, on behalf of the State Council of the Central People's Government, presented certificates of appreciation and medals to three Soviet commercial representatives stationed in China and twenty Soviet experts at the opening ceremony of the Anshan Large Rolling Mill, Seamless Steel Tube Plant, and No. 7 Blast Furnace. The recipients of the certificates of appreciation and medals were: Soviet Commercial Representatives stationed in China, V.I. Migunov, A.K. Navich, G.D. Rybalko; Soviet experts at Anshan Iron and Steel, I.N. Romanenko, A.P. Ivanov, V.B. Kevdorin, I.P. Rugovik, F.A. Kernilov, P.V. Milekin, V.V. Kugajev, O.N. Rudakov, F.P. Anikeev, A.M. Marchenko, N.V. Borbutenko, I.S. Kashkevich, A.G. Kuzmink, M.G. Gladskov, N.M. Shishov, M.I. Gerasimov, D.K. Budannek, A.I. Konyukhov, G.S. Belyretsky, and F.S. Radosev.

In addition, many Soviet experts have made great contributions to the construction of Anshan Iron and Steel Company, and Anshan Iron and Steel Company is preparing to award them medals.

### 高岗副主席在鞍山三大工程开工生产典礼大会上  授予苏联专家感谢状和纪念章

1953-12-27
第2版()
专栏：

    高岗副主席在鞍山三大工程开工生产典礼大会上
    授予苏联专家感谢状和纪念章
    【新华社鞍山二十六日电】为了感谢苏联政府和苏联人民对鞍山钢铁公司建设事业伟大的、无私的援助，感谢苏联专家帮助鞍钢建设事业的卓越贡献，中央人民政府副主席兼国家计划委员会主席高岗，在鞍山大型轧钢厂、无缝钢管厂和第七号炼铁炉开工典礼大会上，代表中央人民政府政务院授予三位苏联驻我国商务代表和二十位苏联专家以感谢状和纪念章。接受感谢状和纪念章的有：苏联驻我国商务代表维·依·米古诺夫、阿·康·那维琪、格·德·雷巴尔克；在鞍钢的苏联专家依·尼·罗曼柯、安·波·依万诺夫、瓦·别·克夫多林、依·波·鲁高维克、福·安·克尔尼洛夫、保·瓦·米列金、维·瓦·库嘉也夫、奥·尼·鲁达科夫、福·波·安尼克也夫、安·米·马尔琴克、尼·瓦·柏布坚克、依·斯·卡什克维奇、安·格·库兹明克、米·格·格拉德斯阔夫、尼·米·什少夫、米·依·格拉西莫夫、顿·基·布丹涅克、阿·依·柯纽霍夫、格·斯·别雷特卡其、福·沙·拉多赛夫。
    此外，还有许多苏联专家在帮助鞍钢的建设事业中作了很大贡献，鞍山钢铁公司准备授予他们纪念章。
