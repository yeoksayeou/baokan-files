Title: Radford Advocates for the Use of American Naval and Air Forces in Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1953-12-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, December 2nd, TASS dispatch: The "News-Journal" published in York, Pennsylvania, carried an article by its Washington correspondent Robert Allen, stating that Admiral Radford, Chairman of the U.S. Joint Chiefs of Staff, advocates for the use of American naval and air power in Indochina.

Allen wrote: "It is no secret in the Pentagon that Radford advocates strengthening the armed forces of France and (Bao Dai) Vietnam with American naval and air force units. The Joint Chiefs of Staff will conduct a detailed study of this issue. The Joint Chiefs of Staff have already held several discussions on this matter."

### 雷德福主张在越南使用美国海空军

1953-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    雷德福主张在越南使用美国海空军
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社纽约二日讯：宾夕法尼亚州约克城出版的“新闻日报”登载了它的华盛顿记者罗伯特·艾伦所写的一篇文章说，美国参谋长联席会议主席雷德福海军上将主张在印度支那使用美国的海空军力量。
    艾伦写道：“雷德福主张用美国的海空军部队加强法国和（保大）越南的武装力量，这在五角大楼已经不是什么秘密了。参谋长联席会议将对这一问题进行详细的研究。参谋长联席会议已对这一问题进行了几次讨论。”
