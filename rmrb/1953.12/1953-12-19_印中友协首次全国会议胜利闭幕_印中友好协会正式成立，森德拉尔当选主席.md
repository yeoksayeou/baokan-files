Title: First National Conference of the India-China Friendship Association Successfully Concludes; India-China Friendship Association Formally Established, Sundarlal Elected Chairman
Author:
Date: 1953-12-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 18th: The first national conference of the India-China Friendship Association concluded on the 13th after three days of meetings. A national organization that conforms to the wishes of the Indian people and consolidates and promotes friendly cooperation between the people of India and China—the India-China Friendship Association—has been formally established.

    After the grand opening of the first national conference of the India-China Friendship Association on the afternoon of the 11th, the plenary session continued on the morning of the 12th. The conference heard work reports from various local India-China Friendship Associations. Jian Chand, convener of the preparatory committee for the national conference of the India-China Friendship Association, reported to the conference on the work of the preparatory committee.

    Ding Xilin, head of the delegation of the China-India Friendship Association, was invited to speak at the morning session on the 12th. He highly praised the efforts of various local India-China Friendship Associations in promoting friendship between the people of India and China and introduced the work of the China-India Friendship Association in China. He said: "We believe that due to this visit by the China-India Friendship Association delegation, the relationship between the China-India Friendship Association and the India-China Friendship Association will become even closer."

    On the 13th, the delegates held group discussions on the draft constitution of the India-China Friendship Association. After necessary revisions, the conference unanimously adopted the constitution of the India-China Friendship Association. Finally, the plenary session elected Pandit Sundarlal, former head of the Indian Goodwill Delegation to China, as Chairman of the India-China Friendship Association; Ru. K. Karanjia, President of the Bombay India-China Friendship Association, Professor Triprari Chakravarti, President of the West Bengal India-China Friendship Association, and Syed Mahmud, Member of Parliament, as Vice-Chairmen; and Chatur Narain Malaviya, Member of Parliament from the Indian National Congress, as Secretary-General. The plenary session also elected the Executive Committee of the India-China Friendship Association, with Jian Chand elected as Chairman of the Executive Committee.

    In his closing remarks, Professor Chakravarti congratulated the conference on its achievements, saying: "This conference will encourage, promote, and support the India-China Friendship Associations in various regions."

    On the afternoon of the 13th, a mass rally of 10,000 people was held at Urdu Square in Delhi to welcome the delegation of the China-India Friendship Association. At the meeting, Pandit Sundarlal and Jian Chand warmly welcomed the delegation of the China-India Friendship Association led by Ding Xilin and reported on the situation of the national conference of the India-China Friendship Association. Ding Xilin was invited to speak. He hoped that the people of China and India would work together to safeguard world peace. After the speech by Head of Delegation Ding Xilin, the Delhi India-China Friendship Association presented gifts to the China-India Friendship Association delegation, and nine writers and poets from Delhi also presented their books to the China-India Friendship Association delegation.

    According to a Xinhua News Agency dispatch from New Delhi: R.K. Nehru (formerly translated as Lu. K. Nehru), Foreign Secretary of the Indian Government's Ministry of External Affairs, and his wife held a cocktail party on the evening of the 12th to entertain the delegation of the China-India Friendship Association led by Ding Xilin.

    Among those present at the reception were: Chopra, Director of Protocol of the Indian Government's Ministry of External Affairs, senior officials from the Indian Ministry of Education and the Ministry of Information and Broadcasting, Kichloo, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, Major General Sokhey, Vice-Chairman, Members of Parliament, university professors, cultural and artistic workers, social welfare workers, and more than 100 people from the India-China Friendship Association and Indian friends who had visited China.

    Also present at the reception were: Yuan Zhongxian, Chinese Ambassador to India, and embassy staff, as well as diplomatic envoys and diplomatic personnel from other countries stationed in India.

### 印中友协首次全国会议胜利闭幕  印中友好协会正式成立，森德拉尔当选主席

1953-12-19
第4版()
专栏：

    印中友协首次全国会议胜利闭幕
    印中友好协会正式成立，森德拉尔当选主席
    【新华社新德里十八日电】印中友好协会第一次全国会议，经过三天的会议，已于十三日闭幕。一个符合于印度人民愿望、巩固和促进印中两国人民友好合作的全国性机构——印中友好协会已正式成立。
    印中友好协会第一次全国会议于十一日下午隆重揭幕后，十二日上午继续举行全体会议。会议听取了各地印中友协的工作报告。印中友协全国会议筹备委员会召集人吉安·昌德向大会报告了印中友协全国会议筹备委员会的工作情况。
    中印友好协会代表团丁西林团长应邀在十二日上午的会议上发言，他对各地印中友协促进印中两国人民友好的努力表示十分赞扬，并介绍了中印友协在中国的工作情况。他说：相信由于这一次中印友协代表团的访问，中印友协和印中友协的关系将更加亲密。
    十三日与会代表分组对印中友协章程草案进行了热烈的认真的讨论。经过必要的修正后，会议一致通过了印中友协的章程。最后全体会议选出了前印度亲善访华团团长潘迪特·森德拉尔为印中友协主席，孟买印中友协会长鲁·克·卡朗吉亚、西孟加拉省印中友协主席特里普拉里·查克拉瓦蒂教授、国会议员赛义德·马茂德三人为副主席，印度国会国大党议员查图尔·纳拉茵·马拉维亚为秘书长。全体会议还选出了印中友好协会执行委员会，吉安·昌德当选为执行委员会主席。
    查克拉瓦蒂教授在闭会词中祝贺大会的成就，他说：这次会议将对各地的印中友协起鼓舞、推动和支持作用。
    十三日下午，德里乌尔都广场举行了一万人的群众大会，欢迎中印友协代表团。会上，潘迪特·森德拉尔和吉安·昌德对以丁西林为首的中印友协代表团表示热烈的欢迎，并报告了印中友协全国会议的情况。丁西林应邀发言。他希望中印两国人民共同为维护世界和平而努力。丁西林团长讲话结束后，德里印中友协向中印友协代表团赠送礼物，德里九个作家和诗人也把他们写的书赠送给中印友协代表团。
    据新华社新德里电：印度政府外交部外事秘书拉·库·尼赫鲁（前译鲁·克·尼赫鲁）夫妇十二日晚举行酒会，招待以丁西林为首的中印友好协会代表团。
    出席招待会的有：印度政府外交部交际司司长乔普拉，印度教育部和宣传与广播部的高级官员，全印和平理事会主席克其鲁、副主席索克少将，国会议员，大学教授，文化艺术工作者，社会福利工作者以及印中友好协会和印度访华友人共百余人。
    出席酒会的还有：中国驻印度大使袁仲贤及使馆人员，其他国家驻印使节及外交人员。
