Title: Southern Laos Army and People Eliminate Over 1,200 Enemies in Ten Months; Vietnamese People's Army Continues to Pursue and Annihilate Enemy Troops Fleeing from Lai Châu
Author:
Date: 1953-12-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 25th: Vietnam News Agency, December 24th: The General Headquarters of the Lao People's Liberation Army issued a communique stating that the army and people of southern Laos, in adhering to guerrilla warfare and anti-sweeping operations, defended the people's base areas and eliminated many enemy troops.
    The communique stated that in the ten months up to September 1953, the army and people of southern Laos killed, wounded, and captured a total of 1,297 enemy troops, including 11 officers; and seized 200 various weapons, including 35 machine guns.
    Xinhua News Agency, December 25th: Vietnam News Agency, December 24th: On December 19th, the Vietnamese People's Army caught up with six companies of fleeing enemy troops in the Nam Nhe area, 50 kilometers northwest of the capital of Lai Châu province. After a fierce battle, the People's Army killed, wounded, and captured 250 enemy troops, seizing two mortars, 15 machine guns, and a large quantity of military supplies. The remaining enemy fled into the jungle, and the People's Army is continuing to pursue them.

### 寮国南部军民十个月歼敌一千二百余人  越南人民军继续追歼从莱州逃跑的敌军

1953-12-26
第4版()
专栏：

    寮国南部军民十个月歼敌一千二百余人
    越南人民军继续追歼从莱州逃跑的敌军
   【新华社二十五日讯】越南通讯社二十四日讯：寮国人民解放军总司令部发表公报说，寮国南部的军民在坚持游击战和反扫荡战中，保卫了人民的根据地和消灭了敌军的许多有生力量。
    公报说，在到一九五三年九月为止的十个月中，寮国南部的军民一共毙、伤、俘敌军一千二百九十七名，其中有军官十一名；缴获各种武器二百件，其中有机枪三十五挺。
    【新华社二十五日讯】越南通讯社二十四日讯：越南人民军在十二月十九日在莱州省会西北五十公里的南椰地区追上了六连逃窜的敌军；经过激战后，人民军毙、伤、俘敌军二百五十名，缴获迫击炮二门、机枪十五挺和大量军用品。残敌都向丛林逃窜，人民军正继续跟踪追击。
