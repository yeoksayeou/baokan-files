Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-12-22
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Shenyang Fan Factory has completed its annual production task 60 days ahead of schedule and the trial production of new products for this year 47 days ahead of schedule. This is a Soviet-style centrifugal multi-blade fan successfully trial-produced. A technician is recording its air volume.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    沈阳扇风机厂已提前六十天完成全年生产任务，提前四十七天完成今年新产品的试制工作。这是试制成功的一种苏联式离心多叶扇风机。技术员正在记录它的风量。
