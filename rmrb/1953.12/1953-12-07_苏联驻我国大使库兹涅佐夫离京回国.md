Title: Soviet Ambassador to China, Kuznetsov, Departs Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1953-12-07
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 6th: Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Kuznetsov, was recalled and departed Beijing for home on the 6th. Those who went to the airport to see him off included: Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Wu Xiuquan; Director of the General Office, Wang Bingnan; Deputy Director, Yan Baohang; Director of the Protocol Department, Wang Zhuoru; Specialist of the Soviet and Eastern European Department, Liu Jingyu; Vice Mayor of Beijing, Zhang Youyu; Deputy Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison, Yang Chengwu; Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Wu Yuzhang; Secretary-General, Qian Junrui; and President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Xiruo, and others. Diplomatic envoys of various countries to China, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, V.I. Vaskov, and all embassy staff members also went to see him off.

### 苏联驻我国大使库兹涅佐夫离京回国

1953-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联驻我国大使库兹涅佐夫离京回国
    【新华社六日讯】苏联驻我国特命全权大使库兹涅佐夫奉调在六日离京回国。前往机场送行的有：中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权，办公厅主任王炳南、副主任阎宝航，交际处处长王倬如，苏联东欧司专员刘靖宇，北京市副市长张友渔，京津卫戍司令部副司令员杨成武，中苏友好协会副会长吴玉章、总干事钱俊瑞，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若等人。各国驻华使节和苏联驻华大使馆临时代办华司考和全体馆员也前往欢送。
