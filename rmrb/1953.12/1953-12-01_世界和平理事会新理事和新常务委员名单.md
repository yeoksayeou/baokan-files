Title: List of New Council Members and New Standing Committee Members of the World Peace Council
Author: 
Date: 1953-12-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, 28th: According to TASS Vienna, the Vienna meeting of the World Peace Council elected the new council members of the World Peace Council and the new members of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council as follows:
    New Council Members of the World Peace Council:
    Jalal Awkati, staff member (Iraq); Balthasar Castro, Speaker of the House of Representatives (Chile); Alberto Cianca, former minister (Italy); Sergei Gerasimov, film director (Soviet Union); Guillen, poet (Cuba); Knut Løfsnes, legal worker (Norway); Abdul Karim Mashta, writer (Iraq); Clarenzo Menotti, teacher (Italy); Erwin Schärf, writer, Chairman of the Socialist Workers' Party (Left-wing Socialist Party) (Austria); Valentin Sorokin, economist (Soviet Union); William Morrow, chemist (Australia).
    New Members of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council:
    Jorge Amado, writer (Brazil); Monica Felton, female writer, winner of the Stalin International Peace Prize "For Strengthening International Peace" (UK); D.N. Pritt, Queen's Counsel, President of the British Peace Committee (UK); Alexei Surkov, poet (Soviet Union); William Wainright, chemist (UK); Maria Rosa Oliver, female writer (Argentina).

### 世界和平理事会新理事和新常务委员名单

1953-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会新理事和新常务委员名单
    【新华社三十日讯】据塔斯社维也纳二十八日讯：世界和平理事会维也纳会议选出了世界和平理事会新理事和世界和平理事会常务委员会新委员如下：
    世界和平理事会新理事：
    贾拉尔·奥卡蒂，职员（伊拉克）；巴尔塔沙尔·卡斯特罗，众议院议长（智利）；艾伯托·昌卡，前部长（意大利）；谢尔盖·格拉西莫夫，电影导演（苏联）；纪廉，诗人（古巴）；克努特·洛弗斯纳斯，法律工作者（挪威）；阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·马什塔，作家（伊拉克）；克拉伦佐·麦诺蒂，教师（意大利）；埃尔文·谢尔夫，作家，社会主义工人党（左翼社会党）主席（奥地利）；瓦连金·索罗金，经济学家（苏联）；威廉·莫罗，化学家（澳大利亚）。
    世界和平理事会常务委员会新委员：
    若热·亚马多，作家（巴西）；蒙尼卡·费尔顿，女作家，“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人（英国）；丹·诺·普里特，女皇顾问，英国和平委员会会长（英国）；阿列克谢·苏尔科夫，诗人（苏联）；威廉·温莱，化学家（英国）；玛丽亚·罗莎·奥利弗，女作家（阿根廷）。
