Title: Acting Secretary-General of the Communist Party of Pakistan Issues Statement Calling on Patriotic Forces of Pakistan to Oppose the US-Pakistan Military Alliance
Author:
Date: 1953-12-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 16th: The "Pakistan Times" published on the 10th a statement by Mansur, the acting secretary-general of the Communist Party of Pakistan, opposing the conclusion of a military alliance between Pakistan and the United States. The statement said: The Pakistani government is currently forming a military alliance with the United States, the leader of the imperialist camp. Through this military alliance, the United States attempts to turn Pakistan into a war base and use Pakistani soldiers as cannon fodder to realize its policy of "using Asians to fight Asians." The statement said: "This alliance will mean political and economic enslavement, and will mean moral and spiritual degradation."
    The statement then pointed out: "Our urgent need is to live in peace with our neighbors and for world peace, so that we can work for the social, economic and cultural development of our people. Our government should strive for world peace. But this alliance under consideration is contrary to all the above noble aspirations."
    The statement called on all political parties, all patriotic groups, and all freedom-loving individuals in Pakistan to form a united front to oppose the conclusion of the US-Pakistan military alliance.

### 巴基斯坦共产党代理总书记发表声明  号召巴基斯坦爱国力量反对美巴军事同盟

1953-12-17
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦共产党代理总书记发表声明
    号召巴基斯坦爱国力量反对美巴军事同盟
    【新华社十六日讯】喀喇蚩消息：“巴基斯坦时报”十日刊载了巴基斯坦共产党代理总书记曼苏尔发表的反对巴基斯坦和美国缔结军事同盟的声明。声明说：巴基斯坦政府目前正和帝国主义阵营的首领美国结成一种军事同盟。美国企图通过这一军事同盟，把巴基斯坦变为战争基地，并把巴基斯坦士兵当作炮灰，以实现它的“用亚洲人打亚洲人”的政策。声明说：“这一同盟将意味着政治的和经济的奴役，将意味着道德和精神的堕落。”
    声明接着指出：“我国的迫切需要是与邻国和平相处，是世界和平，以便我们能为我国人民的社会的、经济的和文化的发展而工作。我国政府应为世界和平而努力。但这项被考虑中的同盟却是违反上述一切高尚愿望的。”
    声明号召巴基斯坦一切政党、一切爱国团体以及爱好自由的个人，组成一个统一阵线，反对缔结美巴军事同盟。
