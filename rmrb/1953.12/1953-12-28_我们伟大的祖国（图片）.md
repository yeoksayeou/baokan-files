Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-12-28
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    This winter's Huai River harnessing project has commenced successively. At the Foziling Reservoir construction site in the middle reaches of the Huai River, preparations for winter concrete pouring are being stepped up. The parapet of the western half of the multiple-arch dam has been completely poured to the top. This is a corner of the multiple-arch dam construction site. Workers are discussing how to learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union and strive to basically complete the reservoir project before the next flood season.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-12-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    今年冬季治淮工程已陆续开工。淮河中游佛子岭水库工地，正在加紧进行冬季浇筑混凝土的准备工作，连拱坝西半部的垛墙已全部浇筑到顶。这是连拱坝工地的一角。工人们正在讨论如何学习苏联先进经验，争取在明年汛期前基本完成水库的工程。
