Title: Preparatory Committee for the Ningxia Provincial Mongolian Autonomous Region Formally Established
Author:
Date: 1953-12-13
Page: 3

    The inaugural meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the Ningxia Provincial Mongolian Autonomous Region was held in Bayanhot from November 30th to December 2nd, formally establishing the Preparatory Committee for the Ningxia Provincial Mongolian Autonomous Region. At the meeting, twenty-nine preparatory committee members from Mongolian, Hui, Manchu, and Han ethnicities were elected. Darizaya (Mongolian) was publicly elected as Chairman, and Li Jian (Han), Yun Xiang Sheng (Mongolian), Chen Erde Ni Batu (Mongolian), and Yang Lisheng (Han) were elected as Vice-Chairmen. During the inaugural meeting of the Autonomous Region Preparatory Committee, it was also decided to organize seven work teams to go deep into various locations within Alxa League and Dengkou County. In conjunction with the work of increasing production and practicing economy, the teams will promote the policy of national regional autonomy. The Ningxia Provincial Mongolian Autonomous Region, which is currently being prepared for establishment, is composed of the current administrative units of Alxa League and Dengkou County, and is an autonomous region equivalent to a special district level. It has a population of over 78,000 and a total land area of approximately 121,700 square kilometers. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 宁夏省蒙古自治区筹备委员会正式成立

1953-12-13
第3版()
专栏：

    宁夏省蒙古自治区筹备委员会正式成立
    宁夏省蒙古自治区筹备委员会成立会议于十一月三十日到十二月二日在巴音浩特举行，正式成立了宁夏省蒙古自治区筹备委员会。会上，推选出蒙、回、满、汉等族的筹备委员二十九人，并公推达理扎雅（蒙族）为主任委员，李健（汉族）、云祥生
  （蒙族）、陈额尔德尼巴图（蒙族）、杨立生（汉族）为副主任委员。在自治区筹备委员会成立会议上，还通过组织七个工作队，深入阿拉善旗和磴口县各地结合增产节约工作，宣传民族区域自治政策。正在筹备成立的宁夏省蒙古自治区，是由现在的阿拉善旗和磴口县两个行政单位组成，是个相当于专区级的自治区。有七万八千多人口，土地面积共约十二万一千七百多平方公里。　　　（新华社）
