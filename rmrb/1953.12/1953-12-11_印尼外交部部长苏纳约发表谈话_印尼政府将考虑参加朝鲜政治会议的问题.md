Title: Indonesian Foreign Minister Sunarjo Speaks: Indonesian Government to Consider Attending Korean Political Conference
Author:
Date: 1953-12-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, December 10th: According to Antara News Agency, Jakarta: Indonesian Foreign Minister Sunarjo, on December 9th, commented on the proposal from the Korean-Chinese side inviting neutral countries, including Indonesia, to participate in the Korean political conference, saying: The Indonesian government will consider the issue of participating in the Korean political conference if invited. Sunarjo added that Indonesia agrees with the view that, in addition to the countries concerned, neutral countries should also be invited to participate in the political conference.

### 印尼外交部部长苏纳约发表谈话  印尼政府将考虑参加朝鲜政治会议的问题

1953-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼外交部部长苏纳约发表谈话
    印尼政府将考虑参加朝鲜政治会议的问题
    【新华社十日讯】据安尼塔通讯社雅加达讯：印度尼西亚外交部部长苏纳约在九日就朝中方面提出的邀请包括印度尼西亚在内的中立国家参加朝鲜政治会议的建议发表谈话说：印度尼西亚政府将考虑参加朝鲜政治会议的问题，如果被邀请的话。苏纳约又说，印度尼西亚同意这样的意见：除了有关国家以外，中立国也应被邀请参加政治会议。
