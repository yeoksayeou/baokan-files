Title: List of Appointments Approved by the 29th Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee
Author:
Date: 1953-12-14
Page: 3

The 29th Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee approved the following lists of appointments:

    I. Supplementary List of Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government
    Vice Minister
    Yuan Renzhi Formerly Vice Chairman of the Hunan Provincial People's Government.
    II. Supplementary List of Vice Chairmen of the Legislative Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government
    Vice Chairman
    Tao Xijin Currently Secretary-General of the Political and Legal Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council.
    III. Supplementary List of Vice Chairmen of the Songjiang Provincial People's Government
    Vice Chairman
    Zhao Qufei Currently Director of the Public Security Department of the Songjiang Provincial People's Government.
    IV. Supplementary List of Vice Chairmen of the Shandong Provincial People's Government
    Vice Chairman
    Li Shiying Currently Director of the Political and Legal Committee of the Shandong Provincial People's Government.
