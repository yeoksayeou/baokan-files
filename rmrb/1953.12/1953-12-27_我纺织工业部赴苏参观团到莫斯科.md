Title: Textile Industry Ministry Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1953-12-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: The eleven-member delegation from the Ministry of Textile Industry of our country, led by Zhang Qinqiu, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Textile Industry of the Central People's Government, arrived in Moscow by plane on the afternoon of the 21st. Representatives from the Soviet Ministry of Consumer Goods Industry and personnel from our country's embassy in the Soviet Union were at the airport to greet them.
    The delegation will visit Soviet textile industrial enterprises for a month and a half to learn from advanced Soviet experience.

### 我纺织工业部赴苏参观团到莫斯科

1953-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我纺织工业部赴苏参观团到莫斯科
  【新华社莫斯科讯】我纺织工业部赴苏参观团十一人，由中央人民政府纺织工业部副部长张琴秋率领，于二十一日下午乘飞机抵达莫斯科。到机场迎接的有苏联日用品工业部代表和我国驻苏联大使馆人员。
    参观团将参观苏联纺织工业企业一个半月，以学习苏联先进经验。
