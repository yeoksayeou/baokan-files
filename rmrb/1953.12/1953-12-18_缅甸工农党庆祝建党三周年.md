Title: Burmese Workers' and Peasants' Party Celebrates Third Anniversary of Founding

Author:

Date: 1953-12-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 16th: The Burmese Workers' and Peasants' Party held a conference on the 8th to celebrate the third anniversary of the party's founding.

At the conference, the Chairman of the conference and General Secretary of the Workers' and Peasants' Party, Tekin Lun, summarized the work of the Burmese Workers' and Peasants' Party in the three years since its founding. He said that the Burmese Workers' and Peasants' Party will continue to strive for peace and the unity of all nationalities in the future.

After the meeting, the attendees held a parade, chanting slogans such as "Defend world peace" and "Expel the Chiang Kai-shek bandits" along the way.

### 缅甸工农党庆祝建党三周年

1953-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸工农党庆祝建党三周年
    【新华社十六日讯】仰光消息：缅甸工农党于八日举行大会，庆祝该党成立三周年。
    大会主席、工农党总书记德钦伦在大会上总结了缅甸工农党成立三年来的工作。他说，缅甸工农党今后决继续为争取和平、团结全国各民族而奋斗。
    会后，会众举行游行，沿途高呼“保卫世界和平”、“驱逐蒋匪军”等口号。
