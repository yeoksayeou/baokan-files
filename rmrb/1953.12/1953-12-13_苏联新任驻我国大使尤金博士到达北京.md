Title: Soviet Union's New Ambassador to China, Dr. Yudin, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-12-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 12th—The new Ambassador of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics to China, Dr. Yudin, arrived in Beijing by plane from Moscow on the afternoon of the 12th.

Those who went to the airport to welcome him included: Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government Wu Xiuquan, Director of the General Office Wang Bingnan, Special Commissioner of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department Liu Jingyu, Director of the Protocol Department Wang Zhuoru, Deputy Director Wang Tuo, as well as Vice Mayor of Beijing Zhang Youyu, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command Zeng Mei, and Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Qian Junrui. Diplomatic envoys and diplomatic personnel from various countries stationed in China, Chargé d'affaires of the Soviet Embassy in China Vaskov, and all members of the embassy also went to welcome him.

### 苏联新任驻我国大使尤金博士到达北京

1953-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联新任驻我国大使尤金博士到达北京
    【新华社十二日讯】苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟新任驻我国大使尤金博士在十二日下午乘飞机由莫斯科到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权、办公厅主任王炳南、苏联东欧司专员刘靖宇、交际处处长王倬如、副处长王拓、以及北京市人民政府副市长张友渔、京津卫戍司令部副参谋长曾美和中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞等。各国驻华外交使节和外交人员、苏联驻华大使馆临时代办华司考和全体馆员也前往欢迎。
