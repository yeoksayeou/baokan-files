Title: Indian President Prasad Receives Delegation from the Sino-Indian Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1953-12-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 12th: Indian President Rajendra Prasad received the delegation from the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, headed by Ding Xilin, at the Presidential Palace at 10:30 AM on the 10th. Chinese Ambassador to India Yuan Zhongxian accompanied the delegation. At noon, Prime Minister Nehru hosted a luncheon for the delegation from the Sino-Indian Friendship Association. Also present were Chinese Ambassador to India Yuan Zhongxian and Indian Ambassador to China N. Raghavan and his wife.

### 印度总统普拉沙德接见中印友协代表团

1953-12-13
第4版()
专栏：

    印度总统普拉沙德接见中印友协代表团
    【新华社新德里十二日电】印度共和国总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德十日上午十时三十分在总统府接见以丁西林团长为首的中印友好协会代表团。陪同代表团前往的有中国驻印度大使袁仲贤。中午，总理尼赫鲁招待中印友协代表团共进午餐。席间还有中国驻印度大使袁仲贤和印度驻中国大使赖嘉文及其夫人。
