Title: China's Minister of Health Li Dequan Invited to Visit India; Chinese Ambassador to India Holds Reception to Welcome Minister Li Dequan
Author:
Date: 1953-12-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 18th—Li Dequan, Minister of Health of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, was invited by Rajkumari Amrit Kaur, Minister of Health of the Republic of India, to visit India and arrived in New Delhi, the capital of India, by plane on the 15th, accompanied by Secretary Xue Gongchuo and Zhang Weixun.

Those who welcomed her at the airport included: the aide-de-camp to the President of India, the Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and the Director-General and Deputy Director-General of the Directorate General of Health Services of the Ministry of Health. Also present at the airport to welcome her were Yuan Zhongxian, the Chinese Ambassador to India, embassy staff, and representatives of overseas Chinese. Diplomatic envoys and officials from the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, and Hungary stationed in India also came to the airport to welcome her.

On the morning of the 16th, Li Dequan, accompanied by the Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Deputy Commissioner of Delhi State, and the Deputy Director-General of the Directorate General of Health Services, laid a wreath at Gandhi's cremation site.

On the same morning, Li Dequan paid separate calls on Dr. Prasad, the President of India, Dr. Radhakrishnan, the Vice President, Prime Minister Nehru, and Health Minister Kaur. During her meeting with Kaur, Li Dequan exchanged views on medical and health work and introduced the development of New China in these areas.

In the afternoon of the 16th, Li Dequan also visited Harding Women's Medical College and the Nursing College affiliated with the University of Delhi. On the evening of the 16th, Minister Li Dequan attended a banquet given by Minister Kaur. Yuan Zhongxian, the Chinese Ambassador to India, was also invited to attend.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 18th—Yuan Zhongxian, the Chinese Ambassador to India, held a reception on the evening of the 16th to welcome Li Dequan, Minister of Health of the Central People's Government of China, who was invited to visit India.

Among those invited to attend the reception were: Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan, Minister of Home Affairs Katju, Minister of Health Kaur, Secretary for External Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs R.K. Nehru, and diplomatic envoys and representatives from various countries stationed in India.

Also attending the reception were: Chairman of the All-India Peace Council Kitchlew, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the India-China Friendship Association Jian Chand, Secretary-General of the India-China Friendship Association Chatur Narain Malaviya, many Indian friends who had visited China in the past, people from the medical, cultural, educational, and journalistic circles of Delhi, social workers, and representatives of overseas Chinese, totaling approximately 500 people.

The reception proceeded in a warm and cordial atmosphere throughout.

### 我国卫生部部长李德全应邀到印度访问  我驻印大使举行招待会欢迎李德全部长

1953-12-20
第4版()
专栏：

    我国卫生部部长李德全应邀到印度访问
    我驻印大使举行招待会欢迎李德全部长
   【新华社新德里十八日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府卫生部部长李德全应印度共和国政府卫生部部长拉志库马里·阿姆里特·考尔的邀请访问印度，已在十五日偕秘书薛公绰、张炜逊乘飞机到达印度首都新德里。
    到机场欢迎的有：印度总统府副官、外交部交际司副司长、卫生部卫生总署署长和副署长等。到机场欢迎的还有我国驻印度大使袁仲贤和使馆人员及华侨代表等。苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利驻印度外交使节及外交官员也到机场欢迎。
    十六日上午，李德全由印度政府外交部交际司副司长、德里邦副专员和卫生总署副署长陪同，到甘地火葬处献花圈。
    同一天上午，李德全分别拜会了印度总统普拉沙德博士、副总统拉德哈克里希南博士、总理尼赫鲁和卫生部部长考尔。李德全在拜会考尔时，曾和考尔交换有关医药卫生工作的意见，并介绍了新中国在这些方面的发展情况。
    十六日下午，李德全还参观了哈定女子医学院和德里大学附设的护士学院。十六日晚，李德全部长出席了考尔部长的宴会。我国驻印度大使袁仲贤也应邀参加。
    【新华社新德里十八日电】我国驻印度大使袁仲贤十六日晚举行招待会，欢迎应邀到印度访问的我中央人民政府卫生部部长李德全。
    应邀出席招待会的有：印度副总统拉德哈克里希南、内政部部长卡特朱、卫生部部长考尔、外交部外事秘书拉·库·尼赫鲁，以及各国驻印度使节和代表。
    出席招待会的还有：全印和平理事会主席克其鲁，印中友好协会执行委员会主席吉安·昌德、印中友好协会秘书长查图尔·纳拉茵·马拉维亚，历次访问中国的许多印度友人，德里医学界、文教界、新闻界人士和社会工作者，以及华侨代表等共约五百人。
    招待会始终在热烈亲切的气氛中进行。
