Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-12-04
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    This is a ceramic handicraft produced in Shiwan, Nanhai County, Guangdong Province. This handicraft captures the distinct features and dynamics of the quail, showing its foraging posture. From this work, we can also see the author's skillful technique and deep observation of animals. It is one of the excellent works displayed in this National Folk Art and Craft Exhibition.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    这是广东省南海县石湾出品的一种陶制工艺品。这一工艺品抓住了鹌鹑的鲜明特征和动态，表现出鹌鹑觅食的神情。从这一作品中也可以看出作者熟练的技巧和对动物深刻的观察力。它是这次全国民间美术工艺品展览会中陈列的优秀作品之一。
