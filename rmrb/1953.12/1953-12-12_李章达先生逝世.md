Title: Mr. Li Zhangda Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1953-12-12
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 11th: Mr. Li Zhangda, a member of the Central People's Government, member of the Central and South Administrative Committee, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Representatives Conference Consultative Committee, member of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, member of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League, and Director of the Southern General Branch of the China Democratic League, passed away in Guangzhou on the 9th at 19:42 due to incurable liver cancer, at the age of 63. After Mr. Li Zhangda's death, Feng Baiju and Zhang Wen, Vice Chairmen of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, Zhu Guang, Wei Jinfei, and Huang Changshui, Vice Mayors of the Guangzhou Municipal People's Government, and representatives from various democratic parties in South China went to pay their respects. Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government sent special telegrams of condolence to Mr. Li's family upon hearing the news. The funeral committee for Mr. Li Zhangda, consisting of 24 members including Ye Jianying, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Lin Boqu, Shen Junru, Dong Biwu, Deng Zihui, He Xiangning, Li Weihan, Tan Zheng, Tao Zhu, Cheng Qian, Zhang Nanxian, Li Xiannian, Chen Mingshu, Zhao Yimin, Zhang Zhiyi, Chen Rutang, Li Da, Chen Manyuan, He Wei, Feng Baiju, Chen Qiyou, and Li Renren, was established on the 10th.
    Mr. Li Zhangda followed Mr. Sun Yat-sen in revolutionary activities in his early years and actively participated in the democratic movement during the Anti-Japanese War and the Third Domestic Revolutionary War. Mr. Li had been suffering from stomach problems for the past two years and developed liver cancer in July and August this year. He passed away despite medical treatment.

### 李章达先生逝世

1953-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    李章达先生逝世
    【新华社广州十一日电】中央人民政府委员、中南行政委员会委员、广东省人民政府副主席、广东省各界人民代表会议协商委员会副主席、中国国民党革命委员会中央委员、中国民主同盟中央委员、中国民主同盟南方总支部主任委员李章达于九日十九时四十二分因肝癌病不治在广州逝世，享年六十三岁。李章达先生病逝后，广东省人民政府副主席冯白驹、张文，广州市人民政府副市长朱光、魏今非、黄长水和华南各民主党派代表都前往吊唁。中央人民政府毛泽东主席、周恩来总理闻讯后均特电李委员家属致唁。李章达先生治丧委员会由叶剑英、李济深、张澜、林伯渠、沈钧儒、董必武、邓子恢、何香凝、李维汉、谭政、陶铸、程潜、张难先、李先念、陈铭枢、赵毅敏、张执一、陈汝棠、李达、陈漫远、何伟、冯白驹、陈其尤、李任仁等二十四人组成，已在十日成立。
    李章达先生早年追随孙中山先生进行革命活动，抗日战争及第三次国内革命战争中努力参加民主运动。先生两年以来即患胃病，今年七、八月间又患肝癌病，医治无效，不幸逝世。
