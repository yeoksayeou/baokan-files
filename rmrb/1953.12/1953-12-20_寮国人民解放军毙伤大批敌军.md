Title: Lao People's Liberation Army Kills and Wounds a Large Number of Enemy Troops
Author:
Date: 1953-12-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 19th: According to Vietnam News Agency on the 18th, the Lao People's Liberation Army crushed the enemy's "mopping-up" operations in the central region.

　　On December 5th, the enemy concentrated its forces to "mop up" the Lao guerrilla area 80 kilometers northeast of Saravane Province in central Laos. The Lao People's Liberation Army and guerrillas immediately launched a fierce counterattack against the enemy on the highway and in the villages, annihilating 30 enemy troops, including a major officer, and wounding a large number of enemy troops. This enemy force was forced to retreat on December 7th.

### 寮国人民解放军毙伤大批敌军

1953-12-20
第4版()
专栏：

    寮国人民解放军毙伤大批敌军
    【新华社十九日讯】据越南通讯社十八日讯：寮国人民解放军在中部地区粉碎敌军的“扫荡”。
    十二月五日，敌军集中兵力“扫荡”寮国中部沙拉湾省会东北八十公里处寮国的游击区。寮国人民解放军和游击队当即对公路上和村子里的敌军发动猛烈的反击，歼灭敌军三十名，其中有一名是少校军官，并击伤大批敌军。这股敌军已被迫在十二月七日撤退。
