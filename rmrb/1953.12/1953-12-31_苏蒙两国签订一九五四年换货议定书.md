Title: Soviet-Mongolian Agreement Signed on Goods Exchange Protocol for 1954
Author:
Date: 1953-12-31
Page: 3

    Soviet-Mongolian Agreement Signed on Goods Exchange Protocol for 1954
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th] According to TASS, after negotiations conducted in a friendly atmosphere of mutual understanding between the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Trade and the Trade Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, a goods exchange protocol for 1954 was signed on the 26th.
    The protocol stipulates that in 1954, the Soviet Union will supply the Mongolian People's Republic with grain and consumer goods, petroleum products, automobiles, and other equipment. The Mongolian People's Republic will supply the Soviet Union with livestock, wool, butter, leather, and other animal products.

### 苏蒙两国签订一九五四年换货议定书

1953-12-31
第3版()
专栏：

    苏蒙两国签订一九五四年换货议定书
    【新华社三十日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联对外贸易部和蒙古人民共和国贸易代表团，在友好和互相谅解的气氛中进行谈判后，已于二十六日签订了一九五四年换货议定书。
    议定书规定，一九五四年苏联将供给蒙古人民共和国粮食和消费品、石油产品、汽车和其他设备。蒙古人民共和国将供给苏联牲畜、羊毛、牛油、皮革和其他畜产品。
