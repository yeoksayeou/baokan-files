Title: Newly Appointed Danish Minister Greve Pays Courtesy Call on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1953-12-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 11th: The newly appointed Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Denmark to China, Mr. Greve, accompanied by the Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Danish Legation in China, Mr. Kristensen, paid a courtesy call on Mr. Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, on the afternoon of the 11th to discuss matters related to the presentation of credentials. Present were: Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Huan Xiang, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 丹麦新任公使格瑞杰生拜会周恩来外长

1953-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    丹麦新任公使格瑞杰生
    拜会周恩来外长
      【新华社十一日讯】丹麦王国新任驻我国特命全权公使格瑞杰生偕丹麦王国驻华公使馆临时代办克里斯顿生，于十一日下午拜会我中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座的有：外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航、西欧非洲司司长宦乡和交际处处长王倬如。
