Title: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Appoints Five Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers
Author:
Date: 1953-12-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 21st. TASS, Moscow, December 21st: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR hereby appoints: 1. Ma. Za. Saburov, Chairman of the State Planning Committee of the USSR, as Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR; 2. Mi. Ge. Pervukhin, Minister of Power Stations and Electrical Industry, as Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR; 3. I. Fe. Tevosian, Minister of the Metallurgical Industry, as Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR; 4. Vi. Ya. Malyshev, Minister of Medium Machine Building Industry, as Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR; 5. A. Ni. Kosygin, Minister of the Consumer Goods Industry of the USSR, as Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR.

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团  任命五名部长会议副主席

1953-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团
    任命五名部长会议副主席
   【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十一日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团兹任命：一、苏联国家计划委员会主席马·扎·萨布罗夫为苏联部长会议副主席；二、电站和电机工业部部长米·格·别尔乌辛为苏联部长会议副主席；三、冶金工业部部长伊·费·捷沃西安为苏联部长会议副主席；四、中型机器制造工业部部长维·亚·马利歇夫为苏联部长会议副主席；五、苏联日用品工业部部长阿·尼·柯西金为苏联部长会议副主席。
