Title: Ye Jizhuang Arrives in Moscow to Participate in Sino-Soviet Trade Negotiations for Next Year
Author:
Date: 1953-12-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow. Ye Jizhuang, head of the Chinese trade delegation to the Soviet Union for the 1954 Sino-Soviet trade negotiations, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government, and Minister of Foreign Trade, arrived in Moscow by plane on the 24th.
    Welcoming him at the airport were: Borisov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union, Kuzmin, member of the Ministry Board, and Migunov, Commercial Representative of the Soviet Union to the People's Republic of China, and others.
    Ge Baoquan, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, Xu Dashen and Yi Qintang, Commercial Counselors, as well as Jiang Ming, deputy head of the Chinese trade delegation who arrived in the Soviet Union earlier, and members of the delegation, also went to the airport to greet him.

### 参加中苏明年贸易谈判  叶季壮到达莫斯科

1953-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    参加中苏明年贸易谈判
    叶季壮到达莫斯科
   【新华社莫斯科讯】到苏联参加中苏一九五四年贸易谈判的我国商务代表团团长、中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会副主任兼对外贸易部部长叶季壮，二十四日乘飞机到达莫斯科。
    到机场欢迎的有：苏联对外贸易部副部长波里索夫、部务委员会委员库兹明，苏联驻中华人民共和国商务代表米古诺夫等。
    我国驻苏联大使馆临时代办戈宝权，商务参赞徐达深、衣钦堂，以及先期到苏联的我国商务代表团副团长江明和代表团人员，也到机场迎接。
