Title: Albanian Labor Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session; Hoxha Delivers Report on Measures to Further Improve the Living Standards of Working People
Author:
Date: 1953-12-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, December 27th: According to TASS, the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party held a plenary session on the 24th and 25th.

    The plenary session listened to and discussed the report made by Enver Hoxha, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Labor Party, on the measures taken by the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Labor Party to further improve the living standards of the working people. The meeting also discussed the measures recently taken by the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Labor Party and the Albanian government to improve the living standards of the urban and rural working people, and stipulated the tasks for further development of the national economy.
    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana: According to TASS, the National Committee of the Albanian Democratic Front held a meeting on the 26th. The meeting listened to a report by M. Shtulla, Deputy General Secretary of the Democratic Front, on the upcoming local People's Council elections and the tasks of the Democratic Front. After discussing the report, the meeting adopted a letter to the people. The letter to the people pointed out that this election will be held at a time when the working people of the whole country are stepping up their efforts to realize the Five-Year Plan (1951-1955) and preparing to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the liberation of the motherland (November 29, 1954). The National Committee of the Democratic Front called on all voters to vote for the candidates of the Democratic Front and elect the most outstanding sons and daughters of their country to the local People's Council.

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央举行全体会议  霍查作关于进一步改善劳动人民生活水平措施的报告

1953-12-29
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央举行全体会议
    霍查作关于进一步改善劳动人民生活水平措施的报告
    【新华社二十八日讯】据塔斯社地拉那二十七日讯：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会在二十四日和二十五日举行了全体会议。
    全体会议听取并讨论了劳动党中央委员会总书记恩·霍查所作的劳动党中央委员会政治局关于进一步改善劳动人民生活水平的措施的报告。会议还讨论了劳动党中央委员会政治局和阿尔巴尼亚政府最近所采取的关于提高城乡劳动人民生活水平的措施，并规定了进一步发展国民经济的任务。
   【新华社二十八日讯】据塔斯社地拉那讯：阿尔巴尼亚民主阵线全国委员会二十六日举行了会议。会议听取了民主阵线副总书记米·什图拉所作的关于即将举行的地方人民会议选举和民主阵线的任务的报告。会议在讨论报告后，通过了告人民书。告人民书中指出，这次选举将在全国劳动人民为实现五年计划（一九五一—一九五五年）而加紧努力的时候，在准备迎接祖国解放十周年（一九五四年十一月二十九日）的时候举行的。民主阵线全国委员会号召全体选民投民主阵线候选人的票，把自己国家最优秀的儿女选入地方人民会议。
