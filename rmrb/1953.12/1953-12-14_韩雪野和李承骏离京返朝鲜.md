Title: Han Xueye and Li Chengjun Depart Beijing to Return to North Korea
Author:
Date: 1953-12-14
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 12th: Han Xueye, head of the North Korean delegation and Chairman of the Korean National Committee for the Defense of Peace, and delegation member Li Chengjun, who passed through our country after attending the Vienna World Peace Council meeting, left Beijing on the evening of the 11th to return to their country. Wu Maosun, Deputy Secretary-General of the China Peace Committee, and others went to the station to see them off. Choi Il, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to our country, also went to the station to see them off.

### 韩雪野和李承骏离京返朝鲜

1953-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    韩雪野和李承骏离京返朝鲜
    【新华社十二日讯】出席维也纳世界和平理事会会议后路过我国的朝鲜代表团团长、朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会委员长韩雪野和团员李承骏已在十一日晚离京返国。前往车站欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副秘书长吴茂荪等多人；前往车站送行的还有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国特命全权大使崔一。
