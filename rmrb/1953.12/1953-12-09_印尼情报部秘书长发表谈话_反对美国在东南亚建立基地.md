Title: Indonesian Intelligence Department Secretary-General Speaks Out Against U.S. Building Bases in Southeast Asia
Author:
Date: 1953-12-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, December 5th: Indonesian Intelligence Department Secretary-General Ruslan Abdulgani, currently in Karachi, told reporters that Indonesia opposes the establishment of military bases in Southeast Asia. He condemned the military agreement signed by the United States, New Zealand, and Australia. He said: "The question of Indonesia joining any military alliance is completely out of the question."

### 印尼情报部秘书长发表谈话  反对美国在东南亚建立基地

1953-12-09
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼情报部秘书长发表谈话
    反对美国在东南亚建立基地
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社喀喇蚩五日讯：目前正在喀喇蚩的印度尼西亚情报部秘书长鲁斯兰·阿卜杜拉·加尼对记者说：印尼反对在东南亚建立军事基地。他谴责美国和新西兰、澳大利亚签订的军事协定。他说：“印尼参加任何军事联盟的事情，是根本谈不到的。”
