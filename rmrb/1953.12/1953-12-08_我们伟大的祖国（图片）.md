Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-12-08
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Preparations for the winter repairs of the Bo Mountain Reservoir on the Huai River are in full swing. A large number of materials needed for winter construction are being continuously transported to Huangshanpo Station along the Beijing-Hankou Railway. This is a scene of porters loading newly unloaded cement onto trucks, ready to be transported to the construction site.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    淮河薄山水库的冬修准备工作，正在积极进行。大批冬季施工所需的物资正沿着京汉路源源运到黄山坡车站。这是搬运工人把刚卸车的水泥装上汽车，准备运往工地。
