Title: PLA Armored Corps Holds First Congress of Model Heroes; Commander-in-Chief Zhu De Attends and Gives Important Instructions
Author:
Date: 1953-12-13
Page: 1

    PLA Armored Corps Holds First Congress of Model Heroes
    Commander-in-Chief Zhu De Attends and Gives Important Instructions
    [Xinhua News Agency, December 12th] The first Congress of Model Heroes of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Armored Corps convened in Beijing on the 5th, lasting seven days, and victoriously concluded on the 11th. At the opening of the congress, Commander-in-Chief Zhu De personally attended and gave important instructions.

    A total of one hundred and thirty-three representatives participated in the congress, along with thirty-five model heroes of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Armored Corps who were invited to attend. They had established meritorious service in learning military technology, eliminating vehicle accidents, and in the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea. Among them were Xu Xuelin, a tank driver who safely drove for four hundred and seventy motor hours without any accidents; Han Yonglin, an electrical repairman who actively studied technology at work, used old parts to repair vehicles, and saved a large amount of state funds; Yang Aru, the vehicle commander of the Volunteer Army’s 215th Merit Citation Vehicle, a special-class hero and first-class merit recipient, who destroyed five enemy tanks, damaged one enemy tank, and destroyed seven enemy machine-gun positions, twenty bunkers, and seven small cannons; and Ouyang Zhonggui, a Volunteer Army tank driver and second-class merit recipient who never had an accident.

    At the opening of the congress, Xu Guangda, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Armored Corps, delivered the opening speech. After reporting on the achievements made in building the armored corps, he pointed out: Our armored units must be built even better, becoming a powerful and modern armored force. Therefore, we must completely eliminate vehicle accidents and humbly learn technology and good experiences. Model heroes are the backbone of the armored corps and must lead the masses to advance together.

    Commander-in-Chief Zhu attended the opening ceremony and gave important instructions at the meeting, which was warmly welcomed by the representatives.

    Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, also gave a report to the representatives at the opening. During the meeting, the representatives also listened to Xiang Zhonghua, Deputy Political Commissar of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Armored Corps, giving a summary report on launching meritorious work to eliminate vehicle accidents, and Sha Feng, Minister of the Technical Department, giving a summary report on technical work to eliminate vehicle accidents. The representatives enthusiastically discussed these reports and shared their experiences with each other. During the meeting, the representatives also visited vehicle yard service and driving demonstration exercises.

    At the closing ceremony, Xu Guangda, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Armored Corps, gave a concluding report on the congress and presented awards to the representatives of model units and model heroes who attended. Finally, Deputy Commander Nie Heting delivered the closing speech.

    During the meeting, the representatives also toured Beijing's historical sites and were respected by the people of the capital. The representatives expressed their determination to resolutely implement Commander-in-Chief Zhu's instructions and strive to build a strong and modern people's armored force.

### 解放军装甲兵举行首届功臣模范代表大会  朱德总司令到会作了重要指示

1953-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军装甲兵举行首届功臣模范代表大会
    朱德总司令到会作了重要指示
    【新华社十二日讯】中国人民解放军装甲兵首届功臣模范代表大会五日在北京召开，历时七天，于十一日胜利闭幕。大会开幕时，朱德总司令曾亲自到会作了重要指示。
    参加大会的代表共一百三十三名，还有应邀参加大会的中国人民志愿军装甲兵功臣模范代表三十五名。他们在学习军事技术、消灭车辆事故和抗美援朝斗争中建立了功勋。其中有安全行驶了四百七十个摩托小时没有发生事故的坦克驾驶员许薛林，有工作上积极钻研技术、利用旧品修理车辆，为国家节省大批资财的电气修理工韩永林，有击毁敌人坦克五辆、击伤坦克一辆、摧毁敌人七个机枪阵地、二十个地堡和小炮七门的志愿军二一五号特等功臣车车长、一等功臣杨阿如，还有从未发生事故的志愿军坦克驾驶员、二等功臣欧阳忠桂。
    大会开幕时，中国人民解放军装甲兵司令员许光达致开幕词。他在报告了建设装甲兵中所取得的成绩后指出：我们装甲部队，必须建设得更好，成为一支强大的、现代化的装甲部队。因此，要彻底消灭车辆事故，虚心学习技术和好的经验。功臣模范是装甲部队的骨干，一定要带领群众一齐前进。
    朱总司令参加了开幕式，并在会上作了重要指示，受到了代表们的热烈欢迎。
    中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任甘泗淇也在开幕时向代表们作了报告。会议期间，代表们还听取了中国人民解放军装甲兵副政治委员向仲华作的开展消灭车辆事故立功工作总结报告，和技术部部长沙风作的消灭车辆事故技术工作总结报告。代表们都热烈地讨论了这些报告，并互相介绍了经验。会议期间代表们还参观了车场勤务和驾驶示范演习。
    闭幕时，中国人民解放军装甲兵司令员许光达作了大会总结报告，并授奖给到会的模范单位代表和功臣模范。最后由副司令员聂鹤亭致闭幕词。
    会议期间，代表们还游览了北京的名胜古迹，受到了首都人民的敬爱。代表们纷纷表示坚决执行朱总司令的指示，为建设一支强大的、现代化的人民装甲部队而努力。
