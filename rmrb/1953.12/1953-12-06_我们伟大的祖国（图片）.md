Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-12-06
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The art staff of the state-owned Tianjin Printing and Dyeing Factory delve into the folk arts and collect patterns from nature, striving to improve the designs of printed cloth in order to meet the needs of the people. These two printed pattern staff members came to Beijing from Tianjin and are sketching in the flower garden of the Western Suburban Park, preparing to draw new printed cloth patterns.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营天津印染厂的美术工作人员深入民间，并向自然界搜集图案，努力改进花布的花样，以满足人民的需要。这两位印染图案工作人员，从天津来到北京，在西郊公园花苑内写生，准备绘制新的花布图案。
