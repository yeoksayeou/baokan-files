Title: Anshan Iron and Steel Company's Entire Staff Reports to Chairman Mao on the Commencement of Three Major Projects; Chairman Mao Sends a Reply Congratulating the Staff of Anshan Iron and Steel Company
Author:
Date: 1953-12-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, December 25th: The entire staff of Anshan Iron and Steel Company wrote a letter to Chairman Mao Zedong, reporting the successful commencement of the Seamless Steel Tube Plant, the Large Rolling Mill, and the No. 7 Blast Furnace. The full text of the original letter is as follows:
	Respected Chairman Mao Zedong:
	As the first year of the first National Five-Year Plan is about to be successfully completed, we are excited to report to you that the Seamless Steel Tube Plant, the Large Rolling Mill, and the No. 7 Blast Furnace have all been completed ahead of schedule and have begun production. The three major projects assigned to us by the state have been successfully completed.
	The successful early completion of the three major projects in Anshan shows that under your wise leadership and the brilliant illumination of the Party's general line, with the full support of the people of the whole country, and with the great assistance of the Soviet government and the enthusiastic guidance of Soviet experts, as long as we continuously improve our awareness, improve our technical level, and give full play to our initiative and creativity to study and work diligently, we can overcome various difficulties encountered in the construction process and successfully and gradually complete the great cause of socialist industrialization of our motherland.
	Respected Chairman Mao, we know that the successful commencement of the Large Rolling Mill, the Seamless Steel Tube Plant, and the No. 7 Blast Furnace is only the beginning of our country's first Five-Year Plan. We are faced with complex and more arduous construction tasks. We must unswervingly follow your teachings, avoid arrogance and impetuosity, be firm and humble, further learn from the Soviet Union, strive to improve our technical and management levels, and continuously improve the ideological awareness of all workers, so as to strive for the victorious realization of Anshan Iron and Steel's entire Five-Year Plan and the realization of our country's first Five-Year Plan.
	Respected Chairman Mao, all the staff of Anshan Iron and Steel Company, with the utmost sincerity of loving the Communist Party and loving the motherland, assure you that in order to implement the state's general line during the transition period, and in order to gradually build our motherland into a great and prosperous socialist society, we will certainly complete all the tasks assigned to us by the motherland. We will respond to your call with greater and more victories in production and construction.
	Long live the victory of the country's socialist industrialization!
	We respectfully wish you a long life!
	The Entire Staff of Anshan Iron and Steel Company
		December 21, 1953

Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, December 25th: After the entire staff of Anshan Iron and Steel Company wrote a letter to Chairman Mao Zedong, reporting the successful commencement of the Seamless Steel Tube Plant, the Large Rolling Mill, and the No. 7 Blast Furnace, they immediately received a reply from Chairman Mao Zedong, extending warm congratulations and heartfelt thanks to all the staff who participated in these three projects, all the staff of Anshan Iron and Steel Company, and all the Soviet comrades who helped with the construction of Anshan; and expressing the hope that they will continue their efforts and strive for greater achievements. The full text of the reply is as follows:
	Comrades of the Entire Staff of Anshan Iron and Steel Company:
	Your letter of December 21, 1953, has been received.
	The early completion and commencement of production of the Anshan Seamless Steel Tube Plant, the Anshan Large Rolling Mill, and the Anshan No. 7 Blast Furnace are major events in the development of China's heavy industry in 1953. I extend warm congratulations and heartfelt thanks to all the staff who participated in these three projects, all the staff of Anshan Iron and Steel Company, and all the Soviet comrades who helped with the construction of Anshan. The people of our country are now united as one, striving to realize the socialist industrialization of our country, and your heroic labor is a major contribution to this goal. I hope you will continue your efforts, learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union, give full play to your wisdom and strength, and strive for greater achievements.
	Mao Zedong
		December 25, 1953

### 鞍钢全体职工向毛主席报告三大工程开工  毛主席复电祝贺鞍钢职工

1953-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    鞍钢全体职工向毛主席报告三大工程开工
    毛主席复电祝贺鞍钢职工
   【新华社鞍山二十五日电】鞍山钢铁公司全体职工写信给毛泽东主席，报告无缝钢管厂、大型轧钢厂和第七号炼铁炉三项工程胜利开工。原信全文如下：敬爱的毛泽东主席：
    当此第一个国家五年建设计划的第一年计划即将顺利完成之际，我们兴奋地向您报告：无缝钢管厂、大型轧钢厂、第七号炼铁炉，都已相继提前竣工，并开始生产，国家交给我们的三大工程任务，已经胜利完成了。
    鞍山三大工程的胜利提前完成，表明了我们在您的英明领导和党的总路线的光辉照耀下，在全国人民的全力支援下，在苏联政府伟大的援助和苏联专家热诚指导下，只要我们不断地提高觉悟，提高技术水平，发挥高度的积极性与创造性来勤奋地学习与劳动，我们可以克服在建设过程中碰到的各种困难，胜利地逐步完成我们祖国社会主义工业化的伟大事业。
    敬爱的毛主席，我们知道大型轧钢厂、无缝钢管厂、第七号炼铁炉的胜利开工，仅仅是我国第一个五年建设计划的开端，摆在我们面前的是复杂和更艰巨的建设任务，我们一定要坚定不移地遵循着您的教导，不骄不躁，坚韧谦虚，进一步向苏联学习，努力提高技术和管理水平，不断提高全体工人的思想觉悟，为胜利地实现鞍钢的五年全部建设计划，实现我国第一个五年建设计划而奋斗。
    敬爱的毛主席，我们鞍山钢铁公司全体职工，谨以热爱共产党、热爱祖国的至诚，向您保证：为了执行国家在过渡时期的总路线，为了将我们的祖国逐步建设成为一个伟大的、富强的社会主义社会，我们一定完成祖国所赋予的一切任务，我们要以更大的、更多的生产和建设上的胜利来回答您的号召。
    国家的社会主义工业化胜利万岁！
    敬祝您万寿无疆！
    鞍山钢铁公司全体职工    一九五三年十二月二十一日
   【新华社鞍山二十五日电】鞍山钢铁公司全体职工在写信给毛泽东主席，报告无缝钢管厂、大型轧钢厂和第七号炼铁炉三项工程胜利开工后，顷收到毛泽东主席的复电，向参加这三项工程的全体职工、鞍山钢铁公司全体职工和帮助鞍山建设事业的全体苏联同志，致以热烈的祝贺和深切的感谢；并希望他们继续努力，争取更大的成就。复电全文如下：鞍山钢铁公司全体职工同志们：
    你们一九五三年十二月二十一日的来信收到了。
    鞍山无缝钢管厂、鞍山大型轧钢厂和鞍山第七号炼铁炉的提前完成建设工程并开始生产，是一九五三年我国重工业发展中的巨大事件。我向参加这三项工程的全体职工、鞍山钢铁公司全体职工和帮助鞍山建设事业的全体苏联同志致以热烈的祝贺和深切的感谢。我国人民现正团结一致，为实现我国的社会主义工业化而奋斗，你们的英勇劳动就是对于这一目标的重大贡献。希望你们继续努力，学习苏联先进经验，发挥你们的智慧和力量，争取更大的成就。
    毛泽东  一九五三年十二月二十五日
