Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Banquet for German Art Song and Dance Troupe
Author:
Date: 1953-12-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 27th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government hosted a reception on the evening of the 27th to entertain all members of the National People's Art Song and Dance Troupe of the German Democratic Republic, who were invited to visit and perform in our country. Mr. Koenig, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, and diplomatic personnel from the embassy were also invited to attend the reception.

Attending the reception as escorts were Xi Zhongxun, Shen Yanbing, Qian Junrui, Zhang Hanfu, Zhou Yang, Liu Zhiming, Wu Han, Fan Changjiang, Hong Shen, and over a hundred well-known figures from the fields of literature, film, drama, music, dance, fine arts, and folk art. At the banquet, Premier Zhou Enlai and the head of the Art Song and Dance Troupe, Pischner, delivered speeches successively. At the meeting, the hosts and guests raised their glasses to the friendship between China and Germany, to the President of the German Democratic Republic Wilhelm Pieck, to the leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong, and to the health of the Soviet party and government leaders headed by Malenkov.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 周总理设宴招待德艺术歌舞团

1953-12-28
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理设宴招待德艺术歌舞团
    【新华社二十七日讯】中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来在二十七日晚举行酒会，招待应邀来我国访问演出的德意志民主共和国国家人民艺术歌舞团全体人员。德意志民主共和国驻我国大使柯尼希和大使馆外交人员也应邀出席了酒会。
    出席酒会作陪的有习仲勋、沈雁冰、钱俊瑞、章汉夫、周扬、刘芝明、吴晗、范长江、洪深，以及文学、电影、戏剧、音乐、舞蹈、美术、曲艺界的著名人士等百余人。宴会上，周恩来总理和艺术歌舞团团长皮什纳曾相继致词。会上宾主并举杯为中德两国的友谊、为德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克、为中国人民领袖毛泽东主席、为以马林科夫为首的苏联党政领袖们的健康干杯。
    宴会在热烈、友好的空气中进行。
