Title: Korean Supreme People's Assembly to Hold Sixth Session
Author: 
Date: 1953-12-06
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 4th, Pyongyang: The Korean Central News Agency broadcast a decree from the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea stating: The Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea will convene its sixth session in Pyongyang on December 20, 1953.

### 朝鲜最高人民会议将举行第六届会议

1953-12-06
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜最高人民会议将举行第六届会议
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社平壤四日讯：朝鲜中央通讯社播发了朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会的政令说：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议将于一九五三年十二月二十日在平壤召开第六届会议。
