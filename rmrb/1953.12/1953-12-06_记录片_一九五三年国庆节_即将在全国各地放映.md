Title: Documentary Film "National Day, 1953" to be Shown Throughout the Country

Author: 
Date: 1953-12-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency:
    The newsreel special "National Day, 1953," produced by the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio, has now been completed and will soon be shown throughout the country.
    The newsreel special "National Day, 1953" vividly and concisely reflects the great scenes of the celebration of the fourth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China in the capital on October 1, 1953. The production of this documentary film was faster than that of other films in the past, which is an improvement in the production of documentary films in our country.

### 记录片“一九五三年国庆节”即将在全国各地放映

1953-12-06
第3版()
专栏：

    记录片“一九五三年国庆节”即将在全国各地放映
    中央新闻记录电影制片厂摄制的“一九五三年国庆节”新闻特辑现已完成，即将在全国各地放映。
    “一九五三年国庆节”新闻特辑，生动而简练地反映了一九五三年十月一日在首都庆祝中华人民共和国第四届国庆节的各种伟大的场面。这部记录影片的制作比过去其他影片的制作都迅速，这是我国记录影片制作上的一种改进。  （新华社）
