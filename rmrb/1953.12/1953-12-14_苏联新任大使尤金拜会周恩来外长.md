Title: New Soviet Ambassador Yudin Calls on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1953-12-14
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 13th: The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics to China, Dr. Yudin, accompanied by Chargé d'Affaires Vasikov, paid a visit to Minister Zhou Enlai of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government on the afternoon of the 13th to discuss matters relating to the presentation of his credentials. Also present were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Director of the General Office Yan Baohang, Director of the Protocol Department Wang Zhuoru, and specialist of the Soviet and Eastern European Department Liu Jingyu.

### 苏联新任大使尤金拜会周恩来外长

1953-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联新任大使尤金
    拜会周恩来外长
    【新华社十三日讯】苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟新任驻我国特命全权大使尤金博士偕临时代办华司考，在十三日下午到中央人民政府外交部拜会周恩来部长，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座的还有外交部副部长伍修权，办公厅副主任阎宝航，交际处处长王倬如，苏联东欧司专员刘靖宇。
