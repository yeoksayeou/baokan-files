Title: China and Indonesia Sign Trade Agreement in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-12-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 30th: Communiqué on the Negotiations between the Economic Delegations of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Indonesia. Full text as follows:

The representatives of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Economic Delegation of the Government of the Republic of Indonesia, based on the common desire of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Indonesia to promote and develop direct trade relations between the two countries on the principle of equality and mutual benefit, held negotiations in Beijing from November 18th to 27th, 1953.

The Chinese side participating in the negotiations included: Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government; Lu Xuzhang, Director of the Third Bureau; Gao Shangneng, Commissioner; Zou Siyi; and Li Yingji, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation.

The Indonesian side participating in the negotiations included: Dr. Asmaun, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary and Head of the Economic Delegation of the Government of the Republic of Indonesia and Director of the Foreign Economic Liaison Department of the Ministry of Economy of the Republic of Indonesia; members: F. Salim, Section Chief of the Industrial and Commercial Section of the Economic and Legal Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Batilagajawani, Section Chief of the Planning Section of the Central Import Bureau of the Ministry of Economy; Sunazo, Deputy Director of the People's Agricultural Bureau of the Ministry of Agriculture; Hasyuno, Member of Parliament; and Daurani, Chairman of the Indonesian National Importers and Exporters Association.

The negotiations were conducted in a friendly atmosphere throughout, and on November 30th, the Trade Agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia was formally signed. This agreement stipulates the principles of trade relations between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia and exchanged lists of commonly exported commodities from both countries. Normal direct trade relations between the two countries will be developed on this successful foundation.

Xinhua News Agency, 30th: After friendly and harmonious discussions, the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Indonesia signed the Trade Agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia in Beijing on the morning of November 30th.

Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, signed on behalf of the Chinese side; Asmaun, Head of the Economic Delegation of the Indonesian Government, signed on behalf of the Indonesian side.

The Chinese side participating in the signing ceremony included: Ye Jizhuang, Vice Chairman of the Financial and Economic Committee of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department; Lu Xuzhang, Director of the Third Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade; Gao Shangneng, Commissioner; Zou Siyi; Yang Mian, Director of the Protocol Department; Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Li Yingji, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation.

The Indonesian side participating in the signing ceremony included: Mononutu, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Indonesia to China; members of the Economic Delegation of the Indonesian Government: F. Salim, Batilagajawani, Sunazo, Hasyuno, Daurani, Xu Mianqing, and Isa Marti, Counselor of the Indonesian Embassy in China, and others.

### 我国和印尼在京签订贸易协定

1953-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我国和印尼在京签订贸易协定
    【新华社三十日讯】中华人民共和国政府和印度尼西亚共和国政府经济代表团谈判公报。全文如下：
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府代表与印度尼西亚共和国政府经济代表团，基于中华人民共和国中央人民政府和印度尼西亚共和国政府在平等互利的原则下，促进和发展两国直接贸易关系的共同愿望，于一九五三年十一月十八日至二十七日在北京举行了谈判。
    中华人民共和国方面参加谈判的有：中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长雷任民、第三局局长卢绪章、专员高尚能、邹斯颐及中国进出口公司副经理李应吉。
    印度尼西亚共和国方面参加谈判的有：印度尼西亚共和国政府经济代表团特命全权大使衔团长、印度尼西亚共和国经济部国外经济联络署署长阿斯玛温博士，团员：外交部经济法律司工商科科长费节·沙林、经济部中央进口局计划科科长巴蒂拉渣哇尼、农业部人民农业局副局长苏纳佐、国会议员哈苏诺及印度尼西亚民族输出入商公会主席达务兰尼。
    谈判始终是在友好的空气中进行的，并在十一月三十日正式签订了中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国贸易协定。这个协定规定了中华人民共和国与印度尼西亚共和国贸易关系的原则，并交换了两国通常出口的商品货单。两国间的正常的直接贸易关系将在此成功的基础上获得发展。
    【新华社三十日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府与印度尼西亚共和国政府之间，经过友好融洽的商谈，于十一月三十日上午在北京签订了中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国贸易协定。
    代表中国方面签字的是中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长雷任民；代表印度尼西亚方面签字的是印度尼西亚政府经济代表团团长阿斯玛温。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的有：中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会副主任兼对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长章汉夫、亚洲司司长陈家康，对外贸易部第三局局长卢绪章、专员高尚能、邹斯颐、交际处处长杨勉，外交部交际处副处长王拓，中国进出口公司副经理李应吉。
    印度尼西亚方面参加签字仪式的有：印度尼西亚驻我国特命全权大使莫诺努图，印度尼西亚政府经济代表团团员：费节·沙林、巴蒂拉渣哇尼、苏纳佐、哈苏诺、达务兰尼、许绵清，印度尼西亚驻我国大使馆参赞伊沙·玛蒂等。
