Title: The Current Interpretation Work Shows Unfairness and Asymmetry; Our Side Demands the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission Take Corrective Measures
Author:
Date: 1953-12-13
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, December 12th) General Li Sang-cho, chief commissioner on the Korean-Chinese side of the Military Armistice Commission, wrote a letter to the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission on the 11th, requesting that it take decisive measures to implement the regulations on isolating prisoners of war, correct the current unfairness and asymmetry in the interpretation work of both sides, so that our side can resume interpretation work.

General Li Sang-cho pointed out: Currently, in the Songgok-ri prisoner of war camp, the US side is conducting interpretations to prisoners of war with full convenience and freedom. The captured US personnel listen quietly to the interpretations without making noise, and demand that the US interpreters give full interpretations. However, the US interpretation personnel force the subordinate bodies of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission and the Indian Custodial Forces to use coercive measures to force them to withdraw from the interpretation camp. In the Dongchang-ri prisoner of war camp, although the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission and the Indian Custodial Forces have arranged isolation camps at the repeated requests of the Korean-Chinese side, they still have not taken decisive measures to implement the regulations on isolating prisoners of war.

### 目前双方解释工作中存在着不公平不对等的情况  我方要求中立国遣返委员会采取纠正措施

1953-12-13
第4版()
专栏：

    目前双方解释工作中存在着不公平不对等的情况
    我方要求中立国遣返委员会采取纠正措施
    【新华社开城十二日电】军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员李相朝将军在十一日写信给中立国遣返委员会，要求采取坚决措施实施隔离战俘的规定，纠正目前双方解释工作中不公平不对等的情况，使我方得以恢复解释工作。
    李相朝将军指出：目前在松谷里战俘营中，美方在享有充分的便利和自由的条件下对战俘进行解释，美方被俘人员不吵不闹安静地听取解释，要求美方解释者给予充分的解释，但美方解释人员却迫使中立国遣返委员会附属机构和印度看管部队使用强制手段强迫他们退出解释营场。而在东场里战俘营，中立国遣返委员会和印度看管部队虽然在朝中方面的多次要求下已安排好隔离营场，但仍然没有采取坚决措施来实施隔离战俘的规定。
