Title: The Soviet Union Appoints P.F. Yudin as New Ambassador to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1953-12-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 3rd: TASS, Moscow, December 2nd: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has appointed Pavel Fedorovich Yudin as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the People's Republic of China.
    The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has relieved Vasily Vasilyevich Kuznetsov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, from his post as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the People's Republic of China.

### 苏联任命帕·费·尤金为新任驻我国大使

1953-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联任命帕·费·尤金为新任驻我国大使
    【新华社三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命帕维尔·费奥多罗维奇·尤金为苏联驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团解除苏联外交部副部长瓦西里·瓦西里耶维奇·库兹涅佐夫的苏联驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使的职务。
