Title: Two Million British Industrial Workers Hold General Strike
Author:
Date: 1953-12-04
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, London, December 3rd: The Confederation of Shipbuilding and Engineering Unions, with three million members, held a 24-hour national general strike on the 2nd in support of their long-standing demand for a 15 percent wage increase. More than two million workers participated in the strike, making it the largest industrial worker strike in Britain since the General Strike of 1926.
    The strike has halted production in thousands of factories across the country, bringing most major heavy industries to a standstill. Simultaneously with the strike, workers in various places held rallies and demonstrations.

### 英国二百万产业工人举行大罢工

1953-12-04
第4版()
专栏：

    英国二百万产业工人举行大罢工
    【新华社三日讯】伦敦消息：拥有三百万会员的造船工人及机械工业工人工会联合会在二日举行了二十四小时的全国总罢工，支持他们提出已久的增加百分之十五的工资的要求。参加罢工的工人在二百万以上，这是英国自一九二六年大罢工以来规模最大的一次产业工人罢工。
    罢工使全国各地成千的工厂断了生产，大部分主要重工业陷于停顿。在罢工的同时，各地工人们并举行集会和示威游行。
