Title: CPSU Central Committee Convened a Meeting of Local Newspaper Editors

Author:

Date: 1953-12-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 5th. TASS reports: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union convened a meeting in Moscow from November 19th to December 2nd for the editors of newspapers from various republics, border regions, and states who are responsible for agricultural reporting. N.S. Khrushchev and P.N. Pospelov, Secretaries of the CPSU Central Committee, both attended the meeting. The meeting heard and discussed the following reports: The CPSU Central Committee's September Plenary Session's measures for improving the Party's political work in rural areas; the tasks of newspapers in promoting the implementation of the CPSU Central Committee's "Resolution on Further Developing Soviet Agriculture"; measures to implement the CPSU Central Committee's decision on further developing Soviet animal husbandry; the tasks of further improving the work of machine tractor stations and strengthening their role in developing collective farm production; and the situation and development prospects of Soviet cotton planting.

The newspaper editors attending the meeting exchanged experiences in reporting and promoting the main tasks of agricultural development. Khrushchev addressed the meeting, discussing how to promote the implementation of the CPSU Central Committee's resolutions, and emphasized the important role of Soviet newspapers in realizing the Soviet Communist Party's and the government's measures for further developing agriculture.

### 苏共中央召开地方报纸编辑人员会议

1953-12-09
第4版()
专栏：

    苏共中央召开地方报纸编辑人员会议
    【新华社五日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会召集各共和国、各边疆区和州的报纸负责农业报道的编辑人员，于十一月十九日到十二月二日在莫斯科举行会议。苏共中央书记尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫、彼·纳·波斯伯洛夫都参加了会议。会议听取并讨论了下列报告：苏共中央九月全会关于改进党在乡村中的政治工作的措施；报纸在推动执行苏共中央“关于进一步发展苏联农业的措施的决议”中的任务；执行苏共中央关于进一步发展苏联饲畜业的决定的措施；进一步改进机器拖拉机站的工作并加强其在发展集体农庄生产中的作用的任务；苏联植棉业的情况及其发展远景。
    出席会议的各报编辑人员交流了他们在报道和宣传发展农业的主要任务的经验。赫鲁晓夫在会上致辞，论述了如何推动执行苏共中央决议的问题，并着重指出了苏联报纸在实现苏联共产党和政府关于进一步发展农业的措施中所起的重大作用。
