    Our Great Motherland
    Wangjiang Tower is located on the south bank of the Fu River under the Jiuyan Bridge in Chengdu, Sichuan Province, about three Chinese miles from the city. It is one of the famous scenic spots in Sichuan Province. This is the Chongli Pavilion of Wangjiang Tower after it was renovated by the Chengdu Municipal People's Government.

    我们伟大的祖国
