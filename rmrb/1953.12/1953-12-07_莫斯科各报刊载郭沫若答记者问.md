Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish Guo Moruo's Answers to Reporter's Questions
Author: 
Date: 1953-12-07
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 6th. Moscow newspapers on the 6th published the answers given by Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, to a Xinhua News Agency reporter regarding the American conspiracy to fabricate "atrocities" committed by our side against prisoners of war and civilians.
