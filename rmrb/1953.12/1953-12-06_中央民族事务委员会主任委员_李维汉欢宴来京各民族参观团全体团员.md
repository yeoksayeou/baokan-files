Title: Chairman of the State Ethnic Affairs Commission, Li Weihan, Hosts a Banquet for All Members of Visiting Ethnic Groups to Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1953-12-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 5th—Chairman of the State Ethnic Affairs Commission, Li Weihan, held a banquet in Beijing on December 3rd to welcome Mu Huaxi (Kazak), head of the visiting ethnic groups from the Gansu, Qinghai, and Xinjiang border regions; deputy heads Atongbek, Ni Hemaiti (both Kazak), Yang Sangjiabu (Mongol), and Danke (Tibetan); and Kong Sayiduo (Tibetan), head of the visiting ethnic groups from the Southwest; deputy heads Nie Wang Suona (Tibetan), Feng Xiuzhai (Dai), Luo Chengcai (Lahu), Jidi A'yue (Yi), Wang Youzhi (Buyi), and all members of the two visiting groups.
    Those attending the banquet included Vice-Chairmen of the State Ethnic Affairs Commission Ulanhu, Liu Geping, and Liu Chun, and members Zhu Zaoguan, Yang Jingren, Sa Kongliao, Fei Xiaotong, Ougen, as well as Director of the General Office Gan Chunlei, Deputy Director A'aisha, and others.
    Chairman Li Weihan spoke at the banquet. He extended a warm welcome to all members of the two visiting groups and wished them fruitful experiences during their visit. Li Weihan said: Our country's planned large-scale economic construction is actively underway. Our country's great economic construction is the common and highest fundamental interest of all our ethnic groups. The people of all our ethnic groups should cherish the unity of our motherland and the solidarity of all ethnic groups as if protecting our own eyes, and strive together to build a great family of our motherland. In economic construction, in all fraternal ethnic minority areas, the relevant higher-level people's governments will actively and to the best of their ability help fraternal ethnic groups improve and develop production, and gradually improve the material and cultural life of the fraternal ethnic groups. The work of helping to improve and develop the production of fraternal ethnic groups should be given a more important position.
    Mu Huaxi, head of the visiting ethnic groups from the Gansu, Qinghai, and Xinjiang border regions, and Kong Sayiduo, head of the visiting ethnic groups from the Southwest, spoke at the meeting successively. They expressed their deep gratitude for the warm reception and care they had received in Beijing. They also stated that when they return, they will definitely convey the gains they have made during their visit to the people of all ethnic groups, work hard to develop production, and jointly build a great family of the motherland.
    During the banquet, the guests frequently toasted to the health of the great leader of all ethnic groups, Chairman Mao.

### 中央民族事务委员会主任委员  李维汉欢宴来京各民族参观团全体团员

1953-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中央民族事务委员会主任委员
    李维汉欢宴来京各民族参观团全体团员
    【新华社五日讯】中央人民政府民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉十二月三日在京举行宴会，欢宴甘、青、新边境各民族参观团团长穆华希（哈萨克族）、副团长阿通伯克、倪合买提（均哈萨克族）、杨桑加布（蒙族）、丹科（藏族），西南各民族参观团团长孔萨益多（藏族）、副团长聂旺索纳（藏族）、俸秀斋（傣族）、罗成才（拉祜族）、吉狄阿约（彝族）、王由植（布依族）和两参观团的全体团员。
    出席宴会的有中央人民政府民族事务委员会副主任委员乌兰夫、刘格平、刘春，委员朱早观、杨静仁、萨空了、费孝通、欧根和办公厅主任甘春雷、副主任阿艾沙等。
    李维汉主任委员在会上讲话。他对两参观团的全体团员表示热烈欢迎，并祝他们在参观中有良好的收获。李维汉说：我们祖国有计划的大规模的经济建设正在积极进行中，我们祖国伟大的经济建设事业，是我们各民族人民共同的最高的根本利益，我们各民族人民应该像维护眼珠一样地维护我们祖国的统一和各民族的团结，共同为建设伟大祖国的大家庭而奋斗。在经济建设中，在一切兄弟民族地区，各有关的上级人民政府都将要积极地尽可能地帮助兄弟民族努力改进与发展生产，逐步改善兄弟民族人民的物质生活和文化生活。把帮助改进与发展兄弟民族生产的工作，提到更重要的地位。
    甘、青、新边境各民族参观团团长穆华希、西南各民族参观团团长孔萨益多先后在会上讲话。他们对在京受到的热烈招待和关怀表示深切感谢。他们并表示将来回去，一定要把参观中所得到的收获，很好地传达给各族人民，努力发展生产，共同建设伟大的祖国大家庭。
    席间，宾主频频为各民族人民伟大领袖毛主席的健康而干杯。
