Title: Finland's New Envoy to China, Mr. Sundström, calls on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1953-12-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 1st—Finland's new Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary to China, Mr. Sundström, called on Mr. Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, on the afternoon of the 1st, to discuss matters relating to the presentation of his credentials. Present were: Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Huan Xiang, Director of the Department of Western European and African Affairs; and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 芬兰新任驻我国公使孙士敦拜会周恩来外长

1953-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    芬兰新任驻我国公使孙士敦拜会周恩来外长
    【新华社一日讯】芬兰共和国新任驻我国特命全权公使孙士敦，于一日下午拜会我中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座的有：外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航、西欧非洲司司长宦乡和交际处处长王倬如。
