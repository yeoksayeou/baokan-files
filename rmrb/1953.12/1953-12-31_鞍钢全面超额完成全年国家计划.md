Date: 1953-12-31
Page: 1

    Anshan Iron and Steel Exceeds National Plan in All Respects
    [Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, December 30] Anshan Iron and Steel Company has comprehensively exceeded the 1953 national plan. As of December 25, the output of fourteen major products of Anshan Iron and Steel has exceeded the annual national plan output; among them, lean iron ore and rich iron ore exceeded by more than 16.8%, sintered ore exceeded by 15.7%, pig iron exceeded by 7.6%, steel ingots exceeded by 3.3%, medium-sized steel exceeded by 17.2%, and thin plates exceeded by 27.9%. The quality of various major products has reached or exceeded the indicators stipulated by the state, and has been significantly improved compared with last year. For example, the state stipulated that the qualified rate of high-quality steel should be 95.64% this year, but the actual qualified rate has reached 96.13%, which is 2.46% higher than last year. The company's cost plan for this year has also been completed relatively well, and it is estimated that it can save hundreds of billions of yuan for the country throughout the year, and labor productivity will also increase by more than 10% compared with the national plan.

### 鞍钢全面超额完成全年国家计划

1953-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    鞍钢全面超额完成全年国家计划
    【新华社鞍山三十日电】鞍山钢铁公司全面超额完成了一九五三年国家计划。到十二月二十五日止，鞍钢十四种主要产品的产量都超过了全年国家计划产量；其中贫铁矿和富铁矿超额百分之十六点八以上，烧结矿超额百分之十五点七，生铁超额百分之七点六，钢胚超额百分之三点三，中型钢材超额百分之十七点二，薄板超额百分之二十七点九。各种主要产品的质量都达到或超过了国家所规定的指标，并比去年有了显著提高。例如今年国家规定优质钢的合格率要有百分之九十五点六四，而实际上合格率已达到百分之九十六点一三，比去年提高了百分之二点四六。全公司今年的成本计划也完成得比较好，预计全年可为国家节省数百亿元，劳动生产率也将比国家计划提高百分之十以上。
