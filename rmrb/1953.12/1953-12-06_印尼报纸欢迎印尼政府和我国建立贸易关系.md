Title: Indonesian Newspapers Welcome the Establishment of Trade Relations Between the Indonesian Government and Our Country
Author:
Date: 1953-12-06
Page: 1
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, The Hague, December 3rd (TASS): The Dutch "Truth Daily" in Yogyakarta reports that Indonesian newspapers welcome the Indonesian government's decision to establish trade relations with the People's Republic of China.

　　"Civil Rights Daily" writes: "We are very happy about this decision. Having fallen into a difficult situation because the United States is unwilling to purchase Indonesian rubber, we hope to find new prospects, the road to which was previously blocked."

　　Surabaya's "Voice of Society" is satisfied to say that Indonesia has gained a market that "accounts for twenty-five percent of the world's population."

### 印尼报纸欢迎印尼政府和我国建立贸易关系

1953-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    印尼报纸欢迎印尼政府和我国建立贸易关系
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社海牙三日讯：荷兰“真理报”日惹消息，印度尼西亚各报欢迎印度尼西亚政府和中华人民共和国建立贸易关系的决定。
    “民权报”写道：“我们对这个决定，感到非常高兴。在陷入了因为美国不愿意购买印度尼西亚橡胶而起的困难处境中，我们希望找到新的前途，通往这一前途的道路过去是封锁着的。”
    泗水“社会呼声报”满意地说：印度尼西亚获得了一个“占全世界人口百分之二十五”的市场。
