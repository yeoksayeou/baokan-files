Title: Hungarian Ambassador to China, Szafaranko, Recalled to Home Country
Author:
Date: 1953-12-01
Page: 1

    Hungarian Ambassador to China, Szafaranko, Recalled to Home Country
    [Xinhua News Agency, 30th] Szafaranko, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, was recalled and departed from Beijing on the 30th to return to his home country. Those who went to the airport to see him off included: Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office; Liu Qingyu, Commissioner of the Soviet and Eastern European Department; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, and others. Diplomatic envoys of various countries to China and all members of the Embassy of the Hungarian People's Republic in China also went to see him off.

### 匈牙利驻我国大使夏法朗柯奉调返国

1953-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利驻我国大使夏法朗柯奉调返国
    【新华社三十日讯】匈牙利人民共和国首任驻华特命全权大使夏法朗柯奉调于三十日离京返国。前往机场送行的有：中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权，办公厅副主任阎宝航，苏联东欧司专员刘清宇，交际处处长王倬如等。各国驻华使节和匈牙利人民共和国驻华大使馆全体馆员也前往送行。
