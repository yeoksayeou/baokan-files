Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-12-19
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Peasants in various places are enthusiastically selling their surplus grain to the state, vividly demonstrating the peasants' patriotism. This is a scene of peasants from the sixth district of Yuanjiang County, Hunan Province, carrying surplus grain to the Xizhakou grain purchasing station for sale.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-12-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地农民纷纷把余粮卖给国家，生动地表现出农民的爱国主义精神。这是湖南省沅江县六区农民把余粮挑到西闸口购粮站去出售的情形。
