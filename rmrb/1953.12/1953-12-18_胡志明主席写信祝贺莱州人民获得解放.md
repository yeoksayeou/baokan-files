Title: President Ho Chi Minh Sends Letter of Congratulations on the Liberation of Lai Chau

Author:

Date: 1953-12-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 17th: The Vietnam News Agency reported on December 16th that Ho Chi Minh, President of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, wrote a congratulatory letter to the people and cadres of Lai Chau, an important town in the Northwest region of Vietnam, congratulating them on the liberation of Lai Chau. In his congratulatory letter, President Ho Chi Minh called on them to unite more closely, help the People's Army wipe out the remaining bandits and traitors, increase production, and ensure that the people of Lai Chau have enough food and clothing.

### 胡志明主席写信祝贺莱州人民获得解放

1953-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席写信祝贺莱州人民获得解放
    【新华社十七日讯】越南通讯社十六日讯：越南民主共和国政府胡志明主席写给越南西北地区重镇莱州的人民和干部一封祝贺信，祝贺莱州的解放。胡志明主席在贺信中号召他们更紧密地团结在一起，帮助人民军肃清残匪和卖国贼，并增加生产，保证莱州人民丰衣足食。
