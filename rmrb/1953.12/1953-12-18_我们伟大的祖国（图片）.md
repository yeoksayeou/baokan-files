Our Great Motherland
Adopting the Soviet advanced "pushing method" of navigation not only increases the speed by 5% to 20% compared to the single-row towing method, but also increases the carrying capacity, which is of great significance for saving transportation costs and completing the production increase plan. This is the scene of a tugboat of the Changjiang (Yangtze) River Shipping Administration pushing five barges downstream.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    采用苏联先进的“顶推法”航行，不仅比一列式拖带法增快百分之五到百分之二十的速度，而且又能增加载重量，对节约运输成本和完成增产计划有重大的意义。这是长江航运管理局的拖轮正顶推五艘驳船开往下游的情形。
