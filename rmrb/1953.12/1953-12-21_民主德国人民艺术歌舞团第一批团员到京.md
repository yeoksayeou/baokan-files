Title: First Group of Members of the German Democratic Republic People's Art Song and Dance Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-12-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 20th. The first group of seven members of the German Democratic Republic National People's Art Song and Dance Troupe, led by Hans Müller, the artistic director of the song and dance troupe, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 20th at the invitation of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement to visit China and perform.

Those who came to the airport to welcome them included Ren Hong, Vice President of the China Youth Art Theater, Zou Difan, Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's Administrative Council, Wu Xiling, representative of the Ministry of Culture, and many young literary and artistic workers from the Central Song and Dance Troupe who had visited the German Democratic Republic.

Also at the airport to welcome them were personnel from the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China.

### 民主德国人民艺术歌舞团第一批团员到京

1953-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    民主德国人民艺术歌舞团第一批团员到京
    【新华社二十日讯】根据中德文化合作协定，应邀来我国访问演出的德意志民主共和国国家人民艺术歌舞团第一批团员七人，在歌舞团艺术总指导汉斯·缪勒率领下，于二十日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国青年艺术剧院副院长任虹、中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局处长邹荻帆、文化部代表邬析零和曾到德意志民主共和国访问演出的中央歌舞团的青年文艺工作者多人。
    到机场欢迎的还有德意志民主共和国驻我国大使馆人员。
