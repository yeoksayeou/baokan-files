Title: The State Council Holds its 198th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 17th: The 198th meeting of the Central People's Government State Council was held on the afternoon of the 17th. The meeting heard a briefing by Liao Luyan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Central People's Government State Council, on the work of developing winter agricultural production. It then discussed and approved the "Instructions of the Central People's Government State Council on Developing Winter Agricultural Production." The meeting heard a briefing by Liu Ruilong, Vice Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Agriculture, on increasing the production of oil crops. It then discussed and approved the "Instructions of the Central People's Government State Council on Increasing the Production of Oil Crops." The meeting also heard a briefing by Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Foreign Trade, on the "Provisional Regulations on Inspection of Export and Import Commodities" and approved these regulations. Finally, the meeting approved various appointment and dismissal lists.

### 政务院举行第一九八次会议

1953-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一九八次会议
    【新华社十七日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百九十八次政务会议于十七日下午举行。会议听了中央人民政府政务院副秘书长廖鲁言所作的关于开展冬季农业生产工作的说明后，讨论并通过了“中央人民政府政务院关于开展冬季农业生产工作的指示”。会议听了中央人民政府农业部副部长刘瑞龙所作的关于增产油料作物的说明后，讨论并通过了“中央人民政府政务院关于增产油料作物的指示”。会议还听了中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长雷任民所作关于“输出输入商品检验暂行条例”的说明并批准了这个条例。最后，会议通过了各项任免名单。
