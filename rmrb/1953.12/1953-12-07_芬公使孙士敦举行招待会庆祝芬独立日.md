Title: Finnish Minister Sun Shidun Holds Reception to Celebrate Finnish Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1953-12-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 6th: Finnish Minister to China, Sun Shidun, held a reception at 7:00 PM on December 6th to celebrate Finnish Independence Day.

Those invited to attend were: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Zhang Xiruo, Minister of Education; Zhang Naiqi, Minister of Food; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Zhou Yang, Vice Minister of Culture; Su Jingguan, Vice Minister of Health; Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Zhang Youyu, Vice Mayor of Beijing; and representatives from various people's organizations and the literary and art circles.

Diplomatic envoys and diplomatic personnel from various countries stationed in China were also invited to attend.

A total of more than 200 people attended the reception, which proceeded in a friendly atmosphere from beginning to end. During the reception, guests and hosts frequently toasted to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong and President Paasikivi.

### 芬公使孙士敦举行招待会庆祝芬独立日

1953-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    芬公使孙士敦举行招待会庆祝芬独立日
    【新华社六日讯】芬兰驻我国公使孙士敦于十二月六日下午七时举行招待会庆祝芬兰独立日。
    应邀出席的有：中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，教育部部长张奚若，粮食部部长章乃器，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，文化部副部长周扬，卫生部副部长苏井观，人民革命军事委员会办公厅主任萧向荣，北京市副市长张友渔及各人民团体与文艺界人士等。
    各国驻我国外交使节及外交人员也应邀赴会。
    到会者共二百余人，招待会自始至终在友好气氛中进行，席间宾主频频为毛泽东主席及巴锡基维总统的健康干杯。
