Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish Foreign Minister Zhou's Statement and Notification
Author:
Date: 1953-12-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 8th. Moscow newspapers on the 8th all published with great length and prominent placement, the statement made by Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, regarding the resolution illegally passed by the United Nations General Assembly slandering the Korean and Chinese People's forces, and the notification from Minister Zhou Enlai to the United Nations General Assembly on the current situation in Korea.

### 莫斯科各报刊载周外长的声明和通知

1953-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各报刊载周外长的声明和通知
   【新华社莫斯科八日电】莫斯科各报八日都以巨大篇幅和显著地位，刊载我中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长为联合国大会非法通过诬蔑朝中人民部队的决议发表的声明和周恩来部长就朝鲜问题目前局势致联合国大会的通知。
