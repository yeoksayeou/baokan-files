Title: Berlin Rally Celebrates Successful Conclusion of German-Soviet Friendship Month; Five Million in East Germany Participate in German-Soviet Friendship Month Activities
Author:
Date: 1953-12-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, December 2nd: TASS, Berlin reports: Berlin held a grand meeting on November 30th to mark the end of German-Soviet Friendship Month. Those attending the meeting included heads of government of the German Democratic Republic and members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, representatives of various democratic parties, mass organizations, and residents of Berlin. Also present were diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union and other People's Democracies, as well as guests from the Soviet Union who came to participate in the German-Soviet Friendship Month.

Professor Otto Nuschke, Chairman of the German-Soviet Friendship Month Committee and Deputy Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, delivered a report on the achievements of the German-Soviet Friendship Month. He emphasized that the various activities organized during this month were a new contribution to strengthening the friendship between the people of Germany and the Soviet Union and to consolidating and expanding economic and cultural ties between the Soviet Union and the German Democratic Republic.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, December 1st: ADN Berlin reports: The recently concluded German-Soviet Friendship Month achieved great success. During the German-Soviet Friendship Month, the German Democratic Republic held approximately 30,000 rallies and concerts to celebrate the Great October Socialist Revolution, with nearly five million people participating in these gatherings. In West Berlin, 210 rallies were held to celebrate the October Revolution.

To celebrate the October Revolution, workers in the German Democratic Republic put forward a total of 49,000 production pledges. Workers at the Wismut factories set new production records in November and fulfilled their monthly production quota on November 23rd. The "Friendship" blast furnace in Stalin City completed its monthly production quota on November 16th.

The German-Soviet Friendship Association added approximately 75,000 new members.

### 柏林举行集会庆祝德苏友好月胜利结束  民主德国有五百万人参加了德苏友好月活动

1953-12-04
第4版()
专栏：

    柏林举行集会庆祝德苏友好月胜利结束
    民主德国有五百万人参加了德苏友好月活动
    【新华社二日讯】据塔斯社柏林讯：柏林在十一月三十日为结束德苏友好月举行了一次大会。出席大会的有：德意志民主共和国政府首长和德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局委员，各民主党派、群众团体和柏林居民的代表。到会的还有苏联及各人民民主国家的外交使节和从苏联来参加德苏友好月的来宾。
    德苏友好月委员会主席、德意志民主共和国副总理奥托·努舍克教授作了一个关于德苏友好月的成就的报告。他着重指出，在这个月中组织的各项活动，是对加强德苏两国人民之间的友谊、对巩固和扩大苏联和德意志民主共和国之间的经济和文化联系的一个新的贡献。
    【新华社二日讯】据德意志通讯社柏林一日讯：刚刚结束的德苏友好月获得了巨大的成就。德意志民主共和国在德苏友好月期间，为了庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命节，一共举行了约三万次集会和音乐演奏会，参加这些集会的有将近五百万人。在西柏林为庆祝十月革命节举行了二百一十次集会。
    德意志民主共和国的工人为了庆祝十月革命节，一共提出了四万九千项生产保证。维斯穆特工厂的工人在十一月份创造了新的生产纪录，并在十一月二十三日就完成了全月的生产定额。斯大林斯塔特的“友好”高炉在十一月十六日就完成了全月的生产定额。
    德苏友好协会增加了约七万五千个新会员。
