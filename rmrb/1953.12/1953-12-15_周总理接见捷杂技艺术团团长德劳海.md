Title: Premier Zhou Receives Mr. Drouha, Head of the Czechoslovakian Acrobatic Troupe
Author:
Date: 1953-12-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 14th: The Czechoslovakian Acrobatic Troupe gave a performance at Huairentang on the evening of the 13th. Attending the performance were Zhu De and Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the Government Administration Council; Dong Biwu, Chen Yun, Huang Yanpei, and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premiers; as well as heads of various departments of the Central People's Government and figures from the capital's literary and art circles.

Also attending the performance were diplomatic personnel from the Czechoslovakian Embassy in China.

Before the program began, Premier Zhou Enlai received Miroslav Drouha, head of the acrobatic troupe, and the acrobatic troupe's artistic director, Marie Blašková, and actors Václav Dvořák, Milan Bláha, and Betuše Žďárská. The Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Tezak, also attended the meeting.

### 周总理接见捷杂技艺术团团长德劳海

1953-12-15
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见捷杂技艺术团团长德劳海
    【新华社十四日讯】捷克斯洛伐克杂技艺术团于十三日晚在怀仁堂举行表演晚会。出席晚会观看表演的有中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇，政务院总理周恩来，副总理董必武、陈云、黄炎培、邓小平，以及中央人民政府各部门负责人和首都文艺界人士等。
    出席观看表演的还有捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使馆的外交人员。
    在节目开始前，周恩来总理接见了杂技艺术团团长米罗斯拉夫·德劳海，和杂技艺术团艺术指导玛丽·布拉什科娃，演员瓦茨拉夫·德沃夏克，米兰·布拉哈，别图塞·兹达尔斯卡。捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻华大使康萨拉也参加了会见。
