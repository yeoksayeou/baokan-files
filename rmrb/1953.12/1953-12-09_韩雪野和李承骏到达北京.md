Title: Han Xueye and Li Chengjun Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-12-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 8th. Han Xueye, head of the Korean delegation to the Vienna World Peace Council meeting and Chairman of the Korean National Committee for the Defense of Peace, and delegation member Li Chengjun arrived in Beijing on the 8th. Wu Maosun, Deputy Secretary-General of the China Peace Committee, and others went to the airport to welcome them. Xu Zhe, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, also went to the airport to greet them.

### 韩雪野和李承骏到达北京

1953-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    韩雪野和李承骏到达北京
    【新华社八日讯】出席维也纳世界和平理事会会议的朝鲜代表团团长、朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会委员长韩雪野和团员李承骏于八日到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副秘书长吴茂荪等多人，前往机场迎接的还有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办徐哲。
