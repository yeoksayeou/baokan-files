Title: The Soviet Union Appoints Zhevronkov as Minister of State Control

Author:
Date: 1953-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 17th: TASS Moscow, December 17th: In view of the fact that the Supreme Procurator's Office of the Soviet Union (formerly translated as Procurator's Office) has determined the anti-state criminal activities committed by V.N. Merkulov during his tenure in the organs of the Soviet Ministry of State Security and the Soviet Ministry of Internal Affairs, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, based on the suggestion of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union on September 17th of this year, has removed V.N. Merkulov from his post as Minister of State Control of the Soviet Union and submitted his case to the court for trial.

The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has appointed Vasily Gavrilovich Zhevronkov as Minister of State Control of the Soviet Union.

### 苏联任命热伏隆科夫为国家监察部部长

1953-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联任命热伏隆科夫为国家监察部部长
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十七日讯：鉴于苏联最高检察署（前译检察所）已确定弗·尼·麦尔库洛夫在苏联国家保安部和苏联内务部机关任职期间所犯的反国家罪恶活动，苏联最高苏维埃主席团根据苏联部长会议今年九月十七日的建议，已撤销弗·尼·麦尔库洛夫的苏联国家监察部部长的职务，并将他的案件提交法院审理。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团已任命瓦西里·加夫里洛维奇·热伏隆科夫为苏联国家监察部部长。
