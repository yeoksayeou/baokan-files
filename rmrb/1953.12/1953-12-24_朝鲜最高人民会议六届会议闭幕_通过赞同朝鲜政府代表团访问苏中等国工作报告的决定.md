Title: The 6th Session of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea Concludes, Adopts Resolution Endorsing the Report on the Work of the North Korean Government Delegation's Visit to the Soviet Union, China, and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1953-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 23rd: The 6th Session of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, which lasted for three days, successfully concluded on the 22nd.

On the 21st, the meeting held a heated discussion on the work report made by Premier Kim Il-sung regarding the visit of the North Korean government delegation to the Soviet Union, China, and various People's Democratic countries.

At the meeting on the 22nd, Premier Kim Il-sung first made a concluding speech on the discussion of the report. Subsequently, the meeting unanimously adopted the resolution on endorsing the work report of the North Korean government delegation's visit to the Soviet Union, China, and various People's Democratic countries.

After hearing the report of Kang Ryang-uk, Secretary-General of the Standing Committee, on the approval of the decrees of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, the meeting unanimously approved the decrees issued by the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly from the 5th to the 6th session that required approval from the Supreme People's Assembly.

The meeting discussed the issue of re-electing the Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly and some members of the Standing Committee, and adopted corresponding decisions. As a result of the re-election, Ri Yong was elected as the Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly, and Ri Wi-min and Hong Ki-hwang were elected as Vice-Chairmen. The meeting decided to relieve Hong Ki-chu, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, and Kim Tal-hyon, Vice-Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly, of their duties, and elected Kim Ung-ki and Ri Kuk-ro as Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee.

### 朝鲜最高人民会议六届会议闭幕  通过赞同朝鲜政府代表团访问苏中等国工作报告的决定

1953-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜最高人民会议六届会议闭幕
    通过赞同朝鲜政府代表团访问苏中等国工作报告的决定
    【新华社平壤二十三日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议第六届会议举行了三天，已经在二十二日胜利闭幕。
    二十一日，会议就金日成首相所作的关于前往苏联、中国及各人民民主国家访问的朝鲜政府代表团的工作报告，进行了热烈的讨论。
    在二十二日的会议上，首先由金日成首相就报告的讨论做了总结发言。接着，会议一致通过了关于赞同访问苏联、中国及各人民民主国家的朝鲜政府代表团的工作报告的决定。
    会议在听了常任委员会书记长康良煜关于批准最高人民会议常任委员会政令的报告后，一致通过了最高人民会议第五届会议到第六届会议期间所发布的需要最高人民会议批准的各项政令。
    会议讨论了关于改选最高人民会议议长和一部分常任委员会委员的问题，并通过了相应的决定。改选结果，李英当选为最高人民会议议长，李维民和洪箕璜当选为副议长。会议决定解除最高人民会议常任委员会副委员长洪箕畴和最高人民会议副议长金达铉的职务，并推选金应基和李克鲁为常任委员会副委员长。
