Title: Japanese Communist Party Central Committee Sends Letter to the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party
Author:
Date: 1953-12-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th Dispatch from Tokyo: To celebrate "Vietnam National Resistance Day," the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party sent a letter of respect to the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party. The text is as follows:

To the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party,
Dear Comrades,

    On the occasion of your great Liberation Day, the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, on behalf of the Japanese working class and all patriots fighting for peace and independence, extends warm respect to the Vietnamese people, your Party, and the great leader President Ho Chi Minh, who are waging a heroic struggle for peace and national liberation.

    Your struggle and our struggle are closely linked under the lofty goal of peace and national liberation in Asia and the world. We are encouraged by your great and victorious struggle and pledge to learn from you, to crush the conspiracy of the US-Japan reactionaries to revive Japanese militarism for the sake of peace in Asia and the world, and we sincerely wish you final victory.

                        The Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party
                        December 1, 1953

### 日本共产党中央写信给越南劳动党中央

1953-12-20
第4版()
专栏：

    日本共产党中央写信给越南劳动党中央
    【新华社十九日讯】东京消息：为庆祝“越南全国抗战日”，日本共产党中央委员会向越南劳动党中央委员会发出了致敬信。文如下：越南劳动党中央委员会亲爱的各位同志：
    欣逢你们伟大的解放纪念日，日本共产党中央委员会代表日本工人阶级及为和平与独立而斗争的全体爱国人士，对为争取和平与民族解放而进行英勇斗争的越南人民、你们的党和伟大的领袖胡志明主席，遥致热烈的敬意。
    你们的斗争和我们的斗争，在亚洲及世界和平与民族解放这一崇高的目的下，已经紧密地结合在一起了。我们受到你们伟大的、胜利的斗争的鼓舞，保证要向你们学习，为了亚洲及世界和平，一定要粉碎美、日反动派复活日本军国主义的阴谋，同时，衷心地预祝你们获得最后胜利。
                        日本共产党中央委员会
                        一九五三年十二月一日
