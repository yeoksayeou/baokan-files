Title: Communist Party of India Third Congress Issues Communiqué
Author:
Date: 1953-12-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, December 29th: The "Hindustan Times Evening News" published the communiqué issued by the Third Congress of the Communist Party of India, which stated:

    The Congress adopted three resolutions proposed by the Presidium of the Congress.

    The first resolution was to observe a moment of silence for J. V. Stalin; the second resolution was to observe a moment of silence for the martyrs who sacrificed themselves for the Indian working class and the democratic movement; the third resolution was to pay tribute to the comrades in prison.

    The Congress received congratulatory messages from fraternal parties abroad, including the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Communist Party of China, the Communist Party of France, the Communist Party of Italy, and the Communist Party of Japan. Harry Pollitt personally represented the Communist Party of Great Britain in offering congratulations to the Congress.

### 印度共产党第三次代表大会发表公报

1953-12-31
第3版()
专栏：

    印度共产党第三次代表大会发表公报
    【新华社讯】塔斯社德里二十九日电：“印度斯坦时代新闻晚报”刊载了印度共产党第三次代表大会发表的公报，公报说：
    代表大会通过了大会主席团提出的三项决议。
    第一项决议是，代表大会为约·维·斯大林默哀；第二项决议是，为印度工人阶级和民主运动而牺牲的烈士默哀；第三项决议是，向牢狱中的同志们致敬。
    代表大会收到了外国兄弟党的贺电，其中包括苏联共产党、中国共产党、法国共产党、意大利共产党和日本共产党的贺电。哈里·波立特亲自代表英国共产党向代表大会致贺。
