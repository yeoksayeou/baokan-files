Title: Guo Moruo Sends Congratulatory Telegrams to Winners of the Stalin International Peace Prize

Author:
Date: 1953-12-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 26th—Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, sent congratulatory telegrams on the 26th to the winners of the 1953 "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prizes, and wished them to make greater contributions in the cause of defending world peace. The congratulatory telegrams were sent separately to the following winners: Pierre Cot, Member of the French National Assembly; Sahib Singh Sokhey, Member of the Indian Parliament; Andrea Gaggero, Italian Priest; Isabelle Blume, Member of the Belgian Parliament; Howard Fast, American Writer; John Bernal, Professor at the University of London, England; Leon Kruczkowski, Polish Writer; Pablo Neruda, Chilean Writer; Dr. Andrea Andreen, Swedish Doctor of Medicine; and Nina Vasilyevna Popova, Secretary of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions.

### 郭沫若电贺斯大林国际和平奖金得奖人

1953-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若电贺斯大林国际和平奖金得奖人
    【新华社二十六日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，于二十六日致电庆贺一九五三年“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人，并祝他们在保卫世界和平的事业中作出更大的贡献。贺电是分别打给下列得奖人的：法国国民议会议员皮埃尔·戈特、印度国会议员萨希布·辛格·索克、意大利牧师安德烈·加杰罗、比利时议员伊莎贝丽·布伦姆夫人、美国作家霍华德·法斯特、英国伦敦大学教授约翰·贝尔纳、波兰作家列昂·克鲁奇科夫斯基、智利作家巴勃罗·聂鲁达、瑞典医学博士安德烈·安德琳和全苏工会中央理事会书记尼娜·瓦西里耶夫娜·波波娃。
