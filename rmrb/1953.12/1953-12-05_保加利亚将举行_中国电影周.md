Title: "Chinese Film Week" to be Held in Bulgaria
Author:
Date: 1953-12-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, December 4th: To strengthen cultural exchange between China and Bulgaria and to further develop the friendship between the two peoples, the Bulgarian government has decided to hold a "Chinese Film Week" throughout Bulgaria from December 7th to 13th. During the film week, the Chinese feature films "People's Fighters," "Red Flag on the Cui Gang," "New Sons and Daughters of China," and the documentaries "National Day of 1952" and "Be Sure to Harness the Huai River" will be screened.

### 保加利亚将举行“中国电影周”

1953-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚将举行“中国电影周”
    【新华社四日讯】索非亚消息：为加强中保两国的文化交流和进一步发展两国人民之间的友谊，保加利亚政府决定从十二月七日到十三日止，在保加利亚全国举行“中国电影周”。在电影周中将放映中国故事片“人民的战士”、“翠岗红旗”、“新儿女英雄传”和纪录片“一九五二年国庆节”、“一定要把淮河修好”。
