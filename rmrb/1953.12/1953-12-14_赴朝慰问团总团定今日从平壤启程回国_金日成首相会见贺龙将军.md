Title: The General Delegation of the People's Delegation to Korea is Scheduled to Return Home Today from Pyongyang; Premier Kim Il-sung Meets with General He Long
Author:
Date: 1953-12-14
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 13th: The General Delegation of the Third Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, led by General He Long, completed its work of consoling the Kaesong area and various front lines in Korea, and returned to Pyongyang, the capital of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, on the 12th. Those who went to greet them included: Kim Chun-hae, Chairman of the Reception Committee for the Third Chinese People's Delegation to Korea and Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Korean Fatherland Unification Democratic Front; Kim Min-san, Minister of the Social Department of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Jung Ryul, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture and Propaganda of the Cabinet; and Ko Bong-ki, Chairman of the Pyongyang City Committee of the Workers' Party. Gan Yetao, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea, and Li Shi, First Secretary, also went to greet them.
　　The General Delegation will depart for home on the 14th.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 13th: Before the Third Chinese People's Delegation to Korea left Pyongyang, Premier Kim Il-sung personally went to the delegation's residence to meet with He Long, the head of the delegation, and Xing Xiping, Zhu Xuefan, Chen Yi, Wu Han, Liu Zhiming, and Kang Keqing, the deputy heads of the delegation. The meeting lasted about two hours, and a photo was taken to commemorate the occasion after the talks.

### 赴朝慰问团总团定今日从平壤启程回国  金日成首相会见贺龙将军

1953-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    赴朝慰问团总团定今日从平壤启程回国
    金日成首相会见贺龙将军
    【新华社平壤十三日电】中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团总团，在贺龙将军率领下完成了对开城地区和朝鲜前线各地的慰问工作之后，在十二日回到朝鲜民主首都平壤。前往迎接的有：接待中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团委员会委员长、朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会议长团议长金天海，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会社会部部长金民山，内阁文化宣传省副相郑律，劳动党平壤市委员会委员长高凤起等。我国驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办甘野陶、一等秘书李石也前往迎接。
    慰问团总团一行将在十四日启程回国。
    【新华社平壤十三日电】金日成首相在中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团离平壤前夕，亲到慰问团住地会见贺龙总团长和邢西萍、朱学范、陈沂、吴晗、刘芝明、康克清副总团长。会谈约两小时，谈毕并摄影留念。
