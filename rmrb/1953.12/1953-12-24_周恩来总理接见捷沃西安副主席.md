Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Deputy Chairman Tevosyan
Author:
Date: 1953-12-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd: Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government received I.F. Tevosyan, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of the Metallurgical Industry, on the afternoon of the 23rd. Tevosyan was invited by our Central People's Government to attend the opening ceremonies of the large-scale rolling mill, seamless steel pipe plant, and No. 7 blast furnace of the Anshan Iron and Steel Company.

Present at the reception were Dr. Yudin, Soviet Ambassador to China, and Mr. Arkhipov, Counselor of the Embassy.

Also present at the reception were Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council; Li Fuchun, Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission; Wang Heshou, Minister of Heavy Industry; Huang Jing, Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building; Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Li Qiang, Commercial Counselor of our Embassy in the Soviet Union.

The reception took place in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

### 周恩来总理接见捷沃西安副主席

1953-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见捷沃西安副主席
    【新华社二十三日讯】我中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来于二十三日下午接见应我中央人民政府邀请前来我国参加鞍山钢铁公司大型轧钢厂、无缝钢管厂和第七号炼铁炉开工典礼的苏联部长会议副主席兼冶金工业部部长伊·费·捷沃西安。
    接见时在座的有：苏联驻我国大使尤金博士和大使馆参赞阿希波夫。
    接见时在座的还有：政务院副总理邓小平，国家计划委员会副主席李富春，重工业部部长王鹤寿，第一机械工业部部长黄敬，外交部副部长伍修权和我国驻苏联大使馆商务参赞李强。
    接见在亲切友好的空气中进行。
