Our Great Motherland
The Anshan Iron and Steel Works' large rolling mill's hot test rolling is performing well, and it has successfully rolled three products: large round steel, seamless steel pipe billets, and heavy-duty steel rails. Soviet expert V. Lukyanov and the workers are observing the rolling mill's test rolling of pipe billets here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    鞍钢大型轧钢厂热试轧的情况良好，已顺利轧出大型圆钢、无缝钢管的管坯和重型钢轨等三种产品。苏联专家伏·鲁可扬诺夫和工人们正在这里观察轧钢机试轧管坯。
