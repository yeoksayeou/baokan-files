Title: The Vietnamese People's Army Smashes Twenty-Four Enemy Battalions in Lai Châu Province
Author:
Date: 1953-12-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. According to the Vietnam News Agency, the General Command of the Vietnamese People's Army announced on the 29th that the Vietnamese People's Army had annihilated and routed a total of twenty-four enemy battalions in Lai Châu Province. Now, with the exception of Điện Biên Phủ, the enemy's last stronghold, the People's Army has liberated the entire Lai Châu Province.

### 越南人民军在莱州省击溃敌军二十四个连

1953-12-31
第3版()
专栏：

    越南人民军在莱州省击溃敌军二十四个连
    【新华社三十日讯】据越南通讯社讯：越南人民军总司令部二十九日发表的公报说，越南人民军已歼灭和击溃莱州省的敌军共二十四个连。现在，除敌军的最后一个据点奠边府外，人民军已解放莱州省全境。
