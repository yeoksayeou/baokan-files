    Our Great Motherland
    In recent years, China's medical equipment industry has made great progress. Short-wave electrotherapy machines, which used to rely on imports, can now be made domestically. This is a short-wave electrotherapy machine produced by the state-owned Shanghai Precision Medical Instrument Factory, which is very popular among medical departments in various regions, and the cost is half that of imported goods.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国医疗器械工业近年来有很大的发展，过去依靠进口的短波电疗机等现在都能自制了。这是国营上海精密医疗器械厂制出的短波电疗机，很受各地医疗部门的欢迎，成本要比进口货低一半。
