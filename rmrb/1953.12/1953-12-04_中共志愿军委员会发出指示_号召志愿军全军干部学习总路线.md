Title: The Chinese Communist Volunteer Army Committee Issues Directive, Calling on All Volunteer Army Cadres to Study the General Line

Author:

Date: 1953-12-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 3rd: The Chinese Communist Party Chinese People's Volunteer Army Committee recently issued a directive to Party organizations at all levels in the Volunteer Army regarding the study of the Party's general line and general task during the transition period.

    The directive calls on all army cadres to conscientiously study the general line and general task. The directive points out: Studying the Party's general line and general task during the transition period is a fundamental ideological construction task for the entire Volunteer Army in the new historical period. Whoever does not attach importance to or conscientiously carry out this study will inevitably make mistakes in struggle and work.

    The directive makes many provisions and explanations regarding the purpose, content, and methods of study. The directive also stipulates that this study should generally begin in mid-December and end by the end of March next year.

### 中共志愿军委员会发出指示  号召志愿军全军干部学习总路线

1953-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中共志愿军委员会发出指示
    号召志愿军全军干部学习总路线
    【新华社平壤三日电】中国共产党中国人民志愿军委员会最近向志愿军中的各级党组织发出了关于学习党在过渡时期总路线和总任务的指示。
    指示中号召全军干部认真进行总路线、总任务的学习。指示中指出：学习党在过渡时期的总路线和总任务，是志愿军全军在新的历史时期中带根本性的思想建设工作，谁要是不重视或不认真进行这一学习，谁在斗争和工作中就不可避免地会犯错误。
    指示中对学习的目的、内容和方法作了许多规定和说明。指示中并规定这一学习一般应在十二月中旬开始，至明年三月底结束。
