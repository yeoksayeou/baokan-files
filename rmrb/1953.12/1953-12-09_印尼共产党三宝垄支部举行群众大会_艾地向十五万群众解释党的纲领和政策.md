Title: Indonesian Communist Party's Semarang Branch Holds Mass Rally; Aidit Explains Party's Program and Policies to 150,000 People
Author:
Date: 1953-12-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 7th: Jakarta News: The Semarang branch of the Indonesian Communist Party held a large-scale mass rally in Semarang on November 29th. Approximately 150,000 people from various parts of Central Java attended the rally.

    D.N. Aidit, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party, explained the program and policies of the Indonesian Communist Party at the mass rally.

    Prior to this, the Semarang branch of the Indonesian Communist Party held a lecture meeting on the evening of November 27th, attended by more than 7,000 people, including officials from the local military and political authorities of Indonesia. Aidit delivered a speech at the meeting, explaining the tasks and goals of the Indonesian Communist Party in the current situation.

### 印尼共产党三宝垄支部举行群众大会  艾地向十五万群众解释党的纲领和政策

1953-12-09
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼共产党三宝垄支部举行群众大会
    艾地向十五万群众解释党的纲领和政策
    【新华社七日讯】雅加达消息：印尼共产党三宝垄支部于十一月二十九日在三宝垄举行了大规模的群众大会，参加大会的约有十五万人，他们来自爪哇中部地区的各个地方。
    印尼共产党中央委员会总书记艾地在群众大会上解释了印尼共产党的纲领和政策。
    在这以前，印尼共产党三宝垄支部曾在十一月二十七日晚举行演讲会，出席者共有七千多人，印尼地方军政当局官员也参加了演讲会。艾地在会上发表演说，解释目前形势下印尼共产党的任务和奋斗目标。
