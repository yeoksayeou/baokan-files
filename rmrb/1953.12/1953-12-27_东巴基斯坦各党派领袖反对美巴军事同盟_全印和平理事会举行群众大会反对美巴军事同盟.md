Title: East Pakistan Party Leaders Oppose US-Pakistan Military Alliance; All-India Peace Council Holds Mass Meeting Against US-Pakistan Military Alliance
Author: 
Date: 1953-12-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 24th: The "Pakistan Times" reports that prominent political and public leaders of East Pakistan's capital and province issued a joint statement urging the Pakistani government not to conclude a military alliance with the United States.

The statement said: "If the Pakistani government signs a military alliance with the United States, Pakistan's independence and sovereignty will be threatened. Such an alliance will drag Pakistan into a devastating war and threaten peace and happiness. Furthermore, it will seriously hinder the development of our young country."

The statement was signed by: Maulana Abdul Hamid Bhashani, Chairman of the East Pakistan People's Muslim League; Dar Alazi Muhammad Danish, Chairman of the Democratic Party; Maulana Abdul Rashid Tarkabagish, Chairman of the East Pakistan Peasants Association; Muhammad Shahidullah, Secretary of the East Pakistan Peasants and Workers Party; and Abdus Samad, Secretary of the East Bengal Provincial Organization of the Communist Party of Pakistan, among others.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 26th: The All-India Peace Council held a mass meeting at the Constitution Club in New Delhi on the 24th to oppose the US-Pakistan military alliance. The meeting was presided over by Dr. Kitchlew, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council.

Kitchlew said in his speech: If anyone is disrupting peace today, it is not the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China, but the United States. He said: The Indian people will never shed a drop of blood or pay a single penny for the interests of American aggressors and warmongers.

Major General Soek, Vice-Chairman of the All-India Peace Council and winner of the 1953 Stalin International Peace Prize for "Strengthening International Peace," said: The US-Pakistan military alliance is a conspiracy by the United States to create chaos in India. He said: We want to live in peace with all our neighbors, and we are the same with Pakistan.

Sadan Gupta and Chatur Narain Malaviya, members of the Indian Parliament, also spoke at the meeting.

The resolution passed at the meeting stated that the US-Pakistan military alliance "wickedly violates the United Nations Charter, brings the Cold War to our borders, and endangers peace and security in Asia." The resolution pointed out that this military alliance will not only undermine Pakistan's freedom, but will also be used to undermine the sovereignty and independence of Asian countries and will be used as a tool to blackmail India.

Xinhua News Agency, 25th: New Delhi News: Indian Prime Minister Nehru, speaking on foreign policy during a debate in the People's Assembly of the Indian Parliament on the 23rd, reiterated India's opposition to the United States giving Pakistan military "aid." Nehru said: This military "aid" "will certainly reduce the chances of peace."

Nehru spoke of Pakistani Prime Minister Muhammad Ali's statement that there had been no negotiations on the issue of bases, and then said: "When military aid arrives, the whole country becomes a base. This is not a question of establishing sporadic bases here or there, but of the entire country being used for the purposes of other countries and other people. Therefore, this is a dangerous step, which is dangerous for many countries, for us and Pakistan, especially for Pakistan. This step will not only not bring security to Pakistan, but will actually hinder the work for peace."

Speaking of the reactions of Pakistan's other neighbors to the United States' military "aid" to Pakistan, Nehru said that everyone jointly "felt that this retrograde practice is a danger to Asia."

### 东巴基斯坦各党派领袖反对美巴军事同盟  全印和平理事会举行群众大会反对美巴军事同盟

1953-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    东巴基斯坦各党派领袖反对美巴军事同盟
    全印和平理事会举行群众大会反对美巴军事同盟
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社喀喇蚩二十四日讯：“巴基斯坦时报”报道：东巴基斯坦首府和省内著名政治和公众领袖发表联合声明，要求巴基斯坦政府不要和美国缔结军事同盟。
    声明略称：“如果巴基斯坦政府和美国签订了军事同盟，那么巴基斯坦的独立和主权就将受到威胁。这样的一个同盟将把巴基斯坦卷入一场毁灭性的战争，并将威胁到和平与幸福。此外，它也将严重地阻碍我们的年青的国家的发展。”
    在声明上签名的有：东巴基斯坦人民回教联盟主席毛拉纳·阿卜杜勒·哈密德·巴沙尼，民主党主席达尔·阿拉志·穆罕默德·丹尼什，东巴基斯坦农民协会主席毛拉纳·阿卜杜勒·拉希德·塔卡巴吉什，东巴基斯坦农工党书记穆罕默德·沙希杜拉，巴基斯坦共产党东孟加拉省组织的书记阿卜杜斯·萨马德等人。
   【新华社新德里二十六日电】全印和平理事会二十四日在新德里宪法俱乐部举行群众大会反对美巴军事同盟。大会由全印和平理事会主席克其鲁博士主持。
    克其鲁在发言中说：假如今天有人在破坏和平的话，那不是苏联和中华人民共和国，而是美国。他说：印度人民决不为美国侵略者和战争贩子的利益而流一滴血，付出一文钱。
    全印和平理事会副主席、一九五三年“加强国际和平”斯大林国际和平奖金获得者索克少将说：美巴军事同盟是美国在印度制造混乱的一个阴谋。他说：我们是要与一切邻国和平相处的，对于巴基斯坦我们也是如此。
    在会上发言的还有印度国会议员萨丹·古普塔和查图尔·纳拉茵·马拉维亚等。
    大会通过的决议说：美巴军事同盟“罪恶地违反了联合国宪章，把冷战带到我们的边界上，并危害亚洲的和平与安全。”决议指出：这一军事同盟不仅将破坏巴基斯坦的自由，同时也将被用来破坏亚洲国家的主权和独立，并将被用来作为对印度进行讹诈的工具。
     【新华社二十五日讯】新德里消息：印度总理尼赫鲁二十三日在印度国会人民院辩论外交问题时发表演说，重申印度反对美国给予巴基斯坦军事
  “援助”。尼赫鲁说：这种军事“援助”“当然会减少和平的机会”。
    尼赫鲁谈到巴基斯坦总理穆罕默德·阿里所说的没有进行关于基地问题的谈判的说法，接着，他说：“当军事援助到来的时候，整个国家都会变成基地。这并不是在这里或那里建立零星基地的问题，而是整个国家被利用为其他国家和其他人的目的服务的问题。因此，这是一种危险的步骤，这对于许多国家，对于我们和巴基斯坦，特别对于巴基斯坦是危险的。这种步骤不仅不会给巴基斯坦带来安全，实际上将阻挠为和平而进行的工作。”
    讲到巴基斯坦的其他邻国对于美国给予巴基斯坦军事“援助”的反应时，尼赫鲁说：大家都共同“感到这种倒行逆施的做法对亚洲是一种危险”。
