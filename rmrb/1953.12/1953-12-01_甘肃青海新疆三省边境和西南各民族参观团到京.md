Title: Border Area Visiting Delegations from Gansu, Qinghai, and Xinjiang, and Southwestern Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-12-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency report:

    The eighty-nine-member visiting delegation comprised of various ethnic groups from the border areas of Gansu, Qinghai, and Xinjiang provinces, led by head of delegation Mu Huaxi (Kazakh ethnicity), arrived in Beijing on November 24th. The forty-eight-member visiting delegation from various ethnic groups in the Southwest, led by head of delegation Kong Sayiduo (Tibetan ethnicity), arrived in Beijing on November 26th. Gan Chunlei, Director of the General Office of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, and more than thirty others were at the station to welcome them.
    Before coming to Beijing, the visiting delegation from various ethnic groups in the border areas of Gansu, Qinghai, and Xinjiang had visited factories, scenic spots, and temples in Shanghai, Nanjing, Hangzhou, Wuxi, Shenyang, Fushun, Changchun, and Harbin.

### 甘肃青海新疆三省边境和西南各民族参观团到京

1953-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    甘肃青海新疆三省边境和西南各民族参观团到京
    据新华社讯：甘肃、青海、新疆三省边境各民族参观团八十九人，由团长穆华希（哈萨克族）率领，于十一月二十四日抵京。西南各民族参观团四十八人，由团长孔萨益多（藏族）率领，于十一月二十六日抵京。到车站欢迎的有中央人民政府民族事务委员会办公厅主任甘春雷等三十余人。
    甘、青、新边境各民族参观团来京前，曾到上海、南京、杭州、无锡、沈阳、抚顺、长春、哈尔滨等地参观了工厂、名胜古迹和寺院。
