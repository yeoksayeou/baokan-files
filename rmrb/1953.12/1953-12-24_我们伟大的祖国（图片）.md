    Our Great Motherland
    The technical cooperation between China and North Korea is of great significance for further consolidating and developing the profound friendship between the Chinese and North Korean people and accelerating the work of restoring the national economy of the North Korean people. Li Jiagen (right), a master worker in the gear workshop of the state-owned Shanghai Machine Tool Factory, is helping Tang Cansan (left), a North Korean trainee sent to our country, to inspect the standard specifications of various gears.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中朝两国的技术合作，对进一步巩固和发展中朝两国人民的深厚友谊和加速朝鲜人民恢复国民经济的工作都有很大意义。国营上海机床厂齿轮车间老师傅李加根（右）正在帮助派来我国的朝鲜实习生唐灿三（左）检验各种齿轮的标准规格。
