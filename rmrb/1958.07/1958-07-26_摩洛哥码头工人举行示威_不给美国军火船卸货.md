Title: Moroccan Dockworkers Demonstrate, Refuse to Unload American Arms Ship
Author:
Date: 1958-07-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, July 25th: Rabat dispatch: Two thousand dockworkers in Safi, Morocco (south of Casablanca), demonstrated on the 23rd, refusing to unload the freighter "Lyin Bank" carrying American strategic materials. The freighter was forced to leave the port immediately after arriving that day.

It is reported that this incident is related to Morocco's demand that the United States remove its military bases and its opposition to the United States using these bases to invade the Middle East.

### 摩洛哥码头工人举行示威  不给美国军火船卸货

1958-07-26
第3版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥码头工人举行示威
    不给美国军火船卸货
    据新华社25日讯　拉巴特消息：摩洛哥的萨非港（在卡萨布兰卡以南）的二千名码头工人23日举行示威，拒绝为载运美国战略物资的货船“累因班号”卸货，这艘货船已经被迫在当天到达后又马上离开港口。
    据报道，这件事与摩洛哥要求美国撤除军事基地和反对美国利用这些基地侵略中东有关。
