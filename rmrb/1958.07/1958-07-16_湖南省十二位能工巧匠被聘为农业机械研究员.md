Title: Twelve Skilled Craftsmen in Hunan Province Hired as Agricultural Machinery Researchers
Author: 
Date: 1958-07-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, 15th: At the Hunan Provincial Representative Conference of Agricultural Technical Innovation Workers, twelve skilled craftsmen were hired as special researchers at the Hunan Provincial Agricultural Machinery Research Institute. They are: Li Xingyong, Cao Wentao, Yang Xixian, Huang Yimiao, Pan Zhenchun, Huang Juchu, Zhou Deguang, Li Zhuyan, Shi Linxiu (Miao nationality), Chen Yinghua (Tujia nationality), Xie Yichu, and Fang Jiaji. They are all local experts who have made outstanding achievements in agricultural technical innovation.

### 湖南省十二位能工巧匠被聘为农业机械研究员

1958-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    湖南省十二位能工巧匠被聘为农业机械研究员
    据新华社长沙15日电　在湖南省农业技术革新工作者代表会议上，有十二位能工巧匠被聘为湖南省农业机械研究所的特约研究员。他们是：李性永、曹文涛、阳希贤、黄贻淼、潘振纯、黄菊初、周德光、李主颜、石林秀（苗族）、陈应华（土家族）、谢裔楚、房家吉。他们都是在农业技术革新中作出了杰出成绩的土专家。
