Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Invades Soviet Airspace from Iran, Soviet Union Protests to U.S. and Iranian Governments
Author:
Date: 1958-07-31
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 30th: TASS reports: Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Patolichev today handed a protest note from the Soviet government to U.S. Ambassador to the Soviet Union Thompson, protesting the intrusion of a U.S. military aircraft, originating from Iran, into Soviet airspace in the Caspian Sea region on July 26th. On the same day, Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Zorin handed a protest note from the Soviet government to Iranian Ambassador to the Soviet Union Sami, lodging a protest with the Iranian government regarding the same incident. Both ambassadors have promised to immediately inform their respective governments of the notes.

### 美军用飞机从伊朗侵犯苏联领空  苏联向美伊政府提出抗议

1958-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

    美军用飞机从伊朗侵犯苏联领空
    苏联向美伊政府提出抗议
    新华社30日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联外交部第一副部长帕托利切夫今天把苏联政府的一件抗议照会交给美国驻苏联大使汤普逊，抗议从伊朗方面起飞的美国军用飞机在7月26日侵入里海地区苏联领空。同一天苏联外交部副部长佐林把苏联政府的一项抗议照会交给伊朗驻苏联大使萨米就同一事件向伊朗政府提出抗议。两位大使已答应把照会立即通知他们本国政府。
