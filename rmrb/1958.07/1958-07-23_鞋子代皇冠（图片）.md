Title: Shoes Replace Crown (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1958-07-23
Page: 5

    Shoes Replace Crown
  In the royal palace of Baghdad, the capital of Iraq, there is a magnificent royal ornament. Originally, a crown hung here, but now the crown has been removed and a shoe has been hung in its place, expressing the people's contempt for the traitorous feudal dynasty.
                                    (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 鞋子代皇冠（图片）

1958-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    鞋子代皇冠
  伊拉克首都巴格达的皇宫内有一个华丽的皇家饰物，原先这里挂的是皇冠，现在皇冠已取了下来，在原处挂了一只鞋，表示了人民对卖国封建王朝的蔑视。
                                    （新华社稿）
