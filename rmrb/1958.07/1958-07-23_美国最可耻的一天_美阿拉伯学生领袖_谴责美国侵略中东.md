Title: America's Most Shameful Day American Arab Student Leader Condemns US Aggression in the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1958-07-23
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, July 21st: According to a TASS dispatch from New York on July 20th: The "Chicago Daily News" published a letter from Mansour, a leader of the American Arab Student Organization. The letter emphasizes that the United States' armed intervention in Lebanon has caused its prestige in the Middle East to "drop to almost zero," and that Arabs will henceforth recall these American actions "with hatred." The day the United States sent troops to intervene in Lebanon "will become America's most shameful day" in their memory.

### 美国最可耻的一天  美阿拉伯学生领袖  谴责美国侵略中东

1958-07-23
第3版()
专栏：

    美国最可耻的一天
    美阿拉伯学生领袖
    谴责美国侵略中东
    新华社21日讯  据塔斯社纽约20日讯：“芝加哥每日新闻”刊登了美国阿拉伯学生组织领袖曼苏尔的一封信。信中着重指出，美国对黎巴嫩的武装干涉使它在中东的威信几乎“已经降低到零”，阿拉伯人今后“将以憎恨的心情”来回忆美国的这些行动。美国出兵干涉黎巴嫩的那一天在他们的记忆中“将成为美国最可耻的一天”。
