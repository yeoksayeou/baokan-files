Title: Vice Premier Kim Il and Others Receive Returning War-Affected Children, Encouraging Them to Actively Build the Motherland
Author: 
Date: 1958-07-02
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 1st: Vice Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Kim Il, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Park Kum-chul, and Xu Zhengyu, head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, who recently returned, along with leaders of the Korean Party, government, and social organizations, received more than 800 Korean war-affected children who recently returned from China on June 30.

Vice Premier Kim Il explained in detail to these war-affected children how the Korean people achieved victory in the war, the achievements made in post-war reconstruction and socialist construction, and the Five-Year Plan. He asked them to actively participate in work, learn advanced technology, and, according to the teachings of Marshal Kim Il-sung, be loyal to the Party and the people to the end, dedicate all their energy and wisdom to the motherland, raise revolutionary vigilance, and defend the achievements of socialist construction.

### 金一副首相等接见归国的战灾儿童  勉励他们积极建设祖国

1958-07-02
第5版()
专栏：

    金一副首相等接见归国的战灾儿童
    勉励他们积极建设祖国
    据新华社平壤1日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相金一、朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴金哲、以及不久前回来的朝鲜人民访华代表团团长许贞琡等朝鲜的党、政府和社会团体的领导人，在6月30日接见了最近从中国回来的八百多名朝鲜战灾儿童。
    金一副首相对这些战灾儿童详尽地介绍了朝鲜人民怎样取得战争胜利、在战后恢复建设、社会主义建设中所取得的成就和五年计划等情况。要求他们积极参加工作，学习先进技术，按照金日成元帅的教导，为党和人民忠诚到底，把一切精力和智慧献给祖国，提高革命警惕，保卫社会主义建设成果。
