Title: Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1958-07-10
Page: 1

    Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
    The Organizational Regulations of the People's Congress and the People's Committee of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region were approved by the 99th meeting of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on July 9, 1958, and are hereby promulgated.
              Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, July 9, 1958

### 中华人民共和国主席令

1958-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国主席令
    广西僮族自治区人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例已由中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会于一九五八年七月九日第九十九次会议批准，现予公布。
              中华人民共和国主席  毛泽东  一九五八年七月九日
