Title: Indian Navy Flagship to Visit Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1958-07-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 9th: According to an official Indian announcement: The Indian Navy flagship "Mysore" will make a friendly visit to Shanghai.

The flagship already arrived in Singapore on the 8th. It will stay in Singapore for two days before heading to Shanghai, and will participate in exercises with British naval vessels en route.

The "Mysore" is expected to stay in Shanghai for five days, after which it will visit Japan.

### 印度海军旗舰将到上海访问

1958-07-10
第5版()
专栏：

    印度海军旗舰将到上海访问
    新华社新德里9日电  据印度官方宣布：印度海军旗舰“迈索尔号”将到上海作友好访问。
    这艘旗舰已经在8日到达新加坡。它将在新加坡停留两天，然后开往上海，途中将参加英国海军舰只的演习。
    “迈索尔号”预计将在上海停留五天，随后将到日本进行访问。
