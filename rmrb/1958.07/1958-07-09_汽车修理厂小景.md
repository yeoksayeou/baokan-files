Title: A Glimpse of the Auto Repair Shop
Author: Gao Shaoru
Date: 1958-07-09
Page: 8

    The iron hammer clangs, the gongs and drums resound,
    A great technical revolution unfolds,
    Red flowers, red flowers, bloom everywhere,
    Masters and apprentices, faces radiant with light.
    Broken cars come in, new cars go out,
    In and out, production leaps forward.
    Improved machinery, production innovations,
    Moving mountains and filling seas, wisdom surpasses Kong Ming.
    The three-wheeled car, a product of Wuhu,
    Driven to Beijing, to participate in the parade.
  [Wuhu "Poetry Leaflet"]

### 汽车修理厂小景
高少如
1958-07-09
第8版()
专栏：

    汽车修理厂小景
    高少如
    铁锤叮当，锣鼓敲响，
    技术革命，大闹一场，
    红花红花，遍地开放，
    师傅徒弟，满脸红光。
    进来破车，出去新车，
    出出进进，生产跃进。
    机具改进，生产革新，
    移山填海，智超孔明。
    三轮汽车，芜湖产品。
    开到北京，参加游行。
  〔芜湖“诗传单”〕
