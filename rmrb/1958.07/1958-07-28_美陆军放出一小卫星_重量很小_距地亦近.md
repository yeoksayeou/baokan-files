Title: U.S. Army Launches Small Satellite: Very Light, Low Altitude
Author:
Date: 1958-07-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, July 27th: The U.S. Army launched a small satellite weighing only one seventy-sixth of the Soviet Union's third artificial satellite into space at Cape Canaveral at 15:00 Universal Time on the 26th using a "Jupiter-C" rocket.

This newly launched U.S. satellite is called "Explorer IV," weighing 17.295 kilograms. The satellite body is conical, with a length of two meters, three centimeters shorter than "Explorer I." According to Agence France-Presse, this U.S. satellite is too small to be seen with the naked eye.

"Explorer IV" flies lower than the previous three U.S. satellites. The lowest point of its orbit is approximately 128 to 288 kilometers, and the highest point is 1,609 kilometers, 271 kilometers lower than the highest point of the Soviet Union's third satellite's orbit. This small U.S. satellite can only reach areas between 51 degrees north and 51 degrees south latitude on Earth, which is 14 degrees less in latitude both north and south than the areas reachable by the first three Soviet artificial satellites.

The instruments equipped on this small U.S. satellite are very simple and can only be used to study particle radiation.

### 美陆军放出一小卫星  重量很小　距地亦近

1958-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    美陆军放出一小卫星
    重量很小　距地亦近
    新华社27日讯　华盛顿消息：美国陆军于26日国际标准时间十五点正在卡纳维拉尔角用“丘辟特—C式”火箭把一个重量只及苏联第三个人造卫星七十六分之一的小卫星发射上天。
    美国这个新发射的小卫星名叫“探险者四号”，重量为一七·二九五公斤，卫星体呈圆锥形，长二公尺，比“探险者一号”还短三公分。据法新社报道，由于这个美国卫星体积太小，肉眼不能看到它。
      “探险者四号”比美国前三个卫星都飞得低，它的轨道的最低点约为一百二十八至二百八十八公里，最高点是一千六百零九公里，比苏联第三个卫星轨道的最高点低二百七十一公里。美国这个小卫星只能到达地球上北纬五十一度至南纬五十一度之间地区，比苏联头三个人造卫星所能达到地区在南、北纬度上各少十四度。
    这个美国小卫星所装备的仪器很简单，只能用来研究微粒辐射。
