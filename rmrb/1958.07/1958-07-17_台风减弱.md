Title: Typhoon Weakens
Author:
Date: 1958-07-17
Page: 7

Central Meteorological Science Research Institute typhoon report issued at 8:00 PM on July 16th: The typhoon moving in from the Southwest Pacific landed near Xiamen at around 10:00 AM today and continues to move in a west-by-north direction. At 5:00 PM today, the center was located near 24.5 degrees North latitude and 116.5 degrees East longitude, which is the border between southwestern Fujian and Guangdong. The typhoon's intensity has weakened and it will gradually dissipate. Heavy to torrential rains will still occur in the vicinity of the typhoon.

### 台风减弱

1958-07-17
第7版()
专栏：气象台

    台风减弱
    本报讯　7月16日下午八时中央气象科学研究所发布台风消息。由西南太平洋上移动来的台风，已于今天上午十时左右在厦门附近登陆，并继续向偏西方向移动。今天下午五时中心位置在北纬二十四点五度、东经一百一十六点五度附近即福建西南部与广东交界处，强度已经减弱并将逐渐消失。台风附近仍将有大雨到暴雨。
