Title: Chen Yi Receives French Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-07-20
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, July 19th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received the French journalists delegation this morning.

Present at the reception were Lu Minggu, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Journalists Association, and Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

The delegation consists of four members: Claude Lanzmann, Robert Clarke, Luce Mouchard, and Chris Marker. After arriving in China on July 2nd, they visited Beijing, Shanghai, Shenyang, Anshan, and other places.

### 陈毅副总理接见法国记者代表团

1958-07-20
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见法国记者代表团
    新华社19日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了法国记者代表团。
    接见时在座的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会副秘书长卢鸣谷、外交部新闻司副司长徐晃。
    这个代表团共四人：克劳德·兰斯曼，罗伯特·克拉克，路斯·苗查尔德，克里斯·马可。他们在7月2日到中国后，曾在北京、上海、沈阳、鞍山等地进行了访问。
