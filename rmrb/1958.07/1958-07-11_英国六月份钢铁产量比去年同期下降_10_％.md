Title: British Steel Production in June Decreased by 10% Compared to the Same Period Last Year
Author:
Date: 1958-07-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, July 10th: Britain's steel production continues to decline. According to figures released today by the Iron and Steel Board, steel production in June fell by 10% compared to the same month last year. In the first half of this year, Britain's steel production was 10.05 million tons, a decrease of 5.4% compared to the first half of 1957.

The continuous decline in steel production is due to a decrease in both steel exports and domestic consumption. Steel exports in the first five months of this year decreased by 13% compared to the same period last year, and domestic consumption decreased by 3%.

### 英国六月份钢铁产量比去年同期下降10％

1958-07-11
第6版()
专栏：

    英国六月份钢铁产量比去年同期下降10％
    据新华社伦敦10日电　英国的钢铁产量继续下降。根据钢铁局今天发表的数字，6月份，钢铁产量比去年同月下降了10％。今年上半年英国的钢产量是一千万零五十万吨，比1957年上半年下降了5.4％。
    钢铁产量不断下降是由于钢的出口和国内的消费量都下降了。今年头五个月的钢出口比去年同期下降了13％，国内的消费量下降了3％。
