Title: Mobilizing the People to Help the Revolutionary Army Defend the Country: The Republic of Iraq Will Establish Civil Defense Forces; The New Government Decides to Promulgate a Decree Releasing Political Prisoners
Author:
Date: 1958-07-19
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 18th: The Iraqi Republic Radio broadcast a statement from the Iraqi Armed Forces Command on the evening of the 17th, saying that the government of the Republic of Iraq has decided to establish civil defense forces. The Iraqi people will receive civil defense training to help the revolutionary army defend the country.

The radio station said that the Iraqi government will soon set up training centers across the country to conscript people to receive training there.

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 18th: According to the Iraqi Republic Radio broadcast on the evening of the 17th, the Iraqi Republican Cabinet decided on the evening of the 17th to promulgate a decree on the release of all political prisoners detained by the traitorous government of Said.

According to the radio broadcast, the Iraqi Republican Cabinet decided at the meeting that all those convicted in political trials by the Said regime should be regarded as political prisoners, canceling police supervision for those convicted in political trials and allowing them to return to their homes. The meeting also decided to restore the civil rights of Iraqi citizens deprived of them by the Said regime for political reasons.

The Iraqi Republican Cabinet also decided that students who were expelled from school by the Said regime for political reasons could return to schools or colleges to resume their studies, and teachers and professors who were dismissed could be reinstated.

The Iraqi Republican Cabinet also decided to instruct the Ministry of Justice to formulate decrees to reduce the sentences of ordinary criminals.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 17th: The Chief of Staff of the Ministry of Defense of the Republic of Iraq told a reporter from the Middle East News Agency through the first telephone connection between Cairo and Baghdad since the Iraqi revolution on the 17th that the Iraqi people were calm and full of confidence during the revolution, and they were united under the leadership of the revolutionary leaders.

When talking about the current situation in Iraq, the Chief of Staff said that the situation in Baghdad and other parts of the country is good.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 18th: According to an official Indian announcement, the Indian Chargé d'Affaires in Iraq, in presenting his views on the Iraqi situation to the Indian government, said that the government of the Republic of Iraq is in complete control of the situation and has been fully consolidated.

### 动员人民帮助革命军队保卫国家  伊拉克共和国将成立民防队  新政府决定颁布释放政治犯法令

1958-07-19
第3版()
专栏：

    动员人民帮助革命军队保卫国家
    伊拉克共和国将成立民防队
    新政府决定颁布释放政治犯法令
    新华社开罗18日电　伊拉克共和国电台17日晚间广播伊拉克武装部队司令部的一项公告说，伊拉克共和国政府决定成立民防队。伊拉克人民将接受民防训练，以帮助革命军队保卫国家。
    这家电台说，伊拉克政府不久将在全国各地设立训练中心，征调人民到那里接受训练。
    新华社大马士革18日电　据伊拉克共和国电台17日晚上广播，伊拉克共和国内阁在17日晚上决定要颁布一项关于释放被赛义德卖国政府拘禁的全部政治犯的法令。
    据这家电台广播，伊拉克共和国内阁在会议上已经决定，把赛义德政权在政治审讯中定罪的人一律看作政治犯；取消对于在政治审判中定罪的人们的警察监督，允许他们回到他们的家中去。会议同时决定恢复伊拉克公民因政治原因被赛义德政权所剥夺的公民权。
    伊拉克共和国内阁还决定，因政治原因被赛义德政权开除学籍的学生可以回到学校或学院恢复学业，被开除的教员和教授可以复职。
    伊拉克共和国内阁同时决定责成司法部制定法令，减轻普通罪犯的刑罚。
    据新华社开罗17日电　伊拉克共和国国防部参谋长17日通过开罗和巴格达之间自从伊拉克革命以来的第一次电话联系对中东通讯社记者说，伊拉克人民在革命中是镇静的，充满信心的，他们在革命领导人的领导下团结一致。
    这位参谋长在谈到伊拉克目前局势的时候说，巴格达的局势以及全国其他地方的局势都很好。
    新华社新德里18日电　据印度官方宣布，印度驻伊拉克大使馆临时代办在向印度政府陈述他对伊拉克局势的看法时说，伊拉克共和国政府完全控制了局势，而且已经十分巩固。
