Title: Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Council Calls on Prime Ministers of Afro-Asian Countries to Support the People of the Middle East in Achieving National Goals, Force the Immediate Withdrawal of Aggressor American and British Troops from the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1958-07-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 21st: The Secretariat of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Council today sent telegrams to the Prime Ministers of fourteen Afro-Asian countries regarding the tense situation in the Middle East caused by the occupation of Lebanon and Jordan by American and British troops. The fourteen countries are: Iraq, the United Arab Republic, China, the Soviet Union, Japan, Indonesia, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, India, Burma, Tunisia, Morocco, Ghana, and Mongolia.
    The telegram stated that the serious situation in the Middle East caused by the armed intervention of the United States and Britain in the internal affairs of Lebanon and Jordan constitutes an imminent threat to world peace. The telegram called on all countries to support the people of the Middle East in achieving their national goals; to protest to the United Nations against the intervention of the United States and Britain in violation of the right of self-determination of small countries; and to mobilize public opinion against the piratical acts of the United States and Britain, urging them to immediately withdraw from the Middle East in order to maintain world peace.
    The Secretariat of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Council also sent telegrams to U.S. President Eisenhower and British Prime Minister Macmillan, demanding the immediate withdrawal of U.S. and British armed forces from the Middle East. The telegrams pointed out that the Afro-Asian people strongly protested against the armed intervention of the United States and Britain in Lebanon and Jordan.
    On July 21st, the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Council also decided to establish a committee to unify the Secretariat's contact with the people of Afro-Asian countries and provide them with truthful information about the situation in the Middle East. This committee, composed of secretaries from the United Arab Republic, Cameroon, and the Soviet Union, will regularly issue announcements on the situation in the Middle East and distribute them to the Afro-Asian Solidarity Committees of various countries.

### 亚非人民团结理事会呼吁亚非各国总理  支持中东人民实现民族目的  迫使美英侵略军立即撤出中东

1958-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

    亚非人民团结理事会呼吁亚非各国总理
    支持中东人民实现民族目的
    迫使美英侵略军立即撤出中东
    新华社开罗21日电  亚非人民团结理事会书记处今天就美英军队占领黎巴嫩和约旦的行为所造成的中东紧张局势，打电报给十四个亚非国家的总理。这十四个国家是：伊拉克、阿拉伯联合共和国、中国、苏联、日本、印度尼西亚、越南民主共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、印度、缅甸、突尼斯、摩洛哥、加纳和蒙古。
    电报中说，美国和英国武装干涉黎巴嫩和约旦内政的行为在中东所造成的严重局势对世界和平构成了迫在眉睫的威胁。电报呼吁各国支持中东人民实现他们的民族目的；就美英的干涉行为违反了小国的自决权利一事向联合国提出抗议；并且动员舆论反对美英的海盗行为，促使它们立即从中东撤退，以便维护世界和平。
    亚非人民团结理事会书记处另外又打电报给美国总统艾森豪威尔和英国首相麦克米伦，要求美英两国武装部队立即撤出中东。电报中指出，亚非人民强烈抗议美英两国对黎巴嫩和约旦的武装干涉。
    亚非人民团结理事会书记处在21日还决定成立一个委员会，以统一支配书记处同亚非各国人民的接触，为他们提供有关中东局势的真实情报。这个委员会由阿拉伯联合共和国、喀麦隆和苏联的书记组成，它将经常就中东局势发表公告分发给各国的亚非团结委员会。
