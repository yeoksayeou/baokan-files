Title: Anti-Aggression Torrents Roll On: Shenyang and Zhengzhou Masses Continue Demonstrations; 1.4 Million People in Harbin Participate in Various Anti-Aggression Activities in One Day
Author:
Date: 1958-07-29
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, July 28th. Thirty thousand people in Shenyang braved intermittent drizzles on the 27th to take to the streets in demonstrations protesting the armed aggression of American and British imperialism in the Middle East.

Starting from early morning, the sounds of gongs and drums echoed through the streets. Batches of demonstrators and propaganda trucks appeared on the streets from time to time. Various artistic groups performed for the masses on the streets.

To prepare for today's massive demonstration, the teachers and students of the Luxun Academy of Fine Arts worked hard for 24 hours on the 26th, creating 86 kinds of propaganda posters and printing and reproducing more than 6,000 propaganda posters and slogans. Their works were posted all over the streets today. Faculty and staff from the Northeast Conservatory of Music formed 47 song-teaching groups and went to the streets and entertainment venues to teach the masses to sing songs against American and British aggression.

Residents, students, mothers of many children, and family members of revolutionary martyrs and soldiers from 14 streets in the Beishi District formed 23 demonstration teams. Zhao Mingzhou, an Ahong of Islam in Shenyang who had visited Arab countries, continuously led the demonstrators in singing "The Chinese People Will Never Agree" in the procession.

The Shenyang garrison also dispatched a 10,000-person cultural troupe, which, together with various local cultural troupes, formed a flood of protests and demonstrations.

Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, July 28th. Another 200,000 people in Zhengzhou held demonstrations on the 27th to denounce the aggressive crimes of American and British imperialism.

On the morning of the 27th, the streets and alleys of Zhengzhou and the buses were plastered with protest slogans and cartoons. People held high red flags and poured into the streets from government offices, factories, schools, and agricultural cooperatives. They shouted slogans angrily, demanding that American and British invading troops immediately get out of the Middle East and that American invading troops immediately get out of Taiwan.

Wherever the demonstration teams went, they gave speeches and carried out propaganda.

Many street residents also launched a signature campaign on the 27th to protest the aggression of American and British imperialism. 1,500 people signed in West Street alone in one day. On the evening of the same day, tens of thousands of people in Zhengzhou held torchlight processions.

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, July 28th. 1.4 million people in Harbin participated in various activities on the 27th to protest the piratical acts of American and British imperialism in the Middle East.

At a meeting held by the medical community in Daoli District to denounce American and British aggressors, 500 medical workers signed the "Protest Against the Atrocious Acts of American and British Aggression in the Middle East." Gray-haired old traditional Chinese medicine practitioners and young nurses just out of school expressed their strong protests against American and British imperialism.

The six major universities and colleges in Harbin City demonstrated while performing newly arranged artistic programs against American and British imperialist aggression in the Middle East. Harbin Agricultural College arranged a total of 18 programs, including "Yankee Devils Get Out!", which were performed during street demonstrations.

On the 27th, factories, government offices, streets, stores, and residential groups in Harbin City also automatically set up 1,365 signature stations to protest American and British imperialist aggression in the Middle East. By 3:00 p.m., more than 400,000 people had signed the protest letter.

### 反侵略的怒涛滚滚相连  沈阳郑州群众继续大示威  哈尔滨一天中就有一百四十万人参加各种反侵略活动

1958-07-29
第2版()
专栏：

    反侵略的怒涛滚滚相连
    沈阳郑州群众继续大示威
    哈尔滨一天中就有一百四十万人参加各种反侵略活动
    新华社沈阳28日电　沈阳市27日又有三十万人冒着时停时续的细雨，到街头举行示威游行抗议美英帝国主义对中东的武装侵略。
    从清晨开始，各个街道就响起了锣鼓声。一批批的示威者和宣传卡车不时在街头出现。各个文艺队伍都在街头向群众作了演出。
    为了准备今天这个声势浩大的示威游行，鲁迅美术学院的师生们26日苦战一昼夜，创作了八十六种宣传画，印制和复制了六千多张宣传画和标语。他们的作品今天贴满了各个街道。东北音乐专科学校的教职员工们组成了四十七个教歌小组，到街头和文娱场所教群众唱反对美英侵略的歌曲。
    北市区十四条街的居民、学生、多子女的母亲和烈属军属们组成了二十三个示威游行大队，曾经访问过阿拉伯国家的沈阳伊斯兰教阿訇赵铭周在游行队伍中不断地领导示威的人们唱着“中国人民决不答应”的歌曲。
    沈阳驻军也出动了一万人的文艺大队和市内的各个文艺队伍一起汇成抗议示威的洪流。
    新华社郑州28日电　郑州市27日又有二十万人举行示威游行声讨美英帝国主义的侵略罪行。
    27日上午，郑州市的大街小巷和公共汽车上到处贴满了抗议标语和漫画，人们高举红旗，从机关、工厂、学校、农业社涌向街头，他们愤怒地高呼口号，要美英侵略军立即从中东滚出去，美国侵略军立即从台湾滚出去。
    游行示威的队伍走到哪里，就在哪里演讲宣传。
    许多街道居民27日还开展了抗议美英帝国主义侵略的签名运动，西大街在一天内就有一千五百人签了名。当晚，郑州市还有数万群众高举着火炬举行了示威游行。
    新华社哈尔滨28日电　哈尔滨市27日有一百四十万人举行各种活动，抗议美英帝国主义在中东的海盗行为。
    在道里区医务界举行的声讨美英侵略者大会上，有五百名医务工作者在“反对美英侵略中东暴行的抗议书”上签了名。白发苍苍的老中医、刚出学校门口的女护士都向美英帝国主义表示强烈的抗议。
    哈尔滨市六个大专学校一面游行示威，一面演出了他们新编排的反对美英帝国主义侵略中东的文艺节目。哈尔滨农学院共排了“美国鬼子快滚蛋”等十八个节目，在街道上示威游行的时候进行了演出。
    27日，哈尔滨市各工厂、机关、街道、商店、居民组还自动设置了一千三百六十五处抗议美英帝国主义侵略中东的签名站，到下午三时，就有四十多万人在抗议书上签了名。
