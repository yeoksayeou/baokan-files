Title: Lock Up the Sun to Keep My Love

Author:

Date: 1958-07-06
Page: 4

Lock Up the Sun to Keep My Love
(New Love Song)

　　My love digs the hole, I plant the seedling,
　　Covering the mountain with a new green robe.
　　My love pulls the manure, I load the cart,
　　Wholeheartedly following my love.
　　The sun sets, the birds return to their nests,
　　The gong is about to sound to end the work.
　　Forge a chain, forge a lock,
　　Lock up the sun to keep my love.
　　(Inner Mongolia, Tumed Right Banner)

### 锁住太阳留住哥

1958-07-06
第4版()
专栏：

    锁住太阳留住哥
    （新情歌）
    情哥挖坑妹放苗，
    遍山披上新绿袍。
    情哥拉粪妹装车，
    一心跟着情哥哥。
    太阳落山鸟归窝，
    眼看就敲收兵锣。
    打把链子打把锁，
    锁住太阳留住哥。
      （内蒙古土默特旗）
