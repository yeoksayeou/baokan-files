Title: US Pilot Threatens to Drop Atomic Bomb Near Britain, British Authorities Scramble in Panic
Author:
Date: 1958-07-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, July 4th: According to reports, Military Intelligence and the headquarters of the Secret Police here, MI5 and Scotland Yard, have begun frantically investigating an anonymous letter, supposedly written by an American pilot, threatening to drop an atomic bomb near Britain.

The letter, dated June 28th, was sent from Ipswich, England, to the Soviet Ambassador to Britain, Malik. A copy of the letter was given to the British Foreign Office by the Soviet Embassy.

The letter stated, "Dulles and NATO are ignoring the goodwill shown to them by the Russian leaders. Dulles must be forced to accept this goodwill.

I intend to drop the deadly thing I carry during one of our routine flights. My target will be somewhere in the North Sea, not too far from the British coast, but far enough away that not too many people will be killed.

The date of our action will be sometime after July 1st.

Hopefully, this one atomic bomb will stop those who want to drop hundreds of atomic bombs."

### 美飞行员扬言要在英国附近投原子弹  英国当局吓得手忙脚乱

1958-07-05
第6版()
专栏：

    美飞行员扬言要在英国附近投原子弹
    英国当局吓得手忙脚乱
    据新华社伦敦4日电　据报道：这里的军事情报和秘密警察总部军事情报局和伦敦警察厅已慌慌张张地着手侦查据说是一个美国飞行员所写的一封扬言要在英国附近投扔原子弹的匿名信案件。
    这封信上的日期是6月28日，它是从英格兰伊普斯威池寄给苏联驻英国大使马立克的。这封信的抄本由苏联大使馆交给英国外交部。
    这封信说，“杜勒斯和北大西洋公约组织不理睬俄国领袖们向他们表示的善意。必须迫使杜勒斯接受这种善意。
    我打算在我们的一次例行飞行中投下我所带的致命的东西。我的目标将是北海中的某处，离开英国沿岸不太远，但是离开的距离足以使被杀的人不会太多。
    我们的行动日期将是7月1日以后的某一个时候。
    希望这一颗原子弹能够制止那些希望投下数以百计的原子弹的人们”。
