Title: Supporting Our Lebanese Brothers
Author: Li Guangtian
Date: 1958-07-19
Page: 8
What talk of protecting American nationals,
What talk of safeguarding the territorial integrity of Lebanon,
No matter what excuses you fabricate,
The fact remains that you have invaded Lebanon!
This is a provocation against the Arab people,
This is a provocation against the Asian and African people,
This is a provocation against the peace-loving people of the world,
This is a provocation against world peace!
The Chinese people resolutely forbid it,
The Asian and African people resolutely forbid it,
The people of the whole world resolutely forbid it,
Forbid you from invading Lebanon!
Lebanese brothers, persevere in your struggle,
Arab people, continue to advance,
The Chinese people are supporting you,
Final victory will surely belong to you!

### 支持黎巴嫩兄弟
李广田
1958-07-19
第8版()
专栏：

    支持黎巴嫩兄弟
    李广田
    说什么保护美国侨民，
    说什么维护黎巴嫩领土的完整，
    不管你制造什么借口，
    反正是你侵略了黎巴嫩！
    这是对阿拉伯人民的挑衅，
    这是对亚非人民的挑衅，
    这是对全世界和平人民的挑衅，
    这是对世界和平的挑衅！
    中国人民坚决不准，
    亚非人民坚决不准，
    全世界人民坚决不准，”
    不准你侵略黎巴嫩！
    黎巴嫩兄弟要坚持斗争，
    阿拉伯人民要继续前进，
    中国人民在支持你们，
    最后胜利一定属于你们！
