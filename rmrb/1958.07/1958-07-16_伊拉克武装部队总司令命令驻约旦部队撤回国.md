Title: Commander-in-Chief of the Iraqi Armed Forces Orders Troops Stationed in Jordan to Withdraw

Author:

Date: 1958-07-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 15th: Baghdad Radio broadcast this morning that the Commander-in-Chief of the Iraqi Armed Forces has ordered all Iraqi troops currently in Jordan to withdraw to Iraq. The order requires these troops to take all necessary measures to defend themselves during the withdrawal process.

Iraqi troops entered Jordan in May after Iraq and Jordan formally established a federation.

### 伊拉克武装部队总司令命令驻约旦部队撤回国

1958-07-16
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克武装部队总司令命令驻约旦部队撤回国
    新华社大马士革15日电　巴格达电台今天早晨广播说，伊拉克武装部队总司令命令现在约旦的伊拉克军队全部撤回伊拉克。命令要求这些军队采取一切必要措施来在撤退过程中保卫自己。
    伊拉克军队是在5月间伊拉克和约旦正式成立联邦后开进约旦的。
