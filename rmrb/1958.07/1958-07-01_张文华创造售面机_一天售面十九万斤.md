    Zhang Wenhua Creates Flour Vending Machine
    Sells 190,000 Jin of Flour Per Day
    (Our Newspaper News) Zhang Wenhua of the Mao'er Xiang Grain Store of the Taiyuan City Grain Bureau has successfully created an automatic flour vending machine.

    This machine can sell more than 20,000 jin of flour per hour. Its efficiency is 60 times faster than using small scales and 20 times faster than using platform scales. An automatic flour vending machine can sell 190,000 jin of flour in an eight-hour day, which is equivalent to the daily flour sales of sixty grain stores. (Liu Quanshan)

### 张文华创造售面机  一天售面十九万斤
刘泉山
1958-07-01
第4版()
专栏：敢想、敢说、敢做！

    张文华创造售面机
    一天售面十九万斤
    本报讯　太原市粮食局帽儿巷粮食门市部张文华创造自动售面机成功。
    这台机器每小时可以卖出面粉二万余斤，效率比小秤快到六十倍，比磅秤快二十倍。一台自动售面机一天八小时可以卖面十九万斤，相当于六十个门市部一天的面粉销售量。　（刘泉山）
