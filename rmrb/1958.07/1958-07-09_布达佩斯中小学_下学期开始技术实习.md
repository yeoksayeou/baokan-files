Title: Technical Internships to Begin in Budapest Primary and Secondary Schools Next Semester
Author:
Date: 1958-07-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, July 8th: Starting in the second half of this year, most primary and secondary schools in Budapest will add technical internship courses. The teaching content of these courses in urban schools will mainly be related to industrial production, while in schools in suburban agricultural areas, the teaching content will mainly be related to agricultural production. According to the plan, when school starts this fall, Budapest will have eleven middle schools and seventy-seven primary schools begin teaching technical internship classes, starting from the fifth grade in primary school and the first grade in middle school.

### 布达佩斯中小学  下学期开始技术实习

1958-07-09
第5版()
专栏：

    布达佩斯中小学
    下学期开始技术实习
    据新华社布达佩斯8日电  从今年下半年起，布达佩斯的大部分中小学校将增设技术实习课程。市区学校增设的这种课程的教学内容主要是同工业生产有关的，在郊区农业地区的学校，这种课程的教学内容主要是同农业生产有关的。根据计划，在今年秋季开学时，布达佩斯将有十一个中学和七十七个小学开始技术实习课的教学，小学从五年级开始，中学从一年级开始。
