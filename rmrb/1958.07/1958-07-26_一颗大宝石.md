Title: A Large Gemstone
Author: Mei Bo
Date: 1958-07-26
Page: 6

    A Large Gemstone
    In the Liulin Quarry, Zhanjiaqiao Township, Linxiang County, Hunan Province, quarry workers Feng Jinhai and a dozen others, while mining in the white rock stratum, unearthed a world-rare, gigantic beryl weighing 2,300 *jin* (1150 kg). According to relevant personnel's inspection, it is of excellent quality and contains more than 8% beryllium oxide, indeed a rare and gigantic gemstone king. This gemstone king will soon be transported to Beijing for exhibition.
                  (Mei Bo)

### 一颗大宝石
梅波
1958-07-26
第6版()
专栏：

    一颗大宝石
    湖南省临湘县詹家桥乡，柳林采石场，采石工人冯金海等十多人，在白石林岩层中，采挖出一颗世界稀有的巨大绿柱石，重达二千三百斤。据有关人员检验品质优良、含氧化铍8％以上，确是一颗稀有的巨大宝石王。这颗宝石王即将运来北京展览。
                  （梅波）
