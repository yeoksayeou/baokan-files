Title: Cartoons Showing the "Free World"
Author:
Date: 1958-07-01
Page: 6

From cartoons showing such a "free world"
　　
　　Rather broken
　　American bosses would rather let a large number of unsalable goods spoil than let the unemployed get a little benefit.
　　　　　　Originally published in the American "United Mine Workers Journal"
　　
　　"Fair" trade
　　Surgeon: He only paid half the surgery fee, I can only do half the surgery for him!
　　　　　　Originally published in the Italian weekly "Il Travaso"
　　
　　Saying sarcastic remarks
　　Manager: I don't like my employees selling matches at night! Why not let your wife do it?
　　(The treatment of junior clerks in British companies is meager, and some people do small business at night to increase their income.)
　　　　　　Originally published in London's "Daily Mirror"
　　
　　Bayonet rule
　　The struggle of the people of the Union of South Africa for political and economic equality is growing. The handful of racists who rely on bayonets to maintain the "white supremacy" are in an increasingly precarious situation.
　　　　　　Originally published in the Union of South Africa's "New Age" newspaper

### 从漫画看如此“自由世界”（图片）

1958-07-01
第6版()
专栏：

    从漫画看如此“自由世界”
    宁可坏掉
    美国的大老板宁可让大批销不出去的货物坏掉，也不肯让失业者得到一点好处。
              原载美国“联合煤矿工人报”
    “公平”交易
    外科医生：他只付了一半手术费，我也只能替他动一半手术！
                    原载意大利“特拉伐若”周刊
    说风凉话
    经理：我不喜欢我的雇员在晚上贩卖火柴！为什么不让你的太太来干这事？
    （英国公司的小职员待遇微薄，有的人在晚上作小生意，以增加收入。）
                  原载伦敦“每日镜报”
    刺刀统治
    南非联邦人民争取政治和经济平等权利的斗争日益浩大，依靠刺刀来维持“白种人优越”的统治地位的一小撮种族主义者，处境已日益不妙。
                        原载南非联邦“新世纪”报
