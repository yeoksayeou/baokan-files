Title: Malayan National Liberation Army Active; Federation Military Imposes Curfew Again in Southern Perak; Defense Minister Says Government Controls Only One-Third of Region
Author:
Date: 1958-07-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 1st, Kuala Lumpur News: According to newspaper reports, the activities of the Malayan National Liberation Army have recently increased.

The Malayan Federation's Minister of Defense, Razak, said at a recent gathering that areas fully controlled by the government currently account for only one-third of the total area of Malaya. He revealed that the Malayan Federation government spends 126 million yuan annually to implement the "state of emergency."

Malayan Federation military authorities recently announced a curfew in the southern Perak area to block the activities of the Malayan National Liberation Army in the region.

### 马来亚民族解放军活跃  联合邦军方又在霹雳州南部实行宵禁  国防部长说政府只控制三分之一地区

1958-07-03
第6版()
专栏：

    马来亚民族解放军活跃
    联合邦军方又在霹雳州南部实行宵禁  国防部长说政府只控制三分之一地区
    据新华社1日讯　吉隆坡消息：据报纸报道，马来亚民族解放军的活动最近已有增加。
    马来亚联合邦国防部长拉扎克最近在一个集会上说，目前完全受政府控制的地区只占马来亚全国面积的三分之一。他透露，为了实行“紧急状态”，马来亚联合邦政府每年要花费一亿二千六百万元。
    马来亚联合邦军事当局最近又宣布在霹雳州南部一带实施宵禁令，以图封锁马来亚民族解放军在这一地区的活动。
