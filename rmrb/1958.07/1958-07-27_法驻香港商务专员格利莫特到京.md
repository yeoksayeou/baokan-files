Title: French Commercial Commissioner to Hong Kong, Grimot, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-07-27
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, July 26th. Mr. Grimot, the French Commercial Commissioner to Hong Kong, arrived in Beijing recently at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

After arriving in Beijing, Grimot has begun to exchange views with officials from the Ministry of Foreign Trade on Sino-French trade issues.

### 法驻香港商务专员格利莫特到京

1958-07-27
第2版()
专栏：

    法驻香港商务专员格利莫特到京
    新华社26日讯　应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请来我国访问的法国驻香港商务专员格利莫特，已经在最近到达北京。
    格利莫特到北京后，已开始同对外贸易部官员就中法贸易问题交换意见。
