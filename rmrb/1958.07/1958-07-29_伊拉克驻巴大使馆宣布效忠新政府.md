Title: Iraqi Embassy in Pakistan Announces Allegiance to New Government
Author: 
Date: 1958-07-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 26th - According to today's "Today's News," the Iraqi Embassy in Pakistan has decided to pledge allegiance to the government of the Republic of Iraq.

It is reported that the diplomats at the embassy said they are working according to the orders of the government of the Republic of Iraq.

### 伊拉克驻巴大使馆宣布效忠新政府

1958-07-29
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克驻巴大使馆
    宣布效忠新政府
    新华社卡拉奇26日电　据“今天报”今天报道，伊拉克驻巴基斯坦大使馆已经决定效忠伊拉克共和国政府。
    据报道，这个大使馆的外交人员说，他们正按照伊拉克共和国政府的命令进行工作。
