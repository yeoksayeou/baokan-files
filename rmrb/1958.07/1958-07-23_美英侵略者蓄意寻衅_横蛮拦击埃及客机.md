Title: US-British Aggressors Deliberately Provoke, Brazenly Intercept Egyptian Passenger Plane
Author:
Date: 1958-07-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd, TASS, Cairo, July 21st: According to "Al-Ahram" newspaper, an Egyptian "Misr" Airlines passenger plane, flying along the normal route from Damascus via Beirut to Cairo on the 20th, was fired upon by US aggressor troops over Lebanon. "Al-Ahram" also stated that on the same day, British military aircraft also pursued another "Misr" company plane flying from Iraq to Damascus.

### 美英侵略者蓄意寻衅  横蛮拦击埃及客机

1958-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    美英侵略者蓄意寻衅
    横蛮拦击埃及客机
    新华社22日讯  塔斯社开罗21日讯：据“金字塔报”报道，埃及
  “密斯尔”航空公司的一架客机20日在沿着从大马士革经过贝鲁特到开罗的正常航线飞行时，在黎巴嫩上空遭到美国侵略军的射击。“金字塔报”还说，同一天，英国军用飞机还追击了“密斯尔”公司的另一架从伊拉克飞往大马士革的客机。
