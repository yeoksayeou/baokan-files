Title: Protest

Author: Tian Jian

Date: 1958-07-20

Page: 8

    Protest
    Tian Jian
    
    I.
    Arabs are lions,
    Why should they fear paper tigers?
    Lions, oh, Arabs,
    Drive away that bunch of American donkeys!
    
    II.
    Iraq and Lebanon,
    Light the torches of battle;
    The pyramids are war flags,
    The Mediterranean is the war drum.
    
    III.
    The people's hands are spearheads,
    Pointing towards the port of Beirut—
    For robbers, use precious swords,
    For jackals, use axes.
    
    IV.
    Beirut is a volcano,
    And the grave of robbers;
    If the American goons don't scram,
    They'll have to leave their bones behind.
    
    V.
    Triangle Hill can testify,
    Nylon vests can't protect you;
    Suez can testify,
    The paper tiger can't move an inch.
    
    VI.
    On the flag of Lebanon,
    It is said there is a tree;
    If this tree is waved,
    The paper tiger will be pierced right away.
    
    VII.
    The steel helmets worn by the Americans,
    Fall to the ground and become dirt;
    In their steel helmets,
    We plant the tree of freedom. (Note)
    
    VIII.
    If the paper tiger falls into the sea,
    Even God can't save him;
    If he wants to keep his life,
    He has to rein in at the edge of the cliff.
    
    IX.
    The righteous banner of Baghdad is raised,
    Flowers bloom in the desert;
    Those who fight for freedom,
    Have friends all over the world.
    
    X.
    Stones may rot, seas may dry,
    Arabia must be free;
    We and our Arab friends—
    Shake hands in battle!
    
    [Note] The Chinese People's Volunteers planted flowers and small trees in the steel helmets of the American aggressors.

### 抗议书
田间
1958-07-20
第8版()
专栏：

    抗议书
    田间
    一
    阿拉伯人是狮子，
    难道还怕纸老虎；
    狮子呀阿拉伯人，
    赶走那群美国驴！
    二
    伊拉克和黎巴嫩，
    点起战斗的火炬；
    金字塔就是战旗，
    地中海就是战鼓。
    三
    人民的手是矛头，
    指向贝鲁特港口——
    对强盗要用宝刀，
    对豺狼要用斧头。
    四
    贝鲁特是火山口，
    也是强盗的墓窟；
    美国佬要是不滚走，
    就得抛下他的骨头。
    五
    上甘岭可以作证，
    尼龙弹衣护不了身；
    苏伊士可以作证，
    纸老虎寸步难行。
    六
    黎巴嫩的旗子上，
    据说是有一棵树；
    这棵树挥动起来，
    纸老虎马上就戳穿。
    七
    美国佬戴的钢盔，
    掉下地就成泥土；
    在他们的钢盔里，
    我们栽上自由树。（注）
    八
    纸老虎掉到海里，
    上帝也没法救他；
    他想要留一条命，
    他就得悬崖勒马。
    九
    巴格达的义旗举起来，
    沙漠上面鲜花开；
    为自由而战的人，
    朋友遍于全世界。
    十
    石可烂，海可枯，
    阿拉伯一定要自由；
    我们和阿拉伯朋友——
    在战斗中握起手！
    〔注〕中国人民志愿军用美国侵略者的钢盔栽上花和小树。
