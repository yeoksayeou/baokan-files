Title: Iraq: Stable Prices; Special High Military Tribunal Established
Author:
Date: 1958-07-23
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 21st – The Iraqi Republic Radio announced on the 21st that the Iraqi Republic Military Governor, Ahmed Saleh al-Abdi, said on the 21st that, due to the Iraqi people’s response to the government’s call, national food prices had decreased and stabilized.

He called on the people to continue to assist the government in suppressing speculative merchants.

The Iraqi Republic Radio broadcast a statement by the Iraqi Republic Minister of Economy, Ibrahim Kubba, on the same day. Kubba said in the statement that Iraq’s imports and exports would be regulated according to the needs of the country and the welfare of the people.

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 22nd – According to the Iraqi Republic Radio’s evening broadcast on the 21st, the Iraqi six-person Special High Military Tribunal, headed by Colonel Fatimi Abbas Mihadawi, was established on the 21st. The Iraqi Cabinet meeting decided to establish this military tribunal on the 20th and authorized the Minister of Defense to implement this decision.

### 伊拉克物价稳定  特别高级军事法庭成立

1958-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克物价稳定
    特别高级军事法庭成立
    据新华社开罗21日电  伊拉克共和国电台21日宣布：伊拉克共和国军事长官艾哈迈德·萨勒·阿卜迪21日说，由于伊拉克人民响应政府的号召，全国食品价格已经下降，并且已经稳定。
    他要求人民继续协助政府镇压投机商人。
    伊拉克共和国电台同一天广播了伊拉克共和国经济部长易卜拉欣·库巴发表的一项声明。库巴在声明中说，伊拉克的进出口将根据国家的需要和人民的福利来调节。
    新华社大马士革22日电  据伊拉克共和国电台21日晚间广播，以法提米·阿巴斯·米哈达维上校为首的伊拉克六人特别高级军事法庭已经在21日成立。伊拉克内阁会议在20日决定成立这个军事法庭，并且授权国防部长执行这个决定。
