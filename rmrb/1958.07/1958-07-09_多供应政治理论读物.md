Title: Supply More Political Theory Readings
Author: Li Feng
Date: 1958-07-09
Page: 7

Readers' Comments

    Supply More Political Theory Readings
    Countless eloquent facts have made people deeply appreciate the truth that "politics is the soul, the commander." The atmosphere of reading in our school has undergone great changes, and more people are reading political theory books. Many people have drawn up leap forward plans for theoretical study. However, at present, we have encountered a problem that needs to be solved quickly. We cannot buy books such as "Socialism: Utopian and Scientific" and "Philosophical Notebooks" in the Pingdu Bookstore in Shandong. We hope that relevant departments will pay attention to solving this problem.
        Li Feng, Pingdu Normal School, Shandong

### 多供应政治理论读物
李锋
1958-07-09
第7版()
专栏：读者的话

    多供应政治理论读物
    无数雄辩的事实使人们深刻地体会到“政治是灵魂是统帅”这一真理，我们学校读书的风气有了很大的变化，阅读政治理论书籍的人多起来了，很多人都订出了理论学习的跃进规划。但是，目前遇到了一个需要迅速解决的问题，就是我们在山东平度书店里买不到“社会主义从空想到科学的发展”、“哲学笔记”等书，希望有关部门注意解决这一问题。
        山东平度师范  李锋
