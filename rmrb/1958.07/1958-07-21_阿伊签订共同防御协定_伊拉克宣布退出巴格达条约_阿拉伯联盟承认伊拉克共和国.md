Title: Iraq and UAR Sign Joint Defense Pact; Iraq Announces Withdrawal from Baghdad Pact; Arab League Recognizes Republic of Iraq
Author:
Date: 1958-07-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 19th. The governments of the United Arab Republic and the Republic of Iraq signed an agreement in Damascus on July 19th declaring joint defense. The agreement stipulates that the two countries will "act as one nation to resist aggression against either of them or against either one of them."

The agreement was signed by Gamal Abdel Nasser, President of the United Arab Republic, and Arif, Vice Premier of the Republic of Iraq.

The full text of the agreement is as follows:

"Agreement signed on the 2nd day of Muharram, 1378 A.H., i.e., July 19, 1958, between the United Arab Republic and the Republic of Iraq.

The delegations of the United Arab Republic and the Republic of Iraq agree as follows:

I. To reaffirm all agreements and treaties linking the two countries, first and foremost the Arab League Charter and the Arab Joint Defense Treaty.

II. To affirm the statements issued separately by the two governments concerning their close relations in the international situation, and agree that the two countries shall act as one nation to resist aggression against either of them or against either one of them, and shall take all necessary and effective steps to that end.

III. To cooperate fully in international affairs to defend the interests of both countries and to actively support the United Nations Charter and strengthen peace in the Middle East and the world.

IV. To take urgent and effective steps to establish close economic and cultural cooperation between the two countries.

V. To maintain continuous contact and consultation on all issues related to the two countries.

Representative of the United Arab Republic: Gamal Abdel Nasser
                                           (Signed)
Representative of the Republic of Iraq: Abdul Salam Arif
                                           (Signed)

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 19th.  Rubai, Chairman of the Supreme Power Committee of the Republic of Iraq, and Vice Premier Arif arrived in Damascus on the 19th to hold talks with Gamal Abdel Nasser, President of the United Arab Republic.  Jamal, Foreign Minister of the Republic of Iraq, Shanshal, Minister of National Guidance, and Habib, Minister of Finance, arrived in Damascus with them.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 19th.  On the 19th, a mass rally was held here in Withdrawal Square to welcome the leaders of the government of the Republic of Iraq.  Arif, Vice Premier of the Republic of Iraq, Jamal, Foreign Minister, Shanshal, Minister of National Guidance, Gamal Abdel Nasser, President of the United Arab Republic, and former Syrian President Kuwatly, spoke at the meeting.

Vice Premier Arif said in his speech that the Iraqi revolution was a comprehensive victory, and that this revolution had the same goals as the revolution of the United Arab Republic. He said: "Today's Iraq is not yesterday's Iraq. If anyone commits any aggression against Iraq, today's Iraq will resist everywhere and will defend every inch of its territory."

President Nasser said in his speech that the United Arab Republic will give the Republic of Iraq all the weapons it needs to defend Iraq, the United Arab Republic, and Arab nationalism. He said that we will definitely defend our country until the last drop of blood is shed. But at the same time, we must do our best to strive for peace.

Their speeches were constantly interrupted by cheers from the crowd.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 19th. Radio of the Republic of Iraq announced tonight that the government of the Republic of Iraq has officially withdrawn from the Baghdad Pact. The radio also said that the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Iraq has notified the envoys of various countries stationed in Iraq of this decision.

Radio of the Republic of Iraq also announced that the Embassy of the Republic of Iraq in Iran has notified the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Iran that the Republic of Iraq has withdrawn from the Baghdad Pact. The embassy also notified the embassies of other countries in Iran of the decision of the Republic of Iraq.

At the same time, the government of the Republic of Iraq has notified its envoys abroad to notify the governments of their host countries that the Republic of Iraq has withdrawn from the Baghdad Pact.

Radio of the Republic of Iraq also announced on the evening of the 19th that the Republic of Iraq has notified the United States government that the Republic of Iraq has withdrawn from the Iraqi-Jordanian Federation.

Saleh Mahdi, Chargé d'Affaires of the Iraqi Embassy in the United States, submitted a memorandum to the U.S. Department of State on this matter.

Mahdi said that King Hussein of Jordan no longer has any relationship with the Republic of Iraq.

He said that if the United States government insists on withdrawing its expatriates from Iraq, the government of the Republic of Iraq is prepared to assist them. He also said that the approximately 1,900 American expatriates now in Iraq are absolutely safe and that the situation within the Republic is stable.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 20th.  A spokesman for the Arab League announced that the Arab League officially recognized the Republic of Iraq today.

According to "Al-Ahram," the Secretariat of the Arab League decided to notify the Iraqi government that the Arab League no longer recognizes the so-called Arab Federation, i.e., the artificially established Iraqi and Jordanian federation.

The Secretariat of the League received a notification from the government of the Republic of Iraq stating that after the revolution in Iraq, Iraq did not recognize the obligations signed by former King Faisal regarding Iraq's participation in this federation, considered itself not bound by these obligations, and declared its withdrawal from this federation.

### 阿伊签订共同防御协定  伊拉克宣布退出巴格达条约　阿拉伯联盟承认伊拉克共和国

1958-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

    阿伊签订共同防御协定
    伊拉克宣布退出巴格达条约
　　阿拉伯联盟承认伊拉克共和国
    新华社大马士革19日电  阿拉伯联合共和国政府和伊拉克共和国政府19日在大马士革签订了一项宣布实行共同防御的协定。协定规定两国“像一个国家一样采取行动来抵抗对它们两国或者对它们当中任何一国的侵略”。
    这项协定是由阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔和伊拉克共和国副总理阿里夫签署的。
    协定全文如下：
    “在希吉勒历1378年1月2日，即公历1958年7月19日，阿拉伯联合共和国和伊拉克共和国签订的协定。
    阿拉伯联合共和国和伊拉克共和国的代表团商定如下：
    一、重新肯定把两国联结在一起的所有协定和条约，首先是阿拉伯联盟公约和阿拉伯联合防御条约。
    二、确认两国政府分别发表的关于它们在国际局势中进行密切联系的声明，并且同意两国像一个国家一样采取行动来抵抗对它们两国或者对它们当中任何一国的侵略，并且将为此采取一切必要的和有效的步骤。
    三、在国际方面进行充分合作，以保卫两国的利益，并且积极支持联合国宪章和加强中东和世界的和平。
    四、采取紧急而有效的步骤来在两国之间建立经济和文化方面的密切合作。
    五、就一切同两国有关的问题进行不断的接触和协商。
    阿拉伯联合共和国代表加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔
                                            （签字）
    伊拉克共和国代表阿卜杜勒·萨拉姆·阿里夫
                                            （签字）
    新华社大马士革19日电  伊拉克共和国最高权力委员会主席鲁巴伊和副总理阿里夫在19日到达大马士革同阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔举行会谈。和他们一起到大马士革的有伊拉克共和国外交部长贾迈尔、国家指导部部长尚沙尔和财政部长哈比卜。
    据新华社大马士革19日电  19日，这里在撤兵广场举行了群众大会，欢迎伊拉克共和国政府领导人。伊拉克共和国副总理阿里夫，外交部长贾迈尔、国家指导部长尚沙尔，阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔，前叙利亚总统库阿特利，先后在会上讲了话。
    阿里夫副总理在讲话时说，伊拉克革命是一个全面的胜利，这次革命和阿拉伯联合共和国的革命有着同样的目的。他说：“今天的伊拉克已经不是昨天的伊拉克了，如果有人对伊拉克进行任何侵略，今天的伊拉克将在到处进行抵抗，并且将捍卫它的每一寸领土。”
    纳赛尔总统在讲话中说，阿拉伯联合共和国将给予伊拉克共和国为了保卫伊拉克、阿拉伯联合共和国和阿拉伯民族主义所需要的一切武器。他说，我们一定保卫我们的国家，直到流尽最后一滴血。但在同时，我们一定尽我们全部力量来争取和平。
    他们的讲话不断被群众的欢呼声所打断。
    新华社开罗19日电  伊拉克共和国电台今晚宣布，伊拉克共和国政府已经正式退出巴格达条约。这个电台又说，伊拉克共和国外交部已经把这个决定通知各国驻伊拉克的使节。
    伊拉克共和国电台还宣布，伊拉克共和国驻伊朗大使馆已经通知伊朗外交部，伊拉克共和国已经退出巴格达条约。大使馆还把伊拉克共和国的决定通知其他各国驻伊朗的大使馆。
    同时，伊拉克共和国政府已经通知伊拉克的驻外使节们，要他们通知他们的驻在国政府，伊拉克共和国已经退出巴格达条约。
    伊拉克共和国电台19日晚上还宣布，伊拉克共和国通知美国政府，伊拉克共和国已经退出伊拉克—约旦联邦。
    伊拉克驻美国大使馆临时代办萨勒·马赫迪就这件事向美国国务院递交了一份备忘录。
    马赫迪说，约旦国王侯赛因同伊拉克共和国已经不再有关系。
    他说，如果美国政府坚持要从伊拉克撤出它的侨民，伊拉克共和国政府准备协助他们。他又说，美国现在在伊拉克的约一千九百名侨民绝对安全，共和国内的情况是平定的。
    新华社开罗20日电  阿拉伯联盟发言人宣布，阿拉伯联盟今天正式承认伊拉克共和国。
    据“金字塔报”报道，阿拉伯联盟秘书处决定通知伊拉克政府，阿拉伯联盟今后不再承认所谓阿拉伯联邦即人为建立的伊拉克和约旦联邦的存在。
    联盟秘书处收到了伊拉克共和国政府的通知，其中说，在伊拉克发生革命之后，伊拉克不承认前国王费萨尔所签订的关于伊拉克参加这个联邦的义务，认为自己不受这些义务的约束，并且声明退出这个联邦。
