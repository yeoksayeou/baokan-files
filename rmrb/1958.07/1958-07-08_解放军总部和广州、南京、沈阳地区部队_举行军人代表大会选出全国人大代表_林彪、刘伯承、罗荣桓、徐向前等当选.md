Title: PLA Headquarters and Guangzhou, Nanjing, and Shenyang Regional Troops Hold Military Representatives Congresses to Elect National People's Congress Deputies; Lin Biao, Liu Bocheng, Luo Ronghuan, Xu Xiangqian, etc. Elected

Author:
Date: 1958-07-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 7th. The General Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army (PLA) and the Guangzhou and Nanjing regional troops recently held military representative congresses to elect deputies to the Second National People's Congress. The deputies elected by the PLA General Headquarters directly affiliated organs, academies, and troops are: Marshal Xu Xiangqian, General Fu Zhong, General Yang Zhicheng, Lieutenant General Li Tianhuan, Lieutenant General Wang Zheng, Senior Colonel Ding Zhihui (female), and six others.

The deputies elected by the Guangzhou regional troops include: Marshal Lin Biao, General Tan Zheng, General Li Tianyou, General Chen Mingren, Lieutenant General Zhan Caifang, combat hero Lieutenant Colonel Liu Xingsheng, and Tashan combat hero Major Cheng Yuanmao, among seven people.

The deputies elected by the Nanjing regional troops are: Marshal Liu Bocheng, General Su Yu, Lieutenant General Xiao Wangdong, Senior Colonel Tang Qiu (female), Major Zhou Wenjiang, Major Chi Haotian, among six people.

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, June 6th. The Chinese People's Liberation Army troops stationed in the Shenyang area solemnly held a military representative congress on the 3rd, electing Marshal Luo Ronghuan, General Lai Chuanzhu, General Zhou Chunquan, General Chen Bojun, General Wang Jian'an, combat hero Lieutenant Colonel Zhao Xingyuan, and second-class combat hero Captain Xu Henglu, among seven people, as deputies to the Second National People's Congress.

### 解放军总部和广州、南京、沈阳地区部队  举行军人代表大会选出全国人大代表  林彪、刘伯承、罗荣桓、徐向前等当选

1958-07-08
第4版()
专栏：

    解放军总部和广州、南京、沈阳地区部队
    举行军人代表大会选出全国人大代表
    林彪、刘伯承、罗荣桓、徐向前等当选
    新华社7日讯  中国人民解放军总部直属机关和广州、南京地区部队，最近分别举行军人代表大会，选举第二届全国人民代表大会代表。解放军总部直属机关、院校和部队选出的代表是：徐向前元帅、傅钟上将、杨至诚上将、李天焕中将、王诤中将、丁志辉（女）上校等六人。
    广州地区部队选出的代表有：林彪元帅、谭政大将、李天佑上将、陈明仁上将、詹才芳中将、战斗英雄刘兴胜中校、塔山战斗英雄程远茂少校等七人。
    南京地区部队选出的代表是：刘伯承元帅、粟裕大将、萧望东中将、唐求（女）上校、周文江少校、迟浩田少校等六人。
    据新华社沈阳6日电  中国人民解放军驻沈阳地区部队，3日隆重举行军人代表大会，选出罗荣桓元帅、赖传珠上将、周纯全上将、陈伯钧上将、王建安上将、战斗英雄赵兴元中校、二级战斗英雄徐恒禄大尉等七人为出席第二届全国人民代表大会代表。
