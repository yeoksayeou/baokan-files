Title: Nehru Speaks Again on the Middle East Situation; Indian National Congress Chairman Opposes U.S. Intervention in Lebanon
Author:
Date: 1958-07-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 16th. Indian Prime Minister Nehru stated on the 16th at a gathering of students at Allahabad University in Uttar Pradesh, India, that "Although we do not intend to intervene in the affairs of Iraq and Lebanon, we are very concerned about the developments in these countries."

	He said, "Undoubtedly, these are their internal affairs. You could say it is a civil war. But if foreign countries interfere in these affairs, there is a great danger of causing a world war." He said, "If a foreign country enters the scene of conflict, another country will have to jump in."

	Prime Minister Nehru pointed out that these developments would have far-reaching effects, affecting not only the Middle East region but also the entire world. He said, "At this critical juncture, the world is being tested. No one will know what will happen."

	The Chairman of the Indian National Congress, Dhebar, stated to the press on the 16th regarding the U.S. troop landing in Lebanon that "So far, the measures taken for foreign troops to intervene have failed to convince many of us that it is justified or beneficial, especially since UN observers in Lebanon have found no evidence of foreign intervention." He expressed opposition to "any kind" of interference in internal affairs.

### 尼赫鲁再谈中东局势  印度国大党主席反对美国干涉黎巴嫩

1958-07-18
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁再谈中东局势
    印度国大党主席反对美国干涉黎巴嫩
    新华社新德里16日电　印度总理尼赫鲁16日在印度北方邦阿拉哈巴德大学学生的集会上发表演说时说，“虽然我们不打算干预伊拉克和黎巴嫩的事务，但是我们非常关心在这些国家里的事态发展。”
    他说，“毫无疑问，这些是他们的内政问题。你们可以说它是内战。但是如果外国干涉这些事务，就有引起世界大战的巨大危险。”他说，“如果一个外国进入斗争的场所，另外一个国家也就不得不跳进去。”
    尼赫鲁总理指出，这些事态发展会有深远的影响，它不仅会影响中东地区而且会影响全世界。他说，“在这个紧急关头，世界正在受考验。没有人会知道将会发生什么事情。”
    印度国大党主席德巴在16日就美国派兵在黎巴嫩登陆的事件对报界发表谈话说，“到目前为止，为了外国军队进行干涉所采取的措施使我们当中许多人不能信服它是正当的或者是有益的，特别是因为联合国观察员在黎巴嫩没有找到外国干涉的证据。”他表示反对“任何种类”的干涉内政的行为。
