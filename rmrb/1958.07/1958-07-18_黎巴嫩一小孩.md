Title: A Lebanese Child
Author: Yuan Ying
Date: 1958-07-18
Page: 8

    A Lebanese Child
    By Yuan Ying
    July 17th reported: The Lebanese people extremely hate the arrival of the American interventionist troops, even children are full of hatred…
    On the streets of Beirut,
    Stands a child,
    Teeth clenched,
    Eyes fixed on the harbor.
    American ships are anchored at sea,
    American soldiers disembark from the ships,
    Leather boots step on the beach,
    One footprint at a time.
    The child clenches his fist,
    Sparks erupt in his heart:
    "We are Arabs,
    We must drive them back home!"
    What the American soldiers are trampling on,
    Is his mother's chest,
    What the American tanks are crushing,
    Is the happiness of the Arab people.
    The Arabs stand up,
    Hot blood pours into the Mediterranean Sea,
    Let that boiling blood,
    Scald all the invaders!
    The Lebanese child's vow,
    Is the heartfelt voice of all Arabs,
    Behind this child,
    Stands the people of the world! (Attached picture)

### 黎巴嫩一小孩
袁鹰
1958-07-18
第8版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩一小孩
    袁鹰
    7月17日报载：黎巴嫩人民极端仇视美国干涉军的到来，即使是小孩子也充满了敌忾……
    贝鲁特街道上，
    站着一个小孩，
    牙齿咬紧嘴唇，
    眼睛凝视港外。
    海上停着美国船，
    船上走下美国兵，
    皮靴踩上沙滩，
    一步一个脚印。
    小孩捏紧拳头，
    心里冒出火花：
    “我们是阿拉伯人，
    一定要赶他们回老家！”
    美国兵踩着的，    
    是他母亲的胸脯，
    美国坦克压着的，
    是阿拉伯人的幸福。
    阿拉伯人站起来，
    热血倾入地中海，
    让那沸腾的血，
    烫死一切侵略者！
    黎巴嫩小孩的誓言，
    是全阿拉伯人的心声，    
    在这小孩背后，
    站着全世界人民！（附图片）
