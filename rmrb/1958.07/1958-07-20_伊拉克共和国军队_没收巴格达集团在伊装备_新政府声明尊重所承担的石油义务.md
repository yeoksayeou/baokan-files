Title: Iraqi Republican Army Confiscates Baghdad Pact Equipment in Iraq; New Government Declares Respect for Petroleum Obligations

Author:

Date: 1958-07-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 19th, Cairo dispatch: It is reported that the Iraqi Republican Army has confiscated equipment, ammunition, and weapons belonging to the Baghdad Military Treaty Organization within Iraq. According to news from Baghdad, the largest British air force base in the Middle East, Habbaniyah, which was established in Iraq, is now under the guard of the Iraqi Republican Army.

Xinhua News Agency, July 19th, Cairo dispatch: Iraqi Republican Radio announced on the 18th that the Iraqi Republican government, for the sake of domestic and international economic interests, respects its obligations to relevant parties regarding petroleum. The Iraqi Republican government has taken all necessary measures to maintain oil wells, pumping stations, pipelines, and other equipment within Iraq.

The radio also stated that the Iraqi Republican government, in order to protect its national interests from damage, will continue to operate the aforementioned important public utilities, and hopes that all relevant parties will respond to this decision.

Xinhua News Agency, July 19th, London dispatch: A spokesman for the Iraq Petroleum Company, invested in by the British, American, and French governments, said in London on the morning of the 18th that the company's mining operations in Iraq are proceeding as usual.

According to news that reached the company's London headquarters on the night of the 17th, the oil transportation activities from the Kirkuk oil field in Iraq to the Mediterranean ports of Tripoli and Baniyas have never been interrupted.

### 伊拉克共和国军队  没收巴格达集团在伊装备  新政府声明尊重所承担的石油义务

1958-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克共和国军队
    没收巴格达集团在伊装备
    新政府声明尊重所承担的石油义务
    新华社19日讯　据塔斯社开罗19日讯：据悉，伊拉克共和国军队没收了伊拉克境内属于巴格达军事条约集团的装备、弹药和武器。据来自巴格达的消息，在伊拉克境内建立的中东最大的英国空军基地哈巴尼雅现在已经处在伊拉克共和国军队的警卫之下。
    新华社开罗19日电　伊拉克共和国电台18日宣布，伊拉克共和国政府为了国内和国际经济的利益，尊重它对有关方面所承担的关于石油的义务。伊拉克共和国政府已经采取了一切必要的措施来维持伊拉克境内的油井、抽油站、油管和其他设备。
    电台又说，伊拉克共和国政府为了保护它的国家利益不受损害，上述重要的公用事业将继续活动，希望一切有关方面响应这个决定。
    新华社19日讯　伦敦消息：由英、美、法三国投资的伊拉克石油公司的发言人18日上午在伦敦说，这家公司在伊拉克的开采工作照常进行。
    根据17日夜间传到这家公司伦敦总部的消息说，从伊拉克基尔库克油田输送到地中海岸的的黎波里和巴尼亚斯港两地的输油管的输油活动从未中断过。
