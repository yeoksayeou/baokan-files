Title: Ulaanbaatar Holds Grand Rally and Parade to Celebrate the 37th Anniversary of the Victory of the Mongolian People's Revolution
Author:
Date: 1958-07-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, July 11th: People from all walks of life in Ulaanbaatar gathered at Sukhbaatar Square this morning to warmly celebrate the 37th anniversary of the victory of the Mongolian People's Revolution.

The celebration rally began at 9:00 AM. After reviewing the Mongolian People's Army, Comrade Dorj, Minister of Military and Public Security of Mongolia, delivered a speech. He pointed out that great changes have taken place in the thirty-seven years since the victory of the Mongolian People's Revolution. Under the leadership of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and with the help of the Soviet Union and other brotherly countries, Mongolia has made great achievements in economic and cultural construction. The material and cultural life of the people has gradually improved. Now, the people of the whole country are actively working to realize the resolution of the 13th National Congress of the Party on the three-year plan for the development of the national economy.

He pointed out that the Mongolian people have always pursued a peaceful foreign policy, but must be vigilant against the existence of the imperialist aggressive groups who are enemies of peace. The Mongolian people always stand together with all peace-loving forces to fight for the defense of peace. He called on the people to exert greater efforts to complete the tasks of production and construction well. Then, the armed forces of the Mongolian people, marching in neat steps, passed the rostrum, accepting the review of the Mongolian Party and government leaders. Subsequently, a procession of people in festive costumes and holding flowers passed the rostrum one after another. The marchers and those gathered in the square chanted slogans celebrating the festival in unison.

### 庆祝蒙古人民革命胜利三十七周年  乌兰巴托举行盛大集会和阅兵

1958-07-12
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝蒙古人民革命胜利三十七周年
    乌兰巴托举行盛大集会和阅兵
    新华社乌兰巴托11日电　乌兰巴托市各界人民今天上午在苏赫巴托尔广场集会热烈庆祝蒙古人民革命胜利三十七周年。
    庆祝大会在上午九时开始。蒙古军事和公安部部长道尔吉中将在检阅了蒙古人民军队以后发表演说。他指出，蒙古人民革命胜利三十七年以来发生了很大的变化。在蒙古人民革命党的领导下，在苏联和其他兄弟国家的帮助下，蒙古的经济文化建设获得了很大成就。人民的物质文化生活逐步提高。现在全国人民正在为实现党的十三次代表大会关于发展国民经济的三年计划的决议而积极劳动着。
    他指出，蒙古人民一贯奉行和平外交政策，但是对于和平的敌人帝国主义侵略集团的存在必须提高警惕。蒙古人民始终和爱好和平的力量一起为保卫和平而斗争。他要求人民发挥更大的力量，很好地完成生产建设的任务。接着，蒙古人民的武装部队迈着整齐的步伐走过主席台，接受蒙古党和政府领导人的检阅。随后，穿着节日服装手持鲜花的群众游行队伍陆续走过主席台。游行的人们和集结在广场上的人们同声欢呼着庆祝节日的口号。
