Title: An Encouragement to the Hussein Group: U.S. Announces a Sum of Money

Author:

Date: 1958-07-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 21st - According to a broadcast by Amman Radio on the evening of the 20th, the United States has announced a loan of $7.5 million to the Hussein Group of Jordan.

According to the announcement of the U.S. Embassy in Amman on the 20th, the loan allocated by the United States is to support Jordan's budget. It also announced that the United States has allocated an additional $5 million in loans to the Hussein Group to pay for the cost of U.S. aircraft airlifting fuel to Amman.

It is reported that the Hussein Group is facing financial difficulties. These two loans are the first batch of "aid" given by the United States to the Hussein Group, which has been clamoring to attack the Republic of Iraq since its establishment.

### 为侯赛因集团打气  美国宣布给一笔钱

1958-07-22
第5版()
专栏：

    为侯赛因集团打气
    美国宣布给一笔钱
    新华社大马士革21日电  据安曼电台20日晚间广播，美国已经宣布给约旦侯赛因集团七百五十万美元贷款。
    据美国驻安曼大使馆20日宣布，美国拨给的这笔贷款是用来支持约旦的预算的。它还宣布，美国已经另外拨付五百万美元的贷款给侯赛因集团，作为支付美国飞机向安曼空运燃料的费用。
    据报道，侯赛因集团正面临着财政困难。这两笔贷款是美国在伊拉克共和国成立以后给予极力叫嚣要进攻伊拉克共和国的侯赛因集团的第一批“援助”。
