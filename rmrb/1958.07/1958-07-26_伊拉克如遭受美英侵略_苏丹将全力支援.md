Title: Sudan Will Fully Support Iraq if It is Subjected to US-British Aggression
Author:
Date: 1958-07-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 24th: According to a Middle East News Agency report from Khartoum on the 23rd, Sudanese Foreign Minister Mahjoub said on the 23rd that if Iraq faces any danger from the US forces currently occupying Lebanon or the British forces in Jordan, the Sudanese government will fully support the Republic of Iraq.
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 25th: According to a Middle East News Agency report from Khartoum, the Sudanese Foreign Ministry announced on the 24th a ban on any foreign military and civil aircraft used for military purposes from landing at Sudanese airports or flying over Sudanese airspace.
    It is reported that the Sudanese government took this measure after discussing the current international situation.

### 伊拉克如遭受美英侵略  苏丹将全力支援

1958-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克如遭受美英侵略
    苏丹将全力支援
    新华社开罗24日电　据中东通讯社24日喀土穆消息：苏丹外交部长马哈古卜在23日说，如果伊拉克受到来自现在占领黎巴嫩的美军或者在约旦的英军的任何危险的话，苏丹政府将全力支援伊拉克共和国。
    新华社开罗25日电　据中东通讯社喀土穆消息，苏丹外交部在24日宣布禁止用于军事目的的任何外国军用飞机和民用飞机在苏丹机场降陆或飞越苏丹领空。
    据报道，苏丹政府是在讨论了当前的国际局势后采取这个措施的。
