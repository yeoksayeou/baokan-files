Title: Canadian Prime Minister Willing to Attend Summit Conference; New Zealand Prime Minister Supports Khrushchev's Proposal
Author:
Date: 1958-07-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 24th, dispatch from Ottawa by TASS: Canadian Prime Minister Diefenbaker told reporters that he is prepared to participate in a high-level meeting of the United Nations Security Council if such a meeting is to be convened to discuss the Middle East situation.

Xinhua News Agency, July 24th, Wellington dispatch: New Zealand Prime Minister Nash stated in the New Zealand Parliament today that he supports convening a summit conference with the participation of Arab countries and India to discuss the Middle East situation.

Nash defended Britain's act of aggression against Jordan. However, he also said that he had told British Prime Minister Macmillan that efforts should be made immediately through the United Nations to eliminate the critical situation in the Middle East.

### 加总理愿参加首脑会议  新西兰总理支持赫鲁晓夫建议

1958-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    加总理愿参加首脑会议
    新西兰总理支持赫鲁晓夫建议
    新华社24日讯　塔斯社渥太华23日讯：加拿大总理迪芬贝克对记者说，他准备参加联合国安理会的高级会议，如果将要召开这种会议来讨论中东局势的话。
      新华社24日讯　惠灵顿消息：新西兰总理纳希今天在新西兰议会中表示，他支持召开能有阿拉伯国家和印度参加的国家首脑会议，来讨论中东局势问题。
    纳希为英国对约旦的侵略行为作了辩解。但是，他还说，他已经告诉英国首相麦克米伦，应该立即设法通过联合国来消除中东的危急局势。
