Title: Railway Tracks Laid to Xinjiang
Author:
Date: 1958-07-25
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, July 24th: The track-laying project for the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway reached Xinjiang territory on the 22nd. By the afternoon of the 22nd, the track laying had passed the Hongliu River Station, which is 1,144.5 kilometers from Lanzhou, and reached the Hongliu River Bridge, 4.5 kilometers west of the Hongliu River Station. The Hongliu River Bridge is the first key project after the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway enters Xinjiang, located 180 kilometers from Hami, a major town in eastern Xinjiang.

### 铁路铺轨到新疆

1958-07-25
第7版()
专栏：

    铁路铺轨到新疆
    据新华社乌鲁木齐24日电　兰新铁路铺轨工程22日铺到了新疆境内。到22日下午，铺轨已经越过了距兰州一千一百四十四点五公里的红柳河车站，到达红柳河车站以西四公里半的红柳河大桥。红柳河大桥是兰新铁路进入新疆后的第一个重点工程，距离新疆东部重镇哈密一百八十公里。
