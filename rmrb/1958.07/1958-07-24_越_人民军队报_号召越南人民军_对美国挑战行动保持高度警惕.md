Title: Vietnamese "People's Army Newspaper" Calls on the Vietnam People's Army to Maintain High Vigilance Against American Provocative Actions
Author:
Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 23rd: The Vietnamese "People's Army Newspaper" (July 3rd edition) recently published an editorial titled "We Must Be Seriously Vigilant Against the Provocative Actions of American Imperialism," calling on the Vietnam People's Army to maintain a high degree of vigilance in the current situation.

The editorial said that in the current tense situation created by American and British imperialism, the Vietnam People's Army cannot be complacent in the slightest and must be ready at all times to defend the cause of socialism, defend Southeast Asia, and defend world peace.

The newspaper said that the strength of the Arab people and the people of the world is stronger now than ever before, and the United States is not to be feared; the power of the people can stop war. However, at the same time, we must be prepared to deal with sudden events. The newspaper demanded that the United States get out of the Middle East and Near East. The newspaper said that safeguarding peace in the Middle East and Near East is the unshirkable responsibility of peace-loving people.

### 越“人民军队报”号召越南人民军  对美国挑战行动保持高度警惕

1958-07-24
第5版()
专栏：

    越“人民军队报”号召越南人民军
    对美国挑战行动保持高度警惕
    新华社河内23日电　最近出版的越南“人民军队报”（3日刊）以“必须严重警惕美帝国主义的挑战行动”为题发表社论，号召越南人民军在目前局势下保持高度的警惕。
    社论说，在当前美英帝国主义所造成的紧张局势下，越南人民军不能有丝毫的松懈，要随时准备保卫社会主义事业、保卫东南亚和世界和平。
    这家报纸说，现在阿拉伯人民和世界人民的力量比过去任何时候都要强大，美国并不可怕，人民的力量是能够制止战争的。但是，同时必须准备对付突然事件。报纸要求美国滚出中近东。报纸说，维护中近东和平是爱好和平人民的义不容辞的责任。
