Title: Fortress of Peace
Author: Yī Ā· Lāhāmǎní
Date: 1958-07-25
Page: 8

    Fortress of Peace
    Jordan　I. A. Rahmani
    You can't break into the fortress of peace,
    Dancers on the ruins! No matter how arrogant, vulgar fools you are,
    You will never extinguish the light of the future! You shall not spill blood in the streets,
    Artificial darkness shall not frighten us. We swear by the battle flag,
    This flag illuminates our broad road!
        Even if you have sharpened the butcher knife, dancers on the ruins!
    You must not slaughter children, sully their
      purity!
        You must not gouge out the children's
            eyes!
            "You can't break into the fortress of peace!"
    This voice echoes in the darkness. You even fantasize about drilling holes in the fortress's copper and iron walls,
    Extending subversive bloody hands—the devil's
        claws. The hearts of hundreds of millions of people are the iron walls of the fortress.
    Can you believe the lies, insisting this isn't
      steel? Those impoverished lower classes,
    Their roars and iron fists tear through the sky
      of Cairo; their footsteps on the streets of Syria,
    Are thunderbolts from a clear sky! The revolutionary people roar:
    —Colonialists are doomed to die!
    —Down with the running dogs! Down with the perfidious
      warmongers! This roar shakes the sun, moon, and stars,
    Awakening them from their dreams. Let those arrogant people know:
    The wheels of time are turning,
        Silence will be replaced by roars, howls,
    Darkness will be conquered by daylight, light. We have the future, we have hope, traitors,
    You only have shame and disgrace, which will forever cling to your heads, shouting:
    —Wretched fate!
    —Tragic ending! Listen! Arrogant people,
    We have hope and the future! We who steer fortune,
    Are stronger than any force, fiercer than raging
      fires! The revolutionary people,
    Their iron fists and roars tear through the sky
      of Cairo! Their footsteps on the streets of Syria,
    Are thunderbolts from a clear sky! The loud songs they sing in Baghdad,
    Are raging waves! Their roars in the dim nights of Amman—
    —Disaster belongs to the enemies of life!
    —Disaster belongs to the warmongers, the creators
      of aggressive blocs, the enemies of the people!
    —Disaster belongs to the enemies under
      our feet!
    —Dawn is flashing with light, the rising sun has
      already risen in the east!

### 和平的堡垒
伊·阿·拉哈玛尼
1958-07-25
第8版()
专栏：

    和平的堡垒
    约旦　伊·阿·拉哈玛尼你们休想闯进和平的堡垒，
    废墟上的舞蹈家们！不管你们多么狂妄，下流的蠢汉，你
    们永远扑灭不了未来的光辉！你们休想使鲜血倾流在大街小巷，
    人造的黑暗也休想吓倒我们。我们用战斗的旗帜宣誓，
    这旗帜照耀着我们的康庄大
        道！即使你们已把屠刀磨好，废墟上的
  舞蹈家们！
    也决不能让你们屠杀幼儿，沾
      污他们的纯洁！
        决不能让你们挖去幼儿的
            眼睛！“你们休想闯进和平的堡垒！”
    这声音在黑暗里回旋震荡。你们竟妄想在堡垒的铜墙铁壁上打孔钻眼，
    伸出颠复的血手——死亡的魔
        爪。亿万人的心就是堡垒的铁壁。
    难道能听信谎言，硬说这不是
      钢铁？那些贫困的下层的人们，
    他们的吼声和铁拳划破开罗的
      天空；他们在叙利亚大街上的脚步声，
    是晴天霹雳！革命的人民吼叫：
    ——殖民主义者必将死亡！
    ——打倒走狗！打倒背信弃义
      的战争贩子！这吼声震撼了日月星辰，
    把它们从睡梦中惊醒。让那些傲慢的人们知道：
    时代的车轮在转动，
        沉默将被怒吼、咆哮代替，
    黑夜将被白昼、光明战胜。我们有未来，我们有希望，叛徒们，
    你们只有羞愧和耻辱，它将永远缠在你们头上喊叫：
    ——倒霉的命运！
    ——悲惨的结局！听着！傲慢不逊的人们，
    我们有希望和未来！驾驭幸运的我们，
    比任何力量都强大，比熊熊的
      大火还猛烈！革命的人民，
    他们的铁拳和吼声划破开罗的
      天空！他们在叙利亚大街上的脚步声，
    是晴天霹雳！他们在巴格达唱起的宏亮歌声，
    是狂风巨浪！他们的在阿曼暗淡无光的夜里吼叫
    ——灾难属于生活的敌人！
    ——灾难属于战争贩子、侵略
      集团的制造者、人民的敌人！
    ——灾难属于我们脚下的敌
      人！
    ——黎明闪烁出光辉，旭日已
      经东升！
