Title: Black African Youth Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-07-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 8th. The Black African Youth Delegation, invited by the All-China Youth Federation to visit our country, arrived in Beijing separately on the 7th and 8th, totaling thirteen people.

Welcoming them at the airport were Wu Xueqian, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, Qian Dawei, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Youth Federation, and others. The delegation was also warmly welcomed by Beijing youth at the airport.

The delegation includes representatives from organizations such as the Senegalese Youth Council, the Congolese Youth Federation, and the Federation of Black African Students in France. The head of the delegation is Diouf Banny Fally.

### 黑非洲青年访华代表团到北京

1958-07-10
第4版()
专栏：

    黑非洲青年访华代表团到北京
    新华社8日讯  应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国访问的黑非洲青年访华代表团一行十三人，已经分别在7、8两日到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有全国青联副主席吴学谦，全国学联主席胡启立，全国青联国际联络部副部长钱大卫等。代表团在机场还受到了北京市青年的热烈欢迎。
    代表团包括塞内加尔青年理事会、刚果青年联合会和黑非洲留法学生联合会等组织的代表。代表团团长是狄乌弗·巴尼·法利。
