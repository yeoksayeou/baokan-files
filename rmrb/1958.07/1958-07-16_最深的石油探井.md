Title: The Deepest Oil Well

Author: 
Date: 1958-07-16
Page: 3

	The deepest oil well in our country belongs to the 3282 drilling team of the Yumen Oil Mine Drilling Company. Recently, they drilled a well in the Ya'erxia exploration area of the Jiuquan Basin, reaching a depth of 3,210.1 meters, which is 4.3 meters deeper than a deep exploration well drilled by Yumen drilling workers in 1956.

### 最深的石油探井

1958-07-16
第3版()
专栏：

    最深的石油探井
    我国最深的石油探井，要算玉门油矿钻井公司三二八二钻井队了，最近在酒泉盆地鸭儿峡探区钻成一口深达三千二百一十点一公尺，比1956年玉门钻井工人钻成的一口深探井还深四点三公尺。
