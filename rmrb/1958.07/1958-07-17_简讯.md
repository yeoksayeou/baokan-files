Title: Brief News

Author: 
Date: 1958-07-17
Page: 6

    Brief News
    △Romania and the German Democratic Republic signed three agreements in Bucharest on the 15th concerning culture, justice, and consular affairs.
    △The government of the Union of Burma and the government of the German Democratic Republic signed a long-term trade agreement in Rangoon on the 15th.
    △U.S. Democratic Party leader Stevenson arrived in Moscow on the 15th for a visit. Prior to this, they had stayed in Leningrad for three days. During their visits to various places, they will learn about the achievements of the Soviet Union in industry, agriculture, science, and culture.
    △The government of the Union of Burma and the government of the German Democratic Republic signed a long-term trade agreement in Rangoon on the 15th.

### 简讯

1958-07-17
第6版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    △罗马尼亚和德意志民主共和国15日在布加勒斯特签订了文化、司法和领事三个协定。
    △缅甸联邦政府和德意志民主共和国政府15日在仰光签订了一项长期贸易协定。
    △美国民主党领袖史蒂文森15日到达莫斯科访问。在这以前，他们曾经在列宁格勒逗留了三天。他们在各地访问期间将了解苏联工农业和科学文化方面的成就。
    △缅甸联邦政府和德意志民主共和国政府15日在仰光签订了一项长期贸易协定。
