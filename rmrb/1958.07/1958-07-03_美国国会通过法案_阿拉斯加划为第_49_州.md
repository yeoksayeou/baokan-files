Title: US Congress Passes Bill, Alaska Designated as the 49th State
Author:
Date: 1958-07-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, July 1st - The United States Senate passed a bill on June 30th by a vote of sixty-four to twenty, which had previously been passed by the House of Representatives, regarding the designation of Alaska as the 49th state of the United States. The United States currently has forty-eight states.

This bill will take effect after being signed by US President Eisenhower.

### 美国国会通过法案  阿拉斯加划为第49州

1958-07-03
第6版()
专栏：

    美国国会通过法案
    阿拉斯加划为第49州
    新华社1日讯　华盛顿消息：美国参议院6月30日以六十四票对二十票通过了前些时候众议院已经通过的关于把阿拉斯加划为美国第四十九州的法案。美国目前有四十八个州。
    这项法案将在美国总统艾森豪威尔签署后生效。
