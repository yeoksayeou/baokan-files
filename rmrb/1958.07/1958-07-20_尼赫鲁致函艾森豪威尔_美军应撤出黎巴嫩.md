Title: Nehru Writes to Eisenhower: American Troops Should Withdraw from Lebanon
Author:
Date: 1958-07-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 19th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru said in a letter to U.S. President Eisenhower on the 18th that India believes American troops should withdraw from Lebanon.

The letter was a reply to a letter from Eisenhower to him, and stated that India believes the United Nations observer group can ensure that there is no external infiltration threatening the Lebanese government.

An Indian Foreign Ministry spokesman said Nehru's letter was delivered to U.S. Ambassador to India Bunker on the 18th.

### 尼赫鲁致函艾森豪威尔  美军应撤出黎巴嫩

1958-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁致函艾森豪威尔
    美军应撤出黎巴嫩
    新华社新德里19日电　印度总理尼赫鲁18日在给美国总统艾森豪威尔的信中说，印度认为美国军队应该撤出黎巴嫩。
    这封信是回答艾森豪威尔给他的一封信的，信中还说，印度认为联合国观察小组可以保证不致有外来的渗入威胁黎巴嫩政府。
    印度外交部发言人说，尼赫鲁这封信已经在18日交给美国驻印度大使班克。
