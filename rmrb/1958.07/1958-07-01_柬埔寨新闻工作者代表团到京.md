Title: Cambodian Journalists Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-07-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. A seven-member Cambodian journalists delegation, led by the head of the delegation, Cambodian Director of Information Chen Wen Chang, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Wang Yi, Secretary-General of the All-China Journalists Association and Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily, Shao Zonghan, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and representatives from various news organizations in Beijing.

Before arriving in Beijing, the delegation had visited Guangzhou and Wuhan.

### 柬埔寨新闻工作者代表团到京

1958-07-01
第2版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨新闻工作者代表团到京
    新华社30日电　柬埔寨新闻工作者代表团一行七人、在团长、柬埔寨新闻局长陈文昌率领下，在今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中华全国新闻工作者协会秘书长，人民日报副总编辑王揖、外交部新闻司副司长邵宗汉和北京各新闻单位代表。
    代表团抵京前，曾先后访问了广州和武汉。
