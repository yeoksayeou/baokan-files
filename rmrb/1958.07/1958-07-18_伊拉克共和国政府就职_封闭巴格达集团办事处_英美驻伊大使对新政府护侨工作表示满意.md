Title: Iraqi Republic Government Inaugurated; Baghdad Pact Offices Closed; British and American Ambassadors to Iraq Express Satisfaction with New Government's Protection of Nationals

Author: 
Date: 1958-07-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 17th: According to reports received here, the first cabinet of the Iraqi Republic, headed by General Kassem, was officially inaugurated on the 16th.

Iraqi Republic Radio broadcast on the 16th that the situation in Iraq is now completely calm. Railways are scheduled to reopen on the 16th.

Reports received here say that all government offices in Baghdad have resumed work, and all shops have reopened for business.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 17th: According to today's "Al-Ahram" newspaper, the government of the Iraqi Republic has closed all offices of the Baghdad Pact Organization in Iraq. The newspaper said that all documents related to the Baghdad Pact Organization have been confiscated for the purpose of studying the conspiracies planned by the Baghdad Pact Organization against Arab countries.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 17th: According to Iraqi Republic Radio broadcast on the 16th, Iraqi Republic Prime Minister Kassem received the British and American ambassadors to Iraq on the 16th at their request.

According to the radio report, "Sir Wright, the British Ambassador, expressed satisfaction with the arrangements made for British nationals." The radio also said, "The American Ambassador expressed admiration for the efforts made by the Iraqi Republic to maintain peace and order, as well as its concern for American citizens."

It is reported that Prime Minister Kassem also received the Japanese Minister on the same day. The Japanese Minister expressed admiration for the Iraqi people's response to the army's actions and their spirit of maintaining public order.

### 伊拉克共和国政府就职  封闭巴格达集团办事处  英美驻伊大使对新政府护侨工作表示满意

1958-07-18
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克共和国政府就职
    封闭巴格达集团办事处
    英美驻伊大使对新政府护侨工作表示满意
    新华社大马士革17日电　据这里得到的消息说，伊拉克共和国以卡塞姆将军为首的第一任内阁已经在16日正式就职。
    伊拉克共和国电台16日广播，伊拉克局势现在完全平静。铁路预定在16日恢复。
    这里得到的消息说，巴格达各政府机关已经恢复工作，所有商店重新开始营业。
    新华社开罗17日电　据“金字塔报”今天报道，伊拉克共和国政府封闭了所有在伊拉克的巴格达条约组织办事处。这家报纸说，有关巴格达条约组织的一切文件都被没收，以便对巴格达条约组织所策划的针对阿拉伯国家的阴谋进行研究。
    新华社开罗17日电　据伊拉克共和国电台在16日广播，伊拉克共和国总理卡塞姆在英国和美国驻伊拉克大使的请求下，在16日接见了他们。
    据这家电台报道，“英国大使赖特爵士对为英国侨民所作的安排表示满意。”电台还说，“美国大使对伊拉克共和国为了维护和平和秩序所作的努力，以及它对美国公民的关心表示钦佩。”
    据报道，卡塞姆总理在同一天还接见了日本公使。日本公使对伊拉克人民响应陆军的行动和维护公共秩序的精神表示钦佩。
