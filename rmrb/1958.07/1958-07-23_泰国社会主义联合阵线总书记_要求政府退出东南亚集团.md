Title: General Secretary of the Thai Socialist United Front Demands Government Withdraw from Southeast Asia Group
Author:
Date: 1958-07-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, July 22nd: Nai Phin Wangnathi, a member of parliament from the Thai Socialist United Front, told reporters on the 18th that the entry of US and British troops into Lebanon and Jordan respectively is an interference in the internal affairs of Lebanon and Jordan, and this behavior violates the United Nations Charter. Nai Phin emphasized that if the US and Britain are allowed to interfere in the internal affairs of Lebanon and Jordan, it may cause a world war. He said that the affairs of Arab countries should be handled by the Arab countries themselves, and the whole world opposes the war-mongering behavior of the United States and Britain.

On the same day, Nai Khlai Narapati, a member of parliament and General Secretary of the Socialist United Front, submitted an urgent interpellation to Thai Prime Minister Nai Thanom, demanding that the Thai government immediately withdraw from the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization to avoid being drawn into another world war.

The editorial of "Eternal Daily" on the 19th said, "We do not agree with the entry of US and British troops into Lebanon and Jordan, and we strongly oppose actions that violate international law, the United Nations Charter, and humanity."

### 泰国社会主义联合阵线总书记  要求政府退出东南亚集团

1958-07-23
第3版()
专栏：

    泰国社会主义联合阵线总书记
    要求政府退出东南亚集团
    新华社22日讯  曼谷消息：泰国社会主义联合阵线的国会议员乃扁·汪纳诗在18日向报界发表谈话说，美英两国军队分别侵驻黎巴嫩和约旦，是对黎巴嫩和约旦内政的干涉，这种行为是违反联合国宪章的。乃扁强调说，如果听任美英两国干涉黎巴嫩和约旦内政，就有可能引起世界战争。他说，阿拉伯国家的事务应当由阿拉伯国家自己处理，全世界都反对美英两国的这种战争冒险行为。
    同一天，国会议员、社会主义联合阵线总书记乃克莱·纳拉帕蒂向泰国总理乃他侬提出一项紧急质询案，要求泰国政府立即退出东南亚条约组织，以免被卷入另一次世界战争的漩涡中。
    “永恒报”19日的社论说，我们不同意美英两国军队进入黎巴嫩和约旦，我们极力反对违反国际法、违反联合国宪章和人道的行为。
