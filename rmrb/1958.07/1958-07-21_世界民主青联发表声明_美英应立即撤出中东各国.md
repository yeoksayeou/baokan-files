Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Issues Statement: U.S. and Britain Should Immediately Withdraw from Middle Eastern Countries
Author:
Date: 1958-07-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 19th, Budapest: The World Federation of Democratic Youth issued a statement expressing deep concern about the serious situation caused by the U.S. and British armed intervention in Lebanon and Jordan.

The statement said that young people all over the world are indignant at this act of aggression and demand the immediate withdrawal of foreign troops from Lebanon and Jordan.

The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth fully agrees with the just demands of the youth. After studying the situation, the Secretariat called on Eisenhower and the U.S. government to immediately recall U.S. troops from Lebanon, called on the British government to immediately withdraw its troops from Jordan, and called on the United Nations Security Council and its member states to condemn the U.S. and British armed intervention in Lebanon and Jordan and to take measures to ensure the immediate withdrawal of foreign troops from these countries.

### 世界民主青联发表声明  美英应立即撤出中东各国

1958-07-21
第4版()
专栏：

    世界民主青联发表声明
    美英应立即撤出中东各国
    新华社19日讯  据塔斯社布达佩斯19日讯：世界民主青年联盟发表声明，表示对由于美英武装干涉黎巴嫩和约旦而造成的严重局势深感不安。
    声明说，全世界的青年对这种侵略行为表示愤慨，并且要求外国军队立即撤出黎巴嫩和约旦。
    世界民主青年联盟书记处完全赞同青年们的这种正义要求。书记处在研究了局势后，呼吁艾森豪威尔和美国政府立即从黎巴嫩召回美国军队，呼吁英国政府立即从约旦撤回它的军队，呼吁联合国安全理事会和它的理事国谴责美英对黎巴嫩和约旦的武装干涉，并且采取措施保证外国军队立即从这些国家撤出去。
