Title: After the US Government Expressed Apology, the Soviet Union Returned Nine American Pilots
Author:
Date: 1958-07-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th: TASS reports: On July 7th, in Astara, on the Soviet-Iranian border, the Soviet Union handed over nine American military aircraft personnel, who had violated the Soviet border, to representatives of the U.S. military.

These nine flight personnel were forced to land and detained by the Soviet Union on June 27th when they flew U.S. military aircraft into Soviet territory in the Yerevan region. On June 30th, the Soviet government issued a note to the U.S. government lodging a firm protest regarding this incident. The U.S. government later replied to the Soviet government, expressing regret over the violation of the Soviet border by the U.S. military aircraft. On July 4th, the Soviet government again sent a note to the U.S. government, hoping that it would take effective and urgent measures to ensure that similar incidents do not occur again.

### 在美国政府表示歉意后  苏联交还九名美飞行员

1958-07-10
第5版()
专栏：

    在美国政府表示歉意后
    苏联交还九名美飞行员
    新华社8日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联7日在苏联伊朗边境的阿斯塔尔城，把侵犯苏联国境的美国军用飞机的九名飞行人员交还给美国军方代表。
    这九名飞行人员是在6月27日驾驶美国军用飞机在埃里温地区侵入苏联国境时被迫降落并被苏联拘留的。6月30日，苏联政府曾就这一事件照会美国政府，表示坚决抗议。美国政府后来复照苏联政府，对美国军用飞机侵犯苏联边境一事表示遗憾。7月4日，苏联政府又照会美国政府，希望它采取有效的紧急措施，保证不再发生类似事件。
