Title: Chairman of the Iraqi Supreme Sovereignty Council, Rifai, Returns Home to Take Office
Author:
Date: 1958-07-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 16th: According to a broadcast from the Iraqi Republic Radio, Najib al-Rifai, Chairman of the Iraqi Supreme Sovereignty Council, returned to Baghdad this morning from Jeddah, Saudi Arabia. He was originally the Iraqi Ambassador to Saudi Arabia.

　　Iraqi Republic Prime Minister Qasim and Deputy Prime Minister Arif welcomed him at the airport.

　　The radio station said that Najib al-Rifai took office today.

### 伊最高权力委员会主席  里法伊回国就职

1958-07-17
第3版()
专栏：

    伊最高权力委员会主席
    里法伊回国就职
    新华社大马士革16日电　据伊拉克共和国电台广播，伊拉克最高权力委员会主席纳吉布·里法伊今天早晨从沙特阿拉伯的吉达回到巴格达。他原是伊拉克驻沙特阿拉伯的大使。
    伊拉克共和国总理卡塞姆和副总理阿里夫到机场欢迎他。
    电台说，纳吉布·里法伊在今天就任新职。
