Title: Night Market
Author:
Date: 1958-07-28
Page: 6

    Night Market
    Starting from the evening of July 2nd, the commercial departments of the southern urban area of Taiyuan City have opened night markets in three locations: Liuxiang, Wuyi Road, and Zhengtai Street. The business hours are from 9:00 PM to 1:00 AM. The night market has over sixty businesses covering eight industries including non-staple food, catering, general merchandise, cotton cloth, cultural supplies, bathhouses, barbershops, and photography. The goods sold include sweets, cold dishes, meats, steamed buns, dumplings, cigarettes, alcohol, fruits, small commodities, and fabrics, greatly facilitating night workers and passing travelers.

### 夜间市场

1958-07-28
第6版()
专栏：

    夜间市场
    太原市南城区商业部门从7月2日晚起，在柳巷、五一路、正太街三个地方，开办了夜间市场，营业时间是晚上九时到一时。夜间市场有副食品、饮食、百货、棉布、文化用品、澡塘、理发、照相等八个行业共六十余户，经营的商品有甜食、冷食、肉食，有饱子、饺子、烟酒、水果，有小百货和布匹等，大大便利夜间工作人员和来往旅客。
