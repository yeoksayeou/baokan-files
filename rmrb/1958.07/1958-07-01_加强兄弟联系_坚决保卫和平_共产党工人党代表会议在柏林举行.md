Title: Strengthen Fraternal Ties, Resolutely Defend Peace: Communist and Workers' Parties Meeting Held in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1958-07-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, June 30th: "Neues Deutschland" published a communique today regarding the meeting of Communist and Workers' Parties held in Berlin.

The communique stated that from June 27th to 29th, 1958, a meeting of Communist and Workers' Parties was held in Berlin at the invitation of the Central Committee of the German Communist Party. Representatives of the following Communist and Workers' Parties participated in the meeting: Communist Party of Belgium, Communist Party of Denmark, Communist Party of Germany, Socialist Unity Party of Germany, French Communist Party, Communist Party of Great Britain, Italian Comunist Party, Communist Party of Luxembourg, Communist Party of the Netherlands, Communist Party of Norway, Communist Part of Austria, Polish United Workers' Party, Communist Party of Sweden, Swiss Labour Party, and Communist Party of Czechoslovakia.

The meeting was conducted in a spirit of proletarian internationalism, with the Communist and Workers' Parties of all countries strengthening fraternal ties and jointly striving for peace, against the aggressive policies of American and German imperialists, and against the atomic armament of the Federal Republic of Germany.

It is reported that a mass rally will be held this afternoon at the German Sports Hall on Stalin Avenue to celebrate the convening of this meeting. Representatives of the fraternal parties participating in the meeting will meet with the citizens of Berlin.

### 加强兄弟联系　坚决保卫和平  共产党工人党代表会议在柏林举行

1958-07-01
第5版()
专栏：

    加强兄弟联系　坚决保卫和平
    共产党工人党代表会议在柏林举行
    新华社柏林30日电　“新德意志报”今天刊载了一则关于在柏林举行的共产党和工人党代表会议的公报。
    公报说，1958年6月27日到29日应德国共产党中央委员会的邀请在柏林举行了共产党和工人党代表会议，参加会议的有下列共产党和工人党的代表：比利时共产党、丹麦共产党、德国共产党、德国统一社会党、法国共产党、英国共产党、意大利共产党、卢森堡共产党、荷兰共产党、挪威共产党、奥地利共产党、波兰统一工人党、瑞典共产党、瑞士劳动党和捷克斯洛伐克共产党。
    会议是在无产阶级国际主义精神与各国共产党和工人党加强兄弟联系、共同为争取和平、为反对美国和德国帝国主义者的侵略政策、为反对德意志联邦共和国的原子武装而斗争的气氛中进行的。
    据悉今天下午将在斯大林大街的德国体育馆举行群众大会庆祝这次会议的召开，参加会议的各兄弟党代表们将同柏林市民见面。
