Title: Communist Party of Australia General Secretary Issues Statement, Calls for Action to Extinguish the Flames of War
Author:
Date: 1958-07-17
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The General Secretary of the Communist Party of Australia, Sharkey, issued a statement this morning condemning the U.S. deployment of troops to land in Lebanon. Sharkey pointed out that the U.S. dispatch of Marines to land in Lebanon shows that imperialism would rather plunge the world into an atomic war than recognize the nationalistic aspirations of the Arab people.
    Sharkey called on all communists and other peace-loving people to take immediate action to contain the warmongers. He also expressed the hope that the Australian people would send delegations or express to the government through telegrams and letters that the people cannot tolerate Australia's participation in this imperialist conspiracy to undermine peace.

### 澳共总书记发表声明  号召行动起来扑灭战火

1958-07-17
第2版()
专栏：

    澳共总书记发表声明
    号召行动起来扑灭战火
    据新华社16日讯　澳大利亚共产党总书记夏基今天早上发表声明，谴责美国派兵在黎巴嫩登陆。夏基指出，美国派遣海军陆战队在黎巴嫩登陆一事表明，帝国主义宁愿准备把世界投入原子战争中，也不愿意承认阿拉伯人民的民族主义愿望。
    夏基号召所有共产党员和其他爱好和平的人都立即采取行动来遏制战争贩子。他还表示希望澳大利亚人民派遣代表团或用电报、信件向政府表明，人民不能容忍澳大利亚参与这个破坏和平的帝国主义阴谋。
