Title: Central Leading Comrades Receive Representatives of Two Conferences and the Xinjiang Ethnic Groups Civil Affairs Cadre Visiting Group

Author:
Date: 1958-07-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: Chairman Mao Zedong and members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Deng Xiaoping and Li Xiannian, received this afternoon all the representatives attending the National Public Security Work Conference, the on-site conference on investment contracting for capital construction convened by the Ministry of Finance and the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry, and the visiting group of civil affairs cadres from various ethnic groups in the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region.

### 中央领导同志接见两个会议的代表  同时接见新疆各族民政干部参观团

1958-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中央领导同志接见两个会议的代表
    同时接见新疆各族民政干部参观团
    新华社9日讯  毛泽东主席和中共中央政治局委员邓小平、李先念、今天下午接见了参加全国公安工作会议、财政部和冶金工业部召开的基本建设投资包干现场会议的代表以及新疆维吾尔自治区各族民政干部参观团的全体人员。
