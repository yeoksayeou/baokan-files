Title: Compatriots from All Circles in Macau Hold Meeting to Support the Just Struggle of the Arab People
Author:
Date: 1958-07-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, July 24th: Compatriots from all circles in Macau held a meeting on the 22nd to resolutely oppose the armed aggression against the Middle East by American and British imperialism.

    He Xian, Chairman of the Macau Chinese General Chamber of Commerce, at the meeting, condemned the American and British armed intervention in the Middle East as threatening world peace. He said that all compatriots in Macau resolutely support the statement of our government and support the just struggle of the Arab people. Ma Wanhui, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese General Chamber of Commerce, said that the internal affairs of Lebanon should be handled by the Lebanese people themselves, and the American and British invading troops should get out of Lebanon and Jordan. Zhang Qinghui, Chairman of the Macau Democratic Women's Federation, said in her speech that Macau women, together with the people of the world, are determined to be the backing of the Middle East people, oppose the aggression of American and British imperialism, and defend world peace. Chen Guojie, a well-known figure in the Macau film industry, said: American imperialism has always been hostile to our country and has occupied our territory of Taiwan. He demanded that American and British imperialism get out of the Middle East, and American imperialism get out of our Taiwan!

    More than 300 representatives from schools, youth groups, chambers of commerce, and all walks of life participated in the meeting. Many people pointed out in their speeches that American and British imperialism will inevitably suffer a tragic defeat under the opposition of peace-loving people all over the world.

### 澳门各界同胞举行大会  声援阿拉伯人民正义斗争

1958-07-26
第2版()
专栏：

    澳门各界同胞举行大会
    声援阿拉伯人民正义斗争
    新华社香港24日电　澳门各界同胞22日举行大会，坚决反对美英帝国主义对中东的武装侵略。
    澳门中华总商会理事长何贤在大会上斥责美英武装干涉中东威胁世界和平。他说，全澳门同胞都坚决拥护我国政府的声明，支持阿拉伯人民的正义斗争。中华总商会副理事长马万祺说，黎巴嫩的内政应由黎巴嫩人民自己处理，美、英侵略军队应该滚出黎巴嫩和约旦。澳门民主妇女联合会主席张晴晖在讲话中表示，澳门妇女和全世界人民一道，决为中东人民后盾，反对美英帝国主义侵略，保卫世界和平。澳门电影业的知名人士陈国杰说：美帝国主义一向敌视我国，并侵占我国领土台湾。他要求美英帝国主义从中东滚出去，美帝国主义从我国台湾滚出去！
    参加大会的有学校、青年团体、商会和各界代表三百多人。许多人在讲话中指出，美英帝国主义在全世界爱好和平人民的反对下，一定会遭到悲惨的失败。
