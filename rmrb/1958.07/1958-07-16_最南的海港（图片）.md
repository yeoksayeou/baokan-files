Title: The Southernmost Seaport (Photo)
Author: Chen Fubei
Date: 1958-07-16
Page: 3

    The Southernmost Seaport
    The southernmost seaport in our country, Basuo Port on Hainan Island, is being restored and expanded. This is the automated ore loading equipment that has been built, set up to load iron sand produced on Hainan Island onto ships.
  　Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Fubei photo

### 最南的海港（图片）
陈福北
1958-07-16

专栏：

    最南的海港
    我国最南的海港——海南岛八所港正在恢复和扩建。这是已经建成的自动化装矿设备，它是为海南岛出产的铁砂装运上船而设置的。
  　新华社记者　陈福北摄
