Title: Tito and Nasser Issue Communiqué of Talks
Author:
Date: 1958-07-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, July 10th: According to a Yugoslav News Agency report from Brioni on July 10th: Yugoslav President Tito and Arab United Republic President Nasser, who was invited to visit, held talks on Brioni Island from July 6th to 9th and issued a joint communiqué.

The communiqué stated that similar views were expressed on all the issues discussed during the talks. The joint communiqué revealed that the two sides discussed issues such as halting the arms race, stopping the production and testing of atomic weapons, and holding summit meetings when discussing the international situation. The communiqué believes that "negative trends have recently emerged in many regions of the world, such as in Indonesia and Lebanon."

The communiqué also touched on the relations and cooperation between the Arab United Republic and Yugoslavia. The communiqué announced that Tito had accepted the invitation to visit the Arab United Republic.

On the afternoon of the 10th, President Nasser left Brioni for a visit to Slovenia.

### 铁托和纳赛尔发表会谈公报

1958-07-12
第6版()
专栏：

    铁托和纳赛尔发表会谈公报
    新华社贝尔格莱德10日电　据南斯拉夫通讯社布里俄尼10日消息：南斯拉夫总统铁托和应邀前来访问的阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔，7月6日到9日在布里俄尼岛举行会谈后，发表了联合公报。
    公报说，在会谈中在所有讨论到的问题上都表现出类似的观点。联合公报透露，双方在讨论国际局势时曾谈到有关停止军备竞赛、停止原子武器生产和试验、举行最高级会议等问题。公报认为，“最近在世界许多地区都出现了消极的倾向，例如在印度尼西亚和黎巴嫩。”
    公报还谈到了阿拉伯联合共和国和南斯拉夫两国的关系和合作的问题。公报宣布，铁托已经接受访问阿拉伯联合共和国的邀请。
    10日下午，纳赛尔总统离开布里俄尼前往斯洛文尼亚访问。
