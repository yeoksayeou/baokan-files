Title: Manila Vice Mayor Roces Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-07-19
Page: 5
　
Xinhua News Agency, July 18th. Jesus Marcos Roces, Vice Mayor of Manila, Philippines, and Chairman of the Manila Municipal Council, along with six others, arrived in Beijing by plane from Guangzhou this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit our country.

Representatives from the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and the All-China Journalists Association welcomed them at the airport.

### 马尼拉市副市长罗赛斯到京

1958-07-19
第5版()
专栏：

    马尼拉市副市长罗赛斯到京
    新华社18日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的菲律宾马尼拉市副市长、马尼拉市政委员会主席赫苏斯·马科斯·罗赛斯等七人，今天下午乘飞机从广州到达北京。
    中国人民外交学会和中华全国新闻工作者协会的代表到机场欢迎。
