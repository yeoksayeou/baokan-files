Title: International Organization of Journalists Issues Call to Action Against US-British Aggression in Lebanon and Jordan
Author:
Date: 1958-07-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 21st. The International Organization of Journalists today published a letter to journalists worldwide, calling on them to oppose the US and British imperialist aggression in the Middle East.

The letter to journalists states: "We cannot stand idly by while the shadow of a new world war looms over the world." This document condemns the United States and Britain's acts of aggression against Lebanon and Jordan, considering them a blatant violation of the universal principles of international law and the United Nations Charter. It demands that journalists from all countries make every effort to eliminate imperialist aggression in the Near and Middle East.

### 国际记协发出号召  反对美英侵略黎约

1958-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

    国际记协发出号召
    反对美英侵略黎约
    据新华社布拉格21日电  国际新闻工作者协会今天发表告全世界新闻工作者书，号召全世界新闻工作者反对美英帝国主义侵略中东。
    告新闻工作者书说：“当新的世界战争威胁的阴影笼罩着全世界的时候，我们不能袖手旁观。”这一文件谴责美国和英国对黎巴嫩和约旦的侵略行为，认为这是公然违反国际法的普遍准则和联合国宪章的行为。它要求各国新闻工作者竭尽一切努力，以便消除帝国主义在中近东的侵略。
