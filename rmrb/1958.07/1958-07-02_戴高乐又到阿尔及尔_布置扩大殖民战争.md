Title: De Gaulle Arrives in Algiers Again, Arranging to Expand the Colonial War
Author:
Date: 1958-07-02
Page: 6

    De Gaulle Arrives in Algiers Again
    Arranging to Expand the Colonial War
    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 1st: De Gaulle arrived in Algiers, the capital of Algeria, by plane today. He will stay in Algeria for three days. "L'Humanité" pointed out that De Gaulle's "real goal of this trip lies in expanding the war based on the request of Salan's staff to send an additional 100,000 reinforcements."

### 戴高乐又到阿尔及尔  布置扩大殖民战争

1958-07-02
第6版()
专栏：

    戴高乐又到阿尔及尔
    布置扩大殖民战争
    据新华社巴黎1日电　戴高乐今天乘飞机到达阿尔及利亚首府阿尔及尔，他将在阿尔及利亚逗留三天。“人道报”指出，戴高乐“这次旅行的真实目标在于：根据萨兰参谋部提出的增派十万援军的要求以扩大战争。”
