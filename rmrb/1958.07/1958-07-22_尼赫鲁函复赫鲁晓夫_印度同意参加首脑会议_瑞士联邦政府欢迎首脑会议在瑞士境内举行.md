Title: Nehru Replies to Khrushchev: India Agrees to Attend Summit Conference; Swiss Federal Government Welcomes Summit Conference to be Held in Switzerland
Author:
Date: 1958-07-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 20th. Indian Prime Minister Nehru has indicated his agreement to attend the summit conference of major powers proposed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev.

Nehru released his reply to Khrushchev late on July 20th in New Delhi.

In his letter, he reiterated his opposition to the armed intervention by one foreign country in another. He said that this is not only in accordance with the justice and freedom of the countries concerned, but can also avoid international complexities that may cause great disasters. Therefore, we have requested the governments of the United States and the United Kingdom to withdraw their troops from Lebanon and Jordan.

In his letter, Nehru expressed his great gratitude for Khrushchev's invitation. He said, "If an agreement is reached on holding such a conference, it will not be difficult to determine a suitable meeting place and an early date." Nehru stated that India would be happy to contribute to this conference.

Nehru received Khrushchev's letter on the afternoon of July 20th. It is reported that after the news of Khrushchev's proposal to convene a summit conference of major powers to resolve the military conflict that began in the Middle East arrived in New Delhi, the Foreign Affairs Subcommittee of the Indian Cabinet held an urgent meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, July 20th. Geneva News: The Swiss Federal Government issued a statement today regarding the Soviet proposal to convene a top-level meeting, saying that as long as the countries concerned agree to hold such a meeting, the Swiss Federal Government reiterates that it welcomes the meeting to be held in Switzerland.

### 尼赫鲁函复赫鲁晓夫  印度同意参加首脑会议  瑞士联邦政府欢迎首脑会议在瑞士境内举行

1958-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　尼赫鲁函复赫鲁晓夫
    印度同意参加首脑会议
    瑞士联邦政府欢迎首脑会议在瑞士境内举行
    新华社新德里20日电  印度总理尼赫鲁表示同意参加苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫建议召开的大国首脑会议。
    尼赫鲁20日深夜在新德里发表了他给赫鲁晓夫的回信。
    他在信中重申他反对一个外国武装干涉另一个国家。他说，这不仅是符合有关国家的正义和自由的，而且能避免可能引起大祸的国际复杂局面。因此我们已经要求美国和英国两国政府从黎巴嫩和约旦撤走它们的军队。
    尼赫鲁在信中表示非常感激赫鲁晓夫的邀请。他说：“如果对举行这样一次会议取得协议，那就不难确定一个合适的会议地点和一个及早的日期。”尼赫鲁表示，印度将乐于对这个会议尽一分力量。
    尼赫鲁是在20日下午收到赫鲁晓夫的信件的。有消息说，在关于赫鲁晓夫建议召开大国首脑会议来解决中近东开始的军事冲突的消息到达新德里之后，印度内阁外交小组委员会曾经举行了急紧会议。
    新华社20日讯  日内瓦消息：瑞士联邦政府今天就苏联关于召开最高级会议的建议发表声明说，只要有关国家同意举行这样的会议，瑞士联邦政府重申它欢迎会议在瑞士举行。
