Title: Premier Zhou Receives Ambassadors from India and Ceylon

Author:

Date: 1958-07-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 28th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council received the newly appointed Indian Ambassador to China, G. Parthasarathi, this afternoon.

Xinhua News Agency, July 28th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council received the newly appointed Ceylonese Ambassador to China, William Gopallawa, this afternoon.

### 周总理接见印锡两国大使

1958-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见印锡两国大使
    新华社28日讯　国务院总理周恩来今天下午接见了印度新任驻华大使高·帕塔萨拉蒂。
    新华社28日讯　国务院总理周恩来今天下午接见了锡兰新任驻华大使威廉·高伯拉瓦。
