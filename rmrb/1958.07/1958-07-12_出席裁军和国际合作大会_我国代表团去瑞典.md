Title: Delegation to Attend Disarmament and International Cooperation Conference Departs for Sweden
Author:
Date: 1958-07-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 11th: The Chinese delegation attending the Disarmament and International Cooperation Conference to be held in Stockholm from July 16th to 22nd departed from Beijing by plane today for Sweden via Moscow.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Ding Xilin, Wang Ziye, Zhang Yan, Shen Zijiu and other leaders from various democratic parties and people's organizations.

The delegation list is as follows:

Head of Delegation: Guo Moruo
Deputy Heads of Delegation: Liao Chengzhi, Bao Erhan

Members:
Qu Tangliang, Bai Yang, Zhu Ziqi, Liu Linrui, Li Hou, Li Shoubao, Li Depei, He Xiquan, Wu Quanheng, Wu Zuoren, Wu Maosun, Jin Zhonghua, Chen Qiyuan, Zhou Xinmin, Hu Yuzhi, Zhao Puchu, Zheng Senyu, Huang Jiasi, Tang Mingzhao, Ma Chungu, Zhang Wenyu, Zhang Zhixiang, Cui Qi, Xu Guangping, Qi Muguang, Yang Shuo, Cai Zuquan, Ji Chao Ding.

### 出席裁军和国际合作大会  我国代表团去瑞典

1958-07-12
第4版()
专栏：

    出席裁军和国际合作大会
    我国代表团去瑞典
    新华社11日讯　出席7月16日到22日在斯德哥尔摩举行的裁军和国际合作大会的中国代表团，今天由北京乘飞机经莫斯科前往瑞典。
    到机场欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南以及各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人丁西林、王子宜、张彦、沈兹九等。
    代表团名单如下：
    团长：郭沫若
    副团长：廖承志、包尔汉
    团员：
    区棠亮、白杨、朱子奇、刘麟瑞、李后、李寿葆、李德培、何锡全、吴全衡、吴作人、吴茂荪、金仲华、陈其瑗、周新民、胡愈之、赵朴初、郑森禹、黄家驷、唐明照、马纯古、张文裕、张致祥、崔奇、许广平、戚慕光、杨朔、蔡祖泉、冀朝鼎。
