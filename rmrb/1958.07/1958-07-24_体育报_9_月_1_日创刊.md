Title: “Sports News” to Launch on September 1st
Author:
Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd. The "Sports News," which is being prepared by the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, is scheduled to launch on September 1st. It is planned to be published twice a week, reporting on major domestic and international political events, promoting the Party's policy and tasks in the field of sports, reporting on domestic and international sports activities, publicizing the scientific value of sports and knowledge of sports physiology and hygiene, and introducing basic knowledge and advanced experiences in various sports techniques.

### “体育报”9月1日创刊

1958-07-24
第7版()
专栏：体育新闻

    “体育报”9月1日创刊
    新华社23日讯　国家体委筹办的“体育报”定9月1日创刊。它预定每周出刊两次，将报道国内外重大政治事件，宣传体育运动的方针任务，报道国内外体育活动，宣传体育的科学价值和运动生理卫生知识，介绍各项运动技术的基本知识和先进经验。
