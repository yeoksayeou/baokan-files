Title: Vietnamese Side Writes to International Commission, Accusing U.S. Air Force Personnel of Illegal Activities in South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1958-07-17
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 16th: The Liaison Delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army recently wrote to the International Commission, accusing two groups of senior U.S. Air Force personnel of illegally entering South Vietnam to conduct intervention activities.
    The letter pointed out that on May 14th of this year, U.S. Air Force Secretary Douglas, accompanied by a group of U.S. generals, arrived in South Vietnam. On June 27th, Decherman, a representative of the U.S. Pacific Air Force, led another U.S. military delegation to Saigon. On June 28th, Decherman openly declared in Saigon that he regarded the South Vietnamese Air Force as part of the U.S. Air Force in the Pacific. He had visited the Nha Trang Air Force Base and other places for inspection.
    The letter pointed out that the purpose of Douglas and Decherman's activities in South Vietnam was to inspect the South Vietnamese air force strength and airport construction. This proves that the South Vietnamese authorities are continuing to allow the United States to violate the terms of the Geneva Accords to strengthen military intervention in South Vietnam.
    The Liaison Delegation requested the International Commission to pay special attention to the above situation and take active measures to prevent the continued occurrence of such illegal actions.

### 越方写信给国际委员会  指责美空军人员在南越非法活动

1958-07-17
第6版()
专栏：

    越方写信给国际委员会
    指责美空军人员在南越非法活动
    新华社河内16日电　越南人民军总司令部联络代表团最近写信给国际委员会，指责两批美国高级空军人员非法进入南越进行干涉活动。
    信中指出，今年5月14日，美国空军部部长道格拉斯偕同一批美国将领到达南越。6月27日，美国驻太平洋空军部队的代表德切耳曼又率领一个美国军事代表团到达西贡。6月28日，德切耳曼在西贡公然宣称，他把南越的空军部队看作是美国在太平洋的空军部队的一部分。他曾经到芽庄空军基地和其他一些地方进行视察。
    信中指出，道格拉斯和德切耳曼在南越进行活动的目的，在于检查南越的空军力量和机场的建设情况。这证明南越当局正在继续让美国违反日内瓦协议的条款来加强对南越的军事干涉。
    联络代表团要求国际委员会特别注意上述情况，并且采取积极的措施，防止继续发生这种违法行动。
