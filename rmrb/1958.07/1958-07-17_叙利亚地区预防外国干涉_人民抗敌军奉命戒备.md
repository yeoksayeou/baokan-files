Title: Syria Region Prevents Foreign Interference; People's Anti-Enemy Army Ordered to Standby
Author:
Date: 1958-07-17
Page: 3

    Syria Region Prevents Foreign Interference
    People's Anti-Enemy Army Ordered to Standby
    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 15th: According to Damascus Radio, all personnel of the People's Anti-Enemy Army in the Syrian region of the United Arab Republic have been ordered to remain on alert and have received weapons in preparation for any unforeseen events.

    Damascus Radio stated that this step was taken following reports by foreign news agencies of possible foreign interference in the Middle East. The Syrian people are determined to repel any interference of this nature by force.

    According to the Syrian News Agency, the Syrian Regional Administrative Committee will hold meetings for several consecutive days starting on the 15th.

### 叙利亚地区预防外国干涉  人民抗敌军奉命戒备

1958-07-17
第3版()
专栏：

    叙利亚地区预防外国干涉
    人民抗敌军奉命戒备
    新华社大马士革15日电　据大马士革电台广播，阿拉伯联合共和国叙利亚地区人民抗敌军的全体人员都已经奉命保持戒备，并且已经领到了武器，以准备应付一切意外事件。
    大马士革电台说，这个步骤是在外国通讯社报道外国可能干涉中东地区之后采取的。叙利亚人民下决心以武力来回击这类性质的任何干涉。
    据叙利亚通讯社说，叙利亚地区行政委员会从15日开始将连日开会。
