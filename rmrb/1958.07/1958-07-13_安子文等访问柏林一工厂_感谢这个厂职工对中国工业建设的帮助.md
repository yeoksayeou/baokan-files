Title: An Ziwen and Others Visit a Berlin Factory, Thanking the Factory Workers for Their Help in China's Industrial Construction
Author:
Date: 1958-07-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 11th: An Ziwen and Wang Guoquan, members of the Chinese Communist Party delegation attending the Fifth Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, attended a staff meeting held at the VEB Berliner Schwachstromwerk Treptow, the largest electrical equipment factory in the German Democratic Republic, this afternoon and met with more than 1,000 employees there.

	An Ziwen, a member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, spoke at the meeting. He first congratulated the workers and thanked them for their assistance to China in terms of design, equipment, products, and training of technical personnel. An Ziwen praised the achievements and experiences of the working people of the German Democratic Republic in socialist construction and the struggle for German reunification. When An Ziwen mentioned that the Chinese people had already achieved great successes in industry and agriculture in the first five months of this year, his words were met with particularly enthusiastic applause from the workers. In his speech, An Ziwen also sternly condemned the revisionism of the leading group of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia.

### 安子文等访问柏林一工厂  感谢这个厂职工对中国工业建设的帮助

1958-07-13
第3版()
专栏：

    安子文等访问柏林一工厂
    感谢这个厂职工对中国工业建设的帮助
    据新华社柏林11日电　参加德国统一社会党第五次代表大会的中国共产党代表团团员安子文和王国权今天下午到民主德国最大的电气器材工厂、国营柏林特莱普托夫电气器材制造厂参加了这个工厂举行的职工大会，同那里的一千多名职工会面。
    中国共产党中央委员会委员安子文在大会上讲了话。他首先向职工们表示祝贺，并且感谢他们在设计、设备、产品和培养技术人材方面给予中国的帮助。安子文赞扬民主德国劳动人民在社会主义建设和争取德国统一斗争方面所取得的成就和经验。安子文谈到中国人民在今年头五个月已经在工农业方面取得巨大成就时，他的话引起工人们特别热烈的掌声。安子文在讲话中还严斥南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟领导集团的修正主义。
