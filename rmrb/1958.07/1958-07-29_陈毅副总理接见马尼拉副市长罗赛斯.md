Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Manila Vice Mayor Roses

Author:
Date: 1958-07-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. Vice Premier Chen Yi received Jesus Marcos Roces, Vice Mayor of Manila, Chairman of the Manila City Council, and six others this evening.

Qiao Guanhua, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, was also present at the reception.

### 陈毅副总理接见马尼拉副市长罗赛斯

1958-07-29
第2版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见马尼拉副市长罗赛斯
    新华社28日讯　陈毅副总理今天晚间接见了菲律宾马尼拉市副市长、马尼拉市议会议长赫苏斯·马科斯·罗赛斯等七人。
    接见时，中国人民外交学会副会长乔冠华等也在座。
