Title: The Working Class Firmly Opposes Aggression; Trade Unions of Various Countries Support the Struggle of the People of the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1958-07-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 21st: The Soviet newspaper "Pravda" today published an appeal from Soviet trade unions regarding the issue of armed intervention by U.S. and British imperialism in the internal affairs of Lebanon and Jordan. It called on trade unions and working people of all countries to rise up to stop the aggressors and demanded that the U.S. and British governments cease their armed intervention in the internal affairs of Arab countries and immediately withdraw their invading troops from Lebanon and Jordan.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 22nd: Hoang Quoc Viet, Chairman of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, on July 18th, on behalf of the trade union organizations and all the working people of Vietnam Democratic Republic, sent a telegram to the trade union organizations and all workers of the Republic of Iraq, warmly congratulating the glorious victory of the Iraqi revolution.

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, July 20th: The Central Council of Trade Unions of Romania sent a telegram to the International Confederation of Arab Trade Unions, congratulating the establishment of the Republic of Iraq and supporting the just struggle of the Arab people against aggression.

The telegram said that the Romanian people stand faithfully with the Arab people who oppose imperialism and strive for social progress.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 19th: The National Committee of the All-Indonesia Central Organization of Trade Unions, in reply to a telegram from the International Confederation of Arab Trade Unions, expressed its firm support for the struggle of the Lebanese people. The telegram to Kamal, Secretary-General of the International Confederation of Arab Trade Unions, stated: "The All-Indonesia Central Organization of Trade Unions, with the participation of more than two and a half million Indonesian workers, condemns and strongly protests the aggression of the United States. If the United States troops do not withdraw from Lebanese territory, the All-Indonesia Central Organization of Trade Unions and all Indonesian workers and people are ready to take action to support the Lebanese people's resistance to the heinous aggression of U.S. imperialism."

### 工人阶级坚决反对侵略  各国工会支持中东人民斗争

1958-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

    工人阶级坚决反对侵略
    各国工会支持中东人民斗争
    据新华社莫斯科21日电  苏联“真理报”今天发表了苏联工会就美、英帝国主义武装干涉黎巴嫩和约旦内政问题发出的呼吁书。它呼吁世界各国工会和劳动人民起来制止侵略者，要求美英政府停止武装干涉阿拉伯国家内政并立即从黎巴嫩和约旦撤出侵略军。
    据新华社河内22讯  越南总工会主席黄国越18日代表越南民主共和国各个工会组织和全越南劳动人民，打电报给伊拉克共和国的各个工会组织和全体劳动者，热烈祝贺伊拉克革命的光辉胜利。
    据新华社布加勒斯特20日电  罗马尼亚工会中央理事会打电报给阿拉伯工会国际联合会，祝贺伊拉克共和国成立，支持阿拉伯人民反侵略的正义斗争。
    电报说，罗马尼亚人民是忠实地和反对帝国主义、争取社会进步的阿拉伯人民站在一起的。
    新华社雅加达19日电  全印度尼西亚中央职工会全国委员会在答复阿拉伯工会国际联合会的一封电报中，表示坚决支持黎巴嫩人民的斗争。这封给阿拉伯工会国际联合会总书记卡迈勒的电报说：“有二百五十多万印度尼西亚工人参加的全印度尼西亚中央职工会谴责并且强烈地抗议美国的侵略。如果美国军队不退出黎巴嫩领土，全印度尼西亚中央职工会以及印度尼西亚全体工人和人民随时准备以行动声援黎巴嫩人民抵抗美帝国主义的罪恶侵略。”
