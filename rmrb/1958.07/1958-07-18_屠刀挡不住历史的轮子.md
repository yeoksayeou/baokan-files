Title: The Butcher's Knife Cannot Stop the Wheel of History
Author: Sha Ou
Date: 1958-07-18
Page: 8

    The mask has been torn off again.
    The people of the world all see,
    The American aggressor's teeth,
    Are inlaid with bayonets.
    It pounces on Lebanon,
    Like a starving beast.
    Blood-red eyes turning.
    And flames,
    Are erupting from the hearts of the people,
    To burn the aggressor to death!

    In the Suez Canal,
    There are still the floating corpses of aggressors;
    Beside the pyramids,
    There are still the white bones of aggressors.
    The American aggressor
    Has once again set foot on the land of Lebanon,
    The butcher's knife cannot stop the wheel of history!
    Colonialism is long outdated.
    The surging tide of national liberation,
    Is unfolding from the Middle East, covering the sky and the earth!

### 屠刀挡不住历史的轮子
沙鸥
1958-07-18
第8版()
专栏：

    屠刀挡不住历史的轮子
    沙鸥
    一
    假脸又撕开了。
    全世界人民都看见，
    美国侵略者的牙齿，
    是用刺刀镶成的。
    它扑向黎巴嫩，
    像一头饿极了的野兽。
    血红的眼睛转动着。
    而火焰，
    正从人民心中喷出来，
    要把侵略者烧死！
    二
    苏伊士运河中，
    还有侵略者的浮尸；
    金字塔旁边，
    还有侵略者的白骨。
    美国侵略者
    又踏上黎巴嫩的国土，
    屠刀挡不住历史的轮子！
    殖民主义早就过时了。
    民族解放的滚滚怒潮，
    正从中东遮天盖地展开！
