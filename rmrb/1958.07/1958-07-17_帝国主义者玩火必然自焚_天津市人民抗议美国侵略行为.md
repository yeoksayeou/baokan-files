Title: Imperialists Who Play with Fire Will Surely Burn Themselves: Tianjin People Protest American Aggression
Author:
Date: 1958-07-17
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, July 16th: Over 100,000 people from all walks of life in Tianjin held hundreds of symposiums and mass rallies today. In the evening, some masses paraded through the streets, beating drums and gongs and carrying torches, cheering the establishment of the Republic of Iraq, opposing American interference in the internal affairs of Arab countries, and demanding that American troops get out of Lebanon.

　　Workers, shop assistants, handicraft cooperative members, and street residents of the Hexi District of Tianjin, who were holding the closing ceremony of a technical innovation conference on the brightly lit streets, immediately held a rally of more than 8,000 people upon hearing the news of the American armed intervention in the internal affairs of the Lebanese people, angrily protesting the aggressive actions of American imperialism. Workers Wang Quanyi from the Tianjin Broadcasting Equipment Factory, Li Endi from the handicraft cooperative, Quyi actor Liu Qingliang, and young cadre Liu Zengdi, among others, stated in their speeches that they firmly stand with the Arab people and oppose the aggression of American imperialism. About 20,000 workers and students in this district also held a grand demonstration, shouting slogans such as "Oppose American Imperialist Armed Intervention in the Internal Affairs of Arab Countries," continuing until late at night.

　　At a symposium held by Muslims in Tianjin, Han Zhi Harunai Gan, who had visited the Middle East, represented more than 80,000 Muslims in Tianjin in expressing their determination to fully support the just struggle of the Lebanese and Iraqi people against imperialism. Zhemalin Tini Malun said that Chinese Muslims regard the struggle of the Lebanese and Iraqi people as their own struggle. The aggressive actions of American imperialism will be met with a bloody nose under the opposition of peace-loving people all over the world.

　　Cadres of various Tianjin municipal organs also held meetings. Three thousand cadres from more than a dozen bureaus and offices under the Municipal People's Committee held separate meetings, warning American imperialists not to play with fire and burn themselves.

　　More than 10,000 faculty members and students from six universities and colleges in Tianjin also held gatherings tonight. More than 7,000 faculty members and students from Tianjin University held a demonstration parade in the evening, holding high banners with slogans such as "American Imperialism is Not Allowed to Play with Fire" and "Support Iraq's National Independence." They angrily protested against American imperialist aggression against Lebanon. More than 3,000 faculty members and students from Nankai University held a broadcast rally, shouting slogans such as "Oppose Imperialist Interference" and "Long Live the Independence of the Iraqi People!" Faculty members and students from Tianjin Normal College, Tianjin Medical College, and other schools also held gatherings in the evening. In their speeches, they supported the just struggle of the Lebanese and Iraqi people, and pointed out that the armed intervention of imperialism cannot frighten the Arab people who are fighting for independence, and the imperialists will inevitably be burned to a frazzle by the flames of the anti-colonialist struggle.

### 帝国主义者玩火必然自焚  天津市人民抗议美国侵略行为

1958-07-17
第2版()
专栏：

    帝国主义者玩火必然自焚
    天津市人民抗议美国侵略行为
    新华社天津16日电　天津市各界人民十万多人今天分别举行了上百次的座谈会和群众集会；晚间有的群众敲锣打鼓、举着火炬到街头游行，欢呼伊拉克共和国的成立，反对美国干涉阿拉伯各国人民的内政，并且要求美国军队滚出黎巴嫩。
    正在灯光辉煌的街头上举行技术革新大会闭幕式的天津市河西区的工人、店员、手工业社员和街道居民们，听到美国武装干涉黎巴嫩人民内政的消息，立即举行了有八千多人参加的大会，愤怒抗议美帝国主义的侵略行为。天津广播器材厂工人王全义、手工业社员李恩弟、曲艺演员刘庆良、机关青年工作人员刘增堤等在发言中表示坚决站在阿拉伯人民一边，反对美帝国主义的侵略。这个区约有两万名工人、学生还举行了盛大的示威游行，高呼着反对美帝国主义武装干涉阿拉伯各国人民的内政等口号，一直到深夜。
    在天津市的穆斯林举行的座谈会上，曾经到中东访问过的罕志·哈鲁乃·干一代表天津市八万多名穆斯林表示，一定全力支援黎巴嫩和伊拉克人民反对帝国主义的正义斗争。哲玛林堤尼·马仑说，中国穆斯林把黎巴嫩和伊拉克人民的斗争，看作是自己的斗争。美帝国主义的侵略行为，在全世界爱好和平人民的反对下，一定会碰得头破血流。
    天津市各机关干部也纷纷举行集会。市人民委员会所属十几个局、处的三千名干部分别举行了集会，他们警告美帝国主义者不要玩火自焚。
    天津市六所高等学校的一万多名师生，今晚也分别举行了集会。天津大学七千多名师生晚间高举“不许美帝国主义玩火”、“支持伊拉克的民族独立”的标语举行了示威游行。他们愤怒抗议美帝国主义侵略黎巴嫩。南开大学师生三千多人举行了广播大会，他们高呼“反对帝国主义的干涉”“伊拉克人民独立万岁！”等口号。天津师范学院、天津医学院等学校的师生晚间也举行了集会，他们在发言中支持黎巴嫩和伊拉克人民的正义斗争，并且指出，帝国主义的武装干涉吓不倒为争取独立而斗争的阿拉伯人民，帝国主义者必然被反殖民主义斗争的烈火烧得焦头烂额。
