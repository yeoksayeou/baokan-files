Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Mendès France

Author: 
Date: 1958-07-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 25th: Premier Zhou Enlai received former French Prime Minister and Radical Socialist Party Member of Parliament Mendès France, and French Democratic and Socialist Union of Resistance Member of Parliament Roland Dumas this morning.

Vice Premier Chen Yi and President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhang Xiruo accompanied the reception.

After the reception, the guests and hosts had lunch together. (Attached Picture)

Premier Zhou Enlai talks with Mendès France. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao photographed)

### 周总理接见孟戴斯—弗朗斯

1958-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见孟戴斯—弗朗斯
    新华社25日讯　周恩来总理今天上午接见了法国前总理、激进社会党议员孟戴斯—弗朗斯和法国民主社会抵抗联盟议员罗兰·杜马。
    陈毅副总理和中国人民外交学会会长张奚若陪同接见。
    接见以后，宾主一起共进午餐。（附图片）
