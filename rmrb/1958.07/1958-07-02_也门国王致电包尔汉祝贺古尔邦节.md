Title: The King of Yemen Sends a Telegram to Burhan Congratulating Him on Eid al-Adha
Author:
Date: 1958-07-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 1st: Burhan, the director of the China Islamic Association, received a telegram on June 30th from Imam Ahmad Ibn Yahya Hamidaddin, the King of Yemen, congratulating him on Eid al-Adha. In the telegram, King Ahmad extended his most heartfelt congratulations to Burhan.

### 也门国王致电包尔汉祝贺古尔邦节

1958-07-02
第4版()
专栏：

    也门国王致电包尔汉祝贺古尔邦节
    新华社1日讯　中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉在6月30日收到也门国王伊马姆·艾哈迈德·伊本·叶海亚·哈米德丁发来的一份祝贺古尔邦节的电报。艾哈迈德国王在电报中向包尔汉表示最衷心的祝贺。
