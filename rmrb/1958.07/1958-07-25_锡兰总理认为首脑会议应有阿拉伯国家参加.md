Title: Ceylon Prime Minister Believes Arab Countries Should Participate in Summit Conference
Author:
Date: 1958-07-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 24th: Colombo dispatch: According to the Indian Press Trust, Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike told Western diplomats in Colombo today that, in his opinion, the immediate withdrawal of American and British troops from the Middle East is the main condition for achieving peace in the region. It is reported that the Prime Minister believes that a summit conference to discuss the Middle East issue cannot be held without the participation of Arab countries, and the conference should discuss and recognize Arab nationalism. According to official sources, Bandaranaike has replied to President Nasser, assuring him that Ceylon will do everything possible to achieve peace in the Middle East.

### 锡兰总理认为首脑会议应有阿拉伯国家参加

1958-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    锡兰总理认为首脑会议应有阿拉伯国家参加
    据新华社新德里24日电　科伦坡消息：据印度报业托辣斯报道，锡兰总理班达拉奈克今天曾经告诉西方国家驻科伦坡的外交官员说，在他看来，美英军队立即撤出中东是争取地区和平的主要条件。有消息说，这位总理认为，讨论中东问题的政府首脑会议不能没有阿拉伯国家参加，会议应该讨论和承认阿拉伯民族主义。据官方人士透露，班达拉奈克已经答复纳赛尔总统，保证锡兰将尽一切努力以获致中东地区的和平。
