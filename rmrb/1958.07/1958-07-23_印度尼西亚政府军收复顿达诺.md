Title: Indonesian Government Forces Recapture Tondano
Author:
Date: 1958-07-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 21st: According to a report by Antara News Agency, the Indonesian Army authorities issued a communiqué today stating that Tondano, one of the last strongholds of the rebels in North Sulawesi, had been recaptured by government forces on July 17th after ten days of fighting.

Tondano was recaptured with the cooperation of air force strafing and bombing.

The communiqué said that the rebels had fled to Tomohon and Luluhgan, leaving behind many casualties.

### 印度尼西亚政府军收复顿达诺

1958-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚政府军收复顿达诺
    新华社雅加达21日电  据安塔拉通讯社报道，印度尼西亚陆军当局今天发表公报说，经过十天的战斗以后，叛乱分子在北苏拉威西最后据点之一顿达诺已经在7月17日被政府军收复。
    顿达诺是在空军扫射和轰炸的配合下收复的。
    这个公报说，叛乱分子留下了许多伤亡的人员后，已经逃往多莫洪和鲁鲁干。
