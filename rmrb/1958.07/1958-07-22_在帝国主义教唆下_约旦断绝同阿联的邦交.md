Title: Under Imperialist Instigation, Jordan Severed Diplomatic Relations with the United Arab Republic
Author:
Date: 1958-07-22
Page: 5

Under Imperialist Instigation
Jordan Severed Diplomatic Relations with the United Arab Republic
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 21st: With American and British troops occupying Lebanon and Jordan, the Chamoun and Hussein cliques are taking further hostile actions against the United Arab Republic. According to a report from the Middle East News Agency on the 20th, Jordan has decided to sever diplomatic relations with the United Arab Republic. The government of the United Arab Republic received a note from the Hussein regime of Jordan on the 20th with the above content.

### 在帝国主义教唆下  约旦断绝同阿联的邦交

1958-07-22
第5版()
专栏：

    在帝国主义教唆下
    约旦断绝同阿联的邦交
    据新华社开罗21日电  在美国和英国军队侵占黎巴嫩和约旦以后，夏蒙和侯赛因集团正在对阿拉伯联合共和国采取进一步的敌对态度。据中东通讯社20日报道，约旦已经决定同阿拉伯联合共和国断绝外交关系。阿拉伯联合共和国政府在20日收到了约旦侯赛因政权的内容如上的照会。
