Title: Albania Warns Italian Government Not to Allow the United States to Establish Atomic Bases
Author:
Date: 1958-07-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, July 5th: Newspapers here today published a note from the Albanian government to the Italian government dated July 2nd. The Albanian government warned the Italian government to be responsible for the consequences of allowing the United States to establish rocket launch installations on Italian territory.

The note pointed out that Albania had acquired materials showing that the Italian government was preparing to conclude an agreement with the United States government to set up rocket launch installations on Italian territory, turning Italian territory into a base for atomic aggression. The note then pointed out that this action was contrary to the obligations undertaken by the Italian government in the Italian Peace Treaty and would pose a serious threat to the cause of peace. Understandably, the Albanian government and people cannot but feel uneasy about this step taken by the Italian government, because the establishment of rocket bases in Italy directly threatens Albania's national independence and sovereignty.

The note stated that in order to justify the establishment of atomic and rocket bases on its territory, the Italian government fabricated the claim that there are also atomic bases and rocket launch installations in Albania.

The note pointed out that the Albanian government has long declared that there are neither atomic bases nor rocket launch installations in Albania. In addition, Albania has never attacked, nor will it ever have any intention of attacking Italy.

The Albanian government pointed out that the Italian government's determination to take such a path is fraught with danger not only for other countries but also for Italy itself. Because once the imperialists launch an aggression, Italy will become the target of atomic retaliation. The Albanian government hopes that the Italian government will abandon this measure and not set up rocket launch installations on Italian territory.

The Albanian government finally warned the Italian government and declared: If the Italian government decides to set up rocket launch installations on Italian territory, then Albania will be forced to take measures to defend national independence and sovereignty, that is, there will also be rocket launch installations on Albanian territory. The Italian government will bear full responsibility for this development.

### 阿尔巴尼亚警告意大利政府  不要允许美国建立原子基地

1958-07-06
第3版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚警告意大利政府
    不要允许美国建立原子基地
    新华社地拉那5日电　今天这里的报纸刊载了阿尔巴尼亚政府本月2日致意大利政府的照会。阿尔巴尼亚政府警告意大利政府要对允许美国在意大利领土上建立火箭发射装置所造成的后果负责。
    照会指出，阿尔巴尼亚已经掌握的材料表明，意大利政府准备同美国政府缔结一项关于在意大利领土上设置火箭发射装置的协定，使意大利的领土变成原子进攻的基地。照会接着指出，这种行动是同对意和约中意大利政府所承担的义务相违背的，并且将对和平事业造成严重的威胁。可以理解，意大利政府采取这一步骤不能不使阿尔巴尼亚政府和人民感到不安，因为在意大利设立火箭基地就直接威胁着阿尔巴尼亚的民族独立和主权。
    照会说，意大利政府为了对在其领土上设置原子和火箭基地进行辩解，硬捏造说在阿尔巴尼亚也有原子基地和火箭发射装置。
    照会指出，阿尔巴尼亚政府早就声明过，在阿尔巴尼亚既不存在原子基地，也没有火箭发射装置。另外，阿尔巴尼亚不仅没有进攻过，而且也永远不会有进攻意大利的念头。
    阿尔巴尼亚政府指出，意大利政府决心走这样的道路不仅对其他国家，而且对意大利本身也充满着危险。因为一旦帝国主义发动侵略的话，意大利就会变成原子反击的对象。阿尔巴尼亚政府希望意大利政府放弃这种措施，不要在意大利领土上设置火箭发射装置。
    阿尔巴尼亚政府最后警告意大利政府，并且声明：如果意大利政府决定在意大利领土上设置火箭发射装置，那么，阿尔巴尼亚将被迫采取措施来保卫民族独立和主权，即在阿尔巴尼亚的领土上也将会有火箭发射装置。对这种形势发展的全部责任将由意大利政府承担。
