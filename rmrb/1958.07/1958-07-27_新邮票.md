Title: New Stamps
Author:
Date: 1958-07-27
Page: 8
    The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications issued a set of commemorative stamps for the "Disarmament and International Cooperation Conference" on July 20th.
    The four-cent stamp design represents the determination of the Chinese people to defend peace. Peonies represent the peaceful construction of the Chinese people, and five doves symbolize the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.
    The eight-cent stamp design advocates international cooperation and peaceful coexistence. Olive leaves symbolize peace, and the three-colored ribbons below represent the friendly unity of people all over the world.
    The twenty-two-cent stamp design advocates prohibiting the testing and use of nuclear weapons, achieving disarmament as soon as possible, and advocating the peaceful use of atomic energy. (Image attached)

### 新邮票

1958-07-27
第8版()
专栏：

    新邮票
    邮电部于7月20日发行“裁军和国际合作大会”纪念邮票一套。
    面值四分图案，是表示中国人民保卫和平的决心。牡丹花表示中国人民的和平建设，五个鸽子象征和平的五项原则。
    面值八分图案，是主张国际合作与和平共处。橄榄叶象征和平，下面三色带子，表示全世界人民友好团结。
    面值二十二分图案，是禁止试验和使用核武器，早日实现裁军，主张和平利用原子能。（附图片）
