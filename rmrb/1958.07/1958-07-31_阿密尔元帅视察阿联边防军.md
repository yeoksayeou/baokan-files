Title: Marshal Amer Inspects United Arab Republic Border Guards
Author:
Date: 1958-07-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 30th. Marshal Amer, Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of the United Arab Republic, continued to inspect the border guards today. He said to the officers and soldiers that the people and government of the United Arab Republic love peace and hate aggression, and they are prepared to repel any aggression.

Marshal Amer began inspecting the troops stationed in the border area of the Sinai Peninsula on the 28th.

### 阿密尔元帅视察阿联边防军

1958-07-31
第3版()
专栏：

   阿密尔元帅视察阿联边防军
    新华社开罗30日电　阿拉伯联合共和国武装部队总司令阿密尔元帅今天继续视察边防军。他对官兵们发表讲话说，阿拉伯联合共和国人民和政府热爱和平，憎恨侵略，他们准备击退任何侵略。
    阿密尔元帅从28日开始视察驻在西奈半岛边境地区的部队。
