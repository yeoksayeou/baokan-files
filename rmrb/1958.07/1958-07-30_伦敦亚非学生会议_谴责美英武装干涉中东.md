Title: London Afro-Asian Student Conference Condemns US-UK Armed Intervention in the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1958-07-30
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The Fourth Afro-Asian Student Conference was held in London with students from sixteen Asian and African countries studying in British schools.

    The conference was held under the slogan "Oppose Colonialism that Hinders the Development of Asian and African Countries." The conference unanimously passed a special resolution condemning the blatant military aggression of the United States and Britain against Lebanon and Iraq. The resolution pointed out that the US-UK military intervention is "a challenge to all freedom-loving people in the world and is the greatest threat to the cause of maintaining peace and security."

### 伦敦亚非学生会议  谴责美英武装干涉中东

1958-07-30
第3版()
专栏：

    伦敦亚非学生会议
    谴责美英武装干涉中东
    据新华社讯　亚、非两洲十六个国家在英国学校学习的学生，在伦敦举行了第四届亚非学生会议。
    会议是在“反对阻碍亚非国家发展的殖民主义”的口号下举行的。会议一致通过一项特别决议，谴责英美对黎巴嫩和伊拉克进行的赤裸裸的军事侵略。决议指出，英美的军事干涉是“向全世界爱好自由的人民的挑战，并且是对维护和平安全事业的最大的威胁”。
