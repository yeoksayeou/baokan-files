Title: Tripoli, Libya Holds General Strike to Protest US-British Aggression
Author: 
Date: 1958-07-22
Page: 4

    To Protest US-British Aggression
    Tripoli, Libya Holds General Strike
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 20th: According to a Middle East News Agency reporter in Tripoli on the 19th, residents of Tripoli held a general strike on the 18th to protest the US-British armed intervention in Middle Eastern countries.
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 20th: The Libyan Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee sent a telegram to the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Council in Cairo, stating that the Arabs of Libya strongly condemned the US military occupation of Lebanon and called on the people of Asian and African countries to do everything possible to urge the immediate withdrawal of US troops from Lebanon.

### 抗议美英侵略  利比亚的黎波里总罢市

1958-07-22
第4版()
专栏：

    抗议美英侵略
    利比亚的黎波里总罢市
    据新华社开罗20日电  据中东通讯社记者19日的黎波里报道，的黎波里的居民在18日举行总罢市，抗议美英对中东国家的武装干涉。
    新华社开罗20日电  利比亚亚非团结委员会打电报给开罗的亚非人民团结理事会书记处说，利比亚的阿拉伯人强烈谴责美军占领黎巴嫩，并且呼吁亚非各国人民尽一切力量促使美军立即从黎巴嫩撤退。
