Title: Algerian National Liberation Front Spokesman Says They Will Boycott the Elections Planned by De Gaulle
Author:
Date: 1958-07-09
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 7th: A spokesman for the Algerian National Liberation Front (FLN) office in Damascus announced on July 7th that the Algerian people would not cease their struggle until Algeria was liberated from the control of French imperialism.

He said that the Algerian people would boycott the elections that De Gaulle intends to hold. The purpose of this election is to strengthen French imperialism's control over Algeria.

The spokesman said that the Algerian National Liberation Front's representative in New York is contacting Asian and African delegations in an effort to include the Algerian question on the agenda of the United Nations General Assembly.

### 阿民族解放阵线发言人说  将抵制戴高乐策划的选举

1958-07-09
第6版()
专栏：

    阿民族解放阵线发言人说
    将抵制戴高乐策划的选举
    新华社大马士革7日电  阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线驻大马士革办事处发言人7日宣布，阿尔及利亚人民不会停止斗争，除非阿尔及利亚从法帝国主义控制下获得解放。
    他说，阿尔及利亚人民将抵制戴高乐打算举行的选举。这次选举的目的是加强法帝国主义对阿尔及利亚的控制。
    发言人说，阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线在纽约的代表正在同亚非代表团进行接触，争取把阿尔及利亚问题列入联合国大会的议程。
