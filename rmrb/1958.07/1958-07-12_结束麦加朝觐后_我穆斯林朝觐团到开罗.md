Title: After Completing the Hajj in Mecca, Our Muslim Hajj Delegation Arrives in Cairo
Author:
Date: 1958-07-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 11th: After completing the Hajj in Mecca, the Chinese Muslim Hajj Delegation arrived in Cairo from Jeddah on the 9th and visited Bakuri, Minister of Religious Foundations of the United Arab Republic and Chairman of the Arab-China Friendship Association, on the 10th.

### 结束麦加朝觐后  我穆斯林朝觐团到开罗

1958-07-12
第5版()
专栏：

    结束麦加朝觐后
    我穆斯林朝觐团到开罗
    据新华社开罗11日电　中国穆斯林朝觐团在麦加朝觐以后，9日从吉达到达开罗，并在10日访问了阿拉伯联合共和国宗教基金部长、阿拉伯—中国友好协会主席巴库里。
