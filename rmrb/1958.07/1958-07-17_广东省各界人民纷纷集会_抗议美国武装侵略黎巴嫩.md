Title: People from All Walks of Life in Guangdong Province Gather to Protest the US Armed Aggression Against Lebanon
Author:
Date: 1958-07-17
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, July 16th: Today, factories, schools, people's organizations, and various democratic parties in Guangzhou City held meetings one after another to protest against the US imperialist armed aggression against Lebanon.
    The workers of Guangzhou General Machinery Plant, the largest machine manufacturing factory in South China, enthusiastically hailed the Iraqi people's overthrow of the reactionary rule and the establishment of a republic. At the evening rally, they expressed their firm support for the struggle of the Lebanese and Iraqi people. The members of the Sanyuanli Agricultural Cooperative in the suburbs of Guangzhou held a mass meeting of more than 300 people tonight, demanding that the American aggressor troops immediately get out of Lebanon.
    At the symposium held this evening by the democratic parties and non-party democrats of Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, people warmly hailed the victory of the Iraqi people's revolution, expressed their support for their struggle, and resolutely condemned the US imperialist aggression of sending troops to interfere in Lebanon's internal affairs and threatening the Iraqi people's revolutionary struggle. They all expressed their support for the just struggle of the Lebanese and Iraqi people.
    More than 3,000 teachers and students of Sun Yat-sen University held a rally in the rain. Teachers and students who spoke at the meeting angrily condemned the aggressive actions of US imperialism. The meeting also passed a declaration against US imperialist aggression against Lebanon. Tonight, the South China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the Guangzhou Branch of the Writers Association, the Guangzhou Branch of the Artists Association, and the Guangzhou Branch of the Dramatists Association urgently convened meetings and unanimously sent telegrams condemning US imperialist aggression against Lebanon and expressing support for the Iraqi revolutionary government.

### 广东省各界人民纷纷集会  抗议美国武装侵略黎巴嫩

1958-07-17
第2版()
专栏：

    广东省各界人民纷纷集会
    抗议美国武装侵略黎巴嫩
    新华社广州16日电　广州市的工厂、学校、人民团体和各民主党派今天纷纷集会，抗议美帝国主义武装侵略黎巴嫩。
    华南最大的机器制造厂广州通用机器厂的工人们热情欢呼伊拉克人民推翻反动统治建立共和国，他们在晚上举行的集会上，表示坚决支援黎巴嫩和伊拉克人民的斗争。广州市郊区三元里农业社社员们今晚举行了一个三百多人参加的群众大会，他们要求美国侵略军立即从黎巴嫩滚出去。
    在今晚举行的广东省、广州市各民主党派和无党派民主人士的座谈会上，人们热烈欢呼伊拉克人民革命的胜利，声援他们的斗争，坚决谴责美帝国主义出兵干涉黎巴嫩内政和威胁伊拉克人民革命斗争的侵略行为。他们都表示支持黎巴嫩和伊拉克人民的正义斗争。
    中山大学的三千多名教师和学生冒雨举行了集会。在会上发言的教师和学生愤怒谴责美帝国主义的侵略行为。会上还通过了反对美帝国主义侵略黎巴嫩的宣言。今晚，华南文学艺术界联合会、作家协会广州分会、美术家协会广州分会、戏剧家协会广州分会等组织都紧急召开会议，一致通电谴责美帝国主义侵略黎巴嫩，并表示支持伊拉克革命政府。
