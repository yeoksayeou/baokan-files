Title: "Washington Post" Article Admits US Increasingly Lagging Behind in Space Race
Author: 
Date: 1958-07-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 11th: The "Washington Post" published an article on July 7th by Drew Pearson entitled: "America Still Fails in Space Race," admitting that American scientists are increasingly pessimistic in this regard. The article stated, "We are falling further and further behind in the race to enter space. ... Our scientists are increasingly feeling hopeless. They realize that the Russians may also be preparing to launch people into space, and they have more intelligence about the conditions outside the Earth's atmosphere, so they can design more efficient rockets and leave the lagging United States even further behind."

The author pointed out, "Despite the Eisenhower administration's optimistic tone, we have not caught up with the Russians. Week after week, their advantage grows."

### “华盛顿邮报”载文承认  美国在空间竞赛方面越来越落后

1958-07-12
第6版()
专栏：

    “华盛顿邮报”载文承认
    美国在空间竞赛方面越来越落后
    据新华社11日讯  华盛顿消息：“华盛顿邮报”7月7日发表了德鲁·皮尔逊写的一篇题为：“美国在空间竞赛方面仍然失败”的文章，承认美国科学家在这方面越来越感到悲观。文章说，“在进入空间的竞赛方面，我们是日益落后了。……我们的科学家日益感到没有希望了。他们认识到，俄国人也可能准备把人射入空间，而且他们掌握了更多的关于地球大气层以外的情况的情报，因此他们能设计出效能更高的火箭，并且把落后的美国抛得更远。”
    作者指出，“尽管艾森豪威尔政府唱出乐观的高调，可是我们并没有赶上俄国人。时间一个星期一个星期地过去，他们的优势越来越大了。”
