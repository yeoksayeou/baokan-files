Title: Brief News

Author: 
Date: 1958-07-15
Page: 6

    Brief News
    △ The Arab-China Friendship Association held a tea party in Cairo on the afternoon of the 13th to entertain the Chinese Muslim pilgrimage delegation.
    Bakuri, Minister of Religious Funds of the United Arab Republic and President of the Arab-China Friendship Association, and Zhang Yue, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in the United Arab Republic, attended the reception.
    △ At the invitation of the Soviet government, the Austrian government delegation is scheduled to visit the Soviet Union on July 21. Members of the delegation include: Prime Minister Raab, Deputy Prime Minister Pittermann, and Foreign Minister Figl.
    △ A U.S. youth delegation of forty-one members arrived in Moscow on the 13th. The purpose of the American guests' visit is to learn about the lives of Soviet youth. The U.S. youth delegation came to the Soviet Union based on an agreement reached between representatives of the Soviet Youth Organizations Committee and the U.S. Student Travel Committee during mutual visits.

### 简讯

1958-07-15
第6版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    △阿拉伯—中国友好协会13日下午在开罗举行茶会招待中国穆斯林朝觐团。
    阿拉伯联合共和国宗教基金部长、阿拉伯—中国友好协会主席巴库里和中国驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使馆代办张越出席了招待会。
    △奥地利政府代表团应苏联政府的邀请，定于7月21日到苏联访问。代表团成员有：总理拉布、副总理皮特曼、外交部长费格尔等。
    △由四十一人组成的美国青年代表团13日到达莫斯科。美国客人访问的目的是了解苏联青年的生活。美国青年代表团是根据苏联青年组织委员会和美国大学生旅行委员会代表在互相访问期间达成的协议前来苏联访问的。
