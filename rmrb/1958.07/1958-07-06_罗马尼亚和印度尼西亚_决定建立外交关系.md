Title: Romania and Indonesia Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1958-07-06
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, July 5th: It was announced here that the Romanian government and the Indonesian government have decided to establish diplomatic relations and exchange diplomatic representatives at the level of ministers.

### 罗马尼亚和印度尼西亚  决定建立外交关系

1958-07-06
第3版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚和印度尼西亚
    决定建立外交关系
    新华社布加勒斯特5日电　此间宣布，罗马尼亚政府和印度尼西亚政府决定建立外交关系，并且互派公使级外交代表。
