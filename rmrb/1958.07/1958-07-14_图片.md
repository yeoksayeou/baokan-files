	Below: Catching Up with the U.S. in Three Products
	Wang Hanying, a young technician at the Tianjin Municipal Joint State-Private Yongming Paint Factory, after more than three years of painstaking research and over 300 experiments, improved three pigments: "Chrome Yellow," "Chrome Green," and "Iron Blue." These pigments surpassed the products of the American "Irgans Brothers" Company in terms of color brightness and sun resistance. The picture shows Wang Hanying (center) and engineer Lu Muyi (left) observing the changes in pigment crystal form. Xinhua News Agency reporter Tian Ming.

    下：赶上美国的三种产品
    天津市公私合营永明油漆厂青年技术员王含英，经过三年多苦心钻研，先后经过三百次以上的试验，改进了“铬黄”“铬绿”“铁兰”三种颜料，使这些颜料在色泽鲜艳和耐晒度方面都超过了美国“依肯司兄弟”公司的产品。图为王含英（中）和工程师鲁慕颐（左）在观察颜料结晶形的变化。　新华社记者　田明摄
