Title: Blackboard Placed by the Field Bank
Author:
Date: 1958-07-14
Page: 8

Busy with production during the rice transplanting season,
A blackboard is placed by the field bank.
Take the rest time to learn to read,
Learning what you do, easy to remember.
    〔Yongxin, Jiangxi〕

### 黑板放在田岸旁

1958-07-14
第8版()
专栏：

    黑板放在田岸旁
    莳田时节生产忙，
    黑板放在田岸旁，
    休息时间来识字，
    做啥学啥不易忘。
          〔江西永新〕
