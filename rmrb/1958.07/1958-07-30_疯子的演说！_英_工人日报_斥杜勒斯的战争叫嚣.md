Title: A Madman's Speech! British "Daily Worker" Condemns Dulles's War-Mongering

Author:

Date: 1958-07-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, July 29th: The British "Daily Worker" published a commentary today stating that U.S. Secretary of State Dulles's rampant roaring at the "Baghdad Pact" Council meeting was "a madman's speech."

It pointed out that this is an egregious and irresponsible brinkmanship of war. It shows the world a clear intention: the United States is prepared to take further action in the Middle East to put the world at risk of another world war.

The commentary said, "The British government must be made to reject Dulles's speech. The British government must disassociate Britain from this suicidal policy." The newspaper concluded: "The British government must stop supporting the American attempts to delay a summit conference."

### 疯子的演说！  英“工人日报”斥杜勒斯的战争叫嚣

1958-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

    疯子的演说！
    英“工人日报”斥杜勒斯的战争叫嚣
    新华社伦敦29日电　英国“工人日报”今天发表评论说，美国国务卿杜勒斯在“巴格达条约”理事会会议上的大肆咆哮是“疯子的演说”。
    它指出，这是一种罪大恶极的不负责任的战争边缘的做法。它向全世界表明了明白的意图：美国准备在中东采取进一步的行动以使世界冒一次大战的危险。
    评论说，“必须使英国政府拒绝杜勒斯的演说。英国政府必须使英国脱离这种自杀政策。”报纸最后说：“英国政府必须停止支持美国的拖延召开最高级会议的企图。”
