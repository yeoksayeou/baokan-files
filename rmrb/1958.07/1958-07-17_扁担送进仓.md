Title: Carrying Poles Sent to the Warehouse
Author:
Date: 1958-07-17
Page: 4

    Carrying Poles Sent to the Warehouse
    For thousands of years, people used carrying poles,
    Generations bent their backs under the load.
    Now with the Party's leadership,
    Carrying poles are sent to the warehouse.
