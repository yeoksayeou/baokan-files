Title: The Strike Movement in Britain Developed Greatly Last Year
Author:
Date: 1958-07-25
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, London, 24th—The annual report published by the British Ministry of Labour and National Service admits that last year's strike movement in Britain was the most vigorous since the General Strike of 1926.
    Strikes reduced the exploitation income of British capitalists by 8.5 million working days last year, 80% of which occurred in the machinery manufacturing, shipbuilding, and long-distance bus transportation sectors.

### 英国的罢工运动  去年有很大发展

1958-07-25
第4版()
专栏：

    英国的罢工运动  去年有很大发展
    新华社24日讯　伦敦消息：英国劳工和国民兵役部发表的年度报告承认，去年英国的罢工运动是1926年大罢工以来最蓬勃的。
    罢工使得英国资本家们在去年减少了八百五十万个劳动日的剥削收入，其中有80％发生在机器制造、造船和长途汽车交通等部门里。
