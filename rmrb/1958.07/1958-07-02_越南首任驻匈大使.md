Title: Vietnam's First Ambassador to Hungary

Author:

Date: 1958-07-02
Page: 5

    Vietnam's First Ambassador to Hungary
    Presents Credentials to Dobi
    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 30th June. Nguyen Thanh Ha, the first Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to Hungary, presented his credentials to Dobi, Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, on June 30th.

### 越南首任驻匈大使

1958-07-02
第5版()
专栏：

    越南首任驻匈大使
    向道比递交国书
    新华社布达佩斯30日电　越南民主共和国首任驻匈牙利大使阮清河6月30日向匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比递交了国书。
