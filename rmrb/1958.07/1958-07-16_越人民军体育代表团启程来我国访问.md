Title: Vietnamese People's Army Sports Delegation Departs for Visit to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1958-07-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 14th. The Vietnamese People's Army Sports Delegation left for Beijing by train this morning. They will stay in Beijing for a month and then travel to Leipzig to participate in the first International Friendly Army Summer Games to be held there in September.

The head of the delegation, Huang Wenyi, is the deputy division commander of the Vietnamese People's Army (currently in the Soviet Union), and the deputy head of the delegation, Wu Lun, is the head of the Propaganda Section of the Vietnamese People's Army. The delegation includes sixty-seven football, basketball, shooting, track and field, and swimming athletes.

### 越人民军体育代表团启程来我国访问

1958-07-16
第6版()
专栏：

    越人民军体育代表团启程来我国访问
    据新华社河内14日电　越南人民军体育代表团今天上午乘火车前往北京。他们将在北京逗留一个月，然后转道赴来比锡，参加9月间在那里举行的第一届国际友军夏季运动大会。
    代表团团长黄文艺是越南人民军副师长（现在苏联），副团长吴伦是越南人民军宣传科科长。代表团包括足球、篮球、射击、田径和游泳运动员六十七名。
