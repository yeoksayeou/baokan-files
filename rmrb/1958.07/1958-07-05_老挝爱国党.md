Author:
Date: 1958-07-05

Small Information

    The Lao Patriotic Party, originally called the Lao Patriotic Front, was established on January 6, 1956, in Sam Neua Province, where the former Lao Fighting Forces were concentrated. Prince Souphanouvong, the leader of the former Lao Fighting Forces, served as its chairman. According to the agreement reached between Prince Souphanouvong and Prince Phouma in December 1956, and the provisions of their joint statement issued on November 2, 1957, after the establishment of the Lao coalition government with representatives of the Lao Fighting Forces participating, the Lao Patriotic Front would become a political party, enjoying the same rights and obligations as other political parties legally established in Laos. On November 18 of last year, the Lao Patriotic Party, with government approval, began to operate publicly.

### 老挝爱国党

1958-07-05
第6版()
专栏：小资料

    老挝爱国党
    老挝爱国党，原来叫做老挝爱国战线，是老挝爱国民主力量在1956年1月6日在前寮国战斗部队集结的桑怒省成立的。前寮国战斗部队领袖苏发努冯亲王担任主席。根据1956年12月富马亲王和苏发努冯亲王达成的协议，和他们在1957年11月2日发表的联合声明的规定，在有寮国战斗部队代表参加的老挝联合政府成立后，老挝爱国战线将成为一个政党，它同在老挝依法建立的其他政党一样，享有同等的权利和义务。去年11月18日，老挝爱国党经政府批准开始公开活动。
