Title: Chairman Mao Receives Cambodian Buddhist Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-07-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th — Chairman Mao Zedong received the Cambodian Buddhist Delegation headed by Dharma Master Hu Ta this morning.

Present at the reception were Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China Zhao Puchu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Ding Xilin, and Secretary General of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council Yu Zheng.

Yang An, head of the Cambodian economic delegation, was also present at the reception.

### 毛主席接见柬埔寨佛教代表团

1958-07-01
第2版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见柬埔寨佛教代表团
    新华社30日讯　毛泽东主席今天上午接见了以胡达法师为首的柬埔寨佛教代表团。
    接见时在座的有副总理兼外交部长陈毅，中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初，对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林，国务院宗教事务局秘书长余峥。
    接见时，柬埔寨经济代表团团长杨安也在座。
