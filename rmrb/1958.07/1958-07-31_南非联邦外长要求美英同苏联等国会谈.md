Title: South African Foreign Minister Calls for US and UK to Hold Talks with the Soviet Union and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1958-07-31
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Cape Town, July 30th: South African Foreign Minister Eric Louw stated in parliament on the 28th that the United Kingdom and the United States should hold talks with the Soviet Union and other countries concerned with the Middle East issue, as the Union of South Africa is deeply concerned about the possibility of a world war.

### 南非联邦外长要求美英同苏联等国会谈

1958-07-31
第2版()
专栏：

    南非联邦外长要求美英同苏联等国会谈
    新华社30日讯　开普敦消息：南非联邦外交部长埃里克·劳28日在议会发言时，要求英国和美国同苏联及其他同中东问题有关的国家举行会谈，因为南非联邦对发生世界大战的可能性深感不安。
