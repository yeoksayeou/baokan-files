Title: Large Flax Textile Mill Built in Jiangxi
Author:
Date: 1958-07-25
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, 22nd – The newly built "August 1st" Flax Textile Mill in Jiangxi Province has recently officially begun production. This factory can produce 13 million flax sacks per year. It will change the unreasonable phenomenon of Jiangxi exporting jute and importing flax sacks in the past.

### 江西建成大型麻纺织厂

1958-07-25
第7版()
专栏：

    江西建成大型麻纺织厂
    据新华社南昌22日电　江西省新建的“八一”麻纺织厂最近正式投入生产。这个厂一年能产一千三百万条麻袋。它将改变江西过去黄麻调出、麻袋调入的不合理的现象。
