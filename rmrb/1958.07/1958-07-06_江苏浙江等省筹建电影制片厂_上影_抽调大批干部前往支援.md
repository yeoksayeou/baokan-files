Title: Jiangsu, Zhejiang, and Other Provinces Prepare to Establish Film Studios; Shanghai Film Studio Transfers a Large Number of Cadres to Provide Support
Author:
Date: 1958-07-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, July 5th. The Shanghai Film Studio has decided to transfer a large number of cadres to support the establishment of film studios in Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Shandong, Jiangxi, Fujian, Hubei, and other provinces. The cadres from "Shanghai Film" supporting the provinces include a full complement of film production personnel, including screenwriters, editors, directors, actors, cinematographers, composers, sound recordists, and art designers. At the same time, "Shanghai Film" will hold a training class of more than 300 people to train cadres for the new factories in the provinces. This training class will begin on August 1st. "Shanghai Film" will also send two working groups to help the new factories in various provinces carry out basic construction work.

From July 1st to 4th, "Shanghai Film" held a cooperation meeting in Shanghai with representatives of the new factories of the relevant provinces. Shi Ximin, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Xu Pingyu, Director of the Shanghai Municipal Bureau of Culture, and others attended the meeting and spoke, expressing that Shanghai would do its best to support the establishment of film studios in various provinces. The representatives of the provinces also proposed to vigorously support the Shanghai Film Studio in terms of creative themes, creative personnel going deep into life, and replenishment of reserve personnel. Most of the new factories in the provinces are preparing to be completed in the first half of next year. In the process of building the factories, many factories have already begun filming. Jiangxi, Zhejiang, Fujian, Shandong and other provinces have produced a number of newsreels and science education films. Jiangxi is also filming the feature film "Jinggang Mountains" and will film the feature film "Fang Zhimin" next year.

### 江苏浙江等省筹建电影制片厂  “上影”抽调大批干部前往支援

1958-07-06
第2版()
专栏：

    江苏浙江等省筹建电影制片厂
    “上影”抽调大批干部前往支援
    新华社上海5日电　上海电影制片公司决定抽调大批干部，支援江苏、浙江、山东、江西、福建、湖北等省建立电影制片厂。“上影”支援各省的干部包括编剧、编辑、导演、演员、摄影、作曲、录音、美工等全套电影创作人员。同时，“上影”将举办一个三百多人的训练班，为各省的新厂培训干部。这个训练班8月1日就要开学。“上影”还要派出两个工作组，帮助各新厂进行基本建设工作。
    7月1日至4日，“上影”与有关各省新厂的代表在上海举行了协作会议。中共上海市委宣传部部长石西民、上海市文化局局长徐平羽等都出席会议讲话，表示上海有关方面一定尽力支援各省建立电影制片厂。各省代表也提出：要在创作题材、创作人员深入生活、后备人员补充等方面，大力支援上海电影制片公司。各省的新厂大多准备在明年上半年建成。在建厂过程中，许多厂都已开始摄制影片。江西、浙江、福建、山东等省都已制成一批新闻片和科学教育片。江西还正在拍摄故事片“井冈山”，明年将拍摄故事片“方志敏”。
