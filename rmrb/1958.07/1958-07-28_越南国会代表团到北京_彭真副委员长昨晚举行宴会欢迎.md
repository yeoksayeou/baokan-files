Title: Vietnamese National Assembly Delegation Arrives in Beijing, Vice Chairman Peng Zhen Hosts Banquet Last Night to Welcome Them
Author:
Date: 1958-07-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th. The delegation of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Chairman Ton Duc Thang and Vice Chairman Tran Huy Lieu, arrived in Beijing by plane from Ulaanbaatar this morning after concluding its visit to six socialist countries in Eastern Europe and the Mongolian People's Republic. The delegation will visit Beijing before returning home.

At the East Suburb Airport, the distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and members Wang Weizhou, Gao Chongmin, Zhang Su, and Mei Gongbin, Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and responsible persons from the All-China Women's Federation and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee, and received flowers.

Pham Binh, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and diplomatic envoys and officials from Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Bulgaria, Albania, Poland, and Mongolia to China, also went to the airport to welcome them. Yang An, head of the Cambodian Economic Delegation in Beijing, and his deputy Nai Warangdan, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th. Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, hosted a banquet this afternoon to entertain the delegation of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Chairman Ton Duc Thang and Vice Chairman Tran Huy Lieu.

NPC Standing Committee members Wang Weizhou, Liu Ningyi, Gao Chongmin, Zhang Su, and Mei Gongbin, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo, and responsible persons from the All-China Women's Federation and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee, attended the banquet.

Pham Binh, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, was invited to attend the banquet.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests had cordial conversations and frequently raised their glasses to wish for the continuous strengthening of the friendly unity and mutual assistance and cooperation between China and Vietnam, and to wish Chairman Mao Zedong and President Ho Chi Minh good health. ### 越南国会代表团到北京  彭真副委员长昨晚举行宴会欢迎

1958-07-28
第2版()
专栏：

    越南国会代表团到北京
    彭真副委员长昨晚举行宴会欢迎
    新华社27日讯　以孙德胜团长、陈辉燎副团长为首的越南民主共和国国会代表团，在结束了对东欧六个社会主义国家和蒙古人民共和国的访问以后，今天上午乘飞机由乌兰巴托到达北京。代表团将在北京参观游览后回国。
    贵宾们在东郊机场上，受到全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真、委员王维舟、高崇民、张苏、梅龚彬，外交部副部长罗贵波以及全国妇联和北京市人民委员会负责人的热烈欢迎，并接受了献花。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办范平，捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、波兰、蒙古驻中国的使节和外交官员，也到机场欢迎。到机场欢迎的还有柬埔寨驻京经济代表团团长杨安、副团长乃瓦朗丹。
    新华社27日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真今天下午举行宴会，招待以孙德胜团长、陈辉燎副团长为首的越南民主共和国国会代表团。
    人大常委会委员王维舟、刘宁一、高崇民、张苏、梅龚彬，外交部副部长罗贵波以及全国妇联和北京市人民委员会的负责人等，出席宴会作陪。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办范平应邀参加了宴会。
    席间，宾主亲切交谈，并且频频举杯，祝中、越两国的友好团结和互助合作不断加强，祝毛泽东主席和胡志明主席健康。（附图片）
    人大常委会副委员长彭真（左）设宴招待越南民主共和国国会代表团。这是在宴会前彭真和代表团团长孙德胜（前右）欢谈。
                        新华社记者　杜修贤摄
