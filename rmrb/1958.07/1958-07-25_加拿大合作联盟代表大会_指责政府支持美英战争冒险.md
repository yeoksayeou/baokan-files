Title: Canadian Cooperative Commonwealth Federation Congress Accuses Government of Supporting US-UK War Adventure
Author:
Date: 1958-07-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Montreal, 24th: The Canadian Cooperative Commonwealth Federation (CCF), at its congress that opened on the 23rd, condemned the US-UK military intervention in the Middle East and blamed the Canadian government for tolerating and supporting this "clearly irresponsible nuclear war adventure."

The CCF believes that the crisis in the Middle East is due to the Western countries' failure to consider "the legitimate aspirations of the Arab people to get rid of foreign rule and achieve social and economic progress."

However, while condemning US-UK intervention in the Middle East, the Canadian CCF also called for the immediate replacement of the US-UK aggressor troops by a so-called "United Nations police force."

### 加拿大合作联盟代表大会  指责政府支持美英战争冒险

1958-07-25
第3版()
专栏：

    加拿大合作联盟代表大会
    指责政府支持美英战争冒险
    新华社24日讯　蒙特利尔消息：加拿大合作联盟在23日开幕的代表大会上谴责美英对中东的军事干涉，并且责怪加拿大政府曾经宽容和支持这种“显然是不负责任的核战争冒险”。
    合作联盟认为中东的危机是由于西方国家没有考虑到“阿拉伯人民要求摆脱外来统治和取得社会和经济进步的合法愿望”。
    但是，加拿大合作联盟在谴责美英干涉中东的同时，却又要求立即由所谓“联合国警察部队”来代替美英侵略军。
