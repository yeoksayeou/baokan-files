Title: CCP Central Military Commission Holds Enlarged Meeting to Discuss Current Situation and National Defense Work
Author:
Date: 1958-07-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 25th - The Chinese Communist Party Central Military Commission held an enlarged meeting from May 27th to July 22nd with over a thousand senior cadres participating. Under the leadership of the Party Central Committee and Comrade Mao Zedong, the meeting used the method of rectification to examine the construction work of the People's Liberation Army over the past eight years since the founding of the country, and determined the future military building policy. At the same time, in view of the current international situation, it discussed national defense work and made decisions. Comrade Mao Zedong spoke at the meeting. Comrades Zhu De, Lin Biao, Deng Xiaoping, Liu Bocheng, He Long, Chen Yi, Luo Ronghuan, Nie Rongzhen, and Ye Jianying also spoke at the meeting. At the end of the discussion, Comrade Peng Dehuai made a concluding speech.

### 中共中央军事委员会举行扩大会议  讨论了当前局势和国防工作

1958-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央军事委员会举行扩大会议
    讨论了当前局势和国防工作
    新华社25日讯　中国共产党中央军事委员会在5月27日至7月22日举行了有高级干部一千余人参加的扩大会议。会议在党中央和毛泽东同志领导下，用整风的方法检查了建国八年多来人民解放军的建设工作，确定了今后的建军方针；同时，针对着目前的国际局势，对国防工作进行了讨论，并且作出了决定。毛泽东同志在大会上讲了话。朱德、林彪、邓小平、刘伯承、贺龙、陈毅、罗荣桓、聂荣臻、叶剑英等同志也都在大会上讲了话。在讨论结束的时候，彭德怀同志作了总结发言。
