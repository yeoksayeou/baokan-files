Title: Scrap the Baghdad Pact! Newspaper Chronicles Mocks US for Not Understanding Political Climate
Author:
Date: 1958-07-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, London, July 30th: The "News Chronicle," a British Liberal Party newspaper, published an article on the 29th by James Cameron titled "Scrap the Baghdad Pact, Mr. Dulles—Let the Summit Work Begin," mocking the clumsy tactics of the United States in delaying a summit meeting of heads of government. It said, "How can the Russians prepare in four days and travel five thousand miles to attend a summit meeting, while the Americans need two weeks to prepare without moving an inch?"
    The article accused Dulles of running to London to attend the "Baghdad Pact" Council meeting when the broad public demanded a summit meeting to discuss the Middle East issue.
    The author satirized, "It is not yet known whether he carries a machine that can record the external political climate—a climate that is far from temperate."
    The author of the article said that the only international event that concerns everyone or has any practical significance today—the summit meeting—is being hampered and obstructed by the most painstaking pretense and delaying tactics since the time of Metternich.
    The article pointed out that the "Baghdad Pact" has become a three-legged stool due to the revolution in Iraq. A "Baghdad Pact" without Baghdad is like a Martini cocktail without Martini.
    The article demanded that this "Baghdad Pact" without Baghdad dissolve the "Baghdad Pact" when it "meets at the funeral roast table in London."

### 把巴格达条约收起来！  新闻纪事报嘲笑美国不懂政治气候

1958-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

    把巴格达条约收起来！
    新闻纪事报嘲笑美国不懂政治气候
    据新华社30日讯　伦敦消息：英国自由党办的“新闻纪事报”29日刊登的由詹姆斯·凯麦隆写的题为“把巴格达条约收起来，杜勒斯先生——开始进行最高级会议的工作吧”的文章，嘲笑美国拖延政府首脑会议的拙劣手法说：“俄国人怎么能够在四天中准备妥当走五千英里的路程去参加一次最高级会议，而美国人却需要两星期的时间来准备原地不动呢？”
    文章指责杜勒斯在广大公众要求举行讨论中东问题的最高级会议时却跑去伦敦参加“巴格达条约”理事会会议。
    文章作者讽刺说，“现在还不知道他是否带有能够记录外界政治气候——这种气候远不是温和的——的机器。”
    文章的作者说，今天每个人所关心的或者具有任何实际意义的唯一国际事件——最高级会议——受到了自从梅特涅时代以来的最煞费苦心的假装方法和拖延手段所妨碍和阻挠。
    文章指出，“巴格达条约”由于伊拉克发生了革命已经成为缺了一条腿的小凳子，没有巴格达的“巴格达条约”就像没有掺有马提尼酒的马提尼鸡尾酒一样。
    文章要求这个没有巴格达的“巴格达条约”“在伦敦的葬礼烤肉桌前开会”时解散“巴格达条约”。
