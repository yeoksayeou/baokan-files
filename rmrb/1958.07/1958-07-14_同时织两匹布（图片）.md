Title: Weaving Two Bolts of Cloth Simultaneously (Picture)
Author: Zhang Gengxin
Date: 1958-07-14
Page: 3

    Weaving Two Bolts of Cloth Simultaneously
    Workers at the Hangzhou Victory Silk Weaving Sample Factory successfully modified a silk weaving machine to weave two bolts of silk satin simultaneously, and the quality of the products meets the standards completely. Left picture: A silk weaving worker is using this machine to weave silk. Right picture: The shuttle races are separated to weave two bolts of silk simultaneously. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Gengxin took the photos.
