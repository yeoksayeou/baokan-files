Title: UAR Chargé d'Affaires in Iraq Presents Letter of Recognition to Qasim
Author:
Date: 1958-07-17
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 15th July. Baghdad Radio announced on the evening of the 15th that the Chargé d'Affaires of the United Arab Republic to Iraq met with the Iraqi Prime Minister Qasim and presented him with the official letter of recognition from the United Arab Republic.

### 阿联驻伊拉克代办向卡塞姆呈递承认书

1958-07-17
第3版()
专栏：

    阿联驻伊拉克代办向卡塞姆呈递承认书
    据新华社开罗15日电　巴格达电台15日晚上宣布，阿拉伯联合共和国驻伊拉克代办会晤伊拉克总理卡塞姆，向他递交了阿拉伯联合共和国的正式承认书。
