Title: Tehran's "World News" says: Summit contacts are beneficial to peace
Author:
Date: 1958-07-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, July 24th: The "World News" commented on Khrushchev's letters to Eisenhower, Macmillan, De Gaulle, and Nehru, expressing its belief that direct contact between the U.S. national leaders and the Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers would help establish further mutual understanding between the United States and the Soviet Union. The newspaper emphasized that "in any case, such a meeting is beneficial to the cause of peace and holds the promise of opening the road to peace in the Middle East."

### 德黑兰“世界报”说：首脑接触有利和平

1958-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

    德黑兰“世界报”说：首脑接触有利和平
    新华社25日讯　据塔斯社德黑兰24日讯：“世界报”评论赫鲁晓夫给艾森豪威尔、麦克米伦、戴高乐和尼赫鲁的信件时表示相信，美国国家领导人同苏联部长会议主席的直接接触将有助于在美国和苏联之间建立更进一步的互相谅解。报纸强调指出，“无论如何，这种会见有利于和平事业，并且有希望为中近东的和平开辟道路”。
