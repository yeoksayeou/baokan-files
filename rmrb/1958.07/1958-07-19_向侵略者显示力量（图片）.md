Date: 1958-07-19
Page: 2

Displaying Strength to the Aggressors

From yesterday afternoon until this morning, the people of the capital held demonstrations and protests against the Anglo-American aggression, and protested at the British Representative Office. The gate and walls of the British Representative Office were covered with layers of slogans and cartoons, and many demonstrators also read out protest letters to the staff of the Representative Office.

### 向侵略者显示力量（图片）

1958-07-19
第2版()
专栏：

    向侵略者显示力量
    从昨日下午起到今日清晨，首都人民游行示威，反对美英侵略行为，并到英代办处抗议。英国代办处的大门和墙头上贴满了重重叠叠的标语和漫画，不少示威群众还对着代办处的工作人员宣读了抗议书。 
    本报记者　吕相友摄
    北京群众向英国代办处工作人员宣读抗议书　新华社记者　傅军摄
