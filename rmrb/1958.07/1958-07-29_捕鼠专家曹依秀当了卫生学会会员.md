Title: Rat-catching Expert Cao Yixiu Becomes Member of Hygiene Society
Author:
Date: 1958-07-29
Page: 7

    Rat-catching Expert Cao Yixiu
    Becomes Member of Hygiene Society
    (Our newspaper dispatch) "Folk expert" Cao Yixiu, due to his profound research into the living habits of wild rats, has made a certain contribution to the thorough elimination of rat infestations. He has been formally approved by the Yunnan Branch of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Specialized Societies and accepted as a member of the Hygiene Society of the Kunming Branch of the Chinese Medical Association.

### 捕鼠专家曹依秀当了卫生学会会员

1958-07-29
第7版()
专栏：

    捕鼠专家曹依秀
    当了卫生学会会员
    本报讯　“土专家”曹依秀，由于对野鼠的生活规律有深刻的研究，为彻底消灭鼠害作出了一定贡献，已经过中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会云南分会正式批准，接受为中华医学会昆明分会卫生学会会员。
