Title: Japanese Communist Party Holds Seventh Congress; Soviet Communist Party Delegation Invited to Attend
Author:
Date: 1958-07-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, July 21st: The Seventh Congress of the Japanese Communist Party opened in Tokyo today. According to today's report in the Japanese Communist Party's newspaper, "Akahata," the congress is scheduled to last eleven days. The first two days will be for preparatory meetings, with plenary sessions starting on the 23rd.

Approximately 500 people are attending the congress, including representatives elected from various regions, members of the Party Central Committee, members of the Control Committee, and leading Party cadres elected at the Sixth Congress.

The main agenda items for the congress are: to hear the political report of the Party Central Committee; to discuss the draft of the Party Constitution; and to elect the Party's leading bodies. A delegation from the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, composed of M.B. Mitin, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and A.M. Satiukov, editor-in-chief of the Soviet newspaper "Pravda," has been invited to attend the congress.

The congress has received congratulatory telegrams and letters from Communist and Workers' Parties in Italy, Tunisia, Great Britain, Australia, France, New Zealand, Sweden, Luxembourg, Israel, Finland, Uruguay, Indonesia, Czechoslovakia, and Vietnam.

### 日本共产党七大开幕　苏联共产党代表团应邀出席大会

1958-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　日本共产党七大开幕
　　苏联共产党代表团应邀出席大会
    新华社21日讯  东京消息：日本共产党第七次代表大会今天在东京开幕。据日本共产党机关报“赤旗报”今天报道，大会会期预定为十一天。头两天举行预备会议，从23日起举行全体会议。
    出席大会的有各地选出的代表、党中央委员会委员、统制委员会委员以及在第六次代表大会上选出的党的领导干部等约五百人。
    大会的主要议程是：听取党中央委员会的政治报告；讨论党章草案；选举党的领导机构。由苏联共产党中央委员会委员米丁和苏联“真理报”总编辑萨丘科夫组成的苏联共产党代表团应邀出席大会。
    大会接到了意大利、突尼斯、英国、澳大利亚、法国、新西兰、瑞典、卢森堡、以色列、芬兰、乌拉圭、印度尼西亚、捷克斯洛伐克、越南等国家的共产党和工人党的贺电和贺信。
