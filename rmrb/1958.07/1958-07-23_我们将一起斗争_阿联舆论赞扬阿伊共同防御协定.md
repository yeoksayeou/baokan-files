Title: "We Will Fight Together" - United Arab Republic Public Opinion Praises the UAR-Iraq Joint Defense Agreement
Author:
Date: 1958-07-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 20th: Newspapers here today prominently featured the joint defense agreement signed by the United Arab Republic and Iraq in Damascus on the 19th.

All Arabic newspapers carried the agreement with red banner headlines. "Al-Akhbar" (The News) was headlined: "We Will Fight Together."

"Al-Ahram" (The Pyramids) published an editorial welcoming the agreement, saying: "Despite all the attempts, all the occupying forces, and all the acts of treason, what happened yesterday will also happen tomorrow. We are the same people, the same nation, adhering to the same unchanging principles."

### “我们将一起斗争”  阿联舆论赞扬阿伊共同防御协定

1958-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

    “我们将一起斗争”
    阿联舆论赞扬阿伊共同防御协定
    新华社开罗20日电  今天这里的各报都以显著地位刊载阿拉伯联合共和国和伊拉克19日在大马士革签订的共同防御协定。
    所有的阿拉伯文报纸都用红字通栏标题刊载了这个协定，“消息报”的标题是：“我们将一起斗争”。
    “金字塔报”发表社论欢迎这个协定说：“尽管有着各种企图，有着各种占领军和各种叛国勾当，昨天发生的事情明天也将会发生。我们是同一种人民，同一个民族，奉行同一个不变的原则。”
