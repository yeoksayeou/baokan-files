Title: Our Agricultural Delegation Arrives in Prague
Author:
Date: 1958-07-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 16th - The Chinese agricultural science delegation, headed by Yang Xiandong, Deputy Minister of Agriculture and Chairman of the Chinese Society of Agronomy, arrived in Prague this afternoon from Berlin. After visiting the German Democratic Republic's 6th Agricultural Exhibition in Berlin, the delegates were invited to Czechoslovakia for a friendly visit and inspection lasting more than a month.

### 我农业代表团到布拉格

1958-07-19
第5版()
专栏：

    我农业代表团到布拉格
    新华社布拉格16日电　以农业部副部长、中国农学会理事长杨显东为首的中国农业科学代表团，今天下午自柏林到达布拉格。代表们在柏林参观了民主德国第六届农业览展会后，应邀到捷克斯洛伐克进行一个多月的友好访问和考察。
