Title: Ulbricht Awarded the Order of Labor Hero Again
Author:
Date: 1958-07-02
Page: 5
　
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 30th - Today is the sixty-fifth birthday of Walter Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany. On the eve of his birthday, President Pieck awarded Ulbricht the "Labor Hero" medal in recognition of his merits in the socialist construction of the German Democratic Republic. This is the second time he has received the title of Labor Hero. The Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany also sent a telegram to Ulbricht to express congratulations.

### 乌布利希再获劳动英雄勋章

1958-07-02
第5版()
专栏：

    乌布利希再获劳动英雄勋章
    新华社柏林30日电　今天是德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希的六十五岁寿辰。皮克总统在他寿辰的前夕授给乌布利希“劳动英雄”的勋章，以表彰他在民主德国社会主义建设中的功绩。这是他第二次获得劳动英雄的称号。德国统一社会党中央委员会也致电乌布利希，表示祝贺。
