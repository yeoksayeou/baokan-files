Title: Pictures

Author:

Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 2

    Bottom Left Picture: Workers at the Shanghai Cable Factory are taking practical actions to support the Arab people's struggle against colonialism. These days, the cable workshop often exceeds its quota, with one shift achieving a maximum output of 5.2 kilometers, far exceeding the target.
                    Xinhua News Agency Reporter: Chen Juanmei

    Right Picture: More than 4,000 farmers in Zhangjiawan Township, Tongzhou District, Beijing, gathered on July 19 to protest against the US-British imperialist aggression in the Middle East. They shouted: "American troops get out of Lebanon!" "British troops get out of Jordan!"
                      Xinhua News Agency Reporter: Na Yi

    Bottom Right Picture: People from all walks of life in Guangzhou held a rally to protest against US-British aggression in the Middle East, support the Republic of Iraq, and support the Lebanese national independence movement. Muslims at the rally shouted: "US-British aggressors get out of the Middle East!"
            Xinhua News Agency Reporter: Cai Zhongzhi, Li Zhizhao (Poster) Xinhua News Agency Draft

### 图片

1958-07-24
第2版()
专栏：

    左下图：上海电缆厂职工正在以实际行动支援阿拉伯人民反殖民主义的斗争。这几天电缆车间常常突破定额，有一班最高的产量达五点二公里。远远超过了指标。
                    新华社记者　陈娟美摄
    右图：北京市通州区张家湾乡四千多名农民在7月19日集会，抗议美英帝国主义侵略中东。他们高呼：“美国军队从黎巴嫩滚出去！”“英国军队从约旦滚出去！”
                      新华社记者　纳　一摄
    右下图：广州市各界人民举行抗议美英侵略中东、支持伊拉克共和国、支持黎巴嫩民族独立运动大会，伊斯兰教徒在大会上高呼：美英侵略者滚出中东去！
            新华社记者　蔡忠植、李志昭摄（招贴画）　新华社稿
