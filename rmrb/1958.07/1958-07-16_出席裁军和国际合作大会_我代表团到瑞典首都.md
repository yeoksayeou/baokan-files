Title: Attending the Disarmament and International Cooperation Conference, Our Delegation Arrives in the Swedish Capital
Author:
Date: 1958-07-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, July 14th: The Chinese delegation attending the Disarmament and International Cooperation Conference arrived here by plane this afternoon.

The 31-member delegation is led by Guo Moruo, head of the delegation, and Liao Chengzhi and Bao Erhan, deputy heads of the delegation.

Some members of the Chinese delegation already arrived here yesterday.

### 出席裁军和国际合作大会  我代表团到瑞典首都

1958-07-16
第6版()
专栏：

    出席裁军和国际合作大会
    我代表团到瑞典首都
    新华社斯德哥尔摩14日电　出席裁军和国际合作大会的中国代表团今天下午乘飞机到达这里。
    这个三十一人的代表团是由团长郭沫若、副团长廖承志和包尔汉率领的。
    中国代表团部分团员已经在昨天到达这里。
