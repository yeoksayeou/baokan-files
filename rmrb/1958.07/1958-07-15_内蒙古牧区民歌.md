Inner Mongolia Pastoral Folk Songs

    On the High Mountain
    On the high mountain,
    The cuckoo sings,
    On the green grassland,
    Cattle and sheep grow freely.
    Blue mist flows around the mountainside,
    The yurt of our pastoral cooperative shines at the foot of the mountain,
    How prosperous is our animal husbandry,
    How beautiful is our home.
    Riding on horseback, grazing,
    Cattle and sheep follow closely by my side,
    Grazing thousands of miles away from home,
    My beloved pastoral cooperative is in my heart.

    Vast Grassland
    On the vast grassland,
    Endless flocks of sheep are moving,
    Flame-like red flags,
    Fly in the sky above the pasture.
    Our land shines with golden light,
    Young people throw themselves into labor like lions,
    The General Line radiates the light of the sun,
    Encouraging us to move forward.
    We are full of enthusiasm,
    Melting steel;
    The great cause of the people,
    Shines with our loyalty to the motherland.

    (Collected by Qimudedaoerji, Translated by Anmiji)

### 内蒙古牧区民歌

1958-07-15
第2版()
专栏：

    内蒙古牧区民歌
    高高的山上
    高高的山上，
    布谷鸟在歌唱，
    碧绿的草原上，
    牛羊在自由成长。
    蓝雾在山腰流动，
    我们牧业社的包顶在山脚下闪光，
    多繁荣呵，我们的牧畜事业，
    多美丽呵，我们的家乡。
    骑上马儿游牧呵，
    牛羊紧跟在我身旁，
    离家千里去游牧呵，
    亲爱的牧业社在我心上。
    广阔的草原
    广阔的草原上，
    无边的羊群在移动，
    烈火般的红旗，
    飘扬在牧场的上空。
    我们的土地闪着金光，
    青年们狮子般投入劳动，
    总路线放出太阳的光辉，
    鼓舞着我们前进。
    我们满腔的热情，
    把钢铁熔化；
    人民伟大的事业，
    闪耀着我们对祖国的忠诚。
    （其木德道尔吉搜集，安谧吉译）
