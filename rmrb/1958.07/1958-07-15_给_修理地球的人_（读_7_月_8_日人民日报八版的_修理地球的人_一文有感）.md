Title: To the "People Who Repair the Earth" (Thoughts after reading the article "The People Who Repair the Earth" on page 8 of the People's Daily, July 8th)
Author: Li Guangtian
Date: 1958-07-15
Page: 8

    To the "People Who Repair the Earth"
    (Thoughts after reading the article "The People Who Repair the Earth" on page 8 of the People's Daily, July 8th)
    Li Guangtian
    Dear comrades, I congratulate you,
    Because you have chosen the best profession,
    Your profession is "repairing the Earth,"
    This team includes "you" and "me."
    Although this Earth has problems,
    It has long had a new life,
    The new life is our camp,
    See how the East wind has already overwhelmed the West's autumn wind.
    Unite all who can be united,
    Connect all our arms together,
    Break all the chains of the enemy,
    Liberate all the suffering people.
    Plow all the barren land,
    Sweep away all the filth,
    Build factories and mines,
    Plant crops and flowers.
    Dig out all the treasures of the Earth,
    Suck out all the milk in its bosom,
    Blaze new trails where no one has gone before,
    Build skyscrapers in places uninhabitable.
    The imperialists want to play a football game,
    Vainly trying to kick the Earth back and forth,
    Their calculations are sure to fail,
    The affairs of the Earth will be arranged by "us"!
    We must carefully repair the Earth,
    We must polish it into the brightest star,
    We must hold it high in the universe,
    We must make it more beautiful than any other planet.
    Dear comrades, I congratulate you,
    Because you have chosen the best profession,
    Let us work together to do this great undertaking well,
    This line of work includes "you" and "me."

### 给“修理地球的人”（读7月8日人民日报八版的“修理地球的人”一文有感）
李广田
1958-07-15
第8版()
专栏：

    给“修理地球的人”
    （读7月8日人民日报八版的“修理地球的人”一文有感）
    李广田
    亲爱的同志，我向你祝贺，
    因为你选择了最好的职业，
    你的职业是“修理地球”，
    这个队伍里有“你”也有“我”。
    这个地球虽然出了毛病，
    可是它早就有了新的生命，
    新生命就是我们的阵营，
    看东风已经压倒了西方的秋风。
    把可以团结的都团结起来，
    把我们的胳臂都连接起来，
    把敌人的锁链都一齐打坏，
    把受难的人们都解放出来。
    把荒芜的土地都耕起来，
    把污秽的东西都扫除开，
    把工厂和矿山都建起来，
    把庄稼和鲜花都种起来。
    把地球的宝藏都挖出来，
    把它怀里的乳汁都吸出来，
    从没有人走的地方踏出大路来，
    在不能居住的地方盖起大厦来。
    帝国主义要来一场足球赛，
    妄想把地球踢去又踢来，
    他的算盘当然要失败，
    地球上的事情要让“我们”来安排！
    我们一定要把地球精心地修理，
    要把它琢磨成一颗最亮的星体，
    要把它在宇宙中高高地举起，
    要叫它比任何星球都美丽。
    亲爱的同志，我向你祝贺，
    因为你选择了最好的职业，
    让我们共同来作好这件事业，
    这一行里有“你”也有“我”。
