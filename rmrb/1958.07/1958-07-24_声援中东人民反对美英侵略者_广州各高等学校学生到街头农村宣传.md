Title: In Support of the Middle Eastern People Against the American and British Aggressors: Students from Various Universities in Guangzhou Conduct Propaganda on the Streets and in Rural Areas

Author:
Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, July 22nd. Students from various universities in Guangzhou have been taking to the streets and rural areas in recent days to carry out propaganda activities against the American and British imperialist aggression in the Middle East and in support of the just struggle of the Arab people.

Students from Zhongshan University, South China Institute of Technology, Zhongshan Medical College, and South China Agricultural College have all participated in this propaganda activity. On the afternoon of the 20th, more than eighty students from the Chinese Department of Zhongshan University performed living newspaper dramas they created themselves at the Guangzhou Railway Station Plaza and in front of the Southern Mansion, the busiest places in the city.

Students from the Physics Department of Zhongshan University went to Yaotou Township and painted many wall posters protesting the American and British imperialist aggression in the Middle East.

### 声援中东人民反对美英侵略者  广州各高等学校学生到街头农村宣传

1958-07-24
第2版()
专栏：

    声援中东人民反对美英侵略者
    广州各高等学校学生到街头农村宣传
    据新华社广州22日电　广州市各高等学校学生连日来纷纷出动到街头和农村，开展反对美英帝国主义侵略中东、声援阿拉伯人民正义斗争的宣传活动。
    中山大学、华南工学院、中山医学院、华南农学院的学生都参加了这一宣传活动。20日下午，中山大学中文系八十多个学生到全市最热闹的广州快车站广场和南方大厦门口，演出他们自己创作的活报剧。
    中山大学物理系的学生们，到瑶头乡画了许多抗议美英帝国主义侵略中东的墙头画。
