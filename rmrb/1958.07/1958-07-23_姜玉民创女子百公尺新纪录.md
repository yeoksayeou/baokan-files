Title: Jiang Yumin Sets New Women's 100m Record
Author:
Date: 1958-07-23
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, July 22nd. Prominent female sprinter Jiang Yumin shortened the national women's 100m record to 11.9 seconds on the 20th in Tianjin. The original national record for this event was 12.1 seconds, set by Jiang Yumin in Shanghai in May 1955.

Jiang Yumin achieved this result in an exhibition match on the same day as Liang Jianxun, during the first sports meeting of Hexi District in Tianjin. This record is currently the best in Asia.

The current world record for the women's 100m is 11.3 seconds.

### 姜玉民创女子百公尺新纪录

1958-07-23
第7版()
专栏：体育新闻

    姜玉民创女子百公尺新纪录
    新华社天津22日电  著名女子短跑选手姜玉民，20日在天津把女子百公尺全国纪录缩短到十一秒九。这个项目原来的全国纪录十二秒一，是1955年5月姜玉民在上海创造的。
    姜玉民是和梁建勋同一天参加天津市河西区首届体育运动会的表演赛中创造这个成绩的。这个纪录在目前亚洲各国中还是第一。
    现在女子百公尺世界纪录是十一秒三。
