Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Cambodian Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-07-06
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Vice Premier Chen Yi received this afternoon the Cambodian Journalists Delegation headed by Chen Wenchang and all members of the delegation.

    Those present at the reception were Mei Yi, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, and Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

    Yang An, head of the Cambodian Economic Delegation to Beijing, was also present at the reception.

### 陈毅副总理接见柬埔寨新闻工作者代表团

1958-07-06
第2版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见柬埔寨新闻工作者代表团
    新华社5日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了柬埔寨新闻工作者代表团团长陈文昌和代表团全体团员。
    接见时在座的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长梅益，外交部新闻司副司长徐晃等人。
    接见时，柬埔寨驻北京经济代表团团长杨安也在座。
