Title: Ulbricht Meets with Dong Biwu; Khrushchev Leads Delegation to Berlin
Author:
Date: 1958-07-09
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 8th: The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the German Socialist Unity Party, Ulbricht, met with Dong Biwu, head of the Chinese Communist Party delegation, and all members of the delegation at the residence of the Chinese Communist Party delegation in the Presidential Palace of the German Democratic Republic on the evening of the 7th. The delegation is in Berlin to attend the Fifth Congress of the Socialist Unity Party, and they held cordial talks for two and a half hours.
    
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 8th: The delegation of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, invited to attend the Fifth Congress of the German Socialist Unity Party, arrived in Berlin by special train at 11:00 a.m. today, led by the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev.
    
    The CPSU delegation was warmly welcomed upon arrival at Berlin's East Railway Station by the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the German Socialist Unity Party, Ulbricht, Politburo member Grotewohl, other party and government leaders, and tens of thousands of working people of Berlin. The First Secretary of the German Communist Party, Lehmann, also came to the station to welcome them. The Chinese Communist Party delegation, headed by Dong Biwu, and other Communist and Workers' Party delegations that had already arrived in Berlin also came to the station to greet them.

### 乌布利希同董必武会晤  赫鲁晓夫率代表团到柏林

1958-07-09
第5版()
专栏：

    乌布利希同董必武会晤
    赫鲁晓夫率代表团到柏林
    新华社柏林8日电  德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希在7日晚间到德意志民主共和国总统府中国共产党代表团住所同前来参加统一社会党第五次代表大会的中国共产党代表团团长董必武和代表团全体团员会晤，并且进行了两个半小时的亲切谈话。
    据新华社柏林8日电  应邀参加德国统一社会党第五次代表大会的苏联共产党代表团今天上午十一时在苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫的率领下乘专车到达柏林。
    苏共代表团到达柏林东车站时受到了德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希、政治局委员格罗提渥以及党和政府的其他领导人和柏林市数万劳动人民的热烈欢迎。德国共产党第一书记雷曼也到车站欢迎。已经到达柏林的以董必武为首的中国共产党代表团和其他国家共产党和工人党代表团也到车站迎接。
