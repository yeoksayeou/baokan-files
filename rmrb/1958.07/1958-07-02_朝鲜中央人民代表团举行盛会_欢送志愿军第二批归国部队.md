Title: Korean Central People's Delegation Holds Grand Meeting to See Off the Second Group of Chinese People's Volunteer Army Troops Returning Home
Author:
Date: 1958-07-02
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 1st. A grand meeting was held this afternoon in a certain location in Korea by the Korean Central People's Delegation, headed by Korean Vice Premier Kim Il, to warmly see off the vanguard of the second group of Chinese People's Volunteer Army troops withdrawing from Korea.

When Vice Premier Kim Il and his delegation arrived, they were greeted by more than two thousand officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army who lined the streets in the rain. General Ding Laifu, the representative of the Volunteer Army Headquarters who had traveled from afar to welcome the delegation, and General Wang Yi, the leader of the vanguard of the second group of troops withdrawing from Korea, accompanied Vice Premier Kim Il in reviewing the guard of honor.

The farewell meeting began with the solemn playing of the national anthems of Korea and China. Vice Premier Kim Il delivered a farewell speech to the officers and soldiers. On behalf of the entire Korean people, the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, and the Republic's government, he extended warm gratitude to the vanguard of the second group of Chinese People's Volunteer Army troops withdrawing from Korea. He praised the great achievements of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army in Korea. He said: You are about to leave Korea, but our love, respect, and friendship for you will forever be etched in our hearts. Vice Premier Kim Il pointed out: The Korean people resolutely oppose the occupation of Korea by American imperialism and strongly demand that they withdraw from South Korea.

Subsequently, Vice Premier Kim Il, on behalf of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Republic's Cabinet, and the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, and Lieutenant General Kim Bong-yul, Vice Minister of National Defense, on behalf of all officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army, and Hong Zeng-sik, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Democratic Front for the Reunification of the Fatherland, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Fatherland Front, presented banners to the officers and soldiers of the vanguard of the second group of troops withdrawing from Korea. Vice Premier Kim Il, on behalf of Premier Kim Il-sung, presented gifts to the leaders of the vanguard troops and presented Sino-Korean Friendship commemorative badges to all officers and soldiers.

After receiving the banners and gifts, General Wang Yi, on behalf of all the officers and soldiers, expressed heartfelt gratitude and high respect to the delegation. He said: After returning to our country, we will certainly convey the deep friendship of the Korean people to the people of our motherland, so that the brilliant flower of Sino-Korean friendship will bear even more abundant fruits.

After the farewell meeting, the Korean People's Army Ensemble, which accompanied the delegation, gave a farewell performance.

The leading organs of the vanguard of the second group of Chinese People's Volunteer Army troops withdrawing from Korea held a grand banquet in the evening to warmly welcome Vice Premier Kim Il and all the distinguished guests.

### 朝鲜中央人民代表团举行盛会  欢送志愿军第二批归国部队

1958-07-02
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜中央人民代表团举行盛会
    欢送志愿军第二批归国部队
    新华社平壤1日电　以朝鲜内阁副首相金一为首的朝鲜中央人民代表团，今天下午在朝鲜某地举行大会热烈欢送中国人民志愿军第二批撤离朝鲜部队的先头部队。
    金一副首相和他率领的代表团到达时，受到志愿军两千多名官兵冒雨夹道欢迎。远道赶来欢迎代表团的志愿军总部代表丁莱夫将军和第二批撤离朝鲜部队先头部队首长汪易将军，陪同金一副首相检阅了仪仗队。
    欢送大会在高奏朝中两国国歌声中开始。金一副首相向官兵们致欢送词。他代表全体朝鲜人民，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会和共和国政府，向中国人民志愿军第二批撤离朝鲜部队先头部队致热烈的感谢。他盛赞中国人民志愿军在朝鲜所建立的丰功伟绩。他说：你们就要离开朝鲜了，但是我们对你们的热爱、尊敬和友情，将永远铭记在心里。金一副首相指出：朝鲜人民坚决反对美帝国主义对朝鲜的强占，并强硬地要求他们撤出南朝鲜。
    接着，金一副首相代表朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会、共和国内阁、朝鲜劳动党中央委员会，民族保卫省副相金奉律中将代表朝鲜人民军全体官兵，朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会书记局局长洪增植代表祖国战线中央委员会，向第二批撤离朝鲜部队先头部队官兵赠旗。金一副首相代表金日成首相向先头部队首长赠礼，向全体官兵赠朝中友谊纪念章。
    汪易将军代表全体官兵受旗受礼后，向代表团表示衷心的感谢和崇高的敬意。他说：我们回国以后，一定把朝鲜人民的深情厚谊转达给祖国人民，让灿烂的中朝友谊之花结出更丰盛的果实。
    欢送大会结束后，随同代表团前来的朝鲜人民军协奏团举行了送别演出。
    志愿军第二批撤离朝鲜部队先头部队领导机关晚间举行了盛大宴会，欢宴以金一副首相为首的全体贵宾。
