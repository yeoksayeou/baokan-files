Title: Six Hundred Million People Cannot Stand Idly By Against the US and UK's War Provocations; People Everywhere Support Arab Brothers in Resisting Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1958-07-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, July 19th: One million people in Shanghai continued to hold demonstrations and rallies today under the scorching sun, and the people's anger towards the armed aggression of the Middle East by US and British imperialism has reached a new peak.

    The demonstrating masses in the city converged on the famous Nanjing Road into an iron torrent. Thirty years ago, the people of Shanghai shed blood here to oppose imperialist colonial oppression. Today, the people who have stood up are raising their fists, shouting slogans, singing songs against wars of aggression, and passing through here with their heads held high.

    Before the demonstrations, all democratic parties, factories, agricultural cooperatives, schools, temples, churches, and neighborhoods held meetings to condemn the armed aggression of the Middle East by US and British imperialism. Qian Zongxiang and Du Xialing, employees of the Shanghai Tobacco Industry Company, used to work for the British Ewo Tobacco Company. They said that when Shanghai was liberated, they saw the imperialists fleeing Shanghai with their tails between their legs. Now the imperialists are stirring up trouble in the Middle East, and they will certainly not escape the same fate.

    Shanghai's literary and art circles used their weapons to participate in activities to support the just struggle of the Arab people. The skit "Three Reports of Mourning" compiled by the Shanghai Opera House exposed the corrupt nature of US and British imperialism and predicted their doom.

    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, July 19th: More than 200,000 people in the cities of Baoding, Tangshan, Shijiazhuang, Zhangjiakou, Handan, Qinhuangdao, and Cangxian in Hebei Province held separate rallies yesterday to protest the crimes of US and British imperialism in armed aggression against Lebanon and Jordan.

    On this day, the vast number of workers, students, and citizens of various cities' institutions, factories, enterprises, and schools, holding high red flags and banners, excitedly shouted slogans and poured into the rally venues. The workers of the Kailuan Coal Mine in Tangshan City had just come up from the mine yesterday afternoon and participated in the rally. Li Yanmin, a representative of the Tangshan Steel Plant workers, said at the mass rally: The Chinese people will never allow US and British imperialism to run rampant, and Chinese workers will definitely fully support the just struggle of the Arab people against aggression. At the mass rally of more than 40,000 people held in Baoding City, Ma Zhenchang, director of the Nandayuan Agricultural Cooperative, issued a solemn warning to US and British imperialism on behalf of all 2,000 members of the cooperative. Zhangjiakou City held a radio rally against the armed aggression of US and British imperialism. More than 100,000 people listened to the broadcast, and the broadcast rally received 547 protest letters from the masses.

    Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, July 19th: Today, farmers in the vast majority of villages in Heilongjiang Province held rallies and demonstrations to oppose the US and British armed aggression against Lebanon and Jordan. From the densely populated towns and villages in the Songhua River basin to the sparsely populated villages in Fuyuan and Raohe counties on the northeastern corner of the border, the villages and intersections were filled with angry shouts of "Americans get out of Lebanon" and "British get out of Jordan." Walls, trees, and blackboard newspapers were plastered with solemn statements supporting our government and the Soviet government, and slogans fully supporting the just struggle of the people of Arab countries to win national independence. Forty to fifty thousand farmers in Chaoyang District in the suburbs of Harbin City held rallies and demonstrations.

    While farmers throughout Heilongjiang Province held rallies and demonstrations against US and British aggression in the Middle East, the activities of the people of Harbin, Qiqihar, Jiamusi, Mudanjiang, and Yichun and other eight cities to protest US and British aggression were more extensive and in-depth. Many professional and amateur art troupes launched extensive publicity activities at the venues of citizen rallies. Small concerts, skits, and street lectures spread throughout the streets and alleys. The Harbin Song and Dance Troupe and the Central Orchestra, which is touring Harbin City, have created dozens of songs such as "Americans Get Out of the Middle East" in recent days, which were broadcast on the radio and performed on the streets.

    Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, July 18th: People of all ethnic groups in Nanning, the capital of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, and other cities such as Guilin, Liuzhou, and Wuzhou, have held rallies and demonstrations in the past few days to oppose the aggressive actions of US and British imperialism in the Middle East. More than 60,000 people in Nanning City held a rally and demonstration today. Representatives from workers, farmers, women, the scientific community, students, democratic parties, and industry and commerce all spoke at the meeting, opposing the evil actions of US and British imperialism in carrying out armed aggression against the Middle East. Yan Guangcai, Second Secretary of the Nanning Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Mayor of Nanning, severely condemned and protested at the meeting against US and British imperialism's rude violation of the principles of the United Nations Charter and their dispatch of troops to invade Lebanon and Jordan. Representatives of Muslims in Nanning City also spoke at the meeting, protesting against US and British aggression.

    More than 40,000 people held a protest rally in Guilin City yesterday.

    Xinhua News Agency, Sichuan, July 18th: More than 100,000 people from all walks of life in Sichuan Province and Chengdu City held a conference today to support the national independence movement in the Republic of Iraq and Lebanon, and to protest against the aggression of US and British imperialism against Lebanon and Jordan.

    Li Dazhang, Secretary of the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Governor of Sichuan Province, said at the meeting that the aggressive plans of US and British imperialists are not only aimed at Lebanon and Iraq, but also at the people of the entire Middle East. We, the people of Sichuan Province, together with the people of the whole country and peace-loving people all over the world, firmly support the just struggle of the people of Iraq, Lebanon, and Jordan against imperialism and for national independence and freedom, and stop the aggressive actions of US and British imperialists.

    Then Li Zonglin, Mayor of Chengdu City, and representatives of workers, farmers, students, democratic parties, mass organizations, and Muslims all spoke at the meeting. They unanimously expressed their firm support for the just struggle of the people of Iraq and Lebanon, and drove the aggressors out of Lebanon and Jordan.

    Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, July 18th: The mass movement of the people of Taiyuan City protesting against the armed aggression of US and British imperialism against Lebanon and Jordan expanded today from 100,000 people yesterday to 300,000 people.

    From morning to night today, agencies, organizations, schools, and street residents throughout the city held separate rallies and demonstrations to support the just struggle of Iraq and Lebanon. This afternoon, more than 40,000 people held a mass rally in Xinghualing Square. Li Qi, First Secretary of the Taiyuan Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the Taiyuan Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, pointed out at the meeting that the aggressive actions of the United States and Britain in the Middle East are doomed to failure, and victory belongs to the people of peace. He called on everyone to take active action and strengthen our strength with practical production leap forward actions to support the Republic of Iraq and support the national independence movement in Lebanon. The masses who participated in this rally held demonstrations in the streets before and after the rally.

    In the past two days, farmers in many townships in the suburbs of Taiyuan City have held rallies and demonstrations by township or cooperative. They proposed to ensure the realization of the plan for the entire suburban area to produce 1,000 jin of grain and 50,000 jin of vegetables per mu this year, in order to strengthen the strength of defending world peace.

    Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, July 18th: At 6:00 p.m. today, more than 60,000 people from all walks of life in Zhengzhou City gathered to denounce the crimes of US and British imperialism in the Middle East. Li Ting, Secretary of the Zhengzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, said at the meeting angrily: The US imperialist aggression against Lebanon and the British imperialist dispatch of troops to Jordan are not only a threat to the people of Arab countries, but also a serious threat to world peace. If US and British imperialism do not immediately stop this war provocation and ignore the warnings of the people of the world, they will surely be battered and bloodied. Su Baiquan, Imam of the Hui ethnic group in Zhengzhou City, said at the meeting: With anger, we denounce the atrocities of US imperialism in invading Lebanon. More than 20,000 Muslims in Zhengzhou City, together with the people of the whole country, fully support the heroic struggle of the Arab people for independence and freedom.

    After the meeting, the masses held a demonstration with thousands of flags and banners, and the slogans "US troops get out of Lebanon" and "British imperialist troops get out of Jordan" rose one after another.

    Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, July 18th: 180,000 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Lanzhou City, including Hui, Han, Mongolian, and Tibetan, held mass rallies today in the industrial zone, cultural and educational zone, coal mining zone, and Chengguan district, protesting against the armed aggression of US and British imperialism against Lebanon and Jordan. The masses who participated in the rallies also held demonstrations separately.

    All kinds of slogans were posted all over Lanzhou City today. At the same time, Gansu Daily and Gansu People's Broadcasting Station received a large number of protest letters and resolutions to defend peace from the masses of citizens protesting against the armed aggression of US and British imperialism against Lebanon and Jordan.

    Xinhua News Agency, Xining, July 18th: Today, more than 40,000 people of all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Xining City, Qinghai Province, held a parade and demonstration to support the just struggle of the Arab people and oppose the armed aggression of US and British imperialism. Many schools that are currently conducting semester exams, teachers and students decided to postpone the exams for one day to attend the conference. Farmers from five nearby rural townships also excitedly rushed to participate. At the venue, angry shouts rose and fell, deafening.

    At the meeting, Ye Xipeng, Secretary of the Xining Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Mayor of Xining City, and representatives of workers, farmers, students, industry and commerce, and religious circles, all angrily condemned the wolfish behavior of US and British imperialism in invading Lebanon and Jordan in their speeches, and warmly celebrated the founding of the Republic of Iraq. They pointed out that the despicable and barbaric behavior of US and British imperialism is a destruction of world peace and a provocation to the people of the world, and will inevitably suffer the same shameful fate as in Korea and the Suez Canal.

    After the meeting unanimously passed a protest letter against the US and British imperialist aggression against Lebanon and Jordan, a parade and demonstration began.

    Yesterday, when the Qinghai Daily published the news that the United States had sent troops to land in Lebanon, many agencies, factories, and schools immediately held rallies that day to express their strong protests.

    Xinhua News Agency, Yinchuan, July 19th: 1.8 million people of Hui and Han nationalities in the Xia Ning area expressed unparalleled anger and strong protests against the armed aggression of US and British imperialism in the Middle East. More than 30,000 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Yinchuan City held a protest rally and demonstration today.

    Starting early this morning, tens of thousands of Hui and Han workers, farmers, students, cadres, and residents gathered towards the South Gate Square, holding high red flags and banners. Even Hui old ladies in their seventies and eighties who rarely go out, young women, and imams and mullahs from twenty-one mosque districts came to participate in the meeting.

    Gan Chunlei (Hui nationality), Secretary of the Ningxia Working Committee of the Communist Party of China, pointed out at the meeting that the aggressive actions of US and British imperialism are threatening the peace of the Middle East and the world. But today, the forces defending peace are unprecedentedly strong. If US and British imperialism do not withdraw their invading troops from the Middle East, they will surely suffer a shameful end. Ma Zhendong (Hui nationality), Imam of the East Great Mosque and member of the China Islamic Association, Jing Bohing (Manchu nationality), Vice Chairman of the Yinchuan Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and others all unanimously demanded in their speeches that US and British imperialism immediately get out of the Middle East!

    In the demonstration procession, Jin Fengshan (Hui nationality), the more than seventy-year-old Imam of the South Gate Great Mosque and member of the China Islamic Association, stood at the forefront of the religious community, shouting "Americans get out of Lebanon." Hui woman Ma Xiuying participated in the demonstration with her child in her arms. She said: "In order to protect the children and ourselves women, I must firmly stand with the Arab women and oppose the aggression of US imperialism."

    Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, July 19th: More than 30,000 people from all walks of life in Shandong Province and Jinan City held a conference at 6:30 yesterday afternoon, representing more than 50 million people in the province to support the national liberation struggle of the Arab people and angrily protest against the armed aggression of US and British imperialism against Lebanon and Jordan.

    Tan Qilong, Secretary of the Shandong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the Shandong Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, said at the meeting that the US imperialist armed aggression against Lebanon and the British imperialist armed aggression against Jordan fully exposed the ferocious wolfish nature of the imperialists. He said that the establishment of the Republic of Iraq caused imperialism to lose a pillar for invading the people of Asia and Africa. This event of great significance greatly encouraged the liberation struggle of the oppressed nations of the world and the world peace movement. The Chinese people will definitely fully support the just struggle of Iraq and Lebanon.

    Many representatives from all walks of life also spoke at the meeting. Mi Changmao, Deputy Director of the Jinan Municipal Ethnic Affairs Committee and Imam of the Qingzhen Beida Mosque, said at the meeting that the Muslims in Shandong Province, like the people of the whole province, will definitely support the Republic of Iraq and support the just struggle of Lebanon to win national independence, and resolutely oppose the armed aggression of US and British imperialism. A demonstration was held after the meeting.

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, July 19th: The fire of anger against the armed aggression of US and British imperialism against Lebanon and Jordan has spread throughout Guangzhou.

    Yesterday, people from all walks of life in the city continued to hold rallies and parades. Many students and workers used blackboard newspapers, big-character posters, cartoons, and other forms to launch extensive publicity activities. More than 25,000 workers in 167 factories in the West District participated in the temporarily formed newspaper reading groups, reading and talking, each filled with righteous indignation. The residents on the Pearl River, who had suffered deeply from imperialist aggression, indignantly condemned the aggressive crimes of US and British imperialism. The entire staff of the Provincial People's Hospital could not contain their soaring anger and wrote 750 big-character posters protesting the armed aggression of US and British imperialism. The literary and art circles in Guangzhou created many poems praising the just struggle of the people of Iraq and Lebanon and opposing imperialist armed aggression. Many poems have been set to music, printed into poetry pamphlets, and spread to every corner of the city.

    Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, July 18th: 80,000 people from various factories, agencies, schools, people's organizations, democratic parties in Nanchang city as well as farmers from the suburbs, held a meeting this afternoon, supporting the uprising of the Lebanese people and the Iraqi revolutionary government.

    At the meeting, Guo Guangzhou, First Secretary of the Nanchang Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the Nanchang Municipal Branch of the China Peace Committee, spoke. He said that the US imperialism brazenly carried out armed intervention in Lebanon, but it cannot scare the people striving for freedom and independence, and cannot stop the progress of the wheel of history. We Chinese people and peace-loving people all over the world firmly stand with the Arab people, and victory will surely belong to the just Arab people. Representatives of workers in Nanchang City, local organizations of democratic parties in Jiangxi Province, and representatives of people's organizations all spoke at the meeting. They angrily warned British and American imperialism to immediately stop the armed aggression, otherwise they will surely suffer the consequences. Some members of the youth delegation from black Africa who had just come to Nanchang from Shanghai for a visit also participated in the meeting, and delegation member Woguo also spoke at the meeting. The meeting unanimously passed a protest letter against the US imperialist armed aggression against Lebanon.

    After the meeting, 80,000 people held a demonstration.

    Last night, the people of Nanchang City held mass rallies and forums separately to warmly celebrate the victory of the Iraqi people's revolution.

    Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, July 19th: 30,000 people in Changsha City gathered yesterday afternoon to oppose the armed aggression of US and British imperialism against Lebanon and Jordan, and held a demonstration after the meeting.

    Tang Lin, member Standing Committee of the Hunan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Chairman of the Hunan Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, pointed out at the protest rally that the US aggressive ambition is aimed at the entire Middle East people, and is a conspiracy to strangle the national independence movement in Arab and the Orient, attempting to expand the colonial rule of US imperialism in Asia and Africa. He called on all forces opposing wars of aggression to unite, stop the aggression of US and British imperialism, and called on the people of the entire province to work hard in their respective positions and strive to comprehensively and overfulfill the state plan, in order to enhance the strength of the motherland to defend peace. The conference also passed a statement by the people of all walks of life in Changsha against the US and British imperialist armed aggression against Lebanon and Jordan and supporting the Republic of Iraq and the people of Lebanon.

    On the 17th, more than 60,000 people in Changsha City held various activities to protest against the aggression of US and British imperialism in the Middle East. Workers, members of handicraft cooperatives, and more than 10,000 farmers and students, literary and art workers, and democratic party organizations in the suburbs held protest rallies, and some also held demonstrations. ### 六亿人民对美英的战争挑衅不能袖手旁观  各地人民支持阿拉伯弟兄反抗侵略者

1958-07-20
第3版()
专栏：

    六亿人民对美英的战争挑衅不能袖手旁观
    各地人民支持阿拉伯弟兄反抗侵略者
    上海
    据新华社上海19日电　上海市今天有一百万人冒着烈日继续举行示威游行和集会，人民对美、英帝国主义武装侵略中东的愤怒达到了新的高峰。
    全市的示威群众在著名的南京路上汇成了一条铁的巨流。三十年前，上海人民为了反对帝国主义的殖民压迫，曾经在这里流过鲜血。今天站起来了的人民高举拳头、喊着口号，唱着反对侵略战争的歌曲，昂首阔步地从这里通过。
    在示威游行之前，各民主党派、各工厂、农业社、学校、寺院、教堂、里弄都举行了谴责美、英帝国主义武装侵略中东的集会。上海烟草工业公司的职工钱宗祥、杜遐龄过去在英国颐中烟草公司工作，他们说，上海解放时看到帝国主义灰溜溜地夹着尾巴从上海逃跑，现在帝国主义又在中东蠢动，它们也一定逃不了同样的命运。
    上海文艺界运用自己的武器参加了支持阿拉伯人民正义斗争的活动。上海歌剧院编的活报剧“三报丧”揭露了美英帝国主义的腐朽本质，预示了它们复灭的命运。
    河北
    新华社天津19日电　河北省保定、唐山、石家庄、张家口、邯郸、秦皇岛、沧县等城市，昨天共有二十多万人分别集会，抗议美英帝国主义武装侵略黎巴嫩和约旦的罪行。
    这一天，各城市的机关、工厂、企业、学校的广大职工、学生和市民，高举红旗、标语牌，激动地喊着口号，涌向大会会场。唐山市开滦煤矿的工人昨天下午刚刚从矿井下上来，便参加了大会。唐山钢厂工人代表李彦民在群众大会上说：中国人民绝不允许美英帝国主义横行霸道，中国工人一定全力支持阿拉伯人民反侵略的正义斗争。在保定市举行的四万多人群众大会上，南大园农业合作社主任马振昌，代表全社两千多社员，对美英帝国主义的侵略行为提出了严正的警告。张家口市举行了反对美英帝国主义武装侵略的广播大会，有十万多人收听广播，广播大会收到了群众五百四十七件抗议书。
    黑龙江
    新华社哈尔滨19日电　今天，黑龙江省绝大部分乡村的农民都集会游行，反对美国和英国武装侵略黎巴嫩和约旦。从松花江流域各县人口密集的乡镇到国境东北角上人烟稀少的抚远、饶河等县的乡村，村头道口到处是愤慨的高喊“美国滚出黎巴嫩去”、“英国滚出约旦去”的声音。墙头上、大树上、黑板报上到处张贴着拥护我国政府和苏联政府的庄严声明、全力支持阿拉伯各国人民争取民族独立的正义斗争的标语。哈尔滨市郊朝阳区四五万农民举行了集会游行。
    在黑龙江全省农民集会游行反对美国和英国侵略中东的同时，哈尔滨、齐齐哈尔、佳木斯、牡丹江和伊春等八个城市人民抗议美英侵略的活动更加广泛和深入。许多专业和业余文艺团体在市民集会的场所展开了广泛的宣传活动，小演唱、活报剧、街头讲演遍及大街小巷，哈尔滨歌舞团和正在哈尔滨市巡回演出的中央乐团连日创作了“美国滚出中东去”等几十支歌曲，到电台广播、到街头演唱。
    广西
    新华社南宁18日电　广西僮族自治区的首府南宁及桂林、柳州、梧州等城市的各族人民，这几天分别举行集会游行，反对美、英帝国主义在中东的侵略行为。南宁市今天有六万多人举行大会和示威游行。工人、农民、妇女、科学界、学生、民主党派和工商界的代表都在会上发言，反对美、英帝国主义对中东进行武装侵略的罪恶行动。中共南宁市委第二书记、市长阎光彩在会上严厉谴责和抗议美、英帝国主义粗暴地破坏联合国宪章的原则，出兵侵略黎巴嫩、约旦。南宁市穆斯林的代表也在会上讲话，抗议美英侵略行为。
    桂林市昨天有四万多人举行了抗议集会。
    四川
    新华社四川18日电　四川省和成都市各界人民十万多人今天开大会，支持伊拉克共和国和黎巴嫩的民族独立运动，抗议美英帝国主义侵略黎巴嫩和约旦。
    中共四川省委书记、四川省省长李大章在会上说，美英帝国主义者的侵略计划不仅是针对着黎巴嫩和伊拉克，而且也针对着整个中东的人民。我们四川全省人民和全国人民以及全世界爱好和平的人民一道，坚决支持伊拉克、黎巴嫩和约旦人民反对帝国主义争取民族独立和自由的正义斗争，制止美英帝国主义者的侵略行动。
    接着成都市市长李宗林，工人、农民、学生代表、各民主党派、各群众团体代表和伊斯兰教徒代表等都在会上讲了话，他们一致表示坚决支持伊拉克、黎巴嫩人民的正义斗争，把侵略者赶出黎巴嫩和约旦去。
    太原
    新华社太原18日电　太原市人民抗议美、英帝国主义武装侵略黎巴嫩、约旦的群众运动今天由昨天的十万人扩大到了三十万人。
    全市的机关、团体、学校和街道居民今天从早到晚陆续分别举行了支持伊拉克、黎巴嫩正义斗争的集会和示威游行。今天下午，有四万多人在杏花岭广场举行了群众集会。中共太原市委第一书记、政协太原市委员会主席李琦在会上指出，美国和英国在中东的侵略行动是注定要失败的，胜利属于和平的人民，他号召大家积极行动起来，用实际的生产跃进行动壮大我们的力量，支持伊拉克共和国，支持黎巴嫩的民族独立运动。参加这次集会的群众在集会前后在街上举行了游行示威。
    两天来，太原市郊区许多乡的农民以乡或社为单位举行了集会和游行。他们提出要保证实现全郊区今年每亩产粮一千斤和蔬菜五万斤的计划，以加强保卫世界和平的力量。
    郑州

    西宁

    银川
    新华社银川19日电　夏宁地区一百八十多万回、汉族人民对美英帝国主义武装侵略中东表示无比的愤怒和强烈的抗议。银川市各族各界今天有三万多人举行了抗议大会和示威游行。
    从今天早晨起，成千上万的回、汉族工人、农民、学生、干部和居民，就高举红旗和标语牌向南门广场集中。连平时很少出门的七、八十岁的回族老太太和年轻妇女，以及二十一个清真寺坊的阿訇、满拉也来参加了大会。
