Title: Soviet-Iranian Exchange of Notes on Ratification of Border Memorandum

Author: 
Date: 1958-07-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 7th, Moscow Dispatch from TASS: The Soviet Embassy in Iran and the Iranian Ministry of Foreign Affairs exchanged notes in Tehran on July 3, 1958, regarding the ratification of the memorandum on the demarcation and re-demarcation of the Soviet-Iranian border. The memorandum was formulated in accordance with the Soviet-Iranian agreement of December 2, 1954, on resolving border and financial issues.

### 就双方批准边界备忘录  苏伊两国互换照会

1958-07-08
第5版()
专栏：

    就双方批准边界备忘录
    苏伊两国互换照会
    新华社7日讯  塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联驻伊朗大使馆和伊朗王国外交部1958年7月3日在德黑兰就双方批准划定和重新划定苏联和伊朗边界备忘录互换了照会。备忘录是根据苏联和伊朗1954年12月2日签订的关于解决边界和财政问题的协定制定的。
