Title: Iraqi Kurds Support Provisional Constitution
Author:
Date: 1958-07-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 28th: Iraq Radio announced on the 27th that several delegations of the Iraqi Kurdish people arrived in Baghdad on the 27th to congratulate the Supreme Authority Committee and the Cabinet on the promulgation of the Iraqi Republic's provisional constitution. The provisional constitution grants Kurdish citizens equal rights.

At the same time, news from Baghdad said that the Iraqi people are continuously donating funds to strengthen their army.

### 伊拉克库尔德族拥护临时宪法

1958-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    伊拉克库尔德族拥护临时宪法
    据新华社开罗28日电　伊拉克共和国电台27日宣布，伊拉克的库尔德族的几个代表团在27日到巴格达，向最高权力委员会和内阁祝贺伊拉克共和国临时宪法的颁布。临时宪法赋予库尔德族公民以平等权利。
    同时，来自巴格达的消息说，伊拉克人民源源不断地捐款项，用来加强自己的军队。
