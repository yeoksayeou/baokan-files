Title: "Swiss Newspaper" Comments: Baghdad Pact is Finished

Author:

Date: 1958-07-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 27th. The "Swiss Newspaper" editorial on the 26th, commenting on the Baghdad Pact Council's London meeting, pointed out that the Western imperialist faithful lackey, former Iraqi Prime Minister "Nuri al-Said is dead, what value does the Baghdad Pact still have?" The editorial said, "Whether as a supplement to the North Atlantic Alliance or as a factor influencing the Arab world, (the Baghdad Pact) is finished."

### “瑞士报”的评论说  巴格达条约完蛋了

1958-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    “瑞士报”的评论说
    巴格达条约完蛋了
    新华社日内瓦27日电　“瑞士报”26日社论在评论巴格达条约理事会伦敦会议时指出，西方帝国主义忠实走狗，伊拉克前首相“努里·赛义德已经死了，巴格达条约还有什么价值呢？”社论说，“无论作为北大西洋联盟的补充来说，还是作为对阿拉伯世界起影响的因素来说，（巴格达条约）都完蛋了。”
