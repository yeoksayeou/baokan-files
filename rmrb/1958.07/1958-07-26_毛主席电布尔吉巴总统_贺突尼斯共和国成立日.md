Title: Chairman Mao Cables President Bourguiba to Congratulate the Founding Day of the Republic of Tunisia
Author:
Date: 1958-07-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 25th. Chairman Mao Zedong today sent a telegram to Habib Bourguiba, President of the Republic of Tunisia, congratulating him on the founding day of the Republic of Tunisia. The telegram is as follows:

To His Excellency Habib Bourguiba, President of the Republic of Tunisia:

On the occasion of the founding day of the Republic of Tunisia, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the Tunisian people on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish the Tunisian people new victories in the struggle to safeguard and consolidate national independence. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness. May the friendly relations between China and Tunisia grow day by day.

                            Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                  July 25, 1958, in Beijing

### 毛主席电布尔吉巴总统  贺突尼斯共和国成立日

1958-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电布尔吉巴总统
    贺突尼斯共和国成立日
    新华社25日讯　毛泽东主席今天致电突尼斯共和国总统哈比布·布尔吉巴，祝贺突尼斯共和国成立日。电文如下：突尼斯共和国总统哈比布·布尔吉巴阁下：
    值此突尼斯共和国成立日之际，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义，向阁下和突尼斯人民表示热诚的祝贺。祝突尼斯人民在维护和巩固民族独立的斗争中取得新的胜利。祝贵国繁荣、人民幸福。祝中、突两国友好关系日益发展。
                            中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                                  1958年7月25日于北京
