Title: Austrian Chancellor Welcomes Summit in Vienna
Author:
Date: 1958-07-31
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, July 30th: Austrian Chancellor Raab said at a press conference held in Vienna on the 29th that the Austrian government would be very honored if Vienna were selected as the venue for a future summit meeting. Vienna has been mentioned as one of the possible meeting locations in the Soviet Union's most recent proposal regarding a summit meeting.

The Chancellor also expressed satisfaction with the results of his talks with Soviet leaders during his visit to Moscow.

### 奥地利总理欢迎在维也纳开首脑会

1958-07-31
第2版()
专栏：

    奥地利总理欢迎在维也纳开首脑会
    新华社30日讯  维也纳消息：奥地利总理拉布29日在维也纳举行的记者招待会上说，如果维也纳被选作未来的最高级会议的开会地点，奥地利政府将感到非常荣幸。在苏联最近一次关于最高级会议的建议中曾经提到维也纳作为可能的会议地点之一。
    这位总理还对他在访问莫斯科期间同苏联领导人会谈结果表示满意。
