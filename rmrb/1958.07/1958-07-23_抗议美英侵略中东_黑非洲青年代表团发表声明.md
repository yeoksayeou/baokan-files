Title: Protesting US-UK Aggression in the Middle East: Statement by the Black African Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-07-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 22nd. The Black African Youth Delegation, currently visiting Shanghai, issued a statement condemning the bandit-like behavior of US-UK imperialism in invading Lebanon and Jordan.

The statement said that this brutal and savage crime has aroused the anger of all freedom-loving people. In the name of the Black African Youth Delegation, we resolutely lodge a stern protest against the aggression of the imperialist countries. Our entire delegation participated in the demonstration held by the people of Shanghai against the US-UK imperialist aggression against Lebanon and Jordan. We firmly believe that the power of all peace-loving people will defeat the dying imperialism's attempt to restore colonial rule.

### 抗议美英侵略中东  黑非洲青年代表团发表声明

1958-07-23
第2版()
专栏：

    抗议美英侵略中东
    黑非洲青年代表团发表声明
    新华社上海22日电  正在上海参观访问的黑非洲青年代表团发表声明，谴责美英帝国主义侵略黎巴嫩和约旦的强盗行为。
    声明说，这一残暴野蛮的罪行，激起了所有爱好自由人民的愤怒。我们以黑非洲青年代表团的名义，坚决提出严正抗议，反对帝国主义国家的侵略。我们全团参加了上海人民举行的反对美英帝国主义侵略黎巴嫩和约旦的示威大会。我们坚信，所有爱好和平人民的力量，必能击败垂死帝国主义的恢复殖民统治的企图。
