    Poetry Handbill

    Easy to enter, hard to exit
    U.S. imperialism meddles in the Mediterranean,
    Easy to enter, hard to exit,
    Fall into the sea and become fish food,
    The world's people clap their hands.
      (Wall poster poem from Shijingshan Power Plant)

  Reprimand Hussein
    King Hussein of Jordan,
    Loses power, humiliates the nation, and loses self-respect,
    Fearing national independence movements,
    Shamelessly invites American troops.
    Hussein, oh Hussein,
    Shares the same fate as Faisal,
    Think about him and look at you,
    Dare I ask, is the future worrying?!
    (Wu Zhongyu, Beijing Jianguo Steel Rolling Plant)

    Eisenhower Doctrine
    The Eisenhower Doctrine,
    Stumbles and hits walls again:
    No one willingly offers oil,
    No one wants to be a "base" anymore!
    The Eisenhower Doctrine,
    Stumbles and hits walls again:
    Nixon just got hit by stones,
    And the banner of liberation is raised in Baghdad!
    (Chao Zhenying, Tianjin Haihe Sluice Construction Site)

  Give Us Guns
      [Reported: Iraqi women
        request military training]
    Give us guns!
    Gentle mothers become brave hunters.
    Serving the vicious wolves that come to the door,
    Give them a shot!
          (Luo Zuhui, Peking University)

        Fire
    The sky is fire, the ground is fire;
    The eyes are fire, the heart is fire.
    A world of fire, a sea of fire.
    Shining on the ranks of Lebanese insurgents,
    Shining on the birth of the Iraqi Republic.
        (Liu Dasen, Yunnan University)
