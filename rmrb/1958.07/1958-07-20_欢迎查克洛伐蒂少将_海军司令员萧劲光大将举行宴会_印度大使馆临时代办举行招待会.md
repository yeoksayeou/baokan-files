Title: Welcome Major General Chakravarti. Navy Commander-in-Chief Xiao Jingguang Holds Banquet. Indian Embassy Chargé d'Affaires Holds Reception.
Author: 
Date: 1958-07-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 19th: Navy Commander-in-Chief Xiao Jingguang held a banquet at the Beijing Hotel at 8:00 PM tonight to welcome Indian Fleet Commander Major General Chakravarti and Captain Nanda of the Indian flagship "Mysore."

At the banquet, Navy Commander-in-Chief Xiao Jingguang said that the people and armed forces of our two countries are working hard to build and defend their motherlands and to safeguard world peace. There is a traditional friendship between the people and armed forces of our two countries, and this friendship has made great progress in recent years. This visit to China by General Chakravarti leading the Indian Navy flagship "Mysore" will make a beneficial contribution to promoting mutual understanding and friendship between the armed forces of the two countries, especially between the two navies.

Major General Chakravarti said that I and all the officers and soldiers of the "Mysore" are proud to visit China, which is an unforgettable moment in our history. India and China have thousands of years of friendly relations. After India's independence, the relations between our two countries entered a new stage. Today, the mission of our Indian Navy is to defend India's construction and world peace, which is our due responsibility.

After the banquet, the hosts and guests exchanged gifts.

Among those present at the banquet were General Zhang Aiping, Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, Vice Admiral Liu Daosheng, and Lieutenant General Cheng Jun of the Air Force.

Xinhua News Agency, July 19th: The Chargé d'Affaires of the Indian Embassy in China, Kannampilly, held a reception at 6:00 PM tonight to welcome Indian Fleet Commander Major General Chakravarti and his wife, and Captain Nanda of the Indian flagship "Mysore."

Marshal Peng Dehuai, Navy Commander-in-Chief Xiao Jingguang, General Zhang Aiping, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, and Lieutenant General Chang Qiankun of the Air Force were invited to attend the reception.

Also invited to attend the reception were: Minister of Justice Shi Liang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Culture Zheng Zhenduo, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association Ding Xilin, and others.

Ambassadors and military attachés from various countries to China also attended the reception.

At 1:00 PM, Indian Military Attaché to China Brigadier General Sharma and his wife held a luncheon to welcome Indian Fleet Commander Major General Chakravarti. Marshal Ye Jianying, Navy Commander-in-Chief Xiao Jingguang, General Xiao Hua and others attended the luncheon.

### 欢迎查克洛伐蒂少将  海军司令员萧劲光大将举行宴会  印度大使馆临时代办举行招待会

1958-07-20
第6版()
专栏：

    欢迎查克洛伐蒂少将
    海军司令员萧劲光大将举行宴会
    印度大使馆临时代办举行招待会
    新华社19日讯　中国人民解放军海军司令员萧劲光海军大将为欢迎印度舰队司令查克洛伐蒂海军少将和印度旗舰“迈索尔”号舰长南达海军上校等，今晚八时在北京饭店举行宴会。
    萧劲光海军大将在宴会上讲话说，我们两国人民和武装力量都在为建设和保卫自己的祖国、为维护世界和平而努力地工作着。我们两国人民和武装部队之间存在着传统的友谊，这种友谊近年来得到长足的进展。这次查克洛伐蒂将军率领印度海军“迈索尔”号旗舰来我国访问，将对促进两国军队、特别是两国海军之间的相互了解和友谊，作出有益的贡献。
    查克洛伐蒂海军少将讲话说，我和“迈索尔”号的全体官兵来中国访问，都感到骄傲，这是我们历史上难忘的时刻。印中两国有几千年友好往来的历史，印度独立以后，我们两国的关系进入了一个新的阶段。今天，我们印度海军的任务，是保卫印度建设和世界和平，这是我们应尽的责任。
    宴会后，宾主双方互相赠送了礼品。
    出席宴会作陪的有张爱萍上将、刘志坚中将、刘道生海军中将、成钧空军中将等。
    新华社19日讯　印度驻我国大使馆临时代办康南皮利今晚六时举行招待会，欢迎印度舰队司令查克洛伐蒂海军少将夫妇和印度旗舰“迈索尔”号舰长南达海军上校等。
    彭德怀元帅、萧劲光海军大将、张爱萍上将、萧向荣中将和常乾坤空军中将等应邀出席了招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的还有：司法部长史良、外交部副部长罗贵波、文化部副部长郑振铎、对外文化联络委员会副主任楚图南、中印友好协会会长丁西林等。
    各国驻华大使和武官也出席了招待会。
    下午一时，印度驻华武官夏尔玛准将和夫人为欢迎印度舰队司令查克洛伐蒂海军少将举行了午宴。叶剑英元帅、萧劲光海军大将、萧华上将等出席了午宴。
