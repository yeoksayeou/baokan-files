Folk Rhymes of the Laborers

	The dam carries two mountains,
	The benefits are endless,
	Sandy beaches turn into oceans,
	Barren mountains turn into orchards;
	Water storage generates electricity,
	Irrigating tens of thousands of acres of fields;
	Preventing drought and flood,
	Dry land turns into paddy fields.

(Wang Zengde)

### 民工歌谣
王增德
1958-07-22
第6版()
专栏：

    民工歌谣
    大坝担两山，
    好处说不完，
    沙滩变大海，
    荒山变果园；
    蓄水能发电，
    灌溉万顷田；
    防旱又防涝，
    旱田变水田。
    （王增德）
