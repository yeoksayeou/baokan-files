Title: Advanced Workers of Vietnam (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1958-07-08
Page: 5

    Vietnamese Advanced Workers
    Left Picture: Nguyen Thi Hieu, an outstanding advanced young female worker at the Nam Dinh Textile Factory in Vietnam. She was awarded the Ho Chi Minh Medal for exceeding her work quota.
    Below Picture: Pham Hong Lien, an advanced worker at the Nam Dinh Textile Factory in Vietnam. Although a new worker, he has made rapid progress and completed 175% of his work quota for the first three months of 1958 in the socialist labor emulation movement of managing six machines per person.
          Manuscript from Vietnam Central Photo Agency (Xinhua News Agency)

### 越南的先进工人（图片）

1958-07-08
第5版()
专栏：

    越南的先进工人
    左图  越南南定纺织厂一个优秀的先进的青年女工——阮氏孝。她曾因超额完成工作定额，而荣获了胡志明纪念章。
    下图  越南南定纺织厂的一个先进工人——范鸿莲。他虽然是一个新工人，但是进步很快，并且在展开一人管理六台机器的社会主义劳动竞赛运动中，完成了一九五八年头三个月工作定额的百分之一百七十五。
          越南中央图片社稿（新华社发）
