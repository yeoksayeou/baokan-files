Title: Mongolia, Hungary, Bulgaria, Albania and the German Democratic Republic Governments Recognize the Republic of Iraq
Author:
Date: 1958-07-19
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, 18th. The government of the Mongolian People's Republic announced today that it officially recognizes the government of the Republic of Iraq.
　　Chairman Tsedenbal of the Mongolian Council of Ministers sent a telegram to Prime Minister Abdul Karim Qasim of the Republic of Iraq today, saying: The Mongolian government has decided to officially recognize the government of the Republic of Iraq. The Mongolian government believes that Mongolia and Iraq will establish friendly relations in the spirit of the Bandung Conference principles. The Mongolian people and the Mongolian government wish the Iraqi people greater achievements in consolidating national independence and safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 17th. According to a broadcast by Berlin Radio, Prime Minister Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic sent a telegram to Prime Minister Qasim of the Republic of Iraq tonight, warmly congratulating the patriotic forces of the Iraqi people on the victory they won in the struggle for national independence and the establishment of the Republic of Iraq. Grotewohl informed Qasim that the government of the German Democratic Republic has decided to recognize the Republic of Iraq.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 17th. Premier Münnich of the Hungarian Revolutionary Workers’ and Peasants’ Government sent a telegram to Prime Minister Qasim of the Republic of Iraq on the 17th, on behalf of the Hungarian Revolutionary Workers’ and Peasants’ Government, officially recognizing the government of the Republic of Iraq.
　　Münnich also extended warm congratulations on behalf of the Hungarian people on the founding of the Republic of Iraq and wished the Iraqi people victory in the struggle to consolidate the independence of the Republic of Iraq.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 17th. Chairman Yugov of the Bulgarian Council of Ministers sent a telegram to Prime Minister Qasim of the Republic of Iraq today, stating that the Bulgarian government has decided to recognize the government of the Republic of Iraq.
　　In his telegram, Yugov said that the Bulgarian government hopes that Bulgaria and the Republic of Iraq, which are geographically close, will establish sincere cooperative relations that will benefit the peoples of both countries and world peace.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 17th. According to a report by the Albanian News Agency on the 17th, Chairman Shehu of the Albanian Council of Ministers sent a telegram to Prime Minister Qasim of the Republic of Iraq, announcing that the Albanian government officially recognizes the government of the Republic of Iraq.
　　In his telegram, Shehu said that the establishment of the Republic of Iraq is the result of the Iraqi people's long-term heroic struggle for freedom and independence and against imperialism and its lackeys. The Albanian government is deeply convinced that the Republic of Iraq will make a great contribution to strengthening the fraternal cooperation of Arab countries and peace in the Middle East.

### 蒙、匈、保、阿和民主德国政府  承认伊拉克共和国

1958-07-19
第3版()
专栏：

    蒙、匈、保、阿和民主德国政府
    承认伊拉克共和国
    新华社乌兰巴托18日电　蒙古人民共和国政府今天宣布正式承认伊拉克共和国政府。
    蒙古部长会议主席泽登巴尔今天打电报给伊拉克共和国总理阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·卡塞姆说：蒙古政府决定正式承认伊拉克共和国政府。蒙古政府相信，蒙伊两国将根据万隆会议原则的精神建立友好关系。蒙古人民和蒙古政府祝伊拉克人民在巩固国家独立和维护亚洲和世界和平的事业中获得更大的成就。
    新华社柏林17日电　据柏林电台广播，德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥今晚致电伊拉克共和国总理卡塞姆，热烈祝贺伊拉克人民爱国力量在争取民族独立的斗争中所赢得的胜利和伊拉克共和国的成立。格罗提渥通知卡塞姆说，德意志民主共和国政府已经决定承认伊拉克共和国。
    新华社布达佩斯17日电　匈牙利工农革命政府总理明尼赫17日打电报给伊拉克共和国总理卡塞姆，代表匈牙利工农革命政府正式承认伊拉克共和国政府。
    明尼赫并代表匈牙利人民热烈祝贺伊拉克共和国的成立，并祝伊拉克人民在为巩固伊拉克共和国独立而进行的斗争中获得胜利。
    新华社索非亚17日电　保加利亚部长会议主席于哥夫今天打电报给伊拉克共和国总理卡塞姆，表示保加利亚政府决定承认伊拉克共和国政府。
    于哥夫在电报中说，保加利亚政府希望，在地理位置上邻近的保加利亚和伊拉克共和国两国将建立真诚合作的关系，以有利于两国人民和世界和平。
    新华社地拉那17日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社17日报道，阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席谢胡打电报给伊拉克共和国总理卡塞姆，宣布阿尔巴尼亚政府正式承认伊拉克共和国政府。
    谢胡在电报中说，伊拉克共和国的成立是伊拉克人民争取自由和独立、反对帝国主义及其奴仆的长期英勇斗争的结果。阿尔巴尼亚政府深信，伊拉克共和国对加强阿拉伯国家的兄弟合作和中近东的和平将作出巨大的贡献。
