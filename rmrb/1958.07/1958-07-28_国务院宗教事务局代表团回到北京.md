Title: State Council Bureau of Religious Affairs Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1958-07-28
Page: 2
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, July 25th. The delegation from the State Council Bureau of Religious Affairs, headed by Director He Chengxiang, concluded its visits to Hungary, Czechoslovakia, the Soviet Union, and Germany, and returned to Beijing on July 21st.

### 国务院宗教事务局代表团回到北京

1958-07-28
第2版()
专栏：

    国务院宗教事务局代表团回到北京
    新华社25日讯　国务院宗教事务局以局长何成湘为首的代表团已结束在匈、捷、苏、德四国的访问，在7月21日回到北京。
