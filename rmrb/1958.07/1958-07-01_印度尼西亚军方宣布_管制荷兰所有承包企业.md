Title: Indonesian Military Announces Control Over All Dutch Contracted Enterprises
Author:
Date: 1958-07-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 30th - Indonesian Army Chief of Staff Nasution announced today that the Indonesian government is implementing control over all Dutch contracted enterprises. The enterprises' repair shops, laboratories, buildings, and all fixed and movable assets and valuable documents are under control. The Indonesian Minister of Public Works and Power is responsible for implementing the control measures.

### 印度尼西亚军方宣布  管制荷兰所有承包企业

1958-07-01
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚军方宣布
    管制荷兰所有承包企业
    据新华社雅加达30日电　印度尼西亚陆军参谋长纳苏蒂安今天宣布，印度尼西亚政府实行管制荷兰所有承包企业。这些企业的修理车间、实验室、建筑物，以及所有的固定和流动资产和有价值的文件都在管制之列。印度尼西亚政府公共工程和动力部长负责执行管制的措施。
