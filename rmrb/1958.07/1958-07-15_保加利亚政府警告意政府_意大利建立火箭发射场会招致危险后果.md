Title: Bulgarian Government Warns Italian Government: Establishing Rocket Launch Site in Italy Will Lead to Dangerous Consequences
Author:
Date: 1958-07-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, July 13th: The Bulgarian Telegraph Agency reports that the Bulgarian government has issued a note regarding the Italian government's preparations to establish a rocket launch site on Italian territory.

The note points out that Bulgaria, as a close neighbor of Italy, cannot remain indifferent to this action, as it will constitute a new factor threatening peace, especially peace in the Balkans, and seriously endangering this part of the world.

The note states that the preparatory work for establishing a rocket launch site is contrary to the international obligations undertaken by Italy. At a time when people of all countries are more eager for peace than ever before and hope to reach a disarmament agreement between major powers, this action will exacerbate international tensions and increase the threat of war.

The note concludes by saying that the Bulgarian government considers it its duty to draw the Italian government's attention to the dangerous consequences that the establishment of a rocket launch site on Italian territory may entail.

### 保加利亚政府警告意政府  意大利建立火箭发射场会招致危险后果

1958-07-15
第6版()
专栏：

    保加利亚政府警告意政府
    意大利建立火箭发射场会招致危险后果
    新华社索非亚13日电　据保加利亚通讯社报道，保加利亚政府就意大利政府正在为在意大利领土上建立火箭发射场进行准备工作一事发出一份照会。
    照会指出，保加利亚作为意大利的近邻，不能对这种行动漠不关心，因为这样作将构成威胁和平，特别是巴尔干地区和平的新因素，使世界这一部分地区受到严重威胁。
    照会说，建立火箭发射场的备战工作是违背意大利所承担的国际义务的。当各国人民比以往任何时候都更加渴望和平，并且希望在大国之间达成裁军协议的时候，这样作会加剧国际紧张局势，增加战争威胁。
    照会最后说，保加利亚政府认为自己有责任提请意大利政府注意，在意大利领土上建立火箭发射场可能招致危险的后果。
