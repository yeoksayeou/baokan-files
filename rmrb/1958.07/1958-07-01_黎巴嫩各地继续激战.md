Title: Fighting Continues in Various Parts of Lebanon
Author:
Date: 1958-07-01
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 30th - Damascus Radio and the Voice of Free Lebanon Radio reported today that in Tripoli, Lebanon's second largest city, the rebel people's forces continued to engage in fierce fighting with the pro-Western government forces on the 29th. This marks the fourth consecutive day of intense fighting.

    On this day, the government forces deployed gunboats to bombard the port area from the sea, and also used armored vehicles to bombard areas controlled by the rebels.

    At the same time, in the capital Beirut, the important southern port of Saida, and the eastern Bekaa Valley region, the rebels continued to fight with government armed forces.

    Damascus Radio said today that another six United Nations observers arrived in Beirut on the 29th, bringing the total number of United Nations observers to one hundred and seven.

    Xinhua News Agency, 29th - New York Dispatch: Joseph Alsop, a commentator for the American "New York Herald Tribune," argued in an article entitled "The Danger in the Lebanese Crisis" on the 27th that developments in the Lebanese situation have placed the United States and Britain in a "dilemma" in the Middle East.

    He pointed out that if the United States and Britain carry out armed intervention in Lebanon, this will bring disaster upon themselves. However, decision-makers in Washington and London believe that "landing troops in Lebanon is a lesser disaster than losing the entire Middle East."

### 黎巴嫩各地继续激战

1958-07-01
第6版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩各地继续激战
    新华社大马士革30日电　据大马士革电台和自由黎巴嫩之声电台今天报道，黎巴嫩第二大城的黎波里的起义人民部队在29日继续同亲西方政府的部队进行激战。这是连续激战的第四天。
    在这一天，政府军方面出动了炮艇从海上轰击港口地区，并且还用装甲车轰击起义者控制的地区。
    同时，在首都贝鲁特，南部重要港口赛义达，以及东部贝卡盆地地区，起义者也在继续同政府武装力量进行战斗。
    大马士革电台今天说，又有六名联合国观察员在29日到达贝鲁特，至此联合国观察员数目已达一百零七人。
    据新华社29日讯　纽约消息：美国“纽约先驱论坛报”评论员艾尔索普27日在一篇题为“黎巴嫩危机中的危险”的文章中认为：黎巴嫩局势的发展，已经使美国和英国在中东处于“进退两难”的境地。
    他指出：美英两国如果对黎巴嫩进行武装干涉，这将给它们自己带来灾难。但是华盛顿和伦敦的决策人认为，“派军队在黎巴嫩登陆比失去整个中东这个灾难要轻得多”。
