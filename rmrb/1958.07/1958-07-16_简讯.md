Title: Brief News

Author: 
Date: 1958-07-16
Page: 6

    Brief News
    △The Polish Ministry of Domestic Trade and the Czechoslovak Ministry of Trade recently reached an agreement on the exchange of food and daily necessities. The first batch of exchanged goods is worth four million rubles.
    △The Cambodian journalists delegation, which was invited to visit North Korea, left Pyongyang by plane on the 15th to return home after a three-day visit to North Korea.
    △The weightlifting team of the United Arab Republic left Cairo by plane on the morning of the 15th for a three-week visit to Beijing.
    The eight-member weightlifting team is preparing to hold friendly weightlifting competitions with Chinese athletes.

### 简讯

1958-07-16
第6版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    △波兰国内贸易部同捷克斯洛伐克贸易部最近达成了交换食品和日用品的协议。头一批交换的货物总值有四百万卢布。
    △应邀到朝鲜访问的柬埔寨记者团在朝鲜进行了三天访问以后，15日乘飞机离开平壤回国。
    △阿拉伯联合共和国举重队15日上午乘飞机离开罗前往北京，进行为期三周的访问。
    这个由八人组成的举重队准备同中国的健儿们举行友谊的举重比赛。
