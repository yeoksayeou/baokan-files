Title: Mabian County Began Planting Early Rice Last Year; This Year's Yield Exceeds 800 Jin Per Mu
Author:
Date: 1958-07-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, July 25th: Mabian County in the Liangshan Yi Autonomous Prefecture of Sichuan Province harvested 812 mu of early rice, with an average yield of 808.2 jin per mu. More than 60% of the early rice fields yielded over 1,000 jin per mu, with the highest reaching 1,581 jin.

Mabian County, inhabited by both Yi and Han people, is located in the Xiaoliang Mountains, with an average elevation of about 1,500 meters and a maximum of over 3,000 meters. The mountains are large and the slopes are steep. Last year, the average yield of grain crops per mu was 273 jin. The county only started planting early rice last year, and this year is the first time it has been planted on a large scale. (Attached picture)

### 马边县去年开始种早稻  今年亩产超过八百斤

1958-07-26
第5版()
专栏：

    马边县去年开始种早稻
    今年亩产超过八百斤
    据新华社成都25日电　四川省凉山彝族自治州马边县八百一十二亩早稻，平均亩产八百零八点二斤。亩产千斤以上的占早稻田的60％多，最高的达到一千五百八十一斤。
    彝、汉杂居的马边县，位于小凉山区，海拔平均一千五百公尺左右，最高三千多公尺，山大坡陡，去年粮食作物平均亩产量是二百七十三斤。去年，这个县才开始种植早稻，今年是第一次大面积种植。（附图片）
