Title: Rubai and Qasim Receive British Ambassador to Iraq
Author:
Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 22nd—According to a broadcast by the Iraqi Republic Radio, at the request of the British Ambassador to Iraq, the Chairman of the Iraqi Supreme Sovereignty Committee, Rubai, and Prime Minister Qasim received the Ambassador on the 21st. Foreign Minister Jamal and Finance Minister Habib were also present.

### 鲁巴伊和卡塞姆接见英驻伊拉克大使

1958-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    鲁巴伊和卡塞姆接见英驻伊拉克大使
    新华社大马士革22日电　据伊拉克共和国电台宣布，伊拉克最高权力委员会主席鲁巴伊和总理卡塞姆在英国驻伊拉克大使的请求下，在21日接见这位大使。在座的还有外交部长贾迈尔和财政部长哈比卜。
