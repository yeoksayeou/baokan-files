Title: Vietnam International Commission Concludes: South Vietnam Repeatedly Violated the Geneva Accords by Continuously Allowing Illegal Entry of US Military Personnel into South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1958-07-11
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 9th: According to today's "People's Daily" report: The Vietnam International Commission recently reached a conclusion, confirming that there have been eleven incidents regarding the South Vietnamese authorities violating Article 16 of the Geneva Accords by allowing US military personnel to illegally enter South Vietnam.

    These eleven incidents are included in the ninety-two incidents of South Vietnamese authorities illegally allowing US military personnel to enter South Vietnam in 1957, which were raised by the Liaison Delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army to the International Commission.

    The International Commission's conclusion stated that the US military personnel who illegally entered South Vietnam successively included one hundred and four US officers and soldiers and some other military personnel, as well as a number of senior US military generals. The entry of these US military generals into South Vietnam "is a violation of the Geneva Accords and the regulations of the Central Joint Armistice Commission."

    The International Commission's conclusion also stated that the International Commission once requested to inspect the matter of the entry and exit of US military personnel into and out of South Vietnam, but the South Vietnamese authorities refused to provide assistance.

### 越南国际委员会作出结论  南越屡次违反日内瓦协定  不断让美国军事人员非法进入南越

1958-07-11
第5版()
专栏：

    越南国际委员会作出结论
    南越屡次违反日内瓦协定
    不断让美国军事人员非法进入南越
    新华社河内9日电　据“人民报”今天报道：越南国际委员会最近作出结论，肯定曾经发生十一起关于南越当局违反日内瓦协议第十六条的规定，让美国军事人员非法进入南越的事件。
    这十一起事件包括在越南人民军总司令部联络代表团向国际委员会提出的关于1957年南越当局让美国军事人员非法进入南越的九十二起事件中。
    国际委员会的结论说，这些先后非法进入南越的美国军事人员包括一百零四名美军官兵和一些其它军事人员，此外还有一批美国高级军事将领。这些美国军事将领进入南越“是违反日内瓦协议和中央联合停战委员会的规定的”。
    国际委员会的结论还说，国际委员会有一次要求检查关于美方军事人员进出南越这件事，南越当局拒绝给予协助。
