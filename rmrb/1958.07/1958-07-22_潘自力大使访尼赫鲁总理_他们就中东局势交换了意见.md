Title: Ambassador Pan Zili Visits Prime Minister Nehru They Exchange Views on the Middle East Situation
Author:
Date: 1958-07-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 21st: Chinese Ambassador to India, Pan Zili, visited Indian Prime Minister Nehru this morning. They exchanged views on the Middle East situation.

### 潘自力大使访尼赫鲁总理  他们就中东局势交换了意见

1958-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    潘自力大使访尼赫鲁总理
    他们就中东局势交换了意见
    新华社新德里21日电  中国驻印度大使潘自力在今天上午访问了印度总理尼赫鲁。他们就中东局势交换了意见。
