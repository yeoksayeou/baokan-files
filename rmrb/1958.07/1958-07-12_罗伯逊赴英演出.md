Title: Robeson to Perform in England
Author:
Date: 1958-07-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 11th — The famous American peace fighter and black singer Paul Robeson flew to London on the 10th. He will hold a concert in London and may then visit and perform in Czechoslovakia, the Soviet Union and elsewhere.

### 罗伯逊赴英演出

1958-07-12
第6版()
专栏：

    罗伯逊赴英演出
    据新华社11日讯　纽约消息：美国著名和平战士、黑人歌唱家保罗·罗伯逊10日乘飞机赴伦敦。他将在伦敦举行音乐会，随后可能前往捷克斯洛伐克、苏联和其他地方作访问演出。
