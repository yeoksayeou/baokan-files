Title: UAR Egyptian Region News Bureau Director Condemns Chamoun for Finding Excuses for Foreign Interference
Author:
Date: 1958-07-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 9th: The Director of the News Bureau of the Egyptian Region of the United Arab Republic, Afra, refuted here on the evening of the 8th the claim made by the pro-Western Lebanese President Chamoun that the United Arab Republic was attempting to annex Lebanon. He said that this shows that Chamoun still wants to ask foreign troops to suppress the Lebanese people and to find excuses for foreign intervention.

Chamoun's remarks were recently published by the American "Newsweek".

### 阿联埃及地区新闻局长  斥夏蒙为外国干涉找借口

1958-07-10
第6版()
专栏：

    阿联埃及地区新闻局长
    斥夏蒙为外国干涉找借口
    据新华社开罗9日电  阿拉伯联合共和国埃及地区新闻局局长阿弗拉8日晚间在这里发表谈话，驳斥黎巴嫩亲西方的总统夏蒙提出的所谓阿拉伯联合共和国企图并吞黎巴嫩的说法。他说，这说明夏蒙仍然想请外国军队来镇压黎巴嫩人民，并且为外国干涉找借口。
    夏蒙的那些说法是美国“新闻周刊”最近发表的。
