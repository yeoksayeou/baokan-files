Author:
Date: 1958-07-09

    Beware of Accidents (Originally published in the British magazine "Punch")
    American bombers carrying hydrogen bombs are constantly flying over Britain. In the event of an accident, it would cause unimaginable disasters in the British Isles. This cartoon reflects the British people's fear of this possible disaster.

### 谨防意外（图片）

1958-07-09
第6版()
专栏：

    谨防意外  （原载英国“笨拙”杂）
    带着氢弹的美国轰炸机不断在英国上空飞行，万一失事，将在英伦三岛酿成不堪设想的灾难。这幅漫画反映了英国人对这种可能的灾难的恐惧情绪。
