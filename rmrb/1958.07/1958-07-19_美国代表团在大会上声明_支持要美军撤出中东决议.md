Title: U.S. Delegation Declares Support for Resolution Demanding U.S. Troop Withdrawal from the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1958-07-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, July 18th: At this morning's session of the Disarmament and International Cooperation Conference, U.S. representative Stott Nearing read a statement from the U.S. delegation, supporting the resolution passed by the conference protesting the U.S. aggression against Lebanon.

The statement said that there can be no peace and international cooperation as long as one country attempts to occupy the territory of another country with military force or carry out police duties on the territory of another country. Therefore, we support the demand put forward by this conference that the U.S. armed forces immediately withdraw from the Middle East and nearby waters.

Nearing's statement won warm applause from the delegates attending the conference. As he stepped down from the podium, many delegates rushed forward to shake his hand.

### 美国代表团在大会上声明  支持要美军撤出中东决议

1958-07-19
第3版()
专栏：

    美国代表团在大会上声明
    支持要美军撤出中东决议
    新华社斯德哥尔摩18日电　出席裁军和国际合作大会的美国代表斯多特·尼林在今天上午的会议上宣读了美国代表团的一份声明，支持大会通过的抗议美国侵略黎巴嫩的决议。
    这份声明说，只要一个国家企图用军事力量占领另一个国家的领土或者在另一个国家的领土上执行警察职务，就不可能有和平和国际合作。因此，我们支持本大会提出的要美国武装部队立即撤出中东和附近海面的要求。
    尼林的声明博得出席大会代表们的热烈鼓掌，当他从讲台上走下来的时候，许多代表都赶上去跟他握手。
