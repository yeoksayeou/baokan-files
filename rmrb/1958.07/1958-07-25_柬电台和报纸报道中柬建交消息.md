Title: Cambodian Radio and Newspapers Report on the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations between China and Cambodia
Author: 
Date: 1958-07-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, July 24th: Cambodian National Radio and local newspapers this morning all reported the news of the establishment of diplomatic relations between Cambodia and China.

### 柬电台和报纸报道中柬建交消息

1958-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    柬电台和报纸报道中柬建交消息
    新华社金边24日电　柬埔寨国家电台和当地报纸今天上午都报道了柬埔寨和中国建立外交关系的消息。
