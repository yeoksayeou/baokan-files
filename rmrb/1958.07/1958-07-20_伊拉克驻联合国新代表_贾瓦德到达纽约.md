Title: New Iraqi Representative to the United Nations, Jawad, Arrives in New York
Author:
Date: 1958-07-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 19th: The newly appointed Permanent Representative of the Republic of Iraq to the United Nations, Hashim Jawad, arrived in New York on the 18th. After arriving at the United Nations headquarters, he held talks with the representative of the United Arab Republic. He will also meet with the United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld.

### 伊拉克驻联合国新代表  贾瓦德到达纽约

1958-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克驻联合国新代表
    贾瓦德到达纽约
    新华社19日讯　纽约消息：伊拉克共和国新任命的常驻联合国代表哈希姆·贾瓦德18日到达纽约。他在来到联合国总部后同阿拉伯联合共和国代表举行了会谈。他还将会见联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德。
