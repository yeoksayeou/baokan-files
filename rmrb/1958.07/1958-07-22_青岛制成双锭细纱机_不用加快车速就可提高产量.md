Title: Qingdao Makes Double-Spindle Spinning Frame: Production Can Be Increased Without Speeding Up the Machine

Author:
Date: 1958-07-22
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Qingdao, 20th – The Qingdao No. 3 State-owned Cotton Textile Factory recently manufactured China's first double-spindle spinning frame and officially put it into production.

The double-spindle spinning frame replaces the single row of spindles on one side of a spinning frame with two rows. Thus, one spinning frame has four rows of spindles, doubling production without increasing the speed. According to the factory's existing spinning frames, this can increase cotton yarn production by 36,000 pieces per year. At the same time, this double-spindle spinning frame can greatly save on equipment and factory buildings.

### 青岛制成双锭细纱机  不用加快车速就可提高产量

1958-07-22
第7版()
专栏：

    青岛制成双锭细纱机
    不用加快车速就可提高产量
    据新华社青岛20日电  国营青岛第三棉纺织厂最近制成了我国第一台双锭细纱机，并正式投入生产。
    双锭细纱机就是把细纱机一面排一排纱锭改为排两排纱锭，这样一台细纱机就有四排纱锭，不用加快车速就可提高产量一倍。根据这个厂现有的细纱机计算，一年即可增产三万六千件棉纱。同时，这种双锭细纱机可以大大节约设备、厂房。
