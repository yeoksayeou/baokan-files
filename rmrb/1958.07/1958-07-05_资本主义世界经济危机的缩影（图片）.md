Title: A Microcosm of the Capitalist World Economic Crisis
Author:
Date: 1958-07-05
Page: 6

A microcosm of the capitalist world economic crisis
    Unemployed workers in Springfield, the capital of Illinois, USA, petition in front of the state government, demanding an extension of the duration of relief payments and an increase in the amount of relief.
    Japanese women waiting for work at a job placement center in Shinjuku, Tokyo, Japan. How long will they have to wait? When will it be their turn?
    The shadow of the American economic crisis also looms over Australia. There are also large numbers of unemployed workers here. The picture shows unemployed people queuing for relief on a street in Sydney.
    Unemployed Canadian workers demand that the government take measures to get rid of American control, protect national industries, and solve the problem of worker unemployment.
                                  (Xinhua News Agency Draft)

### 资本主义世界经济危机的缩影（图片）

1958-07-05
第6版()
专栏：

    资本主义世界经济危机的缩影
    美国伊利诺斯州首府斯普林菲尔德的失业者在州政府前请愿，要求延长救济金期限，增加救济金额。
  在日本东京新宿的一个职业介绍所里等候工作的日本妇女。她们要等待多久呢？什么时候才能轮到呢？
    美国经济危机的魔影也笼罩着澳大利亚。这里也有大批工人失业。图为在悉尼的一条街上排队等候救济的失业者。
    加拿大的失业工人要求政府采取措施，摆脱美国的控制，保卫民族工业，解决工人失业问题。
