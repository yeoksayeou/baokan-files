Title: Statement by Special Envoy of Archbishop Makarios in the UK Opposes British Division of Cyprus
Author:
Date: 1958-07-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, June 30th. Zeno Rossides, the special envoy of Archbishop Makarios in London, said yesterday that Greek Cypriot public opinion strongly opposes the British plan for Cyprus because it will exacerbate the division and conflict on the island.

Rossides arrived in London last week and held secret talks with the British Governor of Cyprus, Foot.

The statement said, "The way to break the deadlock is to formulate a truly democratic autonomous constitution based on the integrity of Cyprus and the unity that has always existed among all its people."

### 马卡里奥斯主教驻英特使声明  反对英国分裂塞浦路斯

1958-07-01
第6版()
专栏：

    马卡里奥斯主教驻英特使声明
    反对英国分裂塞浦路斯
    据新华社伦敦30日电　马卡里奥斯主教驻伦敦特使齐农·罗西迪斯昨天说，希腊血统的塞浦路斯人的舆论激烈反对英国的塞浦路斯计划，因为它将加剧那个岛屿的分裂和冲突。
    罗西迪斯是上星期到伦敦来的，他和英国的塞浦路斯总督富特举行了秘密会谈。
    声明说，“打破僵局的办法是要以塞浦路斯的完整和全体人民一向存在的统一为基础来制订真正民主的自治宪法。”
