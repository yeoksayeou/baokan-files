Title: Soviet Art Exhibition Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-07-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Report: The "1955-1957 Soviet Artists' Works - Oil Paintings, Sculptures, Prints Exhibition" opened on the morning of the 8th at the Wenhua Hall of the Forbidden City in Beijing.

This exhibition was organized by the Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in accordance with the 1958 implementation plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement. The exhibition features over 500 oil paintings, sculptures, and prints, which were carefully selected original works from the more than 10,000 exhibits at the "All-Soviet Art Exhibition" held in Moscow to commemorate the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution. It includes artworks from all fifteen Soviet republics. This exhibition can be regarded as a grand review of the outstanding works of well-known and young Soviet artists in the past two years.

Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and more than 300 other people attended the opening ceremony and visited the exhibition. Foreign diplomatic envoys and cultural officials stationed in China were also invited to attend the opening ceremony and visit the exhibition.

### 苏联美术家作品展览会在北京举行

1958-07-11
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联美术家作品展览会在北京举行
    据新华社讯　“1955—1957年苏联美术家作品——油画、雕塑、版画展览会”，8日上午在北京故宫文华殿开幕。
    这次展览会是根据中苏文化合作协定1958年执行计划，由对外文化联络委员会举办的。会上展出的五百多件油画、雕塑、版画展品，是为纪念十月革命四十周年在莫斯科举行的“全苏艺术展览会”的一万多件展品中精选出来的原作，包括苏联十五个加盟共和国的美术作品。这次展览会可以说是近两年来苏联著名美术家和青年美术家的优秀作品的大检阅。
    对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若等三百多人出席了开幕式，并且参观了展览。应邀参加开幕式和参观展览的还有各国驻华外交使节和文化官员。
