Title: Praise the Double-Share Plow
Author: 
Date: 1958-07-08
Page: 2

    Praise the Double-Share Plow
      (I)
    Two oxen pull the plow swiftly like the wind,
    The double-share turns the soil quickly like a dragon;
    Who says the new agricultural tool is heavy,
    The girl holds it and her face does not turn red.
        (Jiangkou, Guizhou)
      (II)
    Double wheels and double-shares plow deep,
    Removing weeds and eliminating pests with energy;
    If water and fertilizer techniques keep up,
    Grain production will increase by millions of jin.
        (Changtu, Liaoning)

### 赞美双铧犁

1958-07-08
第2版()
专栏：

    赞美双铧犁
      （一）
    两牛拉犁急如风，
    双铧翻土快如龙；
    谁说新式农具重，
    姑娘扶起脸不红。
        （贵州江口）
      （二）
    双轮双铧翻地深，
    除草灭虫地有劲；
    水肥技术跟得上，
    增产粮食千万斤。
        （辽宁昌图）
