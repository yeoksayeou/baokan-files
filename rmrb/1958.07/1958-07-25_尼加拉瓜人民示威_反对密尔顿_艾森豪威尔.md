Title: Nicaraguan People Demonstrate Against Milton Eisenhower
Author:
Date: 1958-07-25
Page: 4

    Nicaraguan People Demonstrate
    Against Milton Eisenhower
    Xinhua News Agency, New York, July 23rd (TASS) -- The Nicaraguan people oppose the "visit" of Milton Eisenhower, the U.S. President's brother, to their country.

      "New York Times" reporter Kennedy reported from Managua, the capital of Nicaragua, that Milton Eisenhower encountered youth parades and demonstrations there. The demonstrating youths protested against U.S. military aid to their country. The protesters held signs with slogans such as: "Less Collusion with Dictators!" and "No Armaments, Just Economic Aid!"

    According to an Associated Press reporter, university students in Managua also distributed a statement pointing out that the purpose of Milton Eisenhower's visit was to further bind Nicaragua's economy and politics for the benefit of the United States.

### 尼加拉瓜人民示威  反对密尔顿·艾森豪威尔

1958-07-25
第4版()
专栏：

    尼加拉瓜人民示威
    反对密尔顿·艾森豪威尔
    新华社24日讯  据塔斯社纽约23日讯：尼加拉瓜人民反对美国总统的弟弟密尔顿·艾森豪威尔到他们的国家进行“访问”。
      “纽约时报”记者肯尼迪自尼加拉瓜的首都马那瓜报道，密尔顿·艾森豪威尔在那里碰到了青年的游行示威，示威青年抗议美国对他们的国家进行军事援助。游行者手里拿着标语，上面写着：“少和独裁者狼狈为奸！”、“不要武装，要经济援助！”
    据美联社记者报道，马那瓜的大学生还散发了声明，声明指出，密尔顿·艾森豪威尔访问的目的是为了美国的利益而进一步束缚尼加拉瓜的经济和政治。
