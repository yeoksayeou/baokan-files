Title: Karachi's "Politician" Weekly Says US Aid Has Harmed Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1958-07-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, July 7th: Karachi's "Politician" weekly commented today on Pakistan's economic situation, saying: Although Pakistan has received 600 million rupees in economic aid from the United States in addition to military assistance, the supply of consumer goods in Pakistan is very poor, and its development plans have not made much progress. The weekly pointed out that the detrimental effects of US loan assistance will have a more serious impact on Pakistan's economy in the future.

### 卡拉奇“政治家”周刊说  美援害了巴基斯坦

1958-07-08
第6版()
专栏：

    卡拉奇“政治家”周刊说
    美援害了巴基斯坦
    据新华社卡拉奇7日电  卡拉奇“政治家”周刊今天评论巴基斯坦经济情况说：尽管巴基斯坦除了得到美国的军事援助以外，还得到美国的六亿卢比的经济援助，但是巴基斯坦的消费品供应情况却很糟糕，它的各项发展计划也没有取得多大进展。这家周刊指出，美国的贷款援助的遗害给予巴基斯坦经济的影响将来会更加严重。
