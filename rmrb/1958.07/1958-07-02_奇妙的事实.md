    Amazing Facts
    △ Drilling has revealed that there is a huge freshwater sea 3,000 meters underground in Australia, its area almost as large as Australia itself. Experts believe that similar freshwater seas may exist deep beneath other continents as well.
    △ The ocean's 800-1200 meter depth is a "low-oxygen zone," and there is almost no life in this range.

### 奇妙的事实

1958-07-02
第8版()
专栏：

    奇妙的事实
    △钻探的结果指出：澳洲地下三千公尺深处，有一个巨大的淡水海，其面积几乎和澳洲等大。专家认为：其他大陆深处，也可能有同样的淡水海存在。
    △海洋800—1200公尺深处，是一条“稀氧带”，在此段范围内，几乎没有生物存在。
