Title: Resisting US-British Aggression in the Middle East: Flames of Protest Burn Worldwide (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1958-07-31
Page: 2

    Opposing US-British Aggression in the Middle East
    Flames of Protest Burn Worldwide
    England
    The London District Committee of the Communist Party of Great Britain organized a mass rally in Hyde Park on July 20 to oppose US-British aggression in the Middle East. The picture shows the masses marching in demonstration holding up banners. The banners read "Stop the Dirty War" and "Stop Interfering in Arab Countries."
                                    Xinhua News Agency reporter Peng Di

    United Arab Republic
    On July 18, more than 60,000 people gathered in Damascus, the Syrian region, to celebrate the founding of the Republic of Iraq. At the meeting, President Nasser of the United Arab Republic called on the people of the UAR to prepare to take up arms to defend the freedom won by the Iraqi people. This is a picture of soldiers, students, workers and people from all walks of life participating in the rally, shouting slogans such as "American troops get out of Lebanon immediately!" and "British troops get out of Jordan immediately!"
                          Xinhua News Agency reporter Cheng Liangping

    Indonesia
    Jakarta citizens held a mass rally on July 21 to protest against the US-British imperialist aggression in the Middle East. Demonstrators held signs that read "No interference in Lebanon!", "Oppose US-British aggressors!", "The US landing in Lebanon is an invasion!"
        Xinhua News Agency draft

    Korea
    About 300,000 Pyongyang residents gathered on July 18 to protest against the US-British imperialist armed aggression against Lebanon and Jordan. The picture shows angry masses shouting slogans against imperialist aggression in the Middle East.
                Korean Central News Agency draft (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 反对美英侵略中东  抗议怒火遍燃全世界（图片）

1958-07-31
第2版()
专栏：

    反对美英侵略中东
    抗议怒火遍燃全世界
    英国
    英国共产党伦敦区委员会7月20日在海德公园组织一次群众大会反对美英侵略中东。图为举着标语牌游行示威的群众。标语牌上写着“停止肮脏战争”、“停止干涉阿拉伯国家”。
                                    新华社记者　彭迪摄
    阿联
    7月18日，叙利亚地区大马士革六万多人集会，庆祝伊拉克共和国成立。在会上阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔号召阿联人民准备拿起武器保卫伊拉克人民取得的自由。这是参加集会的军人、学生、工人及各界人士在高呼“美军立即滚出黎巴嫩！”“英军立即滚出约旦！”等口号。
                          新华社记者　程良平摄
    印度尼西亚
    雅加达市民7月21日举行群众大会，抗议美英帝国主义侵略中东。示威者拿着“不准干涉黎巴嫩！”“反对美英侵略者！”“美国在黎巴嫩登陆是侵略！”等标语。
        新华社稿
    朝鲜
    平壤居民约三十万人7月18日集会、抗议美英帝国主义武装侵略黎巴嫩和约旦。图为激愤的群众高呼口号反对帝国主义侵略中东。
                朝鲜中央通讯社稿（新华社发）
