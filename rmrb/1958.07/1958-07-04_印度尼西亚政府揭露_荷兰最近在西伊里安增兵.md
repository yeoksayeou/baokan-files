Title: Indonesian Government Reveals: Netherlands Recently Increased Troops in West Irian
Author:
Date: 1958-07-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 2nd: Indonesian Foreign Minister Subandrio said in a statement issued today that the Indonesian government cannot help but be astonished by the recent increase of troops by the Dutch government in West Irian.

Subandrio said, "This behavior of the Dutch government will undoubtedly only increase the tension between the two countries."

Subandrio added, "If the Indonesian government and people have not paid serious attention to the military aspect of the West Irian dispute in the past, then the recent actions of the Netherlands cannot but force us to maintain a higher degree of vigilance on this issue."

### 印度尼西亚政府揭露  荷兰最近在西伊里安增兵

1958-07-04
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚政府揭露
    荷兰最近在西伊里安增兵
    据新华社雅加达2日电　印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约在今天发表的一项声明中说，荷兰政府最近在西伊里安增兵一事不能不引起印度尼西亚政府的惊异。
    苏班德里约说，“荷兰政府这种行为将毫无疑问地只会在两国之间增加紧张气氛”。
    苏班德里约又说，“假如说，印度尼西亚政府和人民过去对西伊里安争执的军事方面没有给予严重的注意，那么，最近荷兰的行动不能不迫使我们对这个问题保持更高的警惕”。
