Title: Our Country's Merchant Ship Sails to the Soviet Union for the First Time
Author:
Date: 1958-07-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, July 5th: According to the Sino-Soviet agreement on merchant shipping on national borders and connecting rivers and lakes, China's first merchant ship to the Soviet Union, the "Qianshan" sailed from Harbin to Boli (Khabarovsk) in the Soviet Union at 10:00 am today with four cargo ships fully loaded with 3,000 tons of cement.

Before the departure, a grand celebration was held to commemorate the opening of Sino-Soviet shipping. Li Yanlu, Vice Governor of Heilongjiang Province, and Terefilov, the Soviet Consul General in Harbin, spoke at the meeting.

More than 2,000 people at the Harbin wharf beat drums and gongs to bid farewell to the departure of the "Qianshan". They entrusted the staff of the "Qianshan" to bring the friendship of the people of Harbin to the people of Boli in the Soviet Union.

The first Soviet merchant ship to come to our country under the same agreement arrived in Fujin, China on May 8 this year.

### 我国商船首次开往苏联

1958-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我国商船首次开往苏联
    据新华社哈尔滨5日电　根据中苏两国关于国境及其相通河流和湖泊的商船通航协定，我国首次开往苏联的商船——“千山号”在今天上午十时带四艘货船，满载三千吨水泥由哈尔滨启航开往苏联伯力。
    启航前隆重举行了庆祝中苏船舶通航大会。黑龙江省副省长李延禄和苏联驻哈尔滨总领事特列菲洛夫等在会上讲了话。
    在哈尔滨码头上有二千多名群众敲锣打鼓欢送“千山号”启航。他们嘱托“千山号”的工作人员把哈尔滨人民的友谊带给苏联伯力人民。
    苏联根据同一协定第一次来我国的商船是在今年5月8日到达我国富锦的。
