Title: Welcoming the Commander of the Indian Navy Fleet, Commander and Political Commissar of our East Sea Fleet Held a Banquet, Naval Vessels and Aircraft Held Joint Military Exercises for the Guests

Author: 
Date: 1958-07-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 17th: Tao Yong, Vice Admiral and Commander of the East Sea Fleet of our Navy, and Kang Zhiqiang, Vice Admiral and Political Commissar, held a banquet tonight to welcome Rear Admiral Chakravarti, Commander of the Indian Navy Fleet, and the cruiser "Mysore" to Shanghai for a visit. Kannampilly, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Indian Embassy in China, Brigadier General Sharma, Military Attaché, Indian Consul General in Shanghai, Charles, and Zhang Tong, Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in India, also attended the banquet.

Vice Admiral Tao Yong, Rear Admiral Chakravarti, and Captain Nanda of the "Mysore" cruiser all spoke at the banquet, expressing their pleasure at this friendly meeting.

More than 100 people, including leading members of the Shanghai Garrison and government, as well as people from all walks of life, attended as guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 18th: This morning, a unit of the East Sea Fleet of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy conducted an exercise in the waters off Wusongkou, where torpedo boats, in coordination with naval aviation, launched an attack on a surface vessel formation in a narrow channel, for Rear Admiral Chakravarti, Commander of the Indian Navy Fleet, Captain Nanda of the flagship "Mysore," and over a hundred officers and soldiers. Commander of the East Sea Fleet, Vice Admiral Tao Yong, Political Commissar Vice Admiral Kang Zhiqiang, Deputy Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Lieutenant General Liu Fei, Vice Mayor of Shanghai Xu Jianguo and other military and government leaders accompanied the friends on the "Chengdu" warship to watch the exercise.

On the return voyage, Rear Admiral Chakravarti of the Indian Navy and the Indian officers and soldiers of the flagship "Mysore" who visited the "Chengdu" warship, together with Vice Admiral Tao Yong, Vice Admiral Kang Zhiqiang, Lieutenant General Liu Fei, Vice Mayor Xu Jianguo, and the officers and soldiers of the "Chengdu" warship, took a group photo on the deck of the "Chengdu" warship as a memento. (Photo attached)

After arriving in Shanghai, Rear Admiral Chakravarti, Commander of the Indian Navy Fleet, called on Xu Jianguo, Vice Mayor of Shanghai.

### 欢迎印度海军舰队司令  我东海舰队司令员和政委举行宴会  舰艇飞机为客人举行联合军事演习

1958-07-19
第5版()
专栏：

    欢迎印度海军舰队司令
    我东海舰队司令员和政委举行宴会
    舰艇飞机为客人举行联合军事演习
    据新华社上海17日电　我国海军东海舰队司令员陶勇海军中将、政治委员康志强中将今晚举行宴会，欢迎印度海军舰队司令查克洛伐蒂海军少将和“迈索尔”号巡洋舰来上海访问。印度驻我国大使馆临时代办康南皮利、武官夏尔玛准将，印度驻上海总领事查礼以及中国驻印度大使馆武官张彤大校也参加了宴会。
    陶勇海军中将、查克洛伐蒂海军少将以及上海市副市长许建国、“迈索尔”巡洋舰舰长南达海军上校都在宴会上讲话，他们对这一次友谊的会见都表示高兴。
    上海市驻军和政府首长以及各界人士一百多人出席作陪。
    据新华社上海18日电　今天上午，中国人民解放军海军东海舰队一部在吴淞口外的海面上，为印度海军舰队司令查克洛伐蒂海军少将和“迈索尔”号旗舰舰长南达海军上校等百余官兵，在狭水道进行了一场鱼雷快艇在海军航空兵协同下向水面舰艇编队实施攻击的演习。陪同友人乘“成都”号军舰前来观看的，有东海舰队司令员陶勇海军中将、政治委员康志强海军中将、中国人民解放军上海警备区副司令员刘飞中将和上海市副市长许建国等军政首长。
    返航途中，印度查克洛伐蒂海军少将和在“成都”号军舰上参观的“迈索尔”号旗舰的印度官兵，和陶勇海军中将、康志强海军中将、刘飞中将、许建国副市长和“成都”舰官兵一起，在“成都”舰甲板上摄影留念。（附图片）
    印度海军舰队司令查克洛伐蒂海军少将抵达上海后，拜会上海市副市长许建国。
                新华社稿（无线电传真）
