Title: Commander-in-Chief of the United Arab Republic Sends Congratulations on the Success of the Iraqi Revolution
Author:
Date: 1958-07-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 15th - Commander-in-Chief of the United Arab Republic, Amer, sent a telegram today to Prime Minister and Commander-in-Chief of the Iraqi Republic, Qasim, congratulating him on the success of the Iraqi Revolution.

### 阿联总司令电贺伊拉克革命成功

1958-07-17
第3版()
专栏：

    阿联总司令电贺伊拉克革命成功
    据新华社开罗15日电　阿拉伯联合共和国总司令阿密尔今天打电报给伊拉克共和国总理兼总司令卡塞姆，对伊拉克革命的成功，向他表示祝贺。
