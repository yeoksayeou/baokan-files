Title: Venezuelan Insurgent Leader, Former Defense Minister, Expelled

Author:
Date: 1958-07-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Caracas, 25th: The Venezuelan government announced on the evening of the 24th that Castro León, the leader of the insurgents who launched a reactionary military coup on the 22nd and failed, along with his seven assistants, have been expelled from Venezuela in order to guarantee Venezuela's peace and tranquility. These eight insurgents and their families flew to the United States on the afternoon of the 24th.

### 委内瑞拉叛乱分子首脑  前国防部长被驱逐出国

1958-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

    委内瑞拉叛乱分子首脑
    前国防部长被驱逐出国
    新华社25日讯　加拉加斯消息：委内瑞拉政府24日晚间宣布，22日发动反动军事政变未逞的叛乱分子首领、前国防部长卡斯特罗·莱昂和他的七个助手已被逐出委内瑞拉，以保证委内瑞拉的和平和安宁。这八个叛乱分子同他们的家属24日下午乘飞机飞往美国。
