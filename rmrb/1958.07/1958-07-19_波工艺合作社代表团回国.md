Title: Polish Handicraft Cooperative Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1958-07-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th. The Polish Handicraft Cooperative Delegation, led by Rzebrowski, Chairman of the Central Union of Polish Handicraft Cooperatives, left Beijing by plane today to return home.

The Polish Handicraft Cooperative Delegation visited Beijing, Shanghai, Nanjing, Hangzhou, Suzhou, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Shenyang and other places for more than a month, and visited more than forty handicraft cooperatives and handicraft factories.

### 波工艺合作社代表团回国

1958-07-19
第5版()
专栏：

    波工艺合作社代表团回国
    新华社17日讯　以波兰工艺合作社中央联社主席热布罗夫斯基为首的波兰工艺合作社代表团，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    波兰工艺合作社代表团曾在北京、上海、南京、杭州、苏州、武汉、广州、沈阳等地进行了一个多月的访问，参观了四十多个手工业合作社和手工业合作工厂。
