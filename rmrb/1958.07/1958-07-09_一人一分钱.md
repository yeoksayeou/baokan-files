Title: One Penny Per Person
Author: Xing Yi
Date: 1958-07-09
Page: 8

    One penny per person
    Xing Yi
    A single thread is hard to make into a line,
    A single tree can't cover a house;
    One penny per person,
    Build a large factory.
    [Xi'an "General Line Poetry Leaflet"]
