Title: Flames of Anger Against US-British Aggression Burn Fiercely: People of the United Arab Republic, Sudan, and Other Countries Demand Aggressor Troops Get Out of the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1958-07-23
Page: 4

United Arab Republic

    According to a report in the "Al-Ahram" on the 20th, more than 10,000 workers, farmers, students, youth, and intellectuals held a demonstration in Aleppo, a northern city in the Syrian region, on the 19th. They cheered the birth of the Iraqi Republic, condemned the US-British occupation of Lebanon and Jordan, and demanded that the aggressor troops withdraw from there immediately.

    Workers and students in Cairo, the capital of the United Arab Republic, held a demonstration in the Shubra district on the evening of the 20th, condemning the US-British occupation of Lebanon and Jordan.

    They shouted slogans such as "Down with US-British imperialism!" "Arabs will crush the Sixth Fleet!" "Long live the Iraqi Revolution!" "Long live the Soviet Union and China!"
Sudan

    Cairo's "Al-Masaa" correspondent in Khartoum reported on the 20th that residents of the Sudanese capital, Khartoum, and Omdurman held demonstrations on the 19th, condemning US-British aggression against Arab countries and demanding that the Sudanese government recognize the Iraqi Republic.

    The demonstrators took to the streets after participating in a rally organized by the Sudanese Trade Union Federation.

    Mass demonstrations were also held in the streets of Atbara, a railway hub. The masses held placards demanding recognition of the Iraqi Republic.

    Sudanese university students held a demonstration in front of the US and British embassies in Khartoum on the 20th, condemning the US-British occupation of Lebanon and Jordan.

    The marching students also gathered in front of the government building. There, the chairman of the University Student Union read a memorandum demanding that US troops withdraw from Lebanon and British forces withdraw from Jordan. They also expressed their support for the Iraqi revolution and the Lebanese people's struggle against imperialism.
Argentina

    Hundreds of people in the Argentine capital held another demonstration on the evening of the 21st against the US armed aggression against Lebanon. They shouted anti-aggression slogans in front of the US embassy and threw stones and asphalt at the embassy.

    Three Arab groups in Argentina called on Arab shops on the 21st to strike for one day this week in protest against "US interference in Lebanon's internal affairs."
India

    A group of Congress Party members of the Indian Parliament condemned the armed intervention of the United States and Britain in Lebanon and Jordan at a public gathering here on the evening of the 21st.

    Dasappa, a Congress Party member of parliament from Mysore, condemned the excuses used by the US and Britain to justify their aggression. He warmly welcomed Khrushchev's "great proposal" to hold a five-nation summit including India.

    Muslim leader Hafizur Rehman congratulated the newly established Iraqi Republic.

    Other Congress Party members of parliament who spoke at the meeting included Vyas Andra of Rajasthan, N. G. Ranga, and Arun Chandra Guha of West Bengal. They all demanded that the United States and Britain abandon their colonial policies and demanded the immediate withdrawal of US and British troops from Lebanon and Jordan.

    The gathering was hosted by the Indian Council of World Affairs.
Indonesia

    Citizens of Jakarta strongly condemned the aggression of the United States and Britain in the Middle East at a mass rally held on the 21st.

    Several thousand workers, veterans, students, and women braved the rain to participate in the rally. They held large placards with slogans such as: "No interference in Lebanon!" "Oppose US-British aggressors!" "An attack on the Arab people is an attack on the Indonesian people!"

    The resolution passed by the conference demanded that US-British aggressor troops immediately withdraw from Lebanon and Jordan, and called on all the people of Indonesia to fully oppose the aggressive acts of the United States and Britain by all means. The resolution also expressed warm support for the Indonesian government's recognition of the Iraqi Republic.
Vietnam

    On the 20th (the fourth anniversary of the Vietnam armistice), more than 50,000 people in Hanoi held a massive rally and demonstration against US interference in South Vietnam and the countries of the Near and Middle East.

    The conference passed a proposal to the International Commission, a telegram to the Stockholm Disarmament and International Cooperation Conference, and a circular telegram supporting the people of Iraq and Lebanon.

    A demonstration was held after the conference. Le Duan, Truong Chinh, Pham Hung and other leaders of the Vietnam Workers' Party walked at the front of the procession. In this huge demonstration, there were soldiers who had participated in the August Revolution, cadres who had gathered from the South to the North, workers, farmers, students, as well as ethnic minorities, religious figures, and people from the industrial and commercial sectors who were undergoing socialist transformation.

### 抗议美英侵略怒火越烧越烈  阿联苏丹等国人民要求侵略军滚出中东

1958-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

    抗议美英侵略怒火越烧越烈
    阿联苏丹等国人民要求侵略军滚出中东
    阿联
    据20日的“光明报”报道，一万多名工人、农民、学生、青年和知识分子19日在叙利亚地区北部城市阿勒颇举行示威游行，他们欢呼伊拉克共和国的诞生，谴责美英侵占黎巴嫩和约旦，并且要求侵略军队立即从那里撤走。
    阿拉伯联合共和国首都开罗的工人和学生20日晚上在舒卜拉区举行示威游行，谴责美英侵占黎巴嫩和约旦。
    他们高喊“打倒美英帝国主义！”“阿拉伯人将粉碎第六舰队！”“伊拉克革命万岁！”“苏联和中国万岁！”等口号。苏丹
    开罗“晚报”驻喀土穆记者20日报道，苏丹首都喀土穆和恩图曼两个城市的居民在19日举行示威游行，谴责美英对阿拉伯国家的侵略，并且要求苏丹政府承认伊拉克共和国。
    示威者在参加了苏丹工会联合会所组织的集会以后，就到街道上游行。
    在铁路枢纽阿特巴拉的街道上，也举行了群众示威游行。群众手里拿着要求承认伊拉克共和国的标语牌。
    苏丹大学生20日在喀土穆的美国和英国大使馆前面举行示威，谴责美英侵占黎巴嫩和约旦。
    游行的学生队伍还在政府大厦的门前集会。在那里，由大学生联合会主席宣读了备忘录，要求美国军队撤出黎巴嫩，英国军队撤出约旦。他们还表示支持伊拉克革命和黎巴嫩人民反对帝国主义的斗争。阿根廷
    阿根廷首都数百人21日晚上再一次举行反对美国武装侵略黎巴嫩的示威游行。他们在美国大使馆门前高呼反对侵略的口号，并且向大使馆扔石头和沥青。
    阿根廷三个阿拉伯团体21日号召阿拉伯人的商店在本周内罢市一天，抗议“美国对黎巴嫩内政的干涉”。
    印度
    印度一批国大党国会议员21日晚上在这里的公众集会上，谴责美英两国对黎巴嫩和约旦进行武装干涉。
    迈索尔的国大党议员达萨帕谴责美英两国为它们的侵略进行辩护的借口。他热烈欢迎赫鲁晓夫所提出的关于举行包括印度在内的五国首脑会议的“伟大建议”。
    穆斯林领袖哈菲祖尔·雷赫曼向新成立的伊拉克共和国祝贺。
    在会上发言的国大党议员还有拉贾斯坦的维雅斯·安得拉的纳·格·兰加和西孟加拉的阿隆·钱德拉·古哈。他们都要求美国和英国放弃它们的殖民政策，并要求美英的军队立即撤出黎巴嫩和约旦。
    这次集会是由印度外交协会主办的。印度尼西亚
    雅加达市民在21日举行的一次群众大会上强烈地谴责美英两国在中东地区进行的侵略。
    好几千名工人、退伍军人、学生和妇女冒雨参加了大会。他们拿着很大的标语牌，上面写着：“不准干涉黎巴嫩！”“反对美英侵略者！”“对阿拉伯人民的进攻就是对印度尼西亚人民的进攻！”
    大会通过的决议要求美英侵略军队立即撤出黎巴嫩和约旦，并且号召印度尼西亚全体人民以各种方法全力反对美英两国的侵略行为。决议还表示热烈支持印度尼西亚政府承认伊拉克共和国。
    越南
    河内五万多人20日（越南停战四周年）举行了声势浩大的反对美国干涉南越和中近东国家的集会和示威游行。
    大会通过了给国际委员会的建议书、给斯德哥尔摩裁军和国际合作大会的电报和支持伊拉克、黎巴嫩人民的通电等。
    大会结束之后举行了示威游行。黎笋、长征、范雄等越南劳动党的领导人走在队伍的前面。在这支巨大的示威队伍中，有参加过八月革命的战士，有从南方集结到北方的干部，有工人、农民、学生以及少数民族、宗教界和正在接受社会主义改造的工商界人士等。
