Title: Soviet Military Delegation Returns Home After Visiting the United Arab Republic
Author:
Date: 1958-07-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: After a ten-day visit to the United Arab Republic, the Soviet military delegation headed by Soviet Air Force Chief of Staff Marshal Rudenko left Cairo by special plane on the morning of the 19th to return to Moscow.

### 苏联军事代表团访阿联后已返国

1958-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联军事代表团访阿联后已返国
    据新华社讯  由苏联空军总参谋长鲁金科元帅率领的苏联军事代表团在阿拉伯联合共和国进行了十天的访问以后，19日早晨乘专机离开开罗回莫斯科。
