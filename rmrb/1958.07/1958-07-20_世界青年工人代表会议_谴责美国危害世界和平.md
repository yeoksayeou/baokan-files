Title: World Youth Workers' Conference Condemns US for Endangering World Peace
Author:
Date: 1958-07-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 17th - Participants in the World Youth Workers' Conference, currently being held in Prague, unanimously adopted telegrams to the President of the United Nations Security Council and the United Nations Secretary-General on the 17th, angrily condemning the landing of US troops in Lebanon.

The telegram stated: "The armed intervention of the United States in Lebanon is a threat to peace throughout the world, creating a hotbed of war, and at the same time it grossly violates the right of self-determination of all peoples and grossly violates the United Nations Charter."

The attendees firmly protested this aggression and demanded that the Security Council take necessary measures to ensure the immediate withdrawal of US troops from Lebanon.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 17th - The World Youth Workers' Conference continued its discussions on the 16th. Saillant, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Trade Unions, Hakim, representative of the United Arab Republic, and Han Xiya, head of the Chinese delegation, condemned the US armed intervention in Lebanon at the meeting.

Saillant announced at the meeting that the people of Iraq had overthrown the monarchy and declared the establishment of the Republic of Iraq, and the audience burst into warm applause and cheers. Referring to the US military intervention in the Middle East, Saillant pointed out that this action seriously threatened peace and undermined the right of all peoples to decide their own future.

In his speech, Hakim spoke of the imperialists' aggressive conspiracy against Arab countries. He protested the US armed intervention in Lebanon. His speech was warmly welcomed by the representatives.

Han Xiya spoke at the afternoon session. On behalf of the Chinese youth workers, Han Xiya warmly congratulated the great victory of the Iraqi people and resolutely condemned the crime of US imperialism's armed aggression against Lebanon.

### 世界青年工人代表会议  谴责美国危害世界和平

1958-07-20
第4版()
专栏：

    世界青年工人代表会议
    谴责美国危害世界和平
    新华社布拉格17日电　正在布拉格举行的世界青年工人代表会议的参加者17日一致通过了给联合国安理会主席和联合国秘书长的电报，愤怒地谴责美国军队在黎巴嫩登陆。
    电报说：“美国武装干涉黎巴嫩是对全世界和平的威胁，制造战争策源地，同时也粗暴地破坏了各国人民的自决权和粗暴地破坏了联合国宪章。”
    到会的人坚决抗议这种侵略并且要求安理会采取必要措施，使美国军队立即撤出黎巴嫩。
    新华社布拉格17日电　世界青年工人代表会议16日继续进行讨论。世界工会联合会总书记赛扬、阿拉伯联合共和国代表哈基姆、中国代表团团长韩西雅等人在会上谴责了美国武装干涉黎巴嫩的行动。
    赛扬在会上宣布伊拉克人民推翻了君主政体并宣布成立了伊拉克共和国，全场响起了热烈的掌声和欢呼。赛扬谈到美国在中东的军事干涉，指出这种行动严重威胁和平和破坏各国人民决定自己的未来的权利。
    哈基姆在发言中谈到了帝国主义者对阿拉伯各国的侵略阴谋。他抗议美国武装干涉黎巴嫩。他的发言受到代表们的热烈欢迎。
    韩西雅在下午的会议上发言。韩西雅代表中国青年工人热烈祝贺伊拉克人民的伟大胜利，坚决谴责美帝国主义武装侵略黎巴嫩的罪行。
