Title: Showing the Firm Will of All Ethnic Groups to Defend Peace, People in Hohhot and Other Places Hold Rallies and Demonstrations
Author: 
Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, July 23rd: Two hundred and fifty thousand people of all ethnic groups in Hohhot, the capital of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, held an unprecedented powerful demonstration parade on the evening of the 22nd, braving the rain and holding high torches, lanterns, red flags, and large banners to protest the war provocations of the US and British aggressors. People shouted slogans such as "US and British aggressor troops get out of the Middle East!" and "Support the anti-imperialist struggle of the Arab people!", once again demonstrating the firm will of all ethnic groups in Inner Mongolia to defend peace and stop aggression.

The male and female actors of the Beijing Pingju Opera Troupe, who had just arrived here to prepare for performances, and farmers of all ethnic groups from the fields at the southern foot of the Daqingshan Mountains, disregarded the rain and poured into the demonstration parade. In dozens of contingents composed of factories, organizations, schools, residents, and PLA officers and soldiers, people raised their fists and drove a group of clowns playing US and British pirates in the procession, while cheering and paying tribute to those dressed as Arab people. Clerks from many joint state-private shops in the city participated in the demonstration parade.

More than 10,000 workers and voluntary laborers of all ethnic groups who are rushing to repair the Wusutu large-scale reservoir in the suburbs of Hohhot also held a radio rally at the construction site on the afternoon of the 22nd to oppose US and British imperialism's aggression in the Middle East and defend world peace.

Xinhua News Agency, Hailar, July 23rd: The news of the armed aggression of the Middle East by US and British imperialism has angered the people of Hailar, a grassland city in Inner Mongolia. Fifteen thousand people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in the city, including Mongol, Han, Hui, Manchu, and Daur, held a rally and parade on the 20th to protest the towering crimes of US and British imperialism in undermining world peace, and unanimously expressed their determination to support the just struggle of the Arab people with practical actions to accelerate China's socialist construction and strengthen the unity of all ethnic groups. The representatives who spoke at the meeting unanimously demanded that US and British imperialism get out of the territories of Lebanon and Jordan.

All organs, troops, factories, and schools in Hulunbuir League and Hailar City universally held rallies on the 19th to denounce the gangster behavior of US and British imperialism.

Xinhua News Agency, Yakeshi, July 23rd: People of all ethnic groups and all walks of life in the Greater Khingan Mountains forest area of ​​Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region have held meetings in recent days to protest the crimes of US and British imperialism in invading the Middle East.

Employees of all organs under the Inner Mongolia Forestry Administration gathered on the evening of the 21st. They angrily shouted for the US and British aggressor troops to get out of Lebanon and Jordan. They expressed their firm stand with the Arab people and support for the Arab people's struggle for national independence.

People of all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Xiguitu Banner held a 12,000-person rally on the 22nd. Representatives of workers, farmers, women, students, medical personnel, and engineering technicians of all ethnic groups spoke at the meeting, condemning the crimes of the US and British aggressors. After the meeting, people of all ethnic groups also held a demonstration parade.

More than 7,000 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in the Xiniqi area also held rallies and parades on the 22nd.

### 显示各族人民保卫和平的坚强意志  呼和浩特等地人民纷纷集会游行示威

1958-07-24
第2版()
专栏：

    显示各族人民保卫和平的坚强意志
    呼和浩特等地人民纷纷集会游行示威
    新华社呼和浩特23日电　内蒙古自治区首府呼和浩特市各民族人民二十五万人，22日晚上冒雨高举火炬、灯笼、红旗和大幅标语，举行空前未有的强大示威游行，抗议美英侵略者的战争挑衅。人们高呼“美英侵略军滚出中东！”“支援阿拉伯人民的反帝斗争！”等口号，再一次显示了内蒙古各民族保卫和平制止侵略的坚强意志。
    刚刚到达这里准备上演的北京市评剧团的男女演员们和来自大青山南麓田野的各族农民，都不顾雨淋，涌入示威游行的队伍。在各工厂、机关、学校、居民和解放军官兵组成的几十个大队里，人们举起拳头，驱赶着队伍中扮演美英海盗的一群丑角，同时向着打扮成阿拉伯人民的人欢呼致敬。市内许多公私合营商店的店员，参加示威游行。
    正在呼和浩特市郊区抢修乌苏图大型水库的一万多各族民工和义务劳动者，22日下午也在工地上举行了反对美英帝国主义侵略中东、保卫世界和平广播大会。
    新华社海拉尔23日电　美英帝国主义武装侵略中东的消息，激怒了内蒙古草原城市海拉尔的人民。市内的蒙古、汉、回、满和达呼尔等各族各界一万五千人，20日举行集会游行，抗议美英帝国主义破坏世界和平的滔天罪行，一致表示要用加速我国社会主义建设和加强各民族团结的实际行动，来支持阿拉伯人民的正义斗争。在会上发言的代表们，一致要求美英帝国主义从黎巴嫩和约旦的领土上滚出去。
    呼伦贝尔盟和海拉尔市各机关、部队、工厂、学校19日都普遍举行集会，声讨美英帝国主义的强盗行为。
    新华社牙克石23日电　内蒙古自治区大兴安岭林区各族各界人民，连日来分别集会抗议美英帝国主义侵略中东的罪行。
    内蒙古林业管理局所属各机关的职工，21日晚上纷纷集会。他们愤怒地高呼美英侵略军从黎巴嫩、约旦滚出去。他们表示坚决站在阿拉伯人民一边，支持阿拉伯人民的民族独立斗争。
    喜桂图旗各族各界人民在22日举行了一万二千人的大会。各民族工人、农民、妇女、学生和医务、工程技术人员的代表，纷纷在会上发言，同声谴责美英侵略者的罪行。会后，各族人民还举行了游行示威。
    西尼气地区各族各界七千多人22日也举行了集会和游行。
