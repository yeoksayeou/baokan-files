Title: Displeased with Chamoun for Bringing in the Wolf, Lebanese Finance Minister Resigns from Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd, Beirut dispatch: Lebanese Finance Minister Pierre Eddé has resigned from the Solh cabinet. According to some sources, Eddé condemned Chamoun for "requesting" American intervention without first obtaining the consent of government members. Eddé has not attended cabinet meetings since the landing of the American invasion troops in Lebanon. Following his resignation from the cabinet, citing health reasons, his position as Finance Minister has been taken over by Planning Minister Joseph Chader.

Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd, Beirut dispatch: The American "New York Post" published a special dispatch from Beirut on the 18th, admitting that "opposition to the Marine landing is growing in the Lebanese parliament." Speaker Oueidat joined the movement in parliament protesting the landing of the US Marines. In the sixty-six-member parliament, thirty-six are on Oueidat's side.

### 不满夏蒙引狼入室  黎财政部长退出内阁

1958-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

    不满夏蒙引狼入室
    黎财政部长退出内阁
    新华社23日讯　贝鲁特消息：黎巴嫩财政部长皮埃尔·埃迪退出了索勒哈内阁。据某些人士透露，埃迪谴责夏蒙没有首先征得政府成员的同意就“请求”美国进行干涉。埃迪自从美国侵略军队在黎巴嫩登陆以后就不再参加内阁会议。在他以健康关系为理由退出内阁后，他的财政部长职务已由计划部长约瑟夫·沙德尔接替。
    据新华社23日讯　贝鲁特消息：美国“纽约邮报”在18日发表的来自贝鲁特的专电中承认黎巴嫩议会中“反对海军陆战队登陆的人日益增多”。议长奥塞兰参加了议会中抗议美国海军陆战队登陆的运动。在六十六名议员组成的议会中，有三十六人站在奥塞兰方面。
