Title: Jakarta's "Eastern Star" Newspaper Reveals: The United States Attempts to Buy Time to Expand Aggression
Author:
Date: 1958-07-31
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 30th. The "Eastern Star" newspaper in Jakarta, in criticizing the United States' repeated delays in holding a summit conference, pointed out today that the purpose of this American approach is to divert the attention of people of all countries around the world from its aggression in the Middle East. And more importantly, it attempts to buy time in order to strengthen its intervention in Lebanon.

The newspaper said that in the eyes of the United States, peace is merely other countries submitting to American interests.

"Eastern Star" said that although it is not yet time to despair of holding a summit conference, under the current circumstances, attention should be paid to the possibility that the United States' attitude may cause the summit conference to run aground.

### 雅加达“东星报”揭露  美国企图争取时间扩大侵略

1958-07-31
第2版()
专栏：

    雅加达“东星报”揭露
    美国企图争取时间扩大侵略
    新华社雅加达30日电　“东星报”今天在批评美国一再拖延举行政府首脑会议的行动时指出，美国这种做法的目的是在于转移全世界各国人民对它侵略中东的注意力，而且更重要的是，它企图争取时间，以便加强它对黎巴嫩进行的干涉。
    这家报纸说，在美国眼里，和平不过是要其他国家屈从美国的利益而已。
    “东星报”说，虽然目前还不是对召开政府首脑会议表示绝望的时候，但是，在目前情况下，应该注意美国的态度有可能使政府首脑会议触礁。
