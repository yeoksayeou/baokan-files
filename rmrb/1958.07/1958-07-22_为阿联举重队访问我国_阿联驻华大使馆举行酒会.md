Title: UAE Embassy in China Holds Reception for Visiting UAE Weightlifting Team
Author:
Date: 1958-07-22
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, July 21st - The Embassy of the United Arab Republic in China held a reception today for the visiting weightlifting team from the United Arab Republic.

    Ma Yuehan, Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, Huang Zhong, Vice Chairman, and Rong Gaotang, Deputy Director of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, were invited to attend the reception.

    Also invited to the reception were well-known Chinese weightlifters Li Baiyu, Chen Yongbo, and people from the sports circles in Beijing.

### 为阿联举重队访问我国  阿联驻华大使馆举行酒会

1958-07-22
第7版()
专栏：

    为阿联举重队访问我国
    阿联驻华大使馆举行酒会
    新华社21日讯  阿拉伯联合共和国驻中国大使馆今天为阿拉伯联合共和国举重队访问中国举行酒会。
    中华全国体育总会主席马约翰、副主席黄中，中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任荣高棠等，都应邀出席了酒会。
    应邀参加酒会的还有中国著名举重运动员李白玉、陈永博和北京体育界人士。
