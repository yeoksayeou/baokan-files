Title: Construction Workers Smile Broadly

Author: 
Date: 1958-07-17
Page: 4

    Construction Workers Smile Broadly
    The first time we turned over and seized power,
    The second time we conquered the mountain of science;
    Thanks to the Party's good leadership,
    Construction workers smile broadly.
       (Worker's Song)
