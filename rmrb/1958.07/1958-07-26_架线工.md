Title: Lineman
Author: Hu Wensheng
Date: 1958-07-26
Page: 6

    Lineman
    Coils of wire, dark and shiny,
    Stretched to the horizon, never-ending,
    The master is hailed as the old eagle,
    Soaring through the clouds, binding the Earth.
    Worker of the Tractor Factory, Hu Wensheng
