Title: Three-Story "Building" on the Loom (Picture)
Author: Zhao Cheng
Date: 1958-07-27
Page: 6

    Three-Story "Building" on the Loom
    Workers in the weaving workshop of the Shanghai Yong'an No. 1 Factory have "built" a three-story "building" on the loom, weaving three layers of cloth on one loom, increasing production by 200%. The left picture shows workers using the "three-story building" loom to weave cloth. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhao Cheng photographed this.

### 织布机上的三层“楼”（图片）
赵成
1958-07-27
第6版()
专栏：

    织布机上的三层“楼”
    上海永安一厂织布工场工人在织布机上“盖”起了三层“楼”，在一台织布机上织出了三层布，产量提高了200％。左图是工人正在使用“三层楼”布机织布的情形。　　　新华社记者　赵成摄
