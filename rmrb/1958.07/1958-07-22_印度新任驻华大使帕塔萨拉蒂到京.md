Title: Indian Newly Appointed Ambassador to China, Parthasarathi, Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1958-07-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 21st: The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of India to China, Mr. G. Parthasarathi, arrived in Beijing by train at noon today.

    Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Wenjin, Director of the Asian Department, were at the station to welcome him.

    Kannan Pillai, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indian Embassy in China, and embassy officials were also at the station to greet him.

    Some foreign envoys and diplomatic officials to China were also at the station to welcome him.

### 印度新任驻华大使帕塔萨拉蒂到京

1958-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    印度新任驻华大使帕塔萨拉蒂到京
    新华社21日讯  印度共和国新任驻华特命全权大使高·帕塔萨拉蒂先生在今天中午乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文和亚洲司司长章文晋。
    印度驻华大使馆临时代办康南皮利和大使馆官员也前往车站迎接。
    到车站欢迎的还有部分外国驻华使节和外交官员。
