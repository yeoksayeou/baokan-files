Title: Egyptian Labor Federation Passes Resolution: Resolutely Defend the Arab Homeland
Author:
Date: 1958-07-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 20th: The Egyptian Labor Federation stated on the 20th that it supports the agreement on implementing joint defense signed by the United Arab Republic and the Republic of Iraq.

A resolution passed by the Executive Committee of the Egyptian Labor Federation stated that Egyptian workers are prepared to take up arms at any time to defend the Arab homeland in any part of the Arab world.

### 埃及劳工联合会通过决议  坚决保卫阿拉伯家园

1958-07-22
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及劳工联合会通过决议
    坚决保卫阿拉伯家园
    新华社开罗20日电  埃及劳工联合会20日表示拥护阿拉伯联合共和国和伊拉克共和国签订的关于实行共同防御的协定。
    埃及劳工联合会执行委员会通过的一项决议说，埃及劳动者准备随时拿起武器保卫阿拉伯世界任何一个地方的阿拉伯家园。
