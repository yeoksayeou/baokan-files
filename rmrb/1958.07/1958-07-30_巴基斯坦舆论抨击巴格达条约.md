Title: Pakistani Public Opinion Criticizes the Baghdad Pact
Author:
Date: 1958-07-30
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, July 28th: Some Pakistani newspapers, commenting on the Baghdad Pact Council meeting in London, pointed out that holding this meeting was like staging Shakespeare's famous play "Hamlet" without the Prince of Denmark.
    The Urdu-language "Today's News" published in Lahore said that, with Iraq gone, none of the remaining member states of the Baghdad Pact are Arab countries. Western countries once claimed that the purpose of the Baghdad Pact was to "defend" the interests of Arab countries, but the only Arab country among the members, Iraq, has now left the pact.

### 巴基斯坦舆论抨击巴格达条约

1958-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦舆论抨击巴格达条约
    据新华社卡拉奇28日电　巴基斯坦的一些报纸在评论在伦敦召开的巴格达条约理事会会议时指出，召开这次会议就像上演没有丹麦王子（哈姆雷特）的莎士比亚名剧“哈姆雷特”一样。
    在拉合尔出版的乌尔都文“今天报”说，少掉伊拉克以后，巴格达条约剩下的所有成员国都不是阿拉伯国家了。西方国家曾经宣称，巴格达条约的目的是“保卫”阿拉伯国家的利益，但是成员国中唯一的阿拉伯国家伊拉克目前已经脱离了这项条约。
