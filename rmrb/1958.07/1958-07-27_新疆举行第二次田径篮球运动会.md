Title: Xinjiang Holds Second Track and Field Basketball Games
Author:
Date: 1958-07-27
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency report:

    Xinjiang Holds Second Track and Field Basketball Games
    According to Xinhua News Agency, the Second Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region Track and Field and Basketball Games were recently held in Urumqi. This is the largest sports event in Xinjiang's history. A total of 684 athletes participated, including Han, Uyghur, Kazakh, Mongolian, Kyrgyz, and eleven other ethnic groups. Athletes from ethnic minorities accounted for more than 60% of the total number of athletes.
    During the games, twenty records broke Xinjiang's records from last year, and twenty-nine second-class athletes and one hundred and fifty third-class athletes emerged. Athletes from various ethnic minorities were exceptionally active. They participated in races, all-around events, basketball, and other events that they rarely participated in before, and achieved excellent results in many events. Ten male and female ethnic minority athletes broke four autonomous region records from 1957.

### 新疆举行第二次田径篮球运动会

1958-07-27
第7版()
专栏：体育新闻

    新疆举行第二次田径篮球运动会
    据新华社讯　新疆维吾尔自治区第二次田径、篮球运动大会最近在乌鲁木齐市举行。这是新疆有史以来规模最大的一次运动会。参加的共有六百八十四名运动员，包括汉、维吾尔、哈萨克、蒙古、柯尔克孜等十一个民族。少数民族的运动员占运动员总人数的60％以上。
    运动会上有二十项纪录打破了新疆去年的纪录，出现了二十九名二级运动员和一百五十名三级运动员。各少数民族运动员显得异常活跃，他们参加了以往很少参加的竞走、全能、篮球等项目的比赛，在不少项目中获得了优异成绩。有十个男、女少数民族运动员，分别打破了1957年自治区的四项纪录。
