Title: Chongqing Religious Circles Firmly Demand the U.S. and Britain Get Out of the Middle East

Author: 
Date: 1958-07-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, July 19th: Religious figures in Chongqing have held gatherings in recent days to protest the armed intervention in the internal affairs of the Middle Eastern people by U.S. and British imperialism.

    At a meeting of Islamic representatives, Ding Qishan, the head of the Chongqing Islamic Association, spoke on behalf of all Muslims in the city to indignantly protest the aggression of U.S. and British imperialism against Lebanon and Jordan. He firmly demanded that the U.S. and British imperialist troops immediately get out of the Middle East. Vice President Huang Xianming said in his speech that he would fully support the just struggle of the Middle Eastern people.

    At a meeting of Christian figures, Rev. Li Muqun, Chairman of the Chongqing Christian Three-Self Patriotic Movement Committee, pointed out that if U.S. and British imperialism dare to play with fire in the Middle East, the Middle East will become the graveyard of U.S. and British imperialism. More than twenty representatives spoke at this meeting, and they firmly believed that with the support of peace-loving people all over the world, the struggle of the Middle Eastern people will surely be victorious.

    All the clergy and some members of the Chongqing Catholic Patriotic Association also held a meeting to protest the aggression of U.S. and British imperialism.

### 重庆宗教界坚决要美英从中东滚出去

1958-07-20
第3版()
专栏：

    重庆宗教界坚决要美英从中东滚出去
    新华社重庆19日电　重庆市宗教界人士连日来分别举行集会，抗议美、英帝国主义武装干涉中东人民的内政。
    在伊斯兰教代表人士的集会上，重庆市伊斯兰教教长丁岐山代表全市穆斯林愤慨抗议美、英帝国主义对黎巴嫩、约旦的侵略，他坚决要美英帝国主义军队立即从中东滚出去。副教长黄显名在发言中表示要全力支援中东人民的正义斗争。
    在基督教人士的集会上，重庆市基督教三自爱国运动委员会主席李牧群牧师指出，美、英帝国主义胆敢在中东玩火，中东就将成为美、英帝国主义的坟墓。这个集会上有二十多名代表发了言，他们深信在全世界爱好和平人民的声援下，中东人民的斗争一定会得到胜利。
    重庆市天主教友爱国会的全体神职人员和部分教友也举行了集会，抗议美、英帝国主义的侵略行动。
