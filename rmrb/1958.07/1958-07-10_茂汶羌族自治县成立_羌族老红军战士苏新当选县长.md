Title: Maowen Qiang Autonomous County Established; Qiang Veteran Red Army Soldier Su Xin Elected County Head
Author:
Date: 1958-07-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, July 9th. In the eastern part of the Aba Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture in Sichuan Province, the only Qiang ethnic group settlement area in China, ethnic regional autonomy has been realized. On July 7th, the Maowen Qiang Autonomous County was officially established.

The first session of the Autonomous County's first People's Congress elected Su Xin, a Red Army soldier from that year and an outstanding son of the Qiang people, as County Head, and Ma Fushou (Qiang ethnicity) and Yuan Yongxi (Han ethnicity) as Deputy County Heads. The People's Committee includes committee members from the Qiang, Han, Tibetan, and Hui ethnic groups of the Autonomous County.

In the past few days, Weizhou City, where the Autonomous County People's Committee is located, has been decorated with lanterns and streamers, filled with joy. The 141 representatives of all ethnic groups in the county attending the meeting discussed the government work reports of the original Mao County and Wenchuan County, and formulated the county's economic and cultural construction plan from 1958 to 1962. The representatives unanimously stated that under the leadership of the Party, they would further strengthen ethnic unity, implement the general line, and build the Autonomous County into a beautiful socialist mountainous area.

The Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and various autonomous prefectures of Sichuan Province sent representatives to the meeting to offer congratulations. The Sichuan Provincial People's Committee, all special administrative offices in the province, and more than fifty other units sent congratulatory telegrams, messages, or gifts.

### 茂汶羌族自治县成立  羌族老红军战士苏新当选县长

1958-07-10
第4版()
专栏：

    茂汶羌族自治县成立
    羌族老红军战士苏新当选县长
    新华社成都9日电  在我国唯一的羌族聚居区——四川省阿坝藏族自治州东部，实现了民族区域自治。7月7日，茂汶羌族自治县正式宣告成立。
    自治县的首届人民代表大会第一次会议，选出了当年的红军战士、羌族人民优秀的子弟苏新为县长，马福寿（羌族）、袁永喜（汉族）为副县长。人民委员会中包括了自治县的羌、汉、藏、回各族委员。
    这几天来，自治县人民委员会所在的威州城，张灯结彩，喜气洋洋。出席会议的全县一百四十一名各族代表讨论了原茂县、汶川两县的政府工作报告，制订了全县从1958年到1962年的经济、文化建设规划。代表们一致表示：在党的领导下，进一步加强民族团结，贯彻总路线，把自治县建设成为社会主义的美丽山区。
    中共四川省委、四川省各自治州都派代表到会祝贺。四川省人民委员会、全省各专署等五十多个单位送了贺电、贺词或贺礼。
