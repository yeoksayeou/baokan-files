Title: Australian Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Publicly Support US-UK Aggression in the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1958-07-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Canberra, 19th: Australian Prime Minister Menzies and Foreign Minister Casey jointly issued a communique on the 18th, publicly expressing support for the US and UK aggression in the Middle East.

### 澳总理和外长  公然支持美英侵略中东

1958-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    澳总理和外长
    公然支持美英侵略中东
    新华社19日讯　堪培拉消息：澳大利亚总理孟席斯和外交部长凯西在18日联合发表公报，公然表示支持美英两国侵略中东的行动。
