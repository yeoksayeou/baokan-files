Title: Japanese People's Peace March Reaches Osaka from Hiroshima
Author:
Date: 1958-07-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 10th - The Japanese people's 1,000-kilometer peace march, which departed from Hiroshima on June 20th, arrived in Osaka City at noon on the 9th. So far, this peace army has completed 400 kilometers of its journey, and they are scheduled to arrive in Tokyo on August 12th.

This march is an important activity of the "Month to Stop Nuclear Armament of Japan and Uphold Democracy."

Upon arriving in Osaka, the marching contingent immediately attended the "Osaka Youth General Uprising Rally to Ban Nuclear Weapons, Stop the Performance Evaluation System, and Demand the Restoration of Sino-Japanese Diplomatic Relations," which was being held in Osaka's Nakanoshima Park with over 1,000 participants. After the rally, these peace-loving Japanese people also held a demonstration march in Osaka.

On their way from Hiroshima to Osaka, the peace march was welcomed by representatives from all walks of life and atomic bomb survivors in various counties, cities, towns, and villages. (Attached photo)

### 日本人民和平大军已从广岛走到大阪

1958-07-12
第6版()
专栏：

    日本人民和平大军已从广岛走到大阪
    据新华社10日讯　东京消息：6月20日从广岛出发的日本人民一千公里和平大游行的队伍在9日中午到达大阪市。至此，这支和平大军已经走完了四百公里的路程，他们预定在8月12日到达东京。
    这次大游行是“阻止核武装日本与维护民主月”的一项重要活动。
    游行队伍到达大阪以后，立即出席了正在大阪中之岛公园举行的有一千多人参加的“禁止核武器、阻止勤务评定制度、要求恢复日中邦交大阪青年总奋起大会”。大会结束以后，这些爱好和平的日本人民还在大阪市内举行了示威游行。
    和平游行队伍在从广岛到大阪的途中，受到各县、市、镇、乡的各界代表和原子弹受害者的夹道欢迎。（附图片）
