Title: China and Cambodia Establish Diplomatic Relations; Premier Zhou and Prince Sihanouk Exchange Congratulations; Foreign Minister Chen Yi Sends Telegram of Congratulations to Cambodian Acting Foreign Minister Mau Say
Author:
Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 24th—Joint Communiqué of the Governments of the People’s Republic of China and the Kingdom of Cambodia
    In order to realize the common desire of both parties to establish diplomatic relations and exchange diplomatic envoys, the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Kingdom of Cambodia have decided to mutually dispatch ambassadors and establish embassies.

    Xinhua News Agency, July 24th—Exchange of letters between Premier Zhou Enlai and Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, on the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Cambodia.

    Premier Zhou Enlai's reply to Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia:
    Your Royal Highness Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia:
    I am honored to receive Your Highness' notification regarding the decision of the Cambodian Cabinet meeting on July 17, 1958, to legally recognize the Government of the People's Republic of China. On behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China, I would like to express my sincere welcome to this friendly decision by your government. The Chinese government also fully agrees with your government's proposal to establish normal diplomatic relations at the ambassadorial level between our two countries. I firmly believe that the friendship between our two peoples, which has been continuously developing since the Asian-African Conference, will be further consolidated by this significant step, thereby benefiting the common cause of promoting friendly cooperation among Asian-African countries and safeguarding world peace.
    In the name of Chairman Mao Zedong, the Chinese people, and myself, I warmly wish His Majesty King Norodom Suramarit and Queen long health and longevity, the happiness of the Cambodian people, and the prosperity of the Kingdom of Cambodia.
    Please accept, Your Royal Highness, my highest respect and best wishes.
                                  Zhou Enlai (signed)
                                  Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                                  July 19, 1958, in Beijing

    Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, to Premier Zhou Enlai:
    To His Excellency Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Beijing
    Mr. Prime Minister:
    The government of the Kingdom of Cambodia, at its cabinet meeting on July 17, 1958, decided to legally recognize the Government of the People's Republic of China.
    As Prime Minister, I have the honor and great pleasure of requesting you to convey this legal recognition of the Kingdom to your government. I also, on behalf of His Majesty the King of Cambodia, the Cambodian people, and myself, warmly wish His Excellency Chairman Mao Zedong health and longevity, the happiness of the Chinese people, and the prosperity of our friendly nation, China.
    With sincere and friendly regards, please accept, Mr. Prime Minister, my highest respect.
                                  Norodom Sihanouk (signed)
                                  July 18, 1958, in Phnom Penh
    Attachment:
    The Government of the Kingdom of Cambodia has the honor to suggest to the Government of the People's Republic of China the establishment of normal diplomatic relations at the ambassadorial level between the two countries.

                                  Prime Minister Norodom Sihanouk (signed)
                                  Acting Foreign Minister Mau Say (countersigned)
                                  July 18, 1958, in Phnom Penh

    Xinhua News Agency, July 24th—Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to Mau Say, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Cambodia, on July 23, congratulating him on the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Cambodia. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Mau Say, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Government of the Kingdom of Cambodia:
    On the occasion of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Cambodia, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency. May the friendly relations between China and Cambodia be increasingly consolidated and developed.

                                  Chen Yi
                                  Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
                                  July 23, 1958

### 中柬建立外交关系  周总理和西哈努克亲王互致祝贺  陈毅外长电贺柬代理外交大臣毛赛

1958-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

    中柬建立外交关系
    周总理和西哈努克亲王互致祝贺
    陈毅外长电贺柬代理外交大臣毛赛
    新华社24日讯　中华人民共和国政府和柬埔寨王国政府联合公报
    中华人民共和国政府和柬埔寨王国政府为了实现双方关于建立外交关系和互换外交使节的共同愿望，决定互派大使并且互设大使馆。
    新华社24日讯　周恩来总理同柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王关于中柬建立外交关系问题的来往信件。周恩来总理给柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王的复信柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王殿下：
    我荣幸地接到殿下关于贵国内阁会议于1958年7月17日决定在法律上承认中华人民共和国政府的通知。对于贵国政府这一友好的决定，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府表示热诚的欢迎。中国政府并且完全同意贵国政府关于我们两国之间建立大使级的正常外交关系的建议。我深信，自从亚非会议以来不断得到新的发展的我们两国人民之间的友谊，将由于这一重大步骤而得到进一步的巩固，并从而有利于促进亚非国家的友好合作和维护世界和平的共同事业。
    我谨以毛泽东主席、中国人民和我个人的名义，热烈地祝贺诺罗敦·苏拉马里特国王和王后陛下健康和长寿，祝柬埔寨人民的幸福和柬埔寨王国的繁荣。
    亲王殿下，请接受我对你的最崇高的敬意和最良好的祝愿。
                  中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来（签署）
                                      1958年7月19日于北京柬埔寨王国首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王给周恩来总理的来信北京中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来阁下总理先生：
    柬埔寨王国政府于1958年7月17日的内阁会议上决定在法律上承认中华人民共和国政府。
    我以首相的身份荣幸地和很高兴地请你将王国此项法律上的承认转告你的政府。我并以柬埔寨国王陛下、柬埔寨人民和我个人的名义热烈地祝毛泽东主席先生阁下健康和长寿，祝中国人民幸福和中国友邦的繁荣。
    怀着诚挚和友好的回忆，我请你，总理先生，接受我最崇高的敬意。
                                  诺罗敦·西哈努克（签署）
                                      1958年7月18日于金边
    附件：
    柬埔寨王国政府荣幸地向中华人民共和国政府建议建立两国之间的大使级的正常外交关系。
                              首相诺罗敦·西哈努克（签署）
                                代理外交大臣　毛赛（副署）
                                      1958年7月18日于金边
    新华社24日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅23日致电柬埔寨王国政府代理外交大臣毛赛，祝贺中柬两国建立外交关系。电文如下：柬埔寨王国政府代理外交大臣毛赛阁下：
    值此中柬两国建立外交关系之际，我谨向阁下致以热烈的祝贺。祝中柬两国间的友好关系日益巩固和发展。
                         中华人民共和国外交部长　陈　毅
  　1958年7月23日
