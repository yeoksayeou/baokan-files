Title: Morocco Recognizes the Republic of Iraq; Personnel of the Iraqi Embassy in Turkey Announce Allegiance to the New Government
Author:
Date: 1958-07-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, July 26th: Rabat dispatch: The special cabinet meeting held on the 25th, personally presided over by King Mohammed V, entrusted Moroccan Prime Minister Bela Frej to send a telegram notifying the Iraqi government that the Moroccan government has decided to officially recognize the Republic of Iraq.
    Xinhua News Agency, July 26th: Ankara dispatch: A senior official of the Iraqi Embassy in Turkey announced that the personnel of this embassy are loyal to the government of the Republic of Iraq.

### 摩洛哥承认伊拉克共和国  伊驻土大使馆人员宣布效忠新政府

1958-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥承认伊拉克共和国
    伊驻土大使馆人员宣布效忠新政府
    新华社26日讯　拉巴特消息：25日举行的由国王穆罕默德五世亲自主持的内阁特别会议委托摩洛哥首相贝拉弗里杰打电报通知伊拉克政府，摩洛哥政府已经决定正式承认伊拉克共和国。
    新华社26日讯　安卡拉消息：伊拉克驻土耳其大使馆的一位高级官员宣布，这个大使馆的人员效忠伊拉克共和国政府。
