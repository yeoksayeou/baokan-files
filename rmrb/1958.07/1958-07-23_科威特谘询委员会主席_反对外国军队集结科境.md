Title: Chairman of the Kuwaiti Consultative Council Opposes the Gathering of Foreign Troops in Kuwait

Author:

Date: 1958-07-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd: According to a Reuters reporter from Cairo, the Chairman of the Kuwaiti Emir's Consultative Council, Muhammad Sulaiman Onaibi, said in Cairo on the 21st, "Kuwait opposes the gathering of foreign troops in Kuwait." He emphasized: "The Kuwaiti army is capable of defending its own oil."

Onaibi commented on the American military's invasion of Lebanon, saying: The American action "is a political mistake, similar to the British military's mistake in attacking Egypt for the Suez Canal."

He also said: "We have friendly and fraternal relations with the Republic of Iraq."

Kuwait is adjacent to southeastern Iraq. It is the largest oil-producing country in the Middle East, and more than half of Britain's oil is imported from Kuwait. News indicates that Britain is concentrating troops around Iraq in an attempt to occupy Kuwait as a military springboard for attacking Iraq.

It is reported that the Emir of Kuwait, Salim Al-Sabah, recently held talks with President Nasser in Damascus on the situation in the Middle East.

### 科威特谘询委员会主席  反对外国军队集结科境

1958-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    科威特谘询委员会主席
    反对外国军队集结科境
    新华社22日讯  据路透社记者自开罗报道，科威特酋长的谘询委员会主席穆罕默德·苏莱曼·奥奈比21日在开罗说，“科威特反对外国军队在科威特集结。”他强调说：“科威特军队能够自己保卫石油”。
    奥奈比评论美国军队侵略黎巴嫩这件事说：美国的行动“是一个政治错误，同英国军队为了苏伊士运河而进攻埃及的错误相似”。
    他又说：“我们同伊拉克共和国有友好的和兄弟的关系”。
    科威特同伊拉克的东南部毗邻。它是中东地区产石油最多的国家，英国的石油有一半以上是从科威特输入的。有消息说，英国正在向伊拉克周围集中军队，企图侵占科威特作为进攻伊拉克的军事跳板。
    据报道，科威特酋长萨利姆·阿萨巴最近曾经在大马士革就中东局势同纳赛尔总统举行了会谈。
