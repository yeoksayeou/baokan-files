Title: Morocco Bans Entry of French Military Personnel
Author: 
Date: 1958-07-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, July 5th: The Moroccan government announced on the 4th that it would prohibit all French military personnel coming from France from entering the country. The Moroccan government has notified the French Embassy in Morocco of this decision.

According to this decision, French military personnel in both military and civilian clothing are prohibited from entering the country, including French military personnel returning to Morocco after taking leave in France.

### 摩洛哥禁止法军人入境

1958-07-07
第6版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥禁止法军人入境
    据新华社5日讯　拉巴特消息：摩洛哥政府4日宣布，禁止一切从法国来的法国军事人员入境。摩洛哥政府已经把这项决定通知法国驻摩洛哥大使馆。
    根据这项决定，穿军服和穿平民服装的法军人员都在禁止入境之列，其中包括从法国休假以后重新回到摩洛哥的法国军事人员。
