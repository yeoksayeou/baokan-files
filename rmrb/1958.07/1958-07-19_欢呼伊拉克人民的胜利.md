Title: Hurray for the Victory of the Iraqi People
Author: Wang Yafan
Date: 1958-07-19
Page: 8

    Hurray for the Victory of the Iraqi People
    Wang Yafan
    The July sun is scorching hot,
    Our hearts are surging and boiling,
    All the Chinese people are cheering
    The birth of the Republic of Iraq.
    Descendants of the creators of Babylonian civilization,
    Today you bravely stand up,
    Dispelling the clouds over Mesopotamia,
    Bringing liberation and light to your nation.
    This is an era when the people are masters of their own affairs,
    The east wind has prevailed over the west wind.
    Smashing the shackles of imperialism to pieces,
    We cheer the awakening of the Arab nation.
    On the advancing streets,
    You spit on the corpses of the traitors,
    Even the old fox disguised as a woman
    Cannot escape the people's grasp.
    We Chinese people understand
    Never show mercy to the enemy,
    Strike them hard.
    The American paper tiger cannot scare the fighting people.
    We once used the floods of the Yellow River and the Yangtze River
    To wash away the humiliation they brought us.
    Now we use the leaping roar of the Three Mountains and Five Peaks
    To support your just struggle.
    The July sun is scorching hot,
    Our hearts are surging and boiling,
    All the Chinese people are cheering
    The birth of the Republic of Iraq.
