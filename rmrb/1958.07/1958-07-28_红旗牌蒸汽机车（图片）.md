Title: Red Flag Brand Steam Locomotive (Picture)
Author: Jiang Guoxian
Date: 1958-07-28
Page: 6

Column: Create More and Better New Products

    Red Flag Brand Steam Locomotive
    A Red Flag brand small steam locomotive suitable for use in counties, townships, and factories and mines has recently been successfully trial-produced at the Baoji Engineering Machinery Repair Factory. This locomotive has 60 horsepower. On flat terrain, it can pull up to 100 tons at a time, with a maximum speed of 30 kilometers per hour, and it only burns more than 200 kilograms of coal per day.
            Xinhua News Agency Reporter Jiang Guoxian Photo
