Title: The U.S. and British Aggressors Lifted a Rock Only to Drop it on Their Own Feet

Author:

Date: 1958-07-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The U.S. and British aggression in the Middle East has aroused the stern condemnation of just public opinion throughout the world. Even in Western countries, this gunboat diplomacy is extremely unpopular. Many Western newspapers and political figures have criticized the aggressive actions of the U.S. and British governments. Some newspapers, while expressing anxiety about the national independence movements of the Middle Eastern people, also believe that armed intervention cannot save the failure of colonialism.

### 美英侵略者搬起石头砸了脚

1958-07-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　美英侵略者搬起石头砸了脚
    本报综合报道  据新华社消息：美英两国对中东的侵略行动已经引起了全世界正义舆论的严正谴责。甚至在西方国家内，这种炮舰外交政策也是极其不得人心的。西方国家的很多报刊和政界人士纷纷抨击美英政府的侵略行动。有些报纸虽然对中东人民的民族独立运动表示不安，但也认为武装干涉不能挽救殖民主义的失败。
