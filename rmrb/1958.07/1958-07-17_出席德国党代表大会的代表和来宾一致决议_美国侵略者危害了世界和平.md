Title: Delegates and Guests Attending the German Party Congress Unanimously Resolve: American Aggressors Endanger World Peace
Author:
Date: 1958-07-17
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 16th: The delegates and guests attending the Fifth Congress of the German Socialist Unity Party today unanimously adopted a resolution strongly condemning the United States for violating international law and invading Lebanon.

The resolution stated that the participants of the meeting learned of the American invasion of Lebanon with great indignation.

The resolution pointed out that this invasion is a blatant interference in Lebanon's internal affairs. The landing of American troops in Lebanon is an aggression against the Arab people who are striving for freedom and independence, a gross violation of the United Nations Charter, and an act endangering world peace.

### 出席德国党代表大会的代表和来宾一致决议  美国侵略者危害了世界和平

1958-07-17
第2版()
专栏：

    出席德国党代表大会的代表和来宾一致决议
    美国侵略者危害了世界和平
    新华社柏林16日电　德国统一社会党第五次代表大会全体代表和应邀参加大会的来宾今天一致通过了一项决议，严正地谴责美国违反国际公法进犯黎巴嫩的罪行。
    决议说，会议参加者以巨大的愤慨获悉了美国进犯黎巴嫩的消息。
    决议指出，这种进犯是对黎巴嫩内政的粗暴干涉。美军在黎巴嫩登陆是对正在争取自由和独立的阿拉伯人民的侵略，是粗暴违反联合国宪章和危害世界和平的行为。
