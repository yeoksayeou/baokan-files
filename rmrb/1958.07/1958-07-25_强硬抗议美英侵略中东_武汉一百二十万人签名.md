Title: Strong Protest Against US-British Aggression in the Middle East: 1.2 Million Signatures in Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1958-07-25
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, July 24th: A signature campaign against the armed aggression of the US-British imperialists in the Middle East has been fully launched in Wuhan. According to statistics from relevant departments, more than 1.2 million people in the city, from government offices and factories to schools and streets, have signed their names to strongly protest against the armed aggression of the US-British imperialists in the Middle East. In recent days, thousands upon thousands of big-character posters, protest letters, and denunciations with signatures have been sent to the Wuhan branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and grassroots street organizations. Wherever you go in any alley, you can see people vying to sign their names.

The signature campaign in Wuhan began on July 18th. At residents' group meetings of dozens of people or mass rallies of more than 10,000 people, white-haired elderly people, mothers with many children, and emotionally激愤 youth all angrily wrote their names on the protest letters and denunciations.

### 强硬抗议美英侵略中东  武汉一百二十万人签名

1958-07-25
第2版()
专栏：

    强硬抗议美英侵略中东
    武汉一百二十万人签名
    新华社武汉24日电　反对美英帝国主义武装侵略中东的签名运动已经在武汉市全面展开。据有关部门统计，全市从机关、工厂到学校、街道，已有一百二十多万人签名强硬抗议美英帝国主义武装侵略中东。连日来，千千万万张签了名的大字报、抗议书、声讨书，送到了中国人民保卫世界和平委员会武汉分会和基层街道组织。随便走到那条里弄，都可以看到人们争相签名的情景。
    武汉市人民从18日开始了签名运动。在几十人的居民小组会上或者一万多人的群众集会上，白发苍苍的老人、多子女的母亲和情绪激愤的青年，都愤怒地在抗议书、声讨书上写下了自己的名字。
