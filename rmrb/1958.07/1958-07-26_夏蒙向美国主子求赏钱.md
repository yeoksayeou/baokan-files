Title: Chamoun Asks American Master for Reward Money
Author:
Date: 1958-07-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 24th. The Iraqi Republic Radio quoted Lebanese newspapers on the 24th as saying that the Chamoun regime has requested the United States to provide another 30 million US dollars to make up for its deficit.
The radio also said that American officials in Beirut are discussing this issue with the Chamoun regime's Minister of Planning and Acting Minister of Finance, Shadel.

### 夏蒙向美国主子求赏钱

1958-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

    夏蒙向美国主子求赏钱
    新华社大马士革24日电　伊拉克共和国电台援引24日黎巴嫩报纸的消息说，夏蒙政权已经要求美国再给予三千万美元来弥补它的赤字。
    电台又说，贝鲁特的美国官员们正在同夏蒙政权的计划部长兼代理财政部长沙德尔讨论这个问题。
