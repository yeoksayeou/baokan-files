Title: Appeal

Author:

Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 5

    Appeal

    The Conference on Disarmament and International Cooperation is being held in Stockholm at a tense and tragic moment. The unwarranted military intervention by the United States and the United Kingdom in the Middle East has placed the world on the brink of nuclear war. The forces of peace are increasingly raising the same demands that this conference seeks to express.

    Therefore, we issue a solemn appeal to the people and governments of all countries:

    Foreign troops must be withdrawn from the Middle East immediately to end armed intervention in the Middle East.

    A summit conference must be convened to make it possible to take the first step on the path of international cooperation.

    Nuclear weapons testing must be stopped, paving the way for universal and supervised disarmament, which will enable the resources now used for war preparations to be used to develop the economy and improve people's living standards.

    Governments must give people the right to independence and freedom, and to manage their own resources and control their own destiny autonomously.

    We call on the forces of peace to eliminate their differences and unite. The task of the forces of peace is to awaken and mobilize public opinion so that the people participate in the action for peace.

    In this way, humanity, finally freed from nuclear threat and nuclear terror, can build a reliable future in a peaceful environment.

### 呼吁书

1958-07-24
第5版()
专栏：

    呼吁书
    裁军和国际合作大会是在一个紧张和悲剧性的时刻在斯德哥尔摩举行的。美国和英国在中东的无端的军事干涉行为使全世界面临着核战争的威胁。和平的力量今天越来越多地提出了同本大会寻求表达的同样的要求。
    因此我们向各国人民和政府发出庄严的呼吁：
    中东的外国军队必须立即撤出以结束对中东的武装干涉行为。
    最高级会议必须召开，使得在国际合作的道路上迈开第一步成为可能。
    核武器试验必须停止，为普遍的和有监督的裁军开辟道路，这种裁军将使得现在用于战争准备的资源能够用来发展经济和提高人民生活水平。
    各国政府必须给人民以独立和自由以及自主地处理自己的资源和掌握自己的命运的权利。
    我们要求和平力量消除它们的分歧，团结起来。和平力量的任务就是唤醒和动员舆论，以便使人民参加争取和平的行动。
    这样，终于从核威胁和核恐怖中解脱出来的人类才可以在和平的环境中建立一个可靠的未来。
