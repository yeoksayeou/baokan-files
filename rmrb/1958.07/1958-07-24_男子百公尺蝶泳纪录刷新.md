Title: Men's 100-meter Butterfly Record Broken
Author:
Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, July 22nd. Chen Yunpeng, an athlete from the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy swimming team, broke the national record of 1 minute 6.1 seconds for the men's 100-meter butterfly with a time of 1 minute 5.9 seconds during a swimming test in Shanghai's Xincheng District on the 20th.

### 男子百公尺蝶泳纪录刷新

1958-07-24
第7版()
专栏：体育新闻

    男子百公尺蝶泳纪录刷新
    据新华社上海22日电　中国人民解放军海军游泳队运动员陈运鹏，20日参加上海新成区游泳测验时，以一分五秒九的成绩打破了一分六秒一的百公尺蝶泳全国纪录。
