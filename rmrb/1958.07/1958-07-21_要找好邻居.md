Title: Seek Good Neighbors

Author: 
Date: 1958-07-21
Page: 8

To find a companion, do so before setting out;
To build a house, do so by finding good neighbors.
The donkey sought horns, and in the end lost its ears.
    --Arab Proverb

### 要找好邻居

1958-07-21
第8版()
专栏：

    要找好邻居
    动身之前，要找好伴侣；
    盖屋之前，要找好邻居。
    驴子去寻找牛角，结果丢掉了耳朵。
      ——阿拉伯谚语
