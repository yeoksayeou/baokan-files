Title: Ceylonese Prime Minister and Burmese Government Demand Withdrawal of US and British Troops from the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd: According to a TASS report from Colombo on July 22nd: The "Ceylon Daily News" reports that Ceylonese Prime Minister Bandaranaike has sent telegrams to UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld and the President of the United Arab Republic, Nasser.

In his telegram to Hammarskjöld, Bandaranaike said that the people of Ceylon condemn the Anglo-American aggression against the Arab East and demand the withdrawal of foreign troops from the region.

In his telegram to Nasser, he assured that the people of Ceylon support the national liberation movements of the countries of the Near and Middle East.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, July 22nd: The government of the Union of Burma issued a statement on the evening of July 22nd regarding the situation in the Middle East, saying that the government of the Union of Burma requests the United Nations to intensify its efforts to find a way to rapidly withdraw foreign troops from the Middle East, so that stability can be restored to the region.

The statement said that, in light of the background of the current situation in the Middle East, and especially the forces at play in that region, the government of the Union of Burma believes that the entry of foreign troops into the region will only create more problems than it can solve. The government of the Union of Burma also believes that as the length of stay of foreign troops increases, these problems will become more complex and difficult.

The statement said that the government of the Union of Burma has watched the recent developments in the Middle East with great concern. The government believes that the international situation has reached such a serious and tense level that any wrong step taken at this moment could trigger the kind of conflict that the whole world fears.

### 锡兰总理和缅甸政府  要美英军队从中东撤出

1958-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰总理和缅甸政府
    要美英军队从中东撤出
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社科伦坡22日讯：据“锡兰每日新闻”报道，锡兰总理班达拉奈克打电报给联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德和阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔。
    班达拉奈克在给哈马舍尔德的电报中说，锡兰人民谴责英美侵略阿拉伯东方，并且要求外国军队撤出这个地区。
    他在给纳赛尔的电报中保证说，锡兰人民支持中近东各国人民的民族解放运动。
    新华社仰光22日电　缅甸联邦政府在22日晚上就中东局势发表的声明说，缅甸联邦政府要求联合国加紧努力找出某种办法，使中东的外国军队迅速撤退，使那个地区可以恢复稳定。
    声明说，从中东当前局势的背景、特别是在那个地区起作用的力量看来，缅甸联邦政府认为，外国军队进入这个地区，只会制造出比它所能解决的更多问题。缅甸联邦政府并且认为，随着外国军队停留的时间的延长，这些问题就会变得更加复杂和困难。
    声明说，缅甸联邦政府以十分关注的心情注视了最近中东局势的发展。政府认为，国际局势已经到了这样严重和紧张的程度，在这个时刻采取的任何错误步骤都有可能引起全世界所害怕的那种冲突。
