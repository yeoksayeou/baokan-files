Typhoon Warning

    (Our report) Beijing, July 14, 1958. The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued an urgent typhoon warning at 8:00 PM on July 14th. At 2:00 PM on July 14th, the typhoon in the Pacific Ocean was centered near 21 degrees North latitude and 126 degrees East longitude. Currently, the typhoon center is moving northwest at a speed of 7-9 knots per hour. It is expected that within the next 24 hours, the wind force in the southern East China Sea, the northeastern South China Sea, and the coastal areas of Fujian will increase to level 7-9, and the wind force in the Taiwan Strait will increase to level 8-10. The typhoon is expected to make landfall in southern Fujian on the afternoon of the 16th. In the next 24-48 hours, the wind force in the Taiwan Strait, the southern East China Sea, the northeastern South China Sea, and the coastal areas of Fujian will increase to level 10-12, and the wind force in southern Zhejiang, northeastern Guangdong, western Fujian, and the northern East China Sea will increase to level 7-9. Coastal areas of Fujian will also experience heavy rain. After landfall, the typhoon will gradually turn towards the north and weaken in intensity. In the next 48 to 72 hours, western Fujian, Jiangxi, and southern Zhejiang will experience strong winds of level 6-7 and heavy rain.

### 台风警报

1958-07-15
第7版()
专栏：气象台

    台风警报
    本报讯　1958年7月14日下午8时中央气象科学研究所发布台风紧急警报。太平洋上的台风14日下午二时中心位置在北纬二十一度东经一百二十六度附近，目前台风中心正以每小时7—9浬的速度向西北方向移动，预计未来24小时内东海南部、南海东北部、福建沿海风力将加大到7—9级，台湾海峡风力将加大到8—10级。16日下午台风将在福建南部登陆。未来24—48小时内，台湾海峡、东海南部、南海东北部、福建沿海风力将加大到10—12级，浙江南部、广东东北部、福建西部、东海北部风力将加大到7—9级。福建沿海并将有暴雨。台风登陆以后将逐渐转向偏北方向移动，强度减弱。未来48到72小时内福建西部、江西和浙江南部将有6、7级大风，并将有暴雨。
