Title: Stone - One of Village Flutes
Author: Qin Mu
Date: 1958-07-17
Page: 8

    Stone
    ——One of Village Flutes
    Qin Mu
    A village toilet is quite unusual,
    Speaking of it might be surprising.
    Two stepping stones have quite a history,
    Both bearing the inscription "Land God Position".
    Two "Old Land Gods" awed by the power of the times,
    Returned to being mere stones.
    Letting new believers of atheism step upon them,
    Contemplating how to conquer nature.

### 石块——村笛之一
秦牧
1958-07-17
第8版()
专栏：

    石块
    ——村笛之一
    秦牧
    一个乡村厕所很不平常，
    讲出来也许令人吃惊。
    两块踏脚石大有来历，
    上面都有“土地神位”的字样。
    两个“老土地”懍于时代力量，
    又还原为石块。
    让无神论的新信徒跨在上边，
    思索着怎样去征服自然。
