Title: What Exactly Is the Attitude Towards the Geneva Experts Conference? The Soviet Union Demands a Definitive Answer from the United States
Author:
Date: 1958-07-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th - According to TASS, the Soviet government, in a memorandum to the U.S. government on the 9th, reiterated that the Soviet government expects the U.S. government to clearly indicate that the work of the Geneva Experts Conference should be subordinate to the solution of the main task—the universal and immediate cessation of nuclear weapons testing.

The Soviet government's memorandum pointed out that the U.S. government, in its memorandum to the Soviet government on June 30, still avoided giving a definitive answer to this question.

The Soviet government's memorandum emphasized that the U.S. government has not linked the experts conference with the main goal of stopping nuclear testing. The U.S. government continues to use the excuse that effective supervision is impossible to justify its refusal to stop nuclear weapons testing.

### 对日内瓦专家会议态度究竟如何  苏联要求美国作肯定答复

1958-07-11
第5版()
专栏：

    对日内瓦专家会议态度究竟如何
    苏联要求美国作肯定答复
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联政府9日给美国政府的一份备忘录再一次表示，苏联政府期望美国政府对于日内瓦专家会议的工作应该服从于解决主要任务——普遍和立刻停止试验核武器的问题作出明确表示。
    苏联政府的备忘录指出，美国政府在它6月30日给苏联政府的备忘录中仍然避免对这一个问题作出肯定的答复。
    苏联政府的备忘录强调指出，美国政府没有把专家会议同停止核试验主要目的联系起来。美国政府仍然企图以不可能进行有效监督为借口，来为它拒绝停止核武器试验的行为作辩护。
