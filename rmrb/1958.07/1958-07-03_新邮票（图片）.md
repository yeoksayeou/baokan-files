    New Stamps
    The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications issued a set of commemorative stamps on July 1st for the "110th Anniversary of the Publication of the Communist Manifesto," with two designs. The first design has a face value of four cents, is purple-red in color, and features a profile of Marx and Engels. The second design has a face value of eight cents, is blue-green in color, and features the 1848 German version of the "Communist Manifesto" as its theme, with peony flowers as a backdrop, symbolizing the peace and happiness the manifesto has brought to mankind.

### 新邮票（图片）

1958-07-03
第8版()
专栏：

    新邮票
    邮电部7月1日发行“共产党宣言发表一百一十周年”纪念邮票一套，二个图案。第一图面值四分，紫红色，图案是马克思、恩格斯侧面像。第二图面值八分，蓝绿色，图案是以“共产党宣言”1848年德文版本为主题，下面以牡丹花作衬托，象征宣言给人类带来了和平与幸福。
