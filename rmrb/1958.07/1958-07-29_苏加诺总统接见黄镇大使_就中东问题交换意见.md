Title: President Sukarno Receives Ambassador Huang Zhen, Exchanges Views on the Middle East Issue
Author:
Date: 1958-07-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 28th - Indonesian President Sukarno received Huang Zhen, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Indonesia, this morning in Bogor. They exchanged views on the Middle East issue and held friendly talks. The meeting lasted for more than an hour.

### 苏加诺总统接见黄镇大使  就中东问题交换意见

1958-07-29
第4版()
专栏：

    苏加诺总统接见黄镇大使
    就中东问题交换意见
    新华社雅加达28日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺今天上午在茂物接见了中华人民共和国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇。他们就中东问题交换了意见，并且进行了友好的交谈，这次会见延续了一个多小时。
