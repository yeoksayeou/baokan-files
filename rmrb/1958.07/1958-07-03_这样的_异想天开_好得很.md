Title: Such "Wild Ideas" Are Wonderful
Author: Wang Lei
Date: 1958-07-03
Page: 7

Such "Wild Ideas" Are Wonderful
    Wang Lei, Beijing Medical College, Department of Stomatology

    Our department's ten young teachers and technicians formed a youth technical innovation group. With the support and encouragement of the Party organization, they completed eleven innovations related to precious materials commonly used in dental treatments and new methods of installing teeth. The white inlay wax we manufactured using domestic materials has been proven to surpass similar American products after testing, while the cost is only one-sixteenth of the price of imported goods. The method we researched for installing fixed dentures for patients without electric grinding is a world first. The manufacturing of white inlay wax initially involved us following information from foreign literature for experiments, but we failed more than ten times. Later, we simply discarded the foreign literature and conducted our own experiments based on the properties of various materials and medical requirements, finally succeeding on the forty-seventh attempt. The difficulties we encountered in researching the method of installing fixed dentures without electric grinding were even greater. According to Chinese and foreign statutory regulations, reducing the size of teeth during the installation of fixed dentures is considered a mistake, let alone not grinding the teeth at all. Therefore, some people laughed at us for having "wild ideas" from the very beginning. When we made the fixed bridge without grinding the teeth, some people still didn't believe it, saying, "No matter what, I don't appreciate this method." We believe that no matter how cold-heartedly these people speak, we will continue to create. Our work is appreciated by patients and appreciated by 600 million people. (Attached picture)

    Wang Quan, a twenty-two-year-old young doctor from the Department of Stomatology at Beijing Medical College, is installing teeth using the "loop-type fixed bridge" [denture] he created.

### 这样的“异想天开”好得很
王镭
1958-07-03
第7版()
专栏：

    这样的“异想天开”好得很
    北京医学院口腔学系　王镭
    我们系里的十位青年教师和技术员成立了青年技术革新小组，在党组织的支持和鼓励下，完成了十一项有关口腔科治疗上常用的贵重材料和新的安装牙齿方法的创造。我们所用国产材料制造的白嵌体腊经过鉴定证明已经超过了美国的同类产品，而成本只有进口货价钱的十六分之一。我们研究出来的不用电磨牙为病人安装固定假牙的方法是世界上的一项创举。白嵌体腊的制造工作，最初我们按照外国文献上的资料进行试验，十多次都失败了，后来我们干脆丢掉了外国文献，自己根据各种资料的性质以及医疗上的要求进行试验，继续到四十七次才告成功。我们研究不用电磨牙安装固定假牙的方法，所遭受到的阻难更大。按照中外法定的常规，安装固定假牙牙齿磨少了都被认为是犯错误，更不允许不磨牙了。因此我们一开始行动就有些人笑我们“异想天开”。当我们把不磨牙齿的固定桥做成后，还有人仍不相信，他们说“不管它怎么样，反正我不欣赏这种方法。”我们认为不管这些人怎样冷言闲语，我们还要创造，我们的工作有病人欣赏，有六亿人民欣赏。（附图片）
    北京医学院口腔系的二十二岁青年医生王鐉，正在用他创造的“环式固定桥”〔假牙〕在装牙。　　　　本报记者　傅军摄
