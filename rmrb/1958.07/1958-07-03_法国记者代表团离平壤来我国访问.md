Title: French Journalist Delegation Leaves Pyongyang to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1958-07-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 2nd: After a visit of more than a month in Korea, five members of the French journalist delegation left Pyongyang by train this morning to visit China. The remaining three members will continue their visit in Korea and will travel to China later.

### 法国记者代表团离平壤来我国访问

1958-07-03
第5版()
专栏：

    法国记者代表团离平壤来我国访问
    新华社平壤2日电　法国记者代表团中的五人在朝鲜作了一个多月的访问以后，今天上午乘火车离开平壤，前往中国访问。其余三人将在朝鲜继续访问之后，晚些时候前往中国访问。
