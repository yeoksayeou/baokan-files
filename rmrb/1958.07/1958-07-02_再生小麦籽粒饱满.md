Title: Regenerated Wheat Kernels are Plump

Author:

Date: 1958-07-02
Page: 2

Regenerated Wheat Kernels are Plump
Yang Taosheng, a technician from the Macun Production Team of the Xizhanggeng Township in Jieyu County, Shanxi Province, conducted a spring sowing experiment with Bima No. 1 wheat on four-tenths of a mu of land in 1956. As a result, he harvested a total of 180 jin. On June 20, 1957, he used the spring-sown wheat for a summer sowing experiment. After more than ninety days of growth and development, he finally harvested wheat with plump kernels on September 24. After this summer-sown wheat was harvested, the roots were not removed, and this spring (March 7), wheat seedlings three inches high sprouted again. After three applications of fertilizer and two irrigations, these wheat seedlings smoothly passed through the stages of jointing, heading, flowering, and grain filling. Now, they have grown into wheat ears with plump kernels.

### 再生小麦籽粒饱满

1958-07-02
第2版()
专栏：

    再生小麦籽粒饱满
    山西解虞县西张耿乡麻村生产队技术员杨桃盛，1956年在四分地内，用碧蚂一号小麦进行了春播试验，结果，共收了一百八十斤。1957年6月20日，他又用春播收获的小麦，进行了夏播试验。经过九十多天的生长发育，最后在9月24日，收获了籽粒饱满的麦子。这些夏播小麦收割以后，由于根茬没有刨掉，今年春上（3月7日），又长出了三寸高的麦苗。经过三次追肥，两次浇水，这些麦苗顺利地渡过了拔节、抽穗、扬花、灌浆等阶段，现在，又长成籽粒饱满的麦穗了。
