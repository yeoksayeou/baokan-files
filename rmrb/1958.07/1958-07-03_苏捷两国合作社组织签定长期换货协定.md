Title: Soviet-Czech Cooperative Organizations Sign Long-Term Barter Agreement
Author:
Date: 1958-07-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 2nd: The Central Union of Consumer Cooperatives of the Soviet Union and the Central Council of Cooperatives of the Czechoslovak Republic signed a long-term barter agreement in Moscow on June 30th, covering the years 1958 to 1965, according to TASS.

### 苏捷两国合作社组织签定长期换货协定

1958-07-03
第5版()
专栏：

    苏捷两国合作社组织
    签定长期换货协定
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联消费合作社中央联合会与捷克斯洛伐克共和国合作社中央理事会，6月30日在莫斯科签定了1958年到1965年的长期换货协定。
