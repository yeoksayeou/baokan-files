Title: Vietnamese National Assembly Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1958-07-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - The delegation of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by President Ton Duc Thang, left Beijing by train this morning to return home.

Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and members Wang Weizhou, Liu Ningyi, Zhang Su, and Mei Gongbin, and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo were at the station to see them off.

Pham Binh, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and diplomatic envoys and officials from the Embassies of Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, Bulgaria, Albania, and Mongolia in China were also at the station to see them off. Yang An, head of the Cambodian Economic Delegation to Beijing, and Nai Warangdan, deputy head, were also at the station to see them off.

In the two days prior to this, the Vietnamese National Assembly delegation visited the United Textile Factory, the Metal Industry Factory, and the Evergreen Agricultural Production Cooperative in Beijing, and also toured the Ming Tombs Reservoir and the Summer Palace.

### 越南国会代表团离京回国

1958-07-31
第4版()
专栏：

    越南国会代表团离京回国
    新华社30日讯　越南民主共和国国会代表团在孙德胜团长率领下，今天上午乘火车离北京回国。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真、委员王维舟、刘宁一、张苏、梅龚彬，外交部副部长罗贵波，到车站欢送。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办范平，捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、蒙古驻中国使节和外交官员，也到车站欢送。到车站欢送的还有柬埔寨驻京经济代表团团长杨安、副团长乃瓦朗丹。
    在这以前的两天里，越南国会代表团曾在北京参观了联合纺织厂、金属工业工厂和四季青农业生产合作社，还游览了十三陵水库和颐和园等地。
