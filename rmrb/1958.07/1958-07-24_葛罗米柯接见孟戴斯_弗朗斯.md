Title: Gromyko Receives Mendès-France
Author:
Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 23rd – TASS, Moscow: Soviet Foreign Minister Andrei Gromyko received prominent French political figure Pierre Mendès-France in Moscow on the 22nd and held talks with him.

　　Mendès-France was passing through Moscow on his way to visit China.

### 葛罗米柯接见孟戴斯—弗朗斯

1958-07-24
第5版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯接见孟戴斯—弗朗斯
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯22日在莫斯科接见了法国著名政治活动家孟戴斯—弗朗斯，和他进行了谈话。
    孟戴斯—弗朗斯是在前往中国访问的途中路过莫斯科的。
