Title: Iraqi Envoys and Personnel Abroad Pledge Allegiance to New Government; Jordanian Chargé d'Affaires and Secretary in Iraq Seek Political Asylum
Author:
Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd dispatch from New York: All members of the Iraqi delegation to the United Nations announced on the 21st that they pledge allegiance to the government of the Republic of Iraq. They will obey the leadership of Jawad, the permanent representative to the United Nations appointed by the new Iraqi government, and disregard Abbas, the representative of the Faisal dynasty.

According to a dispatch from Rome, all personnel of the embassy in Rome, including Iraqi Minister to Rome Abdul Malik Khedri, also announced on the 21st their allegiance to the government of the Republic of Iraq.

Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd dispatch from Beirut: The Iraqi Embassy in Beirut notified the Lebanese Ministry of Foreign Affairs on the 22nd, declaring its allegiance to the government of the Republic of Iraq.

Under the pretext of not recognizing the new Iraqi government, the Lebanese Ministry of Foreign Affairs actually revoked the diplomatic immunity of the Iraqi ambassador and embassy staff.

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 22nd: According to Damascus Radio, Hosseini Shawish, Secretary of the Jordanian Embassy in Iraq, has applied for political asylum in Iraq.

The radio station also said that Jordanian Chargé d'Affaires Badruddin had applied for political asylum three days ago.

### 伊拉克驻外使节和人员  纷纷效忠新政府  约旦驻伊代办和秘书申请政治避难

1958-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克驻外使节和人员
    纷纷效忠新政府
    约旦驻伊代办和秘书申请政治避难
    新华社22日讯　纽约消息：伊拉克驻联合国代表团全部团员21日宣布效忠于伊拉克共和国政府，他们将服从伊拉克新政府任命的常驻联合国代表贾瓦德的领导，而不理费萨尔王朝的代表阿巴斯。
    另据罗马消息，包括伊拉克驻罗马公使阿卜杜勒·马利克·赫德里在内的公使馆全体人员21日也宣布效忠伊拉克共和国政府。
    新华社23日讯　贝鲁特消息：伊拉克驻贝鲁特大使馆22日通知黎巴嫩外交部，宣布效忠于伊拉克共和国政府。
    黎巴嫩外交部以不承认伊拉克新政府为借口，竟然撤销了伊拉克大使和使馆人员的外交豁免权。
  新华社大马士革22日电　据大马士革电台报道，约旦驻伊拉克大使馆秘书侯赛尼·沙维什申请在伊拉克政治避难。
    这家电台又说，约旦驻伊拉克代办巴德尔丁在三天以前已经申请政治避难。
