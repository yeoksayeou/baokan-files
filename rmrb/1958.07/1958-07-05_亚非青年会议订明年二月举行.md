Title: Afro-Asian Youth Conference Scheduled for February Next Year
Author: 
Date: 1958-07-05
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 3rd. The Afro-Asian Youth Conference is scheduled to be held in Cairo in February 1959, and youth organizations from forty-five countries have been invited to participate. The conference is being prepared in accordance with a resolution of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Conference. The organizer of the conference, the secretary of the Supreme Council of Youth of the United Arab Republic, announced the news at a press conference held by the Permanent Secretariat of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Council, which initiated the conference.

### 亚非青年会议订明年二月举行

1958-07-05
第5版()
专栏：

    亚非青年会议
    订明年二月举行
    据新华社开罗3日电　亚非青年会议决定在1959年2月在开罗举行，已经有四十五个国家的青年组织被邀请参加这次会议。会议是根据亚非人民团结大会的一项决议准备召开的。会议的组织者、阿拉伯联合共和国青年最高理事会的秘书在发起这次会议的亚非人民团结理事会常设秘书处所召开的记者招待会上宣布了这则消息。
