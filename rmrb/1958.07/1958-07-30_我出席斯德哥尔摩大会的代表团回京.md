Title: Delegation Returning to Beijing After Attending the Stockholm Conference

Author: 
Date: 1958-07-30
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 29th - The Chinese delegation led by Guo Moruo, Liao Chengzhi, and Burhan, after attending the Stockholm Conference for Disarmament and International Cooperation, returned to Beijing in batches on the 28th and 29th.

### 我出席斯德哥尔摩大会的代表团回京

1958-07-30
第2版()
专栏：

    我出席斯德哥尔摩大会的代表团回京
    新华社29日讯　由郭沫若、廖承志、包尔汉率领的中国代表团，在出席斯德哥尔摩举行的裁军和国际合作大会以后，已于28日和29日分批回到北京。
