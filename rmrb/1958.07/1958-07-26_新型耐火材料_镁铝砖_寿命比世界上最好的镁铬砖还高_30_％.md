Title: New Type of Refractory Material - Magnesia-Alumina Brick: Lifespan 30% Higher Than the World's Best Magnesia-Chrome Brick
Author:
Date: 1958-07-26
Page: 5

Strive to Scale the Heights of Science and Technology

    New Type of Refractory Material - Magnesia-Alumina Brick
    Lifespan 30% Higher Than the World's Best Magnesia-Chrome Brick
    A new type of refractory material for open-hearth furnace roofs – magnesia-alumina brick – has been successfully produced at the Institute of Metal Research of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. Actual trials in Anshan Iron and Steel's large open-hearth furnaces have shown that its service life is over 520 heats, exceeding the world's acknowledged best magnesia-chrome brick for open-hearth furnace roofs by more than 30%. The test results also show that the open-hearth furnace roofs built with magnesia-alumina bricks can withstand higher heat loads, shorten smelting time, and increase steel production.
    This new type of magnesia-alumina brick fully adapts to China's resource situation of abundant magnesite and alumina, and can be produced without chromite. Therefore, the successful trial production of magnesia-alumina brick is a new creation in China's refractory material industry, providing a strong guarantee for the leap forward in China's steelmaking industry.
                                        (Shenyang Daily)

### 新型耐火材料——镁铝砖  寿命比世界上最好的镁铬砖还高30％

1958-07-26
第5版()
专栏：努力攀上科学技术的高峰

    新型耐火材料——镁铝砖
    寿命比世界上最好的镁铬砖还高30％
    一种新型的炼钢平炉炉顶耐火材料——镁铝砖已在中国科学院金属研究所制成。经鞍钢大平炉实际试用结果，其使用寿命达五百二十炉以上，比目前世界各国公认平炉炉顶耐火材料中最好的镁铬砖超过30％以上。试验结果还表明，用镁铝砖砌筑的平炉炉顶，可以承受较高的热负荷，缩短冶炼时间，提高钢的产量。
    这种新型的镁铝砖，充分适应我国富产的美石和氧化铝的资源情况，可以不用铬铁矿。因此，镁铝砖的试制成功是我国耐火材料工业上一个新的创造，为我国炼钢工业的跃进提供了有力的保证。
                                         （沈阳日报）
