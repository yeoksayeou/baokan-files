Title: Another Wool Mill Built in Jining
Author:
Date: 1958-07-25
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot — The second large-scale wool processing factory in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region recently went into production in Jining City.

    This wool processing factory, equipped with modern equipment, can process more than 10,000 tons of various types of wool annually. In the future, wool, cashmere, cattle hair, and camel hair produced in the western part of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and two special districts of Shanxi and Shaanxi Provinces will be transported to this factory to be processed into wool, which will serve as raw material for woolen textile mills.

### 集宁又建一座绒毛厂

1958-07-25
第7版()
专栏：

    集宁又建一座绒毛厂
    据新华社呼和浩特讯　内蒙古自治区第二座规模较大的绒毛加工厂最近在集宁市投入生产。
    这座拥有现代化设备的绒毛加工厂，每年可加工各种绒毛一万吨以上。今后内蒙古自治区西部、山西省和陕西省的两个专区出产的羊毛、羊绒、牛毛、驼毛，都将运到这个厂加工成绒毛，作为毛纺织厂的原料。
