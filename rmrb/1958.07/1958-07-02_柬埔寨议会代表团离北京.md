Title: Cambodian Parliamentary Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-07-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch: The Cambodian Parliamentary Delegation led by the Speaker of the Cambodian National Assembly, Sang Nhean, left Beijing by plane this morning for Moscow, to visit the Soviet Union and Czechoslovakia.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Vice-Chairmen Li Jishen and Shen Junru, Standing Committee member of the National People's Congress, Hu Yuzhi, Vice-President of the Chinese Buddhist Association, Zhao Puchu, Vice-Mayor of Beijing, Wang Kunlun, the Soviet, Czechoslovak, and Vietnamese Charge d'Affaires ad interim and embassy counselors, and the head of the Cambodian Economic Delegation to Beijing, Yang An.

### 柬埔寨议会代表团离北京

1958-07-02
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨议会代表团离北京
    新华社1日讯　由柬埔寨王国会议议长桑·年率领的柬埔寨议会代表团，今天早晨由北京乘飞机去莫斯科，前往苏联和捷克斯洛伐克访问。
    到机场送行的，有副委员长李济深、沈钧儒，人大常委会委员胡愈之，中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初，北京市副市长王昆仑，苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、越南的驻华临时代办和使馆参赞，以及柬埔寨驻北京经济代表团团长杨安。
