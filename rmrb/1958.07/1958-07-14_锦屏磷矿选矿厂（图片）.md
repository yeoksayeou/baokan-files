Title: Jinping Phosphate Mine Concentrator (Picture)
Author: Li Xundun
Date: 1958-07-14
Page: 3

Jinping Phosphate Mine Concentrator
The expansion project of the Jinping Phosphate Mine, with an annual output of 300,000 tons, has been basically completed. The concentrator, one of the expansion projects, was put into production on July 1st, half a year ahead of schedule. This is the concentrator's factory building.
　　Photo by Li Xundun (Draft from Xinhua Lian Daily)

    锦屏磷矿选矿厂
