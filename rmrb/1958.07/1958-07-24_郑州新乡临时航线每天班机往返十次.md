Title: Zhengzhou-Xinxiang Temporary Air Route Has Ten Round Trip Flights Daily
Author:
Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 7
    Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 22nd. To solve the transportation problem before the restoration of the Beijing-Guangzhou Railway's Yellow River Bridge, the Civil Aviation Administration of China has opened a temporary air route between Zhengzhou and Xinxiang from July 22, with ten round trips daily. Passengers on the Beijing-Guangzhou Railway who need to travel south or north for important matters can transfer to airplanes in Zhengzhou or Xinxiang respectively.

### 郑州新乡临时航线每天班机往返十次

1958-07-24
第7版()
专栏：

    郑州新乡临时航线每天班机往返十次
    新华社郑州22日电　为解决京广铁路黄河大桥修复前的交通问题，中国民用航空局自7月22日起开辟郑州—新乡间临时航线，每天班机往返飞行十次。在京广铁路上有要事南下或北上的旅客，可以由郑州或新乡分别换乘飞机。
