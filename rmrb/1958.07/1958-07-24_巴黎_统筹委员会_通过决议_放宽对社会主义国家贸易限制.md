Title: Paris "CoCom" Passes Resolution Relaxing Trade Restrictions on Socialist Countries
Author:
Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, London, July 22nd, via TASS: The Paris "CoCom", established specifically to restrict trade between Western countries and the socialist bloc, has passed a resolution on relaxing trade restrictions with the Soviet Union and other socialist countries.

This resolution reflects the profound dissatisfaction of European countries participating in the North Atlantic Treaty Organization with the United States' forced trade restrictions, which are very harmful to these countries' economies.

### 巴黎“统筹委员会”通过决议  放宽对社会主义国家贸易限制

1958-07-24
第5版()
专栏：

    巴黎“统筹委员会”通过决议
    放宽对社会主义国家贸易限制
    据新华社23日讯　据塔斯社伦敦22日讯：专为限制西方国家同社会主义阵营国家的贸易而设立的巴黎“统筹委员会”，通过了一项关于放宽同苏联和其他社会主义国家贸易限制的决议。
    这项决议反映了参加北大西洋集团的欧洲国家对美国强行限制贸易的深刻不满，这种限制对这些国家的经济有很大害处。
