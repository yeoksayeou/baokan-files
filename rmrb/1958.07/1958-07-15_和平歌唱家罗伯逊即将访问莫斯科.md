Title: Peace Singer Robeson to Visit Moscow Soon
Author:
Date: 1958-07-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 13th: Paul Robeson, who is currently visiting London, intends to visit Moscow in three or four weeks. The famous American singer told this wish to a reporter from "Moscow Truth" during a telephone conversation.

Robeson also talked about his future plans. He will soon play the role of the famous black tragic actor Aldridge in a television short film. Robeson will play King Lear, Macbeth, and other roles that Aldridge was good at. Robeson also plans to participate in the shooting of other films.

For several years, the singer has spent a lot of time studying folk songs. He paid special attention to folk songs from Africa, Russia, Bulgaria, and Armenia. There are many new songs in his singing program.

### 和平歌唱家罗伯逊即将访问莫斯科

1958-07-15
第6版()
专栏：

    和平歌唱家罗伯逊即将访问莫斯科
    新华社14日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科13日讯：正在伦敦访问的保罗·罗伯逊打算在三四个星期之后访问莫斯科。这位著名的美国歌唱家是在和“莫斯科真理报”记者进行电话谈话的时候把这一愿望告诉记者的。
    罗伯逊还谈到了他今后的计划。他不久将在电视短片中担任著名的黑人悲剧演员阿尔德里奇扮演的角色。罗伯逊将扮演李尔王、麦克贝斯，和阿尔德里奇所擅长的其他角色。罗伯逊还打算参加其他电影的拍摄工作。
    几年来，这位歌唱家花了很多时间研究民间歌曲。他对非洲、俄罗斯、保加利亚、亚美尼亚的民间歌曲特别注意。在他的演唱节目中有许多新的歌曲。
