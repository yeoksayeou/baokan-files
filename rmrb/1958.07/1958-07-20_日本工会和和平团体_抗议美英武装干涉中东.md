Title: Japanese Trade Unions and Peace Groups Protest US-UK Armed Intervention in the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1958-07-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 18th: The General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (referred to as "Sohyo") passed a resolution at its standing directors' meeting today, strongly protesting the armed intervention by the United States and the United Kingdom in Lebanon and Jordan, and demanding the immediate withdrawal of US and British troops from these two countries. The letter of protest will be sent to US President Eisenhower and British Prime Minister Macmillan.

The letter of protest accuses the United States and the United Kingdom of openly using force to interfere in Lebanon and Jordan, attempting to suppress the struggle of the people of Arab countries for national independence. The letter of protest believes that this move by the United States and the United Kingdom is a threat to peace in the Middle East and the world.

The Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs issued a statement today protesting the US armed intervention in Lebanon and demanding the immediate withdrawal of US troops from Lebanon.

The statement said that the Japanese people believe that the US armed intervention is not only a threat to the people of Lebanon, Iraq and other Arab countries, but also a threat to peace-loving people all over the world.

### 日本工会和和平团体  抗议美英武装干涉中东

1958-07-20
第4版()
专栏：

    日本工会和和平团体
    抗议美英武装干涉中东
    据新华社18日讯  东京消息：日本工会总评议会（简称“总评”）在今天举行的常任干事会会议上通过决议，严重抗议美国和英国武装干涉黎巴嫩和约旦，并且要求美英军队立即撤出这两个国家。抗议书将送给美国总统艾森豪威尔和英国首相麦克米伦。
    抗议书指责美、英两国公然用武力干涉黎巴嫩和约旦，企图镇压阿拉伯各国人民争取民族独立的斗争。抗议书认为美英两国采取的这种举动是对中东和全世界和平的威胁。
    日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会今天发表声明，抗议美国武装干涉黎巴嫩，要求美国军队立即撤出黎巴嫩。
    声明说，日本人民认为，美国武装干涉的行为不仅是对黎巴嫩、伊拉克和其他阿拉伯国家人民的威胁，而且是对全世界爱好和平人民的威胁。
