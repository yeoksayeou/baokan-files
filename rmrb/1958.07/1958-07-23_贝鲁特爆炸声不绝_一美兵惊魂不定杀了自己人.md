Title: Beirut Explosions Continue; U.S. Soldier, Terrified, Kills His Own

Author: 
Date: 1958-07-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, 22nd -- A terrified U.S. Marine, while on guard duty at Beirut International Airport this morning, mistook a fellow soldier on duty with him for a Lebanese rebel and immediately shot and killed him.

Heavy gunfire constantly emanating from the rebel-controlled areas of Beirut on the night of the 21st is believed to have unnerved the U.S. occupation troops and caused this incident.

Explosions and gunfire frequently occurred in Beirut city yesterday and this morning.

### 贝鲁特爆炸声不绝  一美兵惊魂不定杀了自己人

1958-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    贝鲁特爆炸声不绝
    一美兵惊魂不定杀了自己人
    新华社22日讯  贝鲁特消息：一名惊魂未定的美国侵黎海军陆战队士兵今天早上在贝鲁特国际机场站岗时，把一名执行任务同来的同伙看作是黎巴嫩起义者，立即开枪把他打死。
    21日晚上从贝鲁特的黎巴嫩起义者控制地区那里不断传出猛烈的射击声，使得美国侵略军士兵神魂不定，据信这是产生这个事件的原因。
    在昨天和今天早晨，贝鲁特市区中经常发生爆炸和射击。
