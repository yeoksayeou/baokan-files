Title: Latin American People Oppose US-British Arson in the Middle East; Argentine Personages Demand Invading Troops Withdraw from Lebanon; Uruguayan "People's News" Welcomes Soviet Peace Proposals
Author:
Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd – According to a TASS report on the 22nd, Argentine personages and Uruguayan newspapers vehemently condemned the Anglo-American armed intervention in Middle Eastern affairs.

    The 13th Congress of the youth organization of the "Intransigent Radical Civic Union" of Buenos Aires Province, Argentina, passed a resolution expressing solidarity with the Iraqi people and condemning the armed intervention of the United States and Britain in Lebanon and Jordan.

    The city councils of Avellaneda and San Fernando, suburbs of the Argentine capital, also issued statements condemning foreign armed intervention in Lebanon and Jordan.

    The Buenos Aires University Students' Federation requested the Argentine government to instruct its representative to the United Nations to demand the immediate withdrawal of foreign troops from Lebanon and Jordan.

    Large-scale demonstrations were held in Buenos Aires and Mendoza to protest against imperialist aggression against Lebanon and Jordan.

    In Uruguay, many newspapers prominently published Khrushchev's letter proposing a five-power summit conference. An editorial in "People's News" stated: "At a critical moment when the criminal actions of the United States in the Near East are placing the whole world on the brink of a new war, the Soviet Union has once again put forward a particularly important initiative to save the world."

### 拉丁美洲人民反对美英在中东放火  阿根廷各界人士要求侵略军撤出黎约  乌拉圭“人民报”欢迎苏联和平建议

1958-07-24
第3版()
专栏：

    拉丁美洲人民反对美英在中东放火
    阿根廷各界人士要求侵略军撤出黎约
    乌拉圭“人民报”欢迎苏联和平建议
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社22日讯，阿根廷各界人士和乌拉圭报纸猛烈谴责英美武装干涉中东事务。
    阿根廷布宜诺斯艾利斯省“不妥协激进公民联盟”青年组织第十三次代表大会通过了决议，表示声援伊拉克人民，并且谴责美国和英国武装干涉黎巴嫩和约旦。
    阿根廷首都郊区阿维扬内达和圣斐南多城的市议会也发表声明，谴责外国武装干涉黎巴嫩和约旦。
    布宜诺斯艾利斯大学生联合会请求阿根廷政府指示本国驻联合国代表要求立即从黎巴嫩和约旦撤出外国军队。
    在布宜诺斯艾利斯和门多萨举行了声势浩大的游行示威，抗议帝国主义侵略黎巴嫩和约旦。
    在乌拉圭，许多报纸以显著地位刊载了赫鲁晓夫建议召开五大国首脑会议的信件。“人民报”的社论说：“正当美国在近东的犯罪行动把全世界置于新战争边缘的严重时刻，苏联又一次提出了特别重要的倡议来拯救世界。”
