Title: Afghan King Awards Medal to Ambassador Ding Guoyu
Author:
Date: 1958-07-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, 15th: Afghan King Zahir Shah awarded the "Highest Merit" Medal to the outgoing Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan, Ding Guoyu, on July 12, in recognition of his contributions to promoting the friendly relations between China and Afghanistan during his tenure.

### 阿富汗国王授予丁国钰大使勋章

1958-07-17
第6版()
专栏：

    阿富汗国王授予丁国钰大使勋章
    据新华社15日讯　喀布尔消息：阿富汗国王查希尔·沙阿在7月12日把“最高功绩”勋章授予即将卸任的中国驻阿富汗大使丁国钰，表彰他在任职期间为促进中国和阿富汗两国之间的友好关系所作的贡献。
