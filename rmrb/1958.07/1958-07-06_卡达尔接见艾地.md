Title: Kádár Receives Aidit
Author: 
Date: 1958-07-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, July 4th: János Kádár, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, received the Indonesian Communist Party delegation headed by D. N. Aidit this morning and held friendly talks with them. Present at the reception were Károly Kiss, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, and others.

### 卡达尔接见艾地

1958-07-06
第3版()
专栏：

    卡达尔接见艾地
    新华社布达佩斯4日电　匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会第一书记卡达尔，今天上午接见了以艾地为首的印度尼西亚共产党代表团，并且同他们进行了友好的交谈。接见时在座的有匈牙利社会主义工人党中央政治局委员卡拉伊等。
