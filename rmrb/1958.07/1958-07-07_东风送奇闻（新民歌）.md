Title: The East Wind Sends Wonderful News (New Folk Song)
Author:
Date: 1958-07-07
Page: 8

    The East Wind Sends Wonderful News
    (New Folk Song)
    Solar energy can cook,
    Manure can generate electricity,
    Air can light lamps,
    Leaves can weave satin,
    Straw can be used as feed,
    Wild roots can be candied.
    (Zhongjiang, Sichuan)

### 东风送奇闻（新民歌）

1958-07-07
第8版()
专栏：

    东风送奇闻
    （新民歌）
    太阳能煮饭，
    大粪能发电，
    空气能点灯，
    树叶能织缎，
    柴草当饲料，
    野根当蜜饯。
    （四川中江）
