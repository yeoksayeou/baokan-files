Title: In the Battle of Red Flames
Author: Rehemu Haimulayoufu
Date: 1958-07-01
Page: 8

    In the Battle of Red Flames		Uyghur Nationality Rehemu Haimulayoufu
    Today I sing the loudest song,
    Holding high the Party's red battle flag,
    Our Great Leap Forward is a communist battle,
    The great Party is the driving force for our eternal leap forward.
    A flash of light cuts open a path,
    This path allows us to reach the future tomorrow,
    This path is filled with an inextinguishable dawn,
    Like the golden sun shining on the whole earth.
    In the dawn of this great struggle,
    I write my fiery red determination.
    I vow to forever hold tight the Party's red flag,
    I vow to follow the Party towards communism.
    I carry a red oath and a red heart,
    Throwing myself into the great wave of the Great Leap Forward,
    Holding hands tightly with people of all ethnic groups,
    Bravely building the frontier of the motherland.
    On the radiant road to communism,
    We scatter the sweat of labor on the fragrant soil,
    The vast Gobi has become a flowerbed in full bloom,
    The Party leads us to make the beautiful flowers bloom even more brilliantly.
    From the steel-like struggle, arouse red flames,
    Melt away the perennial ice and snow on the high mountains,
    Whether it is the stars in the sky or the moon in the high sky,
    As long as we need it, we can get it in our hands.
    Great Party, you are always our guide,
    You give each of us endless strength.
    Let us hold high the Party's red battle flag,
    Throw ourselves into the great wave of the communist cause!

### 在红色火焰的战斗里
热赫木·海木拉尤夫
1958-07-01
第8版()
专栏：

    在红色火焰的战斗里	维吾尔族  热赫木·海木拉尤夫
    我今天唱出了歌曲的最强音，
    高举着党的战斗的红旗，
    我们的大跃进是共产主义的战斗，
    伟大的党是我们永远跃进的动力。
    一道闪光划开了一条道路，
    这道路使我们明天就能到达未来，
    这道路充满了永不熄灭的曙光，
    像照亮全地球的金光闪闪的太阳。
    我在这巨大斗争的曙光里，
    写出我火焰般的红色的决心。
    我宣誓要永远握紧党的红旗，
    我宣誓要跟着党走向共产主义。
    我带着红色的誓言也带着红色的心脏，
    投入了伟大跃进的巨浪，
    和各族人民手握紧手，
    勇猛的建设着祖国的边疆。
    我们走向共产主义的光芒四射的道路上，
    把劳动的汗珠在芬香的泥土里撒满，
    大戈壁已经变成了百花盛开的花坛，
    党带领我们让美丽的鲜花开的更灿烂。
    从钢铁般的斗争里激起红色的火焰，
    熔尽深山高峰上长年的冰雪，
    不管是天上的星星，还是高空的月亮，
    只要是需要我们就能够把它拿到手上。
    伟大的党啊，你永远是我们的带路人，
    你给我们每个人无尽的力量。
    让我们高举起党的战斗红旗，
    投入共产主义事业的巨浪！
