Title: Thai Parliamentarians and Party Leaders Say the U.S. Cannot Escape Responsibility for Worsening the Situation

Author:
Date: 1958-07-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency report: Some Thai parliamentarians and party leaders have also condemned the United States' armed intervention in the internal affairs of Middle Eastern countries and demanded that the U.S. invading forces withdraw from Lebanon.

Thai Socialist United Front parliamentarians Nai Sawang Da-chu and Nai Suti Puwapan told reporters on July 16 that the civil war in Lebanon and the revolution in Iraq were internal affairs of those countries and did not require foreign intervention. If the situation in the Middle East worsens, the United States cannot escape its responsibility.

Nai Taviset, vice chairman of the Thai Socialist United Front, told reporters on July 17 that the landing of American troops in Lebanon was clearly an act of aggression and violated the will of the Lebanese people. He hoped that all peace-loving people would face up to the development of the Lebanese situation.

### 泰国议员和政党领袖说  美国不能逃避使局势恶化的责任

1958-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

    泰国议员和政党领袖说
    美国不能逃避使局势恶化的责任
    据新华社讯  泰国一些议员和党派团体领袖也纷纷谴责美国武装干涉中近东国家人民的内政，并且要求美国侵略军队撤出黎巴嫩。
    泰国社会主义联合阵线议员乃沙旺·达趋和乃素提·蒲哇攀16日向报界发表谈话说，黎巴嫩的国内战争和伊拉克的革命都是这些国家的内政，用不着外国干涉。如果中东局势更加恶化，那么，美国将不能逃避它应负的责任。
    泰国社会主义联合阵线副主席乃他威塞17日向报界发表谈话说，美国军队登陆黎巴嫩，显然是一种侵略行为，是违反黎巴嫩人民的意志的。他希望所有爱好和平的人民正视黎巴嫩事件的发展。
