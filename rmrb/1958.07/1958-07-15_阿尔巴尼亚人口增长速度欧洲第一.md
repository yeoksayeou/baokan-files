Title: Albania's Population Growth Rate Ranks First in Europe
Author:
Date: 1958-07-15
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, July 14th: According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency on the 13th, Albania's population reached 1.5 million in April this year, an increase of 100,080 people compared to the population census conducted in October 1955.

    In the two and a half years since the last population census, Albania's population has grown at an average annual rate of approximately 30 per thousand. This growth rate is higher than that of any other country in Europe.

    Thanks to the People's Government's special concern for healthcare and the gradual improvement of the living standards of the working people, Albania's population mortality rate has greatly decreased. In the past seven and a half years, Albania's population has increased by a total of 281,000 people, while in the fifteen years before liberation, before 1938, it only increased by 237,000 people.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人口增长速度欧洲第一

1958-07-15
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人口增长速度欧洲第一
    新华社地拉那14日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社13日报道，根据最新材料，阿尔巴尼亚人口在今年4月间已经达到一百五十万，比1955年10月进行人口调查时增加了十万零八千人。
    在上一次进行人口调查以后的两年半中，阿尔巴尼亚人口每年平均增长了约千分之三十。这种增长率比欧洲所有其他国家都高。
    由于人民政府对保健事业特别关怀和劳动人民的生活水平逐步提高，阿尔巴尼亚的人口死亡率大大下降了。在过去七年半中，阿尔巴尼亚人口一共增加了二十八万一千人，而解放前1938年以前的十五年中，却只增加了二十三万七千人。
