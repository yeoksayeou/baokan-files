Title: Rainfall Forecast
Author:
Date: 1958-07-18
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, July 17th—According to the Central Meteorological Research Institute's forecast on the 17th: In the coming week, there will be little rain in the southeastern part of the Northeast, Hebei, and Shandong, and the weather will be relatively hot and humid; there will be more rain in the western part of Henan, the southwestern part of Shanxi, the central and southern parts of Shaanxi, the northwestern part of Hubei, and Sichuan. It is estimated that the rainfall distribution in the above areas will be 50 to 70 millimeters north of the Qinling Mountains and about 100 millimeters south of the Qinling Mountains.

### 雨情预报

1958-07-18
第7版()
专栏：

    雨情预报
    新华社17日讯　据中央气象科学研究所17日预报：在今后的一周内，东北的东南部、河北和山东将少雨，天气较闷热；河南的西部、山西的西南部、陕西的中部和南部、湖北的西北部和四川将多雨。估计以上地区雨量分布，秦岭以北有五十到七十公厘，秦岭以南有一百公厘左右。
