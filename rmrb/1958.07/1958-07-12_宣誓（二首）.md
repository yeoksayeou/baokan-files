Title: Oaths (Two Poems)
Author: Ge Bizhou
Date: 1958-07-12
Page: 8

    Oaths (Two Poems)
    Ge Bizhou
    Oaths
    We raise our hands,
    To make the high mountains march in line!
    We raise our hands,
    To make the great rivers flow backwards!
    We raise our hands,
    To join the great battle!
    Demanding grain from the fields,
    Demanding oil from the mountains,
    Demanding cotton from firewood,
    Demanding wine from weeds.
    A sky-high determination,
    As long as we listen to the Party,
    What is impossible!
    Night Returning to Chengdu on May 2nd
    Going Down with a Plan
    I go down with the spring breeze and spring rain,
    Going down with a plan!
    A hoe,
    A piece of luggage;
    And a heart full of zeal,
    Firm perseverance.
    In the golden autumn,
    To bring back victory!
            May 14th, Chengdu

### 宣誓（二首）
戈壁舟
1958-07-12
第8版()
专栏：

    宣誓（二首）
    戈壁舟
    宣誓
    我们举起手，
    要叫高山排队走！
    我们举起手，
    要叫大河倒着流！
    我们举起手，
    要去参加大战斗！
    向田地要粮，
    向高山要油，
    向柴火要棉，
    向野草要酒。
    一个天大的决心，
    只要听党的话，
    还有甚么不能够！
    5月2日返蓉之夜
    带着计划下去
    我跟春风春雨，
    带着计划下去！
    一把锄头，
    一件行李；
    还有满腔热血，
    坚强毅力。
    要在金色的秋天，
    带回胜利！
            5月14日成都
