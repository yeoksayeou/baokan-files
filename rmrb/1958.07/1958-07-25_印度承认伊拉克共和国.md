Title: India Recognizes the Republic of Iraq
Author:
Date: 1958-07-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 23rd - It was announced here tonight that the Indian government has decided to recognize the Republic of Iraq. This decision has been notified to Baghdad.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 23rd - The Indian Ministry of External Affairs issued a press release on July 23rd announcing that the Indian government has formally recognized the government of the Republic of Iraq. This press release was published after the Indian cabinet held a meeting today.

When the radio broadcast this news on the evening of the 23rd, the people of Delhi expressed a warm welcome.

Official sources in Delhi said that this is a "matter of great significance" at present.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 23rd - Indian Prime Minister Nehru notified the Parliamentary Consultative Committee on the 23rd to hold a meeting on July 30th to discuss the Middle East situation.

This Consultative Committee is composed of members of parliament from various political parties.

### 印度承认伊拉克共和国

1958-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    印度承认伊拉克共和国
    新华社新德里23日电　今天晚上这里宣布，印度政府已经决定承认伊拉克共和国。这个决定已经通知巴格达。
    据新华社新德里23日电　印度外交部23日发表新闻公报宣布，印度政府正式承认伊拉克共和国政府。这个新闻公报是在今天印度内阁举行了会议以后公布的。
    当电台在23日晚上广播这个消息的时候，德里人民表示热烈的欢迎。
    德里官方人士说，这是当前一件“意义重大的事情”。
    新华社新德里23日电　印度总理尼赫鲁在23日通知国会咨询委员会在7月30日举行会议讨论中东局势。
    这个咨询委员会是由各个政党的议员组成的。
