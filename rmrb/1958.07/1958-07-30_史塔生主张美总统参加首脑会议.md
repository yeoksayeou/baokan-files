Title: Stassen Advocates US President to Attend Summit Conference
Author:
Date: 1958-07-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 29th - Stassen, former disarmament advisor to the US President, today advocated that Eisenhower should attend a top-level meeting to discuss the Middle East issue.
    Stassen made this statement to reporters after a 35-minute meeting with Eisenhower at the White House.
    A reporter asked him what his opinion was on disarmament with reference to the Middle East crisis.
    He said, "I think a meeting between President Eisenhower and… Khrushchev in the United Nations would be good for the United States. This is an opinion I have held for a long time."

### 史塔生主张美总统参加首脑会议

1958-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

    史塔生主张美总统参加首脑会议
    新华社29日讯　华盛顿消息：以前曾做过美国总统裁军顾问的史塔生今天主张艾森豪威尔应参加讨论中东问题的最高级会议。
    史塔生是在白宫同艾森豪威尔进行三十五分钟的会谈后向记者发表了他的这个见解的。
    有记者问他参照中东危机对裁军的意见如何。
    他说，“我认为在联合国中举行一次艾森豪威尔总统和……赫鲁晓夫之间的会议将对美国有好处。这是我长久以来就持有的意见。”
