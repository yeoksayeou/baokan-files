Title: Hao Ting Appointed as China's Ambassador to Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1958-07-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 15th: Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has appointed Hao Ting as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Afghanistan, and has removed Ding Guoyu from his post as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Kingdom of Afghanistan.

### 郝汀任我国驻阿富汗大使

1958-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    郝汀任我国驻阿富汗大使
    新华社15日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命郝汀为中华人民共和国驻阿富汗王国特命全权大使，并免去丁国钰驻阿富汗王国特命全权大使职务。
