Title: Chairman Mao Receives Delegation of Black African Youth Visiting China
Author:
Date: 1958-07-13
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, July 12th: Chairman Mao Zedong received Diouf Banny Fally, head of the Delegation of Black African Youth Visiting China, and all members of the delegation at Qin Zheng Hall in Zhongnanhai this afternoon.
    Chairman Mao had a conversation with the guests for about two hours. The guests presented gifts to Chairman Mao, and Chairman Mao signed autographs for the guests as a memento.
    Present at the reception were Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Liu Xiyuan, Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, and Wu Xueqian, Vice Chairman. (Attached picture)
    The third person from the left is Diouf Banny Fally, head of the delegation. Xinhua News Agency reporter Hou Bo photo

### 毛主席接见黑非洲青年访华代表团

1958-07-13
第2版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见黑非洲青年访华代表团
    新华社12日讯　毛泽东主席今天下午在中南海勤政殿接见了黑非洲青年访华代表团团长狄乌弗·巴尼·法利和代表团全体团员。
    毛主席同客人们进行了约两小时的谈话，客人们向毛主席赠送了礼品，毛主席为客人们签字留念。
    接见时在座的，有国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，中华全国青年联合会主席刘西元、副主席吴学谦。（附图片）
    左起第三人是代表团团长狄乌弗·巴尼·法利。新华社记者　侯波摄
