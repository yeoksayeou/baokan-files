Title: Constanța, Romania Holds a Grand Meeting: Farewell to the Departing Soviet Troops

Author:
Date: 1958-07-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, July 3rd: Soviet troops stationed in the Black Sea coastal city of Constanța set off for their return home on July 3rd. The city held a farewell rally of several thousand people.

    V. Fălcu, First Secretary of the Constanța Regional Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, said at the meeting that our friendship with the Soviet army is built on the basis of proletarian internationalism and is unbreakable. We will always be with the Soviet army.

    Lieutenant General V. Arkhipov spoke on behalf of the Soviet army. He thanked the Romanian people for their warm reception and help. He said that the Soviet army assures the Romanian comrades and friends that we will always be loyal to proletarian internationalism and the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

### 罗马尼亚康斯坦萨举行大会  欢送苏联军队启程回国

1958-07-05
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚康斯坦萨举行大会
    欢送苏联军队启程回国
    据新华社布加勒斯特3日电驻在黑海岸边康斯坦萨城的苏联军队3日动身回国，这个城市举行了数千人的欢送大会。
    罗马尼亚工人党康斯坦萨州委员会第一书记瓦·弗尔库在会上讲话说，我们同苏军的友谊是建立在无产阶级国际主义基础上的，是牢不可破的。我们将永远同苏军在一起。
    瓦·阿尔赫波夫中将代表苏军讲话。他感谢罗马尼亚人民对他们的热情接待和帮助。他说，苏军向罗马尼亚的同志和朋友们保证：我们将永远忠实于无产阶级国际主义和苏联共产党。
