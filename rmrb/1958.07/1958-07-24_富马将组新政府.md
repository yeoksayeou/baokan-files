Title: Souvanna Phouma to Form New Government
Author:
Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 22nd: Vientiane Radio announced tonight that Prince Savang Vatthana has delegated the task of forming a new government to Prince Souvanna Phouma today, on behalf of the King of Laos. Prince Savang delegated the task of forming the new government to him after accepting the resignation application submitted by Prime Minister Phouma. Savang also requested the Phouma government to continue working until the new government is formed.

　　Phouma submitted his resignation to Prince Savang this morning. In the government's resignation letter, Phouma wrote that the supplementary election on May 4, 1958, has been conducted according to the provisions of the constitution. With the new National Assembly already established, there must be a new government to work according to new plans.

　　Prince Savang received Souphanouvong, Chairman of the Lao Patriotic Front, and other party chairmen this afternoon. He also received the Chairman of the Royal Council and the Chairman of the National Assembly.

### 富马将组新政府

1958-07-24
第5版()
专栏：

　　富马将组新政府
    新华社河内22日电　据万象电台今天晚上广播，沙旺王子已经在今天代表老挝国王把组织新政府的任务交给梭发那·富马亲王。沙旺王子是在代表国王接受了富马首相提出的政府辞职的申请书之后把组织新政府的任务交给他的。沙旺并要求富马政府继续工作直到新政府成立为止。
    富马是在今天上午向沙旺王子提出辞呈的。富马在政府的辞职书中写道，1958年5月4日的补充选举已经根据宪法的规定进行。在新的国民议会已经产生的情况下，必须有新的政府，以便根据新的计划进行工作。
    沙旺王子在今天下午接见了老挝爱国党主席苏发努冯和其他各党派主席。他还接见了皇庭会议主席和国民议会主席。
