Title: Saluting Friends Fighting for Independence: Beijing Youth Rally Welcomes Black African Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-07-12
Page: 4

    Saluting Friends Fighting for Independence
    Beijing Youth Rally Welcomes Black African Youth Delegation
    According to Xinhua News Agency, July 11th: More than 1,000 young people from all walks of life in Beijing held a rally tonight at the CPPCC Auditorium to welcome the youth delegation from Black Africa.

    Wu Xueqian, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, expressed his respect at the meeting to the young African friends who are bravely fighting against colonialism and for national independence. He said that Chinese youth fully support the struggle of the African people and youth against imperialism and for national independence. We believe that as long as the African people and youth strengthen their unity and persist in the struggle against imperialism, they will surely achieve the final victory in the liberation struggle.

    Wogo, a member of the delegation, introduced to the young people present the harm and suffering that imperialist colonial policies have brought to the youth of Black Africa, and the situation of the Black African youth resolutely opposing colonialism and safeguarding national independence together with the entire people. He said, we firmly believe that victory belongs to the just cause, and progressive people all over the world will support us. His speech was repeatedly interrupted by enthusiastic applause.

    Today's meeting was presided over by the Beijing Youth Federation. Beijing youth presented gifts to the Black African youth representatives at the meeting.

### 向为独立而战的朋友致敬  北京青年集会欢迎黑非洲青年代表团

1958-07-12
第4版()
专栏：

    向为独立而战的朋友致敬
    北京青年集会欢迎黑非洲青年代表团
    据新华社11日讯　北京市各界青年一千多人今天晚间在政协礼堂集会欢迎来自黑非洲的青年代表团。
    中华全国青年联合会副主席吴学谦在会上向为反对殖民主义和争取民族独立而英勇斗争的黑非洲青年朋友表示敬意。他说，中国青年全力支持非洲人民和青年反对帝国主义，争取民族独立的斗争。我们相信，只要非洲人民和青年加强团结、坚持和帝国主义斗争，一定会取得解放斗争的最后胜利。
    代表团团员、沃果在会上向到会青年介绍了帝国主义殖民政策对黑非洲青年带来的危害和痛苦，以及黑非洲青年坚决和全体人民一起反对殖民主义，维护民族独立的斗争情况。他说，我们深信胜利是属于正义事业的，世界进步人类一定会支持我们。他的讲话一再被热情的掌声所打断。
    今天的大会是由北京市青年联合会主持的。北京市青年在会上向黑非洲青年代表赠送了礼物。
