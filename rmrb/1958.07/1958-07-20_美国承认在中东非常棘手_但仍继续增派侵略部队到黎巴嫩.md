Title: The United States Admits the Middle East is Very Tricky, But Continues to Send Aggressor Troops to Lebanon
Author:
Date: 1958-07-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency Comprehensive Report, dated July 19th: Washington Dispatch: When answering questions at a press conference on the 18th, a spokesperson for the US Department of Defense said that the situation in the Middle East is "very tricky," but the United States is still continuously strengthening its forces there.

The spokesperson said that US jet fighters conducted "demonstration flights" over Lebanon on the 18th. These planes flew to Tripoli in northern Lebanon, east of Beirut, and the Sidon area in southern Lebanon. The purpose of the flights was to "let them know they have arrived there."

Beirut Dispatch: US aggressor troops continued to pour into Lebanon on the 19th, bringing the number of US troops invading Lebanon to 8,000.

Early that morning, US transport planes from the NATO military base in Adana, southern Turkey, continuously landed at the international airport south of Beirut. They transported 2,000 paratroopers from the 182nd Regiment of the US 24th Division, a "Pentomic Division" equipped with atomic weapons.

According to a broadcast by All India Radio on the 19th, the commander of US forces in Turkey threatened that the troops currently used in Lebanon are ready to use nuclear and conventional weapons in combat. Meanwhile, a spokesperson for the US Sixth Fleet said that there are currently about seventy warships in the Lebanese waters, including a cruiser equipped with missiles.

According to Reuters, the 1st Airborne Battle Group of the 187th Regiment of the US 24th Infantry Division, which was airlifted from the US Air Force Base in Adana, southern Turkey, to Beirut today, is also equipped with so-called "tactical nuclear weapons." (Attached image)

After landing in Haldi Bay, south of Beirut, US aggressor troops brutally evicted local residents.

### 美国承认在中东非常棘手  但仍继续增派侵略部队到黎巴嫩

1958-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    美国承认在中东非常棘手
    但仍继续增派侵略部队到黎巴嫩
    本报综合报道　据新华社19日讯：华盛顿消息：美国国防部发言人18日在记者招待会上回答问题的时候说，中东的局势“非常棘手”，但是美国仍然在继续不断地加强在那里的兵力。
    这个发言人说，美国喷气式战斗机18日在黎巴嫩上空举行了“示威飞行”，这些飞机曾经飞到黎巴嫩北部的的黎波里、贝鲁特东部和黎巴嫩南部的西敦地区，飞行的目的是为了“让人知道它们已经到了那边”。
    据贝鲁特消息：美国侵略军19日继续涌进黎巴嫩，侵黎美军的人数达到八千人。
    当天一早，从土耳其南部亚达那的北大西洋公约军事基地飞来的美国运输机不断在贝鲁特南面的国际机场着陆。它们运来了美国第二十四师第一百八十二团的两千名伞兵，这个师是装备有原子武器的“五单位原子师”。
    另据全印电台19日广播，美国驻土耳其军队的司令威胁说，目前在黎巴嫩使用的军队已经准备好使用核武器和常规武器作战。同时，美国第六舰队的发言人说，目前在黎巴嫩海面上大约有七十艘军舰，其中包括配备有导弹的一艘巡洋舰。
    据路透社报道，今天从土耳其南部的美国空军基地亚达那被空运到贝鲁特的美军第二十四步兵师一百八十七团第一空运作战部队也装备有所谓“战术核武器”。（附图片）
    美国侵略军在贝鲁特以南哈尔迪湾登陆后，蛮横地驱逐当地居民。
