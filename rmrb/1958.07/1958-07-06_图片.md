    In Renjiaban Village, Jiefang Township, Shaoxing County, Zhejiang Province, the entire village's manure pits have adopted a system of private ownership with public management. This is a picture of the whole village dividing areas by team, centrally providing shade and covering the manure pits.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Gengxin
