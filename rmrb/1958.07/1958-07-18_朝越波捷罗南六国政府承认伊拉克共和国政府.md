Title: North Korea, Vietnam, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Romania, and Yugoslavia Governments Recognize Government of the Republic of Iraq
Author:
Date: 1958-07-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 17th. Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, sent a telegram to Abdul Karim Kassem, Premier of the Republic of Iraq, today, warmly congratulating him on the establishment of the Republic of Iraq and informing him that the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea has decided to recognize the government of the Republic of Iraq.

The telegram said: "The entire Korean people support the just struggle of the Iraqi people against imperialism and for national independence and freedom, and sincerely wish the Iraqi people continued and greater achievements in their future struggles."

The telegram also said: "I am confident that the friendly and cooperative relations between Korea and Iraq and between the peoples of the two countries will continue to develop on the basis of the principles of peaceful coexistence."

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 17th. The government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam announced today its recognition of the government of the Republic of Iraq. A telegram signed by Premier Pham Van Dong has been sent to Abdul Karim Kassem, Premier of the Republic of Iraq.

The full text of the telegram is as follows:

The government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the entire Vietnamese people wholeheartedly welcome the glorious achievements of the Iraqi people in overthrowing the monarchy, the lackey of imperialism, and establishing the Republic of Iraq. This is an achievement of the great unity and struggle of the Arab people.

On behalf of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, I have the honor to inform Your Excellency that the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has decided to recognize the government of the Republic of Iraq, headed by Your Excellency, as the sole legitimate government of Iraq and the true representative of the Iraqi people. The government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam wholeheartedly support the just struggle of the government and people of the Republic of Iraq to defend their newly won independence and sovereignty.

They are convinced that, thanks to the unity and heroic struggle of the Iraqi people, and thanks to the full support and assistance of the entire Arab people, the people of Asia and Africa, and peace-loving people all over the world, the Iraqi people, under the leadership of the Republic of Iraq, will overcome all difficulties, smash the interventionist plans of imperialism, firmly safeguard the republican system, and build the Republic of Iraq into an increasingly prosperous and strong country, thereby contributing to the consolidation of peace in the Middle East, peace in Asia and Africa, and world peace.

May the friendship between the people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the people of the Republic of Iraq be increasingly consolidated.

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 17th. According to the Polish Press Agency on the 17th: The government of the Polish People's Republic recognized the government of the Republic of Iraq on the 16th.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 16th. According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency on the 16th, Czechoslovak Premier Široký sent a telegram to Iraqi Premier Kassem, announcing that the government of the Czechoslovak Republic officially recognized the government of the Republic of Iraq and expressed its willingness to establish diplomatic relations with the Republic of Iraq immediately.

The Czechoslovak government expressed its belief that the establishment of the Republic of Iraq will help consolidate peace in the Middle East and the world, and will provide good conditions for the establishment of friendly cooperation between the two countries. The telegram also stated that the Czechoslovak people congratulate the Iraqi people on their achievements in consolidating the national independence and sovereignty of the Republic of Iraq and in developing the economy and culture.

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, July 16th. According to a report by the Romanian News Agency on the 16th: Romanian Chairman of the Council of Ministers Chivu Stoica sent a telegram to Iraqi Premier Kassem, announcing that the Romanian government officially recognized the government of the Republic of Iraq. Chivu Stoica said in the telegram that he believes that the new Iraqi state will be a factor in consolidating peace in the Middle East and the world. He said that the Romanian people congratulate the Iraqi people on their achievements in the struggle to consolidate the independence of their motherland, and that the Romanian people fully sympathize with the Iraqi people's struggle.

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, July 17th. According to a Tanjug news report from Brioni: Yugoslav President Tito announced in a telegram to Iraqi Premier Kassem on the 16th that the government of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia has decided to recognize the Republic of Iraq and proposed the establishment of normal diplomatic relations between the two countries.

### 朝越波捷罗南六国政府承认伊拉克共和国政府

1958-07-18
第3版()
专栏：

    朝越波捷罗南六国政府承认伊拉克共和国政府
    新华社平壤17日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成今天打电报给伊拉克共和国总理阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·卡塞姆，热烈祝贺伊拉克共和国的创建，并且通知他，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府决定承认伊拉克共和国政府。
    电报说：“全体朝鲜人民支持伊拉克人民为反对帝国主义和争取民族独立和自由的正义斗争，并且衷心地祝伊拉克人民在今后的斗争中继续获得更大的成就。”
    电报还说：“我确信朝鲜和伊拉克两国和两国人民之间的友谊合作关系将以和平共处原则为基础继续得到发展。”
    新华社河内17日电　越南民主共和国政府今天宣布承认伊拉克共和国政府。由范文同总理签署的电报已经发给伊拉克共和国总理阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·卡塞姆。
    电文全文如下：
    越南民主共和国政府和全体越南人民衷心地欢迎推翻了帝国主义的奴仆——君主政权、建立了伊拉克共和国的伊拉克人民的辉煌成就。这是阿拉伯人民伟大的团结和斗争的一个成就。
    我谨代表越南民主共和国政府，荣幸地通知阁下，越南民主共和国政府已经决定承认以阁下为首的伊拉克共和国政府为伊拉克唯一的合法政府和伊拉克人民的真正代表。越南民主共和国政府和越南人民衷心地支持伊拉克共和国政府和人民保卫他们新赢得的独立和主权的正义斗争。
    他们确信，由于伊拉克人民团结一致和英勇斗争，由于他们得到全体阿拉伯人民、亚非各国人民和全世界爱好和平的人民的全力的赞助和支持，伊拉克人民在伊拉克共和国的领导下将克服一切困难，粉碎帝国主义的干涉计划，坚定地维护共和国政体和把伊拉克共和国建成一个日益繁荣和强大的国家，从而对巩固中近东的和平、亚非两洲的和平和全世界和平作出贡献。
    祝越南民主共和国人民和伊拉克共和国人民之间的友谊日益巩固。
    新华社华沙17日电　据波兰通讯社17日讯：波兰人民共和国政府16日承认伊拉克共和国政府。
    新华社布拉格16日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社16日报道，捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理西罗基打电报给伊拉克共和国总理卡塞姆，宣布捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府正式承认伊拉克共和国政府，并且表示愿意同伊拉克共和国立即建立外交关系。
    捷克斯洛伐克政府表示相信，伊拉克共和国的成立将有助于巩固中近东和世界的和平，将为伊捷两国建立友好合作提供良好的条件。电报中还表示，捷克斯洛伐克人民祝贺伊拉克人民在巩固伊拉克共和国的民族独立和主权以及在发展经济和文化方面取得成就。
    新华社布加勒斯特16日电　据罗马尼亚通讯社16日报道：罗马尼亚部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡打电报给伊拉克共和国总理卡塞姆，宣布罗马尼亚政府正式承认伊拉克共和国政府。基伏·斯托伊卡在电报中表示相信，新的伊拉克国家将是巩固中东和世界和平的因素。他说，罗马尼亚人民祝贺伊拉克人民在争取巩固自己祖国的独立的斗争中取得成就，罗马尼亚人民完全同情伊拉克人民的斗争。
    新华社贝尔格莱德17日电　据南斯拉夫通讯社布里俄尼消息：南斯拉夫总统铁托16日在给伊拉克共和国总理卡塞姆的一封电报里宣布，南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国政府决定承认伊拉克共和国，并且建议两国间建立正常的外交关系。
