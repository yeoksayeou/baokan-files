Title: United Arab Republic Troops in Syrian Region Shoot Down Aircraft Invading from Lebanon

Author:
Date: 1958-07-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 24th: On the afternoon of July 24th, two jet fighters of unknown nationality invaded the Syrian region from the Lebanese side and circled over the Quneitra area southwest of Damascus. The armed forces of the United Arab Republic opened fire with anti-aircraft guns and shot down one of them, while the other fled towards Lebanon. The hit invading aircraft caught fire and crashed in Lebanese territory.

### 阿联军队在叙利亚地区击落自黎入侵飞机一架

1958-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

    阿联军队在叙利亚地区击落自黎入侵飞机一架
    新华社大马士革24日电　24日中午，有两架不明国籍的喷气式战斗机从黎巴嫩方面侵入叙利亚地区，在大马士革西南的库奈特拉地区上空盘旋。阿拉伯联合共和国军队的高射炮开火击落其中的一架，另一架向黎巴嫩方面逃窜。被击中的入侵飞机起火，坠毁在黎巴嫩境内。
