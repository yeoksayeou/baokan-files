Title: Drafting a New Constitution to Adapt to the New Era
Author:
Date: 1958-07-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 20th - Baghdad Radio announced this morning that the government of the Iraqi Republic is studying the drafting of a new constitution to adapt to the new era.

The radio station said that a republican decree will be issued soon to dissolve the House of Representatives established by the old dynasty.

The Iraqi Council of Ministers decided to shorten the imprisonment terms of all criminals by 20%.

The head of the Iraqi industrial sector told the Middle East News Agency that the policy of the Iraqi Industrial Committee will be completely changed. Iraqi experts will participate in this committee. The committee will retain previously formulated plans and abide by its previous obligations.

### 起草新宪法  适应新时代

1958-07-22
第5版()
专栏：

    起草新宪法  适应新时代
    新华社开罗20日电  巴格达电台今天上午宣布，伊拉克共和国政府正在研究起草新宪法，以适应新的时代。
    电台说，不久将发布一项共和国的法令，解散旧王朝成立的众议院。
    伊拉克部长会议决定把所有罪犯的监禁期限缩短20％。
    伊拉克工业部门负责人对中东通讯社记者说，伊拉克工业委员会的政策将完全改变。伊拉克专家将参加这个委员会。这个委员会将保留以前制定的计划和遵守它以前的义务。
