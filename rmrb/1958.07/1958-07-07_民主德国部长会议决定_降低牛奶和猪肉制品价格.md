Title: GDR Council of Ministers Decides to Lower Prices of Milk and Pork Products
Author:
Date: 1958-07-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, June 6th: According to a report by the German News Agency from Berlin on the 5th, the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic has decided that, starting from July 7th, the prices of milk and dairy products, as well as pork and pork-related products, will be reduced by 8% to 14.5% respectively. This new price reduction measure will increase the purchasing power of residents by 400,000 marks annually.

### 民主德国部长会议决定  降低牛奶和猪肉制品价格

1958-07-07
第5版()
专栏：

    民主德国部长会议决定
    降低牛奶和猪肉制品价格
    新华社6日讯　据德意志通讯社柏林5日讯：德意志民主共和国部长会议决定，从7月7日开始，牛奶和奶制品、猪肉和猪肉类制品的价格将分别降低8％至14.5％。这项新的降低价格的措施将使得居民的购买力每年提高四十万马克。
