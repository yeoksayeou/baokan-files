Title: The Invader's Dreams Are Bound to Fail: Cambodia's "People's Daily" Opposes U.S. Interference in the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1958-07-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch - Cambodia's "People's Daily" published an article on the 18th, severely condemning the U.S. armed intervention in Lebanon. The newspaper emphasized that the purpose of the U.S. troops entering Lebanon was to suppress the people's just struggle, creating tension in Lebanon and the entire Middle East, and threatening world peace. The newspaper pointed out that the U.S. and Chamoun government's attempt to subdue the Lebanese people is nothing more than a dream. The U.S. must remember its failures in Egypt, Syria, and Iraq.

### 侵略者的梦想一定要失败  柬埔寨“人民报”反对美干涉中东

1958-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

    侵略者的梦想一定要失败
    柬埔寨“人民报”反对美干涉中东
    据新华社讯  柬埔寨“人民报”18日发表文章严厉谴责美国武装干涉黎巴嫩。报纸强调说，美国军队开入黎巴嫩的目的是为了镇压人民的正义斗争。并且在黎巴嫩和整个中东造成紧张局势，威胁世界和平。这家报纸指出，美国和夏蒙政府的压服黎巴嫩人民的企图只不过是梦想。美国必须记取它在埃及、叙利亚和伊拉克的失败。
