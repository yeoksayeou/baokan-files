Title: United Arab Republic Weightlifting Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-07-19
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, July 18th: The United Arab Republic Weightlifting Team, a delegation of twelve, led by the head of the team and Secretary-General of the United Arab Republic Weightlifting Association, Muhammad Ahmad Nasr, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of our country. They were warmly welcomed at the airport by Zhu Debao, member of the Standing Committee of the All-China Sports Federation, and Chinese weightlifters.

    The United Arab Republic Weightlifting Team has an excellent technical level and used to have a very high reputation and sports record in the world of weightlifting. There are a total of eight athletes in this team, with two featherweight athletes and one athlete in each of the remaining six weight classes. They will hold a friendly competition with our National Weightlifting Team in Beijing on the 20th.

### 阿拉伯联合共和国举重队到京

1958-07-19
第7版()
专栏：体育新闻

    阿拉伯联合共和国举重队到京
    新华社18日讯　应邀来我国访问的阿拉伯联合共和国举重队一行十二人，在领队、阿拉伯联合共和国举重协会秘书长穆罕默德·阿赫末德·纳斯尔率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。他们在机场上受到了中华全国体育总会常务委员会委员朱德宝和中国举重运动员们的热烈欢迎。
    阿拉伯联合共和国举重队有着优良的技术水平，过去在世界举重界中曾有过很高的声誉和运动纪录。这个队中共有八名运动员，除有两名次轻量级运动员外，其余六个级的运动员各一名。他们将在20日同我国国家举重队在北京举行一次友谊比赛。
