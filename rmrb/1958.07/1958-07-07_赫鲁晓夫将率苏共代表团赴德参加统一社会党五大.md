Title: Khrushchev to Lead CPSU Delegation to Germany to Attend the Fifth Congress of the Socialist Unity Party

Author:
Date: 1958-07-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th—TASS, Moscow, 6th: The Soviet "Pravda" reported that Khrushchev, member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and First Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee, will lead a delegation of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union to attend the Fifth Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, which will be held in Berlin from July 10 to 16.

The members of the delegation are Kuusinen, member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee; Yefremov, member of the CPSU Central Committee and First Secretary of the CPSU Kuibyshev Oblast Committee; Shcherbitsky, member of the CPSU Central Inspection Commission and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ukraine; Shauro, First Secretary of the Minsk Oblast Committee of the Communist Party of Belarus; and Pervukhin, Alternate Member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and Soviet Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic.

### 赫鲁晓夫将率苏共代表团赴德参加统一社会党五大

1958-07-07
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫将率苏共代表团赴德参加统一社会党五大
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科6日讯：苏联“真理报”报道，苏共中央主席团委员、苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫将率领苏联共产党代表团参加7月10日到16日在柏林举行的德国统一社会党第五次代表大会。
    代表团团员有苏共中央主席团委员、苏共中央书记库西宁、苏共中央委员、苏共古比雪夫州委员会第一书记叶弗烈莫夫、苏共中央检查委员会委员、乌克兰共产党中央委员会书记谢尔比茨基、白俄罗斯共产党明斯克州委员会第一书记夏乌罗、苏共中央主席团候补委员、苏联驻德意志民主共和国大使别尔乌辛。
