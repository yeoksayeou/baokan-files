Title: Nepal and the Federation of Malaya Support Holding a UN General Assembly Emergency Session
Author:
Date: 1958-07-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd, Kathmandu Dispatch: The Nepalese Foreign Ministry issued a statement on July 22nd saying that the Nepalese government has requested the United Nations Security Council to convene an emergency session of the United Nations General Assembly to resolve the Middle East crisis.

The statement said that the Nepalese government is deeply concerned about recent developments in the Middle East. It believes that the situation there "could erupt into an international war at any time."

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd, Kuala Lumpur Dispatch: The Foreign Ministry of the Federation of Malaya issued a statement on July 21st, stating that the situation in the Middle East is serious and constitutes a threat to international peace and security. The statement said that the government of the Federation of Malaya has issued instructions to its delegation to the United Nations, informing the delegation to support the proposal to convene an emergency session of the United Nations General Assembly or to take the initiative to propose this proposal if the Security Council cannot resolve the Middle East situation.

The Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Federation of Malaya, Rahman, convened an emergency cabinet meeting on the morning of July 21st to discuss the Middle East situation.

### 尼泊尔和马来亚联合邦赞同举行联大紧急会议

1958-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔和马来亚联合邦赞同举行联大紧急会议
    新华社22日讯  加德满都消息：尼泊尔外交部22日发表的一项声明说，尼泊尔政府已经要求联合国安全理事会召开联合国大会紧急会议来解决中东危机。
    声明说，尼泊尔政府对中东地区最近的事态发展感到十分忧虑。它认为，那里的局势“可能随时爆发为国际性的战火”。
    据新华社22日讯  吉隆坡消息：马来亚联合邦外交部21日发表声明说，中东局势是严重的，并且构成对国际和平和安全的威胁。声明说，马来亚联合邦政府已经向它的驻联合国代表团发出指示，通知代表团在安全理事会不能解决中东局势问题时，就支持召开联合国大会紧急会议的建议，或者主动提出这个建议。
    马来亚联合邦总理兼外交部长拉赫曼21日早上召开内阁紧急会议，讨论了中东局势。
