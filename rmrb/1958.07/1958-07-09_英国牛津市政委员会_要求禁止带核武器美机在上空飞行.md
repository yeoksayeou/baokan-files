Title: Oxford City Council, UK, Demands a Ban on US Aircraft Carrying Nuclear Weapons Flying Overhead
Author:
Date: 1958-07-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, July 7th: The Oxford City Council in Britain passed a resolution on the evening of July 7th, demanding that the government prohibit American aircraft carrying nuclear weapons from flying over the Oxford area.

The resolution stated: Given the presence of a hydrogen bomb base at Brize Norton near Oxford, the City Council is deeply concerned about the danger posed by the accidental occurrence of US aircraft carrying nuclear weapons flying over the vicinity of Oxford.

The members of the Oxford City Council include thirty-two Labour Party members, twenty-four Conservative Party members, and twelve members representing Oxford University.

### 英国牛津市政委员会  要求禁止带核武器美机在上空飞行

1958-07-09
第6版()
专栏：

    英国牛津市政委员会
    要求禁止带核武器美机在上空飞行
    新华社伦敦7日电  英国牛津市政委员会7日晚上通过一项决议，要求政府禁止携带核武器的美国飞机在牛津地区上空飞行。
    决议说：鉴于在牛津附近的不利兹诺尔敦建有氢弹基地，市政委员会对于携带核武器的美国飞机在牛津市附近上空飞行一旦发生意外所造成的危险深为不安。
    牛津市政委员会的委员中包括三十二名工党党员、二十四名保守党党员和代表牛津大学的十二名委员。
