Title: Comrade Chen Yun Inspects the Northwest Region and Wuhan City
Author:
Date: 1958-07-13
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 12th - Vice Premier of the State Council, Comrade Chen Yun, recently conducted an inspection of the Northwest region and Wuhan City. At the end of June and the beginning of July, Vice Premier Chen Yun inspected the Lanzhou Oil Refinery, the Taolin Agricultural Cooperative on the outskirts of Lanzhou, and also inspected the Yumen Oilfield.

On July 7th and 8th, Vice Premier Chen Yun inspected key enterprises in Wuhan, including the Wuhan Iron and Steel Complex, the Wuhan Boiler Factory, and the Wuhan Heavy Machine Tool Factory. He met with leading cadres, workers, and Soviet experts assisting our country in construction at these factories, and learned in detail about the enterprises' capital construction, production, growth of technical strength, equipment capacity, raw material supply, and trial production of new products.

### 陈云同志视察西北地区和武汉市

1958-07-13
第2版()
专栏：

    陈云同志视察西北地区和武汉市
    新华社12日讯　国务院副总理陈云最近在西北地区和武汉市进行了视察。陈云副总理6月底到7月初视察了兰州炼油厂、兰州市郊桃林农业社，并且到玉门油矿进行了视察。
    7月7、8两日，陈云副总理在武汉视察了武汉钢铁联合企业、武汉锅炉厂和武汉重型机床厂等重点企业。他同这些工厂的领导干部、工人和帮助我国建设的苏联专家见了面，详细地了解了这些企业的基本建设、生产、技术力量的成长、设备能力、原材料供应和试制新产品等情况。
