Title: A Bumper Lychee Harvest (Picture)
Author: Xiang Zhi
Date: 1958-07-14
Page: 2

    A Bumper Lychee Harvest
    On the banks of the Jiulong River in Fujian, tens of thousands of mu of lychee trees have yielded an unprecedented bumper harvest. According to the harvested output, this year's fresh lychee harvest is expected to exceed 25 million jin, an increase of approximately sevenfold compared to last year (a lean year). This photograph depicts the fruit professional production team of an agricultural cooperative harvesting lychees. Xinhua News Agency reporter Xiang Zhi took the photo.

### 荔枝大丰收（图片）
相知
1958-07-14
第2版()
专栏：

    荔枝大丰收
    福建九龙江畔，万亩荔枝获得空前大丰收。据采摘的产量来看，今年可收鲜荔枝二千五百多万斤，比去年（小年）增产七倍左右。这是农业社水果专业生产队在采收荔枝。　　新华社记者　相知摄
