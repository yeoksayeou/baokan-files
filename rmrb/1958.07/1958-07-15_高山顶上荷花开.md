Title: Lotus Blossoms on the Mountaintop

Author: 
Date: 1958-07-15
Page: 8

New Folk Song Selection

    Lotus Blossoms on the Mountaintop
    Roll up your pants and take off your shoes,
    We'll haul the dragon up the mountain,
    Tie the Milky Way around the mountainside,
    Lotus Blossoms on the Mountaintop.
              (Zhenba, Shaanxi)

### 高山顶上荷花开

1958-07-15
第8版()
专栏：新民歌选

    高山顶上荷花开
    卷起裤子脱了鞋，
    要把龙往山上抬，
    山腰拴上银河带，
    高山顶上荷花开。
              （陕西镇巴）
