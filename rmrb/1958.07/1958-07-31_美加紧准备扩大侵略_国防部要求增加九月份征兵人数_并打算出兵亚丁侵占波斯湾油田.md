Title: U.S. Intensifies Preparations to Expand Aggression; Defense Department Requests Increase in September Draft; Plans to Invade Aden and Seize Persian Gulf Oilfields
Author:
Date: 1958-07-31
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, July 30th: Washington dispatch: The U.S. Department of Defense on the 28th requested the draft of 11,000 men in September, an increase of 1,000 from the previous three months. All of these men will join the army.
    The 11,000 men in September will bring the total number of draftees since the resumption of the draft when the United States launched its aggressive war against Korea eight years ago to 2,363,400 plus.
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 29th: According to a report in the Cairo "Liberation" weekly on the 29th, the United States intends to send troops to Aden to "protect" the oilfields in the Persian Gulf.
    The weekly quoted Washington officials as saying that Britain had requested the United States to take this action. The weekly also said that Britain had also asked the United States to clarify what assistance it could provide to control the region, including Kuwait and Bahrain.

### 美加紧准备扩大侵略  国防部要求增加九月份征兵人数  并打算出兵亚丁侵占波斯湾油田

1958-07-31
第3版()
专栏：

    美加紧准备扩大侵略
    国防部要求增加九月份征兵人数
    并打算出兵亚丁侵占波斯湾油田
    新华社30日讯　华盛顿消息：美国防部28日要求在9月份征兵一万一千人，比前三个月征兵人数增加一千人。这些人都将参加陆军。
    9月份的一万一千人，将使得美国八年前发动侵略朝鲜战争时恢复征兵以来的征兵总数达到二百三十六万三千四百多人。
    新华社开罗29日电　据开罗“解放”周刊29日报道，美国打算派遣军队去亚丁“保护”波斯湾的油田。
    这家周刊援引华盛顿官方人士的话说，英国已经请求美国采取这个行动。这家周刊又说，英国还请求美国说明它能够提供什么援助来控制这个地区，其中包括科威特和巴林。
