Author: Fu Jun
Date: 1958-07-03

    The young teachers and students of the Mineral Processing Teaching and Research Group of Beijing Mining College have refined graphite from blast furnace slag with a carbon content of 99.46%, reaching the world's advanced level. This is a photo taken by our reporter Fu Jun of the young teachers and students refining graphite from blast furnace slag.
