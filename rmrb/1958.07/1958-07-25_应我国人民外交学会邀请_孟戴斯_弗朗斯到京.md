Title: Mendès-France Arrives in Beijing at the Invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs
Author:
Date: 1958-07-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 24th: At the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, former French Prime Minister and Radical Socialist Party member Pierre Mendès-France arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to our country.

Roland Dumas, a member of the French Democratic and Socialist Union of Resistance, also arrived with Mendès-France.

Welcoming them at the airport were: Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Lian Guan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and others.

In the evening, Zhang Xiruo held a welcoming banquet for the guests.

### 应我国人民外交学会邀请  孟戴斯——弗朗斯到京

1958-07-25
第4版()
专栏：

    应我国人民外交学会邀请
    孟戴斯——弗朗斯到京
    新华社24日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的法国前总理、激进社会党议员孟戴斯—弗朗斯在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    与孟戴斯—弗朗斯同来访问的还有法国民主社会抵抗联盟议员罗兰·杜马。
    到机场欢迎的有：中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长连贯，北京市副市长吴晗等。
    晚间，张奚若为客人们举行了欢迎宴会。
