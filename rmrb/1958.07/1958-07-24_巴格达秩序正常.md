Title: Order Restored in Baghdad
Author:
Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 23rd: According to today's Baghdad dispatch from "Al-Ahram," as the situation in the Iraqi capital Baghdad has returned to normal, Iraqi troops and tanks began withdrawing from Baghdad on the 22nd. The newspaper said that the curfew there will also be lifted soon.

### 巴格达秩序正常

1958-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    巴格达秩序正常
    据新华社开罗23日电　据“金字塔报”今天的巴格达消息，由于伊拉克首都巴格达的情况已经恢复正常，伊拉克的部队和坦克从22日起开始撤出巴格达。这家报纸说，那里的宵禁也很快就要解除。
