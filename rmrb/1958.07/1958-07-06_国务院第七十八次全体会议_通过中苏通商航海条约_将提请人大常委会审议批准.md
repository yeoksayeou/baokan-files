Title: The 78th Plenary Session of the State Council Passes the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Commerce and Navigation; To Be Submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for Examination and Approval
Author:
Date: 1958-07-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 5th: The 78th plenary session of the State Council was held today.

    The meeting reviewed and approved the "Treaty of Commerce And Navigation between the People’s Republic Of China and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics". This treaty will be submitted to the Standing Committee of The NPC for examination and approval.

    The meeting approved the "Decision of the State Council on the Handling of Rightists Wang Han, Zeng Zhaolun, Lin Handa, Huang Qixiang, and Fei Xiaotong".

    The meeting also approved the "Provisional Regulations of the State Council on the Retirement of Active Duty Officers," the "Several Provisions of the State Council on Improving the Financial Management System of Capital Construction," and the "Provisions of the State Council on Improving the Management System of Insurance Work", and made the "Decision of the State Council on Abolishing the Dalate Rear Banner and Guyang County of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region."

    The meeting reviewed and approved the "Organizational Regulations of the People's Congress and People's Committee of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region." This regulation will be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for examination and approval.
   
    The meeting passed the list of appointments and removals.

### 国务院第七十八次全体会议  通过中苏通商航海条约  将提请人大常委会审议批准

1958-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院第七十八次全体会议
    通过中苏通商航海条约
    将提请人大常委会审议批准
    新华社5日讯　国务院全体会议第七十八次会议今天举行。
    会议审核通过了“中华人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟通商航海条约”，这个条约将提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议通过了“国务院关于处理右派分子王翰、曾昭抡、林汉达、黄琪翔、费孝通的决定”。
    会议还通过了“国务院关于现役军官退休处理的暂行规定”，通过了“国务院关于改进基本建设财务管理制度的几项规定”和“国务院关于改进保险工作管理制度的规定”，并且作出了“国务院关于撤销内蒙古自治区的达拉特后旗和固阳县的决定”。
    会议审议通过了“广西僮族自治区人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例”，这个条例将提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议通过了任免名单。
