Title: Zhang Xiruo Holds Reception to Welcome Manila Vice Mayor
Author:
Date: 1958-07-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 19th. Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, held a reception this afternoon to welcome Jesus Marcos Roces, Vice Mayor of Manila, Philippines, and Speaker of the Manila City Council, and six others. During the reception, the hosts and guests frequently raised their glasses to toast each other's friendship. (Attached picture)
    Left picture: President Zhang Xiruo and Vice Mayor Roces in a warm conversation at the reception.
　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Changzhong

### 张奚若举行酒会欢迎马尼拉副市长

1958-07-20
第6版()
专栏：

    张奚若举行酒会欢迎马尼拉副市长
    新华社19日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今日下午举行酒会，欢迎菲律宾马尼拉市副市长、马尼拉市议会议长赫苏斯·马科斯·罗赛斯等七人。席间，宾主频频举杯互祝友好。（附图片）
    左图：张奚若会长和罗赛斯副市长在招待会上热情交谈
　　新华社记者　刘长忠摄
