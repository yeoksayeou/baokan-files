Title: Song of Joy for the Harvest
Author: Quan Guan
Date: 1958-07-09
Page: 1

    Song of Joy for the Harvest
    A hundred miles of rice paddies ripple with golden waves,
    Songs of joy for the harvest soar to the ninth heaven,
    We are rushing to build thousands upon thousands of carts,
    The rice sings "wa wa" as it rides in the carts.
                (Quan Guan) (Attached picture)
    Early rice harvest in Tucheng Agricultural Cooperative, Sunan Township, Chenghai County, Guangdong. This is a corner of the threshing ground. Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Fubei photo

### 丰收喜歌
泉官
1958-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

    丰收喜歌
    百里稻田泛金浪，
    丰收喜歌冲九霄，
    赶造车子千千辆，
    稻谷乘车哇哇叫。
                （泉官）（附图片）
    广东澄海县苏南乡涂城农业社的早稻丰收。这是晒谷场一角。	新华社记者  陈福北摄
