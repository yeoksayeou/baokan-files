Title: The Days Get Sweeter and Sweeter

Author: 
Date: 1958-07-17
Page: 4

The Days Get Sweeter and Sweeter
　　I carried a shoulder pole for four or five years,
　　Burying my head and not speaking a word,
　　Now the Party calls on us,
　　To make technical innovations in full swing.
　　Now it's revolutionized into wheeled transport,
　　We won't have to rub our shoulders anymore,
　　We must listen to the Party's words,
　　The days get sweeter and sweeter.

### 日子越过心越甜

1958-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

    日子越过心越甜
    我担扁担四五年，
    闷起脑壳不开言，
    如今党来号召咱，
    技术革新闹冲天。
    这下革成车子化，
    咱们不会再磨肩，
    我们要听党的话，
    日子越过心越甜。
