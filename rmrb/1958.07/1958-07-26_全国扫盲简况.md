Title: A Brief Overview of the National Literacy Campaign
Author:
Date: 1958-07-26
Page: 7

Since the Great Leap Forward in industrial and agricultural production, the work of eliminating illiteracy has also entered a new stage, and a mass learning upsurge of "everyone teaches, everyone learns" has been launched throughout the country. As of mid-July, according to incomplete statistics, more than 90 million people have participated in cultural learning, and 32 million people have reached the standard for graduating from literacy classes (Sichuan, Shaanxi, Inner Mongolia, and Shanghai are not included). On a national scale, nearly a quarter of the counties and cities have basically eliminated illiteracy among young and middle-aged adults.
