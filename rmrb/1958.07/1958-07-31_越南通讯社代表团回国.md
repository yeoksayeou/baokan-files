Title: Vietnam News Agency Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1958-07-31
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 29th - The Vietnam News Agency delegation, headed by Comrade Hoang Tung, returned home today from Kunming after a visit to our country lasting more than a month.

　　The delegation visited China at the invitation of Xinhua News Agency and in accordance with the Sino-Vietnamese Cultural Cooperation Agreement. During their stay in China, the delegation visited Beijing, Shanghai, Wuhan, Kunming and other places, visiting the Xinhua News Agency headquarters and local branches, as well as factories, agricultural cooperatives, schools, etc.

### 越南通讯社代表团回国

1958-07-31
第4版()
专栏：

    越南通讯社代表团回国
    新华社昆明29日电　以黄俊同志为首的越南通讯社代表团，在我国进行了一个多月的访问后，今天离开昆明回国。
    代表团是根据中越文化合作协定并应新华通讯社的邀请来我国访问的。代表团在我国期间曾访问了北京、上海、武汉、昆明等地，参观了新华通讯社总社和地方分社以及工厂、农业社、学校等。
