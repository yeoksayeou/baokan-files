    Typhoon Alert

    (Our report) On July 15th at 8 PM the Central Meteorological Research Institute issued an urgent typhoon alert. The typhoon in the Pacific Ocean was at 2 PM today centered approximately 150 kilometers east of Taiwan, specifically at 22.7 degrees North latitude and 122.7 degrees East longitude. The maximum wind speed near the center was 50 meters per second. It is predicted that the typhoon will continue to move in a northwest-by-west direction. Tonight, the typhoon will make landfall in Taiwan and weaken slightly. Tomorrow afternoon, it will cross the Taiwan Strait and make landfall near Xiamen. Within the next 24 hours, the wind force in the Taiwan Strait and along the central and southern coast of Fujian will increase to force 11-12. The wind force in the southern East China Sea, the northeastern South China Sea, eastern Guangdong, and northern and western Fujian will increase to force 7-9. Within the next 24 to 48 hours, the typhoon will weaken and continue to move in a northwest direction. The wind force in southern Jiangxi will increase to force 6-7. Tomorrow and the day after tomorrow, central and southern Fujian will also experience heavy rain.

### 台风警报

1958-07-16
第7版()
专栏：气象台

    台风警报
    本报讯　7月15日下午8时中央气象科学研究所发布台风紧急警报。太平洋上的台风今天下午二时中心位置在台湾以东约一百五十公里左右的洋面上，就是北纬二十二点七度、东经一百二十二点七度的地方，中心附近最大风速为每秒五十公尺，预计未来台风仍将继续向西北偏西方向移动。今天夜间台风将在台湾登陆，并略减弱。明天下午将越过台湾海峡在厦门附近登陆。未来二十四小时内台湾海峡，福建中部、南部沿海，风力将加大十一到十二级，东海南部、南海东北部、广东东部、福建北部和西部风力将加强到七到九级。未来二十四至四十八小时内，台风将减弱，并继续向西北方向移动。江西南部风力将加大到六、七级，明天到后天，福建中部南部并将有暴雨。
