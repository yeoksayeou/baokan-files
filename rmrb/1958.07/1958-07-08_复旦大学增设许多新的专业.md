Title: Fudan University Adds Many New Majors
Author: Yu Huiyin
Date: 1958-07-08
Page: 7
Higher Education Forges Ahead

    Fudan University Adds Many New Majors
    Founded in Shanghai in 1905, Fudan University is one of the oldest comprehensive universities in China.
    Fudan University currently has eleven departments: Chinese, Foreign Languages, History, Journalism, Economics, Law, Philosophy, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. These departments are further divided into fifteen majors: Chinese Language and Literature, English Language and Literature, History, Journalism, Political Economics, Law, Philosophy, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology, Plant Physiology, Human and Animal Physiology, and Anthropology. Encouraged by the Great Leap Forward, in addition to dividing the original Chinese Language and Literature major into Linguistics and Literature, new majors such as Mechanics, Computational Mathematics, Material Structure, Radio Electronics, Biochemistry, Biophysics, Entomology and Parasitology, Genetics, and Microbiology have been added. The subjects studied in these new majors mostly belong to cutting-edge disciplines. In order to strengthen the training of worker-peasant cadres, Fudan University will also establish a worker-peasant preparatory course starting this autumn.
    Fudan University currently has 4,333 students and 70 graduate students. In terms of faculty, there are 686 professors, associate professors, lecturers, and teaching assistants. In the rectification movement of the past year, the principles of education serving politics, combining education with labor, linking theory with practice, and emphasizing the present over the past have gradually taken root in the school, and a major revolution has emerged in teaching and scientific research work. Now, all teachers and students are marching forward on the road to becoming both red and expert.
                      (Yu Huiyin)

### 复旦大学增设许多新的专业
余辉音
1958-07-08
第7版()
专栏：高等学校在前进

    复旦大学增设许多新的专业
    复旦大学在1905年成立于上海，是国内历史比较悠久的综合性大学之一。
    复旦大学现设有中文、外文、历史、新闻、经济、法律、哲学、数学、物理、化学、生物等十一系，分设汉语言文学、英国语言文学、历史学、新闻学、政治经济学、法律学、哲学、数学、物理学、化学、植物学、动物学、植物生理学、人体及动物生理学、人类学等十五个专业。在大跃进形势的鼓舞下，除将原汉语言文学专业分设为语言学、文学两专业外，并增设力学、计算数学、物质结构、无线电电子学、生物化学、生物物理、昆虫与寄生虫、遗传学、微生物学等新专业。这些新专业所研究的对象，大部分属于尖端学科。复旦大学为了加强对工农干部的培养，今秋起还将设置工农预科。
    复旦大学现有学生四千三百三十三人，研究生七十人；在师资方面，现有教授、副教授、讲师、助教六百八十六人。在一年来的整风运动中，教育为政治服务、教育和劳动结合、理论联系实际、厚今薄古等方针在校内已逐步深入人心，教学和科学研究工作上都出现了大革新的局面。现在，全校师生正在向又红又专的道路上迈步前进。
                      （余辉音）
