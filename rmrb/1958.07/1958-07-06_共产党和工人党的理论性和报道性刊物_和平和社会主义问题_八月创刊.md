Title: Communist and Workers' Parties' Theoretical and Reporting Publication "Problems of Peace and Socialism" to be Launched in August
Author:
Date: 1958-07-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The theoretical and reporting monthly publication of Communist and Workers' Parties, "Problems of Peace and Socialism," is scheduled to be launched in Prague in late August.

　　This publication is an international publication unanimously decided upon by representatives of some Communist and Workers' Parties at a meeting held in Prague after the Moscow meeting in November 1957. Its main content includes: theoretically elucidating the basic issues of scientific socialism, the international workers' movement, the national liberation movement, the struggle for world peace and a happy life for mankind, the role of science and culture in social development, international relations and world economic ties, and so on.

　　The "Problems of Peace and Socialism" monthly will be published in Chinese, Russian, Bulgarian, Czech, German, Hungarian, Korean, Polish, Romanian, English, French, Italian, and Spanish. The Chinese version is scheduled for distribution on September 1st.

### 共产党和工人党的理论性和报道性刊物  “和平和社会主义问题”八月创刊

1958-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    共产党和工人党的理论性和报道性刊物
    “和平和社会主义问题”八月创刊
    新华社5日讯　共产党和工人党的理论性和报道性月刊——“和平和社会主义问题”，定8月下旬在布拉格创刊。
    这个刊物是1957年11月莫斯科会议后，一些共产党和工人党的代表在布拉格举行会议，一致决定出版的一种国际性刊物。它的主要内容是：从理论上阐明科学社会主义的基本问题，国际工人运动，民族解放运动，争取世界和平和人类幸福生活的斗争，科学和文化在社会发展中的作用，国际关系和世界经济联系，等等。
    “和平和社会主义问题”月刊将用中文、俄文、保加利亚文、捷克文、德文、匈牙利文、朝鲜文、波兰文、罗马尼亚文、英文、法文、意大利文、西班牙文等文字出版。中文版定9月1日发行。
