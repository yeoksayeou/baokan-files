Title: Philippine Senator Cuenco Reveals Garcia Promised the U.S. Missile Bases in the Philippines
Author:
Date: 1958-07-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Manila, July 7th: According to a United Press International report on the 6th, Philippine Senator Mariano Cuenco, while recently meeting with reporters from the Philippine News Agency in Cebu City, quoted a "highly reliable source" as saying that Philippine President Garcia had "assured the Philippines' support" for the U.S. plan to establish U.S. missile bases in the Philippines.

Cuenco said that Garcia's guarantee to support the U.S. in establishing missile bases in the Philippines was made "in consideration of the loans he obtained from the United States" during his state visit to the U.S.

The senator pointed out that the Filipino people and the Congress oppose the establishment of U.S. missile bases in the Philippines.

### 菲参议员库恩柯透露  加西亚答应美国在菲设导弹基地

1958-07-08
第6版()
专栏：

    菲参议员库恩柯透露
    加西亚答应美国在菲设导弹基地
    据新华社7日讯  马尼拉消息：据合众国际社6日报道，菲律宾参议员马里亚诺·库恩柯最近在宿务城接见菲律宾通讯社记者的时候，引一位“非常可靠的人士”的话透露，菲律宾总统加西亚已经向美国“保证菲律宾支持”在菲律宾设立美国导弹基地的计划。
    库恩柯说，加西亚支持美国在菲律宾建立导弹基地的保证，是他在美国进行国事访问期间“考虑到他从美国获得的贷款”而提出的。
    这位参议员指出，菲律宾人民和国会是反对美国在菲律宾建立导弹基地的。
