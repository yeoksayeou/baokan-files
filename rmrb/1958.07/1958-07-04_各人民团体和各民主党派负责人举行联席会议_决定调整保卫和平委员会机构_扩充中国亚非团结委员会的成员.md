Title: Leaders of People's Organizations and Democratic Parties Hold Joint Meeting Deciding to Adjust the Structure of the Committee for the Defense of Peace Expand the Membership of the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee
Author:
Date: 1958-07-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 3rd: Leaders of people's organizations and democratic parties held a joint meeting this morning. After discussion, they decided to adjust the organizational structure of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace and expand the membership of the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee. The meeting also discussed supporting the "Disarmament and International Cooperation Conference" to be held in Stockholm on July 16 and decided to send a delegation to attend the conference.

The meeting was presided over by Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace. He first introduced the major contributions made by the Committee since its establishment in October 1949 in promoting the nationwide participation in the Resist-America, Aid-Korea movement, defending world peace, vigorously supporting the people of all countries in their struggle for peace and national independence, carrying out just struggles against colonialism, and strengthening friendly cooperation among the people of all countries. He said that the current situation is one where the East Wind prevails over the West Wind, the forces of peace are unprecedentedly strong, and the forces of war are increasingly isolated. However, we must remain vigilant, vigorously oppose the schemes of the aggressive bloc headed by US imperialism to continue to intensify international tension and prepare for new wars, further support the movements of the people of Asia, Africa, Latin America and other countries to safeguard national independence, and strengthen friendly unity with the people of all countries. We resolutely support all recent peace measures taken by our government and support the Soviet Union's series of peace initiatives such as the cessation of nuclear weapons testing.

### 各人民团体和各民主党派负责人举行联席会议  决定调整保卫和平委员会机构  扩充中国亚非团结委员会的成员

1958-07-04
第4版()
专栏：

    各人民团体和各民主党派负责人举行联席会议
    决定调整保卫和平委员会机构
    扩充中国亚非团结委员会的成员
    新华社3日讯　各人民团体和各民主党派负责人今天上午举行联席会议，经过讨论并决定调整中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的组织机构，扩充中国亚非团结委员会的成员。会上还讨论了关于支持7月16日在斯德哥尔摩举行的“裁军和国际合作大会”，并决定派遣一个代表团参加大会。
    会议由中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若主持，他首先介绍了和大从1949年10月成立以来，在推动全国人民积极参加抗美援朝运动，保卫世界和平以及大力支持各国人民争取和平，维护民族独立，并且进行反对殖民主义的正义斗争，以及加强各国人民友好合作等方面所作出的重大贡献。他说，当前的形势是东风压倒西风，和平力量空前强大，战争势力日益孤立，但是我们仍然要提高警惕，要大力反对以美帝国主义为首的侵略集团继续加剧国际紧张局势和准备新战争的阴谋，要进一步支援亚非、拉丁美洲等国人民维护民族独立的运动和加强与各国人民的友好团结。我们坚决拥护我国政府的最近的一切和平措施，支持苏联关于停止核武器试验等一系列和平倡议。
