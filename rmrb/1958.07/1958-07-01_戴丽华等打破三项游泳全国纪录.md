Title: Dai Lihua and Others Break Three National Swimming Records
Author:
Date: 1958-07-01
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency:

    Dai Lihua and Others Break Three National Swimming Records

    According to Xinhua News Agency, on the afternoon of the 29th, three national swimming records were broken during the swimming performance competition of the "Beijing Municipal National Sports Movement Leap Forward Month" held at the Taoranting Swimming Pool in Beijing.

    The famous female swimmer Dai Lihua broke the national record of 6 minutes 37.6 seconds in the 400-meter individual medley swimming performance competition with a time of 6 minutes 25.2 seconds. Female swimmer Fu Cuimei broke the national record of 2 minutes 46.3 seconds in the 200-meter freestyle performance competition with a time of 2 minutes 38.5 seconds. Athlete Liu Furan also broke the national record of 2 minutes 38.7 seconds in the men's 200-meter butterfly performance competition with a time of 2 minutes 38.5 seconds.

### 戴丽华等打破三项游泳全国纪录

1958-07-01
第7版()
专栏：体育新闻

    戴丽华等打破三项游泳全国纪录
    据新华社讯　29日下午，在北京陶然亭游泳场举行的“北京市全民体育运动跃进月”的游泳表演赛中，有三项游泳成绩打破了全国纪录。
    著名的女子游泳运动员戴丽华在四百公尺个人混合式游泳表演赛中，以六分二十五秒二的成绩打破了六分三十七秒六的全国纪录。女子游泳运动员傅翠美在二百公尺自由泳表演赛中，以二分三十八秒五的成绩，打破了二分四十六秒三的全国纪录。运动健将刘复然在男子二百公尺蝶泳的表演赛中，也以二分三十八秒五的成绩，打破了二分三十八秒七的全国纪录。
