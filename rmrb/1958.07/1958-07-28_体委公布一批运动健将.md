Title: The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission Announces a Batch of Master Athletes
Author: 
Date: 1958-07-28
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, 26th: The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China announced on the 25th the names of thirty-three more Master Athletes in gymnastics, track and field, and cycling. The list is as follows:

    Gymnastics: Zheng Linxiang, Men Xiaomin (female).
    Track and Field: Chen Zhengxiu, Wang Jianxia, Lin Xiuyu (all three female).
    Cycling: Yang Jinyuan, Jiang Yumin, Cheng Benqing, Song Xinzhi, Peng Huadeng, Ye Yiren, Zhang Minde, Shang Erli, Wang Qi, Luan Zhichen, Sun Dakun (eleven males above), Xu Yuru, Lin Jianqin, Zhao Fengying, Li Yuzhuo, Huang Guiying, Zhang Muxiang, Qiu Suyun, Yang Jie, Yu Guilan, You Hao, Wang Xiying, Yu Shuqing, Li Guilan, Hu Xiucai, Ding Qiaoling, Tang Meide, Wang Hongfeng (seventeen females above).
    Up to now, China has 309 Master Athletes, of which 107 are female.

### 体委公布一批运动健将

1958-07-28
第7版()
专栏：体育新闻

    体委公布一批运动健将
    新华社26日讯　中华人民共和国体育运动委员会25日又公布了三十三名体操、田径、自行车运动健将。名单如下：
    体操：郑粦祥、门晓敏（女）。
    田径：陈正绣、王剑侠、林秀玉（以上三名女）。
    自行车：杨进元、蒋余民、成本清、宋新芝、彭华登、叶以人、张民德、尚尔历、王琦、栾志臣、孙大坤（以上十一名男）、徐玉如、林剑琴、赵凤英、李玉琢、黄桂英、张木香、丘素云、杨捷、于桂兰、游浩、汪希英、于淑清、李桂兰、胡秀彩、丁巧玲、唐梅德、王鸿凤（以上十七名女）。
    到目前为止，我国已有运动健将三○九名，其中女的占一○七名。
