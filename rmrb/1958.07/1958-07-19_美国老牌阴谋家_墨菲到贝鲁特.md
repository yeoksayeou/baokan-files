Title: U.S. Veteran Conspirator Murphy Arrives in Beirut
Author:
Date: 1958-07-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, July 18th: U.S. Deputy Undersecretary of State Murphy, in Lebanon as the U.S. President's "Special Political Representative," arrived in Beirut on the 17th. He is there to further plot U.S. aggression against Lebanon and Middle Eastern countries.

This veteran of the U.S. State Department, skilled at plotting aggression conspiracies, immediately expressed his desire to confer with Chamoun and Holloway, the commander-in-chief of the U.S. forces invading Lebanon, upon his arrival in Beirut.

### 美国老牌阴谋家  墨菲到贝鲁特

1958-07-19
第3版()
专栏：

    美国老牌阴谋家
    墨菲到贝鲁特
    据新华社18日讯　贝鲁特消息：以美国总统“特别政治代表”的身份前来黎巴嫩的副国务卿帮办墨菲已经在17日到达贝鲁特。他是去进一步策划美国对黎巴嫩和中东国家的侵略活动的。
    这个美国国务院策划侵略阴谋的老手在一到贝鲁特以后，就立即表示要同夏蒙和美国侵略黎巴嫩的军队总司令霍洛威进行商谈。
