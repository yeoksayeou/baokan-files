Title: Kassem Sends Telegram to Grotewohl Thanking Him for Congratulating the Iraqi People
Author:
Date: 1958-07-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 26th: According to a report by the German News Agency: The Prime Minister of the Iraqi Republic, Kassem, sent a telegram to the Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Grotewohl, thanking the German Democratic Republic for congratulating the Iraqi people on their victory.

### 感谢对伊拉克人民的祝贺  卡塞姆致电格罗提渥

1958-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    感谢对伊拉克人民的祝贺
    卡塞姆致电格罗提渥
    据新华社26日讯　据德意志通讯社报道：伊拉克共和国总理卡塞姆打电报给德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥，感谢德意志民主共和国对伊拉克人民胜利的祝贺。
