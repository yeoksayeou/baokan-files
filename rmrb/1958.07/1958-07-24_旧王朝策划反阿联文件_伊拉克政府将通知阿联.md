Title: Old Dynasty Plotted Anti-UAR Documents, Iraqi Government to Notify UAR
Author:
Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 21st: According to a report by the Syrian News Agency, the Iraqi Republic government will notify the United Arab Republic of the contents of all documents related to the conspiracy plotted by the overthrown Faisal dynasty government against the United Arab Republic, particularly against Syria.

### 旧王朝策划反阿联文件  伊拉克政府将通知阿联

1958-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    旧王朝策划反阿联文件
    伊拉克政府将通知阿联
    据新华社大马士革21日电　据叙利亚通讯社报道，伊拉克共和国政府将把有关被推翻的费萨尔王朝政府所策划的反对阿拉伯联合共和国、特别是反对叙利亚的阴谋的所有文件的内容通知阿拉伯联合共和国。
