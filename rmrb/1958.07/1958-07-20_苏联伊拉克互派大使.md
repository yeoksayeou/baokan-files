Title: Soviet Union and Iraq to Exchange Ambassadors
Author:
Date: 1958-07-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 18th: The Soviet government has accepted the proposal of the government of the Republic of Iraq and has decided to exchange diplomatic envoys at the ambassadorial level with the Republic of Iraq.

### 苏联伊拉克互派大使

1958-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联伊拉克互派大使
    据新华社19日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科18日讯：苏联政府已经接受伊拉克共和国政府的建议，决定同伊拉克共和国互派大使级的外交使节。
