Title: General Su Zhenhua Calls on All Officers and Soldiers at the Naval Military Representatives Congress to Strictly Defend the Coast and Fiercely Strike Invading Wolves

Author:
Date: 1958-07-31
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th - At the Second Naval Military Representatives Congress, Naval Admiral Su Zhenhua, Political Commissar of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy, said on the 29th: The US and British imperialism's armed aggression in the Middle East has thoroughly exposed their robber-like faces before the people of the world. We never hope for war, but if the warmongers really go crazy, we will, as in the past, fiercely strike these wolves. All comrades in the army must be ideologically fully prepared, maintain a high degree of vigilance at all times, and celebrate the 31st anniversary of the founding of our army with practical actions to step up war preparations and training, and ensure the security of the motherland's coastal defense.
    The Second Naval Military Representatives Congress lasted eight days and closed on the 29th. This meeting was a review of maritime combat power. More than 300 representatives and attendees at the meeting made comprehensive reports and typical reports on advanced experiences and advanced deeds. These reports reflected that under the brilliant light of the General Line, the Navy has seen an unprecedentedly active situation in combat training and modernization construction, and its combat power has been greatly enhanced.
    During the meeting, the representatives unanimously angrily condemned the aggression of US and British imperialism, and resolved to step up war preparations and training, accelerate the modernization of the Navy, and be ready to contribute everything to opposing aggression, defending the motherland, defending world peace, and liberating the motherland's territory of Taiwan.
    The meeting elected Naval Admiral Wang Hongkun and Major Wang Tianbao as representatives to the Second National People's Congress.

### 苏振华上将在海军军人代表大会上号召全军官兵  严守海防狠狠打击进犯的豺狼

1958-07-31
第4版()
专栏：

    苏振华上将在海军军人代表大会上号召全军官兵
    严守海防狠狠打击进犯的豺狼
    新华社30日讯　中国人民解放军海军政治委员苏振华海军上将29日在海军第二届军人代表大会上说：美英帝国主义武装侵略中东，在全世界人民面前彻底暴露了它们的强盗面目。我们从来不希望战争，但是战争贩子们如果真的要发疯，我们就会像过去一样，狠狠地打击这些豺狼。全军同志在思想上要做好充分准备，时刻保持高度的警惕，以加紧备战训练，确保祖国海防安全的实际行动，庆祝我军建军三十一周年。
    海军第二届军人代表大会历时八天，29日闭幕。这次会议是一次检阅海上战斗力量的大会。出席这次会议的三百多位代表和列席人员在会上作了先进经验和先进事迹的综合报告和典型报告。这些报告反映了在总路线的光辉照耀下，海军在战斗训练和现代化建设等方面，出现了空前活跃的局面，战斗威力已有很大增强。
    会议期间，代表们一致愤怒谴责美英帝国主义的侵略，决心加紧备战训练，加速海军的现代化建设，随时准备为反对侵略、保卫祖国、保卫世界和平和解放祖国的领土台湾贡献自己的一切。
    会议选出了王宏坤海军上将、王天保少校为第二届全国人民代表大会代表。
