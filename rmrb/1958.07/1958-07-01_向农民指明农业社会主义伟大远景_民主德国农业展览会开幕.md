Title: Pointing Out the Great Socialist Prospects for Agriculture to Farmers: Opening of the German Democratic Republic Agricultural Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1958-07-01
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency dispatch: According to a Deutsche Presse-Agentur report on the 23rd, the Sixth Agricultural Exhibition of the German Democratic Republic opened on the 21st near Leipzig.

This year's exhibition surpasses previous ones in terms of both scale and variety. It showcases the achievements of the more than 7,880 agricultural production cooperatives currently existing in the German Democratic Republic on a large area, demonstrating the unparalleled superiority of cooperative large-scale production over individual farmers in all aspects. The exhibition also displays more than 400 types of agricultural machinery and accessories manufactured by the state-owned agricultural machinery industry of the German Democratic Republic. Prime Minister Grotewohl and government delegations from Czechoslovakia, Poland, Hungary, Romania, Mongolia, North Korea, and other countries attended the opening ceremony on the 21st.

In its congratulatory message to the Sixth Agricultural Exhibition, the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic pointed out that the main task of this year's exhibition is to point out the great socialist prospects in agriculture to all farmers.

### 向农民指明农业社会主义伟大远景  民主德国农业展览会开幕

1958-07-01
第5版()
专栏：

    向农民指明农业社会主义伟大远景
    民主德国农业展览会开幕
    据新华社讯　据德意志通讯社23日报道，民主德国第六届农业展览会21日在来比锡近郊开幕。
    今年展览会在规模巨大和丰富多彩方面都超过以往各届。它以很大的面积展出了民主德国现有的七千八百八十多个农业生产合作社的成就，显示了合作化大规模生产在各方面胜过单干农户的无比优越性。展览会还展出了民主德国国营农业机器工业制造的四百多种农业机器和附件。格罗提渥总理和捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、蒙古、朝鲜等国的政府代表团参加了21日的开幕典礼。
    民主德国部长会议在给第六届农业展览会的贺辞中指出，今年展览会的主要任务是要向全体农民指明农业中社会主义的伟大远景。
