Title: Where to Buy a Powerless Radio

Author:

Date: 1958-07-03
Page: 3

Column: Letters from Readers

    Where to Buy a Powerless Radio
    After this newspaper published the news that the Nanjing Radio Factory had produced a powerless radio, we frequently received letters from readers asking when this radio would be available on the market. According to our information: The Nanjing Radio Factory has been producing this radio since April, and all of it is exclusively sold by the Shanghai Comprehensive Industrial Company (66 Nanjing East Road, Shanghai). If you want to buy one, please contact them directly.

### 向哪里买无电源收音机

1958-07-03
第3版()
专栏：来信

    向哪里买无电源收音机
    本报发表南京有线电厂制成无电源收音机的消息后，经常有读者来信问这种收音机什么时候上市。据我们了解：南京有线电厂已自4月份开始生产这种收音机，全部由上海综合工业公司（上海南京东路66号）包销。要买的，请直接和他们联系。
