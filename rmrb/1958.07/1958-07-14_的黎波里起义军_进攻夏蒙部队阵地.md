Title: Tripoli Insurgents Attack Positions of Chamoun's Troops
Author:
Date: 1958-07-14
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 13th: According to reports in the newspapers here today, Lebanese insurgent forces attacked positions held by Chamoun's troops in the port of Tripoli on July 12th.

The newspapers also said that government troops, under the cover of heavy artillery and armored vehicles and aided by fascist elements from the National Socialist Party, attempted to enter the old city but suffered a crushing defeat.

Reports from Lebanon indicate that on July 12th, fierce explosions occurred in Zahlé, east of the capital Beirut, and in Rayak, north of Zahlé. Sources say that the government building in Zahlé and the post and telecommunications office and customs office in Rayak have been damaged.

Damascus' "Al-Ra'i al-A'am" newspaper today quoted the Middle East News Agency as saying that Israeli convoys crossed the Lebanese border on July 12th and handed over weapons to members of the National Socialist Party.

### 的黎波里起义军  进攻夏蒙部队阵地

1958-07-14
第6版()
专栏：

    的黎波里起义军
    进攻夏蒙部队阵地
    据新华社大马士革13日电　据这里的报纸今天报道，黎巴嫩起义人民部队在12日曾经向的黎波里港的夏蒙部队阵地进攻。
    报纸又说，其后政府军在重炮和装甲车的掩护下试图在国家社会党法西斯党徒的帮助下进入旧城，但是遭到了惨败。
    来自黎巴嫩的消息说，12日，在首都贝鲁特以东的扎勒和扎勒以北的拉亚克都曾经发生猛烈爆炸。有消息说，扎勒的政府大厦和拉亚克的邮电局和海关都已经损坏。
    大马士革“舆论报”今天援引中东通讯社的消息说，以色列车队在12日曾经越过黎巴嫩的边界，并且把武器交给国家社会党人员。
