Title: Iraqi Armed Forces Command Orders Troops in Lebanon to Join Rebel Army
Author:
Date: 1958-07-15
Page: 5

    Iraqi Armed Forces Command
    Orders Troops in Lebanon to Join Rebel Army
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 14th. Baghdad Radio announced this morning that the Iraqi Armed Forces Command has instructed Iraqi troops in Lebanon to join the Lebanese people's rebel forces.
    The radio station said that the Iraqi Armed Forces Command also instructed Iraqi pilots in Lebanon to join the air force of the Lebanese people's rebel forces.

### 伊拉克武装部队司令部  指令在黎部队参加起义军

1958-07-15
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克武装部队司令部
    指令在黎部队参加起义军
    新华社开罗14日电　巴格达电台今天上午宣布，伊拉克武装部队司令部已经指令在黎巴嫩的伊拉克部队参加黎巴嫩人民起义部队。
    这个电台说，伊拉克武装部队司令部还指令伊拉克在黎巴嫩的驾驶员参加黎巴嫩人民起义部队的空军。
