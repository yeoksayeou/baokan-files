Title: "Thirteen Tombs Reservoir Completion Ceremony" Premieres in the Capital Today

Author:

Date: 1958-07-02
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 1st -- The newsreel short "Thirteen Tombs Reservoir Completion Ceremony" produced by the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio, is prepared to meet audiences in the capital on the evening of July 2nd.

    The film content is divided into three parts: (1) In the final stages of the reservoir's construction, this part shows the sky-high enthusiasm of the 100,000-strong labor force, displaying the great power of the people. (2) Introducing the reservoir, depicting the scenery of the reservoir. (3) Recording the celebration rally, including the enthusiastic scenes of victory and revelry.

### 首都今天上映  “十三陵水库落成典礼”

1958-07-02
第7版()
专栏：

    首都今天上映
    “十三陵水库落成典礼”
    新华社1日讯中央新闻纪录电影制片厂摄制的“十三陵水库落成典礼”短片，准备在7月2日晚上和首都观众见面。
    影片内容分为三个部分：（一）在水库的最后工程中，这一部分表现了十万劳动大军的干劲冲天，显示人民的伟大力量。（二）介绍水库，描写水库的风光。（三）纪录庆祝大会，包括祝捷和狂欢的热烈场面。
