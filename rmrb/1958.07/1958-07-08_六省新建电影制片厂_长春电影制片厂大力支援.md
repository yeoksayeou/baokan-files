Title: Six Provinces Establish New Film Studios: Changchun Film Studio Provides Strong Support
Author:
Date: 1958-07-08
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, July 7th: Changchun Film Studio has decided to vigorously support the establishment of new film studios in Shaanxi, Inner Mongolia, Guangxi, Heilongjiang, Liaoning, and Shanxi.

According to the agreement signed on the 5th, "Changying" will first transfer more than one hundred technical cadres to equip each new factory with technical backbones, including technical负责人 (technical directors), developing and printing technicians, feature film cinematographers, sound recordists, and sound recording mechanics. At the same time, through intensive training and internships, it will cultivate a complete set of technical talents for each new factory, approximately three hundred fifty to sixty people. In addition, "Changying" will also be responsible for the process design of several new factories and support some machinery and equipment.

### 六省新建电影制片厂  长春电影制片厂大力支援

1958-07-08
第7版()
专栏：

    六省新建电影制片厂
    长春电影制片厂大力支援
    新华社长春7日电  长春电影制片厂决定大力支援陕西、内蒙古、广西、黑龙江、辽宁、山西等地新建电影制片厂。
    根据5日签订的协议书，“长影”将首先调出一百多名技术干部，为每个新建厂配备技术骨干，包括技术负责人、洗印技师、故事片摄影师、录音师、录音机械员等；同时，采取集训实习的办法，为各新厂培养全套技术人才约有三百五、六十人。此外，“长影”还要担负几个新厂的工艺设计，和支援部分机器设备。
