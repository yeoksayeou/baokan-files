Title: Noun Explanation: 成材 (chéngcái) - "Mature Timber"
Author:
Date: 1958-07-08
Page: 2

    Noun Explanation
    Mature Timber:
    According to the general acquisition standards of purchasing departments, if the diameter (through the center) of a tree at a height of three chi and nine cun (approximately 1.3 meters or 4.27 feet) from the ground reaches five cun and four fen (approximately 18 centimeters or 7.09 inches), it is considered mature timber. Generally, it takes fifteen to eighteen years for a fir tree to grow to maturity from planting.

### 名词解释  成材：

1958-07-08
第2版()
专栏：

    名词解释
    成材：
    按照收购部门一般的收购标准，树木离地面三尺九寸高的地方，直径（过心）达到五寸四分，就算成材。一般杉树从种植到成材需要十五年到十八年。
