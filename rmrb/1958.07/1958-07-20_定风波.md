To the Tune of Ding Fengbo
Li Jishen
Oppose the US and British imperialist aggression against Lebanon and Jordan.

	Anti-imperialist Middle East surges with angry tides,
	Colonialism's fate wavers and slides.
	Baghdad city startles from its illusory dreams, real pain!
	Afro-Asian solidarity grows more firm.
	The United States and Britain collude in disgrace with the Jews, provoking disaster.
	How can peace and justice be spared.
	In the blink of an eye, the treasure mountain becomes a fiery pit, burning away.
	Tenaciously resisting, they will certainly topple their nests.

### 定风波
李济深
1958-07-20
第2版()
专栏：

    定风波
    李济深
    反对美、英帝国侵略黎巴嫩、约旦。
    反帝中东涌怒潮，
    殖民主义命漂摇。
    巴格达城惊幻梦，真痛！
    亚非团结更坚牢。
    狼狈美英犹合伙，挑祸。
    和平正义岂能饶。
    转眼宝山成火窟，烧没。
    负嵎蛮干定倾巢。
