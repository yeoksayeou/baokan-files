Title: Chairman Mao, Chairman Liu, Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Messages on the 14th Anniversary of Polish National Day; Foreign Minister Chen Yi Sends Congratulatory Message to Foreign Minister Rapacki
Author:
Date: 1958-07-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 21st: Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai today sent a telegram to Władysław Gomułka, First Secretary of the Polish United Worker's Party, Aleksander Zawadzki, Chairman of the Council of State, and Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, congratulating them on the 14th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of Poland. The telegram reads as follows:

To Comrade Władysław Gomułka, First Secretary of the Polish United Worker's Party:
To Comrade Aleksander Zawadzki, Chairman of the Council of State of the People's Republic of Poland:
To Comrade Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Poland:

On the occasion of the 14th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of Poland, we, on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, extend our warm congratulations to you, and through you, to the government and people of the People's Republic of Poland.

The Chinese people are watching with joy as the Polish people, under the leadership of the Polish United Worker's Party and the government of the People's Republic of Poland, achieve great successes in the struggle to develop the national economy and consolidate the socialist system, and in the cause of easing international tensions and safeguarding peace in Europe and the world.

Over the past year, the friendship and cooperation between the people of China and Poland have achieved new developments in all aspects. The development of this friendly and cooperative relationship not only helps to accelerate the socialist construction of our two countries, but also strengthens the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

May the fraternal friendship between the people of China and Poland be increasingly consolidated and developed.

Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party
Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
July 21, 1958, Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, July 21st: Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, today sent a telegram to Adam Rapacki, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Poland, congratulating him on the 14th anniversary of Polish National Day. The telegram reads as follows:

To Comrade Adam Rapacki, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Poland:

On the occasion of the 14th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of Poland, please accept my heartfelt congratulations, Comrade Minister. I wish you new achievements in your struggle to implement the Polish government's peaceful foreign policy and safeguard peace in Europe and the world. I wish the Polish people prosperity and strength. I wish the fraternal friendship between the people of China and Poland will be increasingly consolidated.

Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
July 21, 1958, Beijing

### 毛主席  刘委员长  周总理  电贺波兰国庆十四周年  陈毅外长电拉帕茨基外长祝贺

1958-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席  刘委员长  周总理
    电贺波兰国庆十四周年
    陈毅外长电拉帕茨基外长祝贺
    新华社21日讯  毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理今天致电波兰统一工人党第一书记哥穆尔卡、国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基、部长会议主席西伦凯维兹，祝贺波兰人民共和国国庆十四周年。电文如下：波兰统一工人党第一书记瓦迪斯瓦夫·哥穆尔卡同志：波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席亚历山大·萨瓦茨基同志：波兰人民共和国部长会议主席约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹同志：
    当波兰人民共和国国庆十四周年的时候，我们谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向你们，并且通过你们向波兰人民共和国政府和人民致以热烈的祝贺。
    中国人民怀着欢欣的心情注视着波兰人民在波兰统一工人党和波兰人民共和国政府的领导下，在发展国民经济和巩固社会主义制度的斗争中，在缓和国际紧张局势、维护欧洲和世界和平的事业中取得了很大的成就。
    一年来，中波两国人民的友好和合作在各方面又获得了新的发展，这种友好合作关系的发展，不仅有助于加速我们两国的社会主义建设，而且加强了以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结。
    祝中波两国人民的兄弟友谊日益巩固和发展。中华人民共和国主席中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇中华人民共和国国务院总理  周恩来
                1958年7月21日于北京
    新华社21日讯  中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅，今天致电波兰人民共和国外交部长拉帕茨基，祝贺波兰国庆十四周年。电文如下：波兰人民共和国外交部长阿达姆·拉帕茨基同志：
    值此波兰人民共和国国庆十四周年之际，部长同志，请接受我衷心的祝贺。祝你在执行波兰政府的和平外交政策、维护欧洲和世界和平事业的斗争中获得的新成就。祝波兰人民繁荣昌盛。祝中波人民的兄弟友谊日益巩固。
          中华人民共和国外交部长  陈  毅
                        1958年7月21日于北京
