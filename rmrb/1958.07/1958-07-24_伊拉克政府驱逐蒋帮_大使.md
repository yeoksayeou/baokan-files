Title: Iraqi Government Expels Chiang Kai-shek Gang "Ambassador"
Author:
Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 22nd. The Iraqi Republic Radio broadcasted that the Iraqi Foreign Minister Jamal met with Chen Chih-ping, the "ambassador" of the Chiang Kai-shek clique in Taiwan, on the 22nd to discuss the matter of his departure from Iraq.

### 伊拉克政府驱逐蒋帮“大使”

1958-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

    伊拉克政府驱逐蒋帮“大使”
    新华社开罗22日电　据伊拉克共和国电台广播，伊拉克外交部长贾迈尔22日接见了台湾蒋介石集团的“大使”陈质平，讨论要他离开伊拉克的问题。
