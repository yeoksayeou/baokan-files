Title: Korean People Sign Letters of Thanks to the Chinese People's Volunteers

Author:

Date: 1958-07-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 19th. According to the Korean Central News Agency: The activities of people from all walks of life in northern Korea signing letters of thanks to the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Chinese people are continuing enthusiastically. The Central Committee of the Korean People's Committee to Welcome and See Off the Chinese People's Volunteers reported that, as of July 15, 5.5 million people had signed the letters of thanks.

The people from various regions who participated in the signing expressed their determination to repay the selfless assistance of the 600 million Chinese people by enhancing Korean-Chinese friendship and successfully completing the First Five-Year Plan.

### 朝鲜人民签名感谢志愿军

1958-07-20
第6版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民签名感谢志愿军
    新华社平壤19日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社消息：朝鲜北部各界人民在给中国人民志愿军和中国人民的感谢信上签名的活动，正继续热烈进行。朝鲜欢送中国人民志愿军中央委员会统计：到7月15日，已有五百五十万人在感谢信上签名。
    参加签名的各地人民表示决心以增进朝中友谊，胜利完成第一个五年计划，来报答六亿中国人民的无私援助。
