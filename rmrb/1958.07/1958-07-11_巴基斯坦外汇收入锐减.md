Title: Pakistan's Foreign Exchange Earnings Plummet

Author: 
Date: 1958-07-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, July 9th: Under the influence of the economic crisis in the United States and other Western countries, Pakistan's foreign exchange earnings have been greatly reduced. It has been announced that Pakistan's foreign exchange earnings in 1957 were 152 million pounds, and in 1958 it is likely to drop to about 120 million pounds.

The economic crisis in the United States and other Western countries has affected the sales of raw materials produced in Pakistan. Demand for cotton from Western countries has decreased, and the price of cotton has also fallen by 25%.

### 巴基斯坦外汇收入锐减

1958-07-11
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦外汇收入锐减
    新华社卡拉奇9日电　在美国等西方国家经济危机的影响下，巴基斯坦的外汇收入大大减少。据宣布，巴基斯坦1957年的外汇收入为一亿五千二百万英镑，1958年有可能下降到一亿二千万英镑左右。
    美国和其他西方国家的经济危机影响到巴基斯坦出产的原料的销路。西方国家对棉花的需求已经减少，棉花的价格也下降了25％。
