Title: Austrian Government Delegation Leaves Soviet Union for Home; Khrushchev Says Soviet-Austrian Talks Help Develop Bilateral Relations
Author:
Date: 1958-07-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 28th (TASS): The government delegation of the Republic of Austria, headed by Prime Minister Raab, left Moscow by plane for home on the 28th.

Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Khrushchev, and others were at Vnukovo Airport to see them off.

The Austrian government delegation warmly said goodbye to the well-wishers at the airport. In his speech at the airport, Raab especially thanked the Soviet government for its warm reception and once again invited Khrushchev to visit Austria.

In his speech, Khrushchev expressed his gratitude for the Austrian Prime Minister's invitation. He said that the talks in Moscow between the two countries helped to further improve bilateral relations. The consensus of the two countries' interests in the development of economic relations and the safeguarding of peace and international security is the basic starting point for the development of bilateral relations.

### 奥政府代表团离苏返国  赫鲁晓夫说苏奥会谈有助于发展两国关系

1958-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

    奥政府代表团离苏返国
    赫鲁晓夫说苏奥会谈有助于发展两国关系
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：以拉布总理为首的奥地利共和国政府代表团28日乘飞机离莫斯科回国。
    苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫等亲到伏努科夫机场送行。
    奥地利政府代表团在机场上亲切地同欢送者告别。拉布在机场上讲话中特别感谢苏联政府的热情接待，并再一次邀请赫鲁晓夫访问奥地利。
    赫鲁晓夫在讲话中对奥地利总理的邀请表示感谢。他说，两国在莫斯科的会谈有助于进一步改善两国关系，两国在经济关系的发展和保卫和平和国际安全问题上利益的一致是发展两国关系的基本出发点。
