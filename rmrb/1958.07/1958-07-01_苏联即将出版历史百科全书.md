Title: Soviet Union to Publish Historical Encyclopedia
Author:
Date: 1958-07-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 29th: The Soviet Union has decided to publish a twelve-volume "Soviet Historical Encyclopedia," according to TASS. This major reference work will chronicle the history of both the Soviet Union and foreign countries. It is the world's first encyclopedia to record the history of all countries from primitive communes to the modern world.

### 苏联即将出版历史百科全书

1958-07-01
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联即将出版
    历史百科全书
    据新华社30日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科29日讯：苏联决定出版十二卷本的“苏联历史百科全书”。这部大参考书将记述苏联本国和外国的历史。这是世界上第一部记载从原始公社一直到现代世界各国历史的百科全书。
