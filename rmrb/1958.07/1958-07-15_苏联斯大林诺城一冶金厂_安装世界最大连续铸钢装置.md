Title: Soviet Stalino Metallurgical Plant Installs World's Largest Continuous Steel Casting Device
Author:
Date: 1958-07-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 14th. TASS reports: A metallurgical plant in Stalino, Soviet Union, is installing the world's largest continuous steel casting device.

Using this device, carbon steel and low-alloy steel can be cast simultaneously into four rolling grooves from a 140-ton molten steel tank. Four intermediate cast iron buckets, each with a capacity of fourteen tons, will be installed in the casting yard and hydraulic lifting turntables.

This type of device can cast slabs of various thicknesses from one hundred and twenty to two hundred and fifty millimeters thick and six hundred to one thousand two hundred millimeters wide. The device consists of four independently operated machines, which are placed in a large pit with a diameter of twenty-five meters. The casting yard will be three meters higher than the workshop floor, and the bottom of the pit will be twenty-four meters lower than the workshop floor.

The entire production process of this device is mechanized and automated. Staff can control it through the console. In addition, television screens are prepared to be installed to remotely inspect the operation of the device.

### 苏联斯大林诺城一冶金厂  安装世界最大连续铸钢装置

1958-07-15
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联斯大林诺城一冶金厂
    安装世界最大连续铸钢装置
    新华社14日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联斯大林诺城的一个冶金厂正在安装世界上最大的一套连续铸钢装置。
    利用这套装置，可以由一百四十吨的钢水罐同时向四个轧槽浇铸碳素钢和低合金钢。在浇铸场和水压升降回转台将安设四个中间铸铁桶，其容量各为十四吨。
    这种结构的装置可以浇铸厚一百二十到二百五十公厘，宽六百到一千二百公厘的各种板坯。这种装置是由四个单独操纵的机器组成的，它安置在直径为二十五公尺的大坑内。浇铸场将比车间地平面高出三公尺，而坑底要比车间地平面低二十四公尺。
    这种装置的整个生产过程都是机械化和自动化的。工作人员可以通过操纵台来控制。此外还准备装设电视屏来对这套装置的工作情况进行远距离的视察。
