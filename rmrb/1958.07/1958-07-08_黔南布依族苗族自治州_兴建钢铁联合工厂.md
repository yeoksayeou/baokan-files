Title: Qiannan Buyei and Miao Autonomous Prefecture to Build Iron and Steel Joint Factory
Author:
Date: 1958-07-08
Page: 3

In high-speed progress

    Qiannan Buyei and Miao Autonomous Prefecture
    To Build Iron and Steel Joint Factory
    Xinhua News Agency, Guiyang, July 7th: The Qiannan Buyei and Miao Autonomous Prefecture is building an iron and steel joint factory in Duyun City.
    This steel plant will build five main workshops for ironmaking, steelmaking, steel rolling, power, and machine repair. The first phase of the project will be completed by the end of this year and put into production in the first quarter of next year. The entire construction project is expected to be completed in 1962. The annual production capacity is 90,000 tons of pig iron, 50,000 tons of steel, and 40,000 tons of steel products.
    Iron ore is abundant near Duyun. The iron ore discovered between Dushan and Sandu alone has reserves of approximately 170 million tons.

### 黔南布依族苗族自治州  兴建钢铁联合工厂

1958-07-08
第3版()
专栏：在高速度前进中

    黔南布依族苗族自治州
    兴建钢铁联合工厂
    新华社贵阳7日电  黔南布依族苗族自治州在都匀市兴建钢铁联合工厂。
    这座钢铁厂将要建设炼铁、炼钢、轧钢、动力和机修等五个主要车间。第一期工程将在今年年底完成，明年第一季度就可以投入生产。全部建设工程预计在1962年完成。年产能力是生铁九万吨、钢五万吨、钢材四万吨。
    都匀附近铁矿蕴藏丰富。仅独山和三都之间发现的铁矿，储量在一亿七千万吨左右。
