Title: Soviet Military Delegation Visits the United Arab Republic
Author:
Date: 1958-07-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 8th: A six-member Soviet military delegation, led by Soviet Air Force Chief of Staff, Air Marshal Rudenko, arrived in Cairo on the afternoon of the 8th. The delegation is visiting the United Arab Republic for ten days at the invitation of Marshal Amer, Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of the United Arab Republic.

The Chief of Staff of the Air Force of the Egyptian Region of the United Arab Republic, Air Force Lieutenant General Sidqi Mahmoud, and other senior military officers greeted the delegation at the airport.

The Soviet military delegation will visit military installations, air bases, and other locations in the two regions of the United Arab Republic.

### 苏军事代表团访问阿联

1958-07-10
第5版()
专栏：

    苏军事代表团访问阿联
    新华社开罗8日电  由苏联空军总参谋长鲁金科空军元帅率领的由六人组成的苏联军事代表团在8日下午到达开罗。这个代表团应阿拉伯联合共和国武装部队总司令阿密尔元帅的邀请，前来阿拉伯联合共和国进行十天的访问。
    阿拉伯联合共和国埃及地区空军参谋长西德基·马哈茂德空军中将和其他高级军官都到机场迎接。
    苏联军事代表团将参观阿拉伯联合共和国两个地区的军事设备、空军基地和其他一些地方。
