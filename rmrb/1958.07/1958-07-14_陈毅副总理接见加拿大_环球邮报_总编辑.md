Title: Chen Yi, Vice Premier, Receives Editor-in-Chief of Canadian "The Globe and Mail"
Author:
Date: 1958-07-14
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, July 13th. Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi received Dalglish, the Editor-in-Chief of the Canadian "The Globe and Mail" this evening.
    Present at the meeting were Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Shen Jian, Deputy Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs.

### 陈毅副总理接见加拿大“环球邮报”总编辑

1958-07-14
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见加拿大“环球邮报”总编辑
    新华社13日讯　陈毅副总理兼外交部长在今天晚间接见了加拿大“环球邮报”总编辑达耳格里希。
    在座的，有外交部新闻司副司长徐晃和美澳司副司长申健。
