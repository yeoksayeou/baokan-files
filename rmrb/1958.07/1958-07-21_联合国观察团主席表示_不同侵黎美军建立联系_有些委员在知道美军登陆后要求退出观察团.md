Title: UN Observer Group Chairman States They Are Not Establishing Contact with Invading U.S. Troops in Lebanon; Some Members Requested to Withdraw from Observer Group After Learning of U.S. Troop Landing
Author:
Date: 1958-07-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, 20th -- The chairman of the United Nations observer group in Lebanon, Mr. Prasath, told reporters that when the first detachment of U.S. Marines landed in Lebanon on July 15, he recalled the observers from their work locations to headquarters. Prasath said: "We do not intend to establish contact with these (U.S.) Marines. Our mandate does not permit this."

According to the Indian Press Trust, the UN observer group considers the American troops here as "foreign troops on Lebanese territory" and said, "In Lebanon, there is no basis for establishing any contact between the observer group and any non-Lebanese troops."

It is reported that some members of the observer group, upon learning that American troops had landed in Lebanon, requested to withdraw from the observer group, believing that they were "of little use here" after the U.S. Marines landed. It was only due to the persuasion of UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld that they did not withdraw.

### 联合国观察团主席表示  不同侵黎美军建立联系  有些委员在知道美军登陆后要求退出观察团

1958-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    联合国观察团主席表示
    不同侵黎美军建立联系
    有些委员在知道美军登陆后要求退出观察团
    新华社20日讯  贝鲁特消息：在黎巴嫩的联合国观察团主席蒲拉沙对记者说，在7月15日美国第一支海军陆战队在黎巴嫩登陆时，他就把观察员从工作地点召回到总部去。蒲拉沙说：“我们不打算同这些（美国）海军陆战队建立联系。我们的职权不允许这样做。”
    据印度报业托辣斯报道，联合国观察团认为美国军队在这里是“黎巴嫩领土上的外国军队”，并且说，“在黎巴嫩，观察团和任何非黎巴嫩军队之间没有建立任何联系的基础”。
    据悉，观察团有些委员一知道美国军队在黎巴嫩登陆后，就要求退出观察团，他们认为美国海军陆战队登陆后，他们在这里就“没有多大用处了”。只是由于联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德的劝说，他们才没有退出。
