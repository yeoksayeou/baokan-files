Title: Turkish "Newspaper" Condemns US Interference in Lebanese Internal Affairs
Author:
Date: 1958-07-22
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Istanbul, 20th - The Turkish "Newspaper" condemned the United States for invading Lebanon after the United Nations Observer Group submitted its report. The newspaper said that this has further worsened the situation in the Near East.

　　The newspaper believes that the Lebanese event is an internal struggle between the opposition and the government. The opposition wants to make the country an independent and free country, while the government wants to cooperate with Britain and the United States.

　　The newspaper wrote that interference in Lebanon's internal affairs may cause serious consequences.

### 土耳其“新报”  斥美干涉黎内政

1958-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

    土耳其“新报”
    斥美干涉黎内政
    新华社21日讯  塔斯社伊斯坦布尔20日讯：土耳其“新报”谴责美国在联合国观察团提出报告之后侵略黎巴嫩。报纸说，这使中近东的局势更加恶化。
    报纸认为，黎巴嫩事件是反对派与政府之间的内部斗争，反对派想使国家成为独立的和自由的国家，政府则想同英美合作。
    报纸写道，对黎巴嫩内政的干涉可能引起严重后果。
