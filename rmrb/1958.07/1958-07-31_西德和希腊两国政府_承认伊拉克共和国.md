Title: West Germany and Greece Recognize the Republic of Iraq
Author: 
Date: 1958-07-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 30th. According to a report from the West German News Agency in Bonn: The West German government recognized the Republic of Iraq on the 30th. The West German ambassador to Iraq has informed the government of the Republic of Iraq of the West German government's decision.

Xinhua News Agency, July 30th, Athens dispatch: Greek Foreign Minister Averoff announced in Athens on the 30th that he had telegraphed the Foreign Minister of the Republic of Iraq, notifying him that the Greek government recognizes the Republic of Iraq.

Greece is the first country in the North Atlantic Treaty Organization to recognize the Republic of Iraq.

### 西德和希腊两国政府  承认伊拉克共和国

1958-07-31
第3版()
专栏：

    西德和希腊两国政府
    承认伊拉克共和国
    新华社柏林30日电　据西德德意志新闻社波恩消息：西德政府已经在30日承认伊拉克共和国，西德驻伊拉克大使已经把西德政府的决定告诉伊拉克共和国政府。
    新华社30日讯　雅典消息：希腊外交大臣阿维罗夫30日在雅典宣布，他已经打电报给伊拉克共和国外交部长，通知他希腊政府承认伊拉克共和国。
    希腊是北大西洋集团中第一个承认伊拉克共和国的国家。
