Title: Chairman Mao and Central Committee Leaders Receive Representatives of Three Conferences and a Visiting Group
Author: 
Date: 1958-07-17
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Chairman Mao Zedong and CCP Central Committee leaders Deng Xiaoping, Peng Zhen, Peng Dehuai, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, and Nie Rongzhen, this afternoon successively received the representatives of the National Folk Literature Workers Conference, the delegation from Guizhou Province visiting the National Agricultural Tool Exhibition, the representatives of the National Women's Work Conference, and the representatives of the National United Front Work Conference.

### 毛主席和中共中央领导人接见三个会议的代表和一个参观团

1958-07-17
第5版()
专栏：

    毛主席和中共中央领导人接见三个会议的代表和一个参观团
    新华社16日讯　毛泽东主席和中共中央领导人员邓小平、彭真、彭德怀、陈毅、李富春、李先念、聂荣臻，今天下午先后接见了全国民间文学工作者大会的代表、贵州省参观全国农具展览会的代表团、全国妇女工作会议的代表和全国统战工作会议的代表。
