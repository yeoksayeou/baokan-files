Title: "Protest Against American Aggression in Lebanon"
Author:
Date: 1958-07-19
Page: 8

    "Protest Against American Aggression in Lebanon"
    The Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio has produced a documentary short film of the rally of 500,000 people in Tiananmen Square in Beijing on the 17th of this month to protest against American aggression in Lebanon. Determined to support the Arab people's liberation struggle with practical actions, the crew worked tirelessly overnight and completed the film in only seventeen hours. The film, entitled "Protest Against American Aggression in Lebanon," was screened in news cinemas in Beijing yesterday afternoon.

### “抗议美国侵略黎巴嫩”

1958-07-19
第8版()
专栏：舞台与银幕

    “抗议美国侵略黎巴嫩”
    北京五十万人本月17日在天安门广场集会抗议美国侵略黎巴嫩的实况，已由中央新闻纪录电影制片厂摄制成纪录短片。摄制组工作人员决心以实际行动支援阿拉伯人民的解放斗争，连夜紧张地工作，只用十七小时就将这部影片摄制完成。影片的名字是“抗议美国侵略黎巴嫩”，已于昨天下午在北京各新闻影院映出。
