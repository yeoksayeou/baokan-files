Title: Dulles Discusses New Soviet Proposals
Author:
Date: 1958-07-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 4th July: U.S. Secretary of State Dulles, at the White House after meeting with Eisenhower, briefly commented at reporters' request on the letter of July 2, 1958, from Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev to Eisenhower. Dulles said that the new Soviet proposals on preventing surprise attacks brought "some hope" for technical military talks between East and West. But he also said that some of the Soviet proposals were unacceptable.

It is reported that Eisenhower is currently studying the Soviet proposals.

### 杜勒斯谈苏联新建议

1958-07-05
第5版()
专栏：

    杜勒斯谈苏联新建议
    新华社4日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国务卿杜勒斯在白宫同艾森豪威尔会谈后应记者请求对苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫1958年7月2日给艾森豪威尔的信作了简短的评述。杜勒斯说，苏联对防止突然进攻问题的新建议为东西方举行技术性的军事会谈带来“一些希望”。但是他又说，苏联建议有某些是不能接受的。
    据报道，苏联的建议目前正由艾森豪威尔研究中。
