Title: Global Public Opinion Celebrates Iraqi Revolution Victory, Calls for Immediate Halt to U.S. Adventurism in the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1958-07-17
Page: 3

(Comprehensive report from our newspaper) Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The great victory of the Iraqi People's Revolution has become the focus of attention worldwide, arousing enthusiastic cheers from people of all countries. Many newspapers, including Vietnam's "People's Daily," Cairo's "Al-Ahram" and "Al-Gomhuria," Damascus' "Al-Nasr" and "Al-Nour," India's "United India," "National Herald," Pakistan's "Today," Jakarta's "People's Daily," "Merdeka," Albania's "Bashkimi," Romania's "Scînteia," East Germany's "Neues Deutschland," Sweden's "Ny Dag," Poland's "Trybuna Ludu," and others, have published commentaries celebrating the establishment of the Republic of Iraq. They unanimously point out that this is a tremendous victory for the Afro-Asian national liberation movement and a significant defeat for imperialism. "Al-Ahram" stated that the imperialist fortress in the Middle East has finally been breached. Cairo's "Al-Masaa" said that with the loss of its huge base in Iraq, imperialism's bases elsewhere will fall one after another.

Global public opinion has angrily condemned the U.S. armed aggression against Lebanon, calling for an immediate halt to the imperialist robbers' military adventurism in the Middle East. "The Indian Express" pointed out that the U.S. invasion "is a blatant violation of international law and international conventions." The British "Daily Worker" pointed out that the U.S. and British military adventurism seriously threatens world peace. It called on the British people to immediately protest and demonstrate against the U.S. and British armed aggression in the Middle East.

Maulana Bhashani, General Secretary of the Pakistan National Awami Party, and Azhari, leader of the Sudanese National Union Party, also issued statements on the 15th congratulating the victory of the Iraqi revolution. The Liaison Committee of Afro-Asian Students in Britain also issued a statement congratulating the establishment of the Republic of Iraq. The All-Japan Federation of Student Associations submitted a letter of protest to the U.S. Embassy in Tokyo on the 16th, firmly opposing U.S. aggression. The General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, the Socialist Party, the Communist Party, and others have all issued statements demanding the withdrawal of U.S. troops from Lebanon.

### 各国舆论欢庆伊拉克革命胜利  呼吁立即制止美国在中东的冒险行动

1958-07-17
第3版()
专栏：

    各国舆论欢庆伊拉克革命胜利
    呼吁立即制止美国在中东的冒险行动
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：伊拉克人民革命的伟大胜利，已经成为全世界舆论注意的中心，引起了各国人民的热烈欢呼。越南“人民报”、开罗“金字塔报”和“共和国报”、大马士革“胜利报”和“光明报”、印度“团结报”、“国民先驱报”、巴基斯坦“今天报”、雅加达“人民日报”、“独立报”、阿尔巴尼亚“团结报”、罗马尼亚“火花报”、民主德国“新德意志报”、瑞典“新日报”、波兰“人民论坛报”等许多报纸纷纷发表评论，欢呼伊拉克共和国的成立，一致指出这是亚非民族解放运动的巨大胜利，是帝国主义的重大失败。“金字塔报”说，帝国主义在中东的堡垒终于被攻破了。开罗“晚报”说，由于帝国主义失掉了伊拉克的巨大基地，它在其他地方的基地也要一个接一个地完蛋。
    各国舆论愤怒地谴责美国武装侵略黎巴嫩，呼吁立即制止帝国主义强盗在中东的军事冒险行动。“印度快报”指出，美国的侵略行动“是悍然破坏国际法和国际公约的行为”。英国“工人日报”指出，美英的军事冒险严重地威胁世界和平。它号召英国人民立即提出抗议和举行示威，反对美英在中东的武装侵略。
    巴基斯坦民族人民党总书记乌斯马尼和苏丹民族联合党领袖阿扎里也在15日分别发表谈话，祝贺伊拉克革命的胜利。在英国留学的亚非国家学生联络委员会也发表声明，祝贺伊拉克共和国成立。全日本学生自治会总联合会16日向美国驻东京大使馆提交抗议书，坚决反对美国的侵略活动，日本工会总评议会、社会党、共产党等都发表声明，要求美军撤出黎巴嫩。
