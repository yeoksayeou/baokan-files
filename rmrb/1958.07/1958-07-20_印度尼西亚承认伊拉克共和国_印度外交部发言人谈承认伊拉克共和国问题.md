Title: Indonesia Recognizes the Republic of Iraq; Indian Foreign Ministry Spokesman Discusses the Recognition of the Republic of Iraq

Author:
Date: 1958-07-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 19th: The Indonesian cabinet held an emergency meeting today from 17:00 to 20:00, at which it was decided to recognize the Republic of Iraq.

The cabinet issued a communique after the meeting stating that the cabinet meeting on July 19th, after studying the situation in the Middle East, considered: 1. Avoiding the outbreak of a third world war; 2. Reducing or eliminating foreign interference, especially interference in small countries; 3. Helping the development of sound nationalism; 4. Implementing and strengthening the Bandung Conference principles; and decided to recognize the Republic of Iraq.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 19th: An Indian Foreign Ministry spokesman explained to Xinhua reporters this evening the reasons why the Indian government has not yet recognized the Republic of Iraq, saying that so far, India has not received a formal request from the Iraqi side regarding the recognition issue. The spokesman said, "If there are any signs or phenomena of foreign interference in Iraq, we will not wait for a request and will immediately recognize the new government."

Diplomatic activities continued here today. The Soviet Chargé d'Affaires met with Prime Minister Nehru this morning. The British High Commissioner and the American Ambassador met with Dutt, the Foreign Secretary of the Ministry of External Affairs. The details of the activities remain undisclosed.

### 印度尼西亚承认伊拉克共和国  印度外交部发言人谈承认伊拉克共和国问题

1958-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚承认伊拉克共和国
    印度外交部发言人谈承认伊拉克共和国问题
    新华社雅加达19日电　印度尼西亚内阁在今天十七时到二十时之间举行了一次紧急会议，会上决定承认伊拉克共和国。
    内阁在会后发表公报说，7月19日的内阁会议在研究中东局势后，考虑到：一、避免第三次世界大战的爆发，二、减少或消除外国干涉，特别是对小国的干涉，三、帮助健全的民族主义的发展，四、执行和加强万隆会议原则；决定承认伊拉克共和国。
    新华社新德里19日电　印度外交部发言人今天晚间对新华社记者解释印度政府尚未承认伊拉克共和国的原因时候说，到目前为止，印度还没有接到伊拉克方面关于承认问题的正式要求。这位发言人说：“如果有外国干涉伊拉克的任何迹象或现象出现，我们将不等待要求，马上承认新政府。”
    今天这里的外交活动继续进行。苏联代办今天上午会见了尼赫鲁总理。英国高级专员和美国大使会见了外交部外交秘书杜特。活动情况仍然是不公开的。
