Title: Iraqi Republican Government Controls the Entire Territory; Deputy Prime Minister Calls on the People to Strengthen Unity and Be Vigilant Against Imperialist Conspiracies; Large-Scale Demonstrations in Baghdad Support and Endorse the Revolution
Author:
Date: 1958-07-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 15th. According to Baghdad Radio, the entire territory of Iraq now recognizes the authority of the Republican Government and is controlled by it. The situation throughout the country is stable.

The authorities call on residents not to believe various provocative rumors spread by certain malicious individuals, and to obey the orders of the official gazettes of the Republican Government.

The people of the Iraqi capital held large-scale demonstrations on the 14th to express their support and endorsement of the revolution. The demonstrators shouted slogans: "Long live the revolution!" "Long live Arab unity!" "All Arabs, unite in the struggle, with a common goal!".

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 14th. According to Baghdad Radio on the evening of the 14th, martial law has been implemented throughout Iraq starting from the 14th, and Iraqi Army Chief of Staff Ahmed Saleh Al-Azeh has been appointed as military governor.

At the same time, according to a communiqué issued by the Iraqi Revolutionary Command on the 14th, all ports and borders of Iraq have been closed since the 14th until further notice.

The Supreme Power Committee also issued an order on the 14th removing thirteen senior army officers from their posts, including Rafik Aref, Chief of Staff of the Faisal regime, and his assistant, Abdul Aziz Ghalib.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 14th. Iraqi Deputy Prime Minister Arif said to the delegations of the people who came to congratulate the birth of the new republic on the afternoon of the 14th that the army will carry out the revolution to the end until the goals announced by Baghdad Radio are achieved.

He asked the people to unite and support the struggle for freedom. He also called on them not to let the imperialists create divisions among the people, and advised them to remain calm and vigilant.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 15th. According to the "Al-Ahram" newspaper today, the Iraqi Republican Government has decided to release all imprisoned political prisoners.

The newspaper said that some ministers in the new Iraqi government who are now in exile in Damascus have all been recalled.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 14th. According to a report by the Middle East News Agency on the 14th, Rashid Ali al-Kilani, the former Iraqi Prime Minister currently in Cairo, called on the Iraqi people on the 14th to unanimously support the Iraqi armed forces and the revolution they are carrying out.

He said that the revolution in Iraq was expected because the imperialist agents in Iraq had turned Iraq into a base for imperialist conspiracies. He specifically pointed out the conspiracy of the Baghdad Pact Organization to use Iraqi troops against the Lebanese people.

Kilani said that today's victory is a great victory for the forces of freedom and Arab nationalism in Iraq and the Arab world.

Xinhua News Agency, July 15th. According to TASS, Beirut, July 15th: According to Baghdad Radio, in order to avoid any provocative actions that may be used by imperialist countries, the Iraqi Republican Government announced the following measures: prohibition of all gatherings, prohibition of carrying weapons, implementation of martial law, and closure of Iraq's land and air borders until further special decisions are made. In addition, a formal statement was issued stating that all foreigners residing in Iraq and their property will be reliably protected by the Republican Armed Forces, guaranteeing absolute safety. ### 伊拉克共和国政府控制全境  副总理号召人民加强团结警惕帝国主义阴谋  巴格达人民大规模示威游行支持和拥护革命

1958-07-16
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克共和国政府控制全境
    副总理号召人民加强团结警惕帝国主义阴谋
    巴格达人民大规模示威游行支持和拥护革命
    新华社大马士革15日电　据巴格达电台广播，现在，伊拉克全境都承认共和国政府的政权，并且为这个政府所控制。全国情况稳定。
    当局号召居民不要轻信某些居心险恶的歹徒所散布的各种挑衅性谣言，而要听从共和国政府官方公报的命令。
    伊拉克首都人民14日举行了大规模示威游行，表示支持和拥护革命。游行者高呼口号：“革命万岁！”“阿拉伯团结万岁！”“全体阿拉伯人，进行统一的斗争，抱着一致的目标！”。
    新华社大马士革14日电　据巴格达电台14日晚上报道，从14日起，伊拉克全国实行戒严，伊拉克军队参谋长艾哈迈德·萨勒·阿拉奏埃赫已被任命为军事长官。
    同时，据伊拉克革命司令部14日发表公报说，伊拉克的一切港口和边境自14日起封锁，直到再下其他的命令的时候为止。
    最高权力委员会还在14日颁布命令，撤除十三名陆军高级军官的职务，其中包括费萨尔政权的参谋长拉菲克·阿里夫和他的助手阿卜杜勒·阿齐兹·加利卜。
    新华社开罗14日电　伊拉克副总理阿里夫14日下午向前来祝贺新共和国诞生的各人民代表团说，陆军将把革命进行到底，直到巴格达电台所宣布的目标实现为止。
    他要求人民团结起来支持争取自由的斗争。他还号召他们不要让帝国主义者在人民中间制造分歧，并且劝告他们要保持冷静和警惕。
    新华社开罗15日电　据“金字塔报”今天报道说，伊拉克共和国政府已经决定释放被监禁的全部政治犯。
    这家报纸说，现在流亡在大马士革的伊拉克新政府中的一些部长已经被全部召回。
    新华社开罗14日电　据中东通讯社14日报道，目前在开罗的前伊拉克首相拉希德·阿里·基拉尼在14日要求伊拉克人民一致支持伊拉克的武装部队和它进行的革命。
    他说，由于帝国主义在伊拉克的代理人把伊拉克变为帝国主义阴谋的基地，因此伊拉克发生革命是在意料之中。他特别指出了巴格达条约组织使用伊拉克军队反对黎巴嫩人民的阴谋。
    基拉尼说，今天的胜利是伊拉克和阿拉伯世界的自由力量和阿拉伯民族主义力量取得的巨大胜利。
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社贝鲁特15日讯：据巴格达电台广播，为了避免任何可能被帝国主义国家利用的挑衅行动，伊拉克共和国政府宣布采取以下措施：禁止各种集会、禁止携带武器、实施戒严、封锁伊拉克的地面和空中的国界直到再作出特别决定为止。此外还发表了一项正式声明说，居住在伊拉克的一切外国人以及他们的财产都将受到共和国武装部队的可靠保护，保证绝对安全。（附图片）
    在开罗大学的伊拉克学生，七月十四日在开罗街上为伊拉克共和国的成立而欢呼。
                          新华社稿
