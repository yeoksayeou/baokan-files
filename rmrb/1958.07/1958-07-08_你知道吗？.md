    Did you know?
    △The total length of roads walked by a 30-year-old middle-aged person over the years can be added up to circle the earth twice.
    △There are a total of 20,000 species of snails on Earth.
    △One hundred lightning strikes occur every second in the sky above the Earth.

### 你知道吗？

1958-07-08
第8版()
专栏：

    你知道吗？
    △一个三十岁的中年人，历年所步行的道路总长，加起来可以环绕地球两转。
    △地球上一共有两万种蜗牛。
    △地球上空，每秒钟要发生一百次闪电。
