Title: Soviet Army Begins Exercises in Transcaucasian and Turkestan Military Districts
Author:
Date: 1958-07-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 17th - TASS reports: Newspapers report that the Soviet Army and Air Force will conduct military exercises in the Transcaucasian and Turkestan Military Districts starting July 18th.

Editor's Note: Transcaucasia includes the regions of the Azerbaijan, Georgia, and Armenia Union Republics south of the Caucasus Mountains. Turkestan includes the regions of the Turkmen, Uzbek, Tajik, and Kyrgyz Union Republics in Central Asia.

### 南高加索和土耳其斯坦军区  苏军开始演习

1958-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    南高加索和土耳其斯坦军区
    苏军开始演习
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：报纸报道，苏联陆军和空军将从7月18日起在南高加索和土耳其斯坦军区进行军事演习。
    编者注　南高加索包括高加索山脉以南阿塞拜疆、格鲁吉亚和亚美尼亚等加盟共和国地区。土耳其斯坦包括中亚细亚土库曼、乌兹别克、塔吉克和吉尔吉斯等加盟共和国地区。
