Author: Ai·Xi·Bageda
Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 8

Oath

Iraq Ai·Xi·Bageda
	For a pure and happy future like a child; to let the awakening of the people spread throughout the world like the fragrance of roses; to overcome the darkness and welcome the beautiful prospects that are about to appear in the world;
	So that the people can live a peaceful and happy life, I hope:
		Never give in!
		Even if the last drop of blood is shed!
	For those who are despised; so that free people can
	find happiness in the ocean of light; to spread my thunderous voice
	in all directions, I swear:
		Determined to use blood and loyalty
		to realize the people's noble ideals! For the deep hatred buried in my heart, for the torch burning brightly in the darkness, for the lion-like warriors—they despise baseness,
		laugh at groans,
		and spare no effort in their youth! To make that faint flame
		turn into a raging fire,
		burning the world's disasters and darkness! Listen, you tyrants! I swear:
		We are determined to rise up and fight before dawn! For the kind people weeping in the ruins; for the glorious culture in human history; for the peasants—
		they use sickles to cut off the enemy's claws; for the workers—
		they use hammers to smash the war criminals' weapons, I swear:
		No disaster, bloodshed, or pain will
		scare me! In order to prevent flowers from being destroyed, in order to prevent my joyful face from
	having melancholy tears, in order to make our loud roar
	drown out all shrinking and timidity; I swear:
		We will carry out a bloody struggle!
		Eliminate all running dogs and demons! For the soldiers ready to go, for the mothers living in sadness and pain, for the pure girls seeking happiness, so that our children will no longer
		see darkness and pain, I swear:
		We are determined to maintain peace,
		--This is a powerful weapon to defeat war criminals!
			I swear:
		For the liberation of the people,
		For the victory of the workers,
		I will dedicate my blood! With an angry iron fist, with the light of freedom, with the indomitable heart of the people, I swear:
		We must rescue the people in darkness,
		And never allow aggressors to destroy world peace!

### 誓言
爱·西·巴格达
1958-07-24
第8版()
专栏：

    誓言
    伊拉克　爱·西·巴格达为了像孩子那样
    有着纯洁幸福的未来；为了让人民的觉醒
    像玫瑰花芳香那样传遍世界；为了战胜黑暗，
    迎接即将出现在世界上的美好前景；
    为了人民能过和平幸福的生活，我希望：
    决不屈服！
    哪怕流尽最后一滴血！
    为了那遭受卑视的人们；为了自由的人们能从光明的海洋中
    寻找到幸福；为了把我霹雳般的声音
    传布四方，我宣誓：
    决心用鲜血和忠诚
    实现人民崇高的理想！为了那埋藏在心头的血海深仇，为了在黑暗中熊熊发光的火炬，为了猛狮般的勇士——他们，蔑视卑贱，
    嘲笑呻吟，
    毫不吝惜自己的青春！为了使那微弱的火苗
    变成熊熊烈火，
    焚毁世上的灾难与黑暗！听着，暴虐者！我宣誓：
    黎明前夕，我们决心奋起而战！为了在废墟上痛哭的善良人民；为了人类历史上光辉灿烂的文化；为了农民——
    他们用镰刀斩断敌人的魔爪；为了工人——
    他们用锤子砸碎战犯的武器，我宣誓：
    任何灾难、流血、痛苦都不会
      把我吓倒！为了不使鲜花遭到摧残，为了不使我充满喜悦的脸上
    挂上忧郁的泪水，为了使我们宏亮的吼声，
    淹没一切畏缩和胆怯；我宣誓：
    我们将进行流血的斗争！
    消灭一切走狗和鬼魔！为了整装待发的士兵，为了生活在忧郁和痛苦中的母亲，为了寻觅幸福的纯洁的姑娘，为了使我们的儿女们不再
    看到黑暗和痛苦，我宣誓：
    我们决心维护和平，
    ——这是击溃战犯的有力武
        器！我宣誓：
    为了人民解放，
    为了劳动者的胜利，
    我将献出我的鲜血！用愤怒的铁拳，用自由的光芒，用威武不屈的人民的心，我宣誓：
    一定要解救在黑暗中的人们，
    决不让侵略者破坏世界和平！
