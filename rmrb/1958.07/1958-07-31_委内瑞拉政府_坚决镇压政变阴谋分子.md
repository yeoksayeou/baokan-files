Title: Venezuelan Government Resolutely Suppresses Coup Conspirators
Author:
Date: 1958-07-31
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Caracas, July 30th: After crushing the reactionary coup conspiracy instigated by former Defense Minister Castro León, the Venezuelan government has taken suppressive measures against the conspirators who participated in the coup.

The suppression of the coup conspirators' activities has received the support and assistance of the Venezuelan people. Citizens across the country have held grand mass rallies for days, guaranteeing their full support for the government. Various political organizations and people from all over the country have demanded the purging of supporters of former dictator Jiménez from state organs and demanded the swift punishment of the conspirators. Many people have also reported conspirators to the authorities.

### 委内瑞拉政府  坚决镇压政变阴谋分子

1958-07-31
第3版()
专栏：

    委内瑞拉政府  坚决镇压政变阴谋分子
    据新华社30日讯　加拉加斯消息：委内瑞拉政府在粉碎了前国防部长卡斯特罗·莱昂所策动的反动政变阴谋后，已经对参加这次政变的阴谋分子采取了镇压措施。
    镇压政变阴谋分子的活动得到了委内瑞拉人民的支持和协助。全国各地的市民连日举行盛大的群众大会，保证全力支持政府。各种政治组织和各地的群众都要求从国家机关中清洗前独裁者希门尼斯的支持者，并且要求迅速惩办阴谋分子。还有许多人向当局告发阴谋分子。
