Title: Curbing US-British Aggression in the Middle East: Nasser-Khrushchev Talks
Author:
Date: 1958-07-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 18th - According to TASS, after visiting Yugoslavia, President Nasser of the United Arab Republic arrived in Moscow recently and held talks with Soviet Minister of the Council Khrushchev. The talks took place in a friendly atmosphere. They discussed the issue of curbing aggression by the United States and other colonialist countries in the Near and Middle East, and safeguarding the peace and independence of Arab countries.

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 18th - According to a Damascus radio announcement, President Nasser arrived in Damascus from Moscow this afternoon.

An official communique issued here shortly after his arrival stated: "President Nasser of the United Arab Republic met with Soviet Minister of the Council Khrushchev in Moscow on July 17, 1958. They discussed the development of the international situation and the necessary measures to consolidate peace. The talks were filled with an atmosphere of friendship and understanding."

### 制止美英侵略中东  纳赛尔赫鲁晓夫会谈

1958-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    制止美英侵略中东
    纳赛尔赫鲁晓夫会谈
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔在南斯拉夫访问后，日前到了莫斯科，同苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫举行了会谈。会谈是在友好的气氛中进行的。他们交谈了关于制止美国以及其他殖民主义国家在中近东的侵略、保障阿拉伯国家的和平和独立的问题。
    新华社大马士革18日电　据大马士革电台宣布，纳赛尔总统今天下午从莫斯科到达大马士革。
    这里在他到达以后不久发表的一项官方公报说：“阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔于1958年7月17日在莫斯科会见了苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫。他们讨论了国际局势的发展和巩固和平的必要措施。会谈充满了友好和谅解的气氛。”
