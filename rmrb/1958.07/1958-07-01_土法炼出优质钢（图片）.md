Indigenous Methods Produce High-Quality Steel

The Guangzhou City Dongqu Handicraft Third Hardware Machinery Cooperative used a blower-equipped, firebrick furnace to produce its first batch of high-quality medium carbon steel, containing 0.30% carbon, on June 16th. Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Zhizhao photographed this.

### 土法炼出优质钢（图片）

    土法炼出优质钢
    广州市东区手工业第三五金机制合作社于6月16日用鼓风机耐火砖炉炼出第一炉含碳百分之三十的优质中炭钢。新华社记者　李志昭摄
