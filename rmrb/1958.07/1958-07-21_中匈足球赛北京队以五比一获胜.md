Title: China-Hungary Football Match: Beijing Team Wins 5-1
Author:
Date: 1958-07-21
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 20th - Beijing's football team won a friendly match against the Hungarian "Pánczél" football team today, with a score of 5-1. The entire match was intense, with over 20,000 spectators applauding and cheering for the excellent performances of both teams.

### 中匈足球赛北京队以五比一获胜

1958-07-21
第7版()
专栏：体育新闻

    中匈足球赛北京队以五比一获胜
    据新华社20日讯  北京足球队今天在同匈牙利“潘久捷尔”足球队举行一场友谊比赛中，以五比一获胜。全场比赛进行得十分紧张，两万多名观众不时为双方队员的精采表演鼓掌欢呼。
