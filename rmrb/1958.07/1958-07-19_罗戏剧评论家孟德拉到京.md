Title: Romanian Drama Critic Mengdrea Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-07-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th: Vigu Mengdrea, Romanian drama critic and editor-in-chief of "Literary Gazette," arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to China under the 1958 executive plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 罗戏剧评论家孟德拉到京

1958-07-19
第5版()
专栏：

    罗戏剧评论家孟德拉到京
    新华社17日讯　根据中罗文化合作协定1958年执行计划前来我国访问的罗马尼亚戏剧评论家、“文学报”主编维古·孟德拉今天乘飞机到达北京。
