Title: Vietnamese "Literary Gazette" Publishes Chairman Mao's Poems
Author:
Date: 1958-07-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 15th: The Vietnamese "Literary Gazette," published today, features seven poems written by Chairman Mao Zedong: "Snow," "Jinggangshan," "New Year's Day," "Beidaihe," "Yellow Crane Tower," "Liupanshan," and "Loushan Pass."

This is the first time Chairman Mao's poems have been translated into Vietnamese and introduced to Vietnamese readers. The translator, Hoang Trung Thông, is a renowned Vietnamese poet.

The "Literary Gazette" also translated and published a letter from Chairman Mao to the Beijing "Poetry Journal" today.

### 越南“文学报”刊载毛主席的词

1958-07-16
第6版()
专栏：

    越南“文学报”刊载毛主席的词
    新华社河内15日电　今天出版的越南“文学报”刊载了毛泽东主席写的“雪”、“井冈山”、“元旦”、“北戴河”、“黄鹤楼”、“六盘山”、“娄山关”七首词。
    这是毛主席的词第一次被译成越文介绍给越南读者。译者黄忠通是越南著名诗人。
    “文学报”今天还译载了毛主席写给北京“诗刊”的一封信。
