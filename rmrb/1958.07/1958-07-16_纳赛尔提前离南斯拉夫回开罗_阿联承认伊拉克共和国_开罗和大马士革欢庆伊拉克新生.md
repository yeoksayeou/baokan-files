Title: Nasser Returns to Cairo Early from Yugoslavia; UAR Recognizes Iraqi Republic; Cairo and Damascus Celebrate Iraqi Rebirth
Author: 
Date: 1958-07-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 15th: Cairo Radio broadcast shortly before midnight on the 14th that the United Arab Republic has recognized the Iraqi Republic.

According to the Middle East News Agency on the evening of the 14th, United Arab Republic President Nasser sent a telegram to the Iraqi Supreme Power Committee saying: "I have received your telegram recognizing the United Arab Republic. I express my respect to you, and I also pay tribute to the Iraqi people in the name of the people of the United Arab Republic. May God guide you to seek the interests of the Arabs."

Xinhua News Agency, July 15th: Belgrade dispatch: United Arab Republic President Nasser canceled his last day of visit to Yugoslavia and left Brioni Island twenty-four hours early on the evening of the 14th to return to Cairo.

Sources say that President Nasser decided to return home immediately after learning of the armed coup in Iraq.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 14th: The people of Cairo welcomed the news of the Iraqi revolution with great satisfaction and joy.

Since Cairo Radio broadcast the news on the morning of the 14th, the citizens of Cairo have gathered around radios to pay close attention to the news.

The atmosphere in various workplaces was obviously very enthusiastic, and people congratulated each other on the liberation of an Arab sister country from the rule of imperialist agents.

The Cairo "Evening News" published twice, using banner headlines to publish the Baghdad Radio's declaration of the revolution and the establishment of the first Iraqi Republic. An editorial in the "Evening News" congratulated the Iraqi people, saying: "Despite the Sixth Fleet, the Baghdad Pact, Said's reign of terror, and Faisal and Hussein's prisons, they still broke free from the chains of enslavement.” The article then pointed out, "This is the greatest uprising, the Baghdad Pact is finished, Eisenhower's doctrine and all the chains are finished."

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 15th: Political leaders here and other Arab leaders here on the 14th all welcomed the victory of the military coup in Iraq. They believe that this is an important step towards realizing Pan-Arab unity.

Former Syrian President Kuwatly said that this long-awaited great event has realized the hopes of the Arabs. He said: "It will become the starting point for the rest of the Arab people to follow towards freedom."

Former Jordanian Minister of State for Foreign Affairs Rimawi said that the Iraqi people's feat of clearing imperialism and its lackeys from the most dangerous base of the Arab nation "will have a rapid response in Jordan. This will be a major step towards liberating the Arabs."

Yusuf Ruessi, director of the Algerian office, also pointed out that the Iraqi incident will have a huge impact on the future of the Arab nation.

Iraqi and Jordanian residents living in Damascus marched with Syrians on the 14th, dancing and singing through the streets of Damascus, celebrating the end of Iraqi tyranny.

### 纳赛尔提前离南斯拉夫回开罗  阿联承认伊拉克共和国  开罗和大马士革欢庆伊拉克新生

1958-07-16
第5版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔提前离南斯拉夫回开罗
    阿联承认伊拉克共和国
    开罗和大马士革欢庆伊拉克新生
    新华社开罗15日电　开罗电台14日午夜前不久广播，阿拉伯联合共和国已经承认伊拉克共和国。
    另据中东通讯社14日晚上报道，阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔打电报给伊拉克最高权力委员会说：“我接到了你们承认阿拉伯联合共和国的电报。我向你们表示我的敬意，同时我以阿拉伯联合共和国人民的名义向伊拉克人民致敬。让真主引导你们为阿拉伯人谋利益。”
    据新华社15日讯　贝尔格莱德消息：阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔取消了他在南斯拉夫最后一天的访问计划，提前二十四小时于14日晚间离开布里俄尼岛返回开罗。
    有消息说，纳赛尔总统是在得悉伊拉克发生武装政变的消息后决定立即回国的。
    据新华社开罗14日电　开罗人民以非常满意和愉快的心情欢迎伊拉克革命的消息。
    自从开罗电台14日清晨广播这个消息以后，开罗市民纷纷聚集在收音机周围十分关心地注意收听这方面的消息。
    各个工作场所的气氛显然都很热烈，人们彼此祝贺一个阿拉伯姊妹国家从帝国主义代理人统治下获得解放。
    开罗“晚报”出版了两次，用通栏标题刊载了巴格达电台宣布革命和成立第一个伊拉克共和国的声明。“晚报”刊载的一篇专栏文章祝贺伊拉克人民说：“尽管有第六舰队、巴格达条约、赛义德的恐怖统治，以及费萨尔和侯赛因的监狱，他们还是挣断了奴役的锁链。文章接着指出，“这是一次最大的起义，巴格达条约完蛋了，艾森豪威尔主义和所有的锁链也完蛋了”。
    据新华社大马士革15日电　这里政界领袖和在这里的其他阿拉伯领袖在14日都表示欢迎伊拉克的军事政变的胜利。他们认为，这是实现全阿拉伯团结的重要步骤。
    前叙利亚总统库阿特利说，这个意料中的伟大事件实现了阿拉伯人的希望。他说：“它将成为其余阿拉伯人民跟着走向自由的起点”。
    前约旦外交国务大臣里马维说，伊拉克人民把帝国主义及其走卒清除出阿拉伯民族的最危险的基地的壮举，“将在约旦有很快的反响。这将是朝着解放阿拉伯人的方向迈一大步”。
    阿尔及利亚办事处主任优素福·鲁塞西也指出，伊拉克事件对阿拉伯民族的未来将有巨大的影响。
    侨居大马士革的伊拉克和约旦居民14日同叙利亚人一道举行游行，他们载歌载舞地通过大马士革的街道，欢庆伊拉克暴政的结束。
