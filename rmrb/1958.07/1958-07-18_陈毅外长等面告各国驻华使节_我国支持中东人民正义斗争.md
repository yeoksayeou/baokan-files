Title: Foreign Minister Chen Yi and Others Meet with Ambassadors to China: China Supports the Just Struggle of the Middle Eastern People

Author: 
Date: 1958-07-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th. Vice Premier of the State Council and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, Vice Foreign Ministers Luo Guibo and Zeng Yongquan, on the 17th, separately received ambassadors to China from various countries, explained the attitude of the Chinese government in supporting the Republic of Iraq and opposing the United States' aggression against Lebanon, and asked them to convey this to their respective governments.

### 陈毅外长等面告各国驻华使节  我国支持中东人民正义斗争

1958-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅外长等面告各国驻华使节
    我国支持中东人民正义斗争
    新华社17日讯　国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，外交部副部长罗贵波、曾涌泉在17日分别接见了各国驻华使节，就我国政府支持伊拉克共和国、反对美国侵略黎巴嫩的态度作了说明，请他们转达本国政府。
