Title: Striving for Peace, Opposing West German Atomic Armament: Mass Rally Held in Berlin; Representatives of Fraternal Parties Participating in the Berlin Conference Attend

Author:
Date: 1958-07-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 1st Dispatch from TASS: The working people of Berlin, capital of the German Democratic Republic, held a mass rally on June 30th. The slogan of the rally was: Strive for peace, oppose West German atomic armament.

Tens of thousands of workers and staff members, women, and youth from various enterprises in Berlin participated in the rally. When the representatives of the parties of various countries attending the meeting of representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties in Berlin appeared in the presidium, they were warmly welcomed.

Kiefert, First Secretary of the Greater Berlin District Party Organization of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, delivered the opening speech. He pointed out that the meeting of representatives of fraternal parties held in the capital of the German Democratic Republic is of great significance.

Speaking at the rally were Villon, member of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party; Dłuski, member of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party; Pellegrini, member of the leadership of the Italian Communist Party; Koucky, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party; Matern, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany; Reimann, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Germany; and Larsen, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Denmark.

They emphasized that the representatives of the fifteen fraternal parties participating in the meeting discussed the issue of ways to safeguard peace in Europe. They also pointed out that in order to achieve this goal, it is necessary to mobilize the working class and working people, and unite the people of all countries to fight against the forces plotting aggressive conspiracies.

### 争取和平　反对西德原子武装  柏林举行群众大会  参加柏林会议的各兄弟党代表出席

1958-07-02
第5版()
专栏：

    争取和平　反对西德原子武装
    柏林举行群众大会
    参加柏林会议的各兄弟党代表出席
    新华社1日讯　据塔斯社柏林讯：德意志民主共和国首都柏林的劳动人民6月30日举行了群众大会。大会的口号是：争取和平，反对西德原子武装。
    参加大会的有柏林各企业的成千上万的工人和职员、妇女、青年。当在柏林参加共产党和工人党代表会议的各国党的代表在主席团中出现的时候，他们受到了热烈的欢迎。
    德国统一社会党大柏林专区党组织第一书记基弗特致开会词。他指出，在德意志民主共和国首都举行的兄弟党代表会议具有重大意义。
    在大会上讲话的有法国共产党中央委员维戎，波兰统一工人党中央委员德乌斯基、意大利共产党领导机构委员贝勒格林尼、捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会书记考茨基、德国统一社会党中央政治局委员马特恩、德国共产党中央委员会第一书记雷曼和丹麦共产党中央委员会主席拉森。
    他们着重指出，参加会议的十五个兄弟党的代表讨论了有关维护欧洲和平的道路问题。他们并且指出，为了达到这个目的，必须把工人阶级和劳动人民动员起来，把各国人民联合起来为反对策划侵略阴谋的势力而斗争。
