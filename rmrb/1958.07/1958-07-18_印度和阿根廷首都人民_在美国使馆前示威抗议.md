Title: People in the Capitals of India and Argentina Demonstrate in Front of US Embassies

Author:
Date: 1958-07-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 16th: Citizens of New Delhi demonstrated in front of the US Embassy in India on the evening of the 16th, braving heavy rain, to protest against the US armed intervention in Lebanon.

The demonstrators marched through the major streets of Delhi, carrying flags and placards, and gathered in front of the US Embassy. Speakers at the meeting strongly condemned the US aggression against Lebanon, an Afro-Asian brother country of India.

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th: Buenos Aires Dispatch: Two thousand people in the Argentine capital demonstrated on the evening of the 16th to protest against the US armed intervention in Lebanon. They marched through the streets of Buenos Aires, shouting slogans such as "Down with US imperialism and its accomplice Britain, who are aggressors against Lebanon," and threw asphalt at the US Embassy building and the Bank of Boston.

### 印度和阿根廷首都人民  在美国使馆前示威抗议

1958-07-18
第3版()
专栏：

    印度和阿根廷首都人民
    在美国使馆前示威抗议
    据新华社新德里16日电　新德里市民群众在16日晚上冒着倾盆大雨在美国驻印度大使馆前面举行示威，抗议美国武装干涉黎巴嫩。
    参加示威的人手执旗帜和标语牌，在德里各大街道上游行，并且在美国大使馆前面集会。在会上演说的人猛烈谴责美国侵略印度的一个亚非兄弟国家黎巴嫩。
    据新华社17日讯  布宜诺斯艾利斯消息：阿根廷首都两千人16日晚上举行示威，抗议美国武装干涉黎巴嫩。他们在布宜诺斯艾利斯的大街上游行，高呼打倒侵略黎巴嫩的美帝国主义及其同谋者英国的口号，并且向美国大使馆的大楼和波士顿银行投掷沥青。
