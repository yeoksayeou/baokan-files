Title: Manure Collection Folk Song
Author:
Date: 1958-07-16
Page: 2

    Manure collection of ten million dan,
    Transportation of manure in a single line;
    Crops grow into forests,
    Grain piles up like mountains.
    The collected fertilizer surpasses the mountain tops,
    The nourished land is overflowing with oil,
    Cotton grows into lotus petals,
    Sweet potatoes are bigger than gourd heads.
            (Liaocheng, Shandong)

### 积肥民歌

1958-07-16
第2版()
专栏：

    积肥民歌
    积肥千万担，
    运肥一条线；
    庄稼长成林，
    粮食堆成山。
    积的肥料过山头，
    喂的土地滚流油，
    棉花长成莲花瓣，
    地瓜赛过葫芦头。
            （山东聊城）
