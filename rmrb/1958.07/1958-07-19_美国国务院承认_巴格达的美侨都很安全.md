Title: U.S. State Department Admits American Nationals in Baghdad Are Safe
Author:
Date: 1958-07-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, July 18th: U.S. State Department Press Officer White acknowledged in a statement to reporters on the 17th that the U.S. State Department's information indicates that all American nationals in Baghdad have been safe since the establishment of the Republic of Iraq. Their lives and property are being protected by the armed forces of the Republic of Iraq.

According to a report by Agence France-Presse on the 17th, citing information provided by the Swiss Embassy in Baghdad, which is responsible for protecting French interests, the lives and property of the six hundred French nationals in Iraq have not been harmed in any way since the establishment of the Republic of Iraq government.

### 美国国务院承认  巴格达的美侨都很安全

1958-07-19
第3版()
专栏：

    美国国务院承认
    巴格达的美侨都很安全
    据新华社18日讯　美国国务院新闻发布官怀特在17日对新闻记者发表谈话时承认，美国国务院得到的情报表明，自从伊拉克共和国成立以来，在伊拉克首都巴格达的全部美国侨民都很安全。他们的生命和财产正受到伊拉克共和国武装部队的保护。
    另据法新社17日引负责保护法国利益的瑞士驻巴格达公使馆提供的消息报道，自从伊拉克共和国政府成立以来，在伊拉克的六百名法国侨民的生命和财产都没有受到任何损害。
