Title: Chairman Mao Approves Sino-Soviet Treaty of Commerce and Navigation
Author:
Date: 1958-07-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, in accordance with the decision of the 99th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, approved on July 14, 1958, the Treaty of Commerce and Navigation between the People’s Republic of China and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, signed in Beijing on April 23, 1958.

### 毛主席批准中苏通商航海条约

1958-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席批准中苏通商航海条约
    新华社14日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第九十九次会议的决定，在一九五八年七月十四日批准了一九五八年四月二十三日在北京签订的中华人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟通商航海条约。
