Title: Khrushchev Receives Delegation from the Indian Youth Congress
Author:
Date: 1958-07-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 4th: TASS reports: Chairman of the USSR Council of Ministers, Khrushchev, received the delegation from the Indian Youth Congress, which is currently visiting the Soviet Union, on July 3rd.

The head of the delegation was Ramakrishna Bajaj.

During the meeting, Khrushchev held cordial talks with the members of the delegation.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见印青年大会代表团

1958-07-05
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见印青年大会代表团
    据新华社4日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫3日接见了正在苏联访问的印度青年大会代表团。
    代表团团长是拉姆克里斯纳·巴德贾德兹。
    在接见时，赫鲁晓夫和代表团成员进行了亲切的谈话。
