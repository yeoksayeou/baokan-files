Title: Brief News

Author:
Date: 1958-07-31
Page: 3

    Brief News
    △ The government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, headed by First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Tsedenbal, left Ulaanbaatar by plane for Moscow on the 29th.
    △ The Korean art troupe, led by Li Uk-kyu, Vice Chairman of the Korean Foreign Cultural Liaison Association, left Pyongyang on the 30th for a performance tour of Mongolia.
    △ The Cambodian journalists delegation, led by Chen Wenchang, arrived in Hanoi by train on the 30th after visiting China and North Korea.
    △ The Chinese film "The Silent Mountain Forest" began showing at the "Patria Cinema" in Bucharest on the 28th, and was warmly welcomed by the people of Bucharest.
    △ The Soviet "Uzbekfilm Studio" and the Indian "Malaya Film Company" have agreed that India and the Soviet Union will jointly produce a new color widescreen film "Poem of Two Hearts".
    △ The Chinese Changchun Football Team, a group of twenty-one people, led by Liu Ziran, Deputy Director of the Changchun Municipal Sports Commission, left Beijing by plane for the Soviet Union on the 29th for a visit.

### 简讯

1958-07-31
第3版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    △以部长会议第一副主席曾德率领的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团29日从乌兰巴托乘飞机去莫斯科。
    △由朝鲜对外文化联络协会副委员长李诩求率领的朝鲜艺术团30日离开平壤前往蒙古访问演出。
    △由陈文昌率领的柬埔寨新闻工作者代表团一行七人在访问中国和朝鲜后，30日乘火车抵达河内。
    △中国影片“寂静的山林”28日开始在布加勒斯特的“祖国电影院”上映，受到了布加勒斯特人的热烈欢迎。
    △苏联“乌兹别克电影公司”同印度“马来亚电影公司”议定，印度和苏联将协同摄制一部新的彩色宽银幕影片“两颗心的诗篇”。
    △中国长春足球队一行二十一人，29日在长春市体委副主任刘自然率领下，由北京乘飞机去苏联访问。
