Title: No Devils Allowed to Run Wild!
Author: Ma Shaobo
Date: 1958-07-18
Page: 8

    No Devils Allowed to Run Wild!
    Ma Shaobo
    Eisenhower's狰狞(zhengning, ferocious) jackal face,
    We are not unfamiliar with;
    The sounds of American aggressors killing in Lebanon,
    How can we turn a deaf ear!
    Today, tomorrow,... day and night,
    The entire New China,
    The entire city of Beijing:
    Everyone takes to the streets,
    Everyone raises their iron fists,
    Like a surging sea tide!
    Like a thunderclap shaking the earth!
    The wounds left by the American aggressors in Korea
    Have not yet had their bandages removed,
    We shouldn't forget the pain so quickly!
    If you don't quickly withdraw your black hands,
    We will resolutely make you suffer pain upon pain!
    The giant wheel of the era will smash your head to pieces!
    The iron cavalry of mankind will flatten your lair!
    The East Wind prevails over the West Wind,
    No devils allowed to run wild!

### 不准魔鬼乱动！
马少波
1958-07-18
第8版()
专栏：

    不准魔鬼乱动！
    马少波
    艾森豪威尔狰狞的豺狼面孔，
    我们并不陌生；
    美国侵略军在黎巴嫩的杀声，
    我们岂能置若罔听！
    今天、明天、……的日日夜夜，
    整个新中国，
    整个北京城：
    人人走上街头，
    个个举起铁拳，
    像海涛般的澎湃汹涌！
    像迅雷样的天惊地动！
    美国侵略者侵朝留下来的伤口
    还没有揭下纱布，
    总不该立刻忘痛！
    你如果不赶快抽回黑手，
    就坚决叫你痛上加痛！
    时代的巨轮会把你头颅砸烂！
    人类的铁骑会把你老巢踏平！
    东风压倒西风，
    不准魔鬼乱动！
