Title: Seven Mass Organizations Support the Struggle of the People of the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1958-07-25
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo dispatch: To support the just struggle of the Arab people, the Japan National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations, the Japan-China Trade Promotion Association, the Japan Asian Solidarity Committee, and the Japan Peace Committee held a joint meeting on the 19th. They decided to send telegrams to the government of the Republic of Iraq and the Lebanese insurgent forces to express their support for their just struggle and decided to establish a "Middle East Issues Discussion Group."

　　The "Middle East Issues Discussion Group" sent representatives to the United States Embassy in Japan on the 23rd, demanding that the United States immediately withdraw its invading troops from Lebanon.

　　Subsequently, the representatives met with Japanese Prime Minister Nobusuke Kishi, requesting that the Kishi government not recognize the fait accompli of the US and British troops' invasion of Lebanon and Jordan, and urged the US and Britain to withdraw their invading troops from the Middle East.

　　The representatives also visited the Soviet and Indian Embassies in Japan, handing over letters to Soviet Premier Nikita Khrushchev and Indian Prime Minister Jawaharlal Nehru to the embassies. The letters expressed respect for the positive policies adopted by the Soviet and Indian leaders to eliminate tensions in the Middle East.

　　The "Middle East Issues Discussion Group" also wrote to United Nations Secretary-General Dag Hammarskjöld, requesting him to work through UN agencies to prevent the deterioration of the situation in the Middle East.

　　Representatives participating in this action included seven Japanese people's organizations that participated in the "Middle East Issues Discussion Group": the Japan Asian Solidarity Committee, the Japan Peace Committee, the Japan National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations, the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, the Tokyo Regional Council of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, the Japan Democratic Women's Association, and the Okinawa Prefectural Association.

　　The General Association of Korean Residents in Japan also sent representatives to participate in this action.

### 七群众团体支持中东人民斗争

1958-07-25
第3版()
专栏：

    七群众团体支持中东人民斗争
    据新华社讯　东京消息：为了支持阿拉伯人民正义斗争，日本恢复日中邦交国民会议、日中贸易促进会、日本亚洲团结委员会和日本和平委员会在19日举行的联席会议上，决定打电报给伊拉克共和国政府和黎巴嫩起义部队表示支持它们的正义斗争，并且决定成立“中东问题恳谈会”。
    “中东问题恳谈会”在23日派代表到美国驻日本大使馆，要求美国立即从黎巴嫩撤出它的侵略军队。
    随后，代表们会见了日本首相岸信介，要求岸信介政府不要承认美英军队侵入黎巴嫩和约旦的既成事实，并且促使美英从中东撤出侵略军队。
    代表们还访问了苏联和印度驻日本大使馆，把写给苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫和印度总理尼赫鲁的信件交给了大使馆。信中对苏印两国首脑为消除中近东紧张局势所采取的积极政策表示敬意。
    “中东问题恳谈会”还写信给联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德，要求他通过联合国机构努力防止中近东局势的恶化。
    派代表参加这次行动的有参加“中东问题恳谈会”的七个日本人民团体：日本亚洲团结委员会、日本和平委员会、日本恢复日中邦交国民会议、日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会、日本工会总评议会东京地方评议会、日本民主妇女协议会和冲绳县人会。
    旅日朝鲜人总联合会也派代表参加了这次行动。
