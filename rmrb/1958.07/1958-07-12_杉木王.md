Title: King of Chinese Fir

Author:

Date: 1958-07-12
Page: 2

A "King of Chinese Fir" has recently been discovered in the Shuxikou Forestry Agricultural Cooperative in Huitong County, Hunan Province. This large Chinese fir tree is 120 years old, reaching a height of ten zhang, with a diameter at breast height of 294 centimeters. It can yield more than 100 cubic meters of timber. The tree is currently still very vigorous.

### 杉木王

1958-07-12
第2版()
专栏：珍闻

    杉木王
    最近在湖南省会同县疏溪口林农业合作社发现了一株生长一百二十年的大杉木，树高达十丈，胸高直径二百九十四公分，可产木材一百立方公尺以上。现在这棵树还很旺盛。
