Title: Baltic Sea Peace Week Concludes, Demonstrating Determination to Defend Peace
Author:
Date: 1958-07-15
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 13th (TASS): The "Baltic Sea Peace Week," which began on July 5th in the Rostock District, concluded on the 13th.

The "Baltic Sea Peace Week" powerfully demonstrated the close ties between the peoples of the Baltic Sea countries and their growing determination to ensure peace and security in the Baltic Sea region.

During the "Peace Week," representative conferences of workers and women from the Baltic Sea countries, as well as international student research conferences, youth days, meetings of representatives of the working people from various countries, artistic performances, and sports meets were held. Representatives from various countries attending the "Peace Week," as well as representatives from the German Democratic Republic, emphasized in their speeches that the peoples of all countries must unite in the face of the threat of atomic war.

On the final day of "Peace Week," a sports meet was held in Rostock starting in the morning. This was followed by a celebration attended by more than 30,000 people.

### 显示了保卫和平的决心  波罗的海和平周结束

1958-07-15
第6版()
专栏：

    显示了保卫和平的决心
    波罗的海和平周结束
    新华社14日讯　据塔斯社柏林13日讯：7月5日起在罗斯托克专区举行的“波罗的海和平周”已经在13日结束。
    “波罗的海和平周”有力地显示了波罗的海各国人民之间的密切联系和他们保证波罗的海地区和平和安全的日益增长的决心。
    在“和平周”内举行了波罗的海各国工人和妇女的代表会议以及国际学生研究会，举行了青年日和各国劳动人民代表的会见以及文艺演出和运动会。前来参加“和平周”的各国代表以及德意志民主共和国的代表都在发言中强调指出，各国人民在反对原子战争的威胁中必须团结一致。
    在“和平周”的最后一天，罗斯托克从早上起举行运动会。接着举行了有三万多人参加的庆祝大会。
