Title: Omani Nationalists Wipe Out British Aggressors

Author:
Date: 1958-07-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus. A telegram received on the 27th by the Omani Imam's office in Damascus stated that Omani nationalists recently engaged in fierce battles with British imperialist troops heading to Muscat in the Sahama Desert. Omani fighters ambushed British patrols in the area, and their commandos fought British troops supported by armored vehicles, killing twelve British soldiers and destroying two British armored vehicles.

### 阿曼民族主义者歼击英国侵略军

1958-07-31
第3版()
专栏：

    阿曼民族主义者
    歼击英国侵略军
    新华社大马士革电　阿曼教长驻大马士革办事处27日接到的电报说，阿曼民族主义者最近在萨哈马沙漠与开往马斯喀特的英帝国主义军队进行了激烈的战斗。阿曼的战斗员在这个地区伏击了英国巡逻队，他们的突击队同在装甲车支持下的英国军队打了仗，击毙了十二名英国兵，摧毁了两辆英国装甲车。
