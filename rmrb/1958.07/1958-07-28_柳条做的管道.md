Title: Willow-Woven Pipes
Author: Li Xianglin
Date: 1958-07-28
Page: 6
Creating More and Better New Products

    Willow-Woven Pipes
    The local state-owned willow weaving factory in Xinmin County, Liaoning Province, has successfully produced ventilation pipes and water pipes for mines using willow weaving. After use in the mines, the ventilation effect is good, and mass production has now begun.
    In the past, ventilation ducts used in mines were made of steel. This factory plans to produce 20,000 meters this year, which can save the country 600 tons of steel.
                      (Li Xianglin)

### 柳条做的管道
李相林
1958-07-28
第6版()
专栏：创造更多更好的新产品

    柳条做的管道
    辽宁省新民县地方国营柳条编织厂用柳条编织矿山用的通风管和输水管成功。经过矿山使用，通风效果良好，现在已经开始大量生产。
    过去矿山使用的通风筒都是用钢材制成的。这个厂今年计划生产二万公尺，可以为国家节省六百吨钢材。
                      （李相林）
