Title: Former Jordanian Officer Colonel Tal Calls on Jordanian Army to Resist Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1958-07-19
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 18th—Former Jordanian officer Colonel Abdullah Tal, now in Cairo, published a statement in today's newspapers, calling on the Jordanian army to resist imperialist occupation and to follow the example of other Arab countries in overthrowing imperialism and autocratic rule.
　　This Jordanian military man, who served as the military governor of Jerusalem in 1949, said that the purpose of the US and British occupation of Jordan is to make Jordan a central stronghold for imperialism to suppress the Arab national independence movement, and to conspire to annex other parts of Palestine to Israel.
　　He called on the Jordanian people to be vigilant against the new imperialist occupation threatening Jordan's independence and sovereignty.

### 前约旦军官塔勒上校  号召约旦陆军抵抗侵略者

1958-07-19
第3版()
专栏：

    前约旦军官塔勒上校
    号召约旦陆军抵抗侵略者
    新华社开罗18日电　现在开罗的前约旦军官阿卜杜拉·塔勒上校在今天的报纸上发表声明，号召约旦陆军抵抗帝国主义占领，并且效法其它阿拉伯国家推翻帝国主义和专制统治的榜样。
    这位曾在1949年任耶路撒冷军事总督的约旦军人说，美英占领约旦的目的是要使约旦成为帝国主义镇压阿拉伯民族独立运动的中心据点，并且阴谋使以色列并吞巴勒斯坦其他地方。
    他号召约旦人民警惕新的帝国主义占领威胁约旦的独立主权。
