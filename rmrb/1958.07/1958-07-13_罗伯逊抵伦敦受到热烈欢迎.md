Title: Robeson Arrives in London to a Rousing Welcome
Author:
Date: 1958-07-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, London, July 11th: World-renowned African-American singer Paul Robeson arrived in London today for his first overseas performance tour in eight years.

More than two hundred people warmly welcomed his arrival at London Airport. As soon as he stepped out of the plane, people cheered. On the airport grounds, many people rushed to embrace, kiss, and shake hands with him, creating a very moving scene.

### 罗伯逊抵伦敦受到热烈欢迎

1958-07-13
第3版()
专栏：

    罗伯逊抵伦敦受到热烈欢迎
    据新华社伦敦11日电　世界闻名的美国黑人歌唱家保罗·罗伯逊今天到达伦敦，这是他在八年来第一次出国作访问演出。
    在伦敦机场上有两百多人热烈地欢迎他的到来。他一踏出了飞机机舱，人们就欢呼起来。在机场的空地上许多人跑过去同他拥抱、亲吻和握手，情景非常动人。
