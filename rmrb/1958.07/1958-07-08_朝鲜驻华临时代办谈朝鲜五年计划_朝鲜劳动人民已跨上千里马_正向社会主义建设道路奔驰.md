Title: Korean Chargé d'Affaires ad interim in China Discusses North Korea's Five-Year Plan: Korean Working People Have Mounted the Chollima and are Galloping Towards Socialist Construction

Author:
Date: 1958-07-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 7th. The Korean Embassy in China held a press conference today, where Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, Mun Jae-su, delivered a speech on the promulgation of the First Five-Year People's Economic Plan of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

At the press conference, Mun Jae-su said that all Korean workers have mounted the Chollima and are galloping towards socialism.

Mun Jae-su stated that thanks to the correct leadership of the Korean Workers' Party, headed by Comrade Kim Il-sung, and the patriotic, selfless struggle of the Korean people, as well as the fraternal assistance of the Soviet Union, China, and other countries of the socialist camp, the Korean people have overfulfilled the Three-Year (1954-1956) Plan for the Rehabilitation and Development of the National Economy, laying the material foundation for the Korean people to formulate long-term perspective plans. Mun Jae-su introduced that the basic task of the First Five-Year (1957-1961) Plan is to further consolidate the socialist economic foundation in the northern half of the Republic and to basically solve the people's problems of clothing, food, and housing. By 1961, the total industrial output value will increase to more than 2.6 times that of 1956, with priority given to the development of heavy industry; the total agricultural output value will reach twice that of 1956. Other construction undertakings will also experience significant development, thereby further greatly improving the material and cultural living standards of the people. Mun Jae-su said that the Korean people firmly believe that the successful completion of the Five-Year Plan, laying the socialist construction and self-reliant economic foundation in the northern half, will become a guarantee for the peaceful reunification of the fatherland and will contribute to further promoting the victory of socialism in the East.

Mun Jae-su also answered questions raised by Chinese and foreign reporters at the press conference.

### 朝鲜驻华临时代办谈朝鲜五年计划  朝鲜劳动人民已跨上千里马  正向社会主义建设道路奔驰

1958-07-08
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜驻华临时代办谈朝鲜五年计划
    朝鲜劳动人民已跨上千里马  正向社会主义建设道路奔驰
    新华社7日讯  朝鲜驻华大使馆今天举行记者招待会，大使馆临时代办文在洙就朝鲜民主主义人民共和国颁发第一个五年人民经济计划发表了谈话。
    文在洙在招待会上说，朝鲜全体劳动者已经跨上千里马，向着社会主义奔驰。
    文在洙说，由于以金日成同志为首的朝鲜劳动党的正确领导和朝鲜人民爱国的忘我斗争，由于苏联、中国和社会主义阵营各国兄弟般的援助，朝鲜人民已经超额完成了恢复和发展国民经济的三年（1954—1956）计划，奠定了朝鲜人民制定长期远景计划的物质基础。文在洙介绍了第一个五年（1957—1961）计划的基本任务是，进一步巩固共和国北半部的社会主义经济基础，基本上解决人民衣、食、住的问题。到1961年，工业总产值将增加为1956年的二点六倍以上，其中重工业将得到优先发展；农业总产值将达到1956年的两倍。其它建设事业也要有很大发展，人民物质文化生活水平因此将进一步大大改善。文在洙说，朝鲜人民深信，成功地完成五年计划，在北半部奠定社会主义建设和自立的经济基础，将会成为祖国和平统一的保证，并且将会在进一步促进东方社会主义的胜利方面作出贡献。
    文在洙还在招待会上回答了中外记者们提出的问题。
