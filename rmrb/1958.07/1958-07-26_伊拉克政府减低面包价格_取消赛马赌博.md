Title: Iraqi Government Reduces Bread Prices; Cancels Horse Racing Gambling
Author:
Date: 1958-07-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, 25th: The Iraqi government announced on the 24th that it was reducing the price of bread. After the price reduction, bread is nearly one-tenth cheaper than before.
    According to a broadcast from the Iraqi Republic Radio, the Republic government has announced a ban on horse racing and gambling through horse racing, and has ordered the reversal of all measures taken by the old dynasty to dismiss employees for political reasons.

### 伊拉克政府减低面包价格  取消赛马赌博

1958-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克政府  减低面包价格  取消赛马赌博
    新华社25日讯　巴格达消息：伊拉克政府24日宣布减低面包的价格。减价以后，面包价格比原来便宜近十分之一。
    另据伊拉克共和国电台广播，共和国政府已经宣布禁止赛马和通过赛马进行赌博，并且命令撤消旧王朝由于政治理由而解雇职员的一切措施。
