Title: Refugees Fleeing Dynastic Tyranny Begin to Return from Syria
Author:
Date: 1958-07-22
Page: 5

    Refugees Fleeing Dynastic Tyranny
    Begin to Return from Syria
    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 20th – Iraqi refugees who had previously fled to this area from Iraq because they could not bear the tyrannical rule of the feudal dynasty have begun to return to the Republic of Iraq. The authorities here are providing them with various conveniences for their return.
    The border between the Iraqi and Syrian regions is now open twice a week to those who have obtained permission from the authorities here.

### 逃避王朝暴政的难民开始由叙利亚返国

1958-07-22
第5版()
专栏：

    逃避王朝暴政的难民
    开始由叙利亚返国
    新华社大马士革20日电  以前因为不堪封建王朝的暴虐统治而从伊拉克逃到这里的伊拉克难民，开始返回伊拉克共和国。这里当局对于他们回国给予种种便利。
    伊拉克和叙利亚地区之间的边境现在为得到这里当局许可的那些人每周开放两次。
