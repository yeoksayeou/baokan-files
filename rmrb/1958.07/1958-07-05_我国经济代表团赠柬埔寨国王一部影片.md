Title: Chinese Economic Delegation Presents Film to Cambodian King
Author: 
Date: 1958-07-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, July 4th: The head of the Chinese Economic Delegation, Ye Jinghao, presented the color documentary film "Flowers of Cambodian Art" to Cambodian King Suramarit and Queen on the evening of July 3rd. This documentary was specially filmed by the Beijing Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio about the Cambodian Cultural and Art Delegation's performance in Beijing during their visit to China last year.

### 我国经济代表团赠柬埔寨国王一部影片

1958-07-05
第5版()
专栏：

    我国经济代表团
    赠柬埔寨国王一部影片
    据新华社金边4日电　中国经济代表团团长叶景灏在3日晚上把彩色纪录片“柬埔寨艺术花朵”赠送给柬埔寨国王苏拉玛里特和王后。这部纪录片是柬埔寨文化艺术代表团去年访问中国时，北京中央新闻纪录电影制片厂专门拍摄的关于他们在北京演出的情况。
