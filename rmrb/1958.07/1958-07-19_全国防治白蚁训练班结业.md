Title: National Anti-Termite Training Class Graduates
Author:
Date: 1958-07-19
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, July 18th. The national anti-termite training class and the Guangdong Province anti-termite training class, organized by the Guangdong Provincial Science and Technology Commission, graduated on the 16th. One hundred and eighty-three trainees from provinces and cities such as Guangxi, Fujian, Jiangxi, Hunan, Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Shanghai, and various special zones of Guangdong Province will return to their respective regions to train anti-termite teams and launch a large-scale termite eradication campaign.

During the past month, these graduates, under the guidance of termite expert Li Shimei, coordinated with the Guangzhou city's termite eradication campaign, learning while doing. They successively carried out termite eradication inspections in nine streets in Guangzhou, including Liurong, Jinhua, Shishu, and Duobao, and conducted practical training in eradicating termites from trees and ships in Yuexiu Mountain and Zhujiang District. They inspected a total of 4,126 households, discovering termites in 520 households and finding 141 termite nests. Through practice, the trainees have basically mastered the technical knowledge of termite prevention and control. (Picture attached)

### 全国防治白蚁训练班结业

1958-07-19
第7版()
专栏：

    全国防治白蚁训练班结业
    新华社广州18日电　广东省科学工作委员会举办的全国防治白蚁训练班和广东省防治白蚁训练班，16日结业。来自广西、福建、江西、湖南、江苏、浙江、上海等省市和广东省各专区的一百八十三名学员，将回到各地训练防治白蚁的队伍，开展大规模的消灭白蚁运动。
    这些毕业的学员们，一个月来在白蚁专家李始美的指导下，配合了广州市的灭蚁运动，边学边干，先后在广州市的六榕、金花、诗书、多宝等九条街进行了消灭白蚁的复查工作，在越秀山、珠江区进行了消灭树木和船舶白蚁的实习。他们共检查了四千一百二十六户，发现五百二十户有白蚁，找到一百四十一个蚁巢。经过实习，学员们已基本掌握了防治白蚁的技术知识。（附图片）
