Title: Tunisia Recognizes the Republic of Iraq
Author: 
Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, July 23rd: The Tunisian government announced on the evening of the 22nd its decision to recognize the Republic of Iraq. This decision was announced after a cabinet meeting presided over by the new President Bourguiba.

### 突尼斯承认伊拉克共和国

1958-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    突尼斯承认伊拉克共和国
    新华社23日讯　突尼斯消息：突尼斯政府22日晚宣布决定承认伊拉克共和国。这个决定是新总统布尔吉巴主持的一次内阁会议结束以后宣布的。
