Title: Traitor Jamali Arrested But Not Dead; Iraqi Government Invites Foreign Journalists to Visit Him in Prison
Author:
Date: 1958-07-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 19th: According to a broadcast on the evening of July 19th by the Iraqi Republic Radio, the authorities of the Iraqi Republic invited a number of foreign journalists in Baghdad on July 19th to visit Jamali, the foreign minister of the overthrown Faisal dynasty, in a prison near Baghdad.

Lodge, the U.S. representative to the United Nations Security Council, had clamored that Jamali had been murdered by the Iraqi revolutionaries.

### 卖国贼贾马利被捕未死伊政府邀外国记者探监

1958-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    卖国贼贾马利被捕未死伊政府邀外国记者探监
    新华社开罗19日电  据伊拉克共和国电台19日晚间广播，伊拉克共和国当局在19日邀请在巴格达的一些外国记者到巴格达附近的一座监狱中去看已经被推翻的费萨尔王朝的外交大臣贾马利。
    美国出席联合国安全理事会的代表洛奇曾经叫嚷说，贾马利已经被伊拉克的革命者谋害了。
