    New Stamps
    The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications issued a set of commemorative stamps for the "Moscow Conference of Ministers of Posts and Telecommunications of Socialist Countries" on July 10th. The design uses "geese" to symbolize postal services and transmitting towers to symbolize telecommunications. The denominations are four fen in blue and eight fen in green.
