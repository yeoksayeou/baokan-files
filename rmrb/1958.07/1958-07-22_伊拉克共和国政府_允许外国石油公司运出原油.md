Title: Iraqi Republic Government Permits Foreign Oil Companies to Export Crude Oil
Author:
Date: 1958-07-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 19th: The Iraqi Republic Radio broadcast on the evening of July 19th that the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Iraqi Republic announced on the 19th that oil companies in Iraq could transport crude oil abroad.

The radio station said that this decision, made at the request of some companies, is in line with the policy announced by the Iraqi Republic Government to respect the interests of foreign oil companies.

### 伊拉克共和国政府  允许外国石油公司运出原油

1958-07-22
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克共和国政府
    允许外国石油公司运出原油
    新华社开罗19日电  伊拉克共和国电台19日晚间广播，伊拉克共和国外交部在19日宣布，在伊拉克的石油公司可以把原油运往国外。
    这家电台说，这个根据一些公司的要求而作出的决定，是符合伊拉克共和国政府宣布的尊重外国石油公司利益的政策的。
