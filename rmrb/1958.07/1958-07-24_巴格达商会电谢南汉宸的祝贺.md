Title: Baghdad Chamber of Commerce Cables Thanks to Nan Hanchen for Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1958-07-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, received a reply telegram on the 20th from the Baghdad Chamber of Commerce of the Republic of Iraq, thanking him for the congratulations on the establishment of the Republic of Iraq.

The telegram stated: "We received with heartfelt gratitude your telegram congratulating us on the founding of our republic. Foreign troops invading some areas of Arab countries will encounter strong resistance and opposition from all Arab peoples and other peace-loving countries. We extend our thanks for your noble sympathy and cooperation. We hope this cooperation will be the beginning of sound economic and trade relations between the two countries."

### 巴格达商会电谢南汉宸的祝贺

1958-07-24
第2版()
专栏：

    巴格达商会电谢南汉宸的祝贺
    新华社23日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸收到伊拉克共和国巴格达商会在20日发来的复电，感谢对伊拉克共和国的祝贺。
    电报说：“我们以衷心感激的心情接获你们祝贺我们的共和国成立的电报。侵入阿拉伯国家一些地区的外国军队将遇到所有阿拉伯民族及其他爱好和平国家的坚强抵抗和反对。对你们崇高的同情与合作谨致谢意。我们希望这种合作将是两国间健全的经济与贸易关系的开端。”
