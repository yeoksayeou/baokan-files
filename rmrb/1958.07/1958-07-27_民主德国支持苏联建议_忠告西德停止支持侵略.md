Title: The GDR Supports Soviet Proposals, Advises West Germany to Stop Supporting Aggression
Author:
Date: 1958-07-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 26th – The “Neues Deutschland” today published a statement by the government of the German Democratic Republic (GDR) on the current international situation, supporting the Soviet government's efforts for peace and opposing imperialist aggression.

The statement said that the steps and proposals taken by the Soviet government to stop the US-British aggression in the Middle East, especially the proposal to hold a five-power summit, are of great significance. The Soviet Union's practical and feasible proposals make it possible to find a solution to stop US-British aggression. The statement said that the GDR government fully supports the efforts made by the Soviet government and demands that the US and British governments immediately take measures to stop the aggression in order to eliminate the serious threat to world peace.

The statement pointed out that the West German government's public statement on the 23rd that it supports the war actions of the aggressors and is prepared to continue providing West German territory as an offensive and reserve base for Western countries once again exposes the West German government's lie that it is a friend of the Arab peoples. The statement said that the GDR government once again demands that the Federal Government immediately stop supporting the US-British aggressors.

### 民主德国支持苏联建议  忠告西德停止支持侵略

1958-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联建议深入人心
    美国政府处于窘境
    民主德国支持苏联建议
    忠告西德停止支持侵略
    据新华社柏林26日电　“新德意志报”今天刊载了德意志民主共和国政府关于目前国际局势的一项声明，支持苏联政府为和平所作的努力，并反对帝国主义侵略。
    声明说，苏联政府为制止美英对中东的侵略行为所采取的步骤和建议，特别是关于举行五国首脑会议的建议具有重大意义。苏联的切实可行的建议，使得有可能找到一个制止美英侵略的解决办法。声明说，德意志民主共和国政府全力支持苏联政府所作的努力，并且要求美英两国政府立即采取停止侵略的措施，以便消除对世界和平的严重威胁。
    声明指出，西德政府23日公开地声明它支持侵略者的战争行动，并且准备继续为西方国家提供西德领土作为进攻和后备基地，这再一次地揭穿了西德政府宣称自己是阿拉伯各国人民的朋友的谎言。声明说，德意志民主共和国政府再一次要求联邦政府立即停止对美英侵略者的支持。
