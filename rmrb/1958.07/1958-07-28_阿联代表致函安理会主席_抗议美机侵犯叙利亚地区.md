Title: Arab League Representative Sends Letter to Security Council President Protesting US Aircraft Incursion into Syrian Region
Author:
Date: 1958-07-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, July 27th: Arab League Representative to the United Nations, Lutfi, wrote to the President of the Security Council on the 26th, protesting the recent daily violations of the airspace of the Syrian region of the Arab League by United States aircraft, and the harassment of aircraft between the Syrian and Egyptian regions of the Arab League. The letter pointed out that U.S. aircraft have been constantly obstructing and attempting to attack civilian aircraft of the Arab League. Lutfi requested that the letter be circulated as an official document of the Security Council. He pointed out that the government of the Arab League reserves "the right to take any measures it deems necessary."

### 阿联代表致函安理会主席  抗议美机侵犯叙利亚地区

1958-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    阿联代表致函安理会主席
    抗议美机侵犯叙利亚地区
    新华社27日讯　纽约消息：阿拉伯联合共和国驻联合国代表卢特菲26日写信给安全理事会主席，抗议美国飞机最近每天都在侵犯阿拉伯联合共和国叙利亚地区的领空和骚扰阿拉伯联合共和国叙利亚地区和埃及地区之间的航机。这封信指出，美国飞机不断阻拦和企图袭击阿拉伯联合共和国的民航机。卢特菲请求把这封信件作为安全理事会的正式文件散发。他指出，阿拉伯联合共和国政府保留“采取它认为必要的任何措施的权利”。
