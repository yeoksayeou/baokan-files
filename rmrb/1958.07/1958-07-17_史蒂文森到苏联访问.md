Title: Stevenson Visits Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1958-07-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. TASS reports that U.S. Democratic Party leader Stevenson arrived in Moscow on the 15th for a visit.
	He was accompanied by his son and a group of friends. Previously, they had stayed in Leningrad for three days. During their visits to various locations, they will learn about the Soviet Union's achievements in industry, agriculture, science, and culture.

### 史蒂文森到苏联访问

1958-07-17
第6版()
专栏：

    史蒂文森到苏联访问
    新华社16日讯　据塔斯社报道，美国民主党领袖史蒂文森15日到达莫斯科访问。
    和他一道来的有他的儿子和一批朋友。在这以前，他们曾经在列宁格勒逗留了三天。他们在各地访问期间将了解苏联工农业和科学文化方面的成就。
