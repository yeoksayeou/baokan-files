Title: Leader of Jordan's National Front Condemns British Occupation of Jordan
Author:
Date: 1958-07-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 20th: TASS Damascus, July 19th: Ahedat, former Jordanian minister and one of the leaders of the National Front, which unites all patriotic and progressive forces in the country, condemned the landing of British troops in Jordan. He told reporters: "The British occupation of Jordan is a blatant imperialist aggression against Jordan's freedom, independence, and integrity. This practice clearly violates the United Nations Charter and international law and threatens peace and security in the Middle East and the world."

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 19th: Here, the leaders of Jordan's National Front sent telegrams on the 18th to United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld and Security Council President Afnan, condemning the British occupation of Jordan and demanding the immediate withdrawal of British invading troops from Jordan.

The telegrams were signed by five former members of parliament, Jordanian Communist Party General Secretary Fad Nasar, Jordanian Arab Nationalist representative Habash, and Jordanian Free Officers representative Mohammed Musa.

### 约旦全国阵线领袖  谴责英国侵占约旦

1958-07-21
第4版()
专栏：

    约旦全国阵线领袖
    谴责英国侵占约旦
    新华社20日讯  塔斯社大马士革19日讯：约旦前部长、团结国内一切爱国和进步力量的全国阵线的领袖之一阿希达特，谴责英军在约旦登陆。他对新闻记者说：“英国侵占约旦是对约旦的自由、独立和完整的公开的帝国主义侵略。这种做法显然破坏联合国宪章和国际法，并且威胁中东和全世界的和平和安全。”
    据新华社大马士革19日电在这里的约旦全国阵线的领袖们18日打电报给联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德和安全理事会主席阿芬霍，谴责英国侵占约旦，并且要求立即从约旦撤出英国侵略军队。
    在电报上署名的有五名前议会议员，约旦共产党总书记法德·纳赛尔、约旦的阿拉伯民族主义者代表哈巴希和约旦自由军官代表穆罕默德·穆萨。
