Title: Vietnamese National Assembly Delegation Arrives in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1958-07-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 11th: A delegation from the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, headed by Ton Duc Thang, arrived in Warsaw by plane at noon on July 11th at the invitation of the Polish Parliament.

The delegation will visit Poland for eight days.

### 越南国会代表团到华沙

1958-07-13
第3版()
专栏：

    越南国会代表团到华沙
    新华社华沙11日电　以孙德胜为首的越南民主共和国国会代表团，应波兰议会邀请在11日中午乘飞机到达华沙。
    代表团将在波兰访问八天。
