Title: China's First Gravity Arch Dam, Xianghongdian Reservoir Dam (Picture)
Author: Wang Yongjia
Date: 1958-07-28
Page: 6

    China's First Gravity Arch Dam, Xianghongdian Reservoir Dam
    China's first concrete gravity arch dam - the Xianghongdian Reservoir dam, was successfully completed on July 10.
    This reservoir is located in the upper reaches of the western source of the Pi River in Jinzhai County, Anhui Province. The arc length of the dam crest is 361 meters, the maximum height of the dam body is 87.5 meters, and the width of the crest is 5 meters. This reservoir is the largest of the Pi River reservoir group (the reservoir group includes four reservoirs: Fozi嶺, Mozi Tan, Xianghongdian, and Lianghekou), with a storage capacity of 2.642 billion cubic meters and a power generation capacity of 40,000 kilowatts.

### 我国第一座重力拱坝响洪甸水库拦河坝（图片）
王永嘉
1958-07-28
第6版()
专栏：

    我国第一座重力拱坝响洪甸水库拦河坝
    我国第一座混凝士重力拱坝——响洪甸水库拦河坝，已于7月10日胜利建成。
    这个水库在安徽省金寨县渒河西源上游，坝顶弧长三百六十一公尺，坝身最大高度八十七点五公尺，顶宽五公尺。这个水库是渒河水库群中最大的一个（水库群包括佛子岭，磨子潭，响洪甸，两河口等四个水库）能蓄水二十六亿四千二百万立方，发电量四万瓩。　  
    王永嘉摄
