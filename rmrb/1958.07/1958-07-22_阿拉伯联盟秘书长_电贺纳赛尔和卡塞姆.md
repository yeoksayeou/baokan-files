Title: Arab League Secretary-General Sends Congratulatory Telegrams to Nasser and Kassem
Author:
Date: 1958-07-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, July 20th - Arab League Secretary-General Hassouna today sent telegrams to President Nasser of the United Arab Republic and Prime Minister Kassem of the Republic of Iraq, congratulating them on the signing of the joint defense agreement in Damascus on the 19th.
    In the telegram, Hassouna particularly pointed out that this agreement is for the purpose of safeguarding the Arab League Charter and the Arab Joint Defense Treaty.

### 阿拉伯联盟秘书长  电贺纳赛尔和卡塞姆

1958-07-22
第4版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯联盟秘书长
    电贺纳赛尔和卡塞姆
    新华社开罗20日电  阿拉伯联盟秘书长哈苏纳今天打电报给阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔和伊拉克共和国总理卡塞姆，祝贺他们19日在大马士革签订共同防御协定。
    哈苏纳在电报中特别指出，这项协定是维护阿拉伯联盟公约和阿拉伯联合防御条约的。
