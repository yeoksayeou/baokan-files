Title: The Miyun Reservoir Basically Completed; Grand Inauguration Ceremony Held This Afternoon
Author:
Date: 1958-07-01
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 30th – After five months of diligent labor by nearly 400,000 volunteer laborers in the capital, the Thirteen Tombs Reservoir has been basically completed. The builders of the reservoir will hold a grand inauguration ceremony on the afternoon of July 1st. The first program of the Central People's Broadcasting Station and the 820 kilocycle frequency of the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station will jointly broadcast the live inauguration ceremony starting at 15:45 on the 1st.

### 十三陵水库基本建成  今日下午举行盛大落成典礼

1958-07-01
第2版()
专栏：

    十三陵水库基本建成
    今日下午举行盛大落成典礼
    新华社30日讯　经过首都将近四十万义务劳动大军五个月的辛勤劳动，十三陵水库已经基本建成。水库的建设者将在“七一”下午举行盛大的落成典礼。中央人民广播电台的第一种节目和北京人民广播电台的八二○千周，将从1日15时45分开始，联合转播落成典礼的实况。
