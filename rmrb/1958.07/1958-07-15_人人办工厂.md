Title: Everyone Runs Factories
Author: Guo Guoying
Date: 1958-07-15
Page: 3

　　Raw materials are taken locally, with hammers and bellows.
　　Producing while learning, factories are all over the urban and rural areas.
　　Today a small iron shop, next year a small Anshan Iron and Steel.

### 人人办工厂

    人人办工厂
    郭国英
    原料就地取，铁锤加风箱。

    今日小铁铺，来年小鞍钢。
