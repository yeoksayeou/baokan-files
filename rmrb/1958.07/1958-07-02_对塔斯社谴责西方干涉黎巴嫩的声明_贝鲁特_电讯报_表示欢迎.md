Title: Beirut's "Telegraph" Welcomes TASS Statement Condemning Western Interference in Lebanon
Author:
Date: 1958-07-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, June 30th: Commenting on the TASS statement regarding the situation in Lebanon, the "Telegraph" wrote: Lebanese nationalists are very satisfied with the Soviet statement on the international provocations planned by Western countries to interfere in Lebanon's internal affairs. The newspaper pointed out that this statement exposes the Western countries' deliberate imperialist plans for the Middle East.

The "Telegraph" pointed out that the Western countries' policies towards the Middle East put Lebanon at risk of suffering a devastating war. This war can only be avoided by resolving the Lebanese crisis without foreign interference.

### 对塔斯社谴责西方干涉黎巴嫩的声明  贝鲁特“电讯报”表示欢迎

1958-07-02
第6版()
专栏：

    对塔斯社谴责西方干涉黎巴嫩的声明
    贝鲁特“电讯报”表示欢迎
    新华社1日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特6月30日讯：“电讯报”在评论塔斯社关于黎巴嫩局势的声明时写道：黎巴嫩民族主义人士对苏联就西方国家为了干涉黎巴嫩内政而策划的国际挑衅所发表的声明，感到十分满意。报纸指出，这个声明揭露了西方国家对中东的处心积虑的帝国主义计划。
    “电讯报”指出，西方国家对中东的政策使黎巴嫩有遭受毁灭性战争的危险。只有不进行外国干涉来解决黎巴嫩危机，才能避免这种战争。
