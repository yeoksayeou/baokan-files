Title: Chen Yi Speaks at Polish National Revival Day Celebration, Warns US and Britain to Stop Aggression

Author:
Date: 1958-07-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd: Deputy Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi stated tonight at the reception celebrating the 14th anniversary of Polish National Revival Day: The Chinese people once again warn the US and British aggressors that if they do not stop their acts of aggression, 600 million Chinese people and peace-loving countries and people around the world will never stand idly by. Chen Yi said, we firmly believe that the US and British imperialists' aggressive plans will be shamefully defeated! The just struggle of the Arab people will surely be victorious.

### 陈毅在波兰复兴节庆祝会上讲话  警告美英停止侵略行为

1958-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅在波兰复兴节庆祝会上讲话
    警告美英停止侵略行为
    本报讯  据新华社22日消息：陈毅副总理兼外交部长今晚在庆祝波兰国家复兴节十四周年招待会上的讲话中说：中国人民再一次警告美英侵略者，如果再不停止侵略行为，六亿中国人民和全世界爱好和平的国家和人民绝不会袖手旁观。陈毅说，我们坚信：美英帝国主义的侵略计划必将遭到可耻的失败！阿拉伯人民的正义斗争必然获得胜利。
