Song of the Oil Workers

    Erect a Benchmark at Yumen Pass
    "The leaping red flag flutters,
    Striving to be the first,
    Songs shake the heavens and the earth,
    We declare war on the earth.
    Conquer the Gobi Desert,
    Penetrate the Qilian Mountains,
    Whip the horses onward to Turpan,
    Erect a benchmark at Yumen Pass!"

    Open the Flaming Mountains
    "The Flaming Mountains, are treasure mountains,
    Countless treasures locked inside.
    Heroic sons and daughters make a heroic oath,
    Holding the golden key to unlock them.
    Rumbling, the lock opens;
    Rushing, tens of thousands of tons of black gold flow out."

### 石油工人之歌

1958-07-29
第6版()
专栏：

    石油工人之歌
    玉门关上立标杆“跃进红旗招展，力争一马当先，歌声惊天动地，我们向大地宣战。战胜戈壁滩，穿透祁连山，快马加鞭进军吐鲁番，玉门关上立标杆！”
    打开火焰山“火焰山，是宝山，无数宝藏锁里边。英雄儿女立下英雄志，拿着金钥匙把锁来开。轰隆隆，锁开了；哗啦啦，万吨黑金流出来。”
