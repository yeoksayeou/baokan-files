Title: A Sailor's Song
Author: Tan Jiquan
Date: 1958-07-27
Page: 8

    A Sailor's Song
    Sailing out before the sun rises,
    Returning when the moon hides at the bottom of the sea,
    The enthusiasm for training is like fire in a furnace,
    Boiling the sea and the ocean.
      (Sailor Tan Jiquan)
