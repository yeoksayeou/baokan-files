Title: Moroccan Rif Tribal Leader Condemns US Aggression Against Lebanon
Author:
Date: 1958-07-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency: Moroccan Rif tribal leader Khattabi sent a telegram from Cairo on the 19th to US President Eisenhower, condemning the US aggression against Lebanon. Khattabi demanded that Eisenhower re-examine the US position on Arab nationalism and end the current regrettable situation.

The previous day, Khattabi also sent a telegram to Iraqi Prime Minister Qassem, congratulating him on the birth of the Republic of Iraq. He said that Iraq's liberation from monarchical autocracy and the rule of imperialist agents embodies the aspirations of all Arab people. Khattabi condemned foreign interference in the internal affairs of Arab countries, stating that this is a serious violation of Arab nationalism. Khattabi also sent telegrams to the governments of Tunisia and Morocco, urging them to immediately recognize the Republic of Iraq.

### 摩洛哥里弗部落领袖  谴责美国侵占黎巴嫩

1958-07-25
第3版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥里弗部落领袖
    谴责美国侵占黎巴嫩
    据新华社讯　摩洛哥里弗部落领袖哈塔比在19日从开罗打电报给美国总统艾森豪威尔，谴责美国侵占黎巴嫩。哈塔比要求艾森豪威尔重新检查美国对阿拉伯民族主义所采取的立场，并且结束目前的令人遗憾的局势。
    哈塔比在前一天还打电报给伊拉克总理卡塞姆，祝贺伊拉克共和国的诞生。他说，伊拉克摆脱君主专政和帝国主义代理人的统治这件事，体现了阿拉伯全体人民的愿望。哈塔比谴责外国干涉阿拉伯国家的内部事务。他说，这是对阿拉伯民族主义的严重侵犯。哈塔比还打电报给突尼斯和摩洛哥两国政府，要求它们立即承认伊拉克共和国。
