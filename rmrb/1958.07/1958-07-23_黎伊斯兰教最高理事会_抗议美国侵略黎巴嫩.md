Title: Lebanese Supreme Islamic Council Protests American Aggression Against Lebanon
Author:
Date: 1958-07-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 21st: Damascus Radio reported that the Lebanese Supreme Islamic Council sent telegrams on July 20th to U.S. President Eisenhower, the President of the United Nations Security Council, and its member states, protesting the aggressive act of the U.S. military landing in Lebanon. The telegram said: "The Americans must bear full responsibility for disrupting peace and security in the Middle East."

The telegram was signed by the council's chairman, Hussein Oweini, and other members. The telegram also said: "The Lebanese people are determined to defend the independence and sovereignty of their motherland, and all people, regardless of gender, age, will certainly participate in the struggle against the enemy."

### 黎伊斯兰教最高理事会  抗议美国侵略黎巴嫩

1958-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

    黎伊斯兰教最高理事会
    抗议美国侵略黎巴嫩
    新华社大马士革21日电  据大马士革电台消息，黎巴嫩伊斯兰教最高理事会在20日打电报给美国总统艾森豪威尔、联合国安全理事会主席和各理事国，抗议美国军队在黎巴嫩登陆的侵略行为。电报说：“美国人必须对扰乱中东的和平与安全负全部责任。”
    这封电报是由这个理事会的主席侯赛尼·乌埃尼和其他成员签署的。电报还说：“黎巴嫩人民决心保卫自己祖国的独立和主权，而且所有的人不分男女老幼，都一定参加反对敌人的斗争。”
