Title: Saudi Arabia, Afghanistan, Ceylon Announce Recognition of the Republic of Iraq
Author:
Date: 1958-07-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, July 24th: According to an announcement by the Republic of Iraq Radio this evening, Saudi Arabia has recognized the Republic of Iraq.

The Saudi Arabian government said in a telegram to the Prime Minister of the Republic of Iraq, Qassem, that the government of the Saudi King "has decided to recognize the fraternal Republic of Iraq. May God grant us all victories that conform to Arabism and safeguard peace."

Xinhua News Agency, July 25th: TASS, Kabul: The Afghan government formally recognized the government of the Republic of Iraq on the 24th.

Xinhua News Agency, July 25th: Colombo dispatch: Ceylon officials announced today: Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike today notified the Republic of Iraq of the Ceylon government's decision to recognize the government of the Republic of Iraq. This decision of recognition is effective from today.

### 沙特阿拉伯、阿富汗、锡兰宣布承认伊拉克共和国

1958-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

    沙特阿拉伯、阿富汗、锡兰宣布承认伊拉克共和国
    新华社大马士革24日电　据伊拉克共和国电台今天晚上宣布，沙特阿拉伯已经承认伊拉克共和国。
    沙特阿拉伯政府在给伊拉克共和国总理卡塞姆的电报中说，沙特国王的政府“已经决定承认兄弟般的伊拉克共和国。祈求真主赐给我们符合阿拉伯主义和维护和平的一切胜利”。
    新华社25日讯　塔斯社喀布尔讯：阿富汗政府24日正式承认伊拉克共和国政府。
    新华社25日讯　科伦坡消息：锡兰官方今天宣布：锡兰总理班达拉奈克今天已经把锡兰政府承认伊拉克共和国政府的决定通知了伊拉克共和国。这项承认的决定从今天开始生效。
