Title: The Rebirth of Iraq (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1958-07-22
Page: 5

    The Rebirth of Iraq

    Residents of Baghdad, the capital of Iraq, cheer the establishment of the republic.

    Qasim (left), the leader of the Iraqi uprising and Prime Minister of the Republic, delivers a speech to the masses. He said that the Iraqi revolution was to eliminate imperialism and its agents. Beside him is Siddiq Shansal (right), the Minister of the National Guidance Department.

    Tank forces participating in the uprising.

    The Iraqi traitor and former Prime Minister Said has been killed by the people. The picture shows the uprising soldiers searching his home. (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 伊拉克的新生（图片）

1958-07-22
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克的新生
    伊拉克首都巴格达居民欢呼共和国的成立。 
    伊拉克起义领袖共和国总理卡塞姆（左）向群众发表演说。他说伊拉克革命是为了消灭帝国主义和它的代理人。在他旁边的是西迪克·尚沙尔（右）国家指导部的部长。
    参加起义的坦克部队。
    伊拉克卖国贼前首相赛义德已被人民击毙。图为起义士兵在他家中进行搜索的情形。  （新华社稿）
