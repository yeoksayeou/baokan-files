Title: Dreaming of Building a Building
Author:
Date: 1958-07-04
Page: 8

    Dreaming of Building a Building
    (New Folk Song)
    In May, the wheat fills the granaries,
    In autumn, the corn crowds the barns,
    The old man closes his eyes in the middle of the night,
    Dreaming of the commune building a building.
        (Liquan, Shaanxi)

### 梦见盖楼房

1958-07-04
第8版()
专栏：

    梦见盖楼房
    （新民歌）
    五月麦子装满囤，
    秋后玉米挤满仓，
    老头半夜合上眼，
    梦见社里盖楼房。
        （陕西醴泉）
