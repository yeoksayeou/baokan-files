Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea Dynamics

　　　Heilongjiang Province's Signature and Voting Numbers Exceed 80% of the Population
    The Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea movement in Heilongjiang Province has been further developed after extensive publicity and education around the May Day holiday. According to statistics, 2,849,802 people in the province participated in accusation meetings against the crimes of the US, Japan, and the Chiang Kai-shek bandits. Based on this ideological education, 4,560,000 people signed in support of the conclusion of a peace pact and voted against the US arming Japan, accounting for 81.4% of the province's population. The Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea movement has promoted the progress of various practical works. 110 provincial and county-level model worker mutual aid teams have challenged the Li Shunda mutual aid team and formulated patriotic production plans. General mutual aid teams have also formulated patriotic production increase plans. In Tailai County, only two mutual aid teams originally had plans. After the Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea movement was launched, 35 mutual aid teams formulated plans. In terms of industrial production, patriotic production emulation campaigns have been enthusiastically launched in various places. In Qiqihar City alone, 30 of the 44 state-owned factories participated in the emulation campaign, and 19 factories completed their April production tasks ahead of schedule. The industrial and commercial circles throughout the province have generally established patriotic covenants, and the Qiqihar City industrial and commercial circles have also established a patriotic covenant inspection committee to inspect the implementation of the patriotic covenants. In this large-scale publicity and education campaign, 1,860 reporters and 48,512 propagandists throughout the province played a significant role. The provincial branch of the Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea Association, the Propaganda Department of the Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, and the provincial branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association have successively printed and distributed more than 98,900 copies of propaganda materials. Local film projection teams, theater troupes, blackboard newspapers, slides, etc., also actively participated in the Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea propaganda activities.
　　　
            Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea Movement in Yunnan Province
    The Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea movement in Yunnan Province, located on the frontier of the motherland, has made great progress. On May 1st, people of all ethnic groups in various counties of Yunnan Province held grand patriotic demonstrations. According to statistics from 18 counties including Yiliang, Mengzi, Baoshan, Lijiang, and Dali, the number of people participating in the demonstrations reached 900,000. These included 25 ethnic groups such as Han, Hui, Miao, Tibetan, Yi, and Lisu. On the same day, the number of people who signed in support of the peace declaration and voted against the United States arming Japan reached more than 500,000 in only 13 counties and cities including Yiliang, Binchuan, Menghua, and Xiaguan. People of all ethnic groups in Baoshan also established patriotic covenants at the demonstration meeting. In order to popularize patriotic education for Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea, Yunnan extensively carried out current affairs studies and publicity and education work in April. Most of the masses in Yiliang County who participated in the May 1st demonstration had heard current affairs reports, and some had participated in the Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea representative meeting. Among the 50,000 residents in Zhaotong urban area, 80% participated in newspaper reading groups.

            Many Areas in Fujian Formulate Plans to Continue Developing the Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea Movement
    6,039,191 people in Fujian Province have participated in signing in support of the conclusion of a peace pact and voting against the United States arming Japan. The donations from the people of the province to comfort the Chinese and Korean People's Army and to relieve Korean refugees have reached 3,550 million yuan in Renminbi. Since April, special zones, cities, and counties throughout the province have successively established Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea Associations and convened people's representative meetings centered on Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea. The Party committees of many places have also organized forces to conduct key inspections, and based on the inspection results, formulated specific plans for popularizing and deepening the Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea movement in the future.

### 抗美援朝动态

1951-05-30
第4版()
专栏：抗美援朝动态

　　抗美援朝动态
            黑龙江省签名投票人数超过人口的百分之八十
    黑龙江省的抗美援朝运动，经过“五一”前后广泛的宣传教育，获得了进一步的开展。据统计，全省有二百八十四万九千八百零二人参加了对美、日、蒋匪罪行的控诉会，在这个思想教育的基础上，签名拥护缔结和平公约和投票反对美国武装日本的各有四百五十六万余人，占全省人口的百分之八十一点四。抗美援朝运动推动了各种实际工作的进行，全省已有一百一十个省级的和县级的劳动模范互助组向李顺达互助组应战，并制订了爱国丰产计划。一般的互助组也制订了爱国增产计划。泰来县八区原来只有两个互助组订计划。抗美援朝运动开展后，就有三十五个互助组订了计划。在工业生产方面，各地都热烈地展开了爱国生产竞赛运动，仅齐齐哈尔市四十四个公营工厂，就有三十个工厂参加了竞赛运动；十九个工厂提前完成了四月份生产任务。全省工商界都普遍地订立了爱国公约，齐齐哈尔市工商界并成立了爱国公约检查委员会，检查爱国公约的执行情况。在这次大规模的宣传教育运动中，全省一千八百六十名报告员和四万八千五百一十二名宣传员发挥了很大的作用。该省抗美援朝分会、中共省委宣传部、中苏友好协会分会，先后印发了九万八千九百多册宣传材料。各地电影放映队、剧团、黑板报、幻灯等也积极参加了抗美援朝宣传活动。
            云南省的抗美援朝运动
    处在祖国边疆的云南省，抗美援朝运动已有很大开展。五月一日，云南省各县各族人民都举行了盛大的爱国示威游行。据宜良、蒙自、保山、丽江、大理等十八个县统计，参加示威游行的人数达九十万。其中包括汉族、回族、苗族、藏族、彝族、傈僳等二十五种民族。当日参加拥护和平宣言签名和反对美国武装日本投票的，仅宜良、宾川、蒙化、下关等十三个县市即达五十万余人。保山各族人民并在示威大会上订立了爱国公约。云南各地为普及抗美援朝爱国教育，四月份内曾广泛进行了时事学习和宣传教育工作。宜良县参加五一示威游行的群众，大部分都听过时事报告，有的并参加过抗美援朝代表会议。昭通城区五万人口中，百分之八十的居民参加了读报小组。
            福建许多地区订继续开展抗美援朝运动计划
    福建省已有六百零三万九千一百九十一人参加了拥护缔结和平公约的签名及反对美国武装日本的投票。全省人民慰劳中朝人民军和救济朝鲜难民的捐款已达人民币三十五亿五千余万元。四月份以来，全省各专区、市、县都先后成立了抗美援朝会，并召开了以抗美援朝为中心的各界人民代表会。很多地方的中共党委会并曾组织力量，进行重点检查，根据检查结果，制订了今后普及和深入抗美援朝运动的具体计划。
