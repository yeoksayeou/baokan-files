Title: Tibetan Local Government Delegation Visits Tianjin

Author:

Date: 1951-05-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, May 29th. The delegation of the Tibetan local government, including Plenipotentiary Representatives Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Kheme Sonam Wangdi, and Thubten Tenthar, arrived in Tianjin today by special train from Beijing for a visit. Huang Jing, Mayor of Tianjin, Deputy Mayors Xu Jianguo, Zhou Shutao, and more than 500 others from the Tianjin Municipal People's Government greeted them at the station.

### 西藏地方政府代表团赴天津市参观

1951-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　西藏地方政府代表团赴天津市参观
    【新华社天津二十九日电】西藏地方政府全权代表阿沛·阿旺晋美、凯墨·索安旺堆、土丹旦达等一行于今日专车自北京来天津参观。天津市人民政府市长黄敬、副市长许建国、周叔弢等五百余人前往车站迎接。
