Author:

    Our Great Motherland
    Chinese People's Volunteer Army soldiers in their stationed areas affectionately care for Korean children who have lost their parents, treating them as if they were their own children. Their actions represent the highest combination of patriotism and internationalism of the Chinese people.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民志愿军战士在驻地亲发地爱护失去父母的朝鲜儿童年，如同对待自己的孩子一样。他们的行动，代表了中国人民爱国主义精神和国际主义精神的最高的结合。
