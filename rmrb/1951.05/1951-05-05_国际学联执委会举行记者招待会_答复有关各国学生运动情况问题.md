Title: IUS Executive Committee Holds Press Conference, Answers Questions Regarding Student Movements in Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1951-05-05
Page: 4

　　International Student Union Executive Committee Holds Press Conference
    Answers Questions Regarding Student Movements in Various Countries
    [Xinhua News Agency, April 4th] The Executive Committee of the International Student Union (IUS), which held a five-day meeting in Beijing, held a press conference on the 3rd. Participants included representatives from various countries attending the current Executive Committee and nineteen Chinese and foreign reporters from the capital. IUS President Grohman delivered a speech at the conference, elaborating on the achievements of the current meeting and emphasizing that this meeting would advance students worldwide in achieving the tasks of striving for peace, national independence, and democratic education. Representatives of students from various countries answered questions raised by reporters regarding the student movements in their respective countries.

### 国际学联执委会举行记者招待会  答复有关各国学生运动情况问题

1951-05-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际学联执委会举行记者招待会
    答复有关各国学生运动情况问题
    【新华社四日讯】在北京举行了五天会议的国际学生联合会执行委员会于三日举行招待记者会，参加招待会的有出席本届执委会的各国代表和首都中外记者十九人。国际学联主席格罗曼在会上发表谈话，详细阐述了本届会议的成就，并着重指出这次会议将使全世界学生在实现争取和平、民族独立和民主教育的任务中前进一步。会上各国学生代表分别就记者提出有关各国学生运动情况的问题作了答复。
