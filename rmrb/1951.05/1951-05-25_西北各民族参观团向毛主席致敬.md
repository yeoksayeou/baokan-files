Title: Northwest Ethnic Groups' Visiting Delegation Pays Respects to Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1951-05-25
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 24th: The Northwest Ethnic Groups' Visiting Delegation, representing the various fraternal ethnic groups of the Northwest region, presented a flag to and paid respects to Chairman Mao of the Central People's Government, Commander-in-Chief Zhu of the People's Liberation Army, and Premier Zhou of the Government Administration Council at 10:00 AM on the 24th. Zabuding, the head of the delegation, stated in his presentation speech: Under the leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao, the people of all ethnic groups in the Northwest have united closely. In the future, we must continue to consolidate this unity, so that the People's Republic of China will forever be a great family of solidarity, cooperation, mutual assistance, and fraternal love among all ethnic groups. People of all ethnic groups should universally carry out education on resisting America and aiding Korea, resolutely suppress counter-revolutionaries, and develop economic construction and cultural and educational undertakings.

### 西北各民族参观团向毛主席致敬

1951-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　西北各民族参观团向毛主席致敬
    【新华社二十四日讯】西北各民族参观团于二十四日上午十时代表西北地区的各兄弟民族，向中央人民政府毛主席、人民解放军朱总司令和政务院周总理献旗致敬。该团团长扎布丁在献词中称：西北各民族人民在伟大的领袖毛主席领导下，已亲密地团结起来，今后必须继续巩固这种团结，使中华人民共和国永远成为各民族人民团结合作、互助友爱的大家庭。各民族人民应普遍进行抗美援朝的教育、坚决镇压反革命、发展经济建设和文化教育事业。
