Title: Romanian People's Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang with Relief Supplies
Author:
Date: 1951-05-08
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 7th. The Romanian People's Delegation, dispatched by the Romanian people to support the Korean people, arrived in Pyongyang on April 27th, carrying 1,645 tons of clothes, leather shoes, and medicine. The delegation was led by Ms. Anna Lungu (Director of the International Department of the Romanian Democratic Women's League) and was warmly welcomed by representatives from all walks of life in Korea. The Romanian Medical Team arrived in Korea at the same time as the People's Delegation. The team, led by Dr. Bălărio, consists of 22 members and has gone to a certain place on the front line to set up a field hospital.

### 罗马尼亚人民代表团携带慰问物资抵平壤

1951-05-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚人民代表团携带慰问物资抵平壤
    【新华社平壤七日电】罗马尼亚人民为支援朝鲜人民而派遣的代表团，携带一六四五吨的衣服、皮鞋、药品，在安娜·伦古女士（罗马尼亚民主妇联国际部主任）率领之下，于四月二十七日到达平壤，受到朝鲜各界代表盛大的欢迎。与人民代表团同时到达朝鲜的，有罗马尼亚医疗队，该队由柏拉里翁博士率领，队员二十二人，已赴前线某处，设立野战病院。
