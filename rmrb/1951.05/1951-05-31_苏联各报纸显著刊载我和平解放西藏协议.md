Title: Soviet Newspapers Prominently Publish the Agreement on the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet

Author:

Date: 1951-05-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th. Moscow newspapers on the 29th prominently published the agreement between the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Tibetan Local Government on measures for the peaceful liberation of Tibet; the news of Chairman Mao Zedong's banquet on the evening of the 24th to celebrate the agreement on measures for the peaceful liberation of Tibet; the speech of Vice Chairman Zhu De of the Central People's Government at the signing ceremony of the agreement on measures for the peaceful liberation of Tibet; the speech of Apé Ngawang Jigme, the chief representative of the Tibetan Local Government; the speeches of the Panchen Erdeni and Apé Ngawang Jigme at the banquet specially hosted by Chairman Mao; and the news of the celebrations of the peaceful liberation of Tibet by people and newspapers all over China.

### 苏联各报纸显著刊载我和平解放西藏协议

1951-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联各报纸显著刊载我和平解放西藏协议
    【新华社莫斯科三十日电】二十九日莫斯科各报均以显著地位刊载中华人民共和国中央人民政府和西藏地方政府关于和平解放西藏办法的协议，毛泽东主席于二十四日晚设宴庆祝和平解放西藏办法的协议的新闻，中央人民政府朱德副主席在举行关于和平解放西藏办法的协议的签字仪式上的讲话和西藏地方政府首席代表阿沛·阿旺晋美的讲话，班禅额尔德尼和阿沛·阿旺晋美在毛主席特设的宴会上的演说，以及中国各地人民和报纸庆祝和平解放西藏的消息。
