　　Cultural Life Updates
    The All-China Federation of Art Workers Presents a Batch of Painting Materials to Korean Art Workers
    After learning that Korean art workers lacked painting materials, the All-China Federation of Art Workers purchased a box of various oil paints, traditional Chinese painting and poster paints, and wood carving knives, and presented them to Korean art workers. The gift was delivered to the Korean Embassy in China on April 17 for shipment to Korea.
  Central People's Broadcasting Station to Launch Military Programs on May Day
    The Central People's Broadcasting Station will launch military programs for troops across the country (especially those stationed on the frontiers of the motherland) starting May 1st. The schedule is as follows: Daily oral broadcasts from 12:15 PM to 12:45 PM, and documentary broadcasts from 12:45 PM to 2:00 PM. Wavelengths: 468.7 meters, 640 kilocycles; 416.6 meters, 720 kilocycles; 49.1 meters, 6100 kilocycles; 42.2 meters, 7100 kilocycles; 40.0 meters, 7500 kilocycles; 33.1 meters, 9040 kilocycles; 29.24 meters, 10260 kilocycles; 25.66 meters, 11690 kilocycles. The prelude will be "The Three Main Rules of Discipline and the Eight Points for Attention."

### 文化生活动态

专栏：文化生活动态

　　文化生活动态
    中华全国美术工作者协会以绘画材料一批赠给朝鲜美术工作者
    中华全国美术工作者协会知道朝鲜美术工作者缺乏绘画材料的消息以后，购置了各色油画颜料，国画、广告画颜料及木刻刀等一箱，赠送朝鲜美术工作者。该项赠品已于四月十七日送交朝鲜驻华大使馆转运朝鲜。
  中央人民广播电台五一起举办部队节目
    中央人民广播电台从五月一日起为全国部队（特别是驻守祖国边疆的部队）举办部队节目。时间为：每日中午十二时十五分至十二时四十五分为口语广播，十二时四十五分至二时为纪录广播。波长为：四六八·七公尺，六四○千周；四一六·六公尺，七二○千周；四九·一公尺，六一○○千周；四二·二公尺，七一○○千周；四○·○公尺，七五○○千周；三三·一公尺，九○四○千周；二九·二四公尺，一○二六○千周；二五·六六公尺，一一六九○千周。前奏曲为“三大纪律八项注意歌”。
