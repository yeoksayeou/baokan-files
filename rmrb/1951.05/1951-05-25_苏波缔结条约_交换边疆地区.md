Title: Soviet-Polish Treaty Concluded, Border Areas Exchanged
Author:
Date: 1951-05-25
Page: 4

　　Soviet-Polish Treaty Concluded
    Exchange of Border Areas
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 24th] The Soviet Union and Poland recently concluded a treaty for the exchange of border areas. According to a TASS dispatch from Moscow on the 22nd: The Polish government recently suggested to the Soviet government an exchange of the Lublin region on the Polish border for an equivalent area of the Drohobych Oblast on the border of the Soviet Ukrainian Republic, because these areas have economically advantageous conditions for the adjacent regions located within the Soviet Union and Poland. The Soviet government has agreed to the Polish government's suggestion. After negotiations in Moscow, the Soviet and Polish governments concluded a treaty, which was signed by Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky and Polish Deputy Prime Minister Zawadzki on behalf of their respective governments.

### 苏波缔结条约  交换边疆地区

1951-05-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏波缔结条约
    交换边疆地区
    【新华社二十四日讯】苏联与波兰最近缔结了交换边疆地区的条约。据塔斯社莫斯科二十二日讯：波兰政府最近向苏联政府建议，以波兰边疆的卢布林地区，交换苏联乌克兰共和国边疆的同等地区德罗荷彪赤州，因为这些地区对位于苏联和波兰境内的邻近地区都具有经济上的有利条件。苏联政府已同意波兰政府的建议。苏波两国政府在莫斯科举行谈判后，已缔结了一项条约，由苏联外交部长维辛斯基和波兰副总理萨瓦德斯基代表两国政府签字。
