Title: Resolutely Fight to Defend World Peace! Korean People Enthusiastically Support the Conclusion of a Peace Pact - Over 1,370,000 People Have Signed the Declaration
Author:
Date: 1951-05-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, May 3rd: The Korean people, standing on the front line of defending peace and fighting heroically, are enthusiastically signing the declaration demanding the conclusion of a peace pact. As of April 25th, a total of 1,374,052 workers, farmers, staff, students, industrialists, businessmen, and religious believers from various regions have signed the declaration. Mass meetings have been held in factories, mines, rural areas, and military units to discuss the decisions of the First Session of the World Peace Council, while enthusiastic signing ceremonies have been held.

　　In Pyongyang's Dong District, within the first half-hour of the signing campaign that began on April 16th, more than 790 women rushed to sign. As of April 25th, 87,000 people in the city had participated in the signing.

　　At the same time, people from all walks of life have sent more than 18,900 letters of determination to the Korean National Committee for the Defense of Peace, expressing their determination to fight resolutely to defend lasting world peace and strive for victory in the Fatherland Liberation War.

### 坚决为保卫世界和平而战斗！  朝鲜人民热烈拥护缔结和平公约  已有一百三十七万余人在宣言上签名
新华社
1951-05-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　坚决为保卫世界和平而战斗！
    朝鲜人民热烈拥护缔结和平公约
    已有一百三十七万余人在宣言上签名
    【新华社平壤三日电】站在保卫和平的第一线上英勇战斗的朝鲜人民，热烈地在要求缔结和平公约的宣言上签名。截至四月二十五日为止，各地工人、农民、职员、学生、工商业家、宗教徒在宣言上签名的，已有一百三十七万四千零五十二人。各工厂、矿山、农村、部队，都召开了群众大会，讨论世界和平理事会第一届会议的各项决定，同时热烈地举行签名。
    平壤市东区在四月十六日开始签名后的最初半小时内，就有七百九十多个妇女争先签名。截至二十五日为止，全市已有八万七千人参加了签名。
    与此同时，各阶层人民已把一万八千九百余件决心书寄给朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会，他们在决心书中表示，要为保卫世界持久和平与争取祖国解放战争的胜利而坚决奋斗。
