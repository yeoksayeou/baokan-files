Title: All-China Democratic Women's Federation Sends Congratulations to the Volunteer Army for "May Day"
Author:
Date: 1951-05-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. On the eve of the great "May Day" International Labor Day, we, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, on behalf of all the women of China, and also on behalf of your beloved mothers, wives, sisters and lovely children, extend our warmest and most respectful greetings to you! We wish you a happy holiday as you achieve even greater victories.

　　We will continue to do everything in our power to support you in all aspects, until we gloriously achieve the final victory in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea.

### 中华全国民主妇联电志愿军祝贺“五一”

1951-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　中华全国民主妇联电志愿军祝贺“五一”
    【新华社三十日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会于“五一”前夕致电中国人民志愿军祝贺。电文如下：
    值此伟大的“五一”劳动节，我们——中华全国民主妇女联合会，代表全中国妇女，也代表着你们亲爱的母亲、妻子、姊妹和可爱的孩子们向你们致最热烈、最崇高的敬礼！祝你们在获得更大的胜利中，欢度这个节日。
    我们将继续竭尽一切力量，从各方面来支援你们。直到光荣地取得抗美援朝的最后胜利。
