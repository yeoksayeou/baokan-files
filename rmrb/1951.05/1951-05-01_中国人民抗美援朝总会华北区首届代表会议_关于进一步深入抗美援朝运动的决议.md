Title: The North China Region First Representative Conference of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea: Resolution on Further Deepening the Resist America and Aid Korea Movement
Author:
Date: 1951-05-01
Page: 1

　　The North China Region First Representative Conference of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea
    Resolution on Further Deepening the Resist America and Aid Korea Movement
    We fully agree with Comrade Nie Zhen's report on the main situation and future tasks of the Resist America and Aid Korea Movement in North China.

    We call on the people of all of North China to resolutely oppose the rearmament of Japan by American imperialism, support the resolutions and declarations of the World Peace Council; universally establish patriotic pacts; actively support the just war of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army; continuously raise awareness of the reactionary nature of American imperialism, promote the great patriotic spirit of the people; eliminate the aggressive forces and influence of American imperialism; continue to expand the anti-American and patriotic united front, and suppress counter-revolutionary activities; further deepen and consolidate the Resist America and Aid Korea Movement, and use more practical actions to demonstrate our strength and determination to resist America and aid Korea to protect our homes and defend our country. We are determined to fight to the end to defend world peace and completely shatter the aggressive conspiracy of American imperialism!

### 中国人民抗美援朝总会华北区首届代表会议  关于进一步深入抗美援朝运动的决议

1951-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民抗美援朝总会华北区首届代表会议
    关于进一步深入抗美援朝运动的决议
    我们完全同意聂真同志关于华北抗美援朝运动的主要情况和今后任务的报告。
    我们号召全华北的人民，要坚决反对美帝国主义重新武装日本，拥护世界和平理事会的决议和宣言；普遍订立爱国公约；积极支援中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军的正义战争；要不断的提高对美帝国主义反动本质的认识，发扬人民的伟大爱国主义精神；肃清美帝国主义侵略势力和影响；要继续扩大反美爱国统一战线，镇压反革命活动；要进一步深入巩固抗美援朝运动，用更多的实际行动来显示我们抗美援朝保家卫国的力量和决心。我们坚决地为保卫世界和平，彻底粉碎美帝国主义的侵略阴谋而奋斗到底！
