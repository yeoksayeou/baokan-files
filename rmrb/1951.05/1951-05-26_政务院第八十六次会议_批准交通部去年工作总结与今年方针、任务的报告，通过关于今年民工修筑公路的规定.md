Title: The 86th Meeting of the Government Administration Council Approves the Ministry of Communications' Work Summary and Report on Guiding Principles and Tasks for Last Year, and Passes Regulations on Civilian Labor for Road Construction This Year
Author:
Date: 1951-05-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 25th: The 86th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 25th. The meeting heard and approved the report made by Zhang Bojun, Minister of the Ministry of Communications of the Central People's Government, on the work summary of the ministry in 1950 and the guiding principles and tasks for 1951, and passed the regulations on civilian labor for road construction in 1951. The meeting also approved several appointments and removals.

### 政务院第八十六次会议  批准交通部去年工作总结与今年方针、任务的报告，通过关于今年民工修筑公路的规定

1951-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院第八十六次会议
    批准交通部去年工作总结与今年方针、任务的报告，通过关于今年民工修筑公路的规定
    【新华社二十五日讯】中央人民政府政务院第八十六次政务会议于二十五日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府交通部章伯钧部长所作的该部一九五零年工作总结与一九五一年方针、任务的报告，通过了关于一九五一年民工修筑公路的规定。会议并通过任免案多起。
