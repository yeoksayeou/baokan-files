Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Delegation warmly welcomed in Soviet Capital
Author:
Date: 1951-05-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 3rd.— The delegation from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, led by Lin Boqu, to attend the Soviet May Day celebrations has been warmly received in Moscow. After arriving in the Soviet Union on April 26, the delegation attended a welcome reception held by the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. On the 27th, they toured Moscow and the exhibition of gifts for Stalin's 70th birthday, and participated in May Day celebrations in the Kirov District and the Proletarian District in groups. On the 28th, they visited the Kremlin. On the 30th, they attended a reception hosted by Yasnov, Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet. On May 1st, they participated in the May Day parade on Red Square, and on the 2nd, they watched a football match between the Dynamo team and the Soviet Army team. Shen Junru, the deputy head of the delegation, is scheduled to broadcast his impressions of participating in the Soviet May Day celebrations to China on Moscow Radio on the 3rd.

### 中苏友协观礼团在苏京备受欢迎

1951-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　中苏友协观礼团在苏京备受欢迎
    【新华社莫斯科三日电】由林伯渠率领的中苏友好协会参加苏联五一节观礼代表团在莫斯科备受热烈招待。代表团于四月二十六日抵苏后，出席了苏联对外文化协会的欢迎酒会。二十七日参观莫斯科市容和斯大林七十寿辰礼品展览会，并分组参加了基洛夫区和无产者区的五一节纪念会。二十八日参观克里姆林宫。三十日出席莫斯科市苏维埃主席雅斯诺夫的招待会。五月一日参加了红场五一节大检阅，二日参观了狄纳莫球队和苏军球队的足球比赛。代表团沈钧儒副团长定于三日在莫斯科广播电台向中国广播参加苏联五一庆典的观感。
