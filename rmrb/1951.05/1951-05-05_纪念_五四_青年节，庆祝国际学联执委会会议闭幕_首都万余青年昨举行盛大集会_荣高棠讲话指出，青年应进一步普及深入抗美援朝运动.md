Title: Commemorating "May Fourth" Youth Day and Celebrating the Closing of the International Union of Students Executive Committee Meeting: Over Ten Thousand Young People in the Capital Held a Grand Gathering Yesterday; Rong Gaotang's Speech Pointed Out That Youth Should Further Popularize and Deepen the Resist America, Aid Korea Movement
Author:
Date: 1951-05-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Over 12,000 young people from all walks of life in the capital held a grand gathering this morning to commemorate the 32nd anniversary of the "May Fourth" Youth Day and celebrate the successful conclusion of the International Union of Students (IUS) Executive Committee meeting. Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretary, Rong Gaotang, Secretary-General, Qian Sanqiang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, Xie Bangding, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and Yang Bozhen, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, attended the meeting.

　　The conference was opened by Yang Bozhen with an opening speech. After a speech by Deputy Mayor Wu Han, Rong Gaotang, on behalf of the China New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, and the youth of the country, paid tribute to the representatives of the IUS Executive Committee, and stated: When commemorating "May Fourth" Youth Day, Chinese youth and students with the revolutionary tradition of the "May Fourth" Movement, especially the Youth League members who believe in Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought, should, under the leadership of the People's Government and the Communist Party, further popularize and deepen the Resist America, Aid Korea Movement, oppose the United States arming Japan, and support the conclusion of a peace pact by the five major powers, in order to further safeguard the security of the motherland and maintain peace in the Far East and the world.

　　Xie Bangding said in his speech: The holding of the IUS Executive Committee meeting in China marks the close connection between Chinese students and students from Eastern countries and students from all over the world. The resolutions passed by the Executive Committee meeting reflect the will and demands of peace-loving students all over the world. Chinese students with the glorious tradition of the "May Fourth" Movement will resolutely fight to implement these resolutions.

　　IUS President Gromov spoke amidst warm applause. He congratulated Chinese Youth Day on behalf of the IUS Executive Committee and the more than 5 million members of the IUS in 71 countries. He said: Chinese youth and students are victoriously launching a vigorous movement to resist America and aid Korea. The victory of Chinese youth and students is the victory of all freedom- and peace-loving people in the world.

　　Soviet representative Yershova spoke. She praised the outstanding contributions of Chinese youth and students in the struggle against imperialism and feudal forces, and in the cause of economic and cultural construction of the new China. She emphasized: Soviet youth and the people of the whole country, together, have brotherly affection for the Chinese people who are fighting to defend world peace and oppose American aggression. Soviet youth and people express their warm respect to the Chinese People's Volunteers who are fighting side by side with the heroic Korean people.

　　Korean representative Park Chol-bok said: In the just war against American aggression, the people and youth of China and Korea have forged a steel-like friendship. The will and strength shown by the Chinese people in the Resist America, Aid Korea Movement will further encourage the fighting will of Korean youth and students, and will further consolidate the friendship and unity between Chinese and Korean youth. Representatives from Vietnam, India, West Africa, Hungary, Brazil, and other countries spoke successively. They praised the great achievements of the new China and expressed their willingness to strengthen friendship and unity with Chinese youth and students.

　　At the meeting, IUS President Gromov, Soviet youth representative Yershova, and Hungarian representative Gozsechey presented gifts and flags to the All-China Students' Federation and the China New Democratic Youth League respectively. IUS Secretary-General and Italian representative Berlinguer presented three boxes of medicines to the Korean representative, expressing support for the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers. The All-China Students' Federation, the Beijing Students' Federation, and college and middle school students in Beijing presented gifts and flags to the IUS. Beijing Young Pioneers presented flowers to the IUS Executive Committee and student representatives from various countries.

　　The conference then adopted a telegram of salute to Chairman Mao and a telegram of salute to the Chinese People's Volunteers. Finally, the conference concluded with the entire audience singing the IUS anthem.

### 纪念“五四”青年节，庆祝国际学联执委会会议闭幕  首都万余青年昨举行盛大集会  荣高棠讲话指出，青年应进一步普及深入抗美援朝运动

1951-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　纪念“五四”青年节，庆祝国际学联执委会会议闭幕
    首都万余青年昨举行盛大集会
    荣高棠讲话指出，青年应进一步普及深入抗美援朝运动
    【新华社四日讯】首都各界青年一万二千余人在今日上午举行盛大集会，纪念“五四”青年节三十二周年，并庆祝国际学联执委会会议的胜利闭幕。中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬、副书记蒋南翔、秘书长荣高棠，中华全国民主青年联合总会副主席钱三强，中华全国学生联合会主席谢邦定，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，中国新民主主义青年团北京市委员会书记杨伯箴均到会。
    大会由杨伯箴致开幕辞。在吴晗副市长讲话后，荣高棠代表中国新民主主义青年团、中华全国民主青年联合总会及全国青年向国际学联执委会代表致敬，并称：在纪念“五四”青年节的时候，有着“五四”革命传统的中国青年和学生，特别是信从马克思列宁主义、毛泽东思想的青年团员，应该在人民政府和共产党的领导下，更进一步地普及与深入抗美援朝运动，反对美国武装日本，拥护五大国缔结和平公约，以进一步保障祖国安全，维护远东和世界和平。
    谢邦定致词称：国际学联执委会会议在中国举行，标志了中国学生与东方各国学生和世界各国学生之间的密切联系。执委会会议通过的各项决议，反映了全世界爱好和平学生的意志和要求。有着“五四”光荣传统的中国学生将为执行这些决议而坚决奋斗。
    国际学联主席格罗曼在热烈掌声中发言，他代表国际学联执委会及国际学联七十一个国家的五百多万会员，庆贺中国青年节。他说：中国青年学生正在胜利地展开如火如荼地抗美援朝的运动。中国青年学生的胜利就是全世界爱好自由与和平的人民的胜利。
    苏联代表叶尔硕娃致词。她盛赞中国青年和学生在反对帝国主义和封建势力的斗争中，在新中国的经济与文化建设事业上的卓越贡献。她强调说：苏联青年和全国人民一道，对为保卫世界和平反对美国侵略而斗争的中国人民怀着兄弟般的情谊。苏联青年和人民谨向与英勇朝鲜人民并肩作战的中国人民志愿军表示热烈的敬意。
    朝鲜代表朴铁福发言称：在反对美国侵略的正义战争中，中朝两国人民和青年结成了钢铁般的友谊。中国人民在抗美援朝运动中表现的意志和力量，将更加鼓舞朝鲜青年和学生的战斗意志，并将进一步巩固中朝两国青年的友谊和团结。越南、印度、西非洲、匈牙利、巴西等各国代表相继讲话。他们盛赞新中国的伟大成就，并表示愿意加强与中国青年学生的友谊与团结。
    会上，国际学联主席格罗曼、苏联青年代表叶尔硕娃、匈牙利代表高兹车益分别向中华全国学生联合会及中国新民主主义青年团献礼献旗。国际学联总书记、意大利代表贝林格以药品三箱献给朝鲜代表，表示对朝鲜人民军及中国人民志愿军的支援。中华全国学生联合会、北京市学联及北京大中学生相继向国际学联献礼献旗。北京少年儿童队员向国际学联执委会及各国学生代表献花。
    大会继通过了向毛主席致敬电及向中国人民志愿军致敬电。最后在全场齐唱国际学联会歌后结束。
