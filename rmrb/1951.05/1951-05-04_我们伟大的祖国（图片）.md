Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-05-04
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Today is the 32nd anniversary of the May Fourth Movement, the beginning of the great Chinese New Democratic Revolution. This is the "Red Building" of Peking University. During the May Fourth period, it was the "command post" of the new anti-imperialist and anti-feudal cultural movement in the North.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    今天是伟大的中国新民主主义革命的开端——“五四”运动三十二周年纪念日。这是北京大学的“红楼”。它在“五四”时期曾是北方反帝反封建的新文化运动的“司令台”。
