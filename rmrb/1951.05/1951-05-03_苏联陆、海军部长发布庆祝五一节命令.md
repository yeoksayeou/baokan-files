Title: Soviet Ministers of the Army and Navy Issue Orders Celebrating May Day

Author: 
Date: 1951-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 1st, Telegraph: Soviet Army Minister Vasilevsky issued the following order celebrating May Day:

Comrades soldiers and non-commissioned officers of the Army! Comrades officers and generals!

Today, the Soviet Army joins all the people of our country in celebrating May Day - the festival of solidarity of the working people of the world, the festival of brotherly friendship of the workers of all countries.

The Soviet people are greeting May Day with outstanding victories in all aspects of economic and cultural development. Under the leadership of the Lenin-Stalin Party, the working people of our country have victoriously completed the Stalin post-war Five-Year Plan and are confidently marching towards new victories in communist construction.

Our Army, with infinite loyalty to its people, to the Communist Party and the Soviet government, vigilantly defends the constructive labor of the Soviet people.

The troops are fulfilling the tasks set by the great Stalin for the Soviet Army and have achieved new successes in combat and political training.

On the occasion of May Day, I extend my congratulations to all personnel of the Soviet Army and wish soldiers, non-commissioned officers, officers and generals more victories in perfecting military technology, strengthening discipline and improving the combat readiness of the Army.

In commemoration of the festival of the working people of the world, I order: Today, May 1st, in Moscow, the capital of our motherland, in the capitals of the various union republics, in Kaliningrad, Khabarovsk, Vladivostok, and in the hero cities of Leningrad, Stalingrad, Sevastopol and Odessa, a salute of twenty guns shall be fired.

Long live our motherland—the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics!

Long live the great Soviet people and their heroic Army!

Long live our Soviet government!

Long live the Bolshevik Communist Party of the Soviet Union!

Long live our leader, teacher and commander, Comrade Stalin!

Minister of the Soviet Army, Marshal of the Soviet Union Vasilevsky (signed)

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 1st, Telegraph: The Soviet Navy Minister issued the following order celebrating May Day:

Comrades soldiers and non-commissioned officers of the Navy! Comrades naval officers and generals!

Today, our Navy joins all the Soviet people in celebrating May Day—the international festival of the working people.

The Soviet people, under the leadership of the Party of Lenin and Stalin, are greeting May Day with new achievements in developing the national economy and culture and consolidating the power of the socialist state.

The Soviet people have victoriously completed the post-war Five-Year Plan and are now implementing Stalin's plan for building communism with great enthusiasm.

The Soviet Navy vigilantly guards the borders of our motherland and celebrates May Day with new achievements in combat and political training.

Comrades soldiers and non-commissioned officers of the Navy! Comrades naval officers and generals! On the occasion of the international festival of the working people—May Day, I extend my congratulations to you! I wish you further success in strengthening the combat power of the Soviet Navy!

Long live our Soviet motherland!

Long live the heroic Soviet people and their Navy!

Long live our Soviet government!

Long live the inspirer and organizer of our victory—the Party of Lenin and Stalin!

Long live our great teacher and leader, Comrade Stalin!

Minister of the Soviet Navy, Admiral Yumashev (signed)

### 苏联陆、海军部长发布庆祝五一节命令

1951-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联陆、海军部长发布庆祝五一节命令
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社莫斯科一日电：苏联陆军部长华西列夫斯基发布庆祝五一节的命令如下：
    陆军战士与士官同志们！校尉与将军同志们！
    今天，苏联陆军与我国全体人民共同欢庆五一节——全世界劳动人民团结的节日，一切国家的工人兄弟友爱的节日。
    苏联人民是以经济与文化发展各方面的卓越胜利来迎接五一节的。在列宁—斯大林党的领导下，我国劳动人民已经胜利地完成了斯大林战后五年计划，并且正在满怀信心地向共产主义建设的新的胜利前进。
    我国陆军对于自己的人民，对于共产党和苏维埃政府，具有无限的忠诚，警惕地保卫着苏联人民的建设性劳动。
    部队执行着伟大的斯大林为苏联陆军规定的任务，在战斗与政治训练方面都获得了新的成就。
    值此五一节，我谨向苏联陆军全体人员致贺，并祝士兵们、士官们、校尉和将军们在使军事技术更臻完善、加强纪律和提高陆军的战斗准备上，取得更多的胜利。
    为了纪念全世界劳动人民的节日，我命令：今天，五月一日，在我们祖国的首都莫斯科，在各加盟共和国的首都，在加里宁格勒、罗夫、伯力、海参崴以及在列宁格勒、斯大林格勒、悉瓦斯托波尔和敖德萨诸英雄城市，一律鸣放礼炮二十响。
    我们的祖国——苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟万岁！
    伟大的苏维埃人民和他们的英勇的陆军万岁！
    我们的苏维埃政府万岁！
    苏联布尔什维克共产党万岁！
    我们的领袖、导师与统帅斯大林同志万岁！
    苏联陆军部部长、苏联元帅华西列夫斯基（签字）
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社莫斯科一日电：苏联海军部长发布庆祝五一节命令如下：
    海军战士与士官同志们！海军校尉与将军同志们！
    今天，我国海军与全体苏联人民一道庆祝五一节——劳动人民的国际节日。
    在列宁与斯大林党领导下的苏联人民，是以发展国民经济和文化、巩固社会主义国家威力方面的新成就，来迎接五一节的。
    苏联人民已胜利地完成了战后五年计划，现正以极大的热忱来实现斯大林建设共产主义的计划。
    苏联海军警觉地守卫着我们祖国的边疆，并以战斗与政治训练方面的新成就来庆祝五一节。
    海军战士与士官同志们！海军校尉与将军同志们！值此劳动人民的国际节日——五一节，我向你们祝贺！我希望你们在加强苏联海军的战斗威力方面获得进一步的成就！
    我们的苏维埃祖国万岁！
    英雄的苏联人民和他们的海军万岁！
    我们的苏维埃政府万岁！
    我们胜利的鼓舞者和组织者——列宁与斯大林的党万岁！
    我们伟大的导师与领袖斯大林同志万岁！
      苏联海军部部长、海军上将尤马谢夫（签字）
