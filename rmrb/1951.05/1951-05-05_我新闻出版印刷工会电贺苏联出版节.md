Title: Chinese News, Publishing, and Printing Workers' Union Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Soviet Publishing Festival
Author:
Date: 1951-05-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Report: For the Soviet Publishing Festival on May 5th, the preparatory committee of the Chinese News, Publishing, and Printing Workers' Union sent a telegram to Comrade Linno, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Soviet News and Publishing Workers' Union, on April 30th, expressing congratulations. The telegram stated: As the "May 5th" festival of Soviet news and publishing workers approaches, we, on behalf of all Chinese news, publishing, and printing workers, extend our heartfelt congratulations to you and the members of your union for your great achievements in communist construction and the struggle for world peace.

### 我新闻出版印刷工会电贺苏联出版节

1951-05-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　我新闻出版印刷工会电贺苏联出版节
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：五月五日为苏联出版节，中国新闻出版印刷工会筹备委员会于四月三十日致电苏联新闻出版工会中央委员会主席林诺，表示祝贺。电文称：当“五·五”这个苏联新闻出版工人的节日快要到来的时候，我们代表全体中国新闻出版印刷工人向您及贵会会员在共产主义建设及争取世界和平的斗争中的伟大成就致衷心的祝贺。
