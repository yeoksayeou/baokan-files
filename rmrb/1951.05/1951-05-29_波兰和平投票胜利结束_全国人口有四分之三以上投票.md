Title: Polish Peace Vote Ends in Victory; More Than Three-Quarters of the National Population Votes
Author:
Date: 1951-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw dispatch, 28th: The “National Peace Vote” in Poland, in support of the conclusion of a peace pact by the five great powers, has ended victoriously. During the ten days from the 17th to the 26th, a total of 18,053,315 people voted, accounting for more than three-quarters of the national population. This fully demonstrates the Polish people's will and strength in defending world peace.

### 波兰和平投票胜利结束  全国人口有四分之三以上投票

1951-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　波兰和平投票胜利结束
    全国人口有四分之三以上投票
    【新华社二十八日讯】据塔斯社华沙讯：波兰人民拥护五大国缔结和平公约的“全国和平投票”已经胜利结束。自十七日至二十六日的十天中，共有一千八百零五万三千三百十五人投了票，占全国人口的四分之三以上，这充分显示了波兰人民保卫世界和平的意志与力量。
