Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-05-28
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet has been signed. Here is the Potala Palace, the political, economic and cultural center of Tibet, located northwest of Lhasa. This palace is situated on Mount Potala and is a large thirteen-story building that is three hundred feet high. This is an important creation of our Tibetan people.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-05-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    西藏和平解放办法的协议签字了。这里是西藏的政治、经济和文化的中心——拉萨城西北的布达拉宫。这座宫殿坐落在布达拉山上，是一个高到三百尺的十三层大建筑。这是我国藏族人民的一个重要创造。
