Title: Ode to Chairman Mao
Author: Songla Cailang
Date: 1951-05-06
Page: 5

　　Ode to Chairman Mao
    (Tibet) Songla Cailang
     The pine trees on the mountains are green,
     The river water is rolling,
     The sunlight is warm,
     Chairman Mao's kindness is even dearer than our parents.
        (This poem is reprinted from "Qinghai Daily")

### 歌颂毛主席
松拉才郎
1951-05-06
第5版()
专栏：

　　歌颂毛主席
    （西藏）松拉才郎
     山上的松树青青的哩、
     河里的流水滚滚的哩，
     太阳的光芒暖暖的哩，
     毛主席的恩情比父母还亲哩。
        （此诗转载自《青海日报》）
