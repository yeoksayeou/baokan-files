Our Great Motherland
The Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions has established two workers' sanatoriums in the nationally renowned scenic West Lake area, allowing Shanghai's model workers to go there for recuperation in batches. This is a picture of model workers on vacation touring the Lake Heart Pavilion.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    上海市总工会在全国闻名的西湖风景区，举办了两个工人休养所，让上海的劳动模范分批前往休养。这是休养中的劳动模范们正在游览湖心亭。
