Title: Sunday at Xinhua Bookstore (Capital Sketches - No. 7)
Author: Shao Yu
Date: 1951-05-30
Page: 3

　　Sunday at Xinhua Bookstore (Capital Sketches - No. 7)
　　By Shao Yu
　　On Sundays,
　　The bookstore becomes extraordinarily busy;
　　Revolutionary theories and scientific publications,
　　Are widely attracting and moving people's hearts!
　　Here are workers, public servants, teachers, students... and people engaged in various labors and professions,
　　There are also suburban farmers reading picture books with their children. (Image attached)

### 新华书店的星期天  （首都速写之七）
邵宇
1951-05-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　新华书店的星期天  （首都速写之七）
    邵宇作
    星期天，
    书店里就显得格外的忙碌；
    革命理论和科学的书刊，
    正在广泛地吸引和打动着人心！
    这里有工人、公务人员、教员、学生……以及从事各种劳动和职业的人，
    也有同孩子们一起读小人书的城郊农民。（附图片）
