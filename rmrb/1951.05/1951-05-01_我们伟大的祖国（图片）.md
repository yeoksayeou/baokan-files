Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-05-01
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    To welcome this year's "May Day" Labor Day, the workers of the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant in Beijing launched a patriotic production competition to resist US aggression and aid Korea, creating unprecedented new records in all aspects. This is the grand scene of the reporting team composed of 160 people's representatives from the plant on April 17th, when they went to the National Federation of Trade Unions to report the good news.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京石景山钢铁厂职工为了迎接今年的“五一”劳动节，展开了抗美援朝爱国主义生产竞赛，在各方面都创造了空前的新纪录。这是该厂一百六十个人大代表组成的报喜队，于四月十七日前往全国总大会报喜时的盛况。
