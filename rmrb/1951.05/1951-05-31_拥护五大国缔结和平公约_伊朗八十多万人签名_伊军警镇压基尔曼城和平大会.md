Title: Supporting the Five Great Powers to Conclude a Peace Pact: Over 800,000 Signatures in Iran; Iranian Military and Police Suppress Peace Conference in Kirman

Author:

Date: 1951-05-31
Page: 4

Supporting the Five Great Powers to Conclude a Peace Pact
Over 800,000 Signatures in Iran
Iranian Military and Police Suppress Peace Conference in Kirman

[This Newspaper’s News] According to Xinhua News Agency: As of the 23rd, over 800,000 people in various parts of Iran had signed the World Peace Council's declaration calling for the five major powers to conclude a peace pact. Among them, more than 500,000 were in Tehran, and 100,000 were in Isfahan. Thousands upon thousands of people have also signed in other cities and villages.

The signatures were collected at mass rallies. In Shiraz and Babol, 20,000 people participated in peace supporter conferences, and in Hamadan, 10,000 people participated in a meeting of the same nature.

[Xinhua News Agency, 30th] TASS, Tehran, 27th telegram citing the "Bisu-Ayangdi Newspaper" reported: On May 25th, in Kirman City, police and troops wielding knives and firing guns dispersed a peace conference attended by 20,000 people. A woman was killed among the masses attending the conference, several people were injured, and fifteen people were arrested and imprisoned. On May 26th, workers at the Kirman "Holshide" factory went on strike for two hours to protest the authorities' actions. Workers' representatives telegraphed Tehran, demanding the immediate release of those arrested and demanding a trial of the chief culprits and accomplices who violently disrupted the peace conference, resulting in bloodshed.

### 拥护五大国缔结和平公约  伊朗八十多万人签名  伊军警镇压基尔曼城和平大会

1951-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　拥护五大国缔结和平公约
    伊朗八十多万人签名
    伊军警镇压基尔曼城和平大会
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：到二十三日为止，伊朗各地已有八十多万人在世界和平理事会要求五大国缔结和平公约的宣言上签了名。其中，德黑兰有五十多万人，伊斯法罕有十万人。其他城市、乡村也都有成千成万的人签名。
    签名是在群众大会上举行的。在设拉子、巴布尔，都有二万人参加了和平拥护者大会，在哈马旦有一万人参加同样性质的会议。
    【新华社三十日讯】塔斯社德黑兰二十七日电引《比苏、阿杨第报》消息：五月二十五日在基尔曼城，警察与军队挥刀和开枪冲散了一个有二万人参加的和平大会。到会群众中有一个妇女被杀死，有几个人受了伤，还有十五个人被捕下狱。五月二十六日，基尔曼“荷尔西德”工厂的工人罢工两小时，抗议当局的行动。工人代表致电德黑兰，要求立即释放被捕的人，并要求开庭审讯以武力捣乱和平大会，以致造成流血事件的首恶与帮凶。
