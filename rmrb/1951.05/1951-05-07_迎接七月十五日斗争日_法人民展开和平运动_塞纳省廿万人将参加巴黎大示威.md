Title: Greeting the July 15th Struggle Day: French People Launch Peace Movement; 200,000 People in the Seine Province to Participate in Paris Demonstration
Author:
Date: 1951-05-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd dispatch: Paris News: The people of France are enthusiastically launching a peace movement, preparing to welcome the July 15th "Day of Struggle for the French People Against the Remilitarization of Germany and for the Five Great Powers to Conclude a Peace Pact." This date was decided by the French National Peace Council on April 15th. The movement in France to collect signatures for the declaration for a peace pact and the protest against the armament of West Germany will reach its climax on this day.

The Seine Province Peace Committee guarantees that the working people of the Seine Province will send 200,000 representatives to participate in the large demonstration in Paris on July 15th, and will collect four million signatures for the declaration demanding the conclusion of a peace pact and the prohibition of the remilitarization of Germany before this day. Six hundred representatives from higher education institutions in Paris and other French cities held a conference on April 22nd, demanding that the five major powers conclude a peace pact. The Seine Province members of the National Union of Journalists of the French General Confederation of Labor recently also held a representative conference for this purpose.

The Seine Province Villeneuve-la-Garenne branch of the Human Rights League, taxi drivers in Paris, some scientific workers in Paris, the Drôme Provincial Council, the Dinant Municipal Council in the Nord Province, and the people of the Gard, Savoie, and other provinces have also signed their names one after another, demanding the conclusion of a peace pact among the five major powers. The people of Lot-et-Garonne and Hérault provinces are enthusiastically signing protests against the armament of West Germany.

### 迎接七月十五日斗争日  法人民展开和平运动  塞纳省廿万人将参加巴黎大示威

1951-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　迎接七月十五日斗争日
    法人民展开和平运动
    塞纳省廿万人将参加巴黎大示威
    【新华社三日讯】巴黎消息：全法国人民正热烈展开和平运动，准备迎接七月十五日“法国人民反对德国重新军国主义化及争取五大国缔结和平公约斗争日”。这个日子，是法国全国和平理事会在四月十五日决定的。法国为和平公约宣言与反对武装西德抗议书征集签名的运动，将在这一天达到最高潮。
    塞纳省和平委员会保证：塞纳省的劳动人民要派出二十万个代表参加七月十五日在巴黎举行的大示威，并且要在这一天以前为要求缔结和平公约和禁止德国重新军国主义化的宣言征集到四百万人的签名。巴黎及法国其他城市高等教育机关的六百个代表，在四月二十二日举行大会，要求五大国缔结和平公约。法国总工会所属全国新闻记者工会的塞纳省会员，最近也为此举行了代表大会。
    人权联盟塞纳省谷诺圃地方支部，巴黎的出租汽车司机，巴黎的若干科学工作者，德罗摩省省政委员会，诺尔省迪南市市政委员会和加尔省、萨瓦省以及其他各省的人民，也纷纷签名要求缔结五大国和平公约。罗特——加隆省和爱罗尔省的人民，都踊跃在反对武装西德的抗议书上签名。
