Title: Our People's Political Consultative Conference's May Day Appeal Prominently Published in Soviet Newspapers
Author:
Date: 1951-05-01
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 29th: Soviet newspapers on the 28th prominently published the call from the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference for the celebration of the 1951 May Day Labor Day.

### 我人民政协五一节号召苏联各报显著刊载

1951-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　我人民政协五一节号召苏联各报显著刊载
    【新华社莫斯科四月二十九日电】二十八日苏联各报均以显著地位刊载了中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会为庆祝一九五一年五一劳动节的号召。
