Title: Strive to Conclude a Peace Pact! Over 17 Million People in Poland Vote for Peace; Czechoslovakia Decides to Begin Signing and Voting

Author:
Date: 1951-05-28
Page: 4

　　Strive to Conclude a Peace Pact!
　　Over 17 Million People in Poland Vote for Peace
　　Czechoslovakia Decides to Begin Signing and Voting
　　[Xinhua News Agency, 26th dispatch] According to TASS, Warsaw: The “National Peace Vote” in Poland, in support of a peace pact, is nearing its end. In the seven days from the 17th to the 23rd, 17,921,868 people have voted, accounting for approximately 74% of the country's population. Peace activists across the country are now holding peace relay races, bringing the news of the vote's conclusion to the provincial committees for defending peace.
　　[Xinhua News Agency, 26th dispatch] According to TASS, Prague: The Central Action Committee of the Czechoslovak National Front and the Czechoslovak Peace Committee held a joint meeting in Bratislava on the 23rd. The meeting discussed urgent issues such as how to complete the expanded Five-Year Plan and implement the resolutions of the World Peace Council. The meeting decided to hold a people's vote throughout the country from May 26th to June 16th, demanding the conclusion of a peace pact among the five major powers and opposing the rearmament of West Germany.
　　荷第诺娃——斯普尔纳[Ho Ti Nuo Wa - Si Pu Er Na], Chairman of the Czechoslovak Peace Committee, read out the Peace Committee's letter to the people at the meeting, calling on the people of the whole country to participate in the people's vote in order to once again demonstrate their unyielding will to defend peace. Those attending the meeting unanimously signed in support of the five major powers concluding a peace pact and opposing the arming of West Germany.

### 争取缔结和平公约！  波一千七百余万人和平投票  捷决定开始举行签名和投票

1951-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　争取缔结和平公约！
    波一千七百余万人和平投票
    捷决定开始举行签名和投票
    【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社华沙讯：波兰人民拥护和平公约的“全国和平投票”即将结束。自十七日至二十三日的七天中，已有一千七百九十二万一千八百六十八人投了票，约占全国人口百分之七十四。全国各地区的和平战士现正举办和平接力跑，把投票结束的消息带交各省保卫和平委员会。
    【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克民族阵线中央行动委员会与捷克斯洛伐克和平委员会于二十三日在布拉的斯拉发举行联席会议。会议讨论了如何完成扩大后的五年计划及实现世界和平理事会的决议等迫切问题。会议决定从五月二十六日到六月十六日在全国举行要求缔结五大国和平公约及反对重新武装西德的人民投票。
    捷克斯洛伐克和平委员会主席荷第诺娃——斯普尔纳在会上宣读了和平委员会的告人民书，号召全国人民参加人民投票，以再次表明他们保卫和平的不屈不挠的意志。出席会议的人一致签名要求五大国缔结和平公约、反对武装西德。
