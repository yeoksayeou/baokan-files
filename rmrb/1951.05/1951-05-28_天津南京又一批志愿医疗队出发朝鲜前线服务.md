Title: Another Batch of Volunteer Medical Teams from Tianjin and Nanjing Depart to Serve the Korean Front

Author:

Date: 1951-05-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, May 26th: The Third Detachment of the Chinese Red Cross International Medical and Epidemic Prevention Service Team and the Third Detachment of the Tianjin Anti-US Aggression and Aid Korea Volunteer Medical Team departed from Tianjin for Korea on the 25th. Xu Jianguo, Deputy Mayor of Tianjin, Zhu Xianyi, Director of the Tianjin Medical Workers' Committee for Anti-US Aggression and Aid Korea, and over a thousand representatives from all walks of life went to the station to see them off. The people of Tianjin held a grand send-off meeting with over two thousand participants on the afternoon of the 23rd. Xu Jianguo, Deputy Mayor of the Municipal People's Government, and Liao Chengzhi, Head of the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, addressed the meeting, both wishing the team members success in completing their mission.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, May 26th: The second batch of volunteer medical teams of medical and health workers in Nanjing departed for Korea on the morning of the 25th to take over the work of the first batch of personnel. The team included six surgical teams, five medical teams, one blood bank team, and one epidemic prevention team. The Nanjing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for Resisting America and Aiding Korea, the medical and health community, and people from all walks of life have successively held meetings to send off the medical team. When the Chinese People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea Headquarters called on various places to organize the second batch of volunteer medical teams, medical and health workers in Nanjing responded enthusiastically. More than 1,900 people have signed up to participate. The number of applicants from the Nanjing University Medical College accounted for 90 percent of the total number of students and faculty. More than 20 practicing physicians also requested to join the medical team.

### 天津南京又一批志愿医疗队出发朝鲜前线服务

1951-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　天津南京又一批志愿医疗队出发朝鲜前线服务
    【新华社天津二十六日电】中国红十字会国际医防服务队第三大队及天津市抗美援朝志愿医疗队第三大队，已于二十五日离津赴朝鲜。天津市副市长许建国、市医务工作者抗美援朝救护委员会主任朱宪彝及各界代表千余人，曾赴车站欢送。天津市各界人民并曾于二十三日下午举行了二千余人的盛大欢送会。市人民政府许建国副市长、中国人民赴朝慰问团廖承志团长均到会致词，一致预祝各位队员胜利地完成任务。
    【新华社南京二十六日电】南京市医药卫生工作者的第二批抗美援朝志愿医疗团，已于二十五日晨出发赴朝鲜，接替首批人员的工作。该团包括六个手术队、五个医疗队、一个血库队和一个防疫队。南京市抗美援朝分会，医药卫生界及各界人民，曾先后集会，欢送该医疗团出发。当中国人民抗美援朝总会号召各地组织第二批志愿医疗队时，南京市医药卫生工作者即热烈响应。先后报名要求参加者达一千九百余人。南京大学医学院报名人数占该院全体人数的百分之九十。开业医师中亦有二十余人要求参加医疗团。
