Title: People's Democratic Countries Warmly Celebrate May Day
Author:
Date: 1951-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The working people of Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Bulgaria, Romania, Albania and Mongolia warmly celebrated International Labor Day on the 1st.

　　Poland

A grand military parade and a demonstration of 350,000 people were held in Warsaw, the capital of Poland. Polish President Bolesław Bierut delivered a speech during the review, summarizing the great achievements of the Polish people in socialist construction since the war. Demonstrators marched in formation, shouting slogans to defend peace, and the parade lasted six hours. The people of Warsaw rejoiced until late at night.

　　Czechoslovakia

One million residents of Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia, participated in the May Day celebrations. A military parade and a demonstration to defend peace were held in Wenceslas Square. President Klement Gottwald of the Republic delivered a May Day speech, praising the great contribution of the Chinese and Korean People's forces fighting side by side in Korea to the cause of world peace.

　　Hungary

In Budapest, the capital of Hungary, the contingents participating in the review and demonstration marched triumphantly across Heroes' Square, paying tribute to Mátyás Rákosi, General Secretary of the Hungarian Working People's Party. The demonstrators carried vivid paintings and charts depicting the outstanding achievements of the working people in implementing the Five-Year Plan. Slogans such as "Defend peace, down with the warmongers, long live the heroic Korean people! Long live the friendship of the people of all countries!" resounded throughout the venue.

　　Bulgaria

In Sofia, the capital of Bulgaria, 300,000 working people held an unprecedented military parade and demonstration. When the mighty contingent passed in front of the Dimitrov Mausoleum, they were welcomed by Vulko Chervenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria. The large banners held by the demonstrators expressed the Bulgarian people's will to support the conclusion of a peace pact among the five great powers and to guarantee the early completion of production plans.

　　Romania

The demonstration of the working people of Bucharest, the capital of Romania, was held in Stalin Square. Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, and other leaders of the Party and government attended the review and were warmly cheered by the masses. The demonstrators held aloft giant portraits of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin, and banners calling for intensified defense of peace. When the first tractor manufactured by the Stalin City Tractor Factory appeared in the parade, the audience burst into thunderous applause.

　　Albania

In Tirana, the capital of Albania, 70,000 working people held a May Day demonstration. Gogo Nushi, Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Council of Albanian Trade Unions, extended warm congratulations to the demonstrators.

　　Mongolia

A festive atmosphere prevailed throughout the Mongolian People's Republic. Mass rallies celebrating May Day were held in enterprises, educational institutions, and clubs in Ulaanbaatar and other places.

### 各人民民主国家热烈庆祝五一劳动节

1951-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　各人民民主国家热烈庆祝五一劳动节
    【新华社二日讯】波、捷、匈、保、罗、阿尔巴尼亚及蒙古七国劳动人民于一日热烈庆祝了五一国际劳动节。
    波
    波兰首都华沙举行盛大的阅兵和三十五万人的示威游行。波兰共和国总统贝鲁特在检阅时发表演说，综述了波兰人民战后在社会主义建设方面的巨大成就。示威者在高呼保卫和平的口号声中列队行进，游行历时六小时。华沙人民狂欢直至深夜。
  捷
    捷克斯洛伐克京城布拉格的一百万居民都参加了庆祝五一节的活动，温西斯拉斯广场举行了阅兵和保卫和平的示威。共和国总统哥特瓦尔德发表五一节演说，盛赞在朝鲜并肩作战的中朝人民部队对世界和平事业的巨大贡献。
  匈
    在匈牙利首都布达佩斯，参加检阅和示威的队伍凯旋走过英雄广场，向匈牙利劳动人民党总书记拉科西致敬。示威者擎着生动的画幅和图表，描绘劳动人民执行五年计划的卓越成就。“保卫和平，打倒战争贩子，英雄的朝鲜人民万岁！各国人民的友谊万岁！”等口号声响彻全场。
  保
    在保加利亚首都索非亚，三十万劳动人民举行了盛况空前的阅兵和示威游行。当浩浩荡荡的队伍走过季米特洛夫陵墓的前面时，受到了保加利亚部长会议主席契尔文科夫的欢迎。示威者高举着的大标语牌，表示了保国人民拥护五大国缔结和平公约和保证提前完成生产计划的意志。
  罗
    罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特劳动人民的示威游行在斯大林广场举行。罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会总书记乔治乌——德治及党与政府的其他首长亲临检阅，受到了群众的热烈欢呼。示威者高举着马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林的巨像，和号召加紧保卫和平的标语牌。当斯大林城拖拉机工厂首次制造的拖拉机出现在游行队伍中时，全场掌声雷动。
  阿
    在阿尔巴尼亚京城地拉那，七万劳动人民举行了五一节示威。阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会主席团主席努什向示威者热烈祝贺。
  蒙
    蒙古人民共和国各地都洋溢着节日的气氛。首都乌兰巴托等地的企业、教育机关和俱乐部都举行了庆祝五一节的群众大会。
