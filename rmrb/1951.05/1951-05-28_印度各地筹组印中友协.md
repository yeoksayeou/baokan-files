Title: Preparations to Form Sino-Indian Friendship Associations in Various Parts of India
Author:
Date: 1951-05-28
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 26th: Following the establishment of Sino-Indian Friendship Associations in Calcutta and Bombay, preparations are underway in various parts of India to establish similar associations. Delhi is preparing to convene a meeting to establish a Sino-Indian Friendship Association. Surat in Bombay Province, Mangalore in Madras Province, Hyderabad State, the Patiala and East Punjab States Union, and many other places are also preparing to form Sino-Indian Friendship Associations.

### 印度各地筹组印中友协

1951-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度各地筹组印中友协
    【新华社新德里二十六日电】继印度加尔各答和孟买二地成立印中友好协会后，印度各地也纷纷准备成立同样的协会。德里正在准备召开成立印中友好协会的会议。孟买省的苏拉特、马德拉斯省的曼加罗尔、海德拉巴邦、巴地阿拉及东旁遮普邦联盟和其他许多地方，也正在筹组印中友好协会。
