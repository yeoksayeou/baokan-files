Title: Democratic Parties Send Congratulatory Messages to the Dalai Lama and Panchen

Author:
Date: 1951-05-30
Page: 1

　　The China Democratic League, the China National Democratic Construction Association, the Chinese Peasants and Workers Democratic Party, and the Jiusan Society sent congratulatory telegrams to the Dalai Lama and Mr. Panchen Erdeni for reaching an agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet. The China Democratic League's telegram stated: "Under the leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao and with the enthusiastic efforts of both of you, the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet has been successfully signed. We firmly believe that both of you will be able to work together harmoniously from now on, enabling the Tibetan people to achieve true unity and ensuring the full implementation of this glorious and great agreement." The telegram from the China National Democratic Construction Association stated: "We believe that under the leadership of Chairman Mao and yourselves, the Tibetan people will rapidly move towards a bright and happy future." The Chinese Peasants and Workers Democratic Party's telegram stated: "We warmly celebrate the Tibetan people's liberation from imperialist aggression, their return to the embrace of the motherland, the unprecedented great unity within the Tibetan people, and the beginning of a new era for the Tibetan people." The Jiusan Society stated in its congratulatory telegram: "We hope that the Tibetan people will further unite and, under the wise leadership of the great leader of all nationalities, Chairman Mao, strive to build a prosperous and happy new Tibet."

### 各民主党派电达赖和班禅致贺

1951-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　各民主党派电达赖和班禅致贺
    【本报讯】中国民主同盟、民主建国会、中国农工民主党、九三学社为和平解放西藏问题取得协议，特电达赖喇嘛及班禅额尔德尼先生致贺。中国民主同盟电称：在伟大领袖毛主席的领导下，在两位先生的热诚努力下，和平解放西藏办法的协议顺利地签订了。我们坚决相信两位先生从此必能和衷共济，使西藏民族得到真正的团结，使这项光荣伟大的协议得到全部的实现。民主建国会的电文中称：“我们相信西藏人民在毛主席和你们领导之下，将会很迅速地走向光明与幸福的前途。”中国农工民主党电称：“我们热烈地为西藏民族和人民脱离帝国主义的侵略，重新回到祖国的怀抱，为西藏民族内部空前的大团结，为西藏民族开始自己的新纪元而庆贺。”九三学社在致敬电中称：“希望西藏人民进一步团结起来，在各民族伟大领袖毛主席英明领导下，为建设繁荣幸福的新西藏而奋斗。”
