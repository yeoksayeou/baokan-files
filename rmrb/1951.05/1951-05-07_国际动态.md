	International News
	The people of Western European countries will hold a congress against the reconstruction of the German Wehrmacht
	Representatives of the committees against the remilitarization of Germany from the Netherlands, Belgium, France, Britain and Luxembourg held a meeting in Brussels. The meeting decided to convene a congress in Paris on May 13th and 14th against the reconstruction of the German Wehrmacht, and will invite people from all Western European countries to send representatives to the congress. The meeting also elected a preparatory committee for the congress.
	The Executive Committee of the International Student Union supports the resolution of the World Peace Council
	The Executive Committee of the International Student Union issued the following resolution on "Resolution of the Berlin Conference of the World Peace Council": "The Executive Committee of the International Student Union has studied the resolutions of the Berlin Conference of the World Peace Council and is deeply convinced that these resolutions are in line with the interests of the people and students in all countries who are resolutely striving for peace. Therefore, the Executive Committee agrees with the resolutions of the Berlin Conference of the World Peace Council and recommends that the Secretariat and national and regional student organizations widely publicize, discuss, and support them."
	Bulgaria protests against provocations by Yugoslav army and air force
	The Bulgarian Ministry of Foreign Affairs presented two notes to the Yugoslav Embassy in Bulgaria on the 4th, protesting against Yugoslav soldiers firing at Bulgarian border outposts three times on May 1st, and Yugoslav aircraft twice intruding into Bulgarian airspace for reconnaissance on April 25th. The Bulgarian government firmly demands that the Yugoslav government take appropriate measures to stop such planned provocative actions.

### 国际动态

1951-05-07
第4版()
专栏：国际动态

　　国际动态
    西欧各国人民将举行反对重建德国国防军代表大会
    荷兰、比利时、法国、英国和卢森堡各国反对德国重新军国主义化委员会的代表在布鲁塞尔举行会议。会议决定五月十三、十四日在巴黎召开反对重建德国国防军的代表大会，将邀请所有西欧国家的人民派遣代表出席大会。会议还选出了大会的筹备委员会。
    国际学联执委会拥护世界和平理事会决议
    国际学生联合会执行委员会发表“关于世界和平理事会柏林会议决议”的决议如下：“国际学联执委会研究了世界和平理事会柏林会议的决议，深信这些决议和一切国家内坚决为和平而努力的人民和学生的利益相符合。因此，执委会同意世界和平理事会柏林会议的决议，并建议书记处和各国的全国性及地区性的学生组织予以广泛宣传、讨论和支持。”
    保抗议南斯拉夫陆空军挑衅
    保加利亚外交部于四日向南斯拉夫驻保加利亚大使馆提出二件照会，分别抗议南斯拉夫士兵于五月一日三次射击保加利亚边防哨，和南斯拉夫飞机于四月二十五日两次侵入保加利亚领空侦察。保加利亚政府坚决要求南斯拉夫政府采取适当措施，以制止这种有计划的挑衅行动。
