Title: Representatives of the Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia United Front Hold Meeting to Draft Action Program
Author:
Date: 1951-05-06
Page: 4

　　Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia United Front Representatives
    Hold Meeting to Draft Action Program
    [Xinhua News Agency, May 5th] According to Vietnam News Agency, the representatives of the national united fronts of Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia officially held a joint meeting of the three countries' representatives on March 11, according to the decision made by the three countries' representatives in November last year regarding the establishment of a joint national united front of the three countries.

    The meeting formally established the organization of the Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia Joint National United Front based on equality, mutual assistance, and mutual respect for national sovereignty. It drafted the common action program of the national united fronts of the three countries and elected the members of the Joint Committee of the three countries, including the authorized representatives of the three countries.

    The meeting also issued a declaration calling on the people of the three countries to unite more closely to strengthen the alliance between the three countries and enable the people's resistance war of the three countries to achieve victory at an early date.

### 越南老挝柬埔寨三国统一阵线代表  举行会议拟制行动纲领

1951-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南老挝柬埔寨三国统一阵线代表
    举行会议拟制行动纲领
    【新华社五日讯】据越南通讯社讯：越南、老挝和柬埔寨的民族统一阵线代表，根据去年十一月三国代表关于成立三国联合民族统一阵线的决定，于三月十一日正式举行了三国代表的联席会议。
    会议正式建立了以平等、互助和互相尊重国家主权为基础的越南、老挝和柬埔寨的联合民族统一阵线组织，拟制了三国民族统一阵线的共同行动纲领，选出了三国联合委员会的委员，其中包括三国的授权代表。
    会议并发表一宣言，号召三国人民更紧密地团结起来，以加强三国之间的联盟，使三国人民的抗战早日获得胜利。
