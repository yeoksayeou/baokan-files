Beijing Anti-U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea Branch Notice

　　This association is scheduled to hold a "Northeast People's Accusation of U.S. Imperialist Aggression and Atrocities, Chinese People's Volunteer Army Reports on Heroic Frontline Battles Meeting" at the Labor Theater of the Labor People's Cultural Palace at 3 pm on the 6th. The live proceedings of the meeting will be broadcast simultaneously by the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station. The people of the city are asked to pay attention to the broadcast.

### 北京市抗美援朝分会通告

1951-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京市抗美援朝分会通告
    本会定于六日下午三时在劳动人民文化宫劳动剧场，举行“东北人民控诉美帝国主义侵略暴行、中国人民志愿军报告前线英勇战斗大会”。大会实况将由北京市人民广播电台同时播出，希全市人民注意收听。
