    At the grand celebration banquet, Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government, before delivering his speech, first raised his glass to celebrate the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet signed between the Central People's Government and the local government of Tibet, to celebrate the great unity of all nationalities in the country, and to celebrate the internal unity of the Tibetan people.
