Title: Lin Boqu Broadcasts in the Soviet Union, Congratulating the Soviet People on Their Brilliant Achievements
Author:
Date: 1951-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 2nd: Lin Boqu, head of the delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to the Soviet May Day celebrations, delivered a radio speech on Moscow Radio on the 1st. On behalf of the Chinese people, Lin Boqu congratulated the Soviet people on the brilliant achievements they have made under the leadership of the Lenin-Stalin Party in the post-war Five-Year Plan. He narrated the profound friendship between the people of the two great nations of China and the Soviet Union since the October Revolution, the achievements of the People's Republic of China since its founding, and the current movement to resist US aggression and aid Korea, which is combined with the defense of world peace. He pointed out that the Chinese people are always standing in the peace and democracy camp headed by the great Soviet Union, and are struggling for lasting world peace and people's democracy.

### 林伯渠在苏广播  祝贺苏联人民的辉煌成就

1951-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　林伯渠在苏广播
    祝贺苏联人民的辉煌成就
    【新华社莫斯科二日电】中苏友好协会参加苏联五一节观礼代表团团长林伯渠，于一日在莫斯科广播电台发表广播讲演。林伯渠代表中国人民祝贺苏联人民在列宁——斯大林党的领导下，在战后五年计划中所获得的辉煌成就。他叙述了十月革命以来中苏两大国人民的深厚友谊、中华人民共和国成立以来的成就和当前与保卫世界和平相结合的抗美援朝运动，并指出中国人民时时刻刻都站在以伟大的苏联为首的和平民主阵营里，为全世界的持久和平与人民民主而斗争。
