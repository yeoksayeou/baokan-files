    Our Great Motherland
    In order to oppose American aggression and defend world peace, our country's heroic soldiers and people unanimously support the resolution of the World Peace Council. This is the East China People's Navy soldiers signing in support of the conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了反对美国侵略，保卫世界和平，我国英勇的战士和人民，一致拥护世界和平理事会的决议。这是华东人民海军的战士们，签名拥护缔结五大国和平公约。
