Title: Agreement on Measures for the Thorough Peaceful Liberation of Tibet: Panchen Sends Telegram to the Dalai Lama Expressing Sincere Unity; Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang, China Association for Promoting Democracy, and China Zhi Gong Party Send Telegrams to the Dalai Lama and Panchen to Express Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1951-05-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 30th:
　　Panchen Erdeni sent a telegram to the Dalai Lama regarding the agreement on measures for the thorough peaceful liberation of Tibet. The text of the telegram is as follows: To the Dalai Buddha:
　　On the occasion of your assumption of power, you responded to the call of Chairman Mao, the great leader of all our nationalities, for the peaceful liberation of Tibet, dispatched your own representatives to negotiate with the central government, and signed the agreement on measures for the peaceful liberation of Tibet. According to this agreement, our Tibetan nationality and all Chinese fraternal nationalities are united unprecedentedly, so that our Tibet will forever get rid of the oppression of imperialism. The religious beliefs and religious undertakings of our Tibetan nationality will be reasonably respected and protected; the lives of our Tibetan nationality and the Tibetan people will be gradually improved and developed, so as to achieve prosperity and happiness. This is a great victory for the monks and lay people of our Tibetan nationality. Panchen is willing to exert his meager efforts and sincerely unite, under the wise leadership of the Central People's Government and Chairman Mao, to assist you and the Tibetan local government to thoroughly implement the agreement and strive for the peaceful liberation of Tibet. I send a special telegram to express my congratulations and sincerely express our respect to you.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Panchen Erdeni May 30, 1951
　　[This newspaper dispatch] The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the China Association for Promoting Democracy, and the China Zhi Gong Party sent telegrams to the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni, supporting and congratulating them on the agreement for the peaceful liberation of Tibet. The congratulatory telegram from the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang expressed congratulations to the Dalai Lama and Panchen for resolutely abandoning their prejudices, uniting in the big family of New China, and breaking away from imperialist aggression and fetters. It also wished: "The Tibetan nationality, with its long history and fine traditions, will regain its glory in the construction of New Democracy." The congratulatory telegram from the China Association for Promoting Democracy stated: "From now on, Tibetan compatriots will forever get rid of imperialist oppression and return to the big family of their great motherland." The congratulatory telegram from the China Zhi Gong Party extended warm congratulations and respect to the Tibetan compatriots who will return to the big family of the great motherland and jointly undertake the great cause of building a new China with all nationalities.

### 为彻底实现和平解放西藏办法的协议  班禅致电达赖表示精诚团结  民革民进致公党电达赖和班禅致贺

1951-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　为彻底实现和平解放西藏办法的协议
    班禅致电达赖表示精诚团结
    民革民进致公党电达赖和班禅致贺
    【新华社三十日讯】班禅额尔德尼为彻底实现和平解放西藏办法的协议致电达赖喇嘛。电文如下：达赖佛：
    在您亲政之日，即响应了我们各民族人民伟大领袖毛主席和平解放西藏的号召，派遣自己的代表来中央谈判，并签订了关于和平解放西藏办法的协议。根据这个协议，我们西藏民族和中国各兄弟民族空前的团结起来，使我们西藏永远脱离帝国主义的压迫。我们西藏民族的宗教信仰和宗教事业得到合理的尊重与保护；我们西藏民族和西藏人民的生活得到逐步提高和发展，从而达到繁荣和幸福的可能。这是我们西藏民族僧俗人民的伟大胜利。班禅愿竭棉薄，精诚团结，在中央人民政府和毛主席的英明领导下，协助您和西藏地方政府，彻底实行协议，为和平解放西藏而奋斗。特电致贺，并衷心地表示我们对您的敬意。
          班禅额尔德尼    一九五一年五月三十日
    【本报讯】中国国民党革命委员会、中国民主促进会、中国致公党致电达赖喇嘛与班禅额尔德尼，拥护和祝贺和平解放西藏的协议。中国国民党革命委员会的贺电，对达赖和班禅毅然决然捐弃成见，团结在新中国的大家庭中，脱离了帝国主义的侵略和羁绊，表示庆贺。并祝：“历史悠久传统优良的西藏民族，在新民主主义的建国中重吐光辉”。中国民主促进会的贺电称：“从此以后，西藏同胞将永远脱离帝国主义的压迫，回到自己伟大祖国的大家庭中来”。中国致公党的贺电，对西藏同胞将回到伟大祖国的大家庭来与各民族共同担负起建设新中国的大业，致以热烈的庆贺和敬意。
