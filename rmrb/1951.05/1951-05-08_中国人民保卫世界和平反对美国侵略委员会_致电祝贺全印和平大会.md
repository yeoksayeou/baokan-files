Title: China Peace Committee Cables Congratulations to the All-India Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1951-05-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th.

The China Peace Committee Cables Congratulations to the All-India Peace Conference

[Xinhua News Agency, 7th] The All-India Peace Conference is being held in Mumbai. The China Peace Committee has sent a telegram today to express congratulations. The telegram is as follows:

To the All-India Peace Conference:

Please accept our warmest congratulations on your conference. American imperialism is invading Korea and China's Taiwan, and is rearming Japan, preparing to expand its war of aggression, and is more seriously threatening peace in Asia and the world. The unity of more than 800 million people in China and India is a powerful force for guaranteeing peace in Asia. Let us mobilize the people of our two countries to unite and wage a resolute struggle against the rearmament of Japan, to promote the conclusion of a peace pact among the five great powers, and to stop imperialist aggression. Peace will surely triumph over war. Long live the unity and friendship of the people of China and India!

China Peace Committee

### 中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会  致电祝贺全印和平大会

1951-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会
    致电祝贺全印和平大会
    【新华社七日讯】全印度和平大会在孟买召开，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会特于今日致电祝贺。电文如下：全印和平大会：
    谨向你们的大会致最热诚的祝贺。美帝国主义侵略朝鲜和中国的台湾，并且正在重新武装日本，准备扩大侵略战争，更严重地威胁亚洲及世界和平。中印两国八亿以上人民的团结，是保障亚洲和平的强大力量。让我们发动我们两国人民团结起来，为反对重新武装日本，为促成五大国和平公约的缔结和制止帝国主义的侵略而进行坚决奋斗。和平必将战胜战争。中印两国人民团结及友谊万岁！
            中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会
