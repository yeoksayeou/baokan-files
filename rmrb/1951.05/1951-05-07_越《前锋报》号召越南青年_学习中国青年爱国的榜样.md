Title: Vietnamese "Pioneer" Newspaper Calls on Vietnamese Youth to Learn from the Patriotic Example of Chinese Youth
Author:
Date: 1951-05-07
Page: 4

　　Vietnamese "Pioneer" Newspaper Calls on Vietnamese Youth
    to Learn from the Patriotic Example of Chinese Youth
    [Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 6th] According to Vietnam News Agency's report from North Vietnam on the 5th: The Vietnamese Youth Salvation Association's newspaper "Pioneer" published an article celebrating China's May Fourth Youth Day. The article stated: The May Fourth Movement demonstrated the patriotic enthusiasm, courage, and self-sacrificing spirit of Chinese youth. This spirit absorbed the majority of Chinese youth into the revolutionary movement, transforming old China into a powerful people's democratic country. It inspired the enthusiasm of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army fighting to resist America and aid Korea to protect their homes and defend their country.
    The article pointed out: The spirit of the May Fourth Movement is a brilliant example for the youth of colonial and dependent countries who are currently fighting for national liberation.
    The article called on Vietnamese youth to learn from the patriotic enthusiasm and persistent fighting spirit of Chinese youth, and to strive for the complete victory of the liberation war against the French colonialists and American interventionists.

### 越《前锋报》号召越南青年  学习中国青年爱国的榜样

1951-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　越《前锋报》号召越南青年
    学习中国青年爱国的榜样
    【新华社六日讯】据越南通讯社北越五日讯：越南青年救国会机关报《前锋报》登载一篇文章，庆祝中国的五四青年节。该文称：五四运动表现了中国青年的爱国热情、勇敢和自我牺牲的精神。这种精神把大部分中国青年吸收到革命运动中，把旧中国变成一个强大的人民民主国家。它鼓舞着为抗美援朝保家卫国而战的中国青年志愿军的热情。
    该文指出：五四运动的精神对于目前正在为争取民族解放而斗争的殖民地和附属国的青年，是一个光辉的榜样。
    该文号召越南青年学习中国青年的爱国热情和坚持斗争的精神，争取抵抗法国殖民者与美国干涉者的解放战争获得完全的胜利。
