Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-05-08
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    This is Li Daoping, a labor model representative attending the National Coal Mine Conference, and an outstanding pneumatic drill operator from Huainan Coal Mine. He uses the Soviet New Method of coal mining, creating a new record of 203 tons of coal mined per person per day, greatly advancing the production competition movement in Huainan Coal Mine. He has now become a glorious member of the Communist Party.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    这是出席全国煤矿会议的劳动模范代表——淮南煤矿的优秀风镐手李道平。他用苏联新法采煤，创造了每人每日采煤二百零三吨的新纪录，使淮南煤矿的生产竞赛运动大大地向前推进了一步。他现在已经成为光荣的共产党员了。
