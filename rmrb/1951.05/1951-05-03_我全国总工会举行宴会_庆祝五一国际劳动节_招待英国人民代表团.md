Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Holds Banquet to Celebrate International Labor Day and Host British People's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1951-05-03
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 1st. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions held a banquet on the evening of April 30th to celebrate International Labor Day and to host the British People's Delegation, headed by York Cain, which participated in the May Day demonstration in our capital. Also invited to the banquet were Mr. Thornton and his wife, representatives of the World Federation of Trade Unions, Mr. Yevstratov, representative of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, and trade union delegations from other brotherly countries. The banquet was hosted by Li Lisan, Zhu Xuefan, and Liu Ningyi, three vice-chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and accompanied by the heads of departments and divisions of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the chairmen of the national committees of various industrial trade unions. During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently rose to toast the unprecedented unity of the working class around the world, the victory of the Korean People's Army and the Korean working class and the Chinese People's Volunteers, the World Federation of Trade Unions, and Stalin, Mao Zedong, and Pollitt. The atmosphere between the hosts and guests was filled with a warm atmosphere of internationalist solidarity from beginning to end. An evening party was held after the banquet.

### 我全国总工会举行宴会  庆祝五一国际劳动节  招待英国人民代表团

1951-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　我全国总工会举行宴会
    庆祝五一国际劳动节  招待英国人民代表团
    【新华社一日讯】中华全国总工会于四月三十日晚举行庆祝“五一”国际劳动节的宴会，并招待以约克·凯恩为首的参加我国首都人民庆祝“五一”劳动节示威大会的英国人民代表团。应邀出席宴会的尚有世界工会联合会代表桑顿先生及其夫人和全苏工会中央理事会代表耶夫斯特拉托夫以及其他兄弟国家的工会代表团。宴会由中华全国总工会李立三、朱学范和刘宁一三位副主席亲自招待，由全国总工会各部、处长和各产业工会全国委员会主席作陪。席间、宾主频频起立为庆祝全世界工人阶级的空前大团结，为朝鲜人民军和朝鲜工人阶级以及中国人民志愿军的胜利，为世界工会联合会，为斯大林、毛泽东、波立特而干杯。宾主间自始至终充满着国际主义的亲密团结的热烈气氛。宴会结束后并举行晚会。
