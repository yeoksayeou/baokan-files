Title: All-China Students' Federation Secretariat Calls on World Students to Expand Signature Campaign for the Peace Pact Declaration
Author:
Date: 1951-05-24
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 20th: The Secretariat of the International Student Union held an enlarged meeting of representatives of national student organizations in Prague. After the meeting, a statement was issued supporting the resolutions adopted by the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council at its Copenhagen meeting from the 5th to the 7th, and the resolution of the Executive Committee of the International Student Union Beijing meeting on "Striving for Peace Between All Countries." It called on world students to widely promote these resolutions, expand the signature campaign for the Peace Pact Declaration, and establish peace committees in various universities and colleges. These committees would exchange experiences with students from other countries by organizing peace rallies and lectures. The statement also called on students around the world to welcome the Third World Youth and Student Peace Festival with new victories in the struggle for peace.

### 国际学联书记处号召世界学生  扩大和平公约宣言签名运动

1951-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际学联书记处号召世界学生
    扩大和平公约宣言签名运动
    【新华社二十二日讯】据塔斯社布拉格二十日讯：国际学生联合会书记处曾在布拉格召开各国全国性学生组织代表扩大会议，会后发表声明，支持世界和平理事会执行局五日至七日在哥本哈根会议上通过的决议，和国际学联执委会北京会议所通过的“争取各国之间的和平”的决议，号召世界学生广泛予以宣传，扩大和平公约宣言的签名运动，并在各高等学校成立和平委员会，通过组织和平集会及讲演会等方式和其他国家的学生交流经验。声明并号召全世界学生在和平斗争中争取新的胜利来迎接第三届世界青年与学生和平联欢节。
