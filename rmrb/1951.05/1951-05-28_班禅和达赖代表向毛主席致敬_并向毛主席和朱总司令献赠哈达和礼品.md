Title: Panchen and Dalai Representatives Pay Respects to Chairman Mao and Present Hada Scarves and Gifts to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu

Author:

Date: 1951-05-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 27th: The Panchen Erdeni, leading the main responsible officials of the Panchen Kampus Assembly Hall, paid respects to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government at 4:00 PM on the 24th. On the same day at 4:30 PM, the negotiating delegation of the Tibetan Local Government, headed by Apé Awang Jigme, representing the Dalai Lama and the Tibetan Local Government, paid respects to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the People's Liberation Army. Both presented Hada scarves and gifts. Those who participated in the two grand ceremonies included Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government; Dong Biwu, Chen Yun, and Huang Yanpei, Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council; Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Li Weihan, Plenipotentiary Representative for Negotiations of the Central People's Government, Peng Zhen, Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, as well as Standing Committee members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, members of the Central People's Government in Beijing, members of the Government Administration Council, heads of various commissions, ministries, committees, academies, administrations, and banks, senior commanders of the People's Liberation Army in Beijing, heads of various departments of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, and responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations, totaling over a hundred people.

The gifts presented by Panchen to Chairman Mao included a red satin brocade banner with the words "The Great Savior of All Ethnic Groups in China" written in both Tibetan and Chinese. There was also a dedication written on yellow satin. There was a gold shield inlaid with "Long Live Chairman Mao," a Tibetan-made longevity bronze Buddha, and a set of Tibetan-made silver mandalas (the highest and most respectful gift offered by the Tibetan people to their supreme leader, shaped like a silver pagoda). In addition, there were over ninety items of precious Tibetan products, including gold silk satin, deer antlers, Tibetan incense, saffron, various animal skins, various pulu fabrics, various clothing items, as well as weapons and ammunition used by Tibetan soldiers fifty years ago to resist British invaders.

The gifts presented by the Dalai Lama and the Tibetan Local Government to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu included a photograph of the Dalai Lama, Tibetan placer gold, Tibetan incense, serge, and pulu fabrics, totaling over thirty items.

### 班禅和达赖代表向毛主席致敬  并向毛主席和朱总司令献赠哈达和礼品

1951-05-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　班禅和达赖代表向毛主席致敬
    并向毛主席和朱总司令献赠哈达和礼品
    【新华社二十七日讯】班禅额尔德尼率领班禅堪布会议厅主要负责官员，于二十四日下午四时，向中央人民政府毛泽东主席致敬。同日下午四时半，以阿沛·阿旺晋美为首的西藏地方政府谈判代表团，代表达赖喇嘛和西藏地方政府，向中央人民政府毛泽东主席和人民解放军朱德总司令致敬。均献赠哈达和礼品。参加两次隆重盛典的，有中央人民政府副主席李济深，政务院副总理董必武、陈云、黄炎培，中国人民政协全国委员会副主席陈叔通，中央人民政府谈判全权代表李维汉等，北京市人民政府市长彭真以及人民政协全国委员会常务委员，中央人民政府在京委员，政务院政务委员，各委、部、会、院、署、行首长，人民解放军在京高级指挥员和人民革命军事委员会各部门首长，各民主党派和各人民团体的负责人等百余人。
    班禅献给毛主席的礼物中，有红缎锦旗一面，上边用藏汉两种文字写着“中国各族人民的大救星”的字样。另有写在黄缎子上的献词一份。有镶嵌“毛主席万岁”的金盾一座、藏制长寿铜佛一尊、藏制银满扎（为西藏人民敬献最高领袖最敬之礼节，状似银塔）一套。此外，尚有西藏珍贵名产金丝缎、鹿茸、藏香、藏红花、各种兽皮、各种氆氇、各种衣饰以及西藏战士五十年前抵抗英国侵略者所用的武器、弹药等九十余件。
    达赖喇嘛和西藏地方政府献给毛主席和朱总司令的礼品中，计有达赖喇嘛的像片、西藏沙金、藏香和哔叽、氆氇等三十余件。
