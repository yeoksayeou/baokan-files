Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-05-06
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The lives of workers in New China are getting happier day by day. This is a group of young coal miners from the Fushun Coal Mine in Northeast China, who, after a day's work, sit by the beautiful Hun River and fish. Defend this happy life, and don't allow the enemy to destroy it!

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新中国的工人，生活一天比一天快乐了。这是东北抚顺煤矿的青年矿工，他们在一天的劳动以后，就坐到美丽的浑河边钓起鱼来了。保卫这幸福的生活，不许敌人来破坏！
