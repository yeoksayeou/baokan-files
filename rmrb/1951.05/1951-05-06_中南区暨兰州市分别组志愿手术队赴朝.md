Title: Central South Region and Lanzhou City Form Volunteer Surgical Teams to Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-05-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Report: The second group of volunteer surgical teams from the Central South Region and the first group of volunteer surgical teams from Lanzhou City departed on April 27th. The Central South Region volunteer surgical team is also carrying a large number of medical devices and valuable medicines. Before the team left Hankou for the north, it was warmly seen off by over ten thousand medical workers in Wuhan. The Lanzhou City volunteer surgical team has a total of thirty-one members, led by Jin Shiyao, associate professor of the Lanzhou University School of Medicine. Before their departure, more than 3,000 representatives from various ethnic groups and all walks of life held a farewell meeting.

### 中南区暨兰州市分别组志愿手术队赴朝

1951-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　中南区暨兰州市分别组志愿手术队赴朝
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中南区第二批志愿赴朝手术队和兰州市首批志愿赴朝手术队，已于四月二十七日出发。中南区志愿手术队，并带着大批医疗器械和贵重药品。该队于离汉口北上前，受到武汉市医务工作者万余人的热烈欢送。兰州市志愿手术队共三十一人，由兰州大学医学院副教授靳士耀任队长。行前，各民族和各界代表三千多人举行了欢送大会。
