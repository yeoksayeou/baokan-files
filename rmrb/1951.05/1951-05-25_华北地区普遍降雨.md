Title: Widespread Rainfall in North China
Author:
Date: 1951-05-25
Page: 1

　　Widespread Rainfall in North China
　　[Our dispatch] Since the evening of the 23rd, Hebei, Chahar, Shanxi, Suiyuan and other places have experienced rainfall. Hebei Province began to experience widespread rainfall on the morning of the 24th. The rain in Handan, Xingtai, Shijiazhuang, Dingxian, Cangxian and other regions has generally soaked the soil. Rainfall began in various parts of Chahar Province on the evening of the 23rd, and the rain had not stopped by the evening of the 24th, with most areas already soaked. Rainfall was less in Shanxi and Suiyuan provinces. Rainfall in the suburbs of Beijing has been about four fingers in depth. This rain will greatly benefit the final completion of spring planting and sowing, cotton seedling emergence, and wheat growth in arid areas.

### 华北地区普遍降雨

1951-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北地区普遍降雨
    【本报讯】河北、察哈尔、山西、绥远等地自二十三日晚起先后下雨。河北全省二十四日早晨开始普遍降雨，邯郸、邢台、石家庄、定县、沧县等专区下的雨，一般已接上湿土。察哈尔省各地在二十三日晚开始下雨，到二十四日晚雨仍未停，大部地区已经湿透。山西、绥远两省下雨较小。北京郊区的雨量已在四指左右。这些雨对干旱地区的最后完成春耕播种、棉苗出土、小麦成长等都有很大好处。
