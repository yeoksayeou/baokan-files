Title: Northwest Ethnic Groups Delegation Returns to Xi'an
Author:
Date: 1951-05-29
Page: 1
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, May 28th: The Northwest Ethnic Groups Delegation, consisting of twenty-five members, returned to Xi'an by train from Beijing on the 27th. Wang Feng, Director of the Nationalities Affairs Committee of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, and more than ten others went to the station to welcome them. Just as the delegation members were getting off the train, the news of the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet Agreement arrived, and they all applauded and celebrated enthusiastically.

### 西北各族参观团返抵西安

1951-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　西北各族参观团返抵西安
    【新华社西安二十八日电】西北各民族参观团一行二十五人，已于二十七日由北京乘车返抵西安。西北军政委员会民族事务委员会主任委员汪锋等十余人赴车站欢迎。当该团全体团员下火车时，和平解放西藏获得协议的消息恰好传来，他们都为此热烈鼓掌欢庆。
