Title: Beijing Youth from All Circles Gather Today to Commemorate "May Fourth"
Author:
Date: 1951-05-04
Page: 1

　　[Our Newspaper] Today is Youth Day and the second anniversary of the founding of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, as well as the 32nd anniversary of the "May Fourth" Movement. Under the joint call of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Youth League, the preparatory committee of the Beijing Municipal Democratic Youth Federation, and the Beijing Municipal Student Union, in addition to actively participating in signing patriotic pacts and voting and signing their names in their respective units, more than 10,000 youth representatives from all walks of life in Beijing will gather at the Working People's Cultural Palace at 8:30 this morning to hold the "Capital Youth Commemorates 'May Fourth' Youth Day, Welcomes the International Student Union Executive Committee Meeting Representatives Conference". At 1:30 in the afternoon, a garden party for more than 70,000 people will be held.
    Beijing's various schools have also decided to hold a bonfire party at the Democracy Square of Peking University tomorrow night, and will invite representatives from 35 countries, led by Grohman, who are participating in the Executive Committee of the International Student Union.
    The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Democratic Youth Federation, and the All-China Student Federation have decided to hold the "Exhibition in Memory of the May Fourth Movement" in Zhongshan Park from May 4th to 14th. The Central Committee of the Youth League will also hold the "Exhibition of Friendship Gifts from the Soviet Union and People's Democratic Countries". The location is the Friendship Hall of the Youth Palace. The time is from May 4th to 11th, from 2:00 PM to 8:00 PM daily.

### 北京市各界青年今天集会纪念“五四”

1951-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京市各界青年今天集会纪念“五四”
    【本报讯】今天是青年节及中国新民主主义青年团成立二周年纪念，又是“五四”运动三十二周年纪念日。在青年团北京市委员会、北京市民主青联筹委会、北京市学生联合会的联合号召下，北京全市青年除积极参加所在单位签订爱国公约、和进行投票、签名外，各界青年代表一万多人将于今天上午八时半在劳动人民文化宫举行“首都青年纪念‘五四’青年节，欢迎国际学联执委会会议代表大会”。下午一时半举行七万多人的游园大会。
    北京市各校并决定于明晚在北京大学民主广场举办营火大会，将邀请以格罗曼为首的参加国际学生联合会执行委员会的三十五个国家的代表参加。
    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国民主青年联合会、中华全国学生联合会，决定于五月四日至十四日在中山公园举行“纪念五四运动展览会”。青年团中央并举办“苏联暨人民民主国家青年友谊礼品展览会”。地点在青年宫交谊厅。时间是五月四日至十一日，每天下午二时至八时。
