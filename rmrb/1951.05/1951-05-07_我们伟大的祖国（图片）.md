Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-05-07
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Recently, people from all walks of life across the country have gathered to vote on the Japanese issue and sign in support of the five major powers concluding a peace treaty. This is a scene of female workers from the Tianjin State-owned Fourth Cotton Textile Factory under the North China Textile Administration Bureau competing to sign their names.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    最近全国各地各界人民，纷纷集会，对日本问题举行投票，并签名拥护五大国缔结和平公约。这是华北纺织管理局所属天津国营第四棉纺织厂的女工们正在争先签名的情形。
