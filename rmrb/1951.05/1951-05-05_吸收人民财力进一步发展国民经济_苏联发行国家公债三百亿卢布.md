Title: Absorbing People's Financial Resources to Further Develop the National Economy: The Soviet Union Issues National Bonds of 30 Billion Rubles
Author:
Date: 1951-05-05
Page: 4

　　Absorbing People's Financial Resources to Further Develop the National Economy
    The Soviet Union Issues National Bonds of 30 Billion Rubles
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 3rd] TASS reported from Moscow on the 3rd: The Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union issued an order regarding the issuance of national bonds worth 30 billion rubles in 1951. The issuance of these bonds is to absorb the financial resources of the people to further develop the national economy and to build the Kuibyshev, Stalingrad and Kakhovka hydroelectric power stations, the Turkmen Main Canal, the South Ukraine Canal and the North Crimean Canal. The bonds will mature in twenty years, and the bonds and lottery winnings will be exempt from national, local and other taxes.

### 吸收人民财力进一步发展国民经济  苏联发行国家公债三百亿卢布

1951-05-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　吸收人民财力进一步发展国民经济
    苏联发行国家公债三百亿卢布
    【新华社四日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科三日讯：苏联部长会议颁布了关于在一九五一年发行国家公债三百亿卢布的命令。发行公债是为了吸收人民的财力，进一步发展国民经济，建造古比雪夫、斯大林格勒和卡霍夫卡水电站，土库曼大运河，南乌克兰运河及北克里米亚运河。公债定期二十年还本，公债券和中签奖金免征国税、地方税及其他捐税。
