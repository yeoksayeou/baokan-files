Title: Chinese People's Delegation to Korea to Arrive in Beijing Today from Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1951-05-29
Page: 1
　　
　　The Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, led by General Leader Liao Chengzhi and Deputy General Leaders Chen Yi and Tian Han, is scheduled to arrive in Beijing from Tianjin this afternoon (29th) by train. People from all walks of life in the capital will hold a grand welcoming ceremony. The Beijing People's Committee to Welcome the Delegation to Korea has been formed a few days ago, and all representatives of the delegation will be invited to report on their visits to Korea and the heroic deeds of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean military and civilians in bravely resisting the enemy. The delegation's itinerary in Beijing has been scheduled: on the 30th, the delegation will hold a meeting of all members to discuss the work of conveying reports; at 7:00 p.m. on the same day, they will attend the cadre report meeting convened by the Beijing People's Committee to Welcome the Delegation to Korea, and the main leaders of the delegation will make their first report at the meeting; from May 31st to June 3rd, the delegation will be divided into several groups to go to factories, schools, institutions, troops, and suburban areas to report to people from all walks of life. As of last night (28th), more than 150 units have requested representatives of the delegation to report to them. On June 2nd, the delegation will participate in the memorial service held by people from all walks of life in the capital in Zhongshan Park for the four martyrs who sacrificed their lives in the delegation to Korea. After the delegation's activities in Beijing come to an end, they will go to all counties and cities across the country to make similar reports.

### 中国人民赴朝慰问团定今日由津来京

1951-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民赴朝慰问团定今日由津来京
    【本报讯】中国人民赴朝慰问团在总团长廖承志和副总团长陈沂、田汉率领下，定于今（二十九）日下午自天津乘车来北京，首都各界人民将举行盛大欢迎会。首都各界人民欢迎赴朝慰问团委员会已于日前组成，届时将请该团全体代表报告赴朝慰问经过和中国人民志愿军及朝鲜军民英勇抗击敌人的英雄事迹。该团在京活动日程现已排定：三十日该团将召开全团人员会议，商讨进行传达报告等工作；当日晚七时出席首都各界人民欢迎赴朝慰问团委员会所召开的干部报告大会，该团主要负责人将在会上作首次传达报告；本月三十一日到六月三日止，该团将分成若干小组，分赴工厂、学校、机关、部队和郊区向各界人民作传达报告。截至昨（二十八）日晚止，已有一百五十多个单位要求慰问团代表向他们作传达报告。六月二日，该团将参加首都各界人民在中山公园举行的追悼赴朝慰问团牺牲四烈士大会。该团在京的活动告一段落后，将分赴全国所有县市作同样的传达报告。
