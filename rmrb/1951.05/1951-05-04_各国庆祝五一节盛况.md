Title: May Day Celebrations Around the World
Author:
Date: 1951-05-04
Page: 4
　　
　　May Day Celebrations Around the World
    Germany
    [News report] According to Xinhua News Agency: All the German people, under the central slogan of opposing the re-militarization of West Germany and demanding the conclusion of a peace treaty with Germany, celebrated this year's May Day with powerful demonstrations against warmongers.
    In Berlin, the capital of the German Democratic Republic, 600,000 working people held grand celebrations and parades on May Day. In addition to workers from various enterprises, members of the Free German Youth League and many housewives in Berlin participated in the parade.
    In West Berlin, peace fighters posted slogans on May Day demanding a popular vote for peace.
    In West Germany, people in Frankfurt, Hamburg, Nuremberg, Helgoland, and other places held demonstrations on May Day, despite the brutal suppression by the occupying authorities and the Bonn "government."
    England
    [News report] According to Xinhua News Agency: On May 1st, the Communist Party of Great Britain held a demonstration and mass rally in Montague Square in central London to celebrate May Day. The venue was decorated with huge portraits of Stalin, Mao Zedong, and Pollitt. Harry Pollitt, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Great Britain, spoke at the meeting, paying tribute to the Korean people who are fighting for independence. He expressed his deep sorrow for the British people's involvement in the unjust Korean War and assured that the Communist Party of Great Britain would do its utmost to withdraw British troops from Korea. Pollitt paid tribute to the Spanish people who are heroically fighting against the Franco regime. Pollitt called on the British working class, small farmers, and shopkeepers to organize a broad alliance to win a parliamentary majority that can establish a people's government and lay the foundation for British socialism.
  Indonesia, Israel, Iran
    [News report] According to Xinhua News Agency: The people of Indonesia, Israel, and Iran held demonstrations to celebrate the "May Day" International Labor Day.
    In Jakarta, the capital of Indonesia, 250,000 workers participated in the demonstration. The marching crowd held up white doves of peace and flags with various slogans, calling for opposition to imperialism and fighting for peace and freedom.
    The people of Tel Aviv, Israel, held a parade on the afternoon of April 30 to celebrate "May Day." Mikunis, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Israel, delivered a speech to the masses on "May Day," advocating that the Israeli Peace Committee immediately launch a signature campaign demanding that the five major powers conclude a peace treaty.
    The working people of Tehran, Iran, also held a demonstration in Parliament Square on May 1 to celebrate "May Day."

### 各国庆祝五一节盛况

1951-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　各国庆祝五一节盛况
    德国
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：全德国人民在反对西德重新军国主义化和要求缔结对德和约的中心口号下，以对战争贩子的强大示威庆祝了今年的五一劳动节。
    在德意志民主共和国首都柏林，六十万劳动人民在五一节举行了盛大的庆祝会和游行示威。参加游行者除了各企业部门的工人以外，还有自由德国青年联盟的盟员及许多柏林的家庭主妇。
    在西柏林，和平战士们于五一节张贴标语，要求举行争取和平的人民投票。
    西德的法兰克福、汉堡、纽伦堡及黑尔郭兰岛等地的人民，在五一节都不顾占领当局与波恩“政府”的残暴镇压，举行了示威游行。
    英国
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：五月一日，英国共产党在伦敦中区蒙塔哥广场举行示威游行和群众集会，庆祝五一节。会场上饰有斯大林、毛泽东和波立特的巨幅画像。英国共产党总书记波立特在会上发言，向正为独立而战的朝鲜人民致敬。他对英国人民之被卷入非正义的朝鲜战争一事深表痛心，并保证英国共产党决尽全力使英国军队撤离朝鲜。波立特向正在对佛朗哥政权作英勇斗争的西班牙人民致敬。波立特号召英国工人阶级与小农和店主们组织广泛的联盟，以赢得能够建立人民政府及为英国社会主义奠定基础的议会多数。
  印尼、以色列、伊朗
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：印尼、以色列和伊朗的人民都举行示威游行，庆祝“五一”国际劳动节。
    在印尼首都雅加达，有二十五万工人参加示威游行。游行队伍高举着和平白鸽及写着各种口号的旗帜，号召反对帝国主义，为争取和平与自由而斗争。
    以色列特拉维夫的人民在四月三十日下午举行庆祝“五一”劳动节的游行。以色列共产党总书记米库尼斯在“五一”节向广大群众发表演说，主张以色列和平委员会立即发起签名运动，要求五大国缔结和平公约。
    伊朗德黑兰的劳动人民也于五月一日在议会广场上举行示威游行，庆祝“五一”节。
