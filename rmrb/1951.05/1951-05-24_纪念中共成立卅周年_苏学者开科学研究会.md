Title: Soviet Scholars Hold Scientific Research Conference to Commemorate the Thirtieth Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1951-05-24
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Leningrad, May 23rd: To commemorate the thirtieth anniversary of the founding of the Chinese Communist Party on July 1st, Leningrad University held a scientific research conference, attended by historians and orientalists. Yefimov, a graduate in history and the main lecturer of the history of Far Eastern countries, delivered the opening speech, in which he described the heroic path traversed by the Chinese Communist Party over the past thirty years. Scientific worker Berezoy delivered a report on "The Revolutionary Army in the Chinese Revolution," quoting many valuable historical materials. The agenda of the meeting also included the following reports: "Proletarian Leadership in the National Liberation Movement," "The Struggle of the People of Southeast Asia Against American Imperialism—the Most Vicious Enemy of the Asian People," and others.
    The Oriental Studies Department of Leningrad University is preparing to hold an exhibition to commemorate "July 1st" and has collected a large number of books on Chinese history, literature, and economics to be presented to scientific institutions of the People's Republic of China as a holiday gift.

### 纪念中共成立卅周年  苏学者开科学研究会

1951-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　纪念中共成立卅周年
    苏学者开科学研究会
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社列宁格勒二十二日讯：为纪念七月一日中国共产党成立三十周年，列宁格勒大学特举行了科学研究会议，参加的有历史学家和东方学家多人。历史学学士、远东各国历史讲座主讲人叶菲莫夫致开幕词，他叙述了中国共产党三十年来所经历的英勇道路。科学工作者贝列佐伊发表了关于《中国革命中的革命军》的报告，引用了许多珍贵的历史材料。会议议程中还有下列报告：《民族解放运动中的无产阶级领导权》，《东南亚人民反对美帝国主义——亚洲人民最恶毒的敌人的斗争》等。
    列宁格勒大学东方系准备举行展览会来纪念“七一”，并已收集了大批关于中国历史、文学和经济的书籍，赠给中华人民共和国的科学机关作为节日礼物。
