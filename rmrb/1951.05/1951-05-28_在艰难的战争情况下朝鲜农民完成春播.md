Title: North Korean Farmers Complete Spring Planting Under Difficult War Conditions
Author:
Date: 1951-05-28
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, May 24th (TASS): Despite the difficult war environment and the barbaric attacks by American aircraft, North Korean farmers have still made great achievements in spring planting. The planting plan for all crops in the North Korean rear, except for beans, has been completed by 100.5%. The rice paddy planting plan has been completed by 106.1%. North Hamgyong Province and South Pyongan Province have achieved the best results in the spring planting campaign.

### 在艰难的战争情况下朝鲜农民完成春播

1951-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　在艰难的战争情况下朝鲜农民完成春播
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社平壤二十四日讯：在困难的战争环境和美国飞机野蛮袭击的情况下，朝鲜农民在春季播种方面，仍然做出了巨大的成绩。朝鲜后方除豆类以外，一切农作物的播种计划已完成百分之一百点五。稻田播种计划完成了百分之一百零六点一。咸镜北道和平安南道在春播运动中成绩最好。
