    Our Great Motherland
    The former Taimiao (Imperial Ancestral Temple) in Beijing has now been transformed into the Cultural Palace of the Working People. This is a corner of its library reading room. Many workers go there to read books and newspapers every day.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京的太庙旧址现在改成了劳动人民文化宫。这是其中的图书阅览室的一角。每天有许多劳动者前往阅读书报。
