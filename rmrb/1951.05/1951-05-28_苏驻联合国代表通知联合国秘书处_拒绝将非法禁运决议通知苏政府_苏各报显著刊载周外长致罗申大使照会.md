Title: Soviet Representative to the UN Informs UN Secretariat of Refusal to Notify Soviet Government of Illegal Embargo Resolution; Soviet Newspapers Prominently Publish Foreign Minister Zhou's Note to Ambassador Roshchin
Author:
Date: 1951-05-28
Page: 4

　　TASS, New York, May 26th: On May 23rd, the Soviet Representative to the United Nations sent a reply to the United Nations Secretariat regarding their telegram of May 21st, 1951, requesting the Soviet Representative to notify the Soviet Government of the General Assembly's resolution of May 18th, 1951 (i.e., the illegal resolution to impose an embargo on the People's Republic of China and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea).

　　The reply stated: "Because the aforementioned resolution, passed under the activities of the aggressive bloc in the United States and the United Nations organization, is illegal (the Soviet representative has already made a statement on this in the First Committee and the plenary session of the General Assembly), the Soviet representative believes that it is impossible to notify the Soviet Government of this illegal resolution which violates the United Nations Charter.

　　For the aforementioned reasons, the Soviet Representative to the United Nations returned the telegram and requested the United Nations Secretariat to publish this letter as a General Assembly document."

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 27th: Today, all Moscow newspapers prominently published on half a page the note from Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, to Soviet Ambassador to China, Roshchin.

### 苏驻联合国代表通知联合国秘书处  拒绝将非法禁运决议通知苏政府  苏各报显著刊载周外长致罗申大使照会

1951-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏驻联合国代表通知联合国秘书处
    拒绝将非法禁运决议通知苏政府
    苏各报显著刊载周外长致罗申大使照会
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社纽约二十六日讯：五月二十三日，苏联驻联合国代表就联合国秘书处一九五一年五月二十一日请苏联代表把大会一九五一年五月十八日的决议（按即对中华人民共和国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国实行禁运的非法决议——）通知苏联政府的电报，复函联合国秘书处。
    该函称：“因为在美国及联合国组织中的侵略集团的活动下通过的上述决议是非法的（苏联代表已在第一委员会及大会全体会议上就此发表过声明），所以苏联代表认为：要把这个违反联合国宪章的非法决议通知苏联政府，是不可能的。
    苏联驻联合国的代表由于上述原因把电报退回，并要求联合国秘书处把本函作为大会文件公布。”
    【新华社莫斯科二十七日电】今日莫斯科各报均在显著地位以半版的篇幅刊载中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长二十二日致苏联驻华大使罗申的照会。
