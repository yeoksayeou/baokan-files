Title: World Peace Council Executive Bureau Holds Meeting; Will Discuss Campaign to Promote the Five Great Powers Concluding a Peace Pact

Author:
Date: 1951-05-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, 7th. TASS reports: The Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council held a meeting on the 5th in Copenhagen, the capital of Denmark. The Executive Bureau members and specially invited guests attending the meeting were: D'Arboussier (Africa), Professor Bernal, Mrs. Davis (England), Farge, Desjardins, Got, Alleaume (France), Ehrenburg, Korneichuk, Guryanov (USSR), Xiao San, Li Dequan (China), Edward, Kosambi (India), Reverend Dahl (USA), Sereni, Finocchiaro (Italy), Infeld (Poland), Hrumadka (Czechoslovakia), Iversen (Finland), Blume (Belgium), Herrmann, Fog (Denmark), Boussahma (Arabia), Mrs. Street (Australia). The meeting issued a communique stating: "The Executive Bureau meeting will discuss the campaign to promote the conclusion of a peace pact by the five great powers; take necessary measures to intensify the peace pact signature campaign to eliminate the growing threat of a new war; and study the results of negotiations with representatives of various international organizations concerned with maintaining peace." The communique pointed out: "In the face of difficulties hindering agreement among the great powers, the movement to strive for a peace pact by the five great powers will enable the people of all countries to express their strong desire for peace."

### 世界和平理事会执行局举行会议  将讨论推进五大国缔结和平公约的运动

1951-05-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界和平理事会执行局举行会议
    将讨论推进五大国缔结和平公约的运动
    【新华社七日讯】据塔斯社哥本哈根讯：世界和平理事会执行局于五日在丹麦首都哥本哈根举行会议。出席会议的执行局委员及特邀人士有：达波赛（非洲），贝尔纳教授、戴维斯夫人（英），法奇、德香布兰、哥德、阿勒（法），爱伦堡、考涅楚克、顾里亚耶夫（苏），萧三、李德全（中），爱德华、科桑比（印度），达尔牧师（美），塞伦尼、芬诺尔塔（意），英费尔德（波），赫鲁玛德卡（捷克），伊维尔逊（芬兰），布伦姆（比利时），何尔曼、佛格（丹麦），布夏马（阿拉伯），斯崔特夫人（澳洲）。会议发表公报称：“执行局会议将讨论推进五大国缔结和平公约的运动；采取必要的措施来加紧进行和平公约的签名运动以消除日益增长的新战争威胁；研究与各个关心维护和平的国际组织的代表进行谈判的结果。”公报指出：“在面临着阻挠大国达成协议的困难的情况下，争取五大国缔结和平公约的运动将使各国人民能够表示他们争取和平的强烈愿望。”
