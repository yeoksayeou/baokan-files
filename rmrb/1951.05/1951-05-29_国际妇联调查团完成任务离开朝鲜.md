Title: International Women's Federation Investigation Group Completes Mission and Leaves North Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 28th. The investigation group sent to Korea by the Women's International Democratic Federation to investigate the atrocities of the American military, after completing their investigation mission in various parts of Korea in groups, left Korea on the 22nd.

The Polish People's Peace Delegation, which came to Korea to express their condolences to the heroic Korean military and civilians fighting the war, stayed in Korea for eight days and has also completed its mission and left for home.

### 国际妇联调查团完成任务离开朝鲜

1951-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际妇联调查团完成任务离开朝鲜
    【新华社平壤二十八日电】国际民主妇女联合会派往朝鲜调查美军暴行的调查团，在朝鲜各地分组完成调查任务后，已于二十二日离开朝鲜。
    来朝鲜慰问英勇抗战的朝鲜军民的波兰人民和平代表团，在朝鲜停留八天，也已经完成任务，离此回国。
