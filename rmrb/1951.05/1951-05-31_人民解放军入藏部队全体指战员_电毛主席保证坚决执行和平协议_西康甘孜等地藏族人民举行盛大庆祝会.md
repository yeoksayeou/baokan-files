Title: People's Liberation Army Troops Entering Tibet Send Telegram to Chairman Mao Guaranteeing Resolute Implementation of Peaceful Agreement; Tibetan People in Ganzi, Xikang and Other Places Hold Grand Celebration Meetings
Author:
Date: 1951-05-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, May 30th: When the news of the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet reached the People's Liberation Army troops entering Tibet, commanders and fighters all cheered enthusiastically. On the evening of the 27th, the propaganda department of a certain unit organized commanders, fighters, and local people to listen to the broadcast. When residents and commanders and fighters heard the loudspeaker broadcasting Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu's speeches celebrating the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet, the crowds gathered in the square and on the rooftops cheered and applauded excitedly. The commanders and fighters unanimously stated that they would better unite with the Tibetan people, carry forward the spirit of hard struggle, consolidate the frontier of the motherland, and give the imperialist aggressive forces an even heavier blow. On the 28th, at the celebration meeting of the directly subordinate units of the headquarters of the unit, the leaders of the unit called on all commanders and fighters to resolutely implement and carry out the various provisions of the agreement, which immediately received the response of the commanders and fighters present. The political department of the unit also issued instructions on the same day to celebrate the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet, requiring the principles and various provisions of the agreement to be implemented among all commanders and fighters of the troops entering Tibet.

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, May 30th: All commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army troops entering Tibet sent a telegram to Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government on the 28th, supporting the signing of the agreement on measures for the peaceful liberation of Tibet. The text of the telegram is as follows: Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government:

	When we, all the commanders and fighters of the troops entering Tibet, heard the news of the signing of the agreement between the Central People's Government and the Tibetan local government on measures for the peaceful liberation of Tibet, we were overjoyed, cheered enthusiastically, and unanimously expressed that, under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government, we will fully prepare for entering Tibet, strengthen the study of ethnic policies, do a good job in ethnic work, strictly abide by discipline, respect the customs and habits and freedom of religious belief of the Tibetan people, better unite with the Tibetan compatriots, and are determined to guarantee the full realization of this great agreement, build a new Tibet, and strive to consolidate national defense!

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, May 30th: The Tibetan people in Ganzi and other places in Xikang Province are welcoming the victorious news of the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet with great joy. People of all strata of Tibetan society said in unison: "The Tibetan people have stood up from now on." They expressed their gratitude to Chairman Mao for his concern and love for the Tibetan people.

On the 28th, Ganzi, an important town in northern Xikang, was filled with five-star red flags. Tibetan people danced the Guozhuang dance in the streets, and the Lama temples celebrated by chanting scriptures and blowing large horns. At 4 p.m., monks, laity, and the garrison in Ganzi held a grand celebration meeting. The Lamas of Ganzi Temple marched in formation, beating drums and holding up large flags to participate in the meeting. Many Tibetan men and women held up small paper flags with Chinese characters to participate in the meeting. Kongsa Yiduo, Deputy Director of the Culture and Education Department of the Tibetan Autonomous Region People's Government of Xikang Province, Gongge Jiangze, County Mayor of Ganzi County People's Government, and Zhongsa Living Buddha of Ganzi Temple spoke successively, unanimously expressing that they would always follow Chairman Mao, unite together, thoroughly drive the imperialist aggressive forces out of Tibet, and build a happy and prosperous new Kangzang. Kongsa Yiduo also conveyed the contents of the seventeen-article agreement to the Tibetan people present in Tibetan. The meeting passed a congratulatory telegram to Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government. The telegram briefly stated: "At today's celebration meeting held by the people of all circles in Ganzi, we unanimously express to you: We wholeheartedly support this agreement, which conforms to the interests of all Chinese people, especially the Tibetan people. We are willing to unite all the Tibetan people, dedicate all our strength, and strive for the complete realization of this agreement. We believe that under the correct spirit of this agreement, the Tibetan people can obtain the help of all brotherly ethnic groups in the country and, under the leadership of Chairman Mao, build a prosperous homeland and a beautiful life. We would like to express to you that the imperialist aggressors and any behavior that dares to obstruct and sabotage the realization of this agreement will be resolutely opposed by us, and we also have the strength to completely crush it."

### 人民解放军入藏部队全体指战员  电毛主席保证坚决执行和平协议  西康甘孜等地藏族人民举行盛大庆祝会

1951-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民解放军入藏部队全体指战员
    电毛主席保证坚决执行和平协议
    西康甘孜等地藏族人民举行盛大庆祝会
    【新华社重庆三十日电】当和平解放西藏问题取得协议的消息传到人民解放军入藏部队时，指挥员战斗员一致热烈欢呼。二十七日晚上，某部宣传部门组织指挥员战斗员和当地人民收听广播。当驻地居民与指挥员战斗员们听到扩音器播送出毛主席朱总司令庆祝和平解放西藏办法的协议的讲话时，涌集在广场上、房顶上的人群兴奋地欢呼鼓掌。指挥员战斗员们一致表示：要更好地团结藏族人民，发扬艰苦奋斗的精神，巩固好祖国边疆，予帝国主义侵略势力以更沉重的打击。二十八日，在该部司令部直属队的庆祝大会上，部队首长号召全体指挥员战斗员坚决执行和贯彻协议的各项规定，立即获得到会指挥员战斗员的响应。该部政治部也在当日发出庆祝和平解放西藏问题协议的指示，要求把协议的原则和各项规定贯彻到入藏部队的全体指挥员战斗员中去。
    【新华社重庆三十日电】中国人民解放军入藏部队全体指挥员战斗员于二十八日致电毛主席、中央人民政府，拥护和平解放西藏办法协议签字。电文如下：毛主席并中央人民政府：
    我们入藏部队全体指挥员战斗员，当听到中央人民政府和西藏地方政府关于和平解放西藏办法的协议签字消息后，欢腾鼓舞，热烈庆祝，并一致表示，在毛主席和中央人民政府领导下，充分做好入藏的一切准备工作，并加强学习民族政策，做好民族工作，严格遵守纪律，尊重西藏人民风俗习惯和宗教信仰的自由，更好地团结藏族同胞，决心为保证全部实现这一伟大的协议，建设新西藏，巩固国防而奋斗！
    【新华社重庆三十日电】西康省甘孜等地藏族人民万分欢欣地迎接和平解放西藏问题取得协议的胜利消息。各阶层藏族人民异口同声地说：“西藏人民从此都站起来了。”他们纷纷感谢毛主席对藏族人民的关怀与爱护。
    二十八日西康北部重镇甘孜飘满了五星国旗，藏族人民在街道上欢跳锅庄舞，喇嘛寺都以念经和吹大号来庆祝。下午四时，甘孜的僧俗人民和驻军召开了盛大的庆祝会。甘孜寺喇嘛整队敲着鼓举着大旗来参加大会。许多藏族男女都举起了汉族文字的小纸旗参加大会。西康省藏族自治区人民政府文教处副处长孔萨以多、甘孜县人民政府县长贡格降泽、甘孜寺仲萨活佛都相继讲话，一致表示要永远跟着毛主席走，团结起来，把帝国主义侵略势力彻底赶出西藏去，建设幸福繁荣的新康藏。孔萨以多并把十七条协议内容用藏语传达给到会藏民。会上通过了致毛主席、中央人民政府致敬电。电文略称：“在我们甘孜各界人民今天举行的庆祝会上，一致向你表示：我们衷心拥护这个符合于全中华人民特别是西藏人民利益的协议。我们愿全西藏人民团结一致，供献一切力量，为完全实现这一协议而努力。我们相信在这一协议的正确精神下，藏族人民能取得全国各兄弟民族的帮助，在毛主席领导下，建设繁荣的家园与美好的生活。我们愿向你表示，帝国主义侵略者和一切敢于阻挠破坏实现这个协议的任何行为，将要受到我们坚决的反对，我们也有力量去给予彻底的粉碎。
