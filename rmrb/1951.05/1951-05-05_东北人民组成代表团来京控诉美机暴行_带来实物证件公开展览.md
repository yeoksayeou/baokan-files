Title: Northeast People's Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Accuse U.S. Aircraft of Atrocities; Exhibits Material Evidence Publicly
Author:
Date: 1951-05-05
Page: 1

　　The Northeast People's Delegation to Accuse U.S. Imperialist Aircraft of Atrocities has arrived in Beijing on April 29th. The delegation is composed of victims of the U.S. aircraft bombings in Andong, Linjiang and other places, relatives of the victims, model rescuers and other staff. The material evidence they brought includes U.S. shrapnel, blood-stained clothes of the victims, various photographs, etc., and has been on public display at the Cultural Palace of the Working People since May 4th. Also on display are photographs of the Chinese People's Volunteers fighting on the Korean front. They have also decided to make accusations at the "Northeast People's Accusation of U.S. Imperialist Aircraft Atrocities and Report on the Combat Situation of Our People's Volunteers in Korea" meeting sponsored by the Beijing Anti-American and Aid Korea Branch on May 6th.

### 东北人民组成代表团来京控诉美机暴行  带来实物证件公开展览

1951-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北人民组成代表团来京控诉美机暴行
    带来实物证件公开展览
    【本报讯】东北人民控诉美帝国主义飞机暴行代表团已经在四月二十九日到达北京。该代表团是由在美国飞机轰炸安东、临江等地时的被难人员、被难人员家属、抢救模范和其他工作人员所组成的。他们带来的实物证件有美国炸弹片、被难者的血衣、各种照片等，已经从五月四日起在劳动人民文化宫公开展览，同时展览的还有中国人民志愿军在朝鲜前线作战情况的照片。他们并已决定于五月六日在北京市抗美援朝分会主办的“东北人民控诉美帝国主义飞机暴行、我人民志愿军在朝鲜作战情况报告大会”上进行控诉。
