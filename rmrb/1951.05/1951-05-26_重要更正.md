　　Important Correction
    Yesterday (May 25th), in the news item "Foreign Minister Zhou's Note to the Soviet Ambassador" published on the first page of this newspaper, some newspapers mistakenly placed the fourth and fifth paragraphs of the original text of the note as the second and third paragraphs. That is, the paragraphs "The Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China pointed out in its declaration of December 4, 1950..." and "Since September 18, 1931..." should be after the paragraph "(1) On the procedural issues of preparing the peace treaty with Japan...".

### 重要更正

1951-05-26

专栏：

　　重要更正
    昨日（二十五日）本报第一版所载《周外长照会苏大使》消息，有部分报纸把照会原文第四、第五两段，误植为第二、第三两段。即“中华人民共和国中央人民政府在其一九五零年十二月四日的声明中即已指出……”及“自一九三一年九月十八日以来……”两段，应该是在“（一）在准备对日和约的程序问题上……”一段之后。
