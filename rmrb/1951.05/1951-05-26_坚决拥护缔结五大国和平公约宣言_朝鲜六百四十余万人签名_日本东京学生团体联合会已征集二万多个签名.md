Title: Resolutely Support the Declaration for the Conclusion of a Peace Pact Among the Five Great Powers
Author:
Date: 1951-05-26
Page: 4

　　Resolutely Support the Declaration for the Conclusion of a Peace Pact Among the Five Great Powers
    Over Six Million Four Hundred Forty Thousand Signatures in Korea
    The Tokyo Student Union in Japan Has Already Collected Over Twenty Thousand Signatures
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 25th] The Korean people continue to enthusiastically sign the Declaration for a Peace Pact. As of the 20th, the number of signatories has reached 6,414,758. Among them, South Pyongan Province has 1,067,219 people, and Pyongyang City has 162,265 people.
    At the same time, the Korean military and civilians are enthusiastically launching a campaign to donate funds for weapons. As of the 18th, the Korean Fatherland Support Association has received 339,751,423 won in cash, 45,193 bags of grain (each bag containing ten small dou), and tens of thousands of other valuables donated by military and civilians across the country.
    [Xinhua News Agency] TASS, 22nd: According to the newspaper *Japanese Student News*, the Tokyo Student Union announced that it has collected more than 20,000 signatures demanding that the five great powers conclude a peace pact.
    Students from Okayama University actively participated in the signature campaign. According to the United Press, students from Kyoto University held a meeting to defend peace on May 12th.

### 坚决拥护缔结五大国和平公约宣言  朝鲜六百四十余万人签名  日本东京学生团体联合会已征集二万多个签名

1951-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　坚决拥护缔结五大国和平公约宣言
    朝鲜六百四十余万人签名
    日本东京学生团体联合会已征集二万多个签名
    【新华社平壤二十五日电】朝鲜人民继续在和平公约宣言上踊跃签名。到二十日为止，签名者已达六百四十一万四千七百五十八人。其中，平安南道有一百零六万七千二百十九人，平壤市有十六万二千二百六十五人。
    与此同时，朝鲜军民正热烈展开捐献武器基金运动。到十八日为止，朝鲜祖国后援会已收到全国各地军民捐献的现金三亿三千九百七十五万一千四百二十三元，粮食四万五千一百九十三包（每包合十小斗）和其他贵重物品数万件。
    【新华社讯】塔斯社二十二日讯：《日本学生新闻》报消息，东京学生团体联合会宣布，该会已经征集了二万多个要求五大国缔结和平公约的签名。
    冈山大学的学生曾积极参加签名运动。据联合通讯社称，京都大学的学生曾于五月十二日举行保卫和平大会。
