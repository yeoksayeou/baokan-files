Title: National Basketball and Volleyball Tournament Opens in Beijing; Vice Chairmen Zhu De and Li Jishen Attend and Watch

Author:
Date: 1951-05-06
Page: 3

　　National Basketball and Volleyball Tournament Opens in Beijing
　　Vice Chairmen Zhu De and Li Jishen Attend and Watch

　　The 1951 National Basketball and Volleyball Tournament officially opened at the Beijing Public Stadium at 2:00 PM on the 4th. This is the first national sports event since the birth of New China. The purpose of this tournament is to further popularize people's sports for national defense and production, gradually making it a regular mass activity; and to select teams to participate in the 3rd World Youth Festival to be held in Berlin in August.

　　Attending the ceremony were Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De and Li Jishen, as well as leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and various ministries of the Central People's Government, and leaders of people's organizations such as Nie Rongzhen, Xiao Hua, Luo Ruiqing, Wu Xiuquan, Lü Zhengcao, Wei Que, Cai Chang, Deng Yingchao, Feng Wenbin, and Xie Bangding. The Panchen Erdeni also attended the ceremony. In addition, the entire delegation of the Executive Committee of the International Union of Students, led by Gromman, and other international friends were present. There were more than 12,000 spectators.

　　The opening ceremony began with a solemn march. Participating in this competition were 593 basketball and volleyball team members from eight units: the People's Liberation Army, Railways, Northeast, North China, Northwest, East China, Central South, and Southwest. After the start of the conference, all the team members, led by the Chinese People's Liberation Army delegation, carried a giant portrait of Chairman Mao, marching around the field with vigorous steps.

　　The conference announced that Feng Wenbin, Cai Chang, Xiao Hua, Liao Chengzhi, Gao Tang, Ma John, and others were the 18-member presidium of the conference. Feng Wenbin delivered the opening speech. He said that this conference is of great significance for promoting the development of national sports. He encouraged everyone to intensify physical exercise and prepare to serve the motherland at any time. Gromman, Chairman of the International Union of Students, paid tribute to all basketball and volleyball players and wished them victory. He presented a banner and gifts to the conference on behalf of the International Union of Students. The conference also adopted a congratulatory telegram to Chairman Mao and the Chinese People's Volunteers.

　　The opening ceremony of the conference ended at 3:30 PM, and the competition began immediately. The conference competition is scheduled for ten days and will close on the 14th.
　　　　　　　　(Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国篮排球比赛大会在京揭幕  朱德、李济深副主席等到场观礼

1951-05-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　全国篮排球比赛大会在京揭幕
    朱德、李济深副主席等到场观礼
    一九五一年全国篮排球比赛大会已于四日下午二时在北京市公共体育场正式开幕。这是新中国诞生后第一次全国性的运动会。这次大会的目的是要进一步普及为国防和生产服务的人民体育运动，使之逐渐成为经常的群众性活动；并选拔参加将于八月份在柏林举行的第三届世界青年联欢节的出国球队。
    到会观礼的有中央人民政府副主席朱德、李济深，以及中国人民解放军和中央人民政府各部会的首长，各人民团体的负责人聂荣臻、萧华、罗瑞卿、伍修权、吕正操、韦悫、蔡畅、邓颖超、冯文彬、谢邦定等。班禅额尔德尼先生亦到会观礼。此外还到有以格罗曼为首的国际学联执委会全体代表与其他国际友人。到会观众共有一万二千余人。
    大会开幕式在庄严的进行曲声中开始。参加这次比赛的有人民解放军、铁路、东北、华北、西北、华东、中南、西南等八个单位的篮、排球代表队队员五百九十三人。大会开始后，全体队员以中国人民解放军代表队为前导，高抬着毛主席的巨像，踏着健壮的步伐，绕场一周。
    大会宣布以冯文彬、蔡畅、萧华、廖承志、高棠、马约翰等十八人为大会主席团。大会由冯文彬致开幕词。他说：这次大会，对于推动全国体育运动的发展有重大的意义。他鼓励大家加紧锻炼身体，准备随时为祖国服务。国际学联主席格罗曼向全体篮、排球队员致敬，并预祝他们胜利，他代表国际学联向大会赠送锦旗与礼物。大会并通过向毛主席和中国人民志愿军致敬电。
    大会开幕式于三时半结束，随即开始比赛。大会比赛日期预定十天，将于十四日闭幕。
                （新华社）
