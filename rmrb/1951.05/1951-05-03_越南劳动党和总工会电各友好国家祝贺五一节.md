Title: Vietnamese Workers' Party and General Confederation of Labour Send Greetings to Friendly Countries on May Day

Author: 
Date: 1951-05-03
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 2nd: The Vietnam News Agency reported on April 30th that the Central Executive Committee of the Vietnamese Workers' Party and the Council of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labour jointly sent telegrams to the Communist Parties, workers and trade unions of friendly countries, as well as the World Federation of Trade Unions, to congratulate them on May Day. The telegram stated: "On the occasion of May Day, we extend our brotherly greetings to you; we pledge to resolutely fight for national independence and democracy, for a life of freedom and happiness. We also pledge to actively participate in the struggle to defend world peace and democracy.
    Uniting and fighting together under the leadership of the Soviet Union, we are confident that we will win the final victory and defeat all imperialist warmongers."

### 越南劳动党和总工会电各友好国家祝贺五一节

1951-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南劳动党和总工会电各友好国家祝贺五一节
    【新华社二日讯】越南通讯社四月三十日讯：越南劳动党中央执行委员会和越南总工会理事会，联名致电各友好国家的共产党、工人和工会以及世界工会联合会，祝贺五一劳动节。电文称：“欣逢五一节，谨向你们致兄弟的敬意；我们保证要为民族独立和民主，为自由和幸福的生活而坚决斗争。我们也保证积极参加保卫世界和平和民主的斗争。
    在苏联的领导下团结起来共同奋斗，我们确信必能赢得最后胜利，并击败一切帝国主义战争贩子。”
