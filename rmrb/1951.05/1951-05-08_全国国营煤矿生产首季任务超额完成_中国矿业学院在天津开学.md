Title: National State-Owned Coal Mines Exceed First Quarter Production Target; China Mining Institute Opens in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1951-05-08
Page: 1

　　National State-Owned Coal Mines Exceed First Quarter Production Target
    China Mining Institute Opens in Tianjin
    [Xinhua News Agency, 7th] National state-owned coal mines have exceeded their first quarter production target for this year by 5.97 percent. During this period, the highest output of pneumatic pick coal mining (eight hours) increased from 146 tons at the Huainan Coal Mine to 254.4 tons at the Fengfeng Coal Mine. Safety work has also made great progress, with the average worker mortality rate in various mines decreasing by 70 percent compared to the same period last year.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 7th] China's first China Mining Institute for training senior mining technical personnel officially opened in Tianjin on April 26. The institute, formerly the Jiaozuo Institute of Technology in Pingyuan Province, now has four departments: coal mining, machinery, electrical engineering, and civil engineering, as well as a "Factory and Mine Cadre Training Class," with a total of 400 students. Chen Yu, Minister of the Central Ministry of Fuel Industry, concurrently serves as the president of the institute.

### 全国国营煤矿生产首季任务超额完成  中国矿业学院在天津开学

1951-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国国营煤矿生产首季任务超额完成
    中国矿业学院在天津开学
    【新华社七日讯】全国国营煤矿已超额百分之五点九七完成了今年第一季度的生产任务。在此期间，风镐采煤最高产量（八小时）由淮南煤矿的一百四十六吨提高到峰峰煤矿的二百五十四点四吨。保安工作也有很大进步，各矿平均工人死亡率比去年同一时期减少了百分之七十。
    【新华社天津七日电】中国第一个培养矿业高级技术人才的中国矿业学院已于四月二十六日正式在天津开学。该院前身为平原省焦作工学院，现设采煤、机械、电机、土木四系和一个“厂矿干部专修班”，共有学生四百人。中央燃料工业部部长陈郁兼任该院院长。
