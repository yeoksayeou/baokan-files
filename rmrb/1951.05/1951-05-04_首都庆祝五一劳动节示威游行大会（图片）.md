Author: Wu Benli Chen Zhengqing Zheng Jingkang Yuan Ling Liu Yousheng Lin Yang Zhang Li

Page: 6

　　Capital Celebrates May Day Labor Day Demonstration Parade

　　Chairman Mao and other leaders of the Central People's Government review the parade on the rostrum of Tiananmen.

　　The glorious Chinese People's Liberation Army troops guarding the capital pass through Tiananmen People's Square.

　　Young Pioneers representatives from Beijing present flowers to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government as a tribute.

　　Banchen Erdini, who was invited to watch the ceremony, presents a Hada to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government on Tiananmen as a tribute.

　　Citizen contingents pass in front of Tiananmen, enthusiastically cheering Chairman Mao.

　　Soviet Ambassador to China, Roshen (third from the left) on the reviewing stand.

　　Representatives from various countries of the International Student Union, invited to watch the ceremony.

　　Demonstration parade contingent of railway workers.

　　Demonstration parade contingent of Mentougou coal mine workers.

　　Waist drum team passing in front of Tiananmen.

　　In the literary and artistic contingent of the demonstration parade, a masquerade contingent showcasing the close unity between the Chinese and Korean people.

　　British people's representatives on the reviewing stand.

### 首都庆祝五一劳动节示威游行大会（图片）
吴本立  陈正青　郑景康  袁　苓　刘有声林扬  张力
1951-05-04
第6版()
专栏：

　　首都庆祝五一劳动节示威游行大会
　　毛主席及中央人民政府各首长在天安门主席台上检阅游行队伍。
　　光荣警卫首都的中国人民解放军部队通过天安门人民广场时的情形。
　　少年儿童队员代表北京市人民向中央人民政府毛泽东主席献花致敬。
　　被邀观礼的班禅额尔德尼先生在天安门上向中央人民政府毛泽东主席献哈达致敬。
　　市民队伍经过天安门前，热烈地向毛主席欢呼。
　　在观礼台上的苏联驻华大使罗申（左起第三人）
　　应邀观礼的国际学生联合会各国代表。
　　铁路工人的示威游行队伍。
　　门头沟煤矿工人的示威游行队伍。
　　通过天安门前的腰鼓队。
　　示威游行队伍的文艺大队中，表现中朝人民紧密团结的化装大队。
　　在观礼台上的英国人民代表。
