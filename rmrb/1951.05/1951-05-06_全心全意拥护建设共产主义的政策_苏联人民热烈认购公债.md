Title: Wholeheartedly Support the Policy of Building Communism: Soviet People Enthusiastically Subscribe to Government Bonds
Author:
Date: 1951-05-06
Page: 4
　　
　　Wholeheartedly Support the Policy of Building Communism
    Soviet People Enthusiastically Subscribe to Government Bonds

    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 5th] According to TASS, Moscow: The Soviet people are enthusiastically subscribing to the newly issued government bonds. As soon as the news was broadcast on the radio, mass meetings were held in enterprises and institutions throughout Moscow. Worker Chaibin of the Stalin Automobile Factory delivered an enthusiastic speech, stating that the prices of consumer goods have been reduced four times since the war, and the working people wholeheartedly support the policy of building communism and the decision to issue new government bonds. Stakhanovite female worker Kalitonova of the Electrical Factory said: This is a bond for the safety and peace of the people of the world. Workers in various factories are enthusiastically signing up to buy bonds, and workers on vacation in sanatoriums are sending telegrams requesting bonds to be purchased on their behalf. The Soviet Academy of Sciences held a meeting, and scientists warmly welcomed the new bonds. Academician Lavrentiev said: Buying bonds is a great honor, reflecting the people's sincere desire to build a happy life. Professors, writers, artists, staff of various institutions, and housewives are all enthusiastically subscribing to the bonds.

    In Leningrad, workers at the Kirov Factory began subscribing to the bonds within an hour of the news being released. Collective farmers in Estonia are vying to subscribe to the bonds. In the cities and villages of Belarus, Ukraine, Latvia, Georgia, Kazakhstan, and other republics, the news is spreading rapidly, and the enthusiastic subscription to the bonds has become a moving expression of the people's love for their socialist motherland.

### 全心全意拥护建设共产主义的政策  苏联人民热烈认购公债

1951-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　全心全意拥护建设共产主义的政策
    苏联人民热烈认购公债
    【新华社五日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联人民热烈认购新发行的公债。当无线电广播了这个消息后，莫斯科各企业各机关纷纷召开了群众大会。斯大林汽车工厂的工人柴宾发表热情的演说称：战后消费品的价格已四次降低，劳动人民全心全意赞助建设共产主义的政策和发行新公债的决定。电气工厂的斯塔哈诺夫式女工卡利托诺娃说：这是全世界人民安全与和平的公债。各工厂工人都在热情签名购买公债，在疗养院度假的工人打电报来要求代购公债。苏联科学院举行了大会，科学家热烈欢迎新公债。拉甫伦提叶夫院士说：购买公债是极大的荣誉，它反映了人民真心建设幸福生活。教授、作家、艺术家、各机关职员以及家庭妇女都在热情认购公债。
    在列宁格勒，基洛夫工厂的工人在消息发表一小时后就在认购公债了。爱沙尼亚的集体农民争先认购公债。在白俄罗斯、乌克兰、拉脱维亚、乔治亚、哈萨克及其他加盟共和国的城乡，消息迅速传播着，认购公债的踊跃已成为人民热爱社会主义祖国的动人表现。
