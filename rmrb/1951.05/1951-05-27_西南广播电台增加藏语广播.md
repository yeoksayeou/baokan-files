Title: Southwest Radio to Add Tibetan Language Broadcasts
Author:
Date: 1951-05-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 25th. In order to strengthen the publicity of ethnic policies, enhance the Tibetan people's understanding of current affairs, and promote unity among ethnic groups, the Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee and the Southwest People's Broadcasting Station have decided to add a Tibetan language program to the Southwest People's Broadcasting Station every Sunday, starting on the 27th. The main content of the Tibetan language program includes speeches on resisting America and aiding Korea, ethnic policy laws and decrees, domestic and international news, and Tibetan music. The broadcast time is from 8:30 PM to 9:00 PM every Sunday afternoon, with a wavelength of 49 meters and a frequency of 6154 kilocycles.

### 西南广播电台增加藏语广播

1951-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　西南广播电台增加藏语广播
    【新华社重庆二十五日电】西南军政委员会民族事务委员会和西南人民广播电台为加强民族政策宣传，提高藏族人民对时事的认识与增进民族间团结，定二十七日开始每周星期日在西南人民广播电台增加藏语节目。藏语节目的主要内容是有关抗美援朝和民族政策法令的讲话、国内外新闻与藏族音乐等。播送时间是每星期日下午八点三十分至九点，波长四九公尺，周率六一五四千周。
