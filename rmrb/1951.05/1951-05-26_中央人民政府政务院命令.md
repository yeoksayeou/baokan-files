　　Order of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government

　　The "Decision on the Division of Administrative Powers Between the Central and Local Authorities in Financial and Economic Work" was adopted at the 83rd Government Affairs Meeting of this Council on May 4, 1951, and is hereby promulgated for implementation.

           This order

                           Premier Zhou Enlai
               May 24, 1951
