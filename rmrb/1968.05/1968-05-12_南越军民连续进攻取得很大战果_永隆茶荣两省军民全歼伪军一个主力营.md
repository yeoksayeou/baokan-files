Title: South Vietnamese Military and Civilians Achieve Significant Victories in Continuous Attacks
Author:
Date: 1968-05-12
Edition: 6

The military and civilians of South Vietnam have achieved significant victories in continuous attacks. The military and civilians of the provinces of Vinh Long and Chau Doc have annihilated an entire enemy main force camp.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 11th — According to a report from the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, the military and civilians of the Thua Thien-Hue region in South Vietnam, in late April and early May, thwarted multiple enemy "sweeping" operations around Hue City and launched attacks on various fronts, achieving significant victories.

According to statistics, the military and civilians of the Thua Thien-Hue region have, during this period, eliminated more than 800 enemy soldiers, destroyed 23 military vehicles, 5 cannons, shot down or destroyed 44 aircraft, sunk 1 warship, and seized or destroyed a large number of weapons and ammunition.

The report states that from April 25th to 30th, the people's liberation armed forces in the areas surrounding Hue City continuously repelled multiple enemy "sweeping" operations in places such as Yong Zhen, Nian Fang, and Guang Tai, eliminating 267 enemy soldiers, the majority of whom were American invaders, and destroying 13 M-113 type armored vehicles.

In Hue City, on April 30th, the liberation army sank a large American warship on the Xiangjiang River and shot down a jet aircraft. At dawn on May 1st, the liberation army attacked the enemy in Xila Village west of Hue City, eliminating 91 enemy soldiers and shooting down a helicopter.

Along the 12th Highway, the people's liberation armed forces, from May 1st to 3rd, continuously attacked the enemy in the Li Ning area, the 1228th Hill, and on the highway, eliminating nearly 100 enemy soldiers and shooting down 2 helicopters.

During the same period, the liberation army and guerrillas also launched fierce attacks on the enemy in the areas east of Da Liang and Ya Lei, eliminating nearly 400 enemy soldiers, shooting down or destroying 40 aircraft, destroying 10 military vehicles, and 5 cannons.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 11th — According to a report from the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, the military and civilians of the provinces of Vinh Long and Chau Doc launched consecutive attacks on the enemy on May 1st and 2nd, annihilating an entire enemy main force camp and sinking or damaging 13 military vessels.

The report states that in the afternoon of May 1st, the people's liberation armed forces of Vinh Long and Chau Doc provinces launched a fierce artillery barrage on the enemy and South Korean puppet forces stationed in Xian Li Town, Vinh Long Province, and Chun He Town, Chau Doc Province, killing and wounding nearly 300 enemy soldiers, and sinking or damaging one military vessel each. At the same time, the liberation army also launched a fierce artillery barrage on the enemy's artillery positions, causing the enemy to suffer considerable losses.

On the morning of May 2nd, the military and civilians of Vinh Long and Chau Doc provinces launched a fierce interception of the enemy's naval convoy on the Meng Ti River. After a ten-minute brave battle, the military and civilians annihilated an entire enemy main force camp, sank 7 military vessels, and damaged 4 landing crafts.

### 南越军民连续进攻取得很大战果  永隆茶荣两省军民全歼伪军一个主力营

1968-05-12
第6版()
专栏：

    南越军民连续进攻取得很大战果
    永隆茶荣两省军民全歼伪军一个主力营
    新华社河内十一日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方承天—顺化地区军民，在四月底和五月初，粉碎了敌人在顺化市周围发动的多次“扫荡”，并在各个战场上袭击敌人，取得了很大战果。
    据统计，承天—顺化地区军民在这期间共消灭美伪军八百多名，击毁军车二十三辆、大炮五门，击落、击毁飞机四十四架，击沉战船一艘，缴获和摧毁许多武器和弹药。
    报道说，从四月二十五日到三十日，顺化市周围地区的人民解放武装力量，连续挫败了敌人对永贞、念放、广泰等地方发动的多次“扫荡”，共歼敌二百六十七名，其中绝大部分是美国侵略军，击毁M—113型装甲车十三辆。
    在顺化市，解放军四月三十日在香江上击沉美国大型战船一艘，并击落喷气式飞机一架。五月一日拂晓，解放军在顺化市西面的西拉字村袭击敌人，歼敌九十一名，击落直升飞机一架。
    在第十二号公路沿线，人民解放武装力量从五月一日到三日期间，接连袭击黎宁地区、一二二八号高地和公路上的敌人，消灭美伪军近一百名，击落直升飞机两架。
    在同一期间，解放军和游击队还连续在达良东面和亚雷等地区猛烈袭击敌人，歼敌近四百名，击落、击毁飞机四十架，击毁军车十辆、大炮五门。
    新华社河内十一日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方永隆、茶荣两省军民，一日和二日接连袭击敌人，全歼伪军一个主力营，击沉、击伤军用船只十三艘。
    报道说，一日下午，永隆、茶荣两省人民解放武装力量，猛烈炮轰永隆省祥利乡和茶荣省春协乡的伪军和南朝鲜帮凶军的驻地，打死、打伤敌人近三百名，击沉、击伤军用船只各一艘。与此同时，解放军还猛烈炮轰敌军的炮兵阵地，使敌人遭受不少损失。
    二日上午，永隆、茶荣两省军民在孟梯河上猛烈截击敌军船队，经过十分钟英勇的战斗，军民们就全歼伪军一个主力营，击沉军用船只七艘，击伤登陆艇四艘。
