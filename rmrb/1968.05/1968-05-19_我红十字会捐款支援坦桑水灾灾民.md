Title: The Chinese Red Cross Donates to Support Tanzanian Flood Victims
Author:
Date: 1968-05-19
Edition: 6

The Chinese Red Cross has donated funds to support Tanzanian flood victims.

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency in Dar es Salaam on the 17th, the first secretary of the Chinese Embassy in Tanzania, Shi Zhichun, handed over 40,000 shillings (equivalent to 14,000 yuan) donated by the Chinese Red Cross to Tanzanian flood victims to the Tanzanian Red Cross. Tanzanian Red Cross Secretary-General Mary McJah accepted the funds on behalf of the Tanzanian Red Cross and expressed heartfelt thanks to the Chinese people for their sympathy and assistance. Earlier, the Chinese Red Cross also sent a message of condolence to the Tanzanian Red Cross.

### 我红十字会捐款支援坦桑水灾灾民

1968-05-19
第6版()
专栏：

    我红十字会捐款支援坦桑水灾灾民
    据新华社达累斯萨拉姆十七日电中国驻坦桑尼亚大使馆一等秘书石智纯十七日在这里把中国红十字会捐赠给坦桑尼亚水灾灾民的四万先令（合人民币一万四千元）交给了坦桑尼亚红十字会。坦桑尼亚红十字会秘书长玛丽·麦克贾代表坦桑尼亚红十字会接受这笔款项，并表示衷心感谢中国人民给予的同情和帮助。这以前，中国红十字会还打给坦桑尼亚红十字会一封慰问电。
