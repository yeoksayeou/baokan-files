Title: South Vietnam Central Region People's Armed Forces Eliminate Nearly 8,000 Enemy Troops in Ten Days
Author:
Date: 1968-05-27
Edition: 6

The People's Liberation Armed Forces in the central region of South Vietnam, from May 5 to 14, annihilated nearly 8,000 enemy troops, including 2,500 American aggressors and South Korean mercenaries. They completely wiped out two enemy battalions and twenty-six companies.

During these ten days, the People's Liberation Armed Forces also shot down or destroyed 181 enemy aircraft, destroyed 300 tanks, armored vehicles, and other military vehicles, and destroyed 27 artillery pieces. The local military and civilians worked closely together, cutting off many of the enemy's supply lines and destroying or forcing the enemy to abandon dozens of outposts and "strategic hamlets," liberating large areas.

The People's Liberation Armed Forces in the provinces of Binh Dinh and Quang Tri in South Vietnam have been continuously attacking American aggressors since mid-May, annihilating a large number of enemy troops.

On the night of May 15, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Binh Dinh Province launched a fierce attack on an American infantry battalion and two artillery companies stationed in the Chu Hoi area. As soon as the battle began, the soldiers divided into several groups and bravely charged towards the enemy, fighting closely together and catching the enemy off guard. After nearly an hour of fighting, the People's Liberation Armed Forces killed and wounded 880 American soldiers, destroyed four enemy artillery pieces and mortars, and shot down two helicopters.

From May 14 to 17, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in the northern part of Quang Tri Province continuously ambushed and attacked the enemy on the Khe Sanh battlefield, killing more than 700 American aggressors, shooting down three enemy aircraft, and destroying one tank.

The People's Liberation Armed Forces in Phu Yen Province launched two days of attacks on the enemy in the county of Tuy Hoa on May 15 and 16, killing nearly 400 American aggressors, destroying 52 tanks and armored vehicles, and seizing a large amount of weapons, ammunition, and military supplies.

The People's Liberation Armed Forces in Kontum Province killed more than 200 American aggressors in two days of fighting on May 15 and 16, including the complete annihilation of one company and one platoon.

The military and civilians in the provinces of Quang Nam and other areas in South Vietnam have frequently launched attacks in the first half of May, achieving new victories.

The report said that the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Quang Nam Province launched an attack on the enemy in the Da Nang area at midnight on May 10, annihilating two companies and one artillery platoon of the puppet forces in one go, and capturing a batch of enemy troops. The People's Liberation Armed Forces in this province also launched continuous attacks on the enemy in the northern part of Quang Nam Province from the night of May 7 to May 8, annihilating four platoons and one "pacification" unit, and destroying two enemy armored vehicles.

The People's Liberation Armed Forces in the province of Tuy Hoa launched an attack on the enemy base at Bai Mountain at midnight on May 12. After a five-minute battle, they completely occupied the base, annihilating nearly a company of puppet forces.

Before this, from May 5 to 7, the military and civilians in the counties of Gang Long and others in this province launched continuous attacks on more than 30 enemy outposts, killing and capturing hundreds of American and puppet forces.

The People's Liberation Armed Forces in the province of Quang Ngai intercepted and attacked the puppet forces reinforcing the enemy in the village of Cam Tho in the county of Chau Thanh at noon on May 11, damaging three enemy ships, and killing and wounding more than 120 enemy troops.

The military and civilians in the province of An Xuyen launched a fierce attack on the enemy in the An Khe commune and the Mekong River on May 10, annihilating more than 250 enemy troops, including more than 100 American soldiers.

The military and civilians in the province of Binh Dinh have recently won many victories, annihilating a large number of enemy troops.

On the early morning of May 13, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Binh Dinh Province launched a fierce attack on an Australian mercenary infantry battalion and an artillery company that were air-dropped into the western suburbs of the city of Tuy Hoa. After a one-and-a-half-hour battle, the Liberation Armed Forces killed and wounded more than 400 enemy troops. They also destroyed three artillery pieces and five mortars, shot down one aircraft, and destroyed two armored vehicles.

### 南越中部中区人民武装十天歼敌近八千  边和、广治等省军民不断袭击美国侵略军，歼敌大批有生力量

1968-05-27
第6版()
专栏：

    南越中部中区人民武装十天歼敌近八千
    边和、广治等省军民不断袭击美国侵略军，歼敌大批有生力量
    新华社河内二十六日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方中部中区的人民解放武装力量，从五月五日到十四日，歼灭敌军近八千名，其中包括美国侵略军和南朝鲜帮凶军二千五百名，全歼敌军两个营和二十六个连。
    在这十天里，人民解放武装力量还击落、击毁敌机一百八十一架，击毁坦克、装甲车和其他军车三百辆，击毁大炮二十七门。各地军民密切配合，切断了敌人的许多交通线，并且摧毁和迫使敌人放弃几十个据点和“战略村”，解放了大片地区。
    新华社河内二十四日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方边和等省的人民解放武装力量，五月中旬接连袭击美国侵略军，歼灭敌人大批有生力量。
    边和省人民解放武装力量，十五日夜间猛烈袭击在楚会地区的美国侵略军的一个步兵营和两个炮兵连驻地。战斗打响后，战士们分成几路向敌人勇猛冲锋，紧密配合作战，打得敌人措手不及。经过近一小时的战斗，人民解放武装力量打死、打伤美军八百八十名，击毁敌人的大炮和迫击炮各四门，击落直升飞机两架。
    广治省北部人民解放武装力量，从十四日到十七日，连续在溪山战场伏击和袭击敌人，共消灭美国侵略军七百多名，击落敌机三架，击毁坦克一辆。
    西宁省人民解放武装力量，十五日和十六日两天，接连在盏盘县打击敌人，消灭美国侵略军近四百名，击毁坦克、装甲车五十二辆，缴获许多武器、弹药和军用物资。
    昆嵩省人民解放武装力量，在十五日和十六日两天的战斗中，消灭美国侵略军二百多名，其中包括全歼一个连和一个排。
    新华社河内电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方广南等省军民，五月上半月频频出击，不断取得了新的战果。
    报道说，广南省人民解放武装力量，十日凌晨进攻在达瓦地区的敌人，一举歼灭伪军两个连和一个炮兵排，俘虏敌人一批。这个省的人民解放武装力量还在七日夜间到八日，连续进攻广南省北部地区敌人，歼敌四个排和一个“绥靖”队，击毁敌人装甲车两辆。
    茶荣省人民解放武装力量，十二日凌晨进攻拜山敌军基地，经过五分钟的战斗，就完全占领了这个基地，全歼伪军近一个连。
    在此以前，五日、六日和七日三天，这个省的岗隆等县军民，接连进攻三十多个敌人据点，消灭和俘虏美伪军数百名。
    金瓯省人民解放武装力量，十一日中午在周城县更桃村附近截击增援的伪军，击伤敌船三艘，打死打伤敌人一百二十多名。
    槟椥省军民，十日在安庆乡和湄公河上痛击敌人，歼敌二百五十多名，其中包括美军一百多名。
    据新华社河内电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方边和省军民最近打了许多胜仗，歼灭大批敌人。
    边和省人民解放武装力量五月十三日凌晨向空投到新渊县城西北的澳大利亚帮凶军一个步兵营和一个炮兵连发动猛烈进攻。经过一个半小时的战斗，解放武装力量共打死打伤敌人四百多名，另外还击毁大炮三门和迫击炮五门，击落飞机一架，击毁装甲车两辆。
