Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Seven U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-05-15
Edition: 5

North Vietnamese military and civilians shot down seven U.S. aircraft.

New China News Agency, Hanoi, May 13 — According to reports from here, on May 12, the military and civilians of Nghe An Province in North Vietnam, in their counterattack against U.S. air pirates, shot down two invading U.S. aircraft. On the same day, the military and civilians of Ninh Binh Province shot down one U.S. unmanned drone.

Prior to this, the military and civilians of Ha Tinh and Quang Binh Provinces shot down four invading U.S. aircraft on May 5, 6, and 9.

### 越南北方军民击落美机七架

1968-05-15
第5版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机七架
    新华社河内十三日电　据这里报道，越南北方义安省军民，十二日在反击美国空中强盗的战斗中，共击落入侵的美机两架，同日，安沛省军民击落了美国无人驾驶飞机一架。
    在这以前，广平和河静两省军民，于五日、六日及九日先后击落入侵的美国强盗飞机四架。
