Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Twelve American Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-05-22
Edition: 5

North Vietnamese military and civilians shoot down twelve American aircraft.

According to Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi on the 20th, it was announced here that the military and civilians of Quang Binh and Nghe An provinces in North Vietnam shot down five enemy aircraft in three days of counterattacks against American air pirates on the 16th, 18th, and 19th. They also captured some American air pirates.

Another report stated that the military and civilians of Vinh Linh and Nghe An provinces shot down two invading American pirate aircraft on May 10th.

Another report stated that the military and civilians of Nghe An and Quang Binh provinces shot down four American aircraft on May 7th, 8th, and 12th.

According to a report by Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi on the 18th, the military and civilians of Hanoi shot down one invading American unmanned spy aircraft in the afternoon of the 17th.

### 越南北方军民击落美机十二架

1968-05-22
第5版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机十二架
    据新华社河内二十日电　据这里公布，越南北方广平省和义安省军民十六、十八和十九日三天在反击美国空中强盗的战斗中，又击落五架敌机，并活捉一些美国空中强盗。
    另据报道，永灵区和义安省军民五月十日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机两架。
    另据报道，义安和广平两省军民五月七日、八日和十二日还击落美机四架。
    新华社河内十八日电　据越南通讯社报道：河内军民十七日下午击落一架入侵的美国无人驾驶间谍飞机。
