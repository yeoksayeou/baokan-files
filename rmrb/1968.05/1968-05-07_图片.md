Title: Beijing Mochengjian Coal Mine Exceeds Production Targets
Author:
Date: 1968-05-07
Edition: 3

Beijing Mochengjian Coal Mine has exceeded its first quarter production targets and has also exceeded its production targets for April. Revolutionary workers, riding on the momentum of their successes, are determined to achieve even greater victories.
