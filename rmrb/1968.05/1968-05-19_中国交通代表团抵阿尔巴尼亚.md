Title: Chinese Transportation Delegation Arrives in Albania
Author:
Date: 1968-05-19
Edition: 6

The Chinese transportation delegation, which came to Albania to participate in the seventh session of the management committee of the Sino-Albanian Shipping Company, arrived in Tirana on May 16 by plane. The delegation members include Bo Hongmin, deputy director of the Maritime Transport Bureau of the Ministry of Communications and a member of the management committee from the Chinese side, and other staff. The delegation is led by Li Tingquan, acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Rambe Leika, deputy minister of the Albanian Ministry of Communications, Gamar Kluosi, manager of the Albanian side of the Sino-Albanian Shipping Company, and Shupresa Fuga, deputy director of the first department of the Albanian Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Zhou Ping, economic counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, and Shan Zhizhong, manager of the Chinese side of the Sino-Albanian Shipping Company, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 中国交通代表团抵阿尔巴尼亚

1968-05-19
第6版()
专栏：

    中国交通代表团抵阿尔巴尼亚
    新华社地拉那十八日电　前来阿尔巴尼亚参加中阿轮船股份公司第七次管理委员会会议的中国交通代表团团员、管理委员会中国方面委员、交通部远洋运输局副局长董华民和工作人员，于十六日乘飞机到达地拉那。这个代表团团长由中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃担任。前往机场欢迎代表团的有，阿尔巴尼亚交通部副部长兰比·莱卡，中阿轮船股份公司阿尔巴尼亚方面经理格马尔·克洛西，阿尔巴尼亚外交部第一司副司长什普雷萨·弗加等人。中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆经济参赞周平和中阿轮船股份公司中国方面经理单志中，也到机场欢迎代表团。
