Title: Chinese Embassy Staff Visit Albanian Youth Blind Person Cera, Praising Cera for Contributing to the Construction of Socialist New Countryside with Strong Will
Author:
Date: 1968-05-21
Edition: 6

Chinese Embassy Staff Visit Albanian Youth Blind Person Cera, Praising Cera for Contributing to the Construction of Socialist New Countryside with Strong Will

According to Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, May 18 - The Chinese Charge d'Affaires to Albania, Li Tingcan, and embassy staff visited Fatmir Cera, a blind youth from the Vilazez Agricultural Cooperative in Skrapar District, Albania, on May 14.

When the Chinese comrades, accompanied by Krroja, Deputy Director of the Second Department of the Albanian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Kartani, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the People's Assembly of Skrapar District, arrived at the Vilazez Agricultural Cooperative, the members of the cooperative shouted slogans such as "Enver-Hoxha-Mao Zedong" and "Long Live Albanian-Chinese Friendship."

Li Tingcan, on behalf of the entire staff of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, extended warm fraternal greetings to Cera and all members of the Vilazez Agricultural Cooperative. He enthusiastically praised Cera for following the teachings of the Albanian Labor Party and the great leader of the Albanian people, Comrade Enver Hoxha, overcoming the difficulties of blindness, and contributing to the construction of socialist new countryside with a strong will. Li Tingcan also expressed that the staff of the Chinese Embassy in Albania would follow the teachings of the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao, learn from the spirit of the Albanian people who dare to fight and dare to win, and strive to work for the strengthening of revolutionary friendship and combat unity between the peoples of China and Albania.

Cera expressed his welcome to the visit of the Chinese comrades and stated that he would better implement the teachings of the Labor Party and Comrade Hoxha, and more actively participate in the socialist revolution and socialist construction in the countryside.

Subsequently, the Chinese comrades worked together with Cera and other members in the fields, participating in the labor of sowing corn.

### 我驻阿使馆工作人员访问阿青年盲人采拉赞扬采拉  以坚强的意志为建设社会主义新农村作出贡献

1968-05-21
第6版()
专栏：

    我驻阿使馆工作人员访问阿青年盲人采拉赞扬采拉
    以坚强的意志为建设社会主义新农村作出贡献
    据新华社地拉那十八日电　中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃和使馆工作人员十四日访问了阿尔巴尼亚斯克拉巴里区的维尔泽泽农业合作社的青年盲人弗阿特·采拉。
    当中国同志在阿尔巴尼亚外交部第二司司长克罗亚同志和斯克拉巴里区人民会议执行委员会主席卡尔塔尼同志的陪同下，来到维尔泽泽农业社时，这个农业社的社员们高呼“恩维尔—毛泽东”、“阿中友谊万岁”等口号。
    李庭荃代办代表中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆全体同志，向采拉同志和维尔泽泽农业合作社全体社员表示亲切的兄弟般的问候，热情赞扬采拉同志遵照阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖霍查同志的教导，克服了双目失明的困难，以坚强的意志，为建设社会主义新农村作出贡献。李庭荃同志还表示，中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆的工作人员将遵照中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席的教导，学习阿尔巴尼亚人民不畏强暴，敢于斗争、敢于胜利的精神，为加强中阿两国人民的革命友谊和战斗团结努力工作。
    采拉同志表示欢迎中国同志的访问，并表示要更好地执行劳动党和霍查同志的教导，更加积极地参加农村社会主义革命和社会主义建设。
    接着，中国同志同采拉同志和其他社员一起在田间参加了播种玉米的劳动。
