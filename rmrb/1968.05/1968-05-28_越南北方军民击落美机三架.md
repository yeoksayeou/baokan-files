Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Three U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-05-28
Edition: 5

North Vietnamese military and civilians have shot down three invading U.S. aircraft in the past three days.

According to a report from here, the military and civilians of Yen Phu Province shot down a U.S. unmanned aircraft on the 26th. The military and civilians of Quang Binh Province shot down a U.S. jet fighter-bomber on the 25th. A U.S. jet reconnaissance aircraft was shot down by the military and civilians of Nghe An Province on the 24th.

### 越南北方军民击落美机三架

1968-05-28
第5版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机三架
    新华社河内二十六日电　据这里公布：越南北方军民最近三天又打落三架入侵的美国强盗飞机。
    永福省军民二十六日打落了一架美国无人驾驶飞机。广平省军民二十五日打落了一架美国喷气式战斗轰炸机。一架美国喷气式侦察机二十四日被河静省军民打落。
