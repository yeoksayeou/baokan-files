Title: The Single-Edition Publication of the Joint Editorial "Carry the Victory Forward" by "People's Daily," "Red Flag" Magazine, and "Liberation Army Daily"
Author:
Date: 1968-05-07
Edition: 3

The single-edition publication of the joint editorial "Carry the Victory Forward" by "People's Daily," "Red Flag" Magazine, and "Liberation Army Daily" has been published by the People's Publishing House. Starting from May 8, it will be available at the Xinhua Bookstore in Beijing and will soon be available at Xinhua Bookstores nationwide.

### 《人民日报》、《红旗》杂志、《解放军报》社论《乘胜前进》单行本出版

1968-05-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　《人民日报》、《红旗》杂志、《解放军报》社论《乘胜前进》单行本出版
    新华社六日讯　《人民日报》、《红旗》杂志、《解放军报》为庆祝“五一”国际劳动节发表的重要社论《乘胜前进》，已由人民出版社出版单行本，五月八日起在北京新华书店发行，并即将在全国各地新华书店陆续发行。
