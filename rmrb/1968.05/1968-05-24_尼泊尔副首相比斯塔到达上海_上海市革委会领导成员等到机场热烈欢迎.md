Title: Nepalese Deputy Prime Minister Bishta Arrives in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1968-05-24
Edition: 1

At the invitation of the Chinese government, Nepalese Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs and Economic Planning Kirtinidhi Bista arrived in Shanghai on his way to Beijing. He was warmly welcomed at the airport by leading members of the Shanghai Revolutionary Committee and officials from the Protocol Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs who had come to Shanghai especially for the occasion. Nepalese Ambassador to China Subba also came to Shanghai to welcome him.

The following is a report from Kathmandu:

At the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, His Excellency Kirtinidhi Bista, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs and Economic Planning of the Kingdom of Nepal, left Kathmandu by air on the morning of May 22 to visit China. Accompanying Bista on the visit were Secretary of the Nepalese Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yadunath Hanal and others.

In an interview with reporters at the airport, Bista said that his visit to China was aimed at strengthening the friendly relations between Nepal and China. He said that China and Nepal have good relations. We will further promote friendship and economic cooperation between the two countries.

Bista also expressed that he would convey the good wishes of King Mahendra, the Royal Government, and the people to Chairman Mao.

Those who went to the airport to see Bista off included: Nepalese Minister of Defense Giriprasad Budhathoki, Minister of Law, Justice, and Commerce Surendra Bahadur Basnet, Minister of Transportation Rudra Prasad Giri, Deputy Ministers, Commander-in-Chief of the Nepalese Royal Army Surendra Bahadur Shah, government officials, and President of the Nepal-China Friendship Association Purna Bahadur.

Those who also went to the airport to see Bista off included Acting Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Nepal Tu Guowei, as well as diplomatic personnel from various countries stationed in Nepal.

### 尼泊尔副首相比斯塔到达上海  上海市革委会领导成员等到机场热烈欢迎

1968-05-24
第1版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔副首相比斯塔到达上海
    上海市革委会领导成员等到机场热烈欢迎
    新华社上海二十三日电　应我国政府邀请前来进行友好访问的尼泊尔王国副首相兼外交、经济计划大臣基尔提·尼迪·比斯塔在赴北京途中，今天下午乘飞机到达上海。
    上海市革命委员会领导成员，专程来上海迎接的我国外交部礼宾司负责人到机场热烈欢迎。尼泊尔驻中国大使苏巴也来上海迎接。
    新华社加德满都二十二日电　尼泊尔王国副首相兼外交、经济计划大臣基尔提·尼迪·比斯塔，应中华人民共和国政府的邀请，二十二日上午乘飞机离开加德满都前往中国进行友好访问。随同比斯塔访问的有尼泊尔外交部秘书雅杜纳特·哈纳尔等。
    基尔提·尼迪·比斯塔在机场对记者谈话时说，他访问中国是为了加强尼泊尔和中国的友好关系。他说，中国和尼泊尔有着良好的关系。我们将进一步增进我们两国之间的友谊和经济合作。
    比斯塔还表示他将把马亨德拉国王陛下、陛下政府和人民的良好祝愿带给毛主席。
    前往机场为比斯塔送行的有：尼泊尔国防大臣吉里·普拉萨德·布达托基，法律、司法和工商大臣苏伦德拉·巴哈杜尔·巴斯奈特，交通运输大臣鲁德拉·普拉萨德·吉里，副大臣们，尼泊尔皇家陆军总司令苏伦德拉·巴哈杜尔·沙阿和政府官员以及尼中友好协会会长普尔纳·巴哈杜尔。
    前往机场送行的还有中国驻尼泊尔大使馆临时代办屠国维，以及各国驻尼泊尔的外交人员。
