On the second anniversary of the issuance of the May 16 Notification, personally drafted by our great leader Chairman Mao, the heroes, models, collective units, and active learners of Chairman Mao's works from the People's Liberation Army, as well as the cadres and fighters engaged in the "three supports, two militaries" work on the front lines, are filled with profound proletarian sentiments of boundless love and loyalty to Chairman Mao. They enthusiastically acclaim the great victories achieved in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution over the past two years under the radiant guidance of this epoch-making document—the Notification. They are determined to raise high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, follow the path indicated by the Notification, advance in victory, and, together with hundreds of millions of revolutionary masses, persistently struggle to seize complete victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.
