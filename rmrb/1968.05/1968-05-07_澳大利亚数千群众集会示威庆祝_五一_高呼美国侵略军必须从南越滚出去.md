Title: Thousands of Australians Rally to Celebrate May Day, Demand U.S. Troops Withdraw from South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1968-05-07
Edition: 5

Thousands of Australians in Melbourne and other cities took to the streets on May 6 to celebrate May Day, the International Labor Day. The demonstrators chanted slogans condemning U.S. aggression in Vietnam and demanding the withdrawal of all U.S. and allied troops from South Vietnam. Some slogans also warned against Johnson's "peace" tricks.

In Melbourne, after the march, a rally was held where speakers criticized the Australian government for following U.S. policies.

In Sydney, hundreds of young people carried flags of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam. This demonstration was one of the largest in Sydney's recent history.

### 澳大利亚数千群众集会示威庆祝“五一”　高呼美国侵略军必须从南越滚出去

1968-05-07
第5版()
专栏：

　　澳大利亚数千群众集会示威庆祝“五一”
　　高呼美国侵略军必须从南越滚出去
    新华社墨尔本六日电　澳大利亚墨尔本等城市的数千名群众五日举行示威游行，庆祝“五一”国际劳动节。
    示威游行中提出的主要口号，是谴责美国侵略越南和要求美国及其仆从从南越撤出它们的全部侵略军。还有些口号警告人们要提防约翰逊的“和平”花招。
    墨尔本的示威者在游行结束后举行了大会，会上发言的人谴责澳大利亚政府执行追随美国的政策。
    悉尼的数百名青年在游行时拿着越南南方民族解放阵线的旗帜。这次示威是悉尼近年来最大的一次示威。
