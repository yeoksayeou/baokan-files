Title: Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin Receive Over 20,000 Revolutionary Soldiers from Across the Nation
Author:
Date: 1968-05-21
Edition: 1

Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms, Vice Chairman Lin, received over 20,000 revolutionary soldiers from all over the country.

Chairman Mao, radiant and smiling, walked briskly into the reception hall, waving and clapping enthusiastically to the crowd. Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Jiang Qing, Yao Wenyuan, Xie Fuzhi, Huang Yongsheng, Wu Faxian, Ye Qun, and Wang Dongxing also participated in the reception.

The revolutionary soldiers, filled with revolutionary fervor, enthusiastically shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Chairman Mao lives ten thousand years!" They vowed to raise the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought even higher, inspired and guided by the "Notice," to advance victoriously and achieve a comprehensive victory in the first proletarian cultural revolution in human history.

Xinhua News Agency, May 20th: At this moment when the people of the entire nation are solemnly commemorating the second anniversary of the issuance of the May 16, 1966, "Notice" of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, personally drafted and presided over by the great leader Chairman Mao, and enthusiastically acclaiming the great victory of the proletarian cultural revolution, Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms, Vice Chairman Lin, along with Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Jiang Qing, Yao Wenyuan, Xie Fuzhi, Huang Yongsheng, Wu Faxian, Ye Qun, and Wang Dongxing, received over 20,000 revolutionary soldiers from all over the country today.

Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin's reception is the greatest care and encouragement for the people and soldiers of the entire nation who are bravely advancing to achieve a comprehensive victory in the proletarian cultural revolution.

Those honored to be received included: representatives of the Chinese People's Liberation Army soldiers and cadres, revolutionary masses participating in the Mao Zedong Thought study class in Beijing, representatives attending the Second Conference of Active Learners and Appliers of Mao Zedong Thought of the National Defense Science and Technology Commission of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the First Conference of Active Learners and Appliers of Mao Zedong Thought of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, revolutionary workers and military control commission leaders attending the coal mine revolution and production conference, and staff involved in receiving revolutionary masses during the proletarian cultural revolution.

Also participating in the reception were: members and alternate members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Dong Biwu, Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Ye Jianying, and Li Xuefeng;

Leaders from various departments of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the National Defense Science and Technology Commission, and related fields, including Su Yu, Li Zuopeng, Qiu Huizuo, Liu Xianquan, Wang Shusheng, Li Tianyou, Wang Xinting, Wen Yuceng, Zhang Chuming, Zhang Lingbin, Tan Furen, Xiao Li, and Luo Xunchu;

Leaders from various military branches and arms of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including Xiao Jinguang, Wang Hongkun, Zhang Xiuquan, Luo Yuanfa, Qian Renrong, Wang Huichu, Cao Lihui, Chen Renqi, Wu Lie, and Chen Shizhang;

Leaders from revolutionary committees (revolutionary preparatory groups) of various provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions, and various military regions and units, including Zheng Weishan, Liu Geping, Chen Xianrui, Teng Haiching, Huang Zuozhen, Wu Zhong, Zeng Shaoshan, Chen Yonggui, Yang Dezhi, Wang Xiaoyu, Zeng Shiyu, Liu Feng, Zhang Guohua, Liang Xingchu, Liu Jieting, Zhang Xiting, Han Xianchu, Chen Kang, Wang Enmao, Saifuding, Guo Peng, Chen Mingyi, and Ren Rong. Also present were Yu Quli and others.

Before the reception, these revolutionary soldiers, under the radiant guidance of Mao Zedong Thought, resolutely implementing Chairman Mao's instructions to "grasp revolution, promote production, work, and preparedness," fought on various fronts. They were filled with deep proletarian emotions, singing "The Boat Needs a Helmsman" and "Wishing Chairman Mao a Long Life" repeatedly, and reading Chairman Mao's quotations.

The great teacher, great leader Chairman Mao, radiant and smiling, walked briskly into the reception hall to meet everyone. The entire hall erupted in joy. Chairman Mao, very pleased, walked from one end of the reception hall to the other, waving and clapping enthusiastically to the crowd.

The revolutionary soldiers, filled with boundless love, faith, admiration, and loyalty to our great commander-in-chief Chairman Mao, waved the red revolutionary treasure book "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and enthusiastically shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Chairman Mao lives ten thousand years!" "Wishing Chairman Mao a long life! A long life!" They shouted, "Long live the comprehensive victory of the proletarian cultural revolution!" "Long live Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line!" "Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought!"

The revolutionary soldiers said, Chairman Mao is the red sun in our hearts. No words can express our love for Chairman Mao, and no songs can sing our admiration for Chairman Mao. We must raise the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought even higher, advance victoriously, and achieve a comprehensive victory in the first proletarian cultural revolution in human history.

The reception ended, and the revolutionary soldiers, immersed in great happiness, continued to sing revolutionary songs enthusiastically, shouting, "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years!" Many gathered in front of the statue of the great leader Chairman Mao in the front hall of the reception hall, solemnly vowing:

"We will forever take Vice Chairman Lin as our shining example, be infinitely loyal to Chairman Mao, infinitely loyal to Mao Zedong Thought, and infinitely loyal to Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line."

"We will resolutely follow Vice Chairman Lin's instructions, read Chairman Mao's books, listen to Chairman Mao's words, act according to Chairman Mao's instructions, and be good soldiers of Chairman Mao!"

### 伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林副主席接见来自全国各地的两万多名革命战士

1968-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

    伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林副主席接见来自全国各地的两万多名革命战士
    毛主席神采奕奕，满面笑容，健步来到接见会场，向大家频频招手，热烈鼓掌周恩来陈伯达康生李富春江青姚文元谢富治黄永胜吴法宪叶群汪东兴等同志参加接见
    被接见的革命战士满怀革命激情，热烈欢呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万万岁！”他们决心更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，在《通知》的指引和鼓舞下，乘胜前进，夺取人类历史上第一次无产阶级文化大革命的全面胜利
    新华社二十日讯　在全国亿万军民隆重纪念伟大领袖毛主席亲自主持制定的中共中央一九六六年五月十六日《通知》发表两周年，热烈欢呼无产阶级文化大革命取得伟大胜利的时刻，毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，以及周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春、江青、姚文元、谢富治、黄永胜、吴法宪、叶群、汪东兴等同志，今天接见了来自全国各地的革命战士两万多人。
    毛主席和林副主席的接见，是对于正在奋勇前进夺取无产阶级文化大革命全面胜利的全国军民的最大关怀，最大鼓舞。
    被光荣接见的有：正在北京参加毛泽东思想学习班学习的中国人民解放军指战员、革命干部和革命群众的代表，出席中国人民解放军国防科委第二次活学活用毛泽东思想积极分子代表大会和中国科学院首届活学活用毛泽东思想积极分子代表大会的代表，出席煤矿抓革命、促生产会议的革命职工代表和军管会负责同志，以及在无产阶级文化大革命中参加接待革命群众工作的人员。
    参加接见的还有：中共中央政治局委员和候补委员董必武、陈毅、李先念、徐向前、聂荣臻、叶剑英、李雪峰；
　中国人民解放军各总部、国防科委和有关方面的负责同志粟裕、李作鹏、邱会作、刘贤权、王树声、李天佑、王新亭、温玉成、张池明、张令彬、谭甫仁、肖力、罗舜初；
    中国人民解放军各军种、各兵种的负责同志萧劲光、王宏坤、张秀川、罗元发、邝任农、王辉球、曹里怀、陈仁麒、吴烈、陈士榘；
    各省、市、自治区革命委员会（革筹小组）和各军区、部队的负责同志郑维山、刘格平、陈先瑞、滕海清、黄作珍、吴忠、曾绍山、陈永贵、杨得志、王效禹、曾思玉、刘丰、张国华、梁兴初、刘结挺、张西挺、韩先楚、陈康、王恩茂、赛福鼎、郭鹏、陈明义、任荣。还有，余秋里等同志。
    接见以前，这些在毛泽东思想的光辉照耀下，坚决执行毛主席“抓革命，促生产，促工作，促战备”的指示，奋战在各条战线上的革命战士，满怀着深厚的无产阶级感情，一遍又一遍地高唱《大海航行靠舵手》、《祝福毛主席万寿无疆》，朗读毛主席语录。
    伟大导师、伟大领袖毛主席神采奕奕，满面笑容，健步来到接见会场，同大家亲切见面。这时全场一片欢腾。毛主席十分高兴地从接见会场主席台的一端走到另一端，向大家频频招手，热烈鼓掌。
    被接见的革命战士怀着对我们伟大统帅毛主席无限热爱、无限信仰、无限崇拜、无限忠诚的革命激情，挥动红色革命宝书《毛主席语录》，热烈欢呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万万岁！”“敬祝毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！”他们高呼：“无产阶级文化大革命的全面胜利万岁！”“毛主席的无产阶级革命路线胜利万岁！”“战无不胜的毛泽东思想万岁！”
    革命战士说，毛主席是我们心中的红太阳，千言万语说不尽我们对毛主席的热爱，千歌万曲唱不完我们对毛主席的敬仰。我们一定要更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，乘胜前进，夺取人类历史上第一次无产阶级文化大革命的全面胜利。
    接见结束了，沉浸在极大幸福中的革命战士，继续纵情高唱革命歌曲，欢呼：“毛主席万岁！万万岁！”许多人汇集到接见会场前厅伟大领袖毛主席塑像的前面，庄严宣誓：
    “我们永远以林副主席为光辉榜样，无限忠于毛主席，无限忠于毛泽东思想，无限忠于毛主席的无产阶级革命路线。”
    “我们坚决遵照林副主席的指示，读毛主席的书，听毛主席的话，照毛主席的指示办事，做毛主席的好战士！”
