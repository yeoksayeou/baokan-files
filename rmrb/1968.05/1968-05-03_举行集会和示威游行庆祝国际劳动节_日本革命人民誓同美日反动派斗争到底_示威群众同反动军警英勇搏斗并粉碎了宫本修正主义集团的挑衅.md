Title: Mass Rallies and Protest Marches Celebrate International Labor Day
Author:
Date: 1968-05-03
Edition: 6

	Revolutionary people across Japan held rallies and protest marches to celebrate International Labor Day, vowing to fight to the end against the U.S. and Japanese reactionaries. The demonstrators bravely fought against reactionary military police and foiled provocations by the Ryohei Kiyohara revisionist clique.

	New China News Agency, May 2nd: Tokyo—Revolutionary people in various parts of Japan held rallies and protest marches on May 1st, hoisting portraits of Chairman Mao, to celebrate International Labor Day.

	In Tokyo, about 500 revolutionary workers, youth, students, and poor residents from the Yamagata slum, carried a large portrait of the great teacher Chairman Mao and red banners reading "Long Live Mao Zedong Thought!" They held high the red book "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and wore Mao badges, reciting Chairman Mao's quotations loudly in the meeting venue, vowing to fight to the end against the U.S. and Japanese reactionaries.

	They also distributed Chairman Mao's statement supporting the struggle of American blacks against violence.

	Vice Chairman Taira Morio of the Japan-China Friendship Association (Genuine) and representatives of the youth and workers from the Yamagata slum all spoke at the rally. They expressed their determination to fight under the guidance of the invincible Mao Zedong Thought against U.S. imperialism, Soviet modern revisionism, the Japanese reactionaries, and the Ryohei Kiyohara revisionist clique of the Japanese Communist Party.

	The demonstrators bravely fought against the large number of military police sent by the Sato reactionary government, ensuring the protest march went on. During the march, they also foiled provocations by the Ryohei Kiyohara revisionist clique, severely condemning their betrayal.

	Workers and other revolutionary people in Yamaguchi Prefecture held rallies and protest marches in cities such as Shimonoseki, Yamaguchi, and Ube.

	At these rallies and marches, revolutionary people shouted slogans such as "Long Live the Great Mao Zedong Thought!", "Long Live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China!", "Firmly support the struggle of American blacks against violence!", "Down with the Sato cabinet!", and "Down with the revisionist clique of the Japanese Communist Party!" They also sang songs based on Chairman Mao's quotations and other revolutionary songs.

	At the rally in Shimonoseki, Chairman Furuya Shoichi of the Yamaguchi Prefecture Branch of the Japan-China Friendship Association (Genuine) spoke. He said that the Japanese people would firmly respond to Chairman Mao's great call to arms, launching a sustained and fierce attack on the common enemy, U.S. imperialism, and its lackeys!

	Furuya enthusiastically hailed the great victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China, expressing firm support for the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. aggression for national liberation.

	Revolutionary people in Kyoto marched singing songs based on Chairman Mao's quotations, with the Mao Zedong Thought propaganda team from the Kyoto Revolutionary Youth Organization carrying the red book "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and performing propaganda for Mao Zedong Thought for the crowd.

	The marching column of revolutionary people in Osaka carried a large banner with a passage from Chairman Mao's statement supporting the struggle of American blacks against violence, calling on people around the world to support the struggle of American blacks.

	During the demonstration, they loudly recited Chairman Mao's quotation: "The central task and highest form of the revolution is to seize political power through armed struggle, to solve problems through war."

	At the front of the marching column of revolutionary people in Fukuoka, there was a float with a large portrait of the great leader Chairman Mao and the quotation: "The Japanese nation is a great nation. It will never allow U.S. imperialism to ride roughshod over it for long." The marching column sang "The East Is Red" and "The Internationale" along the way. They also demonstrated in front of the building of the "American Cultural Center," shouting slogans such as "Oppose U.S. aggression in Vietnam!" and "Yankees, get out of Japan! Get out of Asia!" causing the U.S. imperialists to tremble with fear.

	Revolutionary people in Hyogo Prefecture, Yokohama, Tachikawa, North Kyushu, and Nagoya also held rallies and marches.

	According to the New China News Agency, Tokyo, May 1st: Revolutionary workers in Yamaguchi Prefecture expressed their determination to firmly respond to Chairman Mao's great call to arms in his statement supporting the struggle of American blacks against violence, vowing to unite with the proletariat of the world to fight for the liberation of the Japanese people and the victory of the world revolution. Bus driver Kakinami said that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China, which has epoch-making significance, is achieving comprehensive victory. This victory has consolidated the stronghold of the world revolution—the socialist China, making U.S. imperialism and its lackeys tremble with fear. Vice Chairman Tomita of the Ube Branch of the Yamaguchi Prefecture Teachers' Union emphasized that Mao Zedong Thought is the strongest ideological weapon for the proletariat of the world to unite and overthrow U.S. imperialism. Chairman Mao's great statement supporting the struggle of American blacks against violence points out the direction for the struggle against U.S. imperialism.

### 举行集会和示威游行庆祝国际劳动节　日本革命人民誓同美日反动派斗争到底　示威群众同反动军警英勇搏斗并粉碎了宫本修正主义集团的挑衅

1968-05-03
第6版()
专栏：

　　举行集会和示威游行庆祝国际劳动节
　　日本革命人民誓同美日反动派斗争到底
　　示威群众同反动军警英勇搏斗并粉碎了宫本修正主义集团的挑衅
    新华社二日讯　东京消息：日本各地革命人民一日高举毛主席画像，分别举行集会和示威游行，热烈庆祝“五一”国际劳动节。
    东京的革命工人、青年、学生和山谷贫民区的贫民共五百多人，高举伟大导师毛主席的巨幅画像，以及“毛泽东思想万岁！”的红色巨幅标语，举行了庆祝活动。他们手里捧着红彤彤的宝书《毛主席语录》，胸前戴着金光闪闪的毛主席像章，在会场上高声朗读毛主席语录，表示决心要同美日反动派斗争到底。
    他们还向群众散发了毛主席支持美国黑人抗暴斗争的声明。
    日中友好协会（正统）总部副理事长大森真一郎和青年代表以及山谷贫民区的工人代表都在集会上发表了讲话。他们表示决心在战无不胜的毛泽东思想指导下，为反对美帝国主义、苏联现代修正主义、日本反动派和日共宫本修正主义集团而斗争。
    示威群众同佐藤反动政府派出的大批军警进行了英勇搏斗，使示威游行坚持到底。示威群众在游行中还粉碎了宫本修正主义集团的挑衅，严厉谴责了他们的背叛行径。
    山口县的工人和其他革命人民分别在下关、山口、宇部等市举行了集会和示威游行。
    在这些集会和游行中，革命人民高呼：“伟大的毛泽东思想胜利万岁！”“中国无产阶级文化大革命胜利万岁！”“坚决支持美国黑人的抗暴斗争！”“打倒佐藤内阁！”“打倒日共修正主义集团！”等战斗口号，高唱毛主席语录歌曲和其他革命歌曲。
    在下关举行的集会上，日中友好协会山口县（正统）总部理事长古谷庄一郎发表了讲话。他表示日本人民要坚决响应毛主席的伟大战斗号召，向着共同敌人美帝国主义及其帮凶们发动持久的猛烈的进攻！
    古谷热情欢呼中国无产阶级文化大革命取得的伟大胜利，表示坚决支持越南人民的抗美救国战争。
    京都市革命人民在游行中高唱毛主席语录歌曲，由京都革命青年组织的毛泽东思想宣传队，手里捧着红色宝书《毛主席语录》，为群众表演了宣传毛泽东思想的节目。
    大阪革命人民的游行队伍抬着巨大的标语牌，上面写着毛主席在支持美国黑人抗暴斗争的声明中号召世界各国人民声援美国黑人的斗争的一段话。
    在示威中，他们高声朗诵毛主席语录：“革命的中心任务和最高形式是武装夺取政权，是战争解决问题。”
    福冈市革命人民的游行队伍前头的彩车上，悬挂着伟大领袖毛主席的巨幅画像和毛主席语录：“日本民族是一个伟大的民族。它是绝不会让美帝国主义长期骑在自己头上的。”游行队伍沿途高唱《大海航行靠舵手》和《国际歌》。他们还到“美国文化中心”的大楼前示威，高呼：“反对美帝侵略越南！”“美国佬从日本滚出去！从亚洲滚出去！”等口号，吓得美帝国主义者胆战心惊。
    兵库县、横滨市、立川市、北九州市和名古屋市等地的革命人民也都举行了集会和游行。
    据新华社东京一日电　日本山口县革命工人在庆祝“五一”国际劳动节的时候向中国记者发表谈话，表示坚决响应毛主席在支持美国黑人抗暴斗争的声明中所发出的伟大战斗号召，决心同全世界无产阶级团结起来，为争取日本人民的解放和世界革命的胜利而奋斗。公共汽车司机柿并说，具有划时代意义的中国无产阶级文化大革命正在取得全面胜利。这一胜利，巩固了世界革命的强大根据地——社会主义中国，使美帝国主义及其帮凶们胆战心惊。山口县教职员工会宇部支部副委员长富田强调指出，毛泽东思想是全世界无产阶级团结起来打倒美帝国主义的最强大的思想武器。毛主席支持美国黑人抗暴斗争的伟大声明，指出了打倒美帝国主义的斗争方向。
