Title: Pakistani Government Decides Not to Extend Lease for U.S. Communication Base
Author:
Date: 1968-05-30
Edition: 5

Pakistani Foreign Minister Asad Husain recently announced in the Pakistani National Assembly that the Pakistani government has decided not to extend the lease for the U.S. communication base near Peshawar.

Public opinion in Pakistan welcomes this government decision. The *Pakistan Times* said, "The government's decision reaffirms its sovereignty as a nation and places national interests above all other considerations." The *Nawai Waqt* said, "The presence of any foreign military base on our territory is certainly against our national interests."

The U.S. communication base near Peshawar was established in 1959, with a lease period of ten years.

### 巴基斯坦政府决定  不再延长美国通讯基地的租借期

1968-05-30
第5版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦政府决定
    不再延长美国通讯基地的租借期
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十九日电　巴基斯坦外交部长阿沙德·侯赛因最近在巴基斯坦国民议会上宣布，巴基斯坦政府已经决定不再延长美国在白沙瓦附近的通讯基地的租借期。
    巴基斯坦舆论欢迎政府这一决定。《巴基斯坦时报》说，政府的决定是“重申了它作为一个国家的主权，并把民族利益置于其他一切考虑之上”。《建设报》说：“任何外国军事基地在我国领土的存在肯定是违反我们民族利益的。”
    美国在白沙瓦附近的通讯基地是在一九五九年建立的，当时规定租借期为十年。
