Title: Albanian Workers Commemorate Martyrs' Day; Comrade Enver Hoxha Visits the Martyrs' Cemetery in Tirana to Lay Flowers

Author:

Date: 1968-05-09

Edition: 6

Albanian workers across the country commemorated Martyrs' Day. On the morning of May 5th, Comrade Enver Hoxha, the great leader of the Albanian people and the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour, visited the Martyrs' Cemetery in Tirana to lay flowers and met with many relatives of the martyrs who were sweeping the graves, engaging in warm conversations with them.

On the same morning, other leaders of the Albanian Party and government, including Aliya and Toska, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour, the Presidium of the People's Assembly, the Council of Ministers, the Democratic Front, the People's Army, and various mass organizations, also laid wreaths at the Martyrs' Cemetery.

Those who went to the Martyrs' Cemetery in Tirana to lay wreaths also included Li Tingquan, the acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Albania.

On May 5th, workers from various parts of Albania held memorial meetings, symposiums, exhibitions on the lives and struggles of martyrs, and visits to the families of martyrs, among other commemorative activities. They expressed their determination to further carry out the revolutionization of national life and ideological transformation, to build and defend the socialist motherland.

### 阿尔巴尼亚各地劳动群众纪念烈士节　霍查同志到地拉那烈士陵墓献花

1968-05-09
第6版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚各地劳动群众纪念烈士节
　　霍查同志到地拉那烈士陵墓献花
    新华社地拉那七日电　五月五日是阿尔巴尼亚烈士节。这天上午，阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查同志到地拉那烈士陵墓献了鲜花，并会见了正在那里扫墓的许多烈士的亲属，同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    同天上午，阿尔巴尼亚党政领导人阿利雅、托斯卡等同志也分别代表阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会、人民议会主席团、部长会议、民主阵线、人民军部队和各群众团体，到烈士陵墓献了花圈。
    前往地拉那烈士陵墓献花圈的还有，中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃和各国驻阿尔巴尼亚的外交使节。
    阿尔巴尼亚各地劳动群众五日分别举行纪念会、座谈会、烈士生平和斗争历史展览会、访问烈士家属等各种纪念活动。他们表示要进一步开展国家生活和人的思想革命化运动，建设和保卫社会主义祖国。
