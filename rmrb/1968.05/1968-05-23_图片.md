Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Meets with Foreign Ministers and Delegations from Mali and Guinea
Author:
Date: 1968-05-23
Edition: 2

On May 22, Premier Zhou Enlai met with Mali's Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ousmane Ba, and Guinea's Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lansana Beavogui, along with the joint delegation from Guinea and Mali visiting China for friendly purposes, led by them. The photo shows Premier Zhou Enlai with Ousmane Ba (front, second from the left) and Lansana Beavogui (front, second from the right), as well as their delegation from Mali and Guinea. Photo by Xinhua News Agency.
