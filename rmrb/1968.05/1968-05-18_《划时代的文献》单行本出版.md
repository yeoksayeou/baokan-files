Title: Publication of the Single-Volume Edition of "A Milestone Document"
Author:
Date: 1968-05-18
Edition: 1

The editorial departments of the People's Daily, Red Flag Magazine, and the Liberation Army Daily have written an important article titled "A Milestone Document" to commemorate the second anniversary of the May 16th Circular issued by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China in 1966. This article has been published in a single-volume edition by the People's Publishing House and will begin distribution at the Beijing Xinhua Bookstore starting May 19th. It will also be distributed in Xinhua Bookstores across the country in the near future.

### 《划时代的文献》单行本出版

1968-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

    《划时代的文献》单行本出版
    新华社十七日讯　《人民日报》、《红旗》杂志、《解放军报》编辑部为纪念中共中央一九六六年五月十六日《通知》发表两周年而写的重要文章《划时代的文献》，已由人民出版社出版单行本，五月十九日起开始在北京新华书店发行，并即将在全国各地新华书店陆续发行。
