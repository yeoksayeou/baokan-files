Title: Trade Agreement and Protocol between China and Nepal Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-05-29
Edition: 5

The trade agreement and protocol between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of His Majesty the King of Nepal were signed in Beijing today.

Premier Zhou Enlai attended the signing ceremony.

Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi and Nepal's Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Kirtinidhi Bista signed the agreement and protocol on behalf of their respective governments.

Attending the signing ceremony from the Nepalese side were: Secretary for Foreign Affairs Yadu Nath Kanel and other officials accompanying Deputy Prime Minister Bista, as well as Nepal's Ambassador to China Subba and other diplomatic staff.

From the Chinese side, those present included: Han Nianlong, Lin Haiyun, Xie Huai-de, and other relevant officials.

		Chen Yi, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and Kirtinidhi Bista, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of Nepal, sign the trade agreement and protocol on behalf of their respective governments.

### 中尼两国政府贸易协定和议定书在京签字  周恩来总理参加签字仪式

1968-05-29
第5版()
专栏：

    中尼两国政府贸易协定和议定书在京签字
    周恩来总理参加签字仪式
    新华社二十八日讯　中华人民共和国政府和尼泊尔国王陛下政府贸易协定和议定书，今天在北京签字。
    国务院总理周恩来参加了签字仪式。
    国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅和尼泊尔王国副首相兼外交大臣基尔提·尼迪·比斯塔，分别代表本国政府在贸易协定和议定书上签字。
    参加签字仪式的，尼泊尔方面有：外事秘书雅杜·纳特·卡纳尔和其他随同比斯塔副首相来访的人员，尼泊尔驻中国大使苏巴和使馆外交官员。
    中国方面还有：韩念龙、林海云、谢怀德等有关方面负责人。
