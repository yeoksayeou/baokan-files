Title: Syrian Vice Premier Denounces U.S. Support for Israel's Aggressive Policies
Author:
Date: 1968-05-10
Edition: 5

Syrian Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Ibrahim Makhous recently denounced U.S. imperialism's support for Israeli Zionism's aggressive policies. He also reaffirmed that armed struggle is the only path to liberation for the Arab people.

Makhous made these remarks during a reception for delegations of Arab and other foreign journalists in Damascus. He said, "Zionism's aggression against the Arab nation has exposed the aggressive nature of Israel and the United States to the entire world."

He also condemned Israel's provocative military parade held on May 2 in Jerusalem. He pointed out that Israel's provocations are instigated by the United States.

### 叙利亚副总理谴责美帝支持以色列的侵略政策　武装斗争是阿拉伯人民获得解放的唯一道路

1968-05-10
第5版()
专栏：

　　叙利亚副总理谴责美帝支持以色列的侵略政策
　　武装斗争是阿拉伯人民获得解放的唯一道路
    据新华社大马士革七日电　叙利亚副总理兼外交部长易卜拉欣·马胡斯，最近谴责美帝国主义支持以色列犹太复国主义的侵略政策，并且重申，武装斗争是阿拉伯人民取得解放的唯一道路。
    马胡斯在这里接见阿拉伯和一些其他外国报界代表团时说：“犹太复国主义对阿拉伯民族的侵略，在全世界面前暴露了以色列和美国的侵略本性。”
    他还谴责以色列五月二日在耶路撒冷市举行挑衅性的军事检阅。他指出，以色列的挑衅是得到美国怂恿的。
