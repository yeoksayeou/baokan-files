Title: Artillery Strikes on American and Puppet Facilities Intercept Enemy Naval Fleet
Author:
Date: 1968-05-15
Edition: 5

	Artillery Strikes on American and Puppet Facilities Intercept Enemy Naval Fleet
	Military and Civilians in South Vietnam's Thừa Thiên and Quảng Trị Over Three Days Annihilate More Than a Thousand Enemy Troops
	Down Fourty-Three Enemy Aircraft, Sink or Damage Nine Enemy Ships

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 14th — According to a report from the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, from the 4th to the 6th, the military and civilians of Thừa Thiên Province and the southern region of Quảng Trị Province in South Vietnam continuously launched fierce attacks on the enemy, annihilating more than a thousand enemy troops, shooting down or destroying forty-three enemy aircraft, destroying nearly one hundred enemy vehicles, and sinking or damaging nine enemy ships.

	The report states that in the early hours of the 5th, the People's Liberation Armed Forces launched a fierce bombardment on the West Lai Airport, Phu Bai Airport, Chi Ha puppet military base, Tong Linh American military base, and several enemy facilities in the city of Huế. This caused the enemy to suffer severe losses. At the same time, the Liberation Army and guerrilla forces closely coordinated their efforts to launch fierce attacks on several enemy strongholds and outposts, annihilating hundreds of enemy troops.

	On the morning of the 5th, the Liberation Army intercepted and annihilated more than two hundred puppet troops counterattacking in the western region of Huế. In the afternoon, the Liberation Army again fiercely intercepted American troops counterattacking in the same region, annihilating nearly two hundred and ninety American aggressor troops. That night, the Liberation Army launched a fierce artillery bombardment on the enemy in the Yem Mieu area north of Huế, almost completely destroying eighty enemy vehicles and one hundred and twenty tents.

	On the 5th and 6th, the Liberation Army also continuously intercepted enemy naval fleets on the Hương River, sinking eight enemy ships and damaging one.

	Along the 12th Highway, on the morning of the 4th, the Liberation Army launched a fierce attack on the A Shau American forward airbase, destroying thirty-five helicopters, ten vehicles, and annihilating more than two hundred American troops from the First Cavalry Division. On the same afternoon, the Liberation Army shelled American artillery positions, destroying three cannons. On the afternoon of the 4th and the morning of the 5th, the Liberation Army also continuously intercepted the enemy on the highway, annihilating more than four hundred American and puppet troops and seizing a large number of weapons.

	In Quảng Trị Province, from the night of the 4th to the early hours of the 5th, the Liberation Army launched a fierce artillery bombardment on the La Vang and Ai Tu enemy bases, as well as enemy targets in the towns of Chợ Lớn, Hải Lăng, and Mỹ Lệ, and enemy strongholds south of Quảng Trị City, destroying a large number of enemy combat equipment and annihilating many enemy troops.

### 炮轰美伪设施截击敌军船队  南越承天广治军民三天歼敌千余  击落击毁敌机四十三架，击沉击伤敌船九艘

1968-05-15
第5版()
专栏：

    炮轰美伪设施截击敌军船队
    南越承天广治军民三天歼敌千余
    击落击毁敌机四十三架，击沉击伤敌船九艘
    新华社河内十四日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方承天省和广治省南部地区军民，从四日到六日接连痛击敌军，歼敌一千多名，击落击毁敌机四十三架，击毁军车近一百辆，击沉击伤军用船只九艘。
    报道说，五日凌晨，人民解放武装力量猛烈轰击西禄机场、芙拜机场、慈下伪军基地、同林美军基地以及顺化市的一些敌军设施，使敌人遭到严重的损失。与此同时，解放军和游击队密切配合，猛烈袭击敌军一些驻地和据点，歼敌数百名。
    五日上午，解放军在顺化西面地区截击反扑的伪军，歼敌二百多名。下午，解放军又在同一地区猛烈截击反扑的美军，消灭美国侵略军近二百九十名。当天夜间，解放军猛烈炮轰顺化市北面泳氓地区的敌军，几乎全部摧毁敌人的军车八十辆和帐篷一百二十座。
    五日和六日，解放军还接连在香江上截击敌军船队，击沉军用船只八艘，击伤一艘。
    在第十二号公路沿线，解放军在五月四日早晨猛烈袭击亚雷美军野战机场，一举击毁直升飞机三十五架、军车十辆，消灭美国第一骑兵师的侵略军二百多名。同日下午，解放军炮击美国侵略军炮兵阵地，击毁大炮三门。四日下午和五日上午，解放军还接连在公路上截击敌人，消灭美伪军四百多名，缴获许多武器。
    在广治省，四日夜间到五日凌晨，解放军猛烈炮轰拉汪、爱紫敌军基地以及兆丰、海陵、梅岭县城的敌人目标和广治市南面的敌军驻地，击毁敌人大批作战工具，消灭许多敌人。
