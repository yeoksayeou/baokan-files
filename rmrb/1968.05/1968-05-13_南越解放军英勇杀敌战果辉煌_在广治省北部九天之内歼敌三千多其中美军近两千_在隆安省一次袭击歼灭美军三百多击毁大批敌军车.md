Title: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Bravely Fights the Enemy with Glorious Achievements
Author:
Date: 1968-05-13
Edition: 6

The South Vietnamese Liberation Army has bravely fought the enemy and achieved glorious results. In the northern part of Quang Tri Province over a period of nine days, they annihilated more than 3,000 enemy troops, including nearly 2,000 American soldiers. In an attack in Long An Province, they annihilated more than 300 American soldiers and destroyed a large number of enemy vehicles.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 12 — According to a report from the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, the people's liberation armed forces in the northern part of Quang Tri Province in South Vietnam fought bravely against the enemy from April 29 to May 7, annihilating more than 3,000 enemy troops, including 1,993 American invaders.

	The people's liberation armed forces also shot down and damaged 20 enemy aircraft, sank and damaged 8 enemy warships, destroyed more than 30 military vehicles, burned down several ammunition and fuel depots, and seized a large amount of weapons and military supplies.

	On May 1, the liberation army in the northern part of Quang Tri Province, in close cooperation with guerrilla forces and local troops, launched a fierce attack on an American armored battalion, an armored battalion of the puppet army, and two battalions of American troops protecting them in the Dong Ha area northeast of the river. They annihilated more than 650 American and puppet troops, destroying dozens of tanks and armored vehicles. On the night of May 2, the liberation army launched simultaneous fierce attacks on the enemy strongholds of Phu Hoa, Phu Rai, and many other places, severely defeating three enemy companies and annihilating 400 American troops. After suffering consecutive heavy blows, the enemy hastily sent troops to Dong Ha for rescue. On the morning of May 5, the enemy's reinforcements were bravely intercepted by the liberation army, annihilating nearly 200 American and puppet troops.

	From May 5 to 7, the liberation army in the northern part of Quang Tri Province annihilated more than 1,200 enemy troops, shot down and destroyed 6 enemy aircraft, and destroyed many military vehicles.

	At the same time, the artillery units of the liberation army also continuously and fiercely bombarded the American bases of Vinh Loc and Kon Tum, causing serious losses to the enemy.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 12 — According to a report from the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, on the night of May 8, the people's liberation armed forces in Long An Province launched a fierce attack on an American convoy in My Hoa commune, Deo Khe district. After more than two hours of brave fighting, they annihilated more than 300 American invaders and destroyed 74 tanks and armored vehicles.

	On May 8, the people's liberation armed forces also intercepted the enemy about one kilometer away from Deo Khe town, annihilating more than 100 American invaders and destroying 10 armored vehicles.

	Before this, from May 2 to 4, the military and people of Deo Khe district consecutively intercepted the enemy, annihilating more than 500 American and puppet troops, shooting down 4 enemy aircraft, and destroying and damaging 12 tanks and armored vehicles.

### 南越解放军英勇杀敌战果辉煌  在广治省北部九天之内歼敌三千多其中美军近两千  在隆安省一次袭击歼灭美军三百多击毁大批敌军车

1968-05-13
第6版()
专栏：

    南越解放军英勇杀敌战果辉煌
    在广治省北部九天之内歼敌三千多其中美军近两千
    在隆安省一次袭击歼灭美军三百多击毁大批敌军车
    新华社河内十二日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方广治省北部人民解放武装力量在四月二十九日到五月七日期间英勇杀敌，共消灭敌人三千多名，其中包括美国侵略军一千九百九十三名。
    人民解放武装力量还击落击伤敌机二十架，击沉击伤敌人战船八艘，击毁各种军车三十多辆，烧毁弹药库和汽油库多座，缴获大批武器和军用物资。
    五月一日，广治省北部解放军同游击队和地方部队密切配合，在东河东北地区猛烈进攻一个美军装甲营、一个伪军装甲营及其掩护下的两营美军，消灭美伪军六百五十多名，击毁成十辆坦克和装甲车。二日夜间，解放军同时猛烈袭击富厚、普赖和其它许多敌军据点，重创敌军三个连，消灭美军四百名。敌人遭到接连的沉重打击后，急忙派兵前往东河救援。五日上午，敌人的援军遭到解放军的英勇截击，美伪军被消灭近二百名。
    广治省北部解放军在五日到七日三天的战斗中，消灭敌人一千二百多名，击落击毁敌机六架，击毁军车多辆。
    与此同时，解放军炮兵部队也连续猛烈轰击越门、昆天等美军基地，给敌人造成严重损失。
    新华社河内十二日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方隆安省人民解放武装力量八日夜间在德和县美广乡猛烈袭击美军车队，经过两个多小时的英勇战斗，消灭美国侵略军三百多名，击毁坦克和装甲车七十四辆。
    人民解放武装力量八日还在距离德和县城约一公里的地方截击敌人，消灭美国侵略军一百多名，击毁装甲车十辆。
    在这之前，德和县军民从二日到四日接连截击敌人，消灭美伪军五百多名，击落敌机四架，击毁击伤坦克和装甲车十二辆。
