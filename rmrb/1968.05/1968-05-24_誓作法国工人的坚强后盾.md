Title: Pledge to Be the Strong Backing of French Workers
Author: Anshan Iron and Steel Company Eight Blast Furnace, Nine Blast Furnace All Revolutionary Workers
Date: 1968-05-24
Edition: 6

We steel workers are overjoyed to hear the news that French worker comrades have launched a massive strike struggle. This unprecedented strike struggle fully demonstrates the mighty power of the French working class. In the strike struggle, French workers have occupied more than 300 key factories and mines, halting production and paralyzing transportation, dealing a heavy blow to the reactionary rule of the French monopolistic bourgeoisie. We are thrilled and excited for the revolutionary struggle of the French working class! We salute the French working class!

The revolutionary struggle of the French working class has greatly boosted the morale of revolutionary people and severely dampened the arrogance of imperialists, modern revisionists, and reactionaries. It is a reappearance of the Paris Commune revolutionary spirit in the 1960s. We shout: Long live the Paris Commune revolutionary spirit!

Today, the world has entered a new era under the great banner of Mao Zedong Thought. The world revolutionary situation is excellent and getting better every day. From Asia, Africa, and Latin America to the heart of the capitalist world—Western Europe and North America—people's revolutionary struggles are surging. The days of imperialism, modern revisionism, and reactionaries of all countries are getting worse.

Chinese steel workers, armed with the invincible Mao Zedong Thought, are under the leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao, striving for the complete victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. The unprecedented Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China is having a profound impact on the world revolution, greatly inspiring the fighting will of revolutionary people around the world. Let those imperialists, modern revisionists, and reactionaries who fear, hate, and curse our Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution cry in despair! "Strike while the iron is hot!" We must hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, resolutely carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to the end, and wholeheartedly support the revolutionary struggles of the people of the world. We will, with the firm resolve and actual actions of producing more and better steel and iron, support the revolutionary struggle of the French working class.

Chairman Mao teaches us: "The people who have already won revolutionary victory should support the struggle of the people who are fighting for liberation. This is our internationalist duty." To carry out the instructions of the great leader Chairman Mao is our sacred duty as the Chinese working class; to fulfill our internationalist duty is our glorious mission. We regard the revolutionary struggle of the French working class as our own struggle and their victory as our victory. Seven hundred million Chinese people fully support the revolutionary struggle of the French working class and the just struggles of all revolutionary people. We pledge to be the strong backing of the French people and all revolutionary people of the world!

### 誓作法国工人的坚强后盾
鞍山钢铁公司八号平炉、九号高炉全体革命职工
1968-05-24
第6版()
专栏：

    誓作法国工人的坚强后盾
    鞍山钢铁公司八号平炉、九号高炉全体革命职工
    我们钢铁工人听到法国工人兄弟掀起了规模巨大的罢工斗争的消息，感到无比高兴。这次规模空前的罢工斗争，充分显示了法国工人阶级的强大威力。在罢工斗争中，法国工人占领了三百多个重要工厂、矿山，使生产停顿，交通瘫痪，沉重地打击了法国垄断资产阶级的反动统治。我们怀着无比兴奋的心情，为法国工人阶级的革命斗争叫好！向法国工人阶级致敬！
    法国工人阶级的革命斗争大长了革命人民的志气，大灭了帝修反的威风。它是巴黎公社革命精神在二十世纪六十年代的再现。我们高呼：巴黎公社革命精神万岁！
    今天，世界已经进入了以毛泽东思想为伟大旗帜的新时代。世界革命形势一派大好，而且越来越好。从亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲到资本主义世界的心脏地区——西欧、北美，人民革命斗争风起云涌。帝国主义、现代修正主义和各国反动派的日子越来越不好过了。
    用战无不胜的毛泽东思想武装起来的中国钢铁工人，在伟大领袖毛主席的领导下，正在夺取无产阶级文化大革命的全面胜利。史无前例的中国无产阶级文化大革命对世界革命发生着深远的影响，极大地鼓舞着各国革命人民的战斗意志。让那些恐惧、仇恨、诅咒我们无产阶级文化大革命的帝国主义、现代修正主义老爷们向隅而泣吧！“快把那炉火烧得通红，趁热打铁才能成功！”我们一定要更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，誓把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底，全力支援世界人民的革命斗争。我们要以多炼钢，炼好钢，多出铁，出好铁的坚强决心和实际行动，支持法国工人阶级的革命斗争。
    毛主席教导我们：“已经获得革命胜利的人民，应该援助正在争取解放的人民的斗争，这是我们的国际主义的义务。”执行伟大领袖毛主席的指示，是我们中国工人阶级的神圣职责；履行国际主义义务，是我们中国工人阶级的光荣使命。我们把法国工人阶级的革命斗争看作是自己的斗争，把法国工人阶级斗争的胜利看作是自己的胜利。七亿中国人民全力支持法国工人阶级的革命斗争，全力支持一切革命人民的正义斗争，誓作法国人民和全世界革命人民的坚强后盾！
