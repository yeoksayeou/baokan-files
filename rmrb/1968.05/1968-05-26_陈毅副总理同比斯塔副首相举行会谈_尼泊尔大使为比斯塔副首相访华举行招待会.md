Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Holds Talks with Vice Premier Bista
Author:
Date: 1968-05-26
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi held talks with Nepalese Vice Premier Kirtinidhi Bista

BEIJING, May 25 (Xinhua) — Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council held talks with Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Kirtinidhi Bista of the Kingdom of Nepal today in Beijing.

The talks were held in a friendly atmosphere.

Relevant officials from both sides attended the talks.

Nepalese Ambassador to China Subba holds reception for Vice Premier Bista's visit to China

BEIJING, May 25 (Xinhua) — To welcome the visit of Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Kirtinidhi Bista of the Kingdom of Nepal and his entourage to China for a friendly visit, Nepalese Ambassador to China Subba held a reception tonight at the embassy.

Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council, and relevant officials from the government departments, the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee, including Han Nianlong, Lin Haiyun, Xie Huai-de, Ding Xilin, and Xiang Qian, as well as representatives from Beijing revolutionary mass organizations, were invited to attend the reception.

The reception was held in a friendly atmosphere.

Diplomats from various countries stationed in China were also invited to attend the reception.

### 陈毅副总理同比斯塔副首相举行会谈  尼泊尔大使为比斯塔副首相访华举行招待会

1968-05-26
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理同比斯塔副首相举行会谈
    尼泊尔大使为比斯塔副首相访华举行招待会
    新华社二十五日讯　国务院副总理陈毅同尼泊尔王国副首相基尔提·尼迪·比斯塔，今天在北京举行了会谈。
    会谈在友好的气氛中进行。
    双方有关方面负责人参加了会谈。
    新华社二十五日讯　为欢迎尼泊尔王国副首相兼外交大臣基尔提·尼迪·比斯塔及其随行人员来中国进行友好访问，尼泊尔驻中国大使苏巴今晚在大使馆举行了招待会。
    国务院副总理陈毅，政府有关部门、北京市革命委员会的负责人韩念龙、林海云、谢怀德、丁西林、向前，以及北京革命群众组织的代表，应邀出席了招待会。
    招待会在友好的气氛中进行。
    各国驻中国的使节，应邀出席了招待会。
