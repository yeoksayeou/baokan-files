Title: Military and Civilians in Quang Tri and Other Provinces Annihilate Large Numbers of Enemies in April
Author:
Date: 1968-05-08
Edition: 6

Military and civilians in Quang Tri and other provinces annihilate large numbers of enemies in April

According to the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, the guerrilla forces and local units in the provinces of Quang Tri, My Tho, Gia Dinh, Binh Duong, and Tuyen Duc in South Vietnam have annihilated many enemies in several battles in April.

The guerrilla forces and local units in the southern part of Quang Tri Province, from April 16 to 24, launched counterattacks against the enemy's "sweeping" operations in the areas of Le Xuan, Vinh Chau, and Linh Chau. They annihilated over 400 enemy troops, destroyed 33 oil pipelines, and destroyed or damaged 9 enemy vehicles and 9 enemy aircraft.

The local units and guerrilla forces in the districts of Hoi Nghia and Hoi Xa in My Tho Province, over three days from April 17 to 18 and 23, smashed the "sweeping" operations by the U.S.-puppet forces aimed at reopening the Fourth National Highway. They killed or wounded over 440 enemy troops, severely damaged a puppet army special forces battalion, damaged one warship, and seized a large amount of weapons and military supplies.

The local units and guerrilla forces in the districts of Go Vap and Hoc Mon in Gia Dinh Province, on April 15, ambushed two main force battalions of the puppet army in the village of Trung An, annihilating one company and severely damaging another. They then continued to attack the enemy on April 17 and 18, annihilating 100 puppet troops.

A platoon of local units in Binh Duong Province, in cooperation with the guerrilla forces, on April 6, surrounded and attacked a U.S. army battalion invading D Zone, killing or wounding 160 enemy troops.

The people's liberation armed forces in Tuyen Duc Province, on April 14, annihilated a main force battalion of the puppet army in the area 4 kilometers southwest of the town of Tan Yen. On April 16 and 17, they annihilated two companies and one platoon of the puppet army in the area 7 kilometers northeast of the town of Tan Yen. On April 19, the people's liberation armed forces fiercely intercepted a U.S. army battalion near Ben Cat, annihilating nearly 100 U.S. invaders. On the night of April 26, the guerrilla forces in Ben Cat District of Tuyen Duc Province attacked the U.S. military base at Lai Vung, destroying four barracks, three helicopters, four vehicles, and annihilating over 40 U.S. invaders.

According to reports from here, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province, on the night of May 5, shot down three invading U.S. aircraft in the battle against U.S. air pirates. On the same day, the military and civilians of Quang Tri Province also shot down one invading U.S. aircraft and captured two air pirates alive.

### 南越广治等省军民四月份歼灭大量敌人　越南北方广平、河静省军民击落美国强盗飞机四架

1968-05-08
第6版()
专栏：

　　南越广治等省军民四月份歼灭大量敌人
　　越南北方广平、河静省军民击落美国强盗飞机四架
    据新华社河内七日电  据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方广治、美萩、嘉定、边和、土龙木五省游击队和地方部队，在四月份的一些战斗中，歼灭许多敌人。
    广治省南部游击队和地方部队，在四月十六日到二十四日期间，在礼川、云祥、灵召等地区反击敌人“扫荡”，共歼敌四百多名，毁坏输油管三十三条，击毁击伤敌人军车九辆，击落敌机九架。
    美萩省丐礼、丐?两县地方部队和游击队在四月十七日、十八日和二十三日三天里，粉碎了美伪集团为打通第四号公路而进行的“扫荡”，打死打伤敌人四百四十多名，重创伪军一个别动营，击伤战船一艘，缴获大批武器和军用物资。
    嘉定省鹅邑、旭门两县地方部队和游击队，四月十五日在忠安乡伏击伪军两个主力营，歼灭一个连，重创另一个连。接着，又在四月十七日和十八日接连进攻敌人，消灭伪军一百名。
    边和省地方部队一个班同游击队配合，在四月六日包围和进攻进犯Ｄ战区的一营美军，打死打伤一百六十名敌人。
    土龙木省人民解放武装力量十四日在新渊县城西南四公里的地区，全歼伪军一个主力营，共打死打伤敌人五百多名。十六日和十七日，人民解放武装力量又在新渊县城东北七公里的地区全歼伪军两个连和一个排。十九日，人民解放武装力量在边葛附近猛烈截击一营美军，消灭美国侵略军近一百名。土龙木省边葛县游击队四月二十六日凌晨在袭击莱溪美军基地的战斗中，摧毁美军营房四幢，击毁直升飞机三架、军车四辆，消灭美国侵略军四十多名。
    新华社河内七日电  据这里报道，越南北方广平省军民，五日夜间在反击美国空中强盗战斗中，击落入侵的美国飞机三架。同一天，河静省军民也击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架，并活捉两名飞贼。
