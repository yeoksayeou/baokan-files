Title: Cambodian Deputy Prime Minister Cables Zhou Enlai Premier to Thank China for Donating Cholera Vaccines to Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1968-05-29
Edition: 5

	Cambodian Deputy Prime Minister Cables Zhou Enlai Premier to Thank China for Donating Cholera Vaccines to Cambodia

	BEIJING, May 28 (Xinhua) -- The Cambodian News Agency released a cable sent by Deputy Prime Minister Son Sann of the Kingdom of Cambodia on May 22 to Premier Zhou Enlai, thanking China for donating 2 million milliliters of cholera vaccines to Cambodia.

	The cable said that, in accordance with the will of the Cambodian government and at the personal instruction of Premier Zhou Enlai, China donated 2 million milliliters of cholera vaccines to the Cambodian Ministry of Public Health to help Cambodia carry out its anti-cholera campaign.

	In the cable, Son Sann said: "I take this opportunity to express, on behalf of the Cambodian government, our heartfelt gratitude for this generous gift, which once again demonstrates the friendship and active support of the People's Republic of China for Cambodia."

### 柬埔寨代首相致电周恩来总理  感谢中国赠送柬埔寨抗霍乱疫苗

1968-05-29
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨代首相致电周恩来总理
    感谢中国赠送柬埔寨抗霍乱疫苗
    新华社金边二十八日电　柬埔寨新闻社发表了柬埔寨王国代首相宋双二十二日就中国向柬埔寨赠送二百万毫升抗霍乱疫苗打给周恩来总理的一封感谢电。
    电报中说，根据柬埔寨王国政府的意愿，并由于周恩来总理亲自指示，中国向柬埔寨公共卫生部赠送二百万毫升抗霍乱疫苗，以便柬埔寨开展防治霍乱的运动。
    宋双在电报中说：“我谨借此机会代表王国政府对这一慷慨的赠品向你表示深切的谢意，这再一次体现了中华人民共和国对柬埔寨的友谊和积极的支援。”
