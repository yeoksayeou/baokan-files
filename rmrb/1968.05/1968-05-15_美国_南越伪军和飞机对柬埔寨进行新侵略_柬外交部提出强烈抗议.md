Title: U.S.-South Vietnamese Puppet Army and Aircraft Launch New Invasion of Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1968-05-15
Edition: 6

	U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet army and aircraft launch new invasion of Cambodia
	Cambodian Foreign Ministry lodges strong protest

	BEIJING, May 12 (Xinhua) - According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on May 12, the Cambodian Foreign Ministry, on May 8, sent strong protests through the French and Australian embassies in Cambodia to the U.S. government and the Saigon puppet regime, condemning the new invasion by U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet army and aircraft on May 4. The protest notes stated that on May 4, the U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet army and four helicopters, guided by a reconnaissance plane, attacked the Cambodian border guard post, police station, and checkpoint in the village of Bavei in the province of Chup, injuring seven people and causing serious material damage.

	The protest notes pointed out that the U.S. government must bear the serious consequences arising from this and must compensate for all the losses caused by these deliberate attacks.

	The Cambodian Foreign Ministry also strongly protested on May 9 against the crimes of the Thai military invading Cambodian territory, territorial waters, and airspace on March 20, 26, and April 1 and 7.

### 美国—南越伪军和飞机对柬埔寨进行新侵略  柬外交部提出强烈抗议

1968-05-15
第6版()
专栏：

    美国—南越伪军和飞机对柬埔寨进行新侵略
    柬外交部提出强烈抗议
    新华社金边十二日电据柬埔寨新闻社十二日报道：柬埔寨外交部五月八日通过法国和澳大利亚驻柬埔寨大使馆，分别照会美国政府和西贡傀儡当局，强烈抗议美国—南越伪军和飞机五月四日对柬埔寨的新的侵略行径。照会指出，五月四日，美国—南越伪军和四架直升飞机，在一架侦察机指引下，对柬埔寨柴桢省巴韦村的柬埔寨边防哨所、警察局和关卡发起进攻，使七人受伤并造成了严重的物质损失。
    照会指出，美国政府必须承担由此而产生的严重后果，必须赔偿这些蓄意的进攻所造成的一切损失。
    柬埔寨外交部五月九日还强烈抗议泰国军队在三月二十日、二十六日以及四月一日和七日侵犯柬埔寨领土、领海和领空的罪行。
