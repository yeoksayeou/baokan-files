Title: "I'll Take the Risk!"
Author: Wang Enyu
Date: 1968-05-31
Edition: 4

	I'll Take the Risk!
	Beijing First Machine Tool Factory Wang Enyu

	Fully focused, you are rushing to make earth rockets,
	The harvest picture, always rolling in your heart;
	You snatched the tool for loading explosives from someone else,
	You said: "I'll take the risk!"

	How ordinary a sentence,
	How brilliant the words!
	It depicts your warrior's sublime soul,
	It displays your hero's iron will and courage!

	To save others, you deliberately fell from the motorcycle,
	To protect the crops, you leaped to block the breached dike,
	During live-fire exercises, you bravely covered your comrade,
	In every instance, you cast aside the "I"!

	Until the final moment of heroic sacrifice,
	It was all for the safety of your class brothers!
	You used your own life to protect the lives of twenty-seven people,
	Leaving behind a heart of "no selfishness and no personal gain"!

	"I'll take the risk!"—
	This is the concentrated expression of your spiritual world;
	Chairman Mao's teaching of "never seeking personal gain and always seeking to benefit others,"
	You practiced with your glorious life!

	From Cai Yongxiang and Ou Yanghai sacrificing themselves to save the train,
	To Dong Cunrui blowing up the fort and Huang Jiguang blocking the gun port,
	This sentence echoes Li Wentong's "don't mind me,"
	This sentence is the common language in the hearts of heroes!

	"I'll take the risk!"
	Like thunder, it shakes our hearts—
	Shattering the foundation of "self,"
	Building a great wall of "public"!

	"I'll take the risk!"
	Like a brilliant flash of lightning—
	Illuminating the souls of hundreds of millions of people,
	Capturing countless magnificent scenes of communism!

### “这危险，我来干！”
王恩宇
1968-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

    “这危险，我来干！”
    北京第一机床厂　王恩宇
    全神贯注，你们在赶制土火箭，
    丰收图画，时时在心中翻卷；
    抢过别人装置炸药的工具，
    你说：“这危险，我来干！”
    多么普通的一句话呵，
    多么闪光的语言！
    它描绘了你战士的崇高灵魂，
    它展现了你英雄的钢骨铁胆！
    为救他人你从摩托车上毅然摔倒，
    为护庄稼你纵身堵住决口的渠岸，
    实弹演习时你冒险扑在战友身上，
    哪一次不都把“我”字抛在一边？
    直到最后英勇壮烈牺牲呵，
    也是为了阶级兄弟的安全！
    你以自己生命保护了二十七人的生命，
    把一颗“毫无自私自利之心”长留人间！
    “这危险，我来干！”——
    这是你精神世界的集中表现；
    毛主席“毫不利己专门利人”的教导，
    你用光辉的生命去实践！
    从蔡永祥、欧阳海舍身救列车，
    想到董存瑞炸碉堡、黄继光堵枪眼，
    这句话是李文忠“不要管我”的回响，
    这句话是英雄们心中共同的语言！
    “这危险，我来干！”
    象响雷，在我们心头震撼——
    把“私”字的根基狠狠震毁，
    把“公”字的长城牢牢树建！
    “这危险，我来干！”
    象一道光辉夺目的闪电——
    在亿万人心灵的胶片上感光，
    将摄出无数共产主义壮丽的画卷
