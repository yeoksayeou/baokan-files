Title: U.S.-South Vietnamese Puppet Forces Invade Cambodian Territory
Author:
Date: 1968-05-30
Edition: 5

New York Times, May 29 — According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on May 29, the Cambodian Ministry of Foreign Affairs sent a note on May 26 to the U.S. government and the Saigon puppet regime, strongly protesting the invasion of Cambodian territory by more than 20 U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet forces between the late hours of May 20 and the early hours of May 21. The forces used hand grenades and automatic weapons to attack a Cambodian military outpost, killing two Cambodians and injuring four.

The note demands that the U.S. and the Saigon puppet regime guarantee that such criminal acts will not happen again and compensate for the damages.

Additionally, the Cambodian Ministry of Foreign Affairs sent another note on May 25 to the U.S. government and the Saigon puppet regime, strongly protesting the deliberate invasion of Cambodian territory by U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet forces on April 7 and 9.

### 美国—南越伪军侵犯柬领土  柬埔寨外交部提出强烈抗议

1968-05-30
第5版()
专栏：

    美国—南越伪军侵犯柬领土
    柬埔寨外交部提出强烈抗议
    新华社金边二十九日电　据柬埔寨新闻社二十九日报道，柬埔寨外交部二十六日照会美国政府和西贡傀儡当局，强烈抗议美国—南越伪军二十多人在二十日深夜到二十一日凌晨侵入柬埔寨干丹省领土，用手榴弹和自动武器袭击一个柬埔寨军事哨所，打死柬埔寨人两名、打伤四名。
    照会要求美国和西贡傀儡当局保证不再发生这种罪恶行径，并赔偿损失。
    此外，柬埔寨外交部二十五日还照会美国政府和西贡傀儡当局，强烈抗议美国—南越伪军四月七日和九日蓄意侵犯柬埔寨领土。
