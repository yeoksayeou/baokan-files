Title: People of Some Asian and African Countries Hold Rallies and Demonstrations to Resolutely Support the Armed Struggle of the Palestinian People Against Aggression
Author:
Date: 1968-05-28
Edition: 5

People of some Asian and African countries have held rallies and demonstrations to resolutely support the armed struggle of the Palestinian people against aggression.

According to Xinhua News Agency, people from various sectors in some Asian and African countries continue to hold mass rallies and demonstrations, opposing U.S. imperialism and its tool of aggression, Israel, and resolutely supporting the armed struggle of the Palestinian people against aggression.

About 300 students and young people in Mauritania held a demonstration on the evening of the 15th in Nouakchott to support the armed struggle of the Palestinian people against U.S.-Israeli aggression. The demonstrators held up signs reading "Down with U.S. imperialism!" and "Down with Israel!" and chanted slogans supporting the just struggle of the Palestinian people as they marched through the main streets of the capital. As the demonstrators passed by the Chinese Embassy, they shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao!"

A grand mass demonstration was held in Khartoum, the capital of Sudan, on the 16th. The demonstrators shouted, "Long live the unity of Arab revolutionary forces!" and "Armed struggle is the road to victory!" The demonstrators also angrily shouted, "Down with U.S. imperialism!"

In his speech to the demonstrators, Sudanese Prime Minister Muhammad Ahmad Mahgoub emphasized that the Sudanese government would not accept any compromise with Israel and would not recognize Israel.

According to Aden Radio, the Arab people in Aden held a mass rally on the evening of the 15th to commemorate "Palestine Day." Speakers at the rally expressed their resolute support for the armed struggle of the Palestinian people to liberate their lost lands.

According to Iraqi newspapers, some colleges at Baghdad University held a grand rally on the 15th. Speakers at the rally unanimously strongly condemned the establishment of the so-called "state" of Zionist Israel on Palestinian land by imperialism led by the United States, and turning it into a base for aggression against the Arab nation.

They pointed out that armed struggle is the "only way to restore the rights of the Arabs in Palestine and to enable the Palestinian people to enjoy self-determination."

Students in Pakistan and foreign students studying in Karachi held a rally on the 15th in Karachi to commemorate "Palestine Day."

Mohammed Shukri Kadhimi, representing foreign students studying in Pakistan, said in his speech that as long as the people of small countries mobilize and wage a people's war, they can defeat powerful enemies.

### 亚非一些国家人民分别举行集会和示威游行  坚决支持巴勒斯坦人民反侵略武装斗争

1968-05-28
第5版()
专栏：

    亚非一些国家人民分别举行集会和示威游行
    坚决支持巴勒斯坦人民反侵略武装斗争
    据新华社讯　一些亚非国家的各界人民继续举行群众集会和示威游行，反对美帝国主义及其侵略工具以色列，坚决支持巴勒斯坦人民反侵略的武装斗争。
    大约三百名毛里塔尼亚学生和青年十五日晚上在努瓦克肖特举行示威游行，支持巴勒斯坦人民反对美国—以色列侵略的武装斗争。示威群众高举着“打倒美帝国主义”、“打倒以色列”的标语牌，高呼支持巴勒斯坦人民正义斗争的口号，穿过了首都一些主要街道。示威群众经过中国大使馆附近时高呼：“毛主席万岁！”
    苏丹首都喀土穆十六日举行盛大的群众游行示威。示威者高呼：“阿拉伯革命力量团结万岁！”“武装斗争是胜利的道路！”游行群众还愤怒地高呼：“打倒美帝国主义！”
    苏丹总理穆罕默德·艾哈迈德·马哈古卜对游行队伍讲话时强调指出，苏丹政府将不会接受同以色列的任何妥协，也不会承认以色列。
    据亚丁广播电台报道，在亚丁的阿拉伯人民十五日晚举行群众集会，纪念“巴勒斯坦日”。在会上发言的人表示坚决支持巴勒斯坦人民为解放失地而进行的武装斗争。
    据伊拉克报纸报道，巴格达大学一些学院十五日举行了盛大集会。在会上发言的人们一致强烈谴责以美国为首的帝国主义在巴勒斯坦土地上建立以色列犹太复国主义的所谓“国家”，并把它变成侵略阿拉伯民族的基地。
    他们指出，武装斗争是“恢复阿拉伯人在巴勒斯坦的权利和使巴勒斯坦人民享有自决权利的唯一道路”。
    巴基斯坦学生和在卡拉奇学习的外国留学生十五日在卡拉奇集会纪念“巴勒斯坦日”。
    穆罕默德·舒克里·卡多米代表在巴基斯坦学习的外国留学生在会上讲话时说，小国人民只要动员起来，进行人民战争，就能够打败强大的敌人。
