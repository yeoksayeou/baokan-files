Title: Young Pioneers Study Chairman Mao's Works
Author: Yao Yaping
Date: 1968-05-27
Edition: 3

    In Changsha City, Hunan Province, a young member of the Revolutionary Committee of Zhuzhou City Primary School, 11-year-old Guo Penghui (right), who is an active participant in studying Chairman Mao's works, voluntarily pairs up with his classmate Du Xiangyan to form a "Red Pair." They actively study and apply Chairman Mao's works.
