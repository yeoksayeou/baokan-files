Title: A Friend from Yemen Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-05-05
Edition: 6

A friend from Yemen, Ali Latif Saour, Vice Chairman of the Yemen Development Bank, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of our country for a visit. Relevant officials and Charge d'Affaires Al-Moallem of the Yemeni Embassy in China went to the airport to welcome him.

### 也门一朋友抵京

1968-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

　　也门一朋友抵京
    新华社四日讯　应邀前来我国访问的也门建设发展银行副董事长阿里·勒托夫·骚尔，今天乘飞机到达北京。有关方面的负责人和也门驻中国大使馆临时代办阿勒莫阿替，前往机场欢迎。
