Title: Compilation of Five Important Articles, Including "Advancing in Victory," Published
Author:
Date: 1968-05-14
Edition: 4

The compilation of five important articles, including "Advancing in Victory," has been published. The Xinhua News Agency reported on the 13th that the editorials from "People's Daily," "Red Flag" magazine, and "Liberation Army Daily" titled "Revolutionary Committees Are Good," as well as the editorials from "People's Daily" and "Liberation Army Daily" celebrating the establishment of the Revolutionary Committees of Hunan and Anhui provinces, titled "The Sun Rises Over the Land of Hibiscus" and "Victory of the Revolutionary Masses of the Proletariat," and the commentary from "Red Flag" magazine titled "Class Analysis of Factionalism," and the editorial from "People's Daily," "Red Flag" magazine, and "Liberation Army Daily" celebrating May Day, titled "Advancing in Victory," convey Chairman Mao's latest series of instructions. They summarize the experiences of the Revolutionary Committees and point out the direction of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

To meet the learning needs of the broad revolutionary masses, the People's Publishing House has compiled the above five important articles into a book titled "Advancing in Victory." Starting from the 15th, it will be available at the Xinhua Bookstore in Beijing and will soon be available at Xinhua Bookstores across the country.

### 《乘胜前进》等五篇重要文章汇编出版

1968-05-14
第4版()
专栏：

    《乘胜前进》等五篇重要文章汇编出版
    新华社十三日讯　《人民日报》、《红旗》杂志、《解放军报》社论《革命委员会好》以及《人民日报》、《解放军报》在庆祝湖南省革命委员会和安徽省革命委员会成立时的社论《芙蓉国里尽朝晖》、《无产阶级革命派的胜利》，《红旗》杂志评论员文章《对派性要进行阶级分析》，《人民日报》、《红旗》杂志、《解放军报》庆祝“五一”国际劳动节社论《乘胜前进》，传达了毛主席的一系列最新指示，总结了革命委员会的经验，指出了无产阶级文化大革命的方向。
    为了满足广大革命群众的学习需要，人民出版社已将上述五篇重要文章汇编出版，书名为《乘胜前进》，十五日起开始在北京新华书店发行，并将陆续在全国各地新华书店发行。
