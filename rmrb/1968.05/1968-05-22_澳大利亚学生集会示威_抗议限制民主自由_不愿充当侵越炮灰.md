Title: Australian Students Hold Protest Rally Against Restrictions on Democratic Freedoms and Refusal to Serve as Cannon Fodder in Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1968-05-22
Edition: 5

Two hundred students at Monash University in Melbourne, Australia, held a rally on May 16 to protest against the university administration's attempt to curtail students' democratic freedoms under the guise of amending the "disciplinary regulations."

Following the rally, the students staged a demonstration at the university's administrative building.

On the evening of May 17, La Trobe University in Victoria held a mass meeting to denounce the Australian government's proposed "National Service Act Amendment," which aims to intensify the conscription of Australian youth to serve as cannon fodder in the U.S. aggression against Vietnam.

### 澳大利亚学生集会示威  抗议限制民主自由  不愿充当侵越炮灰

1968-05-22
第5版()
专栏：

    澳大利亚学生集会示威
    抗议限制民主自由  不愿充当侵越炮灰
    新华社墨尔本电　澳大利亚墨尔本莫纳什大学的两百名学生十六日举行集会，抗议学校当局以修改“纪律条例”为名，无理限制学生的民主自由。
    会后，学生们在大学的行政大楼举行了示威。
    维多利亚的拉特罗布大学十七日晚上举行群众大会，谴责澳大利亚当局提出的“国家兵役法修正案”，这个修正案将加紧征召澳大利亚青年充当美国侵越战争的炮灰。
