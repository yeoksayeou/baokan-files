Title: Welcoming the Joint Friendship Delegation from Guinea and Mali
Author:
Date: 1968-05-22
Edition: 3

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs held an evening of performing arts today to warmly welcome the joint friendship delegation from Guinea and Mali, led by Mali's Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ousmane Ba, and Guinea's Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lansana Beavogui.

Vice Premiers Chen Yi and Li Xiannian accompanied the friends from Guinea and Mali to watch the revolutionary modern Peking opera "Taking Tiger Mountain by Strategy."

Guinea's Ambassador to China, Sekou Camara, and Mali's Chargé d'Affaires at the Embassy in China, Koulibaly, as well as other diplomatic personnel from the Guinean and Malian embassies in China, attended today's evening event.

After the performance, Minister Ousmane Ba and Minister Lansana Beavogui, accompanied by Vice Premiers Chen Yi and Li Xiannian, walked onto the stage amid warm applause to shake hands with the performers and congratulate them on their successful performance.

### 欢迎几内亚、马里联合访华友好代表团  我外交部举行文艺晚会  陈毅和李先念副总理陪同贵宾观看《智取威虎山》

1968-05-22
第3版()
专栏：

    欢迎几内亚、马里联合访华友好代表团
    我外交部举行文艺晚会
    陈毅和李先念副总理陪同贵宾观看《智取威虎山》
    新华社二十一日讯　外交部今天举行文艺晚会，热烈欢迎由马里外交部长乌斯曼·巴和几内亚外交部长兰萨纳·贝阿沃吉率领的几内亚、马里联合访华友好代表团。
    国务院副总理陈毅、李先念，陪同几内亚、马里朋友，观看了革命现代京剧《智取威虎山》。
    几内亚驻中国大使塞古·卡马拉和马里驻中国大使馆临时代办库里巴利，以及几内亚、马里驻中国大使馆其他外交官员出席了今天的晚会。
    演出结束后，乌斯曼·巴部长和兰萨纳·贝阿沃吉部长由陈毅、李先念副总理陪同，在热烈的掌声中走上舞台，同演员们亲切握手，祝贺他们演出成功。
