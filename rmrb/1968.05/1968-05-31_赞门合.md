Title: In Praise of the Gate Closure
Author: Zhang Renyuan
Date: 1968-05-31
Edition: 4

	In Praise of the Gate Closure
	The songs of praise roll in like waves,
	The name of Gate Closure is known far and wide.
	Everything is for Chairman Mao,
	The sunflowers bloom forever towards the sun.
	The sunlight shines on all the flowers,
	Mao Zedong Thought shines brilliantly.
	Follow the great leader Chairman Mao,
	Hold high the red flag and march forward.

### 赞门合
张仁远
1968-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

    赞门合
    赞歌如潮滚滚来，
    门合的名字传四海。
    一切为着毛主席，
    葵花永远向阳开。
    阳光普照百花红，
    毛泽东思想放异彩。
    紧跟伟大领袖毛主席，
    高举红旗向前迈。
    （张仁远）
