Title: Dutch Charge d'Affaires Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1968-05-02
Edition: 6

The Dutch Charge d'Affaires to China, Duxsen, held a reception today to celebrate the birthday of the Queen of the Netherlands. Relevant officials from our side, including Hou Xiang, were invited to attend the reception. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China were also invited to attend.

### 荷兰驻中国代办举行招待会

1968-05-02
第6版()
专栏：

　　荷兰驻中国代办举行招待会
    新华社三十日讯　荷兰驻中国代办窦克森为荷兰女王诞辰，今天举行招待会。我有关方面负责人宦乡等应邀出席了招待会。各国驻中国使节也应邀出席。
