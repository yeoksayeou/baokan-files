Title: Chinese Government Trade Delegation Head Leaves Beijing for Visits to Guinea and the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1968-05-03
Edition: 5

	Chinese Government Trade Delegation Head, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Zhou Huamin, left Beijing by plane on April 30 to visit the Republic of Guinea and the German Democratic Republic.

	Those who went to the airport to see him off included Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang and others. The Guinean Ambassador to China, Segou Kamara, and the East German Ambassador to China, Birbach, also went to the airport to see him off.

### 我政府贸易代表团团长离京  前往几内亚和德意志民主共和国访问

1968-05-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　我政府贸易代表团团长离京
    前往几内亚和德意志民主共和国访问
    新华社讯　中国政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长周化民四月三十日乘飞机离开北京，前往几内亚共和国和德意志民主共和国进行访问。
    到机场送行的有对外贸易部副部长李强等。几内亚共和国驻华大使塞古·卡马拉和德意志民主共和国驻华大使比尔巴赫，也到机场送行。
