Title: High Hold the Great Red Banner of Mao Zedong Thought, Invincible
Author:
Date: 1968-05-09
Edition: 3

The workers of the No. 8 open-hearth furnace, Class C shift, at the No. 1 Steel Plant of Anshan Steel and Iron Company have consistently adhered to the principle of putting Mao Zedong Thought in command, focusing on the revolution, and vigorously promoting production. Recently, they set a new record for the fastest steel smelting in the history of the plant.

This is a scene of them studying the "Quotations from Chairman Mao" at the furnace.
