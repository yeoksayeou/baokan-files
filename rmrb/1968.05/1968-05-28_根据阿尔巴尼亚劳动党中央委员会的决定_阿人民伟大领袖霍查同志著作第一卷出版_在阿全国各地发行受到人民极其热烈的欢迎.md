Title: The First Volume of the Works of Comrade Enver Hoxha, Great Leader of the Albanian People, Published According to the Decision of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour
Author:
Date: 1968-05-28
Edition: 5

The first volume of the works of Comrade Enver Hoxha, the great leader of the Albanian people, was published on May 18th and began to be distributed throughout Albania. It has been enthusiastically welcomed by the Albanian people nationwide.

The publication of Comrade Hoxha's works was decided by the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour. This year, the second volume of Comrade Hoxha's works will also be published. The first and second volumes will contain Comrade Hoxha's works from November 1941 to December 1944, during the period of the Albanian national liberation war.

### 根据阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会的决定  阿人民伟大领袖霍查同志著作第一卷出版  在阿全国各地发行受到人民极其热烈的欢迎

1968-05-28
第5版()
专栏：

    根据阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会的决定
   阿人民伟大领袖霍查同志著作第一卷出版
    在阿全国各地发行受到人民极其热烈的欢迎
    新华社地拉那二十六日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖霍查同志的著作第一卷五月十八日开始在阿尔巴尼亚全国各地发行，受到阿尔巴尼亚全国人民极其热烈的欢迎。
    霍查同志的著作是根据阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会的决定出版的。今年，还将出版霍查同志著作的第二卷。在第一、第二卷中收集了霍查同志从一九四一年十一月到一九四四年十二月阿尔巴尼亚民族解放战争时期的著作。
