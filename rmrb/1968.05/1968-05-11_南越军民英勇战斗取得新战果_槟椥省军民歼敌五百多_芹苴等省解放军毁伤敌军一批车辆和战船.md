Title: South Vietnamese Military and Civilians Achieve New Victories in Brave Battles
Author:
Date: 1968-05-11
Edition: 5

South Vietnamese military and civilians have achieved new victories in their brave battles. Military and civilians in the province of Binh Tuy annihilated over 500 enemy soldiers, while the Liberation Army in provinces like Kien Giang destroyed and damaged a batch of enemy vehicles and warships.

New China News Agency, Hanoi, May 10: According to the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, the military and civilians of Binh Tuy province in South Vietnam launched a fierce attack on the enemy on the 6th and 7th along the 6th Highway, achieving new victories.

According to preliminary reports, in this battle, the military and civilians of Binh Tuy province completely annihilated two main enemy battalions and one reconnaissance company, killing and wounding over 530 enemy soldiers, seizing more than 100 weapons, over 10 radios, and other military supplies.

While the Liberation Army was attacking the enemy, thousands of civilians rose up to destroy the 6th Highway and set up numerous obstacles, completely paralyzing the enemy's military transportation.

According to another report, the military and civilians of Phu My district in Binh Dinh province fought continuously for seven times in Nhon Hoa village on the 5th and 6th, annihilating four companies and two platoons of the enemy, including two companies of American troops. They destroyed and seized 28 tanks and armored vehicles, and shot down two helicopters.

New China News Agency, Hanoi, May 9: According to the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, the Liberation Army of Binh Duong province in South Vietnam bravely fought against the enemy on the rivers over three days from April 26 to 29, damaging four enemy warships and killing many American invaders.

The report said that on the morning of April 26, the Liberation Army of Binh Duong province damaged an enemy warship on the Long Tau River southeast of Saigon. Later, on the morning of the 27th, the Liberation Army again used heavy weapons to damage a 5,000-ton American military transport ship traveling from Saigon to Cat Lai, causing the ship to burn fiercely. On the afternoon of the 29th, the provincial Liberation Army bravely intercepted four American warships on the Dong Nai River. In just ten minutes of battle, the Liberation Army damaged two warships and killed many American soldiers.

According to another report, the Liberation Army of Quang Nam province attacked a company of American troops in Ky An commune, Thang Binh district on April 28, killing 60 enemy soldiers and destroying a batch of enemy weapons and military supplies. The provincial Liberation Army, seizing the victory, proactively attacked two companies of American troops heading to Ky An commune on May 1, killing over 50 enemy soldiers on the spot.

New China News Agency, Hanoi, May 10: According to the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, the people's armed forces of Kien Giang province in South Vietnam ambushed an enemy convoy on the 4th on the 4th Highway from Kien Giang to Soc Trang, destroying and damaging 26 military vehicles, killing and capturing over 100 enemy soldiers, and seizing many weapons and military supplies.

The report said that the enemy convoy, consisting of 40 military vehicles, was suddenly attacked by the Liberation Army when passing through the Long Tho area of Phuoc Long district. The enemy was caught off guard and unable to defend themselves. Many military vehicles were destroyed on the spot, and many enemy soldiers were killed in the vehicles. The remaining enemy soldiers fled in panic, and the Liberation Army immediately pursued and, after a short but fierce battle, achieved the aforementioned victories.

### 南越军民英勇战斗取得新战果  槟椥省军民歼敌五百多　芹苴等省解放军毁伤敌军一批车辆和战船

1968-05-11
第5版()
专栏：

    南越军民英勇战斗取得新战果
    槟椥省军民歼敌五百多　芹苴等省解放军毁伤敌军一批车辆和战船
    新华社河内十日电据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方槟椥省军民，从六日夜间到七日，在第六号公路上猛烈打击敌人，取得了新的战果。
    据初步消息，在这次战斗中，槟椥省军民全歼伪军两个主力营和一个侦察连，打死、打伤敌人五百三十多名，缴获武器一百多件、无线电报话机十多部以及其他军用物资。
    在解放军进攻敌人的同时，数以千计的人民群众奋起破坏第六号公路，并在公路上设置了许多障碍物，使敌人的军事运输完全瘫痪。
    另据报道，平定省符美县军民，五日和六日在新屋村连续战斗七次，歼敌四个连和两个排，其中包括美军两个连，击毁、缴获坦克和装甲车二十八辆，击落直升飞机两架。
    新华社河内九日电据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方边和省解放军，在四月二十六、二十七和二十九日的三天中，在河道上英勇打击敌人，共击伤美伪军的战船四艘，消灭美国侵略军多名。
    报道说，边和省解放军四月二十六日上午在西贡市东南的龙都河上击伤敌人的战船一艘。接着，解放军在二十七日上午又用重型武器击伤从西贡开往头顿的一艘载重五千吨的美国军用运输船，使这条船起火猛烈燃烧。二十九日下午，这个省的解放军在洞争河上还英勇截击四艘美国战船。仅经过十分钟的战斗，解放军就击伤战船两艘，消灭美军多名。
    另据报道，广南省解放军四月二十八日在三岐县岐安乡袭击一连美军，打死敌人六十名，击毁敌人武器和军用物资一批。这个省解放军乘胜前进，在五月一日又主动袭击前往岐安乡的两连美军，当场消灭敌人五十多名。
    新华社河内十日电据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方芹苴省人民解放武装力量，四日上午在从芹苴到朔庄的第四号公路上伏击美伪军的一个车队，一举击毁、击伤军车二十六辆，消灭和俘虏一百多名敌人，缴获许多武器和军用物资。
    报道说，这支由四十辆军车组成的敌军车队，在行经凤合县龙盛地区的时候，突然遭到了解放军的迎头痛击。敌人措手不及，无法招架，许多军车当场被击毁，许多敌人被打死在车上。残存的敌人慌忙逃窜，解放军立即乘胜追击，经过短时间的英勇战斗，取得了上述战果。
