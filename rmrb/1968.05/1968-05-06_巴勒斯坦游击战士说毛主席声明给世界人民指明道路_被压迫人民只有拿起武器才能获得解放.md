Title: Palestinian Guerrillas Say Chairman Mao's Statement Points the Way for the World's People
Author:
Date: 1968-05-06
Edition: 6

Palestinian guerrillas say Chairman Mao's statement points the way for the world's people

	The oppressed peoples can only gain liberation by taking up arms

	CAIRO, May 5 (Xinhua) -- Palestinian guerrilla fighter Abdul recently told Xinhua's correspondent here that Chairman Mao's statement supporting the struggle of American blacks against violence has shown the oppressed peoples of the world the correct path: to win their liberation, they must take up arms and fight.

	Abdul pointed out that the assassination of the non-violent advocate Martin Luther King by American imperialists proves that the path of non-violence is wrong. He said, "Chairman Mao teaches us that 'national struggles, in the final analysis, are a matter of class struggle.' Class struggle cannot be resolved through peaceful means. For revolutionary people, the only way to victory is to unite the people of their own country and wage armed struggle."

	Palestinian student Safian noted: Countless facts have proven the correctness of the great teacher Chairman Mao's brilliant thesis that "political power grows out of the barrel of a gun." The Palestinian people will continue to wage armed struggle until the liberation struggle achieves final victory.

	Safian said: "Mao Zedong Thought is the most powerful weapon. More and more Palestinian revolutionary people have come to understand that in the practice of revolutionary struggle, only by arming themselves with great Mao Zedong Thought can we achieve our liberation." He concluded with a shout: "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years!"

### 巴勒斯坦游击战士说毛主席声明给世界人民指明道路　被压迫人民只有拿起武器才能获得解放

1968-05-06
第6版()
专栏：

　　巴勒斯坦游击战士说毛主席声明给世界人民指明道路
　　被压迫人民只有拿起武器才能获得解放
    新华社开罗一日电　巴勒斯坦游击队员阿卜杜勒最近在这里对新华社记者说，毛主席支持美国黑人抗暴斗争的声明，为全世界被压迫人民指出了正确的道路：为了争取自己的解放，他们必须拿起武器进行斗争。
    阿卜杜勒指出，非暴力主义者马丁·路德·金被美帝国主义者暗杀一事证明，非暴力的道路是错误的。他说，毛主席教导我们说：“民族斗争，说到底，是一个阶级斗争问题。”阶级斗争通过和平的手段是不能解决的。对革命人民来说，取得胜利的唯一办法，是靠本国人民团结起来，进行武装斗争。
    巴勒斯坦学生索菲安指出：无数事实证明了伟大导师毛主席的“枪杆子里面出政权”这一光辉论断的正确性。我们巴勒斯坦人民将继续进行武装斗争，直到解放斗争取得最后的胜利。
    索菲安说：“毛泽东思想是威力最大的武器，越来越多的巴勒斯坦革命人民已经懂得，在革命斗争的实践中，只有用伟大的毛泽东思想武装自己，才能实现我们的解放。”他最后高呼：“毛泽东主席万岁！万万岁！”
