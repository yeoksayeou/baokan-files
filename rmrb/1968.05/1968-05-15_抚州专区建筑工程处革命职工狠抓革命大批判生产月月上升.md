Title: Revolutionary Workers of Fuzhou District Construction Engineering Bureau Intensify Revolutionary Criticism and Production Rises Monthly
Author:
Date: 1968-05-15
Edition: 2

The revolutionary committee of the Fuzhou District Construction Engineering Bureau in Jiangxi Province, following the great directive of Chairman Mao "to seize the revolution and promote production," has insisted on using revolution to lead production. By deeply carrying out revolutionary criticism, it has effectively promoted production. This unit's production value for the first quarter of this year has doubled compared to the same period last year, with production rising month by month.

The Construction Engineering Bureau is a large construction enterprise in the Fuzhou area. From May to August last year, a handful of capitalist roaders and class enemies within the Party sabotaged the efforts to seize the revolution and promote production, causing damage to production equipment. After the proletarian revolutionary faction took power, they closely followed Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment. They established Mao Zedong Thought study classes, seriously implemented Chairman Mao's latest instructions, combined revolutionary criticism with the unit's struggle, criticism, and transformation, and led the revolutionary masses in launching wave after wave of fierce attacks against the class enemies. They exposed the class enemies' plots to sabotage the revolution and production, raised the class consciousness of the revolutionary workers, and quickly turned the situation around, achieving victory in seizing the revolution and promoting production.

### 抚州专区建筑工程处革命职工狠抓革命大批判生产月月上升

1968-05-15
第2版()
专栏：

    抚州专区建筑工程处革命职工狠抓革命大批判生产月月上升
    江西抚州专区建筑工程处革命委员会遵照伟大领袖毛主席“抓革命，促生产”的伟大方针，坚持以革命统帅生产，深入开展革命大批判，有力地促进了生产。这个单位今年第一季度产值比去年同期增长了一倍，生产月月上升。
    建筑工程处是抚州地区一个大的建筑企业。去年五月到八月，党内一小撮走资派和阶级敌人破坏抓革命、促生产，使生产设备受到破坏。无产阶级革命派掌权以后，紧跟毛主席的伟大战略部署，大办毛泽东思想学习班，认真落实毛主席的最新指示，把革命大批判和本单位斗批改结合起来，带领革命群众向阶级敌人发动了一次又一次的猛烈进攻，揭露了阶级敌人破坏革命、破坏生产的阴谋，提高了革命职工的阶级觉悟，很快就扭转了局面，取得了抓革命促生产的胜利。
