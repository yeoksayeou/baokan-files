Title: Chinese Government Trade Delegation Head Returns to Beijing After Visiting Guinea and East Germany
Author:
Date: 1968-05-19
Edition: 6

Chinese Government Trade Delegation Head, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Zhou Huamin, has concluded his visit to the Republic of Guinea and the German Democratic Republic and returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 我政府贸易代表团长访几、德后回京

1968-05-19
第6版()
专栏：

    我政府贸易代表团长访几、德后回京
    新华社十八日讯　中国政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长周化民，结束了对几内亚共和国和德意志民主共和国的访问，今天下午乘飞机回到北京。
