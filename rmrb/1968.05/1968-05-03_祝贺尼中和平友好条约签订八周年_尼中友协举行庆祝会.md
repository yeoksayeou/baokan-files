Title: Celebrating the Eighth Anniversary of the Signing of the Sino-Nepalese Peace and Friendship Treaty
Author:
Date: 1968-05-03
Edition: 5

The Sino-Nepalese Friendship Association held a celebration on April 29 to mark the eighth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Nepalese Peace and Friendship Treaty. Over 400 people from various sectors of Nepal attended the event.

Purna Bahadur, Chairman of the Sino-Nepalese Friendship Association, spoke highly of the great achievements made by the Chinese people under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the friendship between the peoples of Nepal and China. He said that the great progress made by the Chinese people has inspired people all over the world. He added that the People's Republic of China has consistently provided cooperation and support to Asian, African, and Latin American countries in their struggles against imperialism and colonialism and for liberation.

When discussing the increasingly close friendship between Nepal and China, he said, "The friendly relations between Nepal and China are becoming stronger and more consolidated every day."

Kesher Bahadur, Chairman of the Nepal Public Works Committee, and other friends also spoke at the event, praising the deepening friendship between the peoples of Nepal and China and the friendly relations between the two countries.

Tou Guowei, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Nepal, spoke about the great victories achieved in China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution under the leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao, and praised the friendship between China and Nepal. A celebration was also held in Bhaktapur on the same day.

### 祝贺尼中和平友好条约签订八周年　尼中友协举行庆祝会

1968-05-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　祝贺尼中和平友好条约签订八周年
　　尼中友协举行庆祝会
    新华社加德满都电　尼泊尔中国友好协会四月二十九日晚举行庆祝会，热烈庆祝尼泊尔中国和平友好条约签订八周年。尼泊尔各界人士四百多人出席了庆祝会。
    尼中友好协会主席普尔纳·巴哈杜尔在讲话中热烈赞扬中国人民在毛主席领导下所取得的伟大成就和尼中两国人民的友谊。他说，中国人民取得的巨大进展，鼓舞了全世界人民。他接着说，中华人民共和国一贯给予反对帝国主义和殖民主义以及争取解放的亚非拉各国以合作和支援。
    他在谈到尼中两国之间日益增进的友谊时说：“尼中两国之间的友好关系正在一天天加强和巩固。”
    尼泊尔公共事业委员会主席凯谢尔·巴哈杜尔和其他朋友在会上发表讲话时，热烈赞扬尼中两国人民之间日益增进的友谊和两国之间的友好关系。
    中国驻尼泊尔大使馆临时代办屠国维在讲话时介绍了在伟大领袖毛主席领导下中国无产阶级文化大革命取得的伟大胜利，并赞扬了中尼两国之间的友谊。巴克塔普尔市在二十九日也举行了庆祝会。
