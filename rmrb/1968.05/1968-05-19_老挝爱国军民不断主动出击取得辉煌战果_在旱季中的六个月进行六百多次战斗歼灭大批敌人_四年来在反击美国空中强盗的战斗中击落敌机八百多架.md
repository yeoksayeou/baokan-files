Title: Lao Patriotic Military and People Achieve Glorious Victories with Proactive Strikes
Author:
Date: 1968-05-19
Edition: 6

The patriotic military and people of Laos have continuously launched proactive strikes and achieved glorious victories. In the six months of the dry season from 1967 to 1968, they fought over 600 battles nationwide, annihilating a large number of enemies.

According to incomplete statistics, from last November to this April, the six months of the dry season, the patriotic military and people of Laos fought 646 battles. They annihilated and disbanded 12,058 enemy personnel, killing 5,848, wounding 1,308, and capturing or forcing the surrender of 4,902. They completely annihilated eight enemy battalions and eleven companies, destroyed two regimental headquarters and seven battalion headquarters, severely damaged four regiments and thirty-six battalions, seized 4,096 weapons of various kinds, shot down or destroyed 97 enemy aircraft, sank 19 motorboats and patrol boats, occupied and blew up four bridges, destroyed nine ammunition depots, two oil depots, and three radar stations.

The patriotic military and people of Laos continue to expand the glorious victories achieved during the dry season, launching continuous attacks on various battlefields from Upper Laos to Lower Laos in April, annihilating a large number of enemies.

According to incomplete statistics, the patriotic military and people of Laos launched 155 attacks on the enemy in April, annihilating 1,245 enemy personnel. They also shot down one enemy aircraft, blew up three bridges, seized 413 weapons of various kinds and a large amount of military supplies, and forced the enemy to withdraw from many strongholds, liberating nearly 10,000 people.

In Upper Laos, the patriotic military and people of Xieng Khouang Province annihilated 394 enemy personnel this month, including 192 in battles at Ban Sathong and other places. The provincial patriotic armed forces also destroyed the nests of bandits raised by the Americans in Ban Sathong, Ban Phang, and other places, and forced a bandit company and 16 officers to surrender to the people.

In Lower Laos, the patriotic military and people of Saravane, Attopeu, Xam Neua, and Savannakhet provinces annihilated 457 enemy personnel this month and liberated thousands of residents from enemy rule. The patriotic armed forces in Lower Laos also cut off many sections of Route 13 and other main roads, causing great difficulties for enemy transportation. The patriotic armed forces in Savannakhet Province, in battles against enemy forces in the Song Khone, La Hang Nam, and Boua regions from April 14 to 24, routed two enemy battalions, with many enemy personnel killed or wounded.

The Patriotic Front of Laos radio station issued a commentary on May 15, enthusiastically hailing the glorious victories achieved by the patriotic military and people of Laos in April. The commentary pointed out that the great victories achieved by the patriotic military and people of Laos in April show that the more stubborn the U.S. imperialists and their lackeys are, the more severe the defeats they will suffer.

The commentary called on the patriotic armed forces of Laos to continue to take the initiative to strike the enemy, continue to expand the victories, and make this dry season a season of more glorious victories than any previous dry season.

The patriotic military and people of Laos have achieved great victories in their battles against American aggressor aircraft, shooting down 801 invading enemy aircraft from May 17, 1964, to April 30 of this year.

The Patriotic Front of Laos radio station issued a commentary on May 17, enthusiastically hailing the glorious victories achieved by the patriotic military and people of Laos in their battles against American air aggressors over the past four years.

The commentary said that the above victories embody the firm will of the patriotic military and people of Laos to dare to struggle and dare to win, and at the same time indicate the heavy defeat of U.S. imperialism.

The commentary said that the more the U.S. imperialists intensify their attacks on the liberated areas, the stronger the will of the patriotic military and people of Laos to fight to the end and win. Wherever the American air aggressors commit crimes, the patriotic military and people of Laos will give them the punishment they deserve there. The commentary called on the patriotic military and people of Laos to advance bravely, launch competitions, shoot down more American aggressor aircraft, and achieve new victories.

### 老挝爱国军民不断主动出击取得辉煌战果  在旱季中的六个月进行六百多次战斗歼灭大批敌人  四年来在反击美国空中强盗的战斗中击落敌机八百多架

1968-05-19
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国军民不断主动出击取得辉煌战果
    在旱季中的六个月进行六百多次战斗歼灭大批敌人
    四年来在反击美国空中强盗的战斗中击落敌机八百多架
    新华社康开十六日电　据巴特寮通讯社报道：老挝爱国军民在一九六七年——一九六八年的旱季中的六个月，在全国各地主动出击，取得了辉煌战果。
    据不完全统计，从去年十一月到今年四月的旱季中的六个月，老挝爱国军民进行了六百四十六次战斗，共歼灭和瓦解敌人一万二千零五十八名，其中打死五千八百四十八名，打伤一千三百零八名，俘虏和迫降四千九百零二名，全歼敌军八个营和十一个连，歼灭两个团的指挥部和七个营的指挥部，重创敌军四个团和三十六个营，缴获各种武器四千零九十六件，击落击毁敌机九十七架，击沉汽艇和机动艇十九艘，占领和炸毁桥梁四座，击毁弹药库九座、汽油库两座和雷达站三座。
    新华社康开十六日电　据巴特寮电台报道：老挝爱国军民继续扩大旱季以来取得的辉煌战果，四月份在从上寮到下寮的各个战场上，连续发动进攻，歼灭大批敌人。
    据不完全统计，老挝爱国军民四月份共袭击敌人一百五十五次，歼敌一千二百四十五名。爱国军民还击落敌机一架，炸毁桥梁三座，缴获各类武器四百十三件以及大批军用物资，并且迫使敌人撤出许多据点，使近万名人民获得了解放。
    在上寮，川圹省爱国军民在这个月里歼灭敌人三百九十四名，其中仅在山座等地的战斗中就歼敌一百九十二名。这个省的爱国武装力量还摧毁了美国在山座、川绵等地豢养的土匪的巢穴，并迫使一个土匪连以及十六名军官向人民投诚。
    在下寮，沙拉湾、阿速坡、甘蒙、沙湾拿吉等省的爱国军民在这个月里共歼敌四百五十七名，并使数千居民从敌人的统治下解放出来。下寮爱国军民还切断了十三号公路和其它公路干线的许多路段，给敌人的交通造成巨大困难。沙湾拿吉省爱国武装力量在从四月十四日到二十四日袭击松孔、拉哈南、布沙地区敌军的战斗中，打垮敌人两个营，大批敌军被打死或打伤。
    巴特寮电台十五日发表评论，热烈欢呼老挝爱国军民在四月份所取得的辉煌胜利。评论指出，老挝爱国军民在四月份取得的巨大胜利表明，美帝国主义及其走狗越是顽固，就越将遭受更加惨重的失败。
    评论号召老挝爱国武装力量继续主动地打击敌人，继续扩大战果，使这个旱季成为比以往任何一个旱季都取得更加辉煌胜利的旱季。
    新华社康开十八日电　据巴特寮电台广播，老挝爱国军民在反击美国强盗飞机的战斗中，取得了巨大的胜利，从一九六四年五月十七日到今年四月三十日，共击落入侵敌机八百零一架。
    巴特寮电台五月十七日发表评论，热烈欢呼老挝爱国军民四年来在反击美国空中强盗的战斗中取得的辉煌胜利。
    评论说，上述胜利，体现了老挝爱国军民敢于斗争，敢于胜利的坚强意志，同时表明了美帝国主义的惨重失败。
    评论说，美帝国主义越是加强袭击解放区，老挝爱国军民决战决胜的斗争意志就越坚强，美国空中强盗在那里行凶作恶，老挝爱国军民就在那里给它以应得的惩罚。评论号召老挝爱国军民奋勇前进，开展竞赛，击落更多的美国强盗飞机，夺取新的胜利。
