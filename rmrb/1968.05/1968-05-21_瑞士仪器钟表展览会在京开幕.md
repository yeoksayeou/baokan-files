Title: The Swiss Instrument and Watch Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-05-21
Edition: 6

The Swiss Instrument and Watch Exhibition opened in Beijing today. The honorary president of the exhibition delegation, Swiss Ambassador to China Rossetti, and Deputy Director of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade Zhang Huadong both spoke at the opening ceremony, expressing their hopes that the exhibition would further promote the development of trade between the two countries and mutual understanding between the peoples of the two countries.

Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, Deputy Minister of the First Light Industry Department Cao Lu, and other officials were invited to attend the opening ceremony. Envoys from various countries also attended the opening ceremony.

The China Council for the Promotion of International Trade held a reception yesterday to welcome the Swiss friends of the exhibition delegation.

### 瑞士仪器钟表展览会在京开幕

1968-05-21
第6版()
专栏：

    瑞士仪器钟表展览会在京开幕
    新华社二十日讯　瑞士仪器钟表展览会今天在北京开幕。展览团名誉团长、瑞士驻中国大使罗赛蒂，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主任张化东在开幕式上讲话，共祝展览会将进一步促进两国贸易的发展和两国人民之间的相互了解。
    对外贸易部副部长李强、第一轻工业部副部长曹鲁等，应邀出席了开幕式。各国驻中国使节也出席了开幕式。
    中国国际贸易促进委员会昨天举行了招待会，欢迎展览团的瑞士朋友。
