Title: Following Mao Zedong, the World Turns Red
Author:
Date: 1968-05-01
Edition: 5

The sun rises, illuminating the blue sky,
Red flags flutter, welcoming the east wind.
Mountains dance, seas sing,
The strong rhythm of the era shakes the heavens.

Ten thousand songs and melodies are dedicated to you,
Great teacher Mao Zedong!

You hold high the banner of Marxism-Leninism,
Pioneering through thorns and brambles!

You paint the grand blueprint of revolution,
The wind roars, thunder rolls, flames blaze!

Ten thousand songs and melodies are dedicated to you,
Great leader Mao Zedong!

You issue the marching orders,
Bravely charging towards the old world.

With a wave of your mighty arm, clouds and waters surge,
Binding the dragon across five continents and four seas!

Ten thousand songs and melodies are dedicated to you,
Great commander Mao Zedong!

You rouse the workers and peasants of the world,
Cannons roar in Asia, Africa, Latin America.

With a single command, heaven and earth shift,
A new world is born from the gun barrel!

Ten thousand songs and melodies are dedicated to you,
Great helmsman Mao Zedong!

You guide the revolutionary ship,
Navigating past countless reefs.

You blaze the trail to victory,
Turning the tide and sweeping away evil winds!

The sun rises, illuminating the blue sky,
Red flags flutter, welcoming the east wind.
Mountains dance, seas sing,
The strong rhythm of the era shakes the heavens:

Following Mao Zedong,
Revolution will triumph;
Following Mao Zedong,
The world turns red!

### 跟着毛泽东，世界红彤彤
红焰哨
1968-05-01
第5版()
专栏：

　　跟着毛泽东，世界红彤彤
　　红焰哨
　　旭日升起照碧空，
　　红旗漫卷迎东风。
　　高山起舞大海唱，
　　时代强音震苍穹。
　　千歌万曲献给您，
　　伟大的导师毛泽东！
　　马列主义的大旗您高举，
　　披荆斩棘打先锋！
　　革命宏图您描绘，
　　风鸣雷吼烈火熊！
　　千歌万曲献给您，
　　伟大的领袖毛泽东！
　　进军的号令您发布，
　　勇向旧世界猛冲锋。
　　您巨臂一挥云水怒，
　　五洲四海缚苍龙！
　　千歌万曲献给您，
　　伟大的统帅毛泽东！
　　全球工农您唤起，
　　亚非拉美炮声隆。
　　您一声号令天地转，
　　新世界诞生在枪杆子中！
　　千歌万曲献给您，
　　伟大的舵手毛泽东！
　　革命的巨船您引导，
　　绕过暗礁千万重。
　　胜利的道路您开辟，
　　力挽狂澜扫妖风！
　　旭日升起照碧空，
　　红旗漫卷迎东风。
　　高山起舞大海唱，
　　时代强音震苍穹：
　　跟着毛泽东，
　　革命就胜利；
　　跟着毛泽东，
　　世界红彤彤！
