Title: Celebrating the Day of Labor
Author:
Date: 1968-05-03
Edition: 6

Thousands of Tanzanian workers and residents of Dar es Salaam gathered for a mass rally on the morning of May 1 to celebrate International Labor Day.

The rally was organized by the Tanganyika Federation of Labor. Banners reading "Long Live International Labor Day," "Support the Workers and People of Zimbabwe and Vietnam," and others were displayed at the venue.

President Nyerere attended the rally and delivered a speech.

Kamaliza, the General Secretary of the Tanganyika Federation of Labor, called on Tanzanian workers to support the revolutionary struggles of the Vietnamese and Zimbabwean peoples during his speech at the rally.

Attendees included: Chairman Kazimoto of the Tanganyika Federation of Labor, government ministers, diplomatic envoys stationed in Tanzania, and representatives of African nationalist organizations in Dar es Salaam.

Bamako, May 1 (Xinhua) - Workers in the Malian capital of Bamako held a parade on the morning of May 1 to celebrate International Labor Day.

In his speech before the parade, President Modibo Keita congratulated Malian workers on their achievements in national economic construction. He urged the people to remain vigilant, safeguard the fruits of the revolution, and defend independence. Keita expressed his unwavering support for the struggles of the peoples of Vietnam, Zimbabwe, Portuguese Guinea, Angola, and Mozambique.

Famadi Sissoko, General Secretary of the Malian Federation of Labor, condemned the imperialist aggression in Vietnam in his speech. He expressed the active support of Malian workers for the struggle of the heroic Vietnamese people and the peoples of various African countries for national independence.

The crowd held up signs reading "U.S. imperialism out of Vietnam!" "Down with imperialism!" and others as they marched.

Brazzaville, May 2 (Xinhua) - The General Union of Workers of the Congo (Brazzaville) held a grand workers' rally and parade in Brazzaville on May 1 to celebrate International Labor Day.

President Massamba-Debat of the Congo (Brazzaville), Speaker of the National Assembly George Mouyabi, members of the Political Bureau of the National Revolutionary Movement, and government ministers attended the rally.

Paul Bondo, General Secretary of the General Union of Workers, called on Congolese workers in his speech at the rally to strive for the construction of a truly independent national economy in the Congo (Brazzaville).

Paul Bondo, on behalf of the Congolese workers, expressed resolute support for the just struggle of the heroic Vietnamese people against American aggression and for national salvation.

Paul Bondo emphasized that the just struggle of the Vietnamese people is the only way to defeat aggression and oppression and liberate the entire country. He expressed his "heartfelt congratulations to the recent brilliant victories achieved by the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, all patriots in South Vietnam, and the military and civilian people of North Vietnam under the wise leadership of President Ho Chi Minh."

At this moment, the entire audience shouted, "Victory to the Vietnamese people! Defeat to American imperialism!"

Paul Bondo also expressed full support for the peoples of other countries in their struggles, saying, "Wherever there is interference by U.S. imperialism and its accomplices, there will be resistance and a people's war for liberation. Just as the people's war in Vietnam, the people's wars in Zimbabwe, Angola, Portuguese Guinea, and Mozambique are all wars for liberation and should receive our full support."

### 庆祝劳动人民的节日  坦桑、马里和刚果（布）工人分别集会

1968-05-03
第6版()
专栏：

　　庆祝劳动人民的节日
    坦桑、马里和刚果（布）工人分别集会
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆一日电　坦桑尼亚工人和达累斯萨拉姆市民数千人一日上午举行群众大会，庆祝“五一”国际劳动节。
    大会是由坦噶尼喀全国工人联合会组织的。会场上悬挂着“国际劳动节万岁”、“声援津巴布韦和越南的工人和人民”等标语。
    尼雷尔总统出席了大会，并且讲了话。
    坦噶尼喀全国工人联合会总书记卡马利扎在会上讲话时，号召坦桑尼亚工人支持越南人民和津巴布韦人民的革命斗争。
    出席大会的还有：坦噶尼喀全国工人联合会主席卡齐莫托，政府部长。驻坦桑尼亚的外交使节，以及非洲民族主义组织驻达累斯萨拉姆的代表也出席了大会。
    新华社巴马科一日电马里首都巴马科工人一日上午举行游行，庆祝“五一”国际劳动节。
    莫迪博·凯塔总统在游行前的讲话中祝贺马里工人在国家经济建设中所取得的成就。他号召人民保持警惕，保卫革命果实，保卫独立。凯塔总统表示始终如一地声援越南、津巴布韦、“葡属”几内亚、安哥拉和莫三鼻给人民的斗争。
    马里全国工人联合会总书记法马迪·西索科在讲话中谴责帝国主义者在越南进行的侵略战争。他代表马里工人对英雄的越南人民和非洲各国人民争取民族独立的斗争表示积极的支持。
    群众高举写着“美帝国主义从越南滚出去！”“打倒帝国主义！”等标语牌举行了游行。
    新华社布拉柴维尔二日电　刚果（布）工会联合会一日在布拉柴维尔举行了盛大的工人集会和游行，庆祝“五一”国际劳动节。
    刚果（布）总统马桑巴—代巴、国民议会议长乔治·穆亚比、全国革命运动政治局委员和政府部长出席了大会。
    工会联合会总书记保尔·邦杜在庆祝大会上讲话时号召刚果（布）工人，为建设刚果（布）真正独立的民族经济而努力。
    保尔·邦杜代表刚果（布）工人，对英勇的越南人民的抗美救国正义斗争，表示坚决的支持。
    保尔·邦杜强调指出，越南人民的正义斗争是越南人民战胜侵略和压迫，解放全部国土的唯一办法。他表示“热烈祝贺越南南方民族解放阵线、越南南方所有的爱国者以及在胡志明主席英明领导下的越南北方的军民最近所取得的辉煌胜利。”
    这时，全场高呼：“越南人民必胜！”“美帝必败！”
    保尔·邦杜还表示全力支持斗争中的其他各国人民，他说：“那里有美帝国主义者及其同伙的干涉，那里就会进行抵抗和掀起争取解放的人民战争。同越南所进行的人民战争一样，津巴布韦、安哥拉、‘葡属’几内亚和莫三鼻给的人民战争也都是争取解放的战争，都应该得到我们的全力支持。”
