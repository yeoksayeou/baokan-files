Title: Vietnamese Water Resources Survey Team Returns to Their Country
Author:
Date: 1968-05-27
Edition: 6

The Vietnamese Water Resources Survey Team led by the Director of the Water Resources and Dike Management Bureau of the Ministry of Water Resources of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Yang Yuwu, has completed their survey in our country and has already left China to return to their country.

### 越南水利考察团回国

1968-05-27
第6版()
专栏：

    越南水利考察团回国
    新华社二十四日讯　由团长、越南民主共和国水利部堤防局局长杨玉武率领的越南水利考察团，结束了在我国的考察，已经离开中国回国。
