Title: Gate and Us Together
Author: Shi Hui
Date: 1968-05-31
Edition: 4

Gate's heroic deeds,
Spread across the country's mountains and rivers,
Countless people open their throats,
Singing praises for the hero.
You climbed the peak of "loyalty,"
A broad chest can hold the entire globe;
You are infinitely loyal to Chairman Mao's revolutionary line,
Truly mastering Mao Zedong Thought.
In the great waves of class struggle,
You stand tall with your chest out;
In the treacherous journey of revolution,
You never leave our great helmsman!
For the revolution, you dare to brave fire and water,
Holding a deep grudge against the enemy;
The People's Liberation Army should support the broad masses of the left,
You raise the Red Book and join the battle!
"Firmly support the left!"
Steel oaths written around the gun holster.
Cut off the evil winds, resist the reverse currents,
Never let the class enemy seize the reins!
With Mao Zedong Thought guiding everything,
The beating pulse never stops;
For Chairman Mao's revolutionary line, charge into battle,
Your precious blood was not shed in vain.
Thinking of you, hero Gate,
The body is filled with strength;
Thinking of you, hero Gate,
Great difficulties bow before us.
The eagle of the Qinghai Plateau soars,
The pine tree on the snow-capped mountain stands tall,
Hero Gate is with us,
Fighting bravely to liberate all mankind!

### 门合和我们在一起
时辉
1968-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

    门合和我们在一起
    时辉
    门合的英雄事迹，
    传遍祖国山河，
    多少人放开喉咙，
    为英雄高唱赞歌。
    你攀上“忠”字的高峰，
    宽阔的心胸能装下整个地球；
    你无限忠于毛主席革命路线，
    真正把毛泽东思想学到了手。
    在阶级斗争的大风浪里，
    你高高地挺起胸膛；
    在艰险密布的革命航程中，
    你寸步不离我们伟大的舵手！
    为革命敢于赴汤蹈火，
    对敌人怀着刻骨深仇；
    人民解放军应该支持左派广大群众，
    你高举红书投入战斗！
    “坚定不移地支持左派！”
    钢铁的誓言写在枪套的四周。
    斩断妖风，顶住逆流，
    决不能让阶级敌人将印把子夺走！
    用毛泽东思想统帅一切，
    跳动的脉搏就没有停止的时候；
    为毛主席革命路线冲锋陷阵，
    你珍贵的鲜血没有白流！
    一想起你英雄门合，
    浑身就充满了力量；
    一想起你英雄门合，
    天大的困难也在我们面前低头。
    青海高原上的雄鹰展翅飞翔，
    雪山顶上的青松高高地昂起头，
    英雄门合和我们在一起，
    为解放全人类英勇战斗！
