Title: Liberation Army of South Vietnam Launches Continuous Attacks on American Puppet Forces in Dong Ha and Other Areas; People and Soldiers in Quang Nam, Tuyen Duc, and Other Provinces Counterattack American Puppet "Sweeping" Operations, Inflicting Heavy Casualties on the Enemy
Author:
Date: 1968-05-04
Edition: 5

The Liberation Army of South Vietnam has launched continuous and fierce attacks on American puppet forces in Dong Ha and other areas. The people and soldiers in Quang Nam, Tuyen Duc, and other provinces have counterattacked the American puppet "sweeping" operations, inflicting heavy casualties on the enemy.

New China News Agency, May 3rd: According to reports from Saigon, the armed forces of the people's liberation in the provinces of Quang Tri and Thua Thien in South Vietnam have, in recent days, launched fierce attacks on the enemy in the Dong Ha area of Quang Tri Province and the A Shau Valley of Thua Thien Province, causing heavy casualties to the American invading forces.

Since April 29th, the armed forces of the people's liberation in Quang Tri Province have continuously bombarded the important American base in Dong Ha, south of the demilitarized zone, with fierce artillery fire. They have also fiercely attacked the American invading forces around Dong Ha, causing heavy casualties. According to incomplete figures released by Western news agencies, from April 29th to May 2nd, nearly nine hundred American puppet forces were killed or wounded, with the majority being American invading forces. On May 2nd alone, the armed forces of the people's liberation eliminated more than five hundred American puppet forces. The power station of the American base in Dong Ha was destroyed by the artillery fire of the people's liberation forces on April 29th. The important waterway, the Vinh River, has been continuously bombarded, making it impossible for American supply ships to navigate.

In the A Shau Valley west of Hue, the invading American puppet forces also suffered heavy blows from the armed forces of the people's liberation. Western news agencies reported that by April 29th, the number of American invading forces killed or wounded had exceeded five hundred, and more than fifty American aircraft had been shot down or damaged.

According to a report from the New China News Agency in Hanoi, the armed forces of the people's liberation in the central region and surrounding areas of Saigon in South Vietnam have, since early April, continuously counterattacked the enemy's "sweeping" operations and attacked some of the enemy's bases and outposts, eliminating a large number of enemy forces.

In the first ten days of April, the people and soldiers of Binh Dinh Province continuously attacked the enemy's bases, transportation lines, and outposts in Suoi Phu, Phu My, Phong Nhat, Hoa Ninh, Hoa An, and Binh An, eliminating and capturing more than one thousand one hundred and sixteen enemy forces, including five hundred and twelve American invading forces and three hundred South Korean mercenary forces. They also destroyed eighty-eight military vehicles and fourteen cannons, shot down or destroyed eighteen aircraft, burned down two fuel depots, and one ammunition depot.

From April 18th to 20th, the armed forces of the people's liberation in Dien Ban County, Quang Nam Province, bravely repelled the enemy's "sweeping" operations, eliminating more than three hundred enemy forces. The guerrilla forces and local troops in other parts of Quang Nam Province launched continuous attacks on April 15th, 16th, 17th, and 24th, eliminating more than two hundred and fifty American puppet forces and South Korean mercenary forces, destroying three armored vehicles, and seizing a large number of weapons and military supplies.

From April 23rd to 26th, the liberation army in the northern part of the Khe Sanh battlefield in Quang Tri Province defeated the American invading forces' multiple counterattacks, continuously bombarding the enemy command posts and artillery positions on Hill 522 and Hill 689, and ambushing the enemy on Highway 9.

On April 2nd, the local troops and guerrilla forces in Lai Thieu County, Tuyen Duc Province, cooperated to repel the enemy's "sweeping" operation of one battalion, annihilating one enemy company and heavily damaging another. On the 8th, the guerrilla forces intercepted the enemy, annihilating one company and one reconnaissance platoon of the puppet forces. In the first twelve days of April, the people and soldiers in the two counties of Duong Minh Chau and Bien Hoa eliminated more than three hundred and seventy American invading forces, destroyed or damaged sixty-nine tanks and armored vehicles, and shot down or destroyed twenty aircraft.

In the first two days of April, the guerrilla forces and local troops in Binh Dinh Province eliminated nearly one hundred and sixty American forces and one platoon of puppet forces. On April 17th, the guerrilla forces in the northern part of the new river area of Tan Uyen County eliminated one hundred and five enemy forces and shot down two helicopters while counterattacking the American puppet "sweeping" operations.

### 南越解放军连续猛攻东河等地美伪军  广南土龙木等省军民反击美伪军“扫荡”，重创敌人

1968-05-04
第5版()
专栏：

　　南越解放军连续猛攻东河等地美伪军
    广南土龙木等省军民反击美伪军“扫荡”，重创敌人
    新华社三日讯　西贡消息：越南南方广治、承天两省人民解放武装力量，近几天来连续在广治省东河地区和承天省阿绍河谷地区猛烈打击敌人，使美国侵略军遭到惨重伤亡。
    从四月二十九日以来，广治省人民解放武装力量连续以猛烈的炮火轰击非军事区以南的美军重要基地东河，并且在东河周围狠打美国侵略军，使敌人伤亡累累。据西方通讯社透露的不完全数字，从四月二十九日到五月二日的四天中，美伪军被打死打伤近九百人，其中大部分是美国侵略军。五月二日一天中，人民解放武装力量就消灭了美伪军五百多名。东河美军基地的发电站在四月二十九日被人民解放武装力量的炮火摧毁。这个基地的重要水运交通线越门河由于不断遭到炮轰，美国的补给船只已无法在这条河上通行。
    在顺化西南的阿绍河谷地区，进犯的美伪军也遭到了人民解放武装力量的沉重打击。西方通讯社透露说，到四月二十九日，美国侵略军的死伤已超过五百名，美国飞机被打落打伤五十多架。
    据新华社河内电  据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方中部中区和西贡周围一些省的人民解放武装力量，从四月初以来连续反击出动“扫荡”的敌人，并且袭击敌军的一些基地和据点，消灭了敌人许多有生力量。
    平定省军民在四月份头十天中连续进攻绥福、符美、蓬山、怀仁、怀恩和平溪等地的敌军基地、交通线和据点，消灭和俘虏敌人一千一百一十六名，其中包括美国侵略军五百一十二名和南朝鲜帮凶军三百名，击毁军车八十八辆和大炮十四门，击落击毁飞机十八架，烧毁汽油库两座和弹药库一座。
    广南省奠盘县人民解放武装力量，从四月十八日到二十日，英勇粉碎了敌人的“扫荡”，共消灭敌人三百多名。广南省其他地方的游击队和地方部队在四月十五日、十六日、十七日和二十四日四天中连续出击，消灭美伪军和南朝鲜帮凶军二百五十多名，击毁装甲车三辆，缴获许多武器和军用物资。
    广治省北部溪山战场解放军在四月二十三日到二十六日四天中打败了美国侵略军的多次反扑，接连炮击五二二和六八九高地的敌军指挥所和炮兵阵地，并且在第九号公路伏击敌人。
    土龙木省莱眺县地方部队同游击队配合，在四月二日粉碎了伪军一个营的“扫荡”，全歼敌人一个连，重创一个连。八日，游击队又截击敌军，全歼伪军一个连和一个侦察排。这个省油汀和边葛两县军民在四月份头十二天中消灭了美国侵略军三百七十多名，击毁击伤坦克和装甲车六十九辆，击落击毁飞机二十架。
    边和省游击队和地方部队，在四月二日和四日两天中，消灭美军近一百六十名和伪军一个排。这个省新渊县北部地区的游击队，四月十七日在反击美伪军“扫荡”的战斗中，消灭敌人一百零五名，击落直升飞机二架。
