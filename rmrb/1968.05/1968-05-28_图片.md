Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Meets with Nepalese Vice Premier and Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1968-05-28
Edition: 2

On May 27, Premier Zhou Enlai received Nepalese Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Kirtinidhi Bista and his entourage, including Secretary for Foreign Affairs Yadu Nath Khanal. The photograph shows Premier Zhou Enlai with Vice Premier Bista (front row, third from the left) and his entourage. Photographed by our reporter.
