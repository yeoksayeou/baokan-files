Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Eight U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-05-06
Edition: 6

	North Vietnamese military and civilians have shot down eight U.S. aircraft.

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, May 4th: According to the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, on the night of April 30th, the people's liberation armed forces of Quang Tri Province in South Vietnam launched a fierce attack on the U.S. military's armored vehicle parking lot in the Upper She region, six kilometers southeast of Quang Tri City. They destroyed 45 military vehicles, most of which were M-41 tanks and M-113 armored personnel carriers, killing and wounding over 300 U.S. aggressor troops.

	The report said that after the fierce artillery bombardment, the liberation army quickly infiltrated the enemy's parking lot, using grenades and hand grenades to blow up one tank and armored vehicle after another. At the same time, another detachment of the liberation army directly attacked the enemy's barracks, catching the enemy off guard. After a short but intense battle, the liberation army completely annihilated the command of this armored vehicle unit, killing and wounding 270 enemy troops.

	Subsequently, the enemy dispatched multiple military vehicles from La Vang and Hai Lang to provide reinforcements, which were immediately intercepted and fiercely attacked by the liberation army. Two enemy-occupied military vehicles were destroyed.

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, May 5th: According to information released here, the military and civilians of Nghe An Province in North Vietnam shot down three invading U.S. aggressor aircraft on May 4th.

	On the same day, the military and civilians of Hanoi shot down one U.S. unmanned reconnaissance aircraft.

	New China News Agency, Hanoi: According to information released here, from May 1st to the morning of May 3rd, the military and civilians of North Vietnam shot down four U.S. aircraft in their counterattacks against U.S. air aggressors.

	In the morning of May 3rd, the militia of Ha Tinh Province shot down one U.S. aircraft. On May 2nd, the military and civilians of Vinh City in Nghe An Province shot down two U.S. aircraft. On May 1st, the military and civilians of White Dragon Tail Island also shot down one U.S. aircraft.

### 越南北方军民又击落美机八架

1968-05-06
第6版()
专栏：

　　越南北方军民又击落美机八架
    新华社河内四日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方广治省人民解放武装力量四月三十日凌晨猛烈袭击广治市东南六公里上舍地区的美军装甲车停车场，一举击毁军车四十五辆，其中大部分是M—41型坦克和M—113型装甲车，打死打伤美国侵略军三百多名。
    报道说，在猛烈的炮火袭击之后，解放军迅速地插入敌人的停车场，用手雷和手榴弹炸毁一辆又一辆坦克和装甲车。同时，另一路解放军直接攻打敌军营房，打得敌人措手不及。经过短时间的激战，解放军就全歼这个装甲车单位的指挥部，共打死打伤敌人二百七十名。
    随后，敌人从拉汪和海陵出动多辆军车前往救援，当即遭到解放军的猛烈截击，两辆满载敌军的军车被击毁。
    新华社河内五日电　据这里公布，越南北方义安省军民四日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机三架。
    同一天，河内军民击落美国无人驾驶侦察机一架。
    新华社河内电　据这里公布，越南北方军民从五月一日到三日上午，在反击美国空中强盗的战斗中，击落美机四架。
    三日上午，广平省民兵击落美机一架。二日，义安省荣市军民击落美机两架。一日，白龙尾岛的军民也击落美机一架。
