Title: Puzhen Vehicle Factory Leads Production with Revolution
Author:
Date: 1968-05-27
Edition: 2

Puzhen Vehicle Factory in Nanjing has seen its proletarian revolutionary factions and revolutionary masses, with the assistance of the People's Liberation Army Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Team stationed at the factory, vigorously seize class struggle. They have been deeply and persistently carrying out a revolutionary big-character poster campaign, leading production with revolution. The entire factory has achieved a great situation of dual victories in revolution and production. Currently, revolutionary committees have been established in all primary units throughout the factory. From January to April this year, the factory has consecutively overfulfilled the state production plan, with the monthly production of passenger cars reaching a record high since the factory's establishment. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 浦镇车辆厂以革命统帅生产  月月超额完成国家生产计划

1968-05-27
第2版()
专栏：

    浦镇车辆厂以革命统帅生产  月月超额完成国家生产计划
    南京浦镇车辆厂的无产阶级革命派和革命群众，在驻厂解放军毛泽东思想宣传队的帮助下，狠抓阶级斗争，深入持久地开展革命大批判，以革命统帅生产，全厂出现了革命、生产双胜利的大好形势。目前，全厂各个基层单位都建立了革命委员会。今年一月到四月又都超额完成了国家生产计划，客车月产创造了建厂以来最高纪录。（据新华社讯）
