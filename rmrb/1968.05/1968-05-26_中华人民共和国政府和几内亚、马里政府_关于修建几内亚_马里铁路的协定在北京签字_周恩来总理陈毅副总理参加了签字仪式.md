Title: Agreement on the Construction of the Guinea-Mali Railway Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-05-26
Edition: 5

The agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China, the Government of the Republic of Guinea, and the Government of the Republic of Mali on the construction of the Guinea-Mali Railway was signed in Beijing on the evening of May 24.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi attended the signing ceremony.

Vice Premier Li Xiannian, Mali's Minister of Foreign Affairs Ousmane Ba, and Guinea's Minister of Foreign Affairs Lansana Beavogui signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments.

Members of the Guinea-Mali joint friendly delegation visiting China included: Kassory Bangoura, Sekou Camara, Alassane Traore, Moussa Coulibaly, Tidiani Traore, and Mamadou Ba.

From the Chinese side, those who attended the signing ceremony included Ji Pengfei, Xie Huai-de, Guo Lu, and others.

### 中华人民共和国政府和几内亚、马里政府  关于修建几内亚—马里铁路的协定在北京签字  周恩来总理陈毅副总理参加了签字仪式

1968-05-26
第5版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国政府和几内亚、马里政府
    关于修建几内亚—马里铁路的协定在北京签字
    周恩来总理陈毅副总理参加了签字仪式
    新华社二十五日讯  中华人民共和国政府和几内亚共和国政府、马里共和国政府关于修建几内亚—马里铁路的协定，二十四日晚在北京签字。
    国务院总理周恩来，副总理陈毅参加了签字仪式。
    国务院副总理李先念和马里外交部长乌斯曼·巴、几内亚外交部长兰萨纳·贝阿沃吉，分别代表本国政府在协定上签字。
    参加签字仪式的几内亚、马里联合访华友好代表团成员有：卡索里·邦古拉、塞古·卡马拉、阿拉萨纳·特拉奥雷、姆萨·库里巴利、提迪亚尼·特拉奥雷、马马杜·巴。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，还有姬鹏飞、谢怀德、郭鲁等。
