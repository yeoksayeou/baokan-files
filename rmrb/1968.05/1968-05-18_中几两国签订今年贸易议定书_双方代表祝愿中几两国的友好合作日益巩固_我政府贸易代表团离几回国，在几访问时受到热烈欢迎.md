Title: China and Guinea Sign 1968 Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1968-05-18
Edition: 6

China and Guinea Sign 1968 Trade Agreement

Both Sides' Representatives Wish for Strengthened Friendly Cooperation Between China and Guinea

Chinese Government Trade Delegation Leaves Guinea, Received Warm Welcome During Visit

NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY, CONAKRY, May 15th - The governments of China and Guinea signed the 1968 trade agreement between the two countries here on the 14th. The agreement was signed by Zhou Huamin, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade and head of the Chinese government trade delegation, and Keta Enfama, Minister of Trade of Guinea, representing their respective governments.

Attending the signing ceremony were Mori Keta, Guinea's State Secretary in charge of transportation, Lin Bingnan, acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Guinea, and the entire Chinese government trade delegation.

At the signing ceremony, both Keta Enfama and Zhou Huamin spoke. They unanimously praised the sincere and friendly cooperation between the peoples of China and Guinea, and wished for the further consolidation and development of the combat friendship between the peoples of China and Guinea in their common struggle against imperialism and old and new colonialism, and for the friendly cooperation between the two countries.

Keta Enfama also, on behalf of President Sekou Toure, the government, and the people of Guinea, expressed heartfelt thanks to the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao, and to the government and people of China.

That evening, Lin Bingnan, acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Guinea, held a reception for the Chinese government trade delegation. Ismail Dur, Minister of Economic Development of Guinea, Keta Enfama, Minister of Trade, and other high-ranking officials of the Guinea government attended the reception.

According to the New China News Agency, Conakry, May 16th - The Chinese government trade delegation, led by Zhou Huamin, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, left Conakry by plane on the 15th after a friendly visit to the Republic of Guinea.

Many high-ranking officials of the Guinea government were at the airport to see off the Chinese delegation. The main officials present included: Ismail Dur, Minister of Economic Development, Keta Enfama, Minister of Trade, Mori Keta, State Secretary in charge of transportation, Bari Sori, State Secretary in charge of postal services, and Fofana Karim, State Secretary in charge of public works.

Lin Bingnan, acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Guinea, was also at the airport to see off the delegation.

During their visit to Guinea, the Chinese delegation was warmly welcomed wherever they went.

On the 12th, when the Chinese government trade delegation visited Kindia, hundreds of workers, farmers, students, and local residents held a gathering to welcome the delegation.

Haji Tierno Bah, the administrative head of Kindia, spoke at the gathering, enthusiastically praising the sincere, selfless, and effective assistance provided by the government and people of China to the people of Guinea.

He emphasized that the People's Republic of China is a great country, and the Chinese people are a great people. The people of Guinea are honored to have the Chinese people as their most sincere and reliable friends.

He repeatedly shouted with the attendees, "Long live the People's Republic of China!" "Long live Chairman Mao!"

On the evening of the 13th, Mori Keta, Guinea's State Secretary in charge of transportation, held a reception for the Chinese government trade delegation in Conakry. Many high-ranking officials of the Guinea government attended the reception.

### 中几两国签订今年贸易议定书  双方代表祝愿中几两国的友好合作日益巩固  我政府贸易代表团离几回国，在几访问时受到热烈欢迎

1968-05-18
第6版()
专栏：

    中几两国签订今年贸易议定书
    双方代表祝愿中几两国的友好合作日益巩固
    我政府贸易代表团离几回国，在几访问时受到热烈欢迎
    新华社科纳克里十五日电　中国和几内亚两国政府十四日在这里签订了两国之间一九六八年贸易议定书。中国政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长周化民和几内亚贸易部部长凯塔·恩法马拉分别代表两国政府在上述议定书上签了字。
    出席签字仪式的有几内亚负责运输的国务秘书莫里·凯塔以及中国驻几内亚大使馆临时代办林秉南和中国政府贸易代表团全体团员。
    在签字仪式上，凯塔·恩法马拉和周化民都讲了话。他们一致热烈颂扬了中几两国人民之间真诚友好的合作，并且祝愿中几两国人民在反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义的共同斗争中所结成的战斗友谊和两国之间的友好合作日益巩固和发展。
    凯塔·恩法马拉还代表塞古·杜尔总统、几内亚政府和人民，向中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席，向中国政府和人民表示衷心的感谢。
    当晚，中国驻几内亚大使馆临时代办林秉南为中国政府贸易代表团举行了招待会。几内亚经济发展部部长伊斯梅尔·杜尔，贸易部长凯塔·恩法马拉以及几内亚政府其他高级官员出席了招待会。
    据新华社科纳克里十六日电　由中国对外贸易部副部长周化民率领的中国政府贸易代表团，在对几内亚共和国进行了友好访问后，十五日乘飞机离开科纳克里回国。
    中国代表团离开时，几内亚政府许多高级官员在机场热烈欢送。其中主要有：经济发展部部长伊斯梅尔·杜尔，贸易部部长凯塔·恩法马拉，负责运输的国务秘书莫里·凯塔，负责邮电的国务秘书巴里·索里，负责公共工程的国务秘书福法纳·卡里姆。
    中国驻几内亚大使馆临时代办林秉南也到机场欢送。
    中国政府贸易代表团在几内亚访问期间，所到之处，都受到了热烈欢迎。
    十二日，当中国政府贸易代表团访问金迪亚时，几百名工人、农民、学生以及当地居民集会欢迎代表团。
    金迪亚行政区长官哈吉·蒂埃尔诺·巴赫，在会上讲话时热烈赞扬中国政府和人民给予几内亚人民的真诚、无私和有效的援助。
    他强调指出：中华人民共和国是一个伟大的国家，中国人民是伟大的人民，几内亚人民为有中国人民作为最真诚和最可靠的朋友而感到荣幸。
    他多次和与会的人一起热情地高呼：“中华人民共和国万岁！”“毛泽东主席万岁！”
    十三日晚，几内亚负责运输的国务秘书莫里·凯塔在科纳克里为中国政府贸易代表团举行招待会，几内亚政府的许多高级官员出席了招待会。
