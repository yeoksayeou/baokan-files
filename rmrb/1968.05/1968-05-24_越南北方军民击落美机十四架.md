Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down 14 U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-05-24
Edition: 6

North Vietnamese military and civilians shoot down 14 U.S. aircraft.

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, May 22 — It is announced here that the military and civilians of Nghe An Province in North Vietnam shot down one invading U.S. aggressor aircraft on May 20. Previously, on May 19 and 18, the military and civilians of this province shot down two U.S. aircraft.

	The military and civilians of Quang Binh Province shot down one U.S. aggressor aircraft each on May 17 and 19.

	The military and civilians of the Vinh Linh area also shot down one U.S. aggressor aircraft on May 18.

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, May 23 — It is announced here that the military and civilians of Nghe An Province in North Vietnam shot down three invading U.S. aggressor aircraft on May 22, and the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province shot down two U.S. aircraft on May 21 and 22.

	According to a supplementary report, the military and civilians of Nghe An and Quang Binh Provinces have recently shot down three more U.S. aircraft.

### 越南北方军民击落美机十四架

1968-05-24
第6版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机十四架
    新华社河内二十二日电　这里公布，越南北方义安省军民二十日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。在这以前，这个省军民在十九日和十八日曾击落美机二架。
    广平省军民十七日和十九日各击落美国强盗飞机一架。
    永灵地区军民十八日也击落美国强盗飞机一架。
    新华社河内二十三日电　据这里公布，越南北方义安省军民二十二日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机三架，广平省军民二十一日和二十二日共击落美机二架。
    另据补充报道，义安省和广平省军民最近还击落美机三架。
