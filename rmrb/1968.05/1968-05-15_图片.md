Title: Workers of Qingdao Cotton Mill No. 3 Raise Class Consciousness and Production
Author:
Date: 1968-05-15
Edition: 2

The revolutionary workers of Qingdao Cotton Mill No. 3 have significantly raised their class consciousness and awareness of line struggle through the revolutionary criticism movement. This has ignited a high tide of enthusiasm for labor. They have channeled their boundless revolutionary spirit into production, resolutely carrying out the great leader Chairman Mao's instruction to "ensure the production of cloth." As a result, they have consistently and comprehensively overfulfilled the state plan month after month.
