Title: Long Live Chairman Mao Forever
Author: Nán Tiě Jūn
Date: 1968-05-10
Edition: 2

	May 8th is the most unforgettable day,
	The Great Hall of the People is brightly lit.
	Revolutionary warriors gather together,
	Our hearts surge higher than the waves of the Yangtze River.
	The voices of the Little Red Book shake the heavens and the earth,
	Red treasures gather like an ocean.
	Great leader Chairman Mao,
	Strides forward to my side.
	My blood boils like a tide,
	My revolutionary will is stronger than steel.
	I vow to defend Chairman Mao,
	Even if it means facing mountains of knives and seas of fire!

### 敬祝毛主席万寿无疆
南铁军
1968-05-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　敬祝毛主席万寿无疆
　　南铁军
　　五月八日最难忘，
　　人大会堂灯辉煌。
　　革命战士聚一起，
　　心潮高过长江浪。
　　语录歌声震天地，
　　红色宝书汇海洋。
　　伟大领袖毛主席，
　　健步走近我身旁。
　　浑身热血如潮涌，
　　革命意志比钢强。
　　誓死捍卫毛主席，
　　刀山火海也敢闯！
