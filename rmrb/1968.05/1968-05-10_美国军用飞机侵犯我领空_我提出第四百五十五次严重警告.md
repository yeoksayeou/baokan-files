Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Violates Our Airspace - We Issue the 455th Severe Warning
Author:
Date: 1968-05-10
Edition: 5

On May 5th, from 5:39 to 6:04 AM, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into our airspace over the southeastern region of Hainan Island, Guangdong Province. On May 6th, from 11:06 AM to 11:55 AM, another U.S. military aircraft intruded into our airspace over the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. In response to this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the ministry, has issued the 455th severe warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵犯我领空　我提出第四百五十五次严重警告

1968-05-10
第5版()
专栏：

　　美国军用飞机侵犯我领空
　　我提出第四百五十五次严重警告
    新华社九日讯　五月五日五时三十九分至六时零四分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省海南岛东南地区领空。六日十一时六分至十一时五十五分美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛地区领空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百五十五次严重警告。
