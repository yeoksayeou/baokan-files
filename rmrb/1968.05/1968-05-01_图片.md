Title: Great Teacher Chairman Mao and His Close Comrade-in-Arms Vice Chairman Lin
Author:
Date: 1968-05-01
Edition: 4

	Great Teacher Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin.

	In celebration of May Day, the International Labour Day, the hundreds of millions of military and civilian people across the nation are determined, under the wise leadership of the proletarian headquarters headed by Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin, to raise high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, and to win a comprehensive victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

	Photograph by our reporter
