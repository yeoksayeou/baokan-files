Title: A Leader of the Palestinian Nationalist Organization "Fatah" Delivers a Speech
Author:
Date: 1968-05-30
Edition: 5

A leader of the Palestinian nationalist organization "Fatah" recently told a reporter from Xinhua News Agency in Cairo: "Without armed struggle, Palestinians have nothing. We will never lay down our weapons. We will not fall into the imperialist trap, and we clearly reject the so-called 'political solution.'"

He said: "Due to the aggression by U.S. imperialism and its lackey Israel on June 5 last year, large tracts of land in Palestine and other Arab countries were occupied by the enemy. The acute and serious national crisis has made the Arab people seriously consider their own problems. All Arab patriots reject surrender and demand resistance. The homeless Palestinians stand at the forefront of this struggle. After the June War, tens of thousands of Palestinians demanded to take up arms and fight. Countless old warriors returned to the battlefield, and a large number of young people joined our guerrilla organizations. In the occupied areas, the armed struggle of our 'Fatah' organization has expanded unprecedentedly. The rapid development of the Palestinian people's struggle is unprecedented in the past twenty years. In the face of the great revolutionary storm of the Palestinian Arab people, U.S. imperialism and its invasion tool Israel, for the first time, felt the actual threat from the power of the Arab people."

This "Fatah" leader said: "U.S. imperialism is terrified by the armed struggle in Palestine. It is conspiring with modern revisionists to cunningly plot schemes. Their so-called 'political solution' aims to sabotage the revolutionary struggle of the Palestinian people." He pointed out that the Palestinian people have seen through the true nature of the United Nations, which is merely a tool of U.S. imperialism. He said: "Any 'political solution' means that the Palestinian people will lose their national rights and forever become refugees."

He emphasized: "The great leader Chairman Mao teaches us: 'Political power grows out of the barrel of a gun.' We firmly believe that insisting on armed struggle, using revolutionary violence to smash counter-revolutionary violence, is the only way to defeat imperialism and Zionism and achieve the liberation of Palestine. No matter how many difficulties we encounter, we are determined to continue the struggle until we achieve final victory."

This "Fatah" leader finally expressed his gratitude to Chairman Mao and the Chinese people led by Chairman Mao for their great support to the struggle of the Palestinian people. He said: "Chairman Mao and the Chinese people are true friends of the Palestinian people and all revolutionary people in the world. China's support has given us great encouragement. With this support, we are even more determined to strengthen our armed struggle and strive for greater victories."

### 巴勒斯坦民族主义组织“法塔赫”一位领导人发表谈话  坚持武装斗争是争取解放的唯一道路  感谢毛主席和中国人民给予巴勒斯坦人民斗争的巨大支持

1968-05-30
第5版()
专栏：

    巴勒斯坦民族主义组织“法塔赫”一位领导人发表谈话
    坚持武装斗争是争取解放的唯一道路
    感谢毛主席和中国人民给予巴勒斯坦人民斗争的巨大支持
    谴责美帝勾结现代修正主义者破坏巴勒斯坦人民革命斗争
    新华社开罗二十八日电　巴勒斯坦民族主义组织“法塔赫”的一位领导人最近在这里对新华社记者说：“没有武装斗争，巴勒斯坦人就没有一切。我们决不放下武器。我们决不会上帝国主义的圈套，我们明确拒绝所谓的‘政治解决’。”
    他说：“由于去年六月五日美帝国主义及其走狗以色列的侵略，整个巴勒斯坦和其他阿拉伯国家的大片领土被敌人侵占了。尖锐、严重的民族危机使得阿拉伯人民认真考虑自己的问题。全体阿拉伯爱国者拒绝投降，要求实行抵抗。无家可归的巴勒斯坦人民站在这场斗争的最前列。在六月战争以后，成千上万的巴勒斯坦人要求拿起武器，进行战斗。无数的老战士重返战场，大批青年人参加了我们的游击队组织。在被占领地区，我们‘法塔赫’组织的武装斗争空前地扩大了。巴勒斯坦人民斗争的迅猛发展，是二十年来所没有见过的。在巴勒斯坦阿拉伯人民的强大革命风暴面前，美帝国主义及其侵略工具以色列，第一次感到了来自阿拉伯人民力量的实际威胁。”
    这位“法塔赫”领导人说：“美帝国主义被巴勒斯坦的武装斗争吓坏了，它勾结现代修正主义者正在狡猾地策划阴谋。它们策划的所谓的‘政治解决’，其目的是要破坏巴勒斯坦人民的革命斗争。”他指出，巴勒斯坦人民已经看穿了联合国的本质，联合国不过是美帝国主义的一个工具。他说：“任何‘政治解决’都意味着巴勒斯坦人民将丧失自己的民族权利，永远沦为难民。”
    他强调说：“伟大的领袖毛主席教导我们说：‘枪杆子里面出政权’。我们坚信，坚持武装斗争，用革命的暴力粉碎反革命的暴力，是打败帝国主义和犹太复国主义、争取巴勒斯坦解放的唯一道路。不管我们会碰到多少困难，我们决心斗争下去，直到取得最后胜利。”
    这位“法塔赫”领导人最后一再表示感谢毛主席和毛主席领导的中国人民给予巴勒斯坦人民斗争的巨大支持。他说：“毛主席和中国人民是我们巴勒斯坦人民和全世界革命人民的真朋友。中国的支持给了我们巨大的鼓舞。有了这种支持，我们更加有决心加强我们的武装斗争，去争取更大的胜利。”
