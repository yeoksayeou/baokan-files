Title: Workers of Qingdao Sifang Locomotive and Rolling Stock Factory Achieve Excellent Results in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1968-05-16
Edition: 4

The revolutionary workers of Qingdao Sifang Locomotive and Rolling Stock Factory, in the unprecedented Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, have stood firm at their production posts with a strong sense of responsibility as masters of the country. They have achieved outstanding results, completing the state plan for the first quarter by 29% over the target. This is a scene of workers assembling the "East is Red" diesel locomotive. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter)
