Title: Norwegian Ambassador Hosts Reception to Celebrate Constitution Day
Author:
Date: 1968-05-18
Edition: 6

	Norwegian Ambassador Hosts Reception to Celebrate Constitution Day

	Xinhua News Agency, May 17th: The Ambassador of the Kingdom of Norway to China, Mr. Orla, held a reception today to celebrate Norway's Constitution Day.

	Luoguibo, Lin Haiyun, and responsible personnel from the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee were invited to attend the reception.

	Ambassadors from various countries to China also attended the reception.

### 挪威大使举行招待会庆祝宪法日

1968-05-18
第6版()
专栏：

    挪威大使举行招待会庆祝宪法日
    新华社十七日讯　挪威王国驻中国大使奥尔高今天举行招待会，庆祝挪威宪法日。
    罗贵波、林海云以及北京市革命委员会负责人应邀出席了招待会。
    各国驻中国使节也出席了招待会。
