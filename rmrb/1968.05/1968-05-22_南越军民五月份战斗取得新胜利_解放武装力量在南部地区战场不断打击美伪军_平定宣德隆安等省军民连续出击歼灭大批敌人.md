Title: South Vietnamese Military and Civilians Achieve New Victories in May
Author:
Date: 1968-05-22
Edition: 5

South Vietnamese military and civilians have achieved new victories in May. The liberation armed forces have been continuously striking American and puppet forces in the southern battlefield. Military and civilians in provinces such as Binh Dinh, Phu Yen, and Long An have launched successive attacks, annihilating large numbers of enemy troops.

According to a report from the Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi on the 20th, the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency reported that the military and civilians in Binh Dinh, Phu Yen, and other areas have recently launched continuous attacks against the enemy, achieving new victories.

The report states that the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Binh Dinh Province engaged in seven battles with American and puppet forces in the northern part of Phu My County on the 5th and 6th. They annihilated seven enemy battalions, including five American battalions, destroyed and captured 35 tanks and armored vehicles, and shot down two enemy aircraft. On the morning of the 11th, many American aggressors, under the cover of armored vehicles, invaded the northern part of Phu My, attempting to open the first highway from Phu My to Quy Nhon. That night, the Binh Dinh Liberation Army launched a fierce attack on these American troops, destroying nine enemy armored vehicles and capturing two. The remaining enemy fled in panic. The Liberation Army, seizing the opportunity, continued to pursue and annihilate more enemy troops.

The military and civilians in Phu Yen Province launched continuous attacks on the enemy in Da Lat City in the first seven days of May and captured and controlled many "strategic hamlets" in rural areas. According to incomplete statistics, the military and civilians in Phu Yen Province annihilated more than 440 enemy troops in these seven days.

According to a report from the Xinhua News Agency in Hanoi on the 19th, the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency reported: The People's Liberation Armed Forces in South Vietnam have been continuously striking the enemy since early May, achieving many new victories on the southern battlefield.

The military and civilians in Long An Province launched attacks on Thu Duc City and the headquarters of the puppet 25th Division on the 5th and 6th, annihilating 470 enemy troops (including the complete annihilation of two puppet battalions and the heavy defeat of two battalions).

On the 8th and 9th, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Long An Province launched a fierce attack on an American military parking lot in Duc Hoa County, killing and wounding 400 American invaders, destroying 108 military vehicles, destroying three cannons, and burning 63 tents.

The military and civilians in Tuyen Duc Province launched simultaneous attacks on 26 enemy military institutions and strongholds in Tuyen Duc City, Chau Doc, Lai Thieu, and other counties from the night of the 4th to the 6th, heavily defeating one puppet battalion and many companies, completely annihilating four companies, and shooting down and destroying 27 aircraft.

The military and civilians in Ba Ria and Long Khanh Provinces launched attacks on more than 20 enemy military institutions and strongholds in various cities and "strategic hamlets" within the province on the night of the 4th and the 5th, annihilating more than 450 enemy troops, including 150 American invaders.

The military and civilians in Chuong Thien Province launched simultaneous attacks on enemy military institutions and strongholds in Chuong Thien City and various counties from the 5th to the 7th, annihilating and disintegrating 600 enemy troops, including the complete annihilation of three battalions, five platoons, and four squads.

The People's Liberation Armed Forces in An Xuyen Province launched a fierce attack on the communication center of the American Second Field Command on Ba Den Mountain east of An Xuyen City on the night of the 13th, completely destroying this heavily fortified communication center, annihilating one American battalion and one puppet battalion, killing a large number of American technical personnel, and capturing all weapons, ammunition, and military supplies.

### 南越军民五月份战斗取得新胜利  解放武装力量在南部地区战场不断打击美伪军  平定宣德隆安等省军民连续出击歼灭大批敌人

1968-05-22
第5版()
专栏：

    南越军民五月份战斗取得新胜利
    解放武装力量在南部地区战场不断打击美伪军
    平定宣德隆安等省军民连续出击歼灭大批敌人
    据新华社河内二十日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方平定、宣德等地军民，最近接连打击敌人，取得了新的胜利。
    报道说，平定省人民解放武装力量，五日和六日在符美县北部连续同美伪军进行七次战斗，共歼敌七个连，其中包括美军五个连，击毁和缴获坦克和装甲车三十五辆，击落敌机两架。十一日上午，许多美国强盗在装甲车掩护下闯入符美北部的中会地区，企图打通从蓬山到归仁的第一号公路。当天晚上，平定省解放军向这伙美军发动猛烈进攻，击毁敌人的装甲车九辆，缴获两辆，残敌慌忙逃跑。解放军乘胜追击，又消灭敌人多名。
    宣德省军民在五月份头七天中，连续袭击了大叻市里的敌人，并攻占和控制了农村的许多“战略村”。据不完全统计，宣德省军民在这七天中，共歼灭敌人四百四十多名。
    据新华社河内十九日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方人民解放武装力量五月初以来，在南部地区战场不断打击敌人，取得许多新胜利。
    隆安省军民五月五日和六日进攻厚义市和伪军第二十五师司令部，共消灭敌人四百七十名（其中包括全歼伪军两个连，重创两个连）。
    八日和九日，隆安省人民解放武装力量又猛烈袭击美军在德和县的一个停车场，打死打伤美国侵略军四百名，击毁军车一百零八辆，击毁大炮三门，烧毁帐篷六十三座。
    土龙木省军民从五月四日夜间到六日，同时袭击土龙木市和周城、莱眺、新渊等县的二十六个敌军机构和据点，重创伪军一个营和许多连，全歼四个连，击落击毁飞机二十七架。
    巴地和隆庆两省军民五月四日夜晚和五日向省内一些城镇和“战略村”里的二十多个敌军机构和据点发动袭击，歼敌四百五十多名，其中包括美国侵略军一百五十名。
    朱笃省军民从五月五日到七日同时袭击朱笃市和各个县城的敌军机构和据点，歼灭和瓦解敌人六百名，其中包括全歼三个连、五个排和四个班。
    西宁省人民解放武装力量，五月十三日夜间向西宁市东面巴奠山上属于美国第二野战指挥部的通讯中心发动猛烈进攻，完全摧毁了这个重重设防的通讯中心，歼灭美国侵略军一个连和伪军一个连，打死大批美军技术人员，缴获全部武器、弹药和军用物资。
