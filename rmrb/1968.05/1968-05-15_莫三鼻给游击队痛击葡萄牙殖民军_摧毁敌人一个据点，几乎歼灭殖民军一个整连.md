Title: Mozambican Guerrillas Deal Heavy Blow to Portuguese Colonial Forces
Author:
Date: 1968-05-15
Edition: 6

Mozambican freedom fighters in the latter half of March achieved brilliant results in their fierce battles against Portuguese colonial forces.

According to a battle report issued by the Mozambican guerrillas here on the 13th, the guerrillas destroyed a Portuguese stronghold in Nambuti on March 24, wiping out almost an entire company of Portuguese colonial troops. They also captured one mortar, five light machine guns, and other military supplies.

In some areas of the province of Delgad, the guerrillas killed more than 30 Portuguese soldiers and injured many more, and destroyed about ten enemy trucks.

On March 25, the guerrillas attacked a Portuguese stronghold in Namacuriana, rescuing 72 Mozambican civilians who had been imprisoned there.

### 莫三鼻给游击队痛击葡萄牙殖民军  摧毁敌人一个据点，几乎歼灭殖民军一个整连

1968-05-15
第6版()
专栏：

    莫三鼻给游击队痛击葡萄牙殖民军
    摧毁敌人一个据点，几乎歼灭殖民军一个整连
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆十四日电　莫三鼻给自由战士三月下半月在痛击葡萄牙殖民军的战斗中取得了辉煌战果。
    据莫三鼻给游击队十三日在这里发表的战报宣布，莫三鼻给游击队在三月二十四日摧毁了葡萄牙设在楠布迪的一个据点，歼灭了葡萄牙殖民军几乎一个整连，缴获了一门迫击炮，五挺轻机枪和其他一些军用物资。
    在德尔加多角省的一些地方，游击队打死了葡萄牙士兵三十多名，打伤多名，击毁敌人卡车约十辆。
    三月二十五日，游击队袭击了葡萄牙人设在喃库纳米亚的据点，救出了被关押在那里的七十二名莫三鼻给群众。
