Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down 10 U.S. Aircraft, Hit One U.S. Ship
Author:
Date: 1968-05-10
Edition: 5

Vietnamese military and civilians shoot down 10 U.S. aircraft, hit one U.S. ship

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, May 8th — It is reported from here that on the 7th, the military and civilians of Nghe An Province in North Vietnam shot down one invading U.S. aggressor aircraft.

	On the 6th, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province shot down two U.S. aggressor aircraft. On the same day, the military and civilians of Vinh Linh area also shot down one U.S. aircraft.

	It is also reported that on the 6th, the Vietnamese People's Army in Quang Binh Province hit one U.S. warship that had intruded into the territorial waters of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, causing the aggressor warship to catch fire and burn.

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, May 8th — It is reported from here that on the 4th and 5th, the military and civilians of Quang Binh, Quang Tri, and Nghe An Provinces in North Vietnam shot down three invading U.S. aircraft in battles against U.S. air aggressors.

	On the 7th, the military and civilians of Nghe An Province shot down two more invading U.S. aircraft. On the 8th, the military and civilians of Quang Tri Province also shot down one more invading U.S. aircraft.

### 越南军民击落美机十架击中美舰一艘

1968-05-10
第5版()
专栏：

　　越南军民击落美机十架击中美舰一艘
    新华社河内八日电　据这里公布，越南北方义安省军民七日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。
    广平省军民六日打下美国强盗飞机两架。同一天，永灵区军民也打下美机一架。
    另据报道，广平省越南人民军部队六日击中侵入越南民主共和国领海的美国军舰一艘，使这艘强盗军舰着火燃烧。
    新华社河内八日电　据这里报道，越南北方广平、河静和义安等省军民，四日和五日在反击美国空中强盗战斗中，击落入侵的美机三架。
    七日，义安省军民又击落入侵的美机两架，河静省军民，八日也再次击落入侵的美机一架。
