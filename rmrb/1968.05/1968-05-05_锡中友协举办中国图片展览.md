Title: The Sri Lanka-China Friendship Association Holds a Chinese Photographic Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1968-05-05
Edition: 6

About 500 people from various circles in Sri Lanka attended the opening ceremony of the photographic exhibition of the People's Republic of China held by the Sri Lanka-China Friendship Association in Colombo on April 29.

The chairman of the Sri Lanka-China Friendship Association, Walpita, presided over the opening ceremony.

Sri Lanka Senate President A. Ranayaka spoke at the opening ceremony. He talked about the traditional friendship between China and Sri Lanka and expressed the hope that this exhibition would further strengthen the relations between the two countries.

In his speech, the acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Sri Lanka, Xi Yezheng, expressed the hope that through this exhibition, mutual understanding and friendship between the peoples of China and Sri Lanka would be promoted.

After the opening ceremony, the attendees visited the exhibition. The exhibits were warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 锡中友协举办中国图片展览

1968-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

　　锡中友协举办中国图片展览
　　新华社科伦坡电　锡兰各界人士约五百人四月二十九日出席了锡中友好协会在科伦坡举办的中华人民共和国图片展览会的开幕式。
    锡中友好协会主席瓦尔皮塔主持开幕式。
    锡兰参议院议长阿·拉纳雅克在开幕式上讲了话。他谈到了锡中两国之间的传统友谊，并表示希望这次展览会将使锡中两国之间的关系更加密切。
    中国驻锡兰大使馆临时代办奚业胜在讲话中表示希望通过这次展览会促进中锡两国人民之间的相互了解和友谊。
    开幕式结束以后，与会者参观了展览会，展品受到了观众的热烈欢迎。
