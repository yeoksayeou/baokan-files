Title: The Capital's Working Class Celebrates "May Day" International Labor Day
Author:
Date: 1968-05-02
Edition: 4

The proletarian working class of the capital, which played a leading role in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, enthusiastically celebrated the "May Day" International Labor Day. They are determined to follow the great teacher Chairman Mao, to advance victoriously, and to achieve a comprehensive victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.
