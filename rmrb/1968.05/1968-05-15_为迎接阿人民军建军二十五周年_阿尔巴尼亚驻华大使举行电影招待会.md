Title: In Celebration of the 25th Anniversary of the Albanian People's Army, the Albanian Ambassador to China Hosts a Film Screening
Author:
Date: 1968-05-15
Edition: 6

For the celebration of the 25th anniversary of the Albanian People's Army, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of Albania to China, Nataneli, hosted a film screening tonight.

	Chinese People's Liberation Army leaders from various headquarters, military branches, and divisions, including Wu Faxian, Li Tianyou, Wang Xinting, Wen Yucheng, and Zhang Xianyao, as well as Deputy Minister of National Defense Wang Shusheng, and representatives of PLA soldiers, were invited to attend the screening.

	Before the screening, Ambassador Nataneli and Vice Chief of the General Staff Wu Faxian toasted to the health of the great leader of the Chinese people and close friend of the Albanian people, Chairman Mao, and the great leader of the Albanian people and close friend of the Chinese people, Comrade Enver Hoxha. They also toasted to the complete victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China and to the fighting unity and friendship between the peoples and armies of China and Albania.

	The film screened was "Conquering Death," which depicts the story of Albanian women during the national liberation war from 1939 to 1944. Under the leadership of the Albanian Communist Party, they bravely responded to the party's call to join the fight against fascism and domestic reactionaries. In the national liberation war, they showed unyielding strength and made outstanding contributions.

	The military attaché, Hakan, and the counselor, Baba, from the Embassy of the People's Republic of Albania in China, also attended the screening.

### 为迎接阿人民军建军二十五周年  阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使举行电影招待会

1968-05-15
第6版()
专栏：

    为迎接阿人民军建军二十五周年
    阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使举行电影招待会
    新华社十四日讯　为迎接阿尔巴尼亚人民军建军二十五周年，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻中国大使纳塔奈利，今晚举行电影招待会。
    中国人民解放军各总部、各军种兵种负责人吴法宪、李天佑、王新亭、温玉成、张贤约，国防部副部长王树声等和人民解放军战士代表应邀出席了招待会。
    招待会前，纳塔奈利大使和吴法宪副总参谋长，为中国人民的伟大领袖、阿尔巴尼亚人民的亲密朋友毛主席和阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖、中国人民的亲密朋友霍查同志的健康，为中国无产阶级文化大革命的全面胜利，为中阿两国人民、两国军队的战斗团结和友谊干杯！
    招待会上放映了反映阿尔巴尼亚妇女在一九三九年至一九四四年民族解放战争期间，在阿尔巴尼亚共产党的领导下，坚决响应党的号召，参加反对法西斯和国内反动派的斗争，在民族解放战争中，坚强不屈，做出杰出贡献的故事影片《战胜死亡》。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻中国大使馆武官哈卡尼和参赞巴巴尼出席了招待会。
