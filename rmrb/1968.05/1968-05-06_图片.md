Title: Congolese (Brazzaville) Civil Defense Corps Soldiers Enthusiastically Cheer Chairman Mao's Statement Supporting the Struggle of American Blacks Against Violence
Author:
Date: 1968-05-06
Edition: 6

Soldiers of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Civil Defense Corps enthusiastically cheer Chairman Mao's statement supporting the struggle of American blacks against violence. After studying this great statement, they enthusiastically shouted: "Long live armed struggle!" "Long live Mao Zedong Thought!"
