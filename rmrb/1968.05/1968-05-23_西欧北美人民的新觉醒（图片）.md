Title: The New Awakening of the People of Western Europe and North America
Author:
Date: 1968-05-23
Edition: 5

Under the fascist dictatorship of Franco in Spain, the student movement is becoming increasingly intense. The photo shows students at the University of Madrid distributing leaflets during an anti-government rally on May 18.

In Rome, Italy, students are holding up portraits of the great revolutionary leaders Lenin and Chairman Mao during an anti-government demonstration. New China News Agency (Telephoto)

Students in Oakland, California, bravely fight against the police who came to suppress their anti-Vietnam War protests.

In Paris, France, thousands of university students joined railway workers and passengers in a struggle at the Saint-Lazare train station.

In France, workers launched a wave of strikes and demonstrations to support the just struggle of French students. The photo shows workers at the Renault car factory in Paris bravely occupying the factory and raising the red flag on the factory roof. New China News Agency (Telephoto)
