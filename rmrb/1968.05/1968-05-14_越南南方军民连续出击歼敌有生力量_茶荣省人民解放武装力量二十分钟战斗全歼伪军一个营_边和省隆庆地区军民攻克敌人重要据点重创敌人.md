Title: South Vietnamese Military and Civilians Launch Continuous Attacks, Annihilating Enemy Forces
Author:
Date: 1968-05-14
Edition: 6

South Vietnamese military and civilians have launched continuous attacks, annihilating large numbers of enemy forces. The People's Liberation Armed Forces in the province of Tra Vinh, in a twenty-minute battle, completely annihilated a camp of puppet troops.

On the night of May 5, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Tra Vinh Province launched an attack on the puppet military base west of Tra Vinh City in the Thach An area. After a fierce twenty-minute battle, they completely annihilated a camp of puppet troops.

At the same time, the People's Liberation Armed Forces also attacked the Tra Vinh Airport, puppet military camps, and several enemy targets in the city, killing many more enemy troops.

On the same day, the military and civilians in the counties of Hoang Diem, Gang Long, Yung Lam, and Kieu Thi in Tra Vinh Province also bravely fought, attacking a series of enemy strongholds and outposts.

From May 4 to May 8, the military and civilians in Quang Nam Province launched continuous attacks on the enemy stationed in the counties of Dai Loc, Duc Duc, Vinh Dien, Hoa Vang, Dien Ban, Hieu Duc, Tam Ky, and Tuy Phong. They annihilated three companies of puppet troops, severely damaged two companies of U.S. invading troops, annihilated and severely damaged twelve platoons of U.S. puppet and South Korean mercenary troops, shot down fourteen enemy aircraft, and destroyed sixty-five military vehicles. In addition, the People's Liberation Armed Forces also assisted the people in destroying many concentration camps, liberating thousands of people.

The People's Liberation Armed Forces in the Long Khanh area of Binh Dinh Province captured the enemy's important military stronghold of Kim Mai on May 5, killing more than 150 U.S. invading troops and a company of puppet troops, destroying twenty-seven tanks and armored vehicles, and seizing a large amount of weapons.

The report said that in the early morning of May 5, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in the Long Khanh area of Binh Dinh Province launched an attack on the Kim Mai stronghold near the base of the U.S. 11th Independent Armored Cavalry Regiment. The liberation forces quickly gained complete control of the area, capturing all the outposts, annihilating a company of puppet troops, and severely damaging a platoon.

Immediately afterward, the People's Liberation Armed Forces fiercely intercepted hundreds of military vehicles from the U.S. 11th Armored Cavalry Regiment. After three hours of continuous fighting, they repelled the enemy's three attacks, killed more than 150 U.S. invading troops, destroyed twenty-seven tanks and armored vehicles, and seized two.

Another report said that on the night of May 4 to the early morning of May 5, the liberation army in Binh Dinh Province heavily shelled the U.S. airbase in Binh Dinh, destroying sixteen enemy aircraft, causing many ammunition and fuel depots to explode and burn, and killing many U.S. invading troops. At the same time, the liberation army also shelled the base of the U.S. 199th Infantry Brigade, causing the enemy to suffer heavy losses.

### 越南南方军民连续出击歼敌有生力量  茶荣省人民解放武装力量二十分钟战斗全歼伪军一个营  边和省隆庆地区军民攻克敌人重要据点重创敌人

1968-05-14
第6版()
专栏：

    越南南方军民连续出击歼敌有生力量
    茶荣省人民解放武装力量二十分钟战斗全歼伪军一个营
    边和省隆庆地区军民攻克敌人重要据点重创敌人
    新华社河内十三日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方茶荣省和广南省军民最近接连袭击敌人，歼灭敌人大批有生力量。
    茶荣省人民解放武装力量，五日凌晨在茶荣市西面的座占地区袭击伪军驻地，经过二十分钟的激战，全歼伪军一个营。
    与此同时，人民解放武装力量还进攻了茶荣机场、伪军营地和市区的一些敌军目标，又歼灭许多敌人。
    同一天，茶荣省横桥、岗龙、泳濂和构棋等县军民，也英勇战斗，袭击了敌人的一系列据点和哨所。
    广南省军民从四日夜间到八日接连袭击驻扎在大禄、德育、维川、和旺、奠盘、孝德、三岐、先福等县的敌人，歼灭伪军三个连，重创美国侵略军二个连，歼灭和重创美伪军和南朝鲜帮凶军十二个排，击落敌机十四架，击毁军车六十五辆。此外，人民解放武装力量还协助人民奋起捣毁许多集中营，解放了数以千计的人民。
    新华社河内十二日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方边和省隆庆地区人民解放武装力量五日攻克敌人重要军事据点锦美，消灭美国侵略军一百五十多名和伪军一个连，击毁坦克和装甲车二十七辆，缴获大批武器。
    报道说，五日凌晨，边和省隆庆地区人民解放武装力量打响了袭击美军第十一独立装甲团基地附近的锦美据点的战斗。解放武装力量很快就完全控制了这一地区，攻克了全部哨岗，歼灭伪军一个连，重创一个排。
    紧接着，人民解放武装力量又猛烈截击美军第十一装甲团的成百辆军车，经过连续三个小时的战斗，粉碎了敌人的三次进攻，消灭美国侵略军一百五十多名，击毁坦克和装甲车二十七辆，缴获两辆。
    另据报道，边和省解放军四日夜间到五日凌晨猛烈炮轰边和美国空军基地，击毁敌机十六架，使敌人的许多弹药库和汽油库中弹起火燃烧，并歼灭许多美国侵略军。与此同时，解放军还炮轰美军第一九九步兵旅的驻地，使敌人遭到严重损失。
