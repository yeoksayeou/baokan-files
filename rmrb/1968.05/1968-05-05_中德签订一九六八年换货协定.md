Title: China and East Germany Sign 1968 Barter Agreement
Author:
Date: 1968-05-05
Edition: 6

The barter agreement for the exchange of goods and payments between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the German Democratic Republic for the year 1968 was signed in Berlin on May 3.

Signing the agreement on behalf of the two governments were Zhou Huamin, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade and head of the Chinese Government Trade Delegation, and Wagner, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations and head of the German Democratic Republic Government Trade Delegation.

### 中德签订一九六八年换货协定

1968-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

　　中德签订一九六八年换货协定
    新华社柏林四日电　中华人民共和国政府和德意志民主共和国政府一九六八年交换货物和付款协定于五月三日在柏林签订。
    代表双方政府在协定上签字的是：中华人民共和国政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长周化民和德意志民主共和国政府贸易代表团团长、对外经济部副部长瓦格纳。
