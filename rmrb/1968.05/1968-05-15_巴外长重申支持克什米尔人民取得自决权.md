Title: Pakistani Foreign Minister Reiterates Support for Kashmiris' Right to Self-Determination
Author:
Date: 1968-05-15
Edition: 6

Pakistani Foreign Minister Azad Hussain said on the 14th that the Pakistani government is determined to support the Kashmiris' right to self-determination.

Hussain, in response to a question in the National Assembly, said that the Kashmir dispute will be resolved by the Kashmiris exercising their right to self-determination. The recent actions taken by the Indian government will not succeed.

He said: "The Pakistani government is resolute in supporting the Kashmiris' struggle for self-determination."

### 巴外长重申支持克什米尔人民取得自决权

1968-05-15
第6版()
专栏：

    巴外长重申支持克什米尔人民取得自决权
    据新华社拉瓦尔品第十四日电　巴基斯坦外长阿沙德·侯赛因十四日对国民议会说，巴基斯坦政府决心支持克什米尔人民取得自决权。
    侯赛因在国民议会上回答问题时说，克什米尔争端将由克什米尔人民通过行使自决权来求得解决。印度政府最近所采取的那些行动是不能得逞的。
    他说：“巴基斯坦政府在支持克什米尔人民争取自决权的斗争方面，是有决心的。”
