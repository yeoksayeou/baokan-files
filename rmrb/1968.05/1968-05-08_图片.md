Title: Workers of the Tumen Railway Division of the Jilin Railway Bureau Maintain Production During the Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1968-05-08
Edition: 3

The revolutionary workers of the Tumen Railway Division of the Jilin Railway Bureau have consistently maintained their production posts during the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, ensuring the smooth transportation of domestic and foreign goods. They have continuously improved the punctuality rate of passenger and freight train services. The image shows the bustling Tumen Station. Photographed by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
