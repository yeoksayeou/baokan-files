Title: Celebrating International Labor Day: People of West Berlin and West Germany March and Rally with Red Flags
Author:
Date: 1968-05-03
Edition: 6

		Celebrating International Labor Day: People of West Berlin and West Germany March and Rally with Red Flags

		Xinhua News Agency, Bonn, May 1st - On May 1st, International Labor Day, tens of thousands of workers and students in West Berlin and various parts of West Germany held joint demonstrations and rallies. They protested against U.S. imperialism's aggression in Vietnam and against the West German government's fascist suppression of the people.

		In West Berlin, 25,000 workers and students held a massive demonstration in the workers' district. They carried countless red flags and placards opposing U.S. imperialism's aggression in Vietnam. Despite police threats and obstruction, the demonstrators linked arms and ran forward, singing "The Internationale."

		At the rally held after the demonstration, a leader of a West German student organization angrily denounced the West German authorities for using fascist methods to suppress the people. Workers' representatives from several Western European countries also spoke at the rally, condemning U.S. imperialism's aggression in Vietnam and emphasizing the need to adopt all means of struggle to strengthen the fight against U.S. imperialism.

		In Munich, West Germany, a rally of 30,000 people was held. The crowd held up portraits of Marx and Chairman Mao, as well as Chairman Ho Chi Minh. They also held up countless red flags and banners reading, "Workers and students unite to fight against West German monopoly capital!"

		In the Ruhr area, in cities like Bochum, Essen, and Oberhausen, rallies of tens of thousands of people were held. The crowds strongly opposed the West German government's push for fascist rule through the "Emergency Powers Act."

		Similar activities were also held in Hamburg, Frankfurt, Cologne, and Mannheim in West Germany.

### 庆祝国际劳动节  西柏林和西德人民高举红旗游行集会

1968-05-03
第6版()
专栏：

　　庆祝国际劳动节
    西柏林和西德人民高举红旗游行集会
    新华社波恩一日电　西柏林和西德各地成千上万的工人和青年学生在“五一”国际劳动节联合举行示威游行和集会，抗议美帝国主义侵略越南，抗议西德政府以法西斯手段镇压人民。
    西柏林两万五千名工人和青年学生在工人区举行了声势浩大的示威游行，他们举着无数的红旗和反对美帝国主义侵略越南的标语牌。示威者不顾警察的威胁和阻拦，手挽着手，跑步前进，并且高唱《国际歌》。
    在示威之后举行的集会上，一个西德学生组织的负责人愤怒谴责西德当局以法西斯手段镇压人民。一些西欧国家的工人代表也在会上讲话，愤怒谴责美帝国主义侵略越南，强调要采取一切斗争手段，加强反对美帝国主义的斗争。
    在西德的慕尼黑举行的三万人群众大会上，群众高举着马克思和毛主席的画像，以及胡志明主席的画像。群众还高举着无数红旗和写着“工人和学生联合起来向西德垄断资本进行斗争！”的标语牌。
    在鲁尔区的波鸿、埃森和奥伯豪森等地举行的数万人的群众集会上，群众强烈地表示反对西德政府推行法西斯统治的“紧急状态法”。
    此外，在西德的汉堡、法兰克福、科隆和曼海姆等地也举行了类似的活动。
