Title: U.S.-Saigon Puppet Forces Again Violate Cambodian Airspace and Territorial Waters
Author:
Date: 1968-05-06
Edition: 6

The U.S.-Saigon puppet forces again violate Cambodian airspace and territorial waters. The Cambodian government lodges a strong protest.

BEIJING, May 5 (Xinhua) — According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on April 4, the Royal Government of Cambodia issued a statement on May 3, strongly protesting the recent blatant provocations by U.S. aggressors and Saigon puppet forces' aircraft invading Cambodian airspace.

At around 7:30 a.m. on April 26, two armed helicopters of the U.S. aggressors and the Saigon puppet forces invaded Cambodian airspace and flew at low altitude over the city of Takeo, the provincial capital of Takeo Province. They repeatedly flew over the city center and threatened with machine-gun fire.

The statement of the Royal Government of Cambodia said that the Cambodian government condemns the deliberate invasion and blatant threats by the United States against Cambodia and strongly protests this new, extremely serious provocation by the United States.

BEIJING, May 3 (Xinhua) — According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on May 3, the Royal Government of Cambodia issued a statement on April 30, strongly condemning the aggression by some U.S.-Saigon puppet force speedboats that invaded Cambodian territorial waters on April 25 and opened fire on a Cambodian outpost in the province of Kampong Cham.

The statement announced that the Royal Cambodian Navy immediately retaliated, hitting one of the speedboats.

The statement also exposed the fact that the U.S.-Saigon puppet forces have been continuously attacking, harassing, and provoking the Cambodian border.

The statement called on the Cambodian army, navy, and air force to be ready at all times to resist any infringement on Cambodian sovereignty and any aggression against Cambodian territory.

According to another report by the Cambodian News Agency, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Cambodia sent notes of protest to the U.S. government and the Saigon puppet government through the French and Australian embassies in Cambodia on April 30, strongly protesting the aggression by U.S.-Saigon puppet soldiers and aircraft that attacked Cambodian farmers in the area of Pim Kok District, Borei Province, on April 7.

### 美国—南越伪军又侵犯柬领空领海　柬埔寨政府提出严重抗议

1968-05-06
第6版()
专栏：

　　美国—南越伪军又侵犯柬领空领海
　　柬埔寨政府提出严重抗议
    新华社金边五日电　据柬埔寨新闻社四日报道，柬埔寨王国政府三日发表声明，严重抗议美国侵略者和南越伪军飞机最近又侵犯柬埔寨王国领空，进行明目张胆的挑衅。
    四月二十六日七时三十分左右，美国侵略者和南越伪军的武装直升飞机两架侵入柬埔寨王国领空，在柴桢省省会柴桢市低空飞行，多次飞越市中心上空，并且用机枪扫射进行威胁。
    柬埔寨王国政府的声明说，柬埔寨王国政府谴责美国对柬埔寨进行这种蓄意的侵犯和明目张胆的威胁，并且对美国这一十分严重的新挑衅提出严重抗议。
    新华社金边三日电　据柬埔寨新闻社三日报道，柬埔寨王国政府四月三十日发表一项声明，强烈谴责美国—南越伪军的一些快艇四月二十五日侵入柬埔寨领海，并且向干丹省的一个柬埔寨哨所开火的侵略行径。
    声明宣布，柬埔寨王家海军舰艇当即进行了还击，击中了其中一艘快艇。
    声明还揭露了美国—南越伪军不断地对柬埔寨边境进行袭击、骚扰和挑衅的事实。
    声明号召柬埔寨的陆、海、空军，随时准备抗击一切对柬埔寨主权的侵犯和对柬埔寨国土的侵略。
    另据柬埔寨新闻社报道，柬埔寨王国政府外交部于四月三十日曾通过法国和澳大利亚驻柬埔寨大使馆，分别向美国政府和西贡傀儡政府发出了照会，强烈抗议美国—南越伪军的士兵和飞机四月七日对柬埔寨波萝勉省皮姆科县境内的柬埔寨农民进行袭击的侵略罪行。
