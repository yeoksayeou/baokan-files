Title: China and Hungary Sign 1968 Barter Agreement
Author:
Date: 1968-05-05
Edition: 6

    China and Hungary's 1968 Barter Agreement for the Exchange of Goods and Payments was signed in Beijing on May 4. Li Qiang, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and Torodi Yenuo, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade and head of the Hungarian Government Trade Delegation, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments. The Hungarian Government Trade Delegation departed Beijing on May 4 to return home.

### 中匈签订一九六八年换货协定

1968-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

　　中匈签订一九六八年换货协定
    新华社四日讯　中国和匈牙利一九六八年度交换货物和付款协定，五月四日在北京签字。对外贸易部副部长李强和匈牙利政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长托尔多伊·耶诺，分别代表两国政府在协定上签字。匈牙利政府贸易代表团于四日离京回国。
