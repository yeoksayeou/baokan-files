Title: Frequent Attacks, Brave Battles, Achieving Great Victories
Author:
Date: 1968-05-10
Edition: 5

Patriotic military and civilians in seven provinces of Laos, including Xieng Khouang, Sam Neua, Luang Prabang, Savannakhet, Saravane, Attapeu, and Udomsai, frequently launched attacks and fought bravely in April, eliminating 682 enemy personnel, shooting down two American planes, and destroying or damaging many enemy vehicles, military supplies, warehouses, and barracks. They also seized a large amount of weapons and ammunition.

The report said that in April, the patriotic military and civilians of Xieng Khouang Province eliminated 294 enemy personnel and shot down two American planes. In this province, on April 8, the patriotic military and civilians launched a surprise attack in the mountain area, annihilating 189 enemy personnel and liberating more than 1,200 local residents.

From April 8 to 10, the patriotic armed forces captured an important base of American-sponsored bandits in the Sezo area and drove the bandits out of Natasia, Xieng Muan, Mung Tue, and other nearby areas, eliminating 177 enemy personnel.

From April 17 to 22, the patriotic armed forces intercepted enemy forces in the Phumane, Mueang Phon, Hapao, Nampeo, and Rohan areas, annihilating 62 enemy personnel.

In the early hours of April 24, the patriotic armed forces attacked an enemy supply base in the Mengsui area, killing 40 enemy personnel.

In April, the patriotic military and civilians of Sam Neua Province engaged in 11 battles with the enemy, eliminating 74 enemy personnel and seizing 82 weapons of various types.

In April, the patriotic military and civilians of Luang Prabang Province engaged in 14 battles, eliminating 117 enemy personnel and seizing 47 weapons.

During this period, the patriotic military and civilians of the four provinces of Savannakhet, Saravane, Attapeu, and Udomsai also eliminated 197 enemy personnel.

Another report said that on April 8 and 24, local troops and militia guerrillas in Vientiane and Phongsali Province conducted anti-bandit and anti-"sweeping" operations, eliminating 48 enemy personnel.

According to a report from the Pathet Lao News Agency, on April 30, the guerrillas in the Mangxai area of Sam Neua Province shot down an American bandit plane that was transporting supplies to bandits.

### 频频出击　英勇战斗　取得巨大战果　老挝川圹等省爱国军民上月歼敌六百多

1968-05-10
第5版()
专栏：

　　频频出击　英勇战斗　取得巨大战果
　　老挝川圹等省爱国军民上月歼敌六百多
    据新华社康开九日电  据巴特寮通讯社报道：老挝川圹、桑怒、琅勃拉邦、沙湾拿吉、沙拉湾、阿速坡和乌多姆塞等七省爱国军民，四月份频频出击，英勇战斗，共消灭敌人六百八十二名，击落美机两架，击毁和摧毁敌人许多军车、军用物资、仓库和营房，缴获大批武器和弹药。
    报道说，川圹省爱国军民在四月份的战斗中，共消灭敌人二百九十四名，击落美机两架。这个省爱国军民，仅四月八日在山座地区的袭击战中，就一举歼敌一百八十九名，解放了当地居民一千二百多人。
    从四月八日到十日，爱国武装力量攻占了美国豢养的匪徒在塞佐地区的一个重要基地，并把匪徒赶出了那士亚、川绵、芒特和附近其他地区，共歼敌一百七十七名。
    从四月十七日到二十二日，爱国武装力量截击了富芒、孟波、哈宝、南比和罗汉地区的敌人，歼灭敌军六十二名。
    四月二十四日凌晨，爱国武装力量袭击了孟绥地区的敌人供应基地，打死敌军四十名。
    桑怒省爱国军民四月份同敌人进行了十一次战斗，共消灭敌人七十四名，缴获各种武器八十二件。
    琅勃拉邦省爱国军民四月份在十四次战斗中，共歼敌一百十七名，缴获武器四十七件。
    沙湾拿吉、沙拉湾、阿速坡和乌多姆塞四省爱国军民在这期间也消灭敌人一百九十七名。
    另据报道：万象和丰沙里省地方部队和民兵游击队，在四月八日和二十四日两天中，进行了剿匪和反“扫荡”战斗，共消灭敌人四十八名。
    新华社康开八日电  据巴特寮通讯社报道，老挝桑怒省芒显地区的游击队，四月三十日击落一架正在给土匪运输物资的美国强盗飞机。
