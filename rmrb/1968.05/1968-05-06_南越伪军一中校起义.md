Title: A Colonel in the Saigon Puppet Army Rebels
Author:
Date: 1968-05-06
Edition: 6

A colonel in the Saigon puppet army recently rebelled in the area of Hue, declaring his departure from the Nguyen Van Thieu-Nguyen Cao Ky clique.

This rebellious colonel, Huang Wenjiang, was the commander of the first transportation command of the puppet army. He joined the rebel troops of the first infantry division of the puppet army. After the rebellion, he called on patriotic soldiers in the puppet army to leave the Nguyen Van Thieu-Nguyen Cao Ky puppet regime, stand on the side of the people, oppose the U.S. aggression war, and contribute to the struggle for national independence and peace.

### 南越伪军一中校起义

1968-05-06
第6版()
专栏：

　　南越伪军一中校起义
    新华社河内电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：南越伪军一名中校不久以前在顺化地区起义，宣布脱离阮文绍—阮高其集团。
    这个起义的伪军中校黄文姜是原伪军第一运输指挥部的总指挥。他同伪军第一步兵师的起义官兵一起，参加了起义军。他在起义后呼吁伪军中的爱国士兵脱离阮文绍—阮高其伪政权，站在人民一边，反对美国的侵略战争，为争取祖国的独立与和平作出贡献。
