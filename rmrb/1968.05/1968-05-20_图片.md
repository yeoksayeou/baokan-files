Title: Japanese Revolutionary People Inspired by China's Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1968-05-20
Edition: 5

The Japanese revolutionary people, in the midst of their struggle, have drawn immense inspiration from the victory of China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. The image shows revolutionary people from Yamaguchi Prefecture in Japan holding up banners that read, "Long live the complete victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China!" as they engage in a demonstration.
