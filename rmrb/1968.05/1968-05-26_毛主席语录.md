	Quotations from Chairman Mao
	The people of the whole world, unite and defeat the American aggressors and all their running dogs! The people of the whole world should be courageous and dare to fight, not fearing difficulties and pressing on wave upon wave. Then the whole world will surely belong to the people. All ghosts and monsters will be eliminated.

### 毛主席语录

1968-05-26

专栏：

    毛主席语录
    全世界人民团结起来，打败美国侵略者及其一切走狗！全世界人民要有勇气，敢于战斗，不怕困难，前赴后继，那末，全世界就一定是人民的。一切魔鬼通通都会被消灭。
