Title: Armed Struggle Blazes Through Israeli-Occupied Areas: Palestinian Guerrillas Launch Over 100 Attacks on Israeli Aggressors This Year
Author:
Date: 1968-05-05
Edition: 5

The guerrilla forces led by the Palestinian nationalist organization "Fatah" have been continuously striking the Israeli aggressors in recent times, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy.

Since the beginning of this year, Palestinian guerrillas have launched over 100 attacks on Israeli aggressors. The flames of armed struggle have spread throughout the entire Israeli-occupied area. In April, they foiled two enemy incursions, killing and wounding more than 90 enemy soldiers, achieving new significant victories.

On April 13, Israeli aggressors dispatched a large number of ground troops and paratroopers, using tanks, armored vehicles, helicopters, and bombers, to attack the important guerrilla activity area of the Nabulus mountain region east of the village of Fuleik. They attempted to surround the guerrillas there. The guerrilla fighters bravely counterattacked with hand grenades, machine guns, and anti-tank cannons. After 22 hours of intense battle, they repelled the enemy's invasion. In this battle, the guerrillas killed and wounded more than 60 Israeli soldiers, shot down one Israeli helicopter, and destroyed several armored vehicles.

On April 14, Israeli aggressors again attacked the guerrillas between the villages of Fuleik and Dagan. The guerrilla fighters bravely resisted, and after three days of fierce battle, they killed and wounded 30 enemy soldiers, achieving another victory.

In recent times, Israeli aggressors have been continuously provoking the Jordanian military on the east bank of the Jordan River. To severely punish the Israeli aggressors, the Palestinian guerrillas have intensified their activities, ambushing the enemy everywhere. In a battle on April 8, the guerrillas fought the Israeli aggressors for more than three hours, killing and wounding 60 enemy soldiers. On April 10, Israeli armored units, under the cover of paratroopers, attempted to cross the Jordan River. The guerrillas ambushed this enemy force in Tel Abain, killing and wounding 15 enemy soldiers. On April 22, the guerrillas again ambushed the enemy in the same area, killing and wounding 12 enemy soldiers.

In the northern Beisan Valley, Golan Heights, and the southern Negev region, the guerrillas frequently take the initiative to attack the enemy. In the western Gaza region, the guerrillas killed 44 Israeli soldiers and two officers in early April.

### 武装斗争烈火燃遍以色列占领区　巴勒斯坦游击队今年来袭击以色列侵略军一百多次

1968-05-05
第5版()
专栏：

　　武装斗争烈火燃遍以色列占领区
　　巴勒斯坦游击队今年来袭击以色列侵略军一百多次
    据新华社讯  巴勒斯坦民族主义组织“法塔赫”所领导的游击队，最近以来不断打击以色列侵略军，使敌人遭受重大损失。
    今年以来，巴勒斯坦游击队向以色列侵略者发动了一百多次袭击，武装斗争的烈火遍及整个以色列占领区；四月中，又粉碎了敌人的两次进犯，打死打伤敌军九十多人，取得了新的重大战果。
    四月十三日，以色列侵略者出动大批陆军和伞兵，用坦克、装甲车、直升飞机和轰炸机，向游击队活动的重要地区之一的纳布卢斯山区以东的福利克村附近发动进攻，妄图包围那里的游击队。游击队战士用手榴弹、机关枪和反坦克炮等武器进行英勇反击，经过二十二个小时的激战，打退了敌人的窜犯。在这次战斗中，游击队打死打伤以色列官兵六十多人，并击落以色列直升飞机一架，击毁装甲车数辆。
    十四日，以色列侵略者又向福利克村和达甘村之间的游击队发动进攻。游击队战士进行英勇抗击，经过三天的激战，打死打伤敌军三十人，取得了又一次新的胜利。
    最近以来，以色列侵略军不断向约旦河东岸的约旦军队进行军事挑衅，巴勒斯坦游击队为了严惩以色列侵略者，加强了活动，到处伏击敌人。在四月八日的一次战斗中，游击队和以色列侵略军经过三个多小时的战斗，打死打伤敌军六十人。十日，以色列装甲部队在伞兵部队的掩护下企图越过约旦河，游击队在特勒阿巴因伏击了这一支敌军，打死打伤敌军十五人。二十二日，游击队又在同一地区伏击敌人，打死打伤敌军十二人。
    在北部的贝桑河谷、戈兰高地，南部的内格夫地区，游击队经常主动出击敌人。在西部的加沙地区，游击队在四月上旬就打死以色列士兵四十四人，军官两人。
