Title: Vice Premier Bista to Visit China at the Invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1968-05-23
Edition: 1

At the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs and Economic Planning of the Kingdom of Nepal, Kirtinidhi Bista, will soon arrive in China for a friendly visit.

### 应中华人民共和国政府的邀请  比斯塔副首相即将来我国访问

1968-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

    应中华人民共和国政府的邀请
    比斯塔副首相即将来我国访问
    新华社二十二日讯　应中华人民共和国政府的邀请，尼泊尔王国副首相兼外交、经济计划大臣基尔提·尼迪·比斯塔，即将来我国进行友好访问。
