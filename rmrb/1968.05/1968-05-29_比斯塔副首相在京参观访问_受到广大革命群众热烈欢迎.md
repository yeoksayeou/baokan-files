Title: Vice Premier Bista's Visit to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-05-29
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Bista of Nepal visited Beijing and received a warm welcome from the broad revolutionary masses.

A Xinhua News Agency report on May 28 stated that Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Nepal, Kirti Nidhi Bista, along with his entourage, visited factories and schools in Beijing over the past few days and received a warm welcome from the broad revolutionary workers and students.

On May 26, Vice Premier Bista, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, visited the famous Shashikou Production Brigade in Yuguzhuang People's Commune, Zunhua County, Hebei Province. The Nepalese friends learned about the achievements of the Shashikou Brigade in following Chairman Mao's teachings on self-reliance and embodying the revolutionary spirit of moving mountains like Yu Gong. Vice Premier Bista praised the achievements of Shashikou Brigade as remarkable and inspiring for the Nepalese people. He wished the Shashikou Brigade greater victories under the leadership of Chairman Mao.

Vice Premier Bista and his entourage also visited the Beijing Knitting Factory, Beijing Carpet Factory, Beijing Timber Factory, and Beijing Institute of Physical Education for Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers.

Nepal's Ambassador to China, Subba, also accompanied Vice Premier Bista on his visits.

### 比斯塔副首相在京参观访问  受到广大革命群众热烈欢迎

1968-05-29
第5版()
专栏：

    比斯塔副首相在京参观访问
    受到广大革命群众热烈欢迎
    新华社二十八日讯　尼泊尔王国副首相兼外交大臣基尔提·尼迪·比斯塔及其随行人员，连日来在北京参观访问了工厂、学校，受到了广大革命职工、革命师生的热烈欢迎。
    二十六日，比斯塔副首相由陈毅副总理陪同，访问了著名的河北省遵化县岳各庄人民公社沙石峪生产大队。尼泊尔朋友了解了沙石峪大队的社员遵照毛主席关于自力更生的教导，发扬愚公移山的革命精神，所取得的伟大成绩。比斯塔副首相称赞沙石峪大队的成绩是了不起的，是对尼泊尔人民的鼓舞。他祝贺沙石峪社员在毛主席的领导下取得更大的胜利。
    比斯塔副首相及其随行人员还访问了北京针织总厂、北京地毯总厂、北京木材厂和北京工农兵体育学院。
    尼泊尔驻中国大使苏巴，也陪同比斯塔副首相参观访问。
