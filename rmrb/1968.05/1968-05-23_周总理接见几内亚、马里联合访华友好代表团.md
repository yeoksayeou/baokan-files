Title: Premier Zhou Receives Joint Friendship Delegation from Guinea and Mali
Author:
Date: 1968-05-23
Edition: 1

Premier Zhou Enlai received the delegation from Guinea and Mali on May 22.

Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council held a friendly and cordial talk with the delegation led by Ousmane Ba, Minister of Foreign Affairs of Mali, and Lansana Beavogui, Minister of Foreign Affairs of Guinea, as well as all members of the joint friendship delegation from Guinea and Mali visiting China.

Present at the reception were relevant responsible persons such as Ji Pengfei, Xie Huai-de, and Guo Lu.

### 周总理接见几内亚、马里联合访华友好代表团

1968-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见几内亚、马里联合访华友好代表团
    新华社二十二日讯　国务院总理周恩来，今天下午接见了马里外交部长乌斯曼·巴和几内亚外交部长兰萨纳·贝阿沃吉，以及由他们率领的几内亚、马里联合访华友好代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，有关方面负责人姬鹏飞、谢怀德、郭鲁等在座。
