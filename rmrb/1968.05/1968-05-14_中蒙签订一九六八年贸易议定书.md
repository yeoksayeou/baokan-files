Title: China and Mongolia Sign the 1968 Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1968-05-14
Edition: 6

The agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic on mutual supply of goods for 1968 was signed in Beijing today.

Li Qiang, head of the Chinese Government Trade Delegation and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and Lushan, head of the Mongolian Government Trade Delegation and Chargé d'Affaires of the Mongolian Embassy in China, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments.

### 中蒙签订一九六八年贸易议定书

1968-05-14
第6版()
专栏：

    中蒙签订一九六八年贸易议定书
    新华社十三日讯　中华人民共和国政府和蒙古人民共和国政府关于一九六八年相互供应货物的议定书，今天在北京签字。
    中国政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长李强和蒙古政府贸易代表团团长、蒙古驻中国大使馆临时代办罗青，分别代表两国政府在议定书上签字。
