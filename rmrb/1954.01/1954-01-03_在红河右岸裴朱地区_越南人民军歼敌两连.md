Title: On the Right Bank of the Red River, in the Bui Chu Area, the Vietnamese People's Army Annihilates Two Enemy Companies
Author:
Date: 1954-01-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 2nd: According to a report by the Vietnam News Agency on December 31, 1953: A communique issued today stated that on December 29, the Vietnamese People's Army ambushed enemy troops on Highway 21, ten kilometers south of the Bui Chu area on the right bank of the Red River, completely annihilating two companies of the puppet army's 706th Light Infantry Battalion. The General Headquarters of the Vietnamese People's Army has awarded the victorious People's Army unit with a First Class Combat Medal.

### 在红河右岸裴朱地区  越南人民军歼敌两连

1954-01-03
第4版()
专栏：

    在红河右岸裴朱地区
    越南人民军歼敌两连
    【新华社二日讯】据越南通讯社一九五三年十二月三十一日讯：今天发表的公报说，越南人民军十二月二十九日在红河右岸裴朱地区以南十公里的第二十一号公路上伏击敌军，全歼伪军第七○六轻装营的两个连。越南人民军总司令部已授予这支胜利的人民军部队以一级战斗奖章。
