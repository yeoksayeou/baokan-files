Title: Vietnam Releases Slogans for "Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month"
Author:
Date: 1954-01-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 8th: Vietnam News Agency reported on the 8th that the Central Organizing Committee of the Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month has released the slogans for the Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month, which will soon be held nationwide in Vietnam. The main slogans are as follows:
    Long live the great Soviet Union, the leading force and fortress of the world's peace and democracy camp!
    Long live the great People's Republic of China, the fortress of the Eastern peace and democracy movement!
    Long live the eternal friendship between the Vietnamese people and the people of socialist countries and people's democratic countries!
    Long live the unbreakable friendship between Vietnam, the Soviet Union, and China!
    Learn from the Soviet and Chinese people's spirit of long-term, arduous, and self-reliant struggle to eliminate French colonialists, American interventionists, and Vietnamese traitors!
    Resolutely implement the land policies of the Vietnam Workers' Party and the Vietnamese government!
    Long live Vietnamese independence and unification!
    Long live Chairman Malenkov!
    Long live Chairman Mao Zedong!
    Long live President Ho Chi Minh!

### 越南发布“越苏中友好月”的口号

1954-01-10
第4版()
专栏：

    越南发布“越苏中友好月”的口号
      【新华社九日讯】越南通讯社八日讯：越苏中友好月中央组织委员会发布了即将在越南全国举行的越苏中友好月的口号，主要口号如下：
    世界和平民主阵营的领导力量和堡垒——伟大的苏联万岁！
    东方和平民主运动的堡垒——伟大的中华人民共和国万岁！
    越南人民与社会主义国家及人民民主国家人民之间的永恒友谊万岁！
    越、苏、中三国的牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    向苏联人民和中国人民学习长期的、坚苦的、自力更生的斗争精神，消灭法国殖民者、美国干涉者和越南的卖国贼！
    坚决实行越南劳动党和越南政府的土地政策！
    越南独立和统一万岁！
    马林科夫主席万岁！
    毛泽东主席万岁！
    胡志明主席万岁！
