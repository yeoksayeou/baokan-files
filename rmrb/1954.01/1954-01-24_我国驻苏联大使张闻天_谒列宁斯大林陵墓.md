Title: Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Zhang Wentian Pays Respects at the Lenin and Stalin Mausoleum
Author:
Date: 1954-01-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 22nd: Zhang Wentian, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, along with Ye Jizhuang, head of the Chinese Commercial Delegation currently in the Soviet Union, and Deng Tuo, head of the Chinese Journalists' Delegation visiting Pravda, on the 30th anniversary of Lenin's death (January 21st), led the staff of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, members of the Commercial Delegation, and members of the Journalists' Delegation in paying respects at the Lenin and Stalin Mausoleum. Ambassador Zhang Wentian also presented a wreath on behalf of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Central People's Government.

### 我国驻苏联大使张闻天  谒列宁斯大林陵墓

1954-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我国驻苏联大使张闻天
    谒列宁斯大林陵墓
   【新华社莫斯科二十二日电】我国驻苏联大使张闻天和现在苏联的我国商务代表团团长叶季壮、我国新闻工作者访问真理报代表团团长邓拓，在列宁逝世三十周年纪念日（二十一日）率领我国驻苏联大使馆工作人员、商务代表团和新闻工作者代表团团员，列队拜谒了列宁斯大林陵墓。张闻天大使并代表中国共产党中央委员会和中央人民政府敬献花圈。
