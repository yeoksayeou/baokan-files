Date: 1954-01-06
Page: 2

Order of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government

The "Provisional Regulations on Inspection of Import and Export Commodities," having been discussed and approved at the 198th meeting of the Government Administration Council on December 17, 1953, are hereby promulgated and implemented.

总理 Zhou Enlai
January 3, 1954
