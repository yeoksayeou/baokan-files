Title: Hungarian Working People's Party Announces Agenda for Third Congress; Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Publishes Draft Amendments to Party Constitution, Calls on Members to Discuss
Author:
Date: 1954-01-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 12th. Hungarian News Agency reported on the 10th that the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party decided to convene the Party's Third Congress on April 18th. The agenda of the Congress is as follows: 1. Report on the activities of the Party Central Committee and the tasks of the Party, presented by: Party Central Committee Secretary Rákosi; 2. On the tasks of the state administrative organs and local people's councils, presented by: Imre Nagy; 3. Report of the Central Auditing Committee, presented by: Károly Kiss; 4. Report on the revision of the Party Constitution, presented by: Lajos Ács; 5. Election of the Party's central organs.
   Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 12th. According to the Bulgarian News Agency: The Bulgarian "Workers' Cause" newspaper published the draft amendments to the Constitution of the Bulgarian Communist Party and a notice to all levels of party organizations across two pages on the 10th. The notice called on all party members and alternate members at all levels of party organizations to conduct extensive discussions on the draft amendments to the Party Constitution and to offer suggestions. The notice announced that the Party Central Committee's organ, "Workers' Cause" newspaper, will open a special column to publish opinions on the draft amendments to the Party Constitution.

### 匈劳动人民党公布第三次代表大会的议程  保共中央公布党章修改草案号召党员讨论

1954-01-13
第4版()
专栏：

    匈劳动人民党公布第三次代表大会的议程
    保共中央公布党章修改草案号召党员讨论
    【新华社布拉格十二日电】匈牙利通讯社十日讯：匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会决定在四月十八日召开党的第三次代表大会。代表大会的议程如下：一、关于党中央委员会的活动和党的任务的报告，报告人：党中央委员会书记拉科西；二、关于国家行政机关和地方人民会议的任务，报告人：伊姆雷·纳吉；三、中央检查委员会的报告，报告人：卡罗伊·基什；四、修改党章的报告，报告人：拉约希·阿契；五、选举党的中央机构。
   【新华社布拉格十二日电】据保加利亚通讯社消息：保加利亚“工人事业报”十日以两版篇幅刊载了保加利亚共产党中央委员会的党章修改草案和给各级党组织的通知。通知号召各级党组织的所有党员和候补党员对党章修改草案进行广泛的讨论，并提出意见。通知宣布：党中央机关报“工人事业报”将辟专栏发表关于党章修改草案的意见。
