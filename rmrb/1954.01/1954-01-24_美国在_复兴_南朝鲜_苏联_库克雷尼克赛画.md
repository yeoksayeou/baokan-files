United States is "Reviving" South Korea
Soviet Kukryniksy Drawing
United Press Washington telegram, August 1, 1953, reported: US Secretary of State Dulles said: US President Eisenhower has approved a plan to "aid" South Korea's "revival" and turn South Korea into a "most beautiful and attractive place." United States "International News Service" Washington telegram, August 10, 1953, reported: US Senator McClelland said: Dulles's proposal is to turn South Korea into a "showcase" for the "free world." It is also reported in newspaper news: Under the name of "aiding" South Korea's "revival," the United States is shipping large quantities of cosmetics to South Korea for sale.

	Post-armistice South Korea,
	Everywhere is hunger, poverty and death,
	The blood of orphans, the tears of widows,
	A scene of ruins, a desolate sight.
	The U.S. President is showing great "compassion,"
	Formulating a "revival" plan,
	"Generously" donating money,
	Saying it is to "aid" South Korea,
	To heal the wounds of war.
	Dulles—
	The advertising tycoon of Wall Street,
	He said: "We want to turn South Korea into the most beautiful and attractive place."
	Look! "Revival" materials are being shipped in steadily:
	Face powder, perfume, rouge, lipstick, all kinds,
	Makeup artists from New York,
	Begin to put makeup on the disheveled Syngman Rhee.
	Ah! What a "beautiful and attractive" model,
	Is about to be put on the "showcase" of the "free world."
	Seeing the goods in this "showcase,"
	Who can not "yearn" for the "free world"!
                        (Poem by Hesheng)
      (The original cartoon was published in the Soviet "Crocodile" magazine, issue 31, 1953) (Attached image)

### 美国在“复兴”南朝鲜  苏联  库克雷尼克赛画
禾生
1954-01-24
第6版()
专栏：

    美国在“复兴”南朝鲜
    苏联  库克雷尼克赛画
    合众社一九五三年八月一日华盛顿电讯报道：美国国务卿杜勒斯说：美国总统艾森豪威尔批准了一项“援助”南朝鲜“复兴”的计划，要把南朝鲜变成一个“最美丽诱人的地方”。美国“国际新闻社”一九五三年八月十日华盛顿电讯报道：美国参议员麦克利莱说：杜勒斯的主张，是要把南朝鲜变为“自由世界”的“橱窗”。又据报纸消息：美国在“援助”南朝鲜“复兴”的名义下，将大批化妆品运到南朝鲜去推销。
    停战后的南朝鲜，
    到处是饥饿、贫困和死亡，
    孤儿的血，寡妇的泪，
    一片废墟，满目荒凉。
    美国总统大发“慈悲”，
    制订了“复兴”计划，
    “慷慨”解囊，
    说是要“援助”南朝鲜，
    医治战争的创伤。
    杜勒斯——
    华尔街的广告大王，
    他说：“我们要把南朝鲜变成最美丽诱人的地方。”
    看吧！“复兴”物资源源运到：
    香粉、香水、胭脂、口红，各色各样，
    来自纽约的化装师，
    开始给焦头烂额的李承晚化装。
    啊！多么“美丽诱人”的模特儿，
    就要摆上“自由世界”的“橱窗”。
    看到这“橱窗”里的货色，
    谁对“自由世界”能不“向往”！
                        （禾生诗）
      （漫画原载苏联“鳄鱼画报”  一九五三年三十一期）（附图片）
