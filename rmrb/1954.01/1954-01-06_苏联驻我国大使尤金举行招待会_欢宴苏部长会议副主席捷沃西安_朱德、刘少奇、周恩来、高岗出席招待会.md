Title: Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin Holds Reception to Fete Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Tevosyan - Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, and Gao Gang Attend Reception
Author:
Date: 1954-01-06
Page: 1
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, January 5th: Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Academician Yudin, held a reception at 8 p.m. on the 5th to fete Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of the Metallurgical Industry I.F. Tevosyan, Director of the Planning Administration of the Soviet Ministry of Transport and Heavy Machine Building Baberev, Director of the Second Export Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade Busigin, and responsible staff member of the Metallurgical Industry Ministry Oblomsky, who were invited by the Central People's Government of China to attend the opening ceremonies of the Anshan Large-Scale Rolling Mill, Seamless Steel Tube Plant, and No. 7 Blast Furnace.
　　Those attending the reception included: Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De and Liu Shaoqi, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai, Chairman of the State Planning Commission Gao Gang, Vice Premiers of the State Council Dong Biwu and Deng Xiaoping, Members of the People's Revolutionary Military Council He Long, Liu Bocheng, Chen Yi, and Ye Jianying, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department Fu Zhong and Xiao Hua, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court Zhang Zhiran, Deputy Chief Procurator of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Gao Kelin, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Chen Shutong, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Wu Yuzhang, Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Peng Zhen, and over 120 heads of departments of the Central People's Government.
　　Ambassador Yudin and Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi delivered speeches at the reception. Vice Chairman Tevosyan gave a reply. At the meeting, the hosts and guests frequently toasted the great friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union, the health of the wise leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong, and the health of the leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and government headed by Comrade Malenkov.
　　The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 苏联驻我国大使尤金举行招待会  欢宴苏部长会议副主席捷沃西安  朱德、刘少奇、周恩来、高岗出席招待会

1954-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联驻我国大使尤金举行招待会
    欢宴苏部长会议副主席捷沃西安
    朱德、刘少奇、周恩来、高岗出席招待会
   【新华社五日讯】苏联驻我国特命全权大使尤金院士，五日下午八时举行招待会，欢宴应我中央人民政府邀请前来我国参加鞍山大型轧钢厂、无缝钢管厂和第七号炼铁炉开工典礼的苏联部长会议副主席兼冶金工业部部长伊·费·捷沃西安、苏联运输和重型机器制造工业部计划管理局局长鲍贝列夫、对外贸易部第二出口管理局局长布西金和冶金工业部负责工作人员奥布洛姆斯基。
    出席招待会的有：中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇，政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，国家计划委员会主席高岗，政务院副总理董必武、邓小平，人民革命军事委员会委员贺龙、刘伯承、陈毅、叶剑英，总政治部副主任傅钟、萧华，最高人民法院副院长张志让，最高人民检察署副检察长高克林，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通，中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章，北京市人民政府市长彭真，及中央人民政府各部门首长一百二十余人。
    尤金大使、刘少奇副主席在招待会上相继致词。捷沃西安副主席致答词。会上，宾主频频为中苏两国人民的伟大友谊，为中国人民的英明领袖毛泽东主席的健康，为以马林科夫同志为首的苏联共产党和政府的领袖们的健康干杯。
    招待会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
