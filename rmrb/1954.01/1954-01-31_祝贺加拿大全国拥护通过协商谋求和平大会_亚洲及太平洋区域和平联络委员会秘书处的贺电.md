Title: Congratulations to the Canadian National Congress for Advocating Peace Through Negotiation: Telegram from the Secretariat of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee
Author:
Date: 1954-01-31
Page: 4

　　Congratulations to the Canadian National Congress for Advocating Peace Through Negotiation
　　Telegram from the Secretariat of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee
    Xinhua News Agency, January 30th: The Secretariat of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee sent a telegram on January 26th to the Canadian National Congress for Advocating Peace Through Negotiation, congratulating them on the convening of the congress. The telegram stated:

    We are delighted to learn of the convening of the Canadian National Congress for Advocating Peace Through Negotiation, and we extend our warm congratulations to the congress. We believe that through this congress, people from all walks of life in Canada will unite more broadly to strive for the easing of international tensions and the defense of world peace. We wish the congress success.

### 祝贺加拿大全国拥护通过协商谋求和平大会  亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书处的贺电

1954-01-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　祝贺加拿大全国拥护通过协商谋求和平大会
　　亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书处的贺电
    【新华社三十日讯】亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书处在一月二十六日致电加拿大全国拥护通过协商谋求和平大会，祝贺大会的召开。电文说：
    欣悉加拿大全国拥护通过协商谋求和平大会的召开，谨向大会致以热烈的祝贺。我们相信通过这次大会，加拿大各阶层的人民定将更广泛地团结起来，为缓和国际紧张局势、保卫世界和平而奋斗。祝大会成功。
