Author:
Date: 1954-01-07
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    After conducting propaganda and education on the general line of the state's transition period, the farmers of Beishan Township, Changle County, Fujian Province, decided to sell more than 520,000 jin of surplus grain to the state and held a collective grain sale campaign. This is a scene of farmers from the brigade carrying surplus grain to the state granary.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    福建省长乐县北山乡，在进行了国家过渡时期总路线的宣传教育后，全乡农民决定把五十二万斤余粮卖给国家，并举行了一次集体售粮运动。这是大队的农民把余粮挑到国家粮库去的情形。
