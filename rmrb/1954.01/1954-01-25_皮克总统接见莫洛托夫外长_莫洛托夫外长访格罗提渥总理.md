Title: President Pieck Receives Foreign Minister Molotov; Foreign Minister Molotov Visits Premier Grotewohl
Author:
Date: 1954-01-25
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 23rd: TASS Berlin, January 23rd: Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, received V.M. Molotov, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, at the President's Office of the Niederschönhausen Palace on January 23rd. Present at the reception were: Otto Grotewohl, Premier of the German Democratic Republic, Walter Ulbricht, Deputy Premier, Otto Winzer, Head of the President's Private Office and State Secretary, and Ferdinand Thun, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Accompanying V.M. Molotov were A.A. Gromyko, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, and V.S. Semyonov, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the German Democratic Republic and Soviet High Commissioner to Germany.
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 23rd: TASS Berlin, January 23rd: V.M. Molotov, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, visited Otto Grotewohl, Premier of the German Democratic Republic, on January 23rd. Present at the visit was Lothar Bolz, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic. Accompanying V.M. Molotov were A.A. Gromyko, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, and V.S. Semyonov, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the German Democratic Republic and Soviet High Commissioner to Germany.

### 皮克总统接见莫洛托夫外长  莫洛托夫外长访格罗提渥总理

1954-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    皮克总统接见莫洛托夫外长
    莫洛托夫外长访格罗提渥总理
    【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社柏林二十三日讯：德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克于一月二十三日在尼纳德申豪森宫总统府接见苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫。接见时在座的有：德意志民主共和国总理奥托·格罗提渥，副总理瓦尔特·乌布利希，总统私人办公厅主任、国务秘书奥托·温舍和外交部交际司司长斐迪南德·图恩。陪同维·米·莫洛托夫前来的有苏联外交部副部长安·阿·葛罗米柯和苏联驻德意志民主共和国特命全权大使、苏联驻德高级专员弗·斯·谢麦诺夫。
    【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社柏林二十三日讯：苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫于一月二十三日访问德意志民主共和国总理奥托·格罗提渥。访问时在座的有德意志民主共和国外交部部长洛塔·博尔茨。陪同维·米·莫洛托夫前来的有苏联外交部副部长安·阿·葛罗米柯和苏联驻德意志民主共和国特命全权大使、苏联驻德高级专员弗·斯·谢麦诺夫。
