    Our Great Motherland
    Su embroidery is a famous folk handicraft in the Suzhou area of Jiangsu Province, China, and also a sideline production of Suzhou women. Now there are more than 20,000 women engaged in embroidery production in Suzhou alone. These two women from Xukou Township, Mudu Town, Suzhou are embroidering a peacock and plum blossom quilt cover.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-01-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    苏绣是我国江苏苏州一带有名的一种民间手工艺，也是苏州妇女的一种副业生产。现在苏州一地从事刺绣生产的妇女就有二万余人。这两位苏州木凟镇胥口乡的妇女正在绣孔雀梅花被面。
