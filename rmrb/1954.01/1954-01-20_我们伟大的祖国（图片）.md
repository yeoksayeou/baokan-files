    Our Great Motherland
    The Huainan Coal Mining Industry College is located in the Huainan mining area and is a new type of higher industrial school. In the past few years, it has provided the country with 322 personnel for coal mining industrial construction. This is a picture of students from the Huainan Coal Mining Industry College interning in the Huainan coal mine.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    淮南煤矿工业专科学校设在淮南矿区，是一所新型的高等工业学校，几年来已为国家输送了三百二十二名煤矿工业建设人材。这是淮南煤矿工业专科学校的学生在淮南煤矿矿井下实习的情形。
