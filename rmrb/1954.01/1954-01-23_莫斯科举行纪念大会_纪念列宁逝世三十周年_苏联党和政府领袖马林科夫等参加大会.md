Title: Memorial Meeting Held in Moscow to Commemorate the 30th Anniversary of Lenin's Death; Soviet Party and Government Leaders Malenkov and Others Attended the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-01-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 22nd: A memorial meeting commemorating the 30th anniversary of the death of V.I. Lenin, the great founder of the Soviet Communist Party and the Soviet state, was held at the Grand Theater in Moscow on the afternoon of the 21st.  Attending the memorial meeting were members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and members of the Moscow Oblast and Moscow City Committees, members of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation, ministers of various ministries of the Soviet government, representatives of the Moscow Oblast and Moscow City Soviets, members of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, members of the Presidium of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, representatives of the Soviet Army, as well as advanced workers, collective farmers, and prominent figures in science, culture, and the arts.

On the platform of the memorial meeting hung a giant portrait of Lenin, surrounded by flowers, with the inscription on both sides: "1924-1954." An honor guard stood under red flags. Above the platform was written the battle slogan: "Under the banner of Lenin, under the leadership of the Communist Party, advance towards the victory of Communism!"

At 6:50 PM, Soviet Party and government leaders G.M. Malenkov, V.M. Molotov, N.S. Khrushchev, N.A. Bulganin, L.M. Kaganovich, A.I. Mikoyan, M.Z. Saburov, M.G. Pervukhin, N.M. Shvernik, and P.K. Ponomarenko appeared on the platform, and all those present applauded enthusiastically.

The memorial meeting was presided over by N.S. Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. In his opening remarks, he spoke of the greatness of the immortal Leninist ideas, and the victory of the invincible Soviet Communist Party, founded by Lenin, leading the Soviet people toward the victory of Communism. Khrushchev suggested that all stand and observe a moment of silence for V.I. Lenin, the great leader and teacher of the working people of the world. After all those present stood in silent tribute, P.N. Pospelov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, delivered a report on the 30th anniversary of Lenin's death. At the end of his report, he shouted: Long live the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the great Soviet people! Long live Leninism! The hall resounded with stormy and prolonged applause.

The memorial meeting then concluded. All those present stood up and sang "The Internationale".

### 莫斯科举行纪念大会  纪念列宁逝世三十周年  苏联党和政府领袖马林科夫等参加大会

1954-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科举行纪念大会
    纪念列宁逝世三十周年
    苏联党和政府领袖马林科夫等参加大会
    【新华社二十二日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党和苏维埃国家的伟大缔造者乌·伊·列宁逝世三十周年纪念大会，二十一日下午在莫斯科的大剧院举行。参加纪念大会的有苏联共产党中央委员会委员及莫斯科州和莫斯科市委员会委员、苏联最高苏维埃主席团和俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团委员、苏联政府各部部长、莫斯科州和莫斯科市苏维埃代表、苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会委员、全苏工会中央理事会主席团委员、苏军代表，以及先进的工人、集体农民和著名的科学、文化、艺术界人士。
    纪念会的主席台上挂着列宁的巨像，像的四周饰着鲜花，两旁写着：“一九二四—一九五四”。红旗下站着仪仗队。主席台的上方写着战斗口号：“在列宁的旗帜下，在共产党的领导下，向共产主义的胜利前进！”
    六时五十分，苏联共产党和政府的领袖格·马·马林科夫、维·米·莫洛托夫、尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫、尼·亚·布尔加宁、拉·莫·卡冈诺维奇、阿·伊·米高扬、马·扎·萨布罗夫、米·格·别尔乌辛、尼·米·什维尔尼克、潘·康·波诺马连科出现在主席台上，全体到会者热烈鼓掌。
    纪念大会由苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫主持。他在开会词中谈到了不朽的列宁思想的伟大，列宁缔造的战无不胜的苏联共产党领导苏联人民走向共产主义的胜利。赫鲁晓夫建议全体起立，为全世界劳动人民的伟大领袖和导师乌·伊·列宁默哀。全体到会者肃立默哀后，苏联共产党中央委员会书记彼·纳·波斯伯洛夫在会上作了列宁逝世三十周年的报告。他在报告结束时高呼苏联共产党和伟大的苏联人民万岁！列宁主义万岁！全场响起暴风雨般的、经久不息的掌声。
    纪念大会至此结束。到会者全体起立高唱“国际歌”。
