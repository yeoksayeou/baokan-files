Author: 
Date: 1954-01-09

    Our Great Motherland
    Last year, the Northeast soybeans not only had a bumper harvest, but also the quality was better than in previous years. Northeast farmers are selling large quantities of soybeans to the state. In Litun Village, Yongji County, Jilin Province, the average soybean yield has doubled compared to the year before, and farmers are selecting good soybeans to sell to the state.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    去年东北大豆不但丰收，而且质量比往年还要好。东北农民正把大量大豆卖给国家。吉林省永吉县李屯村大豆的平均产量比前年增产一倍，农民正挑选好的大豆卖给国家。
