Our Great Motherland
Shanghai Electrical Machinery Factory workers are launching regular labor competitions centered on implementing work plans and organizing balanced production, striving to produce more generators and electric motors for the country. This is a scene of workers drilling holes in the casing of a giant electric motor.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    上海电机厂职工正以贯彻作业计划，组织均衡生产为中心，展开经常性的劳动竞赛，争取为国家生产更多的发电机和电动机。这是工人正在巨型电动机的外壳上打眼的情形。
