    Our Great Motherland
    Ulanhot (Mongolian for "Red City") is a famous city in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and one of the emerging cities in China's ethnic minority areas. This is the Genghis Khan Temple on the North Mountain in the suburbs of Ulanhot. During holidays, people of all ethnic groups come here to visit.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    乌兰浩特（蒙古语即红色的城）是内蒙古自治区的著名城市，也是我国少数民族地区的新兴城市之一。这是乌兰浩特市郊区北山上的成吉思汗庙。每逢假日，各族人民都到这里来参观游览。
