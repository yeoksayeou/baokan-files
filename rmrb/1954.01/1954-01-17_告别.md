Title: Farewell
Author: Du Hualin
Date: 1954-01-17
Page: 6

    Farewell
    Du Hualin
    Okay!
    Drink up this cup of wine.
    Let me take away
    This worn-out blue uniform,
    Together.
    Come on,
    Shake my hand.
    Don't disturb the sea,
    Don't make her sad.
    Comrade,
    You must remember:
    That big gun we used together,
    Mustn't let it rust.
    Ah!
    This is the call of the motherland,
    I'm going to participate in construction,
    You are stationed here.
    Goodbye!
    I'm leaving right away.
    Don't think of me as leaving the army,
    Just think of me as joining another battle.
    From battle to battle,
    I want to learn to operate the machine's button,
    Just like learning to fire a big gun.
    Don't worry!
    I understand:
    Being a glorious soldier,
    I must also be a skilled worker in production.

### 告别
杜华林
1954-01-17
第6版()
专栏：

    告别
    杜华林
    好！
    喝干你这杯酒。
    让我把穿旧的蓝军装，
    一齐带走。
    快来，
    跟我握手。
    别惊动了大海，
    叫她难受。
    同志，
    你要记住：
    咱们伙用的那门大炮，
    不能叫它生锈。
    啊！
    这是祖国的召唤，
    我去参加建设，
    你在这里驻守。
    别了！
    我马上就走。
    不要当我是离开部队，
    只当我去参加一次战斗。
    从战斗走向战斗，
    我要像学开大炮那样，
    学会扳机器的电钮。
    放心吧！
    我懂得：
    既是个光荣的战士，
    就要做生产的能手。
