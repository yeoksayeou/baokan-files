Title: Russia and Ukraine to Hold Celebratory Meetings Commemorating the 300th Anniversary of Ukraine's Reunification with Russia
Author:
Date: 1954-01-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 14th: TASS reported on the 12th that the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Soviet Federated Socialist Republic and the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic have decided that the Supreme Soviets of the two republics will hold separate celebratory meetings in May 1954 to commemorate the 300th anniversary of Ukraine's reunification with Russia.

### 纪念乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年  俄罗斯和乌克兰将举行庆祝大会

1954-01-15
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年
    俄罗斯和乌克兰将举行庆祝大会
   【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社十二日讯：苏联的俄罗斯联邦共和国最高苏维埃主席团和乌克兰共和国最高苏维埃主席团分别决定：两个共和国最高苏维埃于一九五四年五月分别举行庆祝大会，纪念乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年。
