Title: The Fourth Principal Repayment Lottery for the First Phase of People's Victory Indexed Bonds Will Be Held the Day After Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1954-01-30
Page: 2

The Ministry of Finance has decided that the fourth principal repayment lottery for the 1950 First Phase of People's Victory Indexed Bonds will be held publicly in advance at 1:30 PM on February 1, 1954, at the Capital Cinema on West Chang'an Street, Beijing. All bonds selected in the lottery will be redeemed for principal and paid the fourth installment of interest starting from March 31.

### 第一期人民胜利折实公债第四次还本抽签后天举行

1954-01-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　第一期人民胜利折实公债第四次还本抽签后天举行
    一九五○年第一期人民胜利折实公债第四次还本抽签，中央财政部已决定于一九五四年二月一日下午一时半在北京西长安街首都电影院先期公开举行。凡中签债券即自三月三十一日起开始兑付本金并同时支付第四期利息。
