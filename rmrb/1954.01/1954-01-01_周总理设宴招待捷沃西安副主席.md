Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Banquet for Deputy Chairman Tevosyan
Author:
Date: 1954-01-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 31st. Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, Zhou Enlai, held a banquet on the evening of the 31st to entertain the Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers and Minister of Metallurgical Industry, I.F. Tevosyan, who was specially invited by our Central People's Government to attend the opening ceremony of the large rolling mill, seamless steel tube plant, and No. 7 blast furnace of Anshan Iron and Steel Company, as well as members of the delegation he led: Bobylev, Director of the Planning Bureau of the Soviet Ministry of Transport and Heavy Machinery Manufacturing Industry; Busigin, Director of the Second Export Bureau of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Trade; and Oblomsky, a responsible staff member of the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry. Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, and embassy counselors Vasikov, Skachkov, Romanets, Arkhipov, Deputy Commercial Representative Navitsky, and other Soviet experts working in various departments of the Central People's Government were also invited to attend the banquet.

Those attending the banquet as hosts included: Gao Gang, Chairman of the State Planning Commission of the Central People's Government; Vice-Premiers of the State Council Dong Biwu, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Deng Xiaoping; Members of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Liu Bocheng, He Long, Chen Yi, Ye Jianying; Deputy Chief of General Staff Zhang Zongxun; Deputy Directors of the General Political Department Fu Zhong, Xiao Hua, Gan Siqi; Vice President of the Supreme People's Court Zhang Zhirang; Deputy Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Gao Kelin; Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen; Heads of various departments of the Central People's Government; Vice Chairmen of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Li Jishen, Chen Shutong; Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Wu Yuzhang; and approximately 200 people from cultural, educational, scientific, artistic circles.

At the meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a speech. He first welcomed the Soviet delegation to participate in the commencement of production of the three major projects of Angang, and thanked the Soviet people and the Soviet government for their care and assistance to the Chinese people in carrying out socialist industrial construction. He said: The Chinese people are deeply aware that the successful completion and commencement of production of the three major projects of Angang are inseparable from the comprehensive and selfless assistance, as well as the exquisite technology, of the great ally, the Soviet Union. He said: Every achievement of the Chinese people in the cause of socialist construction will strengthen the power of the peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union.

Deputy Chairman Tevosyan delivered a reply. He congratulated the Chinese people on the great achievements they have made in socialist industrialization and socialist transformation. He also emphasized that the great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people has great significance in defending the Far East and world peace.

During the banquet, Premier Zhou Enlai, Deputy Chairman Tevosyan, and Ambassador Yudin all raised their glasses to congratulate the unbreakable great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people, to congratulate Chairman Mao Zedong's health, and to congratulate the health of the leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and government headed by Comrade Malenkov. The guests and hosts also congratulated each other on achieving greater achievements in the coming year of 1954.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere from beginning to end. After the meeting, a music and dance evening party was held. (Photo attached)

### 周总理设宴招待捷沃西安副主席

1954-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理设宴招待捷沃西安副主席
    【新华社三十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来在三十一日晚举行宴会，招待应我中央人民政府邀请特来参加鞍山钢铁公司大型轧钢厂、无缝钢管厂、第七号炼铁炉开工典礼的苏联部长会议副主席兼冶金工业部部长伊·费·捷沃西安及其所率领的代表团团员苏联运输和重型机器制造工业部计划管理局局长鲍贝列夫，苏联对外贸易部第二出口管理局局长布西金和冶金工业部负责工作人员奥布洛姆斯基。苏联驻我国大使尤金院士、大使馆参赞华司考、司高磋、罗迈进、阿希波夫，商务副代表那维琪等，和在中央人民政府各部门工作的苏联专家也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会作陪的有：中央人民政府国家计划委员会主席高岗、政务院副总理董必武、郭沫若、黄炎培、邓小平，人民革命军事委员会委员刘伯承、贺龙、陈毅、叶剑英，副总参谋长张宗逊，总政治部副主任傅钟、萧华、甘泗淇，最高人民法院副院长张志让，最高人民检察署副检察长高克林，北京市人民政府市长彭真，中央人民政府各部门首长，中国人民政治协商会议副主席李济深、陈叔通，中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章，和文化、教育、科学、艺术界人士约二百余人。
    会上，周恩来总理致词。他首先表示欢迎苏联代表团来参加鞍钢三大工程的开工生产典礼，并感谢苏联人民和苏联政府对于中国人民进行社会主义工业建设的关怀和帮助。他说：中国人民深刻地认识到鞍钢三大工程的胜利完工和开始生产，是与伟大盟邦苏联的全面无私以及技术精湛的帮助分不开的。他说：中国人民在社会主义建设事业中的每一成就，都将增强以苏联为首的和平民主阵营的力量。
    捷沃西安副主席致答词。他祝贺中国人民在社会主义工业化和社会主义改造中所取得的巨大成就。他并强调说明中苏两国人民伟大的友谊在保卫远东和世界和平中有着伟大的意义。
    宴会中，周恩来总理、捷沃西安副主席和尤金大使都举杯祝贺中苏两国人民牢不可破的伟大友谊，祝贺毛泽东主席的健康，祝贺以马林科夫同志为首的苏联共产党和政府的领袖们的健康。宾主并互相祝贺在即将到来的一九五四年中取得更大的成就。
    宴会自始至终在热烈友好的空气中进行。会后并举行音乐歌舞晚会。（附图片）
    周恩来总理在宴会上讲话。左起第一人为捷沃西安副主席。右起第二人为尤金大使，第一人为董必武副总理。新华社记者　齐观山摄
