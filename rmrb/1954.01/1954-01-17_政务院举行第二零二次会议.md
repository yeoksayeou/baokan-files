Title: The State Council Holds its 202nd Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-01-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 15th - The 202nd meeting of the Central People's Government State Council was held on the afternoon of the 14th. The meeting listened to and approved the report made by Dong Biwu, Director of the Political and Legal Affairs Committee of the Central People's Government State Council, on the main tasks of political and legal work in 1954. The meeting approved the "Provisional Measures of the Central People's Government State Council Regarding the Revocation of Administrative Punishments for State Organ Staff." In addition, the meeting approved various lists of appointments and removals and various lists of appointments and removals to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第二零二次会议

1954-01-17
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第二零二次会议
    【新华社十五日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二百零二次政务会议在十四日下午举行。会议听了和批准了中央人民政府政务院政治法律委员会主任董必武所作的一九五四年政法工作的主要任务的报告。会议通过了“中央人民政府政务院关于撤销国家机关工作人员行政处分暂行办法”。此外，会议通过了各项任免名单和提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免的各项名单。
