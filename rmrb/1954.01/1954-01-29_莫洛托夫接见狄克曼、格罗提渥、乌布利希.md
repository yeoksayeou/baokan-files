Title: Molotov Receives Dieckmann, Grotewohl, Ulbricht
Author:
Date: 1954-01-29
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 28th: According to TASS, Berlin, January 28th, V.M. Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, received Dr. Johannes Dieckmann, President of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl, Deputy Prime Minister Walter Ulbricht, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Lothar Bolz, Deputy Prime Ministers Otto Nuschke, Hans Loch, and Paul Scholz on January 27th.
　　Present at the reception were F.S. Semyonov, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the German Democratic Republic and Soviet High Commissioner to Germany, and G.M. Pushkin, Member of the Collegium of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.
　　The conversation took place in a friendly and harmonious atmosphere.

### 莫洛托夫接见狄克曼、格罗提渥、乌布利希

1954-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　莫洛托夫接见狄克曼、格罗提渥、乌布利希
    【新华社二十八日讯】据塔斯社柏林二十八日讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席兼苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫，在一月二十七日接见德意志民主共和国人民议院主席约翰尼斯·狄克曼博士、总理奥托·格罗提渥、副总理瓦尔特·乌布利希、副总理兼外交部部长洛塔·博尔茨、副总理奥托·努舍克、汉斯·洛赫和保罗·舒尔茨。
    接见时在座的有苏联驻德意志民主共和国特命全权大使、苏联驻德高级专员弗·斯·谢麦诺夫和苏联外交部部务委员会委员格·马·普希金。
    谈话是在友好和融洽的气氛中进行的。
