Title: Lenin's Works Are Published Extensively in the Soviet Union and Around the World
Author:
Date: 1954-01-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, January 19th: TASS Moscow, January 18th – The Soviet Union is constantly publishing Lenin's various works in large quantities. According to statistics from the All-Soviet Book and Newspaper Agency, during the Soviet era, the Soviet Union has published more than one billion copies of works by Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin in 101 languages. Among them, the works of the great Lenin have been printed more than 6,000 times in 79 languages of the Soviet people and foreign languages, with a total circulation of 265.7 million copies. Among these works of Lenin, "Imperialism, the Highest Stage of Capitalism" has been printed 176 times; "State and Revolution" has been printed 165 times; "What Is To Be Done?" has been printed 139 times; "Two Tactics of Social-Democracy in the Democratic Revolution" has been printed 125 times; "‘Left-Wing’ Communism: An Infantile Disorder" has been printed 112 times; "One Step Forward, Two Steps Back" has been printed 104 times; "What the ‘Friends of the People’ Are and How They Fight the Social-Democrats", "April Theses", "A Great Beginning", "Materialism and Empirio-criticism", "On the State", "The Immediate Tasks of the Soviet Government", "The Development of Capitalism in Russia", "The Three Sources and Three Component Parts of Marxism" and other works have each been printed dozens of times. The circulation of the works printed more than four times accounts for nearly 20 percent of the total circulation. In addition, many pamphlets written by Lenin have also been reprinted many times. For example, "The Tasks of the Youth Leagues" has been printed more than 350 times in 65 languages.
    Xinhua News Agency, January 20th: TASS Moscow, January 20th - The magazine "Communist" published detailed materials on the publication of U.I. Lenin's works in various countries around the world. In the People's Republic of China and the various European People's Democracies, Lenin's various works are the most widely circulated. Bulgaria has already published 35 volumes of Lenin's Collected Works, and the circulation of the Polish edition of Lenin's Collected Works has reached 250,000 copies; the Romanian and Czechoslovak editions of Lenin's Collected Works have each circulated 100,000 copies; the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany has also decided to publish Lenin's Collected Works in German. The People's Republic of China and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea are also preparing to publish Lenin's Collected Works. In addition to Lenin's Collected Works, the People's Democracies have also published a large number of Lenin's Selected Works and separate editions of his various works. In capitalist countries, Italy, France, the United Kingdom, Austria, Greece, Belgium, the Netherlands, Denmark, Norway, Sweden, Israel, Japan, India, and other countries have published Lenin's Selected Works in recent years. In addition, the Soviet Union has printed 13,419,000 copies of Lenin's various works in various languages.

### 列宁的著作在苏联和世界各国大量出版

1954-01-21
第3版()
专栏：

    列宁的著作在苏联和世界各国大量出版
   【新华社十九日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十八日讯：苏联不断大量出版列宁的各种著作。根据全苏书报社的统计，在苏维埃政权的年代中，苏联共用一百零一种文字出版了十亿多册马克思、恩格斯、列宁和斯大林的著作。其中，伟大的列宁的著作共用七十九种苏联各族人民的文字和外国的文字印行了六千多次，总发行量达二亿六千五百七十万册。在列宁的这些著作中，“帝国主义是资本主义的最高阶段”一书印行了一百七十六次；“国家与革命”印行了一百六十五次；“做什么？”印行了一百三十九次；“社会民主党在民主革命中的两个策略”印行了一百二十五次；“共产主义运动中的‘左派’幼稚病”印行了一百一十二次；“进一步，退两步”印行了一百零四次；“什么是‘人民之友’以及他们如何攻击社会民主党人”、“四月提纲”、“伟大的创举”、“唯物主义与经验批判主义”、“论国家”、“苏维埃政权底当前任务”、“俄国资本主义底发展”、“马克思主义底三个来源与三个组成部分”和其他著作各印行了几十次。其中印行过四次的著作的发行量将近占总发行量的百分之二十。此外，列宁写的许多小册子也再版了许多次。其中如“青年团底任务”一书就用六十五种文字印行了三百五十多次。
    【新华社二十日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十日讯：“共产党人”杂志发表了乌·伊·列宁的著作在世界各国出版的详尽材料。在中华人民共和国和欧洲各人民民主国家中，列宁的各种著作流传得最广。现在，保加利亚已经出版了列宁全集三十五卷，波兰出版的列宁全集的发行量共达二十五万册；罗马尼亚和捷克斯洛伐克出版的列宁全集都发行了十万册；德国统一社会党中央委员会也决定用德文出版列宁全集。中华人民共和国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国也准备出版列宁全集。除了列宁全集外，各人民民主国家还大量出版了列宁的选集和他的各种著作的单行本。在资本主义国家中，意大利、法国、英国、奥地利、希腊、比利时、荷兰、丹麦、挪威、瑞典、以色列、日本、印度和其他国家，最近几年来都出版了列宁的选集。此外，苏联还用各国文字印行了一千三百四十一万九千册列宁的各种著作。
