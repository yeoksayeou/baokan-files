Title: Chinese Journalists Delegation Arrives in Moscow at the Invitation of "Pravda"
Author:
Date: 1954-01-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 9th: A fifteen-member Chinese journalists delegation, led by Deng Tuo, Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily, arrived in Moscow on January 8th at the invitation of the editorial board of Pravda to visit the Soviet Union. They were welcomed at Vnukovo Airport by Soviet journalists, personnel from the Chinese Embassy, and Embassy Counselor Ge Baoquan.
    The Chinese journalists were warmly welcomed by D.T. Shepilov, Editor-in-Chief of Pravda.
    In his reply, Deng Tuo, Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily, thanked the hosts for their warm reception and spoke of the delegation's pleasant feelings upon arriving in the Soviet capital, Moscow. He said that the purpose of the Chinese journalists' visit was to comprehensively learn from the advanced experience of Soviet newspapers.

### 应“真理报”邀请赴苏联访问  我新闻工作者代表团到莫斯科

1954-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    应“真理报”邀请赴苏联访问
    我新闻工作者代表团到莫斯科
    据新华社讯：塔斯社莫斯科九日讯：应真理报编辑部的邀请访问苏联的中国新闻工作者代表团一行十五人，在人民日报总编辑邓拓率领下，于一月八日到达莫斯科。在伏努科夫机场欢迎的有苏联新闻工作者、中国大使馆人员和大使馆参赞戈宝权。
    中国新闻工作者受到真理报总编辑德·特·谢皮洛夫的热烈欢迎。
    人民日报总编辑邓拓在答词中感谢主人的热烈接待，并谈到代表团抵达苏联首都——莫斯科时的愉快心情。他说，中国新闻工作者此来是为了全面学习苏联报纸的先进经验。
