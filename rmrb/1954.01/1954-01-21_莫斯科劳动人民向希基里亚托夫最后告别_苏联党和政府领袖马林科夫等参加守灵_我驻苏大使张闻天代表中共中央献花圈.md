Title: Moscow Working People Bid Final Farewell to Shkiryatov; Soviet Party and Government Leaders Malenkov and Others Attend the Wake; Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Zhang Wentian Presents Wreath on Behalf of the CPC Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1954-01-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th: According to a TASS dispatch from Moscow: On January 19th, the working people of Moscow bid their final farewell to Matvey Fedorovich Shkiryatov, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Chairman of the Party Control Committee of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

The Hall of Columns of the House of Trade Unions was immersed in an atmosphere of mourning. Crystal chandeliers and mirrors were draped in black crepe. Shkiryatov's remains were laid on a high platform, surrounded by flowers and greenery. A large red flag with a black border stood diagonally beside the remains, next to many wreaths, one of which was presented by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union. In addition, there were wreaths presented by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, the Moscow Oblast and City Committees of the CPSU, the Party Control Committee of the Central Committee of the CPSU, the Moscow Soviet, and others.

At 2 p.m., the working people entered the Hall of Columns to pay their respects to the deceased.

Mournful music filled the hall. Thousands of Muscovites, representatives of Party organizations, Soviet organizations, and representatives of various factories, institutions, schools, and scientific research institutions, took off their hats and silently walked past the remains with heavy hearts.

The leaders of the Party and the government stood vigil at Shkiryatov's bier: Malenkov, Molotov, Khrushchev, Voroshilov, Bulganin, Kaganovich, Mikoyan, Saburov, Pervukhin, Suslov, Pospelov, and Shatalin. The vigil was changed every five minutes. Those taking turns standing vigil included: Shvernik, Ponomarenko, Tevosyan, Kosygin, Malyshev, Mikhailov, Begov, Yasnov, ministers of various government ministries, marshals, generals of the army and navy, Party and Soviet activists from Moscow, workers, scientists, and cultural workers.

The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation, the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, and representatives of other departments of the Soviet government and people's organizations presented wreaths.

Foreign representatives also came to bid farewell to Matvey Fedorovich Shkiryatov, one of the oldest activists of the Communist Party and the Soviet state. Wreaths were presented at the bier by representatives of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and the communist and workers' parties of other people's democratic countries in Europe and Asia.

At 10 p.m., the Hall of Columns was closed. Subsequently, the cremation of Matvey Fedorovich Shkiryatov took place.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th: TASS Moscow, January 20th: The Funeral Committee of Matvey Fedorovich Shkiryatov announces:

Matvey Fedorovich Shkiryatov was cremated late on January 19th. The urn containing the ashes of Matvey Fedorovich Shkiryatov is placed in the Hall of Columns of the House of Trade Unions. On January 20th, the Hall of Columns will be open from 11:00 a.m. to 2:30 p.m. The funeral ceremony will be held at 3:30 p.m. on January 20, 1954, in Red Square.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th: Our Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Zhang Wentian, and the head of our Commercial Delegation in the Soviet Union, Ye Jizhuang, leading the staff of the Embassy in the Soviet Union, the Commercial Delegation, and members of our delegation of journalists visiting Pravda, lined up on the 19th to go to the House of Trade Unions to express their condolences before the bier of Matvey Fedorovich Shkiryatov, member of the Central Committee of the CPSU, Chairman of the Party Control Committee of the Central Committee of the CPSU, and member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. Ambassador Zhang Wentian also presented a wreath on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

### 莫斯科劳动人民向希基里亚托夫最后告别  苏联党和政府领袖马林科夫等参加守灵  我驻苏大使张闻天代表中共中央献花圈

1954-01-21
第3版()
专栏：

    莫斯科劳动人民向希基里亚托夫最后告别
    苏联党和政府领袖马林科夫等参加守灵
    我驻苏大使张闻天代表中共中央献花圈
    【新华社二十日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：一月十九日，莫斯科劳动人民最后告别了苏联共产党中央委员会委员、苏联共产党中央委员会的党监察委员会主席、苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员马·费·希基里亚托夫。
    工会大厦圆柱大厅沉浸在哀悼的气氛中。水晶大吊灯和镜面上披着黑纱。希基里亚托夫的遗体安放在高台上，四周围着鲜花和绿叶。遗体顶旁斜立着一面黑边大红旗，旁边是许多花圈，其中有一个是苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议献的花圈。此外还有苏联最高苏维埃主席团、苏共莫斯科州委会和市委会、苏共中央委员会的党监察委员会、莫斯科苏维埃等敬献的花圈。
    下午二时，劳动人民进入圆柱大厅瞻仰遗容。
    大厅中响起了哀乐。成千的莫斯科人，党组织、苏维埃组织的代表，各工厂、机关、学校、科学研究机构的代表们都脱下帽子，怀着沉痛的心情默默地列队在灵前走过。
    在希基里亚托夫灵前守灵的有党和政府的领袖：马林科夫、莫洛托夫、赫鲁晓夫、伏罗希洛夫、布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、米高扬、萨布罗夫、别尔乌辛、苏斯洛夫、波斯伯洛夫、夏塔林。守灵者每五分钟换一次。轮流守灵的有：什维尔尼克、波诺马连科、捷沃西安、柯西金、马利歇夫、米哈伊洛夫、别哥夫、雅斯诺夫，政府各部部长、元帅、陆海军将领，莫斯科的党和苏维埃的活动分子，工人、科学工作者和文化工作者。
    俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团、全苏工会中央理事会、苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会、以及苏联政府其他部门和人民团体的代表献了花圈。
    外国代表也前来和共产党及苏维埃国家的最老的活动家之一马·费·希基里亚托夫告别。在灵前献花圈的有：中国共产党中央委员会、朝鲜劳动党中央委员会、越南劳动党中央委员会、德国统一社会党中央委员会及欧洲和亚洲其他人民民主国家共产党和工人党的代表。
    晚上十时，圆柱大厅停止开放。接着，举行了马·费·希基里亚托夫的火葬。
   【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十日电：马·费·希基里亚托夫治丧委员会通告：
    马·费·希基里亚托夫已于一月十九日深夜火葬。马·费·希基里亚托夫的骨灰罐安放在工会大厦圆柱大厅内。一月二十日，圆柱大厅从上午十一时到下午二时三十分开放。葬仪将于一九五四年一月二十日下午三时三十分在红场举行。
   【新华社莫斯科二十日电】我国驻苏联大使张闻天和现在苏联的我国商务代表团团长叶季壮，率领驻苏使馆工作人员和商务代表团及我国新闻工作者访问真理报代表团团员，于十九日列队前往工会大厦，在苏共中央委员会委员、苏共中央委员会的党监察委员会主席、苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员马·费·希基里亚托夫灵前致哀。张闻天大使并代表中国共产党中央委员会敬献花圈。
