Title: Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Tevosian Returns to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1954-01-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 8th: I.F. Tevosian, Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers and Minister of the Metallurgical Industry, and his party, who were invited by the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China to attend the opening ceremonies of the Anshan Large-Scale Rolling Mill, Seamless Steel Tube Plant, and No. 7 Blast Furnace, returned to Moscow by plane on the 7th. Zhang Wentian, China's ambassador to the Soviet Union, went to the airport to greet them.

### 苏联部长会议副主席捷沃西安回到莫斯科

1954-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联部长会议副主席捷沃西安回到莫斯科
    【新华社莫斯科八日电】　应中华人民共和国中央人民政府邀请到我国参加鞍山大型轧钢厂、无缝钢管厂和第七号炼铁炉开工典礼的苏联部长会议副主席兼冶金工业部部长伊·费·捷沃西安等一行，已于七日乘飞机回到莫斯科。我国驻苏联大使张闻天前往机场迎接。
