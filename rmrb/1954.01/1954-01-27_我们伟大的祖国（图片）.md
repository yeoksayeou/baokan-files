Our Great Motherland
　　In the Northeast region, a 220,000-volt ultra-high-voltage power transmission line, more than 360 kilometers long (completed ahead of schedule on January 23), has been erected, which can fully supply the industrial cities of the Northeast with cheap electricity. This is a scene of workers and supporting farmers along the line working hard to lay the lines.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
    在东北地区架设了一条长达三百六十多公里的二十二万伏超高压送电线（在一月二十三日提前完成），可以充分地供应东北各个工业城市以廉价电力。这是工人和沿线支援的农民紧张放线的情形。
