Title: Residents of Phagwara, India Burn Propaganda Materials of the United States Information Service
Author:
Date: 1954-01-16
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, December 12th) According to the "New Age" weekly newspaper, residents of Phagwara in the Patiala and East Punjab States Union strongly expressed their anger against American imperialism. On December 27, 1953, residents of Phagwara collected a large number of "U.S. News & World Report" and other propaganda materials published by the United States Information Service, as well as the publication "Free Asia" issued by the "Committee for Free Asia," which is subsidized by the United States, and burned them in a concentrated manner outside the Gandhi Free Library.

As they lit the fire, they shouted slogans such as "Down with American Imperialism!" and "Oppose the establishment of American bases in Pakistan!"

### 印度法格瓦拉城的居民  焚毁美国新闻处的宣传材料

1954-01-16
第4版()
专栏：

    印度法格瓦拉城的居民
    焚毁美国新闻处的宣传材料
    【新华社十二日讯】据“新世纪”周报报道：巴地阿拉及东旁遮普邦联盟的法格瓦拉城的居民强烈地表示了他们反对美国帝国主义的愤怒。法格瓦拉城的居民于一九五三年十二月二十七日搜集了大批美国新闻处出版的“美国报道”及其他宣传材料，和接受美国津贴的“保卫自由亚洲协会”所出的刊物“自由亚洲”，在甘地自由图书馆外集中地加以焚毁。
    当他们点火焚烧时，他们高喊“打倒美国帝国主义！”“反对美国在巴基斯坦建立基地！”等口号。
