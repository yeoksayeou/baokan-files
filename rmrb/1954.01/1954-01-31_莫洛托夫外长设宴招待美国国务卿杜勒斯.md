Title: Foreign Minister Molotov Hosts Banquet for US Secretary of State Dulles
Author:
Date: 1954-01-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 30th: TASS, Berlin, January 30th: Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov hosted a banquet on January 29th in honor of US Secretary of State John Foster Dulles.

Those attending the banquet included US Ambassador to the Soviet Union Charles E. Bohlen, US High Commissioner for Germany James Conant, Assistant Secretary of State Llewellyn Thompson, Special Assistant to the US President C.D. Jackson, and Assistant Secretary of Defense F. Nash.

On the Soviet side, those attending the banquet included A.A. Gromyko, G.N. Zarubin, V.S. Semyonov, A.Y. Sobolev, and D.A. Zhukov.

### 莫洛托夫外长设宴招待美国国务卿杜勒斯

1954-01-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　莫洛托夫外长设宴招待美国国务卿杜勒斯
    【新华社三十日讯】塔斯社柏林三十日讯：苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫一月二十九日设宴招待美国国务卿约·福·杜勒斯。
    出席宴会的有美国驻苏大使查·埃·波伦、美国驻德高级专员詹·康南特、助理国务卿利·特·麦钱特、美国总统特别助理克·德·杰克逊、国防部助理部长弗·纳希。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有安·阿·葛罗米柯、格·纳·查鲁宾、弗·斯·谢麦诺夫、阿·亚·索波列夫、德·阿·茹科夫。
