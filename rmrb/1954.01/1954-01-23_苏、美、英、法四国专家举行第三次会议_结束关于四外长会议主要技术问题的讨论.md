Title: Soviet, American, British, and French Experts Hold Third Meeting, Concluding Discussions on Key Technical Issues for the Four Foreign Ministers' Conference
Author:
Date: 1954-01-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 21st: TASS reported from Berlin on January 21st: Soviet, American, British, and French experts held their third meeting on the 20th at the American military government building in Berlin. The meeting concluded discussions on key technical issues for preparing the Four Foreign Ministers' Conference and adopted corresponding resolutions. Several details that still require clarification will be discussed and resolved directly by the four countries' experts separately.

### 苏、美、英、法四国专家举行第三次会议  结束关于四外长会议主要技术问题的讨论

1954-01-23
第4版()
专栏：

    苏、美、英、法四国专家举行第三次会议
    结束关于四外长会议主要技术问题的讨论 
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社柏林二十一日讯：苏联、美国、英国和法国的专家二十日在柏林的美国军政府举行了第三次会议。会议结束了有关筹备四国外长会议主要技术问题的讨论，并且通过了相应的决议。若干尚待阐明的细节问题将由四国专家直接商量，分别解决。
