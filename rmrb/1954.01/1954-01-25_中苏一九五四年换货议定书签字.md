Title: Signing of the 1954 Sino-Soviet Trade Protocol
Author:
Date: 1954-01-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 24th: As a result of negotiations conducted in a friendly and mutually beneficial atmosphere between the Chinese trade delegation and the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Trade, the 1954 trade protocol, which stipulates further development of trade between China and the Soviet Union, and the protocol according to the agreement of February 14, 1950, regarding the Soviet Union's supply of goods to China this year by way of loans, were signed in Moscow on January 23rd.

    The signed protocol stipulates that the Soviet Union will supply China with a wide range of goods, including metallurgical equipment, mining equipment, power equipment, automobiles, tractors, agricultural machinery, construction machinery, steel, non-ferrous metal materials, petroleum products, and chemical products.

    China will supply goods such as non-ferrous metals, soybeans, rice, peanuts, vegetable oil, meat, tea, tobacco, fruit, wool, raw silk, silk fabrics, and leather.

    Signing the protocol on behalf of China was Ye Jizhuang, Vice-Chairman of the Financial and Economic Committee of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Trade; signing on behalf of the Soviet Union was S.A. Borisov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union.

### 中苏一九五四年换货议定书签字

1954-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏一九五四年换货议定书签字
    【新华社莫斯科二十四日电】由于我国商务代表团与苏联对外贸易部在友好互助的气氛中进行谈判的结果，规定进一步发展中苏两国之间换货的一九五四年换货议定书，和按照一九五零年二月十四日的协定关于本年苏联以贷款方式供给中国货物的议定书，已于一月二十三日在莫斯科签字。
    签订的议定书规定苏联将供给中国广泛项目的货物，包括冶金设备、采矿设备、动力设备、汽车、拖拉机、农业机器、建筑机器、钢材、有色金属材料、石油产品、化学产品。
    中国将供给有色金属、大豆、大米、花生、植物油、肉类、茶叶、烟草、水果、羊毛、生丝、丝织品、皮革等货物。
    我国方面在议定书上签字的是我国政务院财政经济委员会副主任、对外贸易部部长叶季壮；苏联方面签字的是苏联对外贸易部副部长斯·阿·波里索夫。
