Title: Soviet and American Exchange Views on Procedural Issues of Atomic Energy Talks, This Issue Will Be Further Discussed by Molotov and Dulles in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1954-01-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, January 21st: TASS, Washington, January 21st, reports: The Soviet Ambassador to the United States, Zarubin, again met with US Secretary of State Dulles on the 19th to continue exchanging views on the procedural issues of holding atomic energy talks. They agreed in the meeting that this issue will be further discussed in Berlin by Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov and US Secretary of State Dulles.

### 苏、美两国就原子能会谈程序问题交换意见  这一问题将由莫洛托夫和杜勒斯在柏林继续讨论

1954-01-22
第4版()
专栏：

    苏、美两国就原子能会谈程序问题交换意见
    这一问题将由莫洛托夫和杜勒斯在柏林继续讨论
　　【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社华盛顿二十一日讯：苏联驻美国大使查鲁宾十九日再次访晤美国国务卿杜勒斯，继续就举行原子能会谈的程序问题交换意见。他们在会谈中同意，这一问题将由苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫和美国国务卿杜勒斯继续在柏林讨论。
