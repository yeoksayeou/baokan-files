Title: Soviet-Czech Further Development of Scientific and Technological Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1954-01-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th: The Soviet-Czech Scientific and Technical Cooperation Committee meeting issued a communique stating that the Soviet-Czech Scientific and Technical Cooperation Committee held its fifth meeting in Moscow recently, discussed the issue of further developing scientific and technological cooperation between the two countries, and determined new obligations to be undertaken by each party.

According to the decisions of the meeting, both parties are obligated to exchange design and technical materials related to the basic construction of industrial enterprises and residences, working drawings for the production of the latest types of machines and equipment, instruction manuals for the manufacturing processes of various items, scientific and technical books, and information on business conditions.

The Soviet Union will help Czechoslovak experts understand the scientific and technological achievements and production experience of various sectors of the Soviet national economy, and will allow Czechoslovak experts to intern in Soviet industrial enterprises. Czechoslovakia will help Soviet experts understand the production experience and scientific and technological achievements of certain industrial sectors in Czechoslovakia.

In accordance with the principles of scientific and technological cooperation between the Soviet Union and various people's democratic countries, scientific and technological assistance, as well as the supply of technical materials and intelligence, are provided free of charge.

### 苏捷进一步发展两国间科学技术合作

1954-01-10
第4版()
专栏：

    苏捷进一步发展两国间科学技术合作
    【新华社九日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科八日讯：苏捷科学技术合作委员会会议发表公报说：苏捷科学技术合作委员会不久前在莫斯科举行了第五次会议，讨论了进一步发展两国科学技术合作的问题，并确定了彼此承担的新义务。
    根据会议的决定，双方有义务交换关于工业企业和住宅的基本建设的设计和技术材料，生产最新式机器和设备的工作图，制造各种物品的技术过程的说明书，科学和技术书籍以及业务情况。
    苏联将帮助捷克斯洛伐克专家了解苏联国民经济各部门的科学和技术成就以及生产经验，并将让捷克斯洛伐克专家在苏联工业企业中实习。捷克斯洛伐克将帮助苏联专家了解捷克斯洛伐克某些工业部门的生产经验以及科学和技术成就。
    根据苏联和各人民民主国家进行科学技术合作的原则，科学技术援助以及技术材料和情报的供应都是无偿的。
