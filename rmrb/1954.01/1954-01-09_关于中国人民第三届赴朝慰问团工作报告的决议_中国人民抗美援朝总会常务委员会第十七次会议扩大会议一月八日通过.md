Title: Resolution on the Work Report of the Third Chinese People's Delegation to North Korea – Adopted by the 17th Enlarged Meeting of the Standing Committee of the Chinese People's Committee for Resisting America and Aiding Korea on January 8

Author: 
Date: 1954-01-09
Page: 4

    Resolution on the Work Report of the Third Chinese People's Delegation to North Korea
    —Adopted by the 17th Enlarged Meeting of the Standing Committee of the Chinese People's Committee for Resisting America and Aiding Korea on January 8
    The 17th Enlarged Meeting of the Standing Committee of the Chinese People's Committee for Resisting America and Aiding Korea fully agrees with the work report of He Long, Head of the Third Chinese People's Delegation to North Korea.
    This meeting expresses complete satisfaction with the delegation's successful completion of the task entrusted by the people of the whole country to extend regards to the Korean people and army and the Chinese People's Volunteers, and expresses its gratitude to all comrades of the delegation.
    In order to further strengthen the people's vigilance against the aggressive schemes of American imperialism, encourage the people of the whole country to continue to deepen the movement to resist America and aid Korea, consolidate national defense, follow the general line of the country during the transition period, and actively promote the country's construction, it is necessary to once again conduct propaganda to the people of the whole country regarding the great significance of the victory of the struggle to resist America and aid Korea, publicize the glorious victories won by the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers in the war against aggression, publicize the great help of the victory of the Korean people's anti-aggression struggle to China and the great contribution to peace in the Far East and the world, and publicize the importance of mutual assistance and cooperation between the Chinese and Korean peoples. To this end, it is specially decided that the general and sub-delegations of the Third Chinese People's Delegation to North Korea will go to various parts of the country to give reports to the people, troops, organs, and schools on the work of this visit. The transmission of reports should strive for popularization and depth, and the time for the transmission of reports is set at one month. The local branches of this committee shall be responsible for organizing and assisting the progress of this work.

### 关于中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团工作报告的决议——中国人民抗美援朝总会常务委员会第十七次会议扩大会议一月八日通过

1954-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

    关于中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团工作报告的决议
    ——中国人民抗美援朝总会常务委员会第十七次会议扩大会议一月八日通过
    中国人民抗美援朝总会常务委员会第十七次会议扩大会议完全同意贺龙总团长关于中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团的工作报告。
    本会议对于慰问团胜利地完成了全国人民所委托的慰问朝鲜军民和中国人民志愿军的任务，表示完全满意，并向慰问团全体同志表示感谢。
    为了进一步加强人民群众对美帝国主义侵略阴谋的警惕性，并鼓舞全国人民继续深入抗美援朝运动，巩固国防，遵循国家在过渡时期的总路线，积极推进国家的建设事业，必须向全国人民再一次进行关于抗美援朝斗争伟大胜利意义的宣传，宣传朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军在反侵略战争中所获得的辉煌胜利，宣传朝鲜人民反侵略斗争的胜利对于中国的伟大帮助和对于远东及世界和平的伟大贡献，宣传中朝两国人民互助合作的重要。为此，特决定由中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团各总分团，分赴全国各地向人民群众和部队、机关、学校作关于这次慰问工作的传达报告。传达报告应力求普及和深入，传达报告的时间定为一个月，本会各地分会应负责组织及协助这一工作的进行。
