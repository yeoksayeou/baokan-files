Title: Molotov to Attend the Four-Power Foreign Ministers Conference in Berlin; Gromyko, Malik, Zrublev, and Others to Accompany Him

Author:

Date: 1954-01-22
Page: 4

　　【Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 21st】TASS Moscow, January 20th: Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov will soon depart for Berlin to attend the Four-Power Foreign Ministers Conference of the Soviet Union, France, Britain, and the United States.

　　Accompanying Molotov to Berlin will be Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister A.A. Gromyko, Soviet Ambassador to Great Britain Ya.A. Malik, Soviet Ambassador to the United States G.N. Zrublev, Soviet Ambassador to France S.A. Vinogradov, members of the Collegium of the Soviet Foreign Ministry G.M. Pushkin and L.F. Ilyichev, A.Ya. Sobolev, Fedelin, and other advisors and experts.

### 莫洛托夫即将赴柏林参加四国外长会议  葛罗米柯、马立克、查鲁宾等随同前往

1954-01-22
第4版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫即将赴柏林参加四国外长会议
    葛罗米柯、马立克、查鲁宾等随同前往
　　【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十日讯：苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫日内将赴柏林参加苏联、法国、英国和美国四国外长会议。
    随莫洛托夫去柏林的，将有苏联外交部副部长安·阿·葛罗米柯、苏联驻英国大使雅·亚·马立克、苏联驻美国大使格·纳·查鲁宾、苏联驻法国大使斯·阿·维诺格拉多夫、苏联外交部部务委员会委员格·马·普希金和勒·弗·伊利切夫、阿·亚·索波列夫、费德林及其他顾问和专家。
