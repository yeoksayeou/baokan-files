Our Great Motherland
    The famous Pu'er tea produced in the Xishuangbanna Dai Autonomous Region of Yunnan Province had another bumper harvest last year, with output doubling that of 1952. This is a scene of Dai tea farmers picking tea in Menghai Dam, one of the main tea-producing areas.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
    云南省西双版纳傣族自治区出产的著名普洱茶，去年又获得丰收，产量比一九五二年增加一倍。这是主要产茶区之一——勐海坝的傣族茶农摘茶的情形。
