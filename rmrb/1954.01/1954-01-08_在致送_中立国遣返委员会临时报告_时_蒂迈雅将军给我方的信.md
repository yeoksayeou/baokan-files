Title: General Thimayya's Letter to Our Side When Transmitting the "Interim Report of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission"
Author:
Date: 1954-01-08
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 7th. The full text of the letter from General Thimayya，Chairman of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission, to Marshal Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of Korean People's Army, and General Peng Dehuai, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, when transmitting the "Interim Report of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission" is as follows:

To the Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army and the Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers:

　　(I) I have the honor to submit to you an interim report of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission, which was passed by this Commission by a vote of three to zero, with the Swedish and Swiss Commissioners abstaining. The individual report submitted by the abstaining commissioners is attached to the report of this Commission, and I am also enclosing a letter written by the chairman of the subcommittee established to draft this report as Annex I (omitted). This letter was circulated in this Commission on December 19, 1953. The letters from the heads of the Swedish and Swiss delegations to me submitting their reports are also enclosed. These letters describe the circumstances that made it necessary for the Swedish and Swiss Commissioners of this Commission to submit individual reports.

　　(II) In my capacity as Chairman and Executor of this Commission, I would like to express a hope shared by all other members of this Commission: namely, that Your Excellency will give serious consideration to the treatment of prisoners of war in a manner consistent with the fundamental objectives embodied in the "Terms of Reference," and this Commission eagerly awaits Your Excellency's comments on this report.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Lieutenant General Thimayya (signed)
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Chairman of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　December 28, 1953

### 在致送“中立国遣返委员会临时报告”时  蒂迈雅将军给我方的信

1954-01-08
第2版()
专栏：

    在致送“中立国遣返委员会临时报告”时
    蒂迈雅将军给我方的信
   【新华社平壤七日电】中立国遣返委员会主席蒂迈雅将军在致送“中立国遣返委员会临时报告”时给朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成元帅与中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军的信，全文如下：朝鲜人民军最高司令官与中国人民志愿军司令员：
      （一）我荣幸地把中立国遣返委员会的一份临时报告提交给你们，此一报告经本委员会以三对零票通过，瑞典和瑞士委员弃权，弃权委员提出的那份个别报告附在本委员会的报告中，我并把为起草这一报告而设立的小组委员会的主席所写的一封信作为附件一随函附上（略）。该信已于一九五三年十二月十九日在本委员会中传阅，瑞典和瑞士各代表团团长为提交他们的报告而给我的那封信也随函附上。这些信叙述了使本委员会瑞典和瑞士委员必须提出个别报告的情况。
      （二）我以本委员会的主席和执行人的资格，愿意表达一个为本委员会所有其他委员所共有的希望：即希望贵方对战俘的处理问题以符合于“职权范围”中所体现的基本目标的态度予以认真的考虑，因此本委员会殷切期待你方对于这一报告的评论。
    中立国遣返委员会主席蒂迈雅中将（签名）
                 一九五三年十二月二十八日
