Title: USSR Delineates Electoral Districts for Election of Deputies to the Supreme Soviet

Author:
Date: 1954-01-14
Page: 4

    USSR Delineates Electoral Districts for Election of Deputies to the Supreme Soviet
    (Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th) TASS Moscow, 12th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR issued an order on January 11, 1954:
    In accordance with Articles 34 and 35 of the Constitution of the USSR and Articles 24, 25, and 26 of the "Regulations for Elections to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR," 700 electoral districts for the election of deputies to the Union Council and 631 electoral districts for the election of deputies to the Council of Nationalities are hereby delineated.

### 苏联划定选举最高苏维埃代表的选举区

1954-01-14
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联划定选举最高苏维埃代表的选举区
    【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科十二日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团一九五四年一月十一日命令：
    根据苏联宪法第三十四和三十五条及“苏联最高苏维埃选举条例”第二十四、二十五和二十六条的规定，兹划分七百个选举联盟院代表的选举区和六百三十一个选举民族院代表的选举区。
