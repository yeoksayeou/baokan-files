Title: Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling Participates in Basic-Level Election Voting in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1954-01-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, January 29th. Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling of the Central People's Government, along with Chen Yi, Tan Zhenlin, and other leading officials from the East China region and Shanghai municipality, have fulfilled their civic duty alongside the voters of Shanghai by casting their ballots to elect representatives to the Shanghai Municipal Basic-Level People's Congress.

　　On the morning of January 26th, the auditorium of the East China Administrative Committee, designated as the fourth polling station for the 19th electoral district of Jing'an District in Shanghai, was freshly decorated. Red flags adorned the walkway from the entrance to the venue, and the national flag and a portrait of Chairman Mao hung high on the stage. Colorful lanterns and crimson ballot boxes, along with beautiful flowers, reflected each other in the front of the stage. Voters began to enter the venue at 9:45 a.m. Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling of the Central People's Government, Vice Chairman of the East China Administrative Committee and Third Secretary of the CCP Central Committee East China Bureau Tan Zhenlin, and Vice Chairmen of the East China Administrative Committee Su Yu and Zhang Dingcheng also arrived at the venue. In accordance with the provisions of the Election Law, after the chairman reported on the process of proposing candidates and individually introduced each candidate, voting commenced. At 10:30 a.m., Soong Ching Ling, Tan Zhenlin, Su Yu, Zhang Dingcheng, and other voters successively walked onto the stage to deposit their ballots into the ballot box.

　　The voters from the Shanghai Municipal People's Government organs held their voting on the 19th. In the five or six days prior to the voting, the office building of the Municipal People's Government was carefully decorated by the voters. Rows of large palace lanterns were hung at the entrance of the office building and in the corridors on each floor. Inside the Shanghai Municipal People's Government Auditorium, which served as the polling station, a huge golden slogan celebrating the general election was hung. Voters from the Shanghai Municipal People's Government organs enthusiastically participated in the voting. Chen Yi, Second Secretary of the CCP Central Committee East China Bureau and Mayor of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, and Vice Mayors of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government Pan Hannian, Sheng Pihua, Xu Jianguo, and Liu Jiping all participated in the voting in the electoral district.

　　At the same time, voters from the organs of the CCP Central Committee East China Bureau and the CCP Shanghai Municipal Committee, among others, also held voting in their respective electoral districts. Chen Pixian, Fourth Secretary of the CCP Shanghai Municipal Committee, participated in the voting at the location of the CCP Shanghai Municipal Committee organs.

　　According to the election results statistics from the East China Administrative Committee, the CCP Central Committee East China Bureau, the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, and the CCP Shanghai Municipal Committee, 100% of all voters from these four organs in Shanghai participated in the voting.

### 宋庆龄副主席参加上海市基层选举投票

1954-01-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　宋庆龄副主席参加上海市基层选举投票
    【新华社上海二十九日电】中央人民政府副主席宋庆龄和华东区、上海市一级机关的首长陈毅、谭震林等，已和上海市的选民一道履行了公民的义务，投票选举了上海市基层人民代表大会的代表。
    二十六日上午，作为上海市静安区第十九选区选举大会第四会场的华东行政委员会大礼堂，被装饰得焕然一新。从入口处到会场的走道上插着许多红旗，主席台上高悬着国旗和毛主席画像，挂在台前的彩灯和朱红色的票箱、美丽的鲜花互相辉映。上午九时四十五分，选民们陆续走进会场。中央人民政府副主席宋庆龄和华东行政委员会副主席、中共中央华东局第三书记谭震林，华东行政委员会副主席粟裕、张鼎丞等也来到会场。按照选举法的规定，当主席报告了代表候选人提出的经过和逐一作了候选人的介绍以后，投票即告开始。十时三十分，宋庆龄、谭震林、粟裕、张鼎丞等和其他选民一道，相继走上主席台把选票投入票箱。
    上海市人民政府机关的选民在十九日举行了投票。投票以前的五六天，市人民政府的办公大楼就被选民们精心地装饰起来。办公大楼的门口和每层楼上的走道都挂上成排的大宫灯，作为投票站的市人民政府大礼堂内，悬挂着庆祝普选的巨大的金色标语。市人民政府机关的选民们踊跃地参加了投票。中共中央华东局第二书记、上海市人民政府市长陈毅，上海市人民政府副市长潘汉年、盛丕华、许建国、刘季平等都参加了该选区的投票。
    与此同时，中共中央华东局和中共上海市委员会等机关的选民，也都在所在选区举行了投票。中共上海市委员会第四书记陈丕显在中共上海市委员会机关所在地参加了投票。
    根据华东行政委员会、中共中央华东局、上海市人民政府、中共上海市委员会等单位选举的结果统计，这四个机关所有在上海市的选民，百分之百地参加了投票。
