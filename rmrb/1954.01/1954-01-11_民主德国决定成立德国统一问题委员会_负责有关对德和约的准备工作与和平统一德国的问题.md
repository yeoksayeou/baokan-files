Title: German Democratic Republic Decides to Establish Committee on German Unification to Prepare for a German Peace Treaty and Peaceful Unification of Germany
Author:
Date: 1954-01-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 8th: The German Democratic Republic Prime Minister's Press Office reported: The Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic held a meeting on January 7th.

The Council of Ministers accepted Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl's proposal to establish a Committee on German Unification, responsible for all matters related to the preparation of a German peace treaty and the restoration of German unification, as well as the establishment of a democratic, peace-loving, and independent Germany. The Council of Ministers accepted Grotewohl's proposal to elect Deputy Prime Minister Loch as Chairman of the Committee, and Professor Norden as Secretary; Norden was also appointed State Secretary.

The Council of Ministers then discussed the decision to establish a Ministry of Culture for the German Democratic Republic. State Prize winner and poet Johannes Becher was appointed Minister of Culture of the German Democratic Republic.

In view of the cessation of reparations payments to the Soviet Union starting from January 1954, the Council of Ministers decided to abolish the Reparations Bureau. The Council of Ministers, on behalf of all the German people, expressed gratitude for the generous assistance of the Soviet government.

### 民主德国决定成立德国统一问题委员会  负责有关对德和约的准备工作与和平统一德国的问题

1954-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国决定成立德国统一问题委员会
    负责有关对德和约的准备工作与和平统一德国的问题
    【新华社十日讯】据塔斯社柏林八日讯：德意志民主共和国总理新闻处报道：德意志民主共和国部长会议于一月七日举行会议。
    部长会议接受奥托·格罗提渥总理的建议，决定成立一个德国统一问题委员会，负责有关对德和约的准备工作与恢复德国统一、建立民主、爱好和平与独立的德国的一切问题。部长会议接受格罗提渥的建议，选出副总理洛赫为委员会主席，诺登教授为秘书；诺登同时被任命为国务秘书。
    部长会议然后讨论了关于成立德意志民主共和国文化部的决定。国家奖金得奖人、诗人约翰尼斯·贝希尔被任命为德意志民主共和国文化部部长。
    部长会议鉴于自一九五四年一月起停止支付对苏联的赔款，决定取消赔偿事务局。部长会议代表全体德国人民对于苏联政府的慷慨援助表示感激。
