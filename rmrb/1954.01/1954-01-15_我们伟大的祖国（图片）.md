Our Great Motherland
The Blue Har sheep bred at the Gongnaisi sheep farm in the southeast of Yining County, Xinjiang Province, has an average of four to five hundred heads transported to Northeast China, Shaanxi, Gansu, Qinghai, Ningxia and other places every year, and are welcomed by the majority of herdsmen in pastoral areas. This is the Blue Har sheep flock of the Gongnaisi sheep farm.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-01-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新疆省伊宁县东南的巩乃斯种羊场所培育的蓝哈羊，平均每年有四、五百只运往东北、陕西、甘肃、青海、宁夏等地，受到广大牧区牧民的欢迎。这是巩乃斯种羊场的蓝哈羊群。
