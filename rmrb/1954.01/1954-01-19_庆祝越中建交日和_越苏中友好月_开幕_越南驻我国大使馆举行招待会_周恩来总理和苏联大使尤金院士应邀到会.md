Title: Vietnamese Embassy in Our Country Holds Reception to Celebrate the Anniversary of the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations between Vietnam and China and the Opening of the "Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month"; Premier Zhou Enlai and Soviet Ambassador Academician Yudin Invited to Attend
Author: 
Date: 1954-01-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 18th:

    To celebrate the anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the Democratic of Vietnam and the People’s Republic of China and the commencement of the Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month throughout Vietnam, Zhou Liang, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, held a reception in Beijing on January 18th.

    Those invited to attend included: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government; Guo Moruo, Vice Premier; Huang Kecheng, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Central People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department; Teng Daiyuan, Minister of Railways of the Central People's Government; Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture; Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; and heads of various departments of the Central People's Government.

    Also invited were: Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Liu Ningyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace; Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth; leaders of various people's organizations; and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, totaling over two hundred people.

    Also invited were: Soviet Ambassador to China, Academician Yudin, as well as diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various countries.

    Charge d'Affaires ad interim Zhou Liang said in his speech at the reception: In recent years, thanks to the efforts of the Vietnamese people themselves and the wise leadership of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the Vietnamese government, and President Ho Chi Minh, and with the enthusiastic support of fraternal countries and peace-loving people around the world, the Vietnamese people have achieved many successes and will win even greater victories. He continued: Currently, the Vietnamese people are shouldering glorious and great tasks. To accomplish these tasks, the Vietnamese people believe it is necessary to strengthen the study of the valuable combat and construction experiences of fraternal countries and further enhance solidarity and friendship with fraternal countries.

    Premier Zhou Enlai spoke next. He said: The struggle for freedom and national independence waged under the leadership of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the Vietnamese government, and President Ho Chi Minh has achieved significant victories. The imperialists' dream of enslaving the Vietnamese people has repeatedly failed in the face of the Vietnamese people's heroic resistance. The long-term and unyielding struggle of the Vietnamese people against imperialism has made outstanding contributions to the cause of defending peace in the Far East and the world. Currently, peace-loving people all over the world are demanding a swift end to the war of aggression in Vietnam. He also said: I am deeply convinced that due to the heroic struggle and unremitting efforts of the Vietnamese people, as well as the growing sympathy and support of people all over the world, the just struggle of the Vietnamese people will surely achieve final victory.

    Academician Yudin also spoke at the reception. He said: The Soviet people and peace-loving people all over the world are watching the Vietnamese people's struggle for independence and freedom; we are delighted with every achievement the Vietnamese people have made in their struggle. He also said: The holding of the Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month will bring the people of Vietnam, the Soviet Union, and China more closely together and will strengthen the Vietnamese people's liberation struggle.

    The reception took place in a friendly and warm atmosphere.

### 庆祝越中建交日和“越苏中友好月”开幕  越南驻我国大使馆举行招待会  周恩来总理和苏联大使尤金院士应邀到会

1954-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝越中建交日和“越苏中友好月”开幕
    越南驻我国大使馆举行招待会
    周恩来总理和苏联大使尤金院士应邀到会
     【新华社十八日讯】越南民主共和国驻华大使馆临时代办周亮十八日在北京举行招待会，庆祝越南民主共和国和中华人民共和国建立邦交日和越苏中友好月在越南全国开始举行。
    应邀到会的有：我中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来、副总理郭沫若，中央人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长黄克诚、总政治部副主任萧华，中央人民政府铁道部部长滕代远，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长章汉夫以及中央人民政府各部门首长。
    应邀到会的还有：中苏友好协会总干事钱俊瑞、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘宁一、中华全国民主青年联合会主席廖承志和各人民团体负责人以及北京市副市长吴晗等共二百多人。
    应邀到会的还有：苏联驻华大使尤金院士以及各国驻华使节及使馆人员。
    周亮临时代办在招待会上致词说：近几年来，由于越南人民自身的努力和越南劳动党、越南政府和胡志明主席的英明领导；同时由于各兄弟国家和世界爱好和平人民的热烈支持，越南人民业经取得许多成绩，并将赢得更大的胜利。他接着说：目前越南人民正担负着光荣的伟大的任务。为了完成这些任务，越南人民认为必须加强学习各兄弟国家宝贵的战斗经验和建设经验，进一步增强和各兄弟国家的团结友谊。
    周恩来总理继起讲话。他说：越南人民在越南劳动党、越南政府和胡志明主席领导下所进行的争取自由和民族独立的斗争已经获得了重大的胜利。帝国主义梦想奴役越南人民的企图在越南人民的英勇抗击下已经一再失败。越南人民反对帝国主义的长期的、不屈不挠的斗争，对于保卫远东及世界的和平事业作了卓越的贡献。目前全世界爱好和平的人民都要求迅速停止在越南进行的侵略战争。他又说：我深信：由于越南人民英勇的战斗和不懈的努力，以及全世界人民日益增长的同情和支持，越南人民的正义斗争必将取得最后的胜利。
    尤金院士也在招待会上讲话。他说：苏联人民和全世界爱好和平的人民都在注视着越南人民为争取独立和自由而进行的斗争；越南人民在斗争中所获得的每一个成就，都使我们欢欣鼓舞。他又说：越苏中友好月的举行将使越南、苏联、中国三国人民更紧密地团结起来，并将加强越南人民的解放斗争。
    招待会在友好和热烈的空气中进行。
