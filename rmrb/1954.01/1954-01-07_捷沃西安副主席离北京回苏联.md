Title: Deputy Chairman Tevosyan Leaves Beijing for the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1954-01-07
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, 6th) I.F. Tevosyan, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of the Metallurgical Industry, and the members of the Soviet government delegation he led, namely, Baberev, Director of the Planning Administration of the Ministry of Transportation and Heavy Machinery Manufacturing of the Soviet Union, Busigin, Director of the Second Export Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and Oblomski, a responsible staff member of the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry, who were invited by our Central People's Government to attend the opening ceremonies of the three major projects in Anshan: the large-scale rolling mill, the seamless steel tube plant, and the No. 7 blast furnace, left Beijing by plane this morning to return to their country.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Gao Gang, Chairman of the State Planning Commission of our Central People's Government; Li Fuchun, Vice Chairman; Wang Heshou, Minister of Heavy Industry; Huang Jing, Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building; Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Li Zheren, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Lü Dong and Lai Jifa, Vice Ministers of Heavy Industry; Wang Daohan, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building; Lai Zulie, Director of the Bureau of Experts Affairs of the Government Administration Council; and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, and many embassy staff members also went to the airport to see them off.

In addition, I.F. Tevosyan, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of the Metallurgical Industry, and the members of the Soviet government delegation he led, namely, Baberev, Director of the Planning Administration of the Ministry of Transportation and Heavy Machinery Manufacturing of the Soviet Union, Busigin, Director of the Second Export Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and Oblomski, a responsible staff member of the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry, accompanied by Huang Jing, Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building of our Central People's Government, and Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, visited Nanjing, Shanghai, and other places on the 2nd, and returned to Beijing by special plane on the afternoon of the 5th.

Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, and Counselor Huasike of the Embassy, who accompanied them on the tour, also returned to Beijing on the same plane.

Those who welcomed them at the airport at that time included: Wang Heshou, Minister of Heavy Industry; Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department; and diplomats from the Soviet Embassy in China.

### 捷沃西安副主席离北京回苏联

1954-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

    捷沃西安副主席离北京回苏联
   【新华社六日讯】应我中央人民政府邀请前来我国参加鞍山大型轧钢厂、无缝钢管厂和第七号炼铁炉三大工程开工典礼的苏联部长会议副主席兼冶金工业部部长伊·费·捷沃西安，及其所率领的苏联政府代表团团员苏联运输和重型机器制造工业部计划管理局局长鲍贝列夫、对外贸易部第二出口管理局局长布西金和冶金工业部负责工作人员奥布洛姆斯基，在六日晨乘飞机离北京返国。
    到机场欢送的有：我中央人民政府国家计划委员会主席高岗、副主席李富春，重工业部部长王鹤寿，第一机械工业部部长黄敬，外交部副部长伍修权，对外贸易部副部长李哲人，重工业部副部长吕东、赖际发，第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，政务院专家事务管理局局长赖祖烈，外交部交际处处长王倬如。
    苏联驻我国大使尤金院士和使馆馆员等多人也到机场欢送。
    又讯：苏联部长会议副主席兼冶金工业部部长伊·费·捷沃西安及其所率领的苏联政府代表团团员苏联运输和重型机器制造工业部计划管理局局长鲍贝列夫、对外贸易部第二出口管理局局长布西金和冶金工业部负责工作人员奥布洛姆斯基，由我中央人民政府第一机械工业部部长黄敬、外交部办公厅主任王炳南等陪同，曾于二日赴南京、上海等地参观，在五日下午乘专机回到北京。
    陪同参观的苏联驻我国大使尤金院士、大使馆参赞华司考亦同机返京。
    当时到机场欢迎的有：重工业部部长王鹤寿，外交部副部长伍修权、交际处副处长王拓和苏联驻华大使馆外交官等。
