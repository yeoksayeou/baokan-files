Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish Kim Il-sung and Peng Dehuai's Letter to Thimayya
Author:
Date: 1954-01-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 8th: Moscow newspapers on the 8th all published the letter from Marshal Kim Il-sung and General Peng Dehuai to Lieutenant General Thimayya regarding the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission's interim report and the Commission Chairman's letter and memorandum.
