Title: Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov Informs U.S. Ambassador Bohlen of Agreement to Discuss Procedures for Atomic Energy Talks with the United States
Author:
Date: 1954-01-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th. TASS reports: On December 31, 1953, U.S. Ambassador Charles Bohlen visited Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov and informed him that, in view of the Soviet government's December 21st statement expressing willingness to participate in atomic energy talks in response to President Eisenhower's December 8th speech, the U.S. government wished to exchange views on the procedural issues related to the aforementioned talks, namely, questions concerning the time, place, and progress of the talks. Mr. Charles Bohlen added that this exchange of views could be conducted by the U.S. State Department and the Soviet Ambassador in Washington or the Soviet Representative to the United Nations before the Berlin Conference of the Four Foreign Ministers, or during the aforementioned conference in Berlin.

On January 6th, V.M. Molotov received Charles Bohlen and informed him that the Soviet government had studied the U.S. government's proposal. The Soviet government also believes that discussions should be held on the aforementioned issues and agrees to hold the discussions in Washington. The Soviet government has specifically authorized Soviet Ambassador to the United States N.G. Chalybin to participate in these discussions.

### 苏联外长莫洛托夫通知美驻苏大使波伦  同意和美国讨论有关原子能会谈程序问题

1954-01-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联外长莫洛托夫通知美驻苏大使波伦
    同意和美国讨论有关原子能会谈程序问题
     【新华社七日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科七日讯：一九五三年十二月三十一日，美国大使查尔斯·波伦访问了苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫，并通知他说：由于苏联政府十二月二十一日就艾森豪威尔总统十二月八日的演说所发表的声明表示愿意参加原子能会谈，美国政府希望就有关上述会谈的程序问题，即有关时间、地点与会谈进程的问题交换意见。查尔斯·波伦先生又说：交换意见的工作可由美国国务院和苏联驻华盛顿大使或苏联驻联合国代表在四国外长柏林会议以前举行，或在柏林的上述会议中进行。
    一月六日，维·米·莫洛托夫接见了查尔斯·波伦，并通知他苏联政府已经研究了美国政府的建议。苏联政府也认为应就上述问题进行讨论，并同意讨论在华盛顿进行。苏联政府方面特授权苏联驻美国大使纳·格·查鲁宾参加这次讨论。
