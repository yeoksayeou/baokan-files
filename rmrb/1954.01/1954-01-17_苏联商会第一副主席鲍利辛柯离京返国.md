Title: First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Chamber of Commerce, Borisinko, Departs Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1954-01-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Borisinko, First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Chamber of Commerce, arrived in Beijing on November 17, 1953, to prepare for the "Exhibition of Achievements in Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction" to be held in our country. He also visited Shanghai and Guangzhou. Now that the preparations for the exhibition have come to an end, Borisinko left Beijing by plane on the 16th to return home.
    Among those who went to the airport to see him off were Ji Chao-ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and Song Yuhe, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Construction Engineering of the Central People's Government. Navalicki, Deputy Commercial Representative of the Soviet Embassy in China, also went to the airport to see him off.

### 苏联商会第一副主席鲍利辛柯离京返国

1954-01-17
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联商会第一副主席鲍利辛柯离京返国
    【新华社十六日讯】苏联商会第一副主席鲍利辛柯，为筹备将在我国举办的“苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会”，曾在一九五三年十一月十七日抵达北京，并曾到上海和广州参观。现在展览会的筹备工作已告一段落，鲍利辛柯于十六日乘飞机离京返国。
    前往机场欢送的有中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎和中央人民政府建筑工程部副部长宋裕和等。苏联驻我国大使馆商务副代表那维琪也到机场欢送。
