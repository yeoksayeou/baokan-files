Title: Experts from the Soviet Union, the United States, the United Kingdom, and France Hold Meeting: Agreement Reached on Major Technical Issues for the Four Foreign Ministers' Conference
Author: 
Date: 1954-01-22
Page: 4
　
　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 20th: TASS reports that experts from the Soviet Union, the United States, the United Kingdom, and France held a meeting on the 19th at the Soviet High Commissioner's Office in Germany to discuss technical issues related to the preparation of the Four Foreign Ministers' Conference. The four countries' experts reached agreement on major technical issues and decided to continue discussing technical issues on the 20th at the American military government in Berlin.

### 苏、美、英、法四国专家举行会议  在四外长会议的主要技术问题上达成协议

1954-01-22
第4版()
专栏：

    苏、美、英、法四国专家举行会议
    在四外长会议的主要技术问题上达成协议
　　【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社柏林二十日讯：苏联、美国、英国和法国的专家十九日在柏林的苏联驻德高级专员公署举行会议，讨论了有关筹备四国外长会议的技术问题。四国专家在主要的技术问题上达成了协议，并且决定二十日在柏林的美国军政府继续讨论技术问题。
