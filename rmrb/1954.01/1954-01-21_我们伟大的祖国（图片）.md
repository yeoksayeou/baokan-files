Our Great Motherland
The Lan(zhou)-Ning(xia) railway line, which is currently under survey, will pass through a section of desert area. In order to overcome many technical difficulties in construction, our country's technical personnel are studying geological conditions in the desert area with Soviet line experts and bridge experts. This is a scene of them riding camels to select a route in the desert area.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-01-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    正在勘测中的兰（州）宁（夏）线路，要经过一段沙漠地区。为了克服修筑技术上的许多困难，我国的技术人员正和苏联线路专家、桥梁专家等在沙漠地区研究地质的情况。这是他们骑着骆驼在沙漠地区选择线路的情形。
