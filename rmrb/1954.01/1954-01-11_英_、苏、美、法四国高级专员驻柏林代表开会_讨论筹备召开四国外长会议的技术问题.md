Title: Representatives of the Four Allied High Commissioners in Berlin Meet to Discuss Technical Issues in Preparation for the Four Foreign Ministers Conference
Author:
Date: 1954-01-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 10th: According to TASS, the representatives of the British, Soviet, American, and French High Commissioners in Berlin, Major General Coleman, S. A. Jangen, Major General Timberman, and Brigadier General Moussot-Di Méo, held two meetings on January 7th and 9th, respectively, at the British Military Government Office in Berlin and the Office of the Soviet High Commissioner, to discuss technical issues related to the preparation for the Four Foreign Ministers Conference. The next meeting is scheduled for January 11th at the American Military Government Office.

### 英  、苏、美、法四国高级专员驻柏林代表开会  讨论筹备召开四国外长会议的技术问题

1954-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    英  、苏、美、法四国高级专员驻柏林代表开会
    讨论筹备召开四国外长会议的技术问题
    【新华社十日讯】据塔斯社柏林讯：英国、苏联、美国和法国高级专员驻柏林代表科尔曼少将、谢·阿·詹根、丁伯曼少将和芒梭—迪米奥准将于一月七日和九日先后在柏林英国军政府办公厅和苏联高级专员公署举行了两次会议，讨论了有关筹备召开四国外长会议的技术问题。下一次会议定于一月十一日在美国军政府举行。
