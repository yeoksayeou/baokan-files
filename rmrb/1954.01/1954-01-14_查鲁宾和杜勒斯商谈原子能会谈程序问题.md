Title: Zaruin and Dulles Discuss Procedural Issues for Atomic Energy Talks
Author:
Date: 1954-01-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, January 13th: TASS, Washington, January 13th: On January 11th, the Soviet Ambassador to the United States, Zaruin, visited the United States Secretary of State, Dulles. It is reported that Zaruin and Dulles held talks for about half an hour, exchanging views on procedural issues for talks to be held based on U.S. President Eisenhower's speech on December 8th of last year and the Soviet government's statement on December 21st of last year regarding the use of atomic energy. Before Dulles departs for Berlin to attend the Four Power Foreign Ministers Conference, U.S. Secretary of State Dulles and Soviet Ambassador Zaruin will meet again.

### 查鲁宾和杜勒斯商谈原子能会谈程序问题

1954-01-14
第4版()
专栏：

    查鲁宾和杜勒斯商谈原子能会谈程序问题
    【新华社十三日讯】塔斯社华盛顿十三日讯：一月十一日，苏联驻美大使查鲁宾拜访了美国国务卿杜勒斯。据悉，查鲁宾和杜勒斯进行了约半小时的会谈，就根据美国总统艾森豪威尔去年十二月八日的演说和苏联政府去年十二月二十一日关于原子能使用问题的声明举行会谈的程序问题，交换了意见。在杜勒斯启程赴柏林出席四国外长会议以前，美国国务卿杜勒斯和苏联大使查鲁宾将再会谈一次。
