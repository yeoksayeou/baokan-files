Title: Czechoslovak Government Approves 1954 National Economic Development Plan
Author:
Date: 1954-01-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 9th. According to the "Red Right" newspaper, the government of the Czechoslovak Republic held a special meeting in Prague on the 6th. The meeting discussed and approved the plan for the development of the national economy in 1954, submitted by the chairman of the State Planning Commission. The plan was formulated in accordance with the government's decision in September 1953, with the aim of raising the living standards of the working people on the basis of further developing production, increasing labor productivity, and practicing strict economy. The meeting decided to submit the plan as a draft to the National Assembly for deliberation.

### 捷政府通过一九五四年发展国民经济计划

1954-01-12
第4版()
专栏：

    捷政府通过一九五四年发展国民经济计划
    【新华社布拉格九日电】据“红色权利报”报道：捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府六日在布拉格举行了特别会议。会议讨论并批准了国家计划委员会主席所提出的关于一九五四年发展国民经济的计划。该项计划是根据一九五三年九月政府的决议制订的，目的是在进一步发展生产、提高劳动生产率和厉行节约的基础上来提高劳动人民的生活水平。会议决定将该项计划作为草案提交国民议会审议。
