Title: Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Holds Report Meeting to Promote Further Study of the General Line by Cadres Throughout the Region; Comrade Lin Feng Proposes Key Tasks for the Entire Northeast Region This Year
Author: 
Date: 1954-01-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 20th -- The Propaganda Department of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, to further promote in-depth study and publicity of the general line and general task of the state during the transition period by cadres throughout the region, held three report meetings from January 13th to 15th. Lin Feng, First Deputy Secretary of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Zhang Mingyuan, Third Deputy Secretary, and Zhao Dezun, Minister of the Rural Work Department, respectively made reports on issues such as "The Party's General Line and General Task During the Transition Period," "The Implementation of Socialist Industrialization in Our Country," and "Gradually Realizing the Socialist Transformation of Agriculture." More than 1,700 people attended, including cadres from Northeast first-level organs, responsible cadres from the CPC Northeast Region's provinces and cities, and cadres from the People's Liberation Army Northeast Military Region stationed in Shenyang. In his report, Lin Feng answered some of the main questions raised by cadres in various regions during their study of the general line, provided specific instructions for the study and publicity of the general line, and, after summarizing the basic situation of work in the Northeast region in 1953, proposed the main tasks for the entire Northeast region in 1954.

Regarding the Party's main tasks in the Northeast region in 1954, Lin Feng said: They are, based on the Party's general line and general task during the transition period and the basic tasks of the state's First Five-Year Plan, and on the basis of the work in 1953, to guarantee the comprehensive completion and over-fulfillment of the state's First Five-Year Plan's second-year plan in all aspects, especially to guarantee the completion of the state's key construction projects. Lin Feng pointed out: The Northeast region is a key area of the state's First Five-Year Plan, and it undertakes an extremely important task in realizing the country's socialist industrialization. We should redouble our efforts to do a good job in the construction of the Northeast and successfully complete the glorious and arduous tasks given to us by the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, and vigorously support the socialist construction of the whole country. He said: The key to completing these glorious tasks lies in further strengthening the Party's leadership. To this end, Party committees at all levels must more widely and deeply lead all cadres to study the Party's general line and general task during the transition period, study Marxism-Leninism, and continue to carry out publicity and education among the broad masses, further improve the ideological level of the entire Party, raise the socialist consciousness of the entire working people, and make the Party's general line a great driving force for promoting all work; it is necessary to further establish and consolidate the idea of ​​obeying the national plan and taking care of the overall situation in the entire Party, overcome departmentalism, local viewpoints, and the tendency to deviate from economic construction in certain departments and certain cadres, and put guaranteeing the completion of the state's key construction projects in the first place, so that all work revolves around the central task of national economic construction; we must firmly establish the idea of ​​learning from the Soviet Union and strengthen internationalist education among all the people, and continue to set off a high tide of learning from the Soviet Union in a planned way among all cadres and workers; this year, we require that all aspects of work in the Northeast be greatly improved, which requires every comrade to put in hard work to study and research, improve themselves, and improve the quality of work; it is necessary to give full play to the enthusiasm and creativity of all working people in participating in the national construction cause, further consolidate and strengthen labor discipline among all workers, establish a socialist labor attitude, regularize and improve patriotic labor emulation, and continue to carry out the patriotic production increase movement that combines mutual assistance and cooperation with technological reform in rural areas; it is necessary to further improve the collective leadership system of Party committees and Party groups at all levels, strengthen the Party's unity and unified and centralized leadership, oppose subjectivism, oppose decentralism, strengthen specific leadership, strengthen leadership over grassroots work, especially to strengthen the Party's specific leadership over economic work, conduct in-depth inspections of work at the grassroots level in a planned and focused manner, check the implementation of Party resolutions, and oppose bureaucracy. The whole Party must continue to carry forward the style of hard work and struggle, and use the exemplary actions of Communist Party members to influence the broad masses of the people.

After listening to the report, the responsible cadres of the provincial and municipal Party committees will return to their provinces and cities to convey it to cadres at all levels in order to promote deeper study and publicity of the general line by cadres throughout the region.

### 中共中央东北局宣传部举行报告会  推动全区干部进一步学习总路线  林枫同志提出东北全区今年主要工作任务

1954-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央东北局宣传部举行报告会
    推动全区干部进一步学习总路线
    林枫同志提出东北全区今年主要工作任务
    【新华社沈阳二十日电】中共中央东北局宣传部为进一步推动全区干部深入学习和宣传国家在过渡时期的总路线总任务，从一月十三日到十五日，举行了三次报告会，由中共中央东北局第一副书记林枫、第三副书记张明远、农村工作部部长赵德尊分别作了“关于党在过渡时期的总路线总任务”、“关于我国实行社会主义工业化”和“逐步实现国家对农业的社会主义改造”等问题的报告。到会的有东北一级机关干部、中共东北区各省、市委员会负责干部及中国人民解放军东北军区驻沈阳部队的干部一千七百多人。林枫在报告中解答了各地干部学习总路线中所提出的一些主要问题，对学习和宣传总路线也作了具体指示，并且在对东北全区一九五三年工作的基本情况作出总结后，提出了东北全区一九五四年的主要工作任务。
    关于一九五四年党在东北全区的主要任务，林枫说：就是根据党在过渡时期的总路线总任务及国家第一个五年计划建设的基本任务，在一九五三年工作的基础上，从各方面来保证全面完成和超额完成国家第一个五年计划第二年度的计划，特别要保证国家重点工程建设任务的完成。林枫指出：东北地区是国家第一个五年建设计划的重点，在实现国家的社会主义工业化的事业中担当了极为重要的任务。我们应该加倍努力，搞好东北的建设事业，很好地完成党中央和毛主席给予我们的光荣而艰巨的任务，大力支援全国的社会主义建设。他说：完成这些光荣任务的关键在于进一步加强党的领导。为此，各级党委就必须更加广泛深入地领导全体干部学习党在过渡时期的总路线总任务，学习马克思列宁主义并继续向广大群众进行宣传教育，进一步提高全党思想水平，提高全体劳动人民的社会主义觉悟，使党的总路线成为推动各项工作前进的伟大动力；要进一步在全党树立和确立服从国家计划与照顾全局的思想，克服在某些部门和某些干部中的本位主义、局部观点和脱离经济建设的倾向，把保证国家重点建设工程的完成放在首要地位，使一切工作都围绕国家经济建设这一中心进行；必须巩固地树立学习苏联的思想，在全体人民中加强国际主义的教育，有计划地在全体干部和广大职工中，继续掀起学习苏联的高潮；今年要求东北各方面的工作都要大大地提高一步，这就需要每个同志下苦功夫学习钻研，提高自己，提高工作质量；必须高度发挥全体劳动人民参加国家建设事业的积极性和创造性，在全体职工中，进一步巩固并加强劳动纪律，树立社会主义的劳动态度，使爱国主义的劳动竞赛经常化并提高一步，在农村继续开展互助合作与技术改革相结合的爱国增产运动；必须进一步健全各级党委、党组的集体领导制度，加强党的团结和统一集中的领导，反对主观主义，反对分散主义，加强具体领导，加强对于基层工作的领导，特别是要加强党对经济工作的具体领导，有计划有重点地深入基层检查工作，检查党的决议的执行情况，反对官僚主义。全党要继续发扬艰苦奋斗的作风，以共产党员的模范行动来影响广大人民群众。
    各省、市党委负责干部听了报告后，将返回省、市向各级干部进行传达，以推动全区干部更深入地学习和宣传总路线。
