Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Ministry of Culture Host Soviet Experts
Author:
Date: 1954-01-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 1st: On New Year's Day evening, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government jointly held a song and dance performance by the German Democratic Republic National People's Art Song and Dance Troupe to entertain Soviet experts working in various departments of the Central People's Government.

Personnel from the Soviet Embassy in China were also invited to watch the song and dance performance.

Professor Pischner, head of the German Democratic Republic National People's Art Song and Dance Troupe, and Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, were also present.

### 中苏友协总会和中央文化部招待苏联专家

1954-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友协总会和中央文化部招待苏联专家
    【新华社一日讯】元旦日晚间，中苏友好协会总会和中央人民政府文化部联合举办德意志民主共和国国家人民艺术歌舞团歌舞演出晚会，招待在中央人民政府各部门工作的苏联专家。
    应邀观看歌舞晚会的，还有苏联驻华大使馆人员。
    德意志民主共和国国家人民艺术歌舞团团长皮什纳教授和中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞并在场作陪。
