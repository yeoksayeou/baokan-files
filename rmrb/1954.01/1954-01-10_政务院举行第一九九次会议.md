Title: Government Administration Council Holds 199th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-01-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 8th: The 199th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of December 24, 1953. At the meeting, Shen Yanbing, Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Culture, delivered a report on the work of the Ministry of Culture in 1953. Wang Lanxi, Deputy Director of the Film Bureau of the Central People's Government Ministry of Culture, gave an explanation on strengthening film production and establishing a film screening network and film industry. The meeting discussed and approved the "Work Report of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government in 1953." The meeting adopted the "Decision of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government on Strengthening Film Production" and the "Decision of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government on Establishing a Film Screening Network and Film Industry." Finally, the meeting approved various appointment and removal cases and passed various appointment and removal cases to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第一九九次会议

1954-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一九九次会议
   【新华社八日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百九十九次政务会议在一九五三年十二月二十四日下午举行。会议听了中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰所作一九五三年文化部工作的报告，中央人民政府文化部电影局副局长王阑西所作关于加强电影制片工作和建立电影放映网与电影工业的说明。会议讨论并批准了“中央人民政府文化部一九五三年工作报告”。会议通过了“中央人民政府政务院关于加强电影制片工作的决定”和“中央人民政府政务院关于建立电影放映网与电影工业的决定”。最后，会议通过了各项任免案并通过了提请中央人民政府委员会批准的各项任免案。
