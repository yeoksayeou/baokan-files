Title: Performance of the German Democratic Republic National People's Art Troupe in the Capital (Pictures)
Author: Gao Liang, Zheng Xiaozhen, Zhang Li
Date: 1954-01-11
Page: 5

    Performance of the German Democratic Republic National People's Art Troupe in the Capital
    The German Democratic Republic National People's Art Troupe, invited to our country, arrived in Beijing, the capital of China, on December 24th of last year. They began performing at the newly built Tianqiao Theater on the 28th, and were warmly welcomed by the audience. This is the chorus: "Thank you, Soviet soldiers." (Photo by our reporter Gao Liang)
    Piano solo: Beethoven's famous work "Moonlight Sonata," performed by Liszt Prize winner Günther Kootz. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Zheng Xiaozhen)
    Chorus: Beethoven's famous work "Fantasia." Piano accompaniment: Günther Kootz. Lyrics adapted by Johannes Becher, winner of the Stalin International Prize "For Strengthening Peace Between Nations". (Photo by our reporter Gao Liang)
    Accordion ensemble of German folk dances such as "Old Polka". (Photo by our reporter Gao Liang)
    Folk dance: "Stralsund Fishermen's Dance." Folk dance: "Hessian Group Dance." (Photo by our reporter Gao Liang)
    Violin solo: Romance in F major. Soloist Franz Pilz. (Photo by our reporter Gao Liang)
    Members of the Central Experimental Opera Theater Orchestra learning the oboe from members of the German Democratic Republic National People's Art Troupe. (Photo by our reporter Zhang Li)
    Folk dance: "Bavarian Dance." (Photo by our reporter Gao Liang)
    Folk dance: "Weaving Dance." (Photo by our reporter Zhang Li)
    Members of the German Democratic Republic National People's Art Troupe learning the Chinese folk dance - "Picking Tea and Catching Butterflies." (Photo by our reporter Zhang Li)

### 德意志民主共和国国家人民艺术歌舞团在首都演出（图片）
高粮  郑小箴  张力
1954-01-11
第5版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国国家人民艺术歌舞团在首都演出
    应邀来我国的德意志民主共和国国家人民艺术歌舞团于去年十二月二十四日到达我国首都北京，二十八日开始在新建天桥大剧场演出，受到观众热烈的欢迎。这是合唱：“感谢你们，苏军战士”。（本报记者高粮摄）
    钢琴独奏：贝多芬名作“月光曲”，演奏者为李斯特奖金获得者根特·库茨。　（新华社记者郑小箴摄）
    合唱：贝多芬名作“幻想曲”。钢琴伴奏：根特·库茨。歌词改编者为“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金获得者约翰尼斯·贝希尔。（本报记者高粮摄）
    手风琴合奏德国民间舞曲“古老的波尔卡舞曲”等。（本报记者高粮摄）
    民间舞蹈：“施特拉宋德渔民舞”。民间舞蹈：“黑申组舞”。（本报记者高粮摄）
    小提琴独奏：F大调浪漫曲。独奏者弗朗兹·皮尔。
                                  （本报记者高粮摄）
