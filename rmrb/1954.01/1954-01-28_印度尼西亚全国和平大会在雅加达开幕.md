Title: Indonesian National Peace Conference Opens in Jakarta
Author:
Date: 1954-01-28
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, January 27th: The Indonesian National Peace Conference, organized by the Indonesian National Peace Committee, opened in Jakarta on the 24th. Representatives from all over Indonesia are participating in the conference.

　　Speakers at the conference included the Speaker of the Indonesian Parliament, Sartono, and the Indonesian Minister of Justice, Gondokusumo. Sartono said: The Indonesian people's struggle for peace is also a struggle to defend the freedom and independence of their country. Gondokusumo said: The Indonesian people demand that Indonesia pursue a policy of peace and consolidate its friendly relations with all countries.

　　The conference held a reception on the evening of the 24th.

### 印度尼西亚全国和平大会在雅加达开幕

1954-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚全国和平大会在雅加达开幕
    【新华社二十七日讯】　雅加达消息：印尼全国和平委员会组织的印尼全国和平大会，已于二十四日在雅加达开幕。参加大会的有来自印尼各地的代表。
    在大会上发言的有印尼国会议长沙多诺和印尼政府司法部长龚多古苏莫。沙多诺说：印尼人民为和平而斗争，也就是为保卫本国的自由和独立而斗争。龚多古苏莫说：印尼人民要求印尼奉行和平与巩固它和一切国家的友好关系的政策。
    大会于二十四日晚举行了招待会。
