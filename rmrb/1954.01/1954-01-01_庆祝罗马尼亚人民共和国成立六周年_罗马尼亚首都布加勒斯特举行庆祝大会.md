Title: Celebrating the Sixth Anniversary of the Founding of the Romanian People's Republic, Bucharest, the Capital of Romania, Holds a Celebration Rally
Author:
Date: 1954-01-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 31st: Bucharest, Romanian News Agency – December 30th marked the sixth anniversary of the proclamation of the Romanian People's Republic. On this day, a celebration rally was held in the capital, Bucharest. Attending the celebration rally were government and party leaders headed by Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Romanian Council of Ministers and General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, members of the Presidium of the National Assembly headed by Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly, as well as representatives of various mass organizations, advanced workers, and people from the cultural, scientific, and artistic circles.
　　At the meeting, Lieutenant General Salajan, First Deputy Minister of the Romanian Armed Forces, delivered a speech celebrating the festival. He emphasized the great significance of the proclamation of the Romanian People's Republic in the national life.

### 庆祝罗马尼亚人民共和国成立六周年  罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特举行庆祝大会

1954-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝罗马尼亚人民共和国成立六周年
    罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特举行庆祝大会
    【新华社三十一日讯】罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特讯：十二月三十日是罗马尼亚人民共和国宣布成立的六周年纪念日。这一天，首都布加勒斯特举行了庆祝大会。参加庆祝大会的有以罗马尼亚部长会议主席、罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会总书记乔治乌—德治为首的政府和党的负责人，以国民议会主席团主席格罗查为首的国民议会主席团委员，以及各群众团体的代表、先进工人和文化、科学、艺术界人士。
    会上，罗马尼亚武装部队部第一代理部长萨拉扬中将发表了庆祝节日的演说。他着重指出了罗马尼亚人民共和国的宣布成立在国家生活中的重大意义。
