Title: More Than Fifty Factories in Tianjin Complete Product or Output Value Plans for the First Half of the Month
Author:
Date: 1954-01-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, January 26th: More than fifty state-owned, locally state-owned, and joint state-private factories in Tianjin have completed their product or output value plans for the first ten days and middle ten days of January, creating favorable conditions for completing the production plans for January and the first quarter of this year.

　　The output of steel ingots from the state-owned Tianjin Steel Plant reached 101.35% of the plan for the first and middle ten days of the month, and eight of the nine types of steel products were completed. Tianjin Power Machinery Factory, Bicycle Factory, Textile Machinery Factory, Tianjin Paper Mill, No. 9 Rubber Factory, People's Ink Factory, and Tianjin Dyestuff Factory, etc., all overfulfilled their product plans. The six state-owned cotton textile factories in Tianjin under the North China Textile Administration Bureau all overfulfilled their cotton yarn production plans, and three of them completed their cotton cloth production plans. The output plan for various printed and colored cloths of Tianjin Printing and Dyeing Factory was completed by 104.18%. The Tianjin Municipal Local Industry Bureau system completed 101.36% of the output value plan for the first and middle ten days of the month, and 90 of the 145 products completed the plan. The quality completion of many factories has also improved compared with last year. For example, more than 98% of various papers from Tianjin Paper Mill are qualified, and 100% of pulp boards are qualified, exceeding the national planned target.

　　The enthusiasm of the employees of various factories in Tianjin at the beginning of the year is very high, and the attendance rate is good. According to statistics from forty-one factories in the first half of January, the lowest attendance rate reached 93.46%, and the highest reached 99.8%. The attendance rate of Tianjin No. 5 Cotton Textile Factory in January last year was 89.79%, and it reached 94.96% in the first half of January this year.

### 天津市有五十多个工厂完成本月上、中旬产品或产值计划

1954-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　天津市有五十多个工厂完成本月上、中旬产品或产值计划
    【新华社天津二十六日电】　天津市有五十多个国营、地方国营和公私合营工厂完成了一月份上、中旬的产品或产值计划，为完成今年一月份和第一季度生产计划创造了有利条件。
    国营天津钢厂钢锭的产量完成上、中旬计划百分之一百零一点三五，九种钢材的产量有八种完成。天津动力机厂、自行车厂、纺织机械厂、天津造纸厂、第九橡胶厂、人民制墨厂和天津染料厂等都超额完成了产品计划。华北纺织管理局所属天津六个国营棉纺织厂都超额完成了棉纱产量计划，其中有三个厂完成了棉布产量计划。天津印染厂各种花布、色布的产量计划完成了百分之一百零四点一八。天津市地方工业局系统共完成上、中旬产值计划百分之一百零一点三六，一百四十五种产品中，有九十种产品完成了计划。不少工厂完成质量计划的情况也比去年有进步。如天津造纸厂各种纸张有百分之九十八以上合格，纸浆板百分之百合格，超过了国家计划指标。
    天津各厂职工年初生产情绪十分饱满，出勤情况良好。据四十一个工厂一月上半月统计，出勤率最低的达到百分之九十三点四六，最高的达到百分之九十九点八。天津第五棉纺织厂去年一月份的出勤率是百分之八十九点七九，今年一月上半月达到百分之九十四点九六。
