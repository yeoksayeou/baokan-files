Title: Anshan to Conduct Worker-Peasant Alliance Education During Spring Festival

Author: 
Date: 1954-01-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch:

    Anshan, the steel capital, will extensively carry out worker-peasant alliance propaganda and education work during the Spring Festival.

    The Anshan Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party has decided to widely launch worker-peasant get-together activities in various factories, mines, and suburban areas around the Spring Festival, and will also hold a worker-peasant alliance exhibition. Farmers in the suburbs began organizing visiting groups to visit Anshan Iron and Steel on the 12th. Factories and mines will organize worker-peasant forums to discuss the situation of industrial and agricultural production, in order to promote mutual understanding between workers and peasants and achieve the goal of mutual support and mutual trust. Employees of factories and mines and farmers in the suburbs will also organize mutual visits and condolences. Factory and mine troupes and rural troupes will exchange performances, and film projection teams will go to the countryside.

    In order to strengthen the close friendship between worker and peasant labor models, the Anshan Municipal Federation of Trade Unions will organize labor models, headed by Meng Tai, Zhang Mingshan, Wang Chonglun, Wang Jinzhong, Huang Demao and others, all special-grade labor models of Anshan Iron and Steel, to visit well-known agricultural production cooperatives and famous agricultural labor models in the Northeast region, and will present them with gifts on behalf of Anshan employees. At the same time, famous agricultural labor models from various places will also be invited to visit Anshan and give reports to Anshan employees.

    In order to do a good job in the propaganda and education work of the worker-peasant alliance during the Spring Festival, the Anshan Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party has decided to immediately carry out universal and in-depth propaganda and education activities in factories and mines throughout the city. Employees in basic construction have decided to take worker-peasant alliance education as the main content of the current winter training political education; employees in production factories and mines have decided to use political education time before the Spring Festival for worker-peasant alliance education. Ding Xiu, Deputy Secretary of the Anshan Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, also gave a report on strengthening the worker-peasant alliance to administrative cadres, Party and mass cadres, and all reporters in charge of factories and mines throughout the city on the 12th.

### 鞍山将在春节期间进行工农联盟教育工作

1954-01-16
第1版()
专栏：

    鞍山将在春节期间进行工农联盟教育工作
    据新华社讯：钢都鞍山将在春节期间广泛展开工农联盟宣传教育工作。
    中共鞍山市委员会已决定于春节前后，在各厂矿和市郊广泛展开工农联欢活动，并将举办工农联盟展览会。郊区农民已于十二日开始组织参观团到鞍钢来参观。各厂矿将组织工农座谈会，座谈工业生产和农业生产的情况，以促进工农之间互相了解，达到互相支援互相信任的目的。厂矿的职工和市郊的农民，也将互相组织慰问，厂矿剧团和农村剧团将交流演出，电影队即将下乡。
    为加强工农劳动模范之间的亲密友谊，鞍山市工会联合会将组织以鞍钢特等劳动模范孟泰、张明山、王崇伦、王进忠、黄德茂等为首的劳动模范，访问东北地区著名的农业生产合作社和著名的农业劳动模范，并将代表鞍山职工赠送他们一些礼物。同时，也将邀请各地著名农业劳动模范到鞍山来参观，请他们为鞍山职工做报告。
    为做好春节期间的工农联盟的宣传教育工作，中共鞍山市委已决定立即在全市厂矿中进行普遍而深入的宣传教育活动。基本建设的职工，决定以工农联盟教育作为目前冬训政治教育的主要内容；生产厂矿的职工，决定春节前以政治教育时间进行工农联盟教育。中共鞍山市委副书记丁秀，并已在十二日向全市厂矿负责的行政干部、党群干部和全体报告员作了关于加强工农联盟的报告。
