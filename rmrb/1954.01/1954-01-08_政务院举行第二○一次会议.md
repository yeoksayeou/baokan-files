Title: Government Administration Council Holds 201st Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1954-01-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The 201st Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 7th. The meeting heard a report by Liu Lanbo, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Fuel Industry of the Central People's Government, on the current situation of the fuel industry and future work arrangements. After discussion, the meeting approved the "Report of the Ministry of Fuel Industry of the Central People's Government on the Current Situation of the Fuel Industry and Future Work Arrangements."

### 政务院举行第二○一次会议

1954-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

     政务院举行第二○一次会议
    【新华社七日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二百零一次政务会议在七日下午举行。会议听了中央人民政府燃料工业部副部长刘澜波所作关于目前燃料工业情况及今后工作部署的说明后，讨论并批准了“中央人民政府燃料工业部关于目前燃料工业情况及今后工作部署的报告”。
