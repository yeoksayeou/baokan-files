Title: Japanese Peace Activist Haruo Okada and Others Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-01-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency dispatch:

    Japanese representatives Haruo Okada (Standing Committee Member of the Japan Peace Supporters Committee, Central Executive Committee Member of the Japan Labor-Farmer Party), Tsuge Hideomi (Secretary-General of the Japan Democratic Scientists Association), and Kushida Fuki (Chairman of the Japan Women's Democratic Club), who attended the Vienna Conference of the World Peace Council, have arrived in Beijing by plane on the 9th and 10th respectively at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, to visit China. Tang Mingzhao, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, representatives from the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, as well as Kameda Togo, Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, Kaneko Kenta, Japanese representative of the World Federation of Trade Unions' Asian-Australian Liaison Bureau, and Japanese peace activist Kan'emon Nakamura, were present at the airport to welcome them.

### 日本和平人士冈田春夫等到京

1954-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    日本和平人士冈田春夫等到京
    据新华社讯：出席世界和平理事会维也纳会议的日本代表冈田春夫（日本拥护和平委员会常务委员，日本劳农党中央执行委员）、柘植秀臣（日本民主主义科学者协会干事长）和栉田蕗（日本妇女民主俱乐部主席），应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请来我国参观访问，已先后在九日、十日乘飞机到达北京。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会联络部副部长唐明照和中华全国民主妇女联合会的代表，以及亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长龟田东伍，世界工联亚澳联络局日本代表金子健太和日本和平人士中村翫右卫门等，曾到机场欢迎。
