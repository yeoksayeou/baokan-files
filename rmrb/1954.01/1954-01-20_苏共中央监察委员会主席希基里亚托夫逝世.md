Title: Chairman Shkiryatov of the CPSU Central Auditing Commission Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1954-01-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 19th, Moscow Telegraph Agency: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union sadly announce that Comrade Matvey Fyodorovich Shkiryatov, one of the oldest activists of the Communist Party and the Soviet state, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Chairman of the Party Auditing Commission of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, passed away on January 18th due to a long and serious illness. Comrade M.F. Shkiryatov dedicated his life to serving our Party and fighting for the cause of the working people. His death is a significant loss for the Communist Party and the Soviet people.
               The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union
               The Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union
      Xinhua News Agency, January 19th, Moscow Telegraph Agency: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union sadly announces that Comrade Matvey Fyodorovich Shkiryatov, Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and one of the oldest activists of the Communist Party and the Soviet state, has passed away.
                The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union
    Xinhua News Agency, January 19th, Moscow Telegraph Agency: The Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union have decided to establish a funeral committee for M.F. Shkiryatov. The funeral committee consists of Comrades M.A. Suslov (Chairman), N.M. Pegov, N.A. Mikhailov, and P.T. Komarov.
    Xinhua News Agency, January 19th, Moscow Telegraph Agency: Announcement by the Funeral Committee for M.F. Shkiryatov:
    The coffin of Comrade M.F. Shkiryatov will be placed in the Hall of Columns of the Trade Union Building in Moscow. To allow people to pay their respects to the remains of M.F. Shkiryatov, the Hall of Columns will be open from 2:00 PM to 10:00 PM on January 19th. The date of the burial will be announced later.

### 苏共中央监察委员会主席希基里亚托夫逝世

1954-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    苏共中央监察委员会主席希基里亚托夫逝世
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十九日电：苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议沉痛地宣布：共产党和苏维埃国家的最老的活动家之一、苏联共产党中央委员会委员、苏联共产党中央委员会的党监察委员会主席、苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员马特维·费奥多罗维奇·希基里亚托夫同志，因长期患重病于一月十八日逝世。马·费·希基里亚托夫同志毕生为我们的党服务，为劳动人民的事业而斗争，他的逝世是共产党和苏联人民的重大损失。
               苏联共产党中央委员会
               苏联部长会议
      【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十九日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团沉痛地宣布：苏联最高苏维埃代表、苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员、共产党和苏维埃国家的最老的活动家之一马特维·费奥多罗维奇·希基里亚托夫同志逝世。
                苏联最高苏维埃主席团
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十九日电：苏联部长会议和苏联共产党中央委员会决定：成立马·费·希基里亚托夫治丧委员会。治丧委员会由米·安·苏斯洛夫（主席）、纳·麦·别哥夫、纳·阿·米哈伊洛夫和普·特·科马罗夫同志组成。
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十九日电：马·费·希基里亚托夫治丧委员会通告：
    马·费·希基里亚托夫同志的灵柩停放在莫斯科的工会大厦圆柱大厅。为使人们能够瞻仰马·费·希基里亚托夫的遗容，圆柱大厅于一月十九日下午二时起开放到晚间十时。安葬日期另行通告。
