Title: General Association to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea Holds Enlarged Meeting of the Standing Committee; General He Long Delivers Work Report of the Delegation to Korea; Meeting Passes Resolution on the Work Report of the Delegation to Korea
Author:
Date: 1954-01-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 8th: The Chinese People's General Association to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea held its 17th Enlarged Meeting of the Standing Committee on the afternoon of the 8th. General He Long, head of the Third Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, delivered a report on the work of the Third Delegation to Korea.

Those present included: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's General Association to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea; Vice Chairmen Peng Zhen and Chen Shutong; Standing Committee members Situ Meitang, Li Siguang, Shen Yanbing, Shao Lizi, Zhang Naiqi, Xu Deheng, Xu Baoju, Liang Xi, Peng Zemin, Jiang Nanxiang, Liu Ningyi, Xiao San, Luo Longji; Secretary-General Liu Guanyi; Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government's Revolutionary Military Commission; Nie Zhen, Chairman of the North China General Branch of the Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea Association; and representatives of people's organizations Li Zaiwen, Qian Sanqiang, Zhang Wenhao, Liao Mengxing, and Wu Yunfu.

Also present were He Long, head of the Third Chinese People's Delegation to Korea; Deputy Heads Xing Xiping, Zhang Bojun, Cai Tingkai, Zhu Xuefan, Chen Yi, Wu Han, Liu Zhiming, Kang Keqing, Mei Lanfang, and Zhou Xinfang; Chen Qiyuan, Qian Duansheng, Hou Debang, Huang Qixiang, Hong Shen, Shi Dongshan, Cheng Yanqiu, and Jin Cheng, Deputy Heads of the First General Sub-Delegation; and Shao Shiping, Head of the Fifth General Sub-Delegation, and others.

The meeting was presided over by Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's General Association to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea.

In his report, General He Long reported on the work of the Third Chinese People's Delegation to Korea in extending greetings to the Korean People's Army, the Korean people, and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army in Korea, as well as the encouragement given to the Korean people and the Korean and Chinese People's Armies. The Third Chinese People's Delegation to Korea's visit further consolidated and strengthened the friendship and unity between the Chinese and Korean peoples and encouraged the Volunteer Army's determination to defend the motherland and defend peace.

After General He Long's report, Zhu Xuefan, Deputy Head of the Delegation, Shao Shiping, Head of the Fifth General Sub-Delegation, Hong Shen, Deputy Head of the First General Sub-Delegation, and Chen Yi, Deputy Head of the Delegation, spoke successively at the meeting.

The meeting unanimously adopted a resolution on the work report of the Third Chinese People's Delegation to Korea (the full text will be issued separately).

Finally, Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's General Association to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, spoke. In his speech, he expressed his gratitude to the Third Chinese People's Delegation to Korea and encouraged them to do a good job in conveying the message.

### 抗美援朝总会举行常务委员会扩大会议  贺龙将军作赴朝慰问团工作报告  会议通过关于赴朝慰问团工作报告的决议

1954-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

    抗美援朝总会举行常务委员会扩大会议
    贺龙将军作赴朝慰问团工作报告
    会议通过关于赴朝慰问团工作报告的决议
    【新华社八日讯】　中国人民抗美援朝总会于八日下午举行常务委员会第十七次会议扩大会议。中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团总团长贺龙将军在会上作了第三届赴朝慰问团的工作的报告。
    到会的有：中国人民抗美援朝总会主席郭沫若，副主席彭真、陈叔通，常务委员司徒美堂、李四光、沈雁冰、邵力子、章乃器、许德珩、许宝驹、梁希、彭泽民、蒋南翔、刘宁一、萧三、罗隆基，秘书长刘贯一，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任甘泗淇，华北抗美援朝总分会主席聂真，各人民团体代表栗再温、钱三强、张文浩、廖梦醒、伍云甫等。
    到会的还有中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团总团长贺龙，副总团长邢西萍、章伯钧、蔡廷锴、朱学范、陈沂、吴晗、刘芝明、康克清、梅兰芳、周信芳，第一总分团副团长陈其瑗、钱端升、侯德榜、黄琪翔、洪深、史东山、程砚秋、金城，第五总分团团长邵式平等。
    会议由中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席彭真主持。
    贺龙总团长在报告中报告了中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团在朝鲜对朝鲜人民军、朝鲜人民和中国人民志愿军的慰问工作，以及给予朝鲜人民和朝、中人民军队的鼓舞。中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团在朝鲜的慰问，进一步巩固和加强了中、朝人民的友谊团结，鼓舞了志愿军保卫祖国、保卫和平的决心。
    贺龙总团长报告后，慰问团副总团长朱学范，第五总分团团长邵式平、第一总分团副团长洪深、总团副团长陈沂等，相继在会上作了发言。
    会议一致通过了关于中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团工作报告的决议（全文另发）。
    最后，由中国人民抗美援朝总会主席郭沫若讲话。他在讲话中，对中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团表示感谢。并勉励作好传达工作。
