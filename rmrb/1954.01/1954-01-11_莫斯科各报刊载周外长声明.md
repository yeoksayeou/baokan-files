Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish Foreign Minister Zhou's Statement
Author:
Date: 1954-01-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th. Moscow newspapers on the 10th all published the statement by Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, on the resumption of the bilateral talks on the Korean Political Conference issue.

### 莫斯科各报刊载周外长声明

1954-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各报刊载周外长声明
    【新华社莫斯科十日电】莫斯科各报十日都刊载了我国中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长就恢复关于朝鲜政治会议问题的双方会谈发表的声明。
