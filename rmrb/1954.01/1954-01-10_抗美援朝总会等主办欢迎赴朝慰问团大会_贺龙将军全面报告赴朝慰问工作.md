Title: Anti-American and Aid Korea General Association and others host a welcome meeting for the delegation to North Korea; General He Long comprehensively reports on the work of the delegation to North Korea

Author:
Date: 1954-01-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 9th
The Anti-American and Aid Korea General Association of China, the North China Anti-American and Aid Korea General Sub-Association, and the Beijing Anti-American and Aid Korea Sub-Association hosted a welcome meeting for the Third Chinese People's Delegation to North Korea at Huairen Hall at 3:00 PM on the 9th. General He Long, head of the Third Chinese People's Delegation to North Korea, delivered a report at the meeting.

Present at the meeting were some responsible persons and representatives of the Third Chinese People's Delegation to North Korea and the first, fourth, and sixth general sub-delegations, as well as more than 1,700 representatives from democratic parties, people's organizations, government agencies, military units, factories, and schools in the capital.

The meeting was presided over by Chen Shutong, vice chairman of the Anti-American and Aid Korea General Association of China.

General He Long, head of the Third Chinese People's Delegation to North Korea, comprehensively reported on the work of the Third Chinese People's Delegation to North Korea in Korea, reported on the countless heroic deeds created by the Korean people, the Korean People's Army, and the Chinese People's Volunteers in the struggle against American aggression and in defense of world peace, and the consolidated friendship between the Chinese and Korean peoples. (Full text to be published separately)

Chen Shutong, vice chairman of the Anti-American and Aid Korea General Association of China, expressed his gratitude to General He Long and all members of the delegation in his final speech, and called on everyone to learn from the Chinese People's Volunteers, the Korean people, and the Korean People's Army, improve work efficiency in their respective work positions, and strive for a gradual transition to socialism under the illumination of the general line of the country's transition period.

### 抗美援朝总会等主办欢迎赴朝慰问团大会  贺龙将军全面报告赴朝慰问工作

1954-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    抗美援朝总会等主办欢迎赴朝慰问团大会
    贺龙将军全面报告赴朝慰问工作
    【新华社九日讯】中国人民抗美援朝总会、华北抗美援朝总分会、北京市抗美援朝分会主办的欢迎中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团大会，于九日下午三时在怀仁堂举行。中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团总团长贺龙将军在会上作了报告。
    到会的有中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团总团和第一、第四、第六总分团的部分负责人和代表，及首都各民主党派、各人民团体、各机关、部队、工厂、学校的代表共一千七百多人。
    大会由中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席陈叔通主持。
    中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团总团长贺龙将军在会上全面地报告了中国人民第三届赴朝慰问团在朝鲜的工作情况，报告了朝鲜人民、朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军在反对美国侵略、保卫世界和平斗争中创造的无数可歌可泣的英雄事迹，及朝中两国人民巩固的友谊。（全文另发）
    中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席陈叔通在最后的讲话中，对贺龙总团长和慰问团全体人员表示感谢，并号召大家向中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民、朝鲜人民军学习，在各人的工作岗位上，提高工作效率，在国家过渡时期的总路线的照耀下，为逐步过渡到社会主义而奋斗。
