Title: The Mongolian People Aid in the Construction of Korea
Author:
Date: 1954-01-31
Page: 6

The people of the Mongolian People's Republic, in August 1953, donated 17,000 head of draught horses and studs to Korea; and are prepared to continue to donate various leathers: 10,000 large sheepskins, 15,000 pieces of leather for making shoes and boots, 8,000 pieces of leather for making shoes, and 500 pieces of leather for making soles, as well as 1,000 tons of beef and mutton. Currently, among the aid materials from various fraternal countries that are being continuously transported to Korea, there are Mongolian People's Republic items such as leather coats, deerskin, leather boots, and meat. ### 蒙古人民援助朝鲜建设

1954-01-31
第6版()
专栏：

　　蒙古人民援助朝鲜建设
    蒙古人民共和国人民，在一九五三年八月间，赠送给朝鲜耕马和种马一万七千匹；并准备继续赠送各种皮革：大羊皮一万张，制鞋靴用皮一万五千张、做鞋用皮八千张和做鞋底用皮五百张，以及牛羊肉一千吨。目前，在陆续运到朝鲜的各兄弟国家的援助物资中，有蒙古人民共和国的皮大衣、鹿皮、皮靴和肉类等。（附图片）
