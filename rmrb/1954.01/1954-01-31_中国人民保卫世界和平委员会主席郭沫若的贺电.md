Title: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Sends Congratulatory Telegram

Author:
Date: 1954-01-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 30th: The Canadian National Congress for Peace Through Negotiation convened in Toronto on January 30th. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, sent a telegram of congratulations on January 26th. The telegram stated:

　　On behalf of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, I extend warm congratulations and cordial respect to the Canadian National Congress for Peace Through Negotiation, and through the Congress to the peace-loving people of Canada. We believe that the Congress will help to mobilize a broader spectrum of Canadian society to strive for the settlement of international disputes in a spirit of consultation, to ease international tensions, and thereby to strengthen the worldwide movement for the defense of peace. We wish the Congress complete success.

### 中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若的贺电

1954-01-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若的贺电
    【新华社三十日讯】加拿大全国拥护通过协商谋求和平大会于一月三十日在多伦多召开，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若在一月二十六日致电祝贺。电文说：
    我代表中国人民保卫世界和平委员会向加拿大全国拥护通过协商谋求和平大会，并通过大会向爱好和平的加拿大人民表示热烈的祝贺和亲切的敬意。我们相信，大会定将有助于动员加拿大更广泛的社会阶层来争取以协商精神解决国际争端，缓和国际紧张局势并从而加强全世界的保卫和平运动。谨祝大会获得圆满成功。
