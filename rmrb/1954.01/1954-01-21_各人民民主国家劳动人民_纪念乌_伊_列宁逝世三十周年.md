Title: Laboring People of Various People's Democratic Countries Commemorate the 30th Anniversary of the Death of V.I. Lenin
Author:
Date: 1954-01-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: The laboring people of Romania and Czechoslovakia have begun preparing and carrying out activities to commemorate the 30th anniversary of the death of V.I. Lenin. According to TASS, Romanian People's Republic's industrial enterprises, institutions, and schools are holding symposiums to commemorate the 30th anniversary of Lenin's death. Many cinemas in the Republic's capital, Bucharest, and other cities will show the documentary "Vladimir Ilyich Lenin," as well as the feature films "Lenin in October," "The Man with a Gun," "The Great Dawn," and "The Unforgettable Year 1919." To commemorate the 30th anniversary of Lenin's death, the Romanian Political Books Publishing Bureau will publish Volume 24 of the Complete Works of Lenin and Volume 1 of the Selected Works of Lenin, with a total circulation of 140,000 copies.

In Prague, the capital of the Czechoslovak Republic, the Czechoslovak Journalists Club held an exhibition on January 15th, "Lenin and the Newspaper," to commemorate the 30th anniversary of Lenin's death. The exhibits narrated Lenin's revolutionary activities and introduced Lenin's activities as a political commentator from 1905 to 1911. The exhibition showed the significant role Lenin played in founding Bolshevik newspapers.
     Xinhua News Agency, 20th: The laboring people of Bulgaria, Hungary, Poland, and the Mongolian People's Republic are actively preparing and carrying out activities to commemorate the 30th anniversary of the death of V.I. Lenin.

In recent days, Bulgarian newspapers have published many articles and materials introducing Lenin's life and revolutionary deeds. Factories, institutions, and schools are holding report meetings and symposiums on Lenin. The Sofia Cultural Workers Club is scheduled to hold a memorial evening on the 20th to commemorate the 30th anniversary of Lenin's death, at which a special person will give a report on "Lenin on Art and Culture." These days, the People's Theater in Sofia is staging Popov's play "Family," which describes Lenin's childhood and adolescence. The cinemas are showing "Lenin in October." The Central Council of the Bulgarian-Soviet Association has published a booklet "Thirty Years Since Lenin's Death, Advancing Along Lenin's Road" for festival propaganda.

The Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party and the Council of Ministers will hold a memorial meeting in Budapest on the 21st to commemorate the 30th anniversary of Lenin's death. These days, factories, institutions, and villages in Hungary are holding symposiums on Lenin's life and activities. The Hungarian Institute of Workers' Movement History and the "Iskra" Publishing House have published an illustrated collection of essays "Lenin on Hungary." Hungarian newspapers published articles, memoirs, and essays commemorating Lenin.

Polish newspapers published articles commemorating Lenin. Krakow is where Lenin lived from 1912 to 1914. A Lenin Museum has been built there, and the museum will officially open on the 30th anniversary of Lenin's death. A small place in the south of Poland, Poronin, is now known to the people of Poland. More than forty years ago, Lenin lived there. A house where Lenin held meetings there has been turned into a Lenin Museum. Recently, hundreds of people have visited it every day.

Towns, state-owned farms, and livestock production cooperatives in the Mongolian People's Republic are continuing to hold symposiums, report meetings, and lectures to introduce Lenin's life and revolutionary deeds. Propagandists from the Propaganda Group of the Ulaanbaatar City Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party have recently given reports to the people entitled "Lenin - Founder of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet State" and "Lenin - A Loyal Friend of the Mongolian People." Libraries and reading rooms are holding Lenin's works reading sessions. Clubs and Red Corners are holding photo exhibitions and Lenin's works exhibitions commemorating the 30th anniversary of Lenin's death.

### 各人民民主国家劳动人民  纪念乌·伊·列宁逝世三十周年

1954-01-21
第3版()
专栏：

    各人民民主国家劳动人民
    纪念乌·伊·列宁逝世三十周年
     【新华社十八日讯】罗马尼亚和捷克斯洛伐克劳动人民已经开始筹备和进行纪念乌·伊·列宁逝世三十周年的活动。据塔斯社消息：罗马尼亚人民共和国各工业企业、机关和学校纷纷举行座谈会，纪念列宁逝世三十周年。共和国首都布加勒斯特和其他城市的许多电影院，将上演纪录片“乌拉季米尔·伊里奇·列宁”，以及“列宁在十月”、“带枪的人”、“伟大的曙光”、“难忘的一九一九年”等故事片。为了纪念列宁逝世三十周年，罗马尼亚政治书籍出版局将出版列宁全集第二十四卷和列宁选集第一卷。发行总额为十四万册。
    在捷克斯洛伐克共和国首都布拉格，捷克斯洛伐克记者俱乐部于十五日举办了“列宁与报纸”展览会，纪念列宁逝世三十周年。展览会上的陈列品叙述了列宁的革命活动，介绍了列宁作为一个政论家在一九零五年到一九一一年间的活动。展览会显示了列宁在创办布尔什维克报纸方面所起的重大作用。
      【新华社二十日讯】保加利亚、匈牙利、波兰和蒙古人民共和国的劳动人民积极筹备和进行乌·伊·列宁逝世三十周年的纪念活动。
    保加利亚各报近日来刊载了许多介绍列宁的生平和革命事迹的文章和资料。各工厂、机关、学校纷纷举行关于列宁的报告会和座谈会。索非亚文化工作者俱乐部定于二十日举行列宁逝世三十周年纪念晚会，会上将由专人作“列宁论艺术和文化”的报告。这些天来，索非亚的人民剧院正在上演波波夫的描述列宁的童年和少年时代的剧本“家庭”。各电影院正在放映“列宁在十月”。保苏协会中央理事会已为节日宣传出版了“列宁逝世三十年，沿着列宁的道路前进”的小册子。
    匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会和部长会议将于二十一日在布达佩斯举行列宁逝世三十周年的纪念会。这几天来，匈牙利各工厂、机关和村庄中纷纷举行关于列宁的生平和活动的座谈会。匈牙利工人运动历史研究所和“火花”出版局出版了附有插图的“列宁论匈牙利”的文集。匈牙利各报刊载了纪念列宁的文章、回忆录和随笔。
    波兰报纸发表了纪念列宁的文章。克拉科夫是列宁在一九一二年到一九一四年居住过的地方。那里已建成了一个列宁博物馆，博物馆将在列宁逝世三十周年纪念日正式揭幕。波兰南端的一个小地方波罗尼尼现在是波兰全国人民都知道的地方，在四十多年前，列宁曾在那里住过。有一幢列宁曾在那里举行过会议的房屋，已经改成列宁博物馆。最近每天都有好几百人到那里参观。
    蒙古人民共和国各城镇、国营农场和畜牧业生产合作社正在继续举行座谈会、报告会和讲演会，介绍列宁的生平和革命事迹。蒙古人民革命党乌兰巴托市委员会宣传组的宣传人员，最近已经向人民作了题为“列宁——苏联共产党和苏维埃国家的奠基人”和“列宁——蒙古人民的忠实朋友”的报告。各图书馆和阅览室正在举行列宁著作阅读会。各俱乐部和红角举办有列宁逝世三十周年纪念图片展览会和列宁著作展览会。
