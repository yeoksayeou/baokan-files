Title: Soviet Union Begins Preparations for Supreme Soviet Elections; Nomination of Candidates for Members of the Central Election Commission Has Begun
Author:
Date: 1954-01-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 14th: According to TASS, Moscow, January 14th: The Soviet Union has begun preparations for the election of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. On the 13th, organizations of working people in some cities and rural areas across the country nominated candidates for members of the Central Election Commission of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. N.M. Shvernik, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, A. Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, N. Mikhailov, Secretary of the Moscow Regional Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, A. Shelepin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, V. Kozlov, First Secretary of the Leningrad Regional Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and G. Grishko, Secretary of the Kiev Regional Committee of the Communist Party of Ukraine, have been nominated as candidates for membership in the Central Election Commission. In addition, members of the Gorky Collective Farm in the Fergana Oblast of the Uzbek Republic nominated O. Yuldasheva, a female member of the farm and a renowned high-yield expert, as a candidate.

Note: The duty of the Central Election Commission of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union is to handle matters related to the election of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. According to the provisions of the "Regulations on Elections to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union," the commission is composed of representatives of trade union organizations of workers and staff, cooperative organizations, Communist Party organizations, youth organizations, cultural, technical, and scientific associations, and all other social organizations and groups of working people, as well as representatives of workers' congresses, staff congresses, farmers' congresses, and soldiers' congresses. The Central Election Commission consists of one chairman, one vice-chairman, one secretary, and twenty-four members, whose candidates must be approved by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union fifty days before the election.

### 苏联开始进行最高苏维埃选举的筹备工作  中央选举委员会委员候选人提名工作已经开始

1954-01-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联开始进行最高苏维埃选举的筹备工作
    中央选举委员会委员候选人提名工作已经开始
    【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十四日讯：苏联已开始进行苏联最高苏维埃选举的筹备工作。全国已有一些城市和农村的劳动人民的组织在十三日对苏联最高苏维埃中央选举委员会委员的候选人进行提名。全苏工会中央理事会主席尼·米·什维尔尼克、苏联科学院院长亚·涅斯米扬诺夫、苏联共产党莫斯科州委员会书记尼·米哈伊洛夫、苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记亚·谢列平、苏联共产党列宁格勒州委员会第一书记弗·科兹洛夫、乌克兰共产党基辅州委员会书记格·格里希科等人，已被提名为中央选举委员会委员的候选人。此外，乌兹别克共和国费尔干纳州高尔基集体农庄的庄员提出了该农庄女庄员、著名的丰产能手奥·尤尔达谢娃为候选人。
    按：苏联最高苏维埃中央选举委员会的职责是：办理苏联最高苏维埃的选举事宜。根据“苏联最高苏维埃选举条例”的规定，该委员会由工人和职员的工会组织、合作社组织、共产党组织、青年组织、文化、技术与科学协会，以及其他一切劳动人民的社会组织和团体与工人大会、职员大会、农民大会、军人大会的代表所组成。中央选举委员会的成员是：主席一人、副主席一人、秘书一人和委员二十四人，这些人选须经苏联最高苏维埃主席团在选举日前五十日批准。
