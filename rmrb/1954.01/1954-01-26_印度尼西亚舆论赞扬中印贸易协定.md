Title: Indonesian Public Opinion Praises China-Indonesia Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1954-01-26
Page: 4

Indonesian Public Opinion
    Praises China-Indonesia Trade Agreement
   [Xinhua News Agency, 22nd dispatch] Antara News Agency of Indonesia reported on the 21st: "Editorials in Jakarta newspapers universally praise the signing of the trade agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia." "Dongxing Daily" advocates, "All measures should be taken to realize mutually beneficial cooperation."
    The report also quoted Muhammad Hassan, Chairman of the Economic Subcommittee of the Indonesian Parliament, as saying: "I am convinced that this agreement will open up avenues for expanding the market for Indonesian products."
   [Xinhua News Agency dispatch] Jakarta News: Indonesian public opinion widely welcomed the news that the Indonesian government had approved the China-Indonesia trade agreement.
    The Indonesian-language "People's Daily" published an editorial on the 19th, emphasizing the great significance of Indonesia signing a trade agreement with neighboring China, and said: The China-Indonesia trade agreement contains broad possibilities for promoting mutually beneficial trade relations between the two countries. "Yongheng Daily's" editorial stated: The approval of the China-Indonesia trade agreement is indeed a step towards expanding the market for Indonesian export commodities and raw materials.

### 印度尼西亚舆论赞扬中印贸易协定

1954-01-26
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚舆论
    赞扬中印贸易协定
   【新华社二十二日讯】印度尼西亚安塔拉通讯社二十一日报道：“雅加达报纸的社论普遍赞扬中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国贸易协定的签订。”“东星报”主张，“应采取一切办法以实现对双方有利的合作”。
    报道并引述印尼国会经济小组委员会主席穆罕默德·哈桑的谈话说：“深信这个协定将打开扩大印尼物产市场的途径。”
   【新华社讯】雅加达消息：印尼政府批准中印（尼）贸易协定的消息公布后，印尼舆论广泛表示欢迎。
    印尼文“人民日报”十九日发表社论，强调指出印尼和邻邦中国签订贸易协定的重大意义，并说：中印（尼）贸易协定包含着促进两国间互利的贸易关系的广泛可能性。“永恒报”的社论说：中印（尼）贸易协定的批准，的确是为扩大印尼出口商品和原料的市场而迈进了一步。
