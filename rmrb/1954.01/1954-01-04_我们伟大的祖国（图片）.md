Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-01-04
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Fujian Province had a bumper tangerine harvest last year. The area around Minhou County generally saw a 20% increase in production compared to the previous year. This is a scene of fruit farmers in Yixu Township, Minhou County, picking tangerines. After processing, these tangerines will be transported to Shanghai, Tianjin and other places to meet the needs of the people.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    福建省去年柑桔获得大丰收，闽侯县一带普遍比前年增产二成。这是闽侯县义序乡的果农采摘柑桔的情形。这些柑桔经过加工后将运往上海、天津等地供人民的需要。
