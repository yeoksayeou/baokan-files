Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 203rd Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-01-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd: The 203rd meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 21st. The meeting heard a report from Teng Daiyuan, Minister of Railways of the Central People's Government, on the construction of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge. Afterwards, the meeting passed the "Decision of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government on the Construction of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge." The meeting passed the "Provisional Regulations on the Administration of Seaports of the People's Republic of China." In addition, the meeting approved various lists of appointments and removals.

### 政务院举行第二零三次会议

1954-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第二零三次会议
    【新华社二十二日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二百零三次政务会议在二十一日下午举行。会议听了中央人民政府铁道部部长滕代远所作的关于修建武汉长江大桥的报告后，通过了“中央人民政府政务院关于修建武汉长江大桥的决定”。会议通过了“中华人民共和国海港管理暂行条例”。此外，会议通过了各项任免名单。
