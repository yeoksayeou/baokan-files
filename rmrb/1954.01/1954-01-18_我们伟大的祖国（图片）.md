    Our Great Motherland
   In the Linghu area of Jiaxing Special District, Zhejiang Province, 200,000 people are engaged in freshwater fish farming, making it the largest freshwater fish farming area in East China. In order to promote scientific fish farming methods and better develop the freshwater fish farming industry, the Institute of Hydrobiology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences has set up a fish disease station here. This is a scene of scientific workers studying fish feed.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
   浙江省嘉兴专区菱湖一带，有二十万人从事淡水养鱼事业，是华东最大的淡水养鱼区。为了推行科学的养鱼方法和更好地发展淡水养鱼事业，中国科学院水生生物研究所在这里设立了鱼病工作站，这是科学工作者正在研究鱼类食料的情形。
