Date: 1954-01-27

　　Order of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government
    The "Provisional Regulations on the Administration of Seaports of the People's Republic of China," having been adopted at the 203rd Session of the Government Administration Council on January 21, 1954, are hereby promulgated for implementation.
                   Premier Zhou Enlai
                   January 23, 1954
