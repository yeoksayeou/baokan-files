Title: China Food Company Established in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-01-15
Page: 2

    China Food Company Established in Beijing
    Due to the development of the national economy and the improvement of people's living standards, especially the increasing consumption of non-staple foods by the vast residents in cities and industrial and mining areas, the supply of non-staple foods is related to the daily life of the broad masses of working people. Therefore, the Ministry of Commerce of the Central People's Government established the China Food Company in Beijing on January 1st.
    The tasks of the China Food Company are: to be responsible for organizing and guiding the production and consumption of non-staple foods, to grasp price policies, and, on the basis of closely combining with cooperatives and actively leading private merchants, to guarantee the supply of non-staple foods to large and medium-sized cities and industrial and mining areas, regulate the needs of urban and rural areas, and complete export tasks through business operations.
    The main non-staple foods currently operated by the company include meat, eggs, aquatic products, and fruits.
　(Xinhua News Agency)

### 中国食品公司在北京成立

1954-01-15
第2版()
专栏：

    中国食品公司在北京成立
    由于国民经济的发展，人民生活水平提高，特别是城市和工矿区广大居民对副食品的消费正日渐增加，副食品的供应已关系着广大劳动人民的日常生活。因此，中央人民政府商业部特于一月一日在北京成立中国食品公司。
    中国食品公司的任务是：负责组织和指导副食品的生产与消费，掌握价格政策，并在密切结合合作社、积极领导私商的基础上，通过业务经营，以保证大中城市及工矿区的副食品供应，调节城乡的需要和完成出口任务。
    该公司现已经营的主要副食品有肉类、蛋品、水产品和水果等。
　（新华社）
