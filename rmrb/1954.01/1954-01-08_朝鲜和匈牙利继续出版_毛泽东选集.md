Title: Korea and Hungary Continue to Publish "Selected Works of Mao Zedong"
Author:
Date: 1954-01-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 5th. The Korean Workers' Party Publishing House, following the publication of the Korean translation of the first volume, first part, of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" in May 1953, has recently published the Korean translations of the first volume, second part, and the second volume, first part, of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong," each with a circulation of 30,000 copies.
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 7th. Budapest dispatch: The Hungarian "Iskra" Publishing House recently published the second volume of the Hungarian edition of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong." The first volume of the Hungarian edition of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" was published in 1952.

### 朝鲜和匈牙利继续出版“毛泽东选集”

1954-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜和匈牙利继续出版“毛泽东选集”
    【新华社平壤五日电】朝鲜劳动党出版社继一九五三年五月间出版“毛泽东选集”第一卷上册的朝鲜文译本之后，最近又出版了“毛泽东选集”第一卷下册和第二卷上册的朝鲜文译本，各发行三万部。
    【新华社布拉格七日电】布达佩斯消息：匈牙利“火花”出版局最近出版了“毛泽东选集”匈牙利文版第二卷。“毛泽东选集”匈牙利文版第一卷已在一九五二年出版。
