Title: Vyshinsky Writes to UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld, Agreeing to Resumption of UN General Assembly and Suggesting Meeting on the Twenty-Second of This Month
Author:
Date: 1954-01-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New York, January 17th: TASS, New York, January 17th: The Soviet Union’s Permanent Representative to the United Nations, Vyshinsky, sent a letter to UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld on the 14th. The text of the letter is as follows: "Mr. Secretary-General,
Regarding your letter SG two five—zero one of January 11th and the attached notification from the President of the United Nations General Assembly, Mrs. Pandit, I am instructed by the Soviet government to inform you that the Soviet Union agrees with the suggestion of the President of the United Nations General Assembly to resume the eighth session of the United Nations General Assembly on February 9th of this year.
At the same time, in view of the situation in Panmunjom, the Soviet government considers it necessary to point out that the United Nations General Assembly should preferably meet earlier, namely on January 22nd of this year.
Yours sincerely, Vyshinsky"

### 维辛斯基致函联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德  同意联大复会并建议在本月二十二日开会

1954-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    维辛斯基致函联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德
    同意联大复会并建议在本月二十二日开会
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社纽约十七日讯：苏联驻联合国常任代表维辛斯基，于十四日送了一封信给联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德。信文如下：“秘书长先生阁下
    关于你的一月十一日的SG二五—○一号信件以及附来的联合国大会主席潘迪特夫人的通知，我受苏联政府的指示谨通知你：苏联同意联合国大会主席的关于联合国第八届大会在今年二月九日复会的建议。
    同时，鉴于板门店所发生的情况，苏联政府认为有必要指出：联合国大会最好是提早一些，即在今年一月二十二日开会。
                          维辛斯基谨上”
