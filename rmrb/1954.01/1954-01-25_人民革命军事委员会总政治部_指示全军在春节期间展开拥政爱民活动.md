Title: General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Issues Directive to the Entire Army to Launch Activities to Support the Government and Cherish the People During the Spring Festival

Author: 
Date: 1954-01-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 24th: The General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government recently issued a directive to the entire army on carrying out activities to support the government and cherish the people during the Spring Festival.

The directive pointed out: In order to further strengthen the unity between the army and the people, and to make the army love the motherland and the people even more, and strive to defend and strengthen the country's economic construction and national defense construction, in the activities of supporting the government and cherishing the people, all units should vigorously publicize the general line of the state during the transition period, publicize the state's grain policy and the purchase of national economic construction bonds, etc.; at the same time, all units should check the discipline of the masses and the implementation of government policies and regulations, and thank the people and the government for their care and support for the army.

The directive pointed out: Troops stationed in fraternal ethnic minority areas and border and island areas should pay special attention to helping local people develop production.

The directive said: All units can carry out activities to support the government and cherish the people through military-civilian forums, military-civilian get-togethers, etc.; and can also mobilize family members to support the state's grain policy, participate in the mutual aid and cooperation movement, and strive to participate in labor production through commanders and fighters writing letters to their families, to expand publicity to the people about the state's general line during the transition period, in order to encourage the people to actively participate in national economic construction and national defense construction.

### 人民革命军事委员会总政治部  指示全军在春节期间展开拥政爱民活动

1954-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    人民革命军事委员会总政治部
    指示全军在春节期间展开拥政爱民活动
    【新华社二十四日讯】中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部最近向全军发布了春节期间开展拥政爱民工作的指示。
    指示指出：为了进一步加强军队和人民之间的团结，使军队更加热爱祖国、热爱人民，为保卫和加强国家的经济建设和国防建设而奋斗，在拥政爱民活动中，各部队应大张旗鼓地宣传国家在过渡时期的总路线，宣传国家的粮食政策和购买国家经济建设公债等；同时各部队应检查群众纪律和执行政府政策法令的情形，感谢人民和政府对军队的爱护和支援。
   指示指出：驻在兄弟民族地区和边疆、海岛的部队，应特别注意帮助当地人民发展生产。
    指示说：各部队可以通过与驻地人民举行军民座谈会、军民联欢会等方式展开拥政爱民活动；并可通过指挥员、战斗员向家庭写信，动员家属拥护国家的粮食政策，参加互助合作运动，努力参加劳动生产，向人民扩大宣传国家在过渡时期的总路线，以鼓舞人民积极参加国家经济建设和国防建设的热情。
