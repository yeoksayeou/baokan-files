　　Molotov Hosts East German Government Officials and Political Figures
    [Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 29th] TASS, Berlin, January 29th: V.M. Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, hosted a banquet on the evening of January 28th for government officials and political figures of the German Democratic Republic. On the side of the German Democratic Republic, those attending the banquet were: Chairman of the People's Chamber, J. Dieckmann, Chairman of the Senate, R. Lobedanz, Prime Minister O. Grotewohl, Deputy Prime Ministers W. Ulbricht, L. Bolz, O. Nuschke, H. Loch, P. Scholz, H. Rau, Mayor of Greater Berlin, F. Ebert, Chairman of the Free German Trade Union Federation, H. Warnke, President of the German Academy of Sciences and Chairman of the German Peace Council, Professor Friedrich, Chairman of the National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany, Professor Correns, Minister of Culture, J. Becher, and other prominent figures from the German Democratic Republic. On the Soviet side, those attending the banquet were A.A. Gromyko, Ya.A. Malik, V.S. Semyonov, G.N. Zarubin, S.A. Vinogradov, G.M. Pushkin, I.I. Ilyichev, D.A. Zhukov, V.I. Yerofeyev. The banquet took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 莫洛托夫招待民主德国政府官员和政界人士

1954-01-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　莫洛托夫招待民主德国政府官员和政界人士
    【新华社二十九日讯】塔斯社柏林二十九日讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席兼苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫于一月二十八日晚间设宴招待德意志民主共和国政府官员和政界人士。德意志民主共和国方面出席宴会的有：人民议院主席约·狄克曼，参议院主席兰·罗贝丹兹，总理奥·格罗提渥，副总理瓦·乌布利希、洛·博尔茨、奥·努舍克、汉·洛赫、保·舒尔茨、亨·劳，大柏林市长弗·艾柏特，自由德国工会联合会主席赫·瓦恩克，德国科学院院长、德国和平委员会主席腓特烈教授，民主德国全国阵线全国委员会主席柯伦斯教授，文化部部长约·贝希尔和德意志民主共和国其他著名人士。苏联方面出席宴会的有安·阿·葛罗米柯、雅·亚·马立克、弗·斯·谢麦诺夫、格·纳·查鲁宾、斯·阿·维诺格拉多夫、格·马·普希金、伊·伊·伊利切夫、德·阿·茹科夫、维·伊·叶罗费耶夫。宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
