Title: Renmin University of China to Hold Fourth Scientific Discussion Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-01-25
Page: 3

Renmin University of China is scheduled to hold its Fourth Scientific Discussion Meeting on January 26, 27, and 28. At this discussion meeting, twenty-three scientific reports will be presented on issues such as the general line of the state during the transition period, as well as economic construction and political power construction. Examples include "The Basic Economic Laws of Our Country During the Transition Period" and "On the Worker-Peasant Alliance During China's Transition Period" and "The People's Congress System Based on Universal Suffrage is Our Basic System." In addition to the participation of all teachers and some teaching administrative cadres and graduate students from the university, representatives from relevant business departments and factories and mines, as well as academics, have also been invited to participate in the discussion meeting.

### 中国人民大学将举行第四次科学讨论会

1954-01-25
第3版()
专栏：

    中国人民大学将举行第四次科学讨论会
    中国人民大学定于一月二十六、二十七、二十八日举行第四次科学讨论会。在这次讨论会上，将作关于国家过渡时期总路线以及经济建设和政权建设等问题的二十三个科学报告。如“我国过渡时期的基本经济法则问题”、“论中国过渡时期的工农联盟问题”、“普选的人民代表大会制度是我国基本制度”等。讨论会除有该校全体教师和一部分教学行政干部、研究生参加外，并邀请了有关业务部门和厂矿的代表以及学术界人士参加。
