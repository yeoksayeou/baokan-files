Title: Polish Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Sign This Year's Implementation Plan for the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1954-01-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 21st: The Polish cultural delegation, headed by Pokora, Deputy Minister of Education of Poland, which came to China to sign the 1954 implementation plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 21st. The members of the delegation who came with him were: Habasinski, Deputy Secretary-General of the Polish Committee for Foreign Cultural Cooperation, and Kowalczyk, Chief of the Cultural Section of the Intelligence Department of the Polish Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Liaison, and others went to the airport to welcome them. The Polish Ambassador to China, Kirilok, also went to welcome them.

### 来我国签订中波文化合作协定今年执行计划  波兰文化代表团昨日到达北京

1954-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

    来我国签订中波文化合作协定今年执行计划
    波兰文化代表团昨日到达北京
    【新华社二十一日讯】前来我国签订中波文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划以波兰教育部副部长波科拉为首的文化代表团，在二十一日下午乘飞机到达北京，同来的该团团员有：波兰对外文化合作委员会副秘书长哈巴辛斯基和波兰外交部情报司文化科科长柯娃列柯娃。前往机场欢迎的有我对外文化联络事务局局长洪深等。波兰驻我国大使基里洛克也前往欢迎。
