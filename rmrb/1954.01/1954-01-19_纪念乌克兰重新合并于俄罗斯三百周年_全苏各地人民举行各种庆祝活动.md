Title: Commemorating the 300th Anniversary of Ukraine's Reunification with Russia: Celebrations Held by People Across the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1954-01-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency dispatch:
    Commemorating the 300th Anniversary of Ukraine's Reunification with Russia
    Celebrations Held by People Across the Soviet Union
    According to Xinhua News Agency: The working people throughout the Soviet Union are actively preparing for and holding celebrations for the 300th anniversary of Ukraine's reunification with Russia. In recent days, many report meetings, lectures, exhibitions, and artistic performances have been held across the country. Festive evening parties are being held successively in workers' clubs in Moscow. On the 17th, two thousand staff members of the Stalin Automobile Factory held an evening party, which included reports and wonderful musical programs. The State Library of Foreign Literature in Moscow held a grand exhibition introducing the cultural development of Ukraine throughout the ages and the dissemination of Ukrainian classical works and modern writers' works abroad. According to a decision by the USSR Council of Ministers, a monument commemorating this major event will be erected in Kiev Station Square in Moscow. To commemorate the outstanding political activist Bohdan Khmelnytsky, the Moscow City Soviet Executive Committee decided to rename Maroseyka Street in Moscow to "Bohdan Khmelnytsky Street."
    In Ukraine, tens of thousands of propagandists are publicizing the historical significance of Ukraine's reunification with Russia and the great achievements of the Soviet Union and the Ukrainian Republic among the broad masses. 23,000 choirs, 24,000 theatrical troupes, 12,000 musical groups, and 7,000 dance troupes have prepared festive performances. In anticipation of the festival, the Shevchenko Opera and Ballet Theater in Kiev has staged the operas "Bohdan Khmelnytsky" and "Prince Igor." The theaters in Kharkov, Rovno, and Stalino are also rehearsing the opera "Bohdan Khmelnytsky." Many composers, painters, and sculptors are actively engaged in creative activities. Publishing houses within the republic are also publishing a large number of new books related to the festival.
    The working people of other Soviet republics have also launched various activities to commemorate the festival. The Belarusian Academy of Sciences has convened a special scientific conference and an enlarged meeting of the scientific committees of various research institutes. Stalinabad held a conference of Tajik scientists to commemorate this major day. In Tashkent, the capital of Uzbekistan, a "Ukrainian Music Week" has begun.

### 纪念乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年  全苏各地人民举行各种庆祝活动

1954-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年
    全苏各地人民举行各种庆祝活动
    据新华社讯：苏联全国各地劳动人民积极展开筹备和举行乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年的庆祝活动。这些天来，各地举行了许多报告会、讲演会、展览会以及艺术表演。莫斯科各工人俱乐部陆续举行节日庆祝晚会。十七日，斯大林汽车工厂两千个工作人员举行了晚会。会上有报告，还有精彩的音乐节目。莫斯科的国立外国文学图书馆举办了盛大的展览会，介绍乌克兰历代文化发展和乌克兰古典著作及现代作家作品在外国传播的情况。根据苏联部长会议的决定，莫斯科的基辅车站广场将建立纪念这一重大事件的纪念碑。为了纪念杰出的政治活动家波·赫米尔尼茨基，莫斯科市苏维埃执行委员会决定将莫斯科市的马罗杰伊卡路改名为“波格丹·赫米尔尼茨基路”。
    在乌克兰，成千上万的宣传员正在广大群众中宣传乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯的历史意义，以及苏联和乌克兰共和国的伟大成就。有二万三千个合唱队、二万四千个剧团、一万二千个音乐团和七千个舞蹈队已准备了节日的演出。在迎接节日的日子里，基辅的谢夫钦科歌舞剧院已上演了歌剧“波格丹·赫米尔尼茨基”和“伊戈尔王子”。哈尔科夫、罗夫和斯大林诺的剧院也在排演歌剧“波格丹·赫米尔尼茨基”。许多作曲家、画家、雕刻家都在积极进行创作活动。共和国内各出版局也在大量出版有关节日的新书。
    苏联其他加盟共和国的劳动人民也展开了各种纪念节日的活动。白俄罗斯科学院已召开了一次专门科学会议及各研究所科学委员会的扩大会议。斯大林纳巴德举行了塔吉克科学家代表会议，来纪念这一重大的日子。在乌兹别克的首都塔什干，已开始举行“乌克兰音乐旬”。
