Title: Soviet Union Sets March Date for Supreme Soviet Elections
Author:
Date: 1954-01-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 11th: TASS Moscow, January 11th dispatch: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR issued the following decree regarding the elections to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR:

    In view of the fact that the term of office of the Third Supreme Soviet of the USSR will expire on March 12, 1954, and in accordance with Article 54 of the Constitution of the USSR, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR must announce the holding of new elections within two months after the expiration of the term of office of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and in accordance with Article 72 of the "Regulations on Elections to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR," the date of the elections to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR must be announced no later than two months before the election day, and the elections must be held on a non-working day; the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics hereby resolves:

    To set March 14, 1954 (Sunday) as the date for the elections to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

### 苏联定三月举行最高苏维埃选举

1954-01-13
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联定三月举行最高苏维埃选举
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十一日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团颁布关于苏联最高苏维埃选举的命令如下：
    鉴于第三届苏联最高苏维埃的任期将于一九五四年三月十二日届满，根据苏联宪法第五十四条，苏联最高苏维埃主席团须在苏联最高苏维埃任期届满后两个月以内宣布实行新选举，又根据“苏联最高苏维埃选举条例”第七十二条，苏联最高苏维埃的选举日期至迟须在选举日前两月宣布，而且选举须在非工作日举行；苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟最高苏维埃主席团特决定：
    以一九五四年三月十四日（星期日）为苏联最高苏维埃选举日期。
