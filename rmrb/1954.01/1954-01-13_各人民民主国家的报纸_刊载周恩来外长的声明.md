Title: Newspapers in Various People's Democratic Countries Publish Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's Statement
Author:
Date: 1954-01-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 12th: Newspapers in various People's Democratic countries have universally published the statement by Zhou Enlai, Foreign Minister of the Central People's Government of China, on the resumption of the bilateral talks on the Korean Political Conference issue.

    Czechoslovakia's "Red Right" published the full text of the statement. Other major newspapers in Prague all published summaries of the statement.

    Polish newspapers universally published the statement. "People's Tribune" published the statement under the headline "For the Sake of Peace and the Relaxation of the International Situation, the Korean Talks Should be Resumed Without Delay."

    Hungary's "Free People" and Bulgaria's "Workers' Cause" also published the full text of the statement. Romanian radio stations also rebroadcast the statement on the 10th.

### 各人民民主国家的报纸  刊载周恩来外长的声明

1954-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

    各人民民主国家的报纸
    刊载周恩来外长的声明
    【新华社十二日讯】各人民民主国家的报纸普遍刊载我中央人民政府周恩来外交部长就恢复关于朝鲜政治会议问题的双方会谈发表的声明。
    捷克斯洛伐克“红色权利报”刊载了这个声明的全文。布拉格其他各大报均刊载了声明的摘要。
    波兰各报普遍刊载了这个声明。“人民论坛报”在“为了和平和国际局势缓和的利益，朝鲜的会谈应该毫不迟延地恢复”的标题下刊载了这个声明。
    匈牙利“自由人民报”和保加利亚的“工人事业报”也刊载了这个声明的全文。罗马尼亚的各广播电台也在十日转播了这个声明。
