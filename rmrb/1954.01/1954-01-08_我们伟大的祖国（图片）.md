Our Great Motherland
State-owned trading companies and cooperatives in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and Suiyuan Province have purchased a large quantity of animal products, which will be transported to inland and northeastern regions to be used as raw materials for the wool textile and leather industries. This is a large quantity of wool newly purchased from the pastoral areas of Hulunbuir League by the Hailar Animal Products Company of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. Staff are weighing it.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    内蒙古自治区和绥远省的国营贸易公司和合作社已收购了大批畜产品，将运往关内和东北各地供作毛织、制革工业的原料。这是内蒙古自治区海拉尔畜产公司新从呼纳盟牧区收购来的大批羊毛，工作人员正在过磅。
