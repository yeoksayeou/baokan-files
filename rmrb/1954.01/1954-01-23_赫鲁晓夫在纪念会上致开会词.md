Title: Khrushchev's Opening Address at the Commemorative Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-01-23
Page: 1

Comrades!
    Thirty years ago today, the heart of our great leader and teacher, the brilliant theorist, thinker, and revolutionary organizer, Vladimir Ilyich Lenin, ceased to beat. Lenin created our great Communist Party, closely linked to the people, and tempered and defended it in the face of relentless struggles against countless enemies. Lenin led the greatest revolution that turned the development of human society onto a new, socialist path. Lenin laid the foundation for the first worker-peasant state in human history—the Soviet state—and defended it during the most difficult period of its development, when it was still a young and fragile state besieged by countless enemies at home and abroad. Lenin led the Soviet state onto the broad and bright path of building a socialist society. The immortal genius of Lenin shines on our path toward the transition to communism. Lenin proclaimed the policy of international peace and laid the foundation for the Soviet state's foreign policy line. Lenin was the first to point out the possibility of peaceful coexistence between two different economic systems.
    Our Party and the Soviet state have consistently followed these extremely important instructions of Lenin. Lenin—this is the banner of peace and friendship among all peoples! The life of Vladimir Ilyich Lenin was dedicated to the people. Lenin dedicated all his vigorous energy, brilliant genius, and iron-like indomitable will to the struggle for the liberation of working people from capitalist enslavement.
    Loyal to Lenin's legacy, the Communist Party of the Soviet Union led the people of our motherland to socialist victory. Based on the great Lenin's teachings, our Party constantly strengthens the Soviet state, strengthens the unbreakable worker-peasant alliance, strengthens the friendship of all nationalities in our motherland, and tirelessly cares for the further prosperity of our economy and the improvement of the material and cultural living standards of the Soviet people.
    Leninism has become the invincible doctrine for building a new world. The image of Lenin lives in the hearts and minds of working people of all countries, inspiring them to struggle for peace, democracy, and socialism.
    Long live the great doctrine of Marxism-Leninism, the source of our victorious strength!
    May the cause of the great Lenin live and develop forever!
    Under the banner of Lenin-Stalin, under the leadership of the Communist Party, the Soviet people are confidently advancing toward the complete victory of communism!
    I ask everyone to rise and pay tribute to the brilliant Vladimir Ilyich Lenin. (The entire audience rises)
    Comrades! I declare the solemn commemorative meeting for the thirtieth anniversary of the death of Vladimir Ilyich Lenin open.

### 赫鲁晓夫在纪念会上致开会词

1954-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫在纪念会上致开会词同志们！
    三十年前的今天，我们伟大的领袖和导师、天才的理论家、思想家和革命的组织者乌拉季米尔·伊里奇·列宁的心脏停止了跳动。列宁缔造了我们伟大的、与人民紧密联系的共产党，并在与无数敌人进行不屈不挠的斗争中锻炼了它，捍卫了它。列宁领导了使人类社会的发展转入新的、社会主义道路的最伟大的革命。列宁奠定了人类历史上第一个工农国家——苏维埃国家，并在它发展的最困难的时期中保卫了这个国家，当时这个国家还是处在国内外无数敌人围攻中的一个年青的、不稳固的国家。列宁领导苏维埃国家走上了建设社会主义社会的宽广的光明大道。列宁的永世不灭的天才的光芒，照耀着我国向共产主义过渡的道路。列宁宣布了国际和平的政策并奠定了苏维埃国家外交政策路线的基础。列宁首先指出两种不同的经济制度和平共处的可能性。
    我们的党和苏维埃国家始终不渝地遵循着列宁的这些极其重要的指示。列宁——这是和平与各国人民友好的旗帜！乌·伊·列宁的一生献给了人民。列宁把他的充沛的精力、光辉的天才、钢铁般的不屈不挠的意志全部献给了为解放劳动人民摆脱资本主义奴役的斗争。
    忠于列宁遗训的苏联共产党领导我们祖国的人民取得了社会主义的胜利。我们的党依据伟大列宁的学说，不断地加强苏维埃国家，加强牢不可破的工农联盟，加强我们祖国各族人民的友谊，不倦地关心我国经济的进一步的繁荣和苏联人民物质和文化生活水平的提高。
    列宁主义已成为战无不胜的建设新世界的学说。列宁的形象，活在各国劳动人民的心中和意识中，鼓舞着他们为和平、民主与社会主义而斗争。
    我们胜利的有力的源泉马克思—列宁主义的伟大学说永垂不朽！
    愿伟大列宁的事业永生和发展！
    在列宁—斯大林的旗帜下，在共产党的领导下，苏联人民满怀信心地向共产主义的完全胜利前进！
    我要求大家起立向光辉的乌拉季米尔·伊里奇·列宁致敬。（全场起立）
    同志们！我宣布：乌拉季米尔·伊里奇·列宁逝世三十周年的隆重的纪念会开始。
