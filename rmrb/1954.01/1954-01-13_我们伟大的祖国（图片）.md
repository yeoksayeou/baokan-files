    Our Great Motherland
    The Xiangjiang River Bridge spanning the Xiangjiang River is basically completed. It connects the east and west banks of Xiangtan City and will connect the Zhe-Gan Railway and the Xiang-Qian Railway, which is about to be built. This is a panoramic view of the Xiangjiang River Bridge.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    横跨湘江两岸的湘江大铁桥已基本完工。它把湘潭市的东西两岸联结起来，并要把浙赣铁路和将要兴建的湘黔铁路贯穿起来。这是湘江大桥的全景。
