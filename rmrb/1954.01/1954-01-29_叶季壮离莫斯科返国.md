Title: Ye Jizhuang Departs Moscow for Home

Author:
Date: 1954-01-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th—Ye Jizhuang, Vice-Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government and Minister of Foreign Trade, left Moscow by plane for Beijing on the 26th. Acting Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union Borisov, Vice-Minister Kuzmin and others were at the airport to see him off. Charge d'affaires ad interim Ge Baoquan and Commercial Counselor Li Qiang of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union were also at the airport to see him off.

### 叶季壮离莫斯科返国

1954-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　叶季壮离莫斯科返国
    【新华社莫斯科二十八日电】我国中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会副主任、对外贸易部部长叶季壮，二十六日乘飞机离莫斯科回北京。到机场送行的有苏联对外贸易部代理部长波里索夫、副部长库兹明等。我国驻苏联大使馆临时代办戈宝权、商务参赞李强等也到机场送行。
