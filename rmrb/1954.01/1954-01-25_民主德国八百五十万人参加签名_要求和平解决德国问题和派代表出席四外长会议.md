Title: Eight and a Half Million People in the German Democratic Republic Sign Petition Demanding Peaceful Resolution of the German Question and Representation at the Four Foreign Ministers' Conference
Author:
Date: 1954-01-25
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 24th dispatch: According to TASS, the German News Agency reported that as of January 23, more than eight and a half million people in the German Democratic Republic have signed in support of the German people's demands for a peaceful resolution of the German question and the invitation of representatives from the German Democratic Republic and West Germany to attend the upcoming Four Foreign Ministers' Conference. The National Council of the National Front of the German Democratic Republic is constantly receiving numerous resolutions, letters, and telegrams from people of all walks of life demanding that German representatives attend the Four Foreign Ministers' Conference.

### 民主德国八百五十万人参加签名  要求和平解决德国问题和派代表出席四外长会议

1954-01-25
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国八百五十万人参加签名
    要求和平解决德国问题和派代表出席四外长会议
    【新华社二十四日讯】据塔斯社柏林讯：德意志通讯社报道，截至一月二十三日为止，在德意志民主共和国已有八百五十万以上的人签名支持德国人民的要求：和平解决德国问题和邀请德意志民主共和国和西德的代表出席即将召开的四国外长会议。民主德国全国阵线全国委员会不断收到各阶层人民寄来的许多决议、信件和电报，要求德国代表出席四国外长会议。
