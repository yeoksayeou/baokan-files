Title: Representatives of the British, Soviet, American, and French High Commissioners in Germany Hold Fifth Meeting, Reaching Agreement on the Venue for the Berlin Four-Power Foreign Ministers Conference
Author:
Date: 1954-01-19
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 17th: According to TASS, the representatives of the British, Soviet, American, and French High Commissioners in Germany held their fifth meeting at the British Military Government headquarters in Berlin to discuss the venue for the Berlin Four-Power Foreign Ministers Conference and other technical issues related to the conference. The four representatives agreed that the Four-Power Foreign Ministers Conference would be held at the former Allied Control Council building at 32 Elssholz Street and the Soviet High Commissioner's Office in Germany at 63-65 Unter den Linden. The four representatives also agreed that the first week of the Foreign Ministers Conference would be held at the former Allied Control Council building, the second week at the Soviet High Commissioner's Office in Germany, and the third week at the former Allied Control Council building. The location of subsequent meetings will be decided by the Four-Power Foreign Ministers Conference. Some technical issues have now been entrusted to experts for handling.

### 英、苏、美、法驻德高级专员代表举行第五次会议  就柏林四国外长会议的地址问题达成协议

1954-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    英、苏、美、法驻德高级专员代表举行第五次会议
    就柏林四国外长会议的地址问题达成协议
    【新华社十八日讯】据塔斯社柏林十七日讯：英、苏、美、法四国驻德高级专员的代表在柏林的英国军政府召开了第五次会议，讨论柏林四国外长会议的地址问题以及有关会议的其他技术问题。四国代表同意：四国外长会议在艾伊斯霍尔茨街三十二号前盟国管制委员会大厦和菩提树下街六十三—六十五号苏联驻德高级专员公署举行。四国代表并同意：外长会议第一周在前盟国管制委员会大厦举行，第二周在苏联驻德高级专员公署举行，第三周在前盟国管制委员会大厦举行，其后会议在何处举行将由四国外长会议决定。关于一些技术问题现已委托一些专家办理。
