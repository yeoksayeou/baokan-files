Title: Snowfall in Northern Sichuan and the Chengdu Plain
Author: 
Date: 1954-01-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, January 23rd: Northern Sichuan Province and the Chengdu Plain have been experiencing snowfall since the 21st and 22nd. This snow can aid in winter pest control and promote the healthy growth of small spring crops such as wheat.

### 四川省北部和成都平原降雪

1954-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    四川省北部和成都平原降雪
   【新华社成都二十三日电】四川省北部地区和成都平原从二十一日和二十二日起降雪。这场雪可以有助于冬季治虫工作，并可促进小麦等小春作物的良好生长。
