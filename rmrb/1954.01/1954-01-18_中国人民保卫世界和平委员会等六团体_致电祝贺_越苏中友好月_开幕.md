Title: Six Groups Including the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Send Telegrams Congratulating the Opening of "Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month"
Author:
Date: 1954-01-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and five other national people's organizations sent a telegram on the 17th to the Central Organizing Committee of the Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month, congratulating them on the holding of the Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month. The telegram is as follows:
To the Central Organizing Committee of the Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month:
    On the occasion of the Vietnamese people holding the Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month, we would like to extend our heartfelt congratulations to you on behalf of the Chinese people, and through you to express our respect to all the Vietnamese people who are fighting for independence, freedom and peace.
    Under the leadership of the Vietnam Workers' Party and President Ho Chi Minh, the heroic Vietnamese people have bravely carried out a long and arduous war of resistance, and have continuously achieved brilliant victories in the struggle against imperialist armed aggression. The struggle of the Vietnamese people has been sympathized with and cared for by progressive people all over the world. The Chinese people firmly support the struggle of the Vietnamese people for national independence, freedom and peace, and, like the Vietnamese people, attach importance to the Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month now being held in Vietnam, regarding it as a major event in their own politics. We are convinced that through the activities of the Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month, the close friendship between the peoples of Vietnam and China will be further consolidated and developed. We would like to wish the Vietnamese people greater achievements and brilliant victories in the struggle to win national independence and defend world lasting peace under the leadership of the Vietnam Workers' Party and President Ho Chi Minh.
    Long live the unbreakable friendship of the peoples of Vietnam, the Soviet Union and China!
    Long live the Democratic Republic of Vietnam!
    Long live President Ho Chi Minh!
    Long live the world peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union!
            The Chinese People's Committee for World peace
            All-China Federation of Trade Unions
            All-China Democratic Women's Federation.
            All-china Federation of Democratic Youth
            All-China Students' Federation
            China Federation of Literary and Art Circles
                    January 17, 1954

### 中国人民保卫世界和平委员会等六团体  致电祝贺“越苏中友好月”开幕

1954-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会等六团体
    致电祝贺“越苏中友好月”开幕
    【新华社十七日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会等六个全国性人民团体，十七日致电越南越苏中友好月中央组织委员会，祝贺越苏中友好月的举行。电文如下：越苏中友好月中央组织委员会：
    值此越南人民举行越苏中友好月之际，谨代表中国人民向你们致以衷心的祝贺，并通过你们向为争取独立、自由与和平而斗争的全体越南人民致以敬意。
    英雄的越南人民，在越南劳动党和胡志明主席的领导下，英勇地进行了长期的艰苦抗战，在反对帝国主义武装侵略的斗争中，不断地取得了辉煌的胜利。越南人民的斗争，为全世界进步人类所同情和关怀，中国人民坚决支持越南人民争取民族独立自由与和平的斗争，并像越南人民一样地重视现在越南所举行的越苏中友好月，把它当作是自己政治上的一件大事。我们确信通过越苏中友好月这一活动，越中两国人民的亲密友谊将会更加巩固与发展。我们谨祝越南人民在越南劳动党和胡志明主席的领导下，为赢得民族独立与保卫世界持久和平的斗争中获得更大的成就与辉煌的胜利。
    越苏中三国人民的牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    越南民主共和国万岁！
    胡志明主席万岁！
    以苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营万岁！
            中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
            中华全国总工会
            中华全国民主妇女联合会
            中华全国民主青年联合会
            中华全国学生联合会
            中国文学艺术界联合会
                    一九五四年一月十七日
