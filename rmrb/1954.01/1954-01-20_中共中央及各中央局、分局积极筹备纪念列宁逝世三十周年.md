Title: CCP Central Committee and Various Central Bureaus and Sub-bureaus Actively Prepare to Commemorate the 30th Anniversary of Lenin's Death
Author:
Date: 1954-01-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The 21st of January this year marks the 30th anniversary of the death of the great revolutionary teacher Lenin. The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and various central bureaus, sub-bureaus, and other major cities across the country are actively preparing for commemorations. In recent days, people from all walks of life in the capital have also been actively preparing for the commemoration. The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in Beijing will jointly hold commemorative meetings, report meetings, and film evenings. The Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace will exhibit pictures commemorating Lenin; in addition to holding a picture exhibition, the Beijing Library will also exhibit Lenin's immortal works.

In addition, Shenyang, Shanghai, Wuhan, Chongqing, Xi'an and other places are also actively preparing for commemorative activities. Laboring people in various cities will hold commemorative rallies, with the heads of local organizations of the Chinese Communist Party making reports on commemorating Lenin. Soviet experts who are helping our country with construction in various places will also participate in these commemorative activities. In Shanghai, the Huaihai Road, Changshou Road, and the gallery of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Hall have already been decorated with picture exhibitions about Lenin's life. The East China and Shanghai People's Broadcasting Stations will also broadcast stories about Lenin. Movie theaters in various cities will screen "The Great Dawn" and other films about Lenin's revolutionary activities during the period commemorating the 30th anniversary of Lenin's death. Xi'an City will also organize film teams to show films commemorating Lenin to workers and farmers in the suburbs. In order to promote the people of all circles to earnestly study Lenin's theories on building socialism, the Tianjin Sino-Soviet Friendship Association printed a large number of propaganda booklets "Commemorate Lenin, Study Lenin's Theories on Building Socialism" before the commemoration, and distributed them to reporters at all levels in the city to report to the general public. The Harbin Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Songjiang Provincial Sino-Soviet Friendship Association have also compiled speaking materials for propagandists, supplying them to local newspapers and radio stations, as well as a large number of propagandists for widespread publicity to the people.

### 中共中央及各中央局、分局积极筹备纪念列宁逝世三十周年

1954-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央及各中央局、分局
    积极筹备纪念列宁逝世三十周年
    【新华社十九日讯】今年一月二十一日是伟大革命导师列宁逝世三十周年。中国共产党中央委员会及各中央局、分局和全国其他大城市，正在积极筹备纪念。近几天来，首都各界人民也正积极准备纪念。北京市中苏友好协会和苏联对外文化协会在北京的机关将联合举行纪念会、报告会和电影晚会。北京市劳动人民文化宫将展览纪念列宁的图片；北京图书馆除举办图片展览外，并将展出列宁的不朽著作。
    此外，沈阳、上海、武汉、重庆、西安等地，也正积极筹备纪念活动。各城市劳动人民都将举行纪念大会，由中国共产党当地组织的负责人作关于纪念列宁的报告。正在各地帮助我国建设的苏联专家，也将参加这些纪念活动。这几天，上海市的淮海路、长寿路及中苏友谊馆的画廊，已布置好有关列宁生平的图片展览。华东和上海市人民广播电台并将播送列宁的故事。各城市电影院都将在纪念列宁逝世三十周年的期间，放映“伟大的曙光”及其他有关列宁从事革命活动的影片。西安市并将组织电影队向郊区工人、农民放映有关纪念列宁的影片。为了推动各界人民认真学习列宁关于建设社会主义的学说，天津市中苏友好协会并在纪念前夕印发了大批“纪念列宁，学习列宁关于建设社会主义的学说”的宣传手册，分发给全市各级报告员向广大人民作报告。哈尔滨市中苏友好协会和松江省中苏友好协会也已编印了宣传员讲话材料，供给当地报纸和广播电台及广大宣传员向人民作广泛宣传。
