Title: Lhasa Celebrates the New Year's Day of 1954
Author:
Date: 1954-01-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 3rd. The officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Tibet Military Region stationed in Lhasa, together with people from all walks of life in Lhasa, joyfully celebrated the New Year's Day of 1954. Before the New Year, the organs and troops of the Tibet Military Region stationed in Lhasa prepared a large amount of white flour, meat, and a rich variety of vegetables and other New Year's food, and rehearsed many recreational programs, wrote Tibetan and Chinese wall newspapers, and sent New Year's cards to Tibetan people from all walks of life in Lhasa. Tibetan and Han students of the Cadre School of the Tibet Military Region also wrote New Year's letters to the heroes of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the creators of new records on the economic construction front of the motherland, warmly congratulating them on making greater achievements in defending world peace and building the motherland in the new year.
    On New Year's Day, the organs and troops of the Tibet Military Region stationed in Lhasa and the Tibetan local army held a group worship meeting in the square in front of the Potala Palace. Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government, General Tan Guansan, political commissar of the Tibet Military Region, and Ape A-wang Jinmei, deputy commander, all spoke at the meeting. They unanimously congratulated the great motherland on the brilliant victories achieved in the struggle to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea and on the economic construction front in 1953; and congratulated the increasingly consolidated unity of all ethnic groups in the motherland and within the Tibetan nationality. They called on the People's Liberation Army, working personnel, and the Tibetan people entering Tibet to actively build the Kangzang Highway and strive for the early opening of the entire line to traffic in the new year.
    That afternoon, the headquarters and political department of the Tibet Military Region invited monks and secular officials of the Tibetan local government to hold a grand New Year celebration banquet. The People's Liberation Army troops and people from all walks of life in Tibet happily danced, watched movies, and held joint celebrations together.

### 拉萨各界欢乐度过一九五四年的新年佳节

1954-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    拉萨各界欢乐度过一九五四年的新年佳节
     【新华社拉萨三日电】中国人民解放军西藏军区驻拉萨各机关、部队和拉萨市各界藏族人民欢乐地度过了一九五四年的新年佳节。年前，西藏军区驻拉萨市的各机关、部队准备了大量的白面、肉类和丰富的菜蔬等过年的食品，并排演了许多游艺节目，编写了藏文和汉文墙报，并向拉萨各界藏族人士赠送了贺年片。西藏军区干部学校的藏族和汉族的学员们，还专门给中国人民志愿军的英雄们和在祖国经济建设战线上的新纪录的创造者们写了贺年信，热烈地祝贺他们在新的一年里为保卫世界和平和建设祖国作出更大的成绩。
    元旦这天，西藏军区驻拉萨各机关、部队和西藏地方军队，在布达拉宫前面的广场上，举行了团拜大会。中央人民政府代表张经武将军、西藏军区政治委员谭冠三将军和副司令员阿沛·阿旺晋美都在大会上讲了话，他们一致祝贺伟大祖国在一九五三年内抗美援朝的斗争中和经济建设战线上取得的辉煌胜利；祝贺祖国大家庭内各族人民和西藏民族内部的团结日趋巩固。他们号召进藏的人民解放军、工作人员和西藏人民在新的一年内积极修筑康藏公路，争取全线早日通车。
    当天下午，西藏军区司令部、政治部邀请西藏地方政府的僧俗官员举行了盛大的庆祝新年的宴会。人民解放军部队和藏族各界人民愉快地在一起跳舞、看电影、举行联欢晚会。
