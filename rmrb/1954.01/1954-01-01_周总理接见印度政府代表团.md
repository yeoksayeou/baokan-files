Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Indian Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-01-01
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, 31st. Premier of the State Council and concurrently Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Zhou Enlai, received the Indian Government delegation on the 31st, which is conducting negotiations with the Chinese government on the issue of relations between China and India in the Tibet region of China. Those received included: the head of the Indian Government delegation, Mr. N. Raghavan, Indian Ambassador to China; the deputy head, Mr. R.K. Nehru, Joint Secretary of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs; and advisor Dr. Gopalachari, an official of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs.
    Those present at the reception on our side were: Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Yang Gongsu, Foreign Affairs Assistant of the Central People's Government's representative in Tibet.
    During the reception, both sides confirmed that the Sino-Indian negotiations commenced on December 31, 1953.

### 周总理接见印度政府代表团

1954-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见印度政府代表团
    【新华社三十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来在三十一日接见了就中印两国在中国西藏地方的关系问题与中国政府进行商谈的印度政府代表团。受接见者有：印度政府代表团团长印度驻华大使赖嘉文先生，副团长印度政府外交部联合秘书高尔先生，顾问印度政府外交部官员高巴拉查里博士。
    接见时在座者有我方商谈代表：中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫，外交部亚洲司司长陈家康，中央人民政府驻西藏代表外事帮办杨公素。
    在接见中，双方确定中印谈判自一九五三年十二月三十一日即行开始。
