Title: Hungarian Ambassador to China, Szobek, Calls on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1954-01-27
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, January 26th: The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, Szobek, and Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy, Czátor D'or, paid a courtesy call to Minister Zhou Enlai of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government on the afternoon of the 26th to discuss matters relating to the presentation of credentials. Also present were Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 匈牙利驻我国大使苏贝克拜会周恩来外长

1954-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　匈牙利驻我国大使苏贝克拜会周恩来外长
   【新华社二十六日讯】匈牙利人民共和国新任驻我国特命全权大使苏贝克和大使馆临时代办察托尔道伊，在二十六日下午到中央人民政府外交部拜会周恩来部长，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座的还有外交部苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇、交际处处长王倬如。
