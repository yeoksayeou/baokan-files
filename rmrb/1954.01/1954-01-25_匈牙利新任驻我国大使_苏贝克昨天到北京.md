Title: Hungarian New Ambassador to Our Country, Suba, Arrived in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1954-01-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 24th - The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to our country, Suba, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 24th. Those who went to the airport to welcome him were: Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.
    Heads of diplomatic missions of various countries in China, diplomatic personnel, and Czător Döi, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Hungarian People's Republic in China, and all members of the embassy also went to the airport to welcome him.

### 匈牙利新任驻我国大使  苏贝克昨天到北京

1954-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利新任驻我国大使
    苏贝克昨天到北京
   【新华社二十四日讯】匈牙利人民共和国新任驻我国特命全权大使苏贝克于二十四日下午乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有：中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权、交际处处长王倬如。
    各国驻我国使节、外交人员和匈牙利人民共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办察托尔道伊及全体馆员也到机场欢迎。
