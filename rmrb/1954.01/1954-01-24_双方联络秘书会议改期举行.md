Title: Bilateral Liaison Secretaries Meeting Rescheduled
Author:
Date: 1954-01-24
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, January 23rd: The delegations from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the People's Republic of China participating in the bilateral talks on the Political Conference issue issued the following communiqué: The bilateral liaison secretaries meeting, originally scheduled to continue today, has been postponed to January 26th with the agreement of both parties due to the inability of the Korean-Chinese side's liaison secretary to attend for technical reasons.

### 双方联络秘书会议改期举行

1954-01-24
第4版()
专栏：

    双方联络秘书会议改期举行
    【新华社开城二十三日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国与中华人民共和国参加关于政治会议问题双方会谈的代表团发表公报如下：原定于今日继续举行的双方联络秘书会议，朝中方面联络秘书因技术性原因不能出席，经过双方同意改期于一月二十六日举行。
