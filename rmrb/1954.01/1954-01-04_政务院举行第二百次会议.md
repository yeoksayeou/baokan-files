Title: Government Administration Council Holds 200th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-01-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 2nd—The 200th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of December 31st. The meeting heard and approved the report made by Huang Yanpei, Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Light Industry, on the basic situation and future work arrangements of the Central Ministry of Light Industry. The meeting passed the Government Administration Council's instructions on sorting out several issues in agricultural loans. In addition, the meeting also passed various lists of appointments and removals, and various lists of appointments and removals to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第二百次会议

1954-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第二百次会议
     【新华社二日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二百次政务会议在十二月三十一日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府轻工业部部长黄炎培所作的中央轻工业部工作的基本情况及今后工作部署的报告。会议通过了政务院关于清理农业贷款中若干问题的指示。此外，会议并通过了各项任免名单和提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免的各项名单。
