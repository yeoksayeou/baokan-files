Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish Foreign Minister Zhou's Statement
Author:
Date: 1954-01-31
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 30th: All Moscow newspapers on the 30th prominently published the statement by Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, regarding the United Nations Command's forcible retention of Korean and Chinese prisoners of war.
