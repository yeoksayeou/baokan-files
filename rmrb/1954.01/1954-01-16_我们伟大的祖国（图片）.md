Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-01-16
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The winter construction of the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway's Wushaoling project has fully begun. At the Wushaoling construction site, 3,000 meters above sea level, bulldozer operators are carrying out the massive project of moving mountains and filling rivers. The bulldozer operators have created a parallel bulldozing method, greatly increasing the speed of the project.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-01-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    兰新铁路乌鞘岭工程的冬季施工已全面开始。在拔海三千公尺的乌鞘岭工地上，推土机手们正在进行移山填河的巨大工程。推土机手们创造了推土机并排推土法，大大地提高了工程的速度。
