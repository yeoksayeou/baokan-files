Title: Establishment of the People's Government of Haibei Tibetan Autonomous Region, Qinghai Province
Author:
Date: 1954-01-23
Page: 3

The first session of the First People's Representative Conference of the Haibei Tibetan Autonomous Region, Qinghai Province, was held in Haiyan from December 24 to 31, 1953. A total of 154 representatives from Tibetan, Mongolian, Hui, Han, Tu, and Salar ethnic groups attended the meeting. The meeting acted on behalf of the People's Congress, elected Xia Rong Ga Bu (Tibetan) as Chairman of the Autonomous Region People's Government, Ma Fangfu (Han), Hua Baozang (Tibetan), Tong Quhu (Tibetan), Nanmu Kacaixiang (Tibetan), Ma Luwen (Hui), and Gongbao Dangshiji (Mongolian) as Vice Chairmen, and Sangdaihai (Tibetan), Sanmu Dan (Mongolian), and 16 others as government members, formally establishing the Haibei Tibetan Autonomous Region People's Government, equivalent to a special zone level. At the meeting, the Haibei Tibetan Autonomous Region Consultative Committee was also established, electing Wei Jinde (Han) as Chairman, Niangba (Tibetan), Lin Xinwang Jile (Mongolian), Ma Wenkui (Hui), and Suhu Jiancan (Tibetan) as Vice Chairmen, and Gongcai Diao (Mongolian), Suobu Zhen (Tibetan), and 24 others as members.

The meeting focused on discussing the economic work of the autonomous region. The representatives unanimously agreed that in the future, it is necessary to continue implementing the policy of ethnic equality and unity, vigorously develop animal husbandry production, publicize and guide farmers of all ethnic groups in agricultural areas to take the road of mutual aid and cooperation, and strengthen mutual support between industry, agriculture, and animal husbandry. At the meeting, representatives from various counties also put forward 24 proposals related to ethnic unity, production, and the training of ethnic minority cadres.

At the end of the meeting, Xia Rong Ga Bu, Chairman of the Autonomous Region People's Government, gave a concluding report. He pointed out that the achievements of Haibei Tibetan area in the past four years were achieved under the leadership of the Communist Party of China and the higher-level People's Government, and that it is necessary to continue to earnestly implement the various policies and decrees of the higher-level People's Government in the future. He called on the representatives to bring the spirit of the meeting to the masses and mobilize people of all ethnic groups to make unremitting struggles to build a prosperous and happy Haibei Tibetan Autonomous Region. The meeting also passed a congratulatory telegram to Chairman Mao.

On January 1, more than 2,000 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life gathered in Haiyan County, the seat of the Autonomous Region People's Government, to celebrate the official establishment of the Autonomous Region People's Government. National flags were hung in the streets and alleys, colored arches were set up, and celebration slogans in Tibetan, Mongolian, Han, and other ethnic languages were posted. At the celebration meeting, farmers and herdsmen of all ethnic groups excitedly stated that they would further develop production in the future and work hard for the prosperity of the autonomous region.
(Xinhua News Agency)

Xinhua News Agency. The first joint meeting of the People's Government Committee and the Consultative Committee of the Haibei Tibetan Autonomous Region, Qinghai Province, was held on January 2. The meeting determined the future tasks of the Autonomous Region People's Government, which is to develop various construction undertakings in the autonomous region on the basis of further strengthening and consolidating ethnic equality and unity. In the future, the people's governments at all levels in the autonomous region will continue to adopt democratic consultations to reasonably mediate various disputes between and within ethnic groups, and vigorously train minority ethnic cadres in various tasks; at the same time, continue to publicize the policies of "no division, no struggle, no class division" and "mutual benefit for herdsmen and herdowners" in pastoral areas, lead herdsmen to improve livestock feeding and management methods, and help poor herdsmen settle down and produce in a planned and focused manner; and educate farmers in agricultural areas to gradually develop mutual aid and cooperative organizations. All localities will enrich and develop state-owned trading institutions, continue to pilot supply and marketing cooperatives, further expand the exchange of industrial products, agricultural products, and livestock products, and gradually improve people's lives. At the meeting, Xia Rong Ga Bu, Chairman of the Autonomous Region People's Government, called on all members to further promote the enthusiasm of "being masters of their own affairs" under the leadership of the Communist Party of China and the higher-level People's Government, conscientiously implement the resolutions of the meeting and the various policies and decrees of the higher-level People's Government, and give full play to the effectiveness of the ethnic regional autonomous power.
(Xinhua News Agency)

### 青海省海北藏族自治区人民政府成立

1954-01-23
第3版()
专栏：

    青海省海北藏族自治区人民政府成立
    青海省海北藏族自治区首届第一次人民代表会议于一九五三年十二月二十四日到三十一日在亹源举行。出席会议的有藏、蒙、回、汉、土、撒拉等民族的代表共一百五十四人。会议代行人民代表大会职权，选出夏茸尕布（藏族）为自治区人民政府主席，马芳富（汉族）、华宝藏（藏族）、同曲乎（藏族）、南木卡才巷（藏族）、马录文（回族）、贡保当什吉（蒙族）为副主席，桑代海（藏族）、三木淡（蒙族）等十八人为政府委员，正式成立了相当于专区级的海北藏族自治区人民政府。会上并成立了海北藏族自治区协商委员会，选出魏进德（汉族）为主席，娘巴（藏族）、林心旺济勒（蒙族）、马文奎（回族）、苏乎尖参（藏族）为副主席，贡才刁（蒙族）、索布振（藏族）等二十六人为委员。
    会议着重讨论了自治区的经济工作。代表们一致认为：今后要继续贯彻民族平等团结的方针，大力发展畜牧业生产，在农业区要宣传和引导各族农民走互助合作的道路，加强工、农、牧业的相互支援。会上，各县代表并提出有关民族团结、生产、培养民族干部等提案二十四件。
  会议最后由自治区人民政府主席夏茸尕布作总结报告。他指出：海北藏区过去四年来的各项工作成就，是在中国共产党和上级人民政府领导下取得的，今后要继续认真执行上级人民政府的各项政策法令。他号召与会代表要把会议精神带到群众中去，发动各族人民为建设繁荣幸福的海北藏族自治区作不懈的斗争。会议并通过了给毛主席的致敬电。
    一月一日，在自治区人民政府所在地亹源县城，各族各界人民二千多人集会庆祝自治区人民政府的正式成立。大街小巷都悬挂着国旗，搭起了彩牌，张贴着藏、蒙、汉等民族文字的庆祝标语。庆祝会上，各族农民、牧民都兴奋地表示：今后要进一步发展生产，为自治区的繁荣而努力。
                                  （新华社）
    青海省海北藏族自治区第一次人民政府委员会、协商委员会联席会议在一月二日举行。会议确定了自治区人民政府今后工作任务，是在进一步加强和巩固民族平等团结的基础上，发展自治区的各项建设事业。自治区各级人民政府今后将继续采取民主协商的方式，合理调处各民族间和民族内部的各种纠纷，并在各项工作中大力培养少数民族干部；同时在牧业区继续宣传“不分不斗、不划阶级”和“牧工、牧主两利”的政策，领导牧民改善牲畜饲放管理方法，并有计划、有重点地帮助贫苦牧民安家生产；在农业区教育农民逐步发展互助合作组织。各地将充实和发展国营贸易机构，继续试办供销合作社，进一步扩大工业品和农产品、畜产品的交流，逐步改善人民生活。会上，自治区人民政府主席夏茸尕布号召全体委员在中国共产党和上级人民政府领导下，进一步发挥“当家作主”的积极性，认真贯彻会议决议和上级人民政府的各项政策法令，充分发挥民族区域自治政权的效能。
                                  （新华社）
