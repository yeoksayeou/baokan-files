Title: Premier Kim Il-sung Hosts Banquet to Celebrate New Year
Author:
Date: 1954-01-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 3rd: According to the Korean Central News Agency, Marshal Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, held a banquet on the 1st to celebrate the New Year of 1954.

Korean attendees at the banquet included: Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea; Park Jung-ae, Park Chang-ok, and Kim Il, Vice Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Vice Premiers of the Cabinet Choi Yong-kun, Hong Myung-hui, Choi Chang-ik, Jung Il-ryong, Park Ui-wan, and other government leaders; generals of the Korean People's Army; and heads of various democratic parties and social organizations.

Invited to the banquet were: generals of the Chinese People's Volunteers and economic experts and technical personnel from the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries who came to Korea to assist the Korean people in post-war reconstruction.

Marshal Kim Il-sung delivered a speech at the beginning of the banquet. He said: As we bid farewell to the victorious year of 1953 and welcome the year of 1954, filled with new joy and hope, I, along with the people of the whole country, extend New Year's greetings to all of you present at the banquet. On behalf of the Korean Workers' Party and the Republic's government, Marshal Kim Il-sung extended congratulations and gratitude to the people of all walks of life in Korea, the officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army, the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteers, and the experts and technical personnel of friendly countries in Korea.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly toasted to the health of the beloved leader of the Korean people, Marshal Kim Il-sung, to the brilliant achievements of Korea's post-war reconstruction, to the health of the great Soviet people and the Soviet leaders headed by Comrade Malenkov, to the health of the great Chinese people and the wise leader Chairman Mao Zedong, and to the health of the people of all fraternal countries and their leaders. The banquet was filled with an atmosphere of warmth, friendship, and joy.

### 金日成首相举行宴会祝贺新年

1954-01-04
第4版()
专栏：

    金日成首相举行宴会祝贺新年
    【新华社平壤三日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅在一日举行宴会，祝贺一九五四年新年。
    朝鲜方面出席宴会的有：朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、朴昌玉、金一，内阁副首相崔庸健、洪命熹、崔昌益、郑一龙、朴义琓和其他政府首长，朝鲜人民军将领和各民主政党、社会团体负责人等。
    应邀出席宴会的有：中国人民志愿军将领和来朝鲜援助朝鲜人民进行恢复建设的苏联及各人民民主国家的经济专家与技术人员。
    金日成元帅在宴会开始时致词。他说：送别胜利的一九五三年，迎接充满新的欢欣和希望的一九五四年，我和全国人民一道，向出席宴会的各位致以新年的祝贺。金日成元帅以朝鲜劳动党与共和国政府的名义，向朝鲜各界人民、朝鲜人民军将士、中国人民志愿军将士以及在朝鲜的各友邦国家的专家们与技术人员表示祝贺和感谢。
    席间，宾主频频为朝鲜人民的敬爱领袖金日成元帅的健康，为朝鲜战后恢复建设取得光辉成就，为伟大的苏联人民及以马林科夫同志为首的苏联领袖们的健康，为伟大的中国人民和英明领袖毛泽东主席的健康，为各兄弟国家人民及其领袖们的健康而干杯。宴会充满了热烈、友好和欢欣的空气。
