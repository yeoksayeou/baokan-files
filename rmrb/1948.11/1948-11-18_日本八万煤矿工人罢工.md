Title: Japanese Eighty Thousand Coal Miners Strike

Author: 
Date: 1948-11-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 14th: Tokyo dispatch: Disregarding the warning from Hepler, chief of the Labor Section of MacArthur Headquarters, 80,000 Japanese coal miners went on strike on the 10th according to the union's plan, demanding higher wages and improved safety equipment in the coal mines. In Fukushima Prefecture on Honshu, 40,000 workers at 75 coal mines went on strike for 24 hours. In the Kyushu coal mining area, 30,000 workers in Fukuoka, 8,000 workers in Saga, and 5,000 workers in Nagasaki all went on strike for 24 hours.

### 日本八万煤矿工人罢工

1948-11-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　日本八万煤矿工人罢工
    【新华社陕北十四日电】东京讯：日本八万煤矿工人蔑视麦克阿瑟总部劳工科长赫普勒的警告，于十日按照工会计划举行罢工，要求提高工资和改善煤矿中的安全设备。在本州的福岛县，七十五所煤矿的四万工人罢工二十四小时。在九州煤矿区，福冈的三万工人、佐贺的八千工人、长崎的五千工人均罢工二十四小时。
