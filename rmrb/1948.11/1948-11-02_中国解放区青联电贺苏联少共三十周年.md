Title: China Liberated Areas Youth Federation Sends Congratulatory Message to the Soviet Young Communist League on its 30th Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1948-11-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 30th: The China Liberated Areas Youth Federation recently sent a congratulatory message to the Soviet All-Union Leninist Young Communist League on its 30th anniversary. The telegram stated:

"To Comrade Mikhailov of the Soviet All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, and to all members:

We, representing the youth of China, congratulate the Soviet All-Union Leninist Young Communist League on its 30th anniversary. We will call on Chinese youth to learn from the great and heroic spirit of struggle displayed by you and all Soviet youth in the war to defend the motherland and in the five-year economic construction plan, and to strive with redoubled efforts for an early victory in the struggle against American imperialism and the Kuomintang reactionaries. We hope that the youth of China and the Soviet Union will establish close ties in the future and fight together for lasting world peace and people's democracy.

                                  China Liberated Areas Youth Federation
                                            October 28th"

### 中国解放区青联电贺苏联少共三十周年

1948-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    中国解放区青联电贺苏联少共三十周年
    【新华社陕北三十日电】中国解放区青年联合会顷电贺苏联共产主义青年团三十周年纪念。电称：“苏联共产主义青年团米哈依洛夫，并转全体团员公鉴：
    我们代表中国青年庆贺苏联共产主义青年团三十周年纪念。我们将号召中国青年学习你们及苏联全体青年在保卫祖国战争与战后五年经济建设中的伟大的英勇斗争的精神，以百倍的努力，争取反对美帝国主义及国民党反动派的斗争早日胜利。我们希望今后中苏两国青年建立亲密的联系，共同为全世界的持久和平与人民民主而奋斗。
                                  中国解放区青年联合会
                                            十月二十八日”
