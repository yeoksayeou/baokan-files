Title: Japanese Communist Party Holds Central Enlarged Meeting to Determine Tasks in General Election
Author:
Date: 1948-11-04
Page: 2
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 1st. TASS Tokyo dispatch: The Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party held its fourth enlarged meeting on October 24th. General Secretary Tokuda delivered a report at the meeting outlining the main tasks of the Japanese Communist Party in the upcoming general election. The meeting unanimously adopted Tokuda's report. The JCP's newspaper, Akahata, in commenting on the outcome of the meeting, called on Japanese workers to strengthen the struggle for peace, freedom and national independence in the election campaign.

### 日共召开中央扩大会确定在大选中的任务

1948-11-04
第2版()
专栏：

    日共召开中央扩大会确定在大选中的任务
    【新华社陕北一日电】塔斯社东京讯：日共中央于十月二十四日举行第四次扩大会议。总书记德田在会上发表了概述日共在行将到来的大选中之主要任务的报告。会议一致通过了德田的报告。日共机关报赤旗报在评论会议的结果时，号召日本工人在竞选运动中加强为和平、自由及国家独立的斗争。
