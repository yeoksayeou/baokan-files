Title: North China Bureau and North China Government Send Congratulations on the Liberation of Baoding
Author:
Date: 1948-11-25
Page: 1

　　North China Bureau and North China Government
  　Send Congratulations on the Liberation of Baoding
    [Our Newspaper] The North China People's Government and the CCP Central Committee North China Bureau sent a telegram congratulating the liberation of Baoding. The text of the telegram is as follows: North China Military Region, to all commanders and fighters on the Baoding front:
    The good news has arrived, Baoding has been liberated! This has caused the disintegration of the Chiang-Fu bandit gang's pincers-like strategy on Pingjin and Baoding, creating even more favorable conditions for the liberation of Pingjin. It is hoped that you will continue your efforts and coordinate with other brotherly troops to fight for the liberation of Pingjin, the complete, clean, and thorough annihilation of the Chiang-Fu bandit army on the northern front, and the liberation of all of North China.
    　　　　　　　　　　North China People's Government
    　　　　　　　　　　North China Central Bureau

### 华北局暨华北政府　电贺保定解放

1948-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北局暨华北政府
  　电贺保定解放
    【本报讯】华北人民政府、中共中央华北局电贺保定解放，电文如下：华北军区转保定前线全体指战员同志：
    捷报传来，保定解放，使蒋傅匪帮平津保犄角之势顿告解体，创造了解放平津更为有利的条件。尚望继续努力，协同其他兄弟部队，为解放平津，全部、干净、彻底消灭北线蒋傅匪军、解放全华北而战。
    　　　　　　　　　　华北人民政府
    　　　　　　　　　　华北中央局
