Title: A Batch of Captured KMT Officers, Including Fan Hanjie, Arrive in Harbin
Author:
Date: 1948-11-11
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 9th: A group of Kuomintang military officers at the rank of general and above, captured by our forces during the liberation of Jinzhou, totaling 33 individuals including Fan Hanjie, arrived in Harbin on the 31st of last month. They have now been incorporated into the People's Liberation Army Officers' Training Regiment for study. Among them, in addition to Fan, who is a general, there are four lieutenant generals: He Kuixian, Deputy Commander of the Ji-Cha-Re Border Region Headquarters; Lu Junquan, Commander of the Sixth Army Corps; Yang Hongguang, Deputy Commander of the Sixth Army Corps; and Sheng Jiaxing, Commander of the 93rd Army. There are also 23 major generals and 5 colonels.

### 锦州一批俘官范汉杰等抵哈

1948-11-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　锦州一批俘官范汉杰等抵哈
    【新华社哈尔滨九日电】解放锦州战役中被我俘获之国民党军将校级军官一部计范汉杰以下三十三名，于上月三十一日到达哈市，现已编入解放军官教导团学习。其中除范为上将外，有中将四名，即冀察热边区司令部副司令官贺奎鲜、第六兵团司令官卢浚泉、副司令官杨宏光、九十三军军长盛家兴，少将二十三名，上校五名。
