Title: The People's Government of Rehe Enters Chengde to Conduct Official Business
Author:
Date: 1948-11-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chengde, 18th—With the liberation of the entire Rehe Province, the People's Government has entered the provincial capital of Chengde to conduct official business. The people of Chengde, who had been ravaged by the Kuomintang for more than two years, were overjoyed to see their own government again. Citizens have been accusing the bandit Chiang's army of atrocities. Before their disastrous escape, the Kuomintang army's 13th Corps engaged in a frantic looting, causing great suffering to the citizens.

### 热河人民政府　进入承德办公

1948-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　热河人民政府
  　进入承德办公
    【新华社承德十八日电】热河全省解放，人民政府已进入省会承德城办公。被国民党蹂躏两年余的承德人民，重见自己的政府，欢欣若狂。市民纷纷控诉蒋匪军的暴行。国民党军十三军在狼狈逃窜前，曾进行疯狂抢劫，市民受害甚大。
