Title: US Imperialism Intensifies Control over Chiang Kai-shek's Military: Reorganization of the Nanjing Military Advisory Group
Author:
Date: 1948-11-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 4th. In order to further strengthen its tight control over the military of the Kuomintang bandit Chiang Kai-shek, US imperialism has reorganized its military advisory group stationed in Nanjing. According to an announcement by the US Secretary of Defense on the 1st, the United States formally established a Joint China Military Advisory Group in Nanjing on November 1st to replace the original US Military Advisory Group, which was composed of the US Army Advisory Group and the Naval Advisory Group. The newly formed advisory group will include an Army Advisory Group, an Air Force Advisory Group, a Navy Advisory Group, and a Joint Logistics Advisory Group. General Barr, the former commander of the US Military Advisory Group, will continue to serve as the commander of the group and concurrently as the head of the Army Advisory Group, while Thomas (US Major), Old (US Rear Admiral), and Darrell (US Colonel) will serve as the heads of the Air Force, Navy, and Joint Logistics advisory groups, respectively.

### 美帝加紧控制蒋匪军事　改组南京军事顾问团

1948-11-06
第2版()
专栏：

　　美帝加紧控制蒋匪军事
  　改组南京军事顾问团
    【新华社陕北四日电】美帝国主义为进一步加强对国民党军事上的严密控制，已将其驻南京的军事顾问团改组。据美国防部长一日宣布：美国已于十一月一日在南京正式成立一联合驻华军事顾问团，以代替原来的由美陆军顾问团及海军顾问团合组之美军顾问团。新组成之顾问团将包括陆军顾问组、空军顾问组、海军顾问组及联勤顾问组。原美军顾问团团长巴大维继任该团团长并兼任陆军顾问组组长，汤玛士（美少校）、欧尔德（美海军少将）及达雷（美上校）则分别担任空、海及联勤各顾问组组长。
