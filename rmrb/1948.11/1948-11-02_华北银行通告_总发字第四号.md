North China Bank Notice
General Issue No. 4

In order to meet social needs, this bank has submitted a request to the North China People's Government for approval to issue a new type of 2,000 Yuan "Train" image banknote of the Jinnan Bank. This banknote is scheduled to be issued on the 25th of this month. The design is hereby announced as follows: The banknote measures 12.5 cm in length and 6 cm in width and is printed on white woodfree paper. The front has a dark green border, with the seal characters "贰千" (Two Thousand) printed in yellow undertones in the four corners. A black "Train Image" is in the center, with the seal characters "贰千圆" (Two Thousand Yuan) in the middle, horizontally printed above which is "冀南银行" (Jinnan Bank), and below which is "中华民国三十七年" (Republic of China Thirty-Seventh Year [1948]). The back is vegetable-colored with "BANK OF CHINA" printed horizontally at the top, "TWO THOUSAND YUAN" and other English words printed horizontally in the middle, and "1948" below. The numerical code "2000" is printed at both ends of the four corners and to the left and right of "1948."

This announcement is hereby made for general information.

General Manager Nan Hanchen
Deputy Manager Hu Jing@
            Guan Xuewen
October 21st, Thirty-Seventh Year of the Republic of China [1948]
