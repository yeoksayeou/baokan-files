Title: China Liberated Areas Youth Federation Sends Congratulatory Message to Korean Youth Congress
Author:
Date: 1948-11-12
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 4th. The Korean Democratic Youth League will convene the Korean Youth Congress on the 5th of this month. The China Liberated Areas Youth Federation has sent a congratulatory message. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To the Korean Democratic Youth League and forwarded to the Korean Youth Congress:

We, on behalf of the Chinese youth, extend warm congratulations to the Congress and pay our respects to the Korean youth and the Korean people who have long fought for national independence, democracy, and freedom. The youth of China and Korea once fought side by side and achieved victory in the struggle against Japanese imperialism, and today they are in a common struggle against US imperialism and its running dogs. May our unity be continuously strengthened, and we wish the Congress success.

　　　　China Liberated Areas Youth Federation
　　　　November 2nd

### 中国解放区青联电贺朝鲜青年代表大会

1948-11-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　中国解放区青联电贺朝鲜青年代表大会
    【新华社陕北四日电】朝鲜民主青年同盟将于本月五日召开朝鲜青年代表大会，中国解放区青年联合会顷电致贺。电文如下：朝鲜民主青年同盟并转朝鲜青年代表大会：
    我们谨代表中国青年向大会致热烈的庆贺，并向长期为民族独立民主自由奋斗的朝鲜青年和朝鲜人民致敬。中国和朝鲜的青年过去曾经在反对日本帝国主义的斗争中携手战斗并取得胜利，今天又处在反对美帝国主义及其走狗的共同斗争中。愿我们的团结不断加强，并祝大会的成功。
  　　　　中国解放区青年联合会　　十一月二日
