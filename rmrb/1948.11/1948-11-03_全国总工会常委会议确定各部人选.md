Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Standing Committee Meeting Determines Personnel for Various Departments
Author:
Date: 1948-11-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 1st. The Sixth Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions held its first Standing Committee meeting on October 22nd. The meeting determined the division of labor among the Standing Committee members and the personnel for various departments. In addition to Chen Yun, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Vice-Chairmen Li Lisan, Zhu Xuefan, and Liu Ningyi, who were elected by the first Executive Committee on October 10th, the Standing Committee determined the following division of labor and personnel for various departments: Secretary-General Li Jiebo, Minister of Organization Lin Qiangyun, Minister of Culture and Education Xu Zhizhen, Minister of Production Chen Yu, Minister of Labor Protection Tang Yunchao, Minister of Youth Workers Feng Wenbin, Minister of Women Workers Cai Chang, Minister of International Liaison Liu Ningyi (concurrent), Director of the Secretariat Peng Guangwei, Director of the Private Enterprise Committee Zhang Weizhen, Director of the Kuomintang-Controlled Areas Work Committee Zhu Xuefan (concurrent), Deputy Director Zhu Junxin, Committee Members Gao Lin, Ye Weimin, and Li Qixin. The Standing Committee also decided that the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Cultural and Educational Department of the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions would jointly compile a workers' publication, to be named "Northeast Worker," to be published in the supplement of the Northeast Daily.

### 全国总工会常委会议确定各部人选

1948-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    全国总工会常委会议确定各部人选
    【新华社哈尔滨一日电】中华全国总工会六届执委会于十月二十二日召开首次常委会议。确定常委分工及各部人选。除全总主席陈云、副主席李立三、朱学范、刘宁一已于十月十日首次执委会选出外，常委会确定分工及各部人选如下：秘书长李颉伯，组织部长林锵云，文教部长许之桢，生产部长陈郁，劳动保护部长唐韵超，青工部长冯文彬，女工部长蔡畅，国际联络部长刘宁一（兼），秘书处长彭光伟，私营企业委员会主任张维桢，国民党统治区工作委员会主任朱学范（兼）、副主任朱俊欣、委员高林、叶维民、李启新。常委会并决定由全总及东北职工总会文教部合编一工人刊物，定名为“东北工人”，在东北日报副刊发表。
