Title: International Briefs
Author:
Date: 1948-11-06
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency

　　International Briefs
    ▲ The governments of Albania and the Mongolian People's Republic have cabled the UN Secretary-General, Mr. Lie, once again requesting acceptance of both countries as members of the United Nations.
    ▲ A "Progressive National Liberation Movement" organization has recently been established in the Anglo-Egyptian Sudan in Africa. The purpose of this organization is to oppose British imperialism, especially the "Constitutional Assembly election" plan supported by Britain. Since its establishment, the organization has gained the support of the broad masses of the people, especially young students.
    ▲ The Philippine government, at the behest of its American masters, has declared the Communist Party of the Philippines illegal.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Xinhua News Agency)

### 国际简讯

1948-11-06
第2版()
专栏：

　　国际简讯
    ▲阿尔巴尼亚及蒙古人民共和国政府，顷致电联合国秘书长莱氏，再次要求接受两国为联合国会员国。
    ▲非洲英埃苏丹最近成立了一个“进步民族解放运动”组织。该组织目的在反对英帝国主义，特别是反对英国所支持的“立宪议会选举”的计划。该组织自成立以来，已获得广大人民特别是青年学生的拥护。
    ▲菲律宾政府奉其美国主子之令，已宣布菲律宾共产党为非法。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（新华社）
