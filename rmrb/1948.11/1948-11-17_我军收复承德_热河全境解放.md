Title: Our Army Recovers Chengde, All of Jehol Liberated
Author:
Date: 1948-11-17
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, 15th: Chengde, the capital of Jehol Province, was liberated on the 12th. The defending enemy abandoned the city and fled south at 12:00 noon on the same day, and we are in pursuit. Chengde was occupied by the Chiang bandits on August 29, 1946. With the recovery of the city, there are no more enemy traces in the entire Jehol territory.

### 我军收复承德　热河全境解放

1948-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　我军收复承德
  　热河全境解放
    【新华社东北前线十五日电】热河省会承德已于十二日解放。守敌于当日十二时弃城南逃，我正尾追中。承德系一九四六年八月二十九日为蒋匪侵占。该城收复后，热河全境已无敌踪。
