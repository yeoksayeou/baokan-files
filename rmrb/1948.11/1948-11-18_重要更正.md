Important Correction

　　In the "Full Text of Molotov's Report" on the third page of this newspaper on the 15th, in the section "Regarding Socialist Competition," the twenty-sixth line, "格. This decision helped to quickly eliminate currency circulation," should be moved to the previous section, "New Tasks of the National Economy," after the fourth line of the second paragraph, before the fifth line. We hereby make this correction.
