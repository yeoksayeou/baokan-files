Title: Baotou Military Control Commission Established
Author:
Date: 1948-11-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Suiyuan-Mongolia Front, 1st: (Delayed news) The Baotou Municipal Military Control Commission and military and political organs that entered the city with the army were formally established on the day of the city's liberation (October 23rd). Li Zhimin and Yang Zhenlin were appointed as Director and Deputy Director of the Military Control Commission respectively, Li Weizhong as Mayor, Zhao Kouying as Garrison Commander, and Pan Jiwen as Political Commissar.

### 包头军管会成立

1948-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    包头军管会成立
    【新华社绥蒙前线一日电】迟到消息：随军入城的包头市军事管理委员会暨军政机关于该市解放当日（十月二十三日）即正式成立。由李志民、杨震林分任军管会正副主任，李维中任市长，赵寇英任卫戍司令，潘纪文任政治委员。
