Title: More Than 6,000 Shanghai Primary and Secondary School Teachers Go on Strike
Author:
Date: 1948-11-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 24th. TASS Shanghai reported: Following the strike by more than 1,000 faculty and staff members of twelve municipal middle schools on the 16th, 5,000 primary school teachers in the city also went on strike on the 19th, demanding increased salaries and food rations. TASS also stated: The strike movement in Shanghai is intensifying.

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 20th. The anti-hunger strike movement in the education sector in the Kuomintang-controlled areas has spread from cities such as Peiping, Tianjin, Tangshan, Qingdao, Chengdu, and Nanjing to Shanghai. According to a United Press report on the 16th, 1,300 faculty and staff members from 32 municipal middle schools in Shanghai have taken "collective leave" to demand improved treatment. As a result, more than 20,000 students have been suspended from classes. According to Tianjin newspapers, the anti-hunger movement in the education sector in Peiping and Tianjin is still expanding. Teachers from 234 municipal primary schools in Tianjin began a one-week general strike on the 1st of this month, and teachers from 16 municipal middle schools decided to continue their strike for three days starting from October 31st. In Peiping, the Academy of Arts, senior vocational schools, and the Railway Management College have also joined the strike and class boycott.

### 上海中小学教员　六千多人罢教

1948-11-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　上海中小学教员
  　六千多人罢教
    【新华社陕北二十四日电】据塔斯社上海消息：继十二所市立中学千余教职员于十六日罢教后，该市小学教员五千人亦于十九日罢教，要求增加薪水及粮食配给。塔斯社并称：上海罢工运动正激剧高涨。
    【新华社陕北二十日电】国民党统治区教育界反饥饿罢教运动，已由平、津、唐山、青岛、成都、南京等城市扩展至上海。据合众社十六日电报导：上海三十二所市立中学一千三百名教职员已“集体请假”，要求改善待遇。因而停课的学生达二万余人。另据天津报纸披露：平、津教育界反饥饿仍在扩大中，天津二百三十四所市立小学教员，本月一日起总罢教一周，十六所市立中学教员决定从十月三十一日起继续罢教三日。北平方面续有艺专、高级职业学校、铁路管理学院三校卷入罢教、罢课。
