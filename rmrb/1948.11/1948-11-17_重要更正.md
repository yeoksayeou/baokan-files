　　Important Correction
    In the "Full Text of Molotov's Report" on the third page of this newspaper on the 15th, in the section "Regarding Socialist Competition," line 26, "Ger. This decision helped to quickly eliminate the amount of currency in circulation" should be moved to the previous section, "New Tasks of the National Economy," after the fourth line and before the fifth line of the second paragraph. We hereby make this correction.
