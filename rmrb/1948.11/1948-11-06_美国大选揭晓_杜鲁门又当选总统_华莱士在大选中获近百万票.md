Title: US Election Results Announced: Truman Re-elected President; Wallace Receives Nearly One Million Votes in the Election
Author:
Date: 1948-11-06
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 4th. New York dispatch on the 3rd: The result of the US election on the 2nd of this month is that the Democratic Party's Truman has been re-elected as president. The Democratic Party has already gained a majority in both the Senate and the House of Representatives. According to incomplete statistics, the Progressive Party's Wallace received over 900,000 votes in this election.

### 美国大选揭晓　杜鲁门又当选总统　华莱士在大选中获近百万票

1948-11-06
第2版()
专栏：

　　美国大选揭晓　杜鲁门又当选总统　华莱士在大选中获近百万票
    【新华社陕北四日电】据三日纽约电讯：本月二日美国大选结果，民主党的杜鲁门继续当选总统。民主党已获得参众两院的多数。据不完全统计，进步党的华莱士在此次选举中获得九十余万票。
