Title: Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu Send Telegram Congratulating the Soviet Union on the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1948-11-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 5th. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army have sent a telegram to Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Stalin, congratulating the Soviet Union on the 31st anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution. The telegram reads as follows:

Marshal Stalin:

On the occasion of the 31st anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, I respectfully wish the Soviet Union, the bastion of world peace and democracy, increasing strength and the close cooperation between the people of China and the Soviet Union.

Mao Zedong, Zhu De
November 5, 1948

### 毛主席朱总司令　电贺苏联十月革命节

1948-11-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　毛主席朱总司令
  　电贺苏联十月革命节
    【新华社陕北五日电】中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东、中国人民解放军总司令朱德，顷致电苏联部长会议主席斯大林，祝贺苏联十月社会主义革命三十一周年。电文如下：斯大林大元帅：
    当此伟大的十月社会主义革命三十一周年纪念的时候，敬祝世界和平民主堡垒苏联的日益强盛，中苏两国人民的亲密合作。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　毛泽东　朱德
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四八年十一月五日
