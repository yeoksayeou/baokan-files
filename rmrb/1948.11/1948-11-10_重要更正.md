Important Corrections

　　The following corrections are made to the article "On Internationalism and Nationalism" published in this newspaper on November 7th:
    
　　On the first page, in the first paragraph, tenth line, add a period after "同时" (tóngshí, at the same time).
    
　　In the first section, "The Bourgeois Nationalist View of the Nation," in the twenty-second line, change the comma after "服役" (fúyì, service) to a、 (enumeration comma). In the thirtieth line, add a comma after "利益" (lìyì, interests). In the second to last line of the same section, change "这种资产阶级民族主义的民族观" (zhè zhǒng zīchǎn jiējí mínzú zhǔyì de mínzú guān, this bourgeois nationalist view of the nation) to "这种资产阶级的民族主义，即民族观" (zhè zhǒng zīchǎn jiējí de mínzú zhǔyì, jí mínzú guān, this bourgeois nationalism, i.e., the view of the nation).
    
　　In the second section, "The Proletarian Internationalist View of the Nation," in the thirtieth line, add a comma after "所以" (suǒyǐ, therefore). In the fifty-ninth line, add a comma after "结果" (jiéguǒ, result). On the second page, in the first line of the same section, add a comma after "所以" (suǒyǐ, therefore). In the twenty-second line, change the period after "解放自己" (jiěfàng zìjǐ, liberate oneself) to a comma. In the twenty-fifth line, add a comma after "所以" (suǒyǐ, therefore). In the fifty-eighth line, change "在一定历史条件之下" (zài yīdìng lìshǐ tiáojiàn zhī xià, under certain historical conditions) to "在一定的历史条件下" (zài yīdìng de lìshǐ tiáojiàn xià, under certain historical conditions). In the eighty-fourth line, add a comma after "叛徒们" (pàntúmen, traitors). In the one hundred and eighth line, change the period after "出来" (chūlái, come out) to a comma. In the eleventh to last line of the same section, add the sentence "甚至认为‘资本主义国家对南斯拉夫的危险比苏联对南斯拉夫的危险还要少一些’" (shènzhì rènwéi 'zīběn zhǔyì guójiā duì Nánsīlāfū de wēixiǎn bǐ Sūlián duì Nánsīlāfū de wēixiǎn hái yào shǎo yīxiē', even believing that "the danger of capitalist countries to Yugoslavia is less than the danger of the Soviet Union to Yugoslavia,") before "如铁托" (rú Tiětuō, like Tito).
    
　　In the third section, "The Current Situation of Oppressor Nations and Oppressed Nations in the World," in the thirtieth line, "国各" (guó gè) should be "各国" (gèguó, various countries). In the fifty-second line, the character "国" (guó, country) should be moved below the character "祖" (zǔ, ancestor/fatherland).
    
　　Also, the maps of the Liaoxi area published in this newspaper in the past were drawn according to old maps, and the location of Jinxi was incorrect. Now Jinxi is on the railway line between Jinzhou and Xingcheng, with a railway branch line connecting to Huludao. The location of Jinxi on the old maps is now Jiangjiatun.
    
　　Also, in the article "Military Region Headquarters Issues Communique" on the first page of this newspaper on November 9th, change the 、 (enumeration comma) after "保定" (Bǎodìng) in the fifth line to a comma, and add a 、 (enumeration comma) after "固城" (Gùchéng).
    
　　Also, in the last question of "Questions and Answers" on the second page of this newspaper on November 8th, "方针" (fāngzhēn, policy) should be changed to "方式" (fāngshì, method).

### 重要更正

1948-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　重要更正
    十一月七日本报所载“论国际主义与民族主义”一文有如下更正：
    第一版该文首段第十行“同时”下应加一“点”号。
    第一节“资产阶级民族主义的民族观”第二十二行“服役”下之“，”号应改为“、”号。第三十行“利益”下应加一“，”号。同节倒数第二行“这种资产阶级民族主义的民族观”应改为“这种资产阶级的民族主义，即民族观”。
    第二节“无产阶级国际主义的民族观”第三十行“所以”下应加一“，”号。第五十九行“结果”下应加一“，”号。第二版同节第一行“所以”下应加一“，”号。第廿二行“解放自己”下之“。”号应改为“，”号。第二十五行“所以”下应加一“，”号。第五十八行“在一定历史条件之下”应改为“在一定的历史条件下”。第八十四行“叛徒们”下应加一“，”号。第一○八行“出来”下之“。”号应改为“，”号。同节倒数第十一行“如铁托”上应加“甚至认为‘资本主义国家对南斯拉夫的危险比苏联对南斯拉夫的危险还要少一些’，”一句。
    第三节“世界压迫民族与被压迫民族的现状”第三十行“国各”应为“各国”。第五十二行“国”字应改在“祖”字之下。
    又，本报历次所载辽西一带地图，是根据旧地图画的，锦西的位置都弄错了。现在锦西是在锦州与城之间的铁路线上，有铁路支线和葫芦岛相通。旧地图上锦西的位置，现在是江家屯。
    又，十一月九日本报第一版“军区司令部发表公报”文中第五行“保定”下之“、”号应改为“，”号，“固城”下应加一“、”号。
    又，十一月八日第二版“询问与答复”最后一个题目中“方针”应改为“方式”。
