Title: The Entire Northeast Forever Belongs to the People! 200,000 People Gather in Harbin to Celebrate; Comrade Chen Yun Appointed Director of the Shenyang Military Control Commission
Author:
Date: 1948-11-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 8th: The capital of Northeast China, Harbin, held a rally of 200,000 people to warmly celebrate the liberation of the entire Northeast. On the morning of the 4th, this grand celebration was solemnly held after twelve cannons fired a total of 360 salvos. Afterwards, a grand parade was held inside and outside the city. The evening lantern festival was unprecedentedly grand. The entire city was filled with the sound of drums and music, and the lanterns were brilliantly lit, as bright as day. Speaking at the celebration meeting were Lin Feng, Chairman of the Northeast Administrative Committee; Gao Chongmin, Vice Chairman; Zhou Huan, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Northeast Military Region; Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Zhang Pinghua, Secretary of the Harbin Municipal Committee; and Rao Bin, Mayor of Harbin. Chairman Lin Feng said at the celebration meeting: "From now on, the entire Northeast will forever belong to the people of the Northeast! In the future, the people of the entire Northeast must continue to strengthen the People's Liberation Army, step up production, to support the national liberation war, completely eliminate the Kuomintang army, drive the American imperialists out of China, and build a new China, a new Northeast, for the people." Zhou Huan, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Northeast Military Region, reported in his speech the glorious achievements of the Northeast People's Liberation Army in the past 52 days: annihilating more than 400,000 enemies, with 64 enemy generals already sent to Harbin, including more than ten lieutenant generals. Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, called on the workers of Northeast China to build the Northeast into a large arsenal to support the national liberation war, step up the manufacture of more artillery, machine guns, and shells, and continuously supply the Liberation Army within the Great Wall, to fight to Nanjing, to fight to Guangzhou. The meeting passed a proposal to the government to designate November 2nd as "Northeast Victory Day", and also passed a congratulatory telegram to the soldiers at the front.
    
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 8th: After the liberation of Shenyang, the largest city in Northeast China, revolutionary order is good. All public buildings and various enterprise assets are protected. The Military Control Commission has been established, with Chen Yun, one of the heads of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, as the Director, and Wang Xiuquan, Chief of Staff of the Northeast Military Region, and Tao Zhu, Secretary of the Liaobei Provincial Committee of the CPC, as Deputy Directors. On the second day after the liberation of Shenyang (November 3), the Military Control Commission issued a notice stipulating that the original staff of all agencies should continue to work as usual in their original positions, and be responsible for the safekeeping of all assets and equipment, and submit a list to the Special Municipal Government and relevant agencies within three days. The Shenyang Garrison Command has also been established. The city defense troops are strictly disciplined and deeply supported by the people. Various recovery work is actively underway.

### 整个东北永远是人民的了！哈市二十万人集会庆祝  陈云同志任沈阳军管会主任

1948-11-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　整个东北永远是人民的了！
  　哈市二十万人集会庆祝           
    陈云同志任沈阳军管会主任
    【新华社哈尔滨八日电】东北首府哈尔滨市二十万人集会，热烈庆祝全东北解放。四日上午，此一盛大的庆祝会在十二门大炮共鸣三百六十响之后隆重举行，会后继在市内外举行盛大游行。入晚灯会盛况空前。全市鼓乐喧天，灯彩辉煌，通明如昼。庆祝大会上致词的有东北行政委员会主席林枫，副主席高崇民，东北军区政治部副主任周桓，中华全国总工会副主席朱学范，哈市市委书记张平化，市长饶斌等。林枫主席在庆祝大会上称：“从此以后，整个东北永远是东北人民的了！今后全东北人民更要继续壮大人民解放军，加紧生产，以支援全国解放战争，全部消灭国民党军队，驱逐美帝国主义出中国，建设人民的新中国、新东北。”东北军区政治部副主任周桓在讲话中报告了过去五十二天内东北人民解放军的辉煌战绩：歼敌四十余万，已送到哈市的敌将级军官有六十四名，其中中将十余名。全总副主席朱学范号召东北职工把东北建设为支援全国解放战争的大兵工厂，加紧制造更多的大炮、机枪、炮弹，源源供应关内解放军，打到南京去，打到广州去。大会通过向政府提议规定十一月二日为“东北胜利节”，并通过致前线将士贺电。
    【新华社沈阳八日电】东北第一大城沈阳市解放后，革命秩序良好。所有公共建筑及各种企业资财，均受到保护。军事管制委员会已成立，由中共东北中央局负责人之一的陈云为主任，东北军区参谋长王修权、中共辽北省委书记陶铸为副主任。沈市解放后的第二日（十一月三日），军管会即发出布告，规定原有各机关工作人员，各按原职照常办公，并负责保管一切资财、设备，限于三日内造册，呈报特别市政府及各有关机关。沈市卫戍司令部亦已成立。城防部队纪律严明，深得人民拥护。各项恢复工作正积极进行中。
