　　Important Correction
    In the People's Daily's third page of the fifteenth of this month, in the full text of "Molotov's Report," in the section "Concerning Socialist Competition," in the twenty-sixth line, "Ge. This decision helped to rapidly eliminate the amount of currency in circulation" should be moved to the previous section, "New Tasks of the National Economy," after the fourth line of the second paragraph and before the fifth line. We hereby make this correction.
