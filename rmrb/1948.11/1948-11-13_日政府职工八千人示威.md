Title: 8,000 Japanese Government Employees Demonstrate
Author:
Date: 1948-11-13
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, November 11th: Tokyo Dispatch: Eight thousand Japanese government employees, defying the anti-strike laws and the threat of severe punishment for absenteeism made by the Yoshida Cabinet on the 6th, bravely held a demonstration parade on the 8th, as decided by the Government Employees Union. Before the parade, the fifteen participating unions passed a resolution demanding the resignation of the reactionary Yoshida Cabinet, the dissolution of the parliament, and opposition to the revision of the Civil Service Law or the Labor Law.

### 日政府职工八千人示威

1948-11-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　日政府职工八千人示威
    【新华社陕北十一日电】东京讯：日本政府职工八千人，不顾禁止罢工法令以及吉田内阁在六日所作的严惩旷工者的威胁，英勇地按照政府职工工会决定于八日举行示威游行。参加示威的十五个工会于游行前通过决议，要求反动的吉田内阁辞职，解散议会，反对修改公务员法或劳工法。
