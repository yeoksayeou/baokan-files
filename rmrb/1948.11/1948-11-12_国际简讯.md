　　International Briefs
    ▲ The strike by longshoremen and sailors on the US West Coast has entered its eighth week. As of October 16th, 222 ships had been idled due to the strike. According to the capitalists, if the strike continues, the number of idled ships will reach 375.
    ▲ Cuban Communist leader, Dockworkers' Union Chairman, and Congressman, Iglesias, died in Havana on October 18th after being assassinated by reactionaries.

### 国际简讯

1948-11-12
第2版()
专栏：国际简讯

　　国际简讯
    ▲美国西海岸码头工人及海员的罢工已进入第八周。迄十月十六日止，因罢工而停驶的船只已达二百二十二艘。据资方称，如罢工继续下去，则停驶船只将达三百七十五艘。
    ▲古巴共产党领袖、码头工会主席、国会议员伊格里西亚，于十月十八日在哈瓦那被反动派刺杀后逝世。
