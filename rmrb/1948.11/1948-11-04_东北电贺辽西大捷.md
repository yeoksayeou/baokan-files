Title: Northeast Sends Telegram to Congratulate the Liaoxi Victory
Author:
Date: 1948-11-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 2nd: The Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Northeast Administrative Committee, and the Northeast Military Region have jointly sent a telegram congratulating the Liaoxi Victory, stating: We are delighted to hear that our army has once again achieved a brilliant victory in the Liaoxi battlefield, completely annihilating five armies and twelve divisions of the Chiang bandit main force. The good news has arrived, and cheers are resounding. The party, government, army, and people in the rear are expressing their highest respect, warm congratulations, and condolences to our heroic and good-at-war People's Liberation Army with unprecedented excitement and joy, and are determined to give full support to the front lines. The victory in the Liaoxi battlefield is the most glorious and decisive victory among the countless victories of our army in the Northeast. Comrades, the day of the final victory in the Northeast People's Liberation War has arrived! Advance victoriously! Swiftly, cleanly, thoroughly, and completely eliminate the remaining Chiang bandit troops in the Northeast, fight for the liberation of Shenyang! Glory to the people's heroes who sacrificed for the liberation war!

### 东北电贺辽西大捷

1948-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    东北电贺辽西大捷
    【新华社哈尔滨二日电】中共中央东北局、东北行政委员会、东北军区顷联电祝贺辽西大捷，内称：欣闻我军在辽西战场又一次取得了全歼蒋匪主力五个军十二师的辉煌战绩。捷报传来，欢声雷动。后方党政军民均以空前的兴奋与喜悦，对我英勇善战的人民解放军致以崇高的敬意及热烈的祝贺与慰问，并决以全力支援前线。辽西战场的胜利是东北我军无数次胜利中最光辉、最有决定意义的胜利。同志们，东北人民解放战争的最后胜利日子已经到来了！胜利前进！迅速、干净、彻底、全部消灭东北残余的蒋匪军，为解放沈阳而战！为解放战争而牺牲的人民英雄光荣不朽。
