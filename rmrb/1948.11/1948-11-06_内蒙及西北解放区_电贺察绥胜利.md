Title: Inner Mongolia and Northwest Liberated Areas Send Telegrams Congratulating on Victory in Chahar and Suiyuan
Author:
Date: 1948-11-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 4th. The Inner Mongolia and Northwest Liberated Areas recently sent telegrams congratulating the Second and Third Corps of the North China People's Liberation Army on liberating vast areas of Chahar and Suiyuan, annihilating over 16,000 of Fu Zuoyi's bandit troops. The Inner Mongolia Communist Party Working Committee, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Government, and the Political Department of the Inner Mongolia People's Liberation Army Command, in a joint congratulatory telegram, stated: "Generals Yang Dezhi, Luo Ruiqing, Yang Chengwu, Jing Quan, and all comrades of the North China, Jin-Sui, and Hebei-Rehe-Chahar People's Liberation Army: We extend our highest respect and congratulate you on annihilating over 10,000 enemies within a month since the beginning of the autumn offensive, liberating the famous city of Baotou and fifteen other cities in Chabei and Suimeng, controlling most of the Ping-Sui Railway, isolating Gui Sui and Datong, and liberating the vast Mongol and Han people who have long suffered under the brutal rule of Fu's bandits. Our Inner Mongolia People's Liberation Army will learn from your example, coordinate operations, and strive to completely eradicate the heinous rule of Great Han chauvinists over the Mongolian people and liberate all of Inner Mongolia." The Northwest congratulatory telegram stated: "This victory is a powerful coordination for our Northwest People's Liberation War."

### 内蒙及西北解放区　电贺察绥胜利

1948-11-06
第2版()
专栏：

　　内蒙及西北解放区
  　电贺察绥胜利
    【新华社陕北四日电】内蒙及西北解放区顷电贺华北解放军二、三兵团解放察、绥广大地区，歼灭傅作义匪军一万六千余的胜利。中共内蒙古共产党工作委员会、内蒙古自治政府及内蒙古人民解放军司令部政治部，在联合贺电中称：“杨德志、罗瑞卿、杨成武、井泉诸将军及华北、晋绥、冀热察人民解放军全体同志们：我们谨以崇高的敬意，庆祝你们秋季攻势开始后一个月内歼敌万余，解放名城包头及察北、绥蒙其他城市十五座，控制平绥路大部，孤立归绥、大同，使久苦于傅匪残暴统治下的蒙汉广大人民获得解放的伟大胜利。我内蒙人民解放军当学习你们的榜样，配合作战，为彻底根绝大汉族主义者对蒙古人民的万恶统治，解放全内蒙而奋斗。”西北贺电称：“这一胜利对我西北人民解放战争是一有力的配合。”
