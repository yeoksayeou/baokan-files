Title: Kuomintang Second Pacification Zone Lieutenant General Deputy Commander Mu Zhongheng Captured; Over 4,000 Enemies Annihilated in Yingcheng Battle in Central Hubei
Author:
Date: 1948-11-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, East China, 18th: Mu Zhongheng, Lieutenant General and Deputy Commander of the Kuomintang Second Pacification Zone, has been captured. After the liberation of Jinan, Mu disguised himself and hid, then slipped out of the city, attempting to escape to Qingdao. He was apprehended by our forces in the Gaomi area of the Jiaoji Railway's western section. Mu Zhongheng is a native of Shandong, graduated from the Dingjun Military Academy, and was formerly part of the Northeast Army under Yu Xuezhong's 51st Army, serving as the commander of the 114th Division. In 1941, he served as a member of the Kuomintang Shandong Provincial Government, later succeeding Shen Honglie as the puppet Shandong Province Chairman, a post he left in 1944.

Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, 18th: The results of the Central Plains People's Liberation Army's recapture of Yingcheng, a key town in central Hubei, on October 26th are as follows:

　　A total of 4,374 enemy troops were annihilated, including the 28th Army's direct units and the entire 52nd Regiment of the 49th Division. Among them, 3,805 enemy officers and soldiers were captured alive, including Gu Xinheng, Deputy Commander of the enemy's 28th Army (previously mistaken for Guo Xianfang), Zhang Pinzhuang, Lieutenant Colonel Health Captain of the Army Headquarters, and Li Detai, Major Battalion Commander. 569 enemy soldiers were killed or wounded, including Yin Dongpan, the commander of the 52nd Regiment. The captured war material included one mountain gun, four mortars and 60mm guns, 100 light and heavy machine guns, over 1,600 rifles and other military supplies.

### 国民党二绥区中将副司令　牟中珩被我捕获  鄂中应城之役共歼敌四千余

1948-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　国民党二绥区中将副司令
  　牟中珩被我捕获
    鄂中应城之役共歼敌四千余
    【新华社华东十八日电】国民党第二绥区中将副司令牟中珩已被捕获。牟于济南解放后，化装躲藏，随后即混出城外，企图逃往青岛，在胶济西段高密地区被我查获。牟中珩系山东人，在定军校毕业，原东北军旧部，西安事变前任五十一军于学忠部一一四师师长，一九四一年任国民党山东省政府委员，后继沈鸿烈任伪山东省主席，一九四四年去职。
    【新华社中原十八日电】中原解放军上月二十六日收复鄂中重镇应城战果如下：
    歼灭敌军第二十八军军直属队及四十九师之五十二团全部共四千三百七十四人，其中生俘敌第二十八军副军长顾心衡（前误为郭先方），军部中校卫生队长张品庄、少校营长李德太等以下官兵三千八百零五名，毙伤敌五十二团团长尹东潘以下五百六十九人。缴获山炮一门，迫击炮、六零炮各四门，轻重机枪一百挺，长短枪一千六百余支，其他军用品甚多。
