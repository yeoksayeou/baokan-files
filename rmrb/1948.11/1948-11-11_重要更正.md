Title: Important Correction
Author:
Date: 1948-11-11
Page: 1

　　Important Correction
    Yesterday, in the first title on the first page of some copies of this newspaper, the typesetter carelessly changed "职工运动" (workers' movement) to "职工动运". We hereby issue this correction.
