　　Should Family Members of Revolutionary Staff Members Farm for Others as a Substitute?
    Question: Family members of revolutionary staff members generally do not enjoy the treatment of having others farm for them, but should they still farm for the families of military martyrs and dependents of military personnel? There are now more than two hundred households in our village, of which more than sixty are families of military cadres and martyrs. My family now has four members, and my father is able to work. Apart from business trips, the village cadres also assign him to farm for others as a substitute, so the farm work at home cannot be taken care of. May I ask whether we should farm for others as a substitute in this case?

### 革命职员家属该不该给别人代耕？
杨世民
1948-11-06
第2版()
专栏：询问与答覆

　　革命职员家属该不该给别人代耕？
    问：革命职员家属一般不享受代耕待遇，但自己是否还要给别家军烈属代耕？我村现在有二百多户，其中军干烈属就占六十多户，我家现有人四口，我父亲能劳动，除了出差外，村干部还派他给人代耕，家中的农事无法照管，请问这样是否应给别人代耕？　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　华北党校杨世民
    答：革命职员家属有多余的劳动力，也要给别的家属代耕，因为代耕是按劳力算的，凡村中所有全半劳动力都应负代耕义务。你父亲是否应再给别人代耕，要看其余自耕外是否还有多余的劳动力，由群众讨论而具体决定。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　——民政部
