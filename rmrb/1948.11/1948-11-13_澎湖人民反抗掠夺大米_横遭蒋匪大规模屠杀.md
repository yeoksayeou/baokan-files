Title: Penghu People Resist Grain Plunder, Suffer Large-Scale Massacre by Chiang Kai-shek Bandits
Author:
Date: 1948-11-13
Page: 2

　　Penghu People Resist Grain Plunder
  　Suffer Large-Scale Massacre by Chiang Kai-shek Bandits
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, November 9th: According to a TASS dispatch from Nanjing cited by the Hong Kong Hua Shang Bao, Kuomintang troops on October 19th conducted a large-scale massacre in Magong City, the capital of the Penghu Islands, against residents resisting the Kuomintang government's plunder of rice on the island, and used aircraft for bombing and strafing. According to known information, more than 2,000 citizens were killed or wounded. Note: The Penghu Islands are scattered islands between Fujian and Taiwan, totaling forty-seven islands of various sizes. They were seized from the Qing Dynasty by Japanese imperialism during the Sino-Japanese War, and after the end of the Anti-Japanese War, they were returned to the rule of the Kuomintang bandit gang.

### 澎湖人民反抗掠夺大米　横遭蒋匪大规模屠杀

1948-11-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　澎湖人民反抗掠夺大米
  　横遭蒋匪大规模屠杀
    【新华社陕北九日电】据塔斯社南京电引香港华商报消息：国民党军队于十月十九日大规模屠杀澎湖列岛首府马公城反抗国民党政府在该岛掠夺大米的居民，并使用飞机轰炸扫射。据已知消息，市民死伤达二千余人。按：澎湖列岛系散布于福建与台湾间的岛屿，大小共四十七岛。甲午之战被日本帝国主义从满清手中夺去，抗日战争结束后复为国民党匪帮统治。
