Title: Jianghan Our Army Liberates Yingcheng and Anlu, Annihilating an Army Headquarters and a Regiment of the Enemy
Author:
Date: 1948-11-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jianghan, 29th. A certain unit of the Jianghan People's Liberation Army launched an attack on the Kuomintang bandit army's 28th Army headquarters and the New 52nd Regiment of the 49th Division, stationed about 100 li northwest of Wuhan, on the night of the 24th of this month. The battle ended at 7:00 PM on the 25th, completely annihilating the defending enemy. Over 3,000 enemy troops were captured alive, including the enemy's 28th Army Deputy Commander Guo Xiancai, completely liberating Yingcheng County.

In addition, another unit of the Liberation Army, coordinating with the Yingcheng operation, re-captured Anlu County on the 26th. The defending enemy fled south.

### 江汉我军解放应城安陆  歼敌一个军部一个团

1948-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    江汉我军解放应城安陆
    歼敌一个军部一个团
    【新华社江汉二十九日电】江汉人民解放军某部，本月二十四日夜向驻守武汉西北约百余里之应城国民党匪军二十八军军部及四九师之新五十二团发动攻击，于二十五日下午七时结束战斗，将守敌全部歼灭，生俘敌二十八军副军长郭先才以下三千余名，完全解放应城县。
    又配合应城作战之另部解放军于二十六日再度收复安陆县城。守敌南逃。
