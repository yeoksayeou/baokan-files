Title: CCP Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram on the Recovery of Chengde
Author:
Date: 1948-11-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 16th. The CCP Central Committee recently sent a telegram congratulating the recovery of Chengde. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To Comrades Cheng Zihua and Huang Kecheng, and all comrades of the Jicha Rehe Liaoning People's Liberation Army:

We celebrate your great victory in recovering Chengde and liberating the entire territory of Rehe.

		The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, November 16, 1948

### 中共中央电贺收复承德

1948-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央电贺收复承德
    【新华社陕北十六日电】中共中央顷电贺收复承德。电文如下：
    程子华、黄克诚两同志及冀察热辽人民解放军全体同志们：
    庆祝你们收复承德解放热河全境的大胜利。
    　　　　　　　　　中共中央委员会　一九四八年十一月十六日
