Title: Enemy Henan Security Third Regiment Commander Wang Yeran Leads Uprising with His Troops
Author:
Date: 1948-11-28
Page: 1

[Ji Lu Yu Electric Message] Late news: During the liberation of Zhengzhou and Kaifeng by our Central Plains Army, the entire Kuomintang Henan Security Third Regiment, totaling over 700 people, led by Regiment Commander Wang Yeran and Deputy Regiment Commander Zhang Jinjian, gloriously revolted near Fengqiu City on October 23rd, bringing with them 16 light machine guns, over 200 rifles, and over 5,000 rounds of ammunition.

### 敌河南保安第三团团长　王也然率部起义

1948-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　敌河南保安第三团团长
  　王也然率部起义
    【冀鲁豫电】迟到消息：在我中原大军解放郑州、开封之际，国民党河南省保安第三团全部共七百余人，在团长王也然、副团长张金鉴率领下，于十月二十三日在封邱城附近光荣起义，并带来轻机枪十六挺，步枪二百余支，子弹五千余发。
