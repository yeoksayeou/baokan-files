Title: Austrian Communist Party Holds Fourteenth Congress, Elects New Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1948-11-12
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 8th.  Electric News Agency, Vienna, reports: The Fourteenth Congress of the Austrian Communist Party (ACP) opened on October 29th, on the occasion of the party's thirtieth anniversary. The congress concluded on the 2nd of this month after electing a new Central Committee. Koplenig remains Chairman, and Fürnberg is General Secretary. The Austrian Communist Party has now become a mass party. Last year, the party had only 80,000 members, but now it has 160,000, that is, one communist for every forty people in Austria. The resolution of the congress declared that the Marshall Plan is the main weapon of American imperialism in Europe. The resolution pointed out the need to fight against Pan-Germanism, Nazism, and anti-Slavic tendencies. Brother parties from fourteen European countries sent representatives to attend the Austrian Communist Party Congress. The telegram of congratulations from the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party stated: "The Austrian Communist Party is the vanguard of Austrian democracy."

### 奥共举行十四届大会选出新的中央委员会

1948-11-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　奥共举行十四届大会选出新的中央委员会
    【新华社陕北八日电】电通社维也纳讯：奥地利共产党第十四届大会在该党成立三十周年之际于十月二十九日开幕。大会在选出新的中央委员会后，于本月二日闭幕。主席仍由科布仑尼格担任，总书记为弗伦堡。奥共现在已成为一群众性的党，在去年，该党尚只有党员八万人，现在则已有十六万人，即在奥地利每四十人中，有共产党员一人。大会的决议宣称，马歇尔计划是美帝国主义在欧洲的主要武器。决议指出需要对泛日耳曼主义、纳粹主义及反斯拉夫倾向作斗争。欧洲有十四个国家的兄弟党都派有代表参加奥共大会，联共中央的贺电称：“奥地利共产党是奥国民主的先锋”。
