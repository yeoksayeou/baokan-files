Title: CCP Central Committee Sends Telegram of Congratulations on the Complete Liberation of Northeast China: Shattering the American-Chiang Dream of Using Northeast China to Provoke International War; Laying a Solid Foundation for Liberating All of China and Building a New China
Author:
Date: 1948-11-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 3rd: The CCP Central Committee sent a telegram of congratulations on the liberation of Shenyang and the complete liberation of Northeast China. The telegram stated:
    Comrades Lin Biao, Luo Ronghuan, Gao Gang, and Chen Yun, all comrades of the Northeast People's Liberation Army, and all compatriots in the Northeast:

    We warmly celebrate your liberation of Shenyang, the complete annihilation of the defending enemy, and thus the completion of the great victory of liberating the entire Northeast. The Northeast is China's largest industrial center, especially for heavy industry. Since the winter of 1945, the Kuomintang reactionary government, with the active assistance of American imperialism, has used great force to seize the Northeast, successively deploying troops and incorporating puppet troops and bandits totaling 1.1 million men. Relying on the unity and heroic struggle of all soldiers and civilians in the Northeast's front and rear, and with the victorious coordination of our liberated areas within the passes, we annihilated more than one million enemies in three years of fighting, finally liberating all nine provinces of the Northeast and 37 million compatriots, shattering the American-Chiang reactionaries' dream of enslaving the people of the Northeast and using the Northeast to provoke international war, and laying a solid foundation for liberating all of China within a few years and then gradually building China into an industrialized nation. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China expresses its gratitude and respect to all soldiers and civilians in the Northeast. We hope that you will continue to work hard, closely cooperate with the people within the passes and the People's Liberation Army in various regions, and advance shoulder to shoulder to completely overthrow the rule of the Kuomintang reactionaries, expel the aggressive forces of American imperialism in China, and fight for the liberation of all of China! Eternal glory to the heroes who sacrificed in the Northeast Liberation War!
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　  November 3, 1948

### 中共中央电贺东北完全解放  粉碎了美蒋利用东北挑拨国际战争的迷梦  奠定了解放全中国建设新中国的巩固基础

1948-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央电贺东北完全解放
    粉碎了美蒋利用东北挑拨国际战争的迷梦
    奠定了解放全中国建设新中国的巩固基础
    【新华社陕北三日电】中共中央电贺沈阳的解放和全东北的解放。电称：林彪、罗荣桓、高岗、陈云诸同志，东北人民解放军全体同志和东北全体同胞们：
    热烈庆祝你们解放沈阳，全歼守敌，并从而完成解放东北全境的伟大胜利。东北是中国工业特别是重工业最大的中心，国民党反动政府在美国帝国主义积极援助下，从一九四五年冬季以来就曾经用极大力量来抢占东北，先后投入兵力及收编伪军胡匪共达一百一十万人。依靠我东北前后方全体军民团结一致，英勇奋斗，并得到我关内各解放区的胜利配合，在三年的奋战中歼灭敌人一百余万，终于解放了东北九省的全部地区和三千七百万同胞，粉碎了中美反动派奴役东北人民并利用东北以挑拨国际战争的迷梦，奠定了在数年内解放全中国，然后将中国逐步建设为工业国家的巩固基础。中国共产党中央委员会谨向全东北军民表示感谢与敬意，希望你们继续努力，与关内人民和各地人民解放军亲密合作，并肩前进，为完全打倒国民党反动派的统治，驱逐美国帝国主义在中国的侵略势力，解放全中国而战！在东北解放战争中牺牲的英雄们永垂不朽！
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中国共产党中央委员会
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　  一九四八年十一月三日
