Title: Eastern Henan: We Captured Minquan
Author:
Date: 1948-11-11
Page: 1

　　Eastern Henan: We Captured Minquan
    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] Late-arriving news: Our army unit recaptured Minquan County town on the Longhai Railway on October 26th.
