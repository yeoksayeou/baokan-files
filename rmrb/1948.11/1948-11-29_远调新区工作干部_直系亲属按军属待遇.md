Title: Cadres Transferred to Work in New Areas: Immediate Family Members to Receive Treatment as Military Dependents
Author:
Date: 1948-11-29
Page: 1

　　Cadres Transferred to Work in New Areas:
  　Immediate Family Members to Receive Treatment as Military Dependents
    [Dispatch from this Newspaper] The North China People's Government issued a circular to governments at all levels under its jurisdiction on the 15th of this month, stating: In order to take care of the family difficulties of cadres transferred to work in new areas, and to reduce cadres' concerns, it is specifically stipulated that all immediate family members of local cadres who have been transferred to work in new areas in the past and in the future shall receive treatment as military dependents.

### 远调新区工作干部　直系亲属按军属待遇

1948-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　远调新区工作干部
  　直系亲属按军属待遇
    【本报讯】华北人民政府于本月十五日向所属各级政府发出通令称：为了照顾远调新区工作干部之家庭困难，减少干部顾虑，特规定凡过去及今后远调新区工作之地方干部，其直系亲属一律按军属待遇。
