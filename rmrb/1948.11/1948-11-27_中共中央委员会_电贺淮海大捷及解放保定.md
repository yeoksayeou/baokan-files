Title: CPC Central Committee Cables Congratulations on Huaihai Victory and the Liberation of Baoding
Author:
Date: 1948-11-27
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 25th. The CPC Central Committee sent a congratulatory telegram on the great victory in the Huaihai Campaign. The text of the telegram is as follows:

　　Comrades Liu Bocheng, Chen Yi, Deng Xiaoping, Rao Shushi, Zhang Yunyi, Su Yu, and Tan Zhenlin, and all comrades of the East China Field Army, local armies, Central Plains Field Army, and local armies:

　　From November 7th to 22nd, in the first stage of the Huaihai Campaign, you annihilated ten divisions of the bandit regular army under Huang Botao, one division under Liu Ruming, two divisions under Sun Liangcheng, one division of the garrison of Suxian, and half a division under Feng Zhiyong, a total of fourteen and a half divisions, as well as many other irregular bandit army units; won over three and a half divisions of Feng Zhiyong's troops to revolt and join the People's Liberation Army; and inflicted serious blows on the bandits Qiu Qingquan, Li Mi, and Sun Yuanliang. You cut off the connection between the bandit armies in Xuzhou and Bengbu, liberated important cities, towns, and stations south, east, north, and west of Xuzhou, including Suxian, Suining, Daxujia, Nianzhuang, Xin'anzhen, Donghai, Guanyun, Xinpu, Lianyungang, Tancheng, Pixian, Taierzhuang, Huixian, Zaozhuang, Lincheng, Hanzhuang, Jiawang, Shangqiu, Yucheng, Caoxian, Shanxian, Yutai, Fengxian, Peixian, Dangshan, Huangkou, Yongcheng, and Xiaoxian, placing the enemy in Xuzhou in complete isolation and connecting the two major liberated areas of Shandong and northern Jiangsu into one. These important achievements are deeply gratifying. It is hoped that you will unite all the military and civilians and continue to work hard to completely annihilate the bandit army in front of you.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 25, 1948

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 25th. The CPC Central Committee recently sent a congratulatory telegram on the liberation of Baoding. The text of the telegram is as follows:

　　Comrades Nie Rongzhen, Bo Yibo, Xu Xiangqian, Teng Daiyuan, and Xiao Ke, and all comrades of the North China People's Liberation Army:

　　Congratulations on your victory in liberating Baoding, the capital of Hebei Province, following the smashing of bandit Fu's plan to attack Shijiazhuang!
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 24, 1948

### 中共中央委员会　电贺淮海大捷及解放保定

1948-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央委员会
  　电贺淮海大捷及解放保定
    【新华社陕北二十五日电】中共中央电贺淮海大捷。电文如下：
    刘伯承、陈毅、邓小平、饶漱石、张云逸、粟裕、谭震林诸同志，及华东野战军、地方军、中原野战军、地方军全体同志们：
    你们自十一月七日至二十二日在淮海战役第一阶段中，歼灭匪正规军黄伯韬部十个师，刘汝明部一个师，孙良诚部两个师，宿县守军一个师，冯治安部半个师，共十四个半师，及其他许多非正规匪军部队，争取冯治安部三个半师起义加入人民解放军方面，并给予邱清泉、李弥、孙元良诸匪以严重打击，切断徐州、蚌埠两处匪军的联系，解放徐州以南以东以北以西诸重要城、镇、车站，包括宿县、睢宁、大许家、碾庄、新安镇、东海、灌云、新浦、连云港、郯城、邳县、台儿庄、辉县、枣庄、临城、韩庄、贾汪、商邱、虞城、曹县、单县、鱼台、丰县、沛县、砀山、黄口、永城、萧县诸要地在内，使徐州之敌陷于完全孤立地位，使山东、苏北两大解放区连成一片。此种重要成就，深堪庆贺。切望团结全体军民，继续努力，为全歼当面匪军而奋斗。
    　　　　　　　　　中国共产党中央委员会
    　　　　　　　　　　　一九四八年十一月二十五日
    【新华社陕北二十五日电】中共中央顷电贺解放保定。电文如下：
    聂荣臻、薄一波、徐向前、滕代远、萧克诸同志及华北人民解放军全体同志们：
    庆祝你们继粉碎傅匪袭击石门计划之夜，解放河北省城保定的胜利！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中共中央委员会
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四八年十一月二十四日
