Title: Czechoslovakia Celebrates Thirtieth Anniversary of Independence; Capitalist Period Ends, Marching Towards the Socialist Road
Author:
Date: 1948-11-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 3rd: Prague Dispatch: On the thirtieth anniversary of the proclamation of the Republic (October 28th), Czech President Gottwald issued an order to the Czechoslovak People's Democratic Army stating: In the development of Czechoslovakia, the capitalist period has come to an end. The Czech working people have abandoned the bourgeoisie, who plotted rebellion and division, and have further strengthened their forces, marching towards the socialist road. The order put forward the following common tasks for the Czech army, security forces, and militia: to defend the people's construction efforts, to defend Czechoslovakia's road to socialism, and to avoid attacks from outside and inside. The order stated: We must use our greatest strength to make our army ready, and if necessary, victoriously defend Czechoslovakia's independence and freedom. The order concluded: The people in our cities and countryside are achieving remarkable achievements. The Czech people will continue to march towards our great goal – socialism – under the leadership of the working class headed by the Communist Party.
    On the eve of National Day, an award ceremony was held in Prague. On the same day, the Czech National Assembly unanimously passed the Five-Year Plan bill for the restoration and development of the national economy from 1949 to 1953.

### 捷克庆祝独立三十周年  资本主义时期已结束　向社会主义道路迈进

1948-11-06
第2版()
专栏：

　　捷克庆祝独立三十周年
    资本主义时期已结束
  　向社会主义道路迈进
    【新华社陕北三日电】布拉格讯：捷总统哥特瓦尔德于共和国宣布独立三十周年纪念日（十月二十八日），向捷克人民民主军发布命令称：在捷克的发展中，资本主义时期已告结束。捷克劳动人民弃绝了图谋叛变与分裂的资产阶级，已进一步加强了自己的力量，向社会主义道路迈进。命令给捷军、保安队与民兵提出下列共同任务：保卫人民的建设努力，保卫捷克走向社会主义的道路，免遭来自外部与内部的攻击。命令称：必须以最大的力量使我们的军队整装以待，必要时则须胜利地捍卫捷克的独立与自由。命令结语称：我国城乡人民正取得显著的成就。捷克人民将继续在以共产党为首的工人阶级的领导下，迈步走向我们伟大的目标——社会主义。
    国庆日前夕，布拉格举行授勋典礼。同日，捷克国民议会一致通过一九四九至一九五三年恢复与发展国民经济的五年计划法案。
