Title: The Fourth Regiment of a Certain Brigade in Northwest China Achieves Remarkable Military Exploits; Deputy Commander-in-Chief Peng and Others Issue Commendations
Author:
Date: 1948-11-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northwest Front, 23rd: The Fourth Regiment of a certain brigade of the People's Liberation Army has repeatedly achieved special military exploits in the Yichuan Victory this spring and in the battle to annihilate the 17th and 38th Divisions of the enemy in Northern Lizhou a month ago. It has been commended by Deputy Commander-in-Chief Peng of the People's Liberation Army and Deputy Commanders Zhang Zongxun and Zhao Shoushan of the Northwest Field Army. The commendation order stated: "On the night of October 5th, our army's Fourth Regiment, with its courageous, decisive, swift, and resourceful actions, penetrated the enemy's deep fire net from the positions of Xiguan and Siqian between the defensive positions of the enemy's 48th Brigade, annihilated the headquarters of the enemy's 48th Brigade, and captured the enemy's brigade commander, chief of staff, and other key officers. 1948-11-26
第2版()
专栏：

　　西北某旅四团战功卓著
  　彭副总司令等传令嘉奖
    【新华社西北前线廿三日电】人民解放军某旅四团，在今春宜川大捷与月前荔北歼敌十七师、三十八师战役中，迭建特殊战功，受到人民解放军彭副总司令及西北野战军张宗逊、赵寿山两副司令员传令嘉奖。嘉奖令称：“我军某旅第四团于十月五日之夜，以勇猛、果敢、神速、机敏之行动，从敌四十八旅防御阵地西观、寺前两据点间，钻入敌之纵深火网，消灭敌四十八旅旅部，俘敌旅长，参谋长等主要军官。同时迂回到敌四十八旅旅部驻地南北酥酪以南，由东向西伸入四十八里与十二旅之间，控制要点，隔断两旅之联系，完成我军分割围歼敌人之战役任务，对我歼敌十七师、三十八师起了决定作用。特此通令嘉奖，作为我全军学习之范例。”
