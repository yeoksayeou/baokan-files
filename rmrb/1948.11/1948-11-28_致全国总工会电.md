Title: Telegram to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1948-11-28
Page: 1

To Comrades Chen Yun, Li Lisan, Zhu Xuefan, and Liu Ningyi, Chairman and Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and to be forwarded to the Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions:

　　In order to unify the labor movement in North China, to adapt to the current victorious and developing situation, and to meet the needs of the construction of the North China Liberated Area, the original trade unions of the Jin-Cha-Ji and Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu regions held an enlarged joint meeting of the two regions' trade unions on October 23rd. Twenty-nine representatives attended, representing the general trade unions of the seven administrative regions of North China, the general trade unions of two cities, and the trade unions of factories, transportation, mines, and other enterprises. The meeting lasted four days, discussing the general line and general policy of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. All representatives unanimously supported and implemented all the resolutions of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. Finally, Li Zaiwen, Zhao Guoqiang, and 19 others were elected as preparatory committee members of the North China Federation of Trade Unions, with Li Zaiwen and Zhao Guoqiang elected as Chairman and Vice-Chairman respectively. A temporary leading body for the North China labor movement was established – the Preparatory Committee of the North China Federation of Trade Unions. From now on, under the leadership of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, we will resolutely fulfill the mission entrusted to us by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, unite all workers in North China, study hard, improve our skills, strengthen labor discipline, earnestly solve the welfare of workers, carry out production competitions, actively support the front lines, unite all the people of North China, and fight to the end to completely eliminate the common enemy of the people of North China – the Chiang Kai-shek and Fu Zuoyi bandit troops – and support the whole country to finally eliminate the rule of the American imperialist lackey, the Kuomintang reactionaries, in China, and establish the People's Democratic Republic of China.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Preparatory Committee of the North China Federation of Trade Unions
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 3rd

### 致全国总工会电

1948-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

　  致全国总工会电陈云、李立三、朱学范、刘宁一正副主席并转全总执行委员会：
    为统一华北职工运动，以适应当前胜利发展的形势，与华北解放区建设的需要，原晋察冀与晋冀鲁豫两区职工会，特于十月二十三日召开两区工会的扩大联席会议，到会代表二十九人，代表华北七个行政区总工会，两个市总工会与工厂、交通、矿山等企业的职工会，历时四日，讨论了全总的总路线与总方针，全体代表一致拥护，执行全总的一切决议。最后选出栗再温、赵国强等二十一人为华北总工会筹备委员，栗再温、赵国强被选为正副主任，并成立了华北职工运动临时的领导机关——华北总工会筹备委员会。今后我们在全总领导下，坚决完成全总所赋予我们的使命，团结全华北职工，努力学习，提高技术，加强劳动纪律，认真解决职工的福利，开展生产竞赛，积极支援前线，团结全华北人民，为完全消灭华北人民死敌——蒋傅匪军，并支援全国，最后消灭美帝走狗国民党反动派在中国的统治，建立中华人民民主共和国而奋斗到底。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　华北总工会筹备委员会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十一月三日
