Title: Asian Women's Conference Sets Up New Venue; Secretary-General of the Women's Federation of the Chinese Liberated Areas Arrives in Budapest
Author:
Date: 1948-11-30
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 26th: Electric News Agency, Budapest, 23rd: The Second World Congress of the Women's International Democratic Federation is scheduled to be held here in December. Asian delegates attending the conference have arrived in advance to arrange a new time and location for the Asian Women's Conference. The Asian Women's Conference was originally scheduled to be held in Calcutta in November, but was banned by the Indian government. Zhang Qinqiu, head of the Chinese delegation and Secretary-General of the Women's Federation of the Chinese Liberated Areas, has arrived here. Delegations from India, Malaya, and Indonesia are expected to arrive soon.

### 亚洲妇女大会布置新址　中国解放区妇联秘书长抵布达佩斯

1948-11-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　亚洲妇女大会布置新址
  　中国解放区妇联秘书长抵布达佩斯
    【新华社陕北二十六日电】电通社布达佩斯二十三日讯：国际民主妇女联盟第二次世界代表大会定于十二月在此开会。出席大会的亚洲代表已先期抵此，以布置召开亚洲妇女代表大会的新的时间和地点。亚洲妇女代表大会原定十一月在加尔各答举行，但为印度政府禁止。中国代表团团长中国解放了妇女联合会秘书长张琴秋已抵此。印度、马来亚和印尼的代表团，预料即将抵此。
