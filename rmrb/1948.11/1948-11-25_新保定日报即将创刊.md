Title: New Baoding Daily to be Launched Soon
Author:
Date: 1948-11-25
Page: 1

　　New Baoding Daily to be Launched Soon
    [Baoding Electric] After the liberation of Baoding, the Baoding branch of the New China News Agency was immediately established and has now begun sending稿(gao)稿(gao) means copy but I think it means stories here. The New Baoding Daily will also be launched on the 27th.

### 新保定日报即将创刊

1948-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　新保定日报即将创刊
    【保定电】保定解放后，新华社保定支社立即成立，现已开始发稿。新保定日报，亦将于二十七日创刊。
