Title: Vice Chairman Zhu in Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1956-01-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 25th: Vice Chairman Zhu De of the People's Republic of China and his entourage visited the Lenin Heavy Machinery Manufacturing Plant in Pilsen on January 24th.

    On the same day, Vice Chairman Zhu De also visited the Pilsen Brewery.

    Vice Chairman Zhu De has been taking a short rest in Karlovy Vary for the past few days. He also visited the "Karl Marx" Mineral Spring Sanatorium, where Marx lived from 1874 to 1876.

### 朱副主席在捷克斯洛伐克

1956-01-27
第4版()
专栏：

    朱副主席在捷克斯洛伐克
    据新华社布拉格25日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德和随行人员1月24日参观了比尔森的列宁重型机器制造工厂。
    同日，朱德副主席还参观了比尔森酿酒厂。
    朱德副主席这几天在卡罗维发利作短期的休息。他还访问了“卡尔·马克思”矿泉休养所，马克思在1874年到1876年曾经在那里住过。
