Title: The First Theater in Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture is Completed
Author: Guo Dingcheng
Date: 1956-01-19
Page: 3

The first theater in the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture of Hunan Province, the Jishou Theater, has been completed in Jishou Town, the seat of the Autonomous Prefecture People's Committee. The theater can accommodate 720 spectators. On December 31, 1955, the opening day of the Jishou Theater, the excellent Miao art "Jielong Dance" and Peking Opera, beloved by all ethnic groups, were performed. (Guo Dingcheng)

### 湘西苗族自治州第一个剧院建成
郭鼎诚
1956-01-19
第3版()
专栏：

    湘西苗族自治州第一个剧院建成
    湖南省湘西苗族自治州的第一个剧院——吉首剧院，已经在自治州人民委员会所在地的吉首镇建成了。剧院可容纳720个观众。1955年12月31日吉首剧院开幕这天，演出了苗族人民优秀的艺术“接龙舞”和各族人民喜爱的京剧。　（郭鼎诚）
