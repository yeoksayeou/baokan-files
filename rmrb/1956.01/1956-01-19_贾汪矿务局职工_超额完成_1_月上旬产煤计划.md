Title: Jiawang Mining Bureau Employees Exceed January Early-Ten-Day Coal Production Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-01-19
Page: 2

Having already exceeded the 1957 state plan target for annual coal production, the employees of the Jiawang Mining Bureau completed the production plan for the first ten days of January ahead of schedule, producing 6,052 tons of coal more than the original target. The Zhuang Hongsheng group of the Hanqiao Coal Mine, which completed 16 months of work in one year in 1955, originally planned to excavate 89 meters per month. From January 1st to 10th, they completed 51.4 meters.
              (Our correspondent in Nanjing)

### 贾汪矿务局职工  超额完成1月上旬产煤计划

1956-01-19
第2版()
专栏：

    贾汪矿务局职工
    超额完成1月上旬产煤计划
    煤炭年产量已经超过了1957年国家计划指标的贾汪矿务局的职工，提前完成了1月上旬的生产计划，比原订指标增产了6，052吨煤。1955年1年完成16个月工作量的韩桥煤矿庄洪生小组，原订月计划要掘进89公尺，从1月1日到10日，他们就完成了51.4公尺。
              （本报驻南京记者）
