Title: Old Currency Stamps Limited Time Stop of Use
Author:
Date: 1956-01-02
Page: 2

    Old Currency Stamps Limited Time Stop of Use
    The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications has decided that, starting from January 1, 1956, the sale of old currency ordinary stamps, airmail stamps, commemorative stamps, special stamps, and postcards and postal letters with old currency postage will be stopped nationwide. Old currency stamps, postcards and postal letters in the possession of the public can be used until the end of March 1956. From April 1, 1956, all types of old currency stamps, postcards and postal letters will be discontinued.
    After the old currency stamps are discontinued, those who have old currency stamps, postcards, and postal letters can exchange them for new currency stamps and postcards at the local post office from April 1 to June 30, 1956. Exchanges will not be allowed after the deadline. There are no restrictions on the type of exchange, such as stamps can be exchanged for postcards, airmail stamps can be exchanged for ordinary stamps, etc.

### 旧币邮票限期停止使用

1956-01-02
第2版()
专栏：

    旧币邮票限期停止使用
    邮电部决定从1956年1月1日起，在全国范围内停止出售旧币普通邮票、航空邮票、纪念邮票、特种邮票和带旧币邮资的明信片、邮简等。存在群众中的旧币邮票、明信片和邮简等，可以使用到1956年3月底。从1956年4月1日起，所有各类旧币邮票、明信片和邮简，一律停止使用。
    旧币邮票停止使用后，凡存有旧币邮票、明信片、邮简可在1956年4月1日至6月30日期间向当地邮电局掉换新币邮票和明信片，过期不再掉换。掉换种类不限制，如邮票可换明信片，航空邮票可以换普通邮票等。
