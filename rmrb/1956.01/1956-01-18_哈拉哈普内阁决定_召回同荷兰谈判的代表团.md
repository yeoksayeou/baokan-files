Title: Harahap Cabinet Decides to Recall Delegation Negotiating with the Netherlands
Author:
Date: 1956-01-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, January 17th: Indonesian Minister of Information Makmur announced on the morning of the 16th, after a cabinet meeting, that the Harahap cabinet had decided to recall the Indonesian delegation from Geneva to further report to the government on the results of the negotiations with the Netherlands.

Makmur said that the cabinet made this decision after thoroughly discussing the joint notification submitted by the two ruling parties, the Indonesian Islamic Teachers Association and the Indonesian Islamic League Party, opposing the current Indonesian-Dutch negotiations.

### 哈拉哈普内阁决定  召回同荷兰谈判的代表团

1956-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    哈拉哈普内阁决定
    召回同荷兰谈判的代表团
    据新华社雅加达17日电　印度尼西亚情报部长马克慕尔16日上午在内阁会议之后宣布，哈拉哈普内阁已经决定从日内瓦召回印度尼西亚代表团，来向政府进一步报告同荷兰谈判的结果。
    马克慕尔说，内阁是在详尽地讨论了印度尼西亚回教师联合会和印度尼西亚回教联盟党两个执政党提出的关于反对目前的印度尼西亚—荷兰谈判的联合通知之后作出这个决定的。
