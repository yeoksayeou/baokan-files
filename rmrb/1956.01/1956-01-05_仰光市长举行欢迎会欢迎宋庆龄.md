Title: Mayor of Rangoon Holds Reception to Welcome Soong Ching Ling
Author:
Date: 1956-01-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 4th: The Mayor of Rangoon, U Ba Swe, held a reception at the Rangoon City Hall at 3 PM on the 3rd to welcome Soong Ching Ling.

　　Mayor U Ba Swe of Rangoon first delivered a speech welcoming Soong Ching Ling. He expressed his admiration for Soong Ching Ling's contributions to the liberation of Chinese women, the welfare of Chinese children, and the cause of defending peace.

　　Soong Ching Ling then delivered a reply. She said: Your welcome reception for me today expresses your warm feelings for the entire Chinese people. To continue developing our cultural and economic relations, we must have peace. Burma and China have already contributed to world peace. The Bandung Conference and its resolutions have greatly strengthened the forces of peace. Despite the obstruction of reactionary currents, the tide of peace is increasing its strength. Peace will ultimately triumph.

　　Soong Ching Ling finally stated that she would have the opportunity to learn from Burma's experience and achievements in economic construction during her visit, and congratulated the further development and consolidation of friendship between China and Burma.

### 仰光市长举行欢迎会欢迎宋庆龄

1956-01-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　仰光市长举行欢迎会欢迎宋庆龄
    新华社仰光4日电　仰光市长吴盛佩3日下午3时在仰光市政大厅举行欢迎会欢迎宋庆龄。
    仰光市长吴盛佩首先致词欢迎宋庆龄。他对宋庆龄为中国妇女解放、为中国儿童福利以及为保卫和平事业所作的贡献，表示钦佩。
    宋庆龄接着致答词。她说：你们今天为我举行欢迎会是表示你们对中国全体人民的热烈的感情。要继续发展我们的文化和经济关系，我们必须有和平。缅甸和中国已经对世界和平作了贡献。万隆会议和会议决议已经大大地巩固了和平的力量。尽管有反动的潮流作梗，和平的巨浪正在增加它的力量。和平将最后取得胜利。
    宋庆龄最后表示她在访问期间将有机会学习缅甸在经济建设方面的经验和成绩，并且祝贺中缅两国的友谊获得进一步的发展和巩固。
