Title: Jiangxi Province and Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Have Basically Achieved Agricultural Cooperativization
Author:
Date: 1956-01-15
Page: 1

This newspaper, Nanchang, 14th dispatch: Jiangxi Province has basically achieved semi-socialist agricultural cooperativization. As of the 12th, more than 67,000 new cooperatives have been established throughout the province, and more than 8,000 cooperatives are being established, which can be completed by the end of the month. Together with the members of more than 14,700 old cooperatives, the number of peasant households joining the cooperatives in the province has reached more than 2.93 million, accounting for more than 75% of the total number of peasant households in the province. The Jinggang Mountains, the cradle of the revolution, has completely achieved semi-socialist agricultural cooperativization; in Ruijin County, the old red capital, more than 8,200 family members of revolutionary martyrs, military personnel, disabled revolutionary soldiers, and demobilized construction soldiers have joined the cooperatives, and more than 740 of them have been elected as chairmen of the cooperatives.

With the great development of agricultural producers' cooperatives, a high tide of agricultural production has been set off throughout the province. "Striving to complete the Five-Year Agricultural Production Increase Plan in Four Years" has gradually become the slogan of action for the vast number of peasants in the province.

This newspaper, Hohhot, 14th dispatch: The Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region has basically achieved semi-socialist agricultural cooperativization. As of the 14th, 26,900 agricultural producers' cooperatives have been established in the region, and the number of peasant households joining the cooperatives has reached more than 75% of the total number of peasant households in the region. Most of the Mongolian, Han, Daur, Manchu, Hui, and Korean farmers in the region have joined the cooperatives, and thousands of joint ethnic cooperatives and hundreds of agricultural and pastoral cooperatives have emerged throughout the region.

According to the plans of various localities, about 2,000 advanced cooperatives can be established in the region before the spring plowing this year.

### 江西省和内蒙古自治区  已经基本上实现农业合作化

1956-01-15
第1版()
专栏：

    江西省和内蒙古自治区
    已经基本上实现农业合作化
    本报南昌14日电  江西省已经基本上实现了半社会主义的农业合作化。到12日止，全省已经建成了67，000多个新社，还有8，000多个社正在建立，月底可以建成。加上14，700多个老社的社员，全省入社农户已经达293万多户，占全省总农户的75％以上。革命摇篮井冈山区已经全部实现了半社会主义的农业合作化；红色故都瑞金县有8，200多个烈属、军属和革命残废军人、复员建设军人入社，其中740多人当选为社长。
    随着农业生产合作社的大发展，全省掀起了农业生产高潮。“五年农业增产计划争取四年完成”，已经逐渐成为全省广大农民的行动口号。
    本报呼和浩特14日电  内蒙古自治区已经基本上实现了半社会主义的农业合作化。到14日止，全区已经建成农业生产合作社26，900个，入社农户已达全区总农户的75％以上。全区蒙古、汉、达呼尔、满、回、朝鲜等各族农民大部分入社，全区出现了数千个民族联合社和数百个农牧业合作社。
    根据各地的计划，今年春耕以前，全区可办起高级社2，000个左右。
