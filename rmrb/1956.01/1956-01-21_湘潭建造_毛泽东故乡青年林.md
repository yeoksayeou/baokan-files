Title: Xiangtan to Build "Mao Zedong's Hometown Youth Forest"
Author:
Date: 1956-01-21
Page: 3

    Xiangtan to Build "Mao Zedong's Hometown Youth Forest"
    The Xiangtan Local Committee of the Communist Youth League recently made a decision to build the "Mao Zedong's Hometown Youth Forest." The decision stipulates that all barren mountains on both sides of the nearly 200-li long highway from Changsha to Shaoshan Township and near the highway will be completely afforested before the end of 1957, and it has been decided that February 27-29 and September 16-18 of this and next year will be "Mao Zedong's Hometown Youth Forest" afforestation days. (Communist Youth League Xiangtan Local Committee Communications Group)

### 湘潭建造“毛泽东故乡青年林”

1956-01-21
第3版()
专栏：

    湘潭建造“毛泽东故乡青年林”
    青年团湘潭地方工作委员会最近作出建造“毛泽东故乡青年林”的决定。决定从长沙市到韶山乡近200里长的公路两侧及公路附近的所有荒山，在1957年底以前全部绿化，并决定今明两年的2月27日至29日，9月16日至18日为“毛泽东故乡青年林”造林日。（青年团湘潭地工委通讯组）
