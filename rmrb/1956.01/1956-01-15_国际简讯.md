Author:
Date: 1956-01-15
Page: 4

    International Briefs
    △ Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Pham Van Dong, sent a telegram to the Prime Minister of Sudan, Ismail al-Azhari, congratulating Sudan on its declaration of independence.
    △ On the evening of January 12th, the large-scale color documentary "Glimpses of India," about the visit of Soviet leaders Bulganin and Khrushchev to India, was shown for the first time at the Royal Theatre in New Delhi. The film will be released simultaneously in Calcutta, Bombay, Madras, Bangalore, and other cities on the 13th.
    △ The Polish "Sikor Company" is preparing technical designs for the restoration of the Hejaz Railway, which runs through Syria, Jordan, and Saudi Arabia. At the end of January, 20 Polish experts will travel to Saudi Arabia to participate in the restoration work.

### 国际简讯

1956-01-15
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    △越南民主共和国总理范文同打电报给苏丹总理伊斯梅尔·阿扎里，祝贺苏丹宣布独立。
    △1月12日晚间，在新德里的皇家剧院第一次放映了关于苏联领导人布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫访问印度的大型彩色记录片：“印度一瞥”。这部影片13日将在加尔各答、孟买、马德拉斯、班加罗尔和其他城市同时公映。
    △波兰“西科尔公司”正在着手拟制恢复横贯叙利亚、约旦和沙特阿拉伯的希德扎兹铁路的技术设计图样。在1月底将有20名波兰专家前往沙特阿拉伯参加恢复工作。
