Title: No Alms-Giving "Aid"
Author:
Date: 1956-01-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 10th -- According to the Indian Press Trust, the Indian government has rejected the recent suggestion from the United States to relieve the victims of last year's Orissa flood with 100,000 tons of wheat because the American agency making the suggestion demanded to supervise the distribution of these materials.

The Indian Minister of Industry responsible for industry, Kanungo, said at a gathering of university students that the Indian government rejected this suggestion because the conditions attached to it damaged India's prestige. Kanungo said: India's position is not to accept any "alms-giving aid" from any party.

### 不要施舍式的“援助”

1956-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    不要施舍式的“援助”
    新华社新德里10日电　据印度报业托辣斯消息，印度政府拒绝了美国最近提出的以十万吨小麦救济去年奥里萨水灾灾民的建议，因为提出建议的美国机关要求监督这些物资的分配。
    印度工商部负责工业的部长卡农戈在一次大学生的集会上说，印度政府拒绝这种建议，因为它所附带的条件损害了印度的威信。卡农戈说：印度的立场是，不接受任何方面“施舍式的援助”
