Title: Chairman Tôn Đức Thắng Leaves Beijing to Return to Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1956-01-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 28th: Chairman Tôn Đức Thắng of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam left Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 28th to return to his country.

Those who saw him off at the station included Li Jishen, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China; Zhang Su, Chen Shaoxian, and Luo Longji, members of the Standing Committee; Wu Kejian, Qu Wu, and Yu Xinqing, Deputy Secretaries-General; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Liu Ningyi, Standing Committee Member of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Wang Kunlun, Vice Mayor of Beijing; Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department.

Also present at the station to see him off were Hoàng Văn Hoan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China; Yudin, Ambassador of the Soviet Union to China; Dimov, Ambassador of Bulgaria to China; Effenberger, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China; and Damdin Süren, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of Mongolia in China. Chairman Liu Shaoqi held a banquet on January 27th to entertain Chairman Tôn Đức Thắng. They shook hands cordially before the banquet. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong.

### 孙德胜委员长离北京回越南

1956-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    孙德胜委员长离北京回越南
    新华社28日讯　越南民主共和国国民大会常务委员会委员长孙德胜在28日下午乘火车离开北京返国。
    到车站欢送的，有中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、彭真、陈叔通，委员张苏、陈劭先、罗隆基，副秘书长吴克坚、屈武、余心清，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员刘宁一，北京市副市长王昆仑，外交部部长助理陈家康、礼宾司司长柯华等。
    越南民主共和国驻华大使黄文欢和苏联驻华大使尤金，保加利亚驻华大使迪莫夫，德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆临时代办埃弗哈而茨，蒙古驻华大使馆临时代办达姆丁道尔吉也到车站欢送。（附图片）
