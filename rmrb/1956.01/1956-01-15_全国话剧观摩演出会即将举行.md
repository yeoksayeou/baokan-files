Title: National Drama Performance and Observation Meeting to be Held Soon
Author:
Date: 1956-01-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 14th: The Ministry of Culture's first National Drama Performance and Observation Meeting is being actively prepared. 39 drama troupes have registered with the preparatory committee for the first drama performance, including the National Theatre Company, drama troupes within the military system, and professional drama troupes within the trade union system. There are more than 40 plays participating in the performance, most of which are themed around various important aspects of socialist construction and the Chinese people's struggle to defend peace, and many are new works from 1955. Drama troupes from all over the country will send personnel to form observation groups to observe this performance, and dramatists from the Soviet Union and other countries will also come to observe. The person in charge of the preparatory committee for the first drama performance told reporters today: The first drama performance has been decided to begin in Beijing on March 1st this year. The purpose of holding the performance is to promote excellent plays, exchange experiences in artistic creation and artistic practice, so as to promote the prosperity of drama creation, improve the quality of drama art, and make drama art better serve the country's socialist construction.

### 全国话剧观摩演出会即将举行

1956-01-15
第1版()
专栏：

    全国话剧观摩演出会即将举行
    据新华社14日讯  文化部举办的第一届全国话剧观摩演出会正在积极筹备。向第一届话剧会演筹备委员会报名参加会演的已经有39个话剧团体，包括国家话剧团、部队系统话剧团和工会系统专业话剧团。参加会演的剧目有40多个，大多以社会主义建设的各个重要方面和中国人民保卫和平的斗争为题材，很多是1955年的新作品。全国各地的戏剧团体都将选派人员组成观摩团观摩这次会演，苏联等国也将派戏剧家前来观摩。第一届话剧会演筹备委员会负责人今天对记者说：第一届话剧会演已决定今年3月1日开始在北京举行。举行会演的目的是为了推广优秀剧目，交流艺术创作和艺术实践的经验，以繁荣话剧创作，提高话剧艺术质量，使话剧艺术更好地为国家社会主义建设服务。
