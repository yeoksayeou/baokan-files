Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1956-01-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, January 19th: The Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks held their thirty-third meeting on January 19th. The meeting adjourned at 13:05. The next meeting will be held on January 25th (Wednesday).

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1956-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦19日电　中美两国大使级会谈1月19日举行第三十三次会议。会议在13时零5分休会，下次会议将在1月25日（星期三）举行。
