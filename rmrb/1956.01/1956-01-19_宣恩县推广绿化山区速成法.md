Title: Xuanen County Promotes Quick Method for Greening Mountainous Areas
Author:
Date: 1956-01-19
Page: 2

Xuanen County, Hubei Province, is promoting a quick method of using layering afforestation to green mountainous areas.

More than 100 townships in this county have already adopted this method. They have decided to complete the greening of more than 5 million mu of barren mountains in the county by 1957.

This method changes seedling cultivation to layering afforestation. As long as the branches of the Chinese fir are buried in the soil, dozens of thick and tall Chinese fir seedlings will grow in the same year. This overcomes the difficulties of collecting seeds and raising seedlings in greening barren mountains. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 宣恩县推广绿化山区速成法

1956-01-19
第2版()
专栏：

    宣恩县推广绿化山区速成法
    湖北省宣恩县正在推广一种用压条造林来绿化山区的速成办法。
    这个县里已经有100多个乡采用了这种办法。他们决定在1957年完成全县500多万亩荒山的绿化工作。
    这种办法是把育苗造林改成压条造林。只要把杉树的枝条埋进土里，当年就长出几十根粗壮、高大的杉树苗。这就克服了绿化荒山中采集种子和育苗的困难。　（新华社）
