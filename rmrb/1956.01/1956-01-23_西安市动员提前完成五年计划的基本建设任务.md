Title: Xi'an Mobilizes to Complete Basic Construction Tasks of the Five-Year Plan Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1956-01-23
Page: 1

    Xi'an City Federation of Trade Unions held a conference of advanced producers and production team leaders from basic construction units in the Xi'an area on the evening of the 20th, mobilizing construction personnel throughout the city to complete the basic construction tasks of the first Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule. Tens of thousands of workers and their families at various basic construction sites and factories also listened to the live broadcast of the conference at the same time.
    Fang Zhongru, Second Secretary of the Xi'an Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman of the Xi'an City Federation of Trade Unions, delivered a mobilization report to the workers attending the conference. At the meeting, 13 people, including advanced producer Lu Jinkang and engineering technician Wu Minfu, proposed an initiative to all basic construction workers in the Xi'an area to "complete the basic construction tasks of the first Five-Year Plan in the Xi'an area one year ahead of schedule and strive to overfulfill the basic construction tasks for 1956."
    Fang Zhongru's report and the advanced producers' initiative were warmly responded to by the workers attending the conference and listening to the live broadcast. A total of 209 shock teams, production teams, and functional departments called the conference to respond to the initiative. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 西安市动员提前完成五年计划的基本建设任务

1956-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    西安市动员提前完成五年计划的基本建设任务
    西安市工会联合会在20日晚召开西安地区基本建设单位先进生产者和生产小组长大会，动员全市建设人员提前完成第一个五年计划的基本建设任务。各基本建设工地和各工厂企业的几万名职工和家属也在同时收听了大会的实况广播。
    中共西安市委员会第二书记、西安市工会联合会主席方仲如向到会职工作了动员报告。会上先进生产者陆进康和工程技术人员吴民孚等13人向西安地区全体基本建设职工提出了“为提前一年完成西安地区第一个五年计划的基本建设任务和争取超额完成1956年的基本建设任务”的倡议。
    方仲如的报告和先进生产者的倡议受到到会和收听实况广播职工的热烈响应，共有209个突击队、生产小组和职能科室向大会打来响应倡议的电话。（据新华社讯）
