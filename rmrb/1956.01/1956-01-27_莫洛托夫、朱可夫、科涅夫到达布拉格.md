Title: Molotov, Zhukov, Konev Arrive in Prague
Author:
Date: 1956-01-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 26th: According to TASS, Vyacheslav Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, and Marshal Georgy Zhukov, Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, arrived in Prague by plane on January 25th to attend the Political Consultative Committee meeting of the Warsaw Treaty member states, held in Prague on January 27, 1956. Marshal Ivan Konev, Commander-in-Chief of the Joint Armed Forces of the Warsaw Treaty member states, also arrived on the same plane.
    The President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Antonín Zápotocký, received Molotov, Zhukov, and Konev on the same day.

### 莫洛托夫、朱可夫、科涅夫到达布拉格

1956-01-27
第4版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫、朱可夫、科涅夫到达布拉格
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社布拉格讯：参加华沙条约缔约国1956年1月27日在布拉格举行的政治协商委员会会议的苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长莫洛托夫和苏联国防部长、苏联元帅朱可夫，在1月25日乘飞机到达布拉格。华沙条约缔约国联合武装部队总司令科涅夫元帅也同机到达。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基在当天接见了莫洛托夫、朱可夫和科涅夫。
