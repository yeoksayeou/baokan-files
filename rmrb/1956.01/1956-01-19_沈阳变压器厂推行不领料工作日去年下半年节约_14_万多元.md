Title: Shenyang Transformer Factory Promotes "No Material Requisition Workday," Saving Over 140,000 Yuan in the Latter Half of Last Year
Author: Zhang Quan
Date: 1956-01-19
Page: 2

The Shenyang Transformer Factory, after starting to promote the advanced Soviet experience of "No Material Requisition Workday" in July 1955, had implemented 3,465 "No Material Requisition Workdays" by the end of 1955, saving the state 141,854 yuan. Now, the "No Material Requisition Workday" has become a conscious action for all workers and staff at the Transformer Factory, with over 100 groups incorporating the "No Material Requisition Workday" into their competition guarantee conditions. (Zhang Quan)

### 沈阳变压器厂推行不领料工作日去年下半年节约14万多元
张泉
1956-01-19
第2版()
专栏：

    沈阳变压器厂推行不领料工作日去年下半年节约14万多元
    沈阳变压器厂从1955年7月开始推行苏联“不领原材料工作日”的先进经验以后，到1955年年底，全厂共推行了3，465个“不领原材料工作日”，给国家节约了141，854元。现在，“不领原材料工作日”在变压器厂已经成为全体职工群众的自觉行动，100多个小组都把“不领原材料工作日”制定到竞赛保证条件中去了。（张泉）
