Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Holds First Graduate Student Entrance Examination
Author:
Date: 1956-01-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The first graduate student entrance examination for the Chinese Academy of Sciences was held from the 4th to the 6th in Beijing, Shanghai, and Shenyang. Applicants included graduating university students from various regions, active national government cadres, and research interns from various research units of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. The list of admitted graduate students is expected to be announced in mid-February.

The admitted graduate students will be trained by renowned scientists such as Hua Luogeng, Zhao Zhongyao, Qian Weichang, Wang You, Yang Zhongjian, Bing Zhi, Feng Depei, Bei Shizhang, Hu Sheng, Fan Wenlan, and Luo Changpei.

### 中国科学院举行第一批研究生考试

1956-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国科学院举行第一批研究生考试
    新华社7日讯　中国科学院第一批研究生的考试，4日到6日分别在北京、上海、沈阳三地举行。投考的人有各地应届高等学校毕业生、在职的国家机关干部和中国科学院各研究单位的研究实习员。研究生录取名单预定在2月中旬揭晓。
    被录取的研究生将要分别由华罗庚、赵忠尧、钱伟长、汪猷、杨钟健、秉志、冯德培、贝时璋、胡绳、范文澜、罗常培等著名科学家负责培养。
