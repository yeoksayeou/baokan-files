Title: Lanzhou-Qinghai Railway Surveying Begins

Author:
Date: 1956-01-09
Page: 1

　　Lanzhou-Qinghai Railway Surveying Begins

    (Our dispatch) Surveying has begun on the Lanzhou-Qinghai Railway, another railway trunk line in Northwest China. Currently, surveying personnel are braving the severe cold to conduct field surveys within Qinghai Province. They will complete all surveying work from Lanzhou to Xining before the fourth quarter of this year.

    The Lanzhou-Qinghai Railway will start from Hekou, west of Lanzhou, pass through Xining, the capital of Qinghai Province, and then enter the Qaidam Basin via the Chaka Salt Lake. The construction of the Lanzhou-Qinghai Railway is of great significance for developing the rich mineral resources of the Qaidam Basin, developing the economy of minority areas, and supporting national industrial construction.

### 兰州—青海铁路已经开始勘测

1956-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　兰州—青海铁路已经开始勘测
    本报讯　我国西北地区又一条铁路干线——兰州—青海铁路已经开始勘测。现在，勘测人员正冒着严寒在青海省境内进行野外勘测。他们将在今年第四季度以前，完成兰州到西宁的全部勘测工作。
    兰州—青海铁路将由兰州西边的河口开始，经青海省会西宁，然后经过茶卡盐池进入柴达木盆地。兰青铁路的修建，对于开发柴达木盆地的丰富矿藏、发展少数民族地区的经济和支援国家工业建设具有重大意义。
