Title: Heilongjiang Actively Develops Advanced Cooperatives
Author:
Date: 1956-01-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency

    Heilongjiang Province, based on the new situation of the accelerated development of the agricultural cooperation movement and the demands of the masses, has revised its original plan for piloting advanced agricultural production cooperatives. The revised plan requires the establishment of two thousand advanced cooperatives before the spring plowing in 1956, with the number of members joining advanced cooperatives reaching about 15% of the province's total number of farm households. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 黑龙江积极发展高级社

1956-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

    黑龙江积极发展高级社
    黑龙江省根据农业合作化运动发展加快的新情况和群众的要求，修改了原来试办高级农业生产合作社的计划。修改后的计划要求，在1956年春耕前建成两千个高级社，加入高级社的社员将达到全省农户总数的15％左右。　（据新华社讯）
