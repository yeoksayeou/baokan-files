Title: Hanoi-Nam Dinh Railway Holds Opening Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1956-01-01
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 31st. The Hanoi-Nam Dinh railway, the second railway restored by the Vietnamese people after the armistice, began operations on December 31st. A grand opening ceremony was held at the Hanoi city station that morning.

　　At the opening ceremony, Nguyen Van Tran, Minister of Transport and Posts of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, pointed out that the opening of the Hanoi-Nam Dinh railway is a great achievement for the Vietnamese people in restoring the economy, healing the wounds of war, and improving people's lives. Subsequently, Nguyen Van Tran cut the ribbon. Then, the first train officially opened drove to Nam Dinh amidst prolonged cheers.

　　The Hanoi-Nam Dinh railway is a section of the original Hanoi-Saigon railway. It passes through the fertile fields of Ha Dong, Ha Nam, and Nam Dinh provinces, connecting Hanoi City, Phu Ly City, and Nam Dinh City. The entire line is approximately 87 kilometers long.

### 河内—南定铁路举行通车典礼

1956-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　河内—南定铁路举行通车典礼
    新华社河内31日电　越南人民在停战后修复的第二条铁路河内—南定铁路在12月31日开始通车。当天清晨，在河内市车站举行了隆重的通车典礼。
    在通车典礼上，越南民主共和国政府交通邮电部长阮文珍指出，河内—南定铁路的通车是越南人民恢复经济、医治战争创伤和提高人民生活的一项巨大成绩。随后，由阮文珍剪彩。接着，正式通车的第一次列车在长时间的欢呼声中驶往南定。
    河内—南定铁路是原河内—西贡铁路的一段，它穿过河东、河南、南定三省的肥沃田野，把河内市、府里市和南定市连接起来，全线约长87公里。
