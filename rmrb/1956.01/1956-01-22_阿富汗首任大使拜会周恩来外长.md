Title: Afghanistan's First Ambassador Calls on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1956-01-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 21st: The first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Afghanistan to China, Abdul Samad, called on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai in the afternoon of the 21st to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials.

Present were Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Chen Shuliang, Deputy Director of the Asian Department.

### 阿富汗首任大使拜会周恩来外长

1956-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

    阿富汗首任大使拜会周恩来外长
    新华社21日讯　阿富汗王国驻我国首任特命全权大使阿卜杜尔·萨马德在21日下午拜会我外交部长周恩来，商谈递交国书事宜。
    在座的有外交部礼宾司司长柯华和亚洲司副司长陈叔亮。
