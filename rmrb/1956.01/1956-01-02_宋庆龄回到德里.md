Title: Soong Ching Ling Returns to Delhi
Author:
Date: 1956-01-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 1st: Soong Ching Ling and her entourage returned to Delhi on the evening of December 31st after visiting Agra, Bhopal, Mumbai, Calcutta, Madras, and Bangalore. She was greeted at the airport by Indian Prime Minister Nehru and his daughter, Mrs. Indira Gandhi.

On her way back to Delhi, Soong Ching Ling made a brief stop in Aurangabad and visited the famous ancient Indian rock caves of Ellora by car.

Xinhua News Agency, Bangalore: Soong Ching Ling delivered a speech at the Eighth Annual Conference of the Indian Society of Labour Economics in Bangalore on December 30th.

After extending her warm congratulations to the conference, she said, "Our friendship is growing closer day by day. Prime Minister Nehru said the day before yesterday in Mangalore that when India gained independence, it was of great significance to the world. We live in a great and historically turbulent era. India has entered a new historical stage as a free nation determined to play its role in the world, and we must fulfill this great responsibility. What this outstanding statesman said about your country applies to both of our countries. We have so much in common, and therefore we are truly good friends."

Soong Ching Ling spoke about social work in India and the development of social welfare work in our country after liberation.

Finally, she said, "We especially need peace to engage in our social welfare work. We in China are working with all peace-loving people in the world to safeguard international peace. The peace movement in India is also rapidly developing for the common cause of world peace and the common purpose of building our respective countries. Let us cooperate and work together! Peace and social welfare are inseparable. The people of our two countries must unite to engage in this common cause."

### 宋庆龄回到德里

1956-01-02
第4版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄回到德里
    新华社新德里1日电　宋庆龄和她的随行人员在访问了亚格拉、波保尔、孟买、加尔各答、马德拉斯和班加罗尔之后，在12月31日晚间回到德里。她在机场受到了印度总理尼赫鲁和他的女儿英迪拉·甘地夫人的迎接。
    宋庆龄在回德里途中在阿兰迦巴作了短时的停留，并且乘汽车访问了爱楼罗的著名的古代印度石窟。
    据新华社班加罗尔电　宋庆龄12月30日在班加罗尔印度社会工作会议第八届年会上发表了讲话。
    她在向会议表示热烈地祝贺以后说，我们的友谊日益密切了。尼赫鲁总理前天在曼加罗说，当印度获得独立的时候，那对世界是有重大意义的。我们生活在伟大的、历史性的动荡的时代中。印度已经作为一个决心要在世界上起它的作用的自由民族进入了一个新的历史阶段，我们必须履行这种伟大的责任。这位杰出的政治家对于你们的国家所说的话对我们两国都适用。我们有这样多的共同之处，因此我们真正是好朋友。
    宋庆龄谈到了印度的社会工作和我国解放后社会福利工作的发展。
    最后她说，我们尤其需要和平来从事我们的社会福利工作。我们在中国正在同世界上所有爱好和平的人民一起为维护国际和平而奋斗。印度的和平运动为了我们的世界和平的共同事业和我们的建设我们各自的国家的共同目的，也已经在迅速开展。让我们进行合作，共同努力吧！和平同社会福利是不可分割的。我们两国的人民必须团结起来从事这个共同事业。
