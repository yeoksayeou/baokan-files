Title: Gong Peng, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Hosts Egyptian Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-01-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Report: Gong Peng, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet on the evening of January 11th in honor of the Egyptian journalists delegation headed by Abdul Moneim Mahmoud Sawe.

    Gong Peng said in her speech at the banquet: The Chinese government and people welcome the Egyptian journalists delegation to visit China, because you not only represent the Egyptian press, but also represent the goodwill and concern of the independent Egyptian people towards the Chinese people. Gong Peng also expressed the hope that there would be more frequent news exchanges between China and Egypt in the future to deepen the understanding between the people of the two countries.

    In his speech at the banquet, Sawe praised the construction of New China. He expressed the hope that China and Egypt would work together to engage in their respective national construction endeavors, creating a culture that is more solid and glorious than the history of both China and Egypt.

    Among those present at the banquet were Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, Qiao Guanhua, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, and heads of the All-China Journalists Association and various news organizations in Beijing.

### 外交部新闻司司长龚澎  招待埃及新闻工作者代表团

1956-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    外交部新闻司司长龚澎
    招待埃及新闻工作者代表团
    据新华社讯  中华人民共和国外交部新闻司司长龚澎11日晚设宴招待以阿卜杜勒·莫内姆·马茂德·萨韦为首的埃及新闻工作者代表团。
    龚澎在宴会上致词说：中国政府和人民欢迎埃及新闻工作者代表团来中国访问，因为你们不仅代表着埃及新闻界，而且代表着独立了的埃及人民对中国人民的善意和关心。龚澎并且希望中埃两国今后有更多、更经常的新闻交流，以加深中埃两国人民的了解。
    萨韦在宴会上讲话时，赞扬新中国的建设，他表示希望中埃两国并肩努力从事本国的建设事业，创造出比中埃两国历史上更坚实更光辉的文化。
    参加今晚宴会作陪的，有外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华，对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经，中国人民外交学会副会长乔冠华、秘书长吴茂荪，中国新闻工作者联谊会和北京各新闻机关负责人等。
