Title: Xinjiang to Establish a Batch of State-Owned Pastures
Author:
Date: 1956-01-08
Page: 2

The main pastoral areas of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region—the Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture and the Bortala Mongol Autonomous Prefecture—are actively preparing to establish 15 state-owned pastures. These 15 pastures will have 150,000 to 200,000 head of livestock. At present, the herds have been concentrated in the winter pastures and have begun to be grazed in an organized manner. (Xinjiang Daily Editorial Department)

### 新疆筹建一批国营牧场

1956-01-08
第2版()
专栏：

    新疆筹建一批国营牧场
    新疆维吾尔自治区的主要牧区——伊犁哈萨克族自治州和博尔塔拉蒙古族自治州，正在积极筹建15个国营牧场。这15个牧场将拥有15万到20万头牲畜。目前，畜群已经分别集中在冬牧场上，开始有组织地进行牧放。　（新疆日报编辑部）
