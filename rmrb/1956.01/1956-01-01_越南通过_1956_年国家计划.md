Title: Vietnam Passes "1956 National Plan"
Author:
Date: 1956-01-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 31st December. The Vietnamese newspaper "People's Daily" published on December 31st the communique of the Council of Ministers of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam regarding the "1956 National Plan" of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

　　The communique said that in late December 1955, the Council of Ministers held a special meeting to discuss and approve the "1956 National Plan" of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The purpose of this plan is to basically restore the national economy to pre-war levels and to initially develop culture. This plan is formulated according to the development path of the people's democratic system, the program of the Vietnamese Fatherland Front, and the requirements of the Vietnamese people's political struggle for peaceful reunification, independence, and democracy.

　　The communique said that the "1956 National Plan" is the first plan of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. This plan will bring benefits to people of all strata. It plays a role in consolidating and expanding the Vietnamese Fatherland Front, consolidating the people's democratic system, and will further enhance the influence and prestige of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam at home and abroad.

　　The communique pointed out that the Council of Ministers believes that in order to realize the 1956 plan, we will encounter many difficulties, but these difficulties are temporary. At the same time, we also have many favorable conditions, and these favorable conditions are fundamental. Vietnam has abundant resources, the Vietnamese people are brave and hardworking, and we also have the assistance of fraternal countries. We will develop favorable conditions and overcome difficulties to complete the plan.

### 越南通过“1956年国家计划”

1956-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南通过“1956年国家计划”
    新华社河内31日电　越南“人民报”12月31日刊载了越南民主共和国部长会议关于越南民主共和国“1956年国家计划”的公报。
    公报说，1955年12月下旬，部长会议召开了一次特别会议，讨论并且通过了越南民主共和国的“1956年国家计划”，这个计划的目的在于使国民经济基本上恢复到战前水平并且初步发展文化。这一计划是根据人民民主制度的发展路线，根据越南祖国战线纲领和根据越南民族争取和平统一独立、民主的政治斗争的要求而制定的。
    公报说“1956年国家计划”是越南民主共和国的第一个计划。这一计划将给各阶层人民带来利益。它起着巩固并且扩大越南祖国战线、巩固人民民主制度的作用，并且将会进一步在国内和世界上增强越南民主共和国的影响和威望。
    公报指出，部长会议认为：为了实现1956年的计划，我们是会碰到很多困难的，但是困难是暂时的。同时我们也具备很多有利条件，而有利条件是基本的。越南有丰富的资源，越南人民勇敢、勤劳，又得到兄弟国家的援助，我们是会发展有利条件克服困难来完成计划的。
