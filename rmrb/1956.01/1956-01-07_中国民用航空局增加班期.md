Title: Civil Aviation Administration of China Increases Flights
Author:
Date: 1956-01-07
Page: 2
 
    The Civil Aviation Administration of China will add direct flights from Beijing to Xi'an to Chongqing to Kunming starting from January this year. The Beijing-Shenyang-Harbin route will be extended to Qiqihar, and the number of flights from Beijing to Guangzhou, Lanzhou, and Urumqi will be increased to four flights per week for each destination. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中国民用航空局增加班期

1956-01-07
第2版()
专栏：

    中国民用航空局增加班期
    中国民用航空局从今年1月起增开北京—西安—重庆—昆明直达航班，并且把北京—沈阳—哈尔滨航线延长到齐齐哈尔，把北京到广州、兰州和乌鲁木齐等地的航班增加为每周各四班。　　　　　　（新华社）
