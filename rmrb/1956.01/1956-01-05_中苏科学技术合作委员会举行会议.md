Title: Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee Holds Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1956-01-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 4th. The Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee held its third meeting in Moscow in December 1955. The meeting discussed the mutual exchange of scientific and technological achievements between China and the Soviet Union and the exchange of technical experience between various sectors of the national economy of the two countries.

According to the meeting's resolutions, the Soviet Union will provide China with design data for non-ferrous metallurgical enterprises, coal industry enterprises, and railway building construction; manufacturing blueprints for blast furnace equipment, mineral processing equipment, pumps and compressors, hoisting and transportation machinery, crushing and grinding machinery, various types of agricultural machinery, and chemical industry equipment; and technical data on the technological processes for producing various dyes, pigments, and other chemical products.

The People's Republic of China will provide the Soviet Union with technical data on the production of some chemical products, technical data on pharmaceutical manufacturing processes, manufacturing blueprints for certain equipment in the textile and tobacco industries and construction machinery, seed samples, and certain experimental specimens.

In addition to the mutual exchange of technical data, the Soviet Union also agreed to China sending experts to the Soviet Union to study the experience and achievements in metallurgy, coal, petroleum, textiles, construction, and other sectors. At the same time, China also agreed to the Soviet Union sending experts to China to study the experience and achievements in the cultivation techniques of various crops, as well as research work in medicine, physiology, biochemistry, astronomy, and other fields.

### 中苏科学技术合作委员会举行会议

1956-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏科学技术合作委员会举行会议
    新华社莫斯科4日电　中苏科学技术合作委员会1955年12月在莫斯科举行了第三次会议。会议讨论了中苏相互交流科学技术成就和两国国民经济各部门之间交流技术经验的问题。
    根据会议决议，苏联将供给中国有色冶金企业、煤炭工业企业和铁路建筑物建设的设计资料，高炉设备、选矿设备、泵和压缩机、起重运输机、破碎碾磨机、各种类型的农业机器和化学工业设备的制造图纸，生产各种染料、颜料和其他化学制品的工艺过程的技术资料。
    中华人民共和国将供给苏联部分化学制品生产的技术资料，医药制造工艺的技术资料，纺织、烟草工业的某些设备和建筑机器的制造图纸，种子样品和某些实验标本。
    除相互交流技术资料外，苏联还同意中国派遣专家到苏联考察冶金、煤炭、石油、纺织、建筑和建设等部门的经验和成就；同时，中国也同意苏联派遣专家到中国考察各种农作物的栽培技术经验和成就，以及医药、生理、生物化学、天文和其他方面的研究工作。
