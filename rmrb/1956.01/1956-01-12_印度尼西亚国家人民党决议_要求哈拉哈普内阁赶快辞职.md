Title: Indonesian National People's Party Resolution Demands Harahap Cabinet Resign Quickly
Author:
Date: 1956-01-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, January 10th: According to a January 9th report by Antara News Agency, the National People's Party, a member of the Harahap Cabinet, passed a resolution at its recent Third Congress stating that the National People's Party Congress has absolutely no confidence in the Harahap Cabinet. The resolution states that the policies adopted by this cabinet are not beneficial to the country. The Congress demanded that the cabinet resign within a week. If the cabinet has not resigned within this period, the National People's Party will withdraw its ministers, and if these ministers refuse to resign, the National People's Party will terminate their party membership.

### 印度尼西亚国家人民党决议  要求哈拉哈普内阁赶快辞职

1956-01-12
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国家人民党决议
    要求哈拉哈普内阁赶快辞职
    据新华社雅加达10日电  据安塔拉通讯社1月9日报道，参加哈拉哈普内阁的国家人民党在最近举行的第三次代表大会上通过决议说，国家人民党代表大会绝对不信任哈拉哈普内阁。决议说，这个内阁所采取的方针对国家没有好处。代表大会要求内阁在一星期内辞职。如果内阁在这个期限内还没有辞职，那么国家人民党就要撤回它的部长，而如果这些部长拒绝辞职，国家人民党就要停止他们的党籍。
