Title: China and Burma Sign Contract for Purchase and Sale of Burmese Rice
Author:
Date: 1956-01-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 20th: Representatives of the China National Cereals, Oils and Foodstuffs Export Corporation and the Burmese State Agricultural Marketing Board signed a contract in Rangoon on the afternoon of the 19th for the purchase and sale of Burmese rice. This contract was signed in accordance with the barter protocol between the governments of China and Burma regarding the exchange of Burmese rice for Chinese export commodities in 1955-1956.
    The contract was signed by Bi Kejing, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, and U Ko Gyi, Director of the Burmese State Agricultural Marketing Board.

### 中缅签订购买和出售缅甸大米的合同

1956-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中缅签订购买和出售缅甸大米的合同
    新华社仰光20日电　中国粮谷油脂出口公司和缅甸联邦国营农产品贸易局的代表19日下午在仰光签订了一项购买和出售缅甸大米的合同。这个合同是根据中缅两国政府关于1955—1956年缅甸大米同中国出口商品换货议定书而签订的。
    在合同上签字的是中国驻缅甸大使馆商务参赞毕可敬和缅甸联邦国营农产品贸易局局长吴哥季。
