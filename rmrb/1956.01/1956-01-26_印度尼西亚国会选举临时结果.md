Title: Provisional Results of Indonesian Parliamentary Elections
Author:
Date: 1956-01-26
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, January 25th: According to the Indonesian News Agency's January 24th statistics on the provisional results of the parliamentary elections, in the new parliament, the Indonesian Nationalist Party will obtain 49 seats, the Masyumi Party 43 seats, the Indonesian Islamic Teachers Association 37 seats, and the Communist Party 34 seats.
　　The Indonesian "People's Daily" estimated on January 25th that, excluding the results from Central Sumatra and the Central and Northern Sulawesi regions, the Indonesian Communist Party would obtain 34 seats.

### 印度尼西亚国会选举临时结果

1956-01-26
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国会选举临时结果
    新华社雅加达25日电　据印度尼西亚新闻社1月24日对于国会选举临时结果的统计，在新国会中，印度尼西亚国民党将获得49席，马斯友美党43席，印度尼西亚回教师联合会37席，共产党34席。
    印度尼西亚“人民日报”25日估计，不把苏门答腊中部以及苏拉威西中部和北部各区的结果计算在内，印度尼西亚共产党将得到34席。
