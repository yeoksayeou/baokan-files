Title: Beijing Parachute Tower Holds Completion Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1956-01-16
Page: 3

Beijing Parachute Tower Holds Completion Ceremony

Beijing’s first parachute tower held its completion ceremony on the afternoon of the 15th. Over 10,000 people from all walks of life in Beijing attended the ceremony.

At the completion ceremony, 13 male and female parachute athletes from the Central National Defense Sports Club performed sensory parachuting, helicopter parachuting, tower gate parachuting, and stunt parachuting, respectively. (Xinhua News Agency) (Attached picture)

Athletes from the Central National Defense Sports Club are performing individual helicopter parachuting. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao [photograph]

### 北京跳伞塔举行落成典礼

1956-01-16
第3版()
专栏：

    北京跳伞塔举行落成典礼
    北京市的第一座跳伞塔15日下午举行了落成典礼，参加典礼的有北京各界人士1万多人。
    在落成典礼上，中央国防体育俱乐部的13名男、女跳伞运动员分别表演了感性跳伞、直升跳伞、塔门跳伞和特技跳伞。　（新华社）（附图片）
    中央国防体育俱乐部的运动员正在进行直升个人跳伞表演。　新华社记者　刘东鳌摄
