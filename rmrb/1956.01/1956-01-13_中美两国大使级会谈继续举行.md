Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1956-01-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, January 12th: The Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks held their 32nd meeting at the Palais des Nations at 10:00 a.m. on January 12th. The meeting ended at 1:20 p.m. The next meeting will be held on January 19th.

### 中美两国大使级会谈继续举行

1956-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中美两国大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦12日电  中美两国大使级会谈1月12日上午10时在国联大厦举行第32次会议，会议在下午1时20分结束，下次会议将在1月19日举行。
