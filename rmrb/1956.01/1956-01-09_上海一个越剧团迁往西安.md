Title: Shanghai Yue Opera Troupe Moves to Xi'an
Author:
Date: 1956-01-09
Page: 3

    Shanghai, January 5th (Xinhua News Agency) - The Shanghai Private Professional Opera Troupe, the Xin Xin Yue Opera Troupe, moved from Shanghai to Xi'an on the 5th. This is the first professional opera troupe in Shanghai to relocate according to the needs of inland construction. In the future, they will tour Xi'an, Lanzhou, Baoji and other places to perform for workers.
    The troupe has more than 60 people, and they are all very happy to go to Xi'an and other places to serve the industrial construction cause.

### 上海一个越剧团迁往西安

1956-01-09
第3版()
专栏：

    上海一个越剧团迁往西安
    上海市民间职业剧团新新越剧团5日从上海迁往西安。这是上海专业剧团中根据内地建设事业发展的需要而外迁的第一个剧团。他们今后将在西安、兰州、宝鸡等地为工人作巡回演出。
    这个剧团共有60多人，他们对前往西安等地为工业建设事业服务，都很高兴。（新华社）
