Title: German Democratic Republic Council of Ministers Approves Sino-German Treaty
Author:
Date: 1956-01-05
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 3rd: The Press Office of the Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic announced on January 3rd: Prime Minister Grotewohl presented a report to the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic on January 2nd regarding the visit of the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic to China, North Korea, and Mongolia. The Council of Ministers approved Grotewohl's report. The Council of Ministers also approved the Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation between the German Democratic Republic and China and decided to submit it to the People's Chamber for approval in accordance with constitutional procedures.
    The Council of Ministers also approved the Cultural Cooperation Agreement concluded between the governments of the German Democratic Republic and China, and the Cooperation Agreement on Plant Quarantine and Prevention and Control of Crop Diseases and Insect Pests, as well as the Joint Communiqué issued with the governments of China, North Korea, and Mongolia.

### 民主德国部长会议批准德中条约

1956-01-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　民主德国部长会议批准德中条约
    新华社柏林3日电　德意志民主共和国总理新闻处3日公布：格罗提渥总理在2日向德意志民主共和国部长会议提出关于德意志民主共和国政府代表团在中、朝、蒙三国的访问报告。部长会议批准了格罗提渥的报告。部长会议同时批准了德中友好合作条约，并且决定将它提交人民议院按宪法程序予以批准。
    部长会议还批准了德中两国政府缔结的文化合作协定和关于植物检疫和防治农作物病虫害合作协定以及同中、朝、蒙三国政府发表的联合声明。
