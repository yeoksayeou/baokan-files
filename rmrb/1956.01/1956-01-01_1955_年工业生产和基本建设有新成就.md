Title: New Achievements in Industrial Production and Capital Construction in 1955
Author: 
Date: 1956-01-01
Page: 1

　　1955 witnessed new achievements in industrial production and capital construction in China's third year of implementing the first five-year plan.

　　In terms of industrial production, the total output value plan for 1955 is expected to be fulfilled by 101%. The total output value of the seven industrial ministries is expected to complete 100.41% of the plan. Among them, the Ministry of Coal Industry is expected to reach 101.7%; the Ministry of Electric Power Industry, 107.5%; the Ministry of Petroleum Industry, 109.9%; the Ministry of Heavy Industry, 105.6%; the Ministry of First Machine Building Industry, 107.8%; the Ministry of Textile Industry, 100.9%; and the Ministry of Light Industry, 101.2%.

　　Although industrial production in 1955 was affected by the insufficient supply of raw materials due to the poor agricultural harvest after the severe floods in 1954, the total value of industrial output still increased by 54% compared to 1954, according to incomplete statistics. Among 46 major products, 32 are expected to have completed or exceeded their targets. In accordance with the needs of national socialist construction, industrial enterprises in 1955 extensively trial-produced new types of products. Examples include the 1330-type universal grinder, the 1A62-type lathe, complete sets of 6,000-kilowatt thermal power generators, 40-ton boilers, alloy seamless steel pipes, shipbuilding steel plates, and 31,500-kilovolt-ampere transformers, all of which have been successfully trial-produced. In addition, the vigorous movement to practice strict economy, which was widely launched this year, has also achieved significant results.

　　In terms of capital construction, the total investment in capital construction in 1955 is expected to fulfill 94.9% of the plan. The industrial sector is expected to reach 97.7%. The coal and electricity sectors may even exceed their plans.

　　Key projects above the designated limit that were completed in 1955 include the Tonghua Shi Ren Qi Pit Inclined Shaft, the Jixi Chengzihe Xin Jiu Pit Inclined Shaft, Anshan Iron and Steel's No. 5 blast furnace and four coke ovens, four cotton textile factories including the Beijing State-owned No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory, and the Nanwan Reservoir.

　　In the plan for new production capacity from capital construction in 1955, artificial petroleum, open-hearth steel, and rolled steel are all expected to complete their plans. The new production capacity for electricity is expected to exceed the plan by 12,800 kilowatts; the production capacity for tires is expected to exceed the plan by 112,965 sets.

### 1955年工业生产和基本建设有新成就

1956-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　1955年工业生产和基本建设有新成就
    1955年——我国执行第一个五年计划的第三年，工业生产和基本建设都取得了新的成就。
    在工业生产方面，1955年的总产值计划预计可以完成101％。七个工业部总产值预计完成计划10.41％。其中煤炭工业部为101.7％；电力工业部为107.5％；石油工业部为109.9％；重工业部为105.6％；第一机械工业部为107.8％；纺织工业部为100.9％；轻工业部为101，2％。
    1955年的工业生产虽然受到1954年大水灾后农业歉收，以致原料供应不足的影响，但是工业生产总值据不完全统计还比1954年增长54％。在46种主要产品中，预计有32种主要产品都完成和超额完成任务。根据国家社会主义建设的需要，各工业企业在1955年大量试制了新种类产品。如1330型万能磨床，1A62型车床，6，000瓩火力发电机全套设备，40吨锅炉，合金无缝钢管，造船钢板，31，500千伏安变压器等，都已试制成功。此外，今年广泛开展的厉行节约运动，也取得显著成效。
    在基本建设方面，预计1955年基本建设总投资额可以完成计划94.9％。其中工业部门可以达到97.7％。煤炭、电力部门并可超额完成计划。
    1955年竣工的限额以上的重点工程，有通化石人七坑斜井，鸡西城子河新九井斜井，鞍钢五号高炉和四座炼焦炉，北京国营第二棉纺织厂等四个棉纺织厂，以及南湾水库等。
    在1955年基本建设的新增生产能力计划中，人造石油、平炉钢、轧钢等预计都完成计划。电力的新增生产能力，超额完成12，800瓩；轮胎生产能力，超额完成112，965套。
