Title: Hefei People Celebrate the Victory of Socialist Transformation
Author:
Date: 1956-01-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hefei, 18th: More than 20,000 people from all walks of life in Hefei City held a celebration rally this afternoon to celebrate the great victory of the socialist transformation of Hefei City.

At present, private industrial and commercial enterprises in Hefei City have completed the socialist transformation mainly in the form of joint state-private ownership. All individual handicraft industries in the city have also implemented cooperation, and more than 96% of farmers in the suburbs have participated in advanced agricultural producers' cooperatives of a completely socialist nature.

Li Shinong, Deputy Secretary of the Anhui Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Governor of Anhui Province, and the heads of the Hefei municipal Party and government attended the celebration rally. At the meeting, representatives of workers and staff members of joint state-private enterprises, shop assistants, capitalists, and representatives of members of handicraft production cooperatives and agricultural production cooperatives sent them good news. At the venue, workers, farmers, students and literary and art workers performed dozens of entertainment programs such as flower drum, stilts, land boats, and lion dances. In the evening, about 3,000 workers and staff members of joint state-private enterprises, shop assistants, capitalists, and members of handicraft production cooperatives held a garden party in Xiaoyaojin Park.

### 合肥市人民欢庆社会主义改造的胜利

1956-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    合肥市人民欢庆社会主义改造的胜利
    据新华社合肥18日电　合肥市各界人民两万多人，今天下午举行联欢大会，庆祝合肥市社会主义改造的伟大胜利。
    现在合肥市的私营工商业已经完成了以公私合营为主要形式的社会主义改造，全市的个体手工业也全部实行合作化，郊区有96％以上的农户参加了完全社会主义性质的高级农业生产合作社。
    中共安徽省委副书记、安徽省副省长李世农和合肥市党、政负责人出席了联欢大会。会上，公私合营企业的职工、店员、资本家的代表，手工业生产合作社和农业生产合作社的社员代表分别向他们送了喜报。在会场上，工人、农民、学生和文艺工作者同时演出了花鼓、高蹻、旱船、狮子舞等几十个文娱节目。入夜，全市公私合营企业的工人、店员、资本家和手工业生产合作社的社员约3，000人，在逍遥津公园举行游园联欢。
