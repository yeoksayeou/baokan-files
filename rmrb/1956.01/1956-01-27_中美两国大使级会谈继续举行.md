Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1956-01-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, January 26th. The Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks held their 34th meeting on January 25th. The meeting adjourned at 13:20, and the next meeting will be held on February 3rd.

### 中美两国大使级会谈继续举行

1956-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中美两国大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦26日电　中美两国大使级会谈1月25日举行第34次会议。会议在13时20分休会，下次会议将在2月3日举行。
