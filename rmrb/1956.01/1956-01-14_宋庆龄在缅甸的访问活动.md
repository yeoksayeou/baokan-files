Title: Song Qingling's Visit to Burma

Author:

Date: 1956-01-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency report: Yao Zhongming, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Burma, held a reception on the evening of January 10 to mark Song Qingling's visit to Burma.

Those invited to attend included: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, Chief Justice U Thein Maung, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities of the Union Parliament Sao Hkun Hkio, Speaker of the Chamber of Deputies Bo Mu Aung, Daw Khin Kyi (wife of Aung San), Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs U Khin Maung, Minister of Trade U Ba Swe, Minister of Information U Tun Win, Burmese Ambassador to China U Hla Maung, Chairman of the Burmese Committee for the Defense of World Peace Tekin Kodaw Hmaing, Chairman of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Sin Pho, many foreign envoys and diplomatic officials stationed in Burma, and overseas Chinese leaders, totaling more than 300 people.

On January 11, Song Qingling visited Mandalay. The Commissioner of the Mandalay Division of Burma, U Shin, held a banquet on the evening of January 11 to entertain Song Qingling. U Shin and Song Qingling exchanged speeches at the meeting.

At 4 p.m. on January 12, the Mayor of Mandalay, U Mya, held a tea party in the Mandalay Royal Palace Gardens to welcome Song Qingling. U Mya and Song Qingling exchanged speeches at the tea party.

That evening, Song Qingling had dinner with the Burmese Minister of Home Affairs Bo Khin Maung Gale and others, and then watched Burmese artistic performances.

### 宋庆龄在缅甸的访问活动

1956-01-14
第4版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄在缅甸的访问活动
    据新华社讯  中华人民共和国驻缅甸大使姚仲明1月10日晚间为宋庆龄访问缅甸举行了招待会。
    应邀出席的有：缅甸联邦总理吴努、最高法院院长吴登貌、联邦国会民族院议长萧恢塔、代表院议长波木昂、昂山夫人都庆枝、代理外交部长吴觉迎、贸易部长吴拉实、宣传部长吴顿温、缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂、缅甸保卫世界和平委员会主席德钦哥都迈、缅中友好协会主席信波、许多国家驻缅甸的外交使节和外交官员以及华侨领袖等三百多人。
    1月11日，宋庆龄到曼德勒访问。缅甸曼德勒区专员吴盛却在1月11日晚间举行宴会，招待宋庆龄。吴盛却和宋庆龄在会上相互致词。
    1月12日下午4时曼德勒市市长吴迎在曼德勒皇宫花园里举行茶会欢迎宋庆龄。吴迎和宋庆龄在茶会上相互致词。
    当天晚上，宋庆龄同缅甸内政部长波庆貌格礼等共进晚餐，然后，观看了缅甸艺术表演。
