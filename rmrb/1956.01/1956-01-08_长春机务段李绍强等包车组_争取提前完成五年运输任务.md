Title: Li Shaoqiang and Other Engine Crew Groups of Changchun Locomotive Depot Strive to Complete Five-Year Transportation Tasks Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1956-01-08
Page: 1

    Li Shaoqiang and Other Engine Crew Groups of Changchun Locomotive Depot
　　Strive to Complete Five-Year Transportation Tasks Ahead of Schedule
    The four engine crew groups of Li Shaoqiang, Wang Qishan, Yu Haohai, and Xu Baosen of the Changchun Locomotive Depot recently proposed to strive to complete the transportation tasks stipulated in the first Five-Year Plan seven to ten months ahead of schedule, and to save the country more than 320,000 yuan in funds.
    The National Committee of the Railway Trade Union of China issued a circular on New Year's Day, calling on all railway workers to learn from the four engine crew groups of Li Shaoqiang, etc., and formulate group plans to complete the first Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule.

### 长春机务段李绍强等包车组  争取提前完成五年运输任务

1956-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

    长春机务段李绍强等包车组
　　争取提前完成五年运输任务
    长春机务段李绍强、王岐山、于好海、徐宝森四个包车组，最近提出要争取提前七个月到十个多月完成第一个五年计划规定的运输任务，并且为国家节约资金32万多元。
    中国铁路工会全国委员会元旦发出通报，号召全体铁路职工向李绍强等四个包车组学习，制定提前完成第一个五年计划的小组计划。
