Title: Presidium of the Great People's Hural and Council of Ministers of Mongolia Hold Reception to Celebrate Joint Mongolia-Soviet Union-China Railway Transportation
Author:
Date: 1956-01-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, January 1st: The Presidium of the Great People's Hural and the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic held a grand reception on the evening of December 31st to celebrate the opening of international joint railway transportation between Mongolia, the Soviet Union, and China, and the commencement of operations on the Ulaanbaatar-Zamiin-Uud railway.

    Those attending the reception included: Tsedenbal, Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic; Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party; Zedev, Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia; Shirindib, First Deputy Prime Minister; Maidar, Tsende, and Avirchid, Deputy Prime Ministers; Rabdan, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs, and heads of other ministries.

    Also attending the reception were: the Mongolian government delegation headed by Luvsan, the Soviet government delegation headed by Beshev, the Chinese government delegation headed by Teng Daiyuan, members of the Joint Soviet-Mongolian Government Acceptance Commission, Soviet Ambassador Pisarev, Chinese Ambassador He Ying, Korean Ambassador Hong Dong-cheol, and others.

    Xinhua News Agency, Erenhot, January 1st: Erenhot, a new city on the border between China and Mongolia, officially began providing domestic telegram and long-distance telephone services on January 1st.

### 蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团和部长会议  举行招待会庆祝蒙苏中铁路联运

1956-01-02
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团和部长会议
　　举行招待会庆祝蒙苏中铁路联运
    新华社乌兰巴托1日电　为庆祝蒙苏中三国铁路国际联运通车和乌兰巴托—扎门乌德铁路开始营业，蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团和部长会议在12月31日晚举行盛大招待会。
    出席招待会的有：蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴，蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团副主席泽狄布，第一副总理锡林迪布，副总理迈达尔、曾德、阿维尔齐德，外交部代理部长拉布丹和其他各部负责人。
    出席招待会的还有：以鲁布桑为首的蒙古政府代表团，以别谢夫为首的苏联政府代表团，以滕代远为首的中国政府代表团，苏蒙政府联合验收委员会委员们，苏联大使皮萨列夫，中国大使何英，朝鲜大使洪东哲等。
    新华社二连1日电　中、蒙边疆国境线上的新城市——二连，1日开始正式开办国内电报和长途电话业务。
