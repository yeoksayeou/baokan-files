Title: Guo Moruo and Others Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-01-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Guo Moruo, the head of the Chinese Scientific Delegation to Japan for academic observation and a friendly visit at the invitation of the Science Council of Japan, along with delegation members Feng Naichao, Xiong Fu, Jian Bozan, Mao Yisheng, Xue Yu, Ge Tingsui, and Yin Da, flew from Shanghai via Hangzhou to Beijing on the afternoon of December 31st.
The delegation arrived in Japan on December 1st and spent more than 20 days there.
Welcoming them at the airport were Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, Zhang Jiafu, Tao Menghe, Wu Youxun, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and more than 50 scientists in Beijing.

### 郭沫若等回到北京

1956-01-02
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若等回到北京
    据新华社讯　应日本学术会议的邀请到日本去进行学术视察和友好访问的中国访日科学代表团团长郭沫若和团员冯乃超、熊复、翦伯赞、茅以升、薛愚、葛庭燧和尹达12月31日下午从上海经杭州飞北京。
    代表团是12月1日到达日本的，在日本度过了20多天。
    到机场欢迎的有国务院秘书长习仲勋、中国科学院副院长张稼夫、陶孟和、吴有训以及在北京的科学家等50多人。
