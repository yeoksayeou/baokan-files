Title: Chinese Commercial Representative to Egypt Arrives in Cairo
Author: 
Date: 1956-01-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 16th - According to TASS, personnel from the Commercial Representative Office of the People's Republic of China, led by Commercial Representative Li Yingji, have arrived in Cairo.

According to newspapers, Egyptian Minister of Industry and Commerce Nuseir held a reception on January 15 to welcome the personnel of the Chinese Commercial Representative Office. In a statement to the press, Nuseir emphasized that strengthening trade relations with the People's Republic of China is of great significance to Egypt. He said that the exchange of commercial representatives between Egypt and the People's Republic of China, and the establishment of a permanent commercial representative office of the People's Republic of China in Cairo, "is a positive step towards consolidating the economic relations between the two countries and expanding trade."

### 中国驻埃及商务代表到开罗

1956-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    中国驻埃及商务代表到开罗
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社开罗16日讯：中华人民共和国驻埃及商务代表处人员在李应吉商务代表率领下已经到达开罗。
    据报纸报道，埃及工商部长努赛尔在1月15日举行招待会欢迎中国商务代表处人员。努赛尔在向报界发表的谈话中着重指出，加强同中华人民共和国的贸易关系对于埃及具有很大的意义。他说，埃及和中华人民共和国交换商务代表以及中华人民共和国在开罗开设常驻商务代表处，“是巩固两国之间的经济关系和扩大贸易的一个积极的步骤”。
