Title: Congratulations on the National Day of the Union of Burma
Author:
Date: 1956-01-04
Page: 1

　　Chairman Mao's Message to President Ba U
　　To His Excellency Dr. Ba U, President of the Union of Burma:
　　On the happy occasion of your country's National Day, I extend to Your Excellency and the people of Burma my own and the Chinese people's warm congratulations. I wish the Union of Burma prosperity and the people of Burma happiness, and I wish Your Excellency good health.
                  Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong
                            January 2, 1956, in Beijing
    Premier Zhou's Message to Prime Minister U Nu
　　To His Excellency U Nu, Prime Minister of the Union of Burma:
    On the occasion of the eighth anniversary of the independence of the Union of Burma, I extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the Government of the Union of Burma. I wish the people of Burma even greater achievements in building their country and defending peace in Asia and the world.
    The sincere and friendly relations between China and Burma, built on the solid foundation of the Five Principles, have opened the way for extensive economic and cultural cooperation between our two countries. I hope that in this new year, the friendly relations and cooperation between our two countries will be further consolidated and developed.
            Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai
                            January 2, 1956, in Beijing

### 祝贺缅甸联邦国庆

1956-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　祝贺缅甸联邦国庆
    毛主席致巴宇总统电
　　缅甸联邦总统巴宇博士阁下：
    欣逢贵国国庆，谨向阁下和缅甸人民表示我和中国人民的热烈祝贺。祝缅甸联邦繁荣和缅甸人民幸福，祝阁下身体健康。
                  中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                            1956年1月2日于北京
    周总理致吴努总理电
　　缅甸联邦总理吴努阁下：
    值此缅甸联邦独立八周年之际，我谨向阁下和缅甸联邦政府致诚挚的祝贺。祝缅甸人民在建设自己的国家和保卫亚洲和世界和平的事业中获得更大的成就。
    中缅两国建立在五项原则的巩固基础之上的真诚友好关系，已经打开了我们两国之间广泛的经济和文化合作的道路。我希望，在这新的一年中，我们两国的友好关系和合作将进一步巩固和发展。
            中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                            1956年1月2日于北京
