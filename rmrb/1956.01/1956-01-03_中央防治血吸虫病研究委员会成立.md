Title: Central Research Committee for the Prevention and Control of Schistosomiasis Established
Author:
Date: 1956-01-03
Page: 1

　　The Central Research Committee for the Prevention and Control of Schistosomiasis has been established in Shanghai, and held a meeting from December 26 to 29, 1955. More than 60 professors, staff members of schistosomiasis prevention and control institutes, and experienced doctors of both Chinese and Western medicine from medical colleges, schistosomiasis prevention and control institutes, and related research units in Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Anhui, Jiangxi, Hunan, Shanghai, and Wuhan attended the meeting.

　　The meeting summarized the scientific research achievements in prevention, clinical practice, business research, and traditional Chinese medicine in the prevention and control of schistosomiasis in various regions over the past few years, and decided to compile these research achievements into books to provide a scientific basis for schistosomiasis prevention and control workers in various regions. The meeting believed that scientific workers and cadres for the prevention and control of schistosomiasis must closely cooperate, conduct experiments on the methods for the prevention and control of schistosomiasis, and continue research to achieve the goal of eliminating schistosomiasis nationwide within seven years.

　　The meeting decided to select some areas as pilot areas for research work. The meeting also determined the guiding principles for carrying out scientific research on the prevention and control of schistosomiasis in the future.

　　During the meeting, Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi attended and gave instructions.

### 中央防治血吸虫病研究委员会成立

1956-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央防治血吸虫病研究委员会成立
    本报讯　中央防治血吸虫病研究委员会已经在上海成立，并在1955年12月26日到29日举行了会议。参加这次会议的有江苏、浙江、安徽、江西、湖南、上海、武汉等地的医学院、血吸虫病防治所和有关研究单位的教授、工作人员和对治疗血吸虫病有经验的中西医师等60多人。
    会议总结了过去几年来各地防治血吸虫病的防预、临床、业务研究和中医药方面的科学研究成果，并决定把这些研究成果编写成书，以供各地防治血吸虫病工作者作为科学的根据。会议认为，科学工作者和防治血吸虫病的干部必须密切配合，对防治血吸虫病的方法进行实验，继续研究，以达到七年内在全国消灭血吸虫病的要求。
    会议决定选择一些地区作为进行研究工作的试点。会议并且确定了今后开展防治血吸虫病的科学研究工作的方针。
    会议期间，国务院副总理陈毅曾到会作了指示。
