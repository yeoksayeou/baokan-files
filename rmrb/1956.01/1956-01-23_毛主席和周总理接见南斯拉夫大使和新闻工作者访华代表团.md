Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Receive the Yugoslav Ambassador and the Yugoslav Journalists' Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1956-01-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China and Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council received the Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Mr. Popovic, and his wife, as well as the Yugoslav journalists' delegation to China headed by Mr. Pirpic, Director of the Yugoslav News Agency, on the afternoon of January 22nd.

During the reception, Mr. Popovic presented Chairman Mao Zedong with a gift from the President of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, Josip Broz Tito: a movie projector and two news documentaries.

Also present at the reception were Vice Premiers of the State Council Deng Xiaoping and Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Director of the Chairman's Office Zhang Jingwu, President of the All-China Journalists Association Deng Tuo, Vice Presidents Wu Lengxi, Mei Yi, and Wang Yunsheng, Director of the Protocol Bureau of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Yu Xinqing, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Gong Peng, and Director of the Protocol Department Ke Hua. ### 毛主席和周总理接见南斯拉夫大使和新闻工作者访华代表团

1956-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席和周总理接见南斯拉夫大使和新闻工作者访华代表团
    新华社22日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东和国务院总理周恩来，在22日下午接见了南斯拉夫驻中国大使波波维奇和夫人以及以南斯拉夫通讯社社长皮尔皮奇为首的南斯拉夫新闻工作者访华代表团。
    接见时，波波维奇向毛泽东主席呈献了南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统铁托赠送的礼品——一架电影放映机和两部新闻纪录片。
    接见时在座的，有国务院副总理邓小平、陈毅，外交部副部长张闻天，主席办公厅主任张经武，中国新闻工作者联谊会会长邓拓、副会长吴冷西、梅益、王芸生，全国人民代表大会常务委员会典礼局局长余心清，外交部新闻司司长龚澎、礼宾司司长柯华。（附图片）
    毛泽东主席和周恩来总理同南斯拉夫大使波波维奇和新闻工作者访华代表团人员谈话。　新华社记者邹健东摄
