Title: Chinese People's Volunteer Army Officers and Soldiers Enthusiastically Subscribe to 1956 National Bonds
Author:
Date: 1956-01-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 6th - Officers and soldiers of all units of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army are enthusiastically subscribing to the 1956 National Economic Construction Bonds.

Officers and soldiers of the Volunteer Army's leadership organs began subscribing to the bonds on the 3rd. So far, most units have exceeded their original subscription plans. Officers and soldiers of the leadership organ of a certain unit of the Volunteer Army stationed on the west coast, within half an hour of the start of bond subscriptions, had subscribed to more than twice the expected amount.

### 中国人民志愿军官兵  踊跃认购1956年公债

1956-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民志愿军官兵
　　踊跃认购1956年公债
    新华社平壤6日电　中国人民志愿军各部队的军官和士兵踊跃认购1956年国家经济建设公债。
    志愿军领导机关的军官和士兵在3日开始认购公债，到目前为止，大部分单位已经超额完成原定的认购计划。驻西海岸的志愿军某部领导机关的官兵，在开始认购公债后的半小时，认购的总数就超过了预计认购数额一倍以上。
