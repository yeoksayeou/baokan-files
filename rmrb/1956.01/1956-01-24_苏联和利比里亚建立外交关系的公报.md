Title: Communiqué on the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between the Soviet Union and Liberia
Author:
Date: 1956-01-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th, TASS dispatch: The joint communiqué of the Soviet government delegation and the Government of the Republic of Liberia on the issue of establishing diplomatic relations between the Soviet Union and the Republic of Liberia is as follows:

The government delegation of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, headed by A.P. Volkov, Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR (this delegation was in Monrovia, the capital of the Republic of Liberia, to attend the inauguration ceremony of President William V.S. Tubman) and the Government of the Republic of Liberia discussed the issue of establishing diplomatic relations between the Soviet Union and the Republic of Liberia and exchanging diplomatic missions at the ambassadorial level.

Both sides agreed that the establishment of normal diplomatic relations between the Soviet Union and the Republic of Liberia is beneficial to both countries. Both sides expressed that they jointly believe that relations between countries must be based on the principles of equality, non-interference in internal affairs, non-aggression, mutual respect for territorial integrity and sovereignty, and national independence.

Both sides agreed that the establishment of embassies by the Soviet Union and the Republic of Liberia will be carried out after the Government of the Republic of Liberia obtains the approval of the Liberian legislature and the necessary budget appropriations.

A.P. Volkov, head of the Soviet government delegation, and Mo Dukuli, Acting Secretary of State of the Republic of Liberia, exchanged notes on the issues discussed.

The talks were conducted in a warm and mutually understanding atmosphere.

### 苏联和利比里亚建立外交关系的公报

1956-01-24
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和利比里亚建立外交关系的公报
    新华社讯　塔斯社莫斯科20日电：苏联政府代表团和利比里亚共和国政府之间关于苏联和利比里亚共和国建立外交关系问题的谈判的联合公报如下：
    以苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席亚·彼·沃尔科夫为首的苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府代表团（这个代表团是到利比里亚共和国首都蒙罗维亚参加威廉·瓦·杜伯曼总统的就职典礼的）和利比里亚共和国政府讨论了苏联和利比里亚共和国建立外交关系和互派大使级外交使团的问题。
    双方一致认为，建立苏联和利比里亚共和国之间的正常外交关系对于两国是有益的。双方表示，它们共同认为：各国之间的关系必须以平等、互不干涉内政、互不侵犯、互相尊重领土完整和主权和民族独立的原则为依据。
    双方同意，苏联和利比里亚共和国互设大使馆的工作将在利比里亚共和国政府得到了利比里亚立法机构的批准和必要的预算拨款之后进行。
    苏联政府代表团团长亚·彼·沃尔科夫和利比里亚共和国代理国务卿摩·杜库利就所讨论的问题交换了照会。
    会谈是在热烈和互相谅解的气氛中进行的。
