Title: Final Communiqué of the Meeting of the Political Consultative Committee of the Warsaw Treaty Member States
Author:
Date: 1956-01-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 29th (TASS dispatch): Final Communiqué of the Meeting of the Political Consultative Committee of Warsaw Treaty Member States:

The Political Consultative Committee of the Warsaw Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance, concluded in Warsaw on May 14, 1955, held a meeting in Prague on January 27 and 28, 1956.

The following representatives participated in the Political Consultative Committee's discussions:

Representing the People's Republic of Albania: Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Mehmet Shehu, and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense, Lieutenant General Beqir Balluku;

Representing the People's Republic of Bulgaria: Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Raiko Damyanov, and Minister of National Defense, General Peter Panchevski;

Representing the People's Republic of Hungary: Chairman of the Council of Ministers, András Hegedüs, and Minister of National Defense, Colonel General István Bata;

Representing the German Democratic Republic: First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Walter Ulbricht, and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense, General Willi Stoph;

Representing the Polish People's Republic: Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Józef Cyrankiewicz, and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Minister of National Defense, Marshal of Poland, Konstanty Rokossowski;

Representing the Romanian People's Republic: Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Chivu Stoica, and Minister of the Armed Forces, Colonel General Leontin Sălăjan;

Representing the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics: First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Vyacheslav Molotov, and Minister of National Defense, Marshal of the Soviet Union, Georgy Zhukov;

Representing the Czechoslovak Republic: First Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Defense, General Alexei Čepička, and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Václav David.

Marshal Nie Rongzhen, Vice-Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China, participated in the meeting as an observer from the People's Republic of China.

The meeting was chaired by Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Albania.

The meeting, upon the recommendation of Marshal of the Soviet Union I.S. Konev, Commander-in-Chief of the Joint Armed Forces, reviewed and approved the regulations of the Joint Command and resolved organizational issues related to the activities of the Joint Armed Forces of the Warsaw Treaty member states.

The meeting accepted the proposal of the delegation of the German Democratic Republic that, after the establishment of the National People's Army of the German Democratic Republic, a portion of its armed forces would be incorporated into the Joint Armed Forces. The meeting also decided that the Minister of National Defense of the German Democratic Republic would, according to mutually agreed procedures, become one of the assistants to the Commander-in-Chief of the Joint Armed Forces of the Warsaw Treaty member states.

In accordance with the provisions of the Warsaw Treaty regarding consultations among the contracting states on international issues affecting their common interests, the Political Consultative Committee studied the international situation and reached conclusions as stated in the Declaration of the Warsaw Treaty Member States signed by the representatives of the contracting states. The text of the declaration will be published separately.

Certain organizational aspects related to the work of the Political Consultative Committee have been reviewed.

The meeting decided that the Political Consultative Committee would hold meetings as necessary, but no less than twice a year, to review issues arising from the implementation of the Warsaw Treaty.

The chairmanship of each meeting during the year will be held in rotation by the representatives of the various countries on this Committee.

The following auxiliary bodies will be established under the Political Consultative Committee, with their headquarters in Moscow: 1. A standing committee responsible for drafting recommendations on foreign policy issues;
2. A Joint Secretariat composed of representatives from all Warsaw Treaty member states.

### 华沙条约缔约国政治协商委员会会议最后公报

1956-01-30
第4版()
专栏：

    华沙条约缔约国政治协商委员会会议最后公报
    新华社29日讯　塔斯社布拉格29日电：华沙友好合作互助条约缔约国政治协商委员会会议最后公报：
    1955年5月14日在华沙缔结的友好合作互助条约缔约国政治协商委员会于1956年1月27日和28日在布拉格举行了会议。
    参加政治协商委员会讨论的有下列代表：
    代表阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国的是部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡和部长会议第一副主席兼国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库中将；
    代表保加利亚人民共和国的是部长会议副主席拉伊科·达米扬诺夫和国防部长彼奥特·潘切夫斯基大将；
    代表匈牙利人民共和国的是部长会议主席安德拉希·赫格居斯和国防部长伊斯特万·巴塔上将；
    代表德意志民主共和国的是部长会议第一副主席瓦尔特·乌布利希和部长会议副主席兼国防部长威利·斯多夫上将；
    代表波兰人民共和国的是部长会议主席约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹和部长会议副主席、国防部长、波兰元帅康斯坦蒂·罗科索夫斯基；
    代表罗马尼亚人民共和国的是部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡和武装部队部长利昂廷·萨拉扬上将；
    代表苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟的是部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长维亚切斯拉夫·莫洛托夫和国防部长、苏联元帅格奥尔基·朱可夫；
    代表捷克斯洛伐克共和国的是政府第一副总理兼国防部长阿列克谢·契皮茨卡大将和外交部长瓦茨拉夫·戴维。
    中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席聂荣臻元帅作为中华人民共和国观察员参加了会议。
    会议由阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡主持。
    会议根据联合武装部队总司令、苏联元帅伊·斯·科涅夫的建议，审议并且通过了联合司令部的章程，并且解决了同华沙条约缔约国联合武装部队的活动有关的组织问题。
    会议接受了德意志民主共和国代表团的建议：在德意志民主共和国国家人民军成立以后，它的一部分武装部队将编入联合武装部队。会议还决定，德意志民主共和国国防部长将根据一致协议的程序成为华沙条约缔约国联合武装部队总司令的助手之一。
    根据华沙条约关于缔约各国就影响它们的共同利益的国际问题彼此进行磋商的规定，政治协商委员会研究了国际局势，并且得出了在缔约各国代表所签署的华沙条约缔约国宣言中所说明的结论。宣言本文将另行单独发表。
    某些同政治协商委员会的工作有关的组织方面的问题，已经加以审议。
    会议决定，政治协商委员会将在必要时举行会议，但是每年不得少于两次，以审议执行华沙条约所产生的问题。
    历年中的各次会议的主席，将由本委员会的各国代表轮流担任。
    政治协商委员会之下将设立下列辅助机构，这些机构的总部将设在莫斯科：一，负责拟定关于外交政策问题的建议的常设委员会；
    二，由所有华沙条约缔约国的代表组成的联合秘书处。
