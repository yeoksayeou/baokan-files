Title: American Plane Drops Bomb in Britain
Author:
Date: 1956-01-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 5th (TASS): Agence France-Presse correspondent in London, according to a statement by a U.S. Air Force spokesman, reported that a bomb "accidentally dropped by a U.S. aircraft" exploded near Newport (Essex County, England) on the 4th.

    The explosion created a large crater, and glass from many houses was shattered and scattered. Some people were injured.

### 美国飞机在英国掉落炸弹

1956-01-10
第4版()
专栏：

    美国飞机在英国掉落炸弹
    新华社讯　塔斯社巴黎5日电：法新社驻伦敦记者根据美国空军发言人的谈话报道说，4日在新港（英国埃赛克斯郡）附近“一架美国飞机偶然掉落的”一枚炸弹爆炸。
    爆炸后炸了一个很大的弹坑，许多房屋的玻璃飞扬四处。有一些人受伤。
