Title: GDR Delegation Returns to Berlin
Author:
Date: 1956-01-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 31st. The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, led by Prime Minister Grotewohl, returned to Berlin by plane at 2 p.m. on December 30th after visiting the People's Republic of China, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and the Mongolian People's Republic.

　　A welcoming rally was held at the airport. Chairman Dieckmann of the Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, in his speech, emphasized the great significance of this visit by the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, saying that this visit was a major contribution to further strengthening the friendship between the German people and the people of China, Korea, and Mongolia, and to enhancing the international prestige of the German Democratic Republic.

　　Prime Minister Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic, in his speech, conveyed the greetings of the Chinese, Korean, and Mongolian peoples to the German people. He spoke about his impressions of visiting these three Asian countries and about the achievements of the Chinese, Korean, and Mongolian peoples in peaceful construction. Grotewohl emphasized that the people of these countries harbor friendship and sympathy towards the German Democratic Republic.

### 民主德国代表团回到柏林

1956-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　民主德国代表团回到柏林
    新华社柏林31日电　由格罗提渥总理率领的德意志民主共和国政府代表团在访问了中华人民共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和蒙古人民共和国之后，已经在12月30日下午2时乘飞机回到柏林。
    在机场上举行了欢迎集会。德意志民主共和国人民议院主席团主席狄克曼在讲话中，着重指出德意志民主共和国政府代表团这次访问的重大意义，他说，这次访问是对进一步加强德国人民同中国、朝鲜和蒙古人民的友谊、加强德意志民主共和国的国际威望的重大贡献。
    德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥在讲话中向德国人民转达了中国、朝鲜和蒙古人民的问候，他谈到他访问这三个亚洲国家的印象，谈到中国、朝鲜和蒙古人民在和平建设方面的成就。格罗提渥着重指出，这些国家的人民对德意志民主共和国怀着友情和同情。
