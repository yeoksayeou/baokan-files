Title: Burmese President Ba U Hosts Banquet to Welcome Soong Ching Ling
Author:
Date: 1956-01-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 3rd: Burmese Federal President Ba U hosted a banquet on the evening of the 2nd to welcome Soong Ching Ling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, who is visiting Burma.

Those attending the banquet included Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, Chief Justice U Thein Maung, Judge U Min Thein, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities of the Burmese Federal Parliament Sao Hkun Hkio, Speaker of the Chamber of Deputies Bo Mu Aung, Daw Khin Kyi (wife of Aung San), Minister for Land Nationalization and Local Administration Democratization Tekin Chin, Minister of Agriculture and Forestry Tekin Kyaw Tun, Minister of Trade U Ra Shih, and Minister of Information U Tun Win, among others.

Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming also attended the banquet.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 3rd: Accompanied by Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming, Soong Ching Ling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic, visited Burmese Prime Minister U Nu on the 3rd at noon and had lunch with U Nu at the Prime Minister's residence.

That morning, Soong Ching Ling also visited the Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities of the Burmese Federal Parliament Sao Hkun Hkio and the Speaker of the Chamber of Deputies Bo Mu Aung.

At 9:00 a.m., she laid a wreath at the tomb of General Aung San.

### 缅甸总统巴宇设宴欢迎宋庆龄

1956-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸总统巴宇设宴欢迎宋庆龄
    新华社仰光3日电　缅甸联邦总统巴宇在2日晚举行宴会，欢迎到缅甸访问的中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄。
    出席宴会的有缅甸总理吴努，最高法院院长吴登貌、法官吴敏登，缅甸联邦国会民族院议长萧恢塔，国会代表院议长波木昂，昂山夫人都庆枝，土地国有化和地方行政民主化部长德钦阵，农林部长德钦觉顿，贸易部长吴拉实和宣传部长吴顿温等。
    中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明也出席了宴会。
    新华社仰光3日电　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄在3日中午由中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明陪同，访问了缅甸总理吴努，并且在总理官邸同吴努共进午餐。
    当天上午，宋庆龄还访问了缅甸联邦国会民族院议长萧恢塔和国会代表院议长波木昂。
    上午九时，她在昂山将军的陵墓前献了花圈。
