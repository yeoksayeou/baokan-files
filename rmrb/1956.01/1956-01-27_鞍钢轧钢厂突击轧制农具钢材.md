Title: Anshan Iron and Steel Works Rushes to Roll Agricultural Implement Steel

Author:
Date: 1956-01-27
Page: 1

Anshan Iron and Steel Works Rushes to Roll Agricultural Implement Steel
    The various steel rolling mills of Anshan Iron and Steel Company are rushing to roll various steel materials needed for manufacturing agricultural implements. This includes steel that can be used to manufacture a large number of double-wheeled double-shared plows, water pumps, boiler-powered machines, and other agricultural implements.
    In order to meet the needs of agricultural implement factories in various regions for steel materials needed to manufacture new-style agricultural implements, Anshan Iron and Steel Company took emergency measures on the 23rd and 24th, mobilizing several steel rolling mills to rush to roll agricultural implement steel.
    In February, Anshan Iron and Steel will also supply several thousand tons of steel for manufacturing agricultural implements to agricultural implement factories nationwide, and is also preparing to supply steel for manufacturing telephone line poles and seamless steel pipes for drilling. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 鞍钢轧钢厂突击轧制农具钢材

1956-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

    鞍钢轧钢厂突击轧制农具钢材
    鞍山钢铁公司各轧钢厂正在突击轧制制造农具所需要的各种钢材。其中包括可以制造大批双轮双铧犁、抽水机、锅驼机以及其他农具的钢材。
    鞍山钢铁公司为了满足各地农具厂制造新式农具所需要的钢材。23日和24日采取紧急措施，动员了几个轧钢厂突击轧制农具钢材。
    2月份，鞍钢还将要供给全国农具厂几千吨制造农具的钢材，并且准备供给能够制造架电话线用的钢材和钻井用的无缝钢管。（据新华社讯）
