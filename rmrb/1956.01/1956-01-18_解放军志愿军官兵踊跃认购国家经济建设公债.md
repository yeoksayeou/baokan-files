Title: PLA and CPV Officers and Soldiers Enthusiastically Subscribe to National Economic Construction Bonds
Author:
Date: 1956-01-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 16th: The broad masses of officers and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army (PLA) are enthusiastically subscribing to the 1956 National Economic Construction Bonds.

    In the PLA units stationed in Beijing, many units have already exceeded their pre-determined subscription quotas. According to statistics from the General Staff Department, the General Political Department, and three other units, by January 11th, officers and soldiers had subscribed to a total of 180,000 yuan in bonds. The subscription figure for a nursing school stationed in Nanjing even reached 700% of the pre-determined figure.

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 15th: Many units of the Chinese People's Volunteers (CPV) have already concluded their subscription work for the 1956 National Economic Construction Bonds, with the vast majority of units exceeding their original subscription plans. Many officers are now making payments. Among those in the Logistics Department's directly affiliated units who have subscribed to bonds, half have already made their payments.

### 解放军志愿军官兵踊跃认购国家经济建设公债

1956-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军志愿军官兵踊跃认购国家经济建设公债
    据新华社16日讯　中国人民解放军各部队广大官兵热烈认购1956年国家经济建设公债。
    中国人民解放军驻北京各机关、部队，到目前止，已经有许多单位超额完成预定认购的数额。据总参谋部、总政治部等5个单位统计，到11日止，官兵们一共认购了18万元公债。驻南京某护士学校认购数字甚至达到预定数字的700％。
    据新华社平壤15日电　中国人民志愿军的许多单位已经结束了认购1956年国家经济建设公债的工作，绝大部分单位超额完成原订认购计划。现在很多军官正在缴款。后勤部直属单位认购公债的人中，有一半缴了款。
