Title: Heads of Mongolian and Soviet Government Delegations and Ambassadors of Both Countries Hold Reception
Author:
Date: 1956-01-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The head of the Mongolian government delegation, Rubsan, the head of the Soviet government delegation, Beshev, the Soviet ambassador to China, Yudin, and the Mongolian ambassador to China, Ochirbat, jointly held a reception on the evening of the 11th.

Invited to attend the reception were Deng Xiaoping and Li Xiannian, Vice Premiers of the State Council of the People's Republic of China; Wang Shoudao, Director of the Sixth Office; Teng Daiyuan, Minister of Railways; Lü Zhengcao, Vice Minister; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Wang Zhen, Commander of the Railway Corps of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; and heads of relevant departments.

Members of the Mongolian and Soviet government delegations attended the reception.

At the reception, Soviet Ambassador Yudin, entrusted by Mongolian Ambassador Ochirbat and in his own name, first rose to deliver a speech. Subsequently, Rubsan, head of the Mongolian government delegation, Beshev, head of the Soviet government delegation, and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, spoke successively. They congratulated the further development and consolidation of friendship between the Soviet Union, Mongolia, and China, and wished the people of the three countries new achievements in their construction endeavors.

### 蒙苏政府代表团团长和两国大使举行招待会

1956-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙苏政府代表团团长和两国大使举行招待会
    新华社11日讯  蒙古政府代表团团长鲁布桑、苏联政府代表团团长别谢夫、苏联驻中国大使尤金和蒙古驻中国大使奥其尔巴特，11日晚间联合举行招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的，有中华人民共和国国务院副总理邓小平、李先念，第六办公室主任王首道，铁道部部长滕代远、副部长吕正操，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长雷任民和中国人民解放军铁道兵司令员王震，以及有关各部门的负责人等。
    蒙古和苏联两国政府代表团团员都出席了招待会。
    招待会上，苏联大使尤金受蒙古大使奥其尔巴特的委托和以他本人的名义首先起立致词。接着，蒙古政府代表团团长鲁布桑、苏联政府代表团团长别谢夫和中华人民共和国国务院副总理邓小平先后讲话。他们祝贺苏、蒙、中三国之间的友谊进一步发展和巩固，并预祝三国人民在建设事业中获得新的成就。
