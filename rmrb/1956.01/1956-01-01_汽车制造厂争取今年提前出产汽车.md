Title: Automobile Factory Strives to Produce Cars Ahead of Schedule This Year
Author:
Date: 1956-01-01
Page: 1

The First Automobile Manufacturing Plant strives to produce cars ahead of schedule this year.

The First Automobile Manufacturing Plant has decided to strive to complete the tasks stipulated in the first Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule and to manufacture a batch of cars before October 1, 1956.

The First Automobile Manufacturing Plant has completed the 1955 capital construction investment plan eight days ahead of schedule; the 1955 equipment installation project and civil construction project plans have been completed ahead of schedule. Now, the factory's equipment investment plan has been completed by more than 80%. The adjustment of production in the eight basic production workshops is proceeding smoothly. On December 24, 1955, the entire factory had adjusted 308 kinds of parts.

At present, the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant is taking effective measures to mobilize all employees to do a good job in material preparation to ensure the smooth installation of the foundry and body stamping workshops, which started construction later. The workers are enthusiastically carrying out labor competitions and striving to guarantee the realization of the plan to produce cars ahead of schedule.
    (Our correspondent in Changchun)

### 汽车制造厂争取今年提前出产汽车

1956-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　汽车制造厂争取今年提前出产汽车
    第一汽车制造厂决定争取提前完成第一个五年计划规定的任务，并要提前在1956年10月1日以前制造出一批汽车。
    第一汽车制造厂已经提前八天完成1955年基本建设投资计划；1955年设备安装工程和土木建筑工程计划都已提前完成。现在全厂的设备投资计划已经完成80％以上。八个基本生产车间调整生产进行很顺利。1955年12月24日，全厂已调整好308种零件。
    现在，第一汽车制造厂正在采取有效措施，动员全体职工做好物资准备工作，保证施工较晚的铸工场和车身压制工场安装工作的顺利进行。职工们热烈开展劳动竞赛，努力保证实现提早出产汽车的计划。
            （本报驻长春记者）
