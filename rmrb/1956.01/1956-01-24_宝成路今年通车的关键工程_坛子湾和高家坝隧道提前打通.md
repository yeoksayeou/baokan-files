Title: Key Projects for the Opening of the Baoji-Chengdu Railway This Year: Tanziawan and Gaojiaba Tunnels Are Completed Ahead of Schedule
Author: Mou Shudong
Date: 1956-01-24
Page: 2

    Key Projects for the Opening of the Baoji-Chengdu Railway This Year:
    Tanziawan and Gaojiaba Tunnels Are Completed Ahead of Schedule
    The Tanziawan Tunnel and the Gaojiaba Tunnel on the Baoji-Lueyang South Section of the Baoji-Chengdu Railway were completed ahead of schedule on the 6th and 10th respectively. Both tunnels are key projects for the opening of the Baoji-Chengdu Railway this year.

### 宝成路今年通车的关键工程  坛子湾和高家坝隧道提前打通

1956-01-24
第2版()
专栏：

    宝成路今年通车的关键工程
    坛子湾和高家坝隧道提前打通
    宝成铁路宝鸡略阳南段坛子湾隧道和高家坝隧道分别在6日和10日提前打通。这两座隧道都是今年宝成铁路通车的关键工程。　（牟蜀东）
