Title: Professor Zheng Zhaozhen Achieves Results in Solving Sedimentation Problems in Harnessing the Yellow River
Author:
Date: 1956-01-16
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency

    Professor Zheng Zhaozhen of the Hydraulic Engineering Department of Tianjin University has completed research on solving the problem of silt deposition in the Yellow River Comprehensive Planning Project. This research result is being tested on a large scale by the Sediment Research Institute of the Yellow River Conservancy Commission in the Yellow River Diversion Project to Weihui.
    Based on experimental results in sediment-laden river models, Professor Zheng Zhaozhen has proposed solutions to issues such as how sediment flows in sediment-laden rivers, where sediment deposits, and the amount of deposition. The success of this research will have significant implications for the Yellow River Comprehensive Planning Project.

### 郑兆珍教授研究在治黄中解决泥沙淤积问题获得成果

1956-01-16
第3版()
专栏：

    郑兆珍教授研究在治黄中解决泥沙淤积问题获得成果
    天津大学水利工程系教授郑兆珍已经研究好解决黄河综合规划工程中泥沙淤积的问题。这项研究成果正由黄河水利委员会泥沙研究所在引黄济卫工程中大规模实验。
    经过在挟沙的河流模型中实验结果，郑兆珍教授提出了解决挟沙河流中的泥沙怎样流动、泥沙在什么地方淤积和淤积的数量等问题。这项研究在实验成功以后，对黄河综合规划工程将有重大的意义。（新华社）
