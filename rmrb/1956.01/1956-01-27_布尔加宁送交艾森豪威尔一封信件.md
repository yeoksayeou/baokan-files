Title: Bulganin Sends Letter to Eisenhower

Author: 
Date: 1956-01-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 26th. TASS, Washington, 26th: Soviet Ambassador to the United States, Zarubin, visited U.S. President Eisenhower at the White House on the 25th and delivered a personal letter from the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin.

The Washington press corps is very interested in Bulganin's letter to the U.S. President. Washington's daily newspapers prominently published news about the letter delivered to Eisenhower.

Following Zarubin's meeting with Eisenhower, the White House then issued the following official statement: "Since the Geneva Conference, the President and Premier Bulganin have been in constant correspondence. The Soviet Ambassador delivered a friendly letter on the morning of January 25 containing several proposals, and the Premier asked the President to further study these proposals to promote universal peace."

### 布尔加宁送交艾森豪威尔一封信件

1956-01-27
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁送交艾森豪威尔一封信件
    新华社26日讯　塔斯社华盛顿26日讯：苏联驻美国大使查鲁宾25日到白宫拜会了美国总统艾森豪威尔，并且交给他苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁的一封私人信件。
    华盛顿新闻界人士对布尔加宁给美国总统的这封信非常感到兴趣。华盛顿各家日报在显著地位刊载了有关交给艾森豪威尔的信件的消息。
    在查鲁宾拜会了艾森豪威尔之后，白宫接着就发表正式声明如下：“在日内瓦会议之后，总统和布尔加宁总理不断地有信件往来。苏联大使在1月25日早上交来了一封包含着若干主张的友好信件，总理请总统进一步研究这些主张，以促进普遍和平。”
