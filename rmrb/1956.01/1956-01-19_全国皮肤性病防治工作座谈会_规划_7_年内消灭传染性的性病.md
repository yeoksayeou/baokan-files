Title: National Conference on Prevention and Control of Skin and Venereal Diseases: Planning to Eliminate Infectious Venereal Diseases Within 7 Years
Author: 
Date: 1956-01-19
Page: 3

    National Conference on Prevention and Control of Skin and Venereal Diseases
    Planning to Eliminate Infectious Venereal Diseases Within 7 Years
    Eliminating infectious venereal diseases within 7 years is proposed in the preliminary plan for eliminating venereal diseases formulated at the National Conference on Prevention and Control of Skin and Venereal Diseases, which was held in Beijing from the 5th to the 11th. The conference also proposed a preliminary plan for the prevention and control of leprosy in the next 7 years.
    At the conference, attendees discussed the progress of venereal disease prevention and control work over the past 6 years, as well as the incidence of leprosy and future prevention and control efforts. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国皮肤性病防治工作座谈会  规划7年内消灭传染性的性病

1956-01-19
第3版()
专栏：

    全国皮肤性病防治工作座谈会
    规划7年内消灭传染性的性病
    7年内消灭传染性的性病，这是5日到11日在北京举行的全国皮肤性病防治工作座谈会所制定的消灭性病的初步规划中提出的。座谈会还提出了今后7年内防治麻风病的初步规划。
    在座谈会上，出席的人员交谈了6年来性病防治工作的情况，以及麻风病的发病情况和今后防治工作等问题。　（据新华社讯）
