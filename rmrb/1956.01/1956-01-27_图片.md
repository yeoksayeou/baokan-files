Title: "Glorious Railway Worker" Medal Recipient, Zhuang Minggeng
Author:
Date: 1956-01-27
Page: 1

Zhuang Minggeng, an advanced lathe operator at the Qishuyan Locomotive and Rolling Stock Repair Factory and recipient of the "Glorious Railway Worker" medal, completed five years' worth of work in two years and eight months. This is a picture of him at work.
