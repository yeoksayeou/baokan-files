Title: Zedongbal Hosts Reception to Welcome Chinese and Soviet Government Delegations
Author:
Date: 1956-01-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, December 31st: Mongolian People's Republic Premier Zedongbal held a reception on the afternoon of December 30th to welcome the Chinese government delegation headed by Teng Daiyuan and the Soviet government delegation headed by Beshev.

Among those attending the reception were Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party; Shirindib, First Deputy Premier; Luvsan, Deputy Premier and head of the Mongolian government delegation; Yadamysuren, Minister of Transportation and member of the Mongolian government delegation; and Lavdan, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs.

Diplomatic personnel from various countries stationed in Mongolia and members of the Soviet-Mongolian Joint Acceptance Committee also attended the reception.

### 泽登巴尔举行招待会  欢迎中苏两国政府代表团

1956-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　泽登巴尔举行招待会
    欢迎中苏两国政府代表团
    据新华社乌兰巴托31日电　蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔在12月30日下午举行招待会，欢迎以滕代远为首的中国政府代表团和以别谢夫为首的苏联政府代表团。
    出席招待会的有蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴，第一副总理锡林迪布，副总理、蒙古政府代表团团长鲁布桑，运输部长、蒙古政府代表团团员雅达姆苏伦，外交部代部长拉布丹。
    各国驻蒙古的外交人员和苏蒙政府联合验收委员会委员们也出席了招待会。
