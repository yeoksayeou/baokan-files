Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1956-01-24
Page: 4

    Friendly Exchanges
    △ Chen Banxiu, head of the trade delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and Li Changchun, deputy head of the delegation, returned to their country by plane and train from Beijing on the 23rd, respectively. Members of the delegation had previously left Beijing to return to their country.
    △ Qian Dawei (Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Students' Federation) and two other members of the Chinese student delegation arrived in Adelaide, Australia, by plane on the 17th. They were invited to attend the 20th Council Meeting of the National Union of Australian University Students, which will be held from the 19th to the 29th of this month.

### 友好往来

1956-01-24
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    △朝鲜民主主义人民共和国贸易代表团团长陈班秀、副团长李长春，在23日分别乘飞机和火车离京返国。代表团团员已先期离京返国。
    △中国学生代表团钱大卫（中华全国学生联合会副秘书长）等三人，已经在17日乘飞机抵达澳大利亚阿得雷德城。他们是应邀前往参加澳大利亚全国大学生联合会在本月19至29日举行的第二十届理事会的。
