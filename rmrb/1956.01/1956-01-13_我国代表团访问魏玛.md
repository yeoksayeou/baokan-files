Title: Chinese Delegation Visits Weimar
Author:
Date: 1956-01-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Weimar Dispatch: The Chinese People's Republic delegation led by Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and including members Nie Rongzhen, Liu Lantao, Zeng Yongquan, and accompanying personnel, who were visiting the German Democratic Republic after attending the 80th birthday celebrations of President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic, visited Leuna on the morning of January 10.

	The delegation visited the Buna synthetic rubber factory there in Leuna. After the visit, Zhu De and others also participated in an open-air mass meeting with more than 4,000 workers in the factory. Zhu De spoke at the meeting.

	That afternoon, on the way to Weimar, the delegation also visited the "Hermann Matern" agricultural production cooperative in Magdala village.

	That evening, the delegation arrived in Weimar, the famous cultural city of Germany. A welcome rally of 1,600 people was held there, and Zhu De spoke at the rally.

	On the morning of January 11, accompanied by Deputy Prime Minister Selbmann of the German Democratic Republic, the delegation went from Weimar to the former Buchenwald concentration camp to pay tribute to the martyrs of various countries who were murdered by fascist bandits, and laid a wreath in front of the Thälmann memorial plaque.

	After this, the delegation visited the former residence of the great German poet Goethe in Weimar, and laid wreaths at the tombs of Goethe and Schiller.

	On the afternoon of the 11th, the delegation visited the world-renowned state-owned Carl Zeiss optical instrument factory in Jena, and received a warm welcome from the factory workers and Chinese and Korean interns.

	After leaving the Zeiss factory, the delegation also visited the planetarium in Jena.

### 我国代表团访问魏玛

1956-01-13
第4版()
专栏：

    我国代表团访问魏玛
    据新华社魏玛电  参加德意志民主共和国总统皮克80寿辰庆典后在民主德国参观的中华人民共和国代表团团长、中华人民共和国副主席朱德，团员聂荣臻、刘澜涛、曾涌泉和随行人员，1月10日上午赴洛伊纳访问。
    代表团在洛伊纳参观了那里的布纳人造橡胶厂。参观以后，朱德等还参加了厂内举行的有四千多工人参加的露天群众大会。朱德在会上讲了话。
    当天下午，代表团在赴魏玛途中，又访问了马格达拉村的“赫尔曼·马特恩”农业生产合作社。
    当天晚上，代表团到达了德国著名的文化古城魏玛。在那里举行了一个1，600人的欢迎大会，朱德在会上讲了话。
    1月11日上午，代表团在德意志民主共和国副总理赛尔布曼陪同下，从魏玛市到前布痕瓦尔德集中营凭吊了被法西斯匪徒谋杀的各国烈士受难处，并且在台尔曼纪念牌前献了花圈。
    在此以后，代表团在魏玛市参观了德国伟大的诗人哥德的故居，并且在哥德和席勒墓前献了花圈。
    11日下午，代表团在耶纳参观了世界闻名的民主德国国营蔡斯光学器材造制厂，受到全厂工人和中国及朝鲜实习生的热烈欢迎。
    代表团离开蔡斯工厂以后，还参观了耶纳市内的天象馆。
