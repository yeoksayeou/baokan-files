Title: China and Bulgaria Sign Goods Exchange and Payment Agreement and Cultural Cooperation Agreement Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-01-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 21st: Sofia dispatch: On January 21st, the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Bulgaria signed the 1956 China-Bulgaria Goods Exchange and Payment Agreement in Sofia.

    Signing on behalf of China was Zhou Zhu'an, Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria; signing on behalf of Bulgaria was Berebiev, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of Bulgaria.

    The agreement stipulates that the Chinese side will supply Bulgaria with mineral products, tea, animal products, industrial raw materials and other goods; the Bulgarian side will supply China with chemical fertilizers, agricultural machinery, flue-cured tobacco, cotton textiles and other goods.
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 20th: Sofia dispatch: The 1956 Implementation Plan for the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Bulgaria was signed in Sofia on January 19th.

    Signing on behalf of the People's Republic of China was Zhou Zhu'an, Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria; signing on behalf of the People's Republic of Bulgaria was Todorova, Chairman of the Bulgarian Committee for Foreign Friendship and Cultural Relations.

### 中保签订换货付款协定和文化合作协定执行计划

1956-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中保签订换货付款协定和文化合作协定执行计划
    新华社布拉格21日电　索非亚消息：1月21日，中华人民共和国和保加利亚人民共和国在索非亚签订了中保1956年交换货物和付款协定。
    代表中国签字的，是中国驻保加利亚大使周竹安；代表保加利亚签字的，是保加利亚对外贸易部副部长别列别耶夫。
    协定规定，中国方面将供应保加利亚矿产品、茶叶、畜产品、工业原料和其他货物；保加利亚方面将供应中国化学肥料、农业机械、烤烟、棉纺织品和其他货物。
    新华社布拉格20日电　索非亚消息：中华人民共和国和保加利亚人民共和国文化合作协定1956年执行计划，已经于1月19日在索非亚签字。
    代表中华人民共和国签字的是中国驻保加利亚大使周竹安，代表保加利亚人民共和国签字的是保加利亚对外友好与文化联络委员会主席托多罗娃。
