Title: Chinese Delegation Arrives in Leipzig for Visit
Author:
Date: 1956-01-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 7th: The Head of the Delegation of the People's Republic of China, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China Zhu De, and delegation members Nie Rongzhen, Liu Lantao, Zeng Yongquan, and all other personnel, visited the Otto Grotewohl Synthetic Gasoline United Enterprise and the open-pit lignite mine near Bellen, Leipzig, on the morning of the 6th. Vice Chairman Zhu De and the delegation members were warmly welcomed by Jacobs, Vice Chairman of the Leipzig District Committee, Ludwig, General Manager of the factory, and engineers, workers, and worker pickets.

After the visit, the Leipzig District Committee hosted a luncheon for the delegation in Leipzig.

In the afternoon, Vice Chairman Zhu De and the delegation members met with more than 120 Chinese students studying in the Democratic Republic of Germany at the Workers' and Peasants' College of Karl Marx University. Zhu De, Marshal Nie Rongzhen, and Liu Lantao encouraged the students to study hard so that they could contribute their strength to the socialist construction of their motherland after completing their studies. When the students learned that the socialist construction of their motherland was progressing rapidly, the whole audience burst into lasting, stormy applause and cheers.

Subsequently, the delegation visited the Dimitrov Museum. Vice Chairman Zhu De wrote the following inscription in the commemorative book: "Comrade Dimitrov's spirit of struggle for the cause of communism inspires millions of people. Long live communism!"

### 我国代表团到来比锡参观

1956-01-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国代表团到来比锡参观
    新华社柏林7日电　中华人民共和国代表团团长、中华人民共和国副主席朱德和代表团团员聂荣臻、刘澜涛、曾涌泉等全体人员，在6日晨到来比锡市附近柏伦镇参观了奥托·格罗提渥人造汽油联合企业和开采褐煤的露天矿场。朱德副主席和代表团人员受到来比锡专区委员会副主席雅各布斯、总厂长卢德韦希和工程师、工人和工人纠察队的热烈欢迎。
    参观后，来比锡专区委员会在来比锡市设午餐招待代表团。
    当天下午，朱德副主席和代表团人员到卡尔·马克思大学工农学院同在民主德国学习的中国留学生120多人见面。朱德副主席、聂荣臻元帅和刘澜涛勉励同学们努力学习，以便学成后在祖国社会主义建设事业中贡献自己的力量。当同学们在获悉祖国社会主义建设事业正在突飞猛进的时候，全场爆发了经久不息的暴风雨般的掌声和欢呼声。
    随后，代表团参观了季米特洛夫博物馆。朱德副主席在纪念簿上题词如下：“季米特洛夫同志为共产主义事业斗争的精神感召着千千万万的人们。共产主义万岁！”
