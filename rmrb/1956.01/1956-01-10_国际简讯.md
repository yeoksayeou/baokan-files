International Briefs

	△ Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, sent a telegram to Ismail al-Azhari, Prime Minister of the Republic of Sudan, congratulating Sudan on its declaration of independence and sovereignty, and expressing the hope to establish friendly relations with the people of the Republic of Sudan.

	△ Ismail al-Azhari, Prime Minister of the Republic of Sudan, sent a return telegram to Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, thanking the Korean government and people for their congratulatory telegram and expressing the hope to establish very friendly relations between the two countries and all other countries in the world.

	△ Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, sent a telegram to Mr. I. al-Azhari, Prime Minister of Sudan, recognizing Sudan's independence and expressing the hope to establish diplomatic relations between the two countries.

### 国际简讯

1956-01-10
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    △朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅致电苏丹共和国政府总理伊斯梅尔·阿扎里，祝贺苏丹宣布成为独立自主的国家，并希望同苏丹共和国人民建立友好的关系。
    △苏丹共和国总理伊斯梅尔·阿扎里复电朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅，感谢朝鲜政府和人民的贺电，并且希望两国之间和世界上其他一切国家之间建立非常友善的关系。
    △阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席穆·谢胡，打电报给苏丹总理伊·阿扎里先生，承认苏丹独立，并希望在两国之间建立外交关系。
