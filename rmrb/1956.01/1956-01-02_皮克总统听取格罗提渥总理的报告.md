Title: President Pieck Hears Report from Premier Grotewohl
Author:
Date: 1956-01-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 1st: On the evening of December 30th, Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, received the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, which returned after visiting the People's Republic of China, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and the Mongolian People's Republic.

	Premier Grotewohl, the head of the government delegation, reported to the President on the delegation's visit. The Premier conveyed to Pieck the sincere congratulations from Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China and the national leaders of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Mongolian People's Republic.

	Pieke emphasized that the signing of the Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation between the German Democratic Republic and the People's Republic of Chin, as well as the granting of full sovereignty to the German Democratic Republic due to the signing of the treaty between the German Democratic Republic and the Soviet Union, were particularly important achievements in the foreign policy of the German Democratic Republic in 1955. Pieck pointed out that the Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation signed between the German Democratic Republic and the People's Republic of China undoubtedly strengthens the international position of the German Democratic Republic.

	He went on to say that the People's Republic of China is an excellent trading partner for the German Democratic Republic.

	Pieck stressed that consolidating the German Democratic Republic helps the struggle for a unified, democratic and peace-loving Germany.

### 皮克总统听取格罗提渥总理的报告

1956-01-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　皮克总统听取格罗提渥总理的报告
    新华社柏林1日电　12月30日晚间，德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克接见了访问中华人民共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和蒙古人民共和国后回国的德意志民主共和国政府代表团。
    政府代表团团长格罗提渥总理向总统报告了代表团的访问经过。总理向皮克转达了中华人民共和国毛泽东主席以及朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和蒙古人民共和国国家领导人的真诚祝贺。
    皮克强调指出，德意志民主共和国和中华人民共和国友好合作条约的签订，以及由于德意志民主共和国和苏联关系条约的签订而赋予德意志民主共和国全部主权，是德意志民主共和国外交政策在1955年中的特别重要的成就。皮克指出，德意志民主共和国和中华人民共和国所签订的友好合作条约毫无疑问在加强着德意志民主共和国的国际地位。
    他接着说，中华人民共和国是德意志民主共和国极好的贸易对象。
    皮克强调指出，巩固德意志民主共和国有助于争取统一、民主和爱好和平的德国的斗争。
