Title: Beijing Municipal Public Bond Promotion Committee Established
Author:
Date: 1956-01-03
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Beijing,

　　Beijing Municipal Public Bond Promotion Committee Established
    According to Xinhua News Agency, the Beijing Municipal 1956 Public Bond Promotion Committee has been formally established.
    The Beijing Municipal Public Bond Promotion Committee has established ten sub-committees including industry and commerce, workers, education, literature and art, central government organs, municipal government organs, and the armed forces to be responsible for the daily leadership work of public bond promotion.

### 北京市公债推销委员会成立

1956-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京市公债推销委员会成立
    据新华社讯　北京市1956年公债推销委员会已正式成立。
    北京市公债推销委员会设立工商界、职工、教育界、文艺界、中央机关、市级机关、部队等十个分会，负责推销公债的日常领导工作。
