Title: Hangzhou General Machinery Plant Manufactures Oxygen Generator
Author: Lin Yongnian
Date: 1956-01-15
Page: 2

    Hangzhou General Machinery Plant Manufactures Oxygen Generator
    On January 3rd, workers at the state-owned Hangzhou General Machinery Plant successfully trial-produced an oxygen generator based on Soviet blueprints.
    This oxygen generator can supply the needs of the chemical industry for manufacturing chemical fertilizers and important chemical industrial products such as nitric acid and sulfuric acid. In addition, it has broad applications in the medical industry, aviation, machine manufacturing industry, shipbuilding industry, and mining industry.
                    (Lin Yongnian)

### 杭州通用机器厂制成制氧机
林永年
1956-01-15
第2版()
专栏：

    杭州通用机器厂制成制氧机
    1月3日，国营杭州通用机器厂职工根据苏联图纸，试制制氧机成功。
    这种制氧机可以供给化学工业制造化学肥料和硝酸、硫酸等重要化学工业品的需用。此外，在医疗工业、航空事业、机器制造工业、造船工业和在采矿工业上都有广阔的用途。
                    （林永年）
