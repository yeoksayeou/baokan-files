Title: Guo Moruo Congratulates Pieck on His 80th Birthday
Author:
Date: 1956-01-04
Page: 1

Transmitted by: German Peace Council

    Guo Moruo's Telegram of Congratulations on Pieck's 80th Birthday
　　To Comrade Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic:
    On the occasion of your 80th birthday, I would like to extend my heartfelt and warm congratulations to you on behalf of all peace-loving Chinese people.
    You have carried out a long and heroic struggle for the liberation of the German people and the cause of defending world peace, and have made outstanding contributions. We believe that under your leadership, the people of the German Democratic Republic will achieve new and great successes in the struggle for the unification of their motherland, for peace, democracy, and independence, and for safeguarding European collective security and lasting world peace.
    I wish you good health and longevity!
    Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee
                                        December 31, 1955

### 郭沫若祝贺皮克80寿辰电

1956-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若祝贺皮克80寿辰电
　　德国和平理事会转
　　德意志民主共和国总统敬爱的威廉·皮克同志：
    欣逢你80寿辰之际，我谨代表全体爱好和平的中国人民，向你致衷心的、热烈的祝贺。
    你为德国人民的解放和保卫世界和平的事业进行了长期英勇的斗争，并且作出了卓越的贡献。我们相信，在你的领导下，德意志民主共和国人民在争取祖国的统一、和平、民主、独立，维护欧洲的集体安全和世界的持久和平的斗争中，将获得新的、巨大的成就。
    祝你身体健康，万寿无疆！
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席　郭沫若
                                        1955年12月31日
