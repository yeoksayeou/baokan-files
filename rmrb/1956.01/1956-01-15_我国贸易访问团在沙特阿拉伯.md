Title: China's Trade Delegation in Saudi Arabia
Author:
Date: 1956-01-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, 14th: The People's Republic of China trade delegation, headed by Jiang Ming, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, left Jeddah on the 13th to return to China after a 7-day visit to Saudi Arabia at the invitation of the Saudi Arabian government.

    During its visit, the delegation exchanged views with representatives of the Saudi Arabian government on the development of trade relations between China and Saudi Arabia.

    The delegation was welcomed by government and business figures in Saudi Arabia. Adwan, Deputy Minister of Finance and National Economy, hosted a banquet for the delegation. King Saud presented gifts to the delegation. The delegation also presented gifts to King Saud and Prime Minister Faisal Saud.

    On the eve of his return to China, Jiang Ming held a farewell banquet in Jeddah. In his speech, Jiang Ming first thanked the King and Prime Minister for their friendly hospitality. He said that during the exchange of views, the delegation and the representatives of the Saudi Arabian government expressed their hope to develop trade relations on the basis of equality, mutual benefit, and mutual respect for national sovereignty. The development of such trade relations will be conducive to the peaceful economic construction of both countries, and both sides will work to achieve this common goal. He said that in order to promote further friendship and direct trade exchanges, the Chinese government has invited the Saudi Arabian government to send a trade delegation to visit China. He said that the delegation's visit has increased mutual understanding between the two countries and paved the way for future trade development between the two countries. We believe that with our joint efforts, our trade relations and friendly ties will continue to develop.

    Deputy Minister Adwan thanked the delegation for its friendly visit in his speech. He believes that the relationship between China and Saudi Arabia will definitely develop. He said that the development of this relationship is beneficial to both countries.

### 我国贸易访问团在沙特阿拉伯

1956-01-15
第4版()
专栏：

    我国贸易访问团在沙特阿拉伯
    据新华社贝鲁特14日电  以对外贸易部副部长江明为首的中华人民共和国贸易访问团，应沙特阿拉伯政府的邀请在沙特阿拉伯作了7天的访问以后，已经在13日离开吉达回中国。
    代表团在访问期间同沙特阿拉伯政府的代表就发展中国和沙特阿拉伯的贸易关系的问题交换了意见。
    代表团在沙特阿拉伯受到政府和贸易界人士的欢迎。财政和国民经济部副大臣阿道安曾经设宴招待代表团。国王沙特向代表团赠送了礼品。代表团也向国王沙特和首相费萨尔·沙特赠送了礼品。
    江明在回国的前夕在吉达举行了告别宴会。江明在致词时首先感谢国王和首相所给予的友好款待。他说，在交换意见的时候，代表团和沙特阿拉伯政府的代表们都表示了希望在平等互利和互相尊重国家主权的基础上发展贸易关系的愿望。这种贸易关系的发展将有利于两国的和平经济建设，双方将为实现这个共同的愿望而努力。他说，为了促进更进一步的友谊和直接的贸易交流，中国政府邀请沙特阿拉伯政府派遣贸易代表团访问中国。他说，代表团的访问增加了两国之间相互的了解，并且为今后两国之间的贸易发展铺平了道路。我们相信，在我们的共同努力下，我们之间的贸易关系和友好联系一定会不断地发展。
    阿道安副大臣在讲话中对代表团的友好访问表示感谢。他相信中国和沙特阿拉伯之间的关系一定会得到发展。他说，这种关系的发展对两国都是有好处的。
