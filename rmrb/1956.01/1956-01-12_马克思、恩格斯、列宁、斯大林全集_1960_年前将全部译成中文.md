Title: Complete Works of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin to be Translated into Chinese Before 1960
Author:
Date: 1956-01-12
Page: 3

    The Compilation and Translation Bureau of the Works of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China will translate the first and second volumes of "Complete Works of Marx and Engels" into Chinese this year. The first volume will be published this year. It will translate six volumes of "Complete Works of Lenin," of which the 28th, 29th, and 30th volumes, as well as "Philosophical Notebooks" outside the complete works, may also be published this year. In addition, it will completely translate the 13 volumes of "Complete Works of Stalin" already published in the Soviet Union this year, and publish the Chinese translations of the last five volumes: Volumes 4, 5, 6, 7, and 13. Furthermore, the "Concise Biography of Lenin," the revised second edition of the "Textbook of Political Economy," and the new revised edition of the "Concise Dictionary of Philosophy" translated by the Compilation and Translation Bureau will also be published this year.
    The Compilation and Translation Bureau has formulated a work plan for 1956 to 1960. During these five years, they will translate 14 volumes of the "Complete Works of Marx and Engels" (the complete works have 30 volumes, some of which, such as "Das Kapital," have already been published; another part will be translated and published by other departments); they will completely translate all 35 volumes of the "Complete Works of Lenin"; and they will also translate the "Collection of Resolutions and Decisions of the Congresses, Conferences, and Plenary Sessions of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union," the "Short Course History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union" (second revised edition), "History of Philosophy," "Biography of Marx," "Biography of Engels" (revised edition), and "Brief Biography of Stalin" (revised edition).
    The works of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin began to be translated into Chinese and published more than 30 years ago. According to incomplete statistics, from October 1949 to the end of 1955, a total of 225 kinds of works by Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin were published, amounting to 23,791,600 copies.
           (Xinhua News Agency)

### 马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林全集1960年前将全部译成中文

1956-01-12
第3版()
专栏：

    马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林全集1960年前将全部译成中文
    中共中央马克思恩格斯列宁斯大林著作编译局今年将把“马克思恩格斯全集”的第一卷和第二卷译成中文，第一卷将在今年出版；将翻译“列宁全集”六卷，其中的第28卷、29卷、30卷和全集以外的“哲学笔记”也可以在今年出版；并且将在今年全部译完苏联已经出版的“斯大林全集”13卷，并且出版最后五卷中译本：第4、5、6、7、13卷。另外，编译局翻译的“列宁传略”、“政治经济学教科书”增订第二版和“简明哲学辞典”新的增订本等书，也都要在今年出版。
    编译局已经制定了1956年到1960年的工作规划。在这五年内，他们将译完“马克思恩格斯全集”14卷（全集共有30卷，其中一部分如“资本论”等已经出版；另一部分将由其他部门翻译出版）；将全部译完“列宁全集”35卷；另外还要翻译“苏联共产党代表大会、代表会议和中央全会决议与决定汇编”、“苏联共产党历史简明教程”（第二版增订本）、“哲学史”、“马克思传”、“恩格斯传”（修订本）和“斯大林传略”（增订本）。
    马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林的著作在30多年前就开始在中国翻译成中文出版。据不完全统计，从1949年10月到1955年底，共出版马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林著作225种，23，791，600册。     
           （新华社）
