Title: Sino-Indian Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in New Delhi
Author:
Date: 1956-01-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Sino-Indian Friendship Association cultural delegation, headed by Wu Han, which was invited by the India-China Friendship Association to visit India, arrived in New Delhi by plane on the evening of the 17th.

### 中印友协访印代表团到新德里

1956-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    中印友协访印代表团到新德里
    据新华社讯　应印中友好协会的邀请前往印度访问的以吴晗为首的中印友好协会访印文化代表团17日晚乘飞机到达新德里。
