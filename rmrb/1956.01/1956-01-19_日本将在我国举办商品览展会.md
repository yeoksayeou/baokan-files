Title: Japan to Hold Commodity Exhibition in China
Author:
Date: 1956-01-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 18th: According to the Third China-Japan Trade Agreement, the "Beijing and Shanghai Japan Commodity Exhibition," the executing agency for the commodity exhibition to be held in Beijing and Shanghai this year, was formally established in Tokyo on the 17th. Murata Shozo, President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, and Sukeya Eiichi, President of the Japan-China Machinery Trade Liaison Council, will serve as the presidents and chairman of the board of directors of this organization, respectively.

### 日本将在我国举办商品览展会

1956-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    日本将在我国举办商品览展会
    据新华社18日讯　东京消息：日本方面根据第三次中日贸易协定将于今年在北京和上海举办的商品展览会的执行机构——“北京上海日本商品展览会”，17日在东京正式成立。日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏和日中机械贸易联络协议会会长宿谷荣一分别担任这个机构的总裁和理事长。
