Title: Taiwan Civil Aviation Administration Pilot Wei Dawei and Others Pilot Aircraft Uprising and Return to the Motherland

Author:
Date: 1956-01-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. Wei Dawei, a pilot from the Taipei Travel Service of the Chiang Kai-shek bandit's Taiwan Civil Aviation Administration, along with Liang Feng, a business clerk, and Zhai Xiaowu, a clerk, flew an aircraft back to the motherland in an uprising on the 7th.

On the morning of the 7th, Wei Dawei and others piloted a special tourist plane (model 170A, aircraft number) belonging to Chiang Wei-kuo, the son of the traitor Chiang Kai-shek, and took off from Songshan Airport in Taipei. At 9:10 a.m., it landed safely in a certain area of Fujian Province, where it was warmly welcomed by the local military and civilians.

Wei Dawei is also known as Wei Guiping, from Lipu, Guangxi Province; Liang Feng is also known as Liang Zhengzhong, from Guilin, Guangxi Province; Zhai Xiaowu is from Chengdu, Sichuan Province.

### 台湾民航局飞行员韦大卫等  驾机起义回到祖国大陆

1956-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　台湾民航局飞行员韦大卫等
    驾机起义回到祖国大陆
    新华社8日讯　蒋贼台湾民用航空局台北旅行社飞行员韦大卫、业务员梁枫、事务员翟笑梧三人，7日驾驶飞机一架起义飞回祖国大陆。
    7日晨，韦大卫等驾驶卖国贼蒋介石的儿子蒋纬国的旅行游览专机一架（机号170A型）由台北松山机场起飞，9时10分在福建省某地安全降落，受到当地军民的热烈欢迎。
    韦大卫又名韦桂萍，广西省荔浦人；梁枫又名梁正中，广西省桂林人；翟笑梧四川省成都人。
