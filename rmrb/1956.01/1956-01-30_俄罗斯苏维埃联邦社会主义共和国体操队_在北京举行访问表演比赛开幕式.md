Title: Opening Ceremony of the Visit and Performance Competition of the Gymnastics Team of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-01-30
Page: 3

The Gymnastics Team of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic
Opening Ceremony of the Visit and Performance Competition Held in Beijing

The Gymnastics Team of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic held the opening ceremony of its visit and performance competition in China on the afternoon of the 29th at the competition hall of the Beijing Gymnasium.

Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and Cai Shufan, Huang Qixiang, and Rong Gaotang, Deputy Directors of the Sports Commission, attended the opening ceremony.

Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, also attended the opening ceremony.

Before the opening ceremony, Chen Yi met with Ye. Ya. Siji, the leader of the Gymnastics Team of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, and Te. Ge. Faweiqi, the deputy leader and referee.

At the opening ceremony, Huang Qixiang delivered a speech on behalf of the Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, expressing a warm welcome to the Gymnastics Team of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic for their visit to China. Then, Ye. Ya. Siji, the leader of the Gymnastics Team of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, also spoke. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

The first friendly competition between the Gymnastics Team of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic (hereinafter referred to as the Russian Team), which was invited to visit our country, and the Chinese Gymnastics Team (hereinafter referred to as the Chinese Team) was held on January 29 at the Beijing Gymnasium.

Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi and others watched the competition and met with the athletes of the two teams at the end of the competition.

Huang Qixiang, Deputy Director of the Sports Commission, presented awards to the top three athletes on the spot.

The competition consisted of 5 events, 2 for women and 3 for men. All male and female athletes from the Russian team participating in the competition were Masters of Sports of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic. In women's floor exercise, En. Ke. Kukushkina of the Russian team won first place with 9.57 points (out of a full score of 10, same below); Er. Yi. Bao Fuson of the Russian team won second place; and Chen Xiaozhang of the Chinese team won third place. In women's uneven bars, the first place was Er. Yi. Bao Fuson, with a score of 9.73 points; the second place was Er. Po. Belykh; En. Ke. Kukushkina and Te. De. Liushina tied for third place with 9.5 points. All four of them are from the Russian team.

In men's vault, A. En. Muerqianke won first place with 9.67 points; the second and third places were En. A. Kodyatkin and I. De. Fustrikofu. All three of them are also from the Russian team. In men's parallel bars, the performances of Bao Naijian of the Chinese team and I. De. Fustrikofu of the Russian team were particularly outstanding, and they tied for first place with 9.75 points; there was no second place; the third place was A. En. Muerqianke of the Russian team. In men's rings, the first and second places were won by I. De. Fustrikofu and A. En. Muerqianke of the Russian team, respectively, with the first place scoring 9.7 points; the third place was Bao Naijian of the Chinese team.

After the competition, a women's group exercise performance was also presented by the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic. (By our reporter) (Attached photo)

### 俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国体操队  在北京举行访问表演比赛开幕式

1956-01-30
第3版()
专栏：

    俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国体操队
    在北京举行访问表演比赛开幕式
    俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国体操队29日下午在北京体育馆的比赛馆中，举行来中国访问的表演比赛开幕式。
    中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅和体育运动委员会副主任蔡树藩、黄琪翔、荣高棠等人出席了开幕式。
    苏联驻中国大使尤金院士也出席了开幕式。
    陈毅在开幕式以前接见了俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国体操队领队叶·亚·西集伊和副领队兼裁判特·格·伐维契。
    在开幕式上，黄琪翔代表中华人民共和国体育运动委员会致词，他对俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国体操队来中国访问表示热烈欢迎。接着，俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国体操队领队叶·亚·西集伊也讲了话。　（据新华社讯）
    应邀来我国访问的俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国体操队（以下简称俄罗斯队）和中国体操队（以下简称中国队）的第一场友谊比赛，1月29日在北京体育馆举行。
    国务院副总理陈毅等观看了比赛，并在比赛结束时接见了两队运动员。
    体育运动委员会副主任黄琪翔当场颁发奖品给前三名运动员。
    比赛项目共计5项，女子的2项，男子的3项。俄罗斯队参加比赛的男女运动员全部是俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国运动健将。女子自由体操，俄罗斯队恩·克·库库什金娜以9.57分（满分是10，下同）获得第一名；俄罗斯队尔·伊·鲍夫松获得第二名；第三名是中国队陈孝彰。女子高低杠，第一名尔·伊·鲍夫松，成绩9.73分；第二名尔·泼·别雷赫；恩·克·库库什金娜和特·德·刘希娜，同以9.5分获得第三名。她们四人都是俄罗斯队的。
    男子跳马，阿·恩·牧尔阡科以9.67分获得第一名；第二、第三名是恩·阿·科佳特金和伊·德·伏斯特里科夫。他们三人也都是俄罗斯队的。男子双杠，中国队鲍乃健和俄罗斯队伊·德·伏斯特里科夫的表演特别精采，同以9.75分获得第一名；没有第二名；第三名是俄罗斯队阿·恩·牧尔阡科。男子吊环，第一、第二名，分别为俄罗斯队的伊·德·伏斯特里科夫和阿·恩·牧尔阡科获得，第一名的成绩是9.7分；第三名是中国队鲍乃健。
    比赛结束后，还进行了俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国女子团体操表演。　（本报记者）（附图片）
