Title: The Chinese People's Volunteers Spend Their Sixth New Year in Korea
Author:
Date: 1956-01-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 2nd. Officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteers (CPV) spent a happy sixth New Year in Korea.

    On New Year's Day, the broad masses of officers and soldiers in various units unfolded New Year's greetings activities. Many organs and companies held New Year's symposiums, recollection meetings, sports meets, and cultural competitions. The cultural work troupes of various units performed for officers and soldiers.

    During the New Year, many officers and soldiers of various units wrote a large number of congratulatory letters to workers and peasants in the motherland.

    On the 1st, the leading organs of the CPV generally invited representatives from local Korean Party, government, and military organs and people to the troops to celebrate the New Year and solicited their opinions on the troops. The CPV and Korean People's Army units guarding the front lines side-by-side also held New Year's gatherings.

### 志愿军在朝鲜度过第六个新年

1956-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军在朝鲜度过第六个新年
    据新华社平壤2日电　中国人民志愿军各部队军官和士兵，在朝鲜愉快地度过了第六个新年。
    元旦这天，各部队的广大官兵展开了新年团拜的活动。很多机关、连队都举行了新年座谈会、回忆会、体育运动会和文艺比赛会。各部队文艺工作团为军官和士兵们进行演出。
    新年期间，很多部队的军官和士兵，都给祖国的工人、农民写了大批祝贺的信件。
    1日，志愿军各部队领导机关普遍地邀请了当地的朝鲜党、政、军机关和人民代表到部队过年，并且征求了代表们对部队的意见。并肩守卫阵地的志愿军和人民军部队也举行了新年团拜。
