Title: Rangoon Holds Parade to Celebrate Union of Burma National Day
Author:
Date: 1956-01-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 4th January. January 4th marked the eighth anniversary of the independence of the Union of Burma. The Burmese people celebrated this festival.

　　The celebration parade was held in the morning. The President, cabinet ministers, members of parliament, and foreign envoys reviewed the parade. Song Qingling, a guest of the Burmese government, attended the review in the company of Prime Minister U Nu.

　　President Ba U delivered a speech to the parading troops. He said that since gaining independence, Burma has made progress every year. It has now gained the attention of the world, mainly due to its unwavering adherence to a neutral policy.

　　President Ba U then mentioned the achievements of the Burmese government in the past year, and he pointed out that the Burmese government has made efforts to build industrial projects and increase mineral and agricultural products.

### 仰光举行游行庆祝缅甸联邦国庆

1956-01-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　仰光举行游行庆祝缅甸联邦国庆
    新华社仰光4日电　1月4日是缅甸联邦独立八周年纪念日。缅甸人民庆祝这个节日。
    庆祝游行在上午举行。总统、内阁部长、国会议员、各国外交使节检阅了游行队伍。缅甸政府的客人宋庆龄在吴努总理陪同下出席了检阅。
    巴宇总统对游行队伍发表了一篇演说。他说，自从取得独立以来，缅甸年年都有进步。它现在已经博得了全世界的重视，这主要是由于它坚持不渝地遵守中立政策。
    巴宇总统接着提到缅甸政府在过去一年的成就，他并指出，缅甸政府已经作了努力来建设工业工程，增加矿产和农产品。
