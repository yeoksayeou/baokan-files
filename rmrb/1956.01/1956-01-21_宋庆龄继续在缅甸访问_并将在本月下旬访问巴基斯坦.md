Title: Soong Ching Ling Continues Her Visit to Burma and Will Visit Pakistan Later This Month
Author:
Date: 1956-01-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency: Soong Ching Ling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic, continues her visit to Burma.

On the 15th, Soong Ching Ling visited the Shan State Agricultural Research Experimental Station in Taunggyi, Burma. She also visited a canning manufacturing cooperative with approximately 35 workers and a government-run hand weaving school.

On the 16th, Soong Ching Ling and her entourage visited another scenic area in Shan State - Kalaw, which is 4,200 feet above sea level.

At 4:30 pm on the 18th, Soong Ching Ling returned to Yangon from Taunggyi by plane.

At 5:30 pm on the 18th, Soong Ching Ling received representatives from all walks of life of the overseas Chinese community in Yangon at a tea party held at the Chinese Embassy in Burma. Approximately 1,000 overseas Chinese attended the tea party. The tea party proceeded in a warm and cordial atmosphere.

On the evening of the 18th, the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, invited Soong Ching Ling to dinner at his official residence.

Xinhua News Agency, January 20th. Soong Ching Ling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, will visit Pakistan in late January 1956 at the invitation of the Prime Minister and government of Pakistan.

### 宋庆龄继续在缅甸访问  并将在本月下旬访问巴基斯坦

1956-01-21
第4版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄继续在缅甸访问
    并将在本月下旬访问巴基斯坦
    据新华社讯　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄继续在缅甸访问。
    15日，宋庆龄在缅甸东枝参观了掸邦农业研究实验站。她还参观了大约有35名工人工作的罐头制造生产合作社和政府举办的手工纺织学校。
    16日，宋庆龄和她的随行人员访问了掸邦的另一个风景区——海拔4，200英尺的葛鲁。
    18日下午4时半，宋庆龄从东枝乘飞机回到仰光。
    18日下午5时半，宋庆龄在中国驻缅甸大使馆举行的茶会上接见了在仰光的各界华侨。出席茶会的华侨约1，000人。茶会在热烈和亲切的空气中进行。
    18日晚间，缅甸联邦总理吴努邀请宋庆龄在他的官邸共进晚餐。
    新华社20日讯　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄应巴基斯坦总理和政府的邀请，将在1956年1月下旬访问巴基斯坦。
