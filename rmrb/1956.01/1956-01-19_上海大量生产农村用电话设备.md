Title: Shanghai Mass-Producing Telephone Equipment for Rural Use
Author:
Date: 1956-01-19
Page: 2

    Shanghai has begun mass production of telephone equipment for rural use. The first batch of more than 270 telephone switchboards and 9,000 telephones suitable for use in villages and small towns without electricity supply will be shipped to rural areas before the end of March. Among them, the largest switchboard can guarantee telephone communication between 100 agricultural producers' cooperatives. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 上海大量生产农村用电话设备

1956-01-19
第2版()
专栏：

    上海大量生产农村用电话设备
    上海开始大量生产农村用的电话设备。第一批适于在没有电力供应的乡村和小城镇使用的270多台电话交换总机和9，000只电话机，将在3月底前后全部运往农村。其中，最大的交换总机可以保证100个农业生产合作社互通电话。　（新华社）
