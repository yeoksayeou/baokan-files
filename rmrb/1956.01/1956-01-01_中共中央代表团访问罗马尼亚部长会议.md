Title: CCP Central Committee Delegation Visits the Romanian Council of Ministers
Author:
Date: 1956-01-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 31st – The Chinese Communist Party Central Committee Delegation, headed by Zhu De and including members Nie Rongzhen and Liu Lantao, visited the Romanian People's Republic Council of Ministers on December 29th. They were received by: Chairman of the Council of Ministers Stoica, First Deputy Chairman Bodnaras, Borila, and Deputy Chairman Bârlădeanu. Also present was Comrade Dălea, member of the Romanian Workers' Party Central Committee.

The meeting took place in an extremely warm and friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 31st – Bucharest dispatch: The Chinese Communist Party Central Committee Delegation, headed by Zhu De, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Communist Party Central Committee, visited the Filaret Power Plant and the Agricultural Institute of the Romanian People's Republic Academy of Sciences in Bucharest on the morning of December 29th. Comrade Dălea, member of the Romanian Workers' Party Central Committee, Major General Braun of the Romanian Armed Forces Department, and G. Cioara, Minister of the Romanian Electrical Power and Equipment Industry, accompanied the delegation on the visit.

When Comrade Zhu De and others visited, they were warmly received by their hosts. The Filaret Power Plant also presented Comrade Zhu De with a commemorative album on the long-term design of Romania's electrification plan.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 29th – The Chinese Communist Party Central Committee Delegation, headed by Comrade Zhu De, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Communist Party Central Committee, visited the Chemical Research Institute in Bucharest on December 28th.

Comrade Zhu De and others visited the laboratories of the Chemical Research Institute and spoke with Romanian experts about the experimental work being carried out by the institute and the results obtained. The staff of the institute received the delegation with great enthusiasm.

### 中共中央代表团访问罗马尼亚部长会议

1956-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

    中共中央代表团访问罗马尼亚部长会议
    新华社布加勒斯特31日电　中国共产党中央委员会代表团团长朱德和团员聂荣臻、刘澜涛在12月29日访问了罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议。接见代表团的有：部长会议主席斯托伊卡、第一副主席波德纳拉希、波里勒和副主席伯尔拉德亚努。在座的还有罗马尼亚工人党中央委员达列亚同志。
    宾主在极其热烈友好的气氛中进行了交谈。
    新华社布拉格31日电　布加勒斯特消息：以中国共产党中央委员会书记处书记朱德为首的中国共产党中央委员会代表团，12月29日上午在布加勒斯特参观了非拉列特电力工厂和罗马尼亚人民共和国科学院农业研究所。陪同代表团前往参观的有罗马尼亚工人党中央委员达列亚、罗马尼亚武装部队部布朗少将和罗马尼亚电力和电气设备工业部部长格·乔亚拉。
    朱德同志等参观的时候，受到主人们的殷勤接待，非拉列特电力工厂还送给朱德同志一本有关罗马尼亚电气化计划远景设计的纪念册。
    据新华社布加勒斯特29日电　以中国共产党中央委员会书记处书记朱德同志为首的中国共产党中央委员会代表团，12月28日访问了布加勒斯特的化学研究所。
    朱德同志等参观了化学研究所的试验室，并且同罗马尼亚专家交谈了有关研究所进行的试验工作和研究所获得的成果。研究所的工作人员以极大的热情接待了代表团。
