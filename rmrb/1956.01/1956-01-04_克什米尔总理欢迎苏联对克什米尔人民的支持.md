Title: Kashmir Premier Welcomes Soviet Support for Kashmir People
Author:
Date: 1956-01-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, 2nd (TASS dispatch) "Hindustan Times" reported that Kashmir Premier Ghulam Mohammed Bakshi told reporters in Delhi on January 1 that other major powers should understand, like the Soviet Union, that the future of Kashmir should be decided by the Kashmiri people themselves. Any interference would bring new dangers to the people of Kashmir, Pakistan, and India, and could undermine peace in Asia.

Bakshi pointed out that the people of Kashmir are very satisfied with the direct and full support given to them by Bulganin and Khrushchev.

Bakshi emphasized that the merger of Kashmir with India is final, and the people of Kashmir cannot tolerate any conspiracy to re-raise the issue of Kashmir's future.

### 克什米尔总理欢迎苏联对克什米尔人民的支持

1956-01-04
第4版()
专栏：

    克什米尔总理欢迎苏联对克什米尔人民的支持
    新华社据塔斯社德里2日讯　“印度斯坦时报”报道，克什米尔总理吴拉姆·穆罕默德·巴克希1月1日在德里向新闻记者说，其他的大国应当像苏联那样地了解克什米尔的前途应当由克什米尔人民自己来决定，任何干涉都会给克什米尔、巴基斯坦和印度人民带来新的危险，而且可能破坏亚洲的和平。
    巴克希指出，克什米尔人民对布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫给予他们的直接而充分的支持感到非常满意。
    巴克希着重指出，克什米尔同印度合并是最后的定局，克什米尔人民不能忍受重新提出关于克什米尔的前途问题的任何阴谋。
