Title: Liaoning Establishes a Number of Farm Tool Repair Stations
Author: Zhang Weiyi, Wu Peiruo
Date: 1956-01-21
Page: 2

    Liaoning Establishes a Number of Farm Tool Repair Stations
    Various regions in Liaoning Province have already established 365 farm tool repair stations and farm tool repair factories. According to incomplete statistics, these repair stations and repair factories repaired a total of 5,580 farm tools for farmers last year, and repaired and replaced 219,009 farm tool parts, basically solving the problem of having nowhere to repair farm tools when they break down and nowhere to buy parts when they are lost. (Zhang Weiyi, Wu Peiruo)

### 辽宁建立一批农具修配站
张维义　伍沛若
1956-01-21
第2版()
专栏：

    辽宁建立一批农具修配站
    辽宁省各地已经建立了365处农具修配站和农具修理厂。据不完全统计，这些修配站和修理厂，去年共给农民修理了5，580台农具，修配了219，009件农具部件，基本上解决了农具坏了无处修，部件丢失了无处买的问题。（张维义　伍沛若）
