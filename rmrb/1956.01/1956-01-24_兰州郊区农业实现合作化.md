Title: Agricultural Cooperation Achieved in the Suburbs of Lanzhou
Author: 
Date: 1956-01-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, January 23rd: Agricultural cooperation has been fully socialized in the suburbs of Lanzhou City as of January 22nd. All 214 elementary agricultural producers' cooperatives in the suburbs have been transformed into advanced agricultural producers' cooperatives, with farmer households joining the cooperatives accounting for 90.78% of the total number of farmer households in the suburbs. After the full socialist cooperation of agriculture in the suburbs is realized this year, the production of fruits and vegetables will be further expanded, the planting area will be doubled, and the yield per unit area is planned to increase by about 20% to meet the needs of the city and industrial and mining areas.

### 兰州郊区农业实现合作化

1956-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    兰州郊区农业实现合作化
    据新华社兰州23日电　兰州市郊区在22日实现了全社会主义的农业合作化。全郊区214个初级农业生产合作社已经全部转为高级农业生产合作社，入社农户达到郊区农户总数的90.78％。今年郊区农业实现全社会主义的合作化以后，将进一步扩大瓜果和蔬菜生产，播种面积将扩大一倍，单位面积产量计划提高20％左右，以满足城市和工矿区的需要。
