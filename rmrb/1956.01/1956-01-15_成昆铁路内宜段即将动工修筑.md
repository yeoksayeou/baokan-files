Title: Construction of the Neijiang-Yibin Section of the Chengdu-Kunming Railway to Begin Soon
Author:
Date: 1956-01-15
Page: 1

This newspaper's Chongqing dispatch on the 14th: Construction of the Neijiang-Yibin section of the Chengdu-Kunming Railway will commence in February of this year. The first group of construction personnel responsible for this section of the project has already arrived at the construction site, and relevant departments are actively carrying out technical, construction, and material supply preparations. This section of the railway starts from Neijiang, Sichuan Province, passes through Zigong, and ends in Yibin, with a total length of 115 kilometers. It is planned to lay 81 kilometers of track this year.

### 成昆铁路内宜段即将动工修筑

1956-01-15
第1版()
专栏：

    成昆铁路内宜段即将动工修筑
    本报重庆14日电  成都—昆明铁路内江宜宾段工程将在今年2月份动工修筑。现在，担负这一段工程的第一批施工人员已经到达工地，各有关部门正在积极进行技术、施工和物资供应的准备工作。这段铁路由四川省内江开始，途经自贡到达宜宾，全长115公里，今年计划铺轨81公里。
