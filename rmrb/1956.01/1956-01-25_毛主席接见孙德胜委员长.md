Title: Chairman Mao Receives Chairman Tôn Đức Thắng

Author:

Date: 1956-01-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 24th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received Tôn Đức Thắng, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, on the evening of January 24th.

Present at the meeting were Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council; Chen Yun and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premiers; Guo Moruo and Peng Zhen, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Wang Jiaxiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

Also present at the meeting was Hoàng Văn Hoan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China.

Xinhua News Agency, January 24th: Tôn Đức Thắng, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of January 24th. He came to Beijing for a short stay at the invitation of Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, on his way back home after attending the celebration of the 80th birthday of Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic.

Those who went to the airport to welcome him were Li Jishen and Peng Zhen, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhang Su, Qu Wu, and Yu Xinqing, Deputy Secretaries-General; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Liu Ningyi, Standing Committee Member of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Wang Kunlun, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; and Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

Hoàng Văn Hoan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Dimov, Ambassador of Bulgaria to China, and personnel from the embassies of the Soviet Union, Hungary, Mongolia, and the German Democratic Republic in China also went to the airport to welcome him.

### 毛主席接见孙德胜委员长

1956-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见孙德胜委员长
    新华社24日讯　中华人民共和国毛泽东主席在24日晚间接见了越南民主共和国国民大会常务委员会委员长孙德胜。
    接见时在座的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来、副总理陈云、邓小平，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若、彭真，外交部副部长王稼祥，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏。
    接见时，越南民主共和国驻中国大使黄文欢也在座。
    新华社24日讯　越南民主共和国国民大会常务委员会委员长孙德胜在24日下午乘飞机到达北京。他是应中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇的邀请，在参加德意志民主共和国威廉·皮克总统八十寿辰庆祝典礼后的回国途中，前来北京作短期逗留的。
    前往机场欢迎的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、彭真，副秘书长张苏、屈武、余心清，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员刘宁一，北京市副市长王崑崙，外交部礼宾司司长柯华等人。
    越南民主共和国驻华大使黄文欢和保加利亚驻华大使迪莫夫，以及苏联、匈牙利、蒙古和德意志民主共和国等国驻华大使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
