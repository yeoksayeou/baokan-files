International Briefs

　　The Soviet delegation headed by Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, which attended the celebration of the 80th birthday of Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, left Berlin on January 7 and returned to Moscow after visiting the cities of Weimar, Jena and Leipzig.
　　The Soviet government delegation, led by Volkov, Chairman of the Union Council of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, attended the grand ceremony for the official inauguration of Tubman and Tolbert as President and Vice President of Liberia.
　　Zorin, the Soviet Ambassador to the Federal Republic of Germany, presented his credentials to Federal President Heuss on January 7. Ambassador Zorin and President Heuss exchanged congratulatory speeches and replies.
　　Yugoslav President Tito and his entourage left Egypt on the 6th to return home after a ten-day visit to Egypt.
　　Lebanon and the People's Republic of Poland, after successful negotiations, have signed a trade and payment agreement in Beirut.
　　The Indian Ministry of External Affairs issued a press release on the 6th stating that the King of Iran, Mohammad Reza Pahlavi, and the Queen have accepted the invitation of the President of India and will arrive in India on February 16 for a formal visit.
　　The famous West German pacifist, former German Chancellor Dr. Joseph Wirth, passed away in Freiburg, West Germany on January 3, at the age of 77. In December 1955, Joseph Wirth was awarded the Stalin International Peace Prize for his outstanding contributions to the cause of peace.

### 国际简讯

1956-01-09
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

　　国际简讯
　　以苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫为首的参加德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克80寿辰庆典的苏联代表团，在访问了魏玛·耶纳和来比锡等城市后，已在1月7日离开柏林返回莫斯科。
　　由苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席沃尔科夫率领的苏联政府代表团参加了塔布曼和托尔贝特正式就任利比里亚总统和副总统的隆重典礼。
　　苏联驻德意志联邦共和国大使佐林在1月7日向联邦总统豪斯递交国书。佐林大使和豪斯总统互致颂辞和答辞。
　　南斯拉夫总统铁托和他的随行人员在埃及访问十天以后，在6日离开埃及回国。
　　黎巴嫩和波兰人民共和国经过顺利谈判之后，已经在贝鲁特签订了一项贸易和支付协定。
　　印度外交部6日发表新闻公告说，伊朗国王穆罕默德·礼萨·巴列维和王后已经接受印度总统的邀请，将在2月16日到达印度，作正式访问。
　　西德著名和平人士、前德国总理约瑟夫·维尔特博士1月3日在西德夫赖堡逝世，享年77岁。1955年12月，约瑟夫·维尔特由于他对和平事业的卓越贡献曾经获得斯大林国际和平奖金。
