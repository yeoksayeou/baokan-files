Title: Farmers Love New Agricultural Implements (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1956-01-22
Page: 5

    Farmers Love New Agricultural Implements
    Farmers in Huacun Township, Longhua District, Shanghai, have established an electric pumping station. Photo by Zhang Huchen (News Daily Agency draft)
    Members of the Feilong Agricultural Production Cooperative in Gongping Township, Wenjiang County, Sichuan Province, are using a ten-row seeder to sow winter wheat. Xinhua News Agency reporter Sun Zhongjing photo
    Farmers in Yaobao Village, Ninth District, Hulan County, Heilongjiang Province, who have already cooperativized, are using double-wheeled, double-furrow plows for autumn plowing. Xinhua News Agency reporter Tan Zhiqiang photo
    Members of the China-Soviet Friendship Collective Farm in Lu'an County, Shanxi Province, are actively learning to drive tractors. Our reporter Na Yi photo
