Title: Tianjin Medical Personnel Achieve Success in Scientific Research
Author:
Date: 1956-01-14
Page: 3

Tianjin's medical personnel are conducting research on over one hundred medical science topics.

Fang Xianzhi, director of the Orthopedics Department of the Tianjin Municipal People's Hospital, and deputy director Tao Fu and others have been researching "lesion removal surgery." This method is more effective than previous treatments and can remove sequestrum, abscesses, and tuberculous granulation tissue. In the past four years, they have used "lesion removal surgery" to treat 131 patients with spinal tuberculosis, with a cure rate of over 89%.

Yang Ke, director of Tianjin Municipal Central Obstetrics and Gynecology Hospital, and attending physician Qi Yanling, are conducting chemical analysis and various tests on the components of placental blood. They successfully experimented with using placental blood for blood transfusions for patients in the second half of last year. They used blood from 670 placentas, totaling more than 49,000 cc, and injected it into 209 people with good results.

Zhang Tianhui, deputy director of surgery at Tianjin Municipal General Hospital, is researching a complex "mitral valve splitting surgery." He performed surgery on a patient with mitral stenosis half a month ago, and the patient has recovered. He will continue to conduct research in a planned manner.

(According to Xinhua News Agency report)

### 天津医务人员进行科学研究取得成就

1956-01-14
第3版()
专栏：

    天津医务人员进行科学研究取得成就
    天津市的医务人员正在研究一百多个医学科学题目。
    天津市立人民医院骨科主任方先之和副主任陶黼等人研究的“病灶清除术”比过去的治疗方法效果好，能够清除腐骨、脓包和结核性肉芽。他们在四年中用“病灶清除术”的手术为131个脊椎结核患者治疗的结果，治愈的占89％以上。
    天津市立中心妇产科医院院长杨珂和主治医师戚延龄，正在进行胎盘血液成分的化学分析和各种化验。他们在去年下半年用胎盘血为病人输血已经试验成功。他们使用了670个胎盘的血共49，000多西西，注射了209人次，效果很好。
    天津市立总医院外科副主任张天惠正在研究一种复杂的“心脏二尖瓣分离术”。他在半个月前为一个心脏狭窄症的患者动手术以后，病人已经恢复健康。他将有计划地继续进行研究。
                  （据新华社讯）
