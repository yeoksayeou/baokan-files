Title: Founding of the Academic Committee of the Institute of Applied Chemistry
Author: 
Date: 1956-01-20
Page: 3

    Founding of the Academic Committee of the Institute of Applied Chemistry
    The founding conference of the Academic Committee of the Institute of Applied Chemistry of the Chinese Academy of Sciences was held in Changchun from January 6 to 12. The Academic Committee of the Institute of Applied Chemistry consists of 25 scientists with considerable expertise in chemistry: Zhu Rongzhao, Li Su, Liu Kejing, Liu Dafu, Wu Xuezhou, Shen Qingnang, Ruan Hongyi, Ji Mingshi, Liu Dagang, Ji Yufeng, Sun Shuqi, Sun Jingbin, Tang Aoqing, Gao Guojing, Chen Guofu, Zhang Youchang, Zhang Dingzhao, Liang Shuquan, Fu Ying, Yang Shixian, Ge Chunlin, Cai Liusheng, Qian Renyuan, Qian Baogong, and Yan Renyin.
    During the conference, attendees listened to and discussed the report by Wu Xuezhou, Director of the Institute of Applied Chemistry, on the basic situation and future development direction of the Institute of Applied Chemistry. They also read and discussed in groups 11 academic papers on topics such as tungsten ore analysis, the molecular weight of styrene-butadiene rubber, and the manufacturing of grass pulp using the chlorination method. In addition, they discussed draft plans for the future development prospects in four areas: inorganic and analytical chemistry, organic synthesis, polymer chemistry, and physical chemistry.

### 应用化学研究所学术委员会成立

1956-01-20
第3版()
专栏：

    应用化学研究所学术委员会成立
    中国科学院应用化学研究所学术委员会于1月6日到12日在长春举行成立大会。应用化学研究所学术委员会由在化学方面有相当修养的科学家朱荣昭、李苏、刘克静、刘达夫、吴学周、沈青囊、阮鸿仪、季鸣时、柳大纲、纪育丰、孙书棋、孙景斌、唐敖庆、高国经、陈国符、张有昌、张定钊、梁树权、傅鹰、杨石先、葛春霖、蔡镏生、钱人元、钱保功、严仁荫等25人组成。
    这次会议进行期间，听取和讨论了应用化学研究所所长吴学周关于应用化学研究所基本情况及今后发展方向的报告，分组宣读和讨论了钨矿分析、丁苯橡胶的分子量、氯化法制造草类纸浆等11篇学术论文，并讨论了无机与分析化学、有机合成、高分子化学、物理化学等四个方面的发展远景计划草案。
