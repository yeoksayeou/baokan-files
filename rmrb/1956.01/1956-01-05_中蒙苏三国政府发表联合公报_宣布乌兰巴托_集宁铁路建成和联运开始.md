Title: Joint Communiqué of the Governments of China, Mongolia, and the Soviet Union Announces the Completion of the Ulaanbaatar-Jining Railway and the Commencement of Joint Transport Operations

Author:
Date: 1956-01-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 4th.

　　Joint Communiqué of the Governments of China, Mongolia, and the Soviet Union
　　Announces the Completion of the Ulaanbaatar-Jining Railway and the Commencement of Joint Transport Operations

Xinhua News Agency, January 4th – A joint communiqué from the Government of the People's Republic of China, the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic, and the Government of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics regarding the completion of the railway from Ulaanbaatar to Jining and the commencement of railway joint transport operations between the People's Republic of China, the Mongolian People's Republic, and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics.

In September 1952, the Government of the People's Republic of China, the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic, and the Government of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics conducted negotiations on further developing economic and cultural ties between their respective countries. To this end, the aforementioned governments signed an agreement on September 15, 1952, regarding the organization of railway joint transport operations between the People's Republic of China, the Mongolian People's Republic, and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics. According to this agreement, the Government of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic jointly undertook the construction of the railway from Ulaanbaatar via Zamyn-Üüd to the border of the Mongolian People's Republic, and the Government of the People's Republic of China undertook the construction of the railway from Jining via Erenhot to the border of China.

Between September and October 1955, a conference of railway representatives from China, Mongolia, and the Soviet Union was held in Ulaanbaatar to discuss various issues related to the imminent commencement of joint transport operations between the People's Republic of China, the Mongolian People's Republic, and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics.

On December 31, 1955, a ceremony was held in Ulaanbaatar to celebrate the opening of the direct railway from Ulaanbaatar to Beijing. On January 3, 1956, a ceremony was held at the Sino-Mongolian border for the linking of the two countries' railways.

Railway joint transport operations between the People's Republic of China, the Mongolian People's Republic, and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics commenced on January 1, 1956. January 4, 1956

### 中蒙苏三国政府发表联合公报  宣布乌兰巴托——集宁铁路建成和联运开始

1956-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　中蒙苏三国政府发表联合公报
　　宣布乌兰巴托——集宁铁路建成和联运开始
    新华社4日讯　中华人民共和国政府、蒙古人民共和国政府和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府关于建成从乌兰巴托到集宁的铁路并在中华人民共和国、蒙古人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟之间开始铁路联运的联合公报。
    1952年9月间，中华人民共和国政府、蒙古人民共和国政府和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府就进一步发展各该国间的经济和文化联系进行了谈判。为此目的，上述政府在1952年9月15日签订了一项关于在中华人民共和国、蒙古人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟之间组织铁路联运的协定。根据此项协定，苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府和蒙古人民共和国政府共同负责修建了从乌兰巴托经扎门乌德到蒙古人民共和国国境的铁路，中华人民共和国政府负责修建了从集宁经二连到中国国境的铁路。
    1955年9月到10月间，在乌兰巴托市举行的中华人民共和国、蒙古人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟三国铁路代表会议曾经讨论了有关中华人民共和国、蒙古人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟之间就要开始联运的各项问题。
    1955年12月31日，在乌兰巴托举行了乌兰巴托到北京直达铁路的通车典礼。1956年1月3日在中蒙边境举行了两国铁路接轨典礼。
    中华人民共和国、蒙古人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟之间的铁路联运已经在1956年1月1日开始。　1956年1月4日
