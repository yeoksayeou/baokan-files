Title: Many Technical Science Experts Cooperatively Research Scientific and Technological Problems in Civil Architecture and Hydraulic Engineering
Author:
Date: 1956-01-20
Page: 3

    Many technical science experts working in the Chinese Academy of Sciences, 14 industrial departments, and 15 universities and colleges will, for the first time this year, under a unified plan and arrangement, divide the labor and cooperate in researching more than ten major scientific and technological problems in civil architecture and hydraulic engineering.
    A new type of reinforced concrete structure research being carried out collaboratively can save the country a lot of steel and cement after it is successfully researched and widely applied in capital construction. Considering that China, whose bamboo production ranks first in the world, would be of great significance for saving funds and expanding construction projects if a way could be found to change the properties of bamboo to replace wood and steel as building materials, technical science experts have begun to systematically study this issue.
    Prefabricated structures are the key to realizing the industrialization of construction. The research on this issue includes design standardization, material factory production, and construction mechanization. The use of prefabricated structures can greatly shorten construction time and save building materials.
    The ongoing research on seismic-resistant structures is of great significance for avoiding or reducing the losses caused by earthquake disasters to buildings and ensuring the safety of new industrial zone construction. This research is particularly closely related to huge hydraulic construction projects.
    In addition, the problems that technical science experts will study also include: the engineering properties of loess and the reinforcement of weak engineering foundations, waterproof building materials, architectural structure theory, urban construction planning, river sediment, and the hydro-meteorology of various regions of the country.
    Through this collaborative research work, the previous situation of insufficient contact, lack of prioritization, duplication, or omission in civil architecture and hydraulic science research work has been greatly improved; at the same time, a nationwide civil architecture research network has been initially formed, creating favorable conditions for comprehensive planning and development of civil architecture and hydraulic science research work.
                      (Xinhua News Agency)

### 许多技术科学专家  合作研究土木建筑和水利工程方面的科学技术问题

1956-01-20
第3版()
专栏：

    许多技术科学专家
    合作研究土木建筑和水利工程方面的科学技术问题
    分别在中国科学院、14个产业部门和15个高等学校工作的许多技术科学专家，今年第一次在统一计划和安排下，分工合作研究十多项土木建筑和水利工程方面的重大科学技术问题。
    合作进行的一种新的钢筋混凝土结构的研究，在研究成功并且在基本建设中广泛应用以后，可以为国家节省大量钢材和水泥。技术科学专家考虑到在竹材产量占世界第一位的中国，如果能够研究出改变竹材性能的办法，使竹材代替木材和钢材做建筑材料，对于节约资金和扩大建筑工程将有重大的意义，他们已经开始有系统地在研究这个问题。
    装配式结构是实行建筑工业化的关键问题。这个问题的研究包括设计标准化、材料工厂化和施工机械化等内容。采用装配式结构可以大大缩短施工时间和节省建筑材料。
    正在进行的抗震结构的研究，对于避免或减少建筑物遭受地震灾害的损失，保障新工业区建设的安全有重要意义。这项研究同巨大的水利建筑工程的关系尤其密切。
    此外，技术科学专家所要研究的问题还有：黄土工程性质和软弱工程地基的加固、防水建筑材料、建筑结构理论、城市建设规划、河流泥沙以及全国各地区的水文气象等。
    通过这次分工合作的研究工作，在相当大的程度上改善了以往土木建筑、水利科学研究工作中联系不够、轻重不分、重复或遗漏的情况；同时，初步形成了一个全国范围的土木建筑研究工作网，给全面规划和发展土木建筑、水利科学研究工作创造了有利条件。
                      （新华社）
