Title: Producing Vegetables to Meet the Needs of Urban People (Picture)
Author: Li Darong, Jin Yinzhu
Date: 1956-01-21
Page: 2

    Producing Vegetables to Meet the Needs of Urban People
    The Si Ji Qing Agricultural Production Cooperative, formed by the merger of the "Sijiqing," "Yuanda," and "Jinzhuang" agricultural production cooperatives in Dongran Village Township, Beijing suburbs, is now a large advanced cooperative with 712 households and over 3,000 people. The cooperative has over 5,000 mu of cultivated land and over 600 greenhouses, cultivating large quantities of vegetables such as cucumbers, tomatoes, eggplants, peas, and winter melons, so that the people of the capital can eat fresh vegetables all year round.
                                        Photo by Li Darong and Jin Yinzhu
    Left picture: Li Molin (first person on the right), director of the Sijiqing Agricultural Production Cooperative, discusses the cooperative's seven-year overall plan with the deputy director and the League branch secretary. Left picture: The cooperative's greenhouses.
    Top picture: After being sorted, a large quantity of fresh vegetables is ready to be transported to the city.

### 生产蔬菜，供应城市人民需要（图片）
李大荣　金溵柱
1956-01-21
第2版()
专栏：

    生产蔬菜，供应城市人民需要
    由北京郊区东冉村乡的“四季青”、“远大”、“金庄”等3个农业生产合作社合并成的四季青农业生产合作社，现在已经是一个拥有712户、三千多人的大高级社了。全社有耕地面积五千多亩，温室六百多间，大量培育黄瓜、蕃茄、茄子、豌豆、冬瓜等蔬菜，使首都人民一年四季都能吃到新鲜的蔬菜。
                                        李大荣　金溵柱摄
    左图：四季青农业生产合作社社主任李墨林（右第一人）同副主任、团支书等研究合作社7年全面规划的情况。左图：合作社的温室。
    上图：大批新鲜的蔬菜经过整理以后，就要运往城市去。
