Title: Two Indonesian Muslim Parties Withdraw Ministers from Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1956-01-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, January 20th: Antara News Agency reports that the Indonesian Muslim Scholars Association and the Indonesian Islamic Union Party withdrew all their ministers participating in the Harahap Cabinet on the 19th, and demanded the government's resignation.

The report said that the Labor Party leader, Abidin, told reporters on the 19th that the Labor Party intended to withdraw its ministers because he believed that the Harahap government had no reason to continue in power.

The cabinet members from the Indonesian Muslim Scholars Association are Minister of Interior Sunarjo and Minister of Religious Affairs Ilyas. The cabinet members from the Indonesian Islamic Union Party are Second Deputy Prime Minister Hasono Tjokroaminoto and Minister of Social Affairs Sudibyo. The Labor Party's ministers are Minister of Labor Tejasukmana and Deputy Minister of Transportation Asraruddin.

Antara News Agency reported that 30 political parties and organizations in Bandung, including the Indonesian Nationalist Party, the Indonesian Muslim Scholars Association, and the Indonesian Communist Party, decided at a joint meeting on the evening of the 18th to demand the immediate resignation of the Harahap Cabinet. They will also send a delegation to Jakarta to present their decision to the government.

### 印度尼西亚两个回教政党撤回参加内阁的部长

1956-01-21
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚两个回教政党撤回参加内阁的部长
    据新华社雅加达20日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，印度尼西亚回教师联合会和印度尼西亚回教联盟党已经在19日撤回它们参加哈拉哈普内阁的所有部长，并且要求政府辞职。
    消息说，工党领袖阿比丁在19日对新闻记者说，工党打算撤回它的部长，因为他认为哈拉哈普政府已经没有理由再继续执政了。
    印度尼西亚回教师联合会参加政府的阁员有内政部长苏纳里约和宗教部长伊利雅斯，印度尼西亚回教联盟党的阁员有第二副总理哈索诺·佐克罗阿米诺托和社会事务部长苏迪必约，工党的部长有劳工部长特贾苏克马纳和副交通部长阿斯拉鲁丁。
    安塔拉通讯社报道，在万隆的30个政党和团体，包括印度尼西亚国民党、印度尼西亚回教师联合会和印度尼西亚共产党在内，在18日晚上举行的联席会议上决定要求哈拉哈普内阁立即辞职。它们还将派遣代表团到雅加达向政府提出它们的决定。
