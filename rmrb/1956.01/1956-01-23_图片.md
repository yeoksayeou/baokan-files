To enliven rural cultural life and carry out Spring Festival rural entertainment activities, the Beijing Municipal Bureau of Culture and the preparatory office of the Beijing Mass Art Center held a short-term farmer song and dance training class, organizing active members of cultural and entertainment activities in the rural areas of Beijing's suburbs to study.
