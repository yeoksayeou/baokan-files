Title: Shandong Province Completes Winter Afforestation of Over 770,000 Mu
Author:
Date: 1956-01-27
Page: 2

    Shandong Province Completes Winter Afforestation of Over 770,000 Mu

    Shandong Province has basically completed its winter afforestation efforts. From the beginning of last winter to mid-January of this year, the province afforested over 775,900 mu, exceeding the winter afforestation plan by 72%, more than one and a half times the area afforested in the winter of 1954.

    In the process of afforestation, the youth shock brigades of agricultural producers' cooperatives in various places played a significant role. For example, more than 17,000 young people in Licheng County planted more than 2.65 million trees in one month, exceeding the original plan by more than four times.

    Last winter, the province trained more than 7,000 forestry technicians and held forestry technology training conferences in more than 20 key afforestation counties, such as Yiyuan and Mengyin. (Our correspondent in Jinan)

### 山东省冬季造林77万多亩

1956-01-27
第2版()
专栏：

    山东省冬季造林77万多亩
    山东省冬季造林基本结束。从去年入冬以来到今年1月中旬，全省共造林775，900多亩，超额完成冬季造林计划72％，比1954年冬季造林面积扩大一倍半以上。
    在造林过程中，各地农业生产合作社的青年突击队发挥了很大的作用。如历城县17，000多名青少年，在一个月内就植树265万多棵，超过原订计划四倍以上。
    去年冬季，全省训练了7，000多名林业技术人员，并且在沂源、蒙阴等20多个造林重点县召开了林业技术传授会。　　　　（本报驻济南记者）
