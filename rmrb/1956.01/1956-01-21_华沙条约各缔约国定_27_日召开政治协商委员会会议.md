Title: Warsaw Treaty Member States to Hold Political Consultative Committee Meeting on the 27th
Author:
Date: 1956-01-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 20th: TASS, Moscow, January 20th: According to the Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance concluded in Warsaw on May 14, 1955, by the People's Republic of Albania, the People's Republic of Bulgaria, the People's Republic of Hungary, the German Democratic Republic, the People's Republic of Poland, the Romanian People's Republic, the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, and the Czechoslovak Republic, a Political Consultative Committee has been established.

The Warsaw Treaty member states have reached an agreement to hold a meeting of the Political Consultative Committee in Prague on January 27, 1956, to discuss issues arising from the Warsaw Treaty and joint measures.

### 华沙条约各缔约国定27日召开政治协商委员会会议

1956-01-21
第4版()
专栏：

    华沙条约各缔约国定27日召开政治协商委员会会议
    新华社20日讯　塔斯社莫斯科20日讯：根据阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国、保加利亚人民共和国、匈牙利人民共和国、德意志民主共和国、波兰人民共和国、罗马尼亚人民共和国、苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟和捷克斯洛伐克共和国1955年5月14日在华沙缔结的友好合作互助条约，建立了政治协商委员会。
    华沙条约缔约国已经达成协议，决定在1956年1月27日在布拉格召开政治协商委员会会议，讨论由于华沙条约而产生的问题和共同措施。
