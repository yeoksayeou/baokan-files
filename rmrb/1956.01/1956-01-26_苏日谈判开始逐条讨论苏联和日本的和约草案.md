Title: Soviet-Japanese Negotiations Begin Article-by-Article Discussion of Draft Soviet and Japanese Peace Treaty
Author:
Date: 1956-01-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, London, January 25th: TASS reports: The Soviet and Japanese negotiating delegations in London released a joint communiqué on the Soviet-Japanese negotiations. The communiqué stated:

    At the meeting on January 24, the Soviet representative Malik and the Japanese representative Shunichi Matsumoto began to discuss the draft Soviet-Japanese peace treaty article by article. The next meeting is scheduled to be held on January 31.

### 苏日谈判开始逐条讨论苏联和日本的和约草案

1956-01-26
第4版()
专栏：

    苏日谈判开始逐条讨论苏联和日本的和约草案
    新华社25日讯　塔斯社伦敦讯：苏日谈判双方在伦敦发表了苏日谈判联合公报。公报说：
    在1月24日会议上，苏联代表马立克和日本代表松本俊一开始逐条讨论苏联和日本的和约草案。下次会议订于1月31日举行。
