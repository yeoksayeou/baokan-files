Title: Zhou Gengsheng, Acting President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Holds Banquet to Welcome the Thailand People's Promotion of Friendship Delegation to China
Author: 
Date: 1956-01-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 24th. Zhou Gengsheng, Acting President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, held a banquet on the evening of the 24th to welcome the Thailand People's Promotion of Friendship Delegation to China, headed by Nai Thep Chotnuchit.

　　Zhou Gengsheng said in his welcoming speech at the banquet: The Chinese people have always advocated friendly exchanges and peaceful coexistence among the people of all countries. China and Thailand are neighbors and have a long history of friendly relations. The Chinese people hope that China and Thailand will establish friendly relations. The arrival of the delegation is a good start to friendly exchanges between the people of China and Thailand. Zhou Gengsheng said that we hope that the relations between China and Thailand will become increasingly close and that economic and cultural exchanges will be carried out. This is in line with the interests of the people of both countries and is of great significance for enhancing mutual understanding between the two countries and maintaining peace in Asia.

　　Nai Thep Chotnuchit then delivered a speech, saying that China and Thailand have always maintained friendly relations in history. The delegation's visit to China at the invitation shows that a new beginning has been made in the friendly relations between the two countries. He also said that the people of all countries, especially the Chinese people, advocate peaceful coexistence, which is also the proposition of the Economic People's Party. Only in this way can the people of all countries be happy. Nai Thep Chotnuchit pointed out that the delegation is a bridge to promote friendship between China and Thailand, and he hopes that this bridge can be consolidated, and finally proposed a toast to the permanent friendship between China and Thailand.

　　Among those present tonight were Hu Yuzhi, Qiao Guanhua, and Luo Longji, vice presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and directors; Zhang Su, deputy secretary-general of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Lei Renmin and Ji Chao Ding, vice chairmen of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Wu Lengxi and Wang Yunsheng, vice presidents of the All-China Journalists Association; and Zhao Puchu, vice president of the Buddhist Association of China.

### 中国人民外交学会代理会长周鲠生举行宴会  欢迎泰国人民促进友好访华团

1956-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民外交学会代理会长周鲠生举行宴会
    欢迎泰国人民促进友好访华团
    新华社24日讯　中国人民外交学会代理会长周鲠生24日晚举行宴会，欢迎以乃贴·触的努七为首的泰国人民促进友好访华团。
    周鲠生在宴会上致欢迎词说：中国人民一向主张各国人民友好往来与和平共处。中泰两国是邻邦，而且有着悠久的友好关系。中国人民希望中泰两国建立友好关系。访华团的来到是中泰两国人民友好往来的良好开端。周鲠生说，我们希望中泰两国的关系日益密切，开展经济和文化交流，这是符合两国人民的利益的，对增进两国的相互了解和维护亚洲和平是有着重大的意义的。
    乃贴·触的努七接着致词说，中泰两国在历史上一直友好相处。访华团这次的应邀来中国访问，表明两国的友好关系有了新的开始。他又说，各国人民、特别是中国人民都主张和平共处，这也是经济人党的主张。也只有这样，才能使各国人民得到幸福。乃贴·触的努七指出访华团是促进中泰两国友好的桥梁，他希望这座桥梁能得到巩固，最后并且提议为中泰两国的永久友好干杯。
    今晚出席作陪的，有中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之、乔冠华、罗隆基和理事，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民、冀朝鼎，中国新闻工作者联谊会副会长吴冷西、王芸生，中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初等人。
