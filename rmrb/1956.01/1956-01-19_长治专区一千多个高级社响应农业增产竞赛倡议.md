Title: Over a Thousand Advanced Cooperatives in Changzhi Region Respond to Call for Increased Agricultural Production Competition

Author: 
Date: 1956-01-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, January 18th: In Changzhi Region, Shanxi Province, 1,041 advanced agricultural production cooperatives have responded to the call for increased agricultural production competition jointly initiated by Li Shunda and others. These 1,041 advanced cooperatives have all proposed to strive to achieve a yield of 1,000 jin per mu on 100 mu of grain crops per cooperative this year. Among them, 6 advanced cooperatives have proposed the production target of one million jin of grain from one thousand mu. Currently, many other agricultural production cooperatives in Changzhi Region are considering participating in the competition.

### 长治专区一千多个高级社响应农业增产竞赛倡议

1956-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    长治专区一千多个高级社响应农业增产竞赛倡议
    据新华社太原18日电　山西省长治专区已经有1，041个高级农业生产合作社响应李顺达等联名倡议的农业增产竞赛。这1，041个高级社都提出要争取今年每社有100亩粮食作物的产量达到每亩1，000斤。其中有6个高级社提出了千亩百万斤粮食的增产目标。现在，长治专区还有很多农业生产合作社在酝酿参加竞赛。
