    After the cotton cloth industry in the Shizhong District of Chongqing was approved for joint public-private ownership, the staff were jubilant and congratulated each other. This is a picture of employees pasting the golden-red character "喜" (xi, meaning happiness) on the window of the new Da Xiang silk and cloth shop. Photo by Luo Ziguang (Draft from Chongqing Daily)
