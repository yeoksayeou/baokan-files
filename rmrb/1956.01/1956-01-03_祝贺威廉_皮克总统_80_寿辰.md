Title: Congratulations to President Wilhelm Pieck on his 80th Birthday
Author:
Date: 1956-01-03
Page: 1

Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, and Zhou Enlai's telegram to Wilhelm Pieck

To the President of the German Democratic Republic, dear Comrade Wilhelm Pieck:

　　On the occasion of your 80th birthday, please allow us to extend our heartfelt and warm congratulations to you, the leader of the German Democratic Republic, the most beloved leader of the German people, and a close friend of the Chinese people, on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China.

　　We sincerely wish you good health and longevity, and wish you even more brilliant achievements in the cause of striving for the happiness of the German people and the peaceful reunification of Germany, and in building socialism in the German Democratic Republic.
                         Chairman of the People's Republic of China　Mao Zedong
        Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China　Liu Shaoqi
                         Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China　Zhou Enlai
                                           January 1, 1956
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China's telegram to Wilhelm Pieck
　　Dear Comrade Wilhelm Pieck:

　　On the occasion of your 80th birthday, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm and fraternal congratulations to you—the organizer and leader of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, the leader of the German working class and working people, and a close friend of the Chinese people.

　　We wish you good health and longevity, and wish you greater achievements in the socialist construction of the German Democratic Republic, in the struggle for the peaceful reunification of Germany, and in the activities of defending European and world peace.
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China　January 1, 1956

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 2nd: The delegation of the People's Republic of China to congratulate President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic on his 80th birthday, led by Vice Chairman Zhu De of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Berlin by special train at 3 pm on the 1st.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the station were First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and First Deputy Prime Minister of the government of the German Democratic Republic, Ulbricht, Deputy Prime Ministers Stoph, Nuschke, Bolz, Oesner, and Loch, ministers of various government departments, and generals of the People's Police.

Also at the station to welcome the delegation were First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Germany, Reimann, and members of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Heidewan and Ebert.

Also at the station to welcome the delegation were diplomatic envoys from various countries to Germany and the Romanian government delegation that had already arrived in Berlin.

All the staff of the Chinese Embassy in Germany and various Chinese organizations in Germany, all the staff of the Chinese Trade Delegation, and Chinese students studying in Berlin also went to the station to welcome the delegation.

Zhu De shook hands with the welcomers after getting off the train. The band played the national anthems of China and Germany. Zhu De reviewed the guard of honor.

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd: According to TASS, Moscow: The Soviet delegation headed by K.E. Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, left Moscow on January 1 for Berlin to attend the celebration of the 80th birthday of Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic.

The delegation included P.N. Pospelov, O.V. Kuusinen, E.D. Stasova, and G.M. Pushkin.

### 祝贺威廉·皮克总统80寿辰

1956-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　祝贺威廉·皮克总统80寿辰
    毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来致威廉·皮克电
　　德意志民主共和国总统亲爱的威廉·皮克同志：
    当你80寿辰的时候，请允许我们代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向你、德意志民主共和国的领导者、德国人民最敬爱的领袖、中国人民亲密的朋友致衷心的热烈的祝贺。
    我们衷心地祝你健康长寿，并且祝你在争取德国人民的幸福和德意志的和平统一以及在德意志民主共和国建设社会主义的事业中获得更辉煌的成就。
                         中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
        中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长　刘少奇
                         中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                           1956年1月1日
    中国共产党中央委员会致威廉·皮克电
　　亲爱的威廉·皮克同志：
    当您80寿辰之际，中国共产党中央委员会谨向您——德国统一社会党的组织者和领导者、德国工人阶级和劳动人民的领袖、中国人民的亲密朋友，致热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺。
    祝您健康长寿，祝您在德意志民主共和国的社会主义建设事业中，在争取德国和平统一的斗争中，在保卫欧洲和世界和平的活动中，获得更大的成就。
    中国共产党中央委员会　1956年1月1日
　　中华人民共和国代表团到达柏林
    新华社柏林2日电　祝贺德意志民主共和国总统皮克80寿辰的中华人民共和国代表团，在中华人民共和国副主席朱德率领下，在1日下午3时乘专车抵达柏林。
    到车站欢迎的有德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记、德意志民主共和国政府第一副总理乌布利希，副总理斯多夫、努舍克、博尔茨、厄斯纳、洛赫，政府各部部长和人民警察的将领等。
    到车站欢迎的还有德国共产党中央委员会第一书记雷曼，德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局委员希德万和艾柏特。
    到车站欢迎的还有各国驻德外交使节和已经到达柏林的罗马尼亚政府代表团。
    中国驻德大使馆和中国驻德各机关的全体人员、中国贸易代表团的全体人员以及在柏林学习的中国留学生等也到车站欢迎。
    朱德下车后和欢迎者一一握手。乐队奏中德两国国歌。朱德检阅了仪仗队。
　　伏罗希洛夫率领苏联代表团去柏林
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：以苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席克·叶·伏罗希洛夫为首的苏联代表团1月1日离莫斯科赴柏林去参加德意志民主共和国威廉·皮克总统80寿辰庆祝典礼。
    代表团包括彼·尼·波斯伯洛夫，奥·维·库西宁，叶·德·斯塔索娃和格·马·普希金。
