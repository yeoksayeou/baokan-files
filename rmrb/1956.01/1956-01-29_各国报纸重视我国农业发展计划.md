Title: Foreign Newspapers Emphasize China's Agricultural Development Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-01-29
Page: 4

Foreign Newspapers Emphasize China's Agricultural Development Plan
	Soviet Union: The Soviet newspapers "Pravda," "Izvestia," "Red Star," "Trud," "Komsomolskaya Pravda," and "Agricultural Newspaper" all prominently published Chairman Mao Zedong's speech at the Supreme State Conference on the 25th and news of the meeting on January 27th.
	The Soviet people value the development of agricultural cooperation in China. "Pravda" published the full text of the Draft National Program for Agricultural Development in the People's Republic of China from 1956 to 1967, as proposed by the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, on January 28th.
	Vietnam: On January 28th, the Vietnamese "Nhan Dan" and "Hanoi Daily" both prominently published the news of Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China convening the Supreme State Conference on January 25th. "Nhan Dan" also published an abstract of the Draft National Program for Agricultural Development in China from 1956 to 1967.
	India: The Indian "New Age" weekly published the full text of Chairman Mao Zedong's "Preface to 'The Socialist Upsurge in China's Countryside'" in its latest issue. The "Indian Express" published news on January 28th about Chairman Mao Zedong's speech at the Supreme State Conference on January 25th and the Supreme State Conference's discussion of the National Program for Agricultural Development.

### 各国报纸重视我国农业发展计划

1956-01-29
第4版()
专栏：

    各国报纸重视我国农业发展计划
    苏　联　苏联“真理报”、“消息报”、“红星报”、“劳动报”、“共青真理报”、“农业报”1月27日都以显著地位刊载了毛泽东主席在25日最高国务会议上的讲话和会议消息。
    苏联人民重视我国农业合作化的发展。“真理报”28日刊登了中共中央政治局提出的中华人民共和国1956年到1967年农业发展纲要草案全文。
    越　南　1月28日，越南“人民报”和“河内日报”都以显著地位刊登了中华人民共和国毛泽东主席在1月25日召集最高国务会议的消息。“人民报”还刊登了1956年到1967年中国农业发展纲要草案的摘要。
    印　度　印度“新世纪”周报在最近一期中全文刊登了毛泽东主席所作的“‘中国农村的社会主义高潮’序言”。“印度快报”1月28日刊载了关于毛泽东主席1月25日在最高国务会议上讲话以及最高国务会议讨论全国农业发展纲要的消息。
