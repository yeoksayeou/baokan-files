Title: Chairman of the Japan Socialist Party States that the Time for Normalization of Sino-Japanese Relations Has Arrived
Author: 
Date: 1956-01-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo: Suzuki Mosaburo, Chairman of the Japan Socialist Party, who is currently traveling in Kyushu, said at a press conference in Kumamoto City on the 13th that the Socialist Party believes that the time for normalizing relations between Japan and China has arrived. The method is to end the state of war with China, conclude a peace treaty, and exchange diplomatic envoys.

Suzuki Mosaburo also said that he would request Japanese Prime Minister Hatoyama to send representatives to China to discuss Japan's recognition of the Beijing government and the measures Japan should take regarding Taiwan.

In his speech, Suzuki Mosaburo also stated that he would suggest that the U.S. and Chinese governments hold negotiations between Dulles and Zhou Enlai.

### 日本社会党主席表示  中日关系正常化的时间已经到来

1956-01-15
第4版()
专栏：

    日本社会党主席表示
    中日关系正常化的时间已经到来
    新华社讯  东京消息：正在九州旅行的日本社会党主席铃木茂三郎13日在熊本市的记者招待会上说，社会党认为日本同中国的关系正常化的时候已经到来了，办法是同中国结束战争状态、缔结和约和互派外交使节。
    铃木茂三郎还说，他将要求日本首相鸠山派遣代表前往中国，以便讨论日本关于承认北京政府和日本对台湾应采取的措施。
    铃木茂三郎在谈话中还表示：他将建议美国和中国政府举行杜勒斯和周恩来之间的谈判。
