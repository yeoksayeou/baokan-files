Title: French National Assembly Opens

Author:

Date: 1956-01-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 20th dispatch from TASS: The French National Assembly, elected on January 2nd, opened on the 19th. According to the constitution, the session was presided over by the oldest member, the most famous leader of the French workers' movement, Communist member Cachin.

    In his speech, Cachin congratulated the new members and condemned the attempts by certain reactionary politicians to restore the past reactionary electoral system. Cachin emphasized that proportional representation is the most reasonable system.

    Cachin emphasized that among the French working people, the desire for a Popular Front is growing stronger, and the entire French people are striving to further ease international tensions.

    Cachin then announced the establishment of 10 credentials committees to review the qualifications of the members of the National Assembly.

    After the credentials committees of the National Assembly have reviewed the qualifications of the members, the National Assembly will hold the election of the Speaker. After this, Faure will submit his resignation to the President.

### 法国国民议会开幕

1956-01-21
第4版()
专栏：

    法国国民议会开幕
    据新华社20日讯　据塔斯社巴黎20日讯：1月2日选举选出的法国国民议会在19日开幕。根据宪法规定，会议由最年长的议员、法国工人运动最著名的领袖、共产党员加香担任主席。
    加香在演说中向新议员致贺，并且谴责某些反动政客要恢复过去的反动选举制度的企图。加香强调指出，比例代表制是最合理的制度。
    加香强调指出，在法国劳动人民当中，对人民阵线的渴望越来越强烈，全体法国人民都力求争取进一步缓和国际紧张局势。
    加香随后宣布设立10个资格审查委员会来审查国民议会议员的资格。
    在国民议会资格审查委员会审查了议员的资格之后，国民议会就要举行议长选举。在这以后，富尔将向总统提出政府辞职声明。
