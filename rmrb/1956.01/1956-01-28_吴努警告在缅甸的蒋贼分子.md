Title: U Nu Warns Chiang Kai-shek Bandits in Burma
Author:
Date: 1956-01-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 26th. According to a report from the Indian Press Trust in Rangoon on January 25th, Burmese Prime Minister U Nu warned Chiang Kai-shek elements in Burma at a press conference on the 24th. He told reporters that if they continued to recklessly and maliciously attack foreign guests visiting Burma as state guests, they would be expelled from the country.

### 吴努警告在缅甸的蒋贼分子

1956-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

    吴努警告在缅甸的蒋贼分子
    新华社新德里26日电　据印度报业托辣斯仰光25日消息，缅甸总理吴努24日在记者招待会上警告在缅甸的蒋贼分子记者说，如果他们继续对作为国宾来到缅甸访问的外国客人肆意进行恶毒抨击，那么就要把他们驱逐出境。
