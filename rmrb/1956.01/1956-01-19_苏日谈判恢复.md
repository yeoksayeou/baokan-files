Title: Soviet-Japanese Negotiations Resume
Author:
Date: 1956-01-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, January 18th: TASS London, January 18th: The Soviet and Japanese sides released a joint communiqué in London on January 17th regarding the Soviet-Japanese negotiations.

The communiqué said: "Soviet-Japanese negotiations for the normalization of relations between the Soviet Union and Japan have resumed in London on January 17th. Soviet representative Malik and Japanese representative Shunichi Matsumoto reached an agreement on the future work program.

The next meeting will be held on January 24th."

### 苏日谈判恢复

1956-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    苏日谈判恢复
    新华社18日讯　塔斯社伦敦18日讯：苏日双方1月17日在伦敦公布了关于苏日谈判的联合公报。
    公报说：“关于苏日两国关系正常化的苏日谈判已经在1月17日在伦敦恢复。苏联代表马立克和日本代表松本俊一就今后的工作程序达成了协议。
    下次会议将在1月24日举行。”
