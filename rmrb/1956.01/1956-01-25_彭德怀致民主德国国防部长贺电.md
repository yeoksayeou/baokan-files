Title: Peng Dehuai Sends Congratulatory Telegram to the Minister of National Defense of the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1956-01-25
Page: 1

Peng Dehuai Sends Congratulatory Telegram to the Minister of National Defense of the German Democratic Republic

To Comrade Willi Stoph, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Defense of the German Democratic Republic:

    I am pleased to learn that the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic has passed the law on the establishment of the National People's Army and the Ministry of National Defense, and that you have been appointed as the Minister of National Defense of the German Democratic Republic. I would like to extend my warm congratulations to you and wish you further success in strengthening the national defense forces of the German Democratic Republic. May the unbreakable friendship between the people and armies of China and Germany be increasingly consolidated and developed.
                                 
                                 Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, Marshal of the People's Republic of China
                                 January 24, 1956, Beijing

### 彭德怀致民主德国国防部长贺电

1956-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    彭德怀致民主德国国防部长贺电德意志民主共和国副总理兼国防部长威利·斯多夫同志：
    欣悉德意志民主共和国人民议院通过关于建立国家人民军和国防部的法律，和您被任命为德意志民主共和国国防部长，我谨向您致以热烈的祝贺，并祝您在加强德意志民主共和国国防力量的事业中获得进一步的成就。愿中、德两国人民和军队间牢不可破的友谊日益巩固和发展。中华人民共和国副总理兼国防部长中华人民共和国元帅彭德怀1956年1月24日于北京
