Title: Beijing Holds Ceremony for Inauguration of China-Mongolia-Soviet Union Railway Through Transport

Author:

Date: 1956-01-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th: Tonight, more than a thousand representatives from all walks of life in Beijing and railway workers held a grand ceremony at the railway station, which was decorated with the national flags of the Soviet Union, Mongolia, and China, as well as large portraits of Bulganin, Tsedenbal, and Mao Zedong, to mark the inauguration of China-Mongolia-Soviet Union railway through transport. The first direct passenger train from Beijing to Ulaanbaatar also departed from Beijing Station at 10:50 tonight.

Attending the ceremony were Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China; Wang Shoudao, Director of the Sixth Office of the State Council; Lü Zhengcao, Wu Jingtain, and Liu Jianzhang, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Railways; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade; Feng Jiping, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; and heads of various departments of the Ministry of Railways.

Also attending the ceremony were all members of the Mongolian government delegation headed by Lubsan, the Soviet government delegation headed by Beshev, and the Chinese government delegation headed by Teng Daiyuan. The Mongolian and Soviet government delegations were invited by the government of the People's Republic of China and arrived by special train at 9:30 tonight to attend the inauguration ceremony of the China-Mongolia-Soviet Union railway through transport held in Beijing tonight.

Mongolian Ambassador to China Ochirbat and Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin also attended the ceremony.

The ceremony was presided over by Lü Zhengcao, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Railways.

As the ceremony began, the bands played the national anthems of the Soviet Union, Mongolia, and China.

At the ceremony, Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, spoke first. The next speakers were Lubsan, head of the Mongolian government delegation, and Beshev, head of the Soviet government delegation. In their speeches, they unanimously pointed out that the inauguration of China-Mongolia-Soviet Union railway through transport marks a significant development in the friendly cooperation between the three countries. It will strengthen economic cooperation and cultural exchange between the three countries, promote the overall upsurge of their economies and cultures, and make friendly exchanges between the peoples of the three countries more convenient and prosperous. Thus, it will enhance the unity and strength of the peace, democracy, and socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and will surely make beneficial contributions to the cause of safeguarding world peace.

At the ceremony, Lubsan, head of the government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, also presented a banner to the Chinese railway workers on behalf of the Mongolian railway workers.

At 10:45, Teng Daiyuan, Minister of Railways, cut the ribbon, and the band simultaneously played cheerful music. At this moment, the heads of the three governments of China, Mongolia, and the Soviet Union held hands tightly for a long time. The train, adorned with portraits of Bulganin, Tsedenbal, and Mao Zedong and decorated with ribbons, slowly left the station amidst the cheers and applause of the people, heading towards Ulaanbaatar, the heroic capital of the Mongolian people.

### 北京举行中蒙苏三国铁路联运通车典礼

1956-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京举行中蒙苏三国铁路联运通车典礼
    新华社4日讯　今晚，北京市各界代表和铁路职工一千多人，在悬挂着苏、蒙、中三国国旗和布尔加宁、泽登巴尔、毛泽东的巨幅画像的车站上，举行了盛大的中蒙苏三国铁路联运通车典礼。从北京开往乌兰巴托的第一次直达客车也在今天晚上10时50分开出北京站。
    参加典礼的，有中华人民共和国国务院副总理李先念，国务院第六办公室主任王首道，铁道部副部长吕正操、武竞天、刘建章，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，北京市副市长冯基平以及铁道部各部门的负责人。
    参加典礼的，还有以鲁布桑为首的蒙古政府代表团，以别谢夫为首的苏联政府代表团和以滕代远为首的中国政府代表团的全体人员。蒙古和苏联两国政府代表团是应中华人民共和国政府邀请在今天晚上9时30分乘专车前来参加今晚在北京举行的中蒙苏三国铁路联运通车典礼的。
    蒙古驻中国大使奥其尔巴特、苏联驻中国大使尤金也参加了典礼。
    典礼大会由铁道部副部长吕正操主持。
    典礼开始，乐队奏苏、蒙、中三国国歌。
    在典礼庆祝大会上，中华人民共和国国务院副总理李先念首先讲话。接着讲话的，是蒙古政府代表团团长鲁布桑和苏联政府代表团团长别谢夫。他们在讲话中一致指出，中蒙苏三国铁路的联运通车，标志着三国友好合作事业的重大发展，它将加强三国之间的经济合作和文化交流，促进三国经济和文化的普遍高涨，并使三国人民之间的友好往来更为便利和繁荣，从而增强了以苏联为首的和平、民主、社会主义阵营的团结和力量，对于维护世界和平事业必将作出有益的贡献。
    典礼大会上，蒙古人民共和国政府代表团团长鲁布桑还代表蒙古铁路职工向中国铁路职工赠送了锦旗。
    10时45分，铁道部部长滕代远剪彩，乐队同时奏起欢快的乐曲。这时候中蒙苏三国政府代表团团长长久地把手紧紧地握在一起，悬挂着布尔加宁、泽登巴尔、毛泽东的画像和扎着彩的列车，在人们欢腾的掌声中徐徐驶出车站，向蒙古人民的英雄首都乌兰巴托驶去。
