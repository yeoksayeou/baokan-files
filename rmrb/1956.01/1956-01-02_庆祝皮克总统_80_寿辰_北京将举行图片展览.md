Title: Beijing to Hold Photo Exhibition Celebrating President Pieck's 80th Birthday
Author:
Date: 1956-01-02
Page: 1

    Celebrating President Pieck's 80th Birthday
    Beijing to Hold Photo Exhibition
    Xinhua News Agency, January 1st. A photo exhibition celebrating the 80th birthday of Comrade Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, will begin on January 3rd at the waterside pavilion in Zhongshan Park, organized by the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations.

### 庆祝皮克总统80寿辰  北京将举行图片展览

1956-01-02
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝皮克总统80寿辰
    北京将举行图片展览
    新华社1日讯　1月3日是德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克同志80寿辰。对外文化联络局在中山公园水榭举办的庆祝德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克同志80寿辰图片展览将在3日开始展出。
