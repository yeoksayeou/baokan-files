Title: Ton Duc Thang Receives Stalin Peace Prize
Author:
Date: 1956-01-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 21st: A ceremony was held on January 21st in the Sverdlov Hall of the Moscow Kremlin to award the "International Stalin Prize for Strengthening Peace Among Peoples" to the outstanding Vietnamese social activist Ton Duc Thang. Many representatives from all circles in Moscow attended the ceremony.

### 孙德胜接受斯大林和平奖金

1956-01-23
第4版()
专栏：

    孙德胜接受斯大林和平奖金
    新华社据塔斯社莫斯科讯　1月21日，莫斯科克里姆林宫的斯维德洛夫大厅中举行了授奖仪式，把“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金授予越南杰出的社会活动家孙德胜。莫斯科各界的许多代表参加了这一仪式。
