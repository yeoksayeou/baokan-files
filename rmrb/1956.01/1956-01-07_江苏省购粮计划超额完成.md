Title: Jiangsu Province Exceeds Grain Purchase Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-01-07
Page: 2
Jiangsu Province Overfulfills Grain Purchase Plan
    The peasants of Jiangsu Province are enthusiastically selling their surplus grain to the state after a bumper harvest. By mid-December 1955, the total amount of grain purchased and stored in the province (including circulating grain) had exceeded the annual plan, about 132% of the actual storage performance in the same period of 1954. (Zeng Ping)

### 江苏省购粮计划超额完成

1956-01-07
第2版()
专栏：

    江苏省购粮计划超额完成
    江苏省农民踊跃把丰收后的余粮卖给国家。到1955年12月中旬，全省粮食收购入库总数（包括周转粮在内），已经超额完成年度计划，约为1954年同期入库实绩的132％。（曾萍）
