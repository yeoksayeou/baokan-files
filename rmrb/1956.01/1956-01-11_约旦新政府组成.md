Title: Formation of New Jordanian Government
Author:
Date: 1956-01-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Amman, 10th. The new Jordanian government headed by Samir al-Rifai was formed on January 9th. The new Prime Minister Samir al-Rifai stated that evening, "It is not the policy of my government to participate in any new treaties." Rifai's statement is considered a guarantee not to join the Baghdad Pact.

Rifai said, "We will continue to work to strengthen cooperation with Arab countries, as well as our friendly and fraternal relations with Arab countries." Rifai also said that his government "understands the public's demands and the nation's interests."

It is reported that seven ministers in the new Jordanian government are members of the previous Hashim caretaker cabinet. Prime Minister Rifai concurrently serves as Minister of the Interior. Former Prime Minister Hashim serves as Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of State in the new government.

### 约旦新政府组成

1956-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    约旦新政府组成
    新华社10日讯　安曼消息：以萨米尔·里法伊为首的约旦新政府已经在1月9日组成。新任首相萨米尔·里法伊在当天晚上发表声明说：“参加任何新的条约都不是我的政府的政策。”里法伊的声明被认为是不参加巴格达条约的保证。
    里法伊说：“我们将继续努力加强同阿拉伯国家的合作，以及我们同阿拉伯国家的友好和兄弟关系。”里法伊又说，他的政府“了解公众的要求和国家的利益”。
    据悉，约旦新政府中的七位大臣是前哈希姆看守内阁的阁员。首相里法伊自兼内政大臣。前首相哈希姆在新政府中任副首相兼国务大臣。
