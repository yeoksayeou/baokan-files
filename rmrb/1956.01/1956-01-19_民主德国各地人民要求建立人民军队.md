Title: People in Various Parts of Democratic Germany Demand the Establishment of a People's Army
Author:
Date: 1956-01-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 18th. According to a report by the German News Agency: People in various parts of the German Democratic Republic are responding to the call made during the demonstration in Berlin commemorating Karl Liebknecht and Rosa Luxemburg to establish a people's army in this republic.

	About 320 workers from the Rostock Fishing Cooperative wrote in a resolution submitted to the People's Chamber: Faced with the establishment of mercenary armies in West Germany and the transformation of the border police into regular troops, we cannot stand idly by while our peaceful construction is threatened. We demand the establishment of a combat-ready people's army to safeguard the peaceful construction work of our young republic.

	On January 16th, at a youth rally held at the Ernst Thälmann Heavy Machinery Factory in Suhl, approximately 600 attendees passed a resolution demanding the establishment of a people's army.

	Approximately 1,600 employees of the Miran Palar Wool and Silk Factory (in the Karl-Marx-Stadt district) also demanded that the People's Chamber make a resolution to establish a people's army.

	On January 16th, a grand rally was held in Frankfurt on the Oder River, and the attendees also made the same demand in a telegram to the People's Chamber.

### 民主德国各地人民要求建立人民军队

1956-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国各地人民要求建立人民军队
    新华社柏林18日电　据德意志通讯社报道：德意志民主共和国各地人民纷纷响应在柏林纪念卡尔·李卜克内西和罗萨·卢森堡的示威中提出的在这个共和国中建立一支人民军队的要求。
    罗斯托克捕鱼联营组织的约320个工人在向人民议院提出的决议中写道：在西德建立雇佣军和把边防警察变成正规军队的情况下，我们不能对我们的和平建设受到威胁这一点采取旁观态度。我们要求建立一支有战斗准备的人民军队，以保障我们年轻的共和国的和平建设工作。
    16日，在苏尔的恩斯特·台尔曼重型机器制造工厂举行的青年大会上，约600个到会者通过了一项要求建立人民军队的决议。
    米拉湼·帕拉羊毛和丝织工厂（在卡尔·马克思斯塔特区）的约1，600个职工也要求人民议院作出建立人民军队的决议。
    16日，在奥得河畔的法兰克福举行了一次盛大的集会，到会者在给人民议院的一封电报中也提出了同样的要求。
