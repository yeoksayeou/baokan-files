Title: Two Factories in Tianjin Exceed 1957 Production Targets Last Year
Author:
Date: 1956-01-12
Page: 2

Two Factories in Tianjin
　　Last Year's Total Output Value Exceeded 1957 Targets

　　The total output value of the Tianjin North China Bone Glue Factory and the Tianjin No. 1 Printing and Dyeing Factory in 1955 has already exceeded the total output value targets for 1957. Last year, workers at the North China Bone Glue Factory used the seasonal downtime during the summer when they don't produce hide glue and bone glue to increase the production of bone fertilizer. Last year, workers at the Tianjin No. 1 Printing and Dyeing Factory continuously improved product quality. The printed cloth they produced was welcomed by the broad masses of people in urban and rural areas. As sales increased, production also gradually rose.
　　　　　　　　　　(Xinhua News Agency)

### 天津市两个工厂  去年总产值超过1957年指标

1956-01-12
第2版()
专栏：

    天津市两个工厂
    去年总产值超过1957年指标
    天津市华北骨胶厂和天津市第一印染厂1955年的生产总值都已经超过了1957年的总产值指标。华北骨胶厂的职工去年利用夏季不生产皮胶、骨胶的季节性停工的时间，增加了骨肥的生产。天津市第一印染厂的职工去年不断提高产品质量，所生产的花布受到城乡广大人民的欢迎，销售量增加以后产量也随着逐步上升。
                    （新华社）
