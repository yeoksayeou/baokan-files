Title: 1956 National Ice Sports Meet Ice Hockey Preliminary Round Concludes
Author:
Date: 1956-01-27
Page: 3

The ice hockey preliminary round of the 1956 National Ice Sports Meet, which began on January 20th in Changchun, concluded on the 24th.

Thirteen ice hockey teams from Beijing, Harbin, Changchun, Anshan, Yanji, and other locations participated in the competition, an increase of four teams compared to the 1955 National Ice Sports Meet. As a result of the competition, the ice hockey teams from Beijing, Harbin, Changchun, Shenyang, Jilin, and Qiqihar secured the right to compete in the finals. (Reported by Xinhua News Agency) (Attached Picture)
The ice hockey preliminary round of the 1956 National Ice Sports Meet was held in Changchun. This is a scene from the match between the Shenyang team and the Harbin team. Xinhua News Agency reporter Xue Yin photographed.

### 1956年全国冰上运动会冰球预赛结束

1956-01-27
第3版()
专栏：

    1956年全国冰上运动会冰球预赛结束
    1956年全国冰上运动会的冰球预赛，1月20日开始在长春举行，24日结束。
    参加比赛的有北京、哈尔滨、长春、鞍山、延吉等地的13个冰球代表队，比参加1955年全国冰上运动大会的冰球队增加了4个队。比赛结果，北京、哈尔滨、长春、沈阳、吉林、齐齐哈尔等6个冰球队取得了决赛权。    （据新华社讯）（附图片）
