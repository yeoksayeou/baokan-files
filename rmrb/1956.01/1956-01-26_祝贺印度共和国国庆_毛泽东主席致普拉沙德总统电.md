Title: Congratulations on the National Day of the Republic of India - Chairman Mao Zedong's Telegram to President Prasad
Author:
Date: 1956-01-26
Page: 1

    Congratulations on the National Day of the Republic of India
    Chairman Mao Zedong's Telegram to His Excellency Rajendra Prasad, President of the Republic of India:
    On the occasion of the National Day of the Republic of India, I extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the great Indian people on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish the close friendship and cooperation between China and India will be increasingly consolidated and strengthened in the lofty cause of safeguarding peace in Asia and the world, and I wish Your Excellency and the Indian people new and greater achievements in the work of seeking the prosperity of the Republic of India.
           Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong
             January 24, 1956, in Beijing

### 祝贺印度共和国国庆  毛泽东主席致普拉沙德总统电

1956-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺印度共和国国庆
    毛泽东主席致普拉沙德总统电印度共和国总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德阁下：
    欣逢印度共和国国庆，我谨代表中国人民并且以我个人的名义向阁下和伟大的印度人民致诚挚的祝贺。祝中印两国之间的亲密友谊和合作在维护亚洲和世界和平的崇高事业中日益巩固和加强，祝阁下和印度人民在谋求印度共和国繁荣的工作中获得新的、更大的成就。
           中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
             1956年1月24日于北京
