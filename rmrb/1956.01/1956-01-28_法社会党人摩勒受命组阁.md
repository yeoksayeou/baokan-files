Title: Socialist Mollet Tasked with Forming a Cabinet
Author: 
Date: 1956-01-28
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, January 27th, Paris dispatch: French President Coty summoned the General Secretary of the Socialist Party, Mollet, on the evening of January 26th and entrusted him with the task of forming a new government. Mollet accepted this task.
    The "Republican Front" of the Socialist Party, Radical Socialist Party, etc., controls only 188 seats in the National Assembly, so if Mollet decides to request parliamentary authorization to form a cabinet, he will need the support of parties outside the "Republican Front."

### 法社会党人摩勒受命组阁

1956-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

    法社会党人摩勒受命组阁
    新华社27日讯　巴黎消息：法国总统戈蒂在1月26日晚上召见了社会党总书记摩勒，委托他组织新政府。摩勒接受了这个任务。
    社会党和激进社会党等的“共和阵线”在国民议会中只控制了188席，因此如果摩勒决定要求议会授权他组阁，他就需要“共和阵线”以外的党派的支持。
