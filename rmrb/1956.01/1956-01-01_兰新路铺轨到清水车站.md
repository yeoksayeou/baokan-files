Title: Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway Tracks Laid to Qingshui Station
Author: 
Date: 1956-01-01
Page: 1
The Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway tracks were laid to Qingshui Station on December 27, 1955.

In 1955, the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway laid more than 326 kilometers of tracks, approaching the total track-laying mileage of 1953 and 1954, and exceeding the national plan by 138 kilometers.

Now, the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway has extended westward for more than 676 kilometers, leaving only over 150 kilometers from Baiyang River, the track-laying terminus stipulated in the first Five-Year Plan west of Yumen. The track-laying task of the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway in the first Five-Year Plan can be completed ahead of schedule in 1956. (Our correspondent in Lanzhou)

### 兰新路铺轨到清水车站

1956-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

    兰新路铺轨到清水车站
    兰新铁路已经在1955年12月27日铺轨到清水车站。
    兰新铁路1955年共铺轨326公里多，接近1953年和1954年铺轨里程的总和，超过国家计划138公里。
    现在，兰新铁路已经向西延伸了676公里多，距离第一个五年计划规定的铺轨终点——玉门以西的白洋河只剩下150多公里。兰新铁路在第一个五年计划内的铺轨任务，可以在1956年提前完成。（本报驻兰州记者）
