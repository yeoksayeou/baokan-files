Title: Meishan Reservoir Arch Dam Completed Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1956-01-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hefei, January 13th: On the morning of January 12th, the arch dam of the Meishan Reservoir in the upper reaches of the Shi River, a tributary of the Huai River, was completely poured to the top, 109 days ahead of the original schedule. At that time, there was warm applause and cheers on the construction site, and dozens of red flags were raised on the majestic arch dam. The persons in charge of the reservoir project headquarters rushed to the top of the dam to shake hands and congratulate the workers. In the afternoon, more than 4,000 employees gathered at the construction site and held a grand celebration.

The arch dam of the Meishan Reservoir is 558 meters long in total (including the extended sections on both sides) and 84.4 meters high, and is the main part of the entire reservoir project. At present, the construction of the reservoir's spillway and other projects is continuing.

### 梅山水库连拱坝提前浇筑到顶

1956-01-15
第1版()
专栏：

    梅山水库连拱坝提前浇筑到顶
    新华社合肥13日电  12日上午，淮河支流史河上游梅山水库的连拱坝比原定计划提前109天全部浇筑到顶。当时工地上响起了一片热烈的掌声和欢呼声，雄伟的连拱坝上升起了几十面红旗。水库工程指挥部的负责人都赶到坝顶向工人握手道贺。下午，4，000多名职工齐集在工地上，举行了盛大的庆祝会。
    梅山水库的连拱坝全长558公尺（包括两边延长段），高84.4公尺，是整个水库工程主要部分。现在，水库的溢洪道等工程正在继续修建。
