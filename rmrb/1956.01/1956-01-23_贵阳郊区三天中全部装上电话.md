Title: All Telephones Installed in Guiyang Suburbs in Three Days
Author:
Date: 1956-01-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch:

    The employees of the Guizhou Provincial Postal and Telecommunications管理局 (Burearu) proposed shortening the plan to install telephones in the suburbs of Guiyang from six months to within three days. Starting from the 18th, more than 100 employees were dispatched to various townships to install telephones. By the morning of the 21st, all installation work was completed.

### 贵阳郊区三天中全部装上电话

1956-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    贵阳郊区三天中全部装上电话
    贵州省邮电管理局的职工提出把半年内在贵阳市郊区安上电话的计划缩短成三天内完成。从18日起便出动了100多个职工到各乡安装电话。到21日上午，全部安装工作就结束了。
      （据新华社讯）
