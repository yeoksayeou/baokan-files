Title: Resolution of the Albanian Labor Party Central Committee Plenary Session on the Implementation of the National Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-01-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, January 1st: According to TASS, the Albanian Labor Party Central Committee plenary session, held at the end of December 1955, adopted a resolution on the implementation of Albania's 1955 national plan and on measures for implementing the 1956 national plan.

The resolution pointed out that Albania's major industrial sectors, construction, and transportation have victoriously completed and overfulfilled the 1955 plan. The fulfillment of the plan in major agricultural sectors is also satisfactory.

Compared with 1954, the total industrial output in 1955 increased by 13%. In 1955, oil extraction was 12% higher than in 1954, coal was 30% higher, electricity production increased by 35%, copper was 44% higher, and building materials were 84% higher.

The resolution then pointed out that expanding agricultural cooperation has now become necessary and feasible. We have every possibility to double the number of agricultural cooperatives and their members.

The construction area in 1955 increased by 28.7% compared to 1954. The people's material living standards have been significantly improved, and achievements have also been made in education, culture, and health.

The plenary session approved the basic draft of the 1956 national plan. This draft stipulates that in 1956 the production of means of production will increase by 16%, and the production of consumer goods will increase by 8%. In 1956, the sown area will reach 380,000 hectares, 9,000 hectares of land will be reclaimed, and the total amount of agricultural tillage mechanization will be 28% more than in 1955.

### 阿劳动党中央全会关于执行国家计划的决议

1956-01-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　阿劳动党中央全会关于执行国家计划的决议
    新华社1日讯　据塔斯社地拉那讯：1955年12月底举行的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会全体会议通过了关于阿尔巴尼亚1955年国家计划执行情况和关于执行1956年国家计划的措施的决议。
    决议指出，阿尔巴尼亚各主要工业部门、建筑业和交通运输业已经胜利地完成和超额完成了1955年的计划。各主要农业部门完成计划的情况也是令人满意的。
    和1954年相比，1955年的工业总产量增长了13％。1955年石油的开采量比1954年多12％，煤多30％，电力生产增长了35％，铜多44％，建筑材料多84％。
    决议接着指出，现在，扩大农业合作化已经成为必需的和可行的了。我们有一切可能使农业合作社和社员的数目增加一倍。
    1955年建筑面积比1954年增加了28.7％。人民的物质生活水平已经显著提高，教育、文化和卫生方面也取得了成就。
    全体会议赞同1956年国家计划的基本草案。这个草案规定：1956年生产资料的生产将增加16％，消费品生产将增加8％。1956年播种面积将达38万公顷，将开垦九千公顷土地，农业耕作机械化的总量将比1955年多28％。
