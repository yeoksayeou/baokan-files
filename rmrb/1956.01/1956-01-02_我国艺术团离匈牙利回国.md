Title: Chinese Art Troupe Leaves Hungary for Home
Author:
Date: 1956-01-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague dispatch from Budapest: The Chinese Art Troupe, which was invited to perform in Hungary, left Hungary by train on the evening of December 30th to return home.

　　The Chinese Art Troupe began performing in Budapest on December 22nd. During their performances, they also held friendly meetings with actors from the Budapest National Opera and the Hungarian National People's Cultural Troupe, who had previously visited China, watched their performances, exchanged work experiences, and exchanged gifts.

### 我国艺术团离匈牙利回国

1956-01-02
第4版()
专栏：

    我国艺术团离匈牙利回国
    据新华社布拉格电　布达佩斯消息：应邀到匈牙利演出的中国艺术团在12月30日晚上乘车离匈牙利返国。
    中国艺术团是12月22日开始在布达佩斯演出的。他们在演出期间，还先后同布达佩斯国家歌剧院的演员和曾经访问过中国的匈牙利国家人民文工团作了友好的会见，观看了他们的演出，交流了工作经验，并且互赠了礼品。
