Title: Meeting of the Political Consultative Committee of the Warsaw Treaty Member States
Author:
Date: 1956-01-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 27th: The meeting of the Political Consultative Committee of the Warsaw Treaty member states opened in Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia, on January 27th. The meeting will discuss issues arising from the Warsaw Treaty and joint measures.

At 10:00 AM, representatives of the Warsaw Treaty member states participating in the meeting arrived in the hall of the Czernin Palace, the seat of the Czechoslovak Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and took their seats at the conference table.

The Soviet representatives were Vyacheslav Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, and Georgy Zhukov, Minister of Defense and Marshal of the Soviet Union; the Polish representatives were Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland, and Konstantin Rokossovsky, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Defense, Marshal of Poland; the Czechoslovak representatives were Alexei Čepička, First Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Defense of Czechoslovakia, and Václav David, Minister of Foreign Affairs; the representatives of the German Democratic Republic were Walter Ulbricht, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic, and Willi Stoph, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense, Colonel General; the Hungarian representatives were András Hegedüs, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Hungary, and István Bata, Minister of Defense; the Romanian representatives were Chivu Stoica, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Romania, and Leontin Sălăjan, Minister of Armed Forces, Colonel General; the Bulgarian representatives were Georgi Damyanov, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria, and Pancho Panchevski, Minister of Defense, Colonel General; the Albanian representatives were Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Albania, and Beqir Balluku, Minister of Defense, Lieutenant General.

Also participating in the meeting were Ivan Konev, Commander-in-Chief of the Joint Armed Forces of the Warsaw Treaty Member States, Marshal of the Soviet Union, and Aleksei Antonov, General of the Army of the Soviet Union.

Also participating in the meeting was Nie Rongzhen, observer from the People's Republic of China, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China, Marshal of the People's Republic of China.

As the meeting was about to begin, Czechoslovak and foreign journalists, photographers, and cinematographers entered the venue.

The first meeting began with an opening speech by Alexei Čepička, First Deputy Prime Minister of Czechoslovakia.

It has been determined that Mehmet Shehu, representative of the People's Republic of Albania, will preside over this meeting.

### 华沙条约缔约国开政治协商委员会会议

1956-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

    华沙条约缔约国开政治协商委员会会议
    新华社布拉格27日电　华沙条约缔约国政治协商委员会会议，1月27日在捷克斯洛伐克首都布拉格开幕。会议将讨论由于华沙条约而产生的问题和共同措施。
    10时正，参加会议的华沙条约各缔约国代表，来到了捷克斯洛伐克外交部所在地契尔宁斯基宫的大厅中，在会议桌旁就座。
    苏联代表是苏联部长会议第一副主席兼苏联外交部长莫洛托夫和国防部长、苏联元帅朱可夫；波兰代表是波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹和部长会议副主席、国防部长、波兰元帅罗科索夫斯基；捷克斯洛伐克代表是捷克斯洛伐克第一副总理、国防部长契皮茨卡大将和外交部长戴维；德意志民主共和国代表是德意志民主共和国部长会议第一副主席乌布利希和部长会议副主席兼国防部长斯多夫上将；匈牙利的代表是匈牙利部长会议主席赫格居斯和国防部长巴塔；罗马尼亚的代表是罗马尼亚部长会议主席斯托伊卡和武装部队部长萨拉扬上将；保加利亚的代表是保加利亚部长会议副主席达米扬诺夫和国防部长潘切夫斯基大将；阿尔巴尼亚的代表是阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席谢胡和国防部长巴卢库中将。
    参加会议的还有华沙条约缔约国联合武装部队总司令、苏联元帅科涅夫和苏联陆军大将安东诺夫。
    参加会议的还有中华人民共和国观察员、中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席、中华人民共和国元帅聂荣臻。
    会议快开始的时候，捷克斯洛伐克和外国的新闻记者、摄影记者和电影摄影师进入会场。
    第一次会议开始由捷克斯洛伐克第一副总理契皮茨卡致开幕词。
    已经确定由阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国代表谢胡主持这一次会议。
