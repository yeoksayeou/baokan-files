Title: China and Romania Sign 1956 Trade and Payment Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-01-05
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, January 4th. After sincere and friendly negotiations, the government trade delegation of the People's Republic of China and the government trade delegation of the Romanian People's Republic signed the China-Romania 1956 Trade and Payment Agreement in Bucharest on January 3rd.

　　The agreement stipulates that China will supply Romania with iron ore, asbestos, wool, hides, food, tea, jute, cotton yarn and other materials needed for the national economy. Romania will supply China with power generation equipment, deep well drilling equipment, tractors, tank cars, oil and petroleum products and other materials needed for the national economy.

　　Ke Bonian, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Romania, signed the agreement on behalf of the People's Republic of China, and Nicolae Anghel, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Romanian People's Republic, signed the agreement on behalf of the Romanian People's Republic.

### 中罗签订1956年换货和付款协定

1956-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　中罗签订1956年换货和付款协定
    新华社布加勒斯特4日电　中华人民共和国政府贸易代表团和罗马尼亚人民共和国政府贸易代表团经过诚挚友好的谈判后，1月3日在布加勒斯特签订了中罗1956年换货和付款协定。
    协定规定，中国方面将供给罗马尼亚铁矿砂、石棉、羊毛、皮张、食品、茶叶、黄麻、棉纱等国民经济所需要的物资。罗马尼亚方面将供给中国发电设备、深井钻探设备、拖拉机、油槽车、石油和石油产品等国民经济所需要的物资。
    代表中华人民共和国在协定上签字的是中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年，代表罗马尼亚人民共和国在协定上签字的是罗马尼亚人民共和国对外贸易部副部长尼古拉·安格尔。
