Title: Soviet Union Promulgates Draft of Sixth Five-Year Plan
Author: Li He
Date: 1956-01-16
Page: 4

Moscow Dispatch from Our Newspaper, January 15th: The draft of the Soviet Union's Sixth Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy (1956-1960) was promulgated today. This draft, formulated by the Central Committee of the CPSU, will be submitted to the 20th Congress of the CPSU, to be held in January, for discussion and adoption before being published in the form of directives.

This draft of the Sixth Five-Year Plan, which concentrates the wisdom of the broad masses from bottom to top, will be widely discussed among the Soviet people after its promulgation in order to gather more abundant opinions to be brought to the 20th Congress of the CPSU.

The draft stipulates that in the new Five-Year Plan, the total industrial output will increase by 65%. The production of means of production will increase by about 70%, and the production of consumer goods will increase by about 60%. By 1960, the production of iron will reach 53 million tons, steel 68.37 million tons, steel materials 52.7 million tons, coal 593 million tons, oil 135 million tons, electricity 320 billion kilowatt-hours, and the total grain output will reach 180 million tons (11 billion poods).

The draft points out: "The main task of the Soviet Union's Sixth Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy lies in: ensuring the further strong growth of all sectors of the national economy, realizing a sharp upsurge in agricultural production, and striving to greatly improve the material well-being and cultural level of the Soviet people on this basis, based on the priority development of heavy industry, continuous technological progress and increased labor productivity." (Li He)

### 苏联颁布第六个五年计划草案
李何
1956-01-16
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联颁布第六个五年计划草案
    本报莫斯科15日电　苏联发展国民经济的第六个五年计划（1956—1960年）草案今天颁布。这个由苏共中央制定的草案，将提交1月后举行的苏共第二十次代表大会上讨论和通过后以指令形式发表。
    这个集中着从下而上的广大人们智慧的第六个五年计划草案，颂布后将在苏联人民间进行广泛讨论，以便收集更丰富的意见，提到苏共第二十次代表大会去。
    草案规定在新的五年计划中工业总产量将增长65％。生产资料的生产将增长约70％，消费资料的生产将增长约60％。到1960年将产生铁5，300万吨，钢6，837万吨，钢材5，270万吨，煤59，300万吨，石油13，500万吨，电力3，200亿瓩时，谷物总产量将达1.8亿吨（110亿普特）。
    草案指出：“苏联发展国民经济的第六个五年计划的主要任务在于：在优先发展重工业、技术不断进步和提高劳动生产率的根据上，保证国民经济所有部门的进一步强大增长，实现农业生产的急剧高涨，而在这个基础上争取大大提高苏联人民的物质福利和文化水平。（李何）
