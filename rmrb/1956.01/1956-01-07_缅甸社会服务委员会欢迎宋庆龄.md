Title: Burmese Social Service Council Welcomes Soong Ching Ling
Author:
Date: 1956-01-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 6th: The Burmese Federation of Social Services Council held a tea party at 4:30 PM on the 5th to welcome Soong Ching Ling.

　　Social Services Council Chairwoman Daw Khin Kyi (Aung San's wife) delivered a speech, expressing a warm welcome to Soong Ching Ling. Daw Khin Kyi also paid tribute to Chinese social workers on behalf of all social workers in Burma. She expressed the hope that selfless social work by the people of both countries would build a permanent bridge of friendship and prosperity.

　　In her reply, Soong Ching Ling expressed her gratitude for the warm hospitality of the hosts. She spoke about the inseparable relationship between social work and peace, and mentioned the joint statement by the Prime Ministers of China and Burma regarding the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, as well as the contributions made by China and Burma at the Asian-African Conference.

　　Soong Ching Ling concluded by saying: "In order to jointly safeguard peace, to build our respective new countries, and to improve our respective social work, the people of Burma and China should strengthen our friendship and cooperation. I believe that this friendly cooperation certainly has a bright future."

### 缅甸社会服务委员会欢迎宋庆龄

1956-01-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　缅甸社会服务委员会欢迎宋庆龄
    新华社仰光6日电　缅甸联邦社会服务委员会在5日下午4时半举行茶会，欢迎宋庆龄。
    社会服务委员会主席都庆枝（昂山夫人）致词，对宋庆龄表示热情的欢迎。都庆枝并代表缅甸的全体社会工作者向中国的社会工作者致敬。她表示希望通过两国人民的忘我的社会工作，将建立起一条永久的友好和繁荣的桥梁。
    宋庆龄在致答词的时候对主人的热情招待表示感谢。她谈到了社会工作同和平的不可分离的关系，并且谈到了中缅两国总理的关于五项原则的联合声明，以及中缅两国在亚非会议上所作的贡献。
    宋庆龄最后说：“为了共同维护和平，为了建设我们各自的新的国家，为了改进我们各自的社会工作，缅甸和中国两国人民应该加强我们的友谊和合作。我相信，这种友好合作肯定是有光明的前途的。”
