Title: China-Soviet, China-German Track and Field Teams Hold Friendly Matches in Guangzhou and Shanghai Respectively
Author:
Date: 1956-01-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, January 8th: The Soviet track and field team, currently visiting Guangzhou, held an exciting exhibition match on January 8th with the "August 1st" track and field team of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Guangdong Provincial track and field team. In the 16 events performed, Soviet athletes won all the first places.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, January 8th: The track and field team of the German Democratic Republic held a friendly competition with the Chinese track and field team in Shanghai on the afternoon of January 8th. The Chinese track and field team consisted of track and field athletes from the Central Institute of Physical Education and Shanghai. A total of 15 events were held that day. German athletes participated in 11 events and won nine first places.

### 中苏、中德田径队  分别在广州、上海作友谊赛

1956-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏、中德田径队
    分别在广州、上海作友谊赛
    据新华社广州8日电　正在广州访问的苏联田径队8日同中国人民解放军“八一”田径队、广东省田径队举行了精彩的表演赛。在表演的16个项目中，苏联运动员全部获得第一名。
    据新华社上海8日电　德意志民主共和国田径队8日下午在上海同中国田径队举行了友谊比赛。中国田径队是由中央体育学院和上海市的田径运动员组成的。这天一共进行了15项比赛。德国运动员参加11项，得到了九项第一名。
