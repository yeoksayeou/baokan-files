Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Receives Visiting Delegations from Three Ethnic Groups, and Also Receives Chagan Gegen, Honorary President of the Buddhist Association of China

Author:
Date: 1956-01-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 15th — Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, received at Tiananmen on the afternoon of the 15th, Qiongpei, head of the visiting delegation of various ethnic groups from the Sichuan, Gansu, and Qinghai border areas, deputy heads Acai, Qingrao, and Ganiang, Zongzhe, head of the visiting delegation of various ethnic groups from the Qinghai pastoral areas, deputy heads Tuguan, Guoqing Zhige, and Duola, Chen Beichen, head of the visiting delegation of various ethnic groups from the northeastern provinces and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, deputy heads Naqin Shuanghe'er, Ma Fugang, Xu Yuanzhi, An Haiqian, and Piao Zhongxi, as well as all members of these three visiting delegations. He also received Chagan Gegen, Honorary President of the Buddhist Association of China.

Present at the reception were Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Deng Xiaoping, He Long, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, Vice-Premiers of the State Council, Zhang Bojun, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, Zhang Jingwu, Director of the Chairman's Office, Wang Feng, Liu Chun, and Sa Kongliao, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, and Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

During the reception, the heads and deputy heads of the visiting delegation of various ethnic groups from the Sichuan, Gansu, and Qinghai border areas, the visiting delegation of various ethnic groups from the Qinghai pastoral areas, and Chagan Gegen, Honorary President of the Buddhist Association of China, presented Hada scarves to Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, Deng Xiaoping, He Long, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, and Zhang Bojun respectively.

Chairman Mao Zedong and others also took group photos with all members of the three visiting delegations and Chagan Gegen, Honorary President of the Buddhist Association of China. (Photo attached)

### 毛泽东主席接见3个民族参观团  同时接见了中国佛教协会名誉会长查干葛根

1956-01-16
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席接见3个民族参观团
    同时接见了中国佛教协会名誉会长查干葛根
    新华社15日讯　中华人民共和国毛泽东主席，15日下午在天安门接见了川、甘、青边境各民族参观团团长琼佩、副团长阿才、青绕、噶娘，青海牧区各民族参观团团长宗哲、副团长土观、果青智格、多拉，东北各省暨内蒙古自治区各民族参观团团长陈北辰、副团长那钦双和尔、马富刚、徐元治、安海谦、朴锺希以及这3个参观团的全体人员。同时还接见了中国佛教协会名誉会长查干葛根。
    接见时在座的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通，国务院副总理邓小平、贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，国务院秘书长习仲勋，主席办公厅主任张经武，民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、刘春、萨空了，全国人民代表大会常务委员会典礼局局长余心清。
    接见时，川、甘、青边境各民族参观团、青海牧区各民族参观团的团长、副团长和中国佛教协会名誉会长查干葛根，分别向毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理和李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通、邓小平、贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫、章伯钧等敬献哈达。
    毛泽东主席等并同3个参观团的全体人员及中国佛教协会名誉会长查干葛根合影留念。（附图片）
