Title: More Than 360 County-Level Newspapers to be Published Nationwide
Author:
Date: 1956-01-13
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency:

    This year, provinces and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region across the country will, based on feasible conditions, successively launch more than 360 county-level newspapers aimed at rural readers. Some of these newspapers will be printed using lead type, while others will be printed using lithography. Most will be published once a week. These newspapers will all be under the leadership of the County Committees of the Communist Party of China.

### 全国将办360多种县级报纸

1956-01-13
第3版()
专栏：

    全国将办360多种县级报纸
    今年，全国各省和内蒙古自治区将根据可能的条件，先后创办360多种以农村读者为对象的县级报纸。这些报纸有的是铅印的，有的是石印的。大多数在每星期出刊一次。这些报纸，都将由中国共产党各县委员会领导。  （新华社）
