Title: The First Batch of Variant Characters to be Abolished from February 1st
Author:
Date: 1956-01-08
Page: 3

    The First Batch of Variant Characters to be Abolished from February 1st
    The Ministry of Culture and the Chinese Writing Reform Committee recently notified relevant units across the country that a batch of variant characters will be abolished from February 1st.
    The first batch of variant characters is divided into 810 groups, with each group containing a minimum of two characters and a maximum of six, totaling 1,865 characters. From now on, only one character from each group of variant characters will be used, and the rest will be abolished, streamlining a total of 1,055 characters.
    Newspapers, magazines, and books published nationwide will uniformly cease using the abolished variant characters. However, reprinting ancient books, as well as surnames and shop names, may still use the original characters. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 第一批异体字2月1日起废除

1956-01-08
第3版()
专栏：

    第一批异体字2月1日起废除
    文化部和中国文字改革委员会最近通知全国有关单位，从2月1日起废除一批异体字。
    第一批异体字，共分810组，每组最少二个字，最多六个字，共1，865个字，今后每组异体字中只用一个字，其余的废除不用，共精简掉1，055字。
    全国出版的报纸、杂志、图书，将一律停止使用废除的异体字，但翻印古书，以及姓氏、商店牌号等仍可使用原来的字。　（据新华社讯）
