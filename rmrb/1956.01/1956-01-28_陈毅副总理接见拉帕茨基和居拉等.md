Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Rapacki and Gyula, et al.
Author:
Date: 1956-01-28
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, January 27th) Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received A. Rapacki, Minister of Higher Education of the Polish People's Republic, Jan Wilczek, Vice Minister of Culture and Art, and Fan Michalewska, Deputy Secretary-General of the Committee for Foreign Cultural Cooperation, who came to China to discuss the 1956 implementation plan for the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement, at 3:00 pm on the 27th.

    At 4:30 pm on the same day, Vice Premier Chen Yi received Karoly Gyula, Vice Minister of Culture of the Hungarian People's Republic, and Gereby Kabos, Secretary-General of the Institute for Foreign Cultural Relations, who came to China to discuss the 1956 implementation plan for the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 陈毅副总理接见拉帕茨基和居拉等

1956-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见拉帕茨基和居拉等
    据新华社27日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅27日下午3时接见了来中国商谈中波文化合作协定1956年执行计划的波兰人民共和国高等教育部部长阿·拉帕茨基、文化艺术部副部长扬·维尔契克、对外文化合作委员会副秘书长凡·米哈列夫斯卡。
    同日下午4时30分，陈毅副总理接见了来中国商谈中匈文化合作协定1956年执行计划的匈牙利人民共和国文化部副部长卡拉伊·居拉、对外文化联络院秘书长葛雷克·卡卜尔。
