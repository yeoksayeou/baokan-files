Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Hosts a Banquet for Chairman Ton Duc Thang
Author:
Date: 1956-01-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 27th: Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, held a banquet on the evening of the 27th to host Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

  Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Nguyen Van Huong, Member of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and members of Chairman Ton Duc Thang's entourage were also invited to attend the banquet.

  Also present at the banquet were Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Li Shucheng, Li Zhuchen, Zhou Jianren, Shao Lizi, Gao Chongmin, Ma Yinchu, Zhang Wentian, Zhang Nanxian, Zhang Su, Xu Guangping, Chen Shaoxian, Tan Kah Kee, Cheng Qian, Huang Shaohong, Cai Tingkai, Luo Longji, and Chen Qiyuan, members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Liu Ningyi, Standing Committee Member of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, among others.

  At the beginning of the banquet, Chairman Liu Shaoqi delivered a speech. He proposed a toast to the new victories of the Vietnamese people in their struggle for peace, unification, independence, and democracy, to the greater achievements of the Vietnamese government and people in their construction endeavors, and to the further consolidation and development of the friendship between the Chinese and Vietnamese peoples.

  Chairman Ton Duc Thang then delivered a speech. He said, "We will certainly bring back the profound friendship that China and other brotherly countries have shown us this time. I believe this will definitely inspire the Vietnamese people's will to fight for construction, for peace, unification, independence, and democracy." Finally, he raised his glass to warmly congratulate the growing consolidation and development of the friendship between the Vietnamese and Chinese peoples, and to congratulate the new victories of the Chinese people in their socialist construction and socialist transformation.

### 刘少奇委员长宴请孙德胜委员长

1956-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇委员长宴请孙德胜委员长
    新华社27日讯　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇在27日晚举行宴会，招待越南民主共和国国民大会常务委员会委员长孙德胜。
  越南民主共和国驻华大使黄文欢，越南民主共和国国民大会常务委员会委员阮文享，以及孙德胜委员长的随行人员也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒、黄炎培、彭真、陈叔通，委员李书城、李烛尘、周建人、邵力子、高崇民、马寅初、张闻天、张难先、张苏、许广平、陈劭先、陈嘉庚、程潜、黄绍竑、蔡廷锴、罗隆基、陈其瑗，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员刘宁一等人。
    宴会开始，刘少奇委员长首先讲话。他提议为越南人民在和平、统一、独立、民主的斗争中取得新的胜利，为越南政府和人民在建设事业中获得更大的成就，为中越两国人民的友谊得到进一步巩固和发展而干杯。
    孙德胜委员长接着讲话。他说，我们一定把这次中国和其他兄弟国家给予我们的深情厚意带回国去，我相信这定将鼓舞越南人民为建设，为和平、统一、独立和民主而斗争的意志。最后，他举杯热烈祝贺越中两国人民友谊的日益巩固和发展，祝贺中国人民的社会主义建设和社会主义改造事业获得新的胜利。
