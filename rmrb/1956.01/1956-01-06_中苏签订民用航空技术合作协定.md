Title: China and the Soviet Union Sign Civil Aviation Technology Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-01-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 5th. On January 4th, the Civil Aviation Administration of China's State Council and the General Administration of Civil Aviation under the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union signed a Civil Aviation Technology Cooperation Agreement between China and the Soviet Union in Moscow. Kwong Yun-nung, Director of the Civil Aviation Administration of China, signed the agreement on behalf of China; Zhevronkov, Director of the General Administration of Civil Aviation of the Soviet Union, signed the agreement on behalf of the Soviet Union.

### 中苏签订民用航空技术合作协定

1956-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏签订民用航空技术合作协定
    新华社莫斯科5日电　1月4日，中华人民共和国国务院民用航空局和苏联部长会议民用航空总局，在莫斯科签订了中苏两国民用航空技术合作协定。代表中国方面在协定上签字的，是中国民用航空局局长邝任农；代表苏联方面在协定上签字的，是苏联民用航空总局局长热沃隆科夫。
