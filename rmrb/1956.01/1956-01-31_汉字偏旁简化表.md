Table of Simplified Chinese Character Radicals

This table contains 54 simplified radicals, arranged in order of the number of strokes, from simple to complex, of the original radicals. The radicals in parentheses are the original radicals. Simplified radicals marked with a "＊" symbol are generally only used as left-hand radicals; radicals without the "＊" symbol can generally be used in any part of a Chinese character.
