Title: Comrade Liu Shaoqi Receives Delegation of Yugoslav Journalists
Author:
Date: 1956-01-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th - Liu Shaoqi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, received the Yugoslav journalists delegation headed by the Director of the Yugoslav News Agency, Mr. Pirpic, this afternoon.

Present at the reception were Wang Jiaxiang, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Deng Tuo, President of the All-China Journalists Association, Wu Lengxi, Vice President, Mei Yi, Gong Peng, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Li Bingquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Journalists Association. (Attached photo)

Comrade Liu Shaoqi talking with Mr. Pirpic (right), head of the Yugoslav journalists delegation to China.

　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong

### 刘少奇同志接见南新闻工作者代表团

1956-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇同志接见南新闻工作者代表团
    新华社10日讯　中共中央书记处书记刘少奇，10日下午接见了以南斯拉夫通讯社社长皮尔皮奇为首的南斯拉夫新闻工作者访华代表团。
    接见时在座的，有中共中央委员王稼祥，中国新闻工作者联谊会会长邓拓、副会长吴冷西、梅益，外交部新闻司司长龚澎和中国新闻工作者联谊会副秘书长李炳泉。（附图片）
    刘少奇同志和南斯拉夫新闻工作者访华代表团团长皮尔皮奇（右）谈话。
                                  新华社记者　邹健东摄
