Title: The Second Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Opens. Premier Zhou Delivers Political Report; Worker, Farmer, Handicraft Worker, and Business Representatives Report Good News to Chairman Mao and Others
Author:
Date: 1956-01-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 30th. The Second Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) opened in Beijing this afternoon.

Chairman of the People's Republic of China and Honorary Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, Mao Zedong; Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, Zhou Enlai; Vice Chairmen of the CPPCC National Committee, Dong Biwu, Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, He Xiangning, Li Weihan, Li Siguang, Chen Shutong, Zhang Bojun, Chen Jiageng, and Burhan attended today's opening ceremony.

Also attending today's opening ceremony were Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, and leading personnel of the State Council of the People's Republic of China.

Zhou Enlai, Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee and Premier of the State Council, delivered a political report at today's meeting. Vice Chairman Li Jishen delivered a work report of the Standing Committee of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC. In his political report, Zhou Enlai first spoke about the current international situation. Regarding domestic affairs, he focused on the high tide of the socialist revolution, the issue of intellectuals, and the issue of liberating Taiwan. Zhou Enlai's report was punctuated by enthusiastic applause from the audience.

Today, at the opening ceremony, representatives of workers, farmers, handicraft workers, and business people from across the country, carrying news of the great victories of socialist construction and socialist transformation, reported good news to Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China and Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee. Representatives of the national business community also reported good news to Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Huang Yanpei, Chairman of the Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association, and Chen Shutong, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce.

Amidst enthusiastic applause, the teams reporting good news from workers, farmers, handicraft workers, and business people walked through the venue and onto the stage. They presented red letters of good news, printed with golden "double happiness" characters, to Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and others. After accepting the letters of good news, Chairman Mao Zedong and others warmly shook hands with the representatives. The hall erupted in jubilation, with enthusiastic applause lasting for five minutes.

The reporting teams also brought celebratory banners, letters of guarantee, and gifts, which were displayed on both sides of the stage.

Among the four reporting teams were Huang Demao, special-class labor model of Anshan Iron and Steel Company; Ma Yongshun, forestry labor model and logging worker from Heilongjiang Province; Wang Yukun, head of an agricultural production cooperative established by three poor peasant households in Nanwangzhuang, Anping County, Hebei Province; Li Molin, director of the Sijiqing Vegetable Production Cooperative in the suburbs of Beijing; and Yang Shihui, director of the Beijing Ivory Carving Production Cooperative, and others. The business community's reporting team was led by Li Zhuchen and Sheng Pihua, including representatives of business people and their families from three centrally administered municipalities and 24 provinces (autonomous regions).

Today's meeting lasted from 4:10 PM to 6:40 PM.

586 committee members attended this meeting, and 611 representatives attended as observers. Among the observers were representatives of the CPPCC committees of various provinces, autonomous regions, and cities; representatives of various democratic parties and people's organizations; Catholics and Christians; Ahongs and pastors; overseas Chinese; and people from all walks of life. The largest number of observers were senior intellectuals, including experts, scientists, professors, scholars, engineers, doctors, writers, playwrights, artists, and musicians from various departments of the central state organs. Many industrialists, business people, and their families also attended the meeting as observers. Among the observers were also many representatives who were skilled in various specialized technologies, such as vegetable production models, roast duck technicians, chefs, painting mounters, cloisonné workers, ivory carvers, jade designers, Rongbaozhai watermark workers, Go players, chess players, and table tennis and volleyball players, all of whom were attending such a grand event for the first time.

The meeting will continue on the 31st. (Photo attached)

### 中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第二次全体会议开幕  周总理作政治报告；工人、农民、手工业工人和工商界代表的报喜队向毛主席等报喜

1956-01-31
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第二次全体会议开幕
    周总理作政治报告；工人、农民、手工业工人和工商界代表的报喜队向毛主席等报喜
    新华社30日讯　中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第二次全体会议今天下午在北京开幕。
    中华人民共和国主席、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会名誉主席毛泽东，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会主席周恩来，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席董必武、李济深、郭沫若、彭真、沈钧儒、黄炎培、何香凝、李维汉、李四光、陈叔通、章伯钧、陈嘉庚、鲍尔汉出席了今天的开幕式。
    参加今天开幕式的还有中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇和中华人民共和国国务院领导工作人员。
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会主席、国务院总理周恩来在今天会上作了政治报告，副主席李济深作了中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会常务委员会工作报告。周恩来在政治报告中首先讲到了目前的国际形势，关于国内方面侧重地讲到了社会主义革命的高潮、讲到了关于知识分子问题，并且讲到了解放台湾的问题。在周恩来报告的时候，全场不断地响起了热烈的掌声。
    今天在开幕式上，全国工人、农民、手工业工人和工商界的代表，带着全国各地社会主义建设和社会主义改造的伟大胜利的喜信，分别向中华人民共和国、中国共产党中央委员会毛泽东主席，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，中华人民共和国国务院总理、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会主席周恩来报喜。全国工商界的代表还向中华全国总工会主席赖若愚、中国民主建国会中央委员会主任委员黄炎培和中华全国工商业联合会主任委员陈叔通报喜。
    工人、农民、手工业工人和工商界代表的报喜队，在热烈的掌声中穿过会场走上主席台。他们把印有金色双喜字的红色报喜信，双手献给毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理等。毛泽东主席等接受了报喜信后，亲切地和报喜的代表们握手。这时，会场上一片欢腾，热烈鼓掌达五分钟之久。
    报喜队还带来了喜幛、保证书和礼物，陈列在主席台的两旁。
    在四个报喜队中，有鞍山市特等劳动模范、鞍山钢铁公司的工人黄德茂，黑龙江省林业劳动模范、采伐工人马永顺，有河北省安平县南王庄三户贫农坚持举办的农业生产合作社社长王玉坤、北京郊区四季青蔬菜生产合作社主任李墨林，有北京市象牙雕刻生产合作社主任杨士惠等。工商界报喜队由李烛尘和盛丕华率领，其中包括三个中央直辖市和24个省（自治区）的工商界人士和他们的家属的代表。
    今天会议从下午4时10分进行到6时40分。
    出席这次会议的委员有586人，列席会议的代表有611人。列席代表中，有中国人民政治协商会议各省、自治区和市委员会、各民主党派、各人民团体的代表，有天主教徒和基督教徒，阿訇和牧师，有华侨方面人士和社会各界人士。列席代表中为数最多的是高级知识分子，他们包括中央国家机关各部门的专家，科学家，教授，学者，工程师，医生，作家，戏剧家，美术家和音乐家等。不少工商业者和他们的家属也列席了会议。在列席代表中还有许多擅长各种专门技术的代表人物，如蔬菜生产模范、烤鸭技师、厨师、裱画师、景泰蓝工人、象牙雕刻家、玉器设计师、荣宝斋水印工人、围棋手、象棋手以及乒乓球和排球运动员等，他们都是第一次参加这样的盛会。
    会议将在31日继续举行。（附图片）
