Author:
Date: 1956-01-29
Page: 2

Reactions to the Newspaper's Criticisms

    On December 22, 1955, this newspaper published an article entitled "Don't Blame Heaven and Earth," criticizing the Fourth Store of the Harbin City Company of the China Department Store for failing to organize purchasing according to needs and for poor service attitude. Now, this store has written to say that they have conducted an inspection based on the newspaper's criticism and have formulated measures to improve their work.
    ×　×　×
    On December 16, 1955, this newspaper published an article entitled "Is It Really Irretrievable?", criticizing the leading staff of the Third Construction Site of the Second Engineering Company of the Wuhan Iron and Steel Construction Company for adopting a passive attitude of "resigning themselves to fate and leaving it to heaven" towards completing the state plan. Now, the leading comrades of the Party organization of this construction site have written to examine their leadership's conservative thinking and negative emotions.

### 对本报批评的反应

1956-01-29
第2版()
专栏：对本报批评的反应

    对本报批评的反应
    1955年12月22日，本报发表了“不要怨天尤人”一文，批评中国百货公司哈尔滨市公司第四商店没有根据需要组织进货，服务态度不好。现在，这个商店来信说，他们已经根据报纸的批评进行了检查，并订出了改进工作的措施。
    ×　×　×
    1955年12月16日，本报发表了“真的无法挽回吗”一文，批评武汉钢铁建设公司第二工程公司第三工地的领导工作人员，对完成国家计划采取了“听天由命，成事在天”的消极态度。现在，这个工地党组织的领导同志已来信检讨了领导上的保守思想和消极情绪。
