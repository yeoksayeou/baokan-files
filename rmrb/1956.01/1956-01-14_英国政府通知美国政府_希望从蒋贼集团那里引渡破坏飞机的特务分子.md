Title: The British Government Notifies the U.S. Government, Hoping to Extradite Special Agents from the Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Gang Who Sabotaged the Airplane
Author:
Date: 1956-01-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, London, January 13th: A British Foreign Office spokesman said in London on January 11 that Britain has notified the U.S. government that it hopes to extradite Chou Tzu-ming from the Chiang Kai-shek clique in Taiwan. Chou Tzu-ming was a Chiang Kai-shek special agent who sabotaged the "Kashmir Princess" airplane in Hong Kong last year.

The spokesman declined to reveal what reaction the U.S. government had to this notification.

### 英国政府通知美国政府  希望从蒋贼集团那里引渡破坏飞机的特务分子

1956-01-14
第1版()
专栏：

    英国政府通知美国政府
    希望从蒋贼集团那里引渡破坏飞机的特务分子
    新华社13日讯  伦敦消息：英国外交部发言人1月11日在伦敦说，英国已经通知美国政府，它希望从台湾蒋介石集团那里引渡周梓铭。周梓铭是去年在香港破坏“克什米尔公主号”飞机的蒋介石特务分子。
    发言人拒绝透露美国政府对这个通知有什么反应。
