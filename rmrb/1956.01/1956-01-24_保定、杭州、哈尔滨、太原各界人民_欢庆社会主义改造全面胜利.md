Title: People from All Walks of Life in Baoding, Hangzhou, Harbin, and Taiyuan Celebrate the Comprehensive Victory of Socialist Transformation
Author:
Date: 1956-01-24
Page: 1

    Baoding, Hangzhou, Harbin, and Taiyuan Celebrate the Comprehensive Victory of Socialist Transformation
    Our Newspaper reports: The cities of Baoding (Hebei Province), Hangzhou (Zhejiang Province), Harbin (Heilongjiang Province), and Taiyuan (Shanxi Province) have victoriously completed the socialist transformation tasks of the first Five-Year Plan and have begun to enter socialist society. In recent days, people from all walks of life in these cities have held celebrations.
    Baoding City was already bustling at dawn on the 20th. Workers, shop assistants, farmers, members of handicraft production cooperatives, industrialists and businessmen, representatives of government cadres, the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and students flocked to the Baoding People's Stadium, beating gongs and drums. That morning, more than 35,000 people from all walks of life in Baoding gathered here to celebrate Baoding's entry into socialist society. After the meeting, a grand celebratory parade was also held.
    In Hangzhou City on the morning of the 20th, when a thin mist still shrouded the West Lake, thousands of red flags were already visible in the lakeside trees, and gongs and drums resounded throughout the city's streets and alleys, with firecrackers crackling. On this day, more than 100,000 people in Hangzhou, dressed in festive attire, solemnly held a celebration rally for the victory of socialist transformation on the beautiful banks of West Lake. A grand parade was held after the meeting.
    In Harbin, more than 200,000 people braved the sub-zero temperature of 28 degrees Celsius on the afternoon of the 22nd to gather and celebrate the comprehensive victory of socialist transformation. On this day, the sounds of gongs and drums and firecrackers resounded everywhere in the city, and every household hung red flags in front of their doors. At noon, people poured into the gathering place from all directions, and many elderly people and women who did not often participate in gatherings also joined the celebration. A grand parade was held after the meeting. In the evening, people from all walks of life in Harbin also held a grand get-together.
    In Taiyuan, 100,000 people held a celebration rally on the 23rd to celebrate the victory of socialist transformation in the city. Among those attending the meeting were employees from 20 industrial enterprises who had increased production efficiency by one to five times in the days following the joint state-private ownership transition, members of the Changqing Agricultural Production Cooperative carrying 12 kinds of fresh vegetables, and a large team of more than 10,000 handicraft workers carrying their letters of determination to participate in the meeting, vowing to manufacture agricultural tools and implements worth more than 45 million yuan this year. In the capitalist ranks, led by a huge billboard with the slogan "Mastering Our Destiny, Running into Socialism," there was also a Yangko team composed of capitalists over the age of 50. At night, folk art performances and gatherings were held in six places in Taiyuan.

### 保定、杭州、哈尔滨、太原各界人民  欢庆社会主义改造全面胜利

1956-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    保定、杭州、哈尔滨、太原各界人民
    欢庆社会主义改造全面胜利
    本报讯　河北省省会保定市、浙江省省会杭州市、黑龙江省省会哈尔滨市和山西省省会太原市已经胜利完成了第一个五年计划的社会主义改造任务，开始进入社会主义社会。最近几天，这几个城市各界人民分别举行了庆祝大会。
    保定市在20日黎明就沸腾起来了。工人、店员、农民、手工业生产合作社社员、工商业者和机关干部、中国人民解放军、学生的代表，敲锣打鼓涌向保定市人民体育场。这天上午，保定市各界人民35，000多人在这里集会，庆祝保定市进入社会主义社会。会后，还举行了盛大的庆祝游行。
    杭州市在20日清晨，当薄雾还笼罩着西湖的时候，湖滨的树丛中已经露出千百面红旗，全市大街小巷锣鼓喧天，鞭炮齐鸣。这一天，杭州市10万多人穿着节日的盛装，在美丽的西子湖畔隆重举行了庆祝社会主义改造胜利大会。会后举行了盛大的游行。
    哈尔滨市20多万人在22日下午冒着零下28度的严寒，集会庆祝社会主义改造的全面胜利。这天全市到处响起锣鼓声和鞭炮声，家家户户门前高悬红旗。中午，人们从四面八方涌入集会地点，许多不常参加集会的老年人和妇女也参加了庆祝会。会后举行了盛大的游行。晚间，哈尔滨市各界人民还举行了大联欢。
    太原市10万人在23日举行了庆祝全市社会主义改造胜利大会。参加大会的有在公私合营以后的几天内把生产效率提高一倍到五倍的20个工业企业的职工，有抬着12种新鲜蔬菜的常青农业生产合作社的社员；一万多个手工业者组成的大队，抬着他们的决心书来参加大会，他们要在今年内制造价值4，500多万元的农具和工具。以“掌握命运，跑步进入社会主义”的巨幅标语牌为前导的资本家队伍中，还有一个由50岁以上的资本家组成的秧歌队。入夜，太原市有六个地方举行了许多民间艺术表演和联欢活动。
