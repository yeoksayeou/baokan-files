Title: Yugoslav National Football Team Holds Opening Ceremony for Visit to Shanghai

Author: 
Date: 1956-01-30
Page: 3

    Yugoslav National Football Team
    Holds Opening Ceremony for Visit to Shanghai

    The Yugoslav National Football Team held the opening ceremony for their visit and matches in China on the afternoon of the 29th at the Jiangwan Stadium in Shanghai.

    He Long, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China and Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, watched the match after the opening ceremony and met with Bo. Pisiqi, the leader of the Yugoslav National Football Team, and others before the opening ceremony.

    Members of the Yugoslav Journalist Delegation to China, who are currently visiting Shanghai, and Dakovic, First Secretary of the Yugoslav Embassy in China, also attended the opening ceremony.

    At the opening ceremony, Li Kaiting, head of the Shanghai Municipal Sports Commission, delivered a speech first, warmly welcoming the Yugoslav National Football Team, the first sports team from Yugoslavia to visit China. Bo. Pisiqi, the leader of the Yugoslav National Football Team, then spoke. He said, "Our football team is particularly honored to be the first sports delegation from Yugoslavia to visit your great and beautiful country." He also said that this would be the beginning of friendly cooperation between athletes of the two countries.

    After the opening ceremony, the Yugoslav National Football Team played a friendly match with the Beijing Youth Football Team. The Yugoslav National Football Team won with a score of four to two.

    He Long and Ke Qingshi, Secretary of the Shanghai Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC, went to the field after the game to shake hands and congratulate the members of the Yugoslav National Football Team and the Beijing Youth Football Team. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 南斯拉夫国家足球代表队  在上海举行访问比赛开幕式

1956-01-30
第3版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫国家足球代表队
    在上海举行访问比赛开幕式
    南斯拉夫国家足球代表队29日下午在上海市江湾体育场举行了来中国访问比赛的开幕式。
    中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼体育运动委员会主任贺龙观看了开幕式后的球赛，并且在开幕式以前接见了南斯拉夫国家足球代表队领队勃·彼希契等人。
    正在上海访问的南斯拉夫新闻工作者访华代表团的团员们和南斯拉夫驻中国大使馆一等秘书达科维奇也出席了开幕式。
    在开幕式上，上海市体育运动委员会负责人李凯亭首先致词，对南斯拉夫来中国访问的第一个体育队——南斯拉夫国家足球代表队表示热烈欢迎。南斯拉夫国家足球代表队领队勃·彼希契接着讲话。他说，“我们足球队能够作为南斯拉夫第一个体育代表队到你们伟大而美丽的国家访问，感到特别荣幸。”他还说，这将是两国运动员友好合作的开端。
    开幕式结束以后，南斯拉夫国家足球代表队同北京青年足球队举行了友谊比赛。南斯拉夫国家足球代表队以四比二获胜。
    贺龙和中共中央上海局书记、中共上海市委员会第一书记柯庆施等人在球赛结束以后，曾经到球场上同南斯拉夫国家足球代表队和北京青年足球队队员们握手祝贺。（新华社）
