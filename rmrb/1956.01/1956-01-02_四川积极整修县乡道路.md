Title: Sichuan Actively Repairs County and Township Roads
Author:
Date: 1956-01-02
Page: 2
　
    Sichuan Province's Wenjiang, Fuling, Neijiang, Mianyang, and 8 other special districts are taking advantage of the winter slack season to carry out work on repairing county and township roads. Approximately 1 million civilian workers are participating in road construction, with plans to complete 14 county roads totaling 420 kilometers in length and 4,239 kilometers of post roads and pedestrian/pack animal roads. In the winter of 1956, there are also plans to repair an additional 288 kilometers of county roads and 18,000 kilometers of post roads and pedestrian roads.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（Our correspondent stationed in Chongqing）

### 四川积极整修县乡道路

1956-01-02
第2版()
专栏：

    四川积极整修县乡道路
    四川省温江、涪陵、内江、绵阳等12个专区，利用冬季农闲期间，展开了整修县乡道路的工作。参加修路的民工约达一百多万人，计划完成14条共长420公里的县道和长达4，239公里的驿道、人行驮运道路。在1956年冬天，还计划再整修县道288公里驿道和人行道路18，000公里。
              （本报驻重庆记者）
