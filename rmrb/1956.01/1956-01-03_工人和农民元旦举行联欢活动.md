Title: Workers and Peasants Hold New Year's Celebration

Author:

Date: 1956-01-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 2nd. Workers and peasants in various places held New Year's celebrations.

　　In the suburbs of Beijing, many members of agricultural production cooperatives received workers, students, and government cadres who came to offer New Year's greetings. Employees of the National First Automobile Accessory Factory performed the "Shuangtiao Drum Dance" for the villagers in the Red Star Collective Farm, and the farm's amateur troupe also sang the song "Mutual Aid and Cooperation Have a Bright Future." Cadres from the Beijing Handicraft Production Cooperative Federation went to the two agricultural production cooperatives in Dongba Town to offer New Year's greetings and also presented shovels, rakes, sickles, and other tools.

　　The New Year rural visiting group of the Songjiang Machinery Factory in Heilongjiang Province, composed of more than 60 workers, brought two double-wheeled double-share plows as New Year's gifts to the Fulicun Agricultural Production Cooperative in Shuangcheng County, which is about to become an advanced cooperative. The farm implement repair team of the visiting group also repaired various farm implements for the agricultural production cooperative.

　　In addition, workers in Tianjin, Baoding, Taiyuan, and other cities held celebrations with members of agricultural production cooperatives in the suburbs.

### 工人和农民元旦举行联欢活动

1956-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　工人和农民元旦举行联欢活动
    据新华社2日讯　各地工人和农民元旦纷纷举行联欢活动。
    北京市郊区许多农业生产合作社社员接待了前来贺年的工人、学生和机关干部。国营第一汽车附件厂的职工在红星集体农庄的广场上为庄员们表演了“双条鼓舞”，农庄业余剧团也演唱了“互助合作有奔头”的歌曲。北京市手工业生产合作社联合总社的干部到东坝镇的两个农业生产合作社贺年，还赠送了铁锹、铁耙、镰刀等。
    黑龙江省松江机械厂由60多个工人组成的新年农村访问团，带了两台双轮双铧犁，作为新年礼品送给即将转为高级社的双城县富礼村的农业生产合作社，访问团的农具修理队还为农业生产合作社检修了各种农具。
    此外，天津、保定、太原等城市的工人分别和郊区的农业生产合作社的社员，举行了联欢。
