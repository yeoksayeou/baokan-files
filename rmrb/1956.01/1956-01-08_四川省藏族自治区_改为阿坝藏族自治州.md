Title: Sichuan Province Tibetan Autonomous Region Changed to Aba Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture
Author:
Date: 1956-01-08
Page: 3

Sichuan Province Tibetan Autonomous Region
Changed to Aba Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture

    The first session of the First People's Representative Conference of the Aba Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture of Sichuan Province, held in Shua Jing Si from December 28 to 31, 1955, unanimously adopted a resolution to change the Sichuan Province Tibetan Autonomous Region to the Sichuan Province Aba Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture, in accordance with the provisions of the Constitution. Sangji Yexi was elected as the governor, and Gao Zhiyuan, Suo Guanying, Huaergong Chenglie, Su Xin, and Wang Chunqing were elected as deputy governors. (Minjiang Daily Editorial Department)

### 四川省藏族自治区  改为阿坝藏族自治州

1956-01-08
第3版()
专栏：

    四川省藏族自治区
    改为阿坝藏族自治州
    1955年12月28日至31日在刷经寺举行的四川省阿坝藏族自治州第一届人民代表会议第一次会议，根据宪法规定，会议一致通过将四川省藏族自治区改称四川省阿坝藏族自治州，并选出桑吉悦希为州长，郜志远、索观瀛、华尔功成烈、苏新、王春清等为副州长。　（岷江报编辑部）
