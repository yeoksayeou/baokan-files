Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De's Visit to Hungary
Author:
Date: 1956-01-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, January 16th: Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Zhu De, visited Stalinvaros and the local Stalin Metallurgical United Enterprise on the morning of January 15th.

　　When Zhu De arrived at the Stalinvaros train station by special train, he was welcomed by local party and government leaders and people's representatives. Taularce, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Stalinvaros City Council, delivered a welcoming speech at the station. Vice Chairman Zhu De gave a reply, representing the Chinese people and extending fraternal greetings to the local people.

　　While visiting the iron-smelting and steel-making sections of the United Enterprise, Zhu De warmly shook hands with the Stakhanovite workers of the enterprise. Zhu De also visited the city's Palace of Culture, shops, and residential areas.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, January 16th: The Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, and the Council of Ministers hosted a banquet in honor of Vice Chairman Zhu De at the Parliament Building in Budapest on the evening of the 15th.

　　Among those present at the banquet were Rakosi, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, Hegedus, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and other party and government leaders such as Acz, Apro, Gero, Hidas, Kovacs, Mekis, Szalai, Egri and Vig, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Working People's Party, Ronai, Speaker of the National Assembly, Dan-Nagy, Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the Republic, as well as senior generals of the Hungarian People's Army and representatives from all walks of life.

　　Also present at the banquet were Marshal Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China, Liu Lantao, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Hao Deqing, Chinese Ambassador to Hungary, and staff members of the Chinese Embassy, personnel of the Chinese Trade Delegation, and representatives of the Chinese side of the Sino-Hungarian Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation.

　　Diplomatic envoys from many countries to Hungary also attended the banquet.

　　During the banquet, Rakosi delivered a speech saying that Marshal Zhu De and other Chinese comrades, during their stay in our country, will be able to appreciate that the Hungarian people, under the leadership of the Hungarian Working People's Party, are following the great socialist work carried out by the liberated Chinese people in a short period of time with the greatest love and sympathy.

　　Then Vice Chairman Zhu De delivered a speech. He said that the People's Republic of China and the Hungarian People's Republic are both fraternal countries belonging to the world peace, democracy, and socialism camp headed by the great Soviet Union. Our two countries support and care for each other in the common cause of carrying out socialist construction and defending world peace. The friendly cooperative relations between our two countries in political, economic, scientific, technological, and cultural fields are constantly being consolidated and developed, and the contacts between our two peoples and governments are becoming increasingly close and frequent. With the continuous development of the socialist construction cause of our two countries, the eternal, fraternal friendship between our two peoples will be further consolidated and strengthened.

　　Hegedus also spoke at the banquet.

　　After the banquet, Zhu De, accompanied by Rakosi, Hegedus, and Ronai, visited the Parliament Building.

### 朱德副主席在匈牙利的访问

1956-01-17
第4版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席在匈牙利的访问
    据新华社布达佩斯16日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德，在1月15日上午参观了斯大林瓦罗什和当地的斯大林冶金联合企业。
    朱德乘专车到达斯大林瓦罗什火车站时，受到了当地党政负责人和人民代表的欢迎。斯大林瓦罗什市会议执行委员会主席陶拉尔采在车站上致欢迎词。朱德副主席致答词，代表中国人民向当地人民致兄弟的问候。
    在参观联合企业的冶铁、炼钢部分时，朱德和企业的斯塔哈诺夫式工人亲切地握手。朱德还参观了这个城市的文化宫、商店和住宅区。
    新华社布达佩斯16日电　匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会、匈牙利人民共和国主席团和部长会议，15日晚间在布达佩斯的国会大厦设宴招待朱德副主席。
    出席宴会的有匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记拉科西、部长会议主席赫格居斯以及党和政府的其他领导人阿奇、阿普罗、格罗、希达什、科瓦奇、麦基什、萨拉伊，劳动人民党中央委员会书记处书记埃格里和维格，国民议会议长罗奈伊、共和国主席团副主席但·纳吉以及匈牙利人民军的高级将领和各界代表。
    出席宴会的还有中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席聂荣臻元帅、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员刘澜涛、中国驻匈牙利大使郝德青和中国大使馆人员、中国贸易代表团人员和中匈科学和技术合作联合委员会中国方面的代表。
    许多国家驻匈牙利的外交使节也出席了宴会。
    席间，拉科西致词说，朱德元帅及其他中国同志在我国逗留期间，将能体会到匈牙利人民在匈牙利劳动人民党的领导下，以最大的热爱和同情注视着解放了的中国人民在短短的时期内所进行的巨大的社会主义工作。
    接着朱德副主席讲了话。他说，中华人民共和国和匈牙利人民共和国同是属于以伟大的苏联为首的世界和平、民主和社会主义阵营的两个兄弟国家。我们两国在进行社会主义建设和保卫世界和平的共同事业中相互支持和关怀。我们两国在政治上、经济上、科学技术上和文化上的友好合作关系在不断巩固和发展，我们两国人民和政府之间的接触在日益密切和频繁。随着我们两国社会主义建设事业的不断发展，我们两国人民的永恒的、兄弟般的友谊将会进一步巩固和加强。
    赫格居斯也在宴会上讲了话。
    宴会后，朱德在拉科西、赫格居斯和罗奈伊的陪同下，参观了国会大厦。
