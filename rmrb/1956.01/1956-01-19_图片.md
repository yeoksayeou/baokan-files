    The newly built Yunnan Nationalities Normal School in Kunming is dedicated to training teachers for ethnic minorities in Yunnan Province. More than 200 students from various ethnic groups in Yunnan Province, such as Dai, Yi, Tibetan, Jingpo, and Hani, are participating in the studies. This is a picture of students listening to a teacher explaining the internal structure of the human body in human anatomy and physiology class. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Chuanguo photo
