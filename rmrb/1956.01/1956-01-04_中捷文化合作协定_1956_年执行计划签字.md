Title: Signing of the 1956 Executive Plan for the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-01-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: The 1956 Executive Plan for the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed in Beijing on the morning of January 3rd. This year's executive plan stipulates that China and Czechoslovakia will further strengthen cooperation in science. The executive plan includes rich projects suitable for the needs and possibilities of both parties in literature, fine arts, drama, and music.

Shen Yanbing, Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, signed the executive plan on behalf of the Chinese side, and Grigor, the Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, signed on behalf of the Czechoslovak side.

After the signing, Shen Yanbing and Grigor delivered speeches successively, congratulating the increasing strengthening of cultural cooperation between China and Czechoslovakia.

### 中捷文化合作协定1956年执行计划签字

1956-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　中捷文化合作协定1956年执行计划签字
    据新华社3日讯　中捷文化合作协定1956年执行计划3日上午在北京签字。今年的执行计划规定，中捷两国将进一步加强在科学方面的合作。对于文学、美术、戏剧和音乐各方面，执行计划都列入了适合于双方需要和可能的、内容丰富的项目。
    在执行计划上代表中国方面签字的是中华人民共和国文化部部长沈雁冰，代表捷克斯洛伐克方面签字的是捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使格里哥尔。
    在签字以后，沈雁冰和格里哥尔相继致词，祝贺中捷两国文化合作的日益加强。
