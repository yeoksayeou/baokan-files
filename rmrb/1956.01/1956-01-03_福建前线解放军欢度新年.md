Title: PLA on the Fujian Front Celebrate the New Year
Author:
Date: 1956-01-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, January 2nd. Officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army troops on the Fujian Front have entered 1956 with full confidence in victory.

　　Various departments and companies held New Year's Eve parties or forums. Many departments and units also held get-togethers with local government personnel, workers, students, and farmers on New Year's Eve or New Year's Day. While celebrating the New Year, officers and soldiers of the coastal defense troops formed New Year's greeting teams to pay respects to the families of revolutionary martyrs and revolutionary soldiers in the駐地 [zhù dì] (stationed) area, and promoted the benefits of agricultural cooperativization to the martyrs' families, military families, and the vast number of farmers, helping them to establish agricultural or fishery production cooperatives.

　　In the new year, all officers and soldiers on the Fujian Front are full of confidence in victory. The leading organs and leaders of many units have received a large number of letters of determination from the officers and soldiers of their subordinate units. The officers and soldiers expressed their determination to continue to fight heroically to defend the construction of the motherland and world peace, and to liberate our country's territory, Taiwan!

### 福建前线解放军欢度新年

1956-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

    福建前线解放军欢度新年
    据新华社福建前线2日电　福建前线中国人民解放军部队的军官和士兵，满怀胜利信心地迈进了1956年。
    各机关、连队举行了新年晚会或座谈会。许多机关、部队还在新年前夕或元旦和驻地政府人员、工人、学生、农民进行了联欢。各海防部队的官兵们在欢度新年中，还纷纷组成拜年队向驻地革命烈士家属、革命军人家属拜年，并且向烈属、军属和广大农民宣传农业合作化的好处，帮助他们成立农业或渔业生产合作社。
    在新的一年中，福建前线全体官兵充满了胜利的信心。许多部队的领导机关和首长都收到了所属部队官兵的大批决心书。官兵们表示要继续为保卫祖国建设和世界和平，并且为解放我们祖国的领土台湾而英勇奋斗！
