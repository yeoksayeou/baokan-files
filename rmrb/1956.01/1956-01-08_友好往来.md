Author:
Date: 1956-01-08
Page: 4

　　Friendly Exchanges
　　The head of the cultural delegation of the German Democratic Republic, State Secretary of the Higher Education Administration of the German Democratic Republic, G. Harrich, the delegation member and Secretary-General of the Association for Foreign Cultural Relations of the German Democratic Republic, K. Wiese, the delegation member and Director of the Ministry of Culture of the German Democratic Republic, E. Hoffmann, and the delegation secretary A. Pritske arrived in Beijing by plane on the 7th to negotiate and sign the 1956 executive plan for the Sino-German cultural cooperation agreement.
　　At the invitation of the German Writers' Union, Ba Jin, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Writers' Association, and writer Zhou Libo departed from Beijing on the 5th to attend the Fourth German Writers' Congress in Berlin.

### 友好往来

1956-01-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　友好往来
　　来中国谈判并且签订中德文化合作协定1956年执行计划的德意志民主共和国文化代表团团长、德意志民主共和国高等教育总署国务秘书格·哈里希，团员、德意志民主共和国对外文化联络协会秘书长卡·维塞，团员、德意志民主共和国文化部司长恩·霍夫曼和代表团秘书爱·普莉琦在7日乘飞机到达北京。
　　中国作家协会副主席巴金、作家周立波应德国作家联盟邀请，5日从北京启程，赴柏林参加第四届德国作家代表大会。
