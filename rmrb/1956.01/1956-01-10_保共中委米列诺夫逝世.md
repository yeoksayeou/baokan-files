Title: Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Member Milenov Passes Away
Author: 
Date: 1956-01-10
Page: 4
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, January 7th: The Bulgarian Telegraph Agency reported from Sofia on the 7th that Alexander Milenov, member of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, Secretary of the Central Council of Bulgarian Trade Unions, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Miners' Union, and member of the National Assembly, passed away on January 7, 1956, after suffering from a serious illness.

### 保共中委米列诺夫逝世

1956-01-10
第4版()
专栏：
 　 保共中委米列诺夫逝世
    新华社8日讯　保加利亚通讯社索非亚7日讯：保加利亚共产党中央委员会委员、保加利亚工会中央理事会书记、保加利亚矿工工会中央委员会主席、国民议会议员亚历山大·米列诺夫，在患重病以后于1956年1月7日逝世。
