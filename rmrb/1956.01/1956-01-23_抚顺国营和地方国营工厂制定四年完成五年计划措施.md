Title: Fushun State-Owned and Local State-Owned Factories Formulate Measures to Complete the Five-Year Plan in Four Years
Author:
Date: 1956-01-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch

    Fushun City's state-owned and local state-owned factories have already proposed guarantees to complete the Five-Year Plan in four years.
    Fushun Heavy Machinery Plant has formulated specific measures to complete the Five-Year Plan one year ahead of schedule. The plant's output of various major products this year will increase by 1 to 3 times compared to 1955, and labor productivity will increase by 64%. The Fourth Cement Plant plans to implement Soviet experience such as water cooling and heat exchangers this year, so that cement production this year will be 45.8% higher than last year. Local state-owned factories have also proposed to improve product quality and trial-produce products such as nitrogen fertilizer, diesel engines, and water pumps to support the agricultural cooperative movement.

### 抚顺国营和地方国营工厂制定四年完成五年计划措施

1956-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    抚顺国营和地方国营工厂制定四年完成五年计划措施
    抚顺市各国营和地方国营工厂已经提出四年完成五年计划的保证。
    抚顺重型机器厂已经制定出提前一年完成五年计划的具体措施。这个厂今年各种主要产品的产量要比1955年增加1倍到3倍，劳动生产率提高64％。第四水泥厂准备在今年推行水冷却和热交换器等苏联经验，使今年水泥的产量比去年多45.8％。地方国营工厂也提出要提高产品质量，并且要试制氮肥、柴油机、水泵等产品支援农业合作化运动。（据新华社讯）
