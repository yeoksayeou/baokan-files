Title: Phạm Văn Đồng on the 1956 National Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-01-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 19th.  Phạm Văn Đồng, Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Chairman of the State Planning Commission, published an article in the "People's Daily" on the 19th, entitled: "Some Basic Issues of the 1956 National Plan."

The article stated that the "1956 National Plan" was formulated in accordance with the development path of the people's democratic system. This path is to gradually move towards socialism in accordance with Vietnam's political conditions and the wishes of the Vietnamese people of all strata. This plan is also based on the program of the Vietnamese Fatherland Front, which aims to consolidate peace and achieve unity through peaceful means on the basis of independence and democracy.

The article said that the general task of the "1956 National Plan" is to basically restore the economy to the pre-war level, initially develop culture, strive to heal the wounds of war, alleviate the difficulties of the people, and gradually improve the material and cultural life of the people. This is also the basic and urgent requirement of the political struggle. The "1956 National Plan" will bring benefits to the people of all strata; it will strengthen the unity of all the people and consolidate the people's democratic regime on the basis of the worker-peasant alliance.

The article emphasized that the "1956 National Plan" is a plan for all the people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The article put forward the necessity to strengthen ideological, policy, and organizational preparations in order to realize this plan.

### 范文同论1956年的国家计划

1956-01-21
第4版()
专栏：

    范文同论1956年的国家计划
    新华社河内19日电　越南民主共和国政府总理兼国家计划委员会主席范文同，19日在“人民报”上发表一篇文章，题目是：“1956年国家计划”的若干基本问题。
    文章说，“1956年国家计划”是根据人民民主制度的发展路线制定的。这条路线是按照越南的政治条件和越南各阶层人民的意愿，逐步走向社会主义。这个计划同时也是根据越南祖国战线的纲领制定的，这个纲领的目的在于巩固和平，在独立和民主的基础上通过和平方法实现统一。
    文章说，“1956年国家计划”的总的任务是：使经济基本上恢复到相当于战前水平，初步发展文化，努力医治战争创伤，减轻人民的困难，逐步提高人民的物质和文化生活。这也是政治斗争的基本和迫切要求。“1956年国家计划”将给各阶层人民带来利益；将密切全体人民的团结，并且在工农联盟的基础上巩固人民民主政权。
    文章强调说，“1956年国家计划”是越南民主共和国全体人民的计划。文章提出必须加强思想、政策和组织的准备工作，以实现这个计划。
