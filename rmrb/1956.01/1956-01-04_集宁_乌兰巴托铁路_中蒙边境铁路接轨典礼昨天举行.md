Title: Jining-Ulaanbaatar Railway: China-Mongolia Border Railway Track-Laying Ceremony Held Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1956-01-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Eren, January 3rd: The track-laying ceremony for the Jining-Ulaanbaatar Railway on the China-Mongolia border was held on January 3rd between Erenhot and Zamyn-Üüd on the China-Mongolia border.

    At today's ceremony, after the Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, Tsedenbal, and the Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Ulanhu, tightened the last bolts of the railway track, Tsedenbal, Ulanhu, and the heads of the government delegations from China, the Soviet Union, and Mongolia warmly shook hands and congratulated each other. The people attending the ceremony shouted: "Long live the friendship of the people of China, the Soviet Union, and Mongolia!" After the ribbon-cutting, the first freight train from Ulaanbaatar entered Chinese territory at 10:55 AM; the first train from China departed for the Mongolian People's Republic at 11:40 AM.

    The track-laying ceremony was presided over by Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party.

    Those attending the ceremony included: Tsedenbal, Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic; Zedeb, Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of Mongolia; Shirindib, First Deputy Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic; and Yadan Jaf, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic.

    Also attending the ceremony was Ulanhu, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China.

    The Chinese government delegation headed by Teng Daiyuan, the Mongolian government delegation headed by Lubsan, and the Soviet government delegation headed by Beshev all participated in the track-laying ceremony.

    He Ying, Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, Pisarev, Soviet Ambassador, and Hong Dong-chul, Korean Ambassador, also attended the ceremony.

    Those attending the track-laying ceremony also included nearly one thousand representatives of railway workers from China, Mongolia, and the Soviet Union who were responsible for building the Jining-Ulaanbaatar Railway, responsible cadres from relevant railway management institutions, and representatives from the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region of China and various provinces of the Mongolian People's Republic.

    At the celebration meeting for the track-laying ceremony, Prime Minister Tsedenbal of the Mongolian People's Republic spoke first. Then Ulanhu, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and Beshev, head of the Soviet government delegation, spoke successively. In their speeches, they unanimously pointed out that the completion and opening of the China-Mongolia Railway marks a major development in the great friendship and friendly cooperation between the three countries and will promote economic and cultural exchanges among the three countries.

    Also speaking at the meeting were Ham, a representative of Mongolian railway workers, and Ding Wanchen, a representative of Chinese railway workers and winner of the "Glorious Railway Worker" medal.

    Finally, Yadam Surun, Minister of Transportation of the Mongolian People's Republic, presented a banner to the Chinese railway workers on behalf of the Mongolian railway workers; Teng Daiyuan, Minister of Railways of the People's Republic of China, presented a banner to all the employees of the Soviet-Mongolian Ulaanbaatar Railway Joint Venture Company on behalf of the Chinese railway workers.

    Xinhua News Agency, Eren, January 3rd: Prime Minister Tsedenbal of the Mongolian People's Republic and Dambaa, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, arrived in Erenhot, China, by train from Zamyn-Üüd at 5:50 PM on the afternoon of the 3rd, at the invitation of our country.

    Accompanying the distinguished guests to Eren were Ulanhu, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, the Chinese government delegation headed by Teng Daiyuan, and He Ying, Chinese Ambassador to the Mongolian People's Republic.

    Prime Minister Tsedenbal was warmly welcomed by heads of various departments and railway employees upon his arrival in Erenhot. He reviewed the guard of honor of the People's Republic of China.

    Tsedenbal and Damba were invited to attend the track-laying ceremony of the Jining-Ulaanbaatar Railway on the China-Mongolia border.

    In Erenhot, Prime Minister Tsedenbal and others were invited to attend a welcome banquet hosted by Teng Daiyuan, Minister of Railways of the People's Republic of China. In the evening, Prime Minister Tsedenbal and Damba, accompanied by Ambassador He Ying, returned to Ulaanbaatar.

    Those who came to Erenhot with Prime Minister Tsedenbal today included Shirindib, First Deputy Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, Yadan Jaf, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, the Mongolian government delegation headed by Lubsan, the Soviet government delegation headed by Beshev, the Soviet and Korean Ambassadors to the Mongolian People's Republic, the Mongolian Ambassador to the People's Republic of China, and the heads and employees of the railway authorities responsible for the construction of the Ulaanbaatar-Zamyn-Üüd Railway.

    The Mongolian government delegation headed by Lubsan, the Soviet government delegation headed by Beshev, the Chinese government delegation headed by Teng Daiyuan, and Ochirbat, the Mongolian Ambassador to China, and others left Erenhot for Beijing tonight.

### 集宁—乌兰巴托铁路  中蒙边境铁路接轨典礼昨天举行

1956-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    集宁—乌兰巴托铁路
    中蒙边境铁路接轨典礼昨天举行
    新华社二连3日电　集宁—乌兰巴托铁路中、蒙边境铁路接轨典礼3日在中、蒙边境二连和扎门乌德之间举行。
    在今天的典礼上，当蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔和中华人民共和国国务院副总理乌兰夫拧上铁轨的最后一部分螺丝钉以后，泽登巴尔、乌兰夫和中、苏、蒙三国政府代表团团长互相热烈握手祝贺，参加典礼的人们高呼：“中、苏、蒙三国人民友谊万岁！”剪彩以后，第一列从乌兰巴托开来的货物列车在10时55分开入中国国境；第一列从中国开出的列车在11时40分开往蒙古人民共和国。
    接轨典礼由蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴主持。
    参加典礼的有：蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔、蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团副主席泽狄布、蒙古人民共和国第一副总理锡林迪布、蒙古人民共和国外交部副部长雅丹甲夫。
    参加典礼的还有中华人民共和国国务院副总理乌兰夫。
    以滕代远为首的中国政府代表团，以鲁布桑为首的蒙古政府代表团，以别谢夫为首的苏联政府代表团都参加了接轨典礼。
    驻蒙古的中国大使何英、苏联大使皮萨列夫和朝鲜大使洪东哲也参加了典礼。
    参加接轨典礼的，还有负责修建集宁—乌兰巴托铁路的中、蒙、苏三国铁路职工代表和有关的铁路管理机构负责干部以及中国内蒙古自治区和来自蒙古人民共和国各省代表等近一千人。
    在接轨典礼庆祝大会上，首先由蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔讲话。接着中华人民共和国国务院副总理乌兰夫和苏联政府代表团团长别谢夫先后讲了话。他们在讲话中一致指出：中、蒙铁路的接轨通车，标志着三国人民之间的伟大友谊和友好合作关系的重大发展，并且将促进三国的经济和文化交流。
    在大会上讲话的还有蒙古铁路工人代表哈姆，中国铁路工人代表、“光荣的铁路工作者”奖章获得者丁万臣。
    最后，蒙古人民共和国运输部部长雅达姆苏伦代表蒙古铁路职工向中国铁路职工赠旗；中华人民共和国铁道部部长滕代远代表中国铁路职工向苏、蒙乌兰巴托铁路合营公司全体职工赠旗。
    新华社二连3日电　蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔和蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴应我国邀请在3日下午5时50分从扎门乌德乘火车到达我国二连。
    陪同贵宾来到二连的，有中华人民共和国国务院副总理乌兰夫、以滕代远为首的中华人民共和国政府代表团和中国驻蒙古人民共和国大使何英。
    泽登巴尔总理在抵达二连的时候，受到各部门负责人和铁路员工的热烈欢迎。他检阅了中华人民共和国仪仗队。
    泽登巴尔和达姆巴曾应邀参加了集宁—乌兰巴托铁路中蒙边境接轨典礼。
    在二连，泽登巴尔总理等人应邀出席了中华人民共和国铁道部部长滕代远的欢迎宴会。当晚，泽登巴尔总理和达姆巴等人，由何英大使陪同返回乌兰巴托。
    今天和泽登巴尔总理一起前来二连的，有蒙古人民共和国第一副总理锡林迪布、外交部副部长雅丹甲夫和以鲁布桑为首的蒙古政府代表团，以别谢夫为首的苏联政府代表团以及苏联、朝鲜驻蒙古人民共和国大使，蒙古驻中华人民共和国大使，承建乌兰巴托—扎门乌德铁路的铁路机关负责人和员工等人。
    以鲁布桑为首的蒙古政府代表团，以别谢夫为首的苏联政府代表团，以滕代远为首的中国政府代表团和蒙古驻华大使奥其尔巴特等人，今晚已经离开二连前往北京。
