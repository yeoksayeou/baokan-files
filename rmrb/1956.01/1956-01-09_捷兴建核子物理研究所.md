Title: Czechoslovakia to Build Nuclear Physics Institute
Author:
Date: 1956-01-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 8th: According to the Czechoslovak News Agency, two major projects of the Czechoslovak Nuclear Physics Institute have recently begun construction not far from Prague.

　　This Nuclear Physics Institute will be completed within three years. The reactor transported from the Soviet Union will be tested there within this year.

### 捷兴建核子物理研究所

1956-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷兴建核子物理研究所
    据新华社布拉格8日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道：捷克斯洛伐克核子物理学研究所的两项主要工程，最近已经在距布拉格不远的地方动工兴建。
    这个核子物理学研究所将在三年以内建成。从苏联运来的反应堆将在今年内在那里试用。
