Title: Cheers for the Complete Victory of Socialist Transformation (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1956-01-29
Page: 5

Cheering the Complete Victory of Socialist Transformation

Beijing: More than 200,000 people in Beijing held a rally and celebration at Tiananmen Square on the afternoon of January 15 to celebrate the victory of socialist transformation. The picture shows the venue of the meeting.
　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie

Shanghai: 500,000 people in Shanghai gathered and marched in the rain on January 21 to celebrate Shanghai's entry into socialist society. The picture shows the venue of the meeting.
                     Xinhua News Agency reporter Qi Guanshan

Wuhan: More than 150,000 people in Wuhan held a rally and parade in the rare heavy snow of the South on January 22 to celebrate the great victory of socialist transformation in the city.　Changjiang Daily

Xi'an: The city of Xi'an has entered socialist society. The picture shows the grand occasion on January 15 when more than 100,000 people held a meeting to celebrate the complete joint state-private ownership of private industrial and commercial enterprises and the basic cooperativization of handicrafts in the city.
        Xinhua News Agency reporter Sheng Jirun

Jinan: More than 130,000 people in Jinan held a grand rally and parade on January 20 to celebrate the great victory of socialist transformation in the city. The picture shows the parade of farmers participating in the meeting.
      Sun Baopu (Dazhong Daily)

Shenyang: People from all walks of life in Shenyang gathered on January 16 to celebrate the great victory of socialist transformation in Shenyang. The picture shows the parade passing through the prosperous commercial area of Shenyang—Chengnei Zhongjie.
              Yu Fei (Liaoning Daily)

Chongqing: People from all walks of life in Chongqing held a grand parade on January 21 to celebrate the great victory of socialist transformation. The picture shows the parade flocking to the Liberation Monument.
           Luo Ziguang (Chongqing Daily)

Taiyuan: By the 23rd of this month, Taiyuan had achieved a complete victory in socialist transformation. The picture shows the crowds participating in the city's joint state-private ownership conference for private industrial and commercial enterprises on January 19.
           Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhou Shuming

Changsha: On January 20, thousands of workers, farmers, handicraftsmen, and capitalists in Changsha gathered and marched in the rare severe cold of the South to celebrate the great victory of socialist transformation in the city.
       Zhang Yunlong (New Hunan Daily)

Tianjin: More than 900,000 people in Tianjin gathered on January 22 to celebrate the great victory of socialist transformation. The picture shows the families of industrialists and businessmen participating in the meeting, cheering for Tianjin's entry into socialist society.
		　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Yuan Ling

Nanjing: More than 300,000 people in Nanjing held a grand rally and parade on January 17 to celebrate the great victory of socialist transformation in the city. The picture shows the parade passing in front of the reviewing stand at Xinjiekou.
            Our reporter Zhen Weimin

Guangzhou: By the 24th of this month, Guangzhou had achieved a complete victory in socialist transformation. This is a picture of the workers at the rally held on January 20 at Yuexiu Mountain People's Stadium to approve the complete joint state-private ownership of all private industrial and commercial enterprises in the city.
　　Liao Yanquan, Chen Fubei (Southern Daily)

### 欢呼社会主义改造的全面胜利（图片）

1956-01-29
第5版()
专栏：

    欢呼社会主义改造的全面胜利
    北京市20多万人在1月15日下午在天安门广场举行庆祝社会主义改造胜利联欢大会。图为大会会场。　　新华社记者　钱嗣杰摄
    上海市50万人在1月21日冒雨集会和游行，庆祝上海进入社会主义社会。图为大会会场。
                     新华社记者　齐观山摄
    武汉市15万多人在1月22日冒着南方稀有的大雪举行大会和游行，庆祝全市社会主义改造的伟大胜利。　长江日报社稿
    西安市已经进入社会主义社会。图为1月15日10万多人举行大会，庆祝全市私营工商业全部公私合营和手工业基本合作化的盛况。
        新华社记者　盛继润摄
    济南市13万多人在1月20日举行盛大集会和游行，庆祝全市社会主义改造的伟大胜利。图为参加大会的农民的游行队伍。
      孙保朴摄（大众日报社稿）
    沈阳市各界人民在1月16日集会庆祝沈阳社会主义改造的伟大胜利。图为游行队伍穿过沈阳市的繁荣商业区——城内中街。　　
              于　飞摄（辽宁日报社稿）
    重庆市各界人民在1月21日举行盛大游行，庆祝社会主义改造的伟大胜利。图为游行队伍涌向解放碑。
           罗梓光摄（重庆日报社稿）
    太原市到本月23日社会主义改造已经取得全面胜利。图为1月19日参加全市私营工商业公私合营大会的群众队伍。　　
           新华社记者　周树铭摄
    1月20日，长沙市成千上万的职工、农民、手工业者和资本家，冒着南方少有的严寒集会和游行，庆祝全市社会主义改造的伟大胜利。
       张云龙摄（新湖南报社稿）
    天津市90多万人在1月22日集会庆祝社会主义改造的伟大胜利。图为参加大会的工商业者家属在欢呼天津市进入社会主义社会。		　　新华社记者　袁苓摄
    南京市30多万人在1月17日举行盛大集会游行，庆祝全市社会主义改造的伟大胜利。图为游行队伍通过新街口检阅台前。
            本报记者　甄为民摄
    广州市到本月24日社会主义改造已取得全面胜利。这是参加1月20日在越秀山人民体育场召开的批准全市私营工商业全部公私合营大会上的工人群众。　　廖衍强、陈福北摄（南方日报社稿）
