Title: The Second Plenary Session of the CPPCC National Committee Postponed to the 30th

Author: 
Date: 1956-01-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 24th: The Thirteenth Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference was held on the afternoon of the 24th.

    The meeting decided to postpone the Second Plenary Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference to January 30th. The meeting also approved the agenda of the Second Plenary Session.

    The meeting also discussed and approved the work report of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference to the Second Plenary Session and determined the list of members of the Proposals Examination Committee for the Second Plenary Session.

### 政协全国委员会第二次全体会议延至30日举行

1956-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会第二次全体会议延至30日举行
    新华社24日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会第十三次会议在24日下午举行。
    会议决定中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第二次全体会议延至1月30日召开。会议并通过了第二次全体会议的议程。
    会议还讨论和通过了中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会向第二次全体会议的工作报告，确定了第二次全体会议提案审查委员会名单。
