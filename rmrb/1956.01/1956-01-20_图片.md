Title: Picture
Author: Fan Jie
Date: 1956-01-20
Page: 3

    Shanghai has recently established a cadre training class for schistosomiasis prevention and control. More than 800 people, including doctors of both Chinese and Western medicine, nurses, and laboratory technicians, are participating in the training class. The picture shows Zheng Simin (second from the left), a doctor from the Shanghai Municipal Hygiene and Anti-epidemic Station, explaining the life cycle of schistosomiasis to the trainees. Xinhua News Agency reporter Fan Jie photographed.

专栏：

    上海市最近成立了一个防治血吸虫病的干部训练班。参加训练班学习的有中、西医师、护士、化验员等共八百多人。这是上海市卫生防疫站医师郑思民（左起第二人）在向学员们讲解血吸虫生活史的情形。新华社记者　范杰摄
