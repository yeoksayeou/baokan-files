Title: Gansu Province Basically Achieves Agricultural Cooperativization
Author:
Date: 1956-01-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report:
    Gansu Province has basically achieved agricultural cooperativization of a semi-socialist nature. As of the end of last year, more than 41,800 agricultural producers' cooperatives had been established throughout the province, and with the more than 1,000 cooperatives about to be established, the number of peasant households joining the cooperatives has reached 81.5% of the total number of peasant households. The speed of agricultural cooperativization in ethnic minority areas is also very fast. In the Guyuan Hui Autonomous Prefecture, the number of peasant households joining the cooperatives has reached 66.6%, and in the Wuzhong Hui Autonomous Prefecture, the number of peasant households joining the cooperatives has reached 76.8%. According to inspections in various places, the quality of the newly established cooperatives is generally good. Before the spring plowing this year, the province will also establish 440 advanced agricultural producers' cooperatives.
    At present, 40 counties and cities in Gansu Province have basically completed the stage of establishing cooperatives and are fully turning to rectifying and consolidating the cooperatives and carrying out production work. Other counties can basically complete the cooperative establishment work in January.

### 甘肃省基本实现农业合作化

1956-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

    甘肃省基本实现农业合作化
    甘肃省已经基本上实现了半社会主义性质的农业合作化。截至去年年底止，全省已经建成41，800多个农业生产合作社，加上即将建成的一千多个社，入社农户已经占到了农户总数的81.5％。少数民族地区农业合作化的速度也很快，固原回族自治州入社农户已经达到66.6％，吴忠回族自治州入社农户已经达到76.8％。根据各地的检查，新建立的社的质量一般良好。今年春耕以前，全省还要建立440个高级农业生产合作社。
    现在，甘肃省已经有40个县、市基本上结束了建社阶段，全力转入整顿巩固社和开展生产工作，其他各县在1月份也可基本结束建社工作。
                  （据新华社讯）
