Title: Afghan Ambassador to China States Hope to Enhance Friendly Relations Between China and Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1956-01-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, January 11th. Afghanistan's first ambassador to China, H.E. Abdul Samad, said to a "Ta Kung Pao" reporter in Hong Kong on January 9th, en route to Beijing, "As the first Afghan ambassador to the People's Republic of China, I feel very happy. I look forward to establishing even friendlier relations with China. I should say this friendly relationship has existed for a long time."

The Afghan Ambassador said, "We are neighbors, and good neighbors. China and Afghanistan have always been peace-loving. We not only need peace between our two countries, but we also want to see the whole world living in peace."

He said, "The People's Republic of China is progressing and striving for peace. If a country strives for peace and for humanity, it is a great thing, a thing greater than anything else."

Samad also said that the Afghan government's policy is to never participate in any military blocs.

### 阿富汗驻华大使在香港表示  希望增进中阿友好关系

1956-01-13
第4版()
专栏：

    阿富汗驻华大使在香港表示
    希望增进中阿友好关系
    新华社香港11日电  阿富汗首任驻中国大使赫·埃·阿卜杜勒·萨马德在赴北京途中1月9日在香港对“大公报”记者说，“作为阿富汗首任驻中华人民共和国的大使，我感到非常愉快。我期望同中国建立更友好的关系。我应该说这种友好关系很久以来就存在了。”
    阿富汗大使说，“我们是邻邦，并且是很好的邻邦。中国和阿富汗一向都是维护和平的。我们不仅需要我们两国之间的和平，我们也愿意看到全世界在和平中生活。”
    他说：“中华人民共和国正在进步，并且为和平而努力。如果一个国家为和平为人类而努力，这是一件伟大的事情，这是比任何事情都更为伟大的事情。
    萨马德还说，阿富汗政府的政策是永远不参加任何军事集团。
