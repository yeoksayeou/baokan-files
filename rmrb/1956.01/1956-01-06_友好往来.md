Author:
Date: 1956-01-06
Page: 4
　　
　　Friendly Exchanges
　　Six members of the Soviet scientists' delegation to China, including Samarin, left Beijing for home on the 5th. On the evening of the 4th, Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, held a farewell banquet for them.
　　The Chinese journalists' delegation to Yugoslavia, led by Wang Yi, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily, returned to Beijing on the 4th after a three-week visit to the Soviet Union on its way home.

### 友好往来

1956-01-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　友好往来
　　苏联科学家访华代表团团员萨马林等六人5日离开北京返国。4日晚上，中国科学院院长郭沫若为他们举行了欢送宴会。
　　由人民日报副总编辑王揖率领的中国新闻工作者访问南斯拉夫代表团，在回国途中在苏联访问三星期后，已在4日返抵北京。
