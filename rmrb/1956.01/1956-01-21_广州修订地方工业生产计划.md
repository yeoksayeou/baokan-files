Title: Guangzhou Revises Local Industrial Production Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-01-21
Page: 2

The Guangzhou Municipal Planning Commission and local industrial departments recently revised the draft of the 1956 local industrial production plan. According to the revised draft plan, the total output value of Guangzhou's local industries this year will be increased by 8% compared to the original 1956 draft plan target, a 20% increase compared to the estimated completion figure for 1955, and will reach about 95% of the total output value target for 1957. The output of many products this year will reach more than 90% of the 1957 planned targets; among them, more than ten products such as麻袋(gunny sacks),胶鞋(rubber shoes),柴油机(diesel engines), 麻平布(linen plain cloth), 植物油(vegetable oil) will reach or approach the 1957 planned targets.
              (Southern Daily Editorial Department)

### 广州修订地方工业生产计划

1956-01-21
第2版()
专栏：

    广州修订地方工业生产计划
    广州市计划委员会和地方工业部门，最近修订了1956年度的地方工业生产计划草案。根据修订的计划草案，今年广州市地方工业总产值比原编的1956年计划草案指标提高了8％，比1955年预计完成数增长20％，并且将要达到1957年总产值指标的95％左右。今年很多产品的产量将要达到1957年的计划指标的90％以上；其中麻袋、胶鞋、柴油机、麻平布、植物油等十多种产品，将要达到或接近1957年的计划指标。
              （南方日报编辑部）
