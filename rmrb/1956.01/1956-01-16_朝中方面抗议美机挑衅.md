Title: Korean-Chinese Side Protests US Aircraft Provocations
Author:
Date: 1956-01-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong Dispatch via Korean Central News Agency: At the request of the Korean-Chinese side, the secretaries-general of both sides of the Military Armistice Commission held a meeting on January 14th. The secretary-general of the Korean-Chinese side lodged a stern protest with the US side at the meeting against the provocative acts of US military aircraft violating the armistice agreement, which recently penetrated deep into the rear airspace of the Korean-Chinese side on two occasions.

### 朝中方面抗议美机挑衅

1956-01-16
第4版()
专栏：

    朝中方面抗议美机挑衅
    新华社据朝鲜中央通讯社开城讯　应朝中方面的要求，军事停战委员会双方秘书长在1月14日举行了会议。朝中方面秘书长在会上就美方军用飞机违反停战协定，最近曾两次深入朝中方面后方领空的挑衅行为，向美方提出了严正抗议。
