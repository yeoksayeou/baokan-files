Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Arrives in Prague
Author:
Date: 1956-01-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 18th: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Prague, the capital of the Czechoslovak Republic, by special train from Bratislava on the afternoon of the 17th, at the invitation of the Czechoslovak Republic. Accompanying him on the visit were Marshal Nie Rongzhen, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China, and Liu Lantao, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

At 4 p.m., Vice Chairman Zhu De's special train entered Prague Central Station to the sound of military music.

After alighting from the train, Vice Chairman Zhu De warmly embraced Czechoslovak Premier Široký, who had come to greet him, and shook hands with each of the greeters. Among them were Czechoslovak Communist Party and government leaders Novotný, Čepička, Ďuriš, Fierlinger, Kopecký, Jankovcová, and Šimůnek, leaders of various democratic parties, ministers of various ministries of the Czechoslovak government, senior generals of the Czechoslovak army, and leaders of people's organizations.

Premier Široký delivered a welcome speech. Široký said: We welcome the representative of a country that has played a prominent role in modern human history. The Chinese people's struggle for liberation brilliantly demonstrated the invincible strength of freedom-loving people and made an invaluable contribution to the struggle of working people worldwide to liberate themselves from all oppression and exploitation. Under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Comrade Mao Zedong, the Chinese people have performed the same miracles in the construction of their own country, and, together with the Soviet Union, have made incalculable contributions to the great struggle to maintain and consolidate world peace, strive for friendship and cooperation between countries, and ease international tensions.

Široký continued: Our people have always cherished sincere love, friendship, and admiration for the talented and heroic Chinese people. I firmly believe that your valuable visit will be a new milestone in the development of our mutual relations and the deepening of our close and effective cooperation in various economic and cultural fields, and will contribute to the cause of our two peoples and world peace.

Vice Chairman Zhu De then delivered a speech.

The speeches of Široký and Zhu De were interrupted from time to time by applause and cheers.

After the speeches, Premier Široký and Comrade Novotný accompanied Vice Chairman Zhu De in an open-top car to the villa, where he was warmly welcomed by the surging crowds along the way.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 18th: On the afternoon of the 17th, Vice Chairman Zhu De of the People's Republic of China paid visits to Czechoslovak President Zápotocký, Czechoslovak Premier Široký, and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party Novotný.

### 朱德副主席到达布拉格

1956-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席到达布拉格
    据新华社布拉格18日电　应邀到捷克斯洛伐克共和国访问的中华人民共和国副主席朱德，17日下午乘专车从布拉迪斯拉发抵达捷克斯洛伐克共和国首都布拉格。陪同前来访问的有中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席聂荣臻元帅和全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员刘澜涛。
    下午4时朱德副主席所乘专车在军乐声中进入了布拉格中央车站。
    朱德副主席下车后即同前来欢迎的捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基热烈地拥抱，并且同前来欢迎的人一一握手。这里有捷克斯洛伐克共产党和政府领导人诺沃提尼、契皮茨卡、杜兰斯基、费林格、柯别茨基、扬科夫卓娃、西穆湼克，各民主党派的领导人，捷克斯洛伐克政府各部部长、捷克斯洛伐克军高级将领和人民团体的负责人等。
    西罗基总理致欢迎词。西罗基说：我们欢迎在近代人类历史中起着卓越作用的国家的代表。中国人民争取解放的斗争，光辉地证明了爱好自由人民的不可征服的力量，同时是对全世界劳动人民从一切压迫和剥削下求得解放斗争的不可估价的巨大贡献。中国人民在中国共产党和毛泽东同志的领导下，在自己祖国的建设中表现了同样的奇迹，同苏联一道，中国人民在维护和巩固世界和平、争取各国间的友好和合作、缓和国际紧张局势的伟大斗争作了不可估量的贡献。
    西罗基接着说：我国人民一直对有才能的和英勇的中国人民怀着诚挚的热爱、友谊和钦佩之情。我坚信您珍贵的访问，将是我们相互关系的发展和加深我们在经济和文化各方面紧密和有成效合作中新的里程碑，将有助于我们两国人民和世界和平的事业。
    朱德副主席随后讲话。
    西罗基和朱德的讲话不时被掌声和欢呼声打断。
    讲话结束后，西罗基总理和诺沃提尼同志陪同朱德副主席乘上一辆敞篷汽车驶向别墅，沿途受到了沸腾起来的群众的极为热烈的欢迎。
    据新华社布拉格18日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德，在17日下午拜会了捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基、捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理西罗基和捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记诺沃提尼。
