Title: Highway Transportation System Workers Decide to Launch Industry-Wide Labor Emulation Drive
Author:
Date: 1956-01-21
Page: 1

    The National Committee of the Chinese Highway Transport Trade Union held its Seventh Plenary (Enlarged) Meeting recently in Beijing. The meeting focused on discussing and studying the issue of launching a socialist emulation drive across the highway transportation system.

    The 100 labor models and advanced workers in automobile transport and automobile repair who attended the meeting proposed to automobile transport workers across the country to launch a socialist emulation drive across the entire industry. Representatives from 43 units including transport departments and engineering bureaus and highway administration bureaus of various provinces, cities, and autonomous regions signed an agreement on January 19 to launch a socialist emulation drive across the entire industry.

    An Lifu, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Highway Transport Trade Union, read out the conditions for emulation in five areas: highway transportation, highway construction, surveying and design, highway maintenance, and automobile repair at the signing ceremony. He also announced the annual evaluation and reward methods. The main content of the emulation conditions for highway construction is to comprehensively complete and overfulfill the 1956 highway construction plan ahead of schedule. The main content of the emulation conditions for automobile transportation work is to complete the various targets and quotas stipulated in the Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule, save automobile fuel, and extend the mileage between two major overhauls of automobiles.

    Xinhua News Agency, January 19th: Automobile transport workers of national state-owned transportation enterprises saved more than 2.5 million kilograms of gasoline and diesel in the fourth quarter of last year.

    The Ministry of Communications today rewarded Ai Zhaochang, Yang Maolin, and Ge Zhengzhong, the initiators of the automobile fuel saving campaign. At the same time, it also rewarded the Transportation Bureau of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Transportation Department, the Transportation Bureau of the Sichuan Provincial Transportation Department, and the Tianjin Municipal Transportation Bureau, which actively responded to the initiative to save automobile fuel and saved the most automobile fuel.

### 公路运输系统职工决定开展全产业的劳动竞赛

1956-01-21
第1版()
专栏：

    公路运输系统职工决定开展全产业的劳动竞赛
    本报讯　中国公路运输工会全国委员会第七次全体会议（扩大）最近在北京举行。这次会议着重讨论研究了在公路运输系统开展全产业社会主义竞赛的问题。
    出席会议的100名汽车运输、汽车修理劳动模范、先进工作者，向全国汽车运输职工提出了开展全产业社会主义竞赛的建议。出席会议的各省、市、自治区的运输部门和各工程局、公路管理局43个单位的代表，在19日签订了开展全产业社会主义竞赛的协议书。
    中国公路运输工会全国委员会主席安力夫在签字仪式大会上宣读了公路运输、公路建筑、勘测设计、公路养护和汽车修理5个方面的竞赛条件，并且宣布了年终评比和奖励办法。公路建筑方面竞赛条件的主要内容，是全面地提前和超额完成1956年建筑公路计划。汽车运输工作方面竞赛条件的主要内容，是提前完成五年计划中规定的各项指标和定额，节约汽车用油，延长汽车两次大修之间走行的里程。
    据新华社19日讯　全国国营运输企业的汽车运输职工在去年第四季度节省了250多万公斤汽油和柴油。
    交通部今天奖励了节约汽车用油运动的倡议者艾肇昌、杨茂林和葛正中。同时，还奖励了积极响应节约汽车用油倡议、节省汽车用油最多的新疆维吾尔自治区交通厅运输局、四川省交通厅运输局和天津市交通运输局。
