Title: Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Gunboat Intercepts British Cargo Ship
Author:
Date: 1956-01-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: According to a statement released on the 15th by British naval authorities in Hong Kong, a Chiang Kai-shek bandit gunboat intercepted the British cargo ship "Hai Tung" on the high seas on the 14th, preventing the "Hai Tung" from sailing into Fuzhou.

The statement said that a British warship was sent to stop the interference with British ships sailing on the high seas. On the morning of the 15th, the "Hai Tung" sailed into Fuzhou.

### 蒋贼炮艇拦截英国货船

1956-01-17
第4版()
专栏：

    蒋贼炮艇拦截英国货船
    新华社16日讯　据香港英国海军当局15日发表的公报说，蒋贼炮艇1艘14日曾经在公海上拦截英国货船“航通号”，阻止“航通号”驶入福州。
    公报说，1艘英国军舰被派去制止对英国船只在公海上航行所进行的干涉。15日上午，“航通号”驶入了福州。
