Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Postponed by One Day
Author:
Date: 1956-01-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, January 28th -- With the agreement of both China and the United States, the thirty-fifth meeting of the Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks, originally scheduled for February 3rd, has been rescheduled to February 4th (Saturday).

### 中美大使级会谈延期一天举行

1956-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈延期一天举行
    新华社日内瓦28日电　经中国和美国双方同意，原来定在2月3日举行的中美两国大使级会谈第三十五次会议，改到2月4日（星期六）举行。
