Title: Jordanian Prime Minister Says Jordan Will Not Participate in Any Foreign Military Treaties
Author: 
Date: 1956-01-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, January 18th: Jordanian Prime Minister Samir al-Rifai told a reporter from the Syrian newspaper "Nida al-Watan" that his country will no longer allow anyone to interfere in its internal affairs, and his government will not allow Jordan to participate in any foreign military treaties.

### 约旦首相说  约旦不参加任何外国军事条约

1956-01-20
第4版()
专栏：

    约旦首相说
    约旦不参加任何外国军事条约
    新华社19日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特18日讯：约旦首相萨米尔·里法伊对叙利亚“尼达·阿尔—瓦坦报”的记者说，他的国家不再允许任何人干涉它的内政，他的政府不容许约旦参加任何外国军事条约。
