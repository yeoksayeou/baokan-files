Title: One Hundred Automotive Transportation Labor Models and Advanced Workers Suggest Automobile Transportation Industry Employees Launch Industry-Wide Competition
Author:
Date: 1956-01-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 13th: One hundred labor models and advanced workers in the field of automotive transportation today proposed to the employees of the national automotive transportation industry to launch a comprehensive labor competition across the entire industry, striving to complete the automotive transportation tasks stipulated in the First Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule.
    The people who put forward this proposal held a signing ceremony tonight.
    The proposal puts forward ten specific competition conditions, requiring comprehensive completion and over-fulfillment of national transportation plans, saving automotive fuel and fuel, maximizing the mileage between two major vehicle repairs, and extending tire mileage.
    The All-China Highway Transport Trade Union's National Committee's Seventh Plenary Session, which is currently being held, agreed to the above proposal and made a resolution on launching a nationwide automotive transportation industry labor competition. An Lifu, Chairman of the All-China Highway Transport Trade Union National Committee, read out this resolution at the meeting.
    Those who put forward this proposal include Ai Zhaochang, the nationally famous initiator of saving automotive fuel, Bian Jieqiu, the best worker in vehicle protection, Chen Rongcai, who drove a car for more than 230,000 kilometers without major repairs, Jiang Chunyan, who used one tire for 190,000 kilometers, as well as advanced engineer Wang Hanhua, advanced female technician Gu Nisha from the October Automobile Repair Factory, Bashir, and others.

### 一百个汽车运输劳动模范和先进工作者  建议汽车运输业职工开展全产业竞赛

1956-01-14
第1版()
专栏：

    一百个汽车运输劳动模范和先进工作者
    建议汽车运输业职工开展全产业竞赛
    新华社13日讯  今天有一百个汽车运输方面的劳动模范和先进工作者，向全国汽车运输业的职工提出开展全产业的全面劳动竞赛的建议，争取提前完成第一个五年计划中规定的汽车运输任务。
    提出这个建议书的人在今晚举行了签字仪式大会。
    这个建议书中，提出了十项具体的竞赛条件，要求做到全面完成和超额完成国家运输计划，节约汽车用油和燃料，最大限度地延长汽车两次大修之间的行驶里程和延长轮胎行驶里程等。
    正在举行的中国公路运输工会全国委员会第七次全体会议同意以上的建议，作出了关于开展全国汽车运输业劳动竞赛的决议。中国公路运输工会全国委员会主席安力夫在大会上宣读了这个决议。
    提出这个建议书的有全国著名的节约汽车用油的倡议者艾肇昌、保护车辆最好的工作者卞介秋、驾驶汽车行走23万多公里没有大修的陈荣材、使用一只轮胎行驶里程达到19万公里的蒋纯炎，另外还有先进工程师王汉华、十月汽车修配厂先进女技工古尼沙、巴士尔等人。
