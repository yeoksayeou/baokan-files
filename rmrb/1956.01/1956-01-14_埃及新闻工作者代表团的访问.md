Title: Visit of Egyptian Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-01-14
Page: 4

    Visit of Egyptian Journalists Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, January 13th: The Egyptian journalists delegation, led by Abdel Moneim Mahmoud Sawi, editor-in-chief of the Egyptian News Agency, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 13th to visit Shanghai, Guangzhou, and other places on their way home.
    On the 12th, the head of the delegation, Sawi, editor-in-chief of the Egyptian News Agency, signed a contract with Xinhua News Agency on the exchange of news and pictures on behalf of the Egyptian News Agency. On the same evening, the entire delegation was invited to attend a farewell banquet hosted by the All-China Journalists Association.

### 埃及新闻工作者代表团的访问

1956-01-14
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及新闻工作者代表团的访问
    据新华社13日讯  以埃及通讯社总编辑阿卜杜勒·莫内姆·马茂德·萨韦为首的埃及新闻工作者代表团13日晨乘飞机离开北京，在回国途中前往上海和广州等地参观。
    12日，代表团团长、埃及通讯社总编辑萨韦代表埃及通讯社同新华通讯社签订了关于交换新闻和图片的合同。同日晚间，代表团全体应邀出席了中国新闻工作者联谊会的欢送宴会。
