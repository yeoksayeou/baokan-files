Title: Soong Ching Ling's Visit to India
Author:
Date: 1956-01-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: Soochow Ching Ling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congres of the People's Republic of China, and her entourage arrived in Bangalore, the capital of Mysore State, on the morning of December 30th by plane from Madras.

Xinhua News Agency, Madras, December 30th: The Governor of Madras Province, Prakasa, held a tea party at the Government House on the afternoon of the 29th to welcome Soong Ching Ling. After the tea party, Soong Ching Ling attended a welcome meeting held by women's groups, social welfare groups, and thirty other groups in Madras at the Government House. Mrs. Deshmukh, Chairman of the Central Social Welfare Board, delivered a welcome speech to Soong Ching Ling and introduced the work of social welfare in India.

In her reply, Soong Ching Ling said that although she had only been in Madras for a short time, she had a very deep impression of the city, its natural scenery, its ancient culture, and its social welfare activities. Soong Ching Ling expressed her deep sympathy to the residents of Tanjore and other areas of Madras Province who had recently been affected by the disaster and wished them a speedy recovery.

Soong Ching Ling continued, "During the visit, wherever I went, people welcomed me with the phrase 'Indians and Chinese are brothers.' This represents a common call from the depths of our hearts." She said, "Let this call always linger in our hearts, so as to further strengthen the friendship and cooperation between China and India."

Xinhua News Agency, Bangalore, December 31st: The Chief Minister of Mysore State, Hanumanthaiya, held a tea party at his official residence on the afternoon of December 30th to entertain Soong Ching Ling, the Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic Of China.

Those present at the tea party included H. S. Rudrappa, Speaker of the Mysore Legislative Council, and others.

### 宋庆龄在印度的访问

1956-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　宋庆龄在印度的访问
    据新华社讯　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄和她的随行人员在12月30日上午从马德拉斯乘飞机到达卖索尔邦首府班加罗尔。
    据新华社马德拉斯30日电　马德拉斯省省长普拉卡萨29日下午在政府大厦举行茶会欢迎宋庆龄。茶会后，宋庆龄参加了马德拉斯的妇女团体、社会福利团体以及其他三十个团体在政府大厦举行的欢迎会。中央社会福利委员会主席德希穆克女士向宋庆龄致欢迎辞，并且介绍了印度社会福利工作的情况。
    宋庆龄在致答辞的时候说，虽然她到马德拉斯不久，但是她对这个都市，它的自然风景和古老的文化以及它的社会福利活动有了很深刻的印象。宋庆龄向马德拉斯省的丹卓尔和其他地区最近受灾的居民表示深切的同情，并且祝他们迅速得到恢复。
    宋庆龄接着说：“在访问期间，无论我到哪里，人们都用印度人和中国人是兄弟这句话来欢迎我。这表示了发自我们内心深处的共同呼声。”她说：“让这种呼声永远在我们心里萦回，从而进一步加强中国和印度之间的友谊和合作吧。”
    据新华社班加罗尔31日电　印度卖索尔邦首席部长哈努曼塔亚12月30日下午在他的官邸举行茶会，招待中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄。
    出席茶会的有卖索尔邦立法会议议长赫·斯·鲁德拉帕等人。
