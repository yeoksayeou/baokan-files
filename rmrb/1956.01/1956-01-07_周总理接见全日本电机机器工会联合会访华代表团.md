Title: Premier Zhou Receives Delegation from the All-Japan Electrical Machinery Workers' Union Federation

Author:

Date: 1956-01-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 6th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, received the delegation from the All-Japan Electrical Machinery Workers' Union Federation, headed by Tsutsui Yayoichi, on the evening of the 6th. Present at the reception were Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Kang Yonghe, Chairman of the National Committee of the First Machine Workers' Union of China, and Gao Shoushan, Deputy Secretary-General. Jinzuke Kaneko, a Japanese trade union worker currently in Beijing, was also present.

### 周总理接见全日本电机机器工会联合会访华代表团

1956-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见全日本电机机器工会联合会访华代表团
    据新华社6日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，6日晚间接见了以筒井弥代志为首的全日本电机机器工会联合会访华代表团。接见时在座的，有中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，中国第一机械工会全国委员会主席康永和、副秘书长高首善。现在北京的日本工会工作者金子健太也在座。

　　周总理和筒井弥代志握手。左第一人是全日本电机机器工会联合会访华代表团秘书长斋藤欣也。
