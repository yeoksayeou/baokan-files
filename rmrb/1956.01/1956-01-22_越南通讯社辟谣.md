Title: Vietnam News Agency Denies Rumor
Author:
Date: 1956-01-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 21st: The Vietnam News Agency released the following statement:
    Recently, the American Associated Press, the South Vietnamese Ngo Dinh Diem regime's radio station, and foreign newspapers published news saying that the leaders of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam have agreed to postpone the general election for the reunification of Vietnam until 5 or 10 years later.
    The Vietnam News Agency is authorized to announce that this news is completely fabricated.

### 越南通讯社辟谣

1956-01-22
第4版()
专栏：

    越南通讯社辟谣
    新华社河内21日电　越南通讯社发布声明如下：
    最近美国合众社、南越吴庭艳政权的广播电台以及外国报纸发表消息说：越南民主共和国政府各位领导人同意把统一越南的普选延期到5年或10年之后。
    越南通讯社受权宣布：这种消息完全是捏造的。
