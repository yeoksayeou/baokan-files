Title: Hungarian Government Donates Agricultural Machinery to China; Acceptance Ceremony Held in Ziyang, Shandong
Author:
Date: 1956-01-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ziyang, 6th: The acceptance ceremony for the agricultural machinery donated by the government of the Hungarian People's Republic to China was solemnly held in Ziyang County, Shandong Province, on the afternoon of the 6th. At the ceremony, it was announced that the agricultural machinery station established with the agricultural machinery donated by the Hungarian government would be named "Sino-Hungarian Friendship Agricultural Machinery Tractor Station."

At the ceremony, Zhao Jianmin, Governor of Shandong Province, on behalf of the Shandong Provincial People's Committee and the people of Shandong, expressed warm gratitude to the Hungarian government and people. Zhao Jianmin expressed hope that the "Sino-Hungarian Friendship Agricultural Machinery Tractor Station" would become the core force and school for agricultural technology reform in Shandong Province. He called on members of agricultural producers' cooperatives and government workers in the Ziyang area to care for and cherish this tractor station, to do a good job in production, and to repay the care and assistance of the Hungarian people with practical actions.

Hungarian Ambassador to China, Skala Dan, rose to speak amidst thunderous applause. He said: The government of the Hungarian People's Republic understands the efforts made by the Chinese people in the socialist transformation of agriculture, and understands the friendly feelings of the Hungarian people towards the fraternal Chinese people. It has decided to donate a complete set of equipment needed to equip an agricultural machinery station in order to further deepen the friendship between the two peoples.

Skala Dan concluded by saying: We hope that the "Sino-Hungarian Friendship Agricultural Machinery Tractor Station" established here will demonstrate the lofty ideals of proletarian internationalism, promote the further development of Chinese agriculture and the victory of socialism, and bring joy and happiness to you all.

Subsequently, Ambassador Skala Dan, on behalf of the government of the Hungarian People's Republic, presented a list of the agricultural machinery and equipment donated to China at the meeting, which was accepted by Yang Xiandong, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Agriculture of the People's Republic of China. At this moment, the band played cheerful music, the entire audience stood up, and thunderous applause continued for a long time.

Yang Xiandong spoke after accepting the gift list. He said: These excellent agricultural machines donated by Hungary are the product of the Hungarian working people's own creation, and they bring the great friendship of the Hungarian Labor People's Party, the Hungarian government, and the Hungarian people, as well as their concern and support for China's socialist transformation of agriculture. Now we have decided to establish a model agricultural machinery station here with this entire set of equipment.

He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, then read out Premier Zhou Enlai's telegram of thanks to András Hegedüs, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic.

József Vácerna, an expert sent by the Hungarian government to help build the agricultural machinery tractor station, spoke on behalf of the five Hungarian experts.

Among those attending the conference were Wei Zhenwu, Assistant Minister of Agriculture, Tan Qilong, Second Secretary of the Shandong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, and representatives of farmers from 14 counties and cities including Ziyang County in the Jining District of Shandong Province.

After the meeting, the station's staff, under the guidance of Hungarian experts, performed operations of various agricultural machines.

On the evening of the 6th, Zhao Jianmin, Governor of Shandong Province, hosted a banquet for Hungarian Ambassador Skala Dan and the Hungarian experts.

### 匈牙利政府赠送给我国农业机器  受礼大会在山东滋阳举行

1956-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　匈牙利政府赠送给我国农业机器
    受礼大会在山东滋阳举行
    据新华社滋阳6日电　匈牙利人民共和国政府赠送给中国农业机器受礼大会6日下午在山东省滋阳县城隆重举行。会上宣布，以匈牙利政府赠送的农业机器建立的农业机器站命名为“中匈友谊农业机器拖拉机站”。
    在大会上，山东省省长赵健民代表山东省人民委员会和山东人民对匈牙利政府和人民表示热烈的感谢。赵健民希望“中匈友谊农业机器拖拉机站”将成为山东省农业技术改革的核心力量和学校，他号召滋阳地区的农业生产合作社社员以及政府工作人员关心和爱护这个拖拉机站，搞好生产，以实际行动来答谢匈牙利人民的关怀和帮助。
    匈牙利驻华大使斯克拉丹在全场暴风雨般的掌声中起立讲话。他说：匈牙利人民共和国政府了解中国人民在农业社会主义改造方面所作的努力，了解匈牙利人民对兄弟的中国人民的友好感情，决定赠送装备一个农业机器站所需的全套设备，以进一步加深两国人民的友谊。
    斯克拉丹最后说：我们希望在这里成立的“中匈友谊农业机器拖拉机站”会表现无产阶级国际主义的崇高思想，会促进中国农业的进一步发展和社会主义的胜利，为你们大家带来快乐和幸福。
    接着斯克拉丹大使代表匈牙利人民共和国政府在会上送交了赠送给中国的农业机器设备的清册，由中华人民共和国农业部副部长杨显东接受。这时，乐队奏起欢快的乐曲，全场起立，雷动的掌声经久不息。
    杨显东在接受礼物清册以后讲话。他说：匈牙利赠送的这些优良的农业机器是出于匈牙利劳动人民的亲手创造，它们带来了匈牙利劳动人民党、匈牙利政府和匈牙利人民的伟大友谊和对中国农业社会主义改造事业的关怀和支持。现在我们决定用这一整套装备在这里建立一个模范的农业机器站。
    外交部部长助理何伟接着在会上宣读了周恩来总理致匈牙利人民共和国部长会议主席安·赫格居斯的感谢电。
    匈牙利政府派来帮助建设农业机器拖拉机站的专家约瑟夫·瓦采尔纳代表五位匈牙利专家讲了话。
    参加大会的有农业部部长助理魏震五、中共山东省委员会第二书记谭启龙等和山东省济宁专区滋阳县等14个县市的农民代表。
    会后，这个站的工作人员在匈牙利专家指导下，进行了各种农业机器的操作表演。
    6日晚，山东省省长赵健民宴请匈牙利大使斯克拉丹和匈牙利专家。
