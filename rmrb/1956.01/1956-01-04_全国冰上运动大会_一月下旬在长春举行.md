Title: National Ice Sports Meet to be Held in Changchun in Late January
Author:
Date: 1956-01-04
Page: 3

    National Ice Sports Meet
    To be Held in Changchun in Late January
    The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission has decided to hold the 1956 National Ice Sports Meet in Changchun City in late January. Outstanding male and female ice sports athletes from the Chinese People's Liberation Army and 20 cities including Beijing, Tianjin, Harbin, Taiyuan, and Hohhot will participate in this sports meet.
    The competition events of this meet include: men's and women's speed skating, figure skating, and men's ice hockey. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国冰上运动大会  一月下旬在长春举行

1956-01-04
第3版()
专栏：

    全国冰上运动大会
    一月下旬在长春举行
    体育运动委员会决定1月下旬在长春市举行1956年全国冰上运动大会。中国人民解放军和北京、天津、哈尔滨、太原、呼和浩特等20个市的优秀男、女冰上运动员将参加这次运动大会。
    这次大会的竞赛项目包括：男子和女子的速度滑冰、花样滑冰和男子冰球。　（新华社）
