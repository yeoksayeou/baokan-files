Title: Speech by Peng Zhen, Mayor of Beijing, at the Beijing Municipal Celebration of the Victory of Socialist Transformation

Author:
Date: 1956-01-16
Page: 2

Comrades and compatriots:

Today, all sectors of our capital city are holding a joint celebration to commemorate the great victory of the socialist transformation of our agriculture, handicrafts, and private industry and commerce.

We celebrate the victory of the complete socialist cooperativization of agriculture in the suburbs. We celebrate the victory of the complete public-private partnership of capitalist industry and commerce. We celebrate the victory of the complete cooperativization of handicrafts. We celebrate that we have eradicated the roots of poverty and laid the foundation for a strong country and a common prosperous life for everyone through collective labor. Our capital has entered a socialist society.

For these great victories, we extend warm congratulations to the workers and shop assistants. We extend warm congratulations to the fully cooperative farmers. We extend warm congratulations to the fully cooperative handicraftsmen. We extend warm congratulations to the industrialists and businessmen who have fully implemented public-private partnerships. We extend warm congratulations to all intellectuals who have worked diligently for the cause of socialism. We extend warm congratulations to all staff members.

Our various great victories have been achieved through the active efforts and overcoming of conservatism by all of us under the direct leadership of the CPC Central Committee and the Central People's Government headed by Chairman Mao. Today, Chairman Mao and other leading comrades of the Central Committee are celebrating with us the great victory of our socialist transformation. We pay our respects to Chairman Mao. We pay our respects to the leading comrades of the Central Committee. We are grateful for their correct leadership. Their correct leadership is the guarantee that we will move from victory to victory.

Also participating in our celebration are representatives of the National People's Congress and members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beijing. All our achievements are inseparable from their efforts. We express our heartfelt respect to them.

Also participating in our celebration are the leading comrades of various provinces, cities, and autonomous regions who are attending the meeting on intellectuals convened by the CPC Central Committee in Beijing. We extend a warm welcome to them. All the achievements in socialist construction and socialist transformation in our capital have been achieved with the warm help and support of all parts of the country. We express our heartfelt gratitude to the comrades in various regions.

Today, many senior generals and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army are also celebrating the victory of socialist transformation with us. The Chinese People's Liberation Army is the defender of our peaceful construction, and all our victories and achievements have been achieved under their protection. We express our heartfelt gratitude and respect to them.

We should not be satisfied with the achievements we have made. The achievements we have made are only the first step in a long march towards our great historical task. We should make persistent efforts, and together with the people of the whole country, redouble our efforts to accelerate the completion of our country's socialist industrialization and the complete completion of socialist transformation.

We wish us early and overfulfillment of the 1956 industrial production plan and basic construction plan.

We wish us early and overfulfillment of the 1956 agricultural and sideline production plan.

We wish us early and overfulfillment of the 1956 commercial plan and transportation industry plan, etc.

We wish us new achievements and victories in the work of science, technology, culture, education, health and political science, etc.

We wish our great first five-year plan to be completed early and overfulfilled.

Let us shout:

Long live the Communist Party of China!

Long live the People's Republic of China!

Long live Chairman Mao!

### 在北京市各界庆祝社会主义改造胜利联欢大会上  北京市市长彭真的讲话

1956-01-16
第2版()
专栏：

    在北京市各界庆祝社会主义改造胜利联欢大会上
    北京市市长彭真的讲话同胞们，同志们：
    现在，我们首都各界举行联欢大会，庆祝我们的农业、手工业和私营工商业的社会主义改造的伟大胜利。
    我们庆祝郊区农业的完全社会主义合作化的胜利。庆祝资本主义工商业全部公私合营的胜利。庆祝手工业全部合作化的胜利。庆祝我们已经挖掉了穷根，打下了依靠大家共同劳动、使国家富强、使大家生活共同富裕的基础，我们的首都已经进入了社会主义社会。
    为了这些伟大的胜利，我们向工人、店员致热烈的祝贺。向完全合作化的农民致热烈的祝贺。向全部合作化的手工业者致热烈的祝贺。向已经全部实行公私合营的工商业者致热烈的祝贺。向所有为社会主义事业而辛勤工作的知识分子们致热烈的祝贺。向全体工作人员致热烈的祝贺。
    我们各种伟大的胜利，都是我们大家在以毛主席为首的中共中央和中央人民政府的直接领导下，积极努力和克服了保守主义才取得的。今天，毛主席和中央其他领导同志们和我们大家一道庆祝我们的社会主义改造的伟大胜利。我们向毛主席致敬。向中央各领导同志们致敬。感谢他们给予我们的正确领导。他们的正确领导，是使我们从胜利走向胜利的保证。
    参加我们的庆祝大会的，还有在京的全国人民代表大会的代表和中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会的委员，我们的一切成就，是和他们的努力分不开的，我们向他们表示衷心的敬意。
    参加我们的庆祝大会的，还有正在北京出席中共中央召开的关于知识分子问题的会议的各省市和自治区的领导同志们，我们向他们表示热烈的欢迎。我们首都的一切社会主义建设和社会主义改造的成就，是在全国各地热烈的帮助和支持下取得的，我们向各地的同志们表示衷心的感谢。
    今天，和我们一块庆祝社会主义改造胜利的，还有解放军的许多高级将领和战士们。中国人民解放军是我们和平建设的保卫者，我们所有的胜利和成就，是在他们的保卫下取得的。我们向他们表示衷心的感谢和敬意。
    我们不应该满足于已取得的成就。我们已得的成就，对于我们的伟大的历史任务来说，只不过是万里长征走完了第一步。我们应该再接再厉，和全国人民一道，为加速完成我国的社会主义工业化和彻底完成社会主义的改造而加倍努力。
    预祝我们提早和超额完成1956年的工业生产计划和基本建设计划。
    预祝我们提早和超额完成1956年的农业和副业的生产计划。
    预祝我们提早和超额完成1956年的商业计划和交通运输业等计划。
    预祝我们在科学、技术、文化、教育、卫生和政法等各方面的工作，获得新的成就和胜利。
    预祝我们的伟大的第一个五年计划提早和超额完成。
    让我们高呼：
    中国共产党万岁！
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    毛主席万岁！
