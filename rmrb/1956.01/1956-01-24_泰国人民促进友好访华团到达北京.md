Title: Thailand People's Promotion of Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-01-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 23rd—The Thailand People's Promotion of Friendship Delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit China, arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 23rd. The delegation is led by Nai Dhep Chotinuchit, leader of the Thai Economists Party and Member of Parliament. Members of the delegation include Members of Parliament Nai Klaeo Norabathi and Nai Babot Khongsamai, as well as some businessmen and journalists.
    When the delegation arrived, those present to welcome them included Luo Longji, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Wu Maosun, Secretary-General; Ji Chao Ding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Wang Yunsheng, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association; and Zhao Puchu, Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China.

### 泰国人民促进友好访华团到达北京

1956-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    泰国人民促进友好访华团到达北京
　　新华社23日讯　应中国人民外交学会的邀请，来中国访问参观的泰国人民促进友好访华团已经在23日下午乘火车到达北京。访华团由泰国经济人党领袖、国会议员乃贴·触的努七率领，团员中有国会议员乃克莱·纳拉巴蒂、乃巴博·空沙迈，以及一些商业家和新闻工作者。
    访华团到达的时候，前往欢迎的有中国人民外交学会副会长罗隆基、秘书长吴茂荪，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎，中国新闻工作者联谊会副会长王芸生，中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初等人。
