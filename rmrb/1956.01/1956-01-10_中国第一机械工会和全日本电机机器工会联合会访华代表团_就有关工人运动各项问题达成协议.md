Title: China First Machine Workers' Union and All Japan Electric Machine Workers' Union Federation Delegation to China Reach Agreement on Various Issues Concerning the Workers' Movement
Author:
Date: 1956-01-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The China First Machine Workers' Union and the delegation from the All Japan Electric Machine Workers' Union Federation visiting China reached an agreement on various issues concerning the workers' movement and held a signing ceremony at 11:50 PM on the 9th at the Ziguang Pavilion in Zhongnanhai, Beijing.

    Kang Yonghe, Chairman of the China First Machine Workers' Union, signed the agreement on behalf of the Chinese side.

    Yayoshi Tsutsui, head of the delegation from the All Japan Electric Machine Workers' Union Federation visiting China, signed the agreement on behalf of the Japanese side.

    The Chinese side participating in the signing ceremony included Liu Ningyi and Zhu Xuefan, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Chen Yu, member of the Executive Committee, Ma Chungu, Director of the International Liaison Department, Ma Peixun, Zhou Xifeng, and Ma Xiaodi, Vice-Chairmen of the China First Machine Workers' Union, Li Yunchuan, Secretary-General, Gao Shoushan, Deputy Secretary-General, and Xie Ping, Li Qian, Zhao Guoyou, and Wang Jiachong, heads of various departments and offices.

    The Japanese side participating in the signing ceremony included Saito Kin'ya, Secretary-General of the delegation from the All Japan Electric Machine Workers' Union Federation visiting China, and members Yujiro Shimamura, Masato Ono, Takeo Yoneharu, Yasue Kambori, Chuichi Takeuchi, Akio Usuya, Tadayoshi Hayashi, Fumio Haruyama, Shiro Hamaguchi, Kaoru Ando, Shiro Masaki, and Japanese individuals currently in Beijing, Togo Kameda and Kenta Kaneko.

### 中国第一机械工会和全日本电机机器工会联合会访华代表团　就有关工人运动各项问题达成协议

1956-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国第一机械工会和全日本电机机器工会联合会访华代表团
　　就有关工人运动各项问题达成协议
    新华社9日讯　中国第一机械工会同全日本电机机器工会联合会访华代表团就有关工人运动的各项问题达成了协议，并且在9日下午11时50分在北京中南海紫光阁举行签字仪式。
    中国方面在协议上签字的是中国第一机械工会主席康永和。
    日本方面在协议上签字的是全日本电机机器工会联合会访华代表团团长筒井弥代志。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，有中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一、朱学范，执行委员会委员陈宇，国际联络部部长马纯古，中国第一机械工会副主席马佩勋、周西风、马小弟，秘书长李云川、副秘书长高首善，各部、室负责人谢平、李潜、赵国有、王家宠。
    日本方面参加签字仪式的，有全日本电机机器工会联合会访华代表团秘书长斋藤欣也，团员岛村由次郎、大野政人、米春武夫、神保安卫、竹内忠一、臼谷秋夫、林忠义、春山文夫、滨口四郎、安藤香、真崎四郎和现在北京的日本人士龟田东伍、金子健太。
