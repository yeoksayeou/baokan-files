Title: Chinese and Italian Commercial Counselors in the UK Exchange Views Again on Trade Issues
Author:
Date: 1956-01-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, London, January 24th: On January 21st, the Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Chargé d'Affaires Office in the UK had a second contact with the Commercial Counselor of the Italian Embassy in the UK, and exchanged views again on how to conduct trade negotiations.

### 中意两国驻英商务参赞  再就贸易问题交换意见

1956-01-25
第4版()
专栏：

    中意两国驻英商务参赞
    再就贸易问题交换意见
    新华社24日讯　伦敦消息：1月21日，中华人民共和国驻英代办处商务参赞同意大利驻英大使馆商务参赞进行了第二次接触，就如何进行贸易谈判问题再度交换了意见。
