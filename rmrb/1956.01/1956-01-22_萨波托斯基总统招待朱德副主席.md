Title: President Zápotocký Hosts Vice Chairman Zhu De
Author:
Date: 1956-01-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 21st — Czechoslovak Republic President Zápotocký held a luncheon at Prague Castle on the 20th to entertain Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China.

The entourage accompanying Vice Chairman Zhu De on his visit to Czechoslovakia, as well as Cao Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, attended the luncheon.

Other leaders of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and the government also attended the luncheon.

The luncheon proceeded in a very sincere and friendly atmosphere.
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 21st — Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and his entourage left Prague on the afternoon of the 20th for a sanatorium in Czechoslovakia for a short rest.

### 萨波托斯基总统招待朱德副主席

1956-01-22
第4版()
专栏：

    萨波托斯基总统招待朱德副主席
    新华社布拉格21日电　捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基20日在布拉格宫举行午宴，招待中华人民共和国副主席朱德。
    出席午宴的有和朱德副主席一起访问捷克斯洛伐克的随行人员以及中国驻捷大使曹瑛。
    捷克斯洛伐克共产党和政府的其他领导人也出席了午宴。
    午宴在非常诚挚和友好的气氛中进行。
    新华社布拉格21日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德和随行人员20日下午离布拉格前往捷克斯洛伐克一休养地作短期休息。
