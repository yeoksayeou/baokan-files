Title: Representatives of 17 Production Units in Chongqing Propose Early Completion of First Quarter Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-01-08
Page: 1

Representatives from 17 production units in Chongqing, including the Li Jichang Group of the Chongqing No. 2 Steel Plant, the Zhang Shaocheng Group of the Chongqing Machine Tool Plant, and the Chen Shulan Group of the Shashi Yarn Factory, put forward a joint proposal before the New Year to complete the state plan for the first month and the first quarter of this year ahead of schedule. Now, the enlarged meeting of the Standing Committee of the Chongqing Federation of Trade Unions has made a resolution to support their proposal, calling on workers, engineering and technical personnel, and administrative staff in Chongqing to respond to this proposal.

In their proposal, the representatives of these 17 units all put forward specific guarantees for completing the plan ahead of schedule. For example, the Zhao Furong Youth Steelmaking Group of the Steelmaking Workshop of the Chongqing No. 1 Steel Plant proposed to complete the plan for the first month two days ahead of schedule and the plan for the first quarter six days ahead of schedule, with a quality rate of 100%.

### 重庆17个生产单位代表  倡议提前完成第一季度计划

1956-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

    重庆17个生产单位代表
    倡议提前完成第一季度计划
    重庆第二钢铁厂李吉昌小组、重庆机床厂张绍成小组、沙市纱厂陈树兰小组等17个生产单位的代表，曾在年前提出了提前完成今年第一个月、第一个季度国家计划的联合倡议。现在，重庆市工会联合会常委扩大会议已经作出了支持他们倡议的决议，号召重庆市工人、工程技术人员和行政管理人员响应这一倡议。
    这17个单位的代表在倡议中，都提出了提前完成计划的具体保证。如重庆第一钢铁厂炼钢车间赵福荣青年炼钢小组提出，要提前两天完成第一个月的计划，提前六天完成第一季度计划，质量要达到100％。
