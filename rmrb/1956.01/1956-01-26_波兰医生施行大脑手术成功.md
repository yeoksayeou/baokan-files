Title: Polish Doctors Successfully Perform Brain Surgery
Author:
Date: 1956-01-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 25th. Professor Kunicki and others at the Krakow Medical Academy in Poland recently successfully performed another rare brain surgery to remove a malignant tumor from the center of the brain.

Up to now, the most authoritative surgeons in various countries have refused to perform this extremely dangerous surgery.

Professor Kunicki and others have already performed this type of surgery for the sixth time. The results of each surgery have been very good. The most recent surgery was performed on a 17-year-old boy named Bader. The patient was anesthetized for 10 hours, of which 4 hours were used for the crucial surgery.

### 波兰医生施行大脑手术成功

1956-01-26
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰医生施行大脑手术成功
    新华社华沙25日电　波兰克拉科夫医学院的库尼茨基教授等人，最近又成功地进行了一次世界上极少有的、从大脑中心割去恶性肿瘤的手术。
    到目前为止，世界各国最有权威的外科医生都拒绝施行这种十分危险的手术。
    库尼茨基教授等已经是第六次施行这种手术了。每次手术的结果都很良好，最近的一次手术是为一个17岁的少年巴德热施行的，病人麻醉了10小时，其中有4小时的时间用来进行重要的手术。
