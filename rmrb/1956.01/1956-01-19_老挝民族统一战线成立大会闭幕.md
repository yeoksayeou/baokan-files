Title: Lao National United Front Inaugural Congress Concludes
Author:
Date: 1956-01-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 17th. According to the Vietnam News Agency: The inaugural congress of the Lao National United Front concluded on January 14th after 9 days of work. At this meeting, the Lao Patriotic Front was established, and a Central Executive Committee of the Patriotic Front was elected, composed of representatives of all nationalities and strata of the people of Laos. Prince Souphanouvong is the Chairman of the committee.

### 老挝民族统一战线成立大会闭幕

1956-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝民族统一战线成立大会闭幕
    据新华社河内17日电　据越南通讯社讯：老挝民族统一战线成立大会经过了9天的工作以后，已经在1月14日闭幕。在这次会议上成立了老挝爱国战线，并且选举了由老挝全国各民族和各阶层的人民的代表组成的爱国战线中央执行委员会，委员会的主席是苏发努冯亲王。
