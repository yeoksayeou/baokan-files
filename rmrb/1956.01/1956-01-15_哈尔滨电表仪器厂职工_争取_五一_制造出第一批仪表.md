Title: Harbin Electrical Meter and Instrument Factory Workers Strive to Manufacture the First Batch of Instruments by "May Day"

Author:
Date: 1956-01-15
Page: 1

Harbin Electrical Meter and Instrument Factory Workers
    Strive to Manufacture the First Batch of Instruments by "May Day"
    (Our dispatch) The workers of the Harbin Electrical Meter and Instrument Factory, which is currently under construction, have decided to strive to manufacture the first batch of instruments ahead of schedule by "May Day" this year.
    The factory was originally scheduled to officially begin production in July of this year. In order to meet the needs of national construction, the factory recently re-arranged its production preparation work and formulated specific measures and plans to address existing problems. According to the newly adopted measures, a batch of switchboard instruments can be produced and some automotive instruments can be trial-manufactured by the time of the "May Day" holiday.

### 哈尔滨电表仪器厂职工  争取“五一”制造出第一批仪表

1956-01-15
第1版()
专栏：

    哈尔滨电表仪器厂职工
    争取“五一”制造出第一批仪表
    本报讯  正在建设中的哈尔滨电表仪器厂的职工，决定争取提前在今年“五一”制造出第一批仪表。
    这个厂原计划在今年7月正式投入生产。为了满足国家建设的需要，这个厂最近重新安排了生产准备工作，并且针对存在的问题制定了具体措施计划。按照新采取的措施，“五一”节的时候可以生产出一批开关板仪表和试制出一些汽车仪表。
