Title: Gansu Province to Universally Establish Rural Clubs This Year
Author:
Date: 1956-01-21
Page: 3

Gansu Province plans to establish 15,000 rural clubs based on agricultural production cooperatives this year, averaging four clubs per township. Currently, the Gansu Provincial Bureau of Culture is transferring staff to assist relevant local departments in Zhangye, Yinchuan, Tianshui, Pingliang and other regions in carrying out this work, striving to complete the construction of most clubs around the Spring Festival.

These clubs will explain political current events to farmers through blackboard newspapers, reading groups, and amateur theater troupes, conduct patriotic and socialist education for farmers, and invigorate rural cultural and entertainment life. Some agricultural production cooperatives with better conditions will also equip their clubs with radios and slide projectors, organizing more people to listen to the radio and watch slides. (Our correspondent in Lanzhou)

### 甘肃今年普遍建立农村俱乐部

1956-01-21
第3版()
专栏：

    甘肃今年普遍建立农村俱乐部
    甘肃省今年计划在农村以农业生产合作社为基础，建立15，000个农村俱乐部，平均每个乡有4个俱乐部。现在，甘肃省文化局正在抽调工作人员，准备到张掖、银川、天水、平凉等专区协助当地有关部门进行这一工作，争取大部分俱乐部在春节前后建成。
    这些俱乐部将通过出黑板报、组织读报组和业余剧团，向农民讲解政治时事，对农民进行爱国主义和社会主义的教育，活跃农村的文化娱乐生活。一些条件好的农业生产合作社，建立的俱乐部里还将设有收音机、幻灯机，组织更多的人收听广播和看幻灯片。　（本报驻兰州记者）
