Title: Workers of State-Owned Beijing First and Second Cotton Textile Factories Propose a National Labor Competition Among Textile Factory Workers
Author:
Date: 1956-01-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th. To ensure the early completion of the First Five-Year Plan, workers of the state-owned Beijing First Cotton Textile Factory and the Second Cotton Textile Factory jointly proposed to launch an inter-factory labor competition among textile factories nationwide to improve product quality, practice comprehensive conservation, and increase labor productivity.

　　At a joint worker representative meeting held on December 29th of last year, the two factories adopted a proposal to launch this labor competition. In the proposal, the two factories made the following guarantees:

　　1. Fulfill the state's production, technology, and financial plans comprehensively and evenly on a monthly basis;

　　2. Vigorously improve product quality. The first-grade, first-class yarn rate of Factory One will exceed the national target by 10%; Factory Two will exceed it by 7.67%. The superior-grade cloth rate of Factory One will exceed the national target by 5%; Factory Two will exceed it by 7.95%;

　　3. Practice comprehensive conservation and strive to reduce costs;

　　4. Increase labor productivity;

　　5. Complete the plan to increase new varieties on schedule;

　　6. Aiming at weak links, diligently study the advanced experiences of the Soviet Union and fraternal factories, and continuously improve production technology and enterprise management.

### 国营北京第一和第二棉纺织厂职工  倡议全国纺织厂职工开展劳动竞赛

1956-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    国营北京第一和第二棉纺织厂职工
　　倡议全国纺织厂职工开展劳动竞赛
    新华社7日讯　国营北京第一棉纺织厂和第二棉纺织厂为保证提前完成第一个五年计划，联合倡议全国纺织厂开展提高产品质量、厉行全面节约、提高劳动生产率的厂际劳动竞赛。
    这两个厂在去年12月29日联合召开的职工代表会议上，通过了开展这个劳动竞赛的倡议书。这两个厂在倡议书中提出了以下保证：
    一、按月全面均衡地完成国家的生产技术财务计划；
    二、大力提高产品质量。棉纱一等一级品率，一厂超过国家指标10％；二厂超过7.67％。棉布上等品率，一厂超过国家指标5％；二厂超过7.95％；
    三、厉行全面节约，努力降低成本；
    四、提高劳动生产率；
    五、按期完成增加新品种计划；
    六、针对薄弱环节，努力学习苏联和兄弟厂的先进经验，不断提高生产技术和企业管理水平。
