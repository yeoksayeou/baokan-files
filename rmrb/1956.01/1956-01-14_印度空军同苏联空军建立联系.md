Title: Indian Air Force Establishes Contact with Soviet Air Force
Author:
Date: 1956-01-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 13th: According to the Press Trust of India, the Indian government announced that the Indian Air Force began establishing contact with the Soviet Air Force in 1955. Indian Air Force Chief of Staff Mukerjee and other senior Indian Air Force officers visited Moscow at the invitation of the Soviet government to attend the annual air show. Later, during the visit of Soviet leaders to India, Indian Air Force personnel participated in the work of the Soviet aircraft crew carrying the Soviet leaders, thus making contact again.

### 印度空军同苏联空军建立联系

1956-01-14
第4版()
专栏：

    印度空军同苏联空军建立联系
    据新华社新德里13日讯  印度报业托辣斯消息，据印度官方宣布，印度空军在1955年同苏联空军开始建立了联系。印度空军参谋长慕克吉和其他印度空军高级军官应苏联政府的邀请，到莫斯科去参观了一年一度的空军表演。以后，在苏联领袖们访问印度期间，印度空军人员参加了载送苏联领袖的苏联飞机机上人员的工作，因而又一次进行了接触。
