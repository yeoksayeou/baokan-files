Title: Indian National Development Council Meets
Author:
Date: 1956-01-09
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 7th: The Standing Committee of the Indian National Development Council began a two-day meeting in New Delhi on January 6th.

　　According to Indian newspapers on the 7th, Nehru emphasized the importance of heavy industry as the basic foundation of national economic development at the meeting. He said that the target of 48 billion rupees for the public sector's expenditure in the Second Five-Year Plan is the maximum limit. He mentioned the amount of foreign aid that could be obtained for the implementation of India's Second Five-Year Plan (1956-1961) (listed as 8 billion rupees in the draft memorandum submitted to the meeting). He said that despite the foreign aid, there was still a shortage of 4 billion rupees.

　　The "Hindustan Times" reported on the 7th that the meeting discussed the status of the private and public sectors in India's Second Five-Year Plan. The Planning Commission's memorandum stipulates that the public sector is 48 billion rupees and the private sector is 23 billion rupees.

### 印度国家开发委员会开会

1956-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度国家开发委员会开会
    据新华社新德里7日电　印度国家开发委员会常务委员会1月6日在新德里开始举行为时两天的会议。
    据印度报纸7日报道，尼赫鲁在会上强调指出了构成国家经济发展基本基础的重工业的重要性。他说，第二个五年计划中公营部分经费的目标480亿卢比是最高限度。他提到为执行印度第二个五年计划（1956—1961）能够得到的外援数目（提交会议的备忘录草案中列为80亿卢比）。他说，尽管有外援，但仍短少40亿卢比。
    “印度斯坦时报”7日报道，会议讨论了印度第二个五年计划中私营部分和公营部分的地位。计划委员会的备忘录中规定公营部分为480亿卢比，私营部分为230亿卢比。
