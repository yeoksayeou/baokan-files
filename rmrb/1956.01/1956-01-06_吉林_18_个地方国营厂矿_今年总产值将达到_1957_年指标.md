Title: 18 Local State-Owned Factories and Mines in Jilin to Reach 1957 Output Target This Year

Author: 
Date: 1956-01-06
Page: 1

Jilin 18 Local State-Owned Factories and Mines
    This Year's Total Output Value Will Reach 1957 Targets
    In order to complete the tasks stipulated in the First Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule, 18 local state-owned factories and mines in Jilin Province have decided to strive to make this year's total production value reach the planned targets for 1957. This plan was determined by the Jilin Provincial Department of Industry at a recent meeting of factory directors and mine managers of enterprises directly under the province, after full discussion and research.
    According to the provisions of the First Five-Year Plan, the total industrial output value of enterprises directly under the Jilin Provincial Department of Industry in 1957 should increase by 229.5% compared to 1952. In order to meet this requirement, the total production value of these enterprises this year will increase by 29.9% compared to the original plan. At present, various factories and mines have made specific arrangements to achieve this plan, and the workers of various factories and mines have begun production according to the new plan. (Jilin Daily Editorial Department)

### 吉林18个地方国营厂矿  今年总产值将达到1957年指标

1956-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    吉林18个地方国营厂矿
    今年总产值将达到1957年指标
    为了提前完成第一个五年计划规定的任务，吉林省18个地方国营厂矿决定争取使今年的生产总值达到1957年的计划指标。这个计划是吉林省工业厅最近召开的省直属企业厂矿长会议，经过充分讨论和研究后确定的。
    按照第一个五年计划的规定，吉林省工业厅直属企业1957年的工业生产总值要比1952年提高229.5％。为了达到这个要求，今年这些企业的生产总值将比原定计划提高29.9％。现在，各厂矿已经为实现这个计划作了具体安排，各厂矿的职工并已开始根据新的计划进行生产。（吉林日报编辑部）
