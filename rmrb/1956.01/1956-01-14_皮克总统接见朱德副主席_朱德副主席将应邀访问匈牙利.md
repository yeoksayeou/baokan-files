Title: President Pieck Receives Vice-Chairman Zhu De; Vice-Chairman Zhu De to Visit Hungary at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1956-01-14
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 13th: Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, received Zhu De, head of the delegation of the People's Republic of China and Vice-Chairman of the People's Republic of China, at 4:30 p.m. on January 12.
    Also received were delegation members Nie Rongzhen, Liu Lantao, Zeng Yongquan, and delegation secretary-general Shi Zhe.
    Present at the reception were Otto Nuschke, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic; Wünsche, Director of the President's Private Office and State Secretary; Thum, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.
    During the reception, President Pieck thanked Vice-Chairman Zhu De for coming to Germany to congratulate him on his birthday and asked Vice-Chairman Zhu De to convey his respect and greetings to Chairman Mao Zedong.
    Vice-Chairman Zhu De and others bid farewell to President Pieck and expressed their gratitude for the warm reception from the government and people of the German Democratic Republic.
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 13th: Zhu De, Vice-Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has accepted the invitation of the government of the Hungarian People's Republic and decided to visit Hungary after his tour and visit to the German Democratic Republic.
    Accompanying Vice-Chairman Zhu De on his visit to Hungary will also be Marshal Nie Rongzhen, Vice-Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China, and Liu Lantao, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

### 皮克总统接见朱德副主席  朱德副主席将应邀访问匈牙利

1956-01-14
第4版()
专栏：

    皮克总统接见朱德副主席
    朱德副主席将应邀访问匈牙利
    新华社柏林13日电  德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克在1月12日下午4时30分接见中华人民共和国代表团团长、中华人民共和国副主席朱德。
    同时受接见的还有代表团团员聂荣臻，刘澜涛，曾涌泉和代表团秘书长师哲。
    接见时在座的有德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部部长博尔茨，总统私人办公厅主任、国务秘书温舍，外交部礼宾司司长图恩等。
    在接见时，皮克总统对朱德副主席来德祝贺他的生日表示感谢，并且请朱德副主席向毛泽东主席转致他本人的敬意和问候。
    朱德副主席等向皮克总统告别，并且对德意志民主共和国政府和人民的热情接待表示感谢。
    新华社柏林13日电  中华人民共和国副主席朱德接受匈牙利人民共和国政府的邀请，决定在德意志民主共和国参观、访问以后赴匈牙利访问。
    同朱德副主席一道赴匈牙利访问的还有中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席聂荣臻元帅和全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员刘澜涛。
