Title: Chinese Red Cross Donates Funds to Aid Lebanese Disaster Victims
Author:
Date: 1956-01-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Chinese Red Cross, on behalf of the Chinese people, sent a telegram to the Lebanese Red Cross on the 1st, expressing condolences to the Lebanese people who suffered from the floods; at the same time, it donated RMB 20,000 yuan to aid them. The Lebanese Red Cross has already sent a telegram to the Chinese Red Cross on the 2nd to express its gratitude.

### 中国红十字会捐款救济黎巴嫩灾民

1956-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国红十字会捐款救济黎巴嫩灾民
    据新华社讯　中国红十字会代表中国人民，在1日致电黎巴嫩红十字会，慰问黎巴嫩遭受水灾的人民；同时捐赠人民币两万元救济他们。黎巴嫩红十字会已经在2日打电报向中国红十字会致谢。
