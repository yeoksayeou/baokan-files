Title: Afghanistan's First Ambassador to China, Samad, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-01-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th – Abdul Samad, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Afghanistan to our country, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 19th.

Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was at the station to greet him.

Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries’ embassies in China also went to welcome him.

### 阿富汗首任驻华大使  萨马德到达北京

1956-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    阿富汗首任驻华大使
    萨马德到达北京
    新华社19日讯　阿富汗王国驻我国首任特命全权大使阿卜杜尔·萨马德于19日下午抵达北京。
    前往车站迎接的有我外交部礼宾司司长柯华。
    各国驻华使节和外交官员也前往迎接。
