Title: CPPCC National Committee Standing Committee Holds Meeting to Discuss the State Council's Decision on Implementing Fixed Interest Rates for Joint State-Private Enterprises
Author: 
Date: 1956-01-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 28th: The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held its 14th meeting (enlarged) this afternoon to discuss the "State Council's Decision on Implementing Fixed Interest Rates for Joint State-Private Enterprises" (draft) and the "State Council's Regulations on Several Major Issues Concerning Property Inventory and Valuation When Private Enterprises Implement Joint State-Private Ownership" (draft).

More than 200 people attended the meeting, including members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the CPPCC, members and representatives of the industrial and commercial sectors attending and present at the Second Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC, executive members of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce in Beijing, and members of the China Democratic National Construction Association in Beijing.

Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC, presided over the meeting. Xu Dixing and Sun Qimeng, Deputy Directors of the Eighth Office of the State Council, explained the above two documents at the meeting, followed by a discussion.

Those who spoke at the meeting included Liu Jingji, Chen Qiyou, Sheng Pihua, Miao Hainan, Ye Yutian, Le Songsheng, Guo Dihuo, Jin Runxiang, Hou Debang, Chen Tiaofu, Huang Changshui, Hu Zi'ang, Lu Xiaobo, Li Guowei, Zhang Jingli, Abulizi Musabayev, Huang Shirang, Li Zhuchen, Xu Shanxiang, Chen Yuntian, and family members of people in industry and commerce, Wang Zhengxuan and Zhao Rong, totaling 22 people. Chen Jiageng, Li Weihan, and Chen Shutong also spoke.

The meeting unanimously expressed support for the two documents and suggested that the State Council publish them as soon as possible to further promote socialist transformation.

### 政协全国委员会常务委员会举行会议  讨论国务院对公私合营企业推行定息办法的决定

1956-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会常务委员会举行会议
    讨论国务院对公私合营企业推行定息办法的决定
    新华社28日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会在今日下午举行第十四次会议（扩大），讨论了“国务院关于对公私合营企业推行定息办法的决定”（草案）和“国务院关于私营企业实行公私合营的时候对财产清理估价几项主要问题的规定”（草案）。
    参加会议的有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会委员、出席和列席中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第二次全体会议的工商界委员和代表、中华全国工商业联合会在京的执行委员、中国民主建国会在京委员等共二百多人。
    会议由中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会副主席陈叔通主持。国务院第八办公室副主任许涤新、孙起孟在会上分别对上述两个文件作了说明，接着进行讨论。
    在会上发言的有刘靖基、陈其尤、盛丕华、苗海南、叶雨田、乐松生、郭棣活、金润庠、侯德榜、陈调甫、黄长水、胡子昂、陆小波、李国伟、张敬礼、阿不力孜·木沙巴也夫、黄师让、李烛尘、徐善祥、陈芸田和工商界人士的家属王征萱、赵蓉等22人。陈嘉庚、李维汉、陈叔通也讲了话。
    会议一致表示拥护这两个文件，并且建议国务院早日公布，以便进一步进行社会主义改造工作。
