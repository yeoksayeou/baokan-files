Title: China's Legation in Switzerland Upgraded to Embassy
Author:
Date: 1956-01-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The governments of the People's Republic of China and the Swiss Federal government, in order to strengthen the already existing friendly relations between the two countries, have reached an agreement to upgrade the Legation of the People's Republic of China in Switzerland to an embassy.

### 我国驻瑞士公使馆升格为大使馆

1956-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我国驻瑞士公使馆升格为大使馆
    新华社3日讯　中华人民共和国政府和瑞士联邦政府为了增强两国间业已存在的友好关系，已达成协议，将中华人民共和国驻瑞士公使馆升格为大使馆。
