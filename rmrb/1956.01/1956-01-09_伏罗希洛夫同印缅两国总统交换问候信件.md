Title: Voroshilov Exchanges Greetings with Presidents of India and Burma

Author:

Date: 1956-01-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 8th: TASS reported: Moscow newspapers prominently published on the front page on January 7 letters exchanged between Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and Prasad, President of India, and Ba U, President of the Union of Burma. These letters were exchanged during the visit of Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and Khrushchev, Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, to India and Burma in November and December 1955.

In his letters to Prasad and Ba U, Voroshilov expressed the Soviet people's sincere greetings and best wishes to the great people of India and Burma, and wished for further strengthening of the friendly relations between the Soviet Union, India, and Burma, to benefit the people of the three countries and to contribute to world peace.

In his reply, Prasad said, "I am very concerned about the development of friendly relations between India and the Soviet Union. I have no doubt that the growing friendship and cooperation between our two countries will promote the cause of universal peace."

In his reply, Ba U also expressed his friendly wishes to the Soviet people.

### 伏罗希洛夫同印缅两国总统交换问候信件

1956-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　伏罗希洛夫同印缅两国总统交换问候信件
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社报道：莫斯科的报纸都在1月7日以第一页显著地位刊载了苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫同印度总统普拉沙德和缅甸联邦总统巴宇来往的信件。这些信件都是通过苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫1955年11月和12月间访问印度和缅甸时交换的。
    伏罗希洛夫在给普拉沙德和巴宇的信中，表达了苏联人民对伟大的印度人民和缅甸人民的衷心的问候和诚意的祝愿，并且祝苏联同印度和缅甸之间的友好关系得到进一步的加强，以造福于苏印缅三国人民和有利于世界和平。
    普拉沙德在回信中说，我非常关切地注视着印度同苏联的友好关系的发展。我毫不怀疑，我们两国之间不断发展的友谊和合作将促进普遍和平事业。
    巴宇在回信中也表示了对苏联人民的友好的祝愿。
