Title: China Islamic Association Sends Congratulations on Sudan's Independence
Author:
Date: 1956-01-09
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The China Islamic Association sent a telegram to Sudanese Prime Minister Azhari, congratulating the birth of the independent Republic of Sudan.

　　The telegram stated: We, all Muslims, like all the Chinese people, fully support our government's declaration of recognizing Sudan as an independent republic. We hope that the friendly relations between our two countries, established during the Asian-African Conference, will develop day by day, and that the diplomatic relations between our two countries will be established as soon as possible.

### 我伊斯兰教协会电贺苏丹独立

1956-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　我伊斯兰教协会电贺苏丹独立
    据新华社8日讯　中国伊斯兰教协会致电苏丹共和国政府阿扎里总理，祝贺独立的苏丹共和国的诞生。
    电文说：我们全体穆斯林同全中国人民一样，完全拥护我国政府宣布承认苏丹为独立的共和国的主张，我们希望我们两国在亚非会议期间建立起来的友好关系日益发展，希望我们两国的外交关系早日建立。
