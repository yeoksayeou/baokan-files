Title: Sports Commission Announces 1955 National Records for Various Sports
Author:
Date: 1956-01-12
Page: 3

The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission announced on the 11th the national records for Chinese men's and women's swimming, track and field, cycling, speed skating, and men's weightlifting in 1955. Many of the national records in these events have reached international levels.

The national record for the men's 100-meter breaststroke exceeded the first-place result of the Second International Youth Sports Games at the Fifth World Youth Festival. The national records for the men's 100-meter backstroke, 100-meter freestyle, 200-meter breaststroke, and the total scores for the lightest, featherweight, lightweight, and light heavyweight weightlifting categories exceeded or were equivalent to the second, third, and fourth-place results of the Second International Youth Sports Games, respectively. The national records for women's discus and high jump exceeded the fourth-place results of the 15th Olympic Games.

Of the 102 national records announced by the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, 78 were newly created in 1955, 18 were created in other years after liberation, and only four track and field and two weightlifting individual event national records were created before liberation.

Among the national record holders are workers, People's Liberation Army soldiers, and student athletes, some of whom were largely unknown a year ago. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 体育运动委员会公布  1955年各项运动全国最高纪录

1956-01-12
第3版()
专栏：

    体育运动委员会公布
    1955年各项运动全国最高纪录
    中华人民共和国体育运动委员会11日公布了1955年中国男子和女子的游泳、田径、自行车、速度滑冰和男子举重运动的全国最高纪录。其中有许多项目的全国最高纪录都达到了国际水平。
    男子一百公尺蛙泳的全国最高纪录，超过了第五届世界青年联欢节第二届国际青年运动会第一名的成绩。男子一百公尺仰泳、一百公尺自由泳、二百公尺蛙泳和最轻量级、次轻量级、轻量级、轻重量级举重总成绩的全国最高纪录，分别超过了或相当于第二届国际青年运动会第二、三、四名的成绩。女子铁饼和跳高的全国最高纪录，都超过了第十五届奥林匹克运动会第四名的成绩。
    在体育运动委员会公布的102项全国最高纪录中，有78项是在1955年新创造的，有18项是在解放以后的其他年份创造的，只有四个田径和两个举重单项成绩的全国最高纪录是在解放以前创造的。
    在全国最高纪录保持者中，有职工、中国人民解放军和学生运动员等，其中有些人在一年以前还是人们很不熟悉的。    （据新华社讯）
