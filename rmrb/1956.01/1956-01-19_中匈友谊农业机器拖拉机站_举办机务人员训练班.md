Title: Sino-Hungarian Friendship Agricultural Machinery Tractor Station Holds Training Class for Mechanical Personnel
Author:
Date: 1956-01-19
Page: 2

    Sino-Hungarian Friendship Agricultural Machinery Tractor Station
    Holds Training Class for Mechanical Personnel
    In Ziyang County, Shandong Province, where the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Agricultural Machinery Tractor Station is located, a training class for agricultural machinery tractor station mechanical personnel has been established. This training class officially started on the 12th, with Hungarian agricultural machinery experts Gyula Panhácz, Áron Búsia, and László Pittner, among others, serving as teaching and practical guidance instructors. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 中匈友谊农业机器拖拉机站  举办机务人员训练班

1956-01-19
第2版()
专栏：

    中匈友谊农业机器拖拉机站
    举办机务人员训练班
    山东省在中匈友谊农业机器拖拉机站所在地的滋阳县，举办一个农业机器拖拉机站机务人员训练班。这个训练班在12日正式开课，由匈牙利农业机器专家纠拉·潘哈茨、阿诺斯·布西亚、拉斯洛·皮特等人担任教学指导实习的工作。（据新华社讯）
