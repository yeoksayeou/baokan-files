Title: China's Government Appoints Nie Rongzhen to Attend Warsaw Pact Political Consultative Committee Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1956-01-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 23rd: The Government of the People's Republic of China has decided to appoint Nie Rongzhen, Marshal of the People's Republic of China and Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China, as its representative, accompanied by advisors Cao Ying, Shi Zhe, Wang Yutian, Liao Gailong, and others, to attend the Political Consultative Committee meeting of the Warsaw Pact member states in Prague on January 27, 1956, as an observer.

### 我国政府委派聂荣臻出席华沙条约缔约国政治协商委员会会议

1956-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我国政府委派聂荣臻出席华沙条约缔约国政治协商委员会会议
    新华社23日讯　中华人民共和国政府决定委派中华人民共和国元帅、中华人民共和国国防委员会副主席聂荣臻为代表，偕顾问曹瑛、师哲、王雨田、廖盖隆等，以观察员身份出席华沙条约缔约国1956年1月27日在布拉格召开的政治协商委员会会议。
