Xinhua News Agency

　　Chairman Mao Sends a Congratulatory Telegram to Liberian President Tubman on His Inauguration
Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Chairman Mao Zedong's congratulatory telegram to Liberian President Tubman of the Republic of Liberia.

To His Excellency President Tubman of the Republic of Liberia, Monrovia, Liberia:

　　On the occasion of Your Excellency's assumption of the Presidency of the Republic of Liberia, I extend my congratulations and wish that the friendly relations established between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Liberia during the Asian-African Conference will continue to develop.

                                Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong
                                             January 3, 1956

### 毛主席电贺利比里亚总统杜伯曼就职

1956-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席电贺利比里亚总统杜伯曼就职
    新华社讯　毛泽东主席给利比里亚共和国总统杜伯曼的贺电。利比里亚蒙罗维亚利比里亚共和国总统杜伯曼阁下：
    值阁下就任利比里亚共和国总统之际，谨致祝贺，并且祝中华人民共和国同利比里亚共和国在亚非会议期间建立起来的友好关系日益发展。
                               中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                                             1956年1月3日
