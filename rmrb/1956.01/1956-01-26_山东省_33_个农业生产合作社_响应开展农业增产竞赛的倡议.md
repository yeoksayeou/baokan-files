Title: 33 Agricultural Production Cooperatives in Shandong Province Respond to the Proposal to Launch an Agricultural Production Increase Competition
Author:
Date: 1956-01-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 24th. Thirty-three agricultural production cooperatives in Shandong Province have responded to the proposal jointly put forward by Li Shunda and others to launch an agricultural production increase competition, and have put forward specific conditions for the competition.

    The Aiguo Collective Farm in Ju County, led by the nationally famous agricultural labor model Lü Hongbin, proposed the following competition conditions: the total output of the farm's 18,000 mu of farmland in 1956 will be increased from the average yield of 402 jin per mu last year to 753 jin. The conditions of the Yousheng Advanced Agricultural Production Cooperative in Gaomi County are: this year, the average yield of seed cotton per mu is 500 jin, and 100 mu of high-yield cotton fields will strive to produce 1,000 jin of seed cotton per mu.
    These collective farms and agricultural production cooperatives also jointly proposed an initiative to agricultural production cooperatives, mutual aid teams and farmers in Shandong Province to launch an agricultural production increase competition to strive to over fulfill the Five-Year Plan in four years, based on their respective competition conditions.
    At present, agricultural production cooperatives in various parts of Shandong Province are drafting conditions and preparing to participate in the competition.

### 山东省33个农业生产合作社  响应开展农业增产竞赛的倡议

1956-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    山东省33个农业生产合作社
    响应开展农业增产竞赛的倡议
    据新华社济南24日电　山东省有33个农业生产合作社响应李顺达等人联名提出的开展农业增产竞赛的倡议，并且提出了竞赛的具体条件。
    全国著名的农业劳动模范吕鸿宾领导的莒县爱国集体农庄提出的竞赛条件是：1956年全农庄18，000亩农田的产量，由去年每亩平均产量402斤，提高到753斤。高密县优胜高级农业生产合作社的条件是：今年平均每亩棉花产籽棉500斤，其中100亩丰产棉田争取每亩产籽棉1，000斤。
    这些集体农庄和农业生产合作社还以各自的竞赛条件，联名向山东省的农业生产合作社、互助组和农民，提出了争取四年超额完成五年计划而开展农业增产竞赛的倡议。
    目前，山东省各地农业生产合作社都正在拟订条件，准备参加竞赛。
