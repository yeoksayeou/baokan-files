Title: Anshan Establishes Earthwork Mechanization Engineering Company
Author: Feng Shiming
Date: 1956-01-29
Page: 2

    Anshan Establishes Earthwork Mechanization Engineering Company
    The Anshan Iron and Steel Construction Company established China's first Earthwork Mechanization Engineering Company on January 21. This fully mechanized construction team possesses hundreds of giant, high-efficiency earthmoving machines. These include universal excavators with capacities from 0.25 cubic meters to 3 cubic meters, bucket excavators, various bulldozing, scraping, loosening, and road-rolling machines, and heavy-duty dump trucks.
    The establishment of the Earthwork Mechanization Engineering Company marks a new development in Anshan's construction mechanization along the path of specialization. According to the Anshan Iron and Steel Construction Company's planning requirements, the level of earthwork mechanization will reach 69% this year, an increase of 31% over last year. The Earthwork Mechanization Engineering Company is now working intensively on construction preparations. (Feng Shiming)

### 鞍山成立土方机械化工程公司
冯世明
1956-01-29
第2版()
专栏：

    鞍山成立土方机械化工程公司
    鞍山钢铁建设公司在1月21日成立了我国第一个土方机械化工程公司。这个全部机械化装备的建筑队伍，拥有数百台巨型的高效率的土方机械。其中有容量0.25立方公尺到3立方公尺的万能挖掘机，多斗挖土机，各种推土、铲运、松土、压道等机械和重型自卸卡车等。
    土方机械化工程公司的成立，是鞍山施工机械化在专业化道路上新的发展。根据鞍山钢铁建设公司规划要求，今年土方机械化水平将要达到69％，比去年提高31％。现在土方机械化工程公司正紧张进行施工准备工作。　（冯世明）
