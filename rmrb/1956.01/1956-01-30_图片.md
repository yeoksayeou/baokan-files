Title: The State builds granular fertilizer plants in various cities in Zhejiang Province

Author: 
Date: 1956-01-30
Page: 1

The state has built new granular fertilizer plants in cities such as Hangzhou, Wenzhou, and Jiaxing in Zhejiang Province to meet the needs of cooperative agricultural production. This is a scene of workers at the newly built Wenzhou Granular Fertilizer Plant sun-drying granular fertilizer.
