Title: Complete Socialist Cooperativization Achieved in Wuhan Suburbs
Author:
Date: 1956-01-22
Page: 1

Wuhan, January 21st (From our own correspondent) The suburbs of Wuhan have achieved complete socialist agricultural cooperativization. The original 1,200+ agricultural production cooperatives in the suburbs have been transformed into 600+ advanced agricultural production cooperatives of a completely socialist nature, with the number of peasant households joining the cooperatives reaching 98.8% of the total number of peasant households in the suburbs.

After the suburbs of Wuhan achieved semi-socialist agricultural cooperativization, many peasants began to prepare to transition to advanced cooperatives. The work of transitioning to advanced cooperatives in the entire suburban area was successfully completed in one day and one night on the 19th.

### 武汉市郊区实现全社会主义合作化

1956-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

    武汉市郊区实现全社会主义合作化
    本报武汉21日电　武汉市郊区已经实现了完全社会主义的农业合作化。全郊区原有的1，200多个农业生产合作社已经转为600多个完全社会主义性质的高级农业生产合作社，入社农户达到郊区农户总数的98.8％。
    武汉市郊区实现半社会主义的农业合作化以后，许多农民就开始准备转高级社。全郊区转高级社的工作，在19日的一天一夜中就顺利完成。
