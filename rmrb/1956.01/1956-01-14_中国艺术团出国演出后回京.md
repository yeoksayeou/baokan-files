Title: Chinese Art Troupe Returns to Beijing After Overseas Performances
Author:
Date: 1956-01-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency report: The Chinese Art Troupe returned to Beijing on the evening of the 13th.

The Chinese Art Troupe departed the country last May. For more than eight months, they performed Peking Opera and Chinese music and dance in 30 cities in nine countries: France, Belgium, the Netherlands, the United Kingdom, Italy, Switzerland, Yugoslavia, Czechoslovakia, and Hungary.

### 中国艺术团出国演出后回京

1956-01-14
第4版()
专栏：

    中国艺术团出国演出后回京
    据新华社讯  中国艺术团已经在13日晚上回到北京。
    中国艺术团是去年5月出国的。八个多月以来，他们在法国、比利时、荷兰、英国、意大利、瑞士、南斯拉夫、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利九个国家的30个城市中演出了京剧和中国音乐舞蹈。
