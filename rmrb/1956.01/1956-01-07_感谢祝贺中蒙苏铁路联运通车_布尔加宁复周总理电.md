Title: Bulganin Replies to Premier Zhou, Thanking Him for Congratulations on the Opening of the China-Mongolia-Soviet Railway Through Transport
Author:
Date: 1956-01-07
Page: 1

In response to congratulations on the opening of the China-Mongolia-Soviet railway through transport,
Bulganin's reply to Premier Zhou's telegram

Beijing
To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:

Premier Comrade, I would like to express my heartfelt gratitude to you for your warm congratulations on the completion of the Jining-Ulaanbaatar railway and the commencement of through transport on this line between the People's Republic of China, the Mongolian People's Republic, and the Soviet Union.

The opening of the Moscow-Ulaanbaatar-Beijing railway transport will further expand and strengthen the economic and cultural ties between the Soviet Union, China, and Mongolia, and will promote the overall development of the regions along the railway.

Please convey my heartfelt greetings to the Chinese workers, engineers, and technicians who demonstrated labor heroism in the road construction process.

I wish the fraternal Chinese people further achievements in the cause of building socialism.

May the eternal friendship and fraternal cooperation between the peoples of the People's Republic of China, the Soviet Union, and the Mongolian People's Republic be increasingly consolidated and prosper.

N. Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, January 5, 1956, Moscow Kremlin

### 感谢祝贺中蒙苏铁路联运通车　布尔加宁复周总理电

1956-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

    感谢祝贺中蒙苏铁路联运通车
　　布尔加宁复周总理电
　　北京
　　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志：
    当集宁—乌兰巴托铁路修建竣工和中华人民共和国、蒙古人民共和国和苏联之间在该线上开始联运的时候，承致热烈的祝贺，总理同志，我谨向您表示衷心的谢意。
    莫斯科—乌兰巴托—北京铁路运输的开通，将促使苏、中、蒙三国间的经济和文化联系进一步扩大和加强，并将促进该铁路沿线各地区的全面发展。
    请向在筑路过程中表现了劳动的英雄主义的中国工人、工程师和技师转达我衷心的敬礼。
    谨祝兄弟般的中国人民在建设社会主义的事业中获得进一步的成就。
    愿中华人民共和国、苏联和蒙古人民共和国三国人民的永恒友谊和兄弟般的合作日益巩固和繁荣。
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟
        部长会议主席尼·布尔加宁1956年1月5日于莫斯科克里姆林宫
