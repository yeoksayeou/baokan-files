Title: Preparatory Committee for China's Participation in the 16th Olympic Games Established
Author:
Date: 1956-01-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 28th. The Chinese Olympic Committee established a Preparatory Committee in Beijing today for participation in the 16th Olympic Games. The Preparatory Committee held its first meeting today to discuss issues related to participating in the 16th Olympic Games.

The Preparatory Committee is chaired by Cai Shufan; with Li Dequan, Xiao Hua, Huang Qixiang, Rong Gaotang, Dong Shouyi, and Ma John as vice-chairmen.

The members of the Preparatory Committee are Wei Que, Liu Zizai, Liu Zhiming, Chu Tunan, Zhang Ze, Li Zaiwen, Wu Han, Tian Demin, Huang Zhong, Li Menghua, Wu Xueqian, Hu Xiaofeng, Li Shubin, Wang Ke, Zhang Zhihuai, and Zhang Lianhua. Huang Zhong, a committee member, also serves as the Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee.

### 我国参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会筹委会成立

1956-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我国参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会筹委会成立
    新华社28日讯　中国奥林匹克委员会今天在北京成立了参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会筹备委员会。筹备委员会今天召开第一次会议，讨论了有关参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会的问题。
    筹备委员会由蔡树藩任主任；由李德全、萧华、黄琪翔、荣高棠、董守义、马约翰任副主任。
    筹备委员会的委员有韦悫、刘子载、刘芝明、楚图南、章泽、栗再温、吴晗、田德民、黄中、李梦华、吴学谦、胡晓风、栗树彬、王克、张之槐和张联华。筹备委员会的秘书长由委员黄中兼任。
