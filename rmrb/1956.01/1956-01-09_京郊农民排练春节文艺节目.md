Title: Farmers in the Suburbs of Beijing Rehearse Spring Festival Performances
Author:
Date: 1956-01-09
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency

　　More than 300 amateur farmer theater troupes, singing groups, and other groups in the suburbs of Beijing are starting to rehearse literary and artistic programs for the Spring Festival performances. Many troupes began rehearsing Pingju operas and plays such as "Sharpen Your Eyes," "Female Commune Member," and "Mid-Autumn Night" as early as half a month ago.

　　Haidian District has also opened farmer music classes and farmer amateur theater troupe training classes.

### 京郊农民排练春节文艺节目

1956-01-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　京郊农民排练春节文艺节目
    北京郊区三百多个农民业余剧团、歌咏队等正陆续开始排练春节演出的文艺节目。许多剧团早在半个月前就开始排练“擦亮眼睛”、“女社员”、“中秋之夜”等评剧和话剧。
    海淀区还开办了农民音乐学习班和农民业余剧团讲习班。
                  （新华社）
