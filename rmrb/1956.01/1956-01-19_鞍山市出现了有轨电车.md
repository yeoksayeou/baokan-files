Title: Anshan City Has Streetcars
Author:
Date: 1956-01-19
Page: 2

    Anshan City Has Streetcars
    On the 15th, the first streetcars in Anshan City departed from Taiping Village, a residential area for employees of Anshan Iron and Steel Company. The newly built streetcar line in Anshan City is 13 kilometers long, running directly from Taiping Village to the factory area of Anshan Iron and Steel Company. (According to Xinhua News Agency report)

### 鞍山市出现了有轨电车

1956-01-19
第2版()
专栏：

    鞍山市出现了有轨电车
    15日，从鞍山钢铁公司职工住宅区太平村开出了鞍山市的第一批有轨电车。鞍山市新建成的这条有轨电车线路全长13公里，从太平村直通鞍山钢铁公司工厂区。（据新华社讯）
