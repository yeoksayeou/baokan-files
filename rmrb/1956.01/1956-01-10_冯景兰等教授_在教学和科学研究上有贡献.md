Professors Feng Jinglan and Others
Contribute to Teaching and Scientific Research

　　The Beijing Institute of Geology recently held an exhibition of teaching and scientific research achievements of professors Feng Jinglan, Yuan Fuli, Wang Bingzhang, and Zhang Xiti, to celebrate these four professors' persistence in geological education for two to three decades and their contributions to geological science.
    More than 20 years ago, Feng Jinglan investigated the strata and geological structures of Guangdong and Guangxi provinces, and achieved certain results in the investigation and research of copper mines in Southwest China. Yuan Fuli conducted geological surveys in Xinjiang, Inner Mongolia, Xikang, Yunnan and other places, and produced geological and mineral reports and papers on these areas. Wang Bingzhang and Zhang Xiti have long been engaged in the study of mineralogy, stratigraphy, and paleontology, respectively. They have authored many papers on mineralogy, stratigraphy, and paleontology.
    Since liberation, they have played an active role in learning from advanced Soviet science, reforming teaching, and cultivating new forces in the school.
                  (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 冯景兰等教授  在教学和科学研究上有贡献

1956-01-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　冯景兰等教授
    在教学和科学研究上有贡献
　　北京地质学院最近举办了冯景兰、袁复礼、王炳章、张席禔四位教授教学和科学研究成就展览会，庆祝这四位教授坚持地质教育工作二三十年和他们在地质科学上的贡献。
    冯景兰在20多年前曾对广东、广西两省的地层、地质构造作过调查，并且对我国西南铜矿的调查研究有一定的成绩。袁复礼曾在新疆、内蒙古、西康、云南等地作了地质调查，作出了这些地区的地质、矿产报告和论文。王炳章、张席禔长期以来分别研究矿物学、地层学和古生物学。他们著作了许多有关矿物学、地层学和古生物学的论文。
    解放以来，他们在学习苏联先进科学、教学改革和培养学校里的新生力量等方面都起了积极作用。
                  （据新华社讯）
