Title: Employees of Seven Beijing Construction Companies and Southwest Fourth Engineering Company Initiate Labor Competition Among Construction Enterprise Employees Nationwide
Author:
Date: 1956-01-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 25th: Employees of seven construction engineering companies in Beijing and the Southwest Fourth Engineering Company of the Ministry of Construction Engineering recently initiated a socialist competition among construction engineering companies nationwide.

　　The proposal from the seven construction engineering companies in Beijing was jointly put forward. These companies are the Beijing First, Second, Fourth, Fifth, and Sixth Construction Engineering Companies, the Tianjin Second Construction Engineering Company working in Beijing, and the Engineering Company directly under the General Logistics Department Barracks Management Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The first competition condition they put forward is to complete more project volume than in 1955 without increasing manpower. The second competition condition is to comprehensively improve project quality, ensure that project quality meets the standards required by construction acceptance specifications, and eliminate major rework accidents. The third competition condition is to further reduce project costs based on the 1955 design budget. The fourth competition condition is to ensure increased labor productivity. In addition, they also proposed guarantees to eliminate major work-related injuries and reduce minor injuries. The seven construction engineering companies in Beijing have recently held representative meetings attended by employee representatives, labor models, advanced workers, and captains of youth shock brigades, and have formulated specific measures such as promoting advanced experiences, implementing the suggestions of Soviet experts, and promoting team economic accounting.

　　Employees of the Southwest Fourth Engineering Company of the Ministry of Construction Engineering put forward 4 competition conditions and have formulated specific measures to ensure the realization of the competition conditions.

### 北京七个工程公司和西南第四工程公司职工  倡议全国建筑企业职工开展劳动竞赛

1956-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    北京七个工程公司和西南第四工程公司职工
    倡议全国建筑企业职工开展劳动竞赛
    据新华社25日讯　北京市七个建筑工程公司和建筑工程部西南第四工程公司的职工，最近倡议在全国各建筑工程公司之间开展社会主义竞赛。
    北京市七个建筑工程公司的倡议是联合提出的。这些公司是北京市第一、第二、第四、第五、第六建筑工程公司，在北京市施工的天津市第二建筑工程公司，中国人民解放军总后勤部营房管理部直属工程公司。它们提出的第一个竞赛条件是，在不增加人力的情况下，比1955年完成更多的工程量。第二个竞赛条件是，全面提高工程质量，保证工程质量达到施工验收规范所要求的标准，同时消灭重大返工事故。第三个竞赛条件是，在1955年设计预算的基础上，进一步降低工程成本。第四个竞赛条件是，保证提高劳动生产率。另外，它们还提出消灭重大工伤事故和减少轻伤事故的保证。北京市这七个建筑工程公司，最近都分别召开有职工代表、劳动模范、先进工作者和青年突击队队长参加的代表会议，制定了推广先进经验、贯彻苏联专家建议、推行班组经济核算等具体措施。
    建筑工程部西南第四工程公司的职工提出了4项竞赛条件，并且已经制定出保证实现竞赛条件的具体措施。
