Title: Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway Yumen-Hami Section Survey Completed
Author:
Date: 1956-01-08
Page: 2

The survey work for the Yumen to Hami section of the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway has been basically completed. Surveyors successively surveyed more than 60 alternative routes and finally selected a route that avoided sand dunes and had a relatively gentle slope.

The section from Yumen to Hami is 530 kilometers long and is a key section of the entire Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway survey. In order to complete the survey task of this section ahead of schedule, the surveyors braved the severe cold of minus 20 degrees Celsius and carried out arduous winter surveys. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 兰新路玉门哈密段勘测完成

1956-01-08
第2版()
专栏：

    兰新路玉门哈密段勘测完成
    兰新铁路玉门到哈密段的勘测工作已经基本完成。勘测人员先后勘测了60多条比较线，最后选定了一条既避开了沙丘、坡度又较平缓的线路。
    从玉门到哈密共长530公里，是兰新铁路全线勘测工作的重点地段。勘测人员为了提前完成这一段的勘测任务，冒着零下20多度的严寒进行了艰苦的冬季勘测。　（据新华社讯）
