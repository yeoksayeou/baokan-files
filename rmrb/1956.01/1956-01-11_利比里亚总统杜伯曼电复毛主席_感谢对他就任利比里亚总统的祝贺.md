Title: Liberian President Tubman Cables Chairman Mao in Reply, Thanking Him for His Congratulations on His Inauguration as President of Liberia
Author:
Date: 1956-01-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received a reply cable from Liberian President Tubman. The text of the cable is as follows: His Excellency Chairman Mao Zedong, People's Republic of China, Beijing:

I, the government and the people of Liberia are deeply grateful for your cable of congratulations on my inauguration.

Tubman, January 9, 1956, Monrovia

### 利比里亚总统杜伯曼电复毛主席  感谢对他就任利比里亚总统的祝贺

1956-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    利比里亚总统杜伯曼电复毛主席
    感谢对他就任利比里亚总统的祝贺
    新华社10日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东收到利比里亚总统杜伯曼的复电。电文如下：北京中华人民共和国毛泽东主席阁下：
    在我就职的时候，承阁下来电祝贺，我本人和利比里亚政府和人民深为感激。　　　杜伯曼　1956年1月9日　蒙罗维亚
