Title: "Socialist Upsurge in China's Countryside" to be Published Soon
Author:
Date: 1956-01-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The book "Socialist Upsurge in China's Countryside," edited by the General Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, will be published by the People's Publishing House on January 20th.

"Socialist Upsurge in China's Countryside" collects 176 articles on agricultural cooperativization, totaling approximately 900,000 characters. It is a compilation of the experiences of the agricultural cooperativization movement and various aspects of cooperativization issues from various regions of our country. Comrade Mao Zedong directly led the editing of this book, wrote a preface for it, and wrote editor's notes for the front of 104 articles. The preface and editor's notes have important guiding significance for many issues in our country's agricultural cooperativization movement, socialist construction, and Party work.

The book "Socialist Upsurge in China's Countryside" is divided into three volumes: upper, middle, and lower.

In order to facilitate readers who may not be able to read the entire book, the General Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China has also edited a selection of "Socialist Upsurge in China's Countryside." The selection includes 44 articles, totaling approximately 270,000 characters. The selection will also be published on January 20th.

### “中国农村的社会主义高潮”即将出版

1956-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　“中国农村的社会主义高潮”即将出版
    新华社8日讯　中共中央办公厅编辑的“中国农村的社会主义高潮”一书，将在1月20日由人民出版社出版。
    “中国农村的社会主义高潮”收集了176篇关于农业合作化问题的文章，约有90万字，是我国各个地区的农业合作化运动和关于合作化的各方面问题的经验的总汇。毛泽东同志直接领导了这部书的编辑工作，给这部书写了序言，并且在104篇文章的前面写了编者按语。序言和按语，对于我国农业合作化运动、社会主义建设和党的工作的许多问题，都有重要的指导意义。
    “中国农村的社会主义高潮”一书分订为上、中、下三册。
    为了便利一些不可能阅读全书的读者，中共中央办公厅还编辑了“中国农村的社会主义高潮”的选本。选本包括44篇文章，约有27万字。选本也将在1月20日出版。
