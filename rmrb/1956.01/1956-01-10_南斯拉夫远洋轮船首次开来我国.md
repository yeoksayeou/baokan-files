Title: Yugoslav Ocean Liner Arrives in China for the First Time
Author:
Date: 1956-01-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 9th. (TASS, 7th) According to the "Borba" newspaper, the Yugoslav ocean liner "Bosna," with a displacement of 12,500 tons, has sailed from the Yugoslav port of Rijeka to Shanghai, China, for the first time. The newspaper pointed out that this is a newly opened route between Yugoslavia and China. Yugoslav ocean liners will travel to and from this route once a month.

### 南斯拉夫远洋轮船首次开来我国

1956-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　南斯拉夫远洋轮船首次开来我国
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社贝尔格莱德7日讯：据“战斗报”报道，排水量12，500吨的南斯拉夫远洋轮船“波斯那号”已经从南斯拉夫的里耶卡港第一次开往中国的上海。这家报纸指出，这是南斯拉夫和中国之间新开辟的航线。南斯拉夫的远洋轮船将在这条航线上一月来回一次。
