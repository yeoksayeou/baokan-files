Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions and All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization Strengthen Scientific and Technological Publicity to Workers
Author:
Date: 1956-01-28
Page: 3
The All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, in order to adapt to the new high tide of socialist construction, have recently jointly formulated a collaborative plan outline for conducting scientific and technological publicity to workers in 1956.

The plan proposes that every grassroots trade union club should hold four scientific and technological lectures per month (including demonstrations, science and technology Q&A sessions, etc.); every regional trade union club should hold six scientific and technological lectures per month.

The All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization have jointly agreed on topics. Basic topics include basic scientific knowledge, the Five-Year Plan, knowledge of maternal and child health, general health knowledge, and basic science knowledge. In addition, there are basic production technology knowledge topics, including labor protection knowledge, basic technical knowledge, professional technical knowledge, and conservation knowledge, as well as topics on advanced experiences and construction achievements.

### 全国总工会和全国科学技术普及协会  加强对职工的科学技术宣传工作

1956-01-28
第3版()
专栏：

    全国总工会和全国科学技术普及协会
    加强对职工的科学技术宣传工作
    中华全国总工会和中华全国科学技术普及协会，为了适应社会主义建设新的高涨形势，最近联合制定了1956年对职工进行科学技术宣传工作的协作计划纲要。
    计划提出，每一基层工会俱乐部，每月举办四次科学技术讲演（包括形象表演、科学技术问题解答会等）；每一地区的工会俱乐部，每月举办六次科学技术讲演。
    中华全国总工会和中华全国科学技术普及协会，已经共同商定了选题。基本选题包括基本科学知识、五年计划、妇幼卫生知识、一般卫生知识和基础科学知识等方面的选题。这以外，还有基本生产技术知识选题，包括劳动保护知识、基础技术知识、专业技术知识和节约知识等方面的选题以及关于先进经验和建设成就方面的选题。
