Title: Fierce Artillery Battle Occurs on the Fujian Front
Author:
Date: 1956-01-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th: A fierce artillery battle occurred on the Fujian front on the 19th.

　　At 9:56 AM on the 19th, the Chiang Kai-shek bandit army artillery entrenched on Kinmen Island fired 22 shells at our Dadeng Island. At 10:45 AM, the Chiang bandit army's artillery positions on Greater and Lesser Kinmen Islands once again frantically bombarded the villages and towns of Huang厝, Baishi Battery, Shiweitou, Qianpu, He厝, Yundingyan, and other places in the Xiamen area, firing 1,073 shells. The two artillery attacks by the Chiang bandit army destroyed a total of 65 civilian houses, killing 2 residents and wounding 2 others.

　　The People's Liberation Army artillery units stationed in the Xiamen area retaliated with fierce artillery fire, suppressing the enemy's artillery fire and inflicting heavy casualties on the enemy.

### 福建前线发生激烈炮战

1956-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    福建前线发生激烈炮战
    据新华社20日讯　福建前线在19日发生激烈炮战。
    19日上午9时56分，盘踞金门岛的蒋贼军炮兵向我大嶝岛发射炮弹22发。10时45分，蒋贼军大金门、小金门的炮兵阵地再次向厦门地区的黄厝、白石炮台、石围头、前埔、何厝、云顶岩等地村镇疯狂轰击，发射炮弹1，073发。蒋贼军两次炮击，共计毁坏民房65间，炸死居民2人，炸伤2人。
    中国人民解放军驻厦门地区炮兵部队曾以猛烈炮火还击敌人，压制了敌军炮火，给予敌人重大杀伤。
