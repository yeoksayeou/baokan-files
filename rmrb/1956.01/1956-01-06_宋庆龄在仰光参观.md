Title: Soong Ching-ling Visits Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1956-01-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 5th -- Soong Ching-ling visited the famous Shwedagon Pagoda in Rangoon at 9:00 a.m. on the 5th. After the visit, she wrote in the comments book: "I wish the friendship between China and Burma will grow stronger day by day, and the peace of Asia and the world will be increasingly consolidated."

At 10:00 a.m. on the same day, Soong Ching-ling visited the World Peace Pagoda and the Great Cave. The Buddha's tooth, which was brought from China to Burma, is enshrined in the Great Cave.

### 宋庆龄在仰光参观

1956-01-06
第4版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄在仰光参观
    新华社仰光5日电　宋庆龄5日上午9时在仰光参观了著名的大金塔。参观后，她在意见簿上写道：祝中缅两国友谊日益增进，愿亚洲与世界和平日益巩固。
    同一天上午10时，宋庆龄参观了和平塔和大石窟。在大石窟里供奉着从中国奉迎到缅甸的佛牙。
