Title: Canadian Foreign Minister Pearson Says Canadian Government is Considering Recognizing Our Country
Author:
Date: 1956-01-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 18th. According to an Associated Press reporter in Ottawa, Canadian Foreign Minister Pearson said in the House of Commons on the 17th that the Canadian government "is and will continue to carefully consider" the issue of recognizing the People's Republic of China.

The reporter pointed out that this is the first time the Canadian government has stated that it is now considering recognizing the People's Republic of China.

### 加拿大外交部长皮尔逊说加政府正在考虑承认我国

1956-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    加拿大外交部长皮尔逊说加政府正在考虑承认我国
    新华社18日讯　据美联社记者自渥太华报道，加拿大外交部长皮尔逊17日在众议院说，加拿大政府“正在并且将继续仔细地考虑”承认中华人民共和国的问题。
    记者指出，这是加拿大政府第一次声明它现在正在考虑承认中华人民共和国的问题。
