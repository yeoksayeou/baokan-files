Title: Democratic National Construction Association Holds Report Meeting on Work with Industrial and Commercial Business Family Members
Author:
Date: 1956-01-28
Page: 1

The Central Standing Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association convened a report meeting on the work with family members of industrial and commercial business owners starting on the 25th. The meeting focused on reporting on the situations and experiences of the family members of industrial and commercial business members themselves, as well as their encouragement, assistance, and promotion of relatives to actively accept socialist transformation. Over 100 people attended the conference, including family members of industrial and commercial business owners from Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Nanjing, Wuxi, Hangzhou, Xi'an, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chongqing, Chengdu, as well as members of the Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association, representatives from the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and other relevant parties.

Huang Yanpei, Chairman of the Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association, Vice Chairman Li Zhuchen, and Chen Shutong, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, spoke at the meeting. In their speeches, they unanimously expressed the hope that the family members of industrial and commercial business owners would study diligently, further promote their relatives to actively accept socialist transformation, and strive for a bright future.

Speaking at the meeting on the 25th were Yu Xiu'ai from Beijing, Chen Yong'an from Tianjin, Rong Shuren and Wang Zhengxuan from Shanghai, and Huang Lixue from Wuhan. They unanimously stated that under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, their relatives have joyfully embarked on the path of joint state-private ownership. In the future, they themselves must study hard, transform their thinking, manage their households well, cultivate the habit of labor, and further promote their relatives to abandon exploitation and become glorious laborers who support themselves.

From the 26th, representatives from various regions attending the conference will give their respective reports.

### 民主建国会召开工商界家属工作汇报会

1956-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    民主建国会召开工商界家属工作汇报会
    本报讯　中国民主建国会中央常务委员会从25日起召开工商界家属工作汇报会，汇报工商业者会员家属本人以及鼓励、协助和推动亲属积极接受社会主义改造的情况和经验。出席大会的有北京、天津、上海、南京、无锡、杭州、西安、武汉、广州、重庆、成都等地工商业者的家属，以及民主建国会中央委员、中华全国民主妇女联合会和各有关方面的代表等100多人。
    中国民主建国会中央委员会主任委员黄炎培、副主任委员李烛尘和中华全国工商业联合会主任委员陈叔通等在会上讲了话。他们在讲话中一致希望工商业者的家属能够努力学习，进一步推动自己的亲属积极接受社会主义改造，争取光明前途。
    25日在大会上发言的，有北京的俞秀蔼，天津的陈庸庵，上海的荣漱仁、王征宣，武汉的黄立雪等。她们一致表示：在中国共产党和毛主席的英明领导下，她们的亲属已经欢欣鼓舞地走上了公私合营的道路，今后，她们自己一定要努力学习，改造思想，搞好家务，养成劳动的习惯，进一步推动亲属放弃剥削，成为一个自食其力的光荣的劳动者。
    从26日起，出席大会的各地代表将分别进行汇报。
