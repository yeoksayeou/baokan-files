Title: Sudanese Prime Minister Replies to Premier Zhou Enlai, Thanking China for Recognizing Sudanese Independence and Expressing Welcome for the Exchange of Diplomatic Representatives
Author:
Date: 1956-01-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received a telegram from the Prime Minister of Sudan as follows:
Your Excellency Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

    I would like to express my gratitude for the solemn recognition made by Your Excellency on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China at the time of Sudan's independence, as well as your congratulations. The relationship we began to build in Bandung will undoubtedly produce the best results of cooperation between the two countries. We welcome the exchange of diplomatic representatives and hope to discuss the rank and authority of the representatives soon. I wish you personally and the Chinese people happiness and prosperity, and extend to Your Excellency the most sublime respect.
                  Sudanese Prime Minister, January 7, 1956, Khartoum, Sudan

### 苏丹总理复电周恩来总理  感谢我国承认苏丹独立并表示欢迎交换外交代表

1956-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    苏丹总理复电周恩来总理
    感谢我国承认苏丹独立并表示欢迎交换外交代表
    新华社讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来收到苏丹总理的来电如下：北京中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长阁下：
    谨对阁下在苏丹独立的时候代表中华人民共和国政府所作的庄严的承认以及你的祝贺表示感谢。我们在万隆开始建立的关系，无疑地将在两国之间产生最良好的合作的成果。我们欢迎交换外交代表并且希望不久就能讨论代表的等级和权限。谨祝你本人和中国人民幸福和繁荣，并向阁下致最崇高的敬意。
                  苏丹总理　1956年1月7日　苏丹　喀土穆
