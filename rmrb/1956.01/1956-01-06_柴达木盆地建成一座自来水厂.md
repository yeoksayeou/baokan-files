Title: A Tap Water Plant Built in the Qaidam Basin
Author: 
Date: 1956-01-06
Page: 2

    A Tap Water Plant Built in the Qaidam Basin
    The first tap water plant has been built in the Qaidam Basin of Qinghai Province. From now on, the water used at each construction site in the basin no longer needs to be transported from nine kilometers away. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 柴达木盆地建成一座自来水厂

1956-01-06
第2版()
专栏：

    柴达木盆地建成一座自来水厂
    青海省柴达木盆地建成了第一座自来水厂。从此，盆地上每个工地用的水就不必到九公里以外的地方运输了。　　　（据新华社讯）
