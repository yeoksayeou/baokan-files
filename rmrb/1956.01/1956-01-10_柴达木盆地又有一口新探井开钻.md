Title: New Exploration Well Drilled in Qaidam Basin
Author:
Date: 1956-01-10
Page: 2
　
　　Another new exploration well was drilled in the Qaidam Basin on New Year's Day, 1956. On this day, the drilling team members, filled with festive joy and braving the severe cold, started the drilling rig and drilled ten meters within a few hours. (Qinghai Daily Editorial Department)

### 柴达木盆地又有一口新探井开钻

1956-01-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　柴达木盆地又有一口新探井开钻
    柴达木盆地在1956年元旦又有一口新探井开钻。这一天，钻探队员们怀着节日的喜悦心情，冒着严寒，开动了钻机，在几小时之内就钻进了十公尺。　（青海日报编辑部）
