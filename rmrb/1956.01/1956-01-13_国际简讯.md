	International Briefs
	△ The regional conference of the International Union Against Tuberculosis opened on January 9 in Trivandrum, Travancore-Cochin State. The conference was chaired by Dr. P.V. Benjamin, Tuberculosis Advisor to the Indian Government. Representatives from India, China, Nepal, Thailand, and other countries participated in the meeting.
	△ In a telegram sent to Prime Minister Grotewohl on January 7, the Prime Minister of the Republic of Sudan, Azhari, agreed to the establishment of a trade mission of the German Democratic Republic in Sudan.

### 国际简讯

1956-01-13
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    △国际防痨联合会区域会议1月9日在特拉凡哥尔—柯钦邦的特里凡得琅开幕。会议由印度政府防痨顾问普·维·本杰明博士主持。参加会议的有印度、中国、尼泊尔、泰国和其他国家的代表。
    △苏丹共和国总理阿扎里在1月7日拍给格罗提渥总理的电报中同意德意志民主共和国在苏丹设立一个贸易使团。
