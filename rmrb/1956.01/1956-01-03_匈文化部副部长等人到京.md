Title: Hungarian Deputy Minister of Culture and Others Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-01-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch:
　　Hungarian People's Republic Deputy Minister of Culture, Gyula Kállai, and Secretary-General of the Institute for Foreign Cultural Relations, Gerek Kabler, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of January 2. During their stay in China, they will negotiate and sign the 1956 execution plan for the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement and conduct visits and tours.

### 匈文化部副部长等人到京

1956-01-03
第4版()
专栏：

    匈文化部副部长等人到京
    据新华社讯　匈牙利人民共和国文化部副部长卡拉伊·居拉和对外文化联络院秘书长葛雷克·卡卜尔在2日下午乘飞机到达北京。他们在中国期间将谈判和签订中匈文化合作协定1956年执行计划，并且进行访问参观。
