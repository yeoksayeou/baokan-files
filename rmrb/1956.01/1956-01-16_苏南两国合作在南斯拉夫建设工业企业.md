Title: Soviet-Yugoslav Cooperation to Build Industrial Enterprises in Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1956-01-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Belgrade: The talks between the governments of the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia in Belgrade have concluded, and an agreement on cooperation between the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia in the construction of industrial enterprises in Yugoslavia has been signed.

The agreement stipulates that the Soviet Union will cooperate with Yugoslavia in constructing in the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia a nitrogen fertilizer factory with an annual output of 100,000 tons of ammonium salt and processing it into lime-ammonium nitrate; a superphosphate factory with an annual output of 250,000 tons of superphosphate and 120,000 tons of sulfuric acid; a thermal power station with a generating capacity of 100,000 kilowatts; and the expansion and reconstruction of three mines.

### 苏南两国合作在南斯拉夫建设工业企业

1956-01-16
第4版()
专栏：

    苏南两国合作在南斯拉夫建设工业企业
    据新华社讯　苏联和南斯拉夫两国政府在贝尔格莱德举行的会谈已经结束，并且签订了一项关于苏联和南斯拉夫在南斯拉夫建设工业企业方面实行合作的协定。
    这个协定规定苏联同南斯拉夫合作，在南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国建设一座年产10万吨氨盐并且把它加工成石灰—硝酸氨的氮肥工厂、一座年产25万吨过磷酸盐和12万吨硫酸的过磷酸盐工厂、一座发电能力为10万瓩的热电站，扩大和改建三座矿山。
