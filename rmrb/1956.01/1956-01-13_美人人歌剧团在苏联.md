Title: The Everyman Opera Company in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-01-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th dispatch: According to TASS, the American Everyman Opera Company, which was invited to tour the Soviet Union, performed George Gershwin's opera "Porgy and Bess" at the Stanislavski and Nemirovich-Danchenko Theatre in Moscow on January 11.

Those present at the performance included: Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Suslov, Khrushchev, and Shepilov. The entire theater was filled with audience members.

### 美人人歌剧团在苏联

1956-01-13
第4版()
专栏：

    美人人歌剧团在苏联
    据新华社12日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科讯：应邀到苏联巡回演出的美国人人歌剧团，1月11日在莫斯科斯坦尼斯拉夫斯基和聂米罗维奇—丹钦科剧院演出乔治·格尔什温的歌剧“波吉与白丝”。
    出席观剧的有：卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、苏斯洛夫、赫鲁晓夫和谢皮洛夫等。整个剧院坐满了观众。
