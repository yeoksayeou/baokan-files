Title: Yunnan to Build More Than Two Hundred Factories This Year
Author:
Date: 1956-01-14
Page: 2

    Yunnan Province will build and expand more than two hundred local state-owned factories this year. Among them, more than 60 are farm tool manufacturing plants, repair shops, fertilizer plants, and pesticide plants that directly serve agricultural production. At present, 39 farm tool manufacturing plants and repair shops distributed in various special districts and counties have started construction one after another and will be completed in the second quarter of this year.
    Among the factories that Yunnan Province plans to build and expand this year, there are also oil pressing, sugar making, silk reeling, and cotton ginning factories, as well as processing factories for camphor, rosin, and other local specialty products. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 云南今年将建设二百多个工厂

1956-01-14
第2版()
专栏：

    云南今年将建设二百多个工厂
    云南省今年要新建和扩建二百多个地方国营工厂。其中有60多个是直接为农业生产服务的农具制造厂、修配厂、肥料厂和农药厂。目前，分布在各专区、县的39个农具制造厂、修配厂，已经陆续施工，将在今年第二季度建成。
    云南省今年准备新建和扩建的工厂中，还有榨油、制糖、缫丝、轧花等工厂和樟脑、松香等土产特产品的加工工厂。  （据新华社讯）
