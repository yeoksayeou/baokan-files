Statistics on US Activities Intensifying Tension in the Taiwan Region
(September 10, 1955 – January 5, 1956)

On September 10, 1955, the Sino-US ambassadorial talks reached an agreement on the first item on the agenda, the repatriation of civilians by both sides. After this, the Sino-US talks entered the discussion of the second item on the agenda. The Chinese side proposed the lifting of the embargo and the preparation for a Sino-US foreign ministers' meeting to discuss easing and eliminating tensions in the Taiwan region, while the US side raised the issue of so-called "renunciation of the use of force." However, at this very moment, the US intensified its military activities and threats of force in the Taiwan region. The following incomplete statistics of the military activities and threats of force carried out by the United States in the Taiwan region from September 10, 1955, to January 5, 1956, illustrate that it is the United States itself that should "renounce the use of force." 1. Important military and political figures go to Taiwan. 2. Strengthening military forces in the Taiwan region. 3. Naval and air forces conduct exercises in the Far East and the Taiwan region.

Note: ① The United States already has a full air force wing in Taiwan. The number of military advisory group personnel has been increased to approximately 2,800. On October 22, 1955, the United States established the "US Taiwan Defense Command" in Taiwan. This aggressive organization "will be ready to take on the task of a general headquarters for combat command immediately in the event of hostilities in the Taiwan region."

② The United States has already supplied the Chiang bandits with a large number of jet fighters. On December 29, 1955, the United States also signed a "Reserve and Replenishment System Plan" for the Chiang bandit army with the Chiang bandits, stipulating that the United States would assist the Chiang bandits in establishing nine reserve divisions. Radford said in Taipei on January 6 that more US aircraft would be sent to the Chiang bandit group soon. (Attached chart)

### 美国加剧台湾地区紧张局势的活动统计

1956-01-12
第4版()
专栏：

    美国加剧台湾地区紧张局势的活动统计
    （1955年9月10日——1956年1月5日）
    1955年9月10日，中美两国大使级会谈在第一项议程双方平民回国问题上达成了协议。在这以后，中美会谈进入了第二项议程的讨论。中国方面提出了取消禁运和准备中美外长会议讨论和缓和消除台湾地区的紧张局势问题，而美国方面却提出了所谓“放弃使用武力”的问题。但是就在这个时候，美国却在台湾地区加紧了军事活动和武力威胁。下面所列的从1955年9月10日到1956年1月5日美国在台湾地区进行的军事活动和武力威胁的不完全的统计，就说明了应该“放弃使用武力”的就是美国自己。1、重要军政人物去台湾2、加强台湾地区军事力量3、海空军在远东和台湾地区举行演习
    注：①美国在台湾的空军已共有一个联队。军事顾问团人员已增加为2，800左右。1955年10月22日，美国在台湾成立“美军协防台湾司令部”。这个侵略机构“在一旦在台湾地区发生敌对行动时就将立即准备好负担起作战指挥总部的任务”。
    ②美国已经供给蒋贼大批喷气式战斗机。1955年12月29日，美国又同蒋贼签订了一项蒋贼军“后备及补充制度的方案”，规定美国协助蒋贼建立九个预备师。雷德福1月6日在台北说，不久将有更多的美国飞机送给蒋贼集团。（附图表）
