Title: One New Generator Added Every Ten Days Last Year
Author:
Date: 1956-01-14
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency report:

    Last year, China's electric power industry added a new generator with a capacity of over 1,000 kilowatts to production on average every ten days or so. The newly added power generation capacity last year was greater than the total newly added power generation capacity in 1953 and 1954 combined. Last year, electric power departments in various regions also erected a total of 780 kilometers of new high-voltage transmission lines.

### 去年每十天增加一台新发电机

1956-01-14
第2版()
专栏：

    去年每十天增加一台新发电机
    中国的电力工业部门去年平均十天左右就有一台设备容量在一千瓩以上的新发电机投入生产。去年新增加的发电设备能力，比1953年和1954年两年新增加的发电设备能力总和还要多。各地电力工业部门去年还新架设了总长780公里的高压输电线路。
                  （据新华社讯）
