Title: Menon Advocates for Higher-Level Sino-U.S. Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-01-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, January 11th, New York dispatch: Menon, head of the Indian delegation to the Tenth United Nations General Assembly, expressed concern about the Sino-U.S. talks. Before leaving New York on January 10th, Menon told an Associated Press reporter that greater efforts should be made for peace in the Far East. Menon pointed out that the United States and China are holding ambassadorial-level talks in Geneva, and "more important figures should attend the talks."

In the same conversation, Menon called on the United States to abandon its plan to conduct a new super-bomb test in the Pacific.

### 梅农主张中美举行高一级会谈

1956-01-12
第4版()
专栏：

    梅农主张中美举行高一级会谈
    新华社11日讯  据美联社纽约消息：出席第十届联合国大会的印度代表团团长梅农对中美会谈表示关切。梅农在1月10日离开纽约前向美联社记者表示：应该为远东和平作出更大的努力。梅农指出，美国同中国正在日内瓦进行大使级的会谈，“应该有更重要的人物出席会谈”。
    在同一次谈话中，梅农要求美国放弃它在太平洋进行一次新的超级炸弹试验的计划。
