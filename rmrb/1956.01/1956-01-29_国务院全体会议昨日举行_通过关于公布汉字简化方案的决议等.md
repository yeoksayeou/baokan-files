Title: State Council Plenary Session Held Yesterday: Approved Resolution on Promulgating the Chinese Character Simplification Plan, etc.
Author:
Date: 1956-01-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 28th -- The 23rd Plenary Session of the State Council of the People's Republic of China was held on the afternoon of the 28th. The meeting heard and approved the explanation of Wu Yuzhang, Director of the Chinese Language Reform Committee, and adopted the "Resolution of the State Council on Promulgating the Chinese Character Simplification Plan" and the "Instruction of the State Council on Promoting Putonghua." The meeting approved the establishment of the Central Putonghua Promotion Working Committee and agreed that the Chinese Language Reform Committee should submit the "Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme (Draft)" to the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference to organize discussions and publish it in newspapers to solicit opinions widely, make necessary revisions, and then submit it to the State Council for approval. The meeting also approved several personnel appointments and removals.

### 国务院全体会议昨日举行  通过关于公布汉字简化方案的决议等

1956-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院全体会议昨日举行
    通过关于公布汉字简化方案的决议等
    新华社28日讯　中华人民共和国国务院全体会议第二十三次会议在28日下午举行。会议听了和批准了中国文字改革委员会主任吴玉章的说明，通过了“国务院关于公布汉字简化方案的决议”和“国务院关于推广普通话的指示”。会议批准成立中央推广普通话工作委员会，并同意中国文字改革委员会把“汉语拼音方案（草案）”送请中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会组织座谈讨论，同时在报纸上发表，以便广泛征求意见，作必要的修正后，再提请国务院审定。会议还通过了任免事项多起。
