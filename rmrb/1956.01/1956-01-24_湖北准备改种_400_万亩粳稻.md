Title: Hubei Prepares to Convert 4 Million Mu to Japonica Rice Cultivation
Author:
Date: 1956-01-24
Page: 2

    Hubei Prepares to Convert 4 Million Mu to Japonica Rice Cultivation

    Hubei Province plans to convert 4 million mu of paddy fields to cultivate high-quality japonica rice varieties this year, establishing high-yield demonstration zones for japonica rice in five counties, including Xishui and Mianyang.

    Replacing indica rice with japonica rice is one of the four major agricultural technology reforms in Hubei Province this year. In 1955, trial cultivation of japonica rice in various regions generally resulted in yields of 700-800 jin per mu, with some reaching 1,200 jin, an increase of 30% compared to local indica rice. This year, the 500,000 mu of japonica rice in the demonstration zones are required to achieve an average yield of 1,000 jin per mu. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 湖北准备改种400万亩粳稻

1956-01-24
第2版()
专栏：

    湖北准备改种400万亩粳稻
    湖北省今年准备在400万亩水田里改种粳稻良种，在浠水、沔阳等五县建立粳稻的丰产示范区。
    改籼稻为粳稻是湖北省今年四项农业技术改革的重要内容之一。1955年各地试种粳稻结果，每亩产量一般达到七、八百斤，有的达到1，200斤，比当地籼稻一般要增产3成。今年示范区的50万亩粳稻，每亩平均产量要求达到1，000斤。（据新华社讯）
