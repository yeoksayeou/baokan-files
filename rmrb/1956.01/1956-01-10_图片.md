　　Left: 1,276 private factories in ten industries, including the Beijing Machine Manufacturing Industry, were approved to implement joint public-private ownership on the 9th. This is the scene of the reporting team composed of the factories' employees reporting the good news at the Beijing Capitalist Industry Joint Public-Private Ownership Reporting Conference.
　　Right: More than 1,200 representatives from industry and commerce in the Dongdan District of Beijing went to the Dongdan District Committee of the Chinese Communist Party to report the good news before the grand parade celebrating joint public-private ownership, and asked the District Committee to forward the letter of good news to Chairman Mao on their behalf.

专栏：

　　左：北京市机器制造工业等十个行业1，276家私营工厂9日被批准实行公私合营。这是各厂职工组成的报喜队到北京市召开的资本主义工业公私合营报告会上报喜的情形。
　　右：北京市东单区1，200多名工商界代表在庆祝公私合营大游行前到中国共产党东单区委员会去报喜，并请区委会代为向毛主席送报喜信。　　本报记者　高粮摄
