Title: Albanian Labor Party Central Committee Plenum
Author:
Date: 1956-01-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, December 31st: According to newspaper reports, the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party held a plenary session on December 27th and 28th. The plenary session adopted the report made by Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, on the implementation of the 1955 state plan and measures for the implementation of the 1956 state plan, and passed corresponding resolutions.
    The plenary session also decided to convene the Third Congress of the Albanian Labor Party on May 25, 1956. The agenda of the congress is: the summary report of the Central Committee, the summary report of the Central Inspection Committee, the report on the results of the implementation of the first Five-Year Plan and the instructions on the second Five-Year Plan for the development of the economy and culture of the People's Republic of Albania, the report on the revision of the Constitution of the Albanian Labor Party, and the election of the party's central organs.

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央全会

1956-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央全会
    新华社31日讯　据塔斯社地拉那讯：据报纸报道，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会在12月27日和28日举行了全体会议。全体会议通过了阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席谢胡所作的党中央政治局关于1955年国家计划的执行情况和关于执行1956年国家计划的措施的报告，并且通过了相应的决议。
    全体会议还决定在1956年5月25日召开阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第三次代表大会。代表大会的议程是：中央委员会的总结报告，中央检查委员会的总结报告，关于执行第一个五年计划的结果的报告和关于发展阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国的经济和文化的第二个五年计划的指示，关于修改阿尔巴尼亚劳动党党章的报告以及选举党的中央机关。
