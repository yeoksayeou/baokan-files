Title: Tenth Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of Albania
Author: 
Date: 1956-01-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, January 11th: According to TASS, a grand meeting was held in Tirana, the capital of Albania, on January 10 to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of Albania.

The participants of the meeting warmly welcomed the leaders of the Albanian Labor Party and the government when they appeared on the presidium seats.

Mufti, Vice Chairman of the Albanian Council of Ministers, delivered a report at the meeting. In his report, he talked about the achievements of Albania's socialist construction over the past ten years, pointing out that by the end of 1955 – the last year of the first five-year plan – Albania's total industrial output had increased to 12.5 times that of 1938. The output of the metal processing industry increased to 5.5 times that of 1950. The current cotton fabric output of the Stalin Textile Factory alone has increased to 48 times the total cotton fabric output of Albania in 1938. At present, Albania's agricultural sown area has increased by 70% compared with 1938, and grain output has increased by 75%. There are now 318 agricultural production cooperatives in the country. Cooperatives, together with state farms, produce 13.6% of the country's total grain harvest, 77.4% of the total beet harvest, and 29.8% of the total cotton harvest. The trade network in 1955 expanded by 50% compared with 1950. Due to the overall strengthening of the national economy, the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party and the government recently made a decision to partially abolish the rationing of some industrial products.

Mufti then talked about the situation of further vigorous development of industry and agricultural cooperation movement in 1956.

Mufti then said that the Albanian Labor Party and the government closely combine the construction of socialism and the improvement of the living conditions of the working people with the struggle to safeguard and consolidate world peace. The Albanian people are active and consistent participants in the great and strong camp of peace. They have close, sincere, and fraternal friendship with the Soviet people, Albania's great liberators and protectors. They have established close and fraternal friendly relations with great China and the various people's democratic countries. The People's Republic of Albania has also restored fraternal ties with the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia.

Mufti continued that the participation of the People's Republic of Albania in the United Nations is a major victory for our people. This victory was achieved due to the initiative of the Soviet Union, the great friend of the Albanian people and the defender of the rights of the people of all countries.

Mufti's report won warm applause from the attendees.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国成立十周年

1956-01-12
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国成立十周年
    据新华社11日讯  据塔斯社地拉那讯：1月10日，阿尔巴尼亚首都地拉那举行庆祝大会，庆祝阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国成立十周年。
    当阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和政府的领导人员在主席团席位上出现时，大会参加者热烈鼓掌欢迎。
    阿尔巴尼亚部长会议副主席穆夫蒂乌在会上作了报告。他在报告中谈到阿尔巴尼亚十年来社会主义建设的成就时指出，到1955年——第一个五年计划的最后一年的年底，阿尔巴尼亚的工业总产量已经增加到了1938年的12.5倍。金属加工工业的产量增加到1950年的5.5倍。仅仅一个斯大林纺织工厂现在的棉织品产量就增加到1938年阿尔巴尼亚全国棉织品产量的48倍。目前，阿尔巴尼亚农业的播种面积比1938年增加了70％，谷物产量增加了75％。全国现在有318个农业生产合作社。合作社同国营农场一起生产国家谷物总收获量的13.6％，甜菜总收获量的77.4％，棉花总收获量的29.8％。1955年的贸易网比1950年扩大了50％。由于国家经济的全面加强，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会和政府最近作出了关于部分取消一些工业品的配给制的决定。
    穆夫蒂乌接着还谈到了1956年将要进一步大力发展工业和农业合作化运动的情形。
    穆夫蒂乌随后说，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和政府是把建设社会主义、改进劳动人民的生活条件同为维护和巩固世界和平的奋斗紧密地结合在一起的。阿尔巴尼亚人民是伟大而坚强的和平阵营中的积极和贯彻始终的参加者。他们同苏联人民、阿尔巴尼亚伟大的解放者和保护者有着密切的、真挚的、兄弟的友谊。他们同伟大的中国和各人民民主国家建立了紧密的、兄弟般的友好联系。阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国还恢复了同南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国的兄弟般的联系。
    穆夫蒂乌接着说，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国的参加联合国是我国人民的一大胜利。这个胜利是由于阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大朋友和一切国家人民权利的保卫者——苏联的倡议而取得的。
    穆夫蒂乌的报告博得了到会者的热烈掌声。
    最后，大会在十分热烈的气氛中通过了给苏联共产党中央委员会、苏联政府和苏联最高苏维埃主席团的致敬电。
