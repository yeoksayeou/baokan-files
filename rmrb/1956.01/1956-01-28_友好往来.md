Author:
Date: 1956-01-28
Page: 4

    △ The first group of five members of the Gymnastics Team of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, led by Ye. Ya. Sisiji, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 26th at the invitation of China.
    △ Pannikar, Chairman of the National Executive Committee of the India-China Friendship Association, and the members held a welcome reception at the Constitution Club in New Delhi on the evening of January 25 to welcome the China-India Friendship Association Cultural Delegation to India, headed by Wu Han. On the same evening, Yuan Zhongxian, the Chinese Ambassador to India, held a banquet to welcome the China-India Friendship Association Cultural Delegation to India.

### 友好往来

1956-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

    友好往来
    △应邀来中国访问的俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国体操队第一批人员5人，在领队叶·雅·西集伊率领下，26日乘飞机到北京。
    △印中友好协会全国执行委员会主席潘尼迦和委员们1月25日晚上在新德里宪法俱乐部举行欢迎会，欢迎以吴晗为首的中印友好协会访印文化代表团。同日晚上，中国驻印度大使袁仲贤举行宴会，欢迎中印友好协会访印文化代表团。
