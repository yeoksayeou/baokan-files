Title: Jianghan Bridge Holds Opening Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1956-01-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Report: The Jianghan Bridge, an important component of the recently completed Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge, held a grand opening ceremony on December 31, 1955. Thirty-five festively decorated new cars participating in the opening ceremony drove across the Jianghan Bridge for the first time that morning, reaching the ancient city of Hanyang. This motorcade transported a batch of cotton to the state-owned Wuhan No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory in Hanyang; it transported a crane and timber to the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge construction site and other construction sites; and it transported a batch of grain to the more than 100,000 residents of Hanyang.

### 江汉桥举行通车典礼

1956-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

    江汉桥举行通车典礼
    最近建成的武汉长江大桥重要组成部分之一的江汉桥，1955年12月31日举行了隆重的通车典礼。参加通车典礼的35辆结彩的新汽车，这天上午第一次驶过江汉桥，开到了古老的汉阳。这个汽车队给汉阳的国营武汉第一棉纺织厂运去了一批棉花；给武汉长江大桥工地和其他建筑工地运去了一部起重机和木材；给汉阳十多万居民运去了一批粮食。（据新华社讯）
