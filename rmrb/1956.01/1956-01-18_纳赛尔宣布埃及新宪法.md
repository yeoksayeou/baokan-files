Title: Nasser Announces New Egyptian Constitution
Author:
Date: 1956-01-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 17th: Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser announced Egypt's new constitution at a mass rally in Cairo's Republic Square on the 16th. This marks the end of the transitional period since the overthrow of King Farouk in July 1952. The new constitution will replace the Egyptian constitution of 1923, which was drawn up under British occupation.

The new constitution stipulates that: Egypt is an independent Arab state. The political system is a republic. Egyptians belong to the Arab nation. The state religion is Islam. The official language is Arabic.

Nasser announced that Egyptian citizens will vote on June 23 to approve or reject the new constitution. The presidential election will be held on July 7. He said the election would be conducted by universal, secret ballot.

### 纳赛尔宣布埃及新宪法

1956-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔宣布埃及新宪法
    据新华社17日讯　开罗消息：埃及总理纳赛尔16日在开罗共和国广场举行的一个群众大会上宣布了埃及的新宪法。1952年7月废黜法鲁克国王以来的过渡阶段至此告一结束。新宪法将代替1923年在英国占领下制定的埃及宪法。
    新宪法规定：埃及是一个独立的阿拉伯国家。政体是共和政体。埃及人属于阿拉伯民族。国家的宗教是伊斯兰教。正式的语言是阿拉伯文。
    纳赛尔宣布，埃及国民将在6月23日投票赞成或是反对这个新宪法。总统选举将在7月7日举行。他说，选举将以普遍的、秘密的投票进行。
