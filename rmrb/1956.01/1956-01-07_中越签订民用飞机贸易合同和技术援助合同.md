Title: China and Vietnam Sign Civil Aircraft Trade Contract and Technical Assistance Contract
Author:
Date: 1956-01-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th — The Vietnam Import-Export Corporation recently contacted the China National Technical Import Corporation to purchase five re-exported civil aircraft and some equipment. The two sides signed this trade contract on December 30, 1955.

In addition, the Vietnam Import-Export Corporation recently contacted the China National Technical Import Corporation to hire Chinese technicians to go to Vietnam to provide technical assistance. The two sides signed this technical assistance contract on December 31, 1955.

### 中越签订民用飞机贸易合同和技术援助合同

1956-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　中越签订民用飞机贸易合同和技术援助合同
    新华社6日讯　越南出入口总公司最近向中国技术进口公司接洽购买转口的民用飞机五架及若干器材。双方在1955年12月30日签订了这项贸易合同。
    又讯：越南出入口总公司最近又向中国技术进口公司接洽聘请中国技术人员赴越予以技术上的协助。双方在1955年12月31日签订了这项技术援助合同。
