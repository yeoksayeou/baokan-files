Title: First National Petroleum Exploration Conference Opens
Author:
Date: 1956-01-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 24th – The First National Petroleum Exploration Conference opened today. This conference will, on the basis of summarizing the work of 1955, discuss this year's even greater petroleum exploration tasks.

The Minister of Petroleum Industry, Li Juikui, gave a report at today's meeting. He said that 1955 was a year in which China's petroleum geological exploration work achieved significant accomplishments. With the joint efforts of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the Ministry of Geology, and the Ministry of Petroleum Industry, the 1955 petroleum exploration task was overfulfilled, with total drilling footage increasing by 50% compared to 1954. The various petroleum exploration teams have grown stronger, with the petroleum exploration forces under the Ministry of Petroleum Industry alone increasing by 11% to 88% compared to 1954. Li Juikui said that the achievements of 1955 will bring about tremendous changes in China's natural petroleum exploration work in the next year or two. Li Juikui said that the 1956 petroleum exploration task will be even more arduous than in previous years. He called on petroleum exploration workers across the country to work even harder than last year, strive to complete the First Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule, and prepare for the Second Five-Year Plan.

This conference was convened by the Ministry of Petroleum Industry.

### 第一届全国石油勘探会议开幕

1956-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    第一届全国石油勘探会议开幕
    据新华社24日讯　第一届全国石油勘探会议在今天开幕。这次会议将在总结1955年工作的基础上，讨论今年的更大的石油勘探任务。
    石油工业部部长李聚奎在今天的会上作了报告。他说，1955年是我国石油地质勘探工作获得重大成就的一年。在中国科学院、地质部和石油工业部的共同努力下，1955年的石油勘探任务超额完成了，钻井总进尺比1954年增加了50％，各种石油勘探的队伍壮大了，仅石油工业部系统各种石油勘探力量就比1954年增加了11％到88％。李聚奎说，1955年的成就将使我国天然石油的勘探工作在今后一两年内发生巨大变化。李聚奎说，1956年的石油勘探任务要比往年更加繁重，他号召全国石油勘探工作者要比去年更加努力，争取提前完成第一个五年计划，并且为第二个五年计划作准备。
    这次会议是石油工业部召开的。
