International Briefs

	△ On January 12th, Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Part of the Soviet Union and member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, received the Iranian Parliamentary delegation visiting the Soviet Union in the Kremlin.
	△ The Prime Minister of the Republic of Sudan, Ismail al-Azhari, sent a reply telegram to the China Islamic Association, thanking them for their warm congratulatory telegram on the independence of Sudan.
	△ The Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, considering that the state has been consolidated, and that some criminals have repented of their crimes during their imprisonment, and that the danger they pose to society has disappeared or diminished, has decided to pardon some criminals who have not yet served their full sentences, and to shorten the sentences of another group of criminals.
	At the same time, the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania also issued an order regarding the amnesty of Albanian nationals who have fled abroad.
	△ The President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Sapotocky, received the former Guatemalan President Jacobo Arbenz Guzmán, who is currently in Czechoslovakia, on the 10th and had a friendly conversation with him.

### 国际简讯

1956-01-14
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    △1月12日，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫，苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫和苏联部长会议第一副主席兼苏联外交部长莫洛托夫，在克里姆林宫接见了正在苏联访问的伊朗议会代表团。
    △苏丹共和国总理伊斯梅尔·阿扎里复电中国伊斯兰教协会感谢对苏丹独立的热情的贺电。
    △阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团由于考虑到国家已经巩固，和一部分罪犯在服刑期间已经痛悔他们所犯的罪行，而且他们对社会造成的危险已经消失或者减少，因此，决定赦免某些尚未服满刑期的罪犯，并且缩短另外一批罪犯的刑期。
    同时阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团也发表了关于大赦逃亡到国外的阿尔巴尼亚国民的命令。
    △捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基，在10日接见了目前在捷克斯洛伐克的前危地马拉总统哈科波·阿本斯·古斯曼，并且同他进行了友好的谈话。
