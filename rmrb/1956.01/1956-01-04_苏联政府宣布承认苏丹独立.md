Title: Soviet Government Announces Recognition of Sudan's Independence
Author:
Date: 1956-01-04
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 3rd (TASS) -- Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, telegraphed the Prime Minister of Sudan, Ismail al-Azhari, on the 3rd, stating that the Soviet government, in accordance with the great principle of national self-determination, respects the legitimate national aspirations of Sudan, solemnly declares its recognition of Sudan as an independent and sovereign state, and expresses its willingness to establish diplomatic, consular, and commercial relations with Sudan, as well as exchange diplomatic representatives.
    The telegram concluded by saying that the Soviet people wish the Sudanese people every success in consolidating Sudan's national sovereignty and developing its national economy and culture.

### 苏联政府宣布承认苏丹独立

1956-01-04
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联政府宣布承认苏丹独立
    新华社据塔斯社莫斯科3日讯　苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁在3日打电报给苏丹总理伊斯梅尔·阿扎里说，苏联政府本着伟大的民族自决原则，尊重苏丹的合理的民族愿望，庄严宣布承认苏丹为独立主权国家，并且表示愿意同苏丹建立外交、领事和商务关系以及交换外交代表。
    电报最后说，苏联人民祝苏丹人民在巩固苏丹国家主权、发展它的民族经济和文化方面获得各种成就。
