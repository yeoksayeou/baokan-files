Title: Condolence Message from the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defence of Children

Author: 
Date: 1953-03-17
Page: 3

Condolence Message from the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defence of Children to the Czechoslovak Women's Union:
    We are deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Gottwald, and we extend our deepest condolences. The passing of Comrade Gottwald is a great loss to the Czechoslovak people and to the entire world's movement to defend peace and defend children. We firmly believe that the people of China and Czechoslovakia will unite even more closely and strive for our common cause of defending peace and defending children.
                    Chinese People's National Committee for the Defence of Children
                              March 15, 1953

### 中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会的唁电

1953-03-17
第3版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会的唁电捷克斯洛伐克妇女联合会：
    惊悉哥特瓦尔德同志不幸逝世，我们谨致沉痛的哀悼。哥特瓦尔德同志的逝世，对于捷克斯洛伐克人民，对于整个世界保卫和平，保卫儿童的运动，都是巨大的损失。我们深信中捷两国人民必将更加紧密团结，为我们的保卫和平，保卫儿童的共同事业而奋斗。
                    中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会
                              一九五三年三月十五日
