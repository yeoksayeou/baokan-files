Condolence Message from the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Workers' Union to the Central Committee of the Soviet Railway Transport Workers' Union:

    We are shocked and deeply grieved to learn of the passing of Comrade Stalin, the leader and teacher of the working people of the world and our beloved Comrade Stalin. This is a great misfortune for the Soviet people, the Chinese people, and the working people of the world. We Chinese railway workers will, under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong and the Chinese Communist Party, unite more closely, redouble our efforts in labor, accelerate our learning from the Soviet Union, build our motherland, and consolidate our national defense; we will forever faithfully follow Comrade Stalin's teachings, unite more closely with the Soviet people, heighten our vigilance, and fight to the end for the sacred cause of defending world peace and security!
    National Committee of the Chinese Railway Workers' Union
    March 6, 1953

Condolence Message from the National Committee of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union to the Central Committee of the Soviet Coal Miners' Union:

    The Chinese coal miners express their deep condolences on the passing of Comrade J.V. Stalin, the most beloved teacher and leader of the working people of the world. Comrade Stalin's death is a great loss to the working people of the world. We are determined to, under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong and under the banner of Comrade Stalin, which is glorious and immortal, join hands with the Soviet coal miners and strive to achieve a communist society!
    Eternal glory to Comrade Stalin, the great leader of the people of the world and the great teacher of the Chinese working class!
    National Committee of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union
    March 6, 1953

    各业工会的唁电
    中国铁路工会全国委员会的唁电苏联铁道运输工会中央委员会：
    惊悉全世界劳动人民的领袖和导师，我们敬爱的斯大林同志病逝的消息，中国铁路工人万分悲痛。这是苏联人民、中国人民和全世界劳动人民的巨大不幸。我们中国铁路工人一定在毛泽东主席和中国共产党的领导下，更紧密地团结一致，加倍努力劳动，加紧学习苏联，建设祖国，巩固国防；永远忠诚地遵循斯大林同志的教导，更加紧密地和苏联人民团结一致，提高警惕，为保卫世界和平与安全的神圣事业奋斗到底！
    中国铁路工会全国委员会
