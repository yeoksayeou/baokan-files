Title: Condolence Telegram from the Preparatory Committee of the China Political Science and Law Society
Author:
Date: 1953-03-17
Page: 3

To our Comrades in the Political and Legal Work in Czechoslovakia:

With utmost grief, we learned of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Gottwald, the great leader of the Czechoslovak people, the close friend of the Chinese people, and the outstanding fighter for the world peace and democracy cause. We extend our deepest condolences to you.

The political and legal workers of the People's Republic of China, with sincere mourning for Comrade Gottwald, will unite even more closely with the political and legal workers of Czechoslovakia, heighten vigilance, and fight to the end to defend the cause of world peace and democracy.

						The Preparatory Committee of the China Political Science and Law Society
						March 15, 1953, Beijing

### 中国政治法律学会筹备会的唁电

1953-03-17
第3版()
专栏：

    中国政治法律学会筹备会的唁电捷克斯洛伐克政治法律工作者同志们：
    惊悉捷克斯洛伐克人民的伟大领袖、中国人民的亲密朋友、世界和平民主事业的杰出战士哥特瓦尔德同志不幸逝世，我们以极悲痛的心情，特向您们致以最沉痛的哀悼。
    中华人民共和国的政治法律工作者，怀着对哥特瓦尔德同志的真诚悼念，将与捷克斯洛伐克的政治法律工作者更加紧密地团结一致，提高警惕，为保卫世界和平民主事业而奋斗到底。
                        中国政治法律学会筹备会
                        一九五三年三月十五日北京
