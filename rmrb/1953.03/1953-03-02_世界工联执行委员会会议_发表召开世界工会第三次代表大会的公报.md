Title: WFTU Executive Committee Meeting Issues Communiqué Announcing the Third World Trade Union Congress
Author:
Date: 1953-03-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, 28th – TASS: The Executive Committee of the World Federation of Trade Unions has issued a special communiqué regarding the convening of the Third World Trade Union Congress.

The communiqué states: "The Executive Committee of the World Federation of Trade Unions, at its meeting held from February 25th to February 28th, 1953, studied the proposals put forward by the trade unions of many countries and decided to convene the Third World Trade Union Congress in Vienna in October 1953.

The preliminary agenda of the Congress includes the following issues:

	1. Report on the activities of the WFTU and the tasks of trade unions in strengthening the unity of action of the working people in the struggle to raise living standards and strive for peace.

	2. The tasks of trade unions in capitalist and colonial countries in defending democratic freedoms and national independence.

	3. Developing the trade union movement in colonial and dependent countries.

	4. Election of the leading bodies of the WFTU.

In order to have a clearer understanding of the needs of the working masses and to ensure that this Congress is conducted on a broad basis, the Executive Committee has authorized the Secretariat of the WFTU to ascertain the opinions of the national trade unions and all trade union organizations supporting the WFTU regarding the above agenda, and to convene a meeting of the Executive Bureau on April 20th of this year to formulate various measures for preparing and conducting the Congress, taking into account the opinions put forward by the trade union organizations."

### 世界工联执行委员会会议  发表召开世界工会第三次代表大会的公报

1953-03-02
第4版()
专栏：

    世界工联执行委员会会议
    发表召开世界工会第三次代表大会的公报
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社维也纳二十八日讯：世界工会联合会执行委员会会议发表了关于召开世界工会第三次代表大会的特别公报。
    公报说：“世界工联执行委员会在一九五三年二月二十五日到二月二十八日举行的会议上，研究了许多国家工会提出的建议，决定于一九五三年十月在维也纳召开世界工会第三次代表大会。
    代表大会的初步议程包括下列各项问题：
    一、关于世界工联的活动及工会今后在争取提高生活水准与争取和平的斗争中加强劳动人民行动统一的任务的报告。
    二、工会在资本主义国家与殖民地国家保卫民主自由与民族独立的任务。
    三、开展殖民地与附属国家中的工会运动。
    四、选举世界工联的领导机构。
    为了更加清楚地了解劳动群众的需要，并使这次代表大会在广泛的基础上进行，执行委员会授权世界工联书记处去了解各国工会与所有支持世界工联的工会组织对于上述议程的意见，并在今年四月二十日召开执行局会议，参照各工会组织提出的意见，制订准备与进行代表大会的各项措施。”
