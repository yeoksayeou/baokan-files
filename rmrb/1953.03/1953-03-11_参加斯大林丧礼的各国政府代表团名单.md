Title: List of Government Delegations from Various Countries Attending Stalin's Funeral
Author:
Date: 1953-03-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 10th (TASS): The following is a list of foreign government delegations attending the funeral of Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, J.V. Stalin:

    The delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; the delegation of the Polish People's Republic, led by Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Bolesław Bierut; the delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, led by President Klement Gottwald; the delegation of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, led by Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Valko Chervenkov; the delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic, led by Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Mátyás Rákosi; the delegation of the Romanian People's Republic, led by Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej; the delegation of the Albanian People's Republic, led by Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Spiro Koleka; the delegation of the German Democratic Republic, led by Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl; the delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, led by Prime Minister Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal; the delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Pak Jeong-ae; the delegation of the Republic of Finland, led by Prime Minister Urho Kekkonen; the delegation of Iran, led by Labor Minister Arsalan Alam; the delegation of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, led by Prince Mohammed Daoud Khan, representative of the King of Afghanistan.

    The governments of the United Kingdom, France, the United States, India, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Union of Burma, Switzerland, Australia, Egypt, Denmark, Norway, Austria, Sweden, Canada, Pakistan, Thailand, the Netherlands, Belgium, Italy, Abyssinia, Iraq, Lebanon, Argentina, Greece, Luxembourg, Mexico, Yugoslavia and other countries formally authorized their ambassadors, ministers or chargés d'affaires to the Soviet Union to represent their governments at the funeral of Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, J.V. Stalin. The governments of New Zealand and Ceylon were represented by the British Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and the government of Iceland was represented by the Swedish Ambassador to the Soviet Union.

    The delegation of the Government of the Republic of Turkey, headed by Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, D. Asikalin, laid a wreath before Stalin's remains. (Picture attached)
    Our delegation attended the vigil on the evening of March 8th. From the second person on the right: Zhou Enlai, Guo Moruo, Li Fuchun.
                    (Xinhua News Agency manuscript, TASS radiotelegraph)

### 参加斯大林丧礼的各国政府代表团名单

1953-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    参加斯大林丧礼的各国政府代表团名单
   【新华社十日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十日讯：参加苏联部长会议主席约·维·斯大林丧礼的外国政府代表团的名单如下：
    由中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来率领的中华人民共和国代表团，由部长会议主席波·贝鲁特率领的波兰人民共和国代表团，由总统克·哥特瓦尔德率领的捷克斯洛伐克共和国代表团，由部长会议主席伏·契尔文科夫率领的保加利亚人民共和国代表团，由部长会议主席马·拉科西率领的匈牙利人民共和国代表团，由部长会议主席格·乔治乌—德治率领的罗马尼亚人民共和国代表团，由部长会议副主席斯·科列加率领的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国代表团，由总理奥·格罗提渥率领的德意志民主共和国代表团，由总理尤·泽登巴尔率领的蒙古人民共和国代表团，由朴正爱率领的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团，由总理乌·吉科宁率领的芬兰共和国代表团，由劳工大臣阿勒姆率领的伊朗代表团，由阿富汗国王的代表穆罕默德·达乌德汗亲王率领的阿富汗王国代表团。
    英国、法国、美国、印度、越南民主共和国、缅甸联邦、瑞士、澳大利亚、埃及、丹麦、挪威、奥地利、瑞典、加拿大、巴基斯坦、泰国、荷兰、比利时、意大利、阿比西尼亚、伊拉克、黎巴嫩、阿根廷、希腊、卢森堡、墨西哥、南斯拉夫等国的政府正式授权它们驻苏联的大使、公使或临时代办代表他们的政府参加苏联部长会议主席约·维·斯大林的丧礼。新西兰和锡兰政府由英国驻苏联大使代表，冰岛政府由瑞典驻苏联大使代表。
    以外交部秘书长德·阿西卡林为首的土耳其共和国政府代表团在斯大林的灵前献了花圈。（附图片）
    我国代表团三月八日晚参加守灵，右第二人起为周恩来、郭沫若、李富春。
                    （新华社稿　塔斯社无线电传真）
