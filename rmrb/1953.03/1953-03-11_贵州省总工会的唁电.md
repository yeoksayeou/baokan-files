Title: Condolence Message from the Guizhou Provincial Federation of Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1953-03-11
Page: 3

Condolence Message from the Guizhou Provincial Federation of Trade Unions to the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions:
    Upon hearing the tragic news of the passing of Comrade Stalin, the most beloved great leader and teacher of the working people of the world, the workers of our entire province express our deepest condolences with immense grief.
    The name of the great Stalin will forever remain in our hearts, inspiring us. Under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Comrade Mao Zedong, the workers of our entire province will certainly intensify production with the greatest unity, firmness, and vigilance, continue to support the Korean War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea, strike against the instigators of war, and make our greatest efforts to consolidate the world peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union.
                                China Guizhou Provincial Federation of Trade Unions
                                March 7, 1953

### 贵州省总工会的唁电

1953-03-11
第3版()
专栏：

    贵州省总工会的唁电全苏工会中央理事会：
    惊闻全世界劳动人民最亲爱的伟大领袖和导师斯大林同志不幸逝世，我们全省职工怀着无比悲痛的心情，谨致最深切的哀悼。
    伟大的斯大林的名字将永远留在我们的心中，鼓舞着我们。我们全省职工在中国共产党和毛泽东同志的领导下，一定以最大的团结一致、坚定性与警惕性，加紧生产，继续支援抗美援朝，打击战争挑拨者，为巩固以苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营而尽最大的努力。
                                中国贵州省总工会
                                一九五三年三月七日
