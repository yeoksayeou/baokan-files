Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Returns to China from Moscow
Author:
Date: 1953-03-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 25th: Premier Zhou Enlai, head of the People's Republic of China delegation that went to Moscow to mourn the death of Comrade Stalin, returned to China by special plane from Moscow on the morning of the 24th. Accompanying him were delegation members: Zhang Wentian, China's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union, Luo Ruiqing, Minister of Public Security of the Central People's Government, and Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, among others.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Malik, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Kuznetsov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Fedlin, Director of the First Far East Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Kurazhenkov, Director of the Protocol Department. Zedanbal, Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, also went to the airport to see them off.

Li Fuchun and Ye Jizhuang, Vice Chairmen of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government, Song Shaowen, Secretary-General, and Ge Baoquan, Xu Tiemin, Li Tao, Li Qiang, and Military Attaché Major General Ji He, all from the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, who are currently in Moscow, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 周恩来总理离莫斯科回国

1953-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理离莫斯科回国
    【新华社莫斯科二十五日电】为吊唁斯大林同志逝世而前往莫斯科的中华人民共和国代表团团长周恩来总理在二十四日晨自莫斯科乘专机返国。同行的有代表团团员：我国驻苏联特命全权大使张闻天、中央人民政府公安部部长罗瑞卿、人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长张宗逊等人。前往机场送行的有：苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部部长莫洛托夫，苏联外交部第一副部长马立克，苏联外交部副部长库兹涅佐夫，苏联外交部第一远东司司长费德林和交际司司长库拉仁科夫。到机场送行的还有蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔。现在莫斯科的中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会副主任李富春、叶季壮，秘书长宋劭文，和我国驻苏联大使馆参赞戈宝权、许铁民、李滔、李强及武官吉合少将等人也前往机场送行。
