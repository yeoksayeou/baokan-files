Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-03-12
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    This is Wu YuanYu, a member of the Communist Youth League and the production commander of the No. 1 blast furnace at the Anshan Iron and Steel Works, our country's first female blast furnace chief. She is using a colored glass mirror to observe the furnace temperature through the tuyere sight hole.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-03-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    这是我国第一个女炼铁炉长——鞍山炼铁厂第一号炼铁炉的生产指挥者、青年团员吴元愉。她正在用有色玻璃镜子从风口视孔观察炉温。
