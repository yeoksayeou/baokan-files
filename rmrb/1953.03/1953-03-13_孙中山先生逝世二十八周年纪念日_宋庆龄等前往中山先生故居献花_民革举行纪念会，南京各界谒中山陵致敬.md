Title: 28th Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Death: Soong Ching-ling and Others Lay Wreaths at Sun Yat-sen's Former Residence; Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang Holds Memorial Meeting; Various Circles in Nanjing Pay Respects at Sun Yat-sen's Mausoleum
Author:
Date: 1953-03-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 12th: March 12th marked the 28th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's death. Soong Ching-ling, Vice Chairman, currently in Shanghai, laid a wreath at Sun Yat-sen's former residence this morning. Zhang Dingcheng, Vice Chairman of the East China Administrative Council, Chen Yi, Mayor of Shanghai, Pan Hannian, Sheng Pi Hua, Vice Mayors; representatives of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang and other democratic parties in Shanghai; representatives of the Shanghai People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee; and representatives of various Shanghai people's organizations, including Jin Zhonghua, Wu Yi Wu, Zhao Zu Kang, Chen Si Sheng, and Li Zhao Huan, also went to lay wreaths and pay their respects successively.
    Xinhua News Agency, 12th: The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang held a meeting on the 12th to commemorate the 28th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's death. Attending the memorial meeting were Li Jishen, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang, Central Committee members He Xiangning, Liu Yazi, Shao Lizi, Chen Shaoxian, Cai Tingkai, Chen Qiyuan, Mei Gongbin, Zhu Yunshan, Wang Kunlun, Zhu Xuefan, Long Yun, etc., and united committee members Qu Wu, Zhou Binglin, etc., as well as committee members and party members from the Beijing branch, totaling more than 400 people.

    After the memorial ceremony, Li Jishen spoke. He said: Today, as we commemorate the 28th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's death, it is also the day when people all over the world mourn the death of the great teacher Comrade Stalin, and we feel infinite grief. Then, he gave a detailed report on Sun Yat-sen's revolutionary history and Sun Yat-sen's continuously progressive revolutionary spirit of "taking Russia as a teacher" and learning from the Soviet Union. In his report, he quoted Stalin's telegram of condolence for Sun Yat-sen in 1925, illustrating the Soviet Union's warm support and assistance for the Chinese revolution. Finally, he called on members of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang, in these days of mourning Comrade Stalin and commemorating Sun Yat-sen, to make a hundredfold effort to strengthen their study and work, strengthen unity, and, together with the people of the whole country, under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, give full play to the spirit of patriotism and internationalism, further consolidate and develop the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples, strengthen the world democratic peace camp headed by the Soviet Union, and shatter all the provocations and conspiracies of the imperialist aggressors. He Xiangning then spoke. She described the warm concern and support of the great Lenin and Stalin for the Chinese people, which enabled the Chinese revolution to achieve complete victory. He asked members of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang to continue to follow Sun Yat-sen's legacy of "taking Russia as a teacher," unite with the Soviet people, and fight for the cause of world peace.

    Shao Lizi, Chen Qiyuan, and Yan Rongbing, Standing Committee Member of the Beijing Branch, also spoke at the memorial meeting. They unanimously pointed out that to commemorate the deaths of Sun Yat-sen and Stalin with practical actions, it is necessary to further learn from the Soviet Union and strive to thoroughly complete the three major national tasks of 1953.
    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 12th: People from all walks of life in Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City commemorated the 28th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's death by visiting Sun Yat-sen's Mausoleum and laying wreaths to pay their respects on the morning of the 12th. Those who participated in the mausoleum visit included: Leng Yu, Vice Chairman of the Jiangsu Provincial People's Government, Hui Yuyu, Secretary of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Shao Hengqiu, head of the Nanjing Municipal Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, and members of the provincial and municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committees, representatives of various democratic parties and mass organizations, totaling more than 40 people.

### 孙中山先生逝世二十八周年纪念日  宋庆龄等前往中山先生故居献花  民革举行纪念会，南京各界谒中山陵致敬

1953-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    孙中山先生逝世二十八周年纪念日
    宋庆龄等前往中山先生故居献花
    民革举行纪念会，南京各界谒中山陵致敬
    【新华社上海十二日电】三月十二日是孙中山先生逝世二十八周年纪念日。现在上海的宋庆龄副主席在这天清晨前往中山先生故居献花。华东行政委员会副主席张鼎丞、上海市人民政府市长陈毅、副市长潘汉年、盛丕华，中国国民党革命委员会和其他民主党派上海地方组织的代表，上海市各界人民代表会议协商委员会的代表与上海市各人民团体的代表金仲华、吴艺五、赵祖康、陈巳生、黎照寰等也相继前往献花瞻仰。 【新华社十二日讯】中国国民党革命委员会在十二日集会纪念孙中山先生逝世二十八周年。参加纪念会的有民革中央委员会主席李济深，中央委员何香凝、柳亚子、邵力子、陈劭先、蔡廷锴、陈其瑗、梅龚彬、朱蕴山、王昆仑、朱学范、龙云等和团结委员屈武、周炳琳等以及北京市分部委员、党员共四百余人。
    在举行纪念仪式后，由李济深讲话。他说：我们今天纪念孙中山先生逝世二十八周年，正是全世界人民悼念伟大导师斯大林同志逝世的日子，我们感到无限沉痛。接着，他详细报告了孙中山先生的革命历史和孙中山先生“以俄为师”，向苏联学习的不断进步的革命精神。他在报告中引录了斯大林在一九二五年吊唁孙中山先生的电文，说明苏联对中国革命事业的热烈支持和援助。最后，他号召民革党员在哀悼斯大林同志和纪念孙中山先生的日子里，应当百倍努力，加强学习和工作，加强团结，和全国人民一起，在毛主席和中国共产党的领导下，发挥爱国主义和国际主义的精神，进一步巩固和发展中苏两国人民的友谊，加强以苏联为首的世界民主和平阵营，粉碎帝国主义侵略者的一切挑拨阴谋。何香凝接着讲话。她叙述了伟大的列宁、斯大林对于中国人民的热烈关怀和支持，使得中国的革命取得了完全胜利。他要求民革党员继续遵循孙中山先生“以俄为师”的遗教，和苏联人民团结一致，为全世界的和平事业而斗争。
    在纪念会上讲话的还有邵力子、陈其瑗和北京市分部常务委员阎熔冰。他们一致指出：要以实际行动来纪念孙中山和斯大林的逝世，要进一步向苏联学习，为彻底完成一九五三年的国家三大任务而努力。
    【新华社南京十二日电】江苏省、南京市各界人民纪念孙中山先生逝世二十八周年，在十二日上午到中山陵谒陵并献花圈致敬。参加谒陵的有：江苏省人民政府副主席冷遹，中共南京市委员会书记惠浴宇，中国国民党革命委员会南京市分部负责人邵恒秋和省、市各界人民代表会议协商委员会委员，各民主党派、各群众团体代表等，共四十多人。
