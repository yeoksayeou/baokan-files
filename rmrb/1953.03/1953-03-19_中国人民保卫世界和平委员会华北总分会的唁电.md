Title: Condolences from the North China General Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace

Author:

Date: 1953-03-19
Page: 2

Condolences from the North China General Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace to the Czechoslovak Committee for the Defense of Peace:

    We are deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of President Gottwald, the great leader of the Czechoslovak people and outstanding fighter for the cause of world peace and democracy. We, on behalf of the peace-loving people of North China, extend our deepest condolences! We are determined to unite more closely with the Czechoslovak people, consolidate the peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union, oppose the aggression of American imperialism, and tirelessly strive to defend world peace!
      North China General Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace

### 中国人民保卫世界和平委员会华北总分会的唁电

1953-03-19
第2版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会华北总分会的唁电捷克斯洛伐克保卫和平委员会：
    惊悉捷克斯洛伐克人民的伟大领袖、世界和平民主事业的卓越战士哥特瓦尔德总统不幸病逝的消息，谨代表中国华北区爱好和平的人民致以沉痛的悼唁！我们坚决和捷克斯洛伐克人民更加紧密团结，为巩固以苏联为首的和平民主阵营、反对美国帝国主义的侵略、保卫世界和平而奋斗不懈！
      中国人民保卫世界和平委员会华北总分会
