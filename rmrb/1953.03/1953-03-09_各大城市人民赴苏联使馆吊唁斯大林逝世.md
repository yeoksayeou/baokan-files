Title: People in Major Cities Visit Soviet Embassies to Mourn Stalin's Death
Author:
Date: 1953-03-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: People in eight cities, including the capital, Tianjin, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenyang, Dalian, Harbin, and Dihua, went to the Soviet Embassy, Consulates-General, and Consulates to mourn the death of Comrade Stalin. From 3:00 PM on the 6th to the evening of the 8th, more than 1,000 units in the capital, including various democratic parties, people's organizations, factories and enterprises, government agencies, schools, suburban farmers, and various units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, totaling approximately 200,000 people, went to the Soviet Embassy to offer their condolences. In Tianjin, more than 30,000 people from all walks of life went to the Soviet Consulate-General in Tianjin on the 8th to mourn the death of Comrade Stalin. In Shanghai, a total of 40,000 people went to the Soviet Consulate-General in Shanghai on the 8th to express their condolences. In Guangzhou, more than 50,000 people went to the Soviet Consulate in Guangzhou on the 8th to express their deep condolences. In Shenyang, cadres from government organs and people from all walks of life continued to visit the Soviet Consulate-General in Shenyang on the 8th, with the number of mourners reaching 15,800. In Dalian, from the afternoon of the 7th to the 8th, more than 6,000 people from all walks of life went to the Soviet Consulate-General in Lushun-Dalian to mourn the death of Comrade Stalin. In Harbin, more than 20,000 people went to the Soviet Consulate-General in Harbin on the 8th to offer condolences, and the number of mourners, including those on the 7th, has exceeded 30,000. Representatives of various agencies, organizations, and working people in Xinjiang Province and Dihua City lined up on the afternoon of the 7th to go to the Soviet Consulate-General in Dihua to pay their respects to Stalin, the great leader of the working people of the world. From 3:30 PM to 8:00 PM, the number of mourners totaled more than 6,000.

### 各大城市人民赴苏联使馆吊唁斯大林逝世

1953-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

    各大城市人民赴苏联使馆吊唁斯大林逝世
    据新华社讯：首都及天津、上海、广州、沈阳、大连、哈尔滨、迪化等八个城市的成千上万的人民，前往苏联大使馆和总领事馆、领事馆哀悼斯大林同志的逝世。自六日下午三时开始，到八日晚间止，首都各界包括各民主党派、各人民团体、工厂企业、政府机关、学校、郊区农民以及中国人民解放军各部队等一千余单位，共约有二十万人前往苏联大使馆致悼。天津市八日到苏联驻天津总领事馆哀悼斯大林同志逝世的各界群众有三万多人。上海市八日前往苏联驻上海总领事馆表示哀悼的群众共有四万人。广州市八日有五万多人前往苏联驻广州领事馆表示沉痛的哀悼。沈阳市机关干部和各界人民八日继续前往苏联驻沈阳总领事馆吊唁的人数达一万五千八百人。大连从七日下午到八日的一天半中，前往苏联驻旅大总领事馆痛悼斯大林同志逝世的各界人民有六千多人。哈尔滨市八日有两万多人往苏联驻哈尔滨总领事馆吊唁，连七日吊唁的人数在内，已超过三万人。新疆省和迪化市各机关、团体和劳动人民代表，在七日下午列队往苏联驻迪化总领事馆，向全世界劳动人民的伟大领袖斯大林致哀。从下午三时半到晚间八时止，致哀者共达六千多人。
