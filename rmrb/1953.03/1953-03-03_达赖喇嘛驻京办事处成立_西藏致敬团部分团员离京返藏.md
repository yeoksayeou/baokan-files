Title: Dalai Lama's Beijing Office Established; Part of the Tibetan Tribute Delegation Departs Beijing for Tibet
Author:
Date: 1953-03-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency Report:

    The Dalai Lama's Beijing Office was established on February 24th, and a ceremony was held to mark the occasion. Liu Geping, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, and others attended to offer congratulations. Liu Xia·Tudeng Tabai, head of the Tibetan Tribute Delegation, also participated in the ceremony.

    Ranba·Nangji Wangdui, Director of the Dalai Lama's Beijing Office, reported on the preparations for the office's establishment at the ceremony. Liu Geping, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, delivered a congratulatory speech. Liu Xia·Tudeng Tabai, head of the Tibetan Tribute Delegation, also spoke. He encouraged the office staff to work actively under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the Central People's Government.

    Furthermore, part of the Tibetan Tribute Delegation, led by Ouxie·Tudeng Sangqu, Mendui·Losang Wangdui, and Losang Chilei, left Beijing by train on February 28th to return to Tibet. Liu Geping, Wang Feng, Liu Chun, and others, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, were at the station to see them off.

### 达赖喇嘛驻京办事处成立  西藏致敬团部分团员离京返藏

1953-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛驻京办事处成立
    西藏致敬团部分团员离京返藏
    据新华社讯：西藏达赖喇嘛驻京办事处在二月二十四日成立，并举行成立典礼。中央人民政府民族事务委员会副主任委员刘格平等前往祝贺。西藏致敬团团长柳霞·土登塔巴也参加了成立典礼。
    达赖驻京办事处处长然巴·囊吉旺堆在成立典礼上报告了办事处的筹备经过。中央民族事务委员会副主任委员刘格平致词祝贺。西藏致敬团团长柳霞·土登塔巴也讲了话。他勉励办事处人员在中国共产党、中央人民政府的领导下，积极工作。
    又讯：西藏致敬团部分团员由欧协·土登桑曲、门堆·洛桑旺堆、洛桑池来率领，在二月二十八日乘火车离北京返西藏。到车站欢送的有中央人民政府民族事务委员会副主任委员刘格平、汪锋、刘春等。
