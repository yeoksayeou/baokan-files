Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-03-10
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In mourning the passing of Comrade Stalin, our steelworkers have demonstrated strong perseverance and determination. The model workshop group of the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant – the Li Youzhi Group of the No. 1 Blast Furnace – is shown here taking an oath. They are resolutely guaranteeing to strive to fulfill the 1953 production plan.

### 我们伟大祖国（图片）

1953-03-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大祖国
    在哀悼斯大林同志逝世的时候，我国钢铁工人表示了坚强的毅力和决心。石景山钢铁厂的模范车间小组——第一高炉李友枝小组正在这里宣誓。他们坚决保证为完成一九五三年的生产计划而奋斗。
