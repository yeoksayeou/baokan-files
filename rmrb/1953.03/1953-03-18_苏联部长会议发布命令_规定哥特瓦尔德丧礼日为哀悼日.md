Title: Order Issued by the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union: Gottwald's Funeral Day to be Designated as a Day of Mourning
Author:
Date: 1953-03-18
Page: 1

    Order Issued by the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union
    Gottwald's Funeral Day to be Designated as a Day of Mourning
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 17th] TASS, Moscow, March 17th: The Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union has ordered: that March 19th, the day of the funeral of the President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Klement Gottwald, be designated as a day of mourning, and that flags be flown at half-mast as a sign of mourning.

### 苏联部长会议发布命令  规定哥特瓦尔德丧礼日为哀悼日

1953-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联部长会议发布命令
    规定哥特瓦尔德丧礼日为哀悼日
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十七日电：苏联部长会议命令：规定三月十九日捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统克利门特·哥特瓦尔德的丧礼日为哀悼日，并规定下半旗志哀。
