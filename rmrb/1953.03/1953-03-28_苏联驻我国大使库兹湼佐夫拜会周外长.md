Title: Soviet Ambassador to China Kuznetsov calls on Foreign Minister Zhou

Author:

Date: 1953-03-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 27th: The newly appointed Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Kuznetsov, accompanied by the former Soviet Ambassador to China, Panyushkin, called on Minister Zhou Enlai of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government on the afternoon of the 27th to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Also present were: Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Director of the General Office Yan Baohang, Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Affairs Department Xu Yixin, and Director of the Protocol Department Wang Zhuoru.

### 苏联驻我国大使库兹湼佐夫拜会周外长

1953-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联驻我国大使库兹湼佐夫拜会周外长
    【新华社二十七日讯】苏联新任驻我国特命全权大使库兹湼佐夫由苏联前任驻我国大使潘友新陪同，在二十七日下午到中央人民政府外交部拜会周恩来部长，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座的还有：外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，办公厅副主任阎宝航，苏联东欧司司长徐以新和交际处处长王倬如。
