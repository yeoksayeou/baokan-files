Title: Condolences from the Guangxi Provincial Trade Union

Author:

Date: 1953-03-11
Page: 3

Telegram of Condolence from the Guangxi Provincial Trade Union to the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions:

    We are deeply grieved to hear the unfortunate news of the passing of Comrade J.V. Stalin, the brilliant leader and great teacher of the working class and working people of the world. We extend our deepest condolences to the Soviet working class and all working people on behalf of all the workers of Guangxi Province, China. We will forever follow the path indicated by Comrade Stalin, and under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, we will unite even more closely with you—our closest friends of the Chinese people—to fight to the end for the victorious construction of our motherland and for the defense of world peace.

                                The Guangxi Provincial Trade Union of China
                                March 8, 1953

### 广西省总工会的唁电

1953-03-11
第3版()
专栏：

    广西省总工会的唁电全苏工会中央理事会：
    惊闻全世界工人阶级和劳动人民天才的领袖和伟大的导师约·维·斯大林同志逝世的不幸消息，我们感到万分的哀痛。谨代表中国广西省全体工人向苏联工人阶级和全体劳动人民致以最深切的吊唁。我们永远遵循斯大林同志所指示的道路，在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下，和您们——我们中国人民最亲密的朋友，更加紧密地团结在一起，为胜利地建设自己的祖国，为保卫世界和平而奋斗到底。
                                中国广西省总工会
                                一九五三年三月八日
