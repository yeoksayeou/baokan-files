Title: Condolences from All Commanders and Fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy
Author:
Date: 1953-03-19
Page: 2

    To the Gottwald Funeral Committee:
    Upon hearing the news of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Klement Gottwald, the great leader of the Czechoslovak people and a close friend of the Chinese people, all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy feel deep sorrow and express our sincere condolences. Under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong and the Chinese Communist Party, we will unite closely with the Czechoslovak people and their heroic army to fight for the common cause of opposing imperialist aggression and defending world peace.
      All Commanders and Fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy

### 中国人民解放军海军全体指挥员、战斗员的唁电

1953-03-19
第2版()
专栏：

    中国人民解放军海军全体指挥员、战斗员的唁电哥特瓦尔德同志治丧委员会：
    捷克斯洛伐克人民的伟大领袖、中国人民的亲密朋友克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志不幸逝世的消息传来，中国人民解放军海军全体指挥员和战斗员感到深切的悲痛，谨致衷心的哀悼。我们在毛泽东主席和中国共产党的领导下，将与捷克斯洛伐克人民及其英雄军队紧密团结，为反对帝国主义侵略、保卫世界和平的共同事业而奋斗。
      中国人民解放军海军全体指挥员、战斗员
