Title: The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and the Czechoslovak Government Announce National Mourning; Committee for the Funeral of Comrade Gottwald Established
Author:
Date: 1953-03-16
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 15th: TASS, Prague, March 14th: According to a report from the Czechoslovak News Agency: The Presidium of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic jointly held an emergency meeting on the 14th. The meeting was chaired by Prime Minister Antonín Zápotocký. The meeting decided to announce a period of national mourning until the day of the funeral (including the day of the funeral). During the national mourning period, the national flag and red flags will be flown at half-mast to express grief. During the national mourning period, all performances in theaters and cinemas, concerts, cultural and recreational activities, sports competitions, and entertainment will be suspended.
    The Committee for the Funeral of the President of the Czechoslovak Republic and Chairman of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, Comrade Klement Gottwald, has been established. The committee is composed of the following: Chairman of the Committee: A. Zápotocký; Committee members: V. Široký, K. Bacílek, A. Čepička, A. Novotný, J. Plojhara, E. Šlechta, G. Kliment, B. Köhler; Secretary of the Committee: R. Hrazdec.

### 捷共中央和捷政府宣布全国志哀  哥特瓦尔德同志治丧委员会成立

1953-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    捷共中央和捷政府宣布全国志哀
    哥特瓦尔德同志治丧委员会成立
   【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社布拉格十四日讯：据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社消息：捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会主席团和捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府十四日联合举行紧急会议。会议由安托宁·萨波托斯基总理任主席。会上决定宣布全国志哀期间到举行丧礼日为止（包括葬礼日在内）。在全国志哀期间，国旗和红旗下半旗志哀。在全国志哀期间，各剧院和电影院的演出、音乐会、文娱活动、体育比赛和娱乐一律停止。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统、捷克斯洛伐克共产党主席克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志的治丧委员会已经成立。委员会由下列诸人组成：委员会主席安·萨波托斯基；委员会委员：威·西罗基，卡·巴契列克，阿·契皮茨卡，安·诺沃提尼，约·普洛伊哈，埃·斯列赫塔，古·克利门特，勃·凯勒，委员会秘书鲁·赫拉捷茨。
