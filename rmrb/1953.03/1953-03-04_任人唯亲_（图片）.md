Title: "Nepotism" (Image)
Author: Hua Junwu
Date: 1953-03-04
Page: 3

    "Nepotism"
    "Dad! You're in charge, this is my eldest uncle, this is my second uncle, this is my third aunt, this is my fifth uncle-in-law, this is my third cousin, this is my youngest uncle... Oh, why isn't the uncle who sent us a lot of ham and tea last time here?"
    "He came later."
    By Hua Junwu

### “任人唯亲”（图片）
华君武
1953-03-04
第3版()
专栏：

    “任人唯亲”

    “他是后来的。”
