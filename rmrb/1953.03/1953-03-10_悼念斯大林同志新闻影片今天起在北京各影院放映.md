Title: Newsreel Commemorating Comrade Stalin to be Shown in Beijing Cinemas Starting Today
Author:
Date: 1953-03-10
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 9th: Starting from the 10th, Beijing cinemas will screen a special newsreel entitled "Mourning Our Most Beloved Teacher, the Great Comrade Stalin." The film, produced by the Beijing Film Studio, includes the following content: Chairman Mao Zedong and others going to the Soviet Embassy in China to express condolences; representatives from all sectors of the capital going to the Soviet Embassy in China to express condolences; the delegation of the People's Republic of China participating in Chairman Stalin's funeral departing the country; and the scene of millions of people in the capital deeply mourning Chairman Stalin.

### 悼念斯大林同志新闻影片今天起在北京各影院放映

1953-03-10
第1版()
专栏：

    悼念斯大林同志新闻影片今天起在北京各影院放映
    【新华社九日讯】从十日起，北京各电影院将放映“痛悼我们最敬爱的导师伟大的斯大林同志”新闻影片特辑。该片由北京电影制片厂摄制，内容包括：毛泽东主席等赴苏联驻我国大使馆哀悼、首都各界人民代表赴苏联驻我国大使馆哀悼、参加斯大林主席葬仪的中华人民共和国代表团出国以及首都千百万人民沉痛哀悼斯大林主席的情景。
