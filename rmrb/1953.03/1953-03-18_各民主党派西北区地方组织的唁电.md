Title: Condolence Telegrams from Local Organizations of Democratic Parties in the Northwest Region
Author: 
Date: 1953-03-18
Page: 3

Condolence Telegram from Local Organizations of Democratic Parties in the Northwest Region to the Presidium of the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic:
    We are deeply saddened to hear of the passing of President Gottwald. We, all members of the democratic parties in the Northwest Region of China, extend our deepest condolences! We firmly believe that in these sorrowful days, the people of Czechoslovakia will be even more united, follow the path indicated by President Gottwald, rally around the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, and build and defend their country. We pledge, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, to unite closely with the people of Czechoslovakia and strive to defend world peace!
        Northwest General Branch of the China Democratic League, Xi'an Branch of the China Democratic League, Preparatory Committee of the Shaanxi Provincial Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, Xi'an Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, Preparatory Office of the Xi'an Branch of the Jiusan Society
                                March 15, 1953

### 各民主党派西北区地方组织的唁电

1953-03-18
第3版()
专栏：

    各民主党派西北区地方组织的唁电捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府主席团：
    惊悉哥特瓦尔德总统逝世，我们中国各民主党派在西北区的全体人员，谨致深切的哀悼！我们深信：捷克斯洛伐克的人民，在这悲痛的日子里，将更能团结一致，遵循着哥特瓦尔德总统所指示的道路，团结在捷克斯洛伐克共产党的周围，建设并保卫自己的国家。我们誓在中国共产党领导之下，紧密地与捷克斯洛伐克人民团结在一起，为保卫世界和平而努力奋斗！
        中国民主同盟西北总支部、中国民主同盟西
      安市支部、中国国民党革命委员会陕西省分部
      筹备委员会、中国民主建国会西安市分会、九
      三学社西安分社筹备处
                                一九五三年三月十五日
