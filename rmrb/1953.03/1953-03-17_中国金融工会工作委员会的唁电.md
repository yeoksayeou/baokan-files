Title: Condolence Message from the Working Committee of the Chinese Financial Workers' Union
Author:
Date: 1953-03-17
Page: 3

Condolence Message from the Working Committee of the Chinese Financial Workers' Union
To the Central Council of Czechoslovak Trade Unions, transferred to the Czechoslovak Financial Workers' Union:

    We are deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Gottwald. On behalf of the Chinese financial workers, we extend our deepest condolences to you.

    To mourn our close friend Comrade Gottwald, all Chinese financial workers will further unite closely with the people of Czechoslovakia and jointly strive to defend world peace.

                        Working Committee of the Chinese Financial Workers' Union
                                March 15, 1953

### 中国金融工会工作委员会的唁电

1953-03-17
第3版()
专栏：

    中国金融工会工作委员会的唁电捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会转捷克斯洛伐克金融职工工会：
    惊悉哥特瓦尔德同志不幸逝世的消息，我们怀着悲痛的心情，谨代表中国金融职工，向你们致以深切的哀悼。
    为了悼念我们亲密的朋友哥特瓦尔德同志，中国全体金融职工，将更进一步与捷克斯洛伐克人民紧密地团结一致，共同为保卫世界和平而奋斗。
                        中国金融工会工作委员会
                                一九五三年三月十五日
