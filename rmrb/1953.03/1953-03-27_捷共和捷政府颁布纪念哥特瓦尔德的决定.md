Title: Czechoslovak Republic and Czechoslovak Government Issue Decision to Commemorate Gottwald
Author:
Date: 1953-03-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 25th: The Czechoslovak News Agency reported that the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia and the Presidium of the Government of Czechoslovakia have decided to establish a K. Gottwald Museum and a K. Gottwald Monument in Prague, and to establish a special committee to preside over this work. The chairman of the committee is A. Zapotocky; the vice-chairman is V. Siroky.

Furthermore, the Ostrava Regional Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, the Regional National Committee, and the Action Committee of the Regional National Front have also decided to establish a K. Gottwald Monument in Ostrava, one of the centers of the Czechoslovak steel industry.

### 捷共和捷政府颁布纪念哥特瓦尔德的决定

1953-03-27
第4版()
专栏：

    捷共和捷政府颁布纪念哥特瓦尔德的决定
    【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社布拉格二十五日讯：捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道：捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会主席团和捷克斯洛伐克政府主席团已决定在布拉格建立一个克·哥特瓦尔德博物馆和一个克·哥特瓦尔德纪念碑，并成立了一个专门委员会来主持这项工作。委员会的主席是安·萨波托斯基；副主席是威·西罗基。
    又讯：捷克斯洛伐克共产党俄斯特拉发州委员会、州民族委员会、州民族阵线行动委员会也已决定在捷克斯洛伐克钢铁工业中心之一——俄斯特拉发建立克·哥特瓦尔德纪念碑。
