Title: The Soviet Union Decides to Build a Memorial Hall to Enshrine the Remains of Lenin and Stalin
Author:
Date: 1953-03-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 7th. TASS, Moscow, March 7th dispatch: Decision of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union on building a memorial hall to commemorate the immortal glory of the great people of the Soviet homeland

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union hereby decide:

	In order to permanently commemorate the great leaders Vladimir Ilyich Lenin and Joseph Vissarionovich Stalin, and to enshrine the other outstanding leaders of the Communist Party and the Soviet state under the Kremlin wall in Red Square, a monumental building—the Memorial Hall—will be built in Moscow to commemorate the immortal glory of the great people of the Soviet homeland.

	Once the Memorial Hall is completed, the crystal coffin containing the remains of V.I. Lenin, the crystal coffin containing the remains of J.V. Stalin, and the urns containing the ashes of other outstanding leaders of the Communist Party and the Soviet state enshrined under the Kremlin wall in Red Square will be moved into the hall, and the broad masses of working people will be allowed to enter and pay their respects.

	The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union
	The Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union
	(Signed)

### 苏联决定建筑纪念堂安放列宁斯大林遗体

1953-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联决定建筑纪念堂安放列宁斯大林遗体
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科七日电：苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议关于建筑纪念堂，纪念苏维埃国土的伟大人民的不朽光荣的决定
    苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议兹决定：
    为了永久纪念伟大的领袖乌拉季米尔·伊里奇·列宁和约瑟夫·维萨里奥诺维奇·斯大林以及安放在红场上克里姆林宫墙下的共产党和苏维埃国家其他杰出领袖，在莫斯科建筑一座纪念性的建筑物——纪念堂，纪念苏维埃国土的伟大人民的不朽光荣。
    一俟纪念堂落成，即把装有乌·伊·列宁遗体的水晶棺和装有约·维·斯大林遗体的水晶棺以及安放在红场上克里姆林宫墙下的共产党和苏维埃国家其他杰出领袖的骨灰瓶移入堂内，并让广大的劳动人民入内瞻仰。
    苏联共产党中央委员会
    苏联部长会议
    （签署）
