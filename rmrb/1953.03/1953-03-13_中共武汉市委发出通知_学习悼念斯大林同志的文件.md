Title: CPC Wuhan Municipal Committee Issues Notice to Study Documents Commemorating Comrade Stalin

Author:
Date: 1953-03-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, March 12th: The Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, upon receiving the Central Committee’s notice on studying documents commemorating Comrade Stalin on March 11th, immediately issued a notice in the Yangtze Daily on March 12th, stipulating that Party committees and branches at all levels must immediately comply with and implement the Central Committee's notice. The district committees and industrial committees should provide practical organization and leadership for this study, ensuring that the purpose set by the Central Committee is achieved through study. The Wuhan Municipal Committee of the CPC also decided to issue a notice on March 12th to advanced group trainees, reporters, and theoretical instructors throughout the city, calling on them to first study the designated documents carefully and to earnestly guide cadres throughout the city in learning and discussion. They are also preparing to dispatch reporters to make reports on Comrade Stalin’s life and career to cadres in institutions, workers, and grassroots cadres in the streets. The city's Marxist-Leninist night school will also organize lectures to coordinate with this study.

### 中共武汉市委发出通知  学习悼念斯大林同志的文件

1953-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中共武汉市委发出通知
    学习悼念斯大林同志的文件
     【新华社汉口十二日电】中国共产党武汉市委员会于三月十一日接获中共中央关于学习悼念斯大林同志的文件的通知后，即于十二日长江日报上发出通知，规定各级党委、支部对中央这一通知立即遵照执行，各区委、产业党委对这一学习要加以切实的组织与领导，务期通过学习，达到中央提出的目的。中共武汉市委还决定于十二日向全市高级组学员、报告员、理论教员发出通知，号召他们首先学好规定文件，认真指导全市干部进行学习、讨论；并准备出动报告员向机关干部、工人群众与街道基层干部作关于斯大林同志生平事业的报告。市的马克思列宁主义夜校也将配合这一学习组织讲座。
