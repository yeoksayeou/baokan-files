Title: Telegram of Condolence from the Trade Union Council of Rehe Province
Author:
Date: 1953-03-18
Page: 3

To the Central Council of Trade Unions of Czechoslovakia:

    Upon hearing the sad news of the unfortunate passing of Comrade K. Gottwald, the great leader of the Czechoslovak working people and the most beloved friend of the Chinese people, the entire working class of Rehe Province, China, expresses our deepest condolences to you with utmost grief. We are determined to, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and our wise leader Comrade Mao Zedong, and together with the Czechoslovak and world working class, fight for the common cause of defending world peace forever.

                                The Trade Union Council of Rehe Province, China
                              March 15, 1953

### 热河省总工会的唁电

1953-03-18
第3版()
专栏：

    热河省总工会的唁电捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会：
    惊闻捷克斯洛伐克劳动人民的伟大领袖、中国人民最敬爱的朋友克·哥特瓦尔德同志不幸逝世的消息，中国热河省全体工人在万分悲痛心情中，向你们致以沉痛的哀悼。我们决心在中国共产党和英明领袖毛泽东同志领导下，并和捷克斯洛伐克及全世界工人阶级一道，为保卫世界永久和平的共同事业而奋斗。
                                中国热河省总工会
                              一九五三年三月十五日
