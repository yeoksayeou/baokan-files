Title: Order of the Central People's Government
Author: Mao Zedong
Date: 1953-03-02
Page: 1

Order of the Central People's Government

    The Electoral Law of the National People's Congress and Local People's Congresses of the People's Republic of China is hereby promulgated for implementation.

    Chairman Mao Zedong
    March 1, 1953

### 中央人民政府命令
毛泽东
1953-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府命令
    兹将中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会及地方各级人民代表大会选举法公布施行。
    主席　毛泽东
    一九五三年三月一日
