Title: Local Vietnamese People's Army Forces in the North Vietnamese Plain Area Develop Guerrilla Warfare, Inflicting Heavy Casualties on French Troops
Author:
Date: 1953-03-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 28th: The Vietnam News Agency reported on the 27th that local units of the Vietnamese People's Army continue to develop guerrilla warfare in the North Vietnamese plains and northern Central Vietnam, inflicting heavy casualties on the French army.

    In the area on the left bank of the Red River, in forty days of operations up to January 10th, local units of the People's Army killed or wounded more than 1,300 French troops, and captured two mortars, 116 machine guns, and 531 rifles.

    On the right bank of the Red River, on January 7th, local units of the People's Army repelled several attacks by a French regiment on Phong Village, Ha Nam Province, killing 170 French troops and destroying or damaging 13 French military vehicles.

    Local units of the People's Army operating in northern Central Vietnam also stepped up attacks on French convoys and patrols. In northern Thua Thien alone, local units of the People's Army killed or wounded 250 French troops and destroyed 7 French tanks and trucks in ten days.

### 北越平原地区越南人民军地方部队  发展游击战争大量杀伤法军

1953-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    北越平原地区越南人民军地方部队
    发展游击战争大量杀伤法军
    【新华社二十八日讯】据越南通讯社二十七日讯：越南人民军地方部队继续在北越平原和中越北部地区发展游击战，大量杀伤法军。
    在红河左岸地区，人民军地方部队在截至一月十日为止的四十天作战中，共打死打伤法军一千三百人以上，缴获迫击炮两门、机枪一百一十六挺、步枪五百三十一支。
    在红河右岸，人民军地方部队一月七日击退法军一个团对河南省丰村的几次进攻，打死法军一百七十人，击毁、击伤法国军车十三辆。
    在中越北部作战的人民军地方部队也加紧袭击法军的运输队和巡逻队。仅在承天北部一地，人民军地方部队在十天中就打死打伤法军二百五十人，击毁法军坦克和卡车七辆。
