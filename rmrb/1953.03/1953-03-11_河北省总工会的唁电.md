Title: Condolence Telegram from Hebei Provincial Federation of Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1953-03-11
Page: 2

To the Presidium of the Funeral Committee of Comrade Joseph Vissarionovich Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Secretary of the Central Committe of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Moscow Soviet:

    Upon hearing the shocking news of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Joseph Vissarionovich Stalin, the most beloved and heroic leader of the working people of the world, the brilliant revolutionary teacher and genius successor to the revolutionary theories of Marx, Engels, and Lenin, all staff members of the Hebei Provincial Federation of Trade Unions and all workers in Hebei Province, China, express our deepest condolences with immense grief. The passing of Comrade Stalin is the greatest loss for the working people of the world. We are determined to transform our grief into strength, learn the theories of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin, learn from the construction experience and examples of the Soviet Union, and strive to consolidate the unbreakable fraternal friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union, to build a strong motherland, and to defend the permanent peace of the world.
        All staff of the Hebei Provincial Federation of Trade Unions and all workers of Hebei Province, China
                                March 6, 1953

### 河北省总工会的唁电

1953-03-11
第2版()
专栏：

    河北省总工会的唁电莫斯科苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席、苏联共产党中央委员会书记约瑟夫·维萨里奥诺维奇·斯大林治丧委员会
    惊闻全世界劳动人民最爱戴的英勇领袖，卓越的革命导师马克思、恩格斯、列宁的革命学说的天才继承者——约·维·斯大林同志不幸病逝，中国河北省全体职工及省总工会全体人员，均以万分沉痛的心情，表示最深切的哀悼。斯大林同志的逝世，是全世界劳动人民最巨大的损失。我们决把悲痛化为力量，学习马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林的理论，学习苏联的各项建设经验和榜样，为巩固中苏两国人民牢不可破的兄弟友谊，为建设强大的祖国，为保卫世界的永久和平而奋斗。
        中国河北省总工会全体人员及全省职工
                                一九五三年三月六日
