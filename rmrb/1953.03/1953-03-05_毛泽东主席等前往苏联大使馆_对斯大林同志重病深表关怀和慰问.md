Title: Chairman Mao Zedong and Others Visit the Soviet Embassy to Express Deep Concern and Sympathy for Comrade Stalin's Serious Illness
Author:
Date: 1953-03-05
Page: 1

    Chairman Mao Zedong and Others Visit the Soviet Embassy
    To Express Deep Concern and Sympathy for Comrade Stalin's Serious Illness
    [Xinhua News Agency, 4th] After learning of the announcement issued on the 3rd by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union regarding Comrade Stalin's serious illness, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Central People's Government, specially dispatched Chairman Mao Zedong, accompanied by Comrades Zhu De, Zhou Enlai, and Gao Gang, to the Soviet Embassy in our country to meet with Ambassador Panyushkin, requesting him to convey the concern and sympathy of the Chinese people, the Communist Party of China, and the Central People's Government for Comrade Stalin's illness, and to express their hope for Comrade Stalin's early recovery.

### 毛泽东主席等前往苏联大使馆  对斯大林同志重病深表关怀和慰问

1953-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席等前往苏联大使馆
    对斯大林同志重病深表关怀和慰问
    【新华社四日讯】中国共产党中央委员会和中央人民政府在获知苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议三日发表的关于斯大林同志的严重病况的公告后，特由毛泽东主席偕同朱德、周恩来、高岗三同志前往苏联驻我国大使馆会见潘友新大使，请其转达中国人民、中国共产党和中央人民政府对斯大林同志的病情的关怀与慰问，并表示对斯大林同志早日恢复健康的盼望。
