Title: Condolence Message from the Preparatory Committee of the Jiangsu Provincial Federation of Trade Unions
Author: 
Date: 1953-03-11
Page: 2

To the Central Council of All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions:

We are shocked to hear of the passing of Comrade Stalin, the leader of the working people of the world. The million workers of Jiangsu Province, China, express our deepest condolences with extreme grief. We pledge to continue our efforts to complete the national construction plan under the leadership of Comrade Mao Zedong and the Chinese Communist Party, resolutely unite closely with our Soviet worker brothers, and fight to the end for the great cause of defending world peace!

Preparatory Committee of the Jiangsu Provincial Federation of Trade Unions
March 7, 1953

### 江苏省总工会筹备委员会的唁电

1953-03-11
第2版()
专栏：

    江苏省总工会筹备委员会的唁电全苏工会中央理事会：
    惊闻全世界劳动人民的领袖斯大林同志逝世，中国江苏全省百万工人以万分悲痛的心情致以哀悼。我们誓在毛泽东同志和中国共产党的领导下，继续努力完成国家建设计划，坚决与苏联工人弟兄紧密团结，为保卫世界和平的伟大事业奋斗到底！
                    中国江苏省总工会筹备委员会
                                一九五三年三月七日
