Title: Condolences from the Chinese Medical Workers' Union Working Committee

Author:
Date: 1953-03-17
Page: 3

To the Central Council of Czechoslovak Trade Unions:

    We are deeply saddened to hear of the passing of Comrade Gottwald. The Chinese medical workers express our deepest condolences. We will unite more closely with the medical workers of Czechoslovakia and strive to safeguard the cause of world peace and democracy.

                        Chinese Medical Workers' Union Working Committee
                              March 15, 1953

### 中国医务工会工作委员会的唁电

1953-03-17
第3版()
专栏：

    中国医务工会工作委员会的唁电捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会：
    惊闻哥特瓦尔德同志逝世的消息，中国医务工作者谨以万分沉痛的心情，致以哀悼。我们将与捷克斯洛伐克的医务工作者更紧密地团结起来，为保卫世界和平民主事业而奋斗。
                        中国医务工会工作委员会
                              一九五三年三月十五日
