Title: Moscow Mourns President Gottwald
Author:
Date: 1953-03-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 19th. All buildings in Moscow flew national flags with black borders on March 19th to mourn the death of Klement Gottwald, President of the Czechoslovak Republic and an outstanding activist in the international workers' movement.

### 莫斯科哀悼哥特瓦尔德总统

1953-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科哀悼哥特瓦尔德总统
    【新华社莫斯科十九日电】莫斯科所有建筑物十九日都悬挂带黑底边的国旗，哀悼捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统、国际工人运动的卓越活动家克利门特·哥特瓦尔德的逝世。
