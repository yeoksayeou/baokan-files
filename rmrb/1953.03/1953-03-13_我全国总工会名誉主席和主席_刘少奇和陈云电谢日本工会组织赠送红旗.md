Title: Liu Shaoqi and Chen Yun, Honorary Chairman and Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Telegraphically Thank Japanese Trade Union Organizations for Presenting Red Flags
Author:
Date: 1953-03-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 9th:

The delegation from the three Japanese groups that came to China to discuss assisting Japanese citizens to return to their country brought a red silk flag from the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan and others to Liu Shaoqi, Honorary Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and a red silk flag from the All-Japan Congress of Industrial Unions and others to Chen Yun, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. Liu Shaoqi and Chen Yun replied with telegrams of thanks. The telegrams are as follows:

Mr. Minoru Takano of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan:

The red silk flag jointly presented to me by the ten trade union organizations of Japan, headed by you, has been received. I express my sincere thanks. I wish the Japanese working class new achievements in the struggle to unify their ranks and defend trade union rights!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Honorary Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions　Liu Shaoqi

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　March 6th

Mr. Shigeharu Yoshida, Chairman of the All-Japan Congress of Industrial Unions:

The red silk flag jointly presented to me by the sixteen trade union organizations of Japan, headed by you, has been received. I express my sincere thanks. I wish the Japanese working class new victories in the struggle to unify their ranks and strive for independence, freedom, peace, and democracy.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions　Chen Yun

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　March 6th

### 我全国总工会名誉主席和主席  刘少奇和陈云电谢日本工会组织赠送红旗

1953-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    我全国总工会名誉主席和主席
    刘少奇和陈云电谢日本工会组织赠送红旗
     【新华社九日讯】此次来华商洽协助日侨回国问题的日本三团体组成的代表团，带来日本工会总评议会等送给中华全国总工会名誉主席刘少奇的红色绸旗一面，全日本产业别工会会议等送给中华全国总工会主席陈云的红色绸旗一面，刘少奇和陈云分别复电致谢，电文如下：日本工会总评议会高野实先生：
    以您为首的日本十个工会组织联名送给我的红色绸旗已经收到了，谨致谢意。祝日本工人阶级在统一自己的队伍、为保卫工会权利的斗争中，获得新的成就！中华全国总工会名誉主席　刘少奇
                                      三月六日全日本产业别工会会议主席吉田资治先生：
    以您为首的日本十六个工会组织联名送给我的红色绸旗，已经收到了，谨致谢意。祝日本工人阶级在统一自己队伍，为争取独立、自由、和平、民主的斗争中获得新的胜利。中华全国总工会主席　陈　云
                                三月六日
