Title: Condolence Telegram from the Liaodong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party
Author: 
Date: 1953-03-19
Page: 2

To the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party:

With extreme grief, we have learned of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Klement Gottwald, the great leader of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and the Czechoslovak people, and an outstanding fighter of the international workers' movement. The Liaodong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the people of Liaodong Province express our deepest condolences!

Comrade Gottwald made tremendous contributions to the happiness and freedom of the Czechoslovak people and to the cause of world peace and democracy. His death is an irreparable loss for Czechoslovakia and for the entire world peace and democracy camp!

We, all the Communist Party members and people of Liaodong Province, under the leadership of Comrade Mao Zedong, will work harder to support the struggle to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea, and unite more closely with the Czechoslovak people in the peace and democracy camp headed by the great Soviet Union, to fight to the end for the cause of world peace and democracy and the cause of communism.

                        The Liaodong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party

### 中共辽东省委员会的唁电

1953-03-19
第2版()
专栏：

    中共辽东省委员会的唁电捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会：
    惊闻捷克斯洛伐克共产党和捷克斯洛伐克人民的伟大领袖、国际工人运动的杰出战士克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志不幸逝世，中国共产党辽东省委员会和辽东省人民怀着极悲痛的心情，表示最深切的哀悼！
    哥特瓦尔德同志为捷克斯洛伐克人民的幸福与自由，为世界和平民主事业作了极大的贡献，他的逝世，对于捷克斯洛伐克，对于整个世界和平民主阵营都是一件无可弥补的损失！
    我们辽东省全体共产党员和人民，在毛泽东同志领导下，将更加努力支援抗美援朝斗争，和捷克斯洛伐克人民更加紧密地团结在以伟大苏联为首的和平民主阵营中，为世界和平民主事业与共产主义事业奋斗到底。
                        中国共产党辽东省委员会
