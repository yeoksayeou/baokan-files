Title: Prominent Romanian Painter, Rixa, Visits Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1953-03-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, March 21st: The renowned Romanian People's Republic painter Marcovei Rixa (female) and Taru Eugene, after visiting Shanghai for three days, left Shanghai for Hangzhou on the evening of the 19th. Those who saw them off at the station included representatives from the Foreign Cultural Liaison Office of the East China Administrative Council's Cultural and Educational Committee and the Foreign Cultural Liaison Office of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government's Cultural and Educational Committee, Mi Gu, Chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the All-China Fine Arts Workers Association, and painter Shen Roujian, among others.

During their visit to Shanghai, Marcovei Rixa and others held a symposium with Chinese painters, introducing their country's achievements and existing problems in painting art, and extensively exchanged creative experiences related to painting. The Romanian painters also visited the Museum of the Shanghai Municipal Cultural Relics Management Committee. They were particularly interested in many famous Chinese paintings from past dynasties.

### 罗马尼亚著名画家  里霞等去杭州访问

1953-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚著名画家
    里霞等去杭州访问
    【新华社上海二十一日电】罗马尼亚人民共和国名画家马科威·里霞（女）和塔鲁·尤金在上海访问了三天后，已于十九日晚离开上海赴杭州。到车站欢送的有：华东行政委员会文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务处和上海市人民政府文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务处的代表、中华全国美术工作者协会上海市分会主席米谷、画家沈柔坚等。
    马科威·里霞等在上海访问期间，曾与我国画家举行座谈会，分别介绍了本国绘画艺术上的成就和存在的问题，详尽地交流了有关绘画的创作经验。罗马尼亚画家并参观了上海市文物管理委员会博物馆。他们对中国历代的许多名画特别感到兴趣。
