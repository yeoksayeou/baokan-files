Title: The Funeral of Comrade Stalin, the Great Leader and Teacher of the Working People of the World, Was Held in Red Square, Moscow
Author:
Date: 1953-03-19
Page: 4

On the morning of March 9th, the funeral of Comrade J.V. Stalin, the great leader and teacher of the working people of the world, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and Marshal of the Soviet Union, was held in Red Square, Moscow. The hearse, drawn by six black horses, carried the remains of Comrade Stalin, the giant of human history. The military cap of the Marshal of the Soviet Union was placed on the coffin. Behind the hearse was a long funeral procession, led by Malenkov, Voroshilov, Zhou Enlai, Beria, Kaganovich, Molotov, Bulganin, and Khrushchev, followed by leaders of the Communist Party and the government of the Soviet Union, leading comrades of the Communist and Workers' Parties of various countries, outstanding representatives of the Soviet people, and representatives of many countries.
