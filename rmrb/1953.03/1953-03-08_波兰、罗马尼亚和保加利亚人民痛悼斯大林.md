Title: People of Poland, Romania, and Bulgaria Mourn Stalin
Author:
Date: 1953-03-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The people of Poland, Romania, Bulgaria, and other countries mourned with deep sorrow on the 6th for Comrade J.V. Stalin, the great leader and teacher of the working people of the world.

    Upon learning of Stalin's death, Bolesław Bierut, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland, immediately offered his deepest condolences on behalf of the Polish people to A.A. Sobolev, the Soviet Ambassador to the Polish People's Republic. He stated that the Polish people stand in unbreakable unity with the Soviet people in this moment of grief for all mankind. Chairman of the State Council Aleksander Zawadzki, State Council members, Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers, Ministers, Secretaries of the Polish United Workers' Party, heads of central organs, and representatives of various political parties and people's organizations also expressed their condolences. *The People's Tribune* published an editorial saying: Comrade Stalin is very close to us because he is the leader and teacher of all those who fight for socialism and build socialism, and the helmsman of the great Soviet land. He is dear to us because he was the greatest helmsman of the Russian revolution after Lenin, and he twice brought independence to our country. He is especially close to us because he helped us find the right path at different stages.

    After the news of Stalin's death reached Romania, Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly, as well as government and party leaders such as Iosif Chișinevschi, Gheorghe Apostol, Chivu Stoica, Gheorghe Vidrașcu, Emil Bodnăraș, and Simion Bughici, heads of various countries’ embassies in Romania, and representatives of various organizations in Bucharest, went to the Soviet Embassy in the Romanian People's Republic to express their condolences. Tens of thousands of working people continuously went to the embassy to express their grief. Major factories in Bucharest held solemn memorial services. A city-wide memorial service was held on the 6th in a solemn atmosphere. At the meeting, the telegrams of condolence from Gheorghiu-Dej and Groza were read. At the end of the meeting, a telegram of condolence on the death of Stalin was adopted. Leaders of various denominations in Romania also gathered on the 6th to mourn J.V. Stalin. To mourn Stalin's death, the Romanian Council of Ministers has announced that flags will be flown at half-mast throughout the country, and all entertainment activities will be suspended from the 6th until the day of the funeral.

    Solemn memorial services were held in cities and villages, enterprises and construction sites throughout Bulgaria. At the memorial service held in Sofia, Valko Chervenkov, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, delivered a speech mourning Stalin. Bulgarian newspapers published an announcement issued by the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, and the National Committee of the Fatherland Front to Bulgarian Communist Party members, Fatherland Front members, and all workers on the occasion of Stalin's death, and published an announcement passed at the meeting of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, the Presidium of the National Assembly, and the National Committee of the Fatherland Front mourning Stalin's death.

### 波兰、罗马尼亚和保加利亚人民痛悼斯大林

1953-03-08
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰、罗马尼亚和保加利亚人民痛悼斯大林
    据新华社讯：波兰、罗马尼亚、保加利亚等国人民六日沉痛吊唁全世界劳动人民的伟大领袖和导师约·维·斯大林。
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会主席、波兰部长会议主席贝鲁特，在获悉斯大林逝世的消息后，即代表波兰人民向苏联驻波兰人民共和国大使阿·亚·索波列夫致沉痛的吊唁。他表示：波兰人民在这个全人类沉痛的时刻和苏联人民牢不可破地团结在一起。国务委员会主席亚·萨瓦茨基和国务会议委员、部长会议副主席、各部部长、波兰统一工人党书记们、中央机关首长和各政党和人民团体代表也先后表示吊唁。人民论坛报发表社论说：斯大林同志对我们是非常亲近的，因为他是全体为社会主义和建设社会主义而斗争的人的领袖和导师，是伟大苏维埃国土的舵手。他对我们是亲切的，因为他是列宁以后俄国革命的最伟大的舵手，他曾两度为我们国家带来了独立。他对我们是特别亲近的，因为他在不同的阶段帮助我们找到了正确的道路。
    在斯大林逝世的消息传到罗马尼亚后，罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会总书记、部长会议主席乔治乌—德治、国民议会主席团主席彼·格罗查以及约·基辛涅夫斯基、格·阿波斯托尔、基·斯托伊卡、格·维德拉希库、埃·波德纳拉希、西·布吉奇等政府和党的首长、各国驻罗马尼亚使节以及布加勒斯特各机关团体的代表，先后前往苏联驻罗马尼亚人民共和国大使馆吊唁。成千成万的劳动人民源源不断地到大使馆志哀。布加勒斯特大小工厂纷纷举行隆重的追悼会。全市追悼大会于六日在严肃的气氛中举行。会上宣读了乔治乌—德治和格罗查的唁电。会末并通过了吊唁斯大林逝世的电报。罗马尼亚各教派的领袖们也在六日集会追悼约·维·斯大林。为了哀悼斯大林的逝世，罗马尼亚部长会议已通告各地下半旗志哀，自六日起到丧礼之日止所有娱乐活动一律停止。
    在保加利亚城乡各地、各企业和建筑工地都举行了隆重的追悼会。在索非亚举行的追悼大会上，保加利亚共产党中央委员会总书记、保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席伏·契尔文科夫发表了哀吊斯大林的演说。保加利亚各报刊载了保加利亚共产党中央委员会、保加利亚人民共和国部长会议和祖国阵线全国委员会在斯大林逝世之际向保加利亚共产党员、祖国阵线成员和全体工人发出的公告，并登载了保加利亚共产党中央委员会、保加利亚人民共和国部长会议、国民议会主席团和祖国阵线全国委员会哀悼斯大林逝世的会上通过的公告。
