Title: Soviet Pravda Publishes Chairman Mao's Article
Author:
Date: 1953-03-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th—Today's Pravda published news of the memorial meeting for Comrade Stalin held on Red Square in Moscow on the 9th, and prominently featured a full-page photo of the presidium of the meeting on the front page. The photo shows the leaders of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet government, as well as the leaders of various people's democratic countries and communist and workers' parties, standing on the presidium. Pravda also published the speeches given by Malenkov, Beria, and Molotov at Red Square, and published a full-page photo of Comrade Stalin's hearse entering Red Square on the second page.

Pravda published Chairman Mao Zedong's article "The Greatest Friendship" on the third page, occupying half a page, and above the article was a photo taken at the signing ceremony of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance on February 14, 1950. From left to right in the photo are Stalin, Mao Zedong, and Malenkov. On the same day, Pravda also published news from its Beijing correspondent about the memorial meeting for Comrade Stalin held by more than 600,000 people in Beijing, and published the full text of the eulogy by Zhu De, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

The Pravda of the 9th published photos of Zhou Enlai, Guo Moruo, and the leaders of the governments and parties of Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, the German Democratic Republic, and other countries, as well as the leaders of the Italian Communist Party, guarding the coffin of Stalin. On the same day, Izvestia published an article on the fourth page by Qian Sanqiang, head of the delegation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences visiting the Soviet Union, entitled "The Great Leader of the Working People of the World."

The Pravda of the 8th prominently published on the fifth page the telegrams of condolence from Chairman Mao Zedong, Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai, and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to Shvernik, Vyshinsky, and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, respectively.

### 苏联真理报刊载毛主席文章

1953-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联真理报刊载毛主席文章
   【新华社莫斯科十日电】十日的真理报发表了九日莫斯科红场举行追悼斯大林同志大会的消息，并以首页通栏地位刊载了大会主席台的照片。照片显示：站在主席台上的有苏联共产党与苏联政府的领袖，各人民民主国家、各国共产党与工人党的领导者。真理报同时刊载了马林科夫、贝利亚、莫洛托夫三人在红场发表的演说，并在第二版以通栏地位刊载了斯大林同志灵车进入红场时的照片。
    真理报在第三版以半版篇幅刊载了毛泽东主席撰写的“最伟大的友谊”的文章，并在文章上面刊登了一九五○年二月十四日中苏友好同盟互助条约签字仪式举行时所摄的照片，照片从左到右是斯大林、毛泽东、马林科夫。同日真理报还刊载了该报驻北京记者关于北京六十多万人举行追悼斯大林同志大会的消息，并发表了中国共产党中央委员会书记、中央人民政府副主席、中国人民解放军总司令朱德的悼词全文。
    九日的真理报刊登了周恩来、郭沫若，波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、德意志民主共和国等国政府和党的领袖们，以及意大利共产党领袖在斯大林灵柩旁守灵的照片。同日的消息报在第四版刊载了中国科学院访苏代表团团长钱三强的题为“全世界劳动人民的伟大领袖”的文章。
    八日的真理报在第五版以显著地位刊登了毛泽东主席、周恩来总理兼外交部长和中国共产党中央委员会分别致什维尔尼克、维辛斯基和苏联共产党中央委员会的唁电。
