Title: Announcement by the Central Committee of the CPSU and the Council of Ministers of the USSR on National Mourning. Comrade Stalin's Funeral to be Held on March 9th.
Author:
Date: 1953-03-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th (TASS): Announcement by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the USSR:

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the USSR have decided that, due to the death of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Joseph Vissarionovich Stalin, national mourning will be held on March 6th, 7th, 8th, and 9th, 1953. During these days, all dramas, films, concerts, and other entertainments will be suspended.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th (TASS): Announcement by the Funeral Committee for the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Joseph Vissarionovich Stalin:

The funeral of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Joseph Vissarionovich Stalin, will be held at twelve noon on Monday, March 9th of this year.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th (TASS): Decision of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the USSR on the location of J.V. Stalin's crystal coffin:

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the USSR hereby decide: to place the crystal coffin containing the remains of J.V. Stalin in the Mausoleum on Red Square, alongside the crystal coffin of U.I. Lenin.

(Signed)
The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union
The Council of Ministers of the USSR

### 苏共中央和苏联部长会议公告全国举哀  斯大林同志的丧礼定于三月九日举行

1953-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    苏共中央和苏联部长会议公告全国举哀
    斯大林同志的丧礼定于三月九日举行
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科七日电：苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议的公告：
    苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议决定：由于苏联部长会议主席、苏联共产党中央委员会书记约瑟夫·维萨里奥诺维奇·斯大林逝世，在一九五三年三月六日、七日、八日、九日全国举行哀悼。在这几天里，戏剧、电影、音乐会和其他一切娱乐一律停止。
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科七日电：苏联部长会议主席、苏联共产党中央委员会书记约瑟夫·维萨里奥诺维奇·斯大林治丧委员会公告：
    苏联部长会议主席、苏联共产党中央委员会书记约瑟夫·维萨里奥诺维奇·斯大林的丧礼定于今年三月九日（星期一）中午十二时举行。
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科七日电：苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议关于约·维·斯大林的水晶棺的安放处所的决定：
    苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议兹决定：把安放约·维·斯大林遗体的水晶棺，安放在红场上的陵墓里，和乌·伊·列宁的水晶棺并列。
    苏联共产党中央委员会
    苏联部长会议
    （签署）
