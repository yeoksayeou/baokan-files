Title: Telegram of Condolence from the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs
Author:
Date: 1953-03-17
Page: 3

To the Presidium of the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic:

We were deeply saddened to hear of the unfortunate passing of President Gottwald. All members of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs feel infinite grief and extend our sincere and deep condolences. Our two peoples of China and Czechoslovakia will further consolidate and develop our close friendship and, together with the Soviet people, will fight to the end to defend world peace.

Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs
March 15, 1953

### 中国人民外交学会的唁电

1953-03-17
第3版()
专栏：

    中国人民外交学会的唁电捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府主席团：
    惊闻哥特瓦尔德总统不幸逝世，中国人民外交学会全体会员感觉无限沉痛，谨诚挚地致以深切的哀悼。我们中捷两国人民将进一步巩固和发展我们的亲密友谊，并与苏联人民更加团结一致，为保卫世界和平而奋斗到底。
                                    中国人民外交学会
                              一九五三年三月十五日
