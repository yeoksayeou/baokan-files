Title: Molotov Receives Government Representatives from Finland, Turkey, and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1953-03-14
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 13th: According to TASS, V.M. Molotov, Vice-Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, received on the 11th the members of the Finnish and Turkish government delegations who came to attend the funeral of J.V. Stalin, Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, as well as Prince Mohammed Daoud Khan, representative of the King of Afghanistan, and Abdul Hamid Khan, representative of the Afghan government. U. Kekkonen, head of the Finnish government delegation, Asikalin, head of the Turkish government delegation, and Mohammed Daoud Khan, representative of the King of Afghanistan, respectively expressed their condolences to the Soviet government on the death of J.V. Stalin on behalf of their heads of state and governments.

### 莫洛托夫接见芬、土等国政府代表

1953-03-14
第4版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫接见芬、土等国政府代表
    【新华社十三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十二日讯：苏联部长会议副主席兼苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫，十一日分别接见了前来参加苏联部长会议主席约·维·斯大林丧礼的芬兰、土耳其政府代表团人员，以及阿富汗国王代表穆罕默德·达乌德汗亲王和阿富汗政府代表阿布杜尔·哈密德汗。芬兰政府代表团团长乌·吉科宁、土耳其政府代表团团长阿西卡林和阿富汗国王代表穆罕默德·达乌德汗，分别代表本国元首和政府，对约·维·斯大林的逝世向苏联政府表示吊慰。
