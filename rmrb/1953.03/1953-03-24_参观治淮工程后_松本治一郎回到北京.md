Title: Matsumoto Jiichiro Returns to Beijing After Visiting the Huai River Project
Author:
Date: 1953-03-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Bengbu, March 23rd: Japanese prominent peace figure, Vice-Chairman of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, Matsumoto Jiichiro, Japanese representative to the Vienna World People's Peace Congress, Saiyuanji Kōichi, and Chairman of the Japan-China Trade Promotion Conference, Yamamoto Kumaichi, visited the construction site of the Anhui Province's Fozi Ridge Reservoir, one of the Huai River projects, on March 19th. Wang Huzhen, commander of the Fozi Ridge Reservoir Project Headquarters, and others accompanied them to visit the various construction projects that were in full swing, as well as the woodworking factory and the concrete laboratory. They highly praised this world-famous peace construction project. Matsumoto Jiichiro said, "We visited this great project of world significance and were deeply moved by the high enthusiasm of the Chinese people for peaceful construction. We are convinced that under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong, the Chinese people will achieve even greater victories in the future cause of peaceful construction."
    Matsumoto Jiichiro and others passed through Bengbu and Hefei on the morning of the 21st on their way from the Fozi Ridge Reservoir construction site and were warmly received by the Huai River Commission, the Anhui Provincial Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and the Bengbu Municipal Branch.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd: Japanese prominent peace figure, Vice-Chairman of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, Matsumoto Jiichiro, who was invited to visit China by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, returned to Beijing on the 23rd after visiting Shanghai, Hangzhou, Nanjing, and the Fozi Ridge Huai River Water Conservancy Project.
    Liu Ningyi, Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and others, as well as Vice-Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, Kameda Tōgo, Japanese representatives to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, Nakamura Kanjūrō, Huang Fengjiu, and Japanese peace figure Takahashi Fumio, who are currently in Beijing, welcomed him at the station.

### 参观治淮工程后  松本治一郎回到北京

1953-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

    参观治淮工程后
    松本治一郎回到北京
     【新华社蚌埠二十三日电】日本著名和平人士、亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副主席松本治一郎、出席维也纳世界人民和平大会的日本代表西园寺公一、日本日中贸易促进会议主席山本熊一等，在三月十九日到治淮工程之一安徽省佛子岭水库工地参观。佛子岭水库工程指挥部指挥汪胡桢等陪同他们参观了正在热烈紧张进行着的各项工程建设和木工加工厂、混凝土实验室等。他们对这个世界有名的和平建设工程备加赞扬。松本治一郎说：“我们参观了这座具有世界意义的伟大工程，看到中国人民为和平建设的高度热情，使我们很受感动。我们深信：中国人民在毛泽东主席的英明领导下，在未来的和平建设事业中将会取得更大的胜利。”
    松本治一郎等在二十一日上午离开佛子岭水库工地途经蚌埠和合肥时，受到治淮委员会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会安徽省分会和蚌埠市分会的热情招待。
    【新华社二十三日讯】应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问的日本著名和平人士、亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副主席松本治一郎，在参观上海、杭州、南京及佛子岭治淮水利工程之后，已在二十三日回到北京。
    到车站欢迎的有亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书长刘宁一，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一等人，以及现在北京的亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长龟田东伍、日本出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议代表中村翫右卫门、黄凤九和日本和平人士高桥文雄。
