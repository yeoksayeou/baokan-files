Title: Leaders of government departments, responsible persons of democratic parties, and people from all walks of life in Beijing continue to visit the Czechoslovakian Embassy to mourn the death of Gottwald

Author:

Date: 1953-03-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Leaders of various departments of the Central People's Government, responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations, figures from the cultural and artistic circles of Beijing, and envoys of various countries to China, continued to visit the Czechoslovakian Embassy in China on the 16th to mourn the death of President Gottwald.

The leaders of various departments of the Central People's Government who went to pay their respects included: Chen Boda, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the State Council of the Central People's Government, Pan Zhenya, Deputy Director of the People's Supervisory Committee, Zhao Erlu, Minister of the Second Ministry of Machine Building Industry, Li Shucheng, Minister of Agriculture, Cheng Zihua, Acting Director of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, Chen Shiqu, Commander of the Engineering Corps of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Qiu Chuangcheng, Deputy Commander of the Artillery Corps.

The responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations who went to pay their respects included: Xu Deheng, Chairman of the Jiusan Society, Huang Dingchen, representative of the China Zhi Gong Party, Chen Bingji, representative of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League, Chen Shutong, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, Kang Keqing, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defense of Children, and Qian Duansheng, representative of the China Political Science and Law Society.

The cultural and artistic figures from Beijing who went to pay their respects included: Shakoff, Tian Han, Qu Xixian, Gu Yuan, Liu Kaiqu, Ye Qianyu, Dai Ailian, Sun Shen, and Lian Kuoru.

The envoys of various countries to China who went to pay their respects included: Wu La Moung, Ambassador of Burma, Munk, Minister of Denmark, Knölin, Minister of Finland, Isha Matti, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy, Iftikhar Ali, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Pakistani Embassy, Schüldt, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Swedish Embassy, and Stiner, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Swiss Legation.

The following also visited the embassy to pay their respects: British negotiator Lan Laine, Norwegian negotiator Golan, and Dutch negotiator Van der Put.

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Liu Lantao, Third Secretary of the North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Wang Congwu, Minister of Organization, Zhang Panshi, Minister of Propaganda, and Liu Xiufeng and Zhang Su, Vice Chairmen of the North China Administrative Committee, visited the Czechoslovak Republic Embassy in China on the 16th to express their deep condolences on the unfortunate death of Comrade Klement Gottwald, President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Chairman of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, and a close friend of the Chinese people.

Xinhua News Agency. In recent days, more than 30,000 people from all walks of life in Beijing have continuously visited the Czechoslovak Republic Embassy in China to mourn the death of President Gottwald. The streets near the embassy are lined with people waiting to enter the embassy hall to pay their respects all day long.

The embassy hall opens at eight o'clock in the morning, and until the evening, people continue to line up and pass before President Gottwald's portrait.

Workers' representatives from major factories throughout the city and farmers from the suburbs came to pay their respects and present wreaths today. Among the long lines were workers from the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant, the Shijingshan Power Plant, coal miners from the Jingxi Mining Area, workers from the North China Agricultural Machinery Factory, and female workers from the Qinghe Woolen Mill. The workers know that it is Comrade Gottwald and the people of Czechoslovakia who often support China's economic construction with exquisite machinery.

Among the farmers from the suburbs, there are those from Zhang Guozhuang in Fengtai District, who became close friends with the Czechoslovak people in the winter of 1950. The farmers of Zhang Guozhuang once wrote to President Gottwald, reporting on their happy life after liberation; President Gottwald also replied to their letter and sent someone to give them gifts. When they heard the news of President Gottwald's death, the whole village held a memorial meeting that evening and unanimously expressed their determination to do a good job in spring plowing to commemorate President Gottwald's death. At the meeting, a letter of condolence to the Presidium of the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic was approved.

Students of minority nationalities such as Mongolian, Tibetan, Miao, Yi, Korean, Hui, Russian, Solon, and Daur who are studying in Beijing also sent representatives to the embassy to pay their respects.

### 我政府各部门首长及各民主党派负责人和北京各界人民  继续前往捷大使馆吊唁哥特瓦尔德逝世

1953-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我政府各部门首长及各民主党派负责人和北京各界人民
    继续前往捷大使馆吊唁哥特瓦尔德逝世
    【新华社十六日讯】我中央人民政府各部门首长，各民主党派、人民团体负责人，北京文化艺术界人士以及各国驻我国使节，十六日继续前往捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使馆吊唁哥特瓦尔德总统的逝世。
    中央人民政府各部门首长前往吊唁的有：中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会副主任陈伯达，人民监察委员会副主任潘震亚，第二机械工业部部长赵尔陆，农业部部长李书城，中华全国合作社联合总社代主任程子华，中国人民解放军工兵司令员陈士渠，炮兵副司令员邱创成等。
    各民主党派和人民团体负责人前往吊唁的有：九三学社主席许德珩，中国致公党代表黄鼎臣，台湾民主自治同盟代表陈炳基，中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会主任委员陈叔通，中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会秘书长康克清，中国政治法律学会代表钱端升等。
    北京文化艺术界人士前往吊唁的有：沙可夫、田汉、瞿希贤、古元、刘开渠、叶浅予、戴爱莲、孙慎、连阔如等。
    各国驻我国使节前往吊唁的有：缅甸大使吴拉茂，丹麦公使穆克，芬兰公使克诺林，印尼大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂，巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办伊夫提哈·艾里，瑞典大使馆临时代办修尔德，瑞士公使馆临时代办施禔纳。
    前往大使馆吊唁的还有：英国谈判代表蓝来讷，挪威谈判代表高兰，荷兰谈判代表范德普。
      【新华社十六日讯】中共中央华北局第三书记刘澜涛、组织部长王从吾、宣传部长张磐石和华北行政委员会副主席刘秀峰、张苏等，在十六日前往捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻我国大使馆，对捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统、捷克斯洛伐克共产党主席、中国人民的亲密朋友克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志不幸逝世，致沉痛的悼念。
    【新华社讯】连日，北京市各界人民三万多人不断前往捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻我国大使馆吊唁哥特瓦尔德总统的逝世。大使馆附近的大街上，整日排列着等待进入使馆大厅吊唁的人群。
    大使馆大厅上午八时开放，直到晚间，人们仍然继续不断地列队从哥特瓦尔德总统的遗像前流过。
    全市各大工厂的工人代表和郊区的农民们今天都赶来吊唁和敬献花圈。在长长的行列中，有石景山钢铁厂、石景山发电厂的工人，京西矿区的煤矿工人，华北农业机械厂的工人和清河制呢厂的女工们。工人们知道：就是哥特瓦尔德同志和捷克斯洛伐克的人民，经常以精美的机器，来支援我国的经济建设。
    从郊区来的农民中，有从一九五○年冬天开始，就和捷克斯洛伐克人民成为亲密朋友的。丰台区张郭庄的农民，他们曾经给哥特瓦尔德总统写过信，报告他们在解放后幸福的生活；哥特瓦尔德总统也曾回过他们的信，并派专人送礼物给他们。当他们听到哥特瓦尔德总统逝世的消息后，在当天晚上，全村召开了追悼大会，一致表示要作好春耕，来纪念哥特瓦尔德总统的逝世。在会上并通过了给捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府主席团的吊唁信。
    正在北京学习的蒙、藏、苗、彝、朝鲜、回、俄罗斯、索伦、达呼尔等少数民族的学生们，也派了代表前往大使馆吊唁。
