Title: Khmer Liberation Army Attacks and Wins
Author:
Date: 1953-03-26
Page: 4
　　
    Khmer Liberation Army Attacks and Wins
    [Xinhua News Agency, 25th] The Khmer People's Liberation Army is constantly attacking and achieving victories.
    According to Vietnam News Agency reports: On January 5th, the Khmer People's Liberation Army intercepted a French army transport team on the Kim Son - Kro Lor highway in Kampot province, seizing three machine guns, five submachine guns, many rifles, and a large amount of supplies and equipment.
    On February 6th, the French army launched a "sweep" in the Kompong Seila area, encountering a counterattack from the Khmer People's Liberation Army, suffering a loss of fifty men.
    On February 15th, under the pressure of the Khmer People's Liberation Army, the French army was forced to withdraw from the Kdong stronghold.
    On February 20th, two hundred French troops, in order to retaliate, advanced into the liberated areas under the cover of artillery fire. The Khmer People's Liberation Army repelled the French army's attack and caused the enemy to suffer significant losses.

### 高棉解放军出击获胜

1953-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    高棉解放军出击获胜
    【新华社二十五日讯】高棉人民解放军不断出击，取得胜利。
    据越南通讯社消息：高棉人民解放军一月五日在贡不省的金船—克罗勒公路上截击了法军的一个运输队，缴获机枪三挺、冲锋枪五支、许多步枪和大量物资装备。
    二月六日，法军向贡赛美亚地区发动“扫荡”，遭到高棉人民解放军的反击，损失五十人。
    二月十五日，在高棉人民解放军的压力下，法军被迫从克东据点撤退。
    二月二十日，法军二百名为了进行报复，在炮兵掩护下向解放区进犯。高棉人民解放军击退了法军的进攻，并使敌人遭到重大的损失。
