Title: Condolence Telegrams from People's Organizations of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1953-03-18
Page: 3

    To the Central Council of Trade Unions, the Youth League, and the Women's Federation of Czechoslovakia:
    We, the entire working class, youth, and women of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region of China, are in deep mourning for the passing of Comrade Stalin, the teacher of the working people of the world. We are now further saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Klement Gottwald, a close friend of the Chinese people. This sad news brings us profound grief, and we extend our most sincere condolences.
    We, the entire working class, youth, and women of Inner Mongolia, China, are determined to transform our grief into strength. Under the leadership of our leader Chairman Mao Zedong and the Chinese Communist Party, we vow to unite more closely with the working people of Czechoslovakia in the socialist camp led by the Soviet Union, heighten our vigilance a hundredfold, and fight to the end to shatter all the aggressive conspiracies of American imperialism and to defend our motherland's economic construction and the cause of world peace!
        Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region
        Inner Mongolia Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League
        Inner Mongolia Democratic Women's Federation of China
                              March 15, 1953

### 内蒙古自治区各人民团体的唁电

1953-03-18
第3版()
专栏：

    内蒙古自治区各人民团体的唁电捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会、青年联盟、妇女联合会：
    我们中国内蒙古自治区的全体工人、青年和妇女正以万分沉痛的心情悼念世界劳动人民的导师斯大林同志逝世的时候，又得悉中国人民亲密的朋友克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志不幸逝世的消息，这一噩耗给我们带来了深沉的悲痛，谨致以最衷心的哀悼。
    我们中国内蒙古全体工人、青年和妇女决心化悲痛为力量，在我们的领袖毛泽东主席和中国共产党的领导下，誓和捷克斯洛伐克的劳动人民更加紧密地团结在以苏联为首的和平民主阵营中，百倍地提高警惕，为粉碎美帝国主义的一切侵略阴谋、保卫祖国的经济建设与全世界和平事业而奋斗到底！
        中国内蒙古自治区总工会筹备委员会
        中国新民主主义青年团内蒙古委员会
        中国内蒙古民主妇女联合会
                              一九五三年三月十五日
