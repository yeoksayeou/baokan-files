Title: Romanian, Polish and Other Countries' Newspapers Publish Chairman Mao's Article "The Greatest Friendship"
Author:
Date: 1953-03-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 13th: According to the Romanian News Agency, Bucharest, all newspapers in Bucharest, the capital of Romania, published the full text of Chairman Mao Zedong's article "The Greatest Friendship" on the 11th.

Xinhua News Agency, March 13th: According to the Polish News Agency, Warsaw, all newspapers in Warsaw published the full text of Chairman Mao Zedong's article "The Greatest Friendship" on the 12th.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 13th: The Czechoslovak Communist Party organ "Red Right" published the full text of Chairman Mao Zedong's article "The Greatest Friendship" on the 11th. All other major newspapers in Prague also published excerpts from this article on the 11th and 12th.

The London "Daily Worker" published a summary of this article by its Moscow correspondent on the 11th.

### 罗马尼亚、波兰等国报纸刊载毛主席文“最伟大的友谊”

1953-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚、波兰等国报纸刊载毛主席文“最伟大的友谊”
    【新华社十三日讯】据罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特各报十一日都全文刊载了毛泽东主席撰写的“最伟大的友谊”一文。
    【新华社十三日讯】据波兰通讯社华沙讯：华沙各报十二日全文刊载了毛泽东主席撰写的“最伟大的友谊”一文。
    【新华社布拉格十三日电】捷克斯洛伐克共产党机关报“红色权利报”在十一日全文刊登了毛泽东主席的文章“最伟大的友谊”。布拉格其他一切主要报纸在十一日和十二日也摘要刊登了这篇文章。
    伦敦“工人日报”在十一日刊登了它的驻莫斯科记者对这篇文章的摘要。
