Title: Condolence Telegrams from Democratic Parties' Northeast Region and Shenyang Municipal Organizations

Author: 
Date: 1953-03-18
Page: 3

To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia:

    We are deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of President Klement Gottwald, the most respected leader of the Czechoslovak people, a dear friend of the Chinese people, and an outstanding fighter in the world peace and democracy movement. The passing of President Gottwald is not only a great loss to the Czechoslovak people but also a misfortune for the fraternal countries and the world peace and democracy camp. We firmly stand on the side of the world peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union, and we will unite even more closely with the Czechoslovak people in the future to fight to the end for the cause of peace, democracy, and socialist revolution in China and Czechoslovakia and in the world.

            The Northeast Provisional Working Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the Northeast General Branch Provisional Working Committee of the China Democratic League, the Preparatory Committee of the Shenyang Municipal Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the Preparatory Committee of the Shenyang Municipal Branch of the China Democratic League, the Preparatory Committee of the Shenyang Municipal Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, and the Preparatory Committee of the Shenyang Municipal Branch of the China Association for Promoting Democracy.
                                March 15, 1953

### 各民主党派东北区和沈阳市地方组织的唁电

1953-03-18
第3版()
专栏：

    各民主党派东北区和沈阳市地方组织的唁电捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会：
    惊悉捷克斯洛伐克人民最崇敬的领袖、中国人民亲爱的朋友、世界和平民主运动中卓越的战士克利门特·哥特瓦尔德总统不幸逝世，我们和捷克斯洛伐克人民一样，感到深切的悲痛。哥特瓦尔德总统永别了我们，不仅是捷克斯洛伐克人民的巨大损失，也是兄弟国家及世界和平民主阵营的不幸。我们坚决地站在以苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营一边，今后更加紧密地和捷克斯洛伐克人民团结在一起，为中捷两国及世界和平、民主和社会主义革命事业而奋斗到底。
