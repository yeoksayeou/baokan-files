Title: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Appoints Kuznetsov as Ambassador to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1953-03-26
Page: 1

    The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR
    Appoints Kuznetsov as Ambassador to Our Country
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 25th] The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has appointed Comrade Vasily Vasilyevich Kuznetsov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the USSR to the People's Republic of China. The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has relieved Comrade Pan Youxin of his position as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the USSR to the People's Republic of China; he will be assigned to another post.
    The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the USSR to our country, Kuznetsov, flew from Moscow to Beijing on a special plane on the 24th to assume his new post. Those who went to the airport to see him off included Malik, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, Fedelin, Director of the First Far East Department, and Kulazhenkov, Director of the Protocol Department. Comrade Ge Baoquan, Xu Tiemin, Li Tao, and Military Attaché Major General Ji He of our country's embassy in the Soviet Union also went to the airport to see him off.

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团  任命库兹涅佐夫为驻我国大使

1953-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团
    任命库兹涅佐夫为驻我国大使
    【新华社莫斯科二十五日电】苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命苏联外交部副部长瓦西里·瓦西里耶维奇·库兹涅佐夫同志为苏联驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使。苏联最高苏维埃主席团解除潘友新同志苏联驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使的职务，另有任用。
    新任苏联驻我国特命全权大使库兹涅佐夫在二十四日自莫斯科乘专机飞北京履新。前往机场送行的有：苏联外交部第一副部长马立克，第一远东司司长费德林和交际司司长库拉仁科夫。我国驻苏联大使馆参赞戈宝权、许铁民、李滔和武官吉合少将等也前往机场送行。
