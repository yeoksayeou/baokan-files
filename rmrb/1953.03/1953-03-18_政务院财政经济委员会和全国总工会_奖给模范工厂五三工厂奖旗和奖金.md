Title: The Government Administration Council's Financial and Economic Committee and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Awarded the Model Factory No. 53 Factory a Banner and Bonus

Author:

Date: 1953-03-18
Page: 2

The Government Administration Council's Financial and Economic Committee and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions
Awarded the Model Factory No. 53 Factory a Banner and Bonus
    The Government Administration Council's Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions awarded the model factory - No. 53 Factory with a banner and bonus. The award ceremony was held at the Shenyang Workers' Club on the afternoon of March 14th.
    Among those attending the award ceremony were representatives of the Government Administration Council's Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Zhang Xiushan, Second Deputy Secretary of the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zhang Weizhen, Chairman of the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions, Huang Oudong, Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Mayor of the Shenyang Municipal People's Government, and Zhu Weiren, Chairman of the Shenyang Municipal Federation of Trade Unions. Also attending the award ceremony were the heads of various factories in Shenyang, advanced team leaders, workers, technicians, staff and other guests, totaling more than 1,700 people.
    After the start of the conference, Liu Ningyi, on behalf of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, awarded Factory No. 53 a banner inscribed by Commander-in-Chief Zhu De with the words "You are a model for relying on the working class to run factories and enterprises well" and a bonus of 500 million yuan.
    In his speech after the award, Liu Ningyi said: Factory No. 53 was guided by Chairman Mao's thought and learned from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union to do its work well, and thus won the honorary title of model factory. Liu Ningyi encouraged Factory No. 53 to continue to learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union and strive for greater achievements, to become a model factory for China's millions of industrial and mining enterprises to learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union, and hoped that Factory No. 53 would spread its experience at any time, so that more model factories would emerge in China's millions of factories and mines. Only in this way can we firmly guarantee the early and victorious completion of the country's first five-year plan. After Liu Ningyi's speech, Zhang Xiushan, Zhang Weizhen, Huang Oudong and others also addressed the meeting one after another, encouraging Factory No. 53 to continue to move forward and calling on other factories and mines to earnestly learn from the experience of Factory No. 53.
    Gao Fangqi, director of Factory No. 53, delivered a reply on behalf of all the factory's employees, promising not to be arrogant or complacent, to work harder to learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union, and to answer the awards from the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions with practical actions to complete and overfulfill the national plan.
    Ren Junjie, director of the Northeast Electric Engineering Factory No. 5, Wei Wenfeng, Secretary of the Party Committee of the Shenyang Smelting Plant, Shen Xinyu, Secretary of the Youth League Committee of Factory No. 52, and Li Yingzhou, Chairman of the Trade Union of Machinery Factory No. 7, spoke at the ceremony, unanimously expressing that they would earnestly learn from the experience of Factory No. 53, improve their work, and ensure the overfulfillment of the 1953 national plan.
    After the meeting, all the employees of Factory No. 53 wrote a letter to the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, thanking them for the award. All the employees expressed that they would redouble their efforts to learn from the Soviet experience, strive to complete the first quarter production tasks ahead of schedule, and ensure the overfulfillment of the 1953 full factory action plan, to answer the superiors' awards to them and to greet the convening of the Seventh National Labor Congress. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 政务院财政经济委员会和全国总工会  奖给模范工厂五三工厂奖旗和奖金

1953-03-18
第2版()
专栏：

    政务院财政经济委员会和全国总工会
    奖给模范工厂五三工厂奖旗和奖金
    中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会、中华全国总工会授予模范工厂——五三工厂以奖旗和奖金。授奖典礼大会于三月十四日下午在沈阳市工人俱乐部举行。
    出席授奖大会的有中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会和中华全国总工会代表、中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一、中共中央东北局第二副书记张秀山、东北总工会主席张维桢、中共沈阳市委员会书记、沈阳市人民政府市长黄欧东、沈阳市总工会主席朱维仁。出席授奖大会的还有沈阳市各工厂的负责人，先进的小组长、工人、技术人员、职员和其他来宾等共一千七百多人。
    大会开始后，刘宁一代表中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会和中华全国总工会把上面有朱德总司令题字“你们是依靠工人阶级，搞好工厂企业的模范”的奖旗一面和奖金五亿元奖给五三工厂。
    刘宁一在授奖后致词说：五三工厂是依据毛主席的思想领导、学习苏联先进经验来做好工作，因而获得模范工厂光荣称号的。刘宁一勉励五三工厂今后继续学习苏联先进经验、争取更大的成绩，成为我国千百万工矿企业学习苏联先进经验的模范工厂，并希望五三工厂随时传播自己的经验，使我国千百万厂矿企业中涌现出更多的模范工厂，只有这样，才能巩固地保证国家建设第一个五年计划提前胜利地完成。刘宁一致词后，张秀山、张维桢、黄欧东等也先后向大会致词，勉励五三工厂继续前进，并号召其他厂矿企业认真学习五三工厂经验。
    五三工厂厂长高方启代表全厂职工致答词，保证不骄不傲，更加努力学习苏联先进经验，以完成和超额完成国家计划的实际行动，来回答中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会和中华全国总工会的奖励。
    东北电工五厂厂长任俊杰、沈阳冶炼厂中共党委书记魏文风、五二工厂青年团团委书记沈新裕和机械七厂工会主席李瀛洲四人在典礼大会上发言，一致表示要认真学习五三工厂经验，改进自己的工作，保证超额完成一九五三年的国家计划。
    会后，五三工厂的全体职工写信给中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会和中华全国总工会，感谢给他们的奖励。全体职工表示要加倍努力，学习苏联经验，争取更早地提前完成第一季度生产任务，并保证超额完成一九五三年全厂行动计划，来回答上级对他们的奖励，来迎接第七次全国劳动大会的召开。      （新华社）
