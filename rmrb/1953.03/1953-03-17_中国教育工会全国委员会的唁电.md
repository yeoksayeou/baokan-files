Author:
Date: 1953-03-17
Page: 3

Condolence Telegram from the National Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China

To the Educational Workers' Union of Czechoslovakia:

    We are deeply grieved to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Gottwald, the great leader of the Czechoslovak people and a close friend of the Chinese people. We extend our heartfelt condolences to you and all the educational workers of Czechoslovakia. We are determined to unite even more closely with the Czechoslovak educational workers under the banner of peace, democracy, and socialism led by the Soviet Union, and to fight to the end for our common cause!

                        The National Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China
                              March 15, 1953

### 中国教育工会全国委员会的唁电

1953-03-17
第3版()
专栏：

    中国教育工会全国委员会的唁电捷克斯洛伐克教育工会：
    惊悉捷克斯洛伐克人民的伟大领袖、中国人民亲密的朋友哥特瓦尔德同志的不幸逝世，中国教育工作者感到无限的悲痛，谨向您们和捷克斯洛伐克全体教育工作者致以衷心的哀悼。我们决心与捷克斯洛伐克教育工作者一道，更紧密地团结在以苏联为首的和平、民主、社会主义的旗帜下，为我们的共同事业奋斗到底！
                        中国教育工会全国委员会
                              一九五三年三月十五日
