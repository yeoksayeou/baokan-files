Title: Death of Comrade Gottwald, President of the Czechoslovak Republic and Chairman of the CPCz

Author: 
Date: 1953-03-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: Comrade Kliment Gottwald, President of the Czechoslovak Republic and Chairman of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia (CPCz), passed away on March 14th due to ineffective treatment of acute pneumonia and pleurisy, which he contracted on March 12th.

The Czechoslovak News Agency reported: Since 9:00 AM on March 14th, Comrade Kliment Gottwald had been in a state of severe unconsciousness. Despite continuous artificial maintenance of respiration and blood circulation, severe cardiovascular insufficiency (collapse) occurred. The pallor and cyanosis worsened, breathing ceased, the pulse became undetectable, and blood pressure could not be measured. Symptoms of central nervous system dysfunction reappeared. At 11:00 AM, Comrade Kliment Gottwald passed away. (Photo attached)

### 捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统、捷共主席  哥特瓦尔德同志逝世

1953-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统、捷共主席
    哥特瓦尔德同志逝世
    据新华社讯：捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统、捷克斯洛伐克共产党主席克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志于三月十二日患急性肺炎和肋膜炎，医治无效，三月十四日不幸逝世。
    捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道：三月十四日上午九时以来，克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志处在严重失去知觉的状态中。虽然不断用人工的办法维持呼吸和血液循环，但是严重的心脏血管机能不全的现象（虚脱）发生了。苍白色和青紫色增重了，呼吸停止了，脉搏感觉不到了，血压也测量不到了。神经中枢系统受障碍的症状再度出现。上午十一时，克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志逝世。（附图片）
