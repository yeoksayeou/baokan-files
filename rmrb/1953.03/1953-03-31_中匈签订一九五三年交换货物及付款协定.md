Title: China and Hungary Sign 1953 Agreement on Exchange of Goods and Payment
Author: 
Date: 1953-03-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 30th: The Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Commercial Delegation of the Government of the People's Republic of Hungary, after friendly and harmonious negotiations, signed the 1953 China-Hungary Agreement on Exchange of Goods and Payment in Beijing on March 30th. According to this agreement, Hungary will supply China with important materials such as machinery, telecommunications equipment, and daily necessities; China will supply Hungary with important materials such as minerals, soybeans, grain, other agricultural products, and daily necessities. The trade volume stipulated in this agreement is 51.7 percent higher than that of 1952, showing the further development of trade between China and Hungary. Xu Xuehan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, signed the agreement on behalf of the Chinese side; Mr. Halász, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Hungary to China and head of the Commercial Delegation, signed the agreement on behalf of the Hungarian side.
    Those who attended the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were: Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Li Zheren, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade; Xu Yixin, Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Zhang Huadong, Director of the Import Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade; Fu Shenglin, Deputy Director of the Export Bureau; Jia Shi, Deputy Director of the Second Bureau; Yang Mian, Director of the Protocol Department; Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Wang Hanmin, Commercial Counselor of the Embassy in Hungary.
    Those who attended the ceremony on the Hungarian side were: Mr. Szarvasy, Deputy Head of the Commercial Delegation of the Government of the People's Republic of Hungary, Mr. Nagy, Commercial Counselor of the Hungarian Embassy in China, and all members of the Commercial Delegation.

### 中匈签订一九五三年交换货物及付款协定

1953-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

    中匈签订一九五三年交换货物及付款协定
    【新华社三十日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府对外贸易部与匈牙利人民共和国政府商务代表团之间，经过友好融洽的谈判，于三月三十日在北京签订了一九五三年中匈交换货物及付款协定。根据这个协定，匈牙利将供给中国以机器、电讯器材、人民生活必需品等重要物资；中国将以矿产品、大豆、粮食及其他农产品以及人民生活必需品等重要物资供给匈牙利。这个协定所规定的贸易额比一九五二年增加百分之五十一点七，显示着中、匈两国间贸易进一步的发展。代表中国方面在这个协定上签字的是中华人民共和国中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒；代表匈牙利方面签订这个协定的是匈牙利人民共和国驻华特命全权大使兼商务代表团团长夏法朗柯。
    参加签字典礼的：中国方面有外交部副部长伍修权，对外贸易部副部长李哲人，外交部苏联东欧司司长徐以新，对外贸易部进口局局长张化东、出口局副局长傅生麟、第二局副局长贾石、交际处处长杨勉，外交部交际处副处长沈平及驻匈牙利大使馆商务参赞王汉民等人。
    匈牙利方面参加典礼的有：匈牙利人民共和国政府商务代表团副团长沙鲁辛斯基，匈牙利驻华大使馆商务参赞那吉及商务代表团全体团员。
