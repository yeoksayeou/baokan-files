Title: Condolence Messages from People's Organizations in East China and Shanghai to
Author: 
Date: 1953-03-18
Page: 2

To the Presidium of the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic:

    We are deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of President Gottwald, an outstanding fighter for the world peace and democracy cause and a close friend of the Chinese people. People from all walks of life in East China and Shanghai extend our deepest condolences to you.
    We, the people of East China and Shanghai, together with the entire Chinese people, will redouble our efforts to strengthen the friendly unity between the Chinese and Czechoslovak people, strengthen the power of the world peace, democracy, and socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and unremittingly struggle for our common cause.
    Long live the eternal friendship and unity between the Chinese and Czechoslovak people!
    Eternal glory to President Gottwald!
    East China Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions
    East China Office of the All-China Youth Federation
    China East China Student Federation
    East China Working Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation
    China East China Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
    Shanghai People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee
    Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions
    Preparatory Committee of the Shanghai Democratic Youth Federation
    Shanghai Student Federation
    Shanghai Democratic Women's Federation
    Shanghai Suburban Farmers Association
    Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
    Shanghai Federation of Industry and Commerce
    Shanghai Federation of Literary and Art Circles
    Preparatory Committee of the All-China Journalists Association Shanghai Branch
    Shanghai Branch of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies
    Shanghai Association for the Popularization of Science and Technology
                      March 15, 1953
