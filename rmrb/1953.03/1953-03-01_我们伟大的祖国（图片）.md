Our Great Motherland
In the construction of our country's first Five-Year Plan, the Wuhan Bridge, which will connect the north-south railway transportation of the Yangtze River and the three towns of Wuhan, will soon enter the construction phase from the design stage. This is a scene of the staff of the Wuhan Bridge Survey and Drilling Team measuring the Han River bridge site on the connecting line.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在我国第一个五年计划建设中，贯通长江南北铁路交通及连接武汉三镇的武汉大桥，不久就要由设计阶段进入施工阶段了。这是武汉大桥测量钻探队的工作人员，正在测量联络线上汉水桥址的情形。
