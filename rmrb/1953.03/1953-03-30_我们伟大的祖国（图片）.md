Author:

Our great motherland
The area downstream of the Yangtze River is now full of the scenery of spring. These are male and female workers from the state-owned Shanghai No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory. They are rowing boats in Longhua, spending a happy holiday.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    长江下游一带，现在已经到处充满了春天的景象。这是国营上海第一棉纺织厂的男女工人。他们正在龙华划着小舟，度过愉快的假日。
