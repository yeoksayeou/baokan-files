Title: Panchen Erdini's Condolence Telegram
Author:
Date: 1953-03-11
Page: 2

To the Funeral Committee for Comrade J.V. Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Socialist Republics and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union:

    Upon hearing the grievous news of the passing of Chairman Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Socialist Republics and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, I am filled with indescribable sorrow. We must transform grief into strength. I am determined to lead the monks and laypeople, to unite closely around the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, to constantly heighten our vigilance, to prevent any enemy sabotage, and to redouble our efforts for the construction of the motherland and new Tibet. And with this practical action, we express our condolences for the great Chairman Stalin—our Chinese people's most beloved friend and mentor, and the great leader of the working people of the world.

    The great Stalin lives forever!
                                            Panchen Erdini
                                    March 7, 1953

### 班禅额尔德尼的唁电

1953-03-11
第2版()
专栏：

    班禅额尔德尼的唁电苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席、苏联共产党中央委员会书记约·维·斯大林治丧委员会：
    惊闻苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席、苏联共产党中央委员会书记斯大林主席逝世，我有说不出的沉痛心情。我们要化悲痛为力量。我坚决率领僧俗人民，紧紧地团结在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的周围，时刻提高警惕性，防止敌人任何阴谋破坏，加倍地为祖国和新西藏的建设而努力。并以此实际行动，来为伟大的斯大林主席——我们中国人民最敬爱的朋友和导师、全世界劳动人民的伟大领袖志哀。
    伟大的斯大林永垂不朽！
                                            班禅额尔德尼
                                    一九五三年三月七日
