Title: Condolences from the Trade Union Federation of Xikang Province

Author: 
Date: 1953-03-11
Page: 3

    Telegram of Condolence from the Trade Union Federation of Xikang Province

    To the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions:

    We are deeply saddened to learn that Comrade J.V. Stalin, the great leader and teacher of the working class and all progressive people in the world, passed away on March 5th. All the workers and staff of Xikang Province, China, express their deepest condolences with utmost grief.

    In these sorrowful days, we will unite even more closely, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, redouble our efforts to accurately complete and overfulfill the national production plan, and strive to defend world peace and build a better future.
                                Trade Union Federation of Xikang Province, China
                              March 7, 1953

### 西康省总工会的唁电

1953-03-11
第3版()
专栏：

    西康省总工会的唁电全苏工会中央理事会：
    痛悉全世界工人阶级及全世界进步人类的伟大领袖和导师约·维·斯大林同志在三月五日与世长辞，中国西康省全体职工，以万分悲痛的心情，致以深挚的哀悼。
    我们在沉痛的日子里，将更亲密地团结起来，在中国共产党和毛主席的领导下，加倍努力，准确地完成和超额完成国家生产计划，为保卫世界和平建设美好的将来而奋斗。
                                中国西康省总工会
                              一九五三年三月七日
