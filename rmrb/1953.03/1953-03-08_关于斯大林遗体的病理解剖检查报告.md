Title: Report on the Pathological Anatomy Examination of Stalin's Remains
Author: 
Date: 1953-03-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 7th: TASS Moscow, March 7th dispatch: Report on the Pathological Anatomy Examination of J.V. Stalin's Remains

    The pathological anatomy examination of the remains revealed a large lesion of cerebral hemorrhage in the subcortical layer of the left cerebral hemisphere. The cerebral hemorrhage damaged important parts of the brain and caused irreversible disturbances in respiration and blood circulation. In addition to cerebral hemorrhage, some hypertrophy of the left ventricle, massive bleeding of the myocardium, gastric and small intestinal mucosa, and vascular sclerosis were found, with particularly prominent sclerosis of the cerebral arteries. These are all consequences of hypertension.
    The results of the pathological anatomy examination of the remains completely confirmed the correctness of the diagnosis of the professors who treated J.V. Stalin.
    Based on the materials from the pathological anatomy examination of the remains, it can be definitively determined that J.V. Stalin's illness had been irreversible from the onset of cerebral hemorrhage. Therefore, various vigorous medical measures could not have a positive effect in preventing the occurrence of a fatal outcome.
    A.F. Tretyakov, Minister of Health of the USSR

### 关于斯大林遗体的病理解剖检查报告

1953-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    关于斯大林遗体的病理解剖检查报告
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科七日电：关于约·维·斯大林遗体的病理解剖检查报告
    由遗体的病理解剖检查看出，在左大脑半球的脑皮质下层结的地方有一大块脑溢血的病灶。脑溢血损害了脑子的重要部分并使呼吸和血液循环受到了不可恢复的障碍。除了脑溢血外，还发现左心室有些肥大，心肌、胃和小肠粘膜大量出血，血管硬化，大脑动脉硬化特别明显。这些都是高血压症的后果。
    遗体的病理解剖检查的结果完全证实了为约·维·斯大林治病的教授们的诊断的正确性。
    根据遗体的病理解剖检查的材料可以确知：约·维·斯大林的病从脑溢血一开始就具有不可恢复性。因此各种有力的医疗措施都不能有正面的效果来阻止致命的后果的发生。
    苏联卫生部部长阿·弗·特雷季雅科夫
