Title: Condolence Messages from Various People's Organizations
Author:
Date: 1953-03-08
Page: 3

Condolence Message from the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defense of Children to the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee:

With deep sorrow, we learned of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Stalin, the helmsman of world peace and the most beloved teacher and protector of children worldwide. We, on behalf of all the children and children's workers of China, express our deepest condolences. Although the great Stalin has unfortunately left us, he will forever live in the hearts of children all over the world. We Chinese children and children's workers vow to unite closely with the Soviet people and all peace-loving people around the world, and continue to wage unremitting struggle for the lofty cause of defending peace and defending children.

Chinese People's National Committee for the Defense of Children
March 6, 1953

Condolence Message from the All-China Sports Federation to the Soviet Sports Committee:

Upon hearing the news of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Stalin, friend and mentor of the working people of the world, all of us athletes and sports workers in China feel the deepest sorrow. The passing of Comrade Stalin is a huge loss for all progressive humanity. Under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, our nation's athletes and sports workers are determined to unite even more closely with the Soviet people, heighten our vigilance, work hard, follow the path indicated by Comrade Stalin, and bravely advance to defend peace in the Far East and the world, and to create human happiness!

All-China Sports Federation
March 6, 1953

Condolence Message from the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to the Moscow Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries:

With deep sorrow, we learned of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Stalin, the great leader of the working people of the world, and the most beloved friend and mentor of the Chinese people. We extend our infinite and heartfelt condolences. We firmly believe that the people of China and the Soviet Union will unite even more closely with the greatest determination, and fight to the end to crush the new war plots of imperialism and for the common cause of defending world peace and security.

Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs
March 7, 1953

Condolence Message from Burhan, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the China Islamic Association, to the Stalin Funeral Committee:

Upon hearing the news of the passing of the great Chairman Stalin, the savior of mankind, the most beloved friend of the Chinese people, and the most sincere protector of all ethnic groups in the world who believe in Islam, I express my most pious condolences on behalf of the Muslims of all of China, with a feeling of immense sorrow!

The people of all ethnic groups in China who believe in Islam must unite more closely under the banner of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, the leader of the Chinese people of all ethnic groups, under the leadership of the Central People's Government, unite with the Soviet people, and fight to the end for the great cause of defending world peace and security, in memory of the passing of Chairman Stalin. Salaam!

Burhan, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the China Islamic Association
March 6, 1953

Condolence Message from the Preparatory Office of the Buddhist Association of China to the Stalin Funeral Committee:

Hearing the news of the passing of the great Chairman Stalin, the true savior, loving father, and mentor of mankind, fills our hearts with grief! We – the Buddhists of China – in the midst of immense sorrow, sincerely pray for the peaceful repose of Chairman Stalin. Dear Soviet friends, we vow to you: for the sake of mankind, for the sake of peace, we will unite with you a hundred times more closely. May the light of Stalin's peace shine upon the whole world. We offer these condolences.

Preparatory Office of the Buddhist Association of China
March 6, 1953

Condolence Message from the North China Methodist Church of China and Eighteen Other Units to the Stalin Funeral Committee:

With incomparably heavy hearts, we mourn Chairman Stalin, the leader of progressive humanity worldwide, the standard-bearer of world peace, and the closest friend of the Chinese people. We Christians in Beijing are willing to strengthen unity with our Soviet co-religionists and the Soviet people, and fight for the defense of world peace.

### 各人民团体的唁电

1953-03-08
第3版()
专栏：

    各人民团体的唁电
    中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会的唁电苏联妇女反法西斯委员会：
    惊闻世界和平的舵手、全世界儿童最敬爱的导师和保护者斯大林同志不幸逝世的消息，我们谨代表全中国的儿童和儿童工作者表示最沉痛的哀悼。伟大的斯大林虽然不幸离开了我们，但他永远活在全世界儿童的心里。我们中国儿童和儿童工作者誓与苏联人民和全世界爱好和平的人民紧密团结一致，继续为保卫和平、保卫儿童的崇高事业作坚持不懈的斗争。
                            中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会
                                      一九五三年三月六日
    中华全国体育总会的唁电苏联体育运动委员会：
    惊悉全世界劳动人民的朋友和导师斯大林同志不幸逝世，我们全中国运动员和体育工作者，感到最深切的悲痛。斯大林同志的逝世，是全世界进步人类的巨大损失。我们全国的运动员和体育工作者在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下，决心和苏联人民更加紧密地团结起来，提高警惕，努力工作，遵循斯大林同志所指示的道路，为保卫远东及世界和平与创造人类幸福而奋勇前进！
                                          中华全国体育总会
                                        一九五三年三月六日
    中国人民外交学会的唁电莫斯科苏联对外文化协会：
    惊悉全世界劳动人民的伟大领袖、中国人民最敬爱的朋友和导师斯大林同志不幸逝世，谨致无限沉痛的哀悼。我们深信：中苏两国人民一定会以最大的坚定性更加紧密地团结一致，为粉碎帝国主义新的战争阴谋并为保卫世界和平与安全的共同事业而奋斗到底。
                                    中国人民外交学会
                                   一九五三年三月七日
    中国伊斯兰教协会筹备委员会主任鲍尔汉的唁电斯大林大元帅治丧委员会：
    惊悉人类的救星、中国人民最敬爱的朋友和全世界信仰伊斯兰教各族人民最诚挚的爱护者伟大的斯大林主席的逝世，谨以万分悲恸的心情，代表全中国的伊斯兰教徒敬致虔诚的吊唁！
    全中国信仰伊斯兰教的各民族人民，必定更加紧密地团结在中国共产党和中国各族人民领袖毛泽东主席的旗帜之下，在中央人民政府的领导下，与苏联人民团结一致，为保卫世界和平与安全的伟大事业奋斗到底，以纪念斯大林主席的逝世。色兰！
    中国伊斯兰教协会筹备委员会主任　鲍尔汉
                 一九五三年三月六日
    中国佛教协会筹备处的唁电斯大林大元帅治丧委员会：
    惊闻真正的人类的救星、慈父和导师伟大的斯大林主席逝世的消息，使我们五内如焚！我们——中国的佛教徒，在万分悲痛之余，虔诚地为斯大林主席的安息祈祷。亲爱的苏联朋友们，我们向你们宣誓：为了人类，为了和平，我们要更加紧密百倍地和你们团结在一起。愿斯大林和平之光，照耀于全世界。谨此吊唁。
                       中国佛教协会筹备处
