Title: Premier Zhou and Others Visit the Office of the Czechoslovak Government Presidium to Offer Condolences

Author:
Date: 1953-03-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 19th: Zhou Enlai, head of the People's Republic of China delegation participating in the funeral of Comrade Klement Gottwald, President of the Czechoslovak Republic, along with delegation members Vice Premier Guo Moruo and Ambassador Tan Xilin, visited the Office of the Czechoslovak Government Presidium on the afternoon of the 18th. Premier Zhou Enlai, on behalf of the Central People's Government of China, the Communist Party of China, and the Chinese people, expressed deep condolences and sympathy to Antonín Zápotocký, Premier of the Czechoslovak Republic, on the passing of Klement Gottwald, President of the Czechoslovak Republic and Chairman of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia. The representatives also signed the condolence book. Also present were Czechoslovak Republic Vice Premiers Viliam Široký, Jaromír Dolanský, Karel Bacílek, Alexej Čepička, Václav Kopecký, and Antonín Novotný.

### 周总理等前往捷政府主席团办公厅吊唁

1953-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理等前往捷政府主席团办公厅吊唁
    【新华社布拉格十九日电】参加捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志丧礼的中华人民共和国代表团团长周恩来总理和团员郭沫若副总理及谭希林大使十八日下午前往捷克斯洛伐克政府主席团办公厅。周恩来总理代表我国中央人民政府、中国共产党和中国人民向捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理安托宁·萨波托斯基，对捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统、捷克斯洛伐克共产党主席克利门特·哥特瓦尔德的逝世表示沉痛的哀悼和慰问。代表们还在吊唁册上签了名。在场的还有捷克斯洛伐克共和国副总理威廉·西罗基、雅罗米尔·杜兰斯基、卡雷尔·巴契列克、阿列克谢·契皮茨卡、瓦茨拉夫·柯别茨基、安托宁·诺沃提尼。
