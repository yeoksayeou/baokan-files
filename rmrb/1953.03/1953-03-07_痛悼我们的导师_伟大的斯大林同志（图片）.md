Title: In Deep Grief We Mourn Our Teacher—The Great Comrade Stalin (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1953-03-07
Page: 4

In Deep Grief We Mourn Our Teacher—The Great Comrade Stalin
    The wreath presented by Chairman Mao Zedong. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Yuan Ling)
    Upon learning of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Stalin, Chairman Mao Zedong, together with Comrades Zhu De, Zhou Enlai, Gao Gang, Lin Boqu, Zhang Wentian, Peng Zhen, Rao Shushi, Deng Xiaoping, Zeng Shan, Ye Jianying, Deng Zihui, Wu Yuzhang, Teng Daiyuan, Xu Teli, Bo Yibo, Liao Chengzhi, Wang Jiaxiang, Huang Kecheng, Cheng Zihua, Wan Yi, Luo Ruiqing, Zhang Zongxun, Wang Congwu, Xi Zhongxun, Xiao Jingguang, and Liu Lantao, went to the Soviet Embassy in our country to offer condolences and requested Ambassador Panyushkin to convey the infinite grief and respect of the Chinese people, the Chinese Communist Party, and the Chinese government to the great leader of the working people of the world, and the most beloved friend and teacher of the Chinese people, Comrade J.V. Stalin. (Special photographer Hou Bo of this newspaper)
    Members of the CPC Central Committee in Beijing pay their respects before Comrade Stalin's portrait. (Photo by Zhu Bo of this newspaper)
    People from all walks of life in Beijing offer wreaths before Comrade Stalin's portrait. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Qi Guanshan)
    Workers at the Beijing Clothing Factory listen with deep sorrow to the broadcast about the unfortunate passing of Comrade Stalin. (Photo by Zhang Li of this newspaper)
    Students of all nationalities from the Central Institute for Nationalities pay their respects before Comrade Stalin's portrait at the Soviet Embassy in our country. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Yuan Ling)
    Cadres from various organs go to the Soviet Embassy in our country to offer condolences for Comrade Stalin. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Qingrui)
    Late at night on March 6th, pedestrians in the streets of Beijing listen with infinite grief to the news of Comrade Stalin's death. (Photo by Zhang Li of this newspaper)
    Upon hearing the news of the unfortunate passing of the great Comrade Stalin, the national flag is lowered to half-mast in Tiananmen Square in the capital to express mourning, and passersby stop to stand in silent tribute. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Jiang Guoxian)
    More than 20 representatives from the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and various democratic parties went to the Soviet Embassy in our country on the 6th to meet with Ambassador Panyushkin and express their deep condolences on the death of Chairman Stalin. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Qi Guanshan)
    Representatives of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission pay their respects before Comrade Stalin's portrait. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Qi Guanshan)

### 痛悼我们的导师——伟大的斯大林同志（图片）

1953-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

    痛悼我们的导师——伟大的斯大林同志
    毛泽东主席敬献的花圈。　（新华社记者袁苓摄）
    毛泽东主席在获知斯大林同志不幸逝世的消息后，特偕同朱德、周恩来、高岗、林伯渠、张闻天、彭真、饶漱石、邓小平、曾山、叶剑英、邓子恢、吴玉章、滕代远、徐特立、薄一波、廖承志、王稼祥、黄克诚、程子华、万毅、罗瑞卿、张宗逊、王从吾、习仲勋、萧劲光、刘澜涛等同志，前往苏联驻我国大使馆吊唁，面请潘友新大使转达中国人民、中国共产党和中国政府对全世界劳动人民的伟大领袖、中国人民最敬爱的朋友和导师约·维·斯大林同志的无限沉痛的悼念和崇敬之意。　（本报特约摄影记者侯波摄）
    在北京的中共中央委员们在斯大林同志遗像前志哀。　（本报朱波摄）
    北京市各界人士在斯大林同志遗像前献花圈。（新华社记者齐观山摄）
    北京被服厂的工人们以沉痛的心情收听关于斯大林同志不幸逝世的广播消息。　（本报张力摄）
    中央民族学院的各族同学在苏联驻我国大使馆的斯大林同志遗像前志哀。　（新华社记者袁苓摄）
    前往苏联驻我国大使馆吊唁斯大林同志的各机关干部。　（新华社记者刘庆瑞摄）
    三月六日深夜，北京街头行人仍以无限悲痛的心情在静听关于斯大林同志逝世的消息。　（本报张力摄）
    伟大的斯大林同志不幸逝世的消息传来，首都天安门前广场下半旗志哀，过路的群众都停下来静默哀悼。　（新华社记者姜国宪摄）
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会及各民主党派代表二十余人，六日前往苏联驻我国大使馆会见潘友新大使，对斯大林主席的逝世，面致沉痛的吊唁。　（新华社记者齐观山摄）
    中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会代表在斯大林同志遗像前志哀。　（新华社记者齐观山摄）
