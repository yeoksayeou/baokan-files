Title: Condolence Telegram from the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1953-03-19
Page: 3

To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia:

We were deeply saddened to hear of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Klement Gottwald, leader of the Communist Party and people of Czechoslovakia. The entire Communist Party members and people of Zhejiang Province, China, extend our deepest condolences. The death of Comrade Klement Gottwald is not only an irreparable loss for the people of Czechoslovakia, but also a major loss for the entire world camp of peace and democracy. In these sorrowful days, we vow with the utmost determination to further strengthen the friendly cooperation between the people of China and Czechoslovakia, and to fight to the end to consolidate the world camp of peace and democracy headed by the Soviet Union and to defend the cause of world peace.

                        The Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China

### 中共浙江省委员会的唁电

1953-03-19
第3版()
专栏：

    中共浙江省委员会的唁电捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会：
    惊闻捷克斯洛伐克共产党和人民的领袖克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志不幸病逝，我们中国浙江省全体共产党员和全省人民，谨致沉痛的哀悼。克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志的逝世，不仅是捷克斯洛伐克人民无可补偿的损失，也是整个世界和平民主阵营的重大损失。在这悲痛的日子里，我们誓以最大的坚定性，进一步加强中捷两国人民的友好合作，为巩固以苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营和保卫世界和平的事业而奋斗到底。
                        中国共产党浙江省委员会
