Title: Railway Workers and Medical Personnel from Various Regions Volunteer to Go to the Korean Front, While Performing Arts Troupes from East, Southwest, and Northwest China Go to Korea to Offer Comforts
Author:
Date: 1953-03-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report: Railway workers and medical personnel from various regions, responding to Chairman Mao's call to strengthen the struggle against U.S. aggression and aid Korea, have volunteered to go to Korea to participate in the work.

More than one hundred employees from the Shanghai Railway Administration and the Hangzhou Transportation Branch of the bureau left Shanghai and Hangzhou on the 13th and 10th of this month, respectively, volunteering to go to the Korean front to participate in transportation work. Another group of employees from the Zhengzhou Railway Administration volunteered to go to Korea to participate in the war to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea. They left Zhengzhou on the 25th and 26th, respectively. Prior to this, there had already been thirty-eight groups of employees from the Zhengzhou Railway Administration volunteering to work in Korea, and three hundred and three of them have performed meritorious service on the Korean front. Du Pimo, a volunteer worker from the Chang'an Electrical Section who assisted Korea, was also awarded the Second Class Warrior Honor Medal of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

On the 26th, another sixty railway employees from the Beijing Railway Administration volunteered to go to Korea to participate in the war to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea, and were warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life in Beijing. From January 1953 to the present, 328 railway employees from the Beijing Railway Administration have volunteered to go to Korea to participate in the war to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea.

The surgical team of the Shandong Provincial Medical Team Volunteering to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, consisting of ten people, led by Zhang Guanzeng, the team leader and deputy director of the Provincial First Hospital, set off for the Korean front on March 25, carrying valuable medical equipment.

The Chongqing Volunteer Health Work Team to Aid Korea went to the Korean front on the 14th. The team is led by Wang Huazhou, attending surgeon of the Southwest Hospital, and the entire team includes ten well-known surgeons and nurses from Chongqing. The 19th team of the Medical and Disease Prevention Service Team of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea set off from Guiyang City on the 10th to work on the Korean front. The team is composed of medical workers from Guiyang City, including Yang Rongxun, director of internal medicine at Guiyang Medical College, Liu Yiwan, a doctor from the Guizhou Provincial Ethnic Health Mobile Work Team, and doctors and nurses from hospitals in Guizhou Province and Guiyang City.

The Guangzhou Volunteer Medical Surgery Team to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea and the Guangdong Provincial Volunteer Medical Surgery Team to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, led by team leaders Rao Zhenwei and Kuang Gongdao, departed from Guangzhou on the morning of the 23rd to go to Korea.

More than 400 medical workers from Liuzhou City, Guangxi Province, and employees from the Liuzhou Railway Administration left Liuzhou on the 18th and 20th, respectively, volunteering to go to Korea to perform medical and transportation work.

Railway workers and medical personnel from various regions were warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life in their local areas before their departure.

[Another dispatch] The East China Performing Arts Troupe, the Southwest Performing Arts Troupe, and the Northwest Performing Arts Troupe, which volunteered to go to Korea to offer comfort, recently went to Korea to conduct comfort work. The East China Performing Arts Troupe includes Peking Opera teams, song and dance teams, film teams, and acrobatics teams; the Southwest Performing Arts Troupe is composed of actors from the Sichuan People's Theatre and some Peking Opera, Sichuan Opera, and folk art dramas, etc. The Northwest Performing Arts Troupe includes actors from Qinqiang Opera, Henan Opera, puppet shows, shadow plays, song and dance, and film, etc., and the famous Henan Opera actress Chang Xiangyu also participated. Before the departure of each performing arts troupe, they were warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life in their local areas.

### 各地铁路职工和医务工作者  相继志愿前往朝鲜前线参加工作  华东、西南、西北文艺工作团赴朝进行慰问

1953-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    各地铁路职工和医务工作者
    相继志愿前往朝鲜前线参加工作
    华东、西南、西北文艺工作团赴朝进行慰问
    据新华社讯：各地铁路职工和医务工作者，响应毛主席加强抗美援朝斗争的号召，相继志愿前往朝鲜参加工作。
    上海铁路管理局和该局杭州运输分局职工一百余人，在本月十三日和十日分别离开上海和杭州，志愿前往朝鲜前线参加运输工作。郑州铁路管理局又有一批职工志愿到朝鲜参加抗美援朝的运输工作。他们已分别在二十五、二十六两日离开郑州。在这以前，郑州铁路管理局志愿赴朝工作的职工已经有三十八批，其中有三百零三人在朝鲜前线立了功。长安电务段志愿援朝工人杜丕模还荣获了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国二级战士荣誉勋章。
    二十六日，北京铁路管理局又有六十位铁路员工，志愿赴朝参加抗美援朝工作，受到北京市各界人民的热烈欢送。从一九五三年一月到目前为止，北京铁路管理局已有三百二十八位铁路员工志愿赴朝参加抗美援朝工作。
    山东省医务工作者志愿组成的抗美援朝医疗队外科队十人，在该队队长、省立第一医院副院长张冠增率领下，于三月二十五日携带着贵重的医疗器械启程去朝鲜前线。
    重庆市志愿援朝卫生工作队已于十四日前往朝鲜前线工作。该队由西南医院外科主治医师王化洲任队长，全队包括重庆市著名的外科医师和护士共十人。中国人民抗美援朝总会卫生工作委员会各地医防服务队第十九队，在十日自贵阳市启程赴朝鲜前线工作。该队由贵阳市医务工作者组成，其中有贵阳医学院内科主任杨荣勋，贵州省民族卫生巡回工作队医生刘轶丸，贵州省、贵阳市各医院医生和护士等。
    广州市抗美援朝志愿医疗手术队和广东省抗美援朝志愿医疗手术队，二十三日晨在队长饶振维、邝公道率领下从广州出发前往朝鲜。
    广西省柳州市医务工作者和柳州铁路管理局职工共四百多人，分别在十八、二十两日离开柳州，志愿前往朝鲜担任医疗和运输工作。
    各地铁路职工和医务工作者在临行前受到当地各界人民的热烈欢送。
    【又讯】　志愿赴朝慰问的华东文艺工作团、西南文艺工作团和西北文艺工作团在日前分别前往朝鲜进行慰问工作。华东文艺工作团包括京剧队、歌舞队、电影队和杂技队；西南文艺工作团是以四川人民剧团和部分京剧、川剧、曲艺剧等演员组成。西北文艺工作团包括秦腔、豫剧、木偶戏、皮影、歌舞和电影等的演员，著名的豫剧演员常香玉也参加。各文艺工作团出发前，都受到当地各界热烈欢送。
