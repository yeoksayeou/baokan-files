Title: Condolence Message from the Zhejiang Provincial Federation of Trade Unions
Author: 
Date: 1953-03-11
Page: 3

Condolence Message from the Zhejiang Provincial Federation of Trade Unions to the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions:

    We are shocked to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade J.V. Stalin, the great leader and teacher of the working people of the world. The entire working class of Zhejiang Province, China, expresses its deepest condolences.

    In this sorrowful time, we, the workers of the entire province, must, with the greatest determination, under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, bring into play our high level of creativity and enthusiasm, continue to carry out the patriotic labor emulation movement, guarantee the completion and over-completion of the state production plan to build our great motherland, and unite even more closely with the Soviet Communist Party and the Soviet people, heighten our vigilance, and struggle to the end to defend the security of the motherland and defend world peace!
                                China Zhejiang Provincial Federation of Trade Unions
                                March 7, 1953

### 浙江省总工会的唁电

1953-03-11
第3版()
专栏：

    浙江省总工会的唁电全苏工会中央理事会：
    惊悉全世界劳动人民的伟大领袖和导师约·维·斯大林同志不幸逝世的消息，中国浙江省全体工人万分哀悼。
    在这个悲痛的日子里，我们全省工人一定要以最大的坚定性在毛主席和中国共产党的领导下，发挥高度的创造性与积极性，继续开展爱国主义劳动竞赛运动，保证完成与超额完成国家生产计划来建设我们伟大祖国，并和苏联共产党及苏联人民更加紧密地团结在一起，提高警惕，为保卫祖国安全、保卫世界和平而奋斗到底！
                                中国浙江省总工会
                                一九五三年三月七日
