Title: Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Municipal Federation of Trade Unions Hold Photo Exhibition of Comrade Stalin's Life and Work

Author: 
Date: 1953-03-23
Page: 3

In order to commemorate the great Stalin, the most beloved teacher and friend of the Chinese people, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions are holding a "Photo Exhibition of Comrade Stalin's Life and Work" in the Friendship Hall of the Working People's Cultural Palace. The exhibition is scheduled to open officially on the 22nd, and the content includes: Comrade Stalin's childhood and early revolutionary activities; Comrade Stalin's activities from the First Russian Revolution in 1905 to the Second Russian Revolution in February 1917; Comrade Stalin and Comrade Lenin preparing and leading the great October Socialist Revolution; Comrade Stalin leading the Soviet people to build socialism; Comrade Stalin leading the Soviet people to defeat fascism; Comrade Stalin leading the Soviet people to advance victoriously towards communism, and the situation of the Soviet and Chinese people mourning Comrade Stalin. Comrade Stalin's works are also on display at the exhibition, in both Russian and Chinese versions. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 北京市中苏友协和市总工会  举办斯大林同志生平事业图片展览会

1953-03-23
第3版()
专栏：

    北京市中苏友协和市总工会
    举办斯大林同志生平事业图片展览会
    为了纪念中国人民最敬爱的导师和朋友伟大的斯大林，北京市中苏友好协会和北京市总工会特在劳动人民文化宫友谊厅举办“斯大林同志生平事业图片展览会”。展览会定二十二日正式开放，内容包括：斯大林同志的幼年时代和早期革命活动，斯大林同志在一九○五年俄国第一次革命到一九一七年二月俄国第二次革命时期的活动；斯大林同志和列宁同志在一起准备并领导伟大的十月社会主义革命；斯大林同志领导苏联人民建成了社会主义；斯大林同志领导苏联人民战胜了法西斯主义；斯大林同志领导苏联人民胜利地向共产主义前进以及苏联和中国人民悼念斯大林同志的情况。斯大林同志的著作也陈列在展览会里，有俄文和中文两种版本。（新华社）
