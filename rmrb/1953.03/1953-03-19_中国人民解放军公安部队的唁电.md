Title: Condolences from the Public Security Forces of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1953-03-19
Page: 2

To the Funeral Committee for Comrade Gottwald:

    Upon hearing the news of the passing of Comrade Gottwald, Chairman of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia and President of the Czechoslovak Republic, all personnel of our department are filled with immense grief. Comrade Gottwald dedicated his glorious life to the happiness of the Czechoslovak people and the cause of human peace; his passing is a major loss for the people of Czechoslovakia and the world. We will transform our sorrow into strength, strengthen the unity between the Chinese and Czech peoples, further consolidate and develop the close friendship between the people of China and Czechoslovakia, further enhance the strength of the world peace and democracy camp led by the Soviet Union, and jointly strive to defend world peace.
    All commanders and combatants of the Public Security Forces of the Chinese People's Liberation Army

### 中国人民解放军公安部队的唁电

1953-03-19
第2版()
专栏：

    中国人民解放军公安部队的唁电哥特瓦尔德同志治丧委员会：
    捷克斯洛伐克共产党主席、捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统哥特瓦尔德同志逝世消息传来后，我部全体人员不胜哀悼。哥特瓦尔德同志，为捷克斯洛伐克人民的幸福和人类和平事业，走完了光荣的一生；他的逝世，是捷克斯洛伐克和世界人民的重大损失。我们将化悲痛为力量，加强中捷人民的团结，进一步巩固和发展中捷两国人民的亲密友谊，进一步增强以苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营的力量，为保卫世界和平而共同奋斗。中国人民解放军公安部队全体指挥员、战斗员
