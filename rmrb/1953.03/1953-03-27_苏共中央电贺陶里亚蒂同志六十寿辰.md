Title: CPSU Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram on Comrade Togliatti's Sixtieth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1953-03-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 26th: TASS Moscow, March 26th telegram: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union sent a telegram to Comrade Palmiro Togliatti, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party, congratulating him on his sixtieth birthday. The telegram is as follows: To Comrade Palmiro Togliatti, Rome:

    On the occasion of your sixtieth birthday, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union extends warm and fraternal congratulations to you, the glorious leader of the heroic Italian Communist Party.

    We congratulate you personally, one of the outstanding leaders of the international communist movement, and the long-tested and proven leader of the Italian working people in the struggle for peace and national independence, for democracy and socialism.

    For the benefit of the Italian workers and peasants, for the benefit of all progressive humanity, we wish you, dear Comrade Togliatti, health and longevity.

                        The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union

### 苏共中央电贺陶里亚蒂同志六十寿辰

1953-03-27
第4版()
专栏：

    苏共中央电贺陶里亚蒂同志六十寿辰
     【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十六日电：苏联共产党中央委员会致电意大利共产党中央委员会总书记帕·陶里亚蒂同志，祝贺他的六十寿辰。电文如下：罗马帕尔米罗·陶里亚蒂同志：
    苏联共产党中央委员会在你六十寿辰之际，谨向你，光荣的意大利共产党的英勇的领袖致热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺。
    我们向你本人，国际共产主义运动的杰出领袖之一、意大利劳动人民争取和平和民族独立、争取民主和社会主义的斗争中的久经锻炼和考验的领袖祝贺。
    为了意大利工人和农民的利益，为了全体进步人类的利益，我们祝你，亲爱的陶里亚蒂同志健康和长寿。
                        苏联共产党中央委员会
