Title: Condolence Message from the Working Committee of the Chinese Inland Waterway Transport Trade Union

Author:

Date: 1953-03-17
Page: 3

To the Central Council of Czechoslovak Trade Unions,转 (transmitted to) the Czechoslovak Transport Trade Union:

    We are deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of the leader of the Czechoslovak people, Gottwald. All the staff and workers of the Chinese Inland Waterway Transport Trade Union are extremely grieved and express our deepest condolences.

                    Working Committee of the Chinese Inland Waterway Transport Trade Union
                              March 15, 1953

### 中国内河航运工会工作委员会的唁电

1953-03-17
第3版()
专栏：

    中国内河航运工会工作委员会的唁电捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会转捷克斯洛伐克运输工会：
    痛悉捷克斯洛伐克人民领袖哥特瓦尔德不幸逝世，中国内河航运工会全体职工万分悲痛，谨致深切的哀悼。
                    中国内河航运工会工作委员会
                              一九五三年三月十五日
