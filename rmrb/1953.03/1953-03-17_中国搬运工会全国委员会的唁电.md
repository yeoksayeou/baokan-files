Title: Condolence Telegram from the National Committee of the Chinese Transport Workers' Union
Author:
Date: 1953-03-17
Page: 3

To the Central Council of Czechoslovak Trade Unions, forwarded to the Czechoslovak Transport Workers' Union:

We are shocked to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Gottwald, a dear friend of the Chinese people and a great leader of the Czech working people. We extend our deepest condolences. We, the transport workers of all of China and the people of all of China, under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, will unite even more closely with the great Czechoslovak people and fight to the end to defend world peace.

中国搬运工会全国委员会 (National Committee of the Chinese Transport Workers' Union)
March 15, 1953

### 中国搬运工会全国委员会的唁电

1953-03-17
第3版()
专栏：

    中国搬运工会全国委员会的唁电捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会转捷克斯洛伐克运输工会：
    惊悉中国人民亲爱的朋友、捷克劳动人民伟大的领袖哥特瓦尔德同志不幸逝世的消息，谨致万分沉痛的哀悼。我们全中国的搬运工人和全中国的人民，在毛主席和中国共产党的领导下，将更加紧密地与伟大的捷克斯洛伐克人民永远地团结在一起，为保卫世界和平而奋斗到底。
                        中国搬运工会全国委员会
                              一九五三年三月十五日
