Title: "New China Exhibition" Held in Paris
Author:
Date: 1953-03-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 28th. According to the "l'Humanité", the "New China Exhibition" opened this week at the Renaissance Library in Paris, welcoming the French people to visit. Exhibits include: precious documents, works of art, children's drawings, books, poster paintings, stamps, handicrafts, photographs, and the writings of French progressive journalists and writers who have visited People's China in the past two years.

The exhibition is scheduled to last for two weeks.

### 巴黎举行“新中国展览会”

1953-03-02
第4版()
专栏：

    巴黎举行“新中国展览会”
    【新华社布拉格二十八日电】据“人道报”报道，“新中国展览会”于本周在巴黎文艺复兴图书馆开幕，欢迎法国人民参观。展览品包括：珍贵文献、艺术品、儿童图画、书籍、标语画、邮票、工艺品、照片以及在过去两年中访问过人民中国的法国进步记者和作家的著作。
    展览会期定为两个星期。
