Title: Tianjin Christian Churches and Organizations Hold Meeting to Support the Rosenbergs

Author:
Date: 1953-03-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 2nd — Representatives from forty-six Christian churches and organizations in Tianjin held a meeting last week to protest the Eisenhower administration's deliberate killing of the Rosenbergs. All representatives present first held a prayer for the Rosenbergs and then held a discussion. Gao Menghe, Chairman of the Tianjin District Council of the Church of Christ in China, and Wu Yongtian, pastor of the Desheng Church of Christ in China, and others, unanimously condemned the US government's conspiracy to persecute peace-loving people in the United States in their speeches, and expressed their firm support for the peace-loving Rosenbergs. The meeting concluded with the adoption of a letter of protest.

### 天津基督教教会及团体  集会声援罗森堡夫妇

1953-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    天津基督教教会及团体
    集会声援罗森堡夫妇
    【新华社天津二日电】天津市四十六个基督教教会及团体的代表在上周举行集会，抗议美国艾森豪威尔政府蓄意杀害罗森堡夫妇。全体与会代表首先为罗森堡夫妇举行了祈祷，并进行了座谈。中华基督教会天津区会主席高梦鹤、中华基督教得胜堂牧师吴永田等，在发言中一致斥责美国政府这种迫害美国爱好和平的人民的阴谋，并表示坚决声援和平人士罗森堡夫妇。会议最后通过了抗议书。
