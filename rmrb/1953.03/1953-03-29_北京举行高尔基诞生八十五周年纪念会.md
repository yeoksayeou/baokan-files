Title: Beijing Commemorates the 85th Anniversary of Gorky's Birth
Author:
Date: 1953-03-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 28th: Beijing held a "Commemoration Meeting for the 85th Anniversary of Gorky's Birth" jointly organized by the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. More than 800 people from literary, drama, film, music, fine arts, dance, and folk art circles in the capital attended.

Also present were Zhukov, the representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in China, and many other Soviet friends.

The commemoration meeting was presided over by Lao She, a member of the National Committee of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and Vice President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. At the meeting, Feng Xuefeng gave a report on "Gorky and Chinese Writers." In his report, Feng Xuefeng said: Studying Gorky's immortal works and studying Soviet literature is helpful to our work and struggle. We not only feel this way today, but we have always felt this way in the past. Soviet literature, founded by the great Gorky, is our guide forward and also gives us the strength to move forward.

Soviet literary worker Latynina gave a report entitled "Gorky - A Communist Fighter." She pointed out: Gorky's works have always been a great source of encouragement for the Soviet people in socialist construction and the anti-fascist struggle.

Finally, actors from the Beijing People's Art Theater recited Gorky's work: "The Song of the Stormy Petrel."

After the meeting, the film "The Artamonov Business," adapted from Gorky's famous work, was shown.

### 北京举行高尔基诞生八十五周年纪念会

1953-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    北京举行高尔基诞生八十五周年纪念会
    【新华社二十八日讯】　二十八日是伟大的革命文豪高尔基诞生八十五周年，中华全国文学艺术界联合会、北京市中苏友好协会和苏联对外文化协会联合举办“高尔基诞生八十五周年纪念会”。到会有首都文学、戏剧、电影、音乐、美术、舞蹈、曲艺界等八百多人。
    到会的还有苏联对外文化协会驻我国代表茹克福和苏联友人多人。
    纪念会由中华全国文学艺术界联合会全国委员会委员、北京市中苏友好协会副会长老舍主持。会上，冯雪峰报告“高尔基和中国作家”。冯雪峰在报告中说：学习高尔基的不朽作品和学习苏联文学是有助于我们的工作和斗争的。我们不仅在今天感到如此，在过去也是一样。以伟大的高尔基为其奠基者的苏联文学是我们前进的指标，同时也给我们前进的力量。
    苏联文学工作者拉特尼柯娃以“高尔基——共产主义的战士”为题作报告。她指出：高尔基的作品，一直是苏联人民在社会主义建设和反法西斯斗争中的巨大的鼓舞力量。
    最后，北京人民艺术剧院演员朗诵高尔基的作品：“海燕”。
    会后并放映根据高尔基的名著编成的电影：“阿托莫诺夫一家”。
