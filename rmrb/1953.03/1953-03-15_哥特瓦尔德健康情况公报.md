Title: Gottwald's Health Bulletin
Author:
Date: 1953-03-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 14th: TASS, Prague, March 14th dispatch: The Czechoslovak News Agency reported that the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and the Presidium of the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic issued a bulletin on the health of the President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Klement Gottwald, stating: On the morning of March 12th, Comrade President of the Republic Klement Gottwald suffered from acute pneumonia and pleurisy. In the evening, his body temperature was 37.7 degrees Celsius, his pulse was 108 beats per minute, and his blood pressure was: maximum 120, minimum 80. All necessary measures are being taken to treat Comrade Klement Gottwald's illness.
    The bulletin was issued after being signed by the doctors treating the President.
    The news of Comrade President K. Gottwald's illness was immediately notified to the Party Presidium and the government that evening. The Party Presidium and the government paid great attention to Comrade K. Gottwald's treatment and condition. Diagnostic bulletins will be issued regarding his health and treatment.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 14th: TASS, Prague, March 14th dispatch: According to the Czechoslovak News Agency, the following is a bulletin on the health of the President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Klement Gottwald, at 16:00 on March 13, 1953:
    On the morning of March 13th, the health of Comrade Klement Gottwald, Chairman of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and President of the Republic, deteriorated due to a sudden drop in blood pressure and a general weakening of blood circulation. Immediate medical measures were taken to prevent the continued development of the general weakening of blood circulation. However, Comrade Klement Gottwald's health remains serious. Medical treatment is continuing to eliminate the weakening of blood circulation.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 14th: TASS, Prague, March 14th dispatch: The Czechoslovak News Agency reported a bulletin signed by the doctors treating President Gottwald regarding President Gottwald's health condition at 21:00 on March 13th, stating: Comrade Klement Gottwald's health suddenly deteriorated on the morning of March 13th, with suspected pleural effusion. After his health condition permitted, a chest puncture test was performed, and blood-like secretions were found. It was decided to consult with a surgeon. Medical treatment is continuing. There is no change in his health condition.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 14th: TASS, Prague, March 14th dispatch: The Czechoslovak News Agency reported: The following is a bulletin on the health of the President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Klement Gottwald, at 4:00 on March 14, 1953:
    The surgeon who was called in for consultation decided to perform a chest puncture and blood transfusion. After these medical treatments were administered, Klement Gottwald's health remains serious. The pulse rate is 100 to 140 beats per minute, and the pulse pressure is weakened. Breathing is considerably impaired: the respiratory rate is 26 to 42 breaths per minute. Blood pressure remains low: maximum 130, minimum 80.

### 哥特瓦尔德健康情况公报

1953-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    哥特瓦尔德健康情况公报
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社布拉格十四日电：捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会主席团和捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府主席团关于捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统克利门特·哥特瓦尔德健康情况的公报说：三月十二日上午，共和国总统克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志患急性肺炎和肋膜炎。晚间体温摄氏三十七点七度，脉搏每分钟一百零八次，血压是：最高一百二十，最低八十。正在采取各种必要的措施来治疗克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志的病。
    公报是经过为总统治病的医生们签字后发表的。
    关于共和国总统克·哥特瓦尔德同志患病的消息，当晚就通知党的主席团和政府。党的主席团和政府对克·哥特瓦尔德同志的治疗和病情予以很大的注意。关于健康情况和治疗情况将发表诊断公报。
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社布拉格十四日电：据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，一九五三年三月十三日十六时捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统克利门特·哥特瓦尔德的健康情况的公报如下：
    三月十三日上午，捷克斯洛伐克共产党主席、共和国总统克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志的健康情况，由于血压突然降低和血液循环普遍减弱而趋恶化。经立即采取医疗措施后，才制止住血压循环普遍减弱的继续发展。但是，克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志的健康情况仍然严重。现在正继续施行医疗以消除血液循环减弱的现象。
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社布拉格十四日电：捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道由为总统治病的医生签字的关于哥特瓦尔德总统三月十三日二十一时健康情况的公报说：克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志健康情况在三月十三日上午突然恶化，疑有胸腔溢血。在健康情况许可以后，就进行了胸部穿刺试验，发现有血样分泌物。决定延请外科医生会诊。现正继续施行医疗。健康情况没有变化。
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社布拉格十四日电：捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道：一九五三年三月十四日四时捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统克利门特·哥特瓦尔德健康情况的公报如下：
    召请前来会诊的外科医生决定实行胸部穿刺并输血。施行了这些医疗以后，克利门特·哥特瓦尔德的健康情况仍然严重。脉搏频率每分钟一百次到一百四十次，脉压减弱。呼吸受到相当的障碍：呼吸次数每分钟二十六次至四十二次。血压仍低：最高一百三十，最低八十。
