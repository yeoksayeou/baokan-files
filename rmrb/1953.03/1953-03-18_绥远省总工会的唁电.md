Title: Condolence Message from the Suiyuan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1953-03-18
Page: 3

To the Central Council of Trade Unions of Czechoslovakia:

We, the working class of the entire Suiyuan province, are deeply saddened to hear the unfortunate news of the passing of Comrade Klement Gottwald, Chairman of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, a close comrade and friend of the Chinese working class. The passing of Comrade Gottwald is a misfortune and loss for the working people of Czechoslovakia and all peace-loving people of the world. With utmost grief, we send this telegram of condolence. Under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong and the Chinese Communist Party, we will further strengthen the unity between the people of China and Czechoslovakia and strive to defend world peace!

                                      Suiyuan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions of China
                                      March 15, 1953

### 绥远省总工会的唁电

1953-03-18
第3版()
专栏：

    绥远省总工会的唁电捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会：
    中国工人阶级亲密的同志和朋友、捷克斯洛伐克共产党主席克利门特·哥特瓦尔德不幸逝世的噩耗传来，我们全绥远的工人阶级感到无限悲痛。哥特瓦尔德同志的逝世，这是捷克斯洛伐克劳动人民与全世界爱好和平人民的不幸和损失。我们怀着万分沉痛的心特电悼唁。我们在毛泽东主席和中国共产党的领导下，将更进一步加强中捷两国人民的团结，为保卫世界和平而奋斗！
                                      中国绥远省总工会
                              一九五三年三月十五日
