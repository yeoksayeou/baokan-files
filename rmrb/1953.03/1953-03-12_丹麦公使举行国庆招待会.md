Title: Danish Minister Holds National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1953-03-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 11th: The Danish Minister to China, Mr. Munk, held a reception in Beijing at 5:30 PM on March 11th to celebrate Denmark's National Day.

More than one hundred people were invited to the reception. Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture, and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, were all invited to attend. Diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various countries to China were also present.

### 丹麦公使举行国庆招待会

1953-03-12
第1版()
专栏：

    丹麦公使举行国庆招待会
    【新华社十一日讯】丹麦驻我国公使穆克于三月十一日下午五时半在北京举行招待会，庆祝丹麦国庆日。
    应邀参加招待会的共百余人。中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫、对外贸易部副部长雷任民、文化部副部长丁西林、北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等均应邀参加。到会的并有各国驻华外交使节和使馆人员。
