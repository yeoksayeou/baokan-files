Title: Guo Moruo and Liu Ningyi Congratulate the Convening of the Vietnam National Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1953-03-22
Page: 4

    Guo Moruo and Liu Ningyi
    Congratulate the Convening of the Vietnam National Peace Congress
    [Xinhua News Agency, 21st dispatch] Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, sent a telegram to the Vietnam National Peace Congress on the 19th, congratulating the convening of the congress. The telegram reads as follows: To the Vietnam National Peace Congress:
    Upon learning that the Vietnam National Peace Congress will be held on the 20th of this month, I am sending this telegram to respectfully wish the congress success. We congratulate the Vietnamese people on achieving new and great accomplishments in the struggle against aggression and in defense of peace. We congratulate the increasingly consolidated and developing fraternal solidarity between the Chinese and Vietnamese peoples. We congratulate the common cause of defending Asian and world peace in advancing from victory to victory under the great banner of Stalin.
             Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee
            March 19, 1953
    [Xinhua News Agency, 21st dispatch] Liu Ningyi, Secretary-General of the Peace Liaison Committee of the Asian and Pacific Regions, sent a telegram to the Vietnam National Peace Congress on the 19th, congratulating the convening of the congress. The telegram reads as follows: To the Vietnam National Peace Congress:
    Deeply excited to learn that the Vietnam National Peace Congress will be held on the 20th of this month. The Vietnamese people's struggle to resist aggression and defend Asian and world peace is not isolated. The people of the Asian and Pacific regions and even the peace-loving people of the whole world are sympathizing with you and supporting you. I wish the congress success, and wish the Vietnamese people greater victories in their struggle to defend the freedom and independence of their motherland and to defend world peace.
      Liu Ningyi, Secretary-General of the Peace Liaison Committee of the Asian and Pacific Regions
        March 19, 1953

### 郭沫若和刘宁一  祝贺越南全国和平大会的召开

1953-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若和刘宁一
    祝贺越南全国和平大会的召开
    【新华社二十一日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若十九日打电报给越南全国和平大会，祝贺大会的召开。电文如下：越南全国和平大会：
    获悉越南全国和平大会于本月二十日召开，特驰电敬祝大会成功。我们祝贺越南人民在反抗侵略、保卫和平的斗争中取得新的伟大成就。我们祝贺中越两国人民血肉相连的友好团结日益巩固与发展。我们祝贺保卫亚洲和世界和平的共同事业在伟大的斯大林旗帜下从胜利走向胜利。
             中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席  郭沫若
            一九五三年三月十九日
    【新华社二十一日讯】亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书长刘宁一十九日打电报给越南全国和平大会，祝贺大会的召开。电文如下：越南全国和平大会：
    获悉越南全国和平大会于本月二十日召开，深感兴奋。越南人民为反抗侵略保卫亚洲及世界和平的斗争，不是孤立的，亚洲及太平洋区域以至全世界爱好和平的人民都在同情你们、支持你们。祝大会成功，并祝越南人民保卫祖国的自由独立和保卫世界和平的斗争获得更大胜利。
      亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书长  刘宁一
        一九五三年三月十九日
