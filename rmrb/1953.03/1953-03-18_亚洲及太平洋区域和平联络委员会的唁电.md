Title: Condolences from the Liaison Committee of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace

Author: 
Date: 1953-03-18
Page: 2

Condolences from the Liaison Committee of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace to Chairman Hodinova-Spurna of the Czechoslovak Committee for the Defense of Peace:

    The unfortunate passing of President Gottwald, the beloved leader of the Czechoslovak people and an outstanding fighter for the world peace and democracy cause, is a great loss to the world peace cause. We, along with the 1.6 billion peace-loving people in the Asian and Pacific regions, extend our deepest condolences to the Czechoslovak Committee for the Defense of Peace and all the Czechoslovak people. We firmly believe that peace-loving people in the Asian and Pacific regions and around the world will further unite and unremittingly struggle for the noble cause of opposing war and defending peace.

              Liaison Committee of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace
                                    Secretary-General Liu Ningyi
                              March 16, 1953

### 亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会的唁电

1953-03-18
第2版()
专栏：

    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会的唁电捷克斯洛伐克保卫和平委员会霍迪诺娃—斯普尔纳主席：
    捷克斯洛伐克人民敬爱的领袖、世界和平民主事业的卓越战士哥特瓦尔德总统的不幸逝世，是世界和平事业的巨大损失。我们和亚洲及太平洋区域十六亿爱好和平的人民在一起，谨向捷克斯洛伐克保卫和平委员会和全体捷克斯洛伐克人民致深切的哀悼。我们坚信亚洲及太平洋区域以及全世界爱好和平的人类，将进一步团结起来，为反对战争保卫和平的崇高事业而奋斗不懈。
              亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会
                                    秘书长　刘宁一
                              一九五三年三月十六日
