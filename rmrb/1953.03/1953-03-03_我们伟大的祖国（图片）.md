Author:
Date: 1953-03-03
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The work of publicizing the Marriage Law in Lushan County, Henan Province, has achieved great results. The practice of arranged and forced marriages has been basically eliminated throughout the county. This is a scene of Song Zhixing, a model cadre from Yuzhuang Township, Lushan County, conveying instructions on implementing the Marriage Law.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    河南省鲁山县宣传婚姻法工作，有很大成绩。全县基本上消灭了包办、强迫的婚姻。这是鲁山县余庄乡模范干部宋志兴正在传达关于贯彻婚姻法指示的情形。
