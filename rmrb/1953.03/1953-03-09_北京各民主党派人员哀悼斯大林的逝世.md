Title: Beijing Democratic Parties Mourn Stalin's Death
Author:
Date: 1953-03-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, March 7th: The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang convened an emergency meeting at 10:00 AM on the 7th, attended by members and alternate members of the Central Committee, as well as all cadres from the Central Headquarters and the Beijing Municipal Branch, to express their condolences on the passing of Comrade Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union. At the meeting, Li Jishen, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, first led all attendees in standing and observing three minutes of silence for Chairman Stalin. Subsequently, Chairman Li Jishen delivered a speech, stating: "The Chinese First Revolutionary Civil War received great assistance from Lenin. Later, Chairman Stalin succeeded Lenin and gave the Chinese revolution the greatest encouragement, assistance, and support; therefore, China has today's great victory. We feel extremely saddened by the unfortunate passing of Chairman Stalin!" In her speech, He Xiangning pointed out: "Twenty-nine years ago, at the time of Lenin's death, the Chinese revolution was just beginning, but today is different; under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, and with the assistance of the Soviet Union, China is undertaking its first Five-Year Plan and is not afraid of imperialist aggression. Today, we mourn Chairman Stalin just as Mr. Sun Yat-sen mourned Lenin twenty-nine years ago. We hope that everyone will be of one heart and one mind, unite even more closely, and strive even harder!" Minister Wang Kunlun said in his speech: We must transform grief into strength, shatter all the enemy's rumors and sabotage, unite more closely around Chairman Mao Zedong and the Chinese Communist Party, and strengthen our fighting spirit and strength.
    The Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang has telegraphed its organizations in various localities, instructing them to conduct mourning and memorial services under the unified arrangements of the local People's Representative Conferences and Consultative Committees. All staff members in various localities will suspend entertainment and banquets for three days to express their condolences.

Xinhua News Agency, March 8th: The central organizations and Beijing organizations of the China Democratic League, the China Democratic National Construction Association, the China Association for Promoting Democracy, the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, the China Zhi Gong Party, and the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League expressed their condolences on the passing of Chairman Stalin with boundless grief.

The Central Committee members and all staff of the headquarters of the China Democratic League in Beijing, together with all representatives attending the report meeting on cultural and educational work of local organizations and all members of the Beijing branch of the Democratic League, went to the Soviet Embassy in our country to express their condolences.

All working cadres of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce and the Beijing Municipal Branch jointly held a memorial service on the 7th to mourn Chairman Stalin. At the meeting, Stalin's oath at the Second Soviet Congress after Lenin's death was read out. After the meeting, everyone lined up and went to the Soviet Embassy to offer condolences. The working cadres of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce will study Stalin's biography and related documents about Stalin and the Chinese revolution from the 9th.

The staff of the headquarters and the Beijing branch of the China Association for Promoting Democracy held memorial services for Chairman Stalin for two consecutive days, the 6th and 7th. With unrestrained grief, they spoke of Chairman Stalin's life and career, and recalled the guiding significance of Chairman Stalin's writings for the Chinese revolution and the great inspiration for their personal progress. On the afternoon of the 7th, all members and staff in Beijing from the headquarters and the Beijing branch went to the Soviet Embassy in our country to offer condolences.

All the staff of the Central Executive Bureau and the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party went to the Soviet Embassy in our country on the morning of the 7th, led by Li Boqiu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Central Executive Bureau, and Yan Xinmin, Minister of the Propaganda Department. On the same day, the headquarters also issued two consecutive announcements to its subordinate local organizations, calling on all party members to unite more closely under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, and to struggle unremittingly for the consolidation and development of the great friendship between China and the Soviet Union, and for the sacred cause of defending world peace.

All personnel of the Beijing Office of the China Zhi Gong Party went to the Soviet Embassy in our country on the 7th to present a wreath on behalf of the Central Committee of the Zhi Gong Party to express their condolences. The office also notified its members to seriously study the documents concerning the mourning of Chairman Stalin's death.

After learning of the unfortunate news of Chairman Stalin's death, all personnel of the Beijing Office and the North China General Branch of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League also went to the Soviet Embassy in our country on the 7th to offer condolences.

Xinhua News Agency, March 7th: To mourn the death of the leader and teacher of the working people of the world – Marshal Stalin, the Jiusan Society convened an emergency meeting of the Central Committee members in Beijing at 8:00 PM on the afternoon of the 6th. Those present included Chairman Xu Deheng and Central Committee members Tu Changwang, Pei Wenzhong, Yuan Hanqing, Xue Yu, Yang Zhaolian, Zhang Xiti and more than 20 others. After three minutes of silence at the meeting, Chairman Xu Deheng gave a brief report on Marshal Stalin's guidance and help to the Chinese revolution during his lifetime, and the loss his death brought to the people of the world. The meeting adopted a telegram of condolence to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and decided to instruct local branches to hold memorial services. After the meeting ended in a solemn atmosphere, the attending Central Committee members led all staff to the Soviet Embassy to offer condolences.

### 北京各民主党派人员哀悼斯大林的逝世

1953-03-09
第3版()
专栏：

    北京各民主党派人员哀悼斯大林的逝世
    【新华社七日讯】中国国民党革命委员会在七日上午十时召集中央委员、候补中央委员、中央总部和北京市分部全体干部举行紧急会议，对苏联部长会议斯大林主席的逝世表示哀悼。会上，中国国民党革命委员会李济深主席首先率领全体与会人员起立，为斯大林主席默哀三分钟。接着，李济深主席致词说：“中国第一次国内革命时得到列宁很大的帮助。后来斯大林主席继承列宁，更给中国革命以最大的鼓舞、援助和支持；因而中国才有今日的伟大胜利。我们对于斯大林主席不幸逝世，感到万分沉痛！”何香凝委员在会上讲话时指出：“二十九年前列宁的逝世，中国革命还在开端，今天却不同了；在毛主席和中国共产党领导下和苏联的援助下，中国已进行着第一个五年建设计划，不怕帝国主义的侵略。我们今天哀悼斯大林主席也像中山先生二十九年前哀悼列宁一样，希望大家一心一德，更加团结，更加奋斗！”王昆仑部长在讲话中说：我们必须化悲痛为力量，粉碎敌人的一切造谣和破坏，更加团结在毛泽东主席和中国共产党的周围，加强战斗的感情和力量。
    中国国民党革命委员会中央委员会已电告各地组织，在当地各界人民代表会议协商委员会的统一布置下，进行吊唁和追悼，各地工作人员一律停止娱乐和宴会三日，以志哀悼。
    【新华社八日讯】中国民主同盟、中国民主建国会、中国民主促进会、中国农工民主党、中国致公党、台湾民主自治同盟等民主党派中央组织和北京市组织的全体人员，以无限悲痛的心情，哀悼斯大林主席的逝世。
    中国民主同盟在京中央委员和总部全体人员，以及正在出席地方组织文教工作汇报会的全体代表和民盟北京市支部全体人员一同到苏联驻我国大使馆表示哀悼。
    中国民主建国会总会和北京市分会的全体工作干部，在七日联合举行哀思会，悼念斯大林主席，会上宣读了斯大林主席在列宁逝世后第二次苏维埃代表大会上的誓词。会后，全体列队到苏联大使馆吊唁。民主建国会总会的工作干部将自九日起学习斯大林传略和斯大林与中国革命的有关文件。
    中国民主促进会总会和北京市分会的工作人员，六、七两日连续举行了悼念斯大林主席的哀思会，他们以难以抑制的悲痛心情，谈到斯大林主席的生平和事业，回忆了斯大林主席的著作对中国革命的指导意义和对他们个人进步的巨大启发作用。七日下午，总会和北京市分会全体在京委员和工作人员前往苏联驻我国大使馆吊唁。
    中国农工民主党中央执行局和北京市委员会全体工作人员在七日上午由中央执行局副秘书长李伯球、宣传部部长严信民等率领前往苏联驻我国大使馆吊唁。总部并于同日向所属地方组织连续发出两项通告，号召全体党员：更紧密地团结在中国共产党和毛主席的领导下，为巩固和发展中苏两国伟大友谊，为保卫世界和平的神圣事业而奋斗不懈。
    中国致公党北京办事处的全体人员，七日到苏联驻我国大使馆，代表致公党中央委员会献花圈致哀。该处并通知所属党员认真学习有关哀悼斯大林主席逝世的文件。
    台湾民主自治同盟北京办事处和华北总支部的全体人员，获悉斯大林主席逝世的不幸消息后，也在七日到苏联驻我国大使馆吊唁。
    【新华社七日讯】九三学社为哀悼全世界劳动人民的领袖和导师——斯大林大元帅的逝世，在六日下午八时召集在京中央委员举行紧急会议。到会的有许德珩主席及中央委员涂长望、裴文中、袁翰青、薛愚、杨肇燫、张席禔等二十多人，会议在默哀三分钟后，由许德珩主席就斯大林大元帅生前对于中国革命的指导和帮助，以及他的逝世给全世界人民带来的损失，作简短的报告。会议通过了致苏联共产党中央委员会、苏联部长会议的唁电，并决定指示各地分社进行追悼。会议在沉痛的气氛中结束后，由到会中央委员带领全体工作人员到苏联大使馆吊唁。
