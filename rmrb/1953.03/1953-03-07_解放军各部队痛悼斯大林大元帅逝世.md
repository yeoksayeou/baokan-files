Title: PLA Units Deeply Mourn the Passing of Marshal Stalin
Author:
Date: 1953-03-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 6th — Upon hearing the news of Marshal Stalin's death, the vast numbers of commanders, fighters, and staff of the People's Liberation Army's land, sea, and air forces felt boundless grief. These steel warriors could not help but shed tears. Units on the training grounds immediately ceased their exercises, and units in classrooms immediately stopped their lessons. From the coasts of the motherland to the remote frontiers, from companies to headquarters, millions of people stood in silent tribute before the portraits of Marshal Stalin, adorned with black纱. They firmly expressed their determination to transform grief into strength. Naval combat hero Zhao Xiao'an said: "We must further learn from the Soviet army and strive to build a strong people's navy for our country." Infantry combat hero Li Qiyuan said with grief: "We should unite even more closely with the Soviet people and use the victory of the cause of defending peace to forever commemorate Marshal Stalin." Qi Jianhua, the creator of the rapid literacy method, expressed his determination to actively study Marxism-Leninism. The heroic literary and art soldier Gao Yubao expressed his desire to study hard and actively complete the book he is writing. Commanders expressed their desire to conscientiously study Comrade Stalin's military science in order to accelerate the construction of our country's modern national defense force. Soldiers of the air force, navy, and border defense forces unanimously expressed their determination to vigilantly defend our motherland's territorial waters, airspace, and borders, and be ready at any time to give a fatal blow to invaders who dare to intrude. Many non-Party soldiers and members of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League expressed their desire to intensify their studies and work, and strive to become glorious Communist Party members. While mourning the death of Stalin, the commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army are determined to take practical actions to fight for the defense of peace in the Far East and the world.

### 解放军各部队痛悼斯大林大元帅逝世

1953-03-07
第3版()
专栏：

    解放军各部队痛悼斯大林大元帅逝世
    【新华社六日讯】斯大林大元帅逝世的消息传来后，中国人民解放军陆、海、空军广大指挥员、战斗员和工作人员，都无限地悲痛。钢铁战士们，禁不住流下了眼泪。正在操场的部队立即停止了操作，正在课堂的部队立即停止了授课。从祖国的海岸到遥远的边疆，从连队到机关，千万个人都在围着黑纱的斯大林大元帅的肖像前，脱下军帽默默地肃立致哀。他们坚定地表示，要化悲痛为力量。海军战斗英雄赵孝庵说：“我们要更进一步向苏联军队学习，为建设我国强大的人民海军而奋斗。”步兵部队战斗英雄李启元悲痛地说：“我们应该更加紧密地与苏联人民团结在一起，用保卫和平事业的胜利，来永远纪念斯大林大元帅。”速成识字法创造者祁建华表示，决心积极学习马克思列宁主义。英雄的文艺战士高玉宝表示，要努力学习，积极完成正在写的书。指挥员们表示要认真学习斯大林同志的军事科学，以加速我国现代化的国防军的建设。空军、海军和边防部队的战士们，一致表示要警惕地保卫着我们祖国的领海、领空和边境，随时准备给敢予进犯的侵略者以致命的打击。许多非党员战士和中国新民主主义青年团团员表示，要加紧学习和工作，争取成为一个光荣的共产党员。中国人民解放军的指挥员战斗员们，在痛悼斯大林的逝世时，都决心以实际行动，为保卫远东与世界和平而奋斗。
