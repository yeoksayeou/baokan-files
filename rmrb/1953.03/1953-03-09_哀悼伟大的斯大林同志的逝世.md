Condolence Message from the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union:

The entire party membership and people of Northeast China, with infinite sorrow, mourn the unfortunate passing of Comrade Stalin, our people's most revered teacher and closest friend. The passing of Comrade Stalin is an immeasurable loss not only for the Soviet people but also for the Chinese people and the entire peaceful and democratic camp, as well as all peace-loving people in the world. Comrade Stalin was the leader of the working class of the world and the banner of human liberation. The victory of the Chinese people's revolutionary cause and construction cause cannot be separated from Comrade Stalin's consistent care, encouragement, and support.

The name of Comrade Stalin is especially dear to the entire party membership and people of Northeast China. The people of the Northeast will never forget that after crushing the military power of fascist Germany in 1945, the heroic Soviet army, under the leadership and education of Comrade Stalin, immediately turned eastward, annihilated the elite of Japanese imperialism in the Northeast—the Kwantung Army—at one stroke, directly helping the people of the Northeast liberate themselves from the brutal oppression and enslavement of Japanese fascism, and facilitating the final victory of the Chinese People's War of Resistance Against Japan. During the period of our national economic recovery and transformation and the period of large-scale construction, Comrade Stalin and the Soviet people he cultivated and taught gave us tremendous spiritual, material, and technical assistance, dispatching a large number of outstanding experts to sincerely and selflessly help us with construction. Every victory in our people's revolutionary cause and construction cause is closely linked to the name of Comrade Stalin.

In these sorrowful days of Comrade Stalin's passing, the entire party membership and people of Northeast China must continue to strengthen, with the greatest determination, their trust and support for the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet Socialist Republics Union created by Lenin and Stalin. Under the wise leadership of Comrade Mao Zedong and our Party Central Committee, we will forever unite closely with the great Soviet people, hold high the banner of Lenin and Stalin, and fight to the end for the consolidation and construction of our great motherland, for the cause of defending world peace and democracy, and for the cause of communism!

Stalin's immortal brilliance will forever illuminate the path forward for the Chinese people, and the great Stalin will be immortal!

		Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
		March 7, 1953

Condolence Message from the Northeast Administrative Committee to the Presidium of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Socialist Republics and Funeral Committee of Marshal Joseph Vissarionovich Stalin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union:

Upon hearing the shocking news of the passing of Comrade Stalin, the great leader and teacher of the working people of the world and the closest friend of the Chinese people, the entire people of the Northeast are in extreme grief. We extend our heartfelt condolences.

The people of our Northeast have a deep understanding of this assistance and will never forget it. We will continue to follow the direction indicated by Comrade Stalin, and under the leadership of Chairman Mao, unite closely with the Soviet people for a long time to come, and fight to the end for the construction of our great motherland and the defense of the Far East and world peace.
