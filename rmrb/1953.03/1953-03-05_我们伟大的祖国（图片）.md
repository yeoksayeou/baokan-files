Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-03-05
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Workers at the No. 2 Machinery Factory in Shenyang have created China's first self-made giant "five-ton steam hammer." It is nine meters and fifty centimeters high and weighs one hundred and fifty tons. Its maximum striking power is 43,800 kilogram-meters. This is a picture of technicians and workers at the factory studying the assembly of the "five-ton steam hammer."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    沈阳第二机械厂的工人，创造了我国自制的第一台巨大的“五吨蒸汽锤”。它有九公尺五十公分高，一百五十吨重。它的最大打击能力为四万三千八百公斤公尺。这是该厂技术员和工人们正在研究“五吨蒸汽锤”的装配问题。
