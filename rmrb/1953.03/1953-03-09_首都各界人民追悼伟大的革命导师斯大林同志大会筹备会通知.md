Notice from the Preparatory Committee for the Memorial Service for the Great Revolutionary Teacher Comrade Stalin by People from All Walks of Life in the Capital

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, The National Committee of the People's Political Consultative Conference of China, and the people of all circles in the capital, will hold a memorial service for the great revolutionary teacher Comrade Stalin at Tiananmen Square at 4:54 PM today. At 5:00 PM, the conference will fire salutes, and factories and trains in the city will sound their sirens. At this time, all personnel in factories, mines, enterprises, troops, government agencies, schools, and people's organizations in the city, as well as residents in streets and alleys, and marching people and vehicles, etc., except for work that cannot be interrupted (such as power rooms and furnaces of industrial and mining enterprises in production, trains in motion, and ongoing medical operations and emergency treatments, etc.), should stop their activities for five minutes and stand silently on the spot to express their condolences.

Preparatory Committee for the Memorial Service for the Great Revolutionary Teacher Comrade Stalin by People from All Walks of Life in the Capital
March 9, 1953

### 首都各界人民追悼伟大的革命导师斯大林同志大会筹备会通知

1953-03-09
第3版()
专栏：

    首都各界人民追悼伟大的革命导师斯大林同志大会筹备会通知
    中国共产党中央委员会、中华人民共和国中央人民政府、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会及首都各界人民，定于今天下午四时五十四分在天安门前举行追悼伟大的革命导师斯大林同志大会。在下午五时正，大会将鸣放礼炮，本市各工厂、火车将齐鸣汽笛，这时候，全市一切工矿、企业、部队、机关、学校、人民团体的人员和大街小巷的居民以及行进中的人马和车辆等，除不能中断的工作（如正在生产中的工矿企业的动力间、熔炉、行进中的火车以及进行中的医疗手术和紧急治疗等）外，均应停止活动五分钟，并就地肃立，静默志哀。
    首都各界人民追悼伟大的革命导师斯大林同志大会筹备会
    一九五三年三月九日
