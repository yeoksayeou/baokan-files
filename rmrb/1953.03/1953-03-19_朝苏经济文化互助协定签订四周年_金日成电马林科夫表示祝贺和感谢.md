Title: Fourth Anniversary of the Signing of the Sino-Soviet Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement: Kim Il-sung Sends Telegram to Malenkov to Express Congratulations and Gratitude
Author:
Date: 1953-03-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 18th: March 17th marked the fourth anniversary of the signing of the Korean-Soviet Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of Korea, Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, and Li Dong-geon, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, sent telegrams to Soviet Minister Council Chairman Malenkov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet Voroshilov, and Foreign Minister Molotov on the 16th, respectively, expressing congratulations and gratitude.

Kim Il-sung's congratulatory telegram to Malenkov stated: "On the occasion of the fourth anniversary of the signing of the Economic and Cultural Mutual Assistance Agreement between the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, I would like to express my heartfelt congratulations and gratitude to you, respected Chairman Comrade, and through you to the Soviet government and all the Soviet people, on behalf of the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the entire Korean people, and myself.

"The Korean people, who are waging a Fatherland Liberation War to defend the freedom and independence of their country and oppose the aggression of the American imperialist aggressors, will continue to strengthen the eternal friendship with the Soviet people and further strengthen and develop the economic and cultural ties between our two countries in accordance with the principles of this agreement.

"The support and solidarity of the people of the entire democratic camp, headed by the great Soviet Union, inspires the Korean people in their heroic struggle for the final victory over the enemy in the Fatherland Liberation War.

"May the power of the socialist countries, the bulwark of peace and security for the people of the world, be further strengthened in the future."

### 朝苏经济文化互助协定签订四周年  金日成电马林科夫表示祝贺和感谢

1953-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    朝苏经济文化互助协定签订四周年
    金日成电马林科夫表示祝贺和感谢
   【新华社平壤十八日电】三月十七日是朝苏经济文化互助协定签订四周年纪念日。朝鲜内阁首相金日成、最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉和外务省副相李东建，在十六日分别致电苏联部长会议主席马林科夫、最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫和外交部长莫洛托夫，表示祝贺和感谢。
    金日成给马林科夫的贺电说：值此朝鲜民主主义人民共和国与苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟签订经济与文化互助协定四周年之际，我谨以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府、全体朝鲜人民和我个人的名义，向尊敬的主席同志，您，并通过您向苏联政府和全体苏联人民表示衷心的祝贺和感谢。
    为保卫自己祖国的自由和独立、反对美帝国主义侵略者而进行祖国解放战争的朝鲜人民，今后仍将依照这个协定的各项原则，进一步加强与苏联人民的永恒友谊，并进一步加强和发展我们两国之间经济与文化的联系。
    以伟大的苏联为首的民主阵营的全体人民对朝鲜人民的支持和声援，鼓舞着朝鲜人民在祖国解放战争中为最后战胜敌人而进行的英雄的斗争。
    祝全世界人民和平与安全的堡垒——社会主义国家的威力，在今后得到进一步的加强。
