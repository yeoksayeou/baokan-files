Title: People from All Circles in Japan Participate in Preparatory Work for Peace Liaison Committee
Author:
Date: 1953-03-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Twentieth. According to the "Peace Liaison News," the organ of the Preparatory Committee of the Japan Peace Liaison Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions: many groups and people from all walks of life in Japan have responded to the call to establish the "Japan Peace Liaison Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions" and have enthusiastically written to the preparatory committee expressing their willingness to participate in the preparatory work for the establishment of the "Japan Peace Liaison Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions." These letters include people from a wide range of classes, including workers, farmers, women, students, science, culture, arts, and religious circles. As of January 8, seventeen groups have agreed to serve as members of the preparatory committee. In addition to the Japan Peace Committee, these include: the Japan-China Friendship Association, the Government Office Workers' Union Council, the Japan Farmers' Association, the Japan National Relief Association, the Buddhist Peace Forum, the People's Literature Society, the "Susumu-za" theater troupe, and the Japan War Bereaved Students Memorial Society.
    Among the 178 people who have joined the preparatory committee as preparatory members, in addition to those previously reported, there are also well-known peace activists Ikuo Oyama, Toku Tamba Saburo, Yoshitaro Hirano, former Communist Party member of the House of Representatives, Karoku Hosokawa, member of the House of Representatives of the Labor-Farmer Party, Hisao Kuroda, Umeyo Adachi of the Socialist Party Reconstruction Liaison Council, Jiji Yoshida, chairman of the All Japan Federation of Industrial Organizations, Yuichi Horie, vice chairman of the Japan-Soviet Friendship Association, Kei Hoashi, a member of the House of Representatives of the Left-wing Socialist Party, Senator Tomi Kora, Professor Hiroshi Minami, Abbot Gyozō Abe of the religious circles, Ritsumeikan University President Hiroshi Suekawa, and Hideo Sakurai, Managing Director of Ba Shoji Co., Ltd., and other representatives from all walks of life.

### 日本各界人民参加和平联络委会筹备工作

1953-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    日本各界人民参加和平联络委会筹备工作
    【新华社二十日讯】据亚洲及太平洋区域日本和平联络委员会筹备会机关报“和平联络新闻”消息：日本全国许多团体和各界人士纷纷响应成立“亚洲及太平洋区域日本和平联络委员会”的号召，踊跃写信给筹备会，表示愿意参加成立“亚洲及太平洋区域日本和平联络委员会”的筹备工作。在这些来信中，包括了工人、农民、妇女、学生、科学文化艺术界、宗教界等广泛的各阶层人士。到一月八日为止，答应担任筹备委员的团体已达十七个。其中，除日本拥护和平委员会以外，还有：日中友好协会、官厅工会协议会、日本农民协会、日本国民救援会、佛教徒和平恳谈会、人民文学社、“前进座”剧团、日本战殁学生纪念会等。
    在已参加筹备会担任筹备委员的一百七十八人中，除前已报道的以外，还有著名和平人士大山郁夫、淡德三郎、平野义太郎，共产党前众议员细川嘉六，劳农党的众议员黑田寿男，社会党再建联络会的足立梅市，全日本产业别工会会议主席吉田资治，日苏亲善协会副会长堀江邑一，左派社会党众议员帆足计，参议员高良富，教授南博，宗教界的阿部行藏，立命馆大学校长末川博，巴商事株式会社专务董事樱井英雄等各界的代表人物。
