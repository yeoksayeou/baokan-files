Title: Qi Guanshan
Author: Qi Guanshan
Date: 1953-03-08
Page: 2

    The people of the capital, with immense grief, mourn the passing of Comrade Stalin. Tens of thousands of people, day and night, continuously go to the Soviet Embassy in our country to offer condolences. Xinhua News Agency reporter Qi Guanshan photographed.
