Title: Condolence Telegram from the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese News Publishing and Printing Workers' Union
Author: 
Date: 1953-03-17
Page: 3

To the Central Council of Czechoslovak Trade Unions, transfer to the Czechoslovak Journalists Association:
We are shocked and saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Gottwald, the beloved leader of the Czechoslovak Republic and a close friend of the Chinese people. All staff and workers in the Chinese news publishing and printing industries express our deepest condolences with boundless grief.
Under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, the staff and workers in the Chinese news publishing and printing industries are determined to unite with the people of the Czechoslovak Republic and jointly strive for the cause of defending peace and democracy!

Preparatory Committee of the Chinese News Publishing and Printing Workers' Union
March 15, 1953

### 中国新闻出版印刷工会筹备委员会的唁电

1953-03-17
第3版()
专栏：

    中国新闻出版印刷工会筹备委员会的唁电捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会转捷克斯洛伐克新闻工作者协会：
    惊悉捷克斯洛伐克共和国人民敬爱的领袖、中国人民亲密的朋友哥特瓦尔德同志不幸逝世，中国新闻出版印刷业全体职工，以无限沉痛心情致以深切哀悼。
    中国新闻出版印刷业职工在中国共产党和毛泽东主席领导下，决心与捷克斯洛伐克共和国人民团结一致，共同为保卫和平与民主的事业而奋斗！
              中国新闻出版印刷工会筹备委员会
                              一九五三年三月十五日
