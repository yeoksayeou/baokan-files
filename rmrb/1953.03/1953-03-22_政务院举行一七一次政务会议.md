Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 171st Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1953-03-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 20th: The 171st meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 20th. The meeting listened to and approved the report by Wang Yifu, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government, on the current situation in disaster areas and the prevention of the spring famine. The meeting also approved various appointment and removal proposals to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行一七一次政务会议

1953-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行一七一次政务会议 【新华社二十日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百七十一次政务会议在二十日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府内务部王一夫副部长所作的关于目前灾区情况和预防春荒的报告。会议并通过了提请中央人民政府委员会批准的各项任免案。
