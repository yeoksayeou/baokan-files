Title: Soviet Embassy in China Issues Announcement Thanking Chinese People for Condolences on Stalin's Death
Author:
Date: 1953-03-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 14th:

    The Soviet Embassy in China has issued an announcement expressing gratitude to the Chinese people for their mourning and condolences on the death of the great revolutionary teacher, Comrade Stalin. The announcement reads as follows:

    The Embassy of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics in the People's Republic of China hereby expresses its sincere gratitude to all people's organizations and people from all walks of life who have offered condolences on the passing of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, J.V. Stalin, and on the great loss suffered by the Soviet government and all nationalities of the Soviet Union—the death of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, J.V. Stalin.

### 苏联驻我国大使馆发表公告  答谢中国人民对斯大林逝世的哀悼

1953-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联驻我国大使馆发表公告
    答谢中国人民对斯大林逝世的哀悼
    【新华社十四日讯】苏联驻我国大使馆发表公告，对中国人民哀悼和吊唁伟大革命导师斯大林同志的逝世，表示谢意。公告文如下：
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟驻中华人民共和国大使馆，兹向追悼苏联部长会议主席约·维·斯大林及对于苏联政府与苏联各族人民所遭遇的重大损失——苏联部长会议主席约·维·斯大林逝世——表示慰问的各人民团体和各界人士，敬致深诚的谢意。
