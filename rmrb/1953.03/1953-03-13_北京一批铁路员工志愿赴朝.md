Title: A Batch of Railway Employees in Beijing Volunteer to Go to Korea

Author:
Date: 1953-03-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 12th: The Beijing Railway Administration of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government has recently had another 180 railway employees volunteer to go to Korea to participate in the war to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea's transportation work. Among this group are various skilled workers, and many labor models and advanced workers.

### 北京一批铁路员工志愿赴朝

1953-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    北京一批铁路员工志愿赴朝
   【新华社十二日讯】中央人民政府铁道部北京铁路管理局，最近又有一百八十多位铁路员工，志愿赴朝鲜参加抗美援朝的运输工作。这批人中，有各种技术工人，并且有很多劳动模范和先进工作者。
