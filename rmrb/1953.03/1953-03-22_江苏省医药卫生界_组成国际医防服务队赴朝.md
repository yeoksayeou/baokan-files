Title: Jiangsu Province Medical and Health Community Forms International Medical and Preventive Service Team to North Korea
Author:
Date: 1953-03-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 21st—The Jiangsu Province medical and health community has formed an International Medical and Preventive Service Team, led by team leader Liu Zhengque, and departed on the 17th to work on the Korean front lines. More than 50 people from the Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City medical and health community went to the station to see them off. The team is composed of 12 volunteers, including medical college teachers, surgeons, and nurses from Zhenjiang, Wuxi, and other places, who brought their own complete set of surgical medical equipment and power generation equipment when they departed.

### 江苏省医药卫生界  组成国际医防服务队赴朝

1953-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    江苏省医药卫生界
    组成国际医防服务队赴朝
    【新华社南京二十一日电】江苏省医药卫生界组成国际医防服务队，由队长刘正确率领，于十七日出发赴朝鲜前线工作。江苏省、南京市医药卫生界五十多人到车站欢送。该队系由镇江、无锡等地的医学院教师、外科医生和护士等十二人志愿组成，出发时自带全套外科医疗器械及发电设备等。
