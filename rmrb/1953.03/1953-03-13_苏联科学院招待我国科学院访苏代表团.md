Title: Soviet Academy of Sciences Hosts Delegation from the Chinese Academy of Sciences Visiting the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1953-03-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th—The Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences held a tea party on the 10th to entertain all members of the Chinese Academy of Sciences delegation visiting the Soviet Union. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, who is currently in Moscow, was also invited to attend. On the Soviet side, those present included Academician Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences; Topchiev, Secretary of the Presidium of the Academy of Sciences; Volgin and Bardin, Vice Presidents of the Academy of Sciences; and Academicians Lavrentiev, Oparin, Belyankin, Khristianovich, as well as Petrovsky, President of Moscow University, and Lysenko, President of the Lenin Academy of Agricultural Sciences, among others.

    Academician Nesmeyanov delivered a welcoming speech at the tea party. He said that at the time of the arrival of the Chinese Academy of Sciences delegation visiting the Soviet Union, the Soviet Union and the world were experiencing the painful event of Stalin's death. This grief will make the friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union even more consolidated, because the unity and friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union is one of Comrade Stalin's teachings. The Chinese Academy of Sciences delegation visiting the Soviet Union are our dearest guests, and we will introduce all our things as much as possible; and hope that the scientific circles of the two countries will cooperate more effectively from now on, and better serve the happiness of mankind. Nesmeyanov emphasized: The people of the Soviet Union and China standing together become an invincible force; Soviet scientific workers and Chinese scientific workers standing together will also become an invincible force.

    Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, said in reply: In these sorrowful days, we Chinese scientific workers must strive to learn from the Soviet Union, and first of all, we must learn from our greatest scientist, Comrade Stalin. Guo Moruo said: Chairman Mao wants us to sincerely learn from Soviet scientists. On behalf of the Chinese scientific community, I would like to express my heartfelt thanks to the Soviet Academy of Sciences. Our Chinese scientists will repay Comrade Stalin by earnestly implementing Chairman Mao's instructions on learning from the Soviet Union.

    At the meeting, the Chinese Academy of Sciences delegation presented gifts to the Soviet Academy of Sciences. The gifts included: a model of Zhang Heng's seismograph from the Eastern Han Dynasty, a model of the Peking Man skull, a model of an ancient Chinese compass chariot, Guo Moruo's research works on oracle bone inscriptions and bronze inscriptions, and embroidered portraits of Marx, Engels, Lenin, Stalin, and Mao Zedong produced in Hangzhou, etc. Finally, Nesmeyanov reported to the Chinese Academy of Sciences delegation on the organizational structure of the Soviet Academy of Sciences.

### 苏联科学院招待我国科学院访苏代表团

1953-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联科学院招待我国科学院访苏代表团 【新华社莫斯科十二日电】苏联科学院主席团在十日举行茶会招待中国科学院访苏代表团全体人员。目前在莫斯科的中国科学院院长郭沫若也应邀参加。苏联方面到会的有苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫，科学院主席团秘书托普切夫，科学院副院长沃尔京、巴尔金，以及拉夫连捷夫、奥巴林、别利安金、赫里斯佳诺维奇等院士和莫斯科大学校长彼得罗夫斯基、列宁农业科学院院长李森科等人。
    涅斯米扬诺夫院士在茶会上致欢迎辞。他说，在中国科学院访苏代表团到来的时候，苏联和全世界发生了斯大林逝世这样悲痛的事情。这种悲痛将使我们中苏两国人民的友谊更加巩固，因为中苏两国人民的团结友好是斯大林同志的教言之一。中国科学院访苏代表团是我们最亲爱的来宾，我们将把我们所有的东西尽量介绍出来；并希望两国科学界从此更加有效地进行合作，更好地为人类的幸福服务。涅斯米扬诺夫强调说：苏中两国人民站在一起，就成为不可战胜的力量；苏联科学工作者和中国科学工作者站在一起，也将成为不可战胜的力量。
    中国科学院院长郭沫若致答词说：在这悲痛的日子里，我们中国科学工作者要努力向苏联学习，首先要向我们最伟大的科学家斯大林同志学习。郭沫若说：毛主席要我们诚心诚意地向苏联科学家学习。我代表中国科学界对苏联科学院表示衷心的感谢。我们中国科学家将以好好地执行毛主席关于学习苏联的指示，来报答斯大林同志。
    会上，中国科学院访苏代表团向苏联科学院赠送礼品。礼品有：东汉张衡候风地动仪模型、北京人头骨模型、我国古代指南车模型、郭沫若甲骨金文研究著作和杭州出品的马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林和毛泽东绣像等。最后，涅斯米扬诺夫向中国科学院访苏代表团报告了苏联科学院的组织机构的情况。
