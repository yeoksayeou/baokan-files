Our Great Motherland
“Swearing to the great Comrade Stalin, we must strive to learn from the Soviet Union and build our motherland.” This is the determination expressed by the Chinese working class in these days of deep mourning for Comrade Stalin. This female spinner, Youth League member Meng Shuzhen, and her assistant had just wiped away their tears when they ran back to the lathe, redoubling their efforts in their work.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    “向伟大的斯大林同志宣誓，我们要努力学习苏联，建设我们的祖国。”这是中国工人阶级在沉痛悼念斯大林同志的日子里所表示的决心。这位女旋盘工、青年团员孟淑真和她的助手刚刚擦干了眼泪，就跑回旋床边来，加倍用力地做活。
