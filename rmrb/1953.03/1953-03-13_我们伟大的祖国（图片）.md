Author:
Date: 1953-03-13
Page: 1

Our Great Motherland
    In the construction of our motherland, everyone is striving to master new technologies. This is Ling Manli, a Youth League member and apprentice worker in the tool workshop of the Jingwei Textile Machinery Manufacturing Factory. In one year, she mastered the most precise machine in the factory—the jig boring machine. The working accuracy of this boring machine is one ten-thousandth of an inch, equal to one-thirtieth of a hair.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在祖国的建设事业中，每个人都在努力掌握新的技术。这是经纬纺织机械制造厂工具工场的实习女工、青年团员凌曼丽。她在一年中掌握了厂里最精密的一部机器——钻模镗床。这部镗床的工作准确度是万分之一英寸，等于一根头发的三十分之一。
