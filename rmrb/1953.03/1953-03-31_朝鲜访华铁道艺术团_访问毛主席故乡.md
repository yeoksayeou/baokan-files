Title: Korean Railway Art Troupe Visits Chairman Mao's Hometown
Author:
Date: 1953-03-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: The Korean Democratic People's Republic Railway Art Troupe, since arriving in our country, has performed and visited in Beijing, Tianjin, Jinan, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Liuzhou and other places, and has been warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life and railway employees. After performing and visiting in Liuzhou, the troupe visited Shaoshan Township, Xiangtan County, Hunan Province, the hometown of Chairman Mao, on March 29. Xiao Minsong, Vice Chairman of the Hunan Provincial Anti-US and Aid Korea Branch, and Yang Shousan, Secretary-General of the Xiangtan Prefecture Anti-US and Aid Korea Branch, accompanied them. After arriving in Shaoshan Township, the art troupe first visited Chairman Mao's former residence. Then, the troupe performed eight artistic programs, which were warmly welcomed by the masses.

### 朝鲜访华铁道艺术团  访问毛主席故乡

1953-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜访华铁道艺术团
    访问毛主席故乡
    据新华社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访华铁道艺术团自来我国后，已在北京、天津、济南、南京、上海、杭州、广州、柳州等地演出、访问，受到各界人民和铁路员工的热烈欢迎。该团在柳州演出、访问后，在三月二十九日访问了毛主席故乡——湖南省湘潭县韶山乡。陪同前往的有湖南省抗美援朝分会副主席萧敏颂、湘潭专区抗美援朝分会秘书长杨寿三等人。艺术团到达韶山乡后，首先参观了毛主席的故居。接着该团表演了八个文艺节目，受到了群众热烈的欢迎。
