Title: Telegram of Condolence from the Southwest Military Region
Author: 
Date: 1953-03-18
Page: 2

Telegram of Condolence from the Southwest Military Region
To Comrade Čepička, Minister of National Defense of the Czechoslovak Republic:

	We are deeply saddened to learn of the passing of Comrade Gottwald, the great leader of the Czechoslovak people and an outstanding fighter for the cause of world peace and democracy. We extend our deepest condolences.

	The death of Comrade Gottwald is a great loss to the Czechoslovak people and the entire world peace and democracy camp. The entire commanders and fighters of the Southwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army vow to unite closely with the people of the whole country under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, heighten our vigilance a hundredfold, strengthen the power of the people's democratic camp headed by the Soviet Union, and fight to the end to defend the cause of world peace.

				The Southwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
					March 15, 1953

### 西南军区的唁电

1953-03-18
第2版()
专栏：

      西南军区的唁电捷克斯洛伐克共和国国防部部长契皮茨卡同志：
    惊闻捷克斯洛伐克人民伟大的领袖、世界和平民主事业优秀的斗士哥特瓦尔德同志病逝的噩耗，我们感到分外悲痛，谨致以最深切的哀悼。
    哥特瓦尔德同志的逝世对捷克斯洛伐克人民和整个世界和平民主阵营都是一个巨大的损失。中国人民解放军西南军区全体指挥员、战斗员誓与全国人民一起，在中国共产党和毛泽东主席领导下，加紧与捷克斯洛伐克人民亲密团结，百倍提高警惕，为增强以苏联为首的人民民主阵营的力量，为保卫世界和平的事业而奋斗到底。
                        中国人民解放军西南军区
                              一九五三年三月十五日
