    Mourning the Death of the Great Comrade Stalin
    Telegram of Condolence from the Supreme People's Procuratorate of the Central People's Government to the Council of Ministers of the USSR:
    Having learned of the passing of Chairman Stalin, we are deeply shocked and saddened. We express our sincere condolences with profound sorrow. The death of Chairman Stalin is a great loss to the people of the world. The Chinese people are determined to redouble their efforts, unite with the Soviet people, carry out his遗志 (legacy), and fight to the end to defend world peace.
                Supreme People's Procuratorate of the Central People's Government of China
                                Prosecutor-General Luo Ronghuan
                                Deputy Prosecutor-General Li Liuru
                                        Lan Gongwu
                              March 6, 1953
    Telegram of Condolence from the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade to Chairman Nesterov of the Soviet Chamber of Commerce:
    Upon hearing the shocking news of the passing of Comrade J.V. Stalin on the evening of March 5th, we express our boundless sorrow and mourn the eternal departure of the great leader and teacher of the working people of the world and the most beloved friend of the Chinese people. We vow to move forward with firm confidence under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, striving diligently in our respective posts, following the direction indicated by Comrade Stalin.
                                China Council for the Promotion of International Trade
                                Chairman Nan Hanchen
                                Secretary-General Ji Chaoding
                                March 7, 1953
    Telegram of Condolence from the Northwest Federation of Trade Unions to the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions:
    Having learned with shock of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Stalin, the most respected teacher and leader of the working people of the world, we express our deepest condolences. We, the workers of the entire Northwest and the people of all China, under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong and the Chinese Communist Party, will continue to unite with you—the great Soviet people—forever, and fight to the end to defend world peace and security!
                                     Northwest Federation of Trade Unions of China
                                     March 7, 1953
    Telegram of Condolence from the East China Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions:
    Upon hearing the shocking and unfortunate news of the passing of Comrade J.V. Stalin, the great leader and teacher of the working people of the world, we are filled with boundless grief. This is an irreparable loss for the working people of the world. We extend our heartfelt condolences to you and, through you, to the entire Soviet people. The four million workers of East China are determined to turn grief into strength and, together with the people of the whole country, with greater effort, under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong and the Chinese Communist Party, to unite more closely with the Soviet people, never forgetting to follow all the teachings of Comrade Stalin, and to fight to the end to strengthen the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people and to defend world peace.
                                East China Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions
                                   March 8, 1953

We have decided to turn grief into strength and, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, unite closely, actively learn from the advanced experience and advanced working methods of the Soviet Union, increase production and quality, overfulfill the state plan, and complete the economic construction of our great motherland. We will certainly, with greater determination, unite with the Soviet people, heighten our vigilance, and fight to defend world peace and security.

    Telegram of Condolence from the North China Military Region to the Stalin Funeral Committee:
    Having learned with shock of the passing of Marshal J.V. Stalin, the great leader and teacher of the working people of the world and the most beloved friend of the Chinese people, we, on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the North China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, express our infinitely deep condolences with utmost sorrow.
    All our comrades in the army are determined to turn our profound grief into the strength of unity and combat. We stand firmly on the national defense line, redoubling our vigilance, and constantly watching all the enemy's conspiracies. We place our highest trust in the Soviet Communist Party, which was personally cultivated by Lenin and Stalin and tempered into steel.                      March 7, 1953
    Telegram of Condolence from the Northeast Military Region to the Moscow Stalin Funeral Committee:
    We mourn with utmost sorrow the passing of Marshal Stalin, the leader of the working people of the world and our dearest friend and teacher.
    The name of Marshal Stalin is especially dear to the troops of the Northeast Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The heroic Soviet army, under the leadership of Marshal Stalin, after crushing the military power of fascist Germany, turned its guns eastward and annihilated the elite of the Japanese imperialist army—the Kwantung Army, directly helping us liberate the Northeast and contributing to the victory of the Chinese People's War of Resistance Against Japan, giving tremendous assistance to the people of the Northeast and the Chinese revolution.
    In these sorrowful days, all commanders and fighters of the Northeast Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army vow, under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De, to diligently study Stalin's advanced military science, strengthen our national defense construction, and vigilantly defend the security of our motherland and the peace of the East and the world.
    All commanders and fighters of the Northeast Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army

### 哀悼伟大的斯大林同志的逝世

1953-03-10
第2版()
专栏：

    哀悼伟大的斯大林同志的逝世
    中央人民政府最高人民检察署的唁电苏联部长会议：
    顷悉斯大林主席逝世，至为震悼，谨以悲恸的心情致诚挚的吊唁。斯大林主席的逝世，是全世界人民的重大损失，中国人民决心百倍努力，和苏联人民团结一致，执行他的遗志，为保卫世界和平奋斗到底。
                中国中央人民政府最高人民检察署
                                检察长罗荣桓
                                副检察长李六如
                                        蓝公武
                              一九五三年三月六日
    中国国际贸易促进委员会的唁电苏联商会涅斯捷罗夫主席：
    惊闻约·维·斯大林同志于三月五日晚逝世，谨以无比的悲哀，悼念全世界劳动人民伟大的领袖和导师、中国人民最亲爱的朋友的永别。我们誓以坚强的信心，在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导之下，在我们的岗位上，遵循着斯大林同志指示的方向努力迈进。
                                中国国际贸易促进委员会
                                主席南汉宸
                                秘书长冀朝鼎
                                一九五三年三月七日
    西北总工会的唁电全苏工会中央理事会：
    惊闻全世界劳动人民最敬爱的导师和领袖——斯大林同志不幸逝世，谨致万分沉痛的哀悼。我们全西北的工人和全中国的人民，在毛泽东主席、中国共产党的领导下，将继续和你们——伟大的苏联人民永远地团结在一起，为保卫世界和平与安全而奋斗到底！
                                     中国西北总工会
                                     一九五三年三月七日

                                中华全国总工会华东办事处
