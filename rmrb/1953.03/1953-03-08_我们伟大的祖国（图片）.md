Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-03-08
Page: 1

Our people deeply mourn the passing of the great Comrade Stalin. The broad masses in factories, mines, enterprises, troops, government offices, schools, and villages across the country have unanimously expressed their determination to strive to realize all of Comrade Stalin's behests. This is a picture of the Changxindian Railway workers taking an oath at the workshop. They shouted, "Turn grief into strength, and step up production!"

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国人民沉痛地哀悼伟大的斯大林同志的逝世。各地工厂、矿山、企业、部队、机关、学校和农村的广大群众，一致表示决心为实现斯大林同志的全部遗训而奋斗。这是在工场上宣誓的长辛店铁路工人。他们高呼：“化悲痛为力量，加紧生产！”
