Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-03-06
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    This is one of our country's female drilling workers. They are active in undulating mountain areas, and together with the male workers, they have jointly completed the drilling tasks for the Foziling Reservoir and the Meishan Reservoir. They are currently studying methods to improve the efficiency of the drilling rig.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    这是我国女钻探工人。她们活跃在山峦起伏的地区，她们和男工在一起，共同完成了佛子岭水库和梅山水库的钻探任务，她们正在研究发挥钻探机效能的方法。
