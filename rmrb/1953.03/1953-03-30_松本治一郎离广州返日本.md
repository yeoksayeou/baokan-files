Title: Matsumoto Jiichiro Leaves Guangzhou to Return to Japan
Author:
Date: 1953-03-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 29th. The well-known Japanese peace advocate, Matsumoto Jiichiro, and his secretary, Asakawa Kamaji, arrived in Guangzhou on the evening of the 28th and left Guangzhou on the morning of the 29th, heading to Hong Kong to return to their country. Zhang Lucun, Vice-Chairman of the Guangdong Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Zhong Ming, Vice-Chairman of the Guangzhou Branch, and others went to the station to welcome and see them off.

### 松本治一郎离广州返日本

1953-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    松本治一郎离广州返日本
    【新华社广州二十九日电】日本著名和平人士松本治一郎和他的秘书浅川镰次，在二十八日晚到达广州，二十九日晨离广州取道香港返国。前往车站欢迎、欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广东省分会副主席张醁村，广州市分会副主席钟明等多人。
