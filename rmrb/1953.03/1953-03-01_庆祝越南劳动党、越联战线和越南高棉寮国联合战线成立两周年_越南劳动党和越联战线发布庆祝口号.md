Title: Celebrating the Second Anniversary of the Founding of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the Viet Lien Front, and the Vietnam-Cambodia-Laos United Front, the Vietnam Workers' Party and the Viet Lien Front Issue Celebration Slogans
Author:
Date: 1953-03-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 28th: Vietnam News Agency reports: The Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front have issued the following slogans to celebrate the second anniversary of the founding of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the Vietnam National United Front, and the Joint National United Front of Vietnam, Cambodia, and Laos:

　　1. Completely trust the leadership of President Ho Chi Minh and the Labor Party!
　　2. Fully mobilize the masses to thoroughly implement the policy of rent reduction and interest reduction!
　　3. The people of the whole country unite and actively participate in the emulation movement of killing the enemy to make meritorious deeds and increasing production and practicing economy!
　　4. Long live the Vietnam Workers' Party!
　　5. Long live the Vietnam National United Front!
　　6. Long live the Lao National United Front!
　　7. Long live the Cambodian National United Front!
　　8. Long live the Joint National United Front of Vietnam, Cambodia, and Laos!
　　9. The people of Vietnam, Cambodia, and Laos unite closely to eliminate the French and American imperialists and their puppets!
　　10. Long live the world peace and democracy camp!
　　11. Support the resolutions of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference and the World People's Peace Congress!
　　12. Long live President Ho Chi Minh!
　　13. Long live President Souphanouvong!
　　14. Long live President Sơn Ngọc Minh!
　　15. Long live Chairman Mao Zedong!
　　16. Long live Chairman Stalin!

### 庆祝越南劳动党、越联战线和越南高棉寮国联合战线成立两周年  越南劳动党和越联战线发布庆祝口号

1953-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝越南劳动党、越联战线和越南高棉寮国联合战线成立两周年
    越南劳动党和越联战线发布庆祝口号
    【新华社二十八日讯】越南通讯社讯：越南劳动党中央委员会和越南国民联合战线全国委员会发布庆祝越南劳动党、越南国民联合战线和越南、高棉、寮国三国联合民族统一战线成立两周年口号如下：
    一、完全信任胡志明主席和劳动党的领导！
    二、全力发动群众，彻底执行减租减息政策！
    三、全国人民团结起来，积极参加杀敌立功和增产节约竞赛运动！
    四、越南劳动党万岁！
    五、越南国民联合战线万岁！
    六、寮国民族统一战线万岁！
    七、高棉民族统一战线万岁！
    八、越南、高棉、寮国三国联合民族统一战线万岁！
    九、越南、高棉、寮国三国人民紧密团结起来，消灭法、美帝国主义者和他们的傀儡！
    十、世界和平民主阵营万岁！
    十一、拥护亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议和世界人民和平大会的决议！
    十二、胡志明主席万岁！
    十三、苏发努冯主席万岁！
    十四、山玉明主席万岁！
    十五、毛泽东主席万岁！
    十六、斯大林主席万岁！
