Title: Representatives from Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City Pay Respects to the Seventy-Two Martyrs of Huanghuagang
Author:
Date: 1953-03-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 29th: More than one hundred representatives from all walks of life in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City went to the Huanghuagang Seventy-Two Martyrs Cemetery in the suburbs of Guangzhou on March 29th—the anniversary of the Huanghuagang Seventy-Two Martyrs—to pay their respects.

### 广东省和广州市各界代表  公祭黄花岗七十二烈士

1953-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    广东省和广州市各界代表
    公祭黄花岗七十二烈士
   【新华社广州二十九日电】广东省和广州市各界人民代表一百多人，在三月二十九日——黄花岗七十二烈士纪念日，前往广州市郊黄花岗七十二烈士陵墓公祭。
