    Years of good harvests in our country have eliminated the imbalance between grain supply and demand left over from the old society. This is a huge granary in a certain area. It stores a large amount of grain, which can fully meet the needs of the citizens.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-03-24

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
