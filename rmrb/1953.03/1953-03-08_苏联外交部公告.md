Title: Announcement from the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs
Author:
Date: 1953-03-08
Page: 4

    Announcement from the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th] TASS Moscow, 7th: Announcement from the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs:
    The Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs deeply grieves the most painful loss of the Soviet people - the passing of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the wise leader and teacher of the Communist Party and the Soviet people, and the great standard-bearer of peace, Marshal J.V. Stalin. The immortal name of the great Stalin will forever inspire the Soviet foreign affairs staff to strive for the further enhancement of the international prestige of the peace-loving Soviet state, to consolidate and develop the fraternal friendship with the people's democratic countries, to develop international cooperation and relations with all countries, to oppose new wars, and to fight for world peace.

### 苏联外交部公告

1953-03-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联外交部公告
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科七日电：苏联外交部的公告：
    苏联外交部对苏联人民最沉痛的损失——苏联部长会议主席、苏联共产党中央委员会书记、共产党和苏联人民英明的领袖和导师、伟大的和平旗手约·维·斯大林大元帅的逝世深感悲痛。伟大的斯大林永垂不朽的名字将永远鼓舞着苏联外交工作人员为进一步提高爱好和平的苏维埃国家的国际威信，巩固和发展对人民民主国家的兄弟友谊，发展对一切国家的国际合作和事务关系，反对新战争，争取世界和平而斗争。
