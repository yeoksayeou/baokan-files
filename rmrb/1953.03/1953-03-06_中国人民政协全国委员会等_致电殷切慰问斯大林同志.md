Title: Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference National Committee and Others Send Telegrams of Deepest Sympathy to Comrade Stalin

Author:
Date: 1953-03-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Soong Ching Ling, Guo Moruo, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, and the Liaison Bureau for Asia and Australia of the World Federation of Trade Unions, have sent telegrams of deepest sympathy to Comrade Stalin, and sincerely wish him a speedy recovery. The texts of the telegrams of condolences are as follows:

The telegram of condolence from the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference to Comrade Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics:

    We, the entire Chinese people, with utmost concern, extend our most respectful and sincere condolences to you, and sincerely wish your condition improves and you recover your health.

    Under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, the entire Chinese people will resolutely unite more closely with the Soviet people and fight to the end for the great cause of defending world peace and security.

    The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
    March 5, 1953

Soong Ching Ling's telegram of condolence to Respected Comrade J.V. Stalin:

    Upon hearing the unfortunate news of your illness, I, with the deepest concern, extend my sincere condolences to you, and wish you a speedy recovery and good health, so that the Chinese people and all peace-loving people in the world may soon obtain lasting world peace.

    Soong Ching Ling
    Shanghai, March 5, 1953

Guo Moruo's telegram of condolence to Respected Comrade J.V. Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics:

    Upon learning the unfortunate news of your serious illness, I, on behalf of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, with utmost concern, extend my sincere condolences to you, and sincerely wish your condition improves and you recover your health.

    Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
    March 5, 1953

The telegram of condolence from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to Comrade Denisov, Chairman of the Moscow Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries:

    Upon learning that Comrade Stalin, the great leader of the working people of the world, the dearest friend and teacher of the Chinese people, is seriously ill, we, on behalf of all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, extend our deepest concern and condolences to Comrade Stalin and the Soviet people, and wish Comrade Stalin a speedy recovery and good health. In these difficult days of Comrade Stalin's illness, all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will, under the leadership of Comrade Mao Zedong and the Chinese Communist Party, redouble our vigilance against the enemies of peace and strive for the greater consolidation of the great unity between the Chinese and Soviet peoples.

    Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
    Beijing, March 5, 1953

The telegram of condolence from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace to the Soviet Peace Committee:

    Upon learning the unfortunate news that Chairman Stalin, the teacher and leader of the world peace cause, is seriously ill, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, with the utmost concern, sincerely wishes Chairman Stalin a speedy recovery and good health.

    We are deeply convinced that during Chairman Stalin's illness, the Chinese people will, with even more resolute spirit, unite with the Soviet people and all peace-loving people in the world to make unremitting efforts for the noble cause of opposing war and defending peace.

    The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
    Beijing, March 5, 1953

The telegram of condolence from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions:

    Upon learning that Comrade J.V. Stalin, the leader of the working people of the world, is seriously ill, all Chinese workers are extremely concerned and extend our heartfelt condolences, hoping that Comrade Stalin's condition will improve and he will recover his health soon.

    All-China Federation of Trade Unions
    March 5, 1953

The telegram of condolence from the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League to the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League:

    Upon learning the news that our most respected teacher, Comrade Stalin, has suddenly fallen seriously ill, the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League and all the young people of China are extremely concerned. We extend our most cordial condolences to Comrade Stalin and sincerely wish him a speedy recovery and good health.

    During Comrade Stalin's illness, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, we must work harder to do our work well and unite even more closely with the Soviet youth, advancing along the path pointed out to us by the great Comrade Stalin.

    Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League
    March 5, 1953

The telegram of condolence from the Liaison Bureau for Asia and Australia of the World Federation of Trade Unions to the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions:

    Upon learning the news that Comrade Stalin, the great teacher of the working people of the world, is seriously ill, we and millions of workers in Asia and Australia are extremely concerned about the serious condition of Comrade Stalin's health and extend our heartfelt condolences, hoping that his condition will quickly improve and he will recover his health soon.

    Liaison Bureau for Asia and Australia of the World Federation of Trade Unions
    March 5

### 中国人民政协全国委员会等  致电殷切慰问斯大林同志

1953-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民政协全国委员会等
    致电殷切慰问斯大林同志
    据新华社讯：中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会、宋庆龄、郭沫若、中苏友好协会总会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中华全国总工会、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会和世界工会联合会亚澳联络局，分别致电殷切慰问斯大林同志，并衷心祝盼他早日恢复健康。各慰问电电文如下：
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会的慰问电苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议斯大林主席：
    我们全中国人民谨以万分关切的心情，向您致最敬爱最诚挚的慰问，并衷心地祝盼您的病情好转，恢复健康。
    全中国人民在中国共产党和毛泽东主席领导之下必定坚决地与苏联人民更紧密地团结起来，为保卫世界和平与安全的伟大事业奋斗到底。
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会
    一九五三年三月五日
    宋庆龄的慰问电敬爱的约·维·斯大林同志：
    听到您患病的不幸消息，我怀着最关切的心情向您谨致诚挚的慰问，并祝盼您病情好转，恢复健康，以使中国人民和全世界爱好和平的人民早日获得持久的世界和平。
    宋庆龄
    一九五三年三月五日于上海
    郭沫若的慰问电苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席敬爱的约·维·斯大林同志：
    获悉您患重病的不幸消息，我代表中国人民保卫世界和平委员会，以万分关切的心情，向您致诚挚的慰问，并衷心地祝盼您的病情好转，恢复健康。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席　郭沫若
    一九五三年三月五日
    中苏友好协会总会的慰问电莫斯科苏联对外文化协会主席杰尼索夫同志：
    获悉全世界劳动人民的伟大领袖、中国人民最亲爱的朋友和导师斯大林同志身患重病，谨代表中苏友好协会全体会员向斯大林同志及苏联人民致以深切的关怀和慰问，并祝盼斯大林同志病情好转，恢复健康。在斯大林同志重病的困难日子里，中苏友好协会全体会员一定将在毛泽东同志和中国共产党领导下百倍地提高对和平敌人的警惕，为中苏两国人民的伟大团结的更加巩固而奋斗。
    中苏友好协会总会总干事　钱俊瑞
    一九五三年三月五日北京
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的慰问电苏联拥护和平委员会：
    获悉世界和平事业的导师和领袖斯大林主席得了重病的不幸消息，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会以最为关切的心情，衷诚祝望斯大林主席病况好转，恢复健康。
    我们深信：在斯大林主席病重期间，中国人民一定以更其坚决的精神，与苏联人民和全世界爱好和平的人民，团结一致，为反对战争保卫和平的崇高事业作不懈的奋斗。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
    一九五三年三月五日北京
    中华全国总工会的慰问电全苏工会中央理事会：
    获悉全世界劳动人民的领袖约·维·斯大林同志患了重病，全中国工人万分关切，谨致以衷心的慰问，盼望斯大林同志的病情好转，早日恢复健康。
    中华全国总工会
    一九五三年三月五日
    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会的慰问电苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会：
    得知我们最敬爱的导师斯大林同志突患重病的消息，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会和全中国青年都万分系念。我们谨向斯大林同志致以最亲切的慰问，衷心祝望斯大林同志病情早日好转，恢复健康。
    在斯大林同志患病期间，我们在中国共产党领导下，定要更加努力作好我们的工作，并更紧密地和苏联青年团结一致，向着伟大的斯大林同志指示我们的道路前进。
    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会
    一九五三年三月五日
    世界工联亚澳联络局的慰问电全苏工会中央理事会：
    得悉全世界劳动人民的伟大导师斯大林同志患重病的消息，我们和亚洲澳洲千百万工人都万分关怀着斯大林同志健康的严重情况，谨致以衷心的慰问，盼望他的病情迅速好转，早日恢复健康。
    世界工联亚澳联络局
    三月五日
