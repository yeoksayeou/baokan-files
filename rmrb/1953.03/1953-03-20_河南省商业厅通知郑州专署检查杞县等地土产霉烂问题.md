Title: Henan Provincial Department of Commerce Notifies Zhengzhou Prefecture to Investigate the Issue of Moldy Native Products in Qixian and Other Places
Author: 
Date: 1953-03-20
Page: 2

Response to People's Daily Readers' Criticisms and Suggestions

    Henan Provincial Department of Commerce Notifies Zhengzhou Prefecture to Investigate the Issue of Moldy Native Products in Qixian and Other Places
    Letter from the Henan Provincial People's Government Department of Commerce: On February 12th, the People's Daily published reader Yan Lintai's exposure of the large-scale mold and rot of native products in our province's Qixian, Lanfeng, Tongxu, Kaifeng, Zhongmu, and Xinzheng six counties, when cooperatives accepted the entrustment of local native product companies to purchase native products. Our department has notified the Zhengzhou Prefecture Industry and Commerce Section to be responsible for jointly inspecting and handling the matter together with the cooperative departments. We will inform you of the situation after it is clarified.

### 河南省商业厅通知郑州专署检查杞县等地土产霉烂问题

1953-03-20
第2版()
专栏：对人民日报读者批评建议的反应

    河南省商业厅通知郑州专署检查杞县等地土产霉烂问题
    河南省人民政府商业厅来信：二月十二日人民日报上登载了读者阎林泰揭发我省杞县、兰封、通许、开封、中牟、新郑六县合作社接受当地土产公司委托收购土产时发生大批土产霉烂情况。我厅已通知郑州专署工商科负责会同合作社部门共同检查处理，情况弄清后当可告诉你们。
