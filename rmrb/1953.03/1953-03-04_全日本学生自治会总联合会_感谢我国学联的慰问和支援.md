Title: All-Japan Student Self-Government Association Thanks the All-China Students' Federation for its Condolences and Support
Author:
Date: 1953-03-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd: The All-Japan Student Self-Government Association sent a telegram to the All-China Students' Federation on February 24th, thanking the All-China Students' Federation for writing letters and remitting funds to express condolences and support for Japanese students. The telegram is as follows:

All-China Students' Federation:

Thank you for your enthusiastic relief. In order to respond to your encouragement, we will certainly strengthen our struggle for peace.

All-Japan Student Self-Government Association

### 全日本学生自治会总联合会  感谢我国学联的慰问和支援

1953-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

    全日本学生自治会总联合会
    感谢我国学联的慰问和支援
    【新华社二日讯】全日本学生自治会总联合会在二月二十四日打电报给中华全国学生联合会，感谢中华全国学生联合会写信并汇款慰问和支援日本学生。电文如下：中华全国学生联合会：
    感谢热诚的救济。为了回答你们的鼓励，我们一定加强我们争取和平的斗争。
    全日本学生自治会总联合会
