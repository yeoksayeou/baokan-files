Title: Preparatory Committee for Memorial Meeting for Comrade Stalin Held by People of All Circles in the Capital Begins Work
Author:
Date: 1953-03-08
Page: 2

    [This Newspaper Dispatch] The Preparatory Committee for the Memorial Meeting for Comrade Stalin held by people from all circles in the capital has begun work at the old site of the Xingjian Association in Zhongshan Park. Wreaths sent by people from all circles should be delivered to the Cultural Palace of the Working People and the entrance of Zhongshan Park before 6:00 PM today. Funeral couplets and elegiac banners will not be accepted. The telephone numbers for the Preparatory Committee are (3)5580, (3)1989, and (3)1896.

### 首都各界人民追悼斯大林同志大会筹备会开始办公

1953-03-08
第2版()
专栏：

    首都各界人民追悼斯大林同志大会筹备会开始办公
    【本报讯】首都各界人民追悼斯大林同志大会筹备会在中山公园行健会旧址开始办公。各界人民所送花圈，一律在今日下午六时前送至劳动人民文化宫和中山公园门前，挽联挽幛不收。筹备处的电话是（三）五五八○、（三）一九八九、（三）一八九六。
