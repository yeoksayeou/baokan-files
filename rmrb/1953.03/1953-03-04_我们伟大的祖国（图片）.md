    Our Great Motherland
    The "March 8th" Surveying Team is the first women's surveying team in our country. It is composed of thirty-one young women of Han and Korean ethnicity. Now, they are already capable of carrying out complex surveying work. This shows the team members carrying instruments. They are heading towards the construction site to conduct surveying.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    “三八”测量分队是我国第一支女子测量队。它是由汉族和朝鲜族的青年妇女三十一人组成的。现在，她们已经能够进行复杂的测量工作。这是背着仪器的队员们。她们正走向工地去进行测量。
