Title: Condolence Telegram from the Central South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army
Author:
Date: 1953-03-19
Page: 2

To the Funeral Committee of Comrade Gottwald:

    We are deeply saddened to hear of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Klement Gottwald, President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Chairman of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, outstanding activist in the international workers' movement, and close friend of the Chinese people. All commanders and fighters of the Central South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, with the same sorrow as the people of Czechoslovakia, express our deepest condolences. Our entire army is resolved to join the Chinese people, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, to further enhance the friendship between the peoples of China and Czechoslovakia, strengthen the unity of the world peace, democracy, and socialism camp led by the Soviet Union, and strive to defend peace in the Far East and the world.
                        Lin Biao, Ye Jianying, Luo Ronghuan,
                        Deng Zihui, Tan Zheng, Huang Yongsheng,
     Tao Zhu, leading all commanders and fighters of the Central South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army

### 中南军区、第四野战军的唁电

1953-03-19
第2版()
专栏：

    中南军区、第四野战军的唁电哥特瓦尔德同志治丧委员会：
    惊闻捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统、捷克斯洛伐克共产党主席、国际工人运动的卓越活动家、中国人民的亲密朋友克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志不幸逝世，中国人民解放军中南军区、第四野战军全体指挥员、战斗员，以与捷克斯洛伐克人民同样沉痛的心情，谨致悼念。我们全军决与中国人民一起，在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下，为更加增强中捷两国人民的友谊，增强以苏联为首的世界和平、民主和社会主义阵营的团结，为保卫远东与世界和平而奋斗。
                        林彪、叶剑英、罗荣桓、
                        邓子恢、谭政、黄永胜、
     陶铸率领中国人民解放军中南军区、第四野战军全体指挥员、战斗员
