Title: Czechoslovakian Embassy in Our Country Issues Announcement Acknowledging the Chinese People's Condolences for Gottwald's Death
Author:
Date: 1953-03-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 20th: The Czechoslovakian Embassy in our country has issued an announcement expressing gratitude for the Chinese people's mourning of the death of Comrade Klement Gottwald, the great leader of the Czechoslovakian people. The announcement reads as follows:

　　When the Czechoslovakian people suffered the most grievous loss—the death of the President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Klement Gottwald—we received sincere condolences and mourning from Chinese people's organizations and people from all walks of life, which demonstrated the profound friendship of the Chinese people towards the Czechoslovakian people. The Embassy of the Czechoslovak Republic in the People's Republic of China hereby expresses its most heartfelt gratitude.

### 捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使馆发表公告  答谢中国人民对哥特瓦尔德逝世的悼念

1953-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使馆发表公告
    答谢中国人民对哥特瓦尔德逝世的悼念
    【新华社二十日讯】捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻我国大使馆发表公告，对中国人民悼念捷克斯洛伐克人民的伟大领袖哥特瓦尔德同志的逝世，表示谢意。公告文如下：
    当捷克斯洛伐克人民遭受最沉痛的损失——捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统克利门特·哥特瓦尔德逝世之时，承蒙中国人民团体和各界人士诚挚的慰问和悼念，这表明了中国人民对捷克斯洛伐克人民的深厚友谊，捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻中华人民共和国大使馆特此表示最衷心的谢意。
