Title: Condolences from the Anshan Municipal People's Government

Author:
Date: 1953-03-19
Page: 3

To the Presidium of the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic:

    We are deeply saddened to learn of the passing of Comrade Gottwald, the great leader of the Czechoslovak Republic and our most beloved friend of the Chinese people. Please accept our deepest condolences.

    The people of Anshan City are determined to transform our grief into strength. Under the leadership of the great Chairman Mao, we will actively build Anshan Iron and Steel, the heavy industry base of our motherland, and steadily realize the industrialization of our country. Let us unite closely in the strong peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union, crush the aggressive conspiracies of the imperialists, and fight to the end to defend lasting world peace.

    Comrade Gottwald will live forever!
                              The People's Government of Anshan City, China

### 鞍山市人民政府的唁电

1953-03-19
第3版()
专栏：

    鞍山市人民政府的唁电捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府主席团：
    惊悉捷克斯洛伐克共和国的伟大领袖、我们中国人民最敬爱的朋友，哥特瓦尔德同志逝世的消息，感到无限的悲痛。谨致沉痛的哀悼。
    我们鞍山市全体人民决心把悲痛化为力量，在伟大的毛主席领导下，积极建设我们祖国重工业基地——鞍钢，稳步实现我们国家工业化。让我们共同团结在坚强的以苏联为首的和平民主阵营，粉碎帝国主义者的侵略阴谋，为保卫世界的持久和平而奋斗到底。
    哥特瓦尔德同志永垂不朽！
                              中国鞍山市人民政府
