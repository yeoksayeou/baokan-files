Title: Condolence Telegram from the Suiyuan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1953-03-11
Page: 2

    Condolence Telegram from the Suiyuan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions to the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions:
    Upon hearing the news of the passing of Comrade Stalin, the most beloved leader of the Chinese people and great revolutionary teacher, the working class of Suiyuan Province, China, felt immense grief. We hereby send a telegram of condolence. We, the entire working class of Suiyuan Province, are determined to transform grief into strength, forever follow the wise teachings of Comrade Stalin, and under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, unite closely with the Soviet people and strive to defend world peace!
                                    The Suiyuan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, China
                                       March 8, 1953

### 绥远省总工会的唁电

1953-03-11
第2版()
专栏：

    绥远省总工会的唁电全苏工会中央理事会：
    中国人民最敬爱的领袖和伟大的革命导师斯大林同志逝世消息传来后，中国绥远省工人阶级感到万分悲痛，特电吊唁。我们绥远省全体工人决心化悲痛为力量，永远遵循斯大林同志的英明教导，在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下，与苏联人民紧密地团结一起，为保卫世界和平而奋斗！
                                    中国绥远省总工会
                                       一九五三年三月八日
