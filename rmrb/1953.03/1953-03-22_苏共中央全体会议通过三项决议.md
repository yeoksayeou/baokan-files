Title: CPSU Central Committee Plenum Passes Three Resolutions
Author:
Date: 1953-03-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 21st: TASS, Moscow, March 21st dispatch: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union held a plenary session on March 14, 1953, and passed the following resolutions:

    I. To approve the request of G. M. Malenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, to be relieved of his duties as Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union;

    II. To elect the following persons to form the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union: N. S. Khrushchev, M. A. Suslov, P. N. Pospelov, N. N. Shatalin, S. D. Ignatiev;

    III. In accordance with Article 32 of the Charter of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, to promote N. N. Shatalin, alternate member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, to member of the Central Committee.

### 苏共中央全体会议通过三项决议

1953-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    苏共中央全体会议通过三项决议
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十一日电：苏联共产党中央委员会一九五三年三月十四日举行全体会议，通过下列决议：
    一、批准苏联部长会议主席格·马·马林科夫的请求，解除他的苏联共产党中央委员会书记的职务；
    二、选举下列人员组成苏联共产党中央委员会书记处：尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫，米·安·苏斯洛夫，彼·恩·波斯伯洛夫，纳·纳·夏塔林，斯·德·伊格纳捷夫；
    三、根据苏联共产党党章第三十二条，将苏联共产党中央委员会候补委员纳·纳·夏塔林递补为中央委员会委员。
