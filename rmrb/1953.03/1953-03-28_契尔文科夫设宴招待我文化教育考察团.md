Title: Chervenkov Hosts Banquet for Chinese Cultural and Educational Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1953-03-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, March 27th: Bulgarian Council of Ministers Chairman Chervenkov hosted a banquet on the 24th to entertain the People's Republic of China Cultural and Educational Delegation headed by Wei Que and Peng Kang. More than 30 Bulgarian government leaders and prominent figures from the cultural and educational circles attended the banquet. Cao Xiangren, the Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria, was also invited.

### 契尔文科夫设宴招待我文化教育考察团

1953-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    契尔文科夫设宴招待我文化教育考察团
    【新华社二十七日讯】索非亚消息：保加利亚部长会议主席契尔文科夫在二十四日设宴招待以韦悫、彭康为首的中华人民共和国文化教育考察团。参加宴会的有保加利亚政府首长和文化教育界著名人士三十多人。我驻保大使曹祥仁也应邀参加。
