Title: China-Myanmar Friendship Association Donates Funds to Aid Fire Victims in Yangon
Author:
Date: 1953-03-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 2nd: On February 25th, the third day of the opera performance celebrating the anniversary of the founding of the Burma-China Friendship Association in Rangoon, the Burma-China Friendship Association held a meeting at the theater. At the meeting, a letter from Zheng Zhenduo, President of the China-Myanmar Friendship Association, to Dr. Htin Wah, Chairman of the Burma-China Friendship Association in Rangoon, was read, and the news of the China-Myanmar Friendship Association's donation to aid fire victims in Rangoon was announced. In his letter, Zheng Zhenduo congratulated the success of the Burma-China Friendship Week and expressed his concern and sympathy for the recent fire in Rangoon. The China-Myanmar Friendship Association donated 5,000 Kyats to the Burma-China Friendship Association to provide relief to the fire victims.

Rangoon Mayor U Tun Tin then spoke at the meeting. He expressed his heartfelt gratitude for the sincere concern of the Chinese people and the China-Myanmar Friendship Association, and hoped that the Burma-China Friendship Association in Rangoon would convey the gratitude of the victims to the Chinese people. After the Mayor of Rangoon spoke, the audience sang "The China-Myanmar Friendship Song" together.

### 中缅友好协会捐款  救济仰光遭受火灾的灾民

1953-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

    中缅友好协会捐款
    救济仰光遭受火灾的灾民
    【新华社二日讯】仰光消息：在二月二十五日，仰光举行庆祝缅中友好协会成立周年纪念的歌剧公演的第三天，缅中友好协会在剧场开会，会上宣读了中缅友好协会会长郑振铎给仰光缅中友好协会主席信波博士的信，并且宣布中缅友好协会捐款救济仰光遭受火灾的难民的消息。郑振铎在信中祝贺缅中友好周的成功，并对仰光最近遭受火灾表示关切和同情。中缅友好协会捐赠缅币五千元，请缅中友好协会代为救济遭受火灾的难民。
    仰光市长吴顿阵接着在会上讲话。他表示衷心感谢中国人民和中缅友好协会的诚意关怀，并希望仰光缅中友好协会将灾民的感激心意转告中国人民。仰光市长讲话后，会众齐唱“中缅友好之歌”。
