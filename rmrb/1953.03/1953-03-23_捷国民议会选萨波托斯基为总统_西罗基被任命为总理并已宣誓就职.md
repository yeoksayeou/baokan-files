Title: Czechoslovak National Assembly Elects Zápotocký as President, Široký Appointed Premier and Sworn In
Author:
Date: 1953-03-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 22nd, TASS dispatch from Prague, March 21st: The Czechoslovak National Assembly held a meeting in Prague on March 21st to elect the President of the Republic.

    The meeting was opened and presided over by Dr. Oldřich John, Chairman of the National Assembly. Subsequently, Deputy Viliam Široký spoke at the meeting, representing the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and the Central Action Committee of the National Front, suggesting the election of Antonín Zápotocký as President of the Czechoslovak Republic. Široký pointed out: Zápotocký is a loyal fighter for the cause of the working class and a faithful comrade-in-arms of Klement Gottwald.

    The members of the National Assembly unanimously elected Zápotocký as President of the Czechoslovak Republic in accordance with the Constitution.

    When the Chairman of the National Assembly announced Zápotocký's election, the deputies and guests responded with stormy applause.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 22nd, TASS dispatch from Prague, March 22nd: Antonín Zápotocký, President of the Czechoslovak Republic, appointed Viliam Široký as Prime Minister on March 21st. Prime Minister Viliam Široký has been sworn in.

### 捷国民议会选萨波托斯基为总统  西罗基被任命为总理并已宣誓就职

1953-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    捷国民议会选萨波托斯基为总统
    西罗基被任命为总理并已宣誓就职
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社布拉格二十一日电：捷克斯洛伐克国民议会二十一日在布拉格举行会议，选举共和国总统。
    会议由国民议会主席奥德里赫·约翰博士主持开幕。接着，议员威廉·西罗基在会上发言，代表捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会和民族阵线中央行动委员会建议选举安托宁·萨波托斯基为捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统。西罗基指出：萨波托斯基是工人阶级事业的忠诚战士，是克·哥特瓦尔德的忠实战友。
    国民议会议员们根据宪法一致选举萨波托斯基为捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统。
    当国民议会主席宣布萨波托斯基当选时，议员和来宾们报以暴风雨般的掌声。
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社布拉格二十二日电：捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统安托宁·萨波托斯基二十一日任命威廉·西罗基为总理。威廉·西罗基总理已宣誓就职。
