Title: East China and Central-South China Administrative Committees Hold Meetings Respectively to Discuss and Stipulate Work Tasks for 1953
Author:
Date: 1953-03-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency dispatch:

    The East China Administrative Committee and the Central-South China Administrative Committee have both held meetings successively to discuss and stipulate work tasks for 1953.
    The East China Administrative Committee held a plenary committee meeting from the 18th to the 20th. At the meeting, Committee Member Chen Yi made a political report, conveying Chairman Mao's instructions at the Fourth Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, as well as the spirit and resolutions of the 20th Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee. Vice Chairman Ma Yinchu conveyed the resolutions of the Fourth Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and reported on the situation of the Vienna World People's Peace Congress. Vice Chairman Tan Zhenlin made a report on the work tasks for East China in 1953, proposing that the main tasks for the East China region this year are: 1. Strengthen coastal defense and support the Korean War to Resist U.S. Aggression; 2. Strengthen leadership over industrial production and basic construction; 3. Launch a mutual aid and cooperation movement and improve leadership over rural work; 4. Implement universal suffrage and convene people's congresses at the provincial (municipal), county, and township levels. The meeting also heard work reports from the heads of the Political and Legal, Financial and Economic, Cultural and Educational, and Land Reform Committees. At the meeting, Committee Members Hu Juewen, Kui Yanfang, Chen Shaokuan, Liu Minsheng, Leng Yu, and Vice Chairman Sheng Pihua, among others, spoke successively. They unanimously supported Chairman Mao's call and expressed their determination to work hard to implement the strengthening of the struggle to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea, learn from the Soviet Union, and oppose bureaucracy. They unanimously agreed with the work tasks for the East China region in 1953 proposed by Vice Chairman Tan Zhenlin and the work reports on political and legal affairs, finance and economics, culture and education, and the conclusion of land reform. Finally, Vice Chairman Zhang Dingcheng made a concluding speech. He called on the entire East China region to resolutely obey the central government's requirements for unity and concentration, strengthen discipline, and launch a struggle against bureaucracy, commandism, and violations of law and discipline to ensure the victory of the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea and the completion of the national construction plan.
    The Central-South Administrative Committee held its first committee meeting on the 21st. Vice Chairman Li Xiannian presided over the meeting. Vice Chairman Zhang Nanxian conveyed at the meeting the proceedings and the spirit of various resolutions of the Fourth Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the 22nd and 23rd Meetings of the Central People's Government Committee. Vice Chairman Chen Mingshu and Committee Member Liu Fei made supplementary speeches. During the discussion, Committee Members Li Renren, Wang Yizhai, Wang Renzhong, Huang Changshui, Peng Yihu, Liu Yifeng, Pan Zinian, and Vice Chairman Li Xuefeng spoke successively. They
    unanimously supported Chairman Mao's three major instructions at the Fourth Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the three major tasks proposed by Vice Chairman Zhou Enlai in his political report, as well as all reports and resolutions. At the end of the meeting, Vice Chairman Li Xiannian made a concluding speech. Based on the central government's instructions, he proposed that the work tasks for the Central-South region in 1953 are: First, continue to strengthen the struggle to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea, continue to strengthen ideological education on resisting U.S. aggression and aid Korea, and continue to mobilize the people of the entire region to support the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army. Second, resolutely implement the national construction plan, focusing on heavy industry, and this year the primary task is basic construction, while at the same time not neglecting existing industrial production, and developing agriculture, adjusting commerce, improving transportation, and strengthening cultural education, health, public security, and judicial work to serve national economic construction. Third, convene people's congresses at all levels, starting with summarizing past experiences in democratic governance and organizing the study of election laws, in order to successfully convene people's congresses at all levels. Fourth, launch a struggle against bureaucracy, commandism, and violations of law and discipline to ensure the smooth progress of national construction. Li Xiannian pointed out that the Central-South Administrative Committee should vigorously strengthen its supervisory role in the future, conduct in-depth inspections of work, and discover and solve problems in a timely manner.

### 华东中南两行政委员会分别举行会议  讨论和规定了一九五三年的工作任务

1953-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

    华东中南两行政委员会分别举行会议
    讨论和规定了一九五三年的工作任务
    据新华社讯：华东行政委员会和中南行政委员会都先后举行会议，讨论和规定了一九五三年的工作任务。
    华东行政委员会从十八日到二十日举行了全体委员会议。会上，陈毅委员作了政治报告，传达了毛主席在中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第四次会议上的指示，中央人民政府委员会第二十次会议的精神和决议。马寅初副主席传达了中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第四次会议的决议，并报告了维也纳世界人民和平大会的情况。谭震林副主席作了一九五三年华东工作任务的报告，提出今年华东区的主要任务是：一、加强海防，支援朝鲜抗美斗争；二、加强工业生产的领导和基本建设工作；三、开展互助合作运动，改善农村工作的领导；四、实行普选，召开省（市）、县、乡三级人民代表大会。会议还听取了政治法律、财政经济、文化教育、土地改革四个委员会负责人的工作报告。会上，胡厥文、蒉延芳、陈绍宽、刘民生、冷遹等委员及盛丕华副主席等先后发言。他们一致拥护毛主席的号召，表示要为贯彻加强抗美援朝斗争，学习苏联，反对官僚主义而努力。他们一致同意谭震林副主席提出的一九五三年华东区工作任务，及政法、财经、文教和结束土地改革等工作报告。最后，张鼎丞副主席作了总结发言。他号召全华东区坚决服从中央的统一、集中的要求，加强纪律性，开展反对官僚主义、反对命令主义、反对违法乱纪的斗争，以保证抗美援朝的胜利与国家建设计划的完成。
    中南行政委员会在二十一日举行第一次委员会议。李先念副主席主持会议。张难先副主席在会上传达了中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第四次会议和中央人民政府委员会第二十二次、第二十三次会议的经过与各项决议的精神。陈铭枢副主席、刘斐委员作了补充发言。会议在进行讨论时，李任仁、王毅斋、王任重、黄长水、彭一湖、刘一峰、潘梓年等委员及李雪峰副主席先后发言。他们
    一致拥护毛主席在中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第四次会议上的三项重大指示和周恩来副主席在政治报告中提出的三大任务以及所有报告和决议。会议最后由李先念副主席作总结发言。他根据中央指示，提出中南区一九五三年的工作任务是：第一，继续加强抗美援朝的斗争，继续加强抗美援朝的思想教育，继续动员全区人民支援中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军。第二，坚决执行国家建设计划，以重工业为重点，而今年首要工作是基本建设，同时不能忽视现有工业生产，并要发展农业，调整商业，搞好交通，加强文化教育、卫生和公安、司法等工作，使之为国家经济建设而服务。第三，召开地方各级人民代表大会，从总结过去民主建政经验，组织学习选举法入手，以便开好各级人民代表大会。第四，开展反对官僚主义、反对命令主义和反对违法乱纪的斗争，以保证国家建设事业的顺利进行。李先念指出中南行政委员会今后应大力加强监督作用，深入检查工作，及时发现问题，解决问题。
