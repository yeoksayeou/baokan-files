Title: Our Delegation Paid Respects at Stalin's Lying-in-State and Stood Guard

Author: 
Date: 1953-03-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th: The head of the People's Republic of China delegation attending Comrade Stalin's funeral, Zhou Enlai, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, and Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, led all members of the delegation to the Hall of Columns of the House of Trade Unions at 8 p.m. on the 8th to present a wreath before Comrade Stalin's coffin and pay their respects to Comrade Stalin's remains. Accompanying them were Jasnov, a member of the Stalin Funeral Committee, Kuznetsov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Fedlin, Director of the First Far East Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Ge Baoquan, Counselor of our Embassy in the Soviet Union, and Ji He, Military Attaché. After leading all the members of the delegation into the solemn Hall of Columns, Premier Zhou Enlai first presented a wreath before Comrade Stalin's coffin and then led all the members of the delegation to bow three times to Comrade Stalin's remains. The entire delegation, led by Premier Zhou Enlai, and the personnel of our Embassy in the Soviet Union, then divided into six teams, each with four people, and stood solemnly on both sides of Comrade Stalin's remains to participate in the vigil. After the vigil, Jasnov, a member of the Stalin Funeral Committee, Kuznetsov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, and Yudin, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, received Premier Zhou Enlai and all the members of the delegation. During the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Central People's Government, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, all the Chinese people, and Chairman Mao Zedong, expressed the deepest condolences for the death of Comrade Stalin, the leader of the working people of the world, the most beloved teacher and friend of the Chinese people, and the great Stalin.

### 我国代表团曾到斯大林灵前献花圈并守灵

1953-03-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我国代表团曾到斯大林灵前献花圈并守灵
    【新华社莫斯科九日电】参加斯大林同志丧礼的中华人民共和国代表团团长、中国共产党中央委员会书记、中央人民政府政务院总理、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席周恩来，八日晚八时率领全体团员，前往工会大厦圆柱大厅，在斯大林同志灵前敬献花圈，并瞻仰斯大林同志遗容。陪同前往的有斯大林治丧委员会委员雅斯诺夫、苏联外交部副部长库兹涅佐夫、苏联外交部第一远东司司长费德林和我国驻苏大使馆参赞戈宝权、武官吉合等人。周恩来团长在率领全体团员进入庄严肃穆的圆柱大厅后，首先在斯大林同志灵前敬献花圈，并率领全体团员向斯大林同志遗体行三鞠躬礼。以周恩来团长为首的代表团全体团员和我国驻苏大使馆人员，接着就分为六队，每队四人，分别肃立在斯大林同志遗体的两旁，参加守灵。守灵礼毕后，斯大林治丧委员会委员雅斯诺夫、苏联外交部副部长库兹涅佐夫和苏联共产党中央委员会委员尤金等，接待了周恩来团长及全体团员。在接待时，周恩来团长代表中国共产党中央委员会、中央人民政府、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会、全中国人民和毛泽东主席，对全世界劳动人民的领袖、中国人民最敬爱的导师和朋友、伟大的斯大林同志的逝世，致以最深切的哀悼。
