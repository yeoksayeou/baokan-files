Title: Condolence Message from the Liaoxi Provincial Federation of Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1953-03-18
Page: 3

To the Central Council of Czechoslovak Trade Unions:

The entire workforce of Liaoxi Province, China, with deep sorrow, mourns the passing of our close friend Comrade Gottwald. The death of the outstanding revolutionary fighter Comrade Gottwald is not only a misfortune and loss for the people of Czechoslovakia but also a misfortune and loss for the entire camp of peace and democracy. We are determined to transform our grief into strength and fight to the end for the common cause of defending lasting world peace.

中国辽西省总工会
一九五三年三月十五日

The Liaoxi Provincial Federation of Trade Unions of China
March 15, 1953

### 辽西省总工会的唁电

1953-03-18
第3版()
专栏：

    辽西省总工会的唁电捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会：
    中国辽西省全体职工以十分沉痛的心情，哀悼我们的亲密的朋友哥特瓦尔德同志的逝世。杰出的革命斗士哥特瓦尔德同志的逝世，不仅是捷克斯洛伐克人民的不幸与损失，而且是整个和平民主阵营的不幸与损失。我们决将悲痛化为力量，为保卫世界持久和平的共同事业奋斗到底。
                                中国辽西省总工会
                              一九五三年三月十五日
