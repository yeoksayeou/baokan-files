Title: Telegram of Condolence from the Northeast Military Region

Author: 
Date: 1953-03-18
Page: 2

Telegram of Condolence from the Northeast Military Region to Comrade Čepička, Minister of National Defense of the Czechoslovak Republic:

	With extremely heavy hearts, we mourn the passing of Comrade Gottwald, the great leader of the Czechoslovak people and a close friend of the Chinese people.

	Comrade Gottwald was an outstanding fighter in the Czechoslovak people's liberation cause and the struggle for world peace and democracy. His passing is a great loss to the Czechoslovak people and the world peace and democracy camp.

	We, the commanders and fighters of the Northeast Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, while mourning the passing of Comrade Gottwald, will, under the leadership of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, strengthen the great unity of the world peace and democracy forces headed by the Soviet Union like steel, and cooperate more closely with the Czechoslovak army to fight to the end to defend the cause of world peace and democracy.

		The commanders and fighters of the Northeast Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
							March 15, 1953

### 东北军区的唁电

1953-03-18
第2版()
专栏：

      东北军区的唁电捷克斯洛伐克共和国国防部部长契皮茨卡同志：
    我们以极沉痛的心情，哀悼捷克斯洛伐克共和国人民伟大的领袖、中国人民亲密的朋友哥特瓦尔德同志的逝世。
    哥特瓦尔德同志在捷克斯洛伐克人民解放事业与世界和平民主事业的斗争中是杰出的战士，他的逝世，对于捷克斯洛伐克人民与世界和平民主阵营是
    一个极大的损失。
    我们中国人民解放军东北军区全体指挥员、战斗员在悼念哥特瓦尔德同志逝世的时候，将在毛主席与朱总司令的领导下钢铁般地加强以苏联为首的世界和平民主力量的大团结，并与捷克斯洛伐克军队更加亲密合作，为保卫世界和平民主事业奋斗到底。
        中国人民解放军东北军区全体指挥员、
              战斗员
                                一九五三年三月十五日
