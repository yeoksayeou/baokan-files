Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-03-14
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In order to meet the needs of large-scale basic construction, various construction departments are training a large number of female technicians. This is a scene of Yu Bangrui, a model worker and engineer of the Design Company under the Ministry of Heavy Industry of the Central People's Government, guiding female technicians in their design work.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了适应大规模基本建设的需要，各个建设部门正在培养大批的女技术人员。这是中央人民政府重工业部所属的设计公司的模范工作者俞邦瑞工程师正在指导女技术员设计的情形。
