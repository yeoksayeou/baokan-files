Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Sends a Telegram to Comrade Stalin to Express Concern for His Illness
Author: Mao Zedong
Date: 1953-03-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 4th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to Comrade J.V. Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, to express concern for his illness. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To Comrade J.V. Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union:

Upon learning the unfortunate news of your serious illness, the Chinese people, the Chinese government, and I myself, with the utmost concern, extend our sincere condolences to you and wholeheartedly hope that your condition will improve and you will recover your health, to the consolation of the wishes of the Chinese people and all peace-loving people of the world.

Mao Zedong
March 4, 1953, in Beijing

### 毛泽东主席致电斯大林同志慰问病情
毛泽东
1953-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席致电斯大林同志慰问病情
    【新华社四日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东致电苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席斯大林慰问病情。电文如下：苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席亲爱的约·维·斯大林同志：
    获悉您患重病的不幸消息，中国人民、中国政府和我本人怀着最关切的心情，向您谨致诚挚的慰问，并衷心地希望您的病情好转，恢复健康，以慰中国和全世界爱好和平人民的祝望。
    毛泽东
    一九五三年三月四日于北京
