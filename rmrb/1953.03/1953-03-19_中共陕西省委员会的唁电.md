Title: Condolence Message from the Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1953-03-19
Page: 3

The Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party's Condolence Message to the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party:

With extreme grief, we have learned of the passing of Comrade Klement Gottwald, the great leader of the Czechoslovak people and a close friend of the Chinese people. We express our deepest condolences. We are determined to unite even more closely with the Czechoslovak people in the world peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union, under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong and the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and fight to the end to defend world peace.

                            The Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party

### 中共陕西省委员会的唁电

1953-03-19
第3版()
专栏：

    中共陕西省委员会的唁电捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会：
    惊悉捷克斯洛伐克人民的伟大领袖和中国人民的亲密朋友克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志逝世，我们怀着极悲痛的心情，致以深切的哀悼。我们坚决在毛泽东主席和中国共产党中央委员会的领导下，更加紧密地与捷克斯洛伐克人民团结在以苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营中，为保卫世界和平而奋斗到底。
                            中国共产党陕西省委员会
