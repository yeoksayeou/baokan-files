Title: Condolence Message from the Central Nationalities Affairs Commission

Author:
Date: 1953-03-11
Page: 2

    Condolence Message from the Central Nationalities Affairs Commission
    To the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union:
    To the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union:
    The news of the passing of our most beloved revolutionary teacher, Chairman Stalin, has filled the people of all nationalities in China with immense grief.
    To mourn the passing of Chairman Stalin, the people of all nationalities in China will unite even more closely around Chairman Mao Zedong, the leader of the Chinese people, and the Communist Party of China, further consolidate and strengthen the unity of the people of China and the Soviet Union, better learn from the spirit of unity and friendship of all nationalities in the Soviet Union, learn from the patriotism and internationalism of the people of all nationalities in the Soviet Union, and strive to build our great motherland and defend world peace.
    Chairman Stalin's theories and brilliant instructions on the national question will forever illuminate the victorious advance of the people of all nationalities in China.
    Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
                                    March 7, 1953

### 中央民族事务委员会的唁电

1953-03-11
第2版()
专栏：

    中央民族事务委员会的唁电苏联部长会议：苏联最高苏维埃主席团：
    我们最敬爱的革命导师斯大林主席逝世的消息传来，中国各民族人民都感到万分的沉痛。
    中国各民族人民为哀悼斯大林主席的逝世，将更紧密地团结在中国人民领袖毛泽东主席和中国共产党的周围，更加巩固和增强中苏两国人民的团结，更好地学习苏联各民族团结友爱的精神，学习苏联各民族人民的爱国主义和国际主义精神，为建设自己的伟大祖国和保卫世界和平而奋斗。
    斯大林主席的学说和关于民族问题的英明指示，永远照耀着中国各民族人民胜利前进。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府民族事务委员会
                                    一九五三年三月七日
