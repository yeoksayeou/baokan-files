Title: Telegram of Condolence from the Gansu Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1953-03-19
Page: 3

To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia:

    The Gansu Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party was shocked and deeply grieved to learn of the death of Comrade Gottwald. Comrade Gottwald was the great leader of the Czechoslovak people, an outstanding fighter for the world's cause of peace and democracy, and a close friend of the Chinese people. The passing of Comrade Gottwald is not only a great loss to the Czechoslovak people but also a great loss to the camp of peace and democracy. The Gansu Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party extends its deepest condolences to you.

                        Gansu Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party

### 中共甘肃省委员会的唁电

1953-03-19
第3版()
专栏：

    中共甘肃省委员会的唁电捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会：
    中国共产党甘肃省委员会惊悉哥特瓦尔德同志病逝，极为悲痛。哥特瓦尔德同志是捷克斯洛伐克人民的伟大领袖，是世界和平民主事业的杰出战士，是中国人民的亲密朋友。哥特瓦尔德同志的逝世，不仅是捷克斯洛伐克人民的莫大损失，也是和平民主阵营的莫大损失。中国共产党甘肃省委员会谨向你们表示深切的哀悼。
                        中国共产党甘肃省委员会
