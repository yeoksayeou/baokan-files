Title: All-India Peace Council Calls on Indian People to Demand Immediate Cessation of Korean War and Conclusion of Peace Pact

Author:

Date: 1953-03-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 22nd: The All-India Peace Council issued a declaration calling on the Indian people to launch a movement against the expansion of the war, demanding the immediate cessation of the Korean War and the conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact.

The declaration calls on all peace-loving Indian people and the All-India Peace Council to cooperate and strive for these major steps towards achieving peace. The declaration said: "We appeal to you at this moment – at a time when Stalin's death has stirred in the hearts of all good men and women a fervent desire to act for world peace." The declaration also stated: "We appeal to those who are still outside the peace movement today, requesting them to consult with us so that we can work together on as many issues as possible."

In a notice to peace committees throughout India attached to the declaration, the Secretariat of the All-India Peace Council stated: One should contact new social strata – representatives of important groups and prominent figures – to issue this appeal and invite them to consult with representatives of the peace movement on current peace issues.

### 全印和平理事会号召印度人民  要求立即停止朝鲜战争缔结和平公约

1953-03-23
第4版()
专栏：

    全印和平理事会号召印度人民
    要求立即停止朝鲜战争缔结和平公约
    【新华社新德里二十二日电】全印和平理事会发表宣言，号召印度人民进行一个反对扩大战争、要求立即停止朝鲜战争和缔结五大国和平公约的运动。
    宣言呼吁所有爱好和平的印度人民和全印和平理事会合作，为这些达成和平的主要步骤而努力。宣言说：“我们在这种时刻——在斯大林的逝世已在所有善良的男女心中掀起愿为世界和平而行动的热烈愿望的时刻，特来向你们呼吁。”宣言并说：“我们向那些今天还在和平运动以外的人士呼吁，请求他们和我们来磋商，以便我们可以在尽量多的问题上共同努力。”
    全印和平理事会秘书处在附于宣言上的给印度各地和平委员会的通知上说：应该和新的社会阶层——重要团体的代表和著名人士——接触，向他们发出这种呼吁，请他们来与和平运动的代表们协商当前的和平问题。
