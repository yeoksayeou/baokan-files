Title: Opening of the Fourth Session of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR

Author: 
Date: 1953-03-16
Page: 1

    The Fourth Session of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Opens
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 15th] According to a TASS dispatch from Moscow on the 14th: The newspapers reported that the Fourth Session of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR will open at 2:00 PM on March 15th in the Kremlin.

### 苏联最高苏维埃第四次会议开幕

1953-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃第四次会议开幕
    【新华社十五日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十四日讯：各报报道：苏联最高苏维埃第四次会议将于三月十五日下午二时在克里姆林宫开幕。
