Title: Direct Wireless Telegraph Service Opens Between China and Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1953-03-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 28th -- To facilitate telecommunications between China and Pakistan, the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government of China recently consulted with the Ministry of Communications of Pakistan and agreed to open a direct wireless telegraph circuit between Beijing, the capital of China, and Karachi, the capital of Pakistan, starting from March 1, 1953.

    The opening of this circuit will be conducive to strengthening the ties between the governments and peoples of China and Pakistan.

### 中国和巴基斯坦间  直达无线电报开放

1953-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中国和巴基斯坦间
    直达无线电报开放
    【新华社二十八日讯】我国中央人民政府邮电部为便利我国与巴基斯坦两国间的电信传递，最近与巴基斯坦交通部商定：从一九五三年三月一日起开放我国首都北京与巴基斯坦首都喀喇蚩间的直达无线电报电路。
    这条电路的开放，将有利于加强中、巴两国政府和人民间的联系。
