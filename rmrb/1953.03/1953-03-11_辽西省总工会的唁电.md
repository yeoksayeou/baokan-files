Title: Telegram of Condolence from the Liaoxi Provincial Federation of Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1953-03-11
Page: 2

Telegram of Condolence from the Liaoxi Provincial Federation of Trade Unions to the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions:

    Upon hearing the news of the passing of Comrade Stalin, the great leader and teacher of the working people of the world, the workers and staff of Liaoxi Province, China, express their deepest condolences with utmost grief. We pledge to unite more closely with the Soviet people under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, and to unswervingly follow Comrade Stalin's teachings, and fight to the end to defend world peace and security.
                                    Liaoxi Provincial Federation of Trade Unions of China
                                March 9, 1953

### 辽西省总工会的唁电

1953-03-11
第2版()
专栏：

    辽西省总工会的唁电全苏工会中央理事会：
    惊闻全世界劳动人民的伟大领袖和导师斯大林同志逝世的消息，中国辽西省职工以万分沉痛的心情敬致衷心的哀悼。我们誓在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下，与苏联人民更加团结一致，始终不渝地遵循斯大林同志的遗教，为保卫世界和平与安全而奋斗到底。
                                    中国辽西省总工会
                                一九五三年三月九日
