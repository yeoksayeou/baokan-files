Title: Telegram of Condolence from the Shaanxi Provincial Federation of Trade Unions

Author: 
Date: 1953-03-11
Page: 3

Telegram of Condolence from the Shaanxi Provincial Federation of Trade Unions to the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions:

    Upon hearing the devastating news of the passing of Comrade Stalin, the great leader of the working people of the world and the most beloved friend and teacher of the Chinese people, the entire working masses of Shaanxi Province, China, express our deepest condolences with extreme grief! We vow to transform our sorrow into strength, and under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, unite as one, redouble our efforts in production, further develop and consolidate Sino-Soviet friendship, learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union, and follow the path directed by the great Comrade Stalin, to fight to the end for the construction of our motherland, for the defense of world peace, and for the cause of communism.

    Eternal glory to the great Comrade Stalin!
                                China Shaanxi Provincial Federation of Trade Unions
                                March 7, 1953

### 陕西省总工会的唁电

1953-03-11
第3版()
专栏：

    陕西省总工会的唁电全苏工会中央理事会：
    惊闻全世界劳动人民伟大的领袖、中国人民最敬爱的朋友和导师斯大林同志不幸病逝，中国陕西省全体工人群众以万分沉痛的心情，致以深切的哀悼！我们誓化悲痛为力量，在毛主席和中国共产党的领导下，团结一致加倍努力生产，更进一步发展和巩固中苏友谊，学习苏联先进经验，遵循着伟大的斯大林同志指示的道路，为祖国建设、为保卫世界和平，为共产主义的事业而奋斗到底。
    伟大的斯大林同志永垂不朽！
                                中国陕西省总工会
                                一九五三年三月七日
