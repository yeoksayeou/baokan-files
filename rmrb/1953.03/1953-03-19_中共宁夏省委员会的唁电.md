Title: Condolences from the CPC Ningxia Provincial Committee
Author:
Date: 1953-03-19
Page: 3

To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia:

    The Communist Party of China Ningxia Provincial Committee and the people of all ethnic groups express our deepest condolences on the unfortunate passing of Comrade Gottwald, an outstanding fighter of the international workers' movement.

    Under the wise leadership of Comrade Mao Zedong, all the Communist Party members and people of all ethnic groups in our province are determined to unite with the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia and the people of Czechoslovakia, further consolidate the fraternal friendship between the people of China and Czechoslovakia, and fight to the end to defend world peace and complete the cause of communism!
                            The Communist Party of China Ningxia Provincial Committee

### 中共宁夏省委员会的唁电

1953-03-19
第3版()
专栏：

    中共宁夏省委员会的唁电捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会：
    中国共产党宁夏省委员会及各族人民，对国际工人运动的卓越战士哥特瓦尔德同志的不幸逝世，表示深切的哀悼。
    我们全省共产党员和各族人民，在毛泽东同志的英明领导下，决心与捷克斯洛伐克共产党和人民团结一致，更加巩固中捷两国人民兄弟般的友谊，为保卫世界和平，为完成共产主义事业而奋斗到底！
                            中国共产党宁夏省委员会
