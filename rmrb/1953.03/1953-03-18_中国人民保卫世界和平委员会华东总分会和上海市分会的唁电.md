Title: Condolence Message from the East China General Branch and Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
Author:
Date: 1953-03-18
Page: 3

    Condolence Message from the East China General Branch and Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
    To the Czechoslovak Committee for the Defense of Peace:
    We are deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of President Gottwald, the leader of the Czechoslovak people and an outstanding fighter for the cause of world peace and democracy. We extend our deepest and most heartfelt condolences to you and the Czechoslovak people. We firmly believe that the people of China and Czechoslovakia will further strengthen their unity, consolidate the world peace and democracy camp headed by our great ally, the Soviet Union, and jointly wage unremitting struggle to defend and consolidate world peace and oppose the conspiracies to create and prepare for new wars, and will achieve greater victories. Long live the cause of world peace and democracy!
      The East China General Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
      The Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
                              March 15, 1953

### 中国人民保卫世界和平委员会华东总分会和上海市分会的唁电

1953-03-18
第3版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会华东总分会和上海市分会的唁电捷克斯洛伐克保卫和平委员会：
    惊悉捷克斯洛伐克人民领袖、世界和平民主事业的卓越斗士哥特瓦尔德总统的不幸逝世，我们谨向你们和捷克斯洛伐克人民致最沉痛的、深切的哀悼。我们坚决相信：中捷人民将进一步加强团结，巩固以我们的伟大盟邦——苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营，共同为保卫与巩固世界和平、反对制造与准备新战争的阴谋而坚持不懈的斗争，并将获得更大的胜利。世界和平民主事业万岁！
      中国人民保卫世界和平委员会华东总分会
      中国人民保卫世界和平委员会上海市分会
                              一九五三年三月十五日
