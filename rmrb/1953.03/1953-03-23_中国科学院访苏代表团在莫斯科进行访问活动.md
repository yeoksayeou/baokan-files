Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Delegation Conducts Visit Activities in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1953-03-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: The delegation from the Chinese Academy of Sciences is conducting visit activities in Moscow. The delegates received the warmest reception from Soviet scientists. Academician Alexander Topchiev, Secretary of the Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, introduced them to the development of the Soviet Academy of Sciences since its establishment. Topchiev said that all the efforts of the Soviet Academy of Sciences and its institutes are now focused on completing the task set for Soviet scientists by the great Stalin – to strive to occupy the first place in world science. Academician Vasily Nemchinov, Chairman of the Productivity Research Committee of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, told the Chinese scientists about the work of this committee. Professor Viktor Kovda, a Soviet scientist, spoke about the Soviet scientists' assistance to the great communist construction projects. During the visit, the Soviet scientists provided detailed answers to many questions raised by the Chinese scientists. The Chinese scientists toured Moscow and visited the "Moscow History and Reconstruction Museum" and the "Stalin Seventieth Birthday Gift Exhibition Hall".

On the 17th, delegates from the delegation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences met with Academician Alexander Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and prominent scientists from various departments of the Academy. Nesmeyanov introduced the Chinese scientists to the planning system of Soviet science, mentioning that the current scientific work plan of the Soviet Academy of Sciences and its branches includes approximately five hundred different issues. Nesmeyanov emphasized that the entire activity of Soviet scientific research institutions is coordinated with the tasks of the national economic plan. He explained in detail the work of Soviet scientists in discovering various minerals and assisting in the construction of great communist projects. Nesmeyanov finally pointed out that J.V. Stalin's brilliant work "Economic Problems of Socialism in the USSR" and the resolutions of the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union have opened up new prospects for Soviet scientists to engage in huge and effective work for the benefit of their motherland and for communist construction. On the 18th, the delegates visited the Stalin Automobile Factory. Konstantin Vlasov, the factory manager, introduced the delegates to the history of this factory, which was established under the instructions of Lenin and Stalin. The delegates saw that the workers were manufacturing orders for the People's Republic of China.

On the 21st, the Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences held a reception to entertain the delegation from the Chinese Academy of Sciences. Ni. Na. Anichkov, President of the Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences, delivered a welcoming speech at the meeting. He then spoke about the work of the Academy of Medical Sciences. The Chinese delegates raised many questions at the meeting about the organization and planning of Soviet medical science, which were answered in detail by Academician Anatoly Nesterov, Secretary of the Academy of Medical Sciences.

The Chinese delegation has inspected the activities of the Soviet Academy of Sciences and its institutions, and in the next few days will visit medical research institutions in Moscow.

### 中国科学院访苏代表团在莫斯科进行访问活动

1953-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中国科学院访苏代表团在莫斯科进行访问活动
    据新华社讯：中国科学院访苏代表团在莫斯科进行访问活动。代表们受到苏联科学家最亲切的接待。苏联科学院主席团秘书亚历山大·托普切夫院士向他们介绍了苏联科学院成立以来的发展情况。托普切夫说，苏联科学院和它的研究所的一切努力，现在都集中于完成伟大的斯大林给苏联科学家确定的任务——争取在全世界的科学上占第一位。苏联科学院生产力研究委员会主席瓦西里·涅姆钦诺夫院士向中国科学家讲述了这个委员会的工作情况。苏联科学家维克托·科夫达教授作了关于苏联科学家协助伟大共产主义建设工程的谈话。在访问过程中，中国科学家所提出的许多问题，苏联科学家都给予详细的解答。中国科学家们游览了莫斯科城，参观了“莫斯科历史与重建博物馆”和“斯大林七十寿辰礼品展览馆”。
    十七日，中国科学院访苏代表团的代表们访晤了苏联科学院院长亚历山大·涅斯米扬诺夫院士和该院各部门的著名科学家。涅斯米扬诺夫向中国科学家们介绍了苏联科学的计划制度，提到现在苏联科学院及其分院的科学工作计划，约包括五百个不同的问题。涅斯米扬诺夫着重指出：苏联科学研究机关的整个活动，都是和国民经济计划的任务相配合的。他详细地说明了苏联科学家在发现各种矿物方面的工作，和帮助建设伟大共产主义工程的工作。涅斯米扬诺夫最后指出：约·维·斯大林的天才著作“苏联社会主义经济问题”和苏联共产党第十九次代表大会的决议，替苏联科学家开辟了为他们祖国的利益、为共产主义建设从事巨大有效的工作的新前景。代表们十八日参观了斯大林汽车工厂。工厂经理康士坦丁·弗拉索夫向代表们介绍了根据列宁和斯大林的指示建立的这个工厂的历史。代表们看到工人们正在为中华人民共和国制造订货。
    苏联医药科学院二十一日举行招待会，招待中国科学院访苏代表团。苏联医药科学院院长尼·纳·阿尼奇科夫在会上致欢迎词。他接着谈到了医药科学院的工作。中国代表们在会上提出了许多有关苏联医药科学的组织和计划工作的问题，当由医药科学院秘书安纳托利·涅斯捷罗夫院士详细加以答复。
    中国代表团已考察了苏联科学院的活动及其机构的情形，在今后几天中将访问莫斯科的医药研究机关。
