    Our Great Motherland
    With the commencement of large-scale national economic construction, China's civil aviation industry is steadily developing. Civil aviation aircraft transport various important construction materials to all parts of the country every day. This is a scene of the transport aircraft of the China National Aviation Corporation preparing to take off.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    随着国家大规模经济建设的开始，我国民用航空事业正稳步地向前发展。民航飞机每天装载着各种重要的建设物资运往各地。这是中国人民航空公司的运输机正准备起飞的情形。
