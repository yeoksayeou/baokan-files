Title: Grand Rally in Mongolian Capital Celebrates 7th Anniversary of Sino-Mongolian Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Assistance
Author:
Date: 1953-03-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, February 28th: The Mongolian people are celebrating the seventh anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Mongolian Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Assistance. Throughout the capital, Ulaanbaatar, the national flags of the Mongolian People's Republic and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics are hung, and large buildings are adorned with portraits of the great Stalin and huge slogans, celebrating the unbreakable friendship between Mongolia and the Soviet Union.

On the evening of February 27th, the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the Ulaanbaatar City Committee, the Presidium of the Great People's Khural, the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, and representatives of various people's organizations and the Mongolian army held a celebration meeting at the National Theatre. Diplomatic representatives of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, the People's Republic of China, and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea also attended the celebration.

Members of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, headed by the great Stalin, and members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, headed by Tsedenbal, were elected as honorary presidium.

Ramsuren, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic, delivered a report on the celebration of the seventh anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Mongolian Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Assistance.

The hall burst into thunderous applause when he congratulated the eternal friendship between the Mongolian and Soviet peoples and paid tribute to the great leader and teacher of the working people of the world, the most beloved friend and teacher of the Mongolian people—the great Stalin.

Popov, Chargé d'Affaires of the Soviet Union to the Mongolian People's Republic, also spoke at the meeting.

All participants in the celebration enthusiastically passed a congratulatory telegram to J.V. Stalin. People shouted, "Comrade Stalin, hurrah!" The whole audience stood up, and thunderous applause and cheers rang out.

### 庆祝苏蒙友好互助条约七周年  蒙古首都隆重集会

1953-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝苏蒙友好互助条约七周年
    蒙古首都隆重集会
    【新华社讯】塔斯社乌兰巴托二月二十八日电：蒙古人民庆祝苏蒙友好互助条约签订七周年。在首都乌兰巴托，各处都悬挂着蒙古人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟的国旗，大的建筑物上悬挂着伟大的斯大林的画像和巨幅标语，庆祝蒙古和苏联的牢不可破的友谊。
    二月二十七日晚上，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会和乌兰巴托市委员会、大人民呼拉尔主席团、蒙古人民共和国部长会议以及各人民团体和蒙古军队的代表们在国家剧院举行庆祝会。出席这个庆祝会的还有苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟、中华人民共和国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的外交代表。
    以伟大的斯大林为首的苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员和以泽登巴尔为首的蒙古人民革命党中央委员会政治局委员被选为名誉主席团。
    蒙古人民共和国副总理兼外交部长拉姆苏伦作了关于庆祝苏蒙友好互助条约签订七周年的报告。
    当他祝贺蒙古人民和苏联人民的永恒友谊并向世界劳动人民的伟大领袖和导师、蒙古人民最亲爱的朋友和导师——伟大的斯大林致敬时，全场响起暴风雨般的掌声。
    苏联驻蒙古人民共和国代办波波夫也在会上致词。
    所有参加庆祝会的人，极为热情地通过了给约·维·斯大林的贺电。人们高呼：“斯大林同志乌拉！”全场起立，响起暴风雨般地鼓掌声和欢呼声。
