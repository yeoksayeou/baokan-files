Title: Commander-in-Chief Zhu De's Telegram of Condolence
Author:
Date: 1953-03-15
Page: 1

To Comrade Čepička, Minister of National Defense of the Czechoslovak Republic:
    Upon learning of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Gottwald, I am filled with deep sorrow. On behalf of all the soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and in my own name, I extend to you my heartfelt condolences.
    With sincere mourning for Comrade Gottwald, the Chinese People's Liberation Army will unite even more closely with the Czechoslovak army, vigilantly guarding against any imperialist conspiracies of aggression and provocation, and will fight together to the end to defend world peace.
                                 Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zhu De
                                 March 15, 1953, Beijing

### 朱德总司令的唁电

1953-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德总司令的唁电捷克斯洛伐克共和国国防部部长契皮茨卡同志：
    获悉哥特瓦尔德同志不幸病逝的消息，我怀着非常悲痛的心情，谨代表中国人民解放军全体战士并以我自己的名义，向您致以衷心的哀悼。
    中国人民解放军怀着对哥特瓦尔德同志的真诚悼念，将与捷克斯洛伐克军队更加紧密地团结，警惕着帝国主义者任何侵略挑拨的阴谋，为保卫世界和平而共同奋斗到底。
                                 中国人民解放军总司令  朱德
                                 一九五三年三月十五日　北京
