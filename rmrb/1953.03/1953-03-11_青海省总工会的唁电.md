Title: Condolence Telegram from the Qinghai Provincial Federation of Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1953-03-11
Page: 3

To the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions:

    Upon hearing the news of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Stalin, the great leader of the working class and working people of the world, all workers in Qinghai Province, China, express our deepest condolences with immense grief. We are determined to strive for the defense of world peace under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and our wise leader Comrade Mao Zedong, and together with the Soviet working class and the working classes of all countries around the world.

                                China Qinghai Provincial Federation of Trade Unions
                                    March 8, 1953

### 青海省总工会的唁电

1953-03-11
第3版()
专栏：

    青海省总工会的唁电全苏工会中央理事会：
    惊闻全世界工人阶级和劳动人民的伟大领袖斯大林同志不幸逝世的消息后，我们中国青海省全体工人在万分悲痛心情中谨致沉痛的哀悼。我们决心在中国共产党和英明领袖毛泽东同志领导下，并和苏联工人阶级及全世界各国工人阶级一道，为保卫世界和平而努力奋斗。
                                中国青海省总工会
                                    一九五三年三月八日
