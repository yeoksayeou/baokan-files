Title: 80,000 Japanese Transportation Workers Strike
Author:
Date: 1953-03-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, March 20th: 80,000 workers of the Nippon Express Co., Ltd., which undertakes railway transportation business in Japan, began a 72-hour strike simultaneously across the country on the 19th, demanding wage increases. The strike has brought 60 percent of the national railway freight transport to a standstill throughout Japan.

### 日本八万运输工人罢工

1953-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    日本八万运输工人罢工
    【新华社二十日讯】东京消息：承办日本铁道运输业务的日本通运公司的八万名工人，为了要求增加工资，从十九日起同时在全国各地举行七十二小时的罢工。罢工使日本全国国营铁路货运的百分之六十陷于停顿。
