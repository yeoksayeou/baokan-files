Title: Our Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, Tan Xilin, Expresses Deep Condolences to the Czechoslovak Government
Author:
Date: 1953-03-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 15th: After learning of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Klement Gottwald, President of Czechoslovakia and Chairman of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, our Ambassador to the Czechoslovak Republic, Tan Xilin, immediately went to the Czechoslovak Ministry of Foreign Affairs on the evening of the 14th, accompanied by all members of the embassy, to express his deepest condolences to Dr. Gertruda Sekaninova-Čakrtova, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Czechoslovakia.
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 15th: On the morning of the 15th, our Ambassador to the Czechoslovak Republic, Tan Xilin, accompanied by all members of our embassy, personnel from various Chinese delegations staying in Prague, staff from the Xinhua News Agency Prague branch, and Chinese students studying in Czechoslovakia, went to the Office of the Presidium of the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic to express their deepest condolences to Czechoslovak Premier Antonín Zápotocký on the unfortunate passing of Klement Gottwald, President of the Czechoslovak Republic and Chairman of the Czechoslovak Communist Party. Ambassador Tan Xilin also visited Czechoslovak Deputy Premier Viliam Široký at 10:00 am on the same day to express his condolences. Deputy Premier Široký expressed his gratitude for the solidarity shown by the Chinese people to them after the passing of the most beloved leader of the Czechoslovak people, and especially for Chairman Mao Zedong's personal visit to the Czechoslovak Embassy in our country to offer condolences upon learning of this unfortunate news.

### 我驻捷大使谭希林向捷政府表示深切哀悼

1953-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻捷大使谭希林向捷政府表示深切哀悼
    【新华社布拉格十五日电】我驻捷克斯洛伐克共和国大使谭希林在获知捷克斯洛伐克总统、捷克斯洛伐克共产党主席克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志逝世的不幸消息后，立即在十四日晚偕同大使馆全体人员前往捷克斯洛伐克外交部，向捷外交部副部长格特鲁达·塞卡尼诺娃——查克尔托娃博士面致最深切的哀悼。 【新华社布拉格十五日电】我国驻捷克斯洛伐克共和国大使谭希林十五日晨偕同我大使馆全体人员、我国逗留在布拉格的各代表团人员、新华通讯社布拉格分社工作人员和我留捷学生等，前往捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府主席团办公厅，为捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统、捷克斯洛伐克共产党主席克利门特·哥特瓦尔德的不幸逝世，向捷克斯洛伐克总理安托宁·萨波托斯基表示最深切的哀悼。谭希林大使并于同日上午十时往访捷副总理威廉·西罗基，表示哀悼。西罗基副总理对于中国人民在捷克斯洛伐克人民最敬爱的领袖逝世后对他们表示的团结一致，尤其是对于毛泽东主席获悉了这个不幸的消息即亲往捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使馆吊唁一事，表示感谢。
