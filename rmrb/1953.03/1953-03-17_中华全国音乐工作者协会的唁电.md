Title: Telegram of Condolence from the All-China Musicians Association

Author: 
Date: 1953-03-17
Page: 3

Telegram of Condolence from the All-China Musicians Association to the Czechoslovak Composers' Union in Prague:

	We are deeply saddened by the news of the passing of Comrade Gottwald. On behalf of Chinese musicians, we extend our deepest condolences to you. We are convinced that musicians from China and Czechoslovakia will unite more closely and fight to the end for our common cause.

		Lü Ji, Chairman of the All-China Musicians Association
					March 15th, Beijing

### 中华全国音乐工作者协会的唁电

1953-03-17
第3版()
专栏：

    中华全国音乐工作者协会的唁电布拉格捷克斯洛伐克作曲家联盟：
    哥特瓦尔德同志逝世的噩耗传来，我们深感悲痛，谨代表中国音乐工作者向你们致以深切的哀悼。我们深信：中捷两国音乐工作者将更紧密团结在一起，为我们的共同事业奋斗到底。
        中华全国音乐工作者协会主席　吕　骥
                                    三月十五日于北京
