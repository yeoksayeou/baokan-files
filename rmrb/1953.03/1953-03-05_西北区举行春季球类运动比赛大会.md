    Northwest Region Holds Spring Ball Games Tournament

    The Northwest Region's 1953 Spring Ball Games Tournament was held in Xi'an from February 25th to March 1st. More than 480 athletes participated in the tournament, including Han, Uyghur, Hui, Tibetan, Kazakh, Tatar, Uzbek, Manchu, Dongxiang, Sibe, and Salar nationalities. It was the first grand gathering of ball game athletes from various ethnic groups in the Northwest.

    The tournament included seven events: basketball, volleyball, softball, tennis, badminton, table tennis, and "Kang Le" (recreation) ball. The main achievements of the competition were as follows: The Xi'an City team won the men's basketball and women's volleyball and softball championships; the Xinjiang Province team won the men's volleyball championship; the Gansu Province team won the women's basketball championship; and the Shaanxi Province team won the men's softball championship. The tournament also selected the Northwest Region's ball game teams to attend the National Ball Games Tournament.

### 西北区举行春季球类运动比赛大会

1953-03-05
第3版()
专栏：文化简讯

    西北区举行春季球类运动比赛大会
    西北区一九五三年春季球类运动比赛大会在二月二十五日至三月一日在西安举行。参加大会比赛的运动员有四百八十多人，包括汉、维吾尔、回、藏、哈萨克、塔塔尔、乌兹别克、满、东乡、锡伯、撒拉等十一个民族；是西北各族球类运动员的第一次盛大聚会。
    大会分篮球、排球、垒球、网球、羽毛球、乒乓球、康乐球等七个项目进行比赛。比赛结果的主要成绩：西安市代表队获得男子篮球和女子排球、垒球冠军；新疆省代表队获得男子排球冠军；甘肃省代表队获得女子篮球冠军；陕西省代表队获得男子垒球冠军。大会并选拔了出席全国球类运动比赛大会的西北区各项球类代表队。
