Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 170th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-03-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 13th—The 170th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 13th. The meeting heard a report from Ma Xulun, Minister of the Higher Education Department of the Central People's Government, on the current situation and problems of teaching reform in universities and colleges. The meeting also approved various appointment and removal proposals and appointment and removal proposals to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第一七零次会议

1953-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一七零次会议
    【新华社十三日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百七十次政务会议在十三日下午举行。会议听取了中央人民政府高等教育部马叙伦部长所作的关于目前高等学校教学改革的情况和问题的报告。会议并通过了各项任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案。
