Title: Condolences from the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army
Author: 
Date: 1953-03-18
Page: 2

To Comrade Čepička, Minister of National Defense of the Czechoslovak Republic:

    We are deeply saddened to hear of the unfortunate passing of President Klement Gottwald, the wise leader of the Czechoslovak people and the outstanding fighter of the international communist movement. In the common cause of the world peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union, under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong, we are determined to strengthen the close unity with the people and army of Czechoslovakia, maintain high vigilance against any aggressive acts and sabotage conspiracies of imperialism, and fight to the end to defend the security of our motherland and to defend peace in the East and the world.
        East China Military Region, Third Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
            Commander Chen Yi, Political Commissar Rao Shushi, Deputy
            Commander Su Yu, Deputy Political Commissar Tan Zhenlin, leading all
            commanders and fighters
                            March 16, 1953

### 华东军区、第三野战军的唁电

1953-03-18
第2版()
专栏：

    华东军区、第三野战军的唁电捷克斯洛伐克共和国国防部部长契皮茨卡同志：
    惊闻捷克斯洛伐克人民的英明领袖、国际共产主义运动的卓越斗士克利门特·哥特瓦尔德总统不幸逝世，我们谨致无限沉痛的哀悼。在以苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营的共同事业中，我们在毛泽东主席的领导下，决心加强与捷克斯洛伐克人民及军队的亲密团结，高度警惕帝国主义的任何侵略行为与破坏阴谋，并为保卫祖国的安全，保卫东方与世界和平而奋斗到底。
        中国人民解放军华东军区、第三野战军
            司令员陈毅、政治委员饶漱石、副司
            令员粟裕、副政治委员谭震林率全体
            指挥员、战斗员
                            一九五三年三月十六日
