Title: Our Art Troupes Conclude Performances to Comfort Soviet Troops Stationed in Lushun
Author:
Date: 1953-03-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Dalian, 2nd. The five art troupes led by the Delegation of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China to Comfort Soviet Troops Stationed in the Lushunkou Area held closing ceremonies for their comfort performances in the Lushunkou area on the evening of the 1st.

Attending the closing ceremony in Dalian were: Chen Yi, Minister of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government, who led the five art troupes; Ouyang Qin, Mayor of the Dalian Municipal People's Government and Secretary of the Dalian Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Qiao Chuanjue, President of the Dalian Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; the heads of the five art troupes; and representatives of Soviet officers and soldiers, totaling more than 1,500 people.

After the performance by the art troupes, the closing ceremony was presided over by a Soviet representative who delivered a speech. The Soviet representative expressed deep gratitude to the art troupes for their comfort performances in recent days. Afterwards, Soviet soldiers presented banners to the art troupes. Chen Yi accepted the banners on behalf of the art troupes and delivered a speech at the meeting, extending high respect and gratitude to the Soviet troops who were far from their homeland and guarding Lushunkou. The closing ceremony concluded with the entire audience singing the song "Moscow-Beijing."

The five art troupes led by the Delegation of the Central People's Government to Comfort Soviet Troops Stationed in the Lushunkou Area have been performing in the Lushunkou area since February 22nd, and have been warmly welcomed by Soviet generals and soldiers.

### 我国文艺工作团结束慰问驻旅顺苏军的演出

1953-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我国文艺工作团结束慰问驻旅顺苏军的演出
    【新华社大连二日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团带领的五个文艺工作团，一日晚分别在旅顺口地区举行慰问演出闭幕式。
    在旅大市参加闭幕式的有：率领五个文艺工作团的中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部部长陈沂，旅大市人民政府市长、中国共产党旅大市委员会书记欧阳钦，旅大市中苏友好协会会长乔传珏和五个文艺工作团的负责人以及苏军军官、士兵代表等共一千五百多人。
    闭幕式在文艺工作团演出后由苏军代表主持并致词。苏军代表对各文艺工作团连日来的慰问演出表示深厚的谢意。接着，苏军战士向文艺工作团献旗。陈沂代表文艺工作团受旗后，并在会上讲话，向远离祖国、守卫旅顺口的苏军致崇高的敬礼和谢意。闭幕式在全场齐唱“莫斯科—北京”歌曲后结束。
    中央人民政府慰问驻旅顺口地区苏军代表团带领的五个文艺工作团自二月二十二日起分别在旅顺口地区演出，受到苏军将领和士兵的热烈欢迎。
