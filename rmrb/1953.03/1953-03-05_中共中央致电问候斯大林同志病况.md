Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Telegram to Inquire About Comrade Stalin's Illness

Author:

Date: 1953-03-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 4th.
The CPC Central Committee Sends Telegram to Inquire About Comrade Stalin's Illness

    To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union:
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China is following the serious condition of Comrade Stalin's health with utmost concern, and sincerely hopes that Comrade Stalin's condition will improve and he will recover his health.
    In these difficult days of Comrade Stalin's serious illness, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China likewise believes that the Communist Party of China and the Chinese people will, with the greatest firmness, unite even more closely with the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet people, heighten our vigilance, and fight to the end for our common cause of defending world peace and security.
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    March 4, 1953

### 中共中央致电问候斯大林同志病况

1953-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央致电问候斯大林同志病况
    【新华社四日讯】中国共产党中央委员会致电苏联共产党中央委员会问候斯大林同志病况。电文如下：苏联共产党中央委员会：
    中国共产党中央委员会以万分关切的心情，关怀着斯大林同志健康的严重情况，并衷心地祝盼斯大林同志的病情好转，恢复健康。
    在斯大林同志重病期间的困难的日子里，中国共产党中央委员会同样相信：中国共产党和中国人民一定会以最大的坚定性，和苏联共产党及苏联人民更加紧密地团结一致，提高警惕，为我们保卫世界和平与安全的共同事业而奋斗到底。
    中国共产党中央委员会
    一九五三年三月四日
