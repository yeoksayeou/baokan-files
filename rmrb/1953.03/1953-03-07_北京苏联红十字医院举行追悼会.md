Title: Memorial Service Held at Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital
Author:
Date: 1953-03-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, March 6th: The Soviet and Chinese staff of the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital, along with patients receiving treatment there, mourned Comrade Stalin with profound grief. At noon on the 6th, the news of Comrade Stalin's death reached the hospital from the Soviet Embassy in China, and a solemn atmosphere of sorrow enveloped the entire hospital. The Soviet flag within the hospital was bordered in black and flown at half-mast. Images of Comrade Stalin in offices and patient rooms were draped with black and red ribbons.

　　At 2:30 PM, a memorial service was held by the Soviet and Chinese staff of the entire hospital. Many hospitalized and outpatients who were able to get out of bed also voluntarily participated in the memorial service. Director Furokhalov read the announcement issued by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. All those present were deeply moved to tears. The chairman of the hospital's trade union, Dr. Yegorov, Dr. Maldnova, nurse Yefremova, Chinese doctor Sun Yanqing, and Chinese patient Li Xugang, among others, spoke at the meeting. They unanimously expressed their determination to transform grief into strength, to further improve their work, to continue strengthening the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people, and to strive for lasting world peace. Yegorov said, "Comrade Stalin instructed us to strengthen the eternal friendship with the Chinese people. We vow to fulfill his instruction. Comrade Stalin instructed us to strengthen the defense of peace. We vow to fulfill his instruction." Sun Yanqing said, "We must closely unite around the party of Lenin and Stalin and the Communist Party of China, consolidate the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people, and fight together for peace."

　　After the memorial service, Director Furokhalov convened a meeting of the hospital's key staff, urging everyone to work better and harder, to correct shortcomings in their work, to strive to complete their work tasks, and to continue strengthening the friendship between the Soviet and Chinese people in memory of Comrade Stalin.

### 北京苏联红十字医院举行追悼会

1953-03-07
第3版()
专栏：

    北京苏联红十字医院举行追悼会
    【新华社六日讯】北京苏联红十字医院苏、中两国工作人员和在该院治疗的病人，怀着万分沉痛的心情，悼念斯大林同志。六日中午，斯大林同志逝世的消息从苏联驻华大使馆传到医院后，悲痛肃穆的气氛笼罩了全院。院内苏联国旗周围镶上了黑边，半降致哀。办公室和病房间内的斯大林同志的遗像上都披上了黑红二色的带子。
    下午二时半，全院苏、中两国工作人员举行了追悼会。许多能够起床的住院病人和门诊病人也都自动参加了追悼会。院长甫洛哈洛夫宣读了苏联共产党中央委员会、苏联部长会议和苏联最高苏维埃主席团发出的公告，全体人员都沉痛地流下泪来。该院职工会主席、医师耶果洛夫，医师玛尔德诺娃，护士依芙丽叶娃和中国医师孙衍庆、中国病人李续纲等都在会上讲了话，他们一致表示：要把悲痛化做力量，更进一步做好工作；继续加强中苏两国人民的牢不可破的友谊，为世界持久和平而奋斗。耶果洛夫说：“斯大林同志嘱咐我们要加强和中国人民的永久的友谊，我们宣誓：一定能完成他的嘱咐；斯大林同志嘱咐我们加强保卫和平，我们宣誓：一定能完成他的嘱咐。”孙衍庆说：“我们要紧密地团结在列宁、斯大林党和中国共产党的周围，巩固中、苏两国人民的友谊，共同为争取和平而斗争。”
    追悼会结束后，甫洛哈洛夫院长召集该院主要工作人员谈话，要求大家更好地、更努力地进行工作，改正工作中的缺点，努力完成工作任务，继续加强苏、中两国人民的友谊，以纪念斯大林同志。
