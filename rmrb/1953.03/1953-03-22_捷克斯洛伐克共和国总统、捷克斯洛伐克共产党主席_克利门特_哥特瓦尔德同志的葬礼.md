Title: Funeral of the President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Chairman of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, Comrade Klement Gottwald
Author:
Date: 1953-03-22
Page: 4

    Funeral of the President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Chairman of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, Comrade Klement Gottwald
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 20th -- The funeral of Comrade Klement Gottwald, President of the Czechoslovak Republic and Chairman of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, was held at 1:00 PM on the 19th. In every factory, mine, locomotive, and ship throughout the Republic, sirens sounded to express mourning, and all work and transportation were suspended for five minutes.
    Attending the funeral were: members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia and Slovakia, headed by A. Zápotocký, ministers of various ministries of the government and senior staff of state organs, the Soviet government delegation headed by N.A. Bulganin, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR and Minister of Defense, the delegation of the People's Republic of China headed by Premier Zhou Enlai, the Polish delegation headed by Chairman B. Bierut, the Mongolian delegation headed by Premier Y. Tsedenbal, the Romanian delegation headed by Deputy Chairman G. Apostol, the Bulgarian delegation headed by Todor Zhivkov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, the Hungarian delegation headed by Minister of Defense M. Farkas, the Albanian delegation headed by Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers S. Koleka, the delegation of the German Democratic Republic headed by Deputy Chairman of the People's Chamber H. Matern, the Vietnamese delegation headed by the Vietnamese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Nguyen Luong Bang, and the Korean delegation headed by Vice Premier Zheng Yilong.
    Also attending the funeral were: General Secretary of the Communist Party of Great Britain, Harry Pollitt; Deputy General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party, Luigi Longo; Chairman of the Communist Party of Austria, Johann Koplenig; Member of the Political Bureau of the French Communist Party, Léon Mauvais; and Member of the Political Bureau of the Spanish Communist Party, Enrique Lister.
    Also attending the funeral were: members of the Presidium of the Central Action Committee of the National Front, representatives of various workers' organizations and people from all walks of life in Czechoslovakia, diplomatic envoys from various countries to Czechoslovakia, and members of the World Peace Council.
    Deputy Prime Minister V. Široký delivered a speech at the beginning of the funeral. After the speech, Gottwald's closest comrades-in-arms, Prime Minister Zápotocký, Deputy Prime Ministers Široký, Ďuriš, Bacílek, Kopecký, and Novotný, as well as Marshal Bulganin, Premier Zhou Enlai, Chairman Bierut, and Premier Tsedenbal, carried the coffin draped with the national flag on their shoulders.
    The coffin was carried from the Spanish Hall to the Matthias Gate of the Presidential Palace and placed on a gun carriage drawn by six black horses. The funeral procession was led by a military guard, followed by standard-bearers carrying flags embroidered with black gauze and the words "Truth Prevails." Behind them were army soldiers carrying wreaths dedicated to Comrade Gottwald from all parts of the country and the world. Ahead of the gun carriage walked generals, carrying Gottwald's posthumous medals on red velvet cushions.
    The President's family, responsible cadres of the government and the Party, foreign delegations, foreign envoys, and people's representatives followed the President's coffin from the Presidential Palace to Wenceslas Square. Tens of thousands of Prague citizens stood on both sides of the street to bid their last farewell to their dearest leader.
    When passing Letná Plain, the funeral procession stopped in front of the neatly arranged troops. The entire Czechoslovak Defense Forces, Civil Defense Forces, and Public Security Forces paid their last respects to their deceased Commander-in-Chief. Formations of jet planes and other aircraft flew over the square, bidding farewell to the leader.
    A memorial service was held in Wenceslas Square.
    Prime Minister Zápotocký and Marshal Bulganin successively spoke at the meeting. They recounted the great life of Comrade Gottwald, dedicated to the cause of the motherland and the working class throughout the world.
    At dusk, the gun carriage carrying the President left the square and headed towards the National Memorial on Vítkov Hill—his final resting place.
    At 8:00 PM, a brief ceremony was held in the memorial hall. At this moment, the band played "The Internationale," artillery salutes were fired, and all the people of Czechoslovakia and the working people of the world paid their last respects to Comrade Klement Gottwald, one of the outstanding leaders of the international workers' movement and the leader and mentor of the Czechoslovak people.

### 捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统、捷克斯洛伐克共产党主席  克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志的葬礼

1953-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统、捷克斯洛伐克共产党主席
    克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志的葬礼
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统、捷克斯洛伐克共产党主席克·哥特瓦尔德同志的葬礼在十九日下午一时举行。共和国各地的每一个工厂、矿山、机车和船只都鸣汽笛志哀，一切工作和交通都停止五分钟。
    参加葬礼的有：以安·萨波托斯基为首的捷克斯洛伐克和斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会委员，政府各部部长和国家机关高级人员，以苏联部长会议第一副主席兼国防部长尼·亚·布尔加宁元帅为首的苏联政府代表团，以周恩来总理为首的中华人民共和国代表团，以波·贝鲁特主席为首的波兰代表团，以泽登巴尔总理为首的蒙古代表团，以格·阿波斯托尔副主席为首的罗马尼亚代表团，以保加利亚共产党中央委员会书记托多尔·日夫科夫为首的保加利亚代表团，以国防部长米哈伊·法卡斯为首的匈牙利代表团，以部长会议副主席斯·科列加为首的阿尔巴尼亚代表团，以人民议院副主席赫·马特恩为首的德意志民主共和国代表团，以越南驻苏联大使阮良朋为首的越南代表团，以郑一龙副首相为首的朝鲜代表团。
    参加葬礼的还有英国共产党总书记哈·波立特；意大利共产党副总书记路·隆哥；奥地利共产党主席约翰·柯普勒尼希；法国共产党政治局委员利昂·摩维；和西班牙共产党政治局委员恩里克·李斯特等。
    参加葬礼的还有：民族阵线中央行动委员会主席团人员，捷克斯洛伐克的各个工人组织和各界人士的代表，各国驻捷外交使节和世界和平理事会理事们。
    威·西罗基副总理在葬礼开始时致词。致词毕，哥特瓦尔德的最亲密的战友萨波托斯基总理，副总理西罗基、杜兰斯基、巴契列克、柯别茨基和诺沃提尼，以及布尔加宁元帅、周恩来总理、贝鲁特主席和泽登巴尔总理把盖着国旗的灵柩抬到肩上。
    灵柩从西班牙大厅抬到总统府的马蒂亚斯门，安放到由六匹黑马牵引的炮车上。送葬行列由军事卫队为前导，后面是旗手，举着镶黑纱的写着“真理战胜”字样的旗帜。再后面的是陆军战士，他们抬着全国各地和全世界各地献给哥特瓦尔德同志的花圈。炮车前面走着将军们，他们用红色天鹅绒的垫子抬着哥特瓦尔德生前所得的勋章。
    总统的家属、政府和党的负责干部、外国代表团、外国使节和人民代表跟着总统的灵柩，从总统府走向瓦茨拉夫广场。成千上万的布拉格人民站在街道两旁向他们最亲爱的领袖最后告别。
    在路过勒特尼广场时，送葬行列在排列整齐的部队前停了下来。捷克斯洛伐克全体国防军、民防部队和公安部队向他们已故的总司令致最后的敬礼。喷气式飞机和其他飞机的编队掠过广场上的天空，向领袖告别。
    追悼大会在瓦茨拉夫广场举行。
    萨波托斯基总理和布尔加宁元帅相继在会上讲话。他们叙述了哥特瓦尔德同志的献给祖国和全世界工人阶级事业的伟大的一生。
    黄昏时分，载着总统的炮车离开广场，向着维特科夫山上的民族解放纪念堂——他的安息地前进。
    下午八时，在纪念堂中举行了简短的仪式，这时，乐队奏起“国际歌”，礼炮齐鸣，捷克斯洛伐克的全体人民和全世界劳动人民向国际工人运动的卓越领袖之一、捷克斯洛伐克人民的领袖和导师克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志致最后的敬礼。
    （新华社布拉格二十日电）
