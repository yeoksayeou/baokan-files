Title: Telegrams of Condolence from the Gansu Provincial and Lanzhou City Federation of Trade Unions

Author:

Date: 1953-03-11
Page: 3

Telegrams of Condolence from the Gansu Provincial and Lanzhou City Federation of Trade Unions to the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions:

	We are shocked to learn of the passing of our most beloved revolutionary teacher and the great leader of the working people of the world, Comrade Stalin. The working class of Gansu Province, China, feels boundless sorrow and extends our sincere condolences to all the workers, collective farmers, and intellectuals of the Soviet Union! The working class of Gansu is determined to transform grief into strength, forever follow the direction indicated to us by Comrade Stalin, and under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong and the Chinese Communist Party, resolutely and forever unite closely with the great Soviet people, consolidate and strengthen the peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union, heighten our vigilance, redouble our efforts, increase labor productivity, accelerate the economic construction of New China, and fight to the end for the final victory of the struggle to resist US aggression and aid Korea, and for the common cause of defending world peace!

					China Gansu Provincial Federation of Trade Unions
					China Lanzhou City Federation of Trade Unions
					March 6, 1953

### 甘肃省和兰州市总工会的唁电

1953-03-11
第3版()
专栏：

    甘肃省和兰州市总工会的唁电全苏工会中央理事会：
    惊悉我们最敬爱的革命导师、全世界劳动人民伟大的领袖斯大林同志逝世，中国甘肃全省工人阶级感到无限沉痛，谨向全苏联的工人、集体农庄农民和知识分子致以亲切的慰问！甘肃工人阶级决心化悲痛为力量，永远遵循着斯大林同志所指示给我们的方向前进，在毛泽东主席和中国共产党的领导下，坚决和伟大的苏联人民永远紧密地团结一致，巩固并加强以苏联为首的和平民主阵营，提高警惕，加倍努力，提高劳动生产率，加速新中国的经济建设，为抗美援朝斗争的最后胜利，为保卫世界永久和平的共同事业奋斗到底！
                                中国甘肃省总工会
                                中国兰州市总工会
                                一九五三年三月六日
