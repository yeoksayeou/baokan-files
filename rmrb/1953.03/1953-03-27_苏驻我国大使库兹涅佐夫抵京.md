Title: Soviet Ambassador to China Kuznetsov Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-03-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 26th: The newly appointed Ambassador of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics to China, Kuznetsov, arrived in Beijing by plane from Moscow on the afternoon of the 26th.

Those who went to the airport to welcome him included: Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Qi Yanming, Deputy Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council; Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Yan Baohang, Deputy Director; Xu Yixin, Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department; Wen Ning, Deputy Director; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; Shen Ping, Deputy Director; Peng Zhen, Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; Zeng Mei, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command; Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; and Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretary-General.

Also present at the airport to welcome him were: Pan Youxin, the former Soviet Ambassador to China, and personnel from the Soviet Embassy in China.

Diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various countries to China were also present at the airport to welcome him.

### 苏驻我国大使库兹涅佐夫抵京

1953-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

    苏驻我国大使库兹涅佐夫抵京
     【新华社二十六日讯】苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟新任驻我国大使库兹涅佐夫在二十六日下午乘飞机由莫斯科到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的，有：中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫，政务院副秘书长齐燕铭、外交部办公厅主任王炳南、副主任阎宝航、苏联东欧司司长徐以新、副司长温宁、交际处处长王倬如、副处长沈平，以及北京市人民政府市长彭真，京津卫戍司令部副参谋长曾美和中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章、副总干事李沾吾等。
    前往机场欢迎的，有：苏联前任驻我国大使潘友新以及苏联驻我国大使馆人员。
    前往机场欢迎的，还有各国驻我国使节及使馆人员。
