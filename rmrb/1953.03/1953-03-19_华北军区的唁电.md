Title: Telegram of Condolence from the North China Military Region
Author:
Date: 1953-03-19
Page: 2

To the Funeral Committee of Comrade Gottwald:

We are deeply saddened to hear of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Klement Gottwald, the great leader of the Czechoslovak people, the outstanding fighter of the world communist movement, and the close friend of the Chinese people. The passing of Comrade Gottwald is not only a great loss to the Czechoslovak people, but also a great loss to the world peace and democracy camp. All commanders and fighters of the North China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army express their deepest condolences. We believe that our entire army, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De, will unite more closely with the Czechoslovak people and the Czechoslovak army, redouble our vigilance, further consolidate the world peace and democracy camp headed by the great Soviet Union, and fight together to the end to defend world peace.

Nie Rongzhen, Bo Yibo, Xu Xiangqian, Liu Lantao, Yang Chengwu, Sun Yi, Zhu Liangcai, Zhang Nansheng, and Zhang Zhixiang, leading all commanders and fighters of the North China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 华北军区的唁电

1953-03-19
第2版()
专栏：

    华北军区的唁电哥特瓦尔德同志治丧委员会：
    惊闻捷克斯洛伐克人民伟大的领袖、世界共产主义运动的卓越战士、中国人民的亲密朋友克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志不幸逝世，我们感到非常悲痛。哥特瓦尔德同志的逝世，不仅是捷克斯洛伐克人民的极大损失，同时也是世界和平民主阵营的极大损失，我们中国人民解放军华北军区全体指挥员、战斗员，表示深切的哀悼。我们相信：我们全军，在中国共产党和毛泽东主席、朱德总司令领导下，将与捷克斯洛伐克人民、捷克斯洛伐克军队，更加紧密地团结一致，百倍提高警惕，进一步巩固以伟大的苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营，为保卫世界和平而共同奋斗到底。
    中国人民解放军华北军区聂荣臻、薄一波、徐向前、刘澜涛、杨成武、孙毅、朱良才、张南生、张致祥率中国人民解放华北军区全体指挥员、战斗员
