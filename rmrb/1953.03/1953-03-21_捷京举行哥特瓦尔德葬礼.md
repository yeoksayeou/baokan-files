Title: Funeral of Gottwald Held in Prague
Author:
Date: 1953-03-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 20th: According to TASS, the people of Czechoslovakia bid their final farewell to Klement Gottwald, President of the Czechoslovak Republic and Chairman of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, on the 19th. At 10:00 AM, the Spanish Hall of Prague Castle, where Klement Gottwald's coffin was placed, was closed to the public. At 1:00 PM, all traffic and work across Czechoslovakia ceased for five minutes, with factories and trains sounding their sirens simultaneously for five minutes to express mourning. The funeral ceremony began in the Spanish Hall. After the national anthems of Czechoslovakia and the Soviet Union were played, Deputy Prime Minister Viliam Široký spoke on behalf of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and the government. Then, Klement Gottwald's coffin was moved from the Spanish Hall and placed on a gun carriage. The funeral procession proceeded towards Letná Square. The President's coffin was placed in front of the platform in the square, and troops marched past the platform to bid farewell to President Gottwald amidst mournful music. Then, the funeral procession proceeded to Wenceslas Square. A memorial service was held in Wenceslas Square. Antonín Zápotocký, Prime Minister of the Czechoslovak Republic, spoke at the meeting, and his speech was a pledge of the Czechoslovak people's loyalty to Klement Gottwald's legacy. The head of the Soviet delegation, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and Marshal of the Soviet Union, Nikolai Aleksandrovich Bulganin, also spoke at the meeting. After the meeting, the funeral procession proceeded to the National Memorial on Vítkov Hill. At 7:30 PM, Klement Gottwald's coffin was placed in the mausoleum amidst the singing of "The Internationale" and a salute of guns. The people of Czechoslovakia deeply mourn this irreparable loss. They will never forget their glorious son and leader, Klement Gottwald.

### 捷京举行哥特瓦尔德葬礼

1953-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

    捷京举行哥特瓦尔德葬礼
    【新华社二十日讯】据塔斯社布拉格讯：十九日，捷克斯洛伐克人民最后一次向捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统、捷克斯洛伐克共产党主席克利门特·哥特瓦尔德告别。上午十时，安放克利门特·哥特瓦尔德灵柩的布拉格总统府的西班牙大厅停止开放。下午一时，捷克斯洛伐克全国各地停止一切交通和工作五分钟，各地工厂、火车同时鸣放汽笛五分钟表示哀悼。殡仪在西班牙大厅中开始。在奏了捷克斯洛伐克国歌和苏联国歌后，副总理威廉·西罗基代表捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会和政府讲话。然后，克利门特·哥特瓦尔德的灵柩从西班牙大厅中移出，安放在炮车上。送葬的行列走向勒特尼广场。总统的灵柩安放在广场的台前，军队在哀乐声中从台前走过向哥特瓦尔德总统告别。然后送葬的行列走向瓦茨拉夫广场。在瓦茨拉夫广场举行了追悼大会。捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府总理安托宁·萨波托斯基在会上讲话，他的讲话是捷克斯洛伐克人民忠诚于克利门特·哥特瓦尔德遗训的誓言。苏联代表团团长、苏联部长会议第一副主席、苏联元帅尼·亚·布尔加宁也在会上讲了话。会后，送葬的行列走向维特科夫山的民族解放纪念馆。下午七时三十分，克利门特·哥特瓦尔德的灵柩在“国际歌”和礼炮声中放进陵墓。捷克斯洛伐克人民深切地哀痛这不可挽回的损失。他们永远不会忘记他们的光荣儿子和领袖克利门特·哥特瓦尔德。
