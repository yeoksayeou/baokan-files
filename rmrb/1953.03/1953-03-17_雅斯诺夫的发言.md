YASNOV's Speech

Comrades Delegates! I declare the fourth session of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics open.

Comrades Delegates!

With the utmost grief, with the sentiment of mourning an irreplaceable loss, the Soviet people bid farewell to their beloved leader and teacher, Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Joseph Vissarionovich Stalin.

I ask everyone to rise and observe a moment of silence for Joseph Vissarionovich Stalin. (Everyone rises) Please be seated.

Comrades Delegates!

The President of the Czechoslovak Republic, a faithful friend of the Soviet Union, our dear Klement Gottwald, passed away in Prague on March 14th. All the working people of our country mourn this great loss together with the people of Czechoslovakia. I ask everyone to rise and observe a moment of silence for Klement Gottwald. (Everyone rises) Please be seated.

Comrades Delegates!

The following is a telegram of condolence from the Supreme Soviet of the USSR to the National Assembly of Czechoslovakia on the death of President Klement Gottwald. I propose that it be adopted.

	"To the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Republic,

	Dear Comrades:

	We are deeply saddened to learn of the death of Comrade Klement Gottwald, President of the Czechoslovak Republic, an outstanding statesman and beloved leader of the Czechoslovak people. The Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics extends its deepest condolences to the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Republic.

	Together with you, we are deeply grieved by the death of Comrade Klement Gottwald, the leader of the Czechoslovak people, the tireless organizer of the new Czechoslovak People's Democratic state, and a faithful friend of the Soviet Union.

							The Supreme Soviet of the USSR"

Comrades Delegates, can we consider this telegram adopted? The telegram is adopted.

Comrades Delegates! The following agenda is submitted to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR for discussion:

	1. Election of the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR;
	2. Appointment of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR;
	3. Adoption of the list of the Council of Ministers of the USSR and reorganization of the ministries of the USSR;
	4. Election of the Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR;
	5. Amendment and supplementation of Articles 70, 77, and 78 of the Constitution of the USSR.

Does anyone have any comments on the agenda? No one wishes to speak. Let's vote. Those in favor of adopting the agenda of the Fourth Session as proposed, please raise your hands. Please lower your hands. Are there any objections? No. Are there any abstentions? No one abstains.

The agenda of the Fourth Session is adopted.

Now we will discuss the item on the election of the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

Please give the floor to Deputy Khrushchev to make a proposal. (Stormy and prolonged applause, everyone rises)

### 雅斯诺夫的发言

1953-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    雅斯诺夫的发言
    代表同志们！我宣布苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟最高苏维埃第四次会议开幕。
    代表同志们！
    苏维埃人民怀着万分沉痛的心情，怀着哀悼不可弥补的损失的情绪，向他们亲爱的领袖和导师、苏联最高苏维埃代表、苏联部长会议主席、苏联共产党中央委员会书记约瑟夫·维萨里奥诺维奇·斯大林告别。
    我请全体起立，为约瑟夫·维萨里奥诺维奇·斯大林默哀。（全体起立）请坐下。
    代表同志们！
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统、苏联忠实的朋友、我们亲爱的克利门特·哥特瓦尔德于三月十四日在布拉格逝世了。我们国家的全体劳动人民和捷克斯洛伐克各族人民一起为这个巨大的损失哀悼。我请全体起立，为克利门特·哥特瓦尔德默哀。（全体起立）请坐下。
    代表同志们！
    下面是苏联最高苏维埃致捷克斯洛伐克国民议会的哀悼克利门特·哥特瓦尔德总统逝世的唁电，特建议通过。
      “捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会
      亲爱的同志们：
      惊悉捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统、杰出的政治家和捷克斯洛伐克各族人民的亲爱领袖克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志逝世，苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟最高苏维埃向捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会致最深切的哀悼。
    我们和你们一同为捷克斯洛伐克各族人民的领袖，新捷克斯洛伐克人民民主国家的不倦的组织者，苏联忠实的朋友克利门特·哥特瓦尔德同志的逝世而感到万分沉痛。
                            苏联最高苏维埃”
    代表同志们，我们可以认为这封唁电通过了吗？唁电通过了。
    代表同志们！提交苏联最高苏维埃讨论的有下列议程：
    一、选举苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席；
    二、任命苏联部长会议主席；
    三、通过苏联部长会议名单和改组苏联各部；
    四、选举苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书；
    五、修改和补充苏联宪法第七十条、第七十七条和第七十八条。
    有没有人要发表对于议程的意见？没有人想发言。我们来表决一下。赞成按照建议通过第四次会议议程的请举手。请放下。有谁反对的？没有。有谁弃权的？没有人弃权。
    第四次会议的议程通过。
    现在我们来讨论关于选举苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席的这一项。
    请赫鲁晓夫代表发言，提出建议。（暴风雨般的、经久不息的掌声，全体起立）
