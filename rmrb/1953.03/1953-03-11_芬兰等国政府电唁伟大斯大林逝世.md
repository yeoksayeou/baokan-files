Title: Governments of Finland and Other Countries Send Condolences on the Passing of the Great Stalin
Author:
Date: 1953-03-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency Report: Following the passing of Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, J.V. Stalin, the Soviet government and the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR received condolences from many foreign governments.

    The condolence telegram from the President of the Republic of Finland, Juho Kusti Paasikivi, stated: "Mr. Chairman, please accept my deepest condolences, on behalf of the Finnish people and myself, on the passing of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Joseph Vissarionovich Stalin. The great leader and founder of a powerful Soviet state, sincerely dedicated to consolidating trust and friendship between our two countries, will remain vividly in the memory of the Finnish people."
    The condolence telegram from the Prime Minister of Finland, Kekkonen, stated: "Due to the passing of Marshal of the Soviet Union, Joseph Vissarionovich Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, the Finnish government extends its deepest condolences to the Soviet government and the Soviet people. The great leader of the Soviet people repeatedly expressed great goodwill towards Finland, and his glorious image is deeply engraved in our hearts, inspiring us to continue striving to establish and consolidate the tradition of mutual friendship between our two countries."
    The condolence telegram from the King of Denmark, Frederick, stated: "With a sorrowful heart, I extend my deepest condolences to Your Excellency on the grief felt by the Soviet people due to the passing of Marshal Stalin."
    The condolence telegram from the President of the Swiss Federal Government, Philipp Etter, stated: "On the occasion of the passing of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Marshal Joseph Stalin, I extend my condolences on behalf of the Federal Council and express the Federal Council's sympathy for the sorrow of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics."
    The Soviet government also received condolence telegrams from the governments of France, Italy, the United Kingdom, Norway, Luxembourg, Austria, Iran, Lebanon, Syria, Egypt, Abyssinia, Argentina, Guatemala, and the United States. The President of the Seventh Session of the United Nations General Assembly, Lester Pearson, also sent a telegram to the Soviet government expressing condolences.

### 芬兰等国政府电唁伟大斯大林逝世

1953-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    芬兰等国政府电唁伟大斯大林逝世
    据新华社讯：苏联部长会议主席约·维·斯大林逝世后，苏联政府和苏联最高苏维埃主席团收到许多外国政府的唁电。
    芬兰共和国总统尤·库·巴锡基维的唁电说：“主席先生，请接受我以芬兰人民以及我本人的名义对苏联部长会议主席约瑟夫·维萨里奥诺维奇·斯大林逝世的深切哀悼。真诚致力于巩固我们两国间的信任与友谊的、强大苏维埃国家的伟大的领袖与缔造者，将显明地留在芬兰人民的记忆之中。”
    芬兰内阁总理吉科宁的唁电说：“由于苏联部长会议主席约瑟夫·维萨里奥诺维奇·斯大林大元帅的逝世，芬兰政府谨向苏联政府和苏联人民表示深切的哀悼。苏联人民的伟大领袖屡次对芬兰表示极大的善意，他的光辉形象深刻地留在我们的心里，鼓舞着我们继续为建立我们两国之间的互相友好的传统而奋斗，为巩固这些传统而奋斗。”
    丹麦国王斐德烈的唁电说：“对于苏联人民因斯大林大元帅逝世而感到的悲痛，我怀着悲哀的心情谨向阁下表示深切的慰问。”
    瑞士联邦政府主席菲利普·埃特的唁电说：“当苏联部长会议主席约瑟夫·斯大林大元帅逝世之际，谨代表联邦政务委员会向您表示哀悼，联邦政务委员会并对苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟的悲痛表示同情。”
    苏联政府还收到法国、意大利、英国、挪威、卢森堡、奥地利、伊朗、黎巴嫩、叙利亚、埃及、阿比西尼亚、阿根廷、危地马拉、美国等国政府的唁电。联合国第七届大会主席莱斯特·皮尔逊也致电苏联政府表示吊唁。
