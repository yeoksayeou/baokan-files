Title: Japanese Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1953-03-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 6th: The Japanese delegation consisting of thirteen members, led by Mr. Tadatsugu Shimazu, head of the delegation composed of the Japanese Red Cross Society, the Japanese Peace Liaison Committee, and the Japan-China Friendship Association, who came to negotiate on the issue of assisting Japanese nationals returning to their country, left Beijing for home on the morning of March 6th after the successful conclusion of the talks.

Those who went to see them off included: Liao Chengzhi, chief representative of the Chinese Red Cross Society delegation; representatives Wu Yunfu, Zhao Anbo, Lin Shixiao, Ni Feijun, and Ji Feng; Wu Maosun, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Tang Mingzhao, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department; Ji Chao Ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Xie Xiaonai, Director of the Liaison Department; Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Working Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; and Fang Ming, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China, and many others.

Also present to see them off were Mr. Jiichiro Matsumoto, Vice-Chairman of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee who is visiting China, Mr. Kinkazu Saionji and Mr. Ganemon Nakamura, Japanese representatives attending the World People's Peace Congress who are currently in Beijing, and Mr. Kumaichi Yamamoto, Chairman of the Japan-China Trade Promotion Conference who is visiting China.

### 日本代表团离京返国

1953-03-08
第4版()
专栏：

    日本代表团离京返国
    【新华社六日讯】商洽协助日侨回国问题的由日本红十字会、日本和平联络委员会、日中友好协会三团体组成的日方代表团团长岛津忠承等一行十三人，在商洽协助日侨回国问题的会谈圆满结束后，已于三月六日晨离北京回国。
    前往欢送的有：中国红十字会代表团首席代表廖承志，代表伍云甫、赵安博、林士笑、倪斐君、纪锋，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副秘书长吴茂荪、联络部副部长唐明照，中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎、联络部部长谢筱迺，中华全国民主妇女联合会国际工作部副部长廖梦醒及中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明等多人。
    来中国访问的亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副主席松本治一郎，现在北京的出席世界人民和平大会的日本代表西园寺公一、中村玩右卫门和来中国访问的日中贸易促进会议主席山本熊一等人也前往欢送。
