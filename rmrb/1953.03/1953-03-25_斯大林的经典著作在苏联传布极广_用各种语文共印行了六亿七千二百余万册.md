Title: Stalin's Classic Works Widely Disseminated in the Soviet Union, Totaling 672,205,800 Copies in Various Languages
Author:
Date: 1953-03-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow dispatch from TASS: J.V. Stalin's classic works are widely disseminated in the Soviet Union, and millions of Soviet workers, collective farmers, and intellectuals are studying J.V. Stalin's classic works. According to statistics from the All-Union Book and Newspaper Agency, Stalin's works in the Soviet Union have been translated into 101 languages, published in more than 9,000 editions, totaling 672,205,800 copies. Single editions of Stalin's works have been reprinted many times. The "History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) Short Course" has been published in 301 editions. This book has become a handbook for every Soviet man and woman. "Problems of Leninism" has been translated into 55 languages and published in 260 editions. The report on the draft of the Soviet Constitution made at the Extraordinary Eighth All-Union Congress of Soviets has been translated into 89 languages and published in more than 700 editions, totaling 67 million copies. "On the Great Patriotic War of the Soviet Union" has been published in 377 editions. Stalin's works in recent years - "Marxism and Problems of Linguistics" and "Economic Problems of Socialism in the USSR" have been published in millions of copies. Stalin's historic speech at the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union has been translated into 44 languages and published in more than 60 editions, totaling 17,891,100 copies.

Stalin's brilliant works give the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet people invincible strength to fight for the cause of communism. For the communist parties, workers' parties, and millions of working people of all countries, they are powerful ideological weapons.

### 斯大林的经典著作在苏联传布极广  用各种语文共印行了六亿七千二百余万册

1953-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

    斯大林的经典著作在苏联传布极广
    用各种语文共印行了六亿七千二百余万册
    【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：约·维·斯大林的经典著作在苏联传布极广，苏联亿万工人、集体农民和知识分子都在学习约·维·斯大林的经典著作。据全苏书报社统计，斯大林的著作在苏联一共翻译成了一百零一种语文，出了九千多版，共六亿七千二百零五万八千册。斯大林著作的单行本再版了许多次。“联共（布）党史简明教程”出了三百零一版。这部书已成为每个苏联男女的手册。“列宁主义问题”译成五十五种语文，出了二百六十版。在非常第八次全苏联苏维埃代表大会上所作的论苏联宪法草案的报告译成八十九种语文，出了七百多版，共六千七百万册。“论苏联伟大卫国战争”出了三百七十七版。斯大林在最近几年的著作——“马克思主义与语言学问题”和“苏联社会主义经济问题”出版了几百万册。斯大林在苏联共产党第十九次代表大会上的历史性演说译成四十四种语文，出了六十多版，共一千七百八十九万一千册。
    斯大林的天才著作给苏联共产党和苏联人民以为共产主义事业奋斗的无敌的力量。它们对于各国的共产党、工人党和千百万劳动人民说来，都是强大的思想武器。
