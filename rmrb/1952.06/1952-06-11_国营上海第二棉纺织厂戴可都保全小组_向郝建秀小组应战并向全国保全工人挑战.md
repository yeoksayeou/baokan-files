Title: Dai Ke Do Maintenance Team of State-Owned Shanghai No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory Responds to Hao Jianxiu Team's Challenge and Challenges All National Maintenance Workers
Author:
Date: 1952-06-11
Page: 2

The Dai Ke Do maintenance team of the nationally renowned state-owned Shanghai No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory has responded to the Hao Jianxiu team’s challenge and is now challenging all national textile maintenance workers. Their response and challenge letter is published below:

To the Hao Jianxiu Team and all National Textile Maintenance Workers:

In order to consolidate the victory of the "Three-Anti" Movement, respond to the call of the National Committee of the Textile Workers' Union of China to organize the Hao Jianxiu team competition, launch a patriotic production increase and economy campaign, and strengthen the national defense and economic strength of the motherland to support the Chinese People's Volunteers, we have formulated a production increase and economy plan. The plan is to increase production of 42 strands of cotton yarn and 33.18 pieces in conjunction with the operating departments within the year (starting from May), save the value of machine materials, labor, and electricity equivalent to 94,880 catties of rice, and reduce the manufacturing cost of cotton yarn by 0.11 percent. We are willing to use this goal and the following conditions to respond to the challenge from the Hao Jianxiu team and issue a friendly challenge to the national textile maintenance workers.

(1) Guarantee that the uptime of each large spinning frame is 160 working hours, meeting the technical standards stipulated by the East China Textile Administration Bureau, and increase the cotton yarn quality score by 7.5 percent. Guarantee that the uptime of each small spinning frame is 54 working hours, meeting the technical standards of the East China Textile Administration Bureau. Guarantee the stopping time of each roving frame is 28 minutes (ten people), and ensure the inspection of the collector, cooperate with the promotion and consolidation of the Hao Jianxiu work method, and strengthen the maintenance work.

(2) Implement the machine material pre-budget and final accounting system and do a good job in original record keeping.

(3) Actively participate in the summary of fine spinning maintenance work methods, and ensure that all maintenance work methods are quickly and fully learned.

(4) Sign apprenticeship contracts, and ensure that six skilled technicians are trained by the end of the year, with a technical level reaching 75 percent.

(5) Strengthen cultural, political, and technical learning, and ensure that everyone recognizes 2,000 characters by the end of the year, attends a technical class once a week, and reads the newspaper once a day.

(6) Maintain regular work and rest schedules, do not be late or leave early, and carefully inspect during work and off-work hours to eliminate personal and machine accidents.

(7) Pay attention to hygiene, maintain good health, and achieve an attendance rate of 97 percent.

(8) Check the challenge conditions once a week and make a challenge summary every month.

State-Owned Shanghai No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory Dai Ke Do Team

### 国营上海第二棉纺织厂戴可都保全小组  向郝建秀小组应战并向全国保全工人挑战

1952-06-11
第2版()
专栏：

    国营上海第二棉纺织厂戴可都保全小组
    向郝建秀小组应战并向全国保全工人挑战
    闻名全国的国营上海第二棉纺织厂戴可都保全小组向郝建秀小组应战，并向全国纺织保全工人挑战，现在把他们的应战挑战书发表如下：郝建秀小组并全国纺织保全工人们：
    为了巩固“三反”运动的胜利，响应中国纺织工会全国委员会组织郝建秀小组竞赛的号召，开展爱国增产节约竞赛运动，加强祖国国防力量和经济力量，以支援中国人民志愿军，我们订出了增产节约计划。计划在年内（从五月份开始）配合运转部门增产四十二支棉纱三十三点一八件，节约机物料、人工、电力的价值，相当于九万四千八百八十斤大米，降低棉纱制造成本百分之零点一一。我们愿以这个目标和以下条件向郝建秀小组应战，并向全国纺织保全工人提出友谊挑战。
    （一）保证大平车每台平车时间为一六○工时，符合华东纺织管理局规定的技术标准，提高棉纱品质评分数百分之七点五。保证小平车每台平车时间为五十四工时，符合华东纺织管理局的技术标准。揩车每台停车时间为二十八分钟（十人），并保证检查集合器，配合推广和巩固郝建秀工作法，加强检修工作。
    （二）执行机物料预决算制度和做好原始记录工作。
    （三）积极参加细纱保全工作法总结工作，保证迅速全部学会保全工作法。
    （四）订立师徒合同，保证到年底培养出熟练技工六名，技术水平达到百分之七十五。
    （五）加强文化、政治、技术学习，保证到年底每人识二千字，每星期上技术课一次，每天读报一次。
    （六）按时作息，不迟到，不早退，上、下工时，认真检查，消灭人身和机器事故。
    （七）注意卫生，保持身体健康，出勤率达到百分之九十七。
    （八）每星期检查一次应战条件，每月作出应战总结。
    国营上海第二棉纺织厂戴可都小组
