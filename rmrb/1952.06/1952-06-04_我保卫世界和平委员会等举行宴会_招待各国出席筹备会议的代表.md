Title: China Peace Committee and other organizations hold banquet to entertain representatives of various countries attending the preparatory meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-06-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The China Peace Committee for the Defense of World Peace and the China Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, held a banquet at 7 p.m. on the 3rd, to entertain the representatives from various countries who came to Beijing to attend the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. Those invited to attend were more than 40 representatives from 17 countries, including Australia, Burma, Canada, Chile, India, Indonesia, Japan, North Korea, Malaya, Mexico, Mongolia, New Zealand, Pakistan, the Philippines, the United States, the Soviet Union, and Vietnam. Guryayev and John Darr, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the World Peace Council, Thornton, a member of the Asia-Australia Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions, and peace activists from Australia, India, Indonesia and Thailand who attended the preparatory meeting as observers were also invited to the meeting.

The Chinese side attending the banquet included: Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, Liu Ningyi, Li Dequan, Wu Yaozong, Nan Hanchen, Li Siguang, and more than 80 well-known figures from cultural, scientific, educational, economic, religious and other fields.

Guo Moruo delivered a speech at the meeting, extending a warm welcome to the representatives of various countries. At the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently raised their glasses to congratulate the convening of the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference and the victory of lasting peace throughout the world.

### 我保卫世界和平委员会等举行宴会  招待各国出席筹备会议的代表

1952-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我保卫世界和平委员会等举行宴会
    招待各国出席筹备会议的代表
    【新华社三日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议中国筹备委员会，在三日下午七时举行宴会，招待来北京出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的各国代表。应邀出席的有：澳大利亚、缅甸、加拿大、智利、印度、印度尼西亚、日本、朝鲜、马来亚、墨西哥、蒙古、新西兰、巴基斯坦、菲律宾、美国、苏联、越南等十七个国家的代表四十余人。世界和平理事会书记处秘书顾里亚耶夫和约翰·达尔、世界工会联合会亚澳联络局委员桑顿，以及列席筹备会议的澳大利亚、印度、印度尼西亚和泰国的和平人士也应邀到会。
    中国方面出席宴会的有：郭沫若、陈叔通、刘宁一、李德全、吴耀宗、南汉宸、李四光，以及文化、科学、教育、经济、宗教等各界著名人士八十多人。
    郭沫若在会上致辞，向各国代表表示热诚的欢迎。宴会上宾主频频举杯祝贺亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的召开，和全世界持久和平的胜利。
