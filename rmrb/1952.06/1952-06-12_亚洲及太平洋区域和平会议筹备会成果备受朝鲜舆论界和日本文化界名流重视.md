Title: Preparatory Committee Results of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference Highly Valued by Korean Opinion Circles and Renowned Figures in Japanese Cultural Circles
Author:
Date: 1952-06-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 11th: Korean opinion circles attach great importance to the significant achievements of the Preparatory Meeting of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. "Rodong Sinmun," "Minju Choson," and other newspapers here have successively published the declaration of the preparatory meeting and the proposals regarding various preparatory works for the peace conference on the first page with prominent headlines and extensive coverage on the 9th and 10th. "Rodong Sinmun" also published a photograph of the meeting venue. The Korean Central News Agency broadcast the full text of the two documents to all parts of the country on the 8th. The Korean Central Broadcasting Station broadcast the full text of the two documents six times on the 8th and 9th. "Rodong Sinmun" and "Minju Choson" also published editorials on the 9th and 10th respectively, expressing absolute support for the declaration and proposals of the preparatory meeting.
Xinhua News Agency, June 11th: TASS, Tokyo, June 9th: "World Peace" published statements by many famous figures in Japan on the Preparatory Meeting of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. For example, Professor Ko-Sada of Hiroshima University believes that holding this conference is of extremely great significance. Suekawa, President of Ritsumeikan University, pointed out that it is necessary to mobilize the broad masses of people to actively support the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference as much as possible.

### 亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会成果备受朝鲜舆论界和日本文化界名流重视

1952-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会成果备受朝鲜舆论界和日本文化界名流重视
    【新华社平壤十一日电】朝鲜舆论界十分重视亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的重大成果。此间“劳动新闻”、“民主朝鲜”及其他各报都先后在九、十两日以一版头条地位和大部篇幅全文刊载筹备会议的宣言和关于和平会议各项筹备工作的提议。“劳动新闻”并刊登了会场的照片。朝中社在八日向全国各地广播了两个文件的全文。朝鲜中央广播电台在八、九两日先后播送这两个文件的全文达六次。“劳动新闻”和“民主朝鲜”并分别在九、十两日发表社论，表示绝对支持筹备会议的宣言和提议。
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社东京九日讯：“世界和平”刊载了日本许多名流就亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议所发表的声明。例如：广岛的大学教授小贞认为举行这次会议有极端巨大的重要意义。立命馆大学校长末川指出：必须尽可能发动广大人民积极支持亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。
