Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Executive Bureau Meeting Opens in Vienna
Author:
Date: 1952-06-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna: The Executive Bureau meeting of the World Federation of Trade Unions opened on the 9th in Vienna, the Austrian capital, according to TASS. In addition to the members of the Executive Bureau, representatives of trade union organizations from France, Italy, the United Kingdom, the Soviet Union, China, Germany, Austria, Algeria, Argentina, Mexico, the Netherlands, Hungary, Poland, Romania, Greece, Finland, Cuba, and other countries attended the meeting. The meeting adopted the following agenda: 1. The struggle of working people to defend trade union rights; 2. The situation of trade unions in North Africa; 3. The activities of national trade unions constituting the industrial departments of the World Federation of Trade Unions; 4. Preparations for convening an international conference on social protection and insurance.
　　World Federation of Trade Unions General Secretary Saillant has already made a report on the first item on the agenda at the meeting. The meeting will continue until the 15th.

### 世界工联执行局会议在维也纳开幕

1952-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    世界工联执行局会议在维也纳开幕
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社维也纳讯：世界工会联合会执行局会议于九日在奥地利首都维也纳开幕。出席会议的除执行局委员外，还有法国、意大利、英国、苏联、中国、德国、奥地利、阿尔及利亚、阿根廷、墨西哥、荷兰、匈牙利、波兰、罗马尼亚、希腊、芬兰、古巴及其他国家的工会组织的代表。会议通过议程如下：一、劳动人民保卫工会权利的斗争；二、北非工会的情况；三、构成世界工会联合会产业部的各国工会的活动；四、筹备召开国际社会保护与保险会议。
    世界工会联合会总书记赛扬已在会上就第一项议程作了报告。会议将继续举行到十五日结束。
