Title: Chemical Workers' Union International Calls on Affiliated Workers to Oppose U.S. Bacteriological Warfare
Author:
Date: 1952-06-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 16th: The Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Chemical Workers' Union has received a letter from the Chemical Industries Workers' Union International, dated May 22nd, addressed to affiliated trade union organizations and workers in the chemical and related industries, calling for the organization of an international protest demonstration movement against the U.S. bacteriological warfare. The letter states: The aggressors in Korea, under the guise of the United Nations flag, continue to use bacteriological weapons to attack the Korean people and Chinese territory.

The letter points out: Imperialism uses the labor of chemical workers and workers in related industries, as well as the labor of laboratory workers and scholars, not for peace, but to achieve war. The use of bacteriological weapons is a serious threat to every worker today.

The Union International calls on all trade union member organizations:

	Organize protest movements on June 25th, the second anniversary of the U.S. launch of the war of aggression against Korea, to oppose the use of bacteriological weapons!
	Organize demonstrations, hold mass rallies, or convene meetings!
	Make June 25th a day of active struggle against the use of weapons of mass human destruction!
	Demand that the use of weapons of mass destruction be prohibited by international law!
	Demand an immediate end to the use of bacteriological weapons!

### 化学工人工会国际号召所属工人  反对美国进行细菌战

1952-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    化学工人工会国际号召所属工人
    反对美国进行细菌战
    【新华社十六日讯】中国化学工会筹备委员会收到化学工业工人工会国际五月二十二日发表的告所属工会组织和化学及其有关工业工人书，号召为反对美国进行细菌战组织国际抗议示威运动。号召书称：在朝鲜的侵略者在联合国旗帜掩护下，继续使用细菌武器进攻朝鲜人民和中国领土。
    号召书指出：帝国主义利用化学工人及其有关工业工人的劳动和实验室工人、学者的劳动，其目的不是为了和平，而是为了要达到战争。使用细菌武器，在今天来说，是对每一个工人的一种严重的威胁。
    工会国际号召全体工会会员团体：
    在六月二十五日，即美国发动侵朝战争二周年纪念日，组织抗议运动，反对使用细菌武器！
    组织示威游行，举行群众大会，或召集会议！
    把六月二十五日的那天作为积极反对使用大量毁灭人类武器的斗争日！
    要求应该用国际法禁止使用大量破坏性的武器！
    要求立刻停止使用细菌武器！
