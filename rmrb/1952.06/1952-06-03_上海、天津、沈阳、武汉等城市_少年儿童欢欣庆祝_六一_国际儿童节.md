Title: Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang, Wuhan and other cities: Children joyfully celebrate International Children's Day

Author:

Date: 1952-06-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report: Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chongqing, Xi'an and other cities celebrated International Children's Day with immense joy.

Many cities held grand celebrations and garden parties in various forms. On the eve of "June 1st," Shanghai and Tianjin both held radio rallies. At the Shanghai celebration radio rally, Shanghai Mayor Chen Yi encouraged children to strengthen their bodies, study science, and love their country and Chairman Mao. More than 100,000 children were organized to listen. At the Tianjin radio rally, Zhang Yunying, China's representative to the International Conference for the Defense of Children, spoke, conveying the significance of the International Conference for the Defense of Children. On the morning of the 1st, more than 1,700 people, including children from kindergartens, nurseries, and childcare centers in Shenyang, as well as childcare, health, medical workers, female cadres from various institutions, and street women, gathered to celebrate Children's Day. The children performed song and dance dramas such as "Extinguish Germ Warfare" and "Unity is Strength." To satisfy the children's respect for heroes, model figures, literary writers, and scientists, various cities invited combat heroes and model workers to meet with the children. The Shanghai bonfire party invited government leaders, representatives of the Chinese Communist Party, combat heroes, model workers, scientists, artists, and others to meet with the children. More than 300 children representatives in Shenyang held a gathering, inviting leaders, model workers, combat heroes, writers, artists, and others to participate. Children from the Shenyang Nursery affectionately visited volunteer convalescents and performed wonderful programs for the volunteer uncles. At the Wuhan celebration, Wuhan Deputy Mayors Wang Renzhong and Chen Jingshu, combat hero Guo Junqing, model worker Zhu Jiu, and others spoke to the children; the Deputy Archdeacon of Canterbury, England, Johnson, and his wife also participated in the celebration. At the Chongqing celebration, Southwest Military Region combat hero Liu Zilin and Southwest China's famous textile model worker Chen Shulan reported their model deeds in defending the motherland and actively participating in production and construction. Children in Guangzhou were also introduced to the People's Liberation Army land, air, and navy combat heroes and industrial and agricultural model workers they respected. Nanjing People's Broadcasting Station also invited heroes and model figures to give radio speeches to the children. Children in Xi'an and combat heroes Liu Jiyao and Li Erxiao, model workers Zhao Zhankui, and representatives of Chinese People's Volunteer Army convalescents had a garden party at Xi'an Revolutionary Park. Shanghai's young pioneers also held joint celebrations with Soviet young pioneers and Korean children residing in Shanghai. Chinese and Soviet children in Shenyang also held a party on the eve of the festival. Soviet, Korean, Indian, and Japanese children in Hankou also participated in the celebration.

During various celebrations, children in various cities showed that they were always ready to: exercise, study hard, and become builders and defenders of the motherland in the future. In Tianjin, more than 6,000 children held a glider model airplane performance meeting. Before "June 1st," children in various schools in Xi'an visited factories, made glider models, slides, and crystal radios. Many children in various cities have been approved to join the Chinese Young Pioneers and the Chinese New Democratic Youth League due to their hard work and excellent grades. In Kangding, 52 Tibetan, Han, and Hui children joined the Chinese Young Pioneers at the celebration. Many children in various schools in Xi'an held joining ceremonies. In Lanzhou, 34 outstanding young pioneers joined the New Democratic Youth League. Many children wrote letters to Chairman Mao and the People's Volunteers, reporting on their happy lives and promising to strengthen their bodies, learn their skills, and strive to prepare to participate in the construction of the motherland.

In response to the resolutions of the International Conference for the Defense of Children, literary and art workers, children's book publishing workers, children's education workers, and children who love literature and art in Shanghai held a forum. Everyone focused on discussing how to create a large number of children's literary and artistic works to enrich children's cultural life. Shanghai originally had six children's parks, and the People's Government opened two newly built children's parks on the 1st as a gift for International Children's Day.

### 上海、天津、沈阳、武汉等城市  少年儿童欢欣庆祝“六一”国际儿童节

1952-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    上海、天津、沈阳、武汉等城市
    少年儿童欢欣庆祝“六一”国际儿童节
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：上海、天津、沈阳、武汉、广州、重庆、西安等城市的少年儿童以无限愉快的心情欢度“六一”国际儿童节。
    许多城市举行了各种形式的盛大庆祝会和游园联欢会。“六一”前夕，上海和天津都举行了广播大会。在上海举行的庆祝“六一”广播大会上，上海市市长陈毅勉励儿童炼好身体，学习科学，热爱祖国和毛主席。组织收听的少年儿童有十万多人。在天津的广播大会上，我国出席国际保卫儿童大会代表张云莹等讲话，传达国际保卫儿童大会的意义。一日上午，沈阳市各保育院、幼儿园和托儿所的儿童，以及保育、卫生、医务工作者，各机关女干部和街道妇女等一千七百多人，集会庆祝儿童节。儿童们在会上表演了“扑灭细菌战”、“大家团结力量大”等歌舞剧。各城市为了满足少年儿童对英雄、模范人物、文艺作家和科学家等的敬爱，都分别约请战斗英雄和劳动模范等与少年儿童见面。上海举行的营火会，邀请了人民政府首长、中国共产党的代表、战斗英雄、劳动模范、科学家、艺术家等与少年儿童见面。沈阳市三百多个儿童代表举行集会，邀请了首长、劳动模范、战斗英雄、作家、艺术家等参加。沈阳市保育院儿童并亲切慰问了志愿军休养员，给志愿军叔叔们表演了精彩的节目。武汉庆祝会上，武汉市副市长王任重、陈经畬和战斗英雄郭俊卿、劳动模范朱玖等都和少年儿童们讲了话；英国坎特伯雷副主教约翰逊及其夫人等也参加了庆祝会。在重庆的庆祝大会上，西南军区战斗英雄刘子林、西南区著名纺织劳动模范陈树兰报告了他们在捍卫祖国和积极参加生产建设的模范事迹。广州的少年儿童们也被介绍见到了他们所敬爱的人民解放军陆、空、海军战斗英雄与工农业劳动模范。南京人民广播电台并约请了英雄、模范向少年儿童作广播讲演。西安的少年儿童们和战斗英雄刘继尧、李二小，劳动模范赵占魁、中国人民志愿军休养员的代表等在西安革命公园游园联欢。上海的少年儿童队员们还和侨居上海的苏联少年先锋队员和朝鲜少年儿童进行联欢。沈阳市中苏儿童也在节日前夕举行了联欢会。在汉口的苏联、朝鲜、印度和日本的儿童也参加了庆祝会。
    各城市的少年儿童在各种庆祝活动中，表现他们时刻准备着：锻炼身体，努力学习，将来成为祖国的建设者和保卫者。天津有六千多少年儿童举行了滑翔模型飞机表演大会。西安各校少年儿童在“六一”以前就分别参观工厂、制作滑翔模型、幻灯和矿石收音机等。许多城市的少年儿童由于学习努力，成绩优异，被批准加入中国少年儿童队和中国新民主主义青年团。康定有五十二名藏族、汉族和回族的儿童在庆祝会上加入中国少年儿童队。西安各校许多少年儿童举行了入队仪式。兰州有三十四名优秀的少年儿童队员加入了新民主主义青年团。许多少年儿童们写信给毛主席和人民志愿军，报告他们所过的幸福生活，并保证炼好身体，学好本领，为准备参加祖国建设而努力。
    为了响应国际保卫儿童大会的决议，上海的文学艺术工作者、儿童读物出版工作者、儿童教育工作者及爱好文艺的儿童举行了座谈会。大家着重讨论了如何大量创作儿童文艺作品，以充实儿童文化生活等问题。上海市原已有六个儿童公园，人民政府又在一日开放了两个新辟的儿童公园，作为对国际儿童节的贺礼。
