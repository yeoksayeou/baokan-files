Title: National Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Representative Conference Scheduled to be Held in Beijing in Mid-June
Author:
Date: 1952-06-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd—The preparatory representative conference of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce is scheduled to be held in Beijing in mid-June.

    The preparatory office of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce has been preparing for the preparatory representative conference of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce since November of last year. The preparations are now complete, and with the consent of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, it has been decided to convene the preparatory representative conference in Beijing in mid-June. Representatives from various regions should register in Beijing before June 15th.

### 全国工商联筹备代表会议定于本月中旬在北京举行

1952-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

    全国工商联筹备代表会议定于本月中旬在北京举行
    【新华社三日讯】全国工商业联合会筹备代表会议定于六月中旬在京召开。
    中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会筹备处自去年十一月即开始进行筹备全国工商业联合会筹备代表会议的工作，现已筹备就绪，经中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会同意，决定于六月中旬在京召开筹备代表会议。各地代表限于六月十五日前到京报到。
