Title: The entire line of the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway track-laying project was successfully completed yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-06-14
Page: 1
　
    The entire line of the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway track-laying project was successfully completed yesterday
    [This newspaper's Chongqing dispatch on the 13th] At 3:21 PM on the 13th, the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway tracks had been laid to the Chengdu Station terminus. The Chengdu-Chongqing Railway is 505 kilometers long and construction began on June 15, 1950. By the end of April this year, the roadbed earthwork, bridges, tunnels, and other projects of the entire line were basically completed, and the project quality met the standards stipulated by the Ministry of Railways. When the rails were laid to Chengdu, Locomotive No. 45 simultaneously arrived, pulling the engineering train, and tens of thousands of Chengdu citizens cheered along the route.

### 成渝铁路全线铺轨工程昨天胜利完成

1952-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

    成渝铁路全线铺轨工程昨天胜利完成
    【本报重庆十三日专电】十三日下午三点二十一分，成渝铁路铁轨已铺至成都车站终点。成渝铁路全线长五百零五公里，一九五零年六月十五日开始动工，今年四月底全线路基土石方、桥梁、隧道等工程基本完成，工程质量都符合铁道部所规定的标准。铁轨铺至成都时，第四五号机车牵引工程列车同时到达，成都市民数万人夹道欢呼。
