Title: National Peace Organizations of Korea and Vietnam Unfold Preparatory Work for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-06-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 20th – The Korean National Committee for the Support of Peace and the Korean Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference held a joint meeting in Pyongyang on the 19th. The meeting discussed the issue of further unfolding the preparatory work for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. Park Jung-ae, the chief representative of the Korean delegation who attended the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference in Beijing, reported on the process of participating in the preparatory meeting. Attendees unanimously supported the declaration of the preparatory meeting. Korean prominent peace fighters and social figures Han Seol-ya, Lee Tae-jun, Lee Keu-roo, Kang Ryang-wook, Yoon Hang-jung, Lee Myeon-sang, Lee Hwan-ki, Ki Bu-bok, and Hyun Chil-jong all signed the declaration at the meeting. The joint meeting also nominated Tian Donghe, a member of the Korean National Committee for the Support of Peace, as the Korean representative to the Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. In order to further promote the movement to defend peace in Korea, the meeting decided to convene a national congress for the defense of peace in Korea at an appropriate time, and to widely carry out publicity work throughout the country, publicizing the great significance of convening the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference.
    Xinhua News Agency, 21st – According to Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam: The National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front and the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace issued a joint statement on the 7th, calling on the Vietnamese people to support the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. After exposing the conspiracies and war preparations of the United States and other imperialists in the Asian and Pacific regions, the statement elaborated in detail the purpose of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference scheduled to be held in Beijing in September this year. The statement then said: The Vietnamese people welcome the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference without reservation. The National Committee of the Vietnam United Front and the Vietnam Peace Committee have established a preparatory committee to further mobilize the people of the whole country to support this conference, and have selected representatives to attend the conference. It is firmly believed that the people of Vietnam will actively work with the people of various countries in Asia and the Pacific region to promote the success of this conference. The statement concluded: We believe that with the great strength of the Chinese people headed by Chairman Mao Zedong, and with the unity of the people of Asian and Pacific countries, the forces of peace in Asia and the Pacific and the forces of peace in other parts of the world will surely be able to shatter the conspiracy of US imperialism to wage another world war. We firmly believe that with the determination of the Vietnamese people to carry out long-term resistance under the leadership of President Ho Chi Minh, and with the close unity of the people of Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia, we will surely be able to eliminate the French colonial aggressors and defeat the American interventionists, restore the complete independence of the three nations, and contribute to the cause of defending world peace.

### 朝鲜及越南的拥护和平的全国性组织  展开亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备工作

1952-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜及越南的拥护和平的全国性组织
    展开亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备工作
    【新华社平壤二十日电】朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会与亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议朝鲜筹备委员会，十九日在平壤举行联席会议。会上讨论了进一步展开亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备工作的问题。曾赴北京出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的朝鲜代表团首席代表朴正爱，在会上报告了参加筹备会议的经过。到会者一致拥护筹备会议的宣言。会上，朝鲜著名和平战士和社会名流韩雪野、李泰俊、李克鲁、康良煜、尹行重、李冕相、李焕起、奇不福、玄七钟等都在宣言上签了名。联席会议并推定朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会委员田东赫为亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会的朝鲜代表。为了进一步在朝鲜开展保卫和平运动，会议决定在适当时机召开朝鲜全国保卫和平大会，并在全国范围内广泛进行宣传工作，宣传召开亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的重大意义。
    【新华社二十一日讯】据越南通讯社北越讯：越南国民联合战线全国委员会和越南保卫世界和平委员会七日发表联合声明，号召越南人民拥护亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。声明在揭露了美国和其他帝国主义在亚洲和太平洋区域制造战争的阴谋和进行战争准备的情形后，又详细阐述了定于今年九月在北京举行的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的目的。声明接着说：越南人民毫无保留地欢迎亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。越联战线全国委员会和越南和平委员会已经成立了筹备委员会，以进一步动员全国人民支持这个会议，并且选派了出席会议的代表。深信越南全国人民将积极地和亚洲及太平洋区域各国人民在一起，努力促成这次会议的胜利。声明结语说：我们认为：有着以毛泽东主席为首的中国人民的伟大力量，又有亚洲与太平洋各国人民的团结一致，亚洲与太平洋的和平力量和世界其他部分的和平力量一定能够粉碎美帝国主义进行另一次世界大战的阴谋。我们深信：有着越南人民在胡志明主席领导下进行长期抵抗的决心，又有越南、寮国与高棉人民的亲密团结，我们一定可以消灭法国殖民侵略者并击败美国干涉者，恢复三个民族的完全独立地位，对保卫世界和平的事业有所贡献。
