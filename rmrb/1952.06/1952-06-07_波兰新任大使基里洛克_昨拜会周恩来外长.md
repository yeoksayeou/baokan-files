Title: Polish New Ambassador Kiriloek Visits Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-06-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 6th—The newly appointed Polish Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Kiriloek, accompanied by First Secretary Daoding, visited Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs on the 6th to discuss matters related to presenting his credentials. Also present were Xu Yixin, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 波兰新任大使基里洛克  昨拜会周恩来外长

1952-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰新任大使基里洛克
    昨拜会周恩来外长
    【新华社六日讯】新任波兰驻华特命全权大使基里洛克偕一等秘书道定，于六日赴外交部拜会周恩来部长，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座者尚有外交部苏联东欧司副司长徐以新和交际处处长王倬如。
