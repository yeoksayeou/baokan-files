    Our Great Motherland
    On the south bank of the Yellow River levee near Xiaojiezi Village in Lijin County, Shandong Province, a spring flood overflow weir has recently been built. It can irrigate 400,000 mu of farmland and also facilitate navigation. Now that the overflow weir has released water, these sailboats can pass through without obstruction.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在山东利津县小街子村附近黄河大堤的南岸，最近修建了凌汛溢水堰。它可以灌溉四十万亩农田，还便利了航运。现在溢水堰已经放水，这些帆船可以畅通无阻了。
