Title: Southwest Nationalities Visiting Group Tibetan Representatives Return to Kangding Successively
Author: 
Date: 1952-06-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Ya'an, 17th. More than 40 Tibetan representatives from the Southwest Nationalities Visiting Group belonging to the Tibetan Autonomous Region of Xikang Province and the Qamdo area have successively returned to Kangding County in the Tibetan Autonomous Region, where they were warmly welcomed by people of all ethnic groups and all walks of life. The Autonomous Region People's Government invited the representatives to hold forums on the 8th and 14th respectively. Awang Jigme, Vice Chairman of the Autonomous Region People's Government, introduced the work of the Autonomous Region over the past year to the representatives. The Qamdo Tibetan visiting team will pass through Ganzi on the 15th to return to Qamdo. The representatives of the Autonomous Region have made reports to all lamas and residents in the Kangding urban area and will go to various counties to conduct extensive publicity.

### 西南各民族晋京参观团藏族代表先后返抵康定

1952-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

    西南各民族晋京参观团藏族代表先后返抵康定
    【新华社雅安十七日电】西南各民族晋京参观团所属西康省藏族自治区代表和昌都地区藏族代表四十多人，已先后返抵藏族自治区康定县，受到各族各界人民的热烈欢迎。自治区人民政府在八日和十四日分别邀请代表们举行座谈会。自治区人民政府副主席阿旺嘉错向代表们介绍了最近一年来自治区的工作情况。昌都地区藏族参观队将于十五日取道甘孜返昌都。自治区的代表已向康定城区的全体喇嘛、居民作传达报告，并将分赴各县进行广泛的宣传。
