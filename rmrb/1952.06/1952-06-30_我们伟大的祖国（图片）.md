Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-06-30
Page: 1

    The Chengyu Railway is scheduled to open to traffic on July 1st. The track-laying project for the entire 505-kilometer line was completed at the Chengdu Station terminus on the afternoon of June 13th. This is the first locomotive arriving in Chengdu from Chongqing. Tens of thousands of citizens cheered and rejoiced that their 40-year-long wish could be realized.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    成渝铁路定于七月一日通车。全线五百零五公里的铺轨工程，已于六月十三日下午铺至成都车站终点。这是由重庆抵达成都的第一辆机车，市民数万人都为四十多年的愿望能够实现而夹道歉欢呼。
