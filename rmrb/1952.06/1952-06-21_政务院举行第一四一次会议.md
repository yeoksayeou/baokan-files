Title: Government Administration Council Holds 141st Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1952-06-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 20th: The 141st meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of June 20th. After hearing the explanation made by Zhang Bojun, Minister of the Ministry of Communications of the Central People's Government, the meeting approved the Ministry's work summary for 1951 and the main points of the work plan for 1952. The meeting also approved several appointment and removal cases and submitted several appointment and removal cases for approval by the Central People's Government Committee.

### 政务院举行第一四一次会议

1952-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一四一次会议
    【新华社二十日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百四十一次政务会议在六月二十日下午举行。会议在听取了中央人民政府交通部章伯钧部长所作的说明后，批准了该部一九五一年工作总结及一九五二年工作计划要点。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
