    Our Great Motherland
    Eid al-Fitr is an important festival for Muslims. On June 25th, more than 70,000 Muslims in Beijing held Eid al-Fitr ceremonies simultaneously in 49 mosques and adopted the "Declaration of All Muslims in Beijing Against American Imperialism's Germ Warfare and Atrocities Against POWs in Korea and China." This is the Eid al-Fitr ceremony at the Dongsi Pailou Mosque in Beijing, where Imam Yang Youyi is reciting the "Khutbah" (preaching in Arabic).

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    开斋节是伊斯兰教徒的重要节日。六月二十五日北京市七万多回民在四十九个清真寺同时举行开斋节仪式，并通过了“北京市全体回民反对美帝国主义进行细菌战和对朝中战俘暴行的宣言”。这是北京东四牌楼清真寺开斋节的仪式，教长杨友漪阿訇正在念“呼土白”（用阿剌伯语说教）。
