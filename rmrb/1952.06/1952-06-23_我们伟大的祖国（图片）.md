    Our Great Motherland
    Nineteen-year-old Communist Party member Cui Yuxia is a well-known young woman labor model in the Subei area. The mutual aid team she leads has an average yield this year that is 50% higher than that of local farmers. This is Cui Yuxia's mutual aid team. They are reading and discussing the letter from the whole team to Chairman Mao reporting the bumper harvest.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    十九岁的共产堂员崔玉霞，是苏北区著名的青年妇女劳动模范。她领导的互助组，今年平均产量比当地农民高出五成。这是崔玉霞互助组的组员们，他们正在宣读和讨论全组向毛主席报告丰收的信件。
