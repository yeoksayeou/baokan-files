Title: Ho Chi Minh and Huang Mingjian Send Telegrams to Kim Il-sung and Pak Hon-yong to Congratulate the Korean People on Their Victory in the Two-Year Anti-Imperialist Struggle
Author:
Date: 1952-06-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th: According to the Vietnam News Agency from North Vietnam: On the occasion of the second anniversary of the Korean people's war of resistance against America, Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, sent a telegram to Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, on the 24th. The text of the telegram is as follows:

"On the occasion of the second anniversary of Korea's armed resistance, on behalf of the government, army, and people of Vietnam, and myself, I am honored to extend my warm and friendly respects to you and the Korean army and people.

In the past two years, thanks to the wise leadership of you, the Korean Workers' Party, and the government, the Korean army and people, with the assistance of the Chinese People's Volunteers, have achieved many brilliant victories over the imperialists.

Korea's victory is Vietnam's victory, and it is also the victory of the world peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union.

We believe that the sustained and arduous resistance of the people of our two countries will surely be victorious.

I wish you good health!"

On the same day, Huang Mingjian, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, sent a telegram to Pak Hon-yong, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, stating:

"On the occasion of the second anniversary of Korea's armed resistance, please convey our wishes to the government and people of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and wish the struggle to defend Korea and world peace a great victory."

### 胡志明和黄明鉴分别致电金日成和朴宪永  祝贺朝鲜人民两年来反帝斗争的胜利

1952-06-28
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明和黄明鉴分别致电金日成和朴宪永
    祝贺朝鲜人民两年来反帝斗争的胜利
    【新华社二十五日讯】据越南通讯社北越讯：值此朝鲜人民抗美战争两周年之际，越南民主共和国主席胡志明于二十四日致电朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成，电文如下：
    “值此朝鲜武装抗战二周年，我谨代表越南政府、军队、人民和我自己，很荣幸地向你及朝鲜军队和人民致以热烈的、友爱的敬意。
    过去两年中，由于你以及朝鲜劳动党和政府的英明领导，朝鲜军队及人民在中国人民志愿军的援助之下，对帝国主义者取得了许多辉煌的胜利。
    朝鲜的胜利就是越南的胜利，也是以苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营的胜利。
    我们相信我们两国人民的持久和艰苦的抗战一定会获得胜利。
    祝你身体健康！”
    同日，越南民主共和国政府外交部部长黄明鉴致电朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相朴宪永称：
    “值此朝鲜武装抗战二周年，请你向朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和朝鲜人民转达我们的愿望，祝保卫朝鲜与世界和平的斗争取得伟大的胜利。”
