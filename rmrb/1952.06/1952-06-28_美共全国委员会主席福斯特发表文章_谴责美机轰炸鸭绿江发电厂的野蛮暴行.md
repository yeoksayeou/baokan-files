Title: US Communist Party Chairman Foster Publishes Article Condemning the Barbaric Atrocity of US Planes Bombing the Yalu River Power Plant
Author:
Date: 1952-06-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New York, 27th: TASS reported from New York on the 27th: US Communist Party National Committee Chairman Foster published an article in the "Daily Worker" saying that the US aircraft's bombing of the Yalu River power plant on June 23 was "a shocking example of military barbarism and irresponsibility." This vicious attack was carried out under the flimsy pretext that the Panmunjom armistice negotiations were at a standstill.

Foster said that peace-loving people want to ask whether such air raids are necessary just because the armistice negotiations are at a standstill? Foster pointed out that it is obvious that the Pentagon and the Truman government do not want an armistice in Korea. This is evident not only in the recent large-scale air raids, but also in the continuous air raids during the armistice negotiations.

Foster said: Wall Street's warmongers need the Korean War because they fear that the end of the war will lead to the collapse of Wall Street's current arms expansion movement to arm the United States and the capitalist world, which can bring huge profits to arms manufacturers. "They are worried that if the Korean War is resolved, the masses' demand for a general agreement with the Soviet Union will be irresistible, which will shatter the American big business's determination to dominate the world. The large-scale air offensive against North Korea and People's China is based on this terrible consideration."

Foster concluded: "...The working class and other peace-loving forces need to wake up immediately, face this serious danger, and defeat those who create this danger—the Wall Street big business monopoly capitalists, who hope to become the masters of the world through another world war. The first step for the American people is to demand an immediate armistice agreement in Korea."

### 美共全国委员会主席福斯特发表文章  谴责美机轰炸鸭绿江发电厂的野蛮暴行

1952-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    美共全国委员会主席福斯特发表文章
    谴责美机轰炸鸭绿江发电厂的野蛮暴行
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社纽约二十七日电：美国共产党全国委员会主席福斯特在“工人日报”发表文章说：美国飞机六月二十三日对鸭绿江发电厂的轰炸，是“军事上的野蛮和不负责任的惊人事例”。这种凶恶的袭击乃是以板门店停战谈判陷于僵局这种微不足道的藉口来进行的。
    福斯特说，爱好和平的人民要问，停战谈判陷于僵局，是不是就必须进行这样的空袭呢？福斯特指出，很明显，五角大楼和杜鲁门政府是不想在朝鲜停战的。这不仅表现在最近的大规模的空袭上，而且也表现在停战谈判期间不断进行的空袭上。
    福斯特说：华尔街的战争贩子需要朝鲜战争，因为他们害怕战争结束会造成华尔街目前武装美国和资本主义世界的扩张军备运动的崩溃，这种扩张军备可使军火制造商获得巨额利润。“他们担心，如果朝鲜战争获得解决，群众关于与苏联获致总的协议的要求，就会是不可抗拒的了，这样就会使美国大企业统治世界的决心成为泡影。对北朝鲜和人民中国之所以进行大规模空军攻势就是出于这种可怕的考虑。”
    福斯特最后说：“……工人阶级和其他爱好和平的力量需要马上警醒过来，正视这个严重的危险，并击败造成这种危险的人——华尔街大企业垄断资本家，这批人希望通过另一次世界大战成为世界的主人。美国人民的第一个步骤便是要求立刻在朝鲜缔结停战协议。”
