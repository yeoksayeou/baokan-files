Title: Protesting the Fascistization of Japan, Demanding the Withdrawal of American Troops: Japanese Workers and Students Determine Dates for General Strike and General Class Boycott
Author:
Date: 1952-06-04
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, June 3rd: According to TASS, Tokyo: On May 31st, the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan and the Struggle Committee Against Revising Deteriorated Labor Laws held a joint meeting, deciding to hold a third national general strike on June 7th and 17th to protest the fascistization of Japan.
    The All-Japan Federation of Student Self-Government Associations decided on May 31st to hold a student general class boycott on June 10th, with the slogans:
    "Oppose the reactionary bill 'Subversive Activities Prevention Law,' Overthrow the anti-people Shigeru Yoshida government, Occupying forces immediately withdraw from Japan!"

### 抗议日本法西斯化，要求美军撤退  日本工人学生决定总罢工总罢课日期

1952-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    抗议日本法西斯化，要求美军撤退
    日本工人学生决定总罢工总罢课日期
    【新华社三日讯】据塔斯社东京讯：五月三十一日，日本工会总评议会和反对改劣劳工法规斗争委员会举行联席会议，决定在六月七日和十七日举行第三次全国性的总罢工，抗议日本的法西斯化。
    全日本学生自治会联合会于五月三十一日决定在六月十日举行一次学生总罢课，他们的口号是：
    “反对反动法案‘防止破坏活动法案’，打倒反人民的吉田茂政府，占领军立即撤离日本！”
