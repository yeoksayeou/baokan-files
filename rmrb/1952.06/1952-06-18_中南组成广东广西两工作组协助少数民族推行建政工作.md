Title: Central South Forms Guangdong and Guangxi Work Groups to Assist Minority Nationalities in Promoting Political Construction
Author:
Date: 1952-06-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 17th. The Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central South Military and Administrative Committee, along with cadres drawn from the Ministry of Civil Affairs, the Ministry of Culture, the Ministry of Education, the Ministry of Health, the Ministry of Trade, the Ministry of Agriculture and Forestry, and the Counselor's Office, totaling twenty-eight people, have formed Guangdong and Guangxi work groups. On the 16th of this month, they departed for minority nationality areas in these two provinces to assist in promoting democratic political construction for nationalities.

### 中南组成广东广西两工作组协助少数民族推行建政工作

1952-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中南组成广东广西两工作组协助少数民族推行建政工作
    【新华社汉口十七日电】中南军政委员会民族事务委员会和民政部、文化部、教育部、卫生部、贸易部、农林部、参事室等部门抽调干部二十八人分别组成广东、广西工作组，在本月十六日分赴该两省少数民族地区，协助推行民族民主建政工作。
