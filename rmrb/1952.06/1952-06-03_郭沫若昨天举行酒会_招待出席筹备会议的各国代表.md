Title: Guo Moruo Holds Reception for Delegates Attending Preparatory Meeting

Author:
Date: 1952-06-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, held a reception on the afternoon of June 2nd to welcome the delegates from various countries attending the Preparatory Meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference.  Over forty delegates from eighteen countries were invited, including Australia, Burma, Canada, Ceylon, Chile, India, Indonesia, Japan, North Korea, Malaya, Mexico, Mongolia, New Zealand, Pakistan, the Philippines, the United States, the Soviet Union, and Vietnam. John Darr and Guryayev, Secretaries of the World Peace Council, and Thornton, a member of the World Federation of Trade Unions Asian-Australian Liaison Bureau, were also invited.  Over forty prominent figures from Chinese cultural, scientific, educational, economic, and religious circles attended as hosts.

Chairman Guo Moruo raised his glass to warmly welcome the delegates from various countries. The delegates repeatedly toasted to lasting peace in the Asian and Pacific regions and the world.  The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 郭沫若昨天举行酒会  招待出席筹备会议的各国代表

1952-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若昨天举行酒会
    招待出席筹备会议的各国代表
    【新华社二日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若于二日下午举行酒会，招待出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的各国代表。应邀出席的有澳大利亚，缅甸，加拿大，锡兰，智利，印度，印度尼西亚，日本，朝鲜，马来亚，墨西哥，蒙古，新西兰，巴基斯坦，菲律宾，美国，苏联，越南等十八个国家的代表四十余人。世界和平理事会秘书约翰·达尔和顾里亚耶夫，世界工联亚澳联络局委员桑顿也应邀出席。出席作陪的还有中国文化、科学、教育、经济、宗教等各界著名人士四十余人。
    郭沫若主席举杯向各国代表表示热诚欢迎。代表们频频为亚洲、太平洋区与世界的持久和平而干杯。酒会在友好空气中进行。
