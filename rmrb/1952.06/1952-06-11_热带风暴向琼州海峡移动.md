Title: Tropical Storm Moving Towards Qiongzhou Strait
Author:
Date: 1952-06-11
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, 10th. According to a storm warning issued by the Central Meteorological Observatory at 7:00 PM on the 10th: At 2:00 PM on the 10th, a tropical storm appeared 1,000 *li* south of the Dongsha Islands (Note: A tropical storm is a type of tropical low pressure that can bring strong winds, but is not as strong as a typhoon). The maximum wind speed at the center is 18 meters per second. It is moving in a west-northwest direction and may reach the vicinity of the Qiongzhou Strait in 48 hours. The Leizhou Peninsula and Hainan Island areas will be affected.

### 热带风暴向琼州海峡移动

1952-06-11
第1版()
专栏：

    热带风暴向琼州海峡移动
    【新华社十日讯】据中央气象台十日下午七时发出的风暴警报称：十日下午二时，在东沙群岛正南一千里处，出现了一个热带风暴（注：热带风暴是热带的一种低气压，它也会带来大风，但没有台风那么强烈）。中心最大风速为每秒十八公尺。正向西北偏西的方向移动，四十八小时后可能抵达琼州海峡附近。雷州半岛及海南岛一带将受到影响。
