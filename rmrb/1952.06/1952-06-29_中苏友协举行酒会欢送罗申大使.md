Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Reception to Bid Farewell to Ambassador Roshchin
Author:
Date: 1952-06-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 28th: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a reception in Beijing on the afternoon of the 28th to bid farewell to Roshchin, the first Soviet Ambassador to China, who is being recalled and is about to return to his country.

Ambassador Roshchin and all the diplomatic personnel of the Soviet Embassy were invited to the reception.

More than 200 people attended the farewell reception, including Liu Shaoqi, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Vice Presidents Wu Yuzhang, Shen Junru, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, and Huang Yanpei; and council members Zhu De, Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, Xu Teli, He Xiangning, Peng Zhen, Li Fuchun, Bo Yibo, Ma Xulun, Chen Boda, Zhang Xiruo, Xu Deheng, Liu Yazi, Shao Lizi, Wang Jiaxiang, Zhu Xuefan, Li Dequan, Shi Liang, Luo Longji, Wang Kunlun, Xu Baoju, Qian Junrui, Deng Yingchao, Kang Keqing, Xu Guangping, Feng Wenbin, Liao Chengzhi, Mei Lanfang, and Ouyang Yuqian.

President Liu Shaoqi delivered a farewell speech, stating: Ambassador Roshchin has worked in China for more than twelve years. Ambassador Roshchin has experienced the most difficult times for the Chinese people, and also the victorious times for the Chinese people. At all times, Ambassador Roshchin has consistently represented the Soviet people and the Soviet government in providing sincere assistance to the Chinese people. Under any difficult domestic and international circumstances, Ambassador Roshchin has been completely worthy in carrying out the selfless assistance of the Soviet people to the Chinese people. We thank the Soviet people for their assistance to the Chinese people, thank the Soviet government for their assistance to the Chinese people, and thank the great Stalin for his assistance to the Chinese people. It can be said that the help of the Soviet people is of decisive significance to the victory of the Chinese people's revolution, to the consolidation of the victory of the Chinese people's revolution, and to future construction. Ambassador Roshchin is the prominent and centralized representative who has implemented this assistance in many ways. Liu Shaoqi finally expressed gratitude to the great Soviet people, gratitude to the Soviet government, gratitude to Comrade Stalin, and gratitude to Ambassador Roshchin, and raised his glass to wish the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples to continue to be consolidated, and to wish Marshal Stalin good health.

Ambassador Roshchin then delivered a reply speech. He first thanked the Chinese people for the love and goodwill they hold for the Soviet people. He said: The days of working in China have left unforgettable impressions in my memory. I guarantee that in my future work I will continue to consolidate the friendly relations between the Soviet and Chinese peoples and further develop the constant and indestructible friendship between the two peoples. On the eve of my departure from China to return to my country, I would like to wish the Chinese people new and significant achievements in construction. Finally, Ambassador Roshchin raised his glass to wish good health to Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people.

The reception took place in an extremely cordial and friendly atmosphere.

### 中苏友协举行酒会欢送罗申大使

1952-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友协举行酒会欢送罗申大使
    【新华社二十八日讯】中苏友好协会总会于二十八日下午在北京举行酒会，欢送奉调离任即将返国的苏联驻我国首任大使罗申。
    罗申大使及苏联大使馆全体外交人员都应邀到会。
    参加欢送会的有中苏友好协会总会会长刘少奇，副会长吴玉章、沈钧儒、李济深、张澜、黄炎培，理事朱德、林伯渠、董必武、徐特立、何香凝、彭真、李富春、薄一波、马叙伦、陈伯达、张奚若、许德珩、柳亚子、邵力子、王稼祥、朱学范、李德全、史良、罗隆基、王崑仑、许宝驹、钱俊瑞、邓颖超、康克清、许广平、冯文彬、廖承志、梅兰芳、欧阳予倩等二百多人。
    刘少奇会长致欢送词称：罗申大使在中国工作已十二年多了。罗申大使经过中国人民最困难的时候，也经过中国人民胜利的时候。无论在什么时候，罗申大使一直代表苏联人民、苏联政府给予中国人民以真诚的帮助。在任何困难的国内和国际局势下，罗申大使是完全无愧地执行了苏联人民对中国人民无私的帮助。我们感谢苏联人民对中国人民的帮助，感谢苏联政府对中国人民的帮助，感谢伟大的斯大林对中国人民的帮助。可以说，苏联人民的帮助对中国人民革命的胜利，对中国人民革命胜利的巩固与今后的建设，都是有决定意义的。从多方面执行这种帮助的突出的集中的代表就是罗申大使。刘少奇最后表示感谢伟大的苏联人民、感谢苏联政府、感谢斯大林同志、感谢罗申大使，并举杯祝中国人民和苏联人民的友谊继续巩固，祝斯大林大元帅健康。
    罗申大使接着致答词，他首先感谢中国人民对苏联人民所抱有的热爱和好感。他说：在中国工作的日子，在我的记忆里留下了难以遗忘的印象。我保证在今后的工作中继续巩固苏中两国人民的友好关系，进一步发展两国人民经常的、不可摧毁的友谊。值此离华返国前夕，谨祝中国人民在建设中获得新的重大的成就。最后，罗申大使举杯祝中国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东主席的健康。
    酒会在极其亲切友好的气氛中进行。
