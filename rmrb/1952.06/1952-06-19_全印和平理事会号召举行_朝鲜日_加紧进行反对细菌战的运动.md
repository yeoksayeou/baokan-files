Title: All-India Peace Council Calls for "Korea Day" to Intensify the Movement Against Germ Warfare
Author:
Date: 1952-06-19
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 18th: The All-India Peace Council has called on peace committees throughout India to hold "Korea Day" on June 25th, the second anniversary of the Korean War, to organize public gatherings in various places to express the Indian people's determination to strive for an end to the Korean War.
    The All-India Peace Council called for the intensification of the movement against germ warfare on "Korea Day," to "supervise all governments—especially the U.S. government—to comply with the Geneva Protocol prohibiting germ warfare," and to "recommend the establishment of a court to try criminals guilty of the heinous crime of conducting germ warfare."
    The All-India Peace Council denounced the atrocities committed by the U.S. military against the Korean and Chinese prisoners of war on Geoje Island and pointed out: The full facts must be made known.
    The All-India Peace Council called for a widespread mobilization of the people to participate in the "Korea Day" gatherings and to collect signatures demanding a peace pact among the five major powers in the process of preparing for such gatherings.

### 全印和平理事会号召举行“朝鲜日”  加紧进行反对细菌战的运动

1952-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

    全印和平理事会号召举行“朝鲜日”
    加紧进行反对细菌战的运动
    【新华社新德里十八日电】全印和平理事会已号召印度各地的和平委员会于六月二十五日朝鲜战争两周年纪念日举行“朝鲜日”，在各地组织公共集会，表示印度人民为结束朝鲜战争而努力的决心。
    全印和平理事会号召在“朝鲜日”加紧进行反对细菌战的运动，“监督一切政府——特别是美国政府——遵守禁止细菌战的日内瓦议定书”，并“建议成立法庭，审讯犯有进行细菌战的滔天罪行的罪犯”。
    全印和平理事会斥责美国军队在巨济岛上对朝中战俘所犯的暴行，并指出：必须让人们知道全部事实。
    全印和平理事会号召广泛动员人民参加“朝鲜日”集会，并在筹备这种集会的过程中征集要求五大国缔结和平公约的签名。
