Title: Writer Zhou Libo Donates Stalin Literary Prize Money to the Volunteer Army
Author:
Date: 1952-06-21
Page: 3

    Writer Zhou Libo, who won the Stalin Literary Prize, donated the 25,000 rubles prize money from his novel "The Hurricane" to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army as funds for purchasing books and newspapers. The prize money has been handed over to the Chinese People's Resist America, Aid Korea General Association for forwarding.

    In a letter to Commander Peng Dehuai and all commanders and combatants of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, Zhou Libo said: You, together with the heroic Korean People's Army, are fighting the American aggressors in Korea, safeguarding world peace, protecting the Korean people, and protecting the security of the motherland. Your actions have earned the respect of people all over the world. All the Chinese people regard you as the pride and glory of the motherland. Under the teachings of Chairman Mao and under the leadership of the Party, I wrote a book, and this book won the prestigious Stalin Prize. I now donate the 25,000 rubles prize money to you for the purpose of purchasing books and newspapers. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 作家周立波将所获斯大林文艺奖金献给志愿军

1952-06-21
第3版()
专栏：

    作家周立波将所获斯大林文艺奖金献给志愿军
    荣获斯大林文艺奖金的我国作家周立波，将长篇小说“暴风骤雨”奖金二万五千卢布献给中国人民志愿军作为购买书报的费用。奖金已交中国人民抗美援朝总会转送。
    周立波在写给中国人民志愿军彭德怀司令员和全体指挥员战斗员的信中说：你们在朝鲜和英勇的朝鲜人民军一起，打击美国侵略者，保卫世界和平，保卫朝鲜人民，保卫祖国安全的行动，获得了全世界人民的尊敬。全中国人民把你们引为祖国的骄傲和光荣。在毛主席的教诲之下，在党的领导之下，我写了一本书，这本书获得了崇高的斯大林奖金，我现在把奖金二万五千卢布献给你们，作为购买书报之用。（新华社）
