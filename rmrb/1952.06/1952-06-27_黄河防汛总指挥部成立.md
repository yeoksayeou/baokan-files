Title: Yellow River Flood Control Headquarters Established
Author:
Date: 1952-06-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaifeng, June 26th: The Yellow River Flood Control Headquarters was formally established on June 20th. The Central Flood Control Headquarters appointed Wu Zhipu, Chairman of the Henan Provincial People's Government, as Director, and Luo Yuchuan, Vice Chairman of the Pingyuan Provincial People's Government, Wang Zhuoru, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Shandong Provincial People's Government, and Wang Huayun, Director of the Yellow River Water Conservancy Committee, as Deputy Directors. The Yellow River flood control headquarters of Henan, Pingyuan, Shandong, and Suiyuan Provinces were also established successively in mid-June.

	The flood control task for the Yellow River this year is to ensure a flood flow of 23,000 cubic meters per second at Shanzhou, and to strive to prevent breaches even when the flood flow reaches 29,000 cubic meters per second.

	After the establishment of the Yellow River Flood Control Headquarters, it instructed the provincial headquarters to earnestly inspect flood control preparations to ensure that flood control personnel can reach the dikes in a timely manner after the flood season arrives. It was also decided that all flood reporting stations along the river would begin reporting flood conditions from July 1st.

### 黄河防汛总指挥部成立

1952-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

    黄河防汛总指挥部成立
    【新华社开封二十六日电】黄河防汛总指挥部已于六月二十日正式成立。中央防汛指挥部任命河南省人民政府主席吴芝圃为主任，平原省人民政府副主席罗玉川、山东省人民政府财经委员会副主任王卓如、黄河水利委员会主任王化云为副主任。河南、平原、山东、绥远各省的治黄防汛指挥部亦于本月中旬先后成立。
    今年黄河的防汛任务，是保证陕州洪水流量两万三千秒公方，并争取洪水流量在两万九千秒公方时不发生溃决。
    黄河防汛总指挥部成立后，便指示各省指挥部，切实检查防汛准备，求得在汛期到来之后，防汛人员能够及时上堤。并决定全河各报汛站自七月一日起开始报汛。
