Title: Some Representatives of Countries Attending the Preparatory Meeting for the Peace Conference Visit the Jingjiang Flood Diversion Project Construction Site
Author: 
Date: 1952-06-10
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 9th. A group of twenty-six representatives from various countries attending the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference arrived in Wuhan by plane from Beijing on the afternoon of the 9th. That evening, they boarded a special boat to visit the Jingjiang Flood Diversion Project construction site. Zhao Yimin, Li Erzhong, Wang Renzhong, Zheng Shaowen, and representatives from people's organizations in the Central South Region, Wuhan City, and Hubei Province were at the airport to welcome them.

### 出席和平会议筹备会议各国代表一部  参观荆江分洪工程工地

1952-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    出席和平会议筹备会议各国代表一部
    参观荆江分洪工程工地
    【新华社汉口九日电】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的筹备会议的各国代表一部二十六人，于九日下午由北京乘飞机到达武汉；当晚即搭专轮前往荆江分洪工程工地参观。到机场欢迎的有赵毅敏、李尔重、王任重、郑绍文及中南区、武汉市、湖北省各人民团体代表多人。
