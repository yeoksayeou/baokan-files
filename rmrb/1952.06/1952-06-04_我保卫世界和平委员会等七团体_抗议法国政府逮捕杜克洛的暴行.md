Title: Seven Organizations Including the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Defend World Peace Protest the French Government's Atrocity of Arresting Duclos

Author:
Date: 1952-06-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd:

The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and six other people's organizations sent a telegram to French Prime Minister Pinay on June 3rd, protesting the French government's atrocity of arresting the French people's leader Duclos. The telegram is as follows:

To Mr. Pinay, Prime Minister of France:

The French people's opposition to the bacteriological warfare criminal Ridgway's stationing in France to implement the U.S. war plan and prepare for the mass slaughter of the European people is a glorious and just struggle. Your government not only fails to support it but suppresses it, even arresting Mr. Duclos, the people's leader who defends peace. Such an atrocity has aroused the public indignation of peace-loving people all over the world. We fully sympathize with and support the French people's heroic struggle for peace and freedom and demand that your government immediately restore Mr. Duclos' freedom.

    The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
    All-China Democratic Women's Federation
    All-China Federation of Democratic Youth
    All-China Students' Federation
    All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles
    China Association for the Advancement of Science
    Preparatory Office of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce

                                         June 3, 1952

### 我保卫世界和平委员会等七团体  抗议法国政府逮捕杜克洛的暴行

1952-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    我保卫世界和平委员会等七团体
    抗议法国政府逮捕杜克洛的暴行
    【新华社三日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会等七个人民团体，在六月三日致电法国总理比内，抗议法国政府逮捕法国人民领袖杜克洛的暴行。电文如下：法国总理比内先生：
    法国人民反对细菌战战犯李奇微驻法去执行美国战争计划准备大量屠杀欧洲人民，是光荣的正义的斗争。你的政府非但不予支持，反而加以镇压，竟将维护和平的人民领袖杜克洛先生逮捕入狱。这样的暴行已引起全世界爱好和平人民的公愤。我们完全同情和声援法国人民为和平与自由而进行的英勇斗争，并要求你的政府立即恢复杜克洛先生的自由。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
    中华全国民主妇女联合会
    中华全国民主青年联合总会
    中华全国学生联合会
    中华全国文学艺术界联合会
    中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会
    中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会筹备处
                                         一九五二年六月三日
