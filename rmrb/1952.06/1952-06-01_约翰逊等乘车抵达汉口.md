Title: Johnson and Others Arrive in Hankou by Train
Author:
Date: 1952-06-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, May 31st. The Vice-Dean of Canterbury, England, Johnson, and his wife, and Mr. Day, lecturer on labor education issues at Oxford University, England, arrived in Hankou by train this afternoon from Beijing, accompanied by Xu Ying, representative of the Liaison Department of the China Peace Committee. Those who welcomed them at the station included Zhao Yimin, Chairman of the Central and South China Sub-Committee of the China Peace Committee, Wu Jian'an, Director of the Religious Affairs Office of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central and South Military and Political Committee, Zhang Haisong, Bishop of the E-Xiang Diocese of the Chinese Holy Catholic Church, Chen Jianxun, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Christian Lutheran Church, Liu Nianfen, General Secretary of the Wuhan YWCA, Wan Fulin, President of the Hubei Conference of the Church of Christ in China and President of the Central China Union Theological Seminary, as well as hundreds of workers, women, and students.

### 约翰逊等乘车抵达汉口

1952-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    约翰逊等乘车抵达汉口
    【新华社汉口三十一日电】前来我国参观的英国坎特伯雷副主教约翰逊及其夫人，英国牛津大学工人教育问题讲师戴伊，在中国人民保卫世界和平委员会联络部代表徐盈陪同下，于三十一日下午由北京乘车抵达汉口。到车站欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会中南总分会主席赵毅敏、中南军政委员会文教委员会宗教事务处处长武剑安、中华圣公会鄂湘教区主教张海松、中国基督教信义会副主席陈建勋、武汉市基督教女青年会总干事刘年芬、中国基督教会湖北大会理事长、华中协和神学院院长万福林及工人、妇女、学生等数百人。
