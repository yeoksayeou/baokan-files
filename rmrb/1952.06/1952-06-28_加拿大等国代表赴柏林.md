Title: Representatives from Canada and Other Countries Depart for Berlin
Author:
Date: 1952-06-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, June 26th:

    The representatives attending the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, including Mary Jennison from Canada, José Venturelli from Chile, Jorge Bayona from Colombia, Eduardo Mora from Costa Rica, Suruso from Indonesia, Elí Gordin and Rafael Méndez from Mexico, Antonio Décis from El Salvador, John Kingsbury from the United States, and U Tun Kin, representative from Burma who visited our country after attending the International Conference for the Defence of Children and attended the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference as an observer, a group of eleven people, left Beijing by plane on the 26th for Moscow, and then to Berlin to attend the Third Session of the World Peace Council. Among those who saw them off at the airport were Wu Maosun, Deputy Secretary-General of the China Peace Committee, Meng Yu, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department, Hu Zhongchi, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department, and many others.

### 加拿大等国代表赴柏林

1952-06-28
第4版()
专栏：

    加拿大等国代表赴柏林
    【新华社二十六日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的加拿大代表玛丽·詹尼逊，智利代表何塞·万徒勒里，哥伦比亚代表乔治·巴约纳，哥斯达黎加代表艾杜亚多·莫拉，印度尼西亚代表苏鲁梭，墨西哥代表艾里·哥尔达利、拉斐尔·门德斯，萨尔瓦多代表安东尼欧·第西斯，美国代表约翰·金斯伯利，以及参加国际保卫儿童大会后来我国参观并列席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的缅甸代表都庆纳等一行十一人，二十六日乘飞机离北京赴莫斯科，转赴柏林参加世界和平理事会第三次会议。到机场送行的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副秘书长吴茂荪、联络部副部长孟雨、宣传部副部长胡仲持等多人。
