Title: People of Various Countries Continue to Donate Large Quantities of Gifts to Support the Korean People
Author:
Date: 1952-06-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 24th: In the past year or more, the Korean people have continued to receive enthusiastic support from the people of various countries. The people of various countries have unanimously expressed strong protests against the various bloody atrocities of American imperialism and its crimes of conducting bacteriological warfare. Recently, the World Federation of Trade Unions and the International Democratic Women's Federation have successively issued calls urging working people and women around the world to actively support the heroic Korean people. All of this has infinitely inspired the Korean people's fighting will and confidence in victory.

The people of the Soviet Union, China, and various People's Democratic countries have continued to send large quantities of gifts, condolence money, and letters of sympathy to the Korean people. From February to the end of last year, the Soviet people sent 789 truckloads of medical equipment and medicines, as well as batches of cultural equipment and other gifts. The Chinese people sent 115,628 bags of grain, more than 440,000 pieces of clothing and more than 1,000 boxes, as well as fabrics, food, medical equipment, medicines, daily necessities, etc. In addition, there were thousands of enthusiastic letters of sympathy from all over China. The Mongolian people sent 20,000 pairs of boots and 30,000 pieces of clothing, etc. The Polish people sent more than 7,800 boxes and more than 3,600 bags of clothing, more than 3,000 bags of food, three cars, as well as fabrics, medical supplies, and boots, etc. The Romanian people sent 1,585 bags of grain, more than 1,600 boxes of medical equipment and medicines, more than 4.62 million rubles in condolence money, as well as cars, clothing, fabrics, and food, etc. The Hungarian people sent a total of 15 cars and bicycles, as well as clothing, fabrics, food, and cultural equipment, etc. The Bulgarian people sent more than 1,600 boxes of fabrics, more than 6,200 boxes of clothing, grain, cars, etc. The people of the German Democratic Republic sent more than 2,000 boxes of clothing, two machines, and batches of cars, fabrics, and food. The Albanian people sent five cars. The people of France and Austria also sent batches of medical equipment, medicines, and condolence money.

From January to May of this year, the people of various People's Democratic countries sent more than 400 carloads of grain, clothing, equipment, and medicines to the Korean people. Among them, 316 carloads of materials have been transported to Korea and distributed to various places. The Soviet people also sent a large amount of grain. The Chinese people have sent a large amount of equipment and medicines to assist the Korean people in their struggle against bacteriological warfare. By the end of May, the Chinese people had donated more than 118.6 billion yuan in Renminbi to the Korean-Chinese People's Army and Korean war zone refugees. Women in Poland, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Bulgaria, and other countries have taken care of a group of Korean children on behalf of Korean women in order to spare them the suffering of war. The people of various People's Democratic countries have also continuously sent delegations and慰问团to visit the Korean battlefield.

The Chinese People's Volunteers, who are fighting side by side with the Korean People's Army, have consistently enthusiastically helped the Korean people in their work of recovery and production during lulls in combat, and often save food and give it to Korean refugees ravaged by the American aggressors.

These selfless international aid efforts have strengthened the Korean people's fighting will and confidence in victory. The Korean people have unanimously expressed their determination to repay the internationalist aid with greater achievements in production and combat. They have decided to designate June 25th as "Anti-American Imperialism Struggle Day" to strengthen the struggle and strive for a more brilliant victory in the anti-aggression war.

### 各国人民继续捐献大批礼物支持朝鲜人民

1952-06-26
第4版()
专栏：

    各国人民继续捐献大批礼物支持朝鲜人民
    【新华社平壤二十四日电】在过去一年多中，朝鲜人民继续受到各国人民的热烈声援和支持。各国人民对于美帝国主义的种种血腥暴行和进行细菌战的罪行一致表示强烈的抗议。最近，世界工会联合会和国际民主妇女联合会相继发出号召，敦促全世界劳动人民与妇女积极声援英雄的朝鲜人民。这一切都无限地鼓舞了朝鲜人民的斗争意志和胜利信心。
    苏联、中国与各人民民主国家的人民继续给朝鲜人民送来了大批礼物、慰问金与慰问信。从去年二月到年底，苏联人民送来医疗器材和药品七百八十九汽车，文化器材和其他慰问品各一批。中国人民送来粮食十一万五千六百二十八袋，衣服四十四万多件又一千多箱，以及布疋、食品、医疗器材、药品、日用品等，此外，还有来自全中国各地的数千封热情洋溢的慰问信。蒙古人民送来靴子两万双，衣服等三万件。波兰人民送来衣服七千八百多箱又三千六百多袋，食品三千多袋，汽车三辆，以及布疋、医药用品、靴子等。罗马尼亚人民送来粮食一千五百八十五袋，医疗器材和药品一千六百多箱，慰问金四百六十二万余卢布，以及汽车、衣服、布疋、食品等。匈牙利人民送来汽车和自行车共十五辆，以及衣服、布疋、食品和文化器材等。保加利亚人民送来布疋一千六百多箱，衣服六千二百多箱，粮食、汽车等。德意志民主共和国的人民送来衣服二千多箱，机器两台，汽车、布疋、食品等各
    一批。阿尔巴尼亚人民送来汽车五辆。法国、奥地利人民也送来医疗器材、药品和慰问金各一批。
    从今年一月到五月为止，各人民民主国家人民又给朝鲜人民送来了四百多车皮的粮食、衣服、器材与药品等。其中，三百十六车皮的物资已运抵朝鲜，分配各地。苏联人民亦送来大批粮食。中国人民已送了大批器材、药品，协助朝鲜人民进行反细菌战的斗争。到五月底为止，中国人民并给朝中人民军队与朝鲜战区难民捐献了人民币一千一百八十六亿余元。波兰、匈牙利、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、保加利亚等国的妇女为了使朝鲜儿童免受战争的痛苦，分别代朝鲜妇女抚育了一批朝鲜儿童。各人民民主国家人民并不断派遣代表团与慰问团到朝鲜战地进行慰问。
    与朝鲜人民军并肩作战的中国人民志愿军在战斗空隙中，一贯热心地帮助朝鲜人民进行恢复与生产的工作，并经常节约粮食，送给受美国侵略者摧残的朝鲜难民。
    这些大公无私的国际援助，已使朝鲜人民的斗争意志与胜利信心更加坚强。朝鲜人民一致表示要以更大的生产与战斗的成绩来答谢国际主义的援助。他们决定把六月二十五日作为“反对美帝国主义斗争日”，加强斗争，争取反侵略战争的更加辉煌的胜利。
