Title: Representatives from Various Countries Spoke at the Preparatory Meeting for the Peace Conference Yesterday, Discussing the Importance of Uniting to Defend Peace; Three Organizations Including the Korean National Committee for the Defense of Peace Sent Congratulatory Telegrams
Author:
Date: 1952-06-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 5th: The Preparatory Meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference continued on the 5th.

Today, ?Saravadhana Dullarat (former Minister without Portfolio of the Thai Government) and ?Prakase Saasunthon (former Thai Member of Parliament) reported to attend the meeting. Deqin Qinsu from Myanmar attended the meeting as an observer.

In the morning, the declaration drafting committee held a meeting.

In the afternoon, a plenary meeting was held, chaired by Mr. Alley from New Zealand, and vice-chaired by Mr. Muhammad Khan from Pakistan. When the chairman announced the attendance of the Thai representatives, ?Saravadhana Dullarat and ?Prakase Saasunthon, the whole audience welcomed them with warm applause.

Then, Mr. Jennison from Canada, Ms. Yang Bailian from Vietnam, and Mr. Van Turelli from Chile spoke successively.

After a break, the meeting was chaired by Mr. Cruz from the Philippines and vice-chaired by Mr. Kingsbury from the United States.

Mr. Li Dequan from China and Mr. ?Prakase Saasunthon from Thailand spoke in succession.

The representatives of the countries who spoke at the conference today reported on the peace movements in their respective countries, and discussed the importance of the people of the Asian and Pacific regions uniting to defend peace, pointing out the future of the inevitable victory of peace.

So far, the representatives of the twenty countries who attended the meeting have all spoken. The chairman announced the end of the conference speeches, and the plenary meeting will continue tomorrow. Liu Ningyi, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Meeting, then read out the congratulatory telegrams from the Korean National Committee for the Defense of Peace, the Korean Democratic Women's League, and the Korean Federation of Literary and Art Organizations to the Preparatory Meeting, and announced that many congratulatory telegrams had been received from people's organizations in various parts of China. The Chairman of the Conference requested the Secretary-General to convey to the attending representatives the gratitude for these congratulatory telegrams. The plenary meeting was then adjourned.

In the evening, the declaration drafting committee continued to hold a meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, 5th: The Korean National Committee for the Defense of Peace, the Korean Democratic Women's League, and the Korean Federation of Literary and Art Organizations sent congratulatory telegrams respectively on the 2nd to the Preparatory Meeting of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference held in Beijing. The congratulatory telegram from the Korean National Committee for the Defense of Peace said: "The National Committee for the Defense of Peace of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, on behalf of the peace-loving people of Korea who are fighting for the freedom, independence, and peace of their motherland and against foreign aggressors, extends warm congratulations to the representatives attending the Preparatory Meeting of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. This Committee hopes that the work of your meeting will achieve great success."

### 各国代表昨日在和平会议筹备会议上发言  论述团结起来保卫和平的重要性  朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会等三团体来电祝贺

1952-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

    各国代表昨日在和平会议筹备会议上发言
    论述团结起来保卫和平的重要性
    朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会等三团体来电祝贺
    【新华社五日讯】亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议五日继续进行。
    今日报到出席会议的代表有泰国?沙愿·都拉叻（泰国政府前任不管部长）和?巴色·飒顺吞（泰国前国会议员），列席会议的有缅甸的德钦钦素。
    上午，宣言起草委员会举行了会议。
    下午，举行全体会议，由新西兰代表艾黎任主席，巴基斯坦代表穆汗默德·汗任副主席。当主席宣布泰国代表?沙愿·都拉叻和?巴色·飒顺吞出席会议时，全场热烈鼓掌欢迎。
    接着，加拿大代表詹尼逊，越南代表杨白莲，智利代表万徒勒里先后发言。
    会议经休息后，由菲律宾代表柯鲁兹任主席，美国代表金斯伯利任副主席。
    中国代表李德全和泰国代表?巴色·飒顺吞相继发言。
    今日在大会上发言的各国代表都报告了各该国的和平运动情况，并论述了亚洲及太平洋区域各国人民团结起来保卫和平的重要性，指出了和平必然胜利的前途。
    至此，到会二十个国家的代表均先后发言完毕。主席宣布大会发言结束，明日继续举行全体会议。筹备会议秘书长刘宁一继起宣读了朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会、朝鲜民主妇女同盟和朝鲜文学艺术总同盟致筹备会议的贺电，并且宣布收到中国各地人民团体的许多贺电。大会主席请求秘书长转达到会代表对这些贺电的谢意。全体会议旋即休会。
    晚间，宣言起草委员会继续举行会议。
    【新华社五日讯】朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会、朝鲜民主妇女同盟和朝鲜文学艺术总同盟在二日分别致电在北京召开的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议，表示祝贺。朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会的贺电说：“朝鲜民主主义人民共和国拥护和平全国民族委员会谨代表为自己祖国的自由、独立与和平，为反对外来侵略者而斗争的朝鲜爱好和平的人民，向参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的各位代表致以热烈的祝贺。本委员会希望贵会议的工作获得巨大的成功。”
