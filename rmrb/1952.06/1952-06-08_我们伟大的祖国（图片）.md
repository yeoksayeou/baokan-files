Author:
Date: 1952-06-08

    The Han'en Agricultural Production Cooperative has begun to widely use new-style horse-drawn farm implements. This shows the members using a horse-drawn seeder to sow seeds in the fields. They will complete their sowing in half the time it took last year.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    韩恩农业生产合作社已开始普遍使用新式马拉农具。这是社员们正在用马拉播种机在田地里播种，他们今年播种的时间要比去年缩短一半。
