Title: Northwest Nationalities Education Visiting Group Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-06-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 17th. The Northwest Nationalities Education Visiting Group, consisting of seventeen people led by group leader Ai Zezi (Uyghur), departed from Xi'an on the evening of the 16th to visit the capital. The group is composed of educational cadres, model teachers, and model students from spare-time industrial and agricultural schools from seven ethnic groups, including Uyghur, Kazakh, Tibetan, Mongolian, Manchu, Hui, and Han.

### 西北各民族教育参观团来京

1952-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

    西北各民族教育参观团来京
    【新华社西安十七日电】西北各民族教育参观团一行十七人，在团长艾则孜（维吾尔族）率领下，在十六日晚自西安启程赴首都参观。该团是由维吾尔、哈萨克、藏、蒙、满、回、汉等七个民族的教育工作干部、模范教师和工农业余学校的模范学员等组成的。
