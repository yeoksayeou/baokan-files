    Our Great Motherland
    The people's sports activities in New China are becoming increasingly popular. In Jiantai Village, Huaian County, Chahar Province, young farmers go to do sports activities together every morning without prior agreement. These dozen or so young farmers are practicing running on the long embankment.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新中国人民的体育运动已日益普及。察哈尔省怀安县尖台寨村的青年农民们，每天清早都不约而同地到一起来做体育活动。这十几个青年农民正在长堤上练习跑步。
