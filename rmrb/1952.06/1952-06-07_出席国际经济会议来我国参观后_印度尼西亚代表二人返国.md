Title: Two Indonesian Representatives Return Home After Attending International Economic Conference and Visiting China
Author:
Date: 1952-06-07
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 6th. Two Indonesian representatives, Yoyo Pratno and Hartoyo, who attended the Moscow International Economic Conference and then visited China, returned home via Guangzhou by train to Hong Kong on the 5th. Among those who went to the station to see them off were Li Langru, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangdong Provincial Federation of Industry and Commerce, and Liang Shangli, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Municipal Federation of Industry and Commerce.

### 出席国际经济会议来我国参观后  印度尼西亚代表二人返国

1952-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    出席国际经济会议来我国参观后
    印度尼西亚代表二人返国
    【新华社广州六日电】出席莫斯科国际经济会议后前来我国参观的印度尼西亚代表约约普拉伊特诺和哈托约二人，在五日经由广州乘车转道香港回国。前往车站欢送的有：广东省工商业联合会筹备会副主任委员李朗如、广州市工商业联合会筹备会副主任委员梁尚立等。
