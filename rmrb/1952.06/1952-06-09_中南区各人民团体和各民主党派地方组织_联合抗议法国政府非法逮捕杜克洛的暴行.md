Title: Joint Protest by People's Organizations and Local Organizations of Democratic Parties in Central and Southern China Against the French Government's Illegal Arrest of Duclos

Author:
Date: 1952-06-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 8th. The China Peace Committee of the Asian and Pacific Regions Central and Southern General Branch, the Central and Southern Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee, and thirty-four people's organizations and local organizations of democratic parties in the Central and Southern Region, Wuhan City, and Hubei Province, jointly sent a telegram to the French government under René Mayer, protesting the French government's illegal arrest of French people's leader Jacques Duclos and others. The protest telegram stated: "Jacques Duclos, the son of the French people and secretary of the French Communist Party, Henri Jeanson editor-in-chief of L'Humanité, and other patriots, held demonstrations to fight for national independence, defend peace, oppose the war criminal Matthew Ridgway's arrival in France, and oppose American war plans. This is a patriotic action and a glorious just struggle. The French government authorities' arrest of Duclos, Jeanson, and others is illegal. People's organizations in the Central and Southern Region, Wuhan City, and Hubei Province demand that the French government immediately release Duclos, Jeanson, and other patriotic figures." At the same time, the people's organizations also sent telegrams of condolences to Duclos, Jeanson, and others.

### 中南区各人民团体和各民主党派地方组织  联合抗议法国政府非法逮捕杜克洛的暴行

1952-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中南区各人民团体和各民主党派地方组织
    联合抗议法国政府非法逮捕杜克洛的暴行
    【新华社汉口八日电】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会中南总分会、中南总工会筹备委员会等中南区、武汉市、湖北省三十四个人民团体及各民主党派地方组织，联合致电法国比内政府，抗议法国政府非法逮捕法国人民领袖杜克洛等人的暴行。抗议电文中说：“法国人民的儿子、法国共产党书记杜克洛，人道报总编辑斯梯及其他爱国者，为争取民族独立、保卫和平、反对细菌战战犯李奇微抵法和反对美国战争计划而举行的游行示威，是爱国的行动，是光荣的正义斗争。法国政府当局对杜克洛和斯梯等人的逮捕是非法的。中南区、武汉市、湖北省各人民团体要求法国政府立即释放杜克洛、斯梯及其他爱国人士。”各人民团体同时还分别致电慰问杜克洛和斯梯等人。
