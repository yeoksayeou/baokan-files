Title: Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen Holds Banquet to Host Delegates from Various Countries Attending the Preparatory Meeting for the Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-06-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 6th: Beijing Municipal People's Government Mayor Peng Zhen held a banquet on the evening of the 6th to host delegates from various countries attending the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference in Beijing. Among those invited to the banquet were more than 40 delegates from Australia, Burma, Canada, Chile, India, Indonesia, Japan, North Korea, Malaya, Mexico, Mongolia, New Zealand, Pakistan, the Philippines, Thailand, the United States, the Soviet Union, and Vietnam. Guryayev and John Darr, secretaries of the Secretariat of the World Peace Council, Thornton, a member of the Asian-Australian Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions, and peace activists from Burma, India, Indonesia, and other countries attending the preparatory meeting were also invited.

On the Chinese side, those present at the banquet included Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, Li Dequan, Nan Hanchen, Wu Yaozong, Li Siguang, Zhang Xiruo, Zhang Youyu, Wu Han, and more than 200 well-known figures from cultural, educational, scientific, economic, religious, and other circles in the capital.

Mayor Peng Zhen delivered a welcome speech at the meeting. He first extended warm congratulations on behalf of the Beijing Municipal People's Government and all the people for the great achievements of the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, and expressed his highest respect to all friends who are earnestly fighting for the cause of defending peace. Mayor Peng Zhen said: This meeting concentratedly demonstrates the common and urgent demands of peace-loving people in various countries in Asia and the Pacific region. Its achievements mark the strong will of the broad masses of people in Asia and the Pacific region to strive for and defend peace, and mark that the peace-loving people in Asia and the Pacific region have closely united in the struggle to defend peace and become an invincible force. The achievements of this meeting have laid a good foundation for the upcoming Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. We, the Chinese people, firmly believe that after the delegates return to their countries, they will greatly promote the development of the peace movement. After this meeting, the people of Asia and the Pacific region will further unite, the peace movement in Asia and the Pacific region will have a new leap in development, and our struggle to eliminate the threat of aggressive war will achieve a major victory. I hope that all delegates will bring the determination and confidence of the Chinese people to defend peace to the people of all countries. Finally, Mayor Peng Zhen, on behalf of all the people of Beijing, expressed his sincere support for all the resolutions of the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, and expressed his willingness to contribute all kinds of efforts for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference to be held in Beijing soon.

Soviet representative Grusenko then delivered a speech at the meeting. He elaborated on the significance of holding the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference in Beijing and congratulated the people of Beijing on their new achievements in building a happy life.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. The hosts and guests frequently toasted to the success of the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference and to lasting world peace. After the banquet, a music and dance evening was held for entertainment.

### 北京市彭真市长举行宴会  招待和平会议筹备会议各国代表

1952-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市彭真市长举行宴会
    招待和平会议筹备会议各国代表
    【新华社六日讯】北京市人民政府彭真市长在六日晚间举行宴会，招待来北京参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的各国代表。应邀赴宴的有：澳大利亚、缅甸、加拿大、智利、印度、印度尼西亚、日本、朝鲜、马来亚、墨西哥、蒙古、新西兰、巴基斯坦、菲律宾、泰国、美国、苏联、越南等国代表四十多人。世界和平理事会书记处秘书顾里亚耶夫和约翰·达尔、世界工会联合会亚澳联络局委员桑顿，以及列席筹备会议的缅甸、印度、印度尼西亚等国和平人士也应邀到会。
    我国方面出席宴会的有：郭沫若、陈叔通、李德全、南汉宸、吴耀宗、李四光、张奚若、张友渔、吴晗以及首都文化、教育、科学、经济、宗教等各界著名人士二百多人。
    彭真市长在会上致欢迎辞。他首先代表北京市人民政府和全体人民热烈祝贺亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的伟大成就，并向热诚地为保卫和平事业而奋斗的各位朋友们表示崇高的敬意。彭真市长说：这次的会议，集中地表现了亚洲及太平洋区域各国爱好和平人民共同的迫切要求。它的成就标志着亚洲及太平洋区域广大人民争取与保卫和平的坚强意志，标志着亚洲及太平洋区域爱好和平的人民已经在保卫和平的斗争中，紧紧地团结起来成为不可战胜的力量。这次会议的成就，已经替即将召开的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议奠定了良好的基础。我们中国人民坚信：各位代表先生归国之后，对于保卫和平运动的开展，必将有极大的推进。亚洲及太平洋区域的人民，在这次会议之后，必将进一步团结起来，亚洲和太平洋区域的和平运动必将有新的飞跃的发展，我们消弭侵略战争威胁的斗争，必将获得重大的胜利。我希望各位代表先生将我国人民保卫和平的决心和信心带给各国人民。彭真市长最后代表北京市全体人民表示热诚拥护亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的一切决议，并愿为不久即将在北京召开的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议供献各种力量。
    苏联代表格鲁森科接着在会上致辞。他论述了在北京召开亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的意义，并祝贺北京市人民在建设幸福的生活中获得新的成就。
    宴会在热烈友好的空气中进行。宾主频频为亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的成功，为世界的持久和平而干杯。宴会后并举行音乐舞蹈晚会助兴。
