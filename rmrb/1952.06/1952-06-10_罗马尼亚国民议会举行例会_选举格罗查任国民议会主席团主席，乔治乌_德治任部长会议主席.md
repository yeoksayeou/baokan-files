Title: Romanian National Assembly Holds Session, Elects Groza as Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly, Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej as Chairman of the Council of Ministers
Author: 
Date: 1952-06-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 9th: According to TASS, the Romanian National Assembly held a session on the 2nd. Present at the meeting were: Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Academician Parhon, Groza, Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers Chisinevschi, Apostol, Stoica, Ana Pauker, members of the Presidium of the National Assembly, ministers of various government departments, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Romania. The meeting unanimously elected Groza to succeed Academician Parhon as Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly. Academician Parhon resigned due to his age and heavy scientific research work. The meeting also unanimously elected Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, to succeed Groza as Chairman of the Council of Ministers. The two have been sworn in to their respective offices before the parliament.

### 罗马尼亚国民议会举行例会  选举格罗查任国民议会主席团主席，乔治乌—德治任部长会议主席

1952-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚国民议会举行例会
    选举格罗查任国民议会主席团主席，乔治乌—德治任部长会议主席
    【新华社九日讯】据塔斯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚国民议会于二日举行例会。到会的有：乔治乌—德治、巴洪院士、格罗查、部长会议副主席契西尼夫斯奇、阿波斯托尔、斯托依卡、安娜·波克、国民议会主席团委员、政府各部部长等，各国驻罗外交使节也列席会议。会议一致选举格罗查继巴洪院士为国民议会主席团主席，巴洪院士因年老，并因科学研究工作繁重辞职。会议并一致选举罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会总书记乔治乌—德治继格罗查任部长会议主席。他们两人已先后向议会宣誓就职。
