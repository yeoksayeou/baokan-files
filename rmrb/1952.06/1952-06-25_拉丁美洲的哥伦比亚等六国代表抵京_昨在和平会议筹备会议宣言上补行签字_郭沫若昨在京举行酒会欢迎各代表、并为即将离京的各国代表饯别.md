Title: Latin American Representatives from Six Countries, Including Colombia, Arrive in Beijing; Supplementary Signing of the Declaration of the Preparatory Meeting for the Peace Conference Yesterday; Guo Moruo Held a Reception in Beijing Yesterday to Welcome the Representatives and to Bid Farewell to the Representatives of Various Countries About to Leave Beijing

Author:
Date: 1952-06-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 24th -- Eleven representatives from six Latin American countries—Colombia, Costa Rica, Guatemala, Mexico, Nicaragua, and El Salvador—who were delayed en route, and who are participating in the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, yesterday made a supplementary signing of the declaration of the preparatory meeting. The signing ceremony was held at the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace. Also participating in the signing ceremony were the Chinese initiators Guo Moruo and Liao Chengzhi, Chinese representatives attending the preparatory meeting Chen Shutong, Li Dequan, Nan Hanchen, Wu Yaozong, as well as Chilean representative Wan Tuleli and Mexican representatives Goldari and Mendes. Before the signing ceremony, Guo Moruo delivered a speech expressing welcome, and Liao Chengzhi, on behalf of the Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee Liu Ningyi, introduced the situation of the preparatory meeting. Representatives from various countries spoke successively, expressing the peace aspirations of their peoples and their enthusiasm for supporting the declaration of the preparatory meeting.

The list of representatives from Latin American countries who signed is as follows: George Emig Bayona, Eduardo Mora, Atara Valenzuela, Mario Silva, Otto Raul Gonzalez, Jose Manuel Sanchez, Paula Gomez Alonso, Jose Rogelio Alvarez, Edelmilo Maldonado, Edeberto Torres, Antonio Diaz.

Xinhua News Agency, June 24th -- The eleven representatives from Colombia, Costa Rica, Guatemala, Mexico, Nicaragua, and El Salvador, who made a supplementary signing of the declaration of the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference on the 24th, added the following statement to the declaration after signing: "We, the representatives from Colombia, Costa Rica, Guatemala, Mexico, Nicaragua, and El Salvador, were unable to attend the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference held in Beijing from June 3rd to June 6th, 1952, due to travel reasons. We signed the declaration of the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference in Beijing on June 24th, 1952. We support this declaration because it fully reflects the principles and objectives of the peace movement."

Xinhua News Agency, June 24th -- Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, held a reception in Beijing on the 24th to welcome the representatives from Latin American countries participating in the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, who recently arrived in Beijing due to delays en route, and to bid farewell to the representatives from various countries attending the preparatory meeting who are about to leave Beijing. Twenty-three representatives from countries including Burma, Canada, Chile, Colombia, Costa Rica, Guatemala, Indonesia, Japan, Mexico, New Zealand, Nicaragua, Pakistan, and El Salvador attended the reception. Du Qingna, the Burmese representative attending the preparatory meeting, and Roa, the French representative who came to Beijing to attend the commemoration of the four great cultural figures, were also invited to attend the reception. The Chinese side was represented by Chen Shutong, Li Dequan, Nan Hanchen, Wu Yaozong, Shao Lizi, Xu Baoju, Xu Deheng, Zhang Xiruo, Li Siguang, Liao Chengzhi, and others. The reception was held in a friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, June 24th -- Five representatives from Guatemala and Nicaragua in Latin America, who were to participate in the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 24th due to travel delays. The list of representatives is as follows: Guatemala: Professor and leader of the peace movement Mario Silva, poet Otto Raul Gonzalez, trade union leader and railway mechanic Jose Manuel Sanchez, female teacher Atara Valenzuela; Nicaragua: Secretary-General of the Nicaraguan Committee for the Defense of National Sovereignty Edeberto Torres.

### 拉丁美洲的哥伦比亚等六国代表抵京  昨在和平会议筹备会议宣言上补行签字  郭沫若昨在京举行酒会欢迎各代表、并为即将离京的各国代表饯别

1952-06-25
第4版()
专栏：

    拉丁美洲的哥伦比亚等六国代表抵京
    昨在和平会议筹备会议宣言上补行签字
    郭沫若昨在京举行酒会欢迎各代表、并为即将离京
    的各国代表饯别
    【新华社二十四日讯】因途中延搁而迟到的参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的拉丁美洲哥伦比亚、哥斯达黎加、危地马拉、墨西哥、尼加拉瓜、萨尔瓦多六国代表十一人，二十四日在亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议宣言上补行签字。签名仪式在中国人民保卫世界和平委员会举行。参加签名仪式的还有中国发起人郭沫若和廖承志，出席筹备会议的中国代表陈叔通、李德全、南汉宸、吴耀宗，以及智利代表万徒勒里和墨西哥代表哥尔达利和门德斯。在举行签名仪式前，由郭沫若致词表示欢迎，廖承志代表筹备委员会秘书长刘宁一介绍了筹备会议情况。各国代表先后讲话，表达了各国人民的和平愿望，和他们拥护筹备会议宣言的热忱。
    拉丁美洲各国代表签名名单如下：乔治·爱米格·巴约纳，艾杜亚多·莫拉，阿他拉·瓦伦瑞拉，马利欧·西尔瓦，奥托·罗尔·冈萨莱斯，何塞·曼纽尔·桑切斯，保拉·哥麦斯·阿隆卓，何塞·罗赫里欧·阿尔瓦雷斯，厄德尔米罗·马尔多纳多，埃戴贝尔托·托莱斯，安东尼欧·第亚斯。
    【新华社二十四日讯】二十四日在亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议宣言上补行签名的哥伦比亚、哥斯达黎加、危地马拉、墨西哥、尼加拉瓜和萨尔瓦多的代表十一人，签名后在宣言上附加声明如下：“我们来自哥伦比亚、哥斯达黎加、危地马拉、墨西哥、尼加拉瓜和萨尔瓦多的代表，由于路程关系，未能参加一九五二年六月三日至六月六日在北京举行的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的筹备会议。我们在一九五二年六月二十四日在北京在亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议宣言上签名。我们拥护这一宣言，因为它充分表现了和平运动的道理和目的。”
    【新华社二十四日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若二十四日在北京举行酒会，欢迎因途中延搁最近才抵达北京的参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的拉丁美洲各国代表，并为即将离京的出席筹备会议的各国代表饯别。出席酒会的有缅甸、加拿大、智利、哥伦比亚、哥斯达黎加、危地马拉、印度尼西亚、日本、墨西哥、新西兰、尼加拉瓜、巴基斯坦、萨尔瓦多等国代表二十三人。列席筹备会议的缅甸代表都庆纳和来北京出席四大文化名人纪念会的法国代表罗阿也应邀出席了酒会。中国方面出席作陪的有：陈叔通、李德全、南汉宸、吴耀宗、邵力子、许宝驹、许德珩、张奚若、李四光、廖承志等多人。酒会在友好气氛中进行。
    【新华社二十四日讯】来北京参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的拉丁美洲的危地马拉和尼加拉瓜的代表五人，因路程阻搁，在二十四日下午抵达北京。代表名单如下：危地马拉：教授、和平运动领袖马利欧·西尔瓦，诗人奥托·罗尔·冈萨莱斯，工会领袖、铁路机械师何塞·曼纽尔·桑切斯，女教师阿他拉·瓦伦瑞拉；尼加拉瓜：尼加拉瓜保卫国家主权委员会秘书长埃戴贝尔托·托莱斯。
