Title: Kyoto University China Research Society of Japan Prepares to Stage "Xiao Erhei's Marriage"
Author:
Date: 1952-06-10
Page: 4

    Kyoto University China Research Society of Japan
    Prepares to Stage "Xiao Erhei's Marriage"
    (Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo) The Kyoto University China Research Society of Japan, which staged Lu Xun's "The True Story of Ah Q" at the end of last year and received favorable reviews, is now preparing to stage Zhao Shuli's "Xiao Erhei's Marriage" in early June. During the spring break, this research society organized a rural cultural troupe and held mobile slide shows and seminars in several villages in the Tango area for a week.

### 日本京都大学中国研究会  准备上演“小二黑结婚”

1952-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    日本京都大学中国研究会
    准备上演“小二黑结婚”
    【新华社讯】东京消息：日本京都大学中国研究会在去年年底曾演出鲁迅的“阿Q正传”，获得好评。该研究会现又准备在六月上旬上演赵树理的“小二黑结婚”。这个研究会在春假中曾组织农村文工队，在丹后地方的几个村子里举行了流动的幻灯会和座谈会，为时一个星期。
