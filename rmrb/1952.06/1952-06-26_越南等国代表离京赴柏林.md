Title: Representatives from Vietnam and Other Countries Depart Beijing for Berlin
Author:
Date: 1952-06-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th:

    Yang Bai Lian, representative of Vietnam, S.W.R.D. Bandaranaike, representative of Ceylon, Cruz, representative of the Philippines, ? 巴色·飒顺吞, representative of Thailand, and Zeng Xuehong, representative of Malaya, who attended the preparatory meeting of the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference in Beijing, left Beijing by plane on the 25th for Moscow and then Berlin to attend the Third Session of the World Peace Council. Zhang Xiruo, Luo Longji, Xu Deheng, Xu Baoju, Wu Maosun, Meng Yu and others were at the airport to see them off.

### 越南等国代表离京赴柏林

1952-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    越南等国代表离京赴柏林
    【新华社二十五日讯】在北京出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的越南代表杨白莲，锡兰代表撒拉南卡拉，菲律宾代表柯鲁兹，泰国代表?巴色·飒顺吞，马来亚代表曾雪虹，二十五日乘飞机离京赴莫斯科转赴柏林参加世界和平理事会第三次会议。到机场欢送的有张奚若、罗隆基、许德珩、许宝驹、吴茂荪、孟雨等人。
