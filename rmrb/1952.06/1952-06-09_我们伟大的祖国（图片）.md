    Our Great Motherland
    Sericulture has a long history in our country, and it is most prevalent in the Jiangsu and Zhejiang areas. The Huxuguan Sericulture Vocational School in the Southern Jiangsu Administrative Office is a school that specializes in training sericulture talent. These are students from the Sericulture Vocational School. They feed the silkworms six times a day and night in shifts, so the silkworms grow very well.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    养蚕业在我国是有悠久历史的，在江浙一带最为盛行。苏南行署区浒墅关的蚕业专科学校是专门训练育蚕人材的一个学校。这是蚕业专科学校的学生，她们昼夜轮流喂蚕六遍，所以蚕儿生长得极好。
