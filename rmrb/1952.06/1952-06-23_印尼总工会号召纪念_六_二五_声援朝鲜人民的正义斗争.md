Title: Indonesian General Federation of Trade Unions Calls for Commemoration of "June 25th" to Support the Korean People's Just Struggle
Author:
Date: 1952-06-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 21st – The Central Committee of the Indonesian General Federation of Trade Unions recently issued a statement calling on workers across the country to hold rallies on June 25th, the second anniversary of the Korean War, to support the Korean people's struggle for independence and world peace, and demanding an immediate end to the Korean War.

### 印尼总工会号召纪念“六·二五”  声援朝鲜人民的正义斗争

1952-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼总工会号召纪念“六·二五”
    声援朝鲜人民的正义斗争
    【新华社二十一日讯】雅加达消息：印尼总工会中央委员会最近发出文告，号召全国工人于六月二十五日朝鲜战争两周年纪念日在各地举行集会，声援朝鲜人民为争取独立和世界和平而进行的斗争，并要求立即终止朝鲜战争。
