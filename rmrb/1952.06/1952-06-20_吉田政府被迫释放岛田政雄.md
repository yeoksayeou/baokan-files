Title: Yoshida Government Forced to Release Shimada Masao
Author:
Date: 1952-06-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 19th: Renowned Japanese scholar of Chinese literature and director of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Shimada Masao, was unreasonably arrested by the Yoshida government on May 3rd under the "pretext" of participating in the "May Day" demonstration. Under the support of the Japanese people and the protests of the people of the world, the Yoshida government was forced to release Shimada Masao on May 14th. Shimada Masao is now writing a book, "Introduction to Chinese Characters," and continuing his efforts to strengthen the friendship and cultural exchange between the people of China and Japan.

### 吉田政府被迫释放岛田政雄

1952-06-20
第4版()
专栏：

    吉田政府被迫释放岛田政雄
    【新华社十九日讯】东京消息：日本著名的中国文学研究家、日中友好协会理事岛田政雄，前在五月三日曾被吉田政府以参加“五一”节示威为“罪名”，无理地加以逮捕。在日本人民的声援和世界人民的抗议之下，吉田政府已被迫在五月十四日释放了岛田政雄。现在岛田政雄正在撰写“中国文字入门”一书，继续为加强中日两国人民的友好和文化交流而努力。
