Title: Emergency Notice on Drought Prevention and Replanting Issued by the North China Administrative Council
Author:
Date: 1952-06-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd: The North China Administrative Council of the Government Administration Council issued an emergency notice on drought prevention and replanting on the 21st. The notice pointed out: The drought that has occurred in some areas should be taken seriously as soon as possible. In Pingyuan Province, more than 20 million mu of wheat stubble land cannot be sown. In southern Shanxi and the Xingtai, Handan, and Shijiazhuang prefectures of Hebei Province, as well as part of the Tongxian prefecture, except for irrigated land, drought-resistant planting must be carried out. In Chahar South, parts of Yanqing, Huailai, Zhuolu, Wanquan, and Chicheng counties also have drought conditions. In Chicheng County, one thousand mu of corn has already died from drought. The notice pointed out: All areas that have shown signs of drought should immediately send people to conduct in-depth leadership, mobilize the masses to organize, and make full use of all possible water sources and water tools to carry out drought-resistant planting and seedling protection campaigns. In areas with water conservancy conditions, it is necessary to actively organize rational and economical water use to expand the area of irrigated land. The notice requires all localities to immediately organize the strength of the masses to lead drought-resistant planting. The summer planting time is very short and cannot be relaxed in the slightest.

### 华北行政委员会发出防旱抢种的紧急通知

1952-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

    华北行政委员会发出防旱抢种的紧急通知
    【新华社二十二日讯】政务院华北行政委员会在二十一日发出防旱抢种的紧急通知。通知指出：目前部分地区已发生的旱象应及早重视。平原省已有二千余万亩麦茬地不能播种。山西南部和河北省邢台、邯郸、石家庄三专区及通县专区之一部，除水地外，均须抗旱点种。察南之延庆、怀来、涿鹿、万全、赤城等县部分地区亦有旱象。赤城县有一千亩玉米已旱死。通知指出：所有已呈旱象的地区，应立即派人深入领导，发动群众组织起来，充分利用一切可能利用的水源、水具，开展抗旱点种、保苗运动。有水利条件的地区，必须积极组织合理用水，经济用水，以扩大浇地面积。通知要求各地立即组织群众力量，领导抗旱点种，夏种时间甚短，不能丝毫放松。
