Cultural Briefs

    ▲ All students of Wuhan University have launched a labor-based campus construction movement since May 28th, opening up a large playground of 35,000 square feet. Currently, more than 2,000 students from the entire university have投入 themselves into this enthusiastic and intense movement, forming a labor group for every four people, with each person laboring for three hours per week. A "Labor-Based Campus Construction Working Committee" has also been established to specifically lead this work. It is estimated that the result of this movement will save the country more than 400 million yuan. The students of the university have also incorporated the guarantee of doing a good job in this work into the patriotic pact.
    ▲ The 11th issue of "Current Affairs Handbook" has been published. The articles in this issue include "Vigorously Publicize the Agricultural Production Mutual Aid and Cooperation Movement," "Questions and Answers on the Mutual Aid and Cooperation Policy," and "How Li Shunda and Han En's Agricultural Production Cooperatives Developed." In addition, there are three international current affairs articles, one discussing the happy life of Soviet workers, and the other two explaining the recent situation of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference and the Korean armistice negotiations.
    This issue also has eleven comic strips depicting the heroic deeds of Zhang Jihui, an air force hero of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army.

### 文化简讯

1952-06-14
第3版()
专栏：文化简讯

    文化简讯
    ▲武汉大学全体同学，自五月二十八日起展开劳动建校运动，开辟一个三万五千平方尺的大操场。现全校同学两千多人均已投入这一热烈紧张的运动中，每四人成立一个劳动小组，每人每周劳动三小时。并已成立“劳动建校工作委员会”具体领导这一工作。预计这一运动的结果，将为祖国节约四亿多元。该校同学并已把搞好这一工作的保证，订入了爱国公约。
    ▲“时事手册”第十一期已经出版。这一期的文章有“大力宣传农业生产互助合作运动”、“互助合作政策问答”、“李顺达和韩恩农业生产合作社是怎样发展起来的”。此外还有三篇国际时事文章，一篇是谈苏联职工的幸福生活的，另外两篇分别解释了亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议和朝鲜停战谈判问题的最近情况。
    这一期还有十一幅连环画，描绘中国人民志愿军空军英雄张积慧的英雄事迹。
