Title: Vietnam General Confederation of Labor and Other Organizations Send Telegrams Congratulating the Preparatory Meeting for the Peace Conference; The Preparatory Meeting Continues to Receive Congratulatory Letters and Telegrams from the Chinese People
Author:
Date: 1952-06-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 6th: The Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, the Vietnam Women's Union, the Vietnam Democratic Youth League, and the Vietnam Association of Literary and Art Workers jointly sent a telegram to the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, expressing warm congratulations. The telegram pointed out the great significance of holding this conference at a time when peace in the Asian and Pacific regions is severely threatened. The telegram then said: "This peace conference marks the active struggle of the peace-loving people of the Asian and Pacific regions to defend peace, extinguish the crisis of a new war, and promote mutual understanding, cultural exchange, international trade, and the creation of happy living conditions for mankind among the people of all countries. We firmly believe that this united force is an invincible force, a force that will surely defeat the enemies of peace. The Vietnamese people warmly support this peace conference and wish it success."

Xinhua News Agency, June 6th: The preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference continues to receive many congratulatory telegrams and letters from various people's organizations and people from all walks of life in our country. These congratulatory telegrams and letters were sent by the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the headquarters of the China Democratic League, the Central Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the China People's Relief Association, the Red Cross Society of China, the Modern Buddhist Studies Society, as well as many people's organizations from major cities in the Central South and Northeast Administrative Regions, Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenyang, Wuhan, and Chengdu. All the congratulatory telegrams and letters emphasized the great significance of the conference, congratulated the conference on its success, and expressed their willingness to support all resolutions of the conference with practical actions and strive for their implementation and realization.

### 越南总工会等团体电贺和平会议筹备会议  筹备会议继续收到我国人民的贺信和贺电

1952-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    越南总工会等团体电贺和平会议筹备会议
    筹备会议继续收到我国人民的贺信和贺电
    【新华社六日讯】越南总工会、越南妇女联合会、越南民主青年联盟、越南文学艺术工作者协会联合致电亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议，表示热烈的祝贺。贺电指出在目前亚洲及太平洋区域和平已受到严重的威胁的时候召开这次会议所具有的重大意义。贺电接着说：“这次和平会议标志着酷爱和平的亚洲及太平洋区域的人民，为保卫和平、扑灭新战争的危机和增进各国人民间的互相了解、交流文化、开展国际贸易，以及为人类创造幸福的生活条件而积极斗争。我们深信这种团结的力量是不可战胜的力量，是一定会把和平的敌人击败的力量。越南人民热烈拥护这次和平会议，并祝会议成功。”
    【新华社六日讯】亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议继续收到我国各人民组织及各地各界人民的许多贺电及贺信。这些贺电及贺信是中国国民党革命委员会中央委员会、中国民主同盟总部、中国农工民主党中央委员会、中苏友好协会总会、中国人民救济总会、中国红十字会总会、现代佛学社以及中南及东北大行政区、北京、上海、广州、沈阳、武汉、成都各大城市许多人民团体发来的。所有的贺电及贺信都强调会议的重大意义，祝贺会议成功，并且表示将以实际行动，支持会议的一切决议，为其贯彻和实现而努力。
