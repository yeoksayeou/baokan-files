Title: Burmese Cultural Delegation Sends Telegram to Thank the Central People's Government of China
Author:
Date: 1952-06-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 4th: The Burmese Cultural Delegation, invited to visit our country, sent a telegram to our Central People's Government expressing their gratitude while en route back to their country. The telegram is as follows:

To the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

During their six-week visit to New China, the Burmese Cultural Delegation was pleased to visit many places of special cultural significance and witnessed many achievements of New China in various fields. We express our unspeakable, deep, and sincere gratitude to the central and local governments for their kindness and hospitality towards us. The six-week stay in New China will forever remain in our memories.

 Burmese Cultural Delegation Leader and All Members

### 缅甸文化代表团  电谢我中央人民政府

1952-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸文化代表团
    电谢我中央人民政府 【新华社四日讯】应邀前来访问我国的缅甸文化代表团顷于返国途中致电我中央人民政府表示谢意。电文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府：
    缅甸文化代表团在访问新中国的六周中，满意地参观了许多在文化方面有特殊意义的地方，并且看到了新中国在各方面的许多成就。我们对于中央及地方政府对我们的厚意和款待，表示不可言喻的、深切而诚挚的谢意。在新中国为期六周的逗留将永远留在我们记忆中。
            缅甸文化代表团团长暨全体团员
