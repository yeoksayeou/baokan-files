Author:
Date: 1952-06-12

    Our Great Motherland
    Wuyuan in Jiangxi Province is famous for its green tea production. Thanks to the vigorous efforts of the People's Government in promoting the exchange of goods between urban and rural areas, the production and sales of green tea are thriving, and the living standards of tea farmers have generally improved, which is unprecedented in the past twenty years. This is a scene of tea-picking girls in Wuyuan, Jiangxi, busily picking tea.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国江西婺源出产著名的绿茶。由于人民政府大力开展城乡物资交流工作，绿茶产销畅旺，茶农生活普遍上升，为二十年来所未有。这是江西婺源的采茶女正忙着采茶的情形。
