Title: Burmese Overseas Chinese Visiting Group Leaves Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1952-06-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, June 13th—The Burmese Overseas Chinese Visiting Group, consisting of over thirty people led by head of the group Xu Simin, deputy heads Chen Zhanmei, Huang Tiejin, and Li Yanxing, left Shanghai on the afternoon of the 11th. Deputy Secretary-General of the East China Military and Political Committee, Song Richang, and representatives from Shanghai's industrial and commercial sectors went to the station to see them off.

The Burmese Overseas Chinese Visiting Group arrived in Shanghai on the 6th after visiting construction sites in the Northeast, the Huai River Harnessing Project, and Nanjing. During their time in Shanghai, they visited the East China Inter-city and Rural Materials Exchange Conference, private factories such as Shenxin No. 9 Factory, Yong'an Printing and Dyeing Factory, Xinyi Pharmaceutical Factory, Tianchu Monosodium Glutamate Factory, and Guan Leming Fountain Pen Factory, as well as the Workers' Cultural Palace, Shanghai Middle School, the Shanghai Municipal Teachers College Affiliated Kindergarten, Women's Reformatory, department stores, and historical sites. Members of the visiting group expressed that they were deeply impressed by the protection and development that private industrial and commercial enterprises, beneficial to the national economy and people's livelihood, had received under the leadership of the People's Government.

The day before leaving Shanghai, the visiting group presented a banner to Rao Shushi, Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee.

### 缅甸华侨回国观光团离上海

1952-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸华侨回国观光团离上海
    【新华社上海十三日电】缅甸华侨回国观光团一行三十余人，在团长徐四民、副团长陈占梅、黄铁金、李雁行率领下，于十一日下午离开上海。华东军政委员会副秘书长宋日昌及上海工商界代表等曾前往车站欢送。
    缅甸华侨回国观光团是在参观了东北各地的建设、治淮工程和南京以后，于六日到达上海的。他们在上海期间，曾参观华东区城乡物资交流大会，申新九厂、永安印染厂、信谊药厂、天厨味精厂、关勒铭钢笔厂等私营工厂，工人文化宫、上海中学、上海市市立师范附属幼儿园、妇女教养所、百货公司和名胜古迹等。观光团团员们都表示对于与国计民生有利的私营工商业在人民政府领导下所得到的保护与发展，印象很深。
    观光团在离开上海的前一天，曾向华东军政委员会主席饶漱石献旗。
