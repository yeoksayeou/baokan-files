Title: Trade Union Delegations from India and Other Countries Return Home
Author:
Date: 1952-06-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 29th. The first group of Indian trade union delegates invited to China to attend the "May Day" celebrations, including the head of the delegation, Mr. Sha, members Mr. Singh and Mr. Sha's secretary Ms. Ankriss Alia, the head of the Indonesian Islamic Trade Union delegation, Mr. Kachali, and member Mr. Sunardo, and the members of the Australian trade union delegation, Mr. Baird, Mr. Gardner, and Mr. Bates, a total of eight people, arrived in Guangzhou by train on the 28th from Shanghai, and left Guangzhou by train on the morning of the 29th to return home via Hong Kong. Those who went to the station to see them off included: Lin Qiangyun, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangdong Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, Huang Minwei, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, and Secretary-General Liao Limin, Xuan Rong, Vice Chairman of the South China Regional Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Union, and others.
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 30th. The Indian trade union representatives Bora, Mitra, Chakravarty and others, a group of fourteen people who were invited to China to attend the "May Day" celebrations, arrived in Guangzhou on the 29th from Beijing, and left Guangzhou by train on the morning of the 30th to return home via Hong Kong. Those who went to the station to see them off included: Lin Qiangyun, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangdong Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, and Secretary-General Chen Nengxing, Huang Minwei, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, and Secretary-General Liao Limin, and others.

### 印度等国工会代表团返国

1952-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    印度等国工会代表团返国
    【新华社广州二十九日电】应邀前来我国参加“五一”节观礼的首批印度工会代表团团长沙氏、团员辛格和沙氏的秘书安克利丝艾丽亚，印度尼西亚回教工会代表团团长卡查利和团员苏拿尔多，澳大利亚工会代表团团员柏尔德、加德纳和贝茨等一行八人，在二十八日由上海乘车抵达广州后，已于二十九日上午乘车离广州转道香港返国。前往车站欢送的有：广东省总工会筹备委员会主任林锵云、广州市总工会筹备委员会主任黄民伟和秘书长廖立民、中国海员工会华南区委员会副主席禤荣等。
    【新华社广州三十日电】应邀前来我国参加“五一”节观礼的印度工会代表波拉、米特拉和查克拉伐第等一行十四人，于二十九日由北京抵达广州后，已在三十日上午乘车离广州转道香港返国。前往车站欢送的有：广东省总工会筹备委员会主任林锵云和秘书长陈能兴、广州市总工会筹备委员会主任黄民伟和秘书长廖立民等。
