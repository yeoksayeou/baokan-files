    Our Great Motherland
    Swimming is the main sport in summer. The All-China Sports Federation has decided to focus on promoting this sport this year and plans to hold a national swimming competition in Guangzhou in September. This is a scene of young people in the capital practicing diving at the Beijing People's Swimming Pool.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    游泳是夏季的主要体育活动。中华全国体育总会决定在今年有重点地推广这项运动，并计划于九月间在广州举行全国游泳比赛大会。这是首都的青年在北京人民游泳场练习跳水的情形。
