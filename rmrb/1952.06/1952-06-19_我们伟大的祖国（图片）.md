    Our Great Motherland
    Shanghai Workers' Political School was established to cultivate a large number of worker cadres. The school adopts a variety of educational methods to enrich the teaching content. This is a lesson for the students to learn about communist society. They are looking at pictures of Soviet communist construction.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-06-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    上海工人政治学校是为了大量培养工人干部而设立的。该校采取了多样性的教育方式，使得教学内容更加丰富。这是学员们学习共产主义社会的一课。他们正在看苏联共产主义建设的图片。
