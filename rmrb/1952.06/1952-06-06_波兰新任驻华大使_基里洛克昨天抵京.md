Title: New Polish Ambassador to China, Kirilo, Arrived in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-06-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 5th:

    New Polish Ambassador to China, Kirilo, Arrived in Beijing Yesterday
    [Xinhua News Agency, June 5th] The first Polish Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Mr. Burkin, has been recalled to his country. The Government of the Polish Republic has now appointed Kirilo as the Polish Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to our country.
    Ambassador Kirilo arrived in Beijing by plane on the 5th. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department Xu Yixin, Director of the Protocol Department Wang Zhuoru, and Deputy Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, among others, went to the airport to welcome him. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China also came to the airport to welcome him. Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy, Gura, and all members of the embassy also went to welcome him.

### 波兰新任驻华大使  基里洛克昨天抵京

1952-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰新任驻华大使
    基里洛克昨天抵京
    【新华社五日讯】波兰首任驻华特命全权大使布尔金前已奉调返国，波兰共和国政府现已任命基里洛克为波兰驻我国特命全权大使。
    基里洛克大使于五日乘飞机抵京。赴机场欢迎的有中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权、苏联东欧司副司长徐以新、交际处处长王倬如、北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等人。到机场欢迎的还有各国驻华外交使节。波兰大使馆临时代办古拉及全体馆员亦前往欢迎。
