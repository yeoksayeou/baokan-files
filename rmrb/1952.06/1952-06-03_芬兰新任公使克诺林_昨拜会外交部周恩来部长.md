Title: Finland's New Minister Kråkström Pays Courtesy Call on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-06-03
Page: 1

    Finland's New Minister Kråkström
    Pays Courtesy Call on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai Yesterday

    [Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd] Kråkström, the newly appointed Finnish Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, paid a courtesy call on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 5:00 PM on the afternoon of the 2nd to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Also present were Zhang Yue, Deputy Director of the Department of Western European and African Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 芬兰新任公使克诺林  昨拜会外交部周恩来部长

1952-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    芬兰新任公使克诺林
    昨拜会外交部周恩来部长
    【新华社二日讯】新任芬兰驻华特命全权公使克诺林于二日下午五时赴外交部拜会周恩来部长，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座者尚有西欧非洲司副司长张越、交际处处长王倬如。
