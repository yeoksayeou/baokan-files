Title: At the Request of Delegates from Various Countries Attending the International Conference for the Defense of Children, the National Women's Federation Holds a Meeting to Discuss the Situation of the Women's Movement in China
Author:
Date: 1952-06-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 31st—At the request of delegates from various countries attending the International Conference for the Defense of Children who came to China for a visit, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation held a meeting on the afternoon of the 29th to discuss the situation and experience of the Chinese women's movement. Among those attending the symposium were Vietnamese representatives Nguyen Minh Nha and Le Van Chat; Burmese representatives Daw Khin Na, and Secretary Ma Khin Yun; Australian delegation leader Madeline Ganster, representatives Marie Lewis, Dorothy Fowlette, and Les Fowlette. Also present were Indian trade union representative Yusuf, Secretary Asri, who came to China to attend the "May Day" celebrations; Australian representatives Fan Ild, Stephen Makindo, Ada Bronham, and A. Gitzelt, who attended the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference; Burmese representatives U Hla, U Myo Myint, Thakin Lwin, U Tun Pa Min, U Khun Tee, and U Aye Chi; Canadian representative Mary Jennison; Indian representative Yanik; Indonesian representative Suwati; Japanese representatives Taka Ryoko, Hosoku Kei, and Miyakoshi Kisuke; Korean representative Park Chung-ae; Pakistani representative Shakur; and American representative John Kingsbury.

The symposium was presided over by Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation. In her speech, she warmly welcomed the representatives from various countries who were fighting for the cause of defending children and defending peace, and congratulated the upcoming successful opening of the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference. Subsequently, Luo Qiong, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, introduced the situation and experience of the Chinese women's movement. She emphasized the significant contributions of Chinese women to the cause of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea and defending peace, the equal status gained by women in the new China in political, economic, cultural, educational, and social life, and the situation and experience of participating in the country's various construction undertakings on an equal footing with men.

After the introduction by Deputy Secretary-General Luo Qiong, the heads of various departments of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation answered in detail the questions raised by the representatives regarding Chinese women's organizations and women's and children's health and welfare.

American representative John Kingsbury, Canadian representative Mary Jennison, Indian representatives Yanik and Yusuf, Australian representative Madeline Ganster, Korean representative Park Chung-ae, Japanese representative Taka Ryoko, Burmese representative Daw Khin Na, Vietnamese representative Nguyen Minh Nha, Indonesian representative Suwati, Pakistani representative Shakur and others spoke at the meeting. In addition to unanimously thanking the National Women's Federation for their warm hospitality, they all believed that the Chinese women's movement had achieved great success. After returning to their countries, they were willing to report the experience of the Chinese women's movement to the women and people of their countries. In their speeches, they unanimously expressed the desire of the people and women of their countries to love peace, and their determination to unite with the people and women of Asia and the world to defend peace and the rights of women and children.

The symposium proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 应出席国际保卫儿童大会各国代表的要求  全国妇联开会座谈我国妇女运动情况

1952-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    应出席国际保卫儿童大会各国代表的要求
    全国妇联开会座谈我国妇女运动情况
    【新华社三十一日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会应来我国参观的出席国际保卫儿童大会的各国代表的要求，在二十九日午后开会座谈中国妇女运动的情况和经验。出席座谈会的有越南代表阮明雅、黎文质，缅甸代表都庆纳、秘书马庆云，澳大利亚代表团团长麦德林·甘司特尔、代表玛利·刘易士、陶乐赛·佛勒特和勒斯·佛勒特。在座的还有来我国参加“五一”节观礼的印度工会代表尤索夫、秘书阿斯里、出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的澳大利亚代表范伊尔德、斯蒂芬·马金多、亚达·布龙汉、艾·吉策尔特，缅甸代表吴拉、吴苗密、德钦卢埃、宇登帕敏、吴昆蒂、吴埃支，加拿大代表玛丽·詹尼逊，印度代表雅尼克，印度尼西亚代表苏瓦蒂，日本代表高良富、帆足计、宫腰喜助，朝鲜代表朴正爱，巴基斯坦代表霞库尔和美国代表约翰·金斯伯利等。
    座谈会由中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全主持。她在致词中热烈地欢迎到会的各国为保卫儿童保卫和平事业而奋斗的代表们，并祝贺即将开幕的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的圆满成功。继由中华全国民主妇女联合会副秘书长罗琼介绍了中国妇女运动的情况和经验。她着重说明了：中国妇女对抗美援朝、保卫和平事业的重大贡献，新中国妇女在政治上经济上文化教育上及社会生活中获得与男子平等的地位，和男子平等地参加祖国各种建设事业的情况和经验。
    罗琼副秘书长介绍以后，中华全国民主妇女联合会各部负责人又详细解答了代表们提出的有关中国妇女组织和妇女儿童卫生福利等方面的问题。
    美国代表约翰·金斯伯利、加拿大代表玛丽·詹尼逊、印度代表雅尼克和尤索夫、澳大利亚代表麦德林·甘司特尔、朝鲜代表朴正爱、日本代表高良富、缅甸代表都庆纳、越南代表阮明雅、印度尼西亚代表苏瓦蒂、巴基斯坦代表霞库尔等都在会上发言。他们除了一致感谢全国妇联对他们热烈的招待以外，都认为，中国妇女运动获得了巨大的成就，他们在回国以后，愿意把中国妇女运动的经验报告给本国的妇女和人民。他们在发言中一致地表明了本国人民和妇女热爱和平的愿望，以及和全亚洲全世界人民和妇女团结一致，保卫和平、保卫妇女儿童权利的决心。
    座谈会始终在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
