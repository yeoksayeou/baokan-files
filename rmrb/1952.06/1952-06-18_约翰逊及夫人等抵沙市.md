Title: Johnson and Wife Arrive in Shashi
Author:
Date: 1952-06-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shashi, June 17th: The Archbishop of Canterbury, Johnson, and his wife, along with Mr. Day, a lecturer on workers' education at Oxford University, arrived in Shashi by boat on the 17th. Tang Tianji, General Commander of the Jingjiang Flood Diversion Project, and more than 300 representatives from all walks of life were there to welcome them. Johnson and others will soon visit the Jingjiang Flood Diversion and the Jingjiang Dyke Reinforcement Project sites.

### 约翰逊及夫人等抵沙市

1952-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

    约翰逊及夫人等抵沙市
    【新华社沙市十七日电】英国坎特伯雷教长约翰逊及其夫人，和牛津大学工人教育问题讲师戴伊，十七日乘船抵达沙市。前往欢迎者有荆江分洪工程总指挥唐天际及各界代表三百多人。约翰逊等即将前往荆江分洪和荆江大堤加固工程工地参观。
