Our Home
—Volunteer Army Clapper Rhyme
Xī Píngqiū

\tSolid fortifications are what we dig,
\tIt is the home for our soldiers.
\tEnemy shells can't penetrate it,
\tGreat winds and heavy rain are even less to be feared.
\tThe inside is tidied up meticulously,
\tSurrounded by slogans and cartoons,
\tDoors, windows, kangs, and tables all present,
\tChairman Mao's portrait hangs in the center.
\tWork, study, and recreation,
\tMorale is high, determination is great.
\tThis kind of home is everywhere,
\tThe enemy has no way to deal with it.
\tIf they try to seize our home,
\tA burst of gunfire will kill them! (Attached picture)
\tXiāo Míng Illustration

### 我们的家——志愿军快板诗
西平秋
1952-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    我们的家
    ——志愿军快板诗
    西平秋
    坚固工事是咱挖，
    它是我们战士家。
    敌人炮弹打不透，
    大风大雨更不怕。
    里面收拾很讲究，
    四周标语和漫画，
    门窗炕桌全都有，
    毛主席像当中挂。
    工作学习又娱乐，
    情绪高涨决心大。
    这样家是到处有，
    敌人没法奈何它。
    他想强占俺的家，
    一阵枪炮揍死他。（附图片）
