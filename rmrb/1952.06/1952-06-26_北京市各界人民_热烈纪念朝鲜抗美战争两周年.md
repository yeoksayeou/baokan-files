Title: People from All Circles in Beijing Warmly Commemorate the Second Anniversary of the Korean War to Resist America

Author:

Date: 1952-06-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th: People from all circles in Beijing today warmly commemorated the second anniversary of the Korean People's War to Resist America.

    Workers from state-owned factories and enterprises such as Beijing Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant, People's Printing Factory, a certain automobile repair factory, the tram company, and the water company all held meetings in the midst of their busy schedules to commemorate the event. The workers expressed great anger at the towering crimes of U.S. imperialism in waging bacteriological warfare and its shameless acts of delaying the Korean armistice negotiations. They stated one after another that they would enthusiastically launch a production increase and economy campaign to support the Resist America, Aid Korea movement until complete victory. Many factory workers also collectively wrote letters to the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army to pay their respects and put forward solemn promises. All the workers of the Beijing Tram Company said in their letter: "In the month of June alone, the workers of the entire company found 565 tricks, increasing the nation's wealth by the value of 470 tons of grain. Now, all the workers of the company guarantee to increase the nation's wealth by the value of 6,660 tons of grain in the second half of the year." The workers of the Beijing Machine Factory wrote in their letter: "Due to the extensive trick-finding movement among all the workers, the work efficiency of the entire factory has generally increased by two to three times, and the highest has increased to thirty times." The workers of the North China Agricultural Machinery Branch Factory stated that in order to commemorate the great second anniversary of the Korean People's War to Resist America, they are determined to complete the June production plan five and a half days ahead of schedule, increasing the nation's wealth by the value of 27.5 tons of grain. The Beijing Branch of the Tianjin Railway Administration also held a special meeting to entertain the families of railway employees who volunteered to go to Korea.

    All schools in the city have adopted forms such as meetings, broadcasts, and picture exhibitions to publicize the great victory of Resist America, Aid Korea; calling on all students to study actively and prepare to participate in the construction of the great motherland. On the evening of the 24th, all the teachers and students of Peking University, and all the teachers and students of the Korean teacher and student internship group who were interning at the school, held a joint celebration. The teachers and students of the group stated one after another that they would commemorate the great second anniversary of the War to Resist America with intense study.

    Yang Zaoxin, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, gave a radio speech to the women of the city at 2:00 p.m. today, calling on women from all walks of life in the capital to unite and contribute their greatest strength in various positions to strive for the final victory in the struggle to Resist America, Aid Korea.

    Today, all Beijing newspapers published on the front page the telegrams of greetings from various democratic parties in our country to the Korean people, the Korean People's Army, and the Chinese People's Volunteers, and prominently published on the front page photos of General Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, and Commander Peng Dehuai of the Chinese People's Volunteers. Beijing People's Broadcasting Station also organized a special program to commemorate the second anniversary of the Korean People's War to Resist America.

    "Partisan Boy," a famous film produced by the Korean National Film Studio, which won the "For Freedom and Struggle" award at the 6th International Film Festival, also began showing today in thirteen first-run cinemas in the capital. The famous anti-American and aid-Korea opera "Strike the Aggressors" also continued to be performed today. Tonight, many factories, organizations, and schools held evening parties to celebrate the great victory of Resist America, Aid Korea.

### 北京市各界人民  热烈纪念朝鲜抗美战争两周年

1952-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市各界人民
    热烈纪念朝鲜抗美战争两周年
    【新华社二十五日讯】北京市各界人民今日热烈地纪念朝鲜人民抗美战争两周年。
    北京石景山钢铁厂、人民印刷厂、某汽车修配厂、电车公司、自来水公司等国营工厂企业的职工们，都在百忙中集会纪念。职工们对美帝国主义进行细菌战的滔天罪行和拖延朝鲜停战谈判的无耻行为，表示极大的愤怒，纷纷表示要热烈开展增产节约竞赛，来支援抗美援朝运动，直到彻底胜利为止。很多工厂的工人并集体写信，向中国人民志愿军及朝鲜人民军致敬，并提出了庄严的诺言。北京电车公司全体工人在他们的信中说：“仅六月份一个月内，全公司工人即找到窍门五百六十五件，为国家增产了价值四百七十吨粮食的财富。现在全公司工人又保证，在下半年再为国家增产价值六千六百六十吨粮食的财富。”北京机器厂工人们在信中写道：“由于在全体工人中开展了广泛的找窍门运动，全厂工作效率已普遍提高二倍到三倍，最高的已提高到三十倍。”华北农业机械一分厂工人们表示，为了纪念伟大的朝鲜人民抗美战争两周年，决心将六月份生产计划提早五天半完成，为国家多增产价值二十七吨半粮食的财富。天津铁路管理局北京分局并专门召开会议，招待志愿赴朝铁路员工的家属。
    全市各学校分别采取集会、广播、图片展览等形式，宣传抗美援朝伟大胜利；号召全体同学积极学习，准备参加伟大祖国的建设事业。二十四日晚上，北京大学全体师生，和正在该校实习的朝鲜教师与学生实习团全体师生联欢。该团师生纷纷表示，要以紧张的学习，来纪念伟大的抗美战争两周年。
    北京市民主妇女联合会副主席杨造新，今日下午二时，向全市妇女广播演讲，号召首都各界妇女团结起来，在各种不同的岗位上贡献自己最大的力量，争取抗美援朝斗争的最后胜利。
    今日北京各报，均以一版头条地位刊登我国各民主党派向朝鲜人民、朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军致敬的电报，并在一版显著地位刊出朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成将军，和中国人民志愿军彭德怀司令员的照片。北京人民广播电台，也为纪念朝鲜人民抗美战争两周年组织了专门的节目。
    荣获第六届国际电影节“为自由而斗争”奖的、朝鲜国立制片厂出品的名片“少年游击队”，亦于今日开始在首都十三家首轮影院上演。著名的抗美援朝歌剧“打击侵略者”也在今日继续演出。今日晚上，很多工厂、机关、学校都召开了晚会，庆祝抗美援朝的伟大胜利。
