Title: Johnson and Others Arrive in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1952-06-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, June 25th—At the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Dean of Canterbury, Hewlett Johnson, and his wife, along with Oxford University international affairs lecturer Mr. Dey, accompanied by Xu Ying, a representative from the Liaison Department of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, arrived in Shenyang on the 24th. Gao Chongmin, Chairman of the Northeast General Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Jiao Ruoyu, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, Li Du, Vice-Chairman of the Shenyang Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Sun Pengxi, Chairman of the Northeast General Assembly of the Chinese Christian Church, and others, greeted them at the station.

### 翰逊等抵沈阳

1952-06-28
第4版()
专栏：

    约翰逊等抵沈阳
    【新华社沈阳二十五日电】应邀前来我国参观的英国坎特伯雷教长约翰逊及其夫人、英国牛津大学国际问题讲师戴伊在中国人民保卫世界和平委员会联络部代表徐盈陪同下，于二十四日抵达沈阳。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会东北总分会主席高崇民、沈阳市人民政府副市长焦若愚、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会沈阳市分会副主席李都、中华基督教会东北大会理事长孙鹏翕等前往车站欢迎。
