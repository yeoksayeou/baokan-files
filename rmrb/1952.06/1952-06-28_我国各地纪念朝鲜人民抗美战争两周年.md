Title: Various Regions of China Commemorate the Second Anniversary of the Korean People's War Against American Aggression
Author:
Date: 1952-06-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency Report: To commemorate the second anniversary of the Korean People's War Against American Aggression, people in various regions have held commemorative meetings, symposiums, and other activities, and have sent telegrams or letters to express their respect to the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. People's organizations in the North China Region: The North China Sub-Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, the North China Working Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the North China Working Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, the North China Working Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the Preparatory Committee of the North China General Branch of the All-China Sports Federation, the Preparatory Committee of the North China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and other people's organizations in Hebei, Shanxi, Suiyuan provinces, representing 70 million people in North China, sent a telegram to General Kim Il-sung and General Peng Dehuai, warmly congratulating the great victory of the Korean and Chinese people in the two years of the war against American aggression, and unanimously expressed their determination to continue to strengthen the work of resisting America and aiding Korea, to continue to support the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and to strive for a rapid peaceful resolution of the Korean issue on a fair and reasonable basis.
    The Inner Mongolia Sub-Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, the Preparatory Committee of the Inner Mongolia Federation of Trade Unions, the Inner Mongolia Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, the Inner Mongolia Democratic Women's Federation, and other people's organizations, jointly sent a telegram to the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on the 25th to express their respect, congratulating them on the great victory achieved in the two years of the war against America.
    Representatives from all walks of life in East China and Shanghai held a symposium on the afternoon of the 24th to commemorate the second anniversary of the Korean People's War Against American Aggression and the "International Day of Active Solidarity with the Korean People and Defense of Peace." Among those attending the symposium were Shu Tong, Vice Chairman of the East China Sub-Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, Liu Changsheng, Chairman of the Shanghai Branch, Xia Yan and Jin Zhonghua, Vice Chairmen, standing committee members of the East China Sub-Branch and the Shanghai Branch, members of the Shanghai Municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee, representatives of local organizations of various democratic parties in Shanghai, representatives of various people's organizations in East China and Shanghai, members of the East China Branch of the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea in Shanghai, and representatives of the Shanghai Anti-American Aid Korea Volunteer Medical Surgical Team, totaling more than 80 people. At the meeting, Liu Changsheng first expressed his lofty respect to the heroic Korean people, the Korean People's Army, and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on behalf of the people of all walks of life in Shanghai, and called on the people of all walks of life in East China and Shanghai to actively carry out the patriotic production increase and economization movement and continue to support the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. Subsequently, representatives from all walks of life spoke one after another, expressing their determination to strengthen the work of resisting America and aiding Korea with practical actions. Finally, Shu Tong spoke at the meeting. He said: "Today, the people of East China are, on the basis of the victory of the 'Three-Anti' and 'Five-Anti' campaigns, focusing on carrying out the patriotic production increase and economization movement, deeply conducting patriotic education, universally promoting and consolidating patriotic pledges, carrying out the patriotic health movement, and strengthening preferential treatment work, in order to fully support the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, more heavily strike the American aggressors, until they accept a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue on a fair and reasonable basis." At the meeting, telegrams of respect were adopted to the entire commanders and fighters of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. On June 25, all newspapers in Shanghai published commentaries, commemorative articles, and photos of the heroic resistance of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army against the aggression of American imperialism in large sections.
    In the past few days, various agencies, factories, groups, and schools in Wuhan have held small-scale mass symposiums to commemorate the second anniversary of the Korean people's war against aggression. At these meetings, everyone unanimously expressed their determination to continue to strengthen the work of resisting America and aiding Korea with practical actions, and all passed telegrams of respect to the Korean people and soldiers and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. The Wuhan newspapers received a large number of letters from readers, expressing a common will in all the letters: to continue to strengthen the work of resisting America and aiding Korea and completely defeat the aggression of American imperialism! Various people's organizations and people from all walks of life in Guangzhou wrote many enthusiastic letters of sympathy on this anniversary. Cities such as Changsha, Kaifeng, Nanchang, and Nanning have held warm commemorative activities.
    On the occasion of the second anniversary of the Korean People's War Against American Aggression, people's organizations in the Northwest Region and Shaanxi Province and Xi'an City jointly sent a telegram to the Korean people and soldiers and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army to express their respect. More than 80 representatives of activists from all walks of life in Xi'an City who were resisting America and aiding Korea gathered on the 23rd to commemorate the second anniversary of "June 25th." At the meeting, the representatives exchanged experiences in doing a good job in various tasks of resisting America and aiding Korea, and decided to hold small-scale mass commemorative meetings separately to publicize the great victory of the Korean people's war against aggression over the past two years. Yang Mingxuan, Chairman of the Northwest Sub-Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, and Li Furen, Vice Chairman, called on people of all ethnic groups and all walks of life in the Northwest to carry out the production increase and economization movement and strengthen the struggle against resisting America and aiding Korea in their speeches. Also speaking at the meeting were: industrial and agricultural labor models, health workers, representatives of wounded and sick soldiers of the Volunteer Army, representatives of families of revolutionary soldiers, Henan opera actress Chang Xiangyu, model propagandists, and representatives from the literary and art circles and the business community. They unanimously stated that they would actively strengthen the work of resisting America and aiding Korea. On the 25th, warm commemorative activities were held in Xi'an, Lanzhou, and other places. Many factories in Xi'an held symposiums for their employees and used practical actions to increase production to commemorate this anti-aggression festival. On the eve of "June 25th," women cadres and activists in the Seventh District of Xi'an City visited and comforted the convalescing soldiers of the Volunteer Army.

### 我国各地纪念朝鲜人民抗美战争两周年

1952-06-28
第4版()
专栏：

    我国各地纪念朝鲜人民抗美战争两周年
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：各地人民为纪念朝鲜人民抗美战争两周年，纷纷举行纪念会、座谈会等活动，并致电或写信向朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军致敬。华北区各人民团体：华北区抗美援朝总分会、中华全国总工会华北工作委员会、中国新民主主义青年团华北工作委员会、全国民主妇女联合会华北工作委员会、中华全国体育总会华北总分会筹委会、华北区文学艺术界联合会筹委会等和河北、山西、绥远等省各人民团体，代表华北七千万人民，致电金日成将军和彭德怀将军，热烈祝贺朝中人民反对美国侵略战争两年来的伟大胜利，一致表示要继续加强抗美援朝工作，继续支援中国人民志愿军，为争取在公平合理基础上迅速地和平解决朝鲜问题而斗争。
    内蒙古抗美援朝总分会、内蒙古总工会筹委会、中国新民主主义青年团内蒙古委员会、内蒙古民主妇女联合会等人民团体，于二十五日联名致电朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军表示敬意，祝贺两年来抗美战争所取得的伟大胜利。
    华东暨上海各界人民代表于二十四日下午举行座谈会，纪念朝鲜人民抗美战争两周年及“国际积极声援朝鲜人民和保卫和平日”。出席座谈会的有华东抗美援朝总分会副主席舒同，上海分会主席刘长胜，副主席夏衍、金仲华，华东总分会和上海分会的常务委员，上海市各界人民代表会议协商委员会委员，各民主党派上海地方组织的代表，华东暨上海各人民团体的代表，中国人民赴朝慰问团华东分团在上海的团员，以及上海抗美援朝志愿医疗手术总队代表等共八十多人。会上，刘长胜首先代表上海市各界人民向英勇的朝鲜人民、朝鲜人民军及中国人民志愿军致以崇高的敬意，并号召华东和上海各界人民，积极开展爱国增产节约运动，继续支援朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军。接着，各界代表相继发言，都表示要用实际行动，加强抗美援朝工作。最后，舒同在会上讲话。他说：“今天，全华东人民正在‘三反’‘五反’胜利的基础上，以开展爱国增产节约运动为中心，深入进行爱国主义教育，普遍推进与巩固爱国公约，开展爱国卫生运动，加强优抚工作，以全力支援中国人民志愿军，更沉重地打击美国侵略者，直到它接受在公平合理的基础上和平解决朝鲜问题为止。”会上分别通过了致朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军全体指战员的致敬电。六月二十五日，上海各报都以大幅的篇幅发表了评论、纪念文章以及朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军英勇抗击美帝国主义侵略者的战迹的照片。
    武汉市各机关、工厂、团体、学校在最近几天先后举行了小规模的群众性座谈会，纪念朝鲜人民反侵略战争两周年。在这些会上，大家一致表示要以实际行动继续加强抗美援朝工作，并都通过了向朝鲜军民和中国人民志愿军的致敬电文。武汉各报收到了大批的读者来信，在所有的信中表现了一个共同意志：继续加强抗美援朝工作，彻底打败美帝国主义的侵略！广州市各人民团体和各界人民在这个纪念日中写了许多热情的慰问信。长沙、开封、南昌、南宁等城市都举行热烈的纪念活动。
    西北区和陕西省、西安市的各人民团体在朝鲜人民抗美战争两周年之际，联名发电报向朝鲜军民和中国人民志愿军致敬。西安市各界抗美援朝积极分子代表八十多人，在二十三日集会纪念“六·二五”两周年。会上，代表们交流了作好抗美援朝各项工作的经验，并决定分别召开小规模的群众性纪念会，宣传朝鲜人民反侵略战争两年来的伟大胜利。西北抗美援朝总分会主席杨明轩、副主席李敷仁分别在讲话中，号召西北各族各界人民开展增产节约运动，加强抗美援朝的斗争。在会上发言的还有：工业和农业劳动模范、卫生工作者、志愿军伤病员代表、革命军人家属代表、河南剧名演员常香玉、模范宣传员以及文艺界和工商界代表。他们一致表示：要积极加强抗美援朝工作。二十五日，西安、兰州等地都举行了热烈的纪念活动。西安市有很多工厂的职工举行了座谈会，并且用提高生产的实际行动来纪念这一反侵略的节日。西安市第七区的妇女干部和积极分子在“六·二五”前夕，慰问了志愿军休养员。
