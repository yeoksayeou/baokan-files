Title: Swedish Embassy in China Holds Reception to Celebrate Swedish King's Name Day
Author:
Date: 1952-06-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 6th. The Swedish Embassy in China held a reception on June 6th to celebrate the Name Day of Swedish King Gustaf VI.

Huang Yanpei, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government of China, and Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, were invited to attend the reception. Also invited were Zhu Xuefan, Yao Yilin, Ding Xilin, Zeng Zhaolun, and other heads of various departments of the Central People's Government of China, as well as more than one hundred guests from all walks of life.

Diplomatic envoys of various foreign countries stationed in Beijing were also invited to attend the reception.

The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 瑞典驻我国大使馆举行招待会  庆祝瑞典国王命名日

1952-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典驻我国大使馆举行招待会
    庆祝瑞典国王命名日
    【新华社六日讯】六月六日为瑞典国王葛斯脱夫六世命名日，瑞典驻我国大使馆特举行招待会，以志庆祝。
    我国中央人民政府政务院黄炎培副总理、外交部章汉夫副部长应邀出席招待会。应邀出席招待会的，还有我国中央人民政府各机关首长朱学范、姚依林、丁西林、曾昭抡等暨各界来宾一百余人。
    各外国驻北京外交使节，亦应邀出席招待会。
    招待会在友好气氛中进行。
