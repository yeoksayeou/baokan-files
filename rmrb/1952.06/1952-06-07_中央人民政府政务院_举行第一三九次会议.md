Title: The Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government Holds its 139th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-06-07
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 6th—The 139th Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 6th. The meeting heard and approved the report made by Minister of Railways of the Central People's Government, Teng Daiyuan, on the Ministry's 1951 Railway Work Summary and the tasks for 1952. The meeting also approved several appointment and removal cases and submitted several appointment and removal cases to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 中央人民政府政务院  举行第一三九次会议

1952-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府政务院
    举行第一三九次会议
    【新华社六日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百三十九次政务会议在六日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府铁道部滕代远部长所作的该部一九五一年铁路工作总结与一九五二年的任务的报告。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
