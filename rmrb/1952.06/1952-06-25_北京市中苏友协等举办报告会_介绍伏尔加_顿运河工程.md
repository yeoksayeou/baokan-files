Title: Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Others Hold Report Meeting to Introduce Volga-Don Canal Project
Author:
Date: 1952-06-25
Page: 4
    Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Others Hold Report Meeting
    Introducing the Volga-Don Canal Project
    [Report from this newspaper] The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries will hold a report meeting on the great Soviet communist construction projects at 7:30 PM on Thursday, June 26th, at the Zhongshan Park Music Hall. Comrade Shashkov, a Soviet master, will speak on "The Volga-Don Canal and Its Role in the Soviet National Economy." At that time, an exhibition of pictures of great communist construction will be held, and the film "All Soviet People Enthusiastically Participate in Great Communist Construction" will be shown. Branches of the Municipal Sino-Soviet Friendship Association can bring letters of introduction to the Propaganda Department of the Association to collect admission tickets.

### 北京市中苏友协等举办报告会  介绍伏尔加—顿运河工程

1952-06-25
第4版()
专栏：

    北京市中苏友协等举办报告会
    介绍伏尔加—顿运河工程
    【本报讯】北京市中苏友好协会和苏联对外文化协会订于本月二十六日（星期四）下午七时半在中山公园音乐堂举办苏联伟大共产主义建筑工程报告会由苏联硕士沙什柯夫同志讲：“伏尔加—顿运河及其对苏联国民经济的作用”。届时并展览伟大的共产主义建设图片及放映“全体苏联人民热烈参加伟大共产主义建设”的电影。市中苏友协所属各支会可持介绍信至该会宣传部领取入场券。
