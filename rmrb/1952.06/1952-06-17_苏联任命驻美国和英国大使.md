Title: Soviet Union Appoints Ambassadors to the United States and Britain
Author:
Date: 1952-06-17
Page: 4
TASS, Moscow, June 13th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has relieved Georgy Zarubin of his post as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to Great Britain and appointed him Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the United States.
TASS, Moscow, June 13th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has appointed Andrei Gromyko as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to Great Britain. The Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union has relieved Gromyko of his post as Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.

### 苏联任命驻美国和英国大使

1952-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联任命驻美国和英国大使
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十三日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团解除格奥尔基·查鲁宾原任苏联驻英国特命全权大使职务，任命他为苏联驻美国特命全权大使。
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十三日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命安德列·葛罗米柯为苏联驻英国特命全权大使。苏联部长会议已解除了葛罗米柯原任苏联外交部副部长的职务。
