Title: China Agrees to Export Another 100,000 Tons of Rice to India; Chinese and Indian Representatives Sign Contract in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-06-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. To enhance the friendship and cooperation between the peoples of China and India, China has agreed to sell India another 100,000 tons of rice, following the completion of the previous grain contract signed last year. On May 26th, Chinese and Indian representatives signed a contract in Beijing for China to export 100,000 tons of rice to India. Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Trade of the Central People's Government, signed on behalf of the Central People's Government, and Panikkar, the Indian Ambassador to China, signed on behalf of the Indian government. Those who attended the signing ceremony were Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Yuan Zhongxian, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to India; Chen Jiakang, Acting Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Hu Renkui, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Trade; Jiang Ming and Jia Shi, Deputy Directors of the Foreign Trade Department; and Gao Er, Counselor of the Indian Embassy in China, among others.

### 我国同意再输往印度十万吨大米  中国印度双方代表在北京签订合同

1952-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我国同意再输往印度十万吨大米
    中国印度双方代表在北京签订合同
    【新华社七日讯】为了增进中印两国人民的友谊与合作，我国继去年签订的输往印度的粮食合同执行完毕之后，又同意售给印度大米十万吨。五月二十六日，中印双方代表在北京签订了中国以十万吨大米输往印度的合同。代表我中央人民政府签字者为中央人民政府贸易部部长叶季壮，代表印度政府签字者为印度驻中国大使潘尼迦。参加签字仪式者有中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫，中华人民共和国驻印度大使袁仲贤，外交部亚洲司代司长陈家康，贸易部办公厅主任胡仁奎，国外贸易司副司长江明、贾石，印度驻华大使馆参赞高尔等多人。
