Title: Romanian Youth and Brazilian People Voice Support for the Korean People's Opposition to American Aggression
Author:
Date: 1952-06-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd, dispatch from Bucharest, TASS: Romanian youth's week of solidarity with the Korean and Chinese youth against the struggle of American aggressors began on the 18th. Throughout Romania, various youth meetings are being held in industrial enterprises, government agencies, universities, middle and primary schools, machine and tractor stations, and villages, protesting against the various crimes of the American aggressors in Korea and the atrocities of massacring Korean and Chinese prisoners of war.
    Xinhua News Agency, June 25th, dispatch from Montevideo, TASS: The Brazilian Labor People's Federation, on the second anniversary of the Korean people's heroic war against American aggressors, published a declaration to Brazilian workers in "People's Newspaper."
    The Brazilian Labor People's Federation pointed out that American interference in Korea has aroused the resolute protest of people all over the world. The Federation exposed the cruelty of the American aggressors towards prisoners of war and angrily condemned the crime of using bacteriological weapons. The declaration stated: "All these crimes are committed under the guise of the United Nations flag."
    The declaration called on the Brazilian working people to support the Korean people and demanded that the Brazilian Parliament and government instruct the Brazilian representative to the United Nations to condemn the use of bacteriological weapons and promote the immediate withdrawal of foreign troops from Korea.

### 罗马尼亚青年和巴西人民声  援朝鲜人民反对美国侵略

1952-06-26
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚青年和巴西人民
    声援朝鲜人民反对美国侵略
    【新华社二十三日讯】据塔斯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚青年声援朝鲜和中国青年反对美国侵略者斗争周已在十八日开始。罗马尼亚全国各工业企业、机关、高等学校、中小学、机器与拖拉机站和乡村，都在这一周中举行各种青年会议，抗议美国侵略者在朝鲜的种种罪行及屠杀朝中战俘的暴行。
    【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社蒙得维的亚二十四日讯：巴西劳动人民联合会在朝鲜人民对美国侵略者进行英勇战争的两周年，在“人民报”发表告巴西工人的宣言。
    巴西劳动人民联合会指出：美国干涉朝鲜引起了全世界人民的坚决抗议。该会揭露美国侵略者对战俘的残暴，并愤怒地谴责使用细菌武器的罪行。宣言说：“所有这些罪行都是在联合国旗帜的掩饰之下干出来的。”
    宣言号召巴西劳动人民声援朝鲜人民，并要求巴西议会及政府训令巴西驻联合国代表，谴责使用细菌武器及促使外国军队立即自朝鲜撤退。
