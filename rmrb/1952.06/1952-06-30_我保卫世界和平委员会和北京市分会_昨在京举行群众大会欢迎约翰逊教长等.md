Title: The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Beijing Branch Held a Mass Meeting Yesterday in Beijing to Welcome Dean Johnson and Others

Author:

Date: 1952-06-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 29th: The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and its Beijing branch jointly held a grand mass meeting at the Zhongshan Park Music Hall on the morning of June 29th to welcome Dr. Johnson, the Dean of Canterbury, England, and his wife, who were invited to visit our country as recipients of the Stalin International Peace Prize. Mr. Day, a lecturer on international issues at Oxford University in England, who came to China with Dean Johnson, was also invited to participate. More than 5,000 representatives from all walks of life in Beijing attended the welcoming meeting. At the beginning of the meeting, Zhang Xiruo, Chairman of the Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, delivered a welcoming speech. Subsequently, Dean Johnson, his wife, and Mr. Day delivered speeches in succession. They unanimously praised the great construction achievements of New China and its contribution to world peace, and expressed their willingness to tell the British people the true situation of New China that they have seen after returning home, and to make further efforts for the friendship between the Chinese and British people and for lasting world peace. The meeting ended with the masses singing "People of the World Are of One Mind" and enthusiastic applause.

### 我保卫世界和平委员会和北京市分会  昨在京举行群众大会欢迎约翰逊教长等

1952-06-30
第4版()
专栏：

    我保卫世界和平委员会和北京市分会
    昨在京举行群众大会欢迎约翰逊教长等
    【新华社二十九日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和该会北京分会于二十九日上午在中山公园音乐堂联合举行盛大的群众大会，欢迎应邀前来我国参观的斯大林国际和平奖金获得者、英国坎特伯雷教长约翰逊博士及夫人。和约翰逊教长同来中国的英国牛津大学国际问题讲师戴伊亦应邀参加。参加欢迎会的有北京各界人民代表五千余人。大会开始时，首先由中国人民保卫世界和平委员会北京分会主席张奚若致欢迎词。接着约翰逊教长及其夫人和戴伊先后发表了演说，他们一致盛赞新中国的伟大建设成就和对世界和平的贡献，并表示在回国后愿将所看到的新中国真实情况告诉英国人民，为中英两国人民的友谊和世界持久和平作进一步的努力。大会在群众高唱“全世界人民心一条”的歌声和热烈掌声中结束。
