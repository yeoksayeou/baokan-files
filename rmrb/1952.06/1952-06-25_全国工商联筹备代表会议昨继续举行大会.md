Title: Preparatory Meeting of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Continues to Hold General Session Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-06-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 24th: The preparatory representative meeting of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce continued its plenary session yesterday morning. Chen Yun, Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government, delivered a speech at the meeting (full text to be published separately). The meeting also approved a message of condolence to the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers.

### 全国工商联筹备代表会议昨继续举行大会

1952-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    全国工商联筹备代表会议昨继续举行大会
    【新华社二十四日讯】中华全国工商业联合会筹备代表会议于二十四日上午继续举行全体大会。中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会陈云主任在会上发表讲话（全文另发）。会议并通过了向朝鲜人民军、中国人民志愿军致慰问电。
