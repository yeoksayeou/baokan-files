Title: Pakistani and Japanese Representatives Return Home
Author:
Date: 1952-06-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, June 28th. Pakistani representatives Shakur and Ghulam Muhammad Khan, who attended the preparatory meeting of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, left Guangzhou on the morning of the 28th to return to their country. On the same day, Japanese representative Tomi Kora also left Guangzhou for Hong Kong. Among those who went to the station to see them off were Zhang Luchun, Vice-Chairman of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and others.

### 巴基斯坦和日本代表返国

1952-06-30
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦和日本代表返国
    【新华社广州二十八日电】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的巴基斯坦代表霞库尔和吴拉姆·穆汗默德·汗在二十八日晨离广州回国。同日，日本代表高良富也离广州到香港。前往车站欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广州分会副主席张醁村等多人。
