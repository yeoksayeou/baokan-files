Title: Hungarian National People's Art Troupe Sends Telegram to Thank the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government of China
Author:
Date: 1952-06-11
Page: 1
    [This Newspaper] The Hungarian National People's Art Troupe, which was invited to perform in our country, sent a telegram to the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government of China to express their gratitude on their way back home. The telegram is as follows: To Comrade Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China:

    As we are about to leave the border of your great and free motherland, carrying the friendship and love of the heroic Chinese people, please allow us to thank the Chinese people once again for the unforgettable joy and the many valuable experiences they have given us. These experiences will make us more enriched and stronger. We assure you that these experiences must be contributed to our people and used for the greatest cause of all mankind - defending world peace. Long live the unbreakable friendship between the Hungarian and Chinese people.
     Hungarian National People's Art Troupe
    May 29, Manzhouli

### 匈牙利国家人民文工团电谢我中央人民政府文化部

1952-06-11
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利国家人民文工团电谢我中央人民政府文化部
    【本报讯】应邀前来我国表演的匈牙利国家人民文工团，于返国途中致电我中央人民政府文化部表示谢意。电文如下：中华人民共和国文化部部长同志：
    当我们带着英勇中国人民的友谊和热爱要离开你们伟大自由祖国边境的时候，请允许我们再次感谢中国人民对我们永不能忘怀的欢愉和许多宝贵经验。这些经验将使我们更充实、更坚强。我们向您保证，这些经验一定要贡献给我国人民，并用于全人类最伟大的事业——保卫世界和平上。匈中两国人民牢不可破的友谊万岁。
     匈牙利国家人民文工团
    五月二十九日  满洲里
