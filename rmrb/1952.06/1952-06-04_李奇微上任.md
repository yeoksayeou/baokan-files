    Ridgway Takes Office
    By Wei Qimei
    The bacterial warfare criminal Ridgway, with his hands stained with blood, has taken over the post of Supreme Commander of the North Atlantic Aggression Forces from Eisenhower. When he arrived in Paris to take office, he was "greeted" by massive protest demonstrations by the French people and deafening roars of "Get out!"

### 李奇微上任
韦启美
1952-06-04
第3版()
专栏：

    李奇微上任
    韦启美　作
    满手血污的细菌战犯李奇微，接任了北大西洋侵略军总司令艾森豪威尔的职位。当他到巴黎上任时，“迎接”他的，是法国人民声势浩大的抗议示威运动和震天的“滚回去！”的怒吼声。
