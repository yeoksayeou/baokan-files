The Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government Announces the Second List of Award Recipients for Agricultural Model Production in 1951

(I) High-Yielding State-Owned Farms

The Shuangqiao Practice Farm of the Mechanized Farming School directly under the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government achieved a yield of 296 jin of seed cotton per mu on a large area of 936.5 mu of cotton fields (dry land), which is 79% higher than the local yield.

The Wulidian Farm directly under the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government achieved a yield of 215.8 jin of seed cotton per mu on a large area of 2,622 mu of cotton fields (dry land), which is 79% higher than the local yield.

The Huangfanqu Farm in Henan Province achieved a yield of 209 jin of wheat per mu on a large area of 8,450 mu of newly reclaimed wheat land, which is 50% higher than the local yield.

The Shijiazhuang State-Owned Farm in Hebei Province achieved a yield of 358 jin of seed cotton per mu on a large area of 234.03 mu of cotton fields; a yield of 276 jin of wheat per mu on a large area of 104.1 mu of wheat land; a yield of 550 jin of millet per mu on a large area of 79.3 mu of millet land; a yield of 700 jin of seed cotton per mu on 2.97 mu of high-yield cotton fields; a yield of 1,000 jin of millet per mu on 2.54 mu of high-yield millet land; and a yield of 10,000 jin of sweet potatoes per mu on 3.6 mu of spring potato land, all of which are higher than the local yield.

The State-Owned Farm of the Changsha Special Office in Hunan Province achieved a yield of 641 jin of rice per mu on a large area of 150 mu of paddy fields, which is 42% higher than the local yield.

The State-Owned Cotton Farm in Lixian County, Hunan Province, achieved a yield of 220 jin of seed cotton per mu on a large area of 87 mu of cotton fields, which is 57% higher than the local yield.

The Huairen County Farm in Chahar Province achieved a yield of 617 jin of millet per mu on 7.3 mu of high-yield millet land, which is 2.6 times higher than the local yield. The yield of other crops was also higher than the local yield.

The above seven farms are each awarded a Patriotic High-Yield Banner.
