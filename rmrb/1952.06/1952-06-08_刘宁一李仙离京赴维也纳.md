Title: Liu Ningyi and Li Xian Leave Beijing for Vienna
Author:
Date: 1952-06-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, left Beijing by plane on the 7th to attend the World Federation of Trade Unions Executive Bureau meeting to be held in Vienna from June 9th to 15th. Li Xian, Deputy Director of the Labor Protection Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, also traveled to Vienna on the same plane to participate in the International Social Protection and Insurance Conference convened by the World Federation of Trade Unions in Vienna on June 18th.

### 刘宁一李仙离京赴维也纳

1952-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    刘宁一李仙离京赴维也纳
    【新华社七日讯】中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一七日乘飞机离北京，赴维也纳出席六月九日至十五日举行的世界工会联合会执行局会议。中华全国总工会劳保部副部长李仙，同机赴维也纳参加世界工联六月十八日在维也纳召集的国际社会保护与保险会议。
