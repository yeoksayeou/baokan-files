Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-06-13
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    China's water conservancy construction is being carried out on a large scale. According to actual needs, the Ministry of Water Resources of the Central People's Government has opened water conservancy schools to train a large number of water conservancy construction talents. This is a scene of water conservancy school students participating in actual engineering surveying at a construction site in the western suburbs of Beijing.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国水利建设事业正在大规模地开展。根据实际的需要，中央人民政府水利部开办了水利学校，培养着大批水利建设人才。这是水利学校学生正在北京西郊工地参加实际工程测量的情形。
