Author:
Date: 1952-06-11

    Our Great Motherland
    After liberation, the Wusu Oil Mine in Xinjiang adopted new machinery for exploration and mining, and trained many technical personnel among various ethnic minorities. This is a group of technicians from various ethnic groups in Xinjiang managing and using the new machinery. They are assembling a new drilling rig.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-06-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新疆的乌苏油矿在解放后采用了新式的机械进行探采，并在各个少数民族中培养了许多技术人才。这是管理和使用新式机械的新疆各族的技工。他们正在装配一个新的钻井架。
