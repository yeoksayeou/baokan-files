Title: Sino-German Trade Agreement Signed
Author:
Date: 1952-06-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, May 31st: The 1952 Trade Agreement and its attachments between the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic were signed in Berlin on May 28th by Ji Pengfei, Ambassador-level Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the People's Republic of China to Germany, and Heinrich Rau, Minister of Trade of the Government of the German Democratic Republic. Attending the signing ceremony were Zhang Guangdou, Deputy Head of the Chinese Trade Negotiation Delegation, delegation members, and representatives from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the State Planning Commission, and the German issuing bank of the German Democratic Republic.

Following the signing, Minister Rau hosted a banquet that evening in honor of Head of Delegation Ji Pengfei and all members of the Chinese Trade Negotiation Delegation. During the banquet, both hosts and guests exchanged toasts. Rau said: The conclusion of the Sino-German Trade Agreement will be of great help to the German Democratic Republic's five-year economic construction plan. Ji Pengfei said: The Sino-German Trade Agreement will not only be of great help to the peaceful construction of the two peoples, but also make a great contribution to the cause of defending peace.

### 中德贸易协定签字

1952-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中德贸易协定签字
    【新华社柏林三十一日电】中华人民共和国与德意志民主共和国一九五二年贸易协定与附件，已于五月二十八日在柏林由中华人民共和国驻德外交使团大使衔团长姬鹏飞与德意志民主共和国政府贸易部部长韩克签字。参加签字仪式的有：中国贸易谈判代表团副团长张光斗与代表团团员以及德意志民主共和国外交部、国家计划委员会及德国发行银行的代表。
    签字后，韩克部长于当晚设宴招待姬鹏飞团长及中国贸易谈判代表团全体团员。席间，宾主双方互致祝辞。韩克说：中德贸易协定的缔结对于德意志民主共和国五年经济建设计划将有极大帮助。姬鹏飞说：中德贸易协定不但对于两国人民和平建设将有极大帮助，同时对于保卫和平事业也将有极大贡献。
