Title: Secretary-General of the Peace Conference Preparatory Committee: I Nominate Liu Ningyi
Author:
Date: 1952-06-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 7th

    Secretary-General of the Peace Conference Preparatory Committee
    I Nominate Liu Ningyi
    [Xinhua News Agency, June 7th] The preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference decided on the 6th to establish the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference Preparatory Committee, and decided that China would serve as Secretary-General. The Chinese Preparatory Committee has today nominated Liu Ningyi to serve as Secretary-General.

### 和平会议筹备委员会秘书长  我推定刘宁一担任

1952-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    和平会议筹备委员会秘书长
    我推定刘宁一担任
    【新华社七日讯】亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议在六日决定成立亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会，并决定由中国担任秘书长。中国筹备委员会今日已推定刘宁一担任秘书长职务。
