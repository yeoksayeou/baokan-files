Title: World Peace Council Third Session Scheduled for July 1st; Chinese Delegation Left Beijing Yesterday for Berlin to Attend the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-06-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th: The Third Session of the World Peace Council will convene in Berlin starting July 1st. The Chinese delegation attending the meeting, consisting of twenty-four members headed by Guo Moruo, departed by plane on the 25th for Moscow, en route to Berlin to participate in the session. More than fifty people were at the airport to see them off, including: Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Shen Junru, Zhang Xiruo, Luo Longji, Xu Deheng, Xu Baoju, Fan Changjiang, Wu Maosun, Cui Yueli, and others.

The list of the Chinese delegation is as follows:
Head of Delegation: Guo Moruo.
Council Members: Ma Yinchu, Cai Tingkai, Zhang Bojun, Mei Ru'ao, Wu Yaozong, Chen Hansheng, Xiao San, Li Yimang, Liu Ningyi. (The latter three are abroad).
Special Invited Representatives: Yang Daoxin (worker), Guo Fang (farmer), Rong Yiren, Deng Wenzhao (representing industry and commerce), Fang Shishan (medical expert), Liu Chongle (entomology expert).
Secretary-General: Liu Guanyi.
Deputy Secretaries-General: Zhang Xichou, Chen Dingmin.
Staff: Liang Zhihong, Deng Ziruo, Gu Cui, Li Yuying, Chen Sujuan, Liu Yunying, Wang Wenyi, Pang Qiuying.

### 世界和平理事会第三次会议定七月一日举行  我国代表团昨天离北京赴柏林参加会议

1952-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会第三次会议定七月一日举行
    我国代表团昨天离北京赴柏林参加会议
    【新华社二十五日讯】世界和平理事会第三次会议将于七月一日起在柏林开会，出席会议的我国代表团一行二十四人，由团长郭沫若率领，在二十五日乘飞机赴莫斯科转往柏林参加会议。到机场欢送的有：彭真、陈叔通、沈钧儒、张奚若、罗隆基、许德珩、许宝驹、范长江、吴茂荪、崔月犁等五十多人。
    我国代表团名单团长：郭沫若。理事：马寅初、蔡廷锴、章伯钧、梅汝璈、吴耀宗、陈翰笙、萧三、李一氓、刘宁一。（后三人在国外）特邀代表：杨道新（工人）、郭芳（农民）、荣毅仁、邓文钊（以上工商界）、方石珊（医学专家）、刘崇乐（昆虫学专家）。秘书长：刘贯一。副秘书长、张锡俦、陈定民。工作人员：梁志宏、邓子若、顾萃、李玉莺、陈苏娟、刘芸影、王文仪、庞秋英。
