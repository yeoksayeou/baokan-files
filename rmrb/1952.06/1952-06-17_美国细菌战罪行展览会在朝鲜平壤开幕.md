Title: Exhibition of American Bacterial Warfare Crimes Opens in Pyongyang, Korea
Author:
Date: 1952-06-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 16th: TASS, Pyongyang, June 13th: An exhibition exposing the crimes of American imperialism in conducting bacteriological warfare in Korea has opened in Pyongyang. The exhibition also reflects the heroic struggle of the Korean people against American bacteriological weapons. Li Bingnan, Minister of Public Health of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, pointed out in his opening speech that the photographs, documents, and material evidence displayed at the exhibition expose the towering crimes committed by the American interventionists against humanity and prove that the United States is using Korea as an experimental ground for the use of bacteriological weapons. Government officials, members of various diplomatic missions, Korean and foreign journalists who attended the opening ceremony visited the exhibition with great attention.

### 美国细菌战罪行展览会在朝鲜平壤开幕

1952-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    美国细菌战罪行展览会在朝鲜平壤开幕
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社平壤十三日讯：揭发美帝国主义在朝鲜进行细菌战罪行的展览会在平壤开幕。展览会也反映了朝鲜人民与美国细菌武器进行英勇斗争的情形。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国保健相李炳南在开幕词中指出：展览会陈列的照片、文件与物证揭露了美国干涉者对人类所犯的滔天罪行，同时证明美国利用朝鲜作为使用细菌武器的实验地。参加开幕礼的政府官员、各外交使团人员、朝鲜的和外国的记者们，都以极大的注意参观了展览会。
