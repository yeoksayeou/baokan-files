Title: Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference Preparatory Meeting Continues; Representatives from Various Countries Speak, Expressing People's Desire for Peace; Thai Representative to Preparatory Meeting Arrives in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-06-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 4th—The preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference continued on the 4th. Today, U Thein Maung from Burma reported and attended the meeting as an observer.

	In the morning session, the Chilean representative, Manuel Venturelli, served as chairman, and the Chinese representative, Chen Shutong, served as vice-chairman. Representatives who spoke at the meeting included: Rewi Alley from New Zealand, S.W.R.D. Bandaranaike from Ceylon, and U Hla from Burma.

	After a recess, the Indonesian representative, Soewati, served as chairman, and the Japanese representative, Tomi Kora, served as vice-chairman. Representatives who spoke at the meeting included: Suyono Sukonagoro from Indonesia, Hermenegildo Cruz from the Philippines, and Tseng Hsueh-hung from Malaya.

	Before the morning session adjourned, Liu Ningyi, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Meeting, announced that the Preparatory Meeting had received many congratulatory letters and telegrams from Chinese people's organizations—All-China Federation of Trade Unions, All-China Democratic Women's Federation, All-China Democratic Youth Federation, All-China Students' Federation, All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, Preparatory Office of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Christian Anti-America Aid-Korea Three-Self Reform Movement Committee—as well as from people in various regions. The representatives applauded in gratitude for the enthusiasm expressed in these congratulatory letters and telegrams.

	In the afternoon session, the Korean representative, Pak Jeong-ae, served as chairman, and the Malayan representative, Tseng Hsueh-hung, served as vice-chairman. Representatives who spoke at the meeting included: Tomi Kora from Japan, Bolton from Australia, and Gortari from Mexico.

	After a recess, the Mexican representative, Gortari, served as chairman, and the Mongolian representative, Shirindib, served as vice-chairman. Representatives who spoke at the meeting included: Kozhevnikov from the Soviet Union, Shirindib from Mongolia, and Pak Jeong-ae from Korea.

	The representatives from various countries who spoke at the meeting recounted the development of peace movements in their respective countries and the ardent desires for peace held by the broad masses of people.

	Before the meeting adjourned, Liu Ningyi, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Meeting, read out congratulatory telegrams to the Preparatory Meeting from Jean Lafitte, Secretary-General of the World Peace Council, and the Ecuadorian Peace Committee. The representatives applauded warmly.

	The meeting concluded at this point. The drafting committee for the declaration and the organizing committee continued to meet that evening.

	Xinhua News Agency, June 4th—The Thai representative to the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, Prasert Sapsunthorn, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 4th. Additionally, Sawai Thurarat, a Thai national who attended the preparatory meeting on the 3rd and 4th, has been appointed as the official representative of Thailand to the preparatory meeting by telegram from the Thai Peace Committee. To date, in addition to the two secretaries from the Secretariat of the World Peace Council and the two members of the Asian-Australasian Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions, there are forty-seven representatives from twenty countries in Asia, Australia, and the Americas along the Pacific coast attending the preparatory meeting.

### 亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议继续开会  各国代表发言表达人民和平愿望  泰国出席筹备会议代表昨天到达北京

1952-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

    亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议继续开会
    各国代表发言表达人民和平愿望
    泰国出席筹备会议代表昨天到达北京
    【新华社四日讯】亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议四日继续开会。今日有缅甸的都庆纳报到列席会议。
    上午会议开始时，智利代表万徒勒里担任主席，中国代表陈叔通担任副主席。在会上先后发言的有：新西兰代表艾黎，锡兰代表撒拉南卡拉和缅甸代表吴拉。
    会议经休息后，印度尼西亚代表苏瓦蒂任主席，日本代表高良富任副主席。在会上先后发言的有：印度尼西亚代表苏约尼哥罗，菲律宾代表柯鲁兹，马来亚代表曾雪虹。
    在上午会议休会前，筹备会议秘书长刘宁一宣布：筹备会议收到了中国各人民团体——中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中华全国民主青年联合总会、中华全国学生联合会、中华全国文学艺术界联合会、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、中华全国科学技术普及协会、中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会筹备处、中国基督教抗美援朝三自革新运动委员会筹备委员会，以及各地人民的许多贺信及贺电。代表们对这些贺信、贺电所表现的热忱鼓掌表示感谢。
    下午会议开始时，朝鲜代表朴正爱任主席，马来亚代表曾雪虹任副主席。在会上先后发言的有：日本代表高良富，澳大利亚代表博尔顿和墨西哥代表哥尔达利。
    会议经休息后，墨西哥代表哥尔达利任主席，蒙古代表锡林迪布任副主席。在会上先后发言的有：苏联代表科热夫尼科夫，蒙古代表锡林迪布，朝鲜代表朴正爱。
    在会议上发言的各国代表，都讲述了各国和平运动发展的情况和各国广大人民对于和平的殷切愿望。
    在会议休会前，筹备会议秘书长刘宁一宣读了世界和平理事会总书记拉斐德和厄瓜多尔和平委员会致筹备会议的贺电。代表们热烈鼓掌。
    今日会议至此结束。宣言起草委员会和组织委员会当晚继续开会。
    【新华社四日讯】泰国出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的代表?巴色·飒顺吞于四日下午到达北京。又：三、四两日列席筹备会议的泰国人士?沙愿·都拉叻已由泰国和平委员会来电委派为泰国出席筹备会议的正式代表。至此，出席筹备会议的除世界和平理事会书记处秘书二人、世界工联亚澳联络局委员二人外，共有亚、澳、美各洲太平洋沿岸二十个国家的四十七个代表。
