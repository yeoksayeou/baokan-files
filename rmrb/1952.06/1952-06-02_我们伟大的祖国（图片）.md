Our Great Motherland
Women of the Ninth Agricultural Team of the Huachuan Water Conservancy Farm have played a great role in production. Last year, the harvest from the women's labor accounted for one-third of the total harvest of the team. Now the women of the farm are even more enthusiastic about production, and they are discussing this year's production plan.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    桦川水利农场第九农庄的妇女，在生产中起了很大的作用。去年全庄妇女劳动的收获，占了全部收获量的三分之一。现在农庄的妇女们生产情绪更加高涨，她们正在讨论今年的生产计划。
