Title: Representatives from Burma, Indonesia, and Other Countries Return Home
Author:
Date: 1952-06-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, June 26th: Burmese representatives U Mya Myint, U Khun Tee, and U Aye Kyi, who attended the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, and the head of the Australian delegation, Madeline Ganster, representatives Mary Lewis, Dorothy Follett, Les Follett, and Burmese delegation secretary Ma Khin Yun, a total of eight people, who attended the International Conference for the Defence of Children and then visited our country, left Guangzhou on the 26th to return home. Among those who went to the station to see them off were Du Guoxiang, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Zhang Lucun, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Su Hui, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangdong Democratic Women's Federation; Yu Hui, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Democratic Women's Federation, and dozens of others.

Xinhua News Agency: U Hla, Burmese representative, Suyono Cokro, Indonesian representative, Kozhevnikov and Grusenko, Soviet representatives, Secretary Qiubalov, and Guryayev, Secretary of the Secretariat of the World Peace Council, who came to Beijing to attend the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 18th to return home. Among those who went to the airport to see them off were Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Shen Junru, Standing Committee Member, Cai Tingkai, Chinese Council Member of the World Peace Council, and many others.

### 缅甸印尼等国代表返国

1952-06-28
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸印尼等国代表返国
    【新华社广州二十六日电】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的缅甸代表吴苗密、吴昆蒂、吴埃支和出席国际保卫儿童大会后来我国参观的澳大利亚代表团团长麦德林·甘司特尔，代表玛利·刘易士，陶乐赛·佛勒特，勒斯·佛勒特，缅甸代表团秘书马庆云等八人，二十六日离广州返国。前往车站欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广东分会副主席杜国庠、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广州分会副主席张醁村、广东省民主妇女联合会筹备会副主任苏蕙、广州市民主妇女联合会筹备会副主任余慧等数十人。
    【新华社讯】来北京出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的缅甸代表吴拉，印度尼西亚代表苏约尼哥罗，苏联代表科热夫尼科夫、格鲁森科、秘书邱巴洛夫，世界和平理事会书记处秘书顾里亚耶夫等，已于十八日上午乘飞机离北京回国。前往机场欢送的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通、常务委员沈钧儒，世界和平理事会中国理事蔡廷锴等多人。
