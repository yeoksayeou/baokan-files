Title: Burmese Trade Union Delegation Members Return Home
Author:
Date: 1952-06-06
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 5th. U Shan Din and Tekin Dong, members of the Burmese Trade Union Delegation who were invited to our country to participate in the “May Day” celebrations, arrived in Guangzhou by train on the 4th from Beijing, and have already left Guangzhou by train on the 5th via Hong Kong to return home. Among those who went to the station to see them off were: Lin Qiangyun, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangdong Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, and Chen Nengxing, Secretary-General; Huang Minwei, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, and Liao Limin, Secretary-General, etc.

### 缅甸工会代表团二团员返国

1952-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸工会代表团二团员返国
    【新华社广州五日电】应邀前来我国参加“五一”节观礼的缅甸工会代表团团员宇山登和德钦东二人于四日由北京乘车到达广州后，已在五日乘车离广州转道香港返国。前往车站欢送的有：广东省总工会筹备委员会主任林锵云和秘书长陈能兴、广州市总工会筹备委员会主任黄民伟和秘书长廖立民等。
