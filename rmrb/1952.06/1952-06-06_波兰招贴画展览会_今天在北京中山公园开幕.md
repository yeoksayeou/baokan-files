Title: "Exhibition of Polish Posters" Opens Today in Zhongshan Park, Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-06-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Report:

    "Exhibition of Polish Posters"
    Opens Today in Zhongshan Park, Beijing

    [Xinhua News Agency] The "Exhibition of Polish Posters," sponsored by the Bureau of External Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government, will open on the 6th in the Water Pavilion of Zhongshan Park. A preview reception was held on the 5th. Those present at the reception included Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of External Cultural Relations; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Zou Difan, Director of the Office of the Bureau of External Cultural Relations; Jiang Feng, Vice President of the Central Academy of Fine Arts; Cai Ruohong, Deputy Director of the Bureau of Art Administration of the Ministry of Culture; as well as painters Wang Chaowen, Ye Qianyu, Lang Ding, Jiang Zhaohe, Yan Han, Li Keran, Dong Xiwen, Fang Cheng, Zhong Ling, and more than sixty others. The Polish Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Gula, news commissioner Mr. and Mrs. Luo Buman, and embassy staff also attended the reception.
    The exhibits include more than sixty works by Polish poster artists, depicting the Polish people's struggle for peace, for the completion of the Six-Year Plan, and against imperialism, and reflecting the life of the Polish people. The exhibition will be open to the public from the 6th to the 20th.

### “波兰招贴画展览会”  今天在北京中山公园开幕

1952-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    “波兰招贴画展览会”
    今天在北京中山公园开幕
    【新华社讯】中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局主办的“波兰招贴画展览会”，定六日起在中山公园水榭开幕。五日举行预展招待会，出席招待会的有对外文化联络事务局局长洪深、外交部交际处处长王倬如、对外文化联络事务局办公室主任邹荻帆、中央美术学院副院长江丰、文化部艺术事业管理局副局长蔡若虹，以及画家王朝闻、叶浅予、浪仃、蒋兆和、彦涵、李可染、董希文、方成、钟灵等六十多人，波兰驻华大使馆临时代办古拉、新闻专员罗步曼夫妇及大使馆人员也出席了招待会。
    展览品内容包括波兰招贴画作家的作品六十多幅，表现了波兰人民为和平、为完成六年计划及为反对帝国主义而斗争的情况，并反映了波兰人民的生活。展览会将从六日到二十日公开展出。
