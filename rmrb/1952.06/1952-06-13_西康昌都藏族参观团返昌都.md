Title: Xikang Qamdo Tibetan Delegation Returns to Qamdo
Author:
Date: 1952-06-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, June 9th. The Tibetan delegation from the Qamdo region of Xikang Province, including the Qamdo Lamasery's Sewala Living Buddha and Chaya Zong Kanggu Living Buddha, totaling more than 30 people, has returned to Qamdo on June 5th after visiting Beijing, Shanghai, and other places for over 50 days. During the delegation's stay in Chongqing, the Ethnic Affairs Committee of the Southwest Military and Political Committee invited them to a discussion. The Southwest Military and Political Committee, the Southwest Military Region, and the Chongqing Municipal People's Government jointly hosted a banquet for all members of the delegation. He Long, Commander of the Southwest Military Region, and Deng Xiaoping, Xiong Kewu, and Liu Wenhui, Vice Chairmen of the Southwest Military and Political Committee, and Cao Diqiu, Mayor of Chongqing, personally attended. At the banquet, Deng Xiaoping encouraged the representatives to continue to devote themselves to the construction of the Qamdo region. The members of the delegation all expressed that after returning, they would convey to the local people of all ethnic groups the great achievements and moving facts they witnessed during their visit regarding the various construction projects in the interior over the past two years.

### 西康昌都藏族参观团返昌都

1952-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

    西康昌都藏族参观团返昌都
    【新华社重庆九日电】西康省昌都地区藏族参观团昌都喇嘛寺谢瓦拉活佛、察雅宗康古活佛等一行三十多人，五十多天来在北京、上海等地参观后，已在六月五日离重庆返昌都。参观团在重庆期间，西南军政委员会民族事务委员会曾邀请他们举行座谈；西南军政委员会、西南军区及重庆市人民政府并联合欢宴参观团全体团员，西南军区司令员贺龙和西南军政委员会副主席邓小平、熊克武、刘文辉，重庆市市长曹荻秋都亲自到会。邓小平在宴会上勉励代表们继续致力于昌都地区的建设工作。团员们都表示回去以后要把在参观中亲眼看到的内地两年来各项建设上的伟大成就和各种动人事实向当地各族人民传达。
