Title: China's Democratic Parties and Non-Party Democrats Jointly Protest the French Government's Arrest of Duclos
Author:
Date: 1952-06-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 6th:

    China's democratic parties and non-party democrats have jointly sent a telegram to the French Prime Minister, protesting the French government's illegal arrest of the French people's leader, Duclos. The telegram reads as follows:
    To Mr. Binet, Prime Minister of France:
    We express our utmost anger and protest against the French government's illegal arrest of Mr. Duclos, leader of the French people, Mr. Stie, editor-in-chief of "L'Humanité," and other patriotic peace fighters. The just action of the French people led by Mr. Duclos and others against the bacteriological warfare criminal Ridgway, who slaughtered the Korean people, is the common demand of all peace-loving people in the world. China's democratic parties and non-party democrats unanimously support it and demand that you immediately restore the freedom of Mr. Duclos, Mr. Stie, and all other arrested patriotic peace fighters, and stop the oppression of the French people's patriotic movement.
                 Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang
                 China Democratic League
                 China National Construction Association
                 Non-Party Democrats of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
                 China Democratic Promotion Association
                 Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party
                 China Zhi Gong Party
                 Jiusan Society
                 Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League
                 Chinese New Democratic Youth League
                 June 6, 1952

### 我国各民主党派及无党派民主人士  联合抗议法国政府逮捕杜克洛

1952-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我国各民主党派及无党派民主人士
    联合抗议法国政府逮捕杜克洛
    【新华社六日讯】中国各民主党派及无党派民主人士联合致电法国总理，抗议法国政府非法逮捕法国人民领袖杜克洛。电文如下：法国总理比内先生：
    我们对于法国政府非法逮捕法国人民领袖杜克洛先生、“人道报”总编辑斯梯先生及其他爱国和平战士表示无比的愤怒与抗议。杜克洛先生等领导的法国人民反对屠杀朝鲜人民的细菌战犯李奇微的正义行动是全世界一切爱好和平人士的共同要求，中国各民主党派及无党派民主人士予以一致声援，并要求你们立刻恢复杜克洛先生、斯梯先生及其他一切被捕的爱国和平人士的自由，停止对于法国人民爱国运动的压迫。
                 中国国民党革命委员会
                 中国民主同盟
                 民主建国会
                 中国人民政协无党派民主人士
                 中国民主促进会
                 中国农工民主党
                 中国致公党
                 九三学社
                 台湾民主自治同盟
                 中国新民主主义青年团
                 一九五二年六月六日
