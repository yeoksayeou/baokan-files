Title: "Great Motherland" Special Stamps Scheduled for Release on "July 1st" This Year
Author: 
Date: 1952-06-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: To commemorate the 31st anniversary of the Communist Party of China, the first set of "Great Motherland" special stamps will be issued on "July 1st." The first set of stamps comprises four types, featuring the famous Dunhuang murals of our country as the stamp designs: One, "Hunting" - Wei (tea brown); Two, "Donor" - Sui (brown); Three, "Apsaras" - Tang (Prussian blue); Four, "Dragon" - Tang (celadon). The border decorations of the stamp designs are selected from mural border decorations of the same era, and the face value is all eight hundred yuan. According to reports, the designs for the "Great Motherland" special stamps all take the cultural relics, art, production, and construction of our country as their theme.

### “伟大的祖国”特种邮票定今年“七一”开始发行

1952-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

    “伟大的祖国”特种邮票定今年“七一”开始发行
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：为纪念中国共产党三十一周年，“伟大的祖国”特种邮票第一组将在“七一”开始发行。第一组邮票计四种，以我国著名的敦煌壁画为票面图案，计印有一、“狩猎”——魏（茶褐色）；二、“供养人”——隋（棕色）；三、“飞天”——唐（普蓝色）；四、“龙”——唐（青莲色）。邮票图案的边饰是选自同时代的壁画边饰，面值都是八百元。按“伟大的祖国”特种邮票的图案，都是以我国的文物、艺术、生产、建设为主题。
