Title: Beijing Literary and Art Circles Gather to Commemorate the Sixteenth Anniversary of Gorky's Death
Author:
Date: 1952-06-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 18th: Beijing's literary and art circles held a memorial meeting on June 18th to commemorate the sixteenth anniversary of the death of the great Soviet revolutionary writer Maxim Gorky. More than 700 literary and art workers participated in the meeting.

The meeting was opened by Wang Yaping, Secretary-General of the Beijing Federation of Literary and Art Workers. He called on Beijing's literary and art workers to learn Gorky's revolutionary realist method of creation to overcome the shortcomings of formalism and conceptualization in their creations.

Professor Cao Jinghua then delivered a report entitled "Gorky the Warrior." The report focused on analyzing the great impact of Gorky's works on Chinese new literature and the Chinese people.

The film "My Universities," based on Gorky's writings, was also screened at the meeting.

In addition, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries jointly held a memorial meeting on the evening of the 18th to commemorate the sixteenth anniversary of Gorky's death. Soviet Professor Vinogradova from the Renmin University of China gave a report to the attendees entitled "Gorky - Creator of Soviet Drama."

### 北京市文学艺术界集会纪念高尔基逝世十六周年

1952-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

    北京市文学艺术界集会纪念高尔基逝世十六周年
    【新华社十八日讯】北京市文学艺术界十八日举行苏联伟大革命文学家高尔基逝世十六周年纪念大会。参加大会的有文艺工作者七百多人。
    大会由北京市文学艺术工作者联合会秘书长王亚平致开会词。他号召北京市文艺工作者学习高尔基革命现实主义的创作方法，以克服创作中公式化、概念化的缺点。
    接着由曹靖华教授在会上作了题为“战士高尔基”的报告。报告着重地分析了高尔基的作品对中国新文艺和中国人民所起的巨大影响。
    会上还放映了根据高尔基的著作摄制的影片“我的大学”。
    此外，北京市中苏友好协会和苏联对外文化协会也联合在十八日晚举行了高尔基逝世十六周年纪念会，由中国人民大学苏联教授维娜格拉多娃向与会者作了题为“高尔基——苏联戏剧的创造者”的报告。
