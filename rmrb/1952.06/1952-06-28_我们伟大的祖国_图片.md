Author:
Date: 1952-06-28
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The entire railway track laying, bridge, and tunnel construction of the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway have been completed. Efforts are being made to open it to traffic on July 1st as a gift to the 31st anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China. This is the Wang'erxi Bridge near Ziyang on the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway. The bridge is 300 meters long, with 22 arches, and is the largest stone arch bridge on the entire line.

### 我们伟大的祖国(图片)

1952-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    成渝铁路全线铺轨和桥梁、隧道工程都已完成，争取在七月一日通车，作为给中国共产党三十一周年诞辰的献礼。这是成渝铁路上资阳附近的王二溪桥，桥长三百公尺，有二十二孔，是全线最大的石拱桥。
