Title: Kosambi and Janik Return to India Successively
Author:
Date: 1952-06-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, June 18th: The chief representative of the Indian delegation to the Preparatory Meeting of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, Kosambi, left Guangzhou by train this morning to return to his country. Among those who went to the station to see him off were Du Guoxiang, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Zhang Lucun, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and many others.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, June 14th: Janik, the Indian representative attending the Preparatory Meeting of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, and Yousef, the Indian trade union representative invited to participate in our May Day celebrations, and his party, left Guangzhou by train this morning via Hong Kong to return to their country. Among those who went to the station to see them off were Du Guoxiang, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Zhang Lucun, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Lin Qiangyun, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangdong Federation of Trade Unions, and Huang Minwei, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Federation of Trade Unions, and many others.

### 高善必和雅尼克先后返印度

1952-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

    高善必和雅尼克先后返印度
    【新华社广州十八日电】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的印度代表团首席代表高善必，十八日晨乘火车离广州返国。前往车站欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广东分会副主席杜国庠、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广州分会副主席张醁村等多人。
    【新华社广州十四日电】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的印度代表雅尼克、应邀参加我国五一节庆祝典礼的印度工会代表尤索夫等一行，十四日晨乘火车离广州转道香港回国。前往车站送行的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广东省分会副主席杜国庠、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广州市分会副主席张醁村、广东省总工会筹备委员会主任林锵云和广州市总工会筹备会主任黄民伟等多人。
