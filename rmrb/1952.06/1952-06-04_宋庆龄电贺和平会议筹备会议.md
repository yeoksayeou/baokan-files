Title: Soong Ching-ling Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Preparatory Meeting for Peace Conference
Author: 
Date: 1952-06-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd—Soong Ching-ling, one of the Chinese sponsors of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, sent a telegram on the 3rd to the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference being held in Beijing to express her congratulations. The telegram is as follows:
To Mr. Guo Moruo in Beijing, and to all the representatives attending the preparatory meeting:
    The convening of the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference marks the beginning of a great and unified peace movement in the Asian and Pacific Regions. This good beginning will further mobilize peace-loving people to take on the responsibility of safeguarding the cause of world peace.
    I sincerely wish the conference success, and may we achieve brilliant victories in the struggle to defend the peace movement.
                                   Soong Ching-ling, Shanghai, June 3rd

### 宋庆龄电贺和平会议筹备会议

1952-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄电贺和平会议筹备会议
    【新华社三日讯】亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议中国发起人之一宋庆龄三日致电在北京召开的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议表示祝贺，电文如下：北京郭沫若先生并转出席筹备会议的各位代表：
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的召开，标志着亚洲及太平洋区域的伟大而统一的和平运动的开端。这一良好的开端必将使爱好和平的人民更进一步动员起来担当保卫世界和平事业的责任。
    谨祝会议的成功，并愿我们在保卫和平运动的斗争中取得辉煌的胜利。
                                   宋庆龄六月三日于上海
