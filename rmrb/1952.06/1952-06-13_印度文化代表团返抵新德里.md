Title: Indian Cultural Delegation Returns to New Delhi
Author:
Date: 1952-06-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 12th: The Indian Cultural Delegation returned to the Indian capital, New Delhi, on the evening of June 7th by plane after visiting and touring various parts of the People's Republic of China for five weeks.

At Palam Airport, the delegation was welcomed by Chopra, Director of Protocol of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, other senior Indian government officials, and members of the Indian Parliament. Shen Jian, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in India, also welcomed them at the airport.

Mrs. Pandit, head of the delegation, stated at the airport: "We are returning home today after traveling in China for five weeks. During these weeks, we gained various experiences and rich, pleasant memories.

We cannot express in adequate words how grateful we are to the Chinese government and people for the warm hospitality and friendship they extended to us everywhere. We are very gratified to find that the ties that once bound our two countries together are still very strong today. We hope that this existing friendship will continue to flourish and become a factor in strengthening peace in Asia and the world."

She said that the delegation saw "the high degree of creative spirit in New China and the vigorous vitality that inspires the Chinese people, which greatly moved us."

Chargé d'Affaires Shen Jian held a reception on the evening of June 8th to entertain the Indian Cultural Delegation. Those who attended the reception included: Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan, Prime Minister Nehru, as well as members of the Indian Parliament, prominent Indian cultural and educational workers, and members of various foreign diplomatic missions in India.

Members of the delegation and guests attending the reception spoke enthusiastically about life in New China.

### 印度文化代表团返抵新德里

1952-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    印度文化代表团返抵新德里
    【新华社新德里十二日电】印度文化代表团在中华人民共和国各地访问参观五个星期以后，已在六月七日晚乘飞机回到印度首都新德里。
    在巴拉摩机场欢迎代表团的有印度政府外交部交际司司长乔普拉和其他印度高级政府官员以及印度国会议员等。我国驻印度大使馆代办申健亦到机场欢迎。
    代表团团长潘迪特夫人在机场发表声明说：“我们在中国游历五个星期以后今天回国了。在这几个星期中，我们得到了种种经验和丰富的愉快的记忆。
    我们无法用适当的言语来表达我们是多么感谢中国政府和人民到处给我们的殷勤招待和友情。我们发现过去把我们两个国家结连在一起的联系，在今天仍然是十分坚固的，这使我们非常欣慰。我们希望这种现存的友谊继续发扬光大，并成为一个有助于加强亚洲和世界和平的因素。”
    她说：代表团看到了“新中国的高度的创造精神以及鼓舞中国人民的蓬勃活力，这种精神和活力使我们大为感动”。
    申健代办在六月八日晚举行招待会，招待印度文化代表团。参加招待会的有：印度共和国副总统拉德哈克里斯南、总理尼赫鲁以及印度国会议员、印度著名文化工作者和教育工作者、各国驻印度外交使团人员等。
    代表团团员和参加招待会的来宾们畅谈了新中国的生活。
