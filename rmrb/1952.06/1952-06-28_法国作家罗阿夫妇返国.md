Title: French Writer Loah and Wife Return Home
Author:
Date: 1952-06-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 27th: French writer Loah and his wife, who were invited to our country to attend the Commemorative Conference of World's Four Great Cultural Celebrities, left Beijing by plane on the 27th to return home. Among those who saw them off at the airport were Wu Maosun, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Meng Yu, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department, and poet Yuan Shuipai, among others.

### 法国作家罗阿夫妇返国

1952-06-28
第4版()
专栏：

    法国作家罗阿夫妇返国
   【新华社二十七日讯】应邀来我国参加世界四大文化名人纪念大会的法国作家罗阿及其夫人，二十七日乘飞机离北京返国。到机场送行的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副秘书长吴茂荪、联络部副部长孟雨，以及诗人袁水拍等多人。
