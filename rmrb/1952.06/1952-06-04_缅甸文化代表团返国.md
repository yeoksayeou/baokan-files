Title: Burmese Cultural Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1952-06-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 3rd -- The Burmese Cultural Delegation of fifteen people, headed by U Tun Pe, accompanied by Chen Ying, a specialist from the Asian Department of our Central People's Government's Ministry of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Guangzhou from Shanghai on the 1st. Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, and his wife traveled with them. In Guangzhou, the delegation visited the Guangzhou Children's Palace, which opened on "June 1st," as well as revolutionary historical sites and scenic spots such as Huanghuagang, Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall, and Yuexiu Mountain. On the 3rd, the delegation left Guangzhou by car, transiting through Hong Kong to return home. Yun Guangying, Secretary-General of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, Zhu Guang, Vice Mayor of the Guangzhou Municipal People's Government, Ouyang Shan, Chairman of the South China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and others went to the station to see them off. Before leaving Guangzhou, Fang Fang, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, Zhu Guang, Vice Mayor of the Guangzhou Municipal People's Government, and others hosted a farewell banquet. The delegation and Ambassador U Hla Maung held a farewell reception to entertain leaders of the provincial and municipal people's governments and figures from the cultural circles.

### 缅甸文化代表团返国

1952-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸文化代表团返国
    【新华社广州三日电】以宇吞帕为首的缅甸文化代表团一行十五人，由我中央人民政府外交部亚洲司专员陈应陪同，于一日自上海抵达广州。缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂及其夫人同行。代表团在广州参观了“六一”开幕的广州少年儿童宫和黄花岗、中山纪念堂、越秀山等革命史迹和名胜。三日代表团乘车离广州转道香港回国。前往车站送行的有广东省人民政府秘书长云广英、广州市人民政府副市长朱光和华南文学艺术界联合会主席欧阳山等人。代表团离广州前，广东省人民政府副主席方方和广州市人民政府副市长朱光等曾设宴饯行，代表团和吴拉茂大使曾举行辞行酒会，招待省、市人民政府首长和文化界人士。
