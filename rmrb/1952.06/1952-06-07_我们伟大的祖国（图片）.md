Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-06-07
Page: 1

The "Three-Anti" and "Five-Anti" movements have raised the labor enthusiasm of workers in all parts of the country to an unprecedented level. The workers of the Xicun Power Plant in Guangzhou have repaired a large generator that had been abandoned for twelve years, and it began generating electricity on May 8. The workers are constantly inspecting and guarding this generator.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    “三反”“五反”运动使全国各地工人的劳动热情空前提高。广州西村电厂职工把废弃了十二年的一部大型发电机修复了，并且在五月八日开始发电。工人们正在不断地检查和守护着这部发电机。
