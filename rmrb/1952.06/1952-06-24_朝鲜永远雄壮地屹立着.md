Title: Korea Will Forever Stand Tall and Magnificent
Author: Kelade Luo'a
Date: 1952-06-24
Page: 3

Korea Will Forever Stand Tall and Magnificent
France　Kelade Luo'a
They hate happiness, they hate a free nation, they hate peace. Korea will forever stand tall and magnificent.
They hate the land that has been distributed to the hungry. Korea will forever stand tall and magnificent.
They attack the working people who have become masters of their own lives. Korea will forever stand tall and magnificent.
They set fire to villages, burn down peaceful cottages. Korea will forever stand tall and magnificent.
They massacre fleeing crowds, the blood of the innocent flows like rivers. Korea will forever stand tall and magnificent.
They torture heroes with cruel punishments. They insult and abuse captured soldiers. Korea will forever stand tall and magnificent.
They shoot warriors, they massacre refugees. Korea will forever stand tall and magnificent.
They trample on all that is beautiful and make innocent eyes cry. Korea will forever stand tall and magnificent.
They destroy streets, gardens, and schools with bombs. Korea will forever stand tall and magnificent.
They turn water, air, and the simplest things into poison. Korea will forever stand tall and magnificent.
They make children cry beside the corpses of mothers with disemboweled bellies. Korea will forever stand tall and magnificent.
Korea, you are the shame of the executioners, Korea, you are the hope of free mankind, Korea, you make people feel proud to be human, Korea, you are being slaughtered, you are being betrayed, you are bleeding, but you will triumph!
You will triumph, after much suffering, just like a mother uses her own flesh and blood to nurture the life of her baby.
You will triumph, undoubtedly, just as beauty will surely overcome disaster, and courage will surely overcome persecution.
You will triumph, the principle is so simple, just as night will inevitably be followed by day.
Ah, bleeding land, proud land, you are small, and you are great, you are distressed, and you are infinitely rich, the violence of murderers can never overwhelm you.
For today, for tomorrow, for the happiness of the future, Korea will forever stand tall and magnificent.

　　　　June 6, 1952, Pyongyang

### 朝鲜永远雄壮地屹立着
柯莱德·罗阿
1952-06-24
第3版()
专栏：

    朝鲜永远雄壮地屹立着
    法国　柯莱德·罗阿他们仇视幸福，仇视一个自由的民族，仇视和平。朝鲜永远雄壮地屹立着。他们仇视那已经分给了饥饿者的土地。朝鲜永远雄壮地屹立着。他们向着已经成为自己生活的主人的劳动人民袭击。朝鲜永远雄壮地屹立着。他们放火烧掉村庄，烧掉和平的茅屋。朝鲜永远雄壮地屹立着。他们屠杀流亡的人群，无辜者的鲜血流成了河。朝鲜永远雄壮地屹立着。他们用酷刑拷打英雄。他们侮辱和虐待被俘的士兵。朝鲜永远雄壮地屹立着。他们枪毙勇士，他们屠杀难民。朝鲜永远雄壮地屹立着。他们蹂躏一切美丽的人和物，使天真纯洁的眼睛哭泣。朝鲜永远雄壮地屹立着。他们用炸弹摧毁街道、花园、学校。朝鲜永远雄壮地屹立着。他们把水，把空气，把最单纯的东西，化为毒物。朝鲜永远雄壮地屹立着。他们使孩子们在剖腹的母亲们的尸旁号哭。朝鲜永远雄壮地屹立着。朝鲜，你是刽子手们的耻辱，朝鲜，你是自由人类的希望，朝鲜，你使人们感到作为人的骄傲，朝鲜，你被宰割，你被出卖，你流着血，但是，你将获得胜利！你将获得胜利，在经历了许多痛苦之后，就像母亲用自己的血肉孕育婴儿的生命。你将获得胜利，确定无疑，就像美丽一定会战胜灾难，勇敢一定会战胜迫害。你将获得胜利，这道理如此简单，就像黑夜终了一定是白天的到来。啊，流血的土地，骄傲的土地，你是小小的，也是伟大的，你是困苦的，也是无限丰富的，杀人犯的暴力永不能将你压倒。为了今天，为了明天，为了未来的幸福，朝鲜永远雄壮地屹立着。
            一九五二年六月六日，平壤
