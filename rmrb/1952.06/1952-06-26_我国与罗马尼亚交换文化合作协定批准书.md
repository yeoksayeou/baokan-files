Title: Exchange of Instruments of Ratification of the China-Romania Cultural Cooperation Agreement Between China and Romania
Author: 
Date: 1952-06-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 25th: Liu Jingyu, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Romania, and Bunaşu, First Deputy Minister of the Romanian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, exchanged instruments of ratification of the China-Romania Cultural Cooperation Agreement on behalf of their respective governments in Bucharest on the 23rd. Măgulescu, Chairman of the Romanian Higher Education Committee, other senior Romanian government officials, and personnel from the Chinese Embassy attended the ceremony.

### 我国与罗马尼亚交换文化合作协定批准书

1952-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我国与罗马尼亚交换文化合作协定批准书
    【新华社布拉格二十五日电】我国驻罗马尼亚大使馆临时代办刘靖宇和罗马尼亚外交部第一副部长布纳修于二十三日在布加勒斯特代表两国政府交换中罗文化合作协定批准书。参加仪式的有罗马尼亚高级教育委员会主席墨古莱斯库和罗马尼亚政府的其他高级人员及我国大使馆人员。
