Title: Panchen Erdeni Passes Through Gyangze
Author:
Date: 1952-06-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 21st—Panchen Erdeni arrived in Gyangze, an important town in Rear Tibet, on June 17th. He was warmly welcomed by local government officials, monks and laypeople, and the People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Gyangze. On the 19th, he proceeded to Shigatse. A grand welcoming ceremony was held in Gyangze on the 17th. After accepting flowers and Hada scarves presented by the welcomers, Panchen Erdeni delivered a short speech.

Upon receiving the news of Panchen Erdeni's upcoming passage, monks and laypeople along the route from Lhasa to Shigatse organized welcome stations. Lamas from monasteries northeast of Gyangze organized religious bands, while farmers and herdsmen organized gong and drum teams and various dance troupes to welcome Panchen Erdeni.

### 班禅额尔德尼过江孜

1952-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    班禅额尔德尼过江孜
    【新华社拉萨二十一日电】班禅额尔德尼在六月十七日抵达后藏重镇江孜，受到当地地方政府僧俗官员、全市僧俗人民和中国人民解放军驻江孜部队的热烈欢迎，十九日已转赴日喀则。江孜市区十七日举行了隆重的欢迎仪式，班禅额尔德尼在接受欢迎者呈献的鲜花和哈达后，发表简短谈话。
    从拉萨到日喀则的沿途僧俗人民，得到班禅额尔德尼即将过境的消息，纷纷组织了欢迎站。江孜东北各寺院喇嘛组织了宗教乐队，农牧民组织了锣鼓队和各种舞蹈队，欢迎班禅额尔德尼。
