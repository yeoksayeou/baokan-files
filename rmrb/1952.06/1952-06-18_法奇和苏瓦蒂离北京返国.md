Title: Faggi and Suwati Depart Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1952-06-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 17th. The French representative Faggi and his wife, who were invited to China to attend the commemorative meeting of the four great cultural figures, and the Indonesian representative Suwati, who came to Beijing to attend the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 16th to return home. Among those who went to the airport to see them off were Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and his wife, Vice-Chairman Chen Shutong, Secretary-General Liu Guanyi, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department Meng Yu, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department Liao Gailong, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Li Dequan, Deputy Director of the International Work Department Liao Mengxing and others. The French writer Roa, who came to China to attend the commemorative meeting of the four great cultural figures, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 法奇和苏瓦蒂离北京返国

1952-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    法奇和苏瓦蒂离北京返国
    【新华社十七日讯】应邀来我国参加四大文化名人纪念会的法国代表法奇及夫人，来北京出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的印度尼西亚代表苏瓦蒂，在十六日上午乘飞机离北京返国。前往机场欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若及夫人、副主席陈叔通、秘书长刘贯一、联络部副部长孟雨、宣传部副部长廖盖隆，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全、国际工作部副部长廖梦醒等人。来我国参加四大文化名人纪念会的法国作家罗阿也前往机场送行。
