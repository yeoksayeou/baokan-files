Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-06-22
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The spring silkworm cocoons in various parts of Zhejiang have had a bumper harvest. This is a rural woman in Qiaosi District, Hangxian County, Zhejiang, selecting cocoons. They guarantee to supply the state with good cocoons in order to make beautiful silk fabrics to greatly satisfy the needs of the people.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    浙江各地的春茧已经丰收。这是浙江杭县乔司区的农村妇女正在进行选茧。她们保证要把好茧供给国家，以便制成美丽的丝织品，大量满足人民的需要。
