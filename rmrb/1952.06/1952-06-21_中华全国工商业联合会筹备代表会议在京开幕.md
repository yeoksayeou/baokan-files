Title: Preparatory Representative Meeting of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-06-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 20th: The Preparatory Representative Meeting of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce opened in Beijing on the 20th. After this meeting, the preparatory committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce will be established. The preparatory committee will exercise the functions and powers of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce before the formal establishment of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce.

This meeting has broad representation. There are 414 representatives from industrial and commercial circles from all provinces, cities, Inner Mongolia, Tibet, and Taiwan. This includes 76 representatives from state-owned enterprises and cooperatives, accounting for approximately 18% of the total number of representatives; 18 specially invited representatives, accounting for approximately 4% of the total number of representatives; and 320 representatives from private industry and commerce, including representatives from large, medium, and small industrial and commercial enterprises, handicraftsmen, stallholders, and capital agents in large, medium, and small cities across the country, as well as representatives from industrial and commercial people from ethnic minorities and overseas Chinese.

The guests present included: Huang Yanpei, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, Li Weihan, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, Li Jishen, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, Zhang Naiqi, Convener of the China Democratic National Construction Association, Xu Deheng, Chairman of the Central Council of the Jiusan Society, and others.

At 8:00 AM, after the chairman announced the opening of the meeting, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Office of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, Sha Qianli, first reported on the preparations for the meeting. The meeting then adopted the list of the presidium and elected Sha Qianli as the secretary-general of the conference, and Hu Zi’ang and Sun Fuling as deputy secretaries-general. Chairman of the conference, Chen Shutong, delivered the opening speech. Chairman Chen Shutong first pointed out that this conference is a grand gathering of great unity of the national industrial and commercial circles. Then, he explained the significance of the conference and the tasks it undertakes, and provided four suggestions to the conference. Finally, he said: Our country is about to enter a great era of economic construction. The people of the whole country today require us, industrialists and businessmen, after this "Five-Anti" movement, to completely eliminate the "five evils" and exert greater strength, greater initiative, and greater creativity in the great people's construction cause, so that this effort can play a certain role in accelerating the industrialization of our country.

In the conference, Vice Premier Huang Yanpei of the State Council of the Central People's Government, Chairman Li Jishen of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, and Convener Zhang Naiqi of the China Democratic National Construction Association delivered speeches one after another. In their speeches, they unanimously pointed out that private industry and commerce, which are beneficial to the national economy and the people's livelihood, can be developed, and they hope that after this "Five-Anti" movement, industrialists and businessmen across the country can completely eliminate the "five evils," fully abide by the Common Program, and actively engage in production and operation under the leadership of the state-owned economy and the national economic plan.

The meeting finally adopted the rules of procedure of the Preparatory Representative Meeting of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, the meeting schedule, the grouping rules, and the list of members of the proposal review committee and the drafting committee, and adjourned at 11:00 AM. Group discussions began in the afternoon.
	Chairman of the conference, Chen Shutong, delivering the opening speech.
	(Xinhua News Agency reporter Cao Xinghua photo)

### 中华全国工商业联合会筹备代表会议在京开幕

1952-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国工商业联合会筹备代表会议在京开幕
    【新华社二十日讯】中华全国工商业联合会筹备代表会议二十日在北京开幕。经过这次会议，中华全国工商业联合会的筹备委员会将要成立。筹备委员会在全国工商业联合会正式成立之前，代行中华全国工商业联合会的职权。
    这次会议具有广泛的代表性，到会的有全国各省、市、内蒙、西藏及台湾的工商界代表四一四名。包括国营企业及合作社的代表七六名，约占全部代表总数的百分之十八；特邀代表十八名，约占全部代表总数的百分之四；私营工商业的代表三二○名，包括了全国大、中、小城市中的大、中、小工商企业、手工业者、摊商和资方代理人的代表，以及少数民族、海外侨胞的工商业者的代表。
    到会的来宾有：中央人民政府政务院副总理黄炎培、中共中央统一战线工作部部长李维汉、中国国民党革命委员会中央委员会主席李济深、民主建国会召集人章乃器、九三学社中央理事会主席许德珩等。
    上午八时，大会在主席宣布会议开会后，首先由中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会筹备处沙千里秘书长报告会议筹备经过。会议接着通过了主席团名单，并推选沙千里为大会秘书长，胡子婴、孙孚凌为副秘书长。大会主席陈叔通致开幕词。陈叔通主席首先指出：这次大会是全国工商界大团结的盛会。接着，他说明了这次大会的意义和大会所担负的任务后，并向大会提供了四点意见。最后，他说：我国即将进入一个伟大的经济建设时代，全国人民今天都要求我们工商业者在这次“五反”运动之后，彻底清除五毒，在伟大的人民建设事业中，发挥更大的力量，发挥更大的积极性与创造性，使得这种努力在加速我们国家工业化的过程中，起着一定的作用。
    在大会上相继致词的有前来参加开幕典礼的中央人民政府政务院黄炎培副总理、中国国民党革命委员会中央委员会李济深主席和民主建国会召集人章乃器。他们在致词中一致指出有利于国计民生的私营工商业都能得到发展，并希望全国工商业者经过这次“五反”运动之后，能够彻底清除五毒，完全遵守共同纲领，在国营经济和国家经济计划领导之下，积极从事生产和经营。
    会议最后通过了中华全国工商业联合会筹备代表会议议事规则，会议日程表、分组规则、及提案审查委员会和起草委员会委员名单，于十一时宣布休会。下午开始进行分组讨论。大会主席陈叔通致开幕词
    （新华社记者曹兴华摄）
