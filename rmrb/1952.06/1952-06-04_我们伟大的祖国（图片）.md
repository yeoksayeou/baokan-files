Author:
Date: 1952-06-04

    Our Great Motherland
    Construction workers in Lüshun City have created and improved more than fifty kinds of tools, making great contributions to the construction industry. Lu Baocun, a worker from the Lüshun Civil Engineering and Construction Company, created the "floor-mopping tractor," which can increase work efficiency by 235 percent. This is Lu Baocun demonstrating the "floor-mopping tractor."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    旅顺市建筑工人创造和改进了五十多种工具，对建筑工业很有贡献。旅顺市土木建筑公司的工人卢保存创造了“抹地拖拉机”，能提高工作效率百分之二百三十五。这是卢保存正在表演“抹地拖拉机”的情形。
