Title: Nineteen Nations' Representatives from the Asian and Pacific Region Gather in Beijing; Preparatory Meeting for Peace Conference Opens Today; Representatives from Five Countries Including Guatemala are En Route to Beijing

Author:

Date: 1952-06-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd: The preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference is scheduled to open in Beijing today. As of the 2nd, forty-five representatives from nineteen countries in Asia, Australia, and the Americas along the Pacific coast, including Australia, Burma, Canada, Ceylon, Chile, China, India, Indonesia, Japan, North Korea, Malaya, Mexico, Mongolia, New Zealand, Pakistan, the Philippines, the United States, the Soviet Union, and Vietnam, have gathered in Beijing. Gouriayev and John Darr, secretaries of the Secretariat of the World Peace Council, have also come to participate in the meeting. Also participating in the meeting are Kauli Banov and Thornton, members of the Asian-Australian Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions. In addition, representatives from five countries, including Guatemala, El Salvador, Nicaragua, Costa Rica, and Ecuador, are on their way to Beijing.

The preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference is being convened under the advocacy and support of the broad masses of people in the Asian, Australian, and American countries along the Pacific coast who are striving for peace. On March 21st of this year, eleven prominent figures representing the Chinese people who are committed to the cause of peace, namely Soong Ching Ling, Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Li Siguang, Ma Yinchu, Zhang Xiruo, Liu Ningyi, Cai Chang, Mao Dun, and Liao Chengzhi, representing the Chinese people and accepting the suggestions of India and other peace-loving individuals, jointly issued an initiation letter, inviting peace-loving people from countries in Asia, Australia, and the Americas along the Pacific coast to jointly initiate and convene this conference, and to hold a preparatory meeting in Beijing. After the initiation letter was issued, it received enthusiastic responses and support from peace-loving people in various countries. The convening of the preparatory meeting fully demonstrates the deep desire of the people of various countries in the Asian, Australian, and American regions along the Pacific coast to strive for lasting peace.

Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd: Representatives from various countries attending the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference have arrived in Beijing successively. As of the 2nd, the newly reported representatives include five representatives from Malaya, Mexico, India, and the Philippines. They are:

I. Malaya
    Zeng Xuehong Elementary School Teacher

II. Mexico
    Eli Gordali Chief Representative, Standing Committee Member of the Mexican National Peace Committee, Professor of Philosophy Department of the Mexican University
    Rafael Mendes Secretary-General of the Mexican Federal District Peace Committee, Professor of the Mexican Polytechnic Institute

III. India
    Sardar Gul Bakhsh Singh Vice Chairman of the All-India Peace Council

IV. Philippines
    Manuel Cruz Journalist

### 亚洲及太平洋区域十九国代表齐集北京  和平会议筹备会议今开幕  危地马拉等五国代表正在来北京途中

1952-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    亚洲及太平洋区域十九国代表齐集北京
    和平会议筹备会议今开幕
    危地马拉等五国代表正在来北京途中
    【新华社三日讯】亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议定于今日在北京开幕。截至二日止，已有亚、澳、美各洲太平洋沿岸的澳大利亚、缅甸、加拿大、锡兰、智利、中国、印度、印度尼西亚、日本、朝鲜、马来亚、墨西哥、蒙古、新西兰、巴基斯坦、菲律宾、美国、苏联和越南等十九个国家的代表四十五人齐集北京。世界和平理事会书记处秘书顾里亚耶夫和约翰·达尔也前来参加会议。参加会议的还有世界工会联合会亚澳联络局委员考里班诺夫和桑顿。另有危地马拉、萨尔瓦多、尼加拉瓜、哥斯达黎加和厄瓜多尔五国代表正来北京途中。
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议，是在亚、澳、美各洲太平洋沿岸国家的为和平而努力的广大人民的提倡和赞助之下召开的。今年三月二十一日，中国努力和平事业的著名人士宋庆龄、郭沫若、彭真、陈叔通、李四光、马寅初、张奚若、刘宁一、蔡畅、茅盾、廖承志等十一人代表中国人民，并接受印度及其他和平人士的建议，联名发表发起书，邀请亚、澳、美各洲太平洋沿岸各国爱好和平人士共同发起召开这一会议，并在北京召开筹备会议。发起书发出后，得到各国和平人民的热烈响应和支持。筹备会议的召开，充分显示了亚、澳、美各洲太平洋沿岸区域各国人民争取持久和平的深切愿望。
    【新华社二日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的各国代表陆续到达北京。截至二日止，新报到的代表有马来亚、墨西哥、印度和菲律宾的代表五人。他们是：
    一、马来亚
    曾雪虹小学教师
    二、墨西哥
    艾里·哥尔达利首席代表、墨西哥全国和平委员会常务委员、墨西哥大学哲学系教授
    拉斐尔·门德斯墨西哥联邦区和平委员会秘书长、墨西哥理工学院教授
    三、印度
    沙达尔·古尔·巴克希·辛格　全印和平理事会副主席
    四、菲律宾
    马纽尔·柯鲁兹新闻工作者
