Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-06-25
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Commemorative Medals for Resisting U.S. Aggression and Aiding Korea, issued by the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, have been distributed to the front lines, providing the volunteer soldiers with a profound education in patriotism and internationalism. This is Li Haifeng, a soldier and Youth League member of the Volunteer Army, wearing the Commemorative Medal for Resisting U.S. Aggression and Aiding Korea. He is determined to forever uphold the honor bestowed by the people of the motherland and fight to the end for the liberation of Korea and the attainment of lasting world peace.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会所颁发的抗美援朝纪念章发到前线，给了志愿军的战士们以爱国主义和国际主义的深刻教育。这是志愿军战士、青年团员李海丰，他带上了抗美援朝纪念章，决心永远保持祖国人民所给予的光荣，为解放朝鲜和争取世界持久和平而奋斗到底。
