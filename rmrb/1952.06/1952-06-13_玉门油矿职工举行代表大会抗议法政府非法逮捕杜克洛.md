Title: Yumen Oilfield Workers Hold Representative Meeting to Protest French Government's Illegal Arrest of Duclos
Author:
Date: 1952-06-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, 12th. The entire workforce and families of the Yumen Oilfield held a representative meeting on the 8th, to support the patriotic actions of the French people and protest the French government's illegal arrest of French people's leader, Duclos. Worker representatives from various factories and teams expressed at the meeting their determination to strengthen the Resist America, Aid Korea movement with practical actions and to crush the U.S. imperialists' heinous plot to wage bacteriological warfare. Workers listening to the live broadcast of the meeting at the broadcast listening station sent opinions to the meeting via telephone, guaranteeing to subscribe to, fulfill, or overfulfill their group's patriotic labor emulation pledges, and to safeguard world peace with practical actions. The meeting also passed a telegram of support to the French National Peace Council.

### 玉门油矿职工举行代表大会抗议法政府非法逮捕杜克洛

1952-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

    玉门油矿职工举行代表大会抗议法政府非法逮捕杜克洛
    【新华社兰州十二日电】玉门油矿全体职工和家属，八日举行代表大会，声援法国人民的爱国行动，抗议法国政府非法逮捕法国人民领袖杜克洛。各厂队的职工代表在大会上表示决以实际行动加强抗美援朝运动，粉碎美帝国主义进行细菌战的罪恶阴谋。在广播收听站收听大会实况的职工群众，在会议进行中纷纷用电话向大会表示意见，并保证订好、做到或超额完成本小组的爱国劳动竞赛公约，以实际行动保卫世界和平。大会并通过致法国全国和平理事会的声援电。
