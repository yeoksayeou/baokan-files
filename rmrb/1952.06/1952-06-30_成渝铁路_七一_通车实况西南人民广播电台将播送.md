Title: Chengdu-Chongqing Railway "July 1st" Opening Ceremony to be Broadcast by Southwest People's Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1952-06-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 29th. The Southwest People's Broadcasting Station has decided to broadcast live coverage of the celebrations in Chongqing, Neijiang, and Chengdu on July 1st, marking the full opening of the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway. The broadcast of the Chongqing opening ceremony will begin at 8:00 AM on July 1st; the broadcast of the Neijiang celebration will begin at 7:30 PM; and the broadcast of the Chengdu celebration will begin at 8:00 AM on July 2nd. The station hopes that people in all regions will enthusiastically organize to listen. To ensure widespread listening, the Southwest People's Broadcasting Station will use the following frequencies: shortwave wavelength 49 meters, frequency 6154 kHz; shortwave wavelength 27.27 meters, frequency 1100 kHz; medium wave wavelength 526.3 meters, frequency 570 kHz. Simultaneously, Chongqing People's Broadcasting Station and Western Sichuan People's Broadcasting Station will rebroadcast using all their wavelengths.

### 成渝铁路“七一”通车实况西南人民广播电台将播送

1952-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    成渝铁路“七一”通车实况西南人民广播电台将播送
    【新华社重庆二十九日电】西南人民广播电台决定在七月一日成渝路全线通车时转播重庆、内江、成都三地庆祝通车的实况。转播重庆通车实况时间是七月一日上午八时起；转播内江庆祝实况时间是下午七时半起：转播成都庆祝实况时间是七月二日上午八时起，希望各地人民热烈组织收听。西南人民广播电台为了使各地人民得以普遍收听，特用短波波长四九公尺，周率六一五四千周：短波波长二七点二七公尺，周率一一零零千周，中波波长五二六点三公尺，周率五七零千周播送。同时并由重庆人民广播电台、川西人民广播电台用全部波长联播。
