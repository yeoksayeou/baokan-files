Title: East German Government Issues Order to Further Strengthen National Defense
Author:
Date: 1952-06-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, June 13th, TASS reported: The German News Agency reported that the government of the German Democratic Republic, in order to implement further measures to protect the German Democratic Republic, has issued the following order:

    In order to protect the interests of the people of the German Democratic Republic and to prevent enemy elements from infiltrating the territory of the German Democratic Republic, the government of the German Democratic Republic, in addition to the order promulgated on May 26, 1952, hereby orders again:

    1. The tasks assigned to the Security Department by the order of May 26, 1952, are expanded so that the measures that the department must take include ways to prevent saboteurs, spies, and terrorists from infiltrating the territory of the German Democratic Republic.

    2. In promulgating all the rules, regulations, and orders necessary to implement these measures, it must be taken into account that all these measures can be immediately cancelled if an agreement is reached on holding free all-German elections for the unification of Germany on a democratic and peaceful basis.

    3. Anyone who violates the rules, regulations, and orders promulgated under the first clause of this order or under the order of May 26, 1952, shall be sentenced to imprisonment for up to two years and a fine of up to two thousand marks, or one of these punishments; this does not apply to other regulations that prescribe more severe penalties.

    4. This decision shall take effect from the date of promulgation.

### 民主德国政府发布命令  进一步加强保卫国防

1952-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国政府发布命令
    进一步加强保卫国防
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社柏林十三日讯：德意志通讯社报道，德意志民主共和国政府为实行保卫德意志民主共和国的进一步措施，特颁发命令如下：
    为了保护德意志民主共和国人民的利益，为了防止敌对分子潜入德意志民主共和国境内，德意志民主共和国政府除于一九五二年五月二十六日公布的命令之外，特再下令：
    一、一九五二年五月二十六日的命令给保安部指定的任务予以扩大，使该部所必须采取的措施得包括防止破坏分子、间谍和恐怖分子潜入德意志民主共和国境内的办法在内。
    二、在公布执行这些措施所必需的一切规则、条例和命令时，必须考虑到：如果为了在民主与和平的基础上统一德国而对举行全德自由选举一事获致协议，所有这些措施都可以立即取消。
    三、凡违反根据本命令第一款或根据一九五二年五月二十六日的命令所公布的规则、条例和命令的人，要处两年以下的徒刑和两千马克以下的罚款，或处以两者中的一种处罚，另有其他规定更严厉的惩罚的条例不在此限。
    四、本决定自公布之日起实行。
