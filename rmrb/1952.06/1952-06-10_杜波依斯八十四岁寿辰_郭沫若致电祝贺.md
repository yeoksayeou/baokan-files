Title: W.E.B. Du Bois' Eighty-fourth Birthday: Guo Moruo Sends Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1952-06-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 9th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, recently sent a telegram to congratulate American peace fighter Dr. W.E.B. Du Bois on his eighty-fourth birthday. The telegram reads as follows:

Dear Dr. Du Bois:

On the occasion of your eighty-fourth birthday, please accept my warmest congratulations. The Chinese people have always admired your contributions to science and literature, as well as your resolute and unyielding spirit in fighting for human progress and peace over the years. We are willing to work with you forever to continue our efforts to defend the cause of progress and peace, and wish you good health.

    Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
        Chairman Guo Moruo, June 9, 1952

### 杜波依斯八十四岁寿辰  郭沫若致电祝贺

1952-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    杜波依斯八十四岁寿辰
    郭沫若致电祝贺
    【新华社九日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，顷致电祝贺美国和平战士杜波依斯博士的八十四岁寿辰。电文如下：亲爱的杜波依斯博士：
    值您八十四龄寿诞之日，谨致热烈的祝贺。中国人民素来敬仰您在科学、文艺上所作的贡献，以及您历年来为人类进步与和平而坚决不屈的奋斗精神。我们愿永远和您在一起为保卫进步与和平事业而继续努力，并祝您身体健康。
            中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
                    主席　郭沫若　一九五二年六月九日
