Title: Colombia's Presumed Initiators Respond to the Call for an Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference
Author: 
Date: 1952-06-17
Page: 1

    Responding to the Call for an Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference
    Colombia's Presumed Initiators
    [Xinhua News Agency, 16th] Colombian peace activists, in response to the call for an Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference, have already nominated the initiators of the conference and are carrying out preparatory work for the conference. The list of initiators is as follows: Colombian philosopher and literary critic Bartolomé Sanín Cano, former President of the Supreme Court and university professor Luis Eduardo Gacharna, famous poet Carlos Castro Saavedra, former Member of Parliament, criminal law expert, and Chairman of the Colombian National Peace Committee Luis Carlos Pérez, former Vice-President of the Senate, Secretary-General of the Colombian National Peace Committee, and journalist Jorge Regélos Peralta.

### 响应召开亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  哥伦比亚推定发起人

1952-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

    响应召开亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    哥伦比亚推定发起人
    【新华社十六日讯】哥伦比亚和平人士为响应召开亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议，业已推定了会议的发起人，进行会议的筹备工作。发起人名单如下：哥伦比亚哲学家、文学评论家巴杜美罗·萨宁·加诺，前最高法院院长、大学教授路易斯·爱杜阿多·加查那，名诗人加尔罗斯·加斯脱·萨维达，前议员、刑法专家、哥伦比亚全国和平委员会主席路易斯·卡罗斯·贝勒斯，前上议院副主席、哥伦比亚全国和平委员会秘书长、新闻记者乔治·雷盖洛斯·培拉尔塔。
