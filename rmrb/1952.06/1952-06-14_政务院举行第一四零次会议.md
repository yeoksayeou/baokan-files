Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 140th Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1952-06-14
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 13th. The 140th session of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 13th. The meeting heard the explanation made by Huang Yanpei, Minister of the Ministry of Light Industry of the Central People's Government, and approved the ministry's work summary for 1951 and the main points of the work plan for 1952. The meeting also adopted several cases of appointments and removals and submitted them to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第一四零次会议

1952-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一四零次会议
    【新华社十三日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百四十次政务会议在十三日下午举行。会议听取了中央人民政府轻工业部黄炎培部长所作的说明后，批准了该部一九五一年工作总结与一九五二年工作计划要点。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
