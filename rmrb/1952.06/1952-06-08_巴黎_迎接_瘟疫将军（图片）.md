Title: Paris "Welcomes" the Plague General (Picture)
Author: Kukryniksy
Date: 1952-06-08
Page: 4

    Paris "Welcomes" the Plague General
    Drawn by Kukryniksy
    Editor's note: Before and after the new Supreme Commander of the North Atlantic Aggression Army, Ridgway, arrived in Paris on May 27, the people of Paris and other parts of France, despite police obstruction and persecution, held protests, demonstrations and rallies. They shouted: "Down with the butcher Ridgway!" "Plague General Ridgway, go home!" This cartoon was originally published in the Soviet newspaper Pravda on June 2.

### 巴黎“迎接”瘟疫将军（图片）

1952-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    巴黎“迎接”瘟疫将军
    库克雷尼克赛作
    编者按：新任北大西洋侵略军总司令李奇微五月二十七日到达巴黎前后，巴黎和法国各地人民不顾警察的阻挠、迫害，纷纷举行抗议示威和游行集会。他们高呼：“打倒刽子手李奇微！”“瘟疫将军李奇微滚回去！”这张漫画原载六月二日苏联真理报。
