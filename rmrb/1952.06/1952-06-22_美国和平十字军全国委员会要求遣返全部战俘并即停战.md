Title: US "Peace Crusade" National Committee Demands Repatriation of All POWs and Immediate Ceasefire
Author:
Date: 1952-06-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New York, June 20th: According to TASS, the National Committee of the American "Peace Crusade" demanded that all prisoners be exchanged in accordance with the Geneva Convention, and that an immediate ceasefire be implemented to prevent the Korean War from entering its third year. The committee called for demonstrations across the country on June 25, the second anniversary of the Korean War, to support the delegation of women who will be petitioning for peace at the United Nations headquarters on that day.

### 美国和平十字军全国委员会要求遣返全部战俘并即停战

1952-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    美国和平十字军全国委员会要求遣返全部战俘并即停战
    【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社纽约二十日讯：“美国和平十字军”全国委员会要求依照日内瓦公约交换全部俘虏，并立即停战，以免朝鲜战争进入第三年。委员会号召在六月二十五日——朝鲜战争两周年纪念日在全国各地举行示威，以支援将在那一天向联合国总部作和平请愿的妇女代表团。
