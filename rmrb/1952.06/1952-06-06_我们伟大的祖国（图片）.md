Our great motherland
The Jingjiang Flood Diversion Project is progressing rapidly, demonstrating the great strength of our country's workers, peasants, soldiers, and other working people. This is a scene of close cooperation between People's Liberation Army soldiers and technicians participating in the construction project. Technician Li Peiying is teaching the soldiers how to operate the concrete mixer. The soldiers quickly became skilled technicians.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    荆江分洪工程进展迅速，表现了我国工农兵和其他劳动人民的伟大力量。这是参加建设工程的人民解放军战士和技术工人密切合作的一个场面。技术工人李培英正在教战士们开动混凝土拌合机。战士们也很快都变成了熟练的技工。
