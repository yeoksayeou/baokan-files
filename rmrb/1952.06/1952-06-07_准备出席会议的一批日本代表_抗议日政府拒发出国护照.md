Title: A Group of Japanese Representatives Preparing to Attend a Meeting Protest the Japanese Government's Refusal to Issue Passports
Author: 
Date: 1952-06-07
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, June 6th: The Japanese Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference reports: The Japanese government has formally notified that it will not issue passports to Japanese representatives to go abroad to attend the preparatory meeting. On June 5th, a group of representatives went to the Japanese Foreign Ministry to protest the government's illegal actions.

### 准备出席会议的一批日本代表  抗议日政府拒发出国护照

1952-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    准备出席会议的一批日本代表
    抗议日政府拒发出国护照
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社东京六日讯：亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议日本筹备委员会消息：日本政府正式通告不发给日本代表出国参加筹备会议的护照。六月五日有一批代表到日本外务省抗议政府的非法行动。
