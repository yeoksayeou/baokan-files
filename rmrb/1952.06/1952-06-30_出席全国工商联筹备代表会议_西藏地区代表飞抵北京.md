Title: Tibetan Representatives Arrive in Beijing to Attend the National Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Representative Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-06-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 29th: A group of seven Tibetan representatives—Sando Tsang Losang Tenzin (Tibetan), Dunkhang Gesang Wanggyal (Tibetan), Gyai Xiangba Gondor Namgyal (Tibetan), Li Zhigan, and Xiao Sheng—arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 29th, via India, Singapore, and Guangzhou, to attend the preparatory representative meeting of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce. Chen Shutong, Director of the Preparatory Office of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Committee, and representatives attending the meeting from various regions, including Li Zhuchen (Head of the North China Delegation), Liu Peizhi (Deputy Head of the Northeast China Delegation), Yang Zhupo (Representative of Liaodong Province, Northeast China), Ye Yutian (Deputy Head of the Northwest China Delegation), Abulizi (Representative of Xinjiang Province, Northwest China, Uyghur), Ma Yinghao (Representative of Gansu Province, Northwest China, Hui), Sheng Pihua (Deputy Head of the East China Delegation), Zhang Aiguo (Representative of Shanghai Municipality, East China), Huang Changshui (Deputy Head of the Central South China Delegation), Li Zhuo'an (Deputy Head of the Southwest China Delegation), Peng Yongnian (Representative of Xikang Province, Southwest China, Tibetan), and Puntsok, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, and many others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 出席全国工商联筹备代表会议  西藏地区代表飞抵北京

1952-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    出席全国工商联筹备代表会议
    西藏地区代表飞抵北京
    【新华社二十九日讯】出席中华全国工商业联合会筹备代表会议的西藏地区代表三多仓·罗桑格登（藏族）、顿康·格桑旺君（藏族）、桂香巴·贡朵那嘉（藏族）、黎之淦、萧生等一行七人，已在二十九日下午，经印度、新嘉坡、广州飞抵北京。前往机场欢迎的有：中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会筹备处主任陈叔通，各地区出席会议的代表李烛尘（华北区代表团团长）、刘珮芝（东北区代表团副团长）、杨竹坡（东北区辽东省代表）、叶雨田（西北区代表团副团长）、阿不力支（西北区新疆省代表、维吾尔族）、马英豪（西北区甘肃省代表、回族）、盛丕华（华东区代表团副团长）、张爱国（华东区上海市代表）、黄长水（中南区代表团副团长）、李琢菴（西南区代表团副团长）、彭永年（西南区西康省代表、藏族），中央人民政府民族事务委员会办公厅副主任朋斯克等多人。
