Title: Northwest Ethnic Group Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-06-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th. The Northwest Ethnic Group Delegation left Beijing on the 19th to visit Tianjin, Nanjing, Shanghai, and Hangzhou. Many members and cadres of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government in Beijing went to the station to see them off.

During their stay in Beijing, the delegation presented a flag to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government, attended a banquet hosted by Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government, and visited factories, schools, and historical sites in the capital. The Mongolian, Tibetan, and Hui members of the delegation visited the Yonghe Temple, the Panchen Lama's Pagoda Courtyard, and mosques, respectively. The members of various ethnic groups who believe in Islam were given special care in their lives during the "Ramadan" in Beijing. Through this visit, all members of the delegation had a profound understanding of the greatness and loveliness of the motherland and the national policy and the policy of freedom of religious belief of the Central People's Government. They expressed heartfelt thanks for the cordial reception of the People's Government.

### 西北各民族代表参观团离京

1952-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    西北各民族代表参观团离京
    【新华社二十日讯】西北各民族代表参观团在十九日离京前往天津、南京、上海和杭州等地参观。到车站欢送的有中央人民政府民族事务委员会在京委员及干部多人。
    参观团在京期间，曾向中央人民政府毛泽东主席献旗，参加中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理的招待宴会，并参观了首都的工厂、学校和名胜古迹。参观团的蒙、藏族团员和回族团员曾分别参观雍和宫、班禅塔院和清真寺等；信仰伊斯兰教的各族团员在京适逢“斋月”，生活上都得到特别照顾。经过这次参观，参观团全体团员对祖国的伟大可爱和中央人民政府的民族政策及宗教信仰自由政策，有了深刻的认识，他们对人民政府的亲切招待，表示衷心感谢。
