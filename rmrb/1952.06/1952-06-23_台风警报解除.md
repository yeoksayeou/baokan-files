Title: Typhoon Warning Lifted
Author:
Date: 1952-06-23
Page: 1

    Typhoon Warning Lifted
    [Xinhua News Agency, 22nd] According to the typhoon warning cancellation issued by the Central Meteorological Observatory at 7 PM on the 22nd: The typhoon that appeared on the ocean surface southeast of Taiwan on the afternoon of the 21st had already shifted towards southern Japan at 2 PM this afternoon. Except for a slight impact on the ocean east of the East China Sea, the threat to other parts of our country has been eliminated.

### 台风警报解除

1952-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

    台风警报解除
    【新华社二十二日讯】据中央气象台二十二日下午七时发布的台风解除警报称：二十一日下午在台湾东南洋面上出现的台风，在今日下午二时已经改向日本南部推进。我国沿海除东海东部的洋面上稍有影响外，其余各地威胁已消除。
