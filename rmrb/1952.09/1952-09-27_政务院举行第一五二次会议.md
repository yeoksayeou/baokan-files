Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 152nd Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-09-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 26th

    The 152nd meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 26th. The meeting heard and approved the report by Hu Yuzhi, Director of the Central People's Government Publishing Administration, on the Outline of the 1952 Publishing Work Plan, and approved various appointment and removal proposals.

### 政务院举行第一五二次会议

1952-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一五二次会议
    【新华社二十六日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百五十二次政务会议在二十六日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府出版总署胡愈之署长关于
    一九五二年出版工作计划大纲的报告，并通过了各项任免案。
