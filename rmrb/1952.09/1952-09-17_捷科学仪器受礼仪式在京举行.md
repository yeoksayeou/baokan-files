Title: Ceremony for Receiving Scientific Instruments from Czechoslovakia Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-09-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 15th

    Ceremony for Receiving Scientific Instruments from Czechoslovakia Held in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, September 15th] The ceremony for receiving sixteen boxes of scientific instruments presented by the government of the Czechoslovak Republic to the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government of China was held in Beijing on the 15th. Participants in the ceremony included Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Kousal, and all diplomats of the embassy; Vice-Chairman of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government, Ma Xulun, Director of the General Office, Xu Maijin, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, Hong Shen, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government, Zeng Zhaolun, and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Shen Ping. Also participating in the ceremony were President of Peking University, Ma Yinchu, and other university presidents and professors, totaling forty people.

### 捷科学仪器受礼仪式在京举行

1952-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

    捷科学仪器受礼仪式在京举行
    【新华社十五日讯】捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府赠送我国中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会科学仪器十六箱的受礼仪式，于十五日在北京举行。参加仪式的有捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使康萨拉及大使馆全体外交官；中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会马叙伦副主任、办公厅主任徐迈进、对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，中央人民政府教育部副部长曾昭抡、中央人民政府外交部交际处副处长沈平。参加仪式者尚有北京大学校长马寅初及其他各大学校长、教授等共四十人。
