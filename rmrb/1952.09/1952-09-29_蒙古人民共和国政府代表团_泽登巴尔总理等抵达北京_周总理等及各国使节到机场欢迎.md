Title: Government Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, Premier Tsedenbal, Arrives in Beijing. Premier Zhou and Diplomats from Various Countries Welcome Them at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1952-09-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: The head of the Government Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, Premier Tsedenbal of the Mongolian People's Republic, arrived in Beijing on the 28th. Accompanying him were: Ramsuren, member of the delegation, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic; and delegation staff members, Dulebuerjin and Puerbuzhale.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Deng Xiaoping; Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court; Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Su Yu, Deputy Chief of the General Staff; Bo Yibo, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the State Administration Council and Minister of Finance; Ye Jizhuang, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Trade; Ma Xulun, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the State Administration Council and Minister of Education; Shen Yanbing, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee and Minister of Culture; Peng Zhen, Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; Yang Shangkun, Director of the General Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Luo Ruiqing, Minister of Public Security of the Central People's Government; Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications; Zhang Bojun, Minister of Communications; Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture; Li Siguang, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Hu Jingyun, Vice President of the People's Bank of China; Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Central People's Government; Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Luo Shun Chu, Deputy Commander of the Navy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Wang Bingzhang, Chief of Staff of the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Yang Chengwu, Deputy Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command; Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Yan Baohang, Deputy Director; Xu Yixin, Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Affairs Department; Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Affairs Department; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; and Shen Ping, Deputy Director.

Also welcoming them at the airport were: Xilindibu, Minister of Education of the Mongolian People's Republic and member of the Government Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic; Jalkasaihan, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to China and member of the Government Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, and all staff members of the Embassy; Rudenko, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Romanian People's Republic to China; Koenig, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to China with the rank of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary; Shafaranko, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to China; Petkov, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China; U Hla Maung, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Union of Burma to China; Kwon O-jik, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China; Konsal, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Czechoslovak Republic to China; Kirilok, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Polish People's Republic to China; Gaul, Minister of the Embassy of the Republic of India to China; Goudev, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics to China; Zhou Liang, First Secretary of the Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China; Tang Doumao, Second Secretary of the Embassy of the Republic of Indonesia to China; and Iftikhar Ali, Second Secretary of the Embassy of Pakistan to China.

An honor guard was lined up at the airport, and the national anthems of the Mongolian People's Republic and the People's Republic of China were played.

The national flags of the Mongolian People's Republic and the People's Republic of China were hung at the airport.

### 蒙古人民共和国政府代表团  泽登巴尔总理等抵达北京  周总理等及各国使节到机场欢迎

1952-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民共和国政府代表团
    泽登巴尔总理等抵达北京
    周总理等及各国使节到机场欢迎
    【新华社二十八日讯】蒙古人民共和国政府代表团团长、蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔于二十八日到达北京。同来的有：代表团团员蒙古人民共和国副总理兼外交部部长拉姆苏伦；代表团随员都勒布尔金，普尔布扎勒。
    到机场欢迎的有：中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，副总理陈云、郭沫若、黄炎培、邓小平，人民政协全国委员会副主席陈叔通，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻、副总参谋长粟裕，政务院财政经济委员会副主任兼财政部部长薄一波、副主任兼对外贸易部部长叶季壮，政务院文化教育委员会副主任兼教育部部长马叙伦、副主任兼文化部部长沈雁冰，北京市人民政府市长彭真，中国共产党中央办公厅主任杨尚昆，中央人民政府公安部部长罗瑞卿，邮电部部长朱学范，交通部部长章伯钧，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，文化部副部长丁西林，中国科学院副院长李四光，中国人民银行副行长胡景沄，中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清，人民革命军事委员会办公厅主任萧向荣，中国人民解放军海军副司令员罗舜初，中国人民解放军空军参谋长王秉璋，京津卫戍司令部副司令员杨成武，中央人民政府外交部办公厅主任王炳南、副主任阎宝航、苏联东欧司司长徐以新、亚洲司司长陈家康、交际处处长王倬如、副处长沈平等。
    在机场欢迎的还有：蒙古人民共和国教育部部长、蒙古人民共和国政府代表团团员锡林迪布，蒙古人民共和国驻华特命全权大使、蒙古人民共和国政府代表团团员贾尔卡赛汗和大使馆全体人员，罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华特命全权大使鲁登科，德意志民主共和国驻华特命全权大使衔外交使团团长柯尼希，匈牙利人民共和国驻华特命全权大使夏法朗柯，保加利亚人民共和国驻华特命全权大使彼得科夫，缅甸联邦驻华特命全权大使吴拉茂，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华特命全权大使权五稷，捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻华特命全权大使康萨拉，波兰人民共和国驻华特命全权大使基里洛克，印度共和国驻华大使馆公使高尔，苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟驻华大使馆临时代办顾德夫，越南民主共和国驻华代表团一等秘书周亮，印度尼西亚共和国驻华大使馆二等秘书汤杜茂，巴基斯坦驻华大使馆二等秘书伊夫提加·艾里。
    飞机场上排列着仪仗队，并奏蒙古人民共和国和中华人民共和国的国歌。
    飞机场上挂着蒙古人民共和国和中华人民共和国的国旗。
