Title: Islamic People in Beijing, Dihua, and Other Places Joyfully Celebrate Eid al-Adha
Author:
Date: 1952-09-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report: The people of all ethnic groups in Beijing, Dihua, Jinan, Qingdao, and other places who believe in Islam joyfully celebrated the Islamic Eid al-Adha.

The people of all ethnic groups in Beijing who believe in Islam held commemorative ceremonies on September 1st and joyfully celebrated Eid al-Adha. During the commemorative ceremony, the people of all ethnic groups in Beijing who believe in Islam also sent a telegram to the Preparatory Committee of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, supporting the convening of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference in Beijing. The telegram stated: We are determined to combine patriotism and internationalism, and unite closely with the people of all ethnic groups who believe in Islam and the peace-loving people of the world to strive for peace in the Asian and Pacific regions and lasting world peace.

On the morning of September 1st, the city of Dihua was decorated with Five-Star Red Flags. Men and women of all ethnic groups who believe in Islam wore new clothes and flower hats and went to mosques in groups to pray and celebrate. Burhan Shahidi, director of the Preparatory Committee of the China Islamic Association, prayed at the Nanliang Mosque. Wang Enmao, Secretary of the Xinjiang Sub-bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Zhang Bangying, Third Secretary, Gao Jinchun, Vice Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government, and Wang Zhen, Acting Commander of the Xinjiang Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, all visited Burhan Shahidi's residence to extend their New Year greetings. On the morning of the 1st, more than 600 religious representatives, including Hajj Gani Niyaz, the Grand Imam of the Uyghur Muslims of Dihua, Ma Liangjun, the Grand Imam of the Hui Muslims, and representatives attending the first second session of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Congress, held a grand national solidarity gathering in the newly completed Friendship Building. At the meeting, Director Burhan Shahidi congratulated the representatives, saying: "It is Chairman Mao and the Communist Party who have enabled us to celebrate Eid al-Adha together in unity. Today, peace-loving people around the world, including those who believe in Islam, are being threatened by the US imperialist war clique. We must unite and fight for world peace." In the afternoon, the People's Governments of various districts in Dihua held forums for representatives of all ethnic groups, followed by celebrations or film screenings. People of all ethnic groups also played hand drums and sang and danced under the shade of trees in the famous scenic spot - West Park until late at night.

More than 2,000 Hui representatives in Jinan held a grand celebration on August 30th. On this day, Hui compatriots, both men and women, from the city and suburbs, dressed in festive costumes and drove cattle prepared for slaughter during the festival to participate. At the meeting, Hui compatriots happily talked about their happy lives after liberation. The meeting also sent a letter to Chairman Mao to express their respect.

Hui compatriots in Qingdao also gathered on August 31st to celebrate Eid al-Adha. All cinemas provided free movie tickets for Hui compatriots. Department stores in Jinan and Qingdao offered discounts for three days before the festival to provide preferential treatment to Hui compatriots.

### 北京迪化等地信奉伊斯兰教人民  欢欣度过古尔邦节

1952-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    北京迪化等地信奉伊斯兰教人民
    欢欣度过古尔邦节
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：北京、迪化、济南、青岛等地信奉伊斯兰教的各族人民，欢度伊斯兰教的古尔邦节。
    北京市信奉伊斯兰教的各族人民，九月一日分别举行纪念仪式，愉快地度过古尔邦节。在举行纪念仪式时，北京市信奉伊斯兰教各族人民并致电亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会，拥护在北京召开亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。电文说：我们决本爱国主义与国际主义相结合的精神，和信奉伊斯兰教各族人民以及全世界爱好和平的人民紧密地团结起来，为争取亚洲及太平洋区域的和平以及世界持久和平而奋斗。
    九月一日清晨，迪化市遍悬五星国旗，信仰伊斯兰教的各族男女穿着新衣，戴着花帽，成群结队地到清真寺去礼拜、联欢。中国伊斯兰教协会筹备委员会主任鲍尔汉在南梁清真寺作了礼拜。中共中央新疆分局书记王恩茂、第三书记张邦英，新疆省人民政府副主席高锦纯，人民解放军新疆军区代司令员王震都到鲍尔汉的住所去拜年。一日上午，迪化市伊斯兰教维吾尔族总教长和加尼牙孜阿吉、回族总教长马良骏等宗教界代表和参加新疆省第一届第二次各族各界人民代表会议的代表们共六百多人，在新近落成的交际大楼举行了隆重的民族团拜礼。会上，鲍尔汉主任向到会代表致贺说：“我们各族人民能够团结一起欢度古尔邦节，这是毛主席、共产党给我们的。今天世界爱好和平的人民；包括信仰伊斯兰教的人民在内，正遭受美帝国主义战争集团的威胁，我们要团结起来，为争取世界和平而斗争。”下午，迪化市各区人民政府召开了各族人民代表座谈会，会后并举行欢乐会或放映电影。各族人民并在著名的风景胜地——西公园林荫下敲起手鼓，纵情地歌唱跳舞，直到深夜。
    济南市回族代表两千余人在八月三十日就举行了盛大的庆祝会。这天，市内和市郊的男女回胞都穿着节日的盛装，牵着准备过节宰杀的黄牛赶来参加。会上，回族同胞们都愉快地叙说着解放以后的幸福生活。大会并上书毛主席致敬。
    青岛市回胞亦于八月三十一日集会欢度古尔邦节。各电影院并免费招待回胞看电影。济南和青岛百货公司在节日以前都减价三天优待回胞。
