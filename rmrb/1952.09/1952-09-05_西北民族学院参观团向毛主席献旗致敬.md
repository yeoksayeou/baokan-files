Title: Northwest Nationalities Institute Visiting Group Presents Flag to Chairman Mao as a Tribute
Author:
Date: 1952-09-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 4th: The visiting group from the Northwest Nationalities Institute presented a flag to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government on the afternoon of the 4th. Secretary-General of the Central People's Government, Lin Boqu, accepted the flag on behalf of Chairman Mao.

After accepting the flag, Secretary-General Lin Boqu addressed the members of the visiting group—the graduating students of the Northwest Nationalities Institute. He encouraged everyone to study and work hard to build the Northwest of the motherland.

Present at the flag presentation were Liu Geping, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Central People's Government, and Liu Chun, Vice President of the Central Institute for Nationalities.

The visiting group from the Northwest Nationalities Institute is composed of graduates from the first class of the cadre training program at the Northwest Nationalities Institute. The group arrived in Beijing from Xi'an on August 23rd and has visited the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant, the North China Agricultural Science Research Institute, Peking University, and scenic spots such as the Summer Palace. The group will also visit Tianjin, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places.

### 西北民族学院参观团向毛主席献旗致敬

1952-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    西北民族学院参观团向毛主席献旗致敬
    【新华社四日讯】西北民族学院参观团四日下午向中央人民政府毛泽东主席献旗。由中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠代表毛主席受旗。
    林伯渠秘书长在受旗后向参观团团员——西北民族学院的毕业同学们讲话。他勉励大家努力学习和工作，为建设祖国的西北而奋斗。
    献旗时，在场的有：中央人民政府政务院民族事务委员会副主任委员刘格平、中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清和中央民族学院副院长刘春。
    西北民族学院参观团，是由西北民族学院干部训练班第一期毕业学员组成的。该团于八月二十三日由西安到达北京，连日曾参观了石景山钢铁厂、华北农业科学研究所、北京大学以及颐和园等名胜古迹。该团并将前往天津、上海、杭州等地参观。
