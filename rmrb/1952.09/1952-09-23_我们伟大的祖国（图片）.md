    Our Great Motherland
    Carrying out nursery work is an important condition for developing forestry and large-scale afforestation. The Jingyue Tan Forestry Experimental Farm in Changchun has cultivated a large number of larch seedlings this year, which can be used for afforestation of 4,500 mu. The technicians of the forest farm are weeding in this larch transplant bed.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    开展育苗工作，是发展林业、大规模造林的重要条件。长春静日潭林业试验场今年大量培育了落叶松苗，可造林四千五百亩。林场的技工们正在这个落叶松换床内锄草。
