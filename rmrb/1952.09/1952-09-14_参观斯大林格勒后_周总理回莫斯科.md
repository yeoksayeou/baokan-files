Title: After Visiting Stalingrad, Premier Zhou Returns to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1952-09-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 13th: According to TASS, Premier of the Government Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, and the representatives of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China led by him, traveled by ship along the Lenin Volga-Don Canal from Stalingrad to Kalach on September 11th. Zhou Enlai and the representatives of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China visited the No. 10 lock to see the lock's equipment, as well as the construction and operation of the pumping station. Along the way, they were warmly welcomed by the working people along the canal. Before leaving the ship, Chief Representative of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, wrote in the ship's log: "Thank you for your warm hospitality. Long live the eternal and unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people!"

Zhou Enlai and the representatives of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China returned to Stalingrad on September 12th and flew away from Stalingrad on the same day. Those who came to the airport to see them off included: Pankin, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Stalingrad Oblast Soviet; Grishin, Secretary of the Stalingrad Oblast Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks); and Shapurov, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Stalingrad City Soviet.

Zhou Enlai and the representatives of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China returned to Moscow on September 12th.

### 参观斯大林格勒后  周总理回莫斯科

1952-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

    参观斯大林格勒后
    周总理回莫斯科
    【新华社十三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来和他所率领的中华人民共和国政府代表团的代表们，于九月十一日从斯大林格勒乘坐轮船沿列宁伏尔加河—顿河运河旅行，直到卡拉奇。周恩来和中华人民共和国政府代表团的代表们曾到第十号水闸参观水闸的设备以及抽水站的工程和抽水站的工作情形。他们沿途受到运河沿岸劳动人民的热诚欢迎。中华人民共和国政府代表团首席代表周恩来在离开轮船以前，曾在轮船的航行日志上写道：“感谢你们殷勤的招待。中苏两国人民永恒的牢不可破的友谊万岁！”
    周恩来与中华人民共和国政府代表团的代表们于九月十二日回到斯大林格勒，并于当天飞离斯大林格勒。到机场欢送的有：斯大林格勒州苏维埃执行委员会主席潘金、联共（布）斯大林格勒州委员会书记格里辛、斯大林格勒市苏维埃执行委员会主席沙普罗夫。
    周恩来与中华人民共和国政府代表团的代表们于九月十二日回到莫斯科。
