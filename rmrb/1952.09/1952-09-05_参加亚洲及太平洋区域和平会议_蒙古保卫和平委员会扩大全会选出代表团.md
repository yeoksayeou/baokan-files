Title: Participating in the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference: Mongolian Committee for the Defense of Peace Enlarged Plenary Session Elects Delegation
Author:
Date: 1952-09-05
Page: 4

    Participating in the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference
    Mongolian Committee for the Defense of Peace Enlarged Plenary Session Elects Delegation
    [Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 2nd] According to TASS, the enlarged plenary session of the Mongolian Committee for the Defense of Peace has elected a delegation to participate in the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. The delegation will be led by Sirindib, Chairman of the Mongolian Committee for the Defense of Peace. Currently, the Mongolian people are launching preparations for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference under the slogan of striving for new achievements in labor for peace and struggling to consolidate the camp of peace, democracy, and socialism. Reports and speeches are being given in enterprises, residential areas, institutions, and schools across the country to explain the resolutions of the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference to the working people.

### 参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  蒙古保卫和平委员会扩大全会选出代表团

1952-09-05
第4版()
专栏：

    参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    蒙古保卫和平委员会扩大全会选出代表团
    【新华社四日讯】据塔斯社乌兰巴托二日讯：蒙古保卫和平委员会扩大全体会议已选出代表团参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议，代表团将由蒙古保卫和平委员会主席锡林迪布率领。现在，蒙古人民正在为争取和平劳动的新成就，为巩固和平、民主、社会主义阵营而斗争的口号下，展开亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的筹备工作。全国各企业、住宅区、机关及学校中都有人在作报告和演讲，向劳动人民解释亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议各项决议。
