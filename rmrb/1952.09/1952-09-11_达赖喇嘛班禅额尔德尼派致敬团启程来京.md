Title: Dalai Lama, Panchen Erdeni Send Tribute Delegation to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-09-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 10th – The tribute delegations sent to Beijing by the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni of Tibet departed from Lhasa on August 21st, traveling via India, en route to Beijing. General Fan Ming, Deputy Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Wang Qimei, dozens of military and political cadres from the Tibet Military Region, and Tibetan local government officials (Zasa) Tsarong, and (Kanchen) Tubten Lekmon were present to see them off.

The Dalai Lama's tribute delegation consists of eight people, led by the head of the delegation, Lusha Tubten Tapa (Note: Lusha Tubten Tapa is also the head of the Tibet All-Circles Visiting Group to Beijing. When the group departed on August 7th, Lusha Tubten Tapa stayed in Lhasa), and the deputy head, Dingjia Dorje Jecun. Panchen Erdeni's tribute delegation consists of ten people, led by the head of the delegation, Danba Rije (seven members of whom will travel to Beijing via Xikang). Dorje Zhandong, the representative sent by Sakya Dharma King, the leader of the Red Sect of Tibet, also departed from Lhasa on September 3rd.

The tribute delegations are carrying gifts prepared for Chairman Mao.

### 达赖喇嘛班禅额尔德尼派致敬团启程来京

1952-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛班禅额尔德尼派致敬团启程来京
    【新华社拉萨十日电】西藏达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼派赴北京的致敬团，已于八月二十一日从拉萨出发，取道印度，前往北京。中国人民解放军西藏军区副政治委员范明将军、王其梅将军和西藏军区军、政干部数十人和西藏地方政府扎萨（官名）察绒、堪穹（官名）土登列门等都到场欢送。
    达赖喇嘛的致敬团共八人，由团长柳霞·土登塔巴（按柳霞·土登塔巴又为西藏各界赴京参观团团长，该团八月七日出发时，柳霞·土登塔巴留拉萨）、副团长定甲·多吉杰村率领。班禅额尔德尼的致敬团共十人，由团长丹巴日杰率领（其中七名团员取道西康前往北京）。西藏红教领袖萨迦法王派出的代表多吉占东也于九月三日自拉萨出发。
    致敬团都带有准备献给毛主席的礼物。
