Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish News of the Return of Our Government Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1952-09-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 24th: Moscow newspapers on September 23rd all published on their front pages the news of the departure of our government delegation from Moscow on September 22nd to return to China. The newspapers also published the speech made by Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, at the airport.

### 莫斯科各报刊载我政府代表团返国消息

1952-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各报刊载我政府代表团返国消息
    【新华社莫斯科二十四日电】九月二十三日，莫斯科一切报纸都以首页刊载了我国政府代表团二十二日离莫斯科返国的消息。各报并刊载了我中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来在机场发表的谈话。
