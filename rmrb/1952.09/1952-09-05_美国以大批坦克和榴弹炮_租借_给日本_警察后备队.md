Title: US "Leases" Large Numbers of Tanks and Howitzers to Japanese "Police Reserve Force"
Author:
Date: 1952-09-05
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, September 4th: TASS Tokyo, September 4th: According to a report in "Yomiuri Shimbun", the US authorities have notified the Japanese government that by November, the so-called "Police Reserve Force" can lease 222 Sherman tanks and 210 howitzers. Thirty tanks and thirty howitzers have already been handed over to the "Police Reserve Force". It is said that the United States will provide the "Police Reserve Force" with another 200 tanks and 150 howitzers.

### 美国以大批坦克和榴弹炮  “租借”给日本“警察后备队”

1952-09-05
第4版()
专栏：

    美国以大批坦克和榴弹炮
    “租借”给日本“警察后备队”
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社东京四日讯：“读卖新闻”报道，美国当局已通知日本政府，在十一月以前，所谓“警察后备队”可以租借到二百二十二辆薛尔曼坦克和二百一十门榴弹炮。有三十辆坦克和三十门榴弹炮业已交给“警察后备队”。据说美国还要再给“警察后备队”二百辆坦克和一百五十门榴弹炮。
