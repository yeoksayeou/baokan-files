Our Great Motherland
    Dongting Lake, one of China's largest freshwater lakes, is located in the middle reaches of the Yangtze River, between Hunan and Hubei. The lake area is rich in rice, and the lake produces a large number of fish. The annual catch of black carp, carp, etc., is about 100,000 dan. This is a scene of fishing boats fishing in the lake.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国最大的淡水湖泊之一——洞庭湖，位于长江中游湖南湖北之间。沿湖稻米丰富，湖中产鱼极多，每年捕获的青鱼、鲤鱼等约有十万担左右。这是渔船正在湖中捕鱼的情形。
