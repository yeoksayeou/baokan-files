Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Others Leave the Soviet Union for Home; Molotov and Tsedenbal See Them Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1952-09-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 23rd: Zhou Enlai, Chief Representative of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China, Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, left Moscow by plane on the 22nd to return home. Accompanying him were Chen Yun, Vice Premier of the State Administration Council; Su Yu, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Wang Heshou, Minister of Heavy Industry; Luo Shun'chu, Deputy Commander of the Navy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Xu Yixin, Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; and Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Those who saw them off at the Central Airport in Moscow were: Molotov, Mikoyan, and Bulganin, Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union; Vyshinsky, Minister of Foreign Affairs; Kumykin, Minister of Foreign Trade; Pan Youxin, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the People's Republic of China; Yasnov, Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet; Borisov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade; Migunov, Commercial Representative of the Soviet Union to the People's Republic of China; General of the Army Malinin; Lieutenant General of the Air Force Batitsky; Rear Admiral Yakovlev; Sinilov, Garrison Commander of Moscow; Fedrin, Member of the Special Committee of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Slatkovsky, Director of the Eastern Department of the Ministry of Foreign Trade; and Bastoyev, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Also at the airport to see them off were Tsedenbal, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic; Bat, Minister of National Defence of the Mongolian People's Republic; and Sambu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic.

Also at the airport to see them off were: Li Fuchun, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government; Chen Yu, Minister of Fuel Industry; Song Shaowen, Secretary-General of the Financial and Economic Committee; Liu Yalou, Commander of the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Qiu Chuangcheng, Deputy Commander of the Artillery; Wang Daohan, Deputy Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building; Wang Zheng, Deputy Minister of Posts and Telecommunications; Shi Zhe, Political Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and personnel of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, headed by Zhang Wentian, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union.

Also at the airport to see them off were: Blagoeva, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to the Soviet Union; Iden-khabu, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to the Soviet Union; Zokrátán, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to the Soviet Union; U Aung, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Union of Burma to the Soviet Union; Rim Hai, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Soviet Union; Nguyễn Lương Bằng, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union; Dalea, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Romanian People's Republic to the Soviet Union; Abel, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to the Soviet Union; Nata Nelly, Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Albania to the Soviet Union; Elia Shiv, Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary of Israel to the Soviet Union; Pogorelez, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish People's Republic; and Etsni, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Czechoslovak Republic.

An honor guard was lined up at the airport, and the national anthems of the People's Republic of China and the Soviet Union were played.

The national flags of the People's Republic of China and the Soviet Union were hung at the airport.

The Soviet government specially dispatched Kurachenkov, Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, to escort the Chinese government delegation to the Sino-Soviet border.

### 周恩来总理等离苏返国  莫洛托夫泽登巴尔到机场送行

1952-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理等离苏返国
    莫洛托夫泽登巴尔到机场送行
    【新华社莫斯科二十三日电】中华人民共和国政府代表团首席代表、中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来于二十二日乘飞机离莫斯科返国。同行者有政务院副总理陈云，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长粟裕，重工业部部长王鹤寿，中国人民解放军海军副司令员罗舜初，中央人民政府外交部苏联东欧司司长徐以新，外交部亚洲司司长陈家康等人。
    到莫斯科中央机场送行的有：苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫、米高扬、布尔加宁，外交部部长维辛斯基，对外贸易部部长库米金，苏联驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使潘友新，莫斯科市苏维埃主席雅斯诺夫，对外贸易部副部长鲍里邵夫，苏联驻中华人民共和国商务代表米古诺夫，陆军上将马里宁，空军中将巴蒂茨基，海军少将雅克武列夫，莫斯科市卫戍司令西尼洛夫，苏联外交部专门委员会委员费德林，对外贸易部东方司司长斯拉特科夫斯基，苏联外交部交际司副司长巴斯托耶夫。
    到机场送行的还有蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔，蒙古人民共和国国防部部长巴特，蒙古人民共和国外交部副部长桑布。
    到机场送行的还有：我中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会副主任李富春，燃料工业部部长陈郁，财政经济委员会秘书长宋劭文，中国人民解放军空军司令员刘亚楼，炮兵副司令员邱创成，第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，邮电部副部长王诤，外交部政治秘书师哲以及以我驻苏特命全权大使张闻天为首的驻苏大使馆人员。
    到机场送行的还有：保加利亚人民共和国驻苏特命全权大使布拉果耶娃，蒙古人民共和国驻苏特命全权大使伊丹甲布，匈牙利人民共和国驻苏特命全权大使佐克拉顿，缅甸联邦驻苏特命全权大使宇昂，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻苏特命全权大使林海，越南民主共和国驻苏特命全权大使阮良朋，罗马尼亚人民共和国驻苏特命全权大使达列亚，德意志民主共和国驻苏外交使团团长阿贝尔，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻苏特命全权公使纳塔奈利，以色列驻苏特命全权公使伊利亚希夫，波兰人民共和国临时代办波果雷列斯，捷克斯洛伐克共和国临时代办埃茨尼。
    飞机场上排列着仪仗队，并奏中华人民共和国和苏联的国歌。
    飞机场上挂着中华人民共和国和苏联的国旗。
    苏联政府特派苏联外交部交际司司长库拉仁科夫送我政府代表团到中苏边境。
