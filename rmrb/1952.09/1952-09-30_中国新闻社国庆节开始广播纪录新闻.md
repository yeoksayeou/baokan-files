Title: China News Service to Begin Broadcasting Documentary News on National Day
Author:
Date: 1952-09-30
Page: 3

The China News Service is a news organization formed by people in domestic news circles. Jin Zhonghua is the president. The board of directors consists of thirty-three directors, with the following names:

Wang Yunsheng, Wang Jiyuan, Fang Junzhuang, Situ Huimin, Sima Wensen, Wang Jinding, Shen Zijiu, Jin Zhonghua, Meng Qiujiang, Shao Zonghan, Hu Yuzhi, Hu Weifu, Hu Yisheng, Hong Sisi, Xu Zhucheng, Xu Ying, Gao Tian, Zhang Chukun, Feng Binfu, Chen Hansheng, Chen Hanbo, Lu Yi, Peng Hesheng, Peng Zigang, Fei Zhendong, Huang Yaomian, Yang Gang, Zheng Senyu, Liu Simu, Liu Zunqi, Lu Xinyuan, Sa Kongliao, Xiao Lin.

The agency is scheduled to officially begin broadcasting documentary news and sending out news releases on National Day, October 1st, specifically for overseas Chinese newspapers to publish. In terms of broadcasting, it is currently tentatively scheduled to broadcast twice daily. The first broadcast time is from 8:00 AM to 10:00 AM (Beijing time). Wavelengths are 16.38 meters, 16.9 meters, 19.1 meters, 19.49 meters, and 21.74 meters. Frequencies are 18320, 17740, 15682, and 15426. The second broadcast time is from 7:00 PM to 11:18 PM (Beijing time). Wavelengths are 19.3 meters, 25.15 meters, 31.81 meters, and 30.74 meters. Frequencies are 15572, 11929, 9430, and 9757. As for news releases, they are tentatively scheduled to be sent out once a week, each time approximately 20,000 words.
                                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中国新闻社国庆节开始广播纪录新闻

1952-09-30
第3版()
专栏：

    中国新闻社国庆节开始广播纪录新闻
    中国新闻社，是国内新闻界人士所组织的新闻团体。社长金仲华。理事会由理事三十三人组成，名单如次：
    王芸生、王纪元、方君壮、司徒慧敏、司马文森、汪金丁、沈兹九、金仲华、孟秋江、邵宗汉、胡愈之、胡伟夫、胡一声、洪丝丝、徐铸成、徐盈、高天、张楚琨、冯宾符、陈翰笙、陈翰伯、陆诒、彭赫生、彭子冈、费振东、黄药眠、杨刚、郑森禹、刘思慕、刘尊棋、卢心远、萨空了、萧林。
    该社定十月一日国庆节起正式开始广播纪录新闻及寄发通讯稿，专供海外华侨报纸刊载。广播方面，目前暂定每日广播二次，第一次广播时间是早上八时至十时（北京时间）。波长一六·三八公尺，一六·九公尺，一九·一公尺，一九·四九公尺，二一·七四公尺。频率一八三二○，一七七四○，一五六八二，一五四二六。第二次广播时间是十九时至二十三时十八分（北京时间）。波长一九·三公尺，二五·一五公尺，三一·八一公尺，三○·七四公尺。频率一五五七二，一一九二九，九四三○，九七五七。通讯稿方面，暂定每周寄发一次，每次约二万余字。
                                                  （新华社）
