Title: India's New Ambassador to China Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-09-14
Page: 1
　
    Xinhua News Agency, 13th: The new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of India to China, Mr. R.K. Nehru, arrived in Beijing by car at 8:52 a.m. on the 13th. Those who went to the station to greet him were: Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, He Ying, Deputy Director of the Asian Department, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and Zeng Mei, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command. Also at the station to welcome him were diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China, as well as Mr. K.N. Gahl, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indian Embassy in China, and all members of the embassy.

### 印度新任驻我国大使抵北京

1952-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

    印度新任驻我国大使抵北京
    【新华社十三日讯】印度共和国新任驻我国特命全权大使赖嘉文于十三日上午八时五十二分乘车抵京。赴车站迎接的有：中央人民政府外交部办公厅主任王炳南、亚洲司副司长何英、交际处处长王倬如、北京市人民政府副市长吴晗和京津卫戍司令部副参谋长曾美等。到车站欢迎的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员，以及印度驻华大使馆临时代办高尔及全体馆员。
