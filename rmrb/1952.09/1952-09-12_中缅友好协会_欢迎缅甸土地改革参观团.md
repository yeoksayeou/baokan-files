Title: China-Myanmar Friendship Association Welcomes Myanmar Land Reform Delegation
Author:
Date: 1952-09-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th September — The President of the China-Myanmar Friendship Association, Zheng Zhenduo, and Vice President Qian Weichang, held a reception on the evening of the 11th to welcome the Myanmar Land Reform Delegation.

Invited to the reception were: Minister of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, Li Shucheng, Vice Ministers Yang Xiandong, Vice Minister of Water Resources Department Zhang Hanying, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, He Ying, Director of the Protocol Department, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Central People's Government, Yu Xinqing, Deputy Director of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, Meng Yongqian, Head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation to Burma, Ding Xilin, Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Zhang Youyu, and others.

The Myanmar Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, and diplomatic personnel from the Myanmar Embassy were also invited to attend the reception.

At the meeting, President Zheng Zhenduo delivered a speech first. He expressed a warm welcome to the delegation for coming to China to visit the new countryside after the land reform.

The head of the Myanmar Land Reform Delegation, Tekin Chin, then delivered a speech. He said that he was very happy to see an organization like the China-Myanmar Friendship Association in China. He said: I agree that the friendship between China and Myanmar should be built on economic and cultural grounds, and I believe that the friendship between the two countries will definitely be consolidated.

The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 中缅友好协会　欢迎缅甸土地改革参观团

1952-09-12
第4版()
专栏：

    中缅友好协会
    欢迎缅甸土地改革参观团
    【新华社十一日讯】中缅友好协会会长郑振铎和副会长钱伟长，在十一日晚举行酒会欢迎缅甸土地改革参观团。
    应邀参加酒会的有：中央人民政府农业部部长李书城、副部长杨显东、水利部副部长张含英、外交部亚洲司副司长何英、交际处处长王倬如、中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清、中华全国合作社联合总社副主任孟用潜、我国前赴缅甸文化代表团团长丁西林、北京市人民政府副市长张友渔等。
    缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂和缅甸大使馆外交人员也应邀参加酒会。
    会上，郑振铎会长首先致词。他对参观团来到中国参观土地改革后的新农村表示热烈的欢迎。
    缅甸土地改革参观团团长德钦阵接着致词。他说他很高兴在中国看见中缅友好协会这样的组织。他说：我赞成中缅两国的友谊建立在经济上和文化上，我相信两国友谊一定会巩固的。
    酒会在友好的空气中进行。
