Title: Icelandic Delegation Arrives in Beijing at the Invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs
Author:
Date: 1952-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th—The Icelandic delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 28th, led by the head of the delegation, Mr. Keldal. Welcoming them at the airport was Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and others.

Delegation Leader: Keldal, Famous Poet
Delegation Member: Dúdason, Writer
Delegation Member: Hannesson, Southern Manager of the Icelandic Cooperative Society, Former Member of Parliament (yet to arrive in Beijing)
Delegation Member: Dúttarson, Historian
Delegation Member: Jónasson, Worker (yet to arrive in Beijing)
Delegation Member: Ms. Ólafssdóttir, Reykjavik City Councillor, Women's Magazine Editor.

### 应中国人民外交学会的邀请  冰岛访华代表团抵京

1952-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    应中国人民外交学会的邀请
    冰岛访华代表团抵京
    【新华社二十八日讯】应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的冰岛访华代表团，在团长凯德隆率领下，已在二十八日乘飞机抵北京，到机场欢迎的有中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪等人。团长　凯德隆　名诗人团员　杜达生作家团员　汉那生　冰岛合作社南部经理、
                    前议员（尚未到京）团员　都泰生　历史学家团员　耶那生　工人（尚未到京）团员　奥那道蒂女士　雷克雅未克市政委员、
                    妇女杂志编辑。
