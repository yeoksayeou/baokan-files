Title: Secretary of the Preparatory Committee Secretariat for the Peace Conference, Mrs. Kumudini Mehta of India, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-09-10
Page: 1

    Peace Conference Preparatory Committee Secretariat Secretary
    Indian Mrs. Kumudini Mehta Arrives in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, September 9th] Mrs. Kumudini Mehta of India, one of the secretaries of the Secretariat of the Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, arrived in Beijing on the 9th to participate in the preparatory work for the Peace Conference. Several Chinese staff members of the Secretariat of the Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference went to the station to welcome her.

### 和平会议筹委会秘书处秘书  印度库穆丁妮·梅达夫人抵京

1952-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    和平会议筹委会秘书处秘书
    印度库穆丁妮·梅达夫人抵京
    【新华社九日讯】亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会秘书处的秘书之一、印度的库穆丁妮·梅达夫人，于九日抵达北京，参加和平会议的筹备工作。前往车站欢迎的有亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会秘书处的中国工作人员多人。
