Title: British Labour MP Davies Says Evidence of US Germ Warfare is Conclusive
Author:
Date: 1952-09-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 10th, Dispatch from TASS, London, September 9th: The London "Daily Worker" reported: Labour MP Davies said on the 8th that he was "absolutely convinced that the evidence of germ warfare brought back from China by the Archbishop of Canterbury, Johnson, is true." He added: "When the House of Commons convenes in October, I will definitely raise this issue."

### 英国工党议员戴维斯谈  美国进行细菌战证据确凿

1952-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

    英国工党议员戴维斯谈
    美国进行细菌战证据确凿
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社伦敦九日讯：伦敦“工人日报”报道：工党议员戴维斯在八日说，他“绝对相信坎特伯雷教长约翰逊从中国带回来的关于细菌战的证据是真实的”。他又说：“十月间下议院开会的时候，我一定要把这个问题提出来。”
