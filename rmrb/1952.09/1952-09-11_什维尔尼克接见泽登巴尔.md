Title: Shvernik Receives Tsedenbal
Author:
Date: 1952-09-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 10th: TASS Moscow, September 10th: The Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Shvernik, received the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Tsedenbal, on September 9th.
    Also received were the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic, Zhamtsarangiin Sambuu, and the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to the Soviet Union, IIdamjab. Present at the meeting were Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Podtserob, and Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Gorkin.

### 什维尔尼克接见泽登巴尔

1952-09-11
第4版()
专栏：

    什维尔尼克接见泽登巴尔
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席什维尔尼克于九月九日接见蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔。
    同时被接见的有蒙古人民共和国外交部副部长扎姆索伦—桑布和蒙古人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使伊丹甲布。出席作陪的有苏联外交部副部长波德切罗布和苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书高尔金。
