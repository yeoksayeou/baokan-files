Title: New Indian Ambassador to China, Raghavan, Pays Courtesy Call on Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu
Author:
Date: 1952-09-17
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, 15th. New Indian Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Raghavan, accompanied by the Indian Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, K.K. Chettur, paid a courtesy call to Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 4:00 PM on the 15th to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Also present were: Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, He Ying, Deputy Director of the Asian Department, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 印度新任驻华大使赖嘉文拜会我外交部副部长章汉夫

1952-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

    印度新任驻华大使赖嘉文拜会我外交部副部长章汉夫
    【新华社十五日讯】新任印度驻华特命全权大使赖嘉文偕印度驻华大使馆临时代办高尔于十五日下午四时，赴外交部拜会章汉夫副部长，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座者尚有：外交部办公厅主任王炳南，亚洲司副司长何英及交际处处长王倬如。
