Title: Korean and Mongolian Delegations to Peace Conference Arrived in Beijing Yesterday; Indian Delegation Leader Dr. Kitchlew and Others Arrived in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1952-09-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th: The Korean and Mongolian delegations to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference arrived in Beijing on the 20th by train and plane respectively. Those who went to the station and airport to welcome them included: Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Liu Guan, Secretary-General, Li Dequan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Zeng Zhaolun, Vice Chairman of the China Association for Science and Technology, and Jin Ziming, Deputy Minister of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. Also present at the station and airport to welcome them were: D. W. Wickramanayake, head of the Ceylon delegation, Representative of Sangamkanta, Utojo, Representative of Indonesia, Xu Chunzhi, Representative of Korea (who had arrived in Beijing earlier), Noha, Head of Laos Delegation, Lodejdamva, Representative of Mongolia, Rewi Alley and Archibald, Representatives of New Zealand, Kozhevnikov, Representative of the Soviet Union, Nguyen Xuan Thuy, Deputy Head of the Vietnamese Delegation, Yang Bailian and Wu La of Burma. Kwon O-jik, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and embassy staff also went to welcome the Korean delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 20th: The Indian delegation to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, consisting of eighteen members led by Dr. Saifuddin Kitchlew, arrived in Guangzhou on the afternoon of the 20th. Zhong Ming, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Branch of the China Peace Committee, and more than 200 representatives from all walks of life went to the station to welcome them. The delegates received flowers from the Young Pioneers after getting off the train. In the evening, the Guangzhou Branch of the China Peace Committee hosted a banquet to welcome them.

### 朝鲜蒙古出席和平会议两代表团昨抵京  印度代表团团长克其鲁博士等已抵达广州

1952-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜蒙古出席和平会议两代表团昨抵京
    印度代表团团长克其鲁博士等已抵达广州
    【新华社二十日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的朝鲜代表团和蒙古代表团，于二十日分别乘火车和飞机抵达北京。先后前往车站和机场欢迎的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通、秘书长刘贯一，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全，中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会副主席曾昭抡，中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长金子明等人。先后到车站和机场欢迎的，还有出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的锡兰代表团团长达马拉塔纳、代表桑姆加塔桑，印度尼西亚代表乌托西亚，朝鲜代表徐春植，寮国代表团团长诺哈，蒙古代表洛代丹巴，新西兰代表艾黎和艾启赫，苏联代表科热夫尼科夫，越南代表团副团长阮春水、代表杨白莲及缅甸的吴拉等。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使权五稷及使馆馆员也曾前往欢迎朝鲜代表团。
