Title: After Visiting Ethnic Minorities in Northeast and Other Regions, Central Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-09-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 23rd:

    The Central Delegation, representing the Central People's Government, has successfully completed its mission of visiting various ethnic minorities in the Northeast region, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and Suiyuan Province after more than two months. It returned to Beijing today. Those who went to the station to welcome the delegation included Li Weihan, Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government; Liu Geping, Vice Chairman; Liu Chun, Luo Changpei, Fei Xiaotong, Zhao Fan, and Yang Jingren, members in Beijing; and representatives from nine units including the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government and the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government.

### 访问东北等地少数民族后  中央访问团返抵北京

1952-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    访问东北等地少数民族后
    中央访问团返抵北京
    【新华社二十三日讯】代表中央人民政府访问东北地区和内蒙古自治区及绥远省各少数民族的中央访问团，经两个多月时间，已胜利完成工作任务，今日返抵北京。前往车站欢迎的有中央人民政府民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉、副主任委员刘格平，在京委员刘春、罗常培、费孝通、赵范、杨静仁，和中央人民政府内务部、中央人民政府卫生部等九个单位的代表。
