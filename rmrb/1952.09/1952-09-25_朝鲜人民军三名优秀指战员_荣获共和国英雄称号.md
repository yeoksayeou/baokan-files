Title: Three Outstanding Commanders and Fighters of the Korean People's Army Conferred the Title of Hero of the Republic
Author:
Date: 1952-09-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 22nd: The Korean newspapers published an order issued by the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, which stipulated that the title of Hero of the Republic, as well as the First Class National Flag Order and the "Gold Star" Medal, be awarded to commanders Jiang Shengheng (transliteration), Jin Huayan (transliteration), and Sergeant Li Beibi (transliteration), in recognition of their heroic deeds in the patriotic liberation war against the American interventionist army and its accomplices for the unification and independence of the motherland.

### 朝鲜人民军三名优秀指战员  荣获共和国英雄称号

1952-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民军三名优秀指战员
    荣获共和国英雄称号
    【新华社讯】塔斯社平壤二十二日电：朝鲜各报刊载朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会所发布的命令，该命令规定以共和国英雄称号以及一级国旗勋章与“金星”奖章，授予指挥员江声亨（译音），金华延（译音）与军士李北壁（译音），以表扬他们在反对美国干涉军及其帮凶，争取祖国的统一与独立的爱国解放战争中所创造的英雄事迹。
