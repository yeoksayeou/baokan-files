Title: Representative of China Leaves Beijing for Budapest to Attend Meeting of International Broadcasting Organization Council
Author:
Date: 1952-09-17
Page: 4

    To Attend Meeting of International Broadcasting Organization Council
    Representative of China Leaves Beijing for Budapest
    [Xinhua News Agency, 16th] The 22nd meeting of the Council of the International Broadcasting Organization and the 7th meeting of the Technical Committee will be held in Budapest, the capital of Hungary, on the 23rd. Mei Yi, the representative of China attending the meeting and Director of the Broadcasting Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council, left Beijing by plane on the 15th to attend the meeting. He was accompanied by advisors Liu Yongye, Wu Zhan, and translator Tang Xianyao. The Broadcasting Bureau of our country joined the International Broadcasting Organization in December last year and is a formal member and permanent director of the organization.

### 出席国际广播组织理事会议  我国代表离京赴布达佩斯

1952-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

    出席国际广播组织理事会议
    我国代表离京赴布达佩斯
    【新华社十六日讯】国际广播组织理事会第二十二次会议和技术委员会第七次会议将于二十三日在匈牙利首都布达佩斯举行。我国出席会议的代表、政务院文化教育委员会广播事业局局长梅益，已在十五日乘飞机离北京赴会。随同前往的还有顾问刘永嶫、吴展及翻译唐贤耀三人。按我国广播事业局是在去年十二月参加国际广播组织的，为该组织的正式会员及常任理事。
