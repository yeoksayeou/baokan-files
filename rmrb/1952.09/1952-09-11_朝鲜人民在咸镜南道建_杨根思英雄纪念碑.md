Title: Korean People Erect "Yang Gensi Hero Monument" in South Hamgyong Province
Author:
Date: 1952-09-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 7th. To forever commemorate the Chinese People's Volunteer Army hero company commander Yang Gensi, the Korean people specially established the "Yang Gensi Hero Monument" in Changjin County, South Hamgyong Province, and recently held a grand groundbreaking ceremony. Government staff, representatives of various democratic parties and people's organizations in South Hamgyong Province, Korea, and local Korean people attended the groundbreaking ceremony. Kim Jang-rip, Chairman of the Changjin County Party Committee of the Korean Labor Party, delivered a speech at the ceremony. He said: The winter before last, when our country was in a very critical situation, the great Chinese People's Volunteer Army severely attacked the American invading army in the Changjin Lake area. It was at that time that Comrade Yang Gensi sacrificed heroically for the immortal feats he performed for the Korean people. We will never forget Comrade Yang Gensi's heroic spirit and great internationalist spirit for generations to come.

### 朝鲜人民在咸镜南道建“杨根思英雄纪念碑”

1952-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民在咸镜南道建“杨根思英雄纪念碑”
    【新华社朝鲜前线七日电】为永远纪念中国人民志愿军英雄连长杨根思烈士，朝鲜人民特在咸镜南道长津郡建立“杨根思英雄纪念碑”，并于最近举行了隆重的奠基典礼。朝鲜咸镜南道的政府工作人员、各民主党派、各人民团体代表及当地的朝鲜人民参加了奠基典礼。朝鲜劳动党长津郡党部委员长金长立在典礼上致词，他说：前年冬天，当我们祖国正在十分危急的时候，伟大的中国人民志愿军曾在长津湖地区沉重地打击了美国侵略军。杨根思烈士就是在那时为我们朝鲜人民立下了不朽的功勋而壮烈牺牲的。我们世世代代不会忘记杨根思烈士的英雄气魄和伟大的国际主义精神。
