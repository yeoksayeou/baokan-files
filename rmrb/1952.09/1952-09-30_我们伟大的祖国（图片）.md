    Our Great Motherland
    On the eve of National Day, Tiananmen Square is brightly lit by millions of lights. Beneath the majestic national-style towers and beams, the emerald green and golden paintings are exceptionally vivid; the vermilion columns and white marble railings reflect a solemn radiance. The lights in the square, both near and far, set off this magnificent gate tower, filling people with boundless joy for the coming great festival.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国庆节前夕的天安门，被千万盏灯光照耀得通明。在巍峨的民族的形式的塔下和梁枋上，翠绿色和金色的彩画分外鲜艳；朱红色的圆柱和汉白玉的栏杆映射着肃穆的光辉。广场远近的灯火，衬托着这座壮丽的门楼，使人们充满了伟大的节日来临的无限喜悦。
