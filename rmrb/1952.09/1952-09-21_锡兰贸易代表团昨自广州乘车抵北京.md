Title: Ceylon Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing by Train from Guangzhou Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-09-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 20th. The Ceylon Trade Delegation, led by Mr. S.S. De S. Jayawickrame, arrived in Beijing from Guangzhou on the afternoon of the 20th.

Those who met the delegation at the station included: Mr. Ye Ji-zhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government; Mr. Ji Chao-ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade (representing Mr. Nan Han-chen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade); Mr. Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; and Mr. Lu Xuzhang, Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, and others.

Also present at the station were: U Ohn Maung, Burmese Ambassador to China; Mr. Kaul, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Indian Embassy in China; and Mr. Isa Matari, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy in China.

The Ceylon Trade Delegation received flowers from the welcomers at the station.

The list of the Ceylon Trade Delegation is as follows:

Head of Delegation: Mr. S.S. De S. Jayawickrame, Minister of Commerce and Trade of Ceylon

Members: Mr. Foenander, Ceylon's Minister to Burma.
Mr. Kotalawala, Parliamentary Secretary to the Minister of Agriculture and Food of Ceylon.
Mr. Jayanetti, Permanent Secretary to the Minister of Commerce and Trade of Ceylon.
Mr. Silva, Assistant Secretary to the Minister of Agriculture and Food of Ceylon.
Mr. Manicavasagar, Assistant to the Minister of Commerce and Trade of Ceylon.
Mr. Duraiswamy, Secretary of the Ceylon Overseas Service.

### 锡兰贸易代表团昨自广州乘车抵北京

1952-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    锡兰贸易代表团昨自广州乘车抵北京
    【新华社二十日讯】锡兰贸易代表团一行七人，在团长沈纳那亚克率领下，于二十日下午自广州到达北京。
    到车站迎接的有：中央人民政府对外贸易部部长叶季壮，中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎
    （代表中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸），中央人民政府外交部交际处处长王倬如，中国进出口公司经理卢绪章等人。
    到车站迎接的还有：缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂，印度驻华大使馆临时代办高尔，印度尼西亚驻华大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂。
    锡兰贸易代表团在车站接受了欢迎者的献花。
    锡兰贸易代表团名单如下：
    团长  沈纳那亚克锡兰商务贸易部部长
    团员  方席卡锡兰驻缅甸公使
    柯马拉赛密  锡兰农业粮食部国会秘书
    贾雅诺特尼  锡兰商务贸易部常任秘书
    席尔瓦  锡兰农业粮食部助理秘书
    马延斯亭  锡兰商务贸易部助理
    杜锐斯瓦密  锡兰海外服务部秘书
