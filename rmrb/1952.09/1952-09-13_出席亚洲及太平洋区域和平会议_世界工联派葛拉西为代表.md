Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Sends Grassi as Representative to Attend the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-09-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, September 11th: TASS reported that the Secretariat of the World Federation of Trade Unions (WFTU) stated: The Preparatory Committee of the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference invited the WFTU to send a representative to Beijing to attend the conference. The Secretariat of the WFTU, considering the importance of this conference, decided to send WFTU Secretary Grassi as its representative to attend the conference.

### 出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  世界工联派葛拉西为代表

1952-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    世界工联派葛拉西为代表
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社维也纳十一日讯：世界工会联合会书记处称：亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会邀请世界工联派代表到北京参加会议。世界工联书记处考虑到这个会议的重要性，决定派遣世界工联书记葛拉西为代表出席会议。
