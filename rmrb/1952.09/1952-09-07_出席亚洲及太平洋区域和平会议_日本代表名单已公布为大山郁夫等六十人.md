Title: List of Japanese Representatives to Attend the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference Announced: Ikuo Oyama and Sixty Others

Author: 
Date: 1952-09-07
Page: 1

    Attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference
    List of Japanese Representatives Announced: Ikuo Oyama and Sixty Others
    [Xinhua News Agency, 6th] TASS, Tokyo, 6th: "Tokyo Shimbun" reports: The Japanese Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference has announced the list of sixty Japanese representatives attending the conference. The representatives include: Ikuo Oyama, Jiichiro Matsumoto, and Hiroshi Suekawa, President of Ritsumeikan University.

### 出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  日本代表名单已公布为大山郁夫等六十人

1952-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    日本代表名单已公布为大山郁夫等六十人
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社东京六日讯：“东京新闻”报道：亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议日本筹备委员会，公布了出席会议的六十位日本代表的名单。代表中有：大山郁夫、松本治一郎及立命馆大学校长末川博。
