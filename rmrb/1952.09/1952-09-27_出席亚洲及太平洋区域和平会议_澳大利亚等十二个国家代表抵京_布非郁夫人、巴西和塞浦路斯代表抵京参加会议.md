Title: Representatives from Twelve Countries Including Australia Arrive in Beijing for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference; Madame Bufeiyu, Representatives from Brazil and Cyprus Arrive in Beijing to Attend the Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-09-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: A portion of the representatives from twelve countries, including Australia, Colombia, El Salvador, Ecuador, Guatemala, India, Indonesia, Mexico, New Zealand, Nicaragua, Pakistan, and the United States, attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, arrived in Beijing on the 26th by train and plane successively. Madame Bufeiyu, treasurer of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, the representative of Brazil, and the representative of Cyprus, who came to Beijing to attend the peace conference, also arrived in Beijing on the 26th. Those who went to the train station and airport to welcome them included: Chandra, Deputy Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference; Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Cai Tingkai, member; Wu Yaozong, Hu Wenyao, and Chen Hansheng, Chinese representatives attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference; Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association; Cao Mengjun, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and people from religious and scientific circles. Also going to the train station and airport to welcome them were representatives from Costa Rica, Colombia, Guatemala, India, Indonesia, Mexico, New Zealand, and the United States who had previously arrived in Beijing to attend the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference.

### 出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  澳大利亚等十二个国家代表抵京  布非郁夫人、巴西和塞浦路斯代表抵京参加会议

1952-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    澳大利亚等十二个国家代表抵京
    布非郁夫人、巴西和塞浦路斯代表抵京参加会议
    【新华社二十六日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的澳大利亚、哥伦比亚、萨尔瓦多、厄瓜多尔、危地马拉、印度、印度尼西亚、墨西哥、新西兰、尼加拉瓜、巴基斯坦和美国等十二个国家的一部分代表于二十六日先后乘火车和飞机抵达北京。前来北京参加和平会议的国际民主法律工作者协会司库布非郁夫人、巴西代表和塞浦路斯代表也在二十六日抵京。先后前往车站和机场欢迎的有：亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会副秘书长钱德拉，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通、委员蔡廷锴，出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的中国代表吴耀宗、胡文耀、陈翰笙，中印友好协会会长丁西林，中华全国民主妇女联合会副秘书长曹孟君及宗教界、科学界人士等。先后前往车站和机场欢迎的还有前已到京的哥斯达黎加、哥伦比亚、危地马拉、印度、印度尼西亚、墨西哥、新西兰和美国出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的代表等。
