Title: Liu Ningyi Sends Telegram of Congratulations to the American "New York-Beijing Peace Rally"
Author: 
Date: 1952-09-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. Liu Ningyi, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, sent a telegram of congratulations to the American people who will hold the "New York-Beijing Peace Rally" in New York on the 25th. The telegram reads: To the Committee in New York that promotes the participation of the American People in the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference and to the "New York-Beijing Peace Rally":

    On the eve of the opening of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference in Beijing, I am sending my congratulations to your "New York-Beijing Peace Rally" in New York with great excitement. I believe that this rally will express the will of the vast majority of the American people who are against war and demand peace, and that this rally will closely link the hearts of the American people with the peace-loving hearts of the people of all countries in the Asian and Pacific regions. I wish you further success in your noble cause of fighting for peace.

    Liu Ningyi, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference

### 刘宁一电贺美国“纽约—北京和平集会”

1952-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    刘宁一电贺美国“纽约—北京和平集会”
    【新华社二十二日讯】美国人民将于二十五日在纽约举行“纽约—北京和平集会”，亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会秘书长刘宁一特致电祝贺：电文如下：纽约促使美国人民参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议委员会并转“纽约—北京和平集会”：
    在这亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议在北京揭幕的前夕，我以十分兴奋的心情祝贺你们在纽约举行的“纽约—北京和平集会”。我相信：这个集会将表达美国最大多数人民反对战争要求和平的意志，这个集会将使美国人民和整个亚洲及太平洋区域各国人民的爱好和平的心紧紧连在一起。谨祝你们在为和平而奋斗的崇高事业中取得进一步的成就。
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会秘书长  刘宁一
