Title: Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu Hosts Banquet for Burmese Land Reform Delegation
Author:
Date: 1952-09-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu hosted a banquet on the evening of the 9th to welcome the Burmese Land Reform Delegation.

Those attending the banquet included: Minister of Agriculture of the Central People's Government Li Shucheng, Vice Minister Yang Xiandong, Vice Minister of Water Resources Zhang Hanying, Deputy Director of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives Meng Yongqian, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Zheng Zhenduo, Vice President Qian Weichang, the head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation to Burma Ding Xilin, and Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Zhang Youyu.

Burmese Ambassador to China U Hla Maung and diplomatic personnel from the Burmese Embassy were also invited to attend the banquet.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently raised their glasses to congratulate the increase in friendly relations between China and Burma.

The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 外交部章汉夫副部长  欢宴缅甸土地改革参观团

1952-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    外交部章汉夫副部长
    欢宴缅甸土地改革参观团
    【新华社九日讯】中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫九日晚举行宴会，招待缅甸土地改革参观团。
    出席作陪的有：中央人民政府农业部部长李书城、副部长杨显东、水利部副部长张含英、中华全国合作社联合总社副主任孟用潜、中缅友好协会会长郑振铎、副会长钱伟长、我国前赴缅甸文化代表团团长丁西林和北京市人民政府副市长张友渔等。
    缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂和缅甸大使馆外交人员也应邀参加宴会。
    席间，宾主频频举杯祝贺中缅两国友好关系的增进。
    宴会在友好的气氛中进行。
