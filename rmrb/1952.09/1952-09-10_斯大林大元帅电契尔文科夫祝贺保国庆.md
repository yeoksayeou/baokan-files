Title: Marshal Stalin Sends Congratulations to Chervenkov on Bulgarian National Day
Author:
Date: 1952-09-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 9th: TASS, Moscow, September 9th: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, J.V. Stalin, sent a telegram to Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, Chervenkov, congratulating him on the eighth anniversary of the National Day of Bulgaria. The telegram reads as follows:

    On the occasion of the National Day of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, please accept, Comrade Chairman, my sincere congratulations and wishes for further achievements to the fraternal Bulgarian people in the work of building a new People's Democratic Bulgaria.

    J. Stalin (signed)

Furthermore, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, Chervenkov, sent a telegram to Marshal Stalin to express his respect. Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Shvernik, and Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, Damyanov, as well as Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, Vyshinsky, and Minister of Foreign Affairs of Bulgaria, Najdenov, also sent congratulatory telegrams to each other respectively.

### 斯大林大元帅电契尔文科夫祝贺保国庆

1952-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    斯大林大元帅电契尔文科夫祝贺保国庆
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科九日讯：苏联部长会议主席约·维·斯大林致电保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席契尔文科夫，祝贺保加利亚国庆八周年，电文如下：
    值此保加利亚人民共和国国庆节之际，我请您，主席同志，接受我的热诚的贺意和祝愿，祝兄弟般的保加利亚人民在建设新的人民民主的保加利亚的工作中获得进一步的成就。
    约·斯大林（签字）
    又讯：保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席契尔文科夫致电斯大林大元帅表示敬意。苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席什维尔尼克和保加利亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席达米扬诺夫，苏联外交部长维辛斯基和保加利亚外交部长奈捷夫也分别互相致电祝贺。
