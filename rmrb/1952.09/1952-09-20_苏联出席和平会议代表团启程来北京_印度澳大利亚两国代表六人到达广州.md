Title: Soviet Delegation to Peace Conference Departs for Beijing; Six Representatives from India and Australia Arrive in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1952-09-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th: According to TASS, Moscow: The Soviet delegation to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, led by Professor Anisimov, Head of the Delegation and member of the Soviet Peace Committee, departed from Moscow by plane on the 19th to Beijing. The delegation members traveling to Beijing on the same plane included:贺捷耶夫, Chairman of the Peace Committee of the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic; Philosophy Professor佛米纳; writer萨德; and 叶菲莫夫, member of the Leningrad Peace Committee and lecturer at Leningrad State University.
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 19th: Luo Mi Xi·Qian De La, Deputy Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference and representative of India; Jie An·Chang De, Deputy Head of the Indian Delegation attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference; representatives Sa La·Ge Ba and La Ge Ha Fa Yi Hei; and Australian representatives Gao Tuo and Victor James, a total of six people, arrived in Guangzhou on the 18th. Guo Qiaoran, Deputy Secretary-General of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and representatives from all walks of life, numbering over one hundred, went to the station to welcome them.

### 苏联出席和平会议代表团启程来北京  印度澳大利亚两国代表六人到达广州

1952-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联出席和平会议代表团启程来北京
    印度澳大利亚两国代表六人到达广州
    【新华社十九日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的苏联代表团由代表团团长、苏联拥护和平委员会委员阿尼西莫夫教授率领，在十九日由莫斯科乘飞机来北京。同机来北京的代表团团员包括：乌兹别克苏维埃社会主义共和国和平委员会主席贺捷耶夫，哲学教授佛米纳，作家萨德和列宁格勒和平委员会委员、列宁格勒国立大学讲师叶菲莫夫。
    【新华社广州十九日电】亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会副秘书长、印度代表罗米西·钱德拉和出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的印度代表团副团长杰安·昌德，代表撒拉·格巴和拉格哈伐依黑，澳大利亚代表高脱和维克多·詹姆斯六人，已于十八日到达广州。前往车站欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广州分会副秘书长郭翘然以及各界代表一百余人。
