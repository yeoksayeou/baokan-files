Title: Over a Hundred Cadres and Students of All Ethnic Groups from the CCP Central Committee Xinjiang Bureau Cadre School Join the Party
Author:
Date: 1952-09-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Dihua, 4th: Recently, 140 cadres and students of various ethnic groups from the Cadre School of the Xinjiang Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China gloriously joined the Communist Party of China. These new party members include twelve ethnic groups, including Uyghur, Kazakh, Han, Mongolian, Tatar, Uzbek, Hui, Kirgiz, Sibe, Solon, and Tajik. Over the past year, they have studied the policies of the People's Government, the history of the Communist Party of China, the Party Constitution, and the eight requirements for Party member standards, and their political and class consciousness has greatly improved, basically meeting the conditions of a Communist Party member. At the swearing-in ceremony for new party members, they unanimously stated: In the future, they will forever follow Chairman Mao and the Communist Party, and serve the people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang with loyalty.

### 中共中央新疆分局干部学校  各族学员和干部百余人入党

1952-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央新疆分局干部学校
    各族学员和干部百余人入党
    【新华社迪化四日电】中共中央新疆分局干部学校最近有一百四十名各民族学员和工作干部光荣地加入中国共产党。这些新党员包括维吾尔、哈萨克、汉、蒙古、塔塔尔、乌孜别克、回、柯尔克孜、锡伯、索伦、塔吉克等十二个民族。一年来，他们学习了人民政府的政策，中国共产党党史、党纲党章、党员标准的八项条件，政治觉悟和阶级觉悟都有了很大提高，基本具有共产党员的条件。在新党员入党宣誓大会上，他们一致表示：今后要永远跟着毛主席和共产党走，忠诚地为新疆各民族人民服务。
