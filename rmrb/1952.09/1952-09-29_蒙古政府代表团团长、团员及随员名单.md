Title: List of Heads, Members, and Staff of the Mongolian Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1952-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: The composition of the Government Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, which arrived in Beijing on September 28th, is as follows:

    Head of the delegation: Yu. Tsedenbal, Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic; Members of the delegation: N. Luvsan, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic; B. Shirendev, Minister of Education of the Mongolian People's Republic; B. Jargalsaikhan, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to the People's Republic of China; Staff of the delegation: Dulebuerjin, Puerbuzhale. (Attached picture)

    [Picture]: Yu. Tsedenbal (second from the left in the first row), Head of the Government Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic and Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, inspects the guard of honor at the airport after disembarking. First from the right in the first row is N. Luvsan, member of the Government Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic. First from the left in the first row is Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China. Xinhua News Agency, Chen Bo Photo

### 蒙古政府代表团团长、团员及随员名单

1952-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古政府代表团团长、团员及随员名单
    【新华社二十八日讯】九月二十八日抵达北京的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团的组成如下：
    代表团团长蒙古人民共和国总理尤·泽登巴尔；代表团团员为：蒙古人民共和国副总理兼外交部部长恩·拉姆苏伦，蒙古人民共和国教育部部长巴·锡林迪布，蒙古人民共和国驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使博·贾尔卡赛汗；代表团随员为：都勒布尔金，普尔布扎勒。（附图片）
    图：蒙古人民共和国政府代表团团长、蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔（第一排左起第二人）下飞机后，在机场检阅仪仗队。第一排右第一人为蒙古人民共和国政府代表团团员、蒙古人民共和国副总理兼外交部部长拉姆苏伦。第一排左第一人为我中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来。新华社稿　陈勃摄
