Title: Our Ambassador to Poland, Zeng Yongquan, Arrives in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1952-09-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th—Zeng Yongquan, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Poland, arrived in Warsaw on the 17th. Among those who welcomed him at the station were: Bartel, Director of the Protocol Department of the Polish Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Sobolev, the Soviet Ambassador; Pisecky, the Czechoslovak Ambassador; Gundermann, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic; Choi Il, the Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; Dinulescu, the Romanian Ambassador; Deramalaev, the Bulgarian Ambassador; Drahos, the Hungarian Minister; and Babi, the Albanian Minister. Yang Qiliang, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of our Embassy in Poland, and all diplomatic officials also welcomed him at the station.

### 我驻波大使曾涌泉抵华沙

1952-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻波大使曾涌泉抵华沙
    【新华社二十日讯】我国驻波兰人民共和国特命全权大使曾涌泉已于十七日抵达华沙。到车站欢迎的人有：波兰外交部交际司司长巴尔脱尔，苏联大使索波列夫，捷克斯洛伐克大使彼塞克，德意志民主共和国外交使团团长贡德尔曼，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使崔一，罗马尼亚大使地努列苏，保加利亚大使得拉马立耶夫，匈牙利公使德拉霍斯，阿尔巴尼亚公使巴比。我驻波兰大使馆临时代办杨琪良及全体外交官员也到车站欢迎。
