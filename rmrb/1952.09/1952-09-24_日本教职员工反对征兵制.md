Title: Japanese Teachers and Staff Oppose Conscription

Author: 
Date: 1952-09-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, September 21st (TASS dispatch): According to Kyodo News, the results of a referendum held by the Japan Teachers' Union showed that among the 65,000 voters, 77.2 percent opposed the implementation of conscription, while only 6.8 percent expressed support.

### 日本教职员工反对征兵制

1952-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    日本教职员工反对征兵制
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社东京二十日电：据共同社消息，日本教职员工会所举行的公民投票结果表明，在六万五千个投票人中，反对实行征兵制的有百分之七十七点二，表示赞成的只有百分之六点八。
