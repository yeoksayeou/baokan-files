Title: Mrs. Felton Returned to Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1952-09-24
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 23rd: Mrs. Felton of Britain, who visited China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, returned to Beijing by car on the morning of the 23rd after recently visiting Korea. Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Work Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and others were at the station to welcome her.

### 费尔顿夫人昨日返抵北京

1952-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    费尔顿夫人昨日返抵北京
    【新华社二十三日讯】应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请来中国访问的英国费尔顿夫人，在最近访问朝鲜后于二十三日上午乘车返抵北京。到车站欢迎的有中华全国民主妇女联合会国际工作部副部长廖梦醒等多人。
