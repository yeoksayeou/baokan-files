Title: Paris Newspapers Publish News of International Scientific Commission's Investigation Report; French "L'Humanité" Calls on the World to Stop the United States from Conducting Germ Warfare
Author:
Date: 1952-09-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, September 17th: TASS, Paris, September 17th: All newspapers in Paris published news of the International Scientific Commission's report confirming that the American military used bacteriological weapons against the people of China and Korea. "L'Humanité" published an editorial emphasizing that the facts listed in the International Scientific Commission's report were completely factual and credible, because this commission was composed of prominent scientists from six countries representing different social strata. "L'Humanité" said: "Faced with many convincing evidences confirming the crimes of the Americans, those who previously held skeptical attitudes are increasingly aware that these major facts are irrefutable."

The newspaper said: The world must take measures to stop germ warfare. To this end, all peace fighters must tirelessly expose the heinous crimes of the imperialist aggressors. "L'Humanité" emphasized: "While preparing for the World People's Peace Congress, we must strive to get millions more people to participate in protesting chemical and bacteriological warfare, and participate in the common struggle to defeat the conspiracies of war-makers."

### 巴黎各报刊载国际科学委员会调查报告的消息  法“人道报”呼吁全世界制止美国进行细菌战

1952-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    巴黎各报刊载国际科学委员会调查报告的消息
    法“人道报”呼吁全世界制止美国进行细菌战
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社巴黎十七日讯：巴黎的所有报纸都刊载了国际科学委员会报告书证实美军对中国和朝鲜人民使用细菌武器的消息。“人道报”发表了一篇社论，强调说：国际科学委员会报告书中所列举的事实都是完全确凿可信的，因为这个委员会是由六个国家的代表着不同社会阶层的著名科学家组成的。“人道报”说：“面对着许多证实美国人的罪行的令人信服的证据，以前抱着怀疑态度的人越来越认识到这些重大的事实是无可辩驳的。”
    该报说：全世界必须采取措施制止细菌战，为此目的，全体和平战士都必须不倦怠地揭露帝国主义侵略者的滔天罪行。“人道报”强调说：“在筹备世界人民和平大会的时候，必须多争取千百万人参加抗议化学战与细菌战，参加击败战争制造者的阴谋的共同斗争。”
