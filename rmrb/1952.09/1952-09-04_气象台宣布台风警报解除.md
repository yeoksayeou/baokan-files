Title: Meteorological Observatory Announces Typhoon Warning Lifted
Author:
Date: 1952-09-04
Page: 1

    Meteorological Observatory Announces Typhoon Warning Lifted
    (Xinhua News Agency, 3rd) At 6:00 PM on the 3rd, the Central Meteorological Observatory issued a message lifting the typhoon warning, stating: At 2:00 PM on the 3rd, the center of the typhoon had moved to northeastern Korea, and the threat to coastal areas of our country was eliminated.

### 气象台宣布台风警报解除

1952-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    气象台宣布台风警报解除
    【新华社三日讯】三日下午六时中央气象台发布解除台风警报消息称：三日下午二时台风的中心已移至朝鲜的东北部，我国沿海地区所受威胁消除。
