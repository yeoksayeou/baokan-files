Title: CPC Central Committee Northwest Bureau Congratulates on Early Completion of Track Laying on the Tianshui-Lanzhou Railway; Minister Teng Daiyuan Also Sends Congratulatory Telegram on Behalf of National Railway Workers
Author:
Date: 1952-09-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, August 30th: The CPC Central Committee Northwest Bureau sent a telegram to the Northwest Railway Trunk Line Engineering Bureau on August 30th, congratulating them on the early completion of track laying on the Tianshui-Lanzhou Railway. The telegram reads as follows:

    To Comrade Wang Shitai and all comrades of the Northwest Railway Trunk Line Engineering Bureau:

    We warmly congratulate you on the great victory of completing the track laying task of the Tianshui-Lanzhou Railway ahead of schedule!

    The completion of the Tianshui-Lanzhou Railway is the beginning of large-scale economic construction in the Northwest and will change the face of the Northwest. This is a great happy event for all ethnic groups in the Northwest. We must continue to work hard, in accordance with the state-stipulated plan, to complete the Northwest trunk railway and other necessary railway projects, so that our economic construction, national defense construction, and cultural construction can achieve the necessary development conditions.

    The early completion of the Tianshui-Lanzhou Railway construction project is the result of all employees promoting patriotism, improving labor efficiency, learning from advanced Soviet experience, and courageously carrying out political and production reforms. We hope that you will continue to complete all necessary preparations for opening to traffic on the basis of ensuring project quality and safety, and continue to complete various production reforms. At the same time, summarize the experience gained, consolidate it, and apply it to future transportation management and new line projects to strive for new victories.

    CPC Central Committee Northwest Bureau
    August 30th

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, August 30th: Teng Daiyuan, Minister of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government, on behalf of the national railway workers, sent a telegram to all employees of the Northwest Railway Trunk Line Engineering Bureau on August 23rd, warmly congratulating them on their great victory in completing the track laying to Lanzhou ten months ahead of schedule, and encouraged them to strive for the victorious completion of all work before the opening ceremony on National Day on October 1st. The telegram reads as follows:

    To Comrade Wang Shitai, Director of the Northwest Railway Trunk Line Engineering Bureau, and all comrade employees:

    We are delighted to learn that you victoriously completed the glorious task of laying the track to Lanzhou ahead of schedule at 9:30 pm on August 23rd. On behalf of the national railway workers, I warmly congratulate you. The completion of the Tianshui-Lanzhou Railway plays a very important role in all aspects of our great motherland's national defense, economy, politics, and culture. I hope that you will continue your efforts and, on the basis of ensuring project quality and safety, victoriously complete all work before the opening ceremony on National Day on October 1st.

    Wishing you good health!

    Teng Daiyuan
    Minister of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government
    August 23rd

### 中共中央西北局祝贺天兰路提前完成铺轨  滕代远部长亦代表全国铁路职工致电祝贺

1952-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央西北局祝贺天兰路提前完成铺轨
    滕代远部长亦代表全国铁路职工致电祝贺
    【新华社西安三十日电】中共中央西北局八月三十日致电西北铁路干线工程局，祝贺天兰路提前完成铺轨。电文如下：
    西北铁路干线工程局王世泰同志并全体职工同志们：
    我们热烈祝贺天兰铁路提前完成铺轨任务的伟大胜利！
    修成天兰铁路，是西北大规模的经济建设的开端，将改变西北的面貌，这是西北各族人民的大喜事。我们还要继续努力，按照国家规定计划，完成西北干线铁路及其他必须兴修的铁路工程，使我们的经济建设、国防建设及文化建设取得必要的发展条件。
    天兰铁路修筑工程提前完成，是全体职工发扬爱国主义精神，提高劳动效率，学习苏联先进经验，勇敢地进行政治的和生产上的改革的结果。希望你们在保证工程质量和安全的基础上，继续完成通车的各项必要的准备工作，并继续完成各项生产改革。同时，将已得的经验加以总结，巩固下来，并应用到今后运输管理和新线工程上去，争取新的胜利。中共中央西北局
    八月三十日
    【新华社西安三十日电】中央人民政府铁道部滕代远部长代表全国铁路职工于八月二十三日致电西北铁路干线工程局全体职工，热烈祝贺他们提前十个月铺轨至兰州的巨大胜利，并勉励他们为胜利完成通车典礼前的各项工作而奋斗。电文如下：
    西北铁路干线工程局王世泰局长转全体职工同志们：
    欣悉你们于八月二十三日晚九时三十分提前胜利完成了铺轨到达兰州的光荣任务，我代表全国铁路职工，热烈地向你们祝贺。天兰铁路的修成，对我们伟大祖国的国防、经济、政治、文化各方面，都起着非常重大的作用。希望你们再接再厉，在保证工程质量和安全的基础上，再一次胜利完成十月一日国庆节通车典礼前的各项工作。
    祝你们身体健康！
    中央人民政府铁道部部长  滕代远
    八月二十三日
