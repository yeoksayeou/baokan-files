Title: Mongolian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Idan-jab, Holds Reception Welcoming Mongolian Chairman of the Council of Ministers Tsedenbal. Deputy Chairman Molotov and Premier Zhou Enlai Attend.
Author:
Date: 1952-09-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 18th: TASS Moscow, September 18th: The Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to the Soviet Union, Idan-jab, held a reception on September 17th to welcome the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Tsedenbal.

The Soviet side attending the reception included: Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union Molotov, Voroshilov, Mikoyan, Kaganovich, Kosygin, Tevosyan, and Pervukhin, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation Tarasov, Soviet Ministers Vyshinsky, Vasilevsky, Zverev, Kumykin, Beshchev, Baibakov, Smirnov, and Zhevlonkov, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Gorkin, Soviet Prosecutor General Safonov, Marshal of the Soviet Union Budyonny, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union Pushkin and Podtserob, Soviet Ambassador to the Mongolian People's Republic Ivannikov, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Trade Borisov and Pavlov, Admiral Levchenko, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences Nesmeyanov, General of the Army Zhadov, Lieutenant Generals Batitsky, N. Stakhanov, and Sinilov, Rear Admiral Yakovlev, Executive Secretary of the Soviet Peace Committee Kotov, senior officials from the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ministry of Foreign Trade, Ministry of the Army, and Ministry of the Navy, as well as representatives from social circles, journalists, scientists, and artists.

The Chinese side attending the reception included: Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council Li Fuchun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Su Yu, Minister of Heavy Industry Wang Heshou, Minister of Fuel Industry Chen Yu, Secretary-General of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council Song Shaowen, Commander of the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Liu Yalou, Deputy Commander of the Navy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Luo Shunchu, Deputy Commander of the Artillery of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Qiu Chuangcheng, Deputy Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building Wang Daohan, Deputy Minister of Posts and Telecommunications Wang Zheng, Political Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China Shi Zhe, Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Xu Yixin, and Director of the Department of Asian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Chen Jiakang.

The Mongolian side attending the reception included: Minister of National Defense of the Mongolian People's Republic Bat, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zamtsorol-Sambuu, as well as officials and staff of the Mongolian People's Republic Embassy in Moscow.

Guests attending the reception included: Ambassador of the People's Republic of Bulgaria Blagoeva, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Hungary Zoklaton, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China Zhang Wentian, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Lim Hai, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Nguyen Luong Bang, Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic Dalea, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic Abel, Minister of the People's Republic of Albania Natanaeli, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Polish People's Republic Pogorelies, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Czechoslovak Republic Eitzni, as well as officials from the embassies and legations of the aforementioned countries.

### 蒙古驻苏大使伊丹甲布举行招待会  欢迎蒙古部长会议主席泽登巴尔  莫洛托夫副主席周恩来总理赴会

第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古驻苏大使伊丹甲布举行招待会
    欢迎蒙古部长会议主席泽登巴尔
    莫洛托夫副主席周恩来总理赴会
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十八日电：蒙古人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使伊丹甲布于九月十七日举行招待会，欢迎蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔。
    苏联方面参加招待会的有：苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫、伏罗希洛夫、米高扬、卡冈诺维奇、柯西金、提伏希安和别尔乌辛，俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团主席塔拉索夫，苏联部长维辛斯基、华西列夫斯基、兹维列夫、库米金、别什契夫、巴伊巴科夫、斯米尔诺夫和热伏隆科夫，苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书高尔金，苏联最高检察长萨弗洛夫，苏联元帅布琼尼，苏联外交部副部长普式金和波德切罗布，苏联驻蒙古人民共和国大使伊凡尼科夫，对外贸易部副部长鲍里邵夫和巴夫洛夫，海军上将列甫钦科，苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫，陆军上将热多夫，陆军中将巴蒂茨基、恩·斯达哈诺夫和西尼洛夫，海军少将雅克武列夫，苏联拥护和平委员会执行书记科托夫，苏联外交部、对外贸易部、陆军部和海军部的高级官员，社会名流，新闻记者，科学家和艺术家的代表。
    中国方面参加招待会的有：中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，政务院财政经济委员会副主任李富春，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长粟裕，重工业部部长王鹤寿，燃料工业部部长陈郁，政务院财政经济委员会秘书长宋劭文，中国人民解放军空军司令员刘亚楼，中国人民解放军海军副司令员罗舜初，中国人民解放军炮兵副司令员邱创成，第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，邮电部副部长王诤，中华人民共和国外交部政治秘书师哲，外交部苏联东欧司司长徐以新和外交部亚洲司司长陈家康。
    蒙古方面参加招待会作陪的有：蒙古人民共和国国防部部长巴特、外交部副部长扎姆索伦—桑布以及蒙古人民共和国驻莫斯科大使馆官员和工作人员。
    参加招待会的贵宾有：保加利亚人民共和国大使布拉果耶娃、匈牙利人民共和国大使佐克拉顿、中华人民共和国大使张闻天、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使林海、越南民主共和国大使阮良明、罗马尼亚人民共和国大使达列亚、德意志民主共和国外交使团团长阿贝尔、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国公使纳塔奈利、波兰人民共和国大使馆临时代办波果雷列斯、捷克斯洛伐克共和国大使馆临时代办埃茨尼以及上述国家大使馆和公使馆的官员。
