Title: International Scientific Commission Holds Report Signing Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1952-09-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 15th. The International Scientific Commission investigating the facts of bacteriological warfare in Korea and China held a report signing ceremony in Beijing on the afternoon of August 31st.

The signing ceremony was conducted in a solemn atmosphere. International Scientific Commission members Andreen (Sweden), Mader (France), Joseph Needham (UK), Olivo (Italy), Bressoya (Brazil), Zhukov-Vereshnikov (USSR), successively signed the report, which was exquisitely printed in French, English, Russian, and Chinese and sealed with sealing wax.

In addition to the commission members, those attending the signing ceremony included advisory consultant Graci, translation secretary Kowalski, and Mrs. Bressoya. The Chinese attendees included: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Li Siguang, Tao Menghe, Zhu Kezhen, and Wu Youxun, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China; He Cheng, Honorary Chairman of the Chinese Medical Association, and Fu Lian暲, Chairman; Zhang Xiruo, Chairman of the Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Qian Sanqiang, Liaison Officer of the International Scientific Commission; Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Reception Committee of the International Scientific Commission; Gong Naiquan, Secretary-General; Ji Suhua, Deputy Secretary-General; expert liaison officers Zhong Huilan, Wu Zaidong, Fang Gang, Zhu Hongfu, Yang Shida, Yan Renying, and other Chinese and foreign journalists, totaling forty people.

After the signing, President Guo Moruo delivered a speech. He said: Dear experts! In order to save peace, save mankind, and save science, you have overcome various difficulties and done a serious job. We, the Chinese people, will sincerely support you and thank you. We believe that the Korean people and all peace-loving people in the world will support you and thank you. The achievements of your work will occupy a glorious page in human history. Your honor will be passed down for a long time. Your selfless spirit is inspiring. Great scientists, for the sake of truth and justice, will never yield to violence. Glory forever belongs to the defenders of truth and justice.

International Scientific Commission member Mader (France) delivered a speech on behalf of the commission. He said: I remember that a few months ago, at the Oslo conference, President Guo and representatives of the Korean people welcomed impartial and independent international scientists to investigate in China and Korea. People gave us great honor, believing that we are impartial and independent scientists capable of completing this glorious task. We deeply feel the glory and importance of this task. We came from afar to collect sufficient evidence regarding the United States' bacteriological warfare, and our task is to verify this evidence or to refute it. We initially held a skeptical attitude on some issues, because this is for the sake of truth. Chinese and Korean scientists and scientists from all over the world will forgive us. I believe that the truth we find with caution will be even more powerful. We have indeed seen a lot of evidence and have signed the report to prove it. We can say it again, China and Korea have indeed been attacked by bacteriological warfare, and the U.S. military should be held responsible for this. We will confirm these facts before the people of the world. You must hope that after these crimes are exposed, they will stop these crimes. If they do not stop, the people of the world will sanction them. Our work is to study these issues from a scientific standpoint. Unfortunately, bacteriological warfare is indeed a fact. The content of the report signed today is bleak, but this report will generate hope.

The signing ceremony ended after Mader's speech. (Attached picture)

### 国际科学委员会举行报告书签字仪式

1952-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

    国际科学委员会举行报告书签字仪式
    【新华社十五日讯】调查在朝鲜和中国的细菌战事实国际科学委员会，于八月三十一日下午在北京举行报告书签字仪式。
    签字仪式在严肃的气氛中进行。国际科学委员会委员安德琳（瑞典）、马戴尔（法国）、李约瑟（英国）、欧利佛（意大利）、贝索亚（巴西）、茹科夫—维勒斯尼科夫（苏联），依次在以法、英、俄、中四种文字精印并用火漆固封的报告书上签名。
    参加签字仪式的，除委员会各委员外，还有列席顾问葛拉求西，翻译秘书可华司基和贝索亚夫人。中国方面在场人士有：中国科学院院长、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，中国科学院副院长李四光、陶孟和、竺可桢、吴有训，中国红十字会总会会长李德全，中华医学会荣誉理事长贺诚、理事长傅连暲，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会北京市分会主席张奚若，国际科学委员会联络员钱三强，国际科学委员会招待委员会副主席廖承志、秘书长宫乃泉、副秘书长计苏华，专家联络员锺惠澜、吴在东、方纲、朱弘复、杨士达、严仁英等以及中外记者共四十人。
    签字毕，郭沫若院长致词。他说：各位亲爱的专家们！为了拯救和平，拯救人类，拯救科学，你们排除了种种的困难，做了一项严肃的工作。我们中国人民将热诚地支持你们，感谢你们。我们相信，朝鲜人民和全世界爱好正义的人们都会支持你们，感谢你们。你们的工作成就，将在人类史上占着光辉的篇页。你们的荣誉将传诸久远。你们大公无私的精神是令人感奋的。伟大的科学家们，为了真理和正义，在暴力之下是永不屈服的。光荣永远属于真理和正义的保卫者。
    国际科学委员会委员马戴尔（法国）代表委员会致词。他说：我记得，几个月以前，在奥斯陆会议上，郭院长与朝鲜人民的代表曾表示欢迎国际公正而独立的科学家到中国和朝鲜调查。人们给我们很大的光荣，认为我们是能够完成这一光荣任务的公正而独立的科学家。我们深深感到这个任务的光荣和重大。我们远道而来，为的是搜集充足的有关美国进行细菌战的证据，我们的任务就是来证实这些证据，或者否定这些证据。我们当初在有些问题上曾抱着怀疑的态度，因为，这是为了真理。中朝科学家和世界科学家是会原谅我们的。我相信，我们以谨慎从事找到的真理，力量将会更大。我们确实看到许多证据，并且已在报告书上签字加以证明。我们可以再说一遍，中国和朝鲜的确受到细菌战的攻击，美军是应该对此负责的。我们将在全世界人民面前来证实这些事实。你们一定愿望，这些罪行被揭发之后，他们会停止这种罪行。如果他们不停止，全世界人民将予以制裁。我们的工作就是在科学的立场上研究这些问题，很不幸，细菌战的确是事实。今天签字的报告书的内容是阴暗的，但这个报告书将产生希望。
    签字仪式在马戴尔致词后结束。（附图片）
