Title: Indian Representative Banerjee Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1952-09-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 18th: Indian representative Banerjee, who is attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, arrived in Guangzhou on the afternoon of the 17th. Zhang Lucun, vice-chairman of the Guangzhou branch of the China Peace Committee, and others went to the station to welcome him.

### 印度代表班那吉到达广州

1952-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

    印度代表班那吉到达广州
    【新华社广州十八日电】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的印度代表班那吉于十七日下午到达广州。前往车站欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广州市分会副主席张醁村等多人。
