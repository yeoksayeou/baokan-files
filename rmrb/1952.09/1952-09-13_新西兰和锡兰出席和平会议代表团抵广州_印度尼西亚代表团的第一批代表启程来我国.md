Title: New Zealand and Ceylon Peace Conference Delegations Arrive in Guangzhou; First Group of Indonesian Delegation Departs for Our Country
Author:
Date: 1952-09-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 12th: The New Zealand delegation to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, led by Montagu, consisting of twelve people, and the Ceylon delegation, led by Narawila Damarathana, consisting of three people, a total of fifteen people, arrived in Guangzhou on the afternoon of the 12th. Indian peace activist Disha also arrived at the same time.

The delegates were warmly welcomed upon their arrival at the Guangzhou Railway Station. Among those who went to welcome them were Chen Rutang and Xiao Junying, members of the Guangdong Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Guo Qiaoran, Deputy Secretary-General of the Guangzhou Branch, and more than 1,000 representatives of workers, students, and women. The delegates also received flowers presented by representatives of the Young Pioneers.

That evening, Narawila Damarathana, head of the Ceylon delegation, Archie Hal, a representative from New Zealand, and Disha, an Indian peace activist, took a train to Beijing.

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: Tass, Jakarta, 10th: The first group of the Indonesian delegation to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference departed from Jakarta for Beijing on the 9th. The delegation consists of twenty representatives from all walks of life in Indonesia and is headed by Suroto, a journalist from Antara News Agency.

### 新西兰和锡兰出席和平会议代表团抵广州  印度尼西亚代表团的第一批代表启程来我国

1952-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    新西兰和锡兰出席和平会议代表团抵广州
    印度尼西亚代表团的第一批代表启程来我国
    【新华社广州十二日电】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的新西兰代表团团长蒙太施等十二人及锡兰代表团团长那拉威拉·达马拉塔纳等三人，一行共十五人，于十二日下午到达广州。同时抵达的还有印度和平人士迪沙。
    代表们抵达广州车站时，受到热烈欢迎。前往欢迎者有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广东省分会委员陈汝棠、萧隽英等和广州市分会副秘书长郭翘然以及工人、学生、妇女代表等一千余人。代表们并接受了少年儿童队代表们的献花。
    当晚，锡兰代表团团长那拉威拉·达马拉塔纳、新西兰代表艾启赫、印度和平人士迪沙等三人即转车前赴北京。
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社雅加达十日电：参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的印度尼西亚代表团的第一批代表已于九日从雅加达启程赴北京。代表团由印尼各阶层人民代表二十人组成，团长为安塔拉通讯社记者苏洛多。
