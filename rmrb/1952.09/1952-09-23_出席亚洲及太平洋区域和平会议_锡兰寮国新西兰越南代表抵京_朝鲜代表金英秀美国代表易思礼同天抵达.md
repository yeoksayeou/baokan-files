Title: Representatives from Ceylon, Laos, New Zealand, and Vietnam Arrive in Beijing for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. Korean Representative Kim Young-soo and U.S. Representative Walter Easley Arrive on the Same Day

Author: 
Date: 1952-09-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd: Representatives from Ceylon, Korea, Laos, the New Zealand delegation, the U.S. representative, and the Vietnam delegation arrived in Beijing by train on the morning of September 22nd to attend the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General, Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and representatives from industrial and religious circles went to the station to welcome them. Representatives from Australia, Ceylon, India, Indonesia, Korea, Laos, Malaya, Mongolia, New Zealand, the Philippines, Thailand, Vietnam, and other countries who had already arrived in Beijing to attend the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, as well as Thornton, a member of the Asian-Australian Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions, also went to the station to welcome them. Huang Van Hoan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, also went to the station to welcome the Vietnamese delegation.

Kim Young-soo, the Korean representative who arrived today, is the Vice-Chairman of the Korean Democratic Women's League. Walter Easley, the U.S. representative, is an engineer.

### 出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  锡兰寮国新西兰越南代表抵京  朝鲜代表金英秀美国代表易思礼同天抵达

1952-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    锡兰寮国新西兰越南代表抵京
    朝鲜代表金英秀美国代表易思礼同天抵达
    【新华社二十二日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的锡兰、朝鲜、寮国代表，新西兰代表团，美国代表及越南代表团于二十二日上午乘火车抵达北京。前往车站欢迎的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通、秘书长刘贯一、中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长金子明、中华全国民主妇女联合会国际联络部副部长廖梦醒及工业界、宗教界人士等。前往车站欢迎的还有前已到达北京的澳大利亚、锡兰、印度、印度尼西亚、朝鲜、寮国、马来亚、蒙古、新西兰，菲律宾、泰国、越南等国出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的代表和世界工会联合会亚澳联络局委员桑顿等。越南民主共和国驻我国大使衔代表黄文欢也曾到车站欢迎越南代表团。
