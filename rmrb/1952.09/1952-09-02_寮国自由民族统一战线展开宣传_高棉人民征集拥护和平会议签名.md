Title: Lao Patriotic Front Launches Propaganda; Khmer People Collect Signatures Supporting Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-09-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 31st: The people of Laos and Cambodia, who are engaged in a heroic struggle for national independence, have expressed their warm welcome and are actively preparing to participate in the upcoming Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference to be held in Beijing.

    After receiving the declaration of the preparatory meeting of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, the Lao Patriotic Front launched extensive propaganda among the Lao people. Members of the Executive Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front traveled throughout the country to explain the importance of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference to the people. The Lao people enthusiastically responded to the convening of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference in Beijing. At the Lao National People's Congress held not long ago, a delegation of Laos to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference was elected, and this delegation is actively carrying out preparatory work.

    The Khmer Patriotic Front, in the name of the entire Khmer people, sent a telegram to the Preparatory Committee of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference in Beijing, expressing its sincere support for the convening of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference and guaranteeing the implementation of all resolutions of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. Although Cambodia is currently in a difficult wartime situation, the declaration of the Preparatory Committee of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference has been widely circulated among the Khmer people. Many places in Cambodia have held meetings to collect signatures in support of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference.

### 寮国自由民族统一战线展开宣传  高棉人民征集拥护和平会议签名

1952-09-02
第4版()
专栏：

    寮国自由民族统一战线展开宣传
    高棉人民征集拥护和平会议签名
    【新华社三十一日讯】正在为民族独立而进行英勇斗争的寮国和高棉两国人民，都表示热烈欢迎并积极筹备参加即将在北京召开的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。
    寮国自由民族统一战线在接到亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的宣言后，就在寮国人民中展开广泛宣传。寮国自由民族统一战线执行委员会的委员们曾到全国各地，向人民解释亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的重要意义。寮国人民热烈地响应在北京召开亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。在不久以前召开的寮国全国人民代表的会议上，已选出寮国出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的代表团，这个代表团正在积极进行筹备工作。
    高棉自由民族统一战线已以高棉全国人民的名义，致电设在北京的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会，表示竭诚拥护召开亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议，并保证执行亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的一切决议。虽然高棉目前仍在困难的战时情况下，但是亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的宣言已在高棉人民中广泛流传。高棉许多地方举行会议，进行征集拥护亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的签名。
