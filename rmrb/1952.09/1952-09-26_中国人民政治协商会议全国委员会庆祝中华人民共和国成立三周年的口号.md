Title: Slogans of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference for the Celebration of the Third Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1952-09-26
Page: 1

    Slogans of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference for the Celebration of the Third Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
    1. Celebrate the third anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China!
    2. Celebrate the great victories of the Chinese people on all fronts in the past three years!
    3. Celebrate the great victory of the struggle to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea!
    4. Celebrate the great victory of national defense construction!
    5. Celebrate the great victory of the national land reform!
    6. Celebrate the great victory of the suppression of counter-revolutionaries!
    7. Celebrate the great victory of the movement against corruption, waste, and bureaucracy! Celebrate the great victory of the movement against bribery, tax evasion, embezzlement of state property, shoddy workmanship, and theft of state economic intelligence!
    8. Celebrate the basic completion of the nation's economic recovery and transformation, and celebrate the fundamental improvement of the nation's financial and economic situation!
    9. Celebrate the great victory of cultural and educational work and ideological remolding movement!
    10. Pay tribute to the people of the whole country who have fought heroically against imperialist aggression, to defend peace, and build a new China!
    11. Pay tribute to the People's Liberation Army's Army, Navy, and Air Force, the People's Armed Police, the People's Police, and the militia!
    12. Pay tribute to the heroic Chinese People's Volunteers!
    13. Pay tribute to the stretcher bearers, transport team members, medical and health workers, railway transportation workers, rear service workers, and battlefield cultural workers who support the Chinese People's Volunteers!
    14. Pay tribute to the comrades who were wounded in the struggle to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea!
    15. Eternal glory to the martyrs who sacrificed their lives for resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea!
    16. Pay tribute to the great Soviet people who are victoriously building communism! The great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people is a reliable guarantee against new threats of aggression and a strong bulwark for defending peace in the Far East and the world. Long live the unbreakable friendly alliance between China and the Soviet Union!
    17. Celebrate the great victory of the Korean people's war against aggression! Pay tribute to the heroic Korean people! Long live the close unity and friendship between the Chinese and Korean people in the war against aggression!
    18. Celebrate the great victory of the Vietnamese people's war against aggression! Pay tribute to the heroic Vietnamese people!
    19. Pay tribute to the people of the people's democratic countries who are victoriously building socialism!
    20. Pay tribute to the Japanese people who are fighting against U.S. imperialism and its running dogs, the Japanese reactionaries, and striving for national liberation, democracy, and peace!
    21. Pay tribute to the people of all countries who are fighting against imperialist aggression and striving for national independence and democratic freedom!
    22. Pay tribute to the people of all countries who are fighting against the threat of war and defending peace and democracy!
    23. Celebrate the convening of the Asian and Pacific Peace Conference! Long live the friendship between the people of Asian and Pacific countries and the Chinese people in the struggle to defend peace!
    24. Oppose U.S. imperialism's occupation of Taiwan!
    25. Oppose the inhuman bacteriological warfare waged by U.S. imperialism! Fight to defend humanity and ban bacteriological warfare!
    26. Oppose U.S. imperialism's delaying and sabotaging the Korean armistice negotiations! Oppose U.S. imperialism's crimes of massacring our prisoners of war and indiscriminately bombing peaceful residents of Korea!
    27. Oppose U.S. imperialism's evil plan to expand its military and prepare for a new war, and demand the conclusion of a peace pact among the five major powers of China, the Soviet Union, the United States, Britain, and France!
    28. Oppose the unilateral peace treaty with Japan! Oppose U.S. imperialism's use of Japan as a base for aggression and its remilitarization of Japan!
    29. Oppose U.S. imperialism's use of West Germany as a base for aggression! Oppose the division of Germany and the revival of West German militarist forces!
    30. Oppose U.S. imperialism's blockade and embargo, and develop friendly cooperation and economic and trade relations between the people of all countries!
    31. The people of the whole country should work together to make various preparations to meet the upcoming large-scale economic and cultural construction and strive for national industrialization!
    32. Continue to strengthen the work of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, and strive for the victorious conclusion of the Korean war against aggression and the peaceful settlement of the Korean issue!
    33. Strive to consolidate national defense, safeguard national security and peaceful construction, and safeguard peace in the Far East and the world!
    34. Comrades of the People's Liberation Army! Work hard to study culture, study politics, strengthen your physique, and learn modern military technology, and strive to further build a modern national defense army!
    35. Male and female workers! Strive to carry out the patriotic production increase and economy movement, study politics, culture, and technology, learn advanced experience, create advanced working methods, abide by labor discipline, improve labor productivity, improve product quality, save raw materials, and reduce costs, and strive to complete and exceed the national production plan!
    36. Leaders of state-owned industrial and mining enterprises! Rely on the masses of workers to manage factories and enterprises well, realize the democratic management and unified leadership of factories and enterprises, continue to carry out production reforms, resolutely implement the economic accounting system, and ensure the completion and exceeding of the national production plan!
    37. Trade union workers and members! In order to complete and exceed the national production plan, unite all workers and technicians, carry out patriotic production increase and economy competitions, improve production technology, promote advanced production experience, implement rationalization proposals, gradually improve workers' lives, pay attention to factory and mine safety and health work, and increase workers' welfare!
    38. Male and female farmers! Organize yourselves and, based on the principles of voluntariness and mutual benefit, develop and improve mutual aid and cooperation organizations step by step, improve cultivation techniques, prevent and control diseases and insect pests, build water conservancy projects, prevent floods and droughts, protect forests and afforest, carry out patriotic bumper harvest competitions, and increase the output per unit area, and strive to further improve the level of agricultural production!
    Male and female herdsmen! Improve livestock breeding methods, improve breeds, prevent and control livestock diseases, and strive to increase livestock!
    Male and female fishermen! Strengthen mutual aid and cooperation, improve fishing and fish farming methods, and strive to develop the fishing industry!
    39. State trade workers and cooperative workers! Further improve the quality of state trade and cooperative work, better serve consumers and members, develop supply and marketing cooperative organizations, and strive to develop the exchange of goods between urban and rural areas!
    40. State organ staff! Work actively, study hard, closely concern yourselves with the demands of the people, abide by state discipline, and constantly improve work efficiency!
    41. Young men and women! Study cultural and scientific knowledge hard, improve your political level, carry out patriotic sports activities, exercise your bodies, and prepare to serve the motherland!
    42. Women across the country! Break the shackles of feudal ideology, study politics and culture hard, actively participate in economic, political, and cultural construction undertakings, and strive to fully realize women's equal rights with men in all aspects of political, economic, cultural, educational, and social life!
    43. Educators! Strive to arm yourselves with Marxism-Leninism, use advanced scientific knowledge, revolutionary ideas, and a spirit that combines patriotism and internationalism to educate young people, popularize and improve cultural and educational undertakings, train more talents for the country, and strive to eliminate illiteracy throughout the country!
    44. Scientific and technical workers! Strive to arm yourselves with Marxism-Leninism, do a good job in scientific research, master advanced theories and technologies, closely integrate with the practical experience of workers and peasants, obey the needs of the state and the people, and serve the cause of national construction!
    45. Medical and health workers! Strive to arm yourselves with Marxism-Leninism, learn advanced medical knowledge, develop medical and health undertakings, consolidate the achievements of the patriotic health movement, continue to carry out this movement, and strive to improve the people's health!
    46. Literary, artistic, and film workers! Strive to arm yourselves with Marxism-Leninism, master the orientation of literature and art serving workers, peasants, and soldiers, delve into reality, improve artistic cultivation, and strive to create literary and artistic works with correct ideological content and a high level of artistry!
    47. Judicial workers! Strive to arm yourselves with Marxism-Leninism and realize the national judicial reform!
    48. Industrialists and businessmen! Under the leadership of the working class and the state-owned economy, follow the orbit of the Common Program, follow the unified plan of the state, and strive to develop economic undertakings that are beneficial to the national economy and the people's livelihood!
    49. Religious believers! Promote the spirit of patriotism and actively participate in the struggle against imperialist aggression and for the defense of peace!
    50. All democratic parties, democratic personages from all walks of life, and people's organizations should consolidate and develop their organizations, consolidate and expand the people's democratic united front, thoroughly implement the Common Program, and complete the task of national construction!
    51. All ethnic groups in the country should unite and strive together to oppose imperialist aggression and build an independent, unified, prosperous, and great motherland! Promote national regional autonomy, strive to develop the economy and culture of minority areas, and gradually improve the lives of people of all ethnic groups!
    52. Overseas Chinese should unite, love the motherland, and strive to protect their legitimate rights and interests!
    53. Long live the great unity of the Chinese people!
    54. Long live the great unity of all ethnic groups in China!
    55. Long live the great unity of the people of Asia and the Pacific countries!
    56. Long live the great unity of the people of the world!
    57. Long live the world peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union!
    58. Long live Marshal Stalin, the great leader and teacher of the world's working people!
    59. Long live the great People's Republic of China!
    60. Long live the great Communist Party of the China!
    61. Long live Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people!
