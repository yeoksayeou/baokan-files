Title: China's Football Team Returns Home After Visiting Poland
Author:
Date: 1952-09-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 11th. China's football team of twenty people, who went to Helsinki, Finland, to participate in the 15th Olympic Games, returned home by plane on the 9th and 10th, led by Huang Zhong, Deputy Leader of the Chinese sports delegation, after visiting Poland at the invitation of the Polish government. Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, Xu Yingchao, committee member, and dozens of staff members from the North China Sports Federation and the Beijing Sports Federation went to the airport to welcome them.

### 我国足球队访问波兰后返国

1952-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    我国足球队访问波兰后返国
    【新华社十一日讯】赴芬兰赫尔辛基参加第十五届奥林匹克运动会的我国足球队一行二十人，应邀访问波兰后，在我国体育代表团副总领队黄中的率领下，已于九、十两日先后乘飞机返国抵京。中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠、委员徐英超及华北体育总分会、北京市体育分会工作人员等数十人曾赴机场欢迎。
