Title: Labor Models of the Jingjiang Flood Diversion Project, Luo Zhongjun and 16 Others, Write to Delegates of All Countries Participating in the Preparatory Meeting for the Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-09-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, September 2nd: Sixteen labor models from the Jingjiang Flood Diversion Project construction site, including Luo Zhongjun, wrote a letter on August 28th to delegates from various countries who attended the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference after visiting the Jingjiang Flood Diversion Project. These delegates included: Sardar Guhr Baksh Singh from India; Ghulam Muhammad Khan and Shaqur from Pakistan; Tomi Kora, Kei Hozu and Kisuke Miyakoshi from Japan; John Kingsbury from the United States; Van Ilder, Stephen Mackinder, Ada Bronham, and A. Gitzelt from Australia; Rafael Mendez from Mexico; Mary Jennison from Canada; U Mya Myint, Tekin Lwin, U Tun Ohn Pein, U Khun Ti, and U Aye Gyee from Burma; Louis Alley and Courtney Archer from New Zealand. The original letter is as follows:

Dear friends!

When our Jingjiang Flood Diversion Project was about to be completed, you warmly visited the construction site, giving us great encouragement. We feel honored and grateful for your friendly concern for our country's peaceful construction. Shortly after you left, the main structures of the Jingjiang Flood Diversion Project—the more than 130-kilometer-long Jingjiang Dam and the two large sluice gates, one more than 1,000 meters wide and the other more than 300 meters wide—were completed. Now, the Yangtze River has risen many times, and the newly built structures stand firm on the river bank, protecting the farmland on both sides.

Dear friends! Thanks to your continuous efforts, the peace movement around the world and in the Asian and Pacific regions has made considerable progress. This is your achievement in the great cause of peace. We warmly congratulate you on the new victories you have achieved on the peace front. Dear friends! We are very eager to know your achievements in fighting for the cause of peace and for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. We warmly welcome friends from all countries to actively participate in the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. We, the working people of China, are building our great motherland with unparalleled labor enthusiasm. We are willing to strengthen friendly ties with the people of all countries and exchange information on the peace movement and peaceful construction to strengthen the cause of peace and democracy. We wish the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference a success.

Labor Models of the Jingjiang Flood Diversion Project: Luo Zhongjun, Jiang Yunsheng,
    Tang Ziren, Sun Daxi, Huang Zhihua, Sheng Yongpu,
    Gao Yongqing, Chen Changming, Huang Bingguo, Hou Zhizhong,
    Liu Yansong, Jiang Fangyuan, Zhang Yinbao, Feng Dianfeng,
    Hu Wenfu, Shen Yunqing

### 荆江分洪工程工地劳动模范罗仲俊等十六人  给参加和平会议筹备会议各国代表的信

1952-09-03
第4版()
专栏：

    荆江分洪工程工地劳动模范罗仲俊等十六人
    给参加和平会议筹备会议各国代表的信
    【新华社汉口二日电】荆江分洪工程工地上的劳动模范罗仲俊等十六人，在八月二十八日写信给参观过荆江分洪工程的出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的各国代表，这些代表包括：印度的沙达尔·古尔·巴克希·辛格；巴基斯坦的吴拉姆·穆罕默德·汗和霞库尔；日本的高良富，帆足计和宫腰喜助；美国的约翰·金斯伯利；澳大利亚的范·伊尔德，斯蒂芬·马金多，亚达·布龙汉，艾·吉策尔特；墨西哥的拉斐尔·门德斯；加拿大的玛丽·詹尼逊；缅甸的吴苗密，德钦卢埃，宇登帕敏，吴昆蒂和吴埃支；新西兰的路易·艾黎和柯特奈·艾启赫。原信如下：
    亲爱的朋友们！
    当我们的荆江分洪工程将要完工时，你们曾热情地来工地参观，给了我们极大的鼓励。对于你们对我国和平建设事业的友谊的关怀，我们感到荣幸和感激。你们走后不久，荆江分洪工程的主要的建筑物——一百三十多公里长的荆江大堤、一千多公尺宽的和三百多公尺宽的两个大水闸就修成了。现在，长江已多次涨水，新修的建筑物在江岸上屹立不动，保护着两岸的农田。
    亲爱的朋友们！由于你们不断努力的结果，全世界和亚洲及太平洋沿岸各国的和平运动都有了长足的发展，这是你们在伟大的和平事业上的成就。我们热烈祝贺你们在和平战线上获得的新胜利。亲爱的朋友们！我们非常愿意知道你们为和平事业、为亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议而奋斗的成就，我们热烈地欢迎各国的朋友们踊跃地来参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。我们中国的劳动人民，正以无比的劳动热情建设我们伟大的祖国，我们愿与各国人民加强友谊的联系，在和平运动方面、在和平建设方面互通信息，以加强和平民主事业。我们预祝亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的成功。
    荆江分洪工程劳动模范　罗仲俊　姜运生
    唐子仁　孙大喜　黄志华　盛永谱
    高永庆　陈长明　黄炳国　侯志忠
    刘炎松　姜方元　张银保　冯殿峰
    胡文甫　沈云清
