Title: The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-09-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency: The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held its 40th meeting on the afternoon of the 23rd, and passed the "Slogans for Celebrating the Third Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China." Present at the meeting were the Vice-Chairman, Standing Committee members, and members of the National Committee of the CPPCC, as well as members of the Central People's Government Committee, members of the Government Administration Council, and staff members of the National Committee of the CPPCC, totaling sixty people.

### 中国人民政协全国委员会常委会举行会议

1952-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民政协全国委员会常委会举行会议
    【新华社讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会在二十三日下午举行第四十次会议，通过了“庆祝中华人民共和国成立三周年的口号”。到会的有人民政协全国委员会副主席、常务委员、委员，以及中央人民政府委员会委员、政务院政务委员和人民政协全国委员会工作人员等共六十人。
