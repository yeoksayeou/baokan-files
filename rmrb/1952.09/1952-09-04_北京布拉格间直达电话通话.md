Title: Direct Telephone Call Between Beijing and Prague

Author:
Date: 1952-09-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 3rd—In accordance with the Sino-Czech telecommunications agreement signed in May 1952, direct telephone communication between Beijing and Prague officially began on September 3rd. On September 3rd at 3:30 PM, during the first call between Beijing and Prague, a call ceremony was held. Zhu Xuefan, Minister of the Central People's Government's Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of China, and Minister Norman, Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Czechoslovak Republic, exchanged congratulatory messages. Both sides unanimously expressed that the communication between China and Czechoslovakia would contribute to political, economic, and cultural cooperation between the two countries, and enhance the friendship between the two peoples.

### 北京布拉格间直达电话通话

1952-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    北京布拉格间直达电话通话
    【新华社三日讯】根据一九五二年五月签订的中捷电信协定的规定，北京——布拉格间直达电话已在九月三日起正式开始通话。九月三日下午三时三十分，在北京——布拉格间的第一次通话时，举行了通话式。我中央人民政府邮电部朱学范部长与捷克斯洛伐克共和国邮电部诺曼部长互相致词祝贺。双方并一致表示：中捷的通话，将有助于中捷两国间政治、经济、文化的合作，及增进两国人民间的友谊。
