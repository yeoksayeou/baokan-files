Title: U.S. Intrusion into Northeast China Airspace by U.S. Air Force in Korea Continues

Author: 
Date: 1952-09-14
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 13th: In the past half month, the U.S. Air Force in Korea has continued to intrude into the airspace of Northeast China. From August 26th to September 11th, U.S. aircraft, totaling 130 batches and 740 sorties, successively intruded into the airspace over the cities and counties of Andong City in Liaodong Province and Zhuanghe, Kuandian, Fengcheng, Andong, Ji'an, Linjiang, Huanren, Xiuyan, Changbai, Fusong, Jingyu, Huinan, Hailong, as well as Tumen City and Hunchun County in Jilin Province. The Chinese people are angrily protesting against this outrageous act of provocation by the U.S. aggressors.

### 美侵朝空军继续侵犯我东北领空

1952-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

    美侵朝空军继续侵犯我东北领空
    【新华社沈阳十三日电】最近半月来，美国侵朝空军继续侵犯我国东北领空。自八月二十六日到九月十一日间，美机共一百三十批七百四十架次，先后侵入我辽东省安东市和庄河、宽甸、凤城、安东、辑安、临江、桓仁、岫岩、长白、抚松、靖宇、辉南、海龙以及吉林省图们市、珲春县等市县境内上空。我国人民愤怒地抗议美国侵略者这种狂妄的挑衅行为。
