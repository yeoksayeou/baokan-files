Title: Czechoslovak Sample Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-09-08
Page: 1
    【Xinhua News Agency】Czechoslovak Commercial Office Sample Exhibition opened in Beijing on the 4th. The opening ceremony was presided over by the Czechoslovak Commercial Counselor, Shar. The Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Kang Sala, and diplomatic personnel from the embassy attended. Guests attending the opening ceremony included commercial personnel from Eastern European people's democracies stationed in China. On the Chinese side, there were Ji Chaoding, Deputy Director of the International Economic Affairs Bureau of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government, and more than 30 personnel from the Ministry of Foreign Trade, the Ministry of Light Industry, the Ministry of Heavy Industry, the First and Second Ministries of Machine Building, the Ministry of Health, the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and import companies. The sample exhibition displayed a variety of exquisite industrial products, medical equipment, telecommunications equipment, etc.

### 捷样品展览所在京开幕

1952-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    捷样品展览所在京开幕
    【新华社讯】捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆商务处样品展览所四日在北京开幕。开幕式由捷克斯洛伐克商务参赞沙尔主持，捷驻华大使康萨拉暨使馆外交人员均前往参加。参加开幕式的来宾有东欧各人民民主国家的驻华商务人员，中国方面有中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会国际经济事务局副局长冀朝鼎和对外贸易部、轻工业部、重工业部、第一、第二机械工业部、卫生部、邮电部、外交部和进口公司人员三十余人。样品展览所陈列有多种精美的工业用品、医疗器材、电讯器材等。
