Title: Government Administration Council Holds 151st Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-09-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 19th: The 151st meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 19th. The meeting heard and approved the main points of the 1952 work plan of the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government, as reported by Minister of Health Li Dequan, and also passed several proposals for appointment and removal to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第一五一次会议

1952-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一五一次会议
    【新华社十九日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百五十一次政务会议在十九日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府卫生部李德全部长报告的该部一九五二年工作计划要点，并通过了提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
