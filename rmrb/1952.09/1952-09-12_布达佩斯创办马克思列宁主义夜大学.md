Title: Budapest Establishes Marxist-Leninist Night University
Author:
Date: 1952-09-12
Page: 4

Budapest Establishes Marxist-Leninist Night University

    The first two-year Marxist-Leninist Night University was recently established in Budapest, the capital of Hungary. The task of this school is to train cadres of the Party and the state, as well as workers of mass organizations.

    The school's curriculum includes: History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, dialectical materialism and historical materialism, history of the Hungarian Working People's Party, political economy, and general history. In addition, students will study classical Marxist-Leninist works.

    The school has recently enrolled new students. Applicants include both manual and intellectual workers.

### 布达佩斯创办马克思列宁主义夜大学

1952-09-12
第4版()
专栏：

    布达佩斯创办马克思列宁主义夜大学
    匈牙利首都布达佩斯最近成立了第一个两年制的马克思列宁主义夜大学。这个学校的任务是培养党与国家的干部和群众组织的工作者。
    该校学生的学习课程包括：苏联共产党历史、辩证唯物主义和历史唯物主义、匈牙利劳动人民党的历史、政治经济学与一般的历史等。此外，还要学习经典的马克思列宁主义著作。
    该校最近已招收新生。应考者包括体力劳动者和脑力劳动者。
