Title: Establishment of the Central First Ministry of Machine Building, etc.
Author:
Date: 1952-09-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. In accordance with the resolution of the 17th meeting of the Central People's Government Committee, the Central People's Government's First Ministry of Machine Building, Second Ministry of Machine Building, Ministry of Construction Engineering, Ministry of Geology, and Ministry of Food were officially established and began operations on September 1st.

### 中央第一机械工业部等成立

1952-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中央第一机械工业部等成立
    【新华社四日讯】遵照中央人民政府委员会第十七次会议的决议，中央人民政府第一机械工业部、第二机械工业部、建筑工程部、地质部及粮食部分别于九月一日正式成立，开始办公。
