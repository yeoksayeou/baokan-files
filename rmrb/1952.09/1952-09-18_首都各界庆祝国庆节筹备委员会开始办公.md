Title: Capital's Various Circles' National Day Celebration Preparatory Committee Begins Work
Author:
Date: 1952-09-18
Page: 1

    [This newspaper's report] The Preparatory Committee of the Capital's Various Circles for Celebrating the National Day of the People's Republic of China has already begun its work and is actively carrying out preparatory work. The office of the committee is located in Zhongshan Park. Telephone: Three Bureau Five Eight Three Seven, Five Eight Three Eight.
    In other news: In order to organize and systematically lead the city's industrialists and businessmen to enthusiastically celebrate National Day and welcome the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, and to successfully hold the Beijing Urban and Rural Materials Exchange Conference, the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce has now established a celebration preparatory committee, and sub-committees have also been established in various districts.

### 首都各界庆祝国庆节筹备委员会开始办公

1952-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各界庆祝国庆节筹备委员会开始办公
    【本报讯】首都各界人民庆祝中华人民共和国国庆节筹备委员会业已开始办公，正在积极进行筹备工作。该会办公地点设在中山公园内。电话：三局五八三七、五八三八。
    又讯：北京市工商业联合会为了有组织有计划的领导全市工商业者热烈庆祝国庆节和迎接亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议，并开好北京市城乡物资交流大会，现已成立了庆祝筹备委员会，各区也分别设立分会。
