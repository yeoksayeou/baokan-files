    Our Great Motherland
    Henan tobacco, famous in our country, has a long history of cultivation and excellent quality. After liberation, with the vigorous promotion of the People's Government, it has been fully developed and has had another bumper harvest this year. The tobacco cultivated in the Henan Tobacco Experimental Field is more than two meters eighty centimeters high. This is a scene of the staff of the field measuring it.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国著名的河南烟叶，栽培历史悠久，品质佳良，解放后，经人民政府大力提倡，得到充分的发展，今年又获丰收。河南省烟叶试验场培植的烟叶，高达二公尺八十公分以上。这是该场的工作人员正在测量的情形。
