Title: Typhoon to Invade China's South Sea Again
Author:
Date: 1952-09-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a typhoon bulletin at 12:00 noon on the 4th, stating that another typhoon had formed in the southeastern Pacific Ocean at 2:00 PM on the 4th. At 8:00 PM, the center of the typhoon had moved to the northwest coast of Luzon Island (18 degrees north latitude, 120.2 degrees east longitude). It is currently moving in a west-northwesterly direction at a speed of 50 li per hour. In the next twelve hours, there will be strong winds, heavy rain, and large waves in China's South Sea.

### 我国南海又将有台风侵袭

1952-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我国南海又将有台风侵袭
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中央气象台四日下午十二时发布台风消息称：四日下午二时在东南太平洋产生另一台风。下午八时台风中心移至吕宋岛西北海岸（北纬十八度，东经一二○·二度）。目前正以每小时五十华里的速度，向西北偏西方向进行。未来十二小时内，我国南海将有大风雨与大浪。
