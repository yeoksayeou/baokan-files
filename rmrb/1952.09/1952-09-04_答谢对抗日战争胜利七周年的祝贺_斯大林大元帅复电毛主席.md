Title: Marshal Stalin Replies to Chairman Mao, Thanking Him for Congratulations on the Seventh Anniversary of Victory in the War of Resistance Against Japan
Author:
Date: 1952-09-04
Page: 1

    Answering Congratulations on the Seventh Anniversary of Victory in the War of Resistance Against Japan
    Marshal Stalin Replies to Chairman Mao
    [Xinhua News Agency, 3rd] September 3rd is the seventh anniversary of the victory of the War of Resistance Against Japan. Chairman Mao Zedong of our Central People's Government sent a telegram to Marshal Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, on the 2nd to express congratulations. Marshal Stalin replied to Chairman Mao Zedong on the same day to express his thanks. The full text is as follows:
    To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:
    Comrade Chairman, please accept my thanks for your friendly sentiments expressed towards the Soviet people and the Soviet army on the occasion of the seventh anniversary of the victory over the Japanese imperialists. The Chinese people and their People's Liberation Army played a tremendous role in this historic victory. Their heroism and self-sacrificing spirit promoted the cause of eliminating Japanese aggression. The great friendship between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China is a reliable guarantee against new threats of aggression and a strong fortress for peace in the Far East and the world.
    Comrade Chairman, please accept the congratulations of the Soviet Union and the Soviet Army on the occasion of the seventh anniversary of the Chinese people's liberation from the oppression of Japanese imperialism.
    Long live the unbreakable friendship between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics!
    Long live the People's Liberation Army of the People's Republic of China!
    Yo. Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union
    Moscow, September 2, 1952

### 答谢对抗日战争胜利七周年的祝贺  斯大林大元帅复电毛主席

1952-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    答谢对抗日战争胜利七周年的祝贺
    斯大林大元帅复电毛主席
    【新华社三日讯】九月三日是抗日战争胜利七周年纪念日，我中央人民政府毛泽东主席曾于二日致电苏联部长会议主席斯大林大元帅表示祝贺。斯大林大元帅已于同日复电毛泽东主席致谢。全文如下：北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东同志：
    主席同志，请接受我对您的谢意，感谢您在战胜日本帝国主义者七周年纪念时对苏联人民及苏联军队所表示的友情。中国人民及其人民解放军在这一历史性的胜利中起了巨大的作用，他们的英雄主义和自我牺牲的精神促进了消灭日本侵略的事业。苏联和中华人民共和国之间的伟大友谊是反对新侵略威胁的可靠保证，是远东及全世界和平的坚强堡垒。
    主席同志，值此中国人民从日本帝国主义压迫下获得解放七周年之际，请接受苏联及苏联军队的祝贺。
    中华人民共和国与苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟之间牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    中华人民共和国人民解放军万岁！
    苏联部长会议主席约·斯大林
    一九五二年九月二日于莫斯科
