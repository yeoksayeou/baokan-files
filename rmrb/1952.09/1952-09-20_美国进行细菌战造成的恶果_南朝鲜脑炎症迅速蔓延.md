Title: The Evil Consequences of the U.S. Germ Warfare: Encephalitis Spreads Rapidly in South Korea
Author:
Date: 1952-09-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 19th. The evil consequences of the U.S. germ warfare in South Korea are expanding. According to the South Korean "Hapdong News Agency" on the 16th, encephalitis is spreading rapidly in South Korea. The number of people suffering from encephalitis and the number of deaths from encephalitis are increasing daily. As of September 15, the number of encephalitis cases in Busan had increased to 724, and the number of deaths had increased to 182. In one day alone, from the 14th to the 15th, there were 40 new cases of encephalitis and 13 deaths.

### 美国进行细菌战造成的恶果  南朝鲜脑炎症迅速蔓延

1952-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

    美国进行细菌战造成的恶果
    南朝鲜脑炎症迅速蔓延
    【新华社平壤十九日电】美国进行细菌战在南朝鲜造成的恶果正在扩大。据南朝鲜的合同通讯社十六日透露：脑炎症正在南朝鲜迅速蔓延中。患脑炎症的人数和因患脑炎症而死亡的人数日益增加，截至九月十五日，釜山市患脑炎症的人数已增加到七百二十四人，死亡人数增加到一百八十二人。只在十四日到十五日的一天中，患脑炎者就有四十人，死十三人。
