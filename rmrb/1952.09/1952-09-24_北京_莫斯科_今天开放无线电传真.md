Title: Beijing-Moscow Wireless Facsimile Opens Today
Author:
Date: 1952-09-24
Page: 1

    Beijing—Moscow
    Wireless Facsimile Opens Today
    [Xinhua News Agency, 23rd] The Beijing-Moscow wireless facsimile (photo telegraph), will be officially opened on September 24th. Photos and images of the opening of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference and our country's Third National Day will be transmitted to Moscow that day.
    Note: The Shanghai-Moscow wireless facsimile was opened on April 25, 1950.

### 北京—莫斯科  今天开放无线电传真

1952-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    北京—莫斯科
    今天开放无线电传真
    【新华社二十三日讯】北京—莫斯科无线电传真（相片电报），将在九月二十四日正式开放。亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议开幕和我国第三届国庆节的相片、图片等，当天就能传到莫斯科。
    按：上海—莫斯科无线电传真，已在一九五○年四月二十五日开放。
