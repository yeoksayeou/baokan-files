Title: Seven US Delegates Attending Peace Conference Send Telegram to "New York-Beijing Peace Rally"
Author:
Date: 1952-09-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: The seven US delegates attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, who have already arrived in Beijing, sent a telegram on the 24th to the "New York-Beijing Peace Rally" scheduled to be held in New York on September 25th. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To the Committee to Promote US People's Participation in the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference:

The first batch of a large US delegation, seven representatives, have been honorably welcomed by our Chinese friends and are being hosted in the beautiful, modern Beijing Peace Hotel, which was completed in forty-five days for the historic gathering of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. More than one hundred delegates from the Western Hemisphere are arriving successively. Delegates from twenty-two other countries have also arrived here. Your US delegation is determined to find a way to stop the massacre in Korea, prevent Japanese rearmament, and achieve world peace. Anne T. Wilcox, Henry Wilcox, Isabel Milton Sernai, Edwin Sernai, Louis Suzuki, Geng Lishu, Walter Easley.

### 出席和平会议的美国代表七人  致电“纽约—北京和平集会”

1952-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    出席和平会议的美国代表七人
    致电“纽约—北京和平集会”
    【新华社二十六日讯】已经抵达北京的美国出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议代表七人，于二十四日致电定于九月二十五日在纽约召开的“纽约—北京和平集会”，电文如下：
    促使美国人民参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议委员会：
    人数众多的美国代表团的第一批代表七人，光荣地受到中国朋友们的欢迎，被招待在美丽的、现代化的北京和平宾馆中居住，这个和平宾馆是在四十五天中完成的，以供亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议这个有历史意义的集会之用。从西半球来的一百多位代表正在陆续到达。已经到达此地的还有其他二十二个国家的代表。你们的美国代表团决心设法停止在朝鲜的大屠杀，防止日本重整军备，并取得世界和平。安妮泰·维尔科克斯，亨利·维尔科克斯，依莎贝尔·密尔顿·塞尔奈，艾得温·塞尔奈，路易斯·铃木，耿丽淑，华尔特·易思礼。
