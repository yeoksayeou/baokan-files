Title: French Government Continues to Reinforce Aggressor Troops in Vietnam
Author: 
Date: 1952-09-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, September 17th: The French government continues to reinforce its expeditionary forces that are waging a criminal war against the Vietnamese people. The French ship "Bastille" departed on September 16th carrying 1,600 soldiers bound for Saigon.

### 法国政府继续增援侵越军队

1952-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    法国政府继续增援侵越军队
    【新华社讯】塔斯社巴黎十七日讯：法国政府继续增援正在对越南人民进行罪恶战争的远征军。法舰“巴士底号”已于九月十六日载运一千六百名士兵离此开往西贡。
