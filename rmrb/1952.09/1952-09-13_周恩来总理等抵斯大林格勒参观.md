Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Others Arrive in Stalingrad for a Visit
Author:
Date: 1952-09-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Stalingrad, September 12th: According to TASS, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, and representatives of the government delegation of the People's Republic of China he led, arrived in Stalingrad on the 10th. Greeting them at the airport were the Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Stalingrad Oblast, the Secretary of the Stalingrad Oblast Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks), the Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Stalingrad City, and many representatives of people's organizations in the city. On the same day, Zhou Enlai and his entourage visited the city of Stalingrad and the remains of the Great Patriotic War. They also visited the Stalingrad Museum, which exhibits historical relics of the defense of Tsaritsyn-Stalingrad, as well as the Tractor Factory and the "Red October" Factory. They were warmly welcomed by the working people of this heroic city.

The Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, laid a wreath at the Cemetery of Martyrs in Stalingrad.

The Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Stalingrad Oblast and the Chairman of the City Soviet hosted a banquet for Zhou Enlai and members of the government delegation of the People's Republic of China on the 10th. On the same day, Zhou Enlai and his entourage watched the play "Conscience". On the 11th, they traveled by boat along the Lenin Volga-Don Canal.

### 周恩来总理等抵斯大林格勒参观

1952-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理等抵斯大林格勒参观
    【新华社十二日讯】据塔斯社斯大林格勒讯：中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来和他所率领的中华人民共和国政府代表团的代表于十日抵达斯大林格勒。到机场欢迎的有斯大林格勒州执行委员会主席、联共（布）斯大林格勒州委会书记、斯大林格勒市执行委员会主席以及该市人民团体代表多人。同日周恩来和他的同行人员参观了斯大林格勒市容和卫国战争的遗迹。他们还参观了陈列察里津—斯大林格勒保卫战史迹的斯大林博物馆以及拖拉机工厂和“红十月”工厂。他们受到了这个英雄城的劳动人民热烈的欢迎。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来在斯大林格勒烈士墓前献了花圈。
    斯大林格勒州执行委员会主席和市苏维埃主席曾于十日欢宴周恩来和中华人民共和国政府代表团的团员。同日周恩来和他的同行人员观看了“良心”一剧。十一日他们曾坐轮船沿列宁伏尔加河—顿河运河旅行。
