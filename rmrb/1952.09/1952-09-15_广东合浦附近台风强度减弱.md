Title: Typhoon Intensity Weakens Near Hepu, Guangdong
Author:
Date: 1952-09-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 14th: The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a typhoon bulletin at 6:00 PM on the 14th, stating that at 2:00 PM on the 14th, the center of the typhoon was located near Hepu, Guangdong, and its intensity continued to weaken. The wind force along the western coast of Guangdong remained strong, but other areas were not greatly affected.

### 广东合浦附近台风强度减弱

1952-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    广东合浦附近台风强度减弱
    【新华社十四日讯】十四日下午六时中央气象台发布台风消息称：十四日下午二时台风的中心位置在广东合浦附近，强度仍在继续减弱，广东西部沿海风力仍较大，其他地区无大影响。
