Title: Establishment of the Central Ministry of Foreign Trade and the Ministry of Commerce
Author:
Date: 1952-09-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 3rd:

    In accordance with the resolution of the 17th meeting of the Central People's Government Committee, the Central People's Government Ministry of Foreign Trade and the Central People's Government Ministry of Commerce were formally established and began operations on September 3rd. The original Central People's Government Ministry of Trade was simultaneously abolished.

### 中央对外贸易部、商业部成立

1952-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中央对外贸易部、商业部成立
    【新华社三日讯】遵照中央人民政府委员会第十七次会议的决议，中央人民政府对外贸易部、中央人民政府商业部分别于九月三日正式成立，开始办公。原中央人民政府贸易部同时撤销。
