Title: The Government Administration Council Held its 149th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-09-06
Page: 1

    The Government Administration Council Held its 149th Meeting
    [Xinhua News Agency, 5th] The 149th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 5th. The meeting, after listening to the explanation made by Ma Xulun, Minister of Education of the Central People's Government, approved the main points of the ministry's work plan for 1952. The meeting, after listening to the explanation made by Qu Wu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Central People's Government Administration Council, passed the decision on the establishment of the Research Institute of Culture and History. In addition, the meeting also approved several appointment and removal cases and submitted appointment and removal cases to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第一四九次会议

1952-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一四九次会议
    【新华社五日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百四十九次政务会议在五日下午举行。会议在听取了中央人民政府教育部马叙伦部长所作的说明后，批准了该部一九五二年工作计划要点。会议在听取了中央人民政府政务院屈武副秘书长所作的说明后，通过了关于设置文史研究馆的决定。此外会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
