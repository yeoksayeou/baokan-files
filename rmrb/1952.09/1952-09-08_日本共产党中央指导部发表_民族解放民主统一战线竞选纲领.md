Title: Japanese Communist Party Central Guidance Department Announces National Liberation Democratic United Front Election Platform
Author:
Date: 1952-09-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, September 5th: According to a report in "Akahata," as the House of Representatives election approaches, the Central Guidance Department of the Japanese Communist Party has announced the following National Liberation Democratic United Front election platform:

	(1) Abolish the separate peace treaty, the U.S.-Japan "Security Treaty" and "Administrative Agreement," abolish the U.S.-Japan Fisheries Agreement, and thoroughly implement the Potsdam Proclamation.
	(2) Conclude a comprehensive peace treaty with the participation of the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China, and conclude a five-power peace pact.
	(3) Oppose rearmament, oppose turning the Japanese people into foreign mercenaries, eliminate military bases, and withdraw all occupying forces.
	(4) Establish free trade relations with all countries on the basis of equality and mutual benefit.
	(5) Everyone has the freedom to participate in patriotic and peace movements, abolish the "Subversive Activities Prevention Law," oppose laws prohibiting general strikes and restricting demonstrations, oppose malicious revisions to the election law, oppose malicious revisions to the constitution, and oppose the suppression of other ethnic groups in Japan.
	(6) Raise the people's living standards, abolish heavy taxes used for war purposes, reduce prices, and protect national culture.
	(7) Distribute land to working farmers, oppose feudal exploitation and oppression of farmers and fishermen, oppose seizing farmers' land, oppose compulsory purchase of rice, determine reasonable prices for agricultural products, and provide farmers and fishermen with necessary funds.
	(8) Implement an eight-hour workday, eliminate unemployment, oppose the exploitation of labor, determine wages sufficient to cover minimum living expenses, and establish a comprehensive social insurance system.
	(9) Freely develop peaceful industries, oppose implementing wartime economic controls, oppose engaging in military production, and guarantee entrepreneurs and merchants have access to funds, materials, electricity, and markets.
	(10) Overthrow the Liberal Party's traitorous government and establish a national liberation united government.

### 日本共产党中央指导部发表  民族解放民主统一战线竞选纲领

1952-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

    日本共产党中央指导部发表
    民族解放民主统一战线竞选纲领
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社东京五日讯：据“赤旗报”报道，在国会众院选举快要到来的时候，日本共产党中央指导部发表了下列的民族解放民主统一战线竞选纲领：
    （一）废除单独和约、美日“安全条约”和“行政协定”，废除美日渔业协定，彻底执行波茨坦公告。
    （二）缔结有苏联和中华人民共和国参加的全面和约，缔结五大国和平公约。
    （三）反对重新武装，反对把日本国民变成外国的雇佣军，取消军事基地，撤退全部占领军。
    （四）在平等互惠的基础上与一切国家建立自由贸易关系。
    （五）人人有参加爱国运动与和平运动的自由，废除“防止破坏活动法”，反对禁止总罢工和限制示威的法律，反对对选举法作恶劣的修改，反对对宪法作恶劣的修改，反对镇压在日本的其他民族。
    （六）提高人民生活水平，取消用于战争目的的重税，减低物价，保护民族文化。
    （七）把田地分给劳动农民，反对对农民和渔民进行封建剥削和压迫，反对霸占农民的土地，反对强迫征购大米，确定农产品的合理价格，给农民和渔民以必要的资金。
    （八）实行每日八小时工作制，消灭失业现象，反对剥削劳工，确定足以支付最低生活费用的工资，建立完备的社会保险制度。
    （九）自由发展和平工业，反对实行战时方式的经济管制，反对进行军备生产、保证企业家和商人有资金、物资、电力和市场。
    （十）打倒自由党卖国政府，建立一个民族解放联合政府。
