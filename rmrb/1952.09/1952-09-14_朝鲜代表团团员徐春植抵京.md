Title: DPRK Delegation Member Xu Chunzhi Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-09-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 13th:

    Xu Chunzhi (President of the Korean Central News Agency), a member of the Korean delegation attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, and his entourage, Kim Jae-hou, arrived in Beijing by train on the morning of the 13th. Wang Yi, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily, Deng Gang, Deputy Secretary-General of the Xinhua News Agency, and several Chinese staff members from the secretariat of the Peace Conference Preparatory Committee were at the station to welcome them. The representatives shook hands with the welcomers one by one and accepted flowers from the Young Pioneers.

### 朝鲜代表团团员徐春植抵京

1952-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜代表团团员徐春植抵京
    【新华社十三日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的朝鲜代表团团员徐春植（朝鲜中央通讯社社长）和随员金载厚已于十三日晨乘火车抵达北京。前往车站欢迎的有人民日报副总编辑王揖、新华通讯社副秘书长邓岗，以及和平会议筹备委员会秘书处的中国工作人员多人。代表们与欢迎者一一握手，并接受少年儿童队队员们的献花。
