Title: Marshal Stalin Receives Tsedenbal
Author:
Date: 1952-09-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 6th: TASS, Moscow, September 6th: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, J.V. Stalin, received the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Tsedenbal, on September 5th.

Also received were the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic, Jamsrangiin Sambuu, and the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to the Soviet Union, Idamjab.

Present at the meeting was the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, A.Y. Vyshinsky.

### 斯大林大元帅接见泽登巴尔

1952-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    斯大林大元帅接见泽登巴尔
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社莫斯科六日电：苏联部长会议主席约·维·斯大林在九月五日接见蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔。
    受接见的还有蒙古人民共和国外交部副部长扎姆索伦—桑布和蒙古人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使伊丹甲布。
    在座的还有苏联外交部部长安·扬·维辛斯基。
