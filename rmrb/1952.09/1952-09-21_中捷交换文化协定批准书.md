Title: Exchange of Instruments of Ratification of the Sino-Czech Cultural Agreement
Author:
Date: 1952-09-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 20th. Czechoslovak Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Viliam Široký and our Ambassador Tan Xilin held a ceremony at the Czechoslovak Ministry of Foreign Affairs on the 18th to exchange instruments of ratification of the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

This agreement was signed in Beijing on May 6th this year, together with the Sino-Czech Postal Agreement, the Sino-Czech Telecommunications Agreement, and the Sino-Czech Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement. This agreement was ratified by Czechoslovak President Gottwald and the Central People's Government of our country on July 31st and August 7th respectively.

### 中捷交换文化协定批准书

1952-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中捷交换文化协定批准书
    【新华社布拉格二十日电】捷克斯洛伐克副总理兼外交部长西罗基与我国谭希林大使十八日在捷克斯洛伐克外交部举行中捷文化合作协定批准书的交换仪式。
    这个协定是与中捷邮政协定、中捷电信协定及中捷科学与技术合作协定一道于本年五月六日在北京签订的。这个协定已由捷克斯洛伐克总统哥特瓦尔德和我中央人民政府分别于七月三十一日和八月七日加以批准。
