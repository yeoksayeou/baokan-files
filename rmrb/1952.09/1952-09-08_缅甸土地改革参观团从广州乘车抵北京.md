Title: Burmese Land Reform Delegation Arrives in Beijing by Train from Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1952-09-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The Burmese Land Reform Delegation, a group of four people led by the head of the delegation, Tekin Zhen, accompanied by Wang Jizhi, Deputy Director of the Production Movement Department of the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, and Cao Guisheng, Deputy Section Chief of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Beijing by special train from Guangzhou on the 7th. Yang Xiandong, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, Li Shijun, Director of the Plant Protection and Quarantine Department, Meng Yongqian, Deputy Director of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, Yang Gangyi, Deputy Secretary-General, Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, He Ying, Deputy Director of the Asian Department, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, Zhang Youyu, Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and Zheng Zhenduo, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, went to the station to welcome them.

Also at the station to greet them were the Burmese Ambassador to China, Wu La Mao, and personnel from the Burmese Embassy.

After getting off the train, the delegation shook hands with the welcomers and accepted flowers. The delegation will stay in Beijing for several days and then go to North China and East China to visit the new rural areas after China's land reform.

### 缅甸土地改革参观团从广州乘车抵北京

1952-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸土地改革参观团从广州乘车抵北京
    【新华社七日讯】缅甸土地改革参观团一行四人，在团长德钦阵率领下，由中央人民政府农业部生产运动处副处长王继之和外交部亚洲司副科长曹桂生等陪同，从广州乘专车于七日到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有：中央人民政府农业部副部长杨显东、病虫害防治司司长李世俊、中华全国合作社联合总社副主任孟用潜、副秘书长杨刚毅、中央人民政府外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航、亚洲司副司长何英、交际处处长王倬如、北京市人民政府副市长张友渔和中缅友好协会会长郑振铎等。
    到站迎接的，还有缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂和缅甸大使馆人员。
    参观团在下车后，与欢迎者一一握手并接受献花。参观团将在北京停留数日，然后赴华北、华东等地参观我国土地改革后的新农村。
