Title: Ceylon Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-09-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 17th (delayed dispatch) The Ceylon Trade Delegation, headed by the Ceylon Minister of Commerce and Trade, Mr. Senanayake, arrived in Guangzhou on the afternoon of the 17th and left for Beijing that evening. Those who went to the station to welcome and see them off included: Lin Langtian, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Federation of Industry and Commerce, Deng Wenzhao, Deputy Director of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government Commercial Department, Liang Xiang, Director of the Guangzhou Municipal People's Government Industry and Commerce Bureau, Wang Douguang, Director of the Guangzhou Foreign Trade Administration Bureau, and Cao Ruoming, Director of the Guangzhou Municipal People's Government Foreign Affairs Office.

    Gao Shanglin, the special representative of Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, went to Shenzhen to meet the Ceylon Trade Delegation and accompanied them to Beijing.

    In addition: The Ceylon Trade Delegation arrived in Beijing on the 19th via Hankou. Wen Xiang, Director of the Wuhan Municipal People's Government Industry and Commerce Bureau, Wu Chuanqi, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee of the Wuhan Federation of Industry and Commerce, and Li Dihua, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Central and Southern Military and Political Committee, went to the station to welcome and see them off.

### 锡兰贸易代表团来京

1952-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    锡兰贸易代表团来京
    【新华社广州十七日电】（迟到）以锡兰商务贸易部部长沈纳那亚克为首的锡兰贸易代表团一行七人于十七日下午到达广州，并于当晚离广州赴北京。前往车站欢迎和欢送者有：广州市工商业联合会副主任委员林朗天、广东省人民政府商业厅副厅长邓文钊、广州市人民政府工商局局长梁湘、广州对外贸易管理局局长王斗光、广州市人民政府外事处处长曹若茗等人。
    中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸特派代表高尚林前往深圳迎接锡兰贸易代表团，并陪同前赴北京。
    又讯：锡兰贸易代表团，已于十九日经汉口来北京。武汉市人民政府工商管理局局长文祥、武汉市工商业联合会筹备会秘书长吴传启、中南军政委员会外事处处长李棣华等前往车站欢迎和欢送。
