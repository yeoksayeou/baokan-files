Title: Peace Rally Held in West Bengal, India
Author:
Date: 1952-09-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, September 3rd: The Preparatory Committee of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference in West Bengal, India, held a meeting in the Islamia College Hall in Calcutta on August 30th and 31st. The meeting was presided over by the well-known Indian doctor, Gani. Representatives from more than thirty mass organizations attended. The meeting extended warm congratulations to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference to be held in Beijing.

The representatives attending the meeting unanimously passed a resolution on August 30th, demanding the immediate realization of an armistice in Korea, the abolition of the Treaty of San Francisco, the signing of a genuine peace treaty with Japan, and the removal of obstacles to cultural and economic relations between China and other Asian countries. Speakers at the meeting unanimously called on the people to expand the struggle for peace throughout India.

Beg, a representative of the jute factory workers in Bengal, pointed out the serious impact the working class might suffer in the event of war and advocated expanding the peace movement among the workers and peasants.

On August 31st, five thousand people held a public rally in torrential rain. Dr. Saha, a member of the Indian Parliament and a famous scientist, said that the United States is preparing for war. He said that the imperialists have embarked on the road to war in order to maintain their system of exploitation. If imperialism is eliminated, lasting peace can be established throughout the world. Professor Bhattacharya, a member of the West Bengal Legislative Assembly, said that the upcoming Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference will point out the way for the people of every Asian country to achieve complete freedom and national honor. As long as the people of all countries are united, the imperialists who prevent the people of the world from embarking on the path of peace can be defeated.

### 印度西孟加拉省举行和平集会

1952-09-05
第4版()
专栏：

    印度西孟加拉省举行和平集会
    【新华社新德里三日电】亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议印度西孟加拉省筹备委员会于八月三十日和三十一日在加尔各答回教学院礼堂举行会议。会议由印度著名医生甘尼主持。到会的有三十多个群众团体的代表。会议向即将在北京举行的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议致热烈的祝贺。
    参加会议的代表们在八月三十日一致通过决议，要求立即实现朝鲜停战，废除旧金山条约，签订真正的对日和约，扫除中国与其他亚洲国家之间的文化与经济关系方面的障碍。在会上发言的人一致号召人民扩展印度各地的争取和平的斗争。
    孟加拉黄麻工厂工人代表贝格指出了万一战争爆发时工人阶级可能遭受的严重打击，并主张扩展工人与农民群众中间的和平运动。
    八月三十一日，五千人在倾盆大雨中举行公共集会。印度国会议员、著名科学家沙哈博士说，美国正在准备战争。他说，帝国主义者已走上战争的道路，借以维持他们的剥削制度。如果把帝国主义消灭，就可以在全世界建立持久的和平。西孟加拉省立法议会议员巴塔查利雅教授说，即将举行的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议将给每一个亚洲国家的人民指出取得完全自由和民族荣誉的道路。只要各国人民团结一致，那么不让全世界人民走上和平道路的帝国主义者是可以击败的。
