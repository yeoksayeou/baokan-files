Title: Li Xian and Wu Hongyi, Representatives of China, Depart to Attend Preparatory Meeting for International Social Security Conference
Author: 
Date: 1952-09-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 9th—Li Xian, Deputy Director of the Labor Insurance Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Wu Hongyi, Chairman of the Harbin Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, representatives of China to the preparatory meeting for the International Social Security Conference initiated by the World Federation of Trade Unions, which will be held in Vienna on September 15, departed by plane on the morning of the 9th to attend the meeting.

### 出席国际社会保险会议筹备会  我国代表李仙和吴宏毅启程

1952-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    出席国际社会保险会议筹备会
    我国代表李仙和吴宏毅启程
    【新华社九日讯】世界工联发起的国际社会保险会议筹备会九月十五日在维也纳召开。代表我国出席筹备会的中华全国总工会劳保部副部长李仙和哈尔滨市总工会主席吴宏毅已于九日晨乘飞机前往出席。
