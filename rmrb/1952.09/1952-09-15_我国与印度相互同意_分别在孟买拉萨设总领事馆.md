Title: China and India Mutually Agree to Establish Consulates General in Bombay and Lhasa Respectively
Author:
Date: 1952-09-15
Page: 4

    China and India Mutually Agree
    To Establish Consulates General in Bombay and Lhasa Respectively
    [Xinhua News Agency, 14th] The governments of China and India have mutually agreed that the People's Republic of China will establish a Consulate General in Bombay, India, and the Republic of India will establish a Consulate General in Lhasa, Tibet, China.

### 我国与印度相互同意  分别在孟买拉萨设总领事馆

1952-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    我国与印度相互同意
    分别在孟买拉萨设总领事馆
    【新华社十四日讯】我国政府与印度政府间已相互同意：中华人民共和国将在印度孟买设立总领事馆，印度共和国将在我国西藏拉萨设立总领事馆。
