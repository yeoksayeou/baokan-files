Title: Central Meteorological Observatory Issues Typhoon Warning
Author:
Date: 1952-09-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 31st — The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a typhoon warning at 8:00 PM on August 31st, stating: At 2:00 PM on August 31st, a typhoon of moderate intensity was located between Taiwan and the Philippines. Its center was located in the sea 1,000 li southeast of Tainan City (18.9 degrees north latitude, 122.3 degrees east longitude). It is moving northwest at a speed of approximately 50 li per hour. The maximum wind speed near the center is about 200 li per hour, and the wind speed 500 li away from the center is about 100 to 150 li per hour. The southeastern coast of China, from Wenzhou to Hong Kong, will be threatened; within 24 hours, the wind speed in this area will generally increase to 80 to 120 li per hour, with heavy rain and heavy waves.

### 中央气象台发布台风警报

1952-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中央气象台发布台风警报
    【新华社三十一日讯】三十一日下午八时中央气象台发布台风警报称：三十一日下午二时，在台湾和菲律宾之间，有一中等强度的台风。它的中心位置在台南市东南一千华里的海上（北纬十八点九度，东经一百二十二点三度）。以每小时约五十华里的速度，向西北方向进行。中心附近最大风速约为每小时二百华里，离开中心五百华里的地方，风速约为每小时一百至一百五十华里。我国东南沿海自温州到香港一带，将受到威胁；在二十四小时内，这一带地区的风速，将普遍增加到每小时八十至一百二十华里，并有大雨和大浪。
