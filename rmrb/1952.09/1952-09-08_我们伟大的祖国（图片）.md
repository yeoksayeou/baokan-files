    Our Great Motherland
    In order to welcome the high tide of the motherland's economic construction, some cadres who originally worked in the army have entered various specialized schools to study and have achieved excellent results. This is a female cadre transferred from the army, and she is conducting experiments in the physics laboratory of Beijing Agricultural University.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了迎接祖国经济建设的高潮，一部分原来在部队工作的干部，已进入各种专科学校学习，获得了很好的成绩。这是从部队调来的一个女干部，她正在北京农业大学物理实验室中进行实验。
