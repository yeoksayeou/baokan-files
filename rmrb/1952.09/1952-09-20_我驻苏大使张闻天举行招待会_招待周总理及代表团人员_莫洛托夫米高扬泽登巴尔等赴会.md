Title: Our Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Zhang Wentian, Holds Reception to Host Premier Zhou and Delegation Members; Molotov, Mikoyan, Tsedenbal, and Others Attend

Author: 
Date: 1952-09-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 19th: Our Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union, Zhang Wentian, held a reception on September 18th to host Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Zhou Enlai, and the Chinese Government Delegation he led.

Attending the reception were: Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Financial and Economic Committee of the State Council Li Fuchun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Su Yu, Minister of Heavy Industry Wang Heshou, Minister of Fuel Industry Chen Yu, Secretary-General of the Financial and Economic Committee of the State Council Song Shaowen, Commander of the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Liu Yalou, Deputy Commander of the Navy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Luo Shun Chu, Deputy Commander of the Artillery of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Qiu Chuangcheng, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building of the Central People's Government Wang Daohan, Vice Minister of Posts and Telecommunications Wang Zheng, Political Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government Shi Zhe, Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Affairs Department Xu Yixin, Director of the Asian Affairs Department Chen Jiakang, as well as all staff members of our government delegation and responsible officials and staff of our Embassy in Moscow.

Attending the reception from the Soviet side were: Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union Molotov, Mikoyan, Bulganin, Kosygin, Malyshev, and Pervukhin, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic Tarasov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic Chernousov, Soviet Ministers Vyshinsky, Vasilevsky, Zverev, Kuznetsov, Kumykin, Stoletov, Beshchev, Psurchev, Gorshenin, Baibakov, Zasyadko, Lomako, Khlamov, Tikhomirov, Kazakov, Maksarev, Khrunichev, Benediktov, Zhevoronkov, Smirnov, Bolsakov, and Shashkov, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Gorkin, President of the Supreme Court of the Soviet Union Volin, Procurator General of the Soviet Union Safonov, Marshal of the Soviet Union Budyonny, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union Pushkin and Podtserob, Ambassador of the Soviet Union to the People's Republic of China Panyushkin, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Trade Borisov, Pavlov, Govar, Kuzmin, Zorin, and Loshakov, Commercial Representative of the Soviet Union to the People's Republic of China Migunov, Chairman of the Moscow Soviet Yasnov, President of the Academy of Sciences of the Soviet Union Nesmeyanov, Admirals Levchenko and Sakharov, Lieutenant General of the Air Force Zhevoronkov, Generals of the Army Kuznetsov, Malinin, and Artemyev, Lieutenant Generals of the Army Batitsky and Sinilov, Rear Admirals Bekrenev and Yulin, Executive Secretary of the Soviet Peace Committee Kotov, senior officials from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Foreign Trade, the Ministry of the Army, and the Ministry of the Navy of the Soviet Union, prominent figures from society, journalists, and workers in science and the arts.

Attending the reception from the Mongolian side were: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic Tsedenbal, Minister of National Defense Bat, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Tserendorj, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to the Soviet Union Idamjab, and embassy staff members.

Also among the guests were: Ambassador of the People's Republic of Bulgaria Blagoeva, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Hungary Székely, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Rim Hai, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Nguyen Luong Bang, Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic Dalea, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic Abel, Minister of the People's Republic of Albania Natanaili, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Polish People's Republic Pogorelles, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Czechoslovak Republic Eic and staff members of the aforementioned embassies and legations.

In addition, the guests included: Ambassador of Afghanistan Sultan Ahmad Khan, Ambassador of the Union of Burma U Aung, Minister of Finland Santerström, Minister of Denmark Hegard, Minister of Switzerland Gorgé, Minister of Israel Eliashiv, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of Sweden Huitfeldt-Bergström, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of India Gandevia, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of Pakistan Ali Bin and staff members of the aforementioned embassies and legations.

### 我驻苏大使张闻天举行招待会  招待周总理及代表团人员  莫洛托夫米高扬泽登巴尔等赴会

1952-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻苏大使张闻天举行招待会  
    招待周总理及代表团人员
    莫洛托夫米高扬泽登巴尔等赴会
    【新华社莫斯科十九日电】我国驻苏联特命全权大使张闻天于九月十八日举行招待会，招待我中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来和他所率领的我国政府代表团。
    参加招待会的有：中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，政务院财政经济委员会副主任李富春，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长粟裕，重工业部部长王鹤寿，燃料工业部部长陈郁，政务院财政经济委员会秘书长宋劭文，中国人民解放军空军司令员刘亚楼，中国人民解放军海军副司令员罗舜初，中国人民解放军炮兵副司令员邱创成，中央人民政府第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，邮电部副部长王诤，中央人民政府外交部政治秘书师哲，苏联东欧司司长徐以新，亚洲司司长陈家康、以及我政府代表团全体工作人员和我国驻莫斯科大使馆各负责官员和馆员。
