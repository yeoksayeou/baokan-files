Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station to Begin Broadcasting National Day and Peace Conference Programs Today
Author: 
Date: 1952-09-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency: The Central People's Broadcasting Station will begin broadcasting "Special Programs to Welcome National Day and the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference" starting September 21st. The special programs will be broadcast twice daily, in the morning from 7:15 to 7:30 AM and in the evening from 8:00 to 9:00 PM. Tonight, Minister of the Interior of the Central People's Government, Xie Juezai, will speak about the development of the Chinese People's Democratic Regime.

### 中央人民广播电台今日开始  举办国庆及和平会议节目

1952-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民广播电台今日开始
    举办国庆及和平会议节目
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中央人民广播电台自九月二十一日起举办“迎接国庆、迎接亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议特别节目”。特别节目每日两次，早晨在七时十五分至七时三十分，晚间在二十时至二十一时。今日晚间由中央人民政府内务部部长谢觉哉讲中国人民民主政权的发展。
