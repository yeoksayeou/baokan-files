Title: Marshal Stalin Holds Banquet, Feting Chinese and Mongolian Delegations
Author: 
Date: 1952-09-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 16th: TASS Moscow, September 16th dispatch: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Comrade J.V. Stalin, held a banquet in the Kremlin on September 15th to welcome the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China headed by Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and the Government Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic headed by Zedendenbal, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic.
    The Chinese side present at the banquet included: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Li Fuchun, Vice Chairman of the Financial and Economic Commission of the State Administration Council, Zhang Wentian, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, Su Yu, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Wang Heshou, Minister of Heavy Industry, Chen Yu, Minister of Fuel Industry, Song Shaowen, Secretary-General of the Financial and Economic Commission, Liu Yalou, Commander of the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Luo Shunchu, Deputy Commander of the Navy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Qiu Chuangcheng, Deputy Commander of the Artillery of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Wang Daohan, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, Wang Zheng, Vice Minister of Posts and Telecommunications, Shi Zhe, Political Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Xu Yixin, Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chen Jiakang, Director of the Department of Asian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Ge Baoquan, Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Soviet Union, Li Qiang, Commercial Counselor, and Ji He, Military Attaché with the rank of Major General.
    The Mongolian side present at the banquet included: Zedendenbal, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Zhamso Rong-Sambu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic, and Idan Jiabu, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to the Soviet Union.
    The Soviet side present as guests included: Molotov, Malenkov, Beria, Voroshilov, Mikoyan, Bulganin, Kaganovich, Khrushchev, Saburov, Tevosyan, Malyshev, Pervukhin, Kuznetsov, Vyshinsky, Marshal Vasilyevsky, Zverev, Kuznetsov, Ignatiev, Kumykin, Deputy Minister of the Army Niderin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Podtserob, Soviet Ambassador to the People's Republic of China, Panyushkin, Soviet Ambassador to the Mongolian People's Republic, Ivanov, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Trade Borisov, Pavlov and Goval, Army General Malinin, Navy Vice Admiral Fokin, Soviet Commercial Representative to the People's Republic of China Migunov, and Fedrin, Director of the First Far Eastern Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 斯大林大元帅举行宴会  欢宴我国和蒙古代表团

1952-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

    斯大林大元帅举行宴会
    欢宴我国和蒙古代表团
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十六日电：苏联部长会议主席约·维·斯大林于九月十五日在克里姆林宫欢宴以中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来为首的中华人民共和国政府代表团和以蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔为首的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团。
    中国方面出席宴会的有：中华人民共和国政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，政务院财政经济委员会副主任李富春，中华人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使张闻天，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长粟裕，重工业部部长王鹤寿，燃料工业部部长陈郁，财政经济委员会秘书长宋劭文，中国人民解放军空军司令员刘亚楼，中国人民解放军海军副司令员罗舜初，中国人民解放军炮兵副司令员邱创成，第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，邮电部副部长王诤，中华人民共和国外交部政治秘书师哲，外交部苏联东欧司司长徐以新，外交部亚洲司司长陈家康；中华人民共和国驻苏联大使馆参赞戈宝权，商务参赞李强，武官吉合少将。
    蒙古方面出席宴会的有：蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔，蒙古人民共和国外交部副部长扎姆索伦—桑布和蒙古人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使伊丹甲布。
    苏联方面出席作陪的有：莫洛托夫，马林科夫，贝利亚，伏罗希洛夫，米高扬，布尔加宁，卡冈诺维奇，赫鲁舍切夫，萨布罗夫，提伏希安，马利雪夫，别尔乌辛，库兹涅佐夫，维辛斯基，华西列夫斯基元帅，兹维列夫，库兹聂佐夫，伊格纳切夫，库米金，陆军部副部长尼杰林，外交部副部长波德切罗布，苏联驻中华人民共和国大使潘友新，苏联驻蒙古人民共和国大使伊凡尼科夫，对外贸易部副部长鲍里邵夫、巴夫洛夫和戈瓦尔，陆军上将马里宁，海军中将伏金，苏联驻中华人民共和国商务代表米古诺夫，苏联外交部第一远东司司长费德林。
    宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
