Title: Representatives from Eleven Countries, Including Canada, Arrive in Beijing to Attend the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-09-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 25th. Representatives from eleven countries including Canada, Chile, Costa Rica, Ecuador, Guatemala, Honduras, Indonesia, Mexico, New Zealand, Nicaragua, and the United States arrived in Beijing on the 25th by train and plane to attend the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. Ms. Kalman Sandi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Secretariat of the Women's International Democratic Federation who was invited to attend the Peace Conference, Professor Brandweiner, head of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers' investigation team investigating American atrocities in Korea and germ warfare, and Mr. Burns, a British representative attending the Peace Conference as an observer, also arrived in Beijing on the 25th. Li Dequan, Hu Wenyao, and Jin Zhonghua, the Chinese representatives attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Xu Guangping, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department, Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and representatives from cultural, scientific, and religious circles went to the station and the airport to welcome them. Representatives from Australia, Costa Rica, New Zealand, and the United States who had arrived in Beijing earlier to attend the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference also went to the station and the airport to welcome them.

### 出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  加拿大等十一个国家的代表抵京

1952-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    加拿大等十一个国家的代表抵京
    卡尔曼·桑蒂、布兰德魏纳和伯恩斯同天抵达
    【新华社二十五日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的加拿大、智利、哥斯达黎加、厄瓜多尔、危地马拉、洪都拉斯、印度尼西亚、墨西哥、新西兰、尼加拉瓜及美国等十一个国家的代表于二十五日分别乘火车和飞机抵达北京。应邀参加和平会议的国际民主妇女联合会书记处副总书记卡尔曼·桑蒂、前来参加和平会议的国际民主法律工作者协会调查美军在朝鲜暴行和进行细菌战的调查团团长布兰德魏纳教授以及列席和平会议的英国代表伯恩斯也在二十五日抵京。先后前往车站和机场欢迎的有：出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的中国代表李德全、胡文耀、金仲华，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平、国际工作部副部长廖梦醒，中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长金子明和文化、科学、宗教界人士等。前已到京的出席亚洲及太平洋和平会议的澳大利亚、哥斯达黎加、新西兰和美国代表也曾先后到车站和机场欢迎。
