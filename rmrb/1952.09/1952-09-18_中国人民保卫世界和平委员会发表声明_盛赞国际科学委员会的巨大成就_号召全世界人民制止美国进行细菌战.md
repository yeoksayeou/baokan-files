Title: China Peace Committee Issues Statement Praising International Scientific Commission's Great Achievements, Calling on People Worldwide to Stop U.S. Germ Warfare

Author: 
Date: 1952-09-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th dispatch: The statement of the China Peace Committee regarding the report issued by the International Scientific Commission investigating the facts of bacteriological warfare in Korea and China. The text is as follows:

The International Scientific Commission investigating the facts of bacteriological warfare in Korea and China has victoriously completed its work, further confirming the heinous crime of the American aggressors in carrying out inhuman bacteriological warfare in Korea and Northeast China. The Chinese people express their deep emotion and admiration for the serious work undertaken by the scientists of the International Scientific Commission and their historically significant achievements.

The crime of the American aggressors in carrying out bacteriological warfare in Korea and Northeast China has long aroused unanimous public outrage and righteous condemnation from all mankind. International groups and well-known figures who have visited Korea and Northeast China to conduct investigations have repeatedly confirmed that the American aggressors did indeed use bacteriological weapons. Now, the International Scientific Commission, composed of world-renowned scientists, has presented the people of the world with a rigorous and irrefutable scientific investigation and research conclusion, proving that the people of Korea and Northeast China have indeed become the target of bacteriological weapons attacks. This makes the crimes of the American aggressors even more evident. The International Scientific Commission also told the people of the world that the American aggressors copied and utilized the old tricks of Japanese bacteriological warfare criminals, proving the vicious conspiracy of the American authorities to shelter and collect Japanese bacteriological warfare criminals in order to develop and expand bacteriological warfare.

The great efforts and rigorous achievements of the International Scientific Commission in investigating the bacteriological warfare conducted by the American aggressors are a significant contribution to mankind and will occupy a glorious page in human history. The selfless spirit of the scientists of the International Scientific Commission will further inspire the people of the world to fight for justice, truth, and peace. The crime of the American aggressors in conducting bacteriological warfare is undeniable. For the sake of human dignity, peace-loving and justice-minded people all over the world must unite more closely and resolutely stop it, so that the American bacteriological war criminals will receive the punishment they deserve.

China Peace Committee
September 16, 1952

### 中国人民保卫世界和平委员会发表声明  盛赞国际科学委员会的巨大成就  号召全世界人民制止美国进行细菌战

1952-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会发表声明
    盛赞国际科学委员会的巨大成就
    号召全世界人民制止美国进行细菌战
    【新华社十七日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会对调查在朝鲜和中国的细菌战事实国际科学委员会所发表的报告书的声明。文如下：
    调查在朝鲜和中国的细菌战事实国际科学委员会已经胜利完成工作，进一步证实了美国侵略者在朝鲜及中国东北进行灭绝人性的细菌战罪行。中国人民对于国际科学委员会科学家们所从事的严肃的工作及其有历史意义的成就，表示十分的感奋和敬佩。
    美国侵略者在朝鲜和中国东北等地进行细菌战的罪行，早已激起全人类一致的公愤和正义的谴责。曾经到过朝鲜及中国东北进行调查的国际团体和知名人士，过去已一次接一次地证实了美国侵略者确曾使用细菌武器。现在由世界著名科学家所组成的国际科学委员会，更以谨严的、无可争辩的科学调查研究的结论，向全世界人民证明朝鲜及中国东北的人民确已成为细菌武器肆行攻击的目标，这就使美国侵略者的罪行更加昭著了。国际科学委员会同时还告诉了全世界人民，美国侵略者抄袭并利用了日本细菌战犯旧有的伎俩，证明了美国当局庇护及搜罗日本的细菌战犯，借以发展及扩大细菌战争的恶毒阴谋。
    国际科学委员会关于调查美国侵略者进行细菌战所作的巨大努力及其谨严的成就，对人类是一项重大的贡献，在人类历史上将占光辉的一页。国际科学委员会科学家们的大公无私的精神，将进一步鼓舞全世界人民为正义、真理与和平而斗争。美国侵略者进行细菌战的罪行是无可抵赖的，为了人类的尊严，全世界爱好和平与正义的人民，必须更加紧密地团结起来，坚决地加以制止，使美国细菌战犯们受到应得的制裁。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会  一九五二年九月十六日
