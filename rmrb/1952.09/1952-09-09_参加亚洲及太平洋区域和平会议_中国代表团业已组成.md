Title: Chinese Delegation to Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference Formed
Author:
Date: 1952-09-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The China Peace Committee and various Chinese people's organizations held a joint meeting on the morning of the 8th and elected the Chinese delegation to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. The following attended the meeting: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairman, and members of the Standing Committee, the Chinese initiators of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, representatives of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, the All-China Students' Federation, the All-China Federation Of Literary And art circles , All-China Federation of Science Societies, All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, Chinese Medical Association, Preparatory Committee of All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Chinese Red Cross Society, Chinese People's Relief Association, National Committee for the Protection of Children, Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and representatives of democratic parties and people's organizations such as Situ Meitang, Li Siguang, Shen Yanbing, Xing Xiping, Shao Lizi, Ma Xulun, Ma Yinchu, Zhang Xiruo, Xu Deheng, Liang Xi, Huang Yanpei, Jiang Nanxiang, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Ningyi, Luo Longji, Cai Tingkai, Li Dequan, Xu Guangping, Zhu Xuefan, Sha Kofu, Ding Xielin, Zheng Zhenduo, Qian Junrui, Kang Keqing, Ji Chaoding, Wu Yunfu, Zhu Zaoguan, Fu Lianzhang, Yu Huanzeng, Hou Debang, Guan RuoLuan, etc.
    The joint meeting was presided over by Guo Moruo. After announcing the significance of the meeting, Guo Moruo said: The Preparatory Committee of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference has determined that the number of Chinese representatives attending the meeting should not exceed 30. It is hoped that the joint meeting will elect the Chinese delegation on a broad basis. Subsequently, the joint meeting elected 30 people with extremely broad representation to form a delegation (list to be issued separately), including the Chinese initiators of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, trade union workers, women representatives, youth representatives, religious circles, industrial and commercial circles, the press, film circles, medical circles, scientists, writers, professors, economists, representatives of ethnic minorities, etc.
    Liu Ningyi, one of the Chinese initiators and Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, reported on the progress of the preparations at the meeting. Liu Ningyi said: Since the Preparatory Meeting of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference was held in June this year, the peace movement in various countries in the Asian and Pacific Regions has made very significant progress. The people of all countries have increasingly strengthened their enthusiasm for opposing war and defending peace. Up to now, preparatory work has been launched in 32 countries in the Asian and Pacific Regions, and 31 countries have elected representatives or delegations to attend the peace conference. These delegations have extremely broad representation. The Chinese people warmly welcome the convening of the peace conference. People in many places have launched production increase competitions and various construction works to welcome the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference.

### 参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  中国代表团业已组成

1952-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    中国代表团业已组成
    【新华社八日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国各人民团体于八日上午举行联席会议，选出了参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的中国代表团。出席会议的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，副主席陈叔通及常务委员会委员，亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的中国发起人，中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中国新民主主义青年团、中华全国民主青年联合总会、中华全国学生联合会、中华全国文学艺术界联合会、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、中华全国科学技术普及协会、中华医学会、中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会、中苏友好协会总会、中国红十字会总会、中国人民救济总会、中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会、中国人民外交学会、中国国际贸易促进委员会等各民主党派和人民团体代表司徒美堂、李四光、沈雁冰、邢西萍、邵力子、马叙伦、马寅初、张奚若、许德珩、梁希、黄炎培、蒋南翔、廖承志、刘宁一、罗隆基、蔡廷锴、李德全、许广平、朱学范、沙可夫、丁燮林、郑振铎、钱俊瑞、康克清、冀朝鼎、伍云甫、朱早观、傅连暲、俞寰澄、侯德榜、关若鸾等。
    联席会议由郭沫若主持。郭沫若宣布开会意义后说：亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会业已确定出席会议的中国代表名额不超过三十人，希望联席会议在广泛的基础上选举中国代表团。接着，联席会议选出了代表性极为广泛的三十位人士组成代表团（名单另发），其中包括亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的中国发起人、工会工作者、妇女代表、青年代表、宗教界、工商界、新闻界、电影界、医学界、科学家、作家、教授、经济学家、少数民族代表等。
    中国发起人之一、亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会秘书长刘宁一在会上报告了筹备工作情况。刘宁一说：自从今年六月亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议召开之后，亚洲及太平洋区域各国的和平运动有了极显著的发展。各国人民日益增强了反对战争、保卫和平的积极性。截至目前为止，筹备工作已在亚洲及太平洋区域的三十二个国家中展开，有三十一个国家已经选出了出席和平会议的代表或代表团。这些代表团都有极广泛的代表性。中国人民热烈地欢迎和平会议的召开，许多地方的人民已经展开了迎接亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的增产竞赛及各项建设工作。
