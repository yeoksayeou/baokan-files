Title: Central Agricultural Minister Li Shucheng Hosts Banquet for Burmese Land Reform Delegation
Author:
Date: 1952-09-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: Central People's Government's Minister of Agriculture Li Shucheng hosted a banquet on the evening of the 8th to welcome the Burmese Land Reform Delegation.

Those present included: Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Agriculture Yang Xiandong, Vice Minister of Water Resources Zhang Hanying, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives Meng Yongqian, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Zheng Zhenduo, Vice President Qian Weichang, Head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation to Burma Ding Xilin, and Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Zhang Youyu, among others.

Also invited were: President of Beijing Agricultural University Sun Xiaocun, Professor Dai Fanglan, and agricultural experts Huang Ruilun and Li Kezu.

The Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, and diplomatic personnel from the Burmese Embassy were also invited to the banquet.

Minister Li Shucheng delivered a welcoming speech at the banquet. He expressed a warm welcome to the Burmese Land Reform Delegation. He said: In May of this year, the Burmese Cultural Delegation visited China, and now the Burmese Land Reform Delegation is visiting China again, which marks a further development of the friendly relations between the Chinese and Burmese peoples. This enhanced friendship between the Chinese and Burmese peoples will be beneficial to the cause of peace in Asia.

The head of the Burmese Land Reform Delegation, Tekin Zin, and the deputy head, U Ba Tai, delivered speeches successively. They expressed their pleasure at the Chinese government's acceptance of their request to visit China and observe land reform in rural areas, and expressed their gratitude for the warm welcome they had received.

The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 中央农业部李书城部长  欢宴缅甸土地改革参观团

1952-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中央农业部李书城部长
    欢宴缅甸土地改革参观团
    【新华社八日讯】中央人民政府农业部部长李书城在八日晚设宴欢迎缅甸土地改革参观团。
    出席作陪的有：外交部副部长章汉夫、农业部副部长杨显东，水利部副部长张含英、中华全国合作社联合总社副主任孟用潜、中缅友好协会会长郑振铎、副会长钱伟长、我国前赴缅甸文化代表团团长丁西林和北京市人民政府副市长张友渔等。
    应邀作陪的还有：北京农业大学校长孙晓村、教授戴芳澜、农业专家黄瑞纶、李克佐。
    缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂和缅甸大使馆外交人员，也应邀参加宴会。
    李书城部长在宴会上首先致欢迎词。他对缅甸土地改革参观团的来临表示热烈的欢迎。他说：今年五月间，缅甸文化代表团曾来中国访问，现在缅甸土地改革参观团又来中国参观，这标志着中缅两国人民的友好关系进一步的发展。这种中缅两国人民友谊的增进，将有利于亚洲的和平事业。
    缅甸土地改革参观团团长德钦阵、副团长宇巴太相继致词。他们表示：对中国政府接受他们来中国参观土地改革后的农村的要求感到愉快，并对所受到的热烈欢迎表示感谢。
    宴会在友好的气氛中进行。
