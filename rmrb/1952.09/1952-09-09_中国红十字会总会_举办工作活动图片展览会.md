Title: China Red Cross Society Headquarters Holds Photo Exhibition of Work Activities
Author:
Date: 1952-09-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 7th: To commemorate the second anniversary of its reorganization on September 8th, the China Red Cross Society Headquarters held the opening ceremony of the "China Red Cross Society Work Activities Photo Exhibition" at Beihai Park in Beijing on the 7th. Among those who attended the opening ceremony were Gong Pusheng, Deputy Director of the International Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Cao Mengjun, representative of the All-China Women's Federation, Wu Yunfu, Secretary-General of the China People's Relief Administration, Fang Shishan of the Chinese Medical Association, and representatives from various agencies and organizations, totaling approximately 200 people. President Li Dequan first spoke at the conference, briefly introducing the contents of the exhibition. Subsequently, He Cheng, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government, cut the ribbon. Dr. Pulohalov, Director of the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital, along with Deputy Directors Li Huansan and Dai Zhengqi, and all the staff of the hospital, visited the exhibition.

The exhibition includes six parts: "Defending World Peace," "International Assistance," "Domestic Medical and Prevention Service Teams," "Medical Prevention Work of Various Branches," "Membership Development and First Aid Training," and "Introduction to the Red Cross Societies of the Soviet Union and Various People's Democratic Countries." These photos focus on introducing the work activities of the China Red Cross Society over the past two years and the various efforts made by the China Red Cross Society to defend world peace and uphold humanitarianism.

The "Introduction to the Red Cross Societies of the Soviet Union and Various People's Democratic Countries" section features photos of Red Cross organizations in various countries establishing first aid stations, health stations, training health workers, organizing Red Cross youth, conducting blood transfusion work, and working in factories, mines, farms, schools, residential areas, and other places. These vividly illustrate the organization and training of working people to assist government health agencies in serving their own health. These very meaningful photos also demonstrate the development prospects of the China Red Cross Society.

### 中国红十字会总会  举办工作活动图片展览会

1952-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中国红十字会总会
    举办工作活动图片展览会
    【新华社七日讯】中国红十字会总会为纪念该会九月八日改组二周年纪念日举办的“中国红十字会工作活动图片展览会”，七日在北京北海公园举行开幕式。参加开幕仪式的有中央人民政府外交部国际司副司长龚普生、中华全国妇女联合会代表曹孟君、中国人民救济总会秘书长伍云甫、中华医学会方石珊及各机关团体代表共约二百余人。大会首由李德全会长讲话，她扼要地介绍了展览会的内容。继由中央人民政府卫生部贺诚副部长剪彩。北京苏联红十字医院院长甫罗哈洛夫、副院长李幻三、戴正启及该院全体工作人员都到展览会来参观。
    展览会内容有：“保卫世界和平”、“国际救助”、“国内医防服务队”、“各分会的医疗预防工作”、“发展会员与急救训练”、“苏联及各人民民主国家红十字会介绍”等六部分。这些图片重点地介绍了中国红十字会两年来工作活动的情况和中国红十字会为保卫世界和平与维护人道主义所作的各项努力。
    “苏联及各人民民主国家红十字会介绍”部分中各国红十字会组织急救站、卫生站、训练卫生员、组织红十字少年、输血工作以及在工厂、矿山、农场、学校、住宅区等地方工作的图片，具体地表现了组织与训练劳动人民协助政府卫生机构为他们自己的健康服务的情况。这些很有意义的图片，也显示了中国红十字会的发展前途。
