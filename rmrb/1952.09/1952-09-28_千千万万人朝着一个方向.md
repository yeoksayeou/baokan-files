Title: Thousands Upon Thousands of People Heading in One Direction
Author: Ai Qing
Date: 1952-09-28
Page: 3

    Thousands upon thousands of people heading in one direction
    Ai Qing
    Joyful songs fly everywhere,
    In the songs, there is beautiful hope—
    We sing the praises of our motherland's springtime,
    Life blossoms like flowers,
    Golden sunlight shines upon the earth,
    Songs resound throughout the fields and factories...
    A common belief guides us forward,
    Our steps are aligned, our will is firm,
    Thousands upon thousands of people heading in one direction:
    Defend world lasting peace!
    For the sake of a few who want to make a fortune,
    War merchants are destroying peace—
    Beautiful Korea is being ravaged,
    Countless lives bleed and sacrifice,
    Anger burns in our hearts,
    The heroic people persist in struggle!
    A common belief guides us forward,
    Our steps are aligned, our will is firm,
    Thousands upon thousands of people heading in one direction:
    Defend world lasting peace!
    Our ranks are so magnificent,
    People of all ethnicities unite into one force—
    Like countless rivers converging into the sea,
    The tide of peace surges and swells,
    Quickly extinguish the flames of war!
    Don't let the whole world suffer disaster.
    A common belief guides us forward,
    Our steps are aligned, our will is firm,
    Thousands upon thousands of people heading in one direction:
    Defend world lasting peace!

### 千千万万人朝着一个方向
艾青
1952-09-28
第3版()
专栏：

    千千万万人朝着一个方向
    艾青
    欢乐的歌声到处飞扬，
    歌声里有美好的希望——
    我们歌唱祖国的春天，
    生活像鲜花一样开放，
    金色的阳光照耀着大地，
    歌声响遍了田野和工厂……
    共同的信念引导我们前进，
    我们的步伐整齐，意志坚定，
    千千万万人朝着一个方向：
    保卫世界持久和平！
    为了少数人想发横财，
    战争贩子破坏了和平——
    美丽的朝鲜受到蹂躏，
    无数的生命流血牺牲，
    愤怒燃烧着我们的心，
    英雄的人民坚持着斗争！
    共同的信念引导我们前进，
    我们的步伐整齐，意志坚定，
    千千万万人朝着一个方向：
    保卫世界持久和平！
    我们的队伍多么雄壮，
    各族人民结成一个力量——
    像无数江河汇成大海，
    和平的浪潮汹涌澎湃，
    快快扑灭战争的火焰！
    不让整个世界遭受灾殃。
    共同的信念引导我们前进，
    我们的步伐整齐，意志坚定，
    千千万万人朝着一个方向：
    保卫世界持久和平！
