Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 150th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-09-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 12th — The 150th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 12th. The meeting listened to and approved the main points of the 1952 work plan of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, reported by President Guo Moruo. The meeting also approved several cases of appointments and removals and cases submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第一五○次会议

1952-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一五○次会议
    【新华社十二日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百五十次政务会议在十二日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中国科学院郭沫若院长报告的该院一九五二年工作计划要点。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
