    Commander-in-Chief Zhu Sends Congratulatory Telegram for Hungarian People's Army Day
    [This newspaper reports] September 29th is Hungarian People's Army Day. Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, sent a telegram to Mihály Farkas, Minister of National Defense of the Hungarian People's Republic, on the 28th, expressing congratulations. The original telegram is as follows:
    Comrade Mihály Farkas, Minister of National Defense of the Hungarian People's Republic:
    On the occasion of the founding day of the Hungarian People's Army, I would like to extend brotherly and warm congratulations to you and all commanders and fighters of the Hungarian People's Army on behalf of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and myself.
    May the friendship between China and Hungary become even stronger in the cause of defending world peace.
    Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Beijing, September 28, 1952

### 朱总司令电贺匈牙利人民军节日

1952-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    朱总司令电贺匈牙利人民军节日
    【本报讯】九月二十九日为匈牙利人民军的节日。中国人民解放军总司令朱德，于二十八日致电匈牙利人民共和国国防部部长米海依·法卡斯，表示祝贺。原电如下：匈牙利人民共和国国防部部长米海依·法卡斯同志：
    兹值匈牙利人民军建军节之际，谨以中国人民解放军和我个人名义，向您及匈牙利人民军全体指挥员、战斗员致兄弟般的热烈的祝贺。
    愿中匈两国友谊在保卫世界和平事业上更益坚强。
    中国人民解放军总司令朱德一九五二年九月二十八日于北京
