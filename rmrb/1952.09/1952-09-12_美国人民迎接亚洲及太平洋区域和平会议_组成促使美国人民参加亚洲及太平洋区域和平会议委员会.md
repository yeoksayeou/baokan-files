Title: American People Welcome the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference; Committee Formed to Promote American Participation in the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-09-12
Page: 4
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 11th: The New York "Daily Worker" reported on September 5th: Prominent American figures have formed a committee to promote American participation in the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference. The partial list of committee members is as follows: Brison, Du Bois, Fast, Forbes, Heaney, Conn, Kent, Kingsbury, Ms. Pratt, Uphaus, Richardson, Robertson, Roberts, and Lu Maude. The committee declared: "Our aim is to make the American people understand the purpose of this conference and to interest representatives from all walks of life in organizing an American delegation to attend this conference."
　　
　　The "Daily Worker" also said: The committee to promote American participation in the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference will hold a "New York-Beijing Peace Rally" on September 25th at the Casino Nightclub in downtown New York City, which will "express the desire of the vast majority of the American people for an immediate realization of peace in Korea."

### 美国人民迎接亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  组成促使美国人民参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议委员会

1952-09-12
第4版()
专栏：

　  美国人民迎接亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    组成促使美国人民参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议委员会
    【新华社布拉格十一日电】纽约“工人日报”九月五日消息：美国著名人士已组成促使美国人民参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议委员会。委员会的部分委员名单如下：勃里逊、杜波依斯、法斯特、福贝斯、奚恩、康恩、肯特、金斯伯利、普瑞特女士、厄普豪斯、李查逊、罗伯逊、罗伯兹和陆茂德。委员会宣称：“我们的目的是使美国人民了解这次会议的宗旨，使各行各业的代表人物对于组织美国代表团出席这次会议发生兴趣。”
    “工人日报”又说：促使美国人民参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议委员会，将在九月二十五日在纽约市中心卡西诺夜总会举行“纽约—北京和平集会”，集会将“表达美国最大多数人民要求立刻在朝鲜实现和平的愿望”。
