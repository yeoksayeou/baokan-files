Title: Northwest Military and Political Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram on Early Completion of Track Laying for the Tianshui-Lanzhou Railway
Author:
Date: 1952-09-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, September 1st. The Northwest Military and Political Committee sent a telegram on August 31st to all employees of the Northwest Railway Trunk Line Engineering Bureau, congratulating them on the early completion of the Tianshui-Lanzhou Railway track laying project. The original telegram is as follows:
To Comrade Wang Shitai, Director of the Northwest Railway Trunk Line Engineering Bureau, and all comrades:

    The Tianshui-Lanzhou Railway, which the people of the entire Northwest have longed for for many years, has, in the two years since the national liberation, been completed ahead of schedule due to the correct leadership of the Central People's Government and your highly developed spirit of patriotism and selfless, arduous efforts. This is another great victory for the people of New China under the leadership of Chairman Mao, and it will have a significant impact on the economic, political, and cultural construction undertakings of the entire country, especially the Northwest. The people of the whole country, especially the people of the Northwest, are all excited and encouraged. We extend to you our warm congratulations and cordial greetings. It is hoped that you will consolidate your achievements, summarize your experiences, guard against arrogance and impetuosity, continue to move forward, and strive for new and greater victories.

Northwest Military and Political Committee, August 31st

### 西北军政委员会电贺天兰路提前完成铺轨

1952-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    西北军政委员会电贺天兰路提前完成铺轨
    【新华社西安一日电】西北军政委员会在八月三十一日致电西北铁路干线工程局全体职工，祝贺天兰铁路提前完成铺轨工程。原电如下：西北铁路干线工程局王世泰局长及全体职工同志们：
    全西北人民渴望多年的天兰铁路，在全国解放后两年多来，由于中央人民政府的正确领导，由于你们高度发扬了爱国主义精神，奋不顾身地进行了辛勤艰苦的努力，现在已提前完成铺轨工程。这是新中国人民在毛主席领导下又一个巨大的胜利，这对全国尤其是西北的经济、政治、文化各方面的建设事业，将发生重大的作用。全国人民尤其是西北人民，无不感到欢欣鼓舞。谨向你们致以热烈的祝贺和亲切的慰问。希望你们巩固成绩，总结经验，戒骄戒躁，继续前进，为争取新的更大的胜利而奋斗。
    西北军政委员会　八月三十一日
