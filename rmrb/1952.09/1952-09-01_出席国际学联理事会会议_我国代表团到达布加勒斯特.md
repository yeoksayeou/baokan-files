Title: Delegation from Our Country Arrives in Bucharest to Attend the International Union of Students Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-09-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 29th: According to the Romanian News Agency, the Chinese delegation, led by head of delegation Tian Demin, arrived in Bucharest on the 28th to attend the International Union of Students Council meeting to be held in Bucharest from September 5th to 11th. Accompanying the Chinese delegation were also Chinese athletes who will participate in competitions held during the Council meeting. Konya, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Working Youth League, and officials from the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Bucharest were present to welcome them. Smolenru, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Working Youth League, delivered a welcoming speech on behalf of the youth of Bucharest, saying that the youth of our country warmly welcome the representatives of the Chinese youth, from whose ranks emerged volunteers who sacrificed heroically on the Korean battlefield, creating an outstanding example of proletarian internationalism. Tian Demin delivered a reply speech on behalf of the Chinese students, expressing his gratitude for the warm welcome from the Romanian youth. He said that the Chinese student delegation is participating in the International Union of Students Council meeting to express the desire of all Chinese students to safeguard world peace and improve the lives of youth and students from all countries. The youth who came to welcome the Chinese student delegation paid warm respects to the beloved leader of the Chinese youth and the Chinese people, Mao Zedong.

### 出席国际学联理事会会议  我国代表团到达布加勒斯特

1952-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    出席国际学联理事会会议
    我国代表团到达布加勒斯特
    【新华社二十九日讯】据罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特讯：出席将在九月五日到十一日在布加勒斯特举行的国际学生联合会理事会会议的中国代表团，由团长田德民率领，二十八日抵达布加勒斯特。和中国代表团一起来的还有中国运动员们，他们将参加在理事会会议期间举行的比赛。罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟中央委员会书记柯尼亚和中华人民共和国驻布加勒斯特大使馆官员均前往欢迎。劳动青年联盟中央委员会书记处书记斯摩伦鲁代表布加勒斯特青年致欢迎辞，他说，我国青年热烈地欢迎中国青年的代表，从中国青年的队伍中产生了志愿军，他们在朝鲜战场上英勇牺牲，创造了无产阶级国际主义的出色范例。田德民代表中国学生致答辞，他对罗马尼亚青年的热烈欢迎表示感谢。他说，中国学生代表团参加国际学联理事会会议是为了表达全中国学生的维护全世界和平及改善各国青年及学生的生活的愿望。前来欢迎中国学生代表团的青年对中国青年和中国人民亲爱的领袖毛泽东热烈致敬。
