Title: Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Hosts Banquet for British-Chinese Friendship Association Visiting Delegation
Author:
Date: 1952-09-08
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, September 7th: The Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs hosted a banquet on the evening of September 7th to welcome the British-Chinese Friendship Association visiting delegation invited by the Institute. During the banquet, Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, delivered a welcoming speech. Guests at the banquet delivered impromptu speeches, expressing that the British-Chinese Friendship Association visiting delegation would bring news of the new achievements of the Chinese people's peaceful construction to the British people upon their return home. The banquet proceeded in a harmonious and friendly atmosphere.
    The British-Chinese Friendship Association visiting delegation consists of thirty-one foreign guests in total. Apart from the twenty-four who arrived in Beijing in two batches on September 4th and 5th, four more arrived on September 7th.

### 中国人民外交学会欢宴英中友协访问团

1952-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民外交学会欢宴英中友协访问团
    【新华社七日讯】中国人民外交学会七日晚设宴欢迎应该学会邀请来华的英中友好协会访问团。席间由中国人民外交学会会长张奚若致欢迎辞。宴会上来宾纷纷发表即席演说，表示英中友协访问团回国后将把中国人民和平建设的新成就的消息带给英国人民。宴会在和谐友好的气氛中进行。
    英中友好协会访问团全体外宾共三十一人，除九月四、五两日已有两批共二十四人抵北京外，九月七日续到四人。
