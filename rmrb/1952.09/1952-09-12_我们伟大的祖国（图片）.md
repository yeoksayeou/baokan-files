    Our Great Motherland
    The Jiangxi Paper Mill completed its machine installation task in just four months. The mill began trial operations on August 1st and was fully operational by mid-August, producing 30 tons of newsprint daily. This photo shows workers installing a digester for making pulp.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    江西造纸厂在短短的四个月内完成了机器安装的任务。该厂已于八月一日试车，八月中旬全部开工，每日可产新闻纸三十吨。这是工人正在安装一个作纸浆用的蒸球。
