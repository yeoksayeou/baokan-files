Title: The United States Forces the Japanese People to Sell Blood on a Large Scale
Author:
Date: 1952-09-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 22nd: The American occupiers' exploitation of the Japanese people has truly reached the stage of marrow extraction; they are forcing the Japanese people to sell their blood on a large scale.

The English newspaper "Japan News" said: "Blood has become Japan's newest export commodity." The value of blood shipped from Japan to the United States annually reaches $277,000, equivalent to 99.72 million yen. In Japan, incidents of forcing people to donate blood for wounded American soldiers in the Korean War are also occurring on a large scale.

### 美国迫使日本人民大量卖血

1952-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    美国迫使日本人民大量卖血
    【新华社二十二日讯】东京消息：美国占领者对日本人民的压榨真正到达了敲骨吸髓的阶段，他们正迫使日本人民大量出卖自己的血液。
    英文报纸“日本新闻”说：“血液已成为日本最新的出口货”。日本一年输往美国的血液的价值达二十七万七千美元，合九千九百七十二万日元。在日本，强迫人民为侵朝美军中的伤兵输血的事件，也是在大量发生的。
