    Our Great Motherland
    The Tianshui-Lanzhou Railway is scheduled to open to traffic on October 1st. The track-laying project for the entire 346-kilometer line was completed at 21:30 on August 23rd, extending to Lanzhou East Station. This is the grand scene when the first train from Tianshui arrived in Lanzhou.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    天兰铁路定于十月一日通车。全线三百四十六公里的铺轨工程，已于八月二十三日二十一时三十分铺至兰州东站。这是从天水开来的第一列火车抵达兰州时的盛况。
