    Our Great Motherland
    The "International Peace" Maternity and Child Health Hospital, established with the "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize awarded to Madame Soong Ching Ling, began its work on July 1st. This is a picture of Madame Soong Ching Ling visiting a mother and baby at the "International Peace" Maternity and Child Health Hospital.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    以宋庆龄先生光荣获得的“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金所举办的“国际和平”妇幼保健院，已于七月一日开始工作。这是宋庆龄先生在“国际和平”妇幼保健院探望一位产妇和婴儿的情形。
