Title: International Textile and Garment Workers Union to Hold Congress: Chinese Delegation Left Beijing Yesterday to Attend the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-09-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 9th: The International Textile and Garment Workers Union will hold its Second Congress in Berlin on October 1st. The Chinese delegation attending this meeting is composed of 19 people, including trade union workers and model workers from various regions, and left Beijing by train on the 9th, led by the head of the delegation, Chen Shaomin. Those who saw them off at the station included Lai Ruoyu, Secretary-General of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Li Zaiwen, Director of the General Office; Liu Ziju, Minister of Culture and Education; Yang Zhihua, Deputy Director of the Women Workers Department; Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Department; Qian Zhiguang and Chen Weiji, Vice Ministers of the Textile Industry Department; and Liu Che, Deputy Director of the Textile Workers Union Office, and dozens of others.

### 纺织服装工人工会国际开代表大会  我国代表团昨天离北京赴会

1952-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    纺织服装工人工会国际开代表大会
    我国代表团昨天离北京赴会
    【新华社九日讯】纺织服装工人工会国际十月一日在柏林召开第二次代表大会，出席这一会议的我国代表团由各地区工会工作者、劳动模范十九人组成，并已于九日在团长陈少敏率领下乘车离京赴会。到车站欢送的有中华全国总工会秘书长赖若愚、办公厅主任栗再温、文教部长刘子久、女工部副部长杨之华、国际部副部长金子明、纺织工业部副部长钱之光、陈维稷及纺织工会办公室副主任刘澈等数十人。
