Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish Full Front Pages with News of the Sino-Soviet Communiqué, etc.
Author:
Date: 1952-09-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th: Moscow newspapers on the 16th all prominently published on the entire front page, with large characters, news of the Sino-Soviet Communiqué, notes, photographs of the signing ceremony, and Stalin's banquet in honor of the delegation of the Government of the People's Republic of China headed by Premier Zhou Enlai and the delegation of the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Tsedenbal.  The headline in *Pravda* and *Izvestia* was: "Long Live the Indestructible Friendship Between the People's Republic of China and the Soviet Union!" The headline in *Trud* was: "The Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China are Filled with Determination to Strive for the Further Consolidation and Development of Friendly Cooperation Between the Two Countries, While Striving to Safeguard and Consolidate Peace and International Security with All Their Might." The headline in *Red Star* was: "The Great Friendship between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China is a Reliable Guarantee Against the Threat of New Agression, a Powerful Bastion of Peace in the Far East and the World!" The headline in *Komsomolskaya Pravda* was: "Long Live the Indestructible Friendship Between the People's Republic of China and the Soviet Union – A Powerful Bastion of Peace in the Far East and the World!"

### 莫斯科各报首页全版刊载苏中公报等新闻

1952-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各报首页全版刊载苏中公报等新闻
    【新华社莫斯科十六日电】莫斯科各报十六日都以第一版全版地位大字显著登载苏中公报、照会、签字的照片和斯大林设宴招待以周恩来总理为首的中华人民共和国政府代表团和以蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔为首的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团的新闻。真理报和消息报报端标题为：“中华人民共和国和苏联之间牢不可破的友谊万岁！”劳动报报端标题为：“苏联和中华人民共和国充满着决心为进一步巩固和发展两国之间的友好合作而努力，同时全力维护和巩固和平与国际安全。”红星报报端标题为：“苏联和中华人民共和国之间的伟大友谊，是反对新侵略威胁的可靠保障，是远东和全世界和平的强大堡垒！”共青真理报报端标题为：“中华人民共和国和苏联的牢不可破的友谊——远东和全世界和平的强大堡垒万岁！”
