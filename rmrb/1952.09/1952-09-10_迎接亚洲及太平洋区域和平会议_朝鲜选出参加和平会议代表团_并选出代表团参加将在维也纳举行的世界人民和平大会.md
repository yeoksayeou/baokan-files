Title: Welcoming the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference: Korea Elects Delegation to Peace Conference and Delegation to World People's Peace Congress in Vienna
Author:
Date: 1952-09-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 9th. The Second Korean National Congress for the Defense of Peace was held in Pyongyang on the 8th, under the auspices of the Korean National Committee for the Support of Peace. More than 1,000 representatives from all walks of life across the Democratic People's Republic of Korea participated in the congress, including members of the Korean National Committee for the Support of Peace, members of the Korean Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference in Pyongyang, and representatives of people from all walks of life from across the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The representatives present included people from various democratic parties, social organizations, and workers, farmers, youth, students, women, educators, scientific workers, legal workers, medical workers, literary and art workers, industrial and commercial circles, and religious circles from all over the country, as well as social celebrities. After Zheng Sheng-yan, a member of the Presidium and the Korean National Committee for the Support of Peace, announced the opening of the congress, Han Xue-ye, Chairman of the Korean National Committee for the Support of Peace, delivered a report on "The Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference and the Current Tasks of Korean Peace Supporters." He briefly expounded on the significance and progress of the world peace movement and introduced the general situation of the peace movement of the people of various countries in the Asian and Pacific region. Han Xueye pointed out that the struggle of the Asian people against the US and British imperialist warmongers and for freedom, independence, and democracy is a struggle for peace and security in the whole world, and especially for peace and security in Asia. The heroic struggle of the Korean people to defend the freedom and independence of their motherland and resist the armed aggression of the US and British imperialists is a struggle for peace and security in the whole world, and especially for peace and security in Asia. Han Xueye then analyzed the current international situation, saying that the strength of peace supporters is much greater than the strength of warmongers, and that the Korean people's just struggle against aggression will surely achieve final victory with the active support of peace-loving people all over the world. Han Xueye called on all Korean people to follow the instructions of their leader, General Kim Il-sung, to be vigilant against any provocative conspiracies by the US aggressors, to strive to achieve a Korean armistice on a fair and reasonable basis, to fight to the end to consolidate peace in Asia and the world, and called on all Korean people to fully recognize the great significance of the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference to be held in Beijing, to actively welcome it with practical actions, to elect their own delegation to participate in the conference, and to contribute all their strength to strive for lasting peace in Asia.
    After the report, the representatives had a lively discussion. Those who spoke successively included Kang Liang-wook, Chairman of the North Korean Christian Democratic League, Li Huan-qi, Vice-Chairman of the Korean Democratic Youth League, composer Li Mian-xiang, Kim Chang-jun, Chairman of the South Korean Christian Democratic League, Ryu Jung-hee, a representative of housewives in Pyongyang, Li Yong, a representative of health workers, Kim Gwi-seon, chief writer of the Korean Women's Magazine, and writer Kim Nam-cheon. They unanimously supported Han Xueye's report, agreed to send a delegation to Beijing to participate in the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference, and unanimously expressed their intention to welcome this peace conference of great historical significance in Asia with practical actions.
    Subsequently, the congress unanimously adopted the "Letter to the Peace-loving People of the World" and elected the Korean delegation to attend the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference and the Korean delegation to participate in the World People's Peace Congress to be held in Vienna in December this year. The congress also approved and re-elected twelve members of the Korean National Committee for the Support of Peace.

### 迎接亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  朝鲜选出参加和平会议代表团  并选出代表团参加将在维也纳举行的世界人民和平大会

1952-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    迎接亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    朝鲜选出参加和平会议代表团
    并选出代表团参加将在维也纳举行的世界人民和平大会
    【新华社平壤九日电】朝鲜全国第二届保卫和平大会在朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会主持下八日在平壤举行。参加大会的有朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会委员、亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议朝鲜筹备委员会在平壤的委员和来自朝鲜民主主义人民共和国全国各地的各阶层人民代表共一千多人。出席的代表中包括各民主政党、社会团体和全国各地的工人、农民、青年、学生、妇女、教育工作者、科学工作者、法律工作者、医务工作者、文艺工作者、工商业界和宗教界人士以及社会名流等。大会由主席团主席之一、朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会委员郑圣彦宣布开会后，朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会委员长韩雪野在会上做了关于“亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议与朝鲜和平拥护者的当前任务”的报告，他扼要地阐述了世界和平运动的意义和进展情形，并且介绍了亚洲及太平洋区域各国人民保卫和平运动的概况。韩雪野指出：亚洲人民为反对美、英帝国主义战争贩子和争取自由、独立、民主而进行的斗争，就是为了全世界的和平和安全，特别是为了亚洲的和平和安全的斗争。朝鲜人民为保卫自己祖国的自由、独立而抗击美、英帝国主义武装侵略者的英勇斗争，就是为了全世界的和平和安全，特别是为了亚洲的和平和安全的斗争。韩雪野接着分析了目前的国际局势，他说：和平拥护者的力量已比战争贩子的力量强大得多，朝鲜人民反侵略的正义斗争在全世界爱好和平人民的积极支援下，必将取得最后胜利。韩雪野号召全朝鲜人民遵循自己的领袖金日成将军的指示，对美国侵略者的任何挑衅阴谋提高警惕，争取在公平合理的基础上实现朝鲜停战，为巩固亚洲和世界和平斗争到底，并号召全朝鲜人民充分认识即将在北京召开的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的重大意义，以实际行动来积极迎接它，选出自己的代表团去参加会议，为争取亚洲的持久和平而贡献出自己的一切力量。
    报告完毕后，代表们进行热烈讨论。先后发言的有：北朝鲜基督教徒民主联盟委员长康良煜、朝鲜民主青年同盟副委员长李焕起、作曲家李冕相、南朝鲜基督教徒民主联盟委员长金昌俊、平壤市家庭妇女代表柳贞姬、保健工作者代表李容、朝鲜妇女杂志社主笔金贵善、作家金南天等。他们一致支持韩雪野的报告，赞成选派代表团前往北京参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议，并一致表示要以实际行动来迎接这一在亚洲有重大历史意义的和平会议。
    接着，大会一致通过了“告全世界爱好和平人民书”，选举了出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的朝鲜代表团和参加今年十二月在维也纳召开的世界人民和平大会的朝鲜代表团。大会并批准和补选了朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会委员十二人。
