Title: Philippine People's Liberation Army Launches Extensive Attacks
Author:
Date: 1952-09-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Manila, 4th: Around the anniversary of the Philippine revolution against Spanish rule on August 26th, the Philippine People's Liberation Army launched a series of attacks against the Quiritio puppet government's military police.

On the evening of August 20th, in an ambush in Pampanga province, the Philippine Liberation Army killed a Quiritio puppet army major-level police chief and wounded two policemen. On the same evening, in Capiz province, they attacked two city governments, eliminated a unit of the Quiritio puppet police, and seized a large amount of military supplies.

On August 28th, in Isabela province, less than six miles from a Quiritio puppet army garrison headquarters in Nueva Vizcaya, the Philippine Liberation Army killed and wounded eight Quiritio soldiers in an ambush, seized a truckload of military supplies, and burned a military vehicle.

On August 29th, thirty-five miles south of Manila, a team of Philippine Liberation Army ambushed a unit of Quiritio's Marines, wiping out a portion of them.

### 菲律宾人民解放军广泛出击

1952-09-06
第4版()
专栏：

    菲律宾人民解放军广泛出击
    【新华社四日讯】马尼拉消息：在八月二十六日菲律宾反对西班牙统治大革命纪念日的前后，菲律宾人民解放军向季里诺傀儡政府军警展开了一连串的出击。
    八月二十日晚，菲解放军在邦邦牙省的一场伏击战中，打死季里诺傀儡军少校级警察局长一名、击伤警察二名；同日晚，在加帛示省袭击了两个城市的市政府，消灭了季里诺傀儡警察一部，缴获了大量军用物资。
    八月二十八日，在怡沙迷拉省距离诺危连城季里诺傀儡军的一个驻军总部不到六英里的地方，菲解放军在一场伏击战中，打死、打伤季里诺军八人，缴获了满载一车的军用物资，焚毁军车一辆。
    八月二十九日，在马尼拉南方三十五里，菲解放军一队伏击季里诺的海军陆战队一队，歼灭其一部。
