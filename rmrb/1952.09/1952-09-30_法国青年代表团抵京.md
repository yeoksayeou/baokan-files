Title: French Youth Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-09-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Report: A French youth delegation, led by Raoul Calas, Secretary of the National Committee of the Republican Youth Union of France, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 29th for a visit to China. The delegation includes prominent French peace activist and Secretary of the National Committee of the French Women's Youth Union, Raymonde Dien; Secretary of the National Committee of the Heroic Youth Union, André Masset; Secretary of the National Committee of the Labor Sports Federation, Jean Quimier; Secretary of the Paris District Construction Workers' Union, Joseph Meunier; Secretary of the Seine Province Women's Youth Union, Rolande Bérigot; Secretary of the Seine Province Republican Youth Union, Henri Malpertu; Secretary of the Seine-et-Oise Province Women's Youth Union, Hélène Lucy; Member of the Paris District Steering Committee of the Labor Sports Federation, Georges Fontanilles; Member of the Seine Province Committee of the Women's Youth Union, René Aubassanèque; Secretary of the Nord Province Republican Youth Union, Albert Depauchelle; and Secretary of the Bouches-du-Rhône Province Republican Youth Union, Marcel Tassy, totaling eleven members.

Welcoming them at the airport were Li Chang, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League; Alternate Secretary Qu Tangliang; Qian Sanqiang, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth; Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Tian Demin, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation; and over two hundred youth representatives from various circles. After the delegates disembarked, Young Pioneers enthusiastically presented them with flowers.

### 法国青年代表团抵京

1952-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    法国青年代表团抵京
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：来我国访问的法国青年代表团，在团长、法国共和青年联盟全国委员会书记劳贝·谢尔培率领下，于二十九日乘飞机抵达北京。代表团员有杰出的法国和平战士、法国女青年联盟全国委员会书记雷蒙达·第安，英勇少年联盟全国委员会书记安德烈·马塞，运动与体育劳动联合会全国委员会书记约翰·基米埃，巴黎区建筑工会联合会书记约瑟·摩尼埃，女青年联盟塞纳省书记罗兰·贝里冈，共和青年联盟塞纳省书记亨利·马尔培，女青年联盟塞纳—瓦斯省书记埃里安·露西，运动与体育劳动联合会巴黎区指导委员会委员乔治·丰塔尼勒，女青年联盟塞纳省委员会委员勒内·奥巴萨内克，共和青年联盟诺尔省书记阿尔培·德波谢，共和青年联盟罗尼河口省书记马赛尔·塔西等十一人。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记李昌、候补书记区棠亮，中华全国民主青年联合总会副主席钱三强，中华全国民主妇女联合会国际工作部副部长廖梦醒，中华全国学生联合会主席田德民等，以及各界青年代表二百多人。代表们下机后，少年儿童队热烈地向代表们献花。
