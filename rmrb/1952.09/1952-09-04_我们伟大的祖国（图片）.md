Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-09-04
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The All-China Federation of Trade Unions has established a sanatorium for workers in Zhanqiao, the most beautiful scenic spot in Qingdao, to receive workers from all over the country in four phases each year. These are two female fine yarn workers from a state-owned cotton textile factory in Shanghai. They are overlooking the beautiful scenery of Qingdao on the veranda of the sanatorium.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中华全国总工会在青岛最优美的风景区——湛山设立了工人疗养所，每年分四期接收各地工人来休养。这是上海国营棉纺织厂的两位细纱女工。她们正在休养所的游廊上眺望青岛的美丽风景。
