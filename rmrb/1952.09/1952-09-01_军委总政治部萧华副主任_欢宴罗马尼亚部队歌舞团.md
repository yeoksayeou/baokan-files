Title: Military Commission General Political Department Deputy Director Xiao Hua Hosts Banquet for Romanian Army Song and Dance Troupe
Author:
Date: 1952-09-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 31st:

    Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, hosted a banquet on the evening of August 31st for all members of the Romanian People's Republic Army Song and Dance Troupe. Invited to accompany him were Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Zhu Zaoguan, Deputy Director, Yang Lisan, Minister of the General Logistics Department, Liu Zhijian, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the General Political Department, Chen Yi, Minister of the Ministry of Culture, Shen Yanbing, Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Culture, Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhou Yang, Deputy Minister of Culture, Ding Xilin. Also invited to accompany were Tian Han, Ouyang Yuqian, Zhang Geng, Cao Yu, Guang Weiran, Wu Xue, Ouyang Shanzun and military cultural and artistic workers Chen Qitong, Lan Ma, Cao Weimin, Gao Yubao, Qi Jianhua, Xin Fengxia, Gao Yuanjun and others. Romanian Ambassador to China, Rudenko, and his wife, and embassy officials were also invited to attend.
    The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 军委总政治部萧华副主任  欢宴罗马尼亚部队歌舞团

1952-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    军委总政治部萧华副主任
    欢宴罗马尼亚部队歌舞团
    【新华社三十一日讯】中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华，在八月三十一日晚欢宴罗马尼亚人民共和国部队歌舞团全体人员。应邀作陪的有人民革命军事委员会办公厅主任萧向荣、副主任朱早观，总后勤部部长杨立三，总政治部宣传部部长刘志坚、文化部部长陈沂及中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长伍修权，文化部副部长周扬、丁西林。应邀作陪的还有首都文艺界人士田汉、欧阳予倩、张庚、曹禺、光未然、吴雪、欧阳山尊和部队文化艺术工作者陈其通、蓝马、曹卫民、高玉宝、祁建华、新凤霞、高元钧等多人。罗马尼亚驻我国大使鲁登科及夫人和大使馆官员也应邀参加。
    宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
