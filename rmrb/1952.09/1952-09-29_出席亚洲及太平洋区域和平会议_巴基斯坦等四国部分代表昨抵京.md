Title: Representatives from Pakistan and Three Other Countries Attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference Arrived in Beijing Yesterday
Author: 
Date: 1952-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th—A portion of the representatives, totaling twenty-nine people, from Ecuador, Mexico, Pakistan, and Peru, who will be attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, arrived in Beijing by plane in succession on September 28th. Greeting them at the airport were: Qian Dadchanra, Deputy Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference; Zhang Bojun, member of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General; Li Dequan and Jin Zhonghua, Chinese representatives attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference; Cao Mengjun, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Hong Shen, Director of the Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, as well as figures from the cultural and religious circles. Also present at the airport to welcome them were representatives from India, Malaya, Pakistan, Thailand, and the Soviet Union attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference.

### 出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  巴基斯坦等四国部分代表昨抵京

1952-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    巴基斯坦等四国部分代表昨抵京
    【新华社二十八日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的厄瓜多尔、墨西哥、巴基斯坦和秘鲁四个国家一部分代表共二十九人，于二十八日乘飞机先后抵达北京。前往机场欢迎的有：亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会副秘书长钱德拉，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会委员章伯钧、秘书长刘贯一，出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的中国代表李德全、金仲华，中华全国民主妇女联合会副秘书长曹孟君，中华全国文学艺术界联合会联络部部长洪深及文化和宗教界人士等。到机场欢迎的还有印度、马来亚、巴基斯坦、泰国和苏联出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的代表。
