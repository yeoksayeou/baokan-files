Title: Romanian Army Song and Dance Ensemble Departs for Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1952-09-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency:
    The entire Romanian People's Republic Army Song and Dance Ensemble left Beijing on the evening of the 2nd for Nanjing, and will then proceed to Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Hankou and other places for performance tours. Zhou Weizhi, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture, Ouyang Yuqian, President of the Central Academy of Drama, and Romanian Ambassador to China, Rudenko, and many others went to the station to see them off.
    During its stay in Beijing, the song and dance ensemble performed musical and dance programs for more than 126,000 people from all walks of life in the capital, and received a warm welcome from the audience. The ensemble members visited scenic spots and historical sites and rural areas in the capital; and held enthusiastic and joyful gatherings with literary and art workers of the armed forces and the masses of farmers in the capital. The ensemble members also watched performances of Chinese films, dramas, Yue opera, song and dance, and acrobatics in Beijing.
    Li Chao, Deputy Director of the Drama Department of the Art Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, and others will accompany the song and dance ensemble to perform in various places.

### 罗马尼亚部队歌舞团赴南京

1952-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚部队歌舞团赴南京
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：罗马尼亚人民共和国部队歌舞团全体人员二日晚离京赴南京，再转上海、杭州、广州、汉口等地作访问演出。文化部办公厅副主任周巍峙，中央戏剧学院院长欧阳予倩和罗马尼亚驻我国大使鲁登科等多人曾到车站送行。
    歌舞团在京期间，曾先后向首都各界人民共十二万六千多人演出音乐舞蹈节目，得到观众的热烈欢迎。歌舞团人员在首都曾参观了名胜古迹和访问了农村；并曾分别和首都的部队文艺工作者及农民群众举行了热情和愉快的联欢会。歌舞团人员在京还参观了我国的电影、话剧、越剧、歌舞、杂技等演出。
    歌舞团赴各地演出，有中央人民政府文化部艺术事业管理局戏剧处副处长李超等陪同前往。
