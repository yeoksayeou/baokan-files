Title: Attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, All-India Sponsors' Meeting Selects Attending Representatives; Nepal Preparatory Committee Calls on People to Participate in Preparatory Work
Author:
Date: 1952-09-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 12th - The All-India Sponsors' Meeting of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference was held on September 9th at the New Delhi Municipal Hall. The presidium of the meeting was composed of Dr. Kitchlu, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, Dr. Chand, Secretary of the Indian Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, Chandra, Deputy Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, and Dr. Anand, a famous novelist. About thirty representatives attended.

	In his welcoming speech, Dr. Chand pointed out the current war crisis in Asia. He said that he believed that the unanimous voice of the good people of Asian and Pacific countries could eliminate the danger of war and lay the foundation for lasting peace in Asia and the world. He concluded by saying that the Indian people unanimously responded to the call of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference and were enthusiastically carrying out preparatory work throughout the country.

	Representatives from Madras, Andhra, Orissa, West Bengal, Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, Delhi, Gujarat, Saurashtra, and Bombay provinces (states) or regions successively reported on the progress of preparatory work in various places at the meeting. They pointed out that the enthusiasm of the masses in various places was unprecedented in the Indian peace movement. Peace conferences in various places, as well as meetings of other organizations dedicated to the cause of peace, have elected about 300 candidates for the Indian delegation to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. Widespread publicity in newspapers and magazines has made millions of men and women from all walks of life understand the significance of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. Judging from the news from various places, the Indian delegation participating in the peace conference to be held in Beijing will surely receive the support of the broad masses of the Indian people.

	Chandra presented a "Draft Instructions to the Indian Delegation to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference" at the meeting. He explained that the instructions in the draft are based on the resolutions passed by the All-India Peace Council and the peace conferences and peace committees of various provinces (states) and counties. He emphasized that India can and must contribute to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference.

	The All-India Sponsors' Meeting designated a committee composed of Kitchlu, Chand, Anand, Chandra, Janik, Chairman of the United Farmers Association, and one representative from each province (state) to select sixty representatives from the approximately 300 names received by the Indian Preparatory Committee to participate in the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference to be held in Beijing.
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 12th - The Nepal Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference recently issued a statement calling on Nepalese people at home and abroad to participate in the preparatory work for the peace conference to be held in Beijing. The statement also urged the people to pay attention to the necessity of maintaining peace in the event of a possible war.

	The chairman of the Nepal Preparatory Committee is Bahadur of the Nepal Youth League, and the committee has 27 members, including representatives of writers, artists, poets, professors, traders, students, and youth.

### 参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  全印赞助人会议遴选出席代表  尼泊尔筹备委员会号召人民参加筹备工作

1952-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    全印赞助人会议遴选出席代表
    尼泊尔筹备委员会号召人民参加筹备工作
    【新华社新德里十二日电】亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议全印赞助人会议于九月九日在新德里市政厅举行。会议主席团由全印和平理事会主席克其鲁博士、亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议印度筹备委员会秘书昌德博士、亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会副秘书长钱德拉及著名小说家安纳德博士四人组成。到会的约有三十位代表。
    昌德博士在欢迎词中指出了目前亚洲的战争危机。他说，他相信亚洲及太平洋区域各国的善良人民的一致呼声能够消除战争危险，为亚洲与世界的持久和平打下基础。他在结语中说，印度人民一致响应亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的号召，在全国各地热烈进行筹备工作。
    马德拉斯、安得拉、奥里萨、西孟加拉、比哈尔、北方省、德里、古吉拉特、苏拉史特拉和孟买等省（邦）或地区的代表相继在会上报告各地筹备工作进行的情况。他们指出各地群众响应的热忱是印度和平运动中从来没有见过的。各地和平会议以及致力于和平事业的其他组织的会议，已选出印度出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议代表团候选人约三百名。报章杂志的广泛宣传已使千千万万的各界男女了解亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的意义。从各地的消息看来，参加即将在北京举行的和平会议的印度代表团一定能够得到广大印度人民的支持。
    钱德拉在会上提出了“给参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的印度代表团的指示草案”。他解释说，草案里面的指示，是以全印和平理事会和各省（邦）、县和平会议与和平委员会通过的决议为根据的。他强调说，印度能够而且必须对亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议有所贡献。
    全印赞助人会议指定由克其鲁、昌德、安纳德、钱德拉、联合农民协会主席雅尼克和每省（邦）一个代表组成一个委员会，从印度筹备委员会所接到的约三百人的名字中遴选出六十位代表，参加即将在北京召开的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。 【新华社新德里十二日电】亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议尼泊尔筹备委员会最近发表声明，号召国内外的尼泊尔人参加即将在北京举行的和平会议的筹备工作。声明中并促请人民注意在战争可能发生的情况下保持和平的必要性。
    尼泊尔筹备委员会主席是尼泊尔青年联盟的巴哈杜尔，委员二十七人，其中包括作家、艺术家、诗人、教授、贸易商、学生和青年等的代表。
