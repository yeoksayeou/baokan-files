Title: Two Indian Representatives Arrive in Guangzhou
Author: 
Date: 1952-09-25
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 23rd: Two Indian representatives, including Nahar Lakhanpal Padhak, who will attend the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, arrived in Guangzhou on the afternoon of the 23rd. Ma Hao and He Ming, members of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and representatives from all walks of life, numbering in the dozens, went to the station to welcome them.

### 印度代表二人抵广州

1952-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

    印度代表二人抵广州
    【新华社广州二十三日电】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的印度代表纳赫·拉克汉诺齐·帕达克等二人于二十三日下午到达广州。前往车站欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广州分会委员马皓、何明及各界代表数十人。
