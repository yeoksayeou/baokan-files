Title: Malayan Overseas Chinese Unjustifiably Expelled by British Colonial Authorities Arrive in Guangzhou Again
Author:
Date: 1952-09-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou Telegram: The nineteenth group of 650 Overseas Chinese, including more than 200 children, who were unjustifiably expelled by the British colonial authorities in Malaya, arrived in Guangzhou on the 25th. These refugee overseas Chinese were rubber tappers and farmers who had been imprisoned for a long time in concentration camps in Ipoh, Johor Bahru, Kluang, and Ban Shan Ba before being deported. They were warmly received by relevant overseas Chinese affairs agencies upon their arrival in Guangzhou.

### 被英国殖民当局无理驱逐出境  马来亚华侨又一批返抵广州

1952-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    被英国殖民当局无理驱逐出境
    马来亚华侨又一批返抵广州
    【新华社广州电】被马来亚英国殖民当局无理驱逐出境的第十九批华侨六百五十人，在二十五日到达广州，其中有儿童二百多人。这批难侨都是割胶工人和农民，他们在被逐离境前，曾长期被囚禁在怡保、新山、居銮、半山吧等地集中营中。他们抵达广州时受到有关侨务机关的热情接待。
