Title: National Swimming Competition Successfully Concludes in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1952-09-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Report: The 1952 National Swimming Competition successfully concluded in Guangzhou on the afternoon of the 18th.

The closing ceremony officially began at 5:30 PM. Athletes from seven delegations, representing various administrative regions, the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the Chinese Railway Workers' Union, along with two specially invited overseas Chinese representatives, entered the venue in their colorful swimsuits to the tune of majestic military music. Over 20,000 spectators in the stands on both sides cheered for these brave, healthy, and energetic athletes. Zhu Guang, Deputy Director of the Organizing Committee of the大会, delivered the closing address. He, along with Wang Yanshi, Deputy Minister of the Cultural Department of the Central and Southern Military Region and the Fourth Field Army Political Department, and Ma John, Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, presented awards on behalf of the大会 to outstanding units and individuals. The winning units included the North China delegation, the East China delegation, the Chinese People's Liberation Army delegation, and the Central and Southern China delegation, which had the highest team scores. Individual winners included Mu Xiangxiong, Ye Ziyou, Mu Xianghao, and Yu Shunyuan for highest male scores; and Jin Yunpei, Zheng Sufei, Dong Li, and Lin Meiling for highest female scores. Also awarded were Zheng Sufei, who set a new national record in the women's 200-meter breaststroke (3 minutes 33.4 seconds); Mu Xiangxiong, who set a new national record in the men's 200-meter breaststroke (2 minutes 54.8 seconds); and the People's Liberation Army delegation (10 minutes 43.8 seconds) and the North China delegation, which set new national records in the men's 800-meter freestyle relay. Also receiving awards were Guangzhou Zhujiang Middle School and the Chinese Railway Workers' Union delegation, among other units that participated in the water performance. The Northeast China delegation, the Southwest China delegation, the Chinese Railway Workers' Union delegation, and the specially invited overseas Chinese representatives also received commemorative awards from the大会.

### 全国游泳比赛大会已在广州胜利闭幕

1952-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    全国游泳比赛大会已在广州胜利闭幕
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：一九五二年全国游泳比赛大会，十八日下午在广州胜利闭幕。
    闭幕典礼在下午五时三十分正式开始，穿着各种彩色游泳服的各大行政区和中国人民解放军、中国铁路工会等七个单位的代表队的运动员和两个特邀华侨代表在雄壮的军乐声中进入会场。在两边看台上的两万多观众对这些英勇健康、生气勃勃的选手们响起了一片欢呼。大会筹备委员会副主任委员朱光向大会致闭幕词，他并与中南军区、第四野战军政治部文化部副部长王燕士、中华全国体育总会副主席马约翰代表大会给优秀的单位和个人颁发了奖品。获得奖品的有团体得分最多的华北区代表队、华东区代表队、中国人民解放军代表队和中南区代表队。个人获奖的有：男子得分最多的穆祥雄、叶子由、穆祥豪和俞顺源；女子得分最多的金芸培、郑素绯、董琍和林美玲。得奖的还有创造女子二百公尺俯泳全国新纪录的郑素绯（成绩为三分三十三秒四），创造男子二百公尺俯泳全国新纪录的穆祥雄
    （成绩为二分五十四秒八）和创造男子八百公尺自由式接力全国新纪录的解放军代表队（成绩为十分四十三秒八）和华北区代表队。获得奖品的还有参加水上表演的广州珠江中学和中国铁路工会代表队等单位。东北区、西南区、中国铁路工会的代表队和特邀华侨代表也得到大会颁发的纪念奖品。
