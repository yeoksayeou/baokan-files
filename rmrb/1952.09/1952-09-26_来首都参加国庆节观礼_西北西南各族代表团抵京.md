Title: Northwest and Southwest Ethnic Group Delegations Arrive in Beijing to Attend National Day Observances
Author:
Date: 1952-09-26
Page: 1

    To Attend National Day Observances in the Capital
    Northwest and Southwest Ethnic Group Delegations Arrive in Beijing
    [This newspaper] According to Xinhua News Agency: The Northwest Ethnic Groups National Day Observance Delegation, a group of 45 people led by head Ma Yinu'er (female, Uyghur), deputy heads Ma Guohan (Dongxiang) and Chen Jiangbule (Mongolian), arrived in Beijing on the 23rd. The delegation includes fifteen ethnic groups: Han, Hui, Uyghur, Kazakh, Mongolian, Tibetan, Dongxiang, Uzbek, Manchu, Tatar, Solon, Kyrgyz, Tajik, Xibe, and Russian.
    [Further Dispatch] The Southwest Ethnic Groups National Day Observance Delegation, a group of 72 people led by head Sun Yuting, arrived in Beijing on the 25th. The delegation includes nineteen ethnic groups: Miao, Yi, Hui, Tibetan, etc.

### 来首都参加国庆节观礼  西北西南各族代表团抵京

1952-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    来首都参加国庆节观礼
    西北西南各族代表团抵京
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：西北各民族国庆节观礼代表团一行四十五人在团长马依努尔（女、维吾尔族）、副团长马国汉（东乡族）、陈江布勒（蒙族）率领下，于二十三日到达北京。代表团包括汉、回、维吾尔、哈萨克、蒙、藏、东乡、乌兹别克、满、塔塔尔、索伦、柯尔克兹、塔吉克、锡伯、俄罗斯等十五个民族成分。
    【又讯】西南各民族国庆节观礼代表团一行七十二人在团长孙雨亭率领下，于二十五日到达北京。代表团包括苗、彝、回、藏等十九个民族。
