Title: Korean "People" Magazine Publishes Mao Zedong's Work "On Contradiction"
Author:
Date: 1952-09-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 9th: Chairman Mao Zedong's work "On Contradiction" has been translated into Korean by the "People" magazine here and published in full in its latest issue. It has been warmly welcomed by the Korean people. The Korean translation of Chairman Mao's other philosophical work, "On Practice," was published in full in the January 1952 issue of the magazine. Later, at the request of readers, the "People" magazine combined the Korean translations of "On Practice" and "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship" and published them as a special collection.

### 朝鲜“人民”杂志  刊载毛主席著作“矛盾论”

1952-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜“人民”杂志
    刊载毛主席著作“矛盾论”
    【新华社平壤九日电】毛泽东主席的著作“矛盾论”，已由此间“人民”杂志译成朝鲜文，在该刊最近一期上全文登载。受到了朝鲜人民的热烈欢迎。毛主席的另一哲学著作“实践论”的朝鲜译文，曾在该刊一九五二年一月号上全文刊载。“人民”杂志社以后根据读者的要求，将“实践论”和“论人民民主专政”的朝鲜译文合并刊印成专集发行。
