Title: My Minister of Foreign Trade, Ye Jizhuang, hosted a banquet to welcome the Ceylon trade delegation
Author:
Date: 1952-09-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. Minister of Foreign Trade, Ye Jizhuang of the Central People's Government, held a banquet on the evening of the 22nd to welcome the Ceylon trade delegation.

Those present at the banquet included: Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, Ji Chaoding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and Lu Xuzhang, Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation.

The banquet took place in a friendly atmosphere.

### 我对外贸易部部长叶季壮设宴欢迎锡兰贸易代表团

1952-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我对外贸易部部长叶季壮设宴欢迎锡兰贸易代表团
    【新华社二十二日讯】中央人民政府对外贸易部部长叶季壮于二十二日晚举行宴会，欢迎锡兰贸易代表团。
    出席作陪的有：中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎，中国进出口公司经理卢绪章等人。
    宴会在友好的气氛中进行。
