Title: Chinese Cultural and Educational Delegation Visits Poland
Author:
Date: 1952-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Gdansk, September 26th: According to the Polish Press Agency, the Cultural and Educational Delegation of the People's Republic of China, currently visiting the People's Republic of Poland, has arrived in the Polish coastal region to study the development of vocational schools and their working methods, and to visit the reconstruction work of the ancient city of Gdansk.

At the welcoming ceremony held in the coastal region, Wei Que, Deputy Minister of Education of the People's Republic of China, said that during the short period of their visit to Poland, the delegation had the opportunity to see the great achievements of the Polish people who are sparing no effort to fight for the development of their national country and the consolidation of world peace.

On the first day of their visit to the coastal region, the members of the delegation visited the Marine and Tropical Medicine Department of the Gdansk Medical Academy, as well as the historic district of Gdansk.

### 我文化教育考察团访问波兰

1952-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    我文化教育考察团访问波兰
    【新华社二十六日讯】据波兰通讯社革但斯克二十四日讯：正在访问波兰人民共和国的中华人民共和国文化教育考察团已抵达波兰海岸地区，以便考察职业学校的发展及其工作方法，并参观革但斯克古城的重建工作。
    在海岸地区举行的欢迎会上，中华人民共和国教育部副部长韦悫说，在访问波兰的短短一段时期内，考察团有机会看见不遗余力地为他们人民国家的发展和世界和平的巩固而进行斗争的波兰人民的伟大成就。
    在访问海岸地区的第一天，考察团的团员们参观了革但斯克医学研究院的海洋及热带药物，以及革但斯克市的古迹区。
