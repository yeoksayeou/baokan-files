Title: Metal Workers in Amagasaki, Hyogo Prefecture, Japan, Recommend Communist Party Member to Run for Election
Author:
Date: 1952-09-06
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, September 5th: According to a report in the "Akahata" newspaper, the Daido Steel Plate Company branch of the All-Japan Metal Workers' Union in Amagasaki, Hyogo Prefecture, held a struggle in August against layoffs and demanding summer allowances, and also called for trade with China. The branch also resolved to recommend Mr. Inoue Masao, the current Communist Party member of the House of Representatives originally elected from the second electoral district of Hyogo Prefecture, to continue to be their representative in the upcoming general election, to fight for the realization of Sino-Japanese trade and for peace and independence.

### 日本兵库县尼崎地方金属工人  推荐共产党议员参加竞选

1952-09-06
第4版()
专栏：

    日本兵库县尼崎地方金属工人
    推荐共产党议员参加竞选
    【新华社五日讯】东京消息：据“赤旗报”报道，兵库县尼崎地方的全日本金属工会大同钢板公司分会八月间举行了反对解雇、要求发给夏季津贴的斗争，并要求与中国进行贸易。该分会同时决议推荐原由兵库县第二选区选出的现任共产党众议员井之口政雄继续为他们的代表，参加这次大选，为实现中日贸易与争取和平、独立而斗争。
