Title: All-Burma Students' Union Congress Victoriously Concludes

Author: 
Date: 1952-09-20
Page: 4

    All-Burma Students' Union Congress Victoriously Concludes
    [Xinhua News Agency, 17th] Rangoon dispatch: The All-Burma Students' Union recently convened a national representative congress in early September. The congress formulated working principles for defending world peace, opposing imperialist aggression, and striving for democratic education and student unity. The congress also passed a number of important resolutions, including those opposing American imperialist aggression against Burma, interference in Burmese education, and supporting all resolutions of the World Peace Council. The congress also elected a new executive committee.
    Burmese students warmly welcomed the congratulatory message from the All-China Students' Federation to all Burmese students during the congress.

### 全缅学联代表大会胜利闭幕

1952-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

    全缅学联代表大会胜利闭幕
    【新华社十七日讯】仰光消息：全缅学生联合会曾在九月上旬召开全国代表大会。大会制订了保卫世界和平、反对帝国主义侵略和争取民主教育与学生团结的工作方针。大会还通过了反对美帝国主义侵略缅甸、干涉缅甸教育事业和支持世界和平理事会一切决议案等重要决议案多起。大会并选举了新的执行委员会。
    缅甸学生热烈欢迎中华全国学生联合会在大会举行期间给全缅甸学生的贺电。
