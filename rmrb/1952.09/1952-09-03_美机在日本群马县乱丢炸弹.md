Title: American Plane Randomly Drops Bombs in Gunma Prefecture, Japan
Author: 
Date: 1952-09-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, August 28th (via TASS): According to Tokyo reports, an American plane "accidentally" dropped several bombs on an elementary school and a nickel manufacturing factory in Gunma Prefecture on August 28th.

### 美机在日本群马县乱丢炸弹

1952-09-03
第4版()
专栏：

    美机在日本群马县乱丢炸弹
    【新华社讯】塔斯社上海二十八日电：据东京消息，一架美国飞机八月二十八日在群马县“偶然”向一个初等学校和制镍公司的一个工厂投了几枚炸弹。
