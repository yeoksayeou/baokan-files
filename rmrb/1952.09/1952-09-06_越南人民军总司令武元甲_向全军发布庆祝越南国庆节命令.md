Title: Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnam People's Army, Vo Nguyen Giap, Issues Order to the Entire Army to Celebrate the National Day of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1952-09-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, September 5th: According to Vietnam News Agency, Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnam People's Army, issued an order to the entire army of Vietnam on the occasion of the seventh anniversary of the August Revolution and the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

The order first expounds on the victorious course of the August Revolution and the current domestic and international situation. The order points out: Since the August Revolution, under the wise leadership of President Ho Chi Minh, the Party, and the People's Government, the Vietnamese army has always been with the people, resolutely fighting against the French colonialists and American interventionists, and has achieved glorious victories. In the seven years of the war of resistance, the Vietnamese army has continuously grown stronger and become a powerful people's army.

The order calls on all cadres and soldiers of the Vietnamese field army, local people's army, militia, and guerrillas to commemorate this historic festival and to fulfill President Ho Chi Minh's call by striving to complete the following tasks:

一、Intensify the study and promotion of the heroic fighting spirit of the August Revolution; always remember: the victory of the August Revolution was due to the leadership of the former Indochinese Communist Party and President Ho Chi Minh, the close unity of all the people of our country in the Viet Minh Front, the unity of mind and purpose of our military and civilians, the close coordination of the Vietnam People's Army with the military forces of our people throughout the country, and the major victory achieved by the Soviet Red Army in defeating Japanese fascism.

二、Consistently and unremittingly consolidate and expand the results of the ideological remolding movement, strive to learn tactics and techniques, and intensify the cultivation of the will to persevere in combat and the determination to kill the enemy.

三、The cadres and soldiers currently active in the North Vietnam plains, the Quang Binh, Quang Tri, and Thua Thien areas of central Vietnam, and the enemy's rear areas in South Vietnam, should thoroughly possess guerrilla warfare thinking, resolutely carry out struggles behind enemy lines, unite with other people, so as to advance guerrilla warfare, defend guerrilla base areas, and smash the enemy's conspiracies of "using war to support war" and "using Vietnamese to fight Vietnamese."

四、Implement President Ho Chi Minh's instructions: sincerely learn the virtues of diligence, thrift, integrity, and uprightness, thoroughly understand and implement the policies of the Party and the government, strictly follow the mass line, serve the people wholeheartedly, practice increasing production and practicing economy, implement the government's rural policies, assist in the collection of agricultural taxes and protect crops.

The order finally calls on all cadres and soldiers to actively launch competitions, strive to kill the enemy and make meritorious service, so as to annihilate a large number of enemy troops, achieve new victories, and effectively support the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference.

### 越南人民军总司令武元甲  向全军发布庆祝越南国庆节命令

1952-09-06
第4版()
专栏：

    越南人民军总司令武元甲
    向全军发布庆祝越南国庆节命令
    【新华社五日讯】据越南通讯社北越三日讯：越南人民军总司令武元甲在越南八月革命和越南民主共和国成立七周年纪念日，向越南全国军队发布命令。
    命令首先阐述越南八月革命的胜利经过和当前国内、国际形势。命令指出：自从八月革命以来，越南军队在胡志明主席、党和人民政府的英明领导下，一直和人民在一起，坚决地作战反抗法国殖民者和美国干涉者，并取得了光辉的胜利。在七年抗战中，越南军队已不断地壮大，成为一支强大的人民军。
    命令号召越南全体野战军、地方人民军、民兵和游击队的干部和战士，为纪念这一历史性的节日以及实现胡志明主席的号召，努力完成下列任务：
    一、加紧学习并发扬八月革命的英勇战斗的精神；经常记取：八月革命的胜利是由于前东洋共产党和胡志明主席的领导，由于我国全体人民在前越盟阵线中紧密团结，由于我国军民的一心一德，由于越南人民军与我国人民在全国各地军事力量的密切配合，以及由于苏联红军打败日本法西斯主义者而取得的重大胜利。
    二、始终不懈地巩固与扩大思想改造运动的成果，努力学习战术与技术，加紧锻炼坚持战斗的意志与杀敌的决心。
    三、目前在北越平原、中越南部广平、广治、承天地区以及南越各地的敌后区域活动的干部与战士，应该彻底具备游击战思想，坚决在敌人后方进行斗争，和其他人民团结一致，以便推进游击战，保卫游击根据地，粉碎敌人“以战养战”和“利用越南人打越南人”的阴谋。
    四、执行胡志明主席的训示：诚恳地学习勤、俭、廉、正的美德、彻底了解与执行党及政府的政策，严格遵循群众路线，全心全意为人民服务，实行增产节约，执行政府的农村政策，协助征收农业税与保护庄稼。
    命令最后号召全体干部与战士，积极展开竞赛，努力杀敌立功，以便大量歼灭敌军，获得新的胜利，并有效地支持亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。
