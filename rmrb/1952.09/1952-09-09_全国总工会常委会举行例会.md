Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Standing Committee Holds Regular Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-09-09
Page: 1
    [Reported by this newspaper]
    The All-China Federation of Trade Unions held its 101st Standing Committee regular meeting on September 8th, chaired by Vice Chairman Zhu Xuefan. The meeting approved the "Instructions on Strengthening the Production Increase and Economy Practice Movement to Welcome the Great Third National Day"; and a plan to convene a national work conference of trade unions in private enterprises. The meeting approved the establishment of a preparatory committee for the Sichuan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions through the merger of the trade unions of the four regions of Southern Sichuan, Northern Sichuan, Eastern Sichuan, and Western Sichuan, with Chen Gang, Wu Ziyu, and others responsible for the preparations. The meeting approved the establishment of a preparatory committee for the Anhui Provincial Federation of Trade Unions through the merger of the trade unions of Southern Anhui and Northern Anhui, with Hao Tongsheng and Zhang Shikai as the director and deputy director, respectively. The meeting also approved the appointment of Li Chang as the Minister of the Youth Workers Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 全国总工会常委会举行例会

1952-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    全国总工会常委会举行例会
    【本报讯】中华全国总工会于九月八日举行第一○一次常务委员会例会，由朱学范副主席主持。会议通过了关于加强增产节约运动迎接伟大的第三届国庆节的指示；关于召开全国私营企业工会工作会议的计划。会议通过川南、川北、川东、川西四地区工会合并成立四川省总工会筹备委员会，由陈刚、吴子瑜等负责筹备。会议通过皖南、皖北工会合并成立安徽省总工会筹备委员会，郝桐生、张式楷为正副主任。会议并通过李昌任中华全国总工会青工部部长。
