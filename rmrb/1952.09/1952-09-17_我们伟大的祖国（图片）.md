Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-09-17
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Our capital's newly built "Peace Hotel" was completed ahead of schedule on the 14th of this month to welcome the representatives of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. This eight-story building includes more than two hundred rooms and magnificent restaurants and living rooms. This is the exterior of the "Peace Hotel."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国首都新建的“和平宾馆”已于本月十四日提前完工，以迎接亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的代表们。这座大厦有八层楼，包括二百多个房间和宏丽的餐厅、客厅。这是“和平宾馆”的外貌。
