Title: Participating in the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference - All-Japan Peace Congress Passes Resolution on Composition of Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1952-09-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, September 11th: TASS Tokyo, September 11th dispatch, according to Japanese newspaper reports: The All-Japan Peace Congress was held in Tokyo on September 10th. More than two hundred representatives of political parties, prominent scientists, and leaders of the cultural world attended the congress.

　　The congress passed a resolution on the composition of the Japanese delegation to attend the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, and discussed issues related to the delegation's departure to attend the peace conference. The congress passed a resolution protesting the Yoshida government's decision not to issue passports to the Japanese representatives attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference.

### 参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  全日本和平大会通过出席代表团的组成

1952-09-12
第4版()
专栏：

    参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    全日本和平大会通过出席代表团的组成
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社东京十一日电，日本报纸消息：全日本和平大会九月十日在东京举行。出席大会的有二百多位政党代表、著名科学家和文化界领袖。
    大会通过了出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的日本代表团的组成，讨论了有关代表团出国参加和平会议的问题。大会通过一项决议，对吉田政府决定不发护照给出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的日本代表，提出抗议。
