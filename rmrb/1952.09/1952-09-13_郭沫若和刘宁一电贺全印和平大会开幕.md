Title: Guo Moruo and Liu Ningyi Send Telegrams to Congratulate the Opening of the All-India Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-09-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 12th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, sent a telegram to the All-India Peace Conference, scheduled to open on the 12th, expressing congratulations. The text of the telegram is as follows: To Dr. Kitchlew, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, and forwarded to the All-India Peace Conference:
    On the occasion of the opening of the All-India Peace Conference, please accept my heartfelt congratulations. The sincere efforts of the Indian people in the peace movement will further strengthen the cause of peace in Asia and the world.
                        Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, September 10th
    Xinhua News Agency, 12th: Liu Ningyi, Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference Preparatory Committee, sent a telegram to the All-India Peace Conference, scheduled to open on the 12th, expressing congratulations. The text of the telegram is as follows: To Dr. Kitchlew, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, and forwarded to the All-India Peace Conference:
    Warmly congratulate the All-India Peace Conference. The active efforts of the Indian people in the peace movement will surely achieve brilliant results.
    Liu Ningyi, Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference Preparatory Committee, September 10th

### 郭沫若和刘宁一电贺全印和平大会开幕

1952-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若和刘宁一电贺全印和平大会开幕
    【新华社十二日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会郭沫若主席，曾致电定于十二日开幕的全印度和平大会，表示祝贺。电文如下：全印和平理事会主席克其鲁博士并转全印度和平大会：
    在全印度和平大会开会之际，请接受我衷心的祝贺。印度人民在和平运动中的热诚努力，将进一步加强亚洲及全世界的和平事业。
                        中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席  郭沫若九月十日
    【新华社十二日讯】亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会秘书长刘宁一，曾致电定于十二日开幕的全印度和平大会，表示祝贺。电文如下：全印和平理事会主席克其鲁博士并转全印度和平大会：
    热烈祝贺全印度和平大会。印度人民在和平运动中的积极努力，必将获得辉煌成果。
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会  秘书 长刘宁 一九月十日
