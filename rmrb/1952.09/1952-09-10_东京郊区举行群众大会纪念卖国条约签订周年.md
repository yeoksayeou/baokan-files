Title: Suburban Tokyo Holds Mass Meeting to Commemorate Anniversary of Signing of Traitorous Treaty
Author:
Date: 1952-09-10
Page: 4
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, September 8th: A mass meeting attended by 5,000 people was held in Musashino, a suburb of Tokyo, on September 7th to commemorate the anniversary of Japan's Day of National Shame - the signing of the San Francisco Treaty. This meeting was convened by the Japanese Communist Party, the Labor-Farmer Party, the Japan Farmers' Association, and other democratic organizations.

### 东京郊区举行群众大会纪念卖国条约签订周年

1952-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

    东京郊区举行群众大会纪念卖国条约签订周年
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社东京八日讯：九月七日，在东京郊区武藏野举行了一次有五千人参加的大会，纪念日本的国耻日——旧金山条约签订一周年。这个大会是由日本共产党、劳农党、日本农民协会及其他民主团体召开的。
