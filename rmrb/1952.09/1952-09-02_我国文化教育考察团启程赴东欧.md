Title: China's Cultural and Educational Delegation Departs for Eastern Europe
Author:
Date: 1952-09-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st -- The Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council, based on the cultural cooperation agreements signed between China and the People's Democratic countries of Eastern Europe, has organized a Cultural and Educational Delegation to visit the cultural and educational undertakings of the fraternal countries of Eastern Europe. The delegation, consisting of ten people led by the head of the delegation, Wei Que, and the deputy head of the delegation, Peng Kang, departed on the 1st.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Ma Xulun, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee; Fan Changjiang, Deputy Secretary-General; Zhou Yang, Deputy Minister of Culture; Qian Junrui, Deputy Minister of Education; Ding Zan, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Xue Gongchuo, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Health; Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Chen Zhongjing, Deputy Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations.

Also present at the airport to see them off were: Rudenko, Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to China; König, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to China with the rank of Ambassador; Sándor, Ambassador of the Hungarian People's Republic to China; Petkov, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China; Konsala, Ambassador of the Czechoslovak Republic to China; and Kirylok, Ambassador of the Polish People's Republic to China.

The delegation will first go to Poland and then to Germany, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria and other countries for investigation.

List of the Cultural and Educational Delegation:

	Head of the Delegation: Wei Que, Deputy Minister of Education of the Central People's Government.
	Deputy Head of the Delegation: Peng Kang, Member of the Shandong Provincial People's Government and Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee.
	Members:
		Ha Feng'a, Vice Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government and Minister of Culture and Education.
		Qu Zheng, Deputy Minister of Health of the Northwest Military and Political Committee and President of Lanzhou University.
		Dong Chuncai, Deputy Minister of Education of the Northeast People's Government and Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee.
		Yun Ziqiang, Deputy Director of the Compilation and Translation Bureau of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.
		Zhang Chao, Deputy Minister of the Organization Department of the National Committee of the Chinese Education Trade Union.
	Secretary: Zhao Xun, Vice President of Harbin Foreign Language College.
	Translator: Lu Shijia, Professor of Tsinghua University.
	Xinhua News Agency Reporter: Li Shenzhi, Deputy Director of the International Department of the New China News Agency.

### 我国文化教育考察团启程赴东欧

1952-09-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我国文化教育考察团启程赴东欧
    【新华社一日讯】中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会，依据我国和东欧各人民民主国家所签订的文化合作协定，筹组了文化教育考察团，前往东欧各兄弟国家考察文化教育事业。该团一行十人，在团长韦悫、副团长彭康率领下，已于一日启程。前往机场欢送的有：文化教育委员会副主任马叙伦、副秘书长范长江，文化部副部长周扬，教育部副部长钱俊瑞，科学院办公厅副主任丁瓒，卫生部办公厅副主任薛公绰，外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航，对外文化联络事务局副局长陈忠经等。罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华大使鲁登科，德意志民主共和国驻华外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希，匈牙利人民共和国驻华大使夏法朗柯，保加利亚人民共和国驻华大使彼得科夫，捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻华大使康萨拉，波兰人民共和国驻华大使基里洛克等也均往机场欢送。该团将先赴波兰，再转往德、捷、匈、罗、保等国考察。
