Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1952-09-24
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Our country's workers are greeting the Third National Day and the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference with achievements in patriotic production increase competitions. This is a large batch of newly made ten-inch walking plows from the North China Agricultural Machinery General Factory, and the workers are installing them.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国工人正以爱国增产竞赛的成绩，来迎接第三届国庆和亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。这是华北农业机械总厂新制的大批十寸步犁，工人们正在安装。
