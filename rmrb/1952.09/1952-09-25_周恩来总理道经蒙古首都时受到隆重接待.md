Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Received a Grand Reception When Passing Through the Mongolian Capital

Author:
Date: 1952-09-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, September 24th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government and Vice Premier Chen Yun and their party, on their way back to China from the Soviet Union on the afternoon of the 24th, received a grand reception from the government and people of the Mongolian People's Republic when they passed through Ulaanbaatar, the capital of the Mongolian People's Republic.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Suranjab; Vice Chairmen Maidar, Lubsan, and Sharap; Vice Chairman and Minister of Foreign Affairs Lamseren; Vice Chairman of the Great People's Hural Zedib, as well as ministers of various ministries of the Mongolian People's Republic Government and heads of various people's organizations.

Also present at the airport to welcome them were Novikov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in Mongolia; Kim Yong-jin, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to Mongolia; Ji Yatai, Ambassador of China to Mongolia, and all staff members of the Soviet, Korean, and Chinese embassies. An honor guard was lined up at the airport, and the national anthems of the People's Republic of China and the Mongolian People's Republic were played.

The national flags of the People's Republic of China and the Mongolian People's Republic were flying at the airport.

Premier Zhou alighted from the plane at 1 p.m. amidst the national anthems of China and Mongolia and reviewed the honor guard.

Premier Zhou had a half-hour cordial meeting with the leaders of the Mongolian People's Republic Government and people's organizations at the airport and boarded the plane to leave Ulaanbaatar at 1:30 p.m.

### 周恩来总理道经蒙古首都时受到隆重接待

1952-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理道经蒙古首都时受到隆重接待
    【新华社乌兰巴托二十四日电】我中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理和陈云副总理等一行，在二十四日下午离苏返国途中，道经蒙古人民共和国首都乌兰巴托，受到蒙古人民共和国政府和人民的隆重接待。
    前往机场欢迎的，有：蒙古人民共和国部长会议第一副主席苏兰扎布，副主席迈达尔、鲁布桑、沙拉普，副主席兼外交部部长拉姆苏伦，大人民呼拉尔副主席泽狄布以及蒙古人民共和国政府各部部长，各人民团体的负责人等。
    前往机场欢迎的，还有苏联驻蒙古大使馆临时代办诺维克夫，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻蒙古大使金龙镇，我国驻蒙古大使吉雅泰及苏、朝、中三国大使馆的全体馆员。机场上排列着仪仗队，并奏中华人民共和国和蒙古人民共和国国歌。
    飞机场上挂着中华人民共和国和蒙古人民共和国的国旗。
    周总理于下午一时，在中蒙两国国歌声中下机，并检阅了仪仗队。
    周总理在机场上与蒙古人民共和国政府和人民团体的领袖作了半小时的欢晤，于下午一时半登机离开乌兰巴托。
