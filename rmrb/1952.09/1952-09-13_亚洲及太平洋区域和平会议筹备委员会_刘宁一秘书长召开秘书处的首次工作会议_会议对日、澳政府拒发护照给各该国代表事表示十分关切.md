Title: Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference Preparatory Committee Secretary-General Liu Ningyi Convenes the First Working Meeting of the Secretariat; The Meeting Expresses Grave Concern over the Refusal of the Japanese and Australian Governments to Issue Passports to Representatives of Their Respective Countries

Author:

Date: 1952-09-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 12th: The Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference Preparatory Committee, Liu Ningyi, convened the first working meeting of the Secretariat of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference Preparatory Committee in Beijing on the morning of September 12th. Attendees included preparatory committee members, representatives, secretaries, and Chinese and foreign staff of the secretariat who had arrived in Beijing to participate in the preparatory work, totaling more than 30 people, including: U Hla from Burma, Salazar, Murillo, Gabaza, and Sanz (female) from Costa Rica, Mrs. Meda from India, Utosia and Suruso from Indonesia, Zeng Xuehong from Malaya, Rewi Alley from New Zealand, Cruz from the Philippines, ?Ba Se·Sa Xun Tong from Thailand, Kozhevnikov from the Soviet Union, and Yang Bailian from Vietnam. Thornton, a member of the Asian-Australian Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions, also attended the meeting. At the meeting, peace advocates from various countries reported on the preparations in their respective countries for participating in the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, as well as the development of peace movements in those countries. According to reports received by the Preparatory Committee, representatives from most countries in the Asian and Pacific regions are on their way to Beijing to attend the conference. The meeting expressed grave concern over the refusal of the Japanese and Australian governments to issue passports to representatives of their respective countries to attend the meeting in Beijing. The meeting also broadly exchanged views on various issues that should be discussed at the conference.

### 亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会  刘宁一秘书长召开秘书处的首次工作会议  会议对日、澳政府拒发护照给各该国代表事表示十分关切

1952-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会
    刘宁一秘书长召开秘书处的首次工作会议
    会议对日、澳政府拒发护照给各该国代表事表示十分关切
    【新华社十二日讯】亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会秘书长刘宁一，于十二日上午在北京召开亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会秘书处的首次工作会议。到会的有先后到达北京参加筹备工作的筹备委员、代表、秘书及秘书处的中外工作人员，计有：缅甸的吴拉，哥斯达黎加的萨拉萨尔、穆里洛、加巴扎、圣兹（女），印度的梅达夫人，印度尼西亚的乌托西亚、苏鲁梭，马来亚的曾雪虹，新西兰的艾黎，菲律宾的柯鲁兹，泰国的?巴色·飒顺吞，苏联的科热夫尼科夫，越南的杨白莲等三十多人。世界工会联合会亚澳联络局委员桑顿也出席了会议。会上各国和平人士报告了各该国筹备参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的情况及各国和平运动的发展情况。根据筹备委员会所得的报告，亚洲及太平洋区域大多数国家的代表正在动身前来北京出席会议。会议对于日本及澳大利亚政府拒发护照给各该国代表来北京出席会议一事表示十分关切。会议并广泛交换了关于大会中应加以讨论的各项问题的意见。
