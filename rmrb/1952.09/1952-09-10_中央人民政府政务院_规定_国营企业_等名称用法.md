Title: Central People's Government Administrative Council Stipulates Usage of Names such as "State-Owned Enterprise"
Author:
Date: 1952-09-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: The Administrative Council of the Central People's Government has stipulated the usage of names such as "State-Owned Enterprise." The full text is as follows:

Regarding enterprises operated by governments at all levels, some are currently called "State-Owned Enterprises" and others are called "Publicly-Operated Enterprises," and the names are not consistent. For this reason, the Administrative Council hereby makes the following provisions:

I. All enterprises invested and operated by the central government and the departments of the Greater Administrative Areas (including those entrusted by the Greater Administrative Areas to provinces and cities for management) shall be called "State-Owned Enterprises."

II. All enterprises invested and operated by local governments below the provincial level shall be called "Local State-Owned Enterprises."

III. Enterprises jointly funded by the government and private capital, with the government participating in management, shall be called "Joint State-Private Enterprises."

### 中央人民政府政务院  规定“国营企业”等名称用法

1952-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府政务院
    规定“国营企业”等名称用法
    【新华社八日讯】中央人民政府政务院规定对“国营企业”等名称的用法。全文如下：
    关于各级政府所经营的企业，目前有称“国营企业”的，有称“公营企业”的，名称殊不一致。为此，政务院特作如下规定：
    一、凡中央及大行政区各部门投资经营的企业（包括大行政区委托省市代管），称“国营企业”。
    二、凡省以下地方政府投资经营的企业，称“地方国营企业”。
    三、政府与私人资本合资，政府参加经营管理的企业，称“公私合营企业”。
