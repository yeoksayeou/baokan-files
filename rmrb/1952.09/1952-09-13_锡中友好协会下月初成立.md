Title: Ceylon-China Friendship Association to be Established Early Next Month
Author:
Date: 1952-09-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 12th - The Ceylon-China Friendship Association, aimed at promoting friendship between the people of Ceylon and China, is scheduled to be formally established on October 1st at the Colombo City Hall. The committee that initiated the organization of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association held a meeting on September 10th at the Colombo City Hall. Approximately forty prominent figures from all walks of life in Ceylon attended the meeting. Branches of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association will be established in other regions of Ceylon.

### 锡中友好协会下月初成立

1952-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    锡中友好协会下月初成立
    【新华社新德里十二日电】以促进锡兰和中国人民友谊为目的的锡中友好协会，定于十月一日在科伦坡市政厅正式成立。发起组织锡中友好协会的委员会，曾于九月十日在科伦坡市政厅举行会议。出席会议的有锡兰各界著名人士约四十人。在锡兰的其他地区将成立锡中友好协会分会。
