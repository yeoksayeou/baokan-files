Title: East China and Northeast China National Day Observation Groups Arrive in Beijing; Southwest Minority Nationalities National Day Observation Group Sets Off

Author: 
Date: 1952-09-23
Page: 1

    East China and Northeast China National Day Observation Groups Arrive in Beijing
    Southwest Minority Nationalities National Day Observation Group Sets Off
    [Xinhua News Agency, 21st] The East China Nationalities National Day Observation Delegation, a group of 28 people, arrived in Beijing on the morning of the 21st. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Liu Geping, Vice Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, and Commissioners Liu Chun, Zhu Zaoguan, Ma Yuhuai, Luo Changpei, and Gan Chunlei, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, and more than 20 others.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 22nd] The Northeast Nationalities National Day Observation Delegation, a group of 14 people, arrived in Beijing on the morning of the 22nd. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Liu Geping, Vice Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, and Commissioners Liu Chun, Zhu Zaoguan, Luo Changpei, Fei Xiaotong, and others.
    The delegation includes Mongolian, Korean, Hui, Oroqen, Daur, Kyrgyz, Solon, and Hezhe ethnic groups.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 20th] The Southwest Minority Nationalities National Day Observation Delegation, led by Sun Yuting, Deputy Director of the Southwest Nationalities Affairs Commission and Head of the Delegation, departed from Chongqing on the 19th to go to Beijing. The delegation includes representatives from Tibetan, Yi, Miao, Hui, Dai, Mosuo, Yao, Sani and other nineteen ethnic groups from provinces such as Xikang, Yunnan, Guizhou, and Sichuan. Among the representatives are heads of various ethnic autonomous regions and ethnic democratic united governments, cadres, Living Buddhas, labor models, and work models. Before the trip, the Southwest Military and Political Committee hosted a banquet for all representatives of the delegation on the 18th and held a farewell party on the same day.

### 华东东北各族国庆观礼团抵京  西南少数民族国庆观礼团启程

1952-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    华东东北各族国庆观礼团抵京
    西南少数民族国庆观礼团启程
    【新华社二十一日讯】华东各民族国庆节观礼代表团一行二十八人在二十一日上午到达北京。到车站欢迎的有：中央人民政府民族事务委员会副主任委员刘格平，委员刘春、朱早观、马玉槐、罗常培和民族事务委员会办公厅副主任甘春雷等二十余人。
    【新华社二十二日讯】东北各民族国庆节观礼代表团一行十四人在二十二日上午到达北京，前往车站欢迎的有中央人民政府民族事务委员会副主任委员刘格平、委员刘春、朱早观、罗常培、费孝通等多人。
    代表团包括蒙古、朝鲜、回、鄂伦春、达呼尔、吉尔吉斯、索伦和赫哲等民族成分。
    【新华社重庆二十日电】西南少数民族国庆观礼代表团已于十九日在西南民族事务委员会副主任委员、代表团团长孙雨亭率领下，由重庆启程赴京。代表团包括来自西康、云南、贵州、四川等省的藏、彝、苗、回、傣、摩些、瑶、撒尼等十九个民族的代表，代表中有各民族自治区和民族民主联合政府的负责人，干部及活佛、劳动模范、工作模范等。行前，西南军政委员会于十八日设宴招待代表团全体代表，并于当日举行欢送晚会。
