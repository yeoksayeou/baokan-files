Title: Sichuan Daily Begins Publication on September 1st
Author:
Date: 1952-09-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, September 2nd: The Sichuan Daily, the voice of the 56 million people of Sichuan Province, began publication in Chengdu on September 1st. The inaugural issue of the newspaper published an editorial celebrating the establishment of the Sichuan Provincial People's Government.

### 四川日报于九月一日创刊

1952-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    四川日报于九月一日创刊
    【新华社重庆二日电】四川省五千六百万人民的喉舌——四川日报，已于九月一日在成都创刊。该报创刊号发表社论，庆祝四川省人民政府的成立。
