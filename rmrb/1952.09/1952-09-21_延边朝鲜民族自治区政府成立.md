Title: Establishment of the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Region Government

Author: 
Date: 1952-09-21
Page: 2

    Establishment of the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Region Government
    The first People's Representative Conference of All Ethnic Groups in the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Region of Jilin Province victoriously concluded on the anniversary of the seventh anniversary of the victory of the "September 3rd" Anti-Japanese War, and formally announced the establishment of the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Region People's Government. Sa Kongliao, a member of the Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, was specially sent to attend the grand ceremony for the establishment of the Autonomous Region People's Government. Wang Yifu, representative of the Northeast People's Government, Li Mengling, secretary of the Jilin Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Li Youwen, chairman of the Jilin Provincial People's Government, also went to congratulate. People from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Northeast China sent telegrams of congratulations and presented silk banners.
    At the People's Representative Conference of All Ethnic Groups, 209 Korean representatives and 91 Han, Hui, Manchu, and Mongolian representatives listened to the work report of the Yanbian Commissioner's Office, enthusiastically discussed the issue of implementing national regional autonomy, and adopted the Organizational Regulations of the People's Representative Conference of All Ethnic Groups in the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Region, the Organizational Regulations of the Autonomous Region People's Government, and the Autonomous Region People's Government's Policy Guidelines. This conference exercised the functions and powers of the People's Congress, elected Zhu Dehai (Korean) as the chairman of the Autonomous Region People's Government, Dong Yukun (Han) and Cui Cai (Korean) as vice chairmen, and elected 32 members of the Consultative Committee, including representatives of all ethnic groups in the Yanbian region.
    In the afternoon of the 3rd, more than 30,000 people of all ethnic groups in Yanji City gathered jubilantly to celebrate, and a grand lantern parade was held at night. Korean and people of all ethnic groups, dressed in colorful national costumes, sang and danced until late at night.
    Note: The Yanbian area is located in the eastern part of Jilin Province and is the largest Korean settlement in our country. It governs five counties including Yanji, Helong, Hunchun, Wangqing, and Antu. Among them, there are more than 543,000 Korean people, accounting for 74 percent of the total population of the region.    (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 延边朝鲜民族自治区政府成立

1952-09-21
第2版()
专栏：

    延边朝鲜民族自治区政府成立
    吉林省延边朝鲜民族自治区第一次各族各界人民代表会议，在“九三”抗日战争胜利七周年纪念日胜利闭幕，正式宣布成立延边朝鲜民族自治区人民政府。中央人民政府民族事务委员会特派该会委员萨空了参加自治区人民政府成立的盛典。东北人民政府代表王一夫、中国共产党吉林省委员会书记李梦龄和吉林省人民政府主席栗又文也都前往祝贺。东北地区各族各界人民纷纷致电祝贺，并赠送锦旗。
    在各族各界人民代表会议上，二百零九名朝鲜族代表和九十一名汉、回、满、蒙族代表听取了延边专员公署的工作报告后，热烈地讨论了实行民族区域自治的问题，并通过了延边朝鲜民族自治区各族各界人民代表会议组织条例、自治区人民政府组织条例和自治区人民政府施政方针。这次会议代行了人民代表大会职权，选举了朱德海（朝鲜族）为自治区人民政府主席，董玉昆（汉族）、崔采（朝鲜族）为副主席，并选出包括延边地区各族的三十二名协商委员会委员。
    三日午后，延吉市各族人民三万多人欢欣鼓舞地集会庆祝，夜间并举行了盛大的提灯游行。朝鲜族和各族人民都穿着彩色鲜艳的民族服装高歌欢舞，直到深夜。
    按：延边地区在吉林省东部，是我国朝鲜民族最大的聚居区，辖有延吉、和龙、晖春、汪清、安图等五县，其中朝鲜族人民五十四万三千多人，占该区总人口数的百分之七十四。    （据新华社讯）
