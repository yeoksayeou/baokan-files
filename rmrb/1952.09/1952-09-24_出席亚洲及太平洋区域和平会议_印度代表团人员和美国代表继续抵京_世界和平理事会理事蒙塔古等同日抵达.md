Title: Attendees of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference: Indian Delegation Members and American Representatives Continue to Arrive in Beijing; World Peace Council Member Montagu and Others Arrive on the Same Day
Author:
Date: 1952-09-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: Indian and American representatives attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, as well as Montagu, a member of the World Peace Council, and Grassi, a secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions, who were invited to attend the Peace Conference, arrived in Beijing by train and plane respectively on September 23rd. Those who went to the railway station and airport to welcome them included: Chandra, Deputy Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference; Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the China Peace Committee; Xiao San, the Chinese representative attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference; Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association; Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Liao Mengxing, Deputy Director of the International Work Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Meng Yongqian, Deputy Director of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives; and people from cultural, scientific, and religious circles. Also present at the railway station and airport to welcome them were representatives from Ceylon, India, Indonesia, Korea, Laos, Malaya, New Zealand, the Philippines, the United States, Vietnam, and other countries who had already arrived in Beijing to attend the Peace Conference, as well as Thornton, a member of the Asian-Australian Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions. When the Indian representatives passed through Wuhan, they were warmly welcomed by the Hubei Provincial Branch and Wuhan Branch of the China Peace Committee, as well as representatives from trade unions, women's organizations, youth, and literary and art circles.

### 出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  印度代表团人员和美国代表继续抵京  世界和平理事会理事蒙塔古等同日抵达

1952-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    印度代表团人员和美国代表继续抵京
    世界和平理事会理事蒙塔古等同日抵达
    【新华社二十三日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的印度、美国代表，以及应邀参加和平会议的世界和平理事会理事蒙塔古和世界工会联合会书记处书记葛拉西于二十三日分别乘火车和飞机抵达北京。先后前往车站和机场欢迎的有：亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会副秘书长钱德拉，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一，出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的中国代表萧三，中印友好协会会长丁西林，中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长金子明，中华全国民主妇女联合会国际工作部副部长廖梦醒，中华全国合作社联合总社副主任孟用潜以及文化、科学、宗教界人士等。先后前往车站和机场欢迎的还有前已到京的锡兰、印度、印度尼西亚、朝鲜、寮国、马来亚、新西兰、菲律宾、美国、越南等国出席和平会议的代表及世界工会联合会亚澳联络局委员桑顿等。印度代表们在路过武汉时，受到中国人民保卫世界和平委员会湖北省分会和武汉分会，以及工会、妇女、青年、文学艺术界代表的热烈欢迎。
