Title: 1952 National Swimming Team Selected

Author: 
Date: 1952-09-25
Page: 3

The All-China Sports Federation held the 1952 National Swimming Competition in Guangzhou from September 16th to 18th. During the event, the All-China Swimming Team Selection Committee was established, and fifty-two national swimming athletes were selected for 1952, including thirty-nine official athletes and thirteen alternate athletes. The list is as follows:

    Official Athletes:

    Men (26): Ye Ziyou, Xiong Kaifa, Lin Jinzhu, Li Xiqing, Li Ren, Yang Yongguang, Chen Yunpeng, Deng Qiang, Zhang Tianhui, He Zhi (all above from the People's Liberation Army), Yu Shunyuan, Zhao Zifeng (both above overseas Chinese), Shi Xingzhi (Northeast Region), Mu Xiangxiong, Mu Xianghao, Zhang Jiarui, Fang Youwen, Mu Xiangying, Luo Jinlong, Wu Bao (all above North China Region), Chen Jingzhong, Wu Yufang, Gao Zhan, Wu Yukun, Jiang Haoquan (all above East China Region), Xu Zhixiang (Central-South Region).

    Women (13): Zheng Sufei, Pan Jingxian (both above from the People's Liberation Army), Jin Yunpei, Mu Xiuzhen, Luo Fengqi, Tan Aoshuang (all above North China Region), Dong Li, Dai Lihua, Lin Meiling (all above East China Region), Zhou Guoying, Li Dongmei, Zhao Jinqing, Huang Guohui (all above Central-South Region).

    Alternate Athletes:

    Men (6): Huang Ruqiang (People's Liberation Army), Huang Hongjiu, Chen Gongcheng, Chen Baoyi (all above North China Region), Sun Baozeng (Northeast Region), He Qikuan (Central-South Region).

    Women (7): Yang Jialing, Zhang Yanli, Chen Yili, Ouyang Liju, Luo Xiuxia (all above from the People's Liberation Army), Wang Shouzhen (Northeast Region), Wang Baichun (East China Region). (Xinhua News Agency)

### 一九五二年度全国游泳选手已经选出

1952-09-25
第3版()
专栏：

    一九五二年度全国游泳选手已经选出
    中华全国体育总会于九月十六日至十八日在广州举行的一九五二年全国游泳比赛大会中，成立了中华全国游泳选手选拔委员会，并选出了一九五二年度中华全国游泳选手五十二名，其中正式选手三十九名，候补选手十三名。名单如下：
    正式选手：
    男子二十六名：叶子由、熊开发、林锦珠、李喜庆、李人、杨永光、陈运鹏、邓强、张天辉、贺志
    （以上人民解放军）、俞顺源、赵子风（以上华侨）、史兴隲（东北区）、穆祥雄、穆祥豪、张家瑞、方友文、穆祥英、罗金龙、吴葆（以上华北区）、陈景忠、吴于方、高湛、吴玉崑、蒋浩泉（以上华东区）、徐致祥（中南区）。
    女子十三人：郑素绯、潘静娴（以上人民解放军）、金芸培、穆秀珍、罗凤祺、谭傲霜（以上华北区）、董琍、戴丽华、林美玲（以上华东区）、周帼英、李东妹、赵锦清、黄帼会（以上中南区）。
    候补选手：
    男子六人：黄汝强（人民解放军）、黄鸿九、陈功成、陈宝义（以上华北区）、孙宝增（东北区）、何齐宽（中南区）。
    女子七人：杨家玲、张燕丽、陈伊利、欧阳骊驹、罗秀霞（以上人民解放军）、王守真（东北区）、王白春（华东区）。（新华社）
