Title: American Progressive Writer Howard Fast Launches Campaign for Congress

Author:
Date: 1952-09-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 17th: According to the "Worker Daily," American progressive writer Howard Fast has launched his campaign for Congress. Fast is the Labor Party candidate for Congress in the 23rd Congressional District of the Bronx, New York City.

Fast sent a telegram to U.S. President Truman stating: As the Labor Party candidate for Congress in the 23rd Congressional District of New York, I demand that you order an immediate ceasefire in Korea. This is the desire of the people in my district, who are eagerly hoping to stop their sons from continuing to shed blood.

### 美国进步作家法斯特  展开竞选国会议员运动

1952-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

    美国进步作家法斯特
    展开竞选国会议员运动
    【新华社十七日讯】据塔斯社纽约十六日讯：“工人日报”报道：美国进步作家法斯特已展开竞选运动。法斯特是美国劳工党在纽约市布朗克斯区第二十三国会议员选区的国会议员候选人。
    法斯特打电报给美国总统杜鲁门说：作为美国劳工党在纽约第二十三国会议员选区的国会议员候选人，我要求你下令立即在朝鲜停火。这是我这个选区的人民的愿望，他们切盼不要再使他们的儿子们继续流血。
