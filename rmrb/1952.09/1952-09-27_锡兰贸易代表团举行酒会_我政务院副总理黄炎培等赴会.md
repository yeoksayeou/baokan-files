Title: Ceylon Trade Delegation Holds Reception; Huang Yanpei, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council, Attends

Author:
Date: 1952-09-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: The head of the Ceylon Trade Delegation, S.W.R.D. Bandaranaike, and all members of the delegation held a cocktail reception in Beijing on the evening of the 26th.

Invited to attend the reception were: Huang Yanpei, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government; Ma Yinchu, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council; Ye Jizhuang, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government; Huang Jing, Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building; Teng Daiyuan, Minister of Railways; Zhang Bojun, Minister of Communications; Li Shucheng, Minister of Agriculture; Liang Xi, Minister of Forestry; Fu Zuoyi, Minister of Water Resources; Zhang Naiqi, Minister of Food; Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; as well as Deputy Ministers from the Central People's Government's Ministry of Finance, Ministry of Commerce, Second Ministry of Machine Building, Ministry of Fuel Industry, Ministry of Textile Industry, Ministry of Construction Engineering, and Ministry of Geology; Ji Chaoding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and its members; Lu Xuzhang, Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, and more than 150 other people.

Also invited to attend the reception were: U Hla Maung, Ambassador of Burma to China; K.M. Panikkar, Ambassador of India to China; and Isa Matari, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy in China.

The nine Ceylon representatives attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference were also invited to attend the reception.

The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere. After the reception, the Ceylon Trade Delegation also screened a Ceylon film to entertain the guests.

### 锡兰贸易代表团举行酒会  我政务院副总理黄炎培等赴会

1952-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰贸易代表团举行酒会
    我政务院副总理黄炎培等赴会
    【新华社二十六日讯】锡兰贸易代表团团长沈纳那亚克暨全体团员于二十六日晚在北京举行鸡尾酒会。
    应邀参加酒会的有：中央人民政府政务院副总理黄炎培，政务院财政经济委员会副主任马寅初，政务院财政经济委员会副主任、中央人民政府对外贸易部部长叶季壮，第一机械工业部部长黄敬，铁道部部长滕代远，交通部部长章伯钧，农业部部长李书城，林业部部长梁希，水利部部长傅作义，粮食部部长章乃器，外交部副部长章汉夫以及中央人民政府财政部、商业部、第二机械工业部、燃料工业部、纺织工业部、建筑工程部、地质部等部副部长，中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎和各委员，中国进出口公司经理卢绪章等一百五十多人。
    应邀参加酒会的还有：缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂，印度驻华大使赖嘉文，印度尼西亚驻华大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂。
    出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的锡兰代表九人也应邀参加酒会。
    酒会在友好的气氛中进行。会后，锡兰贸易代表团并放映锡兰电影招待来宾。
