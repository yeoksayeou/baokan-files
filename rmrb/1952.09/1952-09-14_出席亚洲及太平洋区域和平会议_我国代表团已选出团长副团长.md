Title: Delegation to Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference Selects Leader and Deputy Leaders
Author:
Date: 1952-09-14
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 13th: The Chinese delegation to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference held its first meeting on the 13th, electing Soong Ching Ling as the head of the delegation, Guo Moruo and Peng Zhen as deputy heads, and decided to appoint Xing Xiping as Secretary-General.

### 出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  我国代表团已选出团长副团长

1952-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

    出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    我国代表团已选出团长副团长
    【新华社十三日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的中国代表团十三日举行首次会议，选出宋庆龄为代表团团长，郭沫若和彭真为副团长，并决定聘请邢西萍为秘书长。
