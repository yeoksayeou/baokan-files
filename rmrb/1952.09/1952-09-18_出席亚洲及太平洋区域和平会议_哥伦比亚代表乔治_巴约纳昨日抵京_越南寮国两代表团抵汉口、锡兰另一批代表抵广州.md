Title: Attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, Colombian Representative Jorge Bayona Arrives in Beijing Yesterday; Vietnamese and Laotian Delegations Arrive in Hankou; Another Group of Ceylonese Representatives Arrive in Guangzhou
Author: 
Date: 1952-09-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 17th: Colombian representative Jorge Bayona, who is attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 17th. Those who went to the airport to welcome him included Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General, Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and many others.

Bayona is the Secretary of the National Peace Council of Colombia.

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, September 15th: The Vietnamese delegation, headed by Le Dinh Tham, and deputy head Nguyen Xuan Thuy, with nine members and four staff; and the Laotian delegation, headed by No Ha, with one member and one staff member, arrived in Hankou on the evening of the 14th. Wang Tan, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangxi Provincial People's Government, accompanied them. The representatives were warmly welcomed by all circles in Wuhan. Those who welcomed them included Li Erzhong, Chairman of the Wuhan Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Liu Renxi, Deputy Secretary-General, Lu Liang, representative of the Wuhan Federation of Trade Unions, Fang Hengru, Secretary-General of the Wuhan Student Federation, Li Dongqing, Vice Chairman of the Wuhan Democratic Women's Federation, and more than 1,000 representatives of the people from all walks of life. The representatives also received flowers from the children. Some of the Vietnamese and Laotian representatives will go to Beijing soon; others will visit Nanjing.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 16th: The Ceylonese representatives, including Gunawardena, seven people in total, arrived in Guangzhou on the afternoon of the 16th.

Guo Qiaoran, Deputy Secretary-General of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Huang Changshui, a committee member, and others went to the station to meet them. Also welcoming them at the station were more than 100 representatives of children and students from Guangzhou.

The representatives received flowers from the Young Pioneers after getting off the train.

### 出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  哥伦比亚代表乔治·巴约纳昨日抵京  越南寮国两代表团抵汉口、锡兰另一批代表抵广州

1952-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    哥伦比亚代表乔治·巴约纳昨日抵京
    越南寮国两代表团抵汉口、锡兰另一批代表抵广州
    【新华社十七日讯】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的哥伦比亚代表乔治·巴约纳于十七日下午乘飞机抵达北京。前往机场欢迎的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通、秘书长刘贯一、中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长金子明等多人。
    巴约纳是哥伦比亚全国和平理事会书记。
    【新华社汉口十五日电】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的越南代表团团长黎庭探、副团长阮春水和团员九人，工作人员四人；寮国代表团团长诺哈，团员和工作人员各一人，于十四日晚抵汉口。陪同前来的有广西省人民政府交际处处长王谭。代表们受到武汉各界的热烈欢迎。欢迎者有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会武汉分会主席李尔重、副秘书长刘人熙、武汉市总工会代表吕梁、武汉市学生联合会秘书长方衡儒、武汉市民主妇女联合会副主席李冬青及各界人民代表一千余人。代表们并接受了少年儿童们的献花。越南和寮国的一部分代表将于最近赴北京；另一部分将到南京参观。
    【新华社广州十六日电】出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的锡兰代表古纳瓦地尼等七人于十六日下午到达广州。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广州市分会副秘书长郭翘然、委员黄长水等多人前往车站迎接。往车站欢迎者还有广州市少年儿童代表、学生代表等一百余人。
    代表们下车后接受了少年儿童队的献花。
