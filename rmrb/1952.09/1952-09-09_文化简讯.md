Cultural Briefs

Preparatory Committee for National Swimming Competition Established
The Preparatory Committee for the 1952 National Swimming Competition has been formally established in Guangzhou. Ye Jianying, Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, serves as the Chairman, with Zhu Guang, Deputy Mayor of the Guangzhou Municipal People's Government, Du Guoxiang, Director of the Department of Culture and Education of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, and Huang Zhong, Acting Secretary-General of the All-China Sports Federation, as Vice-Chairmen. The National Swimming Competition will be held from September 14th to 16th at the Yuexiu Park Swimming Pool in Guangzhou.

Our Football Team Returns Home After Visiting Poland
Our football team, which visited Poland at the invitation of the Polish Sports Affairs Committee, concluded its visit on the 3rd and departed Poland for home. The football team visited Poland on August 15th after attending the 15th International Olympic Games. From the 19th, the football team played four friendly matches with Polish football teams in Krakow, Katowice, Szczecin, and Warsaw, and visited the Nowa Huta New Industrial City, the Auschwitz Concentration Camp, the Children's City, and the seaport. The Chinese football team received a warm welcome and hospitality from the Polish people everywhere it went, and more than 100,000 spectators watched the football team's matches. After the competition, our football team held two symposiums with Polish sports workers to exchange experiences in sports work in both countries.

Czech Army Art Troupe Visits Hangzhou, Nanchang, Guangzhou
The Czechoslovak "Vít Nejedlý Army Art Ensemble" left Hangzhou on the afternoon of the 3rd. During its stay in Hangzhou, the art troupe gave two wonderful performances and visited the scenic spots and historical sites of West Lake.

The art troupe arrived in Nanchang, the birthplace of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, on the 4th. All members of the troupe visited the general headquarters during the "August 1st" Uprising, now the Jiangxi Provincial People's Government Reception Office, and visited the Jiangwutang, where Commander-in-Chief Zhu De founded the Third Army Officers Education Regiment before the "August 1st" Uprising (now the Cultural Acceleration School of the Central and South Military Region). They were warmly welcomed by all the students of the school during the visit. Five combat heroes who are currently studying at the school, including Guo Junqing, Tian Guangwen, Bao Renchuan, Mao Guo, and Li Shuting, also participated in the welcoming ceremony. Jaroslav Veselý, the head of the troupe, personally awarded the Julius Fučík Medal to the five combat heroes. On the evening of the 4th, the Political Department of the Jiangxi Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held a soiree. At the meeting, the troupe performed wonderful programs such as dances, solos, and choruses. On the 5th, all members of the troupe listened to an introduction about the "August 1st" Uprising by Huang Lin (currently Secretary of the Nanchang Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party), an old soldier who participated in the "August 1st" Uprising at the time, and visited Commander-in-Chief Zhu De's residence during the "August 1st" Uprising (now the "August 1st" Nursery). The art troupe arrived in Guangzhou on the evening of the 6th. Wang Yanshi, Deputy Director of the Cultural Department of the Political Department of the Central and South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, representatives of the Fourth Field Army stationed in Guangzhou, and representatives of workers, youth, women, and students in Guangzhou, totaling more than 1,800 people, went to the station to welcome them. A grand welcoming ceremony was held at the station.

### 文化简讯

1952-09-09

专栏：文化简讯

    文化简讯
    全国游泳比赛大会筹备委员会成立
    一九五二年全国游泳比赛大会筹备委员会已在广州正式成立。广东省人民政府主席叶剑英担任主任委员，广州市人民政府副市长朱光、广东省人民政府文教厅厅长杜国庠及中华全国体育总会代秘书长黄中为副主任委员。全国游泳比赛大会将于九月十四日至十六日在广州越秀公园游泳池举行。
    我国足球队访问波兰后启程返国　　应波兰体育事务委员会邀请访问波兰的我国足球队，已于三日结束访问离波兰返国。我国足球队是在出席第十五届国际奥林匹克运动会后，在八月十五日到波兰访问的。足球队从十九日起在克拉科、卡托维兹、斯才新和华沙等地与波兰的足球队作了四场友谊赛，并参观了诺瓦·胡塔新工业城、奥斯威辛集中营、儿童城及海港。中国足球队到处都受到波兰人民的热烈欢迎和招待，参观足球队比赛的观众共十万多人。在比赛结束后，我国足球队曾与波兰体育工作者举行两次座谈，交流两国体育工作的经验。
    捷军队文工团访问杭州、南昌、广州
　　捷克斯洛伐克“维特·尼耶德利军队文艺工作团”三日下午离开杭州。文艺工作团在杭州期间举行了二次精彩的演出，并参观了西湖的名胜和古迹。
    文艺工作团四日到达中国人民解放军诞生地——南昌。该团全体团员参观了“八一”起义时的总指挥部，现为江西省人民政府交际处，访问了“八一”起义前朱总司令所创办的第三军军官教育团所在地的讲武堂（现为中南军区文化速成学校），访问时受到该校全体学员的热烈欢迎。现在该校学习的全国战斗英雄郭俊卿、田广文、鲍仁川、毛国、李树廷等五位战斗英雄也参加了欢迎会。该团团长雅罗斯拉夫·维赛利亲自给五位战斗英雄佩带尤·伏契克纪念章。四日晚中国人民解放军江西军区政治部举行联欢晚会。会上，该团表演了舞蹈、独唱、合唱等精彩节目。五日该团全体团员听取了当时参加“八一”起义的老战士——黄霖（现任中共南昌市委书记）关于“八一”起义的介绍，并参观了“八一”起义时朱总司令的住处（现为“八一”保育院）。文艺工作团六日晚上到达广州。到车站欢迎的，有中国人民解放军中南军区暨第四野战军政治部文化部副部长王燕士等和第四野战军驻广州部队代表、以及广州市工人、青年、妇女、学生代表共一千八百多人。在车站上举行了盛大的欢迎会。
