Title: Burmese Land Reform Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou on September 2nd
Author:
Date: 1952-09-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 2nd: A Burmese Land Reform Delegation headed by Thakin Tin, Minister of Land Nationalization of Burma, arrived in Guangzhou on the 2nd. Zhu Guang, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou Municipality, Qiu Zhe, Director of the Department of Agriculture and Forestry of Guangdong Provincial People's Government, Cao Ruoming, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of Guangzhou Municipal People's Government, and many others went to the station to welcome them.

Xi Jizhi, Deputy Director of the Production Movement Department of the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, and Cao Guisheng, Deputy Section Chief of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, went to Shenzhen to meet the delegation and accompanied them to Guangzhou.

In the evening, Zhu Guang, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou Municipality, hosted a banquet to entertain the Burmese Land Reform Delegation.

The members of the Burmese Land Reform Delegation are as follows:

    Head of the Delegation: Thakin Tin (Minister of Land Nationalization of Burma)
    Deputy Head of the Delegation: U Ba Thein (Secretary of the Ministry of Land Nationalization of Burma)
    Member: Thakin San Myaing (Parliamentary Secretary of the Ministry of Land Nationalization of Burma)
    Secretary: U Tun Yin.

### 缅甸土地改革参观团九月二日抵达广州市

1952-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸土地改革参观团九月二日抵达广州市
    【新华社广州二日电】以缅甸土地国有部部长德钦阵为首的缅甸土地改革参观团一行四人二日抵达广州。前往车站欢迎的有广州市人民政府副市长朱光、广东省人民政府农林厅厅长丘哲、广州市人民政府外事处处长曹若茗等多人。
    中央人民政府农业部生产运动处副处长犀继之、外交部亚洲司副科长曹桂生等曾前往深圳迎接并陪同到达广州。
    当晚广州市人民政府副市长朱光设宴招待缅甸土地改革参观团。
    缅甸土地改革参观团名单如下：
    团长：德钦阵（缅甸土地国有部部长）
    副团长：宇巴太（缅甸土地国有部秘书）
    团员：德钦山敏（缅甸土地国有部国会秘书）
    秘书：宇吞阵。
