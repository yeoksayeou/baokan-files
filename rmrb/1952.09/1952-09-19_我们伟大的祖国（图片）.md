    Our Great Motherland
    The Chen Dengke Mutual Aid Team, a famous agricultural labor model in Jiangxi Province, has achieved an unprecedented bumper harvest of early rice this year, creating high unit area yields. The yield of ordinary fields has increased by about 40% compared to last year, and the yield of high-yield fields has increased by 100% to 201% compared to last year. This is a scene of the Chen Dengke Mutual Aid Team harvesting early rice.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    江西省著名农业劳动模范陈登科互助组，今年所种的早稻空前丰收，创造了高额单位面积产量，一般田比去年提高产量约百分之四十，丰产田比去年提高产量百分之一百至二百零一。这是陈登科互助组收割早稻的情形。
