Title: Soong Ching-ling Donates All Royalties from Collected Works to Korean People's Army and Volunteers

Author:
Date: 1952-09-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: Soong Ching-ling's collected works, "Struggling for New China," will soon be published simultaneously in Chinese and English by People's Publishing House and Foreign Languages Press. The entire book contains 200,000 words. Soong Ching-ling has donated all the royalties from this book, RMB 500 million, to the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers who are standing on the front line of defending peace.

### 宋庆龄捐出文集全部稿费  赠给朝鲜人民军和志愿军

1952-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄捐出文集全部稿费  赠给朝鲜人民军和志愿军
    【新华社二十七日讯】宋庆龄文集“为新中国奋斗”，即将由人民出版社和外文出版社以中英文同时出版。全书计有二十万字。宋庆龄已将此书的稿费人民币五亿元全部捐赠给站在保卫和平最前线的朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军。
