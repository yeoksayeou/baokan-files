Title: People of Various People's Democratic Countries Continue to Aid Korean Disaster Victims
Author:
Date: 1952-09-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang: The people of various people's democratic countries continue to send large quantities of clothing and grain to aid the Korean people who have suffered from the war. These materials are being rapidly distributed by the Korean War Disaster Relief Committee.
    The 300 tons of grain donated by all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteers to the refugees in Pyongyang in September, and the more than 71,000 packages of aid materials sent by the people of Hungary, have begun to be distributed. Refugees in Pyongyang recently received more than 16,000 pieces of international relief supplies. Refugees in various parts of Jiangwon Province recently received some relief supplies from the people of Romania, Bulgaria and other countries, including more than 20,000 pieces of clothing and 30,000 catties of grain. The relief supplies sent from factories, rural villages, schools and families in the distant German Democratic Republic, Romania, Bulgaria, and Hungary were accompanied by passionate letters expressing friendship and determination to support the heroic Korean people.
    The Korean people are extremely grateful for the assistance of the people of various fraternal countries. They have written letters of thanks, expressing their determination to strike even harder at the American aggressors and to fight to the end with the peace-loving people of the world to defend peace and oppose aggression.

### 各人民民主国家人民  继续援助朝鲜灾民

1952-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

    各人民民主国家人民
    继续援助朝鲜灾民
    【新华社平壤讯】各人民民主国家人民继续送来大批衣物和粮食，援助遭受战争灾难的朝鲜人民。这些物资正由朝鲜战争灾民救济委员会迅速分配中。
    中国人民志愿军全体指挥员战斗员在九月间赠送给平壤市难民的三百吨粮食和匈牙利人民送来的七万一千多包援助物资，已经开始分配。平壤市的难民最近领到国际救济物品一万六千多件。江原道各地的难民最近领到了罗马尼亚、保加利亚等国人民送来的部分救济品，计有衣物二万多件、粮食三万斤。由遥远的德意志民主共和国、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、匈牙利各地的工厂农村学校与家庭中送来的救济品中，并附有热情洋溢的对英雄的朝鲜人民表示友谊与支援决心的信件。
    朝鲜人民极为感激各兄弟国家人民的援助。他们纷纷写信道谢，并表示要更加沉重地打击美国侵略者，和全世界爱好和平的人民一起为保卫和平反对侵略而战斗到底。
