Title: Central Meteorological Observatory Issues Typhoon Warning
Author:
Date: 1952-09-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th: The Central Meteorological Observatory issued the following typhoon warning at 12:00 midnight on September 5th: At 8:00 PM on the 5th, the center of the typhoon was located approximately 540 li south of Hong Kong (19.5 degrees north latitude, 115 degrees east longitude), with a maximum wind speed near the center of approximately 200 li per hour. At a distance of 550 li from the center, the wind speed could also reach 90 to 120 li per hour. Currently, the typhoon center is moving westward at a speed of 50 li per hour. In the next twelve hours, coastal areas of China from Shantou, Guangdong, to the southern part of Hainan Island will experience strong winds, waves, and showers. These areas must take precautions. The wind and rain in the coastal area east of Shantou will gradually decrease.

### 中央气象台发布台风警报

1952-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中央气象台发布台风警报
    【新华社五日讯】中央气象台九月五日晚十二时发布台风警报如下：五日下午八时台风的中心位置在香港以南约五百四十华里的海面上（北纬十九点五度，东经一百一十五度），中心附近最大风速约每小时二百华里。距中心五百五十华里处，风速亦可达每小时九十到一百二十华里。目前台风中心正以每小时五十华里的速度向西方向进行。未来十二小时内，从广东汕头到海南岛南部的我国沿海地区有大风浪和阵雨。这一带地区须注意预防。汕头以东的沿海一带，风雨将逐渐减少。
