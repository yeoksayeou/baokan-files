Title: China, Soviet Union, and Finland Sign This Year's Triangular Trade Agreement, Each Supplying Goods Worth 34 Million Rubles in a Chain
Author:
Date: 1952-09-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 26th—The governments of China, the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, and the Republic of Finland, in a friendly atmosphere, completed triangular trade negotiations in Moscow and signed on the 21st the agreement between China, the Soviet Union, and Finland regarding the supply of goods within 1952, as well as the Sino-Soviet and Sino-Finnish protocols according to this agreement. Representing the Central People's Government of China was Zhang Wentian, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union; representing the government of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics was Kumykin, Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union; and representing the government of the Republic of Finland was Santerström, Minister of the Republic of Finland to the Soviet Union.

The triangular trade agreement stipulates that within 1952, each country will supply goods worth 34 million rubles, with the Soviet Union supplying Finland, Finland supplying China, and China supplying the Soviet Union. The signing of the China-Soviet-Finland triangular trade agreement will promote trade turnover between the three countries and contribute to the development of friendly relations between them.

### 中苏芬签订本年三角贸易协定  各以价值三千四百万卢布的货物连环供应

1952-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏芬签订本年三角贸易协定
    各以价值三千四百万卢布的货物连环供应
    【新华社莫斯科二十六日电】我国政府和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府及芬兰共和国政府之间在友好的气氛中，于莫斯科完成了三角贸易谈判，并于二十一日签订了中苏芬三国关于在一九五二年内供应货物的协定，以及根据此协定之中苏，中芬议定书。代表我中央人民政府签字的是中华人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使张闻天，代表苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府签字的是苏联对外贸易部部长库米金，代表芬兰共和国政府签字的是芬兰共和国驻苏联公使桑斯特罗姆。
    三角贸易协定规定在一九五二年内，各以价值三千四百万卢布之货物，由苏联供应芬兰，由芬兰供应中国，由中国供应苏联。中，苏，芬三角贸易协定的签定，将促进三国间的贸易周转，并有助于三国间友好关系的发展。
