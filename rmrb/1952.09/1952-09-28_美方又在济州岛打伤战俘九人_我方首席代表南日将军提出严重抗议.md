Title: The U.S. Side Again Injures Nine Prisoners of War in Jeju Island; Our Chief Representative General Nam Il Lodges a Serious Protest
Author:
Date: 1952-09-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, September 27th: On September 25th, the U.S. side again injured nine of our captured personnel in the Jeju Island prisoner-of-war camp. General Nam Il, chief representative of our delegation to the Korean armistice talks, lodged a serious protest with the U.S. side regarding this, pointing out that the U.S. side bears full and absolute responsibility for the continuous persecution and massacre of prisoners of war, which constitutes a war crime.
    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, September 27th: The U.S. side injured nine of our captured personnel on Jeju Island again on the 25th. According to the U.S. side, these nine people were injured by U.S. soldiers when the U.S. side entered the camp to capture three prisoners of war. The U.S. side did not provide any information about the whereabouts of the three captured prisoners of war. Through this incident, it is clear that the U.S. side is wantonly seizing prisoners of war in their prisoner-of-war camps and sending them to undisclosed locations.

### 美方又在济州岛打伤战俘九人  我方首席代表南日将军提出严重抗议

1952-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    美方又在济州岛打伤战俘九人
    我方首席代表南日将军提出严重抗议
    【新华社开城二十七日电】九月二十五日美方又在济州岛战俘营打伤我方被俘人员九名。朝鲜停战谈判我方代表团首席代表南日将军对此向美方提出严重抗议，指出美方对于连续不断地迫害与屠杀战俘的战争罪行，负有完全与绝对的责任。
    【新华社开城二十七日电】美方二十五日又在济州岛上打伤我方被俘人员九人。据美方宣布，这九个人是在美方进入营场捕捉三名战俘时被美方士兵打伤的。对于被捕去的三名战俘的下落，美方没有作任何交代。通过这一事件，人们已经可以清楚地看到，美方正在他们的战俘营里肆无忌惮地抓走战俘，并把他们送到不可告人的地方去。
