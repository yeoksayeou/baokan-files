Title: Newspaper of Milan Manufacturers in Italy Demands Restoration of Trade Relations with China
Author:
Date: 1952-09-06
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Rome, September 3rd (TASS dispatch): The financial and economic newspaper of Milan manufacturers, "Il Sole 24 Ore," urges the Italian government to remove the obstacles hindering normal trade between Italy and the People's Republic of China.
    The newspaper recounts that the Chinese government has repeatedly expressed its hope to restore and expand trade with all countries on an equal footing. The newspaper continues, "The issue of restoring trade relations with China is pressing for all countries with substantial exports... Those who waste time will not only lose the opportunity for advantageous specific contracts but also future prospects."
    The newspaper asks the government to clarify its position on this matter, and concludes, "China exists, and if other exporting countries do not neglect China, we Italians cannot afford to neglect that country even more."

### 意大利米兰制造商的报纸  要求与我国恢复贸易关系

1952-09-06
第4版()
专栏：

    意大利米兰制造商的报纸
    要求与我国恢复贸易关系
    【新华社讯】塔斯社罗马三日电：米兰制造商的财政经济报纸“二十四小时报”要求意大利政府除去阻碍意大利和中华人民共和国的正常贸易的障碍物。
    这个报纸追叙说：中国政府已经再三表示希望和各国在平等基础上恢复与扩大贸易。该报接着说：“和中国恢复贸易关系的问题，对于一切有大量出口的国家都是迫切的……凡是浪费时间的人，不但失去有利的具体合同，同时也要失去将来的憧憬。”
    这个报纸要求政府说明它在这一方面的主张，最后又说：“中国存在着，如果其他出口国家不忽视中国，我们意大利人就更加不能忽视那个国家。”
