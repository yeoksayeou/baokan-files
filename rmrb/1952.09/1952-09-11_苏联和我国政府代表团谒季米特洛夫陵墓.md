Title: Soviet and Chinese Government Delegations Visit Dimitrov Mausoleum
Author:
Date: 1952-09-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, September 9th: TASS reports: The Soviet government delegation, headed by Tarasov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation, visited the mausoleum of the Bulgarian people's leader, Georgi Dimitrov, on September 8th and laid a wreath. The Soviet government delegation is in Sofia to attend the eighth anniversary celebration of Bulgaria's liberation from the hateful royal fascist yoke.

The Soviet government delegation also laid a wreath at the tomb of Kolarov, Georgi Dimitrov's faithful friend and comrade-in-arms, and an outstanding leader of the international workers' movement.

Government delegations from the People's Republic of China, the Polish People's Republic, the German Democratic Republic, the Mongolian People's Republic, the Czechoslovak Republic and other People's Republics, as well as many foreign workers' delegations, also laid wreaths at the Georgi Dimitrov Mausoleum.

### 苏联和我国政府代表团谒季米特洛夫陵墓

1952-09-11
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和我国政府代表团谒季米特洛夫陵墓
    【新华社讯】塔斯社索非亚九日电：以俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团主席塔拉索夫为首的苏联政府代表团九月八日晋谒保加利亚人民领袖格奥尔基·季米特洛夫的陵墓，并献花圈。苏联政府代表团是来索非亚参加保加利亚从可恨的保皇法西斯枷锁下解放出来的第八周年庆祝典礼的。
    苏联政府代表团也在科拉罗夫的陵墓上献了花圈，科拉罗夫是格奥尔基·季米特洛夫的忠实友人和战友，国际工人运动中的卓越领袖。
    中华人民共和国、波兰人民共和国、德意志民主共和国、蒙古人民共和国、捷克斯洛伐克共和国和其他人民共和国的政府代表团以及许多外国工人代表团也向格奥尔基·季米特洛夫陵墓敬献花圈。
