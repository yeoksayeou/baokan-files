Title: World Volleyball Championships Continue
Author: 
Date: 1952-09-01
Page: 4

    【本报讯】据新华社讯：世界男女排球锦标赛继续在莫斯科举行。
    [Our dispatch] According to Xinhua News Agency: The World Men's and Women's Volleyball Championships are continuing in Moscow.

    八月二十日，男子三组的预赛已告结束。预赛的结果如下：匈牙利、保加利亚、苏联、罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克和法国六队有资格参加决赛争取第一名到第六名；波兰、芬兰、以色列、黎巴嫩、印度队争取第七名到第十一名。女子组连前共计已举行过六场比赛。波兰、苏联和捷克斯洛伐克的球队最为杰出，各场都获胜。
    The preliminary rounds of the three men's groups concluded on August 20th. The results of the preliminary rounds are as follows: Hungary, Bulgaria, the Soviet Union, Romania, Czechoslovakia, and France are qualified to participate in the finals to compete for first to sixth place; Poland, Finland, Israel, Lebanon, and India will compete for seventh to eleventh place. A total of six matches have been held so far in the women's group. The teams from Poland, the Soviet Union, and Czechoslovakia have been the most outstanding, winning all of their matches.

    锦标赛在二十二日继续举行。男子组：黎巴嫩队胜以色列队，三比二；苏联队胜匈牙利队，三比零；捷克斯洛伐克队胜法国队，三比零；波兰队胜印度队，三比零。女子组；苏联队胜罗马尼亚队，三比零；捷克斯洛伐克队胜法国队，三比零。
    The championships continued on the 22nd. Men's group: Lebanon defeated Israel, 3-2; the Soviet Union defeated Hungary, 3-0; Czechoslovakia defeated France, 3-0; Poland defeated India, 3-0. Women's group: the Soviet Union defeated Romania, 3-0; Czechoslovakia defeated France, 3-0.

    二十三日举行了八场比赛。男子前六名决赛组保加利亚队以三比一获胜，捷克斯洛伐克队以三比二胜匈牙利队，波兰队以三比零胜以色列队，黎巴嫩队以三比一打败芬兰队；女子组保加利亚队以三比一获胜，苏联队胜捷克斯洛伐克队，三比零，波兰队胜罗马尼亚队，三比一，法国队击败印度队，三比零。
    Eight matches were held on the 23rd. In the men's top six finals group, Bulgaria won 3-1, Czechoslovakia defeated Hungary 3-2, Poland defeated Israel 3-0, and Lebanon defeated Finland 3-1; In the women's group, Bulgaria won 3-1, the Soviet Union defeated Czechoslovakia 3-0, Poland defeated Romania 3-1, and France defeated India 3-0.

    二十四日，男子优胜决赛组比赛中苏联队以三比零击败了罗马尼亚队。保加利亚队以三比一胜法国队。争取第七名到十一名的男子决赛组中，波兰队连胜三场，击败了芬兰队，印度队以三比零打败了以色列队。女子组波兰队以三比一的成绩获胜。女子组其余各队的比赛结果如下：保加利亚队胜印度队，三比零；匈牙利队胜法国队，三比一。
    On the 24th, in the men's championship finals group, the Soviet Union defeated Romania 3-0. Bulgaria defeated France 3-1. In the men's finals group competing for seventh to eleventh place, Poland won three consecutive matches, defeating Finland, and India defeated Israel 3-0. In the women's group, Poland won with a score of 3-1. The results of the remaining women's teams' matches are as follows: Bulgaria defeated India, 3-0; Hungary defeated France, 3-1.
