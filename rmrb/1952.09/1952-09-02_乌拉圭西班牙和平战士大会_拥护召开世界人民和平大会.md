Title: Uruguay Spanish Peace Fighters Conference Endorses the Convening of the World People's Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1952-09-02
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, 25th: The Uruguay Spanish Peace Fighters Conference, convened by the Spanish Peace Committee, has concluded in Montevideo. The conference passed several resolutions aimed at expanding the struggle to defend peace and to save Spain from the Franco regime and the enslavement of American imperialism. The conference unanimously endorsed the World Peace Council's decision to convene the World People's Peace Congress, and urged all peace fighters among the Spanish expatriate community in Uruguay to actively prepare for this congress. The conference passed a special resolution paying tribute to the heroic Spanish people who are fighting against Franco and the American imperialists, and elected the leading body of the Spanish Peace Committee, headed by the writer Vega Ming.

### 乌拉圭西班牙和平战士大会  拥护召开世界人民和平大会

1952-09-02
第4版()
专栏：

    乌拉圭西班牙和平战士大会
    拥护召开世界人民和平大会
    【新华社讯】塔斯社蒙得维的亚二十五日电：西班牙和平委员会所召开的乌拉圭西班牙和平战士大会已在蒙得维的亚闭幕。大会通过了若干旨在扩大保卫和平及拯救西班牙摆脱佛朗哥政权和美帝国主义奴役的斗争的决议。大会一致拥护世界和平理事会关于召开世界人民和平大会的决定，并敦促乌拉圭的西班牙侨民中一切和平战士积极为这个大会进行准备工作。大会通过了向那些为反对佛朗哥与美帝国主义者而奋斗的英勇的西班牙人民致敬的特别决议，并且选出了以作家贝加明为首的西班牙人和平委员会的领导机关。
