Title: Message of Condolence from Prince Norodom Sihanouk
Author:
Date: 1989-04-17
Edition: 3

	Message of Condolence from Prince Norodom Sihanouk

	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 17: On April 16, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zhao Ziyang, received a message of condolence from Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Chairman of the Democratic Kampuchea, regarding the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang.

	In his message of condolence, Prince Norodom Sihanouk said: "It is with deep sorrow that we learned of the passing of the great patriot and revolutionary, Hu Yaobang, who made historic contributions to the prosperity and development of his glorious motherland, the People's Republic of China, and to the happiness and prosperity of the Chinese people. The people of Cambodia and the United Government of Democratic Kampuchea, as well as I myself, are deeply grieved. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Your Excellency, to the other leaders of the People's Republic of China, to the great people of China and the Communist Party of China, and to the family of the late distinguished individual."

	The message of condolence further stated: "The outstanding Hu Yaobang made significant contributions to the victory of the Cambodian national liberation struggle. His passing is a great loss for Democratic Kampuchea, as it has lost a great and ardent friend."

### 西哈努克主席的唁电

1989-04-17
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　西哈努克主席的唁电
    新华社北京４月１７日电　中国共产党中央委员会总书记赵紫阳１６日收到民主柬埔寨主席诺罗敦·西哈努克就胡耀邦同志逝世发来的唁电。
    诺罗敦·西哈努克主席的唁电说：“惊悉伟大的爱国者和革命家，为自己光荣的祖国——中华人民共和国的兴旺发达，为中国人民的幸福昌盛做出了历史性贡献的胡耀邦阁下不幸逝世，柬埔寨人民和民主柬埔寨联合政府以及我本人深感悲痛。我们谨向尊敬的阁下，向尊敬的中华人民共和国其他领导人，向伟大的中国人民和中国共产党，并向卓越的已故者家属表示沉痛的哀悼。”
    唁电说：“杰出的胡耀邦阁下曾为柬埔寨民族解放斗争的胜利做了许多工作。他的逝世使民主柬埔寨失去了一位伟大、热忱的朋友。”
