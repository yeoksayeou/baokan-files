Title: Bunnaka Expresses Intent to Take Action to Improve Japan-North Korea Relations
Author:
Date: 1989-04-16
Edition: 3

Japanese Prime Minister Takeshita Noboru today stated that the Japanese government will actively engage with the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

Prime Minister Takeshita said today during a meeting with Liberal Democratic Party Secretary-General Abe Shintaro, regarding the improvement of relations between Japan and North Korea: "As the government, we hope to advance contact." Secretary-General Abe reported to Takeshita that the Liberal Democratic Party has decided to send a delegation to North Korea. This indicates that Japan will take concrete actions to improve relations with North Korea.

### 竹下表示将采取行动改善日朝关系

1989-04-16
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　竹下表示将采取行动改善日朝关系
    新华社东京４月１５日电　日本首相竹下登今天表示，日本政府将积极与朝鲜民主主义人民共和国接触。
    竹下首相今天在与自民党干事长安倍晋太郎会谈时，就改善日本与朝鲜的关系表示：“作为政府，希望推进接触”。安倍干事长向竹下报告说，自民党决定向朝鲜派出代表团。这表明，日本将在改善与朝鲜关系方面，采取具体的行动。
