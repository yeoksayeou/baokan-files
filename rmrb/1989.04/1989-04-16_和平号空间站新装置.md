Title: New Equipment for the Mir Space Station
Author: Qian Jizu
Date: 1989-04-16
Edition: 7

A module equipped with an airlock system and service facilities, as well as a technology module equipped with microgravity production equipment, will be launched this year. The Mir space station will then consist of three modules. Due to the service module being equipped with solar cell panels, the space station's power supply will increase from 10 kilowatts to 30 kilowatts.

Currently, the service module undergoing testing at the Baikonur Cosmodrome will be sent to the Mir space station in April this year. Soviet cosmonauts will be able to use the new manned facilities to operate continuously for six hours at a distance of 50 meters from the space station.

It is expected that by 1991, two additional scientific modules for medical, biophysical, and remote sensing applications will be added. The entire station will reach its planned structural state of 130 tons.

Astronauts from France, Austria, and the United Kingdom will participate in the Mir flights.

### 和平号空间站新装置
钱继祖
1989-04-16
第7版(国际专页（科教）)
专栏：

　　和平号空间站新装置
    带有空气闭锁装置及服务设施的舱段及装备有微重力生产设备的工艺舱段将在今年发射升空，和平号空间站将增加到由３个舱段组成。由于服务舱带有太阳电池帆板，空间站的电力将从１０千瓦增到３０千瓦。
    目前，正在拜科努尔航天港进行测试的服务舱将在今年４月送上和平号空间站。苏联宇航员利用新的载人设施可在距空间站５０公尺的地点进行持续６个小时的操作。
    预计到１９９１年还将再增加用于医学与生物物理及遥感应用的两个科学舱段。全站将达到原定的１３０吨结构状态。
    法国、奥地利与英国等国家的宇航员将参加和平号飞行。
