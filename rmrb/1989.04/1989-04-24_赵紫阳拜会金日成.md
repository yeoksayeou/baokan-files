Title: Zhao Ziyang Meets with Kim Il-sung
Author: Zhou Bizhong
Date: 1989-04-24
Edition: 1

Zhao Ziyang Meets with Kim Il-sung

	BEIJING, April 24 -- Zhao Ziyang, General Secretary of the Communist Party of China, paid a visit to Kim Il-sung, General Secretary of the Workers' Party of Korea, at 6:30 p.m. today.

	Zhao Ziyang conveyed greetings from Deng Xiaoping, Yang Shangkun, Li Peng, and other members of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee to Kim Il-sung. He also conveyed greetings from Deng Yingchao, Peng Zhen, and Wan Li to Kim Il-sung.

	Zhao Ziyang said that he was visiting Korea because the two parties and the two countries have a very friendly relationship. His main purpose was to visit Chairman Kim Il-sung and other leaders of the Workers' Party of Korea. Additionally, he wanted to see with his own eyes the great achievements made by the Korean people under the leadership of Chairman Kim Il-sung in socialist construction since his visit in 1981.

	Zhao Ziyang said that as soon as he got off the train, he was warmly welcomed by Chairman Kim Il-sung, the leaders of the Workers' Party of Korea, and the Korean people. This deep affection moved him and his colleagues deeply. He expressed his gratitude once again for this.

	Chairman Kim Il-sung said that he was very pleased that Zhao Ziyang, as the General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, chose Korea as his first visit abroad. This visit by General Secretary Zhao Ziyang to Korea is a testament to the trust the Korean people have in him. It fully demonstrates the profound friendship between the two parties, the two countries, and the peoples of the two countries.

	Zhao Ziyang's entourage, including Wu Xueqian, Hong Xuezhi, and Zhu Liang, participated in the meeting.

	Present at the meeting were Korean party and state leaders, including Yon Hyong-muk, Pak Sung-chol, Kim Yong-nam, Choe Gwang, and others.

### 赵紫阳拜会金日成
周必忠
1989-04-24
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　赵紫阳拜会金日成
    本报平壤４月２４日电  记者周必忠报道：赵紫阳总书记今天下午６时半拜会了朝鲜劳动党中央委员会总书记金日成同志。
    赵紫阳同志首先转达了邓小平、杨尚昆、李鹏以及其他中共中央常委对金日成的问候，转达了邓颖超、彭真、万里对金日成的问候。
    赵紫阳说，他这次访问朝鲜，是因为中朝两党、两国有着非常友好的关系。他的目的主要是看望金日成主席和朝鲜劳动党的其他领导同志。另外，想亲眼看看自１９８１年访朝后的７年多以来，朝鲜人民在金日成主席领导下在社会主义建设中所取得的伟大成就。
    赵紫阳同志说，他一下火车就受到金日成主席、朝鲜劳动党的领导人和朝鲜人民深情的隆重的欢迎，这种深厚的情谊使我和我的同事们深受感动。对此，我再次表示感谢。
    金日成主席说，赵紫阳同志在担任中共中央总书记后第一个就访问朝鲜，我们非常高兴。赵紫阳总书记这次对朝鲜的访问，是对朝鲜人民的信赖。这充分证明了朝中两党、两国和两国人民的友谊是多么深厚。
    赵紫阳总书记的陪同人员：吴学谦、洪学智、朱良等参加了拜会。
    拜会时在座的朝鲜党和国家领导人有：延亨默、朴成哲、金永南、许锬、崔光等。
