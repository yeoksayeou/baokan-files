Title: Lush Greenery in Shihezi
Author: Fù Chāoyīng Yàlìkùn
Date: 1989-04-17
Edition: 2

Shihezi, a city located on the southern edge of the Gurbantunggut Desert in the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, was once a barren desert. Through the concerted efforts of agricultural reclamation workers over many years, the city has now achieved a forest coverage rate of 40%, ranking first in the country for urban greening.

### 石河子绿葱葱
傅超英　亚力坤
1989-04-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　石河子绿葱葱
    据新华社乌鲁木齐４月１７日电　（通讯员傅超英、记者亚力坤）坐落在古尔班通古特大沙漠南缘的新疆维吾尔自治区石河子市，昔日是戈壁荒滩，经农垦职工多年大力植树造林，目前，林木覆盖率已达４０％，居全国城市绿化首位。
