Title: Polish "Round Table" Consultative Committee Holds First Meeting
Author:
Date: 1989-04-19
Edition: 3

The Polish "Round Table" Consultative Committee, composed of representatives from various political and social forces that participated in the "Round Table" talks, held its first meeting today in Warsaw.

According to a report by the Polish Press Agency (PAP), the meeting was chaired by Polish Interior Minister Kiszczak and Solidarity Trade Union Chairman Lech Wałęsa.

The meeting discussed the working rules of the Consultative Committee and examined urgent measures that need to be taken. It was decided to establish two permanent working groups: the Economic and Social Policy Group and the Mediation Group.

According to the April 5 "Round Table" communiqué, the tasks of the Consultative Committee include evaluating the implementation of the "Round Table" resolutions, jointly seeking solutions to major domestic issues, and exercising mediation functions in cases of conflict.

At the conclusion of the meeting, Polish Council of State Chairman Wojciech Jaruzelski, Polish Sejm Chairman Czesław Kiszczak, and Prime Minister Mieczysław Rakowski met with Kiszczak, Wałęsa, and other members of the Consultative Committee.

### 波“圆桌会议”协商委员会举行首次会议　讨论工作规则及急待采取的措施

1989-04-19
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　波“圆桌会议”协商委员会举行首次会议
　　讨论工作规则及急待采取的措施
    新华社华沙４月１８日电　由参加“圆桌会议”的波兰各政治和社会力量代表商定成立的协商委员会今天在华沙举行第一次会议。
    据波通社报道，波兰内务部长基什查克和团结工会主席瓦文萨主持了会议。
    会议讨论了协商委员会的工作规则并研究了近期急待采取的措施，并决定成立两个常设工作小组：经济和社会政策问题小组和调解小组。
    根据４月５日公布的“圆桌会议”公报，协商委员会的任务是对“圆桌会议”决议贯彻和实施情况进行评价；共同寻求解决国内重大问题的办法，并在发生冲突的情况不行使调解的职能。
    会议结束时，波兰国务委员会主席雅鲁泽尔斯基、波兰议长马林诺夫斯基和政府总理拉科夫斯基等领导人会见了基什查克、瓦文萨等协商委员会成员。
