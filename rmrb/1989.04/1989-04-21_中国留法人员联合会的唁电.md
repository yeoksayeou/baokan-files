Title: Condolences from the Federation of Chinese Students and Scholars in France
Author:
Date: 1989-04-21
Edition: 3

The Federation of Chinese Students and Scholars in France sent a telegram of condolence to the office handling the funeral arrangements for Hu Yaobang at the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on April 20. The full text is as follows:

"Upon learning of the untimely passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang, we, the 3,000 Chinese students and scholars in France, are deeply grieved. Please convey our heartfelt condolences to his family. We will forever remember his indelible contributions to advancing China's modernization, democratization, and reform and opening-up. He will forever be respected and loved by all the people.

We will turn our grief into strength, study diligently, and work hard to comfort the spirit of Comrade Hu Yaobang and to contribute to the modernization of our motherland.

Comrade Hu Yaobang will live forever in our hearts!"

### 中国留法人员联合会的唁电

1989-04-21
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　中国留法人员联合会的唁电
    本报讯  中国留法人员联合会４月２０日给中共中央胡耀邦同志治丧办公室发来唁电，悼念胡耀邦同志逝世。全文如下：
    “惊悉耀邦同志不幸逝世，我们３０００留法人员不胜悲痛，请向耀邦同志的亲属转达我们最亲切的慰问。我们将永远牢记他为推进中国的现代化建设、民主化进程和改革开放事业所作的不可磨灭的丰功伟绩。他将永远受到全体人民的尊敬和爱戴。
    我们将化悲痛为力量，刻苦学习，努力工作，以告慰耀邦同志在天之灵，以报效祖国的现代化事业。
    胡耀邦同志永垂不朽！”
