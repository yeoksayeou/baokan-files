Title: Stone Railway Bureau Achieves 1000 Days of Safety
Author: Su Guangsheng
Date: 1989-04-14
Edition: 2

The Stone Railway Bureau, which handles 1/17 of the nation's total freight volume, has developed a "comprehensive management" scientific approach on its over 1000 kilometers of operational lines. This has basically brought safety in transportation under control. As of yesterday at 18:00, the bureau has achieved 1000 days of safe production without any major or large accidents.

### 石铁分局千日安全
苏广生
1989-04-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　石铁分局千日安全
    本报石家庄４月１３日电　占全国铁路总货运量１／１７的石家庄铁路分局，在其管辖的１０００多公里的运营线上探索出“综合治理”的科学管理方法，基本把安全运输工作纳入一个控制状态，截至昨天１８时，实现安全生产１０００天无重大、大事故的好成绩。　　　　　
　　　（苏广生）
