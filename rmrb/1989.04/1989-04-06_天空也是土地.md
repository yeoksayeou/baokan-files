Title: The Sky is Also Land
Author: Zai Geng
Date: 1989-04-06
Edition: 8

The sky is also land
The earth is yellow land
The sky is blue land
Yellow land has yellow rice, wheat, and red sorghum
Blue land has white cotton, peaches, and small sheep
Yellow land is occupied by footsteps, wheels, and the foundations of buildings
Blue land is occupied by bird wings, aircraft wings, and rising floors
Space is shrinking inch by inch
The world is expanding day by day
One day
Yahweh and Sakyamuni
Will no longer have a place to stand
The doorbell of heaven
Is held in human hands

### 天空也是土地
再耕
1989-04-06
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　天空也是土地
　　再耕
    天空也是土地
    大地是黄色的土地
    天空是蓝色的土地
    黄土地有黄稻麦红高粱
    蓝土地有白棉桃小绵羊
    黄土地被脚步、车轮、夯下的地基征用
    蓝土地被鸟翅、机翼、上升的楼层征用
    空间一寸寸缩小
    世界一天天扩大
    总有一天
    耶和华与释迦牟尼
    不再有立足之地
    天堂的门环
    握在人的手中
