Title: State Economic Restructuring Commission Awards Innovation Prizes
Author: Wú Shī Shēn
Date: 1989-04-13
Edition: 2

The State Economic Restructuring Commission (State Economic Restructuring Commission) awards the Innovation Prize

Anshan Iron and Steel Company and 13 other enterprises receive the "Wind Sail Cup"

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 12th - (Reporter Wu Shishen) Bidding and contracting, leasing operations, enterprise mergers, and enterprise groups, enterprise reform blossoms across the country. Fourteen enterprises that achieved remarkable results in enterprise reform last year were awarded the Wind Sail Cup Enterprise Reform Innovation Award by the State Economic Restructuring Commission today.

The 14 enterprises are: Anshan Iron and Steel Company, Dongfeng Automobile Industrial Joint Venture Company, Nanjing Chemical Industry Company, Beijing Light Automobile Limited Company, Shanghai Pengpu Machinery Factory, Harbin Insulation Materials Factory, Hebei Xuanhua Engineering Machinery Factory, Jiangsu Wuxi Radio Factory, Jilin Siping City Oil and Chemical Industry General Factory, Hunan Zhuzhou Spark Plug Factory, Hebei Baoding Chemical Factory, Wuhan Department Store, Qingdao Refrigerator General Factory, and Wuhan Gold Pen Factory.

The State Economic Restructuring Commission said that these enterprises have made outstanding contributions in deepening enterprise reform, improving and developing the enterprise contracting system, actively exploring, and daring to innovate. It is hoped that enterprises across the country will learn from the reform experience of these enterprises in combination with their actual situations, so that the ship of enterprise reform can hoist its sails and forge ahead.

### 国家体改委颁改革创新奖  鞍钢等十四家企业获“风帆杯”
吴士深
1989-04-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　国家体改委颁改革创新奖
　  鞍钢等十四家企业获“风帆杯”
    新华社北京4月12日电　（记者吴士深）招标承包、租赁经营、企业兼并、企业集团，企业改革朵朵春花开遍全国。去年企业改革卓有成效的14家企业，今天被国家体改委授予风帆杯企业改革创新奖。
    这14家企业是，鞍山钢铁公司、东风汽车工业联营公司、南京化学工业公司、北京轻型汽车有限公司、上海彭浦机器厂、哈尔滨绝缘材料厂、河北宣化工程机械厂、江苏无锡无线电厂、吉林四平市油脂化工总厂、湖南株洲火花塞厂、河北保定市电化厂、武汉商场、青岛电冰箱总厂、武汉金笔厂。
    国家体改委介绍说，这些企业在深化企业改革、完善和发展企业承包制中，积极探索，勇于创新，做出了突出贡献。希望各地企业结合实际学习这些企业的改革经验，让企业改革之船扬起风帆破浪前进。
