Title: A Special Day Designated by the State Council
Author:
Date: 1989-04-07
Edition: 1

    The State Council has designated a special day: April 14—China's "11 Billion Population Day." Citizen, have you developed a sense of population anxiety?

    According to calculations by the State Statistical Bureau, the population of mainland China will reach 1.1 billion on April 14 this year. The State Council has designated this day as China's "11 Billion Population Day." To further heighten the attention of the entire Party, the people of all nationalities across the country to the issue of population, the State Council has approved the holding of activities on April 14 this year to mark "11 Billion Population Day," aiming to enhance the public's sense of population anxiety.

### 国务院确定的一个特殊日子  ４月１４日——我国“１１亿人口日”  公民，你是否已经具有人口忧患意识？

1989-04-07
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　国务院确定的一个特殊日子
    ４月１４日——我国“１１亿人口日”
    公民，你是否已经具有人口忧患意识？
    本报北京４月６日讯  记者在国家计划生育委员会、国家统计局今天召开的新闻发布会上获悉：据国家统计局计算，今年４月１４日我国大陆人口将达到１１亿。国务院确定这一天为我国“１１亿人口日”。为了进一步引起全党、全国各族人民对国家人口问题的高度重视，经国务院批准，将在今年４月１４日举办“１１亿人口日”活动，以增强全民的人口忧患意识。
