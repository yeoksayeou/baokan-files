Title: The Women's Marathon World Cup Ends, China's Team Takes Third Place in the Team Event
Author:
Date: 1989-04-16
Edition: 4

The Women's Marathon World Cup concluded yesterday in Milan, Italy. China's team secured the third place in the team event.

According to foreign news reports, the 34-year-old American athlete, Hugh Machano, led the race from start to finish, finishing 4 minutes ahead of Japan's Misako Kanohara. Machano crossed the finish line with a time of 2 hours, 30 minutes, and 48 seconds, claiming the championship. Akemi Miyano took the second place. East Germany's Uta Pippig finished third with a time of 2 hours, 35 minutes, and 15 seconds.

Chinese athletes Su Juan, Li Yemei, and Xie Lihua finished in the seventh, ninth, and sixteenth places individually, with times of 2 hours, 36 minutes, and 55 seconds, 2 hours, 39 minutes, and 20 seconds, and 2 hours, 40 minutes, and 52 seconds, respectively.

The total time for the three Chinese athletes was 7 hours, 57 minutes, and 7 seconds, placing them third in the team event. The Soviet Union and the United States teams took the first and second places, respectively, with times of 7 hours, 51 minutes, and 29 seconds, and 7 hours, 56 minutes, and 17 seconds.

### 世界杯女子马拉松赛结束　中国队获团体第三名

1989-04-16
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　世界杯女子马拉松赛结束
　　中国队获团体第三名
    新华社北京４月１６日电　据外电报道，１９８９年世界杯女子马拉松赛昨天在意大利米兰举行，中国队获得团体第三名。
    ３４岁的美国选手休·马恰诺在这次比赛中一路领先，最后比日本的官原美佐子早４分钟冲过终点，以２小时３０分４８秒的成绩夺得冠军。宫野明美获得亚军。民主德国的乌塔·皮皮格以２小时３５分１５秒名列第三。
    中国选手粟娟、黎叶梅和谢丽华分获个人第七、九和十六名，成绩为2小时36分55秒、2小时39分20秒和2小时40分52秒。
    中国3名选手的总成绩为7小时57分07秒，列团体第三。获得团体第一、二名的分别为苏联队和美国队，成绩为7小时51分29秒和7小时56分17秒。
