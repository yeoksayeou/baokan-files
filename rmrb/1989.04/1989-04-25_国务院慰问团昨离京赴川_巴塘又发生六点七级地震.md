Title: State Council Delegation Leaves Beijing for Sichuan, 6.7 Magnitude Earthquake Hits Batang
Author: Luo Maocheng
Date: 1989-04-25
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 25th.

The State Council delegation today left Beijing to visit the earthquake and hailstorm-affected areas of Batang and Luzhou in Sichuan Province. The delegation is led by Minister of Civil Affairs Cui Naifu, with Vice Chairman of the State Ethnic Affairs Commission Chen Xin as the deputy leader.

It is reported that this delegation, composed of relevant departments of the State Council, will go deep into the disaster areas to fully understand the situation, comfort the people affected by the disaster, and will work with relevant departments of Sichuan Province to take measures to help the people in the disaster areas to restore production and rebuild their homes.

People's Daily, Chengdu, April 25th.

Reporter Luo Maocheng learned from the Sichuan Provincial Seismological Bureau that at 11:13 AM today, an earthquake of magnitude 6.7 struck the original earthquake zone in Batang. The telephone line between Yajiang and Litang has been cut off, and many houses in the earthquake zone have collapsed. Details are not yet clear.

### 国务院慰问团昨离京赴川  巴塘又发生六点七级地震
罗茂城
1989-04-25
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　国务院慰问团昨离京赴川  巴塘又发生六点七级地震
    新华社北京四月二十五日电  国务院慰问团今天离开北京，赴遭受地震和风雹灾害的四川省巴塘和泸州地区进行慰问。慰问团由民政部部长崔乃夫任团长，国家民委副主任陈欣任副团长。
    据悉，这个由国务院有关部委组成的慰问团，将深入灾区全面了解灾情，慰问受到灾害侵袭的群众，并将会同四川省有关部门一起，采取措施帮助灾区人民恢复生产，重建家园。
    本报成都四月二十五日电  记者罗茂城从四川省地震局获悉：今天上午十一点十三分在巴塘原震区再次发生六点七级地震。雅江至理塘电话中断，震区房屋倒塌不少，详情尚不清楚。
