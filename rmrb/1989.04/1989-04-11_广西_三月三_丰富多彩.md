Title: Guangxi's "Three March Three" is Rich and Colorful
Author: Zhu Sixiong
Date: 1989-04-11
Edition: 2

The integration of culture, tourism, and economic and trade activities has become a prominent feature of the third "Three March Three" Cultural Arts Festival of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, which concluded today in Guilin.

More than 2,200 ethnic minority artists from 13 cities and districts, including Fangchenggang, performed a rich and colorful array of folk songs and dances from the Zhuang, Yao, Dong, and other ethnic groups.

### 广西“三月三”丰富多彩
朱思雄
1989-04-11
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　广西“三月三”丰富多彩
    本报桂林３月１０日电　记者朱思雄报道：文化与旅游、经贸活动相结合，成为今天在此间闭幕的广西壮族自治区第三届“三月三”文化艺术节的显著特点。
    来自全区的１３个地市及防城港区的２２００名各族文艺工作者，表演了丰富多彩的壮、瑶、侗等民族的民间歌舞。
