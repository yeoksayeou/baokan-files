Title: China National Football Team Defeats Sichuan Team 3-0
Author: Yang Li
Date: 1989-04-16
Edition: 4

The Chinese national football team defeated the Sichuan team 3-0 in a friendly match this afternoon in the "Salt City" of Zigong.

The Sichuan team's coach noted that the national team's players showed significant improvement in stamina. Their central breakthroughs demonstrated considerable strength, and their tactical combinations were quite mature.

### 中国足球队以３∶０胜四川队
杨力
1989-04-16
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：

　　中国足球队以３∶０胜四川队
    新华社成都４月１４日电　（记者杨力）中国足球队今天下午在“盐都”自贡与四川队进行了一场友谊赛，结果，国家队以３∶０取胜。
    四川队教练认为，国家队队员体力有明显增强，中路突破显示出一定的实力，小战术配合也比较成熟。
