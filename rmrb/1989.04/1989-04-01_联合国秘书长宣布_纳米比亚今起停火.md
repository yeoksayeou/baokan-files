Title: UN Secretary-General Announces Ceasefire in Namibia

Author: Liú Kāichén

Date: 1989-04-01

Edition: 1

	The United Nations Secretary-General announced that Namibia will cease fire starting today.

		People's Daily, United Nations, March 30 (Reporter Liu KaiChen) The United Nations Secretary-General Perez de Cuellar announced today that the South African authorities and the South West African People's Organization have agreed to a ceasefire in Namibia starting at 4:00 AM (Greenwich Mean Time) on April 1.

		According to de Cuellar's report to the Security Council today, he wrote a letter to the South West African People's Organization and the South African government on March 14, proposing this ceasefire. Both sides responded on March 18 and 21, respectively, expressing their agreement.

		According to the Security Council's resolution on January 16 this year, the implementation of Security Council Resolution 435 on Namibia's independence is set to begin on April 1.

### 联合国秘书长宣布  纳米比亚今起停火
刘开宸
1989-04-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　联合国秘书长宣布
    纳米比亚今起停火
    本报联合国３月３０日电  记者刘开宸报道：联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔今天宣布，南非当局和西南非人民组织已经同意４月１日早晨４时起（格林威治时间）在纳米比亚正式停火。
    根据德奎利亚尔今日向安理会的报告，他在３月１４日写信给西南非人民组织和南非政府，提出此项建议，双方分别在３月１８日和２１日回信表示同意。
    根据安理会今年１月１６日决议，关于纳米比亚独立的安理会４３５号决议定于４月１日开始执行。
