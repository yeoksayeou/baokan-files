Title: International Swimming Competition to be Held in Shanghai
Author: Tan Jun
Date: 1989-04-27
Edition: 4

The "Jianpai Cup" International Swimming Invitational and National Swimming Championships will be held in Shanghai from May 1 to 6.

More than 300 athletes from Australia, Hong Kong, Japan, Pakistan, Singapore, Thailand, the United States, and China will participate in this competition.

This competition features a large number of participants and a high level of competition. Both Australia and Japan have sent their top athletes, and China, with the exception of Yang Wenyi, Lin Li, and Wang Xiaohong who are training in Canada and cannot attend, will have all its top athletes participating in the contest.

### 国际游泳赛将在上海举行
谭军
1989-04-27
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　国际游泳赛将在上海举行
    新华社上海４月２７日电（记者谭军）“健牌杯”国际游泳邀请赛暨全国游泳锦标赛将于５月１日至６日在上海举行。
    澳大利亚、香港、日本、巴基斯坦、新加坡、泰国、美国以及中国的３００多名选手将参加这次比赛。
    这次比赛人数多、规模大，有不少高水平的选手参加。澳大利亚、日本均派出最好的选手参赛，我国除杨文意、林莉、王小红在加拿大训练不能赴赛外，其余好手都将参加角逐。
