Title: Cuba Has Withdrawn 5,000 Troops from Angola
Author:
Date: 1989-04-21
Edition: 3

Cuba has withdrawn 5,000 troops from Angola.

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, April 20th - The Cuban government held a ceremony at Havana Airport on the evening of the 19th to welcome 145 Cuban soldiers returning from Angola.

So far, Cuba has withdrawn over 5,000 troops from Angola. According to the agreement signed last year in New York by Cuba, Angola, and South Africa, Cuba will gradually withdraw its 50,000 troops from Angola starting from April 1st of this year, to be completed within 27 months.

### 古巴已从安撤军五千

1989-04-21
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　古巴已从安撤军五千
    新华社哈瓦那４月２０日电  古巴政府１９日晚在哈瓦那机场举行仪式，欢迎从安哥拉撤回的１４５名古巴士兵。
    到目前为止，古巴已从安哥拉撤回官兵５０００余人。根据古巴、安哥拉和南非去年在纽约签署的古巴从安哥拉撤军协议，古巴从今年４月１日起在２７个月内将逐步撤走驻安哥拉的５万古巴军队。
