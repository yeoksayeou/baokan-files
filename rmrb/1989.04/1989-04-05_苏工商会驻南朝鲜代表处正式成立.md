Title: The Soviet Chamber of Commerce's Representative Office in South Korea Officially Established
Author:
Date: 1989-04-05
Edition: 4

The Soviet Chamber of Commerce's representative office in South Korea was officially established today in Seoul. The head of the representative office, Nazarov, stated that the bilateral relations between the Soviet Union and South Korea should be long-term and forward-looking.

The Soviet newspaper *Izvestia* reported this news this evening. Nazarov told the newspaper's reporter that the main tasks of the representative office are: to abandon the previous economic cooperation model of mainly exporting raw materials and shift towards joint research and application of advanced technologies and processes; to deepen economic relations to the level of enterprises and companies, rather than just at the departmental level.

### 苏工商会驻南朝鲜代表处正式成立

1989-04-05
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏工商会驻南朝鲜代表处正式成立
    据新华社莫斯科４月３日电  苏联工商会驻南朝鲜代表处今天在汉城正式成立。代表处主任纳扎罗夫说，苏联与南朝鲜的双边联系应具有长期性和着眼未来。
    苏联《消息报》今晚报道了这一消息。纳扎罗夫对该报记者说，代表处的主要任务是：放弃原先以出口原料为主的经济合作方式，转向联合研究和应用先进技术和工艺；使经济关系深入企业和公司一级，而不是在部门一级。
