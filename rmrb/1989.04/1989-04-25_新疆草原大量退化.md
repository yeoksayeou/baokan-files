Title: Large-scale Degeneration of Xinjiang Grasslands
Author:
Date: 1989-04-25
Edition: 2

According to Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, April 25th: According to the Xinjiang Daily, the grasslands of Xinjiang, known for their abundant water and grass, have seen their area decrease from 756 million mu in the early 1960s to 701 million mu today. Currently, the average yield of grass per unit area has decreased by nearly 30% compared to the 1960s.

### 新疆草原大量退化

1989-04-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　新疆草原大量退化
    据新华社乌鲁木齐４月２５日电  据《新疆日报》报道：以水草丰美著称的新疆草原，面积已由６０年代初的７．５６亿亩减少到现在的７．０１亿亩；目前草原的平均产草量较６０年代下降近３０％。
