Title: The Soviet Union Enacts Law on Subversion
Author:
Date: 1989-04-11
Edition: 3

The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR on April 9 adopted a decree stipulating that citizens who publicly call for the overthrow of the Soviet state and social system, incite ethnic and racial strife, advocate treason, or instigate terrorism may face up to three years in prison or a fine of 2,000 rubles. For those who use violence or cause serious consequences, or who are directed by foreign organizations, the penalty will be increased, with a maximum sentence of 10 years in prison and a fine of up to 5,000 rubles.

### 苏颁布颠覆罪法令

1989-04-11
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏颁布颠覆罪法令
    新华社莫斯科４月９日电　苏联最高苏维埃主席团４月９日通过的一项法令规定，公民公开号召颠覆苏维埃国家和社会制度，制造民族和种族纠纷，号召叛国和制造恐怖，最多可判处３年徒刑，或课以２０００卢布罚款；公民使用暴力或造成严重后果，受外国组织指使的，要加重处罚，最多可判处１０年徒刑，罚款最高额为５０００卢布。
