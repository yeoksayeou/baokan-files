Title: Beijing Electron-Positron Collider Produces Synchrotron Radiation for the First Time
Author:
Date: 1989-04-29
Edition: 1

The synchrotron radiation application room of the Beijing Electron-Positron Collider successfully produced synchrotron radiation for the first time at 21:29 on April 3. A bright visible light spot of synchrotron radiation was observed, and X-ray synchrotron radiation was also detected in the early hours of April 4. This marks the first time that visible light and X-ray synchrotron radiation have been produced on a domestic device in China.

Currently, the giant spectrometer detector of the Beijing Electron-Positron Collider has been moved to the electron-positron collision point, preparing for joint debugging and high-energy physics experiments.

### 北京正负电子对撞机首次产生同步辐射光

1989-04-29
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　北京正负电子对撞机首次产生同步辐射光
    新华社北京４月２９日电  北京正负电子对撞机同步辐射光应用室于４月３日２１时２９分首次调试出同步辐射光，观察到明亮的同步辐射可见光光斑，４月４日凌晨又观测到Ｘ光波段的同步辐射光。这是我国首次在自己的装置上调试出可见光和Ｘ光波段的同步辐射光。
    目前，北京正负电子对撞机的巨型探测器谱仪已推入正负电子对撞点，准备联调并进行高能物理实验。
