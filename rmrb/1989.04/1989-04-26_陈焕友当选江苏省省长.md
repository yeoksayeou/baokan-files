Title: Chen Huanyou Elected Governor of Jiangsu Province
Author: Gong Yongquan
Date: 1989-04-26
Edition: 1

Chen Huanyou was elected as the Governor of Jiangsu Province today at the conclusion of the second session of the Seventh Jiangsu Provincial People's Congress. Chen Huanyou, who had been serving as acting governor for just 11 days, was elected to the position. Chen Huanyou is 55 years old and has a university education.

### 陈焕友当选江苏省省长
龚永泉
1989-04-26
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　陈焕友当选江苏省省长
    本报南京４月２６日电  记者龚永泉报道：在今天结束的江苏省第七届人民代表大会第二次会议上，刚当了１１天代理省长的陈焕友当选为江苏省省长。陈焕友今年５５岁，大学文化水平。
