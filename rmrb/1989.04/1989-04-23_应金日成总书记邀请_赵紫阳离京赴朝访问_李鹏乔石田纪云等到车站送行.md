Title: At the Invitation of Kim Il Sung, Zhao Ziyang Leaves Beijing for a Visit to North Korea
Author: Sun Yi
Date: 1989-04-23
Edition: 1

At the invitation of Kim Il Sung, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, Zhao Ziyang, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, left Beijing by special train this afternoon to visit the Democratic People's Republic of Korea for a formal friendly visit. This is Zhao Ziyang's first overseas visit since he officially took office as General Secretary in November 1987.

The main accompanying personnel for Zhao Ziyang's visit are: Wu Xueqian, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, Hong Xuezhi, Deputy Secretary of the Central Military Commission, and Zhu Liang, Member of the Central Committee and Director of the International Liaison Department.

Those who went to Beijing Railway Station to see him off include: Li Peng, Member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Premier of the State Council, Qiao Shi, Member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, Tian Jiyun, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, Wen Jiabao, Candidate Member of the Secretariat of the Central Committee and Director of the General Office of the Central Committee, Chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army, Der Hongtian, Minister of Railways Li Senmao, and Vice Minister of the International Liaison Department Li Shucen.

The Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, Bai Rongzhen, also went to the station to see him off.

Zhao Ziyang leaves Beijing by train on April 23 to visit North Korea.

Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Guo Zhenying.

### 应金日成总书记邀请  赵紫阳离京赴朝访问  李鹏乔石田纪云等到车站送行
孙毅
1989-04-23
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　应金日成总书记邀请    
    赵紫阳离京赴朝访问    
    李鹏乔石田纪云等到车站送行
    本报北京４月２３日讯  记者孙毅报道：应朝鲜劳动党中央总书记金日成同志的邀请，中共中央总书记赵紫阳今天下午乘专列离开北京，前往朝鲜民主主义人民共和国进行正式友好访问。这是赵紫阳同志自１９８７年１１月正式就任总书记以来首次出国访问。
    陪同赵紫阳总书记访问的主要随行人员是：中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理吴学谦，中共中央军委副秘书长洪学智，中共中央委员、中联部部长朱良等。
    前往北京车站送行的有：中共中央政治局常委、国务院总理李鹏，中共中央政治局常委乔石，中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理田纪云，中共中央书记处候补书记、中央办公厅主任温家宝，总参谋长迟浩田，铁道部部长李森茂，中联部副部长李淑铮等。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办裴溶在也到车站送行。（附图片）
    赵紫阳４月２３日乘火车离开北京前往朝鲜访问。
    新华社记者　郭占英摄
