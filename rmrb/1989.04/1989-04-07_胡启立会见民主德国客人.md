Title: Hu Qili Meets with Guests from the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1989-04-07
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 6th.

Hu Qili, a member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, met with Herbert Naumann, editor-in-chief of the *Neues Deutschland* newspaper and a member of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and his entourage on April 6 in Zhongnanhai. Hu Qili briefed them on the current political and economic situation in China.

Naumann conveyed greetings from Erich Honecker, the General Secretary of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, to the leaders of the Communist Party of China. Chen Liren, president of the *People's Daily*, was present at the meeting.

### 胡启立会见民主德国客人

1989-04-07
第3版(要闻)
专栏：

　　胡启立会见民主德国客人
    新华社北京４月６日电中共中央政治局常委胡启立４月６日在中南海会见了德意志民主共和国《新德意志报》总编辑、德国统一社会党中央委员赫伯特·瑙曼一行，向他们介绍了中国当前的政治经济情况。
    瑙曼转达了德国统一社会党总书记昂纳克对中国共产党的领导人的问候。《人民日报》社社长钱李仁会见时在座。
