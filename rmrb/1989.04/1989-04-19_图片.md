Title: Water Lips Bridge Completed
Author: Xuyuan Sheng
Date: 1989-04-19
Edition: 5

The Water Lips Bridge, constructed with a collective investment of 2.8 million yuan by the people of Water Lips Town, Luhe County, Guangdong Province, has recently been completed.

Over the past decade of reform and opening up, Guangdong Province has utilized funds totaling 8.124 billion yuan, raised through collective investments, loans, and donations from overseas Chinese, for the construction of highways and bridges. Among these projects, 1,195 new bridges have been built.
