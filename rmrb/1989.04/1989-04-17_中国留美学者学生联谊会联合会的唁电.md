Title: Condolence Telegram from the Federation of Chinese Students and Scholars in the United States
Author:
Date: 1989-04-17
Edition: 3

The Federation of Chinese Students and Scholars in the United States sent a telegram to this newspaper today, expressing deep sorrow over the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

Upon learning of the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang due to illness, we, the entire community of Chinese scholars and students in the United States, are deeply grieved. Comrade Hu Yaobang's life was one of integrity and unwavering dedication to the people. Please convey our heartfelt condolences to Comrade Hu Yaobang's family.

Federation of Chinese Students and Scholars in the United States
April 16, 1989

### 中国留美学者学生联谊会联合会的唁电

1989-04-17
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　中国留美学者学生联谊会联合会的唁电
    本报华盛顿４月１６日电　中国留美学者学生联谊会联合会今天致函本报，沉痛悼念胡耀邦同志病逝。信件全文如下：
    惊悉耀邦同志因病逝世，我全体留美学人深感悲痛。耀邦同志一生光明磊落，对人民忠心耿耿。请贵报向耀邦同志的家属转达我们的深切悼念。
　　　　　　　中华人民共和国留美学者学生联谊会联合会
　　　　　　１９８９年４月１６日
