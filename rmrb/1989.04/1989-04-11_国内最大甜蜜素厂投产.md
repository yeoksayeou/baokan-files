Title: Largest Domestic Sweetener Factory Begins Production
Author:
Date: 1989-04-11
Edition: 2

The largest domestic sweetener factory, designed and built by China, has recently begun production in Gan Nan County, Heilongjiang Province.

Sweetener is a high-quality artificial sweetener with a sweetness 50 times that of sugar. Its characteristics include being sweet without calories, and it does not cause weight gain after consumption, making it particularly suitable for diabetics and those with obesity. This factory uses domestically advanced production equipment and has an annual capacity of 300 tons of sweetener.

### 国内最大甜蜜素厂投产

1989-04-11
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　国内最大甜蜜素厂投产
    据新华社哈尔滨４月９日电　我国自行设计的最大的甜蜜素厂日前在黑龙江省甘南县建成投产。
    甜蜜素是一种人工合成的优质甜味制品，比沙糖甜度高５０倍，其特点是：既甜又无热能，食用后不使人发胖，因而特别适于糖尿病、肥胖症患者食用。这家甜蜜素厂采用国内先进的生产设备，可年产甜蜜素３００吨。
