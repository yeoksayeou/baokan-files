Title: The Full Tang Poetry Quick Check System Completed
Author: Huang Chuanfang
Date: 1989-04-26
Edition: 2

    The Full Tang Poetry Quick Check System Completed

    Our reporter informs that with just one character, one word, or half a line of poetry, you can accurately find the full text of a Tang poem within approximately 2 minutes, and it will display the location in the Full Tang Poetry collection, including the volume, chapter, page, and line. The Institute of Literature at the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences recently successfully developed the Full Tang Poetry Quick Check System, demonstrating this unique function.

    This database stores 53,035 poems by 3,276 authors from the Tang Dynasty over a span of 300 years, totaling 946 volumes and 3,432,869 characters. It is more accurate and reliable than existing similar systems. The linguist Qian Zhongshu praised it as "an important contribution of artificial intelligence to the study of classical Chinese literature."

### 《全唐诗》速检系统制成
黄传芳
1989-04-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　《全唐诗》速检系统制成
    本报讯  仅凭一个字、一个词或半句诗，在大约２分钟内便准确告知你一首唐诗的全文，并显示其所在《全唐诗》中的册、卷、页、行。中国社会科学院文学研究所计算机室最近研制成功的《全唐诗》速检系统，向人们展示了这一奇特功能。
    这个数据库储存了唐代３００年间３２７６位作者的５３０３５首诗歌，共９４６卷、３４３２８６９字，比已有的同类系统更具准确性和可靠性。语言学大师钱钟书称赞“这是人工智能在中国古典文学研究上的重要贡献”。　
               　　　　　　　　（黄传芳）
