Title: Super Marathon
Author: Guo Ji
Date: 1989-04-08
Edition: 4

At the end of February, a 24-hour super marathon was held at the Milton Sports Stadium in the UK. New Zealand's Dick Tott created a new world record with a distance of 261 kilometers, surpassing the previous record holder, Greek Yannis Kouris, by 10 kilometers. The women's champion was Britain's Elena Adams, who ran 227 kilometers, 2 kilometers more than her record from the previous year. After the race, one athlete said, "We are physically and mentally exhausted!"

### 超级马拉松
郭济
1989-04-08
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：五光十色

　　超级马拉松
    ２月底在英国密尔顿体育馆举行的２４小时超级马拉松跑的比赛上，新西兰的迪克·托特以２６１公里的成绩，创造了新的世界纪录。他比原纪录保持者、希腊的雅尼斯·考罗斯多跑了１０公里。女子是英国的埃勒纳·阿达姆斯以２２７公里取得了冠军，比她去年的纪录多了２公里。赛后，一位运动员说：“我们在体力上和精神上都完蛋了！”　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　郭济
