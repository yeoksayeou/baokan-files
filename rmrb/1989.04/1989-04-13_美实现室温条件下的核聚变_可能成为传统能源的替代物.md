Title: U.S. Achieves Nuclear Fusion at Room Temperature
Author:
Date: 1989-04-13
Edition: 3

    U.S. Achieves Nuclear Fusion at Room Temperature
    May Become a Substitute for Traditional Energy Sources

    According to foreign news reports, researchers at Texas A&M University have achieved nuclear fusion at room temperature in an experiment, replicating the results obtained by researchers at the University of Utah last month.

    On March 23, researchers at the University of Utah announced that they had achieved nuclear fusion at room temperature. The two researchers are Stanley Pons of the United States and Martin Fleischmann of the University of Southampton in the United Kingdom.

    Scientists believe that achieving nuclear fusion at room temperature could potentially become a substitute for traditional energy sources, providing a clean, inexpensive, and virtually inexhaustible energy source.

    Nuclear fusion typically involves combining atoms at temperatures of millions of degrees to produce energy, while nuclear power plants generate energy through the fission of atoms. Nuclear fusion produces far less waste than nuclear fission. The researchers in Utah used a 15-centimeter test tube to achieve fusion energy at room temperature. The energy produced by the experiment was three times the energy consumed by the experiment.

### 美实现室温条件下的核聚变　可能成为传统能源的替代物

1989-04-13
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美实现室温条件下的核聚变
　　可能成为传统能源的替代物
    本报讯　据外电报道，美国得克萨斯州农业和机械大学的研究员在一项实验中，实现了室温条件下的核聚变，再一次获得了犹他大学研究员上个月取得的同样成果。
    3月23日，美国犹他大学的研究人员曾经宣布，他们在室温条件下，达到了核聚变。这2名研究人员是美国的斯坦来和英国南安普敦大学的马丁·弗来希曼。
  科学家们认为，在室温条件下实现核聚变有可能成为传统能源的替代物，成为干净、廉价、几乎取之不尽的能源。
    核聚变通常是通过百万度的高温使原子聚合在一起来获得能源，而核电站是通过原子的裂变取得能源的。核聚变产生的废料比核裂变产生的废料少得多。犹他州的研究人员是用一个15厘米的试管在室温条件下获得聚变能源的。实验产生的能量比实验消耗的能量多3倍。（文）
