Title: Desktop Publishing System Launched
Author: Lǐ Chángyún
Date: 1989-04-25
Edition: 2

The third generation of office text processing equipment, the bilingual desktop publishing system, was launched here yesterday.

Developed jointly by the Beijing Sanhai De Real Office Equipment Company and the Chinese Printing Science and Technology Research Institute, this system eliminates the cumbersome, unattractive, and complex control code operation methods, making it simple and practical.

### 桌面印刷系统问世
李长云
1989-04-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　桌面印刷系统问世
    本报北京四月二十五日讯  继机械打字机、电脑打字机之后的第三代办公室文字处理设备——中西文桌面印刷系统，昨天在此间推出。
    由北京三海得实文处理设备公司、中国印刷科技研究所联合开发的这套系统，废弃了难记、难看、复杂的控制码操作方法，简便实用。　
　　（李长云）
