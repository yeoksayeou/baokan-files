Title: The Inter-Continental Women's Field Hockey Championship to Be Held in India
Author:
Date: 1989-04-25
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 25th.

The third Inter-Continental Women's Field Hockey Championship will be held from November 1st to November 11th in New Delhi, the capital of India.

M. A. Damania, the chairman of the Indian Women's Field Hockey Association, told reporters on the 24th that 12 national and regional field hockey teams will participate in this championship. The 12 teams will be divided into two groups for the first round of matches.

### 洲际女子曲棍球锦标赛将在印举行

1989-04-25
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：

　　洲际女子曲棍球锦标赛将在印举行
    新华社新德里４月２５日电  第三届洲际女子曲棍球锦标赛将于今年１１月１日至１１日在印度首都新德里举行。
    印度女子曲棍球协会主席姆·阿·达马尼阿２４日对记者说，１２个国家和地区的曲棍球队将参加这届锦标赛。１２个队将分为两个小组进行第一阶段的比赛。
