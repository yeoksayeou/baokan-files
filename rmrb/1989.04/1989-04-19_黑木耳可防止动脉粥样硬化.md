Title: Black Fungus Can Prevent Arteriosclerosis
Author: Hao Qing
Date: 1989-04-19
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 18th.

Researchers at Beijing Anzhen Hospital have discovered that black fungus has the effect of lowering blood lipids and blood platelet aggregation, and can prevent arteriosclerosis.

### 黑木耳可防止动脉粥样硬化
郝庆
1989-04-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　黑木耳可防止动脉粥样硬化
    新华社北京４月１８日电　（记者郝庆）北京安贞医院研究人员发现，黑木耳有降低血脂和血小板凝聚、防止动脉粥样硬化的作用。
