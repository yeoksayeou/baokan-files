Title: Beach
Author: Liao Yuguang
Date: 1989-04-08
Edition: 8

	Beach
	Liao Yuguang

	Seeds of affection
	From the bosom of the vast sea
	Scattered along the long coastline
	Sun umbrellas and swimsuits
	Form a dazzling
	Harvest season
	The elderly bend down to pick up
	Childhood dreams
	Children skipping and jumping
	Turn dreams into reality
