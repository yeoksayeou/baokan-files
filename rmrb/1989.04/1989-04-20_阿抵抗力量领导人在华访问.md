Title: Leader of Afghan Resistance Forces Visits China
Author:
Date: 1989-04-20
Edition: 2

Afghan resistance leader visits China

New China News Agency, Beijing, April 20

Foreign Ministry spokesman Jin Guihua said today that Gulbuddin, as a leader of the Afghan resistance forces, visited China from April 19 to 20 for a friendly visit.

Jin Guihua said at the press conference this afternoon in response to reporters' questions that during Gulbuddin's visit to China, the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs gave a friendly reception, and Chairman Han Nianlong met and hosted a banquet for Gulbuddin and his entourage. Foreign Minister Qian Qichen and Vice Foreign Minister Liu Shuzhi met with Gulbuddin and his entourage.

The spokesman reiterated in response to the question of whether China would recognize the provisional government of the Afghan resistance forces that China hopes to establish a broad-based and acceptable joint government in Afghanistan to achieve peace as soon as possible, restore Afghanistan's independence, neutrality, and non-aligned status, and enable Afghan refugees to return to their homes safely and with dignity.

### 阿抵抗力量领导人在华访问

1989-04-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　阿抵抗力量领导人在华访问
    新华社北京４月２０日电  外交部发言人金桂华今天说，古尔布丁作为阿富汗抵抗力量的一位领导人于本月１９日至２０日在中国进行友好访问。
    金桂华在下午的新闻发布会上回答记者提问时说，古尔布丁访华期间，中国人民外交学会给予友好接待，韩念龙会长会见并宴请了古尔布丁一行。钱其琛外长、刘述卿副外长分别会见了古尔布丁一行。
    发言人在回答有关中国是否将承认阿富汗抵抗力量临时政府的问题时重申，中国希望在阿富汗建立一个基础广泛、能为各方所接受的联合政府，以早日实现和平，恢复阿富汗的独立、中立、不结盟地位，使阿富汗难民安全体面地返回家园。
