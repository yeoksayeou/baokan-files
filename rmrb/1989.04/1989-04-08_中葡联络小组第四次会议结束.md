Title: The Fourth Meeting of the Sino-Portuguese Liaison Group Concludes
Author:
Date: 1989-04-08
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 7th.

The fourth meeting of the Sino-Portuguese Joint Liaison Group was held in Lisbon from April 3rd to 6th.

According to a news release issued today, “In accordance with the spirit of cooperation and mutual understanding outlined in the Sino-Portuguese Joint Declaration on the Question of Macau, both sides reached agreements on three issues during this meeting: language, civil servants, and legal matters during the transitional period in Macau. They also exchanged views on other issues.” Both sides agreed that the fifth meeting of the Sino-Portuguese Joint Liaison Group will commence on July 31st in Macau.

The meeting was conducted in a friendly and cooperative atmosphere.

### 中葡联络小组第四次会议结束

1989-04-08
第3版(要闻)
专栏：

　　中葡联络小组第四次会议结束
    本报北京４月７日讯  中葡联合联络小组第四次会议于４月３日至６日在里斯本举行。
    据今天发表的新闻公报说，“根据中葡关于澳门问题联合声明的合作和互相谅解精神，双方在本次会议上就澳门过渡时期的语文、公务员和法律等三项议题达成了协议。还就其它问题交换了看法”。双方商定中葡联合联络小组第五次会议将于７月３１日开始在澳门举行。
    会议是在友好合作的气氛中进行的。
