Title: Romania Recalls Ambassador to Britain to Protest Anti-Romania Policy
Author: Chen Te'an
Date: 1989-04-06
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, April 5th.

	Romania has recalled its ambassador to London to protest Britain's "anti-Romania" policy.

	In a note handed to the British Foreign Office, Ambassador Stan Sorli expressed the Romanian government's indignation at Britain's criticism of Romania's human rights record. The note condemned Britain for pursuing a policy "against Romania."

	Official sources at the Foreign Office believe that Romania's displeasure stems from Britain's demand that Romania respect the new human rights standards published in the Vienna Final Act. Additionally, the Romanian government believes that some British and Hungarian individuals are conspiring to interfere with their territorial sovereignty.

	According to well-informed British officials, there is currently no indication that it is necessary to recall the British ambassador to Romania as a retaliatory measure.

### 抗议英国反罗政策  罗召回驻英大使
陈特安
1989-04-06
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　抗议英国反罗政策
    罗召回驻英大使
    本报伦敦４月５日电  记者陈特安报道：罗马尼亚昨天已召回其驻伦敦大使，以抗议英国实行“反对罗马尼亚”的政策。
    斯坦·索里大使在他递交给英国外交部的一份照会中，表示了罗马尼亚政府对英国指责罗马尼亚人权记录的愤慨。照会谴责英国正在实行一项“反对罗马尼亚”的政策。
    白厅官方人士认为，罗马尼亚政府的不快是由于英国要求罗马尼亚尊重维也纳最后声明公布的新人权准则；同时，罗马尼亚政府认为，有一些英国人和匈牙利人阴谋干涉他们的国土主权。
    据英国官方消息灵通人士说，现在看不出有召回英国驻罗马尼亚大使作为报复的必要。
