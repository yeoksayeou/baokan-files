Title: The Organizing Committee for the Beijing International Marathon is Established
Author: Zhou Jianchun
Date: 1989-04-02
Edition: 3

The 1989 Beijing International Marathon will be held on October 15th in Beijing. The organizing committee has been officially established. The deputy director of the State Sports Commission and the president of the Chinese Athletics Association, He Zhenliang, will serve as the committee's chairman. The honorary vice president of the Asian Athletics Association, Li Wenyao, will serve as the executive director of the committee.

This year's marathon will feature a women's event for the first time. Invitations have already been sent to outstanding athletes from 38 countries.

### 北京国际马拉松赛组委会成立
周建春
1989-04-02
第3版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

　　北京国际马拉松赛组委会成立
    本报讯  一九八九年北京国际马拉松赛，将于今年十月十五日在北京举行，日前组委会正式成立，国家体委副主任、中国田径协会主席何振梁任组委会主任，亚洲田径联合会名誉副主席李文耀任组委会执行主任。
    本届马拉松赛将首次增设女子项目。现已向三十八个国家的优秀选手发出了邀请。　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（周建春）
