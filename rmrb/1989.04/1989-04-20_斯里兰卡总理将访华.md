Title: Sri Lankan Prime Minister to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1989-04-20
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 20th.

The spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Jin Guihua, announced today at a press conference that the Prime Minister of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, Ranasinghe Premadasa, will visit China before attending the Asian Development Bank's annual meeting in Beijing in early May. The visit is scheduled from April 30th to May 3rd.

### 斯里兰卡总理将访华

1989-04-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　斯里兰卡总理将访华
    新华社北京４月２０日电  外交部发言人金桂华今天在新闻发布会上宣布，斯里兰卡民主社会主义共和国总理维杰通加在出席５月上旬在北京召开的亚行年会前，将于４月３０日至５月３日顺访中国。
