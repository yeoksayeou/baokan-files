Title: "Today's Soviet Union" Published in Beijing
Author: Lai Renqiong
Date: 1989-04-28
Edition: 4

The book series "Today's Soviet Union" held its inaugural release ceremony on April 26 in Beijing. The series consists of 10 volumes, each covering political, diplomatic, economic, technological, educational, cultural, sports, natural scenery, and daily life aspects of Soviet society during the reform process. It opens a window for the Chinese people to understand Soviet society.

The Soviet Ambassador to China, O. Troyanovskiy, attended the ceremony and delivered a passionate speech.

### 《今日苏联》在京发行
赖仁琼
1989-04-28
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　《今日苏联》在京发行
    《今日苏联》丛书４月２６日在京举行首发仪式。丛书共１０册，分别从政治、外交、经济、科技、教育、文化、体育和自然风情、人民生活等方面反映了改革进程中的苏联社会全貌。它向我国人民打开了一个了解苏联社会的窗口。
    苏联驻华大使奥·特罗扬诺夫斯基出席仪式并发表了热情洋溢的讲话。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（赖仁琼）
