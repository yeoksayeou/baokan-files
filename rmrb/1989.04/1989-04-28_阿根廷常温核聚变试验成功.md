Title: Argentina's Cold Fusion Experiment Succeeds
Author:
Date: 1989-04-28
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Buenos Aires, April 27th.

Argentina's National Atomic Energy Commission announced on April 27th that Argentina has achieved success in its research on cold fusion experiments.

In this experiment, scientists at the Bariloche Atomic Energy Research Center in Argentina used a new technology to control and measure the cold fusion phenomenon.

On March 23rd, two scientists from the United Kingdom and the United States first announced to the world that they had successfully conducted cold fusion experiments at room temperature.

### 阿根廷常温核聚变试验成功

1989-04-28
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　阿根廷常温核聚变试验成功
    新华社布宜诺斯艾利斯４月２７日电  阿根廷国家原子能委员会２７日宣布，阿根廷在研究常温核聚变试验方面已经取得成功。
    在这项试验中，阿根廷巴里罗切原子能研究中心的科学家们采用一项新技术以控制测量常温核聚变现象。
    ３月２３日，英国和美国的两位科学家在世界上首先宣布在常温状态下进行核聚变试验成功。
