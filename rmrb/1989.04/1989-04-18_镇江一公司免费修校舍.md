Title: A Company in Zhenjiang Provides Free School Building Repairs
Author:
Date: 1989-04-18
Edition: 2

A construction company in Zhenjiang City, Jiangsu Province, has taken the initiative to allocate 500,000 yuan to repair school buildings for 29 primary and secondary schools free of charge. So far, over 1,300 square meters have been repaired, and the second phase of repairs began in mid-April. The company plans to allocate a portion of its fixed funds annually to expand the free repair services to all primary and secondary schools in the city and its affiliated counties.

### 镇江一公司免费修校舍

1989-04-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　镇江一公司免费修校舍
    本报南京４月１８日电　江苏省镇江市建筑防水施工公司主动拿出５万元，为２９所中小学免费维修校舍。现已维修１３００多平方米，第二批维修工作已于本月中旬开始。该公司今后每年还将拿出一部分固定资金，把免费维修扩大到全市及市属各县的中小学。
