Title: UK Warns West Germany Not to Harm NATO Defense
Author: Xue Yongxing
Date: 1989-04-26
Edition: 3

	British Prime Minister Margaret Thatcher today warned West Germany not to jeopardize unity among NATO countries or damage NATO's defense strategy over the issue of short-range nuclear weapons.

	She said, "No single country can decide NATO's strategy." She added that NATO's combined conventional and nuclear defense strategy has maintained peace in Europe for 40 years.

	There has been a recent public and serious disagreement among NATO countries over the issue of short-range nuclear weapons. West Germany and Belgium advocate negotiating with the Soviet Union to reduce these types of nuclear weapons, while the United States and Britain insist on retaining and modernizing these short-range nuclear missiles.

	According to British newspapers, U.S. President George H.W. Bush and Thatcher held a 20-minute telephone conversation on April 22 regarding NATO's short-range nuclear weapons. Both sides agreed that NATO's plan to modernize these weapons by the mid-1990s is non-negotiable.

	Thatcher will meet with the Italian Prime Minister and the Dutch Prime Minister in London this weekend. On April 30, she will fly to West Germany to further consult with Chancellor Helmut Kohl.

### 英就短导警告西德不要损害北约防务
薛永兴
1989-04-26
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　英就短导警告西德不要损害北约防务
    新华社伦敦４月２５日电（记者薛永兴）英国首相撒切尔夫人今天下午在议会发表讲话，警告西德不要在短程核武器问题上影响与北约国家之间的团结和损害北约防务战略。
    她说：“任何一个国家都决定不了北约的战略。”她还说，北约的常规武器和核武器混合防务战略已经使欧洲保持了４０年的和平。
    在北约短程核武器问题上，北约国家之间最近发生了公开的严重分歧。西德和比利时主张与苏联谈判削减这类核武器，而美国和英国则坚持保留和更新这些短程核导弹。
    据英国报纸报道，美国总统布什和撒切尔夫人２２日就北约短程核武器问题进行了２０分钟的电话交谈。双方一致认为，北约关于在９０年代中期对此类武器实现现代化的计划是不容谈判的。
    撒切尔夫人本周末将在伦敦与意大利总理和荷兰首相举行会谈。３０日，她将飞往西德，与科尔总理进一步磋商。
