Title: In Memory of Hu Yaobang
Author: Gōng Liú
Date: 1989-04-22
Edition: 6

	You loved the people
	And regarded them
	As a precious jade
	Caressing
	Caressing
	85% smooth or rugged
	Left
	Warmth and trembling
	The people also regarded you as a precious jade
	Perhaps,
	Someone whispered
	Pointing frequently
	At your body—
	That less than 15% of the part...
	After all,
	Flaws do not conceal virtues,
	I think
	These two jades
	Must
	Not be broken
	Must not be broken

		1989.4.15, Hangzhou, hearing the bad news with shock

### 哭胡耀邦
公刘
1989-04-22
第6版(文学作品)
专栏：

　　哭胡耀邦
    公刘
    您爱人民
    把它
    看作一块玉
    摩挲
    摩挲
    ８５％的平坦或者崎岖
    留下了
    战栗的温煦
    人民把您也看作一块玉
    可能吧，
    什么人窃窃私语
    频频指点
    您的身体——
    那不足１５％的部位……
    毕竟
    瑕不掩瑜，
    我想
    这两块玉
    肯定
    都不会碎
    都不能碎
    　　　　　　　　１９８９．４．１５，杭州，惊闻噩耗
