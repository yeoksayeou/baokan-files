Title: Soviet Astronauts Return to Earth
Author:
Date: 1989-04-28
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 27th.

Soviet astronauts Volkov, Krikalev, and Polyakov, aboard the descent module of the "Soyuz TM-7" spacecraft, achieved a soft landing northeast of Dzhezkazgan in Kazakhstan at 6:59 Moscow time today. This was the fourth main crew to work on the "Mir" space station.

The "Mir" space station, launched into space in February 1986, is the third-generation Soviet space station. In April 1987, the astrophysics experiment module "Kvant" was launched and docked with it. The "Mir" space station, along with the manned "Soyuz TM" spacecraft and the cargo spacecraft "Progress," and the "Kvant" experiment module, form a combined orbital structure that is 33 meters long and weighs over 50 tons. Two new experiment modules will be docked with the space station in the latter half of this year.

The "Mir" space station has been in a manned state since February 1987, with four main crews and four temporary crews having worked there. Given that the two new experiment modules will not be launched until the latter half of the year, the Soviet Union has decided not to send any crews to the space station for the time being. The "Mir" will be in an unmanned state until mid-August.

### 苏宇航员返回地面

1989-04-28
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏宇航员返回地面
    新华社莫斯科４月２７日电  今天莫斯科时间６时５９分，苏联宇航员沃尔科夫、克里卡廖夫和波利亚科夫，乘坐“联盟ＴＭ—７”号飞船的降落装置在苏联哈萨克杰兹卡兹甘市东北部实现软着陆。这是在“和平”号轨道站上工作过的第４个基本乘员组。
    “和平”号轨道站是１９８６年２月发射到太空的苏联第三代轨道站。１９８７年４月发射的天文物理实验舱——“量子”号实验舱与其对接。“和平”号同“联盟ＴＭ”号载人飞船和货运飞船“进步”号，加上“量子”号实验舱组成的轨道联合体，总长３３米，重５０多吨。今年下半年还将有两个新实验舱与轨道站对接。
    “和平”号轨道站自１９８７年２月以来一直处于有人驾驶状态，先后有４个基本乘员组和４个临时乘员组在那里工作过。鉴于两个新实验舱要到下半年才能发射上天，苏联决定暂不派乘员组飞往轨道站，“和平”号在８月中旬前将处于无人驾驶状态。
