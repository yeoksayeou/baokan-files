Title: 38 Major Cities in China Have Established Product Quality Tracking Stations
Author: Lǐ Xiǎolín
Date: 1989-04-16
Edition: 1

	38 major cities in China have established 46 product quality tracking stations. According to Xinhua News Agency in Dalian on April 15th, a reporter learned from the national product quality tracking station work meeting held in Dalian that these tracking stations have played a good role in safeguarding the interests of consumers and supervising product quality.

	According to statistics from 25 tracking stations, nearly 11,000 user complaints were received last year, with an average handling rate of 88.4%.

### 我三十八个大中城市已建产品质量跟踪站
李小林
1989-04-16
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　我三十八个大中城市已建产品质量跟踪站
    据新华社大连４月１５日电　（记者李小林）记者从正在大连召开的全国产品质量跟踪站工作会议获悉，我国已在３８个大中城市建立了４６个产品质量跟踪站，这些跟踪站在维护消费者利益、监督产品质量方面起到了良好作用。
    仅据２５个跟踪站的统计，去年接受用户投诉近１．１万次，处理率平均达８８．４％。
