Title: Zeng Xianzhi and Others Donate to the Beijing Asian Games Foundation
Author: Zhang Mingyi
Date: 1989-04-10
Edition: 4

	Zeng Xianzhi and others donate to the Beijing Asian Games Foundation

	The People's Daily, Beijing, April 8th - Hong Kong Chinese General Chamber of Commerce Vice Chairman Zeng Xianzhi donated 100 million Hong Kong dollars to the Beijing Asian Games Foundation today. Additionally, Hong Kong Goldlion (Far East) Limited donated 100 million Hong Kong dollars, and China Silverlion Limited donated 1 million yuan. The vice chairmen of the Asian Games Organizing Committee, Zhang Baifa and He Zhenliang, praised Mr. Zeng's patriotic spirit.

	Furthermore, the Beijing Textile Industry Corporation and six other units donated a total of 10.5 million yuan to the Asian Games Foundation today.

### 曾宪梓等向北京亚运会基金会捐款
张明毅
1989-04-10
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：体育

　　曾宪梓等向北京亚运会基金会捐款
    本报北京４月８日讯  一向关心祖国教育和体育发展事业的香港中华总商会副会长曾宪梓先生今天向北京亚运会基金会捐款１００万元港币；香港金利来（远东）有限公司捐款１００万元港币；中国银利来有限公司捐赠１０万元人民币。亚运会组委会副主任张百发、何振梁赞扬了曾先生这种爱国精神。
    另外北京纺织工业总公司等七单位今天也向亚运会基金会捐款１０５万元人民币。　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（张明毅）
