Title: Zhao Ziyang Answers Questions from Accompanying Journalists: Current Sino-North Korean Relations Are Very Good
Author: Zhou Bizhong
Date: 1989-04-24
Edition: 3

    Zhao Ziyang Answers Questions from Accompanying Journalists
    Current Sino-North Korean Relations Are Very Good

    People's Daily, Pyongyang, April 24 (Special Correspondent Zhou Bizhong) - General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee Zhao Ziyang said: "The current Sino-North Korean relations are very good, and we are very satisfied." Zhao Ziyang made these remarks while answering questions from reporters of the People's Daily, Xinhua News Agency, Central People's Radio, and China Radio International who accompanied him during his visit to North Korea.

    Zhao Ziyang said, "The friendship between China and North Korea is forged in blood and was personally established by Comrade Mao Zedong, Comrade Zhou Enlai, Comrade Deng Xiaoping, and Comrade Kim Il-sung. Persisting in and developing friendly relations between the two parties, two countries, and two peoples is an unwavering and steadfast policy of the Chinese party and government. We are also very pleased to see that the North Korean party and government have consistently adhered to the policy of friendship with China."

    Zhao Ziyang said, "We will, as always, firmly support the North Korean Workers' Party and government's policy of independent and peaceful reunification of the motherland, and support the various reasonable initiatives proposed by Chairman Kim Il-sung and the North Korean government in recent years to ease the situation on the Korean Peninsula and promote North-South dialogue and national reunification."

    Zhao Ziyang expressed his belief that this visit to North Korea will further deepen the friendship and understanding between the two parties, promote friendly relations between the two countries, and strengthen the friendship between the two peoples.

### 赵紫阳答随行记者问  现在中朝关系非常好
周必忠
1989-04-24
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　赵紫阳答随行记者问    
    现在中朝关系非常好
    本报平壤４月２４日专电  记者周必忠报道：中共中央总书记赵紫阳说：“现在的中朝关系是非常好的，我们很满意。”赵紫阳是在访问朝鲜途中就中朝关系问题回答随行的本报、新华社、中央人民广播电台、中国国际广播电台记者提问时说这番话的。
    赵紫阳说，中朝友谊是用鲜血凝成的，是毛泽东同志、周恩来同志、邓小平同志和金日成同志亲自缔造的。坚持和发展中朝两党、两国和两国人民之间的友好关系，是中国党和政府坚定不移的方针、始终不渝的方针。我们也很高兴地看到朝鲜党和政府一直坚持朝中友好的方针。
    赵紫阳说，我们将一如既往、坚决支持朝鲜劳动党和政府自主和平统一祖国的方针，支持近年来金日成主席和朝鲜政府为缓和朝鲜半岛局势，进行北南对话以及统一祖国所提出的各种合理的倡议。
    赵紫阳表示相信，这次对朝鲜的访问，将会进一步加深中朝两党的友谊和了解，促进两国之间的友好关系，增强两国人民之间的友谊。
