Title: Dance of the Qing Dynasty Court
Author: Yè Jìn
Date: 1989-04-08
Edition: 8

To coincide with the international tourism promotion of Beijing, the Beijing Song and Dance Troupe recently premiered a song and dance evening titled "Zong Qing Yue Wu · Sheng Shi Xing," which primarily depicts the life of the Qing Dynasty court. This is a dance scene from the "Xing Ji" segment, showcasing the court ladies' dressing and makeup rituals.
