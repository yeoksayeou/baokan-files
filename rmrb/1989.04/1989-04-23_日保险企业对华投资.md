Title: Japanese Insurance Companies Investing in China
Author: Shi Jiang
Date: 1989-04-23
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency

Japanese insurance companies have been investing in China. Nippon Life Insurance Mutual Company established a "Beijing Liaison Office" in October last year, officially starting its investment business in China. By the end of February this year, the total amount of international loans to China reached approximately 22.7 billion yen, accounting for about one-third of the loans to Asia. The loans are mainly used to support China's energy, transportation, and other infrastructure projects. The company stated that it will further expand its international loans to China in the future, with an expected annual loan amount exceeding 10 billion yen.

### 日保险企业对华投资
施江
1989-04-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　日保险企业对华投资
    本报讯  日本第一生命保险相互会社自去年１０月设立“北京联络处”，正式开展了对华投资业务。截至今年２月底，对中国的国际贷款总额约达２２７亿日元，约占对亚洲贷款的１／３，贷款主要用于支持中国的能源、交通等基础建设。该公司表示，今后要进一步扩大对中国的国际贷款，预计年贷款额将超过１００亿日元。　　　　　　　　　
　　　　（施江）
