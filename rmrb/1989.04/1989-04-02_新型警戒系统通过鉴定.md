Title: New Type of Perimeter Alarm System Passes Inspection
Author: Wenquan
Date: 1989-04-02
Edition: 2

A new type of perimeter pulse high-voltage alarm system, jointly developed by Beijing University of Technology and Jiangsu Jingjiang Scientific Instrument Factory, has recently passed inspection in Nanjing.

This system overcomes the drawback of high power consumption in traditional electric fences, making it a replacement product for labor reform and education, as well as important material and financial departments for perimeter security. When someone intrudes into the secured area, the main control room issues an audio and visual alarm, displaying the exact location, and automatically activates the high-voltage pulse electric fence. In the event of a power outage, the system can automatically switch to battery power to continue normal operation.

### 新型警戒系统通过鉴定
文泉
1989-04-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　新型警戒系统通过鉴定
    本报讯  周界脉冲高压警戒系统由北京工业大学和江苏靖江科学器材厂联合研制成功，最近在南京通过鉴定。
    该系统克服了传统电网耗电量大的弊端，是劳改劳教、重要物资金融部门实行警戒的换代产品。当有人侵入警戒区域，主机控制室产生声、光报警，并显示确切位置，自动合上高压脉冲电网。遇上停电，可自动启用电瓶正常工作。　　
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（文泉）
