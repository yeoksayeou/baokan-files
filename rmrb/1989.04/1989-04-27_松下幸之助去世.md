Title: The Passing of Matsushita Konosuke
Author: Yu Qing
Date: 1989-04-27
Edition: 3

People's Daily, Tokyo, April 27th. Reporter Yu Qing reports: Japanese renowned businessman and former chairman of Matsushita Electric Industrial Co., Ltd., Matsushita Konosuke, passed away this morning in a hospital in Moriguchi City, Osaka Prefecture, due to pneumonia. He was 94 years old.

Matsushita was known as the "God of Management" and was one of the most representative figures in Japan's economic sphere in the 20th century. In 1919, at the age of 24, with only a primary school education, Matsushita Konosuke founded Matsushita Electric Appliance Manufacturing Works (the predecessor of Matsushita Electric Industrial Co., Ltd.). From then on, he worked tirelessly to expand the company's production scale, eventually developing it into a corporate group with approximately 170,000 employees and over 140 subsidiary companies. Throughout his life, he was known for his ability to utilize talent and his extensive participation in social activities. He was also actively involved in promoting economic and technological exchanges between China and Japan.

### 松下幸之助去世
于青
1989-04-27
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　松下幸之助去世
    本报东京４月２７日电  记者于青报道：日本著名企业家、松下电器公司前会长松下幸之助今天上午在大阪府守口市的一家医院中因患肺炎去世，享年９４岁。
    松下先生被誉为“经营之神”，是日本本世纪中有代表性的经济界人士。１９１９年，年仅２４岁只有小学学历的松下幸之助创立了松下电器具制作所（现在松下电器公司的前身）。从此苦心经营，励精图治，生产规模不断扩大，发展成为约１７万职员、１４０多家子公司的产业集团。生前善用人才，广泛参加社会活动，积极致力于中日间的经济和技术交流。
