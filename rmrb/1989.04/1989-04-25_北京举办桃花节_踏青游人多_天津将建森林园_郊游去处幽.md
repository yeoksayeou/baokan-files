Title: Beijing Hosts Peach Blossom Festival; Many People Enjoy Spring Outings, Tianjin to Build Forest Park for Scenic Outings
Author: Zhang Liang
Date: 1989-04-25
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 25th

Beijing's first Peach Blossom Festival kicked off on April 15th at the foot of the Western Hills in the Botanical Garden. The Beijing Botanical Garden offers 1,600 acres of garden greenery for visitors to enjoy. The completed Peach Blossom Garden covers over 40 acres. Visitors entering the Peach Blossom Garden can walk through the Peach Blossom Gate, stroll along the Peach Blossom Path, climb the Peach Blossom Hill, and pass under the Peach Blossom Arches. During the 20-day festival, there will be exhibitions of peach blossom poetry and paintings, kite performances, and bird art performances.

Tianjin plans to build a suburban forest park covering over 35,000 acres in the Da Gang District, about 40 kilometers from the city center. The park will feature a water activity area, a forest horse racing track, a cultural and entertainment forest area, a holiday village, a fishing zone, and a bird and waterfowl observation area. The park is expected to be completed in four years.

The deputy director of the Civil Affairs Office of Shaoyang City, Hunan, Xiao Yunlin, has been repairing clocks for the public free of charge in his spare time since 1978, fixing over 12,000 clocks without taking a single penny.

### 北京举办桃花节　踏青游人多  天津将建森林园　郊游去处幽
张亮　中新社　赵罗
1989-04-25
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：大海浪花

　　北京举办桃花节　踏青游人多    
    天津将建森林园　郊游去处幽
    ●北京市首届桃花节４月１５日在西山脚下的植物园拉开序幕。北京植物园有１６００亩园林绿地可供游人观赏，已建成的碧桃园占地４０余亩。游人踏进碧桃园的桃花门，可过桃花路，上桃花山，穿桃花架。为期２０天的桃花节期间，还举办桃花诗画展、风筝表演、鸟艺表演。
　　　　　　　　　　　（张亮）
    ●天津将在距市中心区４０公里的大港区官港，兴建一所总面积为３５０００余亩的郊野森林公园。公园内将建水上活动区、森林跑马场、文娱活动林地、度假村、垂钓小区和鸟类水禽观赏区。预计四年建成公园。
　　　　（中新社）
    ●湖南邵阳市民政局办公室副主任肖云林自１９７８年以来，利用业余时间为群众义务修钟表１２０００多块，分文不取。　　　　　　
　　　（赵罗）
