Title: Funeral Service for Hu Juewen Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-04-26
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 26th.

Party and state leaders, along with over 600 people from various sectors in the capital, held a farewell ceremony for Hu Juewen, a loyal patriot, renowned political activist, outstanding industrialist, founder and distinguished leader of the China Democratic National Construction Association, and close friend of the Communist Party of China, at Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery this afternoon.

Hu Juewen passed away in Beijing on April 16 at the age of 94. He had served as the Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Sixth National People's Congress and, prior to his death, was the Honorary Chairman of the Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association and the President of the Chinese Vocational Education Society.

The ceremony hall at Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery was solemn and dignified, with mournful music playing. Hu Juewen's body was surrounded by flowers and cypress trees, draped with the national flag of the People's Republic of China.

Yang Shangkun, Li Peng, Wan Li, Qiao Shi, Hu Qili, and Yao Yilin attended the ceremony and sent wreaths. They walked slowly to Hu Juewen's body, bowed in silence, and expressed their condolences to Hu Juewen's wife, Cai Yumin, and other family members.

Cai Chang, Huang Huoqing, Fu Zhong, Xu Dehua, Chu Tuonan, Yang Chengwu, Lü Zhengcao, Bao Erhan, Mao Yisheng, and others also sent wreaths.

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the State Council, the Central Advisory Commission, the Central Discipline Inspection Commission, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Supreme People's Court, the Supreme People's Procuratorate, the Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association, the Shanghai Jiading County leadership, and other democratic parties' central committees sent wreaths.

Hu Juewen's ashes will be interred at Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery.

State Chairman Yang Shangkun expresses deep condolences to Hu Juewen's wife, Cai Yumin, and other family members.

### 胡厥文遗体告别仪式在京举行  杨尚昆李鹏万里乔石胡启立姚依林等和各界六百多人参加

专栏：

　　胡厥文遗体告别仪式在京举行    
    杨尚昆李鹏万里乔石胡启立姚依林等和各界六百多人参加
    新华社北京４月２６日电  党和国家领导人同首都各界６００多人今天下午在八宝山革命公墓，向忠诚的爱国主义者、著名的政治活动家、杰出的实业家、中国民主建国会创建人和卓越的领导人、中国共产党的亲密朋友胡厥文同志的遗体作最后诀别。
    胡厥文同志是４月１６日在北京病逝的，终年９４岁。他曾任六届全国人大常委会副委员长，去世前是民建中央名誉主席、中华职业教育社理事长。
    八宝山革命公墓礼堂庄严肃穆，哀乐低回。胡厥文同志的遗体安放在鲜花翠柏丛中，身上覆盖着中华人民共和国国旗。
    杨尚昆、李鹏、万里、乔石、胡启立、姚依林等参加告别仪式并送了花圈。他们缓步走到胡厥文同志的遗体前鞠躬默哀，并向胡厥文同志夫人蒉毓敏及其亲属表示亲切慰问。
    前去向遗体告别并送花圈的还有：田纪云、李铁映、宋平、秦基伟、芮杏文、阎明复、温家宝、王平、刘澜涛、张劲夫、陈丕显、段君毅、耿飚、姬鹏飞、黄华、习仲勋、彭冲、朱学范、阿沛·阿旺晋美、荣毅仁、倪志福、陈慕华、费孝通、孙起孟、雷洁琼、宋健、邹家华、任建新、刘复之、方毅、谷牧、杨静仁、康克清、王光英、屈武、马文瑞、刘靖基、王恩茂、钱学森、胡绳、孙晓村、程思远、刘华清、杨易辰、陈再道、汪锋等有关方面负责人、有关人士和生前友好。
    赵紫阳、邓小平、陈云、李先念、彭真、邓颖超、徐向前、聂荣臻、王震、江泽民、李瑞环、李锡铭、杨汝岱、吴学谦、丁关根、薄一波、宋任穷、王首道、伍修权、江华、李一氓、李德生、杨得志、肖克、余秋里、宋时轮、张爱萍、陆定一、陈锡联、胡乔木、黄镇、康世恩、程子华、韦国清、赛福鼎·艾则孜、周谷城、严济慈、叶飞、廖汉生、王汉斌、王丙乾、王芳、李贵鲜、陈希同、陈俊生、王任重、帕巴拉·格列朗杰、胡子昂、周培源、邓兆祥、赵朴初、巴金、钱伟长、卢嘉锡、钱正英、苏步青、司马义·艾买提、侯镜如、丁光训、洪学智等，送了花圈。
    蔡畅、黄火青、傅钟、许德珩、楚图南、杨成武、吕正操、包尔汉、茅以升等也送了花圈。
