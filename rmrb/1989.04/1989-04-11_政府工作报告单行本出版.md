Title: Publication of the Government Work Report in Book Form
Author:
Date: 1989-04-11
Edition: 2

The book version of Premier Li Peng's government work report, "Firmly Implement the Policies of Rectification and Deepening Reform," delivered at the Second Session of the Seventh National People's Congress, has been published by the People's Publishing House. It will be available for purchase starting today at bookstores across the country. This book is also being published by the Nationalities Publishing House in Mongolian, Tibetan, Uyghur, Kazakh, and Korean.

The compilation of documents from the Second Session of the Seventh National People's Congress and the Second Session of the Seventh Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference will also be published by the People's Publishing House in the near future.

### 政府工作报告单行本出版

1989-04-11
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　政府工作报告单行本出版
    新华社北京４月１０日电　李鹏总理在第七届全国人民代表大会第二次会议上的政府工作报告《坚决贯彻治理整顿和深化改革的方针》，已由人民出版社出版单行本，即日起在全国各地新华书店发行。这本书还由民族出版社用蒙古、藏、维吾尔、哈萨克、朝鲜五种文字出版。
    七届全国人大二次会议和全国政协七届二次会议文件汇编，也将在近期内由人民出版社出版。
