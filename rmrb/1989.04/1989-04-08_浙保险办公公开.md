Title: Zhejiang Insurance Offices Open to the Public
Author: Hé Xùfāng
Date: 1989-04-08
Edition: 2

Four Corners

Zhejiang Insurance Offices Open to the Public

Our reporter from Hangzhou, April 7th: Recently, insurance companies at various levels in Zhejiang Province have gradually implemented the "two openness and one supervision" work system, aiming to strengthen the construction of clean government. This new work system includes: making the claims process and claims discipline public, and making the claims results public. All matters that are closely related to the interests of policyholders and require their cooperation and knowledge should be made public and subject to supervision.

(He Xufang)

### 浙保险办公公开
何旭芳
1989-04-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　浙保险办公公开
    本报杭州四月七日电  浙江省各级保险公司近来逐步实施“两公开一监督”工作制度，旨在加强廉政建设。这项新的工作制度包括：公开理赔程序和理赔纪律，公开理赔结果；凡是与保户利益密切相关、需要保户协办和知道的事都应公布于众，接受监督。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（何旭芳）
