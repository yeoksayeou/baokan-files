Title: The League of Communists of Yugoslavia Celebrates Its 70th Anniversary
Author: Yan Zheng
Date: 1989-04-20
Edition: 3

The League of Communists of Yugoslavia (LCY) celebrated its 70th anniversary today. In the evening, the LCY Central Committee held a grand gathering at the Kolarčeva Narodna University in Belgrade. LCY President Shuvard delivered a speech in which he expounded on the history of the Yugoslav party, the urgent problems it faces, and its future. He said that the significant achievements and glorious history of the LCY's struggle for socialism cannot be erased or easily destroyed. Our task is to continue the work of our predecessors, but we must also correct past mistakes, eliminate current serious distortions, and surpass the achievements of our forerunners. Yugoslavia has the knowledge, ability, and potential to overcome the crisis, destroy the nationalism and counter-revolution in Kosovo, and maintain the essence, basic direction, and strategy of the Yugoslav socialist revolution.

To commemorate the party's birthday, the "LCY 70th Anniversary Exhibition" opened today at the Tito Memorial Center's "May 25th" Memorial Hall. The LCY Central Committee delegation laid flowers at Tito's tomb.

### 南共联盟庆祝建党７０周年
严正
1989-04-20
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　南共联盟庆祝建党７０周年
    本报贝尔格莱德４月１９日电  记者严正报道：为了庆祝南共联盟（共产党）成立７０周年，今天晚间南共联盟中央在贝尔格莱德科拉尔切夫人民大学举行隆重集会。南共联盟主席团主席舒瓦尔在报告中阐述了南斯拉夫党的历史、面临的迫切问题及其前途。他说，南共联盟（共产党）为社会主义而斗争取得的重大成果和光辉的历史无法抹煞，也不是轻易能够破坏的。我们的任务是继续前辈的事业，但是也要纠正过去的错误，消除现在的严重变态，我们应超过先驱者的业绩。南斯拉夫有知识、有能力、也有潜力克服危机，摧毁科索沃的民族主义和反革命，保持南斯拉夫社会主义革命的本质、基本方向和战略。
    为纪念党的生日而举办的“南共联盟（共产党）７０周年展览”今天在铁托纪念中心“５月２５日”纪念馆开幕，南共联盟中央代表团向铁托墓敬献鲜花。
