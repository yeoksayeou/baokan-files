Title: Yang Shangkun Meets with British Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-04-25
Edition: 1

Yang Shangkun, President of the People's Republic of China, met today with Sir John Kerr, Chairman of the Jardine Matheson Group, and his delegation. Yang reaffirmed that China's fundamental policy towards Hong Kong is to maintain the territory's stability and prosperity.

The Jardine Matheson Group is a British multinational corporation established in 1816, headquartered in London, and operates in numerous countries and regions in industries such as machinery, transportation, trade, real estate, and tourism. It owns over 70 companies in Hong Kong, including Cathay Pacific Airways.

Sir John Kerr told Yang that the Jardine Matheson Group believes in Hong Kong's future and will continue to invest in the territory.

Yang expressed his appreciation for this. He said, "The Jardine Matheson Group's active investment in Hong Kong, and its plans for new investments, are wise and correct decisions."

Yang added, "Your actions will have a positive influence on those who have doubts about Hong Kong's future and will play a beneficial role in maintaining Hong Kong's stability."

Sir John Kerr praised China's decision to implement the "one country, two systems" policy in Hong Kong.

Wang Daohan, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Chairman of the China International Trust and Investment Corporation (CITIC), among others, attended the meeting.

The British guests arrived in Beijing yesterday at the invitation of CITIC.

### 杨尚昆会见英国客人

1989-04-25
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　杨尚昆会见英国客人
    新华社北京４月２５日电  国家主席杨尚昆今天在这里会见英国太古集团主席施雅迪爵士一行时重申，中国对香港的基本政策是保持香港的稳定和繁荣。
    太古集团是英国一家跨国公司，成立于１８１６年，总部设在伦敦，在许多国家和地区经营机械、运输、贸易、地产、旅游等业务，在香港拥有国泰航空公司等７０多家公司。
    施雅迪爵士告诉杨尚昆，太古集团相信香港是有前途的，并将继续向香港投资。
    杨尚昆对此表示赞赏。他说，太古集团在香港积极投资，而且准备有新的投资，这是明智和正确的决定。
    杨尚昆说，你们的作法对香港前途怀有疑虑的少数人将产生积极的影响，对香港的稳定将起有益的作用。
    施雅迪爵士对中国在香港实行“一国两制”的决定表示赞赏。
    全国人大常委会副委员长、中国国际信托投资公司董事长荣毅仁等参加了会见。
    英国客人是应中信公司的邀请于昨天抵京的。
