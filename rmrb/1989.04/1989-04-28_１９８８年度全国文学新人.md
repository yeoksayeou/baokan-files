Title: 1988 National Literary Newcomers
Author:
Date: 1989-04-28
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, April 24th.

Five literary figures received the honor of "1988 National Literary Newcomers" here on April 24th. They are Qiao Yu, Liu Heng, Xie Youyin, Su Xiaokang, and Yang Zhijun.

The five "literary newcomers" presented the following literary works to the people in 1988, for which they received this honor: *The General* (Qiao Yu), *The Marshal Dies on the Eve of Army Day* (Su Xiaokang), *Fuxi, Fuxi* (Liu Heng), *The Sea Retreated Yesterday* (Yang Zhijun), and *Taste* (Xie Youyin).

On the same day, the "Wenhui Literary Award" and the "Blue Sky International Tourism Essay Contest Award" were also announced. Xie Jin, Liu Shaotang, Bian Zhilin, Shi Shuqing, Chen Dancheng, Wang Anyi, and others received these awards.

### １９８８年度全国文学新人

1989-04-28
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　１９８８年度全国文学新人
    新华社上海电  ５位文坛骁将，４月２４日在这里获得“１９８８年度全国文学新人”的殊荣，他们是乔瑜、刘恒、谢友鄞、苏晓康、杨志军。
    ５位“文学新人”在１９８８年度献给人民，并由此获得这一殊荣的文学作品是：《少将》（乔瑜）、《元帅死在建军节前夜》（苏晓康）、《伏羲伏羲》（刘恒）、《海昨天退去》（杨志军）、《滋味》（谢友鄞）。
    同一天还评出了“文汇文艺奖”和“蓝天杯国际旅游征文奖”，谢晋、刘绍棠、卞之琳、施叔青、陈丹晨、王安忆等人分别获得上述奖。
