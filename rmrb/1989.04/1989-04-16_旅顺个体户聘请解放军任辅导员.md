Title: Travelers in Dalian Hire Soldiers as Instructors
Author: Lǐ Bǎo Mín
Date: 1989-04-16
Edition: 2

Travelers in Dalian Hire Soldiers as Instructors

Xinhua News Agency, Dalian, April 15th.

The Dalian City Lvshunkou District Individual Laborers Association, Sun Valley Branch, which has 21 member groups, yesterday hired 5 local garrison officers as instructors. The instructors will go deep into the 21 individual laborer member groups, assist in conducting education on the current situation, legal system, and professional ethics, and cooperate with the individual labor association to do a good job in ideological and political work.

### 旅顺个体户聘请解放军任辅导员
李保民
1989-04-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　旅顺个体户聘请解放军任辅导员
    本报大连４月１５日电　拥有２１个会员小组的大连市旅顺口区个体劳动者协会太阳沟分会，昨天聘请５名当地驻军干部任辅导员。辅导员们将深入２１个个体户会员小组，协助进行形势、法制、职业道德等教育，配合个体劳协做好思想政治工作。　　　　　　　　　（李保民）
