Title: The Tibet Development Assistance Fund Donates to the Batang Earthquake Zone
Author:
Date: 1989-04-29
Edition: 2

Recently, two 6.7 magnitude earthquakes struck Batang County in Sichuan Province in succession. To express condolences to the local people and support their efforts in disaster relief, the Tibet Development Assistance Fund donated 600,000 yuan to the Batang County People's Government today.

### 援藏基金会向巴塘震区捐款

1989-04-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　援藏基金会向巴塘震区捐款
    新华社北京４月２８日电  最近，四川省巴塘县连续两次发生６．７级地震。为了向当地人民表示慰问和支持他们抗震救灾，援助西藏发展基金会今天向巴塘县人民政府捐款６万元人民币。
