Title: World Food Programme Assists Sichuan with 10,000 Tons of Grain
Author:
Date: 1989-04-27
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 27th.

According to information provided by the Ministry of Civil Affairs, on April 26, the Executive Director of the World Food Programme, Ingram, decided to provide 10,000 tons of grain to the severe wind and hail disaster areas in Sichuan Province, China.

### 世界粮食署援助四川万吨粮

1989-04-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　世界粮食署援助四川万吨粮
    新华社北京四月二十七日电  据民政部提供的消息，四月二十六日，世界粮食计划署执行干事英格拉姆决定，给我国四川省特大风雹灾区一万吨粮食的援助。
