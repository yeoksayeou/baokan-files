Title: Ethiopian Runners Dominate World Cup Marathon Men's Team and Individual Events
Author:
Date: 1989-04-18
Edition: 4

Ethiopian athletes today claimed both the team and individual titles in the men's competition of the World Cup Marathon held in Milan, Italy.

Zerakesh Assefa finished first with a time of 2 hours, 10 minutes, and 28 seconds. His teammate, Negari Demissie, was just 8 seconds behind, securing the second place. Together with another teammate, they completed the race in 6 hours, 37 minutes, and 20 seconds, leading Ethiopia to the team championship. The Italian team took the second place in the team competition with a time of 6 hours, 37 minutes, and 51 seconds. Their runner, Gelindo Bordin, finished third individually with a time of 2 hours, 10 minutes, and 49 seconds. The teams from France, the Soviet Union, West Germany, East Germany, Japan, and Spain took the third to eighth places, respectively.

### 世界杯马拉松男团和个人赛　埃塞俄比亚选手力克群雄夺冠

1989-04-18
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：

　　世界杯马拉松男团和个人赛
　　埃塞俄比亚选手力克群雄夺冠
    新华社罗马４月１６日电　埃塞俄比亚选手今天在意大利米兰举行的世界杯马拉松赛男子组的比赛中，赢得了团体冠军和个人前两名。
    扎拉克以２小时１０分２８秒第一个到达终点，他的队友内·迪仅以８秒之差获第二名。这两位选手和他们的另一位队友以６小时３７分２０秒的成绩，使埃塞俄比亚队荣登团体冠军宝座。意大利队获得团体第二名，成绩是６小时３７分５１秒。该队选手波利以２小时１０分４９秒的成绩获得个人第三名。获团体第三至第八名的队依次为法国、苏联、西德、民主德国、日本和西班牙。
