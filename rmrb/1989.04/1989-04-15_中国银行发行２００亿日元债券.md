Title: Bank of China Issues 20 Billion Yen Bonds
Author: Wei Xuejun
Date: 1989-04-15
Edition: 2

The Bank of China issued 20 billion yen in bonds on April 11 in the Tokyo capital market. The bonds have a face interest rate of 5.3% and a term of 10 years. This is the eighth time since November 1984 that the Bank of China has entered the Tokyo market to issue international bonds.

### 中国银行发行２００亿日元债券
韦学军
1989-04-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　中国银行发行２００亿日元债券
    本报讯　中国银行４月１１日在日本东京资本市场发行了２００亿日元债券。这笔债券票面利率为５．３％，期限为１０年。这是继１９８４年１１月以来，中国银行第８次进入东京市场发行国际债券。　　
　　　　　　（韦学军）
