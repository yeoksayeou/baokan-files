Title: Song Renqiong Meets with General Secretary of the Greek Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1989-04-12
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 10th.

Song Renqiong, Vice Chairman of the Central Advisory Commission of the Communist Party of China, met and dined with a delegation from the Communist Party of Greece led by its General Secretary, Florakis, this evening at the Great Hall of the People. This is the first visit by the highest leader of the Greek Communist Party since the two parties resumed relations in 1987. Founded in 1918, the Greek Communist Party is a significant political force in Greece and is the third-largest party in the Greek Parliament.

### 宋任穷会见希共总书记

1989-04-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　宋任穷会见希共总书记
    据新华社北京4月10日电　中共中央顾问委员会副主任宋任穷今天晚上在人民大会堂会见并宴请由中央总书记弗洛拉基斯率领的希腊共产党代表团。这是自1987年中、希两党恢复关系以来希共最高领导人首次来访。成立于1918年的希共是希腊国内一支有广泛影响的政治力量，为希腊议会第三大党。
