Title: Three Leaders of Colombia's "April 19" Movement Assassinated
Author:
Date: 1989-04-09
Edition: 4

Three leaders of Colombia's "April 19" guerrilla movement were gunned down by unidentified assailants near Bogota on the afternoon of April 7.

According to local television reports, one of the three assassinated leaders was Pablo Escobar, who had previously served as the urban militia commander of the guerrilla group. He had frequently engaged in direct dialogue with government representatives on the issue of the peace process.

A spokesperson for the "April 19" movement demanded that the government investigate the incident and bring the perpetrators to justice.

### 哥“四·一九”运动三名领导人被暗杀

1989-04-09
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　哥“四·一九”运动三名领导人被暗杀
    新华社波哥大４月７日电  哥伦比亚游击队“四·一九”运动３名领导人７日下午在波哥大附近被不明身份歹徒枪杀。
    据当地电视台报道，３名被暗杀的“四·一九”运动领导人中有帕拉·古斯曼，他生前曾担任该游击队城市民兵司令，多次同政府代表就和平进程问题直接对话。
    “四·一九”运动发言人要求政府调查事件真相，惩办元凶。
