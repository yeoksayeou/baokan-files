Title: Japan Holds Memorial Gathering for Wang Bingnan
Author: Sun Dongmin
Date: 1989-04-23
Edition: 3

People's Daily, Tokyo, April 22nd. - Reported by Sun Dongmin: Japanese friends of China held a gathering in Tokyo today to commemorate Wang Bingnan, a veteran revolutionary of the Chinese proletariat and an outstanding diplomat who passed away last year.

The gathering was initiated by the Japanese New Drama Association Cultural Center and co-organized by the Japan-China Friendship Association, the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, and the Japan-China Association, among other organizations. Over 200 prominent figures in Sino-Japanese friendship and friends of Wang Bingnan, including Okazaki Kahei and Shimizu Masao, attended the event. True Mountain Miyako, Chida Koreyuki, Ogawa Heishiro, and Huang Shimin, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, delivered memorial speeches. China's Ambassador to Japan, Yang Zhenya, also attended the memorial gathering.

### 日本集会纪念王炳南
孙东民
1989-04-23
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　日本集会纪念王炳南
    本报东京４月２２日电  记者孙东民报道：日中友好人士今天在东京举行集会，缅怀去年去世的我国久经考验的无产阶级革命家、杰出的外交家王炳南。
    这次集会是由友好团体日本新制作座文化中心发起，日中友好协会、日中文化交流协会、日中协会等团体共同举办的。中日友好知名人士和王炳南的生前友好冈崎嘉平太、清水正夫等２００多人出席。真山美保、千田是也、小川平四郎以及对外友协副会长黄世明等发表了纪念讲话。我驻日大使杨振亚出席了纪念集会。
