Title: State Council Appoints State Officials
Author:
Date: 1989-04-12
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 11th.

The State Council appointed state officials on March 21, 1989.

Jiang Guan Zhuang was appointed as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Personnel, and Duan Rui Yi was appointed as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry.

### 国务院任命国家工作人员

1989-04-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　国务院任命国家工作人员
    新华社北京4月11日电　国务院1989年3月21日任命国家工作人员。
    任命蒋冠庄为人事部副部长，段瑞钰为冶金工业部副部长。
