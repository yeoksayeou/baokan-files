Title: Foreign Affairs Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-04-27
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 27th.

    Vice Premier Zhou Jiahuai met with Indian Minister of Steel and Mines Dedeel and his delegation in the Great Hall of the People in the afternoon of April 26th. The delegation, invited by Minister of Geology and Mineral Resources Zhu Xun, arrived in Beijing on April 24th. The purpose of their visit is to promote cooperation and exchange in the fields of geology and mineral resources between China and India.

    Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Ni Zhifu hosted a dinner for the Japanese delegation led by the acting chairman of the Japanese Federation of Private Sector Trade Unions and chairman of the Electric Machinery Labor Union, Kashiwa Mitsuji, at the Great Hall of the People on the evening of April 26th. The delegation's visit aims to understand China's reform and opening-up situation and to investigate the investment environment in China.

    The minutes of the fourth meeting of the China-Hungary Economic, Trade, and Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee were signed in Beijing on April 26th. The Chinese side chairman, Minister of Agriculture He Kang, and the Hungarian side chairman, Deputy Prime Minister Majes signed the minutes.

    Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Zhu Xuefan hosted a dinner for the delegation led by Chairman of the Flick Group of the Federal Republic of Germany, Benningsen, at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse on the evening of April 26th. The Flick Group is one of the largest conglomerates in the Federal Republic of Germany, mainly engaged in the production and operation of energy and chemical equipment. The delegation arrived in Beijing on the same day.

### 外事简讯

1989-04-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　外事简讯
    ▲国务委员邹家华２６日下午在人民大会堂会见印度钢铁矿业部长德戴尔一行。德戴尔一行应地质矿产部部长朱训邀请，于２４日抵京访华。他们此行的目的是推进中印地质矿产领域的合作和交流。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长、中华全国总工会主席倪志福２６日晚在人民大会堂会见并宴请了由日本民间工会联合会代理会长、电机劳联委员长藁科满治率领的日本电机行业劳资代表访华团。访华团此行是为了解中国改革、开放情况，考察在华投资环境等。
    ▲中国和匈牙利经济、贸易、科技合作委员会第四次会议会谈纪要２６日在北京签署。委员会中方主席、农业部长何康和匈方主席、匈牙利副总理迈杰什在纪要上签了字。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长、中国国际友谊促进会理事长朱学范２６日晚在钓鱼台国宾馆会见并宴请了联邦德国费巴集团董事长贝宁森一行。费巴集团是联邦德国最大的集团公司之一，主要经营和生产能源、化工设备。贝宁森一行于当日抵京。
    （据新华社）
