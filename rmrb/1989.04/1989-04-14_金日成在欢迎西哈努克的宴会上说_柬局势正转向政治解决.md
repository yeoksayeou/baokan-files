Title: Kim Il-sung says at the banquet welcoming Sihanouk that the situation in Cambodia is turning towards a political solution
Author: Xú Bǎokāng
Date: 1989-04-14
Edition: 3

	Kim Il-sung said at the banquet welcoming Prince Sihanouk on the evening of the 11th that the current trend of developments is turning towards a direction that is favorable for a just and peaceful political solution to the Cambodian problem. The DPRK will, as always, respect and support the independent stance that Prince Sihanouk has insisted upon.

	Kim Il-sung highly praised Prince Sihanouk's efforts in recent years to strengthen unity among the anti-Vietnamese factions in Cambodia, to promote a comprehensive political solution to the Cambodian problem, and to arouse widespread international support and sympathy. He firmly believed that the Cambodian people will definitely achieve final victory in their struggle to build an independent, self-reliant, peaceful, neutral, democratic, and non-aligned Cambodia.

	Prince Sihanouk, in his speech, expressed his gratitude and eternal remembrance to the Korean Party, government, and people for their active support and solidarity with the struggle of the Cambodian people.

### 金日成在欢迎西哈努克的宴会上说　柬局势正转向政治解决
徐宝康
1989-04-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　金日成在欢迎西哈努克的宴会上说
　　柬局势正转向政治解决
    本报平壤４月１２日电　记者徐宝康报道：金日成主席１１日晚在欢迎西哈努克亲王的宴会上说，目前形势的发展趋势正朝着有利于公正、和平地政治解决柬埔寨问题的方向转变。朝鲜将一如既往地尊重和支持西哈努克亲王所坚持的自主立场。
    金日成高度评价西哈努克亲王近年来在加强柬埔寨抗越各派之间的团结，推进一揽子政治解决柬埔寨问题，以及唤起世界广泛支持和同情上作出的巨大贡献，并坚信柬埔寨人民在为建设一个独立、自主、和平、中立、民主和不结盟的柬埔寨的斗争中，一定会取得最后胜利。
    西哈努克亲王在讲话中，对朝鲜党和政府及人民积极支持和声援柬埔寨人民的斗争，表示感谢和永志不忘。
