Title: Lanzhou University Students Regularly Serve on the Streets
Author: Lǐ Shēngcái
Date: 1989-04-29
Edition: 2

Lanzhou University's Communist Youth League Committee and Student Union have, since the beginning of this school year, organized students to go out onto the streets every Sunday to provide various services such as consulting and repairing household appliances, fixing bicycles, and haircuts. The public has praised them, saying, "Leifeng has returned."

Newspaper reporter Li Shengcai took the photo.

### 兰州大学学生定期上街服务（图片）
李生才
1989-04-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    兰州大学学生定期上街服务
    兰州大学团委和学生会从今年开学以来，每逢星期天都上街开展各种咨询维修家用电器、修理自行车、理发等活动，群众称赞说：“雷锋又回来了。”
    新华社记者　李生才摄
