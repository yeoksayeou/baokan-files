Title: Foreign Ministry Spokesperson: Student Protests Are Purely an Internal Matter of China; White House Spokesperson's Comments Inappropriate
Author:
Date: 1989-04-29
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 29th. The Foreign Ministry spokesperson said today that the White House spokesperson's comments on April 28th regarding the student protests in China are inappropriate.

A reporter asked: What is your comment on the White House spokesperson's remarks on April 28th regarding the student protests in China?

The Foreign Ministry spokesperson replied: "I believe it is inappropriate for the spokesperson to comment on the student protests, which are purely an internal matter of China."

### 外交部发言人说  学生游行问题纯属中国内部事务  白宫发言人就此发表讲话不合适

1989-04-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　外交部发言人说    
    学生游行问题纯属中国内部事务    
    白宫发言人就此发表讲话不合适
    新华社北京４月２９日电  外交部发言人今天在这里说，美国白宫发言人２８日就中国学生游行事发表的讲话是不合适的。
    有记者问：你对美国白宫发言人２８日就中国学生游行事发表的讲话有何评论？
    外交部发言人回答说：“我认为这位发言人评论纯属中国内部事务的学生游行问题是不合适的。”
