Title: Shanghai Invests in the Reconstruction of School Dangerous Buildings
Author:
Date: 1989-04-23
Edition: 2

Shanghai's municipal and district governments have invested and raised funds totaling 160 million yuan over the past three years to reconstruct dangerous buildings in primary and secondary schools and lane schools. Now, the reconstruction of dangerous buildings in suburban primary and secondary schools has been basically completed; 42 lane schools in urban areas have been reconstructed, and another 16 lane schools will be completed by the end of this year.

### 上海投资改造学校危房

1989-04-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　上海投资改造学校危房
    本报上海４月２３日电  上海市各级人民政府三年来投资、多方集资１．６亿元，用于改造中小学危房校舍和弄堂小学。如今，郊县中小学危房校舍基本改造完毕；市区的４２所弄堂小学已得到改造，还有１６所弄堂小学今年年底可按期竣工。
