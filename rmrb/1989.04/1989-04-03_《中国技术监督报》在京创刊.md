Title: China Technical Supervision News Launched in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-04-03
Edition: 2

The newspaper *China Technical Supervision News*, which aims to promote the state's quality management policies and protect consumers' rights, has recently been approved by the State Press and Publication Administration to launch in Beijing. It is a nationwide professional newspaper on technical supervision, published by the State Technical Supervision Bureau.

### 《中国技术监督报》在京创刊

1989-04-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　《中国技术监督报》在京创刊
    本报北京４月２日讯  以宣传国家质量管理方针、政策和维护消费者权益为宗旨的《中国技术监督报》，最近经国家新闻出版署批准在北京创刊。它是国家技术监督局主办的全国性技术监督专业报纸。
