Title: Children's Woodblock Prints from Chifeng Exhibited in Taipei
Author: Xú Hóng
Date: 1989-04-02
Edition: 2

Children's woodblock prints from Chifeng are being exhibited in Taipei. The "Taiwan-Chifeng Children's Woodblock Print Charity Exhibition" will run from today until the 16th at the Hanshe Art Gallery in Taipei.

This was announced recently in Beijing by Mr. Hong Sanxiong, Chairman of the Hanshe Company in Taiwan.

The proceeds from this charity exhibition will be used to purchase books, stationery, drawing pens, and carving knives, which will be donated to six schools in the Chifeng League.

### 哲里木儿童版画出展台北
徐泓
1989-04-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　哲里木儿童版画出展台北
    本报北京４月１日讯  海峡两岸的孩子们首次以画会友：“台湾·内蒙古——中国儿童版画义卖联展会”从今天至１６日在台北市寒舍画廊推出。
    这是台湾寒舍公司董事长洪三雄先生日前在北京宣布的。
    这次义卖联展义卖的收入将全部用于购买画册、图书、画笔、刻刀等，赠送给哲里木盟的６所学校。　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（徐泓）
