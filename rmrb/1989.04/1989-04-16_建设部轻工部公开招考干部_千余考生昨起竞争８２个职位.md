Title: Construction and Light Industry Departments Openly Recruit Officials
Author: Wáng Nán
Date: 1989-04-16
Edition: 2

The Ministry of Construction and the Ministry of Light Industry began administering written examinations today for over 1,100 candidates vying for 82 positions in the two ministries.

The candidates include in-service government employees, educators, researchers, enterprise workers, middle and high school teachers, and college graduates.

Over 80% of the candidates for the Ministry of Construction are under 35 years old. Among them, 35% hold associate degrees, 55% hold bachelor's degrees, and 8% hold master's degrees.

Today, they took tests in five basic subjects including public administration, law, aptitude, and document writing, as well as one specialized subject.

To create conditions for implementing the national civil servant system, government agencies have adopted a policy of open recruitment and selection of the best candidates starting this year.

The State Environmental Protection Administration, the Ministry of Building Materials, and the Ministry of Civil Affairs have already hired over 100 employees through examinations.

### 建设部轻工部公开招考干部　千余考生昨起竞争８２个职位
王楠
1989-04-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　建设部轻工部公开招考干部
　　千余考生昨起竞争８２个职位
    新华社北京４月１６日电　（实习生王楠）继国家环保局和民政部公开招考工作人员之后，建设部和轻工业部今天对报考两个部门８２个职位的１１００多名考生进行笔试。
    报考人员包括在职国家机关工作人员、教学科研人员、企业工作人员以及中小学教师和在校应届毕业生等。
    报考建设部的考生８０％以上在３５岁以下，其中大专、大学本科和硕士研究生毕业的分别占３５％、５５％和８％。
    他们今天接受了包括行政学、法学、能力和公文写作在内的五门基础课和一门专业课的测试。
    为推行国家公务员制度创造条件，国家机关从今年起补充新人一律实行公开招考、择优录取的办法。
    国家环保局、建材局和民政部已先后通过考试录用了１００多名工作人员。
