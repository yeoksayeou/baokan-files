Title: Yang Shangkun to Visit Four Latin American Countries
Author:
Date: 1989-04-27
Edition: 1

Yang Shangkun to Visit Four Latin American Countries

New China News Agency, Beijing, April 27th: At the invitation of Mexican President Salinas, Brazilian President Sarney, Uruguayan President Sanguinetti, and Argentine President Alfonsin, Chairman Yang Shangkun will begin state visits to the aforementioned four countries starting from the end of May.

This was announced by the Foreign Ministry spokesperson at a press conference this afternoon.

Chairman Yang Shangkun's visit this time is the first time a Chinese head of state has visited Latin America since the founding of New China.

### 杨尚昆将访问拉美四国

1989-04-27
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　杨尚昆将访问拉美四国
    新华社北京４月２７日电  应墨西哥合众国总统萨利纳斯、巴西联邦共和国总统萨尔内、乌拉圭东岸共和国总统桑吉内蒂和阿根廷共和国总统阿方辛的邀请，国家主席杨尚昆将自５月底起对上述４国进行国事访问。
    这是外交部发言人今天下午在新闻发布会上宣布的。
    杨尚昆主席这次访问是新中国成立以来中国国家元首首次出访拉丁美洲。
