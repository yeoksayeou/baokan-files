Title: Datong Railway Bureau Sets Daily Car Loading Record
Author: Wang Hong
Date: 1989-04-15
Edition: 2

Datong Railway Bureau Sets Daily Car Loading Record

On April 2nd, the Datong Railway Bureau achieved a daily car loading of 5,001 cars, becoming the first railway bureau in the country to break the 5,000-car daily loading mark.

This railway bureau is located in the Datong coalfield and is primarily responsible for loading and transporting coal. It serves 24 provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions, supplying coal to more than 6,000 industrial and mining enterprises. They have worked to uncover potential and expand capacity, thereby enhancing their transportation capabilities.

### 大同局创日装车纪录
王鸿
1989-04-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　大同局创日装车纪录
    本报讯　４月２日，大同铁路分局日装车达５００１车，在全国各铁路分局中首次突破日装车５０００车大关。
    这个铁路分局地处大同煤海，是个以装运煤炭为主的分局，担负着向２４个省、市、自治区的６０００多家工矿企业运煤任务。他们挖潜扩能，提高了运输能力。　　　　　　　　　
　　　　（王鸿）
