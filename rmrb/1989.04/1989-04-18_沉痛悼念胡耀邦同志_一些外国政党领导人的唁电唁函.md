Title: In Deep Mourning for Comrade Hu Yaobang
Author:
Date: 1989-04-18
Edition: 3

	In Deep Mourning for Comrade Hu Yaobang
	Condolences from Leaders of Foreign Political Parties

	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 18 — More foreign political party leaders have sent telegrams and letters of condolence to mourn the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang.

	Romanian Communist Party General Secretary Nicolae Ceausescu sent a telegram to CCP Central Committee General Secretary Zhao Ziyang on April 18, saying: "I am deeply saddened to learn of the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang, a prominent leader of the Chinese Party and state.

	"For this heavy loss, I extend my deepest condolences to you and to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and I convey our sincere sympathies to the bereaved family."

	Czechoslovakian Communist Party Central Committee General Secretary Milos Jakes sent a telegram to CCP Central Committee General Secretary Zhao Ziyang on April 17, saying: "Please allow me to express my deepest condolences to you and your Party Central Committee on the sudden passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang, a member of the CCP Central Committee Political Bureau. His death is a great loss to your Party and the Chinese people. Comrade Hu Yaobang will be forever remembered!"

	Italian Communist Party General Secretary Alessandro Natta sent a letter to the CCP Central Committee on April 15, saying: "The passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang fills us with deep sorrow. Especially during his wise and energetic leadership, our two parties restored relations and together strove to open a new phase in international relations and the world workers' and progressive movements.

	"His passing is a loss to the Communist Party of China, which has lost a staunch fighter for the reform and modernization of the great People's Republic of China. We have lost a dear, respected friend and comrade.

	"I wish to express the deepest condolences of the Italian Communist Party and myself to his family and to the Chinese communists.

	"While expressing our condolences, and in memory of Comrade Hu Yaobang, I wish to reaffirm to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China our firm desire to further develop our friendly and cooperative relations."

	Italian Communist Party Central Committee Chairman Achille Occhetto sent a letter to the Chinese Embassy in Italy on April 17, expressing deep condolences to the Chinese Party and the family of Comrade Hu Yaobang over his passing. In his letter, Occhetto highly praised Comrade Hu Yaobang's "extraordinary intelligence, passion, and courage as a political leader" and his deep affection for the Italian people and communists. Occhetto pointed out that Comrade Hu Yaobang's passing is a "tremendous loss."

	The Central Committee of the French Communist Party sent a telegram to the CCP Central Committee on April 17, saying: "The Central Committee of the French Communist Party is shocked by the sudden passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang, former General Secretary and member of the Political Bureau of the CCP Central Committee.

	"The French Communist Party and its General Secretary, Georges Marchais, extend their deepest condolences to the Communist Party of China and to the family of our close friend, Hu Yaobang, at this sad time.

	"At this unfortunate time, the French Communist Party expresses its full support to the Communist Party of China."

	Namibian SWAPO General Secretary Hage Geingob, on behalf of Chairman Sam Nujoma, sent a letter to CCP Central Committee General Secretary Zhao Ziyang on April 18, saying: "I and all the leaders of the Namibian SWAPO are deeply saddened to learn of the untimely passing of our respected Comrade Hu Yaobang, former General Secretary of the Communist Party of China. Please accept the deep condolences of the Namibian SWAPO."

	Spanish Communist Party Central Political Bureau member Ramon Maria Rodriguez sent a telephone message to the Chinese Embassy in Italy on April 15, expressing condolences over the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang, a member of the CCP Central Committee Political Bureau. Rodriguez pointed out that Comrade Hu Yaobang made significant contributions to the restoration and development of relations between the two parties.

### 沉痛悼念胡耀邦同志　一些外国政党领导人的唁电唁函

1989-04-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　沉痛悼念胡耀邦同志
　　一些外国政党领导人的唁电唁函
    新华社北京４月１８日电　又有一些外国政党的领导人发来唁电、唁函，悼念胡耀邦同志逝世。
    罗马尼亚共产党总书记尼·齐奥塞斯库４月１８日在给赵紫阳总书记的唁电中说：“我沉痛地获悉中共中央政治局委员、中国党和国家的杰出战士胡耀邦同志逝世。
    “对于这一沉重损失，我谨向您、向中国共产党中央委员会表示深切哀悼，并请向已故者家属转达我们诚挚的慰问。”
    捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会总书记米·雅克什４月１７日在致赵紫阳总书记的唁电中说：“请允许我谨向您和你们党中央对中共中央政治局委员胡耀邦同志突然逝世表示沉痛的哀悼。他的去世使你们党和中国人民失去了一位著名的领导人。胡耀邦同志永垂不朽！”
    意大利共产党总书记阿·奥凯托４月１５日在致中共中央的唁函中说：“胡耀邦同志的逝世使我们充满深切悲痛。尤其是在他英明和生气勃勃的领导时期我们两党恢复了关系，并共同努力为开辟国际关系和世界工人运动及进步运动的新阶段作出了贡献。
    “他的逝世使中国共产党失去了一位为伟大的中华人民共和国的革新和现代化而奋斗的坚强战士。我们失去了一位亲爱的、尊敬的朋友和同志。
    “我愿向他的家属、向中国共产党人表达意大利共产党人和我本人诚挚的、最深切的哀悼。
    “在表达哀悼之情的同时，也是为了缅怀胡耀邦同志，我愿向中国共产党中央委员会重申进一步发展我们之间友好合作关系的坚定愿望。”
    意大利共产党中央委员会主席纳塔４月１７日在致中国驻意大利使馆的信中，就胡耀邦同志的逝世向中国党和胡耀邦同志的亲属表示深切的哀悼。纳塔在信中高度评价胡耀邦同志“作为政治领导人所具有的非凡才智、热情和勇气”以及胡耀邦同志对意大利人民和共产党人所怀有的深厚感情。纳塔在信中指出，胡耀邦同志的逝世是“十分巨大的损失”。
    法国共产党中央委员会４月１７日在致中共中央的唁电中说：“法国共产党中央委员会对中共中央前总书记、政治局委员胡耀邦同志突然去世感到震惊。
    “法国共产党及其总书记乔治·马歇在这悲痛时刻向中国共产党以及法国共产党人的亲密朋友——胡耀邦的家属致以沉痛的哀悼。
    “在此不幸之际，法国共产党向中国共产党表示全力的声援。”
    西南非洲人民组织总书记托伊沃代表努乔马主席４月１８日在致赵紫阳总书记的唁函中说：“我和纳米比亚西南非洲人民组织所有领导人悲痛地惊悉中国共产党前总书记、我们尊敬的胡耀邦同志不幸过早逝世。请接受纳米比亚西南非洲人民组织深切的悼念”。
    西班牙共产党中央政治委员会委员罗·马林４月１５日打电话给中国驻意大利使馆，对中共中央政治局委员胡耀邦同志的逝世表示吊唁。罗·马林指出，胡耀邦同志为恢复和发展西中两党的关系作出了贡献。
