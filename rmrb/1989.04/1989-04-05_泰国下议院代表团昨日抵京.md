Title: Thai House of Representatives Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-04-05
Edition: 2

A delegation from the Thai House of Representatives, led by Speaker Bancha Kaewsongkhram, arrived in Beijing today by airplane. The delegation was invited by the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for a friendly visit.

Vice Chairman Ye Jianying and others welcomed the Thai guests at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse.

The purpose of Bancha's visit is to further enhance mutual understanding and friendship between Thailand and China, and to promote friendly exchanges between the legislative bodies of the two countries. The delegation includes Bancha's wife and the second deputy speaker of the House of Representatives, Sawat Pathano, among others.

### 泰国下议院代表团昨日抵京

1989-04-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　泰国下议院代表团昨日抵京
    新华社北京４月４日电  由班乍·盖索恩通议长率领的泰国下议院代表团，应全国人大常委会的邀请前来进行友好访问，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    叶飞副委员长等在钓鱼台国宾馆迎接泰国贵宾。
    班乍一行此行目的是进一步增进泰中两国的相互了解和友谊，推动两国立法机构的友好往来。代表团成员中包括班乍议长的夫人和下议院第二副议长萨瓦·帕他诺等。
