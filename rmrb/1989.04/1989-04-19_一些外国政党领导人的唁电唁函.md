Title: Condolences from Foreign Political Party Leaders
Author:
Date: 1989-04-19
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 19th.

	More foreign political party leaders have sent telegrams and letters of condolence to mourn the passing of Hu Yaobang.

	On April 18th, the Chairman of the Presidency of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, Stane Dolanc, sent a telegram to Zhao Ziyang, General Secretary, saying: "I express my deepest condolences on behalf of the Presidency of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia and personally to you and the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on the sudden passing of Hu Yaobang, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

	"Hu Yaobang's contributions to the development of relations between the League of Communists of Yugoslavia and the Communist Party of China, and to the comprehensive development of relations between the two countries, will forever be remembered."

	On April 18th, the veteran revolutionary and old friend of the Chinese people, Hoang Van Hoan of Vietnam, sent a letter to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, saying: "I was shocked to learn of the untimely passing of Hu Yaobang, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. I express my deepest condolences to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Chinese people, and the family of Hu Yaobang.

	"Hu Yaobang was a great proletarian revolutionary and statesman of China. He made outstanding contributions to the revolution and construction of the Chinese people. The passing of Hu Yaobang is a great loss to the Communist Party of China and the Chinese people, but his great achievements will forever be remembered by the Chinese people."

	On April 18th, the Secretary-General of the Revolutionary Party of Tanzania, R. K. Kawa, sent a telegram to Zhao Ziyang, General Secretary, saying: "The National Executive Committee of the Revolutionary Party of Tanzania was shocked to learn of the untimely passing of Hu Yaobang, former General Secretary of the Communist Party of China.

	"Since the establishment of the socialist system in China, the late Hu Yaobang has been a passionate revolutionary and an elder statesman of the Chinese revolution. In the course of China's modernization of the socialist system, he was a great fighter in implementing the Four Modernizations policy of the Communist Party of China.

	"The untimely passing of Hu Yaobang is not only a loss to the Communist Party of China and the Chinese people but also to the Revolutionary Party of Tanzania and the Tanzanian people, because he devoted his life to the unremitting and selfless efforts to promote the relations between the two parties, the two countries, and the two peoples."

### 一些外国政党领导人的唁电唁函

1989-04-19
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　一些外国政党领导人的唁电唁函
    新华社北京４月１９日电　又有一些外国政党的领导人发来唁电、唁函，悼念胡耀邦同志逝世。
    南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央主席团主席舒瓦尔４月１８日在致赵紫阳总书记的唁电中说：“对中共中央政治局委员胡耀邦同志的突然逝世，我谨代表南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央主席团，并以我个人名义，向您和中国共产党中央政治局表示最深切的哀悼。
    “胡耀邦同志对发展南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟和中国共产党之间的关系，对发展南中两国的全面关系所作出的贡献，将永远留在我们的记忆之中。”
    越南老一辈革命家、中国人民的老朋友黄文欢４月１８日在致中共中央的唁函中说：“惊悉中共中央政治局委员胡耀邦同志不幸逝世，我谨向中共中央和中国人民以及胡耀邦同志的家属，表示深切的哀悼。
    “胡耀邦同志是中国伟大的无产阶级革命家、政治家，他为中国人民的革命与建设作出了卓越的贡献。胡耀邦同志的逝世是中国共产党和中国人民的重大损失，但他的丰功伟绩将永远铭记在中国人民的心中。”
    坦桑尼亚革命党总书记拉·卡瓦瓦４月１８日在致赵紫阳总书记的唁电中说：“坦桑尼亚革命党全国执行委员会获悉中国共产党前总书记胡耀邦同志过早逝世的噩耗，深表震惊。
    “自从社会主义制度在中国建立以来，已故胡耀邦同志一直是一位热情的革命家和中国革命的老一辈政治家。在中国人民致力于中国社会主义制度现代化的事业中，他在贯彻中国共产党四个现代化方针方面，是一名伟大的斗士。
    “胡耀邦同志的过早逝世不仅是中国共产党和中国人民的损失，而且也是坦桑尼亚革命党和坦桑尼亚人民的损失，因为他毕生为促进我们两党、两国和两国人民的关系进行了不懈和无私的努力。”
