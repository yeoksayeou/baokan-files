Title: Salvadoran Attorney General Killed in Bombing; Guerrillas Attack Government Forces
Author: Guo Weicheng
Date: 1989-04-20
Edition: 3

	Salvadoran Attorney General Garcia Albarado was killed in a bombing today in the capital, San Salvador.

	Salvadoran President-elect Cristiani warned that due to the increasing violence in the capital, civil guarantees may be suspended.

	Meanwhile, Salvadoran guerrillas began attacking government forces in the northern province of Chalatenango this morning. This attack comes after the Farabundo Marti National Liberation Front's peace proposal was rejected by President-elect Cristiani. The Farabundo Marti National Liberation Front demands that the Nationalist Republican Alliance, which won the election, form a transitional government and hold new elections, with the FMLN guerrillas participating in national life as a political force.

### 萨总检察长被炸身亡  游击队向政府军再次发起进攻
郭伟成
1989-04-20
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　萨总检察长被炸身亡    
    游击队向政府军再次发起进攻
    本报墨西哥城４月１９日电  记者郭伟成报道：萨尔瓦多总检察长加西亚·阿尔巴拉多今天在首都圣萨尔瓦多被炸身亡。
    萨尔瓦多当选总统克里斯蒂亚尼警告说，由于首都暴力活动的增多，将可能导致取消公民保障。
    与此同时，萨尔瓦多游击队今天早晨开始向北方恰拉特南戈省的政府军发起进攻。这次进攻是在法拉本多·马蒂阵线的和平建议被当选总统克里斯蒂亚尼拒绝后发起的。法拉本多·马蒂阵线要求大选获胜的民族主义共和联盟组织一个过渡政府，重新举行大选，法·马蒂阵线游击队作为政治力量参与国家生活。
