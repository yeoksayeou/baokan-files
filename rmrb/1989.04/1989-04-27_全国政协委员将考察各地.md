Title: National CPPCC Members to Inspect Various Regions
Author:
Date: 1989-04-27
Edition: 2

	National CPPCC members will be organized this year to inspect Tianjin, Shanxi, Hebei, Inner Mongolia, and the Beijing region.

	It is understood that the focus of this year's inspections will be to investigate and understand the actual progress of rectifying the economic environment, rectifying economic order, and comprehensively deepening reforms. The main contents of the inspections include industrial and agricultural production, social conditions, educational undertakings, and the lives of the people.

### 全国政协委员将考察各地

1989-04-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　全国政协委员将考察各地
    新华社北京４月２７日电  全国政协今年将组织在京的全国政协委员分别到天津、山西、河北、内蒙古和北京地区考察。
    据了解，今年考察的重点是调查了解治理经济环境、整顿经济秩序、全面深化改革的实际进展情况。视察的主要内容包括工农业生产、社会状况、教育事业和人民群众生活等方面。
