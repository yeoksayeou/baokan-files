Title: Central Holds Symposium with Democratic Parties and Non-Party Personnel to Listen to Opinions on Education Development and Reform
Author:
Date: 1989-04-21
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 21st.

The Communist Party of China Central Committee held a symposium with representatives from democratic parties and non-party personnel to listen to their discussions and opinions on the draft of the "Decision of the CPC Central Committee on Several Issues Concerning Education Development and Reform."

The CPC Central Committee held a democratic consultation meeting on April 17th. On April 18th and 19th, representatives from various democratic parties and non-party personnel were divided into 7 groups to discuss the draft decision thoroughly. Everyone, with a sense of responsibility towards the modernization drive of our country, spoke enthusiastically, creating a lively atmosphere.

At today's symposium, Cheng Siyuan, Gao Tian, Fang Rongxin, Wan Guoqian, Chen Shunli, An Taigang, Xu Jialu, Tian Maiji, Wen Zhe, and Huang Fangyi introduced the discussion situation of their respective groups and their main opinions on the draft decision.

At the end of the meeting, Li Tieying, on behalf of the CPC Central Committee, expressed his thanks to the participants. He said, "The symposium was very successful, reflecting the concern of all sectors of society for the education cause and the overall modernization drive and national rejuvenation. It also embodies the spirit of multi-party cooperation under the leadership of the Communist Party. The CPC Central Committee will seriously study everyone's opinions and suggestions."

### 中央召开民主党派和无党派人士座谈会  听取对教育发展和改革意见

1989-04-21
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　中央召开民主党派和无党派人士座谈会    
    听取对教育发展和改革意见
    据新华社北京４月２１日电  中共中央政治局委员李铁映、书记处书记芮杏文今天上午代表党中央在中南海怀仁堂举行座谈会，听取各民主党派和无党派人士讨论《中共中央关于教育发展和改革若干问题的决定（草案）》的情况和意见。
    中共中央４月１７日召开民主协商会之后，４月１８、１９两日，各民主党派和无党派人士的代表分为７个组，对《决定（草案）》进行了认真讨论。大家本着对我国四化大业认真负责的态度，踊跃发言，气氛活跃。
    在今天的座谈会上，程思远、高天、方荣欣、万国权、陈舜礼、安太庠、许嘉璐、田麦久、文喆、黄方毅等介绍了分组讨论的情况和对《决定（草案）》的主要意见。
    会议结束时，李铁映代表中共中央向参加座谈会的同志表示感谢。他说，座谈会开得很好，体现了社会各界对教育事业以至整个四化建设和民族振兴的关心，也体现了共产党领导的多党合作的精神。中共中央将认真研究大家的意见和建议。
