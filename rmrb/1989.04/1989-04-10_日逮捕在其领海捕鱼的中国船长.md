Title: Japan Arrests Chinese Ship Captain Fishing in Its Territorial Waters
Author:
Date: 1989-04-10
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, April 8th.

Japan's Fukuoka Maritime Safety Agency today arrested the captain of the Chinese fishing vessel Liao Yu 721, Zhu Zhitian, for violating Japanese laws regarding foreign fishing.

According to reports, Zhu Zhitian is the captain of a fishing fleet consisting of four vessels belonging to the Dalian Ocean Fisheries Company. Today at 8 AM, the fleet was fishing without permission in Japanese territorial waters about 16 kilometers northwest of Suino Island in the village of Oshima, Fukuoka Prefecture, and caught 10 kilograms of sardines.

### 日逮捕在其领海捕鱼的中国船长

1989-04-10
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　日逮捕在其领海捕鱼的中国船长
    新华社东京４月８日电据共同社报道，日本福冈海上保安部今天以违反日本关于外国人渔业的有关法律为由，逮捕了中国大连海洋渔业公司所属的辽渔７２１号船船长朱志田。
    据报道，朱志田是大连海洋渔业公司由４艘渔船组成的渔船队队长。今天上午８时，该渔船队在福冈县宗像郡大岛村的冲之岛西北约１６公里的日本领海内，未经许可进行捕渔作业，捕获１０公斤沙丁鱼。
