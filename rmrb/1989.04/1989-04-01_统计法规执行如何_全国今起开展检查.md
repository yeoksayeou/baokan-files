Title: Inspection of the Enforcement of Statistical Regulations Nationwide Begins Today
Author: Zhang Hongzhi, Xu Baojian
Date: 1989-04-01
Edition: 2

The State Statistical Bureau, the Legislative Affairs Office of the State Council, and the Ministry of Supervision have decided to jointly conduct a nationwide inspection of the enforcement of statistical regulations starting from April 1.

The focus of this inspection will be to investigate and address various illegal activities involving falsification of statistical data and information for personal gain.

### 统计法规执行如何  全国今起开展检查
张鸿志　许宝健
1989-04-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　统计法规执行如何  全国今起开展检查
    本报北京３月３１日讯  国家统计局、国务院法制局、监察部决定，从４月１日起联合开展全国统计法规执行情况大检查。
    这次大检查的重点是查处在统计数据和信息上弄虚作假、以数谋私的各种违法行为。　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　（张鸿志　许宝健）
