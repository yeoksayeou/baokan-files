Title: Brief News
Author: Hua Chun
Date: 1989-04-23
Edition: 6

	Brief News

	▲ The book "Economic Supervision Practices" edited by Qi Tian is soon to be published by the Beijing Publishing House. The "Compilation of Current Economic Supervision Regulations" will also be published.

		(Hua Chun)

	▲ The book "Exploration·Reform·Revitalization—Economy in the Primary Stage of Socialism," edited by Wei Xinghua and Gu Xuerong, has been published by the People's University of China Press.
