Title: The People's Daily Publishes an Article Introducing the Significant Changes in the Spiritual Outlook of Soviet People
Author:
Date: 1989-04-19
Edition: 1

Today, the seventh page of this newspaper publishes an article by the leader of the Chinese journalists' delegation who recently returned from the Soviet Union, Tan Wenrui, titled "Impressions of a Revisit to the Soviet Union." The article uses numerous firsthand observations to introduce the significant changes in the spiritual outlook of the Soviet people in recent years, as well as the progress and challenges of the Soviet Union's economic and political reforms.

### 本报今天七版发表文章介绍苏联人民精神面貌显著变化

1989-04-19
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　本报今天七版发表文章介绍苏联人民精神面貌显著变化
    本报讯　今天本报七版发表最近访苏归来的中国新闻工作者代表团团长谭文瑞题为《重访苏联印象》一文，以耳闻目睹的大量事实，介绍苏联人民近年来精神面貌发生的显著变化，以及苏联经济、政治体制改革的进程和困扰。
