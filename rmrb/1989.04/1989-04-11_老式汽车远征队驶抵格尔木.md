Title: Vintage Car Expedition Arrives in Golmud
Author: Ma Qianli
Date: 1989-04-11
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Golmud, April 9th — This afternoon, the 15 vintage cars of the Beijing-Paris vintage car expedition, escorting a 1903 Itala vintage car, drove into Golmud City, known as the "City of Cars," amidst the cheers of the local residents.

Golmud City is a key transportation hub in western Qinghai, connecting Tibet, Xinjiang, and Gansu.

The vintage car expedition departed from Xining on April 7th, crossed the Sun and Moon Mountains, traversed the vast desert of the Qaidam Basin, and after a two-day journey of 800 kilometers through wind and snow, finally arrived in Golmud.

### 老式汽车远征队驶抵格尔木
马千里
1989-04-11
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　老式汽车远征队驶抵格尔木
    新华社格尔木４月９日电　（记者马千里）今天下午，北京——巴黎老式汽车远征队的１５辆汽车簇拥着一辆产于１９０３年的伊塔拉老式汽车，在市民的夹道欢迎中驶入有汽车城之称的青海省格尔木市。
    格尔木市是青海西部连结西藏、新疆、甘肃的交通要道。
    老式汽车远征队是７日由西宁出发，翻越了日月山，穿过了柴达木盆地大戈壁，经过两天８００公里风雪征程才到达格尔木的。
