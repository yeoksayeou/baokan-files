Title: Foreign Affairs Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-04-04
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 4th.

**Foreign Affairs Brief**

▲ State Councilor and Minister of the State Education Commission Li Tieying met with New Zealand Foreign Minister Russell Marshall at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse on April 3rd in the afternoon.

▲ Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Liao Hansheng met with former Vice Chairman of the Federal Executive Council and former Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Parliament of Yugoslavia Sukovic at the Great Hall of the People on April 3rd.

▲ Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Ye Fei met with the delegation from the Department of Local Government of the Philippines, led by Deputy Minister Rubilliar, at the Great Hall of the People on April 3rd.

▲ Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the All-China Women's Federation Chen Muxia met and hosted a banquet for the delegation from the Iranian Women's Association, led by Deputy Chairman and Member of Parliament Taheri Hadichi Dabaho, at the Great Hall of the People on April 3rd.

This is the first Iranian women's delegation to visit China in 10 years.

▲ The Zurich Brass Band from Switzerland, consisting of 90 members, performed the national anthems of both China and Switzerland on April 3rd in Beijing, receiving applause from over 1,000 audience members. Founded in 1884, the Zurich Band is one of Switzerland's finest ensembles, performing and rehearsing 90 times a year, with all members except the conductor being amateur musicians.

Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Simayi Aimaidi and President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Zhang Wenjin attended the performance.

▲ Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Zhou Gucheng met with Dr. Pe Dan, Minister of Health of Myanmar, and his delegation at the Great Hall of the People on April 2nd in the afternoon.

### 外事简讯

1989-04-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　外事简讯
    ▲国务委员兼国家教委主任李铁映４月３日下午在钓鱼台国宾馆会见了新西兰外交部长拉塞尔·马歇尔。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长廖汉生４月３日在人民大会堂会见南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会前副主席、议会外委会前主席舒科维奇。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长叶飞４月３日在人民大会堂会见了由副部长鲁比利亚尔率领的菲律宾地方政府部代表团。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长、全国妇联主席陈慕华４月３日在人民大会堂会见并宴请由伊朗妇女协会代主席、国会议员塔海里·哈迪奇·达巴霍夫人率领的伊朗妇女代表团。
    这是１０年来的第一个伊朗妇女代表团来中国访问。
    ▲瑞士苏黎世吹奏乐团一行９０人４月３日在北京演奏了瑞中两国的国歌，赢得１０００多名观众的阵阵掌声。成立于１８８４年的苏黎世乐团是瑞士最优秀的乐团之一，每年排练、演出９０场，除指挥外的所有成员均为业余演奏家。
    全国政协副主席司马义·艾买提、中国人民对外友好协会会长章文晋等观看了演出。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长周谷城４月２日下午在人民大会堂会见了缅甸卫生部部长佩登博士一行。　　
　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
