Title: IOC President Samaranch Congratulates Direct Sports Exchange Across the Taiwan Strait
Author: Jiang Jian Guo Liu Xiao Ming
Date: 1989-04-10
Edition: 4

	IOC President Samaranch Congratulates Direct Sports Exchange Across the Taiwan Strait

	Xinhua News Agency Dortmund, April 9th - Reporters Jiang Jian Guo and Liu Xiao Ming report: IOC President Juan Antonio Samaranch said today at noon that he is "very, very happy" that direct sports exchanges have been achieved between mainland China and Taiwan. He also said that this is just the beginning, and he hopes that the sports relations between the mainland and Taiwan will become increasingly normalized.

	Samaranch said he is very pleased to come to Dortmund to watch the 40th World Table Tennis Championships and is deeply impressed by the enthusiastic audience, the excellent organization, and the athletes' performances.

### 国际奥委会主席萨马兰奇祝贺  海峡两岸实现直接体育交流
江建国　刘小明
1989-04-10
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：体育

　　国际奥委会主席萨马兰奇祝贺
    海峡两岸实现直接体育交流
    本报多特蒙德４月９日电  记者江建国、刘小明报道：国际奥委会主席萨马兰奇今天中午在这里说，他对中国大陆和台北之间实现了直接的体育交流感到“非常、非常高兴”。他还说，这仅仅是开始，他希望大陆与台北之间体育关系越来越正常化。
    萨马兰奇说，他很高兴来到多特蒙德观看第４０届世界乒乓球锦标赛，并对观众的踊跃、出色的组织工作和运动员的表现留下了很深的印象。
