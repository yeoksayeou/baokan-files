Title: Renowned Biologist and Virologist Gao Shangyin Passes Away
Author: Yang Jian Guo
Date: 1989-04-28
Edition: 2

Renowned biologist and virologist Gao Shangyin passed away. The Xinhua News Agency reported from Wuhan on April 28: Gao Shangyin, a renowned biologist and virologist, passed away due to illness on April 24 in Wuhan at the age of 80. Gao Shangyin was a native of Jiaxing, Zhejiang. Before his passing, he served as Vice Chairman of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, a member of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Vice Chairman of the Academic Committee of Wuhan University, and Honorary Director of the Wuhan Institute of Virology, Chinese Academy of Sciences.

### 著名生物学家病毒学家高尚荫逝世
杨建国
1989-04-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　著名生物学家病毒学家高尚荫逝世
    新华社武汉４月２８日电（通讯员杨建国）我国著名生物学家、病毒学家高尚荫同志因病于４月２４日在武汉逝世，终年８０岁。高尚荫同志是浙江嘉兴人。他生前任湖北省政协副主席、中国科学院学部委员、武汉大学校务委员会副主任委员、中科院武汉病毒所名誉所长。
