Title: Spring Rain Falls Across Northern Wheat Regions
Author:
Date: 1989-04-21
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 21st.

From 6:00 on the 19th to 6:00 on the 21st, eastern Gansu, Ningxia, most of Shaanxi, Shanxi, Hebei, Beijing, Tianjin, and western Shandong in the northern winter wheat-producing regions experienced widespread spring rain.

Areas with rainfall between 20-30 millimeters include parts of Ningxia, northern Shaanxi, and Hebei. Regions with rainfall exceeding 30 millimeters include Yinchuan in Ningxia, Shangluo in Shaanxi, Yuncheng in Shanxi, Ba County, Xingtai, and Shijiazhuang in Hebei, Shangqiu in Henan, and Beijing. It is forecasted that from the 21st to the 22nd, central and eastern Inner Mongolia, northern and eastern Hebei, and most of the three northeastern provinces will receive 3 to 15 millimeters of rainfall.

### 春雨普降北方麦区

1989-04-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　春雨普降北方麦区
    新华社北京４月２１日电  从１９日６时至２１日６时，甘肃东部、宁夏、陕西、山西大部、河北、京津地区、山东西部等北方冬小麦产区普降春雨。
    降雨量２０—３０毫米的地区有：宁夏、陕北、河北等地的部分地区，雨量大于３０毫米的地区有：宁夏盐池，陕西商洛，山西运城，河北霸县、邢台、石家庄，河南商丘，北京。预计２１日至２２日，内蒙古中部和东部、河北北部及东北３省大部将有３至１５毫米的降雨。
