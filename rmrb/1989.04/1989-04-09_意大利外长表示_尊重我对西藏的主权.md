Author:
Date: 1989-04-09
Edition: 4

Italian Foreign Minister Expresses Respect for China's Sovereignty Over Tibet

	NEW DELHI, April 7 (Xinhua) -- Italian Foreign Minister Gianni De Michelis today expressed that Italy respects China's sovereignty over Tibet and supports China's unity and territorial integrity.

	De Michelis made the remarks while meeting with Chinese Vice Foreign Minister Zhou Nan.

	Zhou Nan arrived in Rome on April 3 at the invitation of Italian Deputy Foreign Minister Gianni Bonadonna. During his stay in Rome, Zhou exchanged views with Bonadonna on the new developments in the current international situation, the peace process in Cambodia, Afghanistan, and the Middle East, the establishment of a unified European market, and bilateral relations.

	Zhou Nan and his delegation will leave Rome this afternoon to visit the Federal Republic of Germany.

### 意大利外长表示  尊重我对西藏的主权

1989-04-09
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　意大利外长表示
    尊重我对西藏的主权
    新华社罗马４月７日电  意大利外长安德烈奥蒂今天下午在这里表示，意大利尊重中国对西藏的主权，尊重中国的统一和领土完整。
    安德烈奥蒂是在会见中国副外长周南时作这一表示的。
    周南副外长是应意大利副外长博纳卢米的邀请，于３日抵达罗马访问的。在罗马逗留期间，周南同博纳卢米就当前国际局势发展的新特点、柬埔寨、阿富汗和中东和平进程问题、欧洲建立统一大市场问题以及双边关系交换了意见。
    周南副外长一行将于今天下午离开罗马，前往联邦德国继续访问。
