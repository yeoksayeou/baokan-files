Title: Deng Xiaoping Signs Central Military Commission Order to Confer Hero Title on North Sea Fleet Mobile Salvage Team
Author:
Date: 1989-04-17
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 17th.

The Central Military Commission of the People's Republic of China recently issued an order, conferring the honorary title of "Hero Salvage Team" on the mobile salvage team of the rescue ship flotilla of the North Sea Fleet of the People's Liberation Army Navy.

The order, signed by Military Commission Chairman Deng Xiaoping, states that since its establishment in August 1958, the mobile salvage team of the rescue ship flotilla of the North Sea Fleet has always remembered the army's mission of serving the people wholeheartedly. They have carried forward the revolutionary heroic spirit of fearing neither hardship nor sacrifice, and have successfully completed numerous rescue missions.

Since 1980, they have carried out more than 100 rescue missions, saving and salvaging 49 domestic and foreign ships in distress, and rescuing 374 people in danger. They have made significant contributions to ensuring the safety of the lives and property of the fleet and the people, supporting national economic construction, and reducing economic losses.

The order calls on all military personnel to learn from the heroic salvage team, who always prioritize the interests of the state and the people, and are willing to sacrifice their lives to protect national property and the safety of the people's lives. Learn from their fearlessness in the face of danger, their bravery in the face of adversity, and their spirit of overcoming all difficulties with revolutionary heroism. Learn from their resolute execution of orders, their tireless and continuous struggle, and their tenacious spirit. Learn from their diligent training and mastery of solid skills, and their high sense of responsibility. Take them as examples, do a solid job in their respective duties, consider the overall situation, work hard, and dedicate themselves to national defense.

### 邓小平签署中央军委命令　授予海军北海舰队机动救捞中队英雄称号

1989-04-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　邓小平签署中央军委命令
　　授予海军北海舰队机动救捞中队英雄称号
    新华社北京四月十七日电　中华人民共和国中央军事委员会近日发布命令，授予人民解放军海军北海舰队防救船大队机动救捞中队“英雄救捞中队”荣誉称号。
    由军委主席邓小平签署的命令说，海军北海舰队防救船大队机动救捞中队自一九五八年八月组建以来，牢记我军全心全意为人民服务的宗旨，发扬不怕艰难困苦、不怕流血牺牲的革命英雄主义精神，多次出色地完成抢险救生任务。
    一九八○年以来，他们先后执行各种抢险救生任务一百多次，援救打捞国内外遇险船只四十九艘，抢救遇险群众三百七十四人，为保障舰艇部队和人民群众的生命财产安全，为支援国家经济建设，减少经济损失做出了重大贡献。
    军委的命令号召全军指战员要学习英雄救捞中队时刻以国家和人民的利益为重，为保护国家财产和人民的生命安全舍生忘死的崇高品质；学习他们不畏艰险，临危不惧，压倒一切困难的革命英雄主义精神；学习他们坚决执行命令，不怕疲劳，连续奋战的顽强作风；学习他们刻苦训练，掌握过硬本领的高度责任感。以他们为榜样，扎扎实实做好本职工作，顾全大局，艰苦奋斗，献身国防。
