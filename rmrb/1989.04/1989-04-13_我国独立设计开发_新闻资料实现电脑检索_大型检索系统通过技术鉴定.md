Title: China Independently Develops News Information Retrieval System
Author: Xīn Chéngshèng
Date: 1989-04-13
Edition: 2

Beijing Information Engineering University and Xinhua News Agency have jointly developed a large-scale news information retrieval system, which passed a technical appraisal in Beijing today.

This system is China's first independently designed and developed large-scale news information retrieval system, achieving an advanced level in technology comparable to similar systems internationally.

Its successful development marks China's transition from manual to computer-based news information retrieval.

Experts believe that developing a large-scale news information retrieval system using a relational database is a first in China and is also advanced internationally. The system's multiple user-friendly interfaces and clear explanatory capabilities greatly facilitate the use of the system by a wide range of news professionals, editors, and reporters, offering high social and economic benefits.

The appraisal meeting also demonstrated Beijing Information Engineering University's related phase achievements, including full-text retrieval models in Chinese and English and an automatic indexing model for Chinese. These achievements lay the foundation for developing and realizing a large-scale, fast, real-time, and networked national news and newspaper information retrieval system during the "Eighth Five-Year Plan" period.

### 我国独立设计开发　新闻资料实现电脑检索　大型检索系统通过技术鉴定
辛乘胜
1989-04-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　我国独立设计开发
　　新闻资料实现电脑检索
　　大型检索系统通过技术鉴定
    本报北京4月11日讯　北京信息工程学院和新华通讯社合作开发的大型新闻资料检索系统，今天在京通过技术鉴定。
    该系统是我国第一个独立设计、开发的大型新闻资料检索系统，在技术上达到了国际上同类系统的先进水平。
    它的研制成功，使我国新闻资料的检索从原始的手工检索方式进入计算机检索的新时期。
    专家们认为：用关系数据库系统开发大型新闻资料检索系统，在国内还是首例，在国际上也属先进；系统提供的多种友好的用户接口和清晰的解释能力，大大方便新闻界广大资料员、编辑和记者使用，具有很高的社会和经济效益。
    鉴定会还演示了北京信息工程学院的有关阶段成果，包括中、英文全文检索模型和汉语自动标引模型，这为“八五”期间开发和实现大信息量、快速、实时、网络型的全国新闻、报纸资料检索系统奠定了基础。
                （辛乘胜）
