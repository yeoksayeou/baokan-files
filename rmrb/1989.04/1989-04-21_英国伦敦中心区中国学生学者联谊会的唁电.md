Title: Condolence Telegram from the Chinese Students and Scholars Association in Central London, UK
Author:
Date: 1989-04-21
Edition: 3

The Chinese Students and Scholars Association in Central London, UK, sent a condolence telegram to the Office for the Funeral of Comrade Hu Yaobang at the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on April 21, mourning the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang. The full text is as follows:

“It is with deep sorrow that we learned of the untimely passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang, an outstanding leader of the Party and the state. We express our profound condolences.

Comrade Hu Yaobang was a man of integrity and openness, who respected knowledge and was bold in reform. His passing is an irreplaceable loss to the cause of reform and opening up in our motherland.”

### 英国伦敦中心区中国学生学者联谊会的唁电

1989-04-21
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　英国伦敦中心区中国学生学者联谊会的唁电
    本报讯  英国伦敦中心区中国学生学者联谊会４月２１日给中共中央胡耀邦同志治丧办公室发来唁电，悼念胡耀邦同志逝世。全文如下：
    “痛悉党和国家卓越领导人胡耀邦同志不幸逝世，深表哀悼。
    胡耀邦同志一生光明磊落，思想解放，尊重知识，勇于改革。他的逝世是祖国改革、开放事业不可估量的损失。”
