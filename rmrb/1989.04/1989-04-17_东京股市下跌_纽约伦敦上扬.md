Title: Tokyo Stock Market Falls, New York and London Rise
Author:
Date: 1989-04-17
Edition: 3

Last week, the Tokyo stock market declined, while the London and New York stock markets continued to rise.

According to reports, from April 10 to 14, the Nikkei 225 Industrial Average on the Tokyo stock market fell by 34.91 points, closing at 33,150.14 points on the last trading day of April 14. The average daily trading volume for this week was 8.738 billion shares, compared to 16.6 billion shares the previous week. The decline was due to traders' concerns over Japan's political outlook.

The Dow Jones Industrial Average rose by 32.26 points last week, closing at 2,337.06 points on April 14.

### 东京股市下跌　纽约伦敦上扬

1989-04-17
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　东京股市下跌　纽约伦敦上扬
    新华社北京４月１７日电　上周，东京股市转跌，伦敦和纽约股市继续回升。
    据报道，从４月１０日到１４日的交易周内，东京股票市场上日经２２５种工业股票平均指数跌落３４．９１点，到１４日最后一个交易日收盘时降到３３１５０．１４点。这一周内日平均交易量为８．７３８亿股，而前一周为１６．６亿股，下跌原因是交易者对日本政治前景担忧。
    道·琼斯３０种工业股票平均指数上周上升３２．２６点，到１４日交易结束时升到２３３７．０６点。
