Title: Italian Leaders Meet with Walesa, Decide to Aid Poland in Promoting Reforms
Author:
Date: 1989-04-22
Edition: 4

Italian President Cossiga and Prime Minister De Mita today separately met with visiting Polish Solidarity Union Chairman Walesa.

Cossiga told Walesa that Italy and Western European countries need to genuinely cooperate with and aid Poland and other Eastern European countries. This is a necessity for easing tensions and promoting peace.

During his meeting with Prime Minister De Mita, Walesa said that Poland's economic development is slow, and its economic management and technology are backward. De Mita expressed that Italy will provide aid to Poland to promote its political and economic reforms.

On the same day, Italian Christian Democratic Party Secretary Forlani, Communist Party General Secretary Occhetto, and Socialist Party Secretary Craxi also separately met with Walesa and expressed their support for the Polish Solidarity Union.

### 意大利领导人会见瓦文萨时表示  决定对波提供援助促进改革

1989-04-22
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　意大利领导人会见瓦文萨时表示    
    决定对波提供援助促进改革
    新华社罗马４月２１日电  意大利总统科西加和总理德米塔今天在这里分别会见了来访的波兰团结工会主席瓦文萨。
    科西加对瓦文萨说，意大利和西欧国家有必要对波兰和其它东欧国家给予真正的合作和帮助，这是缓和与和平的需要。
    在同德米塔总理的会见中，瓦文萨说，波兰经济发展缓慢，经济管理和技术落后。德米塔总理表示，意大利将向波兰提供帮助，以促进波兰的政治和经济的改革。
    同一天，意大利天民党书记福拉尼、共产党总书记奥凯托和社会党书记克拉克西也分别会见了瓦文萨，并对波兰团结工会表示支持。
