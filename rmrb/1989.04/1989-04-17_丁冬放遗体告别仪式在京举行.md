Title: Funeral for Ding Dongfang Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-04-17
Edition: 2

The funeral for Ding Dongfang, former vice governor of the People's Bank of China, was held in Beijing today. Ding Dongfang passed away on March 23, 1989, at the age of 80 due to illness.

Ding Dongfang was a native of Xinghua City, Jiangsu Province. He joined the revolution in 1929 and became a member of the Communist Party of China in February 1930. Throughout his 60-year revolutionary career, Ding Dongfang firmly believed in Marxism-Leninism, upheld communist convictions, and steadfastly implemented the party's line, principles, and policies. He dedicated himself wholeheartedly to the party and the people's interests.

After the founding of New China, Ding Dongfang consistently worked in the field of finance and economics, making outstanding contributions to the socialist modernization drive and the party's financial cause.

Today's funeral was held at the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery in Beijing. Wreaths were sent by Chen Yun, Li Xiannian, Yao Yilin, Wang Zhen, Hu Qiaomu, Chen Muhua, Wang Baoqian, Li Guixian, Fang Yi, and Cai Chang, as well as the Central Organization Department of the Communist Party of China and the People's Bank of China.

### 丁冬放遗体告别仪式在京举行

1989-04-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　丁冬放遗体告别仪式在京举行
    新华社北京４月１７日电　中国人民银行原副行长丁冬放同志，因病于１９８９年３月２３日在北京逝世，终年８０岁。
    丁冬放同志是江苏省兴化市人。他于１９２９年参加革命，１９３０年２月加入中国共产党。在６０年的革命生涯中，丁冬放同志坚信马列主义，坚持共产主义信念，坚定不移地执行党的路线、方针和政策，忠心耿耿为党和人民利益而奋斗。
    新中国成立后，丁冬放同志一直从事财政金融工作，为社会主义四化建设和党的金融事业作出了卓越的贡献。
    今天上午在北京八宝山革命公墓举行了向丁冬放同志遗体告别仪式。陈云、李先念、姚依林、王震、胡乔木、陈慕华、王丙乾、李贵鲜、方毅、蔡畅同志和中共中央组织部、中国人民银行总行送了花圈。
