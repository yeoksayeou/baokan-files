Title: Construction of the Main Bridge Steel Girders for the Jiujiang Yangtze River Bridge Begins
Author: Fei Weiwei Zhang Liming
Date: 1989-04-20
Edition: 2

The main bridge steel girders for the Jiujiang Yangtze River Bridge in Jiangxi, currently the longest span railway and highway combined bridge in Asia, have recently begun full-scale manufacturing at the Shanhaiguan Bridge Factory.

The Jiujiang Yangtze River Bridge is 7,675.4 meters long in total, with the main bridge steel girders measuring 1,806.6 meters. It consists of four sections with 11 spans, with the largest span reaching 216 meters. The bridge employs new structural designs, materials, technologies, and manufacturing processes, representing the advanced level of bridge construction in the 1980s.

### 九江长江大桥正桥钢梁开制
费伟伟　张立明
1989-04-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　九江长江大桥正桥钢梁开制
    本报山海关４月２０日电  目前亚洲铁路公路两用桥中跨度最大的江西九江长江大桥正桥钢梁，近日在山海关桥梁工厂全面开工制造。
    九江长江大桥全长７６７５．４米，正桥钢梁长为１８０６．６米，共四联１１孔，其中最大孔跨度达２１６米。该桥采用新结构、新材料、新技术、新工艺，具有８０年代世界建桥新水平。　
　　　（费伟伟　张立明）
