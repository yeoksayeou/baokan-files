Title: Photograph
Author: Huang Junying
Date: 1989-04-05
Edition: 5

The photograph was taken on March 2 of this year on Beidajie Street in the city of Qianshan County. The young boy in the picture, Lü Jiaceng, is 13 years old and lives in Liuyang Township, Taihu County, Anhui. Due to having many sisters, his mother passed away early, and his father did not allow him to attend school. He has to go to the streets of a foreign land to mend shoes for a living.
