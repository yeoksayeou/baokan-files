Title: Comrade Hu Yaobang Visits a Peasant's Home
Author: Zhao Desu
Date: 1989-04-26
Edition: 6

On June 18, 1985, Comrade Hu Yaobang visited the home of peasant Guo Shuanwang in Wencheng Township, Jishan County, Shanxi Province. He learned about the production and living conditions of the people in the mountainous areas.
