Title: Shandong Increases Production of Fertilizers and Pesticides
Author: Jià Jiànzhōu
Date: 1989-04-15
Edition: 2

Reporter Jià Jiànzhōu reports from Jinan: The Shandong Provincial Government has mobilized relevant departments to increase the production of fertilizers and pesticides to contribute to achieving a bumper harvest.

The provincial government recently held a teleconference, requiring the coal, power, and materials departments to ensure the supply of energy and raw materials. Various levels of economic commissions and chemical industry management departments should proactively and promptly address the difficulties faced by fertilizer and pesticide production enterprises. For small-scale fertilizer production electricity, the electricity construction fund will be exempted. Various levels of banks should provide preferential support to fertilizer and pesticide enterprises in terms of working capital loans.

### 山东增产化肥农药
贾建舟
1989-04-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　山东增产化肥农药
    本报济南４月１４日电　记者贾建舟报道：山东省政府动员有关部门增产化肥农药，为夺取农业丰收做出贡献。
    省政府日前召开电话会议，要求煤炭、电力、物资部门在能源、原材料方面保证供应；各级经委和化工主管部门主动、及时地为化肥、农药生产企业排忧解难。对于小化肥生产用电，一律免征电力建设基金。各级银行对化肥、农药企业，要在流动资金贷款方面予以重点扶持。
