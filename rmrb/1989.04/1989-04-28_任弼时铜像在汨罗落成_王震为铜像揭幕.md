Title: Statue of Ren Bishi Unveiled in Miluo
Author: Wú Xīnghuá
Date: 1989-04-28
Edition: 2

	Ren Bishi's statue was unveiled in Miluo yesterday.

	Yesterday, the unveiling ceremony for the statue of Ren Bishi was held in front of his former residence in Tangjiaqiao Village, Bishi Town, Miluo City, Hunan Province. Vice Chairman Wang Zhen, Standing Committee Member of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) Yu Qiu-li, and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress (NPC) Liao Han-sheng attended the ceremony. Wang Zhen unveiled the statue and delivered a speech.

	The base of the statue is made of black granite and is 3 meters high. The statue itself is a full-body standing figure, 4.1 meters tall. Behind the statue, there is a wall with the words "Perpetual Spirit" inscribed on it. The statue stands on a flat area in front of the old residence.

	In his speech, Wang Zhen said, "Ren Bishi was a great Marxist and an outstanding revolutionary of the proletariat. He was an excellent leader of our party and the army, and an important leader of the Chinese youth movement." Wang Zhen recalled Ren Bishi's brilliant revolutionary life, pointing out that Ren Bishi had a deep understanding of Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought, a firm communist belief, and a good style of combining theory with practice and seeking truth from facts. He insisted on principles while always considering the overall situation; he was strict with himself but lenient with others, united with comrades, fair and upright, democratic in style, and rich in the spirit of criticism and self-criticism; he was always frugal and simple, closely connected with the masses; he did not calculate personal gains and losses, struggled unceasingly, and never stopped until his death. Ren Bishi's great achievements will forever be remembered, and his noble thoughts, high moral character, and excellent style will forever be worth learning from.

	About 300 cadres and masses attended the unveiling ceremony. Ren Bishi's wife, Chen Lingying, and his children and other relatives also attended the ceremony.

### 任弼时铜像在汨罗落成  王震为铜像揭幕
吴兴华
1989-04-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　任弼时铜像在汨罗落成    
    王震为铜像揭幕
    本报长沙４月２８日电  记者吴兴华报道：今年４月３０日，是任弼时同志诞辰８５周年。昨天，任弼时同志铜像揭幕仪式在任弼时的家乡——湖南省汨罗市弼时镇唐家桥村任弼时故居前举行。国家副主席王震、中顾委常委余秋里、全国人大常委会副委员长廖汉生出席了揭幕仪式，王震为铜像揭幕并讲了话。
    任弼时铜像底座用墨色的花岗石砌成，高３米；铜像是全身站立像，高４．１米；铜像后面的景墙上写着“浩气长存”４个大字。铜像耸立在旧居前的坪地上。
    王震在讲话中说：“任弼时同志是伟大的马克思主义者和杰出的无产阶级革命家，是我们党和军队的卓越领导人，中国青年运动的重要领导者”。王震同志追述了任弼时光辉的革命的一生，指出，任弼时同志具有深厚的马克思列宁主义、毛泽东思想的理论修养，坚定的共产主义信念，理论联系实际和实事求是的优良作风。他坚持原则，又处处顾全大局；他一向严以律己，宽以待人，团结同志，公道正派，作风民主，富于批评和自我批评精神；他一向艰苦朴素，密切联系群众；不计个人得失，奋斗不已，至死不息。任弼时同志的丰功伟绩永远值得我们铭记，他的崇高思想、高尚品德和优良作风，永远值得我们学习。
    约３００名干部和群众参加了揭幕仪式，任弼时的夫人陈琮英及子女等亲属也参加了仪式。
