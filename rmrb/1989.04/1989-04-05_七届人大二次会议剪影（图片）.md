Title: Snapshots from the Second Session of the Seventh National People's Congress
Author: Mèng Rénquán, Liú Jiànshēng, Liú Jiànguó
Date: 1989-04-05
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th.

On April 4th, during the Seventh National People's Congress Second Session, some representatives voted against the decision to authorize Shenzhen City to formulate regulations and rules for the Shenzhen Special Economic Zone as proposed by the State Council.

People's Daily, April 4th.

Wàn Lǐ, the Chairman, enthusiastically signed autographs for the representatives of the National People's Congress and the staff working at the meeting in the rest room.

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th.

On April 4th, the Seventh National People's Congress Second Session concluded. The representatives walked out of the Great Hall of the People with confidence.

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th.

During the intermission of the meeting, government ministers Qián Qíchéng, Cuī Nàifú, Luó Gàn, and Cài Chéng, who were attending the meeting, discussed the issue of the National People's Congress supervising the government.

### 七届人大二次会议剪影（图片）
孟仁泉  刘建生  刘建国
1989-04-05
第3版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：

    七届人大二次会议剪影
    4月4日，在七届人大二次会议表决关于国务院提请审议授权深圳市制定深圳经济特区法规和规章的议案的决定时，一部分代表投反对票。　
                                   新华社记者　刘建国摄
    万里委员长在会议休息室里兴致勃勃地为人大代表和会议工作人员签名。
                                　本报记者　孟仁泉摄
    四月四日，七届人大二次会议闭幕，代表们满怀信心地走出人民大会堂。
                                  新华社记者  刘建生摄
    列席会议的政府部长钱其琛、崔乃夫、罗干、蔡诚在大会间隙议 论人大对政府的监督问题。　
                                  本报记者　孟仁泉摄
