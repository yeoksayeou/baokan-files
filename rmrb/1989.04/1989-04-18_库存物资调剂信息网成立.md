Title: Establishment of the National Inventory Information Network
Author: Zhang Jinli
Date: 1989-04-18
Edition: 2

To adjust long-term accumulated inventory and improve enterprise capital turnover, the National Inventory Information Network was recently established in Beijing. This network is jointly organized by six units: _Market News_, _Economic Reference News_, _Economic News_, the Information Retrieval and Consultation Department of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the Huilun International Economic Cooperation Co., Ltd., and the Hainan Branch of the China Packaging Corporation.

Currently, this information network has established connections with the world's renowned large-scale online information retrieval systems.

### 库存物资调剂信息网成立
张锦力
1989-04-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　库存物资调剂信息网成立
    本报北京４月１８日讯　记者张锦力报道：为调剂长期积压的库存物资、提高企业资金周转率，全国库存物资调剂信息网日前在北京成立。这个信息网由《市场报》、《经济参考》、《经济消息》报、中国科技情报所检索咨询部、汇联国际经济合作有限公司、中国包装总公司海南公司六家单位共同主办。
    目前，该信息网已与世界著名的大型联机情报检索系统建立了联系。
