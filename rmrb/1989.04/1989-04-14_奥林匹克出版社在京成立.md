Title: Olympic Publishing House Established in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-04-14
Edition: 4

The Olympic Publishing House was established in Beijing on April 13. The Olympic Publishing House will primarily be responsible for publishing various types of publications needed for the Asian Games. In addition to completing this heavy publishing task, the Olympic Publishing House will also arrange the publication of sports books and other books. It will proactively contact international sports organizations and publishing houses to actively carry out various forms of cooperative publishing operations.

### 奥林匹克出版社在京成立

1989-04-14
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　奥林匹克出版社在京成立
    本报讯　奥林匹克出版社四月十三日在北京成立。奥林匹克出版社主要承担亚运会所需各类出版物的出版任务，奥林匹克出版社在完成这项繁重的出版任务的同时，还要安排出版有关体育图书和其他的图书，并将主动与国际体育组织、出版机构进行联系，积极开展各种形式的合作出版业务。
