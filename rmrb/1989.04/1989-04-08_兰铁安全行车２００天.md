Title: Lan Steel Safe Driving for 200 Days
Author:
Date: 1989-04-08
Edition: 2

Lan Steel has achieved 200 consecutive days of safe driving without major or large accidents. The total number of accidents has decreased by 46% compared to the same period last year, and cargo-related accidents have decreased by 88.5%. Personal safety accidents have been eliminated, making Lan Steel's safety record the second best in the national railway system. Recently, the Ministry of Railways commended and rewarded the Lan Steel Railway Bureau, presenting them with a banner that reads "Ensure Safety and Punctuality, Lead the Way."

### 兰铁安全行车２００天

1989-04-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　兰铁安全行车２００天
    本报兰州４月７日电  地跨甘、宁、青三省区的兰州铁路局连续２００天无行车重大、大事故，事故总件数比去年同期下降４６％，货运事故下降８８．５％，消灭了人身安全事故，安全天数居全国铁路系统第二位。日前，铁道部对兰州铁路局给予通报表彰和奖励，并颁发了“安全正点，当好先行”的锦旗。
