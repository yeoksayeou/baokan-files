The Recruit Scandal Weighs Heavily on Prime Minister Takeshita

On March 31, Japanese Prime Minister Takeshita Noboru faced questions in the Diet regarding whether the Recruit Company had contributed 20 million yen in 1987 to fund a banquet ticket he had arranged. The left image shows Takeshita listening to the questioning; the middle image shows Takeshita responding to questions from the legislators; the right image shows Takeshita resting with his eyes closed after answering the questions.

### “利案”使竹下首相疲惫不堪（图片）

1989-04-03
第4版(国际新闻)

    “利案”使竹下首相疲惫不堪
    3月31日，日本首相竹下登在国会就利库路特公司是否在1987年出资2000万日元购买他筹措资金的宴会票一事接受议员的质询。左图，竹下在听取质询；中图，竹下在回答议员提出的问题；右图，竹下回答问题后闭目养神。                      新华社发
