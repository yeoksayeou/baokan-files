Title: How Much Longer Will It Sleep?
Author: Wang Da Bin
Date: 1989-04-14
Edition: 1

Due to insufficient railway transport capacity, 170,000 cubic meters of timber from the hometown of red pine—Heilongjiang Province's Dongfanghong Forestry Bureau—cannot be transported out. It is understood that many of these logs have been left by the side of the road for 8 years, resulting in enormous economic losses. Photograph by Wang Da Bin.
