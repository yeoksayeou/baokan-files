Title: Gao Fenglian Wins Gold in Czech Judo Tournament
Author: Yang Yongzhong, Jiao Jian
Date: 1989-04-11
Edition: 4

The Czech International Judo Invitational concluded today in Bratislava. Chinese female athlete Gao Fenglian won the gold medal in the over-72kg category.

The Chinese women's judo team was the only Asian team participating in this competition and competed in six weight classes. Apart from Gao Fenglian, Jiang Fengmei secured the silver medal in the 66kg category, and Yang Xiaoyun took the bronze medal in the 52kg category.

The competition began yesterday and featured female teams from six countries and male teams from three countries.

### 高凤莲获捷柔道赛一项冠军
杨永忠　焦健
1989-04-11
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　高凤莲获捷柔道赛一项冠军
    新华社布拉格四月九日电　（记者杨永忠、焦健）捷克斯洛伐克国际柔道邀请赛今天在布拉迪斯拉发结束。中国女将高凤莲获得七十二公斤以上级第一名。
    中国女子柔道队是参加这次比赛的唯一亚洲队，共参加了六个级别的比赛。除高凤莲外，姜凤梅获得六十六公斤级第二名，杨小云获得五十二公斤级第三名。
    这次比赛昨天开始，有六个国家的女队和三个国家的男队参加。
