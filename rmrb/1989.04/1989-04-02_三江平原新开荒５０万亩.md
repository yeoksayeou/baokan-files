Title: 500,000 Mu of New Reclamation in the Sanjiang Plain
Author: Wǔ Péi Zhēn
Date: 1989-04-02
Edition: 2

With Jiamusi City as the center, the Sanjiang Plain is currently focusing on land reclamation and the improvement of low-yield fields. Since last April, 29 counties and cities, along with 53 state-owned farms, have reclaimed 500,000 mu of land, improved 1,200,000 mu of low-yield fields, planted 600,000 mu of forests, and improved 200,000 mu of grasslands.

### 三江平原新开荒５０万亩
武培真
1989-04-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　三江平原新开荒５０万亩
    本报哈尔滨４月１日电  记者武培真报道：以佳木斯市为中心的三江平原，正抓紧开荒造田和改造低产田。自去年４月以来，２９个县市和５３个国营农场共开荒５０万亩，改造低产田１２０万亩，造林６０万亩，改良草场２０万亩。
