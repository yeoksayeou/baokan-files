Title: Iranian President Khamenei to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1989-04-27
Edition: 1

Iranian President Khamenei to Visit China

BEIJING, April 27 (Xinhua) -- At the invitation of Chairman Yang Shangkun, President of the Islamic Republic of Iran, Sayyid Ali Khamenei, will pay an official visit to the People's Republic of China from May 9 to 14, 1989.

### 伊朗总统哈梅内伊将来访

1989-04-27
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　伊朗总统哈梅内伊将来访
    本报北京４月２７日讯  应杨尚昆主席的邀请，伊朗伊斯兰共和国总统赛义德·阿里·哈梅内伊将于１９８９年５月９日至１４日对中华人民共和国进行正式访问。
