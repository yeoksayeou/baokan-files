Title: The CPC Central Committee and the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Announce with Deep Grief the Passing of Comrade Li Jianquan in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-04-24
Edition: 1

The Chinese Communist Party Central Committee and the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress announce with deep grief:

Comrade Li Jianquan, an outstanding Party member, a loyal and tested communist warrior, a revolutionary of the proletariat, and the Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Fifth National People's Congress, passed away in Beijing at 5:25 AM on April 24, 1989, due to illness. He was 79 years old.

Li Jianquan was born in Cangxia Village, Linchuan County, Jiangxi Province. In 1926, he participated in the patriotic student movement. The following year, he joined the Communist Youth League. In 1930, he became a member of the Communist Party of China and participated in the famous Long March. He held various positions, including Political Commissar of the 35th Division of the Red Fourth Army, Political Commissar of the 21st Army, Political Commissar of the 4th Division of the Red Second Front Army, Political Commissar of the 358th Brigade of the 120th Division, Commander and Political Commissar of the Daqingshan Guerrilla Unit, Political Commissar of the General School of the Anti-Japanese Military and Political University, Secretary of the CPC Jin-Sui Branch and Political Commissar of the Jin-Sui Military Region, and others. After the founding of the People's Republic, he served as the First Secretary of the CPC Sichuan Provincial Committee and Political Commissar of the Sichuan Military Region, First Secretary of the CPC Southwest Bureau and Political Commissar of the Chengdu Military Region, and Chairman of the Southwest Third Front Construction Commission, among other positions.

He was a member of the 8th, 10th, and 11th Central Committees of the Communist Party of China; a member of the Political Bureau of the 8th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; a Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the 3rd, 4th, and 5th National People's Congresses; at the 12th Party Congress, he was elected as a member of the Central Advisory Commission, and at the first plenary session of the Central Advisory Commission elected at the 12th Party Congress, he was elected as a standing member.

Comrade Li Jianquan dedicated his entire life to the revolution and construction of China. His passing is a great loss to our Party and country. Comrade Li Jianquan will live forever in our hearts!

### 中共中央、人大常委会沉痛宣告  李井泉同志在京逝世

1989-04-24
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　中共中央、人大常委会沉痛宣告    
    李井泉同志在京逝世
    新华社北京４月２４日电  中国共产党中央委员会、全国人民代表大会常务委员会讣告
    中国共产党中央委员会、全国人民代表大会常务委员会沉痛宣告：中国共产党的优秀党员，久经考验的忠诚的共产主义战士，无产阶级革命家，第五届全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李井泉同志，因病于１９８９年４月２４日５时２５分在北京逝世，终年７９岁。
    李井泉同志是江西省临川县仓下村人，１９２６年就参加了爱国学生运动，翌年加入了共产主义青年团，１９３０年转为中国共产党党员，参加了著名的二万五千里长征。他历任红四军三十五军政委，二十一军政委，红二方面军第四师政委，一二零师三五八旅政委，大青山游击支队支队长兼政委，抗日军政大学总校政委，中共晋绥分局书记兼晋绥军区政委等职。建国后，他曾任中共川西区委第一书记兼川西军区政委，中共四川省委第一书记兼省军区第一政委，中共西南局第一书记兼成都军区第一政委和西南三线建设委员会主任等职。
    他是中国共产党的第八、十、十一届中央委员，中国共产党第八届中央政治局委员；第三、四、五届全国人大常委会副委员长；在党的十二大上他被选为中央顾问委员会委员，在党的十二大选出的中央顾问委员会第一次全体会议上，他被选为常委。
    李井泉同志为中国的革命和建设事业贡献了毕生精力。他的逝世，是我们党和国家的重大损失。李井泉同志永垂不朽！（附图片）
