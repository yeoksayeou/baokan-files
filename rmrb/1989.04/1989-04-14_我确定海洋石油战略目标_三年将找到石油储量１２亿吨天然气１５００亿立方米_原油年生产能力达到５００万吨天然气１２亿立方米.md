Title: China Sets Strategic Goals for Marine Oil Industry
Author: Zhang Heping
Date: 1989-04-14
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 14th. Reporter Zhang Heping learned from the China National Offshore Oil Corporation that China's marine oil industry has recently set its short-term goals and development strategies. According to these goals, by 1992, China will have discovered geological reserves of 1.2 billion tons of oil and 150 billion cubic meters of natural gas; the annual production capacity will reach 5 million tons of crude oil and 12 billion cubic meters of natural gas.

Currently, China's marine oil industry is in a crucial period of increasing reserves and production. In the upcoming development strategy, the industry will continue to adhere to and develop the policies of foreign cooperation and self-operation. Given the urgent need for energy in Guangdong, Hainan, and the coastal regions of East China, the industry will continue to attract foreign investment, strengthen exploration, and build a large gas area in the western South China Sea, including the Qiongdongnan-Yinggehai Basin and the Zhujiangkou Basin. Efforts will be made to actively develop the oil and gas resources in the East China Sea Basin, the largest sedimentary basin on China's continental shelf, and strive to achieve foreign cooperation as soon as possible. Additionally, suitable foreign partners will be selected to explore new areas in the refining and chemical industries.

### 我确定海洋石油战略目标　三年将找到石油储量１２亿吨天然气１５００亿立方米　原油年生产能力达到５００万吨天然气１２亿立方米
张何平
1989-04-14
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　我确定海洋石油战略目标
　　三年将找到石油储量１２亿吨天然气１５００亿立方米
　　原油年生产能力达到５００万吨天然气１２亿立方米
    本报北京４月１４日讯　记者张何平从中国海洋石油总公司获悉：我国海洋石油工业近期奋斗目标和发展战略于近日确定。按照这个目标，到１９９２年，我国将应找到石油地质储量１２亿吨，天然气１５００亿立方米；原油年生产能力达到５００万吨，天然气达１２亿立方米。
    当前，我国海洋石油工业正处于多拿储量、增加产量的关键时期。在今后实施的发展战略中，将继续坚持和发展对外合作，坚持和发展自营的方针。针对广东、海南和华东沿海地区急需能源的状况，将继续引进外资，加强勘探，在南海西部，包括琼东南——莺歌海盆地和珠江口盆地珠三坳陷海域，有计划有步骤地建设一个海上大气区。积极开发我国沿海大陆架最大的沉积盆地——东海盆地的油气资源，争取早日实现对外合作。此外，还将选择对外合作的合适伙伴，开拓炼油化工新领域。
