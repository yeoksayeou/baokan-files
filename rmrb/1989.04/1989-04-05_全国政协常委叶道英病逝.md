Title: National Committee Member of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Ye Daoying, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-04-05
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 4th.

The Standing Committee Member of the Seventh National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Comrade Ye Daoying, passed away in Beijing on March 28th due to illness, at the age of 83.

Ye Daoying was a native of Meixian, Guangdong. Born in 1906, he studied at Sun Yat-sen University in Guangzhou and the Shanghai Institute of Political Science and Law. Before liberation, Ye Daoying sympathized with and supported the people's revolution under the influence of his elder brother, Ye Jianying. After liberation, he served in various positions, including Advisor to the Guangzhou Municipal Government, Advisor to the Central Committee on Overseas Chinese Affairs, and Advisor to the State Council. He was also a Standing Committee Member of the Fifth and Sixth National Committees of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

### 全国政协常委叶道英病逝

1989-04-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　全国政协常委叶道英病逝
    新华社北京４月４日电  中国人民政治协商会议第七届全国委员会常务委员叶道英同志因病医治无效，于３月２８日在北京逝世，终年８３岁。
    叶道英同志是广东梅县人，１９０６年出生。曾就读于广州中山大学、上海政法大学。解放前，叶道英同志在长兄叶剑英同志的影响下，同情和支持人民革命。解放后，历任广州市人民政府参事，中央侨务委员会参事，国务院参事。他还是第五、六届全国政协常务委员。
