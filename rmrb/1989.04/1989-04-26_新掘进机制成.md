Title: New Excavation Mechanism Achieved
Author: Wáng Déshèng
Date: 1989-04-26
Edition: 2

The first domestically produced sample machine, developed by the Jiamusi Coal Mining Machinery Factory through the digestion and absorption of foreign S100-type excavation technology, recently passed a national-level appraisal at the Yongdingzhuang Mine of the Datong Mining Bureau. This machine is a large-scale equipment used for excavating coal mine tunnels. It resembles a tracked tank in appearance, has high efficiency, and excellent performance. It has now begun mass production.

### 新掘进机制成
王德胜
1989-04-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　新掘进机制成
    本报讯  由佳木斯煤矿机械厂通过消化、吸收国外Ｓ１００型掘进机技术，研制出的首台国产化样机，日前在大同矿务局永定庄矿通过了国家级鉴定。该机是开拓煤矿巷道的大型设备，它貌似履带坦克，功效高，性能好。目前已开始投入批量生产。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
              （王德胜）
