Title: The First Intelligent Computer Emerges at Xi'an Jiaotong University
Author: Zhang Guangqiang
Date: 1989-04-26
Edition: 2

An intelligent computer designed for artificial intelligence applications has recently been developed at Xi'an Jiaotong University.

Artificial intelligence is a key technology being intensively researched by developed countries around the world today. Its goal is to enable computers to mimic human thinking, possessing capabilities for knowledge expression, reasoning, and learning. Professor Zheng Shouqi and his team at Xi'an Jiaotong University have utilized the Lisp artificial intelligence language to develop a high-performance computer. This is a domestic first, providing a powerful tool for the development of artificial intelligence and opening up broad application prospects for high-tech research in areas such as robotics, computer-integrated manufacturing, machine translation, decision-making, and expert systems.

　　首台智能计算机在西安交大问世
    本报讯  一种面向人工智能应用的智能计算机日前在西安交通大学问世。
