Title: Improving the Quality of Managers and Workers: Zhu Houze Calls for Building Enterprise Culture
Author: Lin Gang, Lü Jianzhong
Date: 1989-04-28
Edition: 4

	Improving the Quality of Managers and Workers
	Zhu Houze Calls for Building Enterprise Culture

	XIAOSHING, April 28 (Xinhua) -- Zhu Houze, vice chairman and first secretary of the National Federation of Trade Unions, said at the "1989 Enterprise Culture Seminar" that we must improve the cultural quality of managers and workers through the construction of enterprise culture. On the basis of establishing common goals, common ideals, and shared values, we should form a democratic decision-making mechanism, appropriately adjust the material interests of all parties, including investors, and truly build the enterprise into a community of interests for investors, managers, and workers.

	In the transition period between old and new systems, China is experiencing a boom in the study of enterprise culture. On April 24, more than 100 people from the business, economic, theoretical, news, and cultural circles across the country gathered in the ancient city of Xiaoshing to participate in the "1989 Enterprise Culture Seminar."

	Zhu Houze also said that the study of enterprise culture should be placed in the context of reality and history, and a deep reflection on reality and history should be conducted. Looking back over the past 10 years, China has achieved brilliant achievements in construction and reform, but there have also been some shocks in economic and social political life. Now it is completely necessary to make adjustments to gradually establish a new order for socialist commodity economy, a new order for socialist democratic politics, and a corresponding atmosphere of thought and culture.

	Experts, scholars, and entrepreneurs of all ages who participated in the seminar conducted extensive discussions on the practice of domestic and foreign enterprise culture and the direction and path for the further development of China's enterprise culture.

	This seminar was jointly organized by the People's Daily Overseas Edition, the Institute of Sociology of the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences, the Communist Youth League of Zhejiang Province, the Zhejiang Provincial Youth Federation, and the Xiaoshing Municipal People's Government.

### 提高经营者和劳动者素质  朱厚泽呼吁建设企业文化
林钢　吕建中
1989-04-28
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　提高经营者和劳动者素质    
    朱厚泽呼吁建设企业文化
    本报绍兴电  记者林钢、吕建中报道：全国总工会副主席、书记处第一书记朱厚泽，在“八九企业文化研讨会”上说：我们要通过企业文化的建设，全面提高经营者和劳动者的文化素质。在确立共同目标、共同理想追求共同价值观念的基础上，形成民主参与的决策机制，恰当调节包括投资者在内的各方面的物质利益关系，把企业真正建设成为投资者、经营者和劳动者的利益共同体。
    在新旧体制交换时期，我国正在兴起研究企业文化的热潮。４月２４日，来自全国各地的企业界、经济界及理论界、新闻界和文化界的人士百余人，聚会在古城绍兴市，参加了“八九企业文化研讨会”。
    朱厚泽还说，研究企业文化，要把它放在现实和历史的大背景下来进行，要对现实和历史进行深刻的反思。回顾１０年，我国建设和改革取得了辉煌的成就，同时在经济生活和社会政治生活中也出现一些震荡。现在完全有必要进行调整，以便逐步建立社会主义商品经济的新秩序，建立社会主义民主政治的新秩序，营造与此相适应的思想观念和文化气氛。
    参加研讨会的老、中、青专家，学者和企业家们，就国内外企业文化的实践及我国企业文化进一步发展的方向和道路，进行了广泛的探讨。
    这次研讨会，是由人民日报海外版、中国社会科学院社会学研究所、共青团浙江省委、浙江省青年联合会和绍兴市人民政府共同举办的。
