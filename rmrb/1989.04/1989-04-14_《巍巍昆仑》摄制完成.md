Title: The Filming of "Majestic Kunlun" is Completed
Author: Yì Jiǎnrú
Date: 1989-04-14
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing

The color wide-screen feature film "Majestic Kunlun" has been completed and will soon be released to audiences. Produced by the August First Film Studio, this film depicts the scenes of Chairman Mao leading the Kunlun Column to fight in Shaanxi during the War of Liberation, and his strategic command of various major battlefields across the country to achieve unexpected victories. The film focuses on the scenes of the highest command levels of both the Nationalist and Communist parties outsmarting each other, and vividly portrays the charisma of our party's leaders.

The filmmakers aimed to make this film an epic masterpiece, taking over two years to complete.

### 《巍巍昆仑》摄制完成
易俭如
1989-04-14
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　《巍巍昆仑》摄制完成
    新华社北京电　（记者易俭如）彩色宽银幕故事片《巍巍昆仑》摄制完成，不久可望同观众见面。由八一电影制片厂拍摄的这部影片，反映解放战争时期毛泽东同志率领昆仑纵队转战陕北，并指挥全国各大战场出奇制胜的情景，着力刻画了国共双方最高统帅部斗智斗勇的场面，浓墨重彩地表现我党领导人的神采。
    编导者力图使这部影片成为史诗性巨片，耗时两年多。
