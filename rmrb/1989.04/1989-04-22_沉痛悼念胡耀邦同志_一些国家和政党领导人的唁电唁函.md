Title: Mourn the Passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang
Author:
Date: 1989-04-22
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 22nd.

	Mourn the Passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang
	Condolence Messages from Leaders of Some Countries and Political Parties

	Party and state leaders have received more condolence messages from leaders of some countries and political parties, mourning the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang.

	German Federal Chancellor Helmut Kohl sent a telegram to Comrade Zhao Ziyang on April 21, saying, "His passing has caused your country to lose an active advocate and promoter of modernization and the policy of reform and opening up."

	Togolese President Gnassingbé Eyadéma sent a telegram to Chairman Yang Shangkun on April 20, saying, "The shocking news of Comrade Hu Yaobang's passing has caused us deep sorrow."

	Sri Lankan Prime Minister Ranasinghe Premadasa sent a telegram to Premier Li Peng on April 20, and Democratic Kampuchean Prime Minister Son Sann sent a telegram to Foreign Minister Qian Qichen on April 17, expressing sincere condolences over the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang.

	Guyanese President Desmond Hoyte wrote a letter to Chairman Yang Shangkun on April 18, expressing his deepest sympathies to the Chinese government and people over the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang.

	Chairman of the Socialist International and honorary chairman of the German Social Democratic Party Willy Brandt sent a letter of condolence to Comrade Zhao Ziyang on April 19, saying, "My remembrance of Comrade Hu Yaobang, the former general secretary, will forever be accompanied by those beautiful impressions."

	First Secretary of the French Socialist Party Pierre Mauroy sent a telegram of condolence to Comrade Zhao Ziyang on April 19, expressing deep sorrow over the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang. The telegram said, "We, the Socialist Party, wish to join the world in mourning this courageous activist who promoted China's modernization and reform."

	The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Communist Party of Australia (Marxist-Leninist) Conor O'Carroll also sent telegrams of condolence, expressing their sorrow over the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang.

### 沉痛悼念胡耀邦同志  一些国家和政党领导人的唁电唁函

1989-04-22
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　沉痛悼念胡耀邦同志    
    一些国家和政党领导人的唁电唁函
    新华社北京４月２２日电  党和国家领导人日内又收到一些国家领导人和政党领导人发来的唁电唁函，悼念胡耀邦同志逝世。
    联邦德国总理科尔４月２１日在致赵紫阳总书记的电报中说，“他的逝世使贵国失去了一位现代化和改革开放政策的积极倡导者和推动者。”
    多哥总统埃亚德马４月２０日在致杨尚昆主席的电报中说，胡耀邦逝世“这一使整个中国及其政界感到震惊的噩耗使我们深感悲痛”。
    斯里兰卡总理维杰通加４月２０日致电李鹏总理，民主柬埔寨总理宋双４月１７日致电钱其琛外长，对胡耀邦逝世表示诚挚哀悼。
    圭亚那总统霍伊特４月１８日写信给杨尚昆主席，就胡耀邦逝世向中国政府和人民致以最深切的慰问。
    社会党国际主席、德国社会民主党名誉主席维利·勃兰特４月１９日在致赵紫阳总书记的唁函中说，他对胡耀邦前总书记的怀念，将同他“所获得的那些美好的印象一道永世长存”。
    法国社会党第一书记莫鲁瓦４月１９日在致赵紫阳同志的唁电中，对胡耀邦的逝世表示深切哀悼。电报说：“我们社会党愿与全世界一道，悼念这位推动中国现代化和改革的有魄力的活动家。”
    苏共中央和澳共（马列）主席康韦尔也分别发来唁电唁函，对胡耀邦的逝世表示哀悼。
