Title: National People's Congress Handbook and Administrative Litigation Law and Speeches to Be Published
Author:
Date: 1989-04-05
Edition: 2

The "National People's Congress Handbook" and "The People's Republic of China Administrative Litigation Law and Speeches" will be published and available to readers after the closing of the Second Session of the Seventh National People's Congress.

These two books are edited and published by the China Democratic and Legal System Press, which was recently established by the Office of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

"The National People's Congress Handbook" introduces the history and current working procedures of the National People's Congress, as well as how people's representatives exercise their legal rights. It also collects basic laws, documents, and rich historical materials related to the work of the NPC and its Standing Committee.

"The People's Republic of China Administrative Litigation Law and Speeches" provides an accurate and easy-to-understand explanation of this law by its main drafters, including the procedures for "citizens suing the government."

### 全国人大工作手册　行政诉讼法及讲话将出版

1989-04-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　全国人大工作手册　行政诉讼法及讲话将出版
    新华社北京４月４日电  《全国人民代表大会工作手册》、《中华人民共和国行政诉讼法及讲话》两本书，将于七届全国人大二次会议闭幕后同广大读者见面。
    这两本书是由全国人大常委会办公厅新近成立的中国民主法制出版社编辑出版的。
    《全国人民代表大会工作手册》，介绍了全国人民代表大会的历史与现行工作制度，以及人民代表如何依法行使自己的职权，并收集了有关人大及其常委会工作的基本法律、文件和丰富的历史资料。
    《中华人民共和国行政诉讼法及讲话》，由这部法律的主要起草者准确、通俗地讲解了这部法律，包括如何进行“民告官”的程序等内容。
