Title: Afghan Interim Government to Operate Domestically; Malaysia Recognizes Afghan Interim Government
Author:
Date: 1989-04-14
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Singapore, April 12th.

The foreign minister of the Afghan interim government, Gulbuddin Hekmatyar, who is visiting Singapore, stated that the interim government will begin operating within Afghanistan in the next two to three days.

Hekmatyar made these remarks at a press conference held this afternoon. Prior to this, he held talks with Singapore's foreign minister, S. Rajaratnam.

He also mentioned that some of the interim government's ministers will move to Kunar and Paktiya provinces to establish offices.

He noted that the interim government plans to hold elections within Afghanistan within four months.

Hekmatyar arrived in Singapore yesterday for a two-day visit. The purpose of his visit is to seek international recognition for the Afghan interim government.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 12th.

According to a report by Bernama, Malaysia's foreign minister, Abu Hassan Omar, announced in Kuala Lumpur today that the Malaysian government has recognized the Afghan interim government.

Omar made this announcement after Malaysian Prime Minister Mahathir Mohamad held talks with the visiting foreign minister of the Afghan interim government, Gulbuddin Hekmatyar, in Kuala Lumpur today.

Omar said, "With this recognition, the Malaysian government hopes that the Afghan interim government will be able to unite all the people of Afghanistan and work towards resolving the problems they face."

The Afghan interim government was established on February 23rd of this year. Since then, Bahrain, Saudi Arabia, and Sudan have recognized this government.

### 阿临时政府将在国内办公　马来西亚承认阿临时政府

1989-04-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　阿临时政府将在国内办公
　　马来西亚承认阿临时政府
    新华社新加坡４月１２日电　前来新加坡访问的阿富汗临时政府外长古勒卜丁·希克马蒂亚尔说，两三天内，临时政府将在阿富汗境内办公。
    希克马蒂亚尔是今天下午在这里举行的一次记者招待会上说这番话的。此前，他同新加坡外长黄根成进行了会谈。
    他还说，临时政府的部分部长将转至库纳尔省和帕克蒂亚省办公。
    他提到，临时政府４个月内将在阿富汗境内举行选举。
    希克马蒂亚尔是昨天抵达新加坡进行为期两天的访问的。他此行的目的是寻求国际社会对阿临时政府的承认。
    新华社北京４月１２日电　据马新社报道，马来西亚外长阿布·哈桑·奥马尔今天在吉隆坡宣布，马政府已经承认阿富汗临时政府。
    奥马尔是在马来西亚总理马哈蒂尔·穆罕默德同来访的阿富汗临时政府外长古勒卜丁·希克马蒂亚尔今天在吉隆坡举行会谈后，宣布上述承认的。
    奥马尔说：“随着这一承认，马来西亚政府希望阿富汗临时政府能够团结阿富汗全体人民，以努力寻求解决他们面临的问题。”
    阿富汗临时政府是今年２月２３日成立的。此后，已有巴林、沙特阿拉伯和苏丹承认了这个政府。
