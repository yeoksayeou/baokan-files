Title: Haitian Coup: President Arrested
Author:
Date: 1989-04-03
Edition: 4

    A coup took place in Haiti, a Latin American and Caribbean island nation, on April 2.

    According to local free radio station reports, Haitian President General Avril and the Minister of the Interior have been arrested by soldiers who launched the coup and are currently being held in a military camp.

    The radio station also said that due to the fierce gun battle between the coup forces and the troops loyal to the president throughout the night, the streets of the capital Port-au-Prince were deserted in the early morning of April 2. The radio station did not report the casualties and detailed situation of the coup.

### 海地政变  总统被捕

1989-04-03
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　海地政变  总统被捕
    新华社北京４月２日电  太子港消息：拉美加勒比岛国海地４月２日发生政变。
    据当地自由电台报道，海地总统阿夫里尔将军和内政部长已被发动政变的武装部队士兵逮捕，目前被关在军营内。
    电台还说，由于政变部队与忠于总统的部队进行了一夜激烈枪战，２日清晨，首都太子港大街上空无一人。电台未报道政变的伤亡和详细情况。
