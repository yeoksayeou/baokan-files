Title: Gu Muqi Meets with Taiwan Businesspeople Delegation
Author: Jiang Shaogao, Zheng Guogu
Date: 1989-04-21
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 21st.

Gu Muqi, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, warmly received a delegation of Taiwanese businesspeople in the Great Hall of the People today. Gu Muqi said, "Taiwan's economy has developed rapidly over the past few years, which is a source of pride for Taiwanese compatriots. We welcome Taiwanese businesspeople to visit the mainland, observe the economic development, and invest in business."

The delegation, led by Chen Yuanfa, Chairman of the Board of Chen Specialty Enterprise Company, arrived on the mainland on April 13th. They visited Shenzhen, Guangzhou, Xiamen, and Shanghai before arriving in Beijing today. Their primary purpose is to investigate the economic development situation, investment environment, investment directions, and relevant preferential policies for Taiwanese businesspeople investing and trading on the mainland. The delegation consists of over 50 chairpersons and general managers of Taiwanese medium and small enterprises.

Attending the meeting were Qin Chuan, a member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, and Bao Yujin, President of the China Newspaper Association and Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily.

### 谷牧会见台湾企业家考察团
江绍高　郑固固
1989-04-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　谷牧会见台湾企业家考察团
    本报北京４月２１日讯  记者江绍高、郑固固报道：全国政协副主席谷牧今天在人民大会堂亲切会见台湾企业家考察团。谷牧说，台湾多年来经济发展较快，这是值得台湾同胞骄傲的地方。欢迎台湾企业家回大陆参观考察、投资经商。
    以台湾陈特企业公司董事长陈源发为团长的台湾企业家考察团是４月１３日抵达大陆的，途径深圳、广州、厦门、上海，于今天到达北京。他们来大陆主要是考察大陆经济发展状况、投资环境、投资方向及有关台商投资贸易的优惠政策。考察团由５０余位台湾中小企业董事长、总经理组成。
    参加会见的有全国人大常委会委员、全国记协常务副主席秦川，中国报业协会会长、人民日报副总编辑保育钧。
