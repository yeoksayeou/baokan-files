Title: Commemorating the 30th Anniversary of Democratic Reform
Author:
Date: 1989-04-23
Edition: 4

To commemorate the 30th anniversary of the implementation of democratic reform, the Tibet Autonomous Region organized various cultural and artistic activities in April.

	—The "Exhibition of Photographs Commemorating the Democratic Reform in Tibet" and the "Tibet Today" Art Exhibition showcased precious historical photographs and images of modern Tibet, authentically displaying the region's democratic reform and socialist construction. The exhibits highlighted the significant achievements made by the Tibetan people under the leadership of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the support of all ethnic groups across the country. They also showcased the spirit of the Tibetan people in maintaining national unity and ethnic solidarity, and building a new Tibet that is united, prosperous, and civilized.

	—Performances included the adapted Tibetan opera "White Ma Wenba" and a themed evening of arts with distinctive ethnic characteristics.

	—The Tibet People's Publishing House edited and published the fourth volume of "Revolutionary Memoirs" in both Tibetan and Chinese, and special issues of "Tibet Literature" and "Tibet Arts." The "Selected Contemporary Reportage of Tibet" was also published. Various bookstores held book exhibitions and sales, featuring approximately 1,000 Tibetan and Chinese books, with over 50% published by the Tibet People's Publishing House.

	—From April 1 to April 7, film weeks were held in Lhasa, Shigatse, Zêkang, Chamdo, Nagchu, Military Camp No. 81, and Lion Spring River, screening six feature films including "Songtsen Gampo" and more than ten documentaries and newsreels on Tibetan themes.

### 纪念民主改革三十周年  西藏举办多种文艺活动

1989-04-23
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　纪念民主改革三十周年    
    西藏举办多种文艺活动
    本报讯  为了纪念实行民主改革３０周年，西藏自治区在４月份组织了各种文化艺术活动。
    ——“纪念西藏实行民主改革图片展览”和“西藏今日”美术展览，以珍贵的历史资料照片和今日西藏新貌图片真实地展示了自治区民主改革和社会主义建设的历程，再现了西藏各族人民在党中央的领导和全国各兄弟民族的支援下所取得的巨大成就。表现了西藏各族人民维护祖国统一和民族团结，建设团结、富裕、文明的新西藏的精神面貌。
    ——上演的剧（节）目有改编藏戏《白玛文巴》和具有鲜明民族特色的专题文艺晚会。
    ——西藏人民出版社编辑出版了《革命回忆录》（四）藏、汉文两种版本专辑，《西藏文学》、《西藏文艺》推出专号，《西藏当代报告文学选》也已问世。各新华书店举办的图书展销，约有１０００余种藏、汉文版图书，其中５０％以上是西藏人民出版社出版的。
    ——４月１日至４月７日，分别在拉萨、日喀则、泽当、昌都、那曲、八一镇、狮泉河举办了电影周，上映《松赞干布》等６部故事片和１０多部西藏题材资料片及纪录片。
