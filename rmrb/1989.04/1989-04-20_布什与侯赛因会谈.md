Title: Bush and Hussein Hold Talks
Author: Zhang Qixun
Date: 1989-04-20
Edition: 3

U.S. President Bush and Jordanian King Hussein held talks today in the White House on Middle East issues and bilateral relations. Both sides pledged to maintain close cooperation and coordination. Bush praised Hussein for his contributions to Middle East peace and hoped the king would continue to play his role. Hussein expressed support for Bush's efforts to achieve a just and lasting end to the Arab-Israeli conflict.

After the talks, Bush and Hussein spoke in the White House Rose Garden, outlining their countries' positions on the Middle East issue. Bush reiterated that the U.S. responsibility is to achieve a comprehensive solution through negotiations, ensuring peace, security for Israel and all countries, and the legitimate political rights of the Palestinians.

He believed that arranging appropriate and mutually acceptable elections as a first step could help the negotiation process. He also thought that a properly structured international conference, at the right time, could become a means to facilitate direct talks among all parties.

Hussein said in his speech that their goal is to achieve a comprehensive solution to the Arab-Israeli conflict, based on the principles of implementing the provisions of two UN Security Council resolutions, Israel's withdrawal from the occupied territories in exchange for the establishment of peace. He pointed out that the recent decision by the PLO has contributed to peace, and the U.S. has had substantive dialogue with the PLO. He hoped this would prompt Israel to make a corresponding response, recognizing the legitimate representative of the Palestinian people.

### 布什与侯赛因会谈
张启昕
1989-04-20
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　布什与侯赛因会谈
    本报华盛顿４月１９日电  记者张启昕报道：美国总统布什和约旦国王侯赛因今天上午在白宫就中东问题和两国关系举行会谈，双方保证两国继续保持紧密的合作和协调。布什赞扬侯赛因为中东和平作出的贡献，希望国王继续发挥他的作用。侯赛因表示支持布什为公正持久地结束阿以冲突所作的努力。
    会谈后，布什和侯赛因在白宫玫瑰园发表了讲话，就中东问题阐述两国的主张。布什重申美国的责任是通过谈判实现全面解决，使和平、以色列和所有国家的安全以及巴勒斯坦合法的政治权利能够得以实现。
    他相信，安排适当、互能接受的选举，作为开始的一步能够有助于谈判的进程，同时他认为，结构适当的国际会议，在适当的时候也能成为有利于各方直接谈判的手段。
    侯赛因在讲话中说，我们的目标是争取阿以冲突的全面解决，其原则是执行联合国安理会两决议的规定，以色列撤出被占领土地，换取和平的确立。他指出，巴解最近的决定对和平作出了贡献，美国同巴解进行了实质性的对话。他希望这将促使以色列作出相应的响应，承认巴勒斯坦人民的合法代表。（附图片）
