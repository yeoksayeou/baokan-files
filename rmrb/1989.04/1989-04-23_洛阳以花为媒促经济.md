Title: Luoyang Promotes Economy through Flowers
Author: Cui Guoping Chen Haiming
Date: 1989-04-23
Edition: 2

The annual Luoyang Peony Festival is being held from April 15 to 25. In recent years, the city has allocated special funds to support peony production. To date, there are over 310 varieties.

During the festival, the city receives up to 300,000 visitors daily. Economic, academic, and cultural figures from both domestic and international backgrounds engage in economic and technological negotiations, academic discussions, and material exhibitions and sample orders, bringing about significant economic and social benefits. The Sino-foreign economic and technological negotiation conference alone signed 85 contracts, with a total transaction amount of 26.26 million yuan.

### 洛阳以花为媒促经济
崔国平　陈海明
1989-04-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　洛阳以花为媒促经济
    本报洛阳电  一年一度的洛阳牡丹花会四月十五日至二十五日举行。连年来，该市拨出专款扶持牡丹生产。到目前，品种已达三百一十多个。
    花会期间每日来洛的客人多达三十万人次，国内外经济界、学术界、文艺界人士进行了经济技术洽谈、学术讨论、物资展销看样订货等活动，带来了良好的经济效益和社会效益。仅中外经济技术洽谈会就签订合同八十五项，成交额二千六百二十六万元。　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　（崔国平　陈海明）
