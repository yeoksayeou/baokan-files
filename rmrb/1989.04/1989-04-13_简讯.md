Title: Brief News
Author:
Date: 1989-04-13
Edition: 4

	Xinhua News Agency

	Four Soviet mountaineers conquered the 8,586-meter-high Gangkar Puensum on April 9. The ages of the four mountaineers are 36, 35, 31, and 29 respectively.

	The Norwegian ice hockey team secured the first place in the B group of this year's World Ice Hockey Championship with a record of 5 wins, 1 draw, and 1 loss on April 9. This marks their first promotion to the A group in 25 years.
