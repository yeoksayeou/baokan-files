Title: Deeply Mourn the Outstanding Proletarian Revolutionary and Military Leader: Funeral for Xiao Jingguang Held in Beijing
Author: Huang Caihong
Date: 1989-04-09
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 8th.

Party and state leaders, as well as representatives from various sectors of the capital and the military, numbering over 1,000, bid farewell to the late Xiao Jingguang, an outstanding leader of the state and the military, this afternoon at the General Logistics Department Hall. Xiao Jingguang, who passed away on March 29 due to illness, lay in state dressed in his naval uniform, covered with the flag of the Communist Party of China. Young soldiers stood guard over the body of this outstanding proletarian revolutionary and military leader, who made immortal contributions to the liberation of the Chinese people and the construction of socialism, as well as to the establishment and development of the People's Navy.

In the solemn music of mourning, Zhao Ziyang, Yang Shangkun, Li Peng, Wan Li, Qiao Shi, Hu Qili, Yao Yilin, Wang Zhen, and others bowed to Xiao Jingguang's body and laid wreaths in front of this loyal communist warrior, our army's outstanding military commander, and one of the main founders of the People's Navy. They also expressed their condolences to Xiao Jingguang's family.

Deng Xiaoping, Chen Yun, Li Xiannian, Peng Zhen, Deng Yingchao, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Li Ruihuan, Li Ximing, Yang Ruidai, Hu Yaobang, Ding Guangen, Bo Yibo, Rui Xingwen, Wang Shoudao, Wu Xiuquan, Jiang Hua, Li Yiming, Song Shilun, Zhang Jinfu, Zhang Aiping, Lu Dingyi, Hu Qiaomu, Huang Hua, Cheng Zihua, Saifuddin Azizi, Rong Yiren, Chen Muxia, Fei Xiaotong, Sun Qimeng, Lei Jieqiong, Wang Binguang, Wang Fang, Li Guixian, Chen Xitong, Chen Junsheng, Liu Fuzhi, Kang Keqing, Pabala Gelengjie, Hu Ziyang, Zhou Peiyuan, Wang Guangying, Deng Zhaoxiang, Ba Jin, Liu Jingji, Wang Enmao, Qian Xuesen, Qian Weichang, Hu Sheng, Sun Xiaocun, Su Buqing, Sima Yiming, Ding Guangxun, and others sent wreaths.

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Central Advisory Commission, the Central Discipline Inspection Commission, the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the State Council, the Central Military Commission, the Supreme People's Court, the Supreme People's Procuratorate, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, relevant departments of the Party Central Committee and the State Council, the General Staff Department, the General Political Department, the General Logistics Department, various military regions, the Military Commission Office, Beijing Municipality, Hunan Province, Liaoning Province, Changsha City, and Yan'an City sent wreaths.

Those who attended the funeral and sent wreaths also included: Tian Jiyun, Jiang Zemin, Li Tieying, Wu Xueqian, Song Ping, Qin Jiwei, Song Renqiong, Yan Mingfu, Wen Jiabao, Wang Ping, Liu Lantao, Yang Dezhi, Xiao Ke, Yu Quli, Chen Pishan, Chen Xilian, Duan Jigui, Geng Biao, Ji Pengfei, Huang Zhen, Kang Shien, Xi Zhongxun, Peng Chong, Wei Guoqing, Ju Jing, Apuo Angjin, Zhou Guocheng, Yan Jici, Ye Fei, Liao Hansheng, Ni Zhifu, Wang Hanbin, Song Jian, Zhuo Jiahua, Ren Jianxin, Wang Renmei, Fang Yi, Gu Mu, Yang Jingren, Zhao Puchu, Qu Wu, Ma Wenrui, Cheng Siyuan, Lu Jiaxi, Qian Zhengying, Hong Xuezhi, Liu Huaqing, and others. Representatives from Xiao Jingguang's hometown, Changsha City, Hunan Province, also attended the funeral.

During Xiao Jingguang's illness, Deng Xiaoping, Yang Shangkun, Chen Yun, Wan Li, Li Xiannian, Peng Zhen, Deng Yingchao, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Hu Qili, Wang Zhen, and other leaders visited him in the hospital.

After the funeral, Xiao Jingguang's body was escorted by the responsible comrades of the funeral office and his family to the Babaoshan Crematorium for cremation.

### 深切悼念杰出的无产阶级革命家、军事家  肖劲光遗体告别仪式在京举行  赵紫阳杨尚昆李鹏等领导人和各界千余人参加
黄彩虹
1989-04-09
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　深切悼念杰出的无产阶级革命家、军事家
    肖劲光遗体告别仪式在京举行
    赵紫阳杨尚昆李鹏等领导人和各界千余人参加
    新华社北京４月８日电  （记者黄彩虹）党和国家领导人及首都各界群众、解放军干部战士代表１０００余人，今天下午在总后勤部礼堂向国家和军队的优秀领导人肖劲光同志的遗体告别。安卧在鲜花翠柏丛中的肖劲光同志遗体身穿海军军装，上面覆盖着中国共产党党旗。青年战士为这位在中国人民的解放事业和社会主义建设事业中，在人民海军的创建和发展壮大中建立了不朽功勋的杰出无产阶级革命家、军事家持枪守灵。肖劲光同志因病于３月２９日逝世。
    在悲壮的哀乐声中，赵紫阳、杨尚昆、李鹏、万里、乔石、胡启立、姚依林、王震等向肖劲光同志的遗体鞠躬，并在这位久经考验的忠诚的共产主义战士、我军卓越的军事指挥员、人民海军的主要创建者的灵前献了花圈。他们向肖劲光同志的亲属表示慰问。
    邓小平、陈云、李先念、彭真、邓颖超、徐向前、聂荣臻、李瑞环、李锡铭、杨汝岱、胡耀邦、丁关根、薄一波、芮杏文、王首道、伍修权、江华、李一氓、李德生、宋时轮、张劲夫、张爱萍、陆定一、胡乔木、黄华、程子华、赛福鼎·艾则孜、荣毅仁、陈慕华、费孝通、孙起孟、雷洁琼、王丙乾、王芳、李贵鲜、陈希同、陈俊生、刘复之、康克清、帕巴拉·格列朗杰、胡子昂、周培源、王光英、邓兆祥、巴金、刘靖基、王恩茂、钱学森、钱伟长、胡绳、孙晓村、苏步青、司马义·艾买提、侯镜如、丁光训等献了花圈。
    中共中央、中央顾问委员会、中央纪律检查委员会、全国人大常委会、国务院、中央军委、最高人民法院、最高人民检察院、政协全国委员会、党中央和国务院有关部门、解放军各总部、各军兵种、各大军区、军委办公厅、北京市、湖南省、辽宁省、长沙市、延安市党政机关送了花圈。
    参加遗体告别仪式并送花圈的还有：田纪云、江泽民、李铁映、吴学谦、宋平、秦基伟、宋任穷、阎明复、温家宝、王平、刘澜涛、杨得志、肖克、余秋里、陈丕显、陈锡联、段君毅、耿飚、姬鹏飞、黄镇、康世恩、习仲勋、彭冲、韦国清、朱学范、阿沛·阿旺晋美、周谷城、严济慈、叶飞、廖汉生、倪志福、王汉斌、宋健、邹家华、任建新、王任重、方毅、谷牧、杨静仁、赵朴初、屈武、马文瑞、程思远、卢嘉锡、钱正英、洪学智、刘华清等。肖劲光同志的家乡湖南省长沙市的代表参加了遗体告别仪式。

    告别仪式结束后，肖劲光同志的遗体由治丧办公室的负责同志陪同其家属护送到八宝山火化。
