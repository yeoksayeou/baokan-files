Title: Saudi Arabia to Establish Business Representative Office in Beijing
Author: Guo Xiaoyong
Date: 1989-04-19
Edition: 1

Saudi Arabia to Establish Business Representative Office in Beijing

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 19th — (Reporter Guo Xiaoyong) The Deputy Minister of Finance of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia said today that Saudi Arabia has decided to establish a business representative office in Beijing and has appointed a director for the office. Deputy Minister of Finance and Chairman of the Arab Monetary Fund, Abdulrahman Al-Ghais, revealed this news today during an interview with Xinhua News Agency.

### 沙特将在京设商务代表处
郭晓勇
1989-04-19
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　沙特将在京设商务代表处
    据新华社北京４月１９日电　（记者郭晓勇）沙特阿拉伯王国财经副大臣今天在这里说，沙特已决定在北京建立商务代表处，并任命了一位代表处主任。沙特财经副大臣、阿拉伯货币基金会董事长阿卜杜拉·古维什是今天在接受新华社记者采访时透露这一消息的。
