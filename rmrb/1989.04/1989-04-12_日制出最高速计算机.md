Title: Japan Develops the Fastest Supercomputer
Author:
Date: 1989-04-12
Edition: 3

Japan Develops the Fastest Supercomputer

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, April 10th - According to an announcement by the Japanese company Nippon Denki (NEC) today, this company has developed the world's fastest supercomputer to date.

This supercomputer can perform 22 billion calculations per second. Previously, the fastest computer was an American-made machine, which could perform 9.14 billion calculations per second.

NEC plans to start producing this computer from next June, and over the next four years, it will sell 80 units domestically and 30 units internationally.

### 日制出最高速计算机

1989-04-12
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　日制出最高速计算机
    新华社东京4月10日电　据日本电气公司今天宣布，这家公司研制出迄今世界上运算速度最高的超级电子计算机。
    这种超级电子计算机可以在1秒钟内进行220亿次运算，在此之前最高速运算的是美国研制的电子计算机，它的运算速度为1秒91.4亿次。
    日本电气公司计划从明年6月起生产这种计算机，今后4年中将向国内外分别推销80台和30台。
