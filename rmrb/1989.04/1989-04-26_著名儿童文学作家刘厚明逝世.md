Title: Renowned Children's Literature Author Liu Houming Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-04-26
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 26th.

Renowned children's literature author Liu Houming passed away.

The 55-year-old renowned children's literature author Liu Houming passed away in Beijing on April 22, 1989, due to heart disease.

Liu Houming was an outstanding member of the Communist Party of China, vice chairman of the National Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese, deputy secretary of the Chinese Writers Association's Children's Literature Committee, and deputy secretary-general of the National Youth and Children's Cultural and Artistic Committee. His major works include "By the Arrow River" and "Selected Novels and Plays by Liu Houming."

### 著名儿童文学作家刘厚明逝世

1989-04-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　著名儿童文学作家刘厚明逝世
    新华社北京４月２６日电  ５５岁的著名儿童文学作家刘厚明因患心脏病，于１９８９年４月２２日在北京逝世。
    刘厚明同志是中国共产党优秀党员，全国青联副主席，中国作协儿童文学委员会副主任，全国少年儿童文化艺术委员会副秘书长。他的主要作品有《箭杆河边》、《刘厚明小说剧本选》等。
