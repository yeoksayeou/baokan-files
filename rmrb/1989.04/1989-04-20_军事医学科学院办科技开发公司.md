Title: Military Medical Science Academy Establishes Technology Development Company
Author: Rèn Zhī
Date: 1989-04-20
Edition: 2

A new type of enterprise, the "Beijing Si Huan Technology Development Company," was established today in Beijing. This company leverages the research strengths of the Military Medical Science Academy.

The company, established by the Military Medical Science Academy, has 15 subsidiary companies. It will organize the development, production, and sales of a series of medical and pharmaceutical products, as well as medical instruments.

### 军事医学科学院办科技开发公司
任之
1989-04-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　军事医学科学院办科技开发公司
    本报北京４月２０日讯  记者任之报道：以军事医学科技研究力量为依托的新型企业——“北京四环科技开发公司”，今天在此间成立。
    该公司是由军事医学科学院创办的，下设１５个分公司，将组织医药、医疗仪器等科技系列产品的开发、生产和销售。
