Title: Spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs: China Wishes to Establish Diplomatic Relations with Singapore and is Consulting with Indonesia on Technical Issues for Resuming Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1989-04-14
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 13th. The spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Jin Guihua, said here today that the political issues regarding the resumption of diplomatic relations between China and Indonesia have been resolved, and the two countries' representatives to the United Nations have begun consultations on some technical issues.

At today's afternoon press conference, when asked about the resumption of diplomatic relations between China and Indonesia, Jin Guihua said, "The normalization of relations between China and Indonesia is beneficial to the peoples of both countries and to peace and development in the region. Both sides have a consistent understanding of this."

When asked about China's relations with Singapore, the spokesperson said that although China and Singapore have not formally established diplomatic relations, the friendly cooperative relations between the two countries in many fields have developed well. China is willing to establish diplomatic relations with Singapore on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. He said, "We have noted that the leaders of Singapore have recently also expressed a willingness to formally establish diplomatic relations with China."

### 外交部发言人说　中国愿同新加坡建交　正同印尼磋商复交技术问题

1989-04-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　外交部发言人说
　　中国愿同新加坡建交
　　正同印尼磋商复交技术问题
    新华社北京４月１３日电　外交部发言我金桂华今天在这里说，关于中国同印度尼西亚恢复外交关系的政治上的问题已经解决，两国驻联合国代表已经开始就一些技术上的问题进行磋商。
    在今天下午的新闻发布会上，当有记者问及中国同印度尼西亚恢复外交的问题时，金桂华说，“中国同印度尼西亚关系正常化对两国人民和地区和平与发展有利，双方的认识是一致的。”
    当被问及中国同新加坡的关系时，这位发言人说，中国和新加坡虽然没有正式建交，但两国在许多领域里的友好合作关系发展得很好。中国愿意在和平共处五项原则基础上同新加坡建立外交关系。他说，“我们注意到新加坡领导人最近也表示愿意与中国正式建交”。
