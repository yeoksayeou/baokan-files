Title: 1989 National Shooting Championship Concludes
Author: Jǐng Rùyuè
Date: 1989-04-25
Edition: 4

The 1989 National Shooting Championship concluded today. A batch of new talents surpassed the veterans.

	New China News Agency, Shijiazhuang, April 24 (Reporter Jǐng Rùyuè) In today's concluded 1989 National Shooting Championship, PLA shooter Shi Yujié scored 585 rings in the qualification round, breaking the national record of 583 rings in the men's standard pistol 60-shot event and surpassing the world record of 584 rings in the same event.

	This competition began on April 15. In the rifle and pistol events held from April 22 to 24, 12 champions were decided. In the men's free pistol slow-fire event held on April 22, Liaoning's Wang Yifu scored 569 rings in the qualification round, tying the Asian record. In the final, he scored a total of 662 rings to win the championship and surpass the 660-ring Asian record set by Xu Haifeng in the Olympic event final.

	The champions of other events are:

	Men:
		Air rifle 60 shots: Hebei's Ning Lijia;
		Free pistol rapid-fire: Jiangxi's Luo Bin;
		Air pistol 60 shots: Inner Mongolia's Jin Guozhi;
		Small-bore free rifle 60 shots prone: Shanxi's Zhang Xiaodong;
		Standard pistol 60 shots: PLA's Shi Yujié;
		Centerfire pistol: PLA's Shi Yujié;
		Small-bore free rifle 3x40: Beijing's Xu Xiaojing.

	Women:
		Small-bore standard rifle 60 shots prone: Beijing's Li Dan;
		Small-bore pistol 60 shots: Ningxia's Qi Chunxia;
		Small-bore standard rifle 3x20: Hunan's Zhang Qiuping;
		Air pistol 40 shots: Ningxia's Qi Chunxia;
		Air rifle: Hunan's Zhang Qiuping.

	In this competition, some famous athletes such as Xu Haifeng, Wu Lanying, and Li Yuwei were all defeated by the rising stars.

### 一九八九年全国射击冠军赛结束  一批新秀超过老将
景如月
1989-04-25
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　一九八九年全国射击冠军赛结束    
    一批新秀超过老将
    新华社石家庄４月２４日电（记者景如月）在今天结束的１９８９年全国射击冠军赛上，解放军选手石玉杰以５８５环的成绩，在资格赛中打破男子标准手枪６０发项目５８３环的全国纪录，同时超该项目５８４环的世界纪录。
    这次比赛是本月１５日开始的。在２２日至２４日的步枪、手枪项目比赛中，共决出１２个项目的冠军。２２日进行的男子自选手枪慢射比赛中，辽宁的王义夫以５６９环的资格赛成绩平亚洲纪录。决赛中，他又以６６２环的总成绩获该项目冠军，并超过许海峰创造的６６０环奥运会项目决赛亚洲纪录。
    其他项目冠军是：
    男子：气步枪６０发，河北的宁立佳；个人自选手枪速射，江西的骆彬；气手枪６０发，内蒙古的金国志；小口径自选步枪６０发卧射，山西的张晓东；标准手枪６０发，解放军的石玉杰；中心发火手枪，解放军的石玉杰；小口径自选步枪３×４０，北京的徐小京。
    女子：个人小口径标准步枪６０发卧射，北京的李丹；小口径手枪６０发，宁夏的祈春霞；小口径标准步枪３×２０，湖南的张秋萍；气手枪４０发，宁夏的祈春霞；气步枪，湖南的张秋萍。
    这次比赛一些著名运动员，如许海峰、巫兰英、李玉伟等，均负于后起之秀。
