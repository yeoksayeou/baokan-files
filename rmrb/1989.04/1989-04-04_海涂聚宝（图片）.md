Title: Treasure from the Tidal Flats
Author: Lǐ Yúnqiān
Date: 1989-04-04
Edition: 2

The vast tidal flats are now a treasure trove for the coastal residents of Yuhuan County, Zhejiang. The image shows several thousand acres of tidal flats in front of Dakuang Village and other villages in Huancheng Township that have been organized and are ready for the release of baby clams.
