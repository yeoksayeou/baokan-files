Title: Angolan Ruling Party and SWAPO Discuss the Situation; First Batch of Kenyan Soldiers Head to Namibia
Author:
Date: 1989-04-15
Edition: 3

The ruling party of Angola, the People's Movement for the Liberation of Angola (MPLA), and the South West Africa People's Organization (SWAPO) held a joint meeting in Luanda today.

According to the Angolan National Radio, the chairman of the MPLA, José Eduardo dos Santos, and the chairman of SWAPO, Sam Nujoma, attended the meeting.

The meeting mainly discussed the situation in southwestern Africa since the military conflict between SWAPO and South African forces in northern Namibia.

The Angolan National Radio said that the leaders of both sides will study how SWAPO's guerrilla forces in Namibia can concentrate at the assembly points established by the United Nations Transition Assistance Group, and how to move to the area north of the 16th parallel in Angola. However, the presence of South African forces near these assembly points poses a threat to the concentration of SWAPO fighters.

The Angolan radio emphasized that the results of the Windhoek meeting of the Angola, Cuba, and South Africa Monitoring Committee will be beneficial for the leaders of Angola and SWAPO to find ways to ease the tense situation in northern Namibia and to put the process of Namibian independence back on track.

The first batch of 207 Kenyan soldiers dispatched to the United Nations Transition Assistance Group in Namibia left Nairobi for Namibia on April 13.

This is part of the 850 military personnel that Kenya is sending to the United Nations peacekeeping force in Namibia. The remaining personnel will be sent in three batches over the weekend.

### 安执政党同人组讨论局势　首批肯尼亚军人赴纳米比亚

1989-04-15
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　安执政党同人组讨论局势
　　首批肯尼亚军人赴纳米比亚
    新华社罗安达４月１３日电　安哥拉人运一劳动党和西南非洲人民组织（人组）今天在罗安达举行联席会议。
    据安哥拉国家电台广播，安哥拉人运一劳动党主席多斯桑托斯和人组主席萨姆·努乔马等出席了这次会议。
    会议主要讨论人组与南非部队在纳米比亚北部发生军事冲突以来的西南部非洲局势。
    安哥拉国家电台说，双方领导人将研究人组在纳米比亚境内的游击队如何向联合国过渡时期援助团建立的集结点集中，以及前往安哥拉境内南纬１６度线以北的办法。然而，南非部队呆在这些集结点附近，给人组战士的集中造成了威胁。
    安哥拉电台强调，安哥拉、古巴、南非监督委员会温得和克会议的结果将有利于安哥拉和人组领导人找到缓和纳米比亚北部紧张局势，并使纳米比亚独立进程走上正常轨道的办法。
    新华社内罗毕４月１３日电　肯尼亚派往联合国纳米比亚过渡时期援助团的首批军人２０７人１３日离开内罗毕赴纳米比亚。
    这是肯尼亚派往联合国驻纳米比亚维持和平部队的８５０名军事人员中的一部分。其余人员将分成三批于本周末赴纳米比亚。
