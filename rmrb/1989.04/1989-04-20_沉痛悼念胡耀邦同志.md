Solemnly Mourning Comrade Hu Yaobang

Condolences from Some Foreign Political Parties and Leaders

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 20th - More foreign political party leaders have sent telegrams and letters of condolence to mourn the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang.

The Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on April 19th, stating: "We are deeply saddened to learn of the passing of Hu Yaobang, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee. Please accept our profound condolences, and we extend our deepest sympathies to his family."

The Chairman of the German Social Democratic Party, Vogel, sent a telegram to General Secretary Zhao Ziyang on April 17th, saying, "Hu Yaobang was one of the Chinese politicians who consistently worked to develop relations with the German Social Democratic Party and who strongly promoted the good relations between the People's Republic of China and the Federal Republic of Germany. We will remember his contributions."

The Chairman of the German Communist Party, Mies, sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the CPC on April 17th, describing Hu Yaobang as a great revolutionary, patriot, and internationalist, and expressing the desire to forever remember him.

The Central Committee of the Spanish Communist Party sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the CPC on April 18th, stating that Hu Yaobang's life as a thorough revolutionary has set an example for us, and Spanish communists will never forget Comrade Hu Yaobang's decisive contributions to normalizing relations between the Communist Party of China and the Communist Party of Spain.

The Chairman of the Spanish Workers' Party, Carrillo, sent a telegram to the Chinese Ambassador to Spain, Yuan Dang, on April 16th, expressing deep sorrow over the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang.

The Central Committee of the Ethiopian Workers' Party sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the CPC on April 18th, stating: "Comrade Hu Yaobang was an outstanding communist who dedicated his life to the socialist cause, to his party, and to the Chinese people. His passing is a great loss."

The Chairman of the Thai Social Action Party, Sitti, also sent a letter to General Secretary Zhao Ziyang on April 19th, mourning the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang.

Condolences from Some National Leaders

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 20th - More national and government leaders have sent telegrams and letters of condolence to mourn the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang.

On April 19th, Nigerian President Babangida sent a telegram to Chairman Yang Shangkun, representing the Nigerian government and people, expressing sincere condolences over the passing of Hu Yaobang. The telegram also praised Hu Yaobang's role in formulating and implementing China's policies of reform, modernization, and opening up to the outside world.

On April 19th, Nicaraguan President Ortega sent a letter to General Secretary Zhao Ziyang, expressing deep sorrow over Hu Yaobang's passing and noting that his passing is "an irreplaceable loss."

On April 18th, Spanish Prime Minister Gonzalez sent a telegram to Premier Li Peng, saying: "I represent the Spanish government and, on a personal level, express my condolences. I will always remember his contributions to maintaining and promoting the good relations between China and Spain."

Condolences from Chinese Scholars and Students in Ottawa

Our reporter in Ottawa, April 19th - Chinese visiting scholars and students in the Ottawa area today requested that the reporter's station forward their telegram of condolence to the funeral committee for Comrade Hu Yaobang and the People's Daily. The full text is as follows:

We are deeply saddened to learn of the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang. Please convey our deepest sympathies and regards to his family. We are determined to carry on Comrade Hu Yaobang's will, to push through political and economic reforms, and to achieve the Four Modernizations as soon as possible.

Comrade Hu Yaobang's spirit will forever live in our hearts!

Ottawa Chinese Visiting Scholars and Students Association
April 19, 1989

### 沉痛悼念胡耀邦同志

1989-04-20
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　沉痛悼念胡耀邦同志
    一些外国政党及领导人的唁电唁函
    新华社北京４月２０日电　又有一些外国政党的领导人发来唁电、唁函，悼念胡耀邦同志逝世。
    保加利亚共产党中央委员会４月１９日在致中共中央的唁电中说：“我们悲痛地惊悉中共中央政治局委员胡耀邦逝世，请接受我们沉痛的悼念，并向他的亲属表示深切的慰问。”
    德国社会民主党主席福格尔４月１７日在致赵紫阳总书记的唁电中说，“胡耀邦是那些始终不渝地致力于同德国社会民主党发展关系、并有力地促进了中华人民共和国和德意志联邦共和国良好关系的中国政治家之一。我们将缅怀他的事业。”
    德国的共产党主席米斯４月１７日在致中共中央的唁电中称胡耀邦是伟大的革命家、爱国主义者和国际主义者，并且表示要永远怀念他。
    西班牙共产党中央委员会４月１８日在致中共中央的唁电中说，胡耀邦“作为彻底革命者的一生给我们树立了榜样，西班牙共产党人永远不会忘记胡耀邦同志在使中国共产党和西班牙共产党关系正常化中的决定性贡献。”
    西班牙劳动者党主席卡里略４月１６日在致中国驻西班牙大使原焘的唁电中对胡耀邦同志的逝世表示深切的哀悼。
    埃塞俄比亚工人党中央４月１８日在致中共中央的唁电中说：“胡耀邦同志是一位杰出的共产主义者，他把自己的一生献给了社会主义事业，献给了他的党和中国人民。他的逝世是一个巨大损失。”
    泰国社会行动党主席西提４月１９日也致函赵紫阳总书记，哀悼胡耀邦同志逝世。
    一些国家领导人的唁电唁函
    新华社北京４月２０日电　又有一些国家和政府的领导人发来唁电或唁函，悼念胡耀邦同志逝世。
    尼日利亚总统巴班吉达４月１９日在致杨尚昆主席的唁电中代表尼日利亚政府和人民对胡耀邦的逝世表示诚挚的哀悼，唁电并赞扬了胡耀邦生前对中国制订和执行迈向改革、现代化和对外开放政策所起的作用。
    尼加拉瓜总统奥尔特加４月１９日在致赵紫阳总书记的唁函中对胡耀邦逝世深感悲痛，并指出胡耀邦的逝世是“一个无法弥补的损失”。
    西班牙首相冈萨雷斯４月１８日在致李鹏总理的唁电中说：“我谨代表西班牙政府并以我个人的名义表示哀悼，我将永远铭记他为维护和促进中国和西班牙之间的良好关系所作的贡献。”
    渥太华中国访问学者和留学生的唁电
    本报渥太华４月１９日电　在渥太华地区学习的中国访问学者和留学生，今天请记者站向胡耀邦同志治丧委员会和人民日报转发他们对胡耀邦同志逝世的唁电，全文如下：
    惊悉胡耀邦同志逝世，我们深感悲痛。请向他的家属转达我们深切的同情和问候。我们决心继承胡耀邦同志的遗志，把政治和经济改革进行到底，早日实现四个现代化。
    耀邦同志的精神永远活在我们心中！
    　　　　　　　　　　　渥太华中国访问学者和留学生
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　联谊会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　１９８９年４月１９日
