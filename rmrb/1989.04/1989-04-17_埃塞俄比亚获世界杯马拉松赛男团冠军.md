Title: Ethiopia Wins Men's Team Championship at World Cup Marathon
Author:
Date: 1989-04-17
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, April 16th.

Ethiopian athletes today won the men's team championship and the first two individual spots in the World Cup Marathon held in Milan, Italy.

Zerakeselam "Zerake" Wolde won the race with a time of 2 hours, 10 minutes, and 28 seconds. His teammate Negari "Negari" Desta finished just 8 seconds behind in second place. These two athletes, along with another teammate, secured the team championship for Ethiopia with a combined time of 6 hours, 37 minutes, and 20 seconds.

The Italian team took the second-place team spot with a time of 6 hours, 37 minutes, and 51 seconds. Their athlete Gelindo Bordin finished third individually with a time of 2 hours, 10 minutes, and 49 seconds. The teams finishing in third to eighth place were France, the Soviet Union, West Germany, East Germany, Japan, and Spain, respectively.

### 埃塞俄比亚获世界杯马拉松赛男团冠军

1989-04-17
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　埃塞俄比亚获世界杯马拉松赛男团冠军
    新华社罗马４月１６日电　埃塞俄比亚选手今天在意大利米兰举行的世界杯马拉松赛男子组的比赛中，赢得了团体冠军和个人前两名。
    扎拉克以２小时１０分２８秒第一个到达终点，他的队友内·迪仅以８秒之差获第二名。这两位选手和他们的另一位队友以６小时３７分２０秒的成绩，使埃塞俄比亚队荣登团体冠军宝座。
    意大利队获得团体第二名，成绩是６小时３７分５１秒。该队选手波利以２小时１０分４９秒的成绩获得个人第三名。获团体第三至第八名的队依次为法国、苏联、西德、民主德国、日本和西班牙。
