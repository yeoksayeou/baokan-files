Title: U.S. Aircraft Conduct Reconnaissance Over North Korea
Author:
Date: 1989-04-19
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 18th.

A U.S. U-2 reconnaissance aircraft conducted aerial reconnaissance along the Korean Military Demarcation Line from west to east over North Korea this morning at 9 a.m. Similar reconnaissance activities have been carried out 15 times this month.

Since April, the U.S. has also dispatched RV-1 and RC-12 reconnaissance aircraft to conduct 85 reconnaissance missions over North Korea. U.S. E-3A AWACS aircraft also conducted reconnaissance over the northern interior regions on the 3rd and 13th of this month.

The KCNA criticized these U.S. reconnaissance activities, stating that they have escalated tensions on the Korean Peninsula.

### 美机对朝北方进行侦察

1989-04-19
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美机对朝北方进行侦察
    新华社平壤４月１８日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社今天报道，美国一架Ｕ—２型侦察机今天上午９点沿朝鲜军事分界线由西向东对朝鲜北方进行了空中侦察活动。类似的侦察活动，４月份以来已进行过１５次。
    ４月份以来，美国还出动ＲＶ—１和ＲＣ—１２型侦察机对朝鲜北方进行过８５次侦察活动。美国Ｅ—３Ａ预警飞机也曾于３日和１３日两次对北方纵深地区进行了侦察活动。
    朝中社抨击美国的这些侦察活动加剧了朝鲜半岛的紧张局势。
