Title: Conductor, Attendant, and Pickpocket Collude to Rob Passengers; Train 277 Becomes a "Thieves' Paradise"
Author:
Date: 1989-04-12
Edition: 2

太原市北城公安分局近日查获一起乘警、列车员、扒手沆瀣一气洗劫乘客的铁路盗窃案，演出盗窃、分赃、窝赃丑剧的犯罪分子黄林、高玉龙、李俊、王化诗等已被抓获。

On April 11th, the Beicheng Public Security Bureau of Taiyuan City recently uncovered a case of railway theft involving a conductor, train attendant, and pickpocket colluding to rob passengers. The criminals, Huang Lin, Gao Yulong, Li Jun, and Wang Huashi, who were involved in the theft, division of loot, and hiding of stolen goods, have been apprehended.

2月24日，在太原开往山东坊子277次列车返回途中，山东乘客解金倚的一只装有1万余元现金、6万元汇款单据的密码手提箱被盗。失主将案情报于列车乘警任佐发。任佐发同乘警高玉龙商量后决定查车。但不知为何，列车到终点站太原时，案件仍无线索。

On February 24th, during the return trip of Train 277 from Taiyuan to Shandong Fangzi, a passenger from Shandong, Xie Jinyi, had a password-protected briefcase containing over 10,000 yuan in cash and 60,000 yuan in remittance documents stolen. The victim reported the incident to the train conductor, Ren Zuofa. After consulting with the other conductor, Gao Yulong, they decided to search the train. However, for unknown reasons, by the time the train reached its terminal station in Taiyuan, the case still had no leads.

后来经侦破：手提箱被扒窃惯犯黄林（太铁工人）盗窃后，藏匿于卧铺下的地板上，查车时被列车员李俊发现。黄林答应给李俊“好处”，不让其声张，并把全部赃款交李藏匿。这事被另一乘务员王化诗看见，李塞给王1000元，堵住了他的嘴。李俊自己也揣了1000元，把其余赃款藏匿起来。

Later, through investigation, it was revealed that the briefcase was stolen by a habitual pickpocket, Huang Lin (a worker for Taiyuan Railway), and hidden under the sleeper berth. During the train search, train attendant Li Jun discovered it. Huang Lin promised Li Jun a "reward" to keep quiet and handed over all the stolen money to him for safekeeping. This incident was witnessed by another attendant, Wang Huashi. Li Jun gave Wang 1,000 yuan to keep his mouth shut. Li Jun also kept 1,000 yuan for himself and hid the rest of the stolen money.

乘警高玉龙得知失窃的消息后，即明白是谁干的。他悄悄地诈了惯窃黄林500元后，便对乘警任佐发说：“这个案子我处理吧，你别管了。”他把报案材料锁在抽屉里，直到事情败露也未上报。

After learning of the theft, conductor Gao Yulong immediately knew who was responsible. He quietly extorted 500 yuan from the habitual thief Huang Lin and then told Ren Zuofa, "I'll handle this case. You don't need to worry about it." He locked the report in a drawer and did not submit it until the matter was exposed.

据高玉龙交代：自去年12月以来，他共“吃”黄林等扒手“孝敬”款3000元。犯罪分子供称：277次列车是他们长期扒窃作案的“庇护所”。

Gao Yulong confessed that since last December, he had received a total of 3,000 yuan in "tributes" from Huang Lin and other pickpockets. The criminals admitted that Train 277 was their long-term "sanctuary" for pickpocketing.
