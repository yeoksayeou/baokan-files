Title: Deputy Secretary of the Discipline Inspection Commission of the General Logistics Department Passes Away in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-04-01
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing,

Deputy Secretary of the Discipline Inspection Commission of the General Logistics Department, Xin Fu, passed away in Beijing due to illness on February 21. He was 73 years old.

Xin Fu was a native of Dong'a County, Shandong Province. He joined the Communist Party of China in 1934 and enlisted in the army in 1938. He held various positions, including County Armed Forces Department Director, District Guard Battalion Commander, Political Instructor, Regiment Political Commissar, County Party Secretary and County Magistrate, Military Sub-district Political Department Director and Deputy Director, General Logistics Department Staff Department Director, General Logistics Department Political Department Staff Department Director, and Deputy Director of the General Logistics Department Political Department.

Xin Fu maintained high standards for himself throughout his life, consistently adhering to the standards of a Communist Party member. Whether during wartime or in peacetime, he always upheld and carried forward the fine traditions and styles of our Party and our army.

### 总后纪委副书记新斧在京病逝

1989-04-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　总后纪委副书记新斧在京病逝
    新华社北京电  中国人民解放军总后勤部纪律检查委员会副书记新斧同志因病于２月２１日在北京逝世，终年７３岁。
    新斧同志是山东省东阿县人，１９３４年加入中国共产党，１９３８年入伍，先后任县武装部长、专区警卫大队长、政治教导员、团政委、县委书记兼县长、军分区政治部主任和副政委、总后干部部处长、总后政治部干部部部长、总后政治部副主任等职。
    新斧同志几十年如一日，自觉以共产党员的标准要求自己，无论在战争年代还是和平建设时期，始终保持和发扬了我党我军的优良传统和作风。
