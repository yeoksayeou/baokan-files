Title: The World Economy Is in the Midst of a Major Turn
Author:
Date: 1989-04-08
Edition: 1

The People's Daily reports that since the 1980s, the world economy has witnessed a series of new phenomena. Today, on page seven, the newspaper publishes an article by Guo Zhenyuan titled "The World Economy in the Midst of a Major Turn," which discusses these changes and their impacts.

The article is a detailed exploration of the significant shifts occurring in the global economic landscape. It highlights the transformations that have taken place and analyzes their far-reaching consequences. The piece aims to provide readers with a comprehensive understanding of the current economic environment and its implications for the future.

### 本报今日七版刊登文章阐述  世界经济正处于巨大转折之中

1989-04-08
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　本报今日七版刊登文章阐述
    世界经济正处于巨大转折之中
    本报讯  进入八十年代以来，世界经济出现了一系列新现象。本报七版今天发表了郭震远写的《处于巨大转折中的世界经济》一文，阐述了这些变化及其影响。
