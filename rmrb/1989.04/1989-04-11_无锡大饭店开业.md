Title: Wuxi Grand Hotel Opens
Author: Jǐ Hóngqǐng
Date: 1989-04-11
Edition: 2

Wuxi Grand Hotel Opens
People's Daily, Wuxi

The Wuxi Grand Hotel, praised for having both "software and hardware of the highest quality," officially opened on April 8th. This is the first enterprise jointly established by Tokyo Rapid Transit Corporation of Japan and China.

Wuxi City received 167,000 overseas tourists last year, and during the peak tourist season, accommodations for foreign guests were extremely tight. This hotel, with 342 guest rooms (suites), has as its Chinese partners Wuxi Tourism Corporation and the China International Trust and Investment Corporation.

### 无锡大饭店开业
计泓赓
1989-04-11
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　无锡大饭店开业
    本报无锡电　记者计泓赓报道：被誉为“软硬件均属上乘”的无锡大饭店，４月８日正式开业。这是日本东京急行电铁株式会社与我国合资的第一家企业。
    无锡市去年共接待海外游客１６．７万人次，旅游旺季外宾住房十分紧张。这家拥有３４２间（套）客房的大饭店，中方合资者为无锡市旅游总公司、中国国际信托投资公司。
