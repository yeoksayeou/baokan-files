Title: Luxembourg Prime Minister States that Tibet is Part of China
Author: Zhuge Canglin
Date: 1989-04-05
Edition: 4

Luxembourg Prime Minister States that Tibet is Part of China

LUXEMBOURG, April 3 (Xinhua) — Luxembourg Prime Minister Jacques Santer today solemnly stated that Luxembourg considers Tibet to be a part of Chinese territory, and Luxembourg will not take any actions that could lead to Tibet's separation from China.

Santer made these remarks during a meeting with Zhou Nan, the Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of China, who is currently visiting Luxembourg.

Zhou Nan briefly introduced the facts of the recent Lhasa disturbances to Prime Minister Santer. He emphasized that in order to adhere to and implement the policy of reform and opening up, China not only needs a peaceful international environment but also a stable and united domestic environment.

### 卢森堡首相表示  西藏是中国一部分
诸葛仓麟
1989-04-05
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　卢森堡首相表示
    西藏是中国一部分
    新华社卢森堡４月３日电  （记者诸葛仓麟）卢森堡首相雅克·桑德尔今天郑重表示，卢森堡认为西藏是中国领土的一部分，卢森堡决不采取任何可能把西藏从中国分裂出去的行动。
    桑德尔首相是在会见正在这里访问的中国外交部副部长周南时阐明上述立场的。
    周南向桑德尔首相简要介绍了最近拉萨骚乱事件的真相。他强调说，为了坚持贯彻执行改革开放政策，中国不仅需要和平的国际环境，而且需要安定团结的国内环境。
