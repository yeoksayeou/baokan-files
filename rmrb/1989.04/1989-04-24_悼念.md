Title: In Memoriam
Author: Gāoguāng
Date: 1989-04-24
Edition: 8

You once scaled Kunlun
Yet no halo of illusion adorned your head
You also held sway over China
Yet your face showed no divine majesty
Your heart
Like the bare sun and moon
Joy, anger, sorrow, and delight
Like the untamed tides
Your words
Like arrows from a bow
Once launched from your chest
They never waver or turn
A burning passion, melting away
Countless shackles
Arms outstretched, releasing
Millions of doves of love
No one worshipped you
As a savior to be revered
You were our
Brother who knew our cold and heat
Asked about our hunger and thirst
With a bond as deep as kin
Teacher who dedicated himself to duty
Your life's lessons
Enlightened generations
Traversing mountains and rivers to seek
The value of life
And the dignity of humanity
Under my own roof
I bow my head and reflect
Deeply mourning
No longer shedding bitter tears
(My hot tears have long since dried
In the spring of 1986, my old eyes became a cracked, dry pool)
No longer writing sorrowful memorial banners
(Neither white ink on black paper
Nor red ink on blue paper can express
The trembling of the soul)
No longer arranging pale wreaths
(After closing one's eyes, loyalty and treachery
The good and the wicked, all can enjoy
This lifeless art)
I can only offer my tribute
Like a clear stream
To you
Ten thousand petals of incense
A thousand leaves of loyalty

### 悼念
高光
1989-04-24
第8版(文学作品)
专栏：

　　悼念
    高光
    您曾攀登昆仑
    头上却没有虚幻的光环
    您也执掌中华
    面部竟不显神的威严
    您的心
    如同袒裸的日月
    喜怒哀乐
    恰似无羁的潮汐
    您的话语
    胜过弦上的箭
    胸中一旦发射
    决无回旋转弯
    一腔炽热，销溶掉
    难以计数的镣铐
    双臂潇洒，放飞出
    亿万友爱的信鸽
    没有人把您当作
    顶礼膜拜的救世主
    您是我们的
    知冷知热
    问饥问渴
    情同骨肉的兄长
    鞠躬尽瘁的老师
    您以生命写就的教案
    启迪世世代代
    跋山涉水地寻觅
    生命的价值
    和人类的尊严
    我在自己的屋檐下
    垂首扪心
    深深地哀悼
    不再流酸涩的泪水
    （我的热泪早已在
    丙辰清明流干
    老眼变成龟裂的枯潭）
    不再写哀怨的挽幛
    （不论是白纸黑字
    还是蓝纸血字，都无以表达
    心灵的震颤）
    不再扎苍白的花圈
    （瞑目以后，忠诚和奸佞
    善者与恶棍，都能享用
    这类僵死的艺术）
    我只有清流般的悼念
    献给您
    万瓣心香
    千叶肝胆
    　　　　１９８９年４月１５日夜
