Title: China's Export Commodities Fair Achieves New Record
Author: Liang Zhaoming
Date: 1989-04-23
Edition: 2

The first 10 days of this spring session of the China Export Commodities Fair have seen a favorable trading situation. As of April 21, the total export contract value has surpassed the level of the same period last spring.

It is understood that the top 10 export trading groups in terms of contract value are textiles, light industry, grains and oils, silk, agricultural products, New Era, handicrafts, chemicals, Metals & Minerals Import & Export Corporation, and machinery. The top 10 provinces and municipalities in terms of export contract value are Guangdong, Shanghai, Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Shandong, Hubei, Tianjin, Sichuan, Liaoning, and Henan.

### 广交会成交又创纪录
梁兆明
1989-04-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　广交会成交又创纪录
    本报广州４月２３日电  记者梁兆明报道：本届春交会头１０天成交形势好，截至２１日为止，出口成交总额超过去年春交会上旬的水平。
    据悉，各交易团出口成交金额最多的前１０名是纺织、轻工、粮油、丝绸、土畜、新时代、工艺、化工、五矿、机械；出口成交金额最多的前１０名省市是广东、上海、江苏、浙江、山东、湖北、天津、四川、辽宁、河南。
