Title: Beijing No. 64 Middle School Establishes a Parents' Committee
Author: Lǐ Dé Mín
Date: 1989-04-17
Edition: 2

Beijing No. 64 Middle School Establishes a Parents' Committee

	People's Daily, Beijing, April 17th — Reporter Li Demin reports: In order to form a network of education that combines schools, families, and society, Beijing No. 64 Middle School has established a Parents' Committee. The committee is composed of parents who care about the education cause and others.

### 北京６４中设家长委员会
李德民
1989-04-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　北京６４中设家长委员会
    本报北京４月１７日讯　记者李德民报道：为了形成学校、家庭、社会相结合的教育网络，北京６４中成立家长委员会。委员会由关心教育事业的家长等组成。
