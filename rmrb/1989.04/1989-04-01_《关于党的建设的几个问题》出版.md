Title: On Several Issues Regarding Party Building
Author:
Date: 1989-04-01
Edition: 2

The book "On Several Issues Regarding Party Building," a speech given by General Secretary Zhao Ziyang on January 28, 1989, during a party-building research class, has been published as a standalone volume by the People's Publishing House. It will be available for purchase in bookstores across the country starting today.

### 《关于党的建设的几个问题》出版

1989-04-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　《关于党的建设的几个问题》出版
    新华社北京３月３１日电  赵紫阳总书记１９８９年１月２８日在党建研究班上的讲话《关于党的建设的几个问题》，已由人民出版社出版单行本，即日起在全国各地新华书店发行。
