Title: Samaranch Congratulates the Asian Youth Gymnastics Championships
Author:
Date: 1989-04-20
Edition: 4

The Xinhua News Agency reported this evening that the State Sports Commission has learned that the President of the International Olympic Committee, Juan Antonio Samaranch, sent a telegram from Lausanne, Switzerland, to the International Olympic Committee Executive Member and President of the Chinese Olympic Committee, He Zhenliang, expressing warm congratulations on the upcoming Fourth Asian Youth Gymnastics Championships to be held in Beijing.

Samaranch also entrusted He Zhenliang and the International Olympic Committee Member, Vice President of the Chinese Taipei Olympic Committee, Wu Jingguo, to represent him at the Fourth Asian Youth Gymnastics Championships.

Mr. Wu Jingguo and his wife arrived in Beijing this evening via Hong Kong.

### 萨马兰奇电贺亚洲青年体操锦标赛

1989-04-20
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　萨马兰奇电贺亚洲青年体操锦标赛
    新华社北京４月１９日电  记者今晚从国家体委获悉，国际奥委会主席萨马兰奇从瑞士洛桑打电报给国际奥委会执委、中国奥委会主席何振梁，对即将在北京举行的第四届亚洲青年体操锦标赛表示热烈祝贺。
    萨马兰奇还在电报中委托何振梁和国际奥委会委员、中国台北奥委会副主席吴经国作为他的代表，出席第四届亚洲青年体操锦标赛。
    吴经国先生及其夫人今晚经香港抵达北京。
