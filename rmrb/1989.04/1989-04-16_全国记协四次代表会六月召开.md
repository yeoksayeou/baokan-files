Title: The Fourth National Congress of Journalists to be Held in June
Author: Wen Lu
Date: 1989-04-16
Edition: 2

The Fourth National Congress of the All-China Journalists Association, which has been postponed several times, is scheduled to be held from June 5 to 10 in Beijing. Over 600 delegates are expected to attend the meeting.

The main tasks of this conference are: to adopt the "Code of Professional Ethics for Chinese Journalists"; to discuss the "Press Law" and the "Temporary Regulations for Journalists" drafted by the Press and Publication Administration; to hear the work report of the previous term of the association, to amend the charter of the All-China Journalists Association, and to elect new leaders.

### 全国记协四次代表会六月召开
文璐
1989-04-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　全国记协四次代表会六月召开
    据新华社北京４月１５日电　（记者文璐）已数度推迟的中华全国新闻工作者协会第四届代表大会，订于６月５日至１０日在北京召开，６００多名代表届时将参加会议。
    这次会议的主要任务是：通过《中国新闻工作者职业道德准则》；讨论新闻出版署起草的《新闻法》和《新闻记者暂行条例》等；还将听取上届记协工作报告，修改中国记协章程及换届选举。
