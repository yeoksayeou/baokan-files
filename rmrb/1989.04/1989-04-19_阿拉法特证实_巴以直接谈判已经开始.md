Edition: 1

Arafat Confirms That Direct Talks Between Palestine and Israel Have Begun

	People's Daily, Cairo, April 19 - Correspondent Lin Jiaoming reports: Yesterday in the capital of Tunisia, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Palestine Liberation Organization, Arafat, confirmed that "direct talks" between Palestine and Israel, with the United States as the intermediary, have begun.

	Arafat said, "As the President of the State of Palestine and the Chairman of the Executive Committee of the PLO, I formally confirm that direct dialogue with Israel indeed exists." Arafat said, "This dialogue is conducted through U.S.-Palestine negotiations." The United States has taken on the role of intermediary for the direct talks between the PLO and Israel.

	Arafat said that in the second round of formal talks between the U.S. and Palestine held this March, the U.S. delegation conveyed the "election plan" proposed by Prime Minister Shamir during his visit to the U.S. in April to the PLO. The opinions of the PLO were also conveyed to the Israeli government by the U.S. delegation.

### 阿拉法特证实  巴以直接谈判已经开始
林皎明
1989-04-19
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　阿拉法特证实
　  巴以直接谈判已经开始
    本报开罗４月１９日电　记者林皎明报道：巴勒斯坦解放组织执委会主席阿拉法特昨天在突尼斯首都证实，由美国做中间人的巴以“直接谈判”已经开始。
    阿拉法特说，“作为巴勒斯坦国总统和巴解组织执委会主席，我正式证实，与以色列的直接对话确实存在。”阿拉法特说，“这一对话是通过美巴谈判进行的”。美国已经担当起巴解组织与以色列直接谈判的中间人。
    阿拉法特说，在今年３月举行的美巴第２轮正式会谈中，美国代表团把沙米尔总理４月访美期间提出的“选举方案”转告给巴解组织。巴解组织意见也由美国代表团转给以色列政府。
