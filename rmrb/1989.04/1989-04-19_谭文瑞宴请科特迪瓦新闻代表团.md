Title: Tan Wenrui Hosts Dinner for Ivory Coast Journalists
Author:
Date: 1989-04-19
Edition: 2

	Tan Wenrui, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Journalists Association and Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily, hosted a dinner on April 18 in Beijing to welcome the Ivory Coast journalists delegation led by Abouliang, Editor-in-Chief of the "Aube Hebdo" newspaper. The Ivory Coast journalists delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese Journalists Association.

### 谭文瑞宴请科特迪瓦新闻代表团

1989-04-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　谭文瑞宴请科特迪瓦新闻代表团
    本报讯　中国记协副主席、《人民日报》总编辑谭文瑞于４月１８日晚在北京举行宴会，欢迎以《博爱晨报》总编辑阿布良为团长的科特迪瓦新闻代表团。科特迪瓦新闻代表团是应中国记协邀请前来我国访问的。
