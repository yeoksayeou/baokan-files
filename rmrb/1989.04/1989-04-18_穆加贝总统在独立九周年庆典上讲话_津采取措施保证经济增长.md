Title: Mugabe Speaks at Independence Ninth Anniversary Celebration: Zimbabwe Takes Measures to Ensure Economic Growth
Author: Lǐ Jǐngwèi
Date: 1989-04-18
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Harare, April 18th.

President Mugabe of Zimbabwe said today at the grand celebration of Zimbabwe's ninth independence anniversary that Zimbabwe will take new economic measures to increase employment opportunities and improve the investment environment to ensure sustained economic growth.

Mugabe said the Zimbabwean government has decided to establish an investment center and investment advisory body to create a good investment environment for domestic and foreign investors and to address some issues in the current laws to facilitate investment by domestic and foreign investors.

When talking about past achievements, Mugabe said that in 1988, Zimbabwe made great strides in the political, economic, and social fields, bringing great hope and a bright prospect for 1989. He said that in politics, the merger of the two parties further strengthened national unity.

He said that the economy in 1988, overall, was better than in 1987. Agricultural harvests were good, with farmers selling 11 million tons of grain, setting a historical record. The output value of the manufacturing industry exceeded the 1 billion Zimbabwe dollar mark in 1988, a 20% increase over 1987, making it the best year since independence.

Mugabe hopes that Zimbabwe's economy will see even greater improvements in 1989, with various industries achieving higher output values to meet domestic demand and export needs.

Mugabe said the government will create more job opportunities for the people to address the serious unemployment problem.

### 穆加贝总统在独立九周年庆典上讲话　津采取措施保证经济增长
李景卫
1989-04-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　穆加贝总统在独立九周年庆典上讲话
　　津采取措施保证经济增长
    本报哈拉雷４月１８日电　记者李景卫报道：津巴布韦总统穆加贝今天在盛大的津巴布韦独立九周年庆典上说，津巴布韦将采取新的经济措施，增加就业机会，改善投资环境，以保证经济的持续增长。
    穆加贝说，津巴布韦政府已决定设立投资中心和投资咨询机构，为国内外投资者创造良好的投资环境，并着手解决现行法律上的一些问题，以利于国内外投资者。
    在谈到过去的成就时，穆加贝说，１９８８年津巴布韦在政治、经济及社会的各个领域取得了巨大的成就，并为１９８９年带来了很大的希望和光明的前景。他说，在政治上，两党合并使民族团结进一步得到巩固。
    他说，１９８８年的经济总的来说比１９８７年有所改善。农业收成很好，农民出售１１０万吨粮食，创历史最高纪录。制造业的产值于１９８８年突破１０亿津元大关，比１９８７年增长２０％，是独立以来最好的一年。
    穆加贝希望津巴布韦的经济在１９８９年有更大的起色，各行各业创造出更高的产值，以满足国内需求和出口需要。
    穆加贝说，政府将为人民创造更多的就业机会，以解决严重的失业问题。
