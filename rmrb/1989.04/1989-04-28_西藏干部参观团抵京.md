Title: Tibet Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: Zheng Hongfan
Date: 1989-04-28
Edition: 2

A delegation of Tibetan officials, consisting of 64 members, arrived in Beijing today from Tianjin.

Led by Bu Duoji, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region People's Congress, the delegation departed from Lhasa on April 5th. The delegation members are mostly county-level officials from various counties in the Tibet Autonomous Region, including 49 Tibetan officials, 2 Monba officials, and 1 Naxi official.

This afternoon, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Apuo Awangjinmei, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Minister of the State Ethnic Affairs Commission Sima Yi Aimaidi, and Vice Ministers of the State Ethnic Affairs Commission Wu Jinghua, Zhao Yanniang, Jiang Jiafu, and Zhuojia visited the delegation's residence to greet all members and express their welcome.

### 西藏干部参观团抵京
郑宏范
1989-04-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　西藏干部参观团抵京
    本报北京４月２８日讯  西藏干部参观团一行６４人今天由天津抵达北京。
    以西藏自治区人大常委会副主任布多杰为团长的西藏干部参观团是于本月５日由拉萨出发的，该团成员大多是西藏自治区各县的县委书记、县长，其中，藏族干部４９名，门巴族干部２名，纳西族干部１名。
    今天下午，全国人大常委会副委员长阿沛·阿旺晋美、全国政协副主席、国家民委主任司马义·艾买提、国家民委副主任伍精华、赵延年、江家福、卓加等到参观团驻地看望了全体团员，并对他们的到来表示欢迎。（郑宏范）
