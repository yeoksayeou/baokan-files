Title: Li Peng Meets Pakistani Prime Minister's Special Envoy
Author:
Date: 1989-04-11
Edition: 1

Li Peng Meets Pakistani Prime Minister's Special Envoy

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 10th: Premier Li Peng met with the special envoy of Pakistani Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto, Ikbal Akhund, this morning in the Zhongnanhai Purple Light Pavilion.

Akhund, the special envoy, handed over a letter from Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto to Premier Li Peng and introduced the Pakistani government's views on the situation in Afghanistan.

Li Peng said, China hopes that after the Soviet Union withdraws from Afghanistan, a broad-based united government with various political forces can be established, so that Afghanistan can restore its neutrality and non-aligned status as soon as possible, restore peace, and allow Afghan refugees to return home early.

### 李鹏会见巴基斯坦总理特使

1989-04-11
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　李鹏会见巴基斯坦总理特使
    据新华社北京四月十日电　国务院总理李鹏今天上午在中南海紫光阁会见了巴基斯坦总理贝·布托的特使伊克巴尔·阿洪德。
    阿洪德特使转交了贝·布托总理给李鹏总理的一封信，并且介绍了巴基斯坦政府对阿富汗局势的看法。
    李鹏说，中国希望阿富汗在苏联撤军后能建立一个基础广泛的、有各种政治力量参加的联合政府，使阿富汗尽早恢复中立和不结盟地位、恢复和平，使阿富汗难民早日返回家园。
