Title: Turkish Culture Week Held in Beijing
Author: Liú Zǎoxiáng
Date: 1989-04-26
Edition: 2

The Turkish Culture Week began in Beijing. Our reporter, Liu Zhaoxiang, reports: The Turkish Comprehensive Art Troupe performed on April 24th at the Tianqiao Theater, opening the Turkish Culture Week with their exquisite artistic performance and fashion show. Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Saifuddin Azizi, and his wife, Turkish Ambassador to China, Simşir, and his wife, as well as relevant officials, enjoyed the performance with 2,000 Beijing residents. This is the first time the Turkish Comprehensive Art Troupe has been invited by the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries to hold a cultural week in China.

### 土耳其文化周在京举行
刘兆祥
1989-04-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　土耳其文化周在京举行
    本报北京４月２６日讯  记者刘兆祥报道：土耳其综合艺术团２４日晚在天桥剧场以精湛的艺术表演和时装表演揭开了土耳其文化周的序幕。人大常委会副委员长赛福鼎·艾则孜和夫人、土耳其驻华大使西姆锡尔和夫人及有关方面负责人同首都２０００名观众一起欣赏了表演。这是土耳其综合艺术团应中国对外文化交流协会的邀请，首次在我国举办的文化周活动。
