Title: Love God? Death God
Author:
Date: 1989-04-11
Edition: 6

    The novel "Love God? Death God" is a long-form work by Zhang Rui and Wang Yuliang. Zhang Rui, a young writer from Gansu, previously created "The Story of the Horse Thief." Recently, he collaborated with Wang Yuliang to write the long-form novel "Love God? Death God." The story is set in the vast Yellow Earth Plateau of the northwest, focusing on the lives of Hui people who navigate the Yellow River on rafts. The authors attempt to depict an unforgettable reality of life that continually clashes with history. While painting a tragic hue over the lives of the raft people, they also portray, in a style full of masculine beauty, the intense emotions that are worth cherishing.

### 《爱神？死神》（长篇小说）

1989-04-11
第6版(文艺评论)
专栏：文艺新作

　　《爱神？死神》
　　（长篇小说）
    作者　张锐　汪玉良
　　作家出版社出版
    曾创作过《盗马贼的故事》的甘肃青年作家张锐，最近和汪玉良创作了长篇小说《爱神？死神》。这里仍以西北黄土高原为天地，呈现生活于黄河上回族筏子客的命运。作者试图勾勒出一个不断冲撞着历史的难忘的生活真实，在涂抹筏子客生活的悲剧色彩的同时，也以充满阳刚之美的风格描绘他们值得珍惜的激越感情。　
　　　　　　（辉）
