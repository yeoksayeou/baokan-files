Title: North Korea's Minister of Finance Announces Major Investment in Light Industry
Author: Xú Bǎokāng
Date: 1989-04-08
Edition: 4

North Korea's Minister of Finance announced at the 5th session of the 8th Supreme People's Assembly that North Korea will invest heavily in the development of light industry. Correspondent Xu Baokang reported from Pyongyang on April 7: North Korea's Minister of Finance, Yun Ki-Jin, said at the opening of the 5th session of the 8th Supreme People's Assembly today that this year is North Korea's "Year of Light Industry." The state will invest a large amount of funds in the development of light industry and continuously improve the people's living standards. North Korean party and government leaders Kim Il-sung, Kim Jong-il, and O Jin-u attended the opening ceremony of this session.

### 朝鲜财政部长在议会８届５次会议上说  朝鲜将投巨资发展轻工业
徐宝康
1989-04-08
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　朝鲜财政部长在议会８届５次会议上说
    朝鲜将投巨资发展轻工业
    本报平壤４月７日电  记者徐宝康报道：朝鲜财政部部长尹基贞在今天开幕的朝鲜第８届最高人民会议第５次会议上说，今年是朝鲜的“轻工业年”，国家将把巨额资金投入到发展轻工业上，不断提高人民的生活水平。朝鲜党政领导人金日成、金正日、吴振宇等出席了本次会议的开幕式。
