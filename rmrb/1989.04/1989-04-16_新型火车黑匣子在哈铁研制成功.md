Title: New Type of Train Black Box Successfully Developed by Harbin Railway
Author: Wǔ Péi Zhēn
Date: 1989-04-16
Edition: 2

    Harbin Railway Bureau's 26-year-old female technician, Jiang Lihua, has developed a new type of train "black box" that can alert the driver. This device records audio directly controlled by ground signals, preventing crew members from turning it off, thus ensuring an uninterrupted record of the locomotive's operational sounds.

### 新型火车黑匣子在哈铁研制成功
武培真
1989-04-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　新型火车黑匣子在哈铁研制成功
    本报哈尔滨４月１６日电　记者武培真报道：哈尔滨铁路局２６岁的女技术员姜丽华研制成可向司机报警的火车“黑匣子”。它录音时直接由地面信号控制，乘务人员无法关机，可以不受干扰地为机车作业留下完整的声音依据。
