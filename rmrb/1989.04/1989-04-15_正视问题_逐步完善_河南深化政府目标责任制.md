Title: Confronting Issues and Gradually Improving: Henan Deepens Government Target Responsibility System
Author: Lǐ Érliàng
Date: 1989-04-15
Edition: 2

Recently, seven provincial governors of Henan Province sat together with responsible officials from 17 local areas and 70 provincial-level units to sign their names on this year's target management responsibility contracts.

The implementation of the Henan Provincial Government's target management responsibility system has been in effect for one year, and both upper and lower levels have responded positively.

The provincial government summarized the shortcomings in the implementation process. While signing this year's target management responsibility contracts, it strengthened macro-level guidance and supplemented it with the following specific tasks: First, establish a target coordination and evaluation audit office within the provincial government to be responsible for the formulation and monitoring of targets. Second, strengthen statistical monitoring and enhance statistical methods to form a monitoring network across the province. Third, further strengthen public supervision. Mid-year and year-end evaluations must be strictly conducted, and the execution of target responsibilities should be periodically published to the public through newspapers, radio, and television.

### 正视问题　逐步完善　河南深化政府目标责任制
李而亮
1989-04-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　正视问题　逐步完善
　　河南深化政府目标责任制
    本报郑州电　记者李而亮报道：河南省政府７位省长最近与全省１７个地方、省直７０个单位的负责同志同坐一堂，在今年的目标管理责任书上签下自己的名字。
    河南省政府工作目标管理责任制实行一年，上下反应良好。
    省政府总结实施中的不足，在签订今年目标管理责任制同时，从宏观上加强指导，并辅之以下几项具体工作：一，在省政府建立目标协调和考评审计办公室，具体负责目标的制定和监测考核工作。二，加强统计检测，强化统计手段，在全省范围内形成监测网络。三，进一步加强群众监督。在年中和年终，要严格考核，并通过报纸、广播、电视向广大群众定期公布目标责任执行情况。
