Title: Italy's GDP Growth and Rising Inflation Rate
Author: Luojinbiao
Date: 1989-04-02
Edition: 4

According to a report on the 1988 Italian national economic situation made by Budget Minister Vanvanni at yesterday's cabinet meeting, Italy's GDP grew by 3.9% in 1988, marking the highest growth rate since 1981.

1988 was another "golden year" for the Italian economy, achieving the fifth consecutive year of significant growth. The GDP reached 1078.863 trillion lire (approximately $600 billion).

However, the "worrisome" issue in Italy's 1988 economic situation is that the public fiscal deficit continued to increase, with national debt exceeding 1000 trillion lire. The inflation rate rose to 5%, reversing a seven-year trend of decline to its lowest level since 1969.

### 意大利国内生产总值增长  通货膨胀率又上升
罗晋标
1989-04-02
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　意大利国内生产总值增长  通货膨胀率又上升
    本报罗马４月１日电  记者罗晋标报道：据意大利预算部长范范尼昨天在内阁会议上所作的１９８８年意大利国家经济形势报告指出，去年意大利国内生产总值增长３．９％，为１９８１年来增长最高年份。
    １９８８年是意大利经济的又一个“黄金年”，取得连续第５年较大增长，国内生产总值达１０７８．８６３万亿里拉（约合６０００亿美元）。
    １９８８年意大利经济情况的“令人忧虑”的问题是，公共财政赤字继续增加，国债累计超过１０００万亿里拉；通膨率上升到５％，在连续７年下降至自１９６９年来最低水平之后又出现了回升势头。
