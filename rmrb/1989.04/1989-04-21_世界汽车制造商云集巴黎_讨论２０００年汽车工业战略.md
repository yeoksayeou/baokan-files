Title: Global Automakers Gather in Paris to Discuss 2000 Automotive Industry Strategy
Author: Zhang Qihua
Date: 1989-04-21
Edition: 3

The World Automobile Conference opened yesterday in Paris. Nearly 400 manufacturers from Western Europe, North America, Japan, and other regions, who produce tens of millions of vehicles annually, will spend three days discussing the "strategy for the year 2000."

Developing new automotive technologies is the main topic of this conference. In the exhibition hall, 10 new models of small cars are on display, showcasing significant advancements in safety, fuel efficiency, speed, and comfort for future vehicles. Particularly, the application of electronic devices will be highly emphasized.

Securing a share of the global market is a major concern for all major automakers. Experts predict that by the year 2000, annual automobile production will continue to grow at a rate of 2.6%. Given the application of new technologies and the planned unification of the European market, this conference will address how the automobile market can break through national boundaries and achieve global integration.

### 世界汽车制造商云集巴黎  讨论２０００年汽车工业战略
张启华
1989-04-21
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　世界汽车制造商云集巴黎  讨论２０００年汽车工业战略
    本报巴黎４月１９日电  记者张启华报道：世界汽车会议昨天在巴黎开幕，来自西欧、北美、日本等地的每年生产数千万辆汽车的近４００名制造商，拟用３天时间，就“公元２０００年的战略”问题进行讨论。
    发展汽车工业新技术是这次会议的主要议题。在会议大厅的走廊上展出１０辆新型的小轿车，显示了未来汽车在安全、用油、速度、舒适等方面都将有重大的突破，特别是电子设备的应用将得到高度的重视。
    占领世界市场是各大汽车制造商最关心的问题。专家们预测，到２０００年汽车产量每年仍以２．６％速度向前发展。鉴于新技术的应用和西欧筹划统一市场，这次会议提出了汽车市场如何打破国界、实现市场世界化的问题。
