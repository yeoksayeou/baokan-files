Title: Two Companies in the Aviation Industry Established
Author:
Date: 1989-04-14
Edition: 2

The Ministry of Aeronautics and Astronautics announced today that, with the approval of the State Council, the China Aviation Engine Corporation and the China Aviation Onboard Equipment Corporation were officially established in Beijing.

The establishment of these two specialized corporations is part of the Ministry of Aeronautics and Astronautics' organizational reform. They will respectively engage in the research, production, and domestic and international trade of aviation engines and aircraft electronic, electrical, mechanical, and protective and rescue equipment.

### 航空工业两公司成立

1989-04-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　航空工业两公司成立
    本报北京４月１３日讯　航空航天部今天宣布：经国务院批准，中国航空发动机总公司和中国航空机载设备总公司在北京正式成立。
    组建这两个专业公司，是航空航天部机构改革的一部分。它们分别从事航空发动机和飞机电子、电气、机械、防护救生装置等机载设备的科研、生产和国内外贸易。
