Title: The Healthy Cup China International Billiards Tournament Opens
Author:
Date: 1989-04-19
Edition: 4

The Healthy Cup China International Billiards Tournament will open on April 20th in Beijing. Participants in this competition include top billiards players from China, Taiwan, Australia, New Zealand, Singapore, the Philippines, Thailand, and Hong Kong. All of these players are champions in their respective countries or regions. Among them, Chen Junxiong and Chen Tian from Taiwan have attracted the attention of the media. They were selected through a qualifying tournament in Taiwan.

When asked by a reporter, Chen Junxiong said that although it was their first time on the mainland, they did not feel like they were away from home. Everything in Beijing made them feel very familiar. Currently, there are two sports teams from Taiwan competing in Beijing, and another is the Taipei Youth Gymnastics Team.

This tournament features a high level of competition. The champion will receive a prize of $8,000, and the runner-up will receive $4,000. The competition will be held at the Shijingshan Sports Hall in Beijing.

### 健牌杯中国国际台球大赛开幕

1989-04-19
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　健牌杯中国国际台球大赛开幕
    本报讯　健牌杯中国国际台球大赛４月２０日在北京举行。参加这次比赛的有中国、中国台北、澳大利亚、新西兰、新加坡、菲律宾、泰国和香港的台球高手，他们均是本国或本地区的冠军。其中，来自台湾的陈俊雄、陈田两位选手引起了新闻界的兴趣，他们是经过台湾选拔赛选拔出来的。
    陈俊雄在回答记者提问时说，他们第一次到大陆，但丝毫没有出门在外的感觉，北京的一切都使他们感到十分亲切。目前，有两支来自台湾的运动队在京参赛，还有一支是台北青年体操队。
    本届比赛水平较高，冠军获得者将获得８０００美元奖金、亚军为４０００美元。比赛的场地将在北京石景山体育馆。
