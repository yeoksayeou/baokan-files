Title: The Third French International Gymnastics Championship Decides All-Around Champions
Author: Sūn Xiǎojiàn
Date: 1989-04-16
Edition: 4

The third French International Gymnastics Championship opened today in Paris. In the all-around finals held this evening, Romanian gymnast Da. Shiliwas scored 39.475 points to win the women's all-around title, while Soviet gymnast Va. Mogilni scored 57.650 points to take the men's all-around crown.

East German gymnast A. Wecker and Italian gymnast Bo. Pretti finished second and third with scores of 57.450 and 57.200 points, respectively. Chinese gymnast Wang Chongshan scored 57.150 points to place fourth. Another Chinese gymnast, Fang Min, withdrew from the competition due to injury, forgoing the floor exercise and vault events.

The second and third places in the women's all-around were taken by Soviet gymnast S. Bokinska (39.275 points) and Romanian gymnast K. Bantas (38.525 points). Chinese gymnast Chen Cuiting scored 38.350 points to finish fifth. Another Chinese gymnast, Wang Wenjing, placed 15th with a score of 37.100 points.

The men's and women's individual event finals will be held tomorrow.

Gymnasts from Romania, the Soviet Union, China, Bulgaria, East Germany, France, and 12 other countries participated in this competition.

### 法国体操赛决出全能冠军
孙小健
1989-04-16
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：

　　法国体操赛决出全能冠军
    新华社巴黎４月１４日电　（孙小健）第三届法国国际体操赛今天在这里拉开战幕。在今晚进行的个人全能决赛中，罗马尼亚选手达·希莉瓦斯以３９．４７５分的总成绩获得女子全能冠军，苏联选手瓦·莫吉利尼以５７．６５０分的总成绩夺得男子全能桂冠。
    民主德国选手阿·韦克尔和意大利选手博·普雷蒂分别以５７．４５０分和５７．２００分的总成绩列第二和第三名。中国选手王崇山以５７．１５０分的总成绩名列第四。另一名中国选手方敏因受伤放弃了自由体操和跳马的比赛。
    获女子全能第二和第三名的分别是苏联的斯·博金斯卡娅（３９．２７５分）和罗马尼亚的克·邦塔斯（３８．５２５分）。中国选手陈翠婷以３８．３５０分的总成绩列第５名。另一名中国选手王文静列第１５名，成绩为３７．１００分。
    男、女单项的决赛将在明天进行。
    来自罗马尼亚、苏联、中国、保加利亚、民主德国、法国等１７个国家的选手参加本届角逐。
