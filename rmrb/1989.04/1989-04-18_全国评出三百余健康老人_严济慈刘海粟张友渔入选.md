Title: Over 300 Healthy Elders Selected Nationwide
Author: Liang Jinxiong
Date: 1989-04-18
Edition: 2

The nationwide selection of healthy elders for 1988, with the theme "Healthy and Happy, Respect the Elderly, Love the Elderly," concluded today. A total of 311 elders aged 80 and above were awarded the title of "Healthy Elder."

Among the winners, there are 19 centenarians, with the oldest being 117 years old. Notable figures such as Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Yan Jici (89 years old), art master Liu Haishu (92 years old), and legal scholar Zhang Youyu (90 years old) are among the selected. Except for Tibet, Yunnan, and Hainan provinces, central government agencies and all provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the central government have had elders enthusiastically participate in this activity.

### 全国评出三百余健康老人　严济慈刘海粟张友渔入选
梁金雄
1989-04-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　全国评出三百余健康老人
　　严济慈刘海粟张友渔入选
    据新华社北京４月１８日电　（记者梁金雄）以“康乐长寿、尊老、爱老”为目的的１９８８年全国健康老人评选活动今天结束。全国３１１名８０岁以上的老人荣获“健康老人”称号。
    获奖者中，有百岁以上老人１９名，年龄最长者１１７岁。全国人大常委会副委员长严济慈（８９岁）、艺术大师刘海粟（９２岁）、法学家张友渔（９０岁）榜上有名。除西藏、云南和海南省外，中央机关和各省、自治区、直辖市都有老人踊跃报名参加这次活动。
