Chairman Yang Meets Angolan President's Special Envoy

BEIJING, April 10 (Xinhua) -- Chairman Yang Shangkun met with the special envoy of the President of the People's Republic of Angola and Minister of Justice, Fransa Van Dinen, this morning in the Great Hall of the People. Van Dinen presented two letters from President Dos Santos to Chairman Yang.

Chairman Yang expressed his appreciation for Angola's active contributions in supporting the struggle of the Namibian people and in seeking a just solution to the problem of Southwest Africa. He pointed out that United Nations Resolution 435 has begun implementation as of April 1. China earnestly hopes that the relevant agreements and resolutions will be genuinely implemented to ensure that Namibia holds free and fair elections under the supervision of the United Nations, and achieves independence as scheduled.

### 杨主席会见安哥拉总统特使
孙毅
1989-04-11
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　杨主席会见安哥拉总统特使
    本报北京四月十日讯　记者孙毅报道：国家主席杨尚昆今天上午在人民大会堂会见了安哥拉人民共和国总统特使、司法部长弗兰萨·范迪嫩。范迪嫩特使向杨主席面交了多斯桑托斯总统的两封信件。
    杨主席对安哥拉为支持纳米比亚人民斗争和公正解决西南部非洲地区问题所作的积极贡献表示赞赏。他指出，联合国四三五号决议已经于四月一日开始实施，中国殷切希望有关的协议和决议能够切实得到实现，以确保纳米比亚在联合国的监督下举行自由、公正的选举，如期实现独立。
