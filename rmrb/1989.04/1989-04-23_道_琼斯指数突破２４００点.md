Title: Dow Jones Index Breaks 2400 Points
Author:
Date: 1989-04-23
Edition: 3

	New York's Dow Jones 30 Industrial Average rose by 32.08 points on April 21, reaching 2409.46 points. This is the first time since the stock market crash in October 1987 that this index has surpassed 2400 points.

	From April 17 to April 21, this index increased by a total of 72.40 points compared to the previous week.

	According to Western financial experts, the recent continuous rise in the Dow Jones Industrial Average is due to traders' reduced concerns about U.S. inflation and interest rate hikes, which has increased their interest in purchasing stocks.

### 道·琼斯指数突破２４００点

1989-04-23
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　道·琼斯指数突破２４００点
    新华社北京４月２２日电  纽约消息：纽约股市道·琼斯３０种工业股票平均指数２１日上升３２．０８点，达到２４０９．４６点。这是１９８７年１０月股市暴跌以来这项指数首次突破２４００点。
    在４月１７日到４月２１日的交易周内，这项指数比前一周共计上升７２．４０点。
    据西方金融界专家分析，道·琼斯工业股票指数近一段时间不断上升，是因交易者对美国通货膨胀、利率上升的担忧有所减少，从而增加了购买股票的兴趣。
