Title: To the Night Shift Driller
Author: Yu Xizhong
Date: 1989-04-11
Edition: 8

You always use the drill bit
To drill your dreams
	The dream of rain
	The dream of snowfall
	The dream of wind
	The dream of sweating
	Even the dream of the Mid-Autumn Festival
	Even the dream of New Year's Eve
	All are drilled through
	The underground black dragon
	Is countless nights condensed
	The black dragon flows from your dreams into reality
	Submerging a worldly prejudice
	Submerging a shameful void
	Flowing into an endless power
	So many dreams
	All overlap on your drill bit
	Overlapping into a vast moon
	Overlapping into countless sweet smiles
	You thus reunite with your dreams
	With countless dreams
	With a kind of longing, a kind of hope
	A kind of hazy, sweet, bitter, and spicy happiness
	Reunite

### 给夜班钻工
余系中
1989-04-11
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　给夜班钻工
　　余系中
    你总是用钻杆
    钻你的梦
    　　下雨的梦
    　　落雪的梦
    　　刮风的梦
    　　淌汗的梦
    甚至八月十五的梦
    甚至腊月三十的梦
    都被你钻穿
    那地底的乌龙
    是数不清的黑夜浓缩成的
    乌龙从你的梦中流进现实
    淹没一种世俗的偏见
    淹没一种耻辱的空白
    流成一种无止尽的力量
    好多好多的梦
    都在你的钻杆上重叠
    重叠成偌大无比的月亮
    重叠成无数甜甜的笑脸
    你就这样与你的梦团圆
    与无数的梦团圆
    与一种奇冀一种希望
    一种朦朦胧胧酸甜苦辣的幸福
    　团圆
