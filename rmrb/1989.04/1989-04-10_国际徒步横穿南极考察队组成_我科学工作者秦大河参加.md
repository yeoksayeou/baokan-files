Title: International Team to Trek Across Antarctica Includes Chinese Scientist Qin Daher
Author: Chen Huiming
Date: 1989-04-10
Edition: 1

An international team to trek across Antarctica has been formed, with Chinese scientist Qin Daher participating.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Lan Zhou, April 9th (Reporter Chen Huiming): Sent by the National Antarctic Expedition Committee, Qin Daher, a deputy researcher at the Lanzhou Institute of Glaciology and Permafrost Research under the Chinese Academy of Sciences, will join the international team to trek across Antarctica for scientific exploration.

This Antarctic trek is jointly initiated by the United States and France. The team includes representatives from the five permanent members of the United Nations Security Council—China, the United States, the Soviet Union, the United Kingdom, and France—as well as one Japanese participant. The team plans to traverse Antarctica from July 15, 1989, to March 1, 1990, over a period of seven and a half months. The expedition will conduct scientific research on changes in the Antarctic ozone layer and inland ice sheets. Qin Daher will be responsible for the ice sheet research component.

### 国际徒步横穿南极考察队组成  我科学工作者秦大河参加
陈惠明
1989-04-10
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　国际徒步横穿南极考察队组成
    我科学工作者秦大河参加
    据新华社兰州４月９日电  （记者陈惠明）受国家南极考察委员会派遣，中国科学院兰州冰川冻土研究所副研究员秦大河，将参加国际徒步横穿南极考察队，前往南极进行科学探险考察。
    这次横穿南极考察活动是由美国和法国联合发起的。参加考察队的有中、美、苏、英、法五个联合国安理会常任理事国派出的人员和一位日本人。考察队计划从１９８９年７月１５日至１９９０年３月１日，用７个半月的时间穿越南极。考察队将对南极臭氧层变化、南极内陆冰川进行科学研究。秦大河将承担冰川研究这一课题。
