Title: Qiao Shi Takes on Additional Role as Head of the Central Party School
Author:
Date: 1989-04-12
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 11th.

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China has decided that Comrade Qiao Shi will concurrently serve as the President of the Central Party School.

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China has agreed to accept Comrade Gao Yang's resignation from the position of President of the Central Party School.

### 乔石兼任中央党校校长

1989-04-12
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　乔石兼任中央党校校长
