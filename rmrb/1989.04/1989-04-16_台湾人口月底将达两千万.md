Title: Taiwan's Population to Reach 20 Million by End of Month
Author:
Date: 1989-04-16
Edition: 2

Taiwan's population is expected to reach 20 million by the end of this month. According to data from the "Family Planning Research Institute" of Taiwan Province, the total population of the Taiwan region at the end of December 1988 was 19,903,812. Based on the average rate of increase, Taiwan's population grows by approximately 20,000 people per month. Economists in Taiwan believe that the maximum population capacity of Taiwan should not exceed 30 million. In recent years, relevant authorities have vigorously promoted family planning, and the rate of population increase has gradually slowed.

### 台湾人口月底将达两千万

1989-04-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　台湾人口月底将达两千万
    新华社北京４月１６日电　台湾省人口月底将达２０００万。台湾省“家庭计划研究所”的资料显示，台湾地区总人口数在１９８８年１２月底时，已达１９９０３８１２人，按平均数推估，台湾每月人口增加约２万人。台湾经济学家认为，台湾最高人口容量应不过３０００万人。近年有关方面大力推行家庭计划，人口增加率已逐渐减缓。
