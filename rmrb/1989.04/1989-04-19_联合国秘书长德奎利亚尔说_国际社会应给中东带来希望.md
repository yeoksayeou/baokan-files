Title: UN Secretary-General Perez de Cuellar Says International Community Should Bring Hope to the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1989-04-19
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Luxembourg, April 18th.

UN Secretary-General Perez de Cuellar said today that the international community, especially the five permanent members of the UN Security Council, should bring a "message of hope" to the Middle Eastern countries to achieve peace in the region as soon as possible.

Perez de Cuellar made these remarks to the press after meeting with Luxembourg Prime Minister Jacques Santer to participate in activities commemorating the 150th anniversary of Luxembourg's independence.

In Luxembourg, Perez de Cuellar also met with leaders from the governments of Britain, the Soviet Union, and the Netherlands, discussing the situation in Lebanon and the Arab-Israeli conflict.

Perez de Cuellar said he and the leaders of Britain, the Soviet Union, and the Netherlands are deeply concerned about the situation in Lebanon and the occupied Arab territories. He said that the international community's attention to the future of the Palestinian people will help stabilize the situation in the region. When talking about the situation in Lebanon, he emphasized the importance of "maintaining the unity and independence of the country."

### 联合国秘书长德奎利亚尔说　国际社会应给中东带来希望

1989-04-19
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　联合国秘书长德奎利亚尔说
　　国际社会应给中东带来希望
    新华社卢森堡４月１８日电　联合国秘书长德奎利亚尔今天说，国际社会，特别是联合国安理会的５个常任理事国应该给中东国家带来“希望的信息”，以尽快在该地区实现和平。
    德奎利亚尔是在参加卢森堡大公国独立１５０周年的纪念活动时同卢森堡首相桑特会谈后向新闻界发表这番讲话的。
    德奎利亚尔在卢森堡还会见了英国、苏联及荷兰政府领导人，同他们讨论了黎巴嫩问题和阿以冲突。
    德奎利亚尔说，他和英、苏、荷领导人都深切关注黎巴嫩和阿拉伯被占领土上的局势。他说，国际社会关注巴勒斯坦人民的前途有助于稳定该地区的局势。他在谈到黎巴嫩局势时强调，重要的是“维护国家的统一和独立”。
