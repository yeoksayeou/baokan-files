Title: The Battle for Tarim Oil Commences
Author: Shēn Zūnjìng Yú Fēng Zēng Kūn
Date: 1989-04-12
Edition: 1

The battle for Tarim oil has officially begun. The Tarim Oil Exploration and Development Command Center was established today, marking the start of a strategically significant oil campaign.

Vice President of the China National Petroleum Corporation, Zhou Yongkang, will lead the command center. The initial forces participating in the campaign, including the Xinjiang Petroleum Administration Bureau, Sichuan Petroleum Bureau, North China Petroleum Bureau, Central China Petroleum Exploration Bureau, Daqing Petroleum Bureau, Liaohai Petroleum Exploration Bureau, Geophysical Exploration Bureau, Dagang Petroleum Bureau, Longyuan Company, and the China National Petroleum Corporation's Material Company and Equipment Department, will soon gather here. Thousands of elite personnel have already arrived or are on their way.

Experts estimate that the Tarim oil and gas reserves amount to approximately 30 billion tons.

It is reported that the Tarim Basin oil campaign will rely on China's own strengths while also introducing the latest foreign technologies, equipment, and management systems as much as possible.

To ensure the smooth progress of this campaign, various prefectures in Xinjiang have enthusiastically volunteered to serve as "logistics commanders." The Bayingolin Mongol Autonomous Prefecture, Ili, Aksu, Changji, Hami, and Turpan have established supporting oil organizations. Autonomous region party and government officials Wang Enmao, Song Hanliang, and Tiemuer Dawayiti have expressed their full support for the Tarim Basin oil and gas development campaign at today's command center establishment meeting.

### 塔里木石油会战拉开序幕　勘探开发指挥部已成立　上万精兵强将进入阵地
申尊敬　于峰　曾坤
1989-04-12
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　塔里木石油会战拉开序幕
　　勘探开发指挥部已成立
　　上万精兵强将进入阵地
    本报库尔勒４月１０日电　经国务院批准，塔里木石油勘探开发指挥部今天在这里宣告成立，一场具有战略意义的石油会战正式拉开序幕。
    中国石油天然气总公司副总经理周永康担任指挥部指挥。首批参加大会战的新疆石油管理局、四川石油局、华北石油局、中原石油勘探局、大庆石油局、辽河石油勘探局、物探局、大港石油局、长运公司、中国石油天然气总公司物资公司和装备部等成千上万精兵强将已经或将要会集这里。
    据专家测算，塔里木油气资源为３００亿吨左右。
    据介绍，塔里木盆地石油会战立足于依靠我国自己的力量，同时尽可能引进采用国外最新技术装备和新的管理体制。
    为了确保此次会战的顺利进行，新疆各地州踊跃争当“后勤部长”，巴音郭楞蒙古自治州、伊犁、阿克苏、昌吉、哈密、吐鲁番等地州已成立了支油机构。自治区党政负责同志王恩茂、宋汉良、铁木尔·达瓦买提等，在今天指挥部成立大会上表示，要全力支持塔里木盆地油气开发会战。
　　　　　　　　　（申尊敬　于峰　曾坤）
