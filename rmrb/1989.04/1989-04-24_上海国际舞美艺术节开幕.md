Title: The Shanghai International Stage Art Festival Opens
Author:
Date: 1989-04-24
Edition: 4

The Shanghai International Stage Art Festival opened on April 22 at the Shanghai Art Museum. This is the first time China has hosted an international event focused on stage art.

Invited to participate in the festival are 85 renowned stage art designers from the United States, Britain, France, Japan, Canada, Sweden, Finland, the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Yugoslavia, the Netherlands, and the Hong Kong region, as well as some well-known figures from China's drama and stage art circles.

The festival will last for one week and is jointly organized by the Shanghai Stage Art Association, the Shanghai Cultural Bureau, and the Shanghai Writers' Association.

### 上海国际舞美艺术节开幕

1989-04-24
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　上海国际舞美艺术节开幕
    上海国际舞美艺术节４月２２日在上海美术馆开幕。这是我国首次举办舞台美术国际活动。
    应邀参加艺术节的有美、英、法、日、加拿大、瑞典、芬兰、苏联、捷克、南斯拉夫、荷兰和香港地区的８５名世界著名舞美艺术家，以及国内戏剧界、舞美界一些知名人士。
    本次艺术节为期一周，由上海市舞美协会、上海文化局、上海市文联等单位联合举办。　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
