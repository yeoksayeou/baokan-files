Title: Tbilisi Lifts Curfew and Establishes Command to Maintain Social Order
Author:
Date: 1989-04-18
Edition: 3

The Supreme Soviet of the Georgian Soviet Socialist Republic decided today that the curfew in the Georgian capital of Tbilisi will be lifted starting from April 18.

According to a report by TASS, given that classes have resumed in various universities in Tbilisi and the number of students attending middle schools has significantly increased, work in enterprises, government agencies, public transportation, and service industries is returning to normal. The Georgian Republic Party Central Committee's standing committee held a meeting on April 17 and proposed that the republic's Supreme Soviet issue an order to lift the curfew. At the same time, the standing committee decided to establish a command in Tbilisi to promote the maintenance of social order, in order to assist in maintaining order after the curfew is lifted.

Tbilisi began implementing the curfew after the bloody incident that occurred on the morning of April 9.

### 第比利斯取消宵禁　成立促进维持社会秩序指挥部

1989-04-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　第比利斯取消宵禁
　　成立促进维持社会秩序指挥部
    新华社莫斯科４月１７日电　苏联格鲁吉亚共和国最高苏维埃主席团今天决定，从４月１８日起，格鲁吉亚首都第比利斯将取消宵禁。
    塔斯社的报道说，鉴于第比利斯各大学已经复课，中学生的上课人数明显增加。企业、机关、公共交通及服务行业工作正常。格鲁吉亚共和国党中央常委１７日会议，建议该共和国最高苏维埃主席团发布命令，取消宵禁。与此同时，常委会议决定，在第比利斯市成立促进维持社会秩序指挥部，以便在宵禁取消后协助维持秩序。
    格鲁吉亚首都是在４月９日凌晨发生流血事件后开始实行宵禁的。
