Title: 200,000 Kilometers of Highway Greening Achieves Standard
Author: Dù Mǎichí
Date: 1989-04-25
Edition: 2

	200,000 kilometers of China's main highways have reached the national greening standards, accounting for 81% of the total main highways. In just one year, 22.93 million trees, 4.81 million shrubs, 1.77 million square meters of grass, and 530,000 flowers were planted along the sides of national highways, with an average survival rate of 85%.

### ２０万公里公路绿化达标
杜迈驰
1989-04-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　２０万公里公路绿化达标
    本报讯  我国干线公路已有２０万公里达到部颁绿化标准，占干线公路的８１％。仅去年一年，全国公路两侧就新植乔木２２９３万株，灌木４８１万株，种草１７７万平方米，种花５３万株，平均成活率达到８５％。　（杜迈驰）
