Title: The Overseas Edition of the People's Daily to Hold a Photography Contest
Author:
Date: 1989-04-13
Edition: 4

The overseas edition of the People's Daily will hold a news photography competition starting from May 1st, lasting for three months. This competition, titled "China Window," requires that the submitted works must be news photographs with certain news value, true and reliable content, and a concise and clear photo description.

This competition is jointly organized by the overseas edition of the People's Daily and the China Yuhua Glass Company.

### 本报海外版将办摄影赛

1989-04-13
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　本报海外版将办摄影赛
    本报讯　人民日报海外版将从5月1日起，举行一次为期3个月的新闻摄影比赛。这次题为“中国之窗”的摄影比赛，要求参赛作品必须是新闻照片，有一定的新闻价值，内容真实可信，并配有简明扼要的照片说明。
    这次比赛由本报海外版和中国耀华玻璃公司联合举办。
