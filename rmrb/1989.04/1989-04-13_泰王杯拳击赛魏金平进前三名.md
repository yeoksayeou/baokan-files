Title: Wei Jinping Enters Top Three in the King's Cup Boxing Tournament
Author: Zhang Qiulai
Date: 1989-04-13
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, April 11th.

Chinese boxer Wei Jinping, participating in the 15th King's Cup Boxing Tournament, defeated Nepalese boxer Su Baikale in the 81-kilogram weight class tonight in Bangkok, securing a spot in the top three of this weight class.

Wei Jinping maintained an advantage in all three rounds against Baikale, displaying a more aggressive offensive strategy, and ultimately won with a score of 5:0.

In tonight's 48-kilogram weight class match, China's Zheng Xiangui lost to Thai boxer Ni Xiaoba.

The tournament began on April 9th.

### 泰王杯拳击赛魏金平进前三名
张秋来
1989-04-13
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　泰王杯拳击赛魏金平进前三名
    新华社曼谷4月11日电　（记者张秋来）参加第十五届泰王杯拳击赛的中国队选手魏金平，今晚在这里举行的81公斤级的比赛中战胜尼泊尔选手苏·比卡雷尔，进入这个级别的前三名。
    魏金平在同比卡雷尔的3个回合的比赛中都占有优势，攻势较猛，终以5∶0获胜。
    在今晚举行的48公斤级的比赛中，中国队的郑先贵负于泰国选手尼·绍巴拉。
    这次比赛是9日开始的。
