Title: The First Trans-Pacific Undersea Fiber-Optic Cable Opens
Author: Liu Kai Chen
Date: 1989-04-20
Edition: 3

The first trans-Pacific undersea fiber-optic communication cable has recently been completed and began fiber-optic communication today between the United States and Japan. This new fiber-optic cable, connected with the trans-Atlantic fiber-optic cable that began operation last December, has formed an initial fiber-optic communication network between North America, Europe, and Asia.

### 首条横贯太平洋海底光纤电缆开通
刘开宸
1989-04-20
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　首条横贯太平洋海底光纤电缆开通
    本报纽约４月１８日电  记者刘开宸报道：第一条横贯太平洋海底的光纤通讯电缆最近建成，今天在美国和日本之间开始光纤通讯。这条新的光纤电缆与去年１２月开始使用的横贯大西洋的光纤电缆联在一起，形成了北美、欧洲和亚洲间光纤通讯的初步网络。
