Title: Renowned Writer Li Yingru Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-04-04
Edition: 2

Renowned writer Li Yingru passed away.
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 3rd: Li Yingru, famous for his long novel "Wild Fire, Spring Breeze, Struggle in the Ancient City," passed away on March 6th due to illness. A farewell ceremony was held at his home today.

This veteran writer, who dedicated his life to the military, was born in 1913 in Qingyuan County, Hebei Province. In 1936, he joined the revolution and developed an inseparable bond with literature. Throughout his life, he wrote more than a dozen long novels. Even on his deathbed, he completed his magnum opus, "Souls Broken in Qin Prison." Before his passing, Li Yingru had served as an advisor to the August First Film Studio, among other roles.

### 著名作家李英儒逝世

1989-04-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　著名作家李英儒逝世
    新华社北京４月３日电  以创作长篇小说《野火春风斗古城》闻名于世的著名作家李英儒，３月６日因病诀别人世。今天在其家中举行了遗体告别仪式。
    这位一生报效军队的老作家１９１３年出生于河北省清苑县，１９３６年投身革命，并与文学结下不解之缘。他一生创作了十几部长篇小说，临终前还在病榻上完成了长篇巨著《魂断秦监》。李英儒生前曾任八一电影制片厂顾问等职。
