Title: Zhao Ziyang Meets with General Students of the National Defense University
Author: Xú Jīngyuè, Jǐng Shūzhǎn
Date: 1989-04-08
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 7th. (Reporter Xu Jingyue, Correspondent Jing Shuzhan) CPC Central Committee General Secretary and Vice Chairman of the Central Military Commission Zhao Ziyang today met with all the students of the third session of the National Defense University's National Defense Research Department.

Thirty students, each wearing a golden-yellow military rank insignia, became the first batch of general students to graduate from the National Defense University. They mainly studied and researched major issues related to military strategy and military reform and construction to improve the theoretical level and macro-decision-making capabilities of high-level military leaders.

During the study period, Vice Chairman of the Central Military Commission Yang Shangkun and leaders from the Military Commission and the General Staff Department visited the school several times to discuss military construction with the students. Yang Shangkun exchanged extensive views with the students on military strategy, ideological and political work, military organization structure, education and training, weapons and equipment, national defense education, legal system construction, and production and operation. Liu Huaqing, Yang Baibing, and Zhao Nanqi separately introduced to the students the military's weapons and equipment, troop situation, and military expenditure. Vice Premier Wu Xueqian introduced the international situation and China's foreign policy. The students submitted consultation reports to the Military Commission on the issues they researched.

Hong Xuezhi, Qin Jiwei, Chi Haotian, and Yang Baibing attended the graduation ceremony.

### 赵紫阳会见国防大学将军学员
徐京跃　景抒展
1989-04-08
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　赵紫阳会见国防大学将军学员
    新华社北京４月７日电  （记者徐京跃通讯员景抒展）中共中央总书记、中央军委副主席赵紫阳今天在国防大学会见了国防研究系第３期全体学员。
    ３０名佩戴金黄色军衔标志的学员，今天成为国防大学结业的首批将军学员。他们主要学习、研究军事战略和军队改革、建设中的一些重大问题，以提高军队高层领导干部的理论水平和宏观决策能力。
    学习期间，中央军委副主席杨尚昆和军委、总部的领导同志多次来校，与学员共商军队建设大计。杨尚昆就军事战略、思想政治工作、军队编制体制、教育训练、武器装备、全民国防教育、法制建设、生产经营等问题曾与学员们广泛交换意见。刘华清、杨白冰、赵南起分别向学员们介绍了军队的武器装备、部队形势、军费等情况。国务院副总理吴学谦介绍了国际形势和我国的对外政策。学员们就所研究的问题向军委提交了咨询报告。
    洪学智、秦基伟、迟浩田、杨白冰参加了结业典礼。
