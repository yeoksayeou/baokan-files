Title: At the Request of Samaranch, IOC Member Wu Jingguo Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-04-19
Edition: 4

At the request of International Olympic Committee (IOC) President Juan Antonio Samaranch, IOC Executive Committee member He Zhenliang and member Wu Jingguo, as his representatives, attended the Asian Youth Gymnastics Championships to express their warm congratulations. On the evening of April 19th, Mr. Wu Jingguo and his wife arrived in Beijing from Taipei via Hong Kong. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Vice Secretary-General of the Chinese Olympic Committee Tu Mingde, Committee members Chen Bin, Wan Boyi, and Li Guobin, among others.

### 受萨马兰奇委托　国际奥委会委员台北吴经国飞抵北京

1989-04-19
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　受萨马兰奇委托
　　国际奥委会委员台北吴经国飞抵北京
    本报北京４月１９日讯　国际奥委会主席萨马兰奇委托国际奥委会执行委员何振梁和委员吴经国作为他的代表，参加亚洲青年体操锦标赛，以表示热情祝贺。１９日晚，吴经国先生和夫人从中国台北经香港飞抵北京。到机场迎接的有中国奥委会副秘书长屠铭德、中国奥委会委员陈彬和万伯翱、李国彬等。
