Title: Xiantao Promotes "Optimal Fund Turnover Method"
Author: Bào Hòuchéng Liú Jǐngǎng
Date: 1989-04-23
Edition: 2

     Xiantao Promotes "Optimal Fund Turnover Method"

     The People's Daily reports: Facing the situation of enterprise capital shortages, Hubei Province's Xiantao City vigorously promotes the "Optimal Fund Turnover Method" pioneered by Guohe Town, which has alleviated the capital shortage dilemma.

     The main contents of the "Optimal Fund Turnover Method" are as follows:

     First, using the advanced level of similar enterprises, the highest level in the factory's history, and the factory's average level over the past three years as the basis, calculate the optimal turnover days and optimal total amount of working capital. Second, allocate the total optimal turnover days and optimal total amount to various indicators of fixed and non-fixed working capital, and then implement a working capital management responsibility system. For excess use, the unit using the excess amount will pay a 12% monthly interest rate, which will be deducted from the salaries of cadres and the masses; conversely, the same interest rate will be used to increase the salaries and bonuses of cadres and staff. Third, establish a set of internal buying and selling, paid use, and compensation for idle time, transforming the material exchanges between workshops, departments, and workshops into exchanges using factory internal circulation vouchers.

     In 1989, Xiantao City will fully implement the optimal fund turnover method, which can save the city 57 million yuan in working capital.

### 仙桃推广“资金最优运转法”
鲍厚成　刘景岗
1989-04-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　仙桃推广“资金最优运转法”
    本报讯  面临企业资金紧缺的形势，湖北仙桃市大力推广郭河镇首创的“资金最优运转法”，缓解了资金紧缺的矛盾。
    “资金最优运转法”的主要内容：
    一是以同类企业先进水平、本厂历史上最高水平和本厂近３年来的平均水平为依据，计算出流动资金的最优运转天数和最优占用总额。二是将总的最优运转天数和最优占用总额以最优的比例分解到定额流动资金和非定额流动资金各个指标项目并归并落实到各个核算车间和科室，然后实行流动资金管理责任制，超额占用奖赔制即由超用单位按１２％的利率支付月息，从干部群众的工资中扣除；反之则按同等利率计息增加干部职工的工资和奖金。三是建立与之配套的内部买卖，有偿占用、误工索赔等制度，把车间与车间，科室与科室，车间与科室之间的实物交往变成凭厂内流通券交往。
    １９８９年仙桃市全面推行资金最优运转法可使全市节约流动资金５７００万元。　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　（鲍厚成　刘景岗）
