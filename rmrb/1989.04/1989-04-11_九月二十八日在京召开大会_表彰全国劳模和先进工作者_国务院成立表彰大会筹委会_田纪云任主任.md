Title: National Labor Model and Advanced Worker Award Ceremony to be Held in Beijing on September 28
Author: Li Niangui
Date: 1989-04-11
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 10th

The preparatory committee for the National Labor Model and Advanced Worker Award Ceremony held its first meeting on April 8th. It was decided that the ceremony will be held on September 28th in Beijing. Vice Premier of the State Council, Tian Jiyun, in his speech, hoped that all ethnic groups in the country would work diligently and strive to make new contributions in the rectification of the economic environment, the rectification of the economic order, and the comprehensive deepening of reforms. He urged everyone to welcome the ceremony with practical actions.

The Political Bureau Standing Committee of the Communist Party of China has held two meetings to discuss this work.

The first meeting of the preparatory committee decided that the ceremony will honor 3,000 national labor models and advanced workers. The scope of the awards includes workers, farmers, professional and technical personnel, managerial staff, and other workers in industries such as industry, agriculture, transportation, finance and trade, basic construction, science and technology, culture, education, health, sports, and public security.

The selection criteria are that national labor models and advanced workers must love their country, adhere to the four cardinal principles, support the reform and opening-up policy, and make significant contributions in their respective positions. Tian Jiyun emphasized that the selection must strictly adhere to the standards and must not lower the criteria.

The State Council has officially established the preparatory committee for the award ceremony, which is responsible for the overall leadership of the various tasks of the award ceremony and, on behalf of the State Council, is responsible for approving the national labor models and advanced workers. Tian Jiyun is the chairman of the preparatory committee, with deputy chairmen including Ni Zhifu, Luo Gan, Zhao Dongwen, He Kang, He Dongchang, Yuan Chongwu, and Xu Ruixin. The committee members include responsible comrades from relevant departments of the State Council, the Propaganda Department, the Communist Youth League, the All-China Women's Federation, and Beijing Municipality.

### 九月二十八日在京召开大会　表彰全国劳模和先进工作者　国务院成立表彰大会筹委会　田纪云任主任
李年贵
1989-04-11
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　九月二十八日在京召开大会
　　表彰全国劳模和先进工作者
　  国务院成立表彰大会筹委会　田纪云任主任
    据新华社北京４月１０日电　（记者李年贵）全国劳动模范和先进工作者表彰大会筹备委员会８日召开第一次会议，决定表彰大会于９月２８日在北京举行。国务院副总理田纪云在讲话中希望全国各族人民勤奋工作，努力奋斗，在治理经济环境、整顿经济秩序、全面深化改革中作出新的贡献，以实际行动迎接表彰大会的召开。
    为召开这次表彰大会，中共中央政治局常委会已两次召开会议研究这项工作。
    筹备委员会第一次会议决定，这次大会拟表彰全国劳模和先进工作者３０００人。表彰的范围包括工业、农业、交通、财贸、基本建设、科研、文化、教育、卫生、体育、政法等战线的工人、农民、专业技术人员、管理人员及其他工作人员。
    评选条件是，全国劳模和先进工作者必须热爱祖国、坚持四项基本原则、拥护改革开放总方针，并在各自的工作岗位上做出了重大贡献。田纪云同志强调评选要严格掌握标准，决不能降低条件。
    国务院已正式成立表彰大会筹备委员会，负责全面领导表彰大会的各项工作，并受国务院委托负责审批全国劳动模范和先进工作者。田纪云任筹备委员会主任，副主任有倪志福、罗干、赵东宛、何康、何东昌、阮崇武、徐瑞新，筹委会委员有国务院有关部门及中宣部、团中央、全国妇联和北京市的负责同志。
