Title: Prohibit the Arbitrary Use of Medical Technology to Determine Fetal Sex
Author: Gao Yingming
Date: 1989-04-12
Edition: 6

I was previously a doctor and am now a judge, paying particular attention to legal issues related to medicine. Recently, relevant departments investigated the sex ratio of newborns in a certain county over the past few years, revealing that the number of male newborns in 1986, 1987, and 1988 were 4488, 5778, and 6874, respectively; while the number of female newborns were 4252, 5379, and 6115, respectively. The male-to-female ratio over these three years was 105.6:100, 107.4:100, and 112.4:100.

The primary cause of the imbalance in the sex ratio is the persistence of the feudal ideology of preferring sons over daughters and the belief that "having no son means the end of the family line." Many pregnant women seek medical technology to determine the sex of the fetus, especially those who have already given birth to two children. If the fetus is female, they opt for an abortion; if male, they find ways to ensure the birth. Some people go through connections and backdoors, while some doctors, disregarding medical ethics, comply with these requests.

This phenomenon should be given serious attention by relevant departments, and measures should be taken to prohibit the arbitrary use of modern medical technology for fetal sex determination.

### 禁止随意用医疗技术鉴定胎儿性别
高英明
1989-04-12
第6版(记者来信)
专栏：

　　禁止随意用医疗技术鉴定胎儿性别
    我过去是医生，现在是法官，对涉及医学方面的法律问题尤为关注。最近，有关部门对某县近几年新生儿性别问题调查表明：1986年、1987年、1988年新生男婴分别为4488名，5778名，6874名；新生女婴分别为4252名，5379名，6115名。3年男女性比例分别是105.6∶100，107.4∶100，112.4∶100。
    造成男女性比例失调的重要原因是，重男轻女，“无儿便是断香火”的封建思想占据了一些人的头脑。许多孕妇求助于医疗技术鉴别胎儿性别，特别是两胎以上的生育者，若是女孩就做人工流产，若是男孩便想方设法生下来。有些人为此托关系，走后门，有些医生置医疗道德于不顾，有求必应。
    这一现象应引起有关部门重视，并采取措施，禁止随意用现代医疗技术作胎儿性别鉴定。
                    湖南常德市鼎城区人民法院  高英明
