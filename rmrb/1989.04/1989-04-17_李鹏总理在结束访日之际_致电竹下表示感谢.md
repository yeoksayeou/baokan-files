Title: Premier Li Peng Sends a Telegram to Takeshita Expressing Gratitude Upon Concluding His Visit to Japan
Author:
Date: 1989-04-17
Edition: 3

Premier Li Peng sent a telegram to Japanese Prime Minister Takeshita upon concluding his visit to Japan and departing from the country this afternoon. In the telegram, he expressed his heartfelt gratitude for the warm hospitality extended to him and his delegation during his visit.

Premier Li Peng's telegram stated: "As I am about to conclude this visit and leave your country, I would like to once again express my sincere gratitude to Your Excellency and the Japanese government for the warm hospitality extended to me and my delegation."

The telegram continued: "My visit has been very successful and fruitful. I had the honor of meeting with His Majesty the Emperor and engaging in sincere and friendly talks with Your Excellency. I also had the opportunity to meet with various sectors of Japanese society, which further deepened mutual understanding and friendship. The friendly sentiments of the Japanese people and the achievements of your country's economic development have left a lasting impression on me. I am full of confidence in the future of Sino-Japanese relations. I believe that this visit will help promote the healthy and smooth development of Sino-Japanese relations and push cooperation in various fields between the two countries to a new level."

The telegram concluded: "I wish your country prosperity and the people happiness. I wish Your Excellency good health and success in your work."

### 李鹏总理在结束访日之际　致电竹下表示感谢

1989-04-17
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　李鹏总理在结束访日之际
　　致电竹下表示感谢
    新华社福冈４月１６日电　李鹏总理今天下午在离开日本回国之际，打电报给日本首相竹下登，对他在访问期间受到的盛情款待表示由衷的谢意。
    李鹏总理的电报说：“在即将结束这次访问离开贵国之际，我再次衷心感谢阁下和日本政府对我和我们一行的盛情款待。”
    电报说：“我的访问进行得很顺利，获得圆满的成功。我有幸会见了天皇陛下，同阁下进行了诚挚友好的会谈，还同贵国朝野各界人士广泛接触，进一步增进了相互了解和友谊。日本人民的友好情谊和贵国经济建设的成就给我留下了难忘印象。对于中日关系的前景，我是充满信心的。我相信，这次访问将有助于中日关系继续健康、顺畅地向前发展，推动两国在各个领域里的互利合作迈向新的水平。”
    电报最后说：“祝贵国繁荣昌盛，人民幸福。祝阁下身体健康，工作顺利。”
