Title: Solemnly Mourn the Outstanding Leader of Our Party, Comrade Hu Yaobang
Author:
Date: 1989-04-21
Edition: 4

Solemnly Mourn the Outstanding Leader of Our Party, Comrade Hu Yaobang

	Comrade Hu Yaobang's photograph taken shortly before his passing at home.
		— Photographed on April 6, 1989

	During the War of Resistance Against Japan, Hu Yaobang (right) and his cousin Yang Yong in Shaanxi.

	During the War of Resistance Against Japan, Hu Yaobang in Yan'an.

	In September 1985, Hu Yaobang conversed with the masses in a field during an inspection tour in the mountainous area of Longnan, Gansu.

	On May 25, 1957, Hu Yaobang accompanied Mao Zedong, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Dong Biwu, and other party and state leaders to meet with representatives attending the Third National Congress of the Communist Youth League of China.

	On November 30, 1982, Hu Yaobang and Deng Xiaoping conversed during the Fifth Session of the Fifth National People's Congress.

	On November 6, 1982, Hu Yaobang inspected the San Du Port in Ningde, Fujian, aboard a submarine.

	On October 20, 1984, Hu Yaobang, Zhao Ziyang, Li Xiannian, and Chen Yun had a friendly conversation in the rest room during the Seventh Plenary Session of the Twelfth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

	On September 19, 1982, Hu Yaobang inspected the vanadium extraction workshop of the Panzhihua Steel Company in Dukuo City, Sichuan.

	In June 1986, Hu Yaobang visited the UK and interacted with the locals in the hometown of the famous playwright Shakespeare.

	On July 20, 1982, Hu Yaobang addressed the graduates of the Central Party School. Hu Yaobang had served as the deputy director of the Central Party School.

	On October 19, 1984, Hu Yaobang spoke at a symposium convened by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China with representatives from democratic parties, non-partisan figures, and prominent individuals from various circles, seeking their opinions on the "Decision of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on Economic System Reform."

	On October 13, 1981, Hu Yaobang had a cordial conversation with the renowned writer Ba Jin in the Qinzheng Hall of Zhongnanhai in Beijing. In the middle is the famous writer Zhang Guangnian.

	On September 20, 1982, during an investigation in Xide County, Liangshan Yi Autonomous Prefecture, Sichuan, local residents presented Hu Yaobang with a set of wooden tableware.

	On November 18, 1982, Hu Yaobang invited the Chinese women's volleyball team, who had won the world championship, to Zhongnanhai and had a discussion with them.

### 沉痛悼念我党卓越领导人胡耀邦同志（图片）

1989-04-21
第4版(图片专页)
专栏：

　　沉痛悼念我党卓越领导人胡耀邦同志
    胡耀邦同志病逝前不久在家中的留影。
    ——摄于1989年4月6日
    抗日战争时期，胡耀邦（右）和表哥杨勇在陕北。
    抗日战争时期，胡耀邦在延安。
    1985年9月，胡耀邦在甘肃陇南山区视察时在地头与群众交谈。
    1957年5月25日，胡耀邦陪同毛泽东、周恩来、陈云、董必武等党和国家领导人接见出席中国新民主主义青年团第三次全国代表大会的代表。
    1982年11月30日，胡耀邦、邓小平在五届人大五次会议上交谈。
    1982年11月6日，胡耀邦乘猎潜艇视察福建宁德三都港。
    1984年10月20日，胡耀邦、赵紫阳、李先念、陈云在中国共产党十二届七中全会休息室里亲切交谈。
    1982年9月19日，胡耀邦在四川省渡口市攀枝花钢铁公司提钒车间视察。
    1986年6月，胡耀邦在英国访问时同世界著名剧作家莎士比亚故乡的群众在一起。
    1982年7月20日，胡耀邦向中央党校毕业班学员讲话。胡耀邦曾任中央党校副校长。
    1984年10月19日，胡耀邦在中共中央召集的民主党派、无党派和各界知名人士座谈会上讲话，就《中共中央关于经济体制改革的决定》征求意见。
    1981年10月13日，胡耀邦在北京中南海勤政殿同著名作家巴金亲切交谈。中为著名作家张光年。    （本版照片为新华社发）
    一九八二年九月二十日，胡耀邦在四川省凉山彝族自治州喜德县调查时，当地百姓向他赠送一套木制餐具。
    1982年11月18日，胡耀邦邀请夺得世界冠军的中国女排队员到中南海作客，并同她们一起座谈。
