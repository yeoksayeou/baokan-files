Title: Arafat Elected President of Palestine
Author: Lin Jiaoming
Date: 1989-04-03
Edition: 1

People's Daily, Cairo, April 2nd - Correspondent Lin Jiaoming reports: The Central Committee of the Palestine Liberation Organization unanimously elected Yasser Arafat as the President of the State of Palestine today.

The meeting of the PLO Central Committee began on the evening of April 1st and concluded in the early hours of April 2nd. Arafat, the Chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, and the official spokesman for the PLO, Abu Sherif, formally announced the election results at a press conference in Tunis. At the same time, the PLO Central Committee appointed Farouk Kaddoumi, the head of the PLO Political Department, as the Foreign Minister of the State of Palestine.

### 阿拉法特任巴勒斯坦国总统
林皎明
1989-04-03
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　阿拉法特任巴勒斯坦国总统
    本报开罗４月２日电  记者林皎明报道：巴勒斯坦解放组织中央委员会今天在突尼斯一致选举亚西尔·阿拉法特为巴勒斯坦国总统。
    巴解组织中央委员会会议从４月１日晚开始，直到４月２日凌晨结束。巴解组织执委会主席阿拉法特的新闻顾问、巴解组织正式发言人阿布·谢里夫今天在突尼斯的记者招待会上正式公布了这一选举结果。与此同时，巴解组织中央委员会任命巴解政治部主任卡杜米为巴勒斯坦国外交部长。
