Title: Office Work
Author: Fang Cheng
Date: 1989-04-02
Edition: 8

From the March 14th issue of the *Zhuhai Special Zone Daily*, Fang Cheng's illustration.

The illustration depicts a scene of office work, capturing the essence of daily routines in a typical office setting. The artwork is a reflection of the time, showcasing the professional environment and the diligence of office workers. The detailed drawing highlights the meticulous nature of office tasks, emphasizing the importance of organization and efficiency in the workplace. The illustration serves as a snapshot of the professional life in the late 1980s, providing a glimpse into the work culture of the era.
