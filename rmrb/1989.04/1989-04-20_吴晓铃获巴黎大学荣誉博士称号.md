Title: Wu Xiaoling Awarded Honorary Doctorate by the University of Paris
Author:
Date: 1989-04-20
Edition: 4

	Chinese Academy of Social Sciences researcher and opera history expert Wu Xiaoling has been appointed as a visiting professor at the University of Toronto in Canada. He has opened two courses at the university: "Chinese Classical Novels" for undergraduates and "_The Plum in the Golden Vase_ Research" for graduate students.

	Recently, he was also awarded an honorary doctorate by the University of Paris. This honor was bestowed upon seven internationally renowned scholars: three from the United States, one from Australia, one from Denmark, and one from Algeria. Wu is the only scholar in the humanities to receive this honor and is the first Chinese scholar to do so.

### 吴晓铃获巴黎大学荣誉博士称号

1989-04-20
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　吴晓铃获巴黎大学荣誉博士称号
    本报讯  中国社会科学院研究员、戏曲史专家吴晓铃被加拿大多伦多大学聘为客座教授，在该校开设了本科学生的“中国古典小说”和研究生院的“《金瓶梅》研究”两课。
    最近，他又获得了巴黎大学授予的荣誉博士称号。获此殊荣的国际著名学者共有７人：美国３人，澳洲１人，丹麦１人，阿尔及利亚１人；文科学者仅吴氏一人，亦为我国学者获此荣誉之首位。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据《戏剧电影报》）
