Title: Foolish Words of Life
Author: Jīnmǎ
Date: 1989-04-04
Edition: 8

It seems that to prevent humans from becoming overly arrogant and forgetting that they are members of the biological world, nature has made the developmental stages of the human embryo so varied that they almost replicate the entire history of biological evolution.

Each person is a microcosm of the universe, a model of civilizational evolution. Although people have different appearances and personalities, they all contain the same history, store the same evolutionary code, inherit the same ancestral cultural legacy, and possess the same regional "genes" of civilization.

Life cannot be all joy and no sorrow, all wonder and no fear, all praise and no anger, all contempt and no shame, all delight and no pain. It is the full spectrum of emotional mechanisms and reserves that allows people to smoothly face the frequent onslaughts of joy, anger, sorrow, and delight in the world, and to digest the unpredictable ups and downs of life. Perhaps it can be said that only a life full of great joy, great sorrow, great worry, and great fear shines with a brilliant hue, becoming a "pet" of civilizational evolution.

A life rich in philosophical flavor is often tragic.

Great tragedy embodies great simplicity. Only great conspiracies require great complexity and great disguise. Great joy must necessarily contain great sorrow, because it must bid farewell to the old soul's city of existence and create a new spiritual universe where the new soul can flourish.

### 人生痴语
金马
1989-04-04
第8版(副刊)
专栏：大地漫笔

　　人生痴语
    大约是唯恐人类妄自尊大，忘记了自己是生物界的一个成员，故尔在人类胚胎的发育历程中，其形态之多变，几乎是生物进化史的重演。
    每一个人都是一个大千世界的缩影，一个文明进化的模型。人们虽然形象各异，个性不同，但却都蕴含着同一部历史，贮存着同一部进化的密码，承接着同一祖宗文化的遗产，领有同一地域的文明“基因”。
    人生不能只有快乐没有忧愁，只有惊奇没有恐惧，只有赞许没有愤怒，只有轻蔑没有羞怯，只有欢畅没有痛苦。正是全色调的情绪机制与情绪储备，使人可以顺利地迎接世间频繁袭来的喜怒哀乐，消化难以预料的悲欢离合。或许可以说，惟充满大喜大悲大忧大惧的人生，常辉耀着令人瞩目的强烈色调，成为文明进化的“宠物”。
    哲味浓的人生多悲壮。
    大悲壮寓着大单纯。只有大阴谋需要大复杂，需要大妆扮。大欢喜必然寓着大悲恸，因为它要告别故魂曾赖以生存的城池，再造一个足以令新魂畅发的精神宇宙。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　金马
