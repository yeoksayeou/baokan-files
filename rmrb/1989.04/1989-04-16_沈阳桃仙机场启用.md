Title: Shenyang Taoxian Airport Begins Operation
Author: Zhou Baohua
Date: 1989-04-16
Edition: 2

Today, after passing national inspection, Shenyang Taoxian Airport officially began operations. The airport covers an area of 291 hectares and is equipped with advanced domestic and international communication and navigation facilities. It can accommodate all types of large civilian aircraft currently in service around the world.

### 沈阳桃仙机场启用
周保华
1989-04-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　沈阳桃仙机场启用
    新华社沈阳４月１６日电　（记者周保华）今天沈阳桃仙机场经国家验收后正式投入使用。该机场占地面积２９１公顷，并安装了国内、国际先进的通讯导航设备，可以起降目前世界上已有的各种大型民航客机。
