Title: Egyptian Armed Forces Commander Replaced
Author: Lin Jiaoming
Date: 1989-04-16
Edition: 3

People's Daily, Cairo, April 16th - Reporter Lin Jiaoming reports: Egypt's second most powerful figure, Abdel Halim Abu Ghazala, was dismissed from his post as Minister of Defense yesterday by President Hosni Mubarak and appointed as the President's Assistant.

Mubarak appointed Cairo Governor Field Marshal Taher to return to the military, granting him the rank of Lieutenant General, and appointed him as Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces, concurrently serving as Minister of Defense and Military Production. Locals are surprised by the significant cabinet reshuffle and have various speculations.

### 埃武装部队总司令易人
林皎明
1989-04-16
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　埃武装部队总司令易人
    本报开罗４月１６日电　记者林皎明报道：埃及第２号实力人物加扎拉昨天被总统穆巴拉克免去国防部长职务，改任共和国总统助理。
    穆巴拉克总统调开罗省省长塔利布重归军界，授予四星上将军衔，任命他出任武装部队总司令，兼国防和军工生产部部长。此间人士对内阁重要调整感到突然，并有不同猜测。
