Title: U.S. Releases Plan to Cut Military Budget
Author: Cang Lide
Date: 1989-04-26
Edition: 3

    The U.S. releases plan to cut military budget
    Cheney says it is a fundamental shift in U.S. defense direction

    Our reporter Cang Lide reports from Washington, April 25: U.S. Secretary of Defense Richard Cheney today submitted a comprehensive plan to the House Armed Services Committee to cut $100 billion from the military budget for the 1990 fiscal year. He said that this reduction is "very, very painful" and represents a "fundamental shift in the direction of U.S. defense."

    To reduce the Reagan administration's requested $305.6 billion military budget for the 1990 fiscal year to $295.6 billion, Cheney proposed cutting 17,200 personnel from the Army, Navy, and Air Force, as well as reducing weapons and equipment, and decreasing funding for the "Star Wars" program. Specific plans include:

    Army – Disband one mechanized brigade of the 4th Infantry Division, reduce 8,000 personnel from a force of 772,000; halt the development of the OH-58 type new helicopter, cease production of the AH-64 type attack helicopter by the end of 1991, and cancel the development plan for the M88 type armored recovery vehicle.

    Navy – Retire the "Coral Sea" aircraft carrier; place 4 frigates in reserve; retire 33 old destroyers and 73 P-3 type anti-submarine aircraft, reduce personnel by 6,000.

    Air Force – Delay the development of the B-2 "Stealth" bomber by one year; stop the production of the F-15E type fighter jets planned for purchase in 1991; cancel the development plan for the F-14D type "Tomcat" fighter jet; transfer some aircraft to the National Aeronautics and Space Administration; retire all WC-130 type aircraft, reduce personnel by 3,200.

    Strategic Defense Initiative – Reduce the requested $5.6 billion budget to $4.6 billion, focusing on the development of the orbital interception satellite system.

    Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff General Colin Powell expressed "100% support" for Cheney's military budget cut plan but warned that continuous reduction in military spending would jeopardize U.S. defense capabilities. Cheney presented the above plan at the House Armed Services Committee hearing.

### 美国削减军事预算计划出台  切尼称是美防务方向的根本转变
仓立德
1989-04-26
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美国削减军事预算计划出台    
    切尼称是美防务方向的根本转变
    本报华盛顿４月２５日电  记者仓立德报道：美国国防部长理查德·切尼今天向国会众院军委会提出一项在１９９０年度削减１００亿美元军事预算的全面计划。他说，这一削减是“非常、非常痛苦的”，是美国防务“方向上的一个根本转变”。
    为了将里根政府为１９９０财政年度所要求的３０５６亿美元军事预算缩减为２９５６亿美元，切尼提出削减陆海空三军兵员１．７２万名及武器装备，并减少“星球大战”的研制经费。具体计划包括：
    陆军——撤销第４步兵师的一个机械化旅，从７７．２万人的部队中裁员８０００人；停止ＯＨ５８型新式直升机的研制，在１９９１年末前中止ＡＨ６４型攻击性直升机的生产，取消Ｍ８８型装甲拖车的发展计划。
    海军——“珊瑚海”号航空母舰退出现役；４艘护卫舰退入预备役；３３艘老式驱逐舰、７３架Ｐ３型反潜飞机退役，裁减兵员６０００名。
    空军——延缓１年发展Ｂ２“隐形”轰炸机；停止生产将于１９９１年购买的Ｆ１５Ｅ型战斗机；取消Ｆ１４Ｄ型“雄猫”式战斗机的发展计划；部分飞机转给国家航空航天局；ＷＣ１３０型飞机全部退役，削减兵员３２００名。
    战略防御计划——将里根政府要求的５６亿美元预算减至４６亿美元，集中力量研制地球轨道截击卫星系统。
    参谋长联席会议主席克劳将军表示“百分之百”支持切尼的削减军费方案，但警告说，军费不断下降会危及美国的防务能力。切尼是在众院军委会听证会上提出上述计划的。
