Title: Retractable Roof for Sports Stadium
Author: Yīng Cháo
Date: 1989-04-15
Edition: 4

A new sports center has been constructed in Toronto, Canada. It is a multifunctional sports facility where one can admire the nearby skyscrapers and the blue sky through the reflective glass walls that are 15 meters high. The most remarkable feature is the sports arena's retractable dome roof, a global first. The spherical roof is divided into four sections, with a square steel frame, covered with corrugated steel plates and high-strength composite plastic woven material. The arena has 56,000 seats and can be used for football, baseball, and other sports events. On sunny days, the dome automatically opens, and in case of rain, snow, or strong winds, it closes. Each opening or closing operation takes only 20 minutes.

The nearly circular playing field is covered with artificial turf. The new sports center was built to welcome the upcoming international baseball tournament. It is the masterpiece of architect Rodrick Robie and structural expert Michael Allen.

### 球场屋顶可开合
应朝
1989-04-15
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：五光十色

　　球场屋顶可开合
    加拿大的多伦多建造了一座崭新的体育中心。它是一组多功能的体育建筑群，人们可以从外墙高达１５米的反射玻璃中欣赏到邻近鳞次栉比的玉宇琼楼和蓝天行云。最绝的是体育场采用了全球首创的可以开合式穹顶屋面。圆球形的屋顶分成四片，以方形钢管为骨架，覆盖波形钢板和高强复合塑料编织物。体育场设５６０００位观众座席，可供足球、棒球等比赛用。每遇艳阳天，穹顶自动开启，碰到雨雪大风则可闭合。每次开合只需２０分钟。
    在近似圆形的竞技场地上，铺满了人工合成的绿茵细草。新体育中心是为迎接今年的国际棒球大赛而兴建的，它是建筑师罗德里克·罗比与结构专家迈克尔·艾伦的杰作。　　　　　
　　　　　　应朝
