Title: Brief News
Author:
Date: 1989-04-25
Edition: 3

A delegation of the China Democratic League, led by Fei Xiaotong, concluded a nine-day visit to Poland on April 23. The delegation was invited to attend the 14th National Congress of the Polish Democratic Party and held a friendly meeting with the newly elected chairman of the party's central committee, Jędrzejewicz, exchanging views on further strengthening friendly cooperation between the two parties.

Fei Xiaotong was also received by the first secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and chairman of the Council of State, Wojciech Jaruzelski.

The Chinese ambassador to Pakistan, Tian Ding, on behalf of the Chinese Red Cross, donated medicines worth $80,000 to Afghan refugees living in Pakistan. At the donation ceremony held in Islamabad on April 24, Tian Ding handed over the medicines directly to Ahmad Shidani, the chief representative of the Afghan Refugee Agency.

### 简讯

1989-04-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　简讯
    ●以费孝通为首的中国民盟代表团一行６人，２３日结束在波兰为期９天的访问。中国民盟代表团应邀参加了波兰民主党第１４次代表大会，并同民主党新选出的中央主席尤季维亚克举行了友好会晤，就进一步加强两党友好合作问题交换了意见。
    费孝通还受到波兰统一工人党中央第一书记、波兰国务委员会主席雅鲁泽尔斯基的接见。
    ●中国驻巴基斯坦大使田丁代表中国红十字会，把价值８万美元的药品捐赠给居住在巴基斯坦的阿富汗难民。田丁２４日在伊斯兰堡举行的捐赠仪式上，把这批药品直接交给了阿富汗难民署首席专员艾哈迈德·希丁。　　（据新华社）
