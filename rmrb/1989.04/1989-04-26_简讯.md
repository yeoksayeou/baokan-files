Title: Brief News
Author:
Date: 1989-04-26
Edition: 2

	▲ Vice Chairman of the State, Honorary Chairman of the China International Friendship Liaison Association Wang Zhen met with the couple Mr. and Mrs. Chen Yongcai, Chairman of the Philippine Alliance Bank, at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse on April 25.

	▲ Lieutenant General Xu Huizhi, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army, met with Lieutenant General Di Martino, Chief of the Italian Army General Staff, and his delegation at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse on the afternoon of April 25.

	▲ More than 100 guests from the Spanish autonomous region of Catalonia, led by the regional government president Jordi Pujol, arrived in Beijing by plane on the morning of April 25 for a five-day friendly visit to China.

	▲ Vice Premier Song Jian met with the entire delegation of the Soviet State Standards Committee, led by First Deputy Chairman Sokolov, at the Great Hall of the People on the afternoon of April 25. The two sides discussed issues related to technical supervision in both countries.

	▲ Yan Mingfu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, met with a delegation of four members from the Christian Democratic Party of Chile, led by Sergio Pizarro, a member of the National Committee, in Beijing on April 25. The delegation, invited by the International Liaison Department, arrived in Beijing on April 21.

### 简讯

1989-04-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　简讯
    ▲国家副主席、中国国际友好联络会名誉会长王震２５日在钓鱼台国宾馆会见了菲律宾联盟银行董事长陈永栽夫妇一行。
    ▲中国人民解放军副总参谋长徐惠滋中将２５日下午在钓鱼台国宾馆会见了意大利陆军参谋长迪·马蒂诺中将一行。
    ▲来自西班牙卡塔罗尼亚自治区的１００多名客人，在该区政府主席霍尔迪·贝沃莱的率领下，于２５日上午乘飞机抵达北京，对中国进行为期５天的友好访问。
    ▲国务委员宋健２５日下午在人民大会堂会见了以苏联国家标准委员会第一副主席索科洛夫为团长的苏联标准委员会代表团全体成员，宾主就两国技术监督方面的问题进行了交谈。
    ▲中共中央书记处书记阎明复２５日在北京会见了以全国委员会委员塞尔希奥·派斯为团长的智利基督教民主党代表团一行４人。代表团应中联部邀请访华，于２１日抵京。 　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
