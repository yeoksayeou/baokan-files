Title: Lamenting Comrade Yao Bang
Author: Zhang Aiping
Date: 1989-04-21
Edition: 2

	Upon hearing the grim news, my throat choked up,
	Memories flooded my mind.
	As youths, we fought side by side on the battlefield,
	Together we traversed the Long March.
	Shouldering heavy responsibilities,
	For the country's sake, he planned,
	Chronicling the seasons.
	His deeds are remembered,
	Bringing blessings to the people,
	Illuminating the nation.

### 诉衷情  痛悼耀邦同志
张爱萍
1989-04-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　诉衷情
    痛悼耀邦同志
    张爱萍
    突闻噩耗梗咽喉，
    往事涌心头。
    少年战场携手，
    长征路同游。
    肩重任，
    为国谋，
    谱春秋。
    感君勋业，
    造福人民，
    光耀神州。
    　一九八九年四月十六日
