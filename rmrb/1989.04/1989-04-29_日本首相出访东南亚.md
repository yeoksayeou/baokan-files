Title: Japanese Prime Minister Visits Southeast Asia
Author: Yu Qing
Date: 1989-04-29
Edition: 3

Japanese Prime Minister Visits Southeast Asia

People's Daily, Tokyo, April 29th - Correspondent Yu Qing reports: Japanese Prime Minister Takeshita today boarded a special plane at Tokyo's Haneda Airport in the afternoon, heading to five Southeast Asian countries for a visit. This marks the first time a prime minister has embarked on a foreign trip after announcing his resignation.

During this visit, Takeshita will tour Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia, and the Philippines over a span of nine days. During his visit, Takeshita will hold talks with the leaders of the countries he visits, focusing on economic cooperation in the Asia-Pacific region, yen loans, environmental protection funds, and other economic issues. Political topics such as the peaceful resolution of the Cambodian issue will also be discussed. Additionally, Takeshita is scheduled to deliver a speech in Jakarta, the capital of Indonesia, on Japan's Southeast Asia policy.

During Takeshita's visit, the Foreign Minister will visit the Soviet Union and Mongolia, while the Finance Minister will visit China.

### 日本首相出访东南亚
于青
1989-04-29
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　日本首相出访东南亚
    本报东京４月２９日电  记者于青报道：日本首相竹下今天下午在东京羽田机场登上专机，前往东南亚五个国家访问，踏上了首相在任期间最后一次出访的旅程。日本首相示意辞职后进行出访尚属首次。
    竹下首相这次出访的五个国家先后是：泰国、马来西亚、新加坡、印度尼西亚、菲律宾，历时９天。访问期间，竹下将同访问国的首脑举行会谈，主要围绕亚太地区经济合作、日元贷款、环境保护资金等经济问题，以及和平解决柬埔寨问题等政治话题交换意见。还准备在印度尼西亚首都雅加达以日本的东南亚政策为题发表演说。
    竹下出访期间，外相将访问苏联、蒙古。藏相将访问中国。
