Title: National People's Congress Establishes Two Special Committees
Author:
Date: 1989-04-21
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 21st.

The National People's Congress Standing Committee's Law Committee and the Women and Children's Special Committee and the Youth Special Committee were established in Beijing today. Their main tasks are to participate in the formulation and review of laws and regulations related to women, children, and youth; to assist the National People's Congress in supervising the enforcement of laws; and to conduct investigations and research on major issues within the scope of their work, and to provide opinions and suggestions. National People's Congress Standing Committee members Hu Dexiu and Liu Yandong will serve as the chairpersons of these two committees, respectively.

### 全国人大成立两个专门小组

1989-04-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　全国人大成立两个专门小组
    新华社北京４月２１日电  全国人大内务司法委员会妇女儿童专门小组和青少年专门小组今天在北京成立，主要任务是：参与制订和审议有关妇女儿童和青少年方面的法律、法规；协助人大做好执法监督；就小组工作范围内的一些重大问题进行调查研究，提出意见和建议。全国人大常委会委员胡德华、刘延东分别担任这两个小组的组长。
