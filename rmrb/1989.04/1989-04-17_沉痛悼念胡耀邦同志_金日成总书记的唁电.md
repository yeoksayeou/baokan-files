Title: Solemnly Mourning Comrade Hu Yaobang
Author:
Date: 1989-04-17
Edition: 3

	China Communist Party Central Committee General Secretary Zhao Ziyang received a telegram of condolence from Kim Il-sung, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, on April 16 regarding the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang.

	The telegram from Kim Il-sung reads:

	"Upon learning of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, I, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, the Korean people, and myself, extend our deepest condolences to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Chinese people, and the family of Comrade Hu Yaobang.

	"Comrade Hu Yaobang participated in the Chinese people's revolution in his early years and made tremendous contributions to the Party and the people of China. He was a loyal communist fighter.

	"Comrade Hu Yaobang made great contributions to strengthening and developing the great friendship and combat unity between the peoples of China and Korea.

	"Although Comrade Hu Yaobang has passed away, his achievements will shine forever."

### 沉痛悼念胡耀邦同志　金日成总书记的唁电

1989-04-17
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　沉痛悼念胡耀邦同志
　　金日成总书记的唁电
    新华社北京４月１７日电　中国共产党中央委员会总书记赵紫阳１６日收到朝鲜劳动党中央委员会总书记金日成就胡耀邦同志逝世发来的唁电。
    金日成总书记的唁电说：
    “获悉中国共产党中央委员会政治局委员胡耀邦同志不幸逝世，我谨以朝鲜劳动党中央委员会、朝鲜人民和我个人的名义，通过你，向中国共产党中央委员会和中国人民以及胡耀邦同志的家属，表示深切的哀悼。
    “胡耀邦同志早年参加中国人民革命，为中国党和人民建树了巨大的业绩，他是一位忠诚的共产主义战士。
    “胡耀邦同志为加强和发展朝中两国人民之间的伟大友谊和战斗团结做出了巨大的贡献。
    “胡耀邦同志虽然逝世了，但他建树的业绩将永放光芒。”
