Title: Zhao Ziyang to Visit North Korea in Late April at the Invitation of Kim Il-sung
Author:
Date: 1989-04-08
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 7th.

A spokesperson for the International Liaison Department announced today, "At the invitation of Kim Il-sung, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, Zhao Ziyang, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, will make an official friendly visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in late April 1989."

### 应金日成邀请　赵紫阳四月下旬出访朝鲜

1989-04-08
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　应金日成邀请
  　赵紫阳四月下旬出访朝鲜
    新华社北京４月７日电  中联部发言人今天宣布，“应朝鲜劳动党中央委员会总书记金日成同志的邀请，中国共产党中央委员会总书记赵紫阳同志将于１９８９年４月下旬对朝鲜民主主义人民共和国进行正式友好访问。”
