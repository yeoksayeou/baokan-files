Title: Beijing Military and Civilians Pay Respects at the Grave of Martyr Li Dazhao
Author: He Hua
Date: 1989-04-02
Edition: 2

	Beijing Military and Civilians Pay Respects at the Grave of Martyr Li Dazhao

	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 1st: Today, representatives from the Ministry of Civil Affairs, the Beijing Municipal Government, the Beijing Garrison, and over 600 young pioneers visited the Wanan Public Cemetery in Xishan to pay their respects to Li Dazhao, the pioneer of the communist movement and the great Marxist. This year marks the 100th anniversary of the birth of Martyr Li Dazhao. Representatives from the Ministry of Civil Affairs and the Beijing Municipal Government, as well as relatives of Martyr Li Dazhao, presented wreaths.

### 北京军民祭扫李大钊烈士墓
何华
1989-04-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　北京军民祭扫李大钊烈士墓
    本报北京四月一日讯  今天，民政部、北京市府、首都驻军以及少先队员六百多人前往香山万安公墓，凭吊共产主义运动先驱、伟大的马克思主义者李大钊。今年是李大钊烈士诞生一百周年。民政部和北京市府负责人、李大钊烈士的亲属献了花圈。　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　（何华）
