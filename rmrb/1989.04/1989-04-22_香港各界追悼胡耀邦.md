Title: Hong Kong Communities Mourn Hu Yaobang
Author:
Date: 1989-04-22
Edition: 2

The New China News Agency Hong Kong Branch held a solemn memorial service today to mourn Hu Yaobang. Over 4,000 people from various sectors of Hong Kong, personnel from mainland institutions in Hong Kong, and representatives from foreign institutions in Hong Kong attended.

Amidst the mournful music, mourners stood solemnly in front of Hu Yaobang's portrait, bowing and observing a moment of silence. The walls around the memorial hall and the exterior of the agency's building were adorned with wreaths made of fresh flowers. A total of 400 wreaths and flower baskets were sent by people from various sectors.

Today, a steady stream of people came to the New China News Agency Hong Kong Branch to participate in the mourning activities. Some members of the National People's Congress and the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference from various provinces and cities, members of the Basic Law Drafting Committee, members of the Basic Law Consultative Committee, representatives from the Sino-British Joint Liaison Group and the Sino-British Land Commission, personnel from mainland institutions in Hong Kong, and people from various sectors of Hong Kong attended the ceremony. Wang Hanbin, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, led a delegation of mainland members of the Basic Law Drafting Committee to pay their respects. Political advisor to the Hong Kong government, Kau Yiu-fu, represented the Hong Kong government in paying his respects.

The Director of the New China News Agency Hong Kong Branch, Xu Jiatun, and Deputy Directors Zheng Hua, Mao Junnian, Zhang Junsheng, and She Mengxiao, along with heads of various departments, took turns standing guard at the memorial hall.

### 香港各界追悼胡耀邦

1989-04-22
第2版(要闻)
专栏：

　　香港各界追悼胡耀邦
    本报香港４月２２日电  新华社香港分社今天举行隆重的追悼仪式，沉痛悼念胡耀邦同志。香港各界人士、内地驻港机构人员和外国驻港机构代表共４０００多人参加。
    哀乐声中，吊唁者在胡耀邦遗像前肃立，鞠躬默哀。灵堂四周和分社大厦的周围摆满鲜花做成的花圈。各界人士敬送的花圈、花篮达４００个。
    今天，前往新华社香港分社参加悼念活动的人群络绎不绝。部分在香港的全国和各省市人大代表、政协委员、香港基本法起草委员会委员、基本法咨询委员会委员、中英联合联络小组和中英土地委员会的中方代表，内地驻港机构人员及香港各界人士参加了仪式。正在香港的王汉斌副委员长率部分基本法内地起草委员前往吊唁。香港政府政治顾问寇立夫代表港府前往吊唁。
    新华社香港分社社长许家屯和副社长郑华、毛钧年、张浚生、佘孟孝及各部门负责人轮流在灵堂守灵。
