Title: Statue of Wang Jie Unveiled in Jin County
Author: Yan Yi
Date: 1989-04-18
Edition: 2

On April 5, a granite full-body statue of martyr Wang Jie was unveiled in his hometown, Jin County, Shandong Province. Wang Jie was a soldier of the People's Liberation Army. On July 14, 1965, while on a mission in Zhanglou Township, Pizhou County, Jiangsu Province, he used his body to shield 12 local militiamen and armed cadres from an accidental explosion of dynamite, sacrificing his life heroically.

The statue stands 7 meters high, with Wang Jie's figure measuring 3.8 meters, carved from white granite.

### 王杰塑像在金乡落成
阎毅
1989-04-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　王杰塑像在金乡落成
    本报讯　４月５日，王杰烈士的花岗岩全身雕像在他的家乡——山东省金乡县落成。王杰是人民解放军战士。１９６５年７月１４日，他在江苏邳县张楼乡执行任务，在炸药意外爆炸的瞬间，用身体掩护了在场的１２名民兵和武装干部，英勇献身。
    这座塑像高７米，其中王杰身像高３．８米，用白色花岗岩雕成。
　　　　（阎毅）
