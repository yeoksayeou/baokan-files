	The Burden of Population

	The top image: Located in the impoverished mountainous region of Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region's Sanjiang Dong Autonomous County, Tongle Township is densely populated, economically backward, and the burden of making a living falls too early on these children.

		Photographer: Wang Chengxuan, Xinhua News Agency

	The bottom image: In the suburbs of Urumqi, Xinjiang, on Yamaliq Mountain, Du Rongshun and his wife from Sichuan have given birth to 9 children, living in dire poverty. There are currently over 1 million such "black children" in China who were born outside of the family planning policy and are not registered.

		Photographer: Xialihale, Xinhua News Agency

### 人口的困扰（图片）

1989-04-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　人口的困扰
    上图：地处贫困山区的广西三江侗族自治县同乐乡，人口众多，经济落后，经商谋生的担子过早地压在这些孩子的身上。　　　　　
　　　　新华社记者　王呈选摄
    下图：在新疆乌鲁木齐市郊雅马里克山上，来自四川的杜荣顺夫妇已生了9胎，生活困苦不堪。像这样计划外生育而未上户口的“黑孩子”，中国目前有100多万。
                        新华社记者　夏力哈尔摄
