Title: The 4th Asian Youth Gymnastics Championships Begin Today
Author: Miú Lǔ
Date: 1989-04-20
Edition: 4

The 4th Asian Youth Gymnastics Championships begin today
Host warmly welcomes friends from all sides

	BEIJING, April 20 (Xinhua) -- A grand reception was held in Beijing tonight by the State Sports Commission to welcome athletes, coaches, and sports officials participating in the 4th Asian Youth Gymnastics Championships.

	Seven teams are participating in this event: North Korea, Iraq, Iran, Japan, South Korea, Chinese Taipei, and China. The competition will be held from April 21 to 23 in Beijing. All participating teams have arrived and started their pre-competition training. This championship will feature the rhythmic gymnastics event for the first time.

	At the reception, Secretary-General Kuchira Yoshiharu of the Asian Gymnastics Union expressed his gratitude to the Chinese Gymnastics Association for hosting the event. He said that due to the support of the hosts, Asian gymnastics teams have a valuable opportunity to warm up before the Asian Games next year. He hopes that the participating athletes will show their skills and contribute to raising the level of gymnastics in Asia.

	Before the reception, Wu Shaozu, Minister of the State Sports Commission, and He Zhenliang, President of the Chinese Olympic Committee, met with the team leaders and coaches of the seven teams. The representative of the International Olympic Committee Chairman Juan Antonio Samaranch, Vice Chairman Wu Jingguo of the Chinese Taipei Olympic Committee, also attended the event.

### 亚洲青年体操锦标赛今日开始  东道主热情欢迎各方朋友
缪鲁
1989-04-20
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　亚洲青年体操锦标赛今日开始    
    东道主热情欢迎各方朋友
    本报北京４月２０日讯  记者缪鲁报道：今晚，国家体委在京举行大型招待会，欢迎前来参加第四届亚洲青年体操锦标赛的运动员、教练员和体育官员。
    参加这届赛事的共有七支代表队，它们是朝鲜、伊拉克、伊朗、日本、南朝鲜、中国台北和中国队，比赛将于２１日至２３日在北京举行。各参赛队均已于昨天之前到达，并先后开始了赛前训练。这届比赛将首次列入艺术体操项目。
    在招待会上，亚洲体操协会秘书长古口良治向主办此次比赛的中国体操协会表示感谢。他说，由于主办者的支持，亚洲各体操劲旅得以在明年亚运会前有了一次宝贵的热身机会，他希望参赛选手大显身手，为提高亚洲体操运动水平作出自己的贡献。
    国家体委主任伍绍祖、中国奥委会主席何振梁等在招待会前会见了七支代表队的团长和领队。国际奥委会主席萨马兰奇的代表、中国台北奥委会副主席吴经国也参加了今晚的这一活动。
