Title: Japanese Emperor Meets Premier Li Peng
Author: Huí Yuán Yán
Date: 1989-04-14
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, April 13th - Japanese Emperor Akihito and Empress Michiko this morning received Premier Li Peng and his wife, Zhu Lin, at the Imperial Palace.

The hosts and guests had a friendly conversation. The Emperor expressed a warm welcome to Premier Li Peng's visit. Both sides expressed the hope that the friendly relations between the two countries would continue to develop in a stable manner over the long term.

After the meeting, the Emperor and Empress also met with Premier Li Peng's official entourage and had a conversation with them.

Subsequently, Emperor Akihito and Empress Michiko hosted a luncheon at the palace for Premier Li Peng, his wife, and the entourage.

China's Ambassador to Japan, Yang Zhenya, was present at the meeting and attended the luncheon.

### 日本天皇会见李鹏总理
回瑞岩
1989-04-14
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　日本天皇会见李鹏总理
    新华社东京４月１３日电　（记者回瑞岩）日本明仁天皇和皇后美智子今天上午在皇宫会见了李鹏总理和夫人朱琳。
    宾主进行了友好谈话。天皇对李鹏总理的来访表示热烈欢迎。宾主都表示希望两国的友好关系能长期稳定地发展下去。
    会见结束后，天皇和皇后还会见了李鹏总理的正式陪同人员，并同他们进行交谈。
    随后，明仁天皇和皇后设宫中午宴款待李鹏总理和夫人以及陪同人员。
    中国驻日大使杨振亚会见时在座并出席了午宴。
