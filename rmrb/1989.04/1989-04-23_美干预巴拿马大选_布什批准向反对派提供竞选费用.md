Title: U.S. Interference in Panama's Election: Bush Approves Funds for Opposition
Author:
Date: 1989-04-23
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, April 22nd.

The U.S. President Bush has approved the provision of over $10 million in aid to the Panamanian opposition, aimed at defeating the powerful figure in Panama, Noriega, in the upcoming elections.

According to the latest issue of "U.S. News & World Report," this aid package includes assistance for printing, advertising, transportation, and communication. The Panamanian elections are scheduled for May 7th.

### 美干预巴拿马大选  布什批准向反对派提供竞选费用

1989-04-23
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美干预巴拿马大选    
    布什批准向反对派提供竞选费用
    据新华社华盛顿４月２２日电  美国总统布什已批准向巴拿马反对派提供１０００多万美元的援助，旨在在即将举行的大选中击败巴拿马实权人物诺列加。
    据最新一期《美国新闻与世界报道》杂志说，这项援助计划包括提供印刷、广告、运输和通讯方面的援助。巴拿马大选定于５月７日举行。
