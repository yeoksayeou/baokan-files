Title: Borg Loses Third Match to McEnroe
Author:
Date: 1989-04-16
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 15th. According to foreign news reports: Five-time Wimbledon champion and Swedish star Borg lost to famous American player McEnroe in a third exhibition match held in Taipei on the evening of the 14th, with a score of 0:2.

This was Borg's third farewell match in his planned four-match Asian tour. The first and second matches were held in Singapore and Manila, respectively. Borg lost to McEnroe with scores of 0:2 and 1:2.

### 博格第三场负于麦肯罗

1989-04-16
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：

　　博格第三场负于麦肯罗
    新华社北京４月１５日电　据外电报道：５次温布尔登网球赛冠军、瑞典明星博格１４日晚在台北与著名美国选手麦肯罗进行了第三场表演，结果，以０∶２败北。
    这是博格预计在亚洲进行的４场告别赛中的第三场。第一场和第二场分别已在新加坡和马尼拉举行。博格以０∶２和１∶２输给了麦肯罗。
