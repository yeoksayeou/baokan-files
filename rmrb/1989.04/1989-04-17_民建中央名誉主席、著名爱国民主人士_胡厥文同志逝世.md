Title: Renowned Patriotic Democrat Hu Juewen Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-04-17
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 17th.

The honorary chairman of the Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association, renowned patriotic democrat Hu Juewen, passed away at 23:57 on April 16, 1989, in Beijing due to illness. He was 94 years old.

Hu Juewen was a native of Jiading County, Shanghai. He graduated from the Beijing Higher Industrial School in 1918. He successively founded the Xinmin Machinery Factory, Hezuo Hardware Company, Huangdu Electric Light Company, and Changcheng Machine-Made Brick and Tile Company, serving as the factory director and general manager. He also served as the chairman of the Shanghai Machinery Industry Association and the chairman of the Shanghai Cotton Market. After the "August 13" Battle of Shanghai, to preserve the national industry and support the war of resistance, Hu Juewen organized the relocation of patriotic industrialists from Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Shanghai, and Nanjing to the interior, serving as the chairman of the Central-South Region Industrial Association and the chairman of the Sichuan Relocated Factories Federation. In 1945, he co-founded the China Democratic National Construction Association with Huang Yanpei and others, uniting patriotic industrialists and businessmen with the intellectuals they were connected to, actively participating in the patriotic democratic movement, and making significant contributions to the development of the national industry, national liberation, and the cause of the new democratic revolution.

In September 1949, Hu Juewen attended the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference as a representative of the China Democratic National Construction Association, participating in the preparatory work for the founding of the People's Republic of China. After the founding of New China, Hu Juewen actively promoted the Shanghai business community's efforts to restore production and develop the economy, taking the lead in implementing public-private joint operations in his own enterprises, and encouraging fellow businessmen to actively cooperate with the state's socialist transformation. Over the years, Hu Juewen has actively participated in the consultation and decision-making of major national policies, participated in the management of state affairs, and made significant contributions to the reform and opening up, the consolidation and expansion of the patriotic united front, the adherence to and improvement of the multi-party cooperation system under the leadership of the Communist Party, and the cause of socialist construction.

Hu Juewen had served as the vice chairman and vice mayor of the Shanghai Municipal Political Consultative Conference, a representative of the First National People's Congress, a member of the Standing Committee of the Second and Third National People's Congress, a vice chairman of the Standing Committee of the Fourth, Fifth, and Sixth National People's Congress, a member of the First, Second, Third, and Fourth National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and a standing member of the Fifth National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

During Hu Juewen's illness, comrades Qiao Shi, Hu Qili, Yan Mingfu, Sun Qimeng, Wang Guangying, and Sun Xiaocun visited the hospital to see him.

### 民建中央名誉主席、著名爱国民主人士　胡厥文同志逝世

1989-04-17
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　民建中央名誉主席、著名爱国民主人士
　　胡厥文同志逝世
    新华社北京４月１７日电　第六届全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、中国民主建国会中央委员会名誉主席、中华职业教育社理事长、著名爱国民主人士胡厥文同志，因病于１９８９年４月１６日２３时５７分在北京逝世，终年９４岁。
    胡厥文同志是上海市嘉定县人，１９１８年毕业于北京高等工业学校。他先后创办新民机器厂、合作五金公司、黄渡电灯公司、长城机制砖瓦公司等企业，任厂长、总经理职务，并任上海机器业同业工会主任委员、上海市棉布市场理事长。“八一三”淞沪抗战后，为保存民族工业，支援抗战，胡厥文同志组织江浙沪宁爱国民族工业家内迁，任中南区工业协会理事长、迁川工厂联合会理事长。１９４５年他与黄炎培等发起成立了民主建国会，团结爱国民族工商业家和所联系的知识界人士，积极投入爱国民主运动，为民族工业的发展，为民族解放和新民主主义革命事业作出了重要贡献。
    １９４９年９月，胡厥文同志以民主建国会代表身份出席中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议，参加了建立中华人民共和国的筹备工作。新中国成立后，胡厥文同志积极推动上海工商界为恢复生产发展经济努力工作，率先在自己的企业实行公私合营，带动工商界同仁积极配合国家的社会主义改造事业。长期以来，胡厥文同志积极参加国家大政方针的协商和决策，参加国家事务的管理工作，为改革开放，为爱国统一战线的巩固和扩大，为坚持和完善共产党领导的多党合作制度，为社会主义建设事业，作出了重大贡献。
    胡厥文同志曾任上海市政协副主席、副市长，第一届全国人大代表，第二、三届全国人大常委会委员，第四、五、六届全国人大常委会副委员长，第一、二、三、四届政协全国委员会委员，第五届政协全国委员会常务委员。
    胡厥文同志病重期间，乔石、胡启立、阎明复、孙起孟、王光英、孙晓村等同志曾前往医院看望。
