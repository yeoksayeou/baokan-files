Title: Preparations for the Barcelona Olympics Stall
Author: Guo Jisi
Date: 1989-04-15
Edition: 4

International Olympic Committee officials recently expressed concern over the preparations for the 1992 Barcelona Olympics. Although in public, IOC President Juan Antonio Samaranch does not want to show that the slow progress of the preparations for the Olympics in his hometown is causing him stress, other officials are not hesitant to say, "Apart from constructing a main stadium, Olympic Village, and a gymnasium, nothing else has progressed."

"Within the Barcelona Olympics Organizing Committee, people are constantly arguing, resulting in frequent changes in personnel. Old problems are temporarily resolved, but new ones emerge." The current executive director of the Barcelona Olympics Organizing Committee, Miguel Abad, is the manager of the Barcelona Expo. An IOC executive member said, "He doesn't understand sports, yet he pretends to, even thinking he can lecture us..."

### 巴塞罗那奥运会准备工作无进展
郭季思
1989-04-15
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：周末体育

　　巴塞罗那奥运会准备工作无进展
    国际奥委会的负责人最近对１９９２年巴塞罗那奥运会的准备工作表示忧虑。虽然在公开场合下，国际奥委会主席萨马兰奇不愿让人看到，在他家乡举办奥运会的准备工作进展缓慢给他精神带来压力，但是其他的官员们却毫不隐讳地说，“他们除了建造一个主体育场、奥林匹克村和一个体育馆外，其它事情均无进展。”
    “在巴塞罗那奥运会组委会里，人们互相争吵，结果是不停地变换人马。旧的问题暂时解决了，但新的问题又出来了。”现任巴塞罗那奥运会组委会执行主任米格尔·阿巴德是巴塞罗那博览会经理。一位国际奥委会执委说：“他不懂得体育，还装懂，甚至还以为他可以教训我们……”
　　　　　　　郭季思
