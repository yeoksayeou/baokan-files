Title: Zhao Ziyang Meets with Romanian Deputy Prime Minister
Author: Línéngqīng
Date: 1989-04-27
Edition: 3

    Zhao Ziyang, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, met with Romania's First Deputy Prime Minister Ion Dinca, who is currently visiting North Korea, this afternoon at the Chinese Embassy in North Korea.

    During the meeting, both sides engaged in cordial and friendly discussions, exchanging greetings on behalf of their respective national leaders.

### 赵紫阳会见罗副总理
黎能清
1989-04-27
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　赵紫阳会见罗副总理
    新华社平壤４月２７日电（记者黎能清）中国共产党中央委员会总书记赵紫阳，今天下午在中国驻朝鲜大使馆会见了正在朝鲜访问的罗马尼亚第一副总理杨·丁卡。
    会见中，双方进行了亲切友好的交谈，相互转达了对各自国家领导人的问候。
