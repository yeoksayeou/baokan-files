Title: Bridge Collapse in Tennessee, USA
Author:
Date: 1989-04-04
Edition: 4

On April 1, a bridge on Interstate 51 north of Cowan, Tennessee, suddenly collapsed, causing several vehicles to fall into the river below. The incident resulted in the deaths of four people. The image shows rescue workers conducting rescue operations at the scene.
