Title: Fu Xishou Elected Governor of Anhui
Author: Zhang Zhen Guo
Date: 1989-04-27
Edition: 1

Fu Xishou was elected as the Governor of Anhui Province at the second session of the seventh Anhui Provincial People's Congress. He graduated from Tsinghua University in his early years and is a senior engineer.

The tenth meeting of the seventh Standing Committee of the Anhui Provincial People's Congress accepted the resignation of Lu Rongjing from the position of Governor. He remains as the Secretary of the Anhui Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China.

### 傅锡寿当选安徽省省长
张振国
1989-04-27
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　傅锡寿当选安徽省省长
    本报合肥４月２７日电  记者张振国报道：在安徽省七届人大二次会上，傅锡寿当选为安徽省省长。他早年毕业于清华大学，系高级工程师。
    省七届人大常委会第十次会议接受了卢荣景辞去省长职务。他仍为中共安徽省委书记。
