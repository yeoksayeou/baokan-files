Title: One Year Since the Assassination of Abu Jihad, Tunisia Holds Memorial Activities
Author:
Date: 1989-04-17
Edition: 3

The Palestine Liberation Organization and the ruling Democratic Constitutional Rally of Tunisia held a mass rally in Tunis on April 16 to commemorate the first anniversary of the assassination of Abu Jihad, the deputy commander of the Palestinian revolutionary armed forces.

Palestinian President and PLO Executive Committee Chairman Yasser Arafat said at the rally that the Israeli authorities attempted to suppress the uprising of the Palestinian people in the occupied territories by assassinating Abu Jihad. He said, however, that the uprising continues.

The deputy secretary-general of the Arab League, Fara, Abu Jihad's wife, Wazir, and leaders of the Democratic Constitutional Rally and the "Fatah" Central Committee of Tunisia attended the meeting.

### 杰哈德遭暗杀一周年　突尼斯举行纪念活动

1989-04-17
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　杰哈德遭暗杀一周年
　　突尼斯举行纪念活动
    新华社突尼斯４月１６日电　巴勒斯坦解放组织和突尼斯执政党民主宪政联盟１６日在突尼斯举行群众集会，纪念巴勒斯坦革命武装力量副总司令阿布·杰哈德遭暗杀１周年。
    巴勒斯坦国总统、巴解组织执委会主席阿拉法特在集会上说，以色列当局企图通过暗杀阿布·杰哈德来扼杀被占领土上的巴勒斯坦人民起义。他说，但是，起义仍在继续。
    阿拉伯联盟副秘书长法拉、阿布·杰哈德的妻子瓦齐尔以及突尼斯民主宪政联盟和“法塔赫”中央委员会领导成员参加了会议。
