Title: Photo News
Author:
Date: 1989-04-23
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency

April 16th, a freight train in the Federal Republic of Germany derailed near Bremen. Due to the risk of explosion from two gas cylinders among the 21 derailed carriages, over 1,000 people in the surrounding area have been evacuated, and the railway between Bremen and Hamburg has been suspended.

April 18th, a train derailment occurred in central India, resulting in at least 67 deaths and 137 injuries.
