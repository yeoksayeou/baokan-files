Title: Condolences from Leaders of Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1989-04-21
Edition: 3

Some foreign leaders have sent condolences to Yang Shangkun, Wan Li, and Li Peng following the passing of Hu Yaobang.

On April 21, British Prime Minister Margaret Thatcher sent a telegram to Premier Li Peng, recalling Hu Yaobang's visit to the United Kingdom and his role during Queen Elizabeth II's state visit to China. She expressed condolences on behalf of the British government to the Chinese leadership and people for Hu Yaobang's passing.

Mexican President Carlos Salinas sent a telegram to Chairman Yang Shangkun on April 20, expressing the Mexican government's condolences for Hu Yaobang's passing. Guyanese President Desmond Hoyte also sent a letter to Chairman Yang Shangkun on April 18, expressing his condolences for Hu Yaobang's passing.

New Zealand Prime Minister Geoffrey Palmer sent a letter to General Secretary Zhao Ziyang on April 20, stating that Hu Yaobang's passing "has deprived China of an outstanding citizen, a fervent supporter of your government's open policy, and has also deprived New Zealand of a good friend."

Japanese House of Councillors President Yoshihiko Tsukasa sent a telegram to Chairman Wan Li on April 18, expressing deep condolences on behalf of the Japanese House of Councillors for Hu Yaobang's passing. Japanese House of Representatives President Kenjiro Ueno also sent a telegram to Chairman Wan Li on April 18, expressing deep sorrow for Hu Yaobang's passing.

### 一些国家领导人的唁电唁函

1989-04-21
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　一些国家领导人的唁电唁函
    新华社北京４月２１日电  杨尚昆主席、万里委员长和李鹏总理日内又陆续收到一些外国领导人悼念胡耀邦同志逝世的唁电。
    英国首相撒切尔夫人４月２１日在致李鹏总理的唁电中缅怀胡耀邦对联合王国的访问以及他在英国女王陛下对中国的国事访问中所起的作用。她代表英国政府向中国领导人和中国人民就胡耀邦逝世表示慰问。
    墨西哥总统萨利纳斯４月２０日致电杨尚昆主席，代表墨西哥政府哀悼胡耀邦逝世。圭亚那总统霍伊特也在４月１８日致函杨尚昆主席，对胡耀邦的逝世表示悼念。
    新西兰总理朗伊４月２０日在致赵紫阳总书记的唁函中说，胡耀邦的逝世“使中国失去了一位杰出的公民，一位贵国政府开放政策的热情支持者，也使新西兰失去了一位好朋友。”
    日本国参议院议长土屋义彦４月１８日致电万里委员长，代表日本国国会参议院对胡耀邦的逝世表示衷心哀悼。日本国众议院议长原健三郎４月１８日也致电万里委员长，表示对胡耀邦的逝世深感悲痛。
