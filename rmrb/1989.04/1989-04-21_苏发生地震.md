Title: Earthquake Occurs in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1989-04-21
Edition: 3

According to the Chinese seismic network, an earthquake measuring 6.8 on the Richter scale occurred at 6:59 Beijing time (7:59 Beijing Daylight Saving Time) today in the eastern part of Siberia in the Soviet Union. The epicenter was located at 57.2 degrees north latitude and 123.5 degrees east longitude, approximately 400 kilometers north of Mohe, China, near the North Pole Village.

### 苏发生地震

1989-04-21
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏发生地震
    新华社北京４月２１日电  据我国地震台网测定，今天北京时间６点５９分（北京夏令时７点５９分），苏联西伯利亚东部地区发生６．８级地震。震中在北纬５７．２度，东经１２３．５度，即我国北极村漠河以北大约４００公里处。
