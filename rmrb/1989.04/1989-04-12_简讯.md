Title: Brief News
Author:
Date: 1989-04-12
Edition: 2

Vice Premier Tian Jiyun met with Swedish Minister of Foreign Trade Anita Gradin and her delegation on April 10 at the Great Hall of the People.

State Councilor Chen Junsheng met with a delegation led by Bohuslav, Minister of Labor and Social Affairs of Czechoslovakia, in Beijing on April 10.

Minister of Radio, Film, and Television Ai Zhensheng met with a delegation from the Iranian Broadcasting and Television Organization, led by Ali Mohammad Zolghadr, Deputy Chairman, at the Central Television Color Television Center on April 10. (According to Xinhua News Agency)
