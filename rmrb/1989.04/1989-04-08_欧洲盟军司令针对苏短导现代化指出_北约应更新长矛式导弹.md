Title: NATO Commander Calls for Updating Lance Missiles in Response to Soviet Short-Range Missile Modernization
Author:
Date: 1989-04-08
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, April 6th.

The Supreme Allied Commander Europe of NATO, John Galvin, stated today that NATO should continue with its plan to update the outdated Lance missiles.

This NATO general told the American Columbia Broadcasting Company that the Soviet Union has done a great deal of work in modernizing short-range, ground-to-ground missiles with a range of 500 kilometers over the past 10 years, replacing the Frog missiles with SS-21 missiles and improving the Scud missiles.

He said that NATO has done nothing in missile modernization for more than 20 years.

When discussing the ongoing Geneva arms reduction talks between NATO and the Warsaw Pact, Galvin said that the Warsaw Pact has an advantage in the number of troops and should be reduced to a level equal to NATO.

He emphasized that the Soviet Union should reduce its 59,000 tanks deployed against Western Europe before further negotiations can proceed.

### 欧洲盟军司令针对苏短导现代化指出  北约应更新长矛式导弹

1989-04-08
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　欧洲盟军司令针对苏短导现代化指出
    北约应更新长矛式导弹
    新华社华盛顿４月６日电  北约欧洲盟军最高司令约翰·高尔文今天说，北约应该继续执行更新过时的长矛式导弹的计划。
    这位北约将军对美国哥伦比亚广播公司说，苏联在过去１０年中在射程５００公里以内的短程地对地导弹的现代化方面做了大量的工作，用ＳＳ—２１导弹淘汰了蛙式导弹，改进了飞毛腿式导弹。
    他说，北约２０多年来在导弹现代化方面一直无所作为。
    当谈到目前正在进行的北约和华约日内瓦裁军谈判时，高尔文说，华约集团在军队人数方面占有优势，应该削减到与北约均等的水平。
    他强调指出，苏联应该削减它针对西欧布置的５．９万辆坦克，此后才能进行进一步谈判。
