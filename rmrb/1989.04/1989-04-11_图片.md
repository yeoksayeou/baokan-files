Title: New "Bowen Folk Museum" Opens in Sichuan's Dayi County
Author: Chen Xiao
Date: 1989-04-11
Edition: 4

The "Rent Collection Hall" in Sichuan's Dayi County has opened a new "Bowen Folk Museum." The image depicts a traditional "wedding" scene from the Bowen region: a newlywed couple is paying their respects at the altar.
