Title: Shanxi Aluminum Plant Opens Without Fanfare
Author: Lǐ Dé Mín  Liú Dōng Huá
Date: 1989-04-13
Edition: 2

The Shanxi Aluminum Plant, a key national construction project with an annual production capacity of 200,000 tons of alumina, has recently completed its first-phase construction and passed national inspection, officially starting production. During the inspection process, the plant ensured continuous production, did not hold banquets, did not cut ribbons, did not set off firecrackers, and did not give gifts to the inspectors.

### 山西铝厂开张不铺张
李德民　刘东华
1989-04-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　山西铝厂开张不铺张
    本报山西河津4月12日电　年产20万吨氧化铝的国家重点建设项目山西铝厂一期工程，日前通过国家验收，正式投产。在几天的验收工作中，铝厂做到了不停产、不会餐、不剪彩、不放鞭炮、不给验收人员送礼品。
                              （李德民　刘东华）
