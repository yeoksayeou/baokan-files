Title: Li Peng Signs State Council Decree: Regulations for the Protection of Oil and Gas Pipelines Come into Effect
Author:
Date: 1989-04-02
Edition: 1

Li Peng Signs State Council Decree

Regulations for the Protection of Oil and Gas Pipelines Come into Effect

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 1—To ensure the normal operation of oil and natural gas pipelines, Premier Li Peng recently signed a State Council decree, issuing the "Regulations for the Protection of Oil and Natural Gas Pipelines" (the full text will be published in the documents section of this newspaper).

This regulation consists of 5 chapters and 31 articles. It provides specific provisions for the safety protection of oil and natural gas pipelines, the handling of relationships between pipelines and other construction projects, as well as rewards and penalties.

The "Regulations" clearly state that oil and natural gas pipelines, their ancillary facilities, and the oil and natural gas transported through these pipelines are all state property, protected by law. No unit or individual may appropriate, steal, loot, or damage them. All units and individuals have the obligation to protect the pipelines and their ancillary facilities. They have the right to stop and report to relevant departments any behavior that endangers the pipelines and their ancillary facilities.

This regulation comes into effect from the date of its issuance.

### 李鹏签署国务院令  保护油气管道条例开始施行

1989-04-02
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　李鹏签署国务院令
    保护油气管道条例开始施行
    新华社北京４月１日电  为了保障石油、天然气管道的正常运行，国务院总理李鹏最近签署国务院令，发布《石油、天然气管道保护条例》（本报将全文刊登在文件版）。
    这个条例共有５章３１条。它对石油、天然气管道的安全保护、管道与其他建设工程相遇的关系处理、奖励和惩罚等作出了具体规定。
    《条例》明确指出，石油、天然气管道及其附属设施以及管道输送的石油和天然气都属于国家财产，受法律保护，任何单位和个人不得侵占、盗窃、哄抢、破坏。任何单位和个人都有保护管道及附属设施的义务，对于危害管道及其附属设施的行为，有权制止并向有关部门报告。
    这个条例自发布之日起施行。
