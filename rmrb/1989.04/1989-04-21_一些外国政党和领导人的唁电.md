Title: Condolences from Foreign Political Parties and Leaders
Author:
Date: 1989-04-21
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 21st.

Condolences from Some Foreign Political Parties and Leaders

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and General Secretary Zhao Ziyang continued to receive telegrams of condolence from various political parties or their leaders mourning the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang.

On April 21st, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Jaruzelski, sent a telegram to General Secretary Zhao Ziyang, expressing deep sorrow and sincere condolences on behalf of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and all party members for the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang. The telegram stated: "Comrade Hu Yaobang made immortal contributions in the struggle led by the Communist Party of China for the liberation of the Chinese people and nation, and in the construction and development of the People's Republic of China. He made a valuable contribution to strengthening the friendship and cooperation between the Polish United Workers' Party and the Communist Party of China."

On April 21st, the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, recalling Hu Yaobang's contributions to restoring friendly relations between the two parties and two countries, and deepening the understanding and good relations between the two peoples.

On April 19th, the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Portuguese Communist Party sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to mourn the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang.

On April 16th, General Secretary Moreno of the Communist Party of Peru (Red Fatherland) sent a telegram to General Secretary Zhao Ziyang, expressing deep sorrow over the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang.

On April 16th, Chairman of the Revolutionary Leftist Alliance of Peru and Senator of the Republic of Peru, Brenia, sent a telegram to General Secretary Zhao Ziyang to mourn the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang.

On April 21st, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Reunion sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, mentioning that during Hu Yaobang's tenure as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Communist Party of Reunion and the Communist Party of China restored their party relations that had been interrupted for 25 years. "His passing is a great loss to China, which has lost a great leader, and to Reunion, which has lost a great friend."

Chairman Gunejo and General Secretary Ahmed of the Muslim League of Pakistan also sent separate telegrams to Premier Li Peng, expressing heartfelt condolences for the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang.

### 一些外国政党和领导人的唁电

1989-04-21
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　一些外国政党和领导人的唁电
    新华社北京４月２１日电  中共中央和赵紫阳总书记日内继续收到一些政党或政党领导人悼念胡耀邦同志逝世的电报。
    波兰统一工人党中央第一书记雅鲁泽尔斯基４月２１日在致赵紫阳总书记的唁电中，以波兰统一工人党中央和全体党员的名义，对胡耀邦同志的逝世表示深切的哀悼和诚挚的慰问。电报中说：“胡耀邦同志在由中国共产党领导的中国人民争取社会和民族解放的斗争中，在中华人民共和国的建设和发展中建立了不朽的功绩。”“他为密切波兰统一工人党和中国共产党之间的友谊与合作作出了可贵的贡献。”
    匈牙利社会主义工人党中央４月２１日致中共中央的唁电中表示：铭记着胡耀邦为恢复两党、两国之间的友好关系、为加深两国人民之间的理解和良好关系所作出的贡献。
    葡萄牙共产党中央书记处４月１９日致电中共中央哀悼胡耀邦同志逝世。
    秘鲁共产党（红色祖国）总书记莫雷诺４月１６日在致赵紫阳总书记的唁电中，对胡耀邦同志的去世表示深为悲痛。
    秘鲁革命左派联盟主席、秘鲁共和国参议员布雷尼亚４月１６日致电赵紫阳总书记哀悼胡耀邦同志逝世。
    留尼汪共产党中央４月２１日在致中共中央的唁电中谈到，在胡耀邦任中共中央总书记期间，留尼汪共产党和中国共产党恢复了中断２５年的两党关系，“他的逝世使中国失去了一位伟大的领导人，留尼汪失去了一位伟大的朋友。”
    巴基斯坦穆斯林联盟主席居内久和总书记阿赫迈德也分别致电李鹏总理，对胡耀邦同志的逝世表示衷心的哀悼。
