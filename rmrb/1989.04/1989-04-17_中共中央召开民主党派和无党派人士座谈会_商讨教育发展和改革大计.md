Title: The CPC Central Committee Holds a Symposium with Representatives of Democratic Parties and Non-Party Personages to Discuss the Development and Reform of Education
Author:
Date: 1989-04-17
Edition: 1

The CPC Central Committee held a symposium with representatives of democratic parties and non-party personages to discuss the development and reform of education. Zhao Ziyang presided over the meeting and delivered a speech. Li Peng, Qiao Shi, Hu Qili, Yao Yilin, and others attended the meeting. Lei Jieqiong, Sun Qimeng, Zhou Gu Cheng, Ding Shisun, Chen Mingzhao, and others expressed their opinions.

New China News Agency, Beijing, April 17: The CPC Central Committee held a symposium with representatives of various democratic parties and non-party personages in the Great Hall of the People this morning to solicit opinions on the "Draft Decision of the CPC Central Committee on Several Issues Regarding the Development and Reform of Education."

Zhao Ziyang, Li Peng, Qiao Shi, Hu Qili, Yao Yilin, Li Tieying, Song Ping, Rui Xianwen, Yan Mingfu, Wen Jiabao, and other comrades attended the symposium and listened to the opinions of the attendees.

At the beginning of the symposium, Zhao Ziyang, the General Secretary presiding over the meeting, proposed that all attendees stand in silence to mourn the passing of Comrade Hu Yaobang.

Zhao Ziyang said, "Today, the CPC Central Committee invited leaders of various democratic parties and relevant personages to hold a symposium to listen to everyone's opinions on the 'Draft Decision of the CPC Central Committee on Several Issues Regarding the Development and Reform of Education.' I hope that everyone will speak freely with a spirit of mutual trust, and together we will improve this document."

Zhao Ziyang said, "The democratic parties have always been very concerned and attentive to China's educational cause. In recent years, everyone has repeatedly called for the development of education and put forward many important opinions and suggestions. These opinions have had a significant impact and promotion on the CPC Central Committee. I feel that in the development of China's educational cause, the CPC and democratic parties have engaged in sincere dialogue and mutual learning, which well reflects the spirit of cooperation under the leadership of the Communist Party."

Zhao Ziyang introduced the process of forming the "Draft Decision." He said that during the drafting of the document, the Standing Committee of the Politburo discussed it twice, and the State Council discussed it three times. The document extensively absorbed the valuable opinions of democratic parties and non-party personages on educational issues. On April 8, the CPC Central Committee Politburo held its 17th plenary meeting to discuss the "Draft Decision" and decided to further solicit opinions on this document. The document you see now is the one that has been further revised after the Politburo meeting.

Zhao Ziyang said, "We believe that as long as the entire country and society pay attention to education and take strong measures, China's educational cause has great hope. The 'Draft Decision' expresses this resolve and confidence and proposes some major measures for the development and reform of education. Whether these measures are feasible, and what deletions, revisions, and supplements are needed, please provide your opinions. Democratic parties and some non-party personages not only care about the educational cause but are also knowledgeable. Many of the attendees today are educational experts, and many comrades have conducted in-depth investigations and research on the state of China's educational cause and have insightful views. I hope everyone will not hesitate to offer advice and help improve this document, making new contributions to the development of China's educational cause and the rejuvenation of the Chinese nation."

At the symposium, Lei Jieqiong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League for Self-Improvement and Progress (CDL), Sun Qimeng, Chairman of the Central Committee of the China National Democratic Construction Association (CNDCA), Zhou Gu Cheng, Honorary Chairman of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League of Peasants and Workers (CDLPW), Ding Shisun, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League (CDL), and Chen Mingzhao, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Jiusan Society, successively spoke. They discussed raising the public's awareness of the importance of education, strengthening ideological and political work, strengthening basic education and vocational education, and solving the problems of educational funding and improving teachers' remuneration.

The symposium will begin group discussions starting tomorrow.

### 中共中央召开民主党派和无党派人士座谈会　商讨教育发展和改革大计

1989-04-17
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　中共中央召开民主党派和无党派人士座谈会
　　商讨教育发展和改革大计
    赵紫阳主持并讲话李鹏乔石胡启立姚依林等出席
    雷洁琼孙起孟周谷城丁石孙陈明绍先后发表意见
    新华社北京４月１７日电　中共中央今天上午在人民大会堂召开各民主党派和无党派人士座谈会，征求对《中共中央关于教育发展和改革若干问题的决定（草案）》的意见。
    赵紫阳、李鹏、乔石、胡启立、姚依林、李铁映、宋平、芮杏文、阎明复、温家宝等同志出席座谈会，听取了大家的意见。
    座谈会开始时，主持会议的赵紫阳总书记提议与会人士肃立默哀，对胡耀邦同志的逝世表示深切哀悼。
    赵紫阳说，今天，中共中央邀请各民主党派负责同志和有关人士进行座谈，就是要听取大家对《中共中央关于教育发展和改革若干问题的决定（草案）》的意见，希望同志们本着肝胆相照的精神，畅所欲言，共同把这个文件修改好。
    赵紫阳说，各民主党派一直非常关心、重视中国的教育事业。特别是近年来，大家一再呼吁，为发展教育提出了许多重要的意见和建议。这些意见对中共中央有很大的影响和促进。我觉得，在发展我国教育事业这个问题上，中共和民主党派切磋琢磨、真诚对话，很好地体现了共产党领导的多党合作的精神。
    赵紫阳向大家介绍了《决定（草案）》形成的过程。他说，文件起草过程中，中央政治局常委讨论过两次，国务院讨论过三次。文件中大量吸取了民主党派和无党派人士关于教育问题的宝贵意见。４月８日，中共中央政治局召开第十七次全体会议对《决定（草案）》进行了讨论，决定对这个文件进一步征求意见。现在大家看到的，就是这次政治局会议讨论后又作过一些修改的文件。
    赵紫阳说，我们相信，只要全国、全社会都来重视教育，下决心采取强有力的措施，我国的教育事业是大有希望的。《决定（草案）》表达了这样一个决心和信心，也提出了发展和改革教育的一些重大措施。这些认识和措施行不行，需要进行哪些删除、修正和补充，请同志们提出意见。民主党派和一些无党派人士对教育事业不但热心，而且内行，今天到会的有许多是教育专家，不少同志对我国教育事业的状况作过深入的调查研究，是有真知灼见的。希望大家不吝赐教，把这个文件修改好，为我国教育事业的发展和中华民族的振兴作出新的贡献。
    座谈会上，民进中央主席雷洁琼、民建中央主席孙起孟、农工民主党中央名誉主席周谷城、民盟中央副主席丁石孙、九三学社中央副主席陈明绍先后发言，就提高全民对教育重要性的认识，加强思想政治工作，加强基础教育、职业教育，切实解决教育经费、提高教师待遇等问题，发表了意见。
    座谈会从明天开始进行分组讨论。
