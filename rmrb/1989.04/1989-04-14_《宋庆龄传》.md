Title: Biography of Soong Ching-ling
Author: Jīn Lǐ
Date: 1989-04-14
Edition: 6

This book, written by Liu Jiaquan and published by the Chinese Federation of Literary and Art Circles Press, vividly portrays the great and brilliant life of Soong Ching-ling through meticulous historical records. It showcases her globally admired virtues, her radiant demeanor that illuminates the world, her spirited and enterprising spirit, and her open and magnanimous heart. The book also reveals many lesser-known historical facts and corrects some historical inaccuracies. It is a distinctive biography.
