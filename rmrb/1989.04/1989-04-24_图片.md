Title: The Prosperity of Dairy Farming in Xuzhuang Village
Author: Du Zongwu
Date: 1989-04-24
Edition: 5

Xuzhuang Village in Xinglong Township, Jingyang County, Shaanxi Province, began raising cows in 1984. Initially, there were only 30 dairy cows. By 1988, the number had grown to 224. Each cow generates an average net income of over 2,000 yuan, and the village's total income from dairy farming alone exceeds 120,000 yuan.
