Title: China Women's Volleyball Team Wins First Match in European Training Tour
Author:
Date: 1989-04-01
Edition: 3

The Chinese women's volleyball team, participating in the sixth Swiss National Cup International Women's Volleyball Invitational Tournament, defeated the Japanese team in straight sets on the evening of March 30. The scores for the three sets were 15:13, 15:5, and 15:5. This was the Chinese team's second victory in this tournament.

On the evening of March 29, the Chinese team won their first match by defeating the strong European team from Czechoslovakia with a score of 3:0. The scores for the three sets were 15:9, 15:1, and 15:4.

The Swiss National Cup International Women's Volleyball Invitational Tournament is a significant event in the international volleyball arena, held annually at the beginning of the year. The top women's volleyball teams from around the world typically engage in their first match of the year in this tournament.

A total of nine teams are participating in this year's invitational tournament. The competition is divided into two groups, A and B. The A group includes the newly adjusted Chinese team, the Cuban team in its prime, the 1988 Seoul Olympic silver medalist Peruvian team, the Japanese team, and the Czechoslovakian team. These five teams will compete in a single round-robin format to determine the champion and runner-up of this year's invitational tournament.

### 中国女排访欧练兵初战告捷

1989-04-01
第3版(国内新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

　　中国女排访欧练兵初战告捷
    综合新华社瑞士蒙特勒电 参加第六届瑞士国家杯国际女排邀请赛的中国女子排球队３０日晚直落三局战胜日本队。三局的比分是１５∶１３、１５∶５、１５∶５。这是中国女排在这次比赛中的第二场胜利。
    在２９日晚的比赛中，中国队首战以３∶０击败欧洲劲旅捷克斯洛伐克队，三局的比分是１５∶９、１５∶１和１５∶４。
    瑞士国家杯国际女排邀请赛是国际排坛的一项重要赛事，它于每年年初举行，世界女排诸强通常在这一比赛中进行一年中的第一次交锋。
    参加本届邀请赛的共有９个队。比赛分为甲、乙两组进行。分在甲组的有调整后的中国队、进入鼎盛时期的古巴队、１９８８年汉城奥运会亚军秘鲁队、日本队和捷克斯洛伐克队。这５个球队将进行单循环赛，决出本届邀请赛的冠、亚军。
