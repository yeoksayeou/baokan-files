Title: The First China International Machine Tool Exhibition to Be Held in Shanghai Next Month
Author: Pi Shuyi
Date: 1989-04-08
Edition: 2

The first China International Machine Tool Exhibition will be held in Shanghai from May 9 to 16. The China Machine Tool Industry Association warmly welcomes Taiwanese manufacturers to participate in the exhibition.

According to reports from Taiwanese newspapers, the Taiwanese authorities have forbidden Taiwanese manufacturers from participating in this exhibition. The responsible person from the China Machine Tool Industry Association expressed disappointment over this. He told the reporter, "This exhibition will be beneficial for the development of trade and technological progress. We welcome our colleagues from Taiwan to seize this opportunity."

### 首届中国国际机床博览会下月在沪举行  中国机床协会欢迎台湾厂商参展
皮树义
1989-04-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　首届中国国际机床博览会下月在沪举行
    中国机床协会欢迎台湾厂商参展
    本报北京４月７日讯  记者皮树义报道：中国机床工具工业协会负责人日前表示，热忱欢迎台湾厂商参加将于５月９日至１６日在上海举行的首届中国国际机床博览会。
    据台湾报纸报道，台湾当局不准台湾厂商参加这次展览会。中国机床协会负责人对此表示失望。他对记者说，这次展览会有助于贸易发展和技术进步，欢迎台湾同行利用这一机会。
