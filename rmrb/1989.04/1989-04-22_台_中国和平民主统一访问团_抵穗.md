Title: Delegation from Taiwan's "China Peaceful and Democratic Unification Visiting Group" Arrives in Guangzhou
Author: Chen Wanyun
Date: 1989-04-22
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 21st - A delegation of 15 people led by Taiwan's "Legislative Yuan" member Fei Xiping, known as the Taiwan "China Peaceful and Democratic Unification Visiting Group," arrived in Guangzhou this afternoon via Hong Kong. The delegation is visiting at the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

Taiwan's "Legislative Yuan" member Fei Xiping, originally from Liaoning, is the former chairman of the Taiwan Public Policy Research Association and one of the founders of the Democratic Progressive Party. During a conversation with Guangdong Provincial Political Consultative Conference Vice Chairman Qi Feng and others, he said he advocates for unification and hopes that through various exchanges, mutual understanding can be enhanced, which will be beneficial for the unification of the two sides of the Taiwan Strait.

### 台“中国和平民主统一访问团”抵穗
陈婉雯
1989-04-22
第3版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　台“中国和平民主统一访问团”抵穗
    据新华社广州４月２１日电（记者陈婉雯）以台湾“立法委员”费希平为团长的台湾“中国和平民主统一访问团”一行１５人，今天下午经香港抵达广州。这个团是应全国政协的邀请前来参观访问的。
    台湾“立法委员”费希平籍贯辽宁，是台湾党外公共政策研究会前理事长、台湾民主进步党创始人之一。在与广东省政协副主席祁烽等交谈时，他说他是主张统一的，希望通过各方面的接触，增进互相的了解，有利于海峡两岸的统一。
