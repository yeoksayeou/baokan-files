Title: I Will Produce Passenger Aircraft Through International Cooperation
Author: Bai Guoliang
Date: 1989-04-15
Edition: 2

China will produce 150 new-generation passenger aircraft through international cooperation to meet the growing needs of the domestic aviation industry.

This was learned by reporters from the press conference held in Shanghai today to mark the fourth anniversary of the Sino-American cooperation in producing the MD-82 aircraft. It is predicted that the demand for this new-generation passenger aircraft will reach 150 units by the year 2050; the aircraft will have 150 to 180 seats and a maximum range of 4,000 kilometers. It is understood that China will invite bids for the joint production of new passenger aircraft with foreign partners.

### 我将通过国际合作制造客机
白国良
1989-04-15
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　我将通过国际合作制造客机
    据新华社上海４月１４日电　（记者白国良）我国将通过国际合作和制造１５０架新一代的民用客机，满足日益发展的国内民航业需要。
    这是记者从今天在上海举行的中美合作生产ＭＤ—８２飞机四周年新闻发布会上获悉的。据预测，这种新一代民用客机的需求量，到２０５０年为１５０架；机上将设有１５０至１８０个座位，最大航程４０００公里。据悉，我国将招标与外国合作者合造新客机。
