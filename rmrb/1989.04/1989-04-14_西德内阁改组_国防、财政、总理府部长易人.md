Title: West German Cabinet Reshuffle: Defense, Finance, and Chancellery Ministers Replaced
Author: Jiang Jian Guo
Date: 1989-04-14
Edition: 3

West German Chancellor Helmut Kohl today announced a major reshuffle of his cabinet at a noon press conference.

Former Finance Minister Gerhard Stoltenberg will take over as Defense Minister; Finance Minister will be taken over by Theodor Waigel, the chairman of the CSU; former Chancellery Minister Wolfgang Schäuble will become the Interior Minister; former parliamentary group leader of the CDU/CSU parliamentary group Rudolf Seiters will enter the cabinet for the first time as the Chancellery Minister. Former Defense Minister Manfred Wörner, government spokesman Friedrich Vogt, and Construction Minister Oscar Schneider will step down. Several parliamentary state secretaries of various ministries will also be adjusted.

It is believed that Chancellor Kohl's cabinet reshuffle is aimed at the 1990 general election.

### 西德内阁改组　国防、财政、总理府部长易人
江建国
1989-04-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　西德内阁改组
　　国防、财政、总理府部长易人
    本报波恩４月１３日电　记者江建国报道：西德总理科尔今天中午举行记者招待会，宣布对内阁作重大改组。
    原财政部长施托尔腾贝格改任国防部长；财政部长由基社盟主席特奥多尔魏格尔接任；原总理府部长朔伊布勒改任内政部长；原基民盟—基社盟议会党团干事长鲁道尔夫·赛特斯首次入阁担任总理府部长，原国防部长朔尔茨、政府发言人奥斯特和建筑部长施奈德去职。几个部的议会国务秘书也作了调整。
    据认为，科尔总理这次改组内阁是着眼于１９９０年的大选。
