Title: Angola, Cuba, and South Africa Hold Meeting to Discuss Ceasefire in Northern Namibia
Author:
Date: 1989-04-10
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Harare, April 8th.

Officials from Angola, Cuba, and South Africa held a three-party committee meeting today in a hotel 280 kilometers northwest of Windhoek, Namibia, to discuss the implementation of a ceasefire in northern Namibia. Representatives from the Soviet Union and the United States attended as observers. No further details about the meeting's content were disclosed.

On April 1st, the implementation of United Nations Security Council Resolution 435 and the ceasefire began in Namibia. However, on the same day, South African forces clashed with SWAPO guerrillas in northern Namibia, hindering the progress towards Namibian independence.

The three-party committee was established to monitor the implementation of the agreement aimed at achieving peace in southwestern Africa.

### 安古南非举行会议  讨论纳北实施停火

1989-04-10
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　安古南非举行会议  讨论纳北实施停火
    新华社哈拉雷４月８日电  温得和克消息：安哥拉、古巴和南非的官员今天在纳米比亚温得和克西北２８０公里的一家旅馆举行三方委员会会议，讨论在纳米比亚北部实行停火的问题。
    苏联和美国的代表以观察员的身份与会。会议的内容没有进一步透露。
    ４月１日，在纳米比亚开始执行联合国安理会４３５号决议并实行停火。但在当天，南非军队同西南非洲人民组织游击队在纳北部发生冲突，使纳米比亚独立进程受阻。
    三方委员会是为查核关于实现西南部非洲和平的协议的执行情况而成立的。
