Title: New Zealand Foreign Minister Arrives in Beijing for Friendly Visit
Author:
Date: 1989-04-03
Edition: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 2nd.

At the invitation of Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen, New Zealand Foreign Minister Russell Marshall arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon from Xi'an. He is on a friendly visit to China.

Upon his arrival in Beijing, Marshall was greeted at the airport by Assistant Foreign Minister Liu Huaqiu and others.

Marshall and his delegation entered the country from Shenzhen on March 29th and then visited Guangzhou and Xi'an.

### 应邀来华友好访问  新西兰外长抵京

1989-04-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　应邀来华友好访问
    新西兰外长抵京
    新华社北京４月２日电  新西兰外交部长拉塞尔·马歇尔今天下午乘飞机从西安抵达北京。他是应中国外交部长钱其琛的邀请来华进行友好访问的。
    马歇尔抵京时，中国外交部部长助理刘华秋等到机场迎接。
    马歇尔一行于３月２９日由深圳入境，然后到广州、西安进行了参观访问。
