Title: The Polish Party Central Committee's Plenary Session Passes Resolution on the "Round Table Talks"
Author:
Date: 1989-04-02
Edition: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 31st.

The 11th Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party passed a resolution on March 31st, calling on all representatives participating in the "Round Table Talks" to "adopt a pragmatic spirit and take a responsible attitude towards the stability of the Polish state" to resolve issues that have not yet been addressed.

According to a report by the Polish News Agency, Political Bureau member and Minister of the Interior, Kiszczak, briefed the attendees on the progress of the "Round Table Talks."

The resolution passed by the plenary session expressed satisfaction with the agreements reached at the "Round Table Talks."

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 31st.

Today, in Warsaw, the First Secretary of the Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee, Jaruzelski, stated that the "Round Table Talks" will soon conclude with the representatives reaching a "social agreement on issues of vital importance to the Polish state."

Jaruzelski made these remarks during the opening speech at the 11th Plenary Session of the Polish United Workers' Party.

He pointed out that the "Round Table Talks" have achieved certain results, some of which have already been reflected in the constitutional amendments and other bills submitted to the parliament for review. He urged the party's deputies to strongly support the passage of these bills.

Regarding the situation within the party, Jaruzelski said that the current situation within the party is improving. Although party members express "anxiety and doubt" about political and trade union pluralism, the line established by the 10th Plenary Session "is gaining increasing approval."

### 波党中央全会通过关于“圆桌会议”决议  呼吁代表要发扬求实精神  对国家稳定采取负责态度

1989-04-02
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　波党中央全会通过关于“圆桌会议”决议
    呼吁代表要发扬求实精神  对国家稳定采取负责态度
    新华社华沙３月３１日电  波兰统一工人党中央委员会第１１次全体会议３１日通过决议，呼吁参加“圆桌会议”的各方代表“发扬求实精神，对波兰国家的稳定采取负责态度”，以解决迄今尚未解决的问题。
    据波通社报道，波党中央政治局委员、内务部长基什查克向与会者通报了“圆桌会议”的进展情况。
    全会通过的决议对在“圆桌会议”上达成的协议表示满意。
    新华社华沙３月３１日电　波兰统一工人党中央第一书记雅鲁泽尔斯基今天在这里说，“圆桌会议”不久将以各方代表“在波兰国家至关重要的问题上达成社会协议”而结束。
    雅鲁泽尔斯基是在波党的１１中全会上致开幕词时说这番话的。
    他指出，“圆桌会议”取得了一定成果，这部分已体现在提交给议会审议的宪法修正案和其他法案中。他要求波党议员大力支持这些法案的通过。
    在谈到党内形势时，雅鲁泽尔斯基说，目前党内形势正在好转，尽管党员们对政治和工会多元化表示“不安和怀疑”，但１０中全会制订的路线“获得了越来越多的人的赞同”。
