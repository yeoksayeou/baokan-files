Title: Nuclear Power Continues to Develop, Accounting for One-Sixth of Global Electricity Generation

Author:

Date: 1989-04-16

Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, April 14th.

Nuclear power continued to develop last year, accounting for one-sixth of the world's total electricity generation.

According to a report released by the International Atomic Energy Agency on April 14th, the global nuclear power generation in the previous year reached 17.9 billion kilowatt-hours, making up one-sixth of the world's total electricity output.

Among the 26 countries that possess nuclear reactors, 11 countries have nuclear power accounting for more than one-third of their total electricity generation.

The report states that there were 429 operational nuclear reactors worldwide last year, with a total installed capacity of 310.8 million kilowatts.

The United States has the most nuclear reactors, with 108. Following are the Soviet Union (56), France (55), the United Kingdom (40), Japan (38), West Germany (23), and Canada (18).

France's nuclear power accounted for 69.9% of its total electricity generation last year, the highest in the world. Following are Belgium (65.5%), Hungary (48.9%), Sweden and South Korea (46.9%), Switzerland (37.4%), Spain (36.1%), Finland (36%), Bulgaria (35.6%), and the Federal Republic of Germany (34%).

Among developing countries, those possessing nuclear reactors include Argentina (2), India (6), and Brazil and Pakistan (each with 1).

The report indicates that there are 105 nuclear reactors currently under construction worldwide.

### 核电去年仍呈发展趋势　占全世界发电量六分之一

1989-04-16
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　核电去年仍呈发展趋势
　　占全世界发电量六分之一
    新华社维也纳４月１４日电　据国际原子能机构１４日发表的报告，去年核发电量达１７．９亿兆瓦小时，占全世界发电总量的１／６。
    在２６个拥有核反应堆的国家中，有１１个国家的核电占其全部发电量的１／３以上。
    报告说，去年全世界运营的核反应堆为４２９座，装机容量３１．０８万兆瓦。
    美国有１０８座核反应堆，居世界首位。以下依次为苏联（５６座），法国（５５座），英国（４０座），日本（３８座），西德（２３座），加拿大（１８座）。
    法国去年核电占其总发电量的６９．９％，居世界首位。以下依次为比利时（６５．５％）、匈牙利（４８．９％）、瑞典和南朝鲜（４６．９％）、瑞士（３７．４％）、西班牙（３６．１％）、芬兰（３６％）保加利亚（３５．６％）、联邦德国（３４％）。
    在发展中国家中，拥有核反应堆的国家有阿根廷（２座）、印度（６座），巴西和巴基斯坦（各一座）。
    报告说，世界各地还有１０５座核反应堆正在建设中。
