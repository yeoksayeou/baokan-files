Title: Soldiers Visit Respect-for-the-Elderly Homes in Rural Areas
Author: Xú Jiàn
Date: 1989-04-20
Edition: 5

The political and legal affairs column of the domestic page:

The 141st Hospital of the People's Liberation Army organized its young Communist League members and youth to conduct a survey in several "respect-for-the-elderly homes" in the rural areas where the hospital is stationed as part of their education on the current situation. The picture shows Grandma Wang Yuxuan sharing her happy life in her later years with the soldiers. Photographed by Xu Jian.
