Title: Harbin to Establish Air Link with the Soviet Union
Author: Jiang Yaobo
Date: 1989-04-01
Edition: 2

Harbin to Establish Air Link with the Soviet Union

According to Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, March 31 (Reporter Jiang Yaobo) The first international air route of Heilongjiang Province—from Harbin to the Soviet border city of Khabarovsk—will commence operations in July this year.

### 哈尔滨将同苏联通民航
蒋耀波
1989-04-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　哈尔滨将同苏联通民航
    据新华社哈尔滨３月３１日电  （记者蒋耀波）黑龙江省第一条民航国际航线——哈尔滨至苏联边境城市哈巴罗夫斯克的航班，将于今年７月通航。
