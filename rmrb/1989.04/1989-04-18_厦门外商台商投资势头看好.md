Title: Foreign and Taiwanese Investment in Xiamen Shows Positive Momentum
Author:
Date: 1989-04-18
Edition: 2

	Xiamen's economic zone, building on last year's significant increase in foreign investment, approved 51 new projects in the first quarter of this year. The agreed investment amount reached over $54 million, marking a 17.8% and 12.6% increase respectively compared to the same period last year.

	By the end of March this year, Xiamen had approved a total of 526 foreign and Taiwanese investment projects, with Taiwanese projects accounting for about one-quarter. Among these, 273 foreign and Taiwanese enterprises have started production or operations, with Taiwanese enterprises making up nearly one-fifth. Additionally, 135 foreign companies have been granted permission to establish resident representative offices in Xiamen.

### 厦门外商台商投资势头看好

1989-04-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　厦门外商台商投资势头看好
    据新华社厦门４月１８日电　今年第一季度厦门经济特区在去年外资投资大幅度增长的基础上，又批准了５１个项目，协议投资额达５４００多万美元，分别比去年同期增加１７．８％和１２．６％。
    到今年３月底为止，厦门共批准的外资、台资项目５２６个，其中台资项目占１／４左右。已投产、开业的外资、台资企业２７３家，其中台资企业占近１／５。还有１３５家外国企业获准在厦门设立常驻代表机构。
