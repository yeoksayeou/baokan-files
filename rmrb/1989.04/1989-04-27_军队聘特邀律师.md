Title: The PLA Hires Special Invited Lawyers
Author: Yang Xue Min
Date: 1989-04-27
Edition: 2

The first batch of special invited lawyers for the People's Liberation Army recently received the "Military Lawyer Work Certificate" issued by the General Political Department.

Currently, 54 military-level and above units in the entire army have established legal advisory institutions and assigned full-time or part-time legal personnel. The 89 people in the first batch are all retired political and legal cadres from the military, with rich legal knowledge and experience.

### 军队聘特邀律师
杨学民
1989-04-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　军队聘特邀律师
    新华社北京４月２６日电（通讯员杨学民）解放军首批特邀律师近日接受了总政治部颁发的《军队律师工作证》。
    目前，全军已有５４个军以上单位设立了法律顾问机构，配备专职或兼职法律工作人员。首批受聘的８９人都是军队离退休的政法干部，有丰富的法律知识和经验。
