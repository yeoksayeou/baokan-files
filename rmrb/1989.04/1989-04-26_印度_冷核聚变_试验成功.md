Title: India's "Cold Fusion" Experiment Succeeds
Author:
Date: 1989-04-26
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 26th.

Indian scientists have replicated the room-temperature nuclear fusion experiments conducted by Fleischmann and Pons in an electrolytic cell, achieving nuclear fusion.

The message states that the experiment, conducted by the Indira Gandhi Atomic Research Center, differs slightly from the experiment at the University of Utah. They inserted a titanium cathode and a platinum anode into heavy water, whereas Pons used a very expensive palladium as the cathode.

The Indian scientists' experiment achieved "cold fusion" three days after it began, on April 16th.

### 印度“冷核聚变”试验成功

1989-04-26
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　印度“冷核聚变”试验成功
    新华社北京４月２６日电  新德里消息：印度科学家重复弗莱希曼和庞斯进行室温核聚变的实验，在电解池中实现了核聚变。
    消息说，由英迪拉·甘地原子研究中心进行的这项试验，与美国犹他大学的试验稍有不同。他们是将一个钛阴极和一个铂阳极插入重水，而庞斯他们用的是一种非常昂贵的钯作阴极。
    印度科学家的这项试验进行３天后，于４月１６日实现了“冷核聚变”。
