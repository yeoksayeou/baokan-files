Title: Queen Beatrix of the Netherlands to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1989-04-27
Edition: 1

At the invitation of President Yang Shangkun of the People's Republic of China, Her Majesty Queen Beatrix and Prince Claus of the Netherlands will pay a state visit to China from May 24 to 30, 1989.

### 荷兰女王贝娅特丽克丝将访华

1989-04-27
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　荷兰女王贝娅特丽克丝将访华
    本报北京４月２７日讯  应中华人民共和国主席杨尚昆的邀请，荷兰女王贝娅特丽克丝陛下和克劳斯亲王殿下将于１９８９年５月２４日至３０日对中国进行国事访问。
