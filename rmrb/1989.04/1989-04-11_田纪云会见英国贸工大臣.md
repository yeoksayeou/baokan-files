Title: Vice Premier Tian Jiyun Meets with British Minister of Trade and Industry
Author: 
Date: 1989-04-11
Edition: 2

Vice Premier Tian Jiyun said today that China will place great importance on the inspection of the quality of its exported goods and strive to improve the quality of its products and services.

Tian Jiyun made these remarks this morning at the Great Hall of the People when he met with Lord Young, the British Minister of Trade and Industry. He expressed satisfaction with the development of trade between China and Britain in recent years. He said: "Last year, the total trade volume between China and Britain reached more than $1.5 billion. Although the figure is not large, it shows good prospects for development. We will further open up our domestic market and, through joint efforts, push the development of Sino-British trade to a new height." Tian Jiyun welcomed British businesspeople to invest in China and establish wholly-owned or joint ventures. He hoped that the two sides would cooperate in the field of high technology.

Experts pointed out that in 1988, the total trade volume between China and Britain was $1.55 billion.

### 田纪云会见英国贸工大臣
杨国强
1989-04-11
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　田纪云会见英国贸工大臣
    据新华社北京４月１０日电　（记者杨国强）国务院副总理田纪云今天在这里说，中国将把出口商品质量的检验工作放在十分重要的地位，努力提高商品的质量并改善服务。
    田纪云今天上午在人民大会堂会见英国贸易和工业大臣扬勋爵时，对近年来中英两国贸易的发展表示满意。他说：“去年，中英双边贸易总额达到１５亿多美元，数字虽不大，但显示了较好的发展前景。我们将进一步开放国内市场，并通过双边共同的努力，把中英贸易的发展推向一个新的高度。”田纪云欢迎英国企业家到中国投资，开办独资、合资企业。他希望中英双方在高技术领域开展合作。
    据专家介绍，１９８８年，中英贸易总额为１５．５亿美元。
