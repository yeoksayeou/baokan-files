Title: Domestic 600,000 Kilowatt Unit Begins Trial Operation in Pingxun
Author: Zhao Mingliang
Date: 1989-04-04
Edition: 2

The Ministry of Energy announced that China's first domestically produced 600,000 kilowatt generator set has begun trial operations at the Pingxun Power Plant in Anhui Province and will soon be officially put into production. China's power construction has made new progress this year. Three generator sets have been put into production in the first quarter.

### 国产六十万千瓦机组平圩试运行
赵明亮
1989-04-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　国产六十万千瓦机组平圩试运行
    据新华社北京电  （记者赵明亮）能源部宣布，我国自行制造的第一台６０万千瓦发电机组已在安徽平圩电厂试运行，近期内将可正式投产。我国电力建设今年取得新的进展。第一季度已有３台发电机组投产。
