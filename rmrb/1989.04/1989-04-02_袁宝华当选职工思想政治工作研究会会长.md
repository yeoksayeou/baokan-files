Title: Yuan Baohua Elected Chairman of the Workers' Ideological and Political Work Research Association
Author: Fei Qiang
Date: 1989-04-02
Edition: 2

Yuan Baohua Elected Chairman of the Workers' Ideological and Political Work Research Association

According to Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, April 1 - (Reporter Fei Qiang) The fifth annual meeting of the China Workers' Ideological and Political Work Research Association elected 198 directors. Yuan Baohua was elected as the chairman of the association, and CPC Central Committee Secretariat member Rui Xingwen was elected as honorary chairman.

### 袁宝华当选职工思想政治工作研究会会长
费强
1989-04-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　袁宝华当选职工思想政治工作研究会会长
    据新华社南京４月１日电  （记者费强）中国职工思想政治工作研究会第五届年会今天选出１９８名理事，袁宝华当选为研究会会长，中共中央书记处书记芮杏文为名誉会长。
