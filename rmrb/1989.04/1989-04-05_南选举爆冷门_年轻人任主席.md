Title: Surprise in South: Young Man Elected as Chairman
Author: Yan Zheng
Date: 1989-04-05
Edition: 4

Today, various news media in Yugoslavia prominently reported that the voters of Slovenia Republic elected, through secret ballot, the 39-year-old economist Dr. Janez Drnovšek as a member of the new Federal Presidency to take office next month. According to the established rotation sequence, he will serve as the next chairman of the Federal Presidency and will preside over the Ninth Summit of Non-Aligned Countries to be held this September. This is considered a major surprise in the current process of electing the Federal Presidency.

According to the Yugoslav Constitution, a new Presidency will be formed in mid-May this year, consisting of one representative from each of the six republics and two autonomous provinces.

### 南选举爆冷门  年轻人任主席
严正
1989-04-05
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　南选举爆冷门  年轻人任主席
    本报贝尔格莱德４月４日电  记者严正报道：今天南斯拉夫各新闻媒介以引人注目的方式报道斯洛文尼亚共和国的选民以无记名投票选举出３９岁的经济学博士亚·德尔诺夫舍克为下月上任的南联邦新主席团成员，按照既定的轮换次序，他将担任下一任联邦主席团主席之职，并主持将于今年９月召开的第九次不结盟国家首脑会议，这被认为是当前南联邦主席团选举过程中爆出的特大冷门。
    根据南斯拉夫宪法，今年５月中旬，南联邦将产生新的主席团，由６个共和国、两个自治省各选出１名代表组成。
