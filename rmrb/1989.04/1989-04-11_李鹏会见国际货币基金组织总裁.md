Title: Premier Li Peng Meets with IMF Managing Director
Author: Zhang Rongdian
Date: 1989-04-11
Edition: 1

Premier Li Peng today said that it is necessary to handle the relationship among development, reform, and stability, and to push forward reform while maintaining stability to achieve economic development. Li Peng made these remarks this morning when he met with Michel Camdessus, Managing Director of the International Monetary Fund, and his delegation in the Zhongnanhai's Ziguangge Hall.

Li Peng thanked the IMF for its assistance to China and briefed the guests on China's reform efforts.

Camdessus expressed his understanding of the importance of stability for China's development. He said that the IMF hopes China will maintain its reform momentum and achieve greater success in construction and reform. He also expressed the IMF's willingness to continue providing assistance to China in terms of funds and technology.

### 李鹏会见国际货币基金组织总裁
张荣典
1989-04-11
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　李鹏会见国际货币基金组织总裁
    据新华社北京四月十日电　（记者张荣典）国务院总理李鹏今天在这里说，必须处理好发展、改革、稳定三者之间的关系，在稳定中推进改革，求得经济的发展。李鹏是今天上午在中南海紫光阁会见国际货币基金组织总裁米歇尔·康德苏一行时说这番话的。
    李鹏在会见康德苏一行时感谢国际货币基金组织对中国的帮助，并向客人们介绍了中国的改革情况。
    康德苏表示理解稳定对中国发展的重要性。他说，国际货币基金组织希望中国保持改革的进程，并在建设和改革中取得更大的成就。他表示，国际货币基金组织愿意在资金、技术方面继续对中国提供帮助。
