	Skateboarding

	In 1962, two surfers from California, USA, in order to practice surfing on land, attached four small wheels to a surfboard, drawing public attention. This later evolved into the unique sport of skateboarding, which became popular in some European and American countries. This sport, which simulates surfing on land, now includes speed skating, giant slalom, slalom, and freestyle competitions.

### 滑板（图片）

1989-04-23
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　滑板
    １９６２年，美国加利福尼亚州两名滑水运动员为在陆地上进行滑水训练，在滑水板下面装上四个小轮，引起人们注意，后来逐渐发展成独特的滑板运动，风行于欧美一些国家。这种在陆地上模拟水面滑行的运动，现在已有速滑、大回转、小回转和造型等比赛。
