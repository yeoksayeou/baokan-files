Title: The Great Proletarian Revolutionary and Statesman Comrade Hu Yaobang Lives Forever
Author:
Date: 1989-04-22
Edition: 3

The great proletarian revolutionary and statesman Comrade Hu Yaobang lives forever.

	Left image: In June 1953, Hu Yaobang addressed the Second National Congress of the New Democratic Youth League of China.

	Top image: On March 14, 1982, Hu Yaobang had a cordial conversation with soldiers of the People's Liberation Army who were participating in tree-planting activities in the suburbs of Beijing.

	Left image: On June 13, 1980, Hu Yaobang met with journalists from Yugoslavia stationed in Beijing.

	Top image: On February 16, 1983, Hu Yaobang visited veteran comrades such as Shuai Mengqi (second from the left, front row) in Changsha.

	Right image: In September 1985, Hu Yaobang returned to the Long March route, riding a horse to ascend Huanglong Mountain in Songpan County, Sichuan.

	Top image: On June 12, 1981, Hu Yaobang received students from the Tibetan class of the Shanghai Drama Academy in Zhongnanhai.

1989-04-22
第3版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    伟大的无产阶级革命家政治家胡耀邦同志永垂不朽
    左图：1953年6月，胡耀邦在中国新民主主义青年团第二次全国代表大会上作报告。
    上图：一九八二年三月十四日，胡耀邦在北京郊区同参加植树的解放军战士亲切交谈。
    左图：一九八○年六月十三日，胡耀邦会见南斯拉夫驻北京记者。
