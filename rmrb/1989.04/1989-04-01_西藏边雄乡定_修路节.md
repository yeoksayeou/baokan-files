Title: Tibet's Bianxiong Township Establishes "Road Repair Day"
Author:
Date: 1989-04-01
Edition: 2

	Today, March 9th, is the "Road Repair Day" as stipulated in the village regulations of Bianxiong Township, Dayu County, Tibet. Villagers from various villages have come out to repair and renovate large and small roads and bridges.

	Some villagers have reported to the township government that although life has improved, walking has become a problem. They requested that the township government lead the farmers in repairing the roads once a year. The township government, in response to the wishes of the people, has designated March 9th, just before the spring plowing season, as "Road Repair Day."

### 西藏边雄乡定“修路节”

1989-04-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　西藏边雄乡定“修路节”
    据新华社拉萨３月３１日电  （新华社记者、中央人民广播电台记者）今年３月９日，是西藏日喀则市边雄乡乡规民约的“修路节”，各村村民出动将大小道路、桥梁整修一新。
    一些群众向乡政府反映，如今生活好了，就是走路使人发愁，要求乡政府每年带领农民集中整修一次道路。乡政府根据群众意愿，将春耕来临前的每年３月９日定为“修路节”。
