Title: Conflict in Northern Namibia Continues
Author: Li Kai Chen
Date: 1989-04-04
Edition: 4

Conflict in Northern Namibia Continues
SWAPO Condemns South African Attack

	NAMIBIA, April 2 (Xinhua) -- The armed conflict between South Africa-backed Namibian police and SWAPO guerrillas in northern Namibia continued on April 2. The United Nations has dispatched an investigation team to the affected area.

	The South African police commander in Namibia, Durand, said in Windhoek that more than 120 SWAPO fighters and six police officers had been killed in the ongoing clashes.

	The conflict erupted just before a ceasefire agreement between SWAPO and South Africa came into effect on April 1. According to the agreement, SWAPO's armed forces were to remain in their bases starting from the effective date of the ceasefire.

	The UN Transitional Assistance Group's peacekeeping force commander, Prem Chand, and a four-member investigation team returned to Windhoek on April 2. Chand told Xinhua that the team was busy drafting an investigation report.

	NEW YORK, April 2 (Xinhua) -- South African helicopters attacked two villages in the Ondangwa region of northern Namibia on April 1, killing eight people who were gathered to support the start of Namibia's transition to independence.

	This was announced today by SWAPO spokesperson Gribb.

	He accused South Africa of falsely claiming that SWAPO guerrillas had infiltrated from Angola into northern Namibia, leading to armed clashes with South African "guerrillas." Gribb stated that this claim does not reflect the true situation.

### 纳北部冲突仍在继续  西南非洲人民组织谴责南非袭击
刘开宸
1989-04-04
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　纳北部冲突仍在继续
    西南非洲人民组织谴责南非袭击
    新华社温得和克４月２日电  南非组建的纳米比亚警察和西南非洲人民组织武装人员在北部地区发生的武装冲突２日仍在继续进行。联合国已派调查小组前往出事地区。
    南非驻纳米比亚警察总监杜兰德在温得和克说，１２０多名西南非洲人民组织的战士和６名警察在持续的冲突中丧生。
    这场冲突是在西南非洲人民组织和南非之间的停火协议１日上午生效前爆发的。根据这项协议，自协议生效之日起，西南非洲人民组织的武装部应留在自己的基地里。
    派往纳米比亚北部进行调查的联合国过渡时期援助团维持和平部队司令普雷姆·昌德和４人调查小组２日下午返回温得和克。昌德对新华社记者说，这个小组正忙于起草调查报告。
    本报联合国４月２日电　记者刘开宸报道：南非直升机４月１日飞临纳米比亚北部翁丹格瓦地区两个村庄的上空，袭击正在集会支持纳米比亚独立过渡时期开始的群众，有８人死亡。
    这是西南非洲人民组织（简称“人组”）发言人格里布今天在这里宣布的。
    他指责南非关于“人组”游击队从安哥拉向纳米比亚北部边境“渗透”因而与南非的“游击队”发生武装冲突的说法，是不符合事实的。
