Beijing's Comprehensive Improvement of the Social Environment

	Beijing, April 12 (Xinhua) -- The capital Beijing has launched a comprehensive campaign to improve the social environment, focusing on "strengthening spiritual civilization construction and improving the social environment." The goal is to present the city in its best condition for the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the 11th Asian Games.

	Today's afternoon, the Beijing Municipal Committee and the Municipal Government held a mass mobilization meeting.

	The main tasks for Beijing's environmental improvement over the next two years include strengthening propaganda and education to raise the social ethics and civility of all citizens; improving and beautifying the urban landscape, with a focus on areas around the Asian Games venues and 100 key streets; and enhancing social security and improving service quality.

### 北京综合治理社会环境
赵兴林
1989-04-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　北京综合治理社会环境
    本报北京4月12日讯　记者赵兴林报道：首都北京以加强“精神文明建设，搞好社会环境”为内容的综合治理，业已拉开序幕，争取以最佳状态迎接建国40周年和第11届亚运会。
    今天下午，北京市委、市政府召开了万人动员大会。
    北京市今明两年环境建设主要是加强宣传教育，提高全体市民的社会公德和文明礼貌水平；整治改善市容面貌，重点抓好亚运会赛场周围地区及100条街道；搞好社会治安，改进和提高服务工作。
