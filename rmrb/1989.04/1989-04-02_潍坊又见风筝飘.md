Title: Kites Again Fly in Weifang
Author: Zhong Xi
Date: 1989-04-02
Edition: 2

The annual International Kite Festival opened yesterday in "The World Kite Capital"—Weifang. Weifang City's grand industrial products trade fair will be held simultaneously.

### 潍坊又见风筝飘
钟西
1989-04-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　潍坊又见风筝飘
    本报山东潍坊４月１日电  一年一度的国际风筝会昨天在“世界风筝城”——潍坊再度拉开帷幕。潍坊市盛大的工业产品交易会将同时举行。（钟西）
