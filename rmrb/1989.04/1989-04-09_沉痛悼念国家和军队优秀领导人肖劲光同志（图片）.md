	In mourning of the outstanding leader of the state and the military, Comrade Xiao Jingguang

	(Top) In October 1959, Chairman Mao Zedong met with representatives attending the National Day celebrations and had a cordial conversation with Xiao Jingguang.

	(Middle) On August 4, 1957, Xiao Jingguang accompanied Premier Zhou Enlai in reviewing the Navy units stationed in Qingdao.

	(Bottom) In January 1981, Deng Xiaoping had a cordial conversation with Xiao Jingguang (second from the left) and others.
