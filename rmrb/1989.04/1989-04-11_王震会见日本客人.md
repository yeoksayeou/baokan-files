Author:
Date: 1989-04-11
Edition: 2

Wang Zhen Meets with Japanese Guests

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 10th - Vice Chairman of the State Wang Zhen this afternoon met with a delegation led by former Chairman of the Joint Staff Council of the Japan Self-Defense Forces, Hiroshi Kurusu, at the Great Hall of the People.

Wang Zhen, in response to the guests' inquiries about China's grain situation, said that China has a large population but limited land, and grain production cannot keep up with the population growth. He said, "Although there are difficulties, we can still manage." He informed the guests that since last winter, China has increased investment in agriculture, mobilized farmers to engage in basic construction, and improved the ability to resist natural disasters.

Wang Zhen also responded to questions regarding China's education.

### 王震会见日本客人

1989-04-11
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　王震会见日本客人
    据新华社北京４月１０日电　国家副主席王震今天下午在人民大会堂会见了日本自卫队前参谋长联席会议主席栗栖弘臣一行。
    王震在回答客人们提出的中国粮食问题时说，中国人多地少，粮食增长赶不上人口增长的需要。他说，虽然有困难，还是能够过。他告诉客人们，去年冬天以来中国加强了对农业的投入，动员农民搞基本建设，提高抗御自然灾害的能力。
    王震还回答了有关中国的教育问题。
