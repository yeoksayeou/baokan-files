Title: China Leads the World in Number of Students Studying Abroad
Author:
Date: 1989-04-02
Edition: 8

China is currently the country with the most students studying abroad. In the 1987-1988 academic year, the total number of mainland Chinese students studying abroad was 42,481.

This is according to a recent official report by the International Education Association, published in its annual report titled "Open Doors." The report notes that the most popular destination for mainland Chinese students is the United States, with 25,100 students; followed by Canada with 11,170 students, West Germany with 1,117 students, and the United Kingdom with 410 students. The number of students studying in the Soviet Union was not included in the statistics.

(From the United Daily News, March 3)

### 中国留学生居世界之首

1989-04-02
第8版(每周文摘)
专栏：

　　中国留学生居世界之首
    中国目前是留学生出国人数最多的国家。在１９８７年─１９８８年学年，中国大陆留学生总计４２４８１人。
    这是国际教育协会最近正式发布的一项为《门户开放》的年度报告中所刊列的统计。该报告指出，中国大陆留学生赴美国为最多，共２５１００人；其次为去加拿大的，去联邦德国的１１１７人，去英国的４１０人，留苏的未列入统计。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（３月３日《联合时报》）
