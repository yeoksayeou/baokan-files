Title: Prince Norodom Sihanouk Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-04-24
Edition: 1

Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Chairman of the Democratic Kampuchea, and his wife arrived in Beijing this morning by special plane from Pyongyang.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qi Huaiyuan and the Democratic Kampuchea Ambassador to China, Jiang Yulang, were at the airport to welcome them.

### 西哈努克亲王抵京

1989-04-24
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　西哈努克亲王抵京
    新华社北京４月２４日电  民主柬埔寨主席诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和夫人今天上午乘专机从平壤抵达北京。
    外交部副部长齐怀远、民柬驻华大使江裕朗到机场迎接。
