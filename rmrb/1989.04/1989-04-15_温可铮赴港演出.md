Title: Wen Kezheng Performs in Hong Kong
Author:
Date: 1989-04-15
Edition: 4

At the invitation of the Urban Council of Hong Kong, Professor Wen Kezheng, a male bass singer, will hold a solo concert at the Hong Kong City Hall on April 19. He will also be appointed as a visiting professor at the Chinese University of Hong Kong to give lectures and conduct a vocal art "Master Class" seminar.

### 温可铮赴港演出
文一
1989-04-15
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　温可铮赴港演出
    应香港市政总署的邀请，男低音歌唱家温可铮教授将于四月十九日在香港大会堂举办个人独唱会，并在香港中文大学应聘客座教授讲学，同时举办声乐艺术“大师班”讲座。　　　　
　　　　（文一）
