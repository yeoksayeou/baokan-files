Title: The Wildfire in Hama Gorge Has Been Completely Extinguished
Author:
Date: 1989-04-06
Edition: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 5th.

The State Forest Fire Command Headquarters Office announced at 23:00 today: The wildfire in Hama Gorge, Kerqin Right Front Banner, Xing'an League, Inner Mongolia, has been completely extinguished.

### 蛤蟆沟荒火全部扑灭

1989-04-06
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　蛤蟆沟荒火全部扑灭
    据新华社北京４月５日电  国家森林防火总指挥部办公室今天２３时告知：内蒙古兴安盟科右前旗蛤蟆沟荒火已全部扑灭。
