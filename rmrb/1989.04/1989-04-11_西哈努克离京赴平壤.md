Title: Norodom Sihanouk Leaves Beijing for Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1989-04-11
Edition: 2

Democratic Kampuchea Chairman Prince Norodom Sihanouk and his wife left Beijing this morning for Pyongyang. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qi Huaiyuan saw them off at the airport.

	According to a report from Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 10: Democratic Kampuchea Chairman Prince Norodom Sihanouk and his wife left Beijing this morning for Pyongyang. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qi Huaiyuan saw them off at the airport.

	According to a report from People's Daily, April 8: Minister Qian Qichen hosted a farewell dinner at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse for Prince Norodom Sihanouk, who is about to visit the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. Both sides exchanged views on the Cambodian issue.

### 西哈努克离京赴平壤

1989-04-11
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　西哈努克离京赴平壤
    新华社北京４月１０日电　民主柬埔寨主席诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和夫人今天上午离开北京前往平壤。外交部副部长齐怀远到机场送行。
    本报讯　钱其琛部长４月８日晚在钓鱼台国宾馆设宴，为即将访问朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的西哈努克亲王饯行。双方就柬埔寨问题交换了意见。
