Title: The China Youth Art Theatre Celebrates 40 Years with Over 100 Performances
Author: Hao Qing
Date: 1989-04-17
Edition: 4

Today, the China Youth Art Theatre held a grand reunion to celebrate its 40th anniversary.

The China Youth Art Theatre was born in the same year as the People's Republic of China. It primarily serves the youth, striving to authentically reflect the historical and contemporary struggles of the people, and focusing on creating various character roles that young people love. To adapt to and meet the growing aesthetic needs of the youth, the theatre has, over the past 40 years, performed 122 full-length plays and 77 one-act plays, forming a rich and colorful repertoire.

Over the past 40 years, the theatre has cultivated a large number of famous directors, playwrights, critics, and actors for the arts.

### 青艺四十年演戏百余部
郝庆
1989-04-17
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　青艺四十年演戏百余部
    新华社北京４月１６日电　（记者郝庆）今天，中国青年艺术剧院举行盛大联谊会，庆祝建院４０周年。
    中国青年艺术剧院与中华人民共和国同年诞生。它以青年为主要服务对象，力求真实地反映人民历史和现实斗争生活，着力创造为青年们所喜爱的各种人物形象。为了适应和满足青年日益增长的审美需要，剧院４０年来，共演出多幕剧１２２部、独幕剧７７部，形成了一个丰富多彩的剧目画廊。
    ４０年来，剧院为文艺事业培养出了一大批著名导演、编剧、评论家和演员。
