Title: West German Foreign Minister Genscher Emphasizes Tibet is Part of China
Author:
Date: 1989-04-12
Edition: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Bonn, April 10th.

West German Foreign Minister Hans-Dietrich Genscher today emphasized that Tibet is a part of Chinese territory, which is beyond doubt.

Genscher made these remarks during a meeting with Zhou Nan, the Vice Minister of the Chinese Foreign Ministry, who is currently visiting West Germany.

Genscher also expressed that the West German government respects the sovereignty and territorial integrity of all countries.

Today, West German Minister for Economic Cooperation Klaus Kinkel and State Secretary of the Foreign Ministry Jürgen Sudhoff also held talks with Vice Minister Zhou Nan. Both sides exchanged views on the new developments in the current international situation, regional issues in Cambodia, and the development of bilateral relations.

Vice Minister Zhou Nan arrived in West Germany on April 7th at the invitation of State Secretary Jürgen Sudhoff. Zhou Nan will depart West Germany tomorrow to return to China.

### 西德外长根舍强调　西藏是中国一部分

1989-04-12
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　西德外长根舍强调
　　西藏是中国一部分
    新华社波恩4月10日电　联邦德国外长根舍今天在这里强调，西藏是中国领土的一部分，这是不容置疑的。
    根舍是在会见正在这里访问的中国外交部副部长周南时说这番话的。
    根舍还表示，联邦德国政府尊重各国主权和领土完整的原则。
    联邦德国经济合作部长克莱因和外交部国务秘书聚德霍夫今天分别同周南副外长进行了会谈，双方就当前国际形势发展的新动向、柬埔寨地区性问题和发展双边关系广泛地交换了意见。
    周南副外长是应聚德霍夫的邀请于7日到达联邦德国访问的。周南将于明天离开联邦德国回国。
