Title: Brief News
Author:
Date: 1989-04-27
Edition: 4

	The 4th National Human Body Beauty Exhibition will officially begin on May 3rd at the Beijing Natural History Museum. During the exhibition, knowledge and methods of human body fitness will be introduced, and consultations on fitness training will also be provided.

	The 4th National Sports Goods Exhibition and Trade Fair will be held in Beijing on April 27th. This event aims to promote the development of China's sports goods industry and meet the needs of people for sports training.

### 简讯

1989-04-27
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　简讯
    ▲全国人体美展览将于５月３日正式开始在北京自然博物馆举行，展览期间将介绍人体健美知识和方法，同时还进行健美锻炼咨询。
    ▲全国体育用品展览交易会４月２７日在京举行。这次活动旨在推动我国体育用品的发展，满足人们体育锻炼的需求。
