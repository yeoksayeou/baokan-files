Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Pakistani Friends
Author:
Date: 1968-07-17
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council met today with a delegation from the West Pakistan Industrial Development Corporation led by Bashir, the General Manager of the Heavy Machinery Division. They engaged in friendly conversations. Pakistan's Ambassador to China, Sultan M. Khan, was also present at the meeting.

### 陈毅副总理接见巴基斯坦朋友

1968-07-17
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见巴基斯坦朋友
    新华社十六日讯　国务院副总理陈毅，今天接见了由巴基斯坦西巴工业发展公司重机处总经理巴锡斯率领的巴基斯坦西巴工业发展公司代表团，同他们进行了友好的谈话。接见时，巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗也在座。
