Title: Albanian People Warmly Celebrate the Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Army
Author:
Date: 1968-07-09
Edition: 5

	Albanian people nationwide have recently held various activities to warmly celebrate the 25th anniversary of the founding of the heroic Albanian People's Army (July 10).

	In the capital, Tirana, writers, artists, and cultural workers recently met with the leaders of the People's Army. Dum, a candidate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour and Chief of the General Staff of the People's Army, spoke to the attendees about the glorious path the Albanian People's Army has taken under the leadership of Comrade Enver Hoxha over the past 25 years. He introduced the situation of the armed forces implementing the resolutions of the Fifth Congress of the Labour Party. The Association of Old Warriors recently organized a meeting between old warriors, families of martyrs, and responsible officials of the People's Army. In recent days, amateur performance troupes from various units have been performing for the people of the capital every night in Skanderbeg Square. The amateur cultural troupes of Skodra District and Stalin City are performing the musicals "One Family, Soldiers and Civilians" and "Glory to the Partisans" at the People's Theatre and the Variety Theatre, respectively.

	The troops stationed in Berat, Vlora, Mat, Librazhd, and other districts recently held get-togethers with local workers, members of agricultural cooperatives, and students. They organized discussions on themes such as "The 25 Years of the People's Army," "Our Army is a Political Army," and "Our People's Army Further Carries Forward the Traditions of the National Liberation War." At the same time, the amateur cultural troupes of Korça and Mat districts are touring factories and villages, both locally and externally, to perform with the masses and celebrate the festival together.

	To celebrate the 25th anniversary of the founding of the People's Army, the Albanian Ministry of National Defense and the "New Albania" Film Studio jointly organized a film week, screening films such as "Storm on the Coast." Cultural palaces and creation homes across the country have held lectures and exhibitions of paintings, sculptures, and photographs reflecting the military training, labor, and political activities of the troops. They have also screened documentaries introducing the life of the troops. Workers in Durres and other districts visited military camps, discussing with the soldiers of the People's Army the achievements made in completing the tasks of the third year of the Five-Year Plan and improving political ideology. They also exchanged experiences in studying the teachings of the Party and Comrade Hoxha. Many young people and pioneers organized marches, visited the National Liberation War Museum, and paid tribute to the martyrs' monuments. The Presidium of the Albanian People's Assembly recently awarded military flags to some outstanding units, encouraging them to maintain the battle honors of the martyrs and always hold high the banner of the Party in the struggle to defend the motherland and socialism, as well as in various political activities.

	To celebrate the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Albanian People's Army, the Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of Albania and the Writers and Artists Association jointly organized the "25 Years of the People's Army" exhibition, which opened on July 7 at the Cultural Palace in Tirana.

	The great leader of the Albanian people, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour, Comrade Enver Hoxha, and other Party and state leaders such as Shehu, Charçani, Balluku, Nushi, Toska, Muftiu, Alija, Hazbi, Dum, and Spasiu, Secretary of the Central Committee, and relevant department heads attended the opening ceremony.

	Mero, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Albanian People's Army, spoke at the opening ceremony.

	The exhibition features many oil paintings, sculptures, and photographs that enthusiastically praise the Albanian Party of Labour and the great leader of the Albanian people, Comrade Enver Hoxha. They vividly reflect the glorious fighting journey of the Albanian People's Army over the past 25 years and the high qualities of this army in being loyal to the revolutionary cause and the socialist motherland.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民热烈庆祝人民军建军节  阿人民伟大领袖霍查同志等出席“人民军的二十五年”展览会开幕式

1968-07-09
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民热烈庆祝人民军建军节
    阿人民伟大领袖霍查同志等出席“人民军的二十五年”展览会开幕式
    新华社地拉那八日电阿尔巴尼亚全国人民最近纷纷举行各种活动，热烈庆祝英勇的阿尔巴尼亚人民军建军二十五周年（七月十日）。
    在首都地拉那，作家、艺术家和文艺工作者最近同人民军领导干部举行了会见。阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局候补委员、人民军总参谋长杜米，向出席会见的人讲述了阿尔巴尼亚人民军在霍查同志领导下二十五年来所经历的光荣道路，介绍了武装部队贯彻劳动党第五次代表大会决议等情况。老战士协会不久前也组织了老战士、烈士家属同人民军负责干部的会见。一些部队业余演出团体连日来每天晚上在“斯坎德培”广场为首都人民演出。斯库台区和斯大林城的战士业余文工团，正分别在人民剧院和杂剧院上演歌舞剧《军民一家人》和《光荣属于游击队员》。
    培拉特、发罗拉、马蒂、利布拉什德等区的驻军，最近同当地的工人、农业生产合作社社员和学生举行联欢，组织了以“人民军的二十五年”、“我们的军队是政治的军队”、“我们的人民军进一步发扬民族解放战争的传统”等为主题的座谈会。与此同时，科尔察和马蒂等区的战士业余文工团还到本地和外地的工厂、农村进行巡回演出，同群众一起共庆节日。
    为庆祝人民军建军二十五周年，阿尔巴尼亚国防部和“新阿尔巴尼亚”电影制片厂联合组织了电影周，放映《海岸风雷》等影片。各地文化宫和创作之家纷纷举办讲座和反映部队军训、劳动、政治活动的绘画、雕塑和图片展览会，放映介绍部队生活的纪录片。都拉斯等区的职工参观了驻军营地，同人民军战士畅谈在完成五年计划第三年任务和提高政治思想方面所取得的成就，还交流了学习党和霍查同志教导的经验。许多青年和少先队员还组织行军、参观民族解放战争博物馆以及拜谒烈士纪念碑等活动。阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团最近授予一些优秀部队军旗，勉励他们保持先烈的战斗荣誉，在保卫祖国和社会主义的斗争中以及在各项政治活动中，永远高举党的旗帜。
    新华社地拉那八日电为庆祝阿尔巴尼亚人民军建军二十五周年，由阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国国防部和作家艺术家协会联合举办的“人民军的二十五年”展览会，七日在地拉那文化宫开幕。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩维尔·霍查同志，以及党和国家的其他领导人谢胡、查尔查尼、巴卢库、努什、托斯卡、穆夫蒂乌、阿利雅、哈兹比乌、杜米，党中央书记处书记斯巴修和有关部门的负责人等出席了开幕式。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民军政治部副主任梅罗在开幕式上讲了话。
    展览会展出的许多油画、雕塑和照片，热情地歌颂了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖霍查同志，生动地反映了阿尔巴尼亚人民军二十五年来所走过的光荣战斗道路和这支军队忠于革命事业，忠于社会主义祖国的崇高品质。
