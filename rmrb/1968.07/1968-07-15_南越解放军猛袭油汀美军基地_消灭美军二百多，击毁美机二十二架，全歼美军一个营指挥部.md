Title: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Launches Ferocious Attack on U.S. Military Base at Oil Ting
Author:
Date: 1968-07-15
Edition: 5

The South Vietnamese Liberation Army launched a fierce attack on the U.S. military base at Oil Ting in the early hours of July 3. The attack resulted in the annihilation of over 200 American invaders and the complete destruction of the command center of the U.S. Army's 4th Battalion, 22nd Regiment, 25th Infantry Division.

During this attack, the Liberation Army also destroyed 22 aircraft and 25 military vehicles, demolished two artillery positions, four bunkers, two fuel depots, numerous barracks, and a variety of military equipment.

The report states that after the battle commenced, the Liberation Army first bombarded the heart of the U.S. military base with a barrage of artillery shells. Then, infantry units swiftly breached the various defensive lines and launched a fierce assault on the key targets within the base. The enemy was thrown into chaos, and the base's artillery positions, airfield, and parking lot were completely paralyzed.

	New Hua Xinhua River News Agency, July 14, 1968

	According to a report from the South Vietnamese Liberation News Agency, the Liberation Armed Forces in various provinces of South Vietnam have been actively launching broad and continuous attacks in recent days, inflicting heavy casualties on the enemy.

	The report states that from June 2 to 9, the Liberation Armed Forces in the northern part of Quang Tri Province repeatedly attacked the enemy on the 9th Highway, killing over 700 enemy soldiers and destroying two aircraft and 20 military vehicles.

	On the morning of June 9, at around 8:00 AM, the Liberation Army ambushed a company of U.S. troops and a company of puppet troops near Viet My. As soon as the enemy entered the ambush zone, the soldiers opened fire and charged. A large number of enemy soldiers were killed on the spot, and the remaining enemy fled in disarray. At around 10:00 AM, the enemy sent a company of U.S. troops to reinforce the area, but they were met with fierce resistance from the Liberation Army. After a fierce battle, the enemy left behind 65 bodies. The Liberation Army then pursued the enemy, killing more. In the afternoon, the enemy sent another company to the area, but the Liberation Army's artillery immediately launched a fierce bombardment, and the infantry charged, killing nearly 100 enemy soldiers. By evening, the enemy attempted to reinforce the area again, but the Liberation Army continued to launch fierce attacks, killing nearly 50 more enemy soldiers. In total, the Liberation Army killed over 200 enemy soldiers and wounded many more in this one day.

	Meanwhile, the Liberation Army also ambushed an enemy convoy near Cam Lu, killing 30 U.S. soldiers and destroying seven military vehicles in the convoy. Previously, from June 2 to 8, the Liberation Army's artillery had bombarded enemy strongholds such as Hill 241 and conducted artillery strikes on enemy forces conducting "sweeping" operations in Dong Ha, Kun Tian, and Du Tho, killing 300 enemy soldiers and destroying three tanks and two transport vehicles.

	From June 7 to 9, the Liberation Army also launched fierce attacks on enemy forces parachuted into the southern region of Can Dong and ambushed an enemy convoy in the northeastern region of Gi Thang Village, killing over 170 U.S. invaders, destroying eight military vehicles, and shooting down two U.S. aircraft.

	According to another report, the Liberation Armed Forces in Binh Dinh Province, from May 21 to 31, annihilated and dispersed 508 enemy soldiers in the surrounding areas of Binh Dinh City.

	From May 26 to 27 and June 1 to 4, over four days, the military and civilians of Gia Lai Province attacked three enemy targets near Pleiku City and intercepted two enemy convoys on the 19th Highway, killing and wounding 120 enemy soldiers and destroying or damaging 41 military vehicles.

	On May 16, the Liberation Armed Forces in Tien Giang Province bravely intercepted U.S. aggressors conducting "sweeping" operations in the Dong Xuyen Commune, Anh Nhon Parish, Zhou Cheng County. After five consecutive counterattacks, they killed nearly 100 U.S. soldiers.

### 南越解放军猛袭油汀美军基地  消灭美军二百多，击毁美机二十二架，全歼美军一个营指挥部

1968-07-15
第5版()
专栏：

    南越解放军猛袭油汀美军基地
    消灭美军二百多，击毁美机二十二架，全歼美军一个营指挥部
    新华社河内十四日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方土龙木省解放军三日夜间猛烈袭击油汀美军基地，一举消灭美国侵略军二百多名，全歼美国第二十五步兵师第二十二团第四营指挥部。
    在这次袭击中，解放军还击毁飞机二十二架和军车二十五辆，摧毁敌人两个炮兵阵地、四座碉堡，烧毁汽油库两座、营房多幢和一批作战工具。
    报道说，解放军在战斗打响后，首先向美军基地中心区发射了一排排的炮弹，接着，步兵迅速地越过各道防线，猛烈进攻基地内的各个主要目标，敌人一片混乱，基地内的炮兵阵地、飞机场和停车场完全陷于瘫痪。
    新华社河内电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方各省人民解放武装力量连日来广泛主动出击，打死打伤很多敌人。
    报道说，广治省北部人民解放武装力量六月二日到九日期间在第九号公路上接连进攻敌人，共消灭敌人七百多名，击落飞机两架，击毁军车二十辆。
    六月九日早晨八时，解放军在越门附近伏击了一连美军和一连伪军。战士们等敌人进入伏击圈后就猛烈开火并发起冲锋，当场消灭一批敌人，残敌仓惶逃窜。上午十时，敌人出动一连美军前来增援，又遭到了解放军的猛烈截击。经过激烈的战斗，敌人丢下了六十五具尸体。接着，解放军乘胜追击，又消灭一批敌人。下午，敌人再次出动一连军队到这个地区，解放军炮兵立即予以猛烈炮击，步兵也发起冲锋，共消灭敌人近百名。到晚上，敌人又一次前来送死，解放军再接再厉，予以猛烈截击，歼敌近五十名。在这一天内，解放军共打死敌人二百多名，打伤多名。
    与此同时，解放军还在甘露附近地区伏击敌人车队，一举消灭美军三十名，全歼这个车队的七辆军车。在这以前，解放军炮兵从六月二日到八日，还轰击二四一号高地等敌军据点，并在东河、昆天、育庙等地炮击出来“扫荡”的敌人，共消灭敌人三百名，击毁坦克三辆、运输车两辆。
    解放军六月七日到九日还在溪山地区猛烈截击了空投到达根东南地区的敌人，并在吉塘村东北地区伏击了一支敌军车队，共消灭美国侵略军一百七十多名，击毁军车八辆，击落美机两架。
    另据报道，西宁省人民解放武装力量在五月二十一日到三十一日期间，在西宁市周围地区消灭和瓦解了敌人五百零八名。
    嘉莱省军民于五月二十六日、二十七日和六月一日、四日四天内，进攻了波来古市附近的三个敌军目标，截击了第十九号公路上的两个敌军车队，共打死打伤敌人一百二十名，击毁击伤军车四十一辆。
    永隆省人民解放武装力量，五月十六日在周城县东新润乡英勇截击前去“扫荡”的美国强盗，先后五次击退敌人的进攻，共消灭美军近一百名。
