Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Six American Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-07-04
Edition: 6

North Vietnamese military and civilians shot down six American aircraft.

According to a report from here: On June 30 and July 1, the military and civilians of the Vinh Linh area and Nghe An province in North Vietnam shot down four invading American aircraft in battles against American air pirates.

According to a supplementary report, the military and civilians of Quang Binh and Nghe An provinces also shot down two invading American pirate aircraft on June 26.

### 越南北方军民击落美机六架

1968-07-04
第6版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机六架
    新华社河内二日电　据这里报道：越南北方永灵区和义安省军民，六月三十日和七月一日在反击美国空中强盗的战斗中共击落入侵的美国飞机四架。
    另据补充报道，广平和义安两省军民六月二十六日还击落入侵的美国强盗飞机两架。
