Title: Yemen's Prime Minister Meets Chinese Chargé d'Affaires
Author:
Date: 1968-07-19
Edition: 6

Yemen's Prime Minister and Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces, Hassan Al-Amri, and Foreign Minister Hassan Al-Maiki received the Chinese Chargé d'Affaires to Yemen, Zheng Kangping, on the morning of the 16th in Sana'a and held a friendly and cordial conversation with him.

Those present at the reception included Yemen's Minister of Information, Ahmed Hussein Marwani, and Minister of Finance, Ahmed Abdul Said.

### 也门总理接见我临时代办

1968-07-19
第6版()
专栏：

    也门总理接见我临时代办
     新华社萨那十六日电　也门总理兼武装部队总司令哈桑·阿姆里和外交部长哈桑·迈基十六日上午在这里接见了中国驻也门大使馆临时代办郑康平，并同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    陪同接见的有也门新闻部长艾哈迈德·侯赛因·马尔瓦尼和财政部长艾哈迈德·阿卜杜·赛义德。
