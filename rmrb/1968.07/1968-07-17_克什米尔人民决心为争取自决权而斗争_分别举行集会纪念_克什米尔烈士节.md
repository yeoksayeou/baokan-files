Title: Kashmiris Resolve to Struggle for Self-Determination
Author:
Date: 1968-07-17
Edition: 5

Kashmiris living in the Pakistani-controlled areas of Kashmir and various parts of Pakistan held meetings and other commemorative activities on July 13 to mark "Kashmir Martyrs' Day."

In a public meeting held in the Poonch district, Azad Jammu and Kashmir President Abdul Hamid Khan reaffirmed the Kashmiris' resolve to struggle for self-determination. He said: "Nothing can stop the Kashmiris from fighting to liberate their homeland from the rule of Indian usurpers."

In the Pakistani capital, Rawalpindi, the Azad Jammu and Kashmir Muslim Conference held a meeting. Speakers at the meeting reaffirmed their determination to continue their struggle for self-determination with all their might.

In the capital of West Pakistan, Lahore, a public meeting was also held to commemorate "Kashmir Martyrs' Day."

Kashmir Freedom Movement General Secretary Amanullah Khan told the press that if the Kashmiris stood united and waged a fierce struggle, they would certainly overthrow Indian rule and the Kashmiris would exercise their right to self-determination.

### 克什米尔人民决心为争取自决权而斗争  分别举行集会纪念“克什米尔烈士节”

1968-07-17
第5版()
专栏：

    克什米尔人民决心为争取自决权而斗争
    分别举行集会纪念“克什米尔烈士节”
    新华社拉瓦尔品第十五日电　住在克什米尔巴基斯坦控制区和巴基斯坦各地的克什米尔人民，在十三日分别举行集会和其他纪念活动，纪念“克什米尔烈士节”。
    查谟和克什米尔自由政府主席阿卜杜勒·哈米德汗在庞契县举行的公众集会上发表讲话，重申克什米尔人民争取自决权的决心。他说：没有任何东西能阻止克什米尔人民为从印度篡夺者统治下解放自己的家园而进行的斗争。
    在巴基斯坦首都拉瓦尔品第，自由查谟和克什米尔穆斯林会议举行了集会。发言者在会上重申他们决心继续全力以赴，为争取自决权而斗争。
    在西巴基斯坦首府拉合尔，也举行了纪念“克什米尔烈士节”的公众集会。
    克什米尔公民投票阵线总书记阿曼努阿拉汗向报界发表谈话说，如果克什米尔人民团结一致，坚持展开猛烈的斗争，就一定能推翻印度的统治，克什米尔人民必将行使自己的自决权。
