Title: Thai Puppet Army Invades Cambodian Territory Three Times
Author:
Date: 1968-07-11
Edition: 6

	Thai puppet army invades Cambodian territory three times
	Cambodian Foreign Ministry lodges strong protest

	BEIJING, July 10 (Xinhua) -- According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency, the Cambodian Foreign Ministry sent a note to the Thai puppet regime on July 6, strongly protesting the Thai puppet army's three invasions of Cambodian territory in Battambang Province on June 9, 13, and 27, which injured four Cambodian civilians.

	The note demands that the Thai puppet regime stop these acts of aggression.

### 泰国傀儡军队三次侵犯柬埔寨领土  柬埔寨外交部提出强烈抗议

1968-07-11
第6版()
专栏：

    泰国傀儡军队三次侵犯柬埔寨领土
    柬埔寨外交部提出强烈抗议
    新华社金边十日电　据柬埔寨新闻社报道，柬埔寨外交部七月六日照会泰国傀儡当局，强烈抗议泰国傀儡军队六月九日、十三日和二十七日三次侵犯柬埔寨马德望省领土，打伤四名柬埔寨居民的罪行。
    照会要求泰国傀儡当局停止这种侵略行径。
