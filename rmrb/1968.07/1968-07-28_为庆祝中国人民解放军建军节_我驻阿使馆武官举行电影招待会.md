Title: Chinese Military Attache in Albania Holds Film Screening to Celebrate PLA's Founding Day
Author:
Date: 1968-07-28
Edition: 5

To celebrate the 41st anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the military attache of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, Li Zhongxuan, held a film screening on the evening of July 26 at the embassy.

The deputy head of the Political Department of the Albanian People's Army, Meri, and other officials from the Ministry of Defense and the Ministry of Public Security attended the film screening.

In a very friendly and brotherly atmosphere, the hosts and guests frequently raised their glasses to toast the revolutionary and combat-friendly relationship between the peoples and armies of China and Albania.

The film "The Battle of the Trenches" was shown during the event.

### 为庆祝中国人民解放军建军节  我驻阿使馆武官举行电影招待会

1968-07-28
第5版()
专栏：

    为庆祝中国人民解放军建军节
    我驻阿使馆武官举行电影招待会
    新华社地拉那二十七日电　为庆祝中国人民解放军建军四十一周年，中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆武官李钟玄二十六日晚在大使馆举行电影招待会。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民军政治部副主任梅罗以及国防部和内务部的其他干部出席了电影招待会。
    在十分亲切和兄弟般友好的气氛中，宾主为中阿两国人民和两国军队之间革命的和战斗的友谊，频频举杯祝酒。
    会上，放映了中国影片《地道战》。
