Title: Deputy Head of the Vietnam South National Liberation Front's Permanent Mission to China, Nguyen Minh Phuong, Leaves Post and Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1968-07-09
Edition: 5

	Vietnam South National Liberation Front's Permanent Mission to China Deputy Head Nguyen Minh Phuong left his post and returned to his home country today.

	New China News Agency, July 8th: Vietnam South National Liberation Front's Permanent Mission to China Deputy Head Nguyen Minh Phuong left his post and returned to his home country today.
