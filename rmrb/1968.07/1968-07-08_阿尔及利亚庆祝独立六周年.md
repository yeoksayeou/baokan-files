Title: Algeria Celebrates Six Years of Independence
Author:
Date: 1968-07-08
Edition: 5

Algeria celebrated its sixth anniversary of independence on July 5th.

On the morning of the 5th, Chairman Boumediene of the Algerian National Revolutionary Council, accompanied by leaders of the National Liberation Front, the government, and the military, unveiled the "Emir Abdelkader Square" in the center of Algiers. A statue of Abdelkader on horseback was erected in the square. After Algeria became a French colony, Abdelkader led the Algerian people in a long and courageous struggle against French colonialism. In the evening of the 5th, Boumediene held a grand reception at the People's Palace in Algiers.

In 1830, on July 5th, French colonial forces occupied Algiers, beginning their colonial oppression and rule over Algeria. The brave Algerian people waged a long and unyielding struggle, particularly from 1954 onwards, enduring seven and a half years of armed struggle, finally achieving independence on July 5, 1962.

### 阿尔及利亚庆祝独立六周年

1968-07-08
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚庆祝独立六周年
    新华社阿尔及尔五日电　阿尔及利亚五日在全国举行庆祝独立六周年的活动。
    五日上午，阿尔及利亚全国革命委员会主席布迈丁在民族解放阵线、政府和军队负责人陪同下，为座落在阿尔及尔中心的
  “埃米尔·阿卜杜勒·卡德尔广场”揭幕。在这个广场上建立了阿卜杜勒·卡德尔骑在战马上的塑像。阿尔及利亚沦为法国殖民地后，卡德尔曾经领导阿尔及利亚人民对法国殖民主义进行多年的英勇斗争。五日晚，布迈丁在阿尔及尔人民宫举行了盛大的招待会。
    一八三○年七月五日，法国殖民军占领了阿尔及尔，开始了对阿尔及利亚的殖民压迫和统治。英勇的阿尔及利亚人民进行了长期不屈的斗争，特别是从一九五四年起经历了七年半的武装斗争，终于在一九六二年七月五日取得了独立。
