	Revolutionary ideological struggle and artistic struggle must be subordinate to political struggle, because it is only through politics that the needs of classes and the masses can be concentrated and expressed.

### 毛主席语录

1968-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    革命的思想斗争和艺术斗争，必须服从于政治的斗争，因为只有经过政治，阶级和群众的需要才能集中地表现出来。
