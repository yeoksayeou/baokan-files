Title: Celebrating the 8th Anniversary of the Independence of the Somali Republic
Author:
Date: 1968-07-04
Edition: 6

Thousands of Mogadishu citizens dressed in festive attire gathered in the National Square in the morning to celebrate the 8th anniversary of the independence of the Somali Republic.

The President of the Somali Republic, Sheikh Mukhtar Mohamed Hussein, the President of the National Assembly, Sheikh Mukhtar Mohamed Hussein, the Chief Justice Abdurrahman Sheikh Ali Sheikh Said, and the Prime Minister Egal, accompanied by other high-ranking officials, inspected the military and civilian parade.

The parade lasted nearly two hours.

Standing on the reviewing stand with the Somali leaders were foreign guests from various African countries, including the President of the Republic of Sudan, Azhari, and the Speaker of the National Assembly of Zambia, Nkumbula.

The acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Somalia, Zhang Jingfang, also attended the inspection ceremony.

### 庆祝索马里共和国独立八周年  摩加迪沙数千市民集会游行

1968-07-04
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝索马里共和国独立八周年
    摩加迪沙数千市民集会游行
    新华社摩加迪沙一日电　穿着节日盛装的数千摩加迪沙市民一日上午集合在国民广场，庆祝索马里共和国独立八周年。
    索马里共和国总统舍马克、国民议会议长谢赫·穆克塔尔·穆罕默德·侯赛因、最高法院院长阿卜杜拉赫曼·谢赫·阿里·谢赫·赛义德和总理埃加勒，在其他高级官员的陪同下检阅了军民的游行队伍。
    游行持续了将近两个小时。
    同索马里领导人一起站在检阅台上的有包括来自非洲各国的外宾，其中有苏丹共和国主席阿扎里、赞比亚国民议会议长尼伦达等。
    中国驻索马里大使馆临时代办张景芳也出席了检阅仪式。
