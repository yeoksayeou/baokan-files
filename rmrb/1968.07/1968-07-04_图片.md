Title: The People's Liberation Army's 3127 Unit's Propaganda Team in Liaoning's Xinjin County's Shuangta Commune Focuses on Working with Sent-Down Youth
Author:
Date: 1968-07-04
Edition: 4

The People's Liberation Army's 3127 Unit's propaganda team, stationed in Liaoning's Xinjin County's Shuangta Commune, places great emphasis on working with sent-down youth during their agricultural support efforts. The image shows propaganda team members studying Chairman Mao's latest instructions with the sent-down youth during a break from labor.
