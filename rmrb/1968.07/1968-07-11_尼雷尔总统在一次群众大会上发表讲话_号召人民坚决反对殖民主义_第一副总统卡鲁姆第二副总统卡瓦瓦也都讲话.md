Title: President Nyerere Speaks at a Mass Rally, Urging the People to Resolutely Oppose Colonialism
Author:
Date: 1968-07-11
Edition: 5

President Nyerere of Tanzania, in a grand mass rally held here on July 7 to celebrate the 14th anniversary of the founding of the Tanganyika African National Union, condemned colonialism.

President Nyerere told the thousands of people gathered at Jangwani Square that colonialism is the most barbaric, "because colonialism turns people into commodities that can be bought and sold."

He called on members of the Tanganyika African National Union to resolutely oppose colonialism in the world.

Tanzania's First Vice President Karume and Second Vice President Kawawa also spoke at the meeting.

At the beginning of the meeting, Tanzanian youth presented the Uhuru (Freedom) Torch to President Nyerere. Before this, they had carried this torch throughout Tanzania.

This meeting was the climax of Tanzania's national celebration of "July 7" (July 7). To commemorate the founding of the Tanganyika African National Union on July 7, 1954, this day has been designated as a national holiday. Since July 1, celebrations have been held all over Tanzania.

On July 6, Zanzibar also held a mass celebration rally, where Tanzania's First Vice President Karume spoke.

### 尼雷尔总统在一次群众大会上发表讲话  号召人民坚决反对殖民主义  第一副总统卡鲁姆第二副总统卡瓦瓦也都讲话

1968-07-11
第5版()
专栏：

    尼雷尔总统在一次群众大会上发表讲话
    号召人民坚决反对殖民主义
    第一副总统卡鲁姆第二副总统卡瓦瓦也都讲话
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆八日电　坦桑尼亚总统尼雷尔七日在这里举行的庆祝坦噶尼喀非洲民族联盟成立十四周年的盛大的群众大会上，谴责殖民主义。
    尼雷尔总统向在詹格瓦尼广场上的几千名群众说，殖民主义是最野蛮的，“因为殖民主义把人变成可以买卖的货物。”
    他号召坦噶尼喀非洲民族联盟盟员坚决反对世界上的殖民主义。
    坦桑尼亚第一副总统卡鲁姆和第二副总统卡瓦瓦也在会上讲了话。
    大会开始时，坦桑尼亚青年把乌呼鲁（自由）火炬赠送给尼雷尔总统。在这之前，他们曾经举着这把火炬跑遍了坦桑尼亚全国。
    这个大会是庆祝坦桑尼亚“七七”（七月七日）全国性节日的高潮。为纪念一九五四年七月七日坦噶尼喀非洲民族联盟成立而规定的这个节日，从七月一日以来，坦桑尼亚全国各地到处举行庆祝活动。
    六日，桑给巴尔也举行了群众性的庆祝大会，坦桑尼亚第一副总统卡鲁姆在会上讲了话。
