Title: North European Countries' People Repeatedly Hold Protests to Oppose U.S. Aggression Against Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1968-07-06
Edition: 6

This year, the people of the Nordic countries have repeatedly held rallies and protests, strongly condemning U.S. imperialism's aggression against Vietnam and supporting the Vietnamese people's just struggle. The image shows Danish students marching in protest against U.S. imperialism's war of aggression in Vietnam.
