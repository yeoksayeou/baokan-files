Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Vietnamese Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1968-07-24
Edition: 4

On July 23, Premier Zhou Enlai met with the Vietnamese government economic delegation. Premier Zhou had a warm and friendly conversation with the delegation leader, Deputy Premier Le Thanh Nghi of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam (left three), Deputy Leader Li Ban (left two), Wu Mingluan (left one), and others.
