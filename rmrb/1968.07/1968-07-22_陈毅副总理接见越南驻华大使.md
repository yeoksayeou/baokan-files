Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with the Vietnamese Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1968-07-22
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council met with the Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Vuong Thua My, in the afternoon of July 21. During the meeting, Ambassador Vuong Thua My presented Vice Premier Chen Yi with a call to action issued by Chairman Ho Chi Minh on the occasion of the 14th anniversary of the signing of the Geneva Accords. The call to action urged the Vietnamese people to unite as one and fight resolutely to defeat the U.S. aggressors. Vice Premier Chen Yi had a cordial and friendly conversation with Ambassador Vuong Thua My.

### 陈毅副总理接见越南驻华大使

1968-07-22
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见越南驻华大使
    新华社二十一日讯国务院副总理陈毅，二十一日下午接见了越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾。接见时，吴明鸾大使向陈毅副总理递交了胡志明主席就日内瓦协议签订十四周年发出的号召书，这个号召书号召越南全国人民万众一心，坚决战斗，打败美国侵略者。陈毅副总理同吴明鸾大使进行了亲切友好的谈话。
