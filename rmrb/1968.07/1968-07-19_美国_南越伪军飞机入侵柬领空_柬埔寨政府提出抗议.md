Title: U.S.-Saigon Puppet Airplanes Invade Cambodian Airspace
Author:
Date: 1968-07-19
Edition: 6

According to Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, July 17th, according to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on the 17th, the Royal Government of Cambodia issued a statement protesting the barbaric act of a U.S.-Saigon puppet airplane invading the airspace of Chantrea County, Chup District, on the 10th, killing and injuring three Cambodian people.

The statement pointed out that this is a new attack on Cambodian residents by U.S.-Saigon airplanes after the massacre of residents in Chian Kong, Borai Meanchey Province. This shows that the U.S. military authorities, in defiance of international public opinion, continue to carry out barbaric aggression against Cambodia.

The statement pointed out that measures must be taken against the United States to stop its aggressive actions against the Cambodian people.

### 美国—南越伪军飞机入侵柬领空  柬埔寨政府提出抗议

1968-07-19
第6版()
专栏：

    美国—南越伪军飞机入侵柬领空
    柬埔寨政府提出抗议
    据新华社金边十七日电　据柬埔寨新闻社十七日报道：柬埔寨王国政府发表一项声明，抗议一架美国—南越伪军飞机十日入侵柴桢省昌特雷县上空进行扫射，打死打伤三名柬埔寨人的野蛮罪行。
    声明指出，这是美国—南越飞机在屠杀波萝勉省柴安贡的居民以后，对柬埔寨居民进行的新的袭击。这表明美国军事当局无视国际舆论的反对，仍然继续对柬埔寨进行野蛮的侵略。
    声明指出，必须对美国采取措施，制止它对柬埔寨人民的侵略行径。
