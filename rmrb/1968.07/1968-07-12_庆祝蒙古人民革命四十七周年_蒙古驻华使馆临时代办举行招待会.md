Title: Celebrating the 47th Anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution
Author:
Date: 1968-07-12
Edition: 5

The chargé d'affaires of the Mongolian People's Republic Embassy in China, Luo Qing, held a reception today to celebrate the 47th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution.

Relevant officials from the Chinese government and diplomatic envoys from various countries were invited to attend the reception.

### 庆祝蒙古人民革命四十七周年  蒙古驻华使馆临时代办举行招待会

1968-07-12
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝蒙古人民革命四十七周年
    蒙古驻华使馆临时代办举行招待会
    新华社十一日讯　蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办罗青今天举行招待会，庆祝蒙古人民革命四十七周年。
    我国政府有关部门负责人，各国驻中国使节，应邀出席了招待会。
