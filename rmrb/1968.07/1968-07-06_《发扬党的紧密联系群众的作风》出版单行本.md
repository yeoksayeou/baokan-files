Title: Publication of "Carrying Forward the Party's Style of Close Ties with the Masses" as a Single Volume
Author:
Date: 1968-07-06
Edition: 3

	The important editorial "Carrying Forward the Party's Style of Close Ties with the Masses," published by the People's Daily, Red Flag Magazine, and the Liberation Army Daily to commemorate the 47th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China, has been published as a single volume by the People's Publishing House. The book includes two photographs of the great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao receiving the cadres of the study classes from the Jinan, Guangzhou, and Lanzhou troops on the eve of July 1st. The book will begin to be distributed at the New China Bookstore in Beijing starting July 5th and will be available in New China Bookstores across the country in the near future.

### 《发扬党的紧密联系群众的作风》出版单行本

1968-07-06
第3版()
专栏：

    《发扬党的紧密联系群众的作风》出版单行本
    新华社五日讯　《人民日报》、《红旗》杂志、《解放军报》为纪念中国共产党诞生四十七周年而发表的重要社论《发扬党的紧密联系群众的作风》，已由人民出版社出版单行本。书的前面刊有伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席在“七一”前夕接见济南、广州、兰州部队干部学习班同志的两幅照片。本书从七月五日起开始在北京新华书店发行，并将在全国各地新华书店陆续发行。
