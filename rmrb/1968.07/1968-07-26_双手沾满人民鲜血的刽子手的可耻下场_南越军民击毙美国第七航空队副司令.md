Title: The Shameful End of the Butcher with Blood-Stained Hands
Author:
Date: 1968-07-26
Edition: 6

The shameful end of the butcher with blood-stained hands

South Vietnamese military and civilians shoot down the deputy commander of the U.S. Seventh Air Force

	BEIJING, July 24 (Xinhua) -- According to reports from Saigon, Robert Worley, the deputy commander of the U.S. Seventh Air Force, who was responsible for directing the savage bombing of Vietnam by U.S. warplanes, was shot down by South Vietnamese military and civilians near Da Nang on July 23.

	According to Western news agencies, this American aggressor, whose hands are stained with the blood of the Vietnamese people, was at the time piloting a plane conducting aerial reconnaissance over the northwest of Da Nang. The accurate artillery fire of the South Vietnamese military and civilians hit his aircraft. Reports say that Robert Worley was a "brigadier general," and he was the third American general to be shot down by Vietnamese military and civilians.

### 双手沾满人民鲜血的刽子手的可耻下场  南越军民击毙美国第七航空队副司令

1968-07-26
第6版()
专栏：

    双手沾满人民鲜血的刽子手的可耻下场
    南越军民击毙美国第七航空队副司令
    新华社二十四日讯　西贡消息：专门指挥美国强盗飞机对越南人民进行野蛮轰炸的美国第七航空队副司令罗伯特·沃利，二十三日在岘港附近被越南南方军民打死。
    据西方通讯社透露，这个手上沾满了越南人民鲜血的美国强盗，当时正驾驶飞机在岘港西北上空进行照像侦察，越南南方军民准确的炮火，击中了他的座机。报道说，罗伯特·沃利是个“少将”，他是被越南军民打死的第三个美国将级军官。
