Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Five U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-07-06
Edition: 5

North Vietnamese military and civilians have shot down five U.S. aircraft.

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, July 3rd: According to reports from here, on June 30th, the military and civilians of Hai Phong City in North Vietnam shot down one U.S. unmanned spy plane. Prior to this, on June 28th and 29th, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province and Vinh Linh District also shot down four invading U.S. aggressor planes.

### 越南北方军民击落美机五架

1968-07-06
第5版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机五架
    新华社河内三日电　据这里报道：越南北方海防市军民，六月三十日击落美国无人驾驶间谍飞机一架。在这以前，广平省和永灵区军民，在六月二十八日和二十九日，也曾击落入侵的美国强盗飞机四架。
