Title: Beijing Military Region Artillery Leadership Holds "Support the Army, Love the People" Meeting
Author:
Date: 1968-07-31
Edition: 1

The artillery leadership of the Beijing Military Region invited the local poor and lower-middle peasants, revolutionary cadres, workers, and revolutionary students to hold a "Support the Army, Love the People" meeting to express their gratitude for the help provided to the troops.
