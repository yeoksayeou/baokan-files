Title: Deputy Prime Minister Le Quyet Thich Leads Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-07-10
Edition: 2

By delegation head, Deputy Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Le Quyet Thich, the Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation arrived in Beijing today for a friendly visit to China. The delegation was warmly welcomed by Vice Premier Li Xiannian and over a thousand revolutionary masses from the capital.

A New China News Agency report from the 9th states: The Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation, led by Deputy Prime Minister Le Quyet Thich, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.

Vice Premier Li Xiannian and over a thousand revolutionary masses from the capital warmly welcomed them at the airport.

Today, the flags of the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam flew high in the sky above the capital airport. The revolutionary masses gathered at the airport held up portraits of our great leader Chairman Mao, portraits of the great leader of the Vietnamese people Chairman Ho Chi Minh, and banners reading "Salute to the heroic Vietnamese people," "Firmly support the Vietnamese people's struggle against American aggression for national salvation," and other slogans. They shouted slogans, expressing the resolve of 700 million Chinese people to be a strong rear guard for the Vietnamese people.

Amidst warm applause, delegation head Le Quyet Thich and deputy delegation head, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of Vietnam Le Ban, stepped off the plane. Subsequently, Le Quyet Thich, Le Ban, and another deputy delegation head, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China Vu Minh Quan, who was already in Beijing, were escorted by Vice Premier Li Xiannian to greet the welcoming crowd. At this moment, the revolutionary masses waved red copies of "Quotations from Chairman Mao," beat drums, and shouted slogans: "Long live the brotherly friendship and combat unity between the peoples of China and Vietnam!" "Firmly support the revolutionary struggles of the peoples of Asia, Africa, and Latin America!" "Firmly support the revolutionary struggles of the peoples of Europe and North America!" "Down with U.S. imperialism!" "Down with modern revisionism centered on the Soviet revisionist leadership!" "Victory to the Vietnamese people! Defeat to U.S. imperialism!" "Long live the invincible Marxism-Leninism-Mao Zedong Thought!" "Long live the great victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!" "Long live Chairman Ho Chi Minh!" "Long live Chairman Mao!"

Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation were relevant officials from the Chinese government departments, the People's Liberation Army, and the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee, including Wen Yucheng, Han Nianlong, Li Qiang, Xie Huaiyi, Ding Xilin, and Xiang Qian; responsible members of the revolutionary mass organizations in the capital; and representatives of the Five Good Soldiers from the garrison troops in Beijing.

Diplomatic staff from the Vietnamese Embassy in China, delegation head Nguyen Van Quoc of the Permanent Representative Office of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China, other Vietnamese friends in Beijing, and envoys from various countries to China also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 黎清毅副总理率越南政府经济代表团抵京  国务院副总理李先念和首都革命群众一千多人前往机场热烈欢迎

1968-07-10
第2版()
专栏：

    黎清毅副总理率越南政府经济代表团抵京
    国务院副总理李先念和首都革命群众一千多人前往机场热烈欢迎
    新华社九日讯　由团长、越南民主共和国政府副总理黎清毅率领的越南政府经济代表团，前来我国进行友好访问，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    国务院副总理李先念，和首都革命群众一千多人，前往机场热烈欢迎。
    今天，首都机场上空飘扬着中华人民共和国和越南民主共和国国旗。聚集在机场上的首都革命群众高举我们伟大领袖毛主席的画像，高举越南人民的伟大领袖胡志明主席的画像，举着“向英雄的越南人民致敬”、“坚决支持越南人民的抗美救国战争”等横幅标语，高呼口号，表示七亿中国人民誓作越南人民的坚强后盾。
    黎清毅团长和代表团副团长、越南对外贸易部副部长李班等，在热烈的掌声中走下飞机。随后，黎清毅团长、李班副团长，和已在北京的代表团另一位副团长、越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾等，由李先念副总理陪同，绕场一周，同欢迎群众见面。这时，革命群众挥动红彤彤的《毛主席语录》，敲锣打鼓，高呼口号：“中、越两国人民的兄弟友谊和战斗团结万岁！”“坚决支持亚、非、拉各国人民的革命斗争！”“坚决支持欧洲、北美各国人民的革命斗争！”“打倒以美国为首的帝国主义！”“打倒以苏修领导集团为中心的现代修正主义！”“越南人民必胜，美帝国主义必败！”“战无不胜的马克思主义、列宁主义、毛泽东思想万岁！”“无产阶级文化大革命全面胜利万岁！”“胡志明主席万岁！”“毛主席万岁！”
    到机场欢迎的，还有我国政府部门、中国人民解放军和北京市革命委员会等有关方面的负责人温玉成、韩念龙、李强、谢怀德、丁西林、向前；首都革命群众组织的负责成员，和驻京部队五好战士代表。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆外交官员，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长阮文广，在北京的其他越南朋友，以及各国驻中国使节，也到机场迎接。（附图片）
    下右图：由团长、越南民主共和国政府副总理黎清毅率领的越南政府经济代表团，前来我国进行友好访问，于七月九日乘飞机到达北京。李先念副总理和首都革命群众一千多人到机场热烈欢迎。
         新华社记者摄
