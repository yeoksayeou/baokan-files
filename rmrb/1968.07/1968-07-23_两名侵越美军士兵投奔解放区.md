Title: Two U.S. Soldiers Defect to Liberation Zone
Author:
Date: 1968-07-23
Edition: 5

Two U.S. soldiers have defected to the liberation zone. According to a report by the New China News Agency in Hanoi on the 22nd, the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency reported that two more U.S. soldiers, unwilling to continue fighting in Johnson's war of aggression against Vietnam, recently deserted their units and joined the liberation zone. The two U.S. soldiers were warmly welcomed by the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam and the local people. One of them, a sergeant named McGinn, issued a statement upon arriving in the liberation zone, condemning the criminal war of aggression against Vietnam waged by the U.S. imperialist Johnson.

### 两名侵越美军士兵投奔解放区

1968-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    两名侵越美军士兵投奔解放区
    据新华社河内二十二日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：又有两名侵越美军士兵由于不愿继续为约翰逊政府的侵越战争卖命，在最近脱离敌人的队伍，投奔解放区。这两名美国士兵受到了越南南方民族解放阵线和当地人民的热情接待。其中一个名叫麦根的美军上士在到达解放区的时候发表了一项声明，谴责美帝头子约翰逊进行的罪恶的侵越战争。
