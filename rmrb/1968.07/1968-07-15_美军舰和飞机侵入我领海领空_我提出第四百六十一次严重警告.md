Title: U.S. Warships and Aircraft Intrude into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace
Author:
Date: 1968-07-15
Edition: 6

U.S. warships and aircraft intrude into our territorial waters and airspace

We issue the 460th serious warning

	BEIJING, July 14 (Xinhua) -- On July 11 from 19:21 to 23:55, and on July 12 from 2:40 to 14:42, a U.S. warship intruded into our territorial waters off the east of Baijian Island in Fujian Province on two occasions. On July 12 from 14:06 to 14:23, and from 14:27 to 14:38, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into our airspace over Dong Island and Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province, on two occasions. In response to these provocative military actions by U.S. warships and military aircraft, the spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the Ministry, issues the 460th serious warning.

### 美军舰和飞机侵入我领海领空  我提出第四百六十一次严重警告

1968-07-15
第6版()
专栏：

    美军舰和飞机侵入我领海领空
    我提出第四百六十一次严重警告
    新华社十四日讯　七月十一日十九时二十一分至二十三时五十五分，七月十二日二时四十分至十四时四十二分，美国军舰一艘，两次侵入我福建省白犬列岛以东地区领海。七月十二日十四时零六分至十四时二十三分，同日十四时二十七分至十四时三十八分，又有美国军用飞机一架，两次侵入我广东省西沙群岛的东岛、永兴岛地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅活动，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百六十一次严重警告。
