Title: Lao Patriotic Military and Civilians Annihilate Over 3,000 Enemies in Three Months
Author:
Date: 1968-07-22
Edition: 5

Lao patriotic military and civilians, since the Lao New Year (April 13), have actively responded to the battle call issued by Prince Souphanouvong, continuously striking the enemy and achieving splendid victories.

According to statistics, during this period, Lao patriotic military and civilians have annihilated over 3,000 enemies, shot down 18 enemy aircraft, seized hundreds of various weapons, and liberated tens of thousands of residents.

Currently, Lao patriotic military and civilians are advancing victoriously, continuing to launch fierce attacks on the enemy to achieve greater victories.

According to another report from the Pathet Lao News Agency, the patriotic military and civilians of Udomsai Province in Laos have conducted 130 battles this year, annihilating nearly 1,000 enemies, shooting down 3 enemy aircraft, destroying a large number of enemy vehicles and ships, and seizing over 400 weapons.

According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio station, Lao patriotic military and civilians recently achieved new victories in their counterattacks against bandits who were harassing and destroying liberated areas.

The local forces and guerrillas of Phongsaly Province bravely counterattacked bandit harassment activities in June, annihilating 97 bandits, killing 90, capturing 3 alive, and forcing 4 to surrender.

The local forces and guerrillas of Luang Namtha Province on June 20 struck bandits who were harassing the liberated area, killing 2 and forcing 4 to surrender.

The local forces and guerrillas of Udomsai Province on June 15 attacked bandits who had infiltrated the liberated area, killing 1 and forcing 2 to surrender, and seizing a batch of military supplies.

According to another report, the patriotic military and civilians of Xaisomboun Province on June 23 launched a fierce attack on the enemy, annihilating 40 enemies and seizing a large amount of military supplies.

According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio station, the local forces of Xieng Khouang Province on July 10 shot down an invading U.S. aircraft in their counterattack against U.S. air pirates.

According to a report from the Pathet Lao News Agency, in Phongsaly Province, Laos, over 110 American-sponsored bandits surrendered to the people in June, bringing their weapons with them.

According to another report, in Luang Namtha Province, 12 soldiers from the right-wing Lao army surrendered to the patriotic forces on June 12.

### 老挝爱国军民三个月歼敌三千多  乌多姆塞省爱国军民上半年频频出击歼敌近千

1968-07-22
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国军民三个月歼敌三千多
    乌多姆塞省爱国军民上半年频频出击歼敌近千
    新华社康开电　据巴特寮电台广播：老挝爱国军民，从老挝新年（四月十三日）以来的三个月内，积极响应苏发努冯亲王发出的战斗号召，连续打击敌人，取得了辉煌胜利。
    据统计，在这期间，老挝爱国军民共歼敌三千多名，击落敌机十八架，缴获各类武器数百件，解放了成万的居民。
    目前老挝爱国军民正乘胜前进，继续向敌人发动猛烈进攻，以夺取更大的胜利。
    另据巴特寮通讯社报道，老挝乌多姆塞省爱国军民，在今年上半年，进行了一百三十次战斗，共歼灭敌人近一千名，击落敌机三架，击毁大批敌人军车和船只，缴获四百多件武器。
    新华社康开二十一日电　据巴特寮电台广播：老挝爱国军民最近在反击对解放区进行骚扰破坏活动的土匪的战斗中，取得了新胜利。
    丰沙里省地方部队和游击队，六月份英勇反击土匪骚扰活动，共歼灭土匪九十七名，其中打死九十名，活捉三名，迫降四名。
    琅南塔省地方部队和游击队六月二十日打击来解放区进行骚扰活动的土匪，打死两名，迫降四名。
    乌多姆塞省地方部队和游击队，六月十五日袭击窜犯解放区的土匪，打死一名，迫降两名，缴获一批军用物资。
    另据报道，巴色省爱国军民，六月二十三日猛烈袭击敌人，歼敌四十名，缴获大批军用物资。
    新华社康开电　据巴特寮电台广播：老挝桑怒省地方部队七月十日在反击美国空中强盗的战斗中，击落入侵的美机一架。
    新华社康开电　据巴特寮通讯社报道：在老挝丰沙里省，六月份有一百一十多名美国豢养的土匪携械向人民投诚。
    另据报道，在琅南塔省，六月十二日有十二名老挝右派军队向爱国力量投诚。
