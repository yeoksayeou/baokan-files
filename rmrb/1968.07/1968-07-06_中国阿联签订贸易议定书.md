Title: China and the United Arab Republic Sign a Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1968-07-06
Edition: 5

The governments of the People's Republic of China and the United Arab Republic signed the 1968 Trade Agreement and an exchange of letters regarding the extension of the validity period of the trade and payment agreement between the two countries in Cairo on July 4.

Huang Hua, the Chinese Ambassador to the United Arab Republic, signed on behalf of the Chinese government, and Hussein Khaled Hamdi, the Undersecretary of State for the Economy and Foreign Trade of the United Arab Republic, signed on behalf of the United Arab Republic government.

### 中国阿联签订贸易议定书

1968-07-06
第5版()
专栏：

    中国阿联签订贸易议定书
    新华社开罗四日电　中华人民共和国政府和阿拉伯联合共和国政府四日在开罗签订了一九六八年贸易议定书以及关于延长两国贸易和支付协定有效期限的换文。
    中国驻阿联大使黄华代表中国政府，阿联经济和对外贸易部国务秘书侯赛因·哈立德·哈姆迪代表阿联政府，在议定书和换文上签了字。
