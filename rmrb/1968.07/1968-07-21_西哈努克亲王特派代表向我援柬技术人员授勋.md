Title: Prince Sihanouk's Special Envoy Decorates Chinese Technical Personnel Aiding Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1968-07-21
Edition: 6

Prince Sihanouk, the head of state of Cambodia, specially dispatched the Cambodian Minister of National Education and Arts, Vong Molivong, to Pursat on the 18th to present medals to Chinese technical personnel who completed the construction of the experimental laboratory and training workshop of the Royal University of Pursat, funded by Chinese aid.

The Chinese chargé d'affaires, Zheng Sixiong, was invited to attend the medal-awarding ceremony.

At the ceremony, Vong Molivong delivered a speech, praising China for providing Cambodia with substantial and enthusiastic material and moral support. He expressed the sincere gratitude of the Kingdom's government to the Chinese people led by Chairman Mao Zedong. Finally, Vong Molivong, the minister, shouted, "Long live Cambodian-Chinese friendship!"

### 西哈努克亲王特派代表向我援柬技术人员授勋

1968-07-21
第6版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王特派代表向我援柬技术人员授勋
    新华社金边二十日电　柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王，十八日特派柬埔寨政府国民教育和艺术大臣旺·莫利旺前往磅湛市，向完成由中国援助的磅湛王家大学实验室和实习工场工程的中国技术人员授予勋章。
    中国大使馆临时代办郑斯雄应邀参加了授勋仪式。
    旺·莫利旺在授勋仪式上发表讲话，赞扬中国在物质和道义方面给予柬埔寨大量的和热情的援助。他代表王国政府向毛泽东主席领导下的中国表示诚挚的谢意。最后，旺·莫利旺大臣高呼：“柬中友谊万岁！”
