Title: Military and Civilians in Ba Dinh and Other Provinces of South Vietnam Continuously Annihilate the Enemy
Author:
Date: 1968-07-02
Edition: 6

According to a report from the New China News Agency in Hanoi, the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency reported: In recent days, the military and civilians in Ba Dinh, Can Tho, Soc Trang, Dinh Tuong, Long An, Binh Dinh, Kontum, and Quang Tri provinces in South Vietnam have launched continuous and fierce attacks on the U.S. puppet forces, annihilating a large number of enemy troops.

From June 10 to 13, the military and civilians of Ba Dinh Province attacked the provincial capital of Ba Dinh, a puppet military training school, eight military sub-districts and military zones, and numerous enemy strongholds along the 23rd, 44th, and 52nd highways. They annihilated more than 290 enemy troops. In the battle to attack the puppet military training school, the People's Liberation Armed Forces destroyed many barracks and American "advisors'" residences, killing and wounding more than 140 enemy troops.

From June 5 to 7, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Hong To and Thanh Loc of Ba Dinh Province repeatedly attacked the enemy, annihilating nearly 300 U.S. puppet troops, including completely wiping out an enemy company and a platoon, destroying six armored vehicles, and shooting down one enemy aircraft.

On the night of June 9, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Can Tho Province ambushed an enemy convoy on the Cai River, sinking and damaging three enemy ships. On June 14, the People's Liberation Armed Forces intercepted an enemy convoy on the Soai Rap River, seriously damaging four of the ships. Before this, the People's Liberation Armed Forces had sunk a puppet police patrol boat on this river on June 8, completely annihilating an enemy platoon.

On June 10, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Soc Trang Province intercepted an enemy convoy on the Co Co River, setting two enemy ships on fire and completely annihilating the enemy troops on board. They also damaged five other enemy ships and annihilated many enemy troops.

From June 20 to 22, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Dinh Tuong Province continuously attacked Chau Thanh town and two enemy military sub-districts and one enemy stronghold, annihilating more than 140 enemy troops. They also destroyed or damaged seven enemy armored vehicles, damaged two enemy aircraft, and seized a large number of weapons and military supplies.

From June 11 to 19, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Long An Province attacked the enemy in various areas of Binh My, Tam Phu, and Chau Thanh County, killing and wounding more than 130 enemy troops and destroying four enemy armored vehicles.

From June 4 to 10, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Long An Province continuously attacked the enemy in the Chi Hoa and Luc Hoa regions, 7 kilometers south of Long An City, annihilating a puppet regiment, severely damaging a regiment, a special forces company, and an armored regiment, totaling 420 enemy troops. They also damaged seven enemy armored vehicles, shot down two enemy aircraft, and seized a large number of weapons and military supplies.

From June 20 to 22, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Binh Dinh Province launched continuous attacks on the enemy in the Lo Gia and Dinh Quan regions, completely annihilating a puppet regiment and a company, severely damaging two other regiments, seizing a large number of weapons and ammunition, blowing up the Lo Gia Bridge, and cutting off the road from Chuong Thien to Dinh Quan.

On June 23, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Binh Dinh Province fiercely intercepted a U.S. invasion convoy in the Ong Trao River region, 30 kilometers southeast of Saigon, sinking and damaging six enemy ships.

On June 8, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Kontum Province intercepted the enemy in the Uak Ea region, killing 90 enemy troops. On June 10, the puppet forces sent two companies to the Ngoc Hoi Valley region north of Kontum City to harass. The Liberation Army set an ambush in advance and fiercely attacked the enemy, killing more than 100 enemy troops and seizing a large number of weapons and military supplies.

Additionally, it was reported that on the afternoon of June 20, the artillery forces of the Liberation Army in Quang Tri Province fiercely bombarded the Dong Ha U.S. military base, killing and wounding 400 U.S. invaders, destroying more than 100 enemy vehicles, 12 warehouses, six aircraft, and eight cannons. The command center of the U.S. Marine Corps' 3rd Division was also severely damaged.

### 南越巴地等省军民连连歼敌  广治省解放军猛轰东河美军基地歼美军四百

1968-07-02
第6版()
专栏：

    南越巴地等省军民连连歼敌
    广治省解放军猛轰东河美军基地歼美军四百
    据新华社河内电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方的巴地、芹苴、朔庄、迪石、永隆、边和、昆嵩和广治等省军民最近向美伪军发动了连续的猛烈进攻，歼灭敌人大批有生力量。
    巴地省军民在六月十日到十三日期间，曾接连进攻省会巴地市、一个伪军军事训练学校、八个军事支区和军事区，以及第二十三号公路、第四十四号公路、第五十二号公路沿线的许多敌军据点，共消灭敌人二百九十多名。其中在袭击伪军军事训练学校的战斗中，人民解放武装力量就摧毁了许多营房和美国“顾问”宿舍，打死、打伤敌人一百四十多名。
    巴地省红土和春禄等地的人民解放武装力量，从六月五日到七日多次袭击敌人，消灭美伪军近三百名，其中包括全歼敌人一个连和一个排，击毁装甲车六辆，击落敌机一架。
    芹苴省人民解放武装力量六月九日夜间在丐冷河上伏击敌船队，击沉、击伤敌船三艘。十四日，人民解放武装力量又在沙诺运河上截击敌人船队，严重击伤了其中的四艘。在这之前，人民解放武装力量曾于六月八日在这条运河上击沉伪警察巡逻艇一艘，全歼敌人一个班。
    朔庄省人民解放武装力量六月十日在古固河上截击敌人一支船队，打得两艘敌船起火燃烧，全歼了这两艘船上的敌人；另外又击伤了五艘敌船，消灭了许多敌人。
    迪石省人民解放武装力量在六月二十日到二十二日三天中，接连进攻周城县城和敌人两个军事支区、一个敌人据点，消灭敌人一百四十多名，并且击毁、击伤敌人装甲车七辆，击伤敌人飞机两架，缴获了许多武器和军用物资。
    永隆省人民解放武装力量从六月十一日到十九日在平明、三平和周城县等的一些地区袭击敌人，打死打伤敌人一百三十多名，击毁敌人装甲车四辆。
    永隆省人民解放武装力量，从六月四日到十日，在永隆市以南七公里的岐和、禄和地区连续打击敌人，歼灭伪军一个营，重创一个营、一个别动连和一个装甲营，共歼敌四百二十名，击伤敌人装甲车七辆，击落敌机两架，并缴获大批武器和军用物资。
    边和省人民解放武装力量，从六月二十日到二十二日，在罗芽和定贯两个地区接连袭击敌人，全歼伪军一个营和一个连，重创另外两个营，缴获大批武器和弹药，炸毁了罗芽桥，并切断了从足征到定贯的公路。
    六月二十三日，边和省人民解放武装力量在西贡东南三十多公里的翁交河地区猛烈截击美国侵略军的船队，击沉、击伤船艇六艘。
    昆嵩省人民解放武装力量六月八日在厄克公夭地区截击敌人，打死敌人九十名。十日，伪军又出动两个连到昆嵩市北面的玉公谷地区进行骚扰。解放军预先设下埋伏，猛烈地袭击敌人，打死敌人一百多名，并缴获大批武器和军用物资。
    另据补充报道，广治省解放军炮兵部队，在六月二十日下午猛烈轰击东河美军基地的战斗中，共打死、打伤美国侵略军四百名，击毁敌人军车一百多辆、仓库十二座、飞机六架和大炮八门，美国海军陆战队第三师的指挥部也遭到严重的破坏。
