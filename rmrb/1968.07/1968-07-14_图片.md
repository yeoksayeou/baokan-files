Title: Student Protests Continue in Brazil
Author:
Date: 1968-07-14
Edition: 5

In Brazil, following the nationwide anti-American and anti-dictatorship struggle that took place in April this year, students in June once again defied the dictatorial regime's ban and held successive anti-American and anti-dictatorship demonstrations and marches.
