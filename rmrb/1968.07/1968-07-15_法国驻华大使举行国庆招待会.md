Title: French Ambassador Holds National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1968-07-15
Edition: 5

French Ambassador to China, Peyre, held a reception today to celebrate the National Day of the French Republic.

Relevant officials from the Chinese government, including Luo Guibo, were invited to attend the reception.

Ambassadors from various countries stationed in China also attended the reception.

### 法国驻华大使举行国庆招待会

1968-07-15
第5版()
专栏：

    法国驻华大使举行国庆招待会
    新华社十四日讯　法兰西共和国驻中国大使佩耶，今天举行招待会，庆祝法兰西共和国国庆日。
    我国政府有关部门负责人罗贵波等应邀出席了招待会。
    各国驻中国使节也出席了招待会。
