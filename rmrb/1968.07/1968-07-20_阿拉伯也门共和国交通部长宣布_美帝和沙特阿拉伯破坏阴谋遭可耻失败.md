Title: Yemeni Republic Transportation Minister Announces the Shameful Failure of American and Saudi Arabian Sabotage Plots
Author:
Date: 1968-07-20
Edition: 5

Yemeni Republic Transportation Minister Ahmad Rahumi recently announced that the plot by the United States and Saudi Arabia to establish four military bases in northern Yemen has met with a shameful failure.

Rahumi exposed the crimes committed by American imperialism and the Saudi Arabian reactionaries, stating that their goal was to sabotage the Yemeni Republic. He said: "American imperialism and the Saudi Arabian reactionaries support and arm a group of foreign mercenaries who, on the land of the Yemeni Republic, carry out heinous acts of sabotage and massacre innocent people." Rahumi also exposed the evil deeds of American imperialism and the Saudi Arabian reactionaries in using Yemenis to kill Yemenis. He said, "Now, some tribes that once believed the deceptive propaganda of imperialism and reactionaries have returned to the side of the Republic."

### 阿拉伯也门共和国交通部长宣布  美帝和沙特阿拉伯破坏阴谋遭可耻失败

1968-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯也门共和国交通部长宣布
    美帝和沙特阿拉伯破坏阴谋遭可耻失败
    据新华社萨那十九日电　阿拉伯也门共和国交通部长艾哈迈德·拉胡米最近宣布，美国和沙特阿拉伯企图在也门北部地区建立四个军事据点的阴谋遭到了可耻的失败。
    拉胡米揭露了美帝国主义和沙特阿拉伯反动派所策划的目的在于破坏也门共和国的罪行。他说：“美帝国主义和沙特阿拉伯反动派支持和武装一批外国雇佣军，在阿拉伯也门共和国的土地上，进行丧心病狂的破坏活动，屠杀无辜的人民。”拉胡米还揭露了美帝国主义和沙特阿拉伯反动派利用也门人民屠杀也门人民的罪恶行径。他说，现在“一些曾经相信帝国主义和反动派欺骗宣传的部落，已经回到共和国方面来了。”
