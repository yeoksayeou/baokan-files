Title: Syrian Ambassador to China Leaves Post
Author:
Date: 1968-07-28
Edition: 5

Syrian Ambassador to China Leaves Post

New China News Agency, July 27

The Syrian Ambassador to China, Saadiq, has been recalled and will leave his post. He departed Beijing by train today.
