Title: China's Paper-Cutting Exhibition Opens in Kathmandu
Author:
Date: 1968-07-04
Edition: 6

The China Paper-Cutting Exhibition, sponsored by the Sino-Nepal Friendship Association, opened in the afternoon in Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal.

Purna Bahadur, President of the Sino-Nepal Friendship Association, delivered a speech at the opening ceremony.

He said that under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao, the People's Republic of China has achieved and will continue to achieve great successes in all fields. We are very pleased with these great achievements.

He said that under the leadership of Chairman Mao, China has made great progress in the field of culture and art. Chinese culture and art reflect the true life of the Chinese people in their struggle against imperialism and colonialism, and also reflect the actual situation of socialist construction in China.

Tou Guowei, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Nepal, introduced at the exhibition the great achievements of the Chinese people's culture and art under the brilliant guidance of Mao Zedong Thought.

Attendees at the opening ceremony of the exhibition included people from various sectors of Nepal.

### 我国剪纸展览会在加德满都开幕  尼中友协会长赞扬我国在各方面取得的伟大成就

1968-07-04
第6版()
专栏：

    我国剪纸展览会在加德满都开幕
    尼中友协会长赞扬我国在各方面取得的伟大成就
    新华社加德满都一日电　由尼中友好协会主办的中国剪纸展览会一日下午在尼泊尔首都加德满都开幕。
    尼中友好协会会长普尔纳·巴哈杜尔在开幕式上发表讲话。
    他说，中华人民共和国在伟大领袖毛主席的英明领导下，已经并将继续在各方面取得伟大的成就，我们对这些伟大成就感到非常高兴。
    他说，在毛主席的领导下，中国在文化艺术方面也获得很大的进步。中国的文化艺术反映了中国人民在反对帝国主义和殖民主义的斗争中的真实生活，也反映了中国人民的社会主义建设的实际情况。
    中国驻尼泊尔大使馆临时代办屠国维在展览会上介绍了中国人民的文化艺术在毛泽东思想的光辉照耀下取得的巨大成就。
    出席展览会开幕式的有尼泊尔各界人士。
