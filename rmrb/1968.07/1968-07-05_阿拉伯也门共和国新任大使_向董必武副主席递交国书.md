Title: New Ambassador of the Arab Republic of Yemen Presents Credentials to Vice Chairman Dong Biwu
Author:
Date: 1968-07-05
Edition: 3

	The new ambassador of the Arab Republic of Yemen, Abdel Wahid Harbash, along with other diplomatic staff of the embassy, presented his credentials to Vice Chairman Dong Biwu of the People's Republic of China this morning.

	After accepting the credentials, Vice Chairman Dong Biwu had a friendly conversation with Ambassador Abdel Wahid Harbash. Those present at the credential presentation included Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei.

### 阿拉伯也门共和国新任大使  向董必武副主席递交国书

1968-07-05
第3版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯也门共和国新任大使
    向董必武副主席递交国书
    新华社四日讯　阿拉伯也门共和国新任驻中国大使阿卜杜勒·瓦希德·赫尔巴什同大使馆外交官员，今天上午向中华人民共和国副主席董必武递交国书。
    董必武副主席接受国书以后，同阿卜杜勒·瓦希德·赫尔巴什大使进行了友好的谈话。递交国书时在场的有外交部副部长姬鹏飞。
