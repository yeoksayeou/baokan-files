Title: Warmly Welcoming the Guinean Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1968-07-26
Edition: 6

The Ministry of National Defense held an evening of entertainment to warmly welcome the military delegation from the Republic of Guinea.

The delegation, led by Colonel Kamara Diaby, and the acting charge d'affaires of the Guinean Embassy in China, Kamara, were invited to attend the event.

Vice Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Wang Xinting, accompanied the Guinean guests to watch the revolutionary modern drama "Drums of the Equator," which depicts the armed struggle of the Congolese (Kinshasa) people.

Also present at the evening were responsible officials from various departments and branches of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Beijing Revolutionary Committee, and other relevant units, including Zhang Lingbin and Ding Xilin.

At the end of the performance, Vice Chief of the General Staff Wang Xinting and Colonel Kamara Diaby went on stage to shake hands with the performers, congratulating them on their successful show.

Today, the Guinean military delegation, accompanied by the responsible person of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force, Tan Jiashu, visited and inspected a certain unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force.

### 热烈欢迎几内亚军事代表团  我国防部举行文娱晚会

1968-07-26
第6版()
专栏：

    热烈欢迎几内亚军事代表团
    我国防部举行文娱晚会
    新华社二十五日讯　国防部今晚举行文娱晚会，热烈欢迎几内亚共和国军事代表团。
    以卡曼·迪亚比上校为首的几内亚军事代表团全体成员，几内亚驻中国大使馆临时代办卡马拉应邀出席了晚会。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长王新亭陪同几内亚客人观看了反映刚果（金）人民武装斗争的革命现代话剧《赤道战鼓》。
    出席晚会的还有中国人民解放军各总部、各军种兵种，北京市革命委员会等有关方面的负责人张令彬、丁西林等。
    演出结束时，副总参谋长王新亭和卡曼·迪亚比上校走上舞台，同演员们热烈握手，祝贺他们演出成功。
    今天，几内亚军事代表团在中国人民解放军空军负责人谭家述陪同下，参观访问了中国人民解放军空军部队某部。
