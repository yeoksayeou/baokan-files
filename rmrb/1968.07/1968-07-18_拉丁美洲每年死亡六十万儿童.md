Title: 600,000 Children Die Each Year in Latin America
Author:
Date: 1968-07-18
Edition: 5

Under the brutal rule of U.S. imperialism and pro-U.S. dictatorships, the people of Latin America suffer immense hardships and have extremely high mortality rates. An article published in the U.S. magazine *Horizon* revealed that due to malnutrition, over 600,000 children die each year in Latin America, and 100 million adults suffer from malnutrition. In Mexico, 200 children die every day. In some regions, the child mortality rate reaches as high as 42.5 percent.

### 拉丁美洲每年死亡六十万儿童

1968-07-18
第5版()
专栏：简明新闻

    拉丁美洲每年死亡六十万儿童
    在美帝和亲美独裁政权残暴统治下，拉丁美洲人民苦难深重，死亡率极高。美国《视界》杂志刊载的一篇材料曾透露，拉丁美洲由于营养不足，每年要死六十多万儿童，有一亿成年人受到营养不良的恶果。在墨西哥，每天要死二百个儿童。某些地区儿童死亡率高达百分之四十二点五。
