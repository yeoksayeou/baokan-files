Title: The Indian Reactionary Government Begs West Germany for a Loan
Author:
Date: 1968-07-18
Edition: 5

The Indian reactionary government, after repeatedly seeking aid from the U.S. imperialists and Soviet revisionists, has recently begged the West German authorities for a loan. According to the signed agreement, India will obtain a loan of $62 million from West Germany, mainly for importing goods from West Germany.

### 印度反动政府向西德乞求贷款

1968-07-18
第5版()
专栏：简明新闻

    印度反动政府向西德乞求贷款
    印度反动政府在连连向美帝和苏修求援后，最近又向西德当局乞求贷款。根据签订的协定，印度将从西德那里拿到六千二百万美元贷款，其中主要是从西德进口物资。
