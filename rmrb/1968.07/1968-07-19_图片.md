Title: The Military Representative of the Revolutionary Committee of Dandong City, Liaoning Province, and an Active Learner of Mao Zedong Thought, Zhang Yude, is Elected as Deputy Director of the Revolutionary Committee

Author: Liu Zhesi
Date: 1968-07-19
Edition: 2

    The military representative of the Revolutionary Committee of Dandong City, Liaoning Province, and an active learner of Mao Zedong Thought from the Shenyang Military Region, Zhang Yude, who is the political instructor of the Fifth Company of a certain unit, was elected as the deputy director of the Revolutionary Committee after being selected. He has maintained and carried forward the Party's style of closely connecting with the masses, often going deep into the masses, and listening to the opinions of the masses. The picture shows Zhang Yude in the midst of a conversation with poor and lower-middle-class peasant community members. Photo by Liu Zhesi.
