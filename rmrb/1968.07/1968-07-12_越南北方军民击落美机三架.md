Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Three U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-07-12
Edition: 5

North Vietnamese military and civilians have shot down three invading U.S. aircraft in recent days. On the 2nd and 8th, the military and civilians of Quảng Bình Province shot down two U.S. F-4 type aircraft. On the 5th, the military and civilians of Nghệ An Province shot down one U.S. A-6 type aircraft.

### 越南北方军民击落美机三架

1968-07-12
第5版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机三架
    新华社河内十日电　据这里今天公布：越南北方军民最近几天在战斗中又打落三架入侵的美国强盗飞机。二日和八日，广平省军民先后打落两架美国F—4型强盗飞机。义安省军民在五日击落了一架美国A—6型强盗飞机。
