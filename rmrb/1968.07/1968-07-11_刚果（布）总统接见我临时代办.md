Title: President of Congo (Brazzaville) Meets Chinese Chargé d'Affaires
Author:
Date: 1968-07-11
Edition: 6

President of Congo (Brazzaville) receives Chinese Chargé d'Affaires

BEIJING, July 7 — President of the Congo (Brazzaville) Alphonse Massamba-Débat received Chinese Chargé d'Affaires Li Chenguang at the presidential palace on July 6 and had a cordial and friendly conversation with him.

### 刚果（布）总统接见我临时代办

1968-07-11
第6版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）总统接见我临时代办
    新华社布拉柴维尔七日电　刚果（布）总统马桑巴—代巴六日在总统府接见了中国驻刚果（布）大使馆临时代办李琛光，并且同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
