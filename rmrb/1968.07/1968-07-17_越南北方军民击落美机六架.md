Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Six U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-07-17
Edition: 5

	North Vietnamese military and civilians shot down six U.S. aircraft.

	According to a report from here, on July 14th and 15th, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province in North Vietnam shot down two invading U.S. aggressor aircraft and captured a group of U.S. air pirates.

	According to a report from here, on July 9th and 10th, the military and civilians of Quang Binh and Quang Tri Provinces in North Vietnam shot down three invading U.S. aggressor aircraft. Prior to this, on July 8th, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province also shot down another U.S. aggressor aircraft.

	According to another report, on July 6th, the military and civilians of Hon Giao Island damaged an invading U.S. warship.

### 越南北方军民击落美机六架

1968-07-17
第5版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机六架
    新华社河内十六日电 据这里宣布：越南北方广平省军民，在十四日和十五日的战斗中，又击落两架入侵的美国强盗飞机，并且活捉了一批美国飞贼。
    新华社河内电　据这里公布：越南北方河静和广平省军民，在七月九日和十日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机三架。在这以前，广平省军民在八日还击落了另一架美国强盗飞机。
    另据报道，昏果岛军民在七月六日击伤入侵的美国军舰一艘。
