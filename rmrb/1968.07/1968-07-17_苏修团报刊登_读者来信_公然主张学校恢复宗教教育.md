Title: Soviet Revisionist Youth Newspaper Publishes "Reader's Letter" Openly Advocating for Religious Education in Schools
Author:
Date: 1968-07-17
Edition: 6

Soviet revisionist youth newspaper *Komsomolskaya Pravda* published a so-called "reader's letter" openly advocating for religious education in Soviet schools. The author complained, "The flaw in our education of children and youth lies in the fact that there is no religion in our education." He regarded Christ as the "foundation of all morality" and therefore opposed "driving Christ out of the home and school."

Western journalists reporting on this matter considered it "unusual" for the "main newspaper for Soviet youth" to publish demands for "religious education in schools."

### 苏修团报刊登“读者来信”  公然主张学校恢复宗教教育

1968-07-17
第6版()
专栏：

    苏修团报刊登“读者来信”
    公然主张学校恢复宗教教育
      “没有基督就没有道义，也不可能有任何道义”。莫斯科《共青团真理报》曾经发表一篇所谓“读者来信”，公开宣扬要在苏联学校进行宗教教育。作者抱怨说：“我们对儿童和青年人的教育的缺点就在于我们的教育中没有宗教这一事实。”他把基督当作“一切道义的基础”，因而反对“把基督赶出家庭和学校”。
    西方记者在报道这件事情时认为，“苏联供青年人阅读的主要报纸”，这样公布关于在“学校里进行宗教教育的要求”，是一个“不寻常的做法”。（本报讯）
