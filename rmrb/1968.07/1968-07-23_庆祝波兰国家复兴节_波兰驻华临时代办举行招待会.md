Title: Celebrating Poland's National Revival Day
Author:
Date: 1968-07-23
Edition: 6

Poland's Chargé d'Affaires in China, Mr. Rowinski, held a reception today to celebrate the National Revival Day of the Polish People's Republic. Representatives from relevant government departments and the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee, including Qiao Guanhua, were invited to attend the reception.

Envoys from various countries accredited to China also attended the reception.

### 庆祝波兰国家复兴节  波兰驻华临时代办举行招待会

1968-07-23
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝波兰国家复兴节
    波兰驻华临时代办举行招待会
    新华社二十二日讯　波兰驻中国大使馆临时代办罗文斯基今天举行招待会，庆祝波兰人民共和国国家复兴节。政府有关部门和北京市革命委员会的负责人乔冠华等，应邀出席了招待会。
    各国驻中国使节也出席了招待会。
