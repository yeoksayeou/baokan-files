Title: The Final Settlement of Construction Costs for the Sana'a Cotton Textile Printing and Dyeing Factory, Built with Chinese Aid, is Signed in Sana'a
Author:
Date: 1968-07-12
Edition: 5

The signing ceremony for the final settlement of construction costs for the Sana'a Cotton Textile Printing and Dyeing Factory, built with Chinese aid, was held in Sana'a on the 9th.

Ahmed Abdul Said, Minister of Finance of Yemen, and Zheng Kangping, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Yemen, signed the final settlement on behalf of the Yemeni and Chinese sides, respectively.

Before the signing ceremony, Minister Said and Chargé d'Affaires Zheng had a friendly conversation.

Minister Said said, "The projects aided by China have low investment and quick results. We are grateful for the sincere aid from the Chinese government and people."

Chargé d'Affaires Zheng said, "The Chinese government and people firmly support the just struggle of the Yemeni people against imperialism and reactionaries."

### 我国援助也门建设的萨那棉纺织印染厂建厂费用最终结算书在萨那签字

1968-07-12
第5版()
专栏：

    我国援助也门建设的萨那棉纺织印染厂建厂费用最终结算书在萨那签字
    新华社萨那九日电 由中国援助也门建设的萨那棉纺织印染厂建厂费用最终结算书的签字仪式，九日在萨那举行。
    也门财政部长艾哈迈德·阿卜杜·赛义德和中国驻也门大使馆临时代办郑康平，分别代表也中双方在最终结算书上签了字。
    在签字仪式举行之前，赛义德部长和郑康平临时代办进行了友好的谈话。
    赛义德部长说：“中国援助建设的项目，投资少，收效快。我们感谢中国政府和人民的这种真诚的援助。”
    郑康平临时代办说，中国政府和人民坚决支持也门人民所进行的反对帝国主义和反动派的正义斗争。
