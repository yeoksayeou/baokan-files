Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Five U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-07-22
Edition: 5

The military and civilians of North Vietnam's Ha Tinh and Quang Binh provinces shot down five U.S. aircraft on July 12, 13, and 17 while repelling American air raids.

Additionally, it was reported that on July 16, a U.S. warship intruded into the territorial waters near Quang Binh province. Local military and civilians launched an attack on the invading warship.

### 越南北方军民击落美机五架

1968-07-22
第5版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机五架
    新华社河内二十日电　据这里公布：越南北方河静、广平两省军民，十二日、十三日和十七日在反击美国空中强盗的战斗中，击落美机五架。
    另据报道，十六日，一艘美国军舰侵入越南北方广平省附近的领海，当地军民对这艘强盗军舰进行了袭击。
