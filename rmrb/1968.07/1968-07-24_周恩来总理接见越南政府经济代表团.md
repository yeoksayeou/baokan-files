Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives the Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1968-07-24
Edition: 1

Premier Zhou Enlai today received the Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation led by Vice Premier Le Thanh Nghi of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Premier Zhou Enlai had a cordial and friendly conversation with Vice Premier Le Thanh Nghi and the delegation's deputy heads, Li Ban and Ngo Minh Loan.

Present at the reception were Vice Premiers Chen Yi and Li Xiannian of the State Council, as well as relevant department heads Wen Yucheng, Han Nianlong, Li Qiang, and Xie Huai.

The Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation, led by Vice Premier Le Thanh Nghi, returned to Beijing after visiting and touring Shanghai and other places.

Accompanied by Vice Minister Li Qiang of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, the Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation visited factories, rural people's communes, schools, and the Shanghai Industrial Exhibition in Shanghai and other places. They were warmly welcomed and enthusiastically received by local revolutionary committee leaders and revolutionary masses. The revolutionary masses firmly expressed to the Vietnamese friends that the 700 million Chinese people are determined to be a strong backing for the Vietnamese people, resolutely supporting the Vietnamese people to carry the anti-American national salvation war to the end and achieve final victory. Vice Premier Le Thanh Nghi and others praised the great victories achieved by the revolutionary masses in Shanghai and other places during the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and the tremendous achievements in promoting production while carrying out the revolution.

### 周恩来总理接见越南政府经济代表团

1968-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见越南政府经济代表团
    新华社二十三日讯　国务院总理周恩来，今天接见由越南民主共和国政府副总理黎清毅率领的越南政府经济代表团。周恩来总理同黎清毅副总理和代表团副团长李班、吴明鸾等进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有国务院副总理陈毅、李先念，以及有关方面负责人温玉成、韩念龙、李强、谢怀德。
    新华社二十三日讯　由越南民主共和国政府副总理黎清毅率领的越南政府经济代表团，参观访问了上海等地后，日前回到北京。
    越南政府经济代表团在对外贸易部副部长李强的陪同下，在上海等地参观访问了工厂、农村人民公社、学校和上海工业展览会。他们在参观访问中受到各地革命委员会的负责同志和革命群众的热烈欢迎和热情友好的接待。革命群众向越南朋友坚定地表示：七亿中国人民誓作越南人民的坚强后盾，坚决支持越南人民把抗美救国战争进行到底，直至取得最后的胜利。黎清毅副总理等赞扬上海等地的革命群众在无产阶级文化大革命中取得的伟大胜利，和在抓革命、促生产方面取得的巨大成就。
