Title: Lao Patriotic Military and People Shoot Down One U.S. Plane
Author:
Date: 1968-07-06
Edition: 5

On May 25, a U.S. helicopter attempted to infiltrate and drop paratroopers and spies into the liberated areas of Savannakhet Province. The Lao patriotic military and people, who were well-prepared, launched a brave assault and shot down the enemy aircraft on the spot, killing four American pilots aboard.

### 老挝爱国军民击落美机一架

1968-07-06
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国军民击落美机一架
    新华社康开三日电　据巴特寮电台广播：一架美国直升飞机，五月二十五日阴谋向沙湾拿吉省解放区投送土匪和特务，严阵以待的老挝爱国军民进行英勇袭击，当场击落这架敌机，并打死机上四名飞贼。
