Title: Workers, Peasants, and Students in Japan's Kanto Region Hold Rally
Author:
Date: 1968-07-30
Edition: 5

On July 28, workers, peasants, and students from various prefectures in Japan's Kanto region braved heavy rain to gather in front of the North Fuji military training ground in Yamanashi Prefecture. They held a rally demanding the withdrawal of U.S. imperialist military bases from Japan and the abolition of the Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty."
