Title: Military and Civilians in South Vietnam's Binh Thuan and Other Provinces Bravely Fight, Annihilating Over 3,000 Enemy Troops
Author:
Date: 1968-07-06
Edition: 5

Military and civilians in South Vietnam's Binh Thuan and other provinces bravely fight, annihilating over 3,000 enemy troops

Military and civilians in the central southern region of South Vietnam strike the enemy on water and land transportation routes, achieving new victories

Hanoi, July 5 (Xinhua) According to a report from the Liberation Radio of South Vietnam, military and civilians in Binh Thuan and other provinces in South Vietnam have recently bravely attacked American and puppet troops, achieving great victories.

The report says that from June 1 to 23, military and civilians in Binh Thuan province, in close coordination with battles on various fronts in the south, launched fierce attacks on the enemy, annihilating over 1,200 enemy troops. Among them, they completely annihilated four companies, nine platoons, and one squad of puppet troops, severely damaged two companies and one "pacification" unit, shot down and damaged seven enemy aircraft, destroyed and damaged nine military vehicles, and seized a large amount of weapons and military supplies.

From June 5 to 20, military and civilians in Phu Quoc province attacked the enemy in the provincial capital and surrounding towns, annihilating and capturing over 1,000 enemy troops. They captured five enemy strongholds and outposts, many barracks, sank and damaged 13 warships, shot down and damaged 10 enemy aircraft, and destroyed four cannons.

From June 18 to 24, military and civilians in Long An province's Thuan Duc and Tan Chu districts continuously attacked and ambushed the enemy, annihilating over 520 enemy troops, most of whom were American soldiers. They shot down four enemy aircraft, destroyed several armored vehicles, and seized a large amount of ammunition and military supplies.

Since June 10, military and civilians in Tra Vinh province have intensified their attacks on the enemy, annihilating over 440 enemy troops, destroying two military vehicles, and damaging two aircraft.

From June 14 to 16, guerrilla forces and local troops in the northern part of Quang Nam province bravely fought against the invading American and South Korean troops to protect the people's lives and property. They killed and wounded over 240 American and South Korean troops, destroyed seven military vehicles, almost all of which were armored vehicles.

On June 28, the people's liberation armed forces in the northern part of Binh Dinh province continuously ambushed the enemy three times in Phu Phu town, annihilating one company and severely damaging three companies of puppet troops.

On the night of June 17 to the early morning of June 18, local troops in Tay Ninh province's Tan Bien district shelled the American base in the Luong Nhai area, killing 80 American troops and destroying seven military vehicles and three cannons.

Hanoi, July 5 (Xinhua) According to a report from the Liberation Radio of South Vietnam, military and civilians in the central southern region of South Vietnam, from May 1 to June 15, attacked the enemy multiple times on various water and land transportation routes, achieving significant victories. They destroyed 129 enemy military vehicles and sank and damaged 101 enemy warships.

Military and civilians in My Tho province continuously attacked enemy convoys on the Fourth Highway. In May alone, they destroyed and damaged 79 enemy military vehicles, including many armored vehicles, turning this road into a graveyard for enemy armored vehicles.

People and guerrilla forces in many places such as Ben Tre and My Tho province destroyed many enemy transportation lines. They attacked the enemy on the Sixth Highway, Twenty-First Highway, Twenty-Fourth Highway, and Twenty-Eighth Highway, and built trenches and fortifications along the roads, destroying many enemy military vehicles.

Military and civilians in Ben Tre province sank and damaged 70 American warships on the Ong Cong and Ba Lai rivers, annihilating nearly 1,500 American troops, and smashed the so-called "riverine flotilla tactics" of the American invaders. Guerrilla forces in My Tho, Ben Tre, and Ben Cat provinces also continuously sank and damaged many enemy warships on the Mekong River, My Tho Canal, My Tho River, and My Tho River.

Hanoi, July 3 (Xinhua) According to a report from the Liberation Radio of South Vietnam, the liberation army in the northern part of Quang Tri province, from June 24 to 27, launched multiple attacks, annihilating over 160 American and puppet troops, damaging two enemy transport ships, and setting two enemy military warehouses on fire.

The report says that after bombarding the American base in Sa Ma on June 24 and 25, killing 60 American troops, the liberation army fiercely attacked two battalions of American and puppet troops "sweeping" in the area two kilometers west of Quan Lang on June 26, annihilating over 100 American and puppet troops.

On the night of June 27, American bandits used the cover of darkness to advance a large number of transport ships towards the port of Vinh. The liberation army artillery was ready and timely fired at the enemy ships and fiercely bombarded the enemy military warehouses near the port of Vinh, damaging two enemy transport ships and setting two enemy military warehouses on fire.

### 南越平顺等省军民英勇战斗歼敌三千多  南部中区军民在水陆交通线上打击敌人，取得新战果

1968-07-06
第5版()
专栏：

    南越平顺等省军民英勇战斗歼敌三千多
    南部中区军民在水陆交通线上打击敌人，取得新战果
    新华社河内五日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方平顺等省军民，最近英勇袭击美伪军，获得巨大战果。
    报道说，平顺省军民，紧密配合南方各个战场的战斗，在六月一日至二十三日，接连向敌人发起猛烈的进攻，共歼敌一千二百多名，其中全歼伪军四个连、九个排和一个班，重创两个连和一个“绥靖”队，击落、击伤敌机七架，击毁、击伤军车九辆，缴获许多武器和军用物资。
    芹苴省军民从六月五日到二十日，在省会和各个市镇周围地区袭击敌人，共歼灭和俘虏敌人一千多名，攻克敌人的据点和哨所五个、营房多幢，击沉、击伤战船十三艘，击落、击伤敌机十架，击毁大炮四门。
    隆安省芹德、新柱等县军民，在六月十八日到二十四日期间，接连袭击和截击敌人，共歼敌五百二十多名，其中大部分是美军，击落敌机四架，击毁装甲车多辆，缴获许多弹药和军用物资。
    茶荣省军民，从六月十日以来，加紧袭击敌人，共歼敌四百四十多名，击毁军车两辆，击伤飞机两架。
    广南省北部游击队和地方部队，为了粉碎美国侵略军和南朝鲜帮凶军的“扫荡”，保卫人民的生命财产，在六月十四日到十六日三天中，英勇打击来犯的敌人，共打死、打伤美国侵略军和南朝鲜帮凶军二百四十多名，击毁军车七辆，其中几乎全部是装甲车。
    边和省北部人民解放武装力量，在六月二十八日一天中，连续三次在福平镇截击敌人，共歼灭伪军一个连，重创三个连。
    西宁省盏盘县地方部队，六月十七日夜间到十八日凌晨，炮轰琥耐地区的美军驻地，消灭美国侵略军八十名，击毁军车七辆、大炮三门。
    新华社河内五日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方南部中区军民，在五月一日到六月十五日期间，在各条水、陆交通线上多次袭击敌人，获得重大战果，共击毁敌人的军车一百二十九辆，击沉、击伤敌人的战船一百零一艘。
    美萩省军民接连在第四号公路上袭击敌人车队，仅在五月份，就击毁、击伤敌人的军车七十九辆，其中有装甲车多辆，把这条路变成了敌人装甲车的坟场。
    槟椥、美萩等许多地方的人民和游击队破坏了敌人的许多交通线。他们在第六号公路、第二十一号公路、第二十四号公路和第二十八号公路上袭击敌人，并在路旁挖战壕和建筑工事，击毁了敌人许多军车。
    槟椥省军民在翁宗和巴莱等河流上击沉、击伤美国战船七十艘，歼灭美国侵略军近一千五百名，粉碎了美国侵略军的所谓“河上小舰队战术”。美萩、建祥和建丰等省游击队，也接连在湄公河、米市运河、西万古河和古羶河等河流上击沉、击伤敌人许多战船。
    新华社河内三日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方广治省北部地区的解放军，从六月二十四日到二十七日多次出击，消灭美伪军一百六十多名，击伤敌人运输船两艘，并使敌人两座军用仓库起火燃烧。
    报道说，解放军继二十四日和二十五日炮击沙马美军驻地，消灭美国侵略军六十名之后，于二十六日在关昂西面两公里的地方又猛烈袭击前来“扫荡”的两营美伪军，一举消灭美伪军一百多名。
    二十七日夜间，美国强盗利用黑夜向越门港口开进大批运输船。严阵以待的解放军炮兵及时向敌船开炮，并猛烈轰击越门港口附近的敌人军用仓库，击伤敌人运输船两艘，使敌人两座军用仓库起火燃烧。
