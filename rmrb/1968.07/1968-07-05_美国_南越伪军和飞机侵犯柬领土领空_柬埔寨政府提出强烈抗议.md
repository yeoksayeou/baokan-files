Title: U.S.—South Vietnamese Puppet Army and Aircraft Invade Cambodian Territory and Airspace
Author:
Date: 1968-07-05
Edition: 5

	U.S.—South Vietnamese Puppet Army and Aircraft Invade Cambodian Territory and Airspace
	Cambodian Government Lodges Strong Protest

	Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh: According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency, the Cambodian Ministry of Foreign Affairs on June 26 sent separate notes to the U.S. government and the Saigon puppet regime, strongly protesting the U.S.—South Vietnamese puppet forces' multiple attacks on Kompong Cham Province's Kompong Chhnang District on June 13, which caused material losses to local residents. The notes stated that the Royal Government firmly demands that the U.S. and the Saigon puppet authorities stop these criminal acts.

	Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, July 3: According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency, the Royal Government of Cambodia issued a statement on July 3, protesting the U.S.—South Vietnamese aircraft's intrusion into Cambodian airspace over Prey Veng Province on June 29 to conduct strafing, killing and injuring 18 Cambodian civilians in an unprecedented act of barbarism.

	The Royal Government of Cambodia pointed out in the statement the brutal nature of the U.S.'s massacre of the Cambodian people.

	The statement called on world public opinion to condemn this new crime by the U.S. and to force the U.S. to immediately stop its massacre of the Cambodian people and the people of Indochina.

### 美国—南越伪军和飞机侵犯柬领土领空  柬埔寨政府提出强烈抗议

1968-07-05
第5版()
专栏：

    美国—南越伪军和飞机侵犯柬领土领空
    柬埔寨政府提出强烈抗议
    新华社金边电　据柬埔寨新闻社报道：柬埔寨外交部六月二十六日分别照会美国政府和西贡傀儡政权，强烈抗议美国—南越伪军六月十三日对磅湛省棉末县多次发动袭击，给当地居民造成物质损失。照会说，王国政府坚决要求美国和西贡傀儡当局停止这种罪恶行径。
    新华社金边三日电据柬埔寨新闻社报道，柬埔寨王国政府三日发表声明，抗议美国—南越飞机六月二十九日侵入柬埔寨波萝勉省上空进行扫射，打死、打伤十八名柬埔寨居民的空前的野蛮行径。
    柬埔寨王国政府在声明中指出了美国对柬埔寨人民的这次大屠杀的残暴性质。
    声明呼吁世界舆论对美国的这一新罪行进行必要的谴责，迫使美国立即停止对柬埔寨人民和印度支那人民的屠杀。
