Title: Angolan Guerrillas Severely Defeat Portuguese Colonial Forces
Author:
Date: 1968-07-04
Edition: 6

Angolan guerrillas severely defeat Portuguese colonial forces, killing, wounding, and capturing dozens of Portuguese colonial soldiers.

	CAIRO, July 2 (Xinhua) -- According to a battle report released by the office of the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola (UNITA) in Cairo on July 2, Angolan guerrillas launched six battles from April 6 to May 4, killing, wounding, and capturing dozens of Portuguese colonial soldiers.

	The report reaffirmed the "ironclad determination of the heroic Angolan army to carry out the people's war against Portuguese colonialism and for national liberation to the end," and expressed readiness to fight a protracted war.

	The report said that on April 6, UNITA guerrillas ambushed a batch of Portuguese troops near Cissamba in the Upper Kuvitu region, killing six enemy soldiers and capturing nine. On the same day, guerrillas launched a second attack in the same area, killing fifteen enemy soldiers.

	On April 18, UNITA fighters engaged in combat with Portuguese colonial forces in the Kuando-Kubango province, killing some enemies and seizing fifteen guns and a large amount of ammunition.

	On April 19, Angolan guerrillas fought against Portuguese troops in the Kongongo region, killing eight enemy soldiers and wounding fourteen, and seizing some weapons.

	On April 23, UNITA forces annihilated an enemy force stationed in Dala, Lunda province, destroying the Dala administrative outpost and burning six Portuguese military vehicles.

	The report said that on May 4, Angolan guerrillas ambushed enemy forces near the Zilonji River in the Kassanje district of Moxico province, killing three and wounding four.

### 安哥拉游击队狠揍葡萄牙殖民军  打死打伤和俘虏葡萄牙殖民军数十人

1968-07-04
第6版()
专栏：

    安哥拉游击队狠揍葡萄牙殖民军
    打死打伤和俘虏葡萄牙殖民军数十人
    新华社开罗二日电　据争取安哥拉彻底独立全国联盟驻开罗办事处二日发表的战报说，安哥拉游击队在从四月六日到五月四日的一个月中，发动了六次战斗，共打死、打伤和俘虏葡萄牙殖民军数十人。
    战报重申“英雄的安哥拉军队要把反对葡萄牙殖民主义和争取民族解放的人民战争进行到底的钢铁般的决心”，并且表示准备打持久战。
    战报说，四月六日，争取安哥拉彻底独立全国联盟的游击队在上克维图州的齐辛巴附近伏击一批葡萄牙军队，打死敌军士兵六名，俘虏九名。同一天，游击队在这个地区发动的第二次袭击中，又打死敌军士兵十五名。
    四月十八日，争取安哥拉彻底独立全国联盟的战士在宽多—库班戈省同葡萄牙殖民军交战，打死一些敌人，缴获十五枝枪和大量弹药。
    四月十九日，安哥拉游击队在康贡加地区同葡萄牙军队作战，打死敌军士兵八名，打伤十四名，缴获一些武器。
    四月二十三日，争取安哥拉彻底独立全国联盟的部队全歼驻扎在隆达省达拉的一支敌军，摧毁了达拉的行政据点，焚烧葡军军车六辆。
    战报说，五月四日，安哥拉游击队在穆西库省卡桑巴州齐隆吉河附近的一次伏击中，打死敌人三名，打伤四名。
