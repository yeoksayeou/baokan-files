Title: Strive for Agricultural Bounty: Farmers of Agricultural Cooperative Push for a Bountiful Harvest This Year
Author:
Date: 1968-07-02
Edition: 6

	Farmers across Albania are vigorously expanding irrigation efforts and strengthening field management of crops, striving for a bountiful harvest this year.

	According to reports from the New China News Agency in Tirana and the Albanian News Agency, large-scale irrigation of farmlands has begun in the plains of Albania. Irrigation of crops is continuing uninterrupted in various regions. In Rushnia, one of the country's major agricultural areas, more than one-third of the irrigation zones have already been irrigated.

	Currently, all regions of Albania are utilizing new irrigation canals, reservoirs, and water pumps to further expand the area under irrigation. This can irrigate approximately 53 percent of the total cultivated land.

	While focusing on irrigation work, various regions have also started field management of sown crops, particularly corn, sugar beets, and cotton.

### 大力展开农田灌溉　加强农作物田间管理  阿农业社社员力争今年农业丰收

1968-07-02
第6版()
专栏：

    大力展开农田灌溉　加强农作物田间管理
    阿农业社社员力争今年农业丰收
    据新华社地拉那电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道：阿尔巴尼亚各地正在大力展开农田灌溉，加强农作物的田间管理工作，力争今年农业丰收。
    阿尔巴尼亚平原地区已开始大规模农田灌溉，各地都在继续不断地灌溉农作物。在全国主要农业区之一鲁施尼亚，到目前为止，已经有三分之一以上的灌溉区得到了灌溉。
    目前，阿尔巴尼亚全国各地区，正在利用新灌溉渠、水库和抽水机，来进一步扩大灌溉面积。这大约可以灌溉全部耕地面积的百分之五十三左右。
    在抓灌溉工作的同时，各地还开始了对播种作物的田间管理，特别是玉米、甜菜、棉花等作物。
