Title: U.S. Warships and Aircraft Violate Our Territorial Waters and Airspace
Author:
Date: 1968-07-08
Edition: 5

U.S. warships and aircraft have violated our territorial waters and airspace. We issue the 460th severe warning.

New China News Agency, July 7th: On July 6th, from 11:51 AM to 12:39 PM, a U.S. warship entered our territorial waters south of Danggan Island in Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 4:21 PM to 2:15 AM on July 7th, another U.S. warship entered our territorial waters northeast of Pingtan Island in Fujian Province. On the same day, from 3:58 PM to 4:15 PM, a U.S. military aircraft entered our airspace over Dong Island and Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. On July 7th, from 5:52 AM to 8:51 AM, another U.S. warship entered our territorial waters south of Pingtan Island in Fujian Province. On the same day, from 1:06 PM to 1:38 PM, another U.S. military aircraft entered our airspace over Yongxing Island and Dong Island in the Xisha Islands. In response to the aforementioned military provocations by U.S. warships and military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the ministry, issues the 460th severe warning.

### 美国军舰和飞机侵犯我领海领空  我提出第四百六十次严重警告

1968-07-08
第5版()
专栏：

    美国军舰和飞机侵犯我领海领空
    我提出第四百六十次严重警告
    新华社七日讯　七月六日十一时五十一分至十二时三十九分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我广东省担杆岛以南领海。同日十六时二十一分至七日二时十五分，又有美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省平潭岛东北地区领海。同日十五时五十八分至十六时十五分，又有美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的东岛、永兴岛地区领空。七月七日五时五十二分至八时五十一分，又有美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省平潭岛以南领海。同日十三时零六分至十三时三十八分，又有美国军用飞机一架，侵入我西沙群岛的永兴岛、东岛地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的上述军事挑衅活动，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百六十次严重警告。
