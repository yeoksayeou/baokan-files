Title: China and Cuba Sign 1968 Trade Agreement in Havana
Author:
Date: 1968-07-24
Edition: 6

The trade delegation of the People's Republic of China and the trade delegation of the Revolutionary Government of Cuba, after friendly talks, signed the 1968 Sino-Cuban Trade Agreement in Havana on July 19.

According to the terms of the trade agreement, China will supply Cuba with rice, soybeans, lard, canned meat, textiles, steel, machinery, chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and daily necessities. Cuba will supply China with raw sugar and mineral products.

### 中古在哈瓦那签订今年贸易议定书

1968-07-24
第6版()
专栏：

    中古在哈瓦那签订今年贸易议定书
    新华社二十三日讯　中华人民共和国政府贸易代表团和古巴共和国革命政府贸易代表团经过友好会谈以后，于七月十九日在哈瓦那签订了一九六八年中古贸易议定书。
    根据贸易议定书的规定，中国方面将供应古巴大米、大豆、猪油、肉罐头、纺织品、钢材、机械、化工、医药及日用品等。古巴方面将供应中国原糖及矿产品等。
