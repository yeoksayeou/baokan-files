Title: Celebrating the Cuban National Uprising Day
Author:
Date: 1968-07-27
Edition: 6

The chargé d'affaires of the Cuban Embassy in China, Mo La, held a reception today to celebrate the Cuban National Uprising Day.

Representatives from relevant departments of the Chinese government, the People's Liberation Army, the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee, and other relevant parties, including Qiao Guanhua, Zhang Tianyun, Xiang Qian, and Shen Jian, were invited to attend the reception.

Ambassadors from various countries to China also attended the reception.

### 庆祝古巴全国起义日  古巴驻华使馆临时代办举行招待会

1968-07-27
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝古巴全国起义日
    古巴驻华使馆临时代办举行招待会
    新华社二十六日讯　古巴共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办莫拉今天举行招待会，庆祝古巴全国起义日。
    我国政府有关部门、中国人民解放军、北京市革命委员会和有关方面的负责人乔冠华、张天云、向前、申健等，应邀出席了招待会。
    各国驻中国使节也出席了招待会。
