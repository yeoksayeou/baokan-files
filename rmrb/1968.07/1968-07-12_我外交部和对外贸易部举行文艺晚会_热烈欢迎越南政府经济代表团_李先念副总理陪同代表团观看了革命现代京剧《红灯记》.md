Title: Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Ministry of Foreign Trade Hold Art Evening
Author:
Date: 1968-07-12
Edition: 5

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Ministry of Foreign Trade held an art evening to warmly welcome the Vietnamese government economic delegation led by Deputy Prime Minister Le Thanh Nghi of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Vice Premier Li Xiannian accompanied Deputy Prime Minister Le Thanh Nghi and other Vietnamese friends, including Vice Chairman Li Ban and Wu Mingluan, to watch the revolutionary modern Beijing opera "The Red Lantern."

Diplomatic officials from the Vietnamese Embassy in China, Nguyen Van Khoa, head of the permanent delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China, and other Vietnamese friends in Beijing were invited to attend the evening.

After the performance, Deputy Prime Minister Le Thanh Nghi and others, accompanied by Vice Premier Li Xiannian, went on stage to shake hands warmly with the performers, congratulating them on their successful performance.

Those present at the evening also included relevant officials from the Chinese government departments, the People's Liberation Army, and the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee, including Wen Yuzeng, Han Nianlong, Li Qiang, Xie Huaiyi, Ding Xilin, and Xiang Qian.

### 我外交部和对外贸易部举行文艺晚会  热烈欢迎越南政府经济代表团  李先念副总理陪同代表团观看了革命现代京剧《红灯记》

1968-07-12
第5版()
专栏：

    我外交部和对外贸易部举行文艺晚会
    热烈欢迎越南政府经济代表团
    李先念副总理陪同代表团观看了革命现代京剧《红灯记》
    新华社十一日讯　外交部和对外贸易部今天举行文艺晚会，热烈欢迎由团长、越南民主共和国政府副总理黎清毅率领的越南政府经济代表团。
    国务院副总理李先念陪同黎清毅副总理和代表团副团长李班、吴明鸾等越南朋友，观看了革命现代京剧《红灯记》。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆外交官员，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长阮文广，以及在北京的越南朋友，应邀出席了晚会。
    演出结束，黎清毅副总理等由李先念副总理陪同，走上舞台，同演员们热烈握手，祝他们演出成功。
    出席晚会的，还有我国政府有关部门、中国人民解放军、北京市革命委员会等有关方面的负责人温玉成、韩念龙、李强、谢怀德、丁西林、向前等。
