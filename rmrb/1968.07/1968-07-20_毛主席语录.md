Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1968-07-20
Edition: 1

    The basic experiences of the revolutionary committees are three: one is the representation of revolutionary cadres, one is the representation of the military, and one is the representation of revolutionary masses, achieving the three-in-one combination of the revolution. The revolutionary committees should implement unified leadership, break down overlapping administrative bodies, streamline administration and personnel, and organize a revolutionary and people-connected leading cadre team.

### 毛主席语录

1968-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    革命委员会的基本经验有三条：一条是有革命干部的代表，一条是有军队的代表，一条是有革命群众的代表，实现了革命的三结合。革命委员会要实行一元化的领导，打破重叠的行政机构，精兵简政，组织起一个革命化的联系群众的领导班子。
