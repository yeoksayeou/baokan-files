Title: Tanzania's National Agricultural and Trade Fair Opens
Author:
Date: 1968-07-06
Edition: 5

Tanzania's National Agricultural and Trade Fair opens

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Dar es Salaam, July 4th: Tanzania's annual "National Agricultural and Trade Fair" opened this morning by the First Vice President Karume. Hundreds of people from various sectors of the capital attended the opening ceremony. In the afternoon, President Nyerere visited the fair.

	The "National Agricultural and Trade Fair," held annually since 1964, is part of Tanzania's national celebration of the "Uhuru" (Independence) Day on July 7th. This national holiday, established to commemorate the founding of the Tanganyika African National Union on July 7, 1954, begins its festivities on July 1st and concludes on the 8th.

	This year's fair features a wide variety of locally produced agricultural and industrial products from Tanzania.

	The Tanzanian government's relevant departments have set up exhibition halls at the fair to showcase the progress made in various sectors over the past year. A notable exhibition hall is the "Friendship Textile Factory," built with Chinese aid. Tanzanian visitors were delighted to see fabrics made from local cotton and "kanga" (traditional Tanzanian women's clothing) in this hall.

### 坦桑尼亚“全国农业和贸易博览会”开幕

1968-07-06
第5版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚“全国农业和贸易博览会”开幕
    据新华社达累斯萨拉姆四日电　坦桑尼亚本年度“全国农业和贸易博览会”四日上午在这里由第一副总统卡鲁姆揭幕。参加开幕式的有坦桑尼亚首都各界人士数百人。当天下午，尼雷尔总统参观了这个博览会。
    自从一九六四年以来每年举行一次的“全国农业和贸易博览会”，是坦桑尼亚“七七”（七月七日）全国性节日庆祝活动的一部分。为纪念一九五四年七月 七日坦噶尼喀非洲民族联盟成立而规定的这个节日，从七月一日开始庆祝活动，到八日为止。
    今年博览会的特色是展出品种繁多的坦桑尼亚本国的农产品和工业产品。
    坦桑尼亚政府各个有关的部都在博览会设立了展览馆，展示过去一年各个部门所取得的进步。一个引人注目的展览馆是中国援助建设的“友谊纺织厂”的展览馆。坦桑尼亚观众在这个馆看到用本国棉花生产的花布和“康加”（坦桑尼亚妇女的服装）时，感到非常高兴。
