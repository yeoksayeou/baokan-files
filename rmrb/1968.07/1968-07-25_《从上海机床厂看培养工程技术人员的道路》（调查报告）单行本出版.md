Title: On the Road to Cultivating Engineering and Technical Personnel: A Report from the Shanghai Machine Tool Factory
Author:
Date: 1968-07-25
Edition: 2

The single-volume edition of the investigative report "On the Road to Cultivating Engineering and Technical Personnel: A Report from the Shanghai Machine Tool Factory," which conveys the latest instructions from our great leader Chairman Mao, has been published by the People's Publishing House. Starting from July 24th, it will be available at the Xinhua Bookstore in Beijing and will soon be distributed to Xinhua Bookstores across the country.

### 《从上海机床厂看培养工程技术人员的道路》（调查报告）单行本出版

1968-07-25
第2版()
专栏：

    《从上海机床厂看培养工程技术人员的道路》（调查报告）单行本出版
    新华社二十四日讯　传达了伟大领袖毛主席最新指示的《人民日报》重要编者按和文汇报记者、新华社记者合写的调查报告《从上海机床厂看培养工程技术人员的道路》，已由人民出版社出版单行本，二十四日起开始在北京新华书店发行，并将在全国各地新华书店陆续发行。
