Title: China Red Cross Donates 100,000 Rupees to Aid East Pakistan Flood Victims
Author:
Date: 1968-07-21
Edition: 6

	The Chinese Red Cross has donated 100,000 rupees (equivalent to 52,000 yuan) to aid the flood victims in East Pakistan.

	On the 19th, the Chinese Charge d'Affaires in Pakistan, Xu Ying, presented this donation to the Pakistani Ministry of Foreign Affairs. The Chinese Red Cross also sent a telegram to the Pakistani Red Cross, expressing the Chinese people's deep concern and warm regards to the Pakistani flood victims.

	According to Pakistani newspapers, heavy rains have been falling in East Pakistan since early July, causing the water levels of seven major rivers to rise sharply. The floods have destroyed many houses and crops, leaving a large number of residents affected. The relevant authorities in Pakistan have taken some relief measures to aid the flood victims.

### 我红十字会赠款十万卢比救济东巴基斯坦水灾难民

1968-07-21
第6版()
专栏：

    我红十字会赠款十万卢比救济东巴基斯坦水灾难民
    新华社拉瓦尔品第十九日电　中国红十字会捐赠了十万卢比（合人民币五万二千元），救济东巴基斯坦遭受水灾的难民。
    中国驻巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办徐英十九日把这笔赠款送交巴基斯坦外交部。中国红十字会还打电报给巴基斯坦红十字会，转达中国人民对巴基斯坦受灾难民的巨大关怀和亲切问候。
    据巴基斯坦报纸报道，东巴基斯坦从七月初连降暴雨，当地的七条主要河流的水位急剧上涨。洪水毁坏了许多地方的房屋和庄稼，使大批居民受灾。巴基斯坦有关方面已采取一些救济措施，救济受灾难民。
