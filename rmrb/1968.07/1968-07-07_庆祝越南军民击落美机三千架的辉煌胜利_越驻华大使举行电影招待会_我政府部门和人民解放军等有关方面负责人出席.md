Title: Celebrating the Glorious Victory of Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shooting Down 3,000 U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-07-07
Edition: 6

To celebrate the glorious victory of the Vietnamese military and civilians in shooting down 3,000 U.S. aggressor aircraft, Ambassador Vu Minh Quan of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China held a film screening event in Beijing today. The event featured films such as "Sketches of Guangping" that depict the Vietnamese military and civilians' fierce attacks on U.S. air pirates.

Relevant leaders from the Chinese government departments, the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee, including Wang Xinting, Han Nianlong, and Zhou Rongguo, as well as representatives from the Chinese People's Liberation Army, attended the film screening event.

Representative Nguyen Van Quang of the Permanent Mission of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam to China also attended the film screening event.

### 庆祝越南军民击落美机三千架的辉煌胜利  越驻华大使举行电影招待会  我政府部门和人民解放军等有关方面负责人出席

1968-07-07
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝越南军民击落美机三千架的辉煌胜利
    越驻华大使举行电影招待会
    我政府部门和人民解放军等有关方面负责人出席
    新华社六日讯　为越南军民取得击落美国强盗飞机三千架的辉煌胜利，越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾，今天在北京举行电影招待会。招待会上放映了《广平速写》等反映越南军民痛击美国飞贼的影片。
    我国政府部门、中国人民解放军和北京市革命委员会等有关方面负责人王新亭、韩念龙、周荣国，以及中国人民解放军指战员代表，出席了电影招待会。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长阮文广也出席了电影招待会。
