Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1968-07-24
Edition: 1

	Knowledgeable people, if they do not combine with the workers and peasants, will achieve nothing. The final distinction between revolutionary, non-revolutionary, or counter-revolutionary intellectuals lies in whether they are willing and able to combine with the workers and peasants.

### 毛主席语录

1968-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    知识分子如果不和工农民众相结合，则将一事无成。革命的或不革命的或反革命的知识分子的最后的分界，看其是否愿意并且实行和工农民众相结合。
