Title: Yemen's Prime Minister Inspects the Republic Hospital
Author:
Date: 1968-07-19
Edition: 6

Yemen's Prime Minister and Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces, Hassan Amri, inspected the Republic Hospital here on the 16th and, accompanied by the head of the Chinese medical team, toured the surgical ward and operating room where Chinese medical personnel are working. He said, "Chinese medical personnel have made contributions to the Yemeni people, and we express our gratitude for this."

### 也门总理视察共和国医院

1968-07-19
第6版()
专栏：

    也门总理视察共和国医院
    新华社萨那十六日电　也门总理兼武装部队总司令哈桑·阿姆里十六日视察了这里的共和国医院，并在中国医疗队负责人的陪同下，参观了有中国医务人员参加工作的外科病房和手术室。他说，中国医务人员为也门人民作出了贡献，我们对此表示感谢。
