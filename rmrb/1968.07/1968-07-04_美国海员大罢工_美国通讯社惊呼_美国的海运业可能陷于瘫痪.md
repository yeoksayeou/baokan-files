Title: American Seamen Launch Nationwide Strike
Author:
Date: 1968-07-04
Edition: 5

Washington, July 1: More than fifty thousand American seamen began a nationwide strike on June 29, protesting the management's stubborn refusal to meet their reasonable demand for a wage increase.

The strike has already paralyzed over a hundred American-flagged merchant ships in various U.S. ports, preventing them from sailing. The American news agency warned that the strike could further halt nearly 60 percent of American merchant ships, exclaiming, "America's maritime industry may be paralyzed."

### 美国海员大罢工  美国通讯社惊呼“美国的海运业可能陷于瘫痪”

1968-07-04
第5版()
专栏：

    美国海员大罢工
    美国通讯社惊呼“美国的海运业可能陷于瘫痪”
    新华社一日讯　华盛顿消息：五万多名美国海员从六月二十九日起举行全国性罢工，抗议资方顽固拒绝他们提出的增加工资的合理要求。
    海员们的罢工已经使一百多艘悬挂美国国旗的商船瘫痪在美国各港口，不能行驶。美国通讯社惊呼，罢工有可能进一步使美国将近百分之六十的商船停驶，“美国的海运业可能陷于瘫痪”。
