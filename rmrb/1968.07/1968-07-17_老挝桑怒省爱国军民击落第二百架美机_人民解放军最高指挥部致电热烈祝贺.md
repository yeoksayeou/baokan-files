Title: Patriotic Military and Civilians of Xam Neua Province in Laos Shoot Down the 200th U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-07-17
Edition: 5

Patriotic military and civilians of Xam Neua Province in Laos shoot down the 200th U.S. aircraft

The highest command of the People's Liberation Army sends a telegram to extend warm congratulations

KANGAY, July 16 (Xinhua) — According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio station: On July 11, the patriotic military and civilians of Xam Neua Province shot down one U.S. aircraft during a counterattack against American air pirates. Thus, since May 17, 1964, the patriotic military and civilians of Xam Neua Province have shot down a total of 200 U.S. aggressor aircraft.

On July 15, the highest command of the Lao People's Liberation Army sent a telegram to the patriotic military and civilians of Xam Neua Province, warmly congratulating them on their glorious victory of shooting down the 200th enemy aircraft.

The telegram said: The above-mentioned victory once again demonstrates that the Lao patriotic military and civilians possess the iron will to shatter the so-called air superiority boasted by the United States and to defend the liberated areas.

### 老挝桑怒省爱国军民击落第二百架美机  人民解放军最高指挥部致电热烈祝贺

1968-07-17
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝桑怒省爱国军民击落第二百架美机
    人民解放军最高指挥部致电热烈祝贺
    新华社康开十六日电　据巴特寮电台广播：桑怒省爱国军民，十一日在反击美国空中强盗的战斗中，击落美机一架。这样，桑怒省爱国军民从一九六四年五月十七日以来共击落了美国强盗飞机两百架。
    老挝人民解放军最高指挥部十五日打电报给桑怒省爱国军民，热烈祝贺他们击落第二百架敌机的辉煌胜利。
    贺电说，上述胜利再一次表明，老挝爱国军民具有粉碎美国吹嘘的所谓空中优势和保卫解放区的钢铁决心。
