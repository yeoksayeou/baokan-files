Title: The Rapid Development of Albania's Copper Industry
Author:
Date: 1968-07-18
Edition: 6

Albania's copper industry has seen rapid development during the third year of the Fourth Five-Year Plan (1966-1970). In mining, smelting, and further processing of copper, this industry has made remarkable strides. The workers in this sector are honorably fulfilling their tasks to ensure that the total copper production during the Fourth Five-Year Plan will be nearly three times that of the Third Five-Year Plan.

In the efforts to achieve the great tasks set by the Fourth Five-Year Plan, the geological department has taken the lead. They have discovered a new copper mine, which is already being exploited, and are preparing to open another copper mine this year.

In the first six months of the third year of the Fourth Five-Year Plan, the production plans for copper mining, electrolytic copper, refined copper, and copper wire have all been overfulfilled.

In the realization of the tasks set by the Fourth Five-Year Plan, the copper smelting plant and the copper wire factory have been expanded. Currently, the copper wire factory produces over 200 varieties of copper wire, including bare copper wire, insulated copper wire, and rolled copper wire for the electrical industry. In the copper smelting plant, in addition to producing copper rods and refined copper for the copper wire factory and for export, they also produce brass for various sectors of the national economy.

The Elbasan Steel Rolling Mill is Albania's first newly built steel industry enterprise. It plays a crucial role in the rapid development of the country's agriculture and industry, providing urgently needed steel materials for various construction projects. 专栏：欧洲伟大的社会主义明灯

    阿尔巴尼亚铜矿工业迅速发展
    第四个五年计划期间铜的总产量将比第三个五年计划的总产量增加近三倍
    今年头六个月中，铜矿采掘、泡铜、精制铜和铜丝的生产计划已超额完成
    新华社地拉那十七日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道：阿尔巴尼亚的铜矿工业在第四个五年计划（一九六六——一九七○年）的第三年，无论在采矿、冶炼，还是在铜的进一步加工方面，都有了迅猛的发展。这个工业部门的工人们正在光荣地完成自己的任务，以使第四个五年计划期间铜的总产量比第三个五年计划期间的总产量增产百分之二百九十七。
    在为胜利完成第四个五年计划的伟大任务而作出的努力中，地质部门一马当先，他们发现的一个新铜矿已在开采，而且准备在今年把另一个铜矿提交开采。
    在第四个五年计划的第三年的头六个月中，铜矿采掘，泡铜、精制铜和铜丝的生产计划都超额完成任务。
    在实现第四个五年计划规定的各项任务中，扩建了炼铜厂和铜丝厂。现在，铜丝厂出产供电气技术工业用的素铜丝、绝缘铜丝和卷用铜丝等二百多种品种。在炼铜厂里，除了生产供铜丝厂和出口用的铜条和精制铜之外，还为国民经济各部门生产黄铜。（附图片）
    爱尔巴桑轧钢厂是阿尔巴尼亚第一个新建的钢铁工业企业，它对全国工农业的迅速发展起着重要作用，为各项建设工程提供了急需的钢材。图为爱尔巴桑轧钢厂的一车间。　新华社稿
