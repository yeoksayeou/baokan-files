Title: Chinese Photo Exhibition Opens in Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1968-07-06
Edition: 5

The photo exhibition of the People's Republic of China opened on July 1 in Badulla, central Ceylon.

The exhibition was initiated and organized by the Ceylon-China Friendship Association's Badulla branch. Over 500 people from various walks of life in Badulla attended the opening ceremony. The exhibition received a large number of visitors.

Many visitors enthusiastically praised the great achievements of the Chinese people under the leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao. One visitor wrote in the guest book: "Through visiting the exhibition, I have seen that the people of New China are advancing in big strides under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought."

### 我图片展览会在锡兰开幕

1968-07-06
第5版()
专栏：

    我图片展览会在锡兰开幕
    新华社科伦坡五日电　中华人民共和国图片展览会七月一日在锡兰中部的巴杜拉开幕。
    这个展览会是由锡中友好协会巴杜拉分会发起和组织的。五百多名巴杜拉各阶层人士出席了开幕式。展览会接待了大批观众。
    许多观众热情地赞扬中国人民在伟大领袖毛主席的领导下所取得的巨大成就。一位参观者在观众留言簿上写道：“通过参观展览会，我看到了新中国人民在毛泽东思想的指导下，正在大踏步前进。”
