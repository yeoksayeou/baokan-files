U.S.-South Vietnamese Puppet Forces Violate Cambodian Airspace
Cambodian Foreign Ministry Lodges Strong Protest

NEW DELHI, July 21 -- According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency, the Cambodian Foreign Ministry sent separate notes to the U.S. government and the Saigon puppet regime on July 18, strongly protesting the repeated violations of Cambodian airspace by U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet forces. Between May 28 and June 7, these forces invaded the airspace of Cambodia's Pursat, Prey Veng, and Takeo provinces 14 times, firing rockets and dropping chemical poisons, causing property damage and endangering the lives of Cambodian residents.

The notes stated that the Royal Government of Cambodia demands an immediate halt to these acts of aggression.

### 美国—南越伪军飞机侵犯柬领空  柬埔寨外交部提出强烈抗议

1968-07-24
第6版()
专栏：

    美国—南越伪军飞机侵犯柬领空
    柬埔寨外交部提出强烈抗议
    新华社金边二十一日电　据柬埔寨新闻社报道，柬埔寨外交部七月十八日分别照会美国政府和西贡傀儡集团，强烈抗议美国—南越伪军的飞机从五月二十八日到六月七日期间，先后十四次侵犯柬埔寨磅湛省、波萝勉省和柴桢省的领空，并且多次发射火箭和撒放化学毒剂，给柬埔寨居民造成财产损失和生命危险。
    照会说，柬埔寨王国政府要求停止这种侵略行径。
