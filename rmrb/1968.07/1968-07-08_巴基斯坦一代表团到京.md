Title: A Delegation from Pakistan Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-07-08
Edition: 5

A delegation from the West Pakistan Industrial Development Corporation, led by the General Manager of the Heavy Machinery Department, Bashir, arrived in Beijing by airplane today. The delegation consists of three members.

### 巴基斯坦一代表团到京

1968-07-08
第5版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦一代表团到京
    新华社七日讯　由巴基斯坦西巴工业发展公司重机处总经理巴锡斯率领的巴基斯坦西巴工业发展公司代表团一行三人，今天乘飞机到达北京。
