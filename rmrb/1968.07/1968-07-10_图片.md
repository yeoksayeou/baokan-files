Title: Massive Anti-American Protest in Yemen's Capital
Author:
Date: 1968-07-10
Edition: 6

Facing the armed aggression of U.S. imperialism and Israel, the broad masses of the people in various Arab countries are united in their hatred of the enemy and are determined to resist the U.S.-Israeli aggression. This is a large-scale anti-American demonstration held by the people of Yemen in the capital, Sana'a.
