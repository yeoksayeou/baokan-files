Title: Revolutionary Workers and Intellectuals at Changchun Automobile Factory Unite to Criticize and Fight the Class Enemy
Author:
Date: 1968-07-11
Edition: 4

Revolutionary workers and revolutionary intellectuals at Changchun Automobile Factory, under the leadership of the revolutionary committee, are taking class struggle as the key link. They are carrying out thorough and sustained revolutionary criticism, firmly grasping revolution, and vigorously promoting production. This is a scene of revolutionary workers and revolutionary intellectuals uniting as one to launch a fierce attack on the class enemy.
