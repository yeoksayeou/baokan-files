Title: The Albanian "Unity" Newspaper Hails the Thriving Revolutionary Armed Struggle of the Indonesian People
Author:
Date: 1968-07-18
Edition: 6

The Albanian "Unity" newspaper published a short commentary on July 14, enthusiastically hailing the thriving revolutionary armed struggle of the Indonesian people.

The commentary pointed out that the Indonesian reactionaries, with the support of American imperialism and the Khrushchevite revisionists, are maintaining their regime through counter-revolutionary violence and suppressing revolutionary forces. However, the Indonesian revolutionaries have not lost their fighting spirit. Together with the Indonesian people, they are waging a heroic and resolute struggle against the Indonesian reactionaries.

The commentary said that since the beginning of this year, the revolutionary armed struggle of the Indonesian people has made tremendous progress and is achieving one victory after another.

The commentary pointed out that no force in the world can extinguish the flames of revolutionary war that have already been ignited in Indonesia. Despite the efforts of the imperialists and the Khrushchevite revisionists to support the Suharto-Nasution clique, the Indonesian people will surely wipe out this clique on Indonesian soil.

### 阿《团结报》欢呼印尼人民革命武装斗争蓬勃发展  印尼革命战争烈火任何力量扑灭不了  美帝苏修扶植的印尼反动派必将被印尼人民消灭干净

1968-07-18
第6版()
专栏：

    阿《团结报》欢呼印尼人民革命武装斗争蓬勃发展
    印尼革命战争烈火任何力量扑灭不了
    美帝苏修扶植的印尼反动派必将被印尼人民消灭干净
    新华社地拉那十四日电　阿尔巴尼亚《团结报》十四日发表短评，热烈欢呼印度尼西亚人民革命武装斗争的蓬勃发展。
    评论指出，印度尼西亚反动派在美帝国主义和赫鲁晓夫修正主义者的支持下，正在以反革命的暴力维持其政权，镇压革命力量。但是，印度尼西亚革命者并没有丧失斗志。他们同印度尼西亚人民一起，同印度尼西亚反动派进行着英勇的坚定的斗争。
    评论说，今年以来，印度尼西亚人民的革命武装斗争有了巨大的发展，正在取得一个又一个的胜利。
    评论指出，世界上没有任何力量能够扑灭在印度尼西亚已经点燃起来的革命战争的烈火。尽管帝国主义者和赫鲁晓夫修正主义者竭力扶植苏哈托—纳苏蒂安集团，但是印度尼西亚人民必将在印度尼西亚的土地上把这个集团消灭干净。
