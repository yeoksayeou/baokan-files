Title: Angry Brazilian Students Protest Against U.S. and Dictatorship
Author:
Date: 1968-07-09
Edition: 6

Angry Brazilian students, in a demonstration against the U.S. and the dictatorship, threw stones at the U.S. Embassy, frightening the Americans so much that they dared not show their faces. (New China News Agency)
