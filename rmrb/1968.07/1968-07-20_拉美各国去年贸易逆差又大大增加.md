Title: Trade Deficits of Latin American Countries Significantly Increased Last Year
Author:
Date: 1968-07-20
Edition: 5

American imperialism, under the guise of "economic integration," has intensified its economic plunder of the Central American countries. Due to the policy of buying low and selling high implemented by U.S. monopolistic capital, the economies of Central American countries have suffered severe losses, frequently resulting in huge trade deficits. Last year, these countries' payment deficits reached six hundred million dollars. The trade deficit of Guatemala alone increased by twenty-three million dollars compared to the previous year.

### 拉美各国去年贸易逆差又大大增加

1968-07-20
第5版()
专栏：简明新闻

    拉美各国去年贸易逆差又大大增加
    美帝国主义在“经济一体化”的幌子下，加紧了对中美洲各国的经济掠夺。
    由于美国垄断资本实行贱买贵卖政策，使中美洲国家的经济遭到严重损失，经常出现巨额贸易入超。去年，这些国家支付赤字高达六亿美元。仅危地马拉一国的贸易逆差，就比前年增加了二千三百万美元。
