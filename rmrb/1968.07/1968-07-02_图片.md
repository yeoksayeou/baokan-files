Title: Broad Masses of the People's Liberation Army Lanzhou Troops Celebrate the News of Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin's Reception
Author:
Date: 1968-07-02
Edition: 4

The broad masses of the People's Liberation Army Lanzhou Troops, with deep and boundless proletarian feelings for the great leader Chairman Mao, have been celebrating all night long the joyous news of Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin receiving the Lanzhou Troops cadre class for study.
