Title: Marshal Ye Jianying Says at Ambassador Wang Bingnan's Banquet: Sino-Polish Brotherly Friendship is Growing Stronger; Chairman Zawadzki Receives Our Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-10-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, October 23rd. Chinese Ambassador to Poland, Wang Bingnan, held a farewell banquet on the 23rd for the Chinese military delegation, which is about to conclude its visit to Poland.

Among those attending the banquet were: Polish Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Jaroszewicz, Nowak, Minister of National Defense, Spychalski, senior generals of the Polish People's Army, and diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries stationed in Poland.

Marshal Ye Jianying, head of the delegation, said at the banquet that during our visit, we have been met with a very friendly welcome and reception from the Polish party, government, military, and people everywhere, and everywhere is filled with warmth and friendship. We believe that this visit by the Chinese military delegation has further developed the fraternal friendship between the peoples and armies of China and Poland.

Ye Jianying also said that comrades of the delegation and I have a common feeling: the People's Republic of Poland is a beautiful country, the Polish people are a lovable people, and the Polish People's Army is a powerful army. We have come to this belief during our visit: under the leadership of the Polish United Workers' Party, the Polish people will surely achieve even greater successes in socialist economic construction and national defense construction, and in the struggle to defend European and world peace.

Jaroszewicz said in his speech that every visit by a Chinese comrade to Poland helps to strengthen the friendship between China and Poland. Every Pole who has visited China has pleasant memories and is amazed by China's construction achievements. He expressed his belief that the Chinese people, under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, can accomplish the task of liberating all their territory.

Ambassador Wang Bingnan and Minister Spychalski also spoke at the reception.

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, October 23rd. The Chairman of the Polish Council of State, Zawadzki, received the Chinese military delegation headed by Marshal Ye Jianying on the 23rd.

Present at the meeting were: Polish Minister of National Defense Spychalski, Deputy Minister of National Defense Bożywojewski, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Army Dushinski, and Chinese Ambassador to Poland Wang Bingnan. During the meeting, the guests and hosts had a cordial conversation. Marshal Ye Jianying also presented gifts to Chairman Zawadzki on behalf of Chinese Minister of National Defense Peng Dehuai.

### 叶剑英元帅在王炳南大使宴会上说  中波兄弟友谊日益加强  萨瓦茨基主席接见我军事代表团

1958-10-25
第5版()
专栏：

    叶剑英元帅在王炳南大使宴会上说
    中波兄弟友谊日益加强
    萨瓦茨基主席接见我军事代表团
    新华社华沙23日电　中国驻波兰大使王炳南23日为即将结束在波兰访问的中国军事代表团举行告别宴会。
    出席宴会的有：波兰部长会议副主席雅罗谢维奇、诺瓦克、国防部长斯彼哈尔斯基、波兰人民军高级将领以及各国驻波兰外交使节和武官等。
    代表团团长叶剑英元帅在宴会上讲话说，我们在访问期间到处都受到波兰党政军民非常友好的欢迎和接待，到处都充满着温暖和友谊。我们相信，中国军事代表团的这次访问使中波两国人民和军队之间的兄弟友谊得到了进一步的发展。
    叶剑英还说，我和代表团的同志们有这样一个共同的感觉：波兰人民共和国是一个美丽的国家，波兰人民是可爱的人民，波兰人民军是强大的军队。我们在访问中得出了这样一个信念：在波兰统一工人党的领导下，波兰人民必将在社会主义经济建设和国防建设中、在保卫欧洲和世界和平的斗争中取得更加重大的成就。
    雅罗谢维奇致词说，每一个中国同志对波兰的访问都有助于增强波中两国之间的友谊。每一个到中国访问过的波兰人都在记忆中留下了愉快的回忆，并为中国的建设成就感到惊异。他表示相信，中国人民在中国共产党的英明领导下，可以完成解放全部领土的任务。
    席间王炳南大使和斯彼哈尔斯基部长也分别讲了话。
    新华社华沙23日电　波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基23日接见了以叶剑英元帅为首的中国军事代表团。
    接见时在座的有：波兰国防部长斯彼哈尔斯基、国防部副部长博齐沃夫斯基、陆军副总参谋长杜欣斯基和中国驻波兰大使王炳南。在接见中，宾主进行了亲切的交谈。叶剑英元帅还代表中国国防部长彭德怀向萨瓦茨基主席赠送了礼品。
