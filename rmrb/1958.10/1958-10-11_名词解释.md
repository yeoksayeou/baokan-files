Definition of Terms

   "Tui Zhu Bing Long" (Pushing Plants and Combining Ridges): "Tui Zhu Bing Long" can enhance the sunlight exposure of cotton plants and the ventilation of cotton fields, which is a method to promote the early maturity of cotton. The specific method of "Tui Zhu Bing Long" is: use external force (stepping on the roots, pulling the plants) to make two rows of upright cotton plants first tilt inwards, and after a few days, use external force to make the originally inwardly tilted cotton plants tilt outwards.

### 名词解释

1958-10-11
第3版()
专栏：名词解释

    名词解释
    推株并垅　推株并垅可以增强棉株的光照和棉田的通风，是促使棉花早熟的一个方法。推株并垅的具体作法是：用外力（脚踏根部，手拉植株）使两行直立的棉株先向里倾斜，几天以后，再用外力使原来向里倾斜的棉株向外倾斜。
