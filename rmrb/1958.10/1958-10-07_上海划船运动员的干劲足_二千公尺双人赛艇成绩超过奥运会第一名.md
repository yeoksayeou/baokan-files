Title: Shanghai Rowers Show Great Enthusiasm: Double Sculls 2000m Time Exceeds Olympic First Place

Author:
Date: 1958-10-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, October 6th. In the rowing competition between Shanghai and Wuhan held on East Lake in Wuhan, Shanghai team rowers Sun Jiaxiang, Chen Shilin, and Wu Huaiyi, in the men's 2000-meter double sculls (single oar with coxswain) race, achieved a time of 8 minutes 25.4 seconds, surpassing the time of 8 minutes 26.1 seconds set by the American team, the first-place winner in this event at the 16th Olympic Games. Sun Jiaxiang is a worker at the Jiangnan Shipyard in Shanghai, Chen Shilin is a student at the Shanghai Fisheries College, and Wu Huaiyi is a student at the Shanghai Shipbuilding College.

In the same event, Shanghai athletes Fan Hongbin, Sha Jiping, and Wang Bingkui also broke the national record of 9 minutes 48.4 seconds with a time of 8 minutes 29.9 seconds.

In the men's 2000-meter single sculls race, Shanghai athlete Cheng Jundi also broke the national record with a time of 8 minutes 9.9 seconds. (Attached picture)

### 上海划船运动员的干劲足  二千公尺双人赛艇成绩超过奥运会第一名

1958-10-07
第6版()
专栏：

    上海划船运动员的干劲足
    二千公尺双人赛艇成绩超过奥运会第一名
    新华社武汉6日电　在武汉市东湖举行的上海、武汉两城市划船对抗赛中，上海队赛艇运动员孙佳祥、陈士麟、吴怀益在男子两千公尺双人赛艇（单桨有舵手）的竞赛中，以八分二十五秒四的成绩，超过了第十六届奥运会这项比赛第一名美国队所创造的八分二十六秒一的成绩。孙佳祥是上海江南造船厂的工人，陈士麟是上海水产学院的学生，吴怀益是上海造船学院的学生。
    在同一项目的竞赛中，上海运动员范洪宾、沙济平、王炳奎也以八分二十九秒九的成绩打破九分四十八秒四的全国纪录。
    在男子两千公尺单人双桨赛艇的竞赛中，上海运动员程骏迪也以八分九秒九的成绩打破全国纪录。（附图片）
