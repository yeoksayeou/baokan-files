Title: Soviet Leaders Send Congratulations on China's National Day
Author:
Date: 1958-10-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 30th: Soviet leaders Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, today sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai, congratulating them on China's National Day. The telegram reads as follows:
    To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China and Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, and Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:
    On the occasion of the 9th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, we, on behalf of the Soviet people, the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and the Soviet government, extend our heartfelt congratulations to you, dear friends, and through you to the great Chinese people, the Communist Party of China, and the government of the People's Republic of China. Our fraternal peoples are celebrating this glorious day in the context of the further development of the most sincere friendly relations, comprehensive cooperation, and mutual assistance based on the sacred principles of socialist internationalism. The common goal of striving for communism makes the friendship between the Soviet and Chinese peoples eternally consolidated. The unbreakable unity between the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Communist Party of China is a reliable guarantee for the victory of the common cause of our two peoples. The meeting of the leaders of the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China in Beijing not long ago was an outstanding example, once again demonstrating to the world that our two peoples have a completely consistent view on the most important issues of the present time. This meeting once again proved the invincible strength of Soviet-Chinese friendship, demonstrating our common determination to defend the cause of peace and international security together with the peace-loving people of all countries and to repel the aggressors. The Soviet people are watching with joy and pride the great achievements of the Chinese people in socialist construction. The national economy of the People's Republic of China is at an unprecedented high tide in history. While implementing the general line for socialist construction, the Chinese people are realizing a tremendous leap forward, turning their motherland into powerful socialist country possessing modern industry, modern agriculture, and modern science and culture. These achievements are inseparable from the leadership and guidance of the Chinese people's leading force—the glorious Communist Party of China. The Communist Party of China follows the theory of Marxism-Leninism and creatively develops this theory by combining it with the specific conditions of its own country. It wages a resolute struggle for the purity of Marxism-Leninism, against the distortion of revisionism, and mobilizes the entire people to engage the struggle to achieve the complete victory of socialism in your country. We wish you, dear friends, and through you, the great Chinese people, new and great achievements in socialist construction and in the cause of consolidating the power of great China.
    Long live the People's Republic of China!
    Long live the Communist Party of China and the Government of the People's Republic of China!
    Fraternal greetings to the heroic Chinese people!
    Long live the eternal and unbreakable friendship between the Soviet and Chinese peoples!

        First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union
            Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, N. Khrushchev
            Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, K. Voroshilov
                          Moscow Kremlin, September 30, 1958

### 苏联领导人电贺我国国庆

1958-10-01
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联领导人电贺我国国庆
    新华社30日讯　苏共中央第一书记、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫和苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫，今天致电毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理，祝贺我国国庆。电文如下：中华人民共和国主席、中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志，中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇同志，中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志：
    在中华人民共和国建国九周年的时候，我们谨代表苏联人民、苏联共产党和苏联政府向你们，亲爱的朋友们，并且通过你们向全体伟大的中国人民、中国共产党和中华人民共和国政府表示衷心的祝贺。我们各国的兄弟人民是在以社会主义的国际主义的神圣原则为基础的最真诚的友好关系，全面合作和互助得到进一步发展的情况下庆祝这个光辉的日子。为共产主义奋斗的一致目标使苏中两国人民的友谊永远巩固。苏联共产党和中华人民共和国共产党之间牢不可破的团结是我们两国人民共同事业胜利的可靠保证。苏联和中华人民共和国的领导人不久以前在北京的会见是一个卓越的事例，它再一次向全世界显示出我们两国人民对当前最重要问题的看法是完全一致的。这次会见再次证明了苏中友谊的不可战胜的力量，表明了我们准备同各国爱好和平的人民一起保卫和平与国际安全的事业和给予侵略者以反击的共同决心。苏联人民以愉快和骄傲的心情注视着中国人民在社会主义建设中所取得的伟大成就。中华人民共和国的国民经济正处在历史上空前未有的高潮中。中国人民在实现社会主义建设总路线的同时，实现着十分巨大的跃进，把自己的祖国建成一个具有现代工业、现代农业和现代科学文化的强大的社会主义国家。这些成就是与中国人民的领导和指导力量——光荣的中国共产党的活动分不开的。中国共产党遵循马克思列宁主义的理论并且结合本国的具体情况创造性地发展了这个理论，为马克思列宁主义的纯洁、反对修正主义的歪曲进行坚决的斗争，它发动全体人民投入在你们国家里取得社会主义的完全胜利的斗争。祝你们，亲爱的朋友们，并且通过你们祝全体伟大的中国人民在社会主义建设中、在巩固伟大中国的威力的事业中取得新的巨大的成就。
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    中国共产党和中华人民共和国政府万岁！
    向英勇的中国人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    苏中两国人民之间永恒的、牢不可破的友谊万岁！
        苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记
            苏　联　部　长　会　议　主　席　尼·赫鲁晓夫
            苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席　克·伏罗希洛夫
                          1958年9月30日莫斯科克里姆林宫
