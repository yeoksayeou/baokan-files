Title: German Democratic Republic Building Europe's Largest Power Station (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1958-10-28
Page: 5

    The German Democratic Republic is building Europe's largest coal-fired power station in the Spreewald area south of Berlin. The Lübbenau power station, with a generating capacity of 1.3 million kilowatts, will be fully completed in 1962. The picture shows workers constructing the third 140-meter-high steel-reinforced concrete chimney. Deutsche Presse-Agentur (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 民主德国兴建欧洲最大的发电站（图片）

1958-10-28
第5版()
专栏：

    民主德国兴建欧洲最大的发电站
    最近，民主德国在柏林以南的斯普里瓦尔德地区兴建一座欧洲最大的用烟煤发电的发电站。这座发电能力达一百三十万瓩的吕贝瑙发电站将在一九六二年全部建成。图为工人们正在建筑第三座高达一百四十公尺的用钢骨水泥制的烟囱。　　德意志通讯社稿（新华社发）
