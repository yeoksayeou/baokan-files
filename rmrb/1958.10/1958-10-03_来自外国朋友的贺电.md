Title: Greetings from Foreign Friends

Author: 
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd: As the people of our country joyously celebrate National Day, we have received a large number of congratulatory telegrams from abroad, congratulating our country on National Day.

The All-China Federation of Trade Unions received congratulatory telegrams on our National Day from the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, the Ukrainian Republic Council of Trade Unions, the Free German Trade Union Federation, the Polish Central Council of Trade Unions, the Czechoslovak Central Council of Trade Unions, the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, the Bulgarian Central Council of Trade Unions, the Albanian Central Council of Trade Unions, the Romanian Central Council of Trade Unions, the General Union of Algerian Workers, the Textile, Clothing, Leather and Fur Workers Trade Union International under the Industry Department of the World Federation of Trade Unions, the All-Indonesia Central Trade Union Federation, the Iranian Central Council of Trade Unions, the Sudanese Trade Union Federation, the French General Confederation of Labor, and the Belgian National Executive Committee of Metalworkers. The National Women's Federation received congratulatory telegrams from the Soviet Women's Committee, the German Democratic Women's Federation, the Albanian Women's League, the Polish Women's League, the Czechoslovak Women's Committee, the Hungarian National Council of Women, the Bulgarian Women's Committee, the Romanian National Women's Committee, the Korean Democratic Women's Union, the Vietnam Women's Union, the National Women's Federation of the United Arab Republic, the British Women's Assembly, and the Japan Welcome Chinese Women's Delegation Committee and the Japan-China Women's Exchange Association.

In addition, Song Qingling, president of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Qian Junrui, secretary-general, received a congratulatory telegram from N. Popova, chairman of the Federation of Unions of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; the Chinese Writers Association received a congratulatory telegram from the Soviet Writers Association; the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee received a congratulatory telegram from the Soviet Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee; Guo Moruo, chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace, received a congratulatory telegram from Aziz Sherif, secretary-general of the Iraqi Peace Council; and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries received a congratulatory telegram from the Ceylon-China Friendship Association.

In the congratulatory telegrams from abroad, all congratulated the people of our country on the great achievements they have made in socialist construction.

### 来自外国朋友的贺电

1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    来自外国朋友的贺电
    新华社2日讯　在我国人民欢度国庆节的日子里，我国收到来自国外的大批的贺电，祝贺我国国庆。
    中华全国总工会收到了全苏工会中央理事会，乌克兰共和国工会理事会，自由德国工会联合会，波兰工会中央理事会，捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会，越南总工会，保加利亚工会中央理事会，阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会，罗马尼亚工会中央理事会，阿尔及利亚工人总联合会，世界工会联合会产业部所属纺织、服装和皮革皮毛工人工会国际，全印度尼西亚中央职工会，伊朗工会中央理事会，苏丹工会联合会，法国总工会，比利时五金全国执行委员会祝贺我国国庆的贺电。全国妇女联合会收到了苏联妇女委员会，德国民主妇女联合会，阿尔巴尼亚妇女联盟，波兰妇女同盟，捷克斯洛伐克妇女委员会，匈牙利妇女全国理事会，保加利亚妇女委员会，罗马尼亚全国妇女委员会，朝鲜民主女性同盟，越南妇女联盟，阿拉伯联合共和国全国妇女联合会，全英妇女大会，日本欢迎中国妇女代表委员会、日中妇女交流会的贺电。
    此外，中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄、秘书长钱俊瑞收到了苏联对外友好和文化联系协会联合会委员会主席尼·波波娃的贺电，中国作家协会收到了苏联作家协会的贺电，中国亚非团结委员会收到了苏联亚非团结委员会的贺电，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若收到了伊拉克和平理事会总书记阿齐兹·谢里夫的贺电，中国人民对外文化协会收到了锡中友好协会的贺电。
    在国外来的贺电中，都祝贺我国人民在社会主义建设中所取得的巨大成就。
