Title: Bulgarian National Assembly Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1958-10-23
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 22nd: The Bulgarian National Assembly delegation, headed by Chervenkov, arrived in Pyongyang on the morning of the 22nd after departing from Tianjin by plane on the invitation of Choi Won-taek, Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea. The delegation was warmly welcomed by leaders of the Korean Workers' Party and the Republic government, as well as many members of the public.

Choi Won-taek, Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, said in his welcoming speech at the airport that the delegation's visit to Korea would contribute to the brotherly friendship and solidarity between the people of the two countries.

Chervenkov said in his speech that the Bulgarian people support the Korean people's desire for the peaceful reunification of their motherland. He said that we will strive to further strengthen the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

### 保国民议会代表团抵平壤

1958-10-23
第5版()
专栏：

    保国民议会代表团抵平壤
    据新华社平壤22日电　以契尔文科夫为首的保加利亚国民议会代表团应朝鲜最高人民会议议长崔元泽的邀请，22日上午离天津乘飞机于当日中午到达平壤。代表团受到了朝鲜劳动党和共和国政府领导人以及很多群众的热烈欢迎。
    朝鲜最高人民会议议长崔元泽在机场上致欢迎词说，代表团这次访问朝鲜，将对朝保两国人民间的兄弟般的友谊和团结作出贡献。
    契尔文科夫讲话说，保加利亚人民支持朝鲜人民和平统一祖国的愿望。他说，我们将为进一步加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结一致而努力。
