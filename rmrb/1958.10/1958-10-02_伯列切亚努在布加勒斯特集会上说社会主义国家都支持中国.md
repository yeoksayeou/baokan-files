Title: Burileanu Says at Bucharest Rally that Socialist Countries All Support China
Author:
Date: 1958-10-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, October 1st: People from all walks of life in the Romanian capital held a grand rally at the Trade Union Theater on the evening of September 30th to celebrate the Chinese National Day.

Romanian Party and state leaders Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chivu Stoica, Maurer, and others attended the rally. Chinese Ambassador to Romania Ke Bonian was also invited to attend the rally.

Burileanu, Chairman of the Romanian Association of Legal Workers, spoke at the rally. He said that the establishment of the People's Republic of China has great international significance, and its establishment has strengthened socialism and the forces defending world peace.

Burileanu condemned the United States for using force to oppose China's liberation of its territory of Taiwan, the Penghu Islands, and coastal islands. He said that the people of all socialist countries firmly support the just struggle of the Chinese people.

### 伯列切亚努在布加勒斯特集会上说社会主义国家都支持中国

1958-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    伯列切亚努在布加勒斯特集会上说
    社会主义国家都支持中国
    据新华社布加勒斯特1日电　罗马尼亚首都各界人民9月30日晚在工会剧院举行大会，隆重庆祝中国国庆。
    罗马尼亚党和国家的领导人乔治乌—德治、基伏·斯托伊卡、毛雷尔等参加了大会。中国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年也应邀参加了大会。
    罗马尼亚法律工作者协会主席伯列切亚努在大会上讲了话。他说，中华人民共和国的建立有伟大的国际意义，它的建立加强了社会主义和保卫世界和平的力量。
    伯列切亚努谴责美国用武力来反对中国解放中国的领土台湾、澎湖列岛及沿海岛屿。他说，所有社会主义国家的人民都坚决支持中国人民的正义斗争。
