Title: Thai Parliamentary Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-10-14
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 13th. The Thai parliamentary delegation of seven members, headed by Nai Than Khamsiran, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. Greeting them at the airport were Zhou Gengsheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, and responsible persons from the General Office of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.
    Tran Cao Thanh, First Secretary of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 泰国议员团到京

1958-10-14
第4版()
专栏：

    泰国议员团到京
    新华社13日讯　以乃他汉·堪希兰为首的泰国议员团一行七人，于今日下午乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中国人民外交学会邀请访华的。到机场欢迎的有中国人民外交学会副会长周鲠生、秘书长吴茂荪和全国人民代表大会常务委员会办公厅负责人等。
    越南民主共和国驻华大使馆一等秘书陈高诚也前往机场欢迎。
