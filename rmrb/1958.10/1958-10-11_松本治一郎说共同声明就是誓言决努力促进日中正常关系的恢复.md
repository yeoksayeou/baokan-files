Title: Matsumoto Jiichiro says the joint statement is a vow to strive to promote the restoration of normal relations between Japan and China
Author:
Date: 1958-10-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Matsumoto Jiichiro, head of the Japanese-Chinese Friendship Association delegation visiting China and president of the Japanese-Chinese Friendship Association, said tonight that the joint statement signed today by our group and three Chinese organizations is our vow. The Japanese-Chinese Friendship Association will work hard from now on to ensure that the normal relations between Japan and China, which have been destroyed by the lackeys of American imperialism, the Nobusuke Kishi government, can be restored as soon as possible.

The Japanese-Chinese Friendship Association delegation held a farewell banquet tonight to entertain people from all walks of life in the capital. Matsumoto Jiichiro's above remarks were made at this banquet.

Chu Tunan and Liao Chengzhi delivered speeches at the banquet. They both expressed their gratitude and support to the Japanese-Chinese Friendship Association for its efforts to enhance the friendship between the people of China and Japan and to promote the restoration of normal relations between China and Japan, and wished them victory in their struggle.

Chen Shutong, Zhang Xiruo, Cai Tingkai, Hu Lansheng and others attended this banquet which was full of friendly atmosphere. (Attached picture)
	The China Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries, the China Peace Committee, and the Red Cross Society of China jointly signed a statement with the visiting delegation of the Japan-China Friendship Association. On the right of the signing is Chu Tunan, President of the China Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries, and on the left is Matsumoto Jiichiro, head of the visiting delegation of the Japan-China Friendship Association.

### 松本治一郎说共同声明就是誓言决努力促进日中正常关系的恢复

1958-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    松本治一郎说共同声明就是誓言决努力促进日中正常关系的恢复
    新华社10日讯　日本中国友好协会访华代表团团长、日本中国友好协会会长松本治一郎今天晚上说，今天我们同中国三个团体签字发表的共同声明就是我们的誓言。日本中国友好协会今后誓将努力工作，使被美帝国主义的走狗岸信介政府破坏了的日中两国的正常关系能够早日恢复。
    日本中国友好协会访华代表团在今天晚间举行告别宴会，招待首都各方面的社会人士。松本治一郎的上述一番话就是在这个宴会上讲的。
    楚图南和廖承志在这个宴会上先后致词。他们一致对日中友好协会为增进中日两国人民的友谊，为促进中日两国正常关系的恢复所作的努力，表示感谢和支持，并祝他们斗争胜利。
    陈叔通、张奚若、蔡廷锴、胡兰生等出席了这个充满友好气氛的宴会。（附图片）
    中国人民对外文化协会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国红十字会和日中友好协会访华代表团共同声明签字。签字的右边是中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，左为日中友好协会访华代表团团长松本治一郎。
