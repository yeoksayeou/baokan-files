Title: Song of the Lumberjacks
Author:
Date: 1958-10-19
Page: 6

    Song of the Lumberjacks
    Lumberjacks sweat profusely,
    Great trees fall one by one,
    Fast as steel needles sinking in water,
    Dozens in an instant.
            (Zhao Jingli)
      ×　×　×
    The deep forest is my home,
    The old forests and mountains are picturesque;
    I open the treasure house of gold,
    And take out resources to build the country.
            (Zhang Fengxiang)

### 林业工人之歌

1958-10-19
第6版()
专栏：

    林业工人之歌
    伐木工人汗涔涔，
    棵棵大树倒纷纷，
    快似钢针水里沉，
    霎时之间数十根。
            （赵镜礼）
      ×　×　×
    树海深处为吾家，
    群山老林景如画；
    宝库金门吾打开，
    取出资材建国家。
            （张凤翔）
