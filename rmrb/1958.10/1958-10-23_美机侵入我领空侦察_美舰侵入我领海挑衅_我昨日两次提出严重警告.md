Title: American Plane Intrudes into Our Airspace for Reconnaissance, American Warship Intrudes into Our Territorial Waters for Provocation; We Lodged Two Serious Warnings Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1958-10-23
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: On October 22nd, at 9:20 AM, an American U-2 strategic reconnaissance plane intruded into the airspace over the Fujian and Guangdong provinces of our country to conduct high-altitude reconnaissance.
    The spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to declare: American warships and airplanes have intruded into our territorial waters and airspace, disregarding our more than 30 serious warnings. American military aircraft openly intruded into our airspace in broad daylight. This is a serious infringement upon our country's sovereignty and a heinous act of deliberately engaging in war provocations. To this, the Chinese government lodged its 39th serious warning.
    Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: On October 22nd, two American warships intruded into the territorial waters of the Pingtan and Matsu areas of Fujian Province. Regarding this serious military provocation by the United States, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 40th serious warning.

### 美机侵入我领空侦察　美舰侵入我领海挑衅  我昨日两次提出严重警告

1958-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    美机侵入我领空侦察　美舰侵入我领海挑衅
    我昨日两次提出严重警告
    新华社22日讯　10月22日九时二十分，美国U—2型战略侦察机一架侵入我国福建、广东省地区上空，进行高空侦察。
    我外交部发言人奉命声明：美国军舰飞机侵入我领海领空，不顾我三十多次的严重警告。美国军用飞机公然在白天侵入我国领空。这是严重侵犯我国主权，蓄意进行战争挑衅的罪恶行为。对此，中国政府第三十九次提出严重警告。
    新华社22日讯　10月22日美国军舰两艘，侵入我福建省平潭、马祖地区的领海。对于美国这种严重的军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命第四十次提出严重警告。
