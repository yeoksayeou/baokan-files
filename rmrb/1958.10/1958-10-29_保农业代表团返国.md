Title: Bulgarian Agricultural Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1958-10-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th. The Bulgarian agricultural delegation of seven people, headed by Comrade Buchvarov, Minister of Agriculture of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee, returned home by plane today. Among those who saw them off at the airport were Wang Guanlan, Deputy Director of the Rural Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, and Yang Xiandong and Cai Ziwei, Deputy Ministers of Agriculture.

Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Panchevski, also saw them off at the airport.

During their visit to China, the delegation visited various places to inspect people's communes, farmland water conservancy, soil and water conservation, terraced field construction, and rich harvest experiences.

### 保农业代表团返国

1958-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    保农业代表团返国
    新华社28日讯　以保共中央农业部部长布契瓦洛夫同志为首的保加利亚农业代表团一行七人，今日乘机返国。到机场送行的有中共中央农村工作部副部长王观澜，农业部副部长杨显东、蔡子伟等人。
    保加利亚驻华大使潘切夫斯基也到机场送行。
    代表团访华期间，曾到各地考察了人民公社、农田水利、水土保持、梯田修建以及丰产经验等。
