Title: U.S. Warships and Aircraft Again Invade Our Territorial Waters and Airspace; We Issue the 31st Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1958-10-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th: On October 14th, two more U.S. warships and two fighter planes intruded into the territorial waters of the Matsu area of Fujian Province and the airspace above Pingtan Island. In response to this serious military provocation by the United States, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 31st serious warning.

### 美舰美机又侵入我领海领空  我第31次提出严重警告

1958-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    美舰美机又侵入我领海领空
    我第31次提出严重警告
    新华社14日讯　10月14日，又有美国军舰两艘、战斗飞机两架，侵入我国福建省马祖地区领海和平潭岛上空。对于美国这种严重的军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第三十一次严重警告。
