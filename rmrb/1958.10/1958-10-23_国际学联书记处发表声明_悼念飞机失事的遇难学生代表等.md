Title: International Student Union Secretariat Issues Statement Mourning Student Representatives and Others Killed in Plane Crash
Author:
Date: 1958-10-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 21st: The Secretariat of the International Student Union (ISU) issued a statement here tonight regarding the recent "Tu-104" plane crash.

The statement released the names of the twenty-five representatives who attended the Fifth ISU Congress and were killed in the accident. Among them were seven ISU staff members, including a vice president. After attending the ISU Congress in Beijing, they visited North Korea and Vietnam and tragically died on their way home.

The statement expressed condolences and sympathy to the families of the victims.

The statement said that in mourning our colleagues, we pledge to continue the struggle they had gloriously waged in defense of students' interests and for peace and friendship.

### 国际学联书记处发表声明  悼念飞机失事的遇难学生代表等

1958-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    国际学联书记处发表声明
    悼念飞机失事的遇难学生代表等
    新华社布拉格21日电　国际学生联合会书记处今晚在这里就最近发生的“图—一○四”客机失事事件发表一项声明。
    声明公布了遇难的二十五位参加国际学联第五届代表大会的代表的名单，他们中间有七位是国际学联的工作人员，其中有一位副主席。他们在参加了北京的国际学联代表大会之后，曾到朝鲜和越南进行访问，在归国途中不幸遇难。
    声明向遇难者的家属表示了吊唁和慰问。
    声明说，在悼念我们的同事的时候，我们保证把他们为了保卫学生利益、为了和平和友谊而曾经光荣地进行的斗争继续下去。
