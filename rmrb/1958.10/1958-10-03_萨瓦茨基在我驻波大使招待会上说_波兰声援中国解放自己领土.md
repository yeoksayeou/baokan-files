Title: Sawacki Says at Reception Hosted by Chinese Ambassador to Poland: Poland Supports China in Liberating Its Own Territory
Author:
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, October 1st: Chinese Ambassador to Poland, Wang Bingnan, held a grand reception here on October 1st to celebrate the 9th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Among the more than 500 people attending the reception were Polish party and government leaders such as Gomulka, Zawadzki, and Cyrankiewicz, heads of various government departments, people from all walks of life, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Poland.

Ambassador Wang Bingnan and Polish Council of State Chairman Zawadzki jointly raised their glasses at the reception to congratulate the friendship between the two peoples and to congratulate the Chinese people on achieving greater success.

In his toast, Zawadzki said that the Polish people support the Chinese people's struggle to liberate their own territory.

### 萨瓦茨基在我驻波大使招待会上说  波兰声援中国解放自己领土

1958-10-03
第4版()
专栏：

    萨瓦茨基在我驻波大使招待会上说
    波兰声援中国解放自己领土
    新华社华沙1日电　中国驻波兰大使王炳南1日在这里举行盛大招待会，庆祝中国国庆九周年。
    出席招待会的有哥穆尔卡、萨瓦茨基、西伦凯维兹等波兰党政领导人、政府各部负责人、社会人士和各国驻波兰的外交使节等五百多人。
    王炳南大使和波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基在招待会上共同举杯祝贺两国人民之间的友谊，祝贺中国人民获得更大的成就。
    萨瓦茨基在举杯祝贺时说，波兰人民声援中国人民解放自己领土的斗争。
