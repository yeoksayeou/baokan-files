Title: U.S. Warships Continue to Intrude into Territorial Waters of Pingtan and Matsu Islands; China Issues 30th Serious Warning

Author:
Date: 1958-10-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th – On October 13th, two U.S. warships intruded into the territorial waters of Pingtan and Matsu Islands in Fujian Province. Regarding this military provocation by the United States, a spokesman for the Chinese Foreign Ministry was instructed to issue the 30th serious warning.

### 美舰继续侵入我平潭马祖地区领海  我国第30次提出严重警告

1958-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

    美舰继续侵入我平潭马祖地区领海
    我国第30次提出严重警告
    新华社13日讯　10月13日，有美国军舰两艘，侵入我福建省平潭、马祖地区的领海。对于美国的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命第三十次提出严重警告。
