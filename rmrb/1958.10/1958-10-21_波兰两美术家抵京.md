Title: Two Polish Artists Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-10-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 20th—Polish artists Stefan Samborski, professor at the Warsaw Academy of Fine Arts, and Leon Michalski, lecturer, arrived in Beijing on the 16th. They are visiting China according to the 1958 executive plan of the Sino-Polish cultural cooperation agreement.

### 波兰两美术家抵京

1958-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰两美术家抵京
    新华社20日讯　波兰美术家、华沙美术学院教授斯蒂芬·萨姆博尔斯基和讲师莱昂·米哈尔斯基16日到达北京。他们是根据中波文化合作协定1958年执行计划来我国访问的。
