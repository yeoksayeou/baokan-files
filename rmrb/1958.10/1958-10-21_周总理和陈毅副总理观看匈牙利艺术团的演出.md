Title: Premier Zhou and Vice Premier Chen Yi Watch Performance by Hungarian Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1958-10-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th — Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi attended the splendid performance of the Hungarian Music and Ballet Art Troupe tonight.

    During the intermission of the performance, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi met with all members of the art troupe. Hungarian Ambassador to China, Mr. Nogradi, was also present at the meeting. Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi congratulated the Hungarian artists on their successful performance and their contribution to strengthening cultural exchanges between China and Hungary. They expressed their hope that the artists would visit and perform in more places, factories, and rural areas in China, and took a group photo with them.

    Tonight's performance was the second performance of the Hungarian Art Troupe in China. Every music and dance performance they presented received long and enthusiastic applause. Several performances were repeated due to the audience's enthusiastic requests.

    After the performance, Vice Premier Chen Yi and Vice Chairman Zhang Zhixiang of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, together with Ambassador Nogradi, stepped onto the stage and shook hands with the Hungarian artists one by one amidst the enthusiastic applause of the audience, thanking them for their wonderful performance. ### 周总理和陈毅副总理观看匈牙利艺术团的演出

1958-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理和陈毅副总理观看匈牙利艺术团的演出
    新华社20日讯　周恩来总理和陈毅副总理今晚出席欣赏了匈牙利音乐、芭蕾舞艺术团的精采演出。
    在演出休息期间，周恩来总理和陈毅副总理接见了艺术团全体人员。接见时，匈牙利驻中国大使诺格拉第也在座。周恩来总理和陈毅副总理祝贺匈牙利艺术家们的演出成功和对加强中匈两国文化交流的贡献，希望他们到我国更多的地方和工厂农村去参观访问和表演，并且同艺术家们合影留念。
    今晚的演出是匈牙利艺术团到中国后的第二场表演。他们表演的每一个音乐和舞蹈节目都获得长时间的热烈掌声，不少节目由于观众的热情要求而重演。
    演出结束后，陈毅副总理和对外文化联络委员会张致祥副主任同诺格拉第大使一起步上舞台，在全场观众的热烈掌声中同匈牙利艺术家们一一握手，感谢他们的精彩表演。（附图片）
