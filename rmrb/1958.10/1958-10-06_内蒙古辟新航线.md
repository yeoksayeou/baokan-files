Title: Inner Mongolia Opens New Air Routes
Author:
Date: 1958-10-06
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, 3rd - The civil aviation routes from Hohhot, the capital of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, to the grassland cities of Xilinhot and Hailar, officially opened on October 1st and began handling passenger and freight transportation. The air routes from Hohhot to Chifeng and Tongliao will also be opened soon.

### 内蒙古辟新航线

1958-10-06
第2版()
专栏：

    内蒙古辟新航线
    据新华社呼和浩特3日电　内蒙古自治区首府——呼和浩特市到草原城市锡林浩特、海拉尔的民用航空线，10月1日已正式通航，并开始办理客货运输业务。呼和浩特到赤峰、通辽的航空线，也将在不久以后就要开航。
