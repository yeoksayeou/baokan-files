Title: Pakistani President Mirza Resigns; Prime Minister and Defense Minister Takes Over

Author:

Date: 1958-10-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 28th -- General Ayub Khan, who was just sworn in on the 27th as the Prime Minister, Minister of Defense, and Minister of Kashmir Affairs of Pakistan, assumed the post of President of Pakistan this morning. Ayub Khan announced in a statement that former President Mirza had resigned from the presidency and transferred all power to him.

Pakistani President Mirza declared martial law throughout the country on October 7th, abolished the Pakistani Constitution, dissolved the central and provincial governments, and appointed the then Commander-in-Chief of the Army, Ayub Khan, as the executor of the military martial law. On the 24th, the new Pakistani government, headed by Ayub Khan, was announced and sworn in on the 27th. On the same day he took office as Prime Minister, Ayub Khan was also appointed by Mirza as the Supreme Commander of the Pakistani Armed Forces.

### 巴总统米尔扎辞职  总理兼国防部长接掌职务

1958-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    巴总统米尔扎辞职
    总理兼国防部长接掌职务
    新华社卡拉奇28日电　刚在27日宣誓就任巴基斯坦总理兼国防部长和克什米尔事务部长的阿尤布汗将军今天上午接掌了巴基斯坦总统的职务。阿尤布汗在一项公告中宣布，前总统米尔扎已经辞去总统职务，并且把一切权力移交给他。
    巴基斯坦总统米尔扎在10月7日宣布全国戒严，废除巴基斯坦宪法，解散中央和各省省政府，并且任命当时的陆军总司令阿尤布汗为军事戒严令执行人。24日，以阿尤布汗为首的巴基斯坦新政府宣告组成，并且在27日宣誓就职。阿尤布汗在就任总理的同一天，还被米尔扎任命为巴基斯坦武装部队最高司令。
