Title: Weaving Girl and Cowherd Stick Out Their Tongues
Author: Lan Yongfu
Date: 1958-10-13
Page: 3

    Corn stalks pierce the ninth heaven,
    Covered in ears numbering in the thousands,
    Weaving Girl and Cowherd stick out their tongues:
    How can corn grow among the clouds!
        (Lan Yongfu)

### 织女牛郎吐舌头
兰永福
1958-10-13
第3版()
专栏：

    织女牛郎吐舌头
    玉米秆儿穿九天，
    浑身棒子有几千，
    织女牛郎吐舌头：
    那有玉米长云间！
        （兰永福）
