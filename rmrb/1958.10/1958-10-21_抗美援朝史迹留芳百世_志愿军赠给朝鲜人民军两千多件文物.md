Title: History of Resisting America and Aiding Korea Will Be Remembered Forever; Volunteers Donate Over 2,000 Cultural Relics to the Korean People's Army
Author:
Date: 1958-10-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 20th – Before leaving Korea, the headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteers (CPV) donated a batch of historical relics from the War to Resist America and Aid Korea, collected from various units of the entire army, to their close comrades-in-arms – the Korean People's Army (KPA), as a permanent commemoration.

This batch of precious historical relics is divided into twelve categories, totaling 2,477 items. These include ten types of meritorious cannons, meritorious guns, demolition tubes, hand grenades, and weapons held by heroes such as Qiu Shaoyun; photographs, deeds, objects, and martyrs' belongings of combat heroes of the second grade or higher, such as Huang Jiguang and Hu Xiudao; writing utensils used by Commander Peng Dehuai and General Nam Il during the armistice signing; various objects from the people of the motherland, the Korean people, and the people of various countries supporting the CPV; various objects and materials from the CPV's political offensives against the enemy; various newspapers and books published by the CPV; various daily necessities and cultural supplies made by CPV officers and soldiers to overcome difficulties; objects, materials, and meritorious equipment related to the CPV's logistics supply, wartime hygiene, and vehicle transportation; sand table models of the CPV's field oil depots, underground hospitals, and barracks; operational maps of battles such as the Battle of Triangle Hill (Sangamryung), victory red flags planted on heroic positions during battles like Old Baldy Hill, stained with the blood of soldiers, and various captured items.

The list of these historical relics has been handed over by CPV Commander Yang Yong to General Kim Kwang-hyup when the KPA delegation came to the CPV headquarters to bid farewell. Various objects have now been transported to Pyongyang in batches.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 19th – The Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteers held a banquet tonight to bid farewell to and welcome all personnel of the Political and Cultural Liaison Department of the Korean People's Army stationed at the CPV Political Department.

At the beginning of the banquet, Liang Biye, Deputy Political Commissar and Director of the Political Department of the CPV, spoke, thanking the Political and Cultural Liaison Department of the KPA stationed at the CPV Political Department for its assistance to the CPV over the years.

Liang Biye said: Though we are separated, our hearts connected together cannot be separated. The great friendship built with blood will forever inspire the people of China and Korea to advance bravely for our common cause.

Liang Biye, on behalf of the CPV Political Department, presented gifts to all personnel of the Political and Cultural Liaison Department of the KPA stationed at the CPV Political Department.

### 抗美援朝史迹留芳百世  志愿军赠给朝鲜人民军两千多件文物

1958-10-21
第5版()
专栏：

    抗美援朝史迹留芳百世
    志愿军赠给朝鲜人民军两千多件文物
    新华社平壤20日电　中国人民志愿军总部在撤离朝鲜前夕，把从全军各部队中搜集起来的一批抗美援朝历史文物赠送给亲密的战友——朝鲜人民军，作为永远纪念。
    这批珍贵的历史文物，分为十二类共二千四百七十七件。其中有十种功勋炮、功勋枪、爆破筒、手雷等和丘少云等英雄的手中武器；有黄继光、胡修道等二级以上战斗英雄的照片、事迹、实物及烈士遗物；有彭德怀司令员和南日大将在停战签字时用过的文具，有祖国人民和朝鲜人民及各国人民支援志愿军的各种实物；有志愿军对敌政治攻势的各种实物和资料；有志愿军出版的各种报刊和书籍；有志愿军官兵为克服困难而自制的各种生活用具和文化用品；有志愿军的军需供应、战勤卫生、车辆运输等实物、资料及功勋器材；有志愿军野战油库、地下医院及营房等沙盘模型；有上甘岭战役等作战要图、老秃山等战斗中插上英雄阵地并染着战士鲜血的胜利红旗及各种缴获品。
    这些历史文物的清单，已由志愿军司令员杨勇上将在朝鲜人民军代表团来志愿军总部送别时递交给金光侠大将，各种实物现已分批先后运抵平壤。
    据新华社平壤19日电　中国人民志愿军政治部今天晚上举行宴会，告别和欢送朝鲜人民军驻志愿军政治部的政治文化联络部的全体人员。
    志愿军副政治委员兼政治部主任梁必业中将在宴会开始时讲话，感谢朝鲜人民军驻志愿军政治部政治文化联络部几年来给予志愿军的帮助。
    梁必业中将说：我们人分开了，我们连接在一起的心是不能分离的。用鲜血筑成的伟大友谊将永远鼓舞着中朝两国人民为着共同的事业奋勇前进。
    梁必业中将代表志愿军政治部向朝鲜人民军驻志愿军政治部政治文化联络部的全体人员赠送了礼品。
