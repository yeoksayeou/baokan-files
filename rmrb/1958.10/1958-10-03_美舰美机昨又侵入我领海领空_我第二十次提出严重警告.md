Title: US Warships and Aircraft Again Intruded into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace Yesterday; We Lodge the 20th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd October: On October 2nd, four American warships and two fighter planes intruded into our territorial waters and airspace over the Xiamen area of Fujian Province. A spokesman for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to declare that the Chinese government lodges its twentieth serious warning against this serious military provocation by the United States.

### 美舰美机昨又侵入我领海领空  我第二十次提出严重警告

1958-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

    美舰美机昨又侵入我领海领空
    我第二十次提出严重警告
    新华社2日讯　10月2日，美国军舰四艘、战斗飞机两架侵入我国福建厦门地区的领海和领海上空。我外交部发言人奉命声明，对于美国的这种严重的军事挑衅，中国政府第二十次提出严重警告。
