Title: Telegram of Greetings from Algerian Prime Minister Abbas
Author:
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

To His Excellency Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China:

    On this October 1st, the anniversary of the glorious founding of the People's Republic of China, I extend to Your Excellency our most sincere congratulations on behalf of the Government of the Republic of Algeria and the Algerian people, and please convey our best wishes for the prosperity, strength, and tranquility of your government and the great Chinese people.

                    Ferhat Abbas
                    Prime Minister of the Republic of Algeria

### 阿尔及利亚总理阿巴斯的贺电
费尔哈特·阿巴斯
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚总理阿巴斯的贺电中华人民共和国周恩来总理阁下：
    在这十月一日，光荣的中华人民共和国成立纪念日的时候，我谨代表阿尔及利亚共和国政府和阿尔及利亚人民向阁下表示我们最诚挚的祝贺，并请你转达我们祝贵国政府和伟大的中国人民繁荣、强大和安宁这最良好的愿望。
                    阿尔及利亚共和国总理　费尔哈特·阿巴斯
