Title: Telegram from Czechoslovak Foreign Minister David
Author: Wa Diwei
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

Telegram from Czechoslovak Foreign Minister David to Comrade Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:

Dear Comrade:

On the occasion of the ninth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, please accept my heartfelt wishes, wishing you personal happiness and further achievements in the struggle to safeguard peace in the Far East and the world, and in implementing a policy of friendship and peaceful coexistence between all peoples!

                                                Wa. David
                                        Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Czechoslovak Republic

### 捷克斯洛伐克外交部长戴维的贺电
瓦·戴维
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克外交部长戴维的贺电中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅同志：亲爱的同志：
    值此中华人民共和国成立九周年之际，请接受我衷心的祝愿，祝你个人幸福并在维护远东和世界和平的斗争中以及在执行各国人民之间的友好和和平共处的政策时获得进一步的成就！
                            捷克斯洛伐克共和国外交部长
                                            瓦·戴维
