Title: Bulgarian Guests Visit Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1958-10-19
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, October 18th:

    The Bulgarian National Assembly Delegation, headed by Chervenkov, concluded their two-day tour of Hangzhou and left Hangzhou for Shanghai by special plane this morning. Huo Shilian, Secretary of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Governor of Zhejiang Province, and people from all walks of life in the province and city went to the airport to see them off.
    During their stay in Hangzhou, the delegation visited the Du Jinsheng Silk Weaving Factory, the largest in China known for manufacturing silk woven landscapes and portraits, and the West Lake People's Commune, which produces the famous Longjing tea. They also toured Hangzhou's famous historical sites and enjoyed the scenery of West Lake.
    The Bulgarian guests arrived in Guangzhou by special plane on the afternoon of the 14th after visiting Vietnam.
    Also accompanying them was the Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Panchevski, and his wife.
    The heads of Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, and the heads of fraternal countries' consulates in Guangzhou welcomed the guests at the airport.
    While in Guangzhou, the guests visited the China Export Commodities Fair. Governor Chen Yu hosted a banquet to welcome the guests.

### 保加利亚贵宾去上海访问

1958-10-19
第3版()
专栏：

    保加利亚贵宾去上海访问
    新华社18日讯　以契尔文科夫为首的保加利亚国民议会代表团结束了在杭州为期两天的参观游览后，于今天上午乘专机离杭州去上海。前往机场送行的有中共浙江省委书记处书记、浙江省副省长霍士廉和省市各界人士。
    代表团在杭州期间参观了我国最大的以制造丝织风景、人像著名的都锦生丝织厂和以生产著名龙井茶的西湖人民公社，还游览了杭州名胜古迹，欣赏了西湖风光。
    保加利亚贵宾是在访问越南以后，在14日下午乘专机到达广州的。
    随同前来的还有保加利亚驻华大使潘切夫斯基及夫人。
    广东省和广州市负责人和兄弟国家驻广州领事馆负责人都到机场欢迎贵宾。
    在广州时，贵宾们参观了中国出口商品陈列馆。陈郁省长设宴欢迎贵宾。
