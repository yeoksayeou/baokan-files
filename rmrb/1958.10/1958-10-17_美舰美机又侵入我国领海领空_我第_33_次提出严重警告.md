Title: U.S. Warships and Aircraft Invade China's Territorial Waters and Airspace Again; We Issue the 33rd Serious Warning

Author:

Date: 1958-10-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th - On October 16th, two U.S. warships invaded the territorial waters of the Pingtan and Matsu areas of Fujian Province, and one U.S. aircraft intruded into the airspace over the Da Wanshan Island of Guangdong Province, indicating that the United States is continuing to carry out military provocations and create a tense situation. In response, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 33rd serious warning.

### 美舰美机又侵入我国领海领空  我第33次提出严重警告

1958-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

    美舰美机又侵入我国领海领空
    我第33次提出严重警告
    新华社16日讯　10月16日，美国军舰两艘侵入我国福建省平潭、马祖地区的领海，美国飞机一架侵入我国广东省的大万山岛上空，说明美国仍在继续进行军事挑衅，制造紧张局势。对此，我外交部发言人奉命第三十三次提出严重警告。
