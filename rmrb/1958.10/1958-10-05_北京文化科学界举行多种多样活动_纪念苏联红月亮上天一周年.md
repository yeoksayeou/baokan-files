Title: Beijing Cultural and Scientific Circles Hold Diverse Activities to Commemorate the First Anniversary of the Soviet Red Sputnik

Author:
Date: 1958-10-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 4th: Beijing's cultural and scientific circles are holding diverse activities to commemorate the first anniversary of the launch of the Soviet Union's first artificial Earth satellite.

    The China Association for Science and Technology, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association jointly held a report meeting on October 4th to commemorate the first anniversary of the launch of the Soviet Union's first artificial Earth satellite. Qian Xuesen, Director of the Institute of Mechanics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, gave a report to more than 600 science enthusiasts at the meeting. He said that through the launch of artificial satellites and a series of high-altitude explorations, Soviet scientists elucidated various natural phenomena in the space surrounding the Earth, as well as the physiological conditions of organisms at high altitudes and under weightlessness, thus making a huge contribution to the scientific research of the International Geophysical Year; and opened up a new era of interstellar navigation.

    The "Exhibition of Pictures Commemorating the First Anniversary of the Launch of the Soviet Union's First Artificial Earth Satellite" is being held at the Painting Gallery on Wangfujing Street in Beijing and at the Gulou Cultural Center in the Dongcheng District.

    The Beijing Planetarium has launched a variety of commemorative activities. Today, the planetarium is showing "Artificial Earth Satellite", and the cinema is showing the color science film "Interstellar Travel", with every showing fully booked.

    The Central People's Broadcasting Station held a special program today, broadcasting speeches by prominent Chinese and Soviet scientists such as Academician ? Smirnov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Qian Xuesen, Director of the Institute of Mechanics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Zhao Jiuzhang, Director of the Institute of Geophysics.

### 北京文化科学界举行多种多样活动  纪念苏联红月亮上天一周年

1958-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

    北京文化科学界举行多种多样活动
    纪念苏联红月亮上天一周年
  新华社4日讯　北京文化科学界举行多种多样活动，纪念苏联第一颗人造地球卫星发射一周年。
    中华人民共和国科学技术协会、中苏友好协会总会和北京市中苏友好协会4日联合举办纪念苏联第一颗人造地球卫星发射一周年报告会。中国科学院力学研究所所长钱学森在会上向六百多名科学爱好者做了报告。他说苏联科学家通过发射人造卫星和一系列的高空探测研究，阐述了地球周围空间的各种自然现象以及生物在高空中和失重情况下的生理情况，从而对地球物理年的科学研究工作做出了巨大贡献；并开辟了星际航行的新时代。
    在北京市王府井大街的画廊和东城区鼓楼文化馆等地都举行了苏联第一颗人造地球卫星发射一周年图片展览。
    北京天文馆开展了多种纪念活动。该馆今天上演“人造地球卫星”，电影厅放映彩色科学电影“星际旅行”，场场满座。
    中央人民广播电台今天举办特别节目，播送苏联科学院院长?斯米扬诺夫院士、中国科学院力学研究所所长钱学森、地球物理研究所所长赵九章等中、苏著名科学家的讲话。
