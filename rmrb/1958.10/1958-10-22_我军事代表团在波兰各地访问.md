Title: Our Military Delegation Visits Various Parts of Poland
Author:
Date: 1958-10-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Our military delegation, led by Marshal Ye Jianying, which was invited by the Polish government to participate in the Polish People's Army Day celebrations, arrived in Warsaw on October 10th and is currently visiting various parts of Poland.

The delegation attended the Polish government's celebration of the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Polish People's Army on the evening of the 11th. On the evening of the 12th, they also attended a reception held by Polish Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz, for Army Day.

Afterwards, the delegation paid respects at the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier in Warsaw and the Cemetery of Soviet Soldiers, and visited the Polish People's Army General Staff Academy. On the 14th, they visited the city of Krakow.

On the 18th and 19th, the delegation visited Wroclaw.

On the 19th, after visiting the Kościuszko Infantry School, the delegation attended a banquet hosted by the school's president. Marshal Ye Jianying spoke at the banquet. He expressed his gratitude for the school leaders' support of China's struggle to liberate Taiwan. He emphasized that when imperialism pursues a policy of war, the people of socialist countries and peace-loving people of the world must strengthen unity and cooperation. The unity and cooperation between the people and armies of China and Poland is of great significance.

At the banquet, guests and hosts frequently toasted to the friendship between the peoples and armies of the two countries, and to the construction achievements of the two countries.

### 我军事代表团在波兰各地访问

1958-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

    我军事代表团在波兰各地访问
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：应波兰政府邀请参加波兰人民军建军节的我国军事代表团在叶剑英元帅率领下，于10月10日抵达华沙后，目前正在波兰各地进行访问。
    代表团在11日晚间出席了波兰政府举行的波兰人民军建军十五周年的庆祝大会，12日晚上又出席了波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹为建军节举行的招待会。
    此后代表团拜谒了华沙的无名战士墓和苏军战士墓，参观了波兰人民军总参谋学院，14日又到克拉科夫市访问。
    代表团18日、19日在弗罗茨瓦夫参观访问。
    19日代表团在科什丘什科步兵学校参观后，出席了该校校长的宴会。叶剑英元帅在宴会上讲了话。他表示感谢学校领导人支持中国解放台湾的斗争。他强调指出，当帝国主义执行战争政策的时候，社会主义各国的人民，世界爱好和平的人民要加强团结合作。中波两国人民及其军队间的团结合作有着重要的意义。
    宴会上宾主双方频频为两国人民和军队之间的友谊干杯，为两国的建设成就干杯。
