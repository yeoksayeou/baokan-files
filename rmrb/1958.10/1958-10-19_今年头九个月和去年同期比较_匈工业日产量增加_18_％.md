Title: Hungarian Industrial Daily Output Increased by 18% in the First Nine Months of This Year Compared with the Same Period Last Year
Author:
Date: 1958-10-19
Page: 4

Compared with the same period last year in the first nine months of this year, Hungarian industrial daily output increased by 18%

Hungarian industrial production has greatly developed in the first nine months of this year. According to statistics from the Hungarian Central Bureau of Statistics, during this period, Hungarian state-owned industries overfulfilled the original production plan by 103%, and the average daily output increased by 18% compared with the first nine months of last year.

Compared with the same period last year, in the first nine months of this year, Hungary's total coal mining increased by 18%, with an average daily output of 79,000 tons, pig iron increased by 35%, steel increased by 24%, oil production increased by 21%, electricity increased by 22%, bauxite increased by 16%, aluminum production increased by 60%, and machine industry production increased by 16%. Most of the chemical industry products also overfulfilled the original production plan, with nitrogen fertilizer increasing by 25% compared with the same period last year.

The production of light industry and food industry has also increased. In the first nine months of this year, the average daily output of the textile industry was 9% higher than the average daily output of the entire last year.

Since the beginning of this year, Hungary's agricultural collectivization movement has further developed. From January 1 to September 30, the number of agricultural cooperatives in the country increased by another 21,000 members, and their cultivated land area increased by 84,000 hectares. The sowing area of ​​grains, feed crops and industrial crops in the country has also been greatly expanded.

Transportation and domestic and foreign trade have also been further developed. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 今年头九个月和去年同期比较  匈工业日产量增加18％

1958-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

    今年头九个月和去年同期比较
    匈工业日产量增加18％
    匈牙利的工业生产在今年头九个月中有了很大发展。据匈牙利中央统计局统计，在这一时期，匈牙利国营工业以103％超额完成了原订生产计划，平均日产量比去年头九个月增加了18％。
    和去年同期比较，今年头九个月中，匈牙利的采煤总量增加了18％，平均日产量达七万九千吨，生铁增加了35％，钢增加了24％，石油产量增加了21％，电力增加了22％，铝矾土增加了16％，铝产量增加了60％，机器工业生产增加了16％。化学工业的大部分产品也超额完成了原订生产计划，其中氮肥就比去年同期增加25％。
    轻工业和食品工业的生产也增加了。今年头九个月中，纺织工业的平均日产量就比去年全年的平均日产量高9％。
    今年以来，匈牙利的农业合作化运动有了进一步的发展。从1月1日到9月30日为止，全国农业合作社又增加了二万一千个社员，它们的耕地面积增加了八万四千公顷。全国的谷物、饲料作物和技术作物的播种面积也大大扩大了。
    交通运输和国内外贸易也有了进一步的发展。　（据新华社讯）
