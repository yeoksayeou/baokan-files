Title: High Mountains and Vast Seas Cannot Stop Our Friendship - Speech by Baluku, Head of the Albanian Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-10-01
Page: 7

    Speech at the Inaugural Meeting of the China Friendship Associations with Ten Brotherly Countries
    High Mountains and Vast Seas Cannot Stop Our Friendship
    Speech by Baluku, Head of the Albanian Military Delegation

    Dear comrades and friends,

    On the eve of the great National Day of October 1st and the establishment of the China-Albania Friendship Association, please allow me to convey the warm congratulations of the Albanian people to the fraternal Chinese people, and to congratulate the Chinese people on their greater achievements in building a socialist society.

    The Chinese people, with a high degree of political consciousness, under the leadership of the Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, have achieved, and are constantly achieving, great successes in all aspects of the struggle to complete the great historical task before them—transforming China from a backward agricultural country into an advanced socialist industrial power. China is full of creative and constructive labor everywhere. The Albanian people are infinitely happy about these achievements, because they are also the achievements of the Albanian people, the achievements of the socialist family.

    On the eve of the great festival of the Chinese people, the China-Albania Friendship Association was established. Our long-standing friendship originates from the common struggle for freedom, independence, and sovereignty for centuries. Like the Chinese people, the Albanian people have suffered immense pain under the iron heel of various invaders. But they have never laid down their weapons and have never stopped fighting for freedom. The Albanian people have independently and resolutely resisted the invasion of various aggressors. The great victory of the October Revolution, which established the first socialist country and the first worker-peasant country in the world, also shone in our country. The great October Revolution led by the Bolshevik Party of the Soviet Union headed by Lenin warmed the hearts of the oppressed people and also warmed the hearts of the Albanian people. The October Socialist Revolution opened a new era for mankind.

    Today, in our era, when Marxism-Leninism has triumphed and taken root in the minds of the masses, when the socialist system has formed a world system, the Albanian people have many friends. In the struggle of the people of the socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union for socialist construction and the struggle of the people of the world for the defense of world peace, the Albanian people are the loyal friends of the Chinese people. High mountains and vast seas cannot stop our friendship. Although Albania is geographically far from China, we are one, inseparable friends, because lofty ideals unite us, because we have the leadership of the invincible Marxist-Leninist ideology.

    The Albanian people cherish the deepest friendship for the great Chinese people. Under the leadership of the Marxist-Leninist parties, the two peoples will become closer day by day in their efforts to strengthen the fraternal friendship between the socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union. As always, the Albanian people will continue to understand the long and rich culture of the Chinese people and draw on the rich experience of the Chinese people in socialist construction.

    The socialist camp is growing stronger day by day, it is huge and invincible. Neither the despicable provocative activities of the imperialists, nor the vile activities of the Yugoslav revisionists who serve the aggressive imperialists, can weaken the great unity of the socialist camp. Their conspiracies will suffer a shameful defeat in the face of the great unity of our socialist camp, in the face of the deep fraternal friendship that connects our people, and in the face of the power of the great Marxist-Leninist ideology.

    The People's Republic of China is a powerful force in the world today. The imperialists continue to obstruct the restoration of the People's Republic of China's position in the United Nations, but the 600 million Chinese people play an extremely important role on the international stage. The U.S. imperialists unreasonably protect the zombie Chiang Kai-shek, provoke the People's Republic of China, and occupy China's territory of Taiwan and other islands. The Albanian people are convinced that Taiwan and other islands will surely return to the embrace of the motherland, and the Albanian people are convinced that the just cause of the Chinese people will surely win.

    The establishment of the China Friendship Associations with ten brotherly countries demonstrates the unbreakable friendship between the people of the socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union.

    Long live the unbreakable and eternal friendship between the Albanian people and the fraternal Chinese people!

    Long live the People's Republic of China!

    Long live Chairman Mao Zedong!

    Long live the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union!

### 高山大海阻挡不了我们的友谊  阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团团长巴卢库的讲话

1958-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    在我国同十个兄弟国家友好协会成立大会上的讲话
    高山大海阻挡不了我们的友谊
    阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团团长巴卢库的讲话亲爱的同志们，朋友们：
    请允许我在伟大的10月1日国庆节的前夕和中阿友好协会成立之际，转达阿尔巴尼亚人民对兄弟般的中国人民的热烈的祝贺，祝贺中国人民在建设社会主义社会中取得更大的成就。
    有着高度政治觉悟的中国人民，在共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下，在完成摆在她面前的巨大的历史任务——将中国从落后的农业国变为社会主义的先进的工业国的斗争中，在各方面，都取得了并在不断地取得巨大的成就。中国到处都充满了创造性和建设性的劳动。阿尔巴尼亚人民对这些成就表示无限的高兴，因为这也就是阿尔巴尼亚人民的成就，是社会主义大家庭的成就。
    在中国人民伟大节日的前夕，成立了中阿友好协会。我们悠久的友谊，起源于数世纪以来为自由、独立和主权的共同斗争。阿尔巴尼亚人民同中国人民一样，在各种侵略者的铁蹄践踏下，遭受到无比的痛苦。但是他们从来没有放下过武器，从来没有停止过为争取自由的斗争。阿尔巴尼亚人民曾经独立地坚决地抵抗了各类侵略者的进犯。世界上建立第一个社会主义国家、第一个工农国家的十月革命的伟大胜利，也在我们国家里放出光芒。以列宁为首的苏联共产党所领导的伟大的十月革命温暖着被压迫人民的心，也温暖着阿尔巴尼亚人民的心。十月社会主义革命，给人类开辟了新的纪元。
    今天在我们的时代里，当马列主义取得胜利并且在群众思想中生根的时候，当社会主义的制度已经形成了一个世界体系的时候，阿尔巴尼亚人民有着许多的朋友。在以苏联为首的社会主义国家人民为进行社会主义建设和全世界人民为保卫世界和平的斗争中，阿尔巴尼亚人民是中国人民的忠实的朋友。高山大海阻挡不了我们的友谊。虽然从地理观点上来说阿尔巴尼亚远离中国，但我们是一体的，是不可分隔的朋友，因为崇高的理想把我们团结在一起，因为我们有着战无不胜的马列主义思想的领导。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民对伟大的中国人民怀着最深厚的友谊。在马列主义政党的领导下，在加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营各国间的兄弟般友谊的努力中，两国人民将一天比一天更加亲密。和以往一样，阿尔巴尼亚人民将继续了解中国人民的悠久和丰富的文化，吸取中国人民在社会主义建设中的丰富经验。
    社会主义阵营正一天天地壮大，它是巨大而不可战胜的。不管是帝国主义者卑鄙的挑衅活动，不管是为侵略成性的帝国主义服务的南斯拉夫修正主义者的下流活动，都丝毫不能削弱社会主义阵营的大团结。他们的阴谋在我们社会主义阵营大团结的面前，在联系着我们人民的深厚的兄弟般的友谊的面前，在伟大的马列主义思想的威力面前，都将遭到可耻的失败。
    中华人民共和国是今天世界上的一支强大力量，帝国主义者继续阻挠恢复中华人民共和国在联合国中的地位，但六亿中国人民在国际舞台上起着极重要的作用。美帝国主义者毫无道理地保护着僵尸蒋介石，对中华人民共和国进行挑衅并侵占了中国的领土台湾和其他岛屿。阿尔巴尼亚人民深信，台湾和其他岛屿必将重归祖国怀抱，阿尔巴尼亚人民深信中国人民的正义事业一定会取得胜利。
    中国对十个兄弟国家友好协会的成立，显示了以苏联为首的社会主义阵营各国人民之间的牢不可破的友谊。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民和兄弟般的中国人民之间的牢不可破的永恒的友谊万岁！
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    毛泽东主席万岁！
    以苏联为首的社会主义阵营万岁！
