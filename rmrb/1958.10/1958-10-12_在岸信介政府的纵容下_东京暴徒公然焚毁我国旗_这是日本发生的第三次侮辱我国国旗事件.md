Title: Under the Indulgence of the Nobusuke Kishi Government, Tokyo Thugs Publicly Burn Our National Flag: This is the Third Incident of Insulting Our National Flag in Japan

Author: 
Date: 1958-10-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 11th: Thugs from the fascist organization "Great Japan Patriotic Party," under the wing of the Nobusuke Kishi government, gathered in front of the Shinbashi Station of the Tokyo National Railway tram on the afternoon of the 10th and publicly burned the Chinese national flag.

This is the third incident of the Nobusuke Kishi government condoning thugs publicly insulting the Chinese national flag.

Under the indulgence of the Nobusuke Kishi government, these fascists held a so-called "Anti-Communist Encouragement Taiwan National Assembly." Asanuma Michio, one of the heads of the "Great Japan Patriotic Party" and editor-in-chief of "Anti-Communist News," issued anti-communist howls to the party members of this fascist organization at the meeting. This fascist openly echoed the Nobusuke Kishi government's claim to refuse to apologize to China for the incident of insulting the Chinese national flag in Nagasaki.

Subsequently, this thug took out a lighter and burned a Chinese national flag that he had brought with him.

In order to conceal the serious nature of these fascists' public insult to the Chinese national flag, the Japanese police then arrested Asanuma Michio on the charge of violating the so-called "Fire Prevention Ordinance." The police said they did not apply the criminal law regarding insulting foreign national flags.

Asanuma told the police that his purpose in burning the Chinese national flag was to make relations between Japan and China worse.

### 在岸信介政府的纵容下  东京暴徒公然焚毁我国旗  这是日本发生的第三次侮辱我国国旗事件

1958-10-12
第3版()
专栏：

    在岸信介政府的纵容下
    东京暴徒公然焚毁我国旗
    这是日本发生的第三次侮辱我国国旗事件
    新华社11日讯　东京消息：岸信介政府卵翼下的法西斯组织“大日本爱国党”的暴徒们，10日下午啸聚在东京国营铁道电车新桥车站前面，公然焚毁中国国旗。
    这是岸信介政府纵容暴徒公开侮辱中国国旗的第三次事件。
    在岸信介政府的纵容下，这些法西斯分子举行了一个所谓“反共鼓励台湾国民大会”。“大日本爱国党”头子之一、“防共新闻”总编辑浅沼美智雄在会上向这个法西斯组织的党徒们发出了反共叫嚣。这个法西斯分子公然附和岸信介政府拒绝为在长崎发生的侮辱中国国旗的事件向中国道歉的主张。
    随后，这个暴徒就掏出打火机焚烧他事先带在身上的一面中国国旗。
    日本警察为了掩饰这些法西斯分子公开侮辱中国国旗的严重暴行的性质，当时以所谓违反“反火灾预防条例”的罪名逮捕了浅沼美智雄。警察说，他们并没有应用关于侮辱外国国旗的刑法条例。
    浅沼对警察说，他焚毁中国国旗的目的是要使日本同中国的关系变得更坏。
