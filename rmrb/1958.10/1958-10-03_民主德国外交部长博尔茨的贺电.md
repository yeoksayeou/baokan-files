Title: Telegram of Greetings from Lothar Bolz, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic
Author: Lotha·Bolz
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

      Telegram of Greetings from Lothar Bolz, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic

To Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of the 9th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I extend to you my heartfelt congratulations and fraternal respect. The German Democratic Republic, which is closely linked with the People's Republic of China through a treaty of friendship and cooperation, firmly supports the just demands of the Chinese people for Taiwan and the realization of the legitimate rights of the People's Republic of China in the United Nations. I wish you and the great Chinese people further achievements in building socialism and defending peace in the Far East and the world.

                              Dr. Lothar Bolz
                              Minister of Foreign Affairs

### 民主德国外交部长博尔茨的贺电
洛塔·博尔茨博士
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

      民主德国外交部长博尔茨的贺电中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅：
    值此中华人民共和国成立九周年之际，我谨向你致以衷心的祝贺和兄弟般的敬意。通过友好合作条约同中华人民共和国紧密联系在一起的德意志民主共和国坚决支持中国人民对台湾和实现中华人民共和国在联合国中的合法权利的正当要求。祝你和伟大的中国人民在建设社会主义和保卫远东和世界和平的斗争中取得进一步的成就。
                              外交部长　洛塔·博尔茨博士
