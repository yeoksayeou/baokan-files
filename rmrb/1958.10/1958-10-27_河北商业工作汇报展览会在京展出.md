Title: Hebei Commercial Work Report Exhibition on Display in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-10-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 26th -- The Hebei Province Commercial Work Report Exhibition, currently on display in Beijing, has received attention and welcome from relevant central departments and visitors from various regions. As of the 24th, more than 35,000 people had visited the exhibition. They generally believe that Hebei Province's commercial workers are full of enthusiasm, and their experience is rich and creative, worthy of widespread promotion.

On the afternoon of the 21st, Vice Chairman Zhu De visited the exhibition. He praised the achievements of the great leap forward in Hebei Province's commercial work, and gave instructions on issues such as how commercial work should actively carry out large-scale purchases and sales, vigorously mobilize the masses to develop the mountain economy, and the future direction of commercial departments' operations.

The Hebei Province Commercial Work Report Exhibition is jointly sponsored by the Financial and Trade Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Fifth Office of the State Council.

The exhibition also displayed hundreds of new tools created by Hebei Province's commercial workers in the technical revolution movement, which attracted many visitors.

The exhibition started on October 6th and is scheduled to end in mid-November.

### 河北商业工作汇报展览会在京展出

1958-10-27
第3版()
专栏：

    河北商业工作汇报展览会在京展出
    据新华社26日讯　正在北京市展出的河北省商业工作汇报展览会，受到中央有关部门和各地参观者的重视和欢迎。到24日为止，已经有三万五千多人参观了这个展览会。他们普遍认为河北省商业工作者的干劲冲天，他们的经验丰富而又富于创造性，值得普遍推广。
    21日下午，朱德副主席参观了这个展览会。他对河北省商业工作大跃进的成就表示赞扬，并且就商业工作应积极开展大购大销，大力发动群众开发山区经济，以及商业部门今后的经营方向等问题作了指示。
    河北省商业工作汇报展览会是中共中央财政贸易工作部和国务院第五办公室联合举办的。
    展览会上还展出了河北省商业职工在技术革命运动中创造的几百件新工具，都吸引了很多观众。
    这个展览会是从10月6日开始展出的，预定在11月中旬结束。
