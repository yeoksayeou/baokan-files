Title: Sanjiang Folk Songs
Author:
Date: 1958-10-19
Page: 5

    Sanjiang Folk Songs
    Labor creates a prosperous village,
    Everywhere green shades surround the village,
    The reed pipe plays, moving the white clouds,
    The clear breeze carries the scent of rice.
    Opening a water channel on Mount Motian,
    The high mountain bows its head to me,
    We will build rice paddies on the mountaintop,
    And drink with Chang'e at the same table.
    Good news of steel spreads in all directions,
    Molten iron flows like the ocean,
    Mountain area blast furnace smoke rises,
    Blackening the Jade Emperor in space.

### 三江民歌

1958-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

    三江民歌
    劳动创造富裕乡，
    处处绿荫绕村庄，
    芦笙吹得白云动，
    清风送来稻谷香。
    摩天岭上开水沟，
    高山向我低了头，
    要把水田修山顶，
    同桌嫦娥共饮酒。
    钢铁喜讯传四方，
    铁水奔流如海洋，
    山区高炉烟雾起，
    熏黑太空玉帝皇。
