Title: Lebanese National Front Orders Arrest of Rioters
Author:
Date: 1958-10-14
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 13th -- According to a report in the "Today's News" here today, the Lebanese National Front held a meeting on the 12th to discuss the implementation of martial law in Beirut and certain areas of Lebanon as the situation in Lebanon becomes more critical. It is reported that orders have been issued to the patriotic forces to arrest anyone who dares to create disturbances in the country.

### 黎巴嫩民族阵线下令逮捕骚乱分子

1958-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩民族阵线下令逮捕骚乱分子
    新华社大马士革13日电　据这里的“今天报”今天报道，黎巴嫩民族阵线12日开会讨论了在黎巴嫩局势变得更加危急的情况下，在贝鲁特和黎巴嫩的某些地区实施戒严的问题。据报道，已经下令爱国力量部队逮捕任何敢于在国内制造骚乱的人。
