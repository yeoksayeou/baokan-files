Title: British "News Chronicle" Calls on the United States to End Madness in the Far East
Author:
Date: 1958-10-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, 13th (TASS): The British Liberal Party's "News Chronicle" welcomes the order of the Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China to extend the deadline for ceasing shelling of Jinmen Island for another two weeks.

    The newspaper's editorial said: "China's statement today about stopping the shelling of Jinmen for another two weeks extends a breathing space. So far, there has been no movement to properly utilize this breathing space. News received in the last few days of the seven days since the shelling stopped said that the islands are not only replenishing manpower, but also replenishing weapons, including heavy artillery. Can't this stupid thing be ended?"
    The newspaper emphasized that due to the actions of the Chiang Kai-shek elements and the Americans, "what could emerge is the most meaningless war in history." The newspaper called for an end to this "madness."

### 英国“新闻纪事报”呼吁美国结束在远东的疯狂行为

1958-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

    英国“新闻纪事报”
    呼吁美国结束在远东的疯狂行为
    新华社14日讯　据塔斯社伦敦13日讯：英国自由党的“新闻纪事报”欢迎中华人民共和国国防部关于把停止炮击金门岛的期限再延长两星期的命令。
    报纸的社论说：“中国今天关于再停止炮击金门两星期的声明延长了喘息机会。至今没有任何动向表明正确地利用这个喘息机会。在停止炮击以来的七天中的最后几天所得到的消息说，这些岛屿不仅正在补充人力，而且在补充武器，其中包括重型大炮。难道不能结束这种愚蠢的事情吗？”
    这家报纸着重指出，由于蒋介石分子和美国人的行动，“可能出现历史上最无意义的战争”。报纸呼吁结束这种“疯狂的行为”。
