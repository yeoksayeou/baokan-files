Explanation of Terms

	Modeling: Various machines are assembled from many parts. Most parts of a machine are manufactured using the casting method. Casting involves pouring molten metal (steel or iron) into a pre-made sand mold cavity, the shape of which is identical to the shape of the part. In casting production, the process of making the mold cavity with sand in a sand box is usually called modeling.

	Modeling Area: The workshop space occupied by modeling is usually called the modeling area in the casting industry. In fact, the areas occupied by several processes before and after modeling, such as mold closing (joining the two halves of the sand box together), pouring (injecting molten metal), and mold opening (opening the sand box after pouring), are also included in the modeling area. These processes are the most basic processes in casting. A certain area is designated in the casting workshop, and a certain number of lifting and transportation equipment are equipped for these processes. The modeling area is the effective area of casting production; other areas of the casting workshop are auxiliary areas.

	Monthly Output Per Unit Modeling Area: This is the monthly output of qualified castings per square meter of modeling area. Since the level of casting output depends on the production efficiency of several basic processes such as modeling, and the utilization rate of workshop space and equipment also depends on these basic processes, the indicator of output per unit modeling area is usually used to assess the production efficiency and area utilization rate of the casting workshop. ### 名词解释

1958-10-19
第2版()
专栏：

    名词解释
    造型：各种机器是由许多零件装配出来的。机器上大多数零件都是用铸造方法制造的。铸造就是将熔化后的金属（钢或铁）倒入预先制好的砂制的型腔中，型腔的形状和零件的形状完全一样。铸造生产中，在砂箱中用砂制造型腔的工序通常叫做造型。
    造型面积：造型所占用的车间场地，在铸造行业中通常叫做造型面积，实际上造型前后的几个工序：合箱（将两片砂箱合在一起）、浇注（即注入熔化金属）、开箱（浇注后将砂箱打开）所占用的面积也都计算在造型面积之中。这几道工序是铸造的最基本工序，在铸造车间中都划出一定的面积，并配备一定数量的起重运输设备，供这些工序使用。造型面积是铸造生产的有效面积，铸造车间的其他面积都是属于辅助性质的面积。
    单位造型面积月产量：是每一平方公尺造型面积每月的合格铸件产量，由于铸造产量的高低决定于造型等几个基本工序的生产效率，车间场地和设备的利用率也决定于这几道基本工序，因此单位造型面积产量这个指标，通常被用来考核铸造车间生产效率和面积利用率。（附图表）
