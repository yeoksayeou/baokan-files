Title: UAR Vice President Amer Visits Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1958-10-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 9th:  United Arab Republic Vice President, Minister of Defense, and Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces, Marshal Amer, flew to Prague at noon today. He will be making a ten-day state visit to Czechoslovakia.

In his speech welcoming Marshal Amer at the airport, Premier Široký stressed that the Czechoslovak government and people support the United Arab Republic in its struggle against imperialist aggression and for peace. He expressed his firm belief that Marshal Amer's visit will strengthen the friendship between the two countries.

In his speech, Marshal Amer said that in our people's struggle to defend national independence and oppose imperialist intervention, the Czechoslovak people stand on our side. Our people admire this solemn stance.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 9th: Czechoslovak President Novotný received United Arab Republic Vice President Marshal Amer on the 9th.

On the same day, Marshal Amer paid a visit to Czechoslovak Premier Široký and held cordial talks with him. They discussed the issue of further developing friendly relations between the two countries.

### 阿联副总统阿密尔访捷

1958-10-11
第5版()
专栏：

    阿联副总统阿密尔访捷
    据新华社布拉格9日电　阿拉伯联合共和国副总统、国防部长和武装部队总司令阿密尔元帅今天中午飞抵布拉格。他将在捷克斯洛伐克进行为期十天的国事访问。
    西罗基总理在机场欢迎阿密尔元帅的讲话中着重指出，捷克斯洛伐克政府和人民支持阿拉伯联合共和国反对帝国主义的侵略和争取和平的斗争。他表示深信，阿密尔元帅的这次访问将加强两国的友谊。
    阿密尔元帅在讲话中说，在我国人民保卫国家独立、反对帝国主义干涉的斗争中，捷克斯洛伐克人民站在我们这一边。我国人民对这种严正的立场表示钦佩。
    新华社布拉格9日电　捷克斯洛伐克总统诺沃提尼9日接见了阿拉伯联合共和国副总统阿密尔元帅。
    同一天，阿密尔元帅拜会了捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基，并且同他进行了亲切的谈话。他们谈到了进一步发展两国友好关系的问题。
