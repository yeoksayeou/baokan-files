Title: Congratulations on the Establishment of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, Telegram from the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
Author:
Date: 1958-10-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress sent a congratulatory telegram to the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region People's Congress. The text is as follows:

    To the First Session of the First People's Congress of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region:

    On the occasion of the establishment of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, we extend our warm congratulations to you and, through you, to the people of all ethnic groups in Ningxia.

    The establishment of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region is a great achievement of the Party's ethnic policy. It will further strengthen the unity of the people of all ethnic groups in Ningxia, encourage the enthusiasm and creativity of all ethnic groups, mobilize all forces under the brilliant illumination of the Party's general line for socialist construction, muster revolutionary zeal, and make full use of abundant resources to actively develop agriculture, industry, and other construction undertakings. We wish you greater contributions to the great socialist construction of the motherland.

                        Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
                                            October 24, 1958

### 庆祝宁夏回族自治区成立  人大常委会的贺电

1958-10-26
第2版()
专栏：

    庆祝宁夏回族自治区成立
    人大常委会的贺电
　　新华社25日电　全国人民代表大会常务委员会致宁夏回族自治区人代会的贺电。文如下：
    宁夏回族自治区第一届人民代表大会第一次会议：
    正当宁夏回族自治区成立的时候，谨向你们，并且通过你们向宁夏各族人民致以热烈的祝贺。
    宁夏回族自治区的成立，是党的民族政策的伟大成就，将进一步加强宁夏各族人民的团结，鼓舞各族人民的积极性和创造性，在党的社会主义建设总路线的光辉照耀下，调动一切力量，鼓足革命干劲，充分利用丰富资源，积极发展农业、工业和其他各项建设事业。祝你们在祖国伟大的社会主义建设中做出更大的贡献。
                        全国人民代表大会常务委员会
                                            1958年10月24日
