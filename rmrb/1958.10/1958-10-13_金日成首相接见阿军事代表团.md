Title: Premier Kim Il-sung Receives Albanian Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-10-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 11th - Premier Kim Il-sung of the Korean Cabinet today received the entire Albanian military delegation headed by First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense, General Baluku.

Present at the reception were Pak Kum-chol and Kim Chang-man, Vice-Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, General Kim Kwang-hyup, Minister of National Defense, and Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs, Li Dong-geon.

Also present was the Albanian Ambassador to Korea, Gedeshia.

On the 10th, the delegation laid a wreath at the "Liberation Tower" in Pyongyang, a monument commemorating the Soviet Army's liberation of Korea. The Korean Ministry of National Defense held a welcome rally on the 9th to welcome the delegation.

### 金日成首相接见阿军事代表团

1958-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    金日成首相接见阿军事代表团
    据新华社平壤11日电　朝鲜内阁首相金日成今天接见了以部长会议第一副主席兼国防部长巴卢库上将为首的阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团全体人员。
    接见时在座的有朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴金哲、金昌满，民族保卫相金光侠大将，外务省副相李东建。
    陪同代表团前往的有阿尔巴尼亚驻朝鲜大使格德夏。
    代表团10日曾经在平壤向牡丹峰纪念苏军解放朝鲜的“解放塔”敬献花圈，朝鲜民族保卫省9日曾经举行欢迎大会欢迎代表团。
