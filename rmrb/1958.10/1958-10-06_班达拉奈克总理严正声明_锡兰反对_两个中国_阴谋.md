Title: Bandaranaike's Solemn Declaration: Ceylon Opposes the "Two Chinas" Plot
Author:
Date: 1958-10-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 3rd: According to a report from Colombo by the Indian Press Trust, Ceylon's Prime Minister Bandaranaike, when elucidating Ceylon's attitude towards the issue of restoring China's legitimate seat in the United Nations in the House of Representatives on the 2nd, said that Ceylon supports any action consistent with its opposition to "Two Chinas." He said that there is only one China, which is represented by the government of the People's Republic of China.

Bandaranaike said he might attend the current session of the United Nations General Assembly.

### 班达拉奈克总理严正声明  锡兰反对“两个中国”阴谋

1958-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

    班达拉奈克总理严正声明
    锡兰反对“两个中国”阴谋
    新华社新德里3日电　据印度报业托辣斯自科伦坡报道，锡兰总理班达拉奈克2日在众议院阐明锡兰对于恢复中国在联合国的合法席位这一问题的态度时说，锡兰支持同它的反对“两个中国”的态度相一致的任何行动。他说，只有一个中国，它是由中华人民共和国政府代表的。
    班达拉奈克说，他可能出席本届联合国大会会议。
