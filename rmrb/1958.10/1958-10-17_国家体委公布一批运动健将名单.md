Title: State Physical Culture and Sports Commission Announces a Batch of Master Sportsmen List
Author:
Date: 1958-10-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 16th. The Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China recently announced another eighty-four Master Sportsmen in cycling, track and field, gymnastics, swimming, and diving. The list is as follows:

Cycling Master Sportsmen (57): Yang Changqing, Li Kuan, Gao Jukun, Yang Guorui, Li Yudi, Zhang Linzao, Chen Deying, Wang Daoshan, Guan Xinming, Liu Guangming, Wang Guicai, Xiong Nailiang, Liu Zhenlin, Zhu Jinling, Xie Mingjun, Li Faqing, Quan Xiugen, Fu Mingqi, Kong Fanling, Ju Fengshan, Liu Shaohua, Sun Genshui, Wang Awu, Jin Qiqian, Weng Weisong, Huang Mingxing, Wang Chenglin, Wei Zuofu, Han Guangzhi, Xun Huanggen (the above 30 are male), Wang Han, Hu Xiaofeng, Feng Zuyao, Tong Guihua, Meng Shuzhi, Li Fengying, Wang Keqing, Kong Fanjie, Yang Xiuqin, Yin Shurong, Zheng Zaijin, Du Ruihua, Huang Liwen, Zheng Junru, Kong Huizhen, Zheng Chunmei, He Cuiping, Ye Caidi, Zhuang Hongmei, He Huifen, Zheng Ruixia, Song Yunying, Bao Lixing, Liang Baoyi, Xu Huimian, Weng Biye, Long Yihuang (the above 27 are female).

Track and Field Master Sportsmen (14): Liu Shuqian, Liang Shiqiang, Zhou Wenqing, Chen Tongbin, Chu Qidong, Chen Jiaquan, Wu Zaiyu, Bi Hongfu, Sun Jiuyuan, Peng Weishi (the above 10 are male), Li Yun, Liu Suqiong, Yang Yumin, Xiao Jinying (the above 4 are female).

Gymnastics Master Sportsmen (8): Yu Liefeng, He Zhiliang, Guo Shuzeng, Xia Dejun (the above 4 are male), Wang Shangfen, Zhang Yanyun, Ma Wenjuan, Wang Yumei (the above 4 are female).

Swimming Master Sportsman (1): Fu Dajin (male).

Diving Master Sportsmen (4): Chen Guoneng, Li Zhende, Liang Boxi (the above 3 are male), Liang Xiuying (female).

### 国家体委公布一批运动健将名单
