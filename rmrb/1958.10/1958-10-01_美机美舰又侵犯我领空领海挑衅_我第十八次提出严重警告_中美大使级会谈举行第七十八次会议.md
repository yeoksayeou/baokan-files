Title: U.S. Planes and Warships Again Invade Our Airspace and Territorial Waters for Provocation; We Lodge the Eighteenth Serious Warning; Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Hold the Seventy-Eighth Meeting
Author:
Date: 1958-10-01
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. On September 30th, thirteen U.S. fighter planes invaded the airspace over the coastal area of Xiamen, Fujian Province, and five U.S. warships invaded China's territorial waters off the coast of Fujian Province. A spokesman of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to state: The Chinese government lodges the eighteenth serious warning against this grave military provocation by the United States.
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 30th. The seventy-eighth meeting of the Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks was held at the Myśliwiecki Palace in Warsaw at 2:00 PM today. This meeting was originally scheduled to take place at 10:00 AM today but was postponed to 2:00 PM due to administrative reasons.
    This meeting lasted two hours and fifty minutes. Afterwards, both sides announced that the next meeting would continue on October 4th (Saturday) at 3:00 PM.

### 美机美舰又侵犯我领空领海挑衅  我第十八次提出严重警告  中美大使级会谈举行第七十八次会议

1958-10-01
第2版()
专栏：

    美机美舰又侵犯我领空领海挑衅
    我第十八次提出严重警告
    中美大使级会谈举行第七十八次会议
    新华社30日讯　9月30日美国战斗机十三架侵入我国福建厦门地区的领海上空，美国军舰五艘侵入我国福建地区的领海，我外交部发言人奉命声明：对于美国的这种严重的军事挑衅，中国政府第十八次提出严重警告。
    新华社华沙30日电　中美两国大使级会谈第七十八次会议，今天下午二时在华沙梅希里维兹基宫举行。这次会议原定在今日上午十时举行的，由于行政性的理由，推迟到下午二时举行。
    本次会议历时两小时五十分。会后双方宣布，下次会议将在10月4日（星期六）下午三时继续举行。
