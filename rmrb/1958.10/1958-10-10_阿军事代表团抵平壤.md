Title: Albanian Military Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1958-10-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 9th October: An Albanian military delegation headed by Comrade Baluku, First Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defence, arrived in Pyongyang by plane this morning for a friendly visit to Korea.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included General Kim Kwang-hyup, Minister of National Defence of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Li Dong-geon, and senior generals of the Korean People's Army. Diplomatic envoys and military attachés of various countries stationed in Korea also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, Lushun-Dalian, 9th October: The Albanian military delegation to China, headed by Comrade Baluku, First Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defence of the People's Republic of Albania, left Lushun-Dalian by special plane this morning to visit the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

The heads of the troops stationed in Lushun-Dalian and General Xiao Hua, who accompanied the Albanian military delegation to China to visit Lushun-Dalian, Albanian Ambassador to China Balili, and the responsible persons of the Party and government in Lushun-Dalian went to the airport to see them off.

The Albanian military delegation to China arrived in Lushun-Dalian on the 6th. During the past three days, the distinguished guests visited Dalian Port, the Lushun-Dalian Workers' Sanatorium, the Dalian Locomotive Factory, and the Lushunkou Base of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy. The distinguished guests were delighted and encouraged by the rapid growth and strengthening of China's naval power.

The delegation visited warships at the base and had cordial conversations with the sailors. Sailor Tang Dayao also represented all the sailors in asking the delegation to convey the friendship of the Chinese sailors to the Albanian sailors. Subsequently, the delegation traveled from Lushun Port to Dalian Port by warship. Before disembarking, the distinguished guests and the sailors exchanged souvenirs as keepsakes.

### 阿军事代表团抵平壤

1958-10-10
第5版()
专栏：

    阿军事代表团抵平壤
    新华社平壤9日电　以部长会议第一副主席兼国防部部长巴卢库上将为首的阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团今天上午乘飞机到达平壤，来朝鲜进行友好访问。
    前往机场欢迎的有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫相金光侠大将、外务省副相李东建和朝鲜人民军的高级将领。各国驻朝鲜的外交使节和武官也到机场欢迎。
    新华社旅大9日电　以阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议第一副主席兼国防部部长巴卢库上将为首的阿尔巴尼亚访华军事代表团，今天上午从旅大乘专机前往朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访问。
    驻旅大部队的首长和陪同阿尔巴尼亚访华军事代表团来旅大参观的萧华上将、阿尔巴尼亚驻我国大使巴利里以及旅大市党政负责人等都到机场送行。
    阿尔巴尼亚访华军事代表团6日到达旅大。三天来，贵宾们参观了大连海港、旅大市工人疗养院、大连机车厂和中国人民解放军海军旅顺口基地。贵宾们对我国海军力量的迅速成长壮大感到欢欣鼓舞。
    代表团在基地参观了舰艇，并和水兵们亲切交谈。水兵唐大尧还代表全体水兵请代表团把中国水兵的友谊带给阿尔巴尼亚的水兵兄弟。随后，代表团从旅顺港乘舰艇到大连港。登岸前，贵宾们和水兵们互赠纪念品留念。
