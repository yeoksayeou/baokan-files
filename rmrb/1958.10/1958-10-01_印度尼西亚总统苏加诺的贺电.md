Title: Telegram of Congratulations from Indonesian President Sukarno
Author:
Date: 1958-10-01
Page: 3

To His Excellency Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of the National Day of the People's Republic of China, I am very pleased to express to Your Excellency and, through Your Excellency, to the Chinese people, the most sincere congratulations and best wishes of the Indonesian people and myself.

                              Sukarno
                              President of the Republic of Indonesia

### 印度尼西亚总统苏加诺的贺电

1958-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚总统苏加诺的贺电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东阁下：
    欣逢中华人民共和国国庆节，我很愉快地向阁下并且通过阁下向中国人民表示印度尼西亚人民和我个人的最热诚的祝贺和最良好的愿望。
                              印度尼西亚共和国总统　苏加诺
