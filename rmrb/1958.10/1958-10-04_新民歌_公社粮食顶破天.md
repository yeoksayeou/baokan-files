Title: New Folk Song: The Commune's Grain Pierces the Sky
Author:
Date: 1958-10-04
Page: 3

New Folk Song

    The Commune's Grain Pierces the Sky
  Colorful clouds drift in the sky,
  Green waves reflect the seedlings on the ground;
  Clouds gather in the sky,
  The commune's grain pierces the sky.
        —Gaoyao, Guangdong

### 新民歌  公社粮食顶破天

1958-10-04
第3版()
专栏：

    新民歌
    公社粮食顶破天
  天上彩云朵朵飘，
  地上禾苗映青浪；
  天上云彩聚成团，
  公社粮食顶破天。
        ——广东高要
