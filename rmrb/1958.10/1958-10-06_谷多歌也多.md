Title: More Grain, More Songs

Author: 
Date: 1958-10-06
Page: 3

New Folk Songs Selection

    More grain, more songs,
    Ten thousand dan of grain, ten thousand songs,
    Just finished singing a song on the upper ridge,
    Another few baskets of grain are loaded on the lower ridge,
    Carts and boats transport grain like shuttles.
              ——Guizhou

### 谷多歌也多

1958-10-06
第3版()
专栏：新民歌选

    谷多歌也多
    谷子多，歌也多，
    万担谷子万支歌，
    上垅刚唱歌一首，
    下垅又扮谷几箩，
    车船运谷像穿梭。
              ——贵州
