Title: Telegram of Congratulations from Algerian Foreign Minister Debaghine
Author: 
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

Telegram of Congratulations from Algerian Foreign Minister Debaghine

To His Excellency Mr. Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:

    On this glorious anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China on October 1st, I would like to extend to Your Excellency, on behalf of the Government of the Republic of Algeria and the Algerian people, our most sincere congratulations. And please convey our best wishes for the prosperity, strength and tranquility of your government and the great Chinese people.

                                       Mohammed Lamine Debaghine
                                       Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Algeria

### 阿尔及利亚外交部长德巴金的贺电
穆罕默德·拉明·德巴金
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚外交部长德巴金的贺电中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅先生阁下：
    在10月1日这光荣的中华人民共和国成立纪念日的时候，我谨代表阿尔及利亚共和国政府和阿尔及利亚人民向阁下表示我们最热诚的祝　贺。并请你转达我们祝贵国政府和伟大的中国人民繁荣、强大和安宁这最良好的愿望。
          阿尔及利亚共和国外交部长　穆罕默德·拉明·德巴金
