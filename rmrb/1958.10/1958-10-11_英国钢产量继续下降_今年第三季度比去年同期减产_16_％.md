Title: British Steel Production Continues to Decline; Third Quarter Output Down 16% Compared to Last Year
Author:
Date: 1958-10-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, London, 9th October. Figures released today by the British Iron and Steel Federation show that British steel production in the third quarter of this year fell by 16% compared to the same period last year. Steel production for the months of July, August and September totalled 4,251,000 tons, compared to 5,059,000 tons during the same period last year.

Compared to the second quarter of this year, steel production in the third quarter decreased by 798,000 tons.

In the first half of this year, British steel production fell by 6% compared to the first half of last year.

The Iron and Steel Federation said that in recent months, steel sales have decreased in export trade and domestically in the coal industry, train manufacturing, shipbuilding and construction industries.

### 英国钢产量继续下降  今年第三季度比去年同期减产16％

1958-10-11
第5版()
专栏：

    英国钢产量继续下降
    今年第三季度比去年同期减产16％
    新华社伦敦9日电　英国钢铁业联合会今天发表的数字表明，今年第三季度英国的钢产量比去年同一时期下降了16％。7、8、9三个月份的钢产量为四百二十五万一千吨，而今年同一时期的产量为五百零五万九千吨。
    与今年第二季度相比，第三季度钢的产量减少了七十九万八千吨。
    在今年上半年，英国钢产量比去年上半年下降了6％。
    钢铁业联合会说，在最近几个月中，钢的销路在出口贸易方面以及在国内的煤炭业、火车制造业、造船业和建筑工业方面都减少了。
