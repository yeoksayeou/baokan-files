Title: Large Electric Locomotive
Author:
Date: 1958-10-04
Page: 2
    Xinhua News Agency

    Large Electric Locomotive
    According to Xinhua News Agency, the largest electric locomotive in China has recently been successfully manufactured by the locomotive factory of the Anshan Iron and Steel Transportation Department.

    This electric locomotive weighs 90 tons and has a maximum traction force of 3,500 to 4,000 tons. It can pull forty to fifty fully loaded ore cars at a speed of more than 50 *huali* per hour. Its carrying capacity is more than 12% greater than the 80-ton electric locomotive previously trial-produced in China. (Attached picture)

### 大型电气机车

1958-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    大型电气机车
    据新华社讯　我国目前最大的一台大型电气机车，最近在鞍钢运输部机车厂制造成功。
    这台电气机车自重九十吨，最大的牵引力达三千五百到四千吨，它能拉着四五十辆满载矿石的车辆，每小时行驶五十多华里，载重量比我国过去试制成功的八十吨电气机车大12％以上。（附图片）
