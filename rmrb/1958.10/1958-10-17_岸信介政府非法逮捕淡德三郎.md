Title: Kishi Nobusuke Government Illegally Arrests Awatake Saburo
Author:
Date: 1958-10-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 15th -- The Kishi Nobusuke government's Tokyo Metropolitan Police Department today illegally arrested Awatake Saburo, Secretary-General of the Japan Asia-Africa Solidarity Committee.
    The Tokyo Metropolitan Police Department, using the excuse of "suspicion of violating the Foreign Exchange Control Law," searched Awatake Saburo's residence yesterday. This morning he went to the police station for questioning, but the police arrested him this afternoon.

### 岸信介政府非法逮捕淡德三郎

1958-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

    岸信介政府非法逮捕淡德三郎
    新华社15日讯　东京消息：岸信介政府东京警视厅今天非法逮捕了日本亚非团结委员会事务局长淡德三郎。
    东京警视厅曾经在昨天以“违反外汇管理法的嫌疑”为借口，搜查了淡德三郎的住宅。今天上午他曾经到警视厅应讯，但是警视厅下午就逮捕了他。
