Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Receives Inner Mongolia Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-10-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 13th — Vice Chairman Zhu De received the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region minority youth visiting delegation this morning.
    Present at the reception were Wang Feng, Sa Kongliao, and Yang Jingren, vice chairmen of the Nationalities Affairs Commission.

### 朱德副主席接见内蒙青年代表团

1958-10-14
第4版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席接见内蒙青年代表团
    新华社13日讯　朱德副主席今天上午接见了内蒙古自治区少数民族青年参观团。
    接见时在座的，有民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、萨空了、杨静仁。
