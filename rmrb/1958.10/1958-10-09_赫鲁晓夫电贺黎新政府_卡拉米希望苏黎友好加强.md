Title: Khrushchev Congratulates New Lebanese Government; Karami Hopes to Strengthen Soviet-Lebanese Friendship

Author:

Date: 1958-10-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th. TASS reports: Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Khrushchev sent a telegram to Lebanese Prime Minister Karami on September 30th, congratulating him on the establishment of the new Lebanese government.

The telegram stated that the Soviet people are following the heroic struggle of the Lebanese people, who are defending the independence of their motherland, with great attention and deep sympathy. The Soviet people sincerely hope that the Lebanese people will liberate their country completely from foreign occupation as soon as possible and create conditions for peaceful labor.

In his reply telegram, Karami said that the Lebanese people will cooperate with all friendly countries and devote themselves to consolidating peace and the independence of their country. Karami wrote that the Lebanese people are grateful for the concern and friendly feelings of the Soviet people expressed in your name to me.

In his reply telegram, Karami expressed his belief that the existing friendly relations between Lebanon and the Soviet Union will be further developed in economic and cultural fields and will help to safeguard peace in the Middle East and the world.

### 赫鲁晓夫电贺黎新政府  卡拉米希望苏黎友好加强

1958-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫电贺黎新政府
    卡拉米希望苏黎友好加强
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科7日讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫9月30日致电黎巴嫩总理卡拉米，祝贺黎巴嫩新政府的成立。
    贺电指出，苏联人民以极大的注意和深刻的同情注视着捍卫自己祖国独立的黎巴嫩人民的英勇斗争。苏联人民热烈希望黎巴嫩人民尽快使自己的国家从外国占领下完全解放出来，并为从事和平劳动创造条件。
    卡拉米在复电中说，黎巴嫩人民将同各友好国家进行合作，并致力于巩固和平和自己国家的独立。卡拉米写道，黎巴嫩人民感谢以您的名义向我表示的苏联人民的关怀和友好感情。
    卡拉米在复电中表示相信，黎苏两国现有的友好关系将在经济和文化方面得到进一步的发展，并将有助于保卫中近东和全世界的和平。
