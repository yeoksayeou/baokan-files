Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Writers and Poets from Ten Countries
Author:
Date: 1958-10-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 27th. Premier Zhou Enlai received this afternoon writers and poets from ten Asian, African, and Latin American countries who had attended the Asian-African Writers' Conference and were invited to visit China.

The writers and poets were: Ma. Diop and Sanghan Ousman from Senegal, West Africa; Hafeez from Nigeria; Andradé and Cruz from Angola; Alachari from Somaliland; Senoga from Uganda; Dudu from Ghana; W. Tiberio from Brazil; Gula Sibalit and ? Ben Wannasi from Thailand; Pillai from India; and Wu Dayong, Meng Guozhen Yong, and Wu Kuimu from Burma.

Also present at the reception were Mao Dun, Zhou Yang, Ba Jin, Shao Quanlin, Liu Baiyu, Yan Wenjing, Yang Shuo, Guo Xiaochuan, and Wang Yafan.

### 周总理接见十个国家的作家和诗人

1958-10-28
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理接见十个国家的作家和诗人
　　新华社27日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了参加过亚非作家会议后应邀来我国访问的亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲的十个国家的作家和诗人。
    这些作家和诗人是，西非洲塞内加尔的马·迪奥普、桑边·澳斯曼，尼日利亚的哈费兹，安哥拉的安德拉得、克路兹，索马里兰的阿拉查里，乌干达的森诺迦，加纳的都杜，巴西的威·提别利欧，泰国的古拉·柿巴立、?本·万那西，印度的彼莱，缅甸的吴大勇、孟果真勇和吴奎木。
    接见时在座的有，茅盾、周扬、巴金、邵荃麟、刘白羽、严文井、杨朔、郭小川和王亚凡等。
