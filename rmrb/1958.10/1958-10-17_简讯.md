Date: 1958-10-17
Page: 5

    Brief News
    The Soviet Union is preparing to publish a new version of "Diplomatic History"
    Moscow Dispatch: The Soviet State Political Books Publishing House is preparing to publish a new version of the five-volume "Diplomatic History." The first volume will be published in early 1959.
    Czechoslovakia and Afghanistan sign trade agreement
    Kabul Dispatch: The Republic of Czechoslovakia and Afghanistan signed a trade agreement and a protocol on exchange of goods and payments in Kabul.
    Syrian Regional Authorities Announce Confiscation of an American Company's Property
    Damascus Dispatch: The Secretary-General of the Ministry of Public Works of the Syrian Region of the United Arab Republic, Gazzar, announced on the 14th that all properties, funds, and equipment of the American Manhol Mining Company have been confiscated. Gazzar made this announcement after a meeting with Economic Minister Kalas, during which they discussed the issues arising from the cancellation of Manhol Company's exploration license and the freezing of its funds.
    Syngman Rhee's Lackeys Commit Numerous Crimes
    Seoul Dispatch: According to figures released by the Syngman Rhee authorities, as cited by the South Korean Contract News Agency, in the first half of this year alone, Syngman Rhee's police, wardens, soldiers, and tax collectors committed 1,960 crimes. This does not include cases of crimes committed by senior officials of the Syngman Rhee government.

### 简讯

1958-10-17
第5版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    苏联准备出版新版本的“外交史”
    莫斯科讯：苏联国家政治书籍出版局正在准备出版新版本的五卷集“外交史”。第一卷将在1959年初问世。
    捷阿签订贸易协定
    喀布尔讯：捷克斯洛伐克共和国和阿富汗两国在喀布尔签订了一项贸易协定和关于换货和支付的议定书。
    叙利亚地区当局宣布没收一美国人公司的财产
    大马士革讯：阿拉伯联合共和国叙利亚地区公共工程部秘书长贾扎尔14日宣布，美国人的曼霍尔开采公司所有的一切财产、基金和设备都已经被没收。这是贾扎尔在同经济部长卡拉斯举行会议之后宣布的。在这次会议上，他们讨论了由于取消给予曼霍尔公司的勘察执照和冻结它的基金而引起的问题。
    李承晚爪牙罪恶累累
    汉城消息：据南朝鲜的合同通讯社所援引的李承晚当局公布的数字，今年上半年，李承晚的警察、典狱长、军人和税吏们所犯的罪行有一千九百六十起。这还不包括李承晚政府高级官员犯罪的案件。
