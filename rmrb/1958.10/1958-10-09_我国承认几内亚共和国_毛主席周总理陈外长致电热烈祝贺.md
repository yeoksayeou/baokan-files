Title: Our Country Recognizes the Republic of Guinea; Chairman Mao, Premier Zhou, and Foreign Minister Chen Send Cables of Warm Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1958-10-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 8th—Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram to President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea, congratulating him on the birth of the Republic of Guinea. The telegram reads as follows:
    Guinea, Conakry
    To His Excellency President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea:

    I received your letter of October 5th. On behalf of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, and in my personal name, I warmly congratulate the birth of the Republic of Guinea and congratulate the Guinean people on the great victory they have achieved in the struggle for national independence. I hope that our government's recognition of your country will lead to further development of friendly relations between the two peoples. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness.

    Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong
    October 7, 1958, Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, October 8th—Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea, congratulating him on the birth of the Republic of Guinea. The telegram reads as follows:
    Guinea, Conakry
    To His Excellency President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea:

    It is with great pleasure that I learned of the birth of the Republic of Guinea. I would like to extend my heartfelt congratulations to Your Excellency, and through Your Excellency to the Government and people of the Republic of Guinea, on behalf of the Government and people of the People's Republic of China. The independence achieved by the Guinean people is another important symbol of the African national independence movement moving towards a new upsurge. I wish Your Excellency and the Guinean people new achievements in the cause of safeguarding national independence.

    Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai
    October 7, 1958, Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, October 8th—Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi sent a telegram to President and Foreign Minister Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea, informing him that the People's Republic of China had decided to recognize the Republic of Guinea. The telegram reads as follows:
    Guinea, Conakry
    To His Excellency President and Foreign Minister Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea:

    It is with great pleasure that I learned of the birth of the Republic of Guinea. On behalf of the Government and people of the People's Republic of China, I extend my sincere congratulations to the Guinean people.

    I am honored to notify Your Excellency that the Government of the People's Republic of China has decided to recognize the Republic of Guinea and hopes to conduct discussions with your government on the establishment of diplomatic relations and the mutual dispatch of diplomatic representatives between the two countries. I firmly believe that the friendly relations between China and Guinea and their peoples will further develop.
    Vice Premier and Foreign Minister of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Chen Yi
    October 7, 1958, Beijing

### 我国承认几内亚共和国  毛主席周总理陈外长致电热烈祝贺

1958-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我国承认几内亚共和国
    毛主席周总理陈外长致电热烈祝贺
    新华社8日讯　毛泽东主席致电几内亚共和国总理塞古·杜尔，祝贺几内亚共和国诞生。电文如下：几内亚　科纳克里几内亚共和国总理塞古·杜尔先生阁下：
    10月5日收到你的来信。我代表中华人民共和国和中国人民，并且以我个人的名义，热烈祝贺几内亚共和国的诞生，祝贺几内亚人民在争取民族独立的斗争中所获得的伟大胜利。我希望，我国政府对贵国的承认将导致两国人民之间的友好关系进一步的发展。祝贵国繁荣，人民幸福。
    中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
              1958年10月7日于北京
    新华社8日讯　周恩来总理致电几内亚共和国总理塞古·杜尔，祝贺几内亚共和国诞生。电文如下：几内亚　科纳克里几内亚共和国总理塞古·杜尔先生阁下：
    我以非常愉快的心情获悉几内亚共和国诞生，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向阁下，并通过阁下向几内亚共和国政府和人民致以衷心的祝贺。几内亚人民取得独立是非洲民族独立运动走向新高潮的又一重要标志。祝阁下和几内亚人民在维护民族独立的事业中取得新的成就。
  中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                1958年10月7日于北京
    新华社8日讯　陈毅副总理兼外交部长致电几内亚共和国总理兼外交部长塞古·杜尔，通知他中华人民共和国已经决定承认几内亚共和国。电文如下：几内亚　科纳克里几内亚共和国总理兼外交部长塞古·杜尔先生阁下：
    欣悉几内亚共和国诞生，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向几内亚人民致以诚挚的祝贺。
    我荣幸地通知阁下，中华人民共和国政府已经决定承认几内亚共和国，并且希望就两国建立外交关系和互派外交代表事同贵国政府进行商谈。我深信，中几两国和人民之间的友好关系将进一步发展。中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈　毅1958年10月7日于北京
