Title: Moroccan Prime Minister Receives Lei Renmin
Author:
Date: 1958-10-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, 11th -- Ahmed Balafrej, Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Morocco, received Lei Renmin, head of the Chinese trade delegation, and Ke Hua, deputy head, who are currently visiting here, on the 10th. During the meeting, they discussed the recent trade negotiations between China and Morocco and other issues related to promoting friendly relations between the two countries.

### 摩洛哥首相接见雷任民

1958-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥首相接见雷任民
    新华社拉巴特11日电　摩洛哥王国首相兼外交大臣艾哈迈德·贝拉弗里杰10日接见了正在这里访问的中国贸易代表团团长雷任民和副团长柯华。在接见中他们谈到了最近中国和摩洛哥的贸易谈判以及其他一些有关促进两国之间友好关系的问题。
