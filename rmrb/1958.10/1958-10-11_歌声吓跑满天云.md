Title: Song Scares Away the Clouds
Author: Jian Zhaozhao
Date: 1958-10-11
Page: 8

The electricity flashes, the thunder rolls, the rain pours, the wind blows... It's as if the heavens want to scare the Wuhan Iron and Steel Corporation workers. Old Heavens, you can forget about that! Open your eyes and take a look at us!
　　The arc lights overwhelm the lightning, the pneumatic riveting guns overwhelm the thunder. The wind can only cool us down, the rain can only reduce the temperature.
　　Trucks are still speeding by, cranes are still busy. A burst of chants, a burst of song, the song scares away the clouds!
　　We are the builders of Wuhan Iron and Steel! Nothing in the world can overwhelm us!

### 歌声吓跑满天云
简志昭
1958-10-11
第8版()
专栏：

    歌声吓跑满天云
    武汉钢铁公司工人　简志昭电在闪，雷在打，雨在下，风在刮……好像要把武钢工人吓，老天爷你算了吧，你睁开眼睛看看咱！弧光压住了闪电，风铆枪压过了雷鸣，风只能给我们解暑，雨只能给我们降温。汽车一样在飞驰，吊车依然忙不停。一阵号子一阵歌，歌声吓跑了满天云！我们是武钢建设者，世界上没有什么压倒我们！
