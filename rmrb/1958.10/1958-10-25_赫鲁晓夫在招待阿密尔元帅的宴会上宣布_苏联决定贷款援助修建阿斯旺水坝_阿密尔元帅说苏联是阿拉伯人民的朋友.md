Title: Khrushchev Announces at Banquet Honoring Marshal Amer: Soviet Union Decides to Provide Loan to Aid Construction of Aswan Dam; Marshal Amer Says Soviet Union is a Friend of the Arab People
Author:
Date: 1958-10-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 24th: TASS reports that Soviet Premier Nikita Khrushchev announced at a banquet honoring Marshal Abdel Hakim Amer, Vice President and Minister of Defense of the United Arab Republic (UAR), on the 23rd that the Soviet government has decided to provide the UAR with a loan of 400 million rubles to help the UAR build the Aswan Dam.

Khrushchev said that the Soviet government had studied the UAR government's request, submitted through Marshal Amer, for assistance in building the first stage of the Aswan Dam. Therefore, the Soviet Union is providing this loan so that the UAR can purchase machinery, equipment, and other materials from the Soviet Union.

The Soviet side present at the reception held last night in the Grand Kremlin Palace included: Mikoyan, Mukhitdinov, Suslov, Furtseva, Shvernik, Pospelov, Korotchenko, Kirilenko, Kosygin, and others.

Khrushchev also said at the banquet that the visit of the Arab friends to the Soviet Union is a new contribution to the development of cooperation between the Soviet Union and the UAR. It helps to strengthen the friendship between the two countries and promote peace in the Middle East and the world.

Khrushchev emphasized that the meeting with the Vice President of the UAR was conducted in a sincere and friendly atmosphere. The two countries reached a consensus on major international issues of decisive significance for maintaining and strengthening universal peace.

Khrushchev said, "We agree that the main task of the forces of peace in the Middle East now is to strive for the early withdrawal of US and British troops from Lebanon and Jordan." He wholeheartedly congratulated the friendly people of the UAR on their new achievements in the struggle to strengthen peace, national independence, and economic prosperity, and in the struggle to unite Arab countries.

In his reply, Amer expressed his gratitude to the Soviet leaders for their warm hospitality during his visit to the Soviet Union. He pointed out that the friendly relations between the UAR and the Soviet Union are constantly strengthening and developing. He also pointed out that the exchange of views with the Soviet leaders was conducted in an atmosphere of sincerity and mutual understanding.

Amer also said that the people of the UAR will be very happy to hear the news that the Soviet Union is helping to build the Aswan Dam. He said that the realization of this grand project will further strengthen the UAR's national economy.

Amer then spoke of the Arab people's struggle for freedom and independence. He pointed out that his country attaches great importance to the lofty position taken by the Soviet Union towards the UAR.

He also said, "We will fight for peace together with the Soviet Union. The Soviet Union has proven that it is a friend of all Arab people."

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 24th: TASS reports that Soviet Minister of Defense, Marshal of the Soviet Union Rodion Malinovsky, hosted a banquet on the 23rd in honor of Marshal Abdel Hakim Amer, Vice President and Minister of Defense of the United Arab Republic.

The Soviet side present at the banquet included Soviet Deputy Premiers Anastas Mikoyan and Alexei Kosygin, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Mikhail Georgadze, Marshal of the Soviet Union Vasily Sokolovsky, and others.

In his speech at the banquet, Amer said, "Please allow me to thank you for the warm words you have spoken about the people of the United Arab Republic and its armed forces. We believe that these words come from the heart. We have the same feelings for the Soviet people."

Amer also said that the friendship between the people of the United Arab Republic and the Soviet people is a consolidated friendship. The friendship that exists between the armed forces of the two countries will continue to strengthen.

Amer also emphasized that the Soviet government's position on the economic development of the United Arab Republic has always been lofty.

### 赫鲁晓夫在招待阿密尔元帅的宴会上宣布  苏联决定贷款援助修建阿斯旺水坝  阿密尔元帅说苏联是阿拉伯人民的朋友

1958-10-25
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫在招待阿密尔元帅的宴会上宣布
    苏联决定贷款援助修建阿斯旺水坝
    阿密尔元帅说苏联是阿拉伯人民的朋友
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫在23日招待阿拉伯联合共和国副总统兼国防部长阿密尔元帅的宴会上宣布，苏联政府决定向阿联提供四亿卢布的贷款，帮助阿联修建阿斯旺水坝。
    赫鲁晓夫说，苏联政府研究了阿联政府通过阿密尔元帅提出来的关于援助阿联修建阿斯旺水坝第一期工程的要求。为此，苏联提供了这一笔贷款，以便阿联从苏联购买机器、设备和其他材料。
    苏联方面出席昨晚在大克里姆林宫举行的招待会的还有：米高扬、穆希金诺夫、苏斯洛夫、福尔采娃、什维尔尼克、波斯别洛夫、科罗特钦科、基里连科、柯西金等。
    赫鲁晓夫在宴会上还说，阿拉伯朋友来苏联的访问是对发展苏联和阿联两国之间的合作方面的新贡献，它有助于加强两国之间的友谊，并促进中近东和世界的和平。
    赫鲁晓夫强调指出，同阿联副总统的会见是在诚恳和友好的气氛中进行的。两国在对维护和加强普遍和平具有决定性意义的重大国际问题上取得了一致的观点。
    赫鲁晓夫说，我们一致认为：现在中近东和平力量的主要任务是争取使美国和英国军队早日撤出黎巴嫩和约旦。他衷心祝贺友好的阿联人民在争取加强和平、民族独立和经济繁荣的斗争中，在争取阿拉伯国家的团结的斗争中取得新的成就。
    阿密尔在致答词中对苏联领导人在他访问苏联期间的殷勤招待，表示感谢。他指出，阿联和苏联之间的友好关系正在不断加强和发展。他还指出，同苏联领导人交换意见是在真诚和互相谅解的气氛中进行的。
    阿密尔还说，阿联人民听到苏联帮助建设阿斯旺水坝的消息，一定会非常高兴。他说，实现这个宏伟的工程将更加加强阿联的国民经济。
    阿密尔接着谈到了阿拉伯人民争取自由和独立的斗争。他指出，他的国家非常重视苏联对阿联所采取的崇高立场。
    他还说，我们将同苏联一道为和平而奋斗。苏联证明了它是所有阿拉伯人民的朋友。
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联国防部长、苏联元帅马利诺夫斯基23日设宴招待阿拉伯联合共和国副总统兼国防部长阿密尔元帅。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有苏联部长会议副主席米高扬和柯西金、苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书格奥尔加泽、苏联元帅索科洛夫斯基等。
    阿密尔在宴会上致词说，请允许我感谢你们对阿拉伯联合共和国人民和它的武装部队所说的热情的好话。我们相信这些话都是发自内心的。我们对苏联人民也怀有同样的感情。
    阿密尔还说，阿拉伯联合共和国人民同苏联人民之间的友谊是巩固的友谊。两国武装部队之间存在的友谊将会不断加强。
    阿密尔还强调说，苏联政府对待阿拉伯联合共和国经济发展的立场一向是崇高的。
