Title: Brotherly Countries Support China's Great Leap Forward in Production (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1958-10-28
Page: 5

Brotherly Countries Support China's Great Leap Forward in Production
    Top Left: The Lenin Factory in Leningrad, USSR, is helping China rush to produce smelting equipment. The picture shows workers in the factory assembling a large 1,200-cubic-meter blower being manufactured for China. Photo by B. Utkin, Soviet Union (Xinhua News Agency)
    Top Right: Employees of the drilling machine factory in Budapest, Hungary, completed the order for China ahead of schedule. The picture shows the factory manager and inspector inspecting a batch of new vertical drilling machines manufactured for China. Hungarian News Agency (Xinhua News Agency)
    Bottom Left: Workers at an agricultural machinery factory in the Bratislava region of Czechoslovakia completed China's order fourteen days ahead of schedule. The picture shows a large number of farming tools about to be shipped to China.
    Czechoslovak News Agency (Xinhua News Agency)

### 兄弟国家支援我国生产大跃进（图片）

1958-10-28
第5版()
专栏：

    兄弟国家支援我国生产大跃进
    左上图　苏联列宁格勒的列宁工厂帮助我国赶制冶炼设备。图为该厂的工人正在装配一架为我国制造的一千二百立方公尺的大型鼓风机。  苏联勃·乌特金摄（新华社发）
    右上图　匈牙利布达佩斯的钻床工厂的职工们提前完成了为我国制造的订货。图为该厂的经理和检验员正在检验一批为我国制造的新型的立式钻床。    匈牙利通讯社稿（新华社发）
    左下图　捷克斯洛伐克布拉迪斯拉发地区的农业机械工厂的工人提前十四天完成了我国的订货。图为即将运来我国的大批耕作的农具。
    捷克斯洛伐克通讯社稿（新华社发）
