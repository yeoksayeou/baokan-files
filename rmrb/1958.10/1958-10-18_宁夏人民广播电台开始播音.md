Title: Ningxia People's Broadcasting Station Begins Broadcasting
Author:
Date: 1958-10-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Yinchuan, October 16th: The Ningxia People's Broadcasting Station officially began broadcasting on the 15th. The Ningxia People's Broadcasting Station broadcasts three times daily, featuring the day's news, rebroadcasts of news from the Central People's Broadcasting Station, and programs about the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, including news, science, health, and arts and literature.

### 宁夏人民广播电台开始播音

1958-10-18
第6版()
专栏：

    宁夏人民广播电台开始播音
    新华社银川16日电　宁夏人民广播电台15日起正式广播。宁夏人民广播电台每日播音三次，除广播当天新闻和转播中央人民广播电台新闻外，还有宁夏回族自治区介绍、新闻、科学、卫生和文艺等节目。
