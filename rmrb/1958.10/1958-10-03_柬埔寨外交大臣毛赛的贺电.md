Title: Telegram of Greetings from Cambodian Foreign Minister Mao Sey
Author: Mao Sai
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

Telegram of Greetings from Cambodian Foreign Minister Mao Sey
To His Excellency Marshal Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs:

    On the occasion of your country's National Day, I am delighted to extend, in the name of the Government of the Kingdom of Cambodia and in my own name, my best wishes to Your Excellency, and to wish the Chinese Government and people happiness, prosperity, and success.
                                    Mao Sey
                                    Cambodian Foreign Minister

### 柬埔寨外交大臣毛赛的贺电
毛赛
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨外交大臣毛赛的贺电外交部长陈毅元帅阁下：
    在贵国国庆的时候，我很高兴地以柬埔寨王国政府和我个人的名义向阁下致以最良好的愿望，祝中国政府和人民幸福、繁荣和成功。
                                    柬埔寨外交大臣　毛赛
