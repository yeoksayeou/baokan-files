Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Canadian Reporter
Author:
Date: 1958-10-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received Clark, a reporter from the Montreal Star of Canada, at noon today.

Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was present at the meeting.

### 陈毅副总理接见加记者

1958-10-31
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见加记者
    新华社30日讯　陈毅副总理今天中午接见了加拿大蒙特里尔星报记者克拉克。
    陪同接见的有外交部新闻司副司长徐晃。
