Title: Encouraging the Chiang Kai-shek Clique to Continue the Civil War: The United States Gives the Chiang Army Five More Warships
Author:
Date: 1958-10-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 21st dispatch from Taipei: American aggressors have once again handed over five naval vessels to the Chiang Kai-shek clique in order to support and encourage the Chiang Kai-shek army to continue fighting the civil war.

　　Three warships handed over by the United States to the Chiang Kai-shek clique sailed into the Chiang army's naval base in southern Taiwan this afternoon. These three warships have been named "Chung Chiang," "Chung Fu," and "Chung Cheng" by the Chiang army naval authorities.

　　At the same time, the United States handed over two tank landing ships to representatives of the Chiang army navy in Seattle, USA today in order to "strengthen their strength in the Kinmen area." These two tank landing ships have been named "Chung Chih" and "Chung Ming" by the Chiang army naval authorities.

　　The United States and Chiang Kai-shek's navy are conducting "combined mine-laying and mine-sweeping exercises" in the southern waters of Taiwan. During the US-Chiang naval exercises from the 20th to the 25th, merchant ships and fishing boats are prohibited from passing through a large area of water near Tainan Hengchun.

### 鼓励蒋介石集团继续打内战  美国又给蒋军五艘军舰

1958-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

    鼓励蒋介石集团继续打内战
    美国又给蒋军五艘军舰
    新华社21日讯　台北消息：美国侵略者又把五艘海军舰艇交给蒋介石集团，以便支持和鼓励蒋介石军队继续打内战。
    美国交给蒋介石集团的三艘军舰在今天下午驶抵台湾南部蒋军海军基地。这三艘军舰由蒋军海军当局定名为“中强号”、“中富号”和“中程号”。
    同时，美国今天在美国西雅图把两艘坦克登陆舰交给蒋军海军的代表，以便他们“加强在金门地区”的实力。这两艘坦克登陆舰由蒋军海军当局定名为“中治号”和“中明号”。
    美国和蒋介石海军正在台湾南部海面举行“布雷扫雷混合演习”。在20日到25日美蒋海军演习期间，禁止商船和渔船在台南恒春附近的广阔水域通行。
