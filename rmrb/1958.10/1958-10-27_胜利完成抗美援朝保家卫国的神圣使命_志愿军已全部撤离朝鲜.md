Title: Victoriously Completing the Sacred Mission of Resisting America, Aiding Korea, and Defending the Nation: The Chinese People's Volunteers Have Completely Withdrawn from Korea
Author:
Date: 1958-10-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Andong, 26th. Chinese People's Volunteer Army Headquarters Withdrawal Communiqué

The third batch of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army: the Volunteer Army Headquarters, three divisions, and logistical support units, totaling 70,000 people, returned to the motherland from September 25th to October 26th. As of this point, the entire Chinese People's Volunteer Army has withdrawn from Korea.

The representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army remaining within the Korean Military Armistice Commission will, together with the representatives of the Korean People's Army, continue to carry out the task of supervising the implementation of the Korean Armistice Agreement.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th. Korean Central News Agency, Kaesong Dispatch: Major General Kang Sang-ho, Chief Commissioner of the Korean-Chinese side of the Military Armistice Commission, wrote to the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission on the 26th regarding the complete withdrawal of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army from Korea. The full text of the letter is as follows:

To the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission:

I have the honor to inform your commission that the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, in accordance with the decision announced in the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Headquarters' statement of February 20, 1958, regarding the complete withdrawal from Korea in batches before the end of 1958, has completely withdrawn from Korea in three batches during the period from March 15 to October 26, 1958.

The representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army remaining in the Military Armistice Commission will continue, together with the representatives of the Korean People's Army, to carry out the task of supervising the implementation of the Armistice Agreement.

Kang Sang-ho
Major General, Korean People's Army
Chief Commissioner, Korean People's Army and Chinese People's Volunteer Army side
Military Armistice Commission
October 26, 1958

### 胜利完成抗美援朝保家卫国的神圣使命  志愿军已全部撤离朝鲜

1958-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    胜利完成抗美援朝保家卫国的神圣使命
    志愿军已全部撤离朝鲜
    留在朝鲜军事停战委员会内的志愿军代表，仍将同朝鲜人民军代表一道，继续执行监督朝鲜停战协定实施的任务
    新华社安东26日电　中国人民志愿军总部撤军公报
    中国人民志愿军第三批部队：志愿军总部、三个师和后勤保障部队共七万人，于9月25日至10月26日返回祖国。至此，中国人民志愿军已全部撤离朝鲜。
    留在朝鲜军事停战委员会内的中国人民志愿军代表，仍将同朝鲜人民军代表一道，继续执行监督朝鲜停战协定实施的任务。
    新华社平壤26日电　朝鲜中央通讯社开城讯：军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员姜尚昊少将，26日就中国人民志愿军全部撤出朝鲜事，写信给中立国监察委员会。信的全文如下：中立国监察委员会：
    我荣幸地通知贵委员会，中国人民志愿军根据中国人民志愿军总部1958年2月20日关于在1958年年底以前分批全部撤出朝鲜的声明中宣布的决定，已经在1958年3月15日到10月26日的期间内，分三批全部撤出了朝鲜。
    留在军事停战委员会的中国人民志愿军代表仍旧同朝鲜人民军代表一起，继续执行监督执行停战协定的任务。
    军事停战委员会朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军方面首席委员、朝鲜人民军少将　姜尚昊
              1958年10月26日
