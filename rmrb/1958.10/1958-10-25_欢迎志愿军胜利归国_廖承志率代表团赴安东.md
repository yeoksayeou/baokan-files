Title: Welcoming the Victorious Return of the Chinese People's Volunteers: Liao Chengzhi Leads Delegation to Andong
Author: 
Date: 1958-10-25
Page: 2

    Welcoming the Victorious Return of the Chinese People's Volunteers
    Liao Chengzhi Leads Delegation to Andong
    Xinhua News Agency, 24th – The Chinese People's Delegation to Welcome the Return of the Chinese People's Volunteers, led by Liao Chengzhi, Head of the Delegation and Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea, left Beijing by special train at noon today to welcome the last batch of heroic Chinese People's Volunteers returning to the motherland in Andong.
    Those who came to the station today to see off the delegation included Wang Weizhou, Zhang Zhizhong, Zhang Su, Qi Yanming, Liu Zhijian, Shao Lizi, Zhang Xiruo, Shi Liang, Wang Ziyi, Xia Yan, Hu Yuzhi, Kang Yonghe, Liu Xiyuan, Wu Han, Zhu Yunshan, and others.
    Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yongho, and embassy officials also made a special trip to the station to see off the delegation.
    The First Troupe of the China Peking Opera Theatre also traveled to Andong with the delegation today. Famous actors such as Li Shaochun, Yuan Shihai, and Du Jinfang will give brilliant performances in Andong for the "most beloved people."

### 欢迎志愿军胜利归国  廖承志率代表团赴安东

1958-10-25
第2版()
专栏：

    欢迎志愿军胜利归国
    廖承志率代表团赴安东
    新华社24日讯　中国人民欢迎志愿军归国代表团由团长、中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席廖承志率领，在今天中午乘专车离开北京，前往安东欢迎英雄的中国人民志愿军最后一批部队归返祖国。
    今天到车站为代表团送行的，有王维舟、张治中、张苏、齐燕铭、刘志坚、邵力子、张奚若、史良、王子宜、夏衍、胡愈之、康永和、刘西元、吴晗、朱蕴山等。
    朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐和大使馆官员也特地到达车站为代表团送行。
    中国京剧院第一团今天随同代表团前往安东。李少春、袁世海、杜近芳等著名演员将在安东为“最可爱的人”作精采演出。
