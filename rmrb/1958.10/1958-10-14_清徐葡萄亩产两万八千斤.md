Title: Qingxu Grapes Yield 28,000 Jin Per Mu
Author: Yang Deming, Wu Aiquan
Date: 1958-10-14
Page: 3

    Qingxu Grapes Yield 28,000 Jin Per Mu

    The Red Flag People's Commune in Qingxu County, Shanxi Province, has launched a great "satellite" for grape production: The "Chicken Heart" and "Longan" grapes of the Liu Jiayuan Youth Bumper Harvest Team yielded an average of 28,269.5 jin per mu on an area of 1.01 mu. The experimental plot of 1.87 mu cultivated by the Renyi Village commune cadres yielded an average of 26,115.5 jin per mu; the experimental plot of 1.47 mu cultivated by the village youth production team yielded an average of 23,484 jin per mu. The highest historical yield in the area was 6,000 jin per mu. In addition, there are also high-yielding single "Grape Kings". There are two "Grape Kings" in Renyi Village, one yielding 2,215.6 jin and the other yielding 2,138 jin. There are also two "Grape Kings" in Xiliangquan Village, one yielding 2,100 jin and the other yielding 1,970 jin. These "Grape Kings" are all over a century old.
      (Yang Deming, Wu Aiquan)

### 清徐葡萄亩产两万八千斤
杨德明  武爱全
1958-10-14
第3版()
专栏：

    清徐葡萄亩产两万八千斤
    山西省清徐县红旗人民公社连放葡萄大“卫星”：刘家园青年丰产队的一亩零一厘鸡心与龙眼葡萄，平均亩产二万八千二百六十九斤五两。仁义村社干部的一亩八分七厘试验田，平均亩产二万六千一百一十五斤五两；该村青年生产队的一亩四分七厘试验田，平均亩产二万三千四百八十四斤。而当地历史上最高亩产为六千斤。此外，还有单株高产的“葡萄王”。仁义村有两株葡萄王，一株实产二千二百一十五斤六两，另一株实产二千一百三十八斤。西梁泉村也有两株葡萄王，一株实产二千一百斤，另一株实产一千九百七十斤。这些“葡萄王”都是百年以上的“高龄”。
      （杨德明、武爱全）
