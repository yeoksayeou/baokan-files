Title: Vice Chairman Song Holds Tea Party to Receive Bulgarian Guests
Author:
Date: 1958-10-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 20th - Song Qingling, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, held a tea party in Shanghai this afternoon to receive the Bulgarian National Assembly delegation headed by Chervenkov. The guests and hosts sipped tea and engaged in cordial and friendly conversations, exchanging gifts before parting.

Liu Changsheng, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Lian Guan, Deputy Secretary-General, and Xu Jianguo, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, who accompanied the distinguished guests on their visit to Shanghai, were also present.

### 宋副委员长举行茶会招待保加利亚贵宾

1958-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    宋副委员长举行茶会招待保加利亚贵宾
    新华社上海20日电　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄，今天下午在上海举行茶会招待以契尔文科夫为首的保加利亚国民议会代表团。宾主一边品茗，一边亲切友好地交谈，在临别时还相互赠送了礼品。
    陪同贵宾来上海访问的全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员刘长胜，副秘书长连贯和中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长许建国等都出席作陪。
