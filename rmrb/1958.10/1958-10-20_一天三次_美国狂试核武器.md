Title: Three Times a Day: US Frantically Tests Nuclear Weapons
Author:
Date: 1958-10-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 19th – The United States is now stepping up its nuclear weapons testing at an unprecedented rate. Yesterday, another nuclear explosion was conducted at the Nevada Test Site. It is reported that prior to this, from the 15th to the morning of the 16th, the United States conducted three nuclear weapons tests in less than twenty-four hours.

### 一天三次  美国狂试核武器

1958-10-20
第5版()
专栏：

    一天三次
    美国狂试核武器
    新华社19日讯　华盛顿消息：美国现在正以空前的速度加紧进行核武器试验。昨天在内华达州原子试验基地又进行了一次核爆炸。据报道，在此以前，从15日到16日早上，不到二十四小时的时间里美国就进行了三次核武器试验。
