Title: Ode to the Three Armed Forces
Author: Tian Jian
Date: 1958-10-21
Page: 8

    Ode to the Three Armed Forces
  　Tian Jian
    First Ode to the Artillery: Thousands of cannons face Jinmen; Ten thousand miles of thunder on the East Coast, Thunder rumbles, fiercely striking the enemy; Using both shells to teach a lesson, And proclamations to summon; Stopping and starting the fight as we please, The Americans have no right to interfere.

    Second Ode to the Navy: Torpedo boats penetrate deep into the sea; With blue sky above and the sea below, Firmly guarding the motherland's gate; We shall make the sea listen to our commands, Defending peace in the East; Brave warriors are the masters of the sea, The sea is in the hearts of the brave warriors.

    Third Ode to the Air Force: Divine eagles fly across the vast sky; Divine eagles are like lightning, Roaring and commanding the clouds; A single divine eagle soars into the sky, Bravely charging into the enemy's aircraft group; Golden light flashes in the air, This is the red star of the motherland.

### 赞三军
田间
1958-10-21
第8版()
专栏：

    赞三军
  　田间
    一第一赞来赞炮兵，千门大炮对金门；东海岸上万里雷声，雷声隆隆痛击敌人；既用炮弹来教训，又用文告来召唤；停停打打随我便，美国人无权过问。
    二第二赞来赞海军，鱼雷艇深入海心；头顶青天脚踏海，坚守祖国的大门；要叫大海听号令，保卫东方的和平；勇士是海上的主人，大海是勇士的心。
    三第三赞来赞空军，万里长空飞神鹰；神鹰好像是闪电，?喝天上的风云；一只神鹰飞上天，勇猛冲入敌机群；金光闪闪在空中，这是祖国的红星。
