Title: US Steel Production Down 35% in First Eight Months of This Year Compared to Same Period Last Year
Author:
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd (New York dispatch): According to a report by the American Iron and Steel Institute, in the first eight months of this year, the total steel production in the United States was 51,457,892 tons, a decrease of nearly 35% compared to the same period last year. In the first seven months of this year, the output of finished steel products decreased by more than 35% compared to the same period last year, with steel plates decreasing by 47.7%.
    The reduction in steel production in the United States is due to the impact of the economic crisis. For example, due to the sluggish automobile industry, the amount of steel purchased by automobile companies has been greatly reduced.

### 今年头八个月和去年同期相比  美钢产量下降35％

1958-10-03
第5版()
专栏：

    今年头八个月和去年同期相比
    美钢产量下降35％
    新华社2日讯　纽约消息：据美国钢铁研究所报道，在今年头八个月里，美国钢的生产量总计五千一百四十五万七千八百九十二吨，比去年同一时期减少了将近35％。在今年头七个月，出厂的钢制成品比去年同一时期减少了35％以上，其中钢板减少了47.7％。
    美国钢的生产的减少，是由于经济危机的影响。例如，由于汽车工业不景气，汽车公司购买的钢铁量大大减少。
