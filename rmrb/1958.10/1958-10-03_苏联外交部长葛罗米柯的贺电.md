Title: Telegram of Greetings from Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko
Author: A. Gromyko
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

To Comrade Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:

    Dear Comrade Chen Yi, on the occasion of the ninth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I extend to you my warm congratulations. I firmly believe that the fraternal friendship and comprehensive cooperation between the peoples of the Soviet Union and China, as pillars of socialism and world peace, will be increasingly consolidated and developed. I wish the Chinese people new achievements in the struggle to continue to strengthen the country's strength and consolidate world peace.
                                A. Gromyko
                                Soviet Foreign Minister

### 苏联外交部长葛罗米柯的贺电
安·葛罗米柯
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联外交部长葛罗米柯的贺电中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅同志：
    当中华人民共和国成立九周年的时候，亲爱的陈毅同志，我向你表示热烈的祝贺。我坚信，作为社会主义和世界和平的支柱的苏中两国人民的兄弟友谊和全面合作将会日益巩固和发展。祝中国人民在继续增强国家的实力和巩固世界和平的斗争中取得新的成就。
                                苏联外交部长　安·葛罗米柯
