Title: Afghan and UAR Ambassadors Visit Zhang Xiruo to Offer Condolences for the Deaths of Chinese Cultural Delegation Members
Author:
Date: 1958-10-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th—The Ambassador of the Kingdom of Afghanistan to China, Samad, and the Ambassador of the United Arab Republic to China, Ragab, paid a visit to Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, this morning. On behalf of the governments of the Kingdom of Afghanistan and the United Arab Republic, they expressed their condolences and sympathies to the Chinese government and the families of the deceased members of the Chinese cultural delegation to Afghanistan and the United Arab Republic, led by Zheng Zhenduo, who died in a plane crash.

### 阿富汗和阿联大使拜会张奚若  为我文化代表团遇难表示吊唁

1958-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    阿富汗和阿联大使拜会张奚若
    为我文化代表团遇难表示吊唁
    新华社20日讯　阿富汗王国驻华大使萨马德和阿拉伯联合共和国驻华大使拉加卜今日上午拜会对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若，分别代表阿富汗王国政府和阿拉伯联合共和国政府为以郑振铎为首的我访阿富汗、阿拉伯联合共和国文化代表团全体成员因飞机失事遇难一事，向我国政府及遇难同志家属表示吊唁和慰问。
