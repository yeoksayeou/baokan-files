Title: Indian Cooperation Minister Deshmukh Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-10-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th – Mr. Deshmukh, the Indian Cooperation Minister, arrived in Beijing by plane today from Guangzhou at the invitation of the Ministry of Agriculture for a visit to our country. Deputy Minister of Agriculture Cai Ziwei, Deputy Minister of Forestry Zhang Kexia, and Deputy Director of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives Guo Xianrui, and others were at the airport to welcome him. The Indian Ambassador to China, Parthasarathi, was also at the airport to welcome him.

### 印度合作部长德希穆克抵京

1958-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    印度合作部长德希穆克抵京
    新华社20日讯　应农业部邀请来我国访问的印度合作部长德希穆克先生于今日由广州乘飞机抵京。到机场欢迎的有农业部副部长蔡子伟，林业部副部长张克侠，中华全国供销合作总社副主任郭献瑞等人。印度驻我国大使帕塔萨拉蒂亦到机场欢迎。
