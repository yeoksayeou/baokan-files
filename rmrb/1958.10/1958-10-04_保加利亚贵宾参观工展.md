Title: Bulgarian Guests Visit Industrial Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1958-10-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency News:

    The Bulgarian National Assembly delegation headed by Comrade Chervenkov and the Sofia City People's Council delegation headed by Comrade Kostov visited the "National Industrial and Transportation Exhibition" in Beijing on the morning of the 3rd.

    After the visit, Comrade Chervenkov wrote an inscription for the exhibition, saying: After visiting this industrial and transportation exhibition, the Bulgarian National Assembly delegation is very happy for the great achievements of the great Chinese people. We salute you and express our fraternal friendship and love to you.

    Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Committee Member Liu Changsheng, Deputy Secretary-General Lian Guan, and Vice Mayor of Beijing Feng Jiping and others accompanied the distinguished guests on the visit.

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd: The entire Bulgarian National Assembly delegation and the Sofia City People's Council delegation, accompanied by Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Lian Guan, Deputy Secretary-General, visited the Palace Museum on the morning of the 2nd. (Photo attached)

### 保加利亚贵宾参观工展

1958-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚贵宾参观工展
    据新华社讯　以契尔文科夫同志为首的保加利亚国民议会代表团和以科斯托夫同志为首的索非亚市人民会议代表团，3日上午在北京参观了“全国工业交通展览会”。
    参观结束后，契尔文科夫同志亲笔为展览会题词说：保加利亚国民议会代表团参观了这个工业交通展览会以后，为伟大的中国人民的巨大成就而感到非常高兴，我们向你们致意，向你们表示兄弟般的友谊和热爱。
    陪同贵宾们前往参观的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深，委员刘长胜，副秘书长连贯和北京市副市长冯基平等人。
    新华社2日讯　保加利亚国民议会代表团和索非亚市人民会议代表团的全体人员，2日上午在全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长陈叔通、副秘书长连贯的陪同下参观了故宫博物馆。（附图片）
