Title: Telegram of Congratulations from Nam Il, Vice Premier of the Cabinet and Minister of Foreign Affairs of North Korea
Author:
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

Telegram of Congratulations from Nam Il, Vice Premier of the Cabinet and Minister of Foreign Affairs of North Korea, to Comrade Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:

	On the occasion of the ninth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I would like to extend my heartfelt and warm congratulations to you, esteemed Comrade.
	I wish you even greater achievements in the noble cause of the prosperity and development of the People's Republic of China and for peace in the Far East and the world.

		Nam Il, Vice Premier of the Cabinet and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea

### 朝鲜内阁副首相兼外务相南日的贺电
南日
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

      朝鲜内阁副首相兼外务相南日的贺电中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅同志：
  值此中华人民共和国成立九周年，我谨向尊敬的您衷心致以热烈的祝贺。
    祝您在为中华人民共和国的繁荣发展、为远东和世界和平的崇高事业中获得更大的成就。
            朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相兼外务相　南日
