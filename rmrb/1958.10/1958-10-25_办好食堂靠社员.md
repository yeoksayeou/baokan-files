Title: Running the Canteen Well Depends on the Commune Members
Author: Li Yuxi
Date: 1958-10-25
Page: 4

Running the Public Canteen relies on everyone. To do it well, we still rely on the commune members. If there are problems, opinions should be voiced, helping to improve things is only natural. If the canteen is consolidated and the food is good, we'll have a happy life for ten thousand years.
