Title: Swedish Writer Lundkvist Awarded the International Lenin Peace Prize
Author:
Date: 1958-10-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, 28th: According to TASS, the award ceremony for the "International Lenin Prize for Strengthening Peace Among Peoples" was held on the 27th in the Concert Hall here, awarding the prize to Swedish writer Lundkvist. Mr. Skobeltsyn, Chairman of the International Lenin Prize Committee, Committee member Ehrenburg, Mrs. Lundkvist, the Swedish Minister of Education and Culture, members of parliament, celebrities, and cultural figures attended the award ceremony.

### 瑞典作家伦德奎斯特  荣获列宁国际和平奖金

1958-10-30
第5版()
专栏：

    瑞典作家伦德奎斯特
    荣获列宁国际和平奖金
    新华社29日讯　据塔斯社斯德哥尔摩28日讯：27日在此间音乐馆大厅举行了授奖仪式，把“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金授给瑞典作家伦德奎斯特。列宁国际奖金委员会主席斯科贝尔琴、委员会委员爱伦堡、伦德奎斯特夫人、瑞典教育文化部长、议员、社会名流和文化界人士等参加了授奖仪式。
