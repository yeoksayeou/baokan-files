Title: Chinese People's Volunteer Army Completely Returns to the Motherland

Author: 

Date: 1958-10-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Andong, 27th - Officers and soldiers of the headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army left Andong at 1:14 PM today, heading to the interior of the motherland, warmly seen off by more than 2,000 people from all walks of life in Andong. With this, the entire Chinese People's Volunteer Army has returned to various parts of the motherland via Andong.

Beginning on March 16th of this year, the people of Andong City successively welcomed the "most beloved people" returning in three batches and held more than 200 discussion meetings to introduce the situation of the Great Leap Forward in the motherland to the officers and soldiers of the Volunteer Army. They also wrote more than 15,000 letters of appreciation to the officers and soldiers of the Volunteer Army. The returning heroes also presented the people of Andong with thousands of victory souvenirs and gifts of friendship brought back from Korea.

### 志愿军全部返回祖国各地

1958-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军全部返回祖国各地
    新华社安东27日电　中国人民志愿军总部官兵今天下午一时十四分在安东市各界人民两千多人的热情欢送下，离开安东开赴祖国内地。至此，中国人民志愿军已经全部经过安东返回祖国各地。
    从今年3月16日开始，安东市人民先后欢迎了分三批归国的“最可爱的人”，并举行了二百多次座谈会，向志愿军官兵介绍了祖国大跃进的情况，还先后给志愿军官兵写了一万五千多封慰问信。归国英雄们也赠送给安东人民数千件胜利品和从朝鲜带回的友谊礼品。
