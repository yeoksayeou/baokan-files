Title: Chinese People's Delegation to Korea Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-10-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th:
The Chinese People's Delegation, led by Guo Moruo, returned to Beijing by train at 11:20 this morning after visiting the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

Vice Premier Chen Yi and responsible persons from relevant departments welcomed them at the station.

Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yong-ho, also welcomed them at the station. Female comrades from the embassy presented flowers to Guo Moruo and others.

### 中国人民访朝代表团回京

1958-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民访朝代表团回京
    新华社27日讯　由郭沫若率领的中国人民代表团在访问了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国以后，已在今天上午十一时二十分乘火车回到北京。
    陈毅副总理和有关部门的负责人到车站欢迎。
    朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐也到车站欢迎。大使馆的女同志向郭沫若等人献了鲜花。
