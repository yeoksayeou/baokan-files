Title: "Free Romania" Reports: The Chinese People are Determined to Complete the Unification of the Motherland
Author:
Date: 1958-10-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, October 14th. "Free Romania" published a commentary today on the order issued by our Minister of National Defense Peng Dehuai regarding the two-week extension of the ceasefire on the shelling of Jinmen. The commentary said that the measures taken by the People's Republic of China will certainly have a great impact on the residents of Taiwan, Penghu, Jinmen, and Matsu. Sooner or later, the residents of these areas will realize that Chiang Kai-shek has deceived them, turning them into cannon fodder for launching military adventures. They will understand that only the true People's Republic of China cares about their fate, and only by being closely united with the 600 million Chinese people can their happy future be guaranteed.

The commentary emphasized that no one in world opinion believes Dulles's statement: "China's initiative is a sign of China's weakness." Who doesn't know that China is a strong country? It can inflict a devastating blow on any enemy that invades it. The liberated Chinese people will defend their rights as one person. The movement of millions of men and women participating in the national armed forces demonstrates the strength, not the weakness, of the People's Republic of China.

The commentary also said that the Chinese people are determined to complete the unification of the motherland, and nothing and no one can stop them from achieving this goal.

### “自由罗马尼亚报”说：中国人民有决心完成祖国的统一

1958-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

    “自由罗马尼亚报”说：
    中国人民有决心完成祖国的统一
    新华社布加勒斯斯14日电“自由罗马尼亚报”今天就我国国防部长彭德怀发布的关于对金门炮击再停两星期的命令发表评论说，中华人民共和国的措施，对台、澎、金、马的居民一定会有很大的影响，这些地区的居民们迟早终会认识到蒋介石骗了他们，把他们变为发动军事冒险的炮灰。他们会了解到只有真正的中华人民共和国才关怀他们的命运，只有和六亿中国人民紧密的团结在一起，才能保证他们幸福的未来。
    评论强调说，世界舆论没有任何人相信杜勒斯所说的这句话：“中国的倡议是中国的软弱的表现”。谁不知道，中国是个强国呢？它可以给任何侵略它的敌人以毁灭性的打击。解放了的中国人民，会像一个人一样保卫着他们的权利。千百万男女参加全民武装的运动，这说明了中华人民共和国力量的强大，而不是软弱。
    评论又说，中国人民决心完成祖国的统一，没有任何人和任何东西能阻挡他们达到这个目的。
