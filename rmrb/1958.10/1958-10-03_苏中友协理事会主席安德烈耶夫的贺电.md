Title: Telegram of Greetings from A. Andreyev, Chairman of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Council
Author: An·Andreyev
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

Telegram of Greetings from A. Andreyev, Chairman of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Council

To Comrade Song Qingling, Respected President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association!
Dear Comrades!

On behalf of all members of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Council, I extend to you, our dear friends, and through you to all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, congratulations on the National Day of the Chinese people – the ninth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

The Chinese people are greeting this glorious festival in the midst of a great leap forward in socialist construction and on an unprecedentedly large scale. All the people of the Soviet Union are sincerely happy for the achievements of the People's Republic of China and are delighted by the heroic struggle of the fraternal Chinese people, under the leadership of their glorious Communist Party, to turn China into a socialist industrial power.

The sincere friendship and comprehensive cooperation between our two peoples are developing and strengthening day by day. This fraternal friendship, common goals, and unshakable unity of the people of the Soviet Union and China were vividly demonstrated in the historic communique issued during the recent talks between the leaders of our two countries in Beijing.

Together with all peace-loving people, our common determination to unswervingly defend the cause of peace and strike a head-on blow against any aggressor is unshakable.

Dear friends, once again, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to you and express our best wishes to all the Chinese people engaged in socialist construction.

Long live the People's Republic of China, our great friend and ally!

Long live the great Chinese people – the builders of socialism!

Long live the great, unbreakable, fraternal friendship between the people of the Soviet Union and China!

                                            A. Andreyev, Chairman of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Council

### 苏中友协理事会主席安德烈耶夫的贺电
安·安德烈耶夫
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

      苏中友协理事会主席安德烈耶夫的贺电中苏友好协会总会敬爱的宋庆龄同志！亲爱的同志们！
    苏中友好协会理事会代表本会全体会员向你们，亲爱的朋友们，并通过你们向中苏友好协会的全体会员祝贺中国人民的国庆节——中华人民共和国建国九周年。
    中国人民在社会主义建设的大跃进声中和空前巨大规模的情况下，迎接这一光辉的节日。全苏联人民为中华人民共和国的成就而感到衷心高兴，为兄弟般的中国人民在自己的光荣的共产党的领导下努力把中国变成社会主义工业强国的英勇奋斗而表示欢欣鼓舞。
    我们两国人民之间的真诚友谊与全面的合作正在日益发展与巩固。苏中两国人民的这种兄弟般的友谊、一致的目标和不可动摇的团结，在不久前我们两国领导人在北京举行的会谈时所发表的历史性的公报中，得到了鲜明的表现。
    我们和所有爱好和平的人民一起，始终不渝地捍卫和平事业和给予任何侵略者迎头痛击的共同决心是不可动摇的。
    亲爱的朋友们，再一次衷心地祝贺你们，并对从事社会主义建设的全体中国人民表示我们最良好的祝愿。
    我们伟大的朋友和同盟者——中华人民共和国万岁！
    伟大的中国人民——社会主义的建设者万岁！
    苏中两国人民伟大的牢不可破的兄弟般的友谊万岁！
                    苏中友好协会理事会主席　安·安德烈耶夫
