Title: US "Polaris" Missile Launch Suffers Another Failure
Author:
Date: 1958-10-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The US Navy's "Polaris" medium-range missile suffered another failure in a test launch at Cape Canaveral on the 15th.

At the very beginning of the launch, its second stage separated from the first stage, drew an arc in the air, and exploded one and a half miles from the launch point. However, its first stage remained intact at the launch site. The explosion of the second stage rocket killed a safety officer.

### 美国“北极星”导弹发射又遭失败

1958-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

    美国“北极星”导弹发射又遭失败
    据新华社讯　美国海军的“北极星”中程导弹15日在卡纳维拉尔角发射场的试验发射中再一次遭到失败。
    在刚刚开始发射时，它的第二级就脱离了第一级，在空中画了一道弧线，在离开发射点一哩半的地方爆炸。而它的第一级却原封不动地留在发射场。第二级火箭爆炸时炸死了一名负责安全的官员。
