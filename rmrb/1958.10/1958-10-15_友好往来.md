　　Friendly Exchanges
　　Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet for Thai Parliamentary Delegation
　　Xinhua News Agency, October 14th: Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet tonight for the Thai parliamentary delegation headed by Nai Thanom Khoman.
　　Those present at the banquet included: Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Zhang Su, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Zhou Gengsheng and Qiao Guanhua, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and others.
　　Burmese Education Delegation Arrives in Beijing
　　Xinhua News Agency, October 14th: The Burmese Education Delegation, a group of five led by U Ba Gyaw, Secretary of the Burmese Ministry of Education, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of October 13th.  Wei Que, Vice Minister of Education, Liu Ziyu, Deputy Director of the General Office, Wang Tongqi, Deputy Director of the Beijing Municipal Education Bureau, and others were present at the airport to welcome them.
　　Sofia Parliament Delegation Returns Home
　　Xinhua News Agency, October 14th: The People's Council Delegation from Sofia, People's Republic of Bulgaria, which came to visit our country, returned home by plane at noon today.  Liu Ren, Fan Rusheng, Wan Li, Feng Jiping, Le Songsheng, and others from the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Beijing Municipal Vice Mayors were present at the airport to see them off.

### 友好往来

1958-10-15
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    张奚若宴请泰国议员团
    新华社14日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今晚宴请以乃他汉·堪希兰为首的泰国议员团。
    出席宴会的有：中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员张苏，中国人民外交学会副会长周鲠生、乔冠华等人。
    缅甸教育代表团抵京
    新华社14日讯　缅甸教育代表团一行五人，在缅甸教育部秘书吴巴觉团长率领下，于10月13日下午抵京，前往机场欢迎的有教育部副部长韦悫、办公厅副主任刘子余、北京市教育局副局长汪通祺等人。
    索非亚议会代表团返国
    新华社14日讯　前来我国访问的保加利亚人民共和国索非亚市人民会议代表团，今天中午乘飞机返国。前往机场送行的，有中共北京市委员会书记刘仁、范儒生，北京市副市长万里、冯基平、乐松生等。
