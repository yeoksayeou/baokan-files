Title: Young People on the Fujian Front (Photos)
Author: Qi Guanshan
Date: 1958-10-10
Page: 6

Fujian Frontline Youth

From left to right: (1) The enemy propaganda station organized by the youth of Weitou uses various methods to carry out propaganda against the enemy. This is them preparing to fly kites with propaganda materials. (2) Girls Wu Qiuhong (left) and Hong Xiumei (right) on the Fujian front are washing clothes for the soldiers. (3) Students from Jinjiang No. 3 Middle School in Fujian are giving haircuts to soldiers on the front lines. Xinhua News Agency reporter
