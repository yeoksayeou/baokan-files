Title: Jiangxi Oil Tea Achieves a Bumper Harvest
Author:
Date: 1958-10-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Report: Jiangxi province's more than ten million mu of mature mountain oil tea trees have generally yielded a bumper harvest this year. The province is expected to produce 120 million jin of tea oil, 50 million jin more than the "good year" of 1957. Tuanjie Cooperative in Hengfeng County also produced a high-yield "satellite" with a yield of 1,700 jin per mu.

### 江西油茶大丰收

1958-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    江西油茶大丰收
    据新华社讯　今年江西省一千多万亩熟山油茶普遍丰收。预计全省可产茶油一亿二千万斤，比“大年”1957年高出五千万斤。横峰县团结社还出现了一块亩产一千七百斤的高产“卫星”。
