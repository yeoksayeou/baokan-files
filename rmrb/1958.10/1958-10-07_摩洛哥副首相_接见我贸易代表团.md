Title: Moroccan Deputy Prime Minister Receives Chinese Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, October 3rd: Moroccan Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Economy, Buabid, received the Chinese government trade delegation led by Lei Renmin on the 3rd. The delegation is currently visiting Morocco.

On the same day, the Chinese government trade delegation paid a visit to Moroccan Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Dousetta.

### 摩洛哥副首相  接见我贸易代表团

1958-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥副首相
    接见我贸易代表团
    新华社拉巴特3日电　摩洛哥副首相兼国民经济大臣布阿比德在3日接见了由雷任民率领的中国政府贸易代表团，这个代表团目前正在摩洛哥访问。
    同一天，中国政府贸易代表团拜访了摩洛哥副外交大臣杜塞塔。
