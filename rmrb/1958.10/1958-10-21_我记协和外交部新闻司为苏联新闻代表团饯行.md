Title: All-China Journalists Association and Information Department of the Foreign Ministry Host Farewell Banquet for Soviet News Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-10-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 20th. The All-China Journalists Association and the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs hosted a reception this evening to bid farewell to the Soviet News Delegation headed by Moskovsky.

At the reception, which was filled with an atmosphere of close friendship, Wu Lengxi, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, and Moskovsky, head of the delegation, delivered speeches, congratulating each other on the further consolidation and development of the fraternal friendship between the people and journalists of China and the Soviet Union.

Sudarikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, and others attended the reception.

Also present at the reception tonight were Wang Yunsheng, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and members of the capital's press circles.

### 我记协和外交部新闻司为苏联新闻代表团饯行

1958-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    我记协和外交部新闻司为苏联新闻代表团饯行
    新华社20日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会和外交部新闻司今天晚间举行酒会，欢送以莫斯科夫斯基为首的苏联新闻工作者代表团。
    在洋溢着亲密友好气氛的酒会中，新闻工作者协会副会长吴冷西和代表团团长莫斯科夫斯基分别致词，互相祝贺中苏两国人民和新闻工作者间的兄弟般的友谊进一步巩固和发展。
    苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫等参加了酒会。
    出席今晚酒会的还有新闻工作者协会副会长王芸生、外交部新闻司副司长徐晃以及首都新闻界人士等。
