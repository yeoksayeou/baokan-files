Title: Telegram of Congratulations from Indian Prime Minister Nehru
Author: Jiawahala'er Nihelu
Date: 1958-10-01
Page: 3

To His Excellency Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of your country's National Day, the Government and people of India, as well as myself, extend to you, Mr. Premier, and to the Government and people of China, our warm congratulations and best wishes for the happiness and prosperity of your people.

    I am deeply convinced that the many friendly ties existing between our two countries will be further strengthened in the years to come, for the benefit of our two countries and the cause of world peace.

                                          Jawaharlal Nehru

### 印度总理尼赫鲁的贺电
贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁
1958-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    印度总理尼赫鲁的贺电中华人民共和国政府周恩来总理阁下：
    在贵国国庆的时候，印度政府和人民以及我本人向你，总理先生，以及中国政府和人民致以热诚的祝贺和为贵国人民的幸福和繁荣的良好的祝愿。
    我深信：存在于我们两国之间的许多友好联系在未来的年代里必将得到进一步加强，以利于我们两国和世界和平的事业。
                                          贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁
