Title: Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region to be Established Soon; People of All Nationalities Welcome Their Festival with Practical Actions
Author:
Date: 1958-10-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Yinchuan, 12th – The Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, long-awaited by the people of all nationalities in the Ningxia area, is about to be born. Preparations for the establishment of the autonomous region are actively underway.

According to reports from the Preparatory Committee of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region: The first session of the First People's Congress of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region will hold a preparatory meeting on the 16th, tentatively scheduled to officially convene on the 24th, announcing the formal establishment of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region.

Currently, the first sessions of the Third People's Congresses of the nineteen counties (cities) in this area have concluded, and the work of electing delegates to the First People's Congress of the Autonomous Region has been completed, with a total of more than two hundred delegates elected. They will arrive in Yinchuan successively in the coming days.

Since the Fourth Session of the National People's Congress passed the resolution on the establishment of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region last July, the Hui and Han people in the vast areas of Ningxia have been overjoyed and unanimously expressed their warm support. Over the past year, publicity, deliberation, and discussions about the establishment of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region have been widely carried out among the people of all nationalities in the Ningxia area. Now, the people of all nationalities are even more joyful and energetic, welcoming the establishment of the Autonomous Region with the practical actions of the Great Leap Forward.

### 宁夏回族自治区即将成立  各族人民正以实际行动迎接自己的节日

1958-10-15
第6版()
专栏：

    宁夏回族自治区即将成立
    各族人民正以实际行动迎接自己的节日
    新华社银川12日电　宁夏地区各族人民盼望已久的宁夏回族自治区即将诞生了。成立自治区的各项筹备工作正在积极进行中。
    据记者从宁夏回族自治区筹备委员会获悉：宁夏回族自治区第一届人民代表大会第一次会议，将在16日开预备会议，暂定于24日正式开会，宣告宁夏回族自治区正式成立。
    目前，这个地区十九个县（市）的第三届人民代表大会第一次会议都已结束，已经完成了选举自治区第一届人民代表大会代表的工作，共选出代表二百多名。他们将在最近几日陆续到达银川。
    自去年7月全国人民代表大会第四次会议通过关于成立宁夏回族自治区的决议后，宁夏广大地区的回、汉人民，不胜欢欣鼓舞，一致表示热烈拥护。一年多来，在宁夏地区各族人民中，普遍进行了关于建立宁夏回族自治区的宣传、酝酿和讨论。现在各族人民更是喜气洋洋，干劲冲天地以大跃进的实际行动迎接自治区成立。
