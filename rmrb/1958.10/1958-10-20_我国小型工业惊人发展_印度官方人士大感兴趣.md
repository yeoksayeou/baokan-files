Title: Our Country's Small-Scale Industry Develops at an Astonishing Rate; Indian Officials are Greatly Interested
Author:
Date: 1958-10-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 17th. Today's "Times of India" stated in a report about the Great Leap Forward in China: "Recent reports about the astonishing development of various small-scale industries in China have aroused great interest among officials here."

The newspaper also said that the Indian government may send experts to China to study the progress and significance of this development, and "whether it is possible to adopt it under Indian conditions."

The report devoted considerable space to discussing the situation of various industrial units in China, the establishment of small blast furnaces, the production of steel, and the manufacturing of agricultural implements. The news said that "the Chinese experiment seems to have put the whole country to work, and transformed the countryside into factories producing a wide variety of goods, and turned peasants into primary technical personnel."

### 我国小型工业惊人发展  印度官方人士大感兴趣

1958-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

    我国小型工业惊人发展
    印度官方人士大感兴趣
    新华社新德里17日电　今天的“印度时报”在一篇报道中国大跃进的消息中说：“最近关于中国各种小型工业的惊人发展的报道，引起这里官方人士极大的兴趣。”
    这家报纸还说，印度政府可能派遣专家到中国去研究这种发展的进展情况和重要意义，以及“是否可能在印度的条件下采用”。
    这篇报道用很大篇幅谈到中国各工业单位和建立小高炉、生产钢和制造农具的情况。消息说，“中国的试验似乎把全国都投入了工作，并且把农村变成生产各种各样物品的工厂，而把农民变成初级的技术人材”。
