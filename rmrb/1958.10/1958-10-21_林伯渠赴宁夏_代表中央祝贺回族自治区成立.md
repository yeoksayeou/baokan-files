Title: Lin Boqu Goes to Ningxia to Represent the Central Government in Congratulating the Establishment of the Hui Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1958-10-21
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, October 20th. Lin Boqu, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, left Beijing this afternoon to represent the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Central People's Government, and Chairman Mao in Yinchuan to congratulate the establishment of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region.

Xie Fumin, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress, and Yang Jingren, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China, also went to Yinchuan by the same train today to offer congratulations.

Those who went to the station to see them off included Qi Yanming, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council, Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Sa Kongliao, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission.

Also traveling on the same train to Yinchuan to congratulate the establishment of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region were representatives from the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region and Shandong, Jilin, Hunan, Guangdong, Henan, Anhui, Hebei, Liaoning, Sichuan, and Beijing.

### 林伯渠赴宁夏  代表中央祝贺回族自治区成立

1958-10-21
第6版()
专栏：

    林伯渠赴宁夏
    代表中央祝贺回族自治区成立
    新华社20日讯　代表中国共产党中央委员会、中央人民政府和毛主席，前往银川祝贺宁夏回族自治区成立的中共中央政治局委员、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠，今天下午离开北京。
    全国人民代表大会民族委员会副主任委员谢扶民、中华人民共和国民族事务委员会副主任杨静仁，今天也同车前往银川祝贺。
    前往车站送行的有，国务院副秘书长齐燕铭，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏，民族事务委员会副主任萨空了。
    同车前往银川祝贺宁夏回族自治区成立的，还有广西僮族自治区和山东、吉林、湖南、广东、河南、安徽、河北、辽宁、四川及北京市的代表。
