Title: Barka Receives Lei Renmin, Expresses Hope to Work to Promote Sino-Moroccan Friendship
Author:
Date: 1958-10-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, 16th. Mehdi Ben Barka, President of the Moroccan Consultative Assembly, received Lei Renmin, head of the Chinese government trade delegation, and Ke Hua, deputy head of the delegation, on the 16th.

The Chinese government trade delegation is in Morocco negotiating with the Moroccan government.

Lei Renmin conveyed to Barka the invitation from Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, to visit China. Barka gladly accepted the invitation.

During the hour-long meeting, Barka told the guests that he was extremely interested in China's achievements in recent years. He also expressed his hope to work to promote Chinese-Moroccan friendship.

### 巴尔卡接见雷任民  表示希望为促进中摩友谊努力

1958-10-18
第5版()
专栏：

    巴尔卡接见雷任民
    表示希望为促进中摩友谊努力
    新华社拉巴特16日电　摩洛哥咨询议会主席迈赫迪·本·巴尔卡16日接见了中国政府贸易代表团正团长雷任民和副团长柯华。
    中国政府贸易代表团正在摩洛哥同摩洛哥政府进行谈判。
    雷任民向巴尔卡转达了中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇请他访问中国的邀请，巴尔卡很高兴地接受了这一邀请。
    在历时一小时的会谈中，巴尔卡对客人们说，他对中国近几年的成就极感兴趣。他还表示希望为促进中国—摩洛哥的友谊而努力。
