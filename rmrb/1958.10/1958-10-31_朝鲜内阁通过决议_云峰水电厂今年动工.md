Title: Korean Cabinet Passes Resolution: Yunfeng Hydroelectric Plant to Start Construction This Year
Author:
Date: 1958-10-31
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 30th. The Korean Cabinet passed a resolution on the construction of the Yalu River Yunfeng Hydroelectric Plant on the 29th.

The resolution stated that the government of the Republic recently reached an agreement with the government of the People's Republic of China to jointly construct the Yunfeng Hydroelectric Plant through cooperation between China and Korea. The resolution pointed out that this will make a greater contribution to further developing the friendly and cooperative relations between the people of China and Korea and further consolidating Korea's power base, meeting Korea's growing electricity needs.

The resolution stated that the Yunfeng Hydroelectric Plant, which will have a total generating capacity of 600,000 kilowatts, is scheduled to commence construction this year and be completed in September 1962.

The resolution also requested the relevant central departments and local governments to actively ensure the successful completion of the construction project.

### 朝鲜内阁通过决议  云峰水电厂今年动工

1958-10-31
第7版()
专栏：

    朝鲜内阁通过决议
    云峰水电厂今年动工
    新华社平壤30日电　朝鲜内阁29日通过了关于建设鸭绿江云峰水力发电厂的决议。
    决议说，共和国政府最近同中华人民共和国政府达成协议，由中朝两国合作共同建设云峰水力发电厂。决议指出，这将对进一步发展中朝两国人民的友好合作关系和进一步巩固朝鲜的电力基地，满足朝鲜日益增长的电力需要，作出更大的贡献。
    决议说，这个将具有总发电能力六十万瓩的云峰水力发电厂决定从今年动工兴建，到1962年9月竣工。
    决议并要求中央有关各部门和地方政府积极保证建设工程的胜利完成。
