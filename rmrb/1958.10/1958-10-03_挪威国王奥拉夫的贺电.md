Title: Telegram of Congratulations from King Olav of Norway
Author:
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

To His Excellency Chairman Mao Zedong:

    On the occasion of the National Day of the People's Republic of China, I extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the Chinese people.
                                                    Olav
