Title: Asian-African Writers' Conference Opens; Premier Zhou Enlai Sends Congratulatory Telegram

Author:
Date: 1958-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: Premier Zhou Enlai today sent a telegram to congratulate the opening of the Asian-African Writers' Conference. The telegram reads as follows: Asian-African Writers' Conference, Tashkent, Soviet Union:

The convening of the Asian-African Writers' Conference marks a further development in the cause of unity, friendship, cultural exchange, and peace among the Asian and African peoples. I extend my warm congratulations.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Zhou Enlai, October 6, 1958

### 亚非作家会议开幕  周恩来总理致电祝贺

1958-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    亚非作家会议开幕
    周恩来总理致电祝贺
    新华社6日讯　周恩来总理今天致电祝贺亚非作家会议开幕。电文如下：苏联塔什干亚非作家会议：
    亚非作家会议的召开，标志着亚非人民团结友好、文化交流、保卫和平事业的进一步发展，谨致热诚祝贺。
                               周恩来　1958年10月6日
