Title: President Kekkonen of Finland's Congratulatory Message
Author:
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

    President Kekkonen of Finland's Congratulatory Message

    To His Excellency Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of the anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I am very pleased to extend to Your Excellency my warm congratulations and best wishes, wishing the Chinese people happiness and prosperity.

                          Urho Kekkonen
                          President of the Republic of Finland

### 芬兰总统吉科宁的贺电
乌尔霍·吉科宁
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    芬兰总统吉科宁的贺电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东阁下：
    在中华人民共和国成立纪念的时候，我很愉快地向阁下致以热忱的祝贺和最良好的愿望，祝中国人民幸福和昌盛。
                          芬兰共和国总统　乌尔霍·吉科宁
