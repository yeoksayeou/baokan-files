Title: Telegram of Greetings from Burmese President U Win Maung
Author:
Date: 1958-10-01
Page: 3

To His Excellency Mao Zedong, President of the People's Republic of China:

	On the occasion of the ninth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I am delighted to represent the Government and people of the Union of Burma, and in my own name, to extend warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the People's Republic of China. At the same time, allow me to sincerely wish Your Excellency good health, and the people of the People's Republic of China eternal happiness and continuous progress, and wish the close friendship between our two peoples further development.

					U Win Maung
					President of the Union of Burma

### 缅甸总统吴温貌的贺电
吴温貌
1958-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    缅甸总统吴温貌的贺电中华人民共和国毛泽东先生阁下：
    欣逢中华人民共和国成立九周年纪念，我很愉快地代表缅甸联邦政府和人民，并以我个人的名义，向阁下和中华人民共和国致以热烈的祝贺。同时让我诚挚地祝阁下健康，祝中华人民共和国人民永远幸福和不断进步，祝我们两国人民之间的密切友谊进一步发展。
                                    缅甸联邦总统　吴温貌
