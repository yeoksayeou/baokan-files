Title: Romanian Military Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1958-10-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: The military delegation of the Romanian People's Republic, headed by General Salajan, left Beijing by special plane at 7:30 this morning to return home after its visit to our country.

　　At 6:50 this morning, a farewell team was lined up at the Capital Airport. General Salajan, accompanied by General Huang Kecheng, Deputy Minister of National Defense and Chief of the General Staff, reviewed the guard of honor.

　　In his farewell speech, General Salajan said, "Our military delegation is about to return home after a nearly month-long visit to your great and beautiful country. As we are about to leave your glorious land, we once again express our deepest gratitude to the government of the People's Republic of China, the Chinese people, and Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense, for the care and brotherly hospitality they have extended to us during our visit."

　　General Salajan also praised the historic achievements made by the Chinese people under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party.

　　In his farewell speech, General Huang Kecheng said, "With reluctance, we are here to bid farewell to all the comrades of the delegation. Although your visit to our country was not long, your great internationalist spirit, your enthusiasm, and your brotherly friendship have left a deep impression on the Chinese people and army. Your friendly visit has made a significant contribution to enhancing the mutual understanding between the people and armies of China and Romania, and to strengthening the unity, friendship, and cooperation between the people and armies of our two countries."

　　On behalf of the Chinese people and all officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Huang Kecheng expressed his heartfelt gratitude to General Salajan and all members of the delegation, and wished them a safe journey.

　　Also present at the airport today to see them off were General Zhang Aiping, General Peng Shaohui, General Li Juikui, General Guo Tianmin, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Vice Admiral Luo Shunchu, Lieutenant General Wang Huiqiu of the Air Force, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, and others. The Romanian Ambassador to China, Rudenko, and his wife, as well as military attachés from various countries to China, also came to the airport to see them off.

### 罗马尼亚军事代表团回国

1958-10-26
第3版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚军事代表团回国
    新华社25日讯　以萨拉扬上将为首的罗马尼亚人民共和国军事代表团在我国访问结束后，今天上午七点三十分乘专机离北京回国。
    上午六时五十分，首都机场排列着欢送的队伍。萨拉扬上将在国防部副部长兼总参谋长黄克诚大将的陪同下检阅了仪仗队。
    萨拉扬上将致告别词说，我们军事代表团在你们伟大而美丽的国家进行了将近一个月的访问，现在就要回国了。我们在将要离开你们光荣的国土的时候，再一次向中华人民共和国政府、中国人民和国防部部长彭德怀元帅表示最深刻的谢意，感谢他们在我们访问期间所给予的关怀和兄弟般的热情的款待。
    萨拉扬上将还赞扬中国人民在中国共产党英明领导下所获得的历史性的成就。
    黄克诚大将在致欢送词中说，我们怀着惜别的心情，来送别代表团全体同志们。你们在我国访问虽然时间不长，但是你们伟大的国际主义精神，你们的热情和兄弟般的友谊，在中国人民和军队中，留下了深刻的印象。通过你们这次友好访问，对于增进中、罗两国人民和军队之间的相互了解，对于加强中罗两国人民和军队之间的团结友谊和合作已作出了重大贡献。
    黄克诚大将代表中国人民和中国人民解放军全体官兵，向萨拉扬上将和代表团全体人员表示衷心的感谢，并祝一路平安。
    今天到机场欢送的还有张爱萍上将、彭绍辉上将、李聚奎上将、郭天民上将、萧向荣中将、罗舜初海军中将、王辉球空军中将，外交部副部长曾涌泉等。罗马尼亚驻我国大使鲁登科及夫人，以及各国驻华武官，也到机场送行。
