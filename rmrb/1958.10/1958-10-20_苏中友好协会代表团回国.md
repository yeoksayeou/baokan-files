Title: Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1958-10-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. The delegation of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, headed by Cherednichenko, returned to their country by plane today after concluding their friendly visit to China.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Liao Chengzhi and Shao Lizi, vice-presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, director Liu Qingyang, Cao Jinghua and Wang Zhixiang, vice-presidents of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and representatives from all walks of life in the capital.

The counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China and other staff members also saw them off at the airport.

During their stay in China, the delegation visited Beijing, Tianjin, Wuhan, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places.

### 苏中友好协会代表团回国

1958-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

    苏中友好协会代表团回国
    新华社19日讯　以契列德尼钦科为首的苏中友好协会代表团结束了在中国的友好访问以后，在今天乘飞机离北京返国。
    到机场送行的，有中苏友好协会总会副会长廖承志、邵力子，理事刘清扬，北京市中苏友好协会副会长曹靖华、王之相和首都各界代表。
    苏联驻华大使馆参赞和其他工作人员也到机场送行。
    代表团在中国期间曾在北京、天津、武汉、上海、杭州等地参观访问。
