Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Receives Head of Azerbaijan Song and Dance Ensemble
Author:
Date: 1958-10-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Vice Chairman Zhu De received this afternoon Sayid Zade, head of the Soviet Azerbaijan National Song and Dance Ensemble and Deputy Minister of Culture of Soviet Azerbaijan, artistic director G. Salakhov, and the ensemble's administrative head T. Shahvelgiyev.

Sayid Zade, on behalf of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Azerbaijan Soviet Socialist Republic, presented gifts to Vice Chairman Zhu De.

Present at the reception was Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

Also present at the reception was Sudarikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China.

### 朱德副主席接见  阿塞拜疆歌舞团负责人

1958-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席接见
    阿塞拜疆歌舞团负责人
    新华社9日讯　朱德副主席今日下午接见了苏联阿塞拜疆国家歌舞团团长、苏联阿塞拜疆文化部副部长萨伊特·扎德，艺术指导格·萨拉霍夫和该团行政负责人塔·沙赫维尔吉也夫。
    萨伊特·扎德代表苏联阿塞拜疆苏维埃社会主义共和国最高苏维埃主席团向朱德副主席赠送了礼品。
    接见时在座的有对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥。
    接见时在座的还有苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫。
