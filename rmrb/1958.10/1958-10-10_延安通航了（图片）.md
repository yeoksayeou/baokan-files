Title: Yan'an Opens Air Service (Picture)
Author: Yang Shaofu
Date: 1958-10-10
Page: 2

    Yan'an Opens Air Service
    According to Xinhua News Agency, the Xi'an-Yan'an civil aviation route has officially opened. On the 6th, a civil aviation plane, fully loaded with passengers and supplies, flew from Yan'an to Xi'an, and returned to Yan'an in the afternoon. This route is 280 kilometers long and takes about one and a half hours each way.
    This is the first air route in Shaanxi Province. There will be six flights between the two places every week from now on. In the near future, air services to Yulin, Hanzhong, and Ankang from Xi'an will also be opened.
  Top picture: A domestically produced An-2 aircraft arrives at Yan'an Airport. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yang Shaofu.

### 延安通航了（图片）
杨绍夫
1958-10-10
第2版()
专栏：

    延安通航了
    据新华社讯　西安、延安间民航班机正式通航。6日，一架民航飞机满载旅客和物资由延安飞到西安，下午又飞回延安。这条航线，全程280公里，往返各需一小时半左右。
    这是陕西省的第一条航线。今后每周有六趟班机来往两地。不久，西安到榆林、汉中和安康等地也将通航。
