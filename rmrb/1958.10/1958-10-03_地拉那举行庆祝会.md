Title: Celebration Held in Tirana
Author:
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, October 1st. A celebration was held on the evening of September 30th by representatives from all walks of life in Tirana, the capital of Albania, to celebrate the 9th anniversary of China's National Day.

Attending the celebration were Aliya and Dervish, alternate members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, and others. Luo Shigao, Chinese Ambassador to Albania, was also invited to attend the celebration.

Sa·Bocha Yi, member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party and editor-in-chief of the "People's Voice" newspaper, the central organ of the Labor Party, made a report at the celebration. He detailed the great achievements of the Chinese people in socialist construction. He emphasized that the establishment and development of the People's Commune is an event of great significance. He said that the People's Commune is the best form of transition from socialism to communism. The Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong creatively developed Marxism based on Marxist-Leninist theory, the experience of the Soviet Union, and the specific conditions of China.

In his speech, Bocha Yi also condemned the provocations of the US-Chiang Kai-shek clique in the Taiwan Strait area and talked about the Albanian people's active support for the just struggle of the Chinese people.

The celebration unanimously passed a congratulatory telegram to the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party. The telegram congratulated the Chinese people on their great achievements and expressed firm support for the sacred struggle of the Chinese people to liberate their territory.

### 地拉那举行庆祝会

1958-10-03
第4版()
专栏：

    地拉那举行庆祝会
    新华社地拉那1日电　阿尔巴尼亚首都地拉那各界代表9月30日晚间举行庆祝会，庆祝中国国庆九周年。
    出席庆祝会的有阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局候补委员阿利雅和德尔维什等。中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高也应邀出席了庆祝会。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、劳动党中央机关报“人民之声报”总编辑萨·波查伊在庆祝会上作了报告。他详细地介绍了中国人民在社会主义建设方面所获得的巨大成就。他强调指出，人民公社的建立和发展是一件具有很重要意义的事件。他说，人民公社是从社会主义过渡到共产主义的最好形式。中国共产党和毛泽东主席根据马克思列宁主义理论、苏联的经验和中国的具体条件创造性地发展了马克思主义。
    在讲话中，波查伊还谴责了美蒋在台湾海峡地区的挑衅，谈到了阿尔巴尼亚人民对中国人民正义斗争的积极支持。
    庆祝会最后一致通过了致中华人民共和国政府和中国共产党中央委员会的贺电。电文祝贺中国人民所取得的巨大成就，并表示坚决支持中国人民解放自己领土的神圣斗争。
