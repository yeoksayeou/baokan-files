Title: Soviet Union Overfulfills Third Quarter Industrial Production Plan; Industrial Output in First Nine Months of This Year Increases 10% Compared to Same Period Last Year
Author:
Date: 1958-10-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th. TASS reports: The rapidly developing Soviet industrial production overfulfilled its production plan again in the third quarter of this year. According to a public statement issued on the 11th by the Central Statistical Bureau of the Soviet Council of Ministers, the total Soviet industrial output plan for the past three months was completed at 102%. Due to the excellent completion of the production plan in the first half of this year, the Soviet industrial production plan for the first nine months of this year was completed at 103%, and the total industrial output increased by 10% compared to the same period last year. The labor productivity of workers in the industrial sector increased by 5.6%.

The statement pointed out that, compared with the same period last year, in the first nine months of this year, the production of the Soviet black and non-ferrous metallurgical industry increased by 9%, the production of the fuel and power industry increased by 10%, the machinery manufacturing and metal processing industry increased by 14%, the chemical and rubber industry increased by 12%, the building materials industry increased by 25%, the light industry increased by 6%, and the food industry increased by 4%.

In the first nine months of this year, the Soviet Union produced a total of 29.1 million tons of pig iron, an increase of 1.6 million tons compared to the same period last year; steel - 40.8 million tons, an increase of 3 million tons; steel products - 31.9 million tons, an increase of 2.4 million tons; coal - 371 million tons, an increase of 26 million tons; oil - 83.4 million tons, an increase of 11.4 million tons; cement - 24.4 million tons, an increase of 3.6 million tons.

In the first nine months of this year, the Soviet plan for the production of daily necessities was overfulfilled. Compared with the first three quarters of last year, textile products increased by 237 million meters, leather shoes increased by 28 million pairs, and sugar increased by 645,000 tons.

### 苏联第三季度工业生产计划超额完成  今年头九个月工业产量比去年同期增长10％

1958-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联第三季度工业生产计划超额完成
    今年头九个月工业产量比去年同期增长10％
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：不断迅速发展的苏联工业生产在今年第三季度又超额完成了生产计划。据苏联部长会议中央统计局11日发表的公报，在过去三个月中，苏联工业总产量计划以102％完成了。由于今年头半年的生产计划完成得很好，总计在今年头九个月中苏联工业的生产计划完成了103％，工业总产量比去年同时期增加了10％，工业部门中工人的劳动生产率提高了5.6％。
    公报指出，和去年同期比较，今年头九个月中，苏联黑色和有色冶金工业的生产增加了9％，燃料和动力工业的生产增加了10％，机器制造和金属加工工业增加了14％，化学和橡胶工业增加了12％，建筑材料工业增加了25％，轻工业增加了6％，食品工业增加了4％。
    今年头九个月内，苏联总共生产了两千九百一十万吨生铁，比去年同时期增加了一百六十万吨；钢——四千零八十万吨，增加了三百万吨；钢材——三千一百九十万吨，增加了二百四十万吨；煤——三亿七千一百万吨，增加了两千六百万吨；石油——八千三百四十万吨，增加了一千一百四十万吨；水泥——二千四百四十万吨，增加了三百六十万吨。
    今年头九个月，苏联日用品生产计划超额完成了。和去年头三季度比较，纺织品增加了两亿三千七百万公尺，皮鞋增加了两千八百万双，砂糖增加了六十四万五千吨。
