Title: Soviet Power Station Department Delegation Concludes Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1958-10-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 18th: The delegation from the Soviet Power Station Department concluded its visit to China and left Beijing for the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on the 16th.

　　The delegation arrived in Beijing on September 29th. Premier Zhou Enlai received all members of the delegation on the 16th. During its stay in China, the delegation visited Shanghai, Hangzhou, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Zhengzhou, Sanmenxia, and other places.

### 苏电站部代表团结束在我国的访问

1958-10-19
第3版()
专栏：

    苏电站部代表团结束在我国的访问
    新华社18日讯　苏联电站部代表团结束了在中国的访问，于16日离开北京前往朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访问。
    这个代表团于9月29日到北京。周恩来总理在16日接见了代表团的全体人员。代表团在中国期间曾访问了上海、杭州、武汉、广州、郑州、三门峡等地。
