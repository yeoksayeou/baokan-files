Title: Palestinian Refugees Support Our Country's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1958-10-01
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 29th. Palestinian refugees living in Aleppo, in the northern part of Syria, recently wrote to the Chinese Consulate General in Damascus, expressing their support for the Chinese people's struggle to liberate Taiwan.

　　The letter said: We, Palestinian refugees—victims of the Anglo-American imperialists' piratical acts of plundering our homes—support your great Chinese people's heroic struggle against American imperialist provocations. We also support your struggle to liberate the territories of your motherland, Taiwan, Kinmen, and Matsu.

### 巴勒斯坦难民支持我国斗争

1958-10-01
第6版()
专栏：

    巴勒斯坦难民支持我国斗争
    新华社大马士革29日电　住在叙利亚地区北部的阿勒颇的巴勒斯坦难民最近写信给中国驻大马士革的总领事馆，表示支持中国人民解放台湾的斗争。
    信中说：我们巴勒斯坦难民——英美帝国主义者掠夺我们家园的海盗行为的受害者——支持你们伟大中国人民反对美帝国主义挑衅的英勇斗争。我们还支持你们解放你们祖国的领土台湾、金门和马祖的斗争。
