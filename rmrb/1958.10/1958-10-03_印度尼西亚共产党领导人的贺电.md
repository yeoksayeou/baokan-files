Title: Telegram of Greetings from the Leader of the Indonesian Communist Party
Author: D.N. Aidit
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

Telegram of Greetings from the Leader of the Indonesian Communist Party

To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China:

    On the occasion of the 9th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Indonesian Communists and working people extend warm congratulations to the Chinese Communists and working people. The achievements of the People's Republic of China have greatly strengthened the socialist world system and strengthened the front of national independence and world peace. Under the leadership of the Communist Party of China, the Chinese people are advancing towards even greater victories, including the liberation of Taiwan and the coastal islands.

    Long live the Communist Party of China!
    Long live the friendship between the Indonesian and Chinese people!

                  The Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party, D.N. Aidit

### 印度尼西亚共产党领导人的贺电
迪·努·艾地
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚共产党领导人的贺电中国共产党中央委员会：
    值此中华人民共和国成立九周年之际，印度尼西亚共产党人和劳动人民谨向中国共产党人和劳动人民致以热烈的祝贺。中华人民共和国的成就大大地加强了社会主义世界体系，加强了民族独立和世界和平的阵线。中国人民在中国共产党的领导下，正向着更伟大的胜利前进，包括解放台湾和沿海岛屿。
    中国共产党万岁！
    印度尼西亚人民和中国人民的友谊万岁！
                  印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会　迪·努·艾地
