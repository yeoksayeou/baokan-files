Title: National Day Holiday: Guests Arrive in Droves; 1,400+ Foreign Guests from 76 Countries Attend the Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1958-10-02
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 29th - During our National Day holiday in 1958, over one thousand four hundred representatives and independent travelers from seventy-six countries and regions in Asia, Africa, Europe, the Americas, and Australia came to our country to attend the National Day celebrations. These countries and regions are: Afghanistan, Albania, Algeria, Argentina, Australia, Austria, Belgium, Black Africa, Bolivia, Brazil, Bulgaria, Burma, Cameroon, Canada, Ceylon, Chile, Colombia, Congo, Cuba, Cyprus, Czechoslovakia, Ecuador, Federal Republic of Germany, Finland, France, German Democratic Republic, Guiana, United Kingdom, Greece, Guadeloupe, Guatemala, Hungary, Iceland, India, Indonesia, Iraq, Iran, Italy, Japan, Jordan, Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Laos, Lebanon, Libya, Madagascar, Martinique, Mexico, Mongolia, Morocco, Netherlands, New Zealand, Nicaragua, Norway, Pakistan, Palestine, Peru, Philippines, Poland, Romania, Scotland, Senegal, Sierra Leone, Spain, Sudan, Suriname, Tanganyika, Thailand, Togo, Tunisia, United Arab Republic, Uruguay, United States, Soviet Union, Venezuela, Vietnam, West Africa.

### 国庆佳节　宾客如云  76国的一千四百多外宾参加观礼

1958-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    国庆佳节　宾客如云
    76国的一千四百多外宾参加观礼
    新华社29日讯　前来我国参加1958年国庆节观礼的各国代表、自费旅行者共一千四百多人，他们来自亚洲、非洲、欧洲、美洲、澳洲五大洲的七十六个国家和地区。这些国家和地区是：阿富汗、阿尔巴尼亚、阿尔及利亚、阿根廷、澳大利亚、奥地利、比利时、黑非洲、玻利维亚、巴西、保加利亚、缅甸、喀麦隆、加拿大、锡兰、智利、哥伦比亚、刚果、古巴、塞浦路斯、捷克斯洛伐克、厄瓜多尔、德意志联邦共和国“芬兰、法国、德意志民主共和国、圭亚那、英国、希腊、瓜德罗浦、危地马拉、匈牙利、冰岛、印度、印度尼西亚、伊拉克、伊朗、意大利、日本、约旦、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、老挝、黎巴嫩、利比亚、马达加斯加、马提尼克、墨西哥、蒙古、摩洛哥、荷兰、新西兰、尼加拉瓜、挪威、巴基斯坦、巴勒斯坦、秘鲁、菲律宾、波兰、罗马尼亚、苏格兰、塞内加尔、塞拉勒窝内、西班牙、苏丹、苏里南、坦噶尼喀、泰国、多哥、突尼斯、阿拉伯联合共和国、乌拉圭、美国、苏联、委内瑞拉、越南、西非。
