Title: Supreme People's Assembly Standing Committee of North Korea Awards National Flag Medals to General Yang Yong and Others
Author:
Date: 1958-10-25
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 24th: The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea held a grand award ceremony in Pyongyang at 10:00 am today, awarding General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, and General Wang Ping, Political Commissar, the highest medal of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea—the First Class National Flag Medal, and awarding Lieutenant General Liang Biye, Deputy Political Commissar of the Volunteers, and other senior commanders the Second Class National Flag Medal.

Attending the award ceremony were: Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; Choi Won-taek, Speaker of the Supreme People's Assembly; Han Seol-ya and Ko Jun-taek, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly; General Kim Kwang-hyup, Minister of National Defense; Li Hyo-sun, Ha Ang-cheon, Kim Chang-deok, and Li Song-un, members of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly; General Li Kwon-mu, Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army; Lieutenant General Seo Cheol, Director of the General Political Bureau; and Lieutenant General Kim Bong-ryul, Vice-Minister of National Defense.

Also attending the award ceremony was Qiao Xiaoguang, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea.

After Kang Ryang-uk, Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, read out the decree of award, Chairman Choi Yong-kun personally awarded the First Class National Flag Medal to General Yang Yong and General Wang Ping, and the Second Class National Flag Medal to Lieutenant General Liang Biye and Major General Wang Yunrui, Chief of Staff of the Volunteers.

After awarding the medals, Chairman Choi Yong-kun delivered a speech, saying:

"The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea highly praises the glorious achievements you have made in the struggle to defend the freedom, independence, and peace of the East for the Korean people, and awards you the First Class and Second Class National Flag Medals. This is an expression of the Korean people's respect for you." He said that the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteers had established glorious achievements in the arduous struggle against the US imperialist aggressors, which will last forever along with the beautiful mountains and rivers of Korea.

Chairman Choi Yong-kun said: "The common struggle of the people of Korea and China is a concrete manifestation of the unbreakable friendship between the two peoples guided by Marxism-Leninism, and is a model of a new type of state relations established after the Great October Revolution. I would like to take this opportunity to express my sincere and deep gratitude to the great Chinese people, the Chinese Communist Party, and Comrade Mao Zedong, who sent internationalist fighters and provided us with material and spiritual assistance."

General Yang Yong then delivered a speech of thanks. On behalf of all the officers and soldiers of the Volunteers, he expressed his heartfelt thanks to the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly. He said: "Chairman Choi Yong-kun gave a very high evaluation of our work in Korea over the past few years. However, whether it is our individual efforts or the work done and the achievements made by our entire army in Korea, it is inseparable from the care and support of the Korean Workers' Party, the Republic Government, Premier Kim Il-sung, and all the Korean people, and the assistance of the Korean People's Army. The honor should go to the Korean Workers' Party, the Republic Government, Premier Kim Il-sung, and the heroic Korean people."

General Yang Yong said: "We will regard the honor given to us by the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea as a powerful spur to us. In the future, we will continue to contribute all our strength to consolidate and develop the great friendship between the people of China and Korea, and in the struggle against our common enemy and to defend peace in the Far East and the world!" ### 朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会  授予杨勇上将等国旗勋章

1958-10-25
第2版()
专栏：

    朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会
    授予杨勇上将等国旗勋章
    新华社平壤24日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会今天上午十时在平壤举行隆重的授勋仪式，授予中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇上将、政治委员王平上将以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高勋章——一级国旗勋章，并授予志愿军副政治委员梁必业中将等高级指挥员以二级国旗勋章。
    参加授勋仪式的有：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健，最高人民会议议长崔元泽，最高人民会议常任委员会副委员长韩雪野、高准泽，民族保卫相金光侠大将，最高人民会议常任委员会委员李孝淳、河仰天、金昌德、李松云，朝鲜人民军总参谋长李权武上将，总政治局局长徐哲中将，民族保卫省副相金奉律中将等。
    参加授勋仪式的还有我国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光。
    朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会书记长康良煜宣读授勋政令后，崔庸健委员长亲自把一级国旗勋章授予杨勇上将、王平上将，把二级国旗勋章授予梁必业中将和志愿军参谋长王蕴瑞少将。
    崔庸健委员长授勋后致词说：
  “朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会，高度赞扬你们在保卫朝鲜人民的自由、独立和东方和平的斗争中树立的光辉的伟勋，并授予你们以国旗勋章第一级和第二级。这是朝鲜人民尊敬你们的表示”。他说，中国人民志愿军官兵们在反对美帝国主义侵略者的艰苦斗争中，树立了永放光芒的伟勋。它将同朝鲜美丽的河山一起流芳万世。
    崔庸健委员长说：“朝中两国人民的共同斗争，是马克思列宁主义为指南的两国人民之间的牢不可破的友谊的具体表现，是伟大的十月革命以后建立起来的新型国家关系的榜样。我谨借这个机会，向派遣国际主义战士并在物质上精神上帮助我们的伟大的中国人民、中国共产党和毛泽东同志致以真挚深切的谢意。”
    杨勇上将接着致答谢词。他代表志愿军全体官兵，向最高人民会议常任委员会表示衷心的感谢。他说：“崔庸健委员长对我们在朝鲜几年的工作，给予了很高的评价。但是无论是我们个人，无论是我们全军在朝鲜所做的一点工作，所取得的一些成绩，都是同朝鲜劳动党、共和国政府、金日成首相和全体朝鲜人民对我们的关怀和支援，同朝鲜人民军对我们的帮助分不开的。荣誉应归于朝鲜劳动党、共和国政府、金日成首相和英雄的朝鲜人民。”
    杨勇上将说：“朝鲜最高人民会议给予我们的荣誉，我们将看作是对我们有力的鞭策。我们今后将继续为巩固和发展中朝两国人民的伟大友谊，在反对我们的共同敌人和保卫远东与世界和平的斗争中，贡献出自己的一切力量！”（附图片）
    图为崔庸健委员长为王平上将佩带勋章。左为杨勇上将。
