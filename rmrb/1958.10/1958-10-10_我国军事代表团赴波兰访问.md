Title: Chinese Military Delegation Visits Poland
Author:
Date: 1958-10-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 9th.
    At the invitation of the government of the Polish People's Republic, the Chinese military delegation, headed by Marshal Ye Jianying, departed from Beijing by plane today for a friendly visit to Poland and to participate in the celebration of the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Polish People's Army.
    The members of the delegation are: General Lai Chuanzhu, Air Force Lieutenant General Luo Yuanfa, Navy Major General Yuan YeLie, Major General Yu Manyun, and our military attache in Poland, Lieutenant Colonel Jiang Yizhen.
    When the delegation set off, Deputy Minister of National Defense General Huang Kecheng and General Su Yu, General Chen Xilian, Naval Admiral Wang Hongkun, Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, Air Force Lieutenant General Cheng Jun, Lieutenant General Gan Weihan, Lieutenant General Tang Tianji and other senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army went to the airport to see them off.
    Also present at the airport to see them off were the Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish People's Republic in China, Furato, Military Attaché Colonel Hankiewicz, and Burkin, Secretary-General of the Presidium of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association, who came to visit China.

### 我国军事代表团赴波兰访问

1958-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我国军事代表团赴波兰访问
    新华社9日讯　以叶剑英元帅为首的我国军事代表团，应波兰人民共和国政府的邀请，今天从北京乘飞机动身，前往波兰进行友好访问，并参加庆祝波兰人民军十五周年建军节。
    代表团的团员是：赖传珠上将，罗元发空军中将，袁也烈海军少将，喻缦云少将和我国驻波兰武官姜一震中校。
    在代表团启程时，国防部副部长黄克诚大将和粟裕大将、陈锡联上将、王宏坤海军上将、刘志坚中将、成钧空军中将、甘渭汉中将、唐天际中将等中国人民解放军高级将领，前往机场送行。
    到机场送行的，还有波兰人民共和国驻我国临时代办傅拉托，武官汉凯维奇上校，以及来我国访问的波中友好协会总会主席团总书记布尔金等。
