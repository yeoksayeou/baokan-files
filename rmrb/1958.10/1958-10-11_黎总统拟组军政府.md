Title: Lebanese President Plans to Form Military Government
Author:
Date: 1958-10-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 10th -- "Al Anwar" reported that Lebanese President Chehab and Prime Minister Karami reached an agreement on the 9th, deciding that the director of the Prime Minister's Office, Akkari, should organize a military government.

It is reported that Chehab is conducting extensive contacts to persuade all relevant parties to agree to the formation of a military government. According to reports here, Chehab yesterday met separately with the leaders of the National Front, as well as Phalangist leader Gemayel and "Third Force" leader Pharaon, to discuss the issue of forming a military government.

Damascus' "Al-Jamahir" reported today that U.S. Ambassador to Lebanon McClintock met separately with Chehab and Chamoun on the 9th. Although a U.S. embassy spokesman declined to comment on the purpose of the meeting, well-informed sources revealed that the U.S. ambassador discussed Lebanon's domestic crisis and the proposal to establish a military government with Chehab and Chamoun.

### 黎总统拟组军政府

1958-10-11
第5版()
专栏：

    黎总统拟组军政府
    新华社大马士革10日电　“光明报”报道，黎巴嫩总统谢哈布和总理卡拉米在9日达成协议，决定由总理办公厅主任阿卡里组织军政府。
    据悉，谢哈布正在进行广泛的接触，以说服所有有关的人赞同组织军政府。据这里报纸报道，谢哈布昨天分别接见了民族阵线的领袖们，以及长枪党首脑杰马耶勒和“第三力量”领袖法尔拉昂，讨论成立军政府问题。
    大马士革“群众报”今天报道，美国驻黎巴嫩大使麦克林托克在9日分别会见了谢哈布和夏蒙。虽然美国大使馆发言人拒绝说明会谈的目的，但是消息灵通人士透露，美国大使同谢哈布和夏蒙讨论了黎巴嫩的国内危机和成立军政府的建议。
