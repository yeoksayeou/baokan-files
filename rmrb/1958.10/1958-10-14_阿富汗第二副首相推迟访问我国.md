Title: Afghan Second Deputy Prime Minister Postpones Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1958-10-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, 13th - Kabul Radio announced on the evening of the 12th: "In light of recent developments in Pakistan, Second Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Sardar Mohammad Naim has postponed his visit to the People's Republic of China. Naim was originally scheduled to leave Kabul for China on October 14th. It is understood that Naim has notified the Chargé d'Affaires of the People's Republic of China in Kabul of his decision, requesting the Chargé d'Affaires to express his apologies to the Government of the People's Republic of China and assuring that he will visit China, this friendly neighbor, as soon as possible."

### 阿富汗第二副首相推迟访问我国

1958-10-14
第4版()
专栏：

    阿富汗第二副首相推迟访问我国
    新华社13日讯　喀布尔消息：喀布尔电台12日晚上广播说：“鉴于最近巴基斯坦的局势发展，第二副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆推迟了他对中华人民共和国的访问。纳伊姆原定于10月14日离开喀布尔前往中国。据了解纳伊姆已把他的决定通知中华人民共和国驻喀布尔代办，要求代办向中华人民共和国政府表示歉意，并保证尽早访问中国这个友好的邻国。”
