Title: Two More US Warships Invade China's Territorial Waters; 35th Serious Warning Issued
Author:
Date: 1958-10-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. On October 19th, two U.S. destroyers again intruded into the territorial waters of the Pingtan and Matsu areas of Fujian Province. Regarding the continued military provocations by the United States, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 35th serious warning.

### 美舰两艘又侵入我国领海  我第35次提出严重警告

1958-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

    美舰两艘又侵入我国领海
    我第35次提出严重警告
    新华社19日讯　10月19日，美国驱逐舰两艘又侵入我福建省平潭、马祖地区的领海。对于美国继续进行军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命第三十五次提出严重警告。
