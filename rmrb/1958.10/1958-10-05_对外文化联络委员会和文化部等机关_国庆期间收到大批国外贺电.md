Title: Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, Ministry of Culture and Other Agencies Receive Large Numbers of Foreign Congratulatory Telegrams During National Day

Author:

Date: 1958-10-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. During the National Day holiday, the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, the Ministry of Culture and other agencies and organizations received many telegrams from abroad congratulating the ninth anniversary of the founding of our country. The telegrams congratulated the great achievements made by the Chinese people in socialist construction and the cause of defending world peace. The Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee received congratulatory telegrams from the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Korean Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Mongolian Bureau for Foreign Cultural Relations, the Vietnamese Bureau for Foreign Cultural Relations, the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, the Romanian Association for Foreign Cultural Relations, the Polish Bureau for Foreign Cultural Relations, the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association, the Icelandic-Chinese Cultural Association, the Finnish-Chinese Association, the Greek-Chinese Friendship Association, and the Belgium-China Friendship Association.
In addition, the Ministry of Culture received a congratulatory telegram from the Ministry of Culture and Art of the People's Republic of Poland, the Chinese Writers Association received a congratulatory telegram from the Preparatory Committee of the Asian-African Writers Conference, and the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce received a congratulatory telegram from the Central Council of the Trade Union of Workers in Trade Cooperation of Romania.

### 对外文化联络委员会和文化部等机关  国庆期间收到大批国外贺电

1958-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

    对外文化联络委员会和文化部等机关
    国庆期间收到大批国外贺电
    新华社4日讯　国庆节期间，对外文化联络委员会、文化部等机关、团体收到了许多来自国外祝贺我国建国九周年的电报。贺电中都祝贺我国人民在社会主义建设和保卫世界和平事业中所取得的巨大成就。对外文化联络委员会收到了苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会、朝鲜对外文化联络协会、蒙古对外文化联络局、越南对外文化联络局、越南越中友好协会、罗马尼亚对外文化联络协会、波兰对外文化联络局、波兰波中友好协会、冰岛冰中文化协会、芬兰芬中协会、希腊希中友好协会和比利时——中国友好协会的贺电。
    此外，文化部收到了波兰人民共和国文化艺术部的贺电，中国作家协会收到了亚非作家代表会议筹备委员会的贺电，中华全国工商业联合会收到了罗马尼亚贸易合作工作者工会中央理事会的贺电。
