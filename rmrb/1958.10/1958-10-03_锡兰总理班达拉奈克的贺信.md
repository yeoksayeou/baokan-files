Title: Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike's Congratulatory Message
Author: 
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

To His Excellency Mr. Zhou Enlai, Premier of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of the ninth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I, on behalf of the government and people of Ceylon, and in my own name, extend warm greetings and sincere good wishes to Your Excellency and, through you, to the government and people of the People's Republic of China.

                                      S.W.R.D. Bandaranaike
                                      Prime Minister of Ceylon

### 锡兰总理班达拉奈克的贺信
班达拉奈克
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    锡兰总理班达拉奈克的贺信中华人民共和国总理周恩来先生阁下：
    在中华人民共和国建国九周年的时候，我代表锡兰政府和人民，并且以我个人的名义，向阁下并且通过阁下向中华人民共和国政府和人民热烈致意并表示诚挚的良好愿望。
                                      锡兰总理　班达拉奈克
