Title: Sino-US Talks Continued Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1958-10-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, October 15th: The 81st meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks was held at the Myśliwiecki Palace in Warsaw at 3:00 p.m. today. The meeting lasted two hours and fifteen minutes. Afterwards, both sides announced that the next meeting will continue on Thursday, October 23rd, at 2:00 p.m.

### 中美会谈昨天继续举行

1958-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

    中美会谈昨天继续举行
    新华社华沙15日电　中美两国大使级会谈第八十一次会议，今天下午三时在华沙梅希里维茨基宫举行。本次会议历时两小时十五分。会后双方宣布，下次会议将在10月23日（星期四）下午二时继续举行。
