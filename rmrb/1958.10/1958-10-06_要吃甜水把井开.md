Title: If You Want Sweet Water, Dig a Well
Author: 
Date: 1958-10-06
Page: 3

New Folk Song Selection

    If you want sweet water, dig a well,
    If you want fresh fruit, plant a tree;
    If you want a good life,
    Build the commune.
        ——Inner Mongolia

### 要吃甜水把井开

1958-10-06
第3版()
专栏：新民歌选

    要吃甜水把井开
    要吃甜水把井开，
    要吃鲜果把树栽；
    要想日子过的美，
    要把公社建起来。
        ——内蒙古
