Title: International Federation of Women Issues Statement Condemning U.S. Aggression Against China, Calls on Women of All Countries to Demand U.S. Troops Withdraw from Taiwan Strait Area
Author:
Date: 1958-10-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, October 10th: The Secretariat of the Women's International Democratic Federation issued a statement to the press, angrily condemning the United States' aggression against the People's Republic of China. The statement said that those forces attempting to obstruct the Arab people from achieving national independence are today openly interfering in the internal affairs of the Chinese people.

The Women's International Democratic Federation called on women of all countries to demand that the U.S. armed forces withdraw from the Taiwan Strait area, and to demand that their governments implement policies of peaceful coexistence and disarmament. The Women's International Democratic Federation demanded that the great Chinese people be given their legitimate place in the United Nations.

In a letter specifically addressed to Chinese women, the Women's International Democratic Federation assured Chinese women and the Chinese people of its full support.

### 国际妇联发表声明谴责美国侵略中国  号召各国妇女要美军撤出台湾海峡地区

1958-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    国际妇联发表声明谴责美国侵略中国
    号召各国妇女要美军撤出台湾海峡地区
    新华社柏林10日电　国际民主妇女联合会书记处向报界发表声明，愤怒地谴责美国对中华人民共和国的侵略。声明说，那些企图阻挠阿拉伯人民取得民族独立的势力今天公开地干涉中国人民的内政。
    国际民主妇联号召各国的妇女要求美国的武装力量撤出台湾海峡地区，要求本国政府实行和平共处和裁军的政策。国际民主妇联要求给予伟大的中国人民在联合国中的合法地位。
    在专门发给中国妇女的信件中，国际民主妇联向中国妇女和中国人民保证给以完全的支持。
