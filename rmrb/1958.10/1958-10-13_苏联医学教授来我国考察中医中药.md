Title: Soviet Medical Professors Come to China to Investigate Traditional Chinese Medicine
Author:
Date: 1958-10-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: In order to implement the 1958 plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Professor Nikolai Alexandrovich Talsuyev, director of the Department of Skin and Venereal Diseases of the Rostov Medical College of the Soviet Union, and two others arrived in Beijing on the 9th. They will go to various places in our country to understand and investigate the treatment of traditional Chinese medicine, leprosy, and the prevention and treatment of leprosy in our country.

### 苏联医学教授来我国考察中医中药

1958-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联医学教授来我国考察中医中药
    新华社11日讯　为执行中苏文化合作协定1958年计划，苏联罗斯托夫医学院皮肤性病教研组主任尼古莱·阿列克山大罗维奇·塔尔苏也夫教授等三人，于9日到京。他们在我国将赴各地了解和考察中医中药治疗、麻风及我国麻风病防治工作等情况。
