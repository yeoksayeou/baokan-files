Title: Demanding Higher Wages: 100,000 Italian Transportation Workers on General Strike
Author:
Date: 1958-10-22
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rome, 20th - According to TASS, Rome, October 20th: A total of 100,000 streetcar, bus, trolleybus, and local railway workers in Italy began a 24-hour national general strike today.
    The strike was announced after lengthy negotiations between the unions in the aforementioned industries and the administrative authorities failed to produce results. The striking workers are demanding a 7% wage increase. The strike is being carried out with extreme unity throughout the country.
    In Milan, municipal transportation workers began their strike on the morning of the 19th, having decided to hold a 48-hour strike.
    Workers in Italian marble quarries also began a three-day national strike on the 20th to protest the capitalists' cessation of negotiations on the conclusion of a new national labor contract.

### 要求提高工资  意十万运输工人总罢工

1958-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    要求提高工资
    意十万运输工人总罢工
    新华社21日讯　据塔斯社罗马20日讯：意大利的电车、公共汽车、无轨电车职工和地方铁路的部分职工共十万人20日开始举行二十四小时的全国总罢工。
    罢工是在上述各行业的工会同行政管理当局进行了长时期谈判而无结果之后宣布的。罢工职工要求提高工资7％。罢工是在全国极其团结的情况下进行的。
    在米兰，市运输业的职工是在19日早上开始罢工的，因为他们决定举行四十八小时的罢工。
    意大利大理石采石场的工人也在20日开始举行全国性的为期三天的罢工，抗议资方停止关于缔结新的全国劳动合同的谈判。
