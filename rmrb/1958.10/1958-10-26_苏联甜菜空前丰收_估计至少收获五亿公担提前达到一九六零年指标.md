Title: Unprecedented Bumper Harvest of Sugar Beets in the Soviet Union - Estimated Harvest of at Least 500 Million Quintals, Achieving the 1960 Target Ahead of Schedule

Author:

Date: 1958-10-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 24th, TASS: As of October 20th, the Soviet Union had harvested over 447 million quintals of sugar beets nationwide. This is the largest harvest in Soviet history.

This year, the amount of sugar beets delivered to purchasing stations is 75 million quintals more than the same period last year.

According to TASS reporters from the Soviet Ministry of Agriculture: The Soviet Union estimates that it can harvest at least 500 million quintals of sugar beets this year, achieving the 1960 level ahead of schedule. Look! What big sugar beets!

### 苏联甜菜空前丰收  估计至少收获五亿公担提前达到一九六零年指标

1958-10-26
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联甜菜空前丰收
    估计至少收获五亿公担提前达到一九六零年指标
    新华社25日讯　塔斯社莫斯科24日讯：到10月20日为止，苏联全国收获的甜菜已达四亿四千七百万公担以上。这是苏联有史以来的最大一次丰收。
    今年运到各采购站的甜菜比去年同时期多七千五百万公担。
    据塔斯社记者从苏联农业部获悉：苏联今年估计至少可以收获五亿公担甜菜，而提前达到1960年的水平。瞧！多么大的甜菜啊！
