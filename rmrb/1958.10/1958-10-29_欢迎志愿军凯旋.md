Title: Welcoming the Victorious Return of the Chinese People's Volunteers
Author: Li Jishen
Date: 1958-10-29
Page: 8

    Welcoming the Victorious Return of the Chinese People's Volunteers
    Li Jishen
    After eight years of the Long March,
    A triumphant return is now at hand.
    A reputation that astounds the universe,
    Achievements that fill the human realm.
    Resisting America extinguished fierce flames,
    Aiding Korea protected vulnerable teeth.
    Returning to display your prowess,
    Surely predicting victory among soldiers.
