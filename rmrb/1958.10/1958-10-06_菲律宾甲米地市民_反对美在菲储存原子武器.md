Title: Citizens of Cavite, Philippines, Oppose U.S. Storage of Atomic Weapons in the Philippines
Author:
Date: 1958-10-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 4th: Manila News: According to a report in the "Manila Chronicle" on October 3rd, the Civil Defense Committee of Cavite City, Philippines, passed a resolution on October 1st opposing the storage of atomic weapons by the United States at the U.S. military base at Sangley Point in the Philippines.

Reports say that the United States has stored atomic weapons at Sangley Point and has built missile launch pads at the base.

Alvarez, the head of civil defense in Cavite, admitted that he knew about this news and expressed concern that this would put Cavite City in danger.

It is reported that the Civil Defense Committee has assigned Alvarez to try to hold talks with the U.S. Navy authorities at Sangley Point to resolve this issue.

### 菲律宾甲米地市民  反对美在菲储存原子武器

1958-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

    菲律宾甲米地市民
    反对美在菲储存原子武器
    据新华社4日讯　马尼拉消息：据“马尼拉纪事报”3日报道，菲律宾甲米地市的民防委员会10月1日通过了一个决议，反对美国在菲律宾桑莱岬的美国军事基地储存原子武器。
    有消息说，美国在桑莱岬储存了原子武器，并且在基地上修建了导弹发射台。
    甲米地的民防负责人阿尔瓦雷斯承认他知道了这样的消息，他表示担心这将使甲米地市处于危险中。
    据报道，民防委员会已经指派阿尔瓦雷斯设法同桑莱岬美国海军当局举行会谈来解决这个问题。
