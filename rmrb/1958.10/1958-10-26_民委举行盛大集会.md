Title: Nationalities Affairs Commission Holds Grand Gathering
Author:
Date: 1958-10-26
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 25th: The Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China held a grand gathering at the Central Institute for Nationalities this evening to celebrate the establishment of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region. More than 1,000 people attended the meeting, including representatives from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Beijing, as well as students and staff from various organizations.

    The meeting was presided over by Sa Kongliao, Vice Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission.

    Xie Hechou, Vice Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, warmly congratulated the establishment of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region at the meeting. He said: Our country is now in the great historical period of a comprehensive leap forward in socialist construction, and the establishment of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region at this time is of even greater significance. We wish the people of all ethnic groups in the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, greater achievements in socialist construction and ethnic unity, to develop the region's economy and cultural undertakings at the fastest speed, and to make greater contributions to the socialist and communist construction of the motherland.

### 民委举行盛大集会

1958-10-26
第2版()
专栏：

    民委举行盛大集会
    新华社25日讯　中华人民共和国民族事务委员会今晚在中央民族学院举行盛大集会，庆祝宁夏回族自治区成立。参加大会的有在北京的各民族的各界人士，学生、机关工作人员的代表，共一千多人。
    大会由民族事务委员会副主任萨空了主持。
    民族事务委员会副主任谢鹤筹在会上热烈祝贺宁夏回族自治区的成立。他说：现在我国正处在社会主义建设全面大跃进的伟大历史时期，在这个时候成立宁夏回族自治区更有着重大的意义。我们预祝宁夏回族自治区各族人民，在中国共产党和毛主席的领导下，在社会主义建设和民族团结的事业上获得更大的成绩，以最快的速度发展自治区的经济和文化事业，并为祖国的社会主义和共产主义建设作出更大的贡献。
