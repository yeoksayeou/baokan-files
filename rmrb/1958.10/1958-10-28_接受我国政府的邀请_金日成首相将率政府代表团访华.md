Title: Premier Kim Il-sung to Lead Government Delegation to Visit China at the Invitation of Our Government
Author:
Date: 1958-10-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 27th:

    At the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, Premier Kim Il-sung will lead a government delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to visit China. The Korean government and Premier Kim Il-sung have accepted this invitation. Premier Zhou Enlai's letter of invitation and Premier Kim Il-sung's letter of reply are as follows:

    Premier Zhou Enlai's Letter of Invitation
    To the Honorable Premier Kim Il-sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea:

    I extend a warm invitation, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, for you to lead a government delegation to visit our country. We would be greatly honored by your visit.

    At a time when the socialist construction endeavors in both China and Korea are advancing rapidly and the friendly cooperation between our two peoples is developing comprehensively, I am deeply convinced that your visit will further strengthen the unity and friendship between our two countries and contribute to our common cause of safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.

    Honorable Premier, the people of our country and I eagerly await your visit with great pleasure.

    Please accept my highest respects.

                          Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                                            September 24, 1958

    Premier Kim Il-sung's Reply Letter
    To the Respected Premier Zhou Enlai:

    I am honored to inform you that the government of our country and myself are very pleased to accept your invitation for a government delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by myself, to visit the People's Republic of China.

   On behalf of the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Korean people, I express my heartfelt gratitude to you and, through you, to the Chinese government and people for your invitation.

    Premier, I am confident that this visit to the People's Republic of China by our government delegation, like your visit to our country in February this year, will further strengthen and develop the traditional friendly and cooperative relations established between the peoples of Korea and China.

    Please accept my most profound respects.

                    Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea
                                         October 3, 1958

### 接受我国政府的邀请  金日成首相将率政府代表团访华

1958-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    接受我国政府的邀请
    金日成首相将率政府代表团访华
    新华社27日讯　中华人民共和国政府邀请金日成首相率领朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团访问中国。朝鲜政府和金日成首相已经接受了这一邀请。周恩来总理的邀请信和金日成首相的复信如下：
    周恩来总理的邀请信朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相敬爱的金日成同志：
    我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和中国人民热诚地邀请你率领政府代表团访问我国。你的来访将使我们感到莫大的荣幸。
  在中朝两国的社会主义建设事业都在突飞猛进、两国人民的友好合作正在全面发展的时期，我深信，你的访问，必将进一步加强我们两国之间的团结和友谊，并且对于我们维护亚洲和世界和平的共同事业作出贡献。
    敬爱的首相同志，我和我国人民怀着十分愉快的心情期待着你的来访。
    谨向你致以崇高的敬意。
                          中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                            1958年9月24日
    金日成首相的复信尊敬的周恩来总理同志：
    我谨荣幸地通知您，我国政府和我自己非常高兴地接受了您邀请以我为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团到中华人民共和国进行访问。
    对于您的邀请，我谨代表朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和朝鲜人民向您并通过您向中国政府和人民表示衷心的感谢。
    总理同志，我确信我国政府代表团这次访问中华人民共和国，将和今年2月您来我国的访问一样，进一步加强和发展朝中两国人民之间结成的传统的友好和合作关系。
    谨向您致我的最崇高的敬意。
                    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相　金日成
                                         1958年10月3日
