Title: The Soviet Union's Communist Youth League is a Model for Chinese Youth; Our Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Cables Congratulations on the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Union's Communist Youth League; Capital Youth Gather to Celebrate the Further Strengthening of Sino-Soviet Youth Friendship
Author:
Date: 1958-10-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 28th -- The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League sent a telegram on October 27th to the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union, warmly congratulating the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Union's Communist Youth League.

The telegram stated that the glorious road of struggle experienced by the Soviet Union's Communist Youth League over the past forty years eloquently demonstrates that the Soviet Union's Communist Youth League is worthy of being the loyal assistant and combat reserve force of the great Soviet Communist Party, and has become one of the most active forces in building and defending the Soviet Union.

The telegram stated that we have always regarded the Soviet Union's Communist Youth League as a model for our own learning, and we sincerely thank the great Soviet Union for its selfless assistance to our socialist construction and its firm support for us in the struggle against the war provocations of U.S. imperialism.

The telegram concluded by saying that Chinese youth, like Soviet youth, are filled with boundless confidence in the cause of communism. Chinese youth pledge to strive to continuously consolidate and develop the great unity and friendship between China and the Soviet Union.

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th -- Nearly two thousand youths in the capital gathered this evening to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union.

After Wang Zhaohua, First Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, delivered the opening remarks, Liu Xiyuan, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, spoke at the meeting. Liu Xiyuan called on Chinese youth to learn from the excellent qualities of Soviet youth and to study Marxism-Leninism and the works of Comrade Mao Zedong diligently, to train themselves to become cultured laborers with high socialist consciousness. Finally, he congratulated the people and youth of China and the Soviet Union on uniting even more closely, jointly striving for lasting world peace and the cause of socialism, and advancing hand in hand toward the great goal of communism.

Grovon, Second Secretary of the Soviet Embassy in China, also spoke at the meeting. He described the combat path taken by the Soviet Union's Communist Youth League over the past forty years. He represented eighteen million members of the Soviet Union's Communist Youth League and Soviet youth experts working in China in congratulating the Chinese people and youth on their new achievements in the cause of building socialism.

Zhang Baifa and Cui Guangli, representatives of workers and peasants in the capital, also spoke at the meeting, expressing warm congratulations on the 40th anniversary of the founding of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union.

### 苏联共青团是中国青年的榜样  我团中央电贺苏共青团成立四十周年  首都青年集会祝中苏青年友谊更加强

1958-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联共青团是中国青年的榜样
    我团中央电贺苏共青团成立四十周年
    首都青年集会祝中苏青年友谊更加强
    新华社28日讯　中国共产主义青年团中央委员会27日致电苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会，热烈地祝贺苏联共产主义青年团成立四十周年。
    贺电说，苏联共青团四十年来所经历的战斗的光荣道路，雄辩地显示了苏联共青团不愧为伟大的苏联共产党的忠实助手和战斗后备军，成为建设和保卫苏联的一支最积极的力量。
    贺电说，我们始终以苏联共青团作为自己学习的榜样，我们衷心地感谢伟大苏联对我国社会主义建设事业的无私援助，在反对美帝国主义战争挑衅的斗争中给我们的坚决支持。
    贺电最后说，中国青年和苏联青年一样对共产主义事业充满着无限的信心。中国青年誓将为不断巩固和发展中苏两国伟大的团结和友谊而努力。
    新华社27日讯　首都近两千青年今晚集会，庆祝苏联列宁共产主义青年团建团四十周年。
    共青团北京市委第一书记王照华致开幕词后，共青团中央书记处书记刘西元在大会上讲话。刘西元号召我国青年应该学习苏联青年的优秀品质，应该努力学习马列主义和毛泽东同志的著作，把自己锻炼成为具有高度社会主义觉悟的有文化的劳动者。最后他祝贺中苏两国人民和青年更加亲密地团结起来，为世界持久和平和社会主义事业共同奋斗，并肩携手地向着共产主义的伟大目标胜利前进。
    苏联驻我国大使馆二等秘书葛洛文也在会上讲话。他叙述了苏联共青团在过去四十年内所走过的战斗道路。他代表一千八百万苏联共青团员与在华工作的苏联青年专家祝贺中国人民和青年在建设社会主义的事业中获得新的成就。
    首都工农青年代表张百发和崔广利也在会上讲话对苏联列宁共产主义青年团建团四十周年表示热烈祝贺。
