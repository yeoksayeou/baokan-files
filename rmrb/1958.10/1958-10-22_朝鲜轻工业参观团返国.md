Title: North Korean Light Industry Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1958-10-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Andong, October 21st. The North Korean Light Industry Delegation, headed by Mun Man-yuk, Minister of Light Industry of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, concluded its visit to our country and returned home by train from Andong this morning.

Duan Yongjie, Secretary of the Andong Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Mayor of Andong, and Xing Yihang, Deputy Director of the Basic Construction Department of the Ministry of Light Industry of our country, and Chen Ying, Director of the Liaoning Provincial Light Industry Bureau, who accompanied the delegation on its visit to Andong, were at the station to see them off. The delegation arrived in our country on August 28 and visited more than ten cities.

### 朝鲜轻工业参观团返国

1958-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜轻工业参观团返国
    新华社安东21日电　以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国轻工业相文万郁为首的朝鲜轻工业参观团，已结束在我国的参观访问，今天上午由安东乘火车回国。
    中共安东市委书记、安东市市长段永杰和陪同参观团来安东参观的我国轻工业部基本建设司副司长邢贻行、辽宁省轻工业局局长陈英等人到车站欢送。参观团从8月28日到达我国，曾在十多个城市进行了参观访问。
