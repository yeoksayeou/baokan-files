Title: US Atomic Bomb Leaves Endless Trouble: Another Japanese Youth Killed
Author:
Date: 1958-10-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo: A tragedy involving a victim of the American atomic bomb has recently occurred again in Hiroshima. A Japanese youth, injured by the American atomic bomb, committed suicide by taking poison and left behind an anti-atomic bomb suicide note.

The youth's name was Yukimori Noguchi, and he committed suicide at home on the morning of October 25 by taking sleeping pills.

### 美国原子弹遗患累累  日本又一青年被害死

1958-10-30
第5版()
专栏：

    美国原子弹遗患累累
    日本又一青年被害死
    据新华社讯　东京消息：广岛最近又发生一件美国原子弹受害者的悲剧。一位遭到美国原子弹伤害的日本青年服毒自杀，并且留下了一封反对原子弹的遗书。
    这个青年名叫野口幸守，他在10月25日早晨在家里吃安眠药自杀。
