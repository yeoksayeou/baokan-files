Title: Polish Delegation Meets with Soviet Leaders
Author:
Date: 1958-10-28
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 27th. Polish leaders who are in the Soviet Union for a friendly visit met with Soviet leaders in the Kremlin this morning. The Polish side participating in the meeting included: Gomulka, Zawadzki, Cyrankiewicz, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Naszkowski, and others.
    The Soviet leaders present were Voroshilov, Kirichenko, Mikoyan, Suslov, and Khrushchev. Today, the delegation of the Polish People's Republic, headed by Gomulka, also paid respects at the Lenin and Stalin Mausoleum and laid wreaths.
    The Polish Embassy in the Soviet Union held a luncheon today for the delegation's visit to the Soviet Union. Soviet leaders were invited to attend the luncheon.

### 波代表团会见苏联领导人

1958-10-28
第5版()
专栏：

    波代表团会见苏联领导人
    新华社莫斯科27日电　前来苏联进行友好访问的波兰领导人今天早晨在克里姆林宫会见苏联领导人。波兰方面参加会见的是：哥穆尔卡、萨瓦茨基、西伦凯维兹，外交部副部长纳希科夫斯基等。
    苏联领导人在座的有伏罗希洛夫、基里钦科、米高扬、苏斯洛夫和赫鲁晓夫。今天，以哥穆尔卡为首的波兰人民共和国代表团还拜谒了列宁和斯大林陵墓，并献了花圈。
    波兰驻苏联大使馆今天为代表团访苏举行了早宴。苏联领导人应邀出席了早宴。
