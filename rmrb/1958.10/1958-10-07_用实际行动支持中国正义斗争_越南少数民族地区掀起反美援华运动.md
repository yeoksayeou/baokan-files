Title: Supporting China's Just Struggle with Practical Actions: Anti-American and Aid-China Movement Sweeps Through Vietnamese Ethnic Minority Areas
Author:
Date: 1958-10-07
Page: 4

    Using Practical Actions to Support China's Just Struggle
    Anti-American and Aid-China Movement Sweeps Through Vietnamese Ethnic Minority Areas
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 6th – The movement in North Vietnam against American imperialism’s interference in China’s internal affairs and in support of the Chinese people's liberation of Taiwan has now spread to ethnic minority areas.
    On the 5th, 5,500 residents of various ethnic groups, including the Nung and Yao, as well as local garrison troops, government officials, and student representatives, held an anti-American and Aid-China rally in Thai Nguyen city in the Viet Bac Autonomous Region. At the meeting, ethnic minority people dressed in their various national costumes enthusiastically shouted slogans such as "American Imperialism, Get Out of Taiwan Immediately!" The meeting passed a resolution expressing firm support for the just struggle of the Chinese people. After the meeting, the participants also held a demonstration parade in the city.
    In the Lao Cai-Ha Giang-Yen Bai area, the number of people of all ethnic groups who have participated in anti-American and Aid-China rallies has recently reached more than 20,000. Those who participated in the rallies all guaranteed to support the Chinese people's struggle with practical actions.

### 用实际行动支持中国正义斗争  越南少数民族地区掀起反美援华运动

1958-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    用实际行动支持中国正义斗争
    越南少数民族地区掀起反美援华运动
    新华社河内6日电　越南北方反对美帝国主义干涉中国内政和支持中国人民解放台湾的运动目前已经扩展到少数民族地区。
    越北自治区侬族、寨族等各族居民和当地驻军、机关干部、学生代表等五千五百人，5日在太原市举行了反美援华集会。会上身穿各种民族服装的少数民族人民激昂地高呼“美帝国主义立即滚出台湾”等口号。会议通过了决议，表示坚决支持中国人民的正义斗争。会后，与会者还在市内举行了示威游行。
    在老街—河江—安沛地区，最近先后参加反美援华集会的各族人民已经达到二万多人。参加集会的人们都保证要以实际行动来支援中国人民的斗争。
