Title: Korean Experimental Plot Yields 6,000 Jin of Rice Per Mu
Author:
Date: 1958-10-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 13th: The Unbuk Agricultural Cooperative in Unjon County, North Pyongan Province, Korea, has created a record yield of 6,000 jin of rice per mu in an experimental plot. This is unprecedented in Korea.

According to the "Rodong Sinmun" report, on a 200-pyeong (equivalent to one Chinese mu) experimental plot, the agricultural cooperative applied 5,000 jin of compost and 15 jin of chemical fertilizer, deep plowed to 20 centimeters, and implemented close planting. After harvesting on September 26th, a total of 6,000 jin of rice was threshed. According to Korea's consistent method of calculating unit area yield, this translates to 45 tons of rice per chongbo (approximately 15 Chinese mu).

### 朝鲜一亩试验田亩产稻谷六千斤

1958-10-18
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜一亩试验田亩产稻谷六千斤
    据新华社平壤13日电　朝鲜平安北道云田郡云北农业社创造了亩产六千斤水稻的丰产纪录。这在朝鲜是空前未有的。
    据“劳动新闻”报道，这个农业社在二百坪（合一华亩）的试验田上，施堆肥五千斤，化肥十五斤，深耕二十公分，实行密植，在9月26日收割以后共打出了六千斤稻谷。按朝鲜单位面积产量一贯的计算法，折合每町步（约合十五市亩）产稻谷四十五吨。
