Title: Welcoming the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Delegation to Andong
Author:
Date: 1958-10-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Andong, October 25th: The Chinese people welcomed the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Returning Delegation, which arrived in Andong this morning by special train, led by head of the delegation Liao Chengzhi. The No. 1 Troupe of the China Peking Opera Institute, accompanying the delegation, also arrived on the same train.

### 欢迎志愿军代表团到安东

1958-10-26
第2版()
专栏：

    欢迎志愿军代表团到安东
    新华社安东25日电　中国人民欢迎志愿军归国代表团，在团长廖承志率领下，今天上午乘专车到达安东。随团的中国京剧院第一团也同车到达。
