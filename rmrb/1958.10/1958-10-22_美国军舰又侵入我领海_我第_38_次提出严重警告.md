Title: U.S. Warships Again Invade Our Territorial Waters - Our 38th Serious Warning

Author:
Date: 1958-10-22
Page: 1

    U.S. Warships Again Invade Our Territorial Waters
    Our 38th Serious Warning
    Xinhua News Agency, October 21st. On October 21st, two more U.S. warships intruded into the territorial waters of the Ma-tsu and Pingtan areas of Fujian Province. Regarding this military provocation by the United States, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 38th serious warning.

### 美国军舰又侵入我领海  我第38次提出严重警告

1958-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军舰又侵入我领海
    我第38次提出严重警告
    新华社21日讯　10月21日又有美国军舰两艘，侵入我国福建省马祖、平潭地区的领海。对于美国的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命第三十八次提出严重警告。
