Title: China's First Large-Scale Oil Refinery, Lanzhou Refinery, Produces Oil
Author:
Date: 1958-10-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency: China's first large-scale refinery, the Lanzhou Refinery, has basically completed its first phase of construction, and on September 27th, it refined its first batch of kerosene, diesel, and gasoline. The plant is now undergoing trial operation and will produce tens of thousands of tons of various petroleum products this year.

The Lanzhou Refinery is China's first large-scale, modern refinery. Its design, construction, and main machinery and equipment were all assisted by the Soviet Union. The entire production process is mechanized and automated. The construction of the plant began in April 1956 and was originally scheduled to go into production by the end of 1959. (Attached picture)

### 我国第一个大型炼油厂兰州炼油厂出油

1958-10-09
第2版()
专栏：

    我国第一个大型炼油厂兰州炼油厂出油
    据新华社讯　兰州炼油厂第一期工程已经基本建成，并在9月27日炼出了第一批煤油、柴油和汽油。现在这个厂正在试运转，今年内就将生产出若干万吨各类石油产品。
    兰州炼油厂是我国第一个大型的现代化的炼油厂，它的设计、施工和主要的机器设备，都是苏联帮助解决的。生产过程全部是机械化和自动化。这个厂是1956年4月开工兴建的，原计划1959年年底投入生产。（附图片）
