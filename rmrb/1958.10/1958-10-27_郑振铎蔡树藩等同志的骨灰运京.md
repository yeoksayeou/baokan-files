Title: The Ashes of Comrades Zheng Zhenduo, Cai Shufan, and Others Transported to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-10-27
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 26th: The ashes of Comrades Zheng Zhenduo, Cai Shufan, and the other sixteen comrades who died in the plane crash, escorted by Yermasov, Deputy Director of the International Transportation Bureau of the Soviet Civil Aviation Administration, and Zhang Weilie, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, were flown back to Beijing from Moscow by special plane this afternoon.

Those who went to Beijing Nanyuan Airport to meet the ashes included Zhang Xiruo, Shen Yanbing, Chu Tunan, Zhang Hanfu, Zeng Yongquan, Li Da, Zhang Zhixiang, Qu Wu, Xia Yan, Chen Kehan, Wu Faxian, Lin Haiyun, Rong Gaotang, Huang Zhong, Lu Ping, Sa Kongliao, Zhao Guangyuan, Shen Tu, and others.

Antonov, the Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Union in China, and Sudalikov, the Counselor, also went to the airport to meet the ashes.

Also at the airport to meet the ashes were the family members and relatives of the sixteen comrades, including Zheng Zhenduo and Cai Shufan.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 25th: The ashes of all Chinese personnel who died in the "Tu-104" passenger plane crash on October 17 in the Kanash region of the Chuvash Autonomous Soviet Socialist Republic were flown back to Beijing from Moscow today by a "Tu-104" aircraft dispatched by the Soviet side.

Those who went to the airport to express their condolences and see them off included: Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Charubin, Director of the Far East Department Zimianin, Deputy Director of the Civil Aviation Administration of the Soviet Council of Ministers Zhevlonkov, and staff members of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Liu Xiao, the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and the staff of the Embassy also went to the airport to express their condolences and see them off.

Yermasov, Deputy Director of the International Transportation Bureau of the Soviet Civil Aviation Administration, and Zhang Weilie, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, escorted the ashes to Beijing.

Before the ashes were transported, those who came to see them off paid their respects to the ashes of all the deceased Chinese comrades at the airport. (Attached picture)

Zhang Xiruo, Shen Yanbing, and Antonov, the Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Union in China, silently paid their respects before the ashes of the sixteen martyrs, including Zheng Zhenduo, who died in the plane crash. (Xinhua News Agency Reporter Tagigule photograph)

### 郑振铎蔡树藩等同志的骨灰运京

1958-10-27
第2版()
专栏：

    郑振铎蔡树藩等同志的骨灰运京
    新华社26日电　因飞机失事而牺牲的郑振铎、蔡树藩等十六位同志的骨灰，今天下午在苏联民航总局国际交通局副局长叶尔马索夫和我国驻苏联大使馆参赞张伟烈护送下，从莫斯科由专机运回北京。
    前往北京南苑机场迎接骨灰的，有张奚若、沈雁冰、楚图南、章汉夫、曾涌泉、李达、张致祥、屈武、夏衍、陈克寒、吴法宪、林海云、荣高棠、黄中、陆平、萨空了、赵光远、沈图等。
    苏联驻中国临时代办安东诺夫，参赞苏达利柯夫等也到机场迎接。
    到机场迎接的，还有郑振铎、蔡树藩等十六位同志的家属和亲友。
    新华社莫斯科25日电　由于“图——一○四”客机失事而于10月17日在苏联楚瓦什苏维埃社会主义自治共和国的卡纳什地区遇难的中国全体人员的骨灰，在今天由苏联方面派“图——一○四”飞机从莫斯科运回北京。
    前往机场致哀送别的有：苏联外交部副部长查鲁宾、远东司司长齐米亚宁，苏联部长会议民用航空总局副局长热沃隆科夫和苏联外交部工作人员。
    中国驻苏联大使刘晓和大使馆工作人员也前往机场致哀送别。
    护送骨灰前往北京的有苏联民用航空总局国际交通局副局长叶尔马索夫和中国驻苏联大使馆参赞张伟烈。
    在骨灰启运以前，送别的人们曾经在机场上向中国全体遇难同志的骨灰致哀。（附图片）
    张奚若、沈雁冰、苏联驻中国临时代办安东诺夫在因飞机失事而牺牲的郑振铎等十六位烈士骨灰前静默致哀。　新华社记者　塔吉古勒摄
