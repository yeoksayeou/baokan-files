Title: Soviet Embassy Holds Song and Dance Reception, Premier Zhou and Others Attend

Author:

Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd October: The Soviet Embassy in China held a song and dance reception this evening. The Soviet Azerbaijan National Song and Dance Ensemble from Azerbaijan, the "Land of Song and Dance," performed wonderful programs. Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Wu Yuzhang, and Zhang Wentian, Zhang Zhizhong, Shi Liang, Zhang Hanfu, Qian Junrui, Xia Yan, Zhang Zhixiang, Yang Hansheng, and others were invited to watch the performance.

The Soviet Azerbaijan National Song and Dance Ensemble, which has been awarded the title of "Azerbaijan Honored Collective," performed twenty-four programs at this evening's reception, including the "Oil Workers' Suite," "Shepherd's Dance," and "Motherland Suite." The performance of each program, full of strong national style, won enthusiastic applause from the audience.

### 苏使馆举行歌舞招待会  周总理等应邀出席

1958-10-03
第4版()
专栏：

    苏使馆举行歌舞招待会
    周总理等应邀出席
    据新华社2日讯　苏联驻中国大使馆今晚举行歌舞招待会，由来自“歌舞之乡”——阿塞拜疆的苏联阿塞拜疆国家歌舞团演出了精采节目。周恩来总理、陈毅副总理、中苏友好协会总会吴玉章副会长和张闻天、张治中、史良、章汉夫、钱俊瑞、夏衍、张致祥、阳翰笙等应邀观看了演出。
    曾经荣获“阿塞拜疆功勋集体”称号的苏联阿塞拜疆国家歌舞团，在今晚的招待会上共演出了“石油工人组舞”，“牧人舞”，“祖国组曲”等二十四个节目。富有强烈民族风格的每一个节目的演出，都博得了全场观众的热烈掌声。
