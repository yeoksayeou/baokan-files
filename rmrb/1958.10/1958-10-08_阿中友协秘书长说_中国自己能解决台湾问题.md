Title: Secretary-General of the Arab-China Friendship Association Says China Can Solve the Taiwan Problem Itself
Author:
Date: 1958-10-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 6th - Mr. Mari, Secretary-General of the Arab-China Friendship Association, when interviewed by a Xinhua News Agency reporter today, commented on the letter to Taiwan compatriots from Chinese Minister of National Defense Peng Dehuai, saying, "The cessation of shelling of the coastal islands for a week is a good and humanitarian step. This once again shows China's desire for peace. China's sole purpose is to drive out American imperialism, liberate its own territory, and fully take care of the interests of the people on these islands."

Mari also said that Taiwan is a part of China, "I am certain that without American interference, the Taiwan problem can be solved by the Chinese themselves."

### 阿中友协秘书长说  中国自己能解决台湾问题

1958-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

     阿中友协秘书长说
    中国自己能解决台湾问题
    新华社开罗6日电　阿拉伯—中国友好协会秘书长马里今天在接见新华社记者时就中国国防部长彭德怀告台湾同胞书发表意见说，“对沿海岛屿停止炮轰一星期，这是很好的和人道主义的步骤。这再一次表明中国希望和平。中国的唯一目的是赶走美帝国主义、解放它自己的领土和充分照顾这些岛上人民的利益。”
    马里还说，台湾是中国的一部分，“我确信，没有美国的干涉，台湾问题中国人自己可以解决。”
