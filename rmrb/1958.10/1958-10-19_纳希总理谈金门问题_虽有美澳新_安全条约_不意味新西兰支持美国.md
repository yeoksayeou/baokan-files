Title: Nash Discusses the Quemoy Issue; Although There Is the ANZUS Security Treaty, It Does Not Mean New Zealand Supports the United States
Author:
Date: 1958-10-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 16th, Auckland Dispatch: New Zealand Prime Minister Nash stated on the 15th in Auckland, northern New Zealand, that New Zealand's obligations under the ANZUS Security Treaty do not mean that it must support any war that breaks out over the Quemoy issue.

### 纳希总理谈金门问题  虽有美澳新“安全条约”  不意味新西兰支持美国

1958-10-19
第3版()
专栏：

    纳希总理谈金门问题
    虽有美澳新“安全条约”
    不意味新西兰支持美国
    新华社16日讯　奥克兰消息：新西兰总理纳希15日在新西兰北部的奥克兰市说，新西兰根据美澳新“安全条约”所承担的义务并不意味着它必须支持在金门问题上爆发的任何战争。
