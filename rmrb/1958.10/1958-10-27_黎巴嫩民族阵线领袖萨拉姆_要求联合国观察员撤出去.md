Title: Lebanese National Front Leader Salam Demands Withdrawal of UN Observers
Author:
Date: 1958-10-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 26th. The "Al-Jamahir" newspaper reported today that the leader of the Lebanese National Front, Salam, on the 25th demanded that the Lebanese government facilitate the withdrawal of UN observers, because their continued presence in Lebanon is no longer needed. Salam also requested that small countries urge the United Nations to take necessary measures to ensure that what happened in Lebanon in the past will never happen again in small countries.

### 黎巴嫩民族阵线领袖萨拉姆  要求联合国观察员撤出去

1958-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩民族阵线领袖萨拉姆
    要求联合国观察员撤出去
    新华社大马士革26日电　据此间“群众报”今天报道，黎巴嫩民族阵线领袖萨拉姆在25日要求黎巴嫩政府促使联合国观察员撤出，因为不需要他们再留在黎巴嫩了，萨拉姆还要求小国敦促联合国采取必要措施，使过去在黎巴嫩发生的事情再也不会在小国中发生。
