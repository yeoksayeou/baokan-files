Title: Resolutely Support the Just Struggle of the Chinese People: Japanese Communist Party Issues Slogans on the Taiwan Issue
Author:
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch from Tokyo: Today's "Akahata" published the slogans of the Japanese Communist Party on the Taiwan issue:

	1. Oppose the United States' aggression against China and interference in China's internal affairs! The United States must withdraw from Quemoy, Matsu, and Taiwan!
	2. Support the just struggle of the Chinese people!
	3. Smash the atomic war provocations jointly plotted by the United States and the Chiang Kai-shek clique!
	4. Absolutely do not allow the U.S. military to use bases in Japan and Okinawa for military operations!
	5. Do not produce or transport munitions for the United States!
	6. Abolish U.S. military bases in Japan, withdraw all U.S. troops stationed in Japan, and recover Okinawa and the Ogasawara Islands!
	7. Oppose the conspiracy to sign the Japan-U.S. bilateral "Defense" Treaty, fundamentally revise the San Francisco Treaty, and abolish the Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty" and "Administrative Agreement"!
	8. Abolish the Japan-Chiang Treaty, oppose the Japan-Syngman Rhee talks, and smash the conspiracy to establish a Japan-Chiang-Rhee military alliance!
	9. Smash the Kishida Nobusuke Cabinet's policy of hostility towards China and the conspiracy to create "two Chinas," and strive to restore diplomatic relations between Japan and China!
	10. Restore China's legitimate seat in the United Nations!
	11. Oppose the Kishida Nobusuke Cabinet acting as a partner of the United States in carrying out aggression and reviving Japanese militarism!
	12. Strengthen the unity of the Japanese people with the world's peace forces against U.S. aggression!

### 坚决支持中国人民的正义斗争  日共就台湾问题发表口号

1958-10-03
第5版()
专栏：

    坚决支持中国人民的正义斗争
    日共就台湾问题发表口号
    新华社2日讯　东京消息：今天的“赤旗报”发表了日本共产党关于台湾问题的口号：
    一、反对美国侵略中国和干涉中国内政，美国必须撤出金门、马祖和台湾！
    二、支持中国人民的正义斗争！
    三、粉碎美国和蒋介石集团共谋的原子战争挑衅！
    四、绝不容许美军利用在日本和冲绳的基地进行军事行动！
    五、不为美国生产和运输军火！
    六、撤销美军在日本的基地，撤出一切驻日美军，收复冲绳和小笠原群岛！
    七、反对签订日美双边“防卫”条约的阴谋，对旧金山条约作根本性的修改，废除日美“安全条约”和“行政协定”！
    八、废除日蒋条约，反对日李会谈，粉碎建立日蒋李军事同盟的阴谋！
    九、粉碎岸信介内阁敌视中国的政策和制造“两个中国”的阴谋，争取恢复日中邦交！
    十、恢复中国在联合国的合法席位！
    十一、反对岸信介内阁充当美国进行侵略的伙伴和复活日本军国主义！
    十二、加强日本人民同反对美国侵略的世界和平力量的团结！
