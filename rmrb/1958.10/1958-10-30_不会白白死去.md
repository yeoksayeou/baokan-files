Title: Will Not Die in Vain
Author:
Date: 1958-10-30
Page: 5

　　Will Not Die in Vain
　　The suicide of Japanese youth Yukimori Noguchi, a victim of the American atomic bomb, is a tearful and bloody accusation against the American atomic maniacs. Two generations suffered, and a letter written in blood expressed the Japanese people's full of grief and indignation. Yukimori Noguchi's will said, "I hope I will not die in vain." Yes, he will not die in vain; the Japanese people and all peace-loving people around the world will not let him die in vain.

### 不会白白死去

1958-10-30
第5版()
专栏：编完写起

    不会白白死去
    美国原子弹受害者、日本青年野口幸守的自杀，是对美国原子狂人的血泪控诉。两代受祸，一纸血书，道出了日本人民的满腔悲愤。野口幸守的遗书说：“希望我不是白白的死去”。是的，他不会白白死去的；日本人民和全世界爱好和平人民都不会让他白白死去。
