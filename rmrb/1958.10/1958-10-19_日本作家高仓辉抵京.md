Title: Japanese Writer Teru Takakura Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-10-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: Japanese writer Teru Takakura arrived in Beijing by plane on the 17th at the invitation of the Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee after attending the Asian-African Writers Conference.

Teru Takakura is a famous progressive Japanese writer and former member of the Japanese Diet. His novels "Hakone Fūunroku," "The Wolf," and "Song of the Pigs" have all been translated and published in China.

### 日本作家高仓辉抵京

1958-10-19
第3版()
专栏：欢迎远道而来的朋友

    日本作家高仓辉抵京
    新华社18日讯　出席亚非作家会议后应中国亚非团结委员会邀请来我国访问的日本作家高仓辉于17日乘飞机到达北京。
    高仓辉为日本著名进步作家，日本前国会议员，他所作“箱根风云录”、“狼”、“猪之歌”等小说在我国均有译本。
