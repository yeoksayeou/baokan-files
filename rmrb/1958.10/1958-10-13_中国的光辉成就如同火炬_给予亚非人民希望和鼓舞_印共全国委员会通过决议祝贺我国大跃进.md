Title: China's Brilliant Achievements are Like a Torch, Giving Hope and Inspiration to the People of Asia and Africa; Indian Communist Party National Committee Passes Resolution Congratulating China's Great Leap Forward
Author:
Date: 1958-10-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 10th. The National Committee of the Communist Party of India, at its current meeting in Madras, passed a resolution congratulating the Chinese Communist Party and the Chinese people.

The resolution stated that on the occasion of the ninth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the National Committee of the Communist Party of India pays tribute to the great Chinese people and their heroic Communist Party. People all over the world today are watching with admiration the enormous and unprecedented progress made by the Chinese people in all aspects of their work during the Great Leap Forward.

The resolution said, "All mankind applauds this remarkable progress, the amazing technological revolution taking place in China, and the cultural and educational leaps forward that have accompanied industrial progress."

The resolution pointed out that the imperialists are angered by China's progress and are plotting aggression in a vain attempt to stop the irresistible progress of the 600 million Chinese people.

The resolution concluded, "The National Committee of the Communist Party of India extends warm fraternal congratulations to the comrades of the Chinese Communist Party led by Chairman Mao Zedong and to all the Chinese people. Their brilliant achievements are shining like a torch, giving hope and encouragement to the people of Asian and African countries."

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 11th. R. D. Sharma, secretary of the Secretariat of the Communist Party of India, published an article in the latest issue of the monthly "New Age" entitled: "The Great Leap Forward in China." The article said: "This is a victory for the great Chinese Communist Party, which correctly applied the revolutionary principles of Marxism-Leninism to the specific conditions of China."

The article analyzed in detail the tremendous progress China has recently made economically. It emphasized that the People's Commune movement marks "a new stage in the development of the socialist movement in rural areas of China."

The article pointed out that if it were not for the constant acts of aggression by American imperialism, the progress of the Chinese people would have been even faster, and China now "has sufficient strength to inflict a serious defeat on the American aggressors."

Referring to the understanding and friendly relations established between China and India on the basis of Panchsheel, R. D. Sharma said that the Indian people are very happy to see China's continuous progress in all fields. He warned people to be wary of the conspiracies of imperialists and reactionaries who want to undermine this friendly relationship.

He also emphasized China's relentless struggle against the revisionism of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia. He wrote: "In doing so, China is fulfilling its responsibility to the world communist movement."

### 中国的光辉成就如同火炬  给予亚非人民希望和鼓舞  印共全国委员会通过决议祝贺我国大跃进

1958-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    中国的光辉成就如同火炬
    给予亚非人民希望和鼓舞
    印共全国委员会通过决议祝贺我国大跃进
    新华社新德里10日电　印度共产党全国委员会在目前在马德拉斯举行的会议上通过了一项向中国共产党和人民致贺的决议。
    决议说，印度共产党全国委员会在中华人民共和国建国九周年之际，向伟大的中国人民和他们的英雄的共产党致敬。全世界人民今天都以钦佩的眼光注视着中国人民在工作的各方面的跃进中所取得的巨大的和史无前例的进展。
    决议说：“全人类都赞扬这种非常显著的进展、中国所发生的惊人的技术革命以及随着工业进展而来的文化和教育的跃进”。
    决议指出，帝国主义者对中国的进展感到愤怒，并且阴谋进行侵略，妄图徒然地阻止六亿中国人民的不可抗拒的进展。
    决议最后说：“印度共产党全国委员会向毛泽东主席所领导的中国共产党的同志们和全体中国人民致以热烈的兄弟般的祝贺。他们的光辉的成就正像是给亚洲和非洲各国人民以希望和鼓励的火炬一般照耀着。”
    新华社新德里11日电　印度共产党书记处书记兰那地夫在最近一期“新世纪”月刊上发表了一篇文章，题目是：“中国的大跃进”。这篇文章说：“这是伟大的中国共产党的胜利，中国共产党把马克思列宁主义的革命原则正确地运用于中国具体情况”。
    这篇文章详细分析了中国最近在经济上取得的巨大进展。它强调指出，人民公社运动标志着“社会主义运动在中国农村地区发展的一个新阶段”。
    文章指出，如果没有美帝国主义不断的侵略行动，中国人民的进展还会更快，中国现在已经“有充分的力量，能够使美国侵略者遭到严重的失败”。
    谈到中印之间根据潘查希拉取得的谅解和建立的友好关系时，兰那地夫说，印度人民很高兴看到中国在各方面不断取得进展。他警告人们提防帝国主义和反动派想削弱这种友好关系的阴谋。
    他还强调指出了中国对南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟的修正主义的无情斗争。他写道：“中国这样做，是尽它对世界共产主义运动的责任。”
