Title: Stepping Up Troop Deployment to Expand Provocations Against Us: Two US Warships Sail to the Far East
Author:
Date: 1958-10-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 2nd - "Al Akhbar" reported from Port Said on the 2nd that the American transport ship "Steel Age" and the warship "Haiti," carrying military equipment and supplies, passed through the Suez Canal on the 1st, heading to the Far East region where the United States is escalating its provocative actions.
The British ship "Deodar," loaded with jets and parts, and the destroyer "Puglia," also passed through the Suez Canal on their way to Hong Kong.

### 加紧调兵扩大对我挑衅  美舰二艘开来远东

1958-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    加紧调兵扩大对我挑衅
    美舰二艘开来远东
    新华社开罗2日电　据“消息报”2日从塞得港报道，运载军事装备和物资的美国运输舰“钢号”和战舰“海地号”在1日经过苏伊士运河，前往美国正在扩大挑衅行动的远东地区。
    装载着喷气机和零件的英国船只“迪沃达耳号”和驱逐舰“帕吉·普里号”也经过苏伊士运河前往香港。
