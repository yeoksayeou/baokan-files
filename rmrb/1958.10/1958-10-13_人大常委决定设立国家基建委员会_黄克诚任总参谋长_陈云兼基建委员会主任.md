Title: NPC Standing Committee Decides to Establish State Capital Construction Commission; Huang Kecheng Appointed Chief of General Staff; Chen Yun Concurrently Appointed Director of Capital Construction Commission
Author:
Date: 1958-10-13
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 12th. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, based on the proposal of the State Council, decided to establish the State Capital Construction Commission of the People's Republic of China.
    Xinhua News Agency, 12th. Chairman Mao Zedong, according to the decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, appointed Huang Kecheng as Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and appointed Vice Premier Chen Yun concurrently as Director of the State Capital Construction Commission of the People's Republic of China.

### 人大常委决定设立国家基建委员会  黄克诚任总参谋长　陈云兼基建委员会主任

1958-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

    人大常委决定设立国家基建委员会
    黄克诚任总参谋长　陈云兼基建委员会主任
    新华社12日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会根据国务院提出的建议，决定设立中华人民共和国国家基本建设委员会。
    新华社12日讯　毛泽东主席根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会的决定，任命黄克诚为中国人民解放军总参谋长，任命陈云副总理兼任中华人民共和国国家基本建设委员会主任。
