Title: Albanian Military Delegation Arrives in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1958-10-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 22nd: The Albanian military delegation, led by Balluku, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Defense of Albania, arrived in Hanoi by special plane on the morning of the 22nd after leaving Guangzhou on the same day. They are visiting Vietnam at the invitation of the Ministry of National Defense of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Vietnamese Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Vo Nguyen Giap, Deputy Minister of National Defense Ta Quang Buu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Ung Van Khiem, and many senior generals of the People's Army welcomed the delegation at the airport. Diplomatic envoys from various countries also greeted the delegation at the airport.

### 阿军事代表团到达河内

1958-10-23
第5版()
专栏：

    阿军事代表团到达河内
    据新华社河内22日电　由阿尔巴尼亚部长会议第一副主席兼国防部长巴卢库上将率领的阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团一行九人，22日乘专机离广州当日上午到达河内，他们是应越南民主共和国国防部的邀请到越南进行友好访问的。
    越南副总理兼国防部长武元甲、国防部副部长谢光宝、外交部副部长雍文谦和人民军许多高级将领到机场欢迎。各国外交使节也在机场迎接代表团。
