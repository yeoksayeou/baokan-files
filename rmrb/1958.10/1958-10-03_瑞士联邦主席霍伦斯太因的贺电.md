Title: Telegram of Congratulations from Swiss Federal President Hollenstein
Author: Tomas Hollenstein
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

Telegram of Congratulations from Swiss Federal President Hollenstein

To His Excellency Mr. Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:

On the occasion of the National Day of the People's Republic of China, I would like to extend to Your Excellency the warmest congratulations on behalf of the Federal Council, and express my best wishes for your personal happiness and for the bright future of the Chinese people.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Swiss Federal President　Tomas Hollenstein

### 瑞士联邦主席霍伦斯太因的贺电
托马斯·霍伦斯太因
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    瑞士联邦主席霍伦斯太因的贺电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东先生阁下：
    在中华人民共和国国庆的时候，我谨代表联邦委员会向阁下致以最热烈的祝贺，并表达我对你个人幸福和对中国人民的美好未来的最良好的愿望。
                          瑞士联邦主席　托马斯·霍伦斯太因
