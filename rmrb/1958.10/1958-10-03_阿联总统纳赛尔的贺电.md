Title: President Nasser of the United Arab Republic's Congratulatory Telegram
Author: Jia Mai'er Aibu Du'er Naser
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

    To His Excellency Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China:
    On the occasion of your celebration of the National Day of the People's Republic of China, I am pleased to extend, on behalf of the United Arab Republic and in my personal name, my most sincere congratulations to Your Excellency, wishing you health and happiness, and the great Chinese people glory and prosperity.
                                Gamal Abdel Nasser

### 阿联总统纳赛尔的贺电
加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    阿联总统纳赛尔的贺电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东先生阁下：
    在你们庆祝中华人民共和国国庆节的时候，我愉快地代表阿拉伯联合共和国并以我个人的名义向阁下致以最诚挚的祝贺，祝阁下健康和幸福，祝伟大的中国人民光荣和昌盛。
                                加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔
