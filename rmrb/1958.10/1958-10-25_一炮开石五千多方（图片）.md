Title: One Blast Opens Over 5,000 Cubic Meters of Stone (Picture)
Author: Wang Xin, Liu Xunhua
Date: 1958-10-25
Page: 3

    One Blast Opens Over 5,000 Cubic Meters of Stone
    Look! Across the Yellow River, smoke billows. These are the workers at the Qingtongxia construction site using large-scale blasting methods to quarry stone. This one blast yielded over 5,700 cubic meters of stone.
    The Qingtongxia Water Conservancy Hub Project is one of the largest construction projects currently underway in the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region and is another large-scale Yellow River management project following the Sanmenxia Project.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Xin and Liu Xunhua, photo

### 一炮开石五千多方（图片）
王新著  刘勋华
1958-10-25
第3版()
专栏：

    一炮开石五千多方
    看！黄河对岸，烟雾腾腾，这是青铜峡工地的工人们正在用大爆破的方法开山取石。这一炮就爆破了五千七百多石方。
    青铜峡水利枢纽工程是宁夏回族自治区目前最大的施工工程之一，也是继三门峡工程后又一个规模宏大的治黄工程。
    新华社记者　王新著、刘勋华摄
