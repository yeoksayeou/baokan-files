Title: President Mirza of Pakistan's Congratulatory Message
Author:
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

    President Mirza of Pakistan's Congratulatory Message
    To His Excellency Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China:
    On the occasion of the 9th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I am delighted to extend, in my personal name and on behalf of the government and people of Pakistan, my warmest congratulations and best wishes to Your Excellency and the Chinese government and people, wishing you good health and happiness, and the Chinese people lasting peace and prosperity.
    I hope that the ties between China and Pakistan, based on common goals and understanding, will be further strengthened in the coming year.
             President of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan Iskander Mirza

### 巴基斯坦总统米尔扎的贺电
伊斯堪德尔·米尔扎
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦总统米尔扎的贺电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东先生阁下：
    欣逢中华人民共和国建国九周年纪念日，我很愉快地以我个人的名义并代表巴基斯坦政府和人民向阁下和中国政府和人民致最热烈的祝贺和最良好的愿望，祝阁下健康和幸福，祝中国人民长远安宁和昌盛。
    祝中巴两国之间基于共同目的和谅解的联系在今后一年中得到进一步的加强。
              巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总统　伊斯堪德尔·米尔扎
