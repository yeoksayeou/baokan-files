Title: Our Strength Lies in Unity - Speech by Chervenkov, Head of the Bulgarian National Assembly Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-10-01
Page: 7

Comrade Deputy Chairman, Representatives of all circles of China, Dear Chinese friends, Comrades:

Please allow me, on behalf of the Bulgarian National Assembly, which sent us on a friendly visit to fraternal China, and on behalf of our government and all the Bulgarian people, to express here our deep gratitude for holding this meeting today and establishing many associations for developing and consolidating the friendly relations between the great Chinese people and the people of other socialist countries. We wholeheartedly congratulate this meeting and sincerely support this lofty and beneficial cause. Without a doubt, we Bulgarians will also take the necessary actions on our part to meet the urgent needs and further strengthen the ties between our people and the Chinese people.

Please allow me to take advantage of this meeting to express our sincere joy here for the amazing achievements made by the People's Republic of China in terms of scale and speed. Today is the tenth day of our delegation's visit to China. Wherever we have been, we have seen the complete unity of the people with the Communist Party and the government, and they are working hard and striving for a great leap forward in production. Dear Chinese comrades, the magnificent and unprecedented achievements you are about to obtain make us proud of you, of the glorious Chinese Communist Party, of its long-tested and beloved leader Comrade Mao Zedong, and of our entire socialist camp. These achievements give us greater strength and confidence in our struggle for the complete victory of the great cause.

Our people have also made considerable achievements in selfless labor. Under the leadership of the Bulgarian Communist Party, the Bulgarian people are steadfastly fulfilling the behest of our leader and teacher Georgi Dimitrov, eliminating the system of human exploitation, laying the foundation for socialist production relations, carrying out national industrialization, collectivizing agriculture, greatly improving their welfare, and are now working and fighting unremittingly to further enhance the material and technical basis of socialist society, develop productive forces, and continuously improve their living and cultural standards.

We know that without the selfless and generous assistance of the Soviet Union, there would be no achievements and victories in our socialist construction. Therefore, at this meeting aimed at further developing and strengthening the friendly relations between the Chinese people and the people of other socialist countries, we must first emphasize our common friendship and unbreakable cooperation with the Soviet Union. This is the foundation and guarantee for the true prosperity of all our ties.

Our strength lies in our unity. Our strength lies in our eternal friendship with the Soviet Union. Our strength lies in our common loyalty to Marxism-Leninism and in our common struggle to defend the purity of Marxism-Leninism and oppose modern revisionism, with the Yugoslav leaders as the main representatives.

American imperialism is provoking the powerful six hundred million Chinese people in the Taiwan Strait. But they have been given a very clear warning, that is, any infringement on the People's China is an infringement on the entire socialist camp, and will be met with the full resistance of the entire socialist camp. Our motto is: "All for one, one for all." All peace-loving forces in the world support the just cause of the People's Republic of China. Our people have held many mass rallies and mass meetings, and sent thousands of telegrams and letters to the Chinese Embassy in Bulgaria, expressing this support. This cause will surely triumph. Taiwan belongs to the People's Republic of China, and no force can prevent it from returning to the embrace of the motherland.

Long live the great People's Republic of China!
Long live the friendship between Bulgaria and China!
Long live the friendship and fraternal relations between the people of all countries in the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union!

(Attached picture)
The rostrum of the inaugural meeting of the China Friendship Association with ten fraternal countries.
Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong

### 我们的力量在于团结一致  保加利亚国民议会代表团团长契尔文科夫的讲话

1958-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    我们的力量在于团结一致
    保加利亚国民议会代表团团长契尔文科夫的讲话亲爱的副主席同志，亲爱的中国各界人士代表，亲爱的中国朋友们，同志们：
    请允许我代表派我们前来兄弟中国进行友好访问的保加利亚国民议会，代表我国政府和全体保加利亚人民，在这里，对今天举行这个大会，成立许多发展和巩固伟大中国人民同其他社会主义国家人民友好联系的协会，表示深深的谢意。我们从内心祝贺这个大会，衷心拥护这一崇高的有益的事业。毫无疑问，我们保加利亚人也将从自己方面采取必要的行动来适应迫切的需要，来进一步加强我国人民同中国人民的联系。
    请允许我利用这个大会的机会，在这里对中华人民共和国所取得的在规模和速度上惊人的成就表示我们真诚的喜悦。今天是我们代表团在中国访问的第十天。在我们所到过的地方，我们都看到了人民同共产党和政府的完全团结一致，他们正在鼓足干劲，顺利地力争生产大跃进。亲爱的中国同志们，你们即将获得的规模壮阔的空前巨大的成就，使我们为你们、为光荣的中国共产党、为它的久经考验的敬爱领袖毛泽东同志、为我们整个社会主义阵营感到自豪，这些成就在我们争取伟大事业彻底胜利的斗争中给了我们更大的力量和信心。
    我国人民在忘我的劳动中也获得了不小成就。保加利亚人民在保加利亚共产党的领导下，坚定不移地履行着我们的领袖和导师格奥尔基·季米特洛夫的遗训，消灭了人剥削人的制度，奠定了社会主义生产关系，进行了国家工业化，农业集体化，大大提高了自己的福利水平，现在正在坚持不懈地努力，为进一步增强社会主义社会的物质技术基础、为发展生产力和不断改善自己的生活和文化水平而劳动和斗争着。
    我们知道，如果没有苏联无私的慷慨的援助，就不可能有我们在社会主义建设中的成就和胜利。因此，在这个目的在于进一步发展和加强中国人民同其他社会主义国家人民友好联系的大会上，我们首先要强调我们同苏联的共同友谊和牢不可破的合作。这是我们之间一切联系获得真正繁荣的基础和保证。
    我们的力量在于我们的团结一致。我们的力量在于我们同苏联永远友好。我们的力量在于我们对马克思列宁主义的共同忠诚，在于我们为捍卫马克思列宁主义的纯洁、反对以南斯拉夫领导人为主要代表的现代修正主义的共同斗争。
    美帝国主义在台湾海峡对强大的六亿中国人民进行挑衅。但是他们已经受到非常明确的警告，就是说，对人民中国的任何侵犯，都是对整个社会主义阵营的侵犯，都会遭到整个社会主义阵营的全力抗击。我们的格言是：“人人为我，我为人人”。全世界爱好和平的力量都支持中华人民共和国的正义事业。我国人民举行了许多群众集会、群众大会，向中国驻保加利亚大使馆投递了几千封电报和信件，表示这种支持。这一事业一定会胜利。台湾属于中华人民共和国，任何力量都不能阻挠它回到祖国的怀抱。
    伟大的中华人民共和国万岁！
    保中友好万岁！
    以苏联为首的社会主义阵营各国人民的友谊和亲如手足的兄弟关系万岁！（附图片）
     我国同十个兄弟国家友好协会成立大会的主席台。
     新华社记者　邹健东摄
