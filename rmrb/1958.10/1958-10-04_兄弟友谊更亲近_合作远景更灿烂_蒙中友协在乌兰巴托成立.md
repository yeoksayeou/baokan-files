Title: Brotherly Friendship Grows Closer, Cooperation Prospects Brighter: Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Association Established in Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1958-10-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar Telegram:
    People from all walks of life in the Mongolian People's Republic held a gathering in Ulaanbaatar on the afternoon of the 30th to establish the Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Association.
    The meeting elected sixty-five members to the Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Association. Sharav, Secretary of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia, was elected as the chairman of the association. Natsukdorj, Deputy Doctor of Mongolian History, was elected as vice chairman.
    Sharav delivered a report at the meeting, saying that the people of Mongolia and China have a profound traditional friendship. Since the founding of New China, this friendship has further developed. In the past nine years, China has made great achievements in all aspects and has become a powerful force in maintaining world peace. China's astonishing speed of development far exceeds that of capitalist countries, and it is still continuing its Great Leap Forward.
    He went on to say that the Mongolian people strongly condemn the US imperialist armed provocations against China and support the Chinese people's just struggle to liberate Taiwan. The Mongolian people firmly believe that the Chinese people will surely be victorious.
    In his speech, he also thanked China for its assistance to Mongolia. He said that the close cooperation between the people of Mongolia and China will be forever recorded in the history of Mongolia.
    Xie Fusheng, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, said at the meeting that the establishment of the Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Association marks the closer brotherly friendship between Mongolia and China. It has opened up a brighter prospect for the development of friendly cooperation between the two countries.
    The meeting adopted a congratulatory telegram to the Chinese-Mongolian Friendship Association established in Beijing today.
    Dugsurun, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and Acting Foreign Minister Shagdar Surin, and others attended the meeting.
    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Zhang Zhixiang, President of the China-Mongolia Friendship Association, sent a telegram to Sharav, President of the Mongolia-China Friendship Association, on the 1st, congratulating the establishment of the Mongolia-China Friendship Association. The telegram reads as follows:
    Comrade Sharav, President of the Mongolia-China Friendship Association:
    I am honored to inform you that the China-Mongolia Friendship Association was formally established in Beijing on September 30. I am delighted to learn that the Mongolia-China Friendship Association was established in Ulaanbaatar on the same day. I would like to extend my heartfelt and warm congratulations to you and, through you, to all comrades of the Mongolia-China Friendship Association. I firmly believe that through our joint efforts, the friendly unity between the Chinese and Mongolian people will be further consolidated and developed. With
    Brotherly greetings!
    Zhang Zhixiang, President of the China-Mongolia Friendship Association

### 兄弟友谊更亲近　合作远景更灿烂  蒙中友协在乌兰巴托成立

1958-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    兄弟友谊更亲近　合作远景更灿烂
    蒙中友协在乌兰巴托成立
    据新华社乌兰巴托电　蒙古人民共和国各界人士30日下午在乌兰巴托举行集会，成立蒙中友好协会。
    会上选举了六十五名蒙中友好协会委员。蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团秘书长沙拉布当选为协会主席。蒙古历史学副博士那楚克道尔吉当选为副主席。
    沙拉布在会上作报告说，蒙中两国人民有着深厚的传统友谊。在新中国成立以后，这种友谊更加发展了。九年来中国在各方面取得了伟大成就，它已经成为维护世界和平的强大力量。中国的惊人发展速度远远超过了资本主义国家，现在还在继续大跃进。
    他接着说，蒙古人民严厉谴责美帝国主义对中国的武装挑衅，支持中国人民解放台湾的正义斗争。蒙古人民深信，中国人民一定得到胜利。
    他在讲话中还感谢中国给予蒙古的援助。他说，蒙中两国人民的亲密的合作将永远记载在蒙古的史册上。
    中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生在会上讲话说，蒙中友好协会的成立标志着蒙中两国的兄弟友谊更加亲近。它为两国的友好互助的发展开辟了更加灿烂的远景。
    会上通过了致今天在北京成立的中蒙友好协会的贺电。
    蒙古人民革命党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席杜格苏伦，代理外交部长沙格达尔苏伦等出席了大会。
    据新华社讯　中国蒙古友好协会会长张致祥1日致电蒙古中国友好协会会长沙拉布，祝贺蒙古中国友好协会成立。电文如下：
    蒙古中国友好协会会长沙拉布同志：
    我荣幸地通知您，中国蒙古友好协会在9月30日在北京正式成立。欣悉蒙古中国友好协会在同一天在乌兰巴托成立。谨向您并通过您向蒙古中国友好协会的全体同志，致以衷心的热烈的祝贺。深信，通过我们的共同努力，中蒙两国人民的友好团结将获得进一步的巩固和发展。致以
    兄弟的敬礼！
    中国蒙古友好协会会长张致祥
