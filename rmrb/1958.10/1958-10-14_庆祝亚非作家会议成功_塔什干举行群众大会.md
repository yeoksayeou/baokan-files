Title: Tashkent Holds Mass Meeting to Celebrate Success of Asian-African Writers' Conference
Author:
Date: 1958-10-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tashkent, October 13th: Tashkent working people held a grand mass meeting at the stadium, which can accommodate fifty to sixty thousand people, at 6:00 pm today, to jointly celebrate the successful conclusion of the Asian-African Writers' Conference with writers from Asian and African countries.

    Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic, Rashidov, addressed the meeting. He congratulated the conference on the successful completion of its tasks, and congratulated the Asian and African writers on writing many works expressing the aspirations of the Asian and African people. He said that the meeting was conducted in an atmosphere of friendship, mutual understanding, and great sincerity. He conveyed the fraternal wishes of the Soviet people to the writers' representatives of Asian and African countries, wishing their people success in the struggle for national independence.

    Representatives from all walks of life in Tashkent and Chairman of the Soviet Writers' Union, Tikhonov, also spoke at the meeting, congratulating the success of the conference.

    Mao Dun, head of the Chinese delegation, spoke at the celebration meeting. He said that this conference expressed the aspirations of 1.5 billion people in Asia and Africa. The spirit of Tashkent is the spirit of the Asian and African people, which is an irresistible trend. He expressed his gratitude to the people of Tashkent and said that the unity of the Chinese and Soviet people is an invincible force, and Chinese writers will fully support the struggle of the Asian and African people.

    Representatives from India, the United Arab Republic, and Ghana also spoke at the meeting. They warmly congratulated the success of the conference and expressed that they would bring the spirit of Tashkent to the writers and people of their countries.

    After the meeting, a grand fireworks party was held, with the whole city decorated with lanterns and shining brightly.

### 庆祝亚非作家会议成功  塔什干举行群众大会

1958-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝亚非作家会议成功
    塔什干举行群众大会
    新华社塔什干13日电　塔什干劳动人民今天下午六时在可容纳五、六万人的运动场上举行了隆重的群众大会，同来自亚非各国的作家们共同庆祝亚非作家会议的胜利闭幕。
    苏联乌兹别克共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席拉希多夫在大会上致词。他祝贺会议胜利地完成了自己的任务，祝贺亚非作家写出许多表达亚非人民愿望的作品。他说，会议是在友好、相互谅解和十分诚挚的气氛中进行的。他向亚非国家的作家代表们转达了苏联人民的兄弟般的祝愿，祝他们本国的人民在争取民族独立的斗争中获得成功。
    塔什干各界代表和苏联作家协会主席吉洪诺夫都在会上讲了话，祝贺会议的成功。
    中国代表团团长茅盾在庆祝大会上讲了话。他说，这次会议表达了亚非十五亿人民的愿望，塔什干的精神就是亚非人民的精神，是一种不可抗拒的潮流。他对塔什干人民表示感谢，并说，中苏两国人民的团结是不可战胜的力量，中国作家全力支援亚非人民的斗争。
    印度、阿联、加纳的代表也在大会上讲了话。他们热烈祝贺会议的成功，并表示将把塔什干的精神带给本国的作家和人民。
    大会结束后，接着举行盛大的焰火晚会，全城张灯结彩，大放光明。
