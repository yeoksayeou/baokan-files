Title: Marxist-Leninist Scholars Gather in Berlin to Discuss Party History Research and Textbook Editing
Author:
Date: 1958-10-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, October 8th - According to a report by the German News Agency: An international conference of representatives from Marxist-Leninist Institutes and Party History Institutes and Committees of the Central Committees of Communist and Workers' Parties of various countries opened in Berlin on the 7th.

Participating in the conference were delegations from socialist countries such as the Soviet Union, China, Albania, Bulgaria, North Korea, Mongolia, Poland, Romania, Czechoslovakia, and Hungary, as well as representatives from the Communist and Workers' Parties of Belgium, Britain, France, the Netherlands, Italy, Austria, Norway, Sweden, and Germany.

The conference will discuss issues related to the study of the history of Communist and Workers' Parties and the editing of textbooks, as well as issues related to scientifically summarizing the accumulated experience in the history of the international workers' movement and the communist movement.

The conference will conclude on October 14th.

### 马克思列宁主义学者会师柏林  将讨论党史研究和教材编辑等问题

1958-10-10
第5版()
专栏：

    马克思列宁主义学者会师柏林
    将讨论党史研究和教材编辑等问题
    新华社柏林8日电　据德意志通讯社报道：各国马克思列宁主义研究院以及共产党和工人党中央委员会党史研究所和委员会的代表的国际会议7日在柏林开幕。
    参加会议的有苏联、中国、阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、朝鲜、蒙古、波兰、罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克和匈牙利等社会主义国家的党的代表团，以及比利时、英国、法国、荷兰、意大利、奥地利、挪威、瑞典和德国共产党和工人党的代表。
    会议将讨论有关研究共产党和工人党的历史和编辑教材的问题，以及有关科学地总结国际工人运动和共产主义运动史方面所积累的经验问题。
    会议将在10月14日结束。
