Title: Burma Announces Caretaker Cabinet List
Author:
Date: 1958-10-17
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, October 15th. The Burmese Army Intelligence Department announced the list of the caretaker cabinet to be led by General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces, on the evening of the 14th.
    The members of this cabinet include: Former Chief Justice of the Supreme Court, U Thein Maung; Chief Justice of the High Court, U Chan Tun Aung; Lecturer at Rangoon University, U Ka; Chairman of the Union Bank, U Kyaw Nyein; Secretary-General of the Union Government, U Khin Maung Pyu; Former head of the Burmese Election Commission, U San Nyun; Government Chemical Examiner, U Chit Thoung; and retired judge, U Ba Kha.

### 缅甸公布看守内阁名单

1958-10-17
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸公布看守内阁名单
    据新华社仰光15日电　缅甸陆军情报部在14日晚间公布了将由缅甸国防军总参谋长奈温将军领导的看守内阁的名单。
    这个内阁的成员包括：前最高法院首席法官吴登貌、高等法院首席法官吴千吞昂、仰光大学讲师吴卡、联邦银行董事长吴觉迎、联邦政府秘书长吴钦貌漂、前缅甸选举委员会负责人吴山纽、政府化学检验师吴漆东和已经退休的法官吴巴卡。
