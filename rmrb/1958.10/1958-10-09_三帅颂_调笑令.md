Title: Ode to the Three Marshals—Tune: Tiaoxiaoling
Author: Ye Jianying
Date: 1958-10-09
Page: 8

    Ode to the Three Marshals
    ——Tune: Tiaoxiaoling
        Ye Jianying
    Steel
    Steel, steel, used to produce everything. Blast furnaces and open-hearth furnaces billow smoke, thousands upon thousands of people ascend the mountains. Up the mountains, technical armies point the way.
    Grain
    Grain, grain, water, soil, seeds, fertilizer, close planting. Focus on the central key, deep plowing, debates by the experimental fields. Debates, debates, increase production by many times ten inches.
    Machinery
    Machinery, machinery, need good, economical, many, and fast. Central, local, all the people, large, medium, and small scale. Small scale, small scale, broad coverage, large quantity, little capital.

### 三帅颂——调笑令
叶剑英
1958-10-09
第8版()
专栏：

    三帅颂
    ——调笑令
        叶剑英
    钢铁
    钢铁钢铁，用来生产一切。高炉平炉冒烟，千人万人上山。山上山上，技术大军指向。
    粮食
    粮食粮食，水土种肥密植。中心一窍深耕，试验田边论争。争论争论，增产多翻十寸。
    机械
    机械机械，需要好省多快。中央地方全民，大型中型小型。型小型小，面广量多资少。
