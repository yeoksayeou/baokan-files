Title: Telegram of Greetings from Najib al-Rubai, Chairman of the Iraqi Sovereignty Council
Author: Najib al-Rubai
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

To His Excellency Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of the National Day of the People's Republic of China, I am very pleased to extend my sincere congratulations and best wishes to Your Excellency, wishing you health and happiness, and the great Chinese people prosperity.

                    Najib al-Rubai
                    Chairman of the Iraqi Sovereignty Council

### 伊拉克最高权力委员会主席鲁巴伊的贺电
纳吉布·鲁巴伊
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    伊拉克最高权力委员会主席鲁巴伊的贺电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东先生阁下：
    在中华人民共和国国庆节的时候，我很愉快地向阁下表示诚挚的祝贺和最良好的愿望，祝阁下健康、幸福，祝伟大的中国人民昌盛。
                    伊拉克最高权力委员会主席　纳吉布·鲁巴伊
