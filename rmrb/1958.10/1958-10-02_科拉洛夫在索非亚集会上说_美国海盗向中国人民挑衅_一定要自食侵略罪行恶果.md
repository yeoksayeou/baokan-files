Title: Kolarov Says at Sofia Rally: American Pirates Provoke the Chinese People; They Will Surely Swallow the Bitter Fruit of Their Aggressive Crimes

Author:

Date: 1958-10-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, October 1st: A rally celebrating China's National Day was held on the evening of September 30th at the "People's Army House" in Sofia, organized by the Bulgarian Committee for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, together with the Sofia City Committee of the Fatherland Front and other organizations.

Many prominent figures from Bulgarian cultural, scientific, and educational circles, including Avramova, Chairman of the Bulgarian Committee for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Kiselintchev, Deputy Minister of Education and Culture of Bulgaria, attended the celebration.

Zhu Qiwu, the Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria, and embassy staff members were invited to attend the celebration.

Bulgarian writer Kolarov, who had visited China, delivered a report at the meeting on celebrating China's National Day. In his report, he introduced the heroic revolutionary struggles of the Chinese people and the great achievements made in socialist construction. He condemned the crimes of aggression and provocation committed by U.S. imperialism against China in the Taiwan Strait area. He said that the People's China is invincible. If the American pirates dare to provoke a war against the Chinese people, then they will surely swallow the bitter fruit of their aggressive crimes.

Afterwards, Ambassador Zhu Qiwu spoke at the meeting, emphasizing that China's achievements in socialist construction are inseparable from the sincere and selfless assistance of the Soviet Union, Bulgaria, and other fraternal socialist countries.

After the meeting, a Chinese film was shown.

### 科拉洛夫在索非亚集会上说  美国海盗向中国人民挑衅  一定要自食侵略罪行恶果

1958-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    科拉洛夫在索非亚集会上说
    美国海盗向中国人民挑衅
    一定要自食侵略罪行恶果
    据新华社索非亚1日电　保加利亚对外友好和文化联络委员会会同祖国阵线索非亚市委员会等组织，9月30日晚在索非亚“人民军之家”举行集会，庆祝中国国庆。
    保加利亚对外友好和联络委员会主席阿弗拉莫娃、保加利亚教育和文化部副部长基谢林切夫等许多保加利亚文化、科学、教育界的知名人士都出席了庆祝会。
    中国驻保加利亚大使朱其文和使馆工作人员应邀出席了庆祝会。
    曾经访问过中国的保加利亚作家科拉洛夫在会上作了关于庆祝中国国庆的报告。他在报告中介绍了中国人民的英勇革命斗争的经过和在社会主义建设方面取得的巨大成就。他谴责美帝国主义在台湾海峡地区对中国进行的侵略挑衅罪行。他说，人民中国是不可战胜的。美国海盗要是敢于对中国人民挑起战争，那么，它一定要自食其侵略罪行的恶果。
    接着，朱其文大使在会上讲了话，他强调指出，中国在社会主义建设方面取得的成就是与苏联、保加利亚及其他兄弟社会主义国家的真诚无私的援助分不开的。
    大会结束后，放映了中国影片。
