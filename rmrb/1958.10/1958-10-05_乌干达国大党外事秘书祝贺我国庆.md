Title: Ugandan National Congress Foreign Affairs Secretary Congratulates China on National Day
Author:
Date: 1958-10-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 4th October: John Kale, Foreign Affairs Secretary of the Ugandan National Congress and Director of the Ugandan National Congress Office in Cairo, wrote to Chen Jiakang, Chinese Ambassador to the United Arab Republic, on October 1st, congratulating China on its National Day.

The congratulatory letter said, "On the occasion of the ninth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I would like to extend my heartfelt congratulations to the Chinese people on behalf of the people of Uganda, especially on behalf of the Ugandan National Congress."

The congratulatory letter said that anyone who sees the achievements of the People's Republic of China since its founding, no matter how ignorant he is, cannot but admire and respect the Chinese people. The congratulatory letter said that the Chinese people, with unwavering determination and immense potential, have built their country into a giant economically commensurate with its area and population.

The congratulatory letter said, "Please believe that the people of Uganda, no matter how small their strength, insist on supporting the Chinese people. There is no doubt that wisdom, justice and freedom will ultimately triumph. In the event of any unforeseen event, the forces of freedom are stronger than the reactionary imperialist running dogs."

### 乌干达国大党外事秘书祝贺我国庆

1958-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

    乌干达国大党外事秘书祝贺我国庆
    新华社开罗4日电　乌干达国大党外事秘书、乌干达国大党驻开罗办事处主任约翰·凯尔在10月1日写信给中国驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使陈家康，祝贺中国国庆。
    贺信说，“在中华人民共和国成立九周年之际，我谨代表乌干达人民，特别是代表乌干达国大党，向中国人民致衷心的祝贺。”
    贺信说，任何人看到中华人民共和国建国以来的成就，不管他是多么无知，都不能不佩服和尊敬中国人民。贺信说，中国人民以毫不动摇的决心和无限雄厚的潜力，已把他们的国家建设成为一个在经济上同它的面积和人口相称的巨人。
    贺信说，“请相信，乌干达人民，不管他们的力量怎样小，坚持支持中国人民。毫无疑问，智慧、正义和自由最后将取得胜利。如有任何意外事件，自由的力量是比反动的帝国主义走狗更加强大的。”
