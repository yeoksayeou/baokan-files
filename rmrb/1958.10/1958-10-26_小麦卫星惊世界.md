Title: Wheat Satellite Startles the World
Author: Yuan Ying
Date: 1958-10-26
Page: 5
	
	Wheat Satellite Startles the World
	Yuan Ying
	Wheat satellite emerges from Qinghai,
	Per-mu yield of eight thousand startles the world,
	Thunder roars for miles, lightning flashes far,
	Bringing good news spreading wide!
	Boundless fertile land in sight,
	Lay dormant for thousands of years,
	Heroic era, heroic people,
	Unveiling the secret hand in hand.
	Qinghai wheat grows well,
	Politics in command is the magic weapon,
	The bolder the ambition, the greater the yield,
	Riding a rocket into the clouds.
	The aroma of wheat follows the Yellow River,
	Wheat stacks compare to the height of the Kunlun Mountains,
	Work hard through winter and spring again,
	Even bigger satellites will be seen tomorrow!

### 小麦卫星惊世界
袁鹰
1958-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    小麦卫星惊世界
    袁鹰小麦卫星出青海，亩产八千惊世界，千里的雷声万里的闪，带着喜讯传开来！一望无边富饶地，沉沉睡了千万载，英雄的时代英雄的人，手把秘密来揭开。青海小麦长得好，政治挂帅是法宝，多大的胆就多大的产，坐上火箭上云霄。麦香随着黄河走，麦囤比得昆仑高，再干上一冬又一春，更大的卫星看明朝！
