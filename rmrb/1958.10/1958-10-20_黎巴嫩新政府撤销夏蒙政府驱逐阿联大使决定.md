Title: Lebanese New Government Revokes Chamoun Government's Decision to Expel UAE Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1958-10-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 19th - According to a broadcast by Baghdad Radio, Beirut announced on the 18th that the new Lebanese government had decided to revoke the decision made by the Chamoun government in May of this year to expel the ambassador of the United Arab Republic to Beirut, on the pretext that the United Arab Republic had interfered in Lebanon's internal affairs.

Damascus Radio quoted Lebanese official sources in Beirut on the evening of the 18th as saying that the Lebanese government will now develop its relations with all Arab countries, especially with the United Arab Republic. These sources also said that the Lebanese Ministry of Foreign Affairs is currently preparing to study the issue of developing Lebanon's economic relations with Arab countries and countries around the world. It is reported that the Lebanese Foreign Minister, Oueini, contacted several Lebanese diplomatic envoys abroad by telephone on the 18th to discuss this issue.

### 黎巴嫩新政府撤销夏蒙政府驱逐阿联大使决定

1958-10-20
第5版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩新政府
    撤销夏蒙政府驱逐阿联大使决定
    新华社大马士革19日电　据巴格达电台广播，贝鲁特18日宣布，黎巴嫩新政府决定撤销今年5月间夏蒙政府借口阿拉伯联合共和国干涉黎巴嫩内政而作出的关于驱逐阿拉伯联合共和国驻贝鲁特大使的决定。
    大马士革电台18日晚援引贝鲁特黎巴嫩官方人士的谈话说，黎巴嫩政府目前将发展它同一切阿拉伯国家、特别是同阿拉伯联合共和国的关系。这些人士又说，黎巴嫩外交部目前准备研究发展黎巴嫩同阿拉伯国家和世界各国的经济关系的问题。据报道，黎巴嫩外交部长乌埃尼18日用电话同黎巴嫩驻国外的几个外交使节就这个问题进行了接触。
