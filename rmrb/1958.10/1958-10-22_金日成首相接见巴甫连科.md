Title: Premier Kim Il-sung Receives Pavlenko
Author:
Date: 1958-10-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 20th: According to the Korean Central News Agency, Korean Premier Kim Il-sung received Pavlenko, the Soviet Minister of Power Stations, who is currently visiting Korea, today.

	Pavlenko came to Korea at the invitation of Korean Minister of Electricity Kim Tu-sam. He arrived in Pyongyang by plane on the 16th.

### 金日成首相接见巴甫连科

1958-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    金日成首相接见巴甫连科
    新华社平壤20日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社报道，朝鲜内阁首相金日成今天接见了正在朝鲜访问的苏联电站部部长巴甫连科。
    巴甫连科是应朝鲜电气相金斗三的邀请前来朝鲜访问的。他在16日乘飞机到达平壤。
