Title: The People of All Countries are on Our Side (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1958-10-07
Page: 4

The people of Bulgaria held rallies everywhere to protest the American military provocations and war threats in the Taiwan Strait. From September 14th to 18th, our embassy in Bulgaria received more than 900 telegrams and documents from protest rallies across Bulgaria supporting the Chinese people. This is a scene of cultural workers in Sofia passing a telegram to the Chinese Embassy. Bulgarian Telegraph Agency manuscript (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

Workers, peasants, cadres, teachers, students, and soldiers in Czechoslovakia have expressed their opposition to American aggressive actions in the Taiwan Strait. This is a scene of workers in an assembly workshop of a mechanical factory in Czechoslovakia holding a rally on September 27th to pass a resolution against American aggression in the Taiwan Strait.
		Czechoslovak Telegraph Agency manuscript (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

On September 29th, people in Delhi, India, braved the rain to demonstrate in front of the American Embassy in India, demanding that American troops withdraw from Taiwan and its neighboring areas. The picture shows the demonstrating masses. Xinhua News Agency manuscript (Delhi faxed photo)

The Canadian public demonstrated in front of the American Embassy, opposing American military provocations against China and demanding that the United States withdraw from the Taiwan Strait. Patriotic overseas Chinese also participated in the demonstration.

### 各国人民在我们一边（图片）

1958-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    各国人民在我们一边
    保加利亚各地人民纷纷举行集会，抗议美国在台湾海峡进行的军事挑衅和战争威胁。从9月14日到18日，我国驻保加利亚大使馆收到保加利亚各地抗议集会通过的支持中国人民的电报和文件已达九百多份。这是索非亚的文化工作者们通过致中国大使馆的电报时情形。保加利亚通讯社稿（新华社发）
    捷克斯洛伐克的工人、农民、机关干部、教师、学生和部队战士们纷纷表示反对美国在台湾海峡的侵略行动。这是捷克斯洛伐克一个机械工厂装配车间的工人9月27日举行集会通过反对美国侵略台湾海峡的决议。
                       捷克斯洛伐克通讯社稿（新华社发）
    印度德里人民9月29日冒雨到美国驻印度大使馆前示威。要求美军撤出台湾和它的邻近地区。图为示威的群众队伍。新华社稿（德里传真照片）
  加拿大公众到美国大使馆前示威，反对美国对中国进行军事挑衅，要求美国从台湾海峡撤走。热爱祖国的华侨也参加了示威的行列。
