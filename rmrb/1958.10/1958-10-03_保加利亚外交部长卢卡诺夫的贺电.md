Title: Telegram from Bulgarian Foreign Minister Lukanov
Author: Ka Lukanov
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

Telegram from Bulgarian Foreign Minister Lukanov to Comrade Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of the 9th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, please accept, Comrade Minister, my most sincere and fraternal congratulations. I wish you further achievements in defeating the despicable imperialist provocations and aggressions, defending your country's national independence, returning Taiwan to the motherland, consolidating the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and striving tirelessly for peace in Asia and the world.

                  Ka. Lukanov, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Bulgaria

### 保加利亚外交部长卢卡诺夫的贺电
卡·卢卡诺夫
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    保加利亚外交部长卢卡诺夫的贺电中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅同志：　　在中华人民共和国成立九周年纪念日的时候，部长同志，请接受我最诚挚的、兄弟般的祝贺。祝您在击败卑鄙的帝国主义挑衅和侵略，保卫你们国家的民族独立，使台湾归还祖国、巩固以苏联为首的社会主义阵营和争取亚洲和世界和平的不倦的工作中取得进一步的成就。
                  保加利亚人民共和国外交部长　卡·卢卡诺夫
