Title: Four Athletes Break National 10,000-Meter Track Race Walking Record
Author:
Date: 1958-10-21
Page: 6

    Four Athletes Break National 10,000-Meter Track Race Walking Record
    Additional News: In this vigorous upsurge of sports, four athletes also broke the 1957 national record for the 10,000-meter track race walk today. Their results are: Chen Wanquan 51 minutes 9.2 seconds, Wang Maiheng 51 minutes 11.2 seconds, Zhou Zejun 51 minutes 25.4 seconds, and Su Jichang 51 minutes 33.8 seconds. The 1957 national record was 52 minutes 11.6 seconds, held by Guan Shaoying.

### 四名运动员破一万公尺跑道竞走全国纪录

1958-10-21
第6版()
专栏：

    四名运动员破一万公尺跑道竞走全国纪录
    又讯　在这个体育运动大为上，今天还有四名运动选手打破一万公尺跑道竞走的1957年全国纪录，他们的成绩是：陈万全五十一分九秒二，王迈恒五十一分十一秒二，周则俊五十一分二十五秒四，苏继昌五十一分三十三秒八。1957年的全国纪录是关绍英保持的五十二分十一秒六。
