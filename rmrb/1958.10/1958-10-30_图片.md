    The Youth Satellite Team and the Youth Flying Tiger Team of the Shanghai Transportation Company's No. 2 Automobile Team, in order to ensure the passage of marshal supplies, created a record of a four-ton truck towing twelve trailers, carrying 103 tons of goods. This is the "Giant Dragon" type automobile train, 73.3 meters long, driving on the street. Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Juanmei photographed.
