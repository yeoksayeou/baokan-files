Title: Our National Leaders Receive Military Parade Delegation, Also Receive Several Ethnic Minority Visiting Groups
Author:
Date: 1958-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: Chairman Mao Zedong, along with Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Peng Dehuai, Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, and Bao Erhan, received the National Day Parade Delegation of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the National Day Parade Delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers this morning.

At the same time, they also received visiting groups from various ethnic groups in Xinjiang, Qinghai, Ningxia, and other provinces and autonomous regions, the visiting group from Jianghua Yao Autonomous County in Hunan Province, the minority youth visiting groups from Sichuan, Yunnan, Guangxi, Xinjiang, Qinghai and other provinces and autonomous regions, and representatives attending the National Ethnic Publishing Work Conference. (Attached pictures)

Top right: Chairman Mao shaking hands with the military parade representatives. Bottom right: Chairman Mao receiving several ethnic minority visiting groups. The picture shows the scene during the reception.

　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Hou Bo photographed

### 我国家领导人接见部队观礼代表团  同时接见几个少数民族参观团

1958-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我国家领导人接见部队观礼代表团
    同时接见几个少数民族参观团
    新华社6日讯　毛泽东主席和朱德、刘少奇、彭德怀、李济深、黄炎培、包尔汉等，在今天上午接见了中国人民解放军国庆观礼代表团和中国人民志愿军国庆观礼代表团。
    同时还接见了新疆、青海、宁夏等省和自治区的各民族参观团和湖南省江华瑶族自治县参观团，四川、云南、广西、新疆、青海等省和自治区的少数民族青年参观团，参加全国民族出版工作会议的代表等。（附图片）
