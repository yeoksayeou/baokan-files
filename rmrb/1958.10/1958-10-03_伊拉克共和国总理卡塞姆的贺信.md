Author:
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

Telegram of Greetings from Iraqi Republic Prime Minister Qassem

Dear Prime Minister,

I am pleased to extend to Your Excellency my most sincere greetings. Taking advantage of the visit of the Iraqi Republic delegation to your country, I would like to express to Your Excellency my sincere wishes for the National Day of the People's Republic of China. I express my admiration for the progress and creative development achieved by the noble Chinese people.

I extend to Your Excellency, the Chinese government, and the Chinese people my heartfelt thanks and sincere respect for the noble and friendly stance that the Chinese government and people have taken towards the Iraqi revolution and the intrusion of American and British troops into our brotherly neighbors, Lebanon and Jordan. I hope Your Excellency is confident that Iraq is paying close attention to the developments in the international situation caused by the ambitions stirred up by People's China's demands for territorial waters and natural national boundaries. It expresses sympathy and support for China's legitimate demands, and it strives to support China in all international forums. It hopes that the resolution of this matter will be in line with China's interests and will guarantee world peace.

Finally, I express my sincere friendship to Your Excellency and my most sincere wishes to the great Chinese people.

                        Brigadier General Abdul Karim Qassem
                        Prime Minister of the Iraqi Republic

### 伊拉克共和国总理卡塞姆的贺信
阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·卡塞姆准将
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    伊拉克共和国总理卡塞姆的贺信亲爱的总理：
    我高兴地向阁下致以最真挚的致意。我乘伊拉克共和国代表团访问贵国之便，向阁下表示对中华人民共和国国庆节的诚挚祝愿。我对高贵的中国人民所取得的进步和创造性的发展表示敬佩。
    中国政府和人民对于伊拉克革命和美英军队侵入我们的兄弟邻邦黎巴嫩和约旦都采取了高贵的友好的立场，为此，我向阁下、中国政府和中国人民致以衷心的感谢和诚实的敬意。我希望阁下相信，野心家因人民中国对领海和自然国界的要求而激起的国际局势的发展，伊拉克是十分注意的，它对于中国的合法要求表示同情和支持，它在一切国际场合中为支援中国而努力，它希望这件事的解决符合中国的利益，而且保证世界的和平。
    最后，我对阁下表示真诚的友爱，对伟大的中国人民表示最真诚的祝愿。
                        伊拉克共和国总理
                            阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·卡塞姆准将
