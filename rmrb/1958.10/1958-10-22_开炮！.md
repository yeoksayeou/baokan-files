Title: Fire!
Author: Tian Jian
Date: 1958-10-22
Page: 8

    Fire!
    Tian Jian's storm rises, the waves are high, the robbers do not listen to advice; the motherland's heroic artillerymen, for the sake of peace, fire! Hit them so they lower their heads, hit them so they bend over; a cannon blast shakes the sea, heroic artillerymen fire well! We must punish the enemy and not allow them to clamor again, heroes, artillerymen, fire! Fire! Fire!
  (October 20th, Fujian Front)

### 开炮！
田间
1958-10-22
第8版()
专栏：

    开炮！
    田间暴风起，海浪高，强盗们不听劝告；祖国英勇的炮手，为了和平，开炮！打得他们低下头，打得他们弯下腰；一声炮响大海摇，英雄炮手打得好！我们要惩罚敌人，不准他们再叫嚣，英雄们，炮手们，开炮！开炮！开炮！
  （10月20日福建前线）
