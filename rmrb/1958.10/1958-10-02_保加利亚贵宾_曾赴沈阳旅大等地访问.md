Title: Bulgarian Guests Visited Shenyang, Lushun-Dalian and Other Places

Author:

Date: 1958-10-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch—According to Xinhua News Agency, the Bulgarian National Assembly Delegation and the Sofia City People's Council Delegation returned to Beijing from Tianjin on the afternoon of September 29th. Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General, greeted the distinguished guests at the airport. Prior to this, the Bulgarian National Assembly Delegation and the Sofia City People's Council Delegation visited Shenyang and Anshan. On September 27th, the distinguished guests visited the Lushunkou Base of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy and also visited the Lushun History Museum and the "September 3rd" Victory Memorial Tower.

On the morning of September 28th, the Bulgarian guests visited the Dalian Shipyard, which was developed with Soviet assistance, in Lushun-Dalian. In the afternoon, they flew from Lushun-Dalian to Tianjin and visited the Xinli Village People's Commune.

Liu Changsheng, member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and member of the Standing Committee of National People's Congress, and Lian Guan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, accompanied the distinguished guests on their visits to various places.

### 保加利亚贵宾  曾赴沈阳旅大等地访问

1958-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    保加利亚贵宾
    曾赴沈阳旅大等地访问
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯　保加利亚国民议会代表团和索非亚市人民会议代表团，9月29日下午由天津回到北京。全国人民代表大会常务委员会李济深副委员长和张苏副秘书长等到机场迎接贵宾们。在此以前，保加利亚国民议会代表团和索非亚市人民会议代表团访问了沈阳和鞍山。9月27日，贵宾们访问了中国人民解放军海军旅顺口基地，还参观了旅顺历史博物馆、“九·三”胜利纪念塔。
    保加利亚贵宾9月28日上午在旅大参观了在苏联帮助下发展起来的大连造船厂，下午从旅大乘飞机到天津，访问了新立村人民公社。
    陪同贵宾到各地参观的有中共中央委员、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员刘长胜，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长连贯。
