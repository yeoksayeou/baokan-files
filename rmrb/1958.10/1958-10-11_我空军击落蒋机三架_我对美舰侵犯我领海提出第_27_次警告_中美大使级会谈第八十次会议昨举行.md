Title: Our Air Force Shot Down Three Chiang Planes; We Issue 27th Warning Against U.S. Warship Intrusion into Our Territorial Waters; Eightieth Meeting of Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Held Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1958-10-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, October 10th: At 7:00 AM on October 10th, six Chiang Kai-shek Air Force F-86 type aircraft intruded into the airspace over the Sandu Ao and Longtian areas of Fujian, carrying out harassing activities. Our Air Force immediately met them head-on with a fierce counterattack. Three Chiang planes were shot down by our forces, two of which crashed near Longtian, and one crashed in the sea southeast of Pingtan Island. One Chiang army second lieutenant pilot was captured by our forces.
    Xinhua News Agency, October 10th: On October 10th, three more U.S. warships intruded into the territorial waters of the Ma-tsu and Pingtan areas of Fujian Province, China. This demonstrates that the United States continues to conduct military provocations against our country. In response, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the twenty-seventh serious warning.
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, October 10th: The eightieth meeting of the Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks was held at the Myśliwiecki Palace in Warsaw at 3:00 PM today. The meeting lasted for two hours and thirty-two minutes. After the meeting, both sides announced that the next meeting would continue at 3:00 PM on Wednesday, October 15th.

### 我空军击落蒋机三架  我对美舰侵犯我领海提出第27次警告  中美大使级会谈第八十次会议昨举行

1958-10-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我空军击落蒋机三架
    我对美舰侵犯我领海提出第27次警告
    中美大使级会谈第八十次会议昨举行
    新华社福建前线10日电　10日上午七时，蒋介石空军F—八六型飞机六架侵入我福建三都澳、龙田地区上空，进行袭扰活动，我空军当即予以迎头痛击，蒋机被我击落三架，其中两架坠落于龙田附近，一架坠落于平潭岛东南大海里。蒋军少尉驾驶员一人被我俘获。
    新华社10日讯　10月10日又有美国军舰三艘侵入我国福建省马祖和平潭地区的领海，这说明美国还在继续对我国进行军事挑衅，对此，我外交部发言人奉命第二十七次提出严重警告。
    新华社华沙10日电　中美两国大使级会谈第八十次会议，今天下午三时在华沙梅希里维茨基宫举行。会议历时两小时三十二分。会后双方宣布，下次会议将在10月15日（星期三）下午三时继续举行。
