Title: Broadly Connect with People of All Ethnic Groups to Build Socialism: The CPPCC Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region Committee Established
Author:
Date: 1958-10-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Yinchuan, October 15th. The first plenary session of the First Committee of the CPPCC Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region was held in Yinchuan City from October 12th to 13th. The meeting formally established the CPPCC Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region Committee.

One hundred and ten representatives from the Communist Party of China, various democratic parties, people's organizations, social figures, workers, farmers, industry and commerce, literature and art, science and technology, education, news, medicine and health, religion, and various minority ethnic groups attended the meeting.

The meeting elected Li Jinglin as Chairman of the First Committee of the CPPCC Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, Ma Siyi (Hui), Yuan Jinzhang, Li Chonghe, He Yijiang (Hui), and Hong Qingguo (Hui) as Vice-Chairmen, and Li Jian as Secretary-General. Su Bing (Hui, female) and fifteen others were elected as members of the Standing Committee.

At the meeting, Li Jinglin, Secretary of the Ningxia Working Committee of the CPC and Chairman of the CPPCC Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region Committee, analyzed the basic international and domestic situations in a speech. He reported on great achievements and beautiful prospects of industrial and agricultural production and cultural and educational undertakings in the Ningxia region since the Great Leap Forward, and elaborated on the CPPCC's working principles and tasks. The members unanimously and enthusiastically supported Li Jinglin's speech in their remarks, and reflected many situations and put forward many valuable opinions. In their speeches, they unanimously expressed their firm acceptance of the leadership of the Communist Party of China, and stated that the democratic parties should unite and cooperate with the Communist Party, broadly and closely connect with people of all ethnic groups, and jointly build socialism. In their speeches, the members particularly proposed strengthening the study of Chairman Mao's works, studying the Party's general line and current affairs policies, actively participating in physical labor, and strengthening self-ideological transformation.

### 广泛联系各族人民建设社会主义  政协宁夏回族自治区委员会成立

1958-10-16
第6版()
专栏：

    广泛联系各族人民建设社会主义
    政协宁夏回族自治区委员会成立
    新华社银川15日电　政协宁夏回族自治区第一届委员会第一次全体会议，于10月12日到13日在银川市举行。会议正式成立了政协宁夏回族自治区委员会。
    出席会议的有中国共产党、各民主党派、人民团体、社会人士、工人、农民、工商业、文艺、科学技术、教育、新闻、医药卫生、宗教和各少数民族代表一百一十名。
    会议选举李景林为政协宁夏回族自治区第一届委员会主席，马思义（回族）、袁金章、李冲和、何义江（回族）、洪清国（回族）为副主席，李健为秘书长。并选举苏冰（回族、女）等十六人为常务委员会委员。
    会上，中共宁夏工委书记处书记、政协宁夏回族自治区委员会主席李景林在讲话中分析了国际和国内的基本形势，报告了大跃进以来宁夏地区工农业生产和文化教育事业的伟大成就和美丽前景，并阐述了政协的工作方针和任务。委员们在发言中一致热烈拥护李景林的讲话，并反映了许多情况，提了许多宝贵意见。他们在发言中一致表示坚决接受中国共产党的领导，各民主党派要和共产党团结合作，广泛地密切联系各族人民，共同建设社会主义。各位委员在发言中特别提出要加强学习毛主席著作、学习党的总路线和时事政策，积极地参加体力劳动，加强自我思想改造。
