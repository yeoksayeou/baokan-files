Title: Chinese Delegation Leaves Beijing for Soviet Union to Attend Asian-African Writers' Conference
Author:
Date: 1958-10-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Chinese delegation of twenty-one members, headed by Mao Dun (团长), Zhou Yang (副团长), and Ba Jin (巴金), left Beijing for Tashkent, Soviet Union, in batches by plane on the 1st and 3rd of this month to attend the Asian-African Writers' Conference. Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Shao Quanlin, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association, Yan Wenjing, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Writers Association, Zhang Guangnian, Editor-in-Chief of "Wenyi Bao," Chen Baichen, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "People's Literature," Zang Kejia, Editor-in-Chief of "Poetry," and Chen Bingyi, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "Translation," were at the airport to see them off. Sudarikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, was also at the airport to see them off.

The Asian-African Writers' Conference will be held on the 7th of this month in Tashkent, the capital of the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic. The members of the Chinese delegation are (in order of the number of strokes in their surnames): Ge Baoquan, Ye Junjian, Liu Baiyu, Qu Bo, Zunun Kadir (Uyghur), Xiao San, Ji Xianlin, Zhao Shuli, Yuan Shuipai, Guo Xiaochuan, Na. Sayinchaoketu (Mongolian), Kurban Ali (Kazak), Xu Guangping, Zhang Geng, Yang Shuo, Yang Mo, Malaqinfu (Mongolian), Xie Bingxin.

### 出席亚非作家会议  我国代表团离京赴苏

1958-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    出席亚非作家会议
    我国代表团离京赴苏
    新华社3日讯　出席亚非作家会议的我国代表团一行二十一人，在团长茅盾、副团长周扬、巴金率领下，已于本月1日、3日分批乘机离京赴苏联塔什干。到机场欢送的有中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙、中国作家协会副主席邵荃麟、中国作家协会书记处书记严文井、“文艺报”主编张光年、“人民文学”副主编陈白尘、“诗刊”主编臧克家、“译文”副主编陈冰夷等人。苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫也到机场欢送。
    亚非作家会议将于本月7日在苏联乌兹别克共和国首都塔什干举行。我国代表团团员有（以姓氏笔划为序）：戈宝权　叶君健　刘白羽　曲　波　祖农·哈迪尔（维族）萧　三　季羡林　赵树理　袁水拍郭小川　纳·赛音朝克图（蒙族）库尔班阿里（哈族）许广平　张庚杨　朔　杨　沫　玛拉沁夫（蒙族）谢冰心
