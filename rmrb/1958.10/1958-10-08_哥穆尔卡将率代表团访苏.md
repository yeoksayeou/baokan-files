Title: Gomulka to Lead Delegation to Visit Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1958-10-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 6th - A delegation from the Polish People's Republic, led by Gomulka, will pay a friendly visit to Moscow in the second half of October at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and the Soviet government.

The delegation members include: Chairman of the Council of State, Zawadzki; Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz; member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Morawski; Chairman of the Peasants' Party, Ignar; Chairman of the Democratic Party, Kulczyński; and eleven others.

### 哥穆尔卡将率代表团访苏

1958-10-08
第5版()
专栏：

    哥穆尔卡将率代表团访苏
    新华社华沙6日电　以哥穆尔卡为首的波兰人民共和国代表团应苏联共产党中央委员会、苏联最高苏维埃主席团和苏联政府的邀请将于10月下半月前往莫斯科进行友好访问。
    代表团成员包括：国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基、部长会议主席西伦凯维兹、波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员莫拉夫斯基、农民党主席伊格纳尔、民主党主席库尔钦斯基等十一人。
