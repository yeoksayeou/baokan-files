Title: 10.7 Million Tons Will Be Surpassed
Author: He Bei
Date: 1958-10-14
Page: 8

    10.7 million tons will be surpassed
    Row upon row of blast furnaces we build
    Ton upon ton of molten iron flows from the tap
    Steel bars held firmly in our hands
    A constant roar from the blast furnaces
    Guarantee letters spread throughout the land
    10.7 million tons will surely be surpassed
    Changchun Automotive Factory Worker, He Bei
