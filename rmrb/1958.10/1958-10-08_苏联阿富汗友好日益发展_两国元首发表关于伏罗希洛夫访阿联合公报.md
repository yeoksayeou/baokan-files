Title: Soviet-Afghan Friendship Grows Daily: Heads of State Issue Joint Communiqué on Voroshilov's Visit to Afghanistan
Author: 
Date: 1958-10-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 7th, Moscow Dispatch: TASS reported that the President of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, K.Y. Voroshilov, and the King of Afghanistan, Mohammed Zahir Shah, signed a joint communiqué on Voroshilov's visit to Afghanistan on the 6th in Kabul, the capital of Afghanistan.

The communiqué stated that the Soviet guests were warmly and sincerely received by Afghan officials and residents everywhere.

The communiqué stated that the talks between Voroshilov and his entourage with His Majesty King Mohammed Zahir Shah of Afghanistan and other national leaders of Afghanistan were conducted in an atmosphere of sincerity and mutual deep understanding.

Both sides pointed out the good results of Soviet-Afghan economic and technical cooperation and trade, and expressed their conviction that further achievements will be made in the development of this relationship and cultural ties between the two countries.

During the sincere and friendly talks, the two sides exchanged views on important international issues and issues related to Soviet-Afghan relations, and reaffirmed that the two countries' positions on these issues, based on the Bandung Principles and the principles of the United Nations Charter, are similar and in many respects identical.

Both sides noted with satisfaction that Voroshilov's visit to Afghanistan has further consolidated the friendly relations between the Soviet Union and Afghanistan, which is an example of successful cooperation between countries with different political and social systems.

Both sides expressed their conviction that this will help further consolidate the cause of peace and security in the Middle East and the world.

Xinhua News Agency, October 7th, Stalinabad Dispatch: TASS reported that the President of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Voroshilov, and his entourage arrived in Stalinabad, the capital of the Tajik Republic, on the 6th from Kabul.

In the evening of that day, Voroshilov attended a performance by the artistic masters of the Tajik Opera and Ballet Theatre. (Attached Picture)

Voroshilov visited Afghanistan on October 1. The picture shows Voroshilov visiting the bread joint factory, which was built with the help of Soviet experts.

### 苏联阿富汗友好日益发展  两国元首发表关于伏罗希洛夫访阿联合公报

1958-10-08
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联阿富汗友好日益发展
    两国元首发表关于伏罗希洛夫访阿联合公报
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席克·叶·伏罗希洛夫和阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿6日在阿富汗首都喀布尔签署了一项关于伏罗希洛夫访问阿富汗的联合公报。
    公报说，苏联客人到处都受到阿富汗官员和居民的热烈和真诚的接待。
    公报说，伏罗希洛夫及其随行人员同阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿陛下和阿富汗的其他国家领导人的会谈是在诚恳和互相深切了解的气氛中进行的。
    双方指出了苏联和阿富汗在经济技术合作方面和贸易方面的良好成果，并且表示确信在两国之间的这种关系以及文化联系的发展上将要取得进一步的成就。
    在真诚友好的会谈中，双方就重要的国际问题以及苏联和阿富汗的关系问题交换了意见，并且重申，两国对这些问题所采取的以万隆原则和联合国宪章原则为基础的立场是相近的，在许多方面是相同的。
    双方满意地指出，伏罗希洛夫及其随行人员访问阿富汗这件事使苏联和阿富汗的友好关系更加巩固，这种关系是不同政治制度和社会制度的国家成功地进行合作的范例。
    双方表示确信，这有助于进一步巩固中近东地区和全世界的和平和安全的事业。
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社斯大林纳巴德讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫和他的随员们6日由喀布尔到达了塔吉克共和国首都斯大林纳巴德。
    这一天的晚间，伏罗希洛夫出席了塔吉克歌剧和芭蕾舞剧院的艺术大师表演会。（附图片）
    伏罗希洛夫在10月1日到阿富汗访问，图为伏罗希洛夫参观面包联合工厂，这个工厂是在苏联专家帮助下建成的。
                                        传真稿（新华社发）
