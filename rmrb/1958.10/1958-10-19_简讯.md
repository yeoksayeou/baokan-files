Soviet-Czech Sign Commodity Supply Agreement

Moscow, October 15th (Xinhua News Agency): The Soviet Union and Czechoslovakia signed an agreement in Prague on the 14th. According to the agreement, Czechoslovakia will supply the Soviet Union with products worth approximately 1.85 billion rubles between 1959 and 1965, including carbon steel machinery, high-voltage machinery, equipment for the production of artificial fibers and their products, refrigeration equipment, compressors, machines for manufacturing plastic industrial products, and equipment for the rubber industry.

The Soviet Union will supply Czechoslovakia with various commodities.

U.S. Loans to Iran to Expand Airport

Tehran, October 16th: "Observer" reported that an agreement was signed here in which the United States will provide Iran with a loan of $2.5 million. The loan will be used, with the participation of American engineers, to expand the runway of Tehran's Mehrabad Airport so that the airport can accommodate large jet aircraft.

    简讯
    苏捷签订供应商品协定
    莫斯科15日讯：苏联和捷克斯洛伐克14日在布拉格签订了一项协定。根据这项协定，在1959年到1965年期间，捷克斯洛伐克将供给苏联总值约十八亿五千万卢布的产品，其中包括碳素钢机械、高压机械、生产人造纤维及其制品的装备、冷藏设备、压缩机、制造塑料工业品的机器和橡皮工业的装备。
    苏联将供给捷克斯洛伐克各种商品。
    美国贷款给伊朗扩大机场
    德黑兰16日讯：“观察报”报道，这里签订了美国向伊朗提供二百五十万美元贷款的协定。这笔贷款用来在美国工程师的参加下扩大德黑兰麦赫拉巴德机场的跑道，以使飞机场能够接纳大型喷气式飞机。
