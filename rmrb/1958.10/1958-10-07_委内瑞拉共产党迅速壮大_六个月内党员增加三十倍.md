Title: Venezuelan Communist Party Rapidly Grows: Membership Increases Thirtyfold in Six Months
Author:
Date: 1958-10-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. According to Uruguay's "People's Daily," Venezuelan Communist Party leader Gallegos told the newspaper's reporter that the Venezuelan Communist Party has grown thirtyfold in the past six months and is expected to grow fiftyfold by the end of the year. Gallegos said that the Venezuelan Communist Youth League has also experienced rapid development. Currently, the Venezuelan Communist Party has established its own organizations in all states of the country and has representatives in almost all city councils. Gallegos emphasized that in Venezuela, it is unimaginable that any important activity would take place without the participation of the Communist Party.

### 委内瑞拉共产党迅速壮大  六个月内党员增加三十倍

1958-10-07
第5版()
专栏：

    委内瑞拉共产党迅速壮大
    六个月内党员增加三十倍
    据新华社5日讯　据乌拉圭“人民报”报道，委内瑞拉共产党领导人加莱戈斯对该报记者说，委内瑞拉共产党在今年六个月中壮大了三十倍，年底可望壮大到五十倍。加莱戈斯说，委内瑞拉共产主义青年联盟也获得迅速发展。目前，委内瑞拉共产党在全国各州都建立了自己的组织，并且几乎在所有的市议会中都有它的代表。加莱戈斯强调指出，在委内瑞拉，不能想像会有那一个重要活动没有共产党参加。
