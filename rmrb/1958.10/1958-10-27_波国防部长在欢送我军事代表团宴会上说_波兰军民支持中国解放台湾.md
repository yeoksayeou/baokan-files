Title: Polish Minister of National Defense Says at Banquet Feting Our Military Delegation: Polish Military and Civilians Support China's Liberation of Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1958-10-27
Page: 4

It is Imperative That the United States Get Out of Taiwan
Polish Minister of National Defense Says at Banquet Feting Our Military Delegation
Polish Military and Civilians Support China's Liberation of Taiwan
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, October 25th: Polish Minister of National Defense, Marian Spychalski, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 24th to bid farewell to the Chinese Military Delegation led by Marshal Ye Jianying.

Attending the banquet were senior generals of the Polish army, commanders of various arms of service and military regions. Wang Bingnan, China's ambassador to Poland, was also invited to attend the banquet.

In his speech at the banquet, Spychalski pointed out that in the Chinese people's struggle to liberate Taiwan, the Polish people and the Polish army stand with the Chinese people and the Chinese army. In his speech, he also emphasized the growing friendship between the Chinese and Polish armies and expressed his gratitude for China's sending a military delegation to visit Poland.

In his speech, Marshal Ye Jianying said that the friendship between the people and armies of China and Poland is built on the foundation of great Marxism-Leninism and internationalism. Therefore, in the Chinese people's struggle to oppose the military provocations of the American imperialists and to safeguard their sovereignty and territorial integrity, they have received the firm support of the Polish people and army. Similarly, the Chinese people and army firmly support all the just demands of the Polish People's Republic in the international arena and the Oder-Neisse peaceful border. The fraternal friendship and fighting unity of the people and armies of socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union is the most powerful force in the world, and is a reliable guarantee for defending the great socialist cause, defending world peace, and thwarting the aggression and war policies of the American imperialists.

Marshal Ye Jianying pointed out that the Polish People's Army has become a powerful modern army. He said that the Polish army has many things worthy of our study and research in terms of modernization.

At the banquet, the hosts and guests were in high spirits, constantly toasting to the friendship between the two peoples and armies. The two sides also exchanged gifts. The gifts presented by the Chinese military delegation included two films, "Flying Together in the Sky" and "Severely Punish the Chiang Bandit Troops".

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, October 26th: The Chinese Military Delegation led by Marshal Ye Jianying, after being invited to participate in the celebration of the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Polish People's Army and paying a friendly visit to Poland, left Warsaw by plane this morning to return home.

Those who came to the airport to see them off included Polish Minister of National Defense Spychalski, Deputy Minister and Chief of the General Staff Boziwowski, and Deputy Minister and Director of the Political Department Sarnecki, among others.

Wang Bingnan, the Chinese Ambassador to Poland, and staff of the embassy also went to the airport to see them off.

Marshal Ye Jianying sent a telegram to Polish Minister of National Defense Spychalski upon leaving Polish territory to express his gratitude.

### 波国防部长在欢送我军事代表团宴会上说  波兰军民支持中国解放台湾

1958-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

    美国不滚出台湾是不成的
    波国防部长在欢送我军事代表团宴会上说
    波兰军民支持中国解放台湾
    新华社华沙25日电　波兰国防部部长马里安·斯彼哈尔斯基24日晚设宴欢送以叶剑英元帅为首的中国军事代表团。
    出席宴会的有波兰军队的高级将领、各兵种和各军区司令。我国驻波兰大使王炳南也应邀出席了宴会。
    斯彼哈尔斯基在宴会上致词时指出，在中国人民解放台湾的斗争中，波兰人民和波兰军队是同中国人民和军队站在一起的。他在讲话中还强调指出了波中两国军队的友谊的日益发展，并对中国派遣军事代表团到波兰访问表示感谢。
    叶剑英团长在致词时说，中、波两国人民和军队的友谊是建筑在伟大的马克思列宁主义和国际主义的基础之上的。因此，在中国人民为反对美帝国主义者的军事挑衅、维护自己主权和领土完整的斗争中得到了波兰人民和军队的坚决支持。同样，中国人民和军队也坚决支持波兰人民共和国在国际上的一切正义要求和奥得—尼斯河的和平边界。以苏联为首的社会主义阵营各国人民和军队的兄弟友谊和战斗团结，是世界上最强大的力量，是保卫伟大的社会主义事业、保卫世界和平、粉碎美帝国主义侵略政策和战争政策的可靠保证。
    叶剑英团长指出，波兰人民军已经成为一支强大的现代化军队。他说，波兰军队在现代化建设方面有很多东西值得我们学习和研究。
    宴会上，宾主双方情绪热烈，不断为两国人民和军队的友谊干杯。双方并互相赠送了礼品，我国军事代表团赠送的礼品中有“长空比翼”和“严惩蒋匪军”两部影片。
    新华社华沙26日电　由叶剑英元帅率领的中国军事代表团，应邀参加了波兰人民军建军十五周年庆祝典礼和在波兰进行了友好访问以后，今天上午乘飞机离开华沙返国。
    到机场欢送的有波兰国防部长斯彼哈尔斯基、副部长兼总参谋长博齐沃夫斯基、副部长兼政治部主任萨热茨基等。
    中国驻波兰大使王炳南和使馆人员也到机场送行。
    叶剑英元帅在离开波兰国境时，曾致电波兰国防部长斯彼哈尔斯基表示感谢。
