Title: Polish Poet, Essayist Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-10-19
Page: 3
　
    Polish Poet, Essayist Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, October 13th—According to the 1958 implementation plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Polish art historian, poet Tadeusz Różewicz and essayist Witold Zalewski arrived in Beijing today. They will visit China to learn about the situation in the fields of culture and art.

### 波兰诗人、散文家到京

1958-10-19
第3版()
专栏：欢迎远道而来的朋友

    波兰诗人、散文家到京
    新华社13日讯　根据中波文化合作协定1958年执行计划，波兰艺术史家、诗人塔载乌什·罗宰维奇和散文家维托哈德·萨莱夫斯基于今日到达北京。他们将在我国访问文化艺术等方面的情况。
