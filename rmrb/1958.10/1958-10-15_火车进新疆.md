Title: Train Enters Xinjiang
Author:
Date: 1958-10-15
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, 14th. The first passenger train from Lanzhou to the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region entered the first station within Xinjiang - Hongliuhe Station - on the morning of the 11th. Passengers can then travel 180 kilometers west by car to reach Hami.

The opening of the railway to Hongliuhe Station will greatly help to alleviate the transportation tension in the Xinjiang region and support the construction of Xinjiang. (Attached picture)

### 火车进新疆

1958-10-15
第2版()
专栏：

    火车进新疆
    新华社乌鲁木齐14日电　第一列从兰州开往新疆维吾尔自治区的客车，在11日清晨驶进了新疆境内的第一个车站——红柳河车站，旅客由这里再乘汽车西行一百八十公里，便可到达哈密。
    火车通到红柳河车站以后，对于缓和新疆地区运输紧张情况，支援新疆建设事业有很大作用。（附图片）
