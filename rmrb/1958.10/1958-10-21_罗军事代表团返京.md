Title: Romanian Military Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-10-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 20th—The Romanian military delegation headed by General Salajan flew back to Beijing by special plane this morning after visiting Wuhan.
    During their stay in Wuhan, the distinguished guests visited the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge, Wuhan Iron and Steel No. 1 Blast Furnace, Wuhan University, and visited the Dongfanghong People's Commune on Wuhan Tongyi Street, and attended a welcome banquet given by Lieutenant General Li Chengfang, head of the garrison.

### 罗军事代表团返京

1958-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    罗军事代表团返京
    新华社武汉20日电　以萨拉扬上将为首的罗马尼亚访华军事代表团，在武汉参观访问后，今日上午乘专机飞返北京。
    贵宾们在武汉期间，参观了武汉长江大桥、武钢一号高炉、武汉大学，并访问了武汉统一街的东方红人民公社，并出席了驻军首长李成芳中将的欢迎宴会。
