Title: Zheng Zhenduo, Cai Shufan and Other Comrades' Funeral Committee Established
Author:
Date: 1958-10-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 21st. The funeral committee for Comrades Zheng Zhenduo, Cai Shufan and sixteen others was established on the 20th. The list of members of the funeral committee is as follows:
    Chen Yi, He Long, Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, Bao Erhan, Liao Chengzhi, Zhang Xiruo, Shen Yanbing, Ding Xilin, Wang Yechiu, Lu Xuzhang, Qi Yanming, Liu Zhiming, Lu Ping, He Qifang, He Xilin, Xiao San, Wu Lengxi, Chen Kehan, Chen Yuan, Chen Zhongjing, Su Lingyang, Qu Wu, Ma Yinchu, Rong Gaotang, Xu Senyu, Xia Yan, Huang Zhong, Zhang Hanfu, Zeng Yongquan, Zhang Jie, Zhang Su, Zhang Jingfu, Zhang Zhixiang, Chu Tunan, Cai Tingkai, Qian Junrui, Sa Kongliao.

### 郑振铎蔡树藩等同志  治丧委员会成立

1958-10-22
第6版()
专栏：

    郑振铎蔡树藩等同志
    治丧委员会成立
    新华社21日讯　郑振铎、蔡树藩等十六位同志治丧委员会在20日成立，治丧委员会委员名单如下：
    陈毅，贺龙，郭沫若，陈叔通，包尔汉，廖承志，张奚若，沈雁冰，丁西林，王冶秋，卢绪章，齐燕铭，刘芝明，陆平，何其芳，何锡麟，萧三，吴冷西，陈克寒，陈垣，陈忠经，苏灵扬，屈武，马寅初，荣高棠，徐森玉，夏衍，黄中，章汉夫，曾涌泉，张杰，张苏，张劲夫，张致祥，楚图南，蔡廷锴，钱俊瑞，萨空了。
