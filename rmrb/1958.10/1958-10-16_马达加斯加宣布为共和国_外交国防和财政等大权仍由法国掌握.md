Title: Madagascar Declares Republic, but France Retains Control of Diplomacy, Defense, and Finance
Author:
Date: 1958-10-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tananarive, October 14th: The Territorial Assembly of Madagascar Island held a meeting on the 14th and passed a resolution declaring Madagascar a republic. It is reported that this declaration was made in accordance with the provisions of French President De Gaulle's "Constitution" regarding the status of overseas territories. According to these provisions, the Republic of Madagascar will continue to remain within the French Community as a "member state." Its major powers in diplomacy, defense, and finance will still be controlled by France.

### 马达加斯加宣布为共和国  外交国防和财政等大权仍由法国掌握

1958-10-16
第5版()
专栏：

    马达加斯加宣布为共和国
    外交国防和财政等大权仍由法国掌握
    据新华社14日讯　塔那那利佛消息：马达加斯加岛的领地议会14日举行会议，通过宣布马达加斯加为共和国。据报道，这一宣布是按照法国戴高乐“宪法”中关于海外属地地位的规定而作出的。根据这种规定，马达加斯加共和国将继续留在法兰西联邦里，成为一个“联邦成员国”。它的外交、国防和财政等大权将仍然由法国掌握。
