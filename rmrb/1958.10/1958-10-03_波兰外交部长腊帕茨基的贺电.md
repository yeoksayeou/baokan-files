Title: Telegram of Congratulations from Polish Foreign Minister Rapacki
Author: Ya. Lapatski
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

    Telegram of Congratulations from Polish Foreign Minister Rapacki
    To Comrade Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:
    Dear Comrade Minister, please accept my most sincere congratulations on the occasion of the ninth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.
    The fraternal friendship between our two peoples, based on the common goal of building socialism, is a great contribution to the struggle to defend peace and oppose the attempts of imperialist forces.
    The Polish people warmly support the Chinese people's just struggle to defend the integrity and sovereignty of Chinese territory.
    Comrade Minister, I wish you good health and further achievements in our fruitful work for the cause of peace and socialism.
                      Ya. Rapacki, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Poland

### 波兰外交部长腊帕茨基的贺电
亚·腊帕茨基
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    波兰外交部长腊帕茨基的贺电中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅同志：
    当中华人民共和国成立九周年的时候，亲爱的部长同志，请接受我最真诚的祝贺。
    基于建设社会主义的共同目的的我们两国人民的兄弟友谊是对保卫和平、反对帝国主义势力的企图斗争的巨大贡献。
    波兰人民热烈支持中国人民为保卫中国领土的完整和主权而进行的正义斗争。
    部长同志，祝你身体健康，在我们为和平和社会主义事业而进行的富有成效的工作中取得进一步的成就。
                      波兰人民共和国外交部长　亚·腊帕茨基
