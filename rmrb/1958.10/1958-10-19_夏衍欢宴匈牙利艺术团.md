Title: Xia Yan Hosts Banquet for Hungarian Art Troupe
Author: 
Date: 1958-10-19
Page: 3

    Xia Yan Hosts Banquet for Hungarian Art Troupe
    Xinhua News Agency, October 18th. Xia Yan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, held a banquet this evening to welcome the Hungarian Music and Ballet Art Troupe led by团长霍尔瓦特·尤赛夫.
    In his speech at the banquet, Xia Yan extended a warm welcome to the distinguished guests who had come from afar.
    巴拉沙·久尔吉, the artistic director of the Hungarian Art Troupe, also spoke at the meeting.
    诺格拉第, the Ambassador of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, also attended today's banquet.
    Also attending today's banquet were famous figures from the capital's music, dance, and other literary and artistic circles.
    Xinhua News Agency, October 17th. According to the 1958 implementation plan of the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, the Hungarian Music and Ballet Art Troupe, consisting of fifteen members, arrived in Beijing this afternoon at our invitation. The art troupe will conduct a six-week performance tour in our country.

### 夏衍欢宴匈牙利艺术团

1958-10-19
第3版()
专栏：

    夏衍欢宴匈牙利艺术团
    新华社18日讯　中华人民共和国文化部副部长夏衍今天晚间举行宴会，欢迎由霍尔瓦特·尤赛夫团长率领的匈牙利音乐、芭蕾舞艺术团。
    夏衍在会上的讲话中对远道而来的贵宾表示热烈的欢迎。
    匈牙利艺术团艺术指导巴拉沙·久尔吉也在会上讲了话。
    匈牙利人民共和国驻华大使诺格拉第也出席了今天的宴会。
    参加今天宴会的还有首都音乐、舞蹈等文艺界著名人士。
    新华社17日讯　根据中匈文化合作协定1958年执行计划，应邀来我国访问的匈牙利音乐、芭蕾舞艺术团一行十五人，在今天下午到达北京。艺术团将在我国作六个星期的访问演出。
