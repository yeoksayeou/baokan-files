Title: UAR to Establish Central Government; Vice President Asali Resigns
Author: 
Date: 1958-10-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, October 6th: It was officially announced here tonight that the President of the United Arab Republic will issue an order on the 7th to establish a central government for the Republic and executive committees for the two regions of Egypt and Syria.

    The central government will include three vice presidents and fifteen ministers. The three vice presidents are Boghdadi (concurrently Minister of Planning), Amir (concurrently Minister of Defense), and Hourani (concurrently Minister of Justice).

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, October 6th: Vice President Asali of the United Arab Republic submitted his resignation today.

    In a public statement issued tonight, Asali mentioned that Cairo newspapers recently implicated him in reports regarding the interrogation of former Iraqi Foreign Minister Jamali by Baghdad.

### 阿联将成立中央政府  副总统阿萨利提出辞职

1958-10-08
第5版()
专栏：

    阿联将成立中央政府
    副总统阿萨利提出辞职
    新华社开罗6日电　此间今天晚上正式宣布，阿拉伯联合共和国总统将在7日发布命令，成立共和国的中央政府和埃及、叙利亚两个地区的执行委员会。
    中央政府将包括三位副总统和十五名部长。三位副总统是波格达迪（兼计划部长）、阿密尔（兼国防部长）和胡拉尼（兼司法部长）。
    据新华社开罗6日电　阿拉伯联合共和国副总统阿萨利在今天提出辞职。
    阿萨利在今晚发表的一项公开声明中，提到了最近开罗报纸在关于巴格达对伊拉克前外交大臣贾马利进行的审讯的报道中曾经牵涉到他。
