Title: Deng Zihui and Vice Premier Chen Yi Receive Four Foreign Delegations Respectively
Author:
Date: 1958-10-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 16th: Vice Premier Deng Zihui received all members of the Bulgarian agricultural delegation led by Buchvarov, Minister of Rural Work Department of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee, this morning.
    Present at the reception were Yang Xiandong, Vice Minister of Agriculture, Cai Ziwei, and others.

    Xinhua News Agency, October 16th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received all members of two delegations from Thailand, consisting of members of the National Assembly, this afternoon. These two delegations are: the Thai Parliamentarian Delegation led by Nai Thahan Khamsiran, and the Thai Private Economic Liaison Delegation led by Nai Sain Marangon.
    Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Asian-African Solidarity, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and others.

    Xinhua News Agency, October 16th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received all members of the Greek Political and Cultural Delegation led by Greek National Assembly member Mavros this noon.
    Present at the reception were Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Lu Xuzhang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and others.

### 邓子恢和陈毅副总理分别接见四个外国代表团

1958-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

    邓子恢和陈毅副总理分别接见四个外国代表团
    新华社16日讯　邓子恢副总理今天上午接见了以保共中央农村工作部部长布契瓦洛夫为首的保加利亚农业代表团全体人员。
    接见时在座的，有农业部副部长杨显东、蔡子伟等人。
    新华社16日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了由泰国国会议员分别组成的两个代表团的全体人员。这两个代表团是：以乃他汉·堪希兰为首的泰国议员团，以乃沙茵·玛兰恭为首的泰国民间经济联络代表团。
    接见时在座的，有中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志，中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪等人。
    新华社16日讯　陈毅副总理今天中午接见了以希腊国会议员马布洛斯为首的希腊政治文化代表团全体人员。
    接见时，在座的有中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南和对外贸易部副部长卢绪章等人。
