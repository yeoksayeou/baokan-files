Title: U.S. Warships Intrude into My Matsu and Pingtan Area Territorial Waters, Provoking: Our Twenty-Fifth Serious Warning

Author: 
Date: 1958-10-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. On October 8th, two more U.S. warships intruded into the territorial waters of the Matsu and Pingtan areas of Fujian Province. This shows that the United States is continuing its military provocations against our country. In response, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the twenty-fifth serious warning.

### 美舰侵入我马祖和平潭地区领海挑衅  我第二十五次提出严重警告

1958-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    美舰侵入我马祖和平潭地区领海挑衅
    我第二十五次提出严重警告
    新华社8日讯　10月8日，又有美国军舰两艘侵入我国福建省的马祖和平潭地区的领海。这说明，美国还在继续对我国进行军事挑衅。对此，我外交部发言人奉命第二十五次提出严重警告。
