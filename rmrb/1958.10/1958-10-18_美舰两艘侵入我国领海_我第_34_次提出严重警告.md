Title: Two US Warships Invade China's Territorial Waters; We Issue 34th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1958-10-18
Page: 1

    Two US Warships Invade China's Territorial Waters
    We Issue 34th Serious Warning
    Xinhua News Agency, October 17th: On October 17th, two US warships invaded the territorial waters of the Pingtan and Matsu areas of Fujian Province. Regarding this military provocation by the United States, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 34th serious warning.

### 美舰两艘侵入我国领海  我第34次提出严重警告

1958-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    美舰两艘侵入我国领海
    我第34次提出严重警告
    新华社17日讯　10月17日，美国军舰两艘侵入我福建省平潭、马祖地区的领海。对于美国这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命第三十四次提出严重警告。
