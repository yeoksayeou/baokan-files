Title: African People Firmly Believe that Final Victory Belongs to the Chinese People, Vice Chairman of the Cameroon People's Union Firmly Supports Our Country's Just Struggle
Author: 
Date: 1958-10-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, October 20th - Ernest Ouandié, leader of the Cameroon National Liberation Movement and Vice Chairman of the Cameroon People's Union, told reporters in Hong Kong on the 18th: "All African people, especially the people of Cameroon, firmly support the Chinese people's just struggle to liberate Taiwan and safeguard the integrity of their territorial sovereignty."

Ouandié said: "Now, in the Taiwan Strait region, the Chinese people's struggle against American imperialist aggression is also a part of the entire struggle of the Asian and African people against imperialism. All peace-loving and freedom-loving countries and people support this struggle, so we believe that final victory will surely belong to the Chinese people, to the people who love peace and freedom."

This African people's leader believes that "The successful Chinese revolution provided a good example, showing that imperialism is not indestructible. China's revolutionary struggle inspired the national liberation struggles of the Asian and African people, opening a brilliant new page for the Asian and African national liberation movements." Ouandié continued, "I not only support China's armed revolution, but also the socialist construction that the Chinese people are now carrying out." He believes that "China's industrialization is a great contribution to the Asian and African people's struggle against imperialism."

Speaking about the Cameroon people's struggle for national independence and liberation, Ouandié said: "The Chinese people support our struggle. Because our struggle belongs to the struggle of the Asian and African people against imperialism, and the Chinese people support the national independence movements of all Asian and African countries."

Ouandié visited various parts of China for a month and participated in the celebrations of the ninth Chinese National Day in Beijing.

### 非洲人民深信最后胜利必属中国人民  喀麦隆人民联盟副主席坚决支持我国正义斗争

1958-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    非洲人民深信最后胜利必属中国人民
    喀麦隆人民联盟副主席坚决支持我国正义斗争
    新华社香港20日电　喀麦隆民族解放运动领袖、喀麦隆人民联盟副主席欧内斯特·乌安迪18日在香港对记者说：“所有非洲人民，尤其是喀麦隆人民，是坚决支持中国人民解放台湾、维护他们领土主权完整的正义斗争的。”
    乌安迪说：“现在在台湾海峡地区，中国人民进行的反对美帝国主义侵略的斗争，也是亚非人民反抗帝国主义的整个斗争的一部分。所有爱好和平与自由的国家和人民都支持这个斗争，因此我们相信最后胜利必定属于中国人民，属于爱好和平与自由的人民。”
    这位非洲人民领袖认为，“中国人民的革命成功，提供了一个良好榜样，说明了帝国主义并不是不可摧毁的。中国的革命斗争鼓舞了亚非人民的民族解放斗争，为亚非民族解放运动揭开了辉煌的、新的一页。”乌安迪接着说：“我不单支持中国的武装革命，同时也包括中国人民现在正在进行的社会主义建设。”他认为，“中国工业化对亚非人民反对帝国主义斗争是有很大贡献的。”
    在谈到喀麦隆人民进行民族独立解放斗争的情况时，乌安迪说：“中国人民是支持我们的斗争的。因为我们的斗争同属于亚非人民反抗帝国主义的斗争的一部分，而中国人民是支持亚非各国人民民族独立运动的。”
    乌安迪曾在中国各地作了一个月的访问，并在北京参加了第九届中国国庆节庆祝典礼。
