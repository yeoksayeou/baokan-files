Title: Nanjing and Chongqing Can See Carrier Rocket on the 25th and 26th
Author:
Date: 1958-10-24
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 23rd - TASS reports: In our country, Nanjing and other places on the 25th, and Chongqing and other places on the 26th, can see the Soviet Union's third artificial satellite carrier rocket with the naked eye.

The rocket will pass over Nanjing at 18:23 Beijing time on the 25th, appearing in the southwest direction. At 4:30 on the morning of the 26th, the rocket will pass over Chongqing, appearing in the north. Areas south of 34 degrees north latitude in our country will see it before sunrise, and areas between 27 and 46 degrees north latitude will potentially see it after sunset, near the aforementioned route at the aforementioned times.

### 南京重庆25、26日可看到运载火箭

1958-10-24
第2版()
专栏：

    南京重庆25、26日可看到运载火箭
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科消息：我国南京等地25日、重庆等地26日可以用肉眼看到苏联第三个人造卫星的运载火箭。
    25日傍晚北京时间十八点二十三分火箭经过南京，在西南方向出现。26日清晨四点三十分，火箭经过重庆，在北方出现。我国北纬三十四度以南地区在日出前出现，北纬二十七度到四十六度之间地区在日落后，于上述火箭经过时刻上述路线附近都有可能看到。
