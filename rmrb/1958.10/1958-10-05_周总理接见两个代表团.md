Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Two Delegations
Author:
Date: 1958-10-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 4th: Premier Zhou Enlai received this afternoon the Third Deputy Speaker of the Indonesian Parliament, Zainal Abidin Achmad, and the entire Indonesian National Committee delegation to China, headed by Nodo Hami Prazo. Also received were Indonesian parliamentarians Anwar Tjokroaminoto, Dalan, and Professor Abdurrahman.

　　Present at the reception were Vice Premier Chen Yi, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhang Xiruo, and Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission Wang Guangwei, among others.

　　Xinhua News Agency, 4th: Premier Zhou Enlai received this afternoon the entire Iraqi Cultural Delegation, headed by Abed Jarjara.

　　Present at the reception was Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhang Zhixiang.

### 周总理接见两个代表团

1958-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见两个代表团
    新华社4日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了印度尼西亚国会第三副议长宰纳尔·阿比丁·艾哈迈德和以诺多哈米普拉佐团长为首的印度尼西亚民族委员会访华代表团全体人员。同时被接见的还有印度尼西亚国会议员安瓦尔·佐克罗阿米诺托，国会议员达兰，和阿卜杜拉曼教授。
    接见时在座的有：陈毅副总理、中国人民外交学会会长张奚若和国家计划委员会副主任王光伟等。
    新华社4日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了以阿布德·扎尔扎拉团长为首的伊拉克文化代表团全体人员。
    接见时在座的有对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥。
