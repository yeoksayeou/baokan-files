Title: People's Rallies in Various Parts of North Korea Support Government Statement, Demanding Immediate Withdrawal of US Troops from South Korea
Author:
Date: 1958-10-31
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 30th: People in various parts of North Korea have been holding rallies for days in support of the statement issued by the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on October 28 regarding the complete withdrawal of the Chinese People's Volunteers from North Korea, and resolutely demanding the immediate withdrawal of US troops from South Korea.

In recent days, the Ministry of Commerce of North Korea, the Pyongyang Textile Machinery Manufacturing Factory, the Pyongan South Province Electric Power Company, and other institutions and factories have held rallies. Employees spoke at the meetings, condemning the US military for occupying South Korea and obstructing the peaceful reunification of Korea, and angrily demanding that the US military get out of South Korea.

At the rally at the Pyongyang Textile Machinery Manufacturing Factory, factory director Li Xixi said that the Chinese People's Volunteers had voluntarily withdrawn completely from North Korea in order to promote the peaceful resolution of the Korean issue, and the US aggressor troops no longer had any excuse to continue to stay in South Korea. He also said that in order to promote the peaceful reunification of the motherland, all workers in the factory are determined to complete the Five-Year Plan two years and two months ahead of schedule, and are determined to increase labor productivity to 4.7 times in the near future.

The turner Xu Changyi of this factory said in his speech at the rally that the Chinese People's Volunteers had completely withdrawn from North Korea for peace, but the United States was carrying out various military provocations in order to permanently occupy South Korea. They are building missile bases in South Korea, creating a threat of a new war. He said that the US military's occupation of South Korea is the root cause of all the suffering and disasters of the Korean people, and the US military must immediately get out of South Korea.

These rallies unanimously adopted resolutions, expressing their determination to wage an unyielding struggle to drive the US military out of South Korea and strive for the peaceful reunification of the motherland.

### 朝鲜各地人民集会拥护政府声明  要求美军立即撤出南朝鲜

1958-10-31
第6版()
专栏：

    朝鲜各地人民集会拥护政府声明
    要求美军立即撤出南朝鲜
    新华社平壤30日电　朝鲜各地人民连日举行集会，支持朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府在10月28日就中国人民志愿军从朝鲜全部撤出一事发表的声明，并且坚决要求美军立即撤出南朝鲜。
    连日来，朝鲜商业省、平壤纺织机器制造工厂、平安南道电业公司等机关和工厂都举行了集会。职工们在会上纷纷发言，谴责美军强占南朝鲜，阻挠朝鲜和平统一，并且愤怒地要求美军从南朝鲜滚出去。
    在平壤纺织机器制造工厂的集会上，厂长李硕熙说，中国人民志愿军为了促进和平解决朝鲜问题，主动地从朝鲜全部撤出，美国侵略军队再没有任何借口继续留在南朝鲜。他还说，全厂工人为了促进祖国的和平统一，决心提前两年零两个月完成五年计划，并且决心在最近把劳动生产率提高到四点七倍。
    这个工厂的旋工徐昌乙在集会上发言说，中国人民志愿军为了和平，已经全部从朝鲜撤出，但是美国却为了永久强占南朝鲜，进行各种军事挑衅活动，他们在南朝鲜建立导弹基地，造成新战争的威胁。他说，美军对南朝鲜的强占是朝鲜人民一切痛苦和灾难的根源，美军必须立即滚出南朝鲜。
    这些集会都一致通过决议，表示要为把美军赶出南朝鲜、争取和平统一祖国进行百折不挠的斗争。
