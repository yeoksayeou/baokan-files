Title: NPC Standing Committee and Five Other Units Hold Evening Party to Welcome Volunteer Army Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-10-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th. To welcome the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Delegation led by Commander Yang Yong and Political Commissar Wang Ping, and the Volunteer Army Art Troupe, the NPC Standing Committee, the National Committee of the CPPCC, the Chinese People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea, the Beijing Municipal People's Committee, the Municipal Committee of the CPPCC, and the Municipal Branch of the Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea held a Peking opera evening party at Huairentang tonight.

Vice Chairman Zhu De, Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, and Bo Yibo, Vice Chairmen of the NPC Standing Committee Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, and Cheng Qian, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea Liao Chengzhi, responsible persons of relevant units Zhang Xiruo, Wu Han, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and responsible persons of various people's organizations and democratic parties attended the evening party.

When Vice Chairman Zhu De and others accompanied Generals Yang Yong, Wang Ping, and others into the venue, the venue erupted in warm applause.

At the evening party, famous Peking opera actors from the capital performed wonderful programs such as "The Gathering of Heroes."

After the performance, the Volunteer Army Delegation, accompanied by Peng Zhen and Guo Moruo, met with the actors and presented flower baskets. (Attached picture)

### 人大常委会等六单位举行晚会欢迎志愿军代表团

1958-10-31
第3版()
专栏：

    人大常委会等六单位举行晚会欢迎志愿军代表团
    新华社30日讯　为了欢迎以杨勇司令员和王平政治委员为首的中国人民志愿军代表团和志愿军文艺工作团，人大常委会、政协全国委员会、中国人民抗美援朝总会、北京市人民委员会、市政协和市抗美援朝分会，今天晚上在怀仁堂举行京剧晚会。
    朱德副主席，陈云、陈毅、李富春、李先念、薄一波副总理，李济深、郭沫若、彭真、程潜副委员长，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，国防委员会副主席张治中，中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席廖承志，有关单位的负责人张奚若、吴晗、中国人民解放军高级将领，各人民团体和各民主党派的负责人等出席了晚会。
    当朱德副主席等陪同杨勇、王平将军等进入会场的时候，会场上顿时响起一片热烈的欢迎掌声。
    晚会上，首都著名京剧演员演出了“群英会”等精采节目。
    演出结束后，志愿军代表团由彭真、郭沫若陪同，同演员们会见，并赠送了花篮。（附图片）
