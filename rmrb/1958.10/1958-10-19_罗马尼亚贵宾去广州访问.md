Title: Romanian Guests Depart for Guangzhou for Visit
Author:
Date: 1958-10-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 15th - The Romanian military delegation headed by General Salajan, accompanied by General Peng Shaohui, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, left Shanghai by train on the evening of the 14th for Guangzhou after a day and a half of visits in Shanghai.

Those who went to the station to see them off included Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng, Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Xu Jianguo, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, as well as officers from the three services of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai.

### 罗马尼亚贵宾去广州访问

1958-10-19
第3版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚贵宾去广州访问
    据新华社上海15日电　以萨拉扬上将为首的罗马尼亚访华军事代表团在中国人民解放军副总参谋长彭绍辉上将陪同下，在上海进行了一天半的参观访问以后，14日晚上乘火车去广州。
    前往车站欢送的有中国人民解放军上海警备区司令员王必成中将，中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长许建国以及中国人民解放军驻沪陆海空三军部队的军官们。
