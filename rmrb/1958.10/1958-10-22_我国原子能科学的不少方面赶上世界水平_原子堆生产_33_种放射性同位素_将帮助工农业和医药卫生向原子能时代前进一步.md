Title: Many Aspects of China's Atomic Energy Science Catch Up with World Levels; Atomic Pile Produces 33 Kinds of Radioactive Isotopes; Will Help Industry, Agriculture, and Healthcare Advance Towards the Atomic Energy Era
Author:
Date: 1958-10-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 21st dispatch: China's first experimental atomic reactor has already produced thirty-three kinds of radioactive isotopes. In the near future, this experimental atomic pile, the most powerful in Asia, will produce all radioactive isotopes, about one hundred or more.

Among the thirty-three radioactive isotopes that can now be produced are the well-known Cobalt-60, Sodium-24, Phosphorus-32, Calcium-45, and so on. Radioactive Cobalt-60 can be used medically to treat cancer; in industry, it can be used to detect internal metal flaws; and in agriculture, it is being studied for irradiating agricultural products to delay sprouting and prevent spoilage, which is beneficial for storage. Radioactive Phosphorus-32 can be used as a tracer atom to study complex problems such as how plants absorb nutrients. Other radioactive isotopes have various uses in industrial and agricultural production and healthcare.

Currently, China's atomic energy science and technology have caught up with the world's advanced levels in many aspects.

Nuclear emulsion is used to record the traces of various radiations, like the function of photographic film. It is one of the important tools in atomic energy science. The electron-sensitive and neutron-detecting nuclear emulsions that China has now researched and manufactured have performance exceeding that of similar British products currently known in the world. Many kinds of counters for measuring the types, energies, and intensities of various radiations, as well as counter telescopes for recording cosmic ray intensities, have been researched and manufactured. These instruments have a wide range of uses in nuclear physics research and isotope applications. During the recent Great Leap Forward upsurge, many new counters with special purposes have been produced, and their performance mostly reaches world levels.

The completion of the hundred-channel pulse analyzer and the fifty-channel pulse analyzer demonstrates that China has mastered complex nuclear electronics technology.

On the eve of the ninth anniversary of National Day, an electron cyclotron accelerator was born in Beijing. It can accelerate electrons to an energy of 4.5 million electron volts. Other types of electron accelerators were also born at the same time. In addition to being used for nuclear physics research, electron accelerators can also be used for metal flaw detection and other places where radiation applications are needed, such as preventing agricultural product spoilage.

A series of metal fractionation oil diffusion pumps (vacuum pumps) recently manufactured can achieve a very high standard of vacuum; the largest can pump 40,000 liters of air per second, placing it among the world's largest vacuum pumping equipment. This type of equipment is indispensable in atomic energy science, electronics, metallurgy, and the chemical industry.

In addition, various spectrometers for measuring radiation intensity, "nanosecond pulse oscilloscopes" capable of distinguishing nuclear changes occurring within a billionth of a second, isotope separators, and other important atomic energy scientific instruments made in China are also advancing towards world levels.

### 我国原子能科学的不少方面赶上世界水平  原子堆生产33种放射性同位素  将帮助工农业和医药卫生向原子能时代前进一步

1958-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我国原子能科学的不少方面赶上世界水平
    原子堆生产33种放射性同位素
    将帮助工农业和医药卫生向原子能时代前进一步
    新华社21日讯　我国第一座实验性原子反应堆已经生产出三十三种放射性同位素。不久的将来，这座亚洲功率最大的实验性原子堆将要出产全部放射性同位素，约一百多种。
    现在已能生产的三十三种放射性同位素中，有人们熟知的钴60、钠24、磷32、钙45等等。放射性钴60，在医学上可以用来医治癌症；在工业上可以用于探测金属内部伤痕；在农业上正在研究用来照射农产品，使之延迟发芽和防止腐坏，以利贮存。放射性磷32，可以作为示踪原子，用来研究植物如何吸收营养等复杂问题。其他各种放射性同位素，在工农业生产和医药卫生等方面都有各种不同的用途。
    现在我国的原子能科学技术在不少方面已经赶上世界先进水平。
    原子核乳胶是用来记录各种射线的踪迹的，像照相底片的作用一样。它是原子能科学中的重要工具之一。我国现在研究制成的对电子感应灵敏的和用来探测中子的原子核乳胶，它们的性能已经超出目前世界知名的英国同类产品。用来测量各种射线的种类、能量和强度的许多种计数管和记录宇宙线强度的计数管望远镜都已研究制造出来。这些仪器在原子核物理研究上和同位素的应用上有很广泛的用途。在最近的大跃进浪潮中，又制成了多种新的、有特殊用途的计数管，性能大多达到世界水平。
    百道脉冲分析器和五十道脉冲分析器的制成，说明我国已掌握了复杂的原子核电子学技术。
    在国庆九周年前夕，一架电子回旋加速器在北京诞生。它可以使电子加速到四百五十万电子伏的能量。同时诞生的还有其他类型的电子加速器。电子加速器除了用以研究原子核物理以外，还可用于金属探伤，以及其他需要应用辐射的地方，例如防止农产品腐烂等。
    最近制成的一系列金属分馏油扩散泵（真空泵），真空度能达到很高标准；最大的每秒钟可以抽四万升空气，它属于目前世界上最大的抽真空设备之列。这种设备在原子能科学、电子学、冶金和化学工业等方面都是不可缺少的。
    此外，我国自制的用来测量射线强度的各种谱仪、能分辨在十亿分之一秒时间内出现的原子核变化情况的“毫微秒脉冲示波器”、同位素分离器等重要的原子能科学仪器，也都正在向世界水平迈进。
