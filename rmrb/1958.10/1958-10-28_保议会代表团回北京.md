Title: Bulgarian Parliamentary Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-10-28
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, October 27th. The Bulgarian National Assembly Delegation headed by Chervenkov returned to Beijing by plane today after concluding its visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. Kang Ryang-uk, Secretary-General of the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, accompanied the delegation on the same flight to Beijing.
    Welcoming them at the airport were Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Liu Changsheng, member, and Lian Guan, Deputy Secretary-General. The Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Panchevski, as well as personnel from the Korean and Vietnamese Embassies in China, were also at the airport to welcome them. In the afternoon, the delegation visited the Temple of Heaven and Beihai Park in Beijing.

### 保议会代表团回北京

1958-10-28
第2版()
专栏：

    保议会代表团回北京
    新华社27日讯　以契尔文科夫为首的保加利亚国民议会代表团结束对朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的访问以后，已在今天乘飞机回到北京。朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会书记长康良煜陪同代表团同机到北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深，委员刘长胜，副秘书长连贯。保加利亚驻华大使潘切夫斯基以及朝鲜和越南驻中国大使馆人员也到机场欢迎。下午，代表团在北京游览了天坛和北海。
