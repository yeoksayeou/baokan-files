Title: Italian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-10-19
Page: 3

Column: Welcoming Friends from Afar

    Italian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency dispatch) The Italian cultural delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane on the morning of October 10. The delegation includes five members, including Pacini, Dean of the Oriental Institute of the University of Naples, and economist Latini.

### 意文化代表团到北京

1958-10-19
第3版()
专栏：欢迎远道而来的朋友

    意文化代表团到北京
    据新华社讯　应中国人民对外文化协会的邀请来我国访问的意大利文化代表团，10日上午乘飞机到北京。代表团成员包括那不勒斯大学东方学院院长帕契尼和经济学家拉提尼等五人。
