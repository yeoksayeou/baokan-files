Title: To the People's Republic of China
Author: Suǒlìyà Shābīn
Date: 1958-10-05
Page: 4

Editor's Note: This is a poem sent from Belgium by a Peruvian listener of the Central People's Broadcasting Station. She warmly congratulates the Chinese people, and we also deeply bless this distant friend here.

	With deep love and excitement in my heart,
	Ah, noble China, I salute you!
	I express my inner feelings to you in my simple language.
	In your glorious and joyful days,
	For your happy and fruitful land,
	For the land irrigated by the blood of your children,
	The whole world sings hymns of joy.
	How many victories have you won in successfully destroying slavery,
	You rejoicing and inspiring people, don't forget the past of suffering.
	Glory belongs to your immortal heroes!
	In this happy and unforgettable day,
	Glory belongs to you, great republic and your children with lofty ideals.
	Ah, China of thousands of years, powerful China!
	How can the imperialists stop your progress?
	You answer them with peaceful labor and firm struggle.
	Red clouds appear in your clean sky
	The brilliant sun shines on your future,
	You are now and will always be one China,
	No one can separate you!

### 致中华人民共和国
索丽亚·莎尔宾
1958-10-05
第4版()
专栏：

    致中华人民共和国
    秘鲁　索丽亚·莎尔宾
    编者按：这是中央人民广播电台的一位秘鲁听众从比利时寄来的诗。她向中国人民热情地祝贺，让我们在这里也为这位远方的朋友深深地祝福。怀着深深的挚爱和激动的心，啊，崇高的中国，我向你致敬！用我纯朴的语言向你表达我内心的感情。在你光荣而欢欣的日子里，为你幸福而丰硕的土地，为你儿女的鲜血灌溉过的土地，全世界都唱出欢乐的颂歌。你获得过多少次的胜利成功地摧毁了奴役，你欢腾鼓舞的人们呀，别忘记苦难的往昔。光荣属于你的不死的英雄们！在这幸福的难忘的日子里，光荣属于你，伟大的共和国和你的有着崇高理想的儿女们。啊，千年的中国，强大的中国！帝国主义者岂能阻止你的进程？你用和平的劳动用坚定的斗争回答它们。红霞出现在你净洁的天穹绚丽的太阳，照耀着你的未来，你现在是、将来也将永远是一个中国，谁也不能把你分开！
