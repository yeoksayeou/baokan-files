Title: Opening of Vietnam-Korea-Mongolia-China Volleyball Friendship Tournament
Author:
Date: 1958-10-26
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, October 25th. The 1958 Vietnam, Korea, Mongolia, and China Four-Nation Volleyball Friendship Tournament opened tonight at the Beijing Gymnasium.

　　Tonight, the national flags of Vietnam, Korea, Mongolia, and China were hung high in the Beijing Gymnasium. At 7:15 PM, the opening ceremony began. Athletes from various countries, wearing sportswear of different colors, entered the stadium.

　　Huang Zhong, Chairman of the Tournament Organizing Committee and Deputy Director of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, spoke at the opening ceremony. He first extended a warm welcome to the athletes from brotherly countries for coming to China to participate in the tournament. He then said that in recent years, with the vigorous development of sports in Vietnam, Korea, Mongolia, and China, sports exchanges between our four countries have further developed. This connection has played a significant role in strengthening and consolidating the traditional friendship between our four countries.

　　Rong Gaotang, Deputy Director of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, also attended the opening ceremony.

　　Li Yong-ho, the Korean Ambassador to China, Sonam Lubsan, the Mongolian Ambassador to China, and Pham Binh, the Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, also attended the opening ceremony.

　　After the opening ceremony, two matches were held. The first was a women's team match between Mongolia and Korea. The Korean team won three to zero with hook serves and powerful spikes. The second was a men's team match between Vietnam and Korea. The Korean team also won three to zero.

　　This tournament will be conducted using a round-robin system. The competition will continue tomorrow.

### 越朝蒙中排球友谊赛开幕

1958-10-26
第2版()
专栏：

    越朝蒙中排球友谊赛开幕
    新华社25日讯　1958年越、朝、蒙、中四国排球友谊比赛，今天晚上在北京体育馆开幕。
    今天晚上，北京体育馆内高高地挂起了越、朝、蒙、中四国的国旗。七点十五分，开幕式开始。各国运动员穿着不同颜色的运动服走进了体育场。
    比赛大会组织委员会主席、中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任黄中，在开幕式上讲了话。他首先对兄弟国家运动员到我国来参加比赛，表示热烈欢迎。接着他说，近几年来随着越、朝、蒙、中四国体育运动的蓬勃发展，我们四国之间的体育联系有了进一步的发展。这种联系对加强和巩固我们四国之间的传统友谊，起了很大作用。
    参加开幕式的还有中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任荣高棠。
    朝鲜驻我国大使李永镐，蒙古驻我国大使索诺曼·鲁布桑和越南驻我国大使馆临时代办范平，也参加了开幕式。
    开幕式结束后进行了两场比赛，第一场是女子队蒙古对朝鲜，朝鲜队在钩手发球和有力的杀球下，以三比零得胜。第二场男子队越南对朝鲜，朝鲜队也三比零获胜。
    这次比赛是采用循环赛的办法，比赛明日将继续进行。
