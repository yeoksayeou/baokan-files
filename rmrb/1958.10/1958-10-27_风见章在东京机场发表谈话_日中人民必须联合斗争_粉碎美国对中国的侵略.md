Title: Feng Zizhang Delivers Speech at Tokyo Airport: Japanese and Chinese People Must Unite to Struggle and Crush American Aggression Against China
Author:
Date: 1958-10-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 25th: The Japanese National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations delegation to China and the Japan-China Friendship Association delegation to China, led by Feng Zizhang and Hirano Yoshitaro, returned to Tokyo by plane this afternoon. Nakajima Kenzo, Nakazaki Toshi, and representatives from peace and friendship organizations, as well as the Communist and Socialist Parties, greeted them at the airport.

　　In a speech delivered at the welcome meeting held at the airport, Feng Zizhang praised China's great leap forward in all aspects. He pointed out that the tense situation in the Taiwan Strait region is not only China's problem but also has inseparable close ties with Japan. The Japanese people must unite with the Chinese people to struggle to crush American imperialism's aggression against China and safeguard Asian peace. He also pointed out that the peace movement must be based on eliminating the roots of war.

### 风见章在东京机场发表谈话  日中人民必须联合斗争  粉碎美国对中国的侵略

1958-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

    风见章在东京机场发表谈话
    日中人民必须联合斗争  粉碎美国对中国的侵略
    据新华社25日讯　东京消息：日本恢复日中邦交国民会议访华代表团和日中友好协会访华代表团今天下午在风见章和平野义太郎率领下乘飞机回到东京。中岛健藏和中崎敏等和平、友好团体和共产党、社会党的代表到机场迎接。
    风见章在机场内所举行的欢迎会上致词时称赞了中国在各方面的大跃进。他指出，台湾海峡地区的紧张局势不仅是中国的问题，而且同日本有分不开的密切关系。日本人民必须同中国人民联合进行斗争，以粉碎美帝国主义对中国的侵略，并维护亚洲和平。他还指出，和平运动必须以消除战争的根源为基础。
