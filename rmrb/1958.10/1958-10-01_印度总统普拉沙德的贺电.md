Title: President Prasad of India's Congratulatory Message
Author:
Date: 1958-10-01
Page: 3

    To His Excellency Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China:
    "I am very pleased to represent the government and people of India and, in my personal capacity, to extend to Your Excellency and, through Your Excellency, to the government and people of China, the warmest congratulations and best wishes, wishing Your Excellency happiness and the Chinese people prosperity and progress.
                                        Rajendra Prasad

### 印度总统普拉沙德的贺电
拉金德拉·普拉沙德
1958-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    印度总统普拉沙德的贺电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东先生阁下：
    “我很愉快地代表印度政府和人民并以我个人的名义向阁下并通过阁下向中国政府和人民致以最热烈的祝贺和最良好的愿望，祝阁下幸福，祝中国人民昌盛和进步。
                                        拉金德拉·普拉沙德
