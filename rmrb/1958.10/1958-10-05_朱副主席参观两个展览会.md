Title: Vice Chairman Zhu Visits Two Exhibitions
Author:
Date: 1958-10-05
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency report: Vice Chairman Zhu De visited the National Industrial and Transportation Exhibition on the afternoon of September 29.
    Vice Chairman Zhu De spent more than two hours in the exhibition hall, carefully viewing various exhibits in the metallurgy, petroleum, and geology pavilions, and wrote the inscription "Leap Forward Again and Again" in the guestbook.
    Xinhua News Agency report: Comrade Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, visited the New Achievements in Science and Technology Exhibition on the afternoon of the 2nd. Since the opening of the exhibition, leading comrades such as Dong Biwu, Luo Ronghuan, Liu Bocheng, Li Xiannian, Zhang Wentian, Wu Yuzhang, Xu Teli, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Ye Jianying, and Xie Juezai have also visited the exhibition. They gave very good evaluations of the exhibits.
    This exhibition is sponsored by the China Association for Science and Technology and the Central Museum of Natural History. Since its opening, nearly 50,000 visitors have visited. The exhibition is scheduled to close in mid-November. (Attached picture)
    Vice Chairman Zhu viewing the model of the Dongliang Canal in Wu Mountain, Gansu Province, a banner for diverting water to the mountains at the New Achievements in Science and Technology Exhibition. Photo by Zheng Zhensun, reporter of Xinhua News Agency.

### 朱副主席参观两个展览会

1958-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

    朱副主席参观两个展览会
    据新华社讯　朱德副主席9月29日下午参观了全国工业交通展览会。
    朱德副主席在展览馆里停留了两个多小时，仔细参观了冶金、石油、地质等馆的各种展品，并在留言簿上写下了“跃进再跃进”的题字。
    据新华社讯　中共中央副主席朱德同志2日下午，参观了科学技术新成就展览会，展览会开幕以来，先后参观这个展览会的还有董必武、罗荣桓、刘伯承、李先念、张闻天、吴玉章、徐特立、徐向前、聂荣臻、叶剑英、谢觉哉等领导同志。他们对展出的展品给予很好的评价。
    这个展览会是由中国科协和中央自然博物馆举办的，展出以来，已有近五万观众参观过。这个展览会预计11月中旬闭幕。（附图片）
