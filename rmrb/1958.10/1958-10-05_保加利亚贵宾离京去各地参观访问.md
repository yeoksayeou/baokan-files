Title: Bulgarian Guests Leave Beijing to Visit Various Places
Author:
Date: 1958-10-05
Page: 2
    Xinhua News Agency, 4th October. The Bulgarian National Assembly Delegation, headed by Comrade Chervenkov, left Beijing by plane this morning to visit Zhengzhou, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Hangzhou, Shanghai and other places. During their visits to various places and before returning to Beijing, the delegation will also pay short visits to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.
    Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and members Zhang Su and Ji Fang, as well as the Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yonghao, went to the airport to see them off. Liu Changsheng, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Lian Guan, Deputy Secretary-General, and others accompanied the distinguished guests on their visits.
    The Sofia City People's Assembly Delegation, headed by Comrade Kostov, also left by plane this morning to visit Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Wuhan and other places, accompanied by Feng Jiping, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and others.

### 保加利亚贵宾离京去各地参观访问

1958-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

    保加利亚贵宾离京去各地参观访问
    新华社4日讯　以契尔文科夫同志为首的保加利亚国民议会代表团今天早晨乘飞机离开北京，前往郑州、武汉、广州、杭州、上海等地参观访问。代表团在去各地参观期间和回到北京以前，还将到越南民主共和国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国作短期访问。
    前往飞机场送行的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深，委员张苏、季方和朝鲜驻华大使李永镐。陪同贵宾到各地参观访问的有人大常委委员刘长胜和副秘书长连贯等人。
    以科斯托夫同志为首的索非亚市人民会议代表团，也由北京市副市长冯基平等陪同，在今天早晨乘飞机前往上海、杭州、广州、武汉等地参观访问。
