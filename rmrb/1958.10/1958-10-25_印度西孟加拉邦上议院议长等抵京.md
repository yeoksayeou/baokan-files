Title: Speaker of the West Bengal Upper House of India Arrives in Beijing

Author:
Date: 1958-10-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd. At the invitation of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, Suniti Kumar Chatterji, Indian linguist and Speaker of the West Bengal Upper House, and Gubar Hoddar, Member of the West Bengal Upper House and writer, arrived in Beijing on the 20th for a visit to our country.

### 印度西孟加拉邦上议院议长等抵京

1958-10-25
第5版()
专栏：

    印度西孟加拉邦上议院议长等抵京
    新华社22日讯　应中印友好协会邀请来我国访问的印度语言学家、西孟加拉邦上议院议长苏尼蒂·库马·查特吉和西孟加拉邦上议院议员、作家古巴尔·霍达尔于20日抵京。
