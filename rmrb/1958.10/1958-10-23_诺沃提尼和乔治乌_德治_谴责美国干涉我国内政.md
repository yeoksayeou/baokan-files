Title: Novotný and Gheorghiu-Dej Condemn US Interference in Our Internal Affairs
Author:
Date: 1958-10-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 21st: Czechoslovak President and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, Novotný, held a grand reception in Prague this evening to welcome the Romanian Party and Government delegation headed by Gheorghiu-Dej.

　　Novotný and Gheorghiu-Dej spoke at the reception. They unanimously condemned the provocations carried out by US imperialism against China in the Taiwan Strait area.

　　In his speech, Novotný condemned the US occupation of Taiwan and its interference in China's internal affairs. He also severely criticized the slander of the principles of proletarian internationalism adhered to by socialist countries by the Yugoslav revisionists. He emphasized that neither imperialism nor revisionists can hinder the progress of the socialist cause.

　　In his speech, Gheorghiu-Dej expressed his gratitude to Czechoslovakia for the fraternal assistance it had given to Romania in its economic construction. Referring to the provocative actions of US imperialism against the Chinese people, he said that the attempt by US imperialists to obstruct the Chinese people from exercising their rights is bound to fail, because peace-loving people all over the world stand with the Chinese people. He said that US imperialists must withdraw from Taiwan.

　　Gheorghiu-Dej emphasized that the unity of the socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union is a decisive and fundamental factor in resisting imperialist and revisionist attacks.

　　Czechoslovak Party and government leaders Široký, Ďuriš, Kopecký, Barák, Fierlinger and Hendrych attended the reception. Also present at the reception were diplomatic envoys from various countries to Czechoslovakia.

### 诺沃提尼和乔治乌—德治  谴责美国干涉我国内政

1958-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    诺沃提尼和乔治乌—德治
    谴责美国干涉我国内政
    新华社布拉格21日电　捷克斯洛伐克总统、捷共中央第一书记诺沃提尼今天傍晚在布拉格举行盛大招待会，欢迎以乔治乌—德治为首的罗马尼亚党和政府代表团。
    诺沃提尼和乔治乌—德治在招待会上先后讲了话。他们一致谴责美帝国主义在台湾海峡地区对中国进行的挑衅。
    诺沃提尼讲话时谴责美国占领台湾并且干涉中国内政的行为。他还严厉地批评了南斯拉夫修正主义者对社会主义国家奉行的无产阶级国际主义原则进行的诬蔑。他强调指出，不论帝国主义或者是修正主义者都不能够阻挠社会主义事业向前发展。
    乔治乌—德治在讲话中对捷克斯洛伐克在罗马尼亚的经济建设中给罗马尼亚的兄弟般的援助表示感谢。他在谈到美帝国主义对中国人民的挑衅行为时说，美帝国主义者企图阻挠中国人民行使他们的权利，这种企图必定要遭到失败，因为全世界爱好和平的人民都是同中国人民站在一起的。他说，美帝国主义者必须撤出台湾。
    乔治乌—德治强调指出，以苏联为首的社会主义国家的团结，是抵抗帝国主义和修正主义进攻的决定性的和根本性的因素。
    捷克斯洛伐克党和政府的领导人西罗基、杜兰斯基、柯别茨基、巴拉格、费林格和亨德里赫参加了招待会。出席招待会的还有各国驻捷克斯洛伐克的外交使节。
