Title: Mongolian Military Delegation to China Returns Home; General Huang Kecheng Wishes Continuous Development of Sino-Mongolian Friendship
Author:
Date: 1958-10-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 20th. The military delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, headed by Lieutenant General Dorj, left Beijing by special plane at 9:00 AM today to return home after completing its visit to our country.

At 8:30 AM, a farewell crowd lined up at the Capital Airport. Lieutenant General Dorj, accompanied by General Huang Kecheng, reviewed the honor guard.

In his farewell speech, Lieutenant General Dorj said that the Mongolian military delegation had spent more than twenty pleasant days in your beautiful country and is now about to leave you to return home. During our visit, we received the most cordial and fraternal hospitality. On behalf of all members of the delegation, I would like to express our heartfelt gratitude to you, and through you to the brotherly Chinese people and all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Lieutenant General Dorj also praised the historic achievements made by the Chinese people under the glorious leadership of the Chinese Communist Party.

In his farewell speech, General Huang Kecheng said that China and Mongolia are among the closest neighbors. In the past arduous struggle against common enemies, and in today's great cause of building socialism, the people of our two countries have forged the deepest and most intimate brotherly unity and friendship. The successful visit of this delegation to our country is a major event in the development of friendly relations between the peoples and armies of our two countries. It will make an important contribution to the mutual understanding and exchange of military construction experience and socialist construction experience between the peoples and armies of China and Mongolia, to the development and consolidation of the unity and friendship of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and to the cause of maintaining world peace.

On behalf of the Chinese people and all officers and men of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Huang Kecheng expressed his heartfelt gratitude to Lieutenant General Dorj and all members of the delegation, and wished them a safe journey.

Also present at the airport today to see them off were General Wang Shusheng, Senior General Zhang Zongxun, Senior General Li Juikui, Senior General Guo Tianmin, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Naval Lieutenant General Luo Shunchu, Air Force Lieutenant General Chang Qiankun, and Vice Ministers of Public Security Yang Qiqing and Wang Jinxiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and others.

Mongolian Ambassador to China Lubsan and his wife, as well as military attaches from various countries in China, also came to the airport to see them off.

### 蒙古访华军事代表团回国  黄克诚大将祝中蒙友谊不断发展

1958-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古访华军事代表团回国
    黄克诚大将祝中蒙友谊不断发展
    新华社20日讯　以道尔吉中将为首的蒙古人民共和国军事代表团在我国访问结束后，今天上午九时乘专机离北京回国。
    上午八时三十分，首都机场上排列着欢送的队伍。道尔吉中将在黄克诚大将陪同下检阅了仪仗队。
    道尔吉中将致告别词说，我们蒙古军事代表团在你们美丽的国家愉快地度过了二十多天，现在就要离开你们回国去了。在我们进行访问期间，受到最殷勤的兄弟般的款待，我谨代表全体人员向你们，并通过你们向兄弟的中国人民和中国人民解放军全体指战员表示衷心的谢意。
    道尔吉中将还赞扬中国人民在光荣的中国共产党领导下所取得的历史性的成就。
    黄克诚大将在致欢送词中说，中蒙两国是最亲近的邻邦之一。在过去反对共同敌人的艰苦斗争中，在今天建设社会主义的伟大事业中，我们两国人民已经结成了最深厚、最亲密的兄弟般的团结和友谊。这次代表团在我国的成功的访问，是我们两国人民和军队之间友好关系发展上的一件大事。它对中蒙两国人民和军队之间的相互了解，相互交流两国的建军经验和社会主义建设经验，发展和巩固以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结和友谊，以及维护世界和平事业，都将有重要的贡献。
    黄克诚大将代表中国人民和中国人民解放军全体官兵，向道尔吉中将和代表团全体人员表示衷心的谢意，并祝一路平安。
    今天到机场送行的还有王树声大将，张宗逊上将，李聚奎上将，郭天民上将，萧向荣中将，罗舜初海军中将，常乾坤空军中将和公安部副部长杨奇清、汪金祥，外交部副部长姬鹏飞等。
    蒙古驻华大使鲁布桑及夫人和各国驻华武官，也到机场送行。
