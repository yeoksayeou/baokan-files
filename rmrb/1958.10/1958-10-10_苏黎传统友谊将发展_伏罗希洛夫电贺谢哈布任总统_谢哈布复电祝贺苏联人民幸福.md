Title: Soviet-Lebanese Traditional Friendship to Develop: Voroshilov Congratulates President Chehab; Chehab Replies, Wishing the Soviet People Happiness

Author:
Date: 1958-10-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 9th dispatch. TASS, Moscow, 8th dispatch: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Chairman Voroshilov, sent a telegram to Chehab on September 30th, congratulating him on assuming the presidency of the Lebanese Republic.

The telegram stated that the Soviet people fully sympathize with the heroic struggle of the Lebanese people to free themselves from foreign occupation and to consolidate national independence and state sovereignty.

The telegram also stated that the consistent characteristic of relations between the Soviet and Lebanese peoples is a deep mutual sympathy and a traditional friendship that has been tested over many years.

Voroshilov expressed hope in the telegram that the friendly relations between the Soviet and Lebanese peoples will continue to develop smoothly in the future, to the benefit of both peoples and the peace of the Middle East and the world.

In his reply telegram on the 7th, Chehab expressed that the friendly relations that have always existed between the Soviet and Lebanese peoples will develop even more smoothly in the future.

In his reply telegram, Chehab also warmly wished the Soviet people happiness and prosperity.

### 苏黎传统友谊将发展  伏罗希洛夫电贺谢哈布任总统  谢哈布复电祝贺苏联人民幸福

1958-10-10
第5版()
专栏：

    苏黎传统友谊将发展
    伏罗希洛夫电贺谢哈布任总统  谢哈布复电祝贺苏联人民幸福
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科8日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫9月30日打电报给谢哈布，祝贺他就任黎巴嫩共和国总统。
    电文说，苏联人民完全同情黎巴嫩人民争取摆脱外国占领、争取巩固民族独立和国家主权的英勇斗争。
    电文还说，苏黎两国人民关系的一贯特点是充满相互的同情和拥有经过多年考验的传统友谊。
    伏罗希洛夫在电报中表示希望，苏黎两国人民的友好关系今后仍将顺利地发展，以利于两国人民和中近东与全世界的和平。
    谢哈布在7日的复电中表示，苏黎两国人民一直存在的友好关系今后将更加顺利地发展。
    谢哈布在复电中还热烈祝贺苏联人民幸福和繁荣。
