Title: Telegram of Greetings from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Argentina
Author:
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

    Telegram of Greetings from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Argentina
    To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chairman Mao Zedong:
    On the occasion of the ninth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, we extend our warm fraternal congratulations. Together with the freedom- and independence-loving people of Argentina, we watch with deep emotion the historic achievements you have made in the cause of building socialism and in the heroic struggle to liberate your territory still occupied by American interventionists and their running dogs, the Chiang Kai-shek clique. Your struggle is an integral part of the struggle of the people of the world for peace, and we pay tribute to it.
    Long live the glorious Communist Party of China!
    Long live the People's Republic of China!
    Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Argentine people!
                                    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of Argentina

### 阿根廷共产党中央委员会的贺电

1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    阿根廷共产党中央委员会的贺电中国共产党中央委员会毛泽东主席：
    当中华人民共和国成立九周年的时候，我们谨致以热烈的兄弟的祝贺，并同爱好自由和独立的阿根廷人民一道，怀着深挚的感情，注视着你们在建设社会主义的事业中和在争取解放你们的仍为美国干涉者和它们的走狗蒋介石集团所占据的领土的英勇斗争中，所获得的历史性的成就。你们的斗争是全世界人民争取和平斗争的不可分割的一部分，我们谨对此致敬。
    光荣的中国共产党万岁！
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    中国人民和阿根廷人民的友谊万岁！
                                    阿根廷共产党中央委员会
