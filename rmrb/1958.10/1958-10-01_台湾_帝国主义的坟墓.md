Title: Taiwan—Imperialism's Graveyard
Author: Kaier
Date: 1958-10-01
Page: 6

Taiwan—Imperialism's Graveyard
Sultan World Peace Council Secretary-General Kaier

    O East! Our future dreams,
    O Dawn! The hope we await,
    Salute your workers.
    Wish them longevity and constant victory,
    Welcome your wind blowing to our land,
    It shakes the universe and is thought-provoking.
    The morning light illuminates the dark road,
    Unveiling the veil, exposing the shady things.
    The birds sing together,
    Singing victory, singing victory.
    Our pastoral fields
    Are filled with songs and planted with trees everywhere,
    The East has no drought,
    Our clouds are full of raindrops.
    Our hurricane rises happily,
    Destroying tyranny and eradicating evil.
    I am amazed:
    How to concentrate centuries into one hour?
    How to turn the wilderness into a beautiful garden?
    How to melt steel with great hands?
    How to turn decay into magic?
    How to unite hundreds of millions of people into one person?
    Their voices make the mountains tremble,
    They bravely and tenaciously face the stubborn enemy.
    I am amazed:
    How time is controlled by them,
    Their footsteps move forward without stopping.
    All of this amazes me.
    But,
    The madmen on the broken ship also amaze me,
    The sails have been torn by the wind,
    The flames have become black smoke,
    They, shouting in fear,
    Like falling from a high mountain.
    The Chinese people are a hardworking people,
    They have extraordinary leaders.
    Madmen, against the wind of peace,
    Against the giant wheel of the times;
    They don't know that death is coming,
    If they act rashly, China will be their burial place.
    Here, everyone and every hand,
    Is building a red China.
    They don't know that if they dare to act rashly,
    The people here will erase them from history.
    Here, the red flag of freedom,
    Flutters on the high mountain peaks,
    Fluttering in:
    Factories, farms, schools, and above people's heads.
    They are fully armed to fight back,
    Victory will surely belong to them in the struggle!
    They are baptized for life,
    They are building a beautiful tomorrow.
    China! Your wind
    Is filled with all victory and happiness,
    You have brought light to the world.
    The world is being threatened by the culprits of disaster,
    I am happy to follow you forward,
    Our hearts are filled with joy and delight.
    The wind is welcoming you,
    The eagles are welcoming you,
    You are running towards lofty goals!
    We in the East will defend you with blood,
    Revolutionary Africa!
    The enemies have already collapsed there,
    Death is waiting for them,
    We spit on them with disdain.
    Brothers, come on!
    Let us hold hands tightly,
    Let us toast for unity!
    We have a strong front,
    The enemy's front has collapsed.
    Taiwan, is your territory! Your territory!
    Just as Algeria is the land of the Arabs.
    You call in Beijing,
    Our echo will be heard in Aleppo.
    Your voice shakes our East,
    Africa angrily awakens,
    The interests of American murderers
    Will be burned to ashes in the firewood.
    We sing the same song,
    The resounding echoes will be heard everywhere.
    We—Africa, Asia, Arabia,
    Victory will always belong to us!

### 台湾——帝国主义的坟墓　　
凯尔
1958-10-01
第6版()
专栏：

    台湾——帝国主义的坟墓
    苏丹世界和平委员会总书记　　凯尔
    东方啊！我们未来的美梦，
    黎明啊！我们等待的希望，
    向你的工人们致敬。
    祝他们万寿无疆，不断胜利，
    欢迎你的风吹到我们的土地，
    它震撼了宇宙，发人深思。
    晨光照亮了黑暗的道路，
    揭开了面纱，暴露出阴暗的东西。
    鸟儿们齐鸣歌唱，
    歌唱胜利，歌唱胜利。
    我们的田园里
    到处充满了歌声，栽满了树木，
    东方没有任何干旱，
    我们的云彩充满了雨滴。
    我们的飓风欣然而起，
    把暴虐摧毁，使罪恶绝迹。
    我很惊奇：
    怎样使几个世纪集中成为一个小时？
    怎样使荒野变成美丽的园地？
    怎样用伟大的双手使钢铁溶化？
    怎样使腐朽化为神奇？
    怎样使亿万人民团结成为一个人？
    他们的呼声使高山发抖，
    他们英勇顽强地面对着顽敌。
    我很惊奇：
    时间如何被他们支配，
    他们的脚步前进不息。
    所有这些都使我赞叹不已。
    但，
    破船上的疯人们也同样使我惊奇，
    船帆已被大风撕破，
    火焰已经成为黑烟，
    他们，害怕得大喊大叫，
    就像是从高山上跌落。
    中国人民是勤劳的人民，
    他们有着非凡的领袖。
    疯人们，反对和平之风，
    反对时代的巨轮；
    他们不知道，死亡即将来临，
    如要妄动，中国将是他们的葬身之所。
    这里，每一个人和每一只手，
    正在建设着红色的中国。
    他们不知道，如果胆敢妄动，
    这里的人民将把他们从历史上涂抹。
    这里，红色的自由的旗帜，
    飘扬在高高的山巅，
    飘扬在：
    工厂、农场、学校和人们的头顶。
    他们，为了反击而全民武装，
    在斗争中胜利必将属于他们！
    他们，为了生活而浸受洗礼，
    他们建设着美丽的明天。
    中国！你的风
    浸透了一切的胜利和幸福，
    你给世界带来了光明。
    世界正被灾难的祸首威胁着，
    我高兴的跟着你前进，
    我们的内心充满了喜悦和欢欣。
    风，在欢迎着你们，
    雄鹰，在欢迎着你们，
    你们奔向崇高的目的吧！
    我们东方将用鲜血来保卫你们，
    革命的非洲！
    敌人已经在那里溃败，
    死亡正在等待着他们，
    我们蔑视地把他们唾弃。
    兄弟们，来吧！
    让我们紧握双手，
    为了团结而干杯！
    我们有着坚强的阵线，
    敌人的阵线则已经崩溃。
    台湾，是你们的领土！你们的领土！
    正像阿尔及利亚是阿拉伯人的土地一样。
    你们在北京呼唤，
    将在阿勒颇听到我们的回声。
    你们的声音震撼了我们的东方，
    非洲愤怒的醒来，
    美国杀人犯的利益
    将在柴薪中烧成灰烬。
    我们唱着同一只歌子，
    将到处听到响彻云霄的回音。
    我们——非洲、亚洲、阿拉伯，
    胜利将永远属于我们！
