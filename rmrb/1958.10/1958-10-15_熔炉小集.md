Title: Smelting Furnace Miscellany
Author: Ba Mu
Date: 1958-10-15
Page: 8

    Smelting Furnace Miscellany
    Let Steel Flowers Bloom Red All Over the Sky
    Not letting molten iron flow without leaving the furnace,
    Not going down the mountain without seeing iron ore,
    A million soldiers fight together,
    To let steel flowers bloom red all over the sky.
    Secretary Takes Command
    The secretary takes command,
    Molten steel blooms day and night,
    Heroes compete by the furnace,
    Good news spreads throughout the land.
              Ba Mu
