Title: Taylor Also Clamors for Expanding Aggression, Publicly States Not to Neglect Jinmen and Other Strongholds
Author:
Date: 1958-10-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Taipei, 23rd: U.S. Army Chief of Staff Taylor today again issued a clamor for expanding aggression. In Taipei, he told reporters that the "basic military strongholds" such as Jinmen Island "should never be neglected."

Taylor revealed that during the suspension of the shelling of Jinmen, the United States sent Brigadier General Ammerman, artillery commander of the U.S. First Cavalry Division, sneaking from Korea to Jinmen Island to "study the artillery situation," and the Jinmen Chiang army's artillery "will be improved according to his recommendations."

### 泰勒也叫嚣扩大侵略  公然说不忽视金门等据点

1958-10-24
第5版()
专栏：

    泰勒也叫嚣扩大侵略
    公然说不忽视金门等据点
    据新华社23日讯　台北消息：美国陆军参谋长泰勒今天又发出扩大侵略的叫嚣。他在台北对新闻记者们说，“决不应该忽视”金门岛等“基本军事据点”。
    泰勒透露，在金门被暂停炮击期间，美国曾经派美军第一骑兵师炮兵司令艾默曼准将从朝鲜溜到金门岛“研究炮兵的情况”，金门蒋军炮兵“将根据他的建议加以改善”。
