Title: Party and State Leaders Receive Delegation from Yan'an Prefecture
Author:
Date: 1958-10-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, October 29th. Chairman Mao, Zhu De, Zhou Enlai, Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, Bo Yibo and other Party and state leaders received all members of the Yan'an Prefecture delegation who came to Beijing to visit the National Industrial and Transportation Exhibition this afternoon.

### 党和国家领导人接见延安专区代表团

1958-10-30
第2版()
专栏：

    党和国家领导人接见延安专区代表团
    新华社29日讯　毛主席、朱德、周恩来、郭沫若、彭真、陈毅、李富春、薄一波等党和国家的领导人，今天下午接见了前来北京参观全国工业交通展览会的延安专区代表团的全体人员。
