Title: Singing Loudly to Celebrate Victory
Author: Sha Ou
Date: 1958-10-29
Page: 8

    One, in the 20th-century sky, I use colorful clouds to write a poem of praise; on the road from Beijing to the Yalu River, I use forsythia to inlay a poem of praise. Sing loudly to celebrate victory, welcome our heroic brothers!
    Two, the beautiful land is full of spring, with tens of thousands of blast furnaces. Our motherland holds high the fiery banner, welcoming you!
    Three, the golden chariot of time cannot fully record your great achievements! The whole world sees the American paper tiger, bruised and battered, revealing its true colors.
    Four, leaving deep affection on the land of Korea. The friendship between China and Korea is evergreen. Evergreen, an epic of heroes.
    Five, after eight years of separation, meeting again brings even more joy. See the wonders everywhere in our motherland; see how the noose in our hands tightens around America's neck.

### 高歌庆凯旋
沙鸥
1958-10-29
第8版()
专栏：

    高歌庆凯旋
    沙鸥
    一在二十世纪的天空，我用彩云写成颂诗；在北京到鸭绿江边的路
  上，我用迎春花镶成颂诗。高歌庆凯旋，欢迎英雄兄弟！
    二锦绣江山春光好，高炉万万座，祖国高举火焰的旌旗，迎接你！
    三时间的金车，载不完丰功伟绩！全世界都看见美国纸老虎，头青脸肿漏了底。
    四留下深情，在朝鲜的土地。中朝友谊万年青。万年青，英雄的史诗。
    五离别八年，相见更欢。看祖国遍地是神奇；看我们手中的绞索，扣紧了美国的脖子。
