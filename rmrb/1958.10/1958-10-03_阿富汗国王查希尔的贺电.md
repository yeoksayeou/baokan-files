Title: Telegram of Congratulation from King Zahir of Afghanistan
Author: 
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

Telegram of Congratulation from King Zahir of Afghanistan

To His Excellency Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of the ninth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I am very pleased to extend my warmest congratulations to Your Excellency and the Chinese people, and express my most sincere wishes for Your Excellency's happiness and the prosperity of the great Chinese people.

                            Mohammed Zahir, King of Afghanistan

### 阿富汗国王查希尔的贺电
穆罕默德·查希尔
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    阿富汗国王查希尔的贺电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东阁下：
    在中华人民共和国建国九周年的时候，我很愉快地向阁下和中国人民致最热烈的祝贺，并且对阁下的幸福和伟大的中国人民的昌盛表示最热忱的愿望。
                            阿富汗国王　穆罕默德·查希尔
