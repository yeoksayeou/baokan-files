Title: Khrushchev and Amer Hold Talks
Author: 
Date: 1958-10-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st - According to TASS, Soviet Premier Khrushchev held a lengthy meeting on the 21st with Marshal Amer, Vice President and Minister of Defense of the United Arab Republic, who is currently visiting Moscow.

The meeting took place in an atmosphere of mutual understanding and sincere friendship.

Marshal Amer's entourage and Anwar, the Chargé d'affaires of the UAR in the Soviet Union, participated in the meeting.

The Soviet side was represented by: Mukhitdinov, Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet; Gromyko, the Soviet Minister of Foreign Affairs; Marshal Malinovsky, the Soviet Minister of Defense; and General Antonov.

On the same day, the Soviet government hosted a breakfast banquet in the Kremlin in honor of Marshal Amer and his entourage.

Marshal Amer paid a courtesy call on Khrushchev in the Kremlin on the 20th. During the visit, the two sides held talks, extensively discussing the current international situation and relations between the UAR and the Soviet Union.

On the same day, Anwar, the Chargé d'affaires of the UAR in the Soviet Union, held a banquet for Amer's visit to the Soviet Union. Khrushchev and other Soviet leaders attended the banquet. (Attached picture)

### 赫鲁晓夫和阿密尔会谈

1958-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫和阿密尔会谈
    新华社21日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫21日同应邀正在莫斯科访问的阿拉伯联合共和国副总统、国防部长阿密尔元帅举行了长时间的会谈。
    会谈是在相互谅解和真诚友好的气氛中进行的。
    阿密尔元帅的随行人员和阿联驻苏联临时代办安瓦尔参加了会谈。
    苏联方面参加会谈的有：苏联最高苏维埃民族院外交委员会主席穆希金诺夫、苏联外交部长葛罗米柯、苏联国防部长马利诺夫斯基元帅和安东诺夫大将。
    同一天，苏联政府在克里姆林宫设早宴招待了阿密尔元帅和他的随行人员。
    阿密尔元帅20日曾经在克里姆林宫拜会了赫鲁晓夫。在拜会时，双方曾进行了会谈，广泛地谈到了有关当前国际局势以及阿联和苏联之间的关系问题。
    同一天，阿联驻苏联临时代办安瓦尔为阿密尔访问苏联举行了宴会。赫鲁晓夫和苏联其他领导人出席了这个宴会。（附图片）
