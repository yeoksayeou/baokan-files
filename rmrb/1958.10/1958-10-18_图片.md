Title: Picture
Author:
Date: 1958-10-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency manuscript:

    Japanese people in Tokyo held a rally on October 8, demanding that the U.S. troops immediately withdraw from the Taiwan area, and demanding that the Japanese government abolish the "Japan-Chiang Treaty" and restore normal relations with China. The right picture shows the venue of the meeting, and the left picture shows the crowd marching in the demonstration after the meeting.
