Title: After Visiting Wuhan, China, the Bulgarian National Assembly Delegation Arrives in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1958-10-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 9th. The Bulgarian National Assembly Delegation of seventeen people, led by Chervenkov, arrived in Hanoi by plane this afternoon.

    When the plane landed at Gia Lam Airport, which was decorated with the national flags of Vietnam and Bulgaria, Ton Quang Phiet, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Vietnamese National Assembly, and members Hoang Van Hoan, Truong Chinh, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, and Ung Van Khiem, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Vietnam, stepped forward to warmly welcome the delegation.
    Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation were heads of various departments of the Vietnamese government, various parties, and people's organizations, diplomatic envoys from various countries, and more than 700 representatives from all walks of life in Vietnam.
    In his speech, Chervenkov, on behalf of the Bulgarian National Assembly and the people, paid tribute to the heroic Vietnamese people, the Vietnamese Labor Party, and President Ho Chi Minh, and congratulated the Vietnamese people on the achievements they have made in socialist construction. He expressed his firm belief that the Vietnamese people, in their just struggle for the reunification of their motherland, will surely overcome the obstruction of imperialism and achieve final victory.
    In his welcoming speech, Hoang Van Hoan said that he believed that this visit by the Bulgarian National Assembly Delegation would further strengthen the friendship and unity between Vietnam and Bulgaria. He wished the People's Republic of Bulgaria growing prosperity.

    Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, 9th. The delegation of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, headed by Comrade Chervenkov, left Wuhan by special plane at noon today, passing through Nanning, to visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    Wei Guoqing, Chairman of the People's Committee of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, Vice Chairmen Qin Yingji and Mo Naiqun, and Yan Guangcai, Mayor of Nanning, went to the airport to see them off. Nguyen Mai, Acting Consul of the Vietnamese Consulate in Nanning, also went to the airport to see them off.
    After a short visit to Vietnam, the delegation will continue to visit cities such as Guangzhou, Shanghai, and Hangzhou in China.

    Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 9th. The Bulgarian National Assembly Delegation, headed by Chervenkov, left Wuhan by special plane this morning to visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam after visiting Wuhan.
    The delegation arrived in Wuhan on the 7th. During their stay in Wuhan, the distinguished guests successively visited the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge, the Wuhan Heavy Machinery Plant, Wuhan University, and the No. 1 Blast Furnace of the Wuhan Iron and Steel Corporation. Some members of the delegation also visited the Zhongyi People's Commune in Macheng County. During the visits, Chairman Chervenkov repeatedly praised the achievements made by Hubei Province in industry, agriculture, and teaching reform.
    During their stay in Wuhan, the Bulgarian guests were warmly welcomed and received by people from all walks of life. Chen Yixin, Vice Governor of Hubei Province, hosted a banquet for the guests. Chen Yixin, Vice Governor of Hubei Province, Liu Huinong, Mayor of Wuhan, and General Chen Zaidao, Commander of the Wuhan Garrison, were among those who welcomed and saw off the guests.

### 在我国武汉参观访问后  保国民议会代表团抵河内

1958-10-10
第5版()
专栏：

    在我国武汉参观访问后
    保国民议会代表团抵河内
    新华社河内9日电　由契尔文科夫率领的保加利亚国民议会代表团一行十七人今天下午乘飞机到达河内。
    当飞机降落在挂满越、保两国国旗的嘉林机场时，越南国会常务委员会副委员长孙光阀、委员黄文欢、越南劳动党中央政治局委员长征、越南外交部副部长雍文谦等上前向代表团表示热烈欢迎。
    在机场上欢迎的还有越南政府各部门、各党派、各人民团体的负责人，各国外交使节以及越南各界人民代表等七百多人。
    契尔文科夫在致词时代表保加利亚国民议会和人民向英雄的越南人民、越南劳动党和胡志明主席致敬，对越南人民在社会主义建设方面所取得的成就表示祝贺。他表示坚信，越南人民在为祖国的统一而进行的正义斗争中，一定能够排除帝国主义的阻挠，取得最后的胜利。
    黄文欢在致欢迎词时，表示相信保加利亚国民议会代表团这一次访问将进一步加强越、保两国之间的友谊和团结。他祝保加利亚人民共和国日益繁荣。
    新华社南宁9日电　以契尔文科夫同志为首的保加利亚人民共和国国民议会代表团今天中午从武汉乘专机途经南宁前往越南民主共和国访问。
    前往机场欢送的有广西僮族自治区人民委员会主席韦国清、副主席覃应机、莫乃群和南宁市市长阎光彩等。越南驻南宁领事馆代领事阮梅也到机场欢送。
    代表团在越南作短期访问后，将继续回到中国的广州、上海、杭州等城市参观访问。
    新华社武汉9日电　以契尔文科夫为首的保加利亚国民议会代表团，在武汉参观访问后，今天上午乘专机离开武汉前往越南民主共和国访问。
    代表团是在7日到达武汉的。在武汉期间，贵宾们先后参观了武汉长江大桥、武汉重型机床厂、武汉大学、武钢一号高炉，部分团员还曾到麻城县中一人民公社参观。参观、访问中，契尔文科夫团长对湖北省工农业和教学改革方面所取得的成就一再表示赞扬。
    保加利亚贵宾们在武汉期间受到了各界人民的热情欢迎和接待。湖北省副省长陈一新曾设宴招待贵宾。迎接和欢送贵宾的，有湖北省副省长陈一新、武汉市市长刘惠农和武汉驻军首长陈再道上将等。
