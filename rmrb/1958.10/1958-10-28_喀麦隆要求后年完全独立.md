Title: Cameroon Requests Complete Independence in Two Years
Author:
Date: 1958-10-28
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, October 27th, Yaounde Dispatch: The Cameroon Legislative Assembly passed a resolution with forty-six votes in favor and seven votes against, requesting complete independence on January 1, 1960.
    The Assembly wants the Cameroon government to request France to submit a proposal to the United Nations during the current session of the General Assembly to abolish the trusteeship agreement for Cameroon.

### 喀麦隆要求后年完全独立

1958-10-28
第5版()
专栏：

    喀麦隆要求后年完全独立
    新华社27日讯　雅温得消息：喀麦隆立法议会以四十六票对七票通过决议，要求在1960年1月1日完全独立。
    议会要喀麦隆政府要求法国在联合国大会本届会议期间向联合国提出废除对喀麦隆的托管协定的提案。
