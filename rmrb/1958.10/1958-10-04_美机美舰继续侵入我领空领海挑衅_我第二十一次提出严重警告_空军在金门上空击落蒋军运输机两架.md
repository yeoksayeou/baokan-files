Title: US Planes and Warships Continue to Intrude into Our Airspace and Territorial Waters to Provoke; We Issue the Twenty-First Serious Warning; Air Force Shoots Down Two Chiang Kai-shek Transport Planes Over Kinmen

Author:

Date: 1958-10-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd: From the night of October 2nd to the evening of October 3rd, twenty US fighter planes intruded into the airspace over the Fujian Xiamen area, with two of them intruding over the mainland. Four US warships intruded into the territorial waters of the Kinmen and Matsu areas. A spokesman for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs stated under instruction that the Chinese government issues its twenty-first serious warning against this serious military provocation by the United States.

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, October 3rd: On the afternoon of October 3rd, People's Liberation Army Air Force fighters on the Fujian Front shot down two US-made Chiang Kai-shek C-46 transport planes dropping combat supplies over Kinmen. One crashed on Kinmen Island, and the other crashed into the sea near Kinmen.

### 美机美舰继续侵入我领空领海挑衅  我第二十一次提出严重警告  空军在金门上空击落蒋军运输机两架

1958-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    美机美舰继续侵入我领空领海挑衅
    我第二十一次提出严重警告
    空军在金门上空击落蒋军运输机两架
    新华社3日讯　10月2日夜间到3日晚，美国战斗机二十架侵入我国福建厦门地区的领海上空，其中两架侵入大陆上空。美国军舰四艘侵入我国金门、马祖地区的领海。我外交部发言人奉命声明，对于美国的这种严重的军事挑衅，中国政府第二十一次提出严重警告。
    新华社福建前线3日电　福建前线中国人民解放军空军歼击机3日下午在金门上空击落空投作战物资的美制蒋军C—四六型运输机两架。一架坠毁在金门岛上，另一架坠入金门附近大海中。
