Title: GDR Delegation Arrives in Sofia
Author:
Date: 1958-10-09
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 8th. A party and government delegation from East Germany, headed by Walter Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl, arrived in Sofia by special plane this afternoon.

The delegation is on a friendly visit at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the Bulgarian Council of Ministers. During the visit (October 8 to 12), the delegation will hold talks with the Bulgarian party and government delegation on the international situation and on further developing the fraternal relations between Germany and Bulgaria.

The delegation was warmly welcomed at the airport by Bulgarian party and government leaders Zhivkov, Yugov, R. Damyanov, Ganev, diplomatic envoys from some countries stationed in Sofia, and citizens of Sofia.

Yugov and Ulbricht spoke at the airport successively. They unanimously pointed out that this visit by the East German party and government delegation will help to further strengthen and develop the friendship and cooperation between the people of Bulgaria and Germany, and the friendship, unity, and fraternal cooperation among the socialist camp countries headed by the Soviet Union.

### 民主德国代表团抵索非亚

1958-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    民主德国代表团抵索非亚
    新华社索非亚8日电　以德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希和总理格罗提渥为首的民主德国党和政府代表团，今天下午乘专机到达索非亚。
    代表团是应保加利亚共产党中央委员会和保加利亚部长会议的邀请前来进行友好访问的。在访问期间（10月8日到12日），代表团将同保加利亚党和政府代表团就国际形势问题以及进一步发展德保两国之间的兄弟关系问题进行会谈。
    代表团在飞机场上受到了保加利亚党和政府的领导人日夫科夫、于哥夫、拉·达米扬诺夫、加涅夫，一些国家驻索非亚的外交使节和索非亚市民的热烈欢迎。
    于哥夫和乌布利希在机场上先后讲了话。他们一致指出，民主德国党和政府代表团的这次访问，将有助于进一步加强和发展保德两国人民之间的友谊合作和以苏联为首的社会主义阵营各国间的友谊、团结和兄弟合作。
