Title: Brotherly Countries' Newspapers Prominently Publish Order from Our Ministry of National Defense
Author:
Date: 1958-10-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 21st – Major newspapers in the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Romania, Bulgaria, Mongolia, and other countries all prominently published the order issued by our Ministry of National Defense on October 20th regarding the resumption of shelling of Jinmen (Quemoy).

### 兄弟国家报纸显著刊载我国防部命令

1958-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

    兄弟国家报纸显著刊载我国防部命令
    新华社21日讯　苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、蒙古等国的主要报纸，都在21日以显著地位刊登了我国国防部10月20日发布的恢复炮击金门的命令。
