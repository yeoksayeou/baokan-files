Title: My Youth Delegation Arrives in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1958-10-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 3rd. The Chinese Youth Delegation, led by Wang Wei, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, arrived in Warsaw by train from Budapest on the 3rd.
　　The delegation is composed of five people, who will visit Poland for twenty days.

### 我青年代表团抵华沙

1958-10-05
第2版()
专栏：

    我青年代表团抵华沙
    新华社华沙3日电　由共产主义青年团中央委员会书记王伟率领的中国青年代表团，3日从布达佩斯乘火车到达华沙。
    代表团由五人组成，他们将在波兰访问二十天。
