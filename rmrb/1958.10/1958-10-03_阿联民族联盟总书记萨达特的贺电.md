Title: Telegram of Congratulations from Anwar Sadat, Secretary-General of the National Union of the United Arab Republic
Author: Anwar Sadat
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

    Telegram of Congratulations from Anwar Sadat, Secretary-General of the National Union of the United Arab Republic
    To Mr. Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China,

    On the occasion of the success and victory of the Chinese revolution and the ninth anniversary of the establishment of the People's Republic, we, on behalf of the National Union of the United Arab Republic, extend our congratulations to you. We take this opportunity to express our full support for the great struggle of your country's people to recover their territory, repel barbaric imperialist aggression, eliminate imperialist agents, and safeguard world peace.

              Anwar Sadat, Secretary-General of the National Union of the United Arab Republic

### 阿联民族联盟总书记萨达特的贺电
安瓦·萨达特
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    阿联民族联盟总书记萨达特的贺电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东先生
    在中国革命取得成功和胜利建立人民共和国九周年纪念的时候，我们代表阿拉伯联合共和国民族联盟向你祝贺。我们借这个机会表示充分支持伟大的贵国人民为收复领土，击退蛮横的帝国主义侵略，消灭帝国主义代理人，和维护世界和平而进行的斗争。
              阿拉伯联合共和国民族联盟总书记　安瓦·萨达特
