Title: U.S. Warships and Aircraft Again Intrude Into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace to Provoke; Our Country Issues the Nineteenth Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1958-10-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, October 1st. On October 1st, ten U.S. warships intruded into our territorial waters in the Xiamen and Matsu areas of Fujian Province; ten U.S. fighter planes intruded into the airspace over our territorial waters in the Fujian area. A spokesman for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to declare that the Chinese government has issued its nineteenth serious warning regarding this grave military provocation by the United States.

### 美舰美机又侵入我领海领空挑衅  我国提出第十九次严重警告

1958-10-02
第2版()
专栏：

    美舰美机又侵入我领海领空挑衅
    我国提出第十九次严重警告
    新华社1日讯　10月1日，美国军舰十艘侵入我国福建厦门和马祖地区的领海；美国战斗机十架，侵入我国福建地区的领海上空。我外交部发言人奉命声明，对于美国的这种严重的军事挑衅，中国政府第十九次提出严重警告。
