Title: U.S. Warships and Aircraft Again Intrude into the Territorial Waters and Airspace of Our Matsu and Pingtan Areas; We Issue the 26th Serious Warning

Author:
Date: 1958-10-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. On October 9th, the U.S. destroyers "Isherwood" and "Stoddard," along with two U.S. FJ-4 fighter planes, intruded into the territorial waters and airspace of the Matsu and Pingtan areas of Fujian Province. Regarding this serious military provocation by the United States, a spokesman for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 26th serious warning.

### 美舰美机又侵入我马祖和平潭地区领海领空  我第二十六次提出严重警告

1958-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    美舰美机又侵入我马祖和平潭地区领海领空
    我第二十六次提出严重警告
    新华社9日讯　10月9日美国驱逐舰“伊社伍德”号和“斯多大德”号两艘、美国战斗机FJ—4两架侵入我国福建省的马祖和平潭地区的领海和领海上空，对于美国这种严重的军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命第二十六次提出严重警告。
