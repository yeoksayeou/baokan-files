Title: Czechoslovakian Handicraft Cooperative Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-10-19
Page: 3
Welcome, Friends From Afar

    Xinhua News Agency, October 15th. The five-member delegation of the Czechoslovakian Handicraft Cooperative arrived in Beijing by plane today. The head of the delegation is Smrcka, Vice Chairman of the Czechoslovak Central Cooperative Committee and Vice Chairman of the Central Union of Handicraft Cooperatives. Ma Yuzhang, Vice Director of the All-China Handicraft Cooperative, and others welcomed the delegation at the airport today.

### 捷工艺合作社代表团抵京

1958-10-19
第3版()
专栏：欢迎远道而来的朋友

    捷工艺合作社代表团抵京
    新华社15日讯　捷克斯洛伐克工艺合作社访华代表团一行五人，今天乘飞机到达北京。代表团的团长是捷克斯洛伐克中央合作委员会副主席、工艺合作社中央联社副主席斯穆尔契卡。今天前往机场迎接的有中华全国手工业合作总社副主任马豫章等。
