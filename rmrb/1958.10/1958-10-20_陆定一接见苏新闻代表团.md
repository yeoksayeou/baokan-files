Title: Lu Dingyi Receives Soviet News Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-10-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 19th—Lu Dingyi, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, received the entire Soviet news workers delegation headed by Moskovsky and the editor-in-chief of the "Soviet-Chinese Friendship" magazine, Rogov, this afternoon.

Present at the reception was Androsov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China.

Also present were: Wu Lengxi, Mei Yi, Fan Jin, Lu Minggu, Yao Zhen, Wang Yutian, Xu Huang, and others.

Xinhua News Agency, October 19th—Soviet Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Antonov, held a banquet at noon today to bid farewell to the Soviet news workers delegation headed by Moskovsky.

Wu Lengxi, Mei Yi, Wang Yunsheng, and Fan Jin, Vice Presidents of the All-China Journalists Association, Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and responsible persons from the capital's journalistic circles were invited to attend the banquet.

### 陆定一接见苏新闻代表团

1958-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

    陆定一接见苏新闻代表团
    新华社19日讯　中共中央政治局候补委员陆定一在今天下午接见了以莫斯科夫斯基为首的苏联新闻工作者代表团全体人员和“苏中友好”杂志总编辑罗果夫。
    接见时在座的有苏联驻华大使馆参赞安德罗素夫。
    陪同接见的有：吴冷西、梅益、范瑾、卢鸣谷、姚溱、王雨田、徐晃等。
    新华社19日讯　苏联驻中国临时代办安东诺夫今天中午举行宴会，欢送以莫斯科夫斯基为首的苏联新闻工作者代表团。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长吴冷西、梅益、王芸生、范瑾，外交部新闻司副司长徐晃和首都新闻界负责人士，应邀出席了宴会。
