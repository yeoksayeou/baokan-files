Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1958-10-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, October 25th. The 82nd meeting of the Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks was held at the Mysliwiecki Palace in Warsaw at 3:30 p.m. today. The meeting was originally scheduled for 3:00 p.m. on the 24th, but was postponed to today for administrative reasons.
    The meeting lasted one hour and forty-five minutes. Afterwards, both sides announced that the next meeting would continue on November 7th (Friday) at 2:00 p.m.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1958-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社华沙25日电　中美两国大使级会谈第八十二次会议，今天下午三时三十分在华沙梅希里维茨基宫举行。这次会议原订在24日下午三时举行，由于行政性的理由，推迟到今天举行。
    本次会议历时一小时四十五分。会后双方宣布，下次会议将在11月7日（星期五）下午二时继续举行。
