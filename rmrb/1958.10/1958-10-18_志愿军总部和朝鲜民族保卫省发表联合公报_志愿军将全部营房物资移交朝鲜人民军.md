Title: Joint Communiqué Issued by the Headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Ministry of National Defence of Korea: The Chinese People's Volunteers Transfer All Barracks and Materials to the Korean People's Army
Author:
Date: 1958-10-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 17th—This morning at 10:00 AM (Pyongyang time), the signing ceremony for the Joint Communiqué of the Headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Ministry of National Defence of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea was held.

The Headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteers was represented at the signing ceremony by General Yang Yong, Commander of the Volunteers; General Wang Ping, Political Commissar; Lieutenant General Liang Biye, Deputy Political Commissar; and a total of fifteen generals and senior officers from the headquarters.

The Ministry of National Defence of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea was represented at the signing ceremony by General Kim Kwang-hyup, Minister of National Defence; General Lee Kwon-mu, Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army; Lieutenant General Seo Cheol, Director of the General Political Bureau; and generals and senior officers from various services and arms, totaling fifteen people.

Also present at the signing ceremony was Qiao Xiaoguang, China's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Korea.

A huge portrait of Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Kim Il-sung was hung on the front of the signing hall. The national flags of China and Korea were placed on the signing table. At precisely 10:00 AM, General Yang Yong, on behalf of the Headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteers, and General Kim Kwang-hyup, on behalf of the Ministry of National Defence of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, signed the Joint Communiqué written in both Chinese and Korean. Subsequently, they exchanged the texts of the Joint Communiqué. The representatives of both sides shook hands with each other and raised their glasses in congratulation, toasting the eternal friendship between the armies of China and Korea. The signing ceremony ended at 10:15 AM. The representatives of both sides took a group photo together.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 17th—Joint Communiqué of the Headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Ministry of National Defence of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea

In response to the proposal of the Chinese and Korean governments regarding the withdrawal of all foreign troops from North and South Korea and the promotion of a peaceful resolution to the Korean question, the Headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteers already issued a statement on February 20, 1958, deciding to completely withdraw from Korea in batches before the end of 1958. As it is about to completely withdraw from Korea, the Headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteers has decided to transfer all of its barracks, equipment, barracks facilities, materials, and equipment to the Korean People's Army free of charge. The handover has been successfully completed on October 16, 1958. Both sides expressed satisfaction with this and believe that this fact is another manifestation of the fighting friendship between the people of China and Korea.

	General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers
	General Kim Kwang-hyup, Minister of National Defence of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea
			October 17, 1958, in Pyongyang

### 志愿军总部和朝鲜民族保卫省发表联合公报  志愿军将全部营房物资移交朝鲜人民军

1958-10-18
第4版()
专栏：

    志愿军总部和朝鲜民族保卫省发表联合公报
    志愿军将全部营房物资移交朝鲜人民军
    新华社平壤17日电　今天上午十时（平壤时间），中国人民志愿军总部和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫省联合公报举行签字仪式。
    中国人民志愿军总部参加签字仪式的有：志愿军司令员杨勇上将、政治委员王平上将、副政治委员梁必业中将等总部将领和高级军官共十五人。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫省参加签字仪式的有：民族保卫相金光侠大将、朝鲜人民军总参谋长李权武上将、总政治局局长徐哲中将和各军种、兵种将领及高级军官共十五人。
    参加签字仪式的还有我国驻朝鲜特命全权大使乔晓光。
    签字会场正面悬挂着毛泽东主席、金日成首相的巨幅画像。签字桌上插着中、朝两国国旗。十时正，杨勇上将代表中国人民志愿军总部、金光侠大将代表朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫省分别在用中、朝两国文字写成的联合公报上签字。接着，互相交换了联合公报文本。双方代表互相一一握手、并举杯祝贺，为中朝两国军队的永恒友谊干杯。签字仪式于十时十五分结束。双方代表一起合影留念。
    新华社平壤17日电　中国人民志愿军总部和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫省联合公报
    为了响应中朝两国政府关于一切外国军队从南北朝鲜撤出和促进朝鲜问题和平解决的建议，中国人民志愿军总部在1958年2月20日已经发表声明，决定于1958年年底以前，分批地全部撤出朝鲜。在即将从朝鲜全部撤出的时候，中国人民志愿军总部决定将自己的营房、营具、营房设备、物资、器材，全部无偿地移交给朝鲜人民军。交接工作已经在1958年10月16日顺利完成。双方对此表示满意，并且认为这一事实，是中朝两国人民之间的战斗友谊的又一次表现。
        中国人民志愿军司令员上将杨勇
        朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫相大将　金光侠
                              1958年10月17日　于平壤
