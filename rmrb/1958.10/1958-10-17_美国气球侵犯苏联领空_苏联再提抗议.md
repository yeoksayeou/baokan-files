Title: U.S. Balloons Invade Soviet Airspace, Soviet Union Lodges Another Protest
Author:
Date: 1958-10-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: The Soviet government lodged another protest with the U.S. government on the 13th regarding U.S. actions in releasing balloons into Soviet airspace. The note contends that this U.S. behavior flagrantly violates universally recognized norms of international law.

As previously reported, the Soviet Union recently intercepted several balloons with instruments launched by the U.S. Air Force in its airspace. On September 3, the Soviet government sent a note to the U.S. government protesting this matter. The U.S. government replied to the Soviet government on September 5, insisting that the U.S. was releasing these balloons seemingly for so-called "scientific purposes." Therefore, on the 13th, Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Zaharov summoned U.S. Chargé d'Affaires in the Soviet Union, Davis, and handed him a note from the Soviet government refuting the aforementioned U.S. statement.

The Soviet government hopes that the U.S. government will, as it declared in its note to the Soviet government on February 8, 1956, take immediate measures to ensure that similar acts do not occur in the future.

### 美国气球侵犯苏联领空  苏联再提抗议

1958-10-17
第5版()
专栏：

    美国气球侵犯苏联领空
    苏联再提抗议
    据新华社讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联政府13日照会美国政府，再次对美国向苏联领空发放气球的行为提出抗议。照会认为，美国的这种行为粗暴地破坏了公认的国际法准则。
    前已报道，苏联最近时期在它的领空截获了美国空军发射的若干带有仪器的气球。9月3日，苏联政府曾经就这件事照会美国政府，提出抗议。美国政府于9月5日复照苏联政府，硬说美国放射这些气球似乎是为了所谓“科学上的目的”。因此，苏联外交部副部长扎哈罗夫13日召见了美国驻苏临时代办戴维斯，交给他一份苏联政府的照会，对美国的上述说法予以驳斥。
    苏联政府希望美国政府像它在1956年2月8日致苏联政府的照会中所声明的那样，立即采取措施，使今后不再发生类似行为。
