Title: Telegram of Greetings from King Norodom Suramarit of Cambodia
Author: Nuoluodun Sūlāmǎlìtè
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

Cambodian King Suramarit's Telegram of Greetings
To His Excellency Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of your country's National Day, I am very pleased to represent the Khmer people and in my own name to extend to Your Excellency my best wishes for the success of your noble mission and for the prosperity and happiness of the Chinese people. With highest respect.

                                                                                                                                              King of Cambodia, Norodom Suramarit

### 柬埔寨国王苏拉玛里特的贺电
诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨国王苏拉玛里特的贺电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东先生阁下：
    在贵国国庆的时候，我很愉快地代表高棉人民并以我个人的名义，向阁下致以最良好的愿望，祝你的崇高任务获得成功，祝中国人民繁荣和幸福。顺致最高的敬意。
                            柬埔寨国王　诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特
