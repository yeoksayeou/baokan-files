Title: Baotou Steel Plant Produces Iron
Author:
Date: 1958-10-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The Baotou Steel Plant, one of the three medium-sized iron and steel joint enterprises being built in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, produced its first molten iron on September 26th.

After more than three months of arduous struggle, the Mongolian, Han, Hui, Manchu, and Daur workers of the Baotou Steel Plant have now built two 55-cubic-meter small blast furnaces and three 13-cubic-meter small blast furnaces. Among them, the No. 1 13-cubic-meter small blast furnace produced its first furnace of high-quality molten iron at 1:00 PM on the 29th, amidst the sound of gongs and drums.

### 呼和浩特钢铁厂出铁

1958-10-07
第2版()
专栏：

    呼和浩特钢铁厂出铁
    据新华社讯　内蒙古自治区正在兴建的三个中型钢铁联合企业之一呼和浩特钢铁厂，已于9月26日流出第一炉铁水。
    呼和浩特钢铁厂的蒙古、汉、回、满和达斡尔族职工，经过三个多月的苦战，现已建成两个五十五立方公尺的小高炉和三个十三立方公尺的小高炉。其中十三立方公尺的第一号小高炉，29日下午一时在锣鼓喧天声中流出了第一炉质量良好的铁水。
