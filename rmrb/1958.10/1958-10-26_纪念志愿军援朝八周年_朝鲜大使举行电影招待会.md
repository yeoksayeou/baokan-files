Title: Commemorating the Eighth Anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's Aid to Korea: Korean Ambassador Holds Film Reception
Author:
Date: 1958-10-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th. The Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Li Yong-ho, held a film reception in Beijing this evening to commemorate the eighth anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's participation in the Korean War.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi attended the reception.

Also attending the reception were heads of various departments of the state organs and people's organizations of our country, as well as envoys from various fraternal countries to China.

At the reception, two Korean documentaries reporting on the return of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and a Korean feature film about suppressing special agents, "The Dangerous Moment," were shown.

### 纪念志愿军援朝八周年  朝鲜大使举行电影招待会

1958-10-26
第3版()
专栏：

    纪念志愿军援朝八周年
    朝鲜大使举行电影招待会
    新华社25日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使李永镐今天晚间在北京举行电影招待会，纪念中国人民志愿军赴朝作战八周年。
    周恩来总理、陈毅副总理出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的还有我国国家机关各部门、各人民团体的负责人，以及各兄弟国家驻中国的使节等。
    在招待会上放映了两部报道志愿军归国的朝鲜纪录片和一部朝鲜肃特故事片“危险的一刹那”。
