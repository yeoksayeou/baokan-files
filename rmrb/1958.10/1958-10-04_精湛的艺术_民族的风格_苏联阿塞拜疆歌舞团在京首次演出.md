Title: Exquisite Art, National Style: Soviet Azerbaijan Song and Dance Ensemble's First Performance in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-10-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: The Soviet Azerbaijan State Song and Dance Ensemble, from the song and dance homeland of the Soviet Union, received a warm welcome from more than three thousand spectators at its premiere performance in Beijing tonight.

The artists performed a total of twenty-four song and dance programs with strong national characteristics at the Zhongshan Park Concert Hall tonight. Their graceful dance movements, moving singing, and strong emotions in their performances deeply moved the audience. After each program, the audience burst into long-lasting applause, and the artists had to take many curtain calls and replay six of the programs.

This large-scale song and dance troupe, which was founded as early as 1936 and is famous in the Soviet Union, not only introduced to the capital's audience traditional programs showing Azerbaijan's historical customs, the people's heroic sentiments, and love life, but also performed many dances and songs taken from modern life showing the people's socialist happy life and real struggles.

After the entire performance, Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Culture, and others stepped onto the stage to congratulate the song and dance troupe, the first in the Azerbaijan Republic to receive the honorary title of collective, on its successful performance. The capital's literary and art circles also presented flowers to the Soviet artists.

### 精湛的艺术　民族的风格  苏联阿塞拜疆歌舞团在京首次演出

1958-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    精湛的艺术　民族的风格
    苏联阿塞拜疆歌舞团在京首次演出
    据新华社3日讯　来自苏联歌舞之乡的苏联阿塞拜疆国家歌舞团，今晚在北京的首次演出中获得了三千多观众的热烈欢迎。
    艺术家们今晚在中山公园音乐堂共演出了二十四个富有浓厚民族色彩的歌舞节目。他们那优美的舞蹈动作、动人的歌声和表演中的强烈感情，深深地感动了全场观众。在每个节目演出结束后，全场都暴发出经久不息的掌声，艺术家们不得不多次谢幕，并且重演了其中的六个节目。
    这个早在1936年就已成立并且在苏联负有盛名的大型歌舞团，今晚既向首都观众介绍了表现阿塞拜疆的历史习俗、人民的英武情绪和爱情生活的传统节目，同时也演出了表现人民社会主义幸福生活和现实斗争的许多取材于现代生活的舞蹈和歌曲。
    整个演出结束后，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥、文化部副部长郑振铎等人步上舞台，祝贺这个在阿塞拜疆共和国第一个获得功勋集体称号的歌舞团演出成功。首都文艺界并向苏联艺术家们献了鲜花。
