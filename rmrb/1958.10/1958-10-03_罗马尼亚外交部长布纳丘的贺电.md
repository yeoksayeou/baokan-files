Title: Telegram of Congratulations from Romanian Foreign Minister Bunaciu
Author: 
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

Telegram of Congratulations from Romanian Foreign Minister Bunaciu

To Comrade Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:

	On the occasion of the 9th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, please accept my sincere congratulations.

	The Romanian people, who have an unbreakable and eternal friendship with the great Chinese people, are very pleased to note the achievements made by the People's Republic of China in the socialist transformation of its motherland over the past nine years.

	I wish the Chinese people and you personally, Minister, new and greater achievements in implementing the policy of safeguarding peace and promoting international cooperation pursued by the People's Republic of China.

		A. Bunaciu
		Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Romanian People's Republic

### 罗马尼亚外交部长布纳丘的贺电
阿·布纳丘
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚外交部长布纳丘的贺电中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅同志：
    当中华人民共和国建国九周年的时候，请你接受我诚挚的祝贺。
    同伟大的中国人民有着牢不可破的、永恒的友谊的罗马尼亚人民，非常高兴地注视着中华人民共和国九年来，在祖国的社会主义改造中所取得的成就。
    谨祝中国人民和你本人，部长同志，在执行中华人民共和国所奉行的保卫和平和促进国际合作的政策中获得新的、更大的成就。
                罗马尼亚人民共和国外交部长　阿·布纳丘
