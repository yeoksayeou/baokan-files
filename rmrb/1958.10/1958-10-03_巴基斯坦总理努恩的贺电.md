Title: Pakistani Prime Minister Noon's Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

To His Excellency Mr. Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of the ninth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I am very pleased to extend, on behalf of my cabinet colleagues and in my own name, my most heartfelt congratulations and best wishes to Your Excellency and to the government and people of the People's Republic of China.

                                      Noon
                                      Prime Minister of Pakistan

### 巴基斯坦总理努恩的贺电
努恩
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦总理努恩的贺电中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来先生阁下：
    欣逢中华人民共和国建国九周年纪念日，我很愉快地代表我的内阁同事并以我自己的名义向阁下以及中华人民共和国政府和人民致最衷心的祝贺和良好的愿望。
                                      巴基斯坦总理　努恩
