Title: Albanian Military Delegation Goes to Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1958-10-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 20th. The Albanian military delegation headed by General Baluku, after a day of sightseeing in Hangzhou, took a special plane to Guangzhou today for a visit.
    The distinguished guests received a warm reception in Hangzhou and attended a welcoming banquet hosted by Lieutenant General Zhou Guanwu, Second Political Commissar of the Zhejiang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 阿军事代表团去广州

1958-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    阿军事代表团去广州
    新华社杭州20日电　以巴卢库上将为首的阿尔巴尼亚访华军事代表团，在杭州进行了一天的参观游览后，今日乘专机去广州访问。
    贵宾们在杭州受到了热情的接待，并出席了中国人民解放军浙江军区第二政治委员周贯五中将的欢迎宴会。
