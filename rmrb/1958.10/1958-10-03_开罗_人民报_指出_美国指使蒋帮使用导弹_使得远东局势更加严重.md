Title: Cairo "People's News" Points Out: U.S. Instigation of Chiang Gang's Use of Missiles Further Aggravates the Situation in the Far East
Author:
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 5

    Cairo "People's News" Points Out
    U.S. Instigation of Chiang Gang's Use of Missiles Further Aggravates the Situation in the Far East
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, Electric Dispatch. The Cairo "People's News" commentator published a commentary on September 30th, condemning the crime of the U.S. aggressors instigating Chiang Kai-shek's air force to use missiles to attack the Chinese air force.
    The commentator said that just as the United States is obstructing the Warsaw talks, disregarding the sovereignty of People's China over the coastal islands and Taiwan, and obstructing the restoration of China's legitimate seat in the United Nations, this behavior by the United States is making the situation in the Far East even more serious.
    He warned the U.S. aggressors that the Soviet Union, possessing intercontinental missiles, will support China in dealing with U.S. aggression.
    The commentator demanded that the United States stop this adventurous behavior, and demanded that the United Nations stop the United States' "brink of war" policy in order to safeguard world peace.

### 开罗“人民报”指出  美国指使蒋帮使用导弹  使得远东局势更加严重

1958-10-03
第5版()
专栏：

    开罗“人民报”指出
    美国指使蒋帮使用导弹  使得远东局势更加严重
    据新华社开罗电　开罗“人民报”评论员9月30日发表评论，谴责美国侵略者指使蒋介石空军使用导弹攻击中国空军的罪行。
    评论员说，正当美国阻挠华沙会谈、无视人民中国对沿海岛屿和台湾的主权、并阻挠恢复中国在联合国合法席位的时候，美国的这一行为使得远东的局势更加严重了。
    他警告美国侵略者说，拥有洲际导弹的苏联将支持中国来对付美国的侵略。
    评论员要求美国停止这种冒险行为，并且要求联合国制止美国的“战争边缘”政策，以维护世界和平。
