Title: Khrushchev Discusses the Current International Situation: If Imperialism Dares to Invade the Socialist Camp, It Will Lead to the Collapse of the Entire Capitalist System
Author:
Date: 1958-10-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 16th: TASS Moscow, October 16th: Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, said in Krasnodar on the 15th that the current situation is: The imperialists may not dare to launch a war against socialist countries. He said that anyone who can think and judge correctly cannot fail to see that the Soviet Union and the socialist camp countries are now at such a stage of development that any attempt to launch a new war against these countries will end in the collapse of the capitalist system.

Khrushchev delivered a speech at a rally of working people in the Krasnodar Territory celebrating the Territory's receipt of the Order of Lenin.

Khrushchev spoke of the vigorous rise of all socialist countries, whose people are victoriously developing their economies and cultures, and confidently advancing along the socialist road. He also spoke of the unbreakable unity and solidarity of the socialist camp countries.

Khrushchev said that the prestige of the Soviet Union and socialist countries has grown immeasurably among the working people of all countries. The Soviet Union stands on the side of all peoples fighting for freedom and independence.

Speaking of the Soviet Union's economic achievements this year, Khrushchev pointed out that as of October 10 this year, the state had purchased 1.4877 billion poods more grain than in 1953, an increase of 86%; 17.7622 million tons more sugar beets than in 1953, an increase of 156%; 2.006 million tons more potatoes than in 1953, an increase of 76%; 1.475 million tons more meat (gross weight) than in 1953, an increase of 62%; and 9.48 million tons more milk than in 1953, a doubling. Industrial enterprises produced 228,000 tons more animal oil than in 1953, an increase of 73%; and 112,000 tons more wool than in 1953, an increase of 62%. Khrushchev pointed out that these figures indicate that Soviet agriculture has made a leap forward. He said that we still have the potential to achieve greater development in agricultural production.

Khrushchev pointed out that the Soviet Union's industry has achieved great success. Great achievements have been made in the development of science and culture. The material welfare of the people is improving year by year. He said that the whole country is now preparing to welcome the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. The draft seven-year plan for the development of the national economy stipulates a new and powerful upsurge in the economy and, on this basis, an improvement in the living standards of the people.

In his speech, Khrushchev spoke about some of the tasks facing agricultural workers. He pointed out that under the current conditions, raising labor productivity and reducing product costs are the fundamental issues in developing the production of collective farms and state farms.

### 赫鲁晓夫谈目前国际形势  帝国主义如敢进犯社会主义阵营  必将导致整个资本主义体系崩溃

1958-10-17
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫谈目前国际形势
    帝国主义如敢进犯社会主义阵营
    必将导致整个资本主义体系崩溃
    新华社16日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科16日讯：苏共中央第一书记、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫15日在克拉斯诺达说，现在的局势是：帝国主义者未必敢对社会主义国家发动战争。他说，任何一个能够正确思考和判断的人都不能不看到，苏联和社会主义阵营各国现在处在这样的发展阶段：对这些国家发动新战争的企图必将以资本主义体系的崩溃而告终。
    赫鲁晓夫在克拉斯诺达边疆区劳动人民庆祝边疆区荣获列宁勋章的大会上发表了演说。
    赫鲁晓夫谈到了所有社会主义国家的蓬勃高涨，这些国家的人民胜利地发展着自己的经济和文化，满怀信心地沿着社会主义大道前进。他还谈到了社会主义阵营国家的牢不可破的团结和一致。
    赫鲁晓夫说，苏联、社会主义各国的威信在世界各国劳动人民中间不可估量地增长了。苏联站在争取自由和独立的各国人民的一边。
    赫鲁晓夫在谈到苏联今年的经济成就时指出，截至今年10月10日为止，国家收购的谷物比1953年多十四亿八千七百万普特，即增加了86％；甜菜比1953年多一千七百七十六万二千吨，即增加了156％；马铃薯比1953年多二百万零六千吨，即增加了76％；肉类（毛重）比1953年多一百四十七万五千吨，即增加了62％；奶类比1953年多九百四十八万吨，即增加了一倍。工业企业所生产的动物油比1953年多二十二万八千吨，即增加了73％；毛类比1953年多十一万二千吨，即增加了62％。赫鲁晓夫指出，这些数字表明，苏联的农业有了飞跃的发展。他说，但是我们还有潜力来求得农业生产的更大的发展。
    赫鲁晓夫指出，苏联的工业很有成绩。科学和文化的发展取得了巨大的成就。人民的物质福利一年年在提高。他说，现在全国都在准备迎接苏共第二十一次代表大会。发展国民经济七年计划草案规定经济上实行新的强大的高涨和在这个基础上提高人民的生活水平。
    赫鲁晓夫在演说中谈到了农业工作者面临的一些任务。他指出，在当前条件下，提高劳动生产率和降低产品成本是发展集体农庄和国营农场生产的根本问题。
