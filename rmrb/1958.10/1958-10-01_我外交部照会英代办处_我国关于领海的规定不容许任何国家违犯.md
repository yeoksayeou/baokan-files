Title: Our Foreign Ministry Sends Note to British Chargé d'Affaires: China's Regulations on Territorial Waters Do Not Allow Any Country to Violate Them
Author:
Date: 1958-10-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Following the Chinese government's declaration regarding territorial waters, the British government, on September 13th, sent a note to our government expressing disagreement with our government's declaration on territorial waters. Yesterday, our Foreign Ministry sent a note to the British Chargé d'Affaires, rejecting the British government's unreasonable arguments and attitude, and pointed out that the British government's action was an unreasonable interference in our government's exercise of its sovereignty, which our government deeply regrets. The note from our Foreign Ministry also solemnly stated: The Chinese government's declaration and related regulations regarding territorial waters apply equally to all foreign aircraft, ships, and individuals, and no violations will be permitted. Any actions intended to interfere with the Chinese government's exercise of its national sovereignty are resolutely intolerable to our government.

### 我外交部照会英代办处  我国关于领海的规定不容许任何国家违犯

1958-10-01
第6版()
专栏：

    我外交部照会英代办处
    我国关于领海的规定不容许任何国家违犯
    新华社30日讯　在我国政府发表关于领海声明后，英国政府竟然于9月13日照会我国政府，对我国政府关于领海的声明表示异议。昨天我外交部照会英国代办处驳回英国政府的无理论点和态度，并指出英国政府此举是对我国政府行使它的主权的无理干预，我国政府对此深表遗憾。我外交部照会并严正声明：我国政府关于领海的声明和有关规定，对于一切外国飞机、船只和个人都一律适用，绝不容许违犯，任何意图干涉中国政府行使它的国家主权的行为，都是我国政府所坚决不能容许的。
