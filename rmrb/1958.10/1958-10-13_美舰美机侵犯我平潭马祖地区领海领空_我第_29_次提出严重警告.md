Title: US Warships and Warplanes Invade the Territorial Waters and Airspace of Pingtan and Matsu Areas; We Issue the 29th Serious Warning

Author:

Date: 1958-10-13
Page: 1

    US Warships and Warplanes Invade the Territorial Waters and Airspace of Pingtan and Matsu Areas
    We Issue the 29th Serious Warning
    Xinhua News Agency, 12th – On October 12th, two US warships and two fighter planes intruded into the territorial waters and airspace of the Pingtan and Matsu areas of Fujian Province. Regarding this military provocation by the United States, the spokesman of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 29th serious warning.

### 美舰美机侵犯我平潭马祖地区领海领空  我第29次提出严重警告

1958-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

    美舰美机侵犯我平潭马祖地区领海领空
    我第29次提出严重警告
    新华社12日讯　10月12日有美国军舰两艘和战斗机两架侵入我福建省平潭、马祖地区的领海和领海上空。对于美国的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命第二十九次提出严重警告。
