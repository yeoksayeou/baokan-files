Title: Japanese General Council of Trade Unions Delegation Issues Statement in Beijing: Firmly Supports China's Counterattack Against American Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1958-10-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd — The Japanese General Council of Trade Unions delegation held a press conference in Beijing this morning, issuing a statement on the attitude and action plan adopted by the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan regarding the United States' military actions in the Taiwan Strait area.

The statement said that at the third meeting of the Executive Council on September 30, the General Council unanimously passed the following resolution on further and vigorously promoting the movement for friendship and restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and China: The General Council expresses its serious protest against the new aggressive military actions taken by the American warmongers in the Taiwan Strait area and the American occupation of Taiwan, which has always been Chinese territory, and its continued interference in China's internal affairs. It demands that the U.S. military immediately withdraw from Taiwan and the Taiwan Strait area, and also withdraw from Okinawa and mainland Japan. It demands that the United Nations General Assembly restore the legitimate seat of the People's Republic of China in the United Nations. It demands that the Japanese government immediately abolish the Japan-Chiang treaty and immediately cease its hostile policy towards China. At the same time, it strongly protests against Foreign Minister Fujiyama's attitude in the United Nations. The statement said that the General Council has called on Asian and African trade union organizations to issue a joint demand that the United States immediately cease its military intervention in China and immediately withdraw its troops, and to immediately launch joint actions to expedite the withdrawal of U.S. troops from the Asian and African regions. The General Council also wholeheartedly supports and endorses the Chinese people's resolute counterattack against American interference in internal affairs, and expresses its respect to all peace-loving people.

The statement concluded by saying that the General Council has instructed local trade union councils and regional councils throughout the country to conduct street activities between October 1st and 8th, in order to achieve greater results in the national movement for Sino-Japanese friendship, restoration of Sino-Japanese diplomatic relations, and the world peace movement. In order to promote this national movement, the Socialist Party, the Communist Party, the General Council, the Japan-China Friendship Association, the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations, and various peace organizations have decided to form a committee to guide the development of the movement.

### 日本总评代表团在北京发表声明  坚决支持中国回击美国侵略者

1958-10-05
第3版()
专栏：

    日本总评代表团在北京发表声明
    坚决支持中国回击美国侵略者
    新华社3日讯　日本总评代表团今天上午在北京举行记者招待会，就日本工会总评议会对美国在台湾海峡地区的军事行动所采取的态度和行动方针发表声明。
    声明说，总评在9月30日第三次干事会上，就进一步强有力地开展日中两国友好和恢复邦交的运动，全体一致通过如下决定：总评对美国战争挑拨者在台湾海峡地区所采取的新的侵略性的军事行动和美国霸占从来就是中国领土的台湾，继续干涉中国内政，提出严重抗议。要求美军立即从台湾和台湾海峡地区撤退，并且从冲绳和日本本土撤退。要求联合国大会恢复中华人民共和国在联合国的合法席位。要求日本政府立即废除日蒋条约，立即停止敌视中国的政策。同时，对藤山外相在联合国的态度提出强烈抗议。声明说，总评已呼吁亚非工会组织发出把要求美国立即停止对中国的军事干涉和立即撤退美军作为共同要求，为了使美军从速撤出亚非地区而立即掀起联合行动。总评并且衷心地支持和拥护中国人民对美国干涉内政的坚决回击，同时对一切爱好和平的人民表示敬意。
    声明最后说，总评已指示全国各地方工会评议会及地区工会在10月1日至8日之间，到街头进行活动，以便使日中友好、恢复日中邦交的国民运动及世界和平运动收到更大的效果。为了推进这一国民运动，社会党、共产党、总评、日中友好协会、恢复日中邦交国民会议及各和平团体等已决定组成委员会，以指导运动的发展。
