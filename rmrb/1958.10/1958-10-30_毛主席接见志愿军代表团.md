Title: Chairman Mao Receives Delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers
Author:
Date: 1958-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Chairman Mao Zedong and Vice Chairmen of the CPC Central Committee Zhou Enlai and Zhu De, Political Bureau members Peng Zhen, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, and Alternate Political Bureau member Bo Yibo, this afternoon at Huairen Hall in Zhongnanhai received the entire delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers, headed by General Yang Yong and General Wang Ping, and the art troupe of the Chinese People's Volunteers.

Present at the reception were Wang Jiaxiang, Huang Kecheng, Li Xuefeng, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Hu Qiaomu, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat, and Generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Yang Chengwu, Zhang Zongxun, Xiao Hua, Gan Siqi, Air Force General Liu Yalou, Navy General Wang Hongkun, General Chen Xilian, General Li Jukui, and Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian.

Also present at the reception were Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea, and Vice Chairman Liao Chengzhi. (Attached pictures)

### 毛主席接见志愿军代表团

1958-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见志愿军代表团
    新华社29日讯　毛泽东主席和中共中央副主席周恩来、朱德，政治局委员彭真、陈毅、李富春，政治局候补委员薄一波，今天下午在中南海怀仁堂接见了以杨勇上将、王平上将为首的中国人民志愿军代表团和志愿军文艺工作团的全体人员。
    接见时在座的，有中共中央书记处书记王稼祥、黄克诚、李雪峰、书记处候补书记胡乔木，中国人民解放军杨成武上将、张宗逊上将、萧华上将、甘泗淇上将、刘亚楼空军上将、王宏坤海军上将、陈锡联上将、李聚奎上将和刘志坚中将等。
    接见时，中国人民抗美援朝总会主席郭沫若和副主席廖承志也在座。（附图片）
