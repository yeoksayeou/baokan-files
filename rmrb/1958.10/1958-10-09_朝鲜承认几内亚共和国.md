Title: Korea Recognizes the Republic of Guinea
Author:
Date: 1958-10-09
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 8th. Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, sent a telegram to Sekou Toure, Prime Minister of the Republic of Guinea, today, warmly congratulating the birth of the Republic of Guinea, informing him that the Democratic People's Republic of Korea has decided to recognize the Republic, and expressing his full agreement with Sekou Toure's proposal to establish diplomatic relations between Korea and Guinea.
    The telegram said that the establishment of the Republic of Guinea is a brilliant victory achieved by the people of Guinea in their long and arduous struggle against colonialism and for freedom and national independence, and it is also a great encouragement to the struggle of all African people for liberation. ### 朝鲜承认几内亚共和国

1958-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜承认几内亚共和国
    新华社平壤8日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成今天打电报给几内亚共和国总理塞古·杜尔，热烈祝贺几内亚共和国的诞生，通知他朝鲜民主主义人民共和国决定承认这个共和国，并且表示完全同意塞古·杜尔关于建立朝鲜、几内亚两国间的外交关系的建议。
    电报说，几内亚共和国的成立，是几内亚人民在反对殖民主义和为自由与民族独立的长期的艰苦斗争中所取得的辉煌胜利，这也是对全体非洲人民争取解放的斗争的巨大鼓舞。（附图片）
