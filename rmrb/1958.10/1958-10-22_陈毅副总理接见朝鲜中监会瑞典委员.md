Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Swedish Member of the Korean Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission
Author:
Date: 1958-10-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 21st - Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council received Brigadier General Redensvärd, the Swedish member of the Korean Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, at Zhongnanhai at 4:00 pm today.

　　Present at the meeting was Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense.

　　On the afternoon of the 20th, General Huang Kecheng, Deputy Minister of National Defense and Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, also received Brigadier General Redensvärd and held a luncheon.

### 陈毅副总理接见朝鲜中监会瑞典委员

1958-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见朝鲜中监会瑞典委员
    新华社21日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天下午四时在中南海接见了朝鲜中立国监察委员会瑞典委员雷德思维准将。
    接见时在座的有国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将。
    20日下午，国防部副部长、中国人民解放军总参谋长黄克诚大将也接见了雷德思维准将，并举行了便宴。
