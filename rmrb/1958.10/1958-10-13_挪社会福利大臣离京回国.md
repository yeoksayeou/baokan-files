Title: Norwegian Minister of Social Welfare Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1958-10-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 12th -- Norwegian Minister of Social Welfare, Mr. Harlem, concluded his visit to China and left Beijing by plane this morning to return home. Minister Harlem arrived in Beijing on October 8th at the invitation of the Chinese Ministry of Health.
    On the afternoon of the 9th, Minister Harlem paid courtesy calls on Minister of Internal Affairs Xie Juezai and Vice Minister of Health Qian Xinzhong.
    During his stay in Beijing, Minister Harlem also visited factories, rural areas, and hospitals.

### 挪社会福利大臣离京回国

1958-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    挪社会福利大臣离京回国
    据新华社12日讯　挪威社会福利大臣哈勒姆先生结束在我国的访问后，已于今天早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。哈勒姆大臣是应我卫生部邀请在本月8日到达北京的。
    哈勒姆大臣在9日下午分别拜会了我内务部部长谢觉哉和卫生部副部长钱信忠。
    哈勒姆大臣在北京期间还曾参观访问了工厂、农村和医院等。
