Title: PLA Track and Field, Swimming, and Handball Games Open; Xu Wangcai Creates New National Record in Men's 100-meter Sidestroke
Author:
Date: 1958-10-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 14th: The Chinese People's Liberation Army's 1958 Track and Field, Swimming, and Handball Games opened here on the 12th. This is a major review of the PLA's mass sports movement. More than half of the six hundred and twenty-one athletes participating in this conference are ordinary soldiers. They include heroic artillerymen from the Fujian front, and representatives of troops from the border areas of the motherland, including the Kangzang Plateau, the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, and the Inner Mongolian grasslands. Many of them are activists in their units and soldiers who have repeatedly performed meritorious service for the people.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 14th: Xu Wangcai, a soldier of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy, broke the national record for the men's 100-meter sidestroke with a time of one minute and ten point five seconds in the preliminary round of the Chinese People's Liberation Army 1958 Track and Field, Swimming, and Handball Games on the morning of the 13th.

### 解放军田径游泳手球运动会开幕  徐旺彩创男子百公尺侧泳全国新成绩

1958-10-15
第6版()
专栏：

    解放军田径游泳手球运动会开幕
    徐旺彩创男子百公尺侧泳全国新成绩
    据新华社广州14日电　中国人民解放军1958年田径、游泳、手球运动大会12日在这里开幕。这是一次检阅全军群众性体育运动的大会。参加这次大会的六百二十一名运动员中半数以上是普通战士。他们有福建前线的英勇炮手，有来自祖国边陲康藏高原、新疆维吾尔自治区和内蒙古草原的部队代表。他们当中许多是部队的积极分子和多次为人民立功的战士。
    新华社广州14日电　中国人民解放军海军战士徐旺彩，13日上午在中国人民解放军1958年田径、游泳、手球运动大会上的预赛中，以一分十秒五的成绩打破了男子一百公尺侧泳全国纪录。
