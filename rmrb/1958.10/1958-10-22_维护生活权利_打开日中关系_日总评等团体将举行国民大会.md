Title: Safeguard the Right to Life, Open Sino-Japanese Relations: General Council of Trade Unions of Japan and Other Organizations to Hold National Convention
Author:
Date: 1958-10-22
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 21st Dispatch: The General Council of Trade Unions of Japan and twenty-three other organizations will jointly hold a "Safeguard the Right to Life and Education, Open Sino-Japanese Relations" National Convention in Tokyo on the 28th. It is estimated that approximately 100,000 workers, youth, women, students, and citizens will participate. A large-scale demonstration parade will also be held after the meeting.

According to today's report in the "Akahata" newspaper, the executive committee of the convention has decided that, in addition to the two central slogans of "Safeguard the Right to Life and Education" and "Open Sino-Japanese Relations," the central slogan of "Crush the Revised Police Officer Duties Execution Law, which is a revival of the police state" will be added.

Representatives from various parts of Japan participating in this National Convention are departing by train on three routes, gathering representatives from more than twenty major cities along the way to Tokyo.

Among them, twenty-five representatives from Hokkaido departed from Sapporo on the 20th. They will participate in local prefectural conventions when passing through Morioka, Sendai, Fukushima, and other places, and will form the northern route delegation with representatives from various regions. Twelve representatives from Fukuoka in the western route delegation departed from Hakata on the 20th, and representatives from Kagoshima, Kumamoto, and Saga prefectures also joined the western route delegation. In addition, a central route delegation will depart from Kanazawa on the 22nd.

### 维护生活权利　打开日中关系  日总评等团体将举行国民大会

1958-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    维护生活权利　打开日中关系
    日总评等团体将举行国民大会
    新华社21日讯　东京消息：日本工会总评议会等二十四个团体订于28日在东京联合举行“维护生活权利和教育、打开日中关系”国民大会。预计大约有十万名工人、青年、妇女、学生和市民参加。会后还将举行声势浩大的示威游行。
    据“赤旗报”今天报道，大会实行委员会已经决定，除了“维护生活权利和教育”、“打开日中关系”两项中心口号之外，将加上“粉碎恢复警察国家的警察官职务执行法修正案”的中心口号。
    参加这个国民大会的日本各地代表分三路坐火车出发，汇集沿途二十多个主要城市的代表前往东京。
    其中，北海道代表二十五名已经于20日从札幌启程，他们在路过盛冈、仙台、福岛等地时将参加当地的县民大会，并汇合各地代表形成北路代表团。西路代表团的福冈代表十二名，已经在20日从博多启程，鹿儿岛、熊本和佐贺县的代表也参加了西路代表团。此外，还有中路代表团将在22日从金泽启程。
