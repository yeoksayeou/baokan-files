Title: Summing Up the Experience of the High-Yield Week, Anshan Iron and Steel Decides to Organize High Yields Every Day
Author:
Date: 1958-10-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, 23rd. Anshan Iron and Steel, based on its experience in organizing high yields in the past week, has decided to organize high yields every day from now on to ensure a greater leap forward in production in the second half of the month.

During the week from the 15th to the 21st, Anshan Iron and Steel, due to organizing high yields every day, increased its average daily steel output by 1,135 tons compared to the average daily output from the 1st to the 14th, 1,784 tons compared to the average daily output in September, and 3,193 tons compared to the average daily output in August. The output of these seven days has been basically stabilized at the level of the daily output record created by Anshan Iron and Steel on September 25, and two of those days even greatly exceeded this level.

The Anshan Municipal Party Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Anshan Iron and Steel Party Committee summarized the experience of organizing high yields in the past week on the 21st, believing that in order to continuously increase steel production, it is necessary to further liberate thinking, deeply mobilize the masses, and organize high yields every day; to greatly improve the level of iron and steel production.

### 总结高产周的经验  鞍钢决定天天组织高产

1958-10-24
第2版()
专栏：

    总结高产周的经验
    鞍钢决定天天组织高产
    新华社鞍山23日电　鞍钢根据最近一周来组织高产的经验，决定从现在起天天组织高产，保证下旬生产有一个更大的跃进。
    在15日到21日的一周内，鞍钢由于天天组织高产，每天的平均钢产量比1日到14日的平均日产量增长一千一百三十五吨，比9月份平均日产量增长一千七百八十四吨，比8月份平均日产量增长三千一百九十三吨。这七天的产量，已经基本稳定在鞍钢9月25日创造的日产纪录的水平上，其中有两天还大大超过了这个水平。
    中共鞍山市委和鞍钢党委在21日总结了最近一周组织高产的经验，认为要使钢产量不断提高，必须进一步解放思想，深入发动群众，天天组织高产；把钢铁生产水平再大大地提高一步。
