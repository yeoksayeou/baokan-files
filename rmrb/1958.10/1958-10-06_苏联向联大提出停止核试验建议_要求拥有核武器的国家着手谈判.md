Title: The Soviet Union Proposed to the UN General Assembly a Proposal to Cease Nuclear Testing, Demanding that Countries Possessing Nuclear Weapons Begin Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1958-10-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 5th of October. TASS, New York, 5th of October: The Soviet delegation submitted a draft resolution entitled "On the Cessation of Testing of Atomic and Hydrogen Weapons" to the United Nations General Assembly for consideration on October 4, 1958.

The draft resolution states:

The General Assembly,

Guided by the desire to spare mankind from the dangerous consequences of atomic radiation resulting from the experimental explosions of atomic and hydrogen weapons, believes that the cessation of nuclear tests would be the first important step towards a complete ban on atomic and hydrogen weapons and would seriously impede the manufacture of new, more destructive weapons of mass destruction, noting the deep concern felt by the peoples of the world about the continued testing of nuclear weapons, calls upon all countries conducting atomic and hydrogen weapon tests to cease these tests without delay, recommends that countries possessing nuclear weapons begin negotiations to conclude appropriate agreements between them, and calls upon all countries to participate in this agreement.

### 苏联向联大提出停止核试验建议  要求拥有核武器的国家着手谈判

1958-10-06
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联向联大提出停止核试验建议
    要求拥有核武器的国家着手谈判
    新华社5日讯　塔斯社纽约5日讯：苏联代表团在1958年10月4日把一项“关于停止试验原子武器和氢武器”的决议草案提交联合国大会审议。
    决议草案说：
    大会，
    本着使人类免除由于原子武器和氢武器试验性爆炸所产生的原子辐射的危险后果的愿望，认为停止核试验将是走向完全禁止原子武器和氢武器的第一个重要步骤，并将使制造新的破坏力更大的大规模毁灭性武器受到严重障碍，注意到世界各国人民对继续试验核武器所感到的深刻不安，呼吁所有进行原子武器和氢武器试验的国家毫不拖延地停止这些试验，建议拥有核武器的国家着手举行谈判，以便在它们之间缔结相应的协定，呼吁所有国家都参加这个协定。
