Title: Ye Gongchao Exposes American Propaganda Smoke Screen, Shouting that the Chiang Kai-shek Group Will Not Give Up Using Force
Author:
Date: 1958-10-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th, Taipei Dispatch: Just as the American propaganda machine is vigorously publicizing that Dulles has forced Chiang Kai-shek to "abandon the use of force to attack the mainland," Ye Gongchao, the Chiang Kai-shek group's "ambassador" to the United States, denied today that the Chiang Kai-shek group intends to "give up the use of force to return to the mainland."
    According to a report by United Press International, Ye Gongchao told reporters at Taipei Airport today before transiting through Tokyo to Washington that "it is absolutely wrong to say that we (the Chiang Kai-shek group) have given up the use of force to liberate our compatriots on the mainland."
    Ye Gongchao made these remarks when talking about the US-Chiang talks communique. He said that the US-Chiang talks communique only "guarantees" that the Chiang Kai-shek group will "avoid using force as the main method of returning to the mainland."

### 叶公超戳穿美国宣传烟幕  叫嚷蒋介石集团不放弃使用武力

1958-10-28
第4版()
专栏：

    叶公超戳穿美国宣传烟幕
    叫嚷蒋介石集团不放弃使用武力
    新华社27日讯　台北消息：正当美国宣传机器大肆宣传杜勒斯已经迫使蒋介石“放弃以武力进攻大陆”时，台湾蒋介石集团驻美国“大使”叶公超今天否认蒋介石集团打算“放弃使用武力重返大陆”。
    据合众国际社报道，叶公超今天在取道东京前往华盛顿前，在台北飞机场上对新闻记者说，“说我们（台湾蒋介石集团）放弃使用武力来解放我们大陆上的同胞，这是绝对错误的”。
    叶公超是在谈到美蒋会谈公报的时候说这番话的。他说，美蒋会谈公报只“保证”蒋介石集团“避免使用武力作为重返大陆的主要方法”。
