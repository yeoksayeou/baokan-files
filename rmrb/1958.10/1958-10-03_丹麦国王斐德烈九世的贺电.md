Title: Telegram of Congratulations from King Frederick IX of Denmark
Author:
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

To His Excellency Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of the National Day of the People's Republic of China, please accept my sincere congratulations and best wishes for your personal happiness and the prosperity of the Chinese people.

                                                  Frederick IX

### 丹麦国王斐德烈九世的贺电
斐德烈九世
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    丹麦国王斐德烈九世的贺电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东先生阁下：
    在中华人民共和国国庆的时候，我谨向阁下表达诚挚的祝贺和最良好的愿望，祝你个人幸福，祝中国人民昌盛。
                                                  斐德烈九世
