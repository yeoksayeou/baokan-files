Title: Albanian Foreign Minister Shtylla's Congratulatory Message
Author: Behar Shtylla
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

Albanian Foreign Minister Shtylla's Congratulatory Message
To Comrade Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of the ninth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, dear Comrade Chen Yi, please allow me to extend to you my warmest congratulations and sincerely wish you new achievements in your fruitful work for the prosperity of the People's Republic of China, the strengthening of the fraternal friendship and close cooperation between the fraternal peoples of our two countries, and for our great cause of socialism and peace.

                        Behar Shtylla
                        Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Albania

### 阿尔巴尼亚外交部长什图拉的贺电
贝哈尔·什图拉
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚外交部长什图拉的贺电中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅同志：
    当中华人民共和国成立九周年的时候，亲爱的陈毅同志，请允许我向你致以最热烈的祝贺，并衷心地祝你在为中华人民共和国的强盛，加强我们两国兄弟人民间的兄弟友谊和紧密合作，为我们伟大的社会主义和和平事业而进行的富有成效工作中获得新的成就。
                        阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国外交部长
                                              贝哈尔·什图拉
