Title: The U.S. Intensifies Arming of the Chiang Kai-shek Troops

Author:

Date: 1958-10-22
Page: 4

The U.S. Intensifies Arming of the Chiang Kai-shek Troops
    The American aggressors have recently intensified arming the Chiang Kai-shek troops, attempting to continue military provocations and expand their aggression against China. They will transfer the missile equipment of the "Nike-Hercules" missile battalion currently stationed in Taiwan to the Chiang Kai-shek clique, encouraging the Chiang Kai-shek clique to continue the civil war. (Attached pictures)
    The vehicle is loaded with missile fuel, napalm.
    U.S. Air Force personnel in Taipei are unloading the delivered "Nike-Hercules" missiles.
    U.S. Air Force personnel are installing "Sidewinder" missiles at an airport in Taiwan.
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch

### 美国加紧装备蒋军

1958-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

    美国加紧装备蒋军
    美国侵略者最近加紧武装蒋军，企图继续进行军事挑衅和扩大对中国的侵略活动，将把目前驻在台湾的“奈克—赫尔克里士”式导弹营的导弹设备交给蒋介石集团，鼓励蒋介石集团继续进行内战。（附图片）
    汽车上装的是导弹的燃料，凝固汽油。
    在台北的美国空军人员正在卸下运到的“奈克—赫尔克里士”式导弹。
    美国空军人员在台湾机场上安装“响尾蛇”式导弹。
    新华社稿
