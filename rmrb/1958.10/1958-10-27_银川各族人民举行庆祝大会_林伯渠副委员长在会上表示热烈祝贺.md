Title: People of All Ethnic Groups in Yinchuan Hold Celebration Rally; Vice Chairman Lin Boqu Expresses Warm Congratulations at the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1958-10-27
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Yinchuan, October 26th: Today, Yinchuan City was adorned everywhere with colorful flags and banners, a riot of color. On the streets, drums and gongs resounded, and crowds of happy people in festive attire thronged everywhere.

This afternoon, in the verdant Zhongshan Park, a celebration rally was held to celebrate the establishment of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, attended by more than 20,000 people. A huge portrait of the beloved great leader Chairman Mao Zedong, revered by all ethnic groups, hung on the rostrum. Lin Boqu, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, attended the rally and was greeted by people waving bouquets on both sides of the road. When he appeared on the rostrum, the venue erupted in thunderous applause and cheers. Xie Fumin, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress, Yang Jingren, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China, and representatives from various provinces, regions, and municipalities who came to congratulate the establishment of the autonomous region also attended the celebration rally.

Wang Feng, First Secretary of the CPC Ningxia Working Committee, Liu Geping, Chairman of the People's Committee of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, Vice Chairmen, members, and representatives attending the first session of the first People's Congress of the Autonomous Region all attended the celebration rally. Members of the China Hui Nationality Culture Advancement Association also attended the celebration.

Wang Zhiqiang, First Secretary of the CPC Yinchuan Municipal Committee and Mayor of Yinchuan City, spoke at the meeting. He first extended a warm welcome and respect on behalf of the more than 100,000 people of Yinchuan City to Vice Chairman Lin Boqu and other guests. He said that the establishment of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region marks another glorious and great victory for the Party's ethnic policy and marks the further consolidation and development of the unity of all ethnic groups in the motherland. Now that the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region has been established, Yinchuan City is the capital of the autonomous region and one of the key points of industrial construction in the autonomous region. In the future, the people of the city will be more confident, exert greater revolutionary vigor, and carry out various constructions.

Vice Chairman Lin Boqu spoke at the meeting. He extended warm congratulations to the people of all ethnic groups in the autonomous region. He said: We are all here to rejoice and celebrate the birth of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region. This is a major achievement in our country's implementation of ethnic regional autonomy and another glorious victory for the Chinese Communist Party's ethnic policy. He said: "The Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region was established under the cordial care and leadership of the Party and Chairman Mao. We firmly believe that after the establishment of the autonomous region, the people of all regions and ethnic groups in Ningxia will unite more closely around the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, resolutely support the leadership of the Party, and always advance in the direction pointed out by the Party."

Tonight, more than 10,000 people in Yinchuan City held a grand garden party and get-together in Zhongshan Park. The Central Nationalities Song and Dance Troupe, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Acrobatic Troupe, and the Lanzhou Qin Opera Troupe, who came to Ningxia to perform in celebration, all performed many wonderful programs.

Beautiful and brilliant fireworks appeared in the night sky over Yinchuan. People of all ethnic groups sang and danced, happily celebrating the festive night.

### 银川各族人民举行庆祝大会  林伯渠副委员长在会上表示热烈祝贺

1958-10-27
第2版()
专栏：

    银川各族人民举行庆祝大会
    林伯渠副委员长在会上表示热烈祝贺
    新华社银川26日电　银川市今天到处都被彩旗和布幅标语装饰得万紫千红。街头上，锣鼓喧天，到处拥塞着穿着节日新装的欢乐的人群。
    下午，在绿树成荫的中山公园，举行了庆祝宁夏回族自治区成立的大会，参加庆祝的有二万多人。主席台上挂着各族人民敬爱的伟大领袖毛泽东同志的巨幅画像。中共中央政治局委员、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠参加了大会，人们挥舞着花束夹道欢迎。当他出现在主席台上时，会场响起了暴风雨般的掌声和欢呼声。全国人民代表大会民族委员会副主任委员谢扶民、中华人民共和国民族事务委员会副主任杨静仁和前来祝贺自治区成立的各省、区、市的代表也参加了庆祝大会。
    中共宁夏工委第一书记汪锋、宁夏回族自治区人民委员会主席刘格平和副主席、委员以及出席自治区第一届人民代表大会第一次会议的代表，都参加了庆祝大会。参加庆祝会的还有中国回民文化协进会的委员。
    中共银川市委第一书记、银川市市长王志强在会上讲了话。他首先代表银川市十多万人民对林伯渠副委员长和其他来宾表示热烈的欢迎和敬意。他说，宁夏回族自治区的成立，标志着党的民族政策又一次光辉伟大的胜利，标志着祖国各民族的团结更加巩固与发展。宁夏回族自治区成立了，银川市是自治区的首府，又是自治区工业建设的重点之一，今后全市人民将更加信心充沛，鼓足更大的革命干劲，进行各项建设。
    林伯渠副委员长在会上讲了话。他向自治区各族人民表示热烈的祝贺。他说：我们大家来欢欣鼓舞地庆祝宁夏回族自治区的诞生。这是我国推行民族区域自治的一个重大成就，也是中国共产党的民族政策的又一次光辉胜利。他说：“宁夏回族自治区是在党和毛主席的亲切关怀与领导下成立的。我们深信，自治区成立以后，宁夏各地区各民族人民一定会更加紧密地团结在党中央和毛主席的周围，坚决地拥护党的领导，永远向着党所指引的方向前进。
    今晚，银川市有一万多人在中山公园举行了盛大的游园联欢晚会。前来宁夏作庆贺演出的中央民族歌舞团、内蒙古自治区杂技团和兰州秦剧团都表演许多精彩节目。
    银川的夜空，出现了美丽灿烂的焰火，各族人民载歌载舞，欢度节日的夜晚。
