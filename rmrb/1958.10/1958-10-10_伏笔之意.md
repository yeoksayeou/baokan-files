    The Meaning of Foreshadowing
    The United States has announced that it will withdraw its troops from Lebanon by the end of this month "unless unforeseen circumstances arise."
    The United States has been trying its best to interfere in Lebanon's internal affairs and incite disturbances recently, obviously to create such "unforeseen circumstances."

### 伏笔之意

1958-10-10
第5版()
专栏：编完写起

    伏笔之意
    美国宣布“除非出现无法预料的局势”，将在本月底以前从黎巴嫩撤军。
    美国近来在黎巴嫩竭力干涉内政、煽动骚动，显然正是在制造这种“无法预料的局势”也。
