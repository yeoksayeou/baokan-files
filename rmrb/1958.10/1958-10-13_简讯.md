    Brief News

    Soviet Union Publishes "Achievements of Ancient Chinese Medicine"
    Moscow dispatch: The Moscow National Medical Book Publishing Bureau has published a book titled "Achievements of Ancient Chinese Medicine" by Zhu Yan. Professor Polderev, who lived in China for several years, wrote the preface for the Russian version of this book.
    American Small Lunar Satellite Goes Off Course
    Washington dispatch: The United States launched a small lunar satellite called "Pioneer" on the 11th, using a device pieced together from three different rockets. It deviated from its intended orbit shortly after launch.
    The largest first stage of the three-stage rocket is a "Thor" intermediate-range missile. The lunar satellite is on top of the third stage, which is thirty-five inches high and twenty-nine inches in diameter, weighing eighty-five pounds. The detection equipment it carries weighs only twenty-five pounds.
    United States Conducts Another Nuclear Weapon Test
    Washington dispatch: The U.S. government conducted another nuclear weapon test at the Nevada Test Site on the 10th. This is the fourth in a series of nuclear tests currently being conducted in Nevada.
    The message said that another nuclear weapon test will be conducted on the 12th. U.S. Warship "Visits" Fourteen African Countries
    Washington dispatch: The U.S. Navy Department announced that the warship "Bayfield" of the U.S. South Atlantic Naval Command left Port of Spain, Trinidad, on the 10th for so-called "visits" to fourteen African countries.
    The message said that the "Bayfield" warship is the newest and largest destroyer in the U.S. fleet.
                  (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 简讯

第5版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    苏联出版“中国古代医学的成就”
    莫斯科讯：莫斯科国家医学书籍出版局出版了朱颜著的“中国古代医学的成就”一书。在中国住过几年的波尔德雷夫教授为这本书的俄文版写了序言。
    美国小月球卫星出轨
    华盛顿消息：美国11日用三种不同的火箭拼凑起来的装置发射了一个小小的名为“先驱者”的月球卫星，上天不久即偏离预定轨道。
    三级火箭的最大的第一级是一个“雷神”式中程导弹，月球卫星就在第三节顶上，它的高度是三十五英寸，直径是二十九英寸，重八十五磅，它携带的探测仪器只有二十五磅重。
    美国又进行核武器试验
    华盛顿消息：美国政府10日又在内华达州核武器试验场进行了一次核武器试验。这是目前在内华达州进行的一系列核试验的第四次。
    消息说，12日将进行另一核武器试验。美舰“访问”十四个非洲国家
    华盛顿消息：美国海军部宣布，美国南大西洋海军司令部的“拜吉娄号”军舰10日离开西班牙的特立尼达港前往十四个非洲国家进行所谓“访问”。
    消息说，“拜吉娄号”军舰是美国舰队中最新式和最大的驱逐舰。
                  （据新华社讯）
