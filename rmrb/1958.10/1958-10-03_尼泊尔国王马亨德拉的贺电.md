Title: King Mahendra of Nepal's Congratulatory Message
Author:
Date: 1958-10-03
Page: 3

To His Excellency Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of the ninth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, we would like to express our warm congratulations on behalf of the government and people of Nepal and in our own name, and sincerely wish your country and your people continued prosperity and happiness. We firmly believe that the growing friendly relations between our two countries will contribute to peace in Asia and the world.
                                    Mahendra
                                    King of Nepal

### 尼泊尔国王马亨德拉的贺电
马亨德拉
1958-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔国王马亨德拉的贺电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东先生阁下：
    值此中华人民共和国建国九周年之际，我们谨代表尼泊尔政府和人民并以我们自己的名义表示我们的热情的祝贺，并衷心地祝贵国、贵国人民不断繁荣幸福。我们坚信，我们两国之间的日益加强的友好关系将对亚洲和世界和平作出贡献。
                                    尼泊尔国王　马亨德拉
