Title: U.S. Steps Up Nuclear Testing on Eve of Geneva Talks
Author:
Date: 1958-10-17
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 16th: On the eve of the talks on the cessation of nuclear weapons tests, which are scheduled to begin in Geneva on October 31st, the United States is stepping up its nuclear weapons testing program. According to an announcement by the U.S. Atomic Energy Commission, the United States conducted another nuclear weapons test at the Nevada Test Site on the 15th. The explosive device was mounted on a fifty-foot high tower. On the same day as this explosion, an underground explosion test was also conducted. This is said to be one of the largest of the newly designed tests by the United States.
    The U.S. Atomic Energy Commission announced that the United States will continue to conduct a series of such explosion tests before October 31st.

### 在日内瓦谈判前夕  美国加紧进行核试验

1958-10-17
第5版()
专栏：

    在日内瓦谈判前夕
    美国加紧进行核试验
    新华社16日讯　华盛顿消息：在10月31日开始在日内瓦举行停止核武器试验的谈判之前，美国正在加紧进行核武器试验。据美国政府原子能委员会宣布，15日美国在内华达州原子试验基地又进行了一次核武器试验。爆炸物是装置在五十英尺高的塔上。在这次爆炸的同一天，还要进行一次地下装置的爆炸试验。据说这是美国新设计的最大的几次这种试验中的一次。
    美国原子能委员会宣布，在10月31日之前，美国还要继续进行一系列这种爆炸试验。
