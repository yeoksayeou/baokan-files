Title: Reception Held by Indonesian Acting Military Attaché to Celebrate Indonesian Armed Forces Day
Author:
Date: 1958-10-06
Page: 4

    Celebrating Indonesian Armed Forces Day
    Reception Held by Indonesian Acting Military Attaché
    According to Xinhua News Agency on the 5th, Major Ratumahsina, Acting Military Attaché of the Indonesian Embassy in China, and his wife, held a reception this evening to celebrate the 13th anniversary of the founding of the Indonesian Armed Forces. Senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including General Zhang Zongxun, General Chen Xilian, General Guo Tianmin, Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, Lieutenant General Li Xuesan, Lieutenant General Yuan Ziqin, and Vice Admiral Fang Qiang, were invited to attend the reception. Also attending the reception were the Indonesian Ambassador to China, Sukardjo Wirjopranoto, and diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries to China.
    Major Ratumahsina and General Zhang Zongxun spoke at the reception successively, unanimously praising the Indonesian Armed Forces for their achievements in resisting aggression and safeguarding national unity and independence, and praising the friendship between the people and armies of China and Indonesia.

### 庆祝印度尼西亚建军节  驻华代理武官举行招待会

1958-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝印度尼西亚建军节
    驻华代理武官举行招待会
    据新华社5日讯　印度尼西亚驻华代理武官拉图玛希纳少校及夫人今晚举行招待会，庆祝印度尼西亚共和国武装部队建军十三周年。中国人民解放军高级将领张宗逊上将，陈锡联上将，郭天民上将，刘志坚中将，李雪三中将，袁子钦中将，方强海军中将等，应邀出席了招待会。参加招待会的，还有印度尼西亚驻华大使苏卡佐·维约普拉诺托和各国驻华使节及武官。
    拉图玛希纳少校和张宗逊上将先后在招待会上讲了话，他们一致赞扬印度尼西亚武装部队在反抗侵略和维护国家的统一和独立的斗争中的功迹，赞扬中国和印度尼西亚两国人民及其军队之间的友谊。
