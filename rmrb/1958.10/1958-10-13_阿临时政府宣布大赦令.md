Title: Algerian Provisional Government Announces Amnesty Decree
Author:
Date: 1958-10-13
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, 11th – The Algerian Republican Cabinet, in order to celebrate the establishment of the Provisional Government of the Algerian Republic and the recognition of this government by some countries, has announced an amnesty decree. The amnesty decree includes the pardon of those currently imprisoned by the National Liberation Front and the National Liberation Army, with the exception of those who have been tried or sentenced for treason or collusion with the enemy. In addition, some French soldiers captured by the National Liberation Army will be released unconditionally and sent home through representatives of the International Committee of the Red Cross in Geneva.

### 阿临时政府宣布大赦令

1958-10-13
第5版()
专栏：

    阿临时政府宣布大赦令
    新华社11日讯　突尼斯消息：阿尔及利亚共和国内阁为了庆祝阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府的成立和一些国家承认这个政府，宣布大赦令。大赦令中予以赦免的包括，现在正在为民族解放阵线和民族解放军监禁的人，但是由于叛国或勾结敌人而被审讯或判刑者例外。此外，关于一些被民族解放军俘虏的法国兵也将无条件予以释放，并且通过日内瓦红十字国际委员会的代表使他们回家。
