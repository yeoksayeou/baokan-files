Title: Foreign Envoys to China Congratulate China's National Day
Author:
Date: 1958-10-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd - As the Chinese people joyously celebrate National Day, foreign envoys to China sent letters to Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, congratulating them on China's National Day.

The envoys who wrote to Chairman Mao Zedong included: Antonov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy; Li Yong-ho, Ambassador of North Korea; Rubsang, Ambassador of Mongolia; Balili, Ambassador of Albania; Parthasarathi, Ambassador of India; Ula Maung, Ambassador of Burma; and Rahman, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Pakistani Embassy.

The envoy who wrote to Chairman Liu Shaoqi was Antonov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy.

The envoys who wrote to Premier Zhou Enlai included: Antonov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy; Li Yong-ho, Ambassador of North Korea; Rubsang, Ambassador of Mongolia; Parthasarathi, Ambassador of India; Thunell, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Swedish Embassy; Ula Maung, Ambassador of Burma; Benoît, Ambassador of Switzerland; Rahman, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Pakistani Embassy; Ragab, Ambassador of the United Arab Republic; and Goonewardene, Ambassador of Ceylon.

The envoys who wrote to Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi included: Antonov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy; Panchevski, Ambassador of Bulgaria; Rudenko, Ambassador of Romania; Nogradi, Ambassador of Hungary; Li Yong-ho, Ambassador of North Korea; Vasec, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Czechoslovak Embassy; Fralato, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy; Rubsang, Ambassador of Mongolia; Wang Daewder, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic; Nguyen Khang, Ambassador of Vietnam; Andersen, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Danish Embassy; Wijopranoto, Ambassador of Indonesia; Benoît, Ambassador of Switzerland; Sundström, Ambassador of Finland; Lydd, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Norwegian Embassy; Milicevic, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Yugoslav Embassy; Abdul Samad, Ambassador of Afghanistan; Julian Wathen, Chargé d'Affaires of the British Office; and Slingerbergh, Chargé d'Affaires of the Dutch Office.

Bohuslav Chňoupek, the Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, who is currently in New York attending the United Nations General Assembly, sent a telegram to Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi from New York on the 2nd, congratulating him on China's National Day. The former Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, and the former Ceylon Ambassador to China, Perera, also sent telegrams to Premier Zhou Enlai congratulating him on China's National Day.

### 各国驻华使节祝贺我国庆

1958-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    各国驻华使节祝贺我国庆
    新华社3日讯　在我国人民欢度国庆节的日子里，各国驻华使节分别写信给毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理、陈毅副总理兼外交部长，祝贺我国国庆节。
    写信给毛泽东主席的，有苏联大使馆临时代办安东诺夫、朝鲜大使李永镐、蒙古大使鲁布桑、阿尔巴尼亚大使巴利里、印度大使帕塔萨拉蒂、缅甸大使吴拉茂、巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办拉曼。
    写信给刘少奇委员长的有苏联大使馆临时代办安东诺夫。
    写信给周恩来总理的，有苏联大使馆临时代办安东诺夫、朝鲜大使李永镐、蒙古大使鲁布桑、印度大使帕塔萨拉蒂、瑞典大使馆临时代办图内尔、缅甸大使吴拉茂、瑞士大使贝努义、巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办拉曼、阿拉伯联合共和国大使拉加卜、锡兰大使高伯拉瓦。
    写信给陈毅副总理兼外交部长的，有苏联大使馆临时代办安东诺夫、保加利亚大使潘切夫斯基、罗马尼亚大使鲁登科、匈牙利大使诺格拉第、朝鲜大使李永镐、捷克斯洛伐克大使馆临时代办瓦塞克、波兰大使馆临时代办傅拉托、蒙古大使鲁布桑、德意志民主共和国大使汪戴尔、越南大使阮康、丹麦大使馆临时代办安徒生、印度尼西亚大使维约普拉诺托、瑞士大使贝努义、芬兰大使孙士敦、挪威大使馆临时代办李德、南斯拉夫大使馆临时代办米里切维奇、阿富汗大使阿卜杜勒·萨马德、英国代办处代办文郁生、荷兰代办处代办司灵博。
    目前正在纽约参加联合国大会的捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使布希尼亚克，2日从纽约打电报给陈毅副总理兼外交部长，祝贺我国国庆。印度前任驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁、锡兰前任驻华大使佩雷拉也致电周恩来总理祝贺我国国庆。
