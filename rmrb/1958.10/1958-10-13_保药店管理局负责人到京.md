Title: Head of Bulgarian Pharmaceutical Administration Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-10-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: Mr. Vasil Asenov Novatskov, Deputy Director of the Pharmaceutical Administration Bureau of the Bulgarian Ministry of Health and Social Welfare, arrived in Beijing on the 10th for a tour and visit to China according to the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. During his stay in China, he will investigate China's pharmaceutical administration, drug production, and supply.

### 保药店管理局负责人到京

1958-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    保药店管理局负责人到京
    新华社11日讯　按中保文化合作协定前来我国参观和访问的保加利亚卫生社会福利部药店管理局副局长瓦西里·阿森诺夫·诺瓦茨科夫已于10日到京。在华期间，他将考察我国药政管理、药品生产和供应等情况。
