Title: Burmese Ambassador to China U Hla Maung Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1958-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, and his wife departed Beijing on the 5th to return home. Seeing them off at the train station were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and his wife, Director of the Asian Department Zhang Wenjin, Director of the Protocol Department Yu Peiwen, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Zheng Zhenduo and his wife. Diplomats from various countries' embassies in China and the Burmese Embassy in China also went to see them off.

### 缅驻华大使吴拉茂返国

1958-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    缅驻华大使吴拉茂返国
    新华社5日讯　缅甸联邦驻华大使吴拉茂及夫人于5日离京返国。前往车站送行的有外交部副部长章汉夫及夫人，亚洲司司长章文晋，礼宾司司长俞沛文和中缅友好协会会长郑振铎及夫人。各国驻华使节和缅甸驻华大使馆的外交官也前往送行。
