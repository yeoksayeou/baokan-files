New Stamps
    The special stamps "Soviet Artificial Earth Satellite" have three designs. The first design features ancient Chinese astronomical instruments in the foreground, with the artificial satellite orbiting in the sky. The second design shows the third satellite orbiting the Earth. The third design shows the red satellite orbiting the entire world.

    新邮票
