Title: Chivu Stoica Cables Touré Agreeing to Establish Diplomatic Relations with Guinea
Author:
Date: 1958-10-22
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, October 21st. Romanian Chairman of the Council of Ministers Chivu Stoica telegraphed Guinean Republic Prime Minister Sékou Touré, expressing agreement to the establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries.

### 基伏·斯托伊卡电杜尔  同意与几内亚建交

1958-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    基伏·斯托伊卡电杜尔
    同意与几内亚建交
    据新华社布加勒斯特21日电罗马尼亚部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡打电报给几内亚共和国总理塞古·杜尔，表示同意两国建立外交关系。
