Title: People's Communes Have a Bright Future: Australian Union Leader Praises Our Country
Author:
Date: 1958-10-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 17th, Sydney News: George Neilly, secretary of the Australian Miners' Federation and Labor Party member of parliament, who recently returned from a visit to China, said that the Chinese people are "happy, proud and enthusiastic." He said that China will become the world's largest industrial country. He believes that the Australian government's non-recognition of China is "stupid and fails to face the truth."

Referring to the people's communes in China, Neilly said that he had visited a people's commune in China and saw that it was a step forward from agricultural cooperatives. He said that the people's communes have a bright future.

### 人民公社前途远大  澳工会领袖赞扬我国

1958-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

    人民公社前途远大
    澳工会领袖赞扬我国
    新华社17日讯　悉尼消息：刚从中国访问回国的澳大利亚矿工联合会书记、工党议员乔治·尼利说，中国人民是“愉快的、自豪的和热心的”。他说，中国将成为世界上最大的工业国。他认为，澳大利亚政府不承认中国是“愚蠢的，而且也没有正视真象”。
    尼利在谈到中国的人民公社时说，他在中国访问过一个人民公社，看到它比农业合作社更向前迈进了一步。他说，人民公社有远大的前途。
