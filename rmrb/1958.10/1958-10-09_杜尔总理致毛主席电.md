Title: Premier Touré's Telegram to Chairman Mao
Author: 
Date: 1958-10-09
Page: 1

To Chairman Mao of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

    I have the honor to inform you that the Guinean National Assembly declared the establishment of the Republic of Guinea on October 2, 1958. The Guinean government was formed on the same day. As an independent and sovereign state, Guinea is ready to establish diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of China on the basis of international cooperation.

                            Sékou Touré
                            Prime Minister of the Republic of Guinea
                                      Conakry, October 4, 1958

### 杜尔总理致毛主席电

1958-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    杜尔总理致毛主席电北京中华人民共和国主席：
    我荣幸地通知你，几内亚国民会议于1958年10月2日宣布了几内亚共和国成立。几内亚政府已于当天组成。作为独立和主权的国家，几内亚愿在国际合作的基础上同中华人民共和国建立外交关系。
                            几内亚共和国总理　塞古·杜尔
                                      1958年10月4日科纳克里
