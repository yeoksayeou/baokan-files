Title: Argentina's Four Million Worker Strike Ends
Author:
Date: 1958-10-14
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Buenos Aires, 13th: The 24-hour strike staged by Argentine workers on the 11th to protest high domestic prices has ended. The strike was initiated by a federation of sixty-two trade unions and was supported by employees belonging to another thirty-two trade unions. Student organizations also participated in the protest.
    This nationwide strike paralyzed Argentina's economic life. According to "The Times," more than four million working people participated in the strike.
    The general strikes of railway workers, chemical industry workers, and teachers in Buenos Aires Province are still continuing.

### 阿根廷四百万人罢工结束

1958-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

    阿根廷四百万人罢工结束
    新华社13日讯　布宜诺斯艾利斯讯：阿根廷工人在11日为抗议国内物价高涨而举行的二十四小时的罢工运动已经结束。这次罢工是由六十二个工会联合发起的，并且得到加入其他三十二个工会的职工的支持。学生组织也参加了这次抗议运动。
    这次波及全国的罢工瘫痪了阿根廷的经济生活。据“时报”报道，参加罢工的有四百多万劳动人民。
    铁路工人、化学工业工人和布宜诺斯艾利斯省教师的总罢工还在继续中。
