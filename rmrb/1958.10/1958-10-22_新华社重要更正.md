Date: 1958-10-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency Important Correction
    Regarding the dispatch "Defense Ministry Spokesman's Statement Refutes the U.S. Taiwan Defense Command's Denial of U.S. Naval Escort Statement" transmitted by this agency on the 21st (published on page one of this newspaper on the 21st), in the third paragraph, within the parentheses "(24 degrees 18 seconds north latitude, 118 degrees 26 seconds east longitude)," both instances of "seconds" should be "minutes."

### 新华社重要更正
