Title: Manila Vice Mayor Rosales Delivers Speech Demanding the Return of Coastal Islands to China
Author:
Date: 1958-10-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Manila, 3rd -- According to a Philippine News Agency report from Manila on the 2nd: Manila Vice Mayor Rosales delivered a speech at a Rotary Club meeting in Lao Niu Ban, north of Manila, on the 2nd, demanding the return of Jinmen, Mazu, and other coastal islands to China.

Rosales also said that the Philippines should recognize the government of the People's Republic of China. However, Rosales also advocated making Taiwan "independent" and having the "United Nations guarantee its sovereignty."

### 马尼拉副市长罗赛斯发表演说  要求把沿海岛屿交还中国

1958-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

    马尼拉副市长罗赛斯发表演说
    要求把沿海岛屿交还中国
    新华社3日讯　据菲律宾通讯社马尼拉2日消息：马尼拉副市长罗赛斯2日在马尼拉以北捞牛坂的扶轮社集会上发表演说，要求把金门、马祖和其它沿海岛屿交还给中国。
    罗赛斯还说，菲律宾应该承认中华人民共和国政府。但是，罗赛斯又主张使台湾“独立”而由“联合国来保证它的主权”。
