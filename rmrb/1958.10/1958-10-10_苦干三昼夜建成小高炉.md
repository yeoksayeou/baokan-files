Title: Small Blast Furnace Built in Three Days and Nights of Hard Work
Author:
Date: 1958-10-10
Page: 5

The first small blast furnace built in Vietnam, with a daily output of one to two tons and constructed with bricks, has already produced its first batch of molten iron on the 1st. This small blast furnace was proposed by a group of Chinese experts and technicians working in Hanoi and actively supported by the leaders of the Vietnamese Ministry of Industry. It was built after three days and nights of hard work, and its cost was only equivalent to just over 300 Chinese yuan.

### 苦干三昼夜建成小高炉

1958-10-10
第5版()
专栏：

    苦干三昼夜建成小高炉
    越南新建成的第一座日产一至二吨的用砖砌的小高炉已经在1日流出了第一炉铁水，这座小高炉是在河内工作的一批中国专家和技术员提议并受到越南工业部领导方面积极的支持，经过三昼夜的苦战而建成的，它的成本只合中国人民币三百多元。
