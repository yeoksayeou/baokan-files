Title: British Yorkshire Peace Organization Demands US, UK Accept New Soviet Proposal
Author:
Date: 1960-03-25
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, London, March 21st: A peace conference convened by the Yorkshire Joint Action Committee for Peace in Britain on the 20th demanded that the British and American governments accept the new Soviet proposals regarding the prohibition of nuclear weapons testing.

The conference was attended by representatives and observers from eleven peace organizations, twenty-one trade union organizations, and other public groups. Nearly one hundred and fifty delegates attending the meeting unanimously and warmly welcomed the new proposals put forward by the Soviet delegation at the Geneva Nuclear Weapons Test Ban Conference on the 19th.

Professor Bernal, Executive Chairman of the Presidium of the World Peace Council and Vice-President of the British Peace Committee, delivered a speech at the meeting. He called for the immediate launch of a nationwide campaign in Britain to urge the British government and the US government to accept the Soviet proposal.

### 英国约克郡和平组织  要求美英接受苏联新建议

1960-03-25
第6版()
专栏：

    英国约克郡和平组织
    要求美英接受苏联新建议
    据新华社伦敦21日电　英国约克郡争取和平联合行动委员会召开的一次和平会议，20日要求英国和美国的政府接受苏联提出的关于禁止核武器试验的新建议。
    这个会议有十一个和平组织、二十一个工会组织和其他公众团体的代表和观察员参加。出席会议的将近一百五十名代表一致热烈欢迎苏联代表团19日在日内瓦不继续核武器试验会议上提出的新建议。
    世界和平理事会主席团执行主席、英国和平委员会副会长贝尔纳教授在会上发表演说。他要求立即在英国全国发动一个要英国政府和美国政府接受苏联这一建议的运动。
