Title: Socialist Countries' Agricultural Science Conference Concludes; Decision Made to Strengthen Agricultural Science Cooperation Among Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-03-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th: According to TASS, the conference of representatives of agricultural science organs from Albania, Bulgaria, Hungary, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the German Democratic Republic, the People's Republic of China, Poland, Romania, the Soviet Union, and Czechoslovakia concluded in Moscow on the 11th.

The conference was held to discuss the coordination of scientific work in agriculture and forestry. Representatives from the Standing Committee on Agricultural Economics, Science, and Technology Cooperation of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance (COMECON), and representatives from the COMECON Secretariat also participated in the work of the conference.

The scientists summarized the work outlined at the Third Conference on Cooperation in Agricultural and Forestry Scientific Work held in Moscow in October 1958, and discussed plans for future joint work.

The conference unanimously pointed out that scientific ties among socialist countries in the field of agriculture have significantly expanded and strengthened in recent years. In 1959, socialist countries held more than twenty-five systematic scientific conferences on pressing agricultural issues.

Participants advocated further consolidating and strengthening scientific cooperation among fraternal countries, especially in jointly studying the most important theoretical issues of agricultural science. The next conference on cooperation in agricultural and forestry scientific work is planned to be held in Sofia in November of this year.

### 社会主义国家农业科学会议闭幕  决定加强各国农业科学合作

1960-03-14
第5版()
专栏：

    社会主义国家农业科学会议闭幕
    决定加强各国农业科学合作
    新华社12日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、匈牙利、越南民主共和国、德意志民主共和国、中华人民共和国、波兰、罗马尼亚、苏联和捷克斯洛伐克农业科学机关代表会议，11日在莫斯科闭幕。
    代表会议是为讨论农林业方面科学工作的配合问题而举行的。经济互助委员会农业经济、科学和技术合作常设委员会的代表和经济互助委员会书记处的代表也参加了这次会议的工作。
    科学家们对1958年10月在莫斯科举行的第三次农林业科学工作合作会议所拟定的工作进行了总结，并且讨论了今后的共同工作计划。
    会议一致指出，社会主义各国农业方面的科学联系在最近几年有了显著扩大和巩固。1959年，社会主义国家就农业迫切问题举行了二十五次以上的有系统的科学会议。
    与会者主张进一步巩固和加强兄弟国家之间的科学合作，特别是共同研究农业科学的最重要的理论性的问题。下一次农林业科学工作合作会议，计划于今年11月在索非亚召开。
