　International Briefs
　　
　　Cambodian King Appoints Two Ministers
　　Phnom Penh dispatch, according to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on the 3rd, according to the order of the Cambodian King, the former Minister of Education, Nhiek Tioulong, was appointed Minister of the Interior, and the former State Secretary of the Interior, Chau Xeng Ua, was appointed Minister of Education.
　　
　　Snowstorm in the United States
　　New York dispatch, a snowstorm occurred in the northeastern states of the United States on the 4th, killing at least eighty people.
　　In the areas hit by the snowstorm, schools were closed and shops were forced to shut down. Many cars were buried under snowdrifts on the roads. Railway and air traffic were severely affected.
　　New York, the largest city in the United States, was one of the hardest-hit areas. By early morning on the 4th, the snow had already reached more than fourteen inches.

　　Pakistan Adopts Decimal Currency System
　　Karachi dispatch, the Pakistani government has decided to adopt a decimal currency system starting from January 1, 1961.
　　At a meeting held on the 2nd, the Pakistani government also decided to gradually replace the British system of weights and measures with the metric system.

### 国际简讯

1960-03-06
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    柬埔寨国王任命两个大臣
    金边讯　据柬埔寨新闻社3日报道，根据柬埔寨国王的命令，原任教育大臣的涅刁隆担任内政大臣，原任内政国务大臣的谢振国担任教育大臣。
    美国发生暴风雪
    纽约讯　美国东北部各州4日发生一场暴风雪，至少有八十人死亡。
    受到暴风雪袭击的地区，学校都关了门，商店也被迫停止营业。许多汽车在马路上被雪堆埋了起来。铁路和空中交通都受到严重影响。
    美国第一大城市纽约是这次受灾最重的地区之一，在4日清早，积雪便已达十四英寸以上。
    巴基斯坦采用十进位货币制
    卡拉奇讯　巴基斯坦政府已经决定从1961年1月1日起采用十进位的货币制度。
    巴基斯坦政府在2日举行的会议上还决定逐渐用米制来代替英国的度量衡制度。
