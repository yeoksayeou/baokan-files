Title: Guangxi Builds New Medium-Sized Blast Furnace, Henan Shuiye Iron Plant Completes 100-Cubic-Meter Blast Furnace
Author:
Date: 1960-03-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Liuzhou Electric Dispatch: A newly built 255-cubic-meter blast furnace at the Liuzhou Iron and Steel Plant in Guangxi formally produced iron on February 25th.

At 5:00 PM that day, He Ximing, Secretary of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, cut the colored ribbon tied in front of the blast furnace tapping area, and the molten iron, red and bright, rushed out. At this moment, bursts of cheers and applause erupted from around the tapping area, warmly congratulating the No. 1 blast furnace on officially going into production. Before the ribbon-cutting, the workers of the Liuzhou Iron and Steel Plant and more than 6,200 representatives of various ethnic groups from all over gathered in front of the blast furnace for a celebration meeting.

He Ximing spoke at the meeting. On behalf of the Autonomous Region Party Committee and the Autonomous Region People's Committee, he expressed warm congratulations to all the workers who built the Liuzhou Iron and Steel Plant, and expressed heartfelt thanks to the people of the whole country for their enthusiastic support in the construction of the Liuzhou Iron and Steel Plant. He Ximing said that the commissioning of the No. 1 blast furnace of the Liuzhou Iron and Steel Plant marks a new stage in Guangxi's industrial construction. All workers should continue to exert their energy and carry out the movement to increase production and practice economy, focusing on technological innovation and technological revolution, and strive to complete the Liuzhou Iron and Steel Plant as soon as possible.

Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 28th: The newly built 100-cubic-meter blast furnace at the Henan Shuiye Iron Plant was recently formally put into production. It is the largest modernized small blast furnace in terms of volume among the small blast furnace groups in Henan, and the entire production process is operated by mechanical or electrical means.

### 广西新建中型高炉出铁  河南水冶炼铁厂建成百立米高炉

1960-03-02
第2版()
专栏：

    广西新建中型高炉出铁
    河南水冶炼铁厂建成百立米高炉
    新华社柳州电　柳州钢铁厂新建的一座二百五十五立方米的高炉在2月25日正式出铁。
    当天下午五点钟，中共广西僮族自治区委员会书记贺希明剪断了系在高炉出铁场前的彩绸，红通通的铁水奔腾而出。这时，出铁场的周围立即迸发出一阵又一阵的欢呼和掌声，热烈祝贺一号高炉正式投入生产。剪彩之前，柳州钢铁厂的职工和来自各地的六千二百多名各族代表，一起在高炉前举行了庆祝大会。
    贺希明在会上讲了话。他代表自治区党委和自治区人民委员会，向建设柳州钢铁厂的全体职工表示热烈祝贺，并且对热情支援柳钢建设的全国人民表示衷心的感谢。贺希明说，柳州钢铁厂的一号高炉投入生产，标志着广西的工业建设进入了一个崭新的阶段，全体职工应该继续鼓足干劲，开展以技术革新和技术革命为中心的增产节约运动，争取尽快地全部建成柳州钢铁厂。
    新华社郑州28日电　河南水冶炼铁厂新建的一百立方米的高炉在最近正式投入生产。它是河南目前小高炉群中容积最大的一座现代化的小型高炉，整个生产过程都由机械或电气操作。
