Title: Prime Minister Koirala Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-03-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th - The Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Koirala, and his wife, along with accompanying dignitaries, returned to Beijing this afternoon by special plane from Hangzhou after visiting Zhengzhou, Shanghai, and Hangzhou, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo, our Ambassador to Nepal Pan Zili and his wife, and Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yu Peiwen.

Vice Premier Chen Yi greeted the distinguished guests at the airport.

In the afternoon, Prime Minister Koirala's wife and the Nepalese Minister of Engineering and Transportation, Singh, and other distinguished guests, accompanied by our Ambassador to Nepal, Pan Zili and his wife, and Vice Mayor of Beijing, Wang Kunlun, visited the Beijing Planetarium.

### 柯伊拉腊首相回到北京

1960-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

    柯伊拉腊首相回到北京
    新华社20日讯　尼泊尔王国首相柯伊拉腊和夫人以及随行的贵宾们，由外交部副部长罗贵波，我国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和夫人，外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文陪同，在郑州、上海、杭州参观访问之后，于今天下午乘专机由杭州回到北京。
    陈毅副总理到机场迎接贵宾们。
    下午，柯伊拉腊首相的夫人和尼泊尔工程和交通大臣辛格等贵宾，在我国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和夫人以及北京市副市长王昆仑陪同下，参观了北京天文馆。
