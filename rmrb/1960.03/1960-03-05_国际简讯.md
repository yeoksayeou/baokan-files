International Briefs

Two Thousand Snakes Found in an Indian Tree Hollow

New Delhi Dispatch: Delhi newspapers reported that in Jind, Punjab, India, workers discovered approximately two thousand snakes in the hollow of a tree. Each of these snakes was six to seven feet long.

Singapore Labour Front Dissolved

Singapore Dispatch: The Singapore Labour Front formally announced its dissolution on February 28th. The party passed this decision at a special meeting. The Labour Front was founded in 1954 by former Singapore Chief Minister Lim Yew Hock and others. In 1955, it formed a coalition government with the Singapore branch of the Malayan Chinese Association.

Sudan Establishes Central Bank

Khartoum Dispatch: Sudanese Prime Minister Abboud presided over the opening ceremony of the Central Bank of Sudan on February 25th. The Central Bank of Sudan will replace the National Bank of Egypt, which has been operating as the central bank of the country.

### 国际简讯

1960-03-05
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    印度一个树洞中发现两千条蛇
    新德里讯　德里报纸报道，在印度旁遮普邦的今德，工人们在一株树的树洞里发现约两千条蛇。这些蛇每条有六、七英尺长。
    新加坡劳工阵线解散
    新加坡讯　新加坡劳工阵线2月28日正式宣告解散。这个政党在一个特别会议上通过了这个决定。劳工阵线是在1954年由新加坡前首席部长林有福等发起组成的。它在1955年曾同新加坡马华联盟组织联合政府。
    苏丹成立中央银行
    喀土穆讯　苏丹总理阿布德2月25日主持了苏丹中央银行的开幕典礼。苏丹中央银行将代替过去一直在苏丹经营这个国家的中央银行的业务的埃及国家银行。
