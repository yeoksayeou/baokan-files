Title: Chinese National Song and Dance Ensemble Holds Farewell Performance in Yangon; Chu Tunan Delivers Speech Thanking Burmese Government and People for Warm Hospitality
Author:
Date: 1960-03-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, 29th – The Chinese National Song and Dance Ensemble gave a farewell performance here tonight. Among the approximately seven thousand audience members were Burmese Chief Justice of the Supreme Court U Myint Thein, Minister of Culture U Chit Thoung, government officials, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Burma.

    Before the performance, Burmese Minister of Culture U Chit Thoung delivered a speech. He expressed his firm belief that the friendly relations between Burma and China would be further developed and consolidated through this kind of cultural exchange.

    Chu Tunan, head of the Chinese Cultural Friendship Delegation, thanked the Burmese government and people in his speech for their warm hospitality to the Chinese delegation during their visit to Burma. He firmly believes that under the guidance of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, and after the successful conclusion of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Agreement and the Treaty of Mutual Non-Aggression, the friendship between the two countries will be further strengthened.

    The Chinese Cultural Friendship Delegation and the Chinese National Song and Dance Ensemble arrived here on January 24th. Over the course of more than a month, the Chinese artists performed twenty times in Yangon and other cities. More than 100,000 people watched the performances.

    Burmese Prime Minister General Ne Win, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities Sao Hkun Hkio, and cabinet ministers warmly and cordially received the Chinese Cultural Friendship Delegation and the Chinese National Song and Dance Ensemble. They also watched the performances of the Chinese artists.

    In Taunggyi, the capital of Shan State, approximately 50% of the population watched the performance of the Chinese National Song and Dance Ensemble.

### 中国民族歌舞团在仰光举行告别演出  楚图南致词感谢缅甸政府和人民的热情招待

1960-03-02
第5版()
专栏：

    中国民族歌舞团在仰光举行告别演出
    楚图南致词感谢缅甸政府和人民的热情招待
    新华社仰光29日电　中国民族歌舞团28日晚在这里举行告别演出。在大约七千位观众中，有缅甸最高法院首席法官吴敏登、文化部长吴漆东、政府官员们和各国驻缅甸的外交使节。
    在演出前，缅甸文化部长吴漆东致词。他表示深信缅甸和中国友好关系通过这种文化交流将会进一步发展和巩固。
    中国文化友好代表团团长楚图南在致词时感谢缅甸政府和人民在中国代表团访问缅甸期间对他们的热情招待。他深信在和平共处五项原则的指导下以及在成功地缔结了中缅边界协定和互不侵犯条约以后，两国的友谊将会进一步加强。
    中国文化友好代表团和中国民族歌舞团是在1月24日到达这里的。中国艺术家在缅甸的一个多月的时间里，在仰光和其他城市演出了二十次。有十万多人观看了演出。
    缅甸总理奈温将军、民族院议长萧恢塔和内阁部长们热诚和友好地接待了中国文化友好代表团和中国民族歌舞团。他们还观看了中国艺术家的演出。
    在掸邦首府东枝，大约50％的人观看了中国民族歌舞团的演出。
