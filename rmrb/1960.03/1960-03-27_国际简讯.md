Title: International Briefs
Author:
Date: 1960-03-27
Page: 5

　　Wu Nu Returns Home After Visiting Japan
　　Rangoon Dispatch: After a five-day visit to Japan and participation in the Ninth Congress of the International Press Institute, Wu Nu returned to Rangoon by plane on the 26th.

　　Strike of Employees of State Bank of India Ends
　　New Delhi Dispatch: The twenty-one-day strike by employees of the State Bank of India ended on the evening of the 24th. It has been announced that the government and bank authorities have agreed to negotiate on the employees' demands for bonuses, wage increases, and payment of wages during the strike.

　　Philippines Increases Military Spending
　　Manila Dispatch: The 1960-1961 national budget recently passed by the Philippine House of Representatives Appropriations Committee stipulates military expenditure of 190 million pesos. This figure is an increase of 7 million pesos over the current year's estimated military expenditure.

### 国际简讯

1960-03-27
第5版()
专栏：

    国际简讯
    吴努访问日本后回国
    仰光讯　吴努在日本进行五天访问并参加国际新闻学会第九届大会后，于26日乘飞机返回仰光。
    印度国家银行雇员罢工结束
    新德里讯　印度国家银行雇员历时二十一天的罢工，已经在24日晚上结束。据宣布，政府和银行当局已经表示同意就雇员提出的奖金、提高工资和罢工期间照付工资等要求进行谈判。
    菲律宾增加军费
    马尼拉讯　菲律宾众议院拨款委员会最近通过的1960—1961年国家预算规定军费为一亿九千万比索。这个数字比本年度军费估计数字增加七百万比索。
