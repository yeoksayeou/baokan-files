	U.S. Fleet with Missiles
	to Make a "Courtesy Visit" to India
	
	New Delhi Dispatch: According to the "Hindustan Times" on the 21st, a U.S. Navy fleet led by the aircraft carrier "Goodman Richard," equipped with "Regulus" missiles, will make a "courtesy visit" to Indian territorial waters in the first half of April.
	
	U.S. Launch of Small Satellite Fails
	
	Cape Canaveral (United States) Dispatch: At 8:00 a.m. local time on the 23rd, the U.S. National Aeronautics and Space Administration prepared to launch a satellite weighing only thirty-odd pounds with a four-stage rocket. However, the third stage rocket engine failed to start after the rocket ascended, and radio contact with the ground was interrupted. Fifty minutes after the rocket launch, it was officially announced that the satellite had failed to enter orbit.

### 国际简讯

    国际简讯
    带有导弹的美国舰队
    将到印度进行“礼节访问”
    新德里讯　据21日“印度斯坦时报”报道，拥有“雷古拉斯”式导弹装备的“好人李查德号”航空母舰率领的一个美国海军舰队，将在4月上半月到印度领海进行“礼节访问”。
    美国发射小卫星失败
    卡纳维拉尔角（美国）讯　23日当地时间上午八时，美国全国星际和空间航行局准备用一个四级火箭发射一个只有三十多磅重的卫星。但是，火箭上升后第三级火箭的发动机未能开动，与地面的无线电联系即告中断。火箭发射五十分钟后，即正式宣布卫星未能进入轨道。
