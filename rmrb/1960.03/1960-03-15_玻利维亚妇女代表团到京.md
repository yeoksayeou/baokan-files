Title: Bolivian Women's Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-03-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 14th -- The Bolivian Women's Delegation, invited by the All-China Women's Federation to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation consisted of five people, led by the head of the delegation, Aranda Bedregal de Konitze.

Cao Mengjun, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 玻利维亚妇女代表团到京

1960-03-15
第4版()
专栏：

    玻利维亚妇女代表团到京
    新华社14日讯　应全国妇女联合会邀请来我国访问的玻利维亚妇女代表团一行五人，在团长亚兰达·贝德雷加尔·德·科尼策率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
  到机场欢迎的有全国妇联主席团委员曹孟君等。
