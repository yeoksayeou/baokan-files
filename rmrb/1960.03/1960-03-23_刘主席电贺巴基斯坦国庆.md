Title: Chairman Liu Sends Congratulations on Pakistan's National Day
Author:
Date: 1960-03-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, sent a telegram to the President of Pakistan, Ayub Khan, on the 21st to congratulate him on Pakistan's National Day. The telegram reads as follows:

His Excellency Field Marshal Mohammad Ayub Khan, President of Pakistan:

On the occasion of Pakistan's National Day, I, on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name, extend my best wishes to Your Excellency and the people of Pakistan. I wish Pakistan prosperity and its people happiness.

Liu Shaoqi
Chairman of the People's Republic of China
March 21, 1960

### 刘主席电贺巴基斯坦国庆

1960-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席电贺巴基斯坦国庆
    新华社22日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇在21日打电报给巴基斯坦总统阿尤布汗，祝贺巴基斯坦国庆。电文如下：
    巴基斯坦总统穆罕默德·阿尤布汗元帅阁下：
    值此巴基斯坦国庆之际，我代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义，向阁下和巴基斯坦人民表示良好的祝愿。祝巴基斯坦国家繁荣、人民幸福。中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇　一九六○年三月二十一日
