Title: Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association Presents a Film to the Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Association
Author: 
Date: 1960-03-19
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 16th. Xie Fusheng, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, visited Lubusang, the Chairman of the Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Association, on the afternoon of the 16th, and presented him with a Chinese color documentary film, "Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of the Founding of the Nation." This film was presented to the Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Association by the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association, and Lubusang expressed his gratitude for it.

### 中蒙友协赠送蒙中友协一部影片

1960-03-19
第5版()
专栏：

    中蒙友协赠送蒙中友协一部影片
    新华社乌兰巴托16日电　中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生16日下午拜会了蒙中友好协会主席鲁布桑，转交给他一部中国彩色纪录片——“庆祝建国十周年”。这部影片是中蒙友好协会赠送给蒙中友好协会的，鲁布桑对此表示感谢。
