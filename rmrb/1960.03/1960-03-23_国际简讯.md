    Vietnam's Ambassador to Guinea Presents Credentials
    Conakry Dispatch: Nguyen Thuong, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Republic of Guinea, presented his credentials to President Sékou Touré on the 21st.
    
    Iraq and Nepal Establish Diplomatic Relations
    Baghdad Dispatch: The Iraqi News Agency reported on the evening of the 15th that the Government of the Republic of Iraq had decided to establish diplomatic relations with the Kingdom of Nepal and to exchange diplomatic representatives at the level of minister.
    Kasim Hassan, the Iraqi Ambassador to India, has been appointed to concurrently serve as the Minister to Nepal.
    
    U.S. Television Drama Writers Strike
    New York Dispatch: Following the strike by the Writers Guild of America and the actors of Hollywood's seven major film companies, American freelance film and television writers went on strike starting at midnight on the 18th. These writers write scripts for many high-level television programs.
    The purpose of this strike is to demand that television drama writers receive the compensation they should be entitled to due to the sale of television programs abroad and due to television programs being broadcast more than once.

### 国际简讯

1960-03-23
第6版()
专栏：

    国际简讯
    越南驻几内亚大使递交国书
    科纳克里讯：越南民主共和国驻几内亚共和国大使阮商21日向塞古·杜尔总统递交了国书。
    伊拉克同尼泊尔建交
    巴格达讯　伊拉克通讯社15日晚报道，伊拉克共和国政府已经决定同尼泊尔王国建立外交关系，并互派公使级的外交代表。
    伊拉克驻印度大使卡西姆·哈桑已被任命兼任驻尼泊尔公使。
    美国电视剧作家罢工
    纽约讯　继美国作家协会和好莱坞七大影片公司的演员罢工之后，美国自由影剧作家18日午夜起，举行罢工。这些剧作家是为许多高级电视片写剧本的。
    这次罢工的目的是要求让电视剧作家能得到由于在国外出售电视片和由于电视片放映一次以上而应得到的报酬。
