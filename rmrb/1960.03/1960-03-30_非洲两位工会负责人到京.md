Title: Two African Trade Union Leaders Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-03-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, March 28th. M'Vee Louis, General Secretary of the Gabonese Trade Union Confederation, and Mambella Naim, Secretary of the Chadian Trade Union Confederation, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They are visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

    Li Zaizhen, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others welcomed the guests at the airport.

### 非洲两位工会负责人到京

1960-03-30
第2版()
专栏：

    非洲两位工会负责人到京
    新华社28日讯　加蓬工会联合会总书记姆维·路易和乍得工会联合会书记曼勃拉·纳伊姆，今天乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中华全国总工会的邀请来我国访问的。
    中华全国总工会书记处书记栗再温等人到机场欢迎客人。
