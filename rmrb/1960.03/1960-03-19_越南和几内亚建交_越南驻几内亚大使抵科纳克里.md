Title: Vietnam and Guinea Establish Diplomatic Relations; Vietnamese Ambassador to Guinea Arrives in Conakry
Author:
Date: 1960-03-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 16th. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam announced on the 15th that the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Republic of Guinea have decided to establish diplomatic relations and exchange ambassadors.
    Nguyen Thuong, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, has departed for Guinea.
    Guinea is the first African country to establish diplomatic relations with the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 17th. According to a report by the Vietnam News Agency today: Nguyen Thuong, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Republic of Guinea, arrived in Conakry, the capital of Guinea, on the 15th.
    The Secretary-General and the Director of Protocol of the Guinean Ministry of Foreign Affairs, diplomatic envoys of friendly countries to Guinea, and representatives of Vietnamese expatriates in Guinea welcomed him at the airport.

### 越南和几内亚建交  越南驻几内亚大使抵科纳克里

1960-03-19
第5版()
专栏：

    越南和几内亚建交
    越南驻几内亚大使抵科纳克里
    新华社河内16日电　越南民主共和国外交部15日宣布，越南民主共和国和几内亚共和国决定建立外交关系，并且互派大使级代表。
    越南民主共和国首任特命全权大使阮商已经动身去几内亚。
    几内亚是非洲第一个同越南民主共和国建立外交关系的国家。
    新华社河内17日电　据越南通讯社今天报道：越南民主共和国首任驻几内亚共和国特命全权大使阮商15日到达几内亚首都科纳克里。
    到机场欢迎他的有几内亚外交部秘书长和礼宾司长，各友好国家驻几内亚外交使节和在几内亚的越南侨民代表。
