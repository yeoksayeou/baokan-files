Title: Burmese "Guardian" Publishes Editorial Praising the Rice Planters Presented to Prime Minister Ne Win by Our Country
Author:
Date: 1960-03-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 9th -- Burmese newspapers praised the two rice planting machines presented by the Chinese government to Prime Minister Ne Win.

    The "Guardian" published an editorial today saying that the demonstration of the Chinese rice planting machines proved successful. Therefore, there is no doubt that the most important part of rice farming in Burma can be innovated.
    The newspaper also said that in Burma, rice planting is a tiring job. This has always been done by rural girls and women. Therefore, the two machines donated by China will be welcomed by those engaged in agriculture.

### 缅甸“卫报”发表社论  赞扬我国赠给奈温总理的插秧机

1960-03-10
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸“卫报”发表社论
    赞扬我国赠给奈温总理的插秧机
    新华社仰光9日电　缅甸报纸赞扬中国政府赠送给奈温总理的两架水稻插秧机。
    “卫报”今天发表社论说，中国水稻插秧机的表演证明是成功的，因此，毫无疑问，在缅甸，大米耕作法中最重要的部分能够加以革新。
    这家报纸又说，在缅甸，插秧是一种累人的工作。这向来是由农村的姑娘和妇女们干的。因此，中国赠送的这两架机器将受到从事农业的那些人的欢迎。
