Title: Korean Women's Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-03-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 21st: The Korean women's delegation, invited by the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China to participate in the celebrations of the 50th anniversary of the "March 8th" International Working Women's Day, left Beijing by train this morning to return to their country after visiting and touring Shanghai, Hangzhou, Nanchang, Guangzhou and other places.

Shi Liang, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, Huang Ganying, Director of the International Liaison Department, and others were at the station to see them off.

### 朝鲜妇女代表团回国

1960-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜妇女代表团回国
    新华社21日讯　应中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会邀请来我国参加“三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年庆祝活动的朝鲜妇女代表团，在上海、杭州、南昌、广州等地参观访问后，今天上午乘火车离开北京回国。
    到车站欢送的有全国妇联副主席史良、国际联络部部长黄甘英等。
