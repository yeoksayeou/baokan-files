Title: Lieutenant Colonel Soe Myint, Commander of the 7th Brigade of the Union Defense Force of Burma, Leaves Mangshi for Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-03-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, March 16th: Lieutenant Colonel Soe Myint, Commander of the 7th Brigade of the Union Defense Force of Burma, at the invitation of Colonel Ding Yi, commander of our Tengchong garrison, attended the mass rally in Mangshi, capital of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture in Yunnan, to celebrate the signing of the Sino-Burmese Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Non-Aggression and the Sino-Burmese Boundary Agreement. After a tour and visit in Mangshi, he left Mangshi for home yesterday.

On the 14th, Lieutenant Colonel Soe Myint and other Burmese friends were invited to attend a banquet held by Wang Zhixiang, Deputy Governor of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture. During the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly toasted to the friendship between China and Burma. That evening, the Burmese friends watched ethnic song and dance performances by the Autonomous Prefecture Song and Dance Troupe.

Yesterday morning, Lieutenant Colonel Soe Myint and his Burmese friends, accompanied by Li Linwu, Deputy Governor of the Autonomous Prefecture, Colonel Ding Yi, and others, toured the streets of Mangshi and visited department stores, state-run canteens, cinemas, Xinhua bookstores, and post and telecommunications bureaus.

Before Lieutenant Colonel Soe Myint and others left Mangshi, Wang Zhixiang, Deputy Governor of the Autonomous Prefecture, and Colonel Ding Yi presented some exquisite handicrafts and other gifts to the Burmese friends as souvenirs. Lieutenant Colonel Soe Myint also presented gifts in return. Colonel Ding Yi and others accompanied the Burmese friends to the Wanding Bridge on the border before reluctantly saying goodbye.

### 缅甸联邦国防军第七旅旅长  苏敏上校离芒市回国

1960-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸联邦国防军第七旅旅长
    苏敏上校离芒市回国
    新华社昆明16日电　缅甸联邦国防军第七旅旅长苏敏上校应我国腾冲驻军指挥官丁一上校的邀请，到云南德宏傣族景颇族自治州首府芒市参加了庆祝中缅友好和互不侵犯条约、中缅两国边界问题协定签订的群众大会，并在芒市进行了参观访问以后，已经在昨天离开芒市回国。
    14日，苏敏上校和其他缅甸朋友应邀出席了德宏傣族景颇族自治州副州长王之祥举行的宴会。席间，宾主一再为中缅两国的友谊干杯。当天晚上，缅甸朋友们观看了自治州歌舞团表演的民族歌舞。
    昨天上午，苏敏上校和缅甸朋友们在自治州副州长李临五和丁一上校等人陪同下，游览了芒市的街道，并参观了百货商店、国营食堂、电影院、新华书店和邮电局等。
    在苏敏上校等离开芒市前，自治州副州长王之祥和丁一上校把一些精致的工艺品等礼品赠送给缅甸朋友作纪念，苏敏上校也回赠了礼品。丁一上校等一直把缅甸朋友们送到国境线上的畹町桥头，才依依惜别。
