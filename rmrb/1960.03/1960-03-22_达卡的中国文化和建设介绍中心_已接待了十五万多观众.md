Title: The Chinese Culture and Construction Introduction Center in Dhaka Has Received 150,000 Visitors
Author:
Date: 1960-03-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 16th—The Chinese Culture and Construction Introduction Center at the East Pakistan International Industrial Exposition in Dhaka, the capital of East Pakistan, has received more than 150,000 visitors since it opened on January 31. The center displays pictures, books, and drawings introducing China's economic construction, culture, and art, and also shows Chinese films.

The Chinese Culture and Construction Introduction Center has been warmly welcomed by visitors. One visitor wrote in the guestbook that China has a very long culture and has now achieved new achievements on a remarkable foundation. A professor at Dhaka Medical College said after visiting: "I think China is an advanced civilized country and has full freedom."

The Chinese Culture and Construction Introduction Center was held at the invitation of the organizers of the East Pakistan International Industrial Exposition. Many countries, including the United States, Britain, West Germany, and Japan, have also established their propaganda centers at the exposition.

### 达卡的中国文化和建设介绍中心  已接待了十五万多观众

1960-03-22
第6版()
专栏：

    达卡的中国文化和建设介绍中心
    已接待了十五万多观众
    新华社卡拉奇16日电　在东巴基斯坦首府达卡举行的东巴基斯坦国际工业博览会中的中国文化和建设介绍中心，自从1月31日开放以来，已经接待了十五万多名观众。这个中心陈列着介绍中国经济建设、文化和艺术的图片、书籍和绘图等，并且放映中国影片。
    中国文化和建设介绍中心受到参观者的热烈欢迎。一位参观者在留言簿上写道，中国的文化十分悠久，现在又在了不起的基础上取得了新的成就。达卡医学院的一位教授在参观以后说：“我认为，中国是一个文明的先进国家，并且有着充分的自由。”
    中国文化和建设介绍中心是应东巴基斯坦国际工业博览会的组织者的邀请而举办的。美、英、西德和日本等许多国家也在博览会中设立了它们的宣传中心。
