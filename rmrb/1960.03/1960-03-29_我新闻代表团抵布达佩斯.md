Title: Our News Delegation Arrives in Budapest
Author:
Date: 1960-03-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 28th. The Chinese journalists' delegation, led by Miao Haileng, Deputy Director of the Xinhua News Agency, arrived in Budapest by plane on the 28th. The delegation is visiting Hungary for a four-week friendly visit at the invitation of the Hungarian National Association of Journalists.

Welcoming the delegation at the airport were: Sakasich, Chairman of the Hungarian National Association of Journalists, Darvasi, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Socialist Workers' Party, and Vakonyi, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, among others. Lin Ye, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Hungary, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 我新闻代表团抵布达佩斯

1960-03-29
第5版()
专栏：

    我新闻代表团抵布达佩斯
    新华社布达佩斯28日电　由新华社副社长缪海棱率领的中国新闻工作者代表团，在28日乘飞机到达布达佩斯。代表团是应匈牙利全国记者协会的邀请前来作为期四周的友好访问的。
    到机场欢迎的有：匈牙利全国记者协会主席萨卡希奇，社会主义工人党中央宣传鼓动部副部长达尔瓦西，外交部新闻司司长瓦康尼等。中国驻匈牙利大使馆文化参赞林耶也前往机场欢迎。
