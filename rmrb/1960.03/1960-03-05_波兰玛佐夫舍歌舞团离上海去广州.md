Title: Polish Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble Leaves Shanghai for Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-03-05
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 2nd. The Polish Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble, led by Bogusław Płasa, representative of the Polish Ministry of Culture and Arts, and Mira Zimińska-Sygietyńska, head of the Song and Dance Ensemble, left Shanghai by special train last night for a visit and performance in Guangzhou.

### 波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团离上海去广州

1960-03-05
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团离上海去广州
    新华社上海2日电　波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团在波兰文化艺术部代表鲍古斯瓦夫·普瓦萨和歌舞团团长米拉·齐明斯卡·塞格廷斯卡率领下，昨晚乘专车离开上海前往广州访问演出。
