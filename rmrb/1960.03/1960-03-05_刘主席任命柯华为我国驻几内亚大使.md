Title: Liu Chairman Appoints Ke Hua as China's Ambassador to Guinea
Author:
Date: 1960-03-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has appointed Ke Hua as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Guinea.

### 刘主席任命柯华为我国驻几内亚大使

1960-03-05
第3版()
专栏：

    刘主席任命柯华为我国驻几内亚大使
    新华社4日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇任命柯华为中华人民共和国驻几内亚共和国特命全权大使。
