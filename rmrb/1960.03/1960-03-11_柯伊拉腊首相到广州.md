Title: Prime Minister Koirala Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-03-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 10th: Bishweshwar Prasad Koirala, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, and his wife, along with twelve other distinguished Nepalese guests, arrived in Guangzhou at 1:40 PM today via Shenzhen from Hong Kong by special train, at the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai for a friendly visit to China. They were warmly welcomed by Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province, and his wife, Vice Governor Lin Qiangyun, and 1,500 people from all walks of life in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou.

Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Jinchuan, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee, greeted the distinguished guests in Shenzhen and accompanied them to Guangzhou.

Today, Chinese and Nepalese national flags were hung on the platform of Guangzhou Railway Station. After Prime Minister Koirala and other distinguished Nepalese guests alighted from the train, a group of children presented flowers to the guests. After the band played the national anthems of the Kingdom of Nepal and the People's Republic of China, Prime Minister Koirala, accompanied by Governor Chen Yu, reviewed the guard of honor.

Governor Chen Yu delivered a welcome speech at the station, extending a warm welcome to the distinguished guests on behalf of the people of Guangdong Province. He said, "We believe that Prime Minister Koirala's visit to China will further promote the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Nepal, and will make a beneficial contribution to the noble cause of safeguarding peace in Asia and the world."

Prime Minister Koirala delivered a reply amidst warm applause. He said that Nepal and China have a long tradition of friendship, and the current friendly relations are based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. The Prime Minister said that China has made great achievements in all aspects of construction, and he hopes to take advantage of this visit to see more of China's construction achievements. He said that he firmly believes that this visit to China will not only promote and develop the traditional friendship between the two countries, but also learn some experience beneficial to the construction of Nepal.

The welcoming crowds lined up on the station platform, holding welcome banners such as "Long Live Sino-Nepalese Friendship" and "Long Live the Solidarity of Asian and African Countries", beating gongs and drums, and performing lion dances to express their warm welcome to the distinguished guests.

Among those who went to the station to welcome the distinguished guests today were Major General Liu Xinglong, Naval Major General Gui Shaobin, Air Force Senior Colonel Liu Shichang, leading members of the Guangzhou Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, responsible persons of relevant departments of Guangdong Province, responsible persons of the Guangdong and Guangzhou Municipal Committees of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, local organizations of various democratic parties in the province and city, and various people's organizations, as well as people from Buddhist circles. The heads of the Czechoslovak, Polish, and Vietnamese Democratic Republic consulates in Guangzhou also went to the station to welcome the guests. Basnyat, Consul General of the Kingdom of Nepal in Lhasa, traveled from Beijing to Kowloon Station to greet the Prime Minister and accompanied the Prime Minister to Guangzhou today.

In the evening, Governor Chen Yu held a banquet to entertain Prime Minister Koirala and his wife, as well as other distinguished Nepalese guests.

### 柯伊拉腊首相到广州

1960-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    柯伊拉腊首相到广州
    新华社广州10日电　应周恩来总理邀请来我国进行友好访问的尼泊尔王国首相毕什韦什瓦·普拉萨德·柯伊拉腊和夫人，以及随同来访的尼泊尔其他贵宾一行十二人，今天下午一时四十分由香港经深圳乘专车到达广州，受到广东省省长陈郁和夫人，副省长林锵云和广东省、广州市各界人民一千五百多人的热烈欢迎。
    外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文、广东省人民委员会外事处处长王锦川到深圳迎接贵宾，并陪同贵宾来到广州。
    今天广州车站月台上悬挂着中、尼两国国旗。柯伊拉腊首相和尼泊尔其他贵宾下车后，一群少年儿童上前向贵宾献花。乐队奏尼泊尔王国和中华人民共和国国歌后，柯伊拉腊首相由陈郁省长陪同检阅了仪仗队。
    陈郁省长在车站上致欢迎词，代表广东省人民热烈欢迎贵宾。他说，我们相信，这次柯伊拉腊首相来中国访问，将进一步促进中国和尼泊尔两国之间的友好合作关系，并将对维护亚洲和世界和平的崇高事业作出有益的贡献。
    柯伊拉腊首相在热烈的欢迎掌声中致答词。他说，尼泊尔和中国有着悠久的传统友谊，现在的友好关系是建立在五项原则基础上的。首相说，中国各方面建设都取得伟大成就，他希望利用这次访问机会，多参观中国的建设成就。他说，他深信这次来中国访问不仅将促进和发展两国的传统友谊，而且将学到一些有利于尼泊尔的建设经验。
    排列在车站月台上的欢迎群众，举着“中尼友好万岁”和“亚非国家团结万岁”等欢迎横额，敲锣打鼓，舞着狮子，向贵宾表示热烈欢迎。
    今天到车站欢迎贵宾的，还有中国人民解放军广州驻军首长刘兴隆少将、桂绍彬海军少将、刘世昌空军大校，以及广东省各有关部门负责人，政协广东省和广州市委员会、各民主党派省市地方组织和各人民团体的负责人，以及佛教界人士等。捷克斯洛伐克、波兰和越南民主共和国驻广州领事馆的负责人也到车站欢迎。尼泊尔王国驻拉萨总领事巴斯尼亚特从北京前往九龙车站迎接首相，今天陪同首相到达广州。晚上，陈郁省长举行宴会，招待柯伊拉腊首相和夫人以及尼泊尔其他贵宾。
