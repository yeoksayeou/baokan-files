Title: Algerian National Liberation Army Heroically Strikes Out: More Than 400 Enemies Annihilated in Two Days, French Military Strongholds Smashed in Multiple Locations
Author:
Date: 1960-03-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 29th – The Supreme Command of the Algerian National Liberation Army today released a military communique here stating that on February 25th and 26th, the Algerian National Liberation Army killed 286 French invading soldiers and wounded 137, inflicting significant losses on the enemy.

The communique said that during these two days, the Algerian National Liberation Army launched attacks on eleven French military strongholds, some of which were completely destroyed, while others were severely damaged. The Algerian National Liberation Army launched thirteen ambushes against the enemy and engaged in battle ten times.

It was announced that Algerian commandos launched lightning strikes against French military barracks and police centers in twenty-four cities and villages in Algeria. The Algerian National Liberation Army destroyed sixteen French military vehicles, one armored vehicle, and one military train, shot down one military aircraft, captured four French soldiers, and seven French soldiers surrendered to the Algerian National Liberation Army with all their equipment.

The communique concluded by stating that the Algerian National Liberation Army seized 214 weapons, a large quantity of ammunition, and hand grenades.

### 阿尔及利亚民族解放军英勇出击  两天内歼敌四百余人击溃法军据点多处

1960-03-02
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚民族解放军英勇出击
    两天内歼敌四百余人击溃法军据点多处
    新华社开罗29日电　阿尔及利亚民族解放军最高司令部今天在这里发表的军事公报说，在2月25日和26日阿尔及利亚民族解放军击毙法国侵略军二百八十六名，打伤一百三十七名，使敌人蒙受重大损失。
    公报说，阿尔及利亚民族解放军在这两天中对法军的十一个据点发动进攻，其中有些据点被彻底击溃，其它的据点受到严重破坏。阿尔及利亚民族解放军向敌军发动了十三次伏击，交战十次。
    据宣布，阿尔及利亚突击队向阿尔及利亚二十四个城市和乡村的法军兵营和警察中心发动了闪电式的袭击。阿尔及利亚民族解放军击溃了法军的十六辆军用车，一辆装甲车，一列军用火车，打落一架军用飞机，俘虏四名法国兵，并有七名法国兵带着全副装备向阿尔及利亚民族解放军投诚。
    公报最后说，阿尔及利亚民族解放军缴获了二百一十四件武器，大量弹药和手榴弹。
