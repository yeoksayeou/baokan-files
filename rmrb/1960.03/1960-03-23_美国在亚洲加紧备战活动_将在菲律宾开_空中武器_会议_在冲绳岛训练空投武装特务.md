Title: The United States Intensifies War Preparations in Asia: To Hold "Air Weapons" Conference in the Philippines; Training Airborne Armed Agents on Okinawa

Author:

Date: 1960-03-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, March 20th, Manila Dispatch: The United States, gathering some of its lackeys in Asia, will hold an "Air Weapons" conference at the U.S. Clark Air Base in the Philippines from March 28th to early April to coordinate with U.S. war preparations.

Participating in the conference will be personnel from the U.S. Thirteenth Air Force, the Philippines, Thailand, Britain, as well as the Chiang Kai-shek clique and the Syngman Rhee clique's air forces.

Xinhua News Agency, March 21st, Naha Dispatch: The United States is actively training assault troops on Okinawa whose sole purpose is to engage in espionage and sabotage activities. According to United Press International, the unit stationed in Okinawa is designated the First Special Forces, known as "Exterminators—Assault Troops." They are divided into small groups to carry out "special missions," conducting raids.

Colonel Mills, commander of the First Special Forces, told a United Press International reporter that "these personnel are trained in various amphibious and airborne operations." They will be trained to proficiently use various weapons, "including night combat, killing with knives or any other weapon available." These personnel are also given foreign language training. Mills said that the motto of the assault troops is, "No matter where, no matter when, no matter how."

### 美国在亚洲加紧备战活动  将在菲律宾开“空中武器”会议　在冲绳岛训练空投武装特务

1960-03-23
第5版()
专栏：

    美国在亚洲加紧备战活动
    将在菲律宾开“空中武器”会议　在冲绳岛训练空投武装特务
    据新华社20日讯　马尼拉消息：美国纠集它在亚洲的一些仆从，将从3月28日至4月初在菲律宾的美国克拉克空军基地举行“空中武器”会议，以配合美国的备战活动。
    参加会议的将有美国第十三航空队、菲律宾、泰国、英国以及蒋介石集团和李承晚集团的空军人员。
    据新华社21日讯　那霸消息：美国正在冲绳积极训练以从事特务破坏活动为专职的突击部队。据合众国际社透露，驻在冲绳的这支部队的番号是第一特别部队，号称“歼灭者——突击手”。他们分成小组，执行“特别任务”，进行突袭。
    第一特别部队司令米尔斯上校对合众国际社记者说，“这些人员受各种两栖和空运作战的训练”。他们将被训练成会熟练地使用各种武器，“这一点包括夜间作战，用小刀或者可能得到的其他任何武器来杀人”。还对这些人员施行外国语训练。米尔斯说，突击部队的格言是，“不论在什么地方，不论在什么时候，不论用什么方法。”
