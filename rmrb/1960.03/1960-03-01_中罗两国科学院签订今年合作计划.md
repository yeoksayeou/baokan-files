Title: China and Romania Academies of Sciences Sign This Year's Cooperation Plan
Author:
Date: 1960-03-01
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 29th - The 1960 Implementation Plan of the Scientific Cooperation Agreement between the Chinese and Romanian Academies of Sciences was signed in Beijing on the afternoon of the 29th, following discussions between the delegations of the two academies.

	Wu Youxun, head of the Chinese Academy of Sciences delegation and Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Academician Alexandru Codarcea, head of the Romanian Academy of Sciences delegation and Director of the Geology and Geography Department of the Romanian Academy of Sciences, signed the implementation plan on behalf of their respective academies.

	Members of the delegations from both the Chinese and Romanian Academies of Sciences attended the signing ceremony.

	Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Teodor Zamfirescu, the Romanian Ambassador to China, and others attended the signing ceremony.

	After the signing, members of both delegations raised their glasses to congratulate the further development of the fraternal cooperation between the two Academies of Sciences.

### 中罗两国科学院签订今年合作计划

1960-03-01
第5版()
专栏：

    中罗两国科学院签订今年合作计划
    新华社29日讯　中国和罗马尼亚两国科学院科学合作协定1960年执行计划，经两院代表团商谈之后，于29日下午在北京签字。
    中国科学院代表团团长、中国科学院副院长吴有训，罗马尼亚科学院代表团团长、罗马尼亚科学院地质地理学部主任亚力克山特鲁·科达尔查院士，分别代表两国科学院在执行计划上签字。
    中罗两国科学院代表团的团员参加了签字仪式。
    参加签字仪式的，有中国科学院院长郭沫若，罗马尼亚驻中国大使查哈勒斯库等。
    签字以后，双方代表团人员举杯祝贺两国科学院兄弟般合作的进一步发展。
