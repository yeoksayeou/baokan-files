Title: List of Newly Elected Members of the Standing Committee, Secretaries of the Secretariat, and Alternate Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Third Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League at its Sixth Plenary Session

Author:
Date: 1960-03-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 10th - The Sixth Plenary Session of the Third Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League elected Wu Liangpu, Li Qitao, Lu Jindong, Lu Zhao, Qian Liren, Huang Tianxiang, Li Peigen, Chen Fusheng, Tong Yun, Sun Yiqing, Fang Qun, and Lin Ping as members of the Standing Committee of the Communist Youth League.

Yang Haibo, Zhang Chao, Li Qitao, and Wang Zhaohua were elected as Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Lu Jindong and Zeng Delin were elected as Alternate Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League.

### 中国共产主义青年团第三届中央委员会第六次全体会议增选的常务委员会委员、中央书记处书记、候补书记名单

1960-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    中国共产主义青年团第三届中央委员会第六次全体会议增选的常务委员会委员、中央书记处书记、候补书记名单
  新华社10日讯　中国共产主义青年团第三届中央委员会第六次全体会议增选了吴亮璞、李琦涛、路金栋、鲁钊、钱李仁、黄天祥、李培根、陈扶生、同云、孙轶青、方群、林萍为共青团常务委员会委员。
    增选了杨海波、张超、李琦涛、王照华为共青团中央书记处书记，路金栋、曾德林为共青团中央书记处候补书记。
