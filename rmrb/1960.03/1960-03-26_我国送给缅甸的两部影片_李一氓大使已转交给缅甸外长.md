Title: China Presents Two Films to Burma; Ambassador Li Yimang Has Transferred Them to the Burmese Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1960-03-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon Telegram – On the afternoon of March 18th, Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang officially presented two documentary films to Burmese Foreign Minister U Chit Thoung here on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and Premier Zhou Enlai to the Burmese government and Premier Ne Win. The documentary "Celebrating Ten Years" was presented by the Chinese government to the Burmese government, and the other documentary "Burmese Premier General Ne Win Visits China" was presented by Premier Zhou Enlai to Burmese Premier General Ne Win.

### 我国送给缅甸的两部影片  李一氓大使已转交给缅甸外长

1960-03-26
第5版()
专栏：

    我国送给缅甸的两部影片
    李一氓大使已转交给缅甸外长
    据新华社仰光电　中国驻缅甸大使李一氓18日下午在这里把中华人民共和国政府和国务院总理周恩来送给缅甸政府和奈温总理的两部纪录影片正式交给缅甸外交部长吴千吞昂。纪录片“欢庆十年”是中国政府送给缅甸政府的，另一部纪录片“缅甸总理奈温将军访问中国”是周恩来总理送给缅甸总理奈温将军的。
