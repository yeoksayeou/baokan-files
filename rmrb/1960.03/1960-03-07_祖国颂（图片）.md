Title: Ode to the Motherland (Picture)
Author: Fu Zuoren, Dong Zhenfeng
Date: 1960-03-07
Page: 8

    Ode to the Motherland (Paper Cut)
    Fu Zuoren, Dong Zhenfeng
    First embroidery: Chairman Mao's boundless longevity
    Second embroidery: Our great motherland becomes richer and stronger every year
    Third embroidery: The general line shines with boundless brilliance
    Fourth embroidery: The Great Leap Forward, golden dragons soaring
    Fifth embroidery: The people's commune, a beautiful landscape
