Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into Fujian Territorial Waters; Our 93rd Serious Warning

Author:
Date: 1960-03-22
Page: 4

    U.S. Warship Intrudes into Fujian Territorial Waters
    We Issue the 93rd Serious Warning

    Xinhua News Agency, March 19th: From 21:20 on March 18th to 1:20 on March 19th, a U.S. warship intruded into the sea area of Baiquan region, Fujian Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was ordered to issue the 92nd serious warning.

    Xinhua News Agency, March 21st: From 2:29 to 5:20 on March 21st, a U.S. warship intruded into the sea area of Pingtan and Matsu regions, Fujian Province. On the same day, from 13:18 to 16:14, a U.S. warship once again intruded into the aforementioned sea area. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was ordered to issue the 93rd serious warning.

### 美国军舰侵入我福建领海  我提出第九十三次严重警告

1960-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

    美国军舰侵入我福建领海
    我提出第九十三次严重警告
    新华社19日讯　3月18日二十一时二十分到19日一时二十分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省白犬地区海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第九十二次严重警告。
    新华社21日讯　3月21日二时二十九分至五时二十分，美国军舰一艘侵入我福建省平潭、马祖地区海域。同日十三时十八分到十六时十四分，美国军舰一艘再次侵入我上述地区海域。对于美国军舰这种军事挑衅行为，我外交部发言人奉命提出第九十三次严重警告。
