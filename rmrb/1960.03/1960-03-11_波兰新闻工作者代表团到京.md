Title: Polish Journalists Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-03-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 9th. A seven-member Polish journalists delegation, led by Mr. M. Rakowski, editor-in-chief of the Polish "Political Weekly" and chairman of the Polish Journalists Association, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to China under the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Welcoming them at the airport were Mei Yi, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, Kang Maozhao, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Wang Yi, Secretary-General of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association, and people from the capital's press circles.

### 波兰新闻工作者代表团到京

1960-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰新闻工作者代表团到京
    新华社9日讯　根据中国波兰文化合作协定来我国访问的波兰新闻工作者代表团一行七人，在团长、波兰“政治周刊”主编、波兰新闻工作者协会主席米·拉科夫斯基率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长梅益、外交部新闻司副司长康矛召、中波友好协会秘书长王仪以及首都新闻界人士。
