Title: Prime Minister Koirala Visits Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1960-03-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 21st: Prime Minister Koirala and his wife, accompanied by Vice Minister of Light Industry Luo Shuzhang, Chinese Ambassador to Nepal Pan Zili and his wife, and others, toured the Tuan Cheng (Round City), Beihai Park, and the Beijing Zoo this morning.

    At Tuan Cheng, Prime Minister Koirala and his wife visited the national arts and crafts exhibition hall, viewing jade artifacts, ivory carvings, pottery, porcelain, and embroidery. The Prime Minister and his wife wrote inscriptions in the guest book, expressing their praise for these exquisite handicrafts. At the Beijing Zoo, the Prime Minister and his wife viewed the rhinoceroses, precious gifts presented to the Chinese government by the King of Nepal in 1958 and 1959.

    This morning, the Nepalese Minister of Engineering and Transport Singh, the Secretary of the Ministry of Commerce and Industry Shukla, and other distinguished guests visited the Badaling section of the Great Wall and the Dingling Tomb. Major General Rana visited a farm run by a unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

    In the afternoon, Prime Minister Koirala's wife, and Minister of Interior and Justice Upadhyaya toured the Temple of Heaven and visited the gymnasium. Minister of Engineering and Transport Singh and other distinguished guests toured Beihai Park and the Beijing Zoo.

### 柯伊拉腊首相在京参观

1960-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

    柯伊拉腊首相在京参观
    新华社21日讯　柯伊拉腊首相和他的夫人今天上午由轻工业部副部长罗叔章、中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和夫人等陪同，游览了团城、北海公园和北京动物园。
    在团城，柯伊拉腊首相和夫人参观了全国工艺品陈列馆的玉器、象牙雕刻、陶器、瓷器和刺绣等手工艺品。首相和夫人在留言簿上题词，对这些精致的手工艺品表示赞扬。在北京动物园里，首相和夫人观看了尼泊尔国王在1958年和1959年送给中国政府的珍贵礼物——犀牛。
    今天上午，尼泊尔工程和交通大臣辛格、商业和工业部秘书舒克拉等贵宾，游览了八达岭，参观了定陵。拉纳少将参观了中国人民解放军部队办的一个农场。
    下午，柯伊拉腊首相夫人、内政和司法大臣乌帕德亚亚游览了天坛，参观了体育馆。工程和交通大臣辛格等贵宾，游览了北海公园和北京动物园。
