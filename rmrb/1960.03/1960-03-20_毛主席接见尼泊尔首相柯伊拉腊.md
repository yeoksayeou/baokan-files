Title: Chairman Mao Meets Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala
Author:
Date: 1960-03-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, March 19th. Chairman Mao Zedong met with the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Bishweshwar Prasad Koirala, and his wife in Hangzhou on the 18th.

Also present at the meeting were Ganesh Man Singh, Nepalese Minister of Engineering and Transportation; Surya Prasad Upadhyaya, Minister of Interior and Justice; Vishwa Shankar Shukla, Secretary of the Ministry of Commerce and Industry; Major General Chetra Bikram Rana; Mani Harsha Jyoti, Member of the Upper House; Kumar Mani Acharya Dixit, Private Secretary to the Prime Minister; and Mahendra Narasingh Rana, Engineering Technical Officer, who accompanied Prime Minister Koirala on his visit to China.

Also present at the meeting was the entire delegation of Nepalese journalists.

During the meeting, Chairman Mao Zedong had friendly conversations with Prime Minister Koirala and his wife, as well as the accompanying personnel.

Present at the meeting were Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Pan Zili, China's Ambassador to Nepal, and his wife; and Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Also present were Jiang Hua, First Secretary of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Huo Shilian, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province.

Basnyat, Consul General of Nepal in Lhasa, was also present at the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, March 19th. Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala and his wife, along with the accompanying Nepalese dignitaries, continued their sightseeing tour of Hangzhou today, accompanied by Ren Yili, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province, and Zhang Shixiang, Vice Mayor of Hangzhou.

The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by the workers when they visited the Hangzhou Du Jinsheng Silk Weaving Factory. Prime Minister Koirala and other distinguished guests visited the factory in detail and inquired about the production and development of the factory, as well as the silk production process. Prime Minister Koirala praised the exquisite silk produced by the workers and expressed his gratitude for the warm reception from the hosts.

The distinguished guests also cruised on West Lake, admiring the lake scenery, and visited famous scenic spots and historical sites of West Lake such as Lingyin Temple and Liuhe Pagoda.

V. S. Shukla, Secretary of the Ministry of Commerce and Industry of the Kingdom of Nepal, M. Jyoti, Member of the Upper House, and other distinguished guests also visited the Banshan Iron and Steel Plant in Zhejiang. (Attached picture)

### 毛主席接见尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊

1960-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊
    新华社杭州19日电　毛泽东主席18日在杭州接见了尼泊尔王国首相毕什韦什瓦·普拉萨德·柯伊拉腊和他的夫人。
    同时被接见的，还有随同柯伊拉腊首相来我国访问的尼泊尔工程和交通大臣加奈什·曼·辛格，内政和司法大臣苏里亚·普拉萨德·乌帕德亚亚，商业和工业部秘书维什瓦·山卡尔·舒克拉，少将切特拉·比克拉姆·拉纳，上院议员马尼哈尔沙·乔提，首相私人秘书库马尔·马尼·阿查里雅·迪克希特，工程技术官员马亨德拉·纳拉辛格·拉纳。
    同时被接见的，还有尼泊尔新闻工作者代表团全体成员。
    接见的时候，毛泽东主席同柯伊拉腊首相和夫人以及随行人员，进行了友好的交谈。
    接见时在座的，有外交部副部长罗贵波，我国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和夫人，外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文。
    在座的还有中国共产党浙江省委员会第一书记江华，浙江省副省长霍士廉。
    接见时，尼泊尔驻拉萨总领事巴斯尼亚特也在座。
    新华社杭州19日电　尼泊尔王国首相柯伊拉腊和夫人及随行的尼泊尔贵宾，今天由浙江省副省长任一力、杭州市副市长张世祥陪同，继续在杭州参观游览。
    贵宾们在参观杭州都锦生丝织厂时，受到工人们的热烈欢迎。柯伊拉腊等贵宾详细地参观并且询问了这个厂生产发展的情况，以及丝绸的生产过程。柯伊拉腊首相对工人制出的精美绸缎表示赞扬，对主人的热情接待表示感谢。
    贵宾们还泛舟西湖，欣赏了湖光景色，游览了灵隐寺、六和塔等西湖名胜古迹。
    尼泊尔王国商业和工业部秘书维·山·舒克拉，上院议员马·乔提等贵宾还参观了浙江半山钢铁厂。（附图片）
