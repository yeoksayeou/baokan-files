Title: Returning After a Thousand Miles, Eagerly Asking About Warmth and Comfort: Fang Fang and Others Extend Cordial Greetings to Returned Overseas Chinese, Their Families, and Young Students in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-03-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 1st: Fang Fang, a member of the Reception and Settlement Committee for Returned Overseas Chinese and Deputy Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, Zhuang Xiquan, and representatives of returned overseas Chinese, their families, and young students in Guangzhou, visited various guest houses in Guangzhou today to extend cordial greetings to the overseas Chinese who returned to Guangzhou from Indonesia yesterday.

　　Fang Fang and Zhuang Xiquan visited the Overseas Chinese Guest House yesterday afternoon to see the returned overseas Chinese. This morning, Fang Fang, along with Li Mei, a member of the Reception and Settlement Committee for Returned Overseas Chinese, also visited the First Affiliated Hospital of Zhongshan Medical College to see "Hai Sheng" (Sea Born), who was born on the ship returning to China, and his mother, Yan Guilian. They were well taken care of in the hospital and are in good health. Fang Fang and others sent them flowers, fruit, and floral fabric, and also told Yan Guilian to rest well. Yan Guilian said gratefully that she was very happy and satisfied.

　　Wu Yixiu, a member of the Reception and Settlement Committee for Returned Overseas Chinese, Fu Weidan, Chairman of the Returned Overseas Chinese Federation of Quanzhou City, Fujian Province, and Chen Yonghe, Standing Committee Member of the Returned Overseas Chinese Federation of Xiamen City, also visited the Sanyuanli Overseas Chinese Guest House this morning to express their regards to the returned overseas Chinese.

　　More than sixty representatives of returned overseas Chinese and their families in Guangzhou, led by Fang Junzhuang, Chairman of the Guangzhou Returned Overseas Chinese Federation, and Yu Junsheng, Vice Chairman, went to various Overseas Chinese Guest Houses early this morning to greet the overseas Chinese who returned yesterday. Liao Ketong, an overseas Chinese who returned from Indonesia last year, shared his feelings after returning to China with the newly returned overseas Chinese, saying that the motherland's care for overseas Chinese is meticulous, making us feel like we are living in a warm family. Yang Jinchuan, a seventy-two-year-old overseas Chinese who returned yesterday, nodded and smiled repeatedly, saying: "The motherland truly cares for us. With the strong motherland built under the leadership of the Communist Party and Chairman Mao, we overseas Chinese are no longer helpless."

　　More than one hundred youth representatives from all walks of life in Guangzhou also went to various guest houses and the Guangzhou Overseas Chinese Secondary Remedial School today to express their regards to the newly returned overseas Chinese. The youth brought warm letters of greetings to the overseas Chinese and performed cultural programs.

### 千里归来日　争相问寒暖  方方等及广州归侨、侨眷和青年学生亲切慰问归侨

1960-03-02
第4版()
专栏：

    千里归来日　争相问寒暖
    方方等及广州归侨、侨眷和青年学生亲切慰问归侨
    新华社广州1日电　接待和安置归国华侨委员会委员、华侨事务委员会副主任方方、庄希泉等及广州市归侨、侨眷、青年学生的代表，今天到广州各招待所亲切慰问昨天从印度尼西亚回到广州的侨胞。
      方方、庄希泉昨天下午就到华侨招待所探望归侨们。今天上午，方方同接待和安置归国华侨委员会委员李梅，又到中山医学院第一附属医院，探望在归国的轮船上诞生的“海生”和他的母亲颜桂莲。她们母子在医院里受到很好的照顾，身体都很健康。方方等给她们送了鲜花、水果和花布，还叮嘱颜桂莲好好休息。颜桂莲感激地说，她很幸福，很满意。
    接待和安置归国华侨委员会委员吴益修，福建省泉州市归国华侨联合会主席傅维丹和厦门市归国华侨联合会常务委员陈永鹤等，今天上午也到三元里华侨招待所慰问归国侨胞。
    六十多名广州市归侨、侨眷代表，今天一清早由广州市归国华侨联合会主席方君壮、副主席余俊生等人率领，到各华侨招待所慰问昨天回来的侨胞。去年才从印度尼西亚归国的华侨廖可通，向新归侨们讲述了他回国后的感想，他说，祖国对华侨的照顾无微不至，使我们感到生活在一个亲切的大家庭中。昨天回来的七十二岁侨胞杨金川笑着连连点头说：“祖国对我们真关怀。有了共产党、毛主席领导建设起来的强大祖国，我们海外华侨就不再是无依无靠了。”
    广州各界青年代表一百多人今天也分别到各招待所及广州市归国华侨中等补习学校，慰问新归侨。青年们给侨胞带来了热情洋溢的慰问信，并表演了文艺节目。
