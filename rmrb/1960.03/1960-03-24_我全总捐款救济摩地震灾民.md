Title: ACFTU Donates Funds to Aid Moroccan Earthquake Victims
Author:
Date: 1960-03-24
Page: 5

    ACFTU Donates Funds to Aid Moroccan Earthquake Victims
    Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd – The All-China Federation of Trade Unions (ACFTU) today donated 1.4 million Moroccan francs to aid the victims of the earthquake in Agadir, Morocco. In a telegram to the Moroccan Labour Union, Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the ACFTU, stated: “To express the deep sympathy and concern of the Chinese workers for the victims and their families of the Agadir earthquake, we hereby donate 1.4 million Moroccan francs. We hope that the unfortunate consequences of this event will be overcome soon.”
    After learning of the earthquake in Agadir, Morocco, the ACFTU sent a telegram of condolence on March 3rd. Abdelkader Azzak, Deputy Secretary-General of the Moroccan Labour Union, sent a telegram to the ACFTU on March 10th, expressing his deep gratitude.

### 我全总捐款救济摩地震灾民

1960-03-24
第5版()
专栏：

    我全总捐款救济摩地震灾民
    新华社22日讯　中华全国总工会今天捐款一百四十万摩洛哥法郎，救济摩洛哥阿加迪因地震受害的灾民。全国总工会主席刘宁一在给摩洛哥劳工联合会的电报中说：“为了表达中国工人对阿加迪地震受难者及其家属的深切同情和关怀，我们谨捐助一百四十万摩洛哥法郎。愿这一事件的不幸后果早日获得克服。”
    全国总工会在得到摩洛哥阿加迪发生地震的消息后，曾在3月3日致电慰问。摩洛哥劳工联合会副总书记阿卜杜勒扎克在3月10日打电报给中华全国总工会，表示深切的感谢。
