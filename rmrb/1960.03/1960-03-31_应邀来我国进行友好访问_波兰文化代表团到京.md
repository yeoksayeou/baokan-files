Title: Polish Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing for Friendly Visit to China at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1960-03-31
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, March 30th. A Polish cultural delegation of three people, led by Tadeusz Galinski, Minister of Culture and Art of Poland, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture of China, for a friendly visit to China. Members of the delegation included Stanislaw Neumark, Director of the General Office of the Polish Ministry of Culture and Art, and journalist Mostowicz. Welcoming them at the airport were Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, Xu Guangxiao, Vice Minister, Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Wang Yi, Secretary-General of the Chinese-Polish Friendship Association, and others.

### 应邀来我国进行友好访问  波兰文化代表团到京

1960-03-31
第6版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国进行友好访问
    波兰文化代表团到京
    新华社30日讯　由团长、波兰文化艺术部部长塔德乌什·加林斯基率领的波兰文化代表团一行三人，今天乘飞机到达北京，他们是应我国文化部部长沈雁冰的邀请，来我国进行友好访问的。代表团团员有波兰文化艺术部办公厅主任斯塔尼斯瓦夫·奈乌马尔克和记者莫斯托维
  奇。到机场欢迎的有文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长徐光霄、外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清、中国波兰友好协会秘书长王仪等。
