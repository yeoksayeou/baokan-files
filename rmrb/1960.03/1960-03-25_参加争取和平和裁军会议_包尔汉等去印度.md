Title: Burhan and Others Go to India to Attend Meeting for Peace and Disarmament
Author:
Date: 1960-03-25
Page: 4

    To Attend Meeting for Peace and Disarmament
    Burhan and Others Go to India
    Xinhua News Agency, March 24th: Burhan, delegate to the National People's Congress and Vice-Chairman of the China Peace Committee, and Yao Nian, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, left Beijing by plane this morning for India to attend the Indian meeting for peace and disarmament. The meeting will be held in New Delhi, the capital of India, from March 27th to 29th.
    Those who went to the airport to see them off included Qu Tangliang, Secretary-General of the China Peace Committee; Wang Mingyuan, Acting Secretary-General of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity; Shen Bochun, Deputy Secretary-General of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association; and Zhang Jie, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association.

### 参加争取和平和裁军会议  包尔汉等去印度

1960-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

    参加争取和平和裁军会议
    包尔汉等去印度
    新华社24日讯　出席印度争取和平和裁军会议的全国人民代表大会代表、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席包尔汉，中国亚非团结委员会副秘书长姚念，今天上午乘飞机离开北京前往印度。这次会议将于3月27日到29日在印度首都新德里举行。
    前往机场送行的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长区棠亮、中国亚非团结委员会代秘书长王明远、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副秘书长申伯纯、中国印度友好协会会长丁西林、中国伊斯兰教协会副主任张杰等。
