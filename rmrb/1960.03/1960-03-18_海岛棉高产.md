Title: High Yield of Sea Island Cotton
Author:
Date: 1960-03-18
Page: 3

Brief News

    High Yield of Sea Island Cotton
    Last year, Xintang Commune in Zengcheng County, Guangdong Province, cultivated a one-mu experimental plot that yielded 823 jin of Sea Island cotton (seed cotton), setting a high-yield record for Sea Island cotton.
    This experimental plot was cultivated under the guidance of Yu Shaojie, a cotton planting expert from the South China Agricultural Science Research Institute.

### 海岛棉高产

1960-03-18
第3版()
专栏：简明新闻

    海岛棉高产
    广东增城县新塘公社去年种植一块一亩面积的试验田，实收海岛棉（籽棉）八百二十三斤，创造了海岛棉的高产纪录。
    这块试验田，是在华南农业科学研究所植棉专家于绍杰的指导下培育的。
