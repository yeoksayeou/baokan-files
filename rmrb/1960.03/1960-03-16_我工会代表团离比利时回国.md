Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation Leaves Belgium for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-03-16
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Brussels, March 14th: The Chinese trade union delegation led by Liu Changsheng, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, left Brussels by plane today for home after a seven-day visit to Belgium.

The delegation was invited by the Liège Provincial Trade Union Organization of the Belgian General Federation of Labor to visit. They visited Liège Province, the eastern region of Belgium, and the city of Antwerp.

On the evening of March 11th, they attended a reception hosted by the Belgium-China Association.

On the same day, leaders of the Central Committee of the Belgian Communist Party received them.

### 我工会代表团离比利时回国

1960-03-16
第5版()
专栏：

    我工会代表团离比利时回国
    新华社布鲁塞尔14日电　由中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜率领的中国工会代表团到比利时访问了七天。他们今天乘飞机离开布鲁塞尔回国。
    代表团是应比利时总工会列日省工会组织的邀请来访问的。他们访问了列日省，比利时东部地区和安特卫普市。
    3月11日晚间，他们出席了比中协会的招待会。
    同一天，比利时共产党中央委员会的领导人接见了他们。
