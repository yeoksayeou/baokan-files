Title: Condolences to the Earthquake Victims in Agadir, Morocco Premier Zhou Enlai Sends a Message of Condolence to the Moroccan Prime Minister; Liu Ningyi Sends a Message of Condolence to the Earthquake Victims in Agadir, Morocco
Author:
Date: 1960-03-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram today to the Prime Minister of Morocco, Abdallah Ibrahim, expressing condolences for the earthquake disaster in Agadir, Morocco. The telegram is as follows:
His Excellency Abdallah Ibrahim, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Morocco:
The Government of the People's Republic of China has learned of the severe earthquake that occurred in Agadir, Morocco. On behalf of the Chinese government and the Chinese people, I would like to express my deep condolences to the government of the Kingdom of Morocco and the people of Agadir who have unfortunately suffered from the disaster.
　　　　　　　　　　Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai
　　　　　　　　　　March 3, 1960, in Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, sent a telegram today to Mahjoub Ben Seddik, Secretary-General of the Moroccan Labour Union, expressing condolences to the earthquake victims in the port of Agadir and their families.
The telegram said: "We are shocked to hear of the serious earthquake event that occurred in the port of Agadir in your country. Please convey the deep sympathy and sincere condolences of the Chinese workers to the victims and their families."

### 对摩洛哥阿加迪市地震受灾灾民表示慰问  周恩来总理致电摩洛哥首相  刘宁一致电慰问摩洛哥阿加迪市的地震受害者

1960-03-04
第5版()
专栏：

    对摩洛哥阿加迪市地震受灾灾民表示慰问
    周恩来总理致电摩洛哥首相
    刘宁一致电慰问摩洛哥阿加迪市的地震受害者
    新华社3日讯　周恩来总理今天打电报给摩洛哥首相阿卜杜拉·易卜拉欣，对摩洛哥阿加迪市遭受地震灾害表示慰问。电文如下：摩洛哥王国政府首相阿卜杜拉·易卜拉欣阁下：
    中华人民共和国政府获悉摩洛哥阿加迪市发生猛烈地震，我谨代表中国政府和中国人民向摩洛哥王国政府和不幸受灾的阿加迪市人民表示深切的慰问。
          中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                        1960年3月3日于北京
    新华社3日讯　中华全国总工会主席刘宁一今天致电摩洛哥劳工联合会总书记马久伯，向阿加迪港的地震受害者及其家属表示慰问。
    电报说：“惊悉你国阿加迪港发生了严重的地震事件，请向受害者及其家属转达中国工人的深刻的同情和亲切的慰问。”
