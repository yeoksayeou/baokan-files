Title: Tibet Autonomous Region Preparatory Committee Dispatches Spring Ploughing Inspection Teams
Author:
Date: 1960-03-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, March 25th: As spring ploughing and sowing work in the Tibet region is about to begin comprehensively, the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region dispatched two spring ploughing production inspection teams on the 23rd to conduct inspection work in the vast agricultural areas of the Lhasa River Valley, both banks of the Yarlung Tsangpo River, and other places.

The personnel participating in this spring ploughing production inspection work group include more than 30 people, including the Deputy Secretary-General and members of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region, division chiefs, and responsible cadres from various departments, the majority of whom are patriotic figures from the upper strata of Tibetan society.

Before the inspection work group departed, Panchen Erdeni, Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region, addressed all members of the inspection group. He said that now spring ploughing and sowing work in the Tibet region is about to begin comprehensively, and the broad masses of farmers are in high spirits and extremely motivated in their production, striving for the first bumper harvest year after their liberation. This is a very good and favorable situation, and these great new situations have emerged under the wise leadership and warm care of the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao. He requested that the Tibetan and Han personnel going down for inspection this time should integrate with the masses, go deep among the masses, listen to the demands and opinions of the masses from all aspects, and further strengthen unity between nationalities.

The inspection work group dispatched this time will go deep into the countryside, fields, and make contact with farmers to inspect the production plans and measures formulated by various places, as well as the situation of manure accumulation, irrigation, seed selection, improvement of agricultural tools, and spring ploughing and sowing. They will focus on understanding the good experiences gained by various places in organizing farmers for production after organizing production mutual aid teams. At the same time, the inspection work group will widely solicit opinions from the masses to understand their lives and demands.

### 西藏自治区筹委会派出春耕视察组

1960-03-26
第3版()
专栏：

    西藏自治区筹委会派出春耕视察组
    新华社拉萨25日电　西藏地区春耕播种工作即将全面开始，西藏自治区筹委会23日派出两个春耕生产工作视察组，分别赴拉萨河谷、雅鲁藏布江两岸等地的广大农业区进行视察工作。
    参加这次春耕生产视察工作组的人员有西藏自治区筹委会副秘书长、委员、处长和各部门的负责干部等三十余人，绝大部分是西藏上层爱国人士。
    视察工作组出发前，西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼召集视察组的全体人员讲了话。他说，现在西藏地区春耕播种工作即将全面开始，广大农民群众为了争取翻身后的第一个丰收年，生产情绪高涨，干劲特别大。这是非常好的有利形势，这种大好的新形势都是在党中央和毛主席的英明领导、亲切关怀下出现的，他要求这次下去视察的藏、汉族工作人员要和群众打成一片，深入群众，从各方面倾听群众的要求和意见，并进一步加强民族之间的团结。
    这次派出的视察工作组，将深入到农村、田间和农民接触，视察各地订出的生产计划、措施以及积肥、灌溉、选种、改良农具和春耕播种情况，并着重了解各地群众组织起生产互助组以后，在组织农民进行生产方面取得的良好经验。与此同时，视察工作组还将广泛征求群众的意见，了解群众的生活和要求。
