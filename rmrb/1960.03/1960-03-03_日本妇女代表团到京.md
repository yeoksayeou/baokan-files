Title: Japanese Women's Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-03-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 1st—The Japanese Women's Delegation, invited by the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China to participate in the celebration of the 50th anniversary of International Working Women's Day, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon, led by the head of the delegation, Michiko Fujiwara. Those who welcomed them at the airport included Shen Zijiou and Cao Mengjun, members of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, and Ni Feijun, an executive member, among others.

### 日本妇女代表团到京

1960-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    日本妇女代表团到京
    新华社1日讯　应中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会邀请前来我国参加庆祝“三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年的日本妇女代表团一行八人，在团长藤原道子率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有全国妇联主席团委员沈兹九、曹孟君，执委倪斐君等人。
