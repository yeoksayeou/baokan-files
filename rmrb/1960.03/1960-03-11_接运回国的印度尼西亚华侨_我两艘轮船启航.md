Title: Ships Set Sail to Bring Back Overseas Chinese from Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1960-03-11
Page: 4

    Bringing Home Overseas Chinese from Indonesia
    Our Two Ships Set Sail
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 10th. The "Russia" and "Yakutia," two ships dispatched by our government to bring overseas Chinese back home, set sail from Zhanjiang Port this morning, heading to Indonesia again.
    The relevant departments allocated a large amount of supplies for these two ships in advance to meet the needs of the returning overseas Chinese during their journey.

### 接运回国的印度尼西亚华侨  我两艘轮船启航

1960-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    接运回国的印度尼西亚华侨
    我两艘轮船启航
    新华社广州10日电　我国政府派出接运华侨回国的“俄罗斯号”和“亚库契亚号”两艘轮船，今天上午从湛江港启航，再次开往印度尼西亚。
    有关部门事先为这两艘轮船调拨了大量物资，供归国华侨在旅途中需要。
