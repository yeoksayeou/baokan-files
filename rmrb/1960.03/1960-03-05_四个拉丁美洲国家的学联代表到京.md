Title: Student Union Representatives from Four Latin American Countries Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-03-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Student Union representatives from four Latin American countries, invited to visit China by the All-China Students' Federation, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They are: Guillermo Estévez Boero from Argentina, Ismael Milo Vibas from Cuba, Mario Raoul Bindo from Honduras, and Augusto Figueroa from Venezuela. Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, and student representatives from Beijing welcomed them at the airport. Representatives of the Brazilian Student Union, who have also been invited to visit China, will arrive in Beijing in the coming days.

### 四个拉丁美洲国家的学联代表到京

1960-03-05
第5版()
专栏：

    四个拉丁美洲国家的学联代表到京
    新华社4日讯　应中华全国学生联合会邀请来我国访问的四个拉丁美洲国家的学生联合会的代表，于今天下午乘飞机到达北京。他们是：阿根廷的吉也尔姆·爱斯代佛茨·保埃罗，古巴的依斯迈利澳·维巴斯，洪都拉斯的玛里奥·拉渥尔·滨多，委内瑞拉的阿澳古斯道·费纪罗阿。中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立和北京的学生代表前往机场欢迎他们。应邀来我国访问的巴西学生联合会代表也将于日内到达北京。
