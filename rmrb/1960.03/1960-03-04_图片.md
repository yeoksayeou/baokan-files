In the northern Kyushu coal mining area, about 5,000 unemployed miners live in poverty. Among them, 20% of the unemployed workers' families can only eat one meal a day, and some rely on fish caught from the river and wild vegetables they dig up for a living. This is one of many unfortunate families.

专栏：

    九州北部的煤矿区，失业的矿工约五千人，他们生活在贫困中。其中20％的失业工人家庭每天只能吃一顿饭，有的靠从河里打上来的鱼和挖来的野菜为生。这是许多不幸的家庭中的一个。
