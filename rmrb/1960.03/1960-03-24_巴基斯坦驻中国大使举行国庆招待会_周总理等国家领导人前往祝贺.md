Title: Pakistani Ambassador to China Holds National Day Reception; Premier Zhou and Other National Leaders Attend to Offer Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1960-03-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd -- Pakistani Ambassador to China, Malik, held a reception this evening at the Beijing Hotel to celebrate Pakistan's National Day.

    Premier Zhou Enlai attended this evening's reception. Also attending were Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yi and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Shen Junru, Chen Shutong, and Lin Feng, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu.

    Ambassador Malik first gave a toast at the reception, thanking everyone for attending. He proposed a toast: to the health of Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, to the prosperity and happiness of the Chinese people, to the health of Premier Zhou Enlai and his wife, and to world peace and mutual understanding among all nations.

    Vice Premier Chen Yi, in his toast, extended heartfelt congratulations on behalf of the Chinese government and people to the Pakistani government and people. He proposed a toast: to the friendship between the Chinese and Pakistani peoples, to the prosperity and happiness of the Pakistani people, to the unity and friendly cooperation between Asian and African countries, to peace in Asia and the world, and to the health of His Excellency President Ayub Khan of Pakistan.

    Also invited to the reception were the heads of various departments of state organs, people's organizations, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, responsible persons from Beijing, and people from all walks of life in the capital. Ambassadors and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China were also invited to the reception.

### 巴基斯坦驻中国大使举行国庆招待会  周总理等国家领导人前往祝贺

1960-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦驻中国大使举行国庆招待会
    周总理等国家领导人前往祝贺
    新华社23日讯　巴基斯坦驻中国大使马立克今天晚间在北京饭店举行招待会，庆祝巴基斯坦国庆节。
    周恩来总理出席了今晚的招待会。出席招待会的，还有国务院副总理陈毅、习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒、陈叔通、林枫，外交部副部长章汉夫等。
    马立克大使首先在会上祝酒，他感谢大家参加这个招待会。他提议：为中华人民共和国主席刘少奇的健康，为中国人民的繁荣和幸福，为周恩来总理和夫人的健康，为世界和平和世界各国间的相互了解，干杯。
    陈毅副总理在祝酒中，代表中国政府和人民向巴基斯坦政府和人民表示衷心祝贺，他提议：为中巴两国人民间的友谊，为巴基斯坦人民昌盛和幸福，为亚非国家之间的团结和友好合作，为亚洲和世界和平，为巴基斯坦总统阿尤布汗阁下的健康，干杯。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有国家机关各部门、各人民团体的负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领，北京市的负责人和首都各方面的人士。各国驻中国的使节和外交官员也应邀出席了招待会。
