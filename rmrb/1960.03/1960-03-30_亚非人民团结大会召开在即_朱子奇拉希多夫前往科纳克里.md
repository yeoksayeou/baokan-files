Title: Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference About to Open: Zhu Ziqi and Rashidov Head to Conakry
Author: 
Date: 1960-03-30
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 29th: The Chinese secretary of the Secretariat of the Asian-African People's Solidarity Council, Zhu Ziqi, and the Soviet secretary, Rashidov, left Cairo this morning for Conakry. They will participate in the Second Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference scheduled to be held there on April 11.
	Secretaries from Cameroon, Uganda, and Japan have already left for Conakry.
	Secretary-General Sibai, as well as the secretaries from India and the United Arab Republic, will leave for Conakry on March 30.

### 亚非人民团结大会召开在即  朱子奇拉希多夫前往科纳克里

1960-03-30
第6版()
专栏：

    亚非人民团结大会召开在即
    朱子奇拉希多夫前往科纳克里
    新华社开罗29日电　亚非人民团结理事会书记处中国书记朱子奇和苏联书记拉希多夫在今天上午离开开罗前往科纳克里。他们将参加预定4月11日在那里举行的第二届亚非人民团结大会。
    喀麦隆、乌干达和日本的书记已经前往科纳克里。
    总书记西巴伊以及印度和阿联的书记将在3月30日前往科纳克里。
