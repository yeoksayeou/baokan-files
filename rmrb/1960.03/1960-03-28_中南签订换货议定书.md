Title: China and Yugoslavia Sign Trade Protocol
Author:
Date: 1960-03-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, March 25th. The trade delegations of China and Yugoslavia signed a trade protocol in Belgrade today for 1960, and exchanged notes on the settlement of Yugoslavia's trade deficit in 1959.

The trade protocol stipulates that this year China will export soybeans, tung oil, tin and other mineral products, silk and silk fabrics, tea and daily necessities to Yugoslavia. Yugoslavia will export non-ferrous and ferrous metal products, pulp and rayon to China.

The trade volume between the two sides is the same as last year, i.e., 1.25 million pounds each for imports and exports.

### 中南签订换货议定书

1960-03-28
第5版()
专栏：

    中南签订换货议定书
    新华社贝尔格莱德25日电　中国和南斯拉夫两国贸易代表团今天在贝尔格莱德签订了1960年换货议定书，并就南斯拉夫方面清偿1959年贸易逆差进行了换文。
    换货议定书规定，今年中国将向南斯拉夫输出大豆、桐油、锡及其他矿产品、丝及丝织品、茶叶及日用百货等。南斯拉夫将向中国输出有色及黑色金属制品、纸浆及人造纤维等。
    双方贸易额和去年相同，即各进出口一百二十五万英镑。
