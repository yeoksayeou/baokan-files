Title: Polish Government Delegation Arrives in US for Visit
Author:
Date: 1960-03-26
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, March 25th. According to a report by the Polish Press Agency, the Polish government delegation, headed by Polish Deputy Prime Minister Jaroszewicz, arrived in the United States on the evening of the 23rd for a two-week visit at the invitation of the US government. The delegation will visit industrial and trade centers in the United States, meet with US officials, and learn about the American economic life.

### 波政府代表团抵美访问

1960-03-26
第5版()
专栏：

    波政府代表团抵美访问
    新华社25日讯　据波兰通讯社报道，以波兰部长会议副主席雅罗谢维奇为首的波兰政府代表团应美国政府的邀请23日晚上抵达美国作为期两周的访问。代表团将参观美国的工业和贸易中心，同美国官方人士会见和了解美国的经济生活。
