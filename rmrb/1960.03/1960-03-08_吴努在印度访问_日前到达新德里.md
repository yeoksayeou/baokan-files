Title: U Nu Visits India, Arrives in New Delhi Recently
Author:
Date: 1960-03-08
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 7th. U Nu, chairman of the Burmese Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League, who is currently visiting India, arrived in New Delhi on the 6th from the Buddhist holy site of Sarnath. Indian Prime Minister Nehru and some members of parliament welcomed him at the station.

U Nu arrived in Calcutta on March 1st from Rangoon. He then visited the Indian Buddhist holy sites of Gaya and Sarnath, among other places.

### 吴努在印度访问  日前到达新德里

1960-03-08
第6版()
专栏：

    吴努在印度访问
    日前到达新德里
    新华社新德里7日电　目前在印度访问的缅甸廉洁的反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努在6日从佛教圣地鹿野苑到达新德里。印度总理尼赫鲁和一些议员曾到车站欢迎他。
    吴努是在3月1日从仰光到达加尔各答的。随后他访问了印度佛教圣地伽耶和鹿野苑等地。
