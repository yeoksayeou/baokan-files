Title: Selected Poems of Martyr Ling Wenming
Author:
Date: 1960-03-28
Page: 8

    Selected Poems of Martyr Ling Wenming
    Ode to Cooperativization
    Another year of cooperativization has come,
    Double happiness keeps adding on,
    Distant mountains and ridges have no wasteland,
    Every ridge and slope is filled with fertile fields.
       (1956)
    Red Flag Flying on Jinggang Mountain
    Red flag flying on Jinggang Mountain,
    The people of Suichuan are in high spirits,
    Men, women, old and young are running the commune,
    Beating drums and gongs to send good news.
        (1958.9)
    The East Wind Blows Ceaselessly
    The east wind blows ceaselessly,
    Happy flowers bloom with smiling faces,
    The whole county has established a large commune,
    The whole county is one big family.
        (1958.9)
    The Whole Party and All the People Leap Forward Together
    The People's Commune is a bridge,
    Built on the road to happiness,
    The whole Party and all the people leap forward together,
    Cheering and drumming towards paradise.
      (1958.9)
    Dig Ore to Refine Good Steel
    There is no shortage of ore on Dalu Ridge,
    Male and female commune members dig ore,
    Dig ore to refine good steel,
    Support the country's industrialization.
       (1958.10)
    The People's Commune Has an Early Spring
    The sun comes out and shines on the mountains,
    The high mountains and plains shine brightly,
    The People's Commune has an early spring,
    Before Qingming, transplanting rice is busy.
       (1959.10)
    Road Building Song
    Not afraid of mountains towering to the sky,
    Not afraid of rivers deep as a ten-thousand-zhang pool,
    Build good roads for cars to pass,
    Mountainous area production will greatly develop.
        (1959.10)

### 凌文明烈士诗选

1960-03-28
第8版()
专栏：

    凌文明烈士诗选
    颂合作化
    合作化来又一年，
    喜事重重往上添，
    远山远?无荒地，
    满垅满坡是良田。
       （1956）
    井冈山上红旗飘
    井冈山上红旗飘，
    遂川人民情绪高，
    男女老幼办公社，
    敲锣打鼓送喜报。
        （1958．9）
    东风阵阵吹不停
    东风阵阵吹不停，
    幸福花开笑盈盈，
    全县成立大公社，
    全县就是大家庭。
        （1958．9）
    全党全民齐跃进
    人民公社是桥梁，
    架在幸福大道上，
    全党全民齐跃进，
    欢欣鼓舞上天堂。
      （1958．9 ）
    挖来矿石炼好铁
    大垅山上矿不差，
    男女社员挖矿砂，
    挖来矿石炼好铁，
    支援国家工业化。
       （1958．10）
    人民公社阳春早
    太阳出来照山岗，
    高山平地放光芒，
    人民公社阳春早，
    清明未到插秧忙。
       （1959．10）
    修路歌
    不怕山高高半天，
    不怕河深万丈潭，
    修好公路过汽车，
    山区生产大发展。
        （1959．10）
