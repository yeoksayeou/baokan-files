Title: Commemorating the 35th Anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's Death: Shanghai, Jiangsu, and Nanjing Party and Government Leaders Visit Former Residence and Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum; Responsible Persons of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang in Beijing Pay Respects at Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall
Author:
Date: 1960-03-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, March 12th: Shanghai Party and government leaders and responsible persons of Shanghai local organizations of democratic parties paid respects to the former residence of Sun Yat-sen this morning to commemorate the 35th anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's death.

    Those who went to pay respects included Ke Qingshi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, First Secretary of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee, and Mayor of Shanghai; Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai; Liu Shuzhou, alternate secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai; and Zhao Zukang, Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang (RCCK); Chen Wangdao, Sheng Pihua, Zhou Gucheng, Wu Ruo'an, and Xie Xuetang, chairmen of the Shanghai local organizations of the China Democratic League, China National Democratic Construction Association, Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, China Association for Promoting Democracy, and Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League; as well as Guo Jian, Director of the Shanghai Women's Federation, and responsible persons of the China Welfare Institute.

    The CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee, the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), and the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the RCCK presented wreaths.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, March 12th: Party and government leaders of Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City, and responsible persons of local organizations of various democratic parties, went to the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum today to pay respects and commemorate the 35th anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's death.

    Those who participated in the visit included Zhou Yifeng, Vice Governor of Jiangsu Province; Chen Yang, Secretary of the CPC Nanjing Municipal Committee and Mayor of Nanjing; Li Leping, Vice Chairman of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the CPPCC; Liu Shuxun and Wang Zhaoquan, Deputy Mayors of Nanjing; Xu Meifeng, Vice Chairman of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the CPPCC; and responsible persons of democratic parties and people's organizations in Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City, as well as members of the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum Management Committee, totaling more than 30 people.

    At 9:00 a.m., the ceremony of paying respects at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum was held in the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall. Vice Governor Zhou Yifeng, Vice Chairman Li Leping, Mayor Chen Yang, and Vice Chairman Xu Meifeng, on behalf of the Jiangsu Provincial People's Committee, the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the CPPCC, the Nanjing Municipal People's Committee, and the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the CPPCC, respectively, presented wreaths in front of the statue of Mr. Sun Yat-sen. Subsequently, all those who paid respects walked around the tomb of Mr. Sun Yat-sen.

Xinhua News Agency, March 12th: Today is the 35th anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's death. Cai Tingkai and Xiong Kewu, Vice Chairmen of the RCCK, Wang Kunlun, Liu Wenhui, Wu Maosun, Shao Lizi, Central Standing Committee members in Beijing, and responsible persons and cadres of various departments of the RCCK Central Committee, totaling more than 80 people, went to the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall at Biyun Temple in the Western Hills outside Beijing this morning to pay respects.

    At 10:00 a.m. sharp, a ceremony of paying respects was held at the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall. Cai Tingkai presented flowers to the portrait of Mr. Sun Yat-sen, and all those who paid respects stood in silence and paid their respects.

    After the ceremony, everyone also viewed the cenotaph of Mr. Sun Yat-sen and the exhibition room of his revolutionary deeds.

### 纪念孙中山三十五周年忌辰  上海、江苏、南京党政领导人瞻谒故居和中山陵  民革在京负责人晋谒中山纪念堂

1960-03-13
第2版()
专栏：

    纪念孙中山三十五周年忌辰
    上海、江苏、南京党政领导人瞻谒故居和中山陵
    民革在京负责人晋谒中山纪念堂
    新华社上海12日电　上海市党政领导人和民主党派上海地方组织的负责人，今天上午瞻谒了中山故居，纪念孙中山先生逝世三十五周年。
    前往瞻谒的，有中共中央政治局委员、中共上海市委第一书记、上海市市长柯庆施，中共上海市委书记处书记上海市副市长曹荻秋，中共上海市委书记处候补书记、上海市副市长刘述周和民革上海市委主任委员赵祖康，民盟、民建、农工、民进、台盟的上海地方组织主任委员陈望道、盛丕华、周谷城、吴若安、谢雪堂，以及上海市妇联主任郭建和中国福利会的负责人等。
    中共上海市委、上海市人民委员会、政协上海市委和民革上海市委等单位都敬献了花圈。
    新华社南京12日电　江苏省和南京市党政负责人和各民主党派地方组织负责人，今天到中山陵谒陵，纪念孙中山先生逝世三十五周年。
    参加谒陵的，有江苏省副省长周一峰，中共南京市委书记、南京市市长陈扬，政协江苏省委员会副主席李乐平，南京市副市长刘树勋、王昭铨，政协南京市委员会副主席徐美峰和江苏省、南京市各民主党派各人民团体负责人以及中山陵园管理委员会委员等共三十余人。
    上午九时，在中山灵堂内举行了谒陵仪式。周一峰副省长、李乐平副主席和陈扬市长、徐美峰副主席，分别代表江苏省人民委员会、政协江苏省委员会和南京市人民委员会、政协南京市委员会，在孙中山先生座像前敬献了花圈。随后，全体谒陵人员在孙中山先生灵寝绕行一周。
    新华社12日讯　今天是孙中山先生逝世三十五周年纪念日，民革副主席蔡廷锴、熊克武，在京中央常务委员王昆仑、刘文辉、吴茂荪、邵力子及民革中央机关各部门负责人、干部等八十多人，上午前往京郊西山碧云寺晋谒孙中山先生纪念堂。
    十时正，在孙中山先生纪念堂举行了参谒仪式，由蔡廷锴向孙中山先生遗像献花，全体参谒的人肃立致敬。
    参谒完毕后，大家还瞻仰了孙中山先生衣冠冢和生平革命事迹展览室。
