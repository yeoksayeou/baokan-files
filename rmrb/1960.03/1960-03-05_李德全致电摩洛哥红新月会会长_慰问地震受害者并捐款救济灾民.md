Title: Li Dequan Sends a Message of Condolence to the President of the Moroccan Red Crescent, Expressing Sympathy for Earthquake Victims and Donating Funds to Aid Survivors
Author:
Date: 1960-03-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, March 4th. Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, today sent a message to Princess Malika, President of the Moroccan Red Crescent, expressing sympathy for the earthquake victims in Agadir and donating RMB 50,000 yuan to help with disaster relief. The telegram said: "We were shocked to learn that Agadir, Morocco, suffered a severe earthquake, resulting in thousands of deaths and injuries. Please convey our deepest sympathy and condolences to the victims of the disaster. We are also remitting RMB 50,000 yuan to help the victims alleviate their suffering."

### 李德全致电摩洛哥红新月会会长  慰问地震受害者并捐款救济灾民

1960-03-05
第5版()
专栏：

    李德全致电摩洛哥红新月会会长
    慰问地震受害者并捐款救济灾民
    新华社4日讯　中国红十字会总会会长李德全今天致电摩洛哥红新月会会长马利卡公主，对阿加迪市的地震受害者表示慰问，并捐款人民币五万元帮助救济灾民。电报说：“惊悉摩洛哥阿加迪市遭受猛烈地震，死伤数千人。请代向受难灾民转致深切的同情和慰问。另汇上人民币五万元，以助灾民减轻他们的痛苦。”
