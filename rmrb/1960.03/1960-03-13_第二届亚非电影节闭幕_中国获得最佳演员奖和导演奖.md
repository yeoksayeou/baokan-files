Title: Second Asian-African Film Festival Concludes; China Wins Best Actor and Director Awards
Author:
Date: 1960-03-13
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 12th. The Second Asian-African Film Festival concluded on the evening of the 10th in Cairo. Films from the Soviet Union, China, India, and the United Arab Republic won awards at the festival. Chinese films received the Best Actor and Director awards.

    The Soviet color feature film "Prince of Samarkand" won the highest award – the Golden Eagle Award.

    Yang Likun, the female lead in the Chinese film "Five Golden Flowers," and Magida, the female lead in the UAR film "Qais and Laila," won the Best Actress awards. Sivaji Ganesan, the lead actor in the Indian film "Hero Kattabomman," won the Best Actor award. Wang Jia-yi, the director of "Five Golden Flowers," won the Best Director award. They were all awarded Silver Eagle medals. Indian musician Rannatan also received a medal. He was responsible for the music in the film "Hero Kattabomman."

    The Indian documentary "Taj Mahal" won the documentary award.

    The film festival opened on February 29th. Films from fourteen countries, including China, the Soviet Union, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, India, Indonesia, Japan, Morocco, and the UAR, participated in the festival.

### 第二届亚非电影节闭幕  中国获得最佳演员奖和导演奖

1960-03-13
第3版()
专栏：

    第二届亚非电影节闭幕
    中国获得最佳演员奖和导演奖
    新华社开罗12日电　第二届亚非电影节10日晚在开罗举行了闭幕式。苏联、中国、印度和阿拉伯联合共和国的影片在电影节中获奖。中国影片获得了最佳演员和导演奖。
    苏联彩色故事片“萨马尔汗王子”获得了最高奖——金鹰奖章。
    中国影片“五朵金花”的女主角杨丽坤和阿联影片“克斯和莱拉”的女主角马吉达获得了最佳女演员奖。印度影片“英雄卡塔本曼”的主角西瓦季·加奈桑，获得了最佳男演员奖。“五朵金花”的导演王家乙获得了最佳导演奖。他们都被授予银鹰奖章。印度音乐家兰纳坦也获得了一枚奖章。他是负责“英雄卡塔本曼”一片的音乐的。
    印度纪录片“泰吉·玛哈尔陵”获得了纪录片奖。
    电影节是2月29日开幕的。参加这届电影节的有：中国、苏联、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、越南民主共和国、印度、印度尼西亚、日本、摩洛哥和阿联等十四个国家的影片。
