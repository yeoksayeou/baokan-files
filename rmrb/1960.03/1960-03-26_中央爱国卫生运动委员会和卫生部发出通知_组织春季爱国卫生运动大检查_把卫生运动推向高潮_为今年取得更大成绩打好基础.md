Title: The Central Patriotic Health Campaign Committee and the Ministry of Health Issue a Notice: Organize a Spring Patriotic Health Campaign General Inspection to Push the Health Campaign to a Climax and Lay a Good Foundation for Achieving Greater Success This Year
Author:
Date: 1960-03-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 25th: The Central Patriotic Health Campaign Committee and the Ministry of Health of the People's Republic of China issued a "Notice on Organizing a Spring Patriotic Health Campaign General Inspection" to relevant departments of provinces, cities, and autonomous regions.

The notice said: This year is a year of greater, more comprehensive, and continuous leap forward in our country's socialist construction. The production increase and conservation movement centered on technological innovation and technological revolution is developing more vigorously. The situation on the health front is also extremely good. All localities have been seizing the time to arrange new pest elimination and disease prevention work, and have been doing extensive publicity to deeply mobilize the masses, cooperate with the production movement, and with the great spirit of transforming customs and habits and transforming the world, vigorously engage in health work. At present, in Beijing, Shanghai, Hunan and other places, an unprecedented, large-scale, high-profile, all-hands-on, large-scale elimination of the four pests (rats, bedbugs, flies, and mosquitoes) and disease eradication patriotic health campaign has been launched, and a competition upsurge of learning from the advanced, comparing with the advanced, catching up with the advanced, and helping the backward has been set off. Now this movement is expanding to the whole country. In order to review the achievements of the movement, exchange work experience, push the movement to a climax, create greater and more outstanding achievements, greet the "May 1st" International Labor Day and the convening of the Hero Conference on the Cultural and Educational Front, and lay a good foundation for achieving great achievements in pest elimination and disease prevention work this year, it is now decided to organize a national patriotic health campaign general inspection from early to late April.

The notice said: This inspection should focus on the situation of the health campaign combined with spring industrial and agricultural production, as well as the extensive and in-depth development of work, study, technological innovation, and technological revolution; the situation of eliminating schistosomiasis and other four major parasitic diseases, endemic diseases, and preventing and controlling spring and summer infectious diseases; and the development of health work in collective production and collective life. The focus of rural inspections should be on combining spring plowing and fertilizer accumulation with the treatment of feces and garbage and mosquito and fly breeding grounds, hygiene of pig raising and fertilizer accumulation, and hygiene work in canteens and nurseries. Water conservancy and transportation construction sites should focus on inspecting diet, environment, personal hygiene, arrangement of work and rest, and prevention and treatment of spring and summer epidemics. The focus of cities and industrial and mining enterprises should be on measures to prevent epidemics, improve environmental sanitation, eliminate the root causes of the four pests, timely measures to eliminate the four pests, as well as occupational health, safety protection, school health, the catering industry, and the hygiene of the food industry. Whether in urban or rural areas, attention should be paid to inspecting the situation of mobilizing the whole people and mobilizing the masses, inspecting the residents' hygiene habits and the growth of new habits of paying attention to hygiene and loving cleanliness, as well as health technical guidance and the combination of health departments with mass organizations and other relevant departments to jointly carry out work.

The notice said that this inspection should combine broad coverage with in-depth focus. At the same time, each inspection team must carry out its work under the leadership of the local party and government.

### 中央爱国卫生运动委员会和卫生部发出通知  组织春季爱国卫生运动大检查  把卫生运动推向高潮　为今年取得更大成绩打好基础

1960-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中央爱国卫生运动委员会和卫生部发出通知
    组织春季爱国卫生运动大检查
    把卫生运动推向高潮　为今年取得更大成绩打好基础
    据新华社25日讯　中央爱国卫生运动委员会、中华人民共和国卫生部向各省、市、自治区有关部门发出“关于组织春季爱国卫生运动大检查的通知”。
    通知说：今年是我国社会主义建设更大更全面持续跃进的一年，以技术革新、技术革命为中心的增产节约运动正在更加蓬勃的发展。卫生战线的形势也极为良好。各地都抓紧布置了新的除害灭病工作，并大做宣传，深入发动群众，配合生产运动，鼓起冲天干劲，以移风易俗，改造世界的伟大气概，大搞卫生工作。目前，在北京、上海、湖南等地已经开展起一个规模空前的大张旗鼓，大造声势，人人动手，大除四害（老鼠、臭虫、苍蝇、蚊子），大灭疾病的爱国卫生运动，并掀起了学先进、比先进、赶先进、帮后进的竞赛热潮。现在这一运动正在向全国范围内扩展。为了检阅运动成绩，交流工作经验，把运动推向高潮，以创造更大更优越的成绩，迎接“五一”国际劳动节和文教战线群英大会的召开，并为在今年内取得除害灭病工作的巨大成绩打好基础，现决定于4月上旬至下旬组织一次全国性的爱国卫生运动大检查。
    通知说：这次检查应着重于卫生运动结合春季工农业生产以及工作、学习、技术革新、技术革命广泛深入开展的情况；消灭血吸虫病等四大寄生虫病、地方病和防治春夏季传染病的情况，以及集体生产、集体生活中的卫生工作开展情况等。农村的检查重点，应放在春耕积肥结合处理粪便垃圾及蚊蝇孳生地，养猪积肥卫生，食堂、托儿所的卫生工作。水利交通工地着重检查饮食、环境、个人卫生、劳逸安排、春夏季流行病的防治等。城市和工矿企业的重点在于预防流行病的措施，改善环境卫生，消灭四害的治本工作，及时扑灭四害的措施以及劳动卫生、安全防护、学校卫生、饮食行业、食品工业的卫生等。不论城市或农村，均应注意检查全民动员和群众发动的情况，检查居民的卫生习惯和讲卫生爱清洁的新风气的增长情况，以及卫生技术指导和卫生部门与群众团体等有关部门相结合共同开展工作的情况。
    通知说，这次检查要作到面广和重点深入相结合，同时，各检查团必须在当地党政领导下进行工作。
