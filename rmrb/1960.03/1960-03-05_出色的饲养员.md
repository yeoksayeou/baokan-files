Author:
Date: 1960-03-05
Page: 5

    Outstanding Swineherd
    Vera Savchenko's name is well-known in the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic. This year, she proposed a guarantee to produce 75,000 kilograms of pork and reduce the cost per 100 kilograms by 60%. In January, she already delivered 17,500 kilograms of pork to the state, more than double the total for the entire previous year.

### 出色的饲养员

1960-03-05
第5版()
专栏：

    出色的饲养员
    维拉·沙温柯的名字，在苏联乌克兰是人所共知的。今年她提出生产七万五千公斤猪肉和每百公斤的成本减少60％的保证。1月份她已经交给国家一万七千五百公斤猪肉，为去年全年的二倍多。
