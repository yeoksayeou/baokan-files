Title: Chinese Women's Federation Representative Visits Australia
Author:
Date: 1960-03-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sydney: Zhao Feng, Executive Committee member of the All-China Women's Federation, arrived in Sydney by plane on the morning of the 17th at the invitation of the Australian International Women's Day Committee. She was warmly welcomed by members of the International Women's Day Committee, as well as representatives from the Australian Communist Party, the Australia-China Association, some trade unions, the New South Wales Peace Committee, and the Eureka Youth League.

### 我妇联代表到澳大利亚访问

1960-03-20
第4版()
专栏：

    我妇联代表到澳大利亚访问
    据新华社讯　悉尼消息：中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会执行委员赵烽应澳大利亚国际妇女节委员会的邀请17日上午乘飞机到达悉尼。她受到国际妇女节委员会委员们以及澳大利亚共产党、澳中协会、一些工会、新南威尔斯和平委员会和攸利卡青年团等团体的代表的热烈欢迎。
