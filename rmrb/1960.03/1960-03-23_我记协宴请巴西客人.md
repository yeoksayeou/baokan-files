Title: All-China Journalists Association Hosts Banquet for Brazilian Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-03-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd. The All-China Journalists Association hosted a banquet tonight for three Brazilian journalists who arrived in China yesterday for a visit. They are: Paulo da Silveira, President of the Brazilian "Ultima Hora" newspaper, Radoyko Guimarães, a reporter from "Newspaper United," and Octavio Malta, Editor-in-Chief of "Ultima Hora."

Also invited to the banquet were Brazilian experts Mr. and Mrs. Carvalho.

The banquet was presided over by Wang Yi, First Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Journalists Association. Among those present at the banquet were Li Wu and Li Bingquan, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the All-China Journalists Association, Zhu Boshen, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Shao Zonghan, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chang Zhiqing, Editor-in-Chief of Ta Kung Pao, and people from the capital's press circles.

The three Brazilian journalists arrived in Beijing on the 21st at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association.

### 我记协宴请巴西客人

1960-03-23
第5版()
专栏：

    我记协宴请巴西客人
    新华社22日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会今晚设宴招待昨天到我国访问的三位巴西新闻工作者。他们是：巴西“最后一点钟报”社长保罗·达西尔维拉，“报刊联营”记者拉多依科·吉马赖恩斯和“最后一点钟报”总编辑奥克塔维奥·马尔塔。
    应邀出席宴会的还有巴西专家卡尔瓦罗夫妇。
    宴会由中华全国新闻工作者协会书记处第一书记王揖主持。出席宴会的有新闻工作者协会书记处书记李伍、李炳泉，中国人民对外文化协会副秘书长朱伯深，外交部新闻司副司长邵宗汉，以及大公报总编辑常芝青和首都新闻界人士。
    巴西三位新闻工作者是应中华全国新闻工作者协会的邀请在21日到达北京的。
