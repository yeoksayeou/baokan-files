Title: Sukarno Suspends the Work of Parliamentarians
Author:
Date: 1960-03-07
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 5th. According to Indonesian Radio tonight, President Sukarno decided today to suspend the tasks and work of members of Parliament. He will "restore" the activities of Parliament in the short term.

　　In his presidential decision, Sukarno said that the decision was made because Parliament had not been able to "cooperate" with the government as expected. He made the decision after listening to the briefing at today's cabinet meeting. It was previously reported that in recent parliamentary sessions, most members of parliament expressed dissatisfaction with the government's proposed 1960 draft budget, making it difficult to pass the budget.

### 苏加诺停止议员的工作

1960-03-07
第5版()
专栏：

    苏加诺停止议员的工作
    新华社雅加达5日电　据印度尼西亚电台今晚报道，苏加诺总统今天决定停止国会议员的任务和工作。他将在短期内“恢复”国会的活动。
    苏加诺在他的总统决定中说，作出这个决定是由于国会没有能够像期望的那样同政府“合作”。他是在听取今天内阁会议的说明以后作出这个决定的。前已报道，在最近的国会会议中，大多数议员对政府提出的1960年预算草案表示不满，以致通过预算就有了困难。
