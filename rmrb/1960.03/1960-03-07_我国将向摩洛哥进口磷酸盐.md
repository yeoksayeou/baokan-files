Title: China to Import Phosphates from Morocco
Author:
Date: 1960-03-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, 4th - According to a contract signed here this afternoon, China will import 600,000 tons of phosphates from Morocco.

Zhang Tingyu, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Morocco, signed the contract on behalf of the China National Import and Export Corporation. The Moroccan side was represented by an official from the Moroccan Phosphate Office.

This contract is part of the trade stipulated in the 1959-1960 trade agreement signed between China and Morocco in Beijing last September.

### 我国将向摩洛哥进口磷酸盐

1960-03-07
第6版()
专栏：

    我国将向摩洛哥进口磷酸盐
    新华社拉巴特4日电　根据今天下午在这里签订的一项合同，中国将从摩洛哥进口六十万吨磷酸盐。
    中国驻摩洛哥大使馆商务参赞张廷禹代表中国进出口公司签署这项合同。摩洛哥方面签署的是摩洛哥磷酸盐办公室的代表。
    这项合同是中国和摩洛哥去年9月在北京签订的1959年至1960年贸易协定规定的贸易的一部分。
