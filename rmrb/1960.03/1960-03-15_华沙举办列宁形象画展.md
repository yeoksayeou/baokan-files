Title: Lenin Image Exhibition Held in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1960-03-15
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw. An exhibition of propaganda posters featuring the image of Lenin solemnly opened on the 10th at the Lenin Museum in Warsaw. The exhibition is being held to commemorate the upcoming 90th anniversary of Lenin's birth.

Approximately one hundred Polish and foreign propaganda posters related to the image of Lenin are on display in the museum's various exhibition halls.

### 华沙举办列宁形象画展

1960-03-15
第5版()
专栏：

    华沙举办列宁形象画展
    新华社华沙电　列宁形象宣传画展览会10日在华沙列宁博物馆隆重开幕。展览会是为纪念即将到来的列宁诞辰九十周年而举办的。
    在博物馆的各展览厅里大约展出了一百幅有关列宁形象的波兰宣传画和外国宣传画。
