	International Briefs

	Nasser to Visit India
	Cairo Dispatch: UAR President Nasser left Cairo on the 29th for New Delhi. He will pay official visits to India and Pakistan. Accompanying Nasser on the visit are UAR Foreign Minister Fawzi, Minister of State for Presidential Affairs Sabri, and Minister of Urban and Rural Affairs of the Northern Region, Awadallah.

	Iran and Afghanistan Issue Joint Communiqué
	Tehran Dispatch: Iran and Afghanistan issued a joint communiqué here on the 26th regarding the official visit of Afghan Prime Minister Daud to Tehran.
	The communiqué stated, "The Afghan Prime Minister and the Iranian Prime Minister exchanged views on issues related to relations between the two countries and the international situation. The Afghan Prime Minister elaborated and reiterated Afghanistan's policy of neutrality and its policy of not participating in military alliances."

### 国际简讯

1960-03-30
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    纳赛尔赴印度访问
    开罗讯　阿联总统纳赛尔29日离开开罗前往新德里。他将在印度和巴基斯坦作正式访问。陪同纳赛尔前往访问的有阿联外交部长法齐、负责总统事务的国务部长萨布里和北部地区城乡事务部长阿瓦达拉。
    伊朗和阿富汗发表联合公报
    德黑兰讯　伊朗和阿富汗26日在这里发表了关于阿富汗首相达乌德在德黑兰进行正式访问的联合公报。
    公报中说，“阿富汗首相和伊朗首相就有关两国之间的关系问题和国际局势问题交换了意见。阿富汗首相阐述并重申了阿富汗所奉行的中立政策和不参加军事同盟的政策。”
