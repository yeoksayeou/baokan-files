Title: Hand-Cranked Sheep Shearing Machine
Author:
Date: 1960-03-22
Page: 2

    Hand-Cranked Sheep Shearing Machine
    Ma Xibatu, a Mongolian worker at the Tiemu Tool Repair Shop in Xianghuang Banner, Shangdu, Inner Mongolia, recently successfully created a hand-cranked sheep shearing machine. With this machine, each worker can shear the wool of 300 to 400 sheep per day, increasing efficiency by about ten times compared to manual shearing.
    This hand-cranked sheep shearing machine, which can simultaneously drive four shears, is small in size, weighing only 49.5 catties, and has low manufacturing costs, making it suitable for use in rural and pastoral areas without electricity.
                              (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 手摇式剪羊毛机

1960-03-22
第2版()
专栏：

    手摇式剪羊毛机
    内蒙古商都镶黄旗铁木工具修配厂蒙古族工人马希巴图，最近创造成功一台手摇式剪羊毛机。用这个机器，每个劳动力每天能剪三百只到四百只羊的毛，比用手工剪毛效率提高十倍左右。
    这台能同时带动四把剪刀的手摇式剪羊毛机，体积不大，只有四十九点五市斤重，制造成本低，适合无电源的农村和牧区使用。
                              （据新华社讯）
