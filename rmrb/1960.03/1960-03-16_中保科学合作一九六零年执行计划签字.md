Title: Sino-Bulgarian Scientific Cooperation 1960 Implementation Plan Signed
Author:
Date: 1960-03-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 15th: The signing ceremony of the 1960 implementation plan for scientific cooperation between the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences was held in Beijing today.

    Signing on behalf of the Chinese Academy of Sciences was Wu Youxun, head of the Chinese Academy of Sciences delegation and Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. Signing on behalf of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences was Corresponding Academician Krestanov, head of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences delegation and Vice President of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences.

    Attending the signing ceremony were Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Panchevski, Bulgarian Ambassador to China, all members of both delegations, and Marinov, Third Secretary of the Bulgarian Embassy in China.

    In the evening, Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, held a banquet to bid farewell to the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences delegation.

### 中保科学合作一九六零年执行计划签字

1960-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    中保科学合作一九六零年执行计划签字
    新华社15日讯　中国科学院和保加利亚科学院科学合作1960年执行计划签字仪式，今天在北京举行。
    代表中国科学院签字的是中国科学院代表团团长、中国科学院副院长吴有训，代表保加利亚科学院签字的是保加利亚科学院代表团团长、保加利亚科学院副院长克雷斯坦诺夫通讯院士。
    参加签字仪式的，有中国科学院院长郭沫若，保加利亚驻中国大使潘切夫斯基，双方代表团的全体成员，保加利亚驻华大使馆三等秘书马里诺夫等。
    晚上，中国科学院副院长吴有训举行宴会欢送保加利亚科学院代表团。
