Title: Accident-Hit Coal Mine in Germany Resumes Production
Author:
Date: 1960-03-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 8th: The "Karl Marx" coal mine in Zwickau, German Democratic Republic, where an unfortunate explosion occurred, resumed production yesterday (7th).

Prior to the resumption of production, the Party, government, and trade union organizations mobilized workers to conduct a comprehensive and careful inspection of production safety issues. Grigore, Chairman of the government committee responsible for handling this incident and First Deputy Chairman of the State Planning Commission, and Pantlelt, Economic Secretary of the Socialist Unity Party's Karl Marx District, personally went down into the mine shafts to inspect. During the shutdown, the miners actively assisted the government in various related tasks and requested an early resumption of production.

### 德国发生事故的煤矿恢复生产

1960-03-09
第5版()
专栏：

    德国发生事故的煤矿恢复生产
    新华社柏林8日电　德意志民主共和国发生不幸爆炸事件的兹维考城“卡尔·马克思”煤矿已经在昨天（7日）开始恢复生产。
    在恢复生产之前，党、政府和工会组织发动工人就有关生产安全问题进行了全面和仔细的检查。负责处理这次事件的政府委员会主席、国家计划委员会第一副主席格里哥尔和统一社会党卡尔·马克思专区经济书记潘特莱特亲自下到矿坑中视察。矿工们在停工期间曾积极协助政府进行各项有关工作，并且要求早日恢复生产。
