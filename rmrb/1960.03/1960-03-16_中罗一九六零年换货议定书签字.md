Title: Sino-Romanian 1960 Trade Protocol Signed
Author:
Date: 1960-03-16
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 15th: The protocol between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Romanian People's Republic on the exchange of goods and payments for 1960 was signed in Beijing today. Negotiations were conducted in an atmosphere of friendly cooperation.

    According to the protocol, the total value of goods exchanged between the two countries in 1960 will increase compared to last year. China will supply Romania with tin, borax, mercury, ferromolybdenum, refined antimony, rice, soybeans, and mechanical products, etc.; Romania will supply China with power station equipment, cement equipment, drilling equipment, tractors, trucks, blowers, and petroleum products, etc.

    Lin Haiyun, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, signed the protocol on behalf of the Chinese side. Anna Toma, First Vice Minister of Trade of the Romanian People's Republic, signed the protocol on behalf of the Romanian side.

    Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Jia Shi, Director of the Second Bureau; Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet and European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others. On the Romanian side were Romania's ambassador to China, Zaharescu, engineer Ciobanu, chairman of the Chamber of Commerce of the Romanian People's Republic, and all members of the Romanian People's Republic government trade delegation.

### 中罗一九六零年换货议定书签字

1960-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    中罗一九六零年换货议定书签字
    新华社15日讯　中华人民共和国政府和罗马尼亚人民共和国政府关于1960年交换货物和付款议定书，今天在北京签字。谈判是在友好合作的气氛中进行的。
    根据议定书规定，1960年两国交换货物的总额将比去年有所增长。中国将供应罗马尼亚锡、硼砂、水银、钼铁、纯锑、大米、大豆和机械产品等；罗马尼亚将供应中国电站设备、水泥设备、钻探设备、拖拉机、卡车、鼓风机和石油产品等。
    代表中国方面在议定书上签字的，是中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长林海云。代表罗马尼亚方面在议定书上签字的，是罗马尼亚人民共和国贸易部第一副部长安娜·托马。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有对外贸易部副部长雷任民，第二局局长贾石，外交部苏欧司副司长陈伯清等；罗马尼亚方面有罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华大使查哈勒斯库，罗马尼亚人民共和国商会主席乔巴努工程师和罗马尼亚人民共和国政府贸易代表团全体人员。
