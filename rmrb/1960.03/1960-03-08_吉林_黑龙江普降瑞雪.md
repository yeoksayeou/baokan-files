Title: Auspicious Snowfall in Jilin and Heilongjiang

Author:

Date: 1960-03-08
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, 6th: Starting from 2 a.m. on the 5th, all parts of Jilin Province experienced snowfall. This is the largest and most widespread snowfall in Jilin Province since last winter. The amount of snowfall in the Baichengzi area was six to eight millimeters.

    Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 7th: Just as various parts of Heilongjiang Province were beginning to plow and sow spring wheat, a spring snow fell universally.

    The snow fell intermittently from the morning of the 5th until 2 p.m. on the 6th. The range was relatively wide, with snowfall in various counties in the Harbin area and in the Mudanjiang and Hejiang areas reaching about two millimeters, and reaching six millimeters around Mudanjiang City.

### 吉林·黑龙江普降瑞雪

1960-03-08
第3版()
专栏：

    吉林·黑龙江普降瑞雪
    据新华社长春6日电　5日早晨两点开始，吉林省各地普遍降雪。这是去冬以来，吉林省最大最普遍的一场雪。白城子地区降雪量六至八毫米。
    据新华社哈尔滨7日电  正当黑龙江省各地开犁播种春麦的时候，普遍降了一场春雪。
    这场雪从5日早晨断断续续降到6日十四点，范围比较广，哈尔滨地区各县和牡丹江、合江等地区降雪量达两毫米左右，牡丹江市周围降到六毫米。
