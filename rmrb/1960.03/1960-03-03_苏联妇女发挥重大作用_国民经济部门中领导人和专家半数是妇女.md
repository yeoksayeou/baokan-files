Title: Soviet Women Play a Significant Role; Half of Leaders and Experts in National Economic Sectors are Women
Author:
Date: 1960-03-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow Telegram: According to materials from a statistical handbook published by the Soviet Central Statistical Bureau entitled "Soviet Women," women constitute 47% of the total number of workers and staff in various sectors of the Soviet national economy. In health organizations, there are 2,613,000 women, accounting for 85% of all medical staff.

This handbook, which uses figures to illustrate the significant role Soviet women play in all aspects of national life, also points out that in various sectors of the national economy, 50% of leaders and experts, and 36% of scientific workers are women.

Soviet women actively participate in the administration of the Soviet state. For example, 366 women have been elected as deputies to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, accounting for 27% of the total number of deputies.

835,000 women have been awarded various orders and medals for their outstanding achievements in industry, agriculture, and cultural arts.

The handbook states that the total number of women in the Soviet Union is now 114,800,000.

### 苏联妇女发挥重大作用  国民经济部门中领导人和专家半数是妇女

1960-03-03
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联妇女发挥重大作用
    国民经济部门中领导人和专家半数是妇女
    新华社莫斯科电　根据苏联中央统计局出版的一本名为“苏联妇女”的统计手册的材料，在苏联国民经济各部门工作的工人和职员总人数中，妇女占47％，在卫生机关工作的妇女有二百六十一万三千人，占全体医务工作人员的85％。
    这本用数字说明苏联妇女在国家生活的各个方面所起的重大作用的手册还指出，在国民经济各部门中，50％的领导人和专家，36％的科学工作者都是妇女。
    苏联妇女积极参加了苏联国家的管理工作。例如，有三百六十六名妇女当选为苏联最高苏维埃代表，占代表总人数的27％。
    有八十三万五千名妇女由于在工业、农业和文化艺术方面的卓越成就而被授予各种勋章和奖章。
    手册说，现在苏联妇女总人数是一亿一千四百八十万人。
