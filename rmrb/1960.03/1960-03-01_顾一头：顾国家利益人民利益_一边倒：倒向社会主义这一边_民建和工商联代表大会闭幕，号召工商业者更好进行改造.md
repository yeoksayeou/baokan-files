Title: Attend to One End: Attend to the Interests of the State and the People; Lean to One Side: Lean to the Side of Socialism; The National Democratic Construction Association and All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Representative Conference Closes, Calling on Industrialists and Businessmen to Better Carry Out Transformation
Author:
Date: 1960-03-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th - The Second National Representative Conference of the China Democratic National Construction Association and the Third Member Representative Conference of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, jointly held in Beijing, closed on February 21st, after lasting more than two months.

From the preparatory meeting held from December 16th of last year to February 18th of this year, the representatives cordially talked and spoke freely through various meeting formats, large, medium, and small, successfully implementing the spirit of "gentle breeze and fine rain," resolving many ideological problems, and improving understanding. Two hundred and ninety-three representatives made oral and written speeches at the preparatory meeting. During the meeting, the representatives conducted in-depth discussions on the estimation of the changes in the industrial and commercial circles in accepting socialist transformation over the past year or so; the relationship between service and transformation; the views on self-education and self-transformation of "gentle breeze and fine rain"; the requirements and measures for the industrial and commercial circles to carry out self-education and self-transformation in the future; and the future work of the two associations, and reached a relatively consistent understanding. Based on these understandings, the conference documents were written.

Beginning on February 19th, a formal meeting was held. Huang Yanpei, Chairman of the China Democratic National Construction Association, delivered an opening speech on behalf of the two associations at the formal meeting, saying that this conference is a conference of great historical significance for the two associations, a conference of unity under the leadership of the Party, a conference of self-education for the industrial and commercial circles, and a conference to mobilize the industrial and commercial circles to actively participate in socialist construction.

Chen Shutong, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, read the conference documents at the conference on behalf of the presidium of the conference. This document called on industrialists and businessmen across the country to resolutely respond to the great call of the Eighth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Party, under the brilliant illumination of the General Line, to raise consciousness, go all out, better engage in the movement to increase production and practice economy, and better carry out political and ideological transformation, so as to make more contributions to the socialist construction of the motherland.

At the closing meeting, all the representatives unanimously approved a congratulatory telegram to Chairman Mao. The congratulatory telegram said: "We must listen to you wholeheartedly, follow the Party wholeheartedly, accept transformation wholeheartedly, and serve socialism wholeheartedly."

The conference also listened to the various reports of the two associations, adopted the constitutions and various resolutions of the two associations, and elected the new central leadership bodies of the two associations: Huang Yanpei, Chairman of the Second Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association; Vice-Chairmen: Li Zhuchen, Nan Hanchen, Sheng Pihua, Shi Fuliang, Hu Juewen, Hu Ziang, Sun Qimeng, and Guo Dihuo. Chen Shutong, Chairman of the Third Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce; Vice-Chairmen: Li Zhuchen, Xu Dixing, Sheng Pihua, Rong Yiren, Chen Jingshe, Huang Changshui, Hu Ziang, Gong Tianmin, Sha Qianli, Wu Xuezhi, Le Songsheng, Deng Wenzhao, Han Wangchen, Liu Guojun, Sun Qimeng, and Luo Shuzhang.

During the conference, Party leaders such as Liu Shaoqi and Zhu De, Vice-Chairmen of the CPC Central Committee, met with all the participants. Vice-Chairman Liu Shaoqi also held talks with the leaders of the two associations. The representatives listened to the reports by Chen Yi and Li Xiannian, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee. Li Weihan, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, made reports to the presidium of the conference, the central and executive committees of the two associations, and the representatives of family members, while Xu Bing, Deputy Minister, made a report to the conference, and Ping Jiesan, Deputy Minister, made a report to the representatives of family members. In addition, the representatives also listened to the reports of four labor models and visited the capital's new construction projects and industrial and agricultural exhibitions, and generally received a great socialist education.

The representatives unanimously believed that this conference was very lively, meticulous, and in-depth. Everyone was equivalent to entering a two-month socialist school and spending two months of rich, colorful, and comfortable collective life. The representatives expressed that they must respond to the Party's cordial care and teachings with practical actions when they return.

The负责人 [Person in charge] of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and the 负责人 [persons in charge] of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the various democratic parties, and the various people's organizations were invited to participate in the opening ceremony of the formal meeting.

### 顾一头：顾国家利益人民利益  一边倒：倒向社会主义这一边  民建和工商联代表大会闭幕，号召工商业者更好进行改造

1960-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

    顾一头：顾国家利益人民利益
    一边倒：倒向社会主义这一边
    民建和工商联代表大会闭幕，号召工商业者更好进行改造
    新华社29日讯　在北京联合举行的中国民主建国会第二次全国代表大会和中华全国工商业联合会第三届会员代表大会，历时两个多月，已在2月21日闭幕。
    大会自去年12月16日到今年2月18日举行的预备会议，通过大、中、小各种会议形式，代表们亲切谈心，畅所欲言，成功地贯彻了和风细雨的精神，解决了许多思想问题，提高了认识。有二百九十三位代表在预备会上作了口头和书面发言。会议期间，代表们就关于工商界一年多来在接受社会主义改造方面变化情况的估计；关于服务和改造的关系问题；对和风细雨的自我教育和自我改造的看法问题；关于工商界今后进行自我教育和自我改造的要求和措施问题以及关于两会今后的工作问题，进行了深入的讨论，取得了比较一致的认识。并且根据这些认识，写成了大会文件。
    2月19日起，举行正式会议，中国民主建国会主任委员黄炎培代表两会在正式会议上致开幕词说，这次大会，是两会具有重大历史意义的大会，是在党的领导下的团结的大会，是工商界进行自我教育的大会，是动员工商界积极参加社会主义建设的大会。
    中华全国工商业联合会主任委员陈叔通代表大会主席团，在大会上宣读了大会文件。这个文件号召全国工商业者，坚决响应党的八届八中全会的伟大号召，在总路线的光辉照耀下，提高自觉，鼓足干劲，更好地投入增产节约运动，更好地进行政治思想改造，为祖国的社会主义建设作出更多的贡献。
    在闭幕会上，全体代表一致通过向毛主席致敬电。致敬电中说：“我们一定要一心一意听您的话，一心一意跟着党走，一心一意接受改造，一心一意为社会主义服务”。
    大会还听取了两会的各项报告，通过了两会会章和各项决议，并且选举两会新的中央领导机构：中国民主建国会第二届中央委员会主任委员黄炎培，副主任委员李烛尘、南汉宸、盛丕华、施复亮、胡厥文、胡子昂、孙起孟、郭棣活。中华全国工商业联合会第三届执行委员会主任委员陈叔通，副主任委员李烛尘、许涤新、盛丕华、荣毅仁、陈经畲、黄长水、胡子昂、巩天民、沙千里、吴雪之、乐松生、邓文钊、韩望尘、刘国钧、孙起孟、罗叔章。
    在会议期间，中共中央副主席刘少奇、朱德等党的领导人接见了全体与会人员，刘少奇副主席并与两会的领导人进行了谈话。代表们听了中共中央政治局委员陈毅、李先念的报告。中共中央统一战线工作部部长李维汉先后向大会主席团和两会中、执委员会以及家属代表们作了报告，副部长徐冰向大会作了报告，副部长平杰三向家属代表们作了报告。此外，代表们还听了四位劳动模范的报告，参观了首都的新建设、工农业展览会等，普遍受到了很大的社会主义教育。
    代表们一致认为，这次大会开得十分生动活泼，细致深入。大家等于进了两个月的社会主义学校，过了两个月的丰富多采、心情舒畅的集体生活。代表们表示：回去一定要以实际行动来回答党的亲切关怀和教导。
    中共中央统一战线工作部的负责人，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会、各民主党派和各人民团体的负责人应邀参加了大会正式会议的开幕式。
