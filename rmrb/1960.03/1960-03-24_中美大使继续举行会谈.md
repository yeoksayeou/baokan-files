Title: Sino-US Ambassadors Continue Talks
Author:
Date: 1960-03-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 23rd: The ninety-seventh meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks was held in Warsaw at 3:00 PM on the 22nd. The meeting lasted for two hours.

After the meeting, both sides announced that the next meeting would be held on May 17th.

### 中美大使继续举行会谈

1960-03-24
第5版()
专栏：

    中美大使继续举行会谈
    新华社华沙23日电　中美两国大使级会谈第九十七次会议，22日下午三时在华沙举行。会议历时二小时。
    会后双方宣布，下次会议将在5月17日举行。
