Title: Danish Ambassador Holds National Day Reception; Premier Zhou and Other National Leaders Attend to Offer Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1960-03-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th March: The Danish Ambassador to China, Mr. Bartelsen, held a reception at the embassy this afternoon to celebrate the birthday of King Frederick IX of Denmark and the National Day of the Kingdom of Denmark.

    Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, Deputy Foreign Minister Luo Guibo, and others were invited to attend the reception.

    In his toast, Ambassador Bartelsen proposed: "To the prosperity and happiness of the Chinese people and to the health and happiness of Chairman Liu Shaoqi, to the health of Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi, to the continued good relations between the People's Republic of China and Denmark and the strengthening of the existing friendship between our two peoples, and to lasting peace between all countries and peoples of the world, I raise my glass."

    In his toast, Vice Premier Chen Yi proposed: "To the prosperity of the Kingdom of Denmark and the happiness of its people, to the health of His Majesty King Frederick IX of Denmark, to the health of Prime Minister Kampmann of Denmark, to the continued development of friendly relations between China and Denmark, to world peace, and to the health of Ambassador Bartelsen and his wife, I raise my glass."

    Also present at the reception were people from all walks of life in the capital, leading members of Beijing Municipality, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries to China.

### 丹麦大使举行国庆招待会  周总理等国家领导人前往祝贺

1960-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    丹麦大使举行国庆招待会
    周总理等国家领导人前往祝贺
    新华社10日讯　丹麦驻中国大使巴特森今天下午在大使馆举行招待会，庆祝丹麦国王斐德烈九世诞辰和丹麦王国国庆。
    周恩来总理，陈毅副总理、郭沫若副委员长，外交部副部长罗贵波等应邀出席了招待会。
    巴特森大使在祝酒时提议：为中国人民的昌盛幸福和刘少奇主席阁下的健康和幸福，为周恩来总理和陈毅副总理的健康，为中华人民共和国和丹麦两国之间继续良好的关系和我们两国人民之间现存的友谊的加强，为世界各国和各国人民之间的持久和平，干杯。
    陈毅副总理在祝酒时提议：为丹麦王国繁荣、人民幸福，为丹麦国王斐德烈九世陛下的健康，为丹麦首相坎普曼阁下的健康，为中国和丹麦两国间的友好关系继续发展，为世界和平，为巴特森大使和他的夫人的健康，干杯。
    出席招待会的，还有首都各方面的人士，北京市的负责人以及各国驻中国的使节和外交官员。
