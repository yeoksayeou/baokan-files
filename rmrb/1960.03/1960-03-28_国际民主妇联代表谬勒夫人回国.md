Title: International Democratic Women's Federation Representative Mrs. Muller Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-03-28
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 25th. Mrs. Grete Muller of Austria, representative of the International Democratic Women's Federation who was invited by the All-China Women's Federation to participate in the celebrations for the 50th anniversary of International Working Women's Day "March 8th" in our country, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 25th to return home after visiting Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places.
    Cao Mengjun, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, and others were at the airport to see her off.

### 国际民主妇联代表谬勒夫人回国

1960-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    国际民主妇联代表谬勒夫人回国
    新华社25日讯　应全国妇女联合会邀请来我国参加“三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年庆祝活动的国际民主妇联代表、奥地利的格雷特·谬勒夫人，在上海、杭州等地参观访问后，25日上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有全国妇联主席团委员曹孟君等。
