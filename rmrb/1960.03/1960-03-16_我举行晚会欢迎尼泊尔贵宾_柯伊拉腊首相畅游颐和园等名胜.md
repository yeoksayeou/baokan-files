Title: China Holds Evening Reception to Welcome Nepalese Guests; Prime Minister Koirala Enjoys the Summer Palace and Other Famous Sites
Author:
Date: 1960-03-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 15th. Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala, his wife, and the accompanying Nepalese dignitaries, enjoyed the Chinese ballet "The Mermaid" performed by the Beijing Dance School and the Central Conservatory of Music at the auditorium of the Nationalities Palace this evening.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Chen Yi and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Shen Junru and Guo Moruo, President of the Sino-Nepalese Friendship Association Zhou Jianren, and Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing accompanied the distinguished guests in watching the ballet.

Tonight's reception was jointly organized by the China-Nepal Friendship Association and the Ministry of Culture to welcome Prime Minister Koirala. After the performance ended, Prime Minister Koirala and other distinguished guests, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and others, stepped onto the stage, warmly shook hands with the performers, and presented them with flower baskets.

Xinhua News Agency, March 15th. The early spring Summer Palace today welcomed Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala, his wife, and the accompanying Nepalese dignitaries. The distinguished guests, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, enjoyed the Summer Palace.

Today, the weather was mild and sunny. Prime Minister Koirala, his wife, and the accompanying distinguished guests visited the Hall of Benevolence and Longevity, the Hall of Pleasure and Longevity, and the Hall of Longevity and Happiness. After viewing the palaces, they strolled along the Long Corridor, admiring the classic Chinese pavilions and towers. Then, the distinguished guests arrived at the magnificent Paiyun Gate and entered the Paiyun Hall for a visit.

At noon, Prime Minister Koirala, his wife, and the accompanying distinguished guests had lunch with Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and others. After the meal, the guests and hosts enthusiastically boated on Kunming Lake.

Before visiting the Summer Palace, Prime Minister Koirala, his wife, and the accompanying distinguished guests, accompanied by Vice Chairman of the State Ethnic Affairs Commission Yang Jingren, Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, and Chinese Ambassador to Nepal Pan Zili and his wife, visited the Central Institute for Nationalities and the Asian-African Students Sanatorium. While visiting the Asian-African Students Sanatorium, the Prime Minister spoke with the eleven Nepalese students who were receiving treatment in the sanatorium and took a group photo with them.

After visiting the Summer Palace, the Prime Minister, his wife, and the accompanying distinguished guests visited the Forbidden City in the afternoon. Prime Minister Koirala, who is skilled in oil painting, carefully appreciated the landscape, figure, and flower-and-bird paintings by famous Chinese Ming, Qing, and modern painters in the painting gallery of the Forbidden City. Prime Minister Koirala said, "Chinese paintings are wonderful. There are not many brushstrokes, but they are very difficult to paint."

### 我举行晚会欢迎尼泊尔贵宾  柯伊拉腊首相畅游颐和园等名胜

1960-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我举行晚会欢迎尼泊尔贵宾
    柯伊拉腊首相畅游颐和园等名胜
    新华社15日讯　尼泊尔王国首相柯伊拉腊和夫人以及随行的尼泊尔贵宾，今天晚上在民族宫礼堂欣赏了北京舞蹈学校和中央音乐学院演出的中国舞剧“鱼美人”。
    陪同贵宾们观看舞剧的，有周恩来总理，陈毅、习仲勋副总理，沈钧儒、郭沫若副委员长，中尼友好协会会长周建人，文化部部长沈雁冰等。
    今天的晚会是为欢迎柯伊拉腊首相，由中国尼泊尔友好协会和文化部联合主办的。演出全部结束后，柯伊拉腊首相等贵宾由周恩来总理等陪同步上舞台，和演员们亲切握手，并且向他们赠送了花篮。
    新华社15日讯　早春的颐和园今天接待了尼泊尔王国首相柯伊拉腊和夫人以及随行的尼泊尔贵宾。贵宾们在陈毅副总理和夫人的陪同下，畅游了颐和园。
    今天风和日暖，柯伊拉腊首相和夫人以及随行的贵宾们，在仁寿殿、颐乐殿和乐寿堂，观看了宫殿后，漫步长廊，欣赏了中国古典式的亭台楼阁。然后贵宾们到了壮丽的排云门前，进入排云殿参观。
    中午，柯伊拉腊首相和夫人以及随行的贵宾，同陈毅副总理和夫人、外交部副部长章汉夫等共进午餐。餐毕，宾主兴致勃勃地划船畅游昆明湖。
    在游览颐和园前，柯伊拉腊首相和夫人以及随行的贵宾们，在民族事务委员会副主任杨静仁、北京市副市长吴晗、中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和夫人的陪同下，参观了中央民族学院和亚非学生疗养院。在亚非学生疗养院参观时，首相同在疗养院疗养的十一名尼泊尔学生进行了谈话，并同他们一起合影。
    首相和夫人以及随行的贵宾们，在游览颐和园后，下午又先后参观了故宫。擅长油画的柯伊拉腊首相，在故宫的绘画馆里仔细地欣赏了中国明、清和近代的著名画家的山水、人物、花鸟画。柯伊拉腊首相说：“中国的画很妙，笔墨不多，但很不容易画”。
