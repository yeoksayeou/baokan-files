Title: Chairman Liu and Premier Zhou Send Telegrams Congratulating the King of Cambodia on His Birthday and National Day
Author:
Date: 1960-03-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd - Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, sent a telegram today to the King of Cambodia, Norodom Suramarit, congratulating him on his birthday and the National Day of the Kingdom of Cambodia. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Majesty King Norodom Suramarit of Cambodia, Phnom Penh:
    On the occasion of Your Majesty's birthday and the National Day of the Kingdom of Cambodia, I would like to express my sincere congratulations to Your Majesty and the people of your country on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish the Kingdom of Cambodia prosperity and its people happiness. I wish Your Majesty good health and longevity.
                                    Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                    March 3, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd - Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, sent a telegram today to Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Royal Government of Cambodia, congratulating him on the King of Cambodia's birthday and the National Day of the Kingdom of Cambodia. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Royal Highness Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Royal Government of Cambodia, Phnom Penh:
    On the occasion of the birthday of His Majesty King Norodom Suramarit of Cambodia and the National Day of your country, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Highness and the government of your country on behalf of the Chinese government and in my own name.
    In the past year, the friendly relations between China and Cambodia have been further developed due to the joint efforts of our two governments and peoples, and the economic and cultural ties between the two countries have become increasingly frequent. We are pleased to see that the Cambodian government and people, under Your Highness's leadership, have made great achievements in developing the national economy and safeguarding national independence and opposing foreign interference.
    I wish the Cambodian people new and greater achievements in implementing the first Five-Year Plan named after Your Highness, and wish your country prosperity.
                                    Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                                    March 3, 1960

### 刘主席　周总理电贺柬埔寨国王寿辰和国庆

1960-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席　周总理电贺柬埔寨国王寿辰和国庆
    新华社3日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇今天打电报给柬埔寨国王诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特，祝贺柬埔寨国王寿辰和柬埔寨王国国庆。电文如下：金边柬埔寨国王诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特陛下：
    欣逢陛下寿辰和柬埔寨王国国庆，我谨代表中国人民并且以我个人的名义向陛下和贵国人民表示诚挚的祝贺。祝柬埔寨王国繁荣，人民幸福。祝陛下健康长寿。　　　　　　中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                              一九六○年三月三日
    新华社3日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给柬埔寨王国政府首相西哈努克亲王，祝贺柬埔寨国王寿辰和柬埔寨王国国庆。电文如下：金边柬埔寨王国政府首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王殿下：
    在柬埔寨国王诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特陛下寿辰和贵国国庆的时候，我谨代表中国政府并且以我个人的名义向殿下和贵国政府致以热烈的祝贺。
    一年来，中柬两国间的友好关系，由于我们两国政府和人民的共同努力，获得了进一步的发展，两国间的经济和文化联系也日益频繁。我们高兴地看到，柬埔寨政府和人民在殿下领导下，在发展民族经济和维护民族独立、反对外国干涉等方面都取得了很大的成就。
    祝柬埔寨人民在执行以殿下的名字命名的第一个五年计划中获得新的、更大的成就，祝贵国繁荣昌盛。
  　　　　　中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                            一九六○年三月三日
