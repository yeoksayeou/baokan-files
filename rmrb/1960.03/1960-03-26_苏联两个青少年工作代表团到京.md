Title: Two Soviet Youth Work Delegations Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-03-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 24th: Two Soviet youth work delegations, the Soviet Lenin Communist Youth League University Worker Delegation and the Soviet Children's Worker Delegation, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China. The two delegations, led by A.S. Kuklinov, First Secretary of the Sverdlovsk Oblast Committee of the Soviet Communist Youth League, and V.I. Fedotova, Deputy Minister of the Central School Department of the Soviet Communist Youth League, respectively, totaled fourteen people.

Wang Wei, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, Du Ping, Deputy Minister of the School Work Department, Zuo Lin, Director and Editor-in-Chief of the China Children’s News, and Wang Jialiu, Deputy Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, were among those who welcomed them at the airport. In the evening, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League hosted a banquet to welcome the guests.

### 苏联两个青少年工作代表团到京

1960-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联两个青少年工作代表团到京
    新华社24日讯　应共青团中央邀请来我国访问的苏联列宁共产主义青年团大学工作者代表团、苏联少年工作者代表团，今天乘飞机到达北京。这两个代表团分别由苏联共青团斯维德洛夫斯克州委第一书记阿·斯·库克利诺夫和苏联共青团中央学校部副部长维·伊·费多托娃率领，一行共十四人。
    到机场欢迎的有中国共青团中央书记处书记王伟、学校工作部副部长杜平、中国少年报社长兼总编辑左林，共青团北京市委副书记汪家镠等。晚上，共青团中央设宴欢迎客人。
