Title: Panchen Reports on Inland Visit to Patriotic Upper-Class Figures in Tibet: Tibet Must Take the Road to Happiness and Prosperity

Author:

Date: 1960-03-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 16th: Panchen Erdeni Qoigyijabu, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region, reported on his visit to the interior of the motherland to patriotic and progressive figures of the upper class in Tibet at the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region on the 14th.

Panchen Erdeni's report was divided into three parts. The first part was "The Motherland's Ten Years of Construction Achievements are Brilliant and Splendid," the second part was "The Radiance of the People's Communes Shines Everywhere," and the third part was "The Glorious Road Taken by Other Brotherly Nationalities of the Motherland is Also the Road to Prosperity and Happiness that the Tibetan People Must Take." In the third part of his report, he said that the unity of the country, equality, solidarity, mutual assistance, and common development among all nationalities are the fundamental principles of the revolutionary victory and common prosperity of all nationalities. We must cherish the unity of the motherland and the unity of all nationalities as we cherish our own eyes. He encouraged the patriotic and progressive figures of the upper class in Tibet that as long as they obey the will of the people and wholeheartedly accept the Party's policies and guidelines, they can transform themselves well and have a bright future.

Panchen Erdeni finally said that we firmly believe that under the wise leadership of the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, under the direct leadership of the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Party of China, and with the active support of the people of all nationalities in the motherland, the Tibetan people will be full of confidence and build the Tibetan plateau into a socialist paradise on earth.

More than 300 people attended the report meeting, including Sangpo Tenzin Dondrup, Deputy Commander of the Tibet Military Region; Langdun Gonga Wangqiu, Standing Committee Member of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region; Ape Tseten Dolma, Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Patriotic Women's Federation; Jiazholin Living Buddha, Vice President of the Tibetan Branch of the Buddhist Association of China; and Dingjie Living Buddha, Deputy Director of the Religious Affairs Committee of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region.

On March 10th, Panchen Erdeni also gave a report to more than 1,000 Tibetan, Han, and Hui cadres from various agencies and troops in Lhasa at the Lhasa Auditorium.

### 班禅向西藏上层爱国人士作内地参观情况报告  西藏一定要走幸福康庄大道

1960-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    班禅向西藏上层爱国人士作内地参观情况报告
    西藏一定要走幸福康庄大道
    新华社拉萨16日电　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，14日在西藏自治区筹备委员会向西藏各界上层爱国进步人士，报告了在祖国内地参观的情况。
    班禅额尔德尼的报告分三部分，第一部分是“祖国十年建设成就灿烂辉煌”，第二部分是“人民公社光芒普照”，第三部分是“祖国其他各兄弟民族人民所走的光辉道路也就是西藏人民一定要走的繁荣幸福的康庄大道”。他在报告的第三部分中说，国家的统一、各民族间的平等、团结、互助和共同发展，是各族人民革命胜利和共同繁荣的根本原则。我们必须像爱护自己的眼珠一样，爱护祖国的统一和各民族的团结。他勉励西藏上层爱国进步人士只要顺从人民意志，全心全意接受党的方针政策，就可以很好地改造自己，也就有自己的光明前途。
    班禅额尔德尼最后说，我们坚信，在党中央和毛主席的英明领导下，在中共西藏工委直接领导下，在祖国各族人民积极支援下，西藏人民定会充满信心，把西藏高原建设成一个社会主义的人间乐园。
    到会听报告的，有西藏军区副司令员桑颇·才旺仁增，西藏自治区筹委会常务委员朗顿·滚噶旺秋，西藏爱国妇女联谊会筹委会代理主任委员阿沛·才旦卓噶，中国佛教协会西藏分会副会长嘉措林活佛，西藏自治区筹委会宗教事务委员会副主任丁杰活佛等三百多人。
    3月10日，班禅额尔德尼还在拉萨大礼堂向拉萨市各机关、部队的一千多名藏、汉、回族干部作了传达报告。
