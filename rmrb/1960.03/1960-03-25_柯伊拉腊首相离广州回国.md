Title: Prime Minister Koirala Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-03-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 24th: The Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Koirala, and his wife, along with the accompanying Nepalese dignitaries, accompanied by Pan Zili, the Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, his wife, and Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Guangzhou from Wuhan this morning at approximately 9:00 a.m. by special train. They then departed Guangzhou for Shenzhen, and will return home via Hong Kong.

The Guangzhou Railway Station today was adorned with the national flags of China and Nepal. Present at the station to bid farewell to the distinguished guests were Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province, Lin Qiangyun, Deputy Governor of Guangdong Province, Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, and other provincial and municipal Party and government leaders, leaders of the Guangzhou garrison, responsible persons of local organizations of various democratic parties and people's organizations, as well as more than 2,000 people from all walks of life. After the distinguished guests alighted from the train, Prime Minister Koirala, accompanied by Governor Chen Yu, reviewed the guard of honor. A group of Young Pioneers presented flowers to the Prime Minister and other Nepalese dignitaries.

Prime Minister Koirala addressed the welcoming crowd at the station. He said, "During my visit to your beautiful and great country, I had the opportunity to visit your country and hold talks with your country's leaders. Especially after free, frank, and successful talks with Premier Zhou Enlai, we signed agreements. These agreements are mutually beneficial and very meaningful to our two countries. Both of our countries strictly adhere to the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, and therefore, I firmly believe that we will be able to further strengthen our friendly relations on the solid foundation of these agreements." Prime Minister Koirala praised the achievements of the Chinese people in construction in his speech, and wished the Chinese people even greater success. Prime Minister Koirala also said, "I am very grateful to the people of your country for their warm welcome and gracious hospitality to us. Now I must bid farewell to your country, and wish you continued greater success and glory."

Governor of Guangdong Province Chen Yu said in his speech, "The visit of His Excellency the Prime Minister to our country has played an important role in enhancing the mutual understanding between the people of China and Nepal and strengthening the cooperative relations between the two countries. We believe that the people of China and Nepal will make significant contributions to promoting the unity of Asian and African countries and world peace in the future."

At 9:30, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Director Yu Peiwen, left Guangzhou for Shenzhen by special train. The Nepalese journalists delegation also left Guangzhou on the same train. Also present at the station today to bid farewell to the distinguished guests were the consuls of Czechoslovakia, Poland, Vietnam, and other countries stationed in Guangzhou.

### 柯伊拉腊首相离广州回国

1960-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

    柯伊拉腊首相离广州回国
    新华社广州24日电　尼泊尔王国首相柯伊拉腊和夫人以及随行的尼泊尔贵宾，在我国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和夫人、外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文陪同下，今天上午九时许乘专车自武汉到达广州，随即离开广州前往深圳，取道香港回国。
    今天的广州车站，飘扬着中、尼两国的国旗。到车站欢送贵宾的有广东省省长陈郁、广东省副省长林锵云、广州市市长朱光等省、市党政负责人，广州驻军首长和各民主党派地方组织、各人民团体的负责人，以及各界人民两千多人。贵宾们下车后，柯伊拉腊首相在陈郁省长陪同下检阅了仪仗队。一群少先队员向首相和其他尼泊尔的贵宾献了鲜花。
    柯伊拉腊首相在车站上向欢送的群众讲了话。他说：“在我访问你们的美丽和伟大的国家期间，我有机会参观了贵国并且同贵国的领导人进行会谈。尤其是同周恩来总理进行了自由、坦率和成功的会谈后，我们签订了协定。这些协定对我们两国是互利的，而且是很有意义的。我们两国都严格遵守和平共处五项原则，因此，我深信，我们能够在这些协定的坚实的基础上进一步加强彼此的友好关系。”柯伊拉腊首相在讲话中赞扬中国人民在建设中所取得的成就，并祝中国人民取得更大的成功。柯伊拉腊首相又说：“我非常感谢贵国人民对我们的热烈欢迎和殷勤招待，现在我就要向贵国告别了，祝你们继续取得更大的成功和光荣。”
    广东省省长陈郁在讲话中说：“首相阁下这次在我国的访问，对增进中尼两国人民的互相了解，加强两国的合作关系起了重要作用。我们相信中、尼两国人民今后将为促进亚非国家团结和世界和平作出重大的贡献。”
    九时三十分，贵宾们在俞沛文司长的陪同下，乘专车离广州去深圳。尼泊尔新闻工作者代表团也同车离开广州。今天到车站欢送贵宾的，还有捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、越南等国驻广州的领事。
