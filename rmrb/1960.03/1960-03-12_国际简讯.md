International Briefs

President Sukarno to Visit Asian and African Countries
Jakarta Dispatch: The Indonesian President's Office issued a press release on the 11th announcing that President Sukarno has accepted invitations from the leaders of Guinea, Iraq, Morocco, the United Arab Republic, and Tunisia, and will visit these countries in April and May of this year.

### 国际简讯

1960-03-12
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    苏加诺总统将访问亚非国家
    雅加达讯　印度尼西亚总统办公室11日发表一项新闻公报宣布，苏加诺总统已经接受了几内亚、伊拉克、摩洛哥、阿联、突尼斯等国领导人的邀请，将在今年4月和5月访问这些国家。
