Title: At the Invitation of the King and Government of Afghanistan, Khrushchev Arrives in Kabul
Author: 
Date: 1960-03-03
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, 2nd: According to TASS, Soviet Minister of Council Chairman Khrushchev and his entourage arrived in Kabul today by plane from Calcutta, India, for a friendly visit at the invitation of the King and government of Afghanistan.
    Welcoming Khrushchev at the airport were King Mohammed Zahir Shah of Afghanistan, Prime Minister Daud, and other senior government officials.
    In his speech, the King of Afghanistan extended a warm welcome to the distinguished Soviet guests. He expressed his belief that Khrushchev's visit would further strengthen the relations between the Soviet Union and Afghanistan.
    In his reply, Khrushchev expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the King and government of Afghanistan for inviting him to visit friendly Afghanistan once again. He said that the friendly and good-neighborly relations between the Soviet Union and Afghanistan are developing smoothly on the basis of mutual respect and equality.
    Khrushchev also praised Afghanistan's consistent policy of neutrality and safeguarding international peace.
    On the way from the airport to the city of Kabul, the distinguished Soviet guests were welcomed by tens of thousands of people.
    Afghan newspapers today all expressed their welcome to the distinguished Soviet guests.

### 应阿富汗国王和政府的邀请  赫鲁晓夫到达喀布尔

1960-03-03
第5版()
专栏：

    应阿富汗国王和政府的邀请
    赫鲁晓夫到达喀布尔
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社喀布尔讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫和他的随行人员应阿富汗国王和政府的邀请，今天乘飞机从印度的加尔各答到达喀布尔进行友好访问。
    在机场欢迎赫鲁晓夫的有阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿、首相达乌德和政府其他高级官员。
    阿富汗国王在致词时，向苏联贵宾表示热烈欢迎。他表示相信，赫鲁晓夫这次访问将进一步加强苏联和阿富汗的关系。
    赫鲁晓夫致答词时对阿富汗国王和政府再次邀请他访问友好的阿富汗表示衷心的感谢。他说，苏联和阿富汗两国的友好睦邻关系正在互相尊重和平等的基础上顺利发展。
    赫鲁晓夫还赞扬阿富汗一贯奉行的中立和保障国际和平的政策。
    从机场到喀布尔市区途中，苏联贵宾受到成千上万人的欢迎。
    阿富汗各报今天都表示欢迎苏联贵宾。
