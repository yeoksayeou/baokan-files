Title: ACFTU and All-China Youth Federation Send Telegrams Supporting the Struggle of the South African People
Author:
Date: 1960-03-24
Page: 6

    ACFTU and All-China Youth Federation Send Telegrams
    Supporting the Struggle of the South African People
    Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions today sent a telegram to the South African Congress of Trade Unions, supporting the South African people's just struggle against racial discrimination and for basic human rights.
    The telegram said: "Chinese workers are extremely indignant at the South African Federation authorities' barbaric suppression of unarmed African people in the Transvaal who held protest demonstrations against the 'Pass Laws'. We strongly condemn this brutal act by the South African authorities and firmly support the South African people's just struggle against racial discrimination and for basic human rights, and believe that your struggle will surely achieve final victory."
    Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd: The All-China Youth Federation today sent telegrams to the South African "National Youth Action Committee" and the "African National Congress Youth League", supporting the South African people's just struggle against imperialism and racial discrimination.
    The telegram said, "We are extremely angry to learn that the South African authorities carried out a bloody suppression of the mass meeting of Africans held in Verreeniging to oppose the 'Pass Laws', causing a large number of casualties among Africans. Chinese youth resolutely protest the barbaric actions of the South African authorities and express our deep condolences to the families of the deceased and the injured. The 130 million Chinese youth fully support your just struggle against imperialism and racial discrimination, and firmly believe that your struggle will be victorious.

### 我全总和青联致电  支持南非人民的斗争

1960-03-24
第6版()
专栏：

    我全总和青联致电
    支持南非人民的斗争
    新华社23日讯　中华全国总工会今天致电南非工会大会，支持南非人民反对种族歧视、争取基本人权的正义斗争。
    电报说：“中国工人对南非联邦当局用野蛮镇压手段，对待德兰斯瓦尔州为反对‘通行证法’举行抗议示威的手无寸铁的非洲人民，感到非常愤怒。我们强烈谴责南非当局这一残暴行为，并坚决支持南非人民这一反对种族歧视、争取基本人权的正义斗争，相信你们这一斗争必将取得最后胜利。”
    新华社23日讯　中华全国青年联合会今天致电南非“全国青年行动委员会”和“非洲人国民大会青年联盟”，支持南非人民反对帝国主义、反对种族歧视的正义斗争。
    电报中说，“获悉南非当局对非洲人在未里尼琴举行的反对‘通行证法’的群众大会进行血腥镇压，造成非洲人大批伤亡，感到无比的愤怒。中国青年坚决抗议南非当局的野蛮行为，并对死难者家属和负伤者表示深切的慰问。一亿三千万中国青年全力支持你们反对帝国主义、反对种族歧视的正义斗争，并坚信你们的斗争必将获得胜利。
