Title: UN Afro-Asian Group Issues Statement Demanding Security Council Discuss South African Situation
Author:
Date: 1960-03-26
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, New York, March 25th: Twenty-nine Afro-Asian countries at the United Nations yesterday firmly demanded that the United Nations Security Council immediately hold a meeting to discuss the South African Federal Government's massacre of Africans who were holding peaceful demonstrations to protest apartheid. This request was made in a statement issued by the Afro-Asian group after a meeting to discuss the situation caused by the South African Federal Government's massacres.
    The statement said that the Afro-Asian group believes that "the United Nations urgently needs to consider this situation immediately."
    The Afro-Asian group decided that this issue should be submitted to the Security Council and "strongly requested the Security Council to hold a meeting immediately."

### 联合国亚非集团发表声明  要求安理会讨论南非局势

1960-03-26
第6版()
专栏：

    联合国亚非集团发表声明
    要求安理会讨论南非局势
    新华社25日讯　纽约消息：联合国二十九个亚非国家昨天坚决要求联合国安全理事会立即举行会议，讨论南非联邦政府屠杀为抗议种族隔离而举行和平示威的非洲人。这个要求是亚非集团在一次会议后发表的声明中提出的。这次会议讨论了由于南非联邦政府进行屠杀所引起的局势。
    声明说，亚非集团认为，“联合国迫切需要立即考虑这一局势”。
    亚非集团决定，这个问题应该提交安全理事会，并且，“强烈要求安全理事会立即举行会议”。
