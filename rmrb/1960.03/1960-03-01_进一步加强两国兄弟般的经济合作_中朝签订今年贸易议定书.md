Title: Further Strengthen Brotherly Economic Cooperation Between the Two Countries: China and North Korea Sign This Year's Trade Protocol
Author:
Date: 1960-03-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 29th—The trade negotiations between the Government Trade Delegation of the People's Republic of China and the Government Trade Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in Pyongyang regarding trade for 1960 have successfully concluded in a friendly and harmonious atmosphere. The two countries signed a protocol in Pyongyang today for the mutual supply of goods for 1960.

According to the provisions of the protocol, China will supply Korea with: coal, coke, soybeans, cotton, tires, ferromanganese, sulfur, gypsum, chemical raw materials, various machinery, etc.; Korea will supply China with: iron ore, copper, zinc, lead, high-speed steel, carbon steel, anthracite coal, fertilizer, calcium carbide, fruits, seafood, various machinery, etc.

The signing of the aforementioned protocol signifies that the trade relations and economic cooperation between China and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea have been further consolidated and strengthened on the basis of brotherly and unbreakable friendship.

Signing the protocol on behalf of the Chinese government was Jiang Ming, head of the Government Trade Delegation of the People's Republic of China and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; signing on behalf of the Korean government was Kim Hyun-guk, head of the Government Trade Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Vice Minister of Trade.

Attending the signing ceremony on the Korean side were: Minister of Trade Rim Hae, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Park Kwang-seon, etc.; on the Chinese side were: Cao Keqiang, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Korea, and members of the trade delegation.

### 进一步加强两国兄弟般的经济合作  中朝签订今年贸易议定书

1960-03-01
第5版()
专栏：

    进一步加强两国兄弟般的经济合作
    中朝签订今年贸易议定书
    新华社平壤29日电　中华人民共和国政府贸易代表团和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府贸易代表团在平壤进行的关于1960年的贸易谈判，已经在友好融洽的气氛中圆满达成协议，今天在平壤签订了1960年两国相互供应货物的议定书。
    根据议定书的规定，中国供应朝鲜的物资有：煤炭、焦炭、大豆、棉花、轮胎、锰铁、硫磺、石膏、化工原料、各种机器等；朝鲜供应中国的物资有：铁矿砂、铜、锌、铅、高速钢、炭素钢、无烟煤、肥料、电石、水果、海产品、各种机器等。
    上述议定书的签订，标志着中国同朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的贸易关系和经济合作在兄弟般的牢不可破的友谊的基础上，得到进一步的巩固和加强。
    代表中国政府在议定书上签字的是中华人民共和国政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长江明；代表朝鲜政府签字的是朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府贸易代表团团长、贸易省副相金贤国。
    参加签字仪式的，朝鲜方面有：贸易相林海、外务省副相朴光善等；中国方面有：中国驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办曹克强和贸易代表团成员。
