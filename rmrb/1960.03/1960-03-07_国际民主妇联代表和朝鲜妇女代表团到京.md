Title: Representatives of the Women's International Democratic Federation and the Korean Women's Delegation Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-03-07
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th - Grete Müller from Austria, a representative of the Women's International Democratic Federation invited by the All-China Women's Federation to participate in the celebration of the 50th anniversary of the "March 8th" International Working Women's Day, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.
    Cao Mengjun, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, and Huang Ganying, Director of the International Liaison Department, were at the airport to welcome her.
    Maria Teresa Callegaro from Italy, another representative of the Women's International Democratic Federation, is currently en route and will arrive in Beijing soon.
    Xinhua News Agency, 6th - The delegation of the Women's Federation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Li Jong-sun and member Li Ran-soon, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon, invited by the All-China Women's Federation to participate in the celebration of the 50th anniversary of the "March 8th" International Working Women's Day.
    Shen Zijiou, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, and Yang Bin, Director of the General Office, were at the station to welcome them.
    Li Yong-ho, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and his wife were also at the station to welcome them.

### 国际民主妇联代表和朝鲜妇女代表团到京

1960-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

    国际民主妇联代表和朝鲜妇女代表团到京
    新华社6日讯　应全国妇女联合会邀请来我国参加“三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年庆祝活动的国际民主妇联代表、奥地利的格雷特·谬勒，今日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有全国妇联主席团委员曹孟君，国际联络部部长黄甘英等。
    国际民主妇联另一代表意大利的玛丽亚·蒂里萨·卡勒果，目前正在途中，即将到京。
    新华社6日讯　应全国妇女联合会邀请来我国参加“三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年庆祝活动的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国妇女代表团团长李贞顺、团员李兰淳，今日下午乘火车到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有全国妇联主席团委员沈兹九、办公厅主任杨彬等。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使李永镐及夫人等也到车站欢迎。
