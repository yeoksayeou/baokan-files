Title: Sowing
Author: Wen Huizhong, Li Cun
Date: 1960-03-10
Page: 8

    Sowing (Artwork from the "March 8th" Art Exhibition)
    Wen Huizhong, Sculpture
    Li Cun, Poem
    Oriole song everywhere beyond the green hills,
    Girls come to the fields to sow,
    Green spring grass by the commune river,
    Birdsong and wildflowers bloom in the mountains.
    Joyful competition resounds across the fields,
    The wind dances with red flags, colorful clouds fly,
    Hearts sweet as honey, affection like the sea,
    How to arrange this bosom full of spring? (Attached picture)

### 播种
文慧中  李村
1960-03-10
第8版()
专栏：

    播种（“三八”美展作品）
    文慧中　雕塑
    李村  　配诗
    莺声处处青山外，
    姑娘播种下田来，
    公社河边春草绿，
    山间鸟语野花开。
    遍野欢腾闹竞赛，
    风舞红旗彩云飞，
    心儿如蜜情如海，
    满怀春光怎安排？（附图片）
