Title: Japanese Women's Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-03-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. The Japanese Women's Delegation, led by Ms. Fujiwara Michiko, which was invited by the All-China Women's Federation to participate in the 50th anniversary celebrations of International Working Women's Day, has returned to Japan on the 22nd from Guangzhou after visiting Shenyang, Fushun, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Guangzhou.

Two other members of the delegation, Gomei Shigeru and Matsubara Kako, will leave Guangzhou for home on the 25th.

### 日本妇女代表团回国

1960-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    日本妇女代表团回国
    新华社23日讯　应全国妇女联合会邀请来我国参加“三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年庆祝活动的日本妇女代表团团长藤原道子等一行六人，在沈阳、抚顺、上海、杭州、广州等地参观访问后，已经在22日由广州回国。
    代表团另外两名团员五明繁和松原加女子，将在25日离开广州回国。
