Title: Ceylonese-Chinese Friendship Association Secretary Kumara Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-03-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd. Kumara, the Secretary of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association, arrived in Beijing by train yesterday afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 锡中友协秘书库玛拉到北京

1960-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    锡中友协秘书库玛拉到北京
    新华社23日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的锡兰中国友好协会秘书库玛拉，昨天下午乘火车到达北京。
