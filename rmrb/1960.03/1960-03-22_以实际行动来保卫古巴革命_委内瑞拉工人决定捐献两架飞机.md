Title: Defend the Cuban Revolution with Practical Actions: Venezuelan Workers Decide to Donate Two Airplanes
Author:
Date: 1960-03-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Caracas, 19th: Mass rallies and demonstrations in support of the Cuban Revolution were held in the Venezuelan capital on the 18th and 19th. At the mass rally, it was decided to donate two airplanes to help defend the Cuban Revolution.

The demonstration in support of the Cuban Revolution was held on the 19th. The demonstrators sang revolutionary songs and shouted slogans such as "Down with imperialism!", "Long live Fidel!", and "Long live the Cuban Revolution!" They departed from Carabobo Park, passed through several main streets of Caracas, and finally arrived at the National Cemetery where the remains of Venezuelan national heroes are kept.

The demonstrators then held a mass rally in the National Cemetery square. Speaking at the meeting were the chairman of the Venezuelan Committee for Solidarity with the Cuban Revolution, leaders of various worker, peasant, and student groups, and some members of parliament. David Salvador, General Secretary of the Executive Committee of the Cuban Workers' Confederation, also participated in the meeting.

On the evening of the 18th, a mass rally in support of the Cuban Revolution was also held at the Venezuelan Labor Union Building in the capital. This rally was convened by the Venezuelan Confederation of Workers, which has one million members.

Venezuelan Confederation of Workers Chairman González Navarro and Organizing Secretary Eloy Torres spoke on behalf of Venezuelan workers at the meeting. When Torres proposed the slogan of donating two airplanes to help defend the Cuban Revolution at the meeting, the entire audience responded enthusiastically and immediately launched a donation drive. Salvador, General Secretary of the Executive Committee of the Cuban Workers' Confederation, spoke at the meeting. He emphasized the necessity of the people of Latin American countries supporting Cuba.

### 以实际行动来保卫古巴革命  委内瑞拉工人决定捐献两架飞机

1960-03-22
第5版()
专栏：

    以实际行动来保卫古巴革命
    委内瑞拉工人决定捐献两架飞机
    据新华社加拉加斯19日电　委内瑞拉首都在18日和19日举行了支持古巴革命的群众大会和示威游行。在这次群众大会上，决定捐献两架飞机来帮助保卫古巴的革命。
    支持古巴革命的示威游行是在19日举行的。示威者高唱革命歌曲，高呼“反对帝国主义！”“菲德尔万岁！”“古巴革命万岁！”等口号，从卡拉博博公园出发，经过加拉加斯的几条主要街道，最后到达安放着委内瑞拉民族英雄遗骨的国家公墓。
    示威者随后在国家公墓广场上举行了群众集会。在会上讲话的，有委内瑞拉声援古巴革命委员会主席，各工人、农民、学生团体的领导人和一些议员。古巴工人联合会执行委员会总书记戴维·萨尔瓦多也参加了这次大会。
    在18日晚上，在委内瑞拉首都工会大厦还举行了一次支持古巴革命的群众大会。这次大会，是由拥有一百万会员的委内瑞拉工人联合会召开的。
    委内瑞拉工人联合会主席冈萨雷斯·纳瓦罗和组织书记埃洛伊·托雷斯代表委内瑞拉工人在会上讲了话。当托雷斯在大会上提出为帮助保卫古巴革命而捐献两架飞机的口号时，全场一致热烈响应，立即展开了捐献的热潮。古巴工人联合会执行委员会总书记萨尔瓦多在这次大会上讲了话。他强调指出了拉丁美洲各国人民支援古巴的必要性。
