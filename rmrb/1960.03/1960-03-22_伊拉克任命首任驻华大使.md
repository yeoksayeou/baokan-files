Title: Iraq Appoints First Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1960-03-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, March 20th: Baghdad Radio reported this afternoon that the Iraqi Foreign Ministry announced today that Abdul Haq Fadhil has been appointed as the first Ambassador of the Republic of Iraq to the People's Republic of China.

It is reported that the new ambassador will leave Baghdad for Beijing soon.

### 伊拉克任命首任驻华大使

1960-03-22
第6版()
专栏：

    伊拉克任命首任驻华大使
    新华社巴格达20日电　据巴格达电台报道，伊拉克外交部今天下午宣布：阿卜杜勒·哈吉·法齐勒被任命为伊拉克共和国驻中华人民共和国第一任大使。
    据悉，新大使不久将离开巴格达前往北京。
