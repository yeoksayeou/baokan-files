Title: Tractor Team Goes to the Fields
Author: Chen Xingmin
Date: 1960-03-12
Page: 8

Column: Spring Plowing Rhyme

    The tractor team goes to the fields, magpies chirp, chirp, the tractor team is in the fields, clever girls, drive iron oxen, rumbling, running all over the ground, mud waves roll up a thousand lines, plowshares are like noodle knives. Old Party Secretary, grinning: "The commune people are good, the machines are good."
    Henan Kaifeng Chen Xingmin

### 机耕队下田
陈醒民
1960-03-12
第8版()
专栏：春耕谣

    机耕队下田花喜鹊，喳喳叫，机耕队，下田了，巧姑娘，开铁牛，轰隆隆，满地跑，泥浪翻起千条线，犁铧好似切面刀。老支书，咧嘴笑：“公社人好机器好。”
    河南开封　陈醒民
