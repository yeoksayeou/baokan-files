Title: Liu Ningyi Sends a Telegram to Algerian Trade Union Secretary to Express Condolences to Victims of Algerian Earthquake
Author: 
Date: 1960-03-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd. Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, today sent a telegram to Masou, Secretary of the General Union of Algerian Workers, expressing condolences to the victims and their families in the Malouza and Beni Ilmane regions affected by the earthquake.

The telegram said: "We were shocked to learn that a violent earthquake occurred in the Malouza and Beni Ilmane regions of your country. Chinese workers extend their deep sympathy and sincere condolences to the Algerian victims and their families."

### 刘宁一致电阿工会书记  慰问阿尔及利亚地震受害者

1960-03-04
第5版()
专栏：

    刘宁一致电阿工会书记
    慰问阿尔及利亚地震受害者
    新华社3日讯　中华全国总工会主席刘宁一今天致电阿尔及利亚工人总联合会书记马舒，向迈芦扎和贝尼伊耳马地区的地震受害者及其家属表示慰问。
    电报说：“惊悉你国迈芦扎和贝尼伊耳马地区发生了猛烈地震事件，中国工人向阿尔及利亚的受害者及其家属致以深刻的同情和亲切的慰问。”
