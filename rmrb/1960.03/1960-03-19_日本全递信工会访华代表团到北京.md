Title: Japanese National Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-03-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 18th: The Japanese National Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union Delegation, invited by the All-China Federation of Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Unions to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane yesterday afternoon. The delegation was led by the head of the delegation, Sugo Shun. Xu Xin, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Unions, and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 日本全递信工会访华代表团到北京

1960-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    日本全递信工会访华代表团到北京
    新华社18日讯　应中国邮电工会全国委员会邀请来我国访问的日本全递信工会访华代表团一行六人，在团长大出俊率领下，昨天下午乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国邮电工会全国委员会副主席徐欣等。
