Title: Premier Zhou and Prime Minister Koirala Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1960-03-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 15th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, continued talks this afternoon with the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Koirala.
