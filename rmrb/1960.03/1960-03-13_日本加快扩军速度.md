Title: Japan Accelerates the Pace of Military Expansion
Author:
Date: 1960-03-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, March 12th: The figures for Japan's military buildup in fiscal year 1960, announced by the "Defense Agency" Director-General Munenori Akagi on the 11th, indicate that Japan's military strength this year will roughly approach the original target for fiscal year 1965, thereby accelerating the pace of military expansion.

Akagi said at the Budget Committee of the House of Councillors that within this fiscal year, the Japanese army will increase to 171,500 personnel; the navy will increase to 115,800 tons of naval vessels and 217 attached aircraft; and the air force will increase to 1,115 aircraft.

Japan originally planned to increase its army to 180,000 personnel, naval vessels to 170,000 tons, and aircraft to 1,100 to 1,300 by fiscal year 1965.

### 日本加快扩军速度

1960-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    日本加快扩军速度
    据新华社12日讯　东京消息：日本“防卫厅”长官赤城宗德11日发表的1960年度日本扩充军备的各项数字表明，今年度的日本军事力量将大体上接近原订的1965年度指标，从而加快了扩军速度。
    赤城在参议院预算委员会上说，今年度内日本陆军将增加到十七万一千五百人；海军舰艇将增加到十一万五千八百吨和附属飞机二百一十七架；空军将增加到一千一百一十五架飞机。
    日本原订在1965年度内把陆军增加到十八万人、海军舰艇增加到十七万吨、飞机增加到一千一百架到一千三百架。
