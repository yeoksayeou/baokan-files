Title: Sino-Polish Friendship Association Presents Film to Polish-Chinese Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1960-03-11
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 9th. The Sino-Polish Friendship Association presented the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association with a Chinese film, "Tenth Anniversary of National Day."
    Chinese Ambassador to Poland, Wang Bingnan, paid a visit to the Chairman of the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association, Jerzy Jodłowski, this morning and handed over the film.
    Jodłowski expressed his gratitude for the Chinese people's friendship.

### 中波友好协会  赠给波中友协影片

1960-03-11
第5版()
专栏：

    中波友好协会
    赠给波中友协影片
    新华社华沙9日电　中波友好协会赠给了波中友好协会一部中国影片“国庆十周年”。
    中国驻波兰大使王炳南今天上午拜会了波中友协主席英德里霍夫斯基，并且转交了这部影片。
    英德里霍夫斯基对中国人民的情谊表示感谢。
