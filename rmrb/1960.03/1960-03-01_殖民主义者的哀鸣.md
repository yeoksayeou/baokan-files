Excerpts from Foreign News

    Lament of the Colonialists
    Editor's Note: The British magazine "Time and Tide" published an article entitled "Africa" on January 3rd, revealing the colonialists' fear in the face of the wave of African national independence movements. Excerpts are translated as follows:
    Although we still have a Colonial Office, it is now openly acknowledged that the colonial phase of African history is nearing its end. Britain, France, and Belgium—the only European countries with real power remaining in Africa—have recognized the goal of independence for their African colonies. The situation in British territories is changing almost daily.
    For those of us in Britain, a de facto situation has emerged: all African countries will become independent. The only question is when this will happen.
    One of the most serious questions is, as nationalism extends to all parts of Africa, what will happen to the small group of Europeans surrounded by the rising tide of black or Arab people? Can Verwoerd and his descendants forever keep black South Africa away from them with whips and guns? Can France's armed farmers and paratroopers brazenly confront the entire Arab world for a long time?
    Will the last white man leave Africa one day with a box of biscuits and a compass? In this important year, we will watch the world determine its fateful history week by week.

### 殖民主义者的哀鸣

1960-03-01
第6版()
专栏：外论摘要

    殖民主义者的哀鸣
    编者按　英国“时与潮”杂志1月3日以“非洲”为题发表文章，表现了殖民主义者在非洲民族独立运动浪潮面前的惶恐心理。现摘译如下：
    虽然我们还有一个殖民地部，但是现在已公开承认非洲历史的殖民地阶段正濒于结束。英国、法国和比利时——仍留在非洲的唯一有实力的欧洲国家，已经承认它们的非洲殖民地要求独立的目标。现在英属领地的情况几乎一日一变。
    对于我们在英国的人来说，出现了一种事实上的局面：所有的非洲国家都要独立。唯一的问题在于，什么时候发生这种情况。
    一个最严重的问题是，当民族主义伸展到非洲各部分的时候，在黑人或阿拉伯浪潮日益高涨的包围中的一小群欧洲人将会发生什么情况？维沃尔德和他的后裔能够永远用鞭子和手枪使黑人南非不能靠近他们吗？法国的武装的农民和伞兵部队能够长期悍然对抗整个阿拉伯世界吗？
    最后一个白人有一天会带着一盒饼干和一个指南针离开非洲吗？在这重要的一年中，我们将注视世界正一个星期一个星期地制定它的决定命运的历史。
