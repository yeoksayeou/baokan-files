The Call for Independence from the African People

    Northern Rhodesia
    Kenneth Kaunda, President of the United National Independence Party of Northern Rhodesia, called on the 1,000 Africans attending a rally in Kalulushi on February 23 to join the United National Independence Party in the struggle for independence and freedom this year.
    Ignoring the fierce opposition of the people of Northern Rhodesia, British colonialists have kept this protectorate as part of the Central African Federation under its control since 1953.
    Madagascar
    President Tsiranana of the Republic of Madagascar said at a press conference in Paris on February 18 that Madagascar should achieve complete independence. He pointed out that the main and feasible consequences of France's recognition of the independence of the Republic of Madagascar are to enable Madagascar to implement an independent foreign policy and establish an army. Madagascar is negotiating with France on the issue of independence.
    Rwanda
    The Rwanda National Union recently issued a statement to the United Nations Trusteeship Council accusing the Belgian government of repeatedly preventing the independence of Rwanda-Urundi, a United Nations trust territory. The alliance calls for independence for Rwanda.
    Representatives from Rwanda-Urundi, who attended the round table conference on the political future of the Congo held in Brussels, the capital of Belgium, some time ago, demanded independence at the same time as the Congo on June 30 this year.
    Equatorial Africa
    The Equatorial Africa Office of the African Association in Cairo issued a statement on February 24 calling on the people of Equatorial Africa to adhere to their demands for independence and to remain fully vigilant to thwart the conspiracies of French imperialism that are trying to prevent the African people from realizing these aspirations.
    According to news from Bangui (capital of the Central African Republic), the prime ministers of Chad, Gabon, the Central African Republic, and Central Congo in Equatorial Africa have met on February 22 and decided to organize a joint committee composed of the prime ministers and speakers of the parliaments of the four countries to plan the steps to achieve independence and form a federation.
    Mauritania
    The Mauritanian United Party, the ruling party of the Islamic Republic of Mauritania, recently passed two resolutions on independence and safeguarding national territorial integrity. The resolution demands that Mauritania become independent in 1961 to get rid of French colonial rule; the other resolution expresses its determination to defend all the territory of Mauritania.
    Sierra Leone
    The Sierra Leone People's Party is calling for Sierra Leone to achieve independence in April next year.
    The chiefs of the Southwest Province of Sierra Leone also demanded independence in April 1961.
    News reports said that the talks on the date of Sierra Leone's independence, originally scheduled to begin in London on March 22, have been postponed to April due to British Colonial Secretary Macleod's trip to Central Africa.
    Sierra Leone, located in West Africa, is made up of a British colony and a British "protectorate." It has an estimated population of 2.5 million.

### 非洲人民要求独立的呼声

1960-03-11
第6版()
专栏：

    非洲人民要求独立的呼声
    北罗得西亚
    北罗得西亚联合民族独立党总主席肯尼斯·卡翁达2月23日在卡路路希举行的一千名非洲人的集会上，号召参加集会的人同联合民族独立党一起争取在今年内获得独立和自由。
    英国殖民者不顾北罗得西亚人民的激烈反对，于1953年起一直把这个保护国作为它控制的中非联邦的一部分。
    马达加斯加
    马达加斯加共和国总统齐腊纳纳2月18日在巴黎举行的记者招待会上说，马达加斯加应该取得完全的独立。他指出，法国承认马达加斯加共和国独立的主要的和可行的后果是使马达加斯加实行独立的外交政策和建立一支军队。马达加斯加正在与法国谈判关于独立的问题。
    卢安达
    卢安达民族联盟不久前在给联合国托管理事会的一篇声明中，指责比利时政府一再阻止这个联合国的托管地卢安达—乌隆迪独立。这个联盟要求给予卢安达独立。
    参加前些时候在比利时首都布鲁塞尔举行的、讨论刚果政治前途的圆桌会议的卢安达—乌隆迪代表们要求与刚果同时在今年6月30日独立。
    赤道非洲
    在开罗的非洲人协会赤道非洲办事处2月24日发表声明，号召赤道非洲人民坚持他们争取独立的要求，并且保持充分的警惕，以挫败竭力阻挠非洲人民实现这些愿望的法帝国主义的阴谋诡计。
    据来自班给（中非共和国首都）的消息说，赤道非洲的乍得、加蓬、中非共和国、中央刚果的总理们已在2月22日开会决定组织一个由四国的总理和议会议长组成的联合委员会，来筹划实现独立和结成联邦的步骤。
    毛里塔尼亚
    毛里塔尼亚伊斯兰共和国执政党毛里塔尼亚联合党最近通过了关于独立和保卫国家领土完整的两项决议。决议要求毛里塔尼亚在1961年独立，以摆脱法国的殖民统治；另一决议表示决心保卫毛里塔尼亚的所有领土。
    塞拉勒窝内
    塞拉勒窝内人民党要求在明年4月实现塞拉勒窝内的独立。
    塞拉勒窝内西南省的酋长们也要求在1961年4月独立。
    消息说，原定在3月22日在伦敦开始讨论塞拉勒窝内独立日期的会谈，由于英国殖民大臣麦克劳德的中非之行而被推迟到4月举行。
    位于西非的塞拉勒窝内是由一个英国殖民地和一个英国“保护地”所组成。它的人口估计有二百五十万。
