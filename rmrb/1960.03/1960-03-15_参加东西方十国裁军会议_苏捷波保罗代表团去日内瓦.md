Title: Soviet, Czechoslovak, Polish Delegations Go to Geneva to Attend East-West Ten-Nation Disarmament Conference
Author:
Date: 1960-03-15
Page: 5

    To Attend East-West Ten-Nation Disarmament Conference
    Soviet, Czechoslovak, Polish Delegations Go to Geneva
    Xinhua News Agency, March 12th - According to TASS: The Soviet delegation, headed by Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Zorin, arrived in Geneva today to prepare for the East-West Ten-Nation Disarmament Conference to be held on the 15th.
    Speaking at the Geneva airport, Zorin emphasized that the Soviet delegation is fully aware of the full importance and responsibility of its work in the Ten-Nation Disarmament Conference.
    He said that the Soviet government has instructed us to make every effort in this meeting to reach an agreement on achieving complete and thorough disarmament under international supervision as soon as possible. Zorin said that the Soviet delegation is fully determined to realize this mandate of the Soviet government and people.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 12th - Delegations from Czechoslovakia, Poland, Bulgaria, and Romania to participate in the Ten-Nation Disarmament Committee meeting scheduled to open on March 15th have already departed for Geneva in the past two or three days.
    The head of the Czechoslovak delegation is Deputy Foreign Minister Nosek, the head of the Polish delegation is Deputy Foreign Minister Naszkowski, the head of the Bulgarian delegation is First Deputy Foreign Minister Tarabanov, and the head of the Romanian delegation is Deputy Foreign Minister Misineanu.

### 参加东西方十国裁军会议  苏捷波保罗代表团去日内瓦

1960-03-15
第5版()
专栏：

    参加东西方十国裁军会议
    苏捷波保罗代表团去日内瓦
    新华社12日讯　据塔斯社讯：以苏联外交部副部长佐林为首的苏联代表团今天到达日内瓦，准备参加将于15日举行的东西方十国裁军会议。
    佐林在日内瓦机场讲话时强调指出，苏联代表团充分意识到它在十国裁军会议中的工作的全部重要性和责任。
    他说，苏联政府责成我们在这次会谈中尽一切努力尽快达成有关实现在国际监督下的全面彻底裁军的协议。佐林说，苏联代表团有充分决心来实现苏联政府和人民的这一委托。
    新华社12日讯　参加定于3月15日开幕的十国裁军委员会会议的捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、保加利亚和罗马尼亚的代表团，已经在最近两、三天内动身前往日内瓦。
    捷克斯洛伐克代表团团长是外交部副部长诺塞克，波兰代表团团长是外交部副部长纳希科夫斯基，保加利亚代表团团长是外交部第一副部长塔拉巴诺夫，罗马尼亚代表团团长是外交部副部长米辛契斯库。
