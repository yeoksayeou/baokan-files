Title: Promoting Friendship and Cultural Exchange Between the People of Two Countries: Indonesian-Chinese Friendship Association Opens Chinese Language Class

Author:

Date: 1960-03-04
Page: 5

    Promoting Friendship and Cultural Exchange Between the People of Two Countries
    Indonesian-Chinese Friendship Association Opens Chinese Language Class
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 1st. The Chinese language class organized by the Indonesian-Chinese Friendship Association held its opening ceremony on the evening of February 29th. The purpose of holding this class is to further promote friendship and cultural exchange between the people of Indonesia and China.
    More than two hundred people are participating in this class. They will study elementary courses for two years.

### 促进两国人民友谊和文化交流  印度尼西亚——中国友协开办汉语学习班

1960-03-04
第5版()
专栏：

    促进两国人民友谊和文化交流
    印度尼西亚——中国友协开办汉语学习班
    新华社雅加达1日电　印度尼西亚—中国友好协会开办的汉语学习班2月29日晚间举行开学典礼。举办这个学习班是为了进一步促进印度尼西亚和中国人民之间的友谊和文化交流。
    有二百多人参加这个学习班。他们将学习初级课程两年。
