Title: Ballads of the Yellow River滩
Author: Yan Cao
Date: 1960-03-03
Page: 8

    Ballads of the Yellow River滩
    Henan Kaifeng　Yan Cao
    The crack of the whip startles the orioles
    Iron oxen roar, yellow oxen leap,
    Spring plowing contests reach a singing high;
    Good news builds an arch of colorful flowers,
    The crack of the whip startles the orioles,
    The earth puts on golden attire
    Yellow River滩, the earth smells sweet,
    Iron oxen moo a mountain song;
    Singing from moonrise until the sun smiles,
    The earth puts on golden attire.
    Competing for "Top Scholar" at the Harvest Festival
    Carts in strings, people in lines,
    Songs shake the sky on the broad road.
    Ox horns bumping ox horns,
    Carrying pole striking carrying pole.
    Cart after cart of chemical fertilizer,
    Exchanged for a pearl mountain.
    Load after load of human manure,
    Exchanged for a garden of sweet melons and fruits.
    Chasing time for spring topdressing,
    Competing for "Top Scholar" at the Harvest Festival.
    Smiling, watching piles of pearls
    Fasten the Yellow Dragon's tail,
    Pry open the Yellow Dragon's mouth,
    Pinch the Dragon King's neck,
    Order him to spit silver water.
    Silver water flows into the ridges,
    Seedlings drink and get drunk.
    Golden summer wheat takes the stage,
    Smiling, watching piles of pearls.
    Three parts water under the hoe
    Three parts water under the hoe,
    Irrigating the seedlings until they are tipsy.
    The spring breeze gently brushes the seedlings awake,
    Flowering overnight, forming golden ears.
    Raising heads and wagging tails, harmonizing a few times
    Yellow River滩, grass turns green,
    Shepherd boy's flute plays "The East is Red";
    Happily, the yellow ox stops drinking water,
    Raising heads and wagging tails, harmonizing a few times.
    Commune members laugh, angering the Dragon King
    Ditch linking to ditch, pond linking to pond,
    Commune members laugh, angering the Dragon King;
    Even if it doesn't rain for three years,
    Still a bumper harvest, granaries full.

    黄河滩散曲
    河南开封　燕草
    鞭响惊起黄鹂叫
    铁牛吼，黄牛跳，
    春耕比武歌声高；
    捷报搭座彩花棚，
    鞭响惊起黄鹂叫，
    地球换上黄金装
    黄河滩，土喷香，
    铁牛哞哞山歌唱；
    月升唱到太阳笑，
    地球换上黄金装。
    丰收会上夺“状元”
    车成串，人成线，
    康庄路上歌震天。
    牛角碰牛角，
    扁担撞扁担。
    车车化学肥，
    换座珍珠山。
    挑挑人粪尿，
    换园瓜果甜。
    春天追肥抓时间，
    丰收会上夺“状元”。
    笑看珍珠堆
    拴紧黄龙尾，
    掰开黄龙嘴，
    掐住龙王颈，
    命令吐银水。
    银水流畦田，
    苗喝酣酣醉。
    金夏麦登场，
    笑看珍珠堆。
    锄头底下三分水
    锄头底下三分水，
    灌得秧苗熏熏醉。
    春风轻拂苗清醒，
    一夜扬花结金穗。
    抬头撅尾和几声
    黄河滩，草发青，
    牧童笛奏“东方红”；
    喜得黄牛停饮水，
    抬头撅尾和几声。
    社员大笑气龙王
    渠连渠，塘连塘，
    社员大笑气龙王；
    凭你三年不落雨，
    照样丰收粮满仓。
