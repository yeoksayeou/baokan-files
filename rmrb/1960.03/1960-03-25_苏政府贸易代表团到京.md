Title: Soviet Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-03-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 24th. The Soviet Government Trade Delegation, headed by Soviet Minister of Foreign Trade, N.S. Patolichev, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Ye Ji-zhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of China, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister, Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others. Antonov, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, Yeremin, Commercial Representative and member of the Soviet Government Trade Delegation, Sudarikov, Counselor, Velikhachkin, Counselor, Fomin, Economic Counselor, and others also greeted them at the airport.

In the afternoon, Minister Ye Ji-zhuang met with Minister Patolichev and all members of the delegation, and hosted a banquet in the evening to welcome the guests. Among those present at the banquet were Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lei Renmin and Li Qiang, Vice Ministers of Foreign Trade, and Liu Mingfu, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission. Antonov, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, Sudarikov, Counselor, Velikhachkin, Counselor, and Fomin, Economic Counselor, were also invited to attend the banquet.

### 苏政府贸易代表团到京

1960-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

    苏政府贸易代表团到京
    新华社24日讯　以苏联对外贸易部部长帕托利切夫为首的苏联政府贸易代表团，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有我对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长雷任民、外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清等。苏联驻华大使馆临时代办安东诺夫，商务代表、苏联政府贸易代表团团员叶烈敏，参赞苏达利柯夫、魏立夏金，经济参赞符明等人也到机场迎接。
    下午，叶季壮部长会见了帕托利切夫部长和代表团的全体人员，并在晚上设宴欢迎客人。出席宴会作陪的有，外交部副部长罗贵波，对外贸易部副部长雷任民、李强，国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫等。苏联驻华大使馆临时代办安东诺夫，参赞苏达利柯夫、魏立夏金，经济参赞符明等也应邀出席了宴会。
