Title: Shandong to Build Large Cotton Textile Mill
Author:
Date: 1960-03-12
Page: 2

Shandong to Build Large Cotton Textile Mill

    Jinan No. 2 Cotton Textile Mill, the first large-scale and modernly equipped cotton textile mill in Shandong Province, has recently officially started construction.

    Once completed, this mill will be able to produce 75,000 bales of cotton yarn and 11,000 tons of canvas annually.

### 山东兴建大棉纺织厂

1960-03-12
第2版()
专栏：

    山东兴建大棉纺织厂
    山东省第一个规模大、设备现代化的棉纺织厂——济南第二棉纺织厂，最近正式动工兴建。
    这个厂建成之后，每年能生产棉纱七万五千件，帆布一万一千吨。
