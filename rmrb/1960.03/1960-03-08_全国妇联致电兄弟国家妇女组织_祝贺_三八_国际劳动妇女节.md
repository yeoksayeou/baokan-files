Title: All-China Women's Federation Sends Telegrams to Women's Organizations of Fraternal Countries, Congratulating Them on "March 8th" International Working Women's Day
Author:
Date: 1960-03-08
Page: 5

    All-China Women's Federation Sends Telegrams to Women's Organizations of Fraternal Countries
    Congratulating Them on "March 8th" International Working Women's Day
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th March. The All-China Women's Federation sent a telegram to the Soviet Women's Committee, congratulating them on the 50th anniversary of "March 8th" International Working Women's Day. The telegram reads as follows: Dear Comrades of the Soviet Women's Committee:
    On the occasion of the 50th anniversary of "March 8th" International Working Women's Day, Chinese women extend to you, and through you to all Soviet sisters, the warmest congratulations.
    Chinese women are watching with infinite joy and admiration the brilliant victories achieved by the Soviet people in the construction of the communist seven-year plan, and we feel honored and proud of your achievements.
    As we warmly celebrate the 50th anniversary of "March 8th" International Working Women's Day, it is precisely the time when our people are cheering the great victory of our country's high-speed socialist construction. Chinese women have now become a major force in construction. Chinese women will forever unite closely with Soviet sisters, and will unite closely with women from socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union, and advance victoriously towards the great goals of peace, socialism and communism.
    The unbreakable friendship and unity between the Chinese and Soviet people and the women of the two countries will last forever.
    All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China
					5th March, 1960
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th March. On March 5th, the All-China Women's Federation sent telegrams to the Polish Women's League, the Czechoslovak Women's Federation, the Hungarian National Council of Women, the Albanian Women's Union, the Romanian National Women's Committee, the Bulgarian Democratic Women's Committee, the German Democratic Women's League, the Korean Democratic Women's League, the Mongolian Women's Committee, and the Vietnamese Women's Federation, warmly congratulating the women of these fraternal countries on the 50th anniversary of "March 8th" International Working Women's Day.
    The telegrams warmly congratulated the women and people of these fraternal countries on the achievements they have made in socialist construction. The telegrams said that Chinese women will forever unite closely with the sisters of socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union, and advance victoriously towards the great goals of peace, socialism and communism.

### 全国妇联致电兄弟国家妇女组织  祝贺“三八”国际劳动妇女节

1960-03-08
第5版()
专栏：

    全国妇联致电兄弟国家妇女组织
    祝贺“三八”国际劳动妇女节
    新华社7日讯　全国妇女联合会致电苏联妇女委员会，祝贺“三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年。电文如下：苏联妇女委员会亲爱的同志们：
    值此“三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年之际，中国妇女向你们，并且通过你们向全苏联的姊妹们致以最热烈的祝贺。
    中国妇女以无限欢欣和敬佩的心情，注视着苏联人民在建设共产主义的七年计划中取得的辉煌胜利，我们为你们的成就感到光荣和自豪。
    当我们热烈庆祝“三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年的时候，正是我国人民欢呼我国高速度建设社会主义获得伟大胜利的时候。我国妇女现已成为建设中的一支重大的力量。中国妇女要永远和苏联姊妹紧紧地团结在一起，要同以苏联为首的社会主义各国妇女紧紧地团结在一起，向着和平、社会主义和共产主义的伟大目标胜利前进。
    中苏两国人民和两国妇女牢不可破的友谊和团结万古长青。
    中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会　　　　　　1960年3月5日
    新华社7日讯　全国妇女联合会5日分别打电报给波兰妇女联盟、捷克斯洛伐克妇女联合会、匈牙利妇女全国委员会、阿尔巴尼亚妇女联合会、罗马尼亚全国妇女委员会、保加利亚民主妇女委员会、德意志民主妇女联合会、朝鲜民主妇女同盟、蒙古妇女委员会、越南妇女联合会，向这些兄弟国家的妇女热烈祝贺“三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年。
    电报中热烈祝贺这些兄弟国家的妇女和人民在社会主义建设中所取得的成就。电报说，中国妇女将永远同以苏联为首的社会主义各国的姐妹紧紧地团结在一起，向着和平、社会主义和共产主义的伟大目标胜利前进。
