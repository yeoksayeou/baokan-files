Title: Soviet Ambassador to China Chervonenko Sends Telegram to Guangdong Provincial Party Committee and Provincial People's Committee to Express Condolences to the Heroes of the "Makou Incident"
Author:
Date: 1960-03-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 27th. Soviet Ambassador to China, Chervonenko, sent a telegram on the 21st to the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee, expressing condolences to the cadres and police officers of the "Makou Incident."

Ambassador Chervonenko's telegram to the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee said: "Please convey our highest respect to the cadres and police officers of the Yingde County police detachment who heroically rescued the people's property, and our deepest condolences to the families of the comrades who sacrificed their lives. At the same time, we sincerely wish the injured comrades a speedy recovery. The heroic actions they demonstrated will forever be examples of high communist consciousness and strong patriotism."

### 苏驻华大使契尔沃年科致电广东省委和省人委  慰问“马口事件”英雄

1960-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    苏驻华大使契尔沃年科致电广东省委和省人委
    慰问“马口事件”英雄
    新华社广州27日电　苏联驻华大使契尔沃年科21日致电中共广东省委和省人民委员会，慰问“马口事件”的干部和民警。
    契尔沃年科大使致中共广东省委和省人民委员会的电报说：“请转达我们对奋不顾身英勇抢救人民财产的英德县民警队的干警们的崇高敬意，并对牺牲了的同志们的家属的深切慰问。同时，由衷地祝受伤的同志们早日恢复健康。他们所表现的英勇行为将永远是高度的共产主义觉悟和强烈的爱国主义的典范。”
