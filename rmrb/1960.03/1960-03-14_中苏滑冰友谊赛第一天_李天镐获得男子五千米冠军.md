Title: First Day of China-Soviet Skating Friendship Competition: Li Tianhao Wins Men's 5000m Championship
Author:
Date: 1960-03-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, March 13th: According to TASS, the China Youth Men's and Women's Speed Skating Team and the Russian Federation Team began their friendly competition on the 12th at the "Labor" stadium in Irkutsk. In the first day of competition, Soviet athletes won three first places, and Chinese athletes won one first place.

    The men's 5000-meter race was very intense. Li Tianhao, a 19-year-old Chinese university student, overtook Soviet athlete Ivan Chernov in the last few laps, winning first place with an excellent time of 8 minutes 27.9 seconds.

### 中苏滑冰友谊赛第一天  李天镐获得男子五千米冠军

1960-03-14
第5版()
专栏：

    中苏滑冰友谊赛第一天
    李天镐获得男子五千米冠军
    据新华社13日讯　据塔斯社报道：中国青年男女速度滑冰队和俄罗斯联邦队的友谊比赛12日在伊尔库次克的“劳动”运动场开始。在第一天比赛中，苏联运动员获得三个第一名，中国运动员获得一个第一名。
    男子五千米竞赛进行得非常激烈。中国十九岁的大学生李天镐在最后几圈才超过了苏联运动员伊凡·契尔诺夫，以八分二十七点九秒的优良成绩获得了第一名。
