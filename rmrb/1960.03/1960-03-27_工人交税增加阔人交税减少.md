Title: Workers' Taxes Increase, Rich People's Taxes Decrease
Author:

Page: 5
Under Capitalism

    Workers' Taxes Increase, Rich People's Taxes Decrease
    Britain
    According to a report by the British Board of Inland Revenue, during the 1950s, the percentage of direct taxes paid by workers increased from 23% to 32%, while the percentage paid by the wealthy decreased from 28% to 20%. The report also mentioned that the government collected half as much tax in the year ending March 31, 1959, as it did in the 1949-1950 fiscal year.

### 工人交税增加阔人交税减少

1960-03-27
第5版()
专栏：在资本主义制度下

    工人交税增加阔人交税减少
    英国
    据英国国内税收专员的报告，在二十世纪五十年代期间，工人缴纳的直接税所占的百分比自23％增至32％，而有钱人所交的百分比则自28％降为20％。报告还提到，政府在到1959年3月31日为止的一年中所征收的税额比1949—1950年度增加了一半。
