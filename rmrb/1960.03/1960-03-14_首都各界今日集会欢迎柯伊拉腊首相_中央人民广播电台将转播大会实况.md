Title: All Circles in the Capital Gather Today to Welcome Prime Minister Koirala; Central People's Broadcasting Station Will Broadcast the Meeting Live
Author:
Date: 1960-03-14
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, March 13th: People from all walks of life in Beijing will hold a grand rally on the afternoon of the 14th to welcome Koirala, the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the proceedings of the meeting live, starting at 2:55 p.m. that day.

### 首都各界今日集会欢迎柯伊拉腊首相  中央人民广播电台将转播大会实况

1960-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各界今日集会欢迎柯伊拉腊首相
    中央人民广播电台将转播大会实况
    新华社13日讯　北京市各界人民将在14日下午举行盛大集会，欢迎尼泊尔王国首相柯伊拉腊。中央人民广播电台在当天下午两点五十五分开始，转播大会的实况。
