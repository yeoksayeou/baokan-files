Title: Statement by the Provisional Government of Algeria Denies Contact with France
Author:
Date: 1960-03-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 25th: The Information Department of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria today categorically denied the following newspaper reports: that the Algerian government, represented by Minister of Armaments and Transport Abdelhafid Boussouf, had contact with France in Rabat.

The statement issued by the Algerian Information Department stated that this news is completely groundless.

### 阿尔及利亚临时政府声明  否认曾与法国进行接触

1960-03-27
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚临时政府声明
    否认曾与法国进行接触
    新华社开罗25日电　阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府宣传部今天断然否认下列报纸消息：阿尔及利亚政府以军备和交通部长阿卜杜勒·哈菲兹·布苏夫为代表同法国在拉巴特进行接触。
    阿尔及利亚宣传部发表的声明说，这个消息是毫无根据的。
