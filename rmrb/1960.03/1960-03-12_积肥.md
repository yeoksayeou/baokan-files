Title: Accumulating Fertilizer
Author: Hu Xingwu
Date: 1960-03-12
Page: 8

Spring Plowing Ballad

　　Accumulating fertilizer, the wilderness is beautiful in the spring dawn,
　　Accumulating fertilizer, soldiers and civilians surge like a tide.
　　Big strides awaken the mountain and river dream,
　　Hoes rise and shovels fall, the earth shakes;
　　Dense fertilizer piles fill the ground,
　　Wheels roll and horses neigh.
　　Today, scatter more pearl-like sweat,
　　After autumn, grain mountains will reach the ninth heaven.
　　Middle Soldier Hu Xingwu

### 积肥

1960-03-12
第8版()
专栏：春耕谣

    积肥清晨原野春色娇，积肥军民涌如潮。大步踏醒山河梦，镐起锹落天地摇；密密肥堆满地面，车轮滚滚马萧萧。今朝多撒珍珠汗，秋后粮山入九霄。
    中士　胡星五
