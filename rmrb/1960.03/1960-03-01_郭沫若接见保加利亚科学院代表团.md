Title: Guo Moruo Receives Delegation from the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences
Author:
Date: 1960-03-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 29th — Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, received Lyubomir Krastanov, Corresponding Member of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences and head of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences delegation, and all members of the delegation this afternoon. Guo Moruo had a cordial conversation with the guests.

Present at the reception were Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Xie Xinhe, Deputy Secretary-General, Yin Zanxun, Director of the Department of Earth Sciences, Zhao Jiuzhang, Director of the Institute of Geophysics, and Wang Tuo, Director of the Liaison Bureau.

### 郭沫若接见保加利亚科学院代表团

1960-03-01
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见保加利亚科学院代表团
    新华社29日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若今天下午接见了保加利亚科学院代表团团长柳鲍米尔·克雷斯坦诺夫通讯院士和全体团员。郭沫若同客人们进行了亲切的交谈。
    接见时在座的，有中国科学院副院长吴有训、副秘书长谢鑫鹤、地学部主任尹赞勋、地球物理研究所所长赵九章和联络局局长王拓等。
