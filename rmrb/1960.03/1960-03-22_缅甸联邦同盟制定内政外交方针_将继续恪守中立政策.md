Title: Burmese Union League Formulates Domestic and Foreign Policy, Will Continue to Adhere to Neutral Policy
Author:
Date: 1960-03-22
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The three-day meeting of the Supreme Council of the Burmese Clean Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League concluded on the 19th.

    A resolution passed at the meeting stated that the Union League, which will replace the current Clean Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League, will continue to pursue a strict policy of neutrality and strive to establish friendly relations with all countries to help promote world peace. The resolution stated that this neutral policy hopes to do its utmost to eliminate suspicions between countries, strengthen friendship and peace, prevent aggression, and prevent world peace from being threatened.

    A resolution on economic policy passed at the meeting stated that the Union League will give priority to agriculture and forestry; when implementing industrial plans, it will encourage foreign investment. A resolution on domestic policy passed at the meeting stated that the Union League will not hold negotiations with any "rebels."

### 缅甸联邦同盟制定内政外交方针  将继续恪守中立政策

1960-03-22
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸联邦同盟制定内政外交方针
    将继续恪守中立政策
    据新华社讯　缅甸廉洁的反法西斯人民自由同盟全国最高委员会历时三天的会议于19日闭幕。
    会议通过的一项决议说，替代目前的廉洁的反法西斯人民自由同盟的联邦同盟将继续奉行严格的中立政策，并且努力同所有的国家建立友好关系从而帮助促进世界和平。决议说，这种中立政策希望尽最大的努力来消除各国之间的猜疑，加强友好和和平，防止侵略和不让世界和平遭到威胁。
    会议通过的关于经济政策的决议说，联邦同盟将给农业和林业以优先地位；在执行工业计划时，鼓励外国投资。会议通过的关于国内政策的决议说，联邦同盟不同任何“叛乱分子”举行谈判。
