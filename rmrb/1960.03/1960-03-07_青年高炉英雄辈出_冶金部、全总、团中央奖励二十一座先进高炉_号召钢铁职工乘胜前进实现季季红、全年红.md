Title: Young Blast Furnace Heroes Emerge; Ministry of Metallurgical Industry, All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Communist Youth League Central Committee Reward Twenty-One Advanced Blast Furnaces; Call on Steel Workers to Advance Victoriously and Achieve Red Every Quarter, Red All Year Round

Author: 
Date: 1960-03-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 6th.

Young Blast Furnace Heroes Emerge
Ministry of Metallurgical Industry, All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Communist Youth League Central Committee Reward Twenty-One Advanced Blast Furnaces
Call on Steel Workers to Advance Victoriously and Achieve Red Every Quarter, Red All Year Round

Xinhua News Agency, 6th –The Ministry of Metallurgical Industries, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League recently issued a joint circular commending a group of youth blast furnaces that achieved outstanding results in the 1959 National Youth Blast Furnace Competition, and awarded these units prize flags.

The units that were rewarded are: Benxi Iron and Steel Company's No. 1 Steel Plant, No. 1 Youth Blast Furnace; Taiyuan Iron and Steel Company's Ironmaking Plant, Youth Blast Furnace; Benxi Iron and Steel Company's Dongfeng [East Wind] Youth Blast Furnace; Benxi Iron and Steel Company's No. 1 Steel Plant, No. 2 Youth Blast Furnace; Anshan Iron and Steel Company's Ironmaking Plant, No. 9 Blast Furnace; Ma'anshan Iron and Steel Company's No. 1 Ironmaking Plant, No. 5 Blast Furnace; Ma'anshan Iron and Steel Company's No. 1 Ironmaking Plant, No. 15 Blast Furnace; Anhui Hefei Zhengfa Iron and Steel Plant, Youth Pioneer Blast Furnace; Nanjing Yuejin Iron and Steel Plant, No. 3 Blast Furnace; Shandong Jinan Printing and Dyeing Factory, Smelting Workshop, 3 Cubic Meter Small Blast Furnace; Fujian Quanzhou Iron and Steel Plant, Communist Youth League Furnace; Jiangxi Yongxin County No. 3 Iron Plant, Youth Furnace; Hunan Chaling Limin Iron Plant, No. 4 Youth Furnace; Hunan Changning Songbai Iron Plant, Communist Youth League Furnace; Henan Anyang City No. 2 Iron and Steel Plant, 8 Cubic Meter Blast Furnace; Shanxi Yuxian County 55 Machinery Factory, August 1st Youth Blast Furnace; Hebei Shijiazhuang Iron and Steel Plant, Youth No. 3 Blast Furnace; Beijing Xuanwu Iron and Steel Plant, No. 7 Blast Furnace; Sichuan Wanfu Iron and Steel Plant's "Leap Forward" Youth Red Flag Blast Furnace; Guizhou Shuicheng Iron and Steel Plant, Youth Furnace; Gansu Province Huicheng County Xiaohe Iron Plant, No. 2 Furnace.

During the Great Leap Forward in 1959, the youth blast furnace workers of Benxi and Taiyuan jointly proposed to the nation's youth steel workers to launch a campaign of "Quality in Every Furnace, Exceeding Quotas Every Day." This proposal expressed the great aspirations of the vast number of young people to actively respond to the Party's call to build socialism at high speed. It was enthusiastically received, and hundreds of youth blast furnaces across the country joined the competition, forming a broad mass movement. The production of the youth blast furnaces participating in the competition has significantly improved, and the vast majority of the blast furnaces have entered 1960 ahead of schedule and at high speed.

The joint circular from Ministry of Metallurgical Industry, All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Communist Youth League Central Committee said: This year is a year of continued leap forward in the national economy. In this new year, the Party has set even more glorious and greater tasks for steel industry production. It is hoped that the young workers and all workers on the steel front will hold high the banner of Mao Zedong even higher, advance victoriously on the basis of a good start, and achieve Red every quarter and Red all year round; continue to invigorate the spirit of dauntless creativity, vigorously promote the mechanization of manual operations and the automation of mechanical operations; practice strict economy, overcome various difficulties, and make new and greater contributions to the high-speed development of China's steel industry.

### 青年高炉英雄辈出  冶金部、全总、团中央奖励二十一座先进高炉  号召钢铁职工乘胜前进实现季季红、全年红

1960-03-07
第2版()
专栏：

    青年高炉英雄辈出
    冶金部、全总、团中央奖励二十一座先进高炉
    号召钢铁职工乘胜前进实现季季红、全年红
    新华社6日讯　冶金工业部、中华全国总工会、共青团中央最近发出联合通报，表扬1959年全国青年高炉竞赛中获得突出成绩的一批青年高炉，并给这些单位发了奖旗。
    受到奖励的单位是：本溪钢铁公司第一钢铁厂一号青年高炉，太原钢铁公司炼铁厂青年高炉，本溪钢铁公司东风号青年高炉，本溪钢铁公司第一钢铁厂二号青年高炉，鞍钢炼铁厂九号高炉，马鞍山钢铁公司第一炼铁厂五号高炉，马鞍山钢铁公司第一炼铁厂十五号高炉，安徽合肥政法钢铁一厂青年先锋号高炉，南京跃进钢铁厂三号高炉，山东济南印染厂冶炼车间三立方米小高炉，福建泉州钢铁厂共青炉，江西永新县第三铁厂青年炉，湖南茶陵利民铁厂四号青年炉，湖南常宁松柏铁厂共青炉，河南安阳市第二钢铁厂八立方米高炉，山西盂县五五机械厂八一青年高炉，河北石家庄市钢铁厂青年三号高炉，北京宣武钢铁厂七号高炉，四川万福钢铁厂“跃进号”青年红旗高炉，贵州水城钢铁厂青年炉，甘肃省徽成县小河铁厂二号炉。
    在1959年大跃进中，本溪、太原青年高炉职工曾联合向全国青年钢铁工人提出了开展“炉炉优质、日日超额”的倡议。这一倡议表达了广大青年积极响应党的号召高速度建设社会主义的雄心壮志，所以得到了热烈响应，全国有几百座青年高炉投入竞赛，形成了广泛的群众运动。参加竞赛的青年高炉，生产都有显著提高，绝大多数高炉都提前高速度地跨进了1960年。
    冶金工业部、中华全国总工会、共青团中央的联合通报说：今年是国民经济继续跃进的一年。在这新的一年中，党对钢铁工业生产提出了更加光荣、更加巨大的任务。希望钢铁战线上的青年职工和全体职工一起，更高地举起毛泽东旗帜，在开门红的基础上乘胜前进，作到季季红、全年红；继续振奋大无畏的创造精神，大搞手工操作机械化和机械操作自动化；厉行节约，战胜各种困难，为高速度发展我国钢铁工业作出新的更大的贡献。
