Title: Gong Peng Hosts a Banquet for the Nepalese Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-03-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 20th: Gong Peng, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, held a banquet tonight to entertain the Nepalese Journalists Delegation, which is currently paying a friendly visit to China.

    Among those present at the banquet were Deng Gang and Li Wu, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the All-China Journalists Association.

### 龚澎宴请尼泊尔新闻工作者代表团

1960-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    龚澎宴请尼泊尔新闻工作者代表团
    新华社20日讯　外交部新闻司司长龚澎今晚举行宴会，招待正在我国进行友好访问的尼泊尔新闻工作者代表团。
    出席宴会作陪的，有中国新闻工作者协会书记处书记邓岗、李伍等。
