Title: Prime Minister Koirala Visits Hangzhou
Author: 
Date: 1960-03-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, March 18th:  The Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Koirala, and his wife, along with the accompanying Nepalese dignitaries, arrived in Hangzhou by special plane at 3:00 PM today from Shanghai. They were accompanied by Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Pan Zili, our ambassador to Nepal, and his wife, and Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

At the Hangzhou Airport, flying the national flags of China and Nepal, Prime Minister Koirala and other distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by Huo Shilian and Ren Yili, Vice Governors of Zhejiang Province, Zhang Shixiang, Vice Mayor of Hangzhou, Yu Jiyi, Vice Chairman of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the CPPCC, Senior Colonel Liang Jinhua, head of the Zhejiang Garrison, and people from all walks of life in Zhejiang Province and Hangzhou City. A group of Young Pioneers presented flowers to the distinguished guests.

At 3:30 PM, Prime Minister Koirala and the Nepalese dignitaries, accompanied by Vice Governor Ren Yili, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo, and others, visited the Jianqiao People's Commune on the outskirts of Hangzhou. The distinguished guests expressed great interest in the new-style agricultural implements manufactured by the workers of the commune's farm machinery manufacturing plant, such as rice transplanters, rice threshers, deep plows, and artificial rainmaking machines. They praised the workers for their efforts in technical innovation and for creating many machines to replace manual labor. After visiting the commune's new village, which has twenty-seven buildings and houses more than a thousand commune members, the distinguished guests expressed satisfaction with the members' good life. The distinguished guests were warmly received and welcomed by the commune members when they visited the new village's kindergarten, canteen, and commune member's homes. In the evening, Vice Governor Huo Shilian hosted a banquet in honor of Prime Minister Koirala and his wife, as well as the Nepalese dignitaries.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, March 18th: The Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Koirala, and his wife, along with the accompanying Nepalese dignitaries, concluded their visit to Shanghai and left Shanghai by special plane this afternoon for Hangzhou, accompanied by Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of our country, Pan Zili, our Ambassador to Nepal, and his wife, and Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Cao Diqiu, Vice Mayor of Shanghai, and his wife, Vice Mayors Liu Shuzhou, Song Jiwen, Zhao Zukang, and Sheng Pihua, Major General Li Binshan, Head of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, responsible persons from various departments of the Municipal People's Committee, local organizations of various democratic parties, and people's organizations went to the airport to see off the distinguished guests.

Consuls-General, consuls and those handling overseas Chinese affairs from various countries in Shanghai also went to the airport to see them off.

Before boarding the plane, Prime Minister Koirala and others shook hands with Vice Mayor Cao Diqiu and other people who came to see them off, and accepted flowers presented by the Young Pioneers.

This morning, accompanied by Vice Mayor Cao Diqiu and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo, the distinguished guests visited the China Paper Company and the Shanghai Cement Factory, where they were warmly welcomed by the workers of the two factories. During the visit to the China Paper Company, Prime Minister Koirala carefully observed the production situation in each workshop. At the cement factory, the Prime Minister listened to a briefing on how the factory's employees, under the guidance of the policy of combining indigenous and foreign methods, had emancipated their minds and vigorously developed the production of cement using indigenous vertical kilns.

### 柯伊拉腊首相到杭州访问

1960-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

    柯伊拉腊首相到杭州访问
    新华社杭州18日电　尼泊尔王国首相柯伊拉腊和夫人以及随行的尼泊尔贵宾，在外交部副部长罗贵波、我国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和夫人，外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文陪同下，今天下午三时乘专机由上海到达杭州。
    在飘扬着中尼两国国旗的杭州飞机场上，柯伊拉腊首相等贵宾受到浙江省副省长霍士廉、任一力，杭州市副市长张世祥，政协浙江省委员会副主席余纪一，浙江驻军首长梁金华大校以及浙江省、杭州市各方面人士的热烈欢迎。一群少年先锋队员向贵宾们献了鲜花。
    下午三时半，柯伊拉腊首相和尼泊尔贵宾由任一力副省长、外交部副部长罗贵波等陪同，参观访问了杭州市郊笕桥人民公社。贵宾们对这个公社农具机械制造厂工人们制造的插秧机、打稻机、深耕犁和人工降雨机等新式农具感到很大兴趣，对工人们大闹技术革新创造出许多代替手工操作的机器表示赞扬。贵宾们在参观了有着二十七幢楼房、居住着一千多社员的公社新村后，对社员的美好生活表示满意。贵宾们在参观这个新村的幼儿园、食堂、社员家庭时，受到了社员热情接待和欢迎。晚间，霍士廉副省长设宴招待柯伊拉腊首相和夫人及尼泊尔贵宾。
  新华社上海18日电　尼泊尔王国首相柯伊拉腊和夫人及随行的尼泊尔贵宾，结束在上海的参观访问，今天下午由我国外交部副部长罗贵波、我国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力及夫人、外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文陪同，乘专机离开上海前往杭州访问。
    到机场欢送贵宾的，有上海市副市长曹荻秋和夫人，副市长刘述周、宋季文、赵祖康、盛丕华，中国人民解放军上海警备区部队首长李彬山少将，市人民委员会各部门及各民主党派地方组织、各人民团体负责人等。
    各国驻上海总领事、领事以及办理侨务人员也到机场欢送。
    柯伊拉腊首相等在登机前，同曹荻秋副市长以及其他送行的人们一一握手告别，并接受了少先队员献给的鲜花。
    今天上午，贵宾们在曹荻秋副市长和外交部副部长罗贵波等陪同下，参观了中国版纸公司和上海水泥厂，受到这两个厂职工的热烈欢迎。在参观中国版纸公司时，柯伊拉腊首相很仔细地观看了各车间的生产情况。在水泥厂，首相听取了这个厂的职工在土洋并举方针指导下，解放思想，大搞土立窑生产水泥情况的介绍。
