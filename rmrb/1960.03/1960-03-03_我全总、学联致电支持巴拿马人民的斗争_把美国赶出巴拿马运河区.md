Title: ACFTU, All-China Students' Federation Send Messages of Support to the Struggle of the Panamanian People: Drive the United States out of the Panama Canal Zone
Author:
Date: 1960-03-03
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, March 2nd: The Panamanian people held demonstrations on March 1st demanding the safeguarding of Panama's sovereignty over the Canal Zone. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions (ACFTU) and the All-China Students' Federation sent telegrams on February 29th to Panamanian workers' and students' organizations, firmly supporting the Panamanian people's just struggle to safeguard national independence and sovereignty.

	Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the ACFTU, said in a telegram to the Latin American Labor Federation for transmission to Panamanian workers: "Chinese workers fully support the just action of our Panamanian worker brothers in holding demonstrations on March 1st to safeguard national sovereignty over the Canal Zone. We wish you success in your struggle against U.S. imperialism's aggression and to defend the integrity of your national territory and sovereignty."

	The All-China Students' Federation said in a telegram to the Panamanian Student Federation and the Panamanian University Student Federation that it extends its respect and firm support on behalf of all Chinese students to the Panamanian people and students who are fighting bravely to safeguard national independence and sovereignty. The telegram said: "We are deeply convinced that with the firm support of the people of Latin America and the world, as long as Panamanian students unite with the people, unite as one, and persevere in the struggle, they will surely be able to drive U.S. imperialism out of the Panama Canal Zone! The Panamanian flag must fly over the Canal Zone!"

### 我全总、学联致电支持巴拿马人民的斗争  把美国赶出巴拿马运河区

1960-03-03
第6版()
专栏：

    我全总、学联致电支持巴拿马人民的斗争
    把美国赶出巴拿马运河区
    新华社2日讯　巴拿马人民在1日举行要求维护巴拿马运河区主权的示威游行。中华全国总工会和中华全国学生联合会在2月29日分别打电报给巴拿马工人和学生组织，坚决支持巴拿马人民为维护民族独立和主权而进行的正义斗争。
    中华全国总工会主席刘宁一在给拉丁美洲劳联转巴拿马工人的电报中说：“中国工人完全支持巴拿马工人兄弟为维护运河区的国家主权于3月1日举行示威游行的正义行动。祝你们在反对美帝国主义侵略，保卫国家领土主权完整的斗争中取得成就。”
    中华全国学生联合会在给巴拿马学生联合会和巴拿马大学生联合会的电报中说，谨代表全中国学生向为维护民族独立和主权而英勇斗争的巴拿马人民和学生表示敬意和坚决支持。电报说：“我们深信，在拉丁美洲和全世界人民的坚决支持下，只要巴拿马学生同人民一道，团结一致，坚持斗争，就一定能够把美帝国主义从巴拿马运河区赶出去！巴拿马国旗一定要在运河区的上空飘扬！”
