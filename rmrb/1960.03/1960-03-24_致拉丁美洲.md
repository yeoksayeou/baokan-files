Title: To Latin America
Author: Yan Ming
Date: 1960-03-24
Page: 8

Column: A Song for the People of Latin America

    To Latin America
    Yan Ming
    Oh, Cuba, Cuba
    Golden coasts,
    Vast sugarcane fields,
    Emerald green coffee plantations,
    Rose-blooming mountain peaks.
    How beautiful,
    How prosperous,
    Oh, Cuba, Cuba,
    Pearl of the Caribbean Sea!
    Larks sing in the woods,
    Colorful flags flutter on the roads,
    Oil gushes from the ground,
    Brass shines in the mines.
    How free,
    How joyous,
    Oh, Cuba, Cuba,
    Your fields are bathed in sunshine…
    Hello, hello,
    Workers in the tobacco factories!
    You support the collapsed factories,
    And let new days grow in your hands.
    Hello, hello,
    Barefoot peasant brothers!
    With the joy of the first harvest,
    Driving ox carts laden with sugarcane.
    Hello, hello,
    Brothers of the rebel army!
    Wearing brimmed straw hats,
    Guarding the coastal outposts…
    The giant statue of José Martí,
    Stands in Havana Square,
    Cuba, like a tall palm,
    You grow in the stormy land!
    I throw myself into your battle
    Forward! Forward!
    Squad after squad of soldiers,
    Chest held high,
    Arm in arm!
    Raise! Raise!
    The fiery banner,
    Fluttering in the wind,
    Cheers erupting!
    A forest of bayonets,
    Gleaming,
    The burning land,
    Boiling and surging.
    Latin America,
    Latin America!
    I throw myself into your battle,
    And breathe with you!
    I enter your阵地,
    With bullets of poetry—
    At the Washington bandits
    Shoot! Shoot!…
    The rainbow of dawn,
    Hangs high in Cuba,
    Throwing the dictators into the sea,
    Tearing apart the black flags of the pirates!
    Latin America,
    Latin America!
    Six hundred million people stand shoulder to shoulder with you,
    Six hundred million people are with you!

### 致拉丁美洲
晏明
1960-03-24
第8版()
专栏：献给拉丁美洲人民的歌

    致拉丁美洲
    晏明
    呵，古巴，古巴
    金黄的海岸，
    辽阔的蔗田，
    翠绿的咖啡园，
    玫瑰盛开的山巅。
    多么美丽，
    多么富庶，
    呵，古巴，古巴，
    加勒比海的明珠！
    云雀在林子里歌唱，
    彩旗在大路上飘扬，
    石油在地下喷涌，
    黄铜在矿山闪亮。
    多么自由，
    多么欢畅，
    呵，古巴，古巴，
    你的田野撒满阳光……
    你好，你好呵，
    烟草厂的工人兄弟！
    你扶起倒闭的工厂，
    让新的日子在你手里生长。
    你好，你好呵，
    赤脚的农民兄弟！
    带着第一次丰收的喜悦，
    赶着载满甘蔗的牛车。
    你好，你好呵，
    起义军的兄弟！
    戴着卷边草帽，
    守卫着海岸的岗哨……
    何塞·马蒂的巨像，
    矗立在哈瓦那广场，
    古巴呵，像挺拔的棕榈，
    你在暴风雨的大地成长！
    我投入你的战斗
    行进！行进！
    一队一队的士兵，
    挺起胸膛，
    挽着臂膀！
    飘起！飘起！
    烈火般的大旗，
    迎风招展，
    欢声四起！
    刺刀的森林，
    闪闪发光，
    燃烧的土地，
    沸腾激荡。
    拉丁美洲呵，
    拉丁美洲！
    我投入你的战斗，
    和你一同呼吸！
    我进入你的阵地，
    用诗的子弹——
    向华盛顿强盗
    射击！射击！……
    黎明的彩虹，
    在古巴高高挂起，
    把独裁者扔进海里，
    撕碎海盗们的黑旗！
    拉丁美洲呵，
    拉丁美洲！
    六亿人和你肩并肩，
    六亿人和你在一起！
