Title: Polish Pianist Berezhinsky Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-03-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 21st:  Polish pianist Jan Berezhinsky, who is visiting China for performances under the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

　　Jan Berezhinsky has received an honorary award at the International Chopin Piano Competition and the Polish Gold Cross Medal.

### 波兰钢琴家贝莱仁斯基到北京

1960-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰钢琴家贝莱仁斯基到北京
    新华社21日讯　根据中波文化合作协定1960年执行计划来中国访问演出的波兰钢琴家杨·贝莱仁斯基，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    杨·贝莱仁斯基曾获得国际萧邦钢琴比赛荣誉奖和波兰金质十字勋章。
