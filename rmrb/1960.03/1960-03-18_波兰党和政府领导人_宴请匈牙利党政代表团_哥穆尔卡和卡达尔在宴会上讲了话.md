Title: Polish Party and Government Leaders Host a Banquet for the Hungarian Party and Government Delegation, Gomulka and Kadar Spoke at the Banquet
Author: 
Date: 1960-03-18
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 16th: Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee First Secretary Gomulka and Chairman of the Council of Ministers Cyrankiewicz hosted a banquet tonight in honor of the Hungarian Party and Government Delegation.

    Gomulka said at the banquet: The visit of Hungarian comrades and friends gives us the opportunity to exchange views on the continued development of friendly relations between Poland and Hungary and on relevant international issues.

    He pointed out that the countries of the socialist camp are persistently and consistently committed to eliminating international tensions. The first socialist country, the Soviet Union, has made significant contributions to the easing of the international situation. The Soviet Union's major peace initiatives, especially the initiative for complete and thorough disarmament, are fully supported by Poland and all socialist countries.

    The head of the Hungarian Party and Government Delegation, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Workers' Party, Kadar, then spoke. After mentioning the long-standing friendship between the two peoples, he said, "We are proud of the brilliant achievements our country has made in the past years, which demonstrate that the socialist system is superior to the capitalist system."

    Kadar said that the countries of the socialist camp are united in safeguarding world peace. "We see it as our responsibility to strengthen the unity of socialist countries."

    Other Polish party and government leaders, prominent figures from all walks of life, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Poland attended the banquet.

    On the same day, the Hungarian Party and Government Delegation paid a visit to Gomulka and Cyrankiewicz.

    The Hungarian Party and Government Delegation arrived in Warsaw on the 16th.

### 波兰党和政府领导人  宴请匈牙利党政代表团  哥穆尔卡和卡达尔在宴会上讲了话

1960-03-18
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰党和政府领导人
    宴请匈牙利党政代表团
    哥穆尔卡和卡达尔在宴会上讲了话
    新华社华沙16日电　波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记哥穆尔卡、部长会议主席西伦凯维兹，今晚设宴招待匈牙利党政代表团。
    哥穆尔卡在宴会上讲话说：匈牙利同志和朋友们的访问，使得我们有可能就有关继续发展波匈两国友好关系和有关的国际问题交换意见。
    他指出，社会主义阵营各国坚持地和始终如一地致力于消除国际紧张局势。第一个社会主义国家苏联对国际局势的缓和作出了重大的贡献。苏联的重大的和平倡议，特别是全面彻底裁军的倡议，得到波兰和所有社会主义国家的充分支持。
    匈牙利党政代表团团长、社会主义工人党中央委员会第一书记卡达尔接着讲了话。他在谈到两国人民长期以来的友谊之后说，我们为我国在过去的年代中所取得的辉煌成就而感到自豪，它表明了社会主义制度比资本主义制度优越。
    卡达尔说，社会主义阵营各国团结一致地保障着世界和平。我们把加强社会主义国家的团结看作是自己的责任。
    波兰其他党政领导人以及各界著名人士、各国驻波兰外交使节出席了宴会。
    同一天，匈牙利党政代表团曾拜会了哥穆尔卡和西伦凯维兹。
    匈牙利党政代表团是在16日到达华沙的。
