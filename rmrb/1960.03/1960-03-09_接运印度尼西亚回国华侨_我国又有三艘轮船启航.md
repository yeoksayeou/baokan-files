Title: To Transport Overseas Chinese from Indonesia Back to China, Three More Ships Set Sail

Author:
Date: 1960-03-09
Page: 4

To Transport Overseas Chinese from Indonesia Back to China
    Our Country Sends Three More Ships to Set Sail

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 7th: Our government has dispatched the "Da Bao Kang," "Hai Huang," and "Fu An" three ships to transport overseas Chinese back to the country, and they are once again sailing to Indonesia. Among them, the "Da Bao Kang" and "Hai Huang" ships set sail from Huangpu Port on the morning of the 6th, and the "Fu An" ship set sail as early as the evening of the 4th.

On the afternoon of the 5th, before the "Da Bao Kang" and "Hai Huang" ships set sail, Zhong Qingfa, Director of the Guangzhou Office of the People's Republic of China Reception and Settlement Committee for Returned Overseas Chinese, met with the staff accompanying the ships. He encouraged them to do a good job in receiving returned overseas Chinese and to transport back to China those compatriots in Indonesia who are displaced, have no means of livelihood, and are unwilling to continue living there.

### 接运印度尼西亚回国华侨  我国又有三艘轮船启航

1960-03-09
第4版()
专栏：

    接运印度尼西亚回国华侨
    我国又有三艘轮船启航
    新华社广州7日电　我国政府派出接运华侨回国的“大宝康”、“海皇”、“福安”三艘轮船，再次开往印度尼西亚。其中“大宝康”、“海皇”两艘轮船在6日早晨从黄埔港启航，“福安”轮早在4日晚启航。
    5日下午，“大宝康”、“海皇”两艘轮船启航前，中华人民共和国接待和安置归国华侨委员会驻广州办公室主任钟庆发，接见了随船工作人员，勉励他们做好接待归侨工作，把在印度尼西亚流离失所无以为生和不愿在当地继续居留的侨胞接运回国。
