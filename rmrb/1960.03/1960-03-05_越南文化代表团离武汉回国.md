Title: Vietnamese Cultural Delegation Leaves Wuhan for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-03-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, March 3rd: The Vietnamese cultural delegation, led by Le Liem, Deputy Minister of Culture of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, left Wuhan by train at 4:00 PM today for home after concluding its visit to Wuhan. The delegation arrived in Wuhan by train from Guangzhou on the 1st. During their stay in Wuhan, they visited the Wuhan Iron and Steel Corporation, the Wuhan Heavy Machine Tool Plant, the Yangtze River Bridge, and the Wuhan People's Art Theater, and attended a performance of the opera "Honghu Red Guards" by the Hubei Provincial Experimental Opera Troupe.

Last night, Song Yiping, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, met with the delegation and hosted a banquet to bid farewell to the guests.

### 越南文化代表团离武汉回国

1960-03-05
第5版()
专栏：

    越南文化代表团离武汉回国
    新华社武汉3日电　由越南民主共和国文化部副部长黎廉率领的越南文化代表团，结束在武汉的访问以后，今天下午四时乘火车离开武汉回国。代表团是在1日由广州乘火车到达武汉的。他们在武汉期间，分别参观了武汉钢铁公司、武汉重型机床厂、长江大桥、武汉人民艺术剧院等，并且出席观看了湖北省实验歌剧团演出的歌剧“洪湖赤卫队”。
    昨晚，中共武汉市委书记处书记宋一平会见了代表团，并设宴为客人们送行。
