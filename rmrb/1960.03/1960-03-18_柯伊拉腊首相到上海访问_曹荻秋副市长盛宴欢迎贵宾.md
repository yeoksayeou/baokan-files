Title: Prime Minister Koirala Visits Shanghai; Deputy Mayor Cao Diqiu Hosts Grand Banquet to Welcome Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-03-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, March 17th: Nepalese Prime Minister B.P. Koirala and his wife, along with accompanying Nepalese dignitaries, arrived in Shanghai by special plane from Zhengzhou at 12:15 PM today, accompanied by Vice Foreign Minister Luo Guibo, Chinese Ambassador to Nepal Pan Zili and his wife, and Director of the Protocol Department of the Foreign Ministry Yu Peiwen. They were warmly welcomed at the airport by over two thousand people, including representatives from the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, local organizations of various democratic parties, people's organizations, and people from all walks of life.

Today, Shanghai's Longhua Airport was filled with a solemn, warm, and friendly atmosphere. The national flags of China and Nepal fluttered in the wind, and a huge banner reading "Welcome Prime Minister B.P. Koirala of the Kingdom of Nepal" hung over the airport terminal. As Prime Minister Koirala and his wife stepped out of the plane amidst enthusiastic applause and the sound of gongs and drums, Shanghai Deputy Mayor Cao Diqiu and his wife approached the plane to greet the distinguished guests. At this moment, a group of Young Pioneers rushed forward to present flowers to Prime Minister and Mrs. Koirala, as well as the visiting Nepalese dignitaries. As Prime Minister Koirala met with the welcoming crowd, cheers, applause, and the sound of gongs and drums merged into one, with people shouting "Welcome Prime Minister Koirala!", "Long Live Sino-Nepalese Friendship!", "Long Live the Solidarity of Asian and African Countries!", and "Long Live World Peace!". Girls repeatedly broke out of the welcoming line to present bouquets of flowers to Prime Minister and Mrs. Koirala, expressing their deep respect.

Also present at the airport to welcome the guests were Shanghai Deputy Mayors Liu Shuzhou, Song Jiwen, Sheng Pihua, and Zhao Zukang, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPPCC Hu Juewen, and Major General Li Binshan, leading officer of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and others.

Consuls-General, consuls, and personnel handling overseas Chinese affairs from various countries stationed in Shanghai also went to the airport to welcome the guests.

In the afternoon, Prime Minister Koirala and his wife, along with the accompanying Nepalese dignitaries, visited the Industrial Exhibition, which reflects Shanghai's industrial construction achievements over the past ten years, accompanied by Deputy Mayor Cao Diqiu and his wife, Vice Foreign Minister Luo Guibo, and others. The distinguished guests successively visited the mechanical and electrical hall, the textile hall, and the light industry hall, viewing the various types of machine tools, a wide variety of textiles, and novel daily necessities on display.

The distinguished guests then visited the Children's Palace. Here, the children presented Prime Minister Koirala and his wife with red scarves and performed dances, choruses, harmonica ensembles, and group dances. This afternoon, the distinguished guests toured Shanghai's city landscape and ascended the Shanghai Mansion, overlooking the city's scenery.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, March 17th: Tonight, Shanghai Deputy Mayor Cao Diqiu held a grand banquet to warmly welcome Prime Minister B.P. Koirala and his wife, as well as the accompanying Nepalese dignitaries.

At the banquet, filled with a friendly atmosphere, the hosts and guests repeatedly raised their glasses, jointly wishing for the further consolidation and development of Sino-Nepalese friendship. Deputy Mayor Cao Diqiu spoke first at the banquet, extending a warm welcome to the distinguished guests.

Prime Minister Koirala then spoke, saying: During our visit to China, whether we went to large cities, small cities, or people's communes, we encountered enthusiastic friendship. On behalf of the Nepalese people, I also express our goodwill and friendship. The Prime Minister spoke of his impressions of visiting various parts of China, praising the construction achievements and efforts of the Chinese people. He said that this afternoon we visited the Shanghai Industrial Exhibition, from which we saw China's progress in industrial construction. Although Nepal and China have different social systems, our tasks of building our own countries and improving the lives of our people are similar. He also said that world peace is indispensable to us. Both Nepal and China need a peaceful international environment to build their own countries. The growing friendship between Nepal and China will contribute to the progress of the cause of peace.

Also attending the banquet were Vice Foreign Minister Luo Guibo, Chinese Ambassador to Nepal Pan Zili and his wife, and Director of the Protocol Department of the Foreign Ministry Yu Peiwen, who accompanied the distinguished guests on their visit to Shanghai.

Also present at the banquet were Shanghai Deputy Mayors Liu Shuzhou, Song Jiwen, Zhao Zukang, and Sheng Pihua, Major General Li Binshan, leading officer of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, heads of various departments of the Municipal People's Committee, local organizations of various democratic parties, and people's organizations.

Consuls-General, consuls, and personnel handling overseas Chinese affairs from various countries stationed in Shanghai were invited to attend the banquet. After the banquet, the distinguished guests watched a wonderful artistic performance.

### 柯伊拉腊首相到上海访问  曹荻秋副市长盛宴欢迎贵宾

1960-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

    柯伊拉腊首相到上海访问
    曹荻秋副市长盛宴欢迎贵宾
    据新华社上海17日电　尼泊尔王国首相柯伊拉腊和夫人以及随行的尼泊尔贵宾，在外交部副部长罗贵波、我国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和夫人、外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文陪同下，今天中午十二时十五分乘专机由郑州到上海。贵宾们在机场上受到上海市人民委员会、各民主党派地方组织、各人民团体负责人和各界群众两千多人热烈欢迎。
    今天，上海龙华机场洋溢着隆重、热烈、友好的气氛，中国和尼泊尔两国国旗迎风飘扬，候机大厦上悬挂着“欢迎尼泊尔王国毕·普·柯伊拉腊首相”的巨大横幅。当柯伊拉腊首相和夫人在热烈的掌声和锣鼓声中步出机舱时，上海市副市长曹荻秋和夫人走近飞机，迎接贵宾。这时，一群少先队员拥上去向柯伊拉腊首相和夫人，以及随同前来访问的尼泊尔贵宾们献了鲜花。当柯伊拉腊首相同欢迎群众见面的时候，欢呼声、掌声、锣鼓声汇成一片，人们高呼“欢迎柯伊拉腊首相”、“中尼友好万岁！”、“亚非国家团结万岁”、“世界和平万岁”。姑娘们不时从欢迎的行列里跑出来向柯伊拉腊首相和夫人献上一束束鲜花，表示深挚的敬意。
    今天，到机场欢迎的还有：上海市副市长刘述周、宋季文、盛丕华、赵祖康，政协上海市委副主席胡厥文，中国人民解放军上海警备区部队首长李彬山少将等。
    各国驻上海的总领事、领事和办理侨务人员也到机场欢迎。
    下午，柯伊拉腊首相和夫人及随行的尼泊尔贵宾们，在曹荻秋副市长和夫人、外交部副部长罗贵波等陪同下，参观了反映上海十年来工业建设成就的工业展览会。贵宾们先后在机电馆、纺织馆和轻工业馆，观看了陈列在这里的各式各样的机床、名目繁多的纺织品、以及式样新颖的日常生活用品。
    贵宾们接着参观了少年宫。在这里，孩子们向柯伊拉腊首相和夫人献了红领巾，表演了舞蹈、合唱、口琴合奏和集体舞等。今天下午，贵宾们参观了上海市容，并登上了上海大厦，眺望上海景色。
    据新华社上海17日电　今晚，上海市副市长曹荻秋举行盛大宴会，热烈欢迎尼泊尔王国首相柯伊拉腊和夫人以及随行的尼泊尔贵宾。
    在充满友好气氛的宴会上，宾主不断举杯，共祝中尼两国友谊进一步巩固和发展。曹荻秋副市长在宴会上首先讲话，向贵宾们表示热烈的欢迎。
    柯伊拉腊首相接着讲话，他说：我们在中国访问，不论是到大城市、小城市还是人民公社，都遇到热情的友谊，我代表尼泊尔人民也向你们表达善意和友谊的感情。首相讲述了他访问中国各地的印象，盛赞中国人民的建设成就和努力。他说，今天下午我们参观了上海工业展览会，从这里我们看到了中国在工业建设上的进展。虽然尼泊尔和中国的社会制度不同，但是我们建设自己的国家和提高人民生活的任务，却是类似的。他还说，世界和平对我们是绝不可少的，尼泊尔和中国都需要一个和平的国际环境来建设自己的国家。尼中两国日益增长的友谊，将有助于和平事业的进展。
    陪同贵宾来上海访问的我国外交部副部长罗贵波、我国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和夫人、外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文等，出席了宴会。
    出席宴会作陪的，还有上海市副市长刘述周、宋季文、赵祖康、盛丕华，中国人民解放军上海警备区部队首长李彬山少将，市人民委员会各部门及各民主党派地方组织、各人民团体负责人等。
    各国驻上海总领事、领事和办理侨务人员应邀出席了宴会。宴会后，贵宾们观看了精彩的文艺表演。
