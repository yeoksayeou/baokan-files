Title: New Cuba Embodies the Aspirations of the Latin American People; Latin American People Support the Cuban People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1960-03-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, March 19th. (TASS dispatch from New York, March 18th): Latin American News Agency reported that people in Latin American countries support the Cuban people's movement to defend and build a new national life.

    The news agency reported from Lima that the Peruvian Committee for the Defense of the Cuban Revolution delivered a letter to the Cuban Embassy in Lima, condemning the atrocities that caused the explosion of the "La Coubre" ship and other counter-revolutionary atrocities aimed at opposing the true revolution in the Americas. The letter expressed solidarity with the Cuban people's struggle.

    The news agency reported from Rio de Janeiro that Brazilian National Democratic Union member and Congressman Doria, speaking in the House of Representatives, agreed with presidential candidate Quadros' decision to visit Cuba and believed that the Cuban revolution was "a political and economic liberation."

    Latin American News Agency reported from San Juan that Senator Rivera, a member of the Puerto Rican Independence Party, submitted a resolution to the Senate expressing the Puerto Rican people's support for a liberated Cuba. Rivera said, "The Republic of Cuba is developing under conditions of complete and legitimate sovereignty... implementing the most extensive social and economic transformation plan in the history of our Latin American continent. The new Cuba embodies the deepest aspirations of the people of Latin American countries. The Cuban revolution has added a concrete form to the hope of freedom and justice."

    Xinhua News Agency, Lima, March 17th: Four thousand workers and students in the Peruvian capital unanimously expressed their warm support for the Cuban revolution at a rally on the evening of the 16th.

    The participants condemned the authorities' policy of serving American monopoly organizations and trampling on public freedoms, and demanded land reform and the abolition of the university charter that deprives students of autonomy.

    Speakers also expressed dissatisfaction with Foreign Minister Barrenechea's delay in taking action on the "La Brea y Pariñas" ruling. The American International Petroleum Company used this ruling to occupy the rich oil deposits in the La Brea y Pariñas area. Recently, eighty-one Peruvian congressmen submitted a request to the House of Representatives to abolish this ruling.

### 新的古巴体现了拉丁美洲人民愿望  拉丁美洲人民支持古巴人民斗争

1960-03-20
第4版()
专栏：

    新的古巴体现了拉丁美洲人民愿望
    拉丁美洲人民支持古巴人民斗争
    新华社19日讯　据塔斯社纽约18日讯：拉丁美洲通讯社报道了拉丁美洲各国人民支持古巴人民保卫和建设国家新生活的运动。
    这家通讯社发自利马的报道说，秘鲁保卫古巴革命委员会交给了古巴驻利马大使馆一封信，谴责制造“勒库布尔号”轮船爆炸的暴行以及其他旨在反对美洲真正革命的反革命暴行。这封信表示声援古巴人民的斗争。
    这家通讯社自里约热内卢报道说，巴西全国民主联盟盟员、议员多里亚在众议院发言时赞同总统候选人夸德罗斯访问古巴的决定，并且认为古巴的革命是“政治上和经济上的解放”。
    拉丁美洲通讯社自圣胡安报道说，争取波多黎各独立党人、参议员里维拉向参议院提出一项决议，表示波多黎各人民支持解放了的古巴。里维拉说，“古巴共和国正在完全的和合法的主权条件下发展……实现着我们拉丁美洲大陆历史上的最广泛的社会和经济改造计划。新的古巴体现了拉丁美洲各国人民最深切的愿望。古巴的革命为自由和正义的希望增添了具体的形式。”
    新华社利马17日电　秘鲁首都四千工人学生在16日晚一次集会上一致表示热烈支持古巴革命。
    与会者谴责了当局为美国垄断组织服务和蹂躏公众自由的政策，并要求进行土地改革和废除剥夺学生自治权的大学章程。
    发言者还对外交部长巴雷内切亚在“拉布雷亚—帕里纳斯”判定书问题上迟迟不采取行动表示不满。美国的国际石油公司利用这项判定书霸占了拉布雷亚—帕里纳斯地区的丰富石油矿藏。最近秘鲁八十一名众议员曾经向众议院提出了废除这项判定书的要求。
