Title: Nepalese Newspapers Comment on Nepalese Prime Minister's Visit to China: Koirala's Visit to China Strengthens Sino-Nepalese Friendship
Author:
Date: 1960-03-23
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 21st - Kathmandu Dispatch: Nepalese newspapers warmly support Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala's visit to China and have published extensive coverage of his activities in China. These newspapers also prominently reported on the friendly reception and warm welcome given to the Nepalese delegation by the Chinese government and people. The "Ideal" newspaper published an editorial on March 15th, believing that Koirala's visit will greatly strengthen Sino-Nepalese friendly relations.

The editorial, when discussing the talks between the Chinese Premier and the Nepalese Prime Minister, particularly pointed out that the Chinese Premier and the Nepalese Prime Minister emphasized the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and the strengthening of friendly relations between the two countries. It expressed its belief that the Sino-Nepalese border issue will be resolved in this harmonious atmosphere.

The "Ideal" newspaper also particularly pointed out in its editorial that the Chinese newspapers attach great importance to the friendly relations between the two countries and gave Prime Minister Koirala a warm welcome.

The "New Society" newspaper published an editorial on March 7th, wishing the Nepalese delegation visiting China success. It said that this is a strong delegation, which is expected to strengthen Sino-Nepalese friendship and resolve existing problems between the two countries. The editorial said, "At such a moment, we condemn all those who attempt to sow seeds of discord between Nepal and China."

The "Current Affairs" newspaper said in an editorial that the delegation will represent the feelings of the Nepalese people and strengthen the relations between the two friendly countries.

### 尼泊尔报纸评论尼首相访华之行  柯伊拉腊访华加强了中尼友好

1960-03-23
第5版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔报纸评论尼首相访华之行
    柯伊拉腊访华加强了中尼友好
    新华社新德里21日电　加德满都消息：尼泊尔报纸热烈支持尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊访问中国，并且大量刊载了他在中国活动的消息。这些报纸还突出地报道了中国政府和人民对尼泊尔代表团的友好接待和热烈欢迎。“理想报”3月15日发表社论认为，柯伊拉腊的访问将大大加强中尼友好关系。
    社论在谈到中国总理和尼泊尔首相举行会谈时特别指出，中国总理和尼泊尔首相强调和平共处五项原则和加强两国的友好关系。它表示相信，中尼边界问题将在这种和谐的气氛中获得解决。
    “理想报”在社论中还特别指出，中国报刊十分重视两国之间的友好关系，并给予柯伊拉腊首相以热烈的欢迎。
    “新社会报”3月7日发表的社论祝访问中国的尼泊尔代表团取得成功。它说，这是一个强大的代表团，它可望加强中尼友谊并解决两国之间现存的问题。社论说，“在这样的时际，我们谴责所有企图在尼泊尔和中国之间散播不和的种子的那些人”。
    “时事报”在社论中说，代表团将代表尼泊尔人民的感情，加强两个友好国家之间的关系。
