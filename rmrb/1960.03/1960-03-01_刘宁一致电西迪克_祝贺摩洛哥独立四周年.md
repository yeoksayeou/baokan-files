Title: Liu Ningyi Sends Telegram to Sidky to Congratulate Morocco on the Fourth Anniversary of Independence
Author:
Date: 1960-03-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, sent a telegram on the 27th to Mahjoub Ben Seddik, General Secretary of the Moroccan Labor Union, congratulating Morocco on the fourth anniversary of its independence.

The telegram stated: "On the occasion of the fourth anniversary of Morocco's independence, I would like to extend warm congratulations to the Moroccan working class on behalf of the Chinese workers. I wish the Moroccan working class new achievements in the struggle against imperialism, safeguarding world peace, developing the national economy, and defending democratic rights and vital interests. I wish the solidarity of the African working class will increase day by day, and the friendship between the Chinese and Moroccan workers and trade unions will develop day by day."

### 刘宁一致电西迪克  祝贺摩洛哥独立四周年

1960-03-01
第6版()
专栏：

    刘宁一致电西迪克
    祝贺摩洛哥独立四周年
    新华社29日讯　中华全国总工会主席刘宁一在27日致电摩洛哥工会联合会总书记马佐·本·西迪克，祝贺摩洛哥独立四周年。
    电报说：“值此摩洛哥独立四周年之际，谨代表中国工人向摩洛哥工人兄弟致以热烈的祝贺。祝摩洛哥工人阶级在反对帝国主义、维护世界和平、发展民族经济、保卫民主权利和切身利益的斗争中取得新的成就。祝非洲工人阶级的团结日益增进，祝中摩两国工人和工会的友谊日益发展。”
