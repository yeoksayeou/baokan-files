Title: Chilean Education Delegation of Four Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-03-23
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, March 21st – At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, a four-member Chilean education delegation headed by Juan Gómez Millas, President of the University of Chile and President of the Latin American University Union, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 智利教育代表团四人到京

1960-03-23
第5版()
专栏：

    智利教育代表团四人到京
    据新华社21日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请，以智利大学校长、拉丁美洲大学联合会主席胡安·戈麦斯·米利亚斯为首的智利教育代表团一行四人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
