Title: Premier Zhou and Prime Minister Koirala Hold Talks
Author: 
Date: 1960-03-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 20th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, and Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Koirala, held talks this evening.
