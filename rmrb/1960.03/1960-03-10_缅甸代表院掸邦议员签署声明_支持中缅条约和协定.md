Title: Burmese House of Representatives Shan State Representatives Sign Statement Supporting Sino-Burmese Treaty and Agreements
Author:
Date: 1960-03-10
Page: 5

 Burmese House of Representatives Shan State Representatives Sign Statement
    Supporting Sino-Burmese Treaty and Agreements
    Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, 8th. The "Burma New Light of Burma" reported today that nineteen Shan State representatives of the Burmese House of Representatives recently signed a statement expressing firm support for the Sino-Burmese Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Non-Aggression and the Sino-Burmese Boundary Question Agreement.

    缅甸代表院掸邦议员签署声明
