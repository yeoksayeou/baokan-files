Title: Burmese President Receives Chinese Cultural Delegation; Chu Tunan Presents President Liu's Gift to President U Win Maung

Author:
Date: 1960-03-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, March 1st. Burmese President U Win Maung received the Chinese Cultural Friendship Delegation headed by Chu Tunan and all members of the delegation, as well as members of the Chinese National Song and Dance Troupe, this afternoon.

Chu Tunan presented the gift from Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, to Burmese President U Win Maung.

This afternoon, the delegation paid a visit to Sao Hkun Hkio, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities of Burma. The delegation and Speaker Sao Hkun Hkio exchanged gifts.

Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang held a reception at the Chinese Embassy this evening for the delegation and the Chinese National Song and Dance Troupe. Invited to the reception were Sao Hkun Hkio, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities of Burma, U Myint Thein, Chief Justice of the Supreme Court, U Khin Tun Aung, Minister of Foreign Affairs, U Chit Thoung, Minister of Culture, former Chief Justice of the Union U Chan Htoon, and famous Burmese artists.

On the evening of February 29th, Burmese Minister of Culture U Chit Thoung held a banquet for the Chinese artists. At the banquet, Minister U Chit Thoung and delegation head Chu Tunan, in their speeches, both emphasized further developing and consolidating the friendship between China and Burma and cultural exchanges between the two countries. Sao Hkun Hkio, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities of Burma, and cabinet ministers attended the banquet. Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang also attended the banquet.

### 缅甸总统接见我文化代表团  楚图南把刘主席赠送的礼物交给吴温貌总统

1960-03-03
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸总统接见我文化代表团
    楚图南把刘主席赠送的礼物交给吴温貌总统
    新华社仰光1日电　缅甸总统吴温貌今天下午接见了中国文化友好代表团团长楚图南和代表团全体团员以及中国民族歌舞团的团员。
    楚图南把中华人民共和国主席刘少奇赠送给缅甸总统的礼品交给吴温貌总统。
    今天下午代表团拜会了缅甸民族院议长萧恢塔。代表团和萧恢塔议长互相赠送了礼物。
    中国驻缅甸大使李一氓今天晚间在中国大使馆为代表团和中国民族歌舞团举行了招待会。应邀出席招待会的有缅甸民族院议长萧恢塔、最高法院首席法官吴敏登、外交部长吴千吞昂、文化部长吴漆东、前联邦首席法官吴登貌和缅甸的著名的艺术家。
    2月29日晚间，缅甸文化部长吴漆东为中国艺术家们举行了宴会。在宴会上，吴漆东部长和楚图南团长发表讲话时，都强调进一步发展和巩固中缅友谊和两国文化交流。出席宴会的有缅甸民族院议长萧恢塔和内阁部长们。中国驻缅甸大使李一氓也出席了宴会。
