Title: Dawn Scenery
Author: Gong Xi
Date: 1960-03-08
Page: 8

    Dawn Scenery
    Spring dawn,
    The airport is quiet,
    Three female pilots,
    Walk onto the straight runway.
    Behind them are azure skies and white clouds,
    In front of them is the vast blue sea,
    Beside them are vigorous aircraft,
    Above them is the whistling of the long wind...
    Three female pilots,
    Gazing into the distance side by side,
    Forget the lingering chill of spring.
    In front of them, the boundless distance,
    A round of the rising sun is illuminating the earth.
      (People's Liberation Army, Gong Xi) (Attached picture)

### 黎明风景
宫玺
1960-03-08
第8版()
专栏：

    黎明风景
    春天的黎明，
    机场静悄悄，
    三个女飞行员，
    走上笔直的跑道。
    背后是碧空白云，
    面前是蓝海滔滔，
    身旁是矫健的机群，
    头顶是长风呼啸……
    三个女飞行员，
    并肩远眺，
    忘记了春寒料峭。
    面前无际的远方，
    一轮旭日正把大地照耀。
      （解放军　宫玺）（附图片）
