Title: Algerian National Liberation Army Kills or Wounds More Than 500 French Troops in Two Days
Author:
Date: 1960-03-01
Page: 6
    Algerian National Liberation Army
    Kills or Wounds More Than 500 French Troops in Two Days

    Cairo, February 28th, Xinhua News Agency: A military communique issued by the Supreme Command of the Algerian National Liberation Army on the 28th stated that in the fighting on the 23rd and 24th of this month, 347 French soldiers were killed, 160 were wounded, and 15 military vehicles were destroyed.

    It was announced that the Algerian National Liberation Army shot down three French military aircraft with anti-aircraft guns and destroyed two military trains.

    The communique also stated that the Algerian National Liberation Army captured twenty-two enemy soldiers, seized fifty-eight weapons, and a large quantity of ammunition and grenades. Twenty-six enemy soldiers, fully equipped, surrendered to the Algerian National Liberation Army.

    The military communique concluded by saying that the Algerian National Liberation Army opened a 130-meter-long gap in the French “Morice Line,” which is a network of electrified fences on the eastern border.

### 阿尔及利亚民族解放军  两天内毙伤法军五百多名

1960-03-01
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚民族解放军
    两天内毙伤法军五百多名
    据新华社开罗28日电　阿尔及利亚民族解放军最高司令部28日发表的一项军事公报说，在本月23日和24日的战斗中，法国军队有三百四十七人被击毙，一百六十人受伤，十五辆军用车被击毁。
    据宣布，阿尔及利亚民族解放军用高射炮击落三架法国军用飞机，并且炸毁两列军用火车。
    公报还说，阿尔及利亚民族解放军俘掳了二十二名敌兵，缴获了五十八件武器以及大量弹药和手榴弹。二十六名敌军带着全付配备，向阿尔及利亚民族解放军投诚。
    军事公报最后说，阿尔及利亚民族解放军在东部边境法军设有电网的“莫里斯防线”上打开了长达一百三十米的缺口。
