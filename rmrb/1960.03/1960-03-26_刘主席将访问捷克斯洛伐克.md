Title: Chairman Liu to Visit Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1960-03-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 25th: Comrade Antonín Novotný, President of the Czechoslovak Republic, has invited Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China to pay a friendly visit to the Czechoslovak Republic at a time he deems suitable. Chairman Liu Shaoqi has accepted this invitation.

### 刘主席将访问捷克斯洛伐克

1960-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席将访问捷克斯洛伐克
    新华社25日讯　捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统安托宁·诺沃提尼同志邀请中华人民共和国主席刘少奇同志在他认为适合的时候到捷克斯洛伐克共和国进行友好访问。刘少奇主席已经接受了这项邀请。
