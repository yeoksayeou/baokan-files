Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into Our Fujian Territorial Waters; We Issue the 92nd Serious Warning

Author:

Date: 1960-03-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, March 19th: From 21:20 on March 18th to 1:20 on March 19th, a U.S. warship intruded into the sea area of the Baiquan area of Fujian Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, a spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 92nd serious warning.

### 美国军舰侵入我福建领海  我提出第92次严重警告

1960-03-20
第2版()
专栏：

    美国军舰侵入我福建领海
    我提出第92次严重警告
    新华社19日讯　3月18日二十一时二十分到19日一时二十分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省白犬地区海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第九十二次严重警告。
