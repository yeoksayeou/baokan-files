Title: Parting Words of Old Friendship, Deep Affection Forever Unforgettable: Indonesian People's Organizations Visit Overseas Chinese Awaiting Ships to Return Home; Both Sides Converse Cordially, Pointing Out That the People of Both Countries Are Friends Forever
Author:
Date: 1960-03-07
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 5th: Representatives of several Indonesian people's organizations today visited the Overseas Chinese from West Java who are gathered in Jakarta awaiting ships to return to China. They represented the Indonesia-China Friendship Association, the Indonesian Women's Movement Association, and the Indonesian Mothers' Committee Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs.

The representatives told the displaced Overseas Chinese that the Indonesian people and the Chinese people will always be friends. They expressed the hope that when the Overseas Chinese return to China, they will continue to maintain their friendship, and they also presented coffee, tea, medicine, and soap to the Overseas Chinese.

The Overseas Chinese were deeply moved by the care of their Indonesian friends. They said that they will always remember the deep friendship of the Indonesian people.

The Indonesian friends had cordial conversations with the displaced Overseas Chinese. Women's representatives warmly embraced Overseas Chinese women who had lived in Indonesia for generations.

Those visiting the displaced Overseas Chinese today included Mrs. Mandagi, vice-chairman of the Jakarta branch of the Indonesia-China Friendship Association, musician Sudarnoto, and Mrs. Sutisman.

Approximately one thousand displaced Overseas Chinese from West Java have already arrived in Jakarta, awaiting to board the second batch of ships sent by the Chinese government to return home.

### 临别话旧谊　深情永难忘  印度尼西亚人民团体代表访候船归国华侨  双方亲切地交谈指出两国人民永远是朋友

1960-03-07
第6版()
专栏：

    临别话旧谊　深情永难忘
    印度尼西亚人民团体代表访候船归国华侨
    双方亲切地交谈指出两国人民永远是朋友
    新华社雅加达5日电　印度尼西亚一些人民团体的代表今天访问了集中到雅加达等候乘船回中国的西爪哇华侨。他们分别代表印度尼西亚—中国友好协会、印度尼西亚妇女运动协会以及印度尼西亚母亲反对原子弹和氢弹委员会。
    代表们对流离失所的华侨们说，印度尼西亚人民和中国人民永远是朋友。他们表示希望，当华侨们回到中国以后，将继续保持彼此的友谊，他们还把咖啡、茶、药品和肥皂赠给华侨们。
    华侨们对印度尼西亚朋友们的关怀深为感动。他们说，他们将永远记住印度尼西亚人民的深厚友谊。
    印度尼西亚朋友们同流离失所的华侨亲切地交谈。妇女代表同在印度尼西亚住了几代的华侨妇女紧紧地拥抱。
    今天访问流离失所的华侨的人包括印度尼西亚—中国友好协会雅加达分会副主席曼达基夫人、音乐家苏达诺托以及苏蒂斯曼夫人。
    已经有大约一千名流离失所的西爪哇华侨来到雅加达，等候搭乘中国政府派来的第二批轮船回国。
