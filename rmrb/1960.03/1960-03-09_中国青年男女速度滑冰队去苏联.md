Title: Chinese Youth Men's and Women's Speed Skating Team to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-03-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 8th: The Chinese youth men's and women's speed skating team, consisting of fourteen members and led by Liu Yongnian, Deputy Director of the Jilin Provincial Sports Commission, left Beijing by plane today for a visit and competition in the Soviet Union. They will visit Irkutsk for a week and are scheduled to hold friendly matches there on the 11th and 12th.
    The skating team includes four female athletes and six male athletes. Most of them are newcomers who have emerged in mass skating movements in the past two years.

### 中国青年男女速度滑冰队去苏联

1960-03-09
第4版()
专栏：

    中国青年男女速度滑冰队去苏联
    据新华社8日讯　应邀到苏联访问比赛的中国青年男女速度滑冰队一行十四人，今天由吉林省体育运动委员会副主任刘永年率领乘飞机离北京出国。他们将到伊尔库次克访问一个星期，预定11日和12日在那里举行友谊比赛。
    滑冰队中包括四名女运动员和六名男运动员。他们大部分都是近两年在群众性滑冰运动中涌现出来的新手。
