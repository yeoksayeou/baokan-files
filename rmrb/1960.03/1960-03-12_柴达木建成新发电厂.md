Title: New Power Plant Built in Qaidam
Author:
Date: 1960-03-12
Page: 2

    A new thermal power plant, the Cold Lake No. 5 Power Plant, has recently been officially supplying electricity in the Cold Lake oil region of Qaidam.
    This power plant, together with the existing power generation equipment in the Cold Lake oil region, will form a power grid that can basically guarantee the electricity needs of the Cold Lake oil region's industry and daily life.

### 柴达木建成新发电厂

1960-03-12
第2版()
专栏：

    柴达木建成新发电厂
    柴达木冷湖油区新建冷湖五号火力发电厂最近正式供电。
    这座电厂将同冷湖油区原有的发电设备构成一个电力网，基本上可以保证冷湖油区工业和生活用电的需要。
