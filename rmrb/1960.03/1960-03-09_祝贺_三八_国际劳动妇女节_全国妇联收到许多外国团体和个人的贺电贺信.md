Title: Congratulations on the "March 8th" International Working Women's Day: All-China Women's Federation Receives Congratulatory Cables and Letters from Many Foreign Groups and Individuals
Author:
Date: 1960-03-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 8th: On the 50th anniversary of the "March 8th" International Working Women's Day, the All-China Women's Federation received congratulatory cables and letters from many foreign women's groups and other organizations, as well as individuals.

Among these congratulatory cables and letters were letters from four Soviet heroes, including Li Litvinova, on behalf of the Soviet Veterans Committee; congratulatory cables from the Vilnius City Women's Committee of the Lithuanian Soviet Socialist Republic, the Bulgarian Democratic Women's Committee, the Bulgarian magazine "Women Today," the Women's Committee of the "Walter Ulbricht" factory in the German Democratic Republic, all the female employees of the Czech-Chinese Friendship Factory in Czechoslovakia, the Central Committee of the Korean Women's Union, and the Mongolian Women's Committee. In addition, there were congratulatory letters and cables from the Sino-Japanese Friendship Association, the All-Japan Women's Organizations Federation, and Japanese musician Seki Akiko.

### 祝贺“三八”国际劳动妇女节  全国妇联收到许多外国团体和个人的贺电贺信

1960-03-09
第4版()
专栏：

    祝贺“三八”国际劳动妇女节
    全国妇联收到许多外国团体和个人的贺电贺信
    新华社8日讯　在“三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年的时候，全国妇女联合会收到了许多外国妇女团体和其他团体以及个人的贺电贺信。
    在这些贺电贺信中，有里·李特维诺娃等四位苏联英雄代表苏联退伍军人委员会发来的贺信，苏联立陶宛共和国维尔纽斯市妇女委员会、保加利亚民主妇女委员会、保加利亚“今日妇女”杂志、德意志民主共和国“瓦·乌布利希”工厂妇女委员会、捷克斯洛伐克捷中友谊工厂全体女职工、朝鲜妇女同盟中央委员会、蒙古妇女委员会发来的贺电。此外，还有日中友好协会、全日本妇女团体联合会、日本音乐家关鉴子发来的贺信和贺电。
