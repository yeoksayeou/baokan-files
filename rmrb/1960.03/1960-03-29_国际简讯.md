International Briefs

    Lebanon Decides to Hold General Elections in May
    Beirut Dispatch: Lebanese Minister of Interior Ali Bazzi said at the cabinet meeting on the 17th that the Lebanese government has finally decided to hold parliamentary elections in May this year.

    Japan Sends Various Delegations to Visit Pakistan
    Karachi Dispatch: A Japanese fisheries delegation currently visiting Pakistan is holding talks with the Pakistani government to "jointly develop" Pakistan's fisheries resources.
    They arrived here at the end of February. In addition to this fisheries delegation, a five-member Japanese cement expert group is also currently visiting Pakistan to investigate the possibility of establishing cement plants in the country. A delegation from the Japanese "Asia Transportation Development Association" will arrive in Pakistan in April for a nine-day visit.

    US and Thailand Discuss "Military Cooperation"
    Bangkok Dispatch: US Secretary of the Navy Frank left Bangkok for Hong Kong on the 25th after a three-day visit to Thailand.
    According to Thai newspapers, during his stay in Thailand, Frank discussed with Thai Prime Minister Nai Sarit, Defense Minister Nai Thanom, and Chief of Staff of the Ministry of Defense Luang Sa-nguan Konlayut "the development trends of the current situation in Southeast Asia" and US-Thai "military cooperation."

### 国际简讯

专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    黎巴嫩决定五月举行大选
    贝鲁特讯　黎巴嫩内政部长阿里·巴齐在17日的内阁会议上说，黎巴嫩政府已经最后决定在今年5月举行议会大选。日本派各种代表团访巴基斯坦
    卡拉奇讯　正在巴基斯坦访问的日本渔业代表团目前在同巴基斯坦政府举行商谈，以便“共同开发”巴基斯坦的渔业资源。
    他们是在2月底到达这里的。除了这个渔业代表团以外，日本的一个水泥专家五人小组现在也在巴基斯坦访问以调查在这个国家建立水泥厂的可能性。日本“亚洲交通发展协会”的一个代表团将在今年4月到达巴基斯坦作九天的访问。
    美泰商讨“军事合作”
    曼谷讯　美国海军部长弗兰克在泰国进行了三天活动以后，已在25日离开曼谷去香港。
    据泰国报纸报道，弗兰克在泰国活动期间，曾经同泰国总理乃沙立、国防部长乃他侬和国防部参谋长銮沙越·功拉育讨论了“目前东南亚地区情势的发展趋向”和美泰的“军事合作”问题。
