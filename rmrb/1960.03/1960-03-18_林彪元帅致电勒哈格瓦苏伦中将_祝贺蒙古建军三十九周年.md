Title: Marshal Lin Biao Sends Telegram to Lieutenant General Lhagvasuren Congratulating on the 39th Anniversary of the Founding of the Mongolian Army
Author:
Date: 1960-03-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Marshal Lin Biao, Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram on the 16th to Lieutenant General Jamyangiin Lhagvasuren, Minister of People's Army Affairs and Commander of the People's Army of the Mongolian People's Republic, congratulating him on the 39th anniversary of the founding of the Mongolian People's Army. The telegram reads as follows:

    To Comrade Lieutenant General Jamyangiin Lhagvasuren, Minister of People's Army Affairs and Commander of the People's Army of the Mongolian People's Republic:
    On the glorious occasion of the 39th anniversary of the founding of the Mongolian People's Army, on behalf of the Chinese people and all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and in my own name, I extend warm and fraternal congratulations to you and all commanders and fighters of the Mongolian People's Army.
	Under leadership of the Mongolian People’s Revolutionary Party, the Mongolian People's Army has achieved brilliant accomplishments in political and ideological education and military training, and it is a strong force for consolidating and building its motherland. I wish you new and greater achievements in strengthening the combat effectiveness of the Mongolian People's Army and in the great cause of building socialism.
    I wish the fraternal friendship between the peoples and armies of China and Mongolia will be increasingly consolidated and developed.
        Lin Biao
        Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China
        Marshal of the People's Republic of China
        March 16, 1960, in Beijing

### 林彪元帅致电勒哈格瓦苏伦中将  祝贺蒙古建军三十九周年

1960-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

    林彪元帅致电勒哈格瓦苏伦中将
    祝贺蒙古建军三十九周年
    新华社17日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪元帅，16日致电蒙古人民共和国人民军事务部长兼人民军司令员扎米扬·勒哈格瓦苏伦中将，祝贺蒙古人民军建军节三十九周年。电文如下：
    蒙古人民共和国人民军事务部长兼人民军司令员扎米扬·勒哈格瓦苏伦中将亲爱的部长同志：
    值此蒙古人民军建军三十九周年光荣的节日，我代表中国人民和中国人民解放军全体指战员，并以我个人名义，向您和蒙古人民军全体指战员致以热烈的兄弟般的祝贺。
    蒙古人民军在蒙古人民革命党的领导下，在政治思想教育和军事训练中，取得了光辉的成就，它是巩固和建设自己祖国的一支坚强力量。祝您在加强蒙古人民军战斗威力的工作中，在建设社会主义的伟大事业中，取得新的更巨大的成就。
    祝中蒙两国人民和军队之间兄弟般的友谊日益巩固和发展。
        中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长
                  中华人民共和国元帅　林　彪
                  一九六○年三月十六日于北京
