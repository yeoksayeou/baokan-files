Title: Soviet Women's Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-03-19
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 18th: The Soviet Women's Delegation, invited by the All-China Women's Federation to participate in the "March 8th" International Working Women's Day celebrations in our country, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home after visiting and touring Shanghai, Hangzhou, Nanchang, Guangzhou and other places. Those who went to the airport to see them off included Li Dequan, Vice-President of the All-China Women's Federation, Dong Bian, Secretary of the Secretariat, and others. The Korean and Mongolian Women's Delegations currently visiting our country also went to the airport to see them off.

### 苏联妇女代表团回国

1960-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联妇女代表团回国
    新华社18日讯　应全国妇联邀请前来我国参加“三八”国际劳动妇女节庆祝活动的苏联妇女代表团，在上海、杭州、南昌、广州等地参观访问后，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场欢送的有全国妇联副主席李德全，书记处书记董边等。正在我国访问的朝鲜、蒙古妇女代表团也到机场欢送。
