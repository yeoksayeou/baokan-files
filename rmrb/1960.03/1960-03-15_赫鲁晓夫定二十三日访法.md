Title: Khrushchev Scheduled to Visit France on the 23rd
Author: 
Date: 1960-03-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 14th: TASS, Moscow, March 14th, reports: Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Khrushchev has decided to visit France on March 23rd. The visit will last until April 3rd.

### 赫鲁晓夫定二十三日访法

1960-03-15
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫定二十三日访法
    新华社14日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科14日讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫已经决定在3月23日到法国访问。访问将进行到4月3日为止。
