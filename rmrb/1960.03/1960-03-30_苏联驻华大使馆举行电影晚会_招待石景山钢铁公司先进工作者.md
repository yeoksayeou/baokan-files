Title: Soviet Embassy in China Hosts Film Evening for Advanced Workers of Shijingshan Iron and Steel Company
Author:
Date: 1960-03-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, March 28th. The Soviet Embassy in China hosted a film evening tonight for advanced workers of the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Company. The films "Atomic Icebreaker" and "The Story of Lenin" were screened at the film evening.

Yao Yukai, Deputy Manager of Shijingshan Iron and Steel Company, Zhao Huanran, Deputy Secretary of the Party Committee, and more than one hundred advanced workers, engineers, and medical staff from the Iron and Steel Company attended the evening.

Soviet Embassy in China Counselor Sudarikov received the Chinese guests.

Hu Weide and Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, also attended the evening.

### 苏联驻华大使馆举行电影晚会  招待石景山钢铁公司先进工作者

1960-03-30
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联驻华大使馆举行电影晚会
    招待石景山钢铁公司先进工作者
    新华社28日讯　苏联驻华大使馆今晚举行电影晚会，招待石景山钢铁公司的先进工作者。电影晚会放映了“原子破冰船”和“列宁的故事”。
    石景山钢铁公司副经理姚聿凯、党委副书记赵焕然和钢铁公司的先进工人、工程师、医务工作者共一百多人参加了晚会。
    苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫接待了中国客人。
    中苏友协总会副秘书长胡韦德、戈宝权也参加了晚会。
