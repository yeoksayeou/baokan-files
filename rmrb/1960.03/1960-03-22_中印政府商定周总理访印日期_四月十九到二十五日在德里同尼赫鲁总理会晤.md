Title: China and India Agree on Premier Zhou's Visit to India; Meeting with Prime Minister Nehru in Delhi from April 19th to 25th
Author:
Date: 1960-03-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 21st – The Chinese and Indian governments have agreed that Premier Zhou Enlai will pay a seven-day visit to Delhi from April 19th to April 25th to meet with Indian Prime Minister Nehru. The full text of the recent correspondence between Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Nehru is published below:

(1) Premier Zhou Enlai's reply to Prime Minister Nehru on March 19, 1960

To His Excellency Jawaharlal Nehru, Prime Minister of the Government of the Republic of India, New Delhi

Dear Mr. Prime Minister,

Ambassador Parthasarathi of India to China delivered your letter to me on March 5th. Your suggestion to schedule my visit to Delhi around April 20th is suitable for me, and I completely agree. If it is convenient for you and the Indian government, I am prepared to visit Delhi for seven days, from April 19th to April 25th.

I warmly look forward to meeting you again and visiting your great country.

With cordial greetings.

Zhou Enlai
Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
March 19, 1960, Beijing

(2) Prime Minister Nehru's letter to Premier Zhou Enlai

(Delivered by the Indian Ambassador to China on March 5, 1960)

Dear Mr. Prime Minister,

Thank you for your letter of February 26th. I am very glad that you have accepted my invitation to visit Delhi so that we can discuss our problems and explore avenues that may lead to a peaceful resolution of these problems. I look forward to your visit in April.

Regarding the dates of your visit here, we will naturally try to accommodate your convenience. If I may make a suggestion, around April 20th may be suitable. I may be leaving India for Europe on April 29th or 30th.

With cordial greetings.

Jawaharlal Nehru

(3) Prime Minister Nehru's reply to Premier Zhou Enlai

(Delivered by the Indian Ambassador to China on March 21, 1960)

To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Beijing

Your Excellency:

I have the honor to convey to Your Excellency the following telegram from my Prime Minister:

"Thank you for your letter of March 19th. The date you suggested for arriving here, April 19th, will be convenient for me, and we will be happy to welcome you here at that time. Warmest greetings. Jawaharlal Nehru."

Your Excellency, please accept the assurances of my highest consideration.

Parthasarathi
Indian Ambassador
March 21, 1960, Beijing

### 中印政府商定周总理访印日期  四月十九到二十五日在德里同尼赫鲁总理会晤

1960-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中印政府商定周总理访印日期
    四月十九到二十五日在德里同尼赫鲁总理会晤
    新华社21日讯　中印两国政府已经商定，周恩来总理将于4月19日到4月25日的期间，在德里进行七天的访问，同印度总理尼赫鲁举行会晤。现将周恩来总理和尼赫鲁总理的最近来往信件全文发表如下：
    （一）周恩来总理1960年3月19日给尼赫鲁总理的复信新德里印度共和国政府贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁总理阁下亲爱的总理先生：
    印度驻华大使帕塔萨拉蒂先生已经在3月5日转交了你的来信。你建议我去德里访问的时间可以定在4月20日左右。这个时间对我是适合的，我完全同意。如果对你和印度政府方便的话，我准备从4月19日起到4月25日，在德里进行七天的访问。
    我热烈地期待着再次同你会晤和访问你的伟大的国家。
    致亲切的问候。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理
                        周恩来
          1960年3月19日于北京
    （二）尼赫鲁总理给周恩来总理的来信
    （1960年3月5日印度驻华大使交来）亲爱的总理先生：
    谢谢你2月26日的来信。我很高兴，你已经接受我的关于访问德里的邀请，以便我们能够会谈我们的问题并且探索可能导致和平解决这些问题的途径。我将期待你在4月的访问。
    关于你来这里访问的日期，我们自然将试图适合你的方便。如果我可以提出建议的话，4月20日左右也许是适合的。我可能将在4月29日或30日离开印度去欧洲。
    致亲切的问候。
          贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁
    （三）尼赫鲁总理给周恩来总理的复信
    （1960年3月21日印度驻华大使转来）北京中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来
  先生阁下阁下：
    我荣幸地将下列来自我的总理的电文转达阁下：
    “感谢你3月19日的来信。你所建议的到达这里的日期，即4月19日，对我将是方便的，届时我们将在这里高兴地欢迎你。致热烈的问候。贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁。”
    阁下，请接受我最崇高的敬意。
            印度大使　帕塔萨拉蒂　1960年3月21日于北京
