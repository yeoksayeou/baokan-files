Title: Liu Ningyi Sends Telegram to General Secretary of the Ghana Trades Union Congress, Congratulating Ghana on its Third Anniversary of Independence
Author:
Date: 1960-03-06
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, March 5th, Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, sent a telegram on the 4th to Meyer, General Secretary of the Ghana Trades Union Congress, congratulating Ghana on its third anniversary of independence.
    The telegram said: "On the occasion of the third anniversary of Ghana's independence, I would like to extend warm congratulations to the Ghanaian workers on behalf of the Chinese workers."
    The telegram said: "We are pleased to see that the friendship between the trade unions and workers of China and Ghana is constantly developing. We sincerely wish the Ghanaian working class new achievements in the struggle against imperialism, safeguarding world peace, developing the national economy and improving the situation of the working class. We wish the cause of unity of the African working class greater development."

### 刘宁一电加纳工会大会总书记  祝贺加纳独立三周年

1960-03-06
第5版()
专栏：

    刘宁一电加纳工会大会总书记
    祝贺加纳独立三周年
    新华社5日讯　中华全国总工会主席刘宁一4日打电报给加纳工会大会总书记迈耶，祝贺加纳独立三周年。
    电报说：“值此加纳独立三周年纪念日之际，谨代表中国工人，向加纳工人兄弟致热烈的祝贺。”
    电报说：“我们高兴地看到，中、加两国工会和工人间的友谊，在不断发展，衷心祝加纳工人阶级在反对帝国主义，维护世界和平，发展民族经济和改善工人阶级处境的斗争中，取得新的成就。祝非洲工人阶级的团结事业获得更大的发展。”
