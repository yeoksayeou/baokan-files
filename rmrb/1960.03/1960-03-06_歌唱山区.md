Title: Singing the Mountain Area
Author:
Date: 1960-03-06
Page: 4

    Singing the Mountain Area
    Light cart goes straight up into the white clouds

    Wheelbarrow Song
    Wheelbarrow, not simple,
    Can go on flat roads, can climb mountains,
    Regardless of the winding mountain road,
    Light cart goes straight up into the white clouds.
            (Ma Changhua)
    Terraced Fields Song
    Terraced fields layered upon layer,
    Only three and a third feet from the sky,
    Grain scattered on the top of the mountain,
    Rice seedlings grow at the edge of the white clouds,
    Open up the Tongtian River,
    River water flies up the mountain,
    Green mountains and fields rise with golden waves,
    Like colorful clouds tumbling in the sky.
          (Li Hong)

### 歌唱山区

1960-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    歌唱山区
    轻车直上白云间
    独轮车，不简单，
    能走平路能爬山，
    不管山路弯又曲，
    轻车直上白云间。
            （马长华）
    梯田歌
    梯田层层迭上边，
    离天只有三尺三，
    谷子撒在山顶上，
    禾苗长在白云端，
    打开通天河，
    河水飞上山，
    绿色山野起金浪，
    好像天上彩云翻。
          （李红）
