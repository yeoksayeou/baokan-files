Title: U.S. Warships and Military Aircraft Invade Fujian Territorial Waters and Guangdong Airspace; I Issue the Ninety-First Serious Warning
Author: 
Date: 1960-03-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: From 7:00 to 10:24 on March 11th, a U.S. warship intruded into the sea area of Haitan and Baiquan regions of Fujian Province. At 12:09, another U.S. Navy patrol aircraft intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island of the Xisha Islands in Guangdong Province for reconnaissance. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. warships and military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the ninety-first serious warning.

### 美国军舰和军用飞机侵入福建领海和广东领空  我提出第九十一次严重警告

1960-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    美国军舰和军用飞机侵入福建领海和广东领空
    我提出第九十一次严重警告
    新华社11日讯　3月11日七时至十时二十四分，美国军舰一艘侵入我福建省海坛、白犬地区海域。十二时零九分，又有美国海军巡逻飞机一架侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛上空盘旋侦察。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅行为，我外交部发言人奉命提出第九十一次严重警告。
