Title: 22 Million People Live in Slums
Author:
Date: 1960-03-27
Page: 5

Under Capitalism

    22 Million People Live in Slums
    United States
    According to a recent document released by the Democratic National Advisory Committee in the United States, there are currently 22 million people living in slums in the United States. The document states that if slum clearance work continues at the current rate, it will take at least a hundred years to clear the existing slums in the United States, while new slums are increasing every year.

### 二千二百万人住在贫民窟里

1960-03-27
第5版()
专栏：在资本主义制度下

    二千二百万人住在贫民窟里
    美国
    据美国民主党全国谘询委员会最近发表的一个文件说，美国目前有二千二百万人居住在贫民窟中。文件说，如果清除贫民窟的工作按照目前的速度进行下去，那么美国现有的贫民窟至少要一百年才能清除掉，而新的贫民窟则在每年增加。
