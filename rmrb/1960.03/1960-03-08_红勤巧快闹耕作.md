Title: Red, Diligent, Clever, and Quick Cultivation
Author: Huang Qiming
Date: 1960-03-08
Page: 8

    Red, Diligent, Clever, and Quick Cultivation
    Green hills and clear waters meet the blue sky,
    A terraced field atop the mountain high.
    Weaving Maid dances with plow in field,
    Urging Cowherd to swiftly yield.
    Ten thousand colorful clouds float below,
    Pearls fall into the mud, golden tides flow.
    Sister scatters grain seeds in the field,
    Each grain grows into jade seedlings revealed.
    Colorful clouds tied around the waist like a sash,
    Girls sow seeds with high dash,
    Leap Forward flowers bloom all around,
    Laying out a bridge to happiness profound.
    (Huang Qiming, Gujiang, Ji'an, Jiangxi) (Attached picture)

### 红勤巧快闹耕作
黄启明
1960-03-08
第8版()
专栏：

    红勤巧快闹耕作
    青山绿水接蓝天，
    一垅梯田在山巅。
    织女扶犁田中舞，
    催得牛郎飞向前。
    万朵彩云足下飘，
    珍珠落泥起金涛。
    妹把谷种田中撒，
    粒粒长出翡翠苗。
    彩云似带系上腰，
    姑娘播种干劲高，
    跃进花朵遍地放，
    铺出一条幸福桥。
    （江西吉安固江　黄启明）（附图片）
