Title: Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala Departs for China at the Invitation of Premier Zhou

Author:

Date: 1960-03-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 6th — Kathmandu Dispatch: Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala left Kathmandu this morning for a visit to China at the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai. On his way to China, he will also visit Burma for three days.

Accompanying Prime Minister Koirala on his visit are Minister of Home Affairs Su. P. Upadhyaya, Minister of Transport Ganesh Man Singh, and Foreign Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs N. Thapa, among others. Prime Minister Koirala and his entourage were warmly greeted at the airport today by senior Nepalese government officials and prominent figures from society.

### 应周总理邀请访问我国  尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊启程来华

1960-03-07
第1版()
专栏：

    应周总理邀请访问我国
    尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊启程来华
    新华社新德里6日电　加德满都消息：尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊应周恩来总理的邀请，今天上午离开加德满都前往中国访问。他在前往中国途中，还将在缅甸访问三天。
    随同柯伊拉腊出国访问的有内政大臣苏·普·乌帕德亚亚、交通大臣加奈什曼·辛格和外交部外交秘书纳·塔帕等。柯伊拉腊首相和他的随行人员今天在机场上受到尼泊尔政府高级官员和社会著名人士的热烈欢送。
