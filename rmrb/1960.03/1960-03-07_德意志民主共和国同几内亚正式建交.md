Title: German Democratic Republic and Guinea Establish Formal Diplomatic Relations

Author:

Date: 1960-03-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 5th: A spokesman for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic announced this afternoon that the German Democratic Republic and the Republic of Guinea have formally established diplomatic relations and exchanged ambassadors.

The first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Guinea to the German Democratic Republic, Seydou Conté, presented his credentials to President Pieck today.

The first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the German Democratic Republic to Guinea, Karl Nohr, is currently en route to his post.

### 德意志民主共和国同几内亚正式建交

1960-03-07
第6版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国同几内亚正式建交
    新华社柏林5日电　德意志民主共和国外交部发言人今天下午宣布，德意志民主共和国和几内亚共和国已经正式建交，并互换大使级外交代表。
    几内亚共和国驻德意志民主共和国第一任特命全权大使塞杜·康特，今天已经向皮克总统递交了国书。
    德意志民主共和国驻几内亚第一任特命全权大使卡尔·诺尔现在正在赴任途中。
