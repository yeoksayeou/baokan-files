Red Diligence Ingenuity Frugality
Shi Jize Paper-cut, Jiang Cheng Verse

	Red
	Aspire to be heroes,
	Listen to the Party, always rush forward.
	
	Diligence
	Actively support the Great Leap Forward,
	A good housekeeper, winning everyone's heart.

	Ingenuity
	Find ways to discover tricks,
	Engage in reform, greatly increase production.

	Frugality
	Carefully calculate every penny,
	Always be thrifty, the future is boundless.
