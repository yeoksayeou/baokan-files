Title: Premier Zhou, Vice Chairman Peng Zhen, and Vice Premier Chen Yi Receive Foreign Guests Invited to Attend the National People's Congress

Author:
Date: 1960-03-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 30th: Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Peng Zhen, and Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, this afternoon received foreign guests who were invited to attend the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress. They held friendly talks with them.

These guests were: the Soviet government trade delegation headed by Patolichev, Chairman of the Vietnam Asian-African People's Solidarity Committee Ton Quang Phiet, the scientific and technological cooperation delegation of the German Democratic Republic headed by Georg Henke, the Vietnam scientific and technological cooperation working group headed by Ngo Thuan Tung, the Vietnam Ministry of Transport and Posts and Telecommunications education delegation headed by Nguyen Nhu Quy, the All-Japan Farmers Union delegation to China headed by Hitoshi Taira, representatives from Thailand, General Secretary of the Gabonese Confederation of Trade Unions Mvi Louis and Secretary of the Chadian Trade Union Confederation Mambella Naim, the Cyprus United Democratic Youth Organization delegation headed by Kiprianides, representatives of the Milan Bank of England Hanmer Temple and Willingson, and foreign peace activists in Beijing, Wanturleri, Cale, etc.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman Peng Zhen, and Vice Premier Chen Yi also received the diplomatic envoys and officials of various countries stationed in China who attended the meeting.

### 周总理、彭真副委员长和陈毅副总理  接见应邀参加人大会议的外宾

1960-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理、彭真副委员长和陈毅副总理
    接见应邀参加人大会议的外宾
    新华社30日讯　国务院总理周恩来、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真、国务院副总理陈毅，今天下午接见了应邀参加第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议的外国来宾。同他们进行了友好的交谈。
    这些来宾是：以帕托利切夫为首的苏联政府贸易代表团，越南亚非人民团结委员会主席孙光阀，盖奥格·亨凯为首的德意志民主共和国科学技术合作代表团，吴俊颂为首的越南科学技术合作工作组，阮如晷为首的越南交通和邮电部教育考察团，平等文成为首的全日本农民组合访华代表团，泰国代表，加蓬工会联合会总书记姆维·路易和乍得工会联合会书记曼勃拉·纳伊姆，基普里亚尼德斯为首的塞浦路斯统一民主青年组织代表团，英国米兰银行代表汉尔默寺和威灵逊，在北京的外国和平人士万徒勒里、凯尔等。
    周恩来总理、彭真副委员长和陈毅副总理，同时还接见了参加会议的各国驻中国的使节和外交官员。
