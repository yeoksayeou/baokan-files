Title: “Spartak” Football Team Wins in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1960-03-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, March 20th – The Soviet Moscow “Spartak” football team defeated the Tianjin City football team today at the Tianjin Minyuan Stadium with a score of 3-0. All three goals were scored by the visiting team in the second half. More than 10,000 people watched this spectacular match.

### “斯巴达克”足球队在天津告捷

1960-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    “斯巴达克”足球队在天津告捷
    据新华社天津20日电　苏联莫斯科“斯巴达克”足球队今天在天津民园体育场以三比○战胜天津市足球队。这三个球都是客队在下半场攻进的。有一万多人观看了这场精采的比赛。
