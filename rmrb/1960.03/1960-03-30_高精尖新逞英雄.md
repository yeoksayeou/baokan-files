Title: High-Precision, Cutting-Edge, and Novel Heroes Display Their Skills
Author:
Date: 1960-03-30
Page: 3

Chemical workers, chemical workers,
	Displaying your talents in the Great Leap Forward year,
	Manufacturing thousands of products,
	Changing endlessly like the Monkey King.
	Chemical workers, chemical workers,
	Clever hands and red hearts,
	Using everything in the universe at our disposal,
	High-precision, cutting-edge, and novel heroes display their skills.
