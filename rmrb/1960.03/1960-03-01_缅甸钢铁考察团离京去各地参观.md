Title: Burmese Steel Delegation Leaves Beijing to Visit Various Locations
Author:
Date: 1960-03-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 29th—The Burmese Steel Delegation, led by U Kyin Maung Gyi, Director of the Burmese National Steel Rolling Mill Management Committee, left Beijing tonight to visit Xinxiang in Henan, Wuhan, Shanghai and other places.

During their stay in Beijing, the delegation visited the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Company and the Xuanwu Steel Plant.

Last night, the Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, held a banquet for the delegation's visit to China. Liu Bin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry, and Zhang Wenjin, Director of the First Asian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, were invited to attend the banquet.

### 缅甸钢铁考察团离京去各地参观

1960-03-01
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸钢铁考察团离京去各地参观
    新华社29日讯　由缅甸国营轧钢厂管理委员会主任吴庆貌季率领的缅甸钢铁考察团，今晚离开北京去河南新乡、武汉、上海等地参观访问。
    考察团在北京期间，曾参观了石景山钢铁公司和宣武钢铁厂等。
    昨晚，缅甸驻我国大使叫温曾为考察团访问中国举行宴会。冶金工业部副部长刘彬和外交部第一亚洲司司长章文晋等应邀出席了宴会。
