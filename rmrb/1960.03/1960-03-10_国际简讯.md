International Briefs

    Soviet Union Issues "March 8th" Commemorative Stamps
    Moscow News: To celebrate the 50th anniversary of International Working Women's Day "March 8th," the Soviet Union has issued colorful commemorative stamps.
    The face value of this new stamp is forty kopecks. The stamp features a beautiful colorful design and the words "Peace to the World" written in several languages.

    Soviet Union Publishes Musical Encyclopedia Dictionary
    Moscow News: The Soviet Encyclopedia Publishing House has recently published a musical encyclopedia dictionary. This is the first such dictionary published in the Soviet Union. This dictionary covers all aspects of music, explains the meaning of musical terminology, introduces the styles, schools and trends that have emerged in the development of music, and describes the theory and history of music. The dictionary also introduces the lives and creative activities of great musicians and famous musical works.

    Soviet-Czech Republic Sign Agreement on High-Voltage Power Transmission Line
    Moscow News: A Soviet-Czech agreement on establishing a high-voltage power transmission line from Romania via the Soviet Union to Czechoslovakia has been signed in Moscow.

### 国际简讯

1960-03-10

专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    苏联发行“三八”节纪念邮票
    莫斯科讯　为庆祝“三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年，苏联发行了彩色纪念邮票。
    这种新邮票的票面值是四十戈比。邮票上印有美丽的彩色图案和用几种文字书写的“给世界以和平”字样。
    苏联出版音乐百科全书字典
    莫斯科讯　苏联百科全书出版社最近出版了一本音乐百科全书字典。这是苏联出版的第一部这样的字典。这本字典包括音乐学科各个方面，阐述了音乐专门用语的意义，介绍了音乐发展中出现的风格、派别和潮流，叙述了音乐的理论和历史。这本字典还分别介绍了大音乐家的生平和创作活动以及著名的音乐作品。
    苏捷签订输电高压线协定
    莫斯科讯：　关于建立一条从罗马尼亚经由苏联到捷克斯洛伐克的输电高压线的苏捷协定在莫斯科签字。
