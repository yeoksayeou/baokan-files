Title: All-China Women's Federation Sends a Telegram to South African Women's Organizations, Resolutely Supporting the South African People's Struggle

Author:

Date: 1960-03-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, March 24th. The All-China Women's Federation today sent a telegram to the Women's League of the African National Congress of South Africa and the Federation of South African Women, supporting the just struggle of the South African people for basic human rights and against racial discrimination.

The telegram stated: "Chinese women express their utmost anger at the brutal massacre by the authorities of the Union of South Africa of the South African people and women who held protest demonstrations against the 'Pass Laws.' We strongly protest this inhuman and bloody atrocity by the South African authorities and extend our heartfelt respect to the South African brothers and sisters who are bravely fighting for basic human rights and against racial discrimination. We extend our deepest condolences to the families of the deceased and the injured. Your just struggle will always receive the resolute support of the Chinese people and women. We believe that as long as the South African people and women unite closely and persevere in their struggle, final victory will surely belong to you."

### 我全国妇联致电南非妇女组织  坚决支持南非人民斗争

1960-03-25
第5版()
专栏：

    我全国妇联致电南非妇女组织
    坚决支持南非人民斗争
    新华社24日讯　全国妇女联合会今天致电南非非洲人国民大会妇女联盟和南非妇女联合会，支持南非人民争取基本人权、反对种族歧视的正义斗争。
    电报中说：“中国妇女对南非联邦当局残酷屠杀为反对‘通行证法’举行抗议示威的南非人民和妇女，表示极大的愤怒。我们强烈抗议南非当局的这种惨无人道的血腥暴行，并向为争取基本人权、反对种族歧视而英勇斗争的南非兄弟姊妹们致以衷心的敬意。我们特向死难者的家属和负伤者致以深切的慰问。你们的正义斗争将永远获得中国人民和妇女的坚决支持。我们相信，只要南非人民和妇女紧密地团结起来，坚持不懈地进行斗争，最后的胜利一定是属于你们的。”
