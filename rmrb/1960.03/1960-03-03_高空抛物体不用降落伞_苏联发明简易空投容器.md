Title: Dropping Objects from High Altitude Without Parachutes: Soviet Union Invents Simple Airdrop Containers
Author:
Date: 1960-03-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd -- TASS reports: Soviet engineer Antrushin has designed two simple airdrop containers that can safely deliver animals, glassware, and heavy objects from high altitudes without the use of parachutes.

	One type of inflatable rubber container created by Antrushin can transport objects weighing up to one ton. It is made by stacking three or four inflatable rubber rings, similar to car or airplane tires, on top of each other. The space inside the rings is filled with the object to be airdropped. The object is surrounded by football or basketball inner tubes. The entire container is then wrapped in a net. This type of container has undergone one hundred tests without being damaged and will not sink in water. It can maintain the object's temperature for up to ten hours.

	Another paper container proposed by Antrushin can be used to drop objects weighing around three kilograms from airplanes without damage. It is a wrinkled paper ball with the airdropped object placed in the center, surrounded by a network of mesh. Both the paper ball and the mesh can be manufactured using waste from printing plants, making it very inexpensive. Each container costs only about ten rubles. When not in use, the paper ball can be folded like a fan for easy transport.

### 高空抛物体不用降落伞  苏联发明简易空投容器

1960-03-03
第5版()
专栏：

    高空抛物体不用降落伞
    苏联发明简易空投容器
    新华社莫斯科2日电　据塔斯社报道：苏联工程师安特鲁申设计了两种简易的空投容器，可以不用降落伞而把动物、玻璃制品及很重的物体从很高的地方抛投下来，安全到达地面。
    安特鲁申创制的一种充气橡皮容器可抛运重达一吨的物体，它的做法是：把三个或四个像汽车或飞机的轮胎那样的充气橡皮环叠在一起，环中间的空间就装上要空投的物体。物体周围用足球或篮球的内胎塞满。然后再用网把全部容器包裹起来。这样装好的容器经过了一百次试验还没有损坏，落在水里也不会下沉，它能够保持物体温度达十小时之久。
    安特鲁申提出的另一个用纸做的容器，可以用来装置三公斤左右的物体从飞机上空投下来，而不受损伤。它是一个有皱褶的纸球，空投物体放在纸球中心，周围装塞许多网状物。纸球和网状物都可以利用印刷厂的废物制造，它的价格很便宜，每一个容器只要十个卢布左右。纸球不用时可以像折扇那样褶叠在一起，运输起来也很方便。
