Title: Khrushchev Arrives in Calcutta
Author:
Date: 1960-03-02
Page: 5

    Khrushchev Arrives in Calcutta
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 1st dispatch: According to a TASS dispatch from Jakarta: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, and his entourage concluded their twelve-day visit to Indonesia and left Jakarta by plane on the morning of the 1st to go to Calcutta, India.
    Those who saw him off at the airport included Indonesian President Sukarno, First Minister Djuanda, Speaker of Parliament Sartono, government officials, leaders of the armed forces, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Indonesia.
    On the evening of February 29, Khrushchev hosted a banquet in honor of Indonesian President Sukarno.
    Khrushchev held a press conference in Jakarta on February 29.
    Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch: According to TASS:
    The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, and his entourage arrived in Calcutta by plane today from Jakarta on their way to visit Afghanistan.
    Indian Prime Minister Nehru, who flew in from Delhi this morning to meet with Khrushchev, welcomed Khrushchev at Dum Dum Airport in Calcutta.

### 赫鲁晓夫到达加尔各答

1960-03-02
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫到达加尔各答
    新华社1日讯　据塔斯社雅加达消息：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫及其随行人员结束了在印度尼西亚为时十二天的访问，1日上午乘飞机离开雅加达，前往印度的加尔各答。
    到机场欢送的有印度尼西亚总统苏加诺、第一部长朱安达、议长苏多诺、政府官员、武装部队领导人和各国驻印度尼西亚的外交使节。
    2月29日晚上，赫鲁晓夫设宴招待印度尼西亚总统苏加诺。
    赫鲁晓夫2月29日在雅加达举行了记者招待会。
    新华社1日讯　据塔斯社消息：
    苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫和他的随行人员在去阿富汗访问途中今天从雅加达乘飞机到达加尔各答。
    今天早晨从德里乘飞机赶来同赫鲁晓夫会面的印度总理尼赫鲁，到加尔各答达姆达姆机场欢迎赫鲁晓夫。
