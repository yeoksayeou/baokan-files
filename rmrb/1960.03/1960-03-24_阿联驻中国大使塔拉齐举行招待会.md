Title: UAR Ambassador to China, Tarazi, Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1960-03-24
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd: UAR Ambassador to China, Tarazi, held a reception this evening for the Chinese film delegation that recently returned from Cairo. This delegation recently participated in the Second Asian-African Film Festival held in Cairo.
    Among those attending the reception were Ding Xilin, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhang Tiesheng, Vice President of the Sino-UAR Friendship Association, He Ying, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Wang, head of the Chinese film delegation, and Yang Likun, the lead actress in the film "Five Golden Flowers," who won the Best Actress Award at the film festival.
    The Chinese color feature film "Five Golden Flowers" was screened at the reception.

### 阿联驻中国大使塔拉齐举行招待会

1960-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    阿联驻中国大使塔拉齐举行招待会
    新华社23日讯　阿联驻中国大使塔拉齐今天晚上举行招待会，招待最近从开罗回国的中国电影代表团。这个代表团不久前参加了在开罗举行的第二届亚非电影节。
    出席招待会的有对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林，中国阿联友好协会副会长张铁生，外交部西亚非洲司司长何英，中国电影代表团团长张望和在这次电影节获得最佳女演员奖的电影“五朵金花”的女主角杨丽坤等。
    会上放映了中国彩色故事片“五朵金花”。
