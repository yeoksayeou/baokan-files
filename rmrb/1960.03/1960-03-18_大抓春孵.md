Author:
Date: 1960-03-18
Page: 3

Brief News

    Vigorously Grasp Spring Hatching
    Shanggao County, Jiangxi Province, is vigorously promoting a spring poultry hatching campaign. Currently, the county has already hatched more than 108,000 chickens, ducks, and geese, with over 140,000 eggs still incubating.
    Shanggao County plans to increase the number of chickens, ducks, and geese from 270,000 to 900,000 this year, averaging more than 25 per household.

### 大抓春孵

1960-03-18
第3版()
专栏：简明新闻

    大抓春孵
    江西上高县大搞家禽春孵运动。现在全县已孵出鸡、鸭、鹅等十万八千多只，正在孵化的种蛋还有十四万多个。
    上高县今年计划把鸡、鸭、鹅等家禽由二十七万只繁殖到九十万只，平均每户达到二十五只以上。
