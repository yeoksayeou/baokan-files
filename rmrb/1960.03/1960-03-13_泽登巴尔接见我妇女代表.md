Title: Tsedenbal Receives Chinese Women's Representative
Author:
Date: 1960-03-13
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, March 11th: Tsedenbal, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, received Zhu Danhua, the Chinese women's representative and Director of the Jiangxi Provincial Women's Federation, who came to participate in the celebration of the 50th anniversary of the "March 8th" International Working Women's Day, at noon today.

    Present at the reception was Yangjima, Chairman of the Mongolian Women's Committee.

    Xie Fusheng, Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, was also present.

    Tsedenbal had a cordial conversation with Zhu Danhua.

### 泽登巴尔接见我妇女代表

1960-03-13
第3版()
专栏：

    泽登巴尔接见我妇女代表
    新华社乌兰巴托11日电　蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔今天中午接见了前来参加“三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年庆祝活动的中国妇女代表、江西省妇联主任朱旦华。
    接见时在座的有蒙古妇女委员会主席扬吉玛。
    中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生也在座。
    泽登巴尔同朱旦华进行了亲切的谈话。
