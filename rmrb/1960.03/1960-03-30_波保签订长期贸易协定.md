Title: Poland and Bulgaria Sign Long-Term Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-03-30
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 28th. Poland and Bulgaria signed a long-term trade agreement here yesterday covering the period from 1961 to 1965. The agreement was signed by Polish Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Zenon Nowak, and Bulgarian Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Zhivko Zhivkov.
    The trade agreement stipulates that the trade volume between the two countries will increase by nearly 100% from 1961 to 1965 compared to 1956 to 1960. According to the agreement, Poland will supply Bulgaria with complete sets of factory equipment, glass, electrical equipment, automobiles, tractors, and chemicals. Bulgaria will supply Poland with the following goods: zinc, carriages, tobacco, corn, vegetable oils, fruits, etc.

### 波保签订长期贸易协定

1960-03-30
第5版()
专栏：

    波保签订长期贸易协定
    新华社华沙28日电  波兰和保加利亚昨天在这里签订了1961至1965年长期贸易协定。在协定上签字的是波兰部长会议副主席泽·诺瓦克和保加利亚部长会议副主席日·日夫科夫。
    这个贸易协定规定1961至1965年期间双方贸易额要比1956至1960年增长近一倍。协定规定，波兰将供给保加利亚成套的工厂设备、玻璃、电力设备、汽车、拖拉机和化学品等。保加利亚应供应波兰以下列货物：锌、车厢、烟草、玉米、植物油脂、水果等。
