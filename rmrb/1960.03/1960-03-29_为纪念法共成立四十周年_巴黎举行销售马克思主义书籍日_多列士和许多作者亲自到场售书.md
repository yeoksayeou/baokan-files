Title: To Commemorate the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the French Communist Party, Paris Holds a Day for Selling Marxist Books, with Thorez and Many Authors Personally Selling Books

Author:
Date: 1960-03-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 28th: According to TASS, Paris, 28th: The day for selling Marxist books held in Paris yesterday to commemorate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the French Communist Party was a great success.

　　In a large lounge of the "Mutual Aid Hall," it was crowded with Parisians from early morning yesterday, who came here specifically to buy the classic works of Marx and Lenin, the works of leaders of the international workers' movement, and the leaders of the French Communist Party.

　　Along the walls of the hall were rows of long tables with books on various political issues. The authors of many of the works sold this time were personally present to sell their books. At the request of the buyers, they also signed their works.

　　In the afternoon, the General Secretary of the French Communist Party, Thorez, also stood behind the table to sell books. For a full four hours, he signed the new edition of his book "Son of the People." Yesterday, more than 1,200 copies of this book were sold in the "Mutual Aid Hall." The display shelves of Soviet books were crowded all day long. Here, you could buy the works of Marx, Engels, Lenin and Stalin, as well as new Soviet literary works.

### 为纪念法共成立四十周年  巴黎举行销售马克思主义书籍日  多列士和许多作者亲自到场售书

1960-03-29
第5版()
专栏：

    为纪念法共成立四十周年
    巴黎举行销售马克思主义书籍日
    多列士和许多作者亲自到场售书
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社巴黎28日讯：昨天在巴黎为纪念法国共产党成立四十周年而举行的销售马克思主义书籍日获得了很大成功。
    在“互助大厅”的一个大休息室里，昨天从一清早起就挤满了巴黎市民，他们是特地来这里购买马克思列宁的经典著作和国际工人运动领导人以及法国共产党领导人的著作的。
    沿着大厅的墙壁摆着一张张长桌子，桌子上放着各种政治问题的书籍。这次出售的许多作品的作者亲自到场售书。在购买者的请求下，他们还在自己的作品上签名。
    下午，法国共产党总书记多列士也站在桌子后面售书。整整四个多钟头他在他所写的新版的“人民的儿子”这本书上签名。昨天这本书在“互助大厅”售出了一千二百多册。在苏联书籍的陈列架旁整天都非常拥挤。在这里可以买到马克思、恩格斯、列宁和斯大林的著作，以及苏联新的文学作品。
