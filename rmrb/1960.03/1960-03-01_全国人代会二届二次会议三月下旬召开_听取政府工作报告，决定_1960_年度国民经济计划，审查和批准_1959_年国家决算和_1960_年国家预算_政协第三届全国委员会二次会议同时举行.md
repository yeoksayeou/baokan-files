    The Second Session of the Second National People's Congress to be Held in Late March
    To Hear the Government Work Report, Decide on the 1960 National Economic Plan, Examine and Approve the 1959 State Final Accounts and the 1960 State Budget
    The Second Session of the Third National Committee of the CPPCC to be Held Simultaneously
    Xinhua News Agency, 29th — The Second Session of the Second National People's Congress of the People's of China and the Second Session of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference will be held simultaneously in Beijing in late March. The 22nd meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the 6th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the CPPCC, held jointly today, decided to issue a joint notice.
    The main agenda of the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress is: to hear the Government Work Report, decide on the 1960 National Economic Plan, examine and approve the 1959 State Final Accounts and the 1960 State Budget.
    Xinhua News Agency, 29th — Joint Notice from the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
                                    February 29, 1960
    The Second Session of the Second National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China is scheduled to be held in Beijing in late March 1960 (the specific date will be notified separately). The main agenda of the meeting is: to hear the Government Work Report, decide on the 1960 National Economic Plan, examine and approve the 1959 State Final Accounts and the 1960 State Budget.
     The Second Session of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative will be held in Beijing in late March 1960 simultaneously with the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress.
     National People's Congress representatives and members of the National Committee of the CPPCC are encouraged to conduct an inspection of work locally or in other areas before the meeting. It is hoped that this inspection work will be completed before March 20.

### 全国人代会二届二次会议三月下旬召开  听取政府工作报告，决定1960年度国民经济计划，审查和批准1959年国家决算和1960年国家预算  政协第三届全国委员会二次会议同时举行

1960-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人代会二届二次会议三月下旬召开
    听取政府工作报告，决定1960年度国民经济计划，审查和批准1959年国家决算和1960年国家预算
    政协第三届全国委员会二次会议同时举行
    新华社29日讯　中华人民共和国第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议和中国人民政治协商会议第三届全国委员会第二次会议，定于3月下旬在北京同时召开。今天联合举行的全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十二次会议和政协全国委员会常务委员会第六次会议，决定发出联合通知。
    第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议的主要议程是：听取政府工作报告，决定1960年度国民经济计划，审查和批准1959年国家决算和1960年国家预算。
    新华社29日讯全国人民代表大会常务委员会中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会联合通知
                                    一九六○年二月二十九日
    中华人民共和国第二届全国人民代表大会定于1960年3月下旬在北京召开第二次会议（具体日期另行通知）。会议的主要议程是：听取政府工作报告，决定1960年度国民经济计划，审查和批准1959年国家决算和1960年国家预算。
    中国人民政治协商会议第三届全国委员会第二次会议定于1960年3月下旬在北京和第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议同时召开。
    全国人大代表、政协全国委员会委员最好能在会议以前就地或者到外地视察一次工作，这次视察工作希于3月20日以前结束。
