Title: Surely Make the Great Earth Green with Seedlings
Author: Fu Shi
Date: 1960-03-05
Page: 8

    Surely Make the Great Earth Green with Seedlings
    
    Few rains last winter, clear skies this spring,
    Stars fill the sky every night,
    “Eliminate the spring drought” a thunderous call,
    Ten thousand people are all water conservancy soldiers.
    Machines sing high notes,
    Factories all make engines,
    Irrigation and drainage machinery flies swiftly,
    Technology is rushed and innovated.
    To make spring plowing and sowing,
    Timely and early;
    Even if a Flaming Mountain flies over,
    Surely make the great earth green with seedlings.
    The three magic weapons are effective,
    We can win;
    Surely make the summer harvest grain pile up to the sky,
    This year, another leap forward!

### 定叫大地万苗青
俯拾
1960-03-05
第8版()
专栏：

    定叫大地万苗青
    俯拾
    去冬少雨今春晴，
    夜夜满天星，
    “消灭春旱”一声雷，
    万人全当水利兵。
    机器唱高音，
    厂厂做引擎，
    排灌机械飞运上，
    技术抢革新。
    要使春耕春播种，
    及时早进行；
    那怕飞来火焰山，
    定叫大地万苗青。
    三大法宝灵，
    我们能打赢；
    定要夏收粮堆天，
    今年再跃进！
