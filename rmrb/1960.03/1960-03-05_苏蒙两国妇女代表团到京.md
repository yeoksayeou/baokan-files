Title: Soviet and Mongolian Women's Delegations Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-03-05
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, March 4th: The head of the Soviet women's delegation, Maria Dmitrievna Kovridima, and member Alexandra Pavlovna Us, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation to participate in the celebrations for the 50th anniversary of International Working Women's Day, March 8th. Li Dequan, Vice-President of the All-China Women's Federation, Huang Ganying, Director of the International Liaison Department, and others welcomed them at the airport. Sudarikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, and Ablova, Second Secretary, also welcomed them at the airport.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 4th: The head of the Mongolian women's delegation, Zhana Heshigte, and member Nyam Dulema, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation to participate in the celebrations for the 50th anniversary of International Working Women's Day, March 8th.
    Shen Zijiou, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, Yang Bin, Director of the General Office, and others welcomed them at the station.
    The wife of the Mongolian Ambassador to China, Sharab, and Counselor Danggsuren also welcomed them at the station.

### 苏蒙两国妇女代表团到京

1960-03-05
第5版()
专栏：

    苏蒙两国妇女代表团到京
    新华社4日讯　应全国妇女联合会邀请来我国参加“三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年庆祝活动的苏联妇女代表团团长玛利亚·季米特里耶芙娜·柯芙利季玛，团员亚历山特拉·巴甫洛夫娜·乌斯，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有全国妇联副主席李德全，国际联络部部长黄甘英等。苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫、二等秘书阿布洛娃也到机场欢迎。
    新华社4日讯　应全国妇女联合会邀请来我国参加“三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年庆祝活动的蒙古妇女代表团团长札纳·赫希格特，团员尼亚木·杜勒玛，今天下午乘火车到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有全国妇联主席团委员沈兹九，办公厅主任杨彬等。
    蒙古驻华大使沙拉布的夫人，参赞丹格苏伦也到车站欢迎。
