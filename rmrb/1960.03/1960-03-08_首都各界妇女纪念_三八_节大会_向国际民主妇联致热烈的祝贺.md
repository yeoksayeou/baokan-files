Title: Women from All Circles in the Capital Hold a Meeting to Commemorate "March 8th" International Women's Day; Send Warm Congratulations to the Women's International Democratic Federation
Author:
Date: 1960-03-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The message of congratulations from the meeting of women from all circles in the capital commemorating the 50th anniversary of "March 8th" International Working Women's Day to the Women's International Democratic Federation. The text of the telegram is as follows:
To Madame Eugénie Cotton, dear friends of the Women's International Democratic Federation:

We, the women from all circles in the capital of the People's Republic of China, solemnly commemorated the 50th anniversary of "March 8th" International Working Women's Day today. As we celebrate this glorious revolutionary festival with immense joy, allow us to extend our warm congratulations to you and the friends of the Women's International Democratic Federation. For the prosperity of our China, for world peace, for the progress of mankind, and for the rights of women and children, we Chinese women will strive to promote the high-speed development of socialist construction, warmly support our government's peaceful foreign policy, unswervingly consolidate and strengthen the unbreakable friendship and unity between the women of China and the Soviet Union, consolidate and strengthen the friendship and unity of women in socialist countries, consolidate and strengthen the friendship and unity with women in Asian, African, and Latin American countries, and consolidate and strengthen the friendship and unity with all peace-loving women from all countries in the world; and inherit and carry forward the glorious revolutionary tradition of "March 8th" International Working Women's Day, and strive for greater victories.

Once again, we extend our best wishes for the festival to you.

		The Meeting of Women from All Circles in the Capital of the People's Republic of China Commemorating the 50th Anniversary of "March 8th" International Working Women's Day
					March 7, 1960, Beijing

### 首都各界妇女纪念“三八”节大会  向国际民主妇联致热烈的祝贺

1960-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各界妇女纪念“三八”节大会
    向国际民主妇联致热烈的祝贺
    新华社7日讯　首都各界妇女纪念“三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年大会致国际民主妇女联合会的贺电。电文如下：国际民主妇女联合会亲爱的欧仁妮·戈登夫人：
    我们——中华人民共和国首都各界妇女，今天隆重纪念了“三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年。当我们万分欢欣地庆祝这个光辉的革命节日的时候，让我们向您和国际民主妇女联合会的朋友们致以热烈的祝贺。为了我们中国的繁荣昌盛，为了世界和平，为了人类进步，为了妇女儿童权利，我们中国妇女将努力促进社会主义建设的高速度发展，热烈拥护我国政府的和平外交政策，坚持不渝地巩固和加强中苏两国妇女的牢不可破的友谊和团结，巩固和加强社会主义国家妇女的友谊和团结，巩固和加强同亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲各国妇女的友谊和团结，巩固和加强同全世界一切爱好和平的各国妇女的友谊和团结；并且继承和发扬“三八”国际劳动妇女节光荣的革命传统，为争取更大的胜利而奋斗。
    再一次向你们表示节日的最好的祝愿。
                             中华人民共和国首都各界妇女纪念
                          “三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年大会
                                            1960年3月7日北京
