International Briefs

    U Nu Returns to Rangoon
    Rangoon Dispatch: U Nu, Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma, returned to Rangoon by plane on the afternoon of March 12, after a twelve-day visit to India.
    Israeli Prime Minister Meets with U.S. Senators
    Washington Dispatch: Israeli Prime Minister Ben-Gurion met with the Chairman and members of the U.S. Senate Foreign Relations Committee for about two hours on the 11th. He later said at a press conference that they discussed the situation in the Middle East, the relations between Israel and Arab countries, the refugee problem, and the relations between Arab countries.
    Japan and India Sign Iron Ore Purchase and Sale Agreement
    New Delhi Dispatch: An agreement signed in New Delhi on March 8 between Japan and India stipulates that Japan will be able to obtain a supply of four million tons of iron ore annually from India starting in mid-1966. The initial validity period of this agreement is set at fifteen years.
    Floods Occur in Brazil
    Rio de Janeiro Dispatch: The eastern region of Brazil has recently been hit by floods due to five consecutive days of torrential rain and river flooding. Hundreds of houses have been washed away by the floodwaters, leaving nearly 10,000 people homeless. It is reported that as of the 11th, 95 people have died.

### 国际简讯

1960-03-13
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    吴努回到仰光
    仰光讯　缅甸廉洁的反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努在印度访问十二天后，12日下午乘飞机回到仰光。以色列总理同美国参议员会谈
    华盛顿讯　以色列总理本—古里安11日同美国参议院外交委员会的主席和委员们会谈约两小时。他随后在记者招待会上说，他们讨论了中东的局势、以色列同阿拉伯国家的关系、难民问题以及阿拉伯国家互相间的关系等。
    日印签订铁矿石购销协定
    新德里讯　日本和印度8日在新德里签订的一项协定规定，从1966年年中开始，日本每年能够从印度获得四百万吨铁矿石的供应。这项协定的有效期初步定为十五年。
    巴西发生水灾
    里约热内卢讯　巴西东部地区最近因连续五天暴雨，河水泛滥，酿成水灾。有好几百所房屋被大水冲走，近万人无家可归。据报道截至11日止，已有九十五人死亡。
