Title: Sukarno Ends Talks with Leaders of Three Major Political Parties
Author:
Date: 1960-03-22
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 19th: Indonesian President Sukarno concluded his talks with the Chairman of the Indonesian National Party, Suwirjo, the Chairman of the Communist Party of Indonesia, D.N. Aidit, and the Chairman of the Nahdatul Ulama, K.H. Khalid, on the issue of parliamentary reorganization, which began on the 16th. He returned to Jakarta from Bali this afternoon.

Suwirjo, Aidit, and Abdoelgani returned to Jakarta on the same plane as Sukarno.

A press officer from the Presidential Palace announced Sukarno's written statement at the airport. In the statement, Sukarno announced that the list of members of the "Cooperative Parliament" he intends to establish would be released before his trip abroad in April. Half of the seats in the "Cooperative Parliament" will be given to representatives of political parties, and the remaining seats will be given to representatives of professional organizations, including military personnel.

Sukarno also said that he would establish a preparatory committee for the National Front. Members of the preparatory committee will be selected from members of political parties, professional organizations, the Cabinet, the Supreme Advisory Council, and the Indonesian National Front for the Liberation of West Irian. The list of members of the preparatory committee is also expected to be announced before he goes abroad. Sukarno also said that preparations are being made to organize the People's Consultative Assembly, and the list for the People's Consultative Assembly will be announced after he goes abroad.

### 苏加诺结束同三大政党领袖的会谈

1960-03-22
第5版()
专栏：

    苏加诺结束同三大政党领袖的会谈
    据新华社雅加达19日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺结束了他从16日开始的同印度尼西亚国民党总主席苏维约、共产党主席艾地和伊斯兰教师联合会主席哈利德就国会改组问题举行的会谈，今天下午从巴厘回到雅加达。
    苏维约、艾地和阿卜杜加尼和苏加诺乘同一架飞机回到雅加达。
    总统府新闻官在机场上宣布了苏加诺的书面声明。苏加诺在声明中宣布，他将要成立的“合作国会”的议员名单将在他4月间出国访问以前公布。“合作国会”中有一半的席位将给政党的代表，其余的席位将给包括军人在内的专业团体的代表。
    苏加诺还说，他将成立民族阵线筹备委员会。筹备委员会的委员将从政党、专业团体、内阁、最高评议院和印度尼西亚争取解放西伊里安民族阵线的成员中选出。筹备委员会成员的名单预料也将在他出国以前宣布。苏加诺还说，正在为组织人民协商会议进行准备，人民协商会议的名单将在他出国以后公布。
