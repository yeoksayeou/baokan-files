Title: Promoting Rice Transplanters
Author:
Date: 1960-03-18
Page: 3
Brief News

    Promoting Rice Transplanters
    The work of promoting rice transplanters has been launched in the Qiandongnan Miao and Dong Autonomous Prefecture, Guizhou. More than 25,000 Type A Lilin No. 2 rice transplanters have been manufactured throughout the prefecture. The vast majority of rice paddies in the prefecture will be transplanted using rice transplanters this spring.

### 推广插秧机

1960-03-18
第3版()
专栏：简明新闻

    推广插秧机
    插秧机推广工作在贵州黔东南苗族侗族自治州展开。全州已制出醴陵二号甲型插秧机二万五千多部。全州绝大部分稻田今春将用插秧机插秧。
