Title: At the Invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai, Nepalese Prime Minister to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1960-03-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd - At the invitation of Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Bishweshwar Prasad Koirala, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, will pay a friendly visit to our country. Prime Minister Koirala is scheduled to arrive in Beijing on March 11.

### 应周恩来总理的邀请  尼泊尔首相将来我国访问

1960-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

    应周恩来总理的邀请
    尼泊尔首相将来我国访问
    新华社3日讯　尼泊尔王国首相毕什韦什瓦·普拉萨德·柯伊拉腊应中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来的邀请，将来我国进行友好访问。柯伊拉腊首相定于3月11日到达北京。
