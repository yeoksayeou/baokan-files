Title: Residents of Mogok Village in Kachin State, Burma, Hold Meeting to Support Sino-Burmese Border Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-03-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, March 10th: According to a report in today's "Burma New Light of Rangoon," residents of Mogok Village in Myitkyina District, Kachin State, Burma, held a public meeting last week to express their support for the Sino-Burmese Border Agreement.

More than two hundred local residents participated in the meeting. Speakers at the meeting pointed out that the people of Kachin State are pleased that this agreement will promote friendship and harmony between the Burmese and Chinese peoples.

### 缅甸克钦邦摩光村居民集会  支持中缅两国边界协定

1960-03-11
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸克钦邦摩光村居民集会
    支持中缅两国边界协定
    新华社仰光10日电　据“缅甸新光报”今天报道，缅甸克钦邦密支那县摩光村在上星期举行了一次公众集会，表示支持中缅边界协定。
    有二百多当地居民参加了这次集会。在会上讲话的人指出，克钦邦人民对这项协定将促进缅中人民之间的友谊和和睦都表示高兴。
