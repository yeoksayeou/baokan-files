Title: Vietnamese Youth Activists Congress Closes

Author: 
Date: 1960-03-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 17th: The first Socialist Labor Youth Activists Congress of North Vietnam, after three days of meetings, concluded victoriously this evening amidst the resounding slogans of striving to build socialism and strengthen the struggle for national reunification.

    The congress unanimously adopted an appeal to the four million young people of North Vietnam. The congress also adopted a letter of guarantee to the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party and President Ho Chi Minh. The letter of guarantee stated that we recognize more clearly than ever that the most meaningful thing in our youth is to struggle and grow under the glorious banner of the Party. The letter of guarantee put forward three guarantees: infinite loyalty to the Party's revolutionary cause; absolute trust in the Party's leadership, and unremitting struggle to realize all the Party's propositions; and resolute overcoming of all difficulties, giving full play to the spirit of daring to think and daring to act, and striving to launch competition to give full play to the leading role of youth.

    The congress also adopted a letter to the youth of South Vietnam.

### 越青年积极分子大会闭幕

1960-03-19
第5版()
专栏：

    越青年积极分子大会闭幕
    据新华社河内17日电　越南北方第一届社会主义劳动青年积极分子大会，经过三天的会议，今天晚上在努力建设社会主义和加强祖国统一斗争的昂扬口号声中胜利闭幕。
    大会一致通过了给越南北方四百万青年的号召书。大会并通过了给越南劳动党中央和胡志明主席的保证书。保证书说，我们比任何时候更认清了在我们的青春时代最有意义的是在党的光荣的旗帜下奋斗和成长。保证书提出了三项保证：无限忠诚于党的革命事业；绝对信赖党的领导，为实现党的一切主张而不懈奋斗；坚决克服一切困难，发挥敢想、敢干的精神，努力展开竞赛，充分发挥青年的带头作用。
    大会还通过了给南越青年的信。
