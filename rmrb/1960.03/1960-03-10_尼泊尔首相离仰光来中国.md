Title: Nepalese Prime Minister Leaves Rangoon for China
Author:
Date: 1960-03-10
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 9th - Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala and his entourage ended their three-day visit to Burma and left Rangoon by plane this morning for China.
　　Those who went to the airport to see off Koirala included Burmese Prime Minister Ne Win, government ministers, representatives from all walks of life, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Burma.
　　Burmese President U Win Maung hosted a banquet for Prime Minister Koirala and his wife on the 7th. The Burmese President and the Nepalese Prime Minister, in their speeches at the banquet, both expressed their hope to strengthen the friendly relations between the two countries. Prime Minister Koirala said that the Nepalese government strictly adheres to the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.
　　On the morning of the 8th, Prime Minister Koirala and Prime Minister Ne Win held talks on issues concerning the two countries. On the same evening, the Nepalese Consul in Rangoon held a reception for Prime Minister Koirala's visit to Burma. During his stay in Rangoon, Prime Minister Koirala also visited the leaders of the Clean Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League and the Stable Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League.

### 尼泊尔首相离仰光来中国

1960-03-10
第5版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔首相离仰光来中国
    新华社仰光9日电　尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊和随行人员结束了在缅甸的三天访问，今天上午乘飞机离开仰光前往中国。
    前往机场欢送柯伊拉腊的有缅甸总理奈温、政府部长们、各界代表和各国驻缅甸外交使节等。
    缅甸总统吴温貌曾经在7日设宴招待柯伊拉腊首相和他的夫人。缅甸总统和尼泊尔首相在宴会上讲话时，都表示希望加强两国之间的友好关系。柯伊拉腊首相说，尼泊尔政府严格奉行和平共处五项原则。
    8日上午，柯伊拉腊首相同奈温总理就有关两国的问题举行了会谈。同一天晚上，尼泊尔驻仰光领事为柯伊拉腊首相访问缅甸举行了招待会。柯伊拉腊首相在仰光期间，还访问了廉洁的反法西斯人民自由同盟和巩固的反法西斯人民自由同盟的领导人。
