Title: U.S. Warships Invade Fujian Territorial Waters; I Issue the 89th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-03-03
Page: 4

    U.S. Warships Invade Fujian Territorial Waters
    I Issue the 89th Serious Warning
    Xinhua News Agency, March 2nd: From 21:21 on March 1st to 1:15 on March 2nd, a U.S. warship intruded into the sea area of Baiquan and Dongyin regions of Fujian Province. From 9:44 to 13:14 on March 2nd, another U.S. warship again intruded into the aforementioned sea area. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. warships, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was ordered to issue the 89th serious warning.

### 美国军舰侵入我福建领海  我提出第八十九次严重警告

1960-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    美国军舰侵入我福建领海
    我提出第八十九次严重警告
    新华社2日讯　3月1日二十一时二十一分至2日一时十五分，美国军舰一艘侵入我福建省白犬、东引地区海域。2日九时四十四分至十三时十四分，又有美国军舰一艘再次侵入我上述海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第八十九次严重警告。
