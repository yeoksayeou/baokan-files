Title: Resolution of the National Committee of the Popular Socialist Party of Cuba: Raising the Level of Marxist-Leninist Ideology, Cultivating a Fighting Spirit to Defend the Revolution, and Smashing All Conspiracies of U.S. Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1960-03-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 5th - The plenary session of the National Committee of the Popular Socialist Party of Cuba concluded on the morning of the 3rd. The plenary session adopted a resolution on the national situation and a resolution on raising revolutionary consciousness and the level of Marxist-Leninist ideology. This plenary session began on February 29th.

The second resolution of the plenary session pointed out that in order to cope with any attacks by counter-revolutionaries and all possible difficulties, to cultivate a fighting spirit to defend the revolution, to expose and smash all the threats, sophistry, lies and confusing practices of U.S. imperialists, and to prevent the masses from being deceived and confused by enemy propaganda, it is necessary to increasingly comprehensively and completely understand the historical experience of the world revolutionary struggle, which is concentrated in the science of Marxism-Leninism. Therefore, it is necessary to improve and strengthen the work and distribution of the Popular Socialist Party's newspaper "Hoy" and other publications of the Party, to improve Marxist-Leninist education, and to enhance the study of the classic works of Marx, Engels, and Lenin, as well as the works of Mao Zedong, by Party members.

### 古巴人民社会党全国委员会决议  提高马克思列宁主义思想水平  培养保卫革命战斗精神　粉碎美帝国主义一切阴谋

1960-03-09
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴人民社会党全国委员会决议
    提高马克思列宁主义思想水平
    培养保卫革命战斗精神　粉碎美帝国主义一切阴谋
    新华社哈瓦那5日电　古巴人民社会党全国委员会全体会议3日清晨结束。全会通过了关于国家局势的决议和关于提高革命觉悟和马克思列宁主义思想水平的决议，这次全会是从2月29日开始举行的。
    全会第二个决议指出，为了对付反革命分子的任何进攻和可能遇到的一切困难，为了培养保卫革命的战斗精神，为了揭穿和粉碎美帝国主义者的一切威胁、诡辩、谎言和混淆视听的做法、为了使群众不致受到敌人宣传的欺骗和蒙混，必须日益全面和完整地了解集中体现在马克思列宁主义科学中的世界革命斗争的历史经验。因此必须改进加强和人民社会党机关报“今日报”和党的其他出版物的工作和发行，提高马克思列宁主义的教育工作和党员对马克思、恩格斯和列宁的经典著作及毛泽东著作的研究。
