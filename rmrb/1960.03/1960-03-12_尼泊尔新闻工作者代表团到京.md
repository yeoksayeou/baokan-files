Title: Nepalese Journalists Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-03-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 11th – A five-member Nepalese journalists delegation, invited by the All-China Journalists Association, arrived in Beijing by plane from Guangzhou this afternoon. The delegation is led by Pa. De. Pande, editor-in-chief of “New Society News.” The members of the Nepalese journalists delegation include: Kashora Raman, editor-in-chief of “Ideal News;” Ja. Ba. Tapalia, editor-in-chief of “Common People News;” Da. La. Sharma, editor-in-chief of “Current Affairs News;” and Madan Sharma, editor-in-chief of “Independent News.”
    Welcoming them at the airport were Deng Gang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Journalists Association, Kang Maozhao, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.
    In the evening, Deng Gang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Journalists Association, hosted a banquet to welcome the Nepalese journalists delegation.

### 尼泊尔新闻工作者代表团到京

1960-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔新闻工作者代表团到京
    新华社11日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问的尼泊尔新闻工作者代表团一行五人，在团长“新社会报”主编帕·德·潘德率领下，今天下午由广州乘飞机到达北京。尼泊尔新闻工作者代表团团员包括：“理想报”主编卡肖拉·拉曼；“平民报”主编贾·巴·塔帕利亚；“时事报”主编达·拉·夏尔马；“独立新闻”主编马丹·夏尔马。
    在机场欢迎的有，中华全国新闻工作者协会书记处书记邓岗、外交部新闻司副司长康矛召等。
    晚间，中华全国新闻工作者协会书记处书记邓岗设宴欢迎尼泊尔新闻工作者代表团。
