Title: Nepalese Dignitaries Visit Agricultural Exhibition Hall, Jade Factory and Ivory Carving Factory
Author:
Date: 1960-03-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th — The Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Mr. B.P. Koirala, his wife, and other accompanying dignitaries, visited the National Agricultural Exhibition Hall this morning. Prime Minister Koirala and his wife, accompanied by Vice Minister of Agriculture Liu Ruilong, Chinese Ambassador to Nepal Pan Zili and his wife, and the director of the National Agricultural Exhibition Hall, Wei Zhenwu, and other responsible personnel, visited the Comprehensive Hall, Water Conservancy Hall, Measures Hall, Tool Hall, and People's Commune Industry Hall. In the Water Conservancy Hall, Prime Minister Koirala stood for a long time in front of the models of the achievements in harnessing the Mang River in Henan Province, the project of diverting water up the mountains in Gansu, and the river network in Anhui, carefully listening to the explanations of the narrators and inquiring in detail about the processes of preventing floods and droughts and diverting water up the mountains. In the Tool Hall, the simple rice transplanter and the electric rope-drawn transplanter attracted Prime Minister Koirala's attention. He personally held the handle of the simple rice transplanter to try it out.

Before leaving the exhibition hall, Prime Minister Koirala wrote in the commemorative book: "The remarkable progress made by China in agriculture due to the implementation of the 'Eight-Point Charter' is indeed very impressive, and this progress can be said to be astonishing. The Chinese people can be completely proud of these achievements. I congratulate you." Vice Minister Liu Ruilong said: "This is Prime Minister's encouragement to the Chinese people." In the afternoon, Prime Minister Koirala's wife, Minister of Engineering and Transportation Ga Man Singh, Minister of Interior and Justice Su. Pu. Upa-dhayaya and other dignitaries, visited the Beijing Jade Factory and the Ivory Carving Factory.

### 尼泊尔贵宾参观农业展览馆、玉器厂和象牙雕刻厂

1960-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔贵宾参观农业展览馆、玉器厂和象牙雕刻厂
    新华社12日讯　尼泊尔王国首相柯伊拉腊和他的夫人以及其他随行贵宾，今天上午参观了全国农业展览馆。柯伊拉腊首相和夫人等贵宾在农业部副部长刘瑞龙，中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和夫人以及全国农业展览馆馆长魏震五等负责人的陪同下，参观了综合馆、水利馆、措施馆、工具馆和人民公社工业馆。在水利馆，柯伊拉腊首相长久地伫立在河南省漭河治理成就、甘肃引水上山和安徽河网化等模型前，仔细听取讲解员的说明，并详细地了解怎样防涝防旱和引水上山的过程。在工具馆，简易插秧机和电力绳索牵引机所牵引的插秧机，吸引了柯伊拉腊首相的注意力，他亲自用手握住简易插秧机的把柄，试试如何使用。
    在离开展览馆前，柯伊拉腊首相在纪念册上留言说：“中国在农业方面由于实施了‘八字宪法’而取得的卓越的进展，的确给予人们以很深刻的印象，这种进展可以说是惊人的。中国人民完全可以为这些成就而感到自豪。我祝贺你们。”刘瑞龙副部长说：“这是首相阁下对中国人民的鼓励。”下午，柯伊拉腊首相的夫人，工程和交通大臣加·曼·辛格，内政和司法大臣苏·普·乌帕德亚亚等贵宾，参观了北京玉器厂和象牙雕刻厂。
