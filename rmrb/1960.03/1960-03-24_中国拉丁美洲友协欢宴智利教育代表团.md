Title: China-Latin America Friendship Association Hosts Banquet for Chilean Education Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-03-24
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd: The China-Latin America Friendship Association hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the Chilean Education Delegation that recently visited our country. The delegation includes: Juan Gómez Millas, President of the University of Chile and President of the Union of Latin American Universities; Francisco Walker Linares, Chairman of the Intellectual Cooperation Committee of the University of Chile, and his wife; and Luis Oyarzún, Dean of the School of Fine Arts of the University of Chile.
    The Chilean peace activist couple, Mr. and Mrs. Ventulelli, and the writer couple, Mr. and Mrs. Tirano, who are in Beijing, were also invited to attend the banquet.
    At the banquet, Chu Tunan, President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, delivered a speech warmly welcoming the Chilean Education Delegation headed by President Gómez. President Gómez spoke at the meeting, thanking the China-Latin America Friendship Association for the invitation, highly praising the great achievements of China's construction, and expressing his desire to be a loyal friend of the Chinese people.
    Attending the banquet as hosts were Zhou Peiyuan, Vice President of Peking University and Director of the Institute of Physics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Pei Wenzhong, Director of the Institute of Vertebrate Paleontology; Zhou Erfu, Vice President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association; Chen Shunyao, Assistant to the President of Tsinghua University; Ai Zhongxin, Director of the Oil Painting Department of the Central Academy of Fine Arts; Zhu Boshen and Jin Ziming, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Zhou Zhenzhong, Director of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, and others.

### 中国拉丁美洲友协欢宴智利教育代表团

1960-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中国拉丁美洲友协欢宴智利教育代表团
    新华社23日讯　中国拉丁美洲友好协会今晚设宴欢迎最近来我国访问的智利教育代表团。他们是：智利大学校长、拉丁美洲大学联合会主席胡安·戈麦斯·米利亚斯，智利大学知识分子合作委员会主席弗朗西斯科·沃克·利纳雷斯和夫人，和智利大学美术学院院长路易士·欧阳逊。
    在北京的智利和平人士万徒勒里夫妇和作家地拉诺夫妇也应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会上，中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南致词，热烈欢迎以戈麦斯校长为首的智利教育代表团。戈麦斯校长在会上讲话，感谢中国拉丁美洲友好协会的邀请，盛赞中国建设的伟大成就，并表示要做中国人民的忠实朋友。
    出席宴会作陪的，有北京大学副校长、中国科学院物理研究所所长周培源，古脊椎动物研究所所长裴文中，中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复，清华大学校长助理陈舜瑶，中央美术学院油画系主任艾中信，中国人民对外文化协会副秘书长朱伯深、金子明，中国拉丁美洲友好协会理事周震中等。
