Title: U.S. Government Strikes a Pose, Issues Statement Expressing "Regret"
Author: 
Date: 1960-03-24
Page: 6

    Beijing – News report: U.S. State Department Press Officer White issued a statement on the 22nd, hypocritically expressing "regret" over the South African government's bloody suppression.
    The statement said, "The United States regrets all forms of violence and hopes that the people of South Africa will be able to redress legitimate grievances through peaceful means. While it is the consistent practice of the United States not to comment on the internal affairs of governments with which it has normal relations, it cannot fail to express its regret at the tragic loss of life caused by the measures taken against demonstrators in South Africa."
    The U.S. Information Service reported that the State Department's statement was "unusual."

### 美国政府故作姿态发表声明表示“遗憾”

1960-03-24
第6版()
专栏：

    美国政府故作姿态发表声明表示“遗憾”
    本报讯　美国国务院新闻发布官怀特22日发表声明，假惺惺地对南非政府的血腥镇压表示“遗憾”。
    声明说，“美国对一切形式的暴力都感到遗憾，并且希望南非人民将能够用和平手段使合法的不满得到补偿。虽然美国的一贯作法通常是不对同它有着正常关系的政府的内政发表意见，但是它无法不对在南非对示威者采取的措施所造成的生命损失的悲剧表示遗憾。”
    美国新闻处在报道中说，美国国务院的这个声明是“异乎寻常的”。
