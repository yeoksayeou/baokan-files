	International Briefs
	Sukarno to Visit Foreign Countries
	Jakarta Dispatch: The Indonesian Presidential Palace officially announced on the 23rd that President Sukarno will embark on a foreign tour of approximately two months on April 1st. Foreign Minister Subandrio will accompany him on the trip. Sukarno is scheduled to visit Iraq, Yugoslavia, Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, the United Arab Republic, Guinea, Tunisia, Morocco, Portugal, and Cuba. He will first stop in New Delhi, the capital of India, for a few hours to hold talks with Prime Minister Nehru.
	
	Segni's Attempt to Form Cabinet Fails Again
	Rome Dispatch: The Italian government crisis, which is entering its second month, still shows no signs of resolution. Former Prime Minister Segni, who had already resigned, abandoned his plan on the 21st to form another government at the request of President Gronchi. Gronchi has now asked Tambroni, the former Minister of Budget in the previous government, to come forward and form a government.
	
	Strong Earthquake Occurs in the Pacific Ocean
	Moscow Dispatch: On March 20th, at 20:18 Moscow time, the Soviet Central Seismic Station "Moscow" recorded a very strong earthquake. According to preliminary information, it occurred in the Pacific Ocean east of Japan, 7,400 kilometers from Moscow. The ground in Moscow shook by 850 microns.

### 国际简讯

1960-03-26
第6版()
专栏：

    国际简讯
    苏加诺将出国访问
    雅加达讯　印度尼西亚总统府23日正式宣布，苏加诺总统将在4月1日出国进行约两个月的访问。外交部长苏班德里约将陪同他出国访问。苏加诺预定访问伊拉克、南斯拉夫、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、阿拉伯联合共和国、几内亚、突尼斯、摩洛哥、葡萄牙和古巴。他将首先在印度首都新德里停留几小时，同尼赫鲁总理举行会谈。
    塞尼组阁又告失败
    罗马讯　即将进入第二个月的意大利政府危机仍然没有解决的希望。已经辞职的前总理塞尼21日放弃了总统格隆基要他再组织一个政府的打算。格隆基已要前政府的预算部长塔姆布罗尼出面筹组政府。
    太平洋中发生强烈地震
    莫斯科讯　3月20日莫斯科时间二十时十八分，苏联中央地震站“莫斯科”纪录到非常强烈的地震。据初步材料，它发生在日本以东太平洋中、距莫斯科七千四百公里的地区。莫斯科土地震动达八百五十微米。
