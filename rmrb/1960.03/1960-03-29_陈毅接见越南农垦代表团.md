Title: Chen Yi Receives Vietnamese State Farm Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-03-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 28th: Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received the members of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic State Farm Delegation, headed by Ambassador Tran Tu Binh and deputy head Le Nam Thang, this afternoon. The host and guests had a cordial and friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were Zhang Zhonghan and Liu Xing, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of State Farms and Land Reclamation; Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Chen Shuliang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

Tran Trong Quat, Counselor of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, was also present at the reception.

### 陈毅接见越南农垦代表团

1960-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅接见越南农垦代表团
    新华社28日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天下午接见了以团长陈子平大使和副团长黎南胜为首的越南民主共和国农垦代表团的成员。接见时宾主进行了亲切友好的交谈。
    接见时在座的，有农垦部副部长张仲翰、刘型，对外贸易部副部长李强，外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮等。
    接见时，越南驻中国大使馆参赞陈仲括也在座。
