Title: Irrigating Thousands of Acres of Fertile Land
Author: Yang Maolin
Date: 1960-03-09
Page: 8

    Irrigating Thousands of Acres of Fertile Land
    Yang Maolin
    With a spirit that swallows mountains and rivers, the sound shakes the heavens,
    The drought-resistant steed gallops forth.
    The horse's hooves churn the yellow earth, gushing forth clear springs,
    People welcome the north wind, calling for rain clouds.
    Machines chug and dragons irrigate the land,
    Rivers and canals form a network, reservoirs like stars.
    Commanding ten thousand dragons to deliver sweet dew,
    Thoroughly irrigating thousands of acres of fertile land.
    Despite the drought, seedlings remain lush and green,
    Though the earth cracks, we strive for a bountiful harvest.

### 灌饱良田千万顷
杨茂林
1960-03-09
第8版()
专栏：

    灌饱良田千万顷
    杨茂林
    气吞山河声震天，
    抗旱骏马在奔腾。
    马踏黄土涌清泉，
    人迎北风呼雨云。
    机器突突龙浇地，
    河渠成网库如星。
    喝令万龙交甘露，
    饱灌良田千万顷。
    天干保得青苗秀，
    地裂争来五谷丰。
