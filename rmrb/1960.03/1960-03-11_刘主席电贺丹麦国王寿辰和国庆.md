Title: Liu Chairman Sends Telegram to Congratulate Danish King on Birthday and National Day
Author: 
Date: 1960-03-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 9th. Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, sent a telegram to King Frederik IX of Denmark, congratulating him on his sixty-first birthday and the Danish National Day (March 11th). The telegram reads as follows:
To His Majesty King Frederik IX of the Kingdom of Denmark:

    On the occasion of Your Majesty's birthday and the National Day of the Kingdom of Denmark, I, on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name, extend my sincere congratulations to Your Majesty and the Danish people. I wish prosperity to the Kingdom of Denmark and happiness to its people. May the friendly relations between China and Denmark continue to develop. I wish Your Majesty good health.

                                        Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                        March 9, 1960

### 刘主席电贺丹麦国王寿辰和国庆

1960-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席电贺丹麦国王寿辰和国庆
    新华社9日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇打电报给丹麦国王斐德烈九世，祝贺他的六十一岁寿辰和丹麦国庆（3月11日）。电文如下：丹麦王国国王斐德烈九世陛下：
    值此陛下寿辰和丹麦王国国庆之际，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义，向陛下和丹麦人民表示热诚的祝贺。祝丹麦王国繁荣、人民幸福。祝中国和丹麦两国间的友好关系继续发展。祝陛下身体健康。
                              中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                    　一九六○年三月九日
