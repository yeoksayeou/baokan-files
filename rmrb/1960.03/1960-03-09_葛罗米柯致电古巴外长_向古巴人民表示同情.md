Title: Gromyko Calls Cuban Foreign Minister, Expresses Sympathy to Cuban People
Author:
Date: 1960-03-09
Page: 6
　
Xinhua News Agency, March 8th, TASS Moscow, March 7th: Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko, on behalf of the Soviet government and the Soviet people, expressed deep sympathy to the government of the Republic of Cuba and the Cuban people regarding the ship explosion in Havana harbor. In a telegram to Cuban Foreign Minister García, Gromyko wrote: We share the deep grief of the families of the victims of this explosion and express our sincere sympathy to the Cuban people.

### 葛罗米柯致电古巴外长  向古巴人民表示同情

1960-03-09
第6版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯致电古巴外长
    向古巴人民表示同情
    新华社8日讯　塔斯社莫斯科7日讯：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯代表苏联政府和苏联人民就哈瓦那港口轮船爆炸事件向古巴共和国政府和古巴人民表示深切的同情。葛罗米柯在致古巴外交部长加西亚的电报中写道：我们同这次爆炸中死难者的家属分担深切的悲痛，并表示我们对古巴人民的真切同情。
