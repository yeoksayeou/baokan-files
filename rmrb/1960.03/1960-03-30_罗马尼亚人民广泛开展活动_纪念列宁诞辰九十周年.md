Title: Romanian People Widely Carry Out Activities to Commemorate the 90th Anniversary of Lenin's Birth

Author:
Date: 1960-03-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 28th. The Romanian working people are enthusiastically carrying out activities to commemorate the 90th anniversary of Lenin's birth.

Many cultural centers, clubs, and schools in various parts of Romania have organized report meetings, exhibitions, and literary evenings on Lenin's life and activities. Theaters are also preparing to stage dramas and programs about Lenin. A number of articles commemorating Lenin have been published in various newspapers and periodicals. These articles discuss the great role of Leninist ideas in the development of literature and art. "Free Romania" also published a lengthy article entitled "Lenin on Romania."

In order to forever commemorate this great revolutionary teacher, the Romanian capital is preparing to erect a bronze statue of Lenin in front of the "Spark Building," and the square where the statue stands will be named "Lenin Square."

### 罗马尼亚人民广泛开展活动  纪念列宁诞辰九十周年

1960-03-30
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚人民广泛开展活动
    纪念列宁诞辰九十周年
    新华社布加勒斯特28日电　罗马尼亚劳动人民正热烈展开纪念列宁诞辰九十周年的活动。
    罗马尼亚各地许多文化馆、俱乐部和学校纷纷组织了有关列宁的生平和活动的报告会、展览会和文学晚会。各剧院也在准备上演有关列宁的戏剧和节目。一些报刊已经发表了不少纪念列宁的文章。这些文章论述了列宁主义思想在发展文学艺术方面的巨大作用。“自由罗马尼亚报”还发表了题为“列宁论罗马尼亚”的长篇文章。
    为了永远纪念这位伟大的革命导师，罗马尼亚首都准备在“火花大厦”前建立一尊列宁的铜像，铜像所在的广场将被命名为“列宁广场”。
