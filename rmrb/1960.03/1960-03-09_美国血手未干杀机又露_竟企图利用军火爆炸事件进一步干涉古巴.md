Title: America's Bloody Hands are Not Yet Dry, Murderous Intentions are Exposed Again: Attempting to Use the Military Explosion Incident to Further Interfere in Cuba

Author:

Date: 1960-03-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 8th: U.S. Secretary of State Herter issued a statement on the evening of the 7th, threatening to respond to the legitimate accusation made by Cuban Prime Minister Castro against the United States regarding the "La Coubre" ship explosion, saying that Prime Minister Castro's accusations "can only further intensify the unpleasant deterioration of relations between our two countries over the past year."

Prime Minister Castro solemnly pointed out in a speech on the 5th that the culprits of this sabotage should be sought among U.S. officials.

In this statement issued to the Cuban Chargé d'Affaires in the United States, Enrique Patterson, on the 7th, Herter falsely accused Prime Minister Castro's accusations of being "extremely provocative" while attempting to shift the responsibility for the United States' interference and sabotage of relations between the two countries. According to U.S. State Department spokesman Tally, the U.S. government will also issue a "formal note of protest" to the Cuban government later this week.

Prior to Herter's threatening statement to Cuba, a United Press International dispatch from Washington on the 6th revealed that the U.S. government was using the "La Coubre" explosion incident to plot further intervention in Cuba. The dispatch said that U.S. State Department officials believe that Prime Minister Castro's accusations against the United States "have strengthened the position of those who advocate a firmer policy." U.S. State Department officials predicted that this event "will accelerate a new crisis in the already strained relations between (Cuba and the United States)."

According to the United Press International dispatch, the conspiracy planned by U.S. government officials and members of Congress includes: issuing a declaration of severing diplomatic relations, instigating the U.S.-controlled "Organization of American States" to "condemn" the Cuban government, thereby paving the way for direct intervention in Cuba.

### 美国血手未干杀机又露  竟企图利用军火爆炸事件进一步干涉古巴

1960-03-09
第6版()
专栏：

    美国血手未干杀机又露
    竟企图利用军火爆炸事件进一步干涉古巴
    据新华社8日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国务卿赫脱7日晚间发表声明，就古巴总理卡斯特罗关于“勒库布尔号”轮船爆炸事件对美国提出的正当指责进行威胁说，卡斯特罗总理的指责“只能使过去一年中我们两国之间的关系的不愉快的恶化进一步加剧”。
    卡斯特罗总理曾经在5日发表的讲话中严正指出，应该从美国官员中寻找这次破坏活动的祸首。
    赫脱在7日向古巴驻美国大使馆临时代办恩里克·派特逊发表的这项声明中，反诬卡斯特罗总理的指责是“具有极大的挑衅性的”，同时企图转嫁美国干涉和破坏两国关系的责任。据美国国务院新闻发布官塔利透露，美国政府在本星期晚些时候，还要向古巴政府提出一项“正式抗议照会”。
    在赫脱向古巴发表这项威胁性的声明以前，合众国际社6日从华盛顿发出的一条电讯透露，美国政府正在利用“勒库布尔号”爆炸事件策划对古巴的进一步干涉行动。这条电讯说，美国国务院官员认为，卡斯特罗总理对美国的指责“已经加强了主张采取一种更加坚定的政策的人的地位”。美国国务院官员预测这件事“将在（古巴和美国）两国间已经紧张的关系中加速促成一个新的危机”。
    据合众国际社的这条电讯还透露，在美国政府官员和国会议员策划的阴谋中包括：发表一项断绝外交关系的声明，策动美国控制下的“美洲国家组织”“谴责”古巴政府从而为对古巴的直接干涉开辟道路。
