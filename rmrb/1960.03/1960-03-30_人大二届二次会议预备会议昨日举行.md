Title: Preparatory Meeting for the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress Held Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1960-03-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 29th
    The Preparatory Meeting for the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress Held Yesterday
    Passed the main agenda of the meeting: to listen to the report on the draft of the 1960 national economic plan, the report on the 1959 national final accounts and the 1960 national budget draft, the report on the struggle to strive for the early realization of the National Agricultural Development Program, the work report of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, etc., and to pass various resolutions.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 29th: The preparatory meeting for the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China was held in the Great Hall of the People this afternoon.
    The preparatory meeting was presided over by Chairman Zhu De.
    At the preparatory meeting, all the representatives first approved the agenda of the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress.
    The main agenda of the meeting includes: listening to Vice Premier Li Fuchun's report on the draft of the 1960 national economic plan, Vice Premier Li Xiannian's report on the 1959 national final accounts and the 1960 national budget draft, Vice Premier Tan Zhenlin's report on the struggle to strive for the early realization of the National Agricultural Development Program (revised draft), the work report of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and passing various resolutions.
    Next, the representatives elected the presidium and secretary-general of the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress, totaling ninety-seven people.
    At today's preparatory meeting, all the representatives also approved the list of the chairman and members of the Proposals Examination Committee of the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress.
    The newly elected Presidium of the Congress held its first meeting after the end of the preparatory meeting.
    The Second Session of the Second National People's Congress will open tomorrow - the 30th. All members attending the Second Session of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference will attend the National People's Congress meeting as observers.
    In the period before the meeting, NPC deputies and CPPCC members had inspected work in various places.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 29th: The Central People's Broadcasting Station will begin broadcasting the live broadcast of the opening of the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress at 14:55 on the 30th, using the frequency of the first program. Beijing Television will also broadcast it simultaneously.

### 人大二届二次会议预备会议昨日举行

1960-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    人大二届二次会议预备会议昨日举行
    通过会议主要议程：听取关于一九六○年国民经济计划草案的报告，关于一九五九年国家决算和一九六○年国家预算草案的报告，关于为争取提前实现全国农业发展纲要而斗争的报告，人大常委会的工作报告等，并通过各项决议
    新华社29日讯　中华人民共和国第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议今天下午在人民大会堂举行预备会议。
    预备会议由朱德委员长主持。
    在预备会议上，全体代表首先通过了第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议的议程。
    会议的主要议程有：听取李富春副总理关于1960年国民经济计划草案的报告，李先念副总理关于1959年国家决算和1960年国家预算草案的报告，谭震林副总理关于为争取提前实现全国农业发展纲要（修正草案）而斗争的报告，全国人民代表大会常务委员会的工作报告等，并且通过各项决议。
    接着，代表们选出了主持第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议的主席团和秘书长，共九十七人。
    在今天的预备会议上，全体代表还通过了第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议提案审查委员会主任委员和委员名单。
    新选出的大会主席团在预备会议结束以后，举行了第一次会议。
    第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议将在明天——30日开幕。出席中国人民政治协商会议第三届全国委员会第二次会议的全体委员，将列席这次全国人民代表大会会议。
    在会议召开以前的一个时期，人大代表和政协委员曾经到各地视察了工作。
    新华社29日讯　中央人民广播电台将在30日下午十四时五十五分开始，用第一种节目的频率转播第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议开幕的实况。北京电视台也将在同时转播。
