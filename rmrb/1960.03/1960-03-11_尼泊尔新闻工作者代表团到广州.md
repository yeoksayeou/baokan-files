Title: Nepalese Journalists Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-03-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 10th: The Nepalese journalists delegation of five members, led by Pa. De. Pande, editor-in-chief of "New Society Newspaper," arrived in Guangzhou today by car from Shenzhen at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association.

### 尼泊尔新闻工作者代表团到广州

1960-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔新闻工作者代表团到广州
    新华社广州10日电　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问的尼泊尔新闻工作者代表团一行五人，在团长、“新社会报”主编帕·德·潘德率领下，今天由深圳乘车到达广州。
