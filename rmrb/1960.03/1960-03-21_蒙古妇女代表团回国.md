Title: Mongolian Women's Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-03-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. The women's delegation from the Mongolian People's Republic, invited by the All-China Women's Federation to participate in the "March 8th" International Working Women's Day celebrations, returned home by train this afternoon after visiting Shanghai, Hangzhou, Nanchang, Guangzhou, and other places.

Liu Qingyang, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, Huang Ganying, Director of the International Liaison Department, and others were at the station to see them off.

### 蒙古妇女代表团回国

1960-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古妇女代表团回国
    新华社19日讯　应全国妇联邀请来我国参加“三八”国际劳动妇女节庆祝活动的蒙古人民共和国妇女代表团，在上海、杭州、南昌、广州等地参观访问后，今天下午乘火车离开北京回国。
    到车站欢送的有全国妇联副主席刘清扬，国际联络部部长黄甘英等。
