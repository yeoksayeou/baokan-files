Title: Ten-Nation Disarmament Committee Holds Secret Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-03-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, March 17th: The Ten-Nation Disarmament Committee of the East and West held its first secret meeting in Geneva on the 16th. A communique was issued after the meeting.

The communique stated that the meeting was held under the chairmanship of the representative of the People's Republic of Bulgaria. The British representative presented at the meeting a joint disarmament plan of the five Western countries: Canada, France, Italy, the United Kingdom, and the United States. The communique indicated that the Ten-Nation Disarmament Committee would hold its next meeting on the morning of the 17th.

### 十国裁军委员会举行秘密会议

1960-03-18
第5版()
专栏：

    十国裁军委员会举行秘密会议
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社日内瓦讯：东西方十国裁军委员会16日在日内瓦举行了第一次秘密会议。会后发表了公报。
    公报说，这次会议在保加利亚人民共和国代表的主持下进行。英国代表在会上提出了加拿大、法国、意大利、英国和美国五个西方国家的联合裁军计划。公报指出，十国裁军委员会将在17日上午举行下次会议。
