Title: Li Dequan Sends Telegram to Cuban Red Cross Protesting U.S.-Made Arms Explosion Crime
Author:
Date: 1960-03-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, March 8th. Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross Society, today sent a telegram to Major Nuñez, President of the Cuban Red Cross, protesting the crime of U.S. imperialism in creating the "La Coubre" ship explosion incident, and expressing condolences and sympathy to the dead and injured in this incident.

The telegram said: "On behalf of all members of the Chinese Red Cross Society, I extend our deep condolences to the deceased and express our deep sympathy to the injured and the families of the victims, and severely condemn the planners of this inhuman crime. They will not scare the Cuban people, but will instead strengthen the Cuban people's determination to carry the revolutionary cause through to the end. We wish your society success in its work of rescuing the injured and other humanitarian work."

### 李德全致电古巴红十字会抗议美国制造军火爆炸事件的罪行

1960-03-09
第6版()
专栏：

    李德全致电古巴红十字会抗议美国制造军火爆炸事件的罪行
    新华社8日讯　中国红十字会会长李德全今天致电古巴红十字会会长努?斯少校，抗议美帝国主义制造“勒库布尔号”轮船爆炸事件的罪行，并向在这次事件中的死难者和受伤者表示哀悼和慰问。
    电报说：“我代表中国红十字会全体会员对死难者致以沉痛哀悼、对伤者和受难者家属表示深切慰问，并严厉谴责这个惨无人道的罪行的策划者。他们吓不倒古巴人民，反而将加强古巴人民把革命事业进行到底的决心。祝贵会在救护伤者和其他人道主义工作中获得胜利。”
