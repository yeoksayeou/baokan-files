Title: The United States Actually Wants to Reenact the Guatemalan Incident; Rubottom Vigorously Trumpets the "Caracas Declaration"
Author:
Date: 1960-03-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, March 14th: Milwaukee News: Assistant Secretary of State for Inter-American Affairs Rubottom openly promoted the "Caracas Declaration" fabricated by the United States at the Tenth Pan-American Conference in 1954, advocating for the maintenance of "Pan-American collective security" in accordance with the spirit of this declaration. The United States used this declaration as a pretext to interfere in Guatemala at that time.

According to a United States Information Service report on the 13th, Rubottom, in this speech delivered on the 12th at a conference at the University of Wisconsin in Milwaukee discussing American foreign policy, repeated the platitude of the "Caracas Declaration" that "international communism" "constitutes interference in American affairs," while emphasizing that Latin American countries "must oppose any form of aggression with collective security measures." He also vigorously touted the "strength of the Pan-American system" controlled by the United States.

Rubottom's speech, following President Eisenhower's repeated clamoring to implement the Rio de Janeiro Military Treaty as a tool of American aggression, is yet another open threat, its spearhead aimed directly at the Latin American national independence movement, and first and foremost at the Cuban people.

### 美国竟想重演危地马拉事件  鲁博特姆大事鼓吹“加拉加斯宣言”

1960-03-16
第6版()
专栏：

    美国竟想重演危地马拉事件
    鲁博特姆大事鼓吹“加拉加斯宣言”
    新华社14日讯　密尔窝基消息：美国国务院美洲事务助理国务卿鲁博特姆公然鼓吹1954年美国在第十届泛美会议上制造的“加拉加斯宣言”，主张根据这个宣言的精神来维护“泛美集体安全”。美国当时就是以这个宣言为借口去干涉危地马拉的。
    据美国新闻处13日消息，鲁博特姆12日在美国密尔窝基威斯康星大学讨论美国对外政策的会议上发表的这次讲话，重复了“加拉加斯宣言”中所谓“国际共产主义”“构成了对美洲事务的干涉”的滥调，同时强调拉丁美洲国家“必须以集体安全措施来反对任何形式的侵略”。他并且竭力吹嘘美国控制下的“泛美制度的力量”。
    鲁博特姆的讲话，是继美国总统艾森豪威尔一再叫嚷要执行作为美国侵略工具的里约热内卢军事公约之后的又一次公开的威胁，其矛头直接针对着拉丁美洲民族独立运动，首先是针对着古巴人民。
