Title: The State Council Held Its Ninety-Seventh Plenary Meeting and Agreed to Sign the Sino-Nepalese Boundary Agreement and Economic Aid Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-03-22
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 21st. The State Council held its ninety-seventh plenary meeting on the 21st to discuss the "Agreement Between the Government of the People's Republic of China and His Majesty's Government of Nepal on the Boundary Question Between the Two Countries" and the "Economic Aid Agreement Between the Government of the People's Republic of China and His Majesty's Government of Nepal." The meeting unanimously agreed to sign these two agreements and decided to submit a request to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress to appoint Premier Zhou Enlai as the plenipotentiary representative for the signing of the agreement on the boundary issue between the governments of China and Nepal.

### 国务院举行第九十七次全体会议  同意签订中尼边界协定和经济援助协定

1960-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第九十七次全体会议
    同意签订中尼边界协定和经济援助协定
    新华社21日讯　国务院在21日举行第九十七次全体会议，讨论同尼泊尔签订“中华人民共和国政府和尼泊尔国王陛下政府关于两国边界问题的协定”和“中华人民共和国政府和尼泊尔国王陛下政府经济援助协定”的问题。会议一致同意签订这两个协定，并且决定提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会委派周恩来总理为签订中尼两国政府关于边界问题协定的全权代表。
