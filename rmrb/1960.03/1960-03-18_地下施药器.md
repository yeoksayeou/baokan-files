Title: Underground Applicator

Author: 
Date: 1960-03-18
Page: 3

Brief News:

    Underground Applicator
    Ji Changjun, a technician at the Culaishan Forest Farm in Tai'an City, Shandong Province, with the leadership and support of the Party, has successfully created an underground applicator for preventing and controlling forest pests. Using this applicator for irrigation and fertilization is more than five times more efficient than using a pot to pour pesticide into holes as was done in the past.

### 地下施药器

1960-03-18
第3版()
专栏：简明新闻

    地下施药器
    山东泰安市徂徕山林场技术员姬长俊，在党的领导支持下，创造成功一种防治林木虫害的地下施药器。使用这种施药器灌药、施肥，比过去插眼用壶灌药水提高效率五倍以上。
