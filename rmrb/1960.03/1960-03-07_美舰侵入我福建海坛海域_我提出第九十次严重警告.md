Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into My Fujian Haitan Waters, I Issue the Ninetieth Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-03-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th—On March 6th, from 1:49 AM to 4:09 AM, a U.S. warship intruded into the waters of the Haitan area of Fujian Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, a spokesman for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the ninetieth serious warning.

### 美舰侵入我福建海坛海域  我提出第九十次严重警告

1960-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

    美舰侵入我福建海坛海域
    我提出第九十次严重警告
    新华社6日讯　3月6日一时四十九分到四时零九分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省海坛地区海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第九十次严重警告。
