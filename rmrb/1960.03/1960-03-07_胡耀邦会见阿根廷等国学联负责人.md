Title: Hu Yaobang Meets with Leaders of Student Unions from Argentina and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-03-07
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, March 6th. Hu Yaobang, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, met this afternoon with the leaders of student unions from Argentina, Cuba, Honduras, Venezuela and other countries who are currently visiting China, and held cordial talks with them. Hu Yaobang, on behalf of the Chinese youth, extended a warm welcome to the guests and expressed his hope that the friendly exchanges between the youth and students of China and various Latin American countries would increase day by day, and that their solidarity would be further strengthened. The guests warmly praised the great achievements made by the Chinese people in socialist construction. They said that the achievements of the Chinese people are inspiring the anti-imperialist struggle of the Latin American people and youth.
    Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, and Qian Dawei, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, were also present at the meeting.

### 胡耀邦会见阿根廷等国学联负责人

1960-03-07
第6版()
专栏：

    胡耀邦会见阿根廷等国学联负责人
    新华社6日讯　共青团中央委员会第一书记胡耀邦，今天下午会见了正在我国访问的阿根廷、古巴、洪都拉斯、委内瑞拉等国学生联合会的负责人，并同他们进行了亲切的谈话。胡耀邦代表中国青年向客人们表示热烈欢迎，并表示希望中国和拉丁美洲各国青年学生之间的友好往来日益增多，团结日益加强。客人们热烈赞扬中国人民在社会主义建设中所取得的伟大成就。他们说，中国人民的成就鼓舞着拉丁美洲人民和青年学生的反帝斗争。
    会见时，中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立和共青团中央国际联络部副部长钱大卫等也在座。
