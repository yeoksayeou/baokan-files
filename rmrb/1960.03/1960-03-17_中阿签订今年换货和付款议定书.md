Title: China and Albania Sign This Year's Trade and Payment Protocol
Author:
Date: 1960-03-17
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, March 15th. After friendly negotiations, the trade delegations of China and Albania signed the 1960 Trade and Payment Protocol and the 1960 Protocol on the Albanian Government's Use of Chinese Loans and Grants in Tirana on the 15th.

    The Trade and Payment Protocol stipulates that this year China will export tin, paper, rice, tea and various textiles to Albania. Albania will export crude oil, gasoline, chromium, copper and other commodities to China.

    The protocols were signed by Luo Shigao, Chinese Ambassador to Albania, and Kiço Ngjela, Albanian Minister of Trade.

    Present at the signing ceremony was Albanian Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Abdyl Këllezi.

### 中阿签订今年换货和付款议定书

1960-03-17
第5版()
专栏：

    中阿签订今年换货和付款议定书
    新华社地拉那15日电　中国和阿尔巴尼亚两国贸易代表团经过友好的商谈后，15日在地拉那签订了1960年换货和付款议定书以及1960年阿尔巴尼亚政府使用中国贷款和无偿援助款项的议定书。
    换货和付款议定书规定，今年中国将向阿尔巴尼亚输出锡、纸张、大米、茶叶和各种纺织品等。阿尔巴尼亚将向中国输出原油、汽油、铬、铜等商品。
    在议定书上签字的是中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高，阿尔巴尼亚贸易部部长基·恩杰拉。
    参加签字仪式的有阿尔巴尼亚部长会议副主席阿·凯列齐。
