Title: Swallowing Clouds and Spitting Out Myriad Treasures
Author: 
Date: 1960-03-30
Page: 3

Swallowing Clouds and Spitting Out Myriad Treasures
Chemical workers have superb skills,
Their hands can create the universe,
Water, fire, cold, and heat obey their commands,
Swallowing clouds and spitting out myriad treasures.

### 吞云吐雾出万宝

1960-03-30
第3版()
专栏：

    吞云吐雾出万宝
    化学工人手艺高，
    双手能把乾坤造，
    水火冷热听使唤，
    吞云吐雾出万宝。
