Date: 1960-03-12

Western Windfall Leaves

    British Unemployed Workers Increase
    According to figures released by the British Labour Department, the number of unemployed in Britain at the end of January this year was 455,000, an increase of 7,000 compared to the end of 1959.

### 英国失业工人增加

1960-03-12
第6版()
专栏：西风残叶

    英国失业工人增加
    根据英国劳工部发表的数字，今年1月底英国的失业人数是四十五万五千人，比1959年年底可增加七千人。
