Western European Coal Production Continues to Decline

    A report compiled by the Secretariat of the United Nations Economic Commission for Europe acknowledges that anthracite production in European socialist countries continued to rise in 1959, while anthracite production in Western European capitalist countries declined for the second consecutive year.

    The most significant declines in anthracite production in Western European capitalist countries were in the United Kingdom (down 9.8 million tons), West Germany (down 7 million tons), and Belgium (down 4.3 million tons). Among these, Belgium experienced the largest proportional decrease, reaching 16%. In other coal-producing countries in Western Europe, production either decreased or remained unchanged.

    At the same time, unsold coal in Western European mines continued to accumulate before last November. In 1958 alone, coal stockpiles in Western European coal mines increased by 29 million tons.

### 西欧煤产量不断减少

1960-03-12
第6版()
专栏：西风残叶

    西欧煤产量不断减少
    联合国欧洲经济委员会秘书处编制的报告承认，1959年欧洲社会主义国家无烟煤产量继续上升，而西欧资本主义国家的无烟煤产量却连续第二年下降。
    西欧资本主义国家无烟煤产量下降最显著的是英国（下降九百八十万吨）、西德（下降七百万吨）、比利时（下降四百三十万吨）。其中比利时下降的比例最大，达到16％，在西欧的其他产煤国家，产量不是下降就是维持不变。
    与此同时，在去年11月以前，西欧国家矿井中没有卖掉的煤不断增加。在1958年一年中，西欧煤矿积存的煤增加了二千九百万吨。
