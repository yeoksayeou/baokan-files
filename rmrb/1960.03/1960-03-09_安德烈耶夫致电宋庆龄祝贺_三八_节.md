Title: Andreyev Sends Congratulations to Soong Ching Ling on International Women's Day

Author:

Date: 1960-03-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 8th. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters today received a telegram from A.A. Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, to President Soong Ching Ling, congratulating her on the "March 8th" International Working Women's Day.

The telegram stated: "On the occasion of the 'March 8th' International Women's Day, we extend our warm and heartfelt congratulations to you and, through you, to all the women of the People's Republic of China. We wish you good health and success in your great and noble activities."

### 安德烈耶夫致电宋庆龄祝贺“三八”节

1960-03-09
第4版()
专栏：

    安德烈耶夫致电宋庆龄祝贺“三八”节
    新华社8日讯　中苏友好协会总会今天收到了苏中友好协会理事会主席安·安德烈耶夫致宋庆龄会长祝贺“三八”国际劳动妇女节的电报。
    电报中说：“值此‘三八’国际妇女节之际，我们谨向您、并通过您向中华人民共和国全体妇女致热烈衷心的祝贺。祝您健康，并祝在您的巨大而崇高的活动中获得成就。”
