Title: U.S. Intensifies Tensions in the Far East, Sends Nuclear Submarine to Operate in Taiwan

Author:
Date: 1960-03-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 9th dispatch from Taipei: In order to continue creating tensions in the Far East, the United States has dispatched a nuclear submarine to operate in Taiwan.

On the morning of March 8th, at approximately 8:00 AM, a U.S. Navy nuclear submarine named "Swordfish" entered Keelung Harbor in Taipei and conducted a "performance" at sea for more than three hours.

U.S. Navy Seventh Fleet Commander Vice Admiral Griffin and the submarine's commander, Lieutenant Commander Ryczek, boasted extensively to the Chiang Kai-shek clique about its combat performance and power.

### 美国加剧远东紧张局势  派遣核子潜艇到台湾活动

1960-03-10
第6版()
专栏：

    美国加剧远东紧张局势
    派遣核子潜艇到台湾活动
    新华社9日讯台北消息：美国为了继续在远东地区制造紧张局势，派遣核子潜艇到台湾活动。
    8日上午八时许，一艘名叫“剑鱼号”的美国海军核子潜艇，开进台北基隆港，在海上进行了三个多小时的“表演”。
    美国海军第七舰队司令格里芬中将和这艘核子潜艇的艇长赖悌克少校，向蒋介石集团人员大肆吹嘘它的作战性能和威力。
