Title: My Women's Representative Arrives in Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1960-03-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 5th: Zhu Danhua, the Chinese women's representative and director of the Jiangxi Provincial Women's Federation, who was invited by the Mongolian Women's Committee to participate in the celebration of the 50th anniversary of the "March 8th" International Women's Day in Mongolia, arrived in Ulaanbaatar this afternoon.

Those who went to the station to welcome her included Shilegamma, Secretary-General of the Mongolian Women's Committee, Vice Minister of Health Gema, and personnel from the Chinese Embassy in Mongolia.

### 我妇女代表抵蒙

1960-03-07
第6版()
专栏：

    我妇女代表抵蒙
    新华社乌兰巴托5日电　应蒙古妇女委员会的邀请到蒙古参加“三八”国际妇女节五十周年庆祝活动的中国妇女代表、江西省妇联主任朱旦华，今天下午到达乌兰巴托。
    前往车站欢迎的有蒙古妇女委员会秘书长希勒格玛，卫生部副部长格玛和中国驻蒙古大使馆的人员。
