Saving Agricultural Land
Slovakia Utilizes Mountains for Sheep Farming

    The Slovak region of Czechoslovakia has a long tradition of sheep farming. The Slovak region has hundreds of thousands of hectares of alpine pastures suitable for sheep breeding. By using the mountains to graze sheep, the productivity of arable land is increased. Currently, the total number of sheep in the Slovak region exceeds 500,000, accounting for approximately two-thirds of the national total. In mountainous areas such as Banska Bystrica, sheep farming is developing particularly rapidly.

### 节省农业用地  斯洛伐克利用山地养羊

1960-03-05
第5版()
专栏：

    节省农业用地
    斯洛伐克利用山地养羊
    捷克斯洛伐克的斯洛伐克地区是有着悠久的养羊业传统的。斯洛伐克地区拥有数十万公顷高山牧场，适合羊的繁殖。由于利用山地放牧羊群，就提高了可耕地的生产率。目前在斯洛伐克地区，羊的总头数已超过五十万，即约占全国羊总头数的三分之二。在班斯卡·比斯特利察州等山区，牧羊事业发展得特别快。
