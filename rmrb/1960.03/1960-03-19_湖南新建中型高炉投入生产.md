Title: New Medium-Sized Blast Furnace in Hunan Goes into Production
Author:
Date: 1960-03-19
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Changsha Telegram: The new 255-cubic-meter medium-sized blast furnace at the Lianyuan Iron and Steel Plant in Hunan Province produced its first batch of molten iron on the afternoon of March 8th. On the day the blast furnace produced iron, all the workers and construction workers of the plant joyfully held a celebration meeting next to the furnace. Yu Mingtao, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Hunan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, cut the ribbon for the blast furnace amidst warm cheers and applause.

The equipment of this blast furnace is modern, and the amount of engineering involved in building this blast furnace system is considerable. Thanks to the construction workers launching a major technical innovation campaign, creating some high-efficiency construction machinery, and promoting many advanced construction experiences, the construction period was shortened by twenty-seven days compared to the plan, and the project quality reached excellent.

### 湖南新建中型高炉投入生产

1960-03-19
第2版()
专栏：

    湖南新建中型高炉投入生产
    据新华社长沙电　湖南省涟源钢铁厂新建的二百五十五立方米的中型高炉，在8日下午流出第一炉铁水。高炉出铁的当天，全厂职工和建炉工人欢欣鼓舞地在炉旁举行了庆祝会，中共湖南省委书记处书记于明涛在热烈的欢呼声和掌声中给高炉剪了彩。
    这座高炉的设备是现代化的，建筑这座高炉系统的工程量相当大。由于建炉职工大闹技术革新，创造了一些高效率的施工机具，推行了许多先进的施工经验，所以建设工期比计划提前了二十七天，而且工程质量达到了优等。
