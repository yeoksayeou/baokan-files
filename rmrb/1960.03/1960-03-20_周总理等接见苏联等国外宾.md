Title: Premier Zhou and Others Receive Foreign Guests from the Soviet Union and Other Countries
Author: 
Date: 1960-03-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 19th. Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, and Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi and other state leaders received foreign guests from the Soviet Union, Bulgaria, the German Democratic Republic, India, Japan and Cyprus visiting China this afternoon.

These foreign guests are: Soviet chemists Krachko, economist Figen, biophysicists Remezova and Sokurova, spectroscopist Shvalevskii, mathematician Vorobyov, paleontologist Aigrenngen, Bulgarian women representatives Zskiranova and Mat Dushkova, physicians Horst Lokel and Werner Krenoisdel from the German Democratic Republic, Indian women representatives Mrs. Sala Sharma and Mrs. Anjali Mukherjee, the entire delegation of the All-Japan Postal Workers' Union to China headed by Ōide Shun, and the entire delegation of the Cyprus United Democratic Youth Organization headed by Kyprianides. Li Dequan, Zhang Xiruo, Chu Tunan, Li Jiebo, Zhang Chao, Cao Mengjun and others were present at the reception.

### 周总理等接见苏联等国外宾

1960-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理等接见苏联等国外宾
    新华社19日讯　国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真和国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅等国家领导人，今天下午接见了在我国访问的苏联、保加利亚、德意志民主共和国、印度、日本和塞浦路斯等国家的外宾。
    这些外宾是：苏联化学家克拉奇柯，经济学家费根，生物物理学家列梅佐娃、索库洛娃，光谱学家史瓦烈夫斯基，数学家沃洛比约夫，古生物专家艾格伦根，保加利亚妇女代表兹斯基拉诺娃、玛特·杜什科娃，德意志民主共和国医师霍尔斯特·洛柯尔、维尔纳·柯仍依斯德尔，印度妇女代表萨拉·沙玛夫人、安家莉·木克吉夫人，以大出俊为首的日本全递信工会访华代表团全体成员，以基普里亚尼德斯为首的塞浦路斯统一民主青年组织代表团全体成员。陪同接见的有：李德全、张奚若、楚图南、李颉伯、张超、曹孟君等。
