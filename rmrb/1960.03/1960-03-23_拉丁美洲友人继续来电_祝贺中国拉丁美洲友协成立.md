Title: Latin American Friends Continue to Send Telegrams Congratulating the Establishment of the China-Latin America Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1960-03-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency dispatch
Latin American Friends Continue to Send Telegrams
Congratulating the Establishment of the China-Latin America Friendship Association

　　This newspaper comprehensively reports, according to Xinhua News Agency: Friendly organizations and prominent figures in some Latin American countries have continued to send telegrams congratulating the establishment of the China-Latin America Friendship Association.

　　These include Miguel Ángel Asturias, a renowned writer from Guatemala, and Olga Poblete, María Marcatt, Olga Urdubia, Elena Caffarena, and other women's peace and cultural figures from Chile.

　　In their telegrams, they all wished the China-Latin America Friendship Association great success in its work.

　　The organizations and prominent figures who sent congratulatory telegrams include: the Argentina-China Cultural Association, Ariovaldo Carvallo, President of the Brazil-China Cultural Association, and Secretary-General Roy Maciel, Sarandá Cabrera, President of the Uruguay-China Cultural Association, Marta Valentini, Secretary, and Elena Noriega, Director of the Mexican Modern National Ballet.

### 拉丁美洲友人继续来电  祝贺中国拉丁美洲友协成立

1960-03-23
第6版()
专栏：

    拉丁美洲友人继续来电
    祝贺中国拉丁美洲友协成立
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯　拉丁美洲一些国家对中国的友好组织和著名人士继续电贺中国拉丁美洲友好协会成立。
    危地马拉著名作家米格尔·安赫耳·阿斯图里亚斯，智利妇女界和平文化人士奥尔加·波布列特、玛丽亚·马尔卡特、奥尔加·乌尔都毕亚、艾雷纳·卡法雷纳等。
    他们在电报中，都预祝中国拉丁美洲友好协会工作中获得巨大成就。
    发来贺电的组织和著名人士有：阿根廷中国文化协会，巴西中国文化协会主席阿里奥瓦多·卡伐洛和秘书长罗依·马西埃尔，乌拉圭—中国文化协会主席萨兰达·卡布雷拉、秘书马尔塔·瓦棱第尼，墨西哥现代民族芭蕾舞团团长艾莱娜·诺利埃哥。
