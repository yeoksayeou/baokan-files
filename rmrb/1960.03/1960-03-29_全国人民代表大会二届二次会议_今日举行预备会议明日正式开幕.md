Title: The Second Session of the Second National People's Congress Will Hold a Preparatory Meeting Today and Officially Open Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1960-03-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 28th – The Second Session of the Second National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China is scheduled to hold a preparatory meeting on the 29th and officially open on the 30th. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 24th session (enlarged meeting) on the afternoon of the 28th, and approved the agenda (draft) of the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress.

The main agenda of the meeting includes: deciding on the 1960 national economic plan, reviewing and approving the 1959 state final accounts and the 1960 state budget, discussing and deciding on striving to achieve the National Agricultural Development Program ahead of schedule, and listening to the work report of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, etc.

Today's enlarged meeting also discussed and approved the list of the Presidium and Secretary-General (draft) of the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress, and the list of the Chairman and members (draft) of the Proposals Examination Committee of the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress.

The meeting also discussed and approved the work report that the Standing Committee will make to the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress, and determined the list of conveners of the delegation groups of the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress.

Xinhua News Agency, March 28th – The State Council held its 98th plenary meeting on the 28th.

The meeting discussed and approved the main indicators (draft) of the 1960 national economic plan and the report on the draft 1960 national economic plan to be made by Vice Premier Li Fuchun to the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress. The meeting discussed and approved the 1959 state final accounts and the 1960 state budget (draft), and the report on the 1959 state final accounts and the 1960 state budget draft to be made by Vice Premier Li Xiannian to the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress.

The meeting finally approved the changes in the administrative divisions of counties and cities, and the list of appointments and removals.

### 全国人民代表大会二届二次会议  今日举行预备会议明日正式开幕

1960-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会二届二次会议  今日举行预备会议明日正式开幕
    人大常委会扩大会议通过了这次会议的议程草案
    新华社28日讯　中华人民共和国第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议定于29日举行预备会议，30日正式开幕。全国人民代表大会常务委员会在28日下午举行第二十四次会议扩大会议，通过了第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议的议程（草案）。
    会议的主要议程有：决定1960年国民经济计划，审查和批准1959年国家决算和1960年国家预算，讨论和决定争取提前实现全国农业发展纲要，听取全国人民代表大会常务委员会工作报告等。
    今天的扩大会议还讨论和通过了第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议主席团和秘书长名单（草案）、第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议提案审查委员会主任委员和委员名单（草案）。
    会议还讨论和通过了常务委员会将向第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议作的工作报告，并且决定了第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议各代表小组召集人的名单。
    国务院会议通过1960年国民经济计划主要指标草案和预决算草案
    新华社28日讯　国务院在28日举行了第九十八次全体会议。
    会议讨论并通过了1960年国民经济计划主要指标（草案）和李富春副总理将向第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议作的关于1960年国民经济计划草案的报告。会议讨论并通过了1959年国家决算和1960年国家预算（草案）、李先念副总理将向第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议作的关于1959年国家决算和1960年国家预算草案的报告。
    会议最后通过了县、市行政区划变更事项和任免名单。
