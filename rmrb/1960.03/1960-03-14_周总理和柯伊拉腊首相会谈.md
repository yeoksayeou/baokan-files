Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Koirala Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1960-03-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 13th—Premier of the State Council of the People’s Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, and Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Koirala, held talks today.

    On the Nepalese side, those participating in the talks were Singh, Nepalese Minister of Engineering and Transportation, Upadhyaya, Minister of the Interior and Justice, and Thapa, Foreign Affairs Secretary.

    On the Chinese side, those participating in the talks were Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Gu Zhuoxin, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Pan Zili, Chinese Ambassador to Nepal.

### 周总理和柯伊拉腊首相会谈

1960-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理和柯伊拉腊首相会谈
    新华社13日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来和尼泊尔王国首相柯伊拉腊，今天举行了会谈。
    尼泊尔方面参加会谈的，有尼泊尔工程和交通大臣辛格，内政和司法大臣乌帕德亚亚，外事秘书塔普。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有外交部副部长章汉夫，国家计划委员会副主任顾卓新，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力。
