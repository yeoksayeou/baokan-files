Vice Chairman Song's Letter of Greetings to Sisters Across the Country

Dear Sisters:

As we solemnly celebrate the 50th anniversary of "March 8th" International Working Women's Day, I extend my warmest congratulations to you – sisters on all fronts across the country, and female heroes on all fronts! Congratulations on the glorious achievements and outstanding contributions you have made to the socialist construction of our country under the wise leadership of the Party and Chairman Mao!

Our country is in a new stage of socialist construction. In the new decade, we must catch up with or surpass the United Kingdom in the output of major industrial products, basically realize the modernization of industry, agriculture, and science and culture, thereby building our country into a strong socialist country, and actively prepare conditions for the future realization of communism in our country. This inspiring goal requires our sisters on all fronts, together with the people of the whole country, to continue to go all out, aim high, set lofty goals, and scale the peaks of science, technology, and culture; it requires us to hold even higher the glorious banner of the General Line, the Great Leap Forward, and the People's Commune, and advance bravely!

Sisters! At present, the people of the whole country are launching a high tide of studying Chairman Mao's works. As everyone knows, Mao Zedong Thought is the unified thought of the universal truth of Marxism-Leninism combined with the concrete practice of the Chinese revolution. It has played a decisive role in the victory of our country's revolutionary struggle and construction cause. Therefore, I sincerely hope that sisters will earnestly study Chairman Mao's works, arm our minds with Mao Zedong Thought—the invincible weapon, actively master scientific and cultural knowledge, scale the peaks of science, technology, and culture; conquer nature, build a strong socialist country; strengthen the great unity with all peace-loving women in the world; defend world peace; and contribute all our strength to safeguarding women's rights and children's happiness.

I wish you all new and greater achievements in production, work, and study!
I wish you all to become red, diligent, skillful, and thrifty female standard-bearers on all fronts!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Song Qingling
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　March 7, 1960

### 宋副主席致全国姐妹们的贺信

1960-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    宋副主席致全国姐妹们的贺信亲爱的姐妹们：
    在隆重庆祝“三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年的时候，我谨向你们——全国各条战线上的姐妹们、各条战线上的女英雄们，致以最热烈的祝贺！祝贺你们在党和毛主席的英明领导下，为我国社会主义建设事业，作出了光辉的成就和卓越的贡献！
    我们的国家正处在社会主义建设的新阶段，我们要在新的十年间，在主要工业产品的产量方面赶上或者超过英国，基本上实现工业、农业和科学文化的现代化，从而把我国建成一个强大的社会主义国家，并为将来在我国实现共产主义积极准备条件。这个振奋人心的奋斗目标，要求我们各条战线上的姐妹们和全国人民一起，继续鼓足干劲、力争上游，树雄心，立大志，攀登科学技术文化高峰；要求我们更高地举起总路线、大跃进、人民公社的光辉旗帜奋勇前进！
    姐妹们！现在全国人民正在掀起一个学习毛主席著作的热潮。大家都知道，毛泽东思想是马克思列宁主义的普遍真理与中国革命具体实践相结合的统一思想，它对于我国革命斗争和建设事业的胜利起了决定性的作用。因此，我衷心地希望姐妹们认真学习毛主席的著作，以毛泽东思想——战无不胜的武器武装我们的头脑，积极掌握科学文化知识，为攀登科学技术文化高峰；为征服大自然，建设社会主义强国；为加强同全世界一切爱好和平的妇女的大团结；为保卫世界的持久和平；为维护妇女权利和儿童的幸福贡献出全部力量。
    祝你们在生产、工作、学习各方面都取得更新更大的成就！
    祝你们在各条战线上成为红、勤、巧、俭的女红旗手！
                         宋庆龄
                      1960年3月7日
