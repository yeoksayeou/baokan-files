International Briefs

    Pakistani Army Chief of Staff Returns Home After Leaving India
    New Delhi Dispatch　Pakistani Army Chief of Staff Musa concluded his two-day visit to New Delhi and left here for home on the 7th.
    It is reported that Musa came to New Delhi at the invitation of Indian Army Chief of Staff Thimayya.
    Strike by Employees of State Bank of India
    New Delhi Dispatch　Employees of the State Bank of India, which is operated by the Indian government, held a strike demanding higher salaries, revised wage grades, and bonuses. The employees said that branches across the country had all gone on strike. The strike began on March 4.
    The State Bank of India has 287 branches across the country with a total of more than 22,000 employees.

### 国际简讯

1960-03-09
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    巴陆军总司令离印回国
    新德里讯　巴基斯坦陆军总司令穆萨结束了在新德里的两天访问，于7日离开这里回国。
    据报道，穆萨是应印度陆军参谋长蒂迈雅的邀请前来新德里的。
    印度国家银行雇员罢工
    新德里讯　印度政府经营的印度国家银行的雇员举行罢工，要求提高薪金，改订工资等级和发奖金等。雇员们说，全国各地分行全都举行了罢工。这次罢工是从3月4日开始的。
    印度国家银行在全国各地有二百八十七家分行，共有二万二千多名职工。
