Title: Khrushchev to Visit Liberia
Author:
Date: 1960-03-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 4th - TASS Moscow, 4th: Soviet Premier Khrushchev has accepted the invitation of Liberia's President Tubman to make a formal visit to Liberia at his convenience.

At the same time, Khrushchev also invited Tubman to visit the Soviet Union as a guest of the Soviet government. The President of the Republic of Liberia also accepted the invitation.

### 赫鲁晓夫将访利比里亚

1960-03-05
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫将访利比里亚
    新华社4日讯　塔斯社莫斯科4日讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫接受了利比里亚共和国总统杜伯曼的邀请，在对他方便的时候到利比里亚作正式访问。
    同时，赫鲁晓夫也邀请杜伯曼作为苏联政府的贵宾访问苏联。利比里亚共和国总统也接受了邀请。
