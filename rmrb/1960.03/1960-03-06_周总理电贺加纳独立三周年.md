Title: Premier Zhou Sends Telegram Congratulating Ghana on the Third Anniversary of Independence
Author:
Date: 1960-03-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 5th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to Ghanaian Prime Minister Nkrumah on the 4th, congratulating Ghana on the third anniversary of its independence. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Kwame Nkrumah, Prime Minister of Ghana:
    On the occasion of the third anniversary of Ghana's independence, I extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency, the government and people of your country on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China.
    I wish the government and people of Ghana new victories in the struggle against colonialism, consolidating national independence, and safeguarding peace in Africa and the world. I wish Ghana national prosperity and the people happiness. May the friendship between China and Ghana be further developed.
            Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                            March 4, 1960

### 周总理电贺加纳独立三周年

1960-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺加纳独立三周年
    新华社5日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在4日打电报给加纳总理恩克鲁玛，祝贺加纳独立三周年。电文如下：加纳总理克瓦米·恩克鲁玛阁下：
    欣逢加纳独立三周年，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向阁下、贵国政府和人民致以热诚的祝贺。
    祝加纳政府和人民在反对殖民主义、巩固民族独立、维护非洲和世界和平的斗争中取得新的胜利。祝加纳国家繁荣、人民幸福。祝中、加两国之间的友谊获得进一步的发展。
            中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                            一九六○年三月四日
