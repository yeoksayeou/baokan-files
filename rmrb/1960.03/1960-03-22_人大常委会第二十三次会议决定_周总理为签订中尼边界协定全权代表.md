Title: NPC Standing Committee's 23rd Meeting Decides Premier Zhou Enlai as Plenipotentiary Representative for Signing China-Nepal Boundary Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1960-03-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 21st -- The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 23rd meeting on the 21st. The meeting heard Premier Zhou Enlai's explanation regarding the signing of the "Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of His Majesty the King of Nepal on the Boundary Question." The meeting decided to appoint Premier Zhou Enlai as the plenipotentiary representative for signing the "Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of His Majesty the King of Nepal on the Boundary Question."

### 人大常委会第二十三次会议决定  周总理为签订中尼边界协定全权代表

1960-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    人大常委会第二十三次会议决定
    周总理为签订中尼边界协定全权代表
    新华社21日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在21日举行了第二十三次会议。会议听取了周恩来总理关于签订“中华人民共和国政府和尼泊尔国王陛下政府关于两国边界问题的协定”的说明。会议决定派周恩来总理为签订“中华人民共和国政府和尼泊尔国王陛下政府关于两国边界问题的协定”的全权代表。
