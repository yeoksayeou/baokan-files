Title: 美共名誉主席福斯特指出 世界资本主义的末日临近了 美国劳工将像巨人一样行动起来 (Honorary Chairman Foster of the American Communist Party Points Out: The End of World Capitalism is Near; American Labor Will Act Like a Giant)
Author:
Date: 1960-03-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 29th: William Foster, Honorary Chairman of the American Communist Party, told Alter Schelz, a reporter for the New York "Worker's Weekly," that the end of world capitalism is near, while world socialism has made astonishing progress.

Yesterday's "Worker's Weekly" published Foster's interview. Schelz visited Foster last week on his seventy-ninth birthday and paid tribute to him. Foster has been involved in the workers' struggle for sixty years and unfortunately suffered from paralysis more than two years ago.

Schelz greeted Foster. Foster replied, "How can I not be happy when I see what is happening in the world?" Foster said, "Now is the era of optimism, an era of great optimism. The forces of peace are winning. The end of world capitalism is near. World socialism has made astonishing progress. This progress is unstoppable. The cause I have fought for all my life is getting closer and closer to victory."

Foster said, "I think we are on the eve of a high tide of American workers moving forward. Black Americans today stand at the forefront of the struggle. Their struggle is a harbinger of the struggles that all Black and white workers will wage together."

Foster repeatedly praised the heroism of Black Americans. He pointed out that Black Americans see their enemies more clearly. They are also encouraged by the achievements of the people of the Soviet Union and China.

Foster condemned the Cold War propaganda carried out by American government agencies, radio stations, television, and newspapers. He said that this propaganda is intended to weaken the vigorous fighting spirit of the American working class.

Foster said, "However, this fighting spirit is indeed recovering. Workers' discontent is increasing. The Taft-Hartley Act, the Griffin-Landrum Act, and the layoffs that accompany automation are infuriating the people. American labor will once again act like a giant."

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 29th: Today, the New York "Worker's Weekly" published an editorial congratulating William Foster, Honorary Chairman of the American Communist Party, on his seventy-ninth birthday.

The editorial said, "On William Foster's seventy-ninth birthday on February 25, we, along with many others in the United States and abroad, express our love and admiration for him." The editorial said, "In the eyes of American labor, Foster's name, which is valued all over the world, is synonymous with nobility and vision. It is precisely for this reason that the enemies of American labor in our government have deprived Foster of the right to accept invitations from many organizations to go abroad for medical treatment. The government's practice is entirely out of hatred and is a remnant of animalistic barbarism." The "Worker's Weekly" editorial concluded by pointing out that on the occasion of Foster's seventy-ninth birthday, all those who respect his contributions to progress, social justice, and peace should strive to uphold Foster's right to seek medical treatment abroad.

### 美共名誉主席福斯特指出  世界资本主义的末日临近了  美国劳工将像巨人一样行动起来

1960-03-01
第6版()
专栏：

    美共名誉主席福斯特指出
    世界资本主义的末日临近了
    美国劳工将像巨人一样行动起来
    新华社日内瓦29日电　美国共产党名誉主席威廉·福斯特向纽约“工人周报”记者阿尔特·薛尔兹发表谈话说，世界资本主义的末日已经临近了，而世界社会主义获得了使人惊异的进展。
    昨天的“工人周报”刊载了福斯特的访问记。薛尔兹上周在福斯特七十九岁寿辰的时候访问了他，并且向他致敬。福斯特参加工人阶级的斗争已经有六十年，两年多以前，他不幸得了麻痹症。
    薛尔兹向福斯特问好。福斯特回答说：“当我看到世界上发生的事情的时候，我怎么能不高兴呢？”福斯特说：“现在是乐观主义的时代，是大好的乐观主义的时代。和平力量正在取得胜利。世界资本主义的末日已经临近。世界社会主义获得了使人惊异的进展。这种进展是挡不住的。我终生为之奋斗的事业已经越来越接近胜利了。”
    福斯特说：“我认为，我们正处在美国工人向前进的高潮的前夕。美国黑人今天站在斗争的前列。他们的斗争是全体黑人和白人工人阶级将要一同进行的那些斗争的先驱。”
    福斯特一再称赞黑人的英勇。他指出，黑人对他们的敌人看得比较清楚。他们还受到苏联和中国人民的成就的鼓舞。
    福斯特谴责美国政府机构、广播电台、电视和报纸所进行的冷战宣传。他说，这种宣传是为了削弱美国工人阶级旺盛的战斗精神。
    福斯特说：“但是，这种战斗精神的确在恢复中。工人的不满在增加。塔夫脱—哈特莱劳资关系法、格里芬—兰德勒姆法案以及伴随自动化而来的解雇正在激怒人民。美国的劳工将再次像一个巨人一样地行动起来。”
    新华社日内瓦29日电　今天纽约“工人周报”发表社论，祝贺美国共产党名誉主席威廉·福斯特的七十九岁寿辰。
    社论说：“在2月25日威廉·福斯特七十九岁寿辰的时候，我们也同在美国和在国外的许许多多人一样，向他表示对他的热爱和钦佩。”社论说：“在美国劳工心目中，全世界都器重的福斯特的名字，是崇高和有远见的同义语。正因为这样，我国政府中的美国劳工的敌人才剥夺了福斯特接受许多组织的邀请到国外去进行医疗的权利。政府这种做法完全是为了仇恨，是动物性的野蛮主义的残余。”“工人周报”的社论最后指出，在福斯特七十九岁寿辰之际，所有敬重他为进步、社会正义和和平而作出贡献的人，都应该为维护福斯特到国外医治疾病的权利而努力。
