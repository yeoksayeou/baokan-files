Title: Our Consul General in Jakarta Holds Film Reception for Overseas Chinese Preparing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-03-03
Page: 4

    Our Consul General in Jakarta Holds Film Reception
    for Overseas Chinese Preparing to Return Home
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 2nd. Li Jusheng, Chinese Consul General in Jakarta, held a film reception at the Consulate General on the evening of the 1st, entertaining more than 400 overseas Chinese who had come to Jakarta to gather and prepare to return to China.
    Huang Zhen, Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia, and Liu Yufeng, Counselor of the Embassy, attended the reception. Ambassador Huang Zhen and Consul General Li Jusheng expressed their deep concern for these overseas Chinese who had been displaced in the anti-Chinese activities in Indonesia.
    Ambassador Huang Zhen had cordial conversations with these overseas Chinese. Some overseas Chinese told Ambassador Huang Zhen that they wanted to contribute their experience in growing tropical fruit trees to the motherland.
    The overseas Chinese who attended the reception watched the large-scale color documentary "Celebrating Ten Years" of the motherland. They were very happy and encouraged by the great achievements of the motherland in the past ten years.
    These displaced overseas Chinese arrived in Jakarta on February 26 to assemble. They will return to China on the second batch of ships sent by the motherland.

### 我驻雅加达总领事举行电影招待会  招待一批准备回国的华侨

1960-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    我驻雅加达总领事举行电影招待会
    招待一批准备回国的华侨
    新华社雅加达2日电　中国驻雅加达总领事李菊生1日晚上在总领事馆举行电影招待会，招待四百多名到雅加达来集合准备回国的华侨。
    中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇和大使馆参赞柳雨峰出席了招待会。黄镇大使和李菊生总领事对这些在印度尼西亚排华活动中流离失所的华侨表示深切的关怀。
    黄镇大使同这些华侨进行了亲切的交谈。有些华侨向黄镇大使表示要把种植热带果树的经验贡献给祖国。
    出席招待会的华侨观看了祖国大型彩色纪录片“欢庆十年”。他们对祖国十年来的伟大成就感到十分欢欣鼓舞。
    这些流离失所的华侨是在2月26日到雅加达来集合的。他们将乘祖国派来的第二批轮船回国。
