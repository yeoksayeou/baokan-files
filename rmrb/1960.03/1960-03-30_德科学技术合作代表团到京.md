Title: GDR Science and Technology Cooperation Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-03-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, March 28th. The German Democratic Republic Science and Technology Cooperation Delegation, consisting of four members, led by Georg Henke, the head of the delegation, Chairman of the German Group of the Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Technical Scientific Cooperation, and member of the State Planning Commission and Director of the Foreign Trade Bureau, arrived in Beijing by plane today to participate in the fifth session of the Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Technical Scientific Cooperation.

Welcoming them at the airport were Wang Daohan, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Committee and Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, and Wang Xinyuan, member and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Light Industry, among others.

Also welcoming them at the airport were staff members of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China.

The remaining members of the delegation will arrive in Beijing in the coming days.

### 德科学技术合作代表团到京

1960-03-30
第2版()
专栏：

    德科学技术合作代表团到京
    新华社28日讯　前来我国参加中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会第五届会议的德意志民主共和国科学技术合作代表团一行四人、在团长、委员会德国组主席、国家计划委员会委员兼外贸局局长盖奥格亨凯率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有委员会中国组主席、第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，委员、轻工业部副部长王新元等人。
    到机场欢迎的还有德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆工作人员。
    代表团其他人员将于日内到北京。
