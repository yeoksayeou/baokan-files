    A Suggestion Regarding the Citation of Classic Works
    I have noticed that some articles cite passages from classic works, generally only noting the edition and page number, without specifying the title of the work. This makes it difficult to check the original text if one does not have the same edition of the classic work. To make it easier for readers, I suggest that authors include the title of the classic work being cited.　Ministry of Communications　Jiang Dejing

### 关于引用经典著作的一点建议
蒋德敬
1960-03-20
第8版()
专栏：读者·作者·编者

    关于引用经典著作的一点建议
    我看到一些文章引用经典著作的语句，一般只注明版本和页数，不注明出于哪一篇著作。这样，若没有同一版本的经典著作，查对原文就有困难。为了便利读者，建议作者将引用的经典著作的题目一并注明。　交通部　蒋德敬
