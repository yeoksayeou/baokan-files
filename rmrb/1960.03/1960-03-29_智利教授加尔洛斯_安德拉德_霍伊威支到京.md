Title: Chilean Professor Carlos Andrade Hoyl arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-03-29
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, March 28th. Carlos Andrade Hoyl, a member of the Chilean education delegation and professor of history and social law at the Teachers College of the University of Chile, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 智利教授加尔洛斯·安德拉德·霍伊威支到京

1960-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    智利教授加尔洛斯·安德拉德·霍伊威支到京
    新华社28日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的智利教育代表团团员、智利大学师范学院历史及社会法律教授加尔洛斯·安德拉德·霍伊威支，今天乘飞机到达北京。
