Title: Japanese National Federation of Transport Workers' Unions Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-03-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 1st: The Japanese National Federation of Transport Workers' Unions Delegation, invited by the National Committee of the China Highway Transport Trade Union, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to our country. The delegation consists of five people and is led by the head of the delegation, Hiroshi Kameda. Feng Fubao, Secretary-General of the National Committee of the China Highway Transport Trade Union, and others were present at the airport to welcome them.

### 日本全日通工会访华代表团到京

1960-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    日本全日通工会访华代表团到京
    新华社1日讯　应中国公路运输工会全国委员会邀请来我国访问的日本全日通工会访华代表团一行五人，在团长龟田弘率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国公路运输工会全国委员会秘书长封夫保等人。
