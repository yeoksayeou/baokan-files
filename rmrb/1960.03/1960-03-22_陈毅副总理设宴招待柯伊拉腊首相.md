Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Hosts a Banquet for Prime Minister Koirala
Author:
Date: 1960-03-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 21st – Vice Premier Chen Yi hosted a banquet at noon today, serving Beijing's specialty, Peking duck, to the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Koirala, and his wife, as well as the accompanying Nepalese dignitaries. All members of the Nepalese journalists delegation were also invited to the banquet.

Premier Zhou Enlai attended the banquet.

At the banquet, Prime Minister Koirala, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Vice Premier Chen Yi, along with Chinese and Nepalese guests, enthusiastically toasted to the everlasting friendship between China and Nepal.

Also present at the banquet were Vice Premier of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Luo Guibo, Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission Gu Zhuoxin, China's Ambassador to Nepal Pan Zili and his wife, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, and Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association Wu Lengxi, among others. ### 陈毅副总理设宴招待柯伊拉腊首相

1960-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理设宴招待柯伊拉腊首相
    新华社21日讯　陈毅副总理今天中午设宴，以北京特产烤鸭招待尼泊尔王国首相柯伊拉腊和夫人以及随行的尼泊尔贵宾。应邀出席宴会的还有尼泊尔新闻工作者代表团全体成员。
    周恩来总理出席了宴会。
    宴会上，柯伊拉腊首相、周恩来总理和陈毅副总理以及中、尼两国人士，都为中、尼两国的永久友谊，互相热情地碰杯。
    出席宴会作陪的，还有国务院副总理习仲勋，对外贸易部代理部长雷任民，外交部副部长章汉夫、罗贵波，国家计划委员会副主任顾卓新，我国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和夫人，国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将，北京市副市长吴晗，中华全国新闻工作者协会主席吴冷西等。（附图片）
    柯伊拉腊首相、周恩来总理、陈毅副总理在宴会上。
