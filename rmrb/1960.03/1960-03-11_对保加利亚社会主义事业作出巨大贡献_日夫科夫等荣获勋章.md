Title: Zhivkov and Others Awarded Medals for Great Contributions to Bulgaria's Socialist Cause
Author:
Date: 1960-03-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, March 10th. Bulgarian Party and state leaders Zhivkov, Yugov, and Ganev, as well as 238 outstanding workers on the economic and cultural fronts, were recently awarded the nation's highest honors—the Dimitrov Medal and the Order of the Bulgarian People's Republic, First Class, respectively. At the award ceremony held on the 8th, Ganev, Chairman of the Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly, presented the medals to the recipients.

The recipients are all active participants in the anti-fascist and anti-capitalist struggles, and they have made great contributions to Bulgaria's socialist construction.
