Title: Bo Yibo Receives Burmese Steel Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1960-03-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 18th: Vice Premier Bo Yibo received the Burmese Steel Delegation, led by U Kyaw Maung Gyi, this morning. The two parties engaged in friendly talks.

Present at the meeting were Liu Bin, Vice Minister of Metallurgical Industry, Zhai Ziqiang, Director of the General Office, and Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the First Asian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

U Aung Khin, First Secretary of the Burmese Embassy in China, was also present.

### 薄一波副总理接见缅甸钢铁考察团

1960-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    薄一波副总理接见缅甸钢铁考察团
    新华社18日讯　薄一波副总理今天上午接见了正在我国访问的由吴庆貌季率领的缅甸钢铁考察团全体团员。宾主进行了友好的交谈。
    接见时在座的有冶金工业部副部长刘彬、办公厅主任翟自强，外交部第一亚洲司副司长沈平。
    缅甸驻中国大使馆一等秘书吴翁钦也在座。
