Title: Vietnam Holds Congress of Socialist Labor Youth Activists; Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League Sends Warm Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1960-03-18
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency: The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labor Youth League on the 9th, warmly congratulating the opening of the Vietnam Congress of Socialist Labor Youth Activists.
    The telegram said: "We are delighted and encouraged by the outstanding achievements made by the Vietnamese youth in the great socialist cause of the motherland, and we are deeply convinced that this congress will further inspire the enthusiasm of the Vietnamese youth for socialist revolution and construction, and under the correct leadership of the Vietnam Labor Party and President Ho Chi Minh, they will make new contributions to overfulfilling this year's construction plan and preparing for the implementation of the Five-Year Plan. We sincerely wish your congress a complete success!"

### 越南举行社会主义劳动青年积极分子大会  我共青团中央致电热烈祝贺

1960-03-18
第5版()
专栏：

    越南举行社会主义劳动青年积极分子大会
    我共青团中央致电热烈祝贺
    新华社讯　共青团中央委员会9日致电越南劳动青年团中央委员会，热烈祝贺越南社会主义劳动青年积极分子大会的召开。
    电报说：“我们对越南青年在祖国的伟大的社会主义事业中所作出的出色成绩而感到欢欣鼓舞，并深信这次大会将进一步鼓舞越南青年的社会主义革命和建设的热情，在越南劳动党和胡志明主席的正确领导下，为超额完成今年的建设计划和为准备执行五年计划而作出新的贡献。我们衷心祝贺你们的大会胜利成功！”
