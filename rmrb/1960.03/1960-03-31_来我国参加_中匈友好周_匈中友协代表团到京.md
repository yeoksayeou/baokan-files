Title: Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Participate in "Sino-Hungarian Friendship Week"
Author:
Date: 1960-03-31
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, March 30th. The delegation of the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association, invited by the Chinese-Hungarian Friendship Association to participate in the "Sino-Hungarian Friendship Week" activities and pay a friendly visit to our country, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The head of the delegation is Pátaki János, Secretary-General of the Hungarian Bureau for Foreign Cultural Relations. The members include: Gálos András, Secretary-General of the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association; Simon István, member of the Presidium of the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association, poet, and Kossuth Prize winner; Márton János, Minister of Scientific Culture of the Hungarian Trade Union Federation; and Vass János, an advanced steel worker.
    He Luting, Vice President of the Chinese-Hungarian Friendship Association, Zhang Lianhua, Secretary-General, Li Guochao, Deputy Minister of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others went to the airport to welcome them.
    Bai Nuoke, Counselor of the Hungarian Embassy in China, and embassy staff also went to greet them.

### 来我国参加“中匈友好周”  匈中友协代表团到京

1960-03-31
第6版()
专栏：

    来我国参加“中匈友好周”
    匈中友协代表团到京
    新华社30日讯　应中国匈牙利友好协会邀请前来我国参加“中匈友好周”活动和进行友好访问的匈中友协代表团，今天乘飞机到达北京。代表团团长是匈牙利对外文化联络局秘书长帕塔基·亚诺什，团员有：匈中友协秘书长高劳·安德烈，匈中友协主席团委员、诗人、科苏特奖金获得者西蒙·依斯特万，匈牙利总工会科学文化部长马尔丁·亚诺什，先进钢铁工人瓦什·亚诺什。
    前往机场欢迎的有中匈友协副会长贺绿汀，秘书长张联华，中华全国总工会副部长李国超等。
    匈牙利驻华大使馆参赞白诺克和使馆工作人员也前往迎接。
