Title: China and East Germany Exchange This Year's Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1960-03-28
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 25th. The 1960 implementation plan for the cultural cooperation agreement between China and the German Democratic Republic was exchanged in Berlin on the 25th. According to the implementation plan, scientists and artists from both countries will visit each other this year and continue to exchange experiences; the two countries will send students and graduate students to each other; and will hold exhibitions and other cultural activities in each other's countries.

### 中德交换今年文化合作执行计划

1960-03-28
第5版()
专栏：

    中德交换今年文化合作执行计划
    新华社柏林25日电　中国和德意志民主共和国文化合作协定1960年执行计划25日在柏林换文。根据执行计划，两国科学家、艺术家今年将相互访问和继续进行经验交流；两国将互派留学生和研究生；并将互办展览会和举行其他文化活动。
