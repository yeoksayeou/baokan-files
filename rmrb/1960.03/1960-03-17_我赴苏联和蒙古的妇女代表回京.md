Title: Women Representatives to the Soviet Union and Mongolia Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-03-17
Page: 4

    Our Women's Delegation to the Soviet Union and Mongolia Returns to Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, March 15th: Luo Shuzhang, head of the Chinese women's delegation invited to the Soviet Union to participate in the celebration of the 50th anniversary of International Working Women's Day on March 8th, and delegation member Wu Quanheng, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon.
    Zhu Danhua, the Chinese women's representative invited to Mongolia to participate in the 50th anniversary celebration of March 8th, returned to Beijing by train on the 14th.

### 我赴苏联和蒙古的妇女代表回京

1960-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    我赴苏联和蒙古的妇女代表回京
    新华社15日讯　应邀赴苏联参加“三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年庆祝活动的我国妇女代表团团长罗叔章，团员吴全衡，今天下午乘飞机回到北京。
    应邀赴蒙古参加“三八”节五十周年庆祝活动的我国妇女代表朱旦华，已在14日乘火车回到北京。
