Title: Busy Delivering Fertilizer
Author: Chen Guichun
Date: 1960-03-11
Page: 8

    Busy Delivering Fertilizer
    Before the first rooster crows, the sky is not yet bright,
    Villages and teams are busy delivering fertilizer,
    Younger sister's feet don't touch the ground,
    Older brother carries four baskets in one load,
    Fertilizer to the fields makes the crops strong,
    We vow to harvest ten thousand piculs of grain in the autumn.
    Sha City Commune, Huailai, Hebei

### 送肥忙
陈桂春
1960-03-11
第8版()
专栏：

    送肥忙
    鸡叫头遍天未亮，
    村村队队送肥忙，
    妹妹双脚不沾地，
    哥哥一担挑四筐，
    肥到地里庄稼强，
    秋后誓打万石粮。
    河北怀来沙城公社　陈桂春
