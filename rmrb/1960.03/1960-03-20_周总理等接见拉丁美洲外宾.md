Title: Premier Zhou and Others Receive Latin American Guests

Author:

Date: 1960-03-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 19th: Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi, and other state leaders, this afternoon received Latin American friends from student, women, literary, medical, and peace circles who are visiting our country, and held cordial conversations.

The Latin American guests received were: Ismaelillo Vibas, representative of the Cuban University Students Federation and Secretary General of the Cuban Medical Students Association; Guillermo Estévez Boero, President of the Argentine University Students Federation; Joaquim Olinto Meirelles, Vice President of the Brazilian National Students Union; Augusto Figueroa, representative of the Venezuelan University Students Federation and Secretary of the Latin American University Students Relations Bureau; Mario Raúl Pinto, Secretary General of the Honduran University Students Federation; all members of the Bolivian Women's Delegation headed by Aranda Bedregal de Konitzer; renowned Brazilian pianist Arnaldo Estrela and his wife, violinist Marifeixa Iacovino; Argentine doctor David Varmont and his wife; and Chilean peace activist and Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, José Venturelli.

Also present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Chu Tunan, Li Dequan, Zhang Zhao, Cao Mengjun, and Shen Jian.

### 周总理等接见拉丁美洲外宾

1960-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理等接见拉丁美洲外宾
    新华社19日讯　国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真，国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅等国家领导人，今天下午接见了在我国访问的拉丁美洲的学生、妇女、文艺、医学界的朋友和和平人士，并且进行了亲切的交谈。
    接见的拉丁美洲外宾有：古巴大学生联合会代表、古巴医学院学生会总书记依斯迈利奥·维巴斯，阿根廷大学生联合会主席吉列尔莫·埃斯特维斯·博埃罗，巴西全国学生联合会副主席何阿克姆·奥林托·梅列宜斯，委内瑞拉大学生联合会代表、拉丁美洲大学生关系局书记奥古斯道·费纪罗阿，洪都拉斯大学生联合会总书记马里奥·劳尔·平托，以亚兰达·贝德雷加尔·德·科尼策团长为首的玻利维亚妇女代表团全体成员，巴西著名钢琴家阿纳耳多·埃斯特雷拉和他的夫人、小提琴家玛丽芙霞·亚科维诺，阿根廷医生达维特·万尔孟和他的夫人，以及智利和平人士、亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长何塞·万徒勒里。
    陪同接见的有：廖承志、楚图南、李德全、张超、曹孟君和申健等。
