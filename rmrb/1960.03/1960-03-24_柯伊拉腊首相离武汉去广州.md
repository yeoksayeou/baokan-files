Title: Prime Minister Koirala Leaves Wuhan for Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-03-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, March 23rd: Prime Minister Koirala of the Kingdom of Nepal, his wife, and accompanying Nepalese dignitaries, accompanied by Pan Zili, China's ambassador to Nepal, his wife, and Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, left Wuhan by train this morning for Guangzhou. The Nepalese journalists' delegation also traveled on the same train.

Prior to their departure, the distinguished guests toured the East Lake scenic area in Wuhan, and sailed on the lake, enjoying the scenery.

Those who went to the station to see off the distinguished guests today included Zhang Tixue, Governor of Hubei Province, Liu Huinong, Mayor of Wuhan, Major General Wu Shi'an, head of the Hubei military garrison, and responsible persons from various agencies, local organizations of democratic parties, and people's organizations.

### 柯伊拉腊首相离武汉去广州

1960-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

    柯伊拉腊首相离武汉去广州
    新华社武汉23日电　尼泊尔王国首相柯伊拉腊和夫人及随行的尼泊尔贵宾，在我国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和夫人、外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文的陪同下，今天上午乘火车离开武汉去广州。尼泊尔新闻工作者代表团也同车前往。
    行前，贵宾们游览了武汉东湖风景区，并泛舟湖面，观赏湖光山色。
    今天到车站欢送贵宾的，有湖北省省长张体学，武汉市市长刘惠农，湖北驻军首长吴世安少将，以及各机关、各民主党派地方组织和各人民团体的负责人。
