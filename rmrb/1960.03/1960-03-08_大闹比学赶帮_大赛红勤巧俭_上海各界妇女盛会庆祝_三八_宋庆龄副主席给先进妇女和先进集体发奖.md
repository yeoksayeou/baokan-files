Title: Launch a Mass Movement of Emulation, Learning, Catching Up, Helping, and Surpassing; Hold Grand Competitions of Redness, Hard Work, Skill, and Thrift: Shanghai's Women's Circles Celebrate "March 8th" International Women's Day; Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling Presents Awards to Advanced Women and Advanced Collectives
Author:
Date: 1960-03-08
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 7th March: Over ten thousand women from all walks of life in Shanghai held a grand gathering at the Cultural Square this afternoon to celebrate the 50th anniversary of "March 8th" International Working Women's Day.

Soong Ching Ling, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China and Honorary Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation; and Ke Qingshi, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC, and Mayor of Shanghai, attended today's celebration.

Also attending today's meeting were Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC and Vice Mayor of Shanghai; Liu Shuzhou, Alternate Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC and Vice Mayor of Shanghai; Song Richang, Song Jiwen, Zhao Zukang, and Sheng Pihua, Vice Mayors of Shanghai; heads of people's organizations such as the Shanghai Women's Federation, the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions, the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, the Shanghai Youth Federation, and the Student Federation; leaders of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai, and heads of local organizations of democratic parties in Shanghai.

The wives of consuls from various countries stationed in Shanghai, experts from the Soviet Union and other brotherly countries in Shanghai, and their wives were also invited to attend the celebration.

As Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling, Mayor Ke Qingshi, Vice Mayor Cao Diqiu, other Party and government leaders of Shanghai, and the women heroes wearing medals on their chests stepped onto the rostrum, the audience burst into prolonged applause.

Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC and Vice Mayor of Shanghai, spoke at the meeting first. On behalf of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC, he extended warm congratulations to the city's "March 8th Red Banner Pacesetters," "March 8th Red Banner Collectives," and women comrades on all fronts, congratulating them on the great achievements they have made in socialist construction, and wishing them greater efforts, continued advancement, and even greater victories.

Cao Diqiu said: Under the leadership of Comrade Mao Zedong, the Communist Party of China and the people of the whole country have found the three magic weapons of the General Line, the Great Leap Forward, and the People's Commune. These are the three magic weapons for accelerating socialist construction and for the thorough emancipation of women. With these three magic weapons, the productive, living, and mental outlook of our people have undergone significant changes, and this change is particularly evident in women.

Cao Diqiu pointed out: The Great Leap Forward and the People's Commune have opened up extremely broad horizons for the vast number of women to participate in social labor. After two consecutive years of Great Leap Forward in 1958 and 1959, the number of female employees in Shanghai's industrial, commercial, cultural, educational, scientific, and other fronts increased from 400,000 to 665,000. Hundreds of thousands of housewives also participated in neighborhood processing production, collective welfare, and collective service undertakings. After the communization of the suburbs, all 860,000 women with labor capacity participated in various production labor, making outstanding achievements on all fronts, and countless advanced individuals and advanced collectives emerged.

Cao Diqiu hoped that under the leadership of the Party and under the brilliant light of the General Line, the women of the city would hold high the red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, unite as one, strive to achieve a continued leap forward in 1960, and forge ahead bravely to win even greater victories!

Guo Jian, Secretary of the Women's Work Committee of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC and Director of the Shanghai Women's Federation, then spoke. After describing in detail the brilliant achievements of the women's movement in Shanghai over the past two years and the great contributions made by women in socialist construction, she said: The women of our city must resolutely listen to the Party, further promote the communist style of daring to think, daring to speak, and daring to do, and the spirit of continuous revolution, everyone should establish lofty ambitions, everyone should have great aspirations, launch a mass movement of emulation, learning, catching up, helping, and surpassing, hold grand competitions of redness, hard work, skill, and thrift, strive to become socialist working women with high awareness, high style, high culture, and high skills, and strive to achieve a better, more comprehensive, and sustained leap forward in 1960, and make better and greater contributions to the high-speed construction of socialism.

Soviet women's representative Manibak also spoke at the meeting, wishing Chinese women new and greater achievements in socialist construction.

After the speeches, the Shanghai Women's Federation presented awards to 3,001 "March 8th Red Banner Pacesetters" and "March 8th Red Banner Collectives" in the city. Soong Ching Ling, Ke Qingshi, Cao Diqiu, Guo Jian, and others presented certificates, medals, and banners to advanced women and advanced women's collectives such as Yi Shijuan, Ni Haibao, Wang Juanjuan, and the Third Group of the Locomotive Repair Workshop of Jiangnan Shipyard.

Female breeders, female principals, female teachers, female actors, and female service station chiefs who won the titles of "March 8th Red Banner Pacesetter" and "March 8th Red Banner Collective" spoke at the meeting, expressing that they would always listen to the Party, hold the advanced red flag even higher, and make greater contributions to the continued leap forward.

### 大闹比学赶帮  大赛红勤巧俭  上海各界妇女盛会庆祝“三八”  宋庆龄副主席给先进妇女和先进集体发奖

1960-03-08
第2版()
专栏：

    大闹比学赶帮  大赛红勤巧俭
    上海各界妇女盛会庆祝“三八”
    宋庆龄副主席给先进妇女和先进集体发奖
    新华社上海7日电　上海各界妇女一万多人今天下午在文化广场举行盛大集会，隆重庆祝“三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年。
    中华人民共和国副主席、全国妇女联合会名誉主席宋庆龄，中共中央政治局委员、中共上海市委第一书记、上海市市长柯庆施出席了今天的庆祝大会。
    出席今天大会的还有，中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长曹荻秋，中共上海市委书记处候补书记、上海市副市长刘述周，上海市副市长宋日昌、宋季文、赵祖康、盛丕华，上海市妇联、市总工会、共青团市委、市青联、学联等人民团体负责人，人民解放军驻上海部队首长和民主党派上海地方组织负责人。
    各国驻上海的领事的夫人、苏联及其他兄弟国家在上海的专家、专家夫人等也应邀出席了庆祝会。
    当宋庆龄副主席、柯庆施市长、曹荻秋副市长和上海市其他党政领导人，以及胸前佩戴着奖章的女英雄们走上主席台时，全场响起了经久不息的掌声。
    中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长曹荻秋首先在会上讲话。他代表中共上海市委向全市的“三八红旗手”、“三八红旗集体”和各条战线上的妇女同志致以热烈的祝贺，祝贺她们在社会主义建设中取得的巨大成绩，并祝她们鼓足更大干劲，乘胜前进，夺取更大的战果。
    曹荻秋说：中国共产党和全国人民在毛泽东同志领导下，找到了总路线、大跃进和人民公社三个法宝，这是加速社会主义建设的三个法宝，也是彻底解放妇女的三个法宝。有了这三个法宝，我国人民的生产面貌、生活面貌和精神面貌都起了显著变化，这个变化在妇女身上表现得非常明显。
    曹荻秋指出：大跃进和人民公社为广大妇女参加社会劳动开辟了极其广阔的天地。经过1958和1959两年连续大跃进，上海工业、商业、文化教育、科学等各条战线上的女职工由四十万增加到六十六万五千人，还有几十万家庭妇女参加了里弄的加工生产、集体福利和集体服务事业，郊区八十六万有劳动能力的妇女，人民公社化以后全部参加了各种生产劳动，在各条战线上作出了出色的成绩，涌现了无数先进人物和先进集体。
    曹荻秋希望全市妇女在党的领导下，在总路线的光辉照耀下，高举毛泽东思想的红旗，团结一致为实现1960年的继续跃进，夺取更大的胜利而奋勇前进！
    中共上海市委妇女工作委员会书记、上海市妇女联合会主任郭建接着讲话。她在详细叙述了两年来上海妇女运动的辉煌成就和妇女在社会主义建设中所作的伟大贡献以后说：我们全市妇女一定要坚决听党的话，进一步发扬敢想、敢说、敢做的共产主义风格和不断革命的精神，人人树雄心，个个立大志，大闹比学赶帮，大赛红勤巧俭，争取成为觉悟高、风格高、文化高、技术高的社会主义劳动妇女，为实现1960年更好的全面的持续跃进而奋勇前进，为高速度地建设社会主义作出更好更大的贡献。
    苏联妇女代表曼依巴克也在会上讲话，祝中国妇女在社会主义建设中获得新的更大的成就。
    讲话结束以后，上海市妇联向全市三千零一名“三八红旗手”和“三八红旗集体”发奖。宋庆龄、柯庆施、曹荻秋、郭建等把奖状奖章和奖旗，颁发给裔式娟、倪海宝、王娟娟和江南造船厂修机车间第三小组等先进妇女和先进妇女集体。
    获得“三八红旗手”、“三八红旗集体”的女饲养员、女校长、女教师、女演员、女服务站长等在会上发言，表示要永远听党的话，更高地举起先进红旗，为继续跃进作出更大的贡献。
