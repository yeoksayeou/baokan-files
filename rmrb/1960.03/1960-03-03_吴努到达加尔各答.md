Title: U Nu Arrives in Calcutta
Author:
Date: 1960-03-03
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 1st - According to the Press Trust of India, U Nu, the former Prime Minister of Burma and leader of the Clean Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League, arrived in Calcutta by plane from Rangoon today. This evening, he will have dinner with Indian Prime Minister Nehru and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev. U Nu will visit India for twelve days.

### 吴努到达加尔各答

1960-03-03
第5版()
专栏：

    吴努到达加尔各答
    新华社新德里1日电　据印度报业托辣斯报道，缅甸前总理、廉洁的反法西斯人民自由同盟领袖吴努今天乘飞机从仰光到达加尔各答。今晚，他将同印度总理尼赫鲁和苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫共进晚餐。吴努将在印度访问十二天。
