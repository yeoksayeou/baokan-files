Title: China Donates Medical Supplies to Nepal
Author:
Date: 1960-03-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 22nd: Kathmandu News: The gifts from the Ministry of Health of the People's Republic of China to the Nepal Anti-Tuberculosis Association were handed over by Chen Luzhi, Second Secretary of the Chinese Embassy in Nepal, to Dixit, Director of the Office of the Nepal Anti-Tuberculosis Association, in Kathmandu on the 21st. The donation ceremony was held at the Nepalese Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

The gifts donated by the Chinese Ministry of Health to the Nepal Anti-Tuberculosis Association included an X-ray machine and three kinds of medicines for treating lung diseases: sodium aminosalicylate, isoniazid, and streptomycin. All of these were made in China and are worth 34,700 yuan.

### 我国赠给尼泊尔一批医疗用品

1960-03-24
第5版()
专栏：

    我国赠给尼泊尔一批医疗用品
    据新华社新德里22日电　加德满都消息：中华人民共和国卫生部赠送给尼泊尔防痨协会的礼物，已由中国驻尼泊尔大使馆二等秘书陈鲁直21日在加德满都交给尼泊尔防痨协会办公室主任迪克希特。赠送仪式在尼泊尔外交部举行。
    中国卫生部赠送给尼泊尔防痨协会的礼物包括一台X光机以及三种医治肺病的药品——对氨基水杨酸纳、异烟肼和链霉素。所有这些都是中国制造的，价值三万四千七百元。
