Title: New Craters and Valleys on the Far Side of the Moon are Named: Chinese Scientist Zu Chongzhi and Others Immortalized on the Moon
Author:
Date: 1960-03-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 19th. According to TASS, Soviet scientists, in further studies of photographs of the far side of the moon, have identified a number of new craters and valleys. These craters and valleys have now been named. One of them is named after Zu Chongzhi, a famous ancient Chinese scientist.

This batch of new craters and valleys are all named after outstanding scientists and cultural figures. These names are Giordano Bruno, Jules Verne, Hertz, Kurchatov, Lobachevsky, Maxwell, Mendeleev, Pasteur, Popov, Skłodowska-Curie, Zu Chongzhi, and Edison.

The naming was decided by a committee of the Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences. The Presidium has approved the committee's recommendations. The diagram showing the naming of the far side of the moon is a TASS draft (issued by Xinhua News Agency).

### 月球背面新环形山和园谷定名  我国科学家祖冲之等名垂月宫

1960-03-20
第4版()
专栏：

    月球背面新环形山和园谷定名
    我国科学家祖冲之等名垂月宫
    新华社莫斯科19日电　据塔斯社报道，苏联科学家在进一步研究月球背面照片的过程中确定了一些新的环形山和园谷。现在这些环形山和园谷已被定名。其中有一个是用中国古代著名科学家祖冲之的名字命名的。
    这一批新的环形山和园谷都用杰出的科学家和文化界人士的名字命名。这些名字是乔达诺·布鲁诺、儒勒·凡尔纳、赫茨、库尔恰托夫、洛巴契夫斯基、马克斯威尔、门德雷叶夫、巴斯德、波波夫、斯克洛多夫斯卡—居里、祖冲之和爱迪生。
    命名是由苏联科学院主席团一个委员会决定的。主席团已经批准了委员会的建议。图为月球背面命名示意图塔斯社稿（新华社发）
