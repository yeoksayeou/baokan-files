Title: Burmese Cabinet Decides to Submit Sino-Burmese Treaties and Agreements to the Chamber of Deputies for Approval
Author:
Date: 1960-03-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, March 25th: According to newspaper reports today, the Burmese Cabinet meeting on March 23rd decided to submit the Sino-Burmese Boundary Question Agreement and the Sino-Burmese Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Non-Aggression to the Chamber of Deputies, which will convene on April 1st, for approval.

### 缅甸内阁会议决定  把中缅条约和协定提交代表院批准

1960-03-26
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸内阁会议决定
    把中缅条约和协定提交代表院批准
    新华社仰光25日电　据报纸消息今天报道，缅甸内阁会议3月23日决定把中缅边界问题协定、中缅友好和互不侵犯条约提交将于4月1日开会的代表院批准。
