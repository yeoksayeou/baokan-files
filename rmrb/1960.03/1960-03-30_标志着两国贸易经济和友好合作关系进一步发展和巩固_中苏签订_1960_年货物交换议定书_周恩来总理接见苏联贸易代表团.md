Title: Marking a Further Development and Consolidation of Trade, Economic and Friendly Cooperative Relations Between the Two Countries, China and the Soviet Union Sign the 1960 Goods Exchange Protocol, Premier Zhou Enlai Receives the Soviet Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-03-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 29th -- The trade negotiations between the government trade delegations of the People's Republic of China and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics in Beijing regarding 1960 have come to a satisfactory conclusion in a friendly and harmonious atmosphere, and the China-Soviet Union 1960 Goods Exchange Protocol was signed in Beijing on March 29, 1960. According to the above-mentioned protocol, the 1960 trade volume between China and the Soviet Union will increase by 10% compared with the trade volume stipulated in the China-Soviet Union 1959 Goods Exchange Protocol. The signing of this Protocol marks a further development and consolidation of trade, economic and friendly cooperative relations between China and the Soviet Union.

    According to the 1960 China-Soviet Union Goods Exchange Protocol, the Soviet Union will increase its supply of many types of equipment and other goods to China. The main goods supplied by the Soviet Union to China include: metal cutting machine tools, forging and pressing equipment, automobiles, tractors, ferrous and non-ferrous metals, cables, petroleum and petroleum products, and chemical products. This year, China's supply of many goods to the Soviet Union will increase. The main goods supplied by China to the Soviet Union include: tin, molybdenum ore, tin ore, woolen fabrics, silks, raw silk, wool, carpets, knitwear, tobacco leaves, soybeans, rice, tea, fruits and many other goods.

    Ye Jiuzhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, signed the protocol on behalf of the Chinese government; N.S. Patolichev, head of the Soviet government trade delegation and Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union, signed on behalf of the Soviet government.

    The Chinese side attending the signing ceremony included: Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lei Renmin and Li Qiang, Vice Ministers of Foreign Trade, Liu Mingfu, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission, and relevant responsible personnel who participated in the negotiations; the Soviet side included: S.V. Chervonenko, Soviet Ambassador to China, I.A. Yeremin, Soviet Commercial Representative to China, members of the Soviet government trade delegation, and responsible personnel from the Soviet Embassy and Commercial Representative Office in China.

    Xinhua News Agency, March 29th -- Premier Zhou Enlai received all members of the Soviet government trade delegation headed by Soviet Foreign Trade Minister Patolichev this afternoon.

    Present at the reception were: Ye Jiuzhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lei Renmin and Li Qiang, Vice Ministers of Foreign Trade, Liu Mingfu, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission, and others.

    Soviet Ambassador to China Chervonenko and Soviet Commercial Representative to China Yeremin were also present. (Attached picture)

### 标志着两国贸易经济和友好合作关系进一步发展和巩固  中苏签订1960年货物交换议定书  周恩来总理接见苏联贸易代表团

1960-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    标志着两国贸易经济和友好合作关系进一步发展和巩固
    中苏签订1960年货物交换议定书
    周恩来总理接见苏联贸易代表团
    新华社29日讯　中华人民共和国政府和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府贸易代表团在北京进行的关于1960年的贸易谈判，已经在友好融洽的气氛中圆满达成协议，并于1960年3月29日在北京签订了中苏1960年货物交换议定书。根据上述议定书规定，1960年中苏贸易额比中苏1959年货物交换议定书规定的贸易额增长10％。这个议定书的签订，标志着中苏两国间的贸易经济和友好合作关系得到了进一步的发展和巩固。
    按照1960年中苏货物交换议定书的规定，苏联供应中国的许多种设备和其他货物都有所增长。苏联供应中国的主要货物有：金属切削机床、锻压设备、汽车、拖拉机、黑色及有色金属、电缆、石油及石油产品和化工产品等。今年中国供应苏联的许多货物将有增长。中国供应苏联的主要货物有：锡、钼砂、锡砂、呢绒、绸缎、生丝、羊毛、地毯、针织品、烟叶、大豆、大米、茶叶、水果和其他许多货物。
    代表中国政府在议定书上签字的是中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮；代表苏联政府签字的是苏联政府贸易代表团团长、苏联对外贸易部部长尼·谢·帕托利切夫。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有：中华人民共和国国务院副总理李先念，外交部副部长曾涌泉，对外贸易部副部长雷任民、李强，国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫和参加谈判的有关负责人员；苏联方面有：苏联驻中国大使斯·瓦·契尔沃年科，苏联驻中国商务代表伊·安·叶烈敏和苏联政府贸易代表团团员，以及苏联驻中国大使馆和商务代表处的负责人员。
    新华社29日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了以苏联对外贸易部部长帕托利切夫为首的苏联政府贸易代表团全体成员。
    接见时在座的有：对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长曾涌泉，对外贸易部副部长雷任民、李强，国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫等。
    苏联驻华大使契尔沃年科和苏联驻我国商务代表叶烈敏也在座。（附图片）
