International Briefs

    U Nu Meets with Indian President and Prime Minister
    New Delhi dispatch: U Nu, Chairman of the Burmese Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League, who is currently visiting India, held talks with Indian President Prasad here on the evening of the 7th. On the same day, U Nu also held talks with Indian Prime Minister Nehru.
    According to the "Indian Express", U Nu informally exchanged views on the international situation and issues of common concern with Nehru during their talks on the 7th.

### 国际简讯

1960-03-11
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    吴努同印度总统和总理会谈
    新德里讯　正在印度访问的缅甸廉洁的反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努7日晚上在这里同印度总统普拉沙德举行了会谈。同一天，吴努还同印度总理尼赫鲁举行了会谈。
    据“印度快报”报道，吴努在7日同尼赫鲁会谈时，就国际局势和共同关心的问题非正式地交换了意见。
