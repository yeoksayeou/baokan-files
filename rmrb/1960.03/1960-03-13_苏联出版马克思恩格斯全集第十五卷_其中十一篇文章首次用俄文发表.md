Title: Soviet Union Publishes Collected Works of Marx and Engels, Volume 15, Including Eleven Articles Published in Russian for the First Time
Author:
Date: 1960-03-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th: TASS reports: Volume 15 of the Collected Works of Marx and Engels, edited by the Institute of Marxism-Leninism of the Central Committee of the CPSU, has been published by the Soviet State Publishing House for Political Literature.

This volume includes articles written by Marx and Engels from January 1860 to September 1864, and works published in the "New York Daily Tribune," the Austrian "Die Presse," and other publications. These articles mainly discuss the national liberation struggles in Europe and the Americas, as well as the tasks of the labor and democratic movements under the new revolutionary upsurge. A series of articles also relate to the domestic and foreign situation in Russia.

Articles about the American Civil War of 1861-1865 occupy a prominent place in this volume. The colonial policies of capitalist countries are exposed in "British Policy," "Armed Intervention in Mexico," and other articles. Engels' writings on military issues occupy a considerable place in this volume.

The newly published Volume 15 includes fifteen articles that were not included in the first edition of the Collected Works, of which eleven are published in Russian for the first time.

### 苏联出版马克思恩格斯全集第十五卷  其中十一篇文章首次用俄文发表

1960-03-13
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联出版马克思恩格斯全集第十五卷
    其中十一篇文章首次用俄文发表
    新华社莫斯科12日电　据塔斯社报道：苏共中央马克思—列宁主义研究院编辑的马克思、恩格斯全集第十五卷已由苏联国家政治书籍出版局出版。
    这一卷内收入了马克思和恩格斯从1860年1月到1864年9月所写的文章和在“纽约每日论坛报”、奥地利的“新闻报”和其它刊物上刊载过的著作。这些文章主要论述了欧洲和美洲的民族解放斗争以及在新的革命高涨条件下工人运动和民主运动的任务。还有一系列文章涉及到俄国的国内外形势。
    关于美国在1861—1865年的国内战争的文章在这一卷内占了显著的地位。在“英国的政策”“在墨西哥的武装干涉”和其它的文章中揭露了资本主义国家的殖民政策。恩格斯写的关于军事问题的著作在这一卷内占了相当大的地位。
    新出版的第十五卷收入了第一次出版文集时没编入的十五篇文章，其中有十一篇还是首次用俄文发表的。
