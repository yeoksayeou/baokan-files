Title: Xiao Xiangrong Meets Major General Rana
Author:
Date: 1960-03-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 20th. Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense, met this afternoon with Nepalese Major General Chetra Bikram Rana, who is accompanying Prime Minister Koirala on his visit to China, in a friendly atmosphere.

Present at the meeting were Major General Gu Jingsheng, Director of the Mass Work Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Senior Colonel Zhang Bingyu, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense. During the meeting, the hosts briefed the distinguished Nepalese guests on the active participation of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in national economic construction and the troops' own engagement in productive labor to increase social wealth and improve the troops' lives.

After the meeting, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong and others had dinner with Major General Rana.

### 萧向荣会见拉纳少将

1960-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    萧向荣会见拉纳少将
    新华社20日讯　国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将今天下午和随同柯伊拉腊首相来我国访问的尼泊尔少将切特拉·比克拉姆·拉纳进行了友好的会见。
    会见时在座的，有中国人民解放军总政治部群众工作部部长谷景生少将，国防部外事处副处长张秉玉上校。会见时，主人向尼泊尔贵宾介绍了中国人民解放军积极参加国家经济建设和部队本身从事生产劳动，以增加社会财富和改善部队生活的情况。
    会见后，萧向荣中将等和拉纳少将共进晚餐。
