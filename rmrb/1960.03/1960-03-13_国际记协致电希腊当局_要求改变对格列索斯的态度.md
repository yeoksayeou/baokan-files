Title: International Organization of Journalists Calls on Greek Authorities to Change Attitude Towards Glezos

Author:
Date: 1960-03-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 11th: The Secretariat of the International Organization of Journalists sent a telegram on March 11th to the President of the Greek Supreme Court, Greek Prime Minister Karamanlis, and Minister of Justice Kalias, requesting that the Greek authorities change their attitude towards Glezos and other Greek patriots when the Greek Supreme Court re-examines the case of Glezos and other Greek patriots on March 12th. The telegram pointed out that Glezos and other Greek patriots are innocent.

### 国际记协致电希腊当局  要求改变对格列索斯的态度

1960-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    国际记协致电希腊当局
    要求改变对格列索斯的态度
    新华社布拉格11日电　国际新闻工作者协会秘书处11日打电报给希腊最高法院院长、希腊首相卡拉曼利斯和司法大臣卡利亚斯，要求希腊当局在希腊最高法院于3月12日重新审理格列索斯和其他希腊爱国者的案件时改变对这些爱国者的态度。电报指出，格列索斯和其他希腊爱国者是无罪的。
