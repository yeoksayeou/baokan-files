Title: "Spartak" Football Team Leaves Hangzhou for Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-03-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, March 10th. The Soviet Moscow "Spartak" football team held a friendly match with the Zhejiang football team in Hangzhou this afternoon, with the visiting team winning 5-0. After the match, Ren Yili, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province, met with the team leader, coach, and all the athletes of the visiting team.

The Soviet Moscow "Spartak" football team arrived in Hangzhou from Shanghai on the 9th, and left Hangzhou for Guangzhou on the evening of the 10th. They are scheduled to hold a friendly match with the Guangzhou football team on the 13th.

### “斯巴达克”足球队离杭州去广州

1960-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    “斯巴达克”足球队离杭州去广州
    据新华社杭州10日电　苏联莫斯科“斯巴达克”足球队今天下午在杭州与浙江足球队举行友谊比赛，客队以五比零获胜。球赛后，浙江省副省长任一力接见了客队领队、教练和全体运动员。
    苏联莫斯科“斯巴达克”足球队是9日从上海来杭州的，10日晚他们已经离开杭州去广州，预定13日同广州足球队举行友谊比赛。
