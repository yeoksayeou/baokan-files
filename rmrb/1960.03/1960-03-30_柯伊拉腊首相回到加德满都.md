Title: Prime Minister Koirala Returns to Kathmandu
Author:
Date: 1960-03-30
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 29th - According to news reports here: Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala returned to Kathmandu by plane from Calcutta on the 28th.
    He was warmly welcomed at the airport by senior Nepalese officials and the masses.

### 柯伊拉腊首相回到加德满都

1960-03-30
第5版()
专栏：

    柯伊拉腊首相回到加德满都
    新华社新德里29日电　据这里报纸消息：尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊在28日从加尔各答乘飞机回到加德满都。
    他在机场上受到尼泊尔高级官员和群众的热烈欢迎。
