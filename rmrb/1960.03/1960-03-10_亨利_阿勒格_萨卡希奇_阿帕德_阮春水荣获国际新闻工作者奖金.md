Title: Henri Alleg, Szakasits Árpád, Nguyễn Xuân Thủy Awarded International Journalism Prize
Author:
Date: 1960-03-10
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 8th. The International Organization of Journalists has decided to award the 1959 International Journalism Prize to journalist Henri Alleg (Algeria), Szakasits Árpád (Hungary) and Nguyễn Xuân Thủy (Democratic Republic of Vietnam). This was announced by the Secretary-General of the International Organization of Journalists, Meisner, at a press conference held here tonight.

The International Organization of Journalists expresses its firm belief that awarding Henri Alleg the prize, like awarding Glezos last year, will encourage all journalists to strengthen the movement to immediately release this fighter who is fighting for the liberation of Algeria.

Szakasits Árpád, President of the Hungarian National Association of Journalists, is a journalist with fifty years of history and an outstanding political commentator.

Nguyễn Xuân Thủy is the President of the Central Organ newspaper of the Vietnam Fatherland Front and the President of the Vietnam Journalists Association, and is a resolute fighter against colonial rule.

### 亨利·阿勒格　萨卡希奇·阿帕德　阮春水荣获国际新闻工作者奖金

1960-03-10
第6版()
专栏：

    亨利·阿勒格　萨卡希奇·阿帕德　阮春水
    荣获国际新闻工作者奖金
    新华社布拉格8日电　国际新闻工作者协会决定将1959年国际新闻工作者奖金授与记者亨利·阿勒格（阿尔及利亚）、萨卡希奇·阿帕德（匈牙利）和阮春水（越南民主共和国）。这是国际新闻工作者协会秘书长麦斯纳今天晚间在这里举行的记者招待会上宣布的。
    国际新闻工作者协会表示深信，授与亨利·阿勒格以奖金，像去年授与格列索斯以奖金一样，将鼓励所有新闻工作者加强争取立即释放为阿尔及利亚的解放而斗争的这位战士的运动。
    匈牙利全国记者协会主席萨卡希奇·阿帕德，是有五十年历史的新闻工作者，又是杰出的政论家。
    阮春水是越南祖国战线中央机关报的社长、越南新闻工作者协会主席，是反对殖民主义统治的坚决战士。
