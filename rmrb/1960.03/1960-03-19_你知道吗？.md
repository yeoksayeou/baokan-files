    Did you know?
    General garbage contains 0.25% nitrogen, 0.62% phosphorus, and 0.6% potassium. Sludge contains 0.24% nitrogen, 0.46% phosphorus, and 1.22% potassium. After composting them, they can become very good fertilizers.

### 你知道吗？

1960-03-19
第8版()
专栏：

    你知道吗？
    一般垃圾中含有0．25％的氮，0．62％的磷和0．6％的钾。污泥中含有0．24％的氮，0．46％的磷和1．22％的钾。把它们堆沤以后，能成为很好的肥料。
