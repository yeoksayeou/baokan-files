Title: Panamanian People Hold Another Anti-American Demonstration; Demonstrators Angrily Attack U.S. Information Service; U.S. Authorities Actually Send Out Military Police to Suppress; Venezuelan Foreign Minister and Colombian Public Opinion Support Panama's Just Struggle
Author:
Date: 1960-03-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch from Panama City: The Panamanian people yesterday turned their Carnival celebrations into demonstrations against the United States and for the restoration of sovereignty over the Canal Zone.

From March 1st to 4th, it is Panama's annual four-day Carnival. On the 1st, Panamanian marchers passed through the U.S.-occupied Canal Zone and hung Panamanian flags along the border of the Canal Zone and along 4th of July Avenue, where the marching procession passed through the Canal Zone.

At the border of the Canal Zone, signs appeared reading "Yankees Go Home!"

According to the Panama National Guard Headquarters, the masses attacked the U.S. Information Service office in Panama City with stones.

On the eve of the carnival, thousands of small white cards with the words "Yankees Go Home!" were distributed in Panama City.

The U.S. authorities in the Canal Zone dispatched a large number of military police to suppress the demonstrations. After the anti-American demonstrations, twelve Panamanian patriots were arrested.

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch from New York according to TASS, 1st dispatch: The "Panama-American Newspaper" (edited by former Panamanian President Ricardo Arias), published in Panama City, recently published an editorial severely criticizing the U.S. attitude towards Panama's legitimate demand to fly the Panamanian flag in the Canal Zone.

The editorial said that under pressure from the Pentagon, the U.S. government has extremely rudely rejected Panama's proposal to fly its flag in the Canal Zone. "Some people have even made blatantly provocative statements, claiming that the entire sovereignty of the Canal Zone belongs to the United States. Organizational pressure has been intensified, leading to the passage of the so-called Selden Resolution by the U.S. House of Representatives, which is the most unreasonable resolution passed through legal procedures in the relations between Panama and the United States."

The editorial emphasized that the resolution stipulates that a discriminatory procedure be used to "interpret" the treaty with Panama, and it does not even hide this: the principles of international law do not apply to relations between the two countries. This resolution contains the perfidy of colonialism.

Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Caracas: Venezuelan Foreign Minister Ignacio Luis Arcaya said recently at a press conference that Panama enjoys sovereignty in the Canal Zone and has every right to fly the Panamanian flag there.

He said: The Panama Canal issue is not only Panama's problem, but the problem of all Latin American people. Referring to the arrogant attitude adopted by the United States towards Panama, he said that the recent remarks by U.S. officials on the Panama issue "reveal traces of militaristic thinking."

Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Bogota: The Colombian weekly "El Espectador" published an editorial on February 26, supporting the demands of Panamanian public opinion for the restoration of sovereignty over the Canal Zone and severely condemning colonialism.

The editorial said, we believe that the best way is to nationalize the Canal Zone. The Canal Zone is part of Panamanian territory and should be under the jurisdiction of that country. Recently, there has been a welcome awakening of nationalism in Panama, and this awakening spirit has won the sympathy of independent public opinion in Latin America.

The editorial said that the incomplete state of nations like Panama is a constant factor in creating instability and suffering. Therefore, eliminating the colonial and semi-colonial conditions that exist in some parts of this hemisphere is a major condition for achieving the desire for peace and unity.

### 巴拿马人民又举行反美大示威  示威群众怒袭美国新闻处  美国当局竟出动军警镇压  委内瑞拉外长和哥伦比亚舆论支持巴拿马的正义斗争

1960-03-03
第6版()
专栏：

    巴拿马人民又举行反美大示威
    示威群众怒袭美国新闻处  美国当局竟出动军警镇压
    委内瑞拉外长和哥伦比亚舆论支持巴拿马的正义斗争
    新华社2日讯　巴拿马城消息：巴拿马人民昨天把狂欢节的庆祝活动变成了反对美国和争取恢复运河区主权的示威游行。
    从3月1日到4日，是巴拿马一年一度为期四天的狂欢节。巴拿马人的游行队伍在1日穿过了美国霸占的运河区，并且在运河区边境以及运河区内游行队伍经过的7月4日大街一带悬挂了巴拿马的国旗。
    在运河区边境，出现了“美国佬滚回去！”的标语牌。
    据巴拿马国民警卫队总部宣布，群众用石块袭击了巴拿马城的美国新闻处办事处。
    在联欢节前夕，在巴拿马城散发了几千张写着“美国佬滚回去！”的白色小卡片。
    运河区美国当局出动了大批军警进行镇压。在反美示威发生以后，有十二名巴拿马爱国者遭到逮捕。
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社纽约1日讯：在巴拿马城出版的“巴拿马—美国人报”
  （前巴拿马总统理卡多·阿里亚斯主编）最近发表社论，严厉地抨击美国对巴拿马关于让巴拿马国旗飘扬在运河区的合法要求所抱的态度。
    社论说，美国政府在五角大楼的压力下，极其蛮横地拒绝了巴拿马提出的在运河区悬挂国旗的建议。“某些人士竟发表带有明显挑衅性的声明，扬言运河地区的整个主权是属于美国的。并且加强组织上的压力，以致美国众议院通过了所谓塞耳丁决议，这是在巴拿马同美国的关系中用法律手续通过的最不合理的决议”。
    社论着重指出，决议规定用歧视的程序来“解释”同巴拿马的条约，它甚至毫不掩饰这一点：国际法的原则不适用于两国之间的关系。在这个决议中包含着殖民主义的奸计。
    新华社讯　加拉加斯消息：委内瑞拉外交部长伊格纳西奥·路易斯·阿卡亚最近在记者招待会上说，巴拿马在运河区享有主权，并且完全有权在运河区悬挂巴拿马国旗。
    他说：巴拿马运河问题不仅仅是巴拿马的问题，而且是所有拉丁美洲人民的问题。他在谈到美国对巴拿马所采取的蛮横态度时说，美国官方人士最近关于巴拿马问题的言论“露出军国主义思想的苗头”。
    据新华社波哥大电　哥伦比亚“公报”周刊于2月26日发表社论，支持巴拿马公众舆论关于恢复运河区主权的要求，严厉谴责殖民主义。
    社论说，我们认为，最好的方式是把运河区收归国有。运河区是巴拿马领土的一部分，应该受这个国家管辖。最近以来，在巴拿马出现了值得庆幸的民族主义的觉醒，这种觉醒精神获得了拉丁美洲独立舆论的同情。
    社论说，像巴拿马这样国家不完整的状况是产生不稳和痛苦的经常因素。因此，消除本半球某些地区存在的殖民地和半殖民地状况，是实现和平和团结愿望的主要条件。
