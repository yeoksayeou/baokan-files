Title: Female Postwoman
Author: Xue Xue
Date: 1960-03-08
Page: 8

    Female Postwoman
    She brings the Party's instructions to the blast furnace,
    And delivers the first good news to the telegraph station.
    She delivers a letter from home reporting victory,
    And imprints a smiling face on her heart.
    She pours out her sweat among happy people,
    Like a diligent white dove, never knowing fatigue.
    The cold wind and scorching sun add youthful rosiness to her face,
    And the sounds of gongs and drums urge her wheels to spin faster.
   (Hebei Tianjin, Xue Xue) (Attached picture)

### 女投递员
薛雪
1960-03-08
第8版()
专栏：

    女投递员
    她把党的嘱咐捎到高炉边，
    把第一个喜讯送到发报站。
    她递过一封报捷的家书，
    心上便印下一张笑脸。
    她在幸福的人儿间抛洒热汗，
    像辛勤的白鸽从不知困倦。
    寒风烈日在她脸上增添青春的红润，
    阵阵锣鼓又催促她车轮飞转。
   （河北天津　薛雪）（附图片）
