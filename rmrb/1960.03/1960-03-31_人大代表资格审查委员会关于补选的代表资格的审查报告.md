Title: Report of the Credentials Committee of the National People's Congress on the Credentials of Newly Elected Deputies
Author:
Date: 1960-03-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 30th. Report of the Credentials Committee of the Second National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on the Credentials of Newly Elected Deputies

Between the first and second sessions, eleven deputies to the Second National People's Congress passed away: Zhang Yuanji, Li Shunzhang, Leng Yu, Li Jishen, Yu Yanong, Zhang Jinyi, Xu Baoju, Wei Lihuang, Jiang Yong, Chen Zhimin, and Tang Hongcheng. The credentials of one deputy, Huo Weide, were revoked. Together with the four who passed away before the first session, sixteen deputies should be newly elected. Four deputies were newly elected between the first and second sessions. Gansu Province newly elected Wang Bingxiang, Liaoning Province newly elected Feng Guan sui, Jiangsu Province newly elected Zhang Jingli, and Zhejiang Province newly elected Wang Jiwu. The Credentials Committee of the Second National People's Congress reviewed the credentials of the above four deputies based on relevant materials. The review results confirm that their credentials as deputies are valid. Now we submit the report to the Congress for deliberation.

### 人大代表资格审查委员会关于补选的代表资格的审查报告

1960-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

    人大代表资格审查委员会关于补选的代表资格的审查报告
    新华社30日讯　中华人民共和国第二届全国人民代表大会代表资格审查委员会关于补选的代表资格的审查报告
    第二届全国人民代表大会代表在第一次会议到第二次会议期间逝世的有张元济、李顺章、冷遹、李济深、余亚农、章靳以、许宝驹、卫立煌、江庸、陈职民、唐洪澄十一人，撤销代表资格的有霍维德一人，连同在第一次会议以前逝世的四人，应该补选代表十六人。在第一次会议到第二次会议期间补选了四人。甘肃省补选了王秉祥，辽宁省补选了凤冠绥，江苏省补选了张敬礼，浙江省补选了王季午。第二届全国人民代表大会代表资格审查委员会根据有关材料，就上述四位代表的代表资格进行了审查。审查结果，确认他们的代表资格有效。现在向大会提出报告，请予审议。
