Title: Burmese Prime Minister and Nepalese Prime Minister Issue Joint Communiqué. The Two Countries Decide to Further Strengthen Friendly Cooperation. Both Sides Agree That the Two Countries Should Establish Diplomatic Relations at the Ambassadorial Level.
Author:
Date: 1960-03-11
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, March 9th: The Burmese government announced this afternoon a joint communiqué from the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, Ne Win, and the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Koirala.

    The communiqué said that during the Nepalese Prime Minister's stay in the Union of Burma, he held discussions with the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma. These discussions, conducted in an extremely friendly atmosphere, allowed them to freely and frankly exchange views on the current international situation and issues directly concerning the interests of both countries.

    The communiqué said that they expressed their desire to continue to work wholeheartedly for the cause of peace and the promotion of good neighborly relations and cooperation among all countries.

    The communiqué also said that they hoped that the spirit of negotiation and reconciliation would prevail, and welcomed efforts to ease international tensions and resolve outstanding international issues through peaceful means. They hoped that these efforts would be successful and would ultimately achieve a just and lasting peace throughout the world.

    The communiqué announced that in order to promote the further development of friendly relations and cooperation between the two countries, they agreed that the two countries should establish diplomatic relations at the ambassadorial level.

    The communiqué said that the Nepalese Prime Minister had invited the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma to visit Nepal at his convenience. The Prime Minister of the Union of Burma has accepted this invitation.

    The Nepalese Prime Minister, Koirala, left Rangoon on the 9th for China after completing his three-day visit to Burma.

### 缅甸总理和尼泊尔首相发表联合公报  两国决定进一步加强友好合作  双方同意两国应建立大使级的外交关系

1960-03-11
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸总理和尼泊尔首相发表联合公报
    两国决定进一步加强友好合作
    双方同意两国应建立大使级的外交关系
    新华社仰光9日电　缅甸政府今天下午发表了缅甸联邦总理奈温和尼泊尔王国首相柯伊拉腊的一个联合公报。
    公报说，尼泊尔首相在缅甸联邦逗留期间，曾同缅甸联邦总理进行了讨论。这些在极为友好的气氛中进行的讨论，使得他们能够就目前的国际局势以及有关两国直接利益的问题，自由和坦率地交换意见。
    公报说，他们表示了他们将为和平事业和增进各国之间的睦邻关系和合作而继续专心一致地工作的愿望。
    公报又说，他们希望谈判与和解的精神将占优势，并且欢迎为缓和国际紧张局势和通过和平方式解决悬而未决的国际问题的努力。他们希望这些努力将获得成功并且最后将在全世界实现公正的和持久的和平。
    公报宣布，为了促进两国之间的友好关系和合作的进一步发展，他们同意两国应建立大使级的外交关系。
    公报说，尼泊尔首相已经邀请缅甸联邦总理在他方便的时候访问尼泊尔。缅甸联邦总理已经接受了这个邀请。
    尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊结束了在缅甸的三天访问后，已经在9日离开仰光前往中国。
