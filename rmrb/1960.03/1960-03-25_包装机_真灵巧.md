Title: Packaging Machine - Truly Ingenious
Author: Jin Di, Zi Dan
Date: 1960-03-25
Page: 2

    Packaging Machine - Truly Ingenious
    Shanghai No. 2 Cigarette Factory, Jin Di, Zi Dan
    Packaging workers, lofty aspirations,
    Manual packaging becomes mechanized,
    Technological innovation offers brilliant plans,
    Riding a fine steed instead of a slow ox.
    Packaging machine, truly ingenious,
    Saves labor, effort, and materials,
    Cigarettes packed quickly and well,
    Even Lu Ban would bow in admiration.

### 包装机  真灵巧
锦娣　子丹
1960-03-25
第2版()
专栏：

    包装机  真灵巧
    上海卷烟二厂　锦娣　子丹
    包装工，壮志气，
    手工包装机械化，
    技术革新献妙计，
    跨下老牛骑骏马。
    包装机，真灵巧，
    省人省力又省料，
    香烟包得快又好，
    鲁班见了忙拜倒。
