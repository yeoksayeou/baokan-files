Title: Hungarian Party and Government Delegation Leaves Poland for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-03-22
Page: 6

(Comprehensive report by this newspaper) According to Xinhua News Agency: The delegation of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and government, headed by János Kádár, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, left Warsaw by special train on the evening of the 20th to return home.

Those who saw them off at the station included Polish party and government leaders Władysław Gomułka, Józef Cyrankiewicz, as well as government officials and senior military officers. Gomułka and Kádár spoke at the station.

Before the Hungarian Party and Government delegation set off, a joint communiqué of the Polish and Hungarian Party and Government delegations' talks and the Polish-Hungarian 1961-1965 trade agreement were signed. The trade agreement stipulates that the trade volume between the two countries will increase by 40% compared with the previous long-term trade agreement.

On the morning of the 21st, the Hungarian Party and Government delegation returned to Budapest.

Those who welcomed them at the station included Hungarian party and government leaders Dögei, Münnich, Marosán, Kiss, ministers of various ministries, representatives of various organizations, and thousands of residents of the capital Budapest.

Kádár spoke at the welcoming rally held at the station. He said that this visit to Poland is of great significance for further strengthening the relations and solidarity between Hungary and Poland. The traditional friendship between the people of Hungary and Poland will continue to develop and strengthen on the basis of Marxism-Leninism and under the banner of proletarian internationalism. Kádár said that we will always be closely united with socialist countries such as the Soviet Union and China.

Speaking of the international situation, Kádár pointed out that as long as the strength of the socialist camp and the growing forces of peace in the world are united, they can overcome the instigators of war.

### 匈党政代表团离波回国

1960-03-22
第6版()
专栏：

    匈党政代表团离波回国
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：以匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会第一书记卡达尔为首的匈牙利党政代表团，20日晚乘专车离华沙回国。
    到车站送行的有波兰党和政府领导人哥穆尔卡、西伦凯维兹等人以及政府官员、高级军官等。哥穆尔卡和卡达尔在车站上讲了话。
    在匈牙利党政代表团启程前，签订了波兰和匈牙利党政代表团会谈联合公报和波兰—匈牙利1961—1965年贸易协定。贸易协定规定，两国贸易额将比上一次长期贸易协定增加40％。
    21日上午，匈牙利党政代表团回到布达佩斯。
    到车站欢迎的有：匈牙利党政领导人道比、明尼赫、马罗山、基什等及各部部长、各团体代表和首都布达佩斯的数千居民。
    卡达尔在车站举行的欢迎大会上讲了话。他说，这次去波兰的访问对进一步加强匈波两国的关系和团结有很大意义。匈波两国人民的传统友谊将在马克思列宁主义的基础上和在无产阶级国际主义的旗帜下不断地得到发展和加强。卡达尔说，我们将永远同苏联和中国等社会主义国家紧密地团结在一起。
    在谈到国际形势时卡达尔指出，只要社会主义阵营的力量和世界上日益增长的和平力量团结一致，就能够战胜战争挑拨者。
