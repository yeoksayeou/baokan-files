Title: China Red Cross Society Sends Letter to Brazilian Red Cross Society Expressing Condolences and Donating Funds to Aid Brazilian Disaster Victims
Author:
Date: 1960-03-22
Page: 6

    China Red Cross Society Sends Letter to Brazilian Red Cross Society
    Expressing Condolences and Donating Funds to Aid Brazilian Disaster Victims
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Red Cross Society of China recently wrote to the Brazilian Red Cross Society, expressing condolences to the victims of the drought in Sao Vicente, Brazil, and donating 3,000 yuan in Renminbi to aid the city's children and adolescents.
    The Sao Vicente agricultural area near Santos City on the southeastern coast of Brazil has suffered continuous drought for the past three years, causing hardship for the people, especially affecting the lives of children and adolescents, leading to malnutrition, and physical weakness. The Mayor of Sao Vicente has appealed for aid for their children and adolescents.

### 我国红十字会致函巴西红十字会  慰问和捐款救济巴西灾民

1960-03-22
第6版()
专栏：

    我国红十字会致函巴西红十字会
    慰问和捐款救济巴西灾民
    新华社讯　中国红十字会最近写信给巴西红十字会，慰问巴西索·维散市遭到旱灾的灾民，并且捐款人民币三千元救济这个市的儿童和青少年。
    巴西东南海岸圣多斯市附近的索·维散农业区，最近三年来连续遭到旱灾，人民生活困苦，对儿童和青少年的生活影响尤大，以致他们营养不良，体弱多病。索·维散市长曾呼吁救济他们的儿童和青少年。
