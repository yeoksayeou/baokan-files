Title: The Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party Issues Directives to Commemorate the 90th Anniversary of Lenin's Birth, the 70th Birthday of President Ho Chi Minh, and the 15th Anniversary of the Founding of the Republic

Author:

Date: 1960-03-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 20th: Hanoi newspapers today published directives from the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party on celebrating the 90th anniversary of Lenin's birth (April 22), the 70th birthday of President Ho Chi Minh (May 19), and the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam (September 2).

Regarding the commemoration of the 90th anniversary of Lenin's birth, the directives stated that the purpose of the commemoration "is to enable everyone to more clearly recognize that Marxism-Leninism is the greatest revolutionary thought and revolutionary theory in human history, an invincible doctrine. With Marxism-Leninism as the guiding ideology, communist parties in all countries have achieved great victories today, and the final victory in building socialism and communism throughout the world is also imminent."

Regarding the celebration of President Ho Chi Minh's 70th birthday, the directives stated that President Ho Chi Minh is the founder and leader of the Labor Party; the great teacher of the Vietnamese revolution and the Vietnamese people; and an outstanding fighter in the international communist movement, the workers' movement, and the world revolutionary movement. Celebrating President Ho Chi Minh's 70th birthday will inspire our cadres, party members, and all the people to learn from President Ho Chi Minh's revolutionary qualities and style, thereby enhancing Party spirit and revolutionary will, and resolutely struggling for the communist ideal and the interests of the motherland for the rest of our lives.

Regarding the celebration of the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the directives stated that the fifteen years since the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam have been a period of great victories, demonstrating the growth, strengthening, and consolidation of a people's democratic state under the leadership of the Party, and demonstrating the superiority of the socialist system in North Vietnam. The purpose of celebrating this year's National Day is to more widely promote production, education, and political mobilization, enhance the people's sense of responsibility to the country and as masters of their own affairs, promote the patriotic production emulation movement, and vigorously promote the cause of socialist construction in the North and the struggle for national reunification.

### 越南劳动党中央发布指示  纪念列宁诞生九十周年、胡志明主席七十寿辰和共和国建国十五周年

1960-03-22
第6版()
专栏：

    越南劳动党中央发布指示
    纪念列宁诞生九十周年、胡志明主席七十寿辰和共和国建国十五周年
    新华社河内20日电　河内各报今天刊登了越南劳动党中央书记处就庆祝列宁诞生九十周年（4月22日）、胡志明主席七十寿辰（5月19日）和越南民主共和国建国十五周年（9月2日）发出的指示。
    关于纪念列宁诞生九十周年，指示说，纪念的目的“是为了使大家更加清楚地认识到，马克思列宁主义是人类历史上最伟大的革命思想和革命理论，是战无不胜的学说，以马克思列宁主义为思想指导，世界各国共产党今天已经取得了伟大的胜利，并且在全世界建设社会主义和共产主义的世界也即将取得最后的胜利”。
    关于庆祝胡志明主席七十寿辰，指示说，胡志明主席是劳动党的创始者和领导者；是越南革命、越南人民的伟大导师，是国际共产主义运动、工人运动和世界革命运动的杰出的战士。庆祝胡志明主席七十寿辰将鼓舞我国干部、党员和全体人民学习胡志明主席的革命品质和作风，从而提高党性和革命意志，终身为共产主义理想、为祖国的利益而坚决奋斗。
    关于庆祝越南民主共和国建国十五周年，指示说，越南民主共和国建国的十五年，是一个巨大的胜利，它证明了有党领导的人民民主国家的成长、壮大和巩固，证明了越南北方社会主义制度的优越性。庆祝今年国庆节的目的是更广泛地展开生产教育和政治动员，提高人民对国家和作为主人翁的责任感等，促进爱国生产竞赛运动，大力促进北方社会主义建设和实现国家统一斗争的事业。
