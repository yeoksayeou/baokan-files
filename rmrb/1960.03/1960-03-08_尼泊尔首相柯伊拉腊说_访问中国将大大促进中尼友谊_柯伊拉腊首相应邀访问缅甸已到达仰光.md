Title: Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala Says Visit to China Will Greatly Promote Sino-Nepalese Friendship, Prime Minister Koirala Arrives in Rangoon on Invited Visit to Burma
Author:
Date: 1960-03-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 7th. According to a Press Trust of India dispatch from Kathmandu: Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala, before his departure yesterday to visit China, issued a letter to the people of the nation regarding the visit.

In the letter to the nation, Koirala said that he firmly believes his visit will greatly help promote friendship between China and Nepal.

He also said that in today's international situation, it is necessary for neighboring countries to have contact and maintain friendly relations.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, March 7th. Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala and his entourage, en route to China, arrived here by plane this afternoon for a three-day visit at the invitation of the Burmese government. Burmese Prime Minister Ne Win and government ministers, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Burma, welcomed him at the airport.

Koirala is scheduled to leave Rangoon for Beijing on the 9th.

### 尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊说  访问中国将大大促进中尼友谊  柯伊拉腊首相应邀访问缅甸已到达仰光

1960-03-08
第6版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊说
    访问中国将大大促进中尼友谊
    柯伊拉腊首相应邀访问缅甸已到达仰光
    新华社新德里7日电　据印度报业托辣斯加德满都消息：尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊在他昨天启程前往中国访问之前，曾经就这次访问发表了告全国人民书。
    柯伊拉腊在告全国人民书中说，他完全相信他的访问将大大有助于促进中国和尼泊尔之间的友谊。
    他还说，在今天的国际局势中邻国之间进行接触和保持友好关系是必要的。
    新华社仰光7日电　尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊和随行人员在前往中国途中，应缅甸政府的邀请，今天下午乘飞机到达这里作为期三天的访问。缅甸总理奈温和政府部长们以及各国驻缅甸的外交使节到机场欢迎。
    柯伊拉腊预定在9日离开仰光前往北京。
