Title: Every Drop Returns to the Field, Every Inch of Soil Must Be Watered
Author:
Date: 1960-03-14
Page: 3

1.  Members of the Coal Gully Brigade of the Hengshui Commune in Mengjin County, Henan Province, divert water over high mountains to irrigate fertile fields.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Tang Maolin photo

2.  People in Qingyuan County, Guangdong Province, build dikes to lock the North River, aiming to make the river water flow backwards to irrigate the fields, ensuring a bumper harvest of dry rice.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Huang Chihe photo

3.  Members of the Baihe Commune in Tang County, Hebei Province, built a winding mountain canal, enabling the terraced fields on the mountain to be irrigated.
    Xinhua News Agency reporters Yuan Hao and Zhang Fan photo
