Title: Relief for Earthquake Victims in Agadir, Morocco: Our Ambassador Presents Donation to Moroccan King; Moroccan Prime Minister Cables Thanks to Premier Zhou for Condolences to Earthquake Victims

Author:

Date: 1960-03-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, 19th:  Chinese Ambassador to Morocco, Bai Ren, presented a check for 500,000 RMB to King Mohammed V of Morocco on the 19th, as a donation from the Chinese government for the relief of earthquake victims in Agadir.

　　Ambassador Bai Ren, on behalf of the Chinese government and people, expressed deep sympathy and concern to the earthquake victims.

　　The King of Morocco expressed his gratitude to the Chinese government and people for their assistance to the victims of Agadir, and asked Ambassador Bai Ren to convey this gratitude to the Chinese government and people.

　　On March 4th, the Red Cross Society of China donated 50,000 RMB through the Moroccan Red Crescent Society to help alleviate the suffering of the victims.

　　Premier Zhou Enlai, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Liu Ningyi, and President of the Red Cross Society of China Li De all sent telegrams of condolences to the earthquake victims.

　　The city of Agadir in southwestern Morocco was struck by a violent earthquake in the early morning of the 1st, with approximately 80% of the city destroyed and an estimated death toll of over 10,000.

Xinhua News Agency, 20th:  Moroccan Prime Minister Abdallah Ibrahim sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai on March 7th, expressing his gratitude for Premier Zhou's condolences regarding the earthquake in Agadir, Morocco. The telegram stated, "Following the unfortunate events in Agadir, I extend my heartfelt gratitude for your telegram of condolences. The Moroccan government, the victims of Agadir, and the Moroccan people express their thanks for your friendly gesture."

### 救济摩洛哥阿加迪城地震灾民  我国大使把捐款交给摩国王  摩首相电谢周总理慰问地震灾民

1960-03-22
第6版()
专栏：

    救济摩洛哥阿加迪城地震灾民
    我国大使把捐款交给摩国王
    摩首相电谢周总理慰问地震灾民
    新华社拉巴特19日电　中国驻摩洛哥大使白认19日把一张人民币五十万元的支票交给摩洛哥国王穆罕默德五世，作为中国政府救济阿加迪城地震灾民的捐款。
    白认大使代表中国政府和人民，向地震受害者表示深切的同情和关怀。
    摩洛哥国王对中国政府和中国人民对阿加迪灾民的援助表示感谢，并且要求白认大使向中国政府和人民转达这种谢意。
    中国红十字总会在3月4日曾经捐款人民币五万元，通过摩洛哥红新月会帮助灾民减轻痛苦。
    周恩来总理、中华全国总工会主席刘宁一和中国红十字会总会会长李德全都致电慰问地震灾民。
    摩洛哥西南部阿加迪城在1日清晨发生猛烈地震，全城约80％被毁，估计死者达万人以上。
    新华社20日讯　摩洛哥首相阿卜杜拉·易卜拉欣3月7日打电报给周恩来总理，向周总理对摩洛哥阿加迪市遭受地震的慰问表示感谢。电文说，“在阿加迪发生不幸事件之后，承蒙来电慰问，我谨表示由衷的感谢。摩洛哥政府、阿加迪遇难者和摩洛哥人民对你的友好表示致以谢意。”
