Title: National Committee of the CPPCC Standing Committee Eighth Meeting Decides: CPPCC Third Session Second Meeting to Open Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1960-03-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 27th—The Standing Committee of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Consultative Conference held its eighth meeting on the morning of the 27th. The meeting was presided over by Zhou Enlai, Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC.
    
    The meeting decided that the Second Session of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference would open in Beijing on March 29, and discussed and approved the agenda (draft) for the session. The meeting also approved the work report of the Standing Committee to the Second Session of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; approved the list of Standing Committee members assisting the Chairman and Vice-Chairmen in taking turns presiding over the meetings of the Second Session of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; and approved the list (draft) of the Proposal Examination Commission for the Second Session of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

### 全国政协常委会第八次会议决定  政协三届二次会议明日开幕

1960-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    全国政协常委会第八次会议决定
    政协三届二次会议明日开幕
    新华社27日讯　中国人民政治协商会议第三届全国委员会常务委员会27日上午举行第八次会议。会议由政协全国委员会主席周恩来主持。
    会议决定中国人民政治协商会议第三届全国委员会第二次会议于3月29日在北京开幕，并且讨论通过了这次会议的议程（草案）。会议还通过了常务委员会向中国人民政治协商会议第三届全国委员会第二次会议的工作报告；通过了中国人民政治协商会议第三届全国委员会第二次会议协助主席、副主席轮流主持会议的常务委员名单；通过了中国人民政治协商会议第三届全国委员会第二次会议提案审查委员会名单（草案）。
