Title: Mrs. Helou Calls Nasser Demanding the Release of Helou
Author:
Date: 1960-03-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, March 22nd: According to a report in "Al-Safaa" on the 20th, Mrs. Virginie Helou, wife of Lebanese Communist Party leader Farajallah Helou, recently sent a telegram to UAR President Nasser demanding the release of her husband.

In the telegram, Mrs. Helou also requested that Nasser allow the lawyers and doctors she has entrusted to defend and treat her husband. Farajallah Helou was arrested by UAR authorities on the streets of Damascus in June 1959.

### 赫卢夫人致电纳赛尔要求释放赫卢

1960-03-25
第6版()
专栏：

    赫卢夫人致电纳赛尔要求释放赫卢
    新华社贝鲁特22日电　据“呼声报”20日报道，黎巴嫩共产党领袖法拉贾拉·赫卢的夫人维尔吉妮·赫卢最近打电报给阿联总统纳赛尔，要求释放她的丈夫。
    赫卢夫人在电报中还要求纳赛尔允许她所委托的律师和医生为她的丈夫进行辩护和治疗。法拉贾拉·赫卢是在1959年6月间在大马士革街上被阿联当局逮捕的。
