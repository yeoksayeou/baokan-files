Title: East Pakistan Governor Hussain Visits the Chinese Exhibition Hall in Dhaka Again, Expressing Great Interest in China's Water Storage Projects
Author:
Date: 1960-03-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Dhaka, March 13th: Zakir Hussain, the Governor of East Pakistan, visited the Chinese Exhibition Hall at the East Pakistan International Industrial Exposition in Dhaka for the second time on the 13th.

Hussain first visited the Chinese Pavilion on February 9th when he presided over the opening ceremony of the Chinese Exhibition Hall.

During his visit to the Chinese Pavilion on the 13th, Hussain showed great interest in the model of the irrigation system in northern Anhui and models of other Chinese water storage projects. He observed these models very carefully and stated that he would send Pakistani engineers to study them.

During his conversation with Gao Shangneng, the head of the Chinese exhibition delegation, Hussain expressed his hope to develop trade relations between Pakistan and China.

On the same day, Pakistani Interior Minister Sheikh and East Pakistan Martial Law Administrator Umrao Khan also visited the Chinese Exhibition Hall. They spoke highly of the exhibits in the Chinese Pavilion.

### 东巴基斯坦省长侯赛因  再次参观达卡的中国展览馆  对中国的蓄水工程表现了极大兴趣

1960-03-15
第5版()
专栏：

    东巴基斯坦省长侯赛因
    再次参观达卡的中国展览馆
    对中国的蓄水工程表现了极大兴趣
    新华社达卡13日电　东巴基斯坦省长扎基尔·侯赛因在13日第二次参观了在达卡举行的东巴基斯坦国际工业博览会中的中国展览馆。
    侯赛因曾经在2月9日主持中国展览馆开幕典礼时第一次参观了中国馆。
    侯赛因13日在中国馆参观时，对安徽北部灌溉系统的模型和中国其他蓄水工程的模型表现了极大的兴趣。他非常仔细地观察了这些模型，并且表示将派遣巴基斯坦的工程师来研究这些模型。
    侯赛因在同中国展览代表团团长高尚能谈话时，表示希望发展巴基斯坦和中国的贸易关系。
    同一天，巴基斯坦内政部长谢赫和东巴基斯坦军事戒严令执行长官乌姆劳汗也参观了中国展览馆。他们对中国馆的展览品评价很高。
