International Briefs

    New Cabinet Ministers of Ceylon Sworn In
    Colombo Dispatch: Seven ministers of the Ceylon United National Party cabinet were sworn in on the morning of the 23rd. Prime Minister Senanayake was sworn in on the 21st.
    The eight members of the new Ceylon cabinet, including the Prime Minister, are all members of the United National Party, six of whom are members of the House of Representatives and two are senators.
    In the new cabinet, Prime Minister Senanayake also serves as Minister of Defense and Foreign Affairs.

    UAR and the United States Sign Loan Agreement
    Cairo Dispatch: The United Arab Republic and the United States signed three agreements on the 26th, under which the United States will grant the UAR loans totaling 16.5 million Egyptian pounds.

### 国际简讯

1960-03-28
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    锡兰新内阁部长宣誓就职
    科伦坡讯　锡兰统一国民党内阁的七位部长23日上午宣誓就职。内阁总理森纳那亚克已经在21日宣誓就职。
    包括总理在内的锡兰新内阁中的八位成员都是统一国民党人，其中六位是众议员，两位是参议员。
    在新内阁中，森纳那亚克总理兼任国防和外交部长。
    阿联和美国签订贷款协定
    开罗讯　阿拉伯联合共和国和美国26日签订了三项协定，美国根据这些协定将给予阿联共为一千六百五十万埃镑的贷款。
