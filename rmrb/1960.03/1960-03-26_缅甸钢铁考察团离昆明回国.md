Title: Burmese Steel Delegation Leaves Kunming for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-03-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, March 24th: The Burmese Steel Delegation, led by U Kyin Maung Gyi, Director of the Burmese State Steel Rolling Mill Management Committee, concluded its visit to Kunming and left Kunming for home on the morning of the 23rd. Liu Yue, Deputy Director of the Yunnan Provincial Metallurgical Industry Department, and relevant personnel from the Foreign Affairs Office of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee went to see them off. U Maung Maung Soe, Consul General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, was also present to see them off.

During their stay in Kunming, the delegation visited the Kunming Iron and Steel Company and the Kunming No. 2 Steel Plant. They also toured scenic spots in Kunming. On the evening of the 21st, Yue Xiaoxia, Director of the Yunnan Provincial Metallurgical Industry Department, hosted a banquet for all members of the delegation.

### 缅甸钢铁考察团离昆明回国

1960-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸钢铁考察团离昆明回国
    新华社昆明24日电　由缅甸国营轧钢厂管理委员会主任吴庆貌季率领的缅甸钢铁考察团，结束了在昆明的参观访问，已经在23日上午离开昆明回国。云南省冶金工业厅副厅长刘岳和云南省人民委员会外事处的有关人员前往送行。前往送行的还有缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭。
    考察团在昆明期间，参观访问了昆明钢铁公司和昆明第二钢铁厂，还游览了昆明的名胜风景区。21日晚，云南省冶金工业厅厅长岳肖峡曾设宴招待考察团的全体人员。
