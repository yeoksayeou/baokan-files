Title: A Flag in a Czechoslovakian Factory: "Czechoslovak-Chinese Friendship Factory" Awarded the Red Banner of Excellence Four Times
Author:
Date: 1960-03-27
Page: 4

    Friendship Window
    
    A Flag in a Czechoslovakian Factory
    "Czechoslovak-Chinese Friendship Factory" Awarded the Red Banner of Excellence Four Times
    The Czechoslovak "Czechoslovak-Chinese Friendship Factory" has won the Red Banner of Excellence in the socialist competition launched by the national heavy machinery industry department due to its outstanding production performance in the fourth quarter of last year.
    This factory won the Red Banner of Excellence for four consecutive quarters last year. It is known as an excellent heavy machinery factory in Czechoslovakia.
    At the flag awarding ceremony held on March 4, representatives of the Czechoslovak Heavy Machinery Industry Department awarded the fourth-quarter Red Banner of Excellence to all the factory's employees. Cao Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, and other personnel from the embassy were invited to attend the flag awarding ceremony. Gao Shirong, the Commercial Counselor of the embassy, spoke at the ceremony.
    The "Czechoslovak-Chinese Friendship Factory" comprehensively fulfilled all production targets in the fourth quarter of last year. Last year, it fulfilled 101.4% of the total output value plan, 102.9% of the production plan, 107% of the export order plan, and 102.5% of the labor productivity target.
    Since the beginning of this year, all the employees of this factory have victoriously completed the production tasks for January and February. Now, they are welcoming the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia with even greater production enthusiasm.

### 捷克斯洛伐克工厂中的一面旗帜  “捷中友谊工厂”四次荣获优胜红旗

1960-03-27
第4版()
专栏：友谊之窗

    捷克斯洛伐克工厂中的一面旗帜
    “捷中友谊工厂”四次荣获优胜红旗
    捷克斯洛伐克“捷中友谊工厂”由于去年第四季度优异的生产成绩，获得了全国重型机械工业部门展开的社会主义竞赛优胜红旗。
    这个工厂去年接连四个季度荣获优胜红旗。它被誉为捷克斯洛伐克优秀的重型机械工厂。
    在3月4日举行的授旗仪式上，捷克斯洛伐克重型机械工业部的代表将第四季度的优胜红旗授予工厂的全体职工。中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛和大使馆其他人员应邀参加了授旗仪式。大使馆商务参赞高仕融在仪式上讲了话。
    “捷中友谊工厂”在去年第四季度全面完成了各项生产指标。它去年全年完成了总产值计划的101．4％、生产计划的102．9％、出口订货计划的107％和劳动生产率指标的102．5％。
    今年年初以来，这个工厂的全体职工胜利地完成了1月份和2月份的生产任务。现在，他们正以更大的生产热情来迎接捷克斯洛伐克解放十五周年。
