Title: Qinghai Builds Antibiotic Factory
Author:
Date: 1960-03-12
Page: 2

The newly built Qinghai Province Antibiotic Manufacturing Plant has recently been officially put into production. It is currently the largest pharmaceutical factory in Qinghai Province, and its main products include refined oxytetracycline, berberine, and solvents.

### 青海建成抗菌素厂

1960-03-12
第2版()
专栏：

    青海建成抗菌素厂
    新建的青海省抗菌素制造厂在最近正式投入生产。它是青海省目前最大的一个制药厂，主要产品有精制土霉素、黄连素、溶剂等。
