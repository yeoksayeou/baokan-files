Title: Xu Guangda, Head of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Delegation, Reports to the Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association on His Visit to the Soviet Union
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 26th - The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association delegation, which recently returned from the Soviet Union, reported this afternoon to the Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association on its visit to the Soviet Union and participation in activities celebrating the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The council meeting held today was presided over by Vice President Li Zhuchen.

Xu Guangda, head of the delegation and member of the CPC Central Committee, said in his report that the delegation had cordial and friendly meetings with people from all walks of life in the Soviet Union for more than two weeks, expressing the Chinese people's love and friendship for the Soviet people. Wherever the delegation went, it was warmly and friendly received by the Soviet people. Xu Guangda said that through this visit, the friendship and unity between the people of China and the Soviet Union has been further enhanced.

In his report, Xu Guangda warmly praised the great achievements made by the Soviet people in communist construction. He also expressed his gratitude to the Soviet people for the friendship they expressed to the Chinese people through the delegation.

### 中苏友协代表团团长许光达  向中苏友协总会理事会报告访苏情况

1960-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

    中苏友协代表团团长许光达
    向中苏友协总会理事会报告访苏情况
    新华社26日讯　最近从苏联回国的中苏友协代表团，今天下午向中苏友协总会理事会报告了到苏联访问和参加庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年活动的情况。今天举行的理事会议由副会长李烛尘主持。
    代表团团长、中共中央委员许光达在报告中说，代表团在苏联两个多星期，和苏联各方面的人士进行了亲切友好的会见，表达了中国人民对苏联人民的热爱和友谊。代表团所到之处，受到苏联人民亲切友好的接待。许光达说，通过这次访问，进一步增进了中苏两国人民的友谊和团结。
    许光达在报告中，热烈赞扬苏联人民在共产主义建设中所取得的巨大成就。他并对苏联人民通过代表团向中国人民表达的友谊表示感谢。
