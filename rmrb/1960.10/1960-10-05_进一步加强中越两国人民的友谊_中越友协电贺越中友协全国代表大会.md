Title: Further Strengthen the Friendship Between the Chinese and Vietnamese Peoples: China-Vietnam Friendship Association Sends Congratulatory Message to the National Congress of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1960-10-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The China-Vietnam Friendship Association today sent a telegram to the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, warmly congratulating the Vietnam-China Friendship Association on convening its Second National Congress on October 5th.

The China-Vietnam Friendship Association’s congratulatory telegram stated: “For the past ten years since its establishment, under the leadership of the Vietnamese Labor Party, the Vietnam-China Friendship Association has made unremitting efforts and tremendous contributions to enhancing the fraternal friendship and unity between the peoples of Vietnam and China.”

The telegram said: "The friendly unity between the Chinese and Vietnamese peoples not only promotes the socialist construction of our two countries, but also further strengthens the strength of the socialist camp, which is conducive to the struggle against imperialist aggression and the defense of peace in Asia and the world. The Chinese people cherish this fraternal friendship between our two peoples very much, and our China-Vietnam Friendship Association will continue to work hard to enhance this friendship.”

The telegram stated: "The convening of the Second National Congress of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association will undoubtedly help to further develop the work of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association. We are convinced that through this Congress, the Vietnam-China Friendship Association will surely make new and greater contributions to the noble cause of further strengthening the friendship and unity between the peoples of China and Vietnam.”

### 进一步加强中越两国人民的友谊  中越友协电贺越中友协全国代表大会

1960-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    进一步加强中越两国人民的友谊
    中越友协电贺越中友协全国代表大会
    据新华社4日讯　中越友好协会今天打电报给越中友好协会，热烈祝贺越中友协将在10月5日召开第二次全国代表大会。
    中越友好协会的贺电说：“越中友好协会成立十年来，在越南劳动党的领导下，为增进越中两国人民之间兄弟般的友谊和团结进行了不懈的努力，作出了巨大的贡献。”
    电报说：“中越两国人民的友好团结不仅促进了我们两国的社会主义建设事业，而且进一步增强了社会主义阵营的力量，有利于反对帝国主义侵略和保卫亚洲和世界和平的斗争。中国人民十分珍视我们两国人民之间的这种兄弟友谊，我们中越友好协会将不断地为增进这种友谊而努力工作。”
    电报说：“越中友好协会第二次全国代表大会的召开，无疑地有助于越中友好协会工作的进一步开展。我们确信，通过这次代表大会，越中友好协会在进一步加强中越两国人民之间的友谊与团结的崇高事业中，定将作出新的更大的贡献。”
