Title: Chairman Liu Receives Moroccan Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th - Chairman Liu Shaoqi received all members of the Moroccan Kingdom Cultural Delegation, headed by Mohammed Aziz Lahbabi, this afternoon.

Present at the reception were Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Qu Wu, Vice-Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and Vice-President of the Chinese-African Peoples' Friendship Association; He Ying, Director of the West Asian and African Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others. ### 刘主席接见摩洛哥文化代表团

1960-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见摩洛哥文化代表团
    新华社20日讯　刘少奇主席今天下午接见了以穆罕默德·阿齐兹·拉赫巴比为首的摩洛哥王国文化代表团全体成员。
    接见时在座的有对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若，对外文委副主任、中国非洲人民友好协会副会长屈武，外交部西亚非洲司司长何英等。（附图片）
