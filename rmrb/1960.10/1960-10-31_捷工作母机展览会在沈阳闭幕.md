Title: Czechoslovakian Machine Tool Exhibition Closes in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1960-10-31
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 28th. The Czechoslovakian Machine Tool Exhibition held in Shenyang closed on the afternoon of the 28th, after receiving the last group of visitors from all over Liaoning.

During the eighteen days of the exhibition, which officially opened on October 8th, a total of more than 35,000 people visited.

### 捷工作母机展览会在沈阳闭幕

1960-10-31
第2版()
专栏：

    捷工作母机展览会在沈阳闭幕
    据新华社沈阳28日电　在沈阳举行的捷克斯洛伐克工作母机展览会，28日下午在接待了从辽宁各地赶来的最后一批观众以后闭幕。
    展览会从10月8日开幕以来正式展出的十八天中，共有三万五千多人前往参观。
