Title: Chinese Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe Performs for the First Time in Berlin
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 9th. The Chinese Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe gave its premiere performance at the Stalin Allee Sports Hall in Berlin on the evening of the 8th.

    The acrobatic troupe's various performances were warmly appreciated and welcomed by more than 1,800 spectators from both East and West Berlin. Today, Berlin newspapers published news and pictures of the acrobatic troupe's premiere performance.

    The Chinese Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe is visiting and performing in accordance with the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 中国重庆杂技团在柏林首次演出

1960-10-10
第6版()
专栏：

    中国重庆杂技团在柏林首次演出
    新华社柏林9日电　中国重庆杂技团8日晚上在柏林斯大林大街体育馆首次演出。
    杂技团的各种表演受到了一千八百多位东西柏林的观众的热烈赞赏和欢迎。今天柏林的报纸发表了杂技团首次上演的消息和图片。
    中国重庆杂技团是根据中德文化合作协定前来作访问演出的。
