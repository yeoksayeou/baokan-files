Title: Chinese Delegation Arrives in Tirana to Attend Sino-Albanian Science and Technology Cooperation Conference
Author:
Date: 1960-10-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, October 1st. The Chinese delegation, which is here to attend the Fifth Conference on Sino-Albanian Scientific and Technological Cooperation, arrived in Tirana on September 30th. The delegation of six was led by Liu Ruilong, Vice Minister of Agriculture.

Greeting them at the airport was Reka, Deputy Minister of Agriculture of Albania, and others. Luo Shigao, Chinese Ambassador to Albania, also greeted them at the airport.

### 参加中阿科技合作会议  中国代表团到达地拉那

1960-10-06
第5版()
专栏：

    参加中阿科技合作会议
    中国代表团到达地拉那
    新华社地拉那1日电　前来参加中国阿尔巴尼亚科学技术合作第五次会议的中国代表团，9月30日到达地拉那。代表团一行六人，由中国农业部副部长刘瑞龙率领。
    到机场欢迎的有阿尔巴尼亚农业部副部长雷卡等。我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高也到机场欢迎。
