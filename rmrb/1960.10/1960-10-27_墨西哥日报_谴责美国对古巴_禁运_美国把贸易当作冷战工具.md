Title: "Mexican Daily" Condemns US "Embargo" Against Cuba; US Uses Trade as a Tool of the Cold War
Author:
Date: 1960-10-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Mexico City, 22nd -- According to TASS, the "Mexican Daily" recently published an editorial condemning the United States for implementing a so-called "embargo" against Cuba, calling it an act of economic aggression.

The newspaper said, "The Yankees state bluntly that this embargo stems from the principle that trade between the United States and other countries must primarily serve the purposes of American foreign policy. This is astonishing." The newspaper pointed out, "In other words, today, trade is a tool of the Cold War."

### “墨西哥日报”谴责美国对古巴“禁运”  美国把贸易当作冷战工具

1960-10-27
第6版()
专栏：

    “墨西哥日报”谴责美国对古巴“禁运”
    美国把贸易当作冷战工具
    据新华社22日讯　据塔斯社墨西哥城讯：“墨西哥日报”最近发表社论，谴责美国对古巴实行所谓“禁运”，是进行经济侵略。
    报纸说，“美国佬直截了当地说，这一禁运是出自这样一个原则，即：美国同其他国家的贸易必须主要是为美国外交政策的目的服务，这是令人吃惊的。”报纸指出，“换句话说，在今天，贸易是冷战的工具。”
