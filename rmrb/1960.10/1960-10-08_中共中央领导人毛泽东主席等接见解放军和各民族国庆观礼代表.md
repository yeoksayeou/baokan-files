Title: CCP Central Leaders Chairman Mao Zedong and Others Receive PLA and Nationalities' National Day Observance Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th October: CCP Central Committee Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice-Chairmen Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, and Lin Biao this afternoon received the People's Liberation Army National Day Observance Delegation.

    Also present at the reception were Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Luo Ruiqing, Director of the General Political Department Tan Zheng, Deputy Director Xiao Hua, and senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Liu Yalou, Zhang Aiping, Li Juikui, and Chen Shiju.
    
Xinhua News Agency, 7th October: CCP Central Committee Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice-Chairmen Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, and Lin Biao, CCP Central Committee Secretariat Secretary Tan Zheng, and State Council Vice-Premier Luo Ruiqing and other comrades this afternoon received the nationalities' visiting delegations attending the National Day celebrations. These visiting delegations are: the Yunnan Province Nationalities' Visiting Delegation headed by Duan Ming, the Tibet Visiting Delegation headed by Zhang Pingfan, the Tibet Youth and Women's Visiting Delegation headed by Gui Zhi, the Ningxia Visiting Delegation headed by Wang Futian, and the Xinjiang Visiting Delegation headed by Tuohuti Abudula. Also present at the reception were the heads of the United Front Work Department of the CCP Central Committee, the Nationalities Committee of The National Peoples Congress, and the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China. ### 中共中央领导人毛泽东主席等接见解放军和各民族国庆观礼代表

1960-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人毛泽东主席等接见解放军和各民族国庆观礼代表
    新华社7日讯　中共中央主席毛泽东，副主席刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、陈云、林彪今天下午接见了中国人民解放军国庆观礼代表。
    接见时，中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿，总政治部主任谭政、副主任萧华，中国人民解放军高级将领刘亚楼、张爱萍、李聚奎、陈士榘等也在场。
    新华社7日讯　中共中央主席毛泽东，副主席刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、陈云、林彪，中共中央书记处书记谭政和国务院副总理罗瑞卿等同志，今天下午接见了参加国庆观礼的各民族参观团。这些参观团是：以段明为首的云南省各民族参观团，以张平凡为首的西藏参观团，以桂芝为首的西藏青年、妇女参观团，以王福田为首的宁夏参观团，以托乎提阿不都拉为首的新疆参观团。接见时，中共中央统一战线工作部、全国人民代表大会民族委员会、中华人民共和国民族事务委员会的负责人也在场。（附图片）
