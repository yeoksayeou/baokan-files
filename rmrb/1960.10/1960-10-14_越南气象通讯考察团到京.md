Title: Vietnamese Meteorological Communications Study Group Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 13th: The Vietnamese Meteorological Communications Study Group, consisting of three members led by Nguyen Thai, arrived in Beijing by train today to study meteorological communications work in our country in accordance with the Sino-Vietnamese Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement.

Liu Ze, Deputy Director of the Central Meteorological Observatory of the Central Meteorological Bureau, and others were at the station to greet them.

### 越南气象通讯考察团到京

1960-10-14
第4版()
专栏：

    越南气象通讯考察团到京
    新华社13日讯　根据中越科学技术合作协定来我国考察气象通讯工作的越南气象通讯考察团一行三人，在团长阮太率领下，今天乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站迎接的有中央气象局中央气象台副台长刘泽等人。
