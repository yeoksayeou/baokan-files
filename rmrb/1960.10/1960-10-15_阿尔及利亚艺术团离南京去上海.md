Title: Algerian Art Troupe Leaves Nanjing for Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-10-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, October 14th: The Algerian Art Troupe left Nanjing for Shanghai today.

After arriving in Nanjing on the morning of October 10th, the Algerian Art Troupe performed two shows for the people of Nanjing, receiving a warm welcome from over 6,000 spectators. During their stay in Nanjing, the art troupe also held discussions with the Chinese People's Liberation Army Frontline Song and Dance Troupe, Frontline Opera Troupe, and Frontline Drama Troupe, and conducted visits. On the evening of the 13th, Wu Yifang, Vice Governor of Jiangsu Province, hosted a banquet to entertain the guests.

### 阿尔及利亚艺术团离南京去上海

1960-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚艺术团离南京去上海
    新华社南京14日电　阿尔及利亚艺术团今天离开南京去上海。
    阿尔及利亚艺术团在10日上午到达南京后，为南京人民演出了二场，受到了六千多观众的热烈欢迎。艺术团在南京期间，还和中国人民解放军前线歌舞团、前线歌剧团、前线话剧团举行了座谈，并且进行了参观访问。13日晚上，江苏省副省长吴贻芳曾设宴招待客人。
