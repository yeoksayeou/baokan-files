Title: Friends from Over Seventy Countries Gather in Beijing to Participate in the Celebration of the Eleventh Anniversary of the Founding of Our Country
Author:
Date: 1960-10-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Our friends are all over the world. On the eve of our great National Day, nearly two thousand foreign guests gathered in Beijing. They come from more than seventy countries in the five continents of the world, including all walks of life, various skin colors and languages, bringing the precious friendship of the people of various countries to celebrate our great national festival with the people of the capital.

### 参加我国建国十一周年庆祝典礼  七十多个国家的朋友云集北京

1960-10-01
第2版()
专栏：

    参加我国建国十一周年庆祝典礼
    七十多个国家的朋友云集北京
    新华社30日讯　我们的朋友满天下，在我们伟大的国庆十一周年前夕，有近两千位外宾云集北京，他们来自世界五大洲七十多个国家，包括各行各业，各种肤色和语言，带来各国人民珍贵的友谊，同我国首都人民共庆我们伟大的全民节日。
