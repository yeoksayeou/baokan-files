Title: Japanese Painter Holds Exhibition of Paintings from Sketches in China in Tokyo
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 3rd dispatch: The exhibition of paintings from sketches in China by the famous Japanese painter Seison Maeda, held in Tokyo, closed on the 2nd. The exhibition featured one hundred and twenty paintings, mainly depicting Chinese scenery.

### 日画家在东京举行访华写生画展览会

1960-10-08
第5版()
专栏：

    日画家在东京举行访华写生画展览会
    新华社3日讯　东京消息：日本著名画家前田青邨在东京举行的访华写生画展览会在2日闭幕。这个展览会展出了以描写中国风景为主的图画一百二十件。
