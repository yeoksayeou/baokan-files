Title: Zhang Chunqiao Holds Reception to Welcome Cultural Delegations from Eight Latin American Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-10-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 21st: Zhang Chunqiao, President of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and Director of the China-Latin America Friendship Association in Shanghai, held a reception tonight to welcome cultural delegations from eight Latin American countries including Peru, Colombia, Bolivia, Uruguay, Argentina, Panama, Chile, and the Dominican Republic.

Shi Ximin, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, attended the reception.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. People from Shanghai's cultural circles and Latin American friends enthusiastically improvised poems and songs, praising the profound friendship between the Chinese and Latin American peoples and celebrating the victory of the struggle against American imperialism.

Also present at tonight's reception were Zhang Junxiang, vice president of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Yuan Donglin, secretary-general, and Cao Shunqin, deputy secretary-general, among others.

The cultural delegations from the eight Latin American countries arrived in Shanghai by ship from Wuhan on the 18th.

### 张春桥举行酒会  欢迎拉丁美洲八国文化代表团

1960-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

    张春桥举行酒会
    欢迎拉丁美洲八国文化代表团
    新华社上海21日电　中国人民对外文化协会上海市分会会长、中国拉丁美洲友好协会在上海理事张春桥今晚举行酒会，欢迎来自拉丁美洲的秘鲁、哥伦比亚、玻利维亚、乌拉圭、阿根廷、巴拿马、智利、多米尼加等八个国家的文化代表团。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会上海市分会副主席石西民出席了酒会。
    酒会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。上海市文化界人士和拉丁美洲的朋友们热情地即席作诗歌唱，歌颂中国人民和拉丁美洲人民之间的深厚友谊、歌颂反对美帝国主义斗争的胜利。
    出席今晚酒会的，还有中国人民对外文化协会上海市分会副会长张骏祥、秘书长袁冬林、副秘书长曹舜琴等。
    拉丁美洲八个国家文化代表团是在18日由武汉乘船来上海的。
