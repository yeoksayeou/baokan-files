Title: Li Dequan Meets Japanese-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross Society, met this afternoon with the entire delegation of the Japanese-Chinese Friendship Association visiting China, headed by Shinichiro Omori and Kazuo Wada.

Present at the meeting were Zhao Anbo, advisor to the Chinese Red Cross Society, and Deputy Secretaries-General Peng Yan and Ni Feijun.

### 李德全会见日中友协代表团

1960-10-01
第5版()
专栏：

    李德全会见日中友协代表团
    新华社30日讯　中国红十字会总会会长李德全今天下午会见了以大森真一郎、和田一夫为首的日中友好协会访华代表团全体团员。
    会见时在座的有中国红十字会总会顾问赵安博、副秘书长彭炎、倪斐君等。
