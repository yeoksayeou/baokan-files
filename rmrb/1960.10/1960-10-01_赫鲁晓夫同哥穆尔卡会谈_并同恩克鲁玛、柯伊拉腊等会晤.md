Title: Khrushchev Meets with Gomulka, Also Meets with Nkrumah, Koirala, and Others
Author:
Date: 1960-10-01
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 30th: According to TASS, Khrushchev, head of the Soviet delegation attending the 15th session of the United Nations General Assembly, held talks with Gomulka, head of the Polish People's Republic delegation, on September 29th.

They exchanged views on the work of the United Nations General Assembly and other international issues. The talks were conducted in a friendly and sincere atmosphere.

In addition, Khrushchev hosted a luncheon for Ghanaian President Nkrumah that morning.

During the luncheon, Khrushchev and Nkrumah exchanged views on the elimination of the colonial system, the situation in the Congo, disarmament, the United Nations General Assembly session, relations between the Soviet Union and Ghana, and other issues of mutual interest.

The luncheon was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: According to TASS, Khrushchev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and head of the Soviet delegation attending the 15th session of the United Nations General Assembly, met with Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala, head of the Nepalese delegation, on September 27th at the Soviet Mission to the United Nations.

They held friendly talks on issues of mutual concern.

On the same day, Khrushchev also received Mohammed Naim, head of the Afghan delegation, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, and held friendly talks on issues of mutual concern.

Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko was also present at the talks.

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: According to TASS, Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev and Yugoslav President Tito held talks on the morning of September 28th at the Soviet Mission to the United Nations.

In a frank conversation, they discussed international issues, the issue of eliminating colonialism, the situation in the Congo, the work of the 15th session of the United Nations General Assembly, Soviet-Yugoslav relations, and other issues.

The conversation took place in a friendly atmosphere.

### 赫鲁晓夫同哥穆尔卡会谈  并同恩克鲁玛、柯伊拉腊等会晤

1960-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫同哥穆尔卡会谈
    并同恩克鲁玛、柯伊拉腊等会晤
    新华社30日讯　据塔斯社纽约讯：出席联合国大会第十五届会议的苏联代表团团长赫鲁晓夫9月29日同波兰人民共和国代表团团长哥穆尔卡举行了会谈。
    他们就有关联合国大会的工作和其他国际问题交换了意见。会谈是在友好真挚的气氛中进行的。
    另外，赫鲁晓夫在当天上午设午宴招待加纳总统恩克鲁玛。
    赫鲁晓夫和恩克鲁玛在午宴中就消除殖民主义制度、刚果局势、裁军、联大会议、苏联和加纳的关系，以及双方感兴趣的其他问题交换了意见。
    午宴是在热烈友好的气氛中进行的。
    新华社29日讯　据塔斯社纽约讯：出席联合国大会第十五届会议的苏联代表团团长、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫9月27日在苏联驻联合国代表处同尼泊尔代表团团长柯伊拉腊首相举行了会见。
    他们就双方关心的问题进行了友好会谈。
    赫鲁晓夫在同一天还接见了阿富汗代表团团长、副首相兼外交大臣穆罕默德·纳伊姆，并就双方关心的问题进行了友好的会谈。
    会谈时，苏联外交部长葛罗米柯也在场。
    新华社29日讯　据塔斯社纽约讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫和南斯拉夫总统铁托9月28日上午在苏联驻联合国代表驻所举行了会谈。
    他们在坦率的谈话中讨论了国际问题、消灭殖民主义问题、刚果局势问题、联合国大会第十五届会议工作问题、苏联南斯拉夫关系问题和其他问题。
    谈话在友好气氛中进行。
