Title: Premier U Nu Receives Telegram from Burmese President's Secretary Stating President U Win Maung is Satisfied with the Signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty
Author:
Date: 1960-10-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch: According to information provided by the press officer of the Burmese government delegation, Burmese Prime Minister U Nu received a telegram today from the secretary of Burmese President U Win Maung stating that President U Win Maung is satisfied with the signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty in Beijing today.

The telegram stated that President U Win Maung has been closely following the progress of the Sino-Burmese border negotiations and is pleased to learn that the treaty will be signed today.

The telegram conveyed the President's most sincere congratulations to Prime Minister U Nu and General Ne Win for their tireless efforts in ensuring the successful conclusion of the treaty, and to Brigadier Aung Gyi and the members of his committee for their services.

The telegram also stated that the President is making arrangements to warmly welcome Prime Minister U Nu, General Ne Win, Brigadier Aung Gyi, and the members of his committee upon their return to Rangoon.

### 吴努总理收到缅甸总统秘书的来电中说  吴温貌总统对签订中缅边界条约表示满意

1960-10-02
第2版()
专栏：

    吴努总理收到缅甸总统秘书的来电中说
    吴温貌总统对签订中缅边界条约表示满意
    新华社1日讯　据缅甸政府代表团新闻官提供的消息，缅甸联邦吴努总理今天收到了缅甸联邦吴温貌总统的秘书发来的一封电报，电报谈到吴温貌总统对于今天在北京签订中缅边界条约一事表示满意。
    电报说，吴温貌总统一直非常关心地注视着中缅边界谈判的进展，并且满意地获悉条约将在今天签订。
    电报说，总统对吴努总理和奈温将军为使条约得以顺利缔结而作出的不倦的努力，对昂季准将和他的委员会的成员所提供的服务，致以最衷心的祝贺。
    电报还说，总统正在为吴努总理、奈温将军、昂季准将和他的委员会成员返回仰光时热烈欢迎他们作好安排。
