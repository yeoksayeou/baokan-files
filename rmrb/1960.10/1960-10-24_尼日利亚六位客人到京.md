Title: Six Nigerian Guests Arrive in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd:
    A group of six Nigerian guests, headed by Mallam Tanko Yakasai, National Organiser of the Northern Elements Progressive Union of Nigeria, arrived in Beijing this morning. They are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.
    Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Shen Wen, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, and others were at the airport to welcome them.

### 尼日利亚六位客人到京

1960-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    尼日利亚六位客人到京
    新华社23日讯　以尼日利亚北方进步分子联盟全国组织者马兰·坦科·亚卡赛为首的尼日利亚客人一行六人，在今天上午到达北京。他们是应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的。
    到机场欢迎的有中国人民外交学会副秘书长吴晓达、中国非洲人民友好协会副总干事沈文等。
