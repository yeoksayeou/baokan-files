Title: Su CP Central Committee, Kim Il-sung, and Saillant Send Condolences on the Death of Inejiro Asanuma
Author:
Date: 1960-10-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 15th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union sent a telegram to the Japanese Socialist Party, expressing deep condolences on the death of Inejiro Asanuma, Chairman of the Japanese Socialist Party.

The telegram stated that Asanuma was a well-known outstanding social activist and patriot of Japan to the Soviet people. He had waged a resolute struggle against American imperialism, against the Japanese reactionaries, and for a peaceful, independent, neutral, and democratic Japan.

The telegram pointed out that the working people of the Soviet Union, together with the Japanese people, denounced the assassins instigated by the Japanese reactionaries and their foreign agitators.

The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, and the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party also sent telegrams to the Japanese Socialist Party to express their condolences.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 14th: Premier Kim Il-sung sent a telegram to the Central Executive Committee of the Japanese Socialist Party on the 13th, expressing deep condolences on the assassination of Inejiro Asanuma by Japanese fascist thugs.

The telegram stated: The despicable terrorist act against Inejiro Asanuma is a blatant challenge to the Japanese people who are fighting for peace and national independence, and truthfully exposes the nature of American imperialism and its running dogs who stop at no means or methods to achieve their sinister goals.

The telegram stated: We join the Japanese people in condemning the enemy's vicious conspiracy and express our full support for the struggle of the Japanese patriotic forces who have risen up against American imperialism and the Japanese reactionary forces colluding with it.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 15th: Louis Saillant, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Trade Unions, today sent a telegram to the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo), offering condolences on the assassination of Inejiro Asanuma. The telegram also extended sincere condolences to Asanuma's family and friends, as well as to the Japanese workers and people.

The telegram stated that the incident of Asanuma's assassination is closely related to the revival of fascism and militarism, which threatens the basic rights of workers. The World Federation of Trade Unions expresses fraternal solidarity with the Japanese workers who are protesting against fascist terror.

### 苏捷德波罗党中央、金日成、赛扬  分别电唁浅沼稻次郎逝世

1960-10-17
第6版()
专栏：

    苏捷德波罗党中央、金日成、赛扬
    分别电唁浅沼稻次郎逝世
    新华社15日讯　苏联共产党中央委员会致电日本社会党，对日本社会党委员长浅沼稻次郎遇害表示深切的哀悼。
    唁电中说，浅沼是苏联人民熟知的、日本杰出的社会活动家和爱国者，他曾为反对美帝国主义、反对日本反动派、争取爱好和平的、独立的、中立和民主的日本进行了坚决的斗争。
    唁电指出，苏联劳动人民同日本人民一起痛斥日本反动派及其外国鼓动者所指使的刽子手。
    捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会、德国统一社会党中央委员会、波兰统一工人党中央委员会和罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会也分别致电日本社会党表示哀悼。
    新华社平壤14日电　金日成首相13日打电报给日本社会党中央执行委员会，对浅沼稻次郎被日本法西斯暴徒杀害表示深切的哀悼。
    电报说：对浅沼稻次郎的卑鄙的恐怖行为，是对争取和平、民族独立而斗争的日本人民的露骨的挑战，并如实地暴露了美帝国主义及其走卒们为了达到他们的阴险目的而不择手段和方法的本性。
    电报说：我们同日本人民一道谴责敌人的凶恶的阴谋，并表明完全支持为反对美帝国主义和同它勾结的日本反动势力而站起来的日本爱国力量的斗争。
    新华社布拉格15日电　世界工联总书记路易·赛扬今天致电日本工会总评议会，吊唁浅沼稻次郎遇刺逝世。电报并向浅沼的家属和朋友以及日本工人和人民致以诚挚的慰问。
    电报说，浅沼遇害事件，同威胁工人基本权利的法西斯主义和军国主义的复活有紧密的联系。世界工联对于抗议法西斯恐怖的日本工人表示兄弟的声援。
