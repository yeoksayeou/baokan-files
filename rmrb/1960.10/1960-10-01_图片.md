    To celebrate the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, Premier Zhou Enlai held a grand National Day banquet on the evening of September 30, hosting nearly two thousand foreign guests from more than seventy countries across five continents.
    Figure 1: On the rostrum of the National Day reception. From left: Chen Yi, Ne Win, Kellezi, Abbas, Dong Biwu, U Nu, Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Lin Biao
　　　　
　Our reporter Lü Xiangyou
    Figure 2: At the National Day reception, the hosts and guests raised their glasses to toast each other, and the whole place was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere.
