Title: Ruslan and Wife Return Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-10-19
Page: 4

    Ruslan and Wife Return Home
    Xinhua News Agency, 18th - The Secretary-General of the Indonesian Cooperation Parliament, Ruslan, and his wife left Beijing on the evening of the 17th to return home.
    Before leaving, Mr. and Mrs. Ruslan attended a farewell banquet hosted by Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. Zhang Su, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Bao Erhan, President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, were present at the banquet.

### 鲁斯里夫妇回国

1960-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

    鲁斯里夫妇回国
    新华社18日讯　印度尼西亚合作议会秘书长鲁斯里和他的夫人，已经在17日晚离开北京回国。
    鲁斯里夫妇临行前，出席了中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之举行的欢送宴会。出席宴会作陪的有全国人民代表大会常务委员张苏、中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长包尔汉等。
