International Briefs

    Afghan King Visits the UAR
    Cairo dispatch: King Mohammed Zahir of Afghanistan arrived in Cairo on the morning of the 22nd to begin a ten-day official visit to the United Arab Republic.

    Cyclone Kills 3,000 in East Pakistan
    Dacca dispatch: Pakistani officials said on the 16th that East Pakistan was hit by a cyclone from the Bay of Bengal on October 10, killing 3,000 people. Following the cyclone, a tsunami occurred, and 80 percent of the residents of East Pakistan were displaced by the storm. According to reports, local residents said that this tsunami was the most severe in their memory.

    UAR to Establish Fifteen More Diplomatic Missions
    Cairo dispatch: According to a Middle East News Agency report on the 22nd, the United Arab Republic will establish fifteen new diplomatic missions before June next year. The countries to which diplomatic missions will be sent include Mali, Senegal, Madagascar in Africa, and Australia.

    Three North African Countries to Jointly Establish News Agency
    Tunis dispatch: Tunisia, Morocco, and Algeria will jointly establish the North African News Agency in January next year. This news agency will include the Moroccan Maghreb Arab Press Agency and the Tunisian News Agency and Algerian News Agency, both of which are yet to be established.

    New Libyan Government Established
    Tripoli dispatch: The new Libyan cabinet, with Muhammad Ben Othman as Prime Minister, was established on the 17th. The Minister of Foreign Affairs is Abdul Qadir Alam, the Minister of Defense is Ahmed Haseri, and the Minister of Finance is Salam Kadi.

### 国际简讯

1960-10-24
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    阿富汗国王到阿联访问
    开罗讯　阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔二十二日上午到达开罗，开始对阿拉伯联合共和国作为期十天的正式访问。
    东巴基斯坦遭旋风袭击死三千人
    达卡讯　巴基斯坦官员十六日说，十月十日东巴基斯坦受到从孟加拉湾来的旋风袭击，死亡了三千人。在旋风以后，又发生了海啸，东巴基斯坦的百分之八十的居民受到暴风雨的袭击而流离失所。消息说，当地居民说这次海啸是他们记忆中的最严重的一次。
    阿联将增设十五个外交代表机构
    开罗讯　据中东通讯社二十二日报道，阿拉伯联合共和国在明年六月以前将设立十五个新的外交代表机构。将要派驻外交代表机构的国家包括非洲的马里、塞内加尔、马尔加什和澳大利亚等。
    北非三国将联合成立通讯社
    突尼斯讯　突尼斯、摩洛哥和阿尔及利亚三个北非国家将在明年一月联合成立北非通讯社。这个通讯社将包括摩洛哥的马格里布阿拉伯新闻社和尚待成立的突尼斯通讯社和阿尔及利亚通讯社。
    利比亚新政府成立
    的黎波里讯　以穆罕默德·本·奥斯曼为首相的利比亚新内阁已于十七日成立。外交大臣为阿卜杜勒·加德尔·阿拉姆，国防大臣为艾哈迈德·哈塞里，财政大臣为萨拉姆·卡迪。
