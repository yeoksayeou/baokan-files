Title: Kelezi Returns to Tirana After Visiting China
Author:
Date: 1960-10-27
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, October 24th: The Albanian China Friendship Association delegation, led by Kelezi, member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor party, Vice Chairman of The Council Of Ministers and Chairman Of Albanian Chinese Friendship Association, returned to Tirana on the 24th after visiting China.
    Alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Tiodosi, Minister of Trade, Njerla, Minister of Finance, Virli and Vice Chairman of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association, Duma, and others welcomed them at the airport.
    Chinese Ambassador to Albania, Luo Shigao, also welcomed them at the airport.

### 凯莱齐访华后回到地拉那

1960-10-27
第5版()
专栏：

    凯莱齐访华后回到地拉那
    新华社地拉那24日电　由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、部长会议副主席、阿中友好协会主席凯莱齐率领的阿尔巴尼亚中国友好协会代表团，在访问中国后已于24日返抵地拉那。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局候补委员、部长会议副主席蒂奥多西、贸易部长恩杰拉、财政部长维尔利和阿中友协副主席杜马等都到机场欢迎。
    中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高也到机场欢迎。
