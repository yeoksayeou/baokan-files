Title: Hungarian Railway Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 23rd: The Hungarian railway delegation, led by Dr. Chanadi, First Deputy Minister of Transportation and Post and Telecommunications of Hungary, concluded its visit to China and returned home by plane at noon today.

Liu Jianzhang, Vice Minister of Railways, and others were at the airport to see them off.

Hungarian Ambassador to China, Martin, and others were also at the airport to see them off.

The Hungarian railway delegation visited cities such as Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, and Hangzhou, and visited units such as railway stations, the Beijing Railway Institute, the cadre school of the Ministry of Railways, and the China-Hungary Friendship People's Commune.

### 匈牙利铁路代表团回国

1960-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利铁路代表团回国
    新华社23日讯　以匈牙利交通邮电部第一副部长恰纳吉博士为首的匈牙利铁路代表团，结束了在我国的访问，今天中午乘飞机回国。
    到机场送行的有铁道部副部长刘建章等。
    匈牙利驻华大使马尔丁等也到机场送行。
    匈牙利铁路代表团曾访问了北京、天津、上海、杭州等城市，参观了车站、北京铁道学院、铁道部干部学校等单位和中匈友好人民公社。
