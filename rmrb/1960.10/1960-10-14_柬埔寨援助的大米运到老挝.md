Title: Cambodian-Aided Rice Arrives in Laos
Author:
Date: 1960-10-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 12th: According to a broadcast by Vientiane Radio today, the first batch of rice aided by the Cambodian government to Laos arrived in Vientiane this morning.
The radio station expressed its gratitude to the Cambodian government and people.

### 柬埔寨援助的大米运到老挝

1960-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨援助的大米运到老挝
    据新华社河内12日电　据万象电台今天广播：柬埔寨政府援助老挝的第一批大米已在今天上午运到万象。
    电台向柬埔寨政府和人民表示感谢。
