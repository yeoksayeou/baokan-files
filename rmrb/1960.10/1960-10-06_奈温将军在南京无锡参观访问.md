Title: General Ne Win Visits Nanjing and Wuxi
Author:
Date: 1960-10-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 5th. General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces of the Union of Burma, and his wife, arrived in Nanjing at noon today on their way to Wuxi. Accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, they paid their respects at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum.

 管文蔚, Vice Governor of Jiangsu Province, and 陈扬, Mayor of Nanjing, welcomed the distinguished guests at Pukou Railway Station and accompanied them to the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum. General Ne Win laid a wreath at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum and paid respects to the tomb. When discussing Mr. Sun Yat-sen's achievements, he said that Mr. Sun Yat-sen was a person who enlightened the awakening of backward countries.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuxi, 5th. General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces of the Union of Burma, and his wife, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, and their entourage, arrived in Wuxi by special train this afternoon.

惠浴宇, Governor of Jiangsu Province, 彭冲, Alternate Secretary of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Chairman of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Duan Huanjing, Major General of the Jiangsu Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, came specially from Nanjing to Wuxi to welcome the distinguished guests.

Also at the station to welcome the distinguished guests were 何冰皓, First Secretary of the Wuxi Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, 江坚, Mayor of Wuxi, and other party and government leaders, as well as about a thousand people from all walks of life.

Today, the national flags of China and Burma fluttered at the Wuxi Railway Station, and huge banners were hung: "Welcome General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces of the Union of Burma!" At 4:30 pm, as the special train carrying the distinguished guests slowly approached the station, thunderous applause and deafening gongs and drums resounded in the welcoming crowd, expressing greetings to the distinguished Burmese guests who brought the deep friendship of the Burmese "Paukphaw".

After General Ne Win and his wife and their party stepped out of the carriage, 惠浴宇, 彭冲, 段焕竞, 何冰皓, 江坚, and others stepped forward to warmly welcome the distinguished guests and warmly shook hands with each of them. General Ne Win and his wife and their party accepted the flowers presented by the welcoming crowd. They walked out of the station in front of the welcoming crowd, waving bouquets from time to time to return the salute to the enthusiastic crowds welcoming them, and also shook hands with the welcoming crowds. When the distinguished guests were escorted by Governor 惠浴宇 and others to the hotel by car, many people waved to the distinguished guests along the way.

That evening, Governor 惠浴宇 held a banquet at the Taihu Hotel to welcome General Ne Win and his wife. Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife also attended the banquet. An artistic performance was held after the banquet.

### 奈温将军在南京无锡参观访问

1960-10-06
第3版()
专栏：

    奈温将军在南京无锡参观访问
    新华社南京5日电　缅甸联邦国防军总参谋长奈温将军和夫人，今天中午在赴无锡途中到达南京，在陈毅副总理和夫人陪同下晋谒了中山陵。
    江苏省副省长管文蔚、南京市市长陈扬到浦口车站欢迎贵宾，并陪同前往中山陵。奈温将军在中山陵敬献了花圈，并瞻仰了陵寝。他在谈到孙中山先生的功绩时说，孙中山先生是一位启发落后国家觉醒的人。
    新华社无锡5日电　缅甸联邦国防军总参谋长奈温将军和夫人在陈毅副总理和夫人陪同下，以及他们的随行人员今天下午乘专车到达无锡。
    江苏省省长惠浴宇，中共江苏省委候补书记、政协江苏省委员会副主席彭冲和中国人民解放军江苏军区负责人段焕竞少将专程从南京前来无锡欢迎贵宾。
    到车站欢迎贵宾的还有中共无锡市委第一书记何冰皓、无锡市市长江坚等党和政府负责人以及各界群众约千人。
    无锡车站上今天飘扬着中缅两国国旗，悬挂着巨幅标语：“欢迎缅甸联邦国防军总参谋长奈温将军！”下午四点三十分，当贵宾们乘坐的专车徐徐驶近车站时，欢迎行列里响起了春雷般的掌声和震耳的锣鼓声，向带来缅甸“胞波”深情厚谊的缅甸贵宾致意。
    奈温将军和夫人等一行走出车厢后，惠浴宇、彭冲、段焕竞、何冰皓、江坚等走上前去，热烈欢迎贵宾光临，并热情地和贵宾们一一握手。奈温将军和夫人一行接受了欢迎群众的献花，他们经过欢迎群众的前面走出车站，并且不时挥舞花束，向热情欢迎他们的群众答礼，还和欢迎群众握手。贵宾们由惠浴宇省长等陪同乘汽车前往宾馆的时候，沿途有很多群众向贵宾招手。
    当晚，惠浴宇省长在太湖饭店举行宴会，欢迎奈温将军和夫人。陈毅副总理和夫人也出席了宴会。宴会后举行了文艺晚会。
