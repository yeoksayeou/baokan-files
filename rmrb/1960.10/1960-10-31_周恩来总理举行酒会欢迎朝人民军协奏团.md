Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Holds Reception to Welcome Korean People's Army Ensemble

Author:
Date: 1960-10-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th: Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai held a reception this evening to welcome the Korean People's Army Ensemble, led by Major General Jang Se-hwan.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Ambassador Ri Yong-ho proposed toasts at the reception, for the victory of the common struggle of the Chinese and Korean people against imperialism and in defense of world peace, and for the unbreakable militant friendship and unity between the Chinese and Korean people to be passed down from generation to generation.

Among those present were Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi and Xi Zhongxun.

Also present were senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including General Tan Zheng, General Wang Shusheng, and General Xiao Hua, as well as representatives of combat heroes and meritorious personnel from the former Chinese People's Volunteers.

Also present were Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the State Council Zhang Yan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan, Vice Chairmen of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, Zhang Zhixiang, and Qu Wu, Vice Presidents of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association Lao She and Wei Chuantong, Vice Mayor of Beijing Wan Li, and other prominent figures from all walks of life in the capital.

The Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Ri Yong-ho, and embassy staff, as well as Korean peace activist Hwang Bong-gu in Beijing, also attended the reception.

The reception was held after the performance of the Korean People's Army Ensemble at the Great Hall of the People. (See photo)
Premier Zhou, Major General Jang Se-hwan, and Ambassador Ri Yong-ho at the reception.　Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian

### 周恩来总理举行酒会欢迎朝人民军协奏团

1960-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理举行酒会欢迎朝人民军协奏团
    新华社30日讯　国务院总理周恩来今晚举行酒会，欢迎张瑞焕少将率领的朝鲜人民军协奏团。
    周恩来总理和李永镐大使在酒会上祝酒，为中朝两国人民反对帝国主义、保卫世界和平的共同斗争的胜利，为中朝两国人民牢不可破的战斗友谊和团结世世代代相传下去而干杯。
    出席作陪的有国务院副总理陈毅、习仲勋。
    出席作陪的，还有谭政大将、王树声大将、萧华上将等中国人民解放军高级将领和前中国人民志愿军的战斗英雄和功臣代表。
    国务院外事办公室副主任张彦，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，文化部副部长夏衍，对外文化联络委员会副主任楚图南、张致祥、屈武，中朝友好协会副会长老舍、魏传统，北京市副市长万里，以及首都各界知名人士等。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使李永镐和大使馆人员，以及在北京的朝鲜和平人士黄凤九，也出席了酒会。
    酒会于朝鲜人民军协奏团在人民大会堂演出以后举行。（附图片）
    周总理、张瑞焕少将和李永镐大使在酒会上。　新华社记者  杜修贤摄
