Title: After Ending Visit to the UAR, Our Military Delegation Visits Albania
Author:
Date: 1960-10-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, October 24th. At the invitation of the Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of Albania, the Chinese military goodwill delegation led by General Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, arrived in Tirana on the 23rd. On the 24th, Albanian Minister of National Defense, General Baluku, received the delegation.

The Chinese military goodwill delegation is visiting Albania on its way home after visiting the United Arab Republic.

When General Baluku received the delegation, the two sides held cordial talks in a sincere and friendly atmosphere. Present at the reception were Lieutenant General Dume, Lieutenant General Bektashi, Major General Paraku, and Major General Alapi of the Albanian People's Army.

On the day of its arrival in Tirana, the delegation laid a wreath at the Albanian Martyrs' Cemetery to pay its respects.

### 结束在阿联的访问后  我军事代表团访问阿尔巴尼亚

1960-10-27
第5版()
专栏：

    结束在阿联的访问后
    我军事代表团访问阿尔巴尼亚
    新华社地拉那24日电　应阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国国防部的邀请，由中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊上将率领的中国军事友好代表团，23日到达地拉那。24日，阿尔巴尼亚国防部长巴卢库上将接见了代表团。
    中国军事友好代表团是在访问阿拉伯联合共和国结束后回国途中到阿尔巴尼亚进行参观访问的。
    在巴卢库上将接见代表团的时候，双方在真诚和友好的气氛中进行了亲切的交谈。接见时在座的有阿尔巴尼亚人民军杜麦中将、别克特希中将、帕拉库少将、阿拉皮少将等人。
    代表团在到达地拉那的当天，即往阿尔巴尼亚烈士墓前献花圈致敬。
