Title: Moscow Holds Mass Rally to Hear Khrushchev's Report
Author:
Date: 1960-10-22
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 20th: According to TASS, Moscow, October 20th, Moscow's working people held a mass rally at the Luzhniki Stadium on the 20th to welcome Khrushchev, head of the Soviet delegation to the 15th Session of the United Nations General Assembly. More than 12,000 people attended the rally.
    Khrushchev and other leaders of the Communist Party and government of the Soviet Union attended the rally.
    Khrushchev spoke at the rally.
    Participants in the rally passed a resolution expressing their support for the activities of the Soviet delegation and its leader, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, at the 15th Session of the UN General Assembly, and for the proposals made by the Soviet Union at the UN meetings.

### 莫斯科举行群众大会听取赫鲁晓夫报告

1960-10-22
第6版()
专栏：

    莫斯科举行群众大会听取赫鲁晓夫报告
    新华社21日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科20日讯：莫斯科劳动人民20日在卢日尼基运动场举行群众大会，欢迎参加联合国大会第十五届会议的苏联代表团团长赫鲁晓夫。参加大会的有一万二千多人。
    赫鲁晓夫及苏联共产党和政府的其他领导人出席了这次大会。
    赫鲁晓夫在大会上讲了话。
    大会参加者通过一项决议，表示拥护苏联代表团及其领导人、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫在联大第十五届会议上的活动和苏联在联合国会议上提出的建议。
