Title: Brigadier Aung Gyi Leaves Our Country
Author:
Date: 1960-10-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 15th: Brigadier Aung Gyi, Chief Representative of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee and Deputy Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces of the Union of Burma, along with the representatives and advisors of the Burmese delegation, left Changsha today by special train for Guangzhou and exited the country via Shenzhen. Yao Zhongming, Chief Representative of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, and the representatives of the Chinese delegation accompanied them to Guangzhou and saw the distinguished guests off to Shenzhen.

When the special train stopped at Guangzhou Railway Station, Wang Jinchuan, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee, and Ruan Keming, Deputy Director of the Guangdong Provincial Foreign Trade Bureau, and others went to greet and see them off.

### 昂季准将离开我国

1960-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    昂季准将离开我国
    新华社广州15日电　中缅边界联合委员会缅甸首席代表、缅甸联邦国防军副总参谋长昂季准将和缅甸代表团的代表、顾问们，今天由长沙乘专车到广州经深圳出境。中缅边界联合委员会中国首席代表姚仲明和中国代表团的代表们陪同来广州，并陪送贵宾们至深圳。
    专车在广州车站停留时，广东省人民委员会外事处处长王锦川及广东省外贸局副局长阮克明等曾前往迎送。
