Title: Cuban Economic Delegation Departs for Visits to Czechoslovakia, Soviet Union, China, North Korea, and Germany
Author:
Date: 1960-10-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, October 21st: A Cuban government economic delegation headed by the President of the National Bank of Cuba, Guevara, departed from Havana by plane tonight for Europe. The delegation will visit Czechoslovakia, the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and the German Democratic Republic.

President Dorticos, the Minister of Economy, the Minister of Trade, the Minister of Transportation, the Director of the National Agrarian Reform Institute, and the Acting President of the National Bank of Cuba, Jimenez, were at the airport to see them off. Also at the airport to see them off were the Soviet Ambassador, the Czechoslovak Ambassador, the Director of the Xinhua News Agency Cuban Branch, Zeng Tao, and the Chinese Trade Representative, Zou Siyi, as well as other individuals.

### 访问捷苏中朝德五国  古巴经济代表团启程

1960-10-23
第5版()
专栏：

    访问捷苏中朝德五国
    古巴经济代表团启程
    新华社哈瓦那21日电　以古巴国家银行行长格瓦拉为首的古巴政府经济代表团今天晚上从哈瓦那启程乘飞机赴欧洲。代表团将访问捷克斯洛伐克、苏联、中华人民共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和德意志民主共和国。
    到机场欢送的有多尔蒂科斯总统、经济部长、贸易部长和交通部长、土地改革全国委员会主任和古巴国家银行代理行长希门尼斯。到机场去欢送的还有苏联大使、捷克斯洛伐克大使、新华社古巴分社社长曾涛和中国贸易代表邹斯颐，以及其他人士。
