Title: Chinese Ambassadors to Guinea Hold Banquet and Film Reception to Celebrate the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and Mali
Author:
Date: 1960-10-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Bamako, October 28th. Chinese Ambassador to Guinea, Ke Hua, held a banquet and film reception here last night to celebrate the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Mali. Modibo Keita, President of the Republic of Mali, attended the banquet and film reception.

Among the three hundred guests attending the banquet were also Mali's Vice President Koné, Minister of Defense and Security Madeira Keita and other ministers, Sudanese Union Party Political Bureau Political Secretary Diarra and other members of the Political Bureau, members of the National Assembly, senior officials from various government departments, representatives from all walks of life, and foreign diplomatic envoys.

At the banquet, Ambassador Ke Hua proposed a toast to the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Mali, to the further development of friendship between the peoples of China and Mali, to the prosperity and happiness of the Republic of Mali, and to the health of President Modibo Keita. President Modibo Keita proposed a toast to the friendship between the people of Mali and China and to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong.

The banquet took place in a friendly and harmonious atmosphere. Hosts and guests repeatedly congratulated each other on the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Mali.

Twelve hundred guests attended the film reception. Three Chinese color films were shown at the reception: "Changing Mountains and Seas," "Joyfully Celebrating," and "Chinese Tea."

The Chinese films received warm applause from the audience. After the screening, President Keita and other senior officials shook hands with Ambassador Ke Hua, congratulating him on the success of the Chinese film reception. Many guests told the Chinese hosts that they were inspired by the spirit of the Chinese people in transforming mountains and seas.

### 我驻几大使举行宴会和电影招待会  庆祝中国马里建立外交关系

1960-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻几大使举行宴会和电影招待会
    庆祝中国马里建立外交关系
    新华社巴马科28日电　中国驻几内亚大使柯华昨晚在这里举行宴会和电影招待会，庆祝中国马里建立外交关系。马里共和国总统莫迪博·凯塔出席了宴会和电影招待会。
    在出席宴会的三百位来宾中，还有马里副总统科奈、国防和安全部长马德拉·凯塔和其他部长们、苏丹联盟党政治局政治书记迪阿腊和政治局其他委员、国民议会的议员们、政府各部门的高级官员、各界代表以及各国外交使节。
    在宴会上，柯华大使建议为中国和马里建立外交关系、为中国和马里两国人民之间友谊的进一步发展、为马里共和国的繁荣和幸福、为莫迪博·凯塔总统的健康而干杯。莫迪博·凯塔总统建议为马里和中国人民的友谊、为毛泽东主席的健康而干杯。
    宴会在友好融洽的气氛中进行。宾主一再互祝中国和马里外交关系的建立。
    出席电影招待会的有一千二百位来宾。在招待会上放映了三部中国彩色影片：“移山填海”、“欢天喜地”和“中国茶叶”。
    中国电影得到观众的热烈掌声。在放映电影后，凯塔总统和其他高级官员同柯华大使握手，祝贺中国电影招待会的成功。许多来宾对中国主人说，他们受到了中国人民移山填海的精神的鼓舞。
