Title: Lei Renmin Holds Banquet in Rangoon to Entertain Burmese Government Officials and Trade Representatives
Author:
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, October 22nd: Lei Renmin, head of the Trade Delegation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet here tonight to entertain senior Burmese government officials and prominent figures in the Burmese trade community.

    Among those attending the banquet were U Cho Khun Kyi, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities of Burma; U Min Thein, Chief Justice of the Supreme Court; U Thun, Minister of Trade Development and Civil Supplies; Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces; U Hla Gyaw, Parliamentary Secretary of the Ministry of Trade Development and Civil Supplies; U Hla, Deputy Director of the State Agricultural Marketing Board; other senior government officials; and directors and general managers of various companies.

    Diplomatic envoys and trade representatives of various countries stationed in Burma also attended the banquet.

    Lei Yang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, and embassy staff also attended the banquet. The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 雷任民在仰光举行宴会  招待缅甸政府官员和贸易界人士

1960-10-24
第5版()
专栏：

    雷任民在仰光举行宴会
    招待缅甸政府官员和贸易界人士
    新华社仰光22日电　中华人民共和国政府贸易代表团团长雷任民今晚在这里举行宴会，招待缅甸政府高级官员和缅甸贸易界著名人士。
    出席宴会的有缅甸民族院议长藻昆基、最高法院首席法官吴敏登、贸易发展和民用物资供应部长吴敦、缅甸国防军副总参谋长昂季准将、贸易发展和民用物资供应部驻议会秘书吴拉觉、国营农产品贸易局副局长吴拉、其它政府高级官员以及各公司董事和总经理等。
    各国驻缅甸的外交使节和贸易代表也出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬和大使馆人员。宴会在热烈和友好的气氛中进行。
