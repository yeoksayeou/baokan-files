Title: Burmese Cultural Delegation Leaves Beijing for Performances in Shanghai and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 10th: The Burmese Cultural Delegation, led by Colonel So Min, left Beijing by train this morning to visit and perform in cities such as Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Kunming.

Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Vice Directors Chu Tunan and Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan, Vice President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Jian Bozan, as well as figures from the capital's literary and art circles and film actors, were at the station to see them off.

Also at the station to see them off was Daw Khin Kyi, wife of the Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyaw Winn.

At the station, Colonel So Min and the members of the delegation received flowers from literary and art workers from the capital.

The Burmese Cultural Delegation gave four performances in Beijing, receiving a warm welcome from the audience. Through the performances of the Burmese Cultural Delegation and the screening of films during the Burmese Union Film Week, Burmese song and dance and film art left a deep impression on the audience in the capital.

### 缅文化代表团离京赴上海等地演出

1960-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    缅文化代表团离京赴上海等地演出
    新华社10日讯　由苏敏上校率领的缅甸文化代表团今天上午乘火车离开北京，去上海、广州、昆明等城市进行访问演出。
    对外文委主任张奚若、副主任楚图南、张致祥，文化部副部长夏衍，中缅友好协会副会长翦伯赞，以及首都文艺界人士和电影演员等，到车站欢送。
    到车站欢送的，还有缅甸驻华大使叫温和夫人。
    在车站上，苏敏上校和代表团团员们接受了首都文艺工作者的献花。
    缅甸文化代表团在北京共演出四场，受到观众热烈欢迎。通过缅甸文化代表团的演出和缅甸联邦电影周影片的放映，缅甸的歌舞、电影艺术给首都观众留下了深刻的印象。
