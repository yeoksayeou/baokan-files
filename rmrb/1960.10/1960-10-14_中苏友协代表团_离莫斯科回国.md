Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Delegation Leaves Moscow for Home

Author:
Date: 1960-10-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 11th. The delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, invited to the Soviet Union to participate in the celebrations of the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, left Moscow by plane on the evening of the 10th to return home. The delegation visited Minsk and Riga. Klimov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Belorussian Republic, and Belshe, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Latvian Communist Party, received the delegation in Minsk and Riga respectively.

### 中苏友协代表团  离莫斯科回国

1960-10-14
第6版()
专栏：

    中苏友协代表团
    离莫斯科回国
    据新华社莫斯科11日电　应邀前来苏联参加庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年庆祝活动的中苏友好协会代表团，10日晚乘飞机离莫斯科返国。代表团曾到明斯克和里加参观。白俄罗斯共和国部长会议第一副主席克利莫夫和拉脱维亚共产党中央委员会第一书记别尔谢曾先后在明斯克和里加接见了代表团。
