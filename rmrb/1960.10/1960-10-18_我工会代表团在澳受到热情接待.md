Title: Our Trade Union Delegation Receives Warm Reception in Australia
Author:
Date: 1960-10-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sydney, 17th: The Chinese trade union delegation, currently on a friendly visit to Australia, has received a warm reception.

　　The delegation was welcomed by hundreds of union members last week at the massive Tallawarra Power Plant construction site on the south coast of New South Wales. Representatives from Tallawarra warmly supported the friendly visit and expressed their hand of friendship to the Chinese people.

　　On October 12th, a grand banquet was held for the delegation at the Wollongong Workers Club. Representatives from major unions and the Australian Council of Trade Unions attended the banquet. Liu Changsheng, head of the Chinese trade union delegation and Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, spoke at the banquet, warmly praising the friendship of the Tallawarra workers towards the Chinese people.

　　Kenny, Vice-Chairman of the Australian Council of Trade Unions, and Winter, Executive Committee member of the Australian Council of Trade Unions, also spoke at the banquet.

### 我工会代表团在澳受到热情接待

1960-10-18
第5版()
专栏：

    我工会代表团在澳受到热情接待
    据新华社17日讯　悉尼消息：正在澳大利亚进行友好访问的中国工会代表团受到热情接待。
    代表团上星期在新南威尔士南海岸巨大的塔拉瓦勒电力厂工地上受到数以百计的工会会员的欢迎。塔拉瓦勒的代表热烈支持这次友好访问，并表示向中国人民伸出友谊之手。
    10月12日，在渥隆贡工人俱乐部为代表团举行了盛大的宴会。出席这次宴会的有各大工会和澳大利亚工会理事会的代表。中国工会代表团团长、中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜在宴会上讲了话，热烈赞扬塔拉瓦勒工人对中国人民的友谊。
    澳大利亚工会理事会副主席肯尼、澳大利亚工会理事会执行委员温特也在宴会上讲了话。
