Title: Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Delegation Leaves Colombo for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 22nd -- The delegation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, led by Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Association, participated in celebrations of China's National Day held in various parts of Ceylon and concluded its visit to Ceylon. Today, they left here by plane to return home.

### 我对外文协代表团离科伦坡回国

1960-10-24
第5版()
专栏：

    我对外文协代表团离科伦坡回国
    据新华社科伦坡22日电　由中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林率领的中国人民对外文化协会代表团参加了在锡兰各地举办的庆祝中国国庆的活动和结束了对锡兰的访问，今天乘飞机离开这里回国。
