Title: Kim Kwang-hyup Appointed Vice-Premier of the North Korean Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1960-10-14
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 13th: This newspaper reported today that the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a decree on the 12th, appointing Kim Kwang-hyup, the Minister of National Defense of Korea, as Vice-Premier of the Cabinet.

### 金光侠任朝鲜内阁副首相

1960-10-14
第6版()
专栏：

    金光侠任朝鲜内阁副首相
    新华社平壤13日电　此间报纸今天报道，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会12日发布政令，任命朝鲜民族保卫相金光侠为内阁副首相。
