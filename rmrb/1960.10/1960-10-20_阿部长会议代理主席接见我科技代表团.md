Title: Albanian Acting Chairman of the Council of Ministers Receives Chinese Scientific and Technological Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 18th. Koleka, the acting Chairman of the Albanian Council of Ministers, received the Chinese Scientific and Technological Delegation led by Liu Ruilong, Vice Minister of Agriculture, on the 17th. The delegation is in Albania to attend the Fifth Meeting of the Sino-Albanian Scientific and Technological Cooperation.
    Koleka had a cordial and friendly conversation with all members of the delegation.
    Reka, the Albanian Deputy Minister of Agriculture, was present at the meeting.

### 阿部长会议代理主席接见我科技代表团

1960-10-20
第6版()
专栏：

    阿部长会议代理主席接见我科技代表团
    新华社地拉那18日电　阿尔巴尼亚部长会议代理主席科列加17日接见了由农业部副部长刘瑞龙率领的中国科学技术代表团。代表团正在阿尔巴尼亚参加中阿科学技术合作第五次会议。
    科列加和代表团全体人员进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有阿尔巴尼亚农业部副部长雷卡。
