Title: A.R.E Air Force Commander-in-Chief Hosts Chinese Military Delegation; Our Delegation Donates Funds to Palestinian Refugee Camp in Damascus
Author:
Date: 1960-10-17
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 15th -- The Commander-in-Chief of the Air Force of the United Arab Republic, Mohammed Sedki Mahmoud, held a banquet here on the evening of the 15th to entertain all members of the People's Republic of China Military Friendship Delegation, which is visiting and is led by General Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.
    Among those attending the banquet were the Minister of State for Military Affairs of the A.R.E., Lieutenant General Mohammed Ibrahim, Chief of Staff of the A.R.E. Armed Forces, Lieutenant General Ali Amer, and other generals and senior officers of the A.R.E. Air Force and Army.
    Chen Jiakang, the Chinese Ambassador to the A.R.E., and other diplomatic officials from the embassy also attended the banquet.
    At the banquet, General Zhang Zongxun and Commander-in-Chief Sedki Mahmoud spoke in a friendly atmosphere.
    This morning, the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation paid a visit to Sedki Mahmoud and presented the A.R.E. Air Force Headquarters with a military flag embroidered with the military emblem of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force and a model of an airplane. Sedki Mahmoud presented the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation with a flag and the military emblem of the A.R.E. Air Force. In the afternoon, Sedki Mahmoud paid a return visit to the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation.
    The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation visited the Aviation Academy that afternoon. To welcome the Chinese guests, an airplane from the academy performed stunts, and twenty-four airplanes forming the words "Chinese Air Force" flew over the academy. All the students of the academy also gave a sports performance, and at the end, they shouted the slogan "Long Live the People's Republic of China."
    In the past four days, the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation has also visited Cairo University, the Women's Sports Academy, Cairo Stadium, and the famous historical sites in Cairo.
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 15th -- The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation, led by General Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, which is currently visiting the A.R.E., has donated 30,000 Syrian pounds to the Palestinian refugee camp in Damascus. The donation has been sent through the Public Relations Department of the A.R.E. Armed Forces here.
    Members of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation visited the Palestinian refugee camp in Damascus on October 3rd, where they were warmly welcomed.

### 阿联空军总司令宴请我军事代表团  我代表团捐款给大马士革巴勒斯坦难民营

1960-10-17
第5版()
专栏：

    阿联空军总司令宴请我军事代表团
    我代表团捐款给大马士革巴勒斯坦难民营
    新华社开罗15日电　阿拉伯联合共和国空军总司令穆罕默德·西德基·马哈茂德15日晚上在这里举行宴会，招待前来访问的由中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊上将率领的中华人民共和国军事友好代表团的全体团员。
    出席宴会的有阿联负责军事事务的国务部长穆罕默德·易卜拉欣中将，阿联武装部队参谋长阿里·阿密尔中将，阿联空军和陆军的其他将军和高级军官。
    中国驻阿联大使陈家康和大使馆的其他外交官员也出席了宴会。
    在宴会上，张宗逊上将和西德基·马哈茂德总司令在友好的气氛中讲了话。
    中国军事友好代表团今天上午拜访了西德基·马哈茂德，并把一面绣着中国人民解放军空军军徽的军旗和一架飞机的模型赠送给阿联空军总部。西德基·马哈茂德把一面旗帜和阿联空军的军徽送给中国军事友好代表团。下午，西德基·马哈茂德回访了中国军事友好代表团。
    中国军事友好代表团当天下午参观了航空学院。为了欢迎中国客人，这个学院的一架飞机作了特技表演，组成“中国空军”字样的二十四架飞机飞过学院的上空。这个学院的全体学生还作了体育表演，最后并高呼“中华人民共和国万岁”的口号。
    在过去四天中，中国军事友好代表团还参观了开罗大学，女子体育学院，开罗体育场，以及开罗的名胜古迹。
    新华社开罗15日电　正在阿联访问的中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊将军率领的中国军事友好代表团捐赠了三万叙利亚镑给大马士革的巴勒斯坦难民营。这笔捐款已经通过这里阿联武装部队的公众关系部送去。
    中国军事友好代表团团员曾在10月3日访问了大马士革的巴勒斯坦难民营，他们在那里受到热烈欢迎。
