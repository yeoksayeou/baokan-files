Title: Li Dequan Receives Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-02
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, 1st October. Li Dequan, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Zhang Zhixiang, Acting Secretary-General, received the entire delegation of activists from the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, led by Shmansky, First Secretary of the Henan District Committee of the CPSU Moscow City Committee, this afternoon. A cordial conversation took place between the hosts and guests. During the reception, Baytemirov, a Kyrgyz poet and member of the delegation, recited an enthusiastic poem: "The Steppe Flies to Beijing."

Present at the reception were Li Xigeng, Bian Cheng, and Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. Romanin, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, was also present.

### 李德全接见苏中友协旅行团

1960-10-02
第7版()
专栏：

    李德全接见苏中友协旅行团
    新华社1日讯　中苏友好协会总会副会长李德全和代理秘书长张致祥，今天下午接见了以苏共莫斯科市河南区区委第一书记什曼斯基为首的苏中友好协会积极分子专业旅行团全体团员，宾主间进行了亲切的交谈。旅行团团员、吉尔吉斯诗人巴伊捷米罗夫在接见时朗诵了热情洋溢的诗：“草原飞向北京”。
    接见时在座的，有中苏友好协会总会副秘书长李希庚、卞诚、李沾吾。苏联驻中国大使馆参赞罗满宁也在座。
