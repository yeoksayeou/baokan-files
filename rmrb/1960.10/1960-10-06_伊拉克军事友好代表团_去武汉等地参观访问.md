Title: Iraqi Military Friendship Delegation Visits Wuhan and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1960-10-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd -- The Iraqi military friendship delegation, headed by Brigadier General Mahmoud Abdul Razzaq, after a seven-day visit to Beijing, accompanied by Lieutenant General Chang Qiankun of the Air Force, representative of the Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, and Colonel Xu Yimin, military attache of the Chinese Embassy in the Republic of Iraq, left this morning on a special plane to visit Wuhan, Shanghai, Nanjing and other places.

During their stay in Beijing, the Iraqi military friendship delegation visited the Chinese People's Liberation Army troops and military academies, and visited the Chinese People's Revolution Military Museum, the National Agricultural Exhibition Hall, the National Culture Palace, the Ming Tombs Reservoir, and the Great Wall and other scenic spots.

### 伊拉克军事友好代表团  去武汉等地参观访问

1960-10-06
第3版()
专栏：

    伊拉克军事友好代表团
    去武汉等地参观访问
    据新华社3日讯　以马哈茂德·阿卜杜勒·拉扎克准将为首的伊拉克军事友好代表团，在北京进行了七天参观访问以后，由中华人民共和国国防部代表常乾坤空军中将和我国驻伊拉克共和国大使馆武官徐毅民上校陪同，今晨乘专机前往武汉、上海、南京等地参观访问。
    伊拉克军事友好代表团在北京期间，曾访问了中国人民解放军部队和军事学校，参观了中国人民革命军事博物馆、全国农业展览馆、民族文化宫、十三陵水库，以及长城等名胜古迹。
