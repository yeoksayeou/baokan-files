Title: Film Week Commemorating the Tenth Anniversary of the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea Opens Across the Country
Author:
Date: 1960-10-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th. Film week commemorating the tenth anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' participation in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea began screening films today in cities including Beijing, Shanghai, Shenyang, Tianjin, Guangzhou, Wuhan, Xi'an, and Chengdu.

    In Shenyang, an opening ceremony attended by nearly two thousand people was held on the evening of the 24th. Zhang Jizhong, Vice President of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association's Liaoning Branch and Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, said in his speech that the film week commemorating the tenth anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' participation in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea would contribute to further consolidating and developing the fraternal friendship between the peoples of China and Korea. Sun Chuansi, Vice President of the Hubei Province and Wuhan Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, said at the opening ceremony of the film week in Wuhan: The films on display vividly expose the aggressive nature of U.S. imperialism and the true face of the paper tiger, and praise the heroic deeds of the heroic sons and daughters of China and Korea in the struggle against the common enemy. It will further inspire and educate us to heighten our vigilance and fight to the end against aggression and in defense of world peace. At the opening ceremonies of the film week in Tianjin, Xi'an, Chengdu and other cities, the heads of the municipal cultural bureaus also spoke.

    The films screened during the film week include the Korean feature films "Comrades," "Reconnaissance Soldier," and "How Can We Live Separated?", the Chinese documentaries "Historical Witness" and the feature films "North of the 38th Parallel" and "Friendship," a total of seven films. After being screened in cinemas across the country today, they were warmly welcomed by the people. Shanghai Mayor Ke Qingshi, Deputy Mayors Cao Diqiu, Zhao Zukang and others watched the film "Historical Witness," which documents the heroic struggle of the Chinese and Korean people against U.S. imperialism, together with the audience. Before the screenings, all cinemas in Shanghai had sold out their tickets. (Attached pictures)

	In the war against aggression, the Korean people fully supported the Volunteer Army in combat. Day and night, they helped the troops transport ammunition to the front lines. Xinhua News Agency

	After the Korean Armistice, Volunteer Army commanders and fighters helped the Korean people restore production. This is a Volunteer Army soldier helping Korean farmers thresh and stack grass. Xinhua News Agency

### 各地纪念抗美援朝十周年电影周开幕

1960-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    各地纪念抗美援朝十周年电影周开幕
    新华社25日讯　纪念中国人民志愿军抗美援朝出国作战十周年电影周的影片，今天在北京、上海、沈阳、天津、广州、武汉、西安、成都等市开始上映。
    沈阳市在24日晚上，举行了有近两千人参加的开幕式。中朝友好协会辽宁省分会副会长、沈阳市副市长张霁中在讲话中说，为纪念中国人民志愿军抗美援朝出国作战十周年的电影周，将为进一步巩固和发展中朝两国人民的兄弟友谊作出贡献。中国人民对外文委湖北省和武汉市分会副会长孙川泗在武汉市举行的电影周开幕式上说：展出的影片生动地揭露了美帝国主义的侵略本性和纸老虎的真面目，歌颂了中朝人民的英雄儿女在反对共同敌人斗争中的英雄事迹，它将进一步鼓舞和教育我们提高警惕，为反对侵略，保卫世界和平而斗争到底。在天津、西安、成都等市电影周开幕式上，市文化局负责人也都讲了话。
    电影周放映的朝鲜艺术片“战友”、“侦察兵”、“分开怎能活下去”，中国纪录片“历史的见证”和艺术片“三八线上”、“友谊”等七部影片，今天在各地电影院上映后，受到人们热忱欢迎。上海市市长柯庆施，副市长曹荻秋、赵祖康等和观众一起，观看了纪录中朝人民反对美帝国主义英雄斗争的影片“历史的见证”。上海市各电影院在放映前，电影票已出售一空。（附图片）
