Title: Yunnan Governor Yu Yichuan Hosts Banquet to Welcome Brigadier General Aung Gyi and Other Burmese Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-10-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, October 29th: Yunnan Province Governor Yu Yichuan hosted a banquet on the evening of the 28th to warmly welcome Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the chief representative of the Burmese side of the China-Burma Boundary Joint Committee, and all the representatives and advisors; the Burmese captain and staff of the China-Burma Boundary Joint Survey Team, as well as Colonel So Min, the head of the Burmese Cultural Delegation, and all its members, and Lieutenant Colonel Maw Win, the head of the Burmese Sports Delegation, and all its members.

At the beginning of the banquet, Governor Yu Yichuan, on behalf of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee and the people of all ethnic groups in the province, extended a warm welcome to the distinguished Burmese guests. In his speech, he said that the Burmese delegation and survey team headed by Brigadier General Aung Gyi, together with our delegation and survey team, have made positive contributions to the friendly relations between China and Burma. For several months, under the direct leadership of the Joint Boundary Committee and with the strong support of the border people of the two countries, the two survey teams have cooperated closely, worked tirelessly, climbed mountains and waded through rivers, overcome difficulties, and successfully completed the glorious mission of the previous stage. We express our cordial greetings and high respect to them, and sincerely welcome the convening of the meeting of the captains of both sides in Kunming. We believe that the glorious task after the signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty will, through the joint efforts of both sides, continue to achieve victorious results in the spirit of friendly cooperation and mutual understanding.

Governor Yu Yichuan thanked the Burmese Cultural Delegation and the Sports Delegation for bringing the profound friendship of the Burmese people. He expressed his belief that through the visits of the delegations, the cultural exchange between China and Burma will be further enhanced, and the mutual understanding and "Paukphaw" (brotherly) friendship between the people of China and Burma will be strengthened.

Brigadier General Aung Gyi then spoke, and on behalf of all the Burmese guests, he expressed his sincere gratitude to the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee for its hospitality. He said that tonight's banquet is a banquet of "Paukphaw" and a banquet of friendship. The Burmese Cultural Delegation and Sports Delegation have made friendly visits in various parts of China, bringing the friendship of the Burmese people to the Chinese people; likewise, they will also bring the Chinese people's friendship to the Burmese people back to Burma.

Brigadier General Aung Gyi recalled the achievements made by the China-Burma Boundary Joint Committee and the China-Burma Joint Survey Team in the previous stage for the signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty. He said that in the past few months, thanks to the friendly cooperation and unity of the Chinese and Burmese surveyors in overcoming difficulties, the previous stage of the task was successfully completed on schedule, enabling the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty to be signed on October 1. The China-Burma Boundary Joint Committee cannot but commend the spirit of friendly cooperation of the surveyors of the two countries. He expressed his firm belief that on the basis of the friendly cooperation already achieved, the Sino-Burmese survey teams will understand and help each other, and the future survey work will be completed smoothly and on schedule as in the past.

Those present at the banquet were: Liu Minghui, Liu Zhuofu, Guo Chao, and Liu Piyun, Vice Governors of Yunnan Province; Lieutenant General Qin Jiwei, Lieutenant General Chen Kang, and Major General Lu Ruilin, leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming; Ding Xilin, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, who is now in Kunming, and Yao Zhongming, the chief representative of the Chinese side of the China-Burma Boundary Joint Committee.

Also present at the banquet were: Pan Shuoduan, Mayor of Kunming; Zhang Zizhai, Secretary-General of the Provincial People's Committee; heads of various departments of the Provincial People's Committee; all representatives, advisors, and captains of the survey team of the Chinese delegation of the China-Burma Boundary Joint Committee; Cun Shusheng, Zhang Tianfang, Wang Shaoyan, Dao Jingban, and Zhao Yuying, Vice-Chairmen of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the CPPCC; heads of local organizations of various people's groups; Lei Chunguo and Si Lasha, Deputy Governors of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture; Hu Zhonghua, Deputy Commissioner of the Lincang Special District; Pei Aqian, Governor of the Nujiang Lisu Autonomous Prefecture; and people from the literary and sports circles of the province and city.

U Maw Maw So, Consul General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, and Vu Van Huong, Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming, were also invited to the banquet.

### 云南省长于一川举行宴会  欢迎昂季准将等缅甸贵宾

1960-10-30
第4版()
专栏：

    云南省长于一川举行宴会
    欢迎昂季准将等缅甸贵宾
    新华社昆明29日电　云南省省长于一川28日晚上举行宴会，热烈欢迎中缅边界联合委员会缅方首席代表昂季准将和全体代表、顾问；中缅边界联合勘察队缅方勘察队长、工作人员，以及缅甸文化代表团团长苏敏上校和全体团员，缅甸体育代表团团长貌温中校和全体团员。
    于一川省长在宴会开始时，代表云南省人民委员会和全省各族人民，对缅甸贵宾们表示热烈欢迎。他在讲话中说，中缅边界联合委员会以昂季准将为首的缅甸代表团和勘察队，同我国代表团和勘察队一起，对中缅友好关系作出了积极的贡献。数月来，双方勘察队在边界联合委员会的直接领导和两国边境人民的大力支援下，密切协作，不辞辛苦，爬山涉水，克服困难，圆满地完成了前一阶段的光荣使命，我们特表示亲切的问候和崇高的敬意，并衷心地欢迎双方队长会议在昆明召开。我们相信，中缅边界条约签订后的光荣任务，将通过双方的共同努力，继续本着友好合作、互相谅解的精神而取得胜利的结果。
    于一川省长感谢缅甸文化代表团和体育代表团所带来的缅甸人民的深厚情谊。他表示相信，通过代表团的访问，必将进一步增进中缅两国的文化交流，增进中缅两国人民的相互了解和“胞波”情谊。
    昂季准将接着讲话，他代表全体缅甸客人对云南省人民委员会的殷勤接待表示衷心感谢。他说，今晚的宴会，是“胞波”的宴会，也是友谊的宴会。缅甸文化代表团和体育代表团曾经在中国各地进行了友好访问，把缅甸人民的友谊带给了中国人民；同样，他们也将把中国人民对缅甸人民的友谊，带回缅甸去。
    昂季准将回溯了前一阶段中缅边界联合委员会及中缅联合勘察队为签订中缅边界条约所作出的成绩。他说，在过去的几个月中，由于中缅两国勘察人员友好合作，团结一致，克服困难，如期圆满地完成了前一阶段的任务，使中缅边界条约能够在10月1日签字。中缅边界联合委员会不能不赞扬两国勘察工作人员这种友好合作的精神。他表示确信，中缅两国勘察队在已经取得的友好合作的基础上，互相谅解，互相帮助，今后的勘察工作也将像过去一样，如期顺利地完成任务。
    出席宴会的有：云南省副省长刘明辉、刘卓甫、郭超、刘披云，中国人民解放军驻昆明部队首长秦基伟中将、陈康中将、鲁瑞林少将，现在昆明的对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林和中缅边界联合委员会中国首席代表姚仲明。
    出席宴会的还有：昆明市市长潘朔端，省人民委员会秘书长张子斋，省人民委员会各部门负责人，中缅边界联合委员会中国代表团全体代表、顾问和勘察队队长，政协云南省委员会副主席寸树声、张天放、王少岩、刀京版、赵毓英，各人民团体地方组织负责人，德宏傣族景颇族自治州副州长雷春国、司拉山；临沧专区副专员胡忠华，怒江傈僳族自治州州长裴阿欠，以及省、市的文艺界、体育界人士等。
    缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭、越南民主共和国驻昆明领事武文享也应邀参加了宴会。
