Title: Cuba Appoints Óscar Pino Santos as Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1960-10-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 18th: According to local newspapers today, by order of the President, Óscar Pino Santos has been appointed as the Cuban Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of China.

### 古巴任命奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯为驻华大使

1960-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

    古巴任命奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯为驻华大使
    新华社哈瓦那18日电　据当地报纸今天报道，根据总统的命令，已任命奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯为古巴驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使。
