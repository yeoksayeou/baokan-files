Title: All-China Journalists Association Holds Reception to Welcome Foreign Journalist Friends Visiting China
Author:
Date: 1960-10-02
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, October 1st. The All-China Journalists Association held a reception this afternoon at the Overseas Chinese Hotel to welcome more than one hundred journalist friends from over twenty countries who were invited to visit China. Emilio Portes Gil, head of the Mexican cultural delegation and former interim president of Mexico, also attended the reception.

Wu Lengxi, Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, spoke at the reception. He pointed out that striving for lasting world peace, opposing imperialism and colonialism, and fighting for the independence, freedom, democracy, happiness, and progress of all nations and human society are the common tasks of journalists. He proposed a toast to the health of all the friends from various countries attending the reception for greater achievements in this common cause.

During the reception, members of the Japanese journalists delegation, amidst rhythmic applause from the audience, sang songs from the Japanese people's struggle against the Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty," as well as the song "Tokyo-Beijing" expressing the friendship between the Japanese and Chinese people. The heroic Cuban journalists enthusiastically sang Cuban folk songs with Chinese journalists. Journalists from Mexico, Lebanon, and other countries also improvised solo performances of their national folk songs. Rabi Sakali, a journalist from the Moroccan National Radio Station, also recited a poem he wrote after coming to China: "I Love China." The guests and hosts frequently toasted to friendship and common cause. The reception was filled with an atmosphere of friendship and unity. Among those attending the reception were Mei Yi, Wang Yunsheng, and Zhu Muzhi, vice-chairmen of the All-China Journalists Association, Wang Yi, An Gang, Deng Gang, and Zhou You, secretaries of the Secretariat, as well as heads of other news organizations in the capital.

### 我新闻工作者协会举行酒会  招待访问我国的外国新闻界朋友

1960-10-02
第7版()
专栏：

    我新闻工作者协会举行酒会
    招待访问我国的外国新闻界朋友
    新华社1日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会今天下午在新侨饭店举行酒会，招待应邀访华的一百多位来自二十多个国家的新闻界的朋友。墨西哥文化代表团团长、墨西哥前临时总统埃米略·波尔特斯·希耳也出席了酒会。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会主席吴冷西在酒会上讲话。他指出争取世界持久和平，反对帝国主义，反对殖民主义政策，为争取各个民族的独立、自由、民主和人类社会的幸福、进步而斗争，是新闻工作者的共同任务。他建议为这一共同事业取得更大的成就，为出席酒会的各国朋友们的健康干杯。
    酒会进行中，日本新闻工作者代表团团员在全场有节奏的掌声中，齐唱日本人民反对日美“安全条约”斗争中的歌曲，以及表达日中人民友谊的歌曲“东京——北京”。英雄的古巴的记者们热情地和中国新闻工作者合唱了古巴的民歌。墨西哥、黎巴嫩等国的记者也都即兴独唱了本国的民歌。摩洛哥国家广播电台的记者拉比·萨卡利还朗诵了他来中国后写的一首诗：“我爱中国”。宾主们频频为友谊、为共同的事业干杯。酒会充满了友好和团结的气氛。出席酒会的有中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席梅益、王芸生、朱穆之，书记处书记王揖、安岗、邓岗、周游，以及首都其他新闻单位的负责人。
