Title: Burmese Military Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Shenyang and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 10th: The Burmese military goodwill delegation, headed by Brigadier General Maung Maung, concluded its visit to Shenyang, Lushun-Dalian (Lüda), and other places. Accompanied by Lieutenant General Yang Xiushan, representative of the Chinese Ministry of National Defense, Senior Colonel Zhu Kaiyin, Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau, and Senior Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in the Union of Burma, the delegation returned to Beijing this afternoon by special train from Lushun-Dalian.

Those who greeted them at the station included Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Chinese Ministry of National Defense, Air Force Major General Chen Xi, Navy Senior Colonel Ma Guansan, and Senior Colonel Ye Xiuzhi, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense, among others.

Colonel Kyaing Saung, Military Attaché of the Burmese Embassy in China, who accompanied the Burmese military goodwill delegation on their visit to Shenyang and other places, also returned to Beijing on the same train.

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, October 10th: The Burmese military goodwill delegation, headed by Brigadier General Maung Maung, left Lushun-Dalian on the evening of the 9th by special train for Beijing after visiting Shenyang, Anshan, Lushun-Dalian, and other places.

During their visit to Shenyang, the Burmese military goodwill delegation, accompanied by Lieutenant General Zeng Siyu, commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Shenyang, and Vice Governor of Liaoning Province, Chu Fengqi, visited the Liaoning Industrial Exhibition Hall, Shenyang Heavy Machinery Plant, Shenyang Cable Factory, the Tiexi Workers' Village in Shenyang, and a military academy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. After arriving in Anshan and Lushun-Dalian, the distinguished guests also visited military academies of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Anshan Iron and Steel Company, the Lushun-Dalian Agricultural Science Research Institute, and the Dalian Locomotive Plant. The friendly visit of the distinguished Burmese guests was warmly welcomed by the officers and soldiers of the stationed troops and the workers.

When the distinguished guests arrived and departed from Shenyang, Anshan, and Lushun-Dalian, Lieutenant General Zeng Siyu, Lieutenant General Zeng Shaoshan, commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Shenyang, Vice Governor of Liaoning Province Chu Fengqi, Vice Mayor of Shenyang Song Guang, Lieutenant General Liu Zhuanlian, commander of the Lushun-Dalian Garrison, and Vice Mayor of Lushun-Dalian Xin Shu, and others, were present at the railway station to welcome and see them off.

### 缅军事代表团访问沈阳等地后回京

1960-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    缅军事代表团访问沈阳等地后回京
    新华社10日讯　以貌貌准将为首的缅甸军事友好代表团，结束在沈阳、旅大等地参观访问后，在我国国防部代表杨秀山中将、外事处处长朱开印大校、我国驻缅甸联邦大使馆武官蒋克定大校陪同下，由旅大乘专车今天下午回到北京。
    到车站迎接的，有我国国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将，中国人民解放军陈熙空军少将、马冠三海军大校，国防部外事处副处长叶修直大校等。
    随同缅甸军事友好代表团前往沈阳等地访问的缅甸联邦驻中国大使馆武官景沙门上校，也同车返回北京。
    新华社沈阳10日电　以貌貌准将为首的缅甸军事友好代表团，在沈阳、鞍山、旅大等地参观访问后，9日晚上离开旅大，乘专车前往北京。
    缅甸军事友好代表团在沈阳访问期间，由中国人民解放军驻沈阳部队首长曾思玉中将、辽宁省副省长褚凤岐陪同，曾参观了辽宁工业展览馆、沈阳重型机械厂、沈阳电缆厂，访问了沈阳市铁西工人村和中国人民解放军军事学校。贵宾们到达鞍山、旅大以后，又先后参观访问了中国人民解放军军事学校、鞍山钢铁公司、旅大市农业科学研究所和大连机车厂等。缅甸贵宾们的友好访问，受到了驻地部队官兵和工人们的热烈欢迎。
    在贵宾们到达和离开沈阳、鞍山、旅大时，中国人民解放军驻沈阳部队首长曾思玉中将、曾绍山中将，辽宁省副省长褚凤岐、沈阳市副市长宋光，旅大驻军首长刘转连中将，旅大市副市长辛束等，都曾分别到车站迎送。
