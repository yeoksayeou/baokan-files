Title: Swedish Peace Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th—The Swedish Peace Delegation, which visited our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, left Beijing by plane today to return home. Wang Mingyuan, Zheng Senyu, Qi Muguan, and other members of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace saw them off at the airport.

### 瑞典和平代表团回国

1960-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典和平代表团回国
    据新华社24日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问的瑞典和平代表团，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会委员王明远、郑森禹、戚慕光等到机场欢送。
