Title: Bao Erhan and Qu Wu, Vice Presidents of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, Meet with Delegation from the Tunisian Neo Destour Party
Author:
Date: 1960-10-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 18th. Bao Erhan and Qu Wu, Vice Presidents of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, met this afternoon with the delegation from the Tunisian Neo Destour Party headed by Ben Ammar Hassib, and held friendly talks with the guests. He Gongkai and others were present at the meeting.
At the end of the meeting, the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association presented the delegation with gifts such as films and pictures.

### 中非友好协会副会长包尔汉和屈武  会见突尼斯新宪政党代表团

1960-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

    中非友好协会副会长包尔汉和屈武
    会见突尼斯新宪政党代表团
    新华社18日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会副会长包尔汉、屈武今天下午会见了以本·阿玛尔·哈西卜为首的突尼斯新宪政党代表团，并且同客人们进行了友好的谈话。会见时，在座的有何功楷等。
    会见结束时，中非友好协会向代表团赠送了电影片、图片等礼品。
