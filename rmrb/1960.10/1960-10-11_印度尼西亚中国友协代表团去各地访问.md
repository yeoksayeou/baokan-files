Title: Indonesian-China Friendship Association Delegation Visits Various Locations

Author: 
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The delegation of the Indonesian-China Friendship Association, led by Professor Suripto, which was invited by the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries to visit our country, left Beijing by train today to visit various places in southern China.
Those who saw them off at the station included Lin Lin, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, and others.

### 印度尼西亚中国友协代表团去各地访问

1960-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚中国友协代表团去各地访问
    新华社10日讯　应中国印度尼西亚友好协会邀请来我国访问的以苏里普托教授为首的印度尼西亚中国友好协会代表团，今天乘火车离开北京去我国南方各地参观访问。
    到车站欢送的有中国印度尼西亚友好协会总干事林林等。
