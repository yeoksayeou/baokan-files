Title: Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun Receives Burmese News Delegation, Gong Peng Hosts Banquet for Burmese Guests, Delegation Leaves Beijing for Visits to Various Locations

Author:
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 6th: Vice Premier of the State Council, Xi Zhongxun, received the Burmese News Delegation headed by U Khin this afternoon and had a cordial and friendly conversation. Present were Gong Peng, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Kang Maozhao, Deputy Director, and Fan Jin, Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association.

Xinhua News Agency, October 6th: Gong Peng, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet at noon today to welcome the Burmese News Delegation headed by U Khin, Deputy Director of the Burmese News Bureau. The delegation accompanied Prime Minister U Nu on his visit to our country and left Beijing this evening to visit various parts of our country.

### 习仲勋副总理接见缅甸新闻代表团  龚澎设宴招待缅甸客人　代表团离京去各地访问

1960-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    习仲勋副总理接见缅甸新闻代表团
    龚澎设宴招待缅甸客人　代表团离京去各地访问
    新华社6日讯　国务院副总理习仲勋今天下午接见了以吴钦为首的缅甸新闻代表团，并且进行了亲切友好的谈话。在座的，有外交部新闻司司长龚澎、副司长康矛召，中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席范瑾。
    新华社6日讯　外交部新闻司司长龚澎今天中午举行宴会招待以缅甸新闻局副局长吴钦为首的缅甸新闻代表团。代表团是随吴努总理来我国访问的，他们已在今晚离开北京去我国各地访问。
