Title: Our National Song and Dance Troupe Gives Final Performance in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 3rd. The Chinese National Song and Dance Troupe gave its final performance in Moscow at the Kremlin Theatre on the evening of the 2nd. Among those who watched the performance were Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Pushkin and responsible comrades from various ministries and commissions of the Soviet government. Liu Xiao, the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, also attended the performance. There were also diplomatic officials from many countries' embassies in the Soviet Union among the audience.

### 我民族歌舞团在莫斯科作最后演出

1960-10-07
第6版()
专栏：

    我民族歌舞团在莫斯科作最后演出
    据新华社莫斯科3日电　中国民族歌舞团2日晚间在克里姆林宫剧院举行访问莫斯科的最后一场演出。观看这场演出的有苏联外交部副部长普希金等苏联政府各部、委的负责同志，中国驻苏联大使刘晓也出席观看了演出。在观众中还有许多国家驻苏使馆的外交官员。
