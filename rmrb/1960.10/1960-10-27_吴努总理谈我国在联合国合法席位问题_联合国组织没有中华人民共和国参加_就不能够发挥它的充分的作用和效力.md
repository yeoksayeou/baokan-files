Title: Prime Minister U Nu Discusses China's Legitimate Seat in the United Nations: The United Nations Organization Cannot Exert Its Full Role and Effectiveness Without the Participation of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1960-10-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, October 25th: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu pointed out yesterday, when discussing the issue of the legitimate seat of the People's Republic of China in the United Nations, that it is unreasonable for a group of people (referring to the Chiang gang) who have long lost the right to speak on behalf of the Chinese people to falsely "represent" the People's Republic of China, which has a population of 650 million.

He made these remarks in a letter sent yesterday to a gathering in Rangoon held to commemorate the fifteenth anniversary of the founding of the United Nations.

Prime Minister U Nu also said, "Until this anomaly is corrected, the United Nations Organization cannot exert its full role and effectiveness."

According to reports in the newspapers here today, the Burmese National United Front, the Burmese Committee for the Defense of World Peace, and the Burmese People's Progressive Party issued statements strongly condemning U.S. imperialism for controlling the United Nations and obstructing the restoration of the legitimate seat of the People's Republic of China in the United Nations.

### 吴努总理谈我国在联合国合法席位问题  联合国组织没有中华人民共和国参加  就不能够发挥它的充分的作用和效力

1960-10-27
第5版()
专栏：

    吴努总理谈我国在联合国合法席位问题
    联合国组织没有中华人民共和国参加  就不能够发挥它的充分的作用和效力
    据新华社仰光25日电　缅甸联邦总理吴努昨天在谈到中华人民共和国在联合国的合法席位问题时指出，一批早已丧失代表中国人民讲话权利的人（指蒋帮）来虚妄地“代表”拥有六亿五千万人民的中华人民共和国，这是不合理的。
    他是在昨天向仰光为联合国成立十五周年举行的一个集会发出的信中说这番话的。
    吴努总理又说，“在这种反常现象得到纠正之前，联合国组织不能够发挥它的充分的作用和效力。”
    据这里的报纸今天报道，缅甸民族团结阵线、缅甸保卫世界和平委员会和缅甸人民进步党发表声明，强烈谴责美帝国主义控制联合国和阻挠恢复中华人民共和国在联合国的合法席位。
