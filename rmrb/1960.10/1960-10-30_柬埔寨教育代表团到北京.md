Title: Cambodian Education Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 29th. At the invitation of the Ministry of Education, the Cambodian education delegation, consisting of three members and led by Mr. Kham Phot, Director of Private Education of the Cambodian Ministry of Education, arrived in Beijing by train tonight.

Those who welcomed them at the station included Cui Zhongyuan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Education, Xu Fangting, Director of the Department of General Education, and others. Chou Sory, First Secretary of the Cambodian Embassy in China, and others were also at the station to greet them.

### 柬埔寨教育代表团到北京

1960-10-30
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨教育代表团到北京
    新华社29日讯　应教育部邀请来我国访问的柬埔寨教育代表团一行三人，在柬埔寨教育部私立教育司司长夏木特率领下，今晚乘火车到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有教育部办公厅主任崔仲远、普通教育司司长徐方庭等。柬埔寨驻华大使馆一等秘书周索里等也到车站迎接。
