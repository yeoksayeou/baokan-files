Title: Burmese Trade Delegation Leaves Tianjin for Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-10-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, October 14th: The Burmese trade delegation, headed by U Myo, a member of the Burmese Federal Parliament, left Tianjin for Shanghai today after a two-day visit to Tianjin.
	The guests visited the Tianjin Textile Machinery Factory, the Tianjin Knitting Factory, and other places in Tianjin.
	On the evening of the 12th, Song Jingyi, Vice Mayor of Tianjin, hosted a banquet for the guests.

### 缅甸贸易代表团离天津去上海

1960-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸贸易代表团离天津去上海
    新华社天津14日电　以缅甸联邦国会议员吴妙为首的缅甸贸易代表团，在天津访问两天后，今天上午离开天津去上海访问。
    客人们在天津参观了天津纺织机械厂、天津针织厂等处。
    12日晚，天津市副市长宋景毅曾设宴招待客人。
