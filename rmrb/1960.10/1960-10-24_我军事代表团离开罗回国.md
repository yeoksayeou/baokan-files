Title: Our Military Delegation Leaves Cairo for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 23rd -- The Chinese military goodwill delegation, led by General Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, left here today for home after a 26-day friendly visit to the United Arab Republic. Lieutenant General Youssef Agroudi, Commander of the Central Military Region of the Egyptian Region of the UAR, saw the Chinese delegation off on behalf of Marshal Abdel Hakim Amer, Vice President and Minister of Defense. Also seeing off the Chinese delegation were Lieutenant General Helal Abdel Helal, Commander of the Artillery, and other generals of the UAR armed forces.
Albanian Ambassador to the UAR, Dello Balili, and military attachés from various socialist countries also went to the airport to see the delegation off.
Also at the airport to welcome the delegation were Chen Jiakang, Chinese Ambassador to the UAR, and diplomatic personnel from the Chinese Embassy in Cairo.

### 我军事代表团离开罗回国

1960-10-24
第5版()
专栏：

    我军事代表团离开罗回国
    据新华社开罗23日电　由中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊上将率领的中国军事友好代表团，在阿拉伯联合共和国作了历时二十六天的友好访问之后，于今天离开这里回国。阿联埃及地区中央军区司令优素福·阿格鲁迪中将代表副总统兼国防部长阿卜杜勒·哈基姆·阿密尔元帅为中国代表团送行。为中国代表团送行的还有：炮兵司令赫拉勒·阿卜杜勒·赫拉勒中将和阿联武装部队的其它将军们。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻阿联大使德洛·巴利里和各个社会主义国家的武官也到机场为代表团送行。
    到机场欢送代表团的还有中国驻阿联大使陈家康和中国驻开罗大使馆的外交人员。
