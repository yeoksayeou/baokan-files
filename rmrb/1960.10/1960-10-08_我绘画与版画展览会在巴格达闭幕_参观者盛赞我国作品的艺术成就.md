Title: My Painting and Print Exhibition Closes in Baghdad; Visitors Praise the Artistic Achievements of Our Country's Works
Author:
Date: 1960-10-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, 5th: The Chinese Painting and Print Exhibition closed here on the afternoon of the 5th. During the ten-day exhibition, approximately one thousand people from Iraqi cultural and artistic circles visited. Visitors praised the works and artistic achievements of Chinese artists. One Iraqi artist wrote in the visitors' book, "Chairman Mao Zedong said that true art must come from the people. When I visited the exhibition of People's China today, I felt this."

### 我绘画与版画展览会在巴格达闭幕  参观者盛赞我国作品的艺术成就

1960-10-08
第5版()
专栏：

    我绘画与版画展览会在巴格达闭幕
    参观者盛赞我国作品的艺术成就
    据新华社巴格达5日电　中国绘画与版画展览会5日下午在这里闭幕。在历时十天的展出期间，伊拉克文化艺术界的大约一千多人参观了展览会。参观者都盛赞中国艺术家的作品和艺术成就。一位伊拉克艺术家在观众留言簿上写道，“毛泽东主席说，真正的艺术必须来自人民，当我今天参观人民中国的展览会时，我感觉到了这一点。”
