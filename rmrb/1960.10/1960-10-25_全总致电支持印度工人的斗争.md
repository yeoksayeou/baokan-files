Title: ACFTU Sends Telegram Supporting Indian Workers' Struggle
Author:
Date: 1960-10-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 24th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions (ACFTU) today sent a telegram to the All India Trade Union Congress, supporting the struggle of Indian workers to defend their vital interests, oppose persecution, and safeguard democratic and trade union rights.

The ACFTU's telegram stated: "Chinese workers are closely watching the heroic struggle of Indian central government employees, as well as the widespread suspensions, dismissals, trials, and violations of trade union rights suffered by the broad masses of workers. We extend our brotherly sympathy and support to you, and through you to the Indian workers, for their struggle to defend their vital interests, oppose persecution, and safeguard democratic and trade union rights. We firmly believe that the fighting spirit of the working masses is indestructible. The Indian working class will become more conscious and strengthen its unity through this struggle, and will achieve final victory."

### 全总致电支持印度工人的斗争

1960-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    全总致电支持印度工人的斗争
    新华社24日讯　中华全国总工会今天打电报给全印工会大会，支持印度工人为维护切身利益、反对迫害、保卫民主权利和工会权利的斗争。
    中华全国总工会的电报说：“中国工人十分关心地注视着印度中央政府雇员所进行的英勇斗争，以及广大工人群众遭受大规模停职、解雇、受审判及工会权利受到破坏的情形。我们谨向你们并通过你们向印度工人为维护切身利益、反对迫害、保卫民主权利和工会权利所进行的斗争表示兄弟般的同情和支援。我们坚信，工人群众的战斗性是不可摧毁的。印度工人阶级通过这一斗争将更加提高觉悟，加强团结，并取得最后胜利。”
