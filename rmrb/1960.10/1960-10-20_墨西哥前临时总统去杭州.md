Title: Former Mexican Interim President Visits Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-10-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 19th: The Mexican cultural delegation led by Emilio Portes Gil, former interim president of Mexico, left Shanghai by train this afternoon for a visit to Hangzhou after concluding its tour and visit in Shanghai.

Those who went to the station to see them off included Liu Shuzhou, Vice Mayor of Shanghai; Hu Juewen, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Zhang Chunqiao, President of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and Director of the China-Latin America Friendship Association in Shanghai.

During its stay in Shanghai, the Mexican cultural delegation visited the Shanghai Industrial Exhibition, the No. 2 State Cotton Factory, Zhangmiao Street, the Liyuan Neighborhood Committee, the Haiyan Film Studio, the Shanghai Municipal Workers' Cultural Palace, and the Children's Palace.

### 墨西哥前临时总统去杭州

1960-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

    墨西哥前临时总统去杭州
    新华社上海19日电　由墨西哥前临时总统埃米略·波尔特斯·希耳率领的墨西哥文化代表团，结束在上海的参观访问后，今天下午乘火车离开上海去杭州访问。
    到车站欢送的有上海市副市长刘述周，政协上海市委员会副主席胡厥文，中国人民对外文化协会上海市分会会长、中国拉丁美洲友好协会在上海理事张春桥等。
    墨西哥文化代表团在上海期间，曾参观访问了上海工业展览会、国棉二厂、张庙街、丽园街道委员会、海燕电影制片厂、上海市工人文化宫和少年宫等。
