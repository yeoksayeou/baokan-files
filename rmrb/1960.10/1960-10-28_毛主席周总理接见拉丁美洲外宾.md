Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Receive Latin American Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-10-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th. Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai met this afternoon with the cultural delegations and representatives from twelve Latin American countries who are currently visiting China.

They are the cultural delegations and representatives from Cuba, Argentina, Bolivia, Brazil, Chile, Colombia, Dominican Republic, Mexico, Panama, Peru, Uruguay, and Venezuela.

During the meeting, Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai warmly shook hands with the foreign guests one by one, expressing their warm welcome. After taking a group photo with Chairman Mao, the foreign guests excitedly surrounded Chairman Mao, enthusiastically applauding to express their love and respect for him. They also shouted, "Long live China!" Chairman Mao also said to them, "Long live the people of Latin America!"

Also present at the meeting were: Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Zhixiang, President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association Chu Tunan, Vice President Zhou Erfu, Deputy Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Lin Ping, and others. ### 毛主席周总理接见拉丁美洲外宾

1960-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席周总理接见拉丁美洲外宾
    十二个国家的文化代表同毛主席合影以后，激动地围着毛主席热烈鼓掌，表达对毛主席的敬爱，他们还高呼：“中国万岁！”毛主席也向他们说：“拉丁美洲人民万岁！”
    新华社27日讯　毛泽东主席、周恩来总理今天下午接见了正在我国访问的拉丁美洲十二个国家的文化代表团和代表。
    他们是古巴、阿根廷、玻利维亚、巴西、智利、哥伦比亚、多米尼加、墨西哥、巴拿马、秘鲁、乌拉圭、委内瑞拉的文化代表团和代表。
    在接见的时候，毛泽东主席、周恩来总理和外宾们一一亲切握手，向他们表示热烈欢迎。外宾们同毛主席合影以后，都激动地围着毛主席热烈鼓掌，表达对毛主席的敬爱，他们还高呼：“中国万岁！”毛主席也向他们说：“拉丁美洲人民万岁！”
    接见时在场的有：国务院副总理陈毅，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥、中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南、副会长周而复、外交部美澳司副司长林平等。（附图片）
