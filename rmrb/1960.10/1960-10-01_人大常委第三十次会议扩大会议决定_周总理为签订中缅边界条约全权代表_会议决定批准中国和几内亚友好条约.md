Title: NPC Standing Committee Thirtieth Enlarged Meeting Decides: Premier Zhou as Plenipotentiary Representative for Signing Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty; Meeting Decides to Approve Sino-Guinean Friendship Treaty
Author:
Date: 1960-10-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 30th — The Thirtieth Enlarged Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress was held this morning. The meeting decided to appoint Premier Zhou Enlai as the plenipotentiary representative for signing the "Boundary Treaty Between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Burma." The meeting decided to approve the "Friendship Treaty Between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Guinea."

### 人大常委第三十次会议扩大会议决定  周总理为签订中缅边界条约全权代表  会议决定批准中国和几内亚友好条约

1960-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    人大常委第三十次会议扩大会议决定
    周总理为签订中缅边界条约全权代表
    会议决定批准中国和几内亚友好条约
    新华社30日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会第三十次会议扩大会议，在今天上午举行。会议决定派周恩来总理为签订“中华人民共和国和缅甸联邦边界条约”的全权代表。会议决定批准“中华人民共和国和几内亚共和国友好条约”。
