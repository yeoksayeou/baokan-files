Title: Abbas Says to Reporter from Cairo's "Al-Ahram": China Supports the Algerian People's Struggle; Algerian National Liberation Army Victoriously Strikes, Killing or Wounding Over 600 French Troops in Two Days
Author:
Date: 1960-10-17
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, October 15th: Algerian Provisional Government Prime Minister Abbas Ferhat told a reporter from Cairo's "Al-Ahram" that "China supports the Algerian people's struggle and will continue to provide unreserved support."

"Al-Ahram" published Abbas Ferhat's interview with the newspaper's reporter on the 15th. He arrived in Cairo after recently visiting China and the Soviet Union.

The newspaper quoted Abbas Ferhat as saying: The most direct path for Algeria to achieve victory is to "continue the war" with the continued support of African, Asian, and other countries.

Referring to de Gaulle's recent statement on the Algerian issue, Abbas Ferhat pointed out that these were clichés aimed at concealing the essence of his proposed plan for Algeria, which is to make the Algerian people surrender to the French colonial forces. He said: "We still believe that de Gaulle's true policy towards Algeria is war. The Melun talks have exposed and proved de Gaulle's true face to the world." He said that the United Nations' failure to resolve the Algerian issue "will not harm the future of the Algerian people who are determined to fight to independence, but it is a danger to the future of the United Nations."

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, October 15th: According to a military communique issued here by the headquarters of the Algerian National Liberation Army, in the battles fought on September 28th and 29th, the Algerian National Liberation Army killed 332 French troops and wounded 290.

The communique said that in these two days, Algerian forces engaged in 13 encounters with French troops, attacked 14 enemy military strongholds, ambushed enemy military convoys five times, and the Algerian National Liberation Army's demolition units carried out multiple sabotage operations against enemy transportation and military facilities.

The communique said that in these battles, Algerian forces also destroyed 17 French military vehicles, shot down four airplanes, destroyed two military trains, and seized 293 enemy weapons.

Xinhua News Agency, October 15th: Algiers dispatch: On the evening of the 13th, the heroic Algerian National Liberation Army ambushed a convoy of French colonialist soldiers near Blida, 25 miles southwest of Algiers.

Preliminary reports said that seven French soldiers were killed and four wounded in the ambush.

### 阿巴斯总理对开罗“金字塔报”记者说  中国支援阿尔及利亚人民斗争  阿尔及利亚民族解放军胜利出击两天毙伤法军六百余名

1960-10-17
第6版()
专栏：

    阿巴斯总理对开罗“金字塔报”记者说
    中国支援阿尔及利亚人民斗争
    阿尔及利亚民族解放军胜利出击两天毙伤法军六百余名
    新华社开罗15日电　阿尔及利亚临时政府总理阿巴斯·费尔哈特向开罗“金字塔报”记者说，“中国支援阿尔及利亚人民的斗争，并且将继续无保留地给予支持。”
    “金字塔报”在15日刊登了阿巴斯·费尔哈特总理向该报记者发表的谈话。他最近在访问中国和苏联以后到达开罗。
    这家报纸引述阿巴斯·费尔哈特的话说：阿尔及利亚走向胜利的最简捷的道路是，在亚非和其他国家继续给予支持的情况下，“继续进行战争”。
    在谈到戴高乐最近发表的关于阿尔及利亚问题的谈话时，阿巴斯·费尔哈特指出，这是一些陈词滥调，其目的在于掩饰他所提出的关于阿尔及利亚问题的计划的实质，即使阿尔及利亚人民在法国的殖民力量面前投降。他说：“我们仍旧认为，戴高乐对阿尔及利亚的真正政策是战争，默伦会谈已向全世界揭穿和证明戴高乐的真面目。”他说，联合国未能解决阿尔及利亚问题，“这并不能危害决心战斗到获得独立为止的阿尔及利亚人民的前途，可是对联合国的前途来说，倒是一种危险。”
    新华社开罗15日电　据阿尔及利亚民族解放军总部在这里发表的一项军事公报说，在9月28日和29日两天进行的战斗中，阿尔及利亚民族解放军击毙法军三百三十二名，击伤二百九十名。
    公报说，在这两天中阿尔及利亚部队同法军进行了十三次遭遇战，袭击了敌人的十四个军事据点，向敌人的军事运输队进行了五次伏击，阿尔及利亚民族解放军的爆破部队还对敌人的交通和军事设施进行了多次破坏。
    公报说，在这些战斗中，阿尔及利亚部队还摧毁了法军十七辆军用车辆，击落四架飞机，炸毁两列军用火车，并缴获敌军二百九十三件武器。
    新华社15日讯　阿尔及尔消息：英勇的阿尔及利亚民族解放军部队13日晚在阿尔及尔西南二十五英里的布利达附近对法国殖民主义者的军用车队进行了伏击。
    初步消息说，在这次伏击中，七名法国士兵被杀，四人受伤。
