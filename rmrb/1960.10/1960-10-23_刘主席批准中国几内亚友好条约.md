Title: Chairman Liu Approves China-Guinea Friendship Treaty
Author:
Date: 1960-10-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, according to the decision of the enlarged meeting of the Thirtieth Session of the Standing Committee of the Second National People's Congress, approved on October 21, 1960, the Friendship Treaty between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Guinea, signed in Beijing on September 13, 1960.

### 刘主席批准中国几内亚友好条约

1960-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席批准中国几内亚友好条约
    新华社22日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇根据第二届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第三十次会议扩大会议的决定，于1960年10月21日批准了1960年9月13日在北京签订的中华人民共和国和几内亚共和国友好条约。
