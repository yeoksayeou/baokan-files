Title: Belgian, Japanese, Canadian, and Australian Trade Union Delegations Depart Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-10-09
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, October 8th: The Belgian Federation of Salaried Employees, Technicians and Managerial Staffs Delegation headed by Oscar LeClerc; the Japanese Harbor Workers Delegation headed by Igarashi Takeo, Secretary-General of the All-Japan Harbor Workers' Union; the Third Japanese Private Railway Workers' Union Confederation Delegation headed by Sato Hiroshi; the Canadian United Electrical, Radio and Machine Workers' Union Delegation headed by Ross Russell; and Williams Clifford Loftus, Assistant Secretary of the Hotel, Club, Restaurant, Caterers, Tea Rooms and Boarding Houses Employees' Union of New South Wales, Australia, have left Beijing for home recently after friendly visits to various parts of our country.

### 比利时　日本　加拿大　澳大利亚  几个工会代表团离京回国

1960-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    比利时　日本　加拿大　澳大利亚
    几个工会代表团离京回国
　　据新华社8日讯　以奥斯卡·勒克莱克为首的比利时职员、技术员和干部工会访华代表团，以全日本港湾工会书记长五十岚武雄为首的日本港湾工人访华代表团，以佐藤弘为首的日本私营铁路工会总联合会第三次访华代表团，以罗斯·鲁塞尔为首的加拿大联合电气、无线电、机械工人工会代表团，以及澳大利亚新南威尔斯州旅馆业工会助理书记威廉斯·克利福德·劳夫塔斯，在我国各地进行友好访问后，近日已先后离开北京回国。
