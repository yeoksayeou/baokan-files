Title: Shanghai People's Committee Holds Reception Welcoming Japan-China Friendship Association Delegation and Japanese Drama Troupe
Author:
Date: 1960-10-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 27th. The Shanghai People's Committee held a reception on the evening of the 26th to warmly welcome the Japan-China Friendship Association Delegation and the Japanese Drama Troupe, who are currently visiting Shanghai.

	Cao Diqiu, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, spoke at the reception. He extended a warm welcome to the Japanese friends visiting Shanghai.

	Shin'ichiro Omori, head of the Japan-China Friendship Association Delegation, said at the reception: We are deeply moved by the warm welcome we have received during our visit to China. Tomoyoshi Murayama, head of the Japanese Drama Troupe, also spoke at the reception.

	Those present at the reception included: Liu Shuzhou, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai; Hu Juewen and Liu Jingji, Vice Chairmen of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Shi Ximin and Zhong Min, Vice Chairmen of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Zhang Chunqiao, President of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Vice Presidents Zhang Yaohui, Chen Yusun, Su Buqing, and Huang Zuolin; and well-known figures from Shanghai's literary and art circles, including Zhou Xinfang, Yu Ling, Zhang Junxiang, Xiong Foxi, Yu Zhenfei, Yuan Xuefen, and Zhang Ruifang.

	The reception took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere. The entire audience repeatedly rose to sing the songs "Tokyo-Beijing" and "Socialism is Good." Chinese and Japanese artists also performed songs and dances at the reception.

	On the 27th, the Japan-China Friendship Association Delegation left Shanghai for Hangzhou.

### 上海市人民委员会举行酒会  欢迎日中友协访华团和日本话剧团

1960-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    上海市人民委员会举行酒会
    欢迎日中友协访华团和日本话剧团
    据新华社上海27日电　上海市人民委员会26日晚上举行酒会，热烈欢迎正在上海访问的日中友好协会访华代表团和日本话剧团。
    上海市副市长曹荻秋在酒会上讲话。他对日本朋友们访问上海表示热烈欢迎。
    日中友好协会访华代表团团长大森真一郎在酒会上说：我们在中国访问期间受到的热烈欢迎，使我们深为感动。日本话剧团团长村山知义也在酒会上讲话。
    出席酒会的有：上海市副市长刘述周，政协上海市委员会副主席胡厥文、刘靖基，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会上海市分会副主席石西民、钟民，中国人民对外文化协会上海市分会会长张春桥，副会长张耀辉、陈虞孙、苏步青、黄佐临，以及上海文艺界知名人士周信芳、于伶、张骏祥、熊佛西、俞振飞、袁雪芬、张瑞芳等。
    酒会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。全场一再起立，高唱歌曲“东京—北京”和“社会主义好”。中国和日本的艺术家们还在酒会上表演了歌舞。
    27日，日中友协访华代表团离开上海去杭州访问。
