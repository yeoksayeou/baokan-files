Title: Burmese Military Friendship Delegation Leaves Beijing for Southern Visit
Author:
Date: 1960-10-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 13th: The Burmese Military Friendship Delegation of 29 people, headed by Brigadier General Maung Maung, and accompanied by Lieutenant General Yang Xiushan, representative of our Ministry of National Defense, Senior Colonel Zhu Kaiyin, Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense, and Senior Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché of our Embassy in Burma, left Beijing by plane this morning to visit various places in the south. Colonel Kyin Shain Mon, Military Attaché of the Burmese Embassy in China, also accompanied the delegation.

General Wang Shusheng, Vice Minister of National Defense, saw them off at the airport.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Senior General Xiao Hua, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Li Zuopeng, Lieutenant General Zhang Lingbin, Vice Admiral Zhou Xihan, Lieutenant General Tan Jiasu of the Air Force, Lieutenant General Liu Jinxuan and other senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and representatives of the land, sea and air forces stationed in Beijing. Members of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe presented flowers to the distinguished Burmese guests.

Hu Chengfang, Deputy Director of the First Asian Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, also saw them off at the airport.

U Tun Lin, Second Secretary of the Burmese Embassy in China, and military attachés of various countries' embassies in China saw them off at the airport.

After returning to Beijing on the 10th from a visit to Northeast China, the Burmese Military Friendship Delegation visited units and military academies of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 缅甸军事友好代表团离京去南方访问

1960-10-14
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸军事友好代表团离京去南方访问
    新华社13日讯　以貌貌准将为首的缅甸军事友好代表团一行二十九人，在我国国防部代表杨秀山中将、国防部外事处处长朱开印大校、我国驻缅甸大使馆武官蒋克定大校陪同下，今晨乘飞机离开北京，前往南方各地参观访问。缅甸驻我国大使馆武官景沙门上校也随同代表团前往。
    国防部副部长王树声大将到机场欢送。
    到机场欢送的，还有萧华上将、萧向荣中将、李作鹏中将、张令彬中将、周希汉海军中将、谭家述空军中将、刘金轩中将等中国人民解放军高级将领和陆、海、空军驻京部队代表。中国人民解放军文工团团员们向缅甸贵宾献了花。
    我国外交部第一亚洲司副司长胡成放也到机场欢送。
    缅甸驻我国大使馆二等秘书吴容林以及各国驻华大使馆武官到机场送行。
    缅甸军事友好代表团10日由我国东北参观访问回北京后，又参观访问了中国人民解放军部队和军事学校。
