Title: Takasaki Tatsunosuke Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-10-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 8th October: Mr. Takasaki Tatsunosuke, former Minister of International Trade and Industry of Japan, and his party of fourteen, who are visiting our country at the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai, arrived in Guangzhou by train from Shenzhen on the 8th.

Xiao Xiangqian, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, went to Shenzhen to welcome them.

When the guests arrived at the Guangzhou Railway Station, they were warmly welcomed by Jiao Linyi, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou, Luo Peiyuan, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Wang Jinchuan, Director of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and responsible persons from relevant departments. Young Pioneers presented flowers to Mr. Takasaki Tatsunosuke. On the evening of the 8th, Lin Qiangyun, Deputy Governor of Guangdong Province, hosted a banquet to welcome Mr. Takasaki Tatsunosuke and other guests.

### 高崎达之助到广州

1960-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    高崎达之助到广州
    新华社广州8日电　应周恩来总理邀请，前来我国进行友好访问的日本前通商产业相高崎达之助和随行人员一行十四人，8日从深圳乘火车抵达广州。
    中国人民外交学会副秘书长萧向前前往深圳迎接。
    客人们抵达广州车站时，受到广州市副市长焦林义，政协广州市委员会副主席罗培元，中国人民外交学会理事王锦川，以及有关方面负责人的热烈欢迎。少先队员向高崎达之助先生献了鲜花。8日晚间，广东省副省长林锵云设宴欢迎高崎达之助先生和其他客人。
