Title: Li Dequan Visits the Mongolian People's Republic
Author:
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: At the invitation of Gu·Tuwang, Minister of Health of the Mongolian People's Republic, a Chinese delegation of three people led by Minister of Health Li Dequan, left Beijing by plane today to Mongolia to participate in the 35th anniversary celebration of the establishment of the People's Health organs of the Mongolian People's Republic and to pay a friendly visit.
    He Biao and Cui Yitian, Vice Ministers of Health, and others saw them off at the airport.
    Toiv, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian Embassy in China, also saw them off at the airport.

### 李德全去蒙古人民共和国访问

1960-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    李德全去蒙古人民共和国访问
    新华社23日讯　应蒙古人民共和国卫生部长古·图旺的邀请，由卫生部部长李德全率领的中国代表团一行三人，今天乘飞机离开北京赴蒙古参加蒙古人民共和国人民卫生机关成立三十五周年庆祝活动，并进行友好访问。
    到机场送行的有卫生部副部长贺彪、崔义田等人。
    蒙古驻华大使馆临时代办托依夫也到机场送行。
