Title: Romanian Military Attaché in China Holds Reception to Celebrate the 16th Anniversary of the Founding of the Romanian People's Army
Author:
Date: 1960-10-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th. Lieutenant Colonel Nicu, the military attaché of the Romanian People's Republic Embassy in China, and his wife, held a reception tonight to celebrate the 16th anniversary of the founding of the Romanian People's Army.

General Wang Shusheng, Vice Minister of National Defense of our country; Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Senior Generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including General Fu Zhong, General Li Da, General Chen Bojun, Lieutenant General Yuan Shengping, Vice Admiral Du Yide, Lieutenant General Chang Qiankun, Lieutenant General Wu Xinquan, Major General Wang Xinggang, and representatives of officers from the People's Liberation Army ground, naval, and air forces stationed in Beijing were invited to attend the reception.

The atmosphere at the meeting was warm and friendly.

The Romanian Ambassador to China, Zaharescu, attended the reception.

Military attachés from various countries' embassies in China also attended the reception.

### 庆祝罗马尼亚人民军建军十六周年  罗驻华武官举行招待会

1960-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝罗马尼亚人民军建军十六周年
    罗驻华武官举行招待会
    新华社25日讯　罗马尼亚人民共和国驻中国大使馆武官尼古中校及夫人，今晚举行招待会，庆祝罗马尼亚人民军建军十六周年。
    我国国防部副部长王树声大将，外交部副部长罗贵波，中国人民解放军高级将领傅钟上将、李达上将、陈伯钧上将、袁升平中将、杜义德海军中将、常乾坤空军中将、吴信泉中将、王兴纲少将等，以及中国人民解放军陆、海、空军驻京部队军官代表应邀出席了招待会。
    会上，洋溢着热烈、友好的气氛。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使查哈勒斯库出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的，还有各国驻华大使馆武官。
