Title: Popova Receives Delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1960-10-01
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th. Popova, Chairman of the Council of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Vice-Chairman Zoyeva received the delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association headed by Xu Guangda on the morning of the 29th. The host and guests had a cordial conversation.
　　Zhang Yingwu, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, was also present at the reception.

### 波波娃接见中苏友协代表团

1960-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    波波娃接见中苏友协代表团
    新华社莫斯科30日电　苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会理事会主席波波娃和副主席佐耶娃29日上午接见了以许光达为首的中苏友协代表团，宾主间进行了亲切的交谈。
    接见时，中国驻苏联大使馆参赞张映吾也在座。
