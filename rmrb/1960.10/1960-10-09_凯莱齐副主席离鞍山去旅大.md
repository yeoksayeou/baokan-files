Title: Kelezi Departs Anshan for Lüda
Author:
Date: 1960-10-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 8th. The Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, headed by Abdyl Këllezi, Member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Vice Chairman of the Albanian Council of Ministers, and Chairman of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association, accompanied by Jiang Nanxiang, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and President of the Chinese-Albanian Friendship Association, Liu Mingfu, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission, and Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and East European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, concluded its visit to Anshan and arrived in Lüda by special train on the morning of the 8th. Albanian Ambassador to China, Piro Pllumbi, and Misto Treska, Chairman of the Albanian Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations, who was invited to visit China, and Shefqet Musaraj, Secretary-General of the Albanian Writers and Artists Association, also traveled on the same train, accompanied by Yu Menglong, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Commission.

The distinguished guests arrived in Anshan on the morning of the 7th and were warmly welcomed by Yang Shijie, First Secretary of the Anshan Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Wang Yixin, Deputy Mayor of Anshan, and others. After a short rest, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Wang Yixin, Deputy Mayor of Anshan, Lin Weisen, Deputy Manager of Ansteel, and others, visited Ansteel's iron smelting, steelmaking, large-scale, and seamless steel pipe factories, and were warmly welcomed by Ansteel's workers. Vice Chairman Këllezi and other distinguished guests praised Ansteel for the great achievements it has made under the correct leadership of the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao. In the afternoon of the 7th, the distinguished guests visited the magnetic separation workshop of the Ansteel Sintering Plant, the small blast furnace at the Cherry Orchard Mine, and the Tanggangzi Sanatorium. The distinguished guests praised the construction speed and production achievements of the small blast furnace. In the evening, Yang Shijie, First Secretary of the Anshan Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, hosted a banquet for the distinguished guests. That evening, the distinguished guests left Anshan by special train to visit Lüda.

On the morning of the 8th, the distinguished guests arrived in Lüda and were welcomed by Bai Qingjiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Lüda Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chen Shaojing, Alternate Secretary of the Municipal Committee Secretariat and Deputy Mayor of Lüda, Liu Zhuanlian, Lieutenant General in charge of the Lüda Garrison, and others, and received flowers from the children.

On the evening of the 8th, Guo Shushen, First Secretary of the Lüda Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, held a banquet to warmly entertain the distinguished Albanian guests. Comrade Guo Shushen spoke first at the banquet, expressing his pleasure and admiration for the great achievements made in socialist construction by the Albanian people under the leadership of the Albanian Labor Party headed by Comrade Hoxha, who are holding high the banner of Marxism-Leninism. He warmly congratulated the increasingly consolidated and developing fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Albanian peoples.

Vice Chairman Abdyl Këllezi also spoke at the banquet. He praised the brilliant achievements made by the Chinese people in all aspects under the leadership of the Party Central Committee headed by Comrade Mao Zedong. He said that the will of our two peoples is the same, the struggle is consistent, we resolutely oppose American imperialism, resolutely oppose modern revisionists—Yugoslavia, and resolutely defend the purity of Marxism-Leninism.

### 凯莱齐副主席离鞍山去旅大

1960-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    凯莱齐副主席离鞍山去旅大
    据新华社沈阳8日电　以阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、阿尔巴尼亚部长会议副主席、阿中友好协会主席阿卜杜勒·凯莱齐为首的阿中友好协会代表团由中共中央候补委员、中阿友好协会会长蒋南翔，国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫，外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清等陪同，结束了在鞍山的参观访问，8日上午由鞍山乘专车到达旅大。阿尔巴尼亚驻我国大使帕里夫蒂和应邀前来我国参观访问的阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会主席米斯托·特雷斯卡，阿尔巴尼亚作家艺术家协会秘书长谢夫卡特·穆萨莱在对外文委副司长于梦龙陪同下也同车前往。
    贵宾们是在7日上午到达鞍山的，受到中共鞍山市委第一书记杨士杰、鞍山市副市长王一新等人的热烈欢迎。在稍事休息后，贵宾们由鞍山市副市长王一新、鞍钢副经理林蔚森等人陪同，参观了鞍钢炼铁、炼钢、大型、无缝钢管等工厂，受到鞍钢职工的热情欢迎。凯莱齐副主席等贵宾，称赞鞍钢在党中央和毛主席的正确领导下所取得的巨大成就。7日下午，贵宾们参观了鞍钢烧结总厂磁选车间、樱桃园矿小高炉和汤岗子疗养院。贵宾们对小高炉的建设速度和生产成就表示赞扬。晚上，中共鞍山市委第一书记杨士杰设宴招待贵宾。当晚，贵宾们乘专车离开鞍山，去旅大访问。
    8日上午，贵宾们到达旅大，受到中共旅大市委书记处书记白清江，市委书记处候补书记、旅大市副市长陈少景，旅大驻军负责人刘转连中将等人的欢迎，并接受了少年儿童的献花。
    8日晚上，中共旅大市委第一书记郭述申举行宴会，热情招待阿尔巴尼亚贵宾。郭述申同志首先在宴会上讲话，对阿尔巴尼亚人民在以霍查同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的领导下，高举马克思列宁主义的旗帜，在社会主义建设中取得的巨大成就，表示高兴和钦佩。他热烈祝贺中阿两国人民兄弟般的友谊日益巩固和发展。
    阿卜杜勒·凯莱齐副主席也在宴会上讲了话。他赞扬中国人民在以毛泽东同志为首的党中央委员会的领导下，在各个方面取得的辉煌成就。他说，我们两国人民的意志是相同的，斗争是一致的，我们坚决反对美帝国主义，坚决反对现代修正主义者——南斯拉夫，坚决地保卫马克思列宁主义的纯洁性。
