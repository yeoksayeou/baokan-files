Title: Supporting the Signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty and Celebrating the Consolidation and Development of Sino-Burmese Friendship: People of All Ethnic Groups in Yunnan Hold a Celebration Rally. Yunnan Governor Yu Yichuan and Shan State Assembly Chairman of the Union of Burma, Sao Shwe Thaik, spoke at the meeting.
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 3rd October. A grand rally celebrating the signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty was held in Kunming on the evening of the 2nd, enthusiastically supporting the signing of the treaty. Yunnan Governor Yu Yichuan and Shan State Assembly Chairman of the Union of Burma, Sao Shwe Thaik, spoke at the meeting, both warmly congratulating the further consolidation and development of Sino-Burmese friendship.

Those attending the celebration rally included Yan Hongyan, First Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China; Yu Yichuan, Governor of Yunnan Province; Ma Jikong, Zhou Chiping, and Sun Yuting, Secretaries of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China; Guo Chao, Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Governor of Yunnan Province; Zhang Chong, Vice Governor of Yunnan Province; Zhao Zengyi, First Secretary of the Kunming Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; Pan Shuoduan, Mayor of Kunming; Lieutenant General Chen Kang and Major General Lu Ruilin, responsible persons of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming; Liu Linyuan and Wang Shaoyan, Vice Chairmen of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; heads of various government departments such as the President of the Provincial People's Court and the Chief Procurator of the Provincial People's Procuratorate; heads of local organizations of various democratic parties and people's organizations; as well as Gong Shou, Si Lasha, Si Hongsheng, and Duo Yong'an, Vice Governors of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture; Dao Dongting, Vice Governor of the Xishuangbanna Dai Autonomous Prefecture; Duan Chengjing, Deputy County Head of Lushui County, Nujiang Lisu Autonomous Prefecture; and more than 1,300 people from all ethnic groups and walks of life in the province and city.

Sao Shwe Thaik, Chairman of the Shan State Assembly of the Union of Burma, and U San Nyun, Chairman of the Kachin State Assembly, and all the distinguished guests of the Shan State and Kachin State Assembly delegation of the Union of Burma, who they led, were invited to attend the celebration rally. U Maung Maung Soe, Consul General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, and Ngo Tan, Vice Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming, were also invited to attend the celebration rally.

Governor Yu Yichuan said in his speech that we are especially pleased to have the opportunity to celebrate the signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty and hail the major development of Sino-Burmese friendly relations together with the Shan State and Kachin State Assembly delegation of the Union of Burma. On behalf of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee and the people of all ethnic groups in the province, I would like to extend warm congratulations to His Excellency Sao Shwe Thaik, His Excellency U San Nyun, and all Burmese friends who have brought us the profound friendship of the people of Shan State and Kachin State.

Sao Shwe Thaik, Chairman of the Shan State Assembly of the Union of Burma, also delivered a speech. He said that on October 1, 1960, Prime Minister U Nu of the Union of Burma and Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China successfully signed the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty in Beijing. We are very pleased to have the opportunity to express our congratulations to you all at this celebration rally for the signing of this treaty.

The speeches by Governor Yu Yichuan and Chairman Sao Shwe Thaik received a warm welcome from the entire audience.

After the speeches, a cultural performance began. The guests watched Peking Opera, Yunnan Opera, and other programs together.

On the evening of the 2nd, Yunnan Governor Yu Yichuan held a banquet to warmly celebrate the signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty.

Those attending the banquet included Yan Hongyan, First Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China; Zhang Chong and Guo Chao, Vice Governors of Yunnan Province; Zhao Zengyi, First Secretary of the Kunming Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; Pan Shuoduan, Mayor of Kunming; Lieutenant General Chen Kang and Major General Lu Ruilin, responsible persons of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming, and other responsible persons from the provincial and municipal Party, government, and military.

Sao Shwe Thaik, Chairman of the Shan State Assembly of the Union of Burma, and U San Nyun, Chairman of the Kachin State Assembly, and all the distinguished guests of the Shan State and Kachin State Assembly delegation of the Union of Burma, were all invited to attend the banquet.

U Maung Maung Soe, Consul General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, and Ngo Tan, Vice Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming, were also invited to attend the banquet.

The banquet was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere. The hosts and guests toasted each other, wishing the signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty success, wishing Sino-Burmese friendship longevity, and wishing the traditional friendship between the peoples of China and Burma eternal!

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 3rd October. ### 拥护中缅边界条约签订　祝贺中缅友谊巩固发展  云南各族各界举行庆祝大会  云南省省长于一川缅甸联邦掸邦议会主席苏辟在会上先后讲了话

1960-10-05
第4版()
专栏：

    拥护中缅边界条约签订　祝贺中缅友谊巩固发展
    云南各族各界举行庆祝大会
    云南省省长于一川缅甸联邦掸邦议会主席苏辟在会上先后讲了话
    据新华社昆明3日电　云南省、昆明市各族各界庆祝中缅边界条约签订大会2日晚在昆明隆重举行，热烈拥护中缅边界条约的签订。云南省省长于一川和缅甸联邦掸邦议会主席苏辟在会上先后讲了话，他们都热情地祝贺中缅友谊进一步巩固和发展。
    出席庆祝大会的，有中共云南省委第一书记阎红彦，云南省省长于一川，中共云南省委书记处书记马继孔、周赤萍、孙雨亭，中共云南省委书记处书记、云南省副省长郭超，云南省副省长张冲，中共昆明市委第一书记赵增益、昆明市市长潘朔端，解放军驻昆明部队负责人陈康中将、鲁瑞林少将，政协云南省委员会副主席刘林元、王少岩，和省人民法院院长、省人民检察院检察长等政府各部门负责人，各民主党派、各人民团体地方组织负责人，以及德宏傣族景颇族自治州副州长龚绶、司拉山、思鸿升、多永安，西双版纳傣族自治州副州长刀栋庭，怒江傈僳族自治州泸水县副县长段承经和省、市各族各界群众一千三百多人。
    缅甸联邦掸邦议会主席苏辟、克钦邦议会主席吴山纽和他们所率领的缅甸联邦掸邦、克钦邦议会代表团全体贵宾，应邀出席了庆祝大会。应邀出席庆祝大会的，还有缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭、越南民主共和国驻昆明副领事吴新等。
    于一川省长在会上讲话说，我们能有机会和缅甸联邦掸邦、克钦邦议会代表团一起庆贺中缅边界条约的签订，欢呼中缅友好关系的重大发展，感到格外高兴。我谨代表云南省人民委员会，和全省各族各界人民对给我们带来掸邦、克钦邦人民深厚友谊的苏辟阁下、吴山纽阁下和全体缅甸朋友们表示热烈的祝贺。
    缅甸联邦掸邦议会主席苏辟也讲了话。他说，1960年10月1日，缅甸联邦总理吴努和中华人民共和国总理周恩来在北京胜利地签订了中缅边界条约。在庆祝这个条约签订的大会上，我们能有机会向诸位表示祝贺，感到非常高兴。
    于一川省长和苏辟主席的讲话受到了全场热烈的欢迎。
    讲话完毕，开始文艺表演。宾主一起观看了京剧、滇剧等节目。
    2日晚上，云南省省长于一川举行宴会，热烈庆祝中缅边界条约的签订。
    出席宴会的，有中共云南省委第一书记阎红彦，云南省副省长张冲、郭超，中共昆明市委第一书记赵增益，昆明市市长潘朔端，中国人民解放军驻昆明部队负责人陈康中将、鲁瑞林少将等省、市党、政、军负责人。
    缅甸联邦掸邦议会主席苏辟、克钦邦议会主席吴山纽和缅甸联邦掸邦、克钦邦议会代表团全体贵宾，都应邀出席了宴会。
    缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭、越南民主共和国驻昆明副领事吴新等也应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。宾主互相敬酒，祝中缅两国边界条约的签订，祝中缅友好万岁！祝中缅两国人民传统友谊万古长青！
    新华社昆明3日电　中共云南省委第一书记阎红彦，2日下午会见了缅甸联邦掸邦议会主席苏辟、克钦邦议会主席吴山纽和缅甸联邦掸邦、克钦邦议会代表团全体贵宾，并和他们进行了亲切、友好的谈话。会见时在座的，有云南省省长于一川、副省长张冲、郭超；中国人民解放军驻昆明部队负责人陈康中将、鲁瑞林少将，和省人民委员会秘书长张子斋，省人委外事处处长李萍，省人委办公厅主任吴鸿宾等。会见时，缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭也在座。
