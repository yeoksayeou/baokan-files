Title: Premier Zhou Receives Mexican Cultural Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 13th: Premier Zhou Enlai received this afternoon the entire members of the Mexican cultural delegation headed by Emilio Portes Gil, former interim president of Mexico, and had a friendly conversation with them. Present at the reception were Chu Tunan, Zhang Zhixiang, Zhou Erfu, and others. (Attached picture)
Premier Zhou receives the Mexican cultural delegation. The picture shows a group photo taken during the meeting. Xinhua News Agency reporters Hou Bo and Du Xiuxian photographed.

### 周总理接见墨西哥文化代表团

1960-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见墨西哥文化代表团
    新华社13日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了以墨西哥前临时总统埃米略·波尔特斯·希耳为首的墨西哥文化代表团全体成员，并且进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的有楚图南、张致祥、周而复等。（附图片）
