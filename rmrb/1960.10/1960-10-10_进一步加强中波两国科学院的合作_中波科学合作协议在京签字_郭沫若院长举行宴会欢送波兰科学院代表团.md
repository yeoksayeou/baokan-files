Title: Further Strengthen Cooperation Between Chinese and Polish Academies of Sciences; Sino-Polish Scientific Cooperation Agreement Signed in Beijing; President Guo Moruo Holds Banquet to See Off Polish Academy of Sciences Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 9th. To further consolidate and develop scientific cooperation between the Chinese and Polish Academies of Sciences, the two sides held fraternal and friendly talks on the basis of existing good scientific mutual assistance and cooperation. The two sides reached a unanimous agreement, and this evening held a signing ceremony for the Scientific Cooperation Agreement between the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Polish Academy of Sciences, and the 1961 Implementation Plan for the Scientific Cooperation Agreement between the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Polish Academy of Sciences.
　　Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Tadeusz Kotarbinski, President of the Polish Academy of Sciences, signed the Scientific Cooperation Agreement and the 1961 Implementation Plan for the Scientific Cooperation Agreement respectively.
　　On the Chinese side, Zhang Jingfu, Zhu Kezhen, Wu Youxun, and Pei Lisheng, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Wu Heng, Deputy Director of the Science and Technology Commission, Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Xu Ming, Deputy Director of the Soviet and European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, attended the signing ceremony. On the Polish side, Jerzy Knothe, Polish Ambassador to China, and Counselor Dembnicki attended the signing ceremony.
　　Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet this evening to bid farewell to the delegation of the Polish Academy of Sciences headed by Tadeusz Kotarbinski.
　　Jerzy Knothe, the Polish Ambassador to China, Counselor Dembnicki, and others were invited to attend the banquet.
　　President Guo Moruo spoke at the banquet. He said that after fraternal and friendly talks between the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Polish Academy of Sciences, the two countries signed a scientific cooperation agreement and a 1961 implementation plan today, which will further strengthen the fraternal and creative cooperation between the Chinese and Polish Academies of Sciences. Guo Moruo said that we must make the greatest efforts to realize the various projects in the agreement, and at the same time, we also hope to develop this cooperation in the future. In this way, it will further strengthen the friendly cooperation between China and Poland, strengthen the strength of the entire socialist camp, strengthen the forces for defending world peace, and will be more conducive to achieving the victory of our common cause.
　　In his speech, President Kotarbinski reviewed the friendly and cooperative relations between the Polish and Chinese Academies of Sciences. He pointed out that the cooperation between the two academies is becoming increasingly close. He said that according to the scientific cooperation agreement and the 1961 implementation plan signed today, the scientific cooperation between our two academies will become more and more frequent in the future. We will not only cooperate in natural sciences, but also in all other sciences. Because this kind of cooperation based on the spirit of proletarian internationalism will further promote the friendship between the people of our two countries. Finally, he expressed his heartfelt thanks to the Chinese comrades for their kind reception.
　　During the banquet, Polish Ambassador to China Knothe, Wu Heng, Deputy Director of the Science and Technology Commission, Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and others raised their glasses to the close scientific cooperation between China and Poland, and to the fraternal friendship and unity between the people of China and Poland. Attending the banquet were Zhang Jingfu, Zhu Kezhen, Wu Youxun, and Pei Lisheng, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Xu Ming, Deputy Director of the Soviet and European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and famous figures from the capital's scientific community.

### 进一步加强中波两国科学院的合作  中波科学合作协议在京签字  郭沫若院长举行宴会欢送波兰科学院代表团

1960-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    进一步加强中波两国科学院的合作
    中波科学合作协议在京签字
    郭沫若院长举行宴会欢送波兰科学院代表团
    新华社9日讯　中国科学院和波兰科学院为了进一步巩固和发展两国科学院之间的科学合作，双方在已有的科学互助合作的良好基础上，进行了兄弟般的友好会谈，双方达成一致协议，并在今晚举行了中国科学院和波兰科学院科学合作协议、中国科学院和波兰科学院科学合作协议1961年执行计划的签字仪式。
    中国科学院院长郭沫若代表中国科学院，波兰科学院院长塔德乌什·科塔宾斯基代表波兰科学院，分别在科学合作协议和科学合作协议1961年执行计划上签字。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的有：中国科学院副院长张劲夫、竺可桢、吴有训、裴丽生，科学技术委员会副主任武衡，对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武，外交部苏欧司副司长徐明等。波兰方面参加签字仪式的有：波兰驻中国大使耶日·克诺泰，参赞邓布尼茨基等。
    新华社9日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若今晚举行宴会，欢送以塔德乌什·科塔宾斯基为首的波兰科学院代表团。
    波兰驻中国大使耶日·克诺泰、参赞邓布尼茨基等应邀出席了宴会。
    郭沫若院长在宴会上讲话。他说，中国科学院和波兰科学院经过兄弟般的友好会谈，今天签订了两国科学合作协议和1961年执行计划，这将进一步加强中波两国科学院之间的兄弟般的和创造性的合作。郭沫若说，我们一定要尽最大努力来实现协议中的各个项目，同时我们还希望今后发展这种合作。这样，将会更进一步加强中波两国之间的友好合作，加强整个社会主义阵营的力量，加强保卫世界和平的力量，将会更有助于取得我们共同事业的胜利。
    科塔宾斯基院长在讲话中回顾了波中两国科学院友好合作的关系。他指出，两院之间的合作正在越来越亲密。他说，根据今天所签订的科学合作协议和1961年执行计划来看，今后我们两院的科学合作将会越来越频繁。我们不仅要在自然科学方面合作，还要在所有其他科学方面进行合作。因为这种根据无产阶级国际主义精神的合作，将会进一步促进我们两国人民的友谊。最后他对中国同志们亲切的接待表示衷心感谢。
    席间，波兰驻华大使克诺泰，科学技术委员会副主任武衡，对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武等纷纷举杯，为中波两国科学密切合作，为中波两国人民兄弟般的友谊和团结干杯。出席宴会的有：中国科学院副院长张劲夫、竺可桢、吴有训、裴丽生，外交部苏欧司副司长徐明，和首都科学界著名人士等。
