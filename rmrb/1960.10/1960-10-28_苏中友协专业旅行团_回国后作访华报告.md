Title: Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Professional Travel Group Delivers Report on China Visit After Returning Home
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-28
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 25th: The Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association activist professional travel group, having returned from a visit to China, held a meeting with activists of the Moscow Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association on the afternoon of the 24th at the "Friendship House." They reported on their impressions of participating in the celebrations for the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and visiting various parts of China. The head of the travel group, Shmansky, the First Secretary of the Soviet Communist Party's Henan District Committee in Moscow, said at the meeting that the visit to China left an extremely deep and unforgettable impression on the members of the travel group. They personally witnessed the tremendous achievements made by the Chinese working people, united around the Chinese Communist Party, in socialist construction.

### 苏中友协专业旅行团  回国后作访华报告

1960-10-28
第6版()
专栏：

    苏中友协专业旅行团
    回国后作访华报告
    新华社莫斯科25日电　从中国访问归来的苏中友好协会积极分子专业旅行团，24日下午在“友谊之家”同莫斯科的苏中友好协会的积极分子举行了会见，报告了参加中华人民共和国成立十一周年庆典和在中国各地参观访问的观感。旅行团团长、苏共莫斯科市河南区区委第一书记什曼斯基在会见会上讲话说，在中国的参观访问给旅行团人员留下了极其深刻和难忘的印象。他们亲眼看到团结在中国共产党周围的中国劳动人民在社会主义建设中所取得的巨大成就。
