Title: General Ne Win Leaves Hangzhou; Brigadier General Aung Gyi Leaves Suzhou for Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 10th: The Chief of Staff of the Burmese Armed Forces, General Ne Win, and his wife, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, left Hangzhou by special train this evening after their visit to Hangzhou.

Those who saw them off at the station included Zhou Jianren, Governor of Zhejiang Province; Wu Xian, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province and Mayor of Hangzhou; Lieutenant General Qian Jun, Head of the Zhejiang Garrison; Zhang Shixiang, Deputy Mayor of Hangzhou; Yu Jiyi, Vice Chairman of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Hu Tianmin, Vice Chairman of the Hangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; and heads of various departments and people's organizations of the Zhejiang Provincial People's Committee.

This morning, General Ne Win and his wife, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, and Vice Governor Wu Xian, visited scenic spots and historical sites such as Longjing, Jiuxi, Hupao, and Liuhe Pagoda, and once again boated on the lake to enjoy the scenery. In the afternoon, Mrs. Ne Win and others visited the Hangzhou Du Jinsheng Silk Weaving Factory.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 9th: The Burmese Chief Representative of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Burmese Armed Forces, and representatives and advisors of the Burmese delegation, accompanied by Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Chief Representative of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, and representatives of the Chinese delegation, visited Wuxi and Suzhou for two days. They left Suzhou this evening for a visit to Shanghai.

In Wuxi and Suzhou, the distinguished guests visited the Wuxi Huishan Clay Figurine Factory, the Taihu Workers' Sanatorium, the Suzhou Dongwu Silk Weaving Factory, and the Suzhou Arts and Crafts Research Institute. They also toured the Taihu Lake Scenic Area, Huishan in Wuxi, and the famous Suzhou gardens such as the Humble Administrator's Garden, the Lingering Garden, and Tiger Hill. When the distinguished guests arrived and departed from Wuxi and Suzhou, Jiang Jian, Mayor of Wuxi, and Li Yunhua, Mayor of Suzhou, and other local government officials went to the station to greet and see them off.

### 奈温将军离杭州  昂季准将离苏州去上海

1960-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    奈温将军离杭州
    昂季准将离苏州去上海
    新华社杭州10日电　缅甸联邦国防军总参谋长奈温将军和夫人在杭州参观访问后，由陈毅副总理和夫人陪同，今晚乘专车离开杭州。
    到车站欢送的，有浙江省省长周建人，浙江省副省长、杭州市市长吴宪，浙江驻军负责人钱钧中将，杭州市副市长张世祥，政协浙江省委员会副主席余纪一，政协杭州市委员会副主席胡天民，以及浙江省人民委员会各部门、各人民团体的负责人。
    今天上午，奈温将军和夫人等由陈毅副总理和夫人、吴宪副省长陪同，游览了龙井、九溪、虎跑、六和塔等名胜古迹，并再度泛舟湖上，欣赏湖光山色。下午，奈温夫人等参观了杭州都锦生丝织厂。
    新华社南京9日电　中缅边界联合委员会缅甸首席代表、缅甸联邦国防军副总参谋长昂季准将和缅甸代表团的代表、顾问们，由中缅边界联合委员会中国首席代表姚仲明和中国代表团的代表们陪同，先后在无锡、苏州进行了两天的参观游览后，今晚离开苏州去上海访问。
    贵宾们在无锡、苏州先后参观了无锡惠山泥人工厂、太湖工人疗养院、苏州东吴丝织厂和苏州市工艺美术研究所，游览了无锡太湖风景区、惠山和苏州名胜拙政园、留园和虎丘等处。贵宾到达和离开无锡、苏州时，无锡市市长江坚、苏州市市长李芸华等当地政府负责人曾分别到车站迎送。
