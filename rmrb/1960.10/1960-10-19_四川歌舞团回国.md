Title: Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe Returns Home
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. The Chinese Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe, which had been on a tour of the United Arab Republic, Lebanon, and Albania, returned to Beijing by plane on the 16th, led by the head of the troupe, Zhang Tiesheng.

### 四川歌舞团回国

1960-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

    四川歌舞团回国
    新华社17日讯　赴阿拉伯联合共和国、黎巴嫩、阿尔巴尼亚访问演出的中国四川歌舞团在团长张铁生率领下，于16日乘飞机回到北京。
