Title: Ceylon Communist Party General Secretary Pieter Keuneman Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 11th -- Pieter Keuneman, General Secretary of the Ceylon Communist Party, and his wife, after participating in our country's 11th anniversary celebration and visiting Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and other places, took a train from Guangzhou yesterday morning via Hong Kong to return home.

Zhao Ziyang, Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Xue Yan, Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Zhang Gengsheng, Secretary-General of the Provincial Committee, and others went to the station to see them off.

### 锡共总书记彼得·克尼曼回国

1960-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    锡共总书记彼得·克尼曼回国
    新华社广州11日电　锡兰共产党总书记彼得·克尼曼和夫人，在参加了我国建国十一周年庆祝典礼和在北京、上海、广州等地参观访问后，已在昨天上午乘火车离开广州经香港回国。
    到车站欢送的，有中共广东省委书记赵紫阳，中共广州市委书记薛焰，省委秘书长张根生等。
