Title: Marshal Lin Biao Sends Congratulations on the Anniversary of the Founding of the Polish People's Army
Author:
Date: 1960-10-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 11th. Marshal Lin Biao, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, sent a telegram on the 10th to General Marian Spychalski, Minister of National Defense of the Polish People's Republic, congratulating him on the 17th anniversary of the founding of the Polish People's Army. The telegram reads as follows:
To Comrade General Marian Spychalski, Minister of National Defense of the Polish People's Republic,

  Respected Comrade Minister:

    On the occasion of the 17th anniversary of the founding of the Polish People's Army, on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and myself personally, I extend my warmest congratulations to you and all comrades of Polish People's Army.
    Under the leadership of the Polish United Workers' Party, the heroic Polish People's Army has made great achievements in political education, military training and national defense construction. I wish the Polish People's Army continued brilliant achievements in its work to continuously improve its combat effectiveness; I wish the Polish People's Army continued new contributions in defending its own country, shattering imperialist aggression and war conspiracies, and in safeguarding world peace.
           Lin Biao, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China
           Marshal of the People's Republic of China
           October 10, 1960, Beijing

### 林彪元帅电贺波人民军建军节

1960-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

    林彪元帅电贺波人民军建军节
    新华社11日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪元帅十日致电波兰人民共和国国防部长马里安·斯彼哈尔斯基上将，祝贺波兰人民军建军十七周年。电文如下：波兰人民共和国国防部长马里安·斯彼哈尔斯基上将
  同志敬爱的部长同志：
    欣逢波兰人民军建军十七周年之际，中国人民解放军全体指战员和我个人，谨向您和波兰人民军全体同志致以最热烈的祝贺。
    英勇的波兰人民军，在波兰统一工人党的领导下，无论在政治教育、军事训练和国防建设方面，都取得了很大的成就。祝波兰人民军在不断提高战斗威力的工作中，继续获得光辉的成就；祝波兰人民军在保卫自己祖国，粉碎帝国主义的侵略和战争阴谋，以及在捍卫世界和平的事业中，继续作出新的贡献。
           中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长
           中华人民共和国元帅  林彪
           一九六○年十月十日于北京
