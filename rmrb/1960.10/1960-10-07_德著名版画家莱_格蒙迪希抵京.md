Title: Renowned German Printmaker Lea Grundig Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 6th. According to the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Lea Grundig, the renowned printmaker from the German Democratic Republic, professor at the Dresden Academy of Fine Arts, and member of the Presidium of the German Association of Visual Artists, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Li Qun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Artists Association, and others were at the airport to welcome her.

### 德著名版画家莱·格蒙迪希抵京

1960-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    德著名版画家莱·格蒙迪希抵京
    新华社6日讯　根据中德文化合作协定1960年执行计划，德意志民主共和国著名版画家、德累斯顿美术学院教授、德造型艺术家协会主席团委员莱·格蒙迪希于今日下午乘飞机到达北京，前往机场欢迎的有中国美术家协会书记处书记力群等。
