Title: Iranian-Asian African Solidarity Committee Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The Iranian-Asian African Solidarity Committee delegation of four, invited by the Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by train today. The delegation members are: Mrs. Gesi Iran Etebar, Professor of Tehran University Abdul Hussein Khalili, Tehran Chamber of Commerce member Ali Asgar Pirzad, and Iranian Senior Economic Committee Advisor Dr. Alinagi Alikhani.

Greeting them at the station were Zhu Ziqi, Secretary-General of the Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee, Committee Member Wang Mingyuan, and Xiao Fangzhou, Secretary of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

### 伊朗亚非团结委员会访华代表团到京

1960-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    伊朗亚非团结委员会访华代表团到京
    新华社11日讯　应中国亚非团结委员会邀请前来我国访问的伊朗亚非团结委员会访华代表团一行四人，今天乘火车到达北京。代表团成员是：格西·伊兰·埃特巴尔夫人、德黑兰大学教授阿卜杜勒·侯赛因·哈利利、德黑兰商会会员阿里·阿斯加勒·皮尔扎德和伊朗高级经济委员会顾问阿里纳吉·阿里汉尼博士。
    到车站欢迎的有中国亚非团结委员会秘书长朱子奇、委员王明远，中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书萧方洲等。
