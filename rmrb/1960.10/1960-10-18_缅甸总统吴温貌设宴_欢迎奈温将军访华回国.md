Title: Burmese President U Win Maung Hosts Banquet Welcoming General Ne Win's Return from China
Author:
Date: 1960-10-18
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, October 16th: Burmese President U Win Maung held a banquet at the Presidential Residence this evening to welcome Burmese Chief of Staff General Ne Win and his wife. General Ne Win and his wife returned here by special plane this afternoon after attending the 11th anniversary celebrations of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the signing ceremony of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty in Beijing.

Burmese Prime Minister U Nu attended the banquet. Also attending the banquet were Chief Justice of the Supreme Court U Myint Thein, Chairman of the Chamber of Nationalities Sao Khun Kyi, Speaker of the Chamber of Deputies Mann Basaing, Minister of Foreign Affairs and Minister for Shan State Affairs Sao Khun Cho, Minister for Trade Development and Civil Supplies U Thun, Attorney General U Yan Aung, and Deputy Chief of Staff (Air Force) Brigadier General Clift.

Also present at the banquet were Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, Lei Yang, and diplomatic envoys of various countries to Burma.

The head of the Chinese Government Trade Delegation, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin, and members of the delegation, who are currently visiting Burma, also attended the banquet.

### 缅甸总统吴温貌设宴  欢迎奈温将军访华回国

1960-10-18
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸总统吴温貌设宴
    欢迎奈温将军访华回国
    新华社仰光16日电　缅甸联邦总统吴温貌今天晚上在总统府举行宴会欢迎缅甸联邦国防军总参谋长奈温将军和夫人。奈温将军和夫人是在参加了中华人民共和国建国十一周年庆祝典礼和在北京举行的中缅边界条约签字仪式后，今天下午乘专机回到这里的。
    缅甸联邦总理吴努参加了宴会。参加宴会的还有最高法院首席法官吴敏登，民族院议长藻昆基，代表院议长曼巴赛，外交部长和掸邦事务部长藻昆卓，贸易发展和民用物资供应部长吴敦，检察总长吴延昂，国防军副总参谋长（空军）克利夫特准将。
    出席宴会的，还有中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬和各国驻缅甸外交使节。
    正在缅甸访问的中国政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长雷任民和代表团团员也出席了宴会。
