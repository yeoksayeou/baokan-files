Title: Our Military Friendship Delegation Leaves Tirana
Author:
Date: 1960-10-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 26th - The Chinese military friendship delegation led by General Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, left Tirana by plane on the 26th after a three-day visit to Albania.

Albanian Deputy Minister of National Defense, Lieutenant General Dume, and other senior generals, as well as the Chinese Ambassador to Albania, Luo Shigao, and others were at the airport to see them off.

The delegation came to visit Albania at the invitation of the Albanian Ministry of National Defense on its way back to China after visiting the United Arab Republic.

On the eve of their departure, the Albanian Ministry of National Defense hosted a banquet for the delegation. Albanian Minister of National Defense, General Baluku, and General Zhang Zongxun repeatedly toasted the friendship between the two peoples. The banquet proceeded in a cordial atmosphere.

### 我军事友好代表团离地拉那

1960-10-28
第6版()
专栏：

    我军事友好代表团离地拉那
    新华社地拉那26日电　由中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊上将率领的中国军事友好代表团在阿尔巴尼亚进行了三天参观访问后，已经在26日乘飞机离开地拉那。
    阿尔巴尼亚国防部副部长杜麦中将等高级将领和中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高等都到机场欢送。
    代表团是在访问阿拉伯联合共和国之后，在回国途中应阿尔巴尼亚国防部的邀请前来访问的。
    临行前夕，阿尔巴尼亚国防部设宴招待代表团，阿尔巴尼亚国防部部长巴卢库上将和张宗逊上将频频为两国人民的友谊祝酒。宴会在亲切气氛中进行。
