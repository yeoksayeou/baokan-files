Title: USSR, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria and Vietnam Recognize the Republic of Nigeria
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Khrushchev, the President of Czechoslovakia, Novotny, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria, Yugov, and the Premier of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Pham Van Dong, have respectively sent telegrams to the Federal Prime Minister of Nigeria, expressing recognition of Nigeria as an independent nation.

### 苏、捷、保、越四国承认尼日利亚共和国

1960-10-10
第6版()
专栏：

    苏、捷、保、越四国承认尼日利亚共和国
    据新华社讯　苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫、捷克斯洛伐克总统诺沃提尼、保加利亚部长会议主席于哥夫和越南民主共和国总理范文同，分别打电报给尼日利亚联邦总理，表示承认尼日利亚为独立国家。
