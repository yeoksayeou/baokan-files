Title: Sino-Romanian Table Tennis Teams Hold First Friendly Match in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-06
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd. The Romanian men's and women's table tennis teams held their first friendly match in China tonight in Beijing against the Chinese women's table tennis team. As a result, the Chinese team won both the men's and women's team events, with the women's team winning 3-1 and the men's team winning 5-1.

### 中罗乒乓球队在京举行首次友谊赛

1960-10-06
第5版()
专栏：

    中罗乒乓球队在京举行首次友谊赛
    新华社3日讯　罗马尼亚男女乒乓球队今晚在北京同中国女乒乓球队举行访华第一场友谊比赛，结果中国队在男女团体赛中都获得胜利，女子队的比数是三比一，男子队是五比一。
