Title: Indonesian Women's Delegation Leaves Kunming for Home

Author:
Date: 1960-10-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 21st - The ten-member delegation of the Indonesian Women's Movement Association, led by Madam Suwarti, left Kunming by plane today to return home after visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation.

The delegation visited Beijing, Xi'an, Harbin, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou and other places.

### 印度尼西亚妇女代表团  离昆明回国

1960-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚妇女代表团
    离昆明回国
    新华社昆明21日电　应全国妇联邀请来我国访问的印度尼西亚妇女运动协会代表团一行十人，在团长苏娃尔蒂夫人率领下，今天乘飞机离开昆明回国。
    代表团曾在北京、西安、哈尔滨、上海、杭州、广州等地参观游览。
