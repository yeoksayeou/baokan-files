Title: Chinese Women Athletes Gao Lianzhen, Wang Suzhen, and Zhao Chengying Break World Women's Parachuting Record, Creating an Excellent Average Distance of 4.85 Meters from the Target Center in Daytime 600-meter Group Combined Parachuting
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, 5th. Recently, in the friendship competition of parachuting athletes from five provinces and autonomous regions held in Chengdu, China's young parachuting athletes once again broke the world record.

On September 22nd, in the women's daytime 600-meter group combined parachuting event, Gao Lianzhen (20 years old), Wang Suzhen (19 years old), and Zhao Chengying (17 years old) from Sichuan Province broke the world record of 5.546 meters set by Du Jufang, Li Wenxiu, and Wu Yonglan of Anhui Province in Hefei on July 21st of this year with an excellent average distance of 4.85 meters from the target center. Their individual distances from the target center were: Gao 3.15 meters, Wang 3.04 meters, and Zhao 8.37 meters.

This is the fourth time that Chinese women parachuting athletes have broken this world record. On November 5, 1958, People's Liberation Army athletes Li Shuhua, Li Shuhui, and Cui Xiuying set the first world record of 12.39 meters; on July 4 of this year, Anhui Province parachuting athletes Wu Yonglan, Tang Hexiu, and Rong Zhixuan set a new record of 8.73 meters. On July 21st and September 22nd, Chinese athletes broke this world record twice in succession.

In this parachuting friendship competition of five provinces and autonomous regions—Sichuan, Yunnan, Xinjiang, Ningxia, and Qinghai—athletes also broke several national records. Among them, Chen Zhonglong, an 18-year-old middle school student from Chongqing, broke the national record of 4.07 meters set by Liu Junsheng in December 1958 in the men's daytime 600-meter individual combined parachuting event with an average distance of 1.585 meters from the target center. His first parachute jump landed 3.17 meters from the target center; when he landed on his second jump, his foot landed right on the red dot in the center of the target.

In addition, Jin Mingzhe (20 years old) from Xinjiang broke the national record of 3.40 meters held by Feng Diange in the men's daytime 1000-meter individual precision parachuting event with an average distance of 2.85 meters from the target center; Su Huiru, Dai Shaomin, and Liang Xiuzhen from Yunnan broke the national record of 7 meters held by Gao Ming, Wang Suzhen, and Zhao Chengying in the women's daytime 600-meter group precision parachuting event with an average distance of 6.01 meters from the target center.

When three male athletes from Sichuan Province, Liu Dexin, Luo Guoqing, and Zhang Baoqi, were performing the men's daytime 1500-meter group combined parachuting, they created an average distance of 2.69 meters from the target center, which exceeded the world record of 2.95 meters created by Chinese athletes Liu Jiawen, Liu Santai, and Qu Yanguo in December of last year, but did not meet the requirements for breaking the world record (the new world record must be at least 10% better than the old record). ### 我国女运动员高联珍、王素珍和赵成英  打破世界女子跳伞纪录  在日间六百米集体综合跳伞中，创平均距靶心四米八十五优异成绩

1960-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    我国女运动员高联珍、王素珍和赵成英
    打破世界女子跳伞纪录
    在日间六百米集体综合跳伞中，创平均距靶心四米八十五优异成绩
    新华社成都5日电　最近在成都举行的五省、自治区跳伞运动员友谊赛中，我国年轻的跳伞运动员又一次打破了世界纪录。
    在9月22日举行的女子日间六百米集体综合跳伞中，四川省二十岁的高联珍、十九岁的王素珍和十七岁的赵成英，以平均距靶心四米八十五的优异成绩，打破了安徽省运动员杜菊芳、李文秀、武永兰今年7月21日在合肥创造的五米五十四点六的世界纪录。她们三人个人与靶心的距离：高是三米十五、王是三米零四、赵是八米三十七。
    这是我国女子跳伞运动员第四次打破这项世界纪录。1958年11月5日，解放军选手李淑花、李淑慧、崔秀英第一次创造的世界纪录是十二米三十九；今年7月4日，安徽省跳伞运动员武永兰、唐荷秀、荣志玄又创造了八米七十三的新纪录。7月21日和9月22日，我国运动员又先后两次打破了这项世界纪录。
    在这次四川、云南、新疆、宁夏、青海五省、自治区跳伞友谊赛中，运动员们还打破了几项全国纪录。其中重庆十八岁的中学生陈仲龙在男子日间六百米个人综合跳伞中，以平均距靶心一米五十八点五的成绩，打破了刘俊圣在1958年12月创造的四米零七的全国纪录。他第一次跳伞降落在离靶心三米十七的地方；第二次跳伞着陆时，他的脚掌刚好踏在靶正中的红点上。
    另外，新疆二十岁的金明哲以平均距靶心二米八十五的成绩，打破了冯殿阁保持的男子日间一千米个人定点跳伞三米四十的全国纪录；云南的苏惠如、戴绍敏、梁秀珍以平均距靶心六米零一的成绩，打破了高明、王素珍、赵成英保持的女子日间六百米集体定点跳伞七米的全国纪录。
    四川省三名男运动员刘德欣、骆国庆、张保齐在进行男子日间一千五百米集体综合跳伞时，创造了平均距靶心二米六十九的成绩，虽然超过了我国运动员刘加文、柳三泰、曲延国在去年12月创造的二米九十五的世界纪录，但没能达到打破世界纪录的要求（新世界纪录至少要比旧纪录好10％）。（附图片）
