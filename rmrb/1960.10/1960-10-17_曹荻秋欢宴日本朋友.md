Title: Cao Diqiu Hosts Banquet for Japanese Friends
Author:
Date: 1960-10-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 16th – Shanghai Vice Mayor Cao Diqiu hosted a banquet this evening, warmly welcoming the delegation from the Japanese National Council to Prevent Revision of the Security Treaty.

　　Vice Mayor Cao Diqiu spoke at the banquet, extending a warm welcome to the Japanese friends on behalf of the people of Shanghai. He said that while the delegation was visiting China, Mr. Inejiro Asanuma, Chairman of the Japan Socialist Party, was unfortunately murdered by Japanese fascists. The people of Shanghai, like the people of the whole country, expressed strong indignation at the murder of Mr. Inejiro Asanuma. Cao Diqiu said that we firmly believe that the Japanese people will turn this anger into strength and lead the struggle against the US-Japanese reactionaries to a new stage.

　　Deputy head of the delegation, Bunjiro Sano, said in his speech that the Japanese people are preparing for the 23rd unified action. The Japanese people have dealt a certain blow to Japanese monopoly capitalists and US imperialism, but the struggle is only just beginning, and the Japanese people must persist in the struggle until final victory.

### 曹荻秋欢宴日本朋友

1960-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

    曹荻秋欢宴日本朋友
    据新华社上海16日电　上海市副市长曹荻秋今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎日本阻止修改安全条约国民会议代表团。
    曹荻秋副市长在宴会上讲话，他代表上海人民对日本朋友们表示热烈欢迎。他说，正当代表团在中国访问期间，日本社会党委员长浅沼稻次郎先生不幸被日本法西斯杀害，上海人民和全国人民一样，对浅沼稻次郎先生被害表示强烈的愤慨。曹荻秋说，我们深信，日本人民一定会把这种愤怒变成力量，把反对美日反动派的斗争，引向一个新的阶段。
    代表团副团长佐野文一郎在讲话中说，日本人民正在准备第二十三次统一行动。日本人民给了日本垄断资本家和美帝国主义一定的打击，但是斗争还仅仅是开始，日本人民一定要坚持斗争到最后胜利。
    出席今晚宴会的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会上海分会副主席钟民，中国人民对外文化协会上海市分会副会长张耀辉及工会、妇联和青联的负责人。
    宴会前，上海市副市长曹荻秋接见了日本朋友，并进行了亲切的交谈。
