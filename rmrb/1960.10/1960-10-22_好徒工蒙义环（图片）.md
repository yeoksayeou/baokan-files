Title: Good Apprentice Meng Yihuan (Picture)
Author: Feng Jinting
Date: 1960-10-22
Page: 2

    Good Apprentice Meng Yihuan
    Meng Yihuan, an apprentice at the No. 8 Workshop of Harbin Electric Machinery Plant, has diligently studied and practiced, rapidly improving his ideological and technical levels. In the two years since entering the factory, he has carried out 152 innovation projects and achieved automation of the spherical milling machine. He completes his tasks ahead of schedule every month, and in terms of workload, he is already working on the second quarter of 1967. He also pays great attention to saving raw materials in production. In the past two years, he has used scrap heads and waste materials collected during his spare time to make more than 13,300 parts of various sizes, saving the country more than 3,000 kilograms of steel and 1,200 kilograms of iron.
      Xinhua News Agency reporter Feng Jinting photographed

### 好徒工蒙义环（图片）
冯锦亭
1960-10-22
第2版()
专栏：

    好徒工蒙义环
    哈尔滨电机厂第八车间徒工蒙义环勤学苦练，迅速提高了思想水平和技术水平。入厂两年来，他搞了一百五十二项革新项目，实现了车元球自动化。他月月提前完成任务，按工作量计算，现在已经在做1967年第二季度的活了。他在生产中也十分注意节约原料材料。两年来，利用工余时间拣回来的料头以及边材废料，共做出了一万三千三百多个大小部件，为国家节约了三千多公斤钢、一千二百公斤铁。
      新华社记者　冯锦亭摄
