Title: Prime Minister Abbas Visits Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. This morning, Prime Minister of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, Abbas Ferhat, and other distinguished guests of the Algerian Provisional Government delegation, accompanied by Vice Premier He Long, visited the Beijing Workers' Stadium and the Beijing Railway Station.

Huang Zhong, Deputy Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, and Yu Chujie, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Railways, received and guided the distinguished guests during their visits. (Attached photo)

### 阿巴斯总理在北京参观

1960-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    阿巴斯总理在北京参观
    新华社30日讯　今天上午，阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理阿巴斯·费尔哈特和阿尔及利亚临时政府代表团的其他贵宾，由贺龙副总理陪同，参观了北京工人体育场和北京车站。
    体育运动委员会副主任黄中和铁道部办公厅副主任喻楚杰，先后接待并引导贵宾们参观。（附图片）
