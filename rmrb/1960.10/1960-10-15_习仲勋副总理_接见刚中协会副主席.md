Title: Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun Receives Vice Chairman of the Congo-China Association
Author:
Date: 1960-10-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 14th: Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun received Antoine Ligobe Mandango, Vice Chairman of the Congo-China Association, who is currently visiting China, this morning and had a friendly conversation with him.

Present at the reception were Qu Wu, Vice President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, and Wu Qing, Secretary-General.

On the 13th, Zhang Zhixiang, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and Standing Director of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, met with Mandango. (Photo attached)

### 习仲勋副总理  接见刚中协会副主席

1960-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

    习仲勋副总理
    接见刚中协会副主席
    新华社14日讯　习仲勋副总理今天上午接见了正在我国访问的刚果中国协会副主席安托万·利戈贝·芒丹戈，并同他进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中国非洲人民友好协会副会长屈武、总干事吴青。
    13日，中国人民对外文化协会副会长、中国非洲人民友好协会常务理事张致祥会见了芒丹戈。（附图片）
    习仲勋副总理和刚中协会副主席安托万·利戈贝·芒丹戈亲切交谈。
