Song of the Chollima
Zhang Yongmei

　　Beijing and Pyongyang are close at heart, I see the "Chollima" galloping,
　　High mountains and vast seas cannot separate us, I hear the call of the Seoul people's struggle.
　　Heroic Korea, heroic people,
　　Struggle, victory, struggle!
　　Growing stronger in struggle, advancing in struggle. ...
　　I feel like I am walking in Gangwon Province again, I feel like I am walking on the Han River again,
　　Ice and snow cannot block the road ahead, the firelight burns the stars red in the sky.
　　Korean sons and daughters rise up together, carrying guns and marching forward,
　　Heroes arrive, fire makes way, warm breath melts a thousand layers of ice.
　　Only through struggle can there be happiness, only through struggle can there be peace!
　　The confidence of victory is tempered a thousand times, the banner of battle is planted ever more firmly!
　　The brutal invading army is a clown, heroic Korea is a giant!
　　The power of justice is indestructible, the people's hands will determine the universe!
　　The history of battle is a war drum, forever inspiring the people of the world:
　　Imperialism is bound to fail, the mad dog fears the dog-beating stick!
　　Korean history is ironclad proof, victory will always belong to us:
　　Oppressed people must be liberated, the "Chollima" must run to the peak of Communism!

### 千里马之歌
张永枚
1960-10-08
第8版()
专栏：

    千里马之歌
    张永枚北京平壤心贴心，我看见“千里马”在飞奔，高山大海隔不断，我听见汉城人民斗争的呼
    声。英雄的朝鲜，英雄的人民，斗争，胜利，斗争！在斗争中壮大，在斗争中前进。……我像又走在江原道，我像又走在汉江滨，冰雪封不住前进路，火光烧红天上星。朝鲜儿女齐奋起，端枪迈步向前进，英雄来了火让路，呵气暖化冰千层。只有斗争才有幸福，只有斗争才有和平！胜利的信心千锤百炼，战斗的旗杆越扎越稳！残暴的侵略军是小丑，英雄的朝鲜是巨人！正义的力量不可摧，人民双手定乾坤！战斗的历史是战鼓，永远振奋天下人：帝国主义必失败，疯狗怕的是打狗棍！朝鲜的历史是铁证，胜利永远属于我们：受压迫的人民一定要解放，“千里马”要跑上共产主义
  的顶峰！
