Title: Chinese Modern National Painting Exhibition Held in Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1960-10-03
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, October 1st: A Chinese modern national painting exhibition opened in Ulaanbaatar, the capital of the Mongolian People's Republic, on September 30th. The exhibition was sponsored by the Mongolian Artists Association.

    R. Lhamsuren, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Luvsanrabdan, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and First Secretary of the Ulaanbaatar Municipal Committee, Chimed, Minister of Propaganda of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association delegation headed by Cao Diqiu, Xie Fusheng, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, and diplomatic officials from various countries' embassies in Mongolia attended the opening ceremony.

    The exhibition displayed a total of 84 works by Chinese national painters, including works by the late national painter Qi Baishi. These works reflect the scenes of socialist construction in China's industry, agriculture, and transportation, as well as China's magnificent rivers and mountains, arousing great interest among the audience.

    Lhamsuren, Luvsanrabdan and others signed inscriptions after the visit saying that the Chinese modern national painting exhibition held during the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Week reflects the beautiful scenes of the Chinese people's production and life. The Mongolian people have gained a lot of inspiration. The cultural cooperation between Mongolia and China will develop day by day.

### 蒙古举行中国现代国画展览

1960-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    蒙古举行中国现代国画展览
    新华社乌兰巴托1日电　中国现代国画展览9月30日在蒙古人民共和国首都乌兰巴托开幕。展览会是由蒙古美术家协会主办的。
    蒙古人民共和国部长会议副主席拉姆苏伦、蒙古人民革命党中央政治局候补委员兼乌兰巴托市委第一书记鲁布桑拉布丹、蒙古人民革命党中央宣传部长奇米德，以曹荻秋为首的中蒙友好协会代表团、中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生和各国驻蒙古大使馆的外交官员等出席了开幕式。
    展览会展出了中国国画家们的作品共八十四幅，其中有已故国画家齐白石的作品。这些作品反映了中国人民在工业、农业、交通运输等方面社会主义建设的场面和中国的壮丽河山，引起了观众很大的兴趣。
    拉姆苏伦、鲁布桑拉布丹等人在参观后签名题词说，在蒙中友好旬期间举行中国现代国画展览，这个画展反映了中国人民生产、生活的美好情景。蒙古人民得到了很多启发。蒙中两国的文化合作将日益发展。
