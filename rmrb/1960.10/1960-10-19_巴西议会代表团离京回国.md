Title: Brazilian Parliamentary Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-10-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Brazilian Parliamentary Delegation of eighteen members, headed by Brazilian Senator and Chairman of the Senate Finance Committee, Gaspar Velloso, left Beijing by plane today to return home after concluding their visit to our country.

### 巴西议会代表团离京回国

1960-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

    巴西议会代表团离京回国
    新华社18日讯　以巴西参议员、参议院财政委员会主席加斯帕·贝略索为首的巴西议会代表团一行十八人，结束了在我国的参观访问以后，在今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
