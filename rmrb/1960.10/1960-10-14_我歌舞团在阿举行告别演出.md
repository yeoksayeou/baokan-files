Title: Our Song and Dance Troupe Holds Farewell Performance in Albania
Author:
Date: 1960-10-14
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 12th. The Chinese Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe grandly held its farewell performance on the evening of the 11th at the Opera and Ballet Theater in Tirana. Haxhi Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, and other party and government leaders attended and watched the performance. Luo Shigao, the Chinese Ambassador to Albania, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Albania also attended and watched the performance.
	On the evening of the 11th, the Albanian Ministry of Education and Culture and the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association hosted a banquet for the song and dance troupe.

### 我歌舞团在阿举行告别演出

1960-10-14
第6版()
专栏：

    我歌舞团在阿举行告别演出
    据新华社地拉那12日电　中国四川歌舞团11日晚在地拉那歌剧和芭蕾舞剧院隆重举行了告别演出。阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希等党政领导人出席观看了演出。中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高和各国驻阿尔巴尼亚外交使节也出席观看。
    11日晚，阿尔巴尼亚教育和文化部，阿中友协设宴招待歌舞团。
