Title: Japanese Peace Committee and Asian-African Solidarity Committee Delegations Leave for Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-10-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 25th. The Japanese Peace Committee delegation led by Kenjuro Yanagida and the Japanese Asian-African Solidarity Committee delegation led by Tokumatsu Sakamoto, left Shanghai for Guangzhou by train on the 24th, after concluding their visit to Shanghai. Zhong Min, Vice-Chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Zhang Junxiang, member of the Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee in Shanghai, and Guo Jian, Director of the Shanghai Women's Federation, and others went to the station to see them off.

After arriving in Shanghai from Nanjing on the 21st, the guests visited the Shanghai Industrial Exhibition, the Malu People's Commune, Zhangmiao Street, Jiaotong University, the Shanghai Workers' Cultural Palace, the Lu Xun Memorial Hall, and the Children's Palace.

### 日和平委员会和亚非团结委员会代表团去广州

1960-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    日和平委员会和亚非团结委员会代表团去广州
    新华社上海25日电　以柳田谦十郎为首的日本和平委员会代表团和以坂本德松为首的日本亚非团结委员会代表团，结束了在上海的访问以后，24日乘火车离开上海去广州。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会上海市分会副主席钟民、中国亚非团结委员会在上海委员张骏祥、上海市妇女联合会主任郭建等到车站欢送。
    客人们在21日从南京到达上海后，参观了上海市工业展览会、马陆人民公社、张庙街、交通大学、上海市工人文化宫、鲁迅纪念馆、少年宫等。
