Title: Ten Chinese Artists Send Telegram to Mrs. Siqueiros Protesting the Arrest of Siqueiros by Mexican Authorities
Author:
Date: 1960-10-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th - Ten Chinese artists recently sent a telegram to the wife of the famous Mexican painter David Alfaro Siqueiros, expressing their indignation at the unreasonable arrest of Siqueiros by the Mexican police authorities.

The telegram from the ten artists stated: "We are shocked and indignant to learn that our close friend David Alfaro Siqueiros has been unreasonably arrested by the police authorities at the instigation of U.S. imperialism. David Alfaro Siqueiros is an outstanding contemporary muralist from Mexico, familiar and beloved by the Chinese people and people of all countries around the world. He has made outstanding contributions to the development of progressive art in Mexico and the world. We fully sympathize with and support the just demands of the Mexican people and artists to end the imprisonment of David Alfaro Siqueiros. We wish him an early release. We hereby send this telegram to express our heartfelt condolences to you and please convey our highest respects to Mr. Siqueiros."

The ten artists are: Wu Zuoren, Liu Kaiqu, Cai Ruohong, Ye Qianyu, Lei Guiyuan, Wang Chaowen, Zhang Ding, Li Hua, Guan Shanyue, and Huang Xinbo.

### 十位中国美术家致电西克罗斯夫人  抗议墨西哥当局逮捕西克罗斯

1960-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    十位中国美术家致电西克罗斯夫人
    抗议墨西哥当局逮捕西克罗斯
    新华社12日讯　中国的十位美术家最近打电报给墨西哥著名画家戴维·阿耳法罗·西克罗斯的夫人，对墨西哥警察当局无理逮捕西克罗斯表示愤慨。
    十位美术家的电报说：“惊悉我们的亲密朋友戴维·阿耳法罗·西克罗斯由于美帝国主义的指使，被警察当局无理逮捕，我们感到非常愤慨。戴维·阿耳法罗·西克罗斯是墨西哥当代杰出的壁画家，他是中国人民和全世界各国人民所熟悉和热爱的。他对墨西哥和世界进步艺术的发展作过杰出的贡献。我们完全同情和支持墨西哥人民和艺术家的停止对戴维·阿耳法罗·西克罗斯监禁的正义要求。愿他早日获得自由。谨此致电表示我们对你的衷心慰问，并请向西克罗斯先生转致崇高的敬意。”
    这十位美术家是：吴作人、刘开渠、蔡若虹、叶浅予、雷圭元、王朝闻、张仃、李桦、关山月、黄心波。
