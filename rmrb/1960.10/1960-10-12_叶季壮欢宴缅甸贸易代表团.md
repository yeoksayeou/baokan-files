Title: Ye Jizhuang Hosts a Banquet for the Burmese Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 11th: Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang hosted a banquet tonight to bid farewell to the Burmese Trade Delegation headed by Burmese Member of Parliament U Mya. The Burmese Trade Delegation is about to leave Beijing to visit various parts of China.

    Ye Jizhuang said at the banquet that the Burmese Trade Delegation's visit to China is not only of great significance for the development of economic and trade relations between China and Burma, but also makes a beneficial contribution to consolidating the friendly relations between the two countries.

    Speaking about the trade talks held between China and Burma, Ye Jizhuang said that both sides exchanged views fully on further expanding trade exchanges between the two countries. Our talks were friendly and satisfactory, and achieved our common expected goals. Ye Jizhuang expressed his gratitude to the Burmese friends for their friendly cooperation in the negotiations.

    In his speech, delegation leader U Mya warmly expressed his hope for the development of friendly and trade relations between Burma and China. He said that the friendship between Burma and China will continue to develop and be consolidated on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, and the trade relations between Burma and China will also be further developed on this good foundation.

    U Mya spoke about his impressions of visiting China and praised China's great achievements in industry and agriculture, saying that they were very happy about this. U Mya said that the Burmese Trade Delegation expressed its heartfelt gratitude to the Chinese hosts for their warm reception.

    Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyaw Winn, was invited to attend the banquet.

    Among those present at the banquet were Vice Ministers of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin and Lu Xuzhang, Deputy Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Shen Ping, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Burma Peng Jinbo, and heads of relevant bureaus and import and export corporations of the Ministry of Foreign Trade.

### 叶季壮欢宴缅甸贸易代表团

1960-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    叶季壮欢宴缅甸贸易代表团
    新华社11日讯　对外贸易部部长叶季壮今晚举行宴会，欢送以缅甸国会议员吴妙为首的缅甸贸易代表团。缅甸贸易代表团即将离开北京去我国各地参观访问。
    叶季壮在宴会上讲话说，缅甸贸易代表团这次来我国访问，不仅对中缅两国经济贸易关系的发展具有重要的意义，而且对巩固两国的友好关系作出了有益的贡献。
    叶季壮在谈到中缅双方举行的贸易会谈时说，我们双方就进一步扩大两国之间的贸易往来充分地交换了意见。我们的会谈是友好的，令人满意的，达到了我们共同预期的目的。叶季壮对缅甸朋友们在谈判中的友好合作精神表示感谢。
    吴妙团长在讲话中热情表达了发展缅中两国友好关系和贸易关系的愿望。他说，缅中两国的友谊将在和平共处五项原则的基础上不断发展和巩固下去，缅中两国的贸易关系也将在这种良好的基础上获得进一步的发展。
    吴妙团长谈到访问我国的观感时，盛赞中国在工业、农业等方面取得了巨大成就，他说他们因此感到十分高兴。吴妙团长说，缅甸贸易代表团对中国主人的热情接待表示衷心感谢。
    缅甸驻中国大使叫温应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会作陪的，有对外贸易部副部长雷任民、卢绪章，外交部第一亚洲司副司长沈平，我国驻缅甸大使馆商务参赞彭金波，以及对外贸易部各有关局和进出口专业公司的负责人。
