Title: Burmese Minister of Trade and Supply U Tun Hosts a Banquet for Chinese Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-22
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, October 20th: Burmese Minister of Trade Development and Civil Supplies, U Tun, hosted a banquet here on the evening of the 20th to welcome the Chinese government trade delegation headed by Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade.

Among those present at the banquet were U Hla Kyawe, Parliamentary Secretary of the Burmese Ministry of Trade Development, U Hla and U Wan Tin, Deputy Directors of the State Agricultural Marketing Board, other senior officials, and prominent figures from Burmese trade circles.

Lei Yang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, and the embassy's counselors also attended the banquet.

The banquet proceeded in a very warm and friendly atmosphere. The hosts and guests frequently toasted the "Paukphaw" (fraternal) friendship and further development of trade between the two countries.

### 缅甸贸易和物资供应部长吴敦  设宴招待我贸易代表团

1960-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸贸易和物资供应部长吴敦
    设宴招待我贸易代表团
    据新华社仰光20日电　缅甸贸易发展和民用物资供应部长吴敦，20日晚上在这里设宴招待以对外贸易部副部长雷任民为首的中国政府贸易代表团。
    出席宴会的有缅甸贸易发展部驻议会秘书吴拉觉，国营农产品贸易局副局长吴拉和吴旺丁和其他高级官员，以及缅甸贸易界著名人士。
    中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬和大使馆的参赞也出席了宴会。
    宴会在十分热烈和友好的气氛中进行。宾主双方频频为两国之间“胞波”般的友谊和贸易的进一步发展干杯。
