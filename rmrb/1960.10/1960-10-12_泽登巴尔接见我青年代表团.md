Title: Tsedenbal Receives Chinese Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, October 11th. Tsedenbal, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers today, met with the Chinese Youth Delegation headed by Zhang Yu this morning.

Present at the meeting were Pulebzhab, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League, and others. Xie Fusheng, Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, was also present.

In recent days, the Chinese Youth Delegation visited factories, schools, and agricultural and pastoral farms in Ulaanbaatar, the capital of Mongolia, and in pastoral areas. Last night, they held a get-together with the Ulaanbaatar Municipal Committee of the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League.

### 泽登巴尔接见我青年代表团

1960-10-12
第6版()
专栏：

    泽登巴尔接见我青年代表团
    新华社乌兰巴托11日电　蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记、部长会议主席泽登巴尔，今天上午接见了以张玉为首的中国青年代表团。
    接见时在座的有蒙古革命青年团中央委员会第一书记普勒布扎布等人。中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生也在座。
    这些天来，中国青年代表团在蒙古首都乌兰巴托和牧区访问了工厂、学校和农牧场，昨天晚上和蒙古革命青年团乌兰巴托市委会举行了联欢。
