Title: The Soviet Union and the Somali Republic Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1960-10-06
Page: 5
　
    Xinhua News Agency, September 30th: TASS reports: The Soviet Union and the Somali Republic have decided to establish diplomatic relations and exchange diplomatic envoys at the ambassadorial level.

### 苏联和索马里共和国决定建立外交关系

1960-10-06
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联和索马里共和国决定建立外交关系
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社9月30日讯：苏联和索马里共和国已决定建立外交关系，并互派大使级外交代表。
