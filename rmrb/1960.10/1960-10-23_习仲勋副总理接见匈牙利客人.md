Title: Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun Receives Hungarian Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-10-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: Vice Premier of the State Council Xi Zhongxun received all members of the Hungarian People's Republic Railway Delegation this afternoon, headed by Csanádi György, First Deputy Minister of Transportation and Postal Affairs, Doctor of Science. Present at the reception were Lü Zhengcao, Vice Minister of Railways, Liu Jianzhang, Vice Minister of Railways, Xu Jianji, Deputy Director of the International Transportation Bureau of the Ministry of Railways, and Yu Zhan, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 习仲勋副总理接见匈牙利客人

1960-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    习仲勋副总理接见匈牙利客人
    新华社22日讯　国务院副总理习仲勋今天下午接见了以匈牙利交通邮电部第一副部长、科学技术博士恰纳吉·吉叶尔基为首的匈牙利人民共和国铁路代表团全体成员。接见时在座的，有铁道部副部长吕正操、刘建章，铁道部国际联运局副局长徐建基，外交部苏联东欧司副司长余湛等。
