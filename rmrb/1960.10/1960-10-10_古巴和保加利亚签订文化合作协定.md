Title: Cuba and Bulgaria Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-10-10
Page: 5
　
Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 8th. The governments of Cuba and Bulgaria signed a cultural cooperation agreement this afternoon.

The agreement stipulates that the contracting parties will strengthen mutual assistance and cooperation in education, science, cultural activities, publishing, and other fields.

In order to specifically implement this agreement, both parties have also stipulated the key points of annual cooperation in education, science, and culture.

The agreement was signed by Cuban Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Carlos Olivares and the head of the Bulgarian trade delegation, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Lushchev Aframov.

### 古巴和保加利亚签订文化合作协定

1960-10-10
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴和保加利亚签订文化合作协定
    新华社哈瓦那8日电　古巴和保加利亚政府今天下午签订了文化合作协定。
    协定规定，订约双方加强教育、科学、文化活动以及出版等方面的互助合作。
    为了具体履行这个协定，双方还规定了教育、科学和文化方面每年的合作要点。
    这个协定是由古巴外交部副部长卡洛斯·奥利瓦雷斯和保加利亚贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部第一副部长卢什切尔·阿弗拉莫夫签字的。
