Title: Guinean President Travels to New York to Attend UN General Assembly
Author:
Date: 1960-10-06
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, 4th. Guinean President Sékou Touré left Conakry by plane this afternoon to attend the United Nations General Assembly in New York.
　　Some time ago, the Guinean government already sent a delegation to attend the United Nations General Assembly.

### 几内亚总统赴纽约出席联合国大会

1960-10-06
第6版()
专栏：

    几内亚总统赴纽约出席联合国大会
　　新华社科纳克里4日电　几内亚总统塞古·杜尔今天下午乘飞机离开科纳克里，前往纽约参加联合国大会。
    前不久，几内亚政府已经派出了一个出席联合国大会的代表团。
