Title: Chairman Mao and Chairman Liu Enjoy Fireworks with Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-10-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 1st: Chairman Mao Zedong and other Party and state leaders, including Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Lin Biao, and Dong Biwu, watched the colorful festive fireworks and the grand gathering of hundreds of thousands of people in Tiananmen Square today on the Tiananmen Rostrum, together with distinguished guests such as Prime Minister U Nu and General Ne Win, Prime Minister Abbas, and Vice-Chairman Kellezi.

Also invited to watch the fireworks and mass celebrations on the Tiananmen Rostrum were distinguished guests from our fraternal and friendly neighbor Burma, heads of delegations and prominent figures from the Soviet Union and other socialist countries, Asia, Africa, Latin America, Western Europe, North America, and Australia, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to China.

Tonight, the Tiananmen Rostrum, like the square, was filled with festive joy. There were wonderful artistic performances on both sides of the rostrum. From the rostrum, the entire scene of the festive night was in full view. Colorful fireworks, brilliant lights, melodious music, joyful crowds, and wonderful songs and dances turned Tiananmen Square into a sea of joy.

### 毛主席刘主席和贵宾共赏焰火

1960-10-02
第5版()
专栏：

    毛主席刘主席和贵宾共赏焰火
    新华社1日讯　毛泽东主席和刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、陈云、林彪、董必武等党和国家的领导人今天在天安门城楼上，同吴努总理和奈温将军、阿巴斯总理、凯莱齐副主席等贵宾一起，观看了节日的多彩焰火和天安门广场上几十万群众的大联欢。
    应邀在天安门城楼上观看焰火和群众大联欢的，还有来自我国亲戚般的友好邻邦缅甸的贵宾，来自苏联和其他社会主义国家，亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲、西欧、北美和澳州各国的代表团负责人和著名人士，以及各国驻中国的使节。
    今晚，天安门城楼同广场上一样地充满节日的欢乐。在城楼两侧有精彩的艺术表演。从城楼眺望，节日夜晚的景象尽收眼底。多彩的礼花、辉煌的灯火、悠扬的乐曲、欢乐的人群和精彩的歌舞，使天安门广场成了一片欢乐的海洋。
