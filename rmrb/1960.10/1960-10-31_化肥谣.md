Title: Fertilizer Rhyme
Author: Yuan Zhian
Date: 1960-10-31
Page: 4

Fertilizer Rhyme

　　Chemical fertilizer, bright and shiny.
　　Vegetables and melons grow lush and green when they eat it,
　　Cotton seedlings spit out silver when they eat it,
　　Rice seedlings sprout gold when they eat it.
　　Golden and silver mountains unfold in the wind,
　　Mountains rise to pierce the ninth heaven.
　　(Yuan Zhian)

### 化肥谣
袁志安
1960-10-31
第4版()
专栏：

    化肥谣
    化学肥，亮晶晶。
    瓜菜吃了绿成荫，
    棉苗吃了吐白银，
    秧棵吃了冒黄金。
    金山银山迎风展，
    山山冲破九霄云。
      （袁志安）
