Title: Chinese Ambassadors to Foreign Countries Hold National Day Receptions
Author:
Date: 1960-10-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 2nd - Zeng Tao, Director of the Xinhua News Agency Havana Branch, and Zou Siyi, Deputy Head of the Chinese Trade Delegation, held a reception at the Hotel Habana Libre in Havana, the capital of Cuba, on the evening of October 1st to celebrate the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.
    Cuban Prime Minister Fidel Castro attended the reception. Also present were Minister of Revolutionary Armed Forces Raúl Castro, President of the National Bank Guevara, Major William Gálvez, Navy Commander Castineira, Minister of Education Art, Minister of Economy Boti, Minister of Trade Bonilla, Minister of Transport Curbelo Morales, Minister of Labor Sánchez, Minister of Finance Díaz Astaraín, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs Olivares, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Llopart, President of the Cuban Foreign Trade Bank Mora, Mayor of Havana Lliánusa, and other senior government officials.
    President Dorticos also sent a representative – Presidential Military Assistant Leon – to attend the reception.
    Prime Minister Castro warmly congratulated China's National Day at the reception and sent a flower basket. He said that he was very pleased to attend this reception held in a cordial and brotherly atmosphere.
    Also attending the reception were diplomatic envoys or trade representatives of socialist countries and other countries to Cuba.
    Also attending the reception were First Secretary of the Cuban People's Socialist Party Blas Roca, member of the Political Bureau of the People's Socialist Party and Director of "Hoy" Carlos Rafael Rodríguez, liaison officer of the "July 26th Movement" Aragones, Chairman of the Cuban Socialist Youth League Isidoro Malmierca, Chairman of the Cuban-China Friendship Association Baldomero Álvarez Ríos, organizational secretary of the Cuban Workers' Confederation Soto, poet Guillén, leader of Casa de las Américas Santa María, Chairman of the Cuban Women's Federation Espín, and friends from cultural and journalistic circles.
    Former Guatemalan President Arbenz and other prominent figures from Latin American countries currently in Havana also attended the reception.
    Youth representatives from China, Vietnam, North Korea and other countries participating in the construction of the Camilo Cienfuegos school city also attended the reception.
    Representatives of overseas Chinese also attended the reception.
    The reception, attended by about 400 guests from all walks of life, took place in a friendly and cordial atmosphere. The guests enthusiastically sang the Chinese song "The East is Red."
        Our Ambassador to Afghanistan
    Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, 2nd - Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan Hao Ting held a reception here on the evening of October 1st to celebrate the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.
    Among those present at the reception were Afghan Prime Minister Sardar Mohammed Daoud, First Deputy Prime Minister Ali Mohammed, King's Uncle Marshal Shah Wali Khan Ghazi, Speakers and Deputy Speakers of the National Assembly, cabinet ministers, the Chief of General Staff, the Commander of the Central Army Corps, the Commander of the Air Force, other senior officials and generals, and journalists, totaling more than 200 people. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Afghanistan also attended the reception.
    The reception took place in a friendly and warm atmosphere. The Afghan Prime Minister and other distinguished guests frequently raised their glasses to congratulate China's National Day.
                Our Ambassador to Sudan
    Xinhua News Agency, Khartoum, 2nd - Chinese Ambassador to Sudan Wang Yutian held a reception to celebrate the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Approximately 300 guests attended the reception. Sudanese Minister of Agriculture, Irrigation and Transportation Makboul Al-Amin represented Chairman of the Supreme Council of the Armed Forces Abbud at the reception. Also attending the reception were Deputy Commander-in-Chief of the Sudanese Armed Forces, Minister of State and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs Major General Hassan Beshir Nasr, Minister of Public Works and Mineral Resources Major General Rida Farid, Minister of Interior Brigadier General Bahari, Minister of Finance and Economy Abdel Magid Ahmed, Chief Justice Abu Kanat, Commander of the Omdurman Army Headquarters Brigadier General Mustafa Ahmadi Kamil, and other senior government officials and senior military officers. Diplomatic envoys from various countries also attended the reception. The reception took place in a friendly atmosphere.
                Our Chargé d'Affaires in the United Kingdom
    Xinhua News Agency, London, 2nd - Huan Xiang, Chargé d'Affaires of the People's Republic of China in the United Kingdom, held a reception today for overseas Chinese to celebrate the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.
    More than 700 people attended the reception, coming from London, Liverpool, Birmingham, Newcastle and Cardiff, some even from distant Jamaica. They included overseas Chinese students, seamen, freelancers, and industrialists and businessmen.
    In his speech, Huan Xiang pointed out that China had made great achievements in many fields such as industry, agriculture and culture in the past year. He said that in order to build the motherland, much more effort was still needed.
    Huan Xiang emphasized that Taiwan must be liberated and the conspiracy of "two Chinas" must be resolutely opposed. His words won loud applause from the guests.

### 我国驻外使节举行国庆招待会

1960-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    我国驻外使节举行国庆招待会
          我驻古巴代表
    新华社哈瓦那2日电　新华社哈瓦那分社社长曾涛和中国贸易代表团副团长邹斯颐1日晚为庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年，在古巴首都哈瓦那的自由饭店举行了招待会。
    古巴总理菲德尔·卡斯特罗出席了招待会。出席招待会的还有革命武装部部长劳尔·卡斯特罗、国家银行行长格瓦拉、维利亚姆·加耳维斯少校、海军司令卡斯蒂内拉、教育部长阿特、经济部长博蒂、贸易部长博尼利亚、交通部长库尔维洛·莫拉莱斯、劳工部长桑切斯、财政部长迪亚斯·阿斯塔拉因、代理外交部长奥利瓦雷斯、外交部副部长略姆帕尔特、古巴对外贸易银行行长莫拉、哈瓦那市长利亚努萨和政府其他高级官员。
    多尔蒂科斯总统也派代表——总统军事助理莱昂出席了招待会。
    卡斯特罗总理在招待会上热烈地祝贺中国国庆，并且送了花篮。他表示，他出席这个在亲切和兄弟般的气氛中举行的招待会，感到非常愉快。
    出席招待会的还有社会主义国家和其他国家驻古巴的使节或贸易代表。
    出席招待会的还有古巴人民社会党总书记布拉斯·罗加、人民社会党政治局委员、“今日报”社长卡洛斯·拉斐尔·罗德里格斯、“七月二十六日运动”联络人阿拉戈内斯、古巴社会主义青年联盟主席伊西多罗·马耳米耶尔卡、古巴—中国友好协会主席巴耳多梅罗·阿耳瓦雷斯·里奥斯、古巴工人联合会组织书记索托、诗人纪廉、美洲之家领导人圣马尔亚、古巴妇女联合会主席埃斯平和文化界及新闻界的朋友们。
    前危地马拉总统阿本斯和现在在哈瓦那的其他拉丁美洲国家的著名人士也出席了招待会。
    参加建筑卡米洛·西恩富戈斯学校城的中国、越南、朝鲜等国的青年代表也出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的还有华侨代表。
    这个约有四百位各界客人参加的招待会在友好、亲切的气氛中进行。客人们热情地高唱中国歌曲“东方红”。
        我驻阿富汗大使
    新华社喀布尔2日电　中国驻阿富汗大使郝汀10月1日晚间在这里举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年。
    出席招待会的有阿富汗首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德，第一副首相阿里·穆罕默德，王叔沙阿·华利汗·加齐元帅，国民议会的议长和副议长，内阁大臣们，总参谋长，中央军团司令，空军司令，其他高级官员和将领以及新闻记者等二百多人。各国驻阿富汗外交使节也出席了招待会。
    招待会是在友好热烈的气氛中进行的。阿富汗首相和其他贵宾们频频举杯祝贺中国国庆。
                我驻苏丹大使
    新华社喀土穆2日电　中国驻苏丹大使王雨田举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年。大约三百位客人出席了招待会。苏丹农业、灌溉和交通部长马克布勒·阿明代表武装部队最高委员会主席阿布德出席了招待会。出席招待会的还有苏丹武装部队副总司令、国务部长和代理外交部长哈桑·贝希尔·纳斯尔少将，公共工程和矿产部长里达·法里德少将，内政部长巴哈里准将，财政和经济部长阿卜杜勒·马吉德·艾哈迈德，首席法官阿布·卡纳特，献迪陆军总部司令穆斯塔法·艾哈迈迪·卡米尔准将，以及其他政府高级官员和高级军官。各国外交使节也出席了招待会。招待会在友好的气氛中进行。
                我驻英国代办
    新华社伦敦2日电　中华人民共和国驻英国代办宦乡今天为华侨举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年。
    出席招待会的有七百多人，他们来自伦敦、利物浦、伯明翰、纽卡斯尔和加尔的夫，其中有些人甚至来自遥远的牙买加。他们包括华侨学生、海员、自由职业者和工商业者。
    宦乡在讲话中指出，过去一年里，中国在工业、农业和文化等许多方面获得了很大的成就。他说，为了把祖国建设起来，还需要作很大的努力。
    宦乡强调指出，台湾必须解放，“两个中国”的阴谋必须坚决反对。他的话赢得了客人们响亮的掌声。
