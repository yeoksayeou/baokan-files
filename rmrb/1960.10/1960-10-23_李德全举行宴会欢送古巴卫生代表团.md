Title: Li Dequan Holds Banquet to Bid Farewell to Cuban Health Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd. Minister of Public Health Li Dequan held a banquet this evening to bid farewell to all members of the Cuban Health Delegation, led by Cuban Minister of Public Health José Ramón Machado Ventura.

Attending the banquet were Vice Ministers of Public Health He Biao and Cui Yitian, Vice President of the Chinese Association for Friendship with Latin America Ji Chaoding, and President of the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences Huang Jia驷, among others.

After the banquet, Minister Li Dequan, on behalf of the Ministry of Public Health of the People's Republic of China, presented the Ministry of Public Health of the Republic of Cuba with sixteen sets of various medical devices and instruments to commemorate the increased development of friendship between the medical and health circles of China and Cuba.

### 李德全举行宴会欢送古巴卫生代表团

1960-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    李德全举行宴会欢送古巴卫生代表团
    新华社22日讯　卫生部部长李德全今晚举行宴会欢送以古巴卫生部长何塞·罗曼·马查多·本图拉为首的古巴卫生代表团全体团员。
    出席宴会作陪的，有卫生部副部长贺彪、崔义田，中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长冀朝鼎，中国医学科学院院长黄家驷等。
    宴会后，李德全部长代表中华人民共和国卫生部赠给古巴共和国卫生部各种医疗器械和仪器共十六套，以志中国和古巴医药卫生界友谊的增进和发展。
