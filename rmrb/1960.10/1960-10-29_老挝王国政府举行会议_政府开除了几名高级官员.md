Title: Lao Kingdom Government Holds Meeting; Several Senior Officials Dismissed
Author:
Date: 1960-10-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 27th. According to a broadcast from Vientiane Radio today, the Prime Minister's Office announced that the Lao Kingdom Government, chaired by Prime Minister Prince Souvanna Phouma, held a meeting at the Prime Minister's Office at 10:00 AM on October 26th. Quinim Pholsena, head of the Kingdom Government's delegation negotiating with the Lao Patriotic Front Party, reported on the results of the negotiations conducted in the past few days at this meeting.

Vientiane Radio also stated that, according to Decree No. 258 promulgated on October 19, 1960, Kampan Panya (former Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Phoui Sananikone government), a ninth-grade senior official, and Phouphi Phanareth (former Secretary of State for Finance and Economy of the Phoui Sananikone government and member of Phoumi Nosavan's People's Social Party), a third-grade senior official, were dismissed for "evading tasks." According to the broadcast, Thongsavath Vongsavanthong, Governor of Bassac Province, a sixth-grade senior official, was removed from his post under Decree No. 262 due to "serious errors in the performance of his duties."

### 老挝王国政府举行会议  政府开除了几名高级官员

1960-10-29
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝王国政府举行会议
    政府开除了几名高级官员
    新华社河内27日电　据万象电台今天广播的首相府通告说，老挝王国政府在首相富马亲王主持下10月26日上午十时在首相府举行会议。同老挝爱国战线党谈判的王国政府代表团团长贵宁·奔舍那，在这次会议上报告了几天来进行谈判的结果。
    万象电台又说，根据1960年10月19日颁布的二五八号法令，第九级高级职员坎番·班雅（前松萨尼特政府外交部长）和第三级高级职员丰菲·法纳雷特（前松萨尼特政府财政经济国务秘书、富米·诺萨万的人民社会党党员）因“逃避任务”而被开除。据广播说，根据二六二号法令，第六级高级职员巴色省省长通沙瓦·冯沙万汤由于“在执行任务时犯严重错误”而被撤除职务。
