Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet for Moroccan Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd October - Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet on the 2nd to welcome the Moroccan Kingdom Cultural Delegation headed by Muhammad al-Aziz Lahbabi.

Among those present at the banquet were Xu Pingyu, Vice Minister of Culture and Vice President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association.

### 张奚若欢宴摩洛哥文化代表团

1960-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    张奚若欢宴摩洛哥文化代表团
    新华社2日讯　对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若，2日设宴欢迎以穆罕黔德·阿齐兹·拉赫巴比为首的摩洛哥王国文化代表团。
    出席宴会作陪的，有文化部副部长、中国非洲人民友好协会副会长徐平羽等。
