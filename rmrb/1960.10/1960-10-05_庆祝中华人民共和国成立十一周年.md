Title: Celebrating the 11th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1960-10-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, October 4th – Chairman Mao Zedong received a telegram from Sukarno congratulating him on China's National Day. The text of the telegram is as follows:
Chairman Mao Zedong, Central Committee of the Communist Party of China,
I congratulate you and the Chinese people on National Day and wish you happiness.
                                                      Sukarno
                                        October 2, 1960, New York

Xinhua News Agency, Caracas, October 3rd – The Central Committee of the Communist Party of Venezuela unanimously passed a congratulatory telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China at its 25th plenary session held on October 1st to celebrate the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The text of the telegram is as follows:

At the opening ceremony of the 25th plenary session, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Venezuela would like to extend the fraternal greetings of the Venezuelan Communists to you and the government of the People's Republic of China, which is victoriously leading socialist construction in your country. Today, October 1st, the Venezuelan Communists feel more closely united than ever before with the people who paved the way for the victory of the great Chinese revolution.

For our party, the lessons of the Chinese Revolution are an invaluable contribution to enriching our political line. Therefore, dear comrades, we once again express our sincere admiration to you.

Long live the Chinese Revolution!
Long live the Communist Party of China!
Long live proletarian internationalism!

### 庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年

1960-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年
    苏加诺的贺电
    新华社4日讯　毛泽东主席收到了苏加诺发来的祝贺我国国庆的电报，电文如下：中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东
    我向您和中国人民祝贺国庆并祝幸福。
                                                      苏加诺
                                        1960年10月2日于纽约
    委内瑞拉共产党中央委员会的贺电
    新华社加拉加斯3日电　委内瑞拉共产党中央委员会在10月1日举行的第二十五次全体会议上，首先一致通过一项发给中国共产党中央委员会的贺电，祝贺中华人民共和国成立十一周年。电文如下：
    委内瑞拉共产党中央委员会在第二十五次全体会议的开幕式上，谨向您们和在您们国家中胜利领导社会主义建设的中华人民共和国政府致以委内瑞拉共产党人的兄弟般的祝贺。今天，10月1日，委内瑞拉共产党人感到比过去任何时候都更加紧密地同为伟大的中国革命开辟了胜利道路的人们团结在一起。
    对我们的党来说，中国革命的教益在丰富我们的政治路线方面是一个不可估价的贡献，因此，亲爱的同志们，我们再一次地向您们表示我们真诚的钦佩。
    中国革命万岁！
    中国共产党万岁！
    无产阶级国际主义万岁！
