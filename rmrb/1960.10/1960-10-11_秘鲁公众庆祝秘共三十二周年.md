Title: Peruvian Public Celebrates the 32nd Anniversary of the Peruvian Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Lima, 8th. The Peruvian public held a mass meeting at the José Carlos Mariátegui College to celebrate the 32nd anniversary of the founding of the Peruvian Communist Party. Parliamentarians and representatives from various democratic parties in Peru, as well as other prominent figures, attended the meeting.

    The Peruvian Communist Party was founded on October 7, 1928. Jorge del Prado, a member of the Central Committee of the Peruvian Communist Party, briefly described the party's history of development at the meeting. Raúl Acosta, the secretary of the party, analyzed the current political situation at the meeting and pointed out that the party is faithfully implementing the national, democratic, unified, and revolutionary line. At the end of the meeting, the participants sang the Internationale.

### 秘鲁公众庆祝秘共三十二周年

1960-10-11
第5版()
专栏：

    秘鲁公众庆祝秘共三十二周年
    据新华社利马8日电　秘鲁公众在何塞·卡洛斯·马里亚特基学院举行了群众大会，庆祝秘鲁共产党建党三十二周年。秘鲁各民主党派的议员和代表以及其他知名人士出席了这次大会。
    秘鲁共产党是在1928年10月7日成立的。秘鲁共产党中央委员豪尔赫·德尔普拉多在会上简要地叙述了党的发展历史，党的书记劳尔·阿科斯塔在会上分析目前的政治局势时指出，党是忠诚地执行着民族、民主、统一和革命的方针。在大会结束时，参加大会的人高唱国际歌。
