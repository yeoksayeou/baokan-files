Title: Aung Gyi Visits Nanjing, Shanghai and Other Places

Author: 
Date: 1960-10-06
Page: 3

    Aung Gyi Visits Nanjing, Shanghai and Other Places
    Xinhua News Agency, October 5th: Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the chief representative of the Burmese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Burmese Federal Defence Forces, and the representatives and advisors of the Burmese delegation, left Beijing by special train this evening to visit Nanjing, Shanghai and other places, after the successful conclusion of the Fourth Meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee.
    Yao Zhongming, the chief representative of the Chinese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, and the representatives of the Chinese delegation accompanied them.
    Among those who saw them off at the station were Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; General Zhang Aiping, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense; Hu Chengfang, Deputy Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Zhao Puchu, Vice President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association. The Burmese Ambassador to China, U Kyaw Winn, and diplomatic officials from the embassy also saw them off at the station.
    Before leaving, the chief representatives of both sides signed the documents adopted at the Fourth Meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee.

### 昂季去南京上海等地访问

1960-10-06
第3版()
专栏：

    昂季去南京上海等地访问
    新华社5日讯　中缅边界联合委员会缅甸首席代表、缅甸联邦国防军副总参谋长昂季准将和缅甸代表团的代表、顾问们，在中缅边界联合委员会第四次会议胜利结束以后，于今晚乘专车离开北京前往南京、上海等地参观访问。
    中缅边界联合委员会中国首席代表姚仲明和中国代表团的代表们陪同前往。
    到车站欢送的，有外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张爱萍上将，国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将，外交部第一亚洲司副司长胡成放，中缅友好协会副会长赵朴初等。缅甸驻中国大使叫温和大使馆外交官员也到车站送行。
    临行前，双方首席代表在中缅边界联合委员会第四次会议所通过的文件上签了字。
