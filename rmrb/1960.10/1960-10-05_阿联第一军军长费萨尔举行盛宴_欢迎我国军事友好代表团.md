Title: First Army Commander of the UAR, Faisal, Holds Grand Banquet to Welcome Chinese Military Friendship Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 2nd – The People’s Republic of China Military Friendship Delegation, led by Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People’s Liberation Army, General Zhang Zongxun, arrived in Damascus today from Cairo after concluding its visit to the Egyptian region.

    The Commander of the First Army of the United Arab Republic, Jamal Faisal, held a grand banquet at noon today to welcome the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation, which is currently visiting here.

    In his speech at the banquet, Faisal said, "The Arab people will never forget the lofty attitude displayed by the friendly people of China towards the Arabs during the tripartite aggression against Egypt in 1956."

    General Zhang Zongxun expressed his gratitude to the host for the hospitality in his reply. He believes that through the Chinese delegation’s visit to the UAR, the mutual understanding and friendship between the two countries will surely be strengthened.

    During the five-day visit to the Egyptian region, General Zhang Zongxun and other members of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation met with Lieutenant General Ali Amer, Chief of Staff of the UAR Armed Forces, and others.

### 阿联第一军军长费萨尔举行盛宴  欢迎我国军事友好代表团

1960-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    阿联第一军军长费萨尔举行盛宴
    欢迎我国军事友好代表团
    据新华社大马士革2日电　由中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊上将率领的中华人民共和国军事友好代表团结束了在埃及地区的访问后，今天从开罗到达大马士革。
    阿拉伯联合共和国第一军军长贾马勒·费萨尔在今天中午为正在这里进行访问的中国军事友好代表团举行盛大宴会。
    费萨尔在宴会上致词时说，“阿拉伯人民永远不能忘掉友好的人民中国在1956年三国侵略埃及期间对阿拉伯人表示的崇高的态度。”
    张宗逊上将在致答词时表示感谢主人的款待。他认为，通过中国代表团对阿联的访问，两国之间的相互了解和友谊一定会得到加强。
    在埃及地区访问的五天期间，张宗逊上将和中国军事友好代表团的其他团员会见了阿联武装部队参谋长阿里·阿密尔中将等人。
