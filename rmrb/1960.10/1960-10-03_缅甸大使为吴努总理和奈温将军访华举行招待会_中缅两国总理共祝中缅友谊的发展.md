Title: Burmese Ambassador Holds Reception for Premier U Nu and General Ne Win's Visit to China; Chinese and Burmese Premiers Jointly Celebrate the Development of Sino-Burmese Friendship
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd: Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyaw Winn, held a reception at the Beijing Hotel tonight in honor of Burmese Prime Minister U Nu and his wife, Burmese Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces General Ne Win and his wife, and the Burmese government delegation's visit to China.

Premier Zhou Enlai was invited to attend the reception.

At the reception, which was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere, Prime Minister U Nu raised his glass and proposed a toast to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, to the health of Chairman Liu Shaoqi, to the health of Premier Zhou Enlai, and to the consolidation of Sino-Burmese friendship.

In his speech, Premier Zhou Enlai proposed a toast to celebrate the completion of the grand edifice of friendship and peace between China and Burma, as described by Prime Minister U Nu, to celebrate the "Pauk-Phaw" (sibling-like) friendship between the peoples of China and Burma, to the unity of the people of the world, to world peace, to the health of Prime Minister U Nu and his wife, to the health of General Ne Win and his wife, and to the health of all the friends present.

Also invited to attend the reception were Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yi, Luo Ruiqing, and Xi Zhongxun; Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Chen Shutong, Saifudin, Cheng Qian, and Panchen Erdini Qoigyijabu; Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Li Siguang and Burhan; Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Fu Zuoyi; President of the Supreme People's Court Xie Juezai; Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng; Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Geng Biao; Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang; heads of various departments of state organs, people's organizations, democratic parties, and Beijing Municipality; generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; and people from all walks of life in the capital.

Vice Premier Chen Yi raised his glass and made a speech at the reception. He said that China and Burma are linked by mountains and rivers, China's water flows south to Burma, and Burma's mountains are connected to China's mountains. Sino-Burmese friendship has been established since ancient times.

Vice Premier Chen Yi said that on the basis of the traditional friendship between the two countries, and because the two governments and peoples were willing to resolve the border issues left over by history, an agreement was reached through talks and the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty was signed. This is a new stage in the development of Sino-Burmese friendly relations and a great happy event. The border between China and Burma has been fixed in legal form. Although China and Burma do not want to say that the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty is a model, it cannot but play a model role. Vice Premier Chen Yi also spoke of the efforts made by the former Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maw, and the current Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyaw Winn, to resolve the Sino-Burmese border issue. He proposed a toast to the health of Ambassador Kyaw Winn and his wife, as well as to all the diplomatic officials of the Burmese Embassy in China.

Ambassador Kyaw Winn delivered a speech at the beginning of the reception. He proposed: to the permanent success of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty, to the consolidation and development of the edifice of friendship and peace between the peoples of China and Burma that has been successfully built, to the prosperity and greater achievements of the People's Republic of China, to the health of Prime Minister U Nu and his wife, General Ne Win and his wife, and the heads and all members of the Burmese government delegations, to the health of Premier Zhou Enlai and other leaders, and to peace in Asia and the world.

Members of the Burmese government delegation, the Burmese delegation to the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, and the heads and members of the Burmese military, cultural, sports, news, and trade delegations attended the reception.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China were invited to attend the reception.

During the reception, actors from the Burmese cultural delegation performed songs and dances. (Photo attached)

### 缅甸大使为吴努总理和奈温将军访华举行招待会  中缅两国总理共祝中缅友谊的发展

1960-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸大使为吴努总理和奈温将军访华举行招待会
    中缅两国总理共祝中缅友谊的发展
    新华社2日讯　缅甸驻中国大使叫温为缅甸总理吴努和夫人、缅甸国防军总参谋长奈温将军和夫人以及缅甸政府代表团访问中国，今天晚上在北京饭店举行招待会。
    周恩来总理应邀出席了招待会。
    吴努总理在洋溢着热烈、友好气氛的招待会上举杯致词，他提议为毛泽东主席阁下的健康，为刘少奇主席阁下的健康，为周恩来总理阁下的健康，为中缅友谊的巩固，干杯。
    周恩来总理在致词中提议，为庆祝吴努总理阁下所说的中缅两国友好与和平大厦的建成，为庆祝中缅两国人民“胞波”般的友谊，为全世界人民的团结，为世界和平，为吴努总理和夫人的健康，为奈温将军和夫人的健康，为在座的各国朋友的健康，干杯。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有国务院副总理陈毅、罗瑞卿、习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒、黄炎培、陈叔通、赛福鼎、程潜、班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，政协全国委员会副主席李四光、包尔汉，国防委员会副主席傅作义，最高人民法院院长谢觉哉，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，外交部副部长章汉夫、耿飚，中国驻缅甸大使李一氓，我国国家机关各部门、人民团体、民主党派和北京市的负责人，中国人民解放军的将军们，以及首都各方面的人士。
    陈毅副总理在招待会上举杯致词。他说，中缅两国山连着山，水连着水，中国的水向南流向缅甸，缅甸的山与中国的山连接着。中缅友谊自古以来就建立了起来。
    陈毅副总理说，在两国传统友谊的基础上，由于两国政府和人民愿意解决历史遗留下来的两国边界问题，所以经过会谈，达成协议，并签订了中缅边界条约，这是中缅友好关系发展的一个新阶段，是一件大喜事，中缅两国的边界已经用法律形式固定了下来。中缅两国虽然不愿意说中缅边界条约是一个范例，但是它不能不起到范例的作用。陈毅副总理还谈到了缅甸前任驻华大使吴拉茂和现任驻华大使叫温对解决中缅边界问题所做的努力。他提议为叫温大使和夫人以及缅甸驻华大使馆的全体外交官员的健康干杯。
    叫温大使在招待会开始时致词，他提议：为中缅边界条约的永久成功，为已经成功地建成了的中缅两国人民的友谊与和平大厦的巩固与发展，为中华人民共和国的繁荣和更大的成就，为吴努总理和夫人、奈温将军和夫人、缅甸政府各代表团的团长和全体团员们的健康，为周恩来总理阁下和其他领导人的健康，为亚洲与世界和平，干杯。
    缅甸政府代表团团员，中缅边界联合委员会缅方代表团、缅甸军事、文化、体育、新闻、贸易代表团的负责人和团员，出席了招待会。
    各国驻中国的使节应邀出席了招待会。
    招待会进行期间，缅甸文化代表团的演员们表演了歌舞节目。（附图片）
