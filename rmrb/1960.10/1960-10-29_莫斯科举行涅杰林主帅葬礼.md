Title: Funeral for Chief Marshal Nedelin Held in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1960-10-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th: According to TASS, the funeral of Chief Marshal of Artillery M.I. Nedelin, commander-in-chief of the Soviet Rocket Forces, who died in a plane crash, was held on Red Square in Moscow on the 27th.

　　Kozlov, member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee, Marshal Malinovsky, Soviet Minister of Defense, and Demichev, Secretary of the Moscow City Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, spoke successively at the memorial service. They pointed out Marshal Nedelin's outstanding military genius and rich organizational skills.

　　Nedelin's ashes were buried inside the Kremlin wall to the sound of gun salutes and the national anthem.

### 莫斯科举行涅杰林主帅葬礼

1960-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    莫斯科举行涅杰林主帅葬礼
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：27日，在莫斯科红场上举行了因飞机失事而遇难的苏联火箭部队总司令、炮兵主帅涅杰林的葬礼。
    苏共中央主席团委员、苏共中央书记科兹洛夫，苏联国防部长马利诺夫斯基元帅和苏联共产党莫斯科市委员会书记杰米契夫在追悼会上先后讲话。他们指出了涅杰林元帅杰出的军事天才和丰富的组织能力。
    涅杰林的骨灰在礼炮和国歌声中安葬于克里姆林宫的墙内。
