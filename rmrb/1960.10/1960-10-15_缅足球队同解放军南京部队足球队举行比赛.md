Title: Burmese Football Team Plays Against PLA Nanjing Unit Football Team
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, October 14th: The Burmese sports delegation's football team played a friendly match against the Chinese People's Liberation Army Nanjing Unit football team at the Wutaishan Stadium in Nanjing this afternoon. This was the fourth match played by the visiting team since arriving in our country. The home team won with a score of four to one.
    The Burmese sports delegation's cane ball team and boxing team also held wonderful performances at the Nanjing Zhongshan East Road Gymnasium yesterday.
    The Burmese sports delegation arrived in Nanjing on the 12th. That evening, Fu Quejian, Major General, and Zhao Jun, Major General, leaders of the Nanjing Unit, hosted a banquet to entertain all members of the delegation. During their stay in Nanjing, the delegation also conducted visits and tours.
    The Burmese sports delegation will leave Nanjing tonight to visit Shanghai.

### 缅足球队同解放军南京部队足球队举行比赛

1960-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

    缅足球队同解放军南京部队足球队举行比赛
    新华社南京14日电　缅甸体育代表团足球队今天下午在南京五台山体育场同中国人民解放军南京部队足球队举行了友谊赛。这是客队来我国后举行的第四场比赛。比赛结果，主队以四比一获胜。
    缅甸体育代表团藤球队和拳击队昨天还在南京中山东路体育馆举行了精彩表演。
    缅甸体育代表团是12日到达南京的。当晚，南京部队负责人符确坚少将、赵俊少将设宴招待了代表团全体人员。代表团在南京期间，还进行了参观访问。
    缅甸体育代表团今天夜间离开南京，前往上海访问。
