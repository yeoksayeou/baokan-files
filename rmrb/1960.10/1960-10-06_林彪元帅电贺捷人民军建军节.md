Title: Marshal Lin Biao Sends Congratulatory Telegram on Czechoslovak People's Army Day
Author:
Date: 1960-10-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, October 5th—Marshal Lin Biao, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, sent a telegram on the 4th to General Bohumír Lomský, Minister of National Defense of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic, congratulating him on the Czechoslovak People's Army Day. The telegram reads as follows:

To Comrade General Bohumír Lomský, Minister of National Defense of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic,

Dear Comrade Minister:

On the occasion of the 16th anniversary of the founding of the Czechoslovak People's Army, please accept the warm congratulations from all the commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and myself to you and all the comrades of the Czechoslovak People's Army.

Under the leadership of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, the Czechoslovak People's Army has made great achievements in building up its combat effectiveness.

I wish the Czechoslovak People's Army continued success in strengthening political and military education, in vigilantly defending its motherland, in smashing imperialist aggression and war conspiracies, and in the struggle to defend world peace.

I wish the militant friendship between the fraternal armies of China and Czechoslovakia will grow stronger day by day.

Lin Biao
Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China
Marshal of the People's Republic of China
October 4, 1960, in Beijing

### 林彪元帅电贺捷人民军建军节

1960-10-06
第2版()
专栏：

    林彪元帅电贺捷人民军建军节
    新华社5日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪元帅4日致电捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国国防部长波古米尔·洛姆斯基大将，祝贺捷克斯洛伐克人民军建军节。电文如下：捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国国防部长波古米尔·洛姆斯基大将同志敬爱的部长同志：
    值此捷克斯洛伐克人民军建军十六周年之际，请您接受中国人民解放军全体指战员和我个人对您和捷克斯洛伐克人民军全体同志的热烈的祝贺。
    捷克斯洛伐克人民军，在捷克斯洛伐克共产党的领导下，在增强自己战斗威力的建设中，已经取得很大成就。
    祝捷克斯洛伐克人民军在继续加强政治和军事教育，在警惕地保卫自己祖国，粉碎帝国主义的侵略和战争阴谋以及在捍卫世界和平的斗争中作出新的贡献。
    祝中、捷两国兄弟军队的战斗友谊日益发展。
    中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长
    中华人民共和国元帅  林彪
    1960年10月4日于北京
