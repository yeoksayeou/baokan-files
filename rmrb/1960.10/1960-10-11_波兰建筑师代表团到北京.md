Title: Polish Architects Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 10th -- At the invitation of the Architectural Society of China, a five-member delegation of Polish architects, led by the Vice Chairman of the Polish Architects Association, Petrovsky, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 9th for a friendly visit to our country.

Yang Chunmao and Liang Sicheng, Vice Chairmen of the Architectural Society of China, and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 波兰建筑师代表团到北京

1960-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰建筑师代表团到北京
    新华社10日讯　应中国建筑学会邀请来我国进行友好访问的波兰建筑师代表团一行五人，在团长、波兰建筑师学会副主席彼得罗夫斯基率领下，在9日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    中国建筑学会副理事长杨春茂、梁思成等到机场欢迎。
