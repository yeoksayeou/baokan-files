Title: Chairman Liu Receives Dominican Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Chairman Liu Shaoqi received the entire membership of the Dominican cultural delegation headed by Juan Ducoudray this afternoon and had a cordial conversation with them. Present were Zhang Zhixiang, Vice-President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Zhou Erfu, Vice-President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association.

### 刘主席接见多米尼加文化代表团

1960-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见多米尼加文化代表团
    新华社5日讯　刘少奇主席在今天下午接见了以胡安·杜古德累为首的多米尼加文化代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切的谈话。在座的，有中国人民对外文化协会副会长张致祥，中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复。
