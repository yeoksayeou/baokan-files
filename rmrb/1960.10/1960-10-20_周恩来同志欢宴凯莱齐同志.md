Title: Comrade Zhou Enlai Fetes Comrade Kellezi
Author:
Date: 1960-10-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 19th: Comrade Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Premier of the State Council, held a banquet at the Great Hall of the People tonight to warmly welcome the Albanian China-Albania Friendship Association delegation headed by Abdyl Kellezi, Member of the Central Committee of theAlbanian Labour Party, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and Chairman of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association.

Misto Treska, Chairman of the Albanian Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Shefket Musaraj, Secretary-General of the Albanian Writers and Artists Association, and Albanian Ambassador to China, Piro Pllumbi, were invited to attend the banquet.

Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, and Li Xiannian, Members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premiers of the State Council; Lin Feng, Member of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Burhan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC; and Jiang Nanxiang, Alternate Member of the CPC Central Committee and President of the China-Albania Friendship Association, attended tonight's banquet.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Chairman Kellezi raised their glasses at the banquet, which was filled with brotherly sentiments, to toast the heroic Albanian people's further achievements in opposing imperialism, safeguarding world peace, and building socialism, to the further prosperity and strength of the People's Republic of China, to the eternal and unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Albanian peoples, to the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, to the unity of the people of the world, and to the victory of the struggle to uphold the truth of Marxism-Leninism and oppose modern revisionism.

Also present at the banquet tonight were Wu Xiuquan and Zeng Shan, Members of the CPC Central Committee; Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Luo Guibo and Geng Biao, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; Wang Shusheng, Vice Minister of National Defense; Liu Ren, Second Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC; Chu Tunan and Zhang Zhixiang, Directors of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Liu Mingfu, Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission; Lin Haiyun, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; and Liu Baiyu, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association. ### 周恩来同志欢宴凯莱齐同志

1960-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来同志欢宴凯莱齐同志
    宾主举杯为英雄的阿尔巴尼亚人民在反对帝国主义、保卫世界和平、建设社会主义中获得进一步的成就，为中华人民共和国进一步繁荣和昌盛，为两国人民永恒的牢不可破的友谊，为以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结，为全世界人民的团结，为坚持马克思列宁主义真理反对现代修正主义斗争的胜利而干杯
    新华社19日讯　中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来今晚在人民大会堂举行宴会，热烈欢迎以阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、部长会议副主席、阿中友好协会主席阿卜杜勒·凯莱齐为首的阿尔巴尼亚中国友好协会代表团。
    阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会主席米斯托·特雷斯卡和阿尔巴尼亚作家艺术家协会秘书长谢夫卡特·穆萨莱和阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使帕里夫蒂应邀出席了宴会。
    中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅、李富春、李先念，中共中央委员、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林枫，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，中共中央候补委员、中阿友协会长蒋南翔出席了今晚的宴会。
    周恩来总理和凯莱齐副主席在洋溢着兄弟般的情谊的宴会上举杯，为英雄的阿尔巴尼亚人民在反对帝国主义、保卫世界和平、建设社会主义中获得进一步的成就，为中华人民共和国进一步繁荣和昌盛，为中阿两国人民永恒的牢不可破的友谊，为以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结，为全世界人民的团结，为坚持马克思列宁主义真理反对现代修正主义斗争的胜利而干杯。
    出席今晚宴会的还有中共中央委员伍修权、曾山，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏，外交部副部长罗贵波、耿飚，国防部副部长王树声，中共北京市委第二书记刘仁，对外文化联络委员会主任楚图南、张致祥，国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫，对外贸易部副部长林海云，中国作家协会副主席刘白羽等。（附图片）
