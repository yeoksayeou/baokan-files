Title: Acting as Accomplices in Aggression in Algeria, More Than 8,000 West German Mercenaries Died
Author:
Date: 1960-10-25
Page: 6

Acting as Accomplices in Aggression in Algeria
More Than 8,000 West German Mercenaries Died
Xinhua News Agency, Bonn, October 22nd: Since the beginning of France's colonial war against Algeria, a total of 8,486 West Germans hired by the French Foreign Legion have died in Algeria.

This was reported by the Social Democratic Party of Germany's "Parliamentary Political Newsletter," citing official materials from the Algerian National Liberation Front. This number does not include German military personnel sentenced to death by French military tribunals or who died from other causes. According to recent reports in the West German press, there are now more than 30,000 West German mercenaries employed by France.

### 在阿尔及利亚充当侵略帮凶  西德雇佣兵八千多人丧命

1960-10-25
第6版()
专栏：

    在阿尔及利亚充当侵略帮凶
    西德雇佣兵八千多人丧命
    新华社波恩22日电　自法国对阿尔及利亚的殖民战争开始以来，共有八千四百八十六名被法国外籍军团雇佣的西德人在阿尔及利亚丧命。
    这是西德社会民主党出版的“议会政治新闻稿”援引阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线的官方材料报道的。这个数字中不包括被法国军事法庭判处死刑或由于其他原因死亡的德国军事人员。据不久前西德报刊透露，现在为法国雇佣的西德雇佣兵共有三万多人。
