Title: Pictures

Author: 
Date: 1960-10-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lü Houmin photographed: The Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and his wife, along with some distinguished guests from the Burmese government delegation, left Beijing on a special plane on October 4th via Kunming to return to their country. The picture shows Prime Minister U Nu shaking hands with Premier Zhou Enlai to say goodbye after speaking at the airport.

Xinhua News Agency, Du Xiuxian photographed: The Chief of Staff of the Burmese National Defence Forces, General Ne Win, and his wife, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, left Beijing by train on October 4th to visit famous places in southern China. The picture shows General Ne Win and his wife, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, entering the station and being warmly welcomed by the people of Beijing.

People from all walks of life in the capital held a grand rally to welcome Abbas Ferhat, Prime Minister of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria. The left picture shows Prime Minister Abbas shaking hands cordially with Premier Zhou Enlai after speaking. The right picture shows Premier Zhou Enlai, Prime Minister Abbas, and Chinese artists who performed wonderful programs after the meeting, taking a group photo. Our reporter, Lü Xiangyou, photographed.

People from all walks of life in the capital held a grand rally to welcome the Algerian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation. The speech by delegation leader Abdul Qadir at the conference was warmly welcomed by the masses attending the conference.
Xinhua News Agency, Lü Houmin and Ge Liqun photographed.

A scene from the song and dance drama "Towards the Light" performed by the Algerian Art Troupe. This drama reflects the current fighting life of the Algerian people. Our reporter, Lü Xiangyou, photographed.
