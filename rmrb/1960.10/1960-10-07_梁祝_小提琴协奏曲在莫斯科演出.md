Title: "The Butterfly Lovers" Violin Concerto Performed in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 6th: To celebrate the eleventh anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the USSR All-Union Radio Symphony Orchestra and Choir held a Chinese music concert in the Hall of Columns of the House of Trade Unions on the evening of the 5th. At the concert, the "The Butterfly Lovers" Violin Concerto by young Chinese composers He Zhanhao and Chen Gang was performed for the first time. The performance was warmly welcomed by the audience. The performer of this concerto was the Soviet violinist Goteshtein, with the All-Union Radio Symphony Orchestra accompanying and Cao Peng, a Chinese student at the Moscow Conservatory, conducting.

### “梁祝”小提琴协奏曲在莫斯科演出

1960-10-07
第6版()
专栏：

    “梁祝”小提琴协奏曲在莫斯科演出
    新华社莫斯科6日电　为庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年，苏联全苏广播交响乐团与合唱队5日晚在工会大厦圆柱大厅举行中国音乐会，晚会上首次演奏了中国青年作曲家何占豪、陈钢的“梁山伯与祝英台”小提琴协奏曲。这次演奏受到听众的热烈欢迎。这首协奏曲的演奏者是苏联的小提琴家戈德什捷因，由全苏广播交响乐团协奏，指挥是莫斯科音乐学院的中国留学生曹鹏。
