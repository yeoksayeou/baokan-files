Title: Kelezi Leaves Shenyang to Visit Anshan

Author:

Date: 1960-10-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, October 7th: The Albanian China Friendship Association delegation, led by Abdyl Këllezi, member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Vice-Chairman of the Albanian Council of Ministers, and Chairman of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association, concluded its visit to Shenyang and left Shenyang by special train this morning to visit Anshan.

Jiang Nanxiang, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the CPC and President of the Sino-Albanian Friendship Association, Liu Mingfu, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission, Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Yu Menglong, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, accompanied them.

Albanian Ambassador to China, Piro Koçi, and Misto Treska, Chairman of the Albanian Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Shevket Musaraj, Secretary-General of the Albanian Writers and Artists Association, who were invited to visit China, also left Shenyang on the same train.

Among those who went to the station to see off the distinguished guests were Li Huang, Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the CPC, Jiao Ruoyu, First Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the CPC, Qiu Youwen, Standing Committee Member and Vice Governor of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the CPC, Hu Yimin, Standing Committee Member and Secretary-General of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the CPC, Ning Wu, Che Xiangchen, Gong Tianmin, and Chen Xianzhou, Vice Governors of Liaoning Province, Shen Zhilan, Secretary-General of the Provincial People's Committee, and Xu Zhi, Lu Guangji, Zhang Panxin, Yang Tianfang, and Zhang Jizhong, Vice Mayors of Shenyang.

Before boarding the train, the distinguished guests shook hands with the leaders of the provincial and municipal Party and government who came to see them off and accepted flowers from a group of young women. As the special train, adorned with the national flags of China and Albania, started moving, the people who came to see them off waved to express their best wishes for a safe journey.

### 凯莱齐离沈阳去鞍山参观

1960-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

    凯莱齐离沈阳去鞍山参观
    新华社沈阳7日电　由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、阿尔巴尼亚部长会议副主席、阿中友好协会主席阿卜杜勒·凯莱齐率领的阿尔巴尼亚中国友好协会代表团，结束了在沈阳的参观访问，于7日晨乘专车离开沈阳去鞍山参观。
    中共中央候补委员、中阿友好协会会长蒋南翔，国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫，外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清，对外文化联络委员会副司长于梦龙等陪同前往。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻我国大使帕里夫蒂和应邀前来我国参观访问的阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会主席米斯托·特雷斯卡，阿尔巴尼亚作家艺术家协会秘书长谢夫卡特·穆萨莱也同车离开沈阳。
    前往车站欢送贵宾的，有中共辽宁省委书记李荒，中共沈阳市委第一书记焦若愚，中共辽宁省委常委、副省长仇友文，中共辽宁省委常委、秘书长胡亦民，辽宁省副省长宁武、车向忱、巩天民、陈先舟，省人民委员会秘书长申之澜，沈阳市副市长徐志、卢广绩、张磐新、杨天放、张霁中等。
    贵宾们登车前，和前往欢送的省、市党和政府领导人一一握手告别，并接受了一群女青年的献花。当插着中阿两国国旗的专车开动时，欢送的人都招手致意，祝贵宾们一路平安。
