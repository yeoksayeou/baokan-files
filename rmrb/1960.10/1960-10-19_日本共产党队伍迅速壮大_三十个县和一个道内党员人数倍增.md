Title: Ranks of Japanese Communist Party Rapidly Expand: Party Membership Doubles in Thirty Prefectures and One "Do"

Author:

Date: 1960-10-19
Page: 5

    Japanese Communist Party Ranks Rapidly Expand
    Party Membership Doubles in Thirty Prefectures and One "Do"

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 18th—News: The number of members of the Japanese Communist Party has more than doubled in thirty prefectures and one "Do."
    According to a report in the Japanese Communist Party Central Committee's official newspaper, "Akahata" [Red Flag] today, the Japanese Communist Party organizations in Kanagawa Prefecture, Mie Prefecture, Akita Prefecture, and Hokkaido completed their goals of doubling their membership on the 16th and 17th respectively.
    According to reports, as of October 15th, JCP membership had increased by 87% compared to August of last year. The majority of new members who joined the Communist Party during this period were workers and working people, especially young workers.

### 日本共产党队伍迅速壮大  三十个县和一个道内党员人数倍增

1960-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

    日本共产党队伍迅速壮大
    三十个县和一个道内党员人数倍增
    新华社18日讯　东京消息：日本共产党的党员人数在三十个县和一个道内已经增加了一倍以上。
    据日共中央机关报“赤旗报”今天报道，日本共产党神奈川县、三重县、秋田县和北海道组织分别在16日和17日完成了党员增加一倍的目标。
    据报道，截至10月15日为止，日共党员比去年八月增加了87％。在这期间参加共产党的新党员大部分是工人和劳动人民，其中尤以青年工人为最多。
