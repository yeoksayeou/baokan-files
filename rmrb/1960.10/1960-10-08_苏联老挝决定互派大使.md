Title: Soviet Union and Laos Decide to Exchange Ambassadors
Author:
Date: 1960-10-08
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th: TASS reported on the 6th that the Soviet government and the Royal Lao government have decided to establish diplomatic relations and exchange diplomatic envoys at the ambassadorial level. Both sides agreed that the Lao ambassador to France would concurrently serve as the ambassador to the Soviet Union. The Soviet ambassador to Cambodia would concurrently serve as the ambassador to Laos.

### 苏联老挝决定互派大使

1960-10-08
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联老挝决定互派大使
    新华社7日讯　塔斯社莫斯科6日讯：苏联政府和老挝王国政府已决定建立外交关系并互换大使级外交代表。双方同意，老挝驻法国大使兼任驻苏大使。苏联驻柬埔寨大使兼任驻老挝大使。
