Title: Director of German Dance School Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 9th -- Mrs. Gret Palucca, a dancer from the German Democratic Republic and Director of the Palucca School of Dance in Dresden, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to our country under the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Chen Jinqing, Vice President of the Beijing Dance School, and others, were at the airport to welcome her.

### 德舞蹈学校校长到京

1960-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    德舞蹈学校校长到京
    新华社9日讯　根据中德文化合作协定1960年执行计划前来我国访问的德意志民主共和国舞蹈家、德累斯顿帕鲁卡舞蹈学校校长格来脱·帕鲁卡夫人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有北京舞蹈学校副校长陈锦清等。
