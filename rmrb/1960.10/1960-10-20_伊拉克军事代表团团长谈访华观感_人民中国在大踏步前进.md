Title: Head of Iraqi Military Delegation Discusses Impressions of Visit to China: People's China is Advancing by Leaps and Bounds
Author:
Date: 1960-10-20
Page: 6

The head of the Iraqi military friendship delegation, Brigadier General Mahmoud Abdul Razzaq, spoke to the Iraqi News Agency about his impressions of his visit to China upon returning to Baghdad.

Mahmoud Abdul Razzaq said, "We have seen that the People's Republic of China is constantly advancing by leaps and bounds and carrying out construction in all aspects of life. We have seen People's China racing against time."

Speaking of the Chinese people's friendship towards the Iraqi people, he said, "We will not forget our wonderful memories of the friendly People's China."

### 伊拉克军事代表团团长谈访华观感  人民中国在大踏步前进

1960-10-20
第6版()
专栏：

    伊拉克军事代表团团长谈访华观感
    人民中国在大踏步前进
    伊拉克军事友好代表团团长马哈茂德·阿卜杜勒·拉扎克准将在访问我国后回到巴格达时，向伊拉克通讯社谈访华观感。
    马哈茂德·阿卜杜勒·拉扎克说，“我们看到，中华人民共和国正在大踏步不断前进，在生活的一切方面进行建设。我们看到人民中国正在同时间赛跑。”
    他在谈到中国人民对伊拉克人民的友谊时说，“我们不会忘掉我们对友好的人民中国的美好的记忆。”
