Title: The Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee's Fourth Meeting's Second Session: Make All Preparations for Signing the Boundary Treaty; Negotiations on the Next Stage of Boundary Work Will Continue

Author:

Date: 1960-10-01
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th - News release from the Second Session of the Fourth Meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee
    The Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee held its second session of the fourth meeting in Beijing on September 30. Attending the meeting were the Chinese Chief Representative Yao Zhongming, the Burmese Chief Representative Brigadier General Aung Gyi, and all representatives and advisors from both sides. Burmese Chief Representative Brigadier General Aung Gyi served as chairman.
    The meeting approved several agreements reached in the several separate talks between the two Chief Representatives, and listened to reports from the treaty group and the treaty map group. Both sides noted with satisfaction that this meeting had completed the work of drafting the Sino-Burmese boundary treaty and producing the treaty maps. Thus far, the Joint Committee has made all necessary preparations for the Prime Ministers of the two countries to sign the boundary treaty on October 1.
    The meeting also reviewed the work of the Joint Survey Team, and both sides noted with satisfaction that the Joint Survey Team had effectively completed all the tasks assigned to it by the Joint Committee on schedule. Both sides decided that negotiations on the next stage of work concerning the boundaries of the two countries will continue after the signing of the boundary treaty.
    The meeting proceeded in a very friendly atmosphere.

### 中缅边界联合委员会第四次会议第二次会谈  为签订边界条约作好一切准备  有关边界的下一阶段工作将继续进行商谈

1960-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    中缅边界联合委员会第四次会议第二次会谈
    为签订边界条约作好一切准备
    有关边界的下一阶段工作将继续进行商谈
    新华社30日讯　中缅边界联合委员会第四次会议第二次会谈新闻公报
    中缅边界联合委员会第四次会议于9月30日在北京举行了第二次会谈。出席会议的有中方首席代表姚仲明、缅方首席代表昂季准将以及双方全体代表和顾问。缅方首席代表昂季准将担任主席。
    会议通过了双方首席代表在几次个别交谈中所达成的若干协议，并且听取了条约小组和条约附图小组的汇报。双方满意地看到，这次会议已经完成了草拟中缅边界条约草案和制做条约附图的工作。至此，联合委员会已经为两国总理在10月1日签订边界条约作好了一切必要的准备。
    会议还检查了联合勘察队的工作，双方满意地看到，联合勘察队已经卓有成效地如期完成了联合委员会所给予的全部任务。双方决定，有关两国边界的下一阶段的工作，将在边界条约签订后继续进行商谈。
    会议在十分友好的气氛中进行。
