Title: Chen Yi Receives Sir Cyril Osborne
Author:
Date: 1960-10-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 22nd - Vice Premier Chen Yi received Sir Cyril Osborne, a British Conservative Member of Parliament who is currently visiting China, this afternoon. Present at the meeting were Ji Chao-ding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and Song Zhiguang, Deputy Director of the Department of West European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 陈毅副总理接见西里尔·奥斯邦

1960-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见西里尔·奥斯邦
    新华社22日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了正在中国访问的英国保守党议员西里尔·奥斯邦。接见时在座的，有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎，外交部西欧司副司长宋之光。
