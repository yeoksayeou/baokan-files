Title: Argentine Youth Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The Argentine youth delegation, a group of five led by the head of the delegation, Horacio Cerrado, arrived in Beijing. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation. Yang Haibo, Zhang Yin'an and others were at the airport to welcome them.

### 阿根廷青年代表团到京

1960-10-01
第5版()
专栏：

    阿根廷青年代表团到京
    新华社30日讯　阿根廷青年代表团一行五人在团长奥拉西欧·塞拉多率领下到达北京。代表团是应全国青联邀请来中国访问的。到机场欢迎的有杨海波、张寅安等人。
