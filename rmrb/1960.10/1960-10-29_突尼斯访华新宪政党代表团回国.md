Title: Tunisian Neo Destour Party Delegation Returns Home After Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1960-10-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, October 26th: The Tunisian Neo Destour Party delegation, headed by Ben Amar Hassib, President of the Socio-Economic Revival Department of the Tunisian Neo Destour Party, returned to Tunisia yesterday after visiting China. The Neo Destour Party delegation visited China at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation and the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

After returning, delegation leader Amar Hassib told reporters: "We had a very excellent trip. I want to thank the leaders of the People's Republic of China for their very warm reception. I can say that we will retain the most beautiful memories of this visit."

Amar Hassib said that Premier Zhou Enlai met with the delegation. The delegation also visited various places in China and personally experienced the efforts made by the Chinese people in industry, agriculture, and society.

### 突尼斯访华新宪政党代表团回国

1960-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    突尼斯访华新宪政党代表团回国
    新华社突尼斯26日电　以突尼斯新宪政党社会经济复兴部主席本·阿玛尔·哈西卜为首的突尼斯新宪政党代表团，在中国进行访问以后于昨天返回突尼斯。新宪政党代表团是应中华全国青年联合会和中国人民外交学会的邀请，到中国访问的。
    代表团团长阿玛尔·哈西卜回来后对新闻记者说：“我们作了一次非常出色的旅行。我要感谢中华人民共和国的领导人对我们的非常热情的接待。我可以说，对这次访问，我们将保持最美好的回忆。”
    阿玛尔·哈西卜说，周恩来总理曾接见了代表团。代表团还在中国各地进行了参观访问，并亲身体会到中国人民在工农业和社会等方面所作的努力。
