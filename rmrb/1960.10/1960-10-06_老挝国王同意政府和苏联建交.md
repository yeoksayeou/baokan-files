Title: Lao King Agrees to Government's Establishment of Diplomatic Relations with the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-10-06
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 5th. According to Vientiane Radio's broadcast yesterday: The King of Laos has agreed to the government's recommendation to establish diplomatic relations with the Soviet Union at the ambassadorial level.

### 老挝国王同意政府和苏联建交

1960-10-06
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝国王同意政府和苏联建交
    新华社河内5日电　据万象电台昨天广播：老挝国王已经同意了政府关于同苏联建立大使级的外交关系的建议。
