Title: General Ne Win Travels from Shanghai to Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-10-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 9th October: General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces of the Union of Burma, and his wife, along with their entourage, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, left Shanghai by special train this afternoon for a visit to Hangzhou.

Chen Pixian, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Liu Shuzhou, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Mayor of Shanghai, Wang Bicheng, Lieutenant General and Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and others were at the station to see them off.

Also at the station to see them off were: Vice Mayors of Shanghai Song Jiwen and Zhao Zukang, Zhou Renjie, Lieutenant General and Jiang Tengjiao, Major General, leaders of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai, Chen Wangdao, Hu Juewen, and Liu Jingji, Vice Chairmen of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, as well as heads of various departments of the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, various people's organizations in Shanghai, responsible persons of Shanghai local organizations of various democratic parties, directors of the China-Burma Friendship Association in Shanghai, and people who had visited Burma.

Consuls from various countries stationed in Shanghai were also at the station today to see them off.

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 9th October: General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces of the Union of Burma, and his wife, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, arrived in Hangzhou this afternoon by special train from Shanghai. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the station by Zhou Jianren, Governor of Zhejiang Province, Wu Xian, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province, First Secretary of the Hangzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Mayor of Hangzhou, Qian Jun, Lieutenant General in charge of the Zhejiang Garrison, Zhang Shixiang, Vice Mayor of Hangzhou, and more than a thousand people.

Today, the national flags of China and Burma fluttered in the breeze on the Hangzhou Railway Station Square, and a huge banner reading "Welcome General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces of the Union of Burma" was hung on the platform. The welcoming masses held flowers, waiting for the arrival of the distinguished guests. At 4 p.m., the special train carrying the distinguished guests entered the station, and General Ne Win stepped out of the carriage to the cheers of the welcoming crowd. Zhou Jianren, Governor of Zhejiang Province, Wu Xian, Vice Governor, and others stepped forward to greet the distinguished guests and shake hands warmly with each of them. A group of Young Pioneers presented bouquets of flowers to General Ne Win and all the distinguished guests. Afterwards, General Ne Win met with people from all walks of life in Zhejiang Province who came to welcome him. Among them were: Fang Qide, Secretary-General of the Zhejiang Provincial People's Committee, Yu Jiyi, Vice Chairman of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Hu Tianmin, Vice Chairman of the Hangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and heads of various departments and people's organizations of the Zhejiang Provincial People's Committee, and others.

When General Ne Win, accompanied by Zhou Jianren, Governor of Zhejiang Province, Wu Xian, Vice Governor, and others, walked out of the station, the welcoming masses on the platform and in the station square beat drums and gongs, played welcoming music, and waved colorful flags and bouquets, warmly welcoming the distinguished guests. At this time, the entire station was filled with an atmosphere of friendship and joy, with applause and music resounding, and General Ne Win smiled and waved frequently to the welcoming crowd.

After arriving in Hangzhou, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, Wu Xian, Vice Governor and his wife, Qian Jun, Lieutenant General, and others, took a boat to enjoy the autumn scenery of the West Lake and visited the Three Pools Mirroring the Moon and the Flower Harbor Park.

In the evening, Zhou Jianren, Governor of Zhejiang Province, held a banquet to warmly welcome the distinguished guests. Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife also attended the banquet. Governor Zhou Jianren and General Ne Win spoke at the banquet successively. Governor Zhou Jianren, on behalf of the Zhejiang Provincial People's Committee, expressed a warm welcome to General Ne Win and his wife and other distinguished Burmese guests. General Ne Win said in his speech that thanks to the signing of the Sino-Burmese border treaty, the friendly relations between the two countries will be further developed. During the banquet, the hosts and guests toasted each other warmly, warmly congratulating the signing of the Sino-Burmese border treaty and congratulating the entry of the friendly relations between the Chinese and Burmese peoples into a new stage.

### 奈温将军由上海到杭州

1960-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    奈温将军由上海到杭州
    新华社上海9日电　缅甸联邦国防军总参谋长奈温将军和夫人以及随行人员，在陈毅副总理和夫人陪同下，今天下午乘专车离开上海前往杭州访问。
    中共上海市委书记处书记陈丕显，中共上海市委书记处候补书记、上海市副市长刘述周，中国人民解放军上海警备区司令员王必成中将等都到车站欢送。
    到车站欢送的还有：上海市副市长宋季文、赵祖康，人民解放军驻上海部队首长周仁杰中将、江腾蛟少将，政协上海市委员会副主席陈望道、胡厥文、刘靖基，以及上海市人民委员会各部门、上海市各人民团体、各民主党派上海地方组织的负责人，中缅友好协会在上海的理事和曾经访问过缅甸的人士等。
    各国驻上海领事今天也到车站欢送。
    新华社杭州9日电　缅甸联邦国防军总参谋长奈温将军和夫人，由陈毅副总理和夫人陪同，今天下午乘专车从上海到达杭州。贵宾们在车站上受到了浙江省省长周建人，中共浙江省委书记处书记、浙江省副省长、中共杭州市委第一书记、杭州市市长吴宪，浙江驻军负责人钱钧中将，杭州市副市长张世祥和一千多名群众的热烈欢迎。
    今天，杭州车站广场上，中缅两国国旗迎风招展，站台上悬挂着“欢迎缅甸联邦国防军总参谋长奈温将军”的巨大横幅。欢迎的群众手持鲜花，迎候着贵宾的莅临。下午四时，贵宾们乘坐的专车进入车站，奈温将军在欢迎群众的欢呼声中走出了车厢。这时，周建人省长、吴宪副省长等迎上前去，和贵宾们一一亲切握手。一群少先队员把一束束鲜花献给奈温将军和全体贵宾。接着，奈温将军和前来欢迎的浙江省各方面人士会见。他们当中有：浙江省人民委员会秘书长方琦德，政协浙江省委员会副主席余纪一，政协杭州市委员会副主席胡天民，及浙江省人民委员会各部门、各人民团体的负责人等。
    当奈温将军由周建人省长、吴宪副省长等陪同走出车站的时候，在站台和车站广场上的欢迎群众，紧敲着锣鼓，奏着欢迎的乐曲，挥舞着彩旗和花束，热烈地欢迎贵宾。这时，整个车站充满着友谊和欢乐的气氛，掌声和鼓乐声响成一片，奈温将军面含笑容，频频向欢迎的群众挥手致意。
    贵宾们到杭州后，由陈毅副总理和夫人、吴宪副省长和夫人、钱钧中将等陪同，乘船欣赏了秋日的西湖景色，游览了三潭印月和花港观鱼公园。
    晚上，周建人省长举行宴会热烈欢迎贵宾。陈毅副总理和夫人也出席了宴会。周建人省长和奈温将军先后在宴会上讲了话。周建人省长代表浙江省人民委员会，向奈温将军和夫人以及缅甸其他贵宾们表示热烈欢迎。奈温将军在讲话中说，由于中缅两国签订了边界条约，两国的友好关系将得到更大的发展。席间，宾主相互祝酒，热烈祝贺中缅边界条约的签订，祝贺中缅两国人民的友好关系进入新的阶段。
