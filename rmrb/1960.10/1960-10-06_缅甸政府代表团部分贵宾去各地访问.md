Title: Part of the Burmese Government Delegation Visits Various Places
Author:
Date: 1960-10-06
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, October 5th. Some distinguished guests from the Burmese Government Delegation: Kachin State Head and Minister of Kachin Affairs of the Federal Government Duwa Sinwa Naw, and Minister of Education and Health of the Shan State Government Su Htun Aye, and their party of thirteen, left Beijing by car tonight to visit various parts of our country. Chen Zhaoyuan, Deputy Director of the International Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, is accompanying them.

Those who saw them off at the station included Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Sakong Lahu, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, Han Nianlong, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhao Puchu, Vice President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, and Chen Keda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee.

The Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyaw Winn, also came to the station to see them off.

### 缅甸政府代表团部分贵宾去各地访问

1960-10-06
第3版()
专栏：

    缅甸政府代表团部分贵宾去各地访问
    新华社5日讯　缅甸政府代表团的部分贵宾：克钦邦邦长、联邦政府克钦邦事务部部长杜瓦信瓦瑙和掸邦政府教育和卫生部部长苏吞埃等一行十三人，今晚乘车离开北京前往我国各地访问。陪同前往的有外交部国际司副司长陈肇源。
    到车站欢送的有全国人民代表大会常委会副秘书长张苏、民族事务委员会副主任萨空了、外交部部长助理韩念龙、中缅友协副会长赵朴初、云南省人民委员会副秘书长陈可大。
    缅甸驻中国大使叫温也到车站送行。
