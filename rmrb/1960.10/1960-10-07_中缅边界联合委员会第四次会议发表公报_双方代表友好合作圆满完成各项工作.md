Title: Communiqué of the Fourth Meeting of the China-Myanmar Boundary Joint Committee: Representatives of both sides completed all tasks in a friendly and cooperative manner

Author: 
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: Communiqué of the Fourth Meeting of the China-Myanmar Boundary Joint Committee

The Fourth Meeting of the China-Myanmar Boundary Joint Committee, which began in Beijing on September 20, successfully concluded on October 5 in a friendly and cordial atmosphere. The meeting was attended by Mr. Yao Zhongming, Chief Representative of the Chinese side, Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Chief Representative of the Burmese side, and all representatives and advisors from both sides.

The meeting completed the drafting of the draft China-Myanmar Boundary Treaty and the production of treaty maps, reviewed the work of the joint survey team; the meeting also formulated a plan for the second stage of surveying and erecting permanent boundary markers after the signing of the boundary treaty between the two countries, and determined that a meeting of the leaders of the joint survey team would be held in Kunming in the fourth week of October to specifically arrange the work.

The delegations of both sides expressed their pleasure that since the establishment of the Joint Committee, they have always implemented the spirit of friendly consultation, mutual understanding and mutual accommodation, and maintained good cooperation, so that they were able to successfully realize the wishes of the two governments and prime ministers to sign the China-Myanmar Boundary Treaty on October 1 in a short period of time, and further strengthened their confidence in the next stage of work of the Joint Committee. The Joint Committee also once again praised the spirit of friendly cooperation, hard work, and joint overcoming of difficulties shown by the surveyors and cartographers of both sides in completing their tasks on schedule. Deep gratitude was expressed for the great support given by the logistics personnel, guard troops, and relevant local governments and border residents of both sides.

During the meeting, the delegations of the Joint Committee from both sides participated in the ceremony of the signing of the China-Myanmar Boundary Treaty by the Prime Ministers of the two countries.

During their stay in China, the Burmese delegation was received by His Excellency Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, paid visits to His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council, His Excellency Chen Yi, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs, and His Excellency Luo Ruiqing, Vice Premier and Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; participated in the National Day observation ceremony for the eleventh anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China; participated in the meeting of people from all walks of life in the Chinese capital celebrating the signing of the China-Myanmar Boundary Treaty; and, arranged by the Chinese side, the Burmese delegation will visit famous cities in the southern provinces of China after the meeting. October 6, 1960, in Beijing

### 中缅边界联合委员会第四次会议发表公报  双方代表友好合作圆满完成各项工作

1960-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    中缅边界联合委员会第四次会议发表公报
    双方代表友好合作圆满完成各项工作
    新华社6日讯　中缅边界联合委员会第四次会议公报
    9月20日在北京开始举行的中缅边界联合委员会第四次会议，于10月5日在友好亲切的气氛中圆满结束。出席这次会议的有中方首席代表姚仲明先生，缅方首席代表昂季准将及双方全体代表和顾问。
    这次会议完成了草拟中缅边界条约草案及制作条约附图的工作，检查了联合勘察队的工作；会议还就两国边界条约签订后第二阶段的勘察和树立永久性界桩的工作制定了计划，并确定10月第四周在昆明举行联合勘察队队长会议，具体布置工作。
    双方代表团对于联合委员会成立以来彼此始终贯彻友好协商互谅互让的精神，保持了良好的合作，从而得以在短期内圆满地实现了两国政府和总理在10月1日签订中缅边界条约的愿望而深感愉快，并且进一步加强了对于联合委员会下一阶段工作的信心。联合委员会还对双方勘察、测绘人员在如期圆满完成任务中所表现的友好合作、吃苦耐劳、共同克服困难的精神再次给予赞扬。对于双方后勤人员、警卫部队以及有关地方政府和边民所给予的巨大支援表示深切的感谢。
    会议期间，联合委员会双方代表团曾参加了两国总理签订中缅边界条约的仪式。
    缅甸代表团在中国逗留期间，曾受到中华人民共和国主席刘少奇阁下的接见，拜会了国务院总理周恩来阁下、副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅阁下、副总理兼中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿大将阁下；参加了中华人民共和国成立十一周年国庆观礼；参加了中国首都各界人民庆祝中缅边界条约签订大会；承中国方面的安排，缅甸代表团将于会后到中国南方各省著名城市进行访问。　1960年10月6日于北京
