Title: A Thunderclap with Every Step
Author: Li Yeguang
Date: 1960-10-28
Page: 8

    A Thunderclap with Every Step
    ——Dedicated to Cuba  Li Yeguang
    I hear a giant marching,
    Roaring, a thunderclap with every step,
    It is Cuba bursting out of the old world,
    Rushing into the new century of the people's territory.
    From the dimly lit port of Tuxpan*,
    To the dense forests of the Sierra Maestra,
    It is not the tsunami and mountain torrents roaring deafeningly,
    But the footsteps of heroes striding through the cold winter.
    It steps onto the vast and desolate plains,
    And the plains awaken with hope;
    It visits every suffering sugar mill,
    And the sugar mills regain their sweetness and youth.
    The sky echoes for it,
    The raised banners flutter and sing;
    The earth bares its heart,
    Millions of militiamen advance with drums.
    Ghosts disappear before it,
    Jackals tremble with fear;
    People all over the world applaud and cheer,
    Cheering its heroic advance…
    The severe winter has passed, the earth returns to spring,
    It breaks its shackles and steps into our century!
    Look, Cuba, a deep thunder with every step,
    Advances victoriously in the flames of battle!
    *Tuxpan Port in Mexico,
    On the night of November 25, 1956, the eighty-two
    Cuban fighters led by Fidel Castro departed from here by speedboat,
    To cross and land in Cuba.

### 一步一个雷声
李野光
1960-10-28
第8版()
专栏：

    一步一个雷声
    ——献给古巴  李野光
    我听见一位巨人在行进，
    隆隆地，一步一个雷声，
    那是古巴从旧世界冲出，
    奔入人民新世纪的国境。
    从夜色苍茫的多士邦海港* ，
    到稠密的马埃斯特腊山林，
    不是海啸和山洪喧腾震耳，
    而是英雄的脚步跨过寒冬。
    它踏上辽阔荒凉的平原，
    平原就满怀希望地苏醒；
    它访问每家苦难的糖厂，
    糖厂就恢复甜蜜和青春。
    天空在为它发出回响，
    高举的旗帜猎猎飞鸣；
    大地袒露自己的胸怀，
    百万民兵擂鼓前进。
    鬼影从它跟前消失，
    豺狼听了胆颤心惊；
    全世界人民拍手欢呼，
    欢呼它英勇地向前挺进……
    隆冬已过，大地回春，
    它挣断枷锁，跨入我们的世纪！
    你看古巴一步一声沉雷，
    在战斗的火光中胜利前进！
    多士邦海港在墨西哥，
    1956年11月25日夜晚，由菲
    德尔·卡斯特罗率领的八十二
    位古巴战士在此乘汽艇出发，
    向古巴强渡登陆。
