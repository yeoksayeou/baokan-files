Title: A Batch of Latin American and Tunisian Student Representatives Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. Rodelfer Murcas Doralsa, representative of the Panamanian University Student Federation; Miguel Roberldo Sanchez, representative of the Honduran University Student Federation; Aiguardo Castro Urutia, representative of the El Salvador General University Student Federation; Aigodor Visampo Castro, member of the Steering Committee of the Puerto Rican University Student Federation for Independence; and the Tunisian student delegation headed by Bennur Ahmed, Deputy Secretary-General of the Tunisian Student Union, who were invited by the All-China Students' Federation to visit our country, have recently left Beijing for home after visiting various parts of China.

### 一批拉丁美洲和突尼斯学生代表回国

1960-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    一批拉丁美洲和突尼斯学生代表回国
    新华社15日讯　应中华全国学生联合会邀请来我国访问的巴拿马大学生联合会代表罗德尔费·姆尔卡斯·多拉尔萨、洪都拉斯大学生联合会代表米盖尔·罗倍尔多·桑切斯、萨尔瓦多大学生总联合会代表艾瓜尔多·卡斯特罗·乌鲁蒂亚、波多黎各大学生争取独立联合会指导委员会委员艾哥多尔·维桑波·卡斯特罗，以突尼斯学生联合会副总书记本努尔·艾哈迈德为首的突尼斯学生代表团，在我国各地参观访问后，已于最近分别离京回国。
