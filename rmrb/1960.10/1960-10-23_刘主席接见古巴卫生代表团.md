Title: Chairman Liu Receives Cuban Health Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: Chairman Liu Shaoqi met this afternoon with all members of the Cuban Health Delegation, led by Cuban Minister of Public Health José Ramón Machado Ventura, who are currently visiting China, and had a cordial conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Minister of Public Health Li Dequan, Vice Minister Cui Yitian, Vice President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association Zhou Erfu, and Deputy Director of the American and Australian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Lin Ping. ### 刘主席接见古巴卫生代表团

1960-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见古巴卫生代表团
    新华社22日讯　刘少奇主席今天下午接见了正在我国访问的由古巴卫生部部长何塞·罗曼·马查多·本图拉率领的古巴卫生代表团全体成员，并且同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有卫生部部长李德全、副部长崔义田，中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复，外交部美澳司副司长林平等。（附图片）
