Title: Chairman Liu, Premier Zhou, and Foreign Minister Chen Send Congratulatory Messages on the National Day of the Kingdom of Yemen
Author:
Date: 1960-10-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd - Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, sent a telegram today to King Ahmad of the Kingdom of Yemen, congratulating him on the National Day of the Kingdom of Yemen. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Majesty Imam Ahmad Ibn Yahya Hamidaddin Nasser Lidinillah, King of the Mutawakkilite Kingdom of Yemen:

On the occasion of the National Day of the Kingdom of Yemen, I extend my warm congratulations to Your Majesty and the people of Yemen on behalf of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, and in my own name. I wish the traditional friendship between China and Yemen will grow stronger and become more consolidated day by day. I wish the Kingdom of Yemen prosperity and its people happiness. I wish Your Majesty good health.

            Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                  October 22, 1960, in Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd - Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, sent a telegram today to Crown Prince Badr, Deputy Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Yemen, congratulating him on the National Day of the Kingdom of Yemen. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Royal Highness Saif al-Islam Muhammad al-Badr, Crown Prince and Deputy Prime Minister of the Mutawakkilite Kingdom of Yemen:

On the occasion of the National Day of the Kingdom of Yemen, I extend my sincere congratulations to Your Highness and the government of the Kingdom of Yemen on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China, and in my own name.

The Chinese people are very pleased to see that in recent years, the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Yemen have further developed on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. I firmly believe that this is not only fully in line with the common interests of both countries, but also conducive to the noble cause of safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.

The Chinese government and people have always sympathized with and supported the just struggle of the Yemeni people against imperialist aggression and to defend national sovereignty and national independence, and firmly believe that this struggle will surely be victorious.

I wish Your Highness and the Yemeni people new achievements in building their own country and safeguarding the cause of world peace.

                          Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                                      October 22, 1960, in Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd - Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram today to Crown Prince Badr, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Yemen, congratulating him on the National Day of the Kingdom of Yemen. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Royal Highness Saif al-Islam Muhammad al-Badr, Crown Prince, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mutawakkilite Kingdom of Yemen:

On the occasion of your country's National Day, I extend to Your Highness my warm congratulations. I wish the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Yemen greater development. I wish Your Highness new achievements in promoting the friendly unity of Asian and African countries and safeguarding peace of Asia and the world.

            Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
                                      October 22, 1960, in Beijing

### 刘主席周总理陈外长  电贺也门王国国庆

1960-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席周总理陈外长
    电贺也门王国国庆
    新华社22日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇今天打电报给也门王国国王艾哈迈德，祝贺也门王国国庆。电文如下：也门穆塔瓦基利亚王国国王伊马姆·艾哈迈德·伊本·叶海亚·哈米德丁·纳赛尔·里迪尼拉陛下：
    欣逢也门王国国庆，我谨代表中华人民共和国和中国人民，并且以我个人的名义，向陛下和也门人民表示热烈的祝贺。祝中、也两国间的传统友谊日益增进和巩固。祝也门王国繁荣昌盛，人民幸福。祝陛下身体健康。
            中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                  1960年10月22日于北京
    新华社22日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给也门王国副首相巴德尔王太子，祝贺也门王国国庆。电文如下：也门穆塔瓦基利亚王国副首相塞弗·伊斯兰·穆罕默德·巴德尔王太子殿下：
    值此也门王国国庆之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府，并且以我个人的名义，向殿下和也门王国政府表示热诚的祝贺。
    中国人民很高兴地看到，近年来，中国和也门两国之间的友好合作关系在和平共处五项原则的基础上有了进一步的发展。我深信，这不仅完全符合于两国的共同利益，而且有利于维护亚洲和世界和平的崇高事业。
    中国政府和人民一贯同情和支持也门人民反对帝国主义侵略、保卫国家主权和民族独立的正义斗争，并且坚信，这一斗争一定能够胜利。
    祝殿下和也门人民在建设自己的国家和维护世界的和平事业中获得新的成就。
                          中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                      1960年10月22日于北京
    新华社22日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅今天打电报给也门王国副首相兼外交大臣巴德尔王太子，祝贺也门王国国庆。电文如下：也门穆塔瓦基利亚王国副首相兼外交大臣塞弗·伊斯兰·穆罕默德·巴德尔王太子殿下：
    欣逢贵国国庆，我谨向殿下致以热烈的祝贺。祝中、也两国的友好合作关系获得更大的发展。祝殿下在促进亚非各国友好团结和维护亚洲和世界和平的事业中获得新的成就。
            中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长　陈毅
                                      1960年10月22日于北京
