Title: Signing Ceremony of Joint Statement by Eight Chinese People's Organizations and Twelve Visiting Delegations from Various Japanese Circles Held in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1960-10-04
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd. A joint statement was issued tonight in Beijing by eight Chinese people's organizations, including the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and twelve visiting delegations from various Japanese circles who were invited to visit China and participate in the celebrations of the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The signing ceremony of the joint statement was held tonight in Beijing.

The eight Chinese people's organizations that issued the joint statement are: Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, All- China Federation Of Trade Unions, All-China Journalists Association, All-China Women's Federation, Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and All-China Youth Federation. The twelve Japanese visiting delegations from various circles are: Japanese National Council Against Revision of the Japan-U.S. Security Treaty Delegation, Japanese National Railway Workers' Union Delegation, Japanese Coal Miners' Union Delegation, Japanese Private Railway Workers' Union Federation Delegation, Japanese Harbor Workers' Union Delegation, Japanese Peace Committee Delegation, Japanese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee Delegation, Japan-China Friendship Association Delegation, Japanese Drama Visiting Performance Troupe, Japanese Journalists Conference Delegation, Japanese National Federation of Commerce and Industry Organizations Delegation, and Japanese Democratic Youth League Delegation.

Signing the joint statement on behalf of the eight Chinese people's organizations were: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Li Jiebo, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Wu Lengxi, Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, Hu Yuzhi, Vice-President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Chao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation. Signing the joint statement on behalf of the twelve Japanese visiting delegations were: Takeshi Toba, Head of the Japanese National Council Against Revision of the Security Treaty Delegation, Soichi Hosoi, Head of the Japanese National Railway Workers' Union Delegation, Reijiro Takagaki, Head of the Japanese Coal Miners' Union Delegation, Hiroshi Sato, Head of the Japanese Private Railway Workers' Union Federation Delegation, Takeo Igarashi, Head of the Japanese Harbor Workers' Union Delegation, Kenjiro Yanagida, Head of the Japanese Peace Committee Delegation, Tokumatsu Sakamoto, Head of the Japanese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee Delegation, Shinichiro Omori, Head of the Japan-China Friendship Association Delegation, Tomoyoshi Murayama, Head of the Japanese Drama Visiting Performance Troupe, Yuichi Kobayashi, Head of the Japanese Journalists Conference Delegation, Teizaburo Kono, Head of the Japanese National Federation of Commerce and Industry Organizations Delegation, and Minoru Maeda, Head of the Japanese Democratic Youth League Delegation.

After the signing, Chinese and Japanese figures applauded warmly and toasted each other in celebration.

Chinese participants in the signing ceremony included: Bao Erhan, Xu Guangping, Hou Debang, Cai Tingkai, Zhao Puchu, Yang Hansheng, Xia Yan, Tian Han, Ouyang Yuqian, Zhu Xuefan, Ji Chaoding, Wu Han, Lao She, Shao Lizi, Zhou Erfu, Mei Lanfang, Wang Yunsheng, Zhang Zhixiang, Huang Changshui, Zhuang Mingli, Lü Ji, and Yang Zhihua, among others.

All members of the twelve Japanese delegations participated in the signing ceremony.

Japanese peace activist in Beijing, Kinkazu Saionji, and his wife also attended the signing ceremony.

### 中国八个人民团体和日本各界十二个访华代表团  共同声明签字仪式昨日在京举行

1960-10-04
第3版()
专栏：

    中国八个人民团体和日本各界十二个访华代表团
    共同声明签字仪式昨日在京举行
    新华社3日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会等八个中国人民团体与应邀访问中国并参加中华人民共和国成立十一周年庆祝典礼的日本各界十二个访华代表团发表共同声明，共同声明的签字仪式今夜在北京举行。
    发表共同声明的，中国八个人民团体是：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国亚非团结委员会、中国人民对外文化协会、中华全国总工会、中华全国新闻工作者协会、中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会、中国人民外交学会、中华全国青年联合会。日本各界十二个访华代表团是：日本阻止修改日美安全条约国民会议访华代表团、日本国营铁路工会访华代表团、日本煤矿工会访华代表团、日本私营铁路工会总联合会访华代表团、日本港湾工会访华代表团、日本和平委员会访华代表团、日本亚非团结委员会访华代表团、日本中国友好协会访华代表团、日本话剧访华演出团、日本新闻工作者会议访华代表团、日本全国商工团体联合会访华代表团、日本民主青年同盟访华代表团。
    分别代表中国八个人民团体在共同声明上签字的是：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，中华全国总工会副主席李颉伯，中华全国新闻工作者协会主席吴冷西，中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会副主席李德全，中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之，中华全国青年联合会副主席张超。分别代表日本各界十二个访华代表团在共同声明上签字的是：日本阻止修改安全条约国民会议代表团团长户叶武，日本国营铁路工会访华代表团团长细井宗一，日本煤矿工会访华代表团团长高垣礼二郎，日本私营铁路工会总联合会访华代表团团长佐藤弘，日本港湾工会访华代表团团长五十岚武雄，日本和平委员会访华代表团团长柳田谦十郎，日本亚非团结委员会访华代表团团长坂本德松，日本中国友好协会访华代表团团长大森真一郎，日本话剧访华演出团团长村山知义，日本新闻工作者会议访华代表团团长小林雄一，日本全国商工团体联合会访华代表团团长河野贞三郎，日本民主青年同盟访华代表团团长前田稔。
    签字以后，中日两国人士热烈鼓掌，并且互相敬酒祝贺。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的有包尔汉、许广平、侯德榜、蔡廷锴、赵朴初、阳翰笙、夏衍、田汉、欧阳予倩、朱学范、冀朝鼎、吴晗、老舍、邵力子、周而复、梅兰芳、王芸生、张致祥、黄长水、庄明理、吕骥、杨之华等。
    日本方面参加签字仪式的有日本各界十二个代表团的全体成员。
    日本在京和平人士西园寺公一及夫人也参加了签字仪式。
