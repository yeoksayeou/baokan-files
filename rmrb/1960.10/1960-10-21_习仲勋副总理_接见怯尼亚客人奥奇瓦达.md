Title: Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun Receives Kenyan Guest Ochwada
Author:
Date: 1960-10-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th - Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun received Ochwada, General Secretary of the Kenya Federation of Labor, General Secretary of the East African Construction Workers' Union, and Deputy General Secretary of the Kenya African National Union, who is currently visiting our country, this morning. Present at the reception were Fang Ming, Cai Ying, and others.

### 习仲勋副总理  接见怯尼亚客人奥奇瓦达

1960-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

    习仲勋副总理
    接见怯尼亚客人奥奇瓦达
    新华社20日讯　习仲勋副总理今天上午接见了正在我国访问的怯尼亚工会大会总书记、东非建筑工会总书记、怯尼亚非洲民族联盟副总书记奥奇瓦达。接见时在座的，有方明、蔡英平等。
