Title: International Association of Democratic Lawyers Holds Congress
Author:
Date: 1960-10-12
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 11th - The Seventh Congress of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers opened on the 10th in the Bulgarian Parliament Hall in Sofia. Thus far, 360 delegates from 48 countries have arrived to participate in the congress, including representatives from twelve socialist countries such as the Soviet Union and China, as well as representatives from countries such as Cuba, Algeria, Japan, India, Ceylon, Britain, France, and the United States.

### 国际法律工作者协会举行代表大会

1960-10-12
第6版()
专栏：

    国际法律工作者协会举行代表大会
    新华社索非亚11日电　国际民主法律工作者协会第七届代表大会，10日在索非亚市的保加利亚议会大厅开幕。到目前为止，到达这里参加大会的有四十八个国家的三百六十名代表，其中有苏联和中国等十二个社会主义国家的代表和古巴、阿尔及利亚、日本、印度、锡兰、英国、法国、美国等国家的代表。
