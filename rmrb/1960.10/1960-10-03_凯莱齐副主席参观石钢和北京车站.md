Title: Vice Chairman Kellezi Visits Shijingshan Iron & Steel and Beijing Railway Station
Author:
Date: 1960-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd October: The Albanian-China Friendship Association delegation, headed by Abdyl Këllezi, Member of the Central Commitee of the Albanian Labour Party, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Chairman of the Albanian-China Friendship Association, and accompanied by Li Xiannian, Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice-Premier of the State Council, visited Shijingshan Iron & Steel Company this morning. Albanian Ambassador to China, Piliqti, also joined the visit. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by the workers of Shijingshan Iron & Steel, who expressed their close friendship.

Vice Chairman Kellezi and other distinguished guests arrived at Shijingshan Iron & Steel at 9:00 a.m. Xiao Ping, Secretary of the Party Committee of Shijingshan Iron & Steel Company, first introduced the history, production and construction of Shijingshan Iron & Steel to the distinguished guests. Then, the distinguished guests visited the blast furnace, coking oven, steelmaking plant and welded steel pipe plant. At each location, the operating workers waved to the distinguished guests. After inquiring about the production situation, Vice Chairman Kellezi warmly shook hands and embraced the veteran workers and the secretary of the factory party committee, and presented them with medals of Sino-Albanian friendship. While looking at the big-character posters of workers dedicating their achievements to the National Day celebration, Vice Chairman Kellezi praised their production achievements. He told his entourage that the delegation would also write a big-character poster to wish the workers to overfulfill their tasks.

The distinguished guests presented Shijingshan Iron & Steel workers with a banner embroidered with "Long Live Sino-Albanian Friendship" and Albanian pictorials. Xiao Ping, on behalf of all the workers of the company, presented the distinguished guests with commemorative products of Shijingshan Iron & Steel.

This afternoon, Vice Chairman Kellezi and other distinguished guests, accompanied by Guo Lu, Vice Minister of Railways, visited the Beijing Railway Station and the Great Hall of the People. Vice Chairman Kellezi wrote a message in the commemorative book of the Beijing Railway Station. He wrote: The magnificent Beijing Railway Station has left us with a deep impression. The glorious Chinese Communist Party and the Chinese government have once again given the passengers a major project to make it more convenient for the working people to travel. This shows what the Chinese people can do under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao.

Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, also accompanied the distinguished guests today.

### 凯莱齐副主席参观石钢和北京车站

1960-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    凯莱齐副主席参观石钢和北京车站
    据新华社2日讯　以阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、部长会议副主席、阿中友好协会主席阿卜杜勒·凯莱齐为首的阿中友协代表团全体贵宾，由中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理李先念陪同，今天上午参观了石景山钢铁公司。阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使帕里夫蒂也同往参观。贵宾们受到了石钢职工的热情欢迎，并和职工们互相表达了亲密无间的友好情谊。
    凯莱齐副主席等贵宾上午九时到达石钢，石钢公司党委书记肖平首先向贵宾们介绍了石钢的历史和生产建设情况。然后，贵宾们参观了高炉、焦炉、炼钢厂和焊接钢管厂。每到一处，正在操作的职工们都向贵宾们招手致意，凯莱齐副主席每问过生产情况后，都和老工人、厂党委书记等热情地握手、拥抱，并给他们佩带中阿友好纪念章。凯莱齐副主席一边看着工人们向国庆献礼的大字报，一边称赞他们的生产成绩，他告诉随同人员，代表团也要写一张大字报，预祝职工们超额完成任务。
    贵宾们向石钢职工赠送了绣着“阿中友谊万岁”的锦旗和阿尔巴尼亚画报，肖平代表全公司职工把石钢的纪念产品赠送给了贵宾。
    今天下午，凯莱齐副主席等贵宾，由铁道部副部长郭鲁陪同，参观了北京火车站和人民大会堂。凯莱齐副主席在北京车站纪念簿上留言，他写道：北京规模宏大的车站给我们留下了深刻的印象，光荣的中国共产党和中国政府又一次送给旅客们一项重大工程，让劳动人民旅行更方便。这说明中国人民在中国共产党和毛主席领导下会做出什么来。
    今天陪同贵宾参观的，还有外交部苏欧司副司长陈伯清。
