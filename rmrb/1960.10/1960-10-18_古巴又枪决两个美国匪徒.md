Title: Cuba Executes Two More American Bandits

Author:
Date: 1960-10-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 16th: Two more Americans, Alan Thompson and Robert Fuller, who landed in Oriente Province, Cuba, on October 5 to invade Cuba, were executed this morning in Santiago, Cuba, according to law. These two American invaders were captured by Cuban farmer militiamen on the 14th, and were sentenced to death yesterday by the Santiago Revolutionary Court. Another American invader was executed last week.

Alan Thompson, who was executed this morning, was a veteran who participated in the Korean War, and Robert Fuller was the son of a large American plantation owner who owned land in Cuba.

The other two Cuban counter-revolutionaries who invaded with the Americans were sentenced to thirty years in prison.

### 古巴又枪决两个美国匪徒

1960-10-18
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴又枪决两个美国匪徒
    新华社哈瓦那16日电　10月5日在古巴奥连特省登陆入侵古巴的另外两个美国人阿兰·汤普森和罗伯特·富勒今天清晨在古巴圣地亚哥城依法被枪决。这两个美国入侵犯是在14日被古巴农民民兵拿获的，昨天被圣地亚哥革命法庭判处死刑。另一名美国入侵犯已在上周被枪决。
    今晨伏法的阿兰·汤普森是一个曾经参加过侵略朝鲜战争的退伍军人，罗伯特·富勒是在古巴拥有土地的一个美国大庄园主的儿子。
    和美国人一起入侵的另外两名古巴反革命分子被判处三十年徒刑。
