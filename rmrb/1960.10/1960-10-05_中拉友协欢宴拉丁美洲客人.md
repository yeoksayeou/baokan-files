Title: China-Latin America Friendship Association Hosts Banquet for Latin American Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-10-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, Chu Tunan, held a banquet tonight to welcome the Brazilian cultural delegation headed by Pelegrino Ronnivor and the Argentinian cultural delegation headed by Isidro López, and to congratulate the head of the Mexican cultural delegation, former interim President of Mexico, Emilio Portes Gil, on his seventieth birthday.

Chilean peace activist Venturieri and his wife were also invited to attend the banquet.

Zhou Erfu, Vice President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, also attended the banquet.

### 中拉友协欢宴拉丁美洲客人

1960-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    中拉友协欢宴拉丁美洲客人
    新华社3日讯　中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南今晚举行宴会，欢迎以贝雷格里诺·龙尼沃尔为首的巴西文化代表团和以伊西特罗·洛佩斯为首的阿根廷文化代表团，并祝贺墨西哥文化代表团团长、墨西哥前临时总统埃米略·波尔特斯·希耳七十寿辰。
    智利和平人士万徒勒里和夫人也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有中拉友好协会副会长周而复。
