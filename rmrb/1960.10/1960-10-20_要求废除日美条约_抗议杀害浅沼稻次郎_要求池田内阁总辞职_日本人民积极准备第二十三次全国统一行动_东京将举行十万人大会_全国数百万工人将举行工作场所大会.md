Title: Demanding the Abolition of the U.S.-Japan Treaty, Protesting the Assassination of Inejiro Asanuma, Demanding the Resignation of the Ikeda Cabinet: Japanese People Actively Prepare for the 23rd National Unified Action; A 100,000-Strong Rally to Be Held in Tokyo; Millions of Workers Nationwide to Hold Workplace Rallies
Author:
Date: 1960-10-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 19th: News from Tokyo: People across Japan are actively preparing to hold the 23rd National Unified Action against the new U.S.-Japan "Security Treaty" on the 20th, demanding the abolition of the U.S.-Japan military alliance treaty, protesting the assassination of Inejiro Asanuma, and demanding the resignation of the Ikeda Cabinet.

　　The General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo), with 3.7 million members, decided today to hold workplace rallies throughout the country on the 20th and decided to observe a minute of silence for Asanuma at 1:00 p.m. on the 20th.

　　The Federation of Independent Unions, with 800,000 members, will also hold workplace rallies. Sohyo and other mass organizations will also hold regional rallies in forty locations across the country to protest the assassination of Asanuma.

　　In Tokyo, approximately 100,000 workers, students, cultural figures, and others will hold a central national rally against the new "Security Treaty," protesting the assassination of Asanuma and demanding the resignation of the Ikeda Cabinet. A demonstration will also be held after the rally.

　　The Socialist Party will hold a party funeral for Inejiro Asanuma at Hibiya Public Hall on the afternoon of the 20th. Representatives from various political parties, trade unions, and mass organizations will attend the funeral.

　　The Tokyo Metropolitan Council for Joint Struggle to Uphold Peace and Democracy, with 500,000 members, decided yesterday that it will continue its struggle after holding the 23rd National Unified Action against the "Security Treaty" on the 20th.

　　The decision passed by the meeting emphasized that in carrying out the movement against the Ikeda Cabinet, those politicians responsible for the forced passage of the new "Security Treaty" should be purged from the political arena.

　　The Joint Struggle Council also decided that it will launch active activities to expose the deceptive policies pursued by the Ikeda Cabinet, to crush the reactionary propaganda of the Ikeda government that so-called "left-wing violence" and right-wing violence should be equally "suppressed," and to fight for the overthrow of the Ikeda Cabinet.

　　According to the decision, member organizations of the Joint Struggle Council will also hold public gatherings, launch signature campaigns, and conduct propaganda campaigns.

### 要求废除日美条约　抗议杀害浅沼稻次郎　要求池田内阁总辞职  日本人民积极准备第二十三次全国统一行动  东京将举行十万人大会　全国数百万工人将举行工作场所大会

1960-10-20
第5版()
专栏：

    要求废除日美条约　抗议杀害浅沼稻次郎　要求池田内阁总辞职
    日本人民积极准备第二十三次全国统一行动
    东京将举行十万人大会　全国数百万工人将举行工作场所大会
    新华社19日讯　东京消息：日本各地人民正在积极准备在20日举行第二十三次反对新日美“安全条约”全国统一行动，要求废除日美军事同盟条约，抗议杀害浅沼稻次郎，要求池田内阁总辞职。
    拥有三百七十万会员的日本工会总评议会今天决定20日在全国各地举行工作场所大会，并且决定在20日下午一点为浅沼默哀一分钟。
    拥有八十万会员的中立工会联合会也将举行工作场所大会。“总评”和其他群众团体还将在全国四十个地方举行抗议杀害浅沼的地区大会。
    在东京，工人、学生、文化界人士等共约十万人将举行反对新“安全条约”，抗议杀害浅沼和要求池田内阁总辞职的中央国民大会。会后还将举行示威游行。
    社会党将于20日下午在日比谷公会堂为浅沼稻次郎举行党葬。参加葬礼的将有各政党、工会和各界群众团体的代表。
    拥有五十万会员的东京都维护和平与民主共同斗争会议昨天决定，它将在20日举行过第二十三次反对“安全条约”全国统一行动以后，继续进行斗争。
    这次会议通过的决定强调指出，在进行反对池田内阁的运动中，应当把那些对强行通过新“安全条约”一事负责的政客从政界中清洗出去。
    共同斗争会议还决定，它将展开积极活动，揭露池田内阁奉行的欺骗政策，粉碎池田政府进行的所谓“左翼暴力”和右翼暴力同样应当受到“取缔”的反动宣传，为推翻池田内阁而斗争。
    根据决定，共同斗争会议的会员团体还将举行公众集会，展开签名运动和宣传运动。
