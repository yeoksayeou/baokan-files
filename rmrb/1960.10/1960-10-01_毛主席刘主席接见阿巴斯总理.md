Title: Chairman Mao and Chairman Liu Receive Prime Minister Abbas
Author:
Date: 1960-10-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th September. Chairman Mao Zedong and Chairman Liu Shaoqi today received Ferhat Abbas, Prime Minister of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, and the members of the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria led by him: Minister of Interior Ben Tobbal Akhdar, Director of the Prime Minister's Office Ben Yahia Mohammed, Director of Political Affairs of the Propaganda Department Boumendjel Ahmed, Director of Political Affairs of the Ministry of Interior Hahn Lamine, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were: Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers He Long and Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Zhang Aiping, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs He Ying, and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department Ge Buhai. ### 毛主席刘主席接见阿巴斯总理

1960-10-01
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席刘主席接见阿巴斯总理
    新华社30日讯　毛泽东主席、刘少奇主席今天接见了阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理阿巴斯·费尔哈特，以及由他率领的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团成员：内政部长本·陶巴勒·阿赫达尔，总理办公室主任本·雅希亚·穆罕默德，宣传部政治事务主任布门杰勒·艾哈迈德，内政部政治事务主任哈恩·拉明，并且和他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有：董必武副主席，朱德委员长，周恩来总理，贺龙、陈毅副总理，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张爱萍上将，对外贸易部副部长李强，外交部西亚非洲司司长何英，礼宾司副司长葛步海。（附图片）
