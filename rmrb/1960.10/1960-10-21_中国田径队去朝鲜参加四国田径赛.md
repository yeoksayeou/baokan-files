Title: Chinese Track and Field Team to Participate in Four-Nation Track and Field Meet in North Korea
Author:
Date: 1960-10-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th — A Chinese men's and women's track and field team of fifteen members left Beijing by train this morning for the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. They were invited to participate in the traditional China, Mongolia, Vietnam, and North Korea four-nation track and field meet to be held in Pyongyang from the 23rd to the 24th.

The Chinese men's and women's track and field team is led by Liu Dexiu, and the coach is Yu Zhangyan. The nine male athletes are: Chen Jiaquan, Gao Zhengquan (sprints), Li Henian (middle distance), Su Wenren (long distance), Zhou Yuguang (hurdles), Tian Zhaozhong (triple jump), Kang Jubei (high jump), Sun Jiuyuan (discus), He Yongxian (shot put); the four female athletes are: Liu Xingyu (long jump), Wang Shiyu (discus), Wu Fushan (high jump), Xu Shizhang (middle distance).

### 中国田径队去朝鲜参加四国田径赛

1960-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    中国田径队去朝鲜参加四国田径赛
    新华社20日讯　中国男女田径队一行十五人，今天上午乘火车离北京前往朝鲜民主主义人民共和国，他们是应邀参加从23日到24日在平壤举行的传统性的中、蒙、越、朝四国田径赛的。
    中国男女田径队由刘德修率领，教练是俞樟炎，九名男选手是：陈家全、高正铨（短跑）、李贺年（中距离跑）、苏文仁（长跑）、周裕光（跨栏）、田兆钟（三级跳远）、康驹培（跳高）、孙久远（铁饼）、贺永宪（铅球）；四名女选手是：刘兴玉（跳远）、王士玉（铁饼）、吴浮山（跳高）、徐世璋（中距离跑）。
