Title: Sino-Albanian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Protocol Signed; Our Scientific and Technological Delegation Leaves Tirana for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-10-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 20th - The Sino-Albanian Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation signed the protocol of its fifth session in Tirana on October 18, 1960.

The fifth session of the Sino-Albanian Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation was conducted in a sincere and friendly atmosphere, with both sides expressing their complete willingness to further strengthen and develop scientific and technological cooperation.

According to the provisions of the protocol, the People's Republic of China will provide scientific and technological assistance to Albania, including the provision of materials, seeds and saplings, as well as the dispatch of experts in agriculture, light industry and handicrafts. Albania will provide China with various agricultural data, seeds of new and improved varieties of crops, fruit tree saplings, and information on hygiene.

The meeting discussed the experiences gained by both China and Albania through the exchange of various materials, and pointed out the valuable assistance received by both sides.

Liu Ruilong, the Chinese Chairman of the Sino-Albanian Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation and Vice Minister of Agriculture, signed the protocol on behalf of the Chinese side. Ilias Reka, the Albanian Chairman of the Sino-Albanian Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation and Vice Minister of Agriculture, signed on behalf of the Albanian side.

Taqi Skëndi, the Albanian Minister of Health, and Luo Shigao, the Chinese Ambassador to Albania, attended the signing ceremony.

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 19th - The Chinese delegation, led by Liu Ruilong, Vice Minister of Agriculture, which came to attend the fifth session of the Sino-Albanian Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation, left Tirana at noon on the 19th.

Albanian Vice Minister of Agriculture Reka, Sokoli, Director of the Technical Cooperation Department of the Council of Ministers, and Luo Shigao, our Ambassador to Albania, were at the airport to see them off.

On the eve of their departure, Ambassador Luo Shigao held a reception to celebrate the successful conclusion of the fifth session of the Sino-Albanian Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation and the delegation's visit to Albania.

The delegation stayed in Albania for a total of nineteen days, visiting thirteen districts including the capital Tirana, Shkodër, and Korçë. While visiting Peshkopi in the north, the delegation was received by Haxhi Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of Albania, who was on vacation there. Koleka, Acting Chairman of the Albanian Council of Ministers, also received all members of the delegation in Tirana on the 17th. During both receptions, the Albanian leaders had cordial and friendly conversations with the delegation.

During its stay in Albania, the delegation also visited agricultural production cooperatives named "Albania-China Friendship" and "New China," conveying greetings from the Chinese people and 500 million farmers to the members of the cooperatives, and presenting them with gifts. The members of both cooperatives warmly welcomed the delegation as if celebrating a festival.

### 中阿科技合作议定书签字  我科技代表团离地拉那回国

1960-10-21
第6版()
专栏：

    中阿科技合作议定书签字
    我科技代表团离地拉那回国
    新华社地拉那20日电　中阿科学技术合作联合委员会于1960年10月18日在地拉那签定了第五届会议议定书。
    中阿科学技术合作联合委员会第五届会议是在诚挚友好的气氛中进行的，双方表示完全愿意进一步加强和发展相互间的科学技术合作。
    根据议定书的规定，中华人民共和国将向阿尔巴尼亚提供科学技术援助，其中包括供给资料、种子和树苗以及派遣农业、轻工业和手工业方面的专家。阿尔巴尼亚将向中国提供农业方面的各种资料，各种新的优良品种的农作物种子和果树苗以及有关卫生方面的资料。
    会议谈到了中阿双方通过相互提供的各种资料所取得的经验，并指出双方得到的宝贵帮助。
    代表中国方面在议定书上签字的是中阿科学技术合作联合委员会中方主席、农业部副部长刘瑞龙、代表阿尔巴尼亚方面在议定书上签字的是中阿科学技术合作联合委员会阿方主席、农业部副部长伊利亚斯·雷卡。
    阿尔巴尼亚卫生部部长塔齐·斯坎迪和中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高出席了签字仪式。
    新华社地拉那19日电　前来参加中阿科技合作联合委员会第五届会议的中国代表团，由农业部副部长刘瑞龙率领，在19日中午离开地拉那。
    阿尔巴尼亚农业部副部长雷卡、部长会议技术合作司司长索科利和我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高都到机场送行。
    临行前夕，罗士高大使为中阿科技合作联合委员会第五届会议的胜利结束和代表团访阿，举行了酒会。
    代表团在阿尔巴尼亚共逗留了十九天，先后在首都地拉那和斯库台、科尔察等十三个区参观访问。代表团在访问北部的佩什科比时，曾经受到正在那里休假的阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席列希的接见。阿尔巴尼亚部长会议代理主席科列加17日在地拉那也接见了代表团全体人员。在两次接见中，阿尔巴尼亚领导人都和代表团作了亲切友好的谈话。
    代表团在阿尔巴尼亚期间还访问了以“阿中友好”和“新中国”命名的农业生产合作社，代表中国人民和五亿农民向合作社的社员们问好，并向他们赠送了礼品。这两个合作社的社员们都像过节似地热情欢迎了代表团。
