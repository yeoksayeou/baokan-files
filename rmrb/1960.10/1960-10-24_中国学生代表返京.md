Title: Chinese Student Representatives Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. Wang Wei, head of the Chinese student delegation to the 6th Congress of the International Union of Students, and some members of the delegation, returned to Beijing by plane from Baghdad this afternoon.

### 中国学生代表返京

1960-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中国学生代表返京
  　新华社23日讯　参加国际学联第六届代表大会的中国学生代表团团长王伟和部分团员，今天下午乘飞机从巴格达回到北京。
