Title: Premier Zhou Continues Talks with Premier Abbas
Author:
Date: 1960-10-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai and Premier of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, Abbas Ferhat, continued talks this morning in a friendly atmosphere.

    Participating in the talks on the Algerian side were Minister of the Interior Ben Tobbal Akhdar, Director of the Prime Minister's Office Ben Yahia Mohammed, Director of Political Affairs of the Propaganda Department Boumendjel Ahmed, and Director of Political Affairs of the Ministry of the Interior Hahn Lamine.

    Participating in the talks on the Chinese side were Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Zhang Aiping, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, and Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs He Ying.

### 周总理同阿巴斯总理继续会谈

1960-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理同阿巴斯总理继续会谈
    新华社3日讯　国务院总理周恩来同阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理阿巴斯·费尔哈特，今天上午在友好的气氛中继续举行会谈。
    阿尔及利亚方面参加会谈的，有内政部长本·陶巴勒·阿赫达尔，总理办公室主任本·雅希亚·穆罕默德，宣传部政治事务主任布门杰勒·艾哈迈德，内政部政治事务主任哈恩·拉明。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有国务院副总理陈毅，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张爱萍上将，对外贸易部副部长李强，外交部西亚非洲司司长何英。
