Title: Prime Minister Koirala Hosts Banquet for Both Delegations to the Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Commission, First Meeting of the Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Commission Concludes
Author:
Date: 1960-10-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th, Kathmandu Dispatch: Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala held a reception on the evening of the 26th at the Singh Durbar, hosting the Chinese delegation and the Nepalese delegation to the China-Nepal Joint Boundary Commission.

The Chinese side attending the banquet included Chief Representative and Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, Zhang Shijie, Representatives Senior Colonel Lü Yishan and Lieutenant Colonel Deng Ting, and advisors Qi Guangbo and Tu Guowei.

The Nepalese side attending the banquet included Chief Representative Major General Padam Bahadur Khatri, Representatives Major Aditya Shumshere, Captain Bharat Keshari Singh, Mr. Narayan Prasad Raj Bhandari, and advisor Netra Bahadur Thapa.

Also attending the banquet were Nepalese cabinet ministers, the Commander-in-Chief, the Chief Justice, senior civil and military officials, and diplomatic envoys stationed in Kathmandu.

The banquet proceeded in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th, Kathmandu Dispatch: The first meeting of the China-Nepal Joint Boundary Commission held its eleventh, and final, session at the Singh Durbar in Kathmandu from 10:00 AM to 11:30 AM on the morning of the 26th.

The talks were conducted in a friendly and cordial atmosphere. During this session, both sides agreed on a communiqué scheduled to be issued simultaneously in Kathmandu and Beijing on October 29, 1960.

### 为参加中尼边界联合委员会的双方代表团  柯伊拉腊首相设宴招待  中尼边界联合委员会第一次会议结束

1960-10-28
第6版()
专栏：

    为参加中尼边界联合委员会的双方代表团
    柯伊拉腊首相设宴招待
    中尼边界联合委员会第一次会议结束
    新华社27日讯　加德满都消息：尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊26日晚上在狮宫举行招待会，招待参加中国—尼泊尔边界联合委员会的中国代表团和尼泊尔代表团。
    中国方面出席宴会的有首席代表、中国驻尼泊尔大使张世杰，代表吕义山大校和邓汀中校，以及顾问齐光波和屠国维。
    尼泊尔方面出席宴会的有首席代表帕达姆·巴哈杜尔·卡特里少将，代表阿迪塔·舒姆谢尔少校，巴拉特·克夏里·辛格上尉，纳拉扬·普拉萨德·拉志·班达里先生，以及顾问内特拉·巴哈杜尔·塔普。
    出席宴会的还有尼泊尔的内阁大臣、总司令、首席法官、高级文武官员，以及驻加德满都的外交使节。
    宴会是在亲切和友好的气氛中进行的。
    新华社27日讯　加德满都消息：中国—尼泊尔边界联合委员会第一次会议26日上午十点到十一点三十分在加德满都狮宫举行了第十一次，也就是最后一次会谈。
    会谈是在友好和亲切的气氛中进行的。双方在这次会谈中同意了预定1960年10月29日在加德满都和北京同时发表的一项公报。
