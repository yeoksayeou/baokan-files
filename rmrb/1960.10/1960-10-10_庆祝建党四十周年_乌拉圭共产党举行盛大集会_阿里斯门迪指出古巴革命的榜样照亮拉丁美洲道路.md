Title: Celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the Party: Uruguayan Communist Party Holds Grand Meeting; Arismendi Points Out that the Example of the Cuban Revolution Illuminates the Path for Latin America
Author:
Date: 1960-10-10
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, 9th. The Uruguayan Communist Party held a grand meeting at the Peñarol Palace in Montevideo, the capital of Uruguay, to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the party's founding.

    Three thousand people, including delegations from all over the country, attended the meeting. When members of the Party's Central Committee and representatives of fraternal parties from Argentina, Chile, Spain, Brazil, and other countries appeared, they were warmly welcomed by the participants who filled the entire venue.

    Enrique Pastorino, Secretary of the Uruguayan Communist Party, delivered the opening speech and read out congratulatory messages from the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Communist Party of China, and fraternal parties from other socialist countries and European and Latin American countries.

    Rodney Arismendi, First Secretary of the Uruguayan Communist Party, summarized the 40-year history of struggle of the Uruguayan Communist Party and analyzed the current international and domestic situation. He pointed out that the people's liberation struggles in various countries are surging, and imperialism and large landowners are constantly suffering defeats. Arismendi said that the example of the Cuban Revolution illuminates the path for Latin America, and the Havana Declaration is a fundamental document. When he mentioned this, the people attending the meeting cheered for the Cuban Revolution for several minutes.

    Arismendi accused the government of implementing policies that favor imperialism and large landowners. He pointed out that the only way to defeat reactionaries and oligarchs is through the unity of all anti-imperialist, progressive, and advanced forces. Rodolfo Ghiold, Secretary of the Argentine Communist Party, and César Godoy Urrutia, a member of the Central Committee of the Chilean Communist Party, delivered congratulatory speeches at the meeting.

    A cultural performance was held after the meeting.

    The Uruguayan Communist Party was founded on September 21, 1920. Celebrations have been held throughout the country since September 21 this year.

### 庆祝建党四十周年  乌拉圭共产党举行盛大集会  阿里斯门迪指出古巴革命的榜样照亮拉丁美洲道路

1960-10-10
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝建党四十周年
    乌拉圭共产党举行盛大集会
    阿里斯门迪指出古巴革命的榜样照亮拉丁美洲道路
  新华社9日讯　蒙得维的亚消息：乌拉圭共产党在乌拉圭首都蒙得维的亚的佩纳罗耳宫举行盛大集会，庆祝共产党成立四十周年。
    有包括全国各地的代表团在内的三千人参加了这次集会。当党的中央委员会成员以及阿根廷、智利、西班牙、巴西等兄弟党的代表出现的时候，他们受到了挤满整个会场的参加者的热烈欢迎。
    乌拉圭共产党的书记恩里克·帕斯托里诺致开幕词，并且宣读了苏联共产党、中国共产党及其它社会主义国家和欧洲、拉丁美洲国家兄弟党的贺电。
    乌拉圭共产党第一书记罗德内·阿里斯门迪在会上总结了乌拉圭共产党四十年来的斗争历史，并且分析了目前的国际和国内形势。他指出，各国人民的解放斗争正在高涨，帝国主义和大庄园主不断遭到失败。阿里斯门迪说，古巴革命的榜样照亮了拉丁美洲的道路，哈瓦那宣言是根本的文件。当他谈到这一点的时候，参加集会的人们欢呼古巴革命达几分钟之久。
    阿里斯门迪指责政府执行有利于帝国主义和大庄园主的政策。他指出：战胜反动派和寡头政治的唯一道路，是一切反帝、进步和先进力量的团结一致。阿根廷共产党书记罗多耳福·基奥迪和智利共产党中央委员会委员塞萨·戈多伊·乌鲁蒂亚在会上致了祝词。
    集会后举行了文艺晚会。
    乌拉圭共产党是在1920年9月21日成立的，今年从9月21日以来，全国各地就展开了庆祝活动。
