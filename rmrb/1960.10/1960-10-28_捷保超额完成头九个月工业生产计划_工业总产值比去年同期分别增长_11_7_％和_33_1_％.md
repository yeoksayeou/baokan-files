Title: Czechoslovakia and Bulgaria Exceed Industrial Production Plans for the First Nine Months; Total Industrial Output Value Increases by 11.7% and 33.1% Respectively Compared to the Same Period Last Year
Author:
Date: 1960-10-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Prague—The Czechoslovak State Statistical Office announced here that the industrial production plan for the first nine months of this year was exceeded by 1.2%, and the total industrial output value increased by 11.7% compared to the same period last year.

	In the first three quarters of this year, electricity production reached 17.637 billion kilowatt-hours, an increase of 12% compared to the same period last year; pig iron production was 3.488 million tons, and steel production was 5.027 million tons, an increase of 10.8% and 11% respectively compared to the same period last year; hard coal production was 22.191 million tons, and lignite was 40.727 million tons, an increase of 5% and 6.9% respectively compared to the same period last year; cement production reached 3.765 million tons, an increase of 5.9% compared to the same period last year.

	The production of tractors reached 25,725 units, an increase of nearly 21% compared to the same period last year.

	The output of electricity, steel, and steel products in the first three quarters of this year equaled the entire year's output in 1957; the output of passenger cars and televisions almost equaled the entire year's output in 1958 and 1959, respectively.

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia—In the first nine months of this year, Bulgaria's total industrial output value plan was fulfilled by 100.6%, and the total output value increased by 33.1% compared to the same period last year, exceeding the plan by 174 million leva worth of products.

	According to a communiqué issued here today by the Central Statistical Bureau of the Bulgarian Council of Ministers regarding the implementation of the national economic plan for the first nine months of this year, Bulgaria produced a total of 3.348 billion kilowatt-hours of electricity in the first nine months, 21% more than the same period last year; 12.568 million tons of coal, 12% more than the same period last year; 149,000 tons of crude oil, 4% more than the same period last year; 183,000 tons of steel, 8% more than the same period last year. Compared with the same period last year, Bulgaria produced 32% more internal combustion engines, 39% more electric motors, 45% more nitrogen and phosphate fertilizers, 6% more cotton cloth, and 46% more sugar during this period.

	Regarding agriculture, the communiqué stated that in the first nine months of this year, the total amount of agricultural work completed by machine tractor stations nationwide was 8% more than the same period last year. During the nine months, the country added a total of 7,150 tractors (calculated at 15 horsepower each), more than 1,400 grain combines, and many other agricultural machines. In the first nine months, the number of livestock and the output of livestock products also increased.

	The communiqué stated that in the first nine months of this year, labor productivity in Bulgaria's electric power, chemical and rubber, non-ferrous metallurgy, and textile industrial sectors increased by more than 2.5% to 20% compared to the same period last year.

### 捷保超额完成头九个月工业生产计划  工业总产值比去年同期分别增长11.7％和33.1％

1960-10-28
第6版()
专栏：

    捷保超额完成头九个月工业生产计划
    工业总产值比去年同期分别增长11.7％和33.1％
    新华社布拉格电　捷克斯洛伐克国家统计局在这里公布，今年头九个月的工业生产计划超额完成了1.2％，工业总产值比去年同期增长了11.7％。
    今年头三个季度的电力产量达到一百七十六亿三千七百万度，比去年同期增加12％；生铁产量为三百四十八万八千吨，钢产量为五百零二万七千吨，分别比去年同期增长10.8％和11％；硬煤产量为二千二百一十九万一千吨，褐煤为四千零七十二万七千吨，分别比去年同期增加5％和6.9％；水泥产量达到三百七十六万五千吨，比去年同期增加5.9％。
    拖拉机的产量达到了二万五千七百二十五台，比去年同期增加了将近21％。
    今年头三季的电力、钢和钢材的产量等于1957年全年的产量；载客汽车和电视机的产量几乎分别等于1958年和1959年全年的产量。
    新华社索非亚电　今年头九个月，保加利亚工业总产值计划完成了100.6％，总产值比去年同期增长了33.1％，超计划生产了价值一亿七千四百万列弗的产品。
    据这里今天发表的保加利亚部长会议中央统计局关于今年头九个月国民经济计划执行情况的公报说，今年头九个月中，保加利亚工业共生产了三十三亿四千八百万度电，比去年同期多21％；一千二百五十六万八千吨煤，比去年同期多12％；十四万九千吨原油，比去年同期多4％；十八万三千吨钢，比去年同期多8％。和去年同期相比，保加利亚在这期间所生产的内燃发动机增多了32％，电动机多39％，氮肥和磷肥多45％，棉布多6％，沙糖多46％。
    在农业方面，公报说，今年头九个月中，全国机器拖拉机站完成的农业工作总量比去年同期多8％。九个月中全国共增加了七千一百五十台拖拉机（每台按十五马力计算）、一千四百台以上谷物联合收割机和许多别的农业机器。在头九个月中，牲畜头数和畜产品产量也增加了。
    公报中说，今年头九个月，保加利亚电力、化学和橡胶、有色冶金和纺织工业部门中的劳动生产率比去年同期提高了2.5％到20％以上。
