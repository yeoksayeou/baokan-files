Title: Cuban Military and Civilians Crush U.S. Armed Aggression (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1960-10-27
Page: 6

    Cuban Military and Civilians Crush U.S. Armed Aggression

    Above: Cuban militiamen are searching for U.S.-backed counter-revolutionary armed bandits in the Escambray Mountains.

    Center: Cuban Minister of Revolutionary Armed Forces, Raúl Castro, interrogates captured counter-revolutionary bandits. This group of bandits landed in Oriente Province in eastern Cuba on October 5th, waving the American flag, but were immediately annihilated.

    Below: Weapons, M-3 submachine guns, military supplies, and the parcels used for airdrops seized by Cuban military and civilians from U.S. airdrops to the armed bandits in the Escambray Mountains. The parcels bear the word "United States."

                                       (Xinhua News Agency)

### 古巴军民粉碎美国武装侵略（图片）

1960-10-27
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴军民粉碎美国武装侵略
    上：古巴民兵们正在埃斯坎布拉伊山搜索美国支持的反革命武装匪徒。
    中：古巴革命武装部部长劳尔·卡斯特罗在审问被俘的反革命匪徒。这股匪徒于10月5日打着美国国旗在东部奥连特省登陆，但立即被歼灭了。
    下：古巴军民缴获的美国给埃斯坎布拉伊山的武装匪徒空投的武器M—3式来福枪、军事物资和空投时所用的邮包，上面有“美国”字样。
                                       （新华社稿）
