Title: Chairman Mao, Chairman Liu, Premier Zhou, and Foreign Minister Chen Send Congratulatory Messages on the Second Anniversary of the Founding of the Republic of Guinea
Author:
Date: 1960-10-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 1st: Chairman Mao Zedong today sent a telegram to President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea, thanking him for his congratulations on China's National Day and expressing sincere congratulations on Guinea's National Day. The telegram reads as follows:
To His Excellency President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea, Conakry:

	I sincerely thank Your Excellency for your warm congratulatory telegram on the occasion of the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	On the occasion of the National Day of the Republic of Guinea, please accept my sincere congratulations. I wish the Republic of Guinea, under Your Excellency's leadership, new victories and achievements in opposing colonialism, safeguarding national independence, and building its own country. May the fraternal friendship between the people of China and Guinea be further consolidated and developed.
			Mao Zedong
	October 1, 1960, Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, October 1st: Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, today sent a telegram to President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea, congratulating him on the second anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Guinea. The telegram reads as follows:
To His Excellency President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea:

	On the occasion of the second anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Guinea, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Guinea on behalf of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, and in my own name.

	The Chinese people are very pleased to see that the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Guinea have been further consolidated and developed through Your Excellency's recent friendly visit to China and the signing of the Treaty of Friendship and other agreements between the two countries. May the militant friendship between the people of China and Guinea, forged in the struggle against imperialism and colonialism and for the maintenance of world peace, last forever. I wish Your Excellency and the heroic people of Guinea greater victories in the cause of safeguarding national independence and promoting national prosperity. May the Republic of Guinea be prosperous and its people happy.

	Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
October 1, 1960, Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, October 1st: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, today sent a telegram to President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea, congratulating him on the second anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Guinea. The telegram reads as follows:
To His Excellency President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea:

	On the occasion of the second anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Guinea, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the Government of the Republic of Guinea on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China, and in my own name.

	In the past two years, the heroic people of Guinea have achieved significant victories in the struggle against imperialism and colonialism and for the maintenance of national independence, thus setting a shining example for the national independence movement in Africa and winning the respect of the people of the world.

	The Chinese people are very pleased to see that the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Guinea, based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, have greatly developed. Your Excellency's recent friendly visit to our country and the signing of the Treaty of Friendship and agreements between China and Guinea mark a new stage in the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Guinea. This not only conforms to the vital interests of our two peoples, but also helps to strengthen the friendship and solidarity of the people of Asia and Africa and to maintain world peace.

	May the militant friendship between China and Guinea be continuously consolidated and developed.

Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
October 1, 1960, Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, October 1st: Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, today sent a telegram to President and Minister of Foreign Affairs Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea, congratulating him on the second anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Guinea. The telegram reads as follows:
To His Excellency President and Minister of Foreign Affairs Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea:

	On the occasion of the second anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Guinea, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency. I wish Your Excellency new achievements in strengthening the friendship and cooperation between China and Guinea, supporting the African people's struggle against colonialism, promoting the unity of the people of Asia and Africa, and maintaining the noble cause of world peace.

Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
October 1, 1960, Beijing

### 毛主席　刘主席　周总理　陈外长  电贺几内亚共和国成立二周年

1960-10-02
第4版()
专栏：

    毛主席　刘主席　周总理　陈外长
    电贺几内亚共和国成立二周年
    新华社1日讯　毛泽东主席今天致电几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔，感谢他对我国国庆的祝贺，并对几内亚国庆表示诚挚的祝贺。电文如下：科纳克里几内亚共和国塞古·杜尔总统阁下：
    衷心地感谢阁下在中华人民共和国建国十一周年的时候，给我们的热情的贺电。
    当此几内亚共和国国庆之际，请接受我诚挚的祝贺。祝几内亚共和国，在总统阁下的领导下，在反对殖民主义、维护民族独立和建设自己的国家方面，获得新的胜利和成就。祝中几两国人民兄弟般的友谊日益巩固和发展。
                        毛泽东
          1960年10月1日于北京
    新华社1日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇今天打电报给几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔，祝贺几内亚共和国成立二周年。电文如下：几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔先生阁下：
    值此几内亚共和国成立二周年之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国和中国人民，并且以我个人的名义向阁下和几内亚人民表示热烈的祝贺。
    中国人民非常高兴地看到，通过阁下不久前对中国的友好访问，和两国友好条约及其他协定的签订，中国和几内亚两国间的友好合作关系得到了进一步的巩固和发展。祝中几两国人民在反对帝国主义和殖民主义、维护世界和平的斗争中所结成的战斗友谊万古常青。祝阁下和英雄的几内亚人民在维护民族独立和促进国家繁荣的事业中取得更大的胜利。祝几内亚共和国昌盛、人民幸福。
  中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
          1960年10月1日于北京
    新华社1日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔，祝贺几内亚共和国成立二周年。电文如下：几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔先生阁下：
    欣逢几内亚共和国成立二周年，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府，并且以我个人的名义向阁下和几内亚共和国政府致热烈的祝贺。
    两年来，英雄的几内亚人民在反对帝国主义和殖民主义、维护民族独立的斗争中取得了重大的胜利，从而为非洲的民族独立运动树立了一个光辉的榜样，并且赢得了世界人民的尊敬。
    中国人民非常高兴地看到，中国和几内亚两国间建立在和平共处五项原则基础上的友好合作关系获得了很大的发展。不久以前，总统阁下对我国的友好访问和中几两国间签定的友好条约和协定，标志着中几两国之间的友好合作关系进入了一个新的阶段。这不仅符合我们两国人民的切身利益，而且有助于加强亚非各国人民的友谊和团结和维护世界和平。
    祝中国和几内亚之间的战斗友谊不断巩固和发展。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理
                        周恩来
          1960年10月1日于北京
    新华社1日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅今天打电报给几内亚共和国总统兼外交部长塞古·杜尔，祝贺几内亚共和国成立二周年。电文如下：几内亚共和国总统兼外交部长塞古·杜尔先生阁下：
    值此几内亚共和国成立二周年之际，我谨向阁下表示热烈的祝贺。祝阁下在加强中几两国间的友谊与合作，支持非洲人民反对殖民主义的斗争、促进亚非各国人民的团结和维护世界和平的崇高事业中获得新的成就。
    中华人民共和国外交部长
                        陈毅
          1960年10月1日于北京
