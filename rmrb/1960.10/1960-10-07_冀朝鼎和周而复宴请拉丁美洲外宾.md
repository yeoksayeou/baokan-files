Title: Ji Chaoding and Zhou Erfu Host Banquet for Latin American Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 6th - Ji Chaoding, Vice President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association and Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and Zhou Erfu, Vice President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, hosted a banquet tonight for cultural delegations and people from the trade, film, and legal fields from countries such as Dominica, Bolivia, Panama, Argentina, and Chile.

### 冀朝鼎和周而复宴请拉丁美洲外宾

1960-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    冀朝鼎和周而复宴请拉丁美洲外宾
    新华社6日讯　中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长、中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎，中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复今晚举行宴会，招待多米尼加、玻利维亚、巴拿马、阿根廷、智利等国的文化代表团和贸易、电影、法律界人士。
