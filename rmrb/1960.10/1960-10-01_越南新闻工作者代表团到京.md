Title: Vietnamese Journalists Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The two-member Vietnamese Journalists Delegation, invited by the All-China Journalists Association to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by train on the morning of the 29th. The head of the delegation is Nguyen Thanh Le, Secretary-General of the Vietnamese Journalists Association and Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "Nhan Dan" newspaper, and the member is Hoang Van Hung, Deputy Director of the Vietnam Broadcasting Bureau and Deputy Director of "Voice of Vietnam" radio station.

An Gang and Deng Gang, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the All-China Journalists Association, and journalists from the capital went to the station to welcome them.

### 越南新闻工作者代表团到京

1960-10-01
第5版()
专栏：

    越南新闻工作者代表团到京
    新华社30日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问的越南新闻工作者代表团一行二人，29日上午乘火车到达北京。代表团团长是越南新闻工作者协会秘书长、“人民报”副总编辑阮成黎，团员是越南广播局副局长、“越南之声”电台副台长黄文兴。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会书记处书记安岗、邓岗以及首都新闻界人士前往车站欢迎。
