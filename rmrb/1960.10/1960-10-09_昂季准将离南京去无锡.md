Title: Brigadier General Aung Gyi Leaves Nanjing for Wuxi
Author:
Date: 1960-10-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, October 7th: Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the chief representative of the Myanmar side of the Sino-Myanmar Joint Boundary Committee and Deputy Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces of the Union of Myanmar, and representatives and advisors of the Myanmar delegation, accompanied by Yao Zhongming, the chief representative of the Chinese side of the Sino-Myanmar Joint Boundary Committee, and representatives of the Chinese delegation, arrived in Nanjing from Beijing on a special train on the 6th. After a day of visiting in Nanjing, they left for Wuxi on a special train on the evening of the 7th.
	During their stay in Nanjing, the distinguished guests paid their respects at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum, toured Linggu Temple and Xuanwu Lake, and visited the Nanjing Museum and the Nanjing Automobile Manufacturing Plant. On the evening of the 6th, Guan Wenwei, Vice Governor of Jiangsu Province, and Chen Yang, Mayor of Nanjing, hosted a banquet to welcome the distinguished guests. After the banquet, the distinguished guests watched a Peking Opera performance.
	When Brigadier General Aung Gyi and other distinguished guests arrived and departed from Nanjing, Guan Wenwei, Vice Governor of Jiangsu Province, Lieutenant General Zhang Caiqian, Commander of the Nanjing Military Region, and Chen Yang, Mayor of Nanjing, and others were at the station to welcome and see them off.

### 昂季准将离南京去无锡

1960-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    昂季准将离南京去无锡
    新华社南京7日电　中缅边界联合委员会缅甸首席代表、缅甸联邦国防军副总参谋长昂季准将和缅甸代表团的代表、顾问，由中缅边界联合委员会中国首席代表姚仲明和中国代表团的代表陪同，6日由北京乘专车到达南京，在南京参观访问了一天以后，7日晚间乘专车前往无锡。
    贵宾们在南京期间，曾晋谒了中山陵，游览了灵谷寺、玄武湖，参观了南京博物院和南京汽车制造厂。6日晚上，江苏省副省长管文蔚和南京市市长陈扬曾设宴欢迎贵宾。宴会后，贵宾们观看了京剧演出。
    昂季准将等贵宾到达和离开南京时，江苏省副省长管文蔚、南京部队首长张才千中将、南京市市长陈扬等，都到车站欢迎和欢送。
