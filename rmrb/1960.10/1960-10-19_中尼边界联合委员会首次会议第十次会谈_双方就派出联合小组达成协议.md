Title: The Tenth Meeting of the First Session of the Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Commission: Both Sides Reach Agreement on Dispatching Joint Teams
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, 18th — It was reported from Kathmandu that the first meeting of the China-Nepal Joint Boundary Commission held its tenth session at the Lion Palace at 11:30 a.m. on the 17th. The meeting was conducted in a friendly and harmonious atmosphere. Both sides unanimously reached an agreement on a series of specific issues concerning the dispatch of joint teams. The next meeting will be held at 10:00 a.m. on October 26th.

### 中尼边界联合委员会首次会议第十次会谈  双方就派出联合小组达成协议

1960-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中尼边界联合委员会首次会议第十次会谈
    双方就派出联合小组达成协议
    新华社18日讯　加德满都消息：中国—尼泊尔边界联合委员会第一次会议17日上午十一时三十分在狮宫举行第十次会谈。会谈是在友好和融洽的气氛中进行的。双方在关于派出联合小组的一系列具体问题上一致达成协议。下次会谈将在10月26日上午十时举行。
