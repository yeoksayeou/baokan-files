Title: Tunisian Neo-Destour Party Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th. The Tunisian Neo-Destour Party delegation, headed by Ben Amar Khassib, concluded its visit to our country and left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.
　　
Those who saw them off at the airport included Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Cao Mengjun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation, Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, and Wang Chuanbin, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Youth Federation.

### 突尼斯新宪政党代表团回国

1960-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    突尼斯新宪政党代表团回国
    新华社20日讯　以本·阿玛尔·哈西卜为首的突尼斯新宪政党代表团结束了在我国的访问，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
  到机场欢送的有中国人民外交学会副秘书长吴晓达，全国妇联书记处书记曹孟君，全国学联主席胡启立，全国青联副秘书长王传斌。
