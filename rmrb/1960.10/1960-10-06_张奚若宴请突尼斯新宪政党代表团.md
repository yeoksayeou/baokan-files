Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet for Tunisian Neo Destour Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-06
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 5th October: Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, held a banquet at the National Culture Palace this evening to warmly welcome all distinguished guests of the Tunisian Neo Destour Party delegation, headed by Ben Amar Hassib, President of the Socio-Economic Revival Department of the Tunisian Neo Destour Party.
　　President Zhang Xiruo first extended a warm welcome on behalf of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to the first delegation from Tunisia to visit China after Tunisia gained independence. He said that the Chinese people and the Tunisian people have a common point: they have both been oppressed by imperialism in the past, and now the people of both countries are working hard to build their own new countries. He said that through the visit of the Tunisian guests, the friendship between the people of the two countries will be further enhanced.
　　Head of Delegation Hassib made an enthusiastic speech at the banquet, saying that after participating in the National Day celebrations in your country, we all personally felt that the Chinese people are organized and are advancing by leaps and bounds. At the same time, we have also learned a lot of experience during our visits, and we will take this experience back and use it in our construction. Head of Delegation Hassib recounted the suffering of the Tunisian people under the oppression of imperialism and introduced the Tunisian people's struggle for independence. He said that we firmly believe that as long as the people are united, victory belongs to the people. Finally, he raised his glass to congratulate the Chinese people on greater leaps forward and to wish the friendship between the people of the two countries to grow day by day.
　　Among those present at the banquet were Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Director of the China Islamic Association, Bao Erhan; Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Lian Guan; Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Qu Wu; Vice President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, Xu Pingyu; Vice Mayor of Beijing, Zhao Pengfei; Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Kang Yonghe; Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation, Cao Mengjun; Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, Wang Zhaohua; Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, Hu Qili, and other people from all walks of life.

### 张奚若宴请突尼斯新宪政党代表团

1960-10-06
第3版()
专栏：

    张奚若宴请突尼斯新宪政党代表团
    新华社5日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，今晚在民族文化宫举行宴会，热烈欢迎以突尼斯新宪政党社会经济复兴部主席本·阿玛尔·哈西卜为首的突尼斯新宪政党代表团的全体贵宾。
    张奚若会长首先代表中国人民外交学会向突尼斯获得独立后第一个来中国访问的代表团表示热烈的欢迎。他说，中国人民和突尼斯人民有个共同点，过去都受过帝国主义的压迫，而现在两国人民都正在为建设自己的新国家而努力。他说，通过突尼斯来宾们的访问，将会进一步增进两国人民之间的友谊。
    哈西卜团长在宴会上热情洋溢地讲话说，我们参加了贵国国庆观礼后，都亲身体会到中国人民是有组织的，正在大踏步前进。同时，在参观访问中，也学到了很多经验，我们将把这些经验带回去，用到我们的建设中去。哈西卜团长讲述了突尼斯人民身受帝国主义压迫的苦难，介绍了突尼斯人民争取独立的斗争。他说，我们坚信只要人民团结一致，胜利就是属于人民的。最后，他举杯，祝贺中国人民更大的跃进，祝贺两国人民之间的友谊与日俱增。
    出席宴会作陪的，有政协全国委员会副主席、中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长连贯，对外文委副主任屈武，中国非洲人民友好协会副会长徐平羽，北京市副市长赵鹏飞，全国总工会书记处书记康永和，全国妇联书记处书记曹孟君，共青团中央书记处书记王照华，全国学联主席胡启立等各方面的人士。
