Title: A Batch of Latin American Cultural Figures Arrive in Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1960-10-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - The Venezuelan cultural delegation headed by Héctor Malavé Guez, the Argentine cultural delegation headed by Isidro López, the Panamanian cultural delegation headed by Rafael A. González, and Chilean cultural figure Osvaldo Fallas and another person, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening. They are all visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association. Ji Chao-ding, Chen Ming, Chen Jin, and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 一批拉丁美洲国家文化界外宾到京

1960-10-01
第5版()
专栏：

    一批拉丁美洲国家文化界外宾到京
    新华社30日讯　以埃克多尔·马尔加诺·古埃约为首的委内瑞拉文化代表团，以伊西特罗·洛佩斯为首的阿根廷文化代表团，以拉法埃尔·阿·冈萨雷斯为首的巴拿马文化代表团，以及智利文化代表奥斯特瓦尔多·法拉斯等二人，今天晚间乘飞机到达北京。他们都是应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请前来中国进行友好访问的。到机场欢迎的有冀朝鼎、陈明、陈金等人。
