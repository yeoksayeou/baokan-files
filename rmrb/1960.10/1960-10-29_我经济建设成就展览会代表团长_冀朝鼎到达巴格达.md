Title: Ji Chao-ding, Head of the Delegation of the Exhibition of Economic Construction Achievements of China, Arrives in Baghdad
Author:
Date: 1960-10-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, October 28th: Ji Chao-ding, Head of the Delegation of the Exhibition of Economic Construction Achievements of the People's Republic of China and Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, arrived in Baghdad by plane this morning. This exhibition will open in Baghdad on November 5th.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included Iraqi Minister of Trade Abdul Latif al-Shawwaf, Director-General of the General Administration of Commerce of the Ministry of Trade Hafiz Tukmachi, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yassoub Rafiq, Acting Director of the Exhibition Bureau of the Ministry of Trade Fadl Abdullah, Director of the Domestic Trade Bureau of the Ministry of Trade Hassan Hakak, and other Iraqi officials.

Also at the airport to welcome him were Chinese Ambassador Zhang Weilie, Commercial Counselor Li Xinnong, and Zhang Ziquan, the curator of the exhibition hall who had arrived earlier.

### 我经济建设成就展览会代表团长  冀朝鼎到达巴格达

1960-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    我经济建设成就展览会代表团长
    冀朝鼎到达巴格达
    新华社巴格达28日电　中华人民共和国经济建设成就展览会代表团团长、中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎今天早晨乘飞机到达巴格达。这个展览会将于11月5日在巴格达开幕。
    到机场欢迎的有伊拉克贸易部长阿卜杜勒·拉蒂夫·沙瓦夫、贸易部商业总管理局局长哈菲兹·图克马奇、外交部礼宾司司长亚苏布·拉菲克、贸易部展览局代理局长法德·阿卜杜拉、贸易部国内贸易局局长哈桑·哈卡克和伊拉克的其他官员。
    到机场欢迎的还有中国大使张伟烈、商务参赞李新农和先已到达这里的展览馆馆长张子泉。
