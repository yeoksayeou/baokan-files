Title: Hungarian Mathematician Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 14th. Academician Béla Szőkefalvi-Nagy, a Hungarian mathematician who is visiting and lecturing in China under the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Hungarian Academy of Sciences scientific cooperation agreement, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

### 匈一数学家到京

1960-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

    匈一数学家到京
    新华社14日讯　根据中匈两国科学院科学合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问讲学的匈牙利数学家谢盖法维—拉其·别拉院士，今天乘飞机到达北京。
