Title: Women's Delegation to Hungary Returns to Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 4
    
    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Chinese women's delegation of four people, led by Li Baoguang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation, returned to Beijing by plane today after concluding its visit to Hungary.

### 我赴匈访问的妇女代表团回京

1960-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    我赴匈访问的妇女代表团回京
    新华社4日讯　由全国妇联书记处书记李宝光率领的中国妇女代表团一行四人，结束在匈牙利的访问后，今天乘飞机回到北京。
