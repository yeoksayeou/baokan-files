Title: Vice Chairman Kellezi Departs Hangzhou for Nanjing
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, October 15th: The Albanian China Friendship Association delegation led by Vice Chairman Abdyl Kellezi, along with the Chairman of the Albanian Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Misto Treska, and the Secretary-General of the Albanian Writers and Artists Association, Shevket Musaraj, arrived in Nanjing this morning by train from Hangzhou for a visit. Albanian Ambassador to China, Prifti, accompanied the delegation.

Alternate Member of the CPC Central Committee and President of the Sino-Albanian Friendship Association, Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission, Liu Mingfu, and Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chen Boqing, accompanied the distinguished Albanian guests to Nanjing.

The guests were warmly welcomed at the Nanjing Railway Station by the Secretary of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the CPC and Governor of Jiangsu Province, Hui Yuyu, Alternate Secretary of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the CPC, Peng Chong, Head of the Jiangsu Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Major General Duan Huanjing, First Secretary of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the CPC and Mayor of Nanjing, Chen Yang, and hundreds of others.

Huge welcome banners and colorful flags were hung at the Nanjing Railway Station today. At around 8 a.m., as the special train carrying the guests entered the station, firecrackers, music, gongs and drums, and applause resounded from the welcoming crowds. Many young men and women dressed in festive attire waved bouquets, extending their warm greetings to the guests. Vice Chairman Kellezi and other distinguished guests also accepted flowers presented by Young Pioneers.

Afterwards, the guests traveled by car to the guesthouse to rest.

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, October 14th : Member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Vice Chairman of the Council of Minister, and Chairman of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association, Abdyl Kellezi, and the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation he led, left Hangzhou by special train tonight for a visit to Nanjing. Chairman of the Albanian Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Misto Treska, Secretary-General of the Albanian Writers and Artists Association, Shevket Musaraj, and Albanian Ambassador to China, Prifti, also traveled on the same train.

Secretary of the Secretariat of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the CPC, Lin Hujia, Standing Committee Member of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the CPC, Zhou Rongxin, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province, Ren Yili, Head of the Zhejiang Garrison, Lieutenant General Qian Jun, as well as responsible persons from the Party and government of Hangzhou, and responsible persons from people's organizations and local organizations of democratic parties in Zhejiang Province and Hangzhou, went to the station to see them off.

This morning, Vice Chairman Kellezi and others visited the Zhejiang Academy of Agricultural Sciences, the farm tool manufacturing factory of the Jianqiao People's Commune, and the Hangzhou Du Jinsheng Silk Weaving Factory. They were warmly welcomed by the commune members and workers.

Chairman of the Albanian Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Misto Treska, and Secretary-General of the Albanian Writers and Artists Association, Shevket Musaraj, visited the Zhejiang Academy of Fine Arts this morning. This afternoon, Vice Chairman Kellezi and all the distinguished guests also visited a water conservancy construction project.

### 凯莱齐副主席离杭州到南京

1960-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    凯莱齐副主席离杭州到南京
    新华社南京15日电　由阿卜杜勒·凯莱齐副主席率领的阿尔巴尼亚中国友好协会代表团，以及阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会主席米斯托·特雷斯卡，阿尔巴尼亚作家艺术家协会秘书长谢夫卡特·穆萨莱，今天上午乘火车从杭州到达南京访问。阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使帕里夫蒂和代表团同车前来。
    中共中央候补委员、中阿友好协会会长蒋南翔，国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫，外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清等陪同阿尔巴尼亚贵宾来到南京。
    贵宾们在南京车站受到中共江苏省委书记、江苏省省长惠浴宇，中共江苏省委候补书记彭冲，中国人民解放军江苏军区首长段焕竞少将，中共南京市委第一书记、南京市市长陈扬等数百人的热烈欢迎。
    南京车站上今天悬挂着巨幅欢迎标语和彩旗。上午八时许，当贵宾们乘坐的专车驶进车站时，欢迎群众的行列里响起了爆竹声、乐曲声、锣鼓声和鼓掌声，许多穿着节日盛装的男女青年挥舞着花束，向贵宾们亲切致意。凯莱齐副主席等贵宾还接受了少先队员的献花。
    接着，贵宾们乘车前往宾馆休息。
    新华社杭州14日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、部长会议副主席、阿中友好协会主席阿卜杜勒·凯莱齐和他率领的阿中友好协会代表团，今晚乘专车离开杭州去南京访问。阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会主席米斯托·特雷斯卡、阿尔巴尼亚作家艺术家协会秘书长谢夫卡特·穆萨莱和阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使帕里夫蒂也同车前往。
    中共浙江省委书记处书记林乎加、中共浙江省委常委周荣鑫、浙江省副省长任一力、浙江驻军首长钱钧中将以及杭州市的党和政府负责人，浙江省和杭州市人民团体、民主党派地方组织的负责人等都到车站欢送。
    今天上午，凯莱齐副主席等参观访问了浙江农业科学院、笕桥人民公社的农具制造厂和杭州都锦生丝织厂。他们受到了公社社员和工人们的热烈欢迎。
    阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会主席米斯托·特雷斯卡和阿尔巴尼亚作家艺术家协会秘书长谢夫卡特·穆萨莱，今天上午参观了浙江美术学院。今天下午，凯莱齐副主席和全体贵宾还参观了水利建设工程。
