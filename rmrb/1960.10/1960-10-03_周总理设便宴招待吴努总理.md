Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Informal Dinner for Prime Minister U Nu
Author:
Date: 1960-10-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd October. Premier Zhou Enlai and his wife hosted an informal dinner today in honor of Prime Minister U Nu and his wife and daughter.

    Dr. U E Maung, Minister of Education and Justice of the Union of Burma, U Myint Aung, Attorney General of the Union of Burma, U Wang, Advisor to the Prime Minister, and Kyao Winn, Burmese Ambassador to China, attended the dinner.

    Also attending the dinner were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang, Director of the Treaty Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yao Zhongming, and Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yu Peiwen.

### 周总理设便宴招待吴努总理

1960-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理设便宴招待吴努总理
    新华社2日讯　周恩来总理和夫人今天设便宴招待吴努总理和夫人及女儿。
    缅甸联邦政府教育和司法部部长吴埃貌博士，缅甸联邦检察总长吴延昂，总理顾问吴旺和缅甸驻华大使叫温，参加了便宴。
    参加便宴的，还有外交部副部长章汉夫、中国驻缅甸大使李一氓，外交部条约司司长姚仲明和外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文。
