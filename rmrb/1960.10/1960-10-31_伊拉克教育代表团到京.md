Title: Iraqi Education Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-31
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th. The Iraqi education delegation of ten members, led by Dr. Muhammad Rashid Filler, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to China according to the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Iraqi Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Cui Zhongyuan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Education, Xu Fangting, Director of the General Education Department, and He Ying, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Iraqi Ambassador to China Fadil and First Secretary Jiafu also welcomed them at the airport.

### 伊拉克教育代表团到京

1960-10-31
第2版()
专栏：

    伊拉克教育代表团到京
    新华社30日讯　根据中伊文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问的伊拉克教育代表团一行十人，在团长穆罕默德·拉希德·菲勒博士率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有教育部办公厅主任崔仲远、普通教育司司长徐方庭以及外交部西亚非洲司司长何英等。
    伊拉克驻华大使法迪尔和一等秘书贾夫也到机场欢迎。
