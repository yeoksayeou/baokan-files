Title: Sino-US Ambassadors Hold 101st Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 18th — The 101st meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks was held in Warsaw this afternoon. The meeting lasted two hours and ten minutes. Afterwards, both sides announced that the next meeting will be held on December 1st.

### 中美大使举行第101次会谈

1960-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使举行第101次会谈
    新华社华沙18日电　中美大使级会谈第一百零一次会议今天下午在华沙举行。这次会谈历时二小时十分钟。会后双方宣布，下次会谈将在12月1日举行。
