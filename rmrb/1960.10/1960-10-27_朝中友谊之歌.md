Title: Song of Korean-Chinese Friendship
Author: Delie
Date: 1960-10-27
Page: 8

    Song of Korean-Chinese Friendship
    [Korean Folk Song]
    I
    On the slopes of Moranbong,
    Cranes are swinging;
    Above Tiananmen Square,
    Peace doves fly freely.
    We sing aloud of spring,
    Sing of the bright spring of a new Asia;
    We sing aloud of peace,
    Sing of the peace won by the struggle
    Of the Korean and Chinese people, hand in hand.

    II The song of the Chollima of Pyongyang,
    Resounds through the clouds,
    The Great Leap Forward of Beijing,
    Like the rising sun of the East,
    Radiates brilliantly.
    Sing!
    Sing of the unbreakable friendship between the Korean and Chinese people.
    We stand on the same glorious battle line,
    For a common purpose,
    Singing heartily forever.

    III The pines and cypresses of Changbai Mountain,
    Stand solemnly on the mountain peaks,
    The bugle call from the trenches of Shangganling,
    Still resonates in our ears today.
    The heroic deeds of the volunteers,
    Like an eternal and immortal precious legend,
    Will be passed down through the ages!
          [Translated by Delie]

### 朝中友谊之歌
德烈译
1960-10-27
第8版()
专栏：

    朝中友谊之歌
    〔朝鲜民歌〕
    一
    在牡丹峰山坡，
    起重机挥动着；
    在天安门上空，
    和平鸽自由飞翔。
    我们放声歌唱春天，
    歌唱，新亚洲明媚的春天；
    我们放声歌唱和平，
    歌唱朝中人民紧握双手，
    用斗争赢得的和平。
    二平壤的千里马歌声，
    响彻云霄，
    北京的大跃进，
    像东方的朝阳，
    光芒四射。
    歌唱吧！
    歌唱朝中人民牢不可破的友谊。
    我们站在光荣的一条战线上，
    为了共同的目的，
    永远纵情高歌。
    三长白山的松柏，
    肃然耸立在山尖上，
    上甘岭战壕的吹号声，
    如今，还像在我们耳边震响。
    志愿军指战员的英雄业绩，
    像永恒不灭的珍奇传说，
    万古流传！
          〔德烈译〕
