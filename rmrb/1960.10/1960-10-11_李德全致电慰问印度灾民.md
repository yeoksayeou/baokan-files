Title: Li Dequan Sends Condolences to Indian Flood Victims
Author:
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th — Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross Society, sent a telegram today to Mrs. Amrit Kaur, Chairman of the Central Managing Committee of the Indian Red Cross Society, expressing condolences for the floods suffered in Orissa, Punjab and other states of India. The Chinese Red Cross Society also remitted 30,000 yuan in Renminbi to the Indian Red Cross Society today to help alleviate the suffering of the disaster victims.

### 李德全致电慰问印度灾民

1960-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    李德全致电慰问印度灾民
    新华社6日讯　中国红十字会总会会长李德全今天打电报给印度红十字会中央理事会主席阿姆里特·考尔夫人，对印度奥里萨、旁遮普等邦遭受水灾表示慰问。中国红十字会今天还汇给印度红十字会人民币三万元，帮助减轻灾民的痛苦。
