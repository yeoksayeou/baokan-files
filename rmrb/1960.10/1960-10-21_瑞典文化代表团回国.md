Title: Swedish Cultural Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th. The Swedish Cultural Delegation of four, led by Head of Delegation Mr. Hellmanson, concluded its visit to China and left Beijing by plane this morning to return home. The delegation was invited to visit our country by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.
    Among those seeing them off at the airport was Wen Pengjiu, council member of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 瑞典文化代表团回国

1960-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典文化代表团回国
    新华社20日讯　由团长海尔曼逊率领的瑞典文化代表团一行四人，结束了在中国的访问，今天早晨乘飞机离京回国。代表团是应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的。
    到机场欢送的有中国人民对外文化协会理事温朋久等。
