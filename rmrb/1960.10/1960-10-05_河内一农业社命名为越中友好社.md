Title: Hanoi Agricultural Cooperative Named Vietnam-China Friendship Cooperative
Author:
Date: 1960-10-05
Page: 6

    Hanoi Agricultural Cooperative Named Vietnam-China Friendship Cooperative
    In order to commemorate the ever-lasting friendship between the peoples of Vietnam and China, and to express the brotherly sentiments between the farmers of the two countries, the Hanoi Municipal Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee decided to name the First Advanced Agricultural Cooperative of Tran Phu Township as the "Vietnam-China Friendship Agricultural Production Cooperative." A naming ceremony was held on the 4th, and more than 2,000 cooperative members of the whole township joyfully participated in the grand naming ceremony. Among those attending the naming ceremony were Tran Duy Hung, Chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Committee, Tran Huy Lieu, Vice President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, Ton Quang Phiet, Secretary-General of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, and some representatives of overseas Chinese. Wang Tao, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam, and other diplomats were also invited to participate in the naming ceremony and were warmly welcomed by the cooperative members. Tran Duy Hung, Chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Committee, Tran Dinh Tuan, Director of the Vietnam-China Friendship Agricultural Cooperative, and Chargé d'Affaires Wang Tao spoke at the naming ceremony.

### 河内一农业社命名为越中友好社

1960-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    河内一农业社命名为越中友好社
    为了纪念越中两国人民之间万古长青的友好关系的发展，为了表示两国农民之间的兄弟情谊，越南劳动党河内市委员会和河内市行政委员会决定把陈富乡第一高级农业社命名为“越中友好农业生产合作社”。四日举行了命名仪式，全乡二千多名社员兴高采烈地参加了隆重的命名仪式。参加命名仪式的有河内市政委员会主席陈维兴、越中友好协会副会长陈辉燎、越中友好协会秘书长孙光阀，以及一些华侨代表。中国驻越南大使馆临时代办汪滔和其他外交人员也应邀参加了命名仪式，受到社员们的热烈欢迎。河内市政委员会主席陈维兴、越中友好农业社主任陈庭遵和汪滔代办在命名仪式上讲了话。
