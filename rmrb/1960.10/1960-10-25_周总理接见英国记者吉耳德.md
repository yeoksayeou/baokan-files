Title: Premier Zhou Meets with British Journalist Guild
Author:
Date: 1960-10-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th: Premier Zhou Enlai met this afternoon with British freelance journalist Stuart Guild and his wife, who are currently visiting China.

Present at the meeting was Gong Peng, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 周总理接见英国记者吉耳德

1960-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理接见英国记者吉耳德
    新华社24日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了正在我国访问的英国自由投稿记者斯图尔特·吉耳德和他的夫人。
    接见时在座的有外交部新闻司司长龚澎。
