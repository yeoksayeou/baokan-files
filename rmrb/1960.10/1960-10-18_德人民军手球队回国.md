Title: East German People's Army Handball Team Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The East German Democratic Republic National People's Army handball team, led by Lieutenant Colonel Muck, returned home by plane from Beijing this morning.
    The East German Democratic Republic National People's Army handball team paid friendly visits to Beijing, Shanghai, Nanjing and other places in our country, and competed with the Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" handball team, as well as the Beijing Military Region, Guangzhou Military Region, Nanjing Military Region and Air Force teams. They achieved five victories in five matches, which was warmly praised by the broad masses of Chinese audiences.

### 德人民军手球队回国

1960-10-18
第4版()
专栏：

    德人民军手球队回国
    新华社17日讯　德意志民主共和国国家人民军手球队，由领队缪克中校率领，于今天早晨由北京乘飞机回国。
    德意志民主共和国国家人民军手球队在我国北京、上海、南京等地进行了友好访问，与中国人民解放军“八一”手球队以及北京部队、广州部队、南京部队和空军部队等手球队进行了比赛，获得了五战五捷的成绩，受到中国广大观众的热烈称赞。
