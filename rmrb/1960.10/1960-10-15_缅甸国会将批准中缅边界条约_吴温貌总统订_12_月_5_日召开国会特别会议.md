Title: Burmese Parliament to Approve Sino-Burmese Border Treaty; President U Win Maung Schedules Special Parliamentary Session for December 5th
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, October 14th. According to today's "Rangoon Daily" report, at the suggestion of the Burmese government, Burmese President U Win Maung will convene a special session of the Burmese Parliament on December 5th to approve the Sino-Burmese Border Treaty.

### 缅甸国会将批准中缅边界条约  吴温貌总统订12月5日召开国会特别会议

1960-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸国会将批准中缅边界条约
    吴温貌总统订12月5日召开国会特别会议
    新华社仰光14日电　据“仰光日报”今天报道，在缅甸政府建议下，缅甸总统吴温貌将在12月5日召开缅甸国会特别会议，来批准中缅边界条约。
