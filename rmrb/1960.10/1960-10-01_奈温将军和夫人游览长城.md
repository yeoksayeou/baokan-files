Title: General Ne Win and Wife Visit the Great Wall
Author:
Date: 1960-10-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 30th -- General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces of Burma, and his wife, visited the Great Wall and the Ming Tombs today, and toured the Thirteen Tombs Reservoir.

General Ne Win and his wife, accompanied by General Luo Ruiqing, Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and his wife, and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, traveled by car through layers of mountains and arrived at Badaling at 11:00 a.m. The distinguished guests ascended the Great Wall with great interest, overlooking the scenery beyond the Great Wall. After lunch, the distinguished guests visited the Changling Tomb and the Dingling Tomb. (Attached picture)

### 奈温将军和夫人游览长城

1960-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    奈温将军和夫人游览长城
    新华社30日讯　缅甸国防军总参谋长奈温将军和夫人今天游览了万里长城和明十三陵，参观了十三陵水库。
    奈温将军和夫人由中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿大将和夫人、北京市副市长吴晗陪同，乘坐汽车穿过层层山峦，在上午十一时到达八达岭。贵宾们兴致勃勃地登上长城，居高眺望了塞上风光。午餐后，贵宾们游览了长陵和定陵。（附图片）
    缅甸国防军总参谋长奈温将军和夫人游览长城、十三陵等地。图为奈温将军（左一）和夫人（左二）在罗瑞卿大将陪同下，游览八达岭。贵宾们兴致勃勃地登上长城，眺望塞上风光。
                                  新华社记者　牛畏予摄
