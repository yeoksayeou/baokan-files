Title: Chairman Zhu De Receives Brazilian Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th October: Chairman Zhu De received this afternoon the entire Brazilian Parliamentary Delegation, led by Brazilian Senator and Chairman of the Finance Committee of the Senate, Gaspar Velloso, who are currently visiting our country, and had a friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Zhang Su, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Lian Guan, Deputy Secretary-General, Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, and Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General.

Also present was Shen Jian, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 朱委员长接见巴西议会代表团

1960-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

    朱委员长接见巴西议会代表团
    新华社16日讯　朱德委员长今天下午接见了正在我国访问的以巴西参议员、参议院财政委员会主席加斯帕·贝略索为首的巴西议会代表团全体成员，并且同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员张苏、副秘书长连贯，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、秘书长吴茂荪、副秘书长吴晓达。
    在座的，还有外交部美澳司司长申健。（附图片）
