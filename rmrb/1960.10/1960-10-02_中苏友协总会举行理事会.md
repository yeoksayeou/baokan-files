Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-10-02
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, October 1st: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held its Fourth Meeting of the Third Council on September 29th. The council meeting was presided over by Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Association. Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Association, reported on the main activities of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association this year. The meeting held discussions.

Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, proposed at the meeting that, since he himself found it difficult to balance the work of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, he suggested that Zhang Zhixiang act as the Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association on his behalf. The meeting approved Qian Junrui's suggestion.

### 中苏友协总会举行理事会

1960-10-02
第7版()
专栏：

    中苏友协总会举行理事会
    新华社1日讯　中苏友好协会总会在9月29日举行第三届理事会第四次会议。理事会会议由总会副会长吴玉章主持。总会秘书长钱俊瑞在会上报告了中苏友好协会今年工作的主要情况。会议进行了讨论。
    中苏友协总会秘书长钱俊瑞在会上提出，由于他本人难以兼顾中苏友好协会的工作，建议由张致祥代理中苏友协总会秘书长的职务。会议同意了钱俊瑞的这一建议。
