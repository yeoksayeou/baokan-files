Title: Our Military Delegation Returns to Cairo from Damascus; Marshal Amer Receives Our Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 5th: Marshal Abdel Hakim Amer, Vice President and Minister of Defense of the United Arab Republic, received here at noon today all members of the People's Republic of China military goodwill delegation led by General Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, who are currently visiting here. Present at the reception was Chen Jiakang, Chinese Ambassador to the United Arab Republic.

General Zhang Zongxun and Marshal Amer held cordial talks on the friendly relations between the people and armies of China and the United Arab Republic, as well as the current situation in the Middle East and Africa.

General Zhang Zongxun presented Marshal Amer with a brocade banner embroidered with the military emblem of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and other gifts.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 5th: The Chinese military goodwill delegation led by General Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, returned to Cairo from Damascus this afternoon after a three-day visit to the Syrian region of the United Arab Republic to continue its visit to the Egyptian region of the United Arab Republic.

General Faisal, Commander of the First Army of the United Arab Republic, led senior officers of the First Army to the Damascus airport to see off the Chinese delegation.

On the evening of October 3, all members of the Chinese delegation, accompanied by Major General Kadi, Chief of Staff of the First Army of the United Arab Republic, attended an evening party jointly held by the First Army of the United Arab Republic, the Department of Culture and Guidance of the Syrian Region, and various schools in Damascus to welcome the Chinese military goodwill delegation.

### 我军事代表团从大马士革回到开罗  阿密尔元帅接见我代表团

1960-10-07
第6版()
专栏：

    我军事代表团从大马士革回到开罗
    阿密尔元帅接见我代表团
    新华社开罗5日电　阿拉伯联合共和国副总统兼国防部长阿卜杜勒·哈基姆·阿密尔元帅今天中午在这里接见了正在这里访问的由中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊上将率领的中华人民共和国军事友好代表团的全体团员。接见时在座的有中国驻阿联大使陈家康。
    张宗逊上将和阿密尔元帅就中国和阿联两国人民和军队之间的友好关系以及目前中东和非洲的形势进行了亲切的交谈。
    张宗逊上将向阿密尔元帅赠送了绣着中国人民解放军军徽的锦旗和其它礼物。
    据新华社开罗5日电　由中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊上将率领的中国军事友好代表团在阿联叙利亚地区访问三天以后，今天下午从大马士革回到开罗，继续在阿联埃及地区进行访问。
    阿联第一军军长费萨尔将军率领第一军的高级军官们曾到大马士革机场给中国代表团送行。
    在10月3日晚间，中国代表团的全体团员在阿联第一军参谋长卡迪少将陪同下，出席了由阿联第一军、叙利亚地区文化和指导部以及大马士革各学校为欢迎中国军事友好代表团而联合举办的晚会。
