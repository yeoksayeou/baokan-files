Title: State Council Holds 104th Plenary Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-01
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 30th dispatch:

    The State Council held its 104th plenary meeting this morning.
    The meeting proposed that Premier Zhou Enlai be appointed as the plenipotentiary representative to sign the "Boundary Treaty between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Burma," and requested the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress to review and decide.
    The meeting adopted the "Friendship Treaty between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Guinea" and decided to submit it to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for review and approval.
    The meeting approved the "Agreement on Exchange of Goods and Payments between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Kingdom of Afghanistan," the "Trade and Payments Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Cuba," and the "Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Cuba on Scientific and Technical Cooperation."
    The meeting also approved changes in the administrative divisions of counties and cities, as well as lists of appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行104次全体会议

1960-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    国务院举行104次全体会议
    新华社30日讯　国务院在今天上午举行第一百零四次全体会议。
    会议提出派周恩来总理为签订“中华人民共和国和缅甸联邦边界条约”的全权代表，请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议决定。
    会议通过了“中华人民共和国和几内亚共和国友好条约”，并决定提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议核准了“中华人民共和国政府和阿富汗王国政府交换货物和支付协定”、“中华人民共和国政府和古巴共和国政府贸易和支付协定”、“中华人民共和国政府和古巴共和国政府关于科学和技术合作协定”。
    会议还通过了县、市行政区划变更事项和任免名单。
