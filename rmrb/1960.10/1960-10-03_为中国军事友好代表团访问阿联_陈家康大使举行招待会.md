Title: Ambassador Chen Jiakang Holds Reception for Visit of Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to UAR
Author:
Date: 1960-10-03
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 29th - Chinese Ambassador to the United Arab Republic, Chen Jiakang, held a reception here on the evening of the 29th to welcome the Military Friendship Delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by General Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, who are visiting here.

    Among those present at the reception were: Lieutenant General Mohammed Ibrahim, Minister of State of the UAR responsible for military affairs, Ali Amir, Chief of Staff of the UAR Armed Forces, and other senior military and political officials of the UAR.

    Also present at the reception were: the ambassadors and military attachés of various socialist countries stationed in Cairo, the ambassadors of Finland and Burma stationed in Cairo, the head of the Algerian diplomatic delegation, the representative of the King of Yemen, and the chargé d'affaires of Iraq in Cairo.

    On the morning of the 29th, all members of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation, accompanied by Lieutenant General Mohammed Ibrahim, visited some ordnance factories, where they received a warm welcome.

### 为中国军事友好代表团访问阿联  陈家康大使举行招待会

1960-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    为中国军事友好代表团访问阿联
    陈家康大使举行招待会
    新华社开罗29日电　中国驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使陈家康29日晚在这里举行招待会，招待来这里访问的由中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊上将率领的中华人民共和国军事友好代表团。
    出席招待会的有：阿联负责军事事务的国务部长穆罕默德·易卜拉欣中将、阿联武装部队参谋长阿里·阿密尔和阿联的其他高级军、政官员。
    出席招待会的还有：各社会主义国家驻开罗的大使和武官、芬兰和缅甸驻开罗的大使、阿尔及利亚外交代表团团长、也门国王的代表和伊拉克驻开罗的代办。
    中国军事友好代表团的全体团员29日上午在穆罕默德·易卜拉欣中将陪同下参观了一些军火工厂，他们在这些工厂中受到了热烈的欢迎。
