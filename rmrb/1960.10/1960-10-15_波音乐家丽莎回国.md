Title: Polish Musician Lisa Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 14th. Polish musician Lisa, who came to China for a visit under the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement, left Beijing by plane today to return home after a month-long visit to China.

### 波音乐家丽莎回国

1960-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

    波音乐家丽莎回国
    新华社14日讯　根据中波文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问的波兰音乐家丽莎，在我国访问一个月以后，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
