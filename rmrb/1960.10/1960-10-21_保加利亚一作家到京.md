Title: Bulgarian Writer Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th. Ye.S. Kalanfilov, a Bulgarian writer and editor of "Flame" magazine, arrived in Beijing this afternoon for a visit to China according to the 1960 execution plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 保加利亚一作家到京

1960-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚一作家到京
    新华社20日讯　根据中保文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问的保加利亚作家、“火焰”杂志编辑叶·斯·卡兰菲罗夫，今天下午到达北京。
