Title: Austrian Cultural Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The Austrian cultural delegation, a group of five people led by the head of the delegation, Robert Schollum, have already left Beijing for home on the 7th and 9th.

### 奥地利文化代表团回国

1960-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    奥地利文化代表团回国
    新华社9日讯　奥地利文化代表团一行五人，在团长罗伯特·邵伦率领下，已经在7日、9日先后离开北京回国。
