Title: Chairman of the Indonesian Peace Committee, Siradjuddin Abbas, Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 9th - Siradjuddin Abbas, one of the chairmen of the Indonesian Peace Committee and a member of the Cooperation Council, who was invited to visit our country, and his wife, left Beijing by plane today to visit Shanghai, Guangzhou and other places.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Wang Mingyuan, Deputy Secretary-General, Liu Kefan, Secretary-General of the Chinese Islamic Theological Seminary, and others.

### 印度尼西亚和平委员会主席  西拉朱丁·阿巴斯离京

1960-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚和平委员会主席
    西拉朱丁·阿巴斯离京
    新华社9日讯　应邀前来我国访问的印度尼西亚和平委员会主席之一、合作议会议员西拉朱丁·阿巴斯和他的夫人，今天乘飞机离开北京去上海、广州等地访问。
    到机场欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席包尔汉、副秘书长王明远、中国伊斯兰教经学院秘书长刘克凡等人。
