Title: Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun Receives Cuban Journalists
Author:
Date: 1960-10-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 15th. Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun received five Cuban journalists, including Antonio Ma. Maycas Dominguez, Secretary General of the Cuban Journalists Association, this afternoon and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Zhu Muzhi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, Zhou Erfu, Vice President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, and Wang Yi, First Secretary of the All-China Journalists Association.

### 习仲勋副总理接见古巴新闻工作者

1960-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    习仲勋副总理接见古巴新闻工作者
    新华社15日讯　习仲勋副总理今天下午接见了古巴新闻工作者协会总书记安东尼奥·马·迈卡斯·多明格斯等五名古巴新闻工作者，并且进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，在座的有中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席朱穆之，中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复，中华全国新闻工作者协会第一书记王揖。
