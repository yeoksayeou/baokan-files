Title: Keita Writes to Foreign Minister Chen Yi: Mali Prepares to Negotiate Establishment of Diplomatic Relations with China
Author:
Date: 1960-10-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: Mali Republic Prime Minister Modibo Keita's letter to Foreign Minister Chen Yi of the People's Republic of China reads in full:

His Excellency Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Beijing,

Your Excellency,

I have the honor to acknowledge receipt of your cable, informing me that the Government of the People's Republic of China has decided to recognize the Republic of Mali as an independent and sovereign nation.

I congratulate you on the mutual recognition between our two countries and express to your government the desire of the people of the Republic of Mali to establish diplomatic relations of mutual trust with the People's Republic of China.

Believing that the friendly relations between our two countries will be in the interests of our two peoples and will help to maintain peace, my government is prepared to hold the necessary discussions with your government on the establishment and strengthening of diplomatic relations between our two republics.

Please accept, Your Excellency, the assurances of my highest consideration.

                    Modibo Keita (signed)
                    Prime Minister of the Republic of Mali
                      Bamako, October 19, 1960

### 凯塔总理写信给陈毅外长  马里准备同中国商谈建交

1960-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    凯塔总理写信给陈毅外长
    马里准备同中国商谈建交
    新华社25日讯　马里共和国总理莫迪博·凯塔给中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅的信，全文如下：北京中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅先生阁下，阁下，
    我荣幸地收到您的来电，通知我，中华人民共和国政府决定承认马里共和国为独立自主的国家。
    我祝贺我们两国间的相互承认，并且向贵国政府表达马里共和国人民愿和中华人民共和国建立相互信任的外交关系的愿望。
    由于相信我们两国间的友好关系将符合于我们两国人民的利益，并且将有助于维护和平，我国政府准备就我们两个共和国之间建立和加强外交关系的问题和贵国政府进行必要的商谈。
    阁下，请接受我崇高的敬意。
                    马里共和国总理
                        莫迪博·凯塔（签字）
                      1960年10月19日于巴马科
