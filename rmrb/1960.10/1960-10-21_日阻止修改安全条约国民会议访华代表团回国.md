Title: Japan Prevents the Return of the National Conference Against Revision of the Security Treaty Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1960-10-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 20th: The twelve-member delegation from the National Conference Against Revision of the Security Treaty, led by Deputy Heads Bunjirō Sano and Kō Tōgū, arrived in Guangzhou by train today and will return to their country via Shenzhen. The head of the delegation, Takeshi Tobae, had already returned to Japan on the 15th.

Today, those who came to the station to see off the delegation included Ge Feng, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Wang Yaoxuan, Secretary-General of the Guangzhou Federation of Trade Unions, and Ruan Ruolin, Chairman of the Guangzhou Youth Federation.

### 日阻止修改安全条约国民会议访华代表团回国

1960-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    日阻止修改安全条约国民会议访华代表团回国
    新华社广州20日电　日本阻止修改安全条约国民会议访华代表团一行十二人，在副团长佐野文一郎、东宫考率领下，今天乘火车到达广州经深圳回国。代表团团长户叶武已经在15日先期回国。
    今天到车站欢送代表团的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广州市分会副主席戈枫、广州市总工会秘书长王耀轩、广州市青年联合会主席阮若琳等人。
