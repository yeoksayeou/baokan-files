Title: China-Latin America Friendship Association President Chu Tunan Hosts Reception to Bid Farewell to Cultural Delegations from Latin American Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-10-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th. Chu Tunan, President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, hosted a reception at the Xinqiao Hotel tonight to bid farewell to the cultural delegations and representatives from various Latin American countries who visited China and participated in the celebrations for the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

At the reception, friends from various Latin American countries, along with Chu Tunan, President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, Vice President Zhou Erfu, council members of the Friendship Association, and people from various circles in the capital, had cordial conversations. The hosts and guests repeatedly raised their glasses to warmly congratulate the increasing friendship between the people of China and Latin American countries in the struggle against imperialism.

Chu Tunan said at the reception that we all have good wishes to promote mutual understanding and increase friendship. The people of China and the people of various Latin American countries support and learn from each other in the struggle against imperialism.

Chu Tunan said that the Chinese people have always supported the just struggle of the people of various Latin American countries to safeguard national independence and oppose U.S. imperialism. The Chinese people regard the victory of the struggle of the people of various Latin American countries as their own victory. In the struggle against U.S. imperialism, we share a common destiny and breathe the same air. Chu Tunan asked friends from various countries to bring the friendship of the Chinese people to the people of various Latin American countries.

Dr. Vicentina Antuña Davio, head of the Cuban cultural delegation, excitedly spoke about their meeting with Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Enlai this afternoon. She said that we want to thank the Chinese people who have achieved a great leap forward in construction. The Cuban people have received the full support of the great Chinese people in their revolutionary struggle.

Nicolás Buenaventura, head of the Colombian cultural delegation, spoke on behalf of the cultural delegations and representatives from Chile, Dominican Republic, Mexico, Panama, Peru, and Uruguay. He said that after our visit to China, we have unanimously come to the conclusion that the people of various Latin American countries should not only drive away the U.S. imperialism that oppresses us, but we will definitely be able to drive it away. In his speech, he condemned U.S. imperialism's occupation of our territory, Taiwan. He said that the day when U.S. imperialism is driven out of this part of China – Taiwan – will surely come.

Hector Malgaro Cueyo, head of the Venezuelan cultural delegation, proposed a toast to the friendship between the people of Latin American countries and the Chinese people, and to the common victory against U.S. imperialism. He also condemned U.S. imperialism's aggression and interference against Cuba, pointing out that the people of Latin American countries firmly stand with Cuba.

Adon Bezerra Nunes, representative of the Brazilian cultural delegation, said that we are all united in the struggle against U.S. imperialism. He proposed a toast to the final victory over U.S. imperialism.

Abelardo Villalpando Borda, head of the Bolivian cultural delegation, said in his speech that China's revolution and victory have given us great inspiration. He wished China's socialist construction continued development. Isidro López, head of the Argentine cultural delegation, spoke highly of the Chinese people's revitalized spirit and the brilliant achievements they have made in socialist construction. He proposed a toast to the unity of the people of the world and to world peace. All the speeches aroused bursts of warm applause. When it came to condemning U.S. imperialism's aggression against Cuba, people rhythmically shouted: "Yes to Cuba, no to the Yankees!" During the reception, artists from the capital performed Chinese and Cuban songs and dances to add to the fun.

### 中拉友好协会会长楚图南举行招待会  欢送拉丁美洲各国文化代表团

1960-10-28
第4版()
专栏：

    中拉友好协会会长楚图南举行招待会
    欢送拉丁美洲各国文化代表团
    新华社27日讯  中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南今晚在新侨饭店举行招待会，欢送来我国访问并参加中华人民共和国成立十一周年庆祝活动的拉丁美洲各国文化代表团和代表。
    在招待会上，拉丁美洲各国的朋友们，同中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南、副会长周而复，友协的理事们以及首都各方面的人士，亲切谈叙，宾主不断举杯，热情祝贺中国和拉丁美洲各国人民的友谊在反对帝国主义的斗争中日益增进。
    楚图南在招待会上讲话说，我们彼此都有促进相互了解、增加友谊的良好愿望。中国人民和拉丁美洲各国人民在反对帝国主义的斗争中相互支持、相互学习。
    楚图南说，中国人民一贯支持拉丁美洲各国人民维护民族独立和反对美帝国主义的正义斗争。中国人民把拉丁美洲各国人民斗争的胜利看做是自己的胜利。在反对美帝国主义的斗争中，我们是共命运、同呼吸的。楚图南请各国的朋友们把中国人民的友谊带给拉丁美洲各国人民。
    古巴文化代表团团长维森蒂娜·安图尼亚·达维奥博士在讲话中兴奋地讲到了今天下午他们同毛主席和周恩来总理的会见。她说，我们要向在建设中获得大跃进的中国人民致谢。古巴人民在自己的革命斗争中，得到了伟大的中国人民的全力支持。
    哥伦比亚文化代表团团长尼古拉斯·布埃纳万徒拉代表智利、多米尼加、墨西哥、巴拿马、秘鲁、乌拉圭文化代表团和代表讲话。他说，我们在中国访问之后一致得出一个结论，这就是拉丁美洲各国人民不仅应该把压迫我们的美帝国主义赶走，而且我们是一定能够把它赶走的。他在讲话中谴责美帝国主义霸占我国领土台湾。他说，把美帝国主义从中国的这一部分——台湾赶出去的那一天，是一定会到来的。
    委内瑞拉文化代表团团长埃克多尔·马尔加诺·古埃约在讲话中，提议为拉丁美洲各国人民和中国人民的友谊，为反对美帝国主义的共同胜利而干杯。他还谴责美帝国主义对古巴的侵略和干涉，指出拉丁美洲各国人民是坚决站在古巴一边的。
    巴西文化代表团代表阿东·贝雷拉·努奈斯讲话说，我们都是在反对美帝国主义的斗争中团结起来的。他提议为最后战胜美帝国主义而干杯。
    玻利维亚文化代表团团长阿贝拉尔多·维约尔邦多博十在讲话中说，中国的革命和胜利给了我们很大的启发。他祝中国的社会主义建设不断发展。阿根廷文化代表团团长伊西特罗·洛佩斯讲话，赞扬中国人民精神焕发，在社会主义建设中取得了光辉成就。他提议为全世界人民的团结，为世界和平，干杯。所有的讲话都激起一阵阵热烈的掌声。在讲到谴责美帝国主义侵略古巴的时候，人们连续有节奏地高呼：“要古巴，不要美国佬！”招待会进行中，首都文艺工作者演出了中国和古巴的歌舞节目助兴。
