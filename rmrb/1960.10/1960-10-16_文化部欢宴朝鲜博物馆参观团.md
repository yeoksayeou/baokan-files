Title: The Ministry of Culture Hosts a Banquet for the Korean Museum Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1960-10-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 15th: Xu Pingyu, Vice Minister of Culture, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 14th to welcome the Korean Museum Delegation led by head of the delegation, Li Zailin, and received all members of the delegation before the banquet.

This nine-member delegation arrived in Beijing on the 13th and will be visiting China for about a month.

### 文化部欢宴朝鲜博物馆参观团

1960-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    文化部欢宴朝鲜博物馆参观团
    新华社15日讯　文化部副部长徐平羽14日晚上设宴欢迎由团长李载林率领的朝鲜博物馆参观团，并在宴会前接见了代表团的全体人员。
    这个一行九人的代表团是在13日到达北京的，他们将在我国作为时一月的参观访问。
