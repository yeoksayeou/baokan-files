Title: Vientiane Radio Announces that the Royal Lao Government Will Establish Diplomatic Relations with the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-10-05
Page: 6

    Vientiane Radio Announces that the Royal Lao Government Will Establish Diplomatic Relations with the Soviet Union
    Vientiane Radio announced on September 30th that the Royal Lao Government will establish diplomatic relations with the Soviet Union.
    The radio station said that the Soviet Embassy will be established in Vientiane.

### 万象电台宣布老挝王国政府将同苏联建交

1960-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    万象电台宣布老挝王国政府将同苏联建交
    万象电台已于九月三十日宣布，老挝王国政府将同苏联建立外交关系。
    电台说，苏联大使馆将在万象设立。
