Title: Our Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Havana
Author:
Date: 1960-10-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 14th. A Chinese trade union delegation led by Kang Yonghe, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, arrived in Havana this afternoon. The delegation, invited by the Cuban Workers' Federation and other trade union organizations, will pay a friendly visit to Cuba and attend the Third International Trade Union Committee for Support of Algerian Workers and People held in Cuba. Luigi Grassi, Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions, who will also attend the meeting, and representatives from trade unions in the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Indonesia, India and Italy arrived in Havana on the same plane.

### 我工会代表团到哈瓦那

1960-10-18
第5版()
专栏：

    我工会代表团到哈瓦那
    据新华社哈瓦那14日电　由中华全国总工会书记处书记康永和率领的中国工会代表团今天下午到哈瓦那。代表团应古巴工人联合会等工会组织的邀请，将在古巴进行友好访问，并且参加在古巴举行的国际工会支援阿尔及利亚工人及人民委员会第三次会议。将参加这次会议的世界工会联合会书记路易吉·葛拉西以及苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、印度尼西亚、印度和意大利等国工会的代表同机到达哈瓦那。
