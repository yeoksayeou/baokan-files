Telegrams of Congratulations from Soviet Party and State Leaders
To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Comrade Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:
Dear Comrades, on the occasion of the 11th anniversary of the People's Republic of China, we, on behalf of the Soviet people, the Communist Party and Government of the Soviet Union, extend our heartfelt congratulations to you, and through you, to all the fraternal Chinese people, the Communist Party of China, and the government of the People's Republic of China. The Chinese people are greeting the 11th anniversary of the People's Republic of China with tremendous achievements in all aspects of socialist construction. After realizing fundamental socio-economic reforms under the leadership of their Communist Party, the working people of China are now fighting tenaciously to build their country into a powerful socialist state with modern industry, highly developed agriculture, advanced science, and culture. The Soviet people are sincerely happy for the achievements of the People's Republic of China. The people of the Soviet Union and China are linked by unbreakable bonds of friendship, which is in line with the fundamental interests of our two peoples. The friendship and unity of the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and all socialist countries is a powerful factor of our time, and it now exerts a decisive influence on the entire course of human development. Socialist countries, resolutely fighting to realize the principles of peaceful coexistence between countries with different social systems and for universal and complete disarmament, consistently pursue a policy of friendly cooperation among all peoples, while exposing the aggressive conspiracies of the imperialist bloc, and defend the cause of world peace and security. We wish the great Chinese people new and great achievements in socialist construction and in the struggle for world peace.
	Long live the 11th anniversary of the People's Republic of China!
	Long live the eternal and unbreakable friendship between the people of the Soviet Union and China!
	N. Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union
	L. Brezhnev, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union

### 庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年

1960-10-01
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年
    苏联党和国家领导人的贺电中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志，中华人民共和国主席刘少奇同志，中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德同志，中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志：
    亲爱的同志们，值此中华人民共和国十一周年之际，我们谨代表苏联人民、苏联共产党和政府向你们，并且通过你们向全体兄弟的中国人民、中国共产党和中华人民共和国政府表示衷心的祝贺。中国人民是以社会主义建设各个方面的巨大成就来迎接中华人民共和国十一周年的。中国的劳动人民在自己的共产党的领导下实现了社会经济的根本改革之后，现在正在为把自己的国家建设成为一个具有现代工业、高度发达的农业、先进科学和文化的强大的社会主义国家而顽强地斗争着。苏联人民为中华人民共和国的成就感到由衷的高兴。苏中两国人民是以牢不可破的友谊的纽带连结在一起的，这个友谊是符合于我们两国人民的根本利益的。苏联、中华人民共和国和所有的社会主义国家的友谊和团结是当代的强大因素，现在它对人类发展的整个进程起着决定性的影响。为争取实现不同社会制度国家和平共处的原则和普遍彻底裁军而坚决奋斗的社会主义国家，在揭露帝国主义集团侵略阴谋的同时，一贯地奉行着各国人民间友好合作的政策，捍卫世界和平和安全的事业。祝伟大的中国人民在社会主义建设和争取世界和平的斗争中取得新的巨大的成就。 　 欢庆中华人民共和国十一周年！
    兄弟的中国人民万岁！
    苏中两国人民永恒的牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记
    苏联部长会议主席  尼·赫鲁晓夫
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席　列·勃列日涅夫

    值此中华人民共和国成立十一周年之际，请你，敬爱的陈毅同志，接受我最诚挚的祝贺。我借此机会预祝伟大的中国人民在建设社会主义，在发展牢不可破的苏中友谊和争取和平的斗争中取得进一步的成就。
                            苏联外交部长　安·葛罗米柯
