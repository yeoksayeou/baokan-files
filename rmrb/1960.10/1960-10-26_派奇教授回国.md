Title: Professor Page Returns Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-10-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 25th. New Zealand pianist Professor Frederick Page left Guangzhou by train on the 24th to return home. Lin Yun, Secretary-General of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese Musicians Association, and others saw him off at the station.

During his stay in Guangzhou, Professor Page visited the site of the Peasant Movement Training Institute, which was founded by Chairman Mao, and the Guangzhou Uprising Martyrs' Cemetery. New Zealand female pianist Jenata McPhers arrived in Guangzhou on the 7th and left Guangzhou on the 10th to return home.

### 派奇教授回国

1960-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    派奇教授回国
    新华社广州25日电　新西兰钢琴家弗莱德利克·派奇教授24日乘火车离广州回国。中国音乐家协会广州分会秘书长林韵等到车站欢送。
    派奇教授在广州期间，参观了毛主席当年主办的农民运动讲习所的旧址、广州公社起义烈士陵园等。新西兰女钢琴家珍那塔·麦佩斯在7日抵达广州，并在10日先行离开广州回国。
