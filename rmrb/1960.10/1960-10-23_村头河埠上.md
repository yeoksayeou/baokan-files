Title: By the Riverbank in the Village
Author: Cui Ruxian
Date: 1960-10-23
Page: 8

　　Red glow in the sky,
　　Purple glow in the sky,
　　By the riverbank in the village,
　　The sound of gongs and drums circles the clouds.
　　What happy event is this,
　　Such a dense crowd of people?
　　Under the willow shade shines a large banner:
　　Welcome worker brothers to help and support!
　　Young Pioneers are busy singing,
　　Old people open their eyes wide,
　　The commune leader stands on the stone steps,
　　His face beaming with smiles.
　　Ah, they're here, they're here,
　　Five colorful boats linked in a line,
　　Decorated with red and green, so lively,
　　The river water is also happy, adding laughing waves.
　　The rushed-out rice threshing machine,
　　The newly painted paint is not yet dry,
　　The newly made fertilizer,
　　Weighs down the boats to the water's edge!
　　And a group of young men,
　　Each with burning enthusiasm in their hearts,
　　A bag of tools and a heart,
　　Determined to pass on their skills to the commune.
　　The factory director stands at the bow,
　　His heart filled with boundless enthusiasm,
　　Supporting agricultural mechanization,
　　The worker-peasant alliance lasts ten thousand years!
　　Before the boat even docks,
　　The applause sounds like waves crashing on the shore,
　　The old grandma brings fragrant tea,
　　The women offer local produce as snacks…
　　The factory director and the commune leader stand shoulder to shoulder,
　　Each word connects heart to heart,
　　Tearing down the red glow as paper,
　　Still hard to write out the friendship between workers and peasants!

### 村头河埠上
崔汝先
1960-10-23
第8版()
专栏：

    村头河埠上
    崔汝先
    红霞天，
    紫霞天，
    村头河埠上，
    锣声鼓声绕云天。
    出什么喜事了，
    黑压压的人群一大片？
    柳荫下映出大横幅：
    欢迎工人大哥来支援！
    红领巾忙着唱歌，
    老年人睁圆了眼，
    社长站在石阶上，
    现出了满脸笑容。
    呀，来了，来了，
    五条彩船一线牵，
    披红挂绿好热闹，
    河水也乐得忙把笑波添。
    突击出来的打稻机，
    新刷的油漆还未干，
    刚造好的化肥，
    压得船船水满舷！
    还有一群小伙子，
    个个热火藏心坎，
    一袋工具一颗心，
    决心到公社把技术来相传。
    厂长立船头，
    无限热情填心间，
    支援农业机械化，
    工农联盟万万年！
    没等船靠近，
    掌声好像浪拍岸，
    老奶奶端来香茶，
    妇女们托出土产当茶点……
    厂长社长肩并肩，
    句句话儿心连心，
    撕下红霞当纸张，
    也难把工农情谊来写尽！
