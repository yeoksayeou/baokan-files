Title: Polish Academy of Sciences Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 10th October: The Polish Academy of Sciences delegation, led by Academician Tadeusz Kotarbiński, President of the Polish Academy of Sciences, which came to China to negotiate a scientific cooperation agreement between the Chinese and Polish Academies of Sciences and the 1961 implementation plan for scientific cooperation between the two academies, left Beijing by plane today to return home.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Wu Youxun, Vice President, Pan Zinian, Deputy Director of the Department of Philosophy and Social Sciences, Yan Jici, Director of the Department of Technological Sciences, Lin Rong, Deputy Director of the Department of Biology, and Pan Chun, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Liaison.

The Polish Ambassador to China, Jerzy Knothe, and Cultural Counselor Dumbnicki were also at the airport to see them off.

During their stay in China, the delegation participated in the celebrations of the 11th anniversary of the founding of our country and visited scientific research institutions, universities, and factories in Beijing, Shanghai, Shenyang, Anshan, and other places.

Academician Witold Stefański and his wife, members of the delegation, will continue to stay in China to visit Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Wuhan, and other places.

### 波兰科学院代表团回国

1960-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰科学院代表团回国
    新华社10日讯　来我国商订中波两国科学院科学合作协议和两院科学合作1961年执行计划的波兰科学院代表团一行四人，在波兰科学院院长塔德乌什·科塔宾斯基院士率领下，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有中国科学院院长郭沫若、副院长吴有训、哲学社会科学部副主任潘梓年、技术科学部主任严济慈、生物学部副主任林镕、对外联络局代局长潘纯等。
    波兰驻中国大使耶日·克诺泰和文化参赞邓布尼茨基也到机场欢送。
    代表团在中国期间，曾参加了我国建国十一周年庆祝活动，并访问了北京、上海、沈阳、鞍山等地的科学研究机构、高等学校和工厂等。
    代表团团员维托尔得·斯捷凡斯基院士和夫人继续留在我国，前往上海、杭州、广州、武汉等地参观访问。
