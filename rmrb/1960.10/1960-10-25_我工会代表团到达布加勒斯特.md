Title: Our Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Bucharest
Author:
Date: 1960-10-25
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 24th. The Chinese Trade Union Delegation, led by Li Yunchuan, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Machinery Trade Union, arrived here by plane on the 23rd, at the invitation of the Fourth Congress of the Romanian Trade Union.

### 我工会代表团到达布加勒斯特

1960-10-25
第6版()
专栏：

    我工会代表团到达布加勒斯特
    新华社布加勒斯特24日电　应邀参加罗马尼亚工会第四次代表大会的中国工会代表团，在中国机械工会全国委员会主席李云川的率领下，于23日乘飞机到达这里。
