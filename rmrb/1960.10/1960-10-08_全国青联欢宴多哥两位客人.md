Title: All-China Youth Federation Hosts Banquet for Two Guests from Togo
Author:
Date: 1960-10-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The All-China Youth Federation hosted a banquet on the evening of the 6th to welcome Duren Faustin, Chairman of the Togo Rural Youth Committee, and Amegebo Joseph, Secretary-General, who were invited to visit our country. They arrived in Beijing on the 4th. Wang Zhaohua, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, and others attended the banquet.

### 全国青联欢宴多哥两位客人

1960-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

    全国青联欢宴多哥两位客人
    新华社7日讯　中华全国青年联合会6日晚设宴欢迎应邀来我国访问的多哥农村青年委员会主席杜伦·福斯廷和总书记阿梅格博·约瑟夫。他们是4日到达北京的。出席宴会的，有中国共产主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记王照华等人。
