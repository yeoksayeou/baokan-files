Title: Soviet Union Holds Chinese Photo Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1960-10-03
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 30th. The photo exhibition introducing China's construction achievements, jointly organized by the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association and Friendship House to celebrate our country's National Day, opened at Friendship House on the afternoon of September 30.

    Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of the Union of Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhuyeva, and others attended the opening ceremony. Our Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Liu Xiao, Cultural Counsellor Zhang Yingwu, and Xu Guangda, head of the delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, were also invited to attend the opening ceremony.

    In her speech at the opening ceremony, Zhuyeva spoke about the remarkable achievements made by the Chinese people in socialist construction over the past eleven years. She said that the Soviet people are overjoyed at all the achievements of the fraternal Chinese people.

    In his speech, Ambassador Liu Xiao said that the Chinese people's completion of the main targets of the Second Five-Year Plan three years ahead of schedule is a great victory for our Party's general line for socialist construction. The great victory of socialist construction in our country is inseparable from the assistance of the Soviet Union. The exhibition will enhance the Soviet people's understanding of China's situation and help strengthen the great friendship and unity between our two peoples.

    The exhibition featured one hundred and fifty pictures introducing the great achievements of our country in completing the Second Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule and the situation of the people's communes.

### 苏联举办中国图片展览会

1960-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联举办中国图片展览会
    新华社莫斯科9月30日电　苏中友好协会和友谊之家为庆祝我国国庆而联合举办的介绍我国建设成就的图片展览会，9月30日下午在友谊之家开幕。
    苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会理事会副主席朱耶娃等出席了开幕式。我驻苏大使刘晓和文化参赞张映吾以及中苏友好协会代表团团长许光达也应邀出席了开幕式。
    朱耶娃在开幕式上讲话时，谈到了中国人民十一年来在社会主义建设中取得的卓越成就。她说，苏联人民为兄弟中国人民的一切成就都感到欢欣鼓舞。
    刘晓大使在讲话时说，中国人民提前三年完成了第二个五年计划的主要指标是我们党的社会主义建设总路线的伟大胜利，我国社会主义建设的伟大胜利是同苏联的援助分不开的，展览会将增进苏联人民对我国情况的了解，有助于加强我们两国人民的伟大友谊和团结。
    展览会展出的一百五十张图片介绍了我国提前完成第二个五年计划的巨大成就以及人民公社的情况。
