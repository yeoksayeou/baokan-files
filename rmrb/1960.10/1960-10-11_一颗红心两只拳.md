Title: A Red Heart and Two Fists
Author: Yang Yonglie
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 2

    A red heart and two fists,
    Not relying on gods, not relying on heaven,
    As long as we listen to the Party's words,
    Fiercely engage in innovation and break through all barriers.
    A red heart and two fists,
    What difficulty is there in technological revolution?
    As long as we listen to the Party's words,
    Achieve high yield, high quality, and multiple increases.
      (Yang Yonglie)

### 一颗红心两只拳
杨勇烈
1960-10-11
第2版()
专栏：

    一颗红心两只拳
    一颗红心两只拳，
    不靠神来不靠天，
    只要咱听党的话，
    狠闹革新破万关。
    一颗红心两只拳，
    革新技术有何难，
    只要咱听党的话，
    高产优质倍加番。
      （杨勇烈）
