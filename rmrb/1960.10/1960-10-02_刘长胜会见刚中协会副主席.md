Title: Liu Changsheng Meets with Vice-Chairman of the Congo-China Association
Author:
Date: 1960-10-02
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 1st October: Liu Changsheng, President of the Chinese-African Peoples' Friendship Association, met with Antoine Ligobe Mundango, Vice-Chairman of the Congo-China Association, this afternoon and had a cordial conversation with the guest. Wu Qing, Secretary-General of the Chinese-African Peoples' Friendship Association, and others were present at the meeting.
    Xinhua News Agency, 1st October: Antoine Ligobe Mundango, Vice-Chairman of the Congo-China Association, who was invited by the Chinese-African Peoples' Friendship Association to visit China, arrived in Beijing today.
    Qu Wu, Vice-President of the Chinese-African Peoples' Friendship Association, Li Jin, Deputy Secretary-General, and others welcomed him at the airport.

### 刘长胜会见刚中协会副主席

1960-10-02
第7版()
专栏：

    刘长胜会见刚中协会副主席
    新华社1日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会会长刘长胜今天下午会见了刚果—中国协会副主席安托万·利戈贝·芒丹戈，并同客人进行了亲切的谈话。会见时在座的有中国非洲人民友好协会总干事吴青等。
    新华社1日讯　应中国非洲人民友好协会邀请来中国访问的刚果—中国协会副主席安托万·利戈贝·芒丹戈，今天到达北京。
    前往欢迎的有中国非洲人民友好协会副会长屈武、副总干事李进等。
