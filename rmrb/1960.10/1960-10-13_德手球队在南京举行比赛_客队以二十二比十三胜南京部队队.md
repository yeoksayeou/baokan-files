Title: German Handball Team Plays in Nanjing; Visiting Team Defeats Nanjing Military Team 22-13
Author:
Date: 1960-10-13
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 11th. The German Democratic Republic National People's Army handball team played its fourth friendly match in China against the Chinese People's Liberation Army Nanjing Military Region handball team this afternoon. The visiting team won 22-13.
　　The German Democratic Republic National People's Army handball team is scheduled to travel to Shanghai by train on the 12th for a tour and visit.

### 德手球队在南京举行比赛  客队以二十二比十三胜南京部队队

1960-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    德手球队在南京举行比赛
    客队以二十二比十三胜南京部队队
    新华社南京11日电　德意志民主共和国国家人民军手球队今天下午同中国人民解放军南京部队手球队举行访华第四场友谊比赛，客队以二十二比十三获胜。
    德意志民主共和国国家人民军手球队预定于12日乘车赴上海参观访问。
