Title: Prime Minister Abbas Leaves Message While Visiting the Chinese Pavilion at the International Exposition: The Chinese Pavilion Embodies the Productive and Peaceful Strength of New China
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, 22nd. The Prime Minister of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, Abbas Ferhat, and his wife, today visited the Chinese Pavilion at the Eighth Tunis International Exposition here.

Accompanied by Yu Liguang, head of the Chinese Exhibition Delegation, Abbas carefully viewed the exhibits in the Chinese Pavilion.

In a cordial conversation with the head and deputy head of the Chinese Exhibition Delegation, Abbas said that he came to visit the Chinese Pavilion first to see the excellent products exhibited by the Chinese Pavilion, and secondly to strengthen the existing friendship and ties between the two countries.

Abbas told Yu Liguang that he had already told Tunisian President Bourguiba everything he saw in China.

Abbas wrote in the guest book: "We have seen a very beautiful exhibition. It enables us to understand the production and peaceful power of New China. With gratitude, we ask our Chinese friends to believe in our friendship and the desire for unity."

### 阿巴斯总理参观国际博览会中国馆时留言  中国馆体现了新中国的生产和和平力量

1960-10-24
第5版()
专栏：

    阿巴斯总理参观国际博览会中国馆时留言
    中国馆体现了新中国的生产和和平力量
    新华社突尼斯22日电　阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理阿巴斯·费尔哈特和夫人今天参观了这里的突尼斯第八届国际博览会中国馆。
    阿巴斯在中国展览团团长于黎光陪同下仔细地观看了中国馆的展品。
    阿巴斯在同中国展览团团长和副团长的亲切交谈时说，他来参观中国馆首先是为了看看中国馆展出的优良产品，其次是为了加强两国现有的友谊和联系。
    阿巴斯对于黎光说，他已经向突尼斯总统布尔吉巴介绍了他在中国见到的一切。
    阿巴斯在留言簿上写道：“我们看到了一个极美的展览会。它使我们了解到新中国的生产和它的和平力量。我们以感激的心情请求我们的中国朋友们相信我们的友谊和团结一致的愿望。”
