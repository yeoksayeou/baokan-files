Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Foreign Guests from Nine Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-10-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 8th October: Premier Zhou Enlai received foreign guests from nine countries who are currently visiting China at Zhongshan Park this afternoon. These foreign guests are: Faisal Samir, Minister of the National Guidance Department of Iraq; the Haitian Cultural Friendship Delegation headed by Jean Latortue; the visiting delegation of the Indonesian-China Friendship Association headed by Suripto; Sirajuddin Abbas, one of the chairmen of the Indonesian Peace Committee and member of the Cooperation Parliament, and his wife; the Burmese Cultural Delegation headed by Colonel Soe Myint; the Japanese Theater Troupe headed by Tomoyoshi Murayama; Kiramat, the Vice-Chancellor of the University of the Punjab, Pakistan, and his wife; Frederick Page, the head of the Music Department of Victoria University, New Zealand; Tabio, the head of the Cuban Journalists Delegation; the Japanese Journalist Delegation headed by Yuichi Kobayashi; and Francisco Gamboa, columnist for the Costa Rican Republic newspaper.

    Today, the sky was high and the clouds were light, the weather was fine, chrysanthemums were in full bloom and osmanthus were fragrant in Zhongshan Park. Guests from various countries and people from all walks of life in our country gathered around tables on the lawn to talk about friendship.
    At 4 p.m., when Premier Zhou Enlai arrived, warm applause broke out on the lawn. Vice-Chairman Guo Moruo and Vice-Premier Xi Zhongxun accompanied Premier Zhou Enlai. Premier Zhou Enlai and others warmly shook hands and talked with the guests from various countries, and took group photos with them. The warm and friendly meeting did not end until the lights were lit.
    Also present at the reception were Zhang Xiruo, Shen Yanbing, Zhang Hanfu, Chu Tunan, Zhang Zhixiang, Qu Wu, Luo Jun, Hu Yuzhi, Yang Hansheng, Wu Lengxi, Lao She, Cao Yu, Zhou Erfu, Chen Qitong, Ma Shaobo, as well as famous film and drama actors from the capital.
    When Premier Zhou Enlai received the Burmese Cultural Delegation, the Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maw, was also present.
    Xinhua News Agency, 8th October: Premier Zhou Enlai received Faisal Samir, Minister of the National Guidance Department of the Republic of Iraq, this afternoon and had a friendly conversation.
    Also present at the reception were Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Luo Jun, Vice-Director and Vice-President of the Chinese-Iraqi Friendship Association, and He Ying, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    The Iraqi Ambassador to China, Fadil, was also present.

### 周恩来总理接见九个国家的外宾

1960-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见九个国家的外宾
    新华社8日讯　周恩来总理今天下午在中山公园接见了正在我国访问的九个国家的外宾。这些外宾是：伊拉克国家指导部部长费萨尔·萨米尔，以让·拉脱为首的海地文化友好代表团，以苏里普托为首的印度尼西亚中国友好协会访华代表团，印度尼西亚和平委员会主席之一、合作议会议员西拉朱丁·阿巴斯和夫人，以苏敏上校为首的缅甸文化代表团，以村山知义为首的日本话剧团，巴基斯坦旁遮普大学副校长克拉马特和夫人，新西兰维多利亚大学音乐系主任弗莱德利克·派奇，古巴记者代表团团长塔比奥，以小林雄一为首的日本新闻工作者代表团，哥斯达黎加共和报专栏作家弗朗西斯科·甘波亚。
    今天天高云淡，风和日丽，中山公园里菊花怒放，丹桂飘香。各国来宾们和我国各方面人士，在草坪上围桌畅叙友谊。
    下午四时，当周恩来总理来到时，草坪上响起一片热烈的鼓掌声。同周恩来总理一起来到的有郭沫若副委员长和习仲勋副总理。周恩来总理等和各国来宾亲切握手、交谈，并且和他们一起合影。热烈、友好的会见一直到华灯竞放之时才告结束。
    接见时在场的还有张奚若、沈雁冰、章汉夫、楚图南、张致祥、屈武、罗俊、胡愈之、阳翰笙、吴冷西、老舍、曹禺、周而复、陈其通、马少波，以及首都著名的电影和话剧演员。
    周恩来总理接见缅甸文化代表团时，缅甸驻中国大使叫温也在场。
    新华社8日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了伊拉克共和国国家指导部部长费萨尔·萨米尔，并且进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时，在座的有对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若，副主任、中国伊拉克友好协会副会长罗俊，外交部西亚非洲司司长何英等。
    伊拉克驻中国大使法迪尔也在座。
