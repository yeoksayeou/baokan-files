Title: Our National Song and Dance Troupe Leaves Moscow for Home After Concluding Performance Tour in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-10-27
Page: 5

    After Concluding Performance Tour in the Soviet Union
    Our National Song and Dance Troupe Leaves Moscow for Home
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 25th -- The Chinese Central National Song and Dance Troupe left Moscow by train on the evening of the 24th to return home after concluding its performance tour in the Soviet Union.
    On the afternoon of the 24th, the Soviet Ministry of Culture held a reception banquet to celebrate the troupe's successful conclusion of its performances in the Soviet Union. Among those present at the banquet were Soviet Deputy Minister of Culture Danilov, Director of the Soviet State Bolshoi Theatre Ovid, and Soviet People's Artist Lepeshinskaya. Zhang Yingwu, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, also attended the banquet.
    The Song and Dance Troupe arrived in the Soviet Union on September 2 for a performance tour, visiting a total of eighteen cities including Moscow, Leningrad, Vilnius, Riga, and Kiev, and performing thirty-nine shows.

### 结束在苏联的访问演出后  我民族歌舞团离莫斯科返国

1960-10-27
第5版()
专栏：

    结束在苏联的访问演出后
    我民族歌舞团离莫斯科返国
    据新华社莫斯科25日电　中国中央民族歌舞团在苏联结束访问演出后，于24日晚乘火车离莫斯科返国。
    24日下午，苏联文化部为歌舞团成功地结束在苏演出举行了招待宴会，出席宴会的有苏联文化部副部长达尼洛夫，苏联国家大剧院院长奥维德和苏联人民演员列皮辛斯卡娅等。我国驻苏联大使馆文化参赞张映吾也出席了宴会。
    歌舞团是9月2日到苏联进行访问演出的，先后共访问了莫斯科、列宁格勒、维尔纽斯、里加、基辅等十八个城市，演出三十九场。
