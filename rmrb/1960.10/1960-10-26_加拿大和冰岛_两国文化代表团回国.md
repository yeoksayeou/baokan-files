Title: Canadian and Icelandic Cultural Delegations Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-26
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, October 25th. The Canadian cultural delegation of five people and the Icelandic cultural delegation of four people, who visited our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, have left Beijing on the 22nd and 25th respectively to return to their countries.

### 加拿大和冰岛  两国文化代表团回国

1960-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    加拿大和冰岛
    两国文化代表团回国
    新华社25日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的加拿大文化代表团一行五人、冰岛文化代表团一行四人，已经分别在22日、25日离开北京回国。
