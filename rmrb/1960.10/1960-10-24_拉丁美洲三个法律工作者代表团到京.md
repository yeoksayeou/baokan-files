Title: Three Latin American Legal Workers Delegations Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: The Chilean legal workers delegation of twenty people, led by Miguel González Castillo, President of the Supreme Court of Chile; the Brazilian legal workers delegation of seven people, led by José Diaguiar Dias, judge of the Rio de Janeiro Court of Appeal; and the Argentine legal workers delegation of twelve people, led by Eduardo Salomon Valschavia, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

The guests were invited by the Chinese Political Science and Law Association to visit our country.

Wu Defeng and Zhang Zhiran, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Political Science and Law Association; Shen Zijiǔ, Director of the Chinese Political Science and Law Association and member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation; Lei Jieqiong, Director of the Chinese Political Science and Law Association and Standing Committee member of the China Association for Promoting Democracy; Wu Xiaoda, Standing Director of the China-Latin America Friendship Association and more than thirty other people welcomed them at the airport.  Mr. Vantuleli, a Chilean peace activist in Beijing, also greeted them at the airport.

### 拉丁美洲三个法律工作者代表团到京

1960-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    拉丁美洲三个法律工作者代表团到京
    新华社23日讯　由团长、智利最高法院法官米格尔·冈萨雷斯·卡斯蒂略率领的智利法律工作者代表团一行二十人，由团长、里约热内卢上诉法院法官若塞·迪亚贵阿尔·迪亚斯率领的巴西法律工作者代表团一行七人，由团长爱德华多·萨洛蒙·瓦尔斯查维尔率领的阿根廷法律工作者代表团一行十二人，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    客人们是应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的。
    中国政治法律学会副会长吴德峰、张志让，中国政治法律学会理事、全国妇联主席团委员沈兹九，中国政治法律学会理事、中国民主促进会常务委员雷洁琼，中国拉丁美洲友好协会常务理事吴晓达等三十多人到机场欢迎。到机场迎接的还有智利在京的和平人士万徒勒里。
