Title: Liu Ningyi Sends Telegram to Cuban Confederation of Trade Unions Supporting Cuba's Expropriation of American Enterprises
Author:
Date: 1960-10-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 28th: Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, today sent a telegram to the Cuban Confederation of Trade Unions, warmly congratulating the Cuban Revolutionary Government on the expropriation of 167 American enterprises, achieving the victory of nationalizing all American enterprises. Liu Ningyi pointed out that this measure "effectively countered the economic blockade imposed on Cuba by American imperialism and further guaranteed the smooth development of Cuba's national economy."

In his telegram, Liu Ningyi wished the Cuban people continued victories in their struggle to defend national independence, develop the national economy, and oppose American imperialism.

### 刘宁一致电古巴工会联合会  支持古巴征收美国企业

1960-10-29
第6版()
专栏：

    刘宁一致电古巴工会联合会
    支持古巴征收美国企业
　　新华社28日讯　中华全国总工会主席刘宁一今天打电报给古巴工会联合会，热烈祝贺古巴革命政府征收一百六十七家美国企业，实现了全部美国企业国有化的胜利。刘宁一指出，这一措施“有力地反击了美帝国主义对古巴的经济封锁，并进一步保证了古巴国民经济的顺利发展”。
    刘宁一在电报中祝古巴人民在保卫民族独立、发展民族经济和反对美帝国主义的斗争中继续取得胜利。
