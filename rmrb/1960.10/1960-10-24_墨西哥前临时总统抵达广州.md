Title: Former Interim President of Mexico Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 23rd: The Mexican cultural delegation, led by former interim President of Mexico, Emilio Portes Gil, arrived in Guangzhou today by train from Hangzhou. Li Guangxiang, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou, and others welcomed the guests at the station.

During their stay in Hangzhou, the guests visited the Pingfengshan Sanatorium, the Du Jinsheng Silk Weaving Factory, and the Longjing Production Team of the West Lake People's Commune, and toured the scenic spots and historical sites of West Lake. On the evening of the 20th, Chen Lijie, Vice Mayor of Hangzhou, hosted a buffet dinner for all members of the delegation.

### 墨西哥前临时总统抵达广州

1960-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    墨西哥前临时总统抵达广州
    新华社广州23日电　由墨西哥前临时总统埃米略·波尔特斯·希耳率领的墨西哥文化代表团，今天从杭州乘火车到达广州。广州市副市长李广祥等到车站欢迎客人。
    客人们在杭州期间，曾参观了屏风山疗养院、都锦生丝织厂和西湖人民公社龙井生产队，并游览了西湖名胜古迹。20日晚上，杭州市副市长陈礼节曾设便宴招待代表团全体人员。
