Title: Devaluation
Author: Tianma, Fang Cheng
Date: 1960-10-31
Page: 8

Poetry and Painting Pairing

    Devaluation
    Tianma Poem, Fang Cheng Painting
    News report: Gold prices are rising and the US dollar is depreciating, sparking a gold rush in the capitalist world.
    You scramble, I grab,
    Loud and clamorous,
    Gold becomes a hot commodity,
    The US dollar is unpopular!
    I only hate that my Western European partners are so unappreciative,
    Ignoring my American boss's suffering!
    What happened to the heavy brotherly love,
    Not only failing to help in a crisis but instead delivering a head-on blow!
    Back then, they bragged recklessly,
    Boasting about economic prosperity being heaven!
    But look now:
    Unemployment is increasing,
    Production is declining,
    A scene of desolation!
    Thinking left and right, no ideas,
    Anxious and at a loss, wandering alone!
    Afraid only that misfortunes never come singly,
    Prestige continues to fall,
    This future,
    Is unimaginable!…

### 贬值
天马　方成
1960-10-31
第8版()
专栏：诗画配

    贬值
    天马诗　方成画
    报载：黄金上涨，美元贬值，资本主义世界正掀起一股抢购黄金的风潮。
    你争我抢，
    闹闹嚷嚷，
    黄金成热货，
    美元不吃香！
    恨只恨西欧伙伴不识相，
    不管我美国老板受凄凉！
    说什么难兄难弟情谊重，
    见死不救反举当头棒！
    想当初牛皮胡乱吹，
    夸什么经济繁荣是天堂！
    看而今：
    失业增加，
    生产下降，
    一片萧条景象！
    左思右想没主张，
    焦头烂额独徬徨！
    怕只怕祸不单行，
    威信继续降，
    这前途也，
    不堪设想！……
