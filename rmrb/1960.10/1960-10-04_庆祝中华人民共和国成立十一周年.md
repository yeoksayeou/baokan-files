Title: Celebrating the 11th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1960-10-04
Page: 3

    Greetings from Sékou Touré, Secretary-General of the Democratic Party of Guinea, to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China:
    On the occasion of the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Democratic Party of Guinea and the Guinean people extend fraternal wishes and heartfelt congratulations to you and the heroic people of your country. The Democratic Party of Guinea, which faithfully reflects the will of the people, is confident that the friendship between our two peoples will help accelerate the establishment of an era of democracy, freedom, progress, and peace. The Democratic Party of Guinea expresses its sincere trust in you and your great Party. At the same time, I express my personal respect and friendship to you.
                                  Sékou Touré
                                  Secretary-General of the Democratic Party of Guinea
                                  October 1, 1960, in Conakry

    Greetings from Guinean President Touré to His Excellency Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:
    On the occasion of the National Day of the People's Republic of China, the people and government of Guinea are pleased to extend to you personally, and to the Chinese people and the Chinese government, warm congratulations and heartfelt fraternal wishes, and wish the Chinese people and the Chinese government comprehensive achievements in the construction that makes the country prosperous. Your country has mobilized the strength of the whole nation to work for the cause of progress, justice, and peace. The heroic actions of the Chinese people and the government of the People's Republic of China have made significant contributions to the cause of freedom for all peoples. The Chinese people and the Chinese government have opened up broader avenues for sincere cooperation and friendship between countries that love justice and progress. The recent visit of the Guinean delegation to the People's Republic of China was met with deep affection and hospitality. This visit strengthened the unity between our two peoples and embodied our common will to swiftly defeat all the plots and intrigues of colonialism and imperialism. I once again express the Guinean people's and government's friendly wishes to the Chinese people, wishing them happiness and prosperity. At the same time, I express my personal fraternal friendship to you.
                                                Sékou Touré
                                  October 1, 1960, in Conakry

      Greetings from Ako Adjei, Chairman of the Presidential Affairs Committee of Ghana, to His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:
    On the occasion of the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I, in my own name and on behalf of the government and people of the Republic of Ghana, extend to Your Excellency the warmest congratulations and express the most sincere wishes for your personal happiness and the prosperity of the Chinese people.
    The sincere relationship between our two countries is gratifying, and we eagerly hope that this relationship will be strengthened with subsequent anniversaries.
                      Sir Ako Adjei
                      Chairman of the Presidential Affairs Committee
                                    October 2, 1960, in Accra
      (The telegram comes from the Presidential Affairs Committee representing Dr. Kwame Nkrumah, President of the Republic of Ghana)

      Greetings from King Ahmad of Yemen to His Excellency Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:
    On the occasion of the National Day of the People's Republic of China, we extend to Your Excellency warm congratulations and good wishes, wishing Your Excellency and your government and the Chinese people everlasting success, prosperity, and well-being. Wishing you long life.
    Imam Ahmad, King of Yemen
                                    October 2, 1960, in Hodeidah

    Greetings from Indonesian President Sukarno

    To His Excellency the Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

    Congratulating you, the Chinese government and the Chinese people on the National Day.

                                Sukarno, President of Indonesia
                                        October 2, 1960, in New York

    Greetings from Cambodian Prime Minister Pholien to His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:
    On the occasion of the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I would like to extend, on behalf of the Royal Government and in my own name, the warmest congratulations and the most sincere wishes to Your Excellency and the distinguished members of your government. I am pleased to take this opportunity to once again express to you the unwavering and loyal friendship of the Cambodian people for the Chinese people.
                                  Pholien, Prime Minister of Cambodia
                                        October 3, 1960, in Phnom Penh

        Greetings from Abdi Boqor Hanuud Salax, Chairman of the Somali National Union, to His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier of People’s China:
    On behalf of the Somali National Union, I extend to you and the Chinese people congratulations on the 11th anniversary of the Chinese Revolution. I would be very happy if I could be in China myself, but regrettably, time does not allow it, and I must stay here. We support the Chinese people.
          Abdi Boqor Hanuud Salax
          Chairman of the Somali National Union
                              October 1, 1960, in Mogadishu

      Greetings from General Mikhailov, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense of Bulgaria, to Comrade Marshal Lin Biao, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China:
    Dear Comrade Marshal:
    On the occasion of the National Day of the fraternal People's Republic of China, I extend to you, on behalf of the Bulgarian commanders and fighters and in my own name, the most heartfelt congratulations.
    I wish all members of the Chinese People's Liberation Army even greater achievements in enhancing the great defense power of China, and in consolidating peace in the Far East and opposing the conspiracies of imperialism.
    Long live the unbreakable friendship between the armies of all countries in the powerful socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union!
    With comradely greetings.
    General Mikhailov
    Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of Bulgaria
                                      September 30, 1960, in Sofia

    Greetings from Acosta, General Secretary of the Peruvian Communist Party

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd -- The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China received a congratulatory message from Acosta, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Peruvian Communist Party, congratulating our country on its National Day.

    The congratulatory message said:
    "On the occasion of celebrating the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Central Committee of the Peruvian Communist Party extends warm congratulations to the Communist Party of China and the Chinese people.
    "Our people are watching with excitement the victories achieved in socialist construction and in the struggle for world peace and the national liberation of all peoples. The increasing strengthening of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union and People's China undoubtedly weakens the forces of imperialism and war.
    "The Cuban revolution has opened up the path of liberation for the peoples of Latin American countries. The peoples of Latin American countries are increasingly fighting against the rule of imperialism with thunderous force and expressing their support for the Cuban revolution with overwhelming combat actions.
    "Thanks to the fighting will of the working class and peasant masses led by the Communist and Workers' Parties of Latin American countries, thanks to the growing unity of democratic forces, and thanks to the growing power of the socialist camp, the reactionary attacks of imperialists and domestic oligarchs against the people and the aggressive attempts against Cuba will surely fail.
    "Dear comrades, I wish you continued success in building socialism and wish our fraternal connections under the immortal banner of Marxism-Leninism to continue to develop.
      "Long live the People's Republic of China!
      "Long live the Communist Party of China!"

    Congratulatory letters from foreign envoys in China to our country's leaders

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd -- On the days when our people warmly celebrate the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, foreign envoys in China wrote letters to Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, congratulating our country on its National Day.

    Those who wrote to Chairman Mao Zedong included: Soviet Ambassador Chervonenko, Bulgarian Ambassador Panchevski, Romanian Ambassador Zaharescu, Czechoslovak Ambassador Šedivý, Mongolian Ambassador Sharav, Ambassador Wang Daal of the German Democratic Republic, and Albanian Ambassador Parifti.

    Those who wrote to Chairman Liu Shaoqi included: Soviet Ambassador Chervonenko, Bulgarian Ambassador Panchevski, Romanian Ambassador Zaharescu, Czechoslovak Ambassador Šedivý, Mongolian Ambassador Sharav, Ambassador Wang Daal of the German Democratic Republic, Albanian Ambassador Parifti, and Norwegian Ambassador Dons.

    Those who wrote to Chairman Zhu De included: Soviet Ambassador Chervonenko, Bulgarian Ambassador Panchevski, Romanian Ambassador Zaharescu, Czechoslovak Ambassador Šedivý, Mongolian Ambassador Sharav, Ambassador Wang Daal of the German Democratic Republic, and Albanian Ambassador Parifti.

    Those who wrote to Premier Zhou Enlai included: Soviet Ambassador Chervonenko, Bulgarian Ambassador Panchevski, Romanian Ambassador Zaharescu, Czechoslovak Ambassador Šedivý, Mongolian Ambassador Sharav, Ambassador Wang Daal of the German Democratic Republic, Albanian Ambassador Parifti, Swiss Ambassador Naville, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Akim of the Afghan Embassy, and Ceylon Ambassador Gooneratne.

    Those who wrote to Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi included: Soviet Ambassador Chervonenko, Bulgarian Ambassador Panchevski, Romanian Ambassador Zaharescu, Hungarian Ambassador Márton Ferenc, Korean Ambassador Li Yong-ho, Czechoslovak Ambassador Šedivý, Polish Ambassador Knothe, Mongolian Ambassador Sharav, Ambassador Wang Daal of the German Democratic Republic, Albanian Ambassador Parifti, Vietnamese Ambassador Tran Tu Binh, Indian Ambassador Parthasarathi, Danish Ambassador Battesen, Swiss Ambassador Naville, Norwegian Ambassador Dons, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Akim of the Afghan Embassy, Ambassador Tarazi of the United Arab Republic, Cambodian Ambassador Lang Nhet, British Chargé d'Affaires Steward, and Dutch Chargé d'Affaires Coda.

    Former Ceylon Ambassador to China, Perera, also sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai congratulating our country on its National Day.

### 庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年

1960-10-04
第3版()
专栏：

    庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年
    几内亚民主党总书记杜尔的贺电中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东先生：
    值此中华人民共和国成立十一周年之际，几内亚民主党和几内亚人民向你并且向英勇的贵国人民表示兄弟般的祝愿和衷心的祝贺。忠实反映人民意愿的几内亚民主党深信我们两国人民之间的友谊将有助于加速建立一个民主自由、进步与和平的时代，几内亚民主党向你和你们伟大的党表示诚挚的信任。同时，我向你表示我个人的敬意和友情。　　　　几内亚民主党总书记　塞古·杜尔
                                  1960年10月1日于科纳克里
    几内亚总统杜尔的贺电中华人民共和国主席刘少奇阁下：
    在中华人民共和国国庆的时候，几内亚人民和几内亚政府愉快地向你个人、并向中国人民和中国政府表示热烈的祝贺和衷心的兄弟般的祝愿，祝中国人民和中国政府在进行使国家繁荣的建设中获得全面的成就，贵国动员了全国的力量为进步、正义与和平的事业而努力。中国人民和中华人民共和国政府的英勇行动对各国人民的自由事业作出了重大的贡献。中国人民和中国政府为爱好正义和进步的国家之间忠诚的合作和诚挚的友谊开辟了更广阔的道路。几内亚代表团最近访问了中华人民共和国，受到充满深情厚意的接待。这次访问加强了我们两国人民之间的团结，并且体现了我们要迅速挫败殖民主义和帝国主义的一切阴谋诡计的共同意志。我向你再一次表示几内亚人民和几内亚政府对中国人民的友好祝愿，祝中国人民幸福昌盛。同时，我向你表示我个人的兄弟般的情谊。
                                                塞古·杜尔
                                  1960年10月1日于科纳克里
      加纳总统事务委员会主席阿库·科萨的贺电中华人民共和国总理周恩来先生阁下：
    在中华人民共和国成立十一周年的时候，我以我个人的名义，并代表加纳共和国政府和人民，向阁下致以最热烈的祝贺，并对你个人的幸福和中国人民的昌盛表示最诚挚的祝愿。
    我们两国间的诚挚关系是令人高兴的，我们热望这种关系将随着以后的周年纪念日而加强。
                      总统事务委员会主席　阿库·科萨爵士
                                    1960年10月2日于阿克拉
      （电文来自代表加纳共和国总统克瓦米·恩克鲁玛博士的总统事务委员会）
      也门国王艾哈迈德的贺电中华人民共和国主席刘少奇阁下：

    秘鲁共产党总书记阿哥斯塔的贺电
    新华社3日讯　中国共产党中央委员会收到了秘鲁共产党中央委员会总书记阿哥斯塔发来的祝贺我国国庆的贺电。
    贺电说：
    “在庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年之际，秘鲁共产党中央委员会谨向中国共产党和中国人民致以热烈的祝贺。
    “我国人民以兴奋的心情注视着在社会主义建设中，在争取世界和平和各国人民民族解放的斗争中所取得的胜利。以苏联和人民中国为首的社会主义阵营的日益加强，无疑地削弱了帝国主义和战争势力。
    “古巴的革命开辟了拉丁美洲各国人民解放的道路。拉丁美洲各国人民日益以雷霆万钧之势对帝国主义的统治进行斗争，并且以排山倒海的战斗行动来表示他们对古巴革命的支援。
    “由于以拉丁美洲各国共产党与工人党为首的工人阶级与农民群众的战斗意志，由于民主力量日益增长的团结，由于社会主义阵营日益增长的威力，帝国主义者与本国寡头对人民的反动进攻和对古巴的侵略妄图必将失败。
    “亲爱的同志们，祝你们继续胜利地建设社会主义，祝我们的兄弟联系在马克思列宁主义的不朽旗帜下继续发展。
      “中华人民共和国万岁！
      “中国共产党万岁！”
