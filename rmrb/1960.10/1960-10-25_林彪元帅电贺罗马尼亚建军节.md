Title: Marshal Lin Biao Sends Congratulations on Romanian Army Day
Author:
Date: 1960-10-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th. Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, Marshal Lin Biao, today sent a telegram to General Salajan, Minister of the Armed Forces of the Romanian People's Republic, congratulating him on the 16th anniversary of the founding of the Romanian People's Army. The telegram reads as follows:
To Comrade General Salajan, Minister of the Armed Forces of the Romanian People's Republic,
Respected Minister Comrade:

	On the occasion of the glorious festival of the 16th anniversary of the founding of the Romanian People's Army, all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and I personally extend our warmest congratulations to you and all officers and men of the Romanian People's Army.

	We are sincerely pleased with the great achievements made by the fraternal Romanian People's Army, under the leadership of the Romanian Workers' Party, in defending the socialist construction of its own country, and in strengthening the political education and combat training of the troops.

	I wish the Romanian comrades-in-arms to make greater contributions to the struggle of defending world peace and opposing imperialist aggression and war conspiracies. I wish the fraternal friendship between the people and armies of China and Romania to continue to develop and strengthen.

	Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China
	Marshal of the People's Republic of China, Lin Biao
	October 24, 1960, in Beijing

### 林彪元帅电贺罗马尼亚建军节

1960-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    林彪元帅电贺罗马尼亚建军节
    新华社24日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪元帅，今天致电罗马尼亚人民共和国武装部队部长萨拉然大将，祝贺罗马尼亚人民军建军十六周年。电文如下：罗马尼亚人民共和国武装部队部长萨拉然大将同志，敬爱的部长同志：
    欣逢罗马尼亚人民军建军十六周年的光荣节日，中国人民解放军全体指战员和我个人，谨向您和罗马尼亚人民军全体官兵致以最热烈的祝贺。
    兄弟的罗马尼亚人民军，在罗马尼亚工人党的领导下，在保卫自己祖国的社会主义建设，在加强部队的政治教育和战斗训练的工作中，所取得的巨大成就，使我们感到由衷的高兴。
    祝罗马尼亚战友们，在捍卫世界和平、反对帝国主义的侵略和战争阴谋的斗争中作出更大的贡献。祝中、罗两国人民和军队之间的兄弟友谊不断发展和增强。
    中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长
    中华人民共和国元帅  林彪
    1960年10月24日于北京
