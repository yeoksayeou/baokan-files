Title: Hard Iron Becomes a Soft Bone
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 2

    Hard Iron Becomes a Soft Bone
    In the past, I used a hammer to forge iron,
    The iron laughed at my lack of strength;
    Now I use machines to forge iron,
    Hard iron becomes a soft bone.
    (Worker from Changzhou)

### 硬铁变成软骨头

1960-10-11
第2版()
专栏：

    硬铁变成软骨头
    过去打铁用鎯头，
    铁笑我的力不够；
    如今打铁用机器，
    硬铁变成软骨头。
    （常州市工人）
