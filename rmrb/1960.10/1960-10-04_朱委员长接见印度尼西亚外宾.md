Title: Chairman Zhu De Receives Indonesian Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-10-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd: Chairman Zhu De received Asmara Hadi, member of the Indonesian Supreme Advisory Council and Chairman of the Indonesian Party, and his wife; Asnawi Said, Secretary General of the Indonesian Party; Rusli, Secretary General of the Indonesian Parliament, and his wife; and Brotodingingrat, First Secretary of the Presidential Office of Indonesia, and his wife, this afternoon. They had a friendly conversation.

Present at the meeting were Zhang Su, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Lian Guan, Deputy Secretary-General; Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; and Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General. (Attached photo)

Chairman Zhu De and the Indonesian guests in a group photo. From left: Mrs. Brotodingingrat, Brotodingingrat, Mrs. Asmara Hadi, Asmara Hadi, Zhu De, Rusli, Mrs. Rusli, Asnawi Said.

### 朱委员长接见印度尼西亚外宾

1960-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    朱委员长接见印度尼西亚外宾
    新华社3日讯　朱德委员长在今天下午接见了印度尼西亚最高评议会议员、印度尼西亚党总主席阿斯马腊·哈迪和夫人，印度尼西亚党秘书长阿斯纳威·赛义德，印度尼西亚国会秘书长鲁斯里和夫人，印度尼西亚总统府办公厅第一秘书布罗托迪宁格拉特和夫人，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    在座的，有人大常委会委员张苏、副秘书长连贯，中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之、副秘书长吴晓达。（附图片）
