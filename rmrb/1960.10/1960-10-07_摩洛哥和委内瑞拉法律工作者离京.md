Title: Moroccan and Venezuelan Legal Workers Leave Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 4
　
    Xinhua News Agency, 6th -- Moroccan legal workers Abdul-Owa El Alami, Mohammed Rougiri, Benarosh Ralph, and Venezuelan legal worker Dr. Jose Sanchez Mihares, who were invited by the China Political Science and Law Society to visit our country, concluded their visit in China and left Beijing today to go to Sofia to attend the Seventh Congress of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers. Wu Defeng, Vice President of the China Political Science and Law Society, and Ma Xiwu, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court, and others saw them off at the airport.

### 摩洛哥和委内瑞拉法律工作者离京

1960-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥和委内瑞拉法律工作者离京
    新华社6日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的摩洛哥法律工作者阿布杜尔奥瓦·埃尔·阿拉米，穆罕默德·鲁吉里，贝纳罗什·拉尔夫和委内瑞拉法律工作者荷塞·桑切斯·米哈雷斯博士结束了在我国的访问，今天离开北京，前往索非亚参加国际民主法律工作者协会第七届大会。中国政治法律学会副会长吴德峰，最高人民法院副院长马锡五等到机场欢送。
