Title: Korean Cabinet Hosts Banquet for Chinese Military Friendship Delegation; Premier Kim Il-sung Attends, Guests Toast to Eternal Friendship Between Chinese and Korean People

Author: 
Date: 1960-10-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 25th: The Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea held a grand banquet tonight at the Okryu Hall in Pyongyang to welcome the Military Friendship Delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council and Vice Premier of the State Council, Marshal He Long, and Vice Premier of the State Council and Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Luo Ruiqing.

At 8 PM, Premier Kim Il-sung of the Korean Cabinet, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly Choi Yong-kun, First Vice Premier of the Cabinet Kim Il, Vice Premier of the Cabinet and Minister of National Defence General Kim Kwang-hyup, and other Korean Party, government, and military leaders, accompanied Marshal He Long, General Luo Ruiqing, and all members of the delegation, into the brightly lit banquet hall. A warm applause erupted throughout the hall.

First Vice Premier of the Korean Cabinet Kim Il and Marshal He Long delivered speeches at the banquet respectively (full texts appear on page six of today's newspaper).

The banquet proceeded in a warm, enthusiastic, and friendly atmosphere. The hosts and guests repeatedly raised their glasses to wish the unbreakable, blood-cemented friendship between the people and armies of China and Korea would last forever, and to wish the great unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union would continue to strengthen.

Also present at today's banquet were Vice Premiers of the Korean Cabinet Hong Myung-hui, Jung Il-ryong, Nam Il, Lee Jong-ok, Jung Jun-taek, and Lee Joo-yeon; Vice Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Park Jung-ae, Park Kum-chul, and Kim Chang-man; Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Lim Hae, Alternate Standing Committee Member Kim Ik-sun; Chairman of the Korea-China Friendship Association Ha Ang-cheon, ministers of the Cabinet, presidium members of the Fatherland Front, heads of various social organizations, and senior generals of the Korean People's Army, including Chief of General Staff Kim Chang-bong.

Chinese Ambassador to Korea Qiao Xiaoguang, Military Attaché Colonel Zhang Leting, and diplomatic officials of the embassy, as well as foreign diplomatic envoys and military attachés stationed in Korea, also attended the banquet.

All comrades of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Art Troupe, who accompanied the delegation to Korea, were also invited to attend the banquet.

Famous Korean artists performed wonderful programs during the banquet. (Photo attached)

### 朝鲜内阁欢宴我军事友好代表团  金日成首相出席宴会，宾主共祝中朝人民友谊万古长青

1960-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜内阁欢宴我军事友好代表团
    金日成首相出席宴会，宾主共祝中朝人民友谊万古长青
    新华社平壤25日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁今天晚上在平壤市玉流馆内举行盛大宴会，欢迎由中国国防委员会副主席、国务院副总理贺龙元帅和国务院副总理、中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿大将所率领的中华人民共和国访朝军事友好代表团。
    晚八时，朝鲜内阁首相金日成和最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健、内阁第一副首相金一、内阁副首相兼民族保卫相金光侠大将等朝鲜党、政、军领导人，陪同贺龙元帅和罗瑞卿大将等代表团全体成员，步入灯火辉煌的宴会大厅。此时，全场响起了热烈的掌声。
    朝鲜内阁第一副首相金一和贺龙元帅先后在宴会上讲了话（全文见今日本报第六版）。
    宴会一直在热情洋溢、亲切友好的气氛中进行，宾主不断举杯祝中朝两国人民和军队之间的以鲜血结成的战斗友谊万古长青，祝以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的伟大团结不断加强。
    参加今天宴会的还有朝鲜内阁副首相洪命熹、郑一龙、南日、李钟玉、郑准泽、李周渊，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、朴金喆、金昌满，劳动党中央常务委员林海、候补常务委员金翊善，朝中友好协会委员长河仰天，内阁各相，祖国战线议长团的议长们，各社会团体负责人，朝鲜人民军总参谋长金昌奉上将等人民军高级将领等。
    中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光、武官张乐亭上校和使馆外交官员，以及各国驻朝鲜外交使节和武官也参加了宴会。
    随同代表团访朝的中国人民解放军文艺工作团的全体同志也应邀参加了宴会。
    朝鲜著名的艺术家们在宴会进行中演出了精彩节目。（附图片）
