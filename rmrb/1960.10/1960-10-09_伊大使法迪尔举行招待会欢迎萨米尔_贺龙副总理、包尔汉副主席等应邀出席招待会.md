Title: Iraqi Ambassador Fadil Holds Reception to Welcome Samir; Vice Premier He Long, Vice Chairman Burhan and Others Invited to Attend

Author:
Date: 1960-10-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 8th. Iraqi Ambassador to China, Fadil, held a reception this evening to welcome Faisal Samir, Minister of the National Guidance Department of the Republic of Iraq, who is visiting our country. Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, and Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Burhan attended the reception.

Ambassador Fadil, Minister Samir, and Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Xiruo spoke at the reception.

Those invited to attend the reception also included: Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Minister of Public Health Li Dequan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Vice President of the China-Iraq Friendship Association Luo Jun, and leaders from the capital's scientific and cultural fields.

### 伊大使法迪尔举行招待会欢迎萨米尔  贺龙副总理、包尔汉副主席等应邀出席招待会

1960-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    伊大使法迪尔举行招待会欢迎萨米尔
    贺龙副总理、包尔汉副主席等应邀出席招待会
  新华社8日讯　伊拉克驻中国大使法迪尔今晚举行招待会，欢迎来我国访问的伊拉克共和国国家指导部部长费萨尔·萨米尔。国务院副总理贺龙，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉出席了招待会。
    法迪尔大使、萨米尔部长和我国对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若在招待会上讲了话。
  应邀出席招待会的还有：文化部部长沈雁冰，卫生部部长李德全，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国伊拉克友好协会副会长罗俊，以及首都科学、文化等方面的负责人。
