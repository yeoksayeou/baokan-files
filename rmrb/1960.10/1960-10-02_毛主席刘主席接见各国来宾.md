Title: Chairman Mao and Chairman Liu Receive Guests from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-10-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 1st: Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, and Chairman Zhu De received the heads of delegations from various countries visiting China and attending the National Day celebrations, as well as prominent figures from all fields, at Tiananmen Gate Tower this evening.

These foreign guests came from more than seventy countries and regions across five continents, including people from political, economic, military, cultural, scientific, trade union, youth, women, news, and sports fields. Also received were the ambassadors of various countries to China and their wives.

During the reception, Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, and Chairman Zhu De warmly shook hands with the guests one by one. The guests enthusiastically extended their greetings to Chairman Mao Zedong and other leaders. Chairman Mao Zedong and others expressed their warm welcome to them, thanking them for bringing the friendship of the people of their countries.

### 毛主席刘主席接见各国来宾

1960-10-02
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席刘主席接见各国来宾
    新华社1日讯　毛泽东主席，刘少奇主席，董必武副主席和朱德委员长，今天晚上在天安门城楼上接见了来我国访问和参加国庆观礼的各国代表团的负责人和各方面的著名人士。
    这些外宾来自五大洲七十多个国家和地区，包括政治、经济、军事、文化、科学、工会、青年、妇女、新闻、体育等各方面的人士。被接见的还有各国驻中国的使节和他们的夫人。
    在接见的时候，毛泽东主席、刘少奇主席、董必武副主席和朱德委员长同来宾们一一亲切握手。各国来宾热情地向毛泽东主席等领导人致意问候。毛泽东主席等向他们表示热烈的欢迎。感谢他们带来了各国人民的友谊。
