Title: East German Men's and Women's Cycling Teams Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 9th October. A delegation of twelve from the East German national men's and women's cycling teams, led by Heinz Dietrich, Secretary-General of the East German Cycling Association, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They were warmly welcomed at the airport by representatives of the National Sports Commission and Chinese cyclists.

East German cycling has a very high level. The team visiting our country this time includes six male athletes and four female athletes. Among them, the male athlete Schur won the individual championship in the 12th Prague-Warsaw-Berlin International Peace Cycling Race last year.

The visiting team is scheduled to hold its first friendly match with the Chinese men's and women's cycling teams in Beijing Velodrome on the 16th.

### 德意志民主共和国男女自行车队到京

1960-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国男女自行车队到京
    新华社9日讯　德意志民主共和国国家男女自行车队一行十二人，今天由德意志民主共和国自行车协会秘书长海因茨·迪特里希率领乘飞机到达北京。他们在机场受到国家体委的代表和我国自行车运动员们的热烈欢迎。
    德意志民主共和国自行车运动有很高的水平，这次来我国的共有六名男运动员和四名女运动员，其中男运动员舒尔，在去年曾经获得第十二届布拉格—华沙—柏林国际和平自行车比赛的个人冠军。
    客队预定16日在北京赛车场，同中国男女自行车队举行访华第一次友谊比赛。
