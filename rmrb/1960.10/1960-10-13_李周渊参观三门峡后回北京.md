Title: Li Zhouyuan Returns to Beijing After Visiting Sanmenxia
Author:
Date: 1960-10-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 12th. Li Zhouyuan, Vice Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and the Korean government economic delegation he led, left Sanmenxia for Beijing by special train this afternoon.

The distinguished guests arrived in Sanmenxia this morning from Luoyang. After arriving in Sanmenxia, accompanied by responsible persons from Sanmenxia City and the Engineering Bureau, they visited the Sanmenxia Hydro-junction Project on the Yellow River and the chariot and horse burial pits, relics from China's Warring States period.

On the 10th and 11th, the distinguished guests, accompanied by the responsible persons of the Party and government of Luoyang City, visited the Luoyang Bearing Factory, the Luoyang Mining Machinery Factory, the Luoyang No. 1 Tractor Factory, and the Guanlin Temple, and toured Longmen.

### 李周渊参观三门峡后回北京

1960-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    李周渊参观三门峡后回北京
    新华社郑州12日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国副首相李周渊和他率领的朝鲜政府经济代表团，今天下午由三门峡乘专车去北京。
    贵宾们是在今天上午从洛阳到三门峡的。到三门峡后，在三门峡市和工程局的负责人陪同下，参观了黄河三门峡水利枢纽工程和我国战国时代的遗迹车马坑。
    10日、11日，贵宾在洛阳由洛阳市的党和政府负责人陪同下，参观了洛阳轴承厂、洛阳矿山机械厂、洛阳第一拖拉机厂，及关林博物馆等，并游览了龙门。
