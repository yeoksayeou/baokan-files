Title: Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun Receives Delegation from the Japanese National Railway Workers' Union
Author:
Date: 1960-10-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 17th: Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun received the delegation from the Japanese National Railway Workers' Union headed by Soichi Hosoi, Central Executive Committee Member of the Japanese National Railway Workers' Union, and Kanji Yoshida, Central Executive Committee Member of the Motive Power Workers' Union, this afternoon and held friendly talks with them.

Present at the reception were Li Jiebo, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Xiao Hongqi, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union. (Attached picture)

Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun talks with Soichi Hosoi (second from right) and Kanji Yoshida (first from right).

### 习仲勋副总理  接见日本国营铁道工会代表团

1960-10-18
第4版()
专栏：

    习仲勋副总理
    接见日本国营铁道工会代表团
    新华社17日讯　习仲勋副总理今天下午接见了以日本国营铁道工会中央执行委员细井宗一和动力车工会中央执行委员吉田辛治为首的日本国营铁道工会代表团，并且进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有中华全国总工会副主席李颉伯，中国铁路工会全国委员会副主席萧洪启等。（附图片）
