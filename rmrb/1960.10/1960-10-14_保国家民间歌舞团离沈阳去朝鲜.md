Title: Bulgarian National Folk Song and Dance Ensemble Leaves Shenyang for North Korea
Author:
Date: 1960-10-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, October 13th. The Bulgarian National Folk Song and Dance Ensemble concluded its five-day visit in Shenyang and left Shenyang by special train tonight for a visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. Luo Ting, Deputy Director of the Liaoning Provincial Department of Culture, Wang Jinshan, Deputy Director of the Shenyang Municipal Bureau of Culture, and heads of units such as the Liaoning Branch of the Chinese Musicians Association, the Liaoning Branch of the Chinese Dancers Association, the Shenyang Conservatory of Music, the Liaoning Opera House, as well as representatives from the literary and art circles of Liaoning Province and Shenyang City, went to the station to see them off.

Before the song and dance ensemble left Shenyang, Zhang Jizhong, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, hosted an informal banquet to bid them farewell.

During their stay in Shenyang, the song and dance ensemble gave three performances for the working people and art workers of Shenyang. The more than twenty Bulgarian folk music and dance programs performed by the Bulgarian artists were warmly welcomed by the audience. During their stay in Shenyang, the song and dance ensemble also visited the Liaoning Industrial Exhibition Hall, the Sino-Korean Friendship People's Commune in the suburbs of Shenyang, the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant, and the city of Shenyang. On the 13th, they also visited Anshan Iron and Steel.

During its forty days in China, the Bulgarian National Folk Song and Dance Ensemble has visited and performed in six cities: Zhengzhou, Beijing, Tianjin, Wuhan, Shanghai, and Shenyang.

### 保国家民间歌舞团离沈阳去朝鲜

1960-10-14
第4版()
专栏：

    保国家民间歌舞团离沈阳去朝鲜
    新华社沈阳13日电　保加利亚国家民间歌舞团结束了在沈阳历时五天的访问，今晚乘专车离开沈阳赴朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访问。辽宁省文化厅副厅长洛汀、沈阳市文化局副局长王金山和中国音乐家协会辽宁分会、中国舞蹈工作者协会辽宁分会、沈阳音乐学院、辽宁歌剧院等单位的负责人以及辽宁省、沈阳市文艺界代表等到车站欢送。
    歌舞团离开沈阳前，沈阳市副市长张霁中曾设便宴欢送。
    歌舞团在沈阳期间，为沈阳市劳动人民和艺术工作者作了三场演出。保加利亚艺术家们表演的二十多个保加利亚民间音乐、舞蹈节目，受到了观众的热烈欢迎，歌舞团在沈阳期间，还先后参观了辽宁工业展览馆、沈阳市郊中朝友谊人民公社、沈阳第一机床厂和沈阳市容。13日还去鞍钢参观。
    保加利亚国家民间歌舞团在我国四十天中，先后在郑州、北京、天津、武汉、上海、沈阳等六个城市进行了访问演出。
