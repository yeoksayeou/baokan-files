Title: Part of the Burmese Government Delegation Leaves Shanghai for Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-10-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 16th. Some distinguished guests of the Burmese Government Delegation, including Duwa Sinwa Nawng, Kachin State Head and Minister of Kachin State Affairs of the Federal Government, and Su Htun E, Minister of Education and Health of the Shan State Government, a party of thirteen, left Shanghai for Hangzhou by train on the 15th after a three-day visit to Shanghai.

Those who saw them off at the station included Song Richang, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, Liu Jingji, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, responsible persons from relevant departments of the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, and directors of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association in Shanghai.

During their stay in Shanghai, the distinguished guests visited the Shanghai Industrial Exhibition, Shanghai Steel Plant No. 1, State Cotton Mill No. 2, Da Zhonghua Rubber Factory, Zhongshan Hospital, Shanghai East Middle School, and the Children's Palace.

### 缅甸政府代表团部分贵宾离沪去杭

1960-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸政府代表团部分贵宾离沪去杭
    新华社上海16日电　缅甸政府代表团的部分贵宾——克钦邦邦长、联邦政府克钦邦事务部部长杜瓦信瓦瑙和掸邦政府教育和卫生部部长苏吞埃等一行十三人，在上海访问三天以后，15日乘火车离上海去杭州。
    到车站欢送的有上海市副市长宋日昌，政协上海市委员会副主席刘靖基，以及上海市人民委员会有关部门的负责人和中缅友好协会在上海的理事等。
    贵宾们在上海期间，曾参观访问了上海市工业展览会、上钢一厂、国棉二厂、大中华橡胶厂、中山医院、市东中学和少年宫等。
