Title: Uruguay Concludes Celebrations for China's National Day
Author:
Date: 1960-10-20
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, 19th: Celebrations for the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China concluded yesterday in the Uruguayan capital. At the closing ceremony held by the Uruguay-China Cultural Association, Dr. Magdalena Sueiro of the association gave a report on the construction and development of China. She introduced the achievements of New China in industry, agriculture, education, housing, public health, and children's education. The meeting hall was packed with listeners, who applauded the excellent report.
    The Uruguay-China Cultural Association also published a booklet of reports by Uruguayan parliamentarians who visited China last year. These reports show the great progress made by the Chinese people in industrializing their country, as well as the creativity and peace-loving spirit of the Chinese people.

### 乌拉圭结束庆祝我国庆活动

1960-10-20
第6版()
专栏：

    乌拉圭结束庆祝我国庆活动
    新华社19日讯　蒙得维的亚消息：庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年的活动昨天在乌拉圭首都闭幕。在乌拉圭—中国文化协会举行的闭幕会上，协会的马格达莱娜·苏埃伊罗博士作了关于中国建设发展情况的报告。她介绍了新中国在工业、农业、教育、住房、公共卫生和儿童教育各方面所获得的成就。会议大厅里挤满了听众，他们鼓掌欢迎这次精彩的报告。
    乌拉圭—中国文化协会还出版了去年访问中国的乌拉圭议员所作的报告的小册子。从这些报告中可以看到中国人民在使国家工业化方面所取得的巨大进展以及中国人民的创造性和爱好和平的精神。
