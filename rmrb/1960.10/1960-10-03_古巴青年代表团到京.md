Title: Cuban Youth Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-03
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. A Cuban youth delegation of five, invited by the All-China Youth Federation, arrived in Beijing on the 1st. The members of the delegation are: Gerardo Figueras, Secretary of Foreign Relations of the Cuban "July 26th" Movement; Captain Fernando Ravello, National Leader of the Cuban Revolutionary Army Youth Association; Oscar Fernandez Badia, Secretary of the National Committee of the Cuban Socialist Youth League; Pedro Martínez Lizarraga, National Leader of the "March 13th" Revolutionary Directorate; Omar Rogas Soler, representative of the Cuban University Students Federation and Vice Chairman of the Student Union of the Faculty of Engineering of the University of Havana.
    Upon their arrival in Beijing, they were welcomed by Zhang Chao, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, and others.

### 古巴青年代表团到京

1960-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    古巴青年代表团到京
    新华社2日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请，古巴青年代表团一行五人在1日到达北京。代表团的成员是：古巴“七月二十六日”运动对外关系书记赫拉尔多·菲格雷斯，古巴起义军青年协会全国领导人费尔南多·拉维洛大尉，古巴社会主义青年联盟全国委员会书记奥斯卡·费尔南德思·巴迪亚，“三月十三日”革命指导委员会全国领导人彼德罗·马尔第内斯·里萨拉加，古巴大学生联合会代表、哈瓦那大学工程学院学生会副主席奥马尔·罗加·索莱尔。
    他们到达北京时，受到中华全国青年联合会副主席、中国共产主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记张超等人的欢迎。
