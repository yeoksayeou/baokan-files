Title: "People's Daily" of Vietnam Publishes Commentary Welcoming Laos Establishing Diplomatic Relations with the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-10-10
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 8th: The Vietnamese newspaper "People's Daily" published a commentary today stating that the decision of the Royal Lao Government, headed by Prince Souvanna Phouma, to establish diplomatic relations with the Soviet Union is clearly in the interests of the people of Laos and the Soviet Union, and in the interests of peace in Indochina and the world.

The commentary added: "As a neighbor of Laos and a signatory to the Geneva Agreements on Laos, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Vietnamese people greatly welcome the relations between the Soviet Union and Laos."

The commentary continued, the Royal Lao Government's decision to establish diplomatic relations with the Soviet Union at the ambassadorial level is an important first step taken by the Lao government and people on the road of pursuing a policy of peace, neutrality, and national harmony. It will help to defend Laos' independence, make Laos prosperous and strong, and enhance Laos' international prestige.

### 越南“人民报”发表评论  欢迎老挝同苏联建交

1960-10-10
第5版()
专栏：

    越南“人民报”发表评论
    欢迎老挝同苏联建交
    据新华社河内8日电　越南“人民报”今天发表评论说，以梭发那·富马亲王为首的老挝王国政府同苏联建立外交关系的决定，显然是符合老挝和苏联两国人民的利益，符合印度支那和世界和平的利益的。
    评论又说：“作为老挝的邻国和签订关于老挝的日内瓦协议的一方，越南民主共和国和越南人民非常欢迎苏联同老挝之间的关系。”
    评论接着说，老挝王国政府决定同苏联建立大使级外交关系，这是老挝政府和人民在奉行和平中立和民族和睦政策道路上迈进的重要的第一步。它将有助于保卫老挝的独立，使老挝富强，提高老挝在国际上的威信。
