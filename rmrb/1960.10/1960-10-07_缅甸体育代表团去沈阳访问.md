Title: Burmese Sports Delegation Leaves for Shenyang to Visit
Author:
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Burmese sports delegation, comprising sixty-five members and led by head of delegation, Maung Wun, and accompanied by Senior Colonel Zhang Shaoting of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, departed by train at 6:25 PM today for a friendly visit to Shenyang.

　　During their stay in Beijing, the Burmese sports delegation participated in our National Day celebrations and activities commemorating the signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty. The delegation's Chinlone (caneball) team and boxing team gave two outstanding performances, while the delegation's football team played two matches against the "August 1st" football team of the People's Liberation Army and the Beijing Military Region football team, all of which won enthusiastic praise from the audience.

　　The distinguished guests received a warm send-off at the railway station today. Chinese athletes presented flowers to the distinguished guests. Those who came to see them off at the station today included Major General Xu Wenlie, Senior Colonel Han Fudong, Senior Colonel Li Wei, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Football Association Li Fenglou, Deputy Director of the Beijing Municipal Sports Commission Jia Guirong, and officers from the Army, Navy, and Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

　　Burmese Ambassador to China Kyao Winn and First Secretary of the Embassy U Ong Kin also came to the station to see them off.

### 缅甸体育代表团去沈阳访问

1960-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸体育代表团去沈阳访问
    新华社6日讯　缅甸体育代表团一行六十五人，在团长貌温率领下，由中国人民解放军张少廷大校陪同，今天下午六时二十五分乘火车去沈阳进行友好访问。
    缅甸体育代表团在北京期间，参加了我国国庆和庆祝中缅边界条约签订活动。代表团的藤球代表队和拳击代表队，进行了两场精彩表演；代表团的足球代表队与解放军“八一”足球队和北京部队足球队进行了两场比赛，都赢得了观众热烈称赞。
    今天在车站上，贵宾们受到了热情欢送。中国运动员向贵宾献了鲜花。今天到车站欢送的有徐文烈少将、韩复东大校、李伟大校和中国足球协会副主席李凤楼、北京市体育运动委员会副主任贾桂荣以及中国人民解放军陆海空军军官。
    缅甸驻我国大使叫温、大使馆一等秘书吴翁钦也到车站送行。
