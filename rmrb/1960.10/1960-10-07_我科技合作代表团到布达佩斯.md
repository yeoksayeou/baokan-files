Title: Chinese Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation Arrives in Budapest
Author:
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, October 5th. The Chinese Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation, headed by Luo Shuzhang, Vice Minister of Light Industry, arrived in Budapest by plane on the 5th.

　　The delegation is here to attend the Sixth Session of the Sino-Hungarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee.

### 我科技合作代表团到布达佩斯

1960-10-07
第6版()
专栏：

    我科技合作代表团到布达佩斯
   新华社布达佩斯5日电　以轻工业部副部长罗叔章为首的中国科学技术合作代表团在5日乘飞机到达布达佩斯。
    代表团是前来参加第六届中匈科学技术合作委员会会议的。
