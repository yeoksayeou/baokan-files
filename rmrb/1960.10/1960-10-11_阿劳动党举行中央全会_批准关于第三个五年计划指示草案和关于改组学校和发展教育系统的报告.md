Title: Albanian Labor Party Holds Central Committee Plenary Session, Approves Draft Directives on the Third Five-Year Plan and Report on Reorganizing Schools and Developing the Education System
Author:
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, October 9th: The Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, chaired by First Secretary Comrade Hoxha, held a plenary session on October 7th and 8th.

    According to the communiqué issued by the meeting, the plenary session discussed and approved the draft directives "On the Third Five-Year Plan for the Development of the Economy and Culture of the People's Republic of Albania from 1961 to 1965," which will be submitted to the Fourth Congress of the Labor Party, and decided to submit this draft directives to the national working people for discussion.

    The plenary session discussed the report by Comrade Mufti, member of the Central Political Bureau and Minister of Education and Culture, on the Central Political Bureau's "Report on Reorganizing Schools and Further Developing the Education System." After discussing the report, the plenary session considered the opinions of the nationwide discussion and unanimously approved the report, deciding to reorganize schools and the education system in accordance with the principles outlined in the outline proposed by the Central Committee of the Labor Party and the Council of Ministers.

    The plenary session also decided to postpone the Fourth Congress of the Albanian Labor Party, originally scheduled for November, to December.

### 阿劳动党举行中央全会  批准关于第三个五年计划指示草案和关于改组学校和发展教育系统的报告

1960-10-11
第5版()
专栏：

    阿劳动党举行中央全会
    批准关于第三个五年计划指示草案和关于改组学校和发展教育系统的报告
    新华社地拉那9日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会在第一书记霍查同志主持下于10月7日到8日举行了全体会议。
    据会议发表的公报说，全会讨论和批准了将在劳动党第四次代表大会上提出的“关于1961年到1965年发展阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国经济和文化的第三个五年计划”的指示草案，并决定将这一指示草案提交全国劳动人民讨论。
    全会讨论了由中央政治局委员、教育和文化部部长穆夫蒂乌同志所作的中央政治局“关于改组学校和进一步发展教育系统”的报告。全会在讨论了报告之后，考虑了全民讨论的意见，一致批准了这一报告，决定按照劳动党中央委员会和部长会议提出的提纲所说明的原则改组学校和教育系统。
    全会并决定将原定在11月召开的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第四次代表大会延期到12月。
