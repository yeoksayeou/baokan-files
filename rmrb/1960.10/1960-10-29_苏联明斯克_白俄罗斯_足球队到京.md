Title: Soviet Minsk "Belarus" Football Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 28th. The Soviet Minsk "Belarus" football team, a group of twenty-five people led by Liventsev, Chairman of the Sports Federation of the Soviet Belarusian Socialist Republic, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning at the invitation of our country. Li Fenglou, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Football Association, and Chinese football players went to the airport to welcome them.

The "Belarus" football team is one of the excellent teams in the Soviet Union. The twenty athletes visiting our country this time include three masters of sports: goalkeeper Denisenko, forwards Derekhov and Albutov, midfielder Zhukov, and excellent forward Khasin, etc. The tallest player on the visiting team is 1.82 meters tall, and the youngest is 19 years old.

The visiting team is scheduled to hold friendly matches with the Beijing football team and the "August 1st" football team in Beijing on October 30th and November 2nd respectively. Afterwards, they will visit and play matches in Wuhan, Guangzhou and other places.

### 苏联明斯克“白俄罗斯”足球队到京

1960-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联明斯克“白俄罗斯”足球队到京
    新华社28日讯　应邀来我国访问的苏联明斯克“白俄罗斯”足球队一行二十五人，在苏联白俄罗斯苏维埃社会主义共和国体联主席利文切夫率领下，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。中国足球协会副主席李凤楼和中国足球运动员等前往机场欢迎。
    “白俄罗斯”足球队是苏联优秀球队之一。这次来我国访问的二十名运动员中，包括三名运动健将：守门员杰尼先科，前锋杰列霍夫、阿尔布托夫，前卫茹科夫和优秀前锋哈辛等。客队中身材最高的是一米八十二，年龄最小的是十九岁。
    客队预定10月30日和11月2日在北京分别和北京足球队、“八一”足球队举行友谊比赛。以后将到武汉、广州等地进行访问比赛。
