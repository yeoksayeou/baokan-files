Title: Wu Nu Receives Chinese Trade Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-17
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, October 16th. Burmese Prime Minister Wu Nu today received the entire delegation of the Chinese Government Trade Delegation, headed by Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade.

　　Prime Minister Wu Nu had a cordial and friendly conversation with the Chinese trade delegation and expressed his good wishes for promoting the development of trade between China and Burma.

　　Present at the meeting were Burmese Minister of Trade Development U Thun, Acting Director of the State Agricultural Products Trade Bureau U Hla, and Prime Minister Wu Nu's advisor U Wan.

　　Also present at the meeting was Lei Yang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma.

　　The Chinese trade delegation yesterday visited Burmese Minister of Trade Development U Thun.

　　The Government Trade Delegation of the People's Republic of China arrived in Rangoon by plane on the afternoon of the 15th at the invitation of the Government of the Union of Burma for a visit.

### 吴努接见我贸易代表团

1960-10-17
第5版()
专栏：

    吴努接见我贸易代表团
    新华社仰光16日电　缅甸联邦总理吴努今天接见了以对外贸易部副部长雷任民为首的中华人民共和国政府贸易代表团全体团员。
    吴努总理同中国贸易代表团进行了亲切和友好的谈话，并且表示了要促进中缅两国贸易发展的良好祝愿。
    接见时在座的有缅甸贸易发展部部长吴敦、国营农产品贸易局代理局长吴拉和吴努总理的顾问吴旺。
    接见时在座的还有中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬。
    中国贸易代表团昨天访晤了缅甸贸易发展部部长吴敦。
    中华人民共和国政府贸易代表团应缅甸联邦政府的邀请于15日下午乘飞机到达仰光进行访问的。
