Title: Liu Ningyi Sends Telegram Congratulating Cuba on its Victory in Annihilating Counter-Revolutionary Bandits
Author:
Date: 1960-10-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 14th: Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, today sent a telegram to the Cuban Workers' Central Union, stating that Chinese workers strongly condemn U.S. imperialism for organizing counter-revolutionary bandits to invade Cuba, and warmly congratulate the Cuban people on their victory in annihilating counter-revolutionary activities in the Escambray Mountains.

The telegram said, "We believe that the Cuban people, vigilantly defending national sovereignty and revolutionary achievements, will further mobilize to give a heavier blow to the interference and aggression of U.S. imperialism. In your just struggle, you will always have the support of the Chinese workers and people."

### 刘宁一电贺古巴胜利歼灭反革命匪徒

1960-10-15
第5版()
专栏：

    刘宁一电贺古巴胜利歼灭反革命匪徒
    新华社14日讯　中华全国总工会主席刘宁一今天致电古巴工联说，中国工人强烈谴责美帝国主义组织反革命匪徒侵入古巴，并热烈祝贺古巴人民在歼灭埃斯坎布拉伊山区反革命活动中取得的胜利。
    电报说，我们相信，警惕地保卫着国家主权和革命成果的古巴人民，必将进一步动员起来，给予美帝国主义的干涉与侵略活动更沉重的打击。在你们的正义斗争中，永远将得到中国工人和人民的支持。
