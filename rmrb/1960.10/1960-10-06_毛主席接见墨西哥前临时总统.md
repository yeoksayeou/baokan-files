Title: Chairman Mao Receives Former Interim President of Mexico
Author:
Date: 1960-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 5th. Chairman Mao Zedong received Emilio Portes Gil, the former interim President of Mexico, at Yinian Hall in Zhongnanhai this morning and had a cordial conversation with him. Present were Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, and Zhang Zhixiang, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. ### 毛主席接见墨西哥前临时总统

1960-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见墨西哥前临时总统
    新华社5日讯　毛泽东主席今天上午在中南海颐年堂接见了墨西哥前临时总统埃米略·波尔特斯·希耳，同他进行了亲切的谈话。在座的，有中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南，中国人民对外文化协会副会长张致祥。（附图片）
