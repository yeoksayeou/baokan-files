Title: Algerian National Liberation Army Severely Punishes Invaders: Annihilates Nearly 2,700 Enemy Troops, Shoots Down 27 Enemy Planes in Half a Month
Author:
Date: 1960-10-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 17th - According to a military communique released today by the headquarters of the Algerian National Liberation Army, from September 27th to October 12th, the Algerian National Liberation Army, in a series of battles with French troops, killed 1,427 French soldiers, wounded 1,266, and seized 1,117 weapons.

The communique also stated that in these battles, the Algerian National Liberation Army destroyed 123 French military vehicles, one tank, one armored vehicle, shot down 27 French aircraft, and destroyed four military trains, and seized a large quantity of ammunition.

The communique said that during this period, Algerian forces launched thirty-six attacks on French positions, engaged the French army in fifty-nine locations, and ambushed French forces and their military convoys seventy-two times. Algerian National Liberation Army commandos also took seventy-one military actions against the enemy throughout Algeria.

### 阿尔及利亚民族解放军痛击侵略者  半月歼敌近二千七百人击落敌机二十七架

1960-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚民族解放军痛击侵略者
    半月歼敌近二千七百人击落敌机二十七架
    新华社开罗17日电　据阿尔及利亚民族解放军总部今天发表的一项军事公报说，从9月27日至10月12日，阿尔及利亚民族解放军在同法国军队进行一系列的战斗中共打死法国兵一千四百二十七人，打伤一千二百六十六人，缴获一千一百一十七件武器。
    公报又说，在这些战斗中，阿尔及利亚民族解放军击毁法军一百二十三辆军用汽车、一辆坦克、一辆装甲车，击落法国飞机二十七架和炸毁军用列车四列，并缴获大量弹药。
    公报说，在这期间，阿尔及利亚部队对法军阵地发动了三十六次攻击，在五十九个地方同法军交战，并对法军及其军用车队进行了七十二次伏击。阿尔及利亚民族解放军突击队还在阿尔及利亚全国各地对敌人采取了七十一次军事行动。
