Title: Algerian Art Troupe Visits and Performs in Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1960-10-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, October 29th: After visiting and performing two splendid shows in Wuhan, the Algerian Art Troupe is taking the train to Beijing tonight.
    The Algerian artists' performances in Wuhan, with their unique national style and beautiful performing arts, left an unforgettable impression on the people of Wuhan.
    Meng Futang, Deputy Governor of Hubei Province, Xiong Fei, Deputy Mayor of Wuhan, and others watched the art troupe's performance, met with Afa Abdul Madi, the representative of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria stationed with the art troupe, and all members of the art troupe, and hosted a banquet to congratulate them on the success of their performances in Wuhan.

### 阿尔及利亚艺术团在武汉访问演出

1960-10-30
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚艺术团在武汉访问演出
    据新华社武汉29日电　阿尔及利亚艺术团在武汉参观访问并作了两场精彩的演出后，今晚乘火车去北京。
    阿尔及利亚艺术家们在武汉的演出，通过独特的民族风格和优美的表演艺术，给武汉市人民留下了难忘的印象。
    湖北省副省长孟夫唐、武汉市副市长熊飞等观看了艺术团的演出，接见了阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府派驻艺术团代表阿法·阿卜杜勒·玛蒂和艺术团全体成员，并设宴祝贺他们在武汉的演出成功。
