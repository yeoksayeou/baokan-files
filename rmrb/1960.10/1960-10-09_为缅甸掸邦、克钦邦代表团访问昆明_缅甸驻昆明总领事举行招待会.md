Title: Burmese Consul General in Kunming Holds Reception for Shan State and Kachin State Delegations from Burma Visiting Kunming
Author:
Date: 1960-10-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, October 6th. The Consul General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, U Maung Maung Soe, held a reception in Kunming on the evening of the 5th for Sao Shwe Kyaik, Chairman of the Shan State Parliament of the Union of Burma, U San Nyunt, Chairman of the Kachin State Parliament, and the Burmese Shan State and Kachin State friendship delegation they led, who are visiting Kunming.

Yu Yichuan, Governor of Yunnan Province, Liu Minghui, Vice Governor, Pan Shuoduan, Mayor of Kunming, Yu Futing, Vice Mayor, Zhang Zizhai, Secretary-General of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee, Wang Shaoyan and Dao Jingban, Vice Chairmen of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Lei Chunguo, Deputy Governor of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture, and Hu Zhonghua, Deputy Commissioner of the Lincang Administrative Region, were invited to attend the reception. The reception was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere.

Sao Shwe Kyaik, Chairman of the Shan State Parliament of the Union of Burma, U San Nyunt, Chairman of the Kachin State Parliament, and the Burmese Shan State and Kachin State friendship delegation they led have been visiting Kunming for several days. On the 4th, the guests visited the Kunming Machine Tool Factory and the Yunnan Institute of Physical Education. On the 5th, they visited the Kunming Medical College and the Kunming Wire Factory, where they were warmly welcomed.

### 为缅甸掸邦、克钦邦代表团访问昆明  缅甸驻昆明总领事举行招待会

1960-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    为缅甸掸邦、克钦邦代表团访问昆明
    缅甸驻昆明总领事举行招待会
    新华社昆明6日电　缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭5日晚在昆明为缅甸联邦掸邦议会主席苏辟、克钦邦议会主席吴山纽和他们率领的缅甸联邦掸邦和克钦邦友好代表团访问昆明举行了招待会。
    云南省省长于一川、副省长刘明辉，昆明市市长潘朔端、副市长于馥亭，省人民委员会秘书长张子斋，政协云南省委员会副主席王少岩、刀京版，德宏傣族景颇族自治州副州长雷春国，临沧专区副专员胡忠华等应邀出席了招待会。招待会上洋溢着热烈、友好的气氛。
    缅甸联邦掸邦议会主席苏辟、克钦邦议会主席吴山纽和他们率领的缅甸联邦掸邦和克钦邦友好代表团连日在昆明进行了参观访问。4日，客人们参观了昆明机床厂和云南体育学院。5日，他们参观了昆明医学院和昆明电线厂，受到这些单位的热情欢迎。
