Title: Two Czechoslovak Composers Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Czechoslovak Socialist Republic composers Josef Stanislav and Alexander Moyzes, who visited our country according to the 1960 implementation plan of the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement, returned to their country on the morning of the 7th after a month-long visit to China.

### 捷两位作曲家回国

1960-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    捷两位作曲家回国
    新华社7日讯　根据中捷文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问的捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国作曲家约瑟夫·斯坦尼斯拉夫和亚历山大·莫伊赛司，在我国进行了为期一个月的访问以后，已于7日晨回国。
