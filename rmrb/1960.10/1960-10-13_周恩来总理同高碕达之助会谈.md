Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Meets with Tatsunosuke Takasaki
Author:
Date: 1960-10-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th - Premier Zhou Enlai met with Tatsunosuke Takasaki, the former chief Japanese delegate to the Bandung Conference, this morning, and held talks in a friendly atmosphere.

### 周恩来总理同高碕达之助会谈

1960-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理同高碕达之助会谈
    新华社12日讯　周恩来总理今天上午会见了前出席万隆会议的日本首席代表高碕达之助，并且在友好的气氛中进行了会谈。
