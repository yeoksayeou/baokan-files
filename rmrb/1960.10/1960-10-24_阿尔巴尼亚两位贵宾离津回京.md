Title: Two Distinguished Albanian Guests Leave Tianjin for Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, October 22nd: Misto Treska, Chairman of the Albanian Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations, and Shefkat Musaraj, Secretary-General of the Albanian Writers and Artists Association, left Tianjin this evening for Beijing after a day's visit to Tianjin.
    During their stay in Tianjin, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Li Jinyu, Director of the Tianjin Municipal Bureau of Culture, visited the Art Museum, the No. 2 Workers' Sanatorium, and the Tianjin Watch Factory.

### 阿尔巴尼亚两位贵宾离津回京

1960-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚两位贵宾离津回京
    新华社天津22日电　阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会主席米斯托·特雷斯卡和阿尔巴尼亚作家艺术家协会秘书长谢夫卡特·穆萨莱，在天津访问一天以后，今晚离开天津回北京。　　贵宾们在天津期间由天津市文化局局长李霁野陪同，参观了艺术博物馆、第二工人疗养院和天津钟表制造厂等。
