Title: Head of Brazilian Cultural Delegation and Others Return Home
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-17
Page: 4
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, 16th - The head of the Brazilian Cultural Delegation, Mr. and Mrs. Beregrino, and delegation members Magronés, Dailes, and Silvera, who visited China at the invitation of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, left Beijing by plane on the 14th and 16th respectively to return to their country. Zhou Erfu, Vice President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, Tian Han, Chairman of the Chinese Dramatists Association, Hou Jingjing and Wei Junyi, directors of the Chinese Writers Association, and others were at the airport to see them off.

### 巴西文化代表团团长等回国

1960-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

    巴西文化代表团团长等回国
    新华社16日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请来我国访问的巴西文化代表团团长贝雷格里诺夫妇和团员马格隆埃斯、戴莱斯、西尔维拉，分别在14日和16日乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场送行的有中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复，中国戏剧家协会主席田汉，中国作家协会理事侯金镜、韦君宜等。
