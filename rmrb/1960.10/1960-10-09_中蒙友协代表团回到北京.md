Title: Delegation of the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 7th October. The delegation of the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association, led by Cao Diqiu, head of the delegation and vice-president of the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association, and Ji Yaotai, deputy head of the delegation and vice-president of the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association, returned to Beijing by train on the 7th, after concluding its visit to the Mongolian People's Republic to participate in the "Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Week" activities at the invitation of the Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Association. Zhang Zhixiang, president of the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association, and others welcomed them at the station. Toiv, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Mongolian People's Republic in China, also welcomed them at the station.

### 中蒙友协代表团回到北京

1960-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    中蒙友协代表团回到北京
    新华社7日讯　应蒙中友好协会邀请到蒙古人民共和国参加“蒙中友好旬”活动的中蒙友好协会代表团，结束了在蒙古的访问，在团长、中蒙友好协会副会长曹荻秋，副团长、中蒙友好协会副会长吉雅泰率领下，于7日乘火车返抵北京。到车站欢迎的，有中蒙友好协会会长张致祥等。蒙古人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办托依夫也到车站欢迎。
