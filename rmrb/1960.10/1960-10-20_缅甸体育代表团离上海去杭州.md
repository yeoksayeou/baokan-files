Title: Burmese Sports Delegation Leaves Shanghai for Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-10-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 19th - After a four-day visit to Shanghai, the Burmese Sports Delegation, accompanied by Senior Colonel Zhang Shaoting of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, left for Hangzhou this afternoon.

Senior Colonel Fang Zhongduo, Deputy Political Commissar of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zhang Zhenya, Deputy Director of the Shanghai Municipal Sports Commission, representatives from the Army, Navy, and Air Force units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai, and members of the Shanghai football team were at the station to see them off.

During their stay in Shanghai, the Burmese Sports Delegation visited the Shanghai Boiler Factory, the Hwafo Fountain Pen Factory, Printing and Dyeing Factory No. 1, the Jiangnan Paper Mill, and Minhang No. 1 Street.

### 缅甸体育代表团离上海去杭州

1960-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸体育代表团离上海去杭州
    新华社上海19日电　缅甸体育代表团在上海参观访问了四天以后，今天下午由中国人民解放军张少廷大校陪同前往杭州。
    到车站欢送的有中国人民解放军上海警备区副政治委员方中铎大校，上海市体育运动委员会副主任张振亚以及中国人民解放军驻上海陆、海、空军部队代表和上海足球队的队员等。
    缅甸体育代表团在上海期间，参观访问了上海锅炉厂、华孚金笔厂、印染一厂、江南造纸厂和闵行一号街等。
