Title: Chairman of the Indonesian Peace Committee Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. One of the chairmen of the Indonesian Peace Committee, Mr. Sirajuddin Abbas, member of the Cooperation Council, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.
	Bao Erhan, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Wang Mingyuan, Deputy Secretary-General, welcomed the guests at the airport.

### 印度尼西亚和平委员会一主席到京

1960-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚和平委员会一主席到京
    新华社3日讯　印度尼西亚和平委员会主席之一、合作议会议员西拉朱丁·阿巴斯和他的夫人应邀来我国访问，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席包尔汉、副秘书长王明远到机场欢迎客人。
