Title: Ghana Appoints Kobina Kesse as Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1960-10-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Accra, October 26th: The government of the Republic of Ghana has announced the appointment of Mr. Kobina Kesse as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Ghana to the People's Republic of China. Mr. Kobina Kesse previously served as Ghana's Ambassador to Liberia.

### 加纳任命科比纳·克西为驻华大使

1960-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    加纳任命科比纳·克西为驻华大使
    新华社26日讯　阿克拉消息：加纳共和国政府宣布，已经任命科比纳·克西先生为加纳共和国驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使。科比纳·克西先生曾任加纳驻利比里亚大使。
