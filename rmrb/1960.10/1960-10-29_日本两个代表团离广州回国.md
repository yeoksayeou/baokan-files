Title: Two Japanese Delegations Leave Guangzhou for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-29
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 27th: The nine-member delegation of the Japanese Peace Committee visiting China and the ten-member delegation of the Japanese Committee for Asian-African Solidarity visiting China, at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Chinese Committee for Asian-African Solidarity, concluded their visit to our country and left Guangzhou for home via Shenzhen today.

### 日本两个代表团离广州回国

1960-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    日本两个代表团离广州回国
    据新华社广州27日电　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国亚非团结委员会邀请，前来我国访问的日本和平委员会访华代表团一行九人和日本亚非团结委员会访华代表团一行十人，结束了在我国的访问，今天离广州经深圳回国。
