Title: Korean Government Economic Delegation Leaves Zhengzhou for Luoyang
Author:
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, October 10th. The Vice-Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Li Joo-yeon, and the Economic Delegation of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea he led, accompanied by Chen Weiji, Vice-Minister of Textile Industry, Chen Shuliang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chang Yanqing, Director of the Complete Plant Equipment Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and Li Fanru, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea, left Zhengzhou this morning to visit Luoyang.

The distinguished guests arrived in Zhengzhou yesterday from Shijiazhuang. After arriving in Zhengzhou, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Zhao Wenfu, Secretary of the Henan Provincial Party Committee Secretariat and Vice Governor of Henan Province, and Wang Lizhi, First Secretary of the Zhengzhou Municipal Party Committee, visited the state-owned Zhengzhou No. 3 Cotton Textile Factory, the state-owned Zhengzhou Textile Machinery Factory, and the Zhengzhou Red Flag People's Commune, the Dongjiao People's Commune's commune-run factories, and collective welfare facilities such as homes for the elderly and kindergartens. Wherever the distinguished guests went, they were warmly received by workers and commune members.

Yesterday evening, Zhao Wenfu, Secretary of the Henan Provincial Party Committee Secretariat and Vice Governor of Henan Province, hosted a luncheon to entertain the distinguished guests.

### 朝政府经济代表团离郑州去洛阳

1960-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    朝政府经济代表团离郑州去洛阳
    新华社郑州10日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国副首相李周渊和他率领的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府经济代表团，在纺织工业部副部长陈维稷、外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮、对外贸易部成套设备局局长常彦卿和我国驻朝鲜大使馆商务参赞李范如等人陪同下，今天上午离开郑州前往洛阳参观访问。
    贵宾们是在昨天由石家庄来到郑州的。到郑州后，贵宾们在中共河南省委书记处书记、河南省副省长赵文甫和中共郑州市委第一书记王黎之陪同下，先后参观了国营郑州第三棉纺织厂、国营郑州纺织机械厂和郑州市红旗人民公社、东郊人民公社社办工厂及敬老院、幼儿园等集体福利事业。贵宾们所到之处，都受到工人、社员群众的热情接待。
    昨天晚上，中共河南省委书记处书记、河南省副省长赵文甫设便宴招待贵宾。
