Title: Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen Receives Swedish Cultural Delegation Head Hellmanson
Author:
Date: 1960-10-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th -- Vice Premier of the State Council Nie Rongzhen received Hellmanson, the head of the Swedish cultural delegation currently visiting China, this afternoon.

When receiving the delegation, Zhou Erfu, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, was present. (Attached photo)

### 聂荣臻副总理  接见瑞典文化代表团团长海尔曼逊

1960-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

    聂荣臻副总理
    接见瑞典文化代表团团长海尔曼逊
    新华社19日讯　国务院副总理聂荣臻今天下午接见正在我国访问的瑞典文化代表团团长海尔曼逊。
    接见时，中国人民对外文化协会秘书长周而复等在座。（附图片）
