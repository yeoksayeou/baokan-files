Title: Lao Patriotic Front Newspaper Resumes Publication
Author:
Date: 1960-10-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 21st: According to a Phnom Penh radio broadcast on the evening of the 20th, the Lao Patriotic Front's newspaper, "Lao Patriotic News," began to be published again in Vientiane on the 19th, after being suspended for fifteen months.

### 老挝爱国战线党机关报复刊

1960-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国战线党机关报复刊
    新华社河内21日电　据金边电台20日晚广播，老挝爱国战线党机关报“老挝爱国报”在停刊十五个月后，19日开始在万象继续出版。
