Title: Ten African and Latin American Legal Workers Leave Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Ten legal workers from Africa and Latin America, including Adan Assi Camille, a legal worker from the Ivory Coast; Nemor, a legal worker from Algeria; Fadilou Diop, a legal worker from Senegal; Dr. Manuel Ravat, a legal worker from Peru, and his wife; Dr. Benito Marianetti, Angel Bustelo, and Dr. Juan Martin Guidi, legal workers from Argentina; Dr. Jorge Siles, a legal worker from Chile; and Dr. Zoelermo Gassi Mayano, a legal worker from Uruguay, who were invited by the Chinese Political Science and Law Association to visit China, concluded their visit to China and left Beijing for Sofia on the 7th.  They were seen off at Beijing by Wu Defeng and Zhang Zhiran, vice presidents of the Chinese Political Science and Law Association, and Ma Xiwu, vice president of the Supreme People's Court, and others.

### 十位非洲拉丁美洲法律工作者离京

1960-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    十位非洲拉丁美洲法律工作者离京
    新华社7日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的象牙海岸法律工作者阿当·阿西·卡米尔，阿尔及利亚法律工作者尼摩尔，塞内加尔法律工作者法迪卢·迪奥普，秘鲁法律工作者曼努埃尔·拉瓦特博士和夫人，阿根廷法律工作者贝尼托·马里亚内蒂博士、安赫尔·布斯特洛、胡安·马丁·吉迪博士，智利法律工作者豪尔赫·希莱斯博士，乌拉圭法律工作者左列尔摩·加西·马雅诺博士等十人，结束了在我国的访问，于7日离开北京，前往索非亚。他们离开北京时，欢送的有中国政治法律学会副会长吴德峰、张志让，最高人民法院副院长马锡五等。
