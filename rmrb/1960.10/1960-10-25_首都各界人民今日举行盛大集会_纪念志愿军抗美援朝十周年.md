Title: People from All Walks of Life in the Capital Hold Grand Rally Today to Commemorate the 10th Anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 24th. People from all walks of life in the capital will hold a grand rally on the 25th to commemorate the 10th anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' participation in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the proceedings of this meeting live on the 25th, starting at 2:50 PM, using the frequency of the first program. Beijing Television will also broadcast it simultaneously.

### 首都各界人民今日举行盛大集会  纪念志愿军抗美援朝十周年

1960-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各界人民今日举行盛大集会
    纪念志愿军抗美援朝十周年
    新华社24日讯　首都各界人民将在25日举行盛大集会，纪念中国人民志愿军抗美援朝出国作战十周年。中央人民广播电台将在25日下午两时五十分开始，用第一套节目的频率转播这次大会的实况。北京电视台也将同时转播。
