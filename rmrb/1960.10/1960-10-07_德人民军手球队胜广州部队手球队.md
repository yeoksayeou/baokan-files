Title: German People's Army Handball Team Defeats Guangzhou Military Region Handball Team
Author:
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th -- The German Democratic Republic's National People's Army Handball Team, possessing world-class skill, defeated the Guangzhou Military Region Handball Team by a score of 23-6 in their second friendly match in China, held this afternoon in Beijing.
    The German Democratic Republic's National People's Army Handball Team is about to leave Beijing for a visit and competition in Nanjing.

### 德人民军手球队胜广州部队手球队

1960-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    德人民军手球队胜广州部队手球队
    据新华社6日讯　具有世界水平的德意志民主共和国国家人民军手球队，今天下午在北京同广州部队手球队举行访华第二场友谊比赛时，以二十三比六获胜。
    德意志民主共和国国家人民军手球队即将离北京，去南京访问比赛。
