Title: Representatives from All Circles in Liaoning Province Pay Homage at the Cemetery of Volunteer Army Martyrs
Author:
Date: 1960-10-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, October 25th: Today, more than 500 representatives from all walks of life in Liaoning Province and Shenyang City paid homage at the Cemetery of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Martyrs in Beiling Martyrs' Cemetery.

Among those who participated in the memorial ceremony were Li Huang, Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and President of the Liaoning Provincial Branch of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association; Chen Xianzhou, Vice Governor of Liaoning Province; Wu Tieming, Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Zhang Jizhong, Vice Mayor of Shenyang City; Major General Tang Zian and Major General Li Boqiu, leaders of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Shenyang; Zhang Xuexuan, Vice Chairman of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Xia Xiufeng, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and heads of various people's organizations in the province and city, as well as meritorious personnel from the former Volunteer Army.

Zheng Fenggui, Consul General of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in Changchun, also made a special trip on behalf of the Korean Embassy in China to participate in the memorial ceremony.

The memorial ceremony began in mournful music. All stood in silence to pay tribute to the martyrs. Consul General Zheng Fenggui first presented wreaths from the Korean Embassy in China and the Korean Consulate General in Changchun to the front of the martyrs' tomb. Leaders of the province, city, Party, government, army and various people's organizations also presented wreaths to the martyrs' tomb one by one. Accompanied by provincial and municipal Party and government leaders, Korean Consul General Zheng Fenggui also viewed the inscriptions in front of the tombs of Chinese People's Volunteer Army martyrs Huang Jiguang, Yang Gensi, Yang Lian'di, Sun Zhanyuan, and Qiu Shaoyun, as well as the portraits displayed in the martyrs' memorial hall.

After the memorial ceremony, Zheng Fenggui met with Li Yingshou, a former Volunteer Army soldier and revolutionary disabled soldier who was awarded the First Class Warrior Honor Medal of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and Wang Erwa, the squad leader of Huang Jiguang before his death and a first-class meritorious hero of the Volunteer Army.

### 辽宁省各界代表祭奠志愿军烈士墓

1960-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    辽宁省各界代表祭奠志愿军烈士墓
    新华社沈阳25日电　今天，辽宁省和沈阳市各界人民代表五百多人，到北陵烈士陵园祭奠中国人民志愿军烈士墓。
    参加祭奠仪式的，有中共辽宁省委书记、中朝友好协会辽宁省分会会长李荒，辽宁省副省长陈先舟，中共沈阳市委书记吴铁鸣，沈阳市副市长张霁中，中国人民解放军驻沈阳部队首长唐子安少将、李伯秋少将，政协辽宁省委员会副主席张雪轩，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副秘书长夏秀峰和省、市各人民团体负责人以及前志愿军有功人员。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻长春市总领事郑凤珪，代表朝鲜驻中国大使馆也专程赶来参加祭奠仪式。
    祭奠仪式在哀乐声中开始，全体肃立，向烈士致哀。朝鲜总领事郑凤珪首先把朝鲜驻我国大使馆和朝鲜驻长春总领事馆的花圈，敬献到烈士墓前。省、市党、政、军和各人民团体等负责人也一一向烈士墓敬献花圈。在省、市党政领导人陪同下，朝鲜驻长春总领事郑凤珪还瞻仰了中国人民志愿军黄继光、杨根思、杨连第、孙占元、丘少云等烈士墓前的碑文和陈列在烈士灵堂里的遗像。
    祭奠仪式后，郑凤珪会见了荣获朝鲜民主主义人民共和国一级战士荣誉勋章的前志愿军战士、革命残废军人李应秀和黄继光烈士的生前班长、志愿军一等功臣王二娃。
