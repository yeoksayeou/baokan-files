Title: All-China Women's Federation Sends a Telegram to Support the Algerian People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1960-10-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, October 28th: The Women's International Democratic Federation sent a telegram to Mrs. Chintouf, President of the Algerian Women's Union, on the 26th, expressing solidarity and full support for the unyielding struggle of Algerian women and people for national independence.

The Women's International Democratic Federation called on women all over the world to support the just cause of the Algerian people.

### 国际民主妇女联合会致电  支持阿尔及利亚人民斗争

1960-10-31
第5版()
专栏：

    国际民主妇女联合会致电
    支持阿尔及利亚人民斗争
    新华社柏林28日电　国际民主妇女联合会26日致电阿尔及利亚妇女联盟主席申图芙夫人，表示声援和完全支持阿尔及利亚妇女和人民争取民族独立的不屈不挠的斗争。
    国际民主妇联呼吁全世界妇女支持阿尔及利亚人民的正义事业。
