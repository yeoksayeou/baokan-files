Title: My Ambassador to Mongolia Sends Letter to Chairman of the Mongolia-China Friendship Association, Thanking Him for Holding "Mongolia-China Friendship Week"
Author:
Date: 1960-10-14
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 12th. Xie Fusheng, Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, wrote a letter today to Baljiniyam, Chairman of the Mongolia-China Friendship Association, thanking the Mongolia-China Friendship Association for holding "Mongolia-China Friendship Week" on the occasion of the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.
    The letter of thanks stated: "Just as the Chinese people are joyfully celebrating the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Mongolia-China Friendship Association has held 'Mongolia-China Friendship Week' to enhance the friendship between the people of Mongolia and China, widely publicizing the great achievements made by the Chinese people under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, and the unbreakable friendship between the people of China and Mongolia. This is yet another manifestation of the boundless friendship of the fraternal Mongolian people towards the Chinese people."
    The letter of thanks concluded by wishing that the traditional friendship between the people of China and Mongolia, built on the principles of Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism, will develop and flourish forever.

### 我驻蒙大使致函蒙中友协主席  感谢举行“蒙中友好旬”

1960-10-14
第6版()
专栏：

    我驻蒙大使致函蒙中友协主席
    感谢举行“蒙中友好旬”
    新华社乌兰巴托12日电　中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生今日写信给蒙中友好协会主席巴尔吉尼雅姆，对蒙中友好协会在中华人民共和国成立十一周年之际举办“蒙中友好旬”表示感谢。
    感谢信说：“正当中国人民欢欣鼓舞地庆祝中华人民共和国建国十一周年的时候，蒙中友好协会为增进蒙中两国人民的友谊举行了
  ‘蒙中友好旬’，广泛地宣传了中国人民在中国共产党和毛泽东主席领导下取得的伟大成就和中蒙两国人民之间的牢不可破的友谊，这是兄弟般的蒙古人民对中国人民无限友好的又一次表现”。
    感谢信最后祝建立在马克思列宁主义原则和无产阶级国际主义基础上的中蒙两国人民的传统友谊日益发展，万古长青。
