Title: Japanese Journalists Delegation Leaves Beijing for Austria
Author:
Date: 1960-10-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 14th. The Japanese journalists delegation of twelve people, led by Yuichi Kobayashi, Chairman of the Japan Congress of Journalists, left Beijing by plane today after a visit to our country to attend the Second World Journalists Meeting in Austria. Wang Yunsheng, Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, and people from the capital's press circles went to the airport to see them off.

### 日本新闻工作者代表团离北京去奥地利

1960-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

    日本新闻工作者代表团离北京去奥地利
    新华社14日讯　以日本记者会议议长小林雄一为首的日本新闻工作者代表团一行十二人，在我国参观访问后，今天乘飞机离开北京，前往奥地利参加第二次世界记者会见。到机场送行的有中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席王芸生以及首都新闻界人士。
