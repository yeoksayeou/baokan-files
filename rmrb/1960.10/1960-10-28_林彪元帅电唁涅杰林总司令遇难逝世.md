Title: Marshal Lin Biao Sends Condolences on the Death of Commander-in-Chief Nedelin

Author:

Date: 1960-10-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th. Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, Marshal Lin Biao, sent a telegram on the 26th to the Minister of National Defense of the Soviet Union, Marshal Malinovsky, to express condolences on the unfortunate death of Deputy Minister of National Defense of the Soviet Union and Commander-in-Chief of the Soviet Rocket Forces, Chief Marshal of Artillery Nedelin. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To Comrade Marshal R. Ya. Malinovsky, Minister of National Defense of the Soviet Union:

I was shocked to learn that Comrade Chief Marshal of Artillery M. I. Nedelin, Deputy Minister of National Defense of the Soviet Union and Commander-in-Chief of the Soviet Rocket Forces, unfortunately died in an aviation accident. The death of Comrade Nedelin is a great loss to the Soviet Armed Forces. I would like to express my deep condolences to you and all officers and soldiers of the Soviet Armed Forces. Please convey my deepest condolences to the family of Comrade Nedelin.

Lin Biao
Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China
Marshal of the People's Republic of China
October 26, 1960, in Beijing

### 林彪元帅电唁涅杰林总司令遇难逝世

1960-10-28
第4版()
专栏：

    林彪元帅电唁涅杰林总司令遇难逝世
    新华社27日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪元帅26日打电报给苏联国防部长马利诺夫斯基元帅，电唁苏联国防部副部长、苏联火箭部队总司令涅杰林炮兵主帅不幸遇难逝世。唁电全文如下：苏联国防部长罗·雅·马利诺夫斯基元帅同志：
    惊悉苏联国防部副部长、苏联火箭部队总司令米·伊·涅杰林炮兵主帅同志由于航空事故不幸牺牲。涅杰林同志的牺牲是苏联武装部队的重大损失，谨向您和苏联武装部队全体官兵致以沉痛的哀悼。并请向涅杰林同志的家属转达我的深切慰问。中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长中华人民共和国元帅林　彪1960年10月26日于北京
