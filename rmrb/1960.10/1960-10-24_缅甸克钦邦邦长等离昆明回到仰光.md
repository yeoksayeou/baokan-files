Title: Kachin State Leader of Burma and Others Return to Rangoon from Kunming
Author:
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 22nd - Duwa Sinwa Nawng, the Kachin State Leader of Burma and Minister of Kachin State Affairs of the Federal Government, Soe Htun Aye, Minister of Education and Health of the Shan State Government, and Daw Than Than Nu, daughter of Prime Minister U Nu, and nine others, returned to Rangoon from Kunming by special plane this afternoon. They had accompanied Prime Minister U Nu to attend the 11th anniversary celebrations of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the signing ceremony of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included U Ba Maw, Director of Protocol of the Burmese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, representatives of Kachin State, and Lei Yang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma.

### 缅甸克钦邦邦长等离昆明回到仰光

1960-10-24
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸克钦邦邦长等离昆明回到仰光
    新华社仰光22日电　缅甸克钦邦邦长、联邦政府克钦邦事务部长杜瓦信瓦瑙，掸邦政府教育和卫生部长苏吞埃以及吴努总理的女儿杜珊珊努等十二人，今天下午乘专机从昆明回到仰光。他们曾经随同吴努总理参加了中华人民共和国建国十一周年庆典和中缅边界条约的签字仪式。
    前往机场欢迎他们的有缅甸外交部礼宾司司长吴巴茂和克钦邦的代表以及中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬。
