Title: Chinese Legal Workers Delegation Leaves for Sofia to Attend the 7th Congress of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers
Author:
Date: 1960-10-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd. A Chinese legal workers delegation of thirteen people, led by the head of the delegation Zhang Youyu and the deputy head Ke Bonian, left Beijing by plane today for Bulgaria to attend the 7th Congress of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, which will be held in Sofia, the capital of Bulgaria, on October 10. Members of the delegation include: Li Guangdou, Han Youtong, Liu Jingxi, Yang Huanan, Sun Yaming, and Wang Liang.

Wu Defeng and Zhang Zhiran, vice presidents of the China Political Science and Law Association, Ma Xiwu, director and vice president of the Supreme People's Court, Wu Xinyu, director and deputy secretary-general of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Huang Huoxing, deputy procurator-general of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, saw them off at the airport.

### 出席国际民主法律工作者第七届大会  我国法律工作者代表团赴索非亚

1960-10-06
第5版()
专栏：

    出席国际民主法律工作者第七届大会
    我国法律工作者代表团赴索非亚
    新华社3日讯　出席10月10日在保加利亚首都索非亚召开的国际民主法律工作者第七届大会的中国法律工作者代表团一行十三人，在团长张友渔、副团长柯柏年率领下，今天乘飞机离开北京去保加利亚。代表团团员有：李光斗、韩幽桐、刘镜西、杨化南、孙亚明和王良。
    中国政治法律学会副会长吴德峰、张志让，理事、最高人民法院副院长马锡五，理事、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长武新宇，最高人民检察院副检察长黄火星等到机场送行。
