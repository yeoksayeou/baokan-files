Title: Cuba Captures Fugitive American Bandits
Author:
Date: 1960-10-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, October 15th: Cuban newspapers reported today that peasant militia forces led by the Cuban Revolutionary Army have captured four fugitives from the armed counter-revolutionary group that landed in Oriente Province, eastern Cuba, on the 5th of this month after departing from the United States.

The captured individuals are: Americans Alan Thompson, Robert Fuller, and two former soldiers of the Batista dictatorship. Robert Fuller is the son of a foreign plantation owner who once owned the Lewiston estate near San German in Oriente Province.

They will be tried by a military tribunal in Santiago today.

### 古巴捕获漏网的美国匪徒

1960-10-17
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴捕获漏网的美国匪徒
    新华社哈瓦那15日电　据古巴各报今天报道，在古巴起义军率领下的农民民兵部队已经俘获本月5日由美国出发到古巴东部奥连特省登陆的武装反革命集团中的四名漏网者。
    被俘者是：美国人阿兰·汤普森，罗伯特·富勒和巴蒂斯塔独裁政权的二名前军人。罗伯特·富勒是曾经拥有奥连特省圣赫尔曼附近刘易斯顿庄园的外国大庄园主的儿子。
    他们今天将在圣地亚哥受到军事法庭的审判。
