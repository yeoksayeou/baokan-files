Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation Visits Cuba
Author:
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. A five-member Chinese trade union delegation, led by Kang Yonghe, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, departed from Beijing for Havana on the 9th at the invitation of the Cuban Workers' Federation and other trade union organizations for a friendly visit to Cuba. The delegation will also attend the third meeting of the International Committee for Support of Algerian Workers and People in Cuba.

### 中国工会代表团前往古巴访问

1960-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    中国工会代表团前往古巴访问
    新华社10日讯　应古巴工人联合会等工会组织的邀请，前往古巴进行友好访问的中国工会代表团一行五人，在中华全国总工会书记处书记康永和率领下，9日从北京启程前往哈瓦那。代表团还将在古巴参加国际支援阿尔及利亚工人及人民委员会第三次会议。
