Title: Iraqi Ambassador to China Holds Reception for Visit of Iraqi Military Friendship Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1960-10-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: Iraqi Ambassador to China, Fadil, held a reception this afternoon for the visit to our country by the Iraqi Military Friendship Delegation headed by Brigadier General Mahmoud Abdul Razzaq.

General Zhang Aiping, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, was invited to attend the reception.

Ambassador Fadil and General Zhang Aiping spoke at the reception one after the other.

At the reception, the hosts and guests repeatedly toasted the friendship between the peoples and armies of China and Iraq, and the friendly unity of peace-loving countries and peoples around the world.

Also invited to the reception were General Xiao Hua, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Vice Admiral Zhao Qimin, Lieutenant General Chang Qiankun, Lieutenant General Zhang Xianyue, Lieutenant General Liu Jinxuan and other senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Also invited to the reception were Burhan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Li Dequan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, Luo Jun, Vice President of the China-Iraq Friendship Association, He Ying, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

Heads of diplomatic missions, military attachés and diplomatic officials of various countries to China were also invited to the reception.

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: The Iraqi Military Friendship Delegation, headed by Brigadier General Mahmoud Abdul Razzaq, returned to Beijing today by special plane, accompanied by Chang Qiankun, Lieutenant General of the Chinese Air Force, representative of the Ministry of National Defense, and Xu Yimin, Colonel, Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Iraq, after a three-day friendly visit to Shanghai.

Those who greeted them at the airport were Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense, Zhao Yiping, Rear Admiral of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, He Tingyi, Air Force Major General, and Ye Xiuzhi, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense, Senior Colonel, and others.

Jaff, First Secretary of the Iraqi Embassy in China, also greeted them at the airport.

During their visit to Shanghai, the distinguished guests visited the Shanghai Industrial Exhibition, Jiangnan Shipyard, etc., visited the naval units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and, accompanied by Vice Admiral Tao Yong, toured the Huangpu River on warships.

### 为伊拉克军事友好代表团访问中国  伊拉克驻华大使举行招待会

1960-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    为伊拉克军事友好代表团访问中国
    伊拉克驻华大使举行招待会
    新华社9日讯　伊拉克共和国驻中国大使法迪尔，今天下午为以马哈茂德·阿卜杜勒·拉扎克准将为首的伊拉克军事友好代表团访问我国，举行招待会。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长张爱萍上将应邀出席了招待会。
    法迪尔大使和张爱萍上将在招待会上先后讲话。
    在招待会上，宾主频频为中、伊两国人民和军队的友谊，为全世界爱好和平的国家和人民的友好团结干杯。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有萧华上将、萧向荣中将、赵启民海军中将、常乾坤空军中将、张贤约中将、刘金轩中将等中国人民解放军高级将领。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉、对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若、中国亚非团结委员会副主席李德全、中国伊拉克友好协会副会长罗俊、外交部西亚非洲司司长何英等。
    各国驻华使节、大使馆武官和外交官员也应邀出席了招待会。
    新华社9日讯　以马哈茂德·阿卜杜勒·拉扎克准将为首的伊拉克军事友好代表团，在上海进行了三天友好访问后，由我国国防部代表常乾坤空军中将和我国驻伊拉克大使馆武官徐毅民上校陪同，今天乘专机回到北京。
    到机场迎接的，有我国国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将和中国人民解放军赵一萍海军少将、何廷一空军少将、国防部外事处副处长叶修直大校等。
    伊拉克共和国驻华大使馆一等秘书贾夫也到机场迎接。
    贵宾们在上海访问期间，曾参观了上海市工业展览会、江南造船厂等，访问了中国人民解放军海军部队，并在陶勇海军中将陪同下，乘军舰游览黄浦江。
