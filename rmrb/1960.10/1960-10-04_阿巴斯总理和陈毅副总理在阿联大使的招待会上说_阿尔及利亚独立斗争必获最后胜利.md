Title: Premier Abbas and Vice Premier Chen Yi said at the reception hosted by the UAE Ambassador: The Algerian struggle for independence will surely win the final victory.
Author:
Date: 1960-10-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Abbas Ferhat, Premier of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, representative of the heroic Algerian people, said today that although France has the support of NATO and the United States, the Algerian people will not yield, and they will continue to struggle until they gain independence. He also said that the Chinese people will always stand by us until we win the final victory.
    Premier Abbas made these remarks at a reception hosted by the United Arab Republic Ambassador to China, Tarazi, tonight, in honor of Premier Abbas' visit to China. Premier Abbas' words were warmly welcomed by Chinese and foreign guests attending the reception.
    Vice Premier Chen Yi was invited to attend the reception.
    After Ambassador Tarazi spoke, Premier Abbas spoke. Premier Abbas said with full confidence that the Algerian people's struggle will be victorious. We are victorious not because we have more people and better equipment, but because our cause is just.
    Premier Abbas said that the just struggle of the Algerian people has won the widespread sympathy and support of the people of all countries in the world. That is why we are able to visit China today as guests of the great Chinese people. Premier Abbas spoke of the Chinese people's support for them, saying that the Chinese people have had the same struggle as we are having today, and the Chinese people understand the significance of our struggle. The Chinese people will always stand by us until we win the final victory. Premier Abbas once again emphasized that no matter whether the Algerian war is long-term or stops tomorrow, the victory of the Algerian people is sure to come.
    In his speech, Premier Abbas also expressed his gratitude to Ambassador Tarazi for his reception and to the people of the United Arab Republic for their support.
    Vice Premier Chen Yi spoke, expressing his great pleasure in attending the reception.
    Vice Premier Chen Yi said that the heroic struggle of the Algerian people for national independence has won the sympathy and respect of the people of all countries in the world. Vice Premier Chen Yi said that the Chinese people have always unswervingly stood together with the people of socialist countries, the Arab people, the Asian and African people, and all peace-loving and justice-upholding people in the world, and firmly support the just struggle of the Algerian people. He said that we firmly believe that as long as the Algerian people further strengthen their unity and persist in the struggle, the final victory will surely belong to the great Algerian people.
    Ambassador Tarazi spoke at the beginning of the reception. He said that the reception was held to welcome a delegation from a country whose people refused to yield and demanded to take up arms to defend their just cause.
    The Ambassador said that the Algerian people have held high the banner of freedom and have been resolutely and bravely fighting their enemies. The Arab people are proud of the great victories achieved by their Algerian brothers in their struggle.
    The Ambassador said that the Algerian people's struggle for liberation has shocked the world and has been supported by peace-loving people of all countries in the world, and by the people of Asian and African countries, especially the Chinese people. The Ambassador warmly praised the friendship between the Chinese and Arab people, pointing out that the Chinese people's support for the Algerian people will be recorded in history as a chapter in the friendly relations between the Chinese and Arab people.
    All the above speeches were warmly welcomed.
    Also invited to attend the reception were Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of our country, General Zhang Aiping, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee, Liu Changsheng, President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, Bao Erhan, Director of the China Islamic Association, and people from all aspects of China.
    Envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China attended the reception.

### 阿巴斯总理和陈毅副总理在阿联大使的招待会上说  阿尔及利亚独立斗争必获最后胜利

1960-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    阿巴斯总理和陈毅副总理在阿联大使的招待会上说
    阿尔及利亚独立斗争必获最后胜利
    新华社3日讯　英雄的阿尔及利亚人民的代表、阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理阿巴斯·费尔哈特今天说，虽然法国有北大西洋公约组织和美国的支持，但是阿尔及利亚人民并不屈服，他们要继续斗争，直到获得独立为止。他还说，中国人民将永远站在我们这一边，一直到我们获得最后胜利。
    阿巴斯总理的这番话，是他在阿拉伯联合共和国驻中国大使塔拉齐今晚为阿巴斯总理访问中国而举行的招待会上讲的。阿巴斯总理的这些话受到出席招待会的中外人士的热烈欢迎。
    陈毅副总理应邀出席了招待会。
    在塔拉齐大使讲话以后，阿巴斯总理讲了话，阿巴斯总理满怀信心地说，阿尔及利亚人民的斗争将会取得胜利，我们胜利不是因为我们人多装备好，而是因为我们的事业是正义的。
    阿巴斯总理说，阿尔及利亚人民的正义斗争获得了世界各国人民的广泛同情和支持，正因为如此，我们今天才能作为伟大的中国人民的客人来到中国访问。阿巴斯总理讲到中国人民对他们的支持时说，中国人民曾经有过和我们今天进行的同样的斗争，中国人民了解我们斗争的意义。中国人民将永远站在我们这一边，一直到我们获得最后胜利。阿巴斯总理再一次强调指出：不管阿尔及利亚战争的时间是长期的，或是明天停止，但是阿尔及利亚人民的胜利是肯定要到来的。
    阿巴斯总理在讲话中还表示感谢塔拉齐大使的招待，感谢阿拉伯联合共和国人民的支持。
    陈毅副总理讲话表示十分高兴来参加这个招待会。
    陈毅副总理说，阿尔及利亚人民争取民族独立的英勇斗争，赢得了世界各国人民的同情和尊敬。陈毅副总理说，中国人民始终不渝地同社会主义各国人民、阿拉伯人民和亚非人民以及全世界爱好和平和主持正义的人民站在一起，坚决支持阿尔及利亚人民的正义斗争，他说，我们深信，只要阿尔及利亚人民进一步加强团结，坚持斗争，最后的胜利一定属于伟大的阿尔及利亚人民。
    塔拉齐大使在招待会开始时讲话。他说，这个招待会是为欢迎一个国家的代表团而举行的。这个国家的人民拒绝屈服，要求拿着武器，保卫他们的正义事业。
    大使说，阿尔及利亚人民高举自由的旗帜，一直坚决英勇地同他们的敌人进行斗争。阿拉伯人民为阿尔及利亚兄弟在斗争中所取得的巨大胜利而感到骄傲。
    大使说，阿尔及利亚人民争取解放的斗争震撼世界，得到了世界各国爱好和平的人民的支持，得到了亚非各国人民特别是中国人民的支持。大使热情称颂中国人民和阿拉伯人民的友谊，指出中国人民对阿尔及利亚人民的支持，将作为中国人民和阿拉伯人民友好关系的一章，载入史册。
    上述所有讲话，都受到了热烈的欢迎。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有我国外交部副部长章汉夫，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张爱萍上将，对外贸易部副部长李强，中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志，中国非洲人民友好协会会长刘长胜，中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉，以及中国各方面的人士。
    各国驻中国的使节和外交官员，出席了招待会。
