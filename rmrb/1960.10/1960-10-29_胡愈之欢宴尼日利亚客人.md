Title: Hu Yuzhi Hosts Banquet for Nigerian Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-10-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th: Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the six Nigerian guests headed by Malam Tanko Yakasai, National Organizer of the Northern Elements Progressive Union of Nigeria. Among those present at the banquet were Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity and President of the China Islamic Association, Zhang Tiesheng, Vice President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, as well as Wu Maosun, Wu Xiaoda, Wu Qing, Hu Gang, and others.

### 胡愈之欢宴尼日利亚客人

1960-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    胡愈之欢宴尼日利亚客人
    新华社28日讯　中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之今晚设宴欢迎以尼日利亚北方进步分子联盟全国组织者马兰·坦科·亚卡赛为首的六位尼日利亚客人。出席宴会的有中国亚非团结委员会副主席、中国伊斯兰教协会会长包尔汉，中国非洲人民友好协会副会长张铁生，以及吴茂荪、吴晓达、吴青、胡刚等。
