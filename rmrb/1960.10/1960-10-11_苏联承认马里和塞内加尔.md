Title: The Soviet Union Recognizes Mali and Senegal
Author:
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 10th: The Soviet Union has announced its recognition of the Republic of Mali and the Republic of Senegal and is prepared to establish diplomatic relations with them, according to TASS, Moscow, October 10th.

This was announced by Chairman Khrushchev of the Soviet Council of Ministers in telegrams to Prime Minister Modibo Keita of the Republic of Mali and Prime Minister Mamadou Dia of the Republic of Senegal.

### 苏联承认马里和塞内加尔

1960-10-11
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联承认马里和塞内加尔
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科10日讯，苏联已经宣布承认马里共和国和塞内加尔共和国，并准备同它们建立外交关系。
    这是苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫在打给马里共和国总理莫迪博·凯塔和塞内加尔共和国总理马马杜·迪亚的电报中宣布的。
