Title: Moroccan Kingdom Cultural Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: The four-member cultural delegation from the Kingdom of Morocco, led by its head, Mohammed Aziz Lahbabi, which visited China at the invitation of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, left Beijing by plane today to return home. Qu Wu, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Liu Ziyu, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Education, were at the airport to see them off. During their stay in China, the delegation visited and inspected industrial and agricultural and cultural and educational construction in Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places. On the evening of the 19th, Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a farewell banquet for them.

### 摩洛哥王国文化代表团回国

1960-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥王国文化代表团回国
    新华社22日讯　应对外文化联络委员会邀请来我国访问的摩洛哥王国文化代表团一行四人，在团长穆罕默德·阿齐兹·拉赫巴比率领下，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。对外文委副主任屈武、教育部办公厅副主任刘子余等到机场欢送。　　代表团在中国期间，参观访问了北京、南京、上海、杭州等地的工农业及文教建设。19日晚，对外文委主任张奚若曾设宴为他们饯行。
