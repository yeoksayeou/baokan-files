Title: Chairman Mao Receives Cuban Guest Rodríguez
Author:
Date: 1960-10-31
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, October 30th: Chairman Mao Zedong met this evening with José Manuel Valdés Rodríguez, contributor to the Cuban newspaper "World", Director of the Film Department of the University of Havana, and a prominent film and drama critic, who is currently visiting our country. Present at the meeting were Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi and Wang Yi, First Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Journalists Association. (Attached image)
    Chairman Mao receives José Manuel Valdés Rodríguez. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Hou Bo

### 毛主席接见古巴外宾罗德里格斯

1960-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见古巴外宾罗德里格斯
    新华社30日讯　毛泽东主席今晚接见了在我国访问的古巴世界报撰稿人、古巴哈瓦那大学电影系主任、著名电影戏剧评论家何塞·曼奴埃尔·瓦尔德斯·罗德里格斯。接见时在座的有国务院副总理陈毅，中华全国新闻工作者协会书记处第一书记王揖。（附图片）
