Title: Mexican, Costa Rican Editors Leave Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 10th: Mexican "Eternal" magazine editor José Rosales and former editor-in-chief of the Costa Rican newspaper "La Republica" and member of the Costa Rican National Unity Committee, Francisco Gamboa, concluded their visit to our country and left Beijing by plane today.

### 墨西哥、哥斯达黎加  两位编辑离京

1960-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    墨西哥、哥斯达黎加
    两位编辑离京
    新华社10日讯　墨西哥“永恒”杂志编辑何塞·罗萨莱斯和哥斯达黎加前“共和报”主编、哥斯达黎加全国团结委员会委员弗朗西斯科·干波亚结束在我国的访问后，今天乘飞机离开北京。
