Title: Soviet Ambassador Presents Credentials to King of Laos
Author:
Date: 1960-10-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 28th - According to a broadcast from Vientiane Radio, the Soviet Ambassador to Laos, Abramov, presented his credentials to the King of Laos, Sisavang Vatthana, at the Royal Palace in Luang Prabang on the morning of the 26th.

On the 27th, Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma held a banquet in Vientiane to entertain Soviet Ambassador Abramov. Government officials and foreign diplomats stationed in Laos attended the banquet.

### 苏联大使向老挝国王递交国书

1960-10-30
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联大使向老挝国王递交国书
    新华社河内28日电　据万象电台广播，苏联驻老挝大使阿布拉莫夫26日上午在琅勃拉邦王宫向老挝国王西萨旺·瓦达纳递交了国书。
    27日，梭发那·富马首相在万象举行宴会，招待苏联大使阿布拉莫夫。王国政府官员和各国驻老挝外交官员参加了宴会。
