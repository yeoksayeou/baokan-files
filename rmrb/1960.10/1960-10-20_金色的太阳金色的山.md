Golden Sun, Golden Mountain [Tibetan Folk Song]
Zhao Zongzao Woodcut

    Golden sun, golden mountain,
    The Communist Party's light shines in our hearts,
    Chairman Mao led us to liberation,
    The Tibetan people smile with joy,
    We raise a cup of barley wine with both hands,
    May the flowers of happiness bloom for ten thousand years! (Image attached)

### 金色的太阳金色的山
赵宗藻
1960-10-20
第8版()
专栏：

    金色的太阳金色的山〔藏族民歌〕
    赵宗藻　木刻
    金色的太阳金色的山，
    共产党的光芒照心间，
    毛主席领导我们得解放，
    藏族人民笑开颜，
    双手举起一杯青稞酒，
    愿幸福花儿红万年。（附图片）
