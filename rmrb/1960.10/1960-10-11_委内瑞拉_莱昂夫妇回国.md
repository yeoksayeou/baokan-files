Title: Venezuelan León couple return home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 4

    Venezuela
    León couple return home
    Xinhua News Agency, 10th—Venezuelan Senator, World Peace Council member, and Executive Committee member of the Venezuelan China Friendship Association, Carlos Augusto León, and his wife, who came to our country for a visit at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.
    Those who saw them off at the airport included Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General, Wang Puqing, Director of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, and Yan Wenjing, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Writers Association.

### 委内瑞拉  莱昂夫妇回国

1960-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    委内瑞拉
    莱昂夫妇回国
    新华社10日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的委内瑞拉参议员、世界和平理事会理事、委内瑞拉中国之友协会执委会委员卡洛斯·奥古斯托·莱昂和他的夫人，今天早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之、副秘书长吴晓达，中国拉丁美洲友好协会理事王普庆，中国作家协会书记处书记严文井等人。
