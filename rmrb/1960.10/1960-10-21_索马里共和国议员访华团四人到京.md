Title: Somali Republic Parliamentary Delegation of Four Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th -- The Somali Republic Parliamentary Delegation of four members, invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They are: Mohamed Giano Giifumaale, Secretary of the Somali Republic Parliament, and Members of Parliament Dahir Noor Egal, Sogoole Mohamed Mohamed, and Noor Mohamed Hussein Osman.

Those who welcomed them at the airport were: Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Lian Guan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhang Jie, Standing Director of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association and Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association; and Liu Kefan, Deputy Head of the Chinese Muslim Hajj Delegation who had visited the Somali Republic.

### 索马里共和国议员访华团四人到京

1960-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    索马里共和国议员访华团四人到京
    新华社20日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的索马里共和国议员访华团成员共四人，今天乘飞机到达北京。他们是：索马里共和国议会秘书穆罕默德·吉雅纳戈·吉符马勒，议员达希尔·努尔·埃加勒、索戈勒·穆罕默德·穆罕默德、努尔·穆罕默德·侯赛因·欧斯曼。
    到机场欢迎的有：中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长连贯，中国非洲人民友好协会常务理事、中国伊斯兰教协会副主任张杰，曾去索马里共和国访问的中国穆斯林朝觐团副团长刘克凡。
