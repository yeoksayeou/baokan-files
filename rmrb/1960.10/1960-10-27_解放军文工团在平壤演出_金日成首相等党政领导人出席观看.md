Title: PLA Performing Arts Troupe Performs in Pyongyang; Premier Kim Il-sung and Other Party and Government Leaders Attend and Watch

Author:
Date: 1960-10-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th—The Chinese People's Liberation Army Performing Arts Troupe, led by the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to Korea, gave its first performance at the Pyongyang Grand Theatre tonight.

    Premier Kim Il-sung watched the performance. Also in attendance were Korean party, government, and military leaders including Choi Yong-kun, Kim Il, Hong Myung-hee, Park Jung-ae, Park Kum-chul, Kim Chang-man, Chung Il-ryong, Nam Il, Kim Kwang-hyup, cabinet ministers, chairmen of the Presidium of the Fatherland Front, heads of social organizations, representatives from all walks of life, and senior generals of the Korean People's Army. The entire Chinese Military Friendship Delegation led by Marshal He Long and General Luo Ruiqing, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea Qiao Xiaoguang, Military Attaché Colonel Zhang Leting, diplomatic officials of the embassy, and Chinese experts in Korea also watched the performance. More than two thousand people from Pyongyang watched the performance.

    The Performing Arts Troupe performed many musical and dance programs from both China and Korea tonight, earning warm applause from the audience. Some programs were performed again and again at the audience's request. When the Performing Arts Troupe performed the dance "Meeting on the Heights," created by the Korean People's Army Ensemble and depicting the joint combat of the Chinese and Korean armies, the entire audience applauded continuously.

    After the performance, Premier Kim Il-sung, Chairman Choi Yong-kun, and other Korean party and government leaders went on stage to present flower baskets and warmly shook hands with the performers.

### 解放军文工团在平壤演出  金日成首相等党政领导人出席观看

1960-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军文工团在平壤演出
    金日成首相等党政领导人出席观看
    新华社平壤26日电　由中华人民共和国访朝军事友好代表团率领的中国人民解放军文艺工作团，今晚在平壤大剧场举行第一次演出。
    金日成首相观看了演出。观看演出的还有崔庸健、金一、洪命熹、朴正爱、朴金喆、金昌满、郑一龙、南日、金光侠等朝鲜党政军领导人，内阁各相，祖国战线议长团的议长们，社会团体负责人，各界代表以及朝鲜人民军高级将领。由贺龙元帅、罗瑞卿大将率领的中国军事友好代表团全体成员、中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光、武官张乐亭上校、大使馆外交官员、中国在朝鲜的专家也观看了演出。平壤市二千多人民群众观看了演出。
    文艺工作团今晚演出了许多中朝两国的音乐舞蹈节目，博得了观众的热烈掌声。其中有不少节目在观众的要求下演了一遍又一遍。文艺工作团在表演朝鲜人民军协奏团创作的、表现中朝两国军队并肩作战的舞蹈“高地相逢”时，全场掌声不断。
    演出后，金日成首相、崔庸健委员长和其他朝鲜党政领导人登台赠了花篮，并同演员亲切地握手。
