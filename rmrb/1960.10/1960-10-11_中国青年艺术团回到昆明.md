Title: Chinese Youth Art Troupe Returns to Kunming
Author:
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 9th. The Chinese Youth Art Troupe, headed by Wen Fei, has recently returned to Kunming after completing its visit and performance tour of Iraq, Afghanistan, and Nepal.

The Chinese Youth Art Troupe departed for its visit and performance tour on July 6th. During the three-month visit and performance tour, they performed twenty-seven shows in Iraq, Afghanistan, and Nepal, and received warm welcome and praise from the people of these countries.

### 中国青年艺术团回到昆明

1960-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    中国青年艺术团回到昆明
    新华社昆明9日电　以文菲为首的中国青年艺术团，在结束了对伊拉克、阿富汗和尼泊尔的访问演出以后，已经在最近回到昆明。
    中国青年艺术团是在7月6日出国访问演出的。他们在为期三个月的访问演出中，曾先后在伊拉克、阿富汗、尼泊尔演出二十七场，受到了各国人民的热烈欢迎和赞扬。
