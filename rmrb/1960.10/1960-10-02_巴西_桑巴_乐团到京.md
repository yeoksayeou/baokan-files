Title: Brazilian "Samba" Band Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-02
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, October 1st. At the invitation of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, the Brazilian "Samba" Band, a group of twelve led by head Victor Simon, arrived in Beijing this afternoon for a performance tour of our country. Qu Wu, director of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and many people from the capital's literary and art circles went to the airport to welcome them.

### 巴西“桑巴”乐团到京

1960-10-02
第7版()
专栏：

    巴西“桑巴”乐团到京
    新华社1日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会的邀请，前来我国访问演出的巴西“桑巴”乐团一行十二人，在团长卫克多·西蒙率领下，于今日下午抵北京。前往机场欢迎的，有对外文协理事屈武和首都文艺界人士多人。
