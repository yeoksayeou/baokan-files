Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Holds Talks with Prime Minister Abbas
Author:
Date: 1960-10-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, October 1st: Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai held talks with Prime Minister of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, Abbas Ferhat, this afternoon. The talks proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

	On the Algerian side, those participating in the talks included Minister of Interior Ben Tobbal Akhdar, Director of the Prime Minister's Office Ben Yahia Mohammed, Director of Political Affairs of the Propaganda Department Boumendjel Ahmed, and Director of Political Affairs of the Ministry of Interior Hane Lamine.

	On the Chinese side, those participating in the talks included Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Zhang Aiping, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, and Director of the Department of West Asian and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs He Ying. (Attached photo)

### 周总理同阿巴斯总理会谈

1960-10-02
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理同阿巴斯总理会谈
    新华社1日讯　国务院总理周恩来同阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理阿巴斯·费尔哈特，今天下午举行了会谈。会谈在友好的气氛中进行。
    阿尔及利亚方面参加会谈的，有内政部长本·陶巴勒·阿赫达尔，总理办公室主任本·雅希亚·穆罕默德，宣传部政治事务主任布门杰勒·艾哈迈德，内政部政治事务主任哈恩·拉明。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张爱萍上将，对外贸易部副部长李强，外交部西亚非洲司司长何英。（附图片）
