Title: Korean Broadcasting and Television Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: A two-person Korean Broadcasting and Television Delegation arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to China in accordance with the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Agreement. The head of the delegation is Kim Yong-hwan, Vice Chairman of the Korean Central Broadcasting Committee.

Li Wu and Dong Lin, Deputy Directors of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau, and others were at the airport to welcome them.

### 朝鲜广播电视代表团到京

1960-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜广播电视代表团到京
    新华社22日讯　根据中朝文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问的朝鲜广播电视代表团一行二人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团团长是朝鲜中央广播委员会副委员长金容焕。
    到机场欢迎的有广播事业局副局长李伍、董林等。
