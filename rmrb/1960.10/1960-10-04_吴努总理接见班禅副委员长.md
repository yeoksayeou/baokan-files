Title: Prime Minister U Nu Receives Vice Chairman Panchen

Author:
Date: 1960-10-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, received Panchen Erdeni, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, at nine o'clock this morning. The host and guest held cordial and friendly talks. Present at the reception on the Burmese side were U Nu's advisor U Wang, Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Ba Lin Dun, and Burmese Ambassador to China Jiao Wen. On the Chinese side was Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 吴努总理接见班禅副委员长

1960-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    吴努总理接见班禅副委员长
    新华社3日讯　缅甸联邦总理吴努，今天上午九时接见了全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长班禅额尔德尼，宾主进行了亲切友好的交谈。接见时在座的，缅甸方面有吴努总理的顾问吴旺、外交部常任秘书巴林顿、缅甸驻华大使叫温。中国方面有外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文。
