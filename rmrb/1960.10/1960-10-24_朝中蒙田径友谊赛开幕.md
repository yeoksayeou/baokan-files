Title: North Korea-China-Mongolia Track and Field Friendship Competition Opens
Author:
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 23rd. The North Korea-China-Mongolia Track and Field Friendship Competition opened this afternoon at the Moranbong Stadium in Pyongyang and held its first day of competition. North Korean party and government leaders including Vice Premier of the Cabinet Hong Myong-hui and Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party Park Kim-chul attended today's opening ceremony.

    In the first day of competition, the Chinese track and field team participated in a total of eleven events, winning championships in ten of them.

    The Korean team also achieved several victories in today's competition.

### 朝中蒙田径友谊赛开幕

1960-10-24
第5版()
专栏：

    朝中蒙田径友谊赛开幕
    新华社平壤23日电　朝、中、蒙三国田径友谊比赛今天下午在平壤市牡丹峰运动场开幕，并举行第一天比赛。朝鲜内阁副首相洪命熹和朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴金喆等朝鲜党政领导人出席了今天的开幕式。
    在第一天的比赛中，中国田径队共参加了十一个项目，其中获得了十个项目的冠军。
    朝鲜队在今天的比赛中也取得了多项的胜利。
