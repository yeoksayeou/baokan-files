Title: Korean People's Army Ensemble Performs in Beijing; Honored Guests Visit Beijing Dance Academy and Receive Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1960-10-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 29th - In Beijing, filled with the warm atmosphere of profound friendship between the Chinese and Korean peoples, the Korean People's Army Ensemble performed for the people of Beijing at the Tianqiao Theater tonight, receiving a warm welcome from all circles of Beijing.

　　Tonight, the Korean People's Army Ensemble performed seventeen song and dance numbers, each of which was warmly welcomed by the audience. Many performances were repeated several times at the audience's enthusiastic request, with curtain calls reaching four or five times.

　　When the news spread that the Korean People's Army Ensemble would be performing in Beijing, the broad masses of Beijing people, filled with boundless joy, vied to watch the artistic performances of the Korean artists. Three days ago, tickets for the two days were sold out in two hours. Tonight, there were construction workers and office cadres who had traveled forty miles from the suburbs to watch the performance. Many audience members said, "We love Korean art, and we love the Korean people and the Korean People's Army even more. Today, we feel especially close and happy to see the honored guests from the heroic land – the Korean People's Army Ensemble led by Major General Zhang Ruihuan – in a theater in Beijing because we have brotherly friendship."

　　During the performance, thunderous applause rose and fell in the theater. When the audience watched the dance "Joy," which depicted the joy of the Korean people in obtaining the fruits of a bountiful harvest and seeing the great communist prospects, everyone was delighted and applauded warmly to congratulate them. After the Korean People's Army Ensemble sang the Chinese song "Plant the Red Flag of Victory on Taiwan" in Chinese, the audience in the entire venue was in a frenzy. People responded with sustained applause, and then the ensemble's artists sang it again. Many audience members were moved by the plot in the dance "Comrades-in-Arms." A steel worker from Changchun who was recuperating in Beijing told reporters that the friendship cemented in blood between the Chinese and Korean peoples and armies is unbreakable. We will cherish this great internationalist friendship and the great unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union like the apple of our eye.

　　After the performance, representatives of the audience presented flowers to the Korean People's Army Ensemble.

　　Xinhua News Agency, 29th - The Korean People's Army Ensemble, accompanied by Major General Wei Chuantong, visited the Beijing Dance Academy today. The honored guests were warmly welcomed by all the teachers and students of the Beijing Dance Academy.

　　This morning, Principal Dai Ailian and more than two hundred teachers and students from the Beijing Dance Academy welcomed them in front of the school gate. When the honored guests arrived, the sound of gongs and drums, firecrackers, and applause intertwined, creating an atmosphere of enthusiastic jubilation. In her welcoming speech, Principal Dai Ailian said that the visit of the Korean People's Army Ensemble was a great encouragement to the school, and that they had learned many valuable things from your visit and performance. She expressed a warm welcome on behalf of all the teachers and students of the school.

　　Next, the honored guests visited the teaching sessions of national dance drama and ballet drama at the Beijing Dance Academy, and also watched performances by students to welcome the honored guests.

　　After the students of the Dance Academy finished their performances, Major General Zhang Ruihuan, the leader of the Korean People's Army Ensemble, walked onto the stage and shook hands warmly with the students. At this time, the emotions of the guests and hosts were in a frenzy, shouting the slogan "Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Korean peoples." The Korean People's Army Ensemble and the Beijing Dance Academy exchanged gifts.

### 朝鲜人民军协奏团在京公演  贵宾们参观北京舞蹈学校受到热烈欢迎

1960-10-30
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民军协奏团在京公演
    贵宾们参观北京舞蹈学校受到热烈欢迎
    新华社29日讯　在北京满城洋溢着中朝两国人民深情厚谊的热烈气氛中，朝鲜人民军协奏团今晚在天桥剧场为北京市人民进行公演，受到了北京各界人民的热烈欢迎。
    今晚，朝鲜人民军协奏团演出了十七个歌舞节目，每一个节目都受到观众的热烈欢迎，许多节目在观众热烈要求下多次重演，并且谢幕达四、五次之多。
    当朝鲜人民军协奏团访华演出将在北京进行公演的消息传出后，北京市广大人民怀着无比喜悦心情，争相观看朝鲜艺术家们的艺术表演，三天前，在两个小时内便把两天的剧票出售一空。今晚，有从郊区四十里外赶来观看演出的建筑工人和机关干部。许多观众说，我们热爱朝鲜艺术，更加热爱朝鲜人民和朝鲜人民军，今天，能在北京的剧场内看到来自英雄土地上的亲密友人——由张瑞焕少将率领的朝鲜人民军协奏团贵宾们，感到格外的亲切和欢喜，因为我们有兄弟般的友情。
    演出中，剧场内的雷动般的掌声此起彼落，当观众们在观看描写朝鲜人民取得丰收的劳动果实和看到伟大的共产主义远景而表现出喜悦心情的舞蹈“欢喜”时，个个欢欣鼓舞，热烈鼓掌祝贺。在朝鲜人民军协奏团用中国话唱中国歌曲“把胜利的红旗插到台湾”后，满场的观众情绪沸腾，人们报以经久不息的掌声，接着，协奏团的艺术家又重唱一遍。许多观众被舞蹈“战友”中的情节激动起来，正在北京休养的一位长春钢铁工人告诉记者，中朝两国人民和军队用鲜血凝成的友谊是牢不可破的，我们会像爱护自己的眼珠一样珍惜这种伟大的国际主义友谊和以苏联为首的社会主义阵营伟大团结。
    演出结束后，观众的代表向朝鲜人民军协奏团献了花。
    新华社29日讯　朝鲜人民军协奏团今天在魏传统少将陪同下，参观访问了北京舞蹈学校。贵宾们受到了北京舞蹈学校全体师生的热烈欢迎。
    今晨，北京舞蹈学校校长戴爱莲和二百多名师生便在学校门前迎接。当贵宾们到达时，锣鼓声、鞭炮声和掌声交织成一片热烈欢腾的气氛。戴爱莲校长在致欢迎词中说，朝鲜人民军协奏团来参观访问是对学校的很大鼓舞，并且从你们的访问演出中学习到很多宝贵的东西。她代表全校师生表示热烈的欢迎。
    接着，贵宾们参观了北京舞蹈学校的民族舞剧课、芭蕾舞剧课的教学情形，还观看了学生们为欢迎贵宾而演出的剧目。
    在舞蹈学校学生演出结束后，朝鲜人民军协奏团团长张瑞焕少将走上舞台和学生们亲切握手。这时，宾主情绪沸腾，高呼“中朝两国人民的友谊万岁”口号。朝鲜人民军协奏团和北京舞蹈学校互相赠送了礼品。
