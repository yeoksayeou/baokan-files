Title: China, North Korea and Mongolia Athletics Competition Concludes
Author:
Date: 1960-10-28
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 24th: The friendly athletics competition between China, North Korea, and Mongolia concluded today. The Chinese athletics team participated in nineteen events during the two-day competition, securing sixteen championships.

    In this afternoon's competition, the renowned North Korean female athlete Shin Kim Dan broke the world record of 53.4 seconds for the women's 400-meter race, previously set by Soviet athlete Itkina, with a time of 53 seconds. This afternoon, the Chinese athletics team participated in eight events and won six championships.

    The North Korean athletics team participated in all twenty-seven events of this friendly competition, winning eleven championships.

### 朝中蒙三国田径赛结束

1960-10-28
第6版()
专栏：

    朝中蒙三国田径赛结束
    据新华社平壤24日电　朝、中、蒙三国田径友谊比赛今天结束。中国田径队在这次为时两天的比赛中，共参加了十九项比赛，取得了十六项冠军。
    在今天下午的比赛中，朝鲜著名女选手申金丹以五十三秒的成绩打破了苏联选手伊特基娜创造的五十三秒四的四百米女子赛跑世界纪录。今天下午中国田径队参加了八项比赛，取得了六项冠军。
    朝鲜田径队参加了这次友谊比赛的所有的二十七项比赛，取得了十一项冠军。
