Title: U.S. Atomic Energy Commission Chairman McCone Clamors: U.S. to Resume Underground Nuclear Testing Soon
Author:
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 23rd: U.S. Atomic Energy Commission Chairman John McCone clamored yesterday that the United States must resume underground nuclear testing soon. McCone emphasized that a decision on resuming nuclear testing must be made within the next few weeks. He pointed out that the United States is ready to resume nuclear testing in Nevada within sixty days. All preparations, including the digging of tunnels required for underground explosions, have been completed.

McCone made these remarks to reporters in Hot Springs, Virginia.

Apparently to shirk the responsibility for the United States resuming nuclear testing, he maliciously slandered the Soviet Union, saying that the Soviet Union "obstructed" the negotiations on banning nuclear tests. He admitted that he could not find "evidence of secret Soviet tests," but he also said that the United States could not continue negotiations without knowing whether the Soviet Union had truly stopped nuclear weapons testing.

### 美原子能委员会主席麦康叫嚣  美国即将恢复地下核试验

1960-10-24
第6版()
专栏：

    美原子能委员会主席麦康叫嚣
    美国即将恢复地下核试验
    新华社23日讯　华盛顿消息：美国原子能委员会主席约翰·麦康昨天叫嚣说，美国必须很快恢复地下核试验。麦康强调说，必须在今后几星期内作出关于恢复核试验的决定。他指出，美国已做好在六十天内在内华达州恢复核试验的准备。一切准备工作，包括挖掘地下爆炸所需的地道等等，均已完成。
    麦康是在弗吉尼亚州的温泉地方对记者发表这些话的。
    显然为了推卸美国恢复核试验的责任，他恶意地诽谤苏联，说苏联“妨碍了”关于禁止核试验的谈判的进行。他承认找不到“苏联进行秘密试验的证据”，但是他又说什么在无法知道苏联究竟是否真正停止了核武器试验的情况下，美国不能继续谈判。
