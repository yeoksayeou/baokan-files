Title: Vice-Chairman Kellezi Visits Jiang Nanxiang and Tours Tsinghua Garden
Author:
Date: 1960-10-04
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Abdyl Këllezi, Member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Vice-Chairman of the Council Of Ministers and President Of the Albania–China friendship association, visited Jiang Nanxiang, Alternate Member of the CPC Central Committee, President of the China-Albania Friendship Association and President of Tsinghua University, this afternoon, and toured Tsinghua Garden. All members of the Albanian China Friendship Association delegation also visited Tsinghua Garden today. When the distinguished guests walked to many blackboards, President Jiang Nanxiang introduced the gains students had written on the blackboard newspapers about participating in agricultural labor. Head of Delegation Këllezi said: This is a victory for the General Line, the Great Leap Forward, and the People's Communes put forward by your Party.

### 凯莱齐副主席拜访蒋南翔参观清华园

1960-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    凯莱齐副主席拜访蒋南翔参观清华园
    据新华社3日讯　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、部长会议副主席、阿中友协主席阿卜杜勒·凯莱齐今天下午拜访了中共中央候补委员、中阿友协会长清华大学校长蒋南翔，并且参观了清华园。阿尔巴尼亚中国友好协会代表团的全体团员今天也参观了清华园。当贵宾们走到许多黑板报面前，蒋南翔校长介绍了黑板报上学生们写的参加农业劳动的收获时，凯莱齐团长说：这是你们党所提出的总路线、大跃进、人民公社的胜利。
