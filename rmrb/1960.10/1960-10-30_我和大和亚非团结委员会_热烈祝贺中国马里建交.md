Title: I and the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association Warmly Congratulate the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and Mali
Author:
Date: 1960-10-30
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, 29th. The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace today sent a telegram to Diallo Moussa Sane, Secretary-General of the Malian Peace Movement, warmly congratulating the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Mali.
    The telegram said that the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace warmly congratulates the establishment of diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Mali, and expresses sincere respect to the Malian people through you. We believe that the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Mali will enable the people of our two countries to cooperate and support each other more closely in the common cause of opposing imperialism and defending world peace. We wish the friendship between the people of China and Mali a continuous development.
    Xinhua News Agency, 29th. The Chinese-African People's Friendship Association today sent a telegram to Modibo Keita, Prime Minister of the Republic of Mali, warmly congratulating the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Mali.
    The telegram said that the Chinese people have always deeply sympathized with and resolutely supported the just struggle of the people of the Republic of Mali against imperialism, colonialism, and for national independence. We believe that the establishment of diplomatic relations between our two countries will strengthen the friendly unity between the people of our two countries and promote the common cause of the people of African countries against imperialism.
    The telegram wished the Malian people continuous new achievements in the great struggle against imperialism, safeguarding national independence, and developing the national economy. It wished the friendly unity between the people of China and Mali a continuous development.

### 我和大和亚非团结委员会  热烈祝贺中国马里建交

1960-10-30
第6版()
专栏：

    我和大和亚非团结委员会
    热烈祝贺中国马里建交
    新华社29日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会今天致电马里和平运动秘书长迪亚洛·摩沙·萨内，热烈祝贺中马两国建立外交关系。
    电报说，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会热烈祝贺中华人民共和国和马里共和国建立外交关系，并通过你们向马里人民致以衷心的敬意。我们相信，中国马里两国建交，将使我们两国人民在反对帝国主义和保卫世界和平的共同事业中更加紧密合作和互相支持。祝中国和马里两国人民的友谊日益发展。
    新华社29日讯　中国亚非团结委员会今天致电马里共和国总理莫迪博·凯塔，热烈祝贺中马两国建立外交关系。
    电报说，中国人民一贯深切同情和坚决支持马里共和国人民反对帝国主义、殖民主义和争取民族独立的正义斗争。我们相信，我们两国建立外交关系将加强我们两国人民间的友好团结和促进亚非各国人民反对帝国主义的共同事业。
    电报祝马里人民在反对帝国主义、维护民族独立及发展民族经济的伟大斗争中不断获得新的成就。祝中国与马里两国人民的友好团结日益发展。
