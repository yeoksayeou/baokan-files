Title: Vice Premier Li Jooyeon Leaves Beijing to Visit Various Places
Author:
Date: 1960-10-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 7th: Li Jooyeon, head of the economic delegation of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Vice Premier of the Korean Cabinet, left Beijing by train this afternoon to visit various places in our country.

	Chen Weiji, Vice Minister of Textile Industry, Chen Shuliang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chang Yanqing, Director of the Complete Equipment Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and Li Fanru, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea, accompanied him.

	Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and others saw him off at the station.

	Kim Choi-seon, member of the Korean government economic delegation and Vice Minister of Trade of Korea, Li Young-ho, Korean Ambassador to China, and embassy officials also saw him off at the station.

	This morning, Vice Premier Li Jooyeon and other distinguished guests, accompanied by Wei Zhenwu, Vice Minister of Agriculture, and Wu Wenhua, Curator of the Agricultural Exhibition Hall, visited the Agricultural Exhibition Hall.

### 李周渊副首相离京到各地参观

1960-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

    李周渊副首相离京到各地参观
    新华社7日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府经济代表团团长、朝鲜内阁副首相李周渊今天下午乘火车离开北京，前往我国各地参观访问。
    纺织工业部副部长陈维稷，外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮，对外贸易部成套设备局局长常彦卿，中国驻朝鲜大使馆商务参赞李范如陪同前往。
    到车站送行的，有对外贸易部副部长李强等。
    朝鲜政府经济代表团团员、朝鲜贸易省副相金最善，朝鲜驻华大使李永镐和大使馆官员也到车站送行。
    今天上午，李周渊副首相和其他贵宾由农业部副部长魏震五和农业展览馆馆长吴文华陪同，参观了农业展览馆。
