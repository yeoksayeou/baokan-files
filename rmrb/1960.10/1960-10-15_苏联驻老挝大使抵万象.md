Title: Soviet Ambassador to Laos Arrives in Vientiane
Author:
Date: 1960-10-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 14th – Vientiane Radio broadcast this morning that the Soviet Ambassador to the Kingdom of Laos, Alexander Nikitich Abramov, and his entourage arrived in Vientiane by plane from Bangkok yesterday afternoon. Abramov is the Soviet Ambassador to Cambodia and was recently appointed by the Soviet government to concurrently serve as Ambassador to the Kingdom of Laos.
    The Soviet Ambassador will stay in Vientiane for two or three days and then go to Luang Prabang to present his credentials to the King of Laos.

### 苏联驻老挝大使抵万象

1960-10-15
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联驻老挝大使抵万象
    新华社河内14日电　据万象电台今天上午广播，苏联驻老挝王国大使亚历山大·尼基季奇·阿布拉莫夫和随行人员在昨天下午从曼谷乘飞机抵达万象。阿布拉莫夫是苏联驻柬埔寨大使，最近苏联政府任命他兼任驻老挝王国大使。
    苏联大使在万象停留两三天后将去琅勃拉邦向老挝国王递交国书。
