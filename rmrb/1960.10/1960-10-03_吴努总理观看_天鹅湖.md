Title: Premier U Nu Watches "Swan Lake"
Author:
Date: 1960-10-03
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch. Burmese Federal Premier U Nu and his wife, along with other distinguished Burmese guests, watched the ballet "Swan Lake" performed by young Chinese dancers at the National Culture Palace tonight.

Accompanying them were Comrade Deng Yingchao, wife of Premier Zhou Enlai, Saifudin, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, and others.

### 吴努总理观看“天鹅湖”

1960-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    吴努总理观看“天鹅湖”
    新华社2日讯　缅甸联邦总理吴努和夫人以及其他缅甸贵宾，今晚在民族文化宫观看了中国青年舞蹈家演出的芭蕾舞剧“天鹅湖”。
    陪同观剧的，有周恩来总理的夫人邓颖超同志，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长赛福鼎，文化部副部长夏衍等人。
