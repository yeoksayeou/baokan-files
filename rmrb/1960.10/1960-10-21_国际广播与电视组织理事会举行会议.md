Title: Council of the International Radio and Television Organization Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-10-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, October 13th. The thirty-third meeting of the Council of the International Radio and Television Organization concluded here today.

The meeting began on the 12th. The meeting approved the work report of the organization's Standing Committee and discussed the issue of assisting the radio organizations of Asian, African, and Latin American countries.

Representatives from the council member countries, including the Soviet Union, China, and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, attended the meeting.

### 国际广播与电视组织理事会举行会议

1960-10-21
第6版()
专栏：

    国际广播与电视组织理事会举行会议
    新华社布加勒斯特13日电　国际广播与电视组织理事会第三十三次会议今天在这里闭幕。
    这次会议是12日开始的。会议通过了这个组织的常务委员会的工作报告，并讨论了援助亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲国家的广播组织问题。
    苏联、中国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国等理事国的代表参加了会议。
