Title: Chinese Ambassador to Nepal Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1960-10-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, October 28th: The Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, Zhang Shijie, held a reception at the Chinese Embassy on the evening of the 27th for the Chinese delegation attending the first meeting of the China-Nepal Joint Boundary Committee.

Among those attending the reception were the Prime Minister of Nepal, Koirala, and his wife, cabinet ministers, the Speaker of the Lower House, the Chief Justice, the chief representative and other members of the Nepalese delegation to the China-Nepal Joint Boundary Committee, and other senior civil and military officials. Foreign diplomatic envoys to Nepal also attended the reception.

Also attending the reception were the Chairman of the Kathmandu Municipal Committee, the President of the Nepal-China Friendship Association, and other prominent figures, totaling more than 100 people.

At the reception, the hosts and guests all proposed a toast to the successful conclusion of the first meeting of the China-Nepal Joint Boundary Committee.

The reception was held in an atmosphere of cordiality, warmth, and friendship.

### 我驻尼泊尔大使举行招待会

1960-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻尼泊尔大使举行招待会
    新华社28日讯　加德满都消息：中国驻尼泊尔大使张世杰，于27日晚间在中国大使馆为参加中尼边界联合委员会第一次会议的中国代表团举行招待会。
    出席招待会的有尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊和夫人，内阁大臣们，下议院议长，首席法官，中尼边界联合委员会尼泊尔代表团首席代表和其他成员以及其他高级文武官员。各国驻尼泊尔的外交使节也出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的还有加德满都市政委员会主席，尼中友好协会会长以及著名人士等共一百多人。
    在招待会上，宾主都提议为中尼边界联合委员会第一次会议的胜利结束干杯。
    招待会是在亲切、热烈和友好的气氛中进行的。
