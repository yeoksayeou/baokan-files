Title: Burmese News Delegation Returns to Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1960-10-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, October 28th. The Burmese news delegation led by U Khin, Deputy Director of the Burmese News Bureau, returned to Rangoon by plane from Kunming this afternoon after concluding its visit to China.

During his visit to China, U Khin attended the celebrations of the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the signing ceremony of the Sino-Burmese Border Treaty.

U Khin and his delegation also visited major cities in China.

### 缅甸新闻代表团回到仰光

1960-10-29
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸新闻代表团回到仰光
    新华社仰光28日电　缅甸新闻局副局长吴钦和他率领的缅甸新闻代表团结束了在中国的访问以后，于今天下午乘飞机从昆明回到仰光。
    吴钦在访问中国期间，曾参加了中华人民共和国成立十一周年的庆典，并参加了中缅边界条约的签字仪式。
    吴钦和他率领的代表团还访问了中国的主要城市。
