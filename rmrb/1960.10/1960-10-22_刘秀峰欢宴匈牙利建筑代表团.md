Title: Liu Xiufeng Hosts a Banquet for the Hungarian Architectural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-22
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 21st. Minister of Building Engineering, Liu Xiufeng, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Hungarian Architectural Delegation led by Váli Jenő Károly.
　　Those attending the banquet included Liu Yumin, Vice Minister of Building Engineering, Liang Sicheng, Vice Chairman of the Architectural Society of China, and others.
　　This delegation arrived in Beijing on the 15th at the invitation of the Ministry of Building Engineering and the Architectural Society of China. They will be visiting our country.

### 刘秀峰欢宴匈牙利建筑代表团

1960-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

    刘秀峰欢宴匈牙利建筑代表团
　　新华社21日讯　建筑工程部部长刘秀峰今晚设宴欢迎由瓦帘金尼·卡洛伊率领的匈牙利建筑代表团。
　　出席宴会作陪的有建筑工程部副部长刘裕民、中国建筑学会副理事长梁思成等。
　　这个代表团是应建筑工程部和中国建筑学会的邀请，在15日到达北京的。他们将在我国进行参观访问。
