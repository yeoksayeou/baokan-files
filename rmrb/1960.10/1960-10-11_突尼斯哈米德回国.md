Title: Tunisian Hamid Returns Home

Author:

Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Ben Mustapha Adour Hamid, a Tunisian legal worker who was invited by the Chinese Political Science and Law Association to visit our country, left Beijing by plane today to return home.
    Wu Defeng, Vice President of the Chinese Political Science and Law Association, and others were at the airport to see him off.

### 突尼斯哈米德回国

1960-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    突尼斯哈米德回国
    新华社9日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的突尼斯法律工作者本·慕斯塔发·阿杜尔·哈米德，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    中国政治法律学会副会长吴德峰等到机场欢送。
