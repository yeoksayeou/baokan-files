Title: International Journalists' Meeting Opens; General Debate on Major Agenda Items
Author:
Date: 1960-10-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, October 18th: The Second International Journalists' Meeting opened today in Baden, near Vienna. Approximately two hundred journalists from forty-one countries attended this morning's meeting. They came from the Soviet Union, China, and other socialist countries, France, and many countries in Latin America, Africa, and Asia. Representatives of journalists from about twenty other countries may arrive here successively after the meeting begins because they have encountered difficulties in obtaining Austrian passports.

The three main issues to be discussed at the meeting are: professional issues for journalists, support for reporting on news undertakings in underdeveloped countries, and the social mission of journalists.

The opening meeting this morning was chaired by Mr. Mesplé, President of the Brazilian Journalists' Union. At his proposal, the conference elected a presidium composed of representatives from fifteen countries. These countries are: Brazil, Chile, China, Cuba, France, Ghana, India, Japan, Lebanon, Mali, Mexico, Poland, the German Democratic Republic, Czechoslovakia, and the Soviet Union. Mr. Ehrmann, a representative of the International Organization of Journalists, was also elected as a member of the presidium.

In the general debate today on the main issues of the conference agenda, the speakers unanimously condemned the hypocritical and truth-obliterating reports of imperialist news agencies, demanded efforts to objectively report on the events and lives of people in various countries, striving for genuine press freedom and improving the working conditions of journalists.

### 国际新闻工作者会见大会开幕  就议程主要问题进行一般性辩论

1960-10-20
第6版()
专栏：

    国际新闻工作者会见大会开幕
    就议程主要问题进行一般性辩论
    新华社维也纳18日电　国际新闻工作者第二次会见大会今天在维也纳附近的巴登开幕。来自四十一个国家的大约二百名新闻工作者，出席了今天上午的会议。他们来自苏联、中国和其他社会主义国家、法国以及拉丁美洲、非洲和亚洲的许多国家。还有二十个左右国家的新闻工作者代表，因为在取得奥地利的护照方面遭到困难，可能在会议开始后才能陆续到达这里。
    会见将讨论的三个主要问题是：新闻工作者的专业问题，支持报道不发达国家的新闻事业，和新闻工作者的社会使命。
    主持今天上午开幕会议的是巴西新闻工作者工会主席梅斯普耳先生。根据他的提议，大会选出了由十五个国家的代表组成的主席团。这些国家是：巴西、智利、中国、古巴、法国、加纳、印度、日本、黎巴嫩、马里、墨西哥、波兰、德意志民主共和国、捷克斯洛伐克和苏联。国际新闻工作者协会的代表埃尔曼也被选为主席团成员。
    在今天对会议议程的主要问题进行的一般性辩论中，发言的代表一致谴责帝国主义新闻机构的虚伪的和抹杀真理的报道，要求争取客观地报道各国人民的事件和生活，争取真正的新闻自由和改善新闻工作者的劳动条件。
