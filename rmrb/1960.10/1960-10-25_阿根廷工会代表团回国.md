Title: Argentine Trade Union Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 24th - The Argentine Trade Union Delegation, consisting of seventeen members led by Juan Iapichino, Secretary of the Argentine National Unified Movement of Food Industry Workers, who visited China at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, returned home by plane this morning.

During their stay in China, the delegation visited Beijing, Luoyang, Zhengzhou, Wuhan, Nanjing, Shanghai, and other cities, and participated in the celebrations for the 11th anniversary of the founding of our country.

Li Jiebo, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Zhang Tianmin, Chairman of the National Committee of the China Building Workers' Union, and Du Yanqing of the National Committee of the China Light Industry Trade Union, were at the airport to see them off.

On the evening of the 23rd, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions also held a farewell banquet for the delegation.

### 阿根廷工会代表团回国

1960-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    阿根廷工会代表团回国
    新华社24日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问的阿根廷工会代表团一行十七人，在阿根廷全国食品业工人统一运动书记胡安·伊阿皮奇诺率领下，今天早晨乘飞机回国。
    代表团在我国期间，访问了北京、洛阳、郑州、武汉、南京、上海等城市，并参加了我国建国十一周年的庆祝活动。
    到机场送行的有中华全国总工会副主席李颉伯，中国建筑工会全国委员会主席张天民、中国轻工业工会全国委员会杜延庆等。
    23日晚上，全总还为代表团举行了送别宴会。
