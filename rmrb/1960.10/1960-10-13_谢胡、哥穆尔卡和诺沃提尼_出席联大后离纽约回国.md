Title: Shehu, Gomułka, and Novotný Leave New York for Home After Attending the UN General Assembly
Author:
Date: 1960-10-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 12th: According to TASS, Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, who led the Albanian delegation to the Fifteenth Session of the United Nations General Assembly, left New York today to return home.

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 12th: Władysław Gomułka, Member of the Council of State of the People's Republic of Poland, who led the Polish delegation to the Fifteenth Session of the United Nations General Assembly, left New York by plane today to return to Warsaw.

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 12th: Antonín Novotný, President of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic, who led the delegation of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic to the Fifteenth Session of the United Nations General Assembly, left New York by plane on the evening of the 10th to return home.

### 谢胡、哥穆尔卡和诺沃提尼  出席联大后离纽约回国

1960-10-13
第6版()
专栏：

    谢胡、哥穆尔卡和诺沃提尼
    出席联大后离纽约回国
    新华社12日讯　据塔斯社纽约消息：率领阿尔巴尼亚代表团出席联合国大会第十五届会议的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡今天离纽约回国。
    新华社12日讯　纽约消息：率领波兰代表团出席联合国大会第十五届会议的波兰人民共和国国务委员会委员哥穆尔卡今天乘飞机离开纽约回华沙。
    新华社12日讯　纽约消息：率领捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国代表团出席联合国大会第十五届会议的共和国总统诺沃提尼在10日晚上乘飞机离开纽约回国。
