Title: Kenya Federation of Labour General Secretary Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-10-26
Page: 5

    Kenya Federation of Labour Congress
    General Secretary Leaves Beijing for Home
    Xinhua News Agency, 25th. Ochwada, General Secretary of the Kenya Federation of Labour Congress, who visited our country at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, left Beijing by plane on the 23rd to return to his country after visiting Shanghai and Wuhan.

### 怯尼亚工会大会  总书记离京回国

1960-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    怯尼亚工会大会
    总书记离京回国
    新华社25日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问的怯尼亚工会大会总书记奥奇瓦达，在参观了上海、武汉后，已经在23日乘飞机离开北京回国。
