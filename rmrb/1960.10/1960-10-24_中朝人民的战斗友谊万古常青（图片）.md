Title: The militant friendship between the Chinese and Korean people will last forever (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 4

    The militant friendship between the Chinese and Korean people will last forever
    The picture shows the warm scene of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army triumphantly joining forces in a certain area of Korea.
    This is a scene of Volunteer Army soldiers chatting with Korean people while peeling potatoes together.
    The Korean People's Visit to China Delegation and the Korean People's Army Ensemble in Chengdu, Sichuan Province, visited Deng Fangzhi, the mother of Huang Jiguang, the special-class hero, special-merit citation recipient, and "Hero of the Republic" of Korea.
    The wife and daughter of Park Jae-geun, a Korean internationalist martyr, bid farewell to the returning Chinese People's Volunteer Army soldiers.
  　(All of the above are Xinhua News Agency drafts)

### 中朝人民的战斗友谊万古常青（图片）

1960-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中朝人民的战斗友谊万古常青
    图为朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军部队在朝鲜某地胜利会师的热烈情景。
    这是志愿军战士同朝鲜人民一块剥土豆谈家常。
    朝鲜人民访华代表团和朝鲜人民军协奏团在四川成都市慰问志愿军特级英雄、特等功臣、朝鲜“共和国英雄”黄继光烈士的母亲邓芳芝。
    朝鲜国际主义战士朴在根烈士的夫人和女孩欢送归国的中国人民志愿军战士。
  　（以上均新华社稿）
