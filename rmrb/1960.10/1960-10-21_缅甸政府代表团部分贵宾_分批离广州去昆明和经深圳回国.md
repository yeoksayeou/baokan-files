Title: Part of the Burmese Government Delegation Leaves Guangzhou in Batches for Kunming and Returns Home via Shenzhen
Author:
Date: 1960-10-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 20th: After visiting Guangzhou on the 18th, a group of twelve distinguished guests from the Burmese Government Delegation left Guangzhou in batches today for Kunming and returned home via Shenzhen.

	Chief Minister of Kachin State and Minister of Kachin State Affairs of the Federal Government, Duwa Sinwa Nau, and Minister of Education and Health of the Shan State Government, Su Htun E, and other distinguished guests, twelve in total, flew to Kunming. Those who went to the airport to see them off included Li Jiarren, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, and Li Guangxiang, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou.

	Duwa Zori, Minister of Education and Public Works of the Kachin State Government, took a train to Shenzhen to return home.

	Wang Jinchuan, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee, saw him off at the railway station.

	The distinguished guests came to Guangzhou after visiting Hangzhou. During their stay in Guangzhou, they visited Sun Yat-sen University and the Guangzhou Cement Factory.

### 缅甸政府代表团部分贵宾  分批离广州去昆明和经深圳回国

1960-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸政府代表团部分贵宾
    分批离广州去昆明和经深圳回国
    新华社广州20日电　缅甸政府代表团部分贵宾一行十二人，在18日到广州参观访问后，今天分批离开广州前往昆明和经深圳回国。
    克钦邦邦长、联邦政府克钦邦事务部长杜瓦信瓦瑙和掸邦政府教育和卫生部部长苏吞埃等贵宾一行十二人乘飞机前往昆明。到机场欢送的有广东省副省长李嘉人、广州市副市长李广祥等。
    克钦邦政府教育和公共工程部部长杜瓦佐里乘火车取道深圳回国。
    广东省人民委员会外事处处长王锦川到车站送行。
    贵宾们是在参观访问杭州后来广州的，在广州期间，曾访问了中山大学、广州水泥厂等。
