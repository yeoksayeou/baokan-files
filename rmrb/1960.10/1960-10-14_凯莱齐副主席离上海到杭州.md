Title: Vice-Chairman Kellezi Leaves Shanghai for Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-10-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 13th October. The Albanian China Friendship Association delegation, led by Abdyl Këllezi, member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Vice Chairman of the Council of Minister and Chairman of the Albanian China Friendship Association, arrived in Hangzhou from Shanghai by special train this morning. Misto Treska, Chairman of the Albanian Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Shefkat Musaraj, Secretary-General of the Albanian Writers and Artists Association, and Piro Pllumbi, the Albanian Ambassador to China, also arrived on the same train.

Lin Hujia, Secretary of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Zhou Rongxin, Standing Committee member of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Ren Yili, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province, Wang Pingyi and Wang Zida, Secretaries of the Hangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Qiu Qiang, Vice Mayor of Hangzhou, Qian Jun, Lieutenant General of the Zhejiang Military Garrison, and other leading figures from the Party, government and military in Zhejiang Province and Hangzhou, along with hundreds of people from all walks of life, warmly welcomed the distinguished guests at the station.

Today, the national flags of China and Albania fluttered in the wind at Hangzhou Station, and a huge banner reading "Welcome to the Albanian China Friendship Association Delegation headed by Comrade Abdyl Këllezi" was hung on the platform. At 11:00 a.m., after the special train carrying the distinguished guests came to a stop, Lin Hujia and others stepped forward and warmly shook hands with the guests one by one. Young Pioneers presented bouquets of flowers to the distinguished guests. Also at the station to welcome the distinguished guests were leading members from the Zhejiang Provincial and Hangzhou Municipal Committees of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Zhejiang Provincial and Hangzhou Municipal branches of the Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries, various departments of the Zhejiang Provincial and Hangzhou Municipal Party and government organs, and various people's organizations, as well as well-known labor models, etc. Albanian students studying in Hangzhou also came to the station to welcome them.

In the afternoon, Vice-Chairman Këllezi and other distinguished guests took a boat trip on West Lake, visiting the Three Pools Mirroring the Moon and the Fish Viewing at Flower Harbor Park.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 13th October. The Albanian China Friendship Association delegation, led by Vice-Chairman Abdyl Këllezi, concluded its visit to Shanghai. This morning, accompanied  by Jiang Nanxiang, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman of the Sino-Albanian Friendship Association, Liu Mingfu, Vice-Chairman of the State Planning Commission, and Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and East European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, they left Shanghai by special train for Hangzhou. Piro Pllumbi, the Albanian Ambassador to China, Misto Treska, Chairman of the Albanian Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and Shefkat Musaraj, Secretary-General of the Albanian Writers and Artists Association, who had been invited to visit China, also traveled on the same train.

Chen Pixian and Ma Tianshui, Secretaries of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Liu Shuzhou, Alternate Secretary of the Municipal Committee and Vice Mayor of Shanghai, and others went to the station to see them off.

Before boarding the train, the distinguished guests cordially shook hands with the leading members of the Shanghai Party and government and people from all walks of life who had come to see them off and bid farewell. They also accepted flowers from the Young Pioneers.

Also at the station to see them off today were: Zhao Zukang, Vice Mayor of Shanghai, Liu Jingji Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, as well as heads of various departments of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, and various people's organizations in Shanghai. Albanian interns in Shanghai also went to the station to see them off.

### 凯莱齐副主席离上海到杭州

1960-10-14
第4版()
专栏：

    凯莱齐副主席离上海到杭州
    新华社杭州13日电　由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、部长会议副主席、阿中友好协会主席阿卜杜勒·凯莱齐率领的阿中友好协会代表团，今天上午乘专车从上海到达杭州。阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会主席米斯托·特雷斯卡、阿尔巴尼亚作家艺术家协会秘书长谢夫卡特·穆萨莱和阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使帕里夫蒂也同车到达。
    中共浙江省委书记处书记林乎加，中共浙江省委常委周荣鑫，浙江省副省长任一力，中共杭州市委书记处书记王平夷、王子达，杭州市副市长丘强，浙江驻军首长钱钧中将等浙江省、杭州市党政军负责人和各界群众数百人，在车站上热烈欢迎贵宾。
    今天，杭州车站上中阿两国国旗迎风飘扬，站台上悬挂着“欢迎以阿卜杜勒·凯莱齐同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚中国友好协会代表团”的巨大横幅。十一时，贵宾们乘坐的专车停稳后，林乎加等迎上前去，和贵宾们一一亲切握手。少先队员把一束束鲜花献给贵宾。今天到车站欢迎贵宾的，还有政协浙江省和杭州市委员会、对外文化协会浙江省和杭州市分会、浙江省和杭州市党政机关各部门、各人民团体等各方面的负责人，以及著名的劳动模范等。在杭州的阿尔巴尼亚留学生也到车站欢迎。
    下午，凯莱齐副主席等贵宾乘船观赏了西湖景色，游览了三潭印月和花港观鱼公园。
    新华社上海13日电　由阿卜杜勒·凯莱齐副主席率领的阿中友好协会代表团结束了在上海的参观访问，今天上午在中共中央候补委员、中阿友好协会会长蒋南翔，国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫，外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清等陪同下，乘专车离开上海，前往杭州。阿尔巴尼亚驻我国大使帕里夫蒂和应邀来我国参观访问的阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会主席米斯托·特雷斯卡，阿尔巴尼亚作家艺术家协会秘书长谢夫卡特·穆萨莱也同车前往。
    中共上海市委书记处书记陈丕显、马天水，市委书记处候补书记、上海市副市长刘述周等都到车站欢送。
    贵宾们登车前，和前往欢送的上海市党和政府领导人以及各方面人士一一亲切握手告别。并接受了少先队员们的献花。
    今天到车站欢送的还有：上海市副市长赵祖康，政协上海市委员会副主席刘靖基，以及中共上海市委、上海市人民委员会各部门、上海市各人民团体的负责人等。阿尔巴尼亚在上海的实习生也到车站送行。
