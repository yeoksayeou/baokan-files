Title: Coordinating with SEATO's Plot to Interfere in Laos: US-Philippine Navies Hold Exercises in the South China Sea
Author:
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 5

    Coordinating with SEATO's Plot to Interfere in Laos
    US-Philippine Navies Hold Exercises in the South China Sea
    According to the "Manila Chronicle" on the 3rd, just as the US-controlled Southeast Asia Treaty Organization (SEATO) aggressive bloc is actively plotting to interfere in Laos' internal affairs, five warships from the US and Philippine navies began five days of mine warfare "exercises" in the South China Sea on the 3rd.
    The newspaper revealed that these "exercises" were jointly planned by the US Military Assistance Advisory Group (MAAG) in the Philippines, the Philippine Navy authorities, and the US Seventh Fleet.

### 配合东南亚集团策划干涉老挝的活动  美菲海军在南中国海举行演习

1960-10-07
第5版()
专栏：

    配合东南亚集团策划干涉老挝的活动
    美菲海军在南中国海举行演习
    据“马尼拉纪事报”3日报道，正当美国控制的东南亚条约侵略集团积极策划干涉老挝内政的时候，美国和菲律宾海军的五艘军舰从3日起在南中国海举行五天的水雷战“演习”。
    报纸透露，这次“演习”是由驻菲美军联合顾问团和菲律宾海军当局协同美国第七舰队共同计划的。
