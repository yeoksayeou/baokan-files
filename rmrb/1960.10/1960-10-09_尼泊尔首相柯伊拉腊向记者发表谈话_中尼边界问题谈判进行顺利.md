Title: Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala Speaks to Reporters: Sino-Nepalese Border Issue Negotiations Proceed Smoothly
Author:
Date: 1960-10-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, 8th - Kathmandu news: Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala said at a press conference held in Kathmandu yesterday that the work being carried out by the Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Commission is satisfactory, and the border issue is expected to be resolved peacefully soon. He also said that the outstanding issues related to the border between China and Nepal have been resolved.

Prime Minister Koirala recently returned to Kathmandu after attending the United Nations General Assembly. On his way back, he also told reporters in Calcutta on the 4th that the negotiations on the Sino-Nepalese border issue were progressing very smoothly.

At the press conference in Kathmandu, Prime Minister Koirala also expressed his support for the restoration of China's legitimate seat in the United Nations.

### 尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊向记者发表谈话  中尼边界问题谈判进行顺利

1960-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊向记者发表谈话
    中尼边界问题谈判进行顺利
    新华社8日讯　加德满都消息：尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊昨天在加德满都举行的记者招待会上说，中尼边界联合委员会正在进行的工作是令人满意的，边界问题不久可望得到和平解决。他还说，有关中国和尼泊尔两国边界的悬而未决的问题都解决了。
    柯伊拉腊首相在参加联合国大会之后最近回到加德满都。他在回国途中4日经过加尔各答时也曾向记者表示，中尼边界问题的谈判进行得很顺利。
    柯伊拉腊首相在加德满都的记者招待会上还表示支持恢复中国在联合国的合法地位。
