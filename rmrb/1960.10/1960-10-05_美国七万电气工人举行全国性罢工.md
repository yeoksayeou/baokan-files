Title: American 70,000 Electrical Workers Stage Nationwide Strike
Author:
Date: 1960-10-05
Page: 6
 
    American 70,000 Electrical Workers Stage Nationwide Strike
    Workers at 55 factories of the American General Electric Company began their first nationwide strike since 1946 on the morning of the 2nd. 70,000 people belonging to the International Union of Electrical Workers are participating in the strike.

### 美国七万电气工人举行全国性罢工

1960-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    美国七万电气工人举行全国性罢工
    美国通用电力公司五十五家工厂的工人二日早晨开始举行一九四六年以来的第一次全国性罢工。参加罢工的有七万人，他们属于美国电气工人工会。
