Title: Greek Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: The Greek Cultural Delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation consists of three people, headed by Mr. Panayotopoulos. Wen Pengjiu, a council member of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Wu Xue, a council member of the Chinese Dramatists Association, and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 希腊文化代表团到京

1960-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    希腊文化代表团到京
    新华社23日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的希腊文化代表团一行三人，在团长帕那约托普路斯率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国人民对外文化协会理事温朋久、中国戏剧家协会理事吴雪等。
