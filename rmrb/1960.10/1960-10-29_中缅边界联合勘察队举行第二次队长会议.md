Title: Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Survey Team Holds Second Captains' Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-10-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 28th. The Second Captains' Meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Survey Team began in Kunming this morning.

The Chinese side attending the meeting included: Yao Zhongming, Chief Representative of the Chinese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Committee; Representatives Cheng Zhiping, Cheng Xueyu, Chen Yanbin, and Huang Fenglin; Advisors Major General Ding Rongchang, Tang Dengmin, and Zhang Guoqi; and the captains of each survey team.

The Burmese side attending the meeting included: Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Chief Representative of the Burmese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Committee; Representatives U San Lun, Colonel So Min, and U Kun Aung Kya; Advisors Brigadier General Shan You, Colonel La Aung, Colonel Ba Than, U Hla Gyaw, and Than Myint; and the captains of each survey team.

### 中缅边界联合勘察队举行第二次队长会议

1960-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    中缅边界联合勘察队举行第二次队长会议
    新华社昆明28日电　中缅边界联合勘察队第二次队长会议今天上午在昆明开始举行。
    中国方面出席会议的有：中缅边界联合委员会中方首席代表姚仲明，代表程之平、成学俞、陈雁彬、黄凤麟，顾问丁荣昌少将、唐登岷、张国器以及各勘察队队长等。
    缅甸方面出席会议的有：中缅边界联合委员会缅方首席代表昂季准将，代表吴山伦、苏敏上校、吴昆翁佳，顾问山友准将、拉昂上校、巴丹上校、吴拉觉、丹民尔，以及各勘察队队长等。
