Title: All-China Federation of Asian and African Solidarity and Chinese-African People's Friendship Association Send Telegrams Congratulating China and Mali on Mutual Recognition

Author: 
Date: 1960-10-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 18th: The All-China Federation of Asian and African Solidarity today sent a telegram to Modibo Keita, Prime Minister of the Republic of Mali, warmly congratulating China and Mali on their mutual recognition.

The telegram stated: "The All-China Federation of Asian and African Solidarity warmly congratulates the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Mali on their mutual recognition and extends sincere respect to the brave and hardworking people of Mali.

The Chinese people have always firmly supported the just struggle of the Malian people to free themselves from colonial oppression and strive for national independence, and will always stand with the Malian people to fight together against imperialism, promote Asian-African solidarity, and defend world peace."

Xinhua News Agency, October 18th: Liu Changsheng, President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, today sent a telegram to Modibo Keita, Prime Minister of the Republic of Mali, warmly congratulating the Republic of Mali on its establishment and on the mutual recognition between China and Mali.

The telegram stated, "We are delighted to learn of the establishment of the Republic of Mali, and I, on behalf of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association and the Chinese people, extend warm congratulations to Your Excellency and, through Your Excellency, to the people of the Republic of Mali."

The telegram stated, "The establishment of the Republic of Mali is the result of the Malian people's long-term struggle against imperialism and colonialism. The Chinese people are heartened by the achievements made by your people in this struggle. The 650 million Chinese people will always stand with your people and firmly support your people's just struggle against imperialism and colonialism and for the safeguarding of national independence."

The telegram stated, "We are very pleased to see that shortly after the establishment of the Republic of Mali, the governments of our two countries announced their mutual recognition. I would like to express my heartfelt congratulations to the people of your country on behalf of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association."

### 我亚非团结委员会和中非友协致电  祝贺中马两国互相承认

1960-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

    我亚非团结委员会和中非友协致电
    祝贺中马两国互相承认
    新华社18日讯　中国亚非团结委员会今天致电马里共和国总理莫迪博·凯塔，热烈庆贺中马两国互相承认。
    电报说：“中国亚非团结委员会热烈庆贺中华人民共和国和马里共和国的互相承认，并向勇敢勤劳的马里人民致以衷心的敬意。
    中国人民一贯坚决支持马里人民为摆脱殖民主义压迫、争取民族独立的正义斗争，并将永远与马里人民一起，为反对帝国主义、促进亚非团结、保卫世界和平而共同奋斗。”
    新华社18日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会会长刘长胜今天致电马里共和国总理莫迪博·凯塔，热烈祝贺马里共和国成立和中马两国互相承认。
    电报说，“欣悉马里共和国宣布成立，我谨代表中国非洲人民友好协会和中国人民向阁下并通过阁下向马里共和国人民致以热烈的祝贺。”
    电报说，“马里共和国的成立，是马里人民长期向帝国主义和殖民主义进行斗争的结果。中国人民为贵国人民在这一斗争中所取得的成就欢欣鼓舞。六亿五千万中国人民将永远同贵国人民站在一起，坚决支持贵国人民反对帝国主义、殖民主义、维护民族独立的正义斗争。”
    电报说，“我们非常高兴地看到，马里共和国成立之后不久，贵我两国政府就宣布了互相承认。我谨代表中国非洲人民友好协会向贵国人民表示衷心的祝贺。”
