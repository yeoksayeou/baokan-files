Title: Our Military Friendship Delegation Pays a Visit to Vice Premier Kim Kwang-hyup; Delegation Attends a Cultural Performance
Author:
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 23rd. The entire members of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to North Korea, led by Marshal He Long and General Luo Ruiqing, paid a visit to Kim Kwang-hyup, Vice Premier of the Cabinet and Minister of National Defense of North Korea, this afternoon.

The members of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation included Liu Yalou, Deputy Minister of National Defense and Commander of the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Air Force General; Yang Yong, former Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, General; Chen Xilian, Commander of the Shenyang Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General; Wang Ping, former Political Commissar of the Chinese People's Volunteers, General; Zhou Xihan, Deputy Commander of the Navy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Navy Vice Admiral; and Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense, Lieutenant General.

Present at the meeting were Kim Chang-bong, Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, Colonel General; Heo Bong-hak, Director of the General Political Bureau, Colonel General; Choe Yong-jin, Vice Minister of National Defense, Colonel General, as well as Kim Bong-ryul, Colonel General, Seok San, Colonel General, Choe Gwang, Commander of the Air Force of the Korean People's Army, Colonel General; Ryu Chang-kwon, Commander of the Navy, Rear Admiral, and others. Also present were Qiao Xiaoguang, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, and Zhang Leting, Military Attaché, Colonel.

The two sides held cordial and friendly talks during the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 23rd. The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation led by Marshal He Long and General Luo Ruiqing, accompanied by Vice Premier Kim Il of the North Korean Cabinet and Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Kim Kwang-hyup, watched a large-scale musical dance narrative poem "Our Glorious Motherland" performed jointly by twenty-three art troupes from Pyongyang, totaling three thousand people, this afternoon.

Also accompanying the delegation to watch the performance were Kim Chang-man, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, Vice Premier of the Cabinet Jeong Il-ryong, Chairman of the Korea-China Friendship Association Ha Ang-cheon, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ryu Jang-shik, as well as Kim Chang-bong, Colonel General, Heo Bong-hak, Colonel General, Choe Yong-jin, Colonel General, and other senior generals of the Korean People's Army. Qiao Xiaoguang, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, and Zhang Leting, Military Attaché, Colonel, also attended the performance.

The Chinese People's Liberation Army Art Troupe led by the delegation and more than 1,500 people from Pyongyang City watched the performance at the same time.

At the end of the performance, Marshal He Long, General Luo Ruiqing, and all members of the delegation went on stage to present flower baskets and cordially shook hands with the performers to congratulate them on the success of the performance.

### 我军事友好代表团  拜会金光侠副首相  代表团观看了文艺演出

1960-10-24
第5版()
专栏：

    我军事友好代表团
    拜会金光侠副首相
    代表团观看了文艺演出
    新华社平壤23日电　由贺龙元帅、罗瑞卿大将率领的中华人民共和国访朝军事友好代表团全体成员今天下午拜会了朝鲜内阁副首相兼民族保卫相金光侠大将。
    中国军事友好代表团团员有国防部副部长、中国人民解放军空军司令员刘亚楼空军上将、前中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇上将、中国人民解放军沈阳驻军司令员陈锡联上将、前中国人民志愿军政治委员王平上将、中国人民解放军海军副司令员周希汉海军中将、国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将。
    拜会时在座的有朝鲜人民军总参谋长金昌奉上将、总政治局长许凤学上将、民族保卫省副相崔勇进上将以及金奉律上将、石山上将、朝鲜人民军空军司令崔光上将、海军司令柳昌权少将等。在座的还有中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光和武官张乐亭上校。
    拜会时双方进行了亲切友好的交谈。
    新华社平壤23日电　由贺龙元帅、罗瑞卿大将率领的中国军事友好代表团在朝鲜内阁金一副首相、副首相兼民族保卫相金光侠大将陪同下，今天下午观看了由平壤二十三个艺术团体共三千人联合演出的大型音乐舞蹈叙事诗“我们光荣的祖国”。
    陪同代表团观看演出的还有朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长金昌满、内阁副首相郑一龙、朝中友好协会委员长河仰天、外务省副相柳章植以及金昌奉上将、许凤学上将、崔勇进上将等朝鲜人民军高级将领。中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光和武官张乐亭上校也出席观看了演出。
    代表团率领的中国人民解放军艺术工作团和平壤市一千五百多名群众同时观看了演出。
    演出结束时，贺龙元帅、罗瑞卿大将和代表团全体团员登台献花篮，并向演员们亲切握手祝贺演出成功。
