Title: First Secretary of the Latvian Communist Party of the Soviet Union Receives Delegation from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1960-10-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Riga, 7th. TASS reported from Riga on the 6th: A. Pelše, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Latvian Communist Party, received the delegation from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association headed by Xu Guangda on the 6th.

The delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association was invited by the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association to go to the Soviet Union to participate in the celebration of the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and to pay a friendly visit. The delegation left Moscow on the 2nd, visited Minsk, and arrived in Riga on the 5th.

### 苏联拉脱维亚共产党第一书记  接见中苏友好协会代表团

1960-10-08
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联拉脱维亚共产党第一书记
    接见中苏友好协会代表团
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社里加6日讯：拉脱维亚共产党中央委员会第一书记阿·别尔谢6日接见了以许光达为首的中苏友好协会代表团。
    中苏友好协会代表团是应苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会和苏中友好协会的邀请，去苏联参加中华人民共和国成立十一周年的庆祝活动并进行友好访问的。代表团于2日离开莫斯科，在明斯克访问后于5日抵达里加。
