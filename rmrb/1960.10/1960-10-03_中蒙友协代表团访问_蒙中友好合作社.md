Title: Chinese-Mongolian Friendship Association Delegation Visits "Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Cooperative"
Author:
Date: 1960-10-03
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 29th - The delegation of the Chinese-Mongolian Friendship Association, led by Cao Diqiu, with Ji Yaotai as deputy head, and its members, visited the "Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Cooperative" in the Central Province of Mongolia today.

    The cooperative held a mass meeting to celebrate the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and to welcome the delegation of the Chinese-Mongolian Friendship Association.

    Dashi Nyam, the chairman of the cooperative, delivered a passionate speech at the meeting. He said that the victory of the Chinese revolution strengthened the power of the socialist camp. Under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, the Chinese people are confidently advancing, holding high the three red banners of the General Line, the Great Leap Forward, and the People's Communes. He said that the friendship between the people of Mongolia and China is constantly developing. He also expressed gratitude for the assistance of the Chinese people. He said that the agricultural and pastoral machinery donated to the cooperative by China has played a significant role in production this year.

    Head of Delegation Cao Diqiu also spoke at the meeting. He said that we feel very cordial and happy to have the opportunity to participate in your celebration of the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. He then talked about the achievements of socialist construction in China and Mongolia, as well as the traditional and profound friendship between the two peoples, and wished the cooperative new achievements in agricultural and pastoral production.

    At the meeting, the hosts and guests exchanged gifts.

    The "Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Cooperative" also held a reception for the delegation, and the reception was filled with a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

### 中蒙友协代表团访问“蒙中友好合作社”

1960-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    中蒙友协代表团访问“蒙中友好合作社”
    新华社乌兰巴托29日电　中蒙友好协会代表团团长曹荻秋、副团长吉雅泰和团员，今天到蒙古中央省访问了“蒙中友好合作社”。
    合作社举行了群众大会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年，并欢迎中蒙友好协会代表团。
    合作社社长达西尼雅木在会上发表了热情洋溢的讲话。他说，中国革命的胜利加强了社会主义阵营的力量，中国人民在中国共产党领导下，高举总路线、大跃进和人民公社三面红旗，正在满怀信心地前进。他说，蒙中两国人民的友谊在不断发展。他并且表示感谢中国人民的援助。他说，中国赠送给合作社的农牧业机器，今年在生产中发挥了很大的作用。
    曹荻秋团长也在会上讲了话。他说，我们有机会参加你们庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年的大会，感到十分亲切和高兴。他接着谈到了中国和蒙古的社会主义建设成就以及两国人民传统的深厚友谊，并祝合作社在农牧业生产方面取得新的成就。
    会上，宾主双方互赠了礼品。
    “蒙中友好合作社”还为代表团举行了招待会，招待会洋溢着亲切友好的气氛。
