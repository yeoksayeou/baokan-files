Title: Twelve Japanese Delegations from All Sectors Thank Chinese People for Supporting the Struggle of Miike Coal Mine Workers
Author:
Date: 1960-10-04
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Twelve Japanese delegations from various circles currently visiting China, represented by Takagaki Reijiro, head of the Japanese Coal Miners' Union delegation to China, read out a resolution of thanks after the Sino-Japanese people's organizations signed a joint statement tonight, and presented the letter containing the resolution of thanks to Li Jiebo, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. The full text of the resolution is as follows:

    Resolution of Thanks
    The twelve Japanese delegations who signed the joint statement between the people's organizations of Japan and China express their heartfelt gratitude for the warm support given by the Chinese people to the struggle of the Miike branch of the Japanese Coal Miners' Union.
                October 3, 1960

### 日本各界十二个访华代表团  感谢中国人民支援三池煤矿工人斗争

1960-10-04
第3版()
专栏：

    日本各界十二个访华代表团
    感谢中国人民支援三池煤矿工人斗争
    新华社3日讯　日本煤矿工会访华代表团团长高垣礼二郎代表目前在中国访问的日本各界十二个代表团，在今夜中日两国人民团体签订共同声明以后，宣读了一项感谢决议，并且将写着感谢决议的信交给了中华全国总工会副主席李颉伯。这项决议的全文如下：
    感谢决议
    在日中两国人民团体共同声明上签字的日本十二个代表团，对于中国人民给予日本煤炭公会三池支部的斗争的热烈支援表示衷心感谢。
                1960年10月3日
