Title: Cuban Cultural Delegation Meets with Chinese Literary and Art Circles
Author:
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The entire Cuban Cultural Delegation, headed by Dr. Vicentina Antuña Davio, met this evening with Guo Xiaochuan, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Writers Association and poet; Xie Bingxin, director of the Writers Association and writer; Wang Yafan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Writers Association and poet; playwright Fu Tuo; painter Fang Cheng; pianist Zhou Guangren and others. The hosts and guests had a cordial conversation. Dr. Davio, on behalf of the Cuban people, expressed deep gratitude to the Chinese literary and art circles for donating funds for the Cuban "Poetry Plane". Our writers and poets expressed their admiration for the heroic Cuban people standing at the forefront of the opposition to U.S. imperialism, and believed that the Cuban people's spirit of resolutely fighting imperialism set an example for the peace-loving people of the world. The hosts and guests toasted each other, wishing the friendship between the Chinese and Cuban people would last forever.

### 古巴文化代表团与我文艺界人士会见

1960-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    古巴文化代表团与我文艺界人士会见
    新华社6日讯　以维森蒂娜·安图尼·达维奥博士为首的古巴文化代表团全体团员，今晚会见了中国作家协会书记处书记、诗人郭小川、作协理事、作家谢冰心、作家协会副秘书长、诗人王亚凡、剧作家傅铎、画家方成、钢琴家周广仁等，宾主进行了亲切的交谈。达维奥博士代表古巴人民对我国文艺界为古巴“诗歌号”飞机捐款表示深切的感谢。我作家、诗人们对英雄的古巴人民站在反对美帝国主义的最前列表示钦佩，认为古巴人民同帝国主义坚决斗争的精神为世界爱好和平的人民树立了榜样。宾主纷纷祝酒，祝愿中古人民的友谊万古长青。
