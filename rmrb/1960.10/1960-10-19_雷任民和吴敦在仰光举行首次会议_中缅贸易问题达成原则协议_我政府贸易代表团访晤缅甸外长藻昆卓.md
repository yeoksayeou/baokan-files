Title: Lei Renmin and U Dun Hold First Meeting in Yangon, Principle Agreement Reached on Sino-Burmese Trade Issues, Our Government Trade Delegation Visits Burmese Foreign Minister Sao Khun Hkio
Author:
Date: 1960-10-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, October 17th. Lei Renmin, head of the Chinese government trade delegation, and U Dun, Burmese Minister of Trade Development, held their first meeting here this afternoon.
    The two sides discussed trade issues between the two countries and reached an agreement on matters of principle. Lei Renmin and U Dun both expressed their firm belief that in the future, trade between China and Burma will be further developed on a new and friendly basis.
    The meeting was held in a cordial and friendly atmosphere. All members of the Chinese government trade delegation and relevant officials from the Burmese Ministry of Trade Development participated in the meeting.
    A subcommittee composed of experts from both sides will continue to meet tomorrow on specific issues related to expanding trade between the two countries.
    All members of the Chinese government trade delegation, led by Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, also paid their respects to the mausoleum of Burmese national hero General Aung San this morning and laid a wreath at the mausoleum.
    Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, October 18th. All members of the People's Republic of China government trade delegation, headed by Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, visited Burmese Foreign Minister Sao Khun Hkio this morning.
    Lei Yang, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, was present at the meeting.
    U Hla Kio, Parliamentary Secretary of the Burmese Ministry of Trade Development, hosted a luncheon today in honor of the Chinese government trade delegation headed by Lei Renmin.

### 雷任民和吴敦在仰光举行首次会议  中缅贸易问题达成原则协议  我政府贸易代表团访晤缅甸外长藻昆卓

1960-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

    雷任民和吴敦在仰光举行首次会议
    中缅贸易问题达成原则协议
    我政府贸易代表团访晤缅甸外长藻昆卓
    新华社仰光17日电　中国政府贸易代表团团长雷任民和缅甸贸易发展部长吴敦今天下午在这里举行第一次会谈。
    双方讨论了两国之间的贸易问题，并在原则问题上达成了协议。雷任民和吴敦都表示坚信，在将来，中国和缅甸之间的贸易将在新的和友好的基础上获得进一步发展。
    会谈是在亲切友好的气氛中进行的。中国政府贸易代表团全体团员和缅甸贸易发展部有关官员参加了这次会谈。
    由双方专家组成的小组委员会将在明天就扩大两国贸易的一些具体问题继续进行会谈。
    由对外贸易部副部长雷任民率领的中国政府贸易代表团全体团员今天上午还晋谒了缅甸民族英雄昂山将军的陵墓，并向陵墓献花圈。
    新华社仰光18日电　以对外贸易部副部长雷任民为首的中华人民共和国政府贸易代表团全体团员今天上午访晤了缅甸外交部长藻昆卓。
    会见时在座的有中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬。
    缅甸贸易发展部议会秘书吴拉觉今天举行午宴，招待以雷任民为首的中国政府贸易代表团。
