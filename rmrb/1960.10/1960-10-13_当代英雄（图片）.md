Contemporary Hero

Wang Tianxiang (Communist Youth League member), a high-yield standard-bearer and young driver from the Third Team of the Xuchang Highway Transportation Company in Henan Province, has been continuously setting new records in vehicle ton-mile production this year. In August, he exceeded his annual production plan by 443%. Recently, he (right) and three young drivers from his team have been developing new leaping-forward plans, determined to create even newer records for hauling more and running faster.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Qingyun photographed

### 当代英雄（图片）
张青云
1960-10-13
第2版()
专栏：当代英雄

    当代英雄
    河南省许昌公路运输公司的高产旗手、第三车队青年司机王天祥（共青团员），今年以来，车吨月产连创新纪录。在8月份，他就超额443％完成了全年生产计划。最近，他（右）又和小组的三个青年司机在一起制订了新的跃进计划，决心创造多拉快跑的更新记录。　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　张青云摄
