Title: Further Developing the Friendship Between the Chinese and Malian People: China and Mali Decide to Recognize Each Other; Premier Zhou and Foreign Minister Chen Exchange Congratulatory Telegrams with Prime Minister Keita
Author:
Date: 1960-10-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th - Premier Zhou Enlai and Foreign Minister Chen Yi of the People's Republic of China sent telegrams to Prime Minister Keita of the Republic of Mali today, notifying him of the Chinese government's decision to recognize the Republic of Mali as an independent and sovereign state, and expressing hope for the early establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries.

Premier Zhou Enlai had received telegrams from Prime Minister Keita on September 22nd and October 14th. In those telegrams, Prime Minister Keita notified that the Republic of Mali was an independent and sovereign state and had decided to recognize the People's Republic of China, expressing hope for the establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries.

The full texts of the exchanged telegrams are as follows:

    Telegram from Premier Zhou Enlai to Prime Minister Keita

    To His Excellency Modibo Keita, Prime Minister of the Republic of Mali, Bamako:

    I am very pleased to have received Your Excellency's telegrams of September 22, 1960, and October 14, 1960. On behalf of the people and government of the People's Republic of China, and in my own name, I extend my heartfelt thanks to the government of the Republic of Mali for its recognition of our country. Foreign Minister Chen Yi of the People's Republic of China has notified Your Excellency of the Chinese government's decision to recognize the Republic of Mali as an independent and sovereign state. I warmly congratulate our two countries on their mutual recognition, wish our two peoples' friendly relations to enter a new stage, and wish the government and people of the Republic of Mali new victories in the struggle against imperialism and for the safeguarding of national independence.

    I also hope that diplomatic relations can be established between our two countries at an early date. I firmly believe that the friendly relations between our two countries will be increasingly developed and consolidated.

    Please accept my highest respects.

                          Zhou Enlai
                Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                          October 17, 1960, Beijing

    Telegram from Foreign Minister Chen Yi to Prime Minister Keita

    To His Excellency Modibo Keita, Prime Minister of the Republic of Mali, Bamako:

    I am honored to notify Your Excellency on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China that the Chinese government is pleased to have received Your Excellency's telegram of September 22, 1960, regarding the Legislative Assembly's declaration of the Republic of Mali as an independent and sovereign state, and Your Excellency's telegram of October 14, 1960, regarding the government of Republic of Mali's recognition of the People's Republic of China. The Chinese government has decided to recognise the Republic of Mali as an independent and sovereign state. The Chinese government hopes to establish diplomatic relations with the Republic of Mali on the basis of mutual respect for sovereignty and territorial integrity, and suggests discussing the issue of establishing diplomatic relations between our two countries through appropriate diplomatic channels. I firmly believe that after the mutual recognition of our two countries, the friendship between the people of China and Mali will be further developed.

    Chen Yi
    Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China
                      October 17, 1960

    Telegram from Prime Minister Keita to Premier Zhou Enlai on October 14

    Beijing
    To His Excellency the Premier of the People's Republic of China:

    I am honored to inform Your Excellency that the Government of the Republic of Mali recognizes the People's Republic of China as an independent and sovereign state. The people and government of Mali, as well as myself, take this pleasant opportunity to express warm congratulations to the Chinese people and the Chinese government and Your Excellency. The Malian government would be pleased to establish diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of China based on fruitful cooperation and sincere friendship. I wish the People's Republic of China to defeat all the conspiracies of imperialism, and wish the People's Republic of China national happiness and prosperity in world peace.

    Please accept my highest respects.

                          Modibo Keita
                   October 14, 1960, Koulikoro

    Telegram from Prime Minister Keita to Premier Zhou Enlai on September 22

    Beijing
    To His Excellency the Premier of the People's Republic of China:

    I am honored to inform Your Excellency and the Chinese people that the Legislative Assembly has today (22nd) declared the Republic of Mali as an independent and sovereign state. Therefore, the Sudanese Republic has been renamed the Republic of Mali. I assure you that the people and government of Mali are willing to maintain friendly relations with the people of all countries in the world based on their ideals of fraternity and peace.

    Please accept my highest respects.

                Modibo Keita
            Prime Minister of the Government of the Republic of Mali
                      September 22, 1960, Bamako

### 进一步发展中马两国人民的友谊  中国马里决定相互承认  周总理、陈外长同凯塔总理相互致电祝贺

1960-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

    进一步发展中马两国人民的友谊
    中国马里决定相互承认
    周总理、陈外长同凯塔总理相互致电祝贺
    新华社17日讯　中华人民共和国总理周恩来和外交部长陈毅在今天分别打电报给马里共和国总理凯塔，通知他中国政府决定承认马里共和国为独立自主的国家，并希望两国早日建立外交关系。
    周恩来总理曾在9月22日和10月14日两次收到凯塔总理的电报。凯塔总理在电报中通知马里共和国为独立自主的国家，和决定承认中华人民共和国，并希望两国建立外交关系。
    来往电报的全文如下：
    周恩来总理致凯塔总理的电报
    巴马科马里共和国总理莫迪博·凯塔阁下：
    我非常高兴地收到了阁下1960年9月22日和1960年10月14日的来电。我谨代表中华人民共和国人民和政府，并且以我个人的名义，衷心感谢马里共和国政府对我国的承认。中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅已将中国政府关于承认马里共和国为独立自主的国家的决定通知了阁下。我热烈地祝贺我们两国的相互承认，祝我们两国人民的友好关系走上一个新的阶段，祝马里共和国政府和人民在反对帝国主义和维护民族独立的斗争中取得新的胜利。
    我并且希望贵我两国之间能够早日建立外交关系。我深信，我们两国之间的友好关系，必将日益发展和巩固。
    顺致最崇高的敬意。
  中华人民共和国国务院总理
                        周恩来
          1960年10月17日于北京
    陈毅外长致凯塔总理的电报
    巴马科马里共和国总理莫迪博·凯塔阁下：
    我荣幸地代表中华人民共和国政府通知阁下：中国政府高兴地收到阁下1960年9月22日关于立法议会宣布马里共和国为独立自主的国家以及1960年10月14日关于马里共和国政府承认中华人民共和国的来电。中国政府决定承认马里共和国为独立自主的国家。中国政府希望能够在互相尊重主权和领土完整的基础上同马里共和国建立外交关系，并且建议通过适当的外交途径商谈贵我两国的建交问题。我深信，在贵我两国相互承认以后，中马两国人民的友谊必将获得进一步的发展。
    中华人民共和国外交部长
                      陈毅
                1960年10月17日
    凯塔总理10月14日致周恩来总理的电报
    北京
    中华人民共和国总理阁下：
    我荣幸地通知阁下，马里共和国政府承认中华人民共和国为独立自主的国家。马里人民和马里政府以及我本人借此愉快的时机向中国人民和中国政府以及阁下表示热烈的祝贺。马里政府如果能在卓有成效的合作和诚挚的友谊的基础上与人民中国政府建立外交关系将感到高兴。我祝中华人民共和国击败帝国主义的一切阴谋，并祝中华人民共和国在世界和平中全国幸福繁荣。
    顺致最崇高的敬意。
                莫迪博·凯塔
        1960年10月14日于库鲁巴
    凯塔总理9月22日致周恩来总理的电报
    北京
    中华人民共和国总理阁下：
    我荣幸地通知阁下和中国人民，立法议会已于今日（22日）宣布马里共和国为独立自主的国家。因此，苏丹共和国已取名为马里共和国。我向你保证，马里人民和马里政府愿意根据他们博爱与和平的理想，同世界各国人民保持友好的关系。
    顺致崇高的敬意。
        马里共和国政府总理
                莫迪博·凯塔
        1960年9月22日于巴马科
