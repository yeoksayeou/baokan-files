Title: General Ne Win Arrives in Shanghai; Shanghai Municipal People's Committee Hosts Banquet for Distinguished Burmese Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-10-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 7th: General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces of the Union of Burma, and his wife, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, arrived in Shanghai this afternoon from Suzhou by special train. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the station by Chen Pixian, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Liu Shuzhou, Alternate Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng, Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and other leaders, as well as thousands of people.

　　Today, the Shanghai Railway Station was filled with colorful flags and flowers. The national flags of China and Burma fluttered in the wind, and a huge banner reading "Welcome General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces of the Union of Burma" was hung on the platform, making the station look solemn and beautiful. At 5:18 p.m., as the train pulled into the station, General Ne Win stepped out of the carriage amidst warm applause and slogans. At this moment, Comrade Chen Pixian and others stepped forward and warmly shook hands with the distinguished guests one by one. A group of Young Pioneers presented flowers to General Ne Win and all the distinguished guests. Afterwards, General Ne Win met with people from all walks of life in Shanghai who came to welcome him, including: Shanghai Deputy Mayors Song Jiwen and Zhao Zukang, leaders of the stationed troops in Shanghai Lieutenant General Zhou Renjie and Major General Jiang Tengjiao, Vice Chairmen of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Chen Wangdao, Hu Juewen, and Liu Jingji, as well as heads of various departments of the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, heads of various people's organizations and local organizations of democratic parties, directors of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association in Shanghai, and people who had visited Burma.

　　As the distinguished guests passed by, the band played welcoming music, and the welcoming crowd beat gongs and drums, enthusiastically waving colorful flags, ribbons, and bouquets, shouting slogans such as "Long live Sino-Burmese friendship!" and "Long live peace in Asia and the world!" A pair of young women presented bouquets of flowers to General Ne Win and his wife. General Ne Win smiled and waved to the welcoming crowd.

　　Consuls and vice-consuls of various countries stationed in Shanghai also went to the station today to welcome them.

　　General Ne Win and his wife arrived in Shanghai from Suzhou. Today, the distinguished guests visited the Suzhou Arts and Crafts Embroidery Research Institute and toured scenic spots such as Tiger Hill and the Humble Administrator's Garden in Suzhou.

　According to Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 7th: On the evening of the 7th, the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee held a banquet at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Building to warmly welcome General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces of the Union of Burma, and his wife. Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, who accompanied the distinguished guests, also attended the banquet.

　　The brightly lit Sino-Soviet Friendship Building was filled with the "pauk-phaw" friendship between the peoples of China and Burma. Chen Pixian, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Liu Shuzhou, Alternate Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, and his wife, Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng, Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and other leaders of the Shanghai municipal party, government, and military, along with General Ne Win and his wife, toasted each other, warmly congratulating the signing of the Sino-Burmese Border Treaty and celebrating the entry of the friendly relations between the peoples of China and Burma into a new stage.

　　Secretary Chen Pixian spoke at the banquet. On behalf of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, and the people of Shanghai, he warmly welcomed the Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces of the Union of Burma, General Ne Win, and his wife, and other distinguished Burmese guests to visit Shanghai.

　　Comrade Chen Pixian expressed his heartfelt thanks and high respect to General Ne Win, who had made valuable contributions to the construction of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Building and to the enhancement of Sino-Burmese friendship. Comrade Chen Pixian said that the signing of the Sino-Burmese Border Treaty has opened a new page in the history of Sino-Burmese friendly relations and has set a good example for Asian countries to coexist peacefully on the basis of the Five Principles.

　　General Ne Win spoke at the banquet. On behalf of all the distinguished guests, he expressed his gratitude to the people of Shanghai for their warm welcome. He said that he came to China to participate in several great happy events. The first happy event is that the Sino-Burmese border issue has been resolved to the satisfaction of both parties. The signing of the Sino-Burmese Border Treaty not only completely resolved the Sino-Burmese border issue, but also contributed to the long-standing Sino-Burmese friendship. General Ne Win said that the second happy event I want to mention is that the Union of Burma sent more than 300 Burmese friends to participate in the grand celebration of the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The third happy event is that I have the opportunity to see China's great achievements, which are vastly different from what I saw five years ago.

　　The speeches of Comrade Chen Pixian and General Ne Win were warmly applauded by the audience.

　　Also present at the banquet were Shanghai Deputy Mayors Song Jiwen and Zhao Zukang, leaders of the stationed troops in Shanghai Lieutenant General Zhou Renjie and Major General Jiang Tengjiao, Vice Chairmen of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Chen Wangdao, Hu Juewen, and Liu Jingji, as well as heads of various departments of the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, heads of various people's organizations and local organizations of democratic parties, directors of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association in Shanghai, and people who had visited Burma.

　　Consuls and vice-consuls of various countries stationed in Shanghai also attended the banquet.

　　After the banquet, the distinguished guests attended a cultural performance.

### 奈温将军到达上海  上海市人民委员会欢宴缅甸贵宾

1960-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

    奈温将军到达上海
    上海市人民委员会欢宴缅甸贵宾
    新华社上海7日电　缅甸联邦国防军总参谋长奈温将军和夫人以及随行人员，由陈毅副总理和夫人陪同，今天下午从苏州乘专车到达上海。贵宾们在车站上受到了中共上海市委书记处书记陈丕显，中共上海市委书记处候补书记、上海市副市长刘述周，中国人民解放军上海警备区司令员王必成中将等负责人和数千群众的热烈欢迎。
    今天，上海车站上布满着各色彩旗和鲜花，中缅两国国旗迎风招展，月台上挂着“欢迎缅甸联邦国防军总参谋长奈温将军”的巨大横幅，车站打扮得庄严而美丽。下午五时十八分，火车靠站后，奈温将军在一片热烈的掌声和口号声中走出了车厢。这时，陈丕显同志等迎上前去，和贵宾们一一亲切握手，一群少先队员向奈温将军和全体贵宾献了鲜花。接着，奈温将军和前来欢迎的上海各方面人士会见，他们是：上海市副市长宋季文、赵祖康，驻沪部队首长周人杰中将、江腾蛟少将，政协上海市委员会副主席陈望道、胡厥文、刘靖基，以及上海市人民委员会各部门负责人，各人民团体、各民主党派地方组织的负责人，中缅友好协会在沪理事和曾经访问过缅甸的人士。
    当贵宾们走过的时候，乐队奏起了欢迎乐曲，欢迎的人群敲锣打鼓，热情地挥舞着彩旗、彩巾和花束，高呼“中缅友好万岁！”“亚洲和世界和平万岁！”等口号。一对对女青年把一束束鲜花献给奈温将军和夫人。奈温将军笑容满面，不停地向欢迎群众挥手致意。
    各国驻上海领事、副领事今天也到车站欢迎。
    奈温将军和夫人是由苏州到达上海的。贵宾们今天在苏州参观了苏州工艺美术刺绣研究所，游览了虎丘、拙政园等名胜。
  据新华社上海7日电　7日晚，上海市人民委员会在中苏友好大厦举行宴会，热烈欢迎缅甸联邦国防军总参谋长奈温将军和夫人。陪同贵宾前来的陈毅副总理和夫人也出席了宴会。
    灯火辉煌的中苏友好大厦，充满了中缅两国人民“胞波”般的情谊。中共上海市委书记处书记陈丕显，中共上海市委书记处候补书记、上海市副市长刘述周和夫人，中国人民解放军上海警备区司令员王必成中将等上海市党政军领导人同奈温将军和夫人一起，互相敬酒，热烈祝贺中缅边界条约的签订，祝贺中缅两国人民的友好关系进入新的阶段。
    陈丕显书记在宴会上讲了话。他代表中共上海市委员会，上海市人民委员会和上海人民热烈欢迎亲戚般的邻邦——缅甸联邦的国防军总参谋长奈温将军和夫人以及其他缅甸贵宾们来上海访问。
    陈丕显同志对曾经为建立中缅友好大厦、为增进中缅友谊作出过宝贵贡献的奈温将军，表示衷心的感谢和崇高的敬意。陈丕显同志说，中缅边界条约的签订，在中缅友好关系史上开始了新的一页，为亚洲国家在五项原则的基础上和平共处作出了良好的榜样。
    奈温将军在宴会上讲了话。他代表全体贵宾向全上海人民对他们的热烈欢迎表示感谢。他说，我来到中国参加了几件大喜事。第一件喜事就是双方满意地解决了中缅边界问题。中缅边界条约的签订，不仅彻底解决了中缅边界问题，同时也为久已存在的中缅友谊作出了贡献。奈温将军说，我要讲的第二件喜事是缅甸联邦派出了三百多位缅甸朋友参加了中华人民共和国建国十一周年盛典。第三件喜事就是我有机会看到中国的伟大成就，这些成就和我在五年前所看到的已经大大不同了。
    陈丕显同志和奈温将军的讲话，受到全场的热烈鼓掌欢迎。
    出席宴会的，还有上海市副市长宋季文、赵祖康，驻沪部队首长周人杰中将、江腾蛟少将，政协上海市委员会副主席陈望道、胡厥文、刘靖基，以及上海市人民委员会各部门负责人，上海市各人民团体、各民主党派地方组织的负责人，中缅友好协会在沪理事和曾经访问过缅甸的人士。
    各国驻上海领事、副领事也出席了宴会。
    宴会后，贵宾们出席了文艺晚会。
