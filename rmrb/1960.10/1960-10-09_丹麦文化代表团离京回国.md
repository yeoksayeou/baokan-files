Title: Danish Cultural Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-10-09
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 7th - The Danish cultural delegation, which visited our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, concluded their visit and left Beijing today for home via Moscow.

### 丹麦文化代表团离京回国

1960-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    丹麦文化代表团离京回国
　　新华社7日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的丹麦文化代表团，结束了在我国的访问，今天离开北京经莫斯科回国。
