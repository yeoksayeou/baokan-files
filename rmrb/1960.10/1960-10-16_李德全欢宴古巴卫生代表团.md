Title: Li Dequan Hosts Banquet for Cuban Health Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-16
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 15th: Minister of Public Health Li Dequan held a banquet this evening to warmly welcome the Cuban Health Delegation headed by Cuban Minister of Public Health José Ramón Machado Ventura.

    In her speech at the banquet, Li Dequan praised the heroic struggle of the Cuban people against the interference and aggression of U.S. imperialism. She stated that the Chinese people and medical and health circles will always stand on the side of the Cuban people and resolutely support the just struggle of the Cuban people.

    Machado said at the banquet that the Cuban people greatly admire the victory of the Chinese people's revolution. Our feelings with the Chinese people have deepened because our two countries have established formal diplomatic relations. We hope that the friendly relations between Cuba and China will achieve further development in political, economic, scientific, and cultural fields.

    Among those present at the banquet were Fu Lianzhang, President of the Chinese Medical Association, Qian Xinzhong and Cui Yitian, Vice Ministers of Public Health, and Huang Jiasi, President of the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences.

    The Cuban Health Delegation, headed by Minister Machado, consisting of four members, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 14th at the invitation of Minister of Public Health Li Dequan for a visit to China. They will visit Beijing, Shanghai, and other places.

### 李德全欢宴古巴卫生代表团

1960-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    李德全欢宴古巴卫生代表团
    新华社15日讯　卫生部部长李德全今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎以古巴卫生部长何塞·罗曼·马查多·本图拉为首的古巴卫生代表团。
    李德全在宴会上讲话，她赞扬了古巴人民为反对美帝国主义的干涉和侵略所进行的英勇的斗争。她表示，中国人民和医药卫生界人士将永远站在古巴人民这一边，坚决支持古巴人民的正义斗争。
    马查多在宴会上说，古巴人民对中国人民革命的取得胜利非常钦佩。我们和中国人民的感情加深了，因为我们两国建立了正式的外交关系。我们希望古中两国之间的友好关系在政治、经济、科学、文化等方面取得进一步的发展。
    出席宴会作陪的，有中华医学会会长傅连暲，卫生部副部长钱信忠、崔义田，中国医学科学院院长黄家驷等。
    以马查多部长为首的古巴卫生代表团一行四人，是应卫生部部长李德全的邀请来我国访问，在14日乘飞机到达北京的，他们将在北京和上海等地进行参观访问。
