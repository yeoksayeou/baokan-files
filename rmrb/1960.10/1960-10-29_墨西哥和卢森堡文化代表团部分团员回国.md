Title: Some Members of Mexican and Luxembourg Cultural Delegations Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th: Some members of the Mexican cultural delegation, Enrique Cabrera and his wife, Esther Chapa, Joaquín Ramírez, Carlos Laguna, and Víctor Flores, as well as members of the Luxembourg cultural delegation, Mr. and Mrs. Auban, concluded their visit to China and left Beijing by plane today and yesterday respectively to return home.

### 墨西哥和卢森堡文化代表团部分团员回国

1960-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    墨西哥和卢森堡文化代表团部分团员回国
    新华社28日讯　墨西哥文化代表团部分团员恩利盖·卡勃来拉夫妇、埃丝代尔·恰巴、霍阿金·拉米雷斯、卡洛斯·拉古纳和维多·弗洛莱斯等，以及卢森堡文化代表团团员欧班尼夫妇，结束了在中国的访问，分别在今天和昨天乘飞机离开北京回国。
