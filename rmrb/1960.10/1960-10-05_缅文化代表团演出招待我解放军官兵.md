Title: Burmese Cultural Delegation Performs for Officers and Soldiers of Our People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1960-10-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 4th: The Burmese Cultural Delegation, currently visiting our country, held a special performance tonight at the Capital Theater for leading organs of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and troops stationed in Beijing. The broad masses of officers and soldiers of the PLA who attended the performance expressed warm welcome to the Burmese artists who brought the profound friendship of the Burmese people.

General Luo Ruiqing, Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and General Aung Gyi, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Burmese Armed Forces, watched tonight's performance.

Also watching the performance tonight were General Xiao Hua, General Gan Siqi, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Tang Tianji, Vice Admiral Zhou Xihan, Lieutenant General Wang Huiqiu, Lieutenant General Yuan Shengping, Major General Jia Ruoyu, Senior Colonel Li Wei, Senior Colonel Yin Chisheng, as well as officers and soldiers of the PLA's ground, naval, and air forces stationed in Beijing.

The performance began at 8 p.m. As the crimson curtain slowly opened, the stage revealed the Huabiao in front of Tiananmen Square in Beijing and the Independence Monument in Yangon. In front of the Huabiao and the Independence Monument, Burmese singers sang "Song of Burmese-Chinese Friendship" and "Chinese-Burmese Friendship Song" in both Chinese and Burmese. Their moving voices singing "The Chinese-Burmese border connects to the border, both friends and relatives" won enthusiastic applause from the audience again and again. The audience felt particularly warm when they saw their close neighbors and friends perform "Tea Picking and Butterfly Catching Dance," "Weaving Net Girl," and sing songs like "The Sun Rises on the Grassland Never Setting," "The Shepherd Girl," and "Ode to the Motherland" in Chinese.

During the performance, the Burmese artists performed beautiful Burmese classical dances: "Burmese Classical Dance," "Long Drum Dance," as well as "Modern Pas de Deux" and the dance drama "Sandakyinna Yi," which retain the legacy of classical dance. They also performed folk dances of the Shan and Jingpo ethnic groups, and the dance drama "The Story of the Irrawaddy River," which reflects the history of the Burmese people's struggle.

During the intermission, General Luo Ruiqing met with the artists of the Burmese Cultural Delegation. At the end of the performance, members of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Ground, Naval, and Air Force Song and Dance Troupe presented flower baskets to the artists. General Luo Ruiqing, General Xiao Hua, General Gan Siqi, General Aung Gyi and others went on stage and shook hands with the artists one by one.

### 缅文化代表团演出招待我解放军官兵

1960-10-05
第4版()
专栏：

    缅文化代表团演出招待我解放军官兵
    据新华社4日讯　来我国访问的缅甸文化代表团，今晚在首都剧场为中国人民解放军领导机关和驻京部队举行招待演出。观看演出的解放军广大官兵，对带来缅甸人民深厚友谊的缅甸艺术家们，表示了热烈的欢迎。
    中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿大将和缅甸国防军副总参谋长昂季将军，看了今晚的演出。
    今晚看演出的还有萧华上将、甘泗淇上将、萧向荣中将、唐天际中将、周希汉海军中将、王辉球空军中将、袁升平中将、贾若瑜少将、李伟大校、尹痴生大校以及解放军驻京陆海空军部队的军官和士兵。
    演出于今晚八时开始。绛紫色的幕布徐徐拉开后，舞台上出现了北京天安门前的华表和仰光的独立塔。在华表和独立塔前，缅甸的歌唱家们用中、缅两国语言唱了“缅中友谊之歌”和“中缅友谊之歌”，他们唱的“中缅边境连边境，又是朋友又是亲”的动人的歌声，赢得了观众一次再次的热烈鼓掌。当观众看到亲密的邻居和朋友演出“采茶扑蝶舞”、“织网姑娘”，以及用中国话歌唱“草原上升起不落的太阳”、“牧羊姑娘”和“歌唱祖国”等歌曲时，感到特别亲切。
    在演出晚会上，缅甸艺术家演出了优美的缅甸古典舞蹈：“缅甸古典舞”、“长鼓舞”，以及保持着古典舞蹈遗风的“现代双人舞”和舞剧“珊达京娜意”。还演出了掸族、景颇族的民间舞蹈，和反映缅甸人民斗争历史的舞剧“伊洛瓦底江的故事”。
    演出中间休息时，罗瑞卿大将接见了缅甸文化代表团的艺术家们。演出结束时，中国人民解放军陆海空军文工团的团员向艺术家们献了花篮。罗瑞卿大将、萧华上将、甘泗淇上将和昂季将军等走上舞台和艺术家们一一握手。
