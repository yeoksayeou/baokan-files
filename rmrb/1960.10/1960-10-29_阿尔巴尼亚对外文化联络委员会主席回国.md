Title: Albanian Chairman of the Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations Returns Home

Author:
Date: 1960-10-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th: The Chairman of the Albanian Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations, Miho Treska, who was invited to visit our country, concluded his visit and left Beijing by plane this morning to return home. Zhang Zhixiang, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet and European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, were at the airport to see him off. Albanian Ambassador to China, P. Parifti, and Secretary-General of the Albanian Writers and Artists Association, Musaraj, were also at the airport to see him off.

### 阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会主席回国

1960-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会主席回国
    据新华社27日讯　应邀来我国访问的阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会主席米·特雷斯卡，结束了在我国的访问，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。前往机场欢送的，有我国对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥，外交部苏欧司副司长陈伯清等。阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使帕里夫蒂、阿尔巴尼亚作家艺术家协会秘书长穆萨莱等也到机场送行。
