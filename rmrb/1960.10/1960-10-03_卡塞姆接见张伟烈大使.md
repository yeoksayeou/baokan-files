Title: Kassem Receives Ambassador Zhang Weilie
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, 30th — Iraqi Prime Minister Kassem, when receiving Chinese Ambassador Zhang Weilie on the afternoon of September 30th, asked Ambassador Zhang Weilie to convey his congratulations on the Chinese National Day to the leaders of the Chinese government.
    During the meeting, Prime Minister Kassem had a cordial conversation with Ambassador Zhang Weilie.

### 卡塞姆接见张伟烈大使

1960-10-03
第3版()
专栏：

    卡塞姆接见张伟烈大使
    新华社巴格达30日电　伊拉克总理卡塞姆在9月30日下午接见中国大使张伟烈时，请张伟烈大使向中国政府领导人转达他对中国国庆的祝贺。
    接见时，卡塞姆总理同张伟烈大使进行了亲切的谈话。
