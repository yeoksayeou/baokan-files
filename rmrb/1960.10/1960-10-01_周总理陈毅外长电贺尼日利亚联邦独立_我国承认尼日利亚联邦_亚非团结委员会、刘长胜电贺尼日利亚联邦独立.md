Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Foreign Minister Chen Yi Send Telegrams to Congratulate Nigeria's Federal Independence; China Recognizes the Federation of Nigeria; Asian-African Solidarity Committee and Liu Changsheng Send Telegrams to Congratulate Nigeria's Federal Independence
Author:
Date: 1960-10-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 30th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram today to Abubakar Tafawa Balewa, Prime Minister of the Federation of Nigeria, congratulating the proclamation of independence of the Federation of Nigeria. The telegram reads as follows:
To His Excellency Abubakar Tafawa Balewa, Prime Minister of the Federation of Nigeria, Lagos, Nigeria:
	On the occasion of the proclamation of independence of the Federation of Nigeria, I extend my sincere congratulations to the government and people of the Federation of Nigeria on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China. I wish the Nigerian people further achievements in the struggle against imperialism and colonialism and in adhering to the path of national independence and development. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness.
Zhou Enlai
Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
September 30, 1960, in Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, September 30th: Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram today to Abubakar Tafawa Balewa, Prime Minister of the Federation of Nigeria, congratulating the proclamation of independence of the Federation of Nigeria and informing him that the Chinese government has decided to recognize the Federation of Nigeria. The telegram reads as follows:
To His Excellency Abubakar Tafawa Balewa, Prime Minister of the Federation of Nigeria, Lagos, Nigeria:
	I am delighted to learn of the proclamation of independence of the Federation of Nigeria, and I extend my heartfelt congratulations to the government of the Federation of Nigeria on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China. It is my honor to inform Your Excellency that the government of the People's Republic of China have decided to recognize the Federation of Nigeria. I hope that our government's recognition of your country will lead to the further development of relations between our two countries.
Chen Yi
Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
September 30, 1960, in Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, September 30th: The Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee sent a telegram on the 29th to Abubakar Tafawa Balewa, Prime Minister of the Federation of Nigeria, congratulating Nigeria on its declaration of independence.
	The telegram said: "Hearing the news of Nigeria's declaration of independence, the Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee extends warm congratulations to the people of your country.
	“Nigeria's declaration of independence is the result of the Nigerian people's long-term struggle against imperialism, colonialism, and for national independence. The Chinese people have always deeply sympathized with and firmly supported the just struggle of the Nigerian people and the African people to win and safeguard national independence. We congratulate the Nigerian people on their continuous new victories in the lofty cause of consolidating independence, developing the national economy, and further eliminating the aggressive forces of colonialism.
	"We wish the friendship between the people of China and Nigeria will develop day by day."

Xinhua News Agency, September 30th: Liu Changsheng, President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, sent a telegram on the 29th to Abubakar Tafawa Balewa, Prime Minister of the Federation of Nigeria, congratulating Nigeria on its declaration of independence.
	The telegram said: "On the occasion of your country's independence, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency and, through Your Excellency, to the people of your country on behalf of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association!
	"Your country's independence is the result of a long struggle by your people. The 650 million Chinese people firmly support your country's just struggle against colonialism and for national independence, and congratulate your country's people on their continuous new victories in this struggle!
	"I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness!
	"May the friendly relations between the Chinese and Nigerian people develop!"

### 周总理陈毅外长电贺尼日利亚联邦独立  我国承认尼日利亚联邦  亚非团结委员会、刘长胜电贺尼日利亚联邦独立

1960-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    周总理陈毅外长电贺尼日利亚联邦独立
    我国承认尼日利亚联邦
    亚非团结委员会、刘长胜电贺尼日利亚联邦独立
    新华社30日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给尼日利亚联邦总理阿布巴卡尔·塔法瓦·巴勒瓦，祝贺尼日利亚联邦宣告独立。电文如下：尼日利亚　拉各斯尼日利亚联邦　阿布巴卡尔·塔法瓦·巴勒瓦总理阁下：
    值此尼日利亚联邦宣告独立之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民，向尼日利亚联邦政府和人民致以诚挚的祝贺。祝尼日利亚人民在反对帝国主义和殖民主义、坚持民族独立发展道路的斗争中取得进一步的成就。祝贵国国家繁荣，人民幸福。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来
      1960年9月30日于北京
    新华社30日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅今天打电报给尼日利亚联邦总理阿布巴卡尔·塔法瓦·巴勒瓦，祝贺尼日利亚联邦宣告独立，并通知他，中国政府决定承认尼日利亚联邦。电文如下：尼日利亚　拉各斯尼日利亚联邦　阿布巴卡尔·塔法瓦·巴勒瓦总理阁下：
    欣闻尼日利亚联邦宣告独立，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府向尼日利亚联邦政府致以衷心的祝贺。我荣幸地通知阁下，中华人民共和国政府已经决定承认尼日利亚联邦。我希望，我国政府对贵国的承认将导致我们两国关系的进一步发展。
  中华人民共和国外交部长　陈毅
          1960年9月30日于北京
    新华社30日讯　中国亚非团结委员会29日打电报给尼日利亚联邦总理阿布巴卡尔·塔法瓦·巴勒瓦，祝贺尼日利亚宣布独立。
    电报说：“欣闻尼日利亚宣布独立，中国亚非团结委员会谨向贵国人民致以热烈的祝贺。
      “尼日利亚宣布独立，是尼日利亚人民长期反对帝国主义、殖民主义和争取民族独立的斗争的结果。中国人民一贯深切同情和坚决支持尼日利亚人民和非洲人民争取和维护民族独立的正义斗争。我们祝贺尼日利亚人民在巩固独立、发展民族经济和进一步消除殖民主义侵略势力的崇高事业中，不断取得新的胜利。
    “祝中国和尼日利亚两国人民的友谊日益发展。”
    新华社30日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会会长刘长胜29日打电报给尼日利亚联邦总理阿布巴卡尔·塔法瓦·巴勒瓦，祝贺尼日利亚宣布独立。
    电报说：“值此贵国独立之际，我谨代表中国非洲人民友好协会向阁下，并通过阁下向贵国人民表示热烈的祝贺！
    “贵国的独立是贵国人民进行长期斗争的结果。六亿五千万中国人民坚决支持贵国人民反对殖民主义、争取民族独立的正义斗争，并祝贺贵国人民在这一斗争中不断取得新的胜利！
      “祝贵国繁荣昌盛，人民幸福！
    “愿中尼两国人民的友好关系获得发展！”
