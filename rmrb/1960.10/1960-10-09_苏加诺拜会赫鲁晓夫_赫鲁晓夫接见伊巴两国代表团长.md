Title: Sukarno Meets Khrushchev; Khrushchev Receives Heads of Iranian and Pakistani Delegations
Author:
Date: 1960-10-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 7th - According to TASS, Indonesian President Sukarno paid a farewell visit to Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev on the afternoon of the 6th before returning to his country.

During the friendly and frank talks, Khrushchev and Sukarno exchanged views on a series of important issues of common interest to both countries.

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 8th - According to TASS, Khrushchev, head of the Soviet delegation to the Fifteenth Session of the UN General Assembly and Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, received Iranian Foreign Minister Ardalan, head of the Iranian delegation, at the Soviet Mission to the United Nations on the morning of the 7th, at Ardalan's request. The two sides held talks on issues of interest to both the Soviet and Iranian governments. Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko was present at the meeting.

Khrushchev also received Bhutto, head of the Pakistani delegation, on the 5th at Bhutto's request.

In addition, Khrushchev attended a banquet hosted by Indian Prime Minister Nehru in his honor on the 6th.

### 苏加诺拜会赫鲁晓夫  赫鲁晓夫接见伊巴两国代表团长

1960-10-09
第6版()
专栏：

    苏加诺拜会赫鲁晓夫
    赫鲁晓夫接见伊巴两国代表团长
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社纽约讯：印度尼西亚总统苏加诺在回国前于6日下午拜会了苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫。
    赫鲁晓夫和苏加诺在友好、坦率的会谈中就两国共同感兴趣的一系列重要问题交换了意见。
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社纽约讯：出席联合国大会第十五届会议的苏联代表团团长、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫，应伊朗代表团团长、伊朗外交大臣阿奏迪的请求，于7日上午在苏联驻联合国代表处接见了他。双方就苏联政府和伊朗政府都感兴趣的问题进行了会谈，会谈时在座的有苏联外交部长葛罗米柯。
    赫鲁晓夫还应巴基斯坦代表团团长巴托的要求，在5日接见了巴托。
    此外，赫鲁晓夫还在6日出席了印度总理尼赫鲁为他而设的宴会。
