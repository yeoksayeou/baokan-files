Title: Guo Moruo Receives Inoue Kiyoshi
Author:
Date: 1960-10-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 8th - Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, received Japanese historian Inoue Kiyoshi on the 7th. Present at the meeting was Liu Danian, Deputy Director of the Institute of Modern History of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and others.

### 郭沫若接见井上清

1960-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见井上清
    新华社8日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若在7日接见日本历史学家井上清。接见时，在座的有中国科学院近代史研究所副所长刘大年等。
