Title: Liu Changsheng and Others Leave for Australia
Author:
Date: 1960-10-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 7th — At the invitation of the Australian Council of Trade Unions, the three-member delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, led by Liu Changsheng, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, left Beijing on the 7th for a friendly visit to Australia.  Those who saw them off at the airport included Li Jiebo, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Yang Zhihua, member of the Presidium of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others.

### 刘长胜等前往澳大利亚

1960-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    刘长胜等前往澳大利亚
    新华社7日讯　应澳大利亚工会理事会的邀请，前往澳大利亚进行友好访问的中华全国总工会代表团一行三人，在中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜率领下，在7日离开北京。到机场欢送的，有中华全国总工会副主席李颉伯，中华全国总工会主席团委员杨之华等人。
