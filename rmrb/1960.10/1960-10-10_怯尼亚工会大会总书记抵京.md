Title: Secretary-General of the Kenya Federation of Labor Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 9th: Mr. Ochwada, Secretary-General of the Kenya Federation of Labor and Secretary-General of the East African Building Trades Union, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 怯尼亚工会大会总书记抵京

1960-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    怯尼亚工会大会总书记抵京
    新华社9日讯　怯尼亚工会大会总书记兼东非建筑工会总书记奥奇瓦达，应中华全国总工会邀请，在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
