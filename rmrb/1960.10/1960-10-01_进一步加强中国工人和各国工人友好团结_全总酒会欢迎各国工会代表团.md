Title: Further Strengthen the Friendly Unity of Chinese Workers and Workers of All Countries: ACFTU Reception Welcomes Trade Union Delegations from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-10-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 30th — The All-China Federation of Trade Unions (ACFTU) held a reception this afternoon to warmly welcome trade union delegations and trade union representatives from five continents visiting China, and celebrated the National Day of the People's Republic of China with the guests.

    The reception was presided over by Li Jiebo, Vice Chairman of the ACFTU, who extended a warm welcome to the guests on behalf of all Chinese workers and the ACFTU. He said: Your visit to China is a testament to the friendship and support of workers from all over the world for our country. Through your visit, the friendly relations and mutual solidarity between Chinese workers and workers of all countries will be further strengthened. The Chinese workers and the Chinese people will always be loyal to the cause of the world working class for liberation, loyal to the cause of liberation for all oppressed nations, and will always stand at the forefront of the struggle against the imperialist war policy, the policy of aggression, and in defense of world peace. The Chinese people will forever stand on the same front with the people of all countries, encourage each other, support each other, and fight to the end against our common enemy, U.S. imperialism. Li Jiebo proposed a toast to the solidarity of the working people of the world and to the victory of the cause of liberation of the working class of the world.

    Among the foreign guests attending today's reception were: the Japanese National Railway Workers' Union delegation headed by Soichi Hosoi, the Third China Visit Delegation of the Japan Private Railway Trade Unions Confederation headed by Hiroshi Sato, the Japanese Coal Miners' Union delegation headed by Reijiro Takagaki, the Japanese Port Workers' China Visit Delegation headed by Takeo Igarashi, the Indonesian Farmers' Front and Village Government Officials Union delegation headed by Ismail, Indonesian Customs Union representative Chasmati, the Cameroon General Confederation of Labor delegation headed by Ekwalla Robert, the Argentine Trade Union delegation headed by Juan Iapichino, the Belgian Union of Employees, Technicians and Executives delegation headed by Oscar Leclercq, the Canadian United Electrical, Radio and Machine Workers of America delegation headed by Ross Russell, and William Clifford Loftus, Assistant Secretary of the Hotel Industry Union of New South Wales, Australia. Liu Changsheng, Zhu Xuefan, Xu Zhizhen, Chen Shaomin, Vice Chairmen of the ACFTU, Li Zaiwen, Zhang Xiuzhu, Kang Yonghe, Secretaries of the Secretariat, and heads of various departments of the ACFTU, various industrial unions, and the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions also attended the reception.

### 进一步加强中国工人和各国工人友好团结  全总酒会欢迎各国工会代表团

1960-10-01
第5版()
专栏：

    进一步加强中国工人和各国工人友好团结
    全总酒会欢迎各国工会代表团
    据新华社30日讯　中华全国总工会今天下午举行酒会，热烈欢迎来自世界五大洲的各国工会访华代表团和工会代表，和客人们一齐欢庆中华人民共和国国庆。
    酒会由中华全国总工会副主席李颉伯主持，他代表中国全体工人和中华全国总工会向客人们表示热烈的欢迎。他说：你们在中国的访问，正是表示世界各国工人对我国的友谊和支持，通过你们的访问，将会进一步加强我国工人和各国工人之间的友好关系和相互团结。中国工人和中国人民永远忠实于世界工人阶级争取解放的事业，忠实于一切被压迫民族争取解放的事业，并且始终站在反对帝国主义战争政策、侵略政策和保卫世界和平的斗争的最前列。中国人民将永远同各国人民站在一条战线上，互相鼓舞，互相支持，为反对我们的共同敌人美帝国主义而斗争到底。李颉伯提议为全世界劳动人民的团结，为全世界工人阶级争取解放的事业的胜利干杯。
    出席今天酒会的外宾有：以细井宗一为首的日本国营铁道工会代表团，以佐藤弘为首的日本私营铁路工会总联合会第三次访华代表团，以高垣礼二郎为首的日本煤矿工会代表团，以五十岚武雄为首的日本港湾工人访华代表团，以伊斯梅尔为首的印度尼西亚农民阵线及乡村公署人员联合会代表团，印度尼西亚关税工会代表查斯玛蒂，以埃克瓦拉·罗贝尔为首的喀麦隆劳工总联合会代表团，以胡安·伊阿皮奇诺为首的阿根廷工会代表团，以奥斯卡·勒克莱克为首的比利时职员、技术员和干部工会代表团，以罗斯·鲁塞尔为首的加拿大联合电气、无线电、机械工人工会代表团，以及澳大利亚新南威尔士州旅馆业工会助理书记威廉斯·克利福德·劳夫塔斯。中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜、朱学范、许之桢、陈少敏，书记处书记栗再温、张修竹、康永和以及全国总工会各部、各产业工会和北京市总工会的负责人等，都出席了酒会。
