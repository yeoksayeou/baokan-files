Title: Japanese Communist Party Ranks Swell: Party Membership More Than Doubles in Twenty-Three Prefectures
Author:
Date: 1960-10-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 11th - According to the "Akahata" newspaper, the ranks of the Japanese Communist Party are continuously growing, with party membership more than doubling in twenty-three prefectures.

　　The communist party organizations in Oita and Yamanashi prefectures respectively completed their goals of doubling party membership on the 9th and 11th. Prior to this, communist party members in Tokushima prefecture and twenty other prefectures had also doubled.

　　The Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party sent telegrams to the party organizations of these prefectures, congratulating them on their achievements in developing the party's strength.

### 日共队伍不断壮大  党员人数在二十三个县增加一倍多

1960-10-12
第5版()
专栏：

    日共队伍不断壮大
    党员人数在二十三个县增加一倍多
    新华社11日讯　东京消息：据“赤旗报”报道，日本共产党的队伍不断壮大，在二十三个县里党员的数目增加了一倍以上。
    大分县和山梨县的共产党组织分别在9日和11日完成了党员增加一倍的目标。在这以前，德岛县和其他二十个县的共产党员也增加了一倍。
    日共中央分别致电这些县的党组织，祝贺它们在发展党的力量方面取得的成就。
