Title: Cuba and Bulgaria Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1960-10-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 7th - The governments of Cuba and Bulgaria today reached an agreement on establishing diplomatic relations and exchanging diplomatic representatives at the ambassadorial level, and signed a trade agreement, a payment agreement, and a protocol on technical and scientific cooperation.

A joint communiqué from the two countries' foreign ministries, published here, stated that the above agreements were reached "in order to enhance the existing friendly relations between the People's Republic of Bulgaria and the Republic of Cuba."

### 古巴和保加利亚决定建交

1960-10-09
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴和保加利亚决定建交
    据新华社哈瓦那7日电　古巴和保加利亚政府今天就建立外交关系和互派大使级外交代表达成协议，并且签订了贸易协定、支付协定和技术科学合作议定书。
    在此间发表的两国外交部的联合公报说，达成上述协议“是为了增进保加利亚人民共和国和古巴共和国现存的友好关系”。
