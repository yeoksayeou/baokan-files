Title: Further Consolidate and Develop the Friendship Between the Chinese and Korean People, Enhance the Great Unity and Strength of the Socialist Camp; China and North Korea Sign Two Economic Agreements; Premier Zhou Attends the Signing Ceremony and Hosts a Banquet for Vice Premier Li Jooyun
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th - During the visit of the Government Economic Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to our country, it held sincere and friendly talks with our government on the issue of further strengthening economic mutual assistance and cooperation between the two countries, and reached a satisfactory agreement. Today, the representatives of the two governments signed in Beijing the "Agreement on the Loan from the People's Republic of China to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea" and the "Agreement on the Supply of Complete Sets of Equipment and the Provision of Technical Assistance from the People's Republic of China to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea."

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long attended the signing ceremony.

Signing the above documents on behalf of the Chinese side was Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian, and signing on behalf of the Korean side was the head of the Government Economic Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Vice Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Li Jooyun.

According to the provisions of the above two agreements, our government will provide the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea with a long-term loan of 420 million rubles between 1961 and 1964, and assist the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in building rubber tire factories, radio communication equipment factories, and a number of light industrial enterprises producing daily necessities. In addition, the agreement also stipulates that China will supply North Korea with cotton textile equipment, radio equipment, and so on.

The achievements made in this meeting between the government economic delegations of China and North Korea mark the further development and consolidation of the unbreakable and lofty friendship between the people of China and North Korea, which was forged in blood, and are a new contribution made by the governments and people of China and North Korea to enhance the great unity and strength of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

The Chinese side participating in the signing ceremony also included: Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building Duan Junyi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission Song Yangchu, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Chemical Industry Li Su, Vice Minister of the Third Ministry of Machine Building Liu Ding, Vice Minister of Textile Industry Chen Weiji, Vice Minister of Posts and Telecommunications Shen Guang, and Commercial Counselor of our Embassy in North Korea Li Fanru, etc.

The Korean side participating in the signing ceremony included: member of the Government Economic Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Ambassador of North Korea to China Li Yongho, Counselor of the North Korean Embassy in China Ma Dongshan, Commercial Counselor Fang Tailv, as well as accompanying staff of the Korean Government Economic Delegation, etc.

Xinhua News Agency, 13th - Premier Zhou Enlai held a banquet in the Anhui Hall of the Great Hall of the People this evening to entertain Vice Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Li Jooyun, and the members of the Korean Government Economic Delegation he led.

Vice Premiers of the State Council He Long and Li Xiannian attended the banquet.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Li Jooyun spoke successively at the banquet, which was filled with a friendly atmosphere. In their speeches, they expressed congratulations on the "Agreement on the Loan from the People's Republic of China to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea" and the "Agreement on the Supply of Complete Sets of Equipment and the Provision of Technical Assistance from the People's Republic of China to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea" signed by the two governments today. They also pointed out that the people of China and North Korea will always unite, support each other, and help each other in socialist construction and in the struggle against the common enemy, U.S. imperialism.

The Ambassador of North Korea to China, Li Yongho, member of the Government Economic Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and diplomatic officials of the North Korean Embassy in China were invited to attend the banquet.

Also attending the banquet were: Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building Duan Junyi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, as well as responsible persons from relevant departments of the government, Beijing Municipality, and the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, etc.

Xinhua News Agency, 13th - Vice Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Li Jooyun, and the Korean Government Economic Delegation he led, returned to Beijing by special train this morning from Sanmenxia.

Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Ge Buhai, and others greeted them at the station.

Before this, the distinguished Korean guests also visited Shijiazhuang, Zhengzhou, Luoyang and other cities. (Attached picture)

### 进一步巩固发展中朝人民友谊　增强社会主义阵营伟大团结和力量  中朝两国签订两项经济协定  周总理参加签字仪式并欢宴李周渊副首相

1960-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

    进一步巩固发展中朝人民友谊　增强社会主义阵营伟大团结和力量
    中朝两国签订两项经济协定
    周总理参加签字仪式并欢宴李周渊副首相
    新华社13日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府经济代表团在我国访问期间，同我国政府就进一步加强两国经济互助合作问题进行了诚挚、友好的会谈，并且圆满地达成了协议。今天，两国政府代表在北京签订了“关于中华人民共和国贷款给朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的协定”和“关于中华人民共和国向朝鲜民主主义人民共和国供应成套设备和提供技术援助的协定”。
    周恩来总理和贺龙副总理参加了签字仪式。
    代表中国方面在上述文件上签字的是国务院副总理李先念，代表朝鲜方面签字的是朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府经济代表团团长、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相李周渊。
    根据上述两个协定的规定，我国政府将在1961年至1964年期间给予朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府四亿二千万卢布的长期贷款，并且帮助朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府建设橡胶轮胎厂、无线电通讯器材等工厂和一批生产日用必需品的轻工业企业。此外，协定还规定我国将供应朝鲜棉纺织设备、无线电设备等。
    这次中朝两国政府经济代表团会谈所取得的成就，标志着中朝两国人民用鲜血凝成的牢不可破的崇高友谊进一步发展和巩固，是中朝两国政府和人民为增强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的伟大团结和力量而作出的新的贡献。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的还有：对外贸易部部长叶季壮，第一机械工业部部长段君毅，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，国家计划委员会副主任宋养初，对外贸易部副部长李强，化学工业部副部长李苏，第三机械工业部副部长刘鼎，纺织工业部副部长陈维稷，邮电部副部长申光，我国驻朝鲜大使馆商务参赞李范如等。
    朝鲜方面参加签字仪式的有：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府经济代表团团员、朝鲜驻我国大使李永镐，朝鲜驻我国大使馆参赞马东山、商务参赞方泰律，以及朝鲜政府经济代表团的随行工作人员等。
    新华社13日讯　周恩来总理今晚在人民大会堂安徽厅举行宴会，招待朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相李周渊和他率领的朝鲜政府经济代表团人员。
    国务院副总理贺龙、李先念出席了宴会。
    周恩来总理和李周渊副首相在洋溢着友好气氛的宴会上先后讲话。他们在讲话中对今天两国政府签订的“关于中华人民共和国贷款给朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的协定”和“关于中华人民共和国向朝鲜民主主义人民共和国供应成套设备和提供技术援助的协定”，表示庆贺。并且指出，中朝两国人民在社会主义建设和反对共同敌人美帝国主义的斗争中，将永远团结一致，互相支持，互相帮助。
    朝鲜驻中国大使、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府经济代表团团员李永镐，朝鲜驻中国大使馆的外交官员，应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会作陪的还有：对外贸易部部长叶季壮，第一机械工业部部长段君毅，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，以及政府各有关部门、北京市和中朝友好协会的负责人等。
    新华社13日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相李周渊和他率领的朝鲜政府经济代表团，今天上午乘专车由三门峡回到北京。
    对外贸易部副部长李强，外交部礼宾司副司长葛步海等人到车站迎接。
    朝鲜贵宾们在这以前，还参观访问了石家庄、郑州、洛阳等城市。（附图片）
