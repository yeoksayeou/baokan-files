Title: Our Military Delegation Continues its Visit to North Korea. Marshal He Long and Others Watch "Chun香传", General Luo Ruiqing Goes to Kaesong. The Delegation Visits Naval and Air Force Units and Tours the Yanfeng Reservoir.
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 29th - The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to North Korea, led by Marshal He Long and General Luo Ruiqing, accompanied by General Kim Chang-bong, Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, watched the famous Korean classical opera "Chun香传" with officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army this evening.

Also accompanying the delegation to watch the performance were General Heo Bong-hak, Director of the General Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army, General Choi Yong-jin, Vice-Minister of National Defence, Senior Generals of the Korean People's Army Kim Bong-ryul, Seok Sang-shan, and Major General Lee Jang-soo, Deputy Director of the General Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army, and others.

Qiao Xiaoguang, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, Colonel Zhang Leting, Military Attaché, and diplomatic officials from the embassy also watched the performance.

All members of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Art Troupe accompanying the delegation also watched the performance.

At the end of the performance, Marshal He Long, General Luo Ruiqing, and all members of the delegation went on stage to present flower baskets and warmly shook hands with the actors to congratulate them on the success of the performance.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 29th - All members of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to North Korea, led by the Deputy Head of the Delegation, General Luo Ruiqing, accompanied by General Choi Yong-jin, Vice-Minister of National Defence of Korea, left Pyongyang by special train late tonight to visit Kaesong.

The Head of the Delegation, Marshal He Long, continues his visit in Pyongyang.

Colonel Zhang Leting, Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea, also accompanied the delegation to Kaesong.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 29th - The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to North Korea conducted separate visits today.

The Head of the Delegation, Marshal He Long, Vice-Chairman of the National Defence Council of China, Vice-Premier of the State Council, General Luo Ruiqing, Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and some members of the delegation continued their visit in Pyongyang.

The member of the delegation, Vice-Commander of the Navy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Vice Admiral Zhou Xihan, accompanied by Rear Admiral Liu Changquan, Commander of the Korean People's Army Navy, visited a naval unit of the Korean People's Army and received a warm welcome from all officers and soldiers of the unit. Vice Admiral Zhou Xihan, on behalf of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to North Korea, presented the naval unit with a banner and military emblem of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy as gifts. The naval unit presented the delegation with an exquisite model of a turtle ship. The turtle ship was a type of warship created by the famous Korean historical general Yi Sun-sin.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 28th - The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to North Korea, accompanied by General Kim Chang-bong, Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, General Heo Bong-hak, Director of the General Political Bureau, General Choi Yong-jin, Vice-Minister of National Defence, and General Choi Kwang, Commander of the Korean People's Army Air Force, made a special trip from Pyongyang to visit an air force unit of the Korean People's Army on the 28th.

The air force unit lined up to warmly welcome the delegation and gave a superb flight performance for the Chinese guests.

After the visit, the delegation presented the Korean People's Army air force unit with a banner, an embroidered military emblem of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force, and other gifts.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 28th - The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to North Korea, accompanied by General Kim Chang-bong, Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, General Choi Yong-jin, Vice-Minister of National Defence, and other senior generals of the Korean People's Army, visited the Yanfeng Reservoir for sightseeing on the 28th.

Pi Chang-lin, Chairman of the South Pyongan Province Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, warmly welcomed and hosted the Chinese guests.

During the visit, the delegation expressed its admiration for the great achievements made by the Korean people in terms of water conservancy and agricultural production.

### 我军事代表团继续在朝鲜参观访问  贺龙元帅等观看“春香传”罗瑞卿大将前往开城  代表团访问海军和空军部队并参观延丰水库

1960-10-30
第5版()
专栏：

    我军事代表团继续在朝鲜参观访问
    贺龙元帅等观看“春香传”罗瑞卿大将前往开城
    代表团访问海军和空军部队并参观延丰水库
    据新华社平壤29日电　由贺龙元帅、罗瑞卿大将率领的中国访朝军事友好代表团，在朝鲜人民军总参谋长金昌奉上将陪同下，今天晚间同朝鲜人民军官兵一起观看了朝鲜著名的古典歌剧“春香传”。
    陪同代表团观看演出的还有朝鲜人民军总政治局长许凤学上将、民族保卫省副相崔勇进上将、朝鲜人民军高级将领金奉律上将、石山上将、朝鲜人民军总政治局副局长李长秀少将等。
    中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光、武官张乐亭上校和大使馆外交官员也观看了演出。
    由代表团率领的中国人民解放军文艺工作团全体人员也观看了演出。
    演出结束时，贺龙元帅、罗瑞卿大将和代表团全体团员登台献花篮，并同演员们亲切握手，祝贺演出成功。
    据新华社平壤29日电　中国访朝军事友好代表团的全体团员，由代表团副团长罗瑞卿大将率领，在朝鲜民族保卫省副相崔勇进上将陪同下，今天深夜从平壤乘专车前往开城进行参观访问。
    代表团团长贺龙元帅在平壤继续参观访问。
    中国驻朝鲜大使馆武官张乐亭上校也随同代表团前往开城访问。
    新华社平壤29日电　中国访朝军事友好代表团今天分组进行参观访问。
    代表团团长、中国国防委员会副主席、国务院副总理贺龙元帅，国务院副总理、中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿大将和代表团的部分团员继续在平壤参观访问。
    代表团团员、中国人民解放军海军副司令员周希汉海军中将在朝鲜人民军海军司令柳昌权海军少将陪同下，访问了朝鲜人民军一支海军部队，受到了这支海军部队全体官兵的热烈欢迎。周希汉海军中将代表中国访朝军事友好代表团向这支海军部队赠送了锦旗和中国人民解放军海军军徽等礼品。这支海军部队赠给代表团一个精致的龟船模型。龟船是朝鲜历史名将李舜臣创制的一种战船。
    新华社平壤28日电　中国访朝军事友好代表团，在朝鲜人民军总参谋长金昌奉上将、总政治局长许凤学上将、民族保卫省副相崔勇进上将、朝鲜人民军空军司令崔光上将陪同下，28日从平壤专程到一支朝鲜人民军空军部队参观访问。
    这支空军部队列队热烈地欢迎代表团，并且向中国客人作了高超的飞行技术表演。
    代表团在参观访问后，赠给这支朝鲜人民军空军部队一面锦旗、锦绣的中国人民解放军空军军徽和其他礼品。
    新华社平壤28日电　中国访朝军事友好代表团，28日在朝鲜人民军总参谋长金昌奉上将、民族保卫省副相崔勇进上将等朝鲜人民军高级将领的陪同下，到延丰水库参观游览。
    朝鲜劳动党平安南道委员会委员长皮昌麟热烈地迎接和招待了中国客人。
    代表团在参观中，对朝鲜人民在水利化方面和农业生产方面取得的巨大成就表示赞扬。
