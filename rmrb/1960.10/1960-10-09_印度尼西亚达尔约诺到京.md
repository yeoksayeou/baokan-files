Title: Indonesian Darjono Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 8th. Darjono, the secretary of the Indonesian Public Works Union, arrived in Beijing on the 8th. Upon his arrival in Beijing, he was welcomed by Li Zaiwen, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and responsible persons from the National Committee of the Chinese Construction Workers' Union.

### 印度尼西亚达尔约诺到京

1960-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚达尔约诺到京
    新华社8日讯　印度尼西亚公共工程工会书记达尔约诺在8日到达北京。他到达北京时，受到中华全国总工会书记处书记栗再温和中国建筑工会全国委员会负责人的欢迎。
