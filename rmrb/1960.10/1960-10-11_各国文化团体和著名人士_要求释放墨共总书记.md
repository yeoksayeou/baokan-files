Title: Cultural Organizations and Celebrities from Various Countries Demand Release of General Secretary of Mexican Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Mexico City, October 9th: Cultural organizations and prominent figures in the cultural world from various countries continue to demand the release of David Alfaro Siqueiros, the imprisoned General Secretary of the Mexican Communist Party and renowned muralist.
    The Mexican newspaper "Excelsior" published on the 9th a list of some organizations and prominent figures demanding the release of Siqueiros. These include the Czechoslovak Writers' Union, the University of Brazil, the Cuban Artists, Painters and Writers Organization, the Peruvian Democratic Writers' Union, and famous Chinese painters, among others.
    Famous painter Picasso, poet Neruda, prominent Chilean figures Salvador Allende, Argentine peace activist Alfredo Varela, newly elected President of Ecuador Velasco Ibarra, famous Spanish poet Rafael Alberti, and American writer Walter Frank also telegraphed the President of Mexico demanding the release of Siqueiros.

### 各国文化团体和著名人士  要求释放墨共总书记

1960-10-11
第5版()
专栏：

    各国文化团体和著名人士
    要求释放墨共总书记
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社墨西哥城9日讯：世界各国许多文化团体和文化界著名人士继续要求释放遭到监禁的墨西哥共产党总书记、著名壁画家戴维·阿耳法罗·西克罗斯。
    墨西哥“至上报”在9日发表了要求释放西克罗斯的一些团体和著名人士的名单，其中包括捷克斯洛伐克作家联盟、巴西大学、古巴艺术家画家和作家组织、秘鲁民主作家联盟以及中国著名画家们等。
    著名画家毕加索、诗人聂鲁达、智利著名人士萨尔瓦多、阿伦德、阿根廷和平人士阿耳弗雷多·巴雷拉、厄瓜多尔新当选的总统贝拉斯科·伊瓦拉、西班牙著名诗人拉斐尔·阿耳贝蒂、美国作家华尔特·弗兰克等也都打电报给墨西哥总统要求释放西克罗斯。
