Title: All-China Women's Federation Hosts Indonesian Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-10-03
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd. Liu Qingyang, Vice-President of the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China, held a tea party today to welcome the wife of Indonesian Party Chairman Hadi, the wife of Indonesian Parliamentary Secretary-General Rusli, and the wife of Indonesian Presidential Palace Office First Secretary Brotodingingrat, who are visiting our country.

Also present at the tea party were Cao Mengjun and Shen Zijiu, members of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, Wu Qing, Secretary of the Secretariat, and Pu Jiexiu, Deputy Director of the Beijing Women's Federation.

The guests and hosts had a cordial conversation in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 全国妇联招待印度尼西亚客人

1960-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    全国妇联招待印度尼西亚客人
    新华社2日讯　中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会副主席刘清扬今天举行茶会，欢迎来我国访问的印度尼西亚党总主席哈迪的夫人、印度尼西亚国会秘书长鲁斯里的夫人和印度尼西亚总统府办公厅第一秘书布罗托迪宁格拉特的夫人。
    出席茶会的，还有全国妇联主席团委员曹孟君、沈兹九，书记处书记吴綪，北京市妇联副主任浦洁修等。
   宾主在热烈友好的气氛中进行了亲切的交谈。
