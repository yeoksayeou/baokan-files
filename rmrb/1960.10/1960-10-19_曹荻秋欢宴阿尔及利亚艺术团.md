Title: Cao Diqiu Hosts a Banquet for the Algerian Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1960-10-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 18th:  Cao Diqiu, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, hosted a banquet last night to entertain the Algerian Art Troupe.

	Deputy Mayor Cao Diqiu spoke at the banquet, warmly congratulating the Algerian artists on the success of their visit and performances in Shanghai. He said that the people of Shanghai, like the people of the whole country, resolutely support the just struggle of the Algerian people. We believe that the heroic struggle of the Algerian people will ultimately achieve complete victory.

	The head of the Algerian Art Troupe, Afa Abdul Madi, said in his speech, thanking the people of Shanghai for their warm welcome to the art troupe and their lofty friendship to the Algerian people. He said that every victory of the people of the world against imperialism is also our victory, and we regard the victory of the great Chinese people as our own victory even more so. He said: Although the struggle of Algeria is still arduous and tortuous, the Algerian people must carry the fight to the end.

	Among those present at the banquet were Meng Bo, Director of the Shanghai Municipal Bureau of Culture and Vice President of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries.

### 曹荻秋欢宴阿尔及利亚艺术团

1960-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

    曹荻秋欢宴阿尔及利亚艺术团
    新华社上海18日电　上海市副市长曹荻秋昨晚设宴招待阿尔及利亚艺术团。
    曹荻秋副市长在宴会上讲话，热烈祝贺阿尔及利亚艺术家们在上海访问演出的成功。他说，我们上海人民和全国人民一样，坚决支持阿尔及利亚人民的正义斗争。我们相信阿尔及利亚人民的英勇斗争，最后必将取得彻底的胜利。
    阿尔及利亚艺术团负责人阿法·阿卜杜勒·玛蒂在讲话中，感谢上海人民对艺术团的热情欢迎和对阿尔及利亚人民崇高的友谊。他说，全世界人民反对帝国主义的每一个胜利也就是我们的胜利，而伟大中国人民的胜利，我们更把它看作是自己的胜利。他说：尽管阿尔及利亚的斗争还是艰巨的、曲折的，但是阿尔及利亚人民一定要把战斗进行到底。
    出席宴会作陪的有上海市文化局局长、中国人民对外文化协会上海市分会副会长孟波等。
