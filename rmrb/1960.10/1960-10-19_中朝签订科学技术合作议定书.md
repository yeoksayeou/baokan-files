Title: China and North Korea sign Scientific and Technological Cooperation Protocol
Author:
Date: 1960-10-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The third meeting of the China-North Korea Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee was held in Beijing on September 29th. During the meeting, the two sides discussed scientific and technological cooperation projects, and signed a meeting protocol on October 18th.

The protocol stipulates that China will send technicians to North Korea in the fields of light industry, chemical industry, and machinery to provide technical assistance, and receive North Korean technicians to conduct inspections and internships in Chinese metallurgical, machinery, chemical, and light industrial enterprises, and provide North Korea with technical data. The Democratic People's Republic of Korea will provide the People's Republic of China with technical data and improved seeds related to agriculture and construction, and receive Chinese technicians to conduct inspections of North Korean chemical enterprises.

The meeting was conducted in an atmosphere of sincere friendship and mutual understanding. The meeting made new contributions to the further development of scientific and technological cooperation between the two countries.

Signing the protocol on the Chinese side was Liu Yin, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Committee and Vice Minister of the Third Ministry of Machine Building; on the Korean side was Park Yong-sung, Chairman of the Korean Group of the Committee and Vice Chairman of the Light Industry Committee.

Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were Zhang Youxuan, Deputy Director of the Science and Technology Commission of the People's Republic of China, Chen Shuliang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others. On the Korean side were Li Yong-ho, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and others.

### 中朝签订科学技术合作议定书

1960-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

    中朝签订科学技术合作议定书
    新华社18日讯　中华人民共和国政府和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府科学技术合作委员会第三届会议，于9月29日在北京举行。会议期间，讨论了两国的科学技术合作项目，并于10月18日签订了会议议定书。
    议定书规定，中华人民共和国将在轻工、化工、机械等方面派遣技术人员赴朝鲜进行技术援助并接待朝鲜技术人员在中国的冶金、机械、化工、轻工等企业进行考察和实习，供给朝鲜技术资料；朝鲜民主主义人民共和国供给中华人民共和国有关农业、建筑方面的技术资料和优良种子，并接待中国技术人员到朝鲜化工企业进行考察。
    本届会议是在真诚友好和互相谅解的气氛中进行的，通过这次会议，对进一步发展两国间的科学技术合作工作作出了新的贡献。
    在议定书上签字的，中国方面是委员会中国组主席、第三机械工业部副部长刘寅；朝鲜方面是委员会朝鲜组主席、轻工业委员会副委员长朴龙成。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有中华人民共和国科学技术委员会副主任张有萱，外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮等；朝鲜方面有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使李永镐等。
