Title: Xiao Fangzhou Hosts Banquet for Japanese International Trade Friends
Author:
Date: 1960-10-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: Xiao Fangzhou, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the delegation of staff members from the Japanese International Trade Promotion Local Assembly Members League, led by Kunio Taihei, who are visiting China. Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, was also invited to attend the banquet.

The delegation of nine people arrived in Beijing by train on the 24th.

### 萧方洲欢宴日本国际贸易界朋友

1960-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    萧方洲欢宴日本国际贸易界朋友
    新华社25日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长萧方洲今晚设宴欢迎以泰平国男为首的日本国际贸易促进地方议员联盟职员访华代表团。在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也应邀出席了宴会。
    代表团一行九人是24日乘火车到达北京的。
