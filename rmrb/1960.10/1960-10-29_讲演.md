Title: Oration
Author: Tianma, Fang Cheng
Date: 1960-10-29
Page: 8

    Oration
    Tianma Poem, Fang Cheng Painting
    Reported: On October 15th, fascist elements in Tokyo held a large-scale lecture at the Kyobashi Public Hall, supporting the assault on Asanuma. In such a large venue, the audience, speakers, and reporters combined amounted to only a few people.
    Outside the venue, slogans echoed,
    A chorus: Punish the murderer!
    Inside the venue, however, it was so cold and desolate,
    Miserable and tragic,
    Only the tables and chairs were staring blankly.
    Shame! Shame!
    In such an awkward scene,
    How can one deliver a lecture?!
    Legs trembling,
    Heart pounding,
    The mouth simply refuses to obey.
    Looking out the window: red flags waving,
    A sea of anger,
    Millions of people raising iron fists!
    Difficult! Difficult!
    Using every ounce of energy,
    But this wheel, ah,
    Simply won't turn!

### 讲演
天马  方成
1960-10-29
第8版()
专栏：诗画配

    讲演
    天马诗  方成画
    报载：10月15日，东京的法西斯分子在京桥公会堂举行大讲演会，支持对浅沼的行凶。偌大的一个会场，听众、讲演者和记者合在一起，只有寥寥数人。
    会场外口号震天，
    一声声：惩办杀人犯！
    会场内却这么冷冷清清，
    凄凄惨惨，
    只有那桌儿椅儿干瞪眼。
    丢脸！丢脸！
    似这般尴尬场面，
    如何作讲演？！
    腿在发抖，
    心在打颤，
    这嘴巴压根儿不听使唤。
    望窗外：红旗招展，
    怒潮一片，
    千万人民举铁拳！
    作难！作难！
    吃奶气力都使尽，
    这车轮儿啊，
    偏偏扭不转！
