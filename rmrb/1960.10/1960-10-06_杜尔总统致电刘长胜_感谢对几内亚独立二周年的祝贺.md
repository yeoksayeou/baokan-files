Title: President Touré Sends Telegram to Liu Changsheng, Thanking Him for Congratulations on Guinea's Second Anniversary of Independence
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-06
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Liu Changsheng, President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, received a telegram from President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea. In the telegram, President Touré thanked Liu Changsheng for his congratulations on the second anniversary of the independence of the Republic of Guinea.

　　President Touré said in the telegram: "We firmly believe that strengthening the ties of solidarity between the peoples of Asian and African countries is a decisive factor in establishing an era of democratic progress, freedom, and world peace. Please convey our friendly and united sentiments to the members of your association."

### 杜尔总统致电刘长胜  感谢对几内亚独立二周年的祝贺

1960-10-06
第6版()
专栏：

    杜尔总统致电刘长胜
    感谢对几内亚独立二周年的祝贺
　　新华社5日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会会长刘长胜收到了几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔发来的一份电报。杜尔总统在电报中感谢刘长胜对几内亚共和国独立二周年的祝贺。
　　杜尔总统在电报中说：“我们深信，加强亚非各国人民之间的团结的联系是建立民主进步、自由和世界和平时代的一个决定性因素。请向贵会会员转达我们友好和团结的情谊。”
