Title: Polish-Chinese Friendship Association Holds Photo Exhibition "Two Years to Complete the Five-Year Plan"
Author:
Date: 1960-10-15
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 11th -- To celebrate the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association today held a photo exhibition in Warsaw entitled "Two Years to Complete the Five-Year Plan." The exhibition featured 59 sets of photographs and charts related to China's achievements in industrial and agricultural construction.
    Gorański, a member of the Presidium of the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association and Minister of Higher Education, Olszewski, Minister of the Economic Department of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, and Wang Bingnan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, attended the opening ceremony. Gorański said in his speech at the opening ceremony: The content of this exhibition demonstrates the great achievements of the great Chinese people. The completion of the Five-Year Plan in two years demonstrates the enormous scale of the efforts made by the Chinese people.

### 波中友协为庆祝我国国庆  举办“五年计划两年完成”图片展览

1960-10-15
第6版()
专栏：

    波中友协为庆祝我国国庆
    举办“五年计划两年完成”图片展览
    新华社华沙11日电　波中友好协会为庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年，今天在华沙举办了中国“五年计划两年完成”的图片展览会。展览会展出了有关中国工农业建设成就的照片和图表五十九套。
    波中友好协会主席团委员、高等教育部部长戈兰斯基，波兰统一工人党中央经济部部长奥尔谢夫斯基等人以及我国驻波兰大使王炳南参加了开幕式。戈兰斯基在开幕式上致词说：这个展览会的内容表现了伟大中国人民的伟大成就。五年计划两年完成，这表示了中国人民所作出的努力的巨大规模。
