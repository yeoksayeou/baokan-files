Title: Capital to Hold Grand Rally Today to Welcome Prime Minister Abbas; Ministry of Culture and Sino-African Friendship Association Held Evening Reception Yesterday to Welcome Distinguished Guests from Algeria
Author:
Date: 1960-10-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The people of the capital will hold a grand rally on the afternoon of the 4th to warmly welcome Abbas Ferhat, Prime Minister of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, and the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria led by him. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the live broadcast of the welcome rally from 3:50 pm on the 4th, using the frequency of the first set of programs. Beijing Television will also broadcast it simultaneously.

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Ministry of Culture and the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association held an evening party today to warmly welcome Prime Minister Abbas Ferhat and all the distinguished guests of the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria led by him.

At the evening party, Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, together with Prime Minister Abbas Ferhat and other distinguished guests, enjoyed the national dance drama "Leifeng Pagoda" performed by the Central Experimental Opera House.

Accompanying the distinguished guests to watch the performance were: Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association Liu Changsheng, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Xiruo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Zhang Aiping, and people from various circles.

### 首都今日盛会欢迎阿巴斯总理  文化部和中非友协昨日举行晚会欢迎阿贵宾

1960-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

    首都今日盛会欢迎阿巴斯总理
    文化部和中非友协昨日举行晚会欢迎阿贵宾
    新华社3日讯　首都各界人民将在4日下午举行盛大集会，热烈欢迎阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理阿巴斯·费尔哈特和由他率领的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团。中央人民广播电台从4日下午三时五十分开始，用第一套节目的频率转播欢迎大会的实况。北京电视台也将同时转播。
    新华社3日讯　文化部和中国非洲人民友好协会今天举行晚会，热烈欢迎阿巴斯·费尔哈特总理和由他率领的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团全体贵宾。
    晚会上，国务院副总理贺龙同阿巴斯·费尔哈特总理等贵宾一起，欣赏了中央实验歌剧院演出的民族舞剧——“雷峰塔”。
    陪同贵宾们观剧的还有：文化部部长沈雁冰，中国非洲人民友好协会会长刘长胜，对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，对外贸易部副部长李强，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张爱萍上将，以及各方面的人士。
