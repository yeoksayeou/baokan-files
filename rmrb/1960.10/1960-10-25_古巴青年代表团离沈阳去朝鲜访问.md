Title: Cuban Youth Delegation Leaves Shenyang for Visit to North Korea
Author:
Date: 1960-10-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, October 24th. The Cuban Youth Delegation, consisting of four members, who were invited by the All-China Youth Federation, left Shenyang by train tonight for a visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

The Cuban Youth Delegation arrived in Shenyang last night after visiting Anshan. This morning, the delegation visited the mechanized threshing ground, home for the elderly, kindergarten, and the Shenyang Heavy Machinery Plant of the China-Korea Friendship People's Commune. Before their departure from Shenyang, Li Huang, Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Zhu Weiren, Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, met with all members of the delegation and held a banquet. Youths from all walks of life in Shenyang also held a get-together with the guests at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Palace.

### 古巴青年代表团离沈阳去朝鲜访问

1960-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    古巴青年代表团离沈阳去朝鲜访问
    新华社沈阳24日电　应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国访问的古巴青年代表团一行四人，今夜乘火车离开沈阳前往朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访问。
    古巴青年代表团是昨天晚上参观了鞍山以后，到达沈阳的。今天上午，代表团在沈阳参观了中朝友谊人民公社机械化脱谷场、敬老院、幼儿园和沈阳重型机器厂。在他们离开沈阳前，中共辽宁省委书记李荒、中共沈阳市委书记朱维仁等接见代表团的全体成员，并举行宴会。沈阳市各界青年还在中苏友谊宫与客人们进行了联欢。
