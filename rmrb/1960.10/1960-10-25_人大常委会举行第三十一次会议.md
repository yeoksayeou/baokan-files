Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds 31st Session
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th: The Standing Committee of the Second National People's Congress held its thirty-first session today. The meeting approved appointments and removals.

### 人大常委会举行第三十一次会议

1960-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    人大常委会举行第三十一次会议
    新华社24日讯　第二届全国人民代表大会常务委员会今天举行第三十一次会议。会议通过了任免事项。
