Title: Thirty Thousand People Rally in Peruvian Capital in Support of Teachers' Strike
Author:
Date: 1960-10-05
Page: 6

Thirty thousand citizens of Lima, the capital of Peru, participated on the 3rd in a mass rally held by the Peruvian National Federation of Education Workers in Bolivar Square in front of the parliament building, supporting the teachers' struggle against American control and for the defense of their right to live.

The strike of fifty thousand teachers across Peru, which began on September 20th to protest the "economic stabilization plan" imposed by the United States and to demand wage increases, is still ongoing. On the 3rd, the teachers held placards and staged a demonstration in front of the parliament building.

### 秘鲁首都三万人集会支持教师罢教

1960-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    秘鲁首都三万人集会支持教师罢教
    秘鲁首都利马的三万市民三日参加了秘鲁全国教育工作者联合会在议会前的博利瓦尔广场上召开的群众大会，支持教师们反对美国控制和维护生活权利的斗争。
    秘鲁全国的五万名教师为抗议美国强加的“稳定经济计划”和要求增加工资从九月二十日起举行的罢教仍在坚持中。教师们三日举着标语牌，在议会前举行了示威游行。
