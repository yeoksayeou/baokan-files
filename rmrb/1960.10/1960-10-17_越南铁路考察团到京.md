Title: Vietnamese Railway Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. A seven-member Vietnamese railway delegation from the Ministry of Transportation and Post and Telecommunications, led by Nguyen Cao Tham, Deputy Director of the Transportation Bureau of the General Railway Bureau of the Ministry of Transportation and Post and Telecommunications, has recently arrived in Beijing by train. Wang Weigong, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Bureau of the Ministry of Railways, and Zhang Yun, Deputy Director of the Beijing Railway Bureau, and others welcomed them at the station.

### 越南铁路考察团到京

1960-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

    越南铁路考察团到京
    新华社16日讯　越南交通邮电部铁路考察团一行七人，在交通邮电部铁路总局运输局副局长阮高参率领下，已经在最近乘火车到达北京。到车站欢迎的有铁道部国际联运局副局长王维恭和北京铁路局副局长张云等。
