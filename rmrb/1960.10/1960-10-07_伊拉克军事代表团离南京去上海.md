Title: Iraqi Military Delegation Leaves Nanjing for Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th — The Iraqi military goodwill delegation headed by Brigadier General Mahmoud Abdul Razzaq, after visiting Wuhan and Nanjing, left Nanjing by special plane on the afternoon of the 6th to visit Shanghai.

Li Yingxi, Major General, and Min Xuesheng, Major General, leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Wuhan, and Zhang Caiqian, Lieutenant General, and Zhang Fan, Lieutenant General, leaders of the troops stationed in Nanjing, went to the airport to greet and send off the guests upon their arrival and departure, respectively. Xie Ziqun, Deputy Mayor of Wuhan, Guan Wenwei, Deputy Governor of Jiangsu Province, and Wang Zhaoquan, Deputy Mayor of Nanjing, also went to the airport to greet and send off the distinguished guests.

### 伊拉克军事代表团离南京去上海

1960-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克军事代表团离南京去上海
    据新华社6日讯　以马哈茂德·阿卜杜勒·拉扎克准将为首的伊拉克军事友好代表团，在武汉、南京参观访问后，6日下午离开南京乘专机前往上海访问。
    中国人民解放军驻武汉部队首长李迎希少将、闵学胜少将和驻南京部队首长张才千中将、张藩中将等，在客人到达和离开时，都分别到机场迎送。武汉市副市长谢兹群、江苏省副省长管文蔚、南京市副市长王昭铨等，也曾分别到机场迎送贵宾。
