Title: Mexican Cultural Delegation Visits Shenyang and Other Places
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 10th: The Mexican cultural friendship delegation, headed by the former interim president of Mexico, Emilio Portes Gil, left Beijing on the 8th to visit Shenyang and other cities.

Among those who saw them off at the station was Ji Chao-ding, vice president of the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association.

### 墨西哥文化代表团去沈阳等地访问

1960-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    墨西哥文化代表团去沈阳等地访问
    新华社10日讯　以墨西哥前临时总统埃米略·波尔特斯·希耳为首的墨西哥文化友好代表团，已经在8日离开北京前往沈阳等城市参观访问。
    到车站送行的有中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长冀朝鼎等。
