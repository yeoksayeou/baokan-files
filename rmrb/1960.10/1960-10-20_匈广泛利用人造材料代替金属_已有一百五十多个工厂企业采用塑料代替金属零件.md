Title: Hungary Widely Uses Artificial Materials to Replace Metal: More Than 150 Factories and Enterprises Use Plastics to Replace Metal Parts
Author:
Date: 1960-10-20
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest -- Hungarian industrial departments are widely using artificial materials to replace metal.

    Currently, in the metallurgical and machine manufacturing industries, more than 150 factories and enterprises have widely adopted plastics to replace metal parts. Last year, these factories saved the country more than 200 tons of metal materials by this method; since the beginning of this year, they have saved more than 300 tons of metal materials. After the Danube Iron and Steel Plant used plastics to replace steel machine parts, it also improved the quality of the machines and reduced costs by half. The Pick Wagon and Machine Factory saves more than 3 million forints (Hungarian currency) annually by using artificial materials to replace imported materials.

    Hungary's telecommunications equipment and textile industries are also increasingly using artificial materials. In the production of telecommunications equipment, Hungary has recently used 100 tons of artificial materials annually, accounting for one-tenth of all telecommunications equipment production materials.

    In order to further promote the use of artificial materials, some factories with relatively sufficient equipment and personnel are responsible for mass-producing various plastic parts to supply other factories and enterprises. To meet the needs of various departments for artificial materials, some chemical joint enterprises are currently being expanded and built.

    During the second five-year plan (1961-1965), artificial materials will be more widely used in various industrial sectors in Hungary.

### 匈广泛利用人造材料代替金属  已有一百五十多个工厂企业采用塑料代替金属零件

1960-10-20
第6版()
专栏：

    匈广泛利用人造材料代替金属
    已有一百五十多个工厂企业采用塑料代替金属零件
    新华社布达佩斯讯　匈牙利工业部门正在广泛利用人造材料来代替金属。
    目前，在冶金和机器制造业方面，已经有一百五十多个工厂企业广泛采用塑料来代替金属零件。这些工厂去年就依靠这种办法为国家节省了二百多吨金属材料；今年年初以来，它们节省的金属材料已超过了三百吨。多瑙钢铁厂在利用塑料代替钢制机器零件以后，还提高了机器的质量，把成本降低了一半。皮克车厢和机器厂由于利用人造材料代替进口物资，每年可以节约三百多万福林（匈币名）。
    匈牙利的电讯器材和纺织工业部门也日益广泛利用人造材料。匈牙利在电讯器材生产方面近来每年都要使用一百吨人造材料，相当于全部电讯器材生产材料的十分之一。
    为了进一步推广对人造材料的利用，一些设备和人才都比较充裕的工厂，正在负责大批生产各种塑料零件，以供应其他工厂企业。为了满足各部门对人造材料的需要，目前正在扩建和兴建一些化学联合企业。
    在第二个五年计划（1961—1965年）期间，人造材料在匈牙利各工业部门中将得到更普遍的利用。
