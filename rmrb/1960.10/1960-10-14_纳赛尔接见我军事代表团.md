Title: Nasser Receives Chinese Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 12th - President Nasser of the United Arab Republic received the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation led by General Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, at the Presidential Palace yesterday.

Chen Jiakang, the Chinese Ambassador to the UAR, accompanied the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation during the reception.

President Nasser and General Zhang Zongxun had a friendly conversation.

General Zhang Zongxun conveyed the heartfelt greetings of Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China to President Nasser, and handed over the two letters addressed to President Nasser from Chairman Liu and Premier Zhou respectively.

General Zhang Zongxun also presented gifts to President Nasser.

### 纳赛尔接见我军事代表团

1960-10-14
第6版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔接见我军事代表团
    新华社开罗12日电　阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔昨天在总统府接见了由中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊上将率领的中国军事友好代表团。
    陪同中国军事友好代表团受接见的有中国驻阿联大使陈家康。
    纳赛尔总统和张宗逊将军进行了友好的交谈。
    张宗逊将军转达了中华人民共和国刘少奇主席和周恩来总理对纳赛尔总统的衷心的致意，并把刘主席和周总理分别致纳赛尔总统的两封信交给了他。
    张宗逊将军还向纳赛尔总统赠送了礼物。
