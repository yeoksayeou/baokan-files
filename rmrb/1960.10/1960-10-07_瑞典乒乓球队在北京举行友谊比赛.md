Title: Swedish Table Tennis Team Holds Friendly Match in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 5th: The Swedish table tennis team was defeated by the Chinese Youth Table Tennis Team in Beijing last night with a score of 0-5. This was the last match of the visiting team's visit to our country. After arriving in our country, the visiting team played a total of seven matches in Guangzhou, Shanghai, and Beijing, with a record of one win and six losses.

### 瑞典乒乓球队在北京举行友谊比赛

1960-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典乒乓球队在北京举行友谊比赛
    据新华社5日讯　瑞典乒乓球队昨晚在北京以零比五败给中国青年乒乓球队，这是客队此次来我国访问后的最末一次比赛。客队到达我国后，先后在广州、上海、北京共进行七场比赛，战绩是一胜六负。
