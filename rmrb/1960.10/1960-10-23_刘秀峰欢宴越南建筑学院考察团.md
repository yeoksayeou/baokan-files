Title: Liu Xiufeng Hosts Banquet for Vietnamese Architectural Institute Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: Minister of Building Engineering Liu Xiufeng held a banquet this evening to bid farewell to all members of the Vietnamese Ministry of Construction's Architectural Institute delegation, led by Nguyen Cao Luyen, Deputy Minister of Construction of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    Attending the banquet were Vice Ministers of Building Engineering Xu Shiping, Song Yuhe, and others.

### 刘秀峰欢宴越南建筑学院考察团

1960-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    刘秀峰欢宴越南建筑学院考察团
    新华社22日讯　建筑工程部部长刘秀峰今晚举行宴会，欢送由越南民主共和国建筑部副部长阮高练率领的越南建筑部建筑学院考察团全体成员。
    出席宴会作陪的有建筑工程部副部长许世平、宋裕和等。
