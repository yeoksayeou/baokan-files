Title: Chinese Government Trade Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Burma

Author:
Date: 1960-10-31
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, October 29th. The People's Republic of China Government Trade Delegation, led by Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 28th, after concluding its friendly visit to Burma. Lu Xuzhang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and others were at the airport to greet them. U Kyaw Winn, Ambassador of the Union of Burma to China, and Second Secretary U Khin Maung Gyi were also at the airport to greet them.

### 我政府贸易代表团访问缅甸后回京

1960-10-31
第2版()
专栏：

    我政府贸易代表团访问缅甸后回京
    新华社29日讯　由对外贸易部副部长雷任民率领的中华人民共和国政府贸易代表团结束了在缅甸的友好访问，已在28日下午乘飞机回到北京。到机场迎接的有对外贸易部副部长卢绪章等。缅甸联邦驻华大使叫温、二等秘书吴容林也到机场迎接。
