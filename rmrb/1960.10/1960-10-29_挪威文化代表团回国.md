Title: Norwegian Cultural Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th: The Norwegian cultural delegation of four people, led by head of delegation Nolum, left Beijing by plane this morning to return to their country, after visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries. ### 挪威文化代表团回国

1960-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    挪威文化代表团回国
    新华社27日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的挪威文化代表团一行四人，在团长诺鲁姆率领下，今天早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。印度尼西亚总统府办公厅
