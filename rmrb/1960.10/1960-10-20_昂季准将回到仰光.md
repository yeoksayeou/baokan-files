Title: Brigadier General Aung Gyi Returns to Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1960-10-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, October 19th: Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the chief representative of the Burmese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee and Deputy Chief of Staff of the Burmese Armed Forces, and the delegation he led, returned here by plane this afternoon from China.

During his stay in China, Brigadier General Aung Gyi attended the fourth meeting of the Joint Committee in Beijing, participated in celebrations for the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, and attended the signing ceremony of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty.

Brigadier General Aung Gyi and his party also visited many cities in eastern and southern China.

At the airport to welcome the Burmese delegation were Brigadier General Than Pe (Navy), Deputy Chief of Staff (Navy), Brigadier General Tin Pe, Director of the Quartermaster General Department, and other senior military officers.

U Wang, advisor to Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, and James Barrington, Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and other senior government officials also went to the airport to welcome them.

Lei Yang, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, and other diplomatic officials from the Chinese Embassy also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 昂季准将回到仰光

1960-10-20
第6版()
专栏：

    昂季准将回到仰光
    新华社仰光19日电　中缅边界联合委员会缅甸首席代表、缅甸联邦国防军副总参谋长昂季准将和他率领的代表团今天下午乘飞机从中国回到这里。
    昂季准将在逗留中国期间在北京出席了联合委员会第四次会议，参加了中华人民共和国建国十一周年庆祝典礼并参加了中缅边界条约签字仪式。
    昂季准将和他的一行人还访问了中国东部和南部的许多城市。
    在机场欢迎缅甸代表团的有（海军）副总参谋长丹佩海军准将、军需署署长丁佩准将。和其他高级军官。
    缅甸总理吴努的顾问吴旺和外交部常任秘书詹姆斯巴林顿和其他高级政府官员也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬和中国大使馆的其他外交官员。
