Title: Czechoslovak State Bank President Bauer Departs Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 4

    Czechoslovak State Bank President Bauer Departs Beijing for Home

    Xinhua News Agency, October 10th. Mr. Bauer, President of the Czechoslovak State Bank, and Mr. Malat, Director of the Foreign Business Administration Bureau, who were visiting our country at the invitation of Cao Juru, President of the People's Bank of China, left Beijing by plane yesterday morning to return home.

    Cao Juru, President of the People's Bank of China, and Vice Presidents Wu Bo, Qiao Peixin, and others were at the airport to see them off. The Ambassador of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic to China, Mr. Cědík, was also at the airport to see them off.

### 捷国家银行行长鲍尔离京回国

1960-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    捷国家银行行长鲍尔离京回国
    新华社10日讯　应中国人民银行行长曹菊如邀请来我国访问的捷克斯洛伐克国家银行行长鲍尔、国外业务管理局局长马拉特，昨天早上乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有中国人民银行行长曹菊如，副行长吴波、乔培新等。捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国驻中国大使赛迪维也到机场送行。
