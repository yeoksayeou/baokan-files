Goodbye, it's time to depart
Angola, Aguostinho Neto

In Luanda, the capital of Angola, the living conditions of the people are appallingly bad due to the long-term medieval, barbaric, and brutal dark rule of the Portuguese colonialists; the city has a population of 150,000, with only two local doctors, Neto being one of them. In July of this year, this doctor, poet, and national fighter, because his poems expressing pain and anger, and calling on his compatriots to rise up and fight, angered the colonialists, was thrown into a dark prison and subjected to hard labor. The people of Luanda and all parts of Angola are fighting to save this national poet whom they love, and to oppose colonial rule.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　——Translator

　　Mother!
　　Mothers of black-skinned sons wandering in foreign lands!
　　In the years of hardship, how patiently you waited and hoped;
　　Although burning thoughts never left your heart.
　　In my heart, life also buried this mysterious hope.
　　Now, I don't have to wait for anything, because, I myself am the hope of others!
　　Hope, this is us, your sons, departing to pursue the great faith that regenerates life!
　　This is us, the children with tattered clothes, growing up in small mud houses among thorns;
　　This is us, the children who have never been to school, using the scorching sun-baked beach as a playground;
　　This is us, the small workers who live from day to day, who are tortured and scarred by the deadly hard work in the coffee fields;
　　This is us, the "ignorant blacks": who should admire the whites! who should run away when they see the rich!
　　This is us, your sons, growing up in a dark land where electric lights cannot reach!
　　This is us, your children who are hungry all day and thirsty all day, and who are apprehensive even to call you mother!
　　This is us, your sons who don't even dare to cross the street and are afraid when they see people;
　　This is us! Now we have become the hope of seeking a happy life!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Gao Fei, translator

### 再见吧，是出发的时候了
阿果斯丹霍·奈陀
1960-10-13
第8版()
专栏：

    再见吧，是出发的时候了
    安哥拉　阿果斯丹霍·奈陀
    在安哥拉首府罗安达，由于葡萄牙殖民者长期以来施行中世纪式的野蛮、残暴的黑暗统治，人民生活条件惊人恶劣；全城人口十五万人，只有两名本地医生，奈陀就是其中之一。今年7月，这位医生、诗人、民族斗士，由于他的抒发痛苦和愤怒、号召同胞起来战斗的诗歌触怒了殖民者，被投入了黑暗牢狱，受到苦役的折磨。罗安达和安哥拉各地的人民，正在为挽救这位受他们爱戴的民族诗人，反对殖民统治而进行斗争。　　　　　　　　　　　　　——译者母亲啊！儿子们流浪他乡的黑皮肤的母
  亲们啊！在艰辛的岁月里，你曾多么耐心地等待和希望；虽说灼念从没离开你的心上。在我心中，生活也曾把这神秘的希望埋
  葬。现在，我不用等待什么了，因为，我自己就是别人的希
    望！希望，这就是我们，你的儿子们，出发追求使生命再生的伟大信仰！就是我们，衣不蔽体的孩子
  们，在荆棘丛中的小土屋里生长；就是我们，没上过学的孩子们，把灼人炙日下的沙滩当成游戏
  场；就是我们，干一天算一天的小
  工们，被咖啡地里要人命的苦活折磨
  得遍体鳞伤就是我们，“无知的黑人们”：应该对白人景仰！应该见富人就滚！就是我们，你的儿子们，在电灯光照不到的黑暗土地上
  成长！就是我们，你的整天挨饿、终日饥渴的孩子们，连叫你一声妈妈都心意惶惶！就是我们，你的连过街都不敢、见了人就害怕的儿子们；就是我们啊！现在成了寻求幸福生活的希
  望！
          高飞译
