Title: My Factory
Author: Lú Míngzhé
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 8

    My Factory
    Korean Chemical Worker Lu Mingzhe
    Though I've only been away for a few days,
    I keep you in my heart every moment.
    Now that I'm back,
    You give me the newest joy.
    Clusters of thick smoke,
    Rise into the sky and become clouds.
    The earth-shaking sound of the machines,
    How lonely I am when I can't hear you...!
    Being with you always fills me with excitement,
    Although the newspapers haven't yet used bold type
    To print words of praise and encouragement,
    Miracles, ah, appear constantly, one after another.
    The oil refining tower, ah,
    Our pride!
    You stand up to the sky,
    Something the Japanese devils never accomplished!
    Your pointed top, overlooking the white clouds,
    Is about to send good news,
    Spreading our songs throughout the country,
    Bringing joy upon joy to the people.
    Who says oil only comes from underground?
    Here, we have the motherland's abundant oil field.
    Taking this final hurdle,
    A brand new symbol will be added to the map of the motherland.
    Factory, ah, please believe:
    Your loyal sons and daughters will always love you.
    For the glory and prosperity of the motherland,
    Even Mount Tai, we will move it!
              Translated by Bing Wei

### 我的工厂
卢明哲
1960-10-24
第8版()
专栏：

    我的工厂
    朝鲜化学工人　卢明哲
    出门没有几天，
    时刻把你记在心怀，
    如今回来，
    你又给我最新的喜悦。
    团团浓烟，
    升入天际变成云朵，
    震撼天地的机器声啊，
    听不到你，我多么寂寞……。
    和你在一起，总是兴奋无已，
    虽然报上还没有用黑体铅字
    印上赞扬、鼓励的话语，
    可奇迹啊，却不断出现，一个接一个。
    化油塔啊，
    我们的骄傲！
    你站到天上去啦，
    日本鬼子从来没有办到！
    你那俯视白云的尖顶，
    就要发出喜报，
    把我们的歌声传遍全国，
    让人们喜上加喜。
    谁说石油只从地下冒？
    我们这里就是祖国丰富的油田，
    拿下这最后一个关口，
    祖国地图上便会增加一个崭新的符号。
    工厂啊，请相信：
    忠实的儿女永远热爱你，
    为了祖国的荣誉和昌盛，
    是泰山，我们也要搬掉！
              冰蔚译
