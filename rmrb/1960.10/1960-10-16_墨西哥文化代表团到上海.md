Title: Mexican Cultural Delegation Arrives in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-10-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 15th: The Mexican cultural delegation, led by former interim President of Mexico, Emilio Portes Gil, arrived in Shanghai by train from Beijing today.

Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Liu Shuzhou, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai; Hu Juewen, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Zhang Chunqiao, President of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Director of the China-Latin America Friendship Association in Shanghai. In the evening, Deputy Mayor Liu Shuzhou held a banquet to entertain the guests.

The Mexican cultural delegation left Beijing on the 14th. Chu Tunan, President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, and Zhou Erfu, Vice President, went to the station to see them off.

### 墨西哥文化代表团到上海

1960-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    墨西哥文化代表团到上海
    据新华社上海15日电　墨西哥前临时总统埃米略·波尔特斯·希耳率领的墨西哥文化代表团，今天乘火车从北京到达上海。
    前往车站欢迎的有：上海市副市长刘述周，政协上海市委员会副主席胡厥文，中国人民对外文化协会上海市分会会长、中国拉丁美洲友好协会在沪理事张春桥等。晚间，刘述周副市长举行宴会招待客人。
    墨西哥文化代表团，是14日离开北京的。前往车站欢送的有中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南、副会长周而复等。
