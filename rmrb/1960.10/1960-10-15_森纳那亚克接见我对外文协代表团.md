Title: Senanayake Receives Delegation from Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-10-15
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 12th - Mr. Senanayake, the Ceylonese Minister of Industries, Internal Affairs and Culture, received the delegation from the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, led by Head of Delegation Ding Xilin and Deputy Head of Delegation Yang Shuo, on the morning of the 12th.

Present at the reception were Zhang Canming, the Chinese Ambassador to Ceylon, and Ananda Kumara, Secretary of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association. The Minister had a sincere conversation with the delegation.

In the past few days, the delegation visited the ancient and beautiful Ceylonese cities of Polonnaruwa, Anuradhapura, Kandy, and Ratnapura, famous for its gem production. There, various branches of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association held public gatherings to celebrate the National Day of the People's Republic of China, where traditional Ceylonese folk dances and music were performed.

Members of the delegation also visited Vidyalankara University and the University of Ceylon. They were warmly received by people from all walks of life in Ceylon.

### 森纳那亚克接见我对外文协代表团

1960-10-15
第6版()
专栏：

    森纳那亚克接见我对外文协代表团
    新华社科伦坡12日电　锡兰工业、内政和文化部长森纳那亚克12日上午接见了由团长丁西林和副团长杨朔率领的中国人民对外文化协会代表团。
    接见时在场的有中国驻锡兰大使张灿明和锡中友好协会秘书阿南达·库马腊。这位部长同代表团进行了诚挚的交谈。
    在过去几天中，代表团访问了古老而美丽的锡兰城市波隆纳鲁瓦、阿努拉达普拉、康提和以盛产宝石著名的拉特纳普拉。在那里，锡中友好协会的各个分会举行了庆祝中华人民共和国国庆的公众集会，在会上表演了锡兰传统的民间舞蹈和音乐。
    代表团的团员们还访问了维迪阿兰卡拉大学和锡兰大学。他们受到锡兰各界人民的热情接待。
