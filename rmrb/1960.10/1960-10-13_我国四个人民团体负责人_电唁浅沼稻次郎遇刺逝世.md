Title: Chinese Four People's Organizations' Leaders Send Condolences on the Assassination of Inejiro Asanuma

Author:

Date: 1960-10-13
Page: 5

    Leaders of Four Chinese People's Organizations
    Send Condolences on the Assassination of Inejiro Asanuma
    Condolence Message from Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
    Xinhua News Agency, 12th -- Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, today sent a telegram to the headquarters of the Japanese Socialist Party, expressing condolences on the assassination of Inejiro Asanuma.
    The telegram said: "We are shocked and saddened to hear of the assassination of Mr. Inejiro Asanuma. Please accept our condolences and convey our sympathies to Mr. Asanuma's family."
    The telegram said: "Mr. Asanuma made great contributions to the promotion of friendship between the people of China and Japan, the opposition to our common enemy, U.S. imperialism, and the defense of peace in Asia and the world."
    The telegram said: "We are convinced that the Japanese people will be even more determined to fight for Japan's independence, democracy, peace, and neutrality against U.S. imperialism and its agents as a result of the assassination of Mr. Asanuma by the reactionaries. The Chinese people firmly support the Japanese people's patriotic and just struggle. Final victory belongs to the Japanese people."
    Condolence Message from Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity
    Xinhua News Agency, 12th -- Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, today sent a telegram to the headquarters of the Japanese Socialist Party, expressing condolences on the unfortunate death of Inejiro Asanuma due to assassination.
    The telegram said: "We are shocked to hear of Mr. Inejiro Asanuma's unfortunate death due to assassination by fascist thugs. Please accept our condolences and convey our deepest sympathies to Mr. Asanuma's family."
    The telegram said: "Mr. Asanuma was an active fighter in the Japanese people's struggle against U.S. imperialist aggression, against the U.S.-Japan military alliance treaty, and for Japan's independence, democracy, peace, and neutrality. He also made important contributions to promoting friendship between the people of China and Japan and developing the solidarity of the people of Asian and African countries. Although the Japanese reactionaries attempt to obstruct the Japanese people's struggle and intimidate Japanese progressive and democratic forces by assassinating Mr. Asanuma, we firmly believe that the heroic Japanese people cannot be intimidated. They will continue to advance with the support of peace-loving people of all countries, and fight to the end to defeat U.S. imperialism and its running dogs."
    Condolence Message from Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions
    Xinhua News Agency, 12th -- Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, today sent a telegram to the headquarters of the Japanese Socialist Party, expressing deep condolences on the murder of Inejiro Asanuma by thugs. The telegram said: "We are shocked to learn of the murder of Mr. Inejiro Asanuma by thugs, and express our deep condolences. We extend our deepest sympathies to Mr. Inejiro Asanuma's family, and express our strong indignation at the U.S.-Japan reactionaries' connivance at the assassination."
    The telegram said: "Mr. Inejiro Asanuma made important contributions to the Japanese people's anti-U.S. patriotic and just struggle. Therefore, the assassination of Mr. Inejiro Asanuma fully proves that the U.S.-Japan reactionaries are the mortal enemies of Japan's independence, peace, democracy, and neutrality. We are confident that this incident will only arouse further unity among Japan's patriotic and democratic forces to carry out the struggle against Japanese militarism and against the U.S.-Japan military alliance treaty."
    Condolence Message from Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs
    Xinhua News Agency, 12th -- Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, today sent a telegram to the headquarters of the Japanese Socialist Party and the family of Inejiro Asanuma, expressing condolences on the death of Inejiro Asanuma.
    The telegram said: "We are shocked to learn that Mr. Inejiro Asanuma, Chairman of the Japanese Socialist Party, was unfortunately killed by fascist thugs. On behalf of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and in my own name, I express my deep condolences and extend my most sincere sympathies to the family of Mr. Inejiro Asanuma."
    The telegram said: "During his lifetime, Mr. Inejiro Asanuma, together with the Japanese people, waged an unyielding struggle against U.S. imperialism and the revival of Japanese militarism, the common enemies of the people of China and Japan, and made valuable contributions to enhancing friendly relations between the people of China and Japan. We are confident that Mr. Inejiro Asanuma's glorious deeds will continue to inspire the Japanese people to fight for the cause of Japan's independence, democracy, peace, and neutrality."

### 我国四个人民团体负责人  电唁浅沼稻次郎遇刺逝世

1960-10-13
第5版()
专栏：

    我国四个人民团体负责人
    电唁浅沼稻次郎遇刺逝世
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席
    郭沫若的唁电
    新华社12日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若今天致电日本社会党本部，吊唁浅沼稻次郎遇刺逝世。
    电报说：“惊悉浅沼稻次郎先生遇刺逝世，谨致哀悼并向浅沼先生家属表示慰问。”
    电报说：“浅沼先生在促进中日两国人民的友好、反对我们共同的敌人美帝国主义以及保卫亚洲和世界和平的斗争中，作出了很大的贡献。”
    电报说：“我们深信，日本人民必将由于浅沼先生被反动派杀害的事件而更加坚定地为争取日本的独立、民主、和平和中立，同美帝国主义及其代理人进行斗争。中国人民坚决支持日本人民的爱国正义斗争。最后胜利是属于日本人民的。”
    中国亚非团结委员会主席
    廖承志的唁电
    新华社12日讯　中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志今天致电日本社会党本部，吊唁浅沼稻次郎被刺不幸逝世。
    电报说：“惊闻浅沼稻次郎先生遭法西斯暴徒行刺不幸逝世，谨致哀悼，并通过你们向浅沼先生家属表示深切的慰问。”
    电报说：“浅沼先生是日本人民反对美帝国主义侵略、反对日美军事同盟条约、争取日本的独立、民主、和平、中立的斗争中的一位积极战士，并且对促进中日两国人民友好、发展亚非各国人民团结的事业作出了重要的贡献。尽管日本反动派企图以刺杀浅沼先生来阻挠日本人民的斗争，恫吓日本进步民主力量，但是我们深信，英勇的日本人民是恫吓不了的，他们必将在爱好和平的各国人民支持之下继续前进，为击败美帝国主义及其走狗而斗争到底。”
    中华全国总工会主席
    刘宁一的唁电
    新华社12日讯　中华全国总工会主席刘宁一今天致电日本社会党本部，对浅沼稻次郎被暴徒杀害，表示沉痛悼念。电报说：“惊悉浅沼稻次郎先生被暴徒杀害，谨致以沉痛悼念之意，对浅沼稻次郎先生的家属表示深切的慰问，同时对日美反动派纵容暴徒的刺杀行为表示强烈的愤慨。”
    电报说：“浅沼稻次郎先生在日本人民反美爱国正义斗争中，作出了重要的贡献。因此，刺杀浅沼稻次郎先生的事件，充分证明美日反动派是日本人民独立、和平、民主、中立的死敌。我们确信，这一事件只能激起日本爱国民主力量的进一步团结，开展反对日本军国主义、反对日美军事同盟条约的斗争。”
    中国人民外交学会会长
    张奚若的唁电
    新华社12日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今天致电日本社会党本部暨浅沼稻次郎家属，吊唁浅沼稻次郎逝世。
      电报说：“惊悉日本社会党委员长浅沼稻次郎先生为法西斯暴徒所刺不幸逝世，谨代表中国人民外交学会和以我个人的名义，表示沉痛的哀悼，并向浅沼稻次郎先生的家属致以最诚挚的慰问。”
    电报说：“浅沼稻次郎先生生前和日本人民一起，为反对中日两国人民的共同敌人美帝国主义和日本军国主义的复活进行了不屈不挠的斗争，为增进中日两国人民之间的友好关系做了可贵的贡献。我们深信，浅沼稻次郎先生的光荣事迹将继续鼓舞日本人民为争取日本的独立民主、和平中立的事业而斗争。”
