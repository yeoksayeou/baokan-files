Title: Commander of Soviet Rocket Forces, NedeLin Dies in Accident
Author:
Date: 1960-10-27
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, October 26th, Moscow (TASS dispatch, October 25th): Marshal of Artillery NedeLin, Commander of the Soviet Rocket Forces, unfortunately died in a plane accident on the 24th while performing his duties.
    This was announced in an official communique by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union. The communique stated that NedeLin, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Hero of the Soviet Union, Deputy Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, and Commander of the Soviet Rocket Forces, was one of the outstanding military strategists and builders of the Soviet armed forces, and a renowned hero of the Great Patriotic War.
    The Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union has decided that a funeral committee, including Marshal Zakharov, will be formed. The funeral will be held in Red Square, Moscow.
    Xinhua News Agency, October 26th, Moscow (TASS dispatch, October 26th): Here is the obituary regarding Marshal of Artillery NedeLin's unfortunate death on October 24th in a plane accident, signed by Khrushchev, Brezhnev, Kozlov, Marshal Malinovsky, and other national leaders and military figures of the Soviet Union. The obituary stated that NedeLin served in the Soviet army for more than forty years, dedicating all his knowledge, energy, and rich experience to the sacred cause of defending the motherland from imperialist invasion, and to strengthening the defense capabilities of the Soviet state and building and improving the artillery of the Soviet armed forces. The obituary pointed out that NedeLin's high organizational skills were particularly powerfully demonstrated during the Great Patriotic War, when he held important positions - Commander of the Artillery Corps, Commander of the Artillery of the Southwestern Front and the Third Ukrainian Front, showing himself to be a talented, hard-working, profoundly knowledgeable, brave and resolute commander, infinitely loyal to the Soviet people and the cause of communism.
    In recent years, the Communist Party and the Soviet government appointed Marshal NedeLin to lead the new type of armed forces, appointed him as Deputy Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union and Commander of the Rocket Forces. In this extremely important position, NedeLin tirelessly, selflessly, with his characteristic energy and sensitivity, and with a high sense of Party responsibility, completed the major tasks of the Party and the government.

### 苏联火箭部队总司令  涅杰林遇难逝世

1960-10-27
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联火箭部队总司令
    涅杰林遇难逝世
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科25日讯：苏联火箭部队总司令、炮兵主帅涅杰林24日在执行任务时因飞机失事不幸遇难。
    这是苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议在一项正式公报中宣布的。公报说，苏联共产党中央委员会候补委员、苏联最高苏维埃代表、苏联英雄、苏联国防部副部长和苏联火箭部队总司令涅杰林，是苏联武装部队的杰出的军事家和建设者之一，是伟大的卫国战争的闻名英雄。
    苏联部长会议已决定由扎哈罗夫元帅等组成治丧委员会，丧礼将在莫斯科红场举行。
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科26日讯：这里公布了由赫鲁晓夫、勃列日涅夫、科兹洛夫、马利诺夫斯基元帅和苏联其他国家领导人和军事活动家签署的关于炮兵主帅涅杰林因飞机失事于10月24日不幸遇难的讣文。讣文说，涅杰林在苏联军队服务四十多年，把他的全部知识、充沛的精力和丰富的经验贡献给了保卫祖国不受帝国主义侵犯的神圣事业，贡献给了加强苏维埃国家的防御力量、建设和改进苏联武装部队炮兵的事业。讣文指出，涅杰林的高度组织才能在卫国战争期间特别有力地表现出来，当时他担任重要的职务——炮兵军团司令、西南战线和乌克兰第三战线炮兵司令，表现出了他是一个有才干的、爱好劳动的深刻通晓军事的勇敢刚毅的指挥官，无限忠于苏联人民和共产主义事业。
    最近几年来，共产党和苏联政府任命涅杰林主帅领导新式武装力量，任命他担任苏联国防部副部长和火箭部队总司令。在这个极其重要的岗位上，涅杰林不倦地、忘我地、以他所特有的精力和敏感、怀抱着高度的党的责任感，完成了党和政府的重大任务。
