Title: Three Japanese Delegations Leave Beijing to Visit Various Locations
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 9th: The National Council for Preventing Revision of the Security Treaty delegation from Japan, headed by delegation leader Takeo Tobe and deputy leaders Bunichiro Sano and Kō Tokumiya, the Japan Peace Committee delegation headed by Kenjuro Yanagida, and the Japan Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee delegation headed by Tokumatsu Sakamoto, have departed Beijing on the 8th and 9th respectively, to visit various locations. Among those who went to the station to see them off were Burhan, Li Dequan, Qu Tangliang, Wu Maosun, Zhao Anbo, Yang Zheng, Wang Mingyuan, Zheng Senyu, Zhu Ziqi, Yang Chunsong, Qi Muguan, and other figures from various circles. Japanese peace figure Kinkazu Saionji and Korean peace figure Hwang Bong-koo, who are in Beijing, also went to the station to see them off.

### 日本三个代表团离京去各地访问

1960-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    日本三个代表团离京去各地访问
    新华社9日讯　以团长户叶武和副团长佐野文一郎、东宫考为首的日本阻止修改安全条约国民会议代表团、以柳田谦十郎为首的日本和平委员会代表团和以坂本德松为首的日本亚非团结委员会代表团，已经分别在8日、9日离开北京去各地参观访问。到车站欢送的有包尔汉、李德全、区棠亮、吴茂荪、赵安博、杨正、王明远、郑森禹、朱子奇、杨春松、戚慕光等各界人士。在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一和朝鲜和平人士黄凤九，也到车站欢送。
