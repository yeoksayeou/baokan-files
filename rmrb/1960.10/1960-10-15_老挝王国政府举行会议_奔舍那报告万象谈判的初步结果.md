Title: Lao Royal Government Holds Meeting; Quinim Pholsena Reports on Preliminary Results of Vientiane Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1960-10-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 14th: Vientiane Radio broadcast today that the Lao Royal Government held a meeting on October 12th, presided over by Prime Minister Phouma.

Lao Minister of Information and Consolidation of Civil Servants, Quinim Pholsena, reported at the meeting on the results of the preliminary negotiations between the Royal Government delegation and the Lao Patriotic Front delegation on October 11th.

Subsequently, Prime Minister Phouma reported on the evolution of the current political and military situation in Laos.

Vientiane Radio also broadcast today that the Lao Royal Government Ministry of Defense issued a communique announcing that the Ministry of Defense convened a meeting of officers with the rank of battalion commander and above on the afternoon of October 11th. The meeting heard a speech by Prince Phouma, Prime Minister, Minister of National Defense and Minister of Veterans Affairs.

### 老挝王国政府举行会议  奔舍那报告万象谈判的初步结果

1960-10-15
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝王国政府举行会议
    奔舍那报告万象谈判的初步结果
    新华社河内14日电　据万象电台今天广播，老挝王国政府10月12日在富马首相主持下举行了会议。
    老挝宣传部和巩固职员部大臣贵宁·奔舍那在会议上报告了王国政府代表团同老挝爱国战线党代表团在10月11日初步谈判的结果。
    随后，富马首相报告了老挝当前政治和军事情况的演变。
    万象电台今天还广播说，老挝王国政府国防部发表公报宣布，国防部在10月11日下午召集了营长以上的军官会议。会议听取了政府首相兼国防部和复员军人部大臣富马亲王的讲话。
