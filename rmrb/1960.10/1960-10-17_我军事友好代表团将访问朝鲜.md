Title: Our Military Friendship Delegation Will Visit North Korea
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 17th. At the invitation of Comrade Kim Kwang-hyup, Vice Premier of the Cabinet and Minister of National Defense of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to Korea will pay a friendly visit to Korea in late October this year.

### 我军事友好代表团将访问朝鲜

1960-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

    我军事友好代表团将访问朝鲜
    新华社17日讯　应朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相兼民族保卫相金光侠同志邀请，中华人民共和国访朝军事友好代表团将于今年10月下旬前往朝鲜进行友好访问。
