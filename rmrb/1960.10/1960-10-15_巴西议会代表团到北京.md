Title: Brazilian Parliamentary Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 14th: An eighteen-member Brazilian parliamentary delegation, headed by Brazilian Senator and Chairman of the Senate Finance Committee, Gaspar Velloso, arrived in Beijing by train tonight. The delegation is visiting our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. Members of the delegation include Senator and Vice Chairman of the Senate Economic Committee, Fernandes Távora; Congressman and Second Vice Speaker of the House of Representatives, Nestor Jost; and Congressman and Deputy Leader of the Opposition Party in the House of Representatives, João Seixas Dória.

Welcoming them at the station were Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Hu Yuzhi, Vice President, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, and Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General, of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and others.

### 巴西议会代表团到北京

1960-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

    巴西议会代表团到北京
    新华社14日讯　以巴西参议员、参议院财政委员会主席加斯帕·贝略索为首的巴西议会代表团一行十八人，今晚乘火车到达北京。代表团是应中国人民外交学会邀请前来我国访问的。代表团团员中包括参议员、参议院经济委员会副主席费尔南德思·塔伏拉，众议员、众议院第二副议长内斯托·何斯特，众议员、众议院反对党派副领袖霍阿沃·塞克萨斯·多里阿。
    到车站欢迎的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏，中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之、秘书长吴茂荪、副秘书长吴晓达等。
