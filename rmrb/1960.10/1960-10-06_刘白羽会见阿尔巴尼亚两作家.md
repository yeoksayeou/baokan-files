Title: Liu Baiyu Meets with Two Albanian Writers
Author:
Date: 1960-10-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd. Liu Baiyu, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association and Vice President of the Sino-Albanian Friendship Association, met on the afternoon of October 1st with Shefket Musaraj, Secretary-General of the Albanian Writers and Artists Association, and Ali Abdihoxha, an Albanian writer, who were visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese Writers Association.

Present at the meeting were Yan Wenjing, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Writers Association, Guo Xiaochuan, Secretary and Secretary-General of the Secretariat of the Chinese Writers Association, and Xing Ye, Deputy Director of the Foreign Literature Committee of the Chinese Writers Association.

Çaci, an Albanian journalist and well-known poet stationed in China, was also present at the meeting.

### 刘白羽会见阿尔巴尼亚两作家

1960-10-06
第3版()
专栏：

    刘白羽会见阿尔巴尼亚两作家
    新华社3日讯　中国作家协会副主席、中阿友协副会长刘白羽1日下午会见了应中国作家协会邀请来我国访问的阿尔巴尼亚作家艺术家协会秘书长谢夫卡特·穆萨莱和阿尔巴尼亚作家阿里·阿布迪霍查。
    陪同会见的有，中国作家协会书记处书记严文井，中国作家协会书记处书记、秘书长郭小川，中国作家协会外国文学委员会副主任邢野。
    会见时，阿尔巴尼亚驻华记者、著名诗人恰奇也在座。
