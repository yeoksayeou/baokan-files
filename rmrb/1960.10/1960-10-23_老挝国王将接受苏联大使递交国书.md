Title: Lao King to Accept Credentials from Soviet Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1960-10-23
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 21st: According to a broadcast by Vientiane Radio this noon, the King of Laos, Sisavang Vatthana, has decided to accept the credentials from Abramov, the Ambassador of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics to the Kingdom of Laos, at the Royal Palace in the old capital of Luang Prabang on the afternoon of the 26th.

### 老挝国王将接受苏联大使递交国书

1960-10-23
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝国王将接受苏联大使递交国书
    新华社河内21日电　据万象电台今天中午广播，老挝国王西萨旺·瓦达纳决定在26日下午在故都琅勃拉邦王宫接受苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟驻老挝王国大使阿布拉莫夫递交国书。
