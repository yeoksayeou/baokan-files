Title: International Mass Organizations, People's Organizations of Various Countries, and People of Peace Send Congratulatory Telegrams to Our People's Organizations on Our National Day

Author:
Date: 1960-10-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 3rd. As we celebrate our National Day, our people's organizations have received congratulatory telegrams from many foreign people's organizations and their leaders, as well as people of peace.

The foreign national trade union organizations and leaders who sent congratulatory telegrams to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions include: the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, Goqo Nushi, Chairman of the Central Council of Trade Unions of Albania, Prahov, Chairman of the Central Council of Trade Unions of Bulgaria, Herbert Warnke, Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Council of the Free German Trade Union Federation, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Mongolia, Ignacy Loga-Sowinski, Chairman of the Central Council of Trade Unions of Poland, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Romania, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Czechoslovakia, Thekin Lun, Chairman of the Burmese Trade Union Congress, Kache Dul, General Secretary of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Iraq, Reza Rousta, General Secretary of the Central Council of the United Trade Unions of Iran, and Novella, General Secretary of the Italian General Confederation of Labour.

In addition, Odon Alvarez Cambara, Secretary of Foreign Relations of the Cuban Federation of Trade Unions, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong to congratulate him on our National Day.

Some international trade union organizations and their leaders also sent congratulatory telegrams to our trade union organizations. These international trade union organizations and leaders are: Mevaldt, Secretary of the Textile and Leather Workers' International, the Secretariat of the Transport, Ports and Fisheries Workers' International, Polkara of the Construction Workers' International, Chaihariv of the Food Workers' International, and Delanu and Bissaro of the Education Workers' International.

The international youth and student organizations and youth and student organizations of various countries that sent congratulatory telegrams and letters to the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, the All-China Youth Federation, and the All-China Students' Federation are: the World Federation of Democratic Youth, the International Student Union, the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union, the Soviet Youth Organizations Committee, the Council of University Students of the Soviet Youth Organizations Committee, the Central Committee of the Albanian Labour Youth League, the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Dimitrov Communist Youth League, the Central Committee of the Hungarian Communist Youth League, the Vietnam Labour Youth Union, the Vietnam Youth Federation, the Vietnam Students' Union, Horst Schumann, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Free German Youth League, the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Youth League, the Korean Student Committee, the Central Committee of the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League, the Central Committee of the Polish Socialist Youth Union, the Polish Youth Organizations Cooperation Committee, the Polish Student Association, the Polish Scout Union, the Central Committee of the Romanian Labour Youth Union, the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Youth League, the Cuban Rebel Army Youth Association, the Executive Committee of the Iraqi Democratic Youth Federation, the Central Committee of the Israeli Communist Youth League, the Sudanese Communist Student League, and the Australian Eureka Youth League.

The organizations that sent congratulatory telegrams to the All-China Women's Federation are: the Soviet Women's Committee, the Albanian Women's Union, Rada Todorova, Chairman of the Bulgarian Democratic Women's Committee, the Vietnam Women's Union, Ilse Thiele, First Chairwoman of the German Democratic Women's Federation, the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Women's League, the Polish Women's League, the Czechoslovak Women's Committee, the Dutch Women's Movement, the All-England Women's Conference, and the Israeli Democratic Women's League. Violetta Teresa Casal, head of the Cuban women's delegation that visited China in 1959 and Chairman of the Cuban Association of Dramatic Artists, also sent a congratulatory telegram to the National Women's Federation.

The national peace organizations and foreign people of peace who sent congratulatory telegrams to the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee are: the Soviet Peace Committee, the Soviet Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, the Albanian Peace Committee, the Vietnam Peace Committee and the Vietnam Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, the Mongolian Peace Committee and the Mongolian Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, the Romanian Committee for the Defense of Peace, and the British peace activists Bernal and Montagu.

The foreign friendship organizations and leaders who sent congratulatory telegrams to the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, our friendship associations with socialist countries and Asian countries, and the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association are: the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association, Petrov, Vice Chairman of the Bulgarian Committee for Friendly and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Nguyen Xuan Thuy, Acting President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, Ha Yang-cheon, Chairman of the Korean-Chinese Friendship Association, Barjinyam, Chairman of the Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Association, Jędrichowski, Chairman of the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association, the Romanian Association for Foreign Cultural Relations, the Japan-China Friendship Association, Tanzan Ishibashi, President of the Japan-Soviet Association, the Japan-China Trade Promotion Association, the Japan-China Trade Promotion Diet Members' League, the Burmese-Chinese Friendship Association, the National Executive Committee of the India-China Friendship Association, the Indonesian-Chinese Friendship Association, Abed Zazala, Chairman of the Iraqi-Chinese Friendship Association, the Ceylon-Chinese Friendship Association, Bahadur, Chairman of the Nepal-China Friendship Association, Shrestha and his wife, Honorary Joint Secretary of the Nepal Chamber of Commerce, the Israeli Committee for Relations with China, Ayedé Santamaría, President of the Cuban "Casa de las Américas," the Cuban-Chinese Friendship Association, Guillermo Mendoyo, President of the Mexican-Chinese Friendship Association, and Alcalde, President and Duran Do, Vice President and Manrique Cavayero, Secretary of the Peruvian-Chinese Friendship Association, the Britain-China Friendship Association, the Norway-China Cultural Association, and the Belgium-China Association.

In addition, the ambassadors of Vietnam, the German Democratic Republic, Korea, Mongolia, Poland, Romania, and Czechoslovakia to China have written letters to our country and the friendship associations of these countries to congratulate our National Day.

### 国际性群众组织、各国人民团体以及和平人士  致电我国人民团体祝贺我国国庆

1960-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    国际性群众组织、各国人民团体以及和平人士
    致电我国人民团体祝贺我国国庆
    新华社3日讯　在庆祝我国国庆节的时候，我国各人民团体收到了国外许多人民团体和负责人以及和平人士的贺电。
    向中华全国总工会发来贺电的外国全国性工会组织和负责人有：全苏工会中央理事会、阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会主席果戈·努什、保加利亚工会中央理事会主席普拉霍夫、自由德国工会联合会中央理事会主席团主席赫伯特·瓦恩克、蒙古工会中央理事会、波兰工会中央理事会主席伊格纳泽·洛加—索文斯基、罗马尼亚工会中央理事会、捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会、缅甸工会大会主席德钦伦、伊拉克工会总联合会总书记卡却杜尔、伊朗统一工会中央理事会总书记礼萨·鲁斯塔、意大利总工会总书记诺维拉。
    此外，古巴工会联合会对外关系书记奥东·阿尔瓦雷斯·坎巴致电毛泽东主席，祝贺我国国庆节。
    一些国际工会组织和负责人也向我国的工会组织发来了贺电。这些国际工会组织和负责人是：纺织、皮革工会国际书记梅伐尔特，运输、港口与渔业工人工会国际书记处，建筑工会国际波尔卡拉、食品工会国际柴哈里夫、教育工会国际德拉努和比萨罗。
