Title: Double Happiness: National Day and the Signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty; People of All Ethnic Groups in Yunnan Border Areas Gather to Celebrate; Burmese Friends Invited to Participate in the Festivities and Receive a Warm Welcome
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 2nd. People of all ethnic groups in Ruili County and other border areas of Yunnan Province held gatherings and various celebrations on October 1st to celebrate the eleventh anniversary of the founding of the People’s Republic of China and the signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty. Friends from the friendly neighbor of Burma were also invited to participate in the local celebrations.

On this day of "double happiness," the people of Yunnan, who live along the more than 2,000-kilometer Sino-Burmese border, were immersed in festive joy. Celebration gatherings and get-togethers were held in Ruili County, Zhangfeng District of Longchuan County, Mengding Town of Gengma Dai and Wa Autonomous County, Cangyuan Wa Autonomous County, and Menglian Dai, Lahu and Wa Autonomous County of Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture. Liu Piyun, Vice Governor of Yunnan Province, and Wu Zhiyuan, Cun Shusheng, and Zhang Tianfang, Vice Chairmen of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, traveled from Kunming to Ruili County and Mengding Town respectively to participate in local celebration gatherings and get-togethers.

In the picturesque border city of Ruili, more than 8,000 people of all ethnic groups held a grand celebration meeting. Burmese friends, led by Lieutenant Colonel Ye Min, the former commander of the Sixth Brigade of the Burmese Federal Defense Force and the current commander of the Chin Mountain Battalion, were invited by Dao Annian, the county head of Ruili County, and Major Fu Yuhua, the commander of the garrison, to attend the celebration meeting. On this day, Ruili County was decorated with lanterns and streamers everywhere. The nearby villages beat gongs and drums. People of all ethnic groups dressed in colorful festive costumes, beating elephant-foot drums, and dancing peacock dances, came to the county town from all directions. When Dao Annian, the county head of Ruili County, announced at the celebration meeting that the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty had been signed in Beijing, the whole audience responded with warm applause and cheers. People shouted in different languages: "Long live the kinship-like friendship between the Chinese and Burmese people!" Liu Piyun, Vice Governor of Yunnan Province, extended a warm welcome at the meeting on behalf of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee and the people of the entire province to friends from the friendly neighbor of Burma. He said: The signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty is an event of great significance. The people of Yunnan Province warmly celebrate the signing of this treaty, firmly support this treaty, and will work together with the Burmese people to continuously consolidate and develop Sino-Burmese friendly relations. Lieutenant Colonel Ye Min was also invited to speak at the celebration meeting. He warmly congratulated the people of Ruili County on the Chinese National Day and also warmly congratulated the signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty. He expressed his firm belief that the friendship between the Chinese and Burmese people will flow forever like the waters of the Salween River. Zhang Tianfang, Vice Chairman of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Dao Annian, the county head of Ruili County, Major Fu Yuhua, the commander of the garrison, and representatives of farmers from Ruili County also spoke at the celebration meeting. They unanimously expressed their support for the signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty and expressed their willingness to strengthen the traditional friendship between the border people of China and Burma with practical actions under the leadership of the government.

In Mengding Town of Gengma Dai and Wa Autonomous County, more than 3,000 people of all ethnic groups held a celebration meeting. The Burmese friends who were invited to participate in the meeting included Captain Niu Ai, the acting county head of the Hpa-an District of the Union of Burma, Captain Jue Sha, the assistant to the county head of Kokang County, Lieutenant Weng Gehe, the garrison officer of Kunlong County, and other military and political officials totaling nine people, as well as twenty-four attendants. They were warmly welcomed. The Burmese border garrison team and the Gengma County agency team also held a friendly friendship match. Zhang Gan, the acting county head of Gengma County, hosted a banquet to entertain the Burmese friends.

In Cangyuan Wa Autonomous County, Menglian Dai, Lahu and Wa Autonomous County, and Zhangfeng District of Longchuan County, celebration meetings with more than 2,000 participants were also held on the 1st, and local Burmese military and political officials and people of all ethnic groups were invited to participate in warm and friendly gatherings. In addition, the people of all ethnic groups in Lushui County of Nujiang Lisu Autonomous Prefecture also held a grand celebration meeting for the eleventh anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty.

### 国庆佳节和中缅签订边界条约双喜临门  云南边境各族人民集会庆祝  缅甸朋友们应邀参加联欢受到热烈欢迎

1960-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

    国庆佳节和中缅签订边界条约双喜临门
    云南边境各族人民集会庆祝
    缅甸朋友们应邀参加联欢受到热烈欢迎
    据新华社昆明2日电　云南边境瑞丽县等地各族人民1日纷纷举行集会和各种庆祝活动，庆祝建国十一周年和中缅边界条约的签订，友好邻邦缅甸的朋友们也应邀参加了当地的庆祝活动。
    在这“双喜临门”的日子里，居住在两千多公里长的中缅边境上的云南人民沉浸在节日的欢乐中。庆祝集会和联欢活动分别在德宏傣族景颇族自治州的瑞丽县、陇川县章凤区、耿马傣族佧佤族自治县孟定镇、沧源佧佤族自治县、孟连傣族拉祜族佧佤族自治县等地举行。云南省副省长刘披云和政协云南省副主席吴志渊、寸树声、张天放分别从昆明赶到瑞丽县和孟定镇参加了当地的庆祝集会和联欢活动。
    在风景如画的边城瑞丽，各族人民八千多人举行了隆重的庆祝大会，以前任缅甸联邦国防军第六旅旅长、现任钦山营营长耶明中校为首的缅甸朋友们，应瑞丽县县长刀安年和驻军指挥官傅育华少校的邀请前来参加了庆祝大会。这天，瑞丽县城处处张灯结彩，附近的村寨鸣锣击鼓，各族人民穿上了鲜艳的节日服装，敲着象脚鼓、跳着孔雀舞，从四面八方来到了县城。当瑞丽县县长刀安年在庆祝大会上宣布中缅边界条约已经在北京签字时，全场报以热烈的鼓掌和欢呼，人们用不同的语言高呼：“中缅两国人民亲戚般的友谊万岁！”云南省副省长刘披云在会上代表云南省人民委员会和全省人民向来自友好邻邦缅甸的朋友表示热烈欢迎。他说：中缅边界条约的签订是一件具有重大意义的事件。云南省人民热情地庆祝这个条约的签订，坚决拥护这个条约，并将同缅甸人民一道为进一步巩固和发展中缅友好关系而不断努力。耶明中校也应邀在庆祝会上讲话，他向瑞丽县人民热烈祝贺中国国庆佳节，并且热烈祝贺中缅边界条约的签订。他表示深信，中缅两国人民的友谊将会像萨尔温江的水一样永流不尽。政协云南省副主席张天放，瑞丽县县长刀安年，驻军指挥官傅育华少校以及瑞丽县农民代表都在庆祝会上讲了话，他们一致表示拥护中缅边界条约的签订，并表示要在政府领导下用实际行动加强中缅边民之间的传统友谊。
    在耿马傣族佧佤族自治县的孟定镇，各族人民三千多人举行了庆祝大会。应邀前来参加大会的缅甸朋友有缅甸联邦户拔县代理县长纽埃上尉，果岗县县长助理觉刹上尉，滚弄县驻军翁哥赫中尉等军政官员九人以及随从人员二十四人。他们受到了热烈欢迎。缅甸边境驻军球队同耿马县的机关球队还举行了联欢友谊赛，耿马县代理县长张干并设宴招待缅甸朋友们。
    在沧源佧佤族自治县、孟连傣族拉祜族佧佤族自治县和陇川县的章凤区，1日也都分别举行了有两千多人参加的庆祝大会，邀请了缅甸的地方军政官员和各族边民前来参加热烈友好的联欢。此外，怒江傈僳族自治州泸水县的各族人民也为建国十一周年和签订中缅边界条约举行了隆重的庆祝大会。
