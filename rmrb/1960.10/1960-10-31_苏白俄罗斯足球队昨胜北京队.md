Title: Belorussian Soccer Team from the Soviet Union Defeats Beijing Team Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1960-10-31
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, October 30th: The "Belarus" football team from Minsk, Soviet Union, played its first friendly match in China against the Beijing football team at the Beijing Workers' Stadium this afternoon. 30,000 spectators watched the wonderful performance of the Chinese and Soviet athletes. The visitors won the game with a score of 2-0; the halftime score was 0-0. After the game, Wan Li, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and Huang Zhong, Deputy Director of the State Sports Commission, met with the athletes of both sides at the stadium and took photos with them.

### 苏白俄罗斯足球队昨胜北京队

1960-10-31
第2版()
专栏：

    苏白俄罗斯足球队昨胜北京队
    据新华社30日讯　苏联明斯克“白俄罗斯”足球队，今天下午在北京工人体育场同北京足球队举行访华第一场友谊比赛。有三万多观众观看了中、苏两国运动员的精彩表演。比赛结果客队以二比零获胜，上半场比数是零比零。比赛结束后，北京市副市长万里、国家体委副主任黄中到球场接见了双方运动员，并同他们在一起照了相。
