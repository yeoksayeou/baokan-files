Title: Support Vientiane Negotiations, Oppose U.S. Interference: Rallies Held by 15,000 People in Four Provinces of Laos
Author:
Date: 1960-10-29
Page: 6
　
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 28th. The Radio of the Laotian Fighting Forces broadcast on the 27th: More than 15,000 people in the Lao provinces of Bassac, Saravane, Khammouane, and Phongsaly held rallies respectively, opposing U.S. imperialism and the Phoumi-Boun Oum traitorous clique, and supporting the negotiations between the Royal Government and the Lao Patriotic Front.

From September 22nd to October 10th, over 10,000 people belonging to the areas of Lao Ngam, Kong Sedone, Saravane County, Ta Oy, Bassac, and Ba Song in the provinces of Bassac and Saravane held rallies in more than ten locations, distributed over 8,000 leaflets, and sent hundreds of letters of recommendation to the King, the government, the coup committee, the Lao Patriotic Front, local governments, and the pro-peace and neutrality policy committee. These recommendations expressed firm opposition to the interference of U.S. imperialism, Thailand and South Vietnam's reactionaries and the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization; firm opposition to the Phoumi Nosavan traitorous clique's conspiracies to split the country, undermine peace and neutrality, and disrupt national harmony; support for the Souvanna Phouma government, the Lao Patriotic Front, the coup committee, and the negotiations between the Royal Government and the Lao Patriotic Front. The recommendations also demanded that local governments not oppress and suppress the people, but grant the people the freedom to move around for livelihood, and oppose arresting young people to serve as soldiers.

The recommendations advocated establishing diplomatic relations with all countries and accepting aid from these countries without any strings attached.

The radio also broadcast today that people from Boualapha, Chang Ho, and Boun Pao villages in Khammouane Province held mass rallies in nine different locations from October 10th to 22nd, with 2,000 people participating, supporting the Lao Patriotic Front Central Committee's statement on the negotiation issue, and resolutely opposing the U.S. imperialism and its lackeys, the Phoumi-Boun Oum traitorous clique, who are attempting to split the motherland and undermine the policy of peace, neutrality and national harmony.

It is also reported that more than 3,000 people from various villages in Nam Ya County, Phongsaly Province, held rallies supporting the Royal Government and the Lao Patriotic Front's policies of peace, neutrality, and national harmony, supporting the negotiations between the Royal Government and the Lao Patriotic Front, and opposing the interference of U.S. imperialism and the activities of the Phoumi-Boun Oum traitorous clique. Local people also passed a recommendation exposing the criminal activities of Khamvinh, the governor of Phongsaly Province, in persecuting the people and provoking the Laotian Fighting Forces.

### 支持万象谈判　反对美国干涉  老挝四省一万五千多人集会

1960-10-29
第6版()
专栏：

    支持万象谈判　反对美国干涉
    老挝四省一万五千多人集会
    新华社河内28日电　寮国战斗部队电台27日广播：老挝巴色、沙拉湾、甘蒙和丰沙里等省的一万五千多人民分别举行集会，反对美帝国主义和富米—文翁叛国集团，并支持王国政府和老挝爱国战线党之间的谈判。
    从9月22日到10月10日，属于巴色和沙拉湾两省的寮银、孔塞东、沙拉湾县、塔廷、巴色、巴松等地的一万多人民，分别在十多个地方举行集会，散发了八千多张传单，向国王、政府、政变委员会、老挝爱国战线党、地方政府和拥护和平中立政策委员会等寄了成百封建议书。这些建议书表示坚决反对美帝国主义、泰国和南越的反动分子以及东南亚集团的干涉；坚决反对富米·诺萨万叛国集团的分裂国家、破坏和平中立和民族和睦的阴谋；支持梭发那·富马政府、老挝爱国战线党、政变委员会，支持王国政府和老挝爱国战线党之间的谈判。建议书还要求地方政府不得压迫和镇压人民，要给人民往来谋生的自由权利，反对逮捕青年当兵。
    建议书主张同一切国家建立外交关系，并接受这些国家的不附带任何束缚条件的援助。
    电台今天还广播，甘蒙省布拉法、昌霍、奔波等乡人民从10月10日到22日在九个不同地方分别举行了有二千人参加的群众集会，支持老挝爱国战线党中央关于谈判问题的声明，坚决反对企图分裂祖国、破坏和平中立和民族和睦政策的美帝国主义及其走狗富米—文翁叛国集团。
    另据报道，丰沙里省南牙县各村三千多人举行集会支持王国政府和老挝爱国战线党的和平、中立和民族和睦政策，支持王国政府同老挝爱国战线党之间的谈判，反对美帝国主义的干涉和富米—文翁叛国集团的活动。当地人民还通过了建议书，揭露丰沙里省省长坎温迫害人民、向寮国战斗部队挑衅等罪恶活动。
