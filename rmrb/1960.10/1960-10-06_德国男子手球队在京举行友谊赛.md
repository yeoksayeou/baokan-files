Title: German Men's Handball Team Holds Friendly Match in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 4th—The German Democratic Republic National People's Army Men's Handball Team, which includes members of the world champion team, held its first friendly match in China this afternoon in Beijing against the Chinese People's Liberation Army Beijing Troops Men's Handball Team, winning with a score of 17-10.

Among those who watched the match today were General Fu Zhong, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Huang Zhong, Deputy Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission. The Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Mr. Wang Daëll, and his wife, as well as Deputy Military Attaché Captain Heim, also watched the match.

### 德国男子手球队在京举行友谊赛

1960-10-06
第5版()
专栏：

    德国男子手球队在京举行友谊赛
    据新华社4日讯　拥有世界冠军队队员的德意志民主共和国国家人民军男子手球队，今天下午在北京同中国人民解放军北京部队男子手球队举行访华首次友谊比赛，以十七比十获胜。
    今天观看比赛的，有中国人民解放军总政治部副主任傅钟上将、国家体育运动委员会副主任黄中等。德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔和夫人、副武官海姆大尉等也观看了比赛。
