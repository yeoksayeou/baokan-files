Title: Romanian National Bank Governor Majorianu Departs Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 10th. Romanian National Bank Governor Majorianu and Deputy Director Stroe, who visited our country at the invitation of Cao Juru, President of the People's Bank of China, left Beijing by plane yesterday morning to return home.
    Cao Juru, President of the People's Bank of China, and Vice Presidents Wu Bo and Qiao Peixin were at the airport to see them off. Romanian People's Republic Ambassador to China, Zaharescu, was also at the airport to see them off.

### 罗国家银行行长马约里亚努离京回国

1960-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    罗国家银行行长马约里亚努离京回国
    新华社10日讯　应中国人民银行行长曹菊如邀请来我国访问的罗马尼亚国家银行行长马约里亚努、副局长斯特罗埃，昨天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有中国人民银行行长曹菊如，副行长吴波、乔培新等。罗马尼亚人民共和国驻中国大使查哈勒斯库也到机场送行。
