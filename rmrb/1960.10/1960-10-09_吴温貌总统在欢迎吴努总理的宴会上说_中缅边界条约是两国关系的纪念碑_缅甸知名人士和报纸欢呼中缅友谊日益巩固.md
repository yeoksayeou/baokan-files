Title: President U Win Maung Says at Banquet Welcoming Premier U Nu: Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty Is a Monument to Relations Between the Two Countries; Burmese Prominent Figures and Newspapers Cheer the Growing Consolidation of Sino-Burmese Friendship
Author:
Date: 1960-10-09
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 7th - Burmese Federal President U Win Maung held a banquet at the Presidential Palace on the evening of the 5th to welcome Premier U Nu and the members of the Burmese government delegation led by him, who returned after signing the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty in Beijing.

Among those present at the banquet were Chief Justice of the Supreme Court U Myint Thein, Speaker of National Races Chamber Zaw Khun Kee, Speaker of the Chamber of Deputies Mann Ba Swe, judges of the High Court, cabinet ministers, and senior military officers.

President U Win Maung spoke at the banquet, warmly welcoming Premier U Nu and his wife, as well as members of the government delegation, who returned after successfully signing the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty.

U Win Maung said, "The century-old, unresolved Sino-Burmese border issue has been resolved through free and peaceful consultation, and the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty will become a new page in the history of Sino-Burmese relations and a monument left to future generations."

President U Win Maung thanked and praised Premier U Nu, General Ne Win, Brigadier Aung Gyi, and others who made efforts for the successful conclusion of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty.

Premier U Nu, in his reply, warmly thanked President U Win Maung and his wife for hosting the banquet and thanked the President for his praise of the conclusion of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty. Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Lei Yang of the Chinese Embassy in Burma and diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Burma attended the banquet. Also present at the banquet were representatives from the Burmese Federal Party, the Burmese National United Front, the Arakan National United Organisation, and other political parties.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 7th - U Ba Swe, Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma, and other prominent figures here have made statements welcoming the signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty.

U Ba Swe told reporters on October 6th that the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League fully supports the resolution of the Sino-Burmese boundary issue, and cannot but be pleased that the neighborly relations between China and Burma have been placed on a solid foundation of harmony.

Thakin Kodaw Hmaing, Chairman of the Burmese Peace Committee, said, "We are very pleased with the signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty." He said that the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty is a treaty of peace, friendship, equality, and justice.

Member of Parliament and leader of the Mon People's Front, Mon Bo Cho, said, "Peaceful consultation is the best way to solve international problems. Burma and China are very friendly countries. The Burmese people call the Chinese people ‘Pauk Phaw’ (brothers). This is much closer than any other kinship relationship." He said he was very happy that the Sino-Burmese boundary issue had been resolved by the two Prime Ministers through peaceful consultation.

Professor U Aung Latt of Rangoon University said that the signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty is beneficial to both countries. U Aung Latt also said that this treaty has enhanced the friendly relations between the two countries and at the same time set a brilliant example for the world.

U Tha Myat, former Director of the Cultural and Social Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, said, "The satisfactory resolution of the Sino-Burmese boundary issue left over from history is a very pleasant thing for the people of the world." He said, "Initially, some people thought that the Sino-Burmese boundary issue was an unsolvable problem. However, thanks to the sincerity and friendly spirit of the two countries, this problem has been completely solved." He said he firmly believes that any problem can be solved by adhering to sincerity and a friendly spirit.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 7th - Burmese newspapers today published editorials and articles, warmly supporting the signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty, and wishing the great edifice of Sino-Burmese friendship to be forever strong.

The "Just Newspaper" published an editorial saying, "The Sino-Burmese boundary issue has been completely resolved, and the two countries have concluded a treaty of non-aggression and strictly abide by the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, so the further development of Sino-Burmese friendship is certainly promising."

The newspaper believes that the two countries will continue to make efforts to further develop Sino-Burmese friendship.

The "Hanthawaddy" published an article saying, "It is precisely because Burma and China have shown a spirit of reconciliation and mutual concession that the long-standing boundary issue has been resolved. The treaty of friendship and non-aggression is being implemented. At the same time, the edifice of Sino-Burmese friendship has been established. This is something that both countries can be proud of." The article said that both sides will certainly make every effort to make this great edifice of Sino-Burmese friendship forever strong.

### 吴温貌总统在欢迎吴努总理的宴会上说  中缅边界条约是两国关系的纪念碑  缅甸知名人士和报纸欢呼中缅友谊日益巩固

1960-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    吴温貌总统在欢迎吴努总理的宴会上说
    中缅边界条约是两国关系的纪念碑
    缅甸知名人士和报纸欢呼中缅友谊日益巩固
    新华社仰光7日电　缅甸联邦总统吴温貌5日晚上在总统府举行宴会，欢迎在北京签订了中缅边界条约后回国的吴努总理和由他率领的缅甸政府代表团团员们。
    出席宴会的有最高法院首席法官吴敏登、民族院议长藻昆基，代表院议长曼巴赛，高等法院法官、内阁部长和高级军官等。
    吴温貌总统在宴会上讲话，热烈欢迎成功地签订了中缅边界条约后回国的吴努总理和他的夫人以及政府代表团的团员们。
    吴温貌说，“一世纪之久的、悬而未决的中缅边界问题已经通过自由与和平的协商获得了解决，中缅边界条约将成为中缅关系史上新的一页和留给后代子孙的纪念碑。”
    吴温貌总统感谢和赞扬吴努总理、奈温将军、昂季准将以及为成功地缔结中缅边界条约作出努力的其他人。
    吴努总理在答辞中热烈感谢吴温貌总统和夫人设宴招待，感谢总统对中缅边界条约的缔结所作的赞扬。中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬和各国驻缅甸外交使节出席了宴会。出席宴会的还有缅甸联邦党、缅甸民族团结阵线、亚拉干民族团结组织和其他政党的代表。
    新华社仰光7日电　缅甸反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴巴瑞和其他知名人士在这里发表谈话，欢迎中缅边界条约的签订。
    吴巴瑞10月6日对记者说，反法西斯人民自由同盟全力支持中缅边界问题的解决，对于中缅两国的邻居关系已被置于和睦的巩固的基础上，不能不感到高兴。
    缅甸保卫世界和平委员会主席德钦哥都迈说，“中缅边界条约的签订使我们感到非常高兴。”他说，中缅边界条约是一个和平、友谊、平等和公正的条约。
    议员、孟族人民阵线领袖孟波乔说，“和平磋商是解决国际问题的最好办法。缅甸和中国是非常友好的国家。缅甸人民称中国人民为
  ‘胞波’（兄弟）。这比其它任何亲戚的关系都要亲密得多。”他说，他很高兴中缅边界问题已经由两国总理通过和平协商求得解决。
    仰光大学教授吴昂拉说，中缅边界条约的签订对两国都有利。吴昂拉又说，这个条约增进了两国之间的友好关系，同时为全世界作出了一个光辉的榜样。
    前文化部文化社会局长吴达密说，“历史上遗留下来的中缅边界问题得到满意的解决，对全世界人民来说是一件非常愉快的事。”他说，“起初，某些人认为，中缅边界问题是一个不能解决的问题。但是，由于两国的诚意和友好精神，这个问题已经完全解决了。”他说，他深信任何问题都能够通过坚持诚恳和友好的精神而求得解决。
    新华社仰光7日电　缅甸报纸今天发表社论和文章，热烈支持中缅边界条约的签订，并祝伟大的中缅友谊大厦永远坚固。
    “公正报”发表社论说，“中缅边界问题已经完全解决，两国已经缔结了互不侵犯条约，并严格遵守和平共处五项原则，因此中缅友谊的进一步发展是肯定有希望的。”
    报纸相信两国将继续作出努力，使中缅友谊获得进一步发展。
    “罕礁越利报”发表文章说，“正是因为缅甸和中国表现出和解和互让的精神，因此长期存在的边界问题才获得了解决。友好和互不侵犯条约正在执行。同时，中缅友谊大厦已经建立。这是两国可以感到骄傲的事情。”文章说，双方一定将以全力来使这座伟大的中缅友谊大厦永远坚固。
