Title: Mangando, Vice-Chairman of the Congo-China Association, Returns Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-10-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 16th: Antoine Ligobé Mangando, Vice-Chairman of the Congo-China Association, who visited our country at the invitation of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, left Beijing by plane today to return home. Those who went to the airport to see him off included Qu Wu, Vice-President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, and Wu Qing, Secretary-General.

During his stay in China, Mangando visited Beijing and Shanghai and toured China's industrial, agricultural, cultural and educational construction. Vice-Premier Xi Zhongxun met with him, and also met with Liu Changsheng, President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, Zhang Zhixiang, Vice-President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and other people from various circles.

The Chinese-African People's Friendship Association hosted a farewell banquet for him at noon on the 15th.

### 刚果中国协会副主席芒丹戈回国

1960-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

    刚果中国协会副主席芒丹戈回国
    新华社16日讯　应中国非洲人民友好协会邀请来我国访问的刚果——中国协会副主席安托万·利戈贝·芒丹戈，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场欢送的有中非友协副会长屈武、总干事吴青等。
    芒丹戈在我国期间，访问了北京和上海，参观了我国的工农业及文教建设。习仲勋副总理接见了他，并会见了中非友协会长刘长胜和对外文协副会长张致祥等各方面人士。
    中非友协曾在15日中午设宴为他送行。
