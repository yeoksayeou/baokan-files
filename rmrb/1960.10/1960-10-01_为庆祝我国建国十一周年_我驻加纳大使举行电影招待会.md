Title: Chinese Ambassador to Ghana Holds Film Reception to Celebrate the 11th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1960-10-01
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Accra, 29th -- To celebrate the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Chinese Ambassador to Ghana, Huang Hua, and his wife held a film reception in Accra, the capital of Ghana, on the evening of September 28.
    At the reception, two Chinese films, "Celebrating Ten Years" and "The Magic Brush," were screened for over 400 viewers.
    Among those attending the film reception were Ghanaian Minister of Defense Dickson, Speaker of the National Assembly Asiedu, members of parliament, government officials, and prominent Ghanaian figures.
    Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Ghana also attended the film reception.
    The Chinese films were welcomed by the audience with repeated and enthusiastic applause.

### 为庆祝我国建国十一周年  我驻加纳大使举行电影招待会

1960-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    为庆祝我国建国十一周年
    我驻加纳大使举行电影招待会
    新华社阿克拉29日电　中国驻加纳大使黄华和夫人为了庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年，9月28日晚在加纳首都阿克拉举行电影招待会。
    会上为四百多名观众放映了两部中国影片“欢庆十年”和“神笔”。
    出席电影招待会的有加纳国防部长迪克森、国民议会议长阿塞杜和议员、政府官员以及加纳著名人士等。
    出席电影招待会的还有各国驻加纳外交使节。
    中国电影受到观众一再热烈的鼓掌欢迎。
