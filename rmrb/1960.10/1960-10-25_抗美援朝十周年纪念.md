Title: Tenth Anniversary of the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea
Author: Huang Yanpei
Date: 1960-10-25
Page: 4

    Tenth Anniversary of the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea
    Huang Yanpei
    Ten years now since resisting America, aiding Korea.
    Comparing strength, the imperialists are shamed.
    Clearly, brothers share weal and woe.
    The Yalu River flows, flesh and blood connected.
    Heartbreak Ridge, the Lord is heartbroken.
    Helplessly, the agreement is made.
    Who will not withdraw troops, who withdraws alone?
    Ask who is warmongering, let public opinion judge.

### 抗美援朝十周年纪念
黄炎培
1960-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    抗美援朝十周年纪念
    黄炎培
    抗美援朝今十年。
    较量强弱帝慙颜。
    分明兄弟安危共。
    鸭绿江流血肉连。
    伤心岭上帝伤心。
    无可如何协议成。
    谁不撤兵谁独撤。
    问谁好战付公评。
