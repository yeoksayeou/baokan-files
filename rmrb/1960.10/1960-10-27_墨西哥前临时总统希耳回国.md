Title: Former Interim President of Mexico, Gil, Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 25th. The Mexican cultural delegation of five people, led by Emilio Portes Gil, the former interim President of Mexico, left Guangzhou today for home via Shenzhen after a two-day visit to Guangzhou.

The guests visited the site of the Peasant Movement Training Institute, which was hosted by Chairman Mao in his early years, the China Export Commodities Fair, and the Guangzhou Commune Martyrs Cemetery. Before leaving Guangzhou, Li Guangxiang, Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou, hosted a banquet in their honor.

### 墨西哥前临时总统希耳回国

1960-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

    墨西哥前临时总统希耳回国
    新华社广州25日电　由墨西哥前临时总统埃米略·波尔特斯·希耳率领的墨西哥文化代表团一行五人，在广州访问两天后，今天离开广州经深圳回国。
    客人们在广州参观了毛主席当年主办的农民运动讲习所的旧址、中国出口商品陈列馆和广州公社烈士陵园。在离开广州前，广州市副市长李广祥曾设宴欢送。
