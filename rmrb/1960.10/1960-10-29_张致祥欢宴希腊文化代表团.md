Title: Zhang Zhixiang Hosts Banquet for Greek Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. Zhang Zhixiang, Vice-President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet at noon today to welcome the Greek cultural delegation headed by Panayotopoulos.

	Those present included Wen Pengjiu, Wu Xue, Guo Xiaochuan, Hu Zheng, and others.

### 张致祥欢宴希腊文化代表团

1960-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    张致祥欢宴希腊文化代表团
    新华社28日讯　中国人民对外文化协会副会长张致祥，今天中午设宴欢迎以帕拿约托普路斯为首的希腊文化代表团。
    出席作陪的有温朋久、吴雪、郭小川、胡铮等。
