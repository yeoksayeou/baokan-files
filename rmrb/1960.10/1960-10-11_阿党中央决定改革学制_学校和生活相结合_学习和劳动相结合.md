Title: Albanian Party Central Committee Decides to Reform the School System: Combining School and Life, Combining Study and Labor
Author:
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, October 9th. The Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party announced on the 9th a decision on "Reorganizing Schools and Further Developing the Education System."

The decision stipulates that Albania will take all necessary measures to reorganize schools on the basis of combining school and life, and combining study and productive labor. The decision stipulates that the existing seven-year schools will be changed to eight-year schools, which is the first stage of education, while the second stage will be decided later to be three or four years. The work of reorganizing ordinary schools will be carried out partly in the 1963-1964 school year and partly in 1964-1965.

### 阿党中央决定改革学制  学校和生活相结合  学习和劳动相结合

1960-10-11
第5版()
专栏：

    阿党中央决定改革学制
    学校和生活相结合
    学习和劳动相结合
    新华社地拉那9日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会9日公布了关于“改组学校和进一步发展教育系统”的决定。
    决定规定，阿尔巴尼亚将在学校和生活相结合、学习和劳动生产相结合的基础上采取一切必要措施来改组学校。决定规定，将现有的七年制学校改为八年制，这是教育的第一阶段，而第二阶段将在以后决定为三年或者四年。改组普通学校的工作，一部分将在1963年到1964年的学年中进行，一部分将在1964年到1965年进行。
