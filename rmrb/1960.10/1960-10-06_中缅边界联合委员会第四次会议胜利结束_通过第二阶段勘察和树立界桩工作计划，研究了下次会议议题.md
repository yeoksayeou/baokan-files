Title: The Fourth Meeting of the China-Burma Joint Boundary Committee Successfully Concludes; Plan for the Second Phase of Surveying and Erecting Boundary Markers Adopted, Agenda for the Next Meeting Studied
Author:
Date: 1960-10-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 5th - The fourth meeting of the China-Burma Joint Boundary Committee held its third session on the morning of October 5th.

    The meeting discussed and adopted the plan for the second phase of surveying and erecting boundary markers, studied the agenda for the next meeting of the Joint Committee, and adopted the various documents and communique of this meeting.

    With this, the fourth meeting of the Joint Committee declared its successful conclusion.

### 中缅边界联合委员会第四次会议胜利结束  通过第二阶段勘察和树立界桩工作计划，研究了下次会议议题

1960-10-06
第3版()
专栏：

    中缅边界联合委员会第四次会议胜利结束
    通过第二阶段勘察和树立界桩工作计划，研究了下次会议议题
    新华社5日讯　中缅边界联合委员会第四次会议于10月5日上午举行了第三次会谈。
    会议讨论并通过了第二阶段勘察和树立界桩工作的计划，研究了联合委员会下次会议的议题，并通过了本次会议的各项文件和公报。
    至此，联合委员会第四次会议宣告胜利结束。
