Title: Zhu Muzhi Hosts Banquet for Cuban Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 10th. Zhu Muzhi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, held a banquet this evening to welcome the Cuban Journalists Delegation. The delegation leader, Antonio Ma. Maicas Dominguez, Secretary General of the Cuban Journalists Association and Dean of the Guanabacoa University Preparatory College, along with delegation members José Manuel Valdés Rodríguez, Head of the Film Department of the University of Havana, and José Tapio Palma, a photographer for Cuba’s "Revolución" Television and "INRA" magazine, were invited to attend the banquet.

Zhou Erfu, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Latin American Countries, Wang Yi, First Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Journalists Association and Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily, Shao Zonghan, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and figures from the capital's press circles were present at the banquet.

### 朱穆之欢宴古巴新闻工作者代表团

1960-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    朱穆之欢宴古巴新闻工作者代表团
    新华社10日讯  中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席朱穆之今晚举行宴会，欢迎古巴新闻工作者代表团。代表团团长、古巴新闻工作者协会总书记、瓜那巴科西大学预备学院院长安东尼奥·马·迈卡斯·多明格斯，及团员、哈瓦那大学电影系主任何塞·曼奴埃尔·瓦尔德斯·罗德里格斯和古巴“革命”电视台、“因拉”杂志摄影记者何塞·塔皮奥·帕耳马应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会作陪的有中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复，中华全国新闻工作者协会书记处第一书记、人民日报副总编辑王揖，外交部新闻司副司长邵宗汉以及首都新闻界人士。
