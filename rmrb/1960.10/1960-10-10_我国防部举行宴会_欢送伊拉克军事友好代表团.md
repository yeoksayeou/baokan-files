Title: Chinese Ministry of National Defense Holds Banquet to See Off Iraqi Military Friendship Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-10
Page: 4

    Chinese Ministry of National Defense Holds Banquet
    To See Off Iraqi Military Friendship Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China held a banquet tonight to bid farewell to the Iraqi Military Friendship Delegation headed by Brigadier General Mahmoud Abdul Razzaq.
    General Luo Ruiqing, Vice Minister of National Defense, on behalf of the Ministry of National Defense and all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, expressed heartfelt thanks to the Iraqi Military Friendship Delegation, which brought the precious friendship of the Iraqi people and army, for its friendly visit to our country.
    General Luo Ruiqing said that the people and armies of China and Iraq have established a deep friendship in the struggle against imperialism. We believe that in the future, in the construction of our respective countries, in the cause of safeguarding national independence and defending peace, on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and the Bandung Spirit, the friendship between the people and armies of China and Iraq will continue to develop and consolidate.
    General Luo Ruiqing said that the Iraqi Military Friendship Delegation's visit to our country has made a beneficial contribution to promoting mutual understanding and friendly relations between the people and armies of China and Iraq. He asked the delegation to bring back to the Iraqi people the Chinese people's and the Chinese People's Liberation Army's lofty respect and deep friendship for the Iraqi people, and to convey the Chinese People's Liberation Army's cordial greetings to the Iraqi army.
    Brigadier General Razzaq then spoke at the banquet, expressing his heartfelt thanks for the warm and friendly reception from the Chinese people and army.
    Brigadier General Razzaq said that during our visit we discovered that the great people of this country are filled with lofty hopes for the future, and the army of this country is simple, well-trained and disciplined. Therefore, the people and army of this country are great.
    Brigadier General Razzaq said that both of our countries are opposed to aggression and love peace. He said that we have long opposed imperialism, and in the future we will continue to carry out the struggle against imperialism. He hoped that the friendly exchanges between the people and armies of Iraq and China will be further enhanced in the future.
    Fadil, Ambassador of the Republic of Iraq to our country, and military attaches of various countries stationed in China were also invited to attend the banquet.
    Also present at the banquet were senior generals such as General Xiao Hua, General Zhang Aiping, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Vice Admiral Zhao Qimin, Lieutenant General Chang Qiankun, Lieutenant General Zhang Xianyue, and Lieutenant General Liu Jinxuan, as well as representatives of officers from the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Beijing.
    Also present at the banquet were He Ying, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Luo Jun, Vice President of the China-Iraq Friendship Association.
    At the banquet filled with a friendly atmosphere, people toasted to the continuous development and consolidation of the friendship between the people and armies of China and Iraq. (Attached picture)

### 我国防部举行宴会  欢送伊拉克军事友好代表团

1960-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    我国防部举行宴会
    欢送伊拉克军事友好代表团
    新华社9日讯　中华人民共和国国防部今晚举行宴会，欢送以马哈茂德·阿卜杜勒·拉扎克准将为首的伊拉克军事友好代表团。
    国防部副部长罗瑞卿大将代表国防部和中国人民解放军全体指战员，对带来伊拉克人民和军队珍贵友谊的伊拉克军事友好代表团，在我国进行友好访问表示衷心的感谢。
    罗瑞卿大将说，中、伊两国人民和军队，在反对帝国主义的斗争中，建立了深厚的友谊。我们相信，今后，在建设我们各自的国家，在维护民族独立和保卫和平的事业中，在和平共处五项原则和万隆精神的基础上，中、伊两国人民和军队之间的友谊，将会继续得到发展和巩固。
    罗瑞卿大将说，伊拉克军事友好代表团这次来我国访问，对于促进中、伊两国人民和军队之间的互相了解和友好关系，作出了有益的贡献。他请代表团把中国人民和中国人民解放军对伊拉克人民的崇高的敬意和深厚的友谊，带回给伊拉克人民，并请转达中国人民解放军对伊拉克军队的亲切问候。
    拉扎克准将接着在宴会上讲话，他对中国人民和军队热情友好的接待，表示衷心的感谢。
    拉扎克准将说，我们在访问期间发现这个国家伟大的人民对未来充满着崇高的希望，这个国家的军队是朴素的、训练有素和有纪律的。因此，这个国家的人民和军队都是伟大的。
    拉扎克准将说，我们两国都是反对侵略，热爱和平。他说，我们曾经长期的反对帝国主义，今后我们还要继续进行反对帝国主义的斗争。他希望今后进一步增进伊中两国人民和军队之间的友好交往。
    伊拉克共和国驻我国大使法迪尔和各国驻华武官也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的，还有萧华上将、张爱萍上将、萧向荣中将、赵启民海军中将、常乾坤空军中将、张贤约中将、刘金轩中将等高级将领以及中国人民解放军驻京部队军官代表。
    出席宴会的，还有外交部西亚非洲司司长何英，中国伊拉克友好协会副会长罗俊。
    在充满着友好气氛的宴会上，人们为中、伊两国人民和军队的友谊不断发展和巩固干杯。（附图片）
