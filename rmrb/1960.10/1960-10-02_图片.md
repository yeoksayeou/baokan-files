Title: Du Haizhen
Author: Du Haizhen
Date: 1960-10-02
Page: 8

    A team composed of hundreds of mountaineers from the Beijing Institute of Geology, surrounded by a huge painting of "Climbing the World's Highest Peak," marched with firm steps through Tiananmen Square. This symbolizes the glorious victory achieved by our mountaineers under the leadership of the Party, and also symbolizes the fearless spirit of our hundreds of millions of people who fear no height and are unbreakable. Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Haizhen photographed.
