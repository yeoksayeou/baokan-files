Title: Burmese Sports Delegation Competes and Performs in Shanghai

Author:

Date: 1960-10-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 17th: The Burmese sports delegation's football team, boxing team, and cane ball team held friendly matches and performances in Shanghai on the 16th, receiving a warm welcome from more than 20,000 spectators.

The Burmese sports delegation's football team held a friendly match with the Shanghai football team at the Hongkou Stadium, with the home team winning 3-2. The home team led 2-1 in the first half. During the match, both sides attacked actively and played brilliantly. Not long after the start of the match, the guest team's forward, Star, scored a goal with a sudden attack, and the audience responded with warm applause.

In the evening, the Burmese sports delegation's boxing team and cane ball team gave performances. The spacious Shanghai Gymnasium was packed. The audience repeatedly applauded and praised the Burmese national-style boxing and the cane ball performance, which was skillful in movement and graceful in posture.

Before the match, Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng, Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Song Jiwen, Vice Mayor of Shanghai, met with Lieutenant Colonel Maw Win, head of the Burmese sports delegation, and other leaders. After the match, they also took photos with the members of each team. U Ba Din, the leader of the cane ball team, also presented cane balls to the leaders of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

The Burmese sports delegation arrived in Shanghai on the 15th. That evening, Senior Colonel Fang Zhongduo, Deputy Political Commissar of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Zhang Zhenya, Deputy Director of the Shanghai Municipal Sports Commission, hosted a banquet to welcome Lieutenant Colonel Maw Win, head of the Burmese sports delegation, and all members of the delegation.

The delegation will also visit and tour Shanghai.

### 缅甸体育代表团在上海比赛和表演

1960-10-18
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸体育代表团在上海比赛和表演
    新华社上海17日电　缅甸体育代表团的足球队、拳击队、藤球队16日在上海分别举行友谊比赛和表演，受到两万多观众的热烈欢迎。
    缅甸体育代表团足球队在虹口体育场与上海市足球队举行友谊比赛，结果主队以三比二获胜。上半场主队以二比一领先。在比赛中，双方都积极进攻，踢得精采。比赛开始不久，客队前锋斯达尔就以突击方式攻进一球，观众报以热烈的掌声。
    晚上，缅甸体育代表团拳击队和藤球队举行了表演。宽广的上海市体育馆座无虚席。观众对缅甸民族形式的拳击和动作熟练、姿态优美的藤球表演，一再鼓掌赞扬。
    比赛前，中国人民解放军上海警备区司令员王必成中将和上海市副市长宋季文，接见了缅甸体育代表团团长貌温中校等领导人。比赛结束后，还分别与各队队员合影。藤球队的领队吴巴丁还向中国人民解放军上海警备区首长赠送了藤球。
    缅甸体育代表团是15日到达上海的。当晚，中国人民解放军上海警备区副政治委员方中铎大校、上海市体育运动委员会副主任张振亚设便宴欢迎缅甸体育代表团团长貌温中校和代表团全体人员。
    代表团还将在上海进行参观、访问。
