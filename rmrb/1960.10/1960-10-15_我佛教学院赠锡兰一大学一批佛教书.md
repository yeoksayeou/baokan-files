Title: Our Buddhist Academy Donates a Batch of Buddhist Books to a University in Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1960-10-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 11th - On the occasion of the 1500th anniversary of Faxian's journey to Ceylon to obtain Buddhist scriptures, Zhang Canming, the Chinese Ambassador to Ceylon, presented a collection of Buddhist scriptures and books to the Vidyalankara University in Ceylon on behalf of the Chinese Buddhist Academy.

### 我佛教学院赠锡兰一大学一批佛教书

1960-10-15
第6版()
专栏：

    我佛教学院赠锡兰一大学一批佛教书
    据新华社科伦坡11日电　在法显到锡兰取经一千五百周年纪念之际，中国驻锡兰大使张灿明代表中国佛教学院向锡兰维迪阿兰卡拉大学赠送一批佛经和佛教书籍。
