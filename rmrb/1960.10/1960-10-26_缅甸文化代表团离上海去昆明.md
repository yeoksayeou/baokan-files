Title: Burmese Cultural Delegation Leaves Shanghai for Kunming
Author:
Date: 1960-10-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 25th: The Burmese Cultural Delegation concluded its visit to Shanghai and departed by special train for Kunming tonight.

The Burmese Cultural Delegation arrived in Shanghai by train from Beijing on the 11th. On the 12th, the delegation held a grand performance at the Shanghai People's Grand Stage. The artists performed fifteen Burmese and Chinese song and dance programs, including "Song of Sino-Burmese Friendship," "Song of the Sino-Burmese Border Treaty," "The Story of the Irrawaddy River," "Karen Folk Dance," and "The Sun Rises Forever on the Prairie," receiving a warm welcome from more than 2,000 spectators. Liu Shuzhou, Vice Mayor of Shanghai, Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng, Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zhang Junxiang, Director of the Shanghai Film Bureau and Council Member of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association in Shanghai, and Zhang Chunqiao President of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, watched the Burmese artists' wonderful performances.

On the evening of the 11th, Vice Mayor of Shanghai Liu Shuzhou held a welcome reception for the delegation. Liu Shuzhou and Colonel So Min, the head of the delegation, spoke at the reception, both expressing their desire to continuously develop the deep friendship between the people of China and Burma.

During their visit and performances in Shanghai, the Burmese artists also visited the Shanghai Conservatory of Music, the Opera School, the National Orchestra, the Shanghai Workers' Cultural Palace, the Children's Palace, and other places. At the Shanghai Haiyan Film Studio, Burmese and Chinese artists worked closely together to film some of the music and dance programs from their performance tour in China into a color documentary.

### 缅甸文化代表团离上海去昆明

1960-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸文化代表团离上海去昆明
    新华社上海25日电　缅甸文化代表团结束了在上海的访问，今晚乘专车去昆明。
    缅甸文化代表团是在11日从北京乘火车来到上海的。12日，代表团在上海人民大舞台举行了盛大演出。艺术家们演出的“中缅友谊之歌”、“中缅边界条约之歌”，“依洛瓦底江的故事”、“吉仁族民间舞”、“草原上升起不落的太阳”等十五个缅甸和中国的歌舞节目，受到全场二千多名观众热烈欢迎。上海市副市长刘述周，中国人民解放军上海警备区司令员王必成中将，上海市电影局局长、中缅友协在沪理事张骏祥，中国人民对外文化协会上海市分会会长张春桥等观看了缅甸艺术家们的精彩演出。
    11日晚上，上海市副市长刘述周为代表团举行了欢迎酒会。刘述周和代表团团长苏敏上校在酒会上讲了话，他们都表示要不断发展中缅两国人民的深厚友谊。
    缅甸艺术家在上海访问演出期间，还分别到上海音乐学院、戏曲学校、民族乐团、上海市工人文化宫、少年宫等地进行了参观访问。在上海海燕电影制片厂，缅甸艺术家和中国艺术家亲密合作，把他们访华演出节目中的部分音乐、舞蹈节目拍摄成彩色纪录片。
