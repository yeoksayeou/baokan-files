Title: Japanese Food Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 6th October. A three-member Japanese food workers delegation headed by Shigeru Someya, chairman of the All-Japan Food Workers Union Council, arrived in Beijing this afternoon. They are visiting China for a short period at the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Light Industry Union Council after attending an international food workers union conference.
    Du Yanqing, chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Light Industry Union Council, and others were at the airport to welcome them.

### 日本食品工人代表团到达北京

1960-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    日本食品工人代表团到达北京
    新华社6日讯　以全日本食品工会协议会议长染谷茂为首的日本食品工人代表团一行三人，今天下午到达北京。他们是在参加食品工会国际会议后应中国轻工业工会全国委员会的邀请来中国作短期访问的。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国轻工业工会全国委员会主席杜延庆等人。
