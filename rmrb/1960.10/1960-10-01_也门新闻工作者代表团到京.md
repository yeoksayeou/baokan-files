Title: Yemenese Journalists Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - The Yemenese journalists delegation, invited by the All-China Journalists Association to visit China and attend the National Day celebrations, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation was led by Saeed Muhammad Hussein Musa, editor-in-chief of the Yemenese "Victory Newspaper".

### 也门新闻工作者代表团到京

1960-10-01
第5版()
专栏：

    也门新闻工作者代表团到京
    新华社30日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问并参加国庆观礼的也门新闻工作者代表团一行三人，在团长、也门“胜利报”主编赛义德·穆罕默德·侯赛因·穆萨率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
