Title: Many Industrial Departments in Bulgaria Fulfill First Nine Months Plan Ahead of Schedule; Some Factories Pay Attention to Saving and Provide Surplus Products to the Country

Author:

Date: 1960-10-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, October 17th: Good news keeps coming from the Bulgarian industrial front that many departments have fulfilled their production plans for the first nine months of this year ahead of schedule.

All the workers of the power plants under the Electricity Bureau of the Bulgarian Industrial Committee completed the production plan for the first nine months of this year five days ahead of schedule, producing a total of 3,163,900,000 kilowatt-hours of electricity. By the end of September, they had produced 72,200,000 kilowatt-hours more electricity for the country.

In the first nine months of this year, coal miners across the country mined a total of 12,567,000 tons of coal, exceeding the original plan by 18,000 tons, and 1,337,000 tons more than the same period last year. The employees of the machine manufacturing department are not lagging behind either. The employees of the largest agricultural machinery factory in Bulgaria, the "Dimitrov Agricultural Machinery Factory," completed the production plan for the first nine months of this year on September 23rd, ahead of schedule, producing more than 16,000 various agricultural machines for the country. The employees of this factory pay great attention to saving in production. In the first eight months of this year, they saved the country the equivalent of 15 million levs by reducing product costs. From September 23rd to 30th, all the employees of the factory produced agricultural machines worth 11 million levs for the country.

### 保很多工业部门提前完成头九个月计划  一些工厂注意节约为国家提供超额产品

1960-10-18
第5版()
专栏：

    保很多工业部门提前完成头九个月计划
    一些工厂注意节约为国家提供超额产品
    新华社索非亚17日电　保加利亚工业战线上，不断传来提前完成今年头九个月生产计划的喜讯。
    保加利亚工业委员会电力局所属的发电站的全体职工，提前五天完成了今年头九个月的生产计划，共生产了三十一亿六千三百九十万度电。到9月底为止，他们为国家多生产了七千二百万度电。
    在今年头九个月里，全国采煤工人共开采了一千二百五十六万七千吨煤，比原计划多开采了一万八千吨，和去年同期比较，多开采了一百三十三万七千吨煤。机器制造业部门的职工也不落后。保加利亚全国最大的农业机器制造厂——“季米特洛夫农业机器制造厂”的职工们，在9月23日就提前完成了今年头九个月的生产计划。为国家生产了一万六千多台各种农业机器。这家工厂的职工们在生产中很注意节约。今年头八个月内，由于降低了产品成本，他们为国家节约了相当于一千五百万列弗的财富。全厂职工从9月23日到30日，又为国家生产了价值一千一百万列弗的农业机器。
