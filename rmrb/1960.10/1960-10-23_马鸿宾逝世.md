Title: Ma Hongbin Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1960-10-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, October 22nd: Ma Hongbin, representative of the National People's Congress, member of the National Defense Council, and Deputy Governor of Gansu Province, passed away in Lanzhou at 4:00 PM on the 21st due to stomach cancer and other illnesses, despite extensive medical treatment. He was 77 years old. Zhang Zhongliang, First Secretary of the Gansu Provincial Committee of the CPC, Governor Deng Baoshan, and other party and government leaders went to pay their respects yesterday upon hearing the news.

### 马鸿宾逝世

1960-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    马鸿宾逝世
    新华社兰州22日电　全国人民代表大会代表、国防委员会委员、甘肃省副省长马鸿宾，因患胃癌等症，经医生多方诊治无效，21日下午四时在兰州逝世，享年七十七岁。中共甘肃省委第一书记张仲良、省长邓宝珊等党政负责人昨日闻讯后前往吊唁。
