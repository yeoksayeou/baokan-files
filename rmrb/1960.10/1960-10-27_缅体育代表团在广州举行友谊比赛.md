Title: Burmese Sports Delegation Holds Friendly Matches in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-10-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 26th - The Burmese sports delegation's football team played a friendly match with the Guangzhou football team at the Yuexiushan Stadium in Guangzhou on the afternoon of the 25th, losing to the home team by a score of two to three. Approximately 40,000 spectators watched the performance.

Before the match, Lieutenant General Wen Niansheng, the head of the Guangzhou garrison, and Li Guangxiang, the vice mayor of Guangzhou, received the leaders of the Burmese sports delegation.

During the intermission of the football match, the Burmese sports delegation's sepak takraw team gave a sepak takraw performance. In the evening, the Burmese sports delegation's sepak takraw team and national boxing team gave a performance at the Guangzhou Gymnasium. The guest team's wonderful performances were met with warm applause from the audience. After the performance, Major General Lai Chunfeng, Major General Xiao Yuanli, and others, received all the athletes of the sepak takraw team and boxing team.

### 缅体育代表团在广州举行友谊比赛

1960-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

    缅体育代表团在广州举行友谊比赛
    据新华社广州26日电　缅甸体育代表团足球队，25日下午，在广州市越秀山体育场与广州足球队举行友谊比赛，结果以二比三败给主队。约四万观众观看了这场表演。
    比赛之前，广州驻军首长文年生中将和广州市副市长李广祥，接见了缅甸体育代表团的领导人。
    足球赛中间休息时，缅甸体育代表团藤球队作了藤球表演。晚上，缅甸体育代表团的藤球队和民族拳击队又在广州体育馆作了表演。客队的精彩表演，博得观众热烈的掌声。表演以后，赖春风少将、萧元礼少将等，接见了藤球队和拳击队全体运动员。
