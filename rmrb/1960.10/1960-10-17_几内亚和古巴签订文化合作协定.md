Title: Guinea and Cuba Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-10-17
Page: 6
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 15th. Guinean President Sékou Touré, who is currently visiting Cuba, and Cuban President Dorticós signed a cultural cooperation agreement between the two countries here today.
    The agreement stipulates that both parties will promote mutual cooperation in education, science, and cultural activities, as well as in publishing and news.

### 几内亚和古巴签订文化合作协定

1960-10-17
第6版()
专栏：

    几内亚和古巴签订文化合作协定
    新华社哈瓦那15日电　正在古巴访问的几内亚总统塞古·杜尔和古巴总统多尔蒂科斯今天在这里签订了两国之间的文化合作协定。
    协定规定，双方将促进教育、科学和文化活动以及出版和新闻方面的相互合作。
