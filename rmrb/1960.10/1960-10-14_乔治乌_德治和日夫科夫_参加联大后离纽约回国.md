Title: Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej and Zhivkov Leave New York for Home After Attending the UN General Assembly
Author:
Date: 1960-10-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 13th: According to TASS, New York: Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, head of the Romanian delegation, and Zhivkov, head of the Bulgarian delegation, who attended the 15th session of the United Nations General Assembly, left New York by ship on the 12th to return home.

### 乔治乌—德治和日夫科夫  参加联大后离纽约回国

1960-10-14
第6版()
专栏：

    乔治乌—德治和日夫科夫
    参加联大后离纽约回国
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社纽约讯：出席联合国大会第十五届会议的罗马尼亚代表团团长乔治乌—德治和保加利亚代表团团长日夫科夫12日乘船离开纽约返国。
