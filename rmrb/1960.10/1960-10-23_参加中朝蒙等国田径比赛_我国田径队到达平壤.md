Title: Our Track and Field Team Arrives in Pyongyang to Participate in Track and Field Competitions of China, North Korea, Mongolia and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-10-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 21st: The Chinese track and field team, led by team leader Liu Dexiu, arrived in Pyongyang today by international train to participate in track and field competitions between China, North Korea, Mongolia, and other countries.

Those who welcomed the Chinese track and field team at the station included Kim Deuk-jun, Vice Chairman of the DPRK Cabinet's Sports Guidance Committee, and Li Lin, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea, and others. The Chinese track and field team has a total of fifteen people, including Master of Sports Chen Jiaquan, Liu Xingyu, and others. The track and field competition will be held in Pyongyang on the 23rd and 24th.

### 参加中朝蒙等国田径比赛  我国田径队到达平壤

1960-10-23
第6版()
专栏：

    参加中朝蒙等国田径比赛
    我国田径队到达平壤
    新华社平壤21日电　前来平壤参加中、朝、蒙等国田径比赛的中国田径队，由领队刘德修率领今天乘国际列车到达平壤。
    到车站欢迎中国田径队的有，朝鲜内阁体育指导委员会副委员长金得俊和中国驻朝鲜大使馆文化参赞李林等人。中国田径队共十五人，其中有运动健将陈家全、刘兴玉等。田径比赛将在23日和24日在平壤举行。
