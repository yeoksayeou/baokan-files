Title: National Weightlifting Championships Open
Author:
Date: 1960-10-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, October 13th: The opening ceremony of the 1960 National Weightlifting Championships was held in the Pearl Spring Auditorium in Jinan on the evening of the 13th.

　　Participating in this competition are representative teams from twenty-two units, including Shanghai, Shandong, Guangdong, Heilongjiang, Xinjiang, and other twenty-one provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions, as well as the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

　　Li Chengzhi, Deputy Governor of Shandong Province, delivered a speech at the opening ceremony. He congratulated the successful opening of the weightlifting championships and extended a warm welcome to the athletes and referees from all over the country.

　　This National Weightlifting Championships is scheduled to begin competition on the afternoon of the 15th at the "August 1st" Gymnasium in Jinan and will end on the 20th.

### 全国举重锦标赛开幕

1960-10-14
第4版()
专栏：

    全国举重锦标赛开幕
    据新华社济南13日电　1960年全国举重锦标赛于13日晚在济南珍珠泉礼堂举行了开幕式。
    参加这次比赛的，有上海、山东、广东、黑龙江、新疆等二十一个省、市、自治区和中国人民解放军共二十二个单位的代表队。
    山东省副省长李澄之在开幕式上致词，他祝贺这次举重锦标赛的胜利开幕，并向来自全国各地的运动员和裁判员表示热烈的欢迎。
    这次全国举重锦标赛预定15日下午在济南“八一”体育馆开始比赛，比赛将在20日结束。
