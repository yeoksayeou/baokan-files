Title: ITTF Vice President Ecke Eliason Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Ecke Eliason, Vice President of the International Table Tennis Federation and Chairman of the Swedish Table Tennis Association, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a friendly visit to our country. Chen Xian, Chairman of the Table Tennis Association of the People's Republic of China, and others greeted him at the airport.

### 国际乒乓球联合会副主席  埃克·埃尔德抵京

1960-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    国际乒乓球联合会副主席
    埃克·埃尔德抵京
    新华社9日讯　国际乒乓球联合会副主席、瑞典乒乓球协会主席埃克·埃尔德于今日乘飞机抵京，来我国进行友好访问。中华人民共和国乒乓球协会主席陈先等到机场迎接。
