Title: Tunisian President Bourguiba Meets with Chinese Delegation, Says Sino-Tunisian Relations Will Develop Further

Author:

Date: 1960-10-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, October 20th - Tunisian President Bourguiba today received Yu Liguang, head of the Chinese delegation to the Eighth Tunis International Fair, Deputy Head Yue Daiheng, and some members of the Chinese delegation.

During the meeting, President Bourguiba said that relations between China and Tunisia will develop further. He expressed his hope that the Chinese people, under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong, will achieve even greater accomplishments, and he asked Yu Liguang to convey his friendly greetings to Chairman Mao.

President Bourguiba stated that Tunisia is willing to learn from other countries' experiences to accelerate its own construction and improve the living standards of its people as quickly as possible.

Yu Liguang, head of the Chinese delegation, stated that China and Tunisia have already established good cultural and economic relations. He expressed his firm belief that the friendly relations between the two countries will further develop. Yu Liguang said he would convey President Bourguiba's good wishes to Chairman Mao and expressed gratitude to President Bourguiba for the Tunisian government and people's friendship towards the Chinese people.

President Bourguiba viewed the gifts presented by the Chinese delegation. He showed great interest in the large "Chinese Painting Album."

Present at the meeting were Tunisian Minister of Information Mohamed Masmoudi and Chief of Protocol of the Presidential Palace, Abdel Mezhiri.

### 突尼斯总统布尔吉巴接见我代表团时说  中突关系将更加向前发展

1960-10-22
第5版()
专栏：

    突尼斯总统布尔吉巴接见我代表团时说
    中突关系将更加向前发展
    据新华社突尼斯20日电　突尼斯共和国总统布尔吉巴今天接见了参加突尼斯第八届国际博览会中国代表团团长于黎光、副团长岳岱衡以及部分中国代表团的团员。
    在接见时，布尔吉巴总统说，中国和突尼斯的关系将更加向前发展。他希望中国人民在毛泽东主席领导下将获得更伟大的成就，他请于黎光向毛主席转达友好的问候。
    布尔吉巴总统说，突尼斯愿意向其他国家吸取经验来加速本国的建设，并用尽可能快的方法来提高人民的生活水平。
    中国代表团团长于黎光表示，中国和突尼斯在文化和经济方面的关系已经有了良好的发展。他深信两国之间的友好关系将进一步发展。于黎光表示将把布尔吉巴总统的良好祝愿转达给毛主席。对突尼斯政府和人民对中国人民的友好表示，向布尔吉巴总统表示感谢。
    布尔吉巴总统观看了中国代表团赠送的礼品。他对巨大的“中国画册”十分感到兴趣。
    接见时在座的有突尼斯情报部长穆罕默德·马斯穆迪和总统府礼宾司司长阿卜杜勒·梅希里。
