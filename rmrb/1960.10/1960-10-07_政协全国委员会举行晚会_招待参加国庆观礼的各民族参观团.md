Title: CPPCC National Committee Holds Reception to Entertain Visiting Ethnic Groups Attending National Day Observance

Author:
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held a reception at the CPPCC Auditorium this evening to entertain visiting ethnic groups from various regions who came to Beijing to attend the National Day celebrations.

Prior to the reception, CPPCC National Committee Vice-Chairmen Chen Shutong and Bao Erhan received the Yunnan Province Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation headed by Duan Ming, the Tibet Visiting Delegation headed by Zhang Pingfan, the Tibet Youth and Women Visiting Delegation headed by Gui Zhi, the Ningxia Visiting Delegation headed by Wang Futian, and the Xinjiang Visiting Delegation headed by Tuo Hu Ti A Bu Du La. During the meeting, Chen Shutong said, Vice-Chairman Bao Erhan and I, on behalf of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, extend our warm welcome and heartfelt congratulations to all of you and the comrades of the ethnic groups visiting delegations who are participating in this year's National Day celebrations. We wish you new and greater achievements in your respective posts!

Chen Shutong said that under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, and under the glorious guidance of the General Line, the Great Leap Forward, and the People's Commune, the people of all ethnic groups in our country are uniting even more closely, working together, and striving arduously. We will surely achieve even greater successes.

Present at the meeting were Zhang Zhiyi, Deputy Head of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and Standing Committee Member of the CPPCC National Committee; Xu Boxin and Xin Zhichao, Deputy Secretaries-General of the CPPCC National Committee; Xie Fumin, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress; Xie Hechou, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission; Xi Rao Jia Cuo, Lu Han, and Sa Kongliao, Standing Committee Members of the CPPCC National Committee; and Zai Tao and Lü Zhenyu, heads of the Ethnic Groups Team of the CPPCC National Committee.

At the reception, the Central Experimental Opera House performed "Five Red Clouds." This dance drama, which depicts the heroic struggle of the Li people of Wuzhi Mountain in Hainan Island for liberation, deeply moved every audience member, who expressed their welcome with enthusiastic applause. After the performance, Vice-Chairman Bao Erhan and other leaders, along with the heads of the ethnic groups visiting delegations, went on stage to shake hands warmly with the performers.

### 政协全国委员会举行晚会  招待参加国庆观礼的各民族参观团

1960-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会举行晚会
    招待参加国庆观礼的各民族参观团
    新华社6日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会今晚在政协礼堂举行晚会，招待各地来北京参加国庆观礼的各民族参观团。
    晚会前，政协全国委员会副主席陈叔通、包尔汉接见了以段明为首的云南省各民族参观团，以张平凡为首的西藏参观团，以桂芝为首的西藏青年、妇女参观团，以王福田为首的宁夏参观团，以托乎提阿不都拉为首的新疆参观团。在接见的时候，陈叔通说，我和包尔汉副主席一道，代表中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会，向各位和参加今年国庆观礼的各民族参观团的同志们，表示热烈的欢迎和衷心的祝贺。祝你们在各自的岗位上取得新的更大的成就！
    陈叔通说，我国各族人民在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下，在总路线、大跃进和人民公社三面红旗的光辉照耀下，更加紧密地团结起来，共同努力，艰苦奋斗，我们一定会获得更大的成就。
    接见时，在座的有中共中央统一战线工作部副部长、政协全国委员会常委张执一，政协全国委员会副秘书长徐伯昕、辛志超，人大民族委员会副主任委员谢扶民，民族事务委员会副主任谢鹤筹，政协全国委员会常务委员喜饶嘉错、卢汉、萨空了，政协全国委员会民族组负责人载涛、吕振羽等。
    在晚会上，中央实验歌剧院演出“五朵红云”。这个描写海南岛五指山黎族人民争取解放的英勇斗争故事的舞剧，深深地激动着每一个观众，观众以热烈的掌声表示欢迎。演出结束后，包尔汉副主席等和各民族参观团团长上台，同演员们亲切地握手。
