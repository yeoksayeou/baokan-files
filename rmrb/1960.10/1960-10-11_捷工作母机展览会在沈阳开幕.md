Title: Czechoslovak Machine Tool Exhibition Opens in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 8th October: The Czechoslovak Machine Tool Exhibition grandly opened in Shenyang on the 8th.

Yu Jingqing, Deputy Secretary-General of the Liaoning Provincial People's Committee, Xu Zhi, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, Zhu Hua, Director of the Liaoning Provincial Foreign Trade Bureau, and Zou Jiahua, Director of the Sino-Czech Friendship Factory in Shenyang, as well as factory leaders and engineering technicians from the Shenyang machinery manufacturing sector, attended the opening ceremony.

Commercial Counselor Josef Simak of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, Miroslav Lašovský, head of the Czechoslovak Machine Tool Exhibition, and the Czechoslovak staff of the exhibition attended the opening ceremony.

Counselor Simak spoke at the opening ceremony. He introduced the development of Czechoslovak socialist industry. He also said that the exhibition in Shenyang demonstrates the further development of mutual cooperation between Czechoslovakia and China.

After the ribbon-cutting, the head of the exhibition guided those attending the opening ceremony to visit the exhibited machine tools and watch operation demonstrations. This exhibition is the largest scale exhibition of machine tools held abroad by Czechoslovakia. The exhibits include seventy metal cutting machine tools and forming machine tools of various types with automatic and semi-automatic program control, many of which are new products exhibited abroad for the first time. Visitors praised the degree of automation and precision of these machine tools.

### 捷工作母机展览会在沈阳开幕

1960-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    捷工作母机展览会在沈阳开幕
    据新华社沈阳8日电　捷克斯洛伐克工作母机展览会，8日在沈阳隆重开幕。
    辽宁省人民委员会副秘书长于镜清、沈阳市副市长徐志、辽宁省对外贸易局局长朱华和沈阳中捷友谊厂厂长邹家华，以及沈阳机械制造部门工厂的负责人、工程技术人员多人出席了开幕式。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆商务专员球赛夫·西麦克，捷克斯洛伐克工作母机展览会负责人米娄斯腊夫·拉绍夫斯基和展览会的捷克斯洛伐克工作人员都出席了开幕式。
    西麦克专员在开幕式上讲了话。他介绍了捷克斯洛伐克社会主义工业的发展情况。他还说，在沈阳开幕的展览会，表现了捷中两国互相合作的进一步发展。
    剪彩以后，展览会负责人引导参加开幕式的人参观了展出的机床和操作表演。这个展览会是捷克斯洛伐克在国外展览工作母机规模最大的一次。展品中包括各种有自动和半自动程序控制的金属切削机床和成型机床七十台，其中有许多是第一次在国外展出的新产品。参观的人对这些机床的自动化程度和精密度表示赞扬。
