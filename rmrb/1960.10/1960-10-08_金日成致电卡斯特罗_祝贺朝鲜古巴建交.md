Title: Kim Il-sung Sends Telegram to Castro Congratulating on Establishment of Diplomatic Relations between North Korea and Cuba
Author:
Date: 1960-10-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 7th—According to the Korean Central News Agency, Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, sent a telegram today to Fidel Castro, Prime Minister of the Revolutionary Government of the Republic of Cuba, congratulating on the establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries.

The telegram stated that the establishment of diplomatic relations between the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Republic of Cuba marks a new stage in the further development of the friendly relations forged between the Korean and Cuban peoples in the struggle against their common enemy, U.S. imperialism.

The telegram said that the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the entire Korean people, who are struggling for the withdrawal of U.S. imperialist aggressor troops from South Korea and for the peaceful reunification of the fatherland, have always stood on the side of the heroic Cuban people against U.S. imperialism, actively supporting and supporting the brave struggle of the Cuban people.

In his telegram, Premier Kim Il-sung wished Prime Minister Fidel Castro greater success in the noble cause of defending the national independence and sovereign rights of his country and striving for world peace.

### 金日成致电卡斯特罗  祝贺朝鲜古巴建交

1960-10-08
第6版()
专栏：

    金日成致电卡斯特罗
    祝贺朝鲜古巴建交
    新华社平壤7日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成今天打电报给古巴共和国革命政府总理菲德尔·卡斯特罗，祝贺两国建立外交关系。
    电报说，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和古巴共和国建立外交关系，对进一步发展朝鲜人民和古巴人民之间在反对共同敌人——美帝国主义的斗争中所结成的友好关系，是一个新的阶段。
    电报说，为使美帝国主义侵略军从南朝鲜撤走和争取和平统一祖国而斗争的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和全体朝鲜人民，一向站在反对美帝国主义的英雄的古巴人民这一边，积极支持和声援古巴人民的勇敢斗争。
    金日成首相在电报中预祝菲德尔·卡斯特罗总理在保卫自己祖国的民族独立和自主权利、争取世界和平的崇高事业中取得更大的成就。
