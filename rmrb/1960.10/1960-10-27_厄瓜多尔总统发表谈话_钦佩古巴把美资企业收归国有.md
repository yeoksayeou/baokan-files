Title: Ecuadorian President Speaks, Admiring Cuba's Nationalization of American-owned Enterprises
Author:
Date: 1960-10-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th dispatch from Quito: Ecuadorian President José María Velasco Ibarra, in a conversation with some civil servants, expressed his admiration for Cuban Prime Minister Fidel Castro and his "actions of nationalizing large American-invested enterprises for the benefit of the people of this small island nation (Cuba)."

The Ecuadorian President also emphasized the importance of the Cuban people's unity.

### 厄瓜多尔总统发表谈话  钦佩古巴把美资企业收归国有

1960-10-27
第6版()
专栏：

    厄瓜多尔总统发表谈话
    钦佩古巴把美资企业收归国有
    新华社25日讯　据拉丁美洲通讯社基多消息：厄瓜多尔总统何塞·玛丽亚·贝拉斯科·伊瓦拉在同一些公务人员的谈话中表示钦佩古巴总理菲德尔·卡斯特罗和他“为了这个小的岛屿国家（古巴）的人民的利益而把美国人投资的大企业收归国有的行动”。
    这位厄瓜多尔总统还强调指出了古巴人民团结的重要性。
