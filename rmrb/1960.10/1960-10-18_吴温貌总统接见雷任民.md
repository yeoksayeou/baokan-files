Title: President U Win Maung Receives Lei Renmin
Author:
Date: 1960-10-18
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, October 17th. Burmese President U Win Maung received Lei Renmin, head of the People's Republic of China Government Trade Delegation, who is currently visiting Burma, last night. President U Win Maung had a cordial and friendly conversation with Head of Delegation Lei Renmin.
	U Win Maung received Lei Renmin shortly before hosting a banquet to welcome General Ne Win back from his visit to China. Lei Renmin and all members of the Chinese Government Trade Delegation were also invited to attend the banquet.

### 吴温貌总统接见雷任民

1960-10-18
第5版()
专栏：

    吴温貌总统接见雷任民
    新华社仰光17日电　缅甸联邦总统吴温貌在昨晚接见了正在缅甸访问的中华人民共和国政府贸易代表团团长雷任民。吴温貌总统同雷任民团长进行了亲切友好的交谈。
    吴温貌是在设宴欢迎从中国访问回来的奈温将军前不久接见雷任民的。雷任民和中国政府贸易代表团全体团员也应邀出席了这一宴会。
