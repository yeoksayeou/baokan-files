Title: China-Latin America Friendship Association Hosts Banquet to Bid Farewell to Haitian Cultural Delegation and Other Foreign Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-10-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 8th - The Vice President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, Zhou Erfu, held a banquet on the evening of the 8th to bid farewell to the Haitian Cultural Friendship Delegation headed by Jean Lattre, Venezuelan-China Friendship Association Executive Committee member, Senator Augusto Leon and his wife, and Venezuelan Cultural Delegation member, thoracic surgery expert Manuel Adrianzan.

Among those attending the banquet were Council members of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, playwright Cao Yu and writer Jia Zhi.

### 中国拉丁美洲友好协会设宴  欢送海地文化代表团等外宾

1960-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    中国拉丁美洲友好协会设宴
    欢送海地文化代表团等外宾
    新华社8日讯　中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复8日晚举行宴会，欢送以让·拉脱为首的海地文化友好代表团，委内瑞拉委中友好协会执行委员会委员、参议员阿古斯多·列昂和夫人，以及委内瑞拉文化代表团团员、胸腔外科专家马努埃尔·阿德里安萨。
    出席宴会的有中拉友协理事、戏剧家曹禺和作家贾芝等人。
