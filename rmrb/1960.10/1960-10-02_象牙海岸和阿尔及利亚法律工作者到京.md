Title: Ivorian and Algerian Legal Workers Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-02
Page: 7
　
Xinhua News Agency, October 1st. At the invitation of the China Political Science and Law Society, Assi Camille Adam, Chairman of the National Liberation Committee of Ivory Coast and a famous lawyer, and Nimol, an Algerian legal worker, arrived in Beijing on the 1st for a visit to our country.
　　Upon their arrival in Beijing, they were welcomed by Pan Handian and others from the Political Science and Law Society.

### 象牙海岸和阿尔及利亚法律工作者到京

1960-10-02
第7版()
专栏：

    象牙海岸和阿尔及利亚法律工作者到京
    新华社1日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的象牙海岸民族解放委员会主席、名律师阿西·卡米尔·阿当，阿尔及利亚法律工作者尼摩尔于1日到达北京。
    他们到达北京时，受到政法学会理事潘汉典等人的欢迎。
