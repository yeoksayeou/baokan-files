Title: Brazilian Architects Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Brazilian architects delegation, led by João Batista Vilanova Artigas, Vice President of the Brazilian Institute of Architects, concluded their friendly visit to China and departed Beijing for home in two groups on September 23rd and October 4th, at the invitation of the Architectural Society of China. Yang Chunmao and Liang Sicheng, Vice Chairmen of the Architectural Society of China, and others saw them off at the airport.

Before the delegation returned home, Liu Xiufeng, Minister of the Ministry of Construction Engineering, met with some members of the delegation.

### 巴西建筑家代表团回国

1960-10-06
第3版()
专栏：

    巴西建筑家代表团回国
    新华社5日讯　应中国建筑学会邀请，以巴西建筑学会副主席约奥·巴蒂斯托·威拉诺瓦·阿蒂格斯为首的巴西建筑家代表团，结束了在我国的友好访问，于9月23日、10月4日分两批离开北京回国。中国建筑学会副理事长杨春茂、梁思成等人到机场送行。
    在代表团回国前，建筑工程部部长刘秀峰接见了代表团部分团员。
