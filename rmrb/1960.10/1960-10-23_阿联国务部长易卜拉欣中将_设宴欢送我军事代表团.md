Title: UAR Minister of State Lt. Gen. Ibrahim Hosts Banquet to See Off Chinese Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, October 21st: The Minister of State for Military Affairs of the United Arab Republic, Lieutenant General Mohammed Ibrahim, held a banquet here tonight to see off the Chinese military friendship delegation headed by General Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Ibrahim spoke at the banquet. In his speech, he expressed gratitude for the warm and cordial welcome he received during his visit to China in 1958 as the head of the UAR military friendship delegation, and praised the achievements of the Chinese people.

He said, "Your people and army, workers and peasants, cooperate shoulder to shoulder, eliminate imperialism and reactionaries in China, and your efforts have created fruitful and constructive undertakings."

He continued, "During our visit to China, we personally witnessed your country's sincere implementation of the Bandung spirit and your support for our struggle against aggression and imperialism. Although our beliefs, languages, and geographical locations are different, our two countries are filled with friendship and harmony." Ibrahim concluded by saying that the friendship between the peoples of our two countries will be further developed and strengthened as time goes on.

General Zhang Zongxun also spoke at the banquet. He first expressed his gratitude for the warm welcome and hospitality the Chinese military friendship delegation received during its visit to the UAR. He said, "We came here with the deep friendship of the Chinese people and army towards the people and army of the UAR, and now we will return to China full of the friendship of the people and army of the UAR. We believe that on the basis of the common struggle against aggression and in defense of peace, the friendship between the people and armies of the two countries will continue to develop. The Chinese people have always supported the struggle of the UAR and the African people against imperialism and colonialism. In this struggle, the 650 million Chinese people will always be the most loyal friends of the Arab and African peoples."

The speeches of Ibrahim and Zhang Zongxun were repeatedly greeted with warm applause.

Among those present at the banquet were Lieutenant General Ali Amer, Chief of Staff of the UAR Army, generals of the UAR armed forces, government ministers, and prominent figures from Cairo.

Also present at the banquet were Chen Jiakang, the Chinese Ambassador to the UAR, and diplomatic personnel from the Chinese Embassy.

### 阿联国务部长易卜拉欣中将  设宴欢送我军事代表团

1960-10-23
第6版()
专栏：

    阿联国务部长易卜拉欣中将
    设宴欢送我军事代表团
    据新华社开罗21日电　阿拉伯联合共和国负责军事事务的国务部长穆罕默德·易卜拉欣中将，今晚在这里举行宴会，欢送以中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊上将为首的中国军事友好代表团。
    易卜拉欣在宴会上讲了话。他在讲话中，对于他在1958年率领的阿联军事友好代表团在访问中国时所受到的热烈和殷勤的欢迎，表示感谢，并且赞扬中国人民取得的成就。
    他说，“你们的人民和军队、工人和农民肩并肩地合作，消灭了在中国的帝国主义和反动派，你们的努力创建了富有成果的、建设性的事业。”
    他接着说，我们在访问中国期间，亲眼看到了贵国真诚地贯彻万隆精神和对我们反对侵略和反对帝国主义的斗争的支持。尽管我们的信仰、语言和地理位置不同，但是我们两国洋溢着友谊和和睦。易卜拉欣最后说，我们两国人民的友谊将随着时间的推进得到进一步的发展和加强。
    张宗逊上将也在宴会上讲了话。他首先对中国军事友好代表团在访问阿联期间所受到的热烈欢迎和款待表示感谢。他说，“我们带着中国人民和军队对阿联人民和军队的深厚友谊来到这里，现在我们将满载阿联人民和军队的友谊返回中国。我们相信，在反对侵略和保卫和平的共同斗争的基础上，两国人民和军队的友谊必将继续发展下去。中国人民一贯支持阿联和非洲人民反对帝国主义和殖民主义的斗争。在这个斗争中，六亿五千万中国人民将永远是阿拉伯和非洲各国人民的最忠实的朋友。”
    易卜拉欣和张宗逊的讲话，一再受到热烈掌声的欢迎。
    出席宴会的有阿联陆军参谋长阿里·阿密尔中将、阿联武装部队的将军们、政府部长们和开罗的知名人士。
    出席宴会的还有中国驻阿联大使陈家康和中国大使馆的外交人员。
