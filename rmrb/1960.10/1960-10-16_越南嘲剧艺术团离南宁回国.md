Title: Vietnamese Cheo Art Troupe Leaves Nanning for Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-10-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, October 15th: The Cheo Art Troupe of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam concluded its visit and performances in China and left Nanning by train for home on the evening of the 14th.

The Vietnamese Cheo Art Troupe arrived in Beijing on August 26th. After performing five shows in Beijing and participating in the capital's celebrations of the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, they left Beijing on September 5th to visit and perform in Shanghai, Changsha, Guangzhou, Nanning, and other places. They were unanimously praised by the audience and warmly received by local party and government leaders. When the guests gave a public performance in Shanghai on the evening of September 8th, Ke Qingshi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, First Secretary of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee, and Mayor of Shanghai, and other party and government leaders of Shanghai attended and watched the performance.

### 越南嘲剧艺术团离南宁回国

1960-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    越南嘲剧艺术团离南宁回国
    据新华社南宁15日电　越南民主共和国嘲剧艺术团结束了在我国的访问演出，14日晚从南宁乘火车回国。
    越南嘲剧艺术团是在8月26日到达北京的。他们在北京演出了五场并参加了首都人民庆祝越南民主共和国成立十五周年活动后，于9月5日离开北京，去上海、长沙、广州、南宁等地访问演出，受到观众一致好评，并受到当地党政领导人员热烈招待。客人们9月8日晚在上海举行公演时，中共中央政治局委员、中共上海市委第一书记、上海市市长柯庆施等上海市党、政领导人出席观看了演出。
