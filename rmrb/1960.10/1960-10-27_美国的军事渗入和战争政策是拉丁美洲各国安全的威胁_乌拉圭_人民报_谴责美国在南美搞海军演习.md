Title: US Military Infiltration and War Policies Threaten the Security of Latin American Countries; Uruguayan "El Popular" Condemns US Naval Exercises in South America
Author:
Date: 1960-10-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th, Montevideo: Uruguay's "El Popular" commented on the US-instigated joint naval exercises involving some Latin American countries, pointing out that the US is now frantically plotting an invasion of Cuba and attempting to create a hysterical atmosphere.

It is reported that under US instigation, warships from Argentina, Brazil, and Uruguay will conduct joint naval exercises with the US in the Rio de la Plata bordering Argentina and Uruguay, starting on November 7th. The exercises will last until November 25th.

Uruguay's "El Popular" wrote: Why does Uruguay need to participate in naval exercises in the Rio de la Plata? As Cuba pointed out at the recent meeting of the Inter-American Defense Board, the only threat to our countries under any circumstances is US military infiltration and its war policies, and the US Navy is serving as the "nerve center" of these naval exercises.

### 美国的军事渗入和战争政策是拉丁美洲各国安全的威胁  乌拉圭“人民报”谴责美国在南美搞海军演习

1960-10-27
第6版()
专栏：

    美国的军事渗入和战争政策是拉丁美洲各国安全的威胁
    乌拉圭“人民报”谴责美国在南美搞海军演习
    据新华社25日讯　蒙得维的亚消息：乌拉圭“人民报”在评论美国策动一些拉丁美洲国家举行联合海军演习时指出，现在美国正在狂热地筹划入侵古巴的阴谋，企图制造歇斯底里的气氛。
    据报道，在美国的策动下，阿根廷、巴西和乌拉圭的军舰将从11月7日起在阿根廷、乌拉圭交界处的拉巴拉他河同美国联合进行海军演习，这次演习将延续到11月25日结束。
    乌拉圭“人民报”写道：为什么乌拉圭需要参加拉巴拉他河的海军演习呢？正如古巴在泛美防务委员会最近一次会议上所指出的，在任何情况下，我们这些国家所存在的唯一威胁是美国的军事渗入及其战争政策，而正是美国的海军充当这次海军演习的“神经中枢”。
