Title: Shan State-Kachin State Friendship Delegation of Burma Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 9th. The Chairman of the Shan State Council of the Union of Burma, Su Pi, the Chairman of the Kachin State Council, Wu Shan Niu, and the Shan State-Kachin State Friendship Delegation of the Union of Burma they led, left Kunming by plane this morning to return home, fully loaded with the profound "Pauk-Phaw" (fraternal) friendship of the people of all nationalities in Yunnan towards the people of all nationalities in the Shan State and Kachin State of the Union of Burma.

Liu Minghui, Wu Zuomin, and Liu Piyun, Vice Governors of Yunnan Province, and Li Xifu, Major General of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming, were at the airport to welcome the distinguished guests. Also at the airport to see them off were Pan Shuo Duan, Mayor of Kunming, Yu Futing, Deputy Mayor, Zhang Zizhai, Secretary-General of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee, Wang Shaoyan, Vice Chairman of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, heads of local organizations of various democratic parties and people's organizations in the province and city, heads of various departments of the Provincial People's Committee, Lei Chunguo, Deputy Governor of the Dehong Dai-Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture, Hu Zhonghua, Deputy Commissioner of the Lincang Special District, Pei Aqian, Governor of the Nujiang Lisu Autonomous Prefecture, and hundreds of people of all nationalities in Kunming. Wu Maung Maung So, Consul General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, and Wu Xin, Vice Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming, also went to the airport to see them off.

The Shan State-Kachin State Friendship Delegation of the Union of Burma came to Kunming for a friendly visit at the invitation of Yu Yichuan, Governor of Yunnan Province. During their stay in Kunming, the distinguished guests participated in the activities of the people of all ethnic groups in Kunming celebrating the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the signing of the Sino-Burmese Border Treaty, visited some factories, people's communes and schools in Kunming, and toured scenic spots.

### 缅甸掸邦克钦邦友好代表团回国

1960-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸掸邦克钦邦友好代表团回国
    新华社昆明9日电　缅甸联邦掸邦议会主席苏辟、克钦邦议会主席吴山纽和他们率领的缅甸联邦掸邦、克钦邦友好代表团，今天上午满载着云南各族人民对于缅甸联邦掸邦和克钦邦各族人民“胞波”般的深厚情谊，乘飞机离开昆明回国。
    云南省副省长刘明辉、吴作民、刘披云和中国人民解放军驻昆明部队负责人黎锡福少将到机场欢送贵宾。到机场欢送的，还有昆明市市长潘朔端、副市长于馥亭，省人民委员会秘书长张子斋，政协云南省委员会副主席王少岩，各民主党派省市地方组织和各人民团体的负责人，省人民委员会各部门负责人，德宏傣族景颇族自治州副州长雷春国，临沧专区副专员胡忠华，怒江傈僳族自治州州长裴阿欠，以及昆明各族群众数百人。缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭和越南民主共和国驻昆明副领事吴新也到机场送行。
    缅甸联邦掸邦、克钦邦友好代表团是应云南省省长于一川的邀请，来昆明作友好访问的。贵宾们在昆明期间，参加了昆明市各族各界人民庆祝建国十一周年的活动和庆祝中缅边界条约签订的活动，参观访问了昆明的一些工厂，人民公社和学校，游览了风景名胜。
