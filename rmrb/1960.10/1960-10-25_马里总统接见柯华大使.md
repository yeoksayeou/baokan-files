Title: Malian President Receives Ambassador Ke Hua
Author:
Date: 1960-10-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bamako, October 24th. Malian Republic President Modibo Keita today received Chinese Ambassador to Guinea Ke Hua here, and had a friendly conversation.

### 马里总统接见柯华大使

1960-10-25
第6版()
专栏：

    马里总统接见柯华大使
    新华社巴马科24日电　马里共和国总统莫迪博·凯塔今天在这里接见了中国驻几内亚大使柯华，并进行了友好的谈话。
