Title: Kellezi Attends Capital Militia Shooting Performance
Author:
Date: 1960-10-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 20th: Abdyl Këllezi, Member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Vice Chairman of the Council of Minister and President of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association, and all distinguished guests of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, watched the capital militia's shooting and other technical performances this afternoon.

Accompanying them were Liu Mingfu, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission, Song Xuefei, Deputy Commander of the Beijing Garrison, Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs.

Some members of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation also visited the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Museum and the National Agricultural Exhibition Center.

### 凯莱齐参观首都民兵射击表演

1960-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    凯莱齐参观首都民兵射击表演
    新华社20日讯　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、部长会议副主席、阿中友协主席阿卜杜勒·凯莱齐和阿中友协代表团全体贵宾，今天下午参观了首都民兵射击等技术表演。
    陪同参观的有国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫，北京卫戍区副司令员宋学飞，外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文和苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清等。
    阿中友协代表团部分团员还参观了中国人民革命军事博物馆和全国农业展览馆。
