Title: General Ne Win Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-10-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 12th. General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces of the Union of Burma, and his wife, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, arrived in Guangzhou this morning from Hangzhou by special train. They were warmly welcomed by thousands of people from all walks of life in Guangzhou.

Today, the Guangzhou Railway Station was decorated with the national flags of China and Burma, and a huge banner reading "Welcome General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces of the Union of Burma." A decorated archway was erected at the entrance of the station. The square in front of the station was filled with welcoming crowds holding banners, slogans, and bouquets.

Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province, and his wife; Zhao Ziyang, Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Lin Qiangyun, Deputy Governor of Guangdong Province; Xue Yan, Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Sun Leyi, Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou; Lieutenant General Wen Niansheng, Commander of the Guangzhou Garrison, and his wife, and others, were at the platform to greet the distinguished guests.

At 9:40 a.m., the train carrying the distinguished guests slowly pulled into the station. The band played a welcoming tune. General Ne Win and his wife stepped out of the train amidst warm applause. Governor Chen Yu and others stepped forward to greet them and shook hands warmly with each of the distinguished guests. A group of Young Pioneers presented the distinguished guests with flowers. General Ne Win then met with the heads of the relevant departments of the Guangdong Provincial and Guangzhou Municipal People's Committees, the army, navy, and air force units of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou, the Guangdong and Guangzhou Municipal Committees of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and various people's organizations and local organizations of democratic parties.

When General Ne Win, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, Governor Chen Yu, and others, met with the welcoming crowds, applause, drumbeats, and slogans of "Welcome General Ne Win!" and "Long Live Sino-Burmese Friendship!" resounded around the station. Girls constantly ran out of the crowd to present flowers to the distinguished guests. General Ne Win and his wife frequently waved their bouquets to acknowledge the welcoming crowds.

Consuls and Vice-Consuls from various countries stationed in Guangzhou were also at the station to welcome the guests today.

The distinguished guests, accompanied by Governor Chen Yu and others, traveled by car to the Yingbin (Guest) Hotel to rest. Along the way, many buildings were decorated with banners welcoming General Ne Win.

After a short rest at the hotel, General Ne Win and his wife, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, and Governor Chen Yu and his wife, visited the former site of the Peasant Movement Training Institute, which was founded by Chairman Mao, the Guangzhou Martyrs' Cemetery, the China Export Commodities Fair, the Guangzhou Ivory Arts Factory, the Guangdong Folk Arts Museum, and the city of Guangzhou. General Ne Win also watched the military exercises of the militia division of the South China Institute of Technology. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by the masses everywhere they went.

### 奈温将军到达广州

1960-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    奈温将军到达广州
    新华社广州12日电　缅甸联邦国防军总参谋长奈温将军和夫人，由陈毅副总理和夫人陪同，今天上午从杭州乘专车到达广州，受到广州市各界群众数千人的热烈欢迎。
    今天，广州车站上悬挂着中缅两国国旗，和写着“欢迎缅甸联邦国防军总参谋长奈温将军”的巨幅标语，车站门口搭了彩牌。车站门前广场上站满了拿着横额、标语和花束的欢迎群众。
    广东省省长陈郁和夫人，中共广东省委书记赵紫阳，广东省副省长林锵云，中共广州市委书记薛焰，广州市副市长孙乐宜，广州驻军首长文年生中将和夫人等，在站台上迎候贵宾。
    上午九时四十分，贵宾们乘坐的火车徐徐进站。乐队奏起欢迎曲。奈温将军和夫人在热烈的掌声中步出车厢，这时陈郁省长等迎上前去，和贵宾们一一亲切握手。一群少先队员向贵宾们献了鲜花。接着，奈温将军和前来欢迎的广东省和广州市人民委员会各有关部门、人民解放军驻广州部队陆海空军部队、政协广东省和广州市委员会以及各人民团体、各民主党派地方组织等各方面的负责人会见。
    当奈温将军由陈毅副总理、陈郁省长等陪同和欢迎群众见面时，车站周围响起了一片掌声、锣鼓声和“欢迎奈温将军！”“中缅友好万岁！”的口号声。姑娘们不断从人群中跑出来，把鲜花献给贵宾。奈温将军和夫人频频挥舞花束，向欢迎的群众答礼。
    各国驻广州的领事和副领事今天也到车站欢迎。
    贵宾们由陈郁省长等陪同，乘车前往迎宾馆休息。沿途许多建筑物上张挂着欢迎奈温将军的标语横额。
    奈温将军和夫人在宾馆稍事休息后，由陈毅副总理和夫人、陈郁省长和夫人陪同，参观了毛主席当年主办的农民运动讲习所旧址、广州烈士陵园、中国出口商品陈列馆、广州象牙艺术工厂、广东民间工艺馆和广州市容。奈温将军还观看了华南工学院民兵师的军事操练。贵宾们到处受到群众的热烈欢迎。
