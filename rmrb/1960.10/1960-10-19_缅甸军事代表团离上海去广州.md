Title: Burmese Military Delegation Leaves Shanghai for Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-10-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 18th: The Burmese Military Friendship Delegation, led by Brigadier General Maung Maung, left Shanghai for Guangzhou by special plane on the 18th after visiting Nanjing and Shanghai, accompanied by Lieutenant General Yang Xiushan, representative of the Chinese Ministry of National Defense. Colonel Kyin Shain Moon, military attaché of the Burmese Embassy in China, also accompanied the delegation.

During their stay in Nanjing, the distinguished guests visited military academies of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, paid respects at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum, and toured scenic spots such as Xuanwu Lake. During their stay in Shanghai, the distinguished guests visited the Shanghai Industrial Exhibition, the Shanghai Watch Factory, and a shipyard. They also watched a fighter jet performance by a certain unit of the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and toured the Huangpu River by warship.

When the Burmese Military Friendship Delegation arrived and departed Nanjing and Shanghai, Lieutenant General Zhang Caiqian and Lieutenant General Zhang Fan, leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Nanjing; Zhou Yifeng, Vice Governor of Jiangsu Province, and others; Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng, Navy Lieutenant General Tao Yong, leaders of the Shanghai garrison; and Song Richang, Vice Mayor of Shanghai, and others, greeted and saw them off.

### 缅甸军事代表团离上海去广州

1960-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸军事代表团离上海去广州
    新华社18日讯　以貌貌准将为首的缅甸军事友好代表团，在南京、上海参观访问后，由我国国防部代表杨秀山中将陪同，18日由上海乘专机飞往广州。缅甸联邦驻中国大使馆武官景沙门上校也随同代表团前往。
    贵宾们在南京期间，曾参观访问了中国人民解放军军事院校，晋谒了中山陵，游览了玄武湖等名胜古迹。贵宾们在上海时，参观了上海市工业展览会、上海手表厂、造船厂，还观看了中国人民解放军空军某部的歼击机飞行表演，乘军舰游览了黄浦江。
    缅甸军事友好代表团在到达和离开南京、上海时，中国人民解放军驻南京部队首长张才千中将、张藩中将，江苏省副省长周一峰等；上海驻军首长王必成中将、陶勇海军中将，上海市副市长宋日昌等，曾前往迎送。
