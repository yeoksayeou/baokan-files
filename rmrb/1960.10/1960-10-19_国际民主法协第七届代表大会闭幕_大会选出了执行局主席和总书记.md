Title: Seventh Congress of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers Closes; Congress Elects Chairman of the Executive Bureau and Secretary-General
Author:
Date: 1960-10-19
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 16th - The Seventh Congress of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers closed on the evening of the 14th, after five days. More than 250 democratic legal workers from about 50 countries, including the Soviet Union and China, discussed relevant issues and adopted corresponding resolutions.

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 16th - Newspapers here today published the election results of the Chairman of the Executive Bureau and Secretary-General of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers' Seventh Congress.

The Congress was held in Sofia from the 10th to the 14th of this month. During the meeting, the congress, on the recommendation of the presidium, newly elected three chairmen of the Executive Bureau: Pierre Cot (France), Rudolf Bystricky (Czechoslovakia), and A.B. Perera (Ceylon). The former Chairman of the Executive Bureau, Pritt (UK), was elected Honorary Chairman of the Executive Bureau. The Association's former Secretary-General, Nordmann (France), was re-elected as Secretary-General.

### 国际民主法协第七届代表大会闭幕  大会选出了执行局主席和总书记

1960-10-19
第6版()
专栏：

    国际民主法协第七届代表大会闭幕
    大会选出了执行局主席和总书记
    新华社索非亚16日电　国际民主法律工作者协会第七届代表大会于14日晚闭幕，大会共开了五天。苏联、中国等约五十个国家的二百五十多名民主法律工作者，讨论了有关的问题，并通过相应的决议。
    新华社索非亚16日电　此间报纸今天发表了国际民主法律工作者协会第七届代表大会关于这个协会的执行局主席和总书记的选举结果。
    本届代表大会于本月10日至14日在索非亚举行。会议期间，大会根据主席团的建议新选出了三个执行局主席：皮埃尔·戈特（法国）、鲁道夫·比斯特里斯基（捷克斯洛伐克）和阿·巴·佩雷拉（锡兰）。执行局前任主席普里特（英国）被选为执行局名誉主席。协会前任总书记诺尔曼（法国）仍然当选为总书记。
