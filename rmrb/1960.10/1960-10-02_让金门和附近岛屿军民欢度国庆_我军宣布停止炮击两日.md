Title: To Allow the Military and Civilians on Kinmen and Nearby Islands to Joyfully Celebrate National Day, Our Army Announces a Two-Day Ceasefire

Author:
Date: 1960-10-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 1st: A spokesman for the Fujian Front Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army announced: In order to allow compatriots and Chiang Kai-shek's army officers and soldiers on islands such as Greater Kinmen, Lesser Kinmen, Greater Tan, and Lesser Tan to celebrate National Day together with the people of the whole country, the artillery units of the People's Liberation Army on the Fujian Front have been ordered to cease shelling on the single-numbered days of October 1st and 3rd, 1960.

### 让金门和附近岛屿军民欢度国庆  我军宣布停止炮击两日

1960-10-02
第5版()
专栏：

    让金门和附近岛屿军民欢度国庆
    我军宣布停止炮击两日
    新华社福建前线1日电　中国人民解放军福建前线司令部发言人宣称：为了使大金门、小金门、大担、二担等岛屿上的同胞和蒋军官兵和全国人民一道欢度国庆，福建前线人民解放军炮兵部队，奉命在1960年10月1日和3日两个单日停止炮击。
