Title: Premier Zhou Continues Talks with Takasaki Tatsunosuke
Author:
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd: Premier Zhou Enlai continued talks with Japanese Mr. Takasaki Tatsunosuke in a friendly atmosphere this afternoon. Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi was present at the talks.

### 周总理同高碕达之助继续会谈

1960-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理同高碕达之助继续会谈
    新华社23日讯　周恩来总理今天下午同日本高碕达之助先生在友好的气氛中，继续举行会谈。会谈时在座的，有国务院副总理陈毅。
