Title: Premier Zhou Receives Vice Premier Li Jooyon
Author:
Date: 1960-10-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 5th. Premier Zhou Enlai received this afternoon Li Jooyon, head of the government economic delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Vice Premier of the Korean Cabinet, as well as delegation members Kim Choi-sun, Vice Minister of Trade, and Li Yong-ho, Ambassador to China, and held cordial talks.

Present at the meeting were Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, and Vice Director of the State Planning Commission Song Yangchu.

### 周总理接见李周渊副首相

1960-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见李周渊副首相
    新华社5日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府经济代表团团长、朝鲜内阁副首相李周渊和代表团团员、贸易省副相金最善、驻华大使李永镐等，并进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时，在座的有国务院副总理李先念，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，对外贸易部副部长李强，国家计划委员会副主任宋养初等。
