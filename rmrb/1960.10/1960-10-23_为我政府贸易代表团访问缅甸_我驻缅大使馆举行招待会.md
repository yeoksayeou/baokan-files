Title: Chinese Embassy in Burma Holds Reception for Chinese Government Trade Delegation's Visit to Burma
Author:
Date: 1960-10-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, October 21st - Chinese Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Lei Yang held a reception in Rangoon tonight to welcome the Chinese Government Trade Delegation, headed by Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin, which is currently visiting Burma.

Among those present at the reception were Speaker of the Burmese Chamber of Nationalities Zaw Khun Kyi, Foreign Minister Zaw Khun Htun, Minister of Trade Development and Civil Supplies U Tun, Prime Minister’s Advisor U Wang, Parliamentary Secretary of the Ministry of Trade Development and Civil Supplies U Hla Gyaw, Deputy Director of the State Agricultural Marketing Board U Hla, and more than 300 other senior Burmese government officials, chairmen and general managers of Burmese companies and joint state-private enterprises. Diplomatic envoys and trade representatives from various countries to Burma also attended the reception.

The reception was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

Both hosts and guests repeatedly proposed toasts to the success of the Sino-Burmese trade talks and the further development of Sino-Burmese friendship.

### 为我政府贸易代表团访问缅甸  我驻缅大使馆举行招待会

1960-10-23
第6版()
专栏：

    为我政府贸易代表团访问缅甸
    我驻缅大使馆举行招待会
    新华社仰光21日电　中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬今晚在仰光举行招待会，欢迎目前正在缅甸访问的以对外贸易部副部长雷任民为首的中国政府贸易代表团。
    出席招待会的有缅甸民族院议长藻昆基、外交部长藻昆卓、贸易发展和民用物资供应部长吴敦、总理顾问吴旺、贸易发展和民用物资供应部议会秘书吴拉觉，国家农产品贸易局副局长吴拉和其他缅甸政府高级官员，缅甸各公司和公私合营企业的董事长和总经理等三百多人。各国驻缅甸外交使节和贸易代表也出席了招待会。
    招待会是在热烈、友好的气氛中举行的。
    宾主双方一再建议为中缅贸易会谈的成功和进一步发展中缅友谊而干杯。
