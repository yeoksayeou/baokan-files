Title: Romanian Education Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-02
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, October 1st. According to the 1960 execution plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, a two-member Romanian education delegation, led by Yang Liviscu, president of the Romanian Bahong University, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Dong Haibo, representative of the Ministry of Education, and others. The Romanian Ambassador to China, Zaharescu, also went to the airport to meet them.

### 罗马尼亚教育代表团到京

1960-10-02
第7版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚教育代表团到京
    新华社1日讯　根据中罗文化合作协定1960年执行计划的规定，罗马尼亚教育代表团一行二人，在罗马尼亚巴洪大学校长杨·利维斯库率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。前往飞机场欢迎的，有教育部代表董海波等。罗马尼亚驻华大使查哈勒斯库也前往机场迎接。
