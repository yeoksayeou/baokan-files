Title: Brigadier Aung Gyi Speaks on Impressions of Visiting China: China's Economic Development is Very Rapid; Sino-Burmese Trade Should Develop Greatly on a Basis of Friendship
Author:
Date: 1960-10-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, October 21st: According to reports in local newspapers today, Brigadier Aung Gyi, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Burmese Federal Defense Forces and Chief Representative of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, paid a visit to U Thun, Minister of Trade Development and Civil Supplies of Burma, yesterday and spoke to U Thun about his impressions of visiting China.

Brigadier Aung Gyi returned to Rangoon from China on October 19th.

Brigadier Aung Gyi told Minister U Thun that the development of the People's Republic of China in industry, agricultural production, and trade is extremely rapid. He believes that trade between China and Burma should develop greatly in the future on a new and friendly basis.

The newspaper also reported that U Thun told reporters here that he firmly believes that after the century-old boundary issue is completely resolved, Sino-Burmese trade will be further developed.

### 昂季准将谈访华观感  中国经济发展非常迅速  中缅贸易应在友好基础上大大发展

1960-10-23
第6版()
专栏：

    昂季准将谈访华观感
    中国经济发展非常迅速
    中缅贸易应在友好基础上大大发展
    新华社仰光21日电　据此间报纸今天报道，缅甸联邦国防军副总参谋长、中缅边界联合委员会首席代表昂季准将昨天拜访了缅甸贸易发展和民用物资供应部长吴敦，并向吴敦谈了他访华的观感。
    昂季准将是10月19日从中国回到仰光的。
    昂季准将对吴敦部长说，中华人民共和国在工业、农业生产和贸易方面的发展是极迅速的。他认为，中国和缅甸的贸易将来应该在新的和友好的基础上大大发展。
    报纸还报道，吴敦向这里的记者们说，他坚信，在彻底解决了百年来悬而未决的边界问题以后，中缅贸易将得到进一步的发展。
