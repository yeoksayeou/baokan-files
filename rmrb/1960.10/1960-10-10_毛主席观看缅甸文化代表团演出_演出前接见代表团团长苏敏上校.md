Title: Chairman Mao Watches Performance by Burmese Cultural Delegation; Receives Delegation Head Colonel So Min Before Performance
Author:
Date: 1960-10-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: Chairman Mao Zedong, along with Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, Chairman Zhu De, Vice Premier Li Fuchun, and others, watched a wonderful performance by the Burmese Cultural Delegation at Huairen Hall in Zhongnanhai tonight. Before the performance began, Chairman Mao Zedong and others received Colonel So Min, the head of the Burmese Cultural Delegation.

At tonight's event, the Burmese artists performed eleven Burmese and Chinese artistic programs. The evening began with the song of Sino-Burmese friendship and ended with the song of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty. Every program performed by the artists was warmly welcomed by more than a thousand spectators.

After the performance, children from the capital presented flowers to the Burmese artists. Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, Chairman Zhu De, and others, accompanied by Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyaw Winn, and the head of the Burmese Cultural Delegation, Colonel So Min, went on stage to meet the artists. At this moment, the Burmese artists applauded for a long time and cheered repeatedly: "Long live Chairman Mao!" (Picture attached)

Chairman Mao Zedong and others watched the performance of the Burmese Cultural Delegation and went on stage to meet the artists. Xinhua News Agency reporters Hou Bo and Du Xiuxian.

### 毛主席观看缅甸文化代表团演出  演出前接见代表团团长苏敏上校

1960-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席观看缅甸文化代表团演出
    演出前接见代表团团长苏敏上校
    新华社9日讯　毛泽东主席和董必武副主席、朱德委员长、李富春副总理等，今晚在中南海怀仁堂观看了缅甸文化代表团的精彩演出。毛泽东主席等在演出开始以前接见了缅甸文化代表团团长苏敏上校。
    在今天的晚会上，缅甸艺术家们共表演了十一个缅甸和中国的艺术节目。晚会在中缅友谊之歌声中开始，又在中缅边界条约颂歌声中结束。艺术家们表演的每一个节目都受到了一千多名观众的热烈欢迎。
    在演出结束以后，首都的少年儿童向缅甸艺术家们献了鲜花。毛泽东主席、董必武副主席、朱德委员长等，由缅甸驻中国大使叫温和缅甸文化代表团团长苏敏上校陪同，登上舞台同艺术家们会见。这时候，缅甸艺术家们长时间地鼓掌并不停地欢呼：“毛主席万岁！”（附图片）
