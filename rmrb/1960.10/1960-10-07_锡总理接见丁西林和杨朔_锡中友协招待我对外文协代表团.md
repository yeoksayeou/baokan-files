Title: Sri Lankan Prime Minister Receives Ding Xilin and Yang Shuo; Sri Lanka-China Friendship Association Hosts Delegation from Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries
Author: 
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 5th – Sri Lankan Prime Minister Mrs. Bandaranaike this morning received Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and council member Yang Shuo, who are here on a friendly visit. They were invited by the Sri Lanka-China Friendship Association to participate in the celebrations for the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, hosted by the Sri Lanka-China Friendship Association.

Present at the meeting were Zhang Canming, Chinese Ambassador to Sri Lanka, and Kumar, Secretary-General of the Sri Lanka-China Friendship Association.

Mrs. Bandaranaike had a cordial and friendly conversation with Ding Xilin and Yang Shuo.

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 5th – The Sri Lanka-China Friendship Association held a tea party tonight to entertain the delegation from the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, led by Ding Xilin.

Approximately two hundred people attended the tea party, including committee members of the Sri Lanka-China Friendship Association, writers, editors, teachers, government officials, members of parliament, and trade union representatives. Zhang Canming, the Chinese Ambassador to Sri Lanka, and diplomatic officials also attended the tea party.

Wilmot Perera, Chairman of the Sri Lanka-China Friendship Association, introduced all the members of the delegation at the tea party and presented gifts to the members of the delegation on behalf of the Sri Lanka-China Friendship Association.

Ding Xilin delivered a speech, thanking the Sri Lanka-China Friendship Association and the people of Sri Lanka for their warm hospitality. Ding Xilin and Yang Shuo arrived in Colombo on the 3rd at the invitation of the Sri Lanka-China Friendship Association.

### 锡总理接见丁西林和杨朔  锡中友协招待我对外文协代表团

1960-10-07
第6版()
专栏：

    锡总理接见丁西林和杨朔
    锡中友协招待我对外文协代表团
    新华社科伦坡5日电　锡兰总理班达拉奈克夫人今天上午接见了到这里进行友好访问的中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林和理事杨朔。他们是应锡兰中国友好协会的邀请前来参加锡中友好协会主办的中华人民共和国成立十一周年庆祝活动的。
    接见时在座的有中国驻锡兰大使张灿明和锡中友好协会秘书长库玛尔。
    班达拉奈克夫人同丁西林和杨朔进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    新华社科伦坡5日电　锡中友好协会今天晚上举行茶会，招待由丁西林率领的中国人民对外文化协会代表团。
    约二百人出席了茶会，其中有锡中友好协会的委员、作家、编辑、教师、政府官员、国会议员和工会代表。中国驻锡兰大使张灿明和外交官员们也出席了茶会。
    锡中友好协会主席威尔莫特·佩雷拉在茶会上介绍了代表团的全体成员之后，代表锡中友好协会向代表团的团员们赠送了礼物。
    丁西林发表讲话，感谢锡中友好协会和锡兰人民给予他们的热诚招待。丁西林和杨朔是应锡中友好协会的邀请于3日到达科伦坡的。
