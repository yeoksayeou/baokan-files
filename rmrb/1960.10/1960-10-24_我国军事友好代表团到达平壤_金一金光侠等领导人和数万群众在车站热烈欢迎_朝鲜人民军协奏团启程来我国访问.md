Title: Our Military Friendship Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang; Leaders such as Kim Il and Kim Kwang-hyop and Tens of Thousands of People Give a Rousing Welcome at the Station; Korean People's Army Ensemble Sets Off for a Visit to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 23rd—At the invitation of Kim Kwang-hyop, Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, General, the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation, led by He Long, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council and Vice Premier of the State Council, and Luo Ruiqing, Vice Premier of the State Council and Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General, arrived in Pyongyang by special train at 1:30 p.m. today, and was warmly welcomed by tens of thousands of people in Pyongyang and officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army.

The special train carrying the delegation entered the station amidst cheers from the crowd waving Chinese and North Korean flags and bouquets. After alighting from the train, He Long, Luo Ruiqing, and other members of the delegation warmly shook hands with Kim Il, First Vice Premier of the Korean Cabinet, Kim Kwang-hyop, Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense, General, and other party, government, and military leaders and representatives from all walks of life who came to welcome the delegation. Then, a group of Korean children in colorful costumes ran up to present flowers to He Long, Luo Ruiqing, and the delegation members.

Among those who came to the station to welcome the delegation were Vice Premiers of the Cabinet Hong Myong-hui, Chong Il-yong, Chong Chun-taek, and Ri Chu-yon, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Kim Chang-man, members of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Lim Hae and alternate member Kim Ik-son, Chairman of the Korea-China Friendship Association Ha Ang-cheon, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly Han Seol-ya, Li Ge-lu, and Kang Ryang-uk, Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly Choe Won-taek, cabinet ministers, chairmen of the Presidium of the Fatherland Front, heads of various political parties and social organizations, and senior generals such as Chief of General Staff of the Korean People's Army, Kim Chang-bong, Colonel General.

All diplomatic officials of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea, as well as Chinese experts and students in North Korea, and military attachés from various countries stationed in North Korea also came to the station to welcome the delegation.

After the national anthems of the two countries were played, He Long, accompanied by Kim Kwang-hyop, Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the Korean Cabinet, General, reviewed the guard of honor. Subsequently, a grand welcoming rally was held in the station square.

Kim Kwang-hyop delivered a welcome speech at the rally, and He Long gave a speech at the rally. The speeches of Kim Kwang-hyop and He Long were repeatedly interrupted by warm applause from the crowd.

After the welcoming rally, He Long, Luo Ruiqing, and others, accompanied by Kim Il, First Vice Premier, and Kim Kwang-hyop, General, walked to the front of the crowd and waved to the crowd, at which point tens of thousands of people in the square waved the flowers and colored flags in their hands, emitting cheers like spring thunder, instantly turning the square into an ocean of flowers and flags.

Subsequently, the delegation, accompanied by their hosts, traveled by car to their residence.

Today, the weather in Pyongyang was clear, and the national flags of China and North Korea fluttered in the sunlight. Huge portraits of Marshal Kim Il-sung and Chairman Mao Zedong were hung high on the station building, along with huge slogans reading "Long Live Marshal Kim Il-sung" and "Long Live Chairman Mao Zedong." Huge portraits of Marx and Lenin were hung high on the tall buildings in front of the station. The station and various buildings in the city were adorned with slogans such as "Long live the unbreakable friendship and unity between the peoples of Korea and China, forged in blood in the struggle against common enemies!" "Long live the Chinese Communist Party and the Government of the People's Republic of China, headed by Comrade Mao Zedong!" "Long live the militant friendship between the armies of Korea and China, forged in blood!" "Long live the unbreakable unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union!" A friendly atmosphere between Korea and China permeated the entire city.

Choe Yong-jin, Vice Minister of National Defense of Korea, Lieutenant General, Qiao Xiaoguang, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, and Zhang Leting, Military Attaché, Colonel, and others went to the border to greet the delegation.

When the delegation entered North Korean territory at 6:00 a.m. this morning, it was welcomed by more than 10,000 people in Sinuiju.

The 321-member Cultural Troupe of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, led by the delegation, also arrived in Pyongyang today.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 23rd—The Korean People's Army Ensemble, led by Major General Jang Se-hwan, departed from Pyongyang by special train today for a friendly performance tour in China at the invitation of the Chinese government.

Among those who saw the ensemble off at the station were: General Heo Bong-hak, Director of the General Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army, and other generals of the Korean People's Army, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of Korea Ryu Jang-shik, Vice Minister of Education and Culture Jo Yeong-chul, and well-known Korean artists and actors. Liu Xianglun, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea, Li Lin, Cultural Counselor, and diplomatic officials of the embassy also saw the ensemble off at the station.

Before boarding the train, the ensemble received flowers from the artists of Pyongyang and the staff of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea. (Photo attached)

The crowd gathered at Pyongyang Station Square to welcome the Chinese military friendship delegation visiting North Korea.

### 我国军事友好代表团到达平壤  金一金光侠等领导人和数万群众在车站热烈欢迎  朝鲜人民军协奏团启程来我国访问

1960-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我国军事友好代表团到达平壤
    金一金光侠等领导人和数万群众在车站热烈欢迎
    朝鲜人民军协奏团启程来我国访问
    新华社平壤23日电　应朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相兼民族保卫相金光侠大将的邀请，由中国国防委员会副主席、国务院副总理贺龙元帅和国务院副总理、中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿大将率领的中国军事友好代表团，今天下午一时半乘专车抵达平壤，受到平壤市数万人和朝鲜人民军官兵的热烈欢迎。
    代表团乘坐的专车在高举着中朝两国国旗和花束的群众的欢呼声中驶进车站。贺龙元帅和罗瑞卿大将等下车后同前来欢迎代表团的朝鲜内阁第一副首相金一、副首相兼民族保卫相金光侠大将等党、政、军领导人和各界代表一一热烈握手。接着，一群穿着彩色服装的朝鲜儿童跑上前去向贺龙元帅、罗瑞卿大将和代表团人员献花。
    到车站欢迎代表团的还有内阁副首相洪命熹、郑一龙、郑准泽、李周渊，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长金昌满，劳动党中央常务委员会委员林海、候补委员金翊善，朝中友好协会委员长河仰天，最高人民会议常任委员会副委员长韩雪野、李克鲁、康良煜，最高人民会议议长崔元泽，内阁各相，祖国战线议长团的议长们，各政党、社会团体的负责人，朝鲜人民军总参谋长金昌奉上将等高级将领们。
    中国驻朝鲜大使馆全体外交官以及在朝鲜的中国专家和留学生和各国驻朝鲜武官也到车站欢迎。
    在高奏两国国歌后，贺龙元帅在朝鲜内阁副首相兼民族保卫相金光侠大将陪同下检阅了仪仗队。接着，在车站广场举行了盛大的欢迎大会。
    金光侠大将在大会上致欢迎词，贺龙元帅在大会上讲了话。金光侠大将和贺龙元帅的讲话不断为群众的热烈掌声所打断。
    欢迎大会结束后，贺龙元帅和罗瑞卿大将等由金一第一副首相和金光侠大将等陪同，走到群众队伍的前面，向群众挥手致意，这时广场上数万群众一齐摇动手中的鲜花和彩旗，发出春雷般的欢呼声，使广场顿时变成一个鲜花和旗帜的海洋。
    随后，代表团由主人陪同乘汽车前往寓所。
    今天，平壤天气晴朗，朝中两国国旗在阳光下迎风飘扬。车站大楼上高悬着金日成元帅、毛泽东主席的巨幅画像，高悬着“金日成元帅万岁”“毛泽东主席万岁”的巨大标语。车站前面的高楼大厦上高悬着马克思和列宁的巨幅画像。车站上和市内各建筑物上高悬着“在反对共同敌人的斗争中以鲜血凝成的朝中两国人民间牢不可破的友谊团结万岁！”“以毛泽东同志为首的中国共产党和中华人民共和国政府万岁！”“以鲜血凝成的朝中两国军队间的战斗友谊万岁！”“以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的牢不可破的团结万岁！”等标语。朝中友好气氛洋溢全城。
    朝鲜民族保卫省副相崔勇进上将、中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光和武官张乐亭上校等曾前往国境迎接代表团。
    代表团今晨六时进入朝鲜国境时，受到了新义州万余人民的欢迎。
    由代表团率领的中国人民解放军文艺工作团三百二十一人今天同时到达平壤。
    新华社平壤23日电　应邀前往中国作友好访问演出的朝鲜人民军协奏团，由张瑞焕少将率领今天从平壤乘专车前往中国。
    到车站送行的有：朝鲜人民军总政治局长许凤学上将等朝鲜人民军将领，朝鲜外务省副相柳章植，教育文化省副相赵灵出以及朝鲜著名的艺术家、演员们。我国驻朝鲜大使馆参赞刘祥纶、文化参赞李林和使馆外交官员们也到车站送行。
    协奏团在登车前接受了平壤市艺术家们和中国驻朝鲜大使馆工作人员们的献花。（附图片）
    聚集在平壤车站广场上欢迎中国访朝军事友好代表团的群众。
