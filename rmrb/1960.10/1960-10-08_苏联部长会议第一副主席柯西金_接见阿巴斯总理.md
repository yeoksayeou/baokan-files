Title: First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Kosygin, Receives Prime Minister Abbas
Author:
Date: 1960-10-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th October: According to TASS, Moscow, 7th October: The First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Kosygin, received the Prime Minister of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, Abbas Ferhat, in the Kremlin in Moscow today. Prime Minister Abbas was in transit through Moscow on his way back from the People's Republic of China.
    During the meeting, they held talks, in which Prime Minister Abbas's entourage and the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Semyonov, were present.

### 苏联部长会议第一副主席柯西金  接见阿巴斯总理

1960-10-08
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联部长会议第一副主席柯西金
    接见阿巴斯总理
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科7日讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席柯西金今天在莫斯科克里姆林宫接见了阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理阿巴斯·费尔哈特，阿巴斯总理是从中华人民共和国返国路过莫斯科的。
    接见时，他们进行了交谈，在座的有阿巴斯总理的随行人员以及苏联外交部副部长谢苗诺夫。
