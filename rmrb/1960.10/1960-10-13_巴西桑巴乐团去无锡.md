Title: Brazilian Samba Troupe to Wuxi
Author:
Date: 1960-10-13
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 11th—After their performance tour in Jinan, the Brazilian Samba Troupe left Jinan by train this evening for Wuxi.
    The Samba Troupe performed three shows in Jinan and were warmly welcomed by the Jinan audience. During their stay in Jinan, the guests also visited the Jinan No. 2 Machine Tool Factory and the Hongqi People's Commune, toured Daming Lake and Baotu Spring, and held a discussion with literary and art circles in Jinan.

### 巴西桑巴乐团去无锡

1960-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    巴西桑巴乐团去无锡
    新华社济南11日电　巴西桑巴乐团在济南访问演出后，今晚乘火车离开济南去无锡。
    桑巴乐团在济南演出了三场，受到济南观众的热烈欢迎。客人们在济南期间，还参观了济南第二机床厂和红旗人民公社，游览了大明湖、趵突泉，还和济南的文艺界举行了座谈。
