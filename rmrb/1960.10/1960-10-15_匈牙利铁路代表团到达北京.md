Title: Hungarian Railway Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, October 14th: The Hungarian railway delegation, invited by the Ministry of Railways to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation is led by Dr. Csanadi Gyorgy, First Deputy Minister of Transport and Post of Hungary.

Welcoming them at the airport were Liu Jianzhang, Vice Minister of Railways, and Xu Jianji, Deputy Director of the International Joint Transportation Bureau, among others.

Mr. Bainok, Counselor of the Hungarian Embassy in China, also welcomed them at the airport. Two other members of the delegation—Mr. Reti Karoly, Deputy Director of the Railway General Administration of the Hungarian Ministry of Transport and Post, and Mr. Csermák Henrik, Director of the Budapest Railway Administration—had arrived in Beijing earlier.

### 匈牙利铁路代表团到达北京

1960-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利铁路代表团到达北京
    新华社14日讯　应铁道部邀请前来我国访问的匈牙利铁路代表团，在匈牙利交通邮电部第一副部长恰纳吉·吉叶尔基博士率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有铁道部副部长刘建章、国际联运局副局长徐建基等。
    匈牙利驻中国大使馆参赞白诺克也到机场欢迎。代表团另外两名团员——匈牙利交通邮电部铁路总局副局长莱得尼·卡洛里和布达佩斯铁路管理局局长常满克·汉立克，已先期到北京。
