Title: Soviet Russian Federation Men's and Women's Swimming and Diving Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-06
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, October 5th. A Soviet Russian Federation men's and women's swimming and diving team of thirty-five members, led by Gubanov, Chairman of the Russian Federation Swimming Association, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 4th. They will hold friendly matches with the Chinese men's and women's swimming teams at the Beijing Gymnasium swimming pool on the evenings of the 6th and 7th.
    The guest team includes twelve male swimmers and eleven female swimmers, three male divers and four female divers. They are all swimming athletes from the Russian Federation. Among them, female athlete Yegorova, who specializes in backstroke and butterfly, visited our country with the Soviet swimming team in 1958.
    The guest team will also visit and compete in Shanghai.

### 苏联俄罗斯联邦男女游泳跳水队到京

1960-10-06
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联俄罗斯联邦男女游泳跳水队到京
    新华社5日讯　苏联俄罗斯联邦男女游泳跳水队一行三十五人由俄罗斯联邦游泳协会主席古巴诺夫率领乘飞机于4日到达北京。他们将在6日和7日晚在北京体育馆游泳馆同中国男女游泳队举行友谊比赛。
    客队中包括十二名男子游泳运动员和十一名女子游泳运动员，三名男子跳水运动员和四名女子跳水运动员。他们都是俄罗斯联邦的游泳选手。其中擅长仰泳和蝶泳的女运动员叶戈罗娃在1958年曾经随苏联游泳队来我国访问。
    客队还将去上海进行访问比赛。
