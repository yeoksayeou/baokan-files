Title: New Zealand Woman Pianist Leaves Beijing for Australia
Author:
Date: 1960-10-09
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, 7th. New Zealand woman pianist Fanetta Manstein, who visited our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, concluded her visit to China and left Beijing for Australia on the 7th.

### 新西兰女钢琴家离京去澳大利亚

1960-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    新西兰女钢琴家离京去澳大利亚
  新华社7日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的新西兰女钢琴家凡奈塔·曼斯蒂女士结束了在我国的访问，在7日离开北京前往澳大利亚。
