Title: Two Burmese Friendship Delegations Arrive in Kunming to Attend Kunming's National Day Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1960-10-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, September 30th: A twenty-member friendship delegation from the Shan and Kachin States of the Union of Burma, led by Sao Phye, Chairman of the Shan State Council, and U San Nu, Chairman of the Kachin State Council, arrived in Kunming by plane from Rangoon at noon today, and were warmly welcomed by more than two thousand people of all ethnic groups in Kunming.
    The friendship delegation from the Shan and Kachin States of Burma is visiting Kunming at the invitation of Yu Yichuan, Governor of Yunnan Province. During their visit to Kunming, they will participate in Kunming's celebrations of the 11th anniversary of the founding of our country and the celebrations of the signing of the Sino-Burmese Border Treaty in Beijing.
    Yu Yichuan, Governor of Yunnan Province, and Deputy Governors Zhang Chong and Wu Zuomin, as well as Major General Li Xifu, Commander of the Kunming Garrison, welcomed the guests at the airport today. Also present at the airport to welcome the guests were Pan Shuoduan, Mayor of Kunming, Zhang Zizhai, Secretary-General of the Provincial People's Committee, and more than 400 people from all walks of life.
    U Maung Maung Soe, Consul General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, and Wu Xin, Deputy Consul of the Consulate of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming, also welcomed the guests at the airport today.

### 应邀参加昆明市庆祝国庆活动  缅甸两个友好代表团到昆明

1960-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    应邀参加昆明市庆祝国庆活动
    缅甸两个友好代表团到昆明
    据新华社昆明30日电　缅甸联邦掸邦和克钦邦友好代表团一行二十人，在掸邦议会主席苏辟、克钦邦议会主席吴山纽率领下，今天中午从仰光乘飞机到达昆明，受到昆明市各族人民二千多人的热情欢迎。
    缅甸掸邦和克钦邦友好代表团是应云南省省长于一川的邀请来昆明作友好访问的。他们在昆明访问期间，将参加昆明市庆祝我国建国十一周年的活动和庆祝即将在北京签订中缅边界条约的活动。
    云南省省长于一川，副省长张冲、吴作民和驻昆明部队首长黎锡福少将今天到机场欢迎客人。到机场欢迎的还有昆明市市长潘朔端，省人民委员会秘书长张子斋，以及各界群众四百多人。
    今天，缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭和越南民主共和国驻昆明领事馆副领事吴新也到机场欢迎客人。
