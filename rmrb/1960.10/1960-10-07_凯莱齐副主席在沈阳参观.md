Title: Vice-Chairman Kellezi Visits Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 6th October: The Albanian China Friendship Association delegation, headed by Abdyl Këllezi, member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Vice Chairman of the Council Of Ministers and President of Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association, accompanied by Jiang Nanxiang, alternate member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and President of the Chinese-Albanian Friendship Association, Liu Mingfu, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission, Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and East European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Panxin, Vice Mayor of Shenyang, visited the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Factory and the Shenyang Cable Factory, visited workers' families and kindergartens in the workers' village, and toured the famous North Tomb.

Vice-Chairman Kellezi and other distinguished guests arrived at the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Factory at 9:00 a.m. Bi Kezhi, Deputy Secretary of the Factory Party Committee, Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Factory, and the workers warmly welcomed the distinguished guests at the factory gate. Bi Kezhi gave the distinguished guests a detailed introduction to the factory's development history and production situation, and then led the distinguished guests to visit the workshop. During the visit, Vice-Chairman Kellezi constantly inquired about the workers' production achievements. Every time he walked in front of the hero list, he asked the translator to introduce the contents of the list. In the first workshop, Vice-Chairman Kellezi met Zhang Xijiu, a famous labor model in Shenyang and a worker-turned-assistant engineer. He introduced Zhang Xijiu to Eftimi Lito, a member of the delegation and a Socialist Labor Hero. The two labor heroes embraced warmly for a long time, congratulating each other on making greater achievements for the socialist construction of their respective countries. When leaving the factory, Vice-Chairman Kellezi congratulated the workers on their new achievements, and on behalf of the Albanian Labor Party, the People's Republic of Albania, and the Albanian people, expressed greetings to the Chinese workers.

In the afternoon, the distinguished guests visited the Shenyang Cable Factory. When Vice-Chairman Kellezi saw the blackboard newspaper in the workshop that read "Warmly Welcome the Delegation of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association," he wrote in chalk, "The delegation of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association extends revolutionary salutes to all employees!" and "Long live the three red flags of the General Line, the Great Leap Forward, and the People's Commune!"

After visiting the No. 1 Machine Tool Factory and the Cable Factory, the distinguished guests presented gifts to the factories. The two factories also presented the distinguished guests with photo albums with various photos of the factories.

Albanian Ambassador to China, Pllumbi, also accompanied the delegation on the visit. Misto Treska, Chairman of the Albanian Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations, and Shefkat Musaraj, Secretary-General of the Albanian Writers and Artists Association, visited the Liaoning Museum and the Shenyang Imperial Palace Museum, and toured the Beiling Park.

In the evening, all the distinguished Albanian guests, accompanied by Jiao Ruoyu, First Secretary of the CPC Shenyang Municipal Committee, and others, watched a song and dance acrobatics performance.

### 凯莱齐副主席在沈阳参观

1960-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    凯莱齐副主席在沈阳参观
    新华社沈阳6日电　以阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、部长会议副主席、阿中友好协会主席阿卜杜勒·凯莱齐为首的阿尔巴尼亚中国友好协会代表团全体贵宾，由中共中央候补委员、中阿友好协会会长蒋南翔，国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫，外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清和沈阳市副市长张盘新等人陪同，参观了沈阳第一机床厂、沈阳电缆厂，访问了工人村的工人家庭和幼儿园，游览了名胜北陵。
    凯莱齐副主席等贵宾在上午九点到达沈阳第一机床厂。工厂党委副书记毕克志、副厂长刘敬余和职工们在工厂门口热烈欢迎贵宾。毕克志向贵宾们详细介绍了工厂的发展历史和生产情况，然后引导贵宾参观了车间。凯莱齐副主席在参观中不断地询问工人的生产成绩。每走到英雄榜前，他都请翻译介绍榜上的内容。在第一车间，凯莱齐副主席遇到了沈阳著名劳动模范、工人出身的助理工程师张锡久，他把张锡久介绍给代表团团员、社会主义劳动英雄埃弗提米·利托见面。两位劳动英雄长时间地热烈拥抱，互相祝贺为自己祖国的社会主义建设事业作出更大的成就。离开工厂时，凯莱齐副主席祝贺职工们取得更新的成就，他并代表阿尔巴尼亚劳动党、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国和阿尔巴尼亚人民向中国工人表示问候。
    下午，贵宾们参观了沈阳电缆厂。凯莱齐副主席看到车间写着“热烈欢迎阿中友好协会代表团”的黑板报，当时就用粉笔写上“阿中友协代表团向全体职工致革命敬礼！”、“总路线、大跃进、人民公社三面红旗万岁！”
    贵宾们在第一机床厂和电缆厂参观后，分别向工厂赠送了礼品。这两个工厂也把贴有本厂各种照片的相册，送给了贵宾。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使帕里夫蒂也随代表团参观。阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会主席米斯托·特雷斯卡和阿尔巴尼亚作家艺术家协会秘书长谢夫卡特·穆萨莱到辽宁博物馆和沈阳故宫博物馆参观，并到北陵公园游览。
    晚间，阿尔巴尼亚全体贵宾由中共沈阳市委第一书记焦若愚等人陪同观看了歌舞杂技表演。
