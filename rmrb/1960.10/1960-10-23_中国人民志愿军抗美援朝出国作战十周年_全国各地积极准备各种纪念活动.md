Title: Tenth Anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea: Various Memorial Activities Being Actively Prepared Throughout the Country
Author:
Date: 1960-10-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 22nd: On the eve of "October 25th," people and troops across the country are actively preparing various commemorative activities to warmly celebrate the tenth anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea.

In Beijing, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and the Sino-Korean Friendship Association are preparing a grand gathering of people from all walks of life in the capital to commemorate the tenth anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea. The Ministry of Culture and the Sino-Korean Friendship Association are actively preparing to hold a film week, during which films about the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea and Korean films will be shown in major cinemas in the capital. The Sino-Korean Friendship Association and the China Photographic Society will hold a photo exhibition on the tenth anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army's participation in the war. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will hold commemorative radio programs and music programs for "October 25th." Central-level newspapers will publish editorials or commemorative articles. The "Liberation Army Daily" has launched a special column from the 21st, publishing stories of Sino-Korean friendship. Many factories, schools, communes, and agencies in the capital are preparing to hold report meetings, symposiums, and commemorative evening parties. Hu Xiudao, Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, former first-class hero of the Volunteer Army, special-class meritorious person, Guo Enzhi, former first-class hero of the Volunteer Army, special-class meritorious person, and representative of the National People's Congress, and Li Baocheng, the former commander of the "Steel Eighth Company" of the Volunteer Army, who led his troops in defending the tunnels in the Battle of Shangganling, and other heroic model figures have also come to Beijing to report on their heroic deeds in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea to workers, students, government cadres, and officers and soldiers of the troops.

Shanghai, Tianjin, Guangzhou, Wuhan, Xi'an, Chengdu, Shenyang, Changchun, Andong, Yanji, Tumen and other places are also actively preparing various commemorative activities such as commemorative meetings, film weeks, report meetings and symposiums these days. Shanghai will hold a grand commemorative meeting on the 25th, and relevant films about the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea and Korean films will be shown simultaneously in twenty cinemas. Many factories and mines, schools and other cities such as Shenyang and Changchun will also invite former Volunteer Army commanders and fighters to give reports, introducing the heroic deeds of the Volunteer Army in bravely and tenaciously fighting hard. The Memorial Hall of the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, which is being prepared in Dong'an City, will be opened on the eve of "October 25th." The cemeteries of Volunteer Army martyrs such as Huang Jiguang and Qiu Shaoyun in Shenyang have also been cleaned and renovated, and people from all walks of life will go to sweep and worship.

In the various units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, various commemorative activities have also been actively prepared recently. The troops in the Guangzhou area will conduct a propaganda and education campaign to fight against the U.S. imperialist policy of aggression and war through commemorative meetings, report meetings, etc. Troops in Beijing, Nanjing, Shenyang, Wuhan, Chengdu and other regions are preparing to hold grand commemorative gatherings, and will organize former Volunteer Army combat heroes and meritorious persons to report to officers and soldiers. A certain unit stationed in Jilin Province recently collected a batch of souvenirs brought back from Korea by former Volunteer Army commanders and fighters, and is preparing to hold an exhibition to conduct propaganda and education on the friendship between the Chinese and Korean people.

### 中国人民志愿军抗美援朝出国作战十周年  全国各地积极准备各种纪念活动

1960-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    中国人民志愿军抗美援朝出国作战十周年
    全国各地积极准备各种纪念活动
    新华社22日讯　在“十·二五”前夕，全国各地人民和部队积极准备各种纪念活动，热烈庆祝中国人民志愿军抗美援朝出国作战十周年。
    在北京，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国朝鲜友好协会，正在筹备首都各界人民纪念中国人民志愿军抗美援朝出国作战十周年的盛大集会。文化部、中朝友好协会正积极筹备举行电影周，将在首都各大电影院放映有关抗美援朝和朝鲜影片。中朝友好协会和中国摄影学会将举办中国人民志愿军出国作战十周年图片展览。中央人民广播电台将举办纪念“十·二五”广播节目和音乐节目。中央级各报将发表社论或纪念文章。“解放军报”从21日起辟出专栏，刊登中朝友谊故事。首都的许多工厂、学校、公社、机关等都准备举行报告会、座谈会和纪念晚会。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国英雄、前志愿军一级英雄、特等功臣胡修道，前志愿军一级英雄、特等功臣、全国人民代表大会代表郭恩志，在上甘岭战役中率领部队坚守坑道作战的前志愿军“钢铁八连”连长李保成等英雄模范人物，也赶来北京，将向工人、学生、机关干部和部队官兵等报告他们在抗美援朝战争中的英雄事迹。
    上海、天津、广州、武汉、西安、成都、沈阳、长春、安东、延吉、图们等地，这几天也在积极筹备纪念会、电影周、报告会和座谈会等各种纪念活动。上海市25日将举行盛大的纪念集会，并在二十家电影院同时放映有关抗美援朝和朝鲜影片。沈阳、长春等市的许多厂矿、学校还将邀请前志愿军指战员做报告，介绍志愿军英勇顽强、艰苦奋斗的英雄事迹。东安市正在筹备的抗美援朝纪念馆，将在“十·二五”前夕开放。在沈阳的黄继光、丘少云等志愿军烈士陵园，也进行了清扫和修整，各界人民将前往扫祭。
    在中国人民解放军各部队里，最近也在积极筹备各种纪念活动。广州地区部队将通过纪念会、报告会等，进行一次为反对美帝国主义侵略政策和战争政策而斗争的宣传教育活动。北京、南京、沈阳、武汉、成都等地区部队都准备举行盛大的纪念集会，并将组织前志愿军战斗英雄、功臣，向部队官兵做报告。驻吉林省某部最近搜集了一批前志愿军指战员从朝鲜带回来的纪念品，准备举行展览会，进行有关中朝人民友谊的宣传教育。
