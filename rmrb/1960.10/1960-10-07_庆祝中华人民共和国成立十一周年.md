Title: Celebrating the 11th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 6th: Premier Zhou Enlai received a telegram of congratulations on the National Day of our country from Ruben Julio Castro, Speaker of the Bolivian Senate. The full text is as follows: To His Excellency Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Bolivian Senate pays tribute to the glorious Chinese people through you, and extends warm and sincere wishes for the prosperity and development of the Chinese people.

          Ruben Julio Castro, Speaker of the Senate
                        La Paz, October 5, 1960

    Indo-China Friendship Association Holds Reception
    Xinhua News Agency, 5th: New Delhi dispatch: The National Executive Committee of the Indo-China Friendship Association held a reception on the afternoon of the 3rd to celebrate the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

    Those present at the reception included: Sundar Lal, Chairman of the Indo-China Friendship Association, Gyan Chand and Tara Chand, Vice-Chairmen of the National Executive Committee of the Indo-China Friendship Association, Prakash and Nandi, Secretaries-General, as well as members of the National Executive Committee and heads of the Delhi branch of the Indo-China Friendship Association.

    Also present at the reception were: Mrs. Rameshwari Nehru, Chairman of the Indian Asian-African Solidarity Committee, Member of Parliament Pannikar, former Mayor of Delhi, Mrs. Asaf Ali, other prominent figures from society, and diplomats from various socialist countries and friendly countries stationed in Delhi.

    Pan Zili, Chinese Ambassador to India, and some diplomats from the embassy were invited to attend the reception.

    More than 100 people attended the reception. The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

    Our Ambassador to Nepal
    Holds Film Reception
    Xinhua News Agency, 6th: Kathmandu dispatch: Zhang Shijie, Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, held a film reception in Kathmandu on the evening of the 5th to celebrate the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

    Among those present were ministers of various ministries of the Kingdom of Nepal, the Speaker of the Lower House, Marshals, other senior government officials, prominent figures from all walks of life, and diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Nepal, totaling approximately 700 people.

    The documentary about the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the feature film "Five Golden Flowers" were screened at the reception.

### 庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年

1960-10-07
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年
    玻利维亚参议院议长的贺电
    新华社6日讯　周恩来总理收到了玻利维亚参议院议长鲁文·胡利奥·卡斯特罗发来的祝贺我国国庆的电报，全文如下：中华人民共和国总理，周恩来先生阁下：
    值此中华人民共和国建国十一周年之际，玻利维亚参议院通过你向光荣的中国人民致敬，并对中国人民的繁荣和发展致以热烈的、诚挚的祝愿。
          参议院议长鲁文·胡利奥·卡斯特罗
                        1960年10月5日于拉巴斯
    印中友好协会举行招待会
    新华社5日讯　新德里消息：印中友好协会全国执行委员会3日下午举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年。
    出席招待会的有：印中友好协会主席森德拉尔，印中友好协会全国执行委员会副主席吉安·昌德和塔拉·昌德，秘书长普腊卡什和南迪，以及全国执行委员会的委员们和印中友好协会德里分会的负责人。
    出席招待会的还有：印度亚非团结委员会主席拉米希瓦里·尼赫鲁夫人，国会议员潘尼迦，前德里市长阿萨夫·阿里夫人和其他社会著名人士，以及各社会主义国家和友好国家驻德里的外交官。
    中国驻印度大使潘自力和使馆一些外交官应邀出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的共百余人。招待会在热烈友好气氛中进行。
    我驻尼泊尔大使
    举行电影招待会
    新华社6日讯　加德满都消息：中国驻尼泊尔大使张世杰5日晚间在加德满都举行电影招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年。
    出席的有尼泊尔王国政府各部大臣、下议院议长、元帅们、政府其他高级官员、各界著名人士和各国驻尼泊尔的外交使节约七百人。
    招待会上放映了关于中华人民共和国成立十周年的纪录片和故事片“五朵金花”。
