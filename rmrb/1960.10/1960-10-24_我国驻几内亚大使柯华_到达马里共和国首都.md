Title: Our Ambassador to Guinea, Ke Hua, Arrives in the Capital of the Republic of Mali
Author:
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bamako, October 22nd: Ke Hua, the Chinese Ambassador to Guinea, arrived in Bamako, the capital of the Republic of Mali, by plane from Conakry tonight.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included Mega Yakouba, Vice-President of the Malian National Assembly, Maira Attaher, Minister of Finance, Gounoualo Coulibaly, Acting Mayor of Bamako, Keita Lamine, Secretary-General of the National Assembly, members of the National Assembly, heads of youth organizations, Bakari, Secretary-General of the Niger Sawaba Party, and diplomatic envoys from various socialist countries stationed in Mali.

### 我国驻几内亚大使柯华  到达马里共和国首都

1960-10-24
第5版()
专栏：

    我国驻几内亚大使柯华
    到达马里共和国首都
    新华社巴马科22日电　中国驻几内亚大使柯华今晚乘飞机从科纳克里到达马里共和国首都巴马科。
    到机场欢迎的有马里国民议会副议长梅加·亚库巴、财政部长迈拉·阿塔哈、巴马科代理市长古努阿洛·库利巴利、国民议会秘书长凯塔·拉明、国民议会议员们、青年团体负责人，以及尼日尔萨瓦巴党总书记巴卡里和各社会主义国家驻马里的外交使节等。
