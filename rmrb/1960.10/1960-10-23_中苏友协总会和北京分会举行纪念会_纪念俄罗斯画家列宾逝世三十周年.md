Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and Beijing Branch Hold Commemorative Meeting to Mark the 30th Anniversary of the Death of Russian Painter Repin
Author:
Date: 1960-10-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a commemorative meeting at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Hall tonight to commemorate the 30th anniversary of the death of the great Russian painter Ilya Yefimovich Repin.
    Bian Cheng, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, spoke at the meeting. He said that Repin was a great Russian realist painter. His works profoundly depicted the social life of Russia at that time, the oppression and exploitation suffered by the people, and their hatred for the oppressors. Many of his important works are well-known and loved by the Chinese people.
    Reproductions of Repin's works were exhibited at the meeting. After the meeting, films related to the painter, "Repin's Paintings" and "Russian Painting Gallery," were screened.
    Those attending the commemorative evening included Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, Wu Zuoren and Liu Kaiqu, Vice-Chairmen of the Chinese Artists Association, Zhang Wuzhen, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Artists Association, Gao Zhendong, Secretary-General of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and more than 300 art enthusiasts from the capital.
    Romanin, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, and others were also invited to attend the commemorative meeting.

### 中苏友协总会和北京分会举行纪念会  纪念俄罗斯画家列宾逝世三十周年

1960-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    中苏友协总会和北京分会举行纪念会
    纪念俄罗斯画家列宾逝世三十周年
    新华社22日讯　为纪念俄罗斯伟大画家伊·叶·列宾逝世三十周年，中苏友好协会总会和北京市中苏友好协会，今晚在中苏友好馆举行纪念会。
    中苏友好协会总会副秘书长卞诚在会上讲了话。他说，列宾是俄罗斯伟大的现实主义画家，在他的作品中深刻地描绘了当时俄国的社会生活、人民所受的压迫和剥削以及对压迫者的憎恨，他的许多重要作品都是中国人民所熟知和喜爱的。
    会上展出了列宾作品的复制品。会后放映了与画家有关的电影：“列宾的画”和“俄罗斯绘画馆”。
    出席纪念晚会的，有中苏友好协会总会副秘书长李霑吾，中国美术家协会副主席吴作人、刘开渠，中国美术家协会副秘书长张悟真，北京市中苏友好协会秘书长高振东，和首都美术爱好者等三百多人。
    苏联驻中国大使馆参赞罗满宁等也应邀出席了纪念会。
