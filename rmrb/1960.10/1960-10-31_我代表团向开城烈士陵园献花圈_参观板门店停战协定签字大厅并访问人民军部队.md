Title: Our Delegation Lays Wreaths at the Kaesong Martyrs' Cemetery, Visits the Panmunjom Armistice Agreement Signing Hall, and Visits the Korean People's Army Troops
Author:
Date: 1960-10-31
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, October 30th: The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to Korea, led by Deputy Head of Delegation General Luo Ruiqing and accompanied by Vice Minister of National Defense of Korea, General Choi Yong-jin, laid wreaths this morning at the Korean People's Army Martyrs' Cemetery and the Chinese People's Volunteers Martyrs' Cemetery in Kaesong.

Also present to lay wreaths with the delegation were: Oh Je-ryong, Chairman of the Kaesong Regional Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Lieutenant General Jeon Moon-seop, Commander of the Korean People's Army Garrison in the Kaesong area; Major General Lee Jang-soo, Deputy Director of the General Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army; Major General Joo Chang-joon, Senior Member of the Korean-Chinese Side of the Korean Military Armistice Commission, Major General Ren Rong, Member of the Chinese People's Volunteers; Senior Colonel Li Jeong-beom, Member of the Korean People's Army; and other Party and government leaders from the Kaesong area. Jeong Jin-seop, President of Songdo Political and Economic University, and Volunteer Army Mother Liu Mei also laid wreaths at these two martyrs' cemeteries with the delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, October 30th: The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to Korea, led by Deputy Head of Delegation General Luo Ruiqing, visited the Korean Armistice Agreement Signing Hall in Panmunjom today, accompanied by Major General Joo Chang-joon, Senior Member of the Korean-Chinese Side of the Korean Military Armistice Commission.

This afternoon, the delegation visited a unit of the Korean People's Army, accompanied by Vice Minister of National Defense of Korea, General Choi Yong-jin, and Lieutenant General Jeon Moon-seop, Commander of the Korean People's Army Garrison in the Kaesong area.

### 我代表团向开城烈士陵园献花圈  参观板门店停战协定签字大厅并访问人民军部队

1960-10-31
第6版()
专栏：

    我代表团向开城烈士陵园献花圈
    参观板门店停战协定签字大厅并访问人民军部队
    新华社开城30日电　中国访朝军事友好代表团在副团长罗瑞卿大将率领下，由朝鲜民族保卫省副相崔勇进上将陪同，今天上午在开城向朝鲜人民军烈士陵园和中国人民志愿军烈士陵园敬献了花圈。
    同代表团一道去献花圈的还有：朝鲜劳动党开城地区委员会委员长吴济龙，朝鲜人民军开城地区驻军首长全文燮中将，朝鲜人民军总政治局副局长李长秀少将，朝鲜军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员、朝鲜人民军朱昌骏少将，委员、中国人民志愿军任荣少将，委员、朝鲜人民军李贞范大校，以及开城地区其他党政领导人。松都政治经济大学校长郑镇燮、志愿军妈妈柳梅等也同代表团一起向这两处烈士陵园献了花圈。
    据新华社开城30日电　中国访朝军事友好代表团由副团长罗瑞卿大将率领，今天在朝鲜军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员朱昌骏少将陪同下，到板门店参观了朝鲜停战协定签字大厅。
    今天下午，代表团在朝鲜民族保卫省副相崔勇进上将和开城地区朝鲜人民军驻军首长全文燮中将陪同下，访问了一支朝鲜人民军部队。
