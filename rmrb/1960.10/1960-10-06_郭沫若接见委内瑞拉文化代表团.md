Title: Guo Moruo Receives Venezuelan Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 5th: Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received the head of the Venezuelan Cultural Delegation, Hector Marcano Guevara, and the members of the delegation in Beijing this afternoon, and had a cordial conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Zhou Erfu, Vice President of the Chinese Association for Friendship with Latin America, and Chen Gongqi, Executive Director, among others.

### 郭沫若接见委内瑞拉文化代表团

1960-10-06
第3版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见委内瑞拉文化代表团
    新华社5日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若，今天下午接见了委内瑞拉文化代表团团长艾克多尔·马尔加诺·古埃约和在京团员，并进行了亲切交谈。
    接见时在座的有中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复和常务理事陈公琪等。
