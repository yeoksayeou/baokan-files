Title: Brigadier General Aung Gyi Leaves Changsha for Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-10-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, 14th - Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the chief representative of the Burmese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee and Deputy Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces of the Union of Burma, along with representatives and advisors of the Burmese delegation, left Changsha by special train tonight for Guangzhou.

　　Shang Zijin, Deputy Governor of Hunan Province, Wu Zili, head of the Hunan Garrison, Yang Rupeng, Deputy Mayor of Changsha, and others were at the station to see them off.

　　The distinguished guests arrived in Changsha by special train on the afternoon of the 13th from Hangzhou. After arriving in Changsha, they carried out visits, and also visited Chairman Mao's former residence in Shaoshan. Yesterday evening, the Hunan Provincial People's Committee and the Hunan Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held a banquet to entertain the distinguished guests.

### 昂季准将离长沙去广州

1960-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

    昂季准将离长沙去广州
    新华社长沙14日电　中缅边界联合委员会缅甸首席代表、缅甸联邦国防军副总参谋长昂季准将和缅甸代表团的代表、顾问，今晚乘专车离开长沙前往广州。
    湖南省副省长尚子锦、湖南驻军首长吴自立、长沙市副市长杨如彭等人到车站欢送。
    贵宾们是在13日下午由杭州乘专车到达长沙的。他们到长沙后，进行了参观访问，并到韶山访问了毛主席故居。昨天晚上，湖南省人民委员会和中国人民解放军湖南军区曾设宴招待贵宾。
