Title: President of the Argentine International Trade Promotion Council Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-01
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 30th - Mr. Freire, President of the Argentine International Trade Promotion Council, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane this evening. They are visiting China at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade. Among those who welcomed them at the airport were Ji Chao-ding and Chen Ming, Executive Director of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association.

### 阿根廷国际贸易促进会主席到京

1960-10-01
第5版()
专栏：

    阿根廷国际贸易促进会主席到京
    新华社30日讯　阿根廷国际贸易促进会主席弗雷伊雷和夫人今天晚间乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请来中国访问的。到机场欢迎的有冀朝鼎、中国拉丁美洲友好协会常务理事陈明等人。
