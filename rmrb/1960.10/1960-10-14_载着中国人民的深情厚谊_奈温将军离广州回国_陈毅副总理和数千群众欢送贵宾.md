Title: Carrying the Profound Affection of the Chinese People: General Ne Win Departs Guangzhou for Home; Vice Premier Chen Yi and Thousands of People See Off Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-10-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 13th: General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces of the Union of Burma, and his wife, carrying the profound affection of the Chinese people, left Guangzhou this morning via Shenzhen to return home. Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, Governor of Guangdong Province Chen Yu and his wife, and thousands of people from all walks of life in Guangzhou went to the station to see off the distinguished guests.

At the station, Governor Chen Yu delivered a speech, thanking General Ne Win and other distinguished Burmese guests for their friendly visit to Guangzhou and thanking them for bringing the deep "Paukphaw" (fraternal) friendship. He said that the Premiers of China and Burma formally signed the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty, successfully and comprehensively resolving the border issue between the two countries, writing a glorious and splendid chapter in the history of Sino-Burmese relations. In his speech, Governor Chen Yu thanked General Ne Win for his valuable contributions to the work of building the Sino-Burmese friendship edifice and wished the distinguished Burmese guests good health and a safe journey.

In his speech, General Ne Win expressed his gratitude to the leaders of the Chinese government and Chinese friends for the warm welcome and hospitality extended to him and the more than 300 Burmese friends. He said that he was pleased to hear that the leaders of the Chinese government would pay a friendly visit to Burma in January next year, which he appreciates, and extended a warm welcome to them in advance. At the end of his speech, General Ne Win wished the Chinese people good health and happiness, the leaders of the Chinese government good health and success in their work, and the People's Republic of China prosperity and strength.

Before the speeches by Governor Chen Yu and General Ne Win, the band played the national anthems of China and Burma. General Ne Win, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and Governor Chen Yu, reviewed the guard of honor and shook hands with the heads of the Party, government, and military in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City who had come to see him off.

Among those present at the station to see off the distinguished guests were: Zhao Ziyang, Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Lin Qiangyun, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province; Xue Yan, Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Sun Leyi, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou; Lieutenant General Wen Niansheng, Commander of the Guangzhou Garrison; and responsible persons from relevant departments of the Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City People's Committees, the People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou (Army, Navy, and Air Force units), the Guangdong and Guangzhou Municipal Committees of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, various people's organizations, and local organizations of democratic parties.

Consuls and Vice Consuls of various countries stationed in Guangzhou also went to the station to see them off.

Thousands of people from all walks of life who were at the station to see off the distinguished guests shouted slogans such as "Farewell, General Ne Win!" and "Long live Sino-Burmese friendship!" Cultural workers performed dances, the band played joyful music, and the dancing lions frequently paid their respects to the distinguished guests. General Ne Win shook hands with the well-wishers from time to time, accepted bouquets from the crowd, watched the dances performed by the actresses, and expressed his gratitude to them. At 8:30, the special train carrying the distinguished guests slowly started, and warm applause and the song "People of the World Are of One Heart" resounded again at the station. General Ne Win waved from the carriage to say goodbye to the well-wishers.

### 载着中国人民的深情厚谊  奈温将军离广州回国  陈毅副总理和数千群众欢送贵宾

1960-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

    载着中国人民的深情厚谊
    奈温将军离广州回国
    陈毅副总理和数千群众欢送贵宾
    新华社广州13日电　缅甸联邦国防军总参谋长奈温将军和夫人，载着中国人民的深情厚谊，今天上午离开广州经深圳回国。国务院副总理陈毅和夫人、广东省省长陈郁和夫人和广州市各界群众数千人到车站欢送贵宾。
    陈郁省长在车站上致词，感谢奈温将军和其他缅甸贵宾们在广州的友好访问，感谢他们带来的深厚的“胞波”情谊。他说，中缅两国总理正式签订了中缅边界条约，顺利地全面地解决了两国的边界问题，在中缅两国的关系史上写下了光辉灿烂的一章。陈郁省长在讲话中感谢奈温将军在建设中缅友好大厦的工作中作出了宝贵的贡献，并祝缅甸贵宾们身体健康，旅途平安。
    奈温将军在讲话中，对中国政府领导人和中国朋友们给予他本人和三百多位缅甸朋友的热烈欢迎和盛情招待表示感谢。他说，我高兴地听到中国政府的领导人将在明年1月到缅甸进行友好访问，我表示感谢，并预先对他们表示热烈的欢迎。奈温将军在讲话结束时，祝中国人民身心健康、愉快，中国政府领导人身体健康、工作顺利，中华人民共和国繁荣、富强。
    在陈郁省长和奈温将军讲话前，乐队奏中、缅两国国歌，奈温将军由陈毅副总理、陈郁省长陪同检阅了仪仗队，并且同前来欢送的广东省、广州市党、政府和驻军负责人握手告别。
    今天到车站欢送贵宾的有：中共广东省委书记赵紫阳，广东省副省长林锵云，中共广州市委书记薛焰，广州市副市长孙乐宜，广州驻军首长文年生中将，以及广东省和广州市人民委员会有关部门、人民解放军驻广州部队陆海空军部队、政协广东省和广州市委员会、各人民团体和民主党派地方组织等各方面的负责人等。
    各国驻广州的领事、副领事也到车站欢送。
    在车站上欢送贵宾的数千名各界人民高呼：“欢送奈温将军！”“中缅友好万岁！”等口号。文艺工作者表演着舞蹈，乐队奏着欢乐的乐曲，醒狮频频向贵宾施礼。奈温将军不时和欢送的群众握手道别，接受群众的献花，还观看了女演员表演的舞蹈，并向她们表示感谢。8时30分，贵宾们乘坐的专车徐徐开动，车站上再次响起热烈的掌声和“全世界人民心一条”的歌声。奈温将军从车厢里挥手向欢送的人们告别。
