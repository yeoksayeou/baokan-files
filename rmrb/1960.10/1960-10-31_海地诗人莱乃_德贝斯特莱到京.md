Title: Haitian Poet René Depestre Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-31
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - Haitian poet René Depestre arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association for a visit to our country. Wang Jiabin, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association, was at the airport to welcome him.

### 海地诗人莱乃·德贝斯特莱到京

1960-10-31
第2版()
专栏：

    海地诗人莱乃·德贝斯特莱到京
    新华社30日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请前来我国访问的海地诗人莱乃·德贝斯特莱今天下午乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国拉丁美洲友好协会副总干事王家宾。
