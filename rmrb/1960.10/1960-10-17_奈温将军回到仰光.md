Title: General Ne Win Returns to Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1960-10-17
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, October 16th: General Ne Win, Chief of Staff of the Armed Forces of the Union of Burma, and his wife, returned to Rangoon by special plane this afternoon after paying a friendly visit to China at the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China.
    During his stay in Beijing, General Ne Win attended the celebrations of the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the signing ceremony of the Sino-Burmese Border Treaty on October 1st, and then visited many cities in East and South China.
    The plane carrying General Ne Win and his wife landed at Mingaladon Airport at 4:15 pm local time. President U Win Maung and his wife, Prime Minister U Nu and his wife, Chief Justice of the Supreme Court U Myint Thein, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities Sao Khun Kyi, Speaker of the Chamber of Deputies Mann Ba Swe, and Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, Lei Yang, stepped forward to shake hands with General Ne Win and his wife.
    When General Ne Win appeared at the door of the cabin, more than 5,000 people waved the Burmese flag and cheered the development of Sino-Burmese friendship. At this time, cheers resounded and gongs and drums were played in unison. Also at the airport to welcome General Ne Win were Minister of Foreign Affairs Sao Khun Hkio, Minister of Trade Development U Tun, Deputy Chief of Staff (Navy) Brigadier General Than Pe, Deputy Chief of Staff (Air Force) Brigadier General Clift, Quartermaster General Tin Pe Brigadier, Colonel Kyeeing Ngwe of the General Staff, Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs James Barrington, Executive Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs U Soe Tin, and Director of Protocol of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs U Ba Maung.
    The trade delegation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, headed by Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, also went to the airport to welcome them.
    Also at the airport to welcome them were diplomatic envoys from various countries and military attachés from various embassies in Burma.
    When General Ne Win and Prime Minister U Nu left the airport in the same car and passed through the streets, they were warmly cheered by the people on both sides of the road.

### 奈温将军回到仰光

1960-10-17
第5版()
专栏：

    奈温将军回到仰光
    新华社仰光16日电　缅甸联邦武装部队总参谋长奈温将军和夫人，应中华人民共和国政府邀请在中国进行友好访问后，于今天下午乘专机返抵仰光。
    在北京逗留期间，奈温将军参加了中华人民共和国成立十一周年的庆典和10月1日的中缅边界条约签订仪式，然后访问了华东和华南的许多城市。
    奈温将军和夫人所搭乘的飞机于当地时间十六点十五分在明加拉顿机场着陆。总统吴温貌和夫人，总理吴努和夫人，最高法院首席法官吴敏登，民族院议长藻昆基，代表院议长曼巴赛和中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬，迎上前去同奈温将军和夫人握手。
    当奈温将军在机舱门口出现时，五千多群众挥舞着缅甸国旗，欢呼中缅友谊的发展。这时欢呼声雷动，锣鼓声齐鸣。在机场欢迎奈温将军的还有外交部长藻昆卓，贸易发展部长吴敦，副总参谋长（海军）丹佩准将，副总参谋长（空军）克利夫特准将，军需署长丁佩准将，总参谋部的庆牛上校，外交部常任秘书詹姆斯·巴林顿，外交部执行秘书吴苏丁，外交部礼宾司司长吴巴茂，联邦党总书记德钦觉顿。
    以对外贸易部副部长雷任民为首的中华人民共和国政府贸易代表团也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有各国外交使节和各国驻缅甸大使馆的武官。
    当奈温将军和吴努总理同乘一辆汽车离开机场经过街道时，受到两旁人们的热烈欢呼致意。
