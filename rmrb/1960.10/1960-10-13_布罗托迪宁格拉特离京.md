Title: Brotodinningrat Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 12th: Brotodinningrat, First Secretary of the Indonesian Presidential Palace Office, and his wife, left Beijing today to visit southern regions of China before returning home. Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and others went to the station to see them off.

### 布罗托迪宁格拉特离京

1960-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    布罗托迪宁格拉特离京
    新华社12日讯　印度尼西亚总统府办公厅第一秘书布罗托迪宁格拉特和夫人，已于今天离开北京前往南方各地参观访问后回国。前往车站送行的有中国人民外交学会副秘书长吴晓达等。
