Title: Indonesian-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The delegation of the Indonesian-Chinese Friendship Association, led by Head of Delegation Suripto and Deputy Head of Delegation Sain Nasution, arrived in Beijing this evening at the invitation of the Chinese-Indonesian Friendship Association.

Welcoming them at the airport were Lin Lin, Wang Futu, Yang Fengzhu, and others, representatives of the Chinese-Indonesian Friendship Association.

### 印度尼西亚中国友协访华代表团到京

1960-10-01
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚中国友协访华代表团到京
    新华社30日讯　应中国印度尼西亚友好协会邀请前来我国访问的印度尼西亚中国友好协会访华代表团，在团长苏里普托、副团长赛因·纳苏蒂安率领下，今天晚上到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国印度尼西亚友好协会代表林林、王负图、杨凤竹等人。
