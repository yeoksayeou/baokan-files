Title: Premier Zhou Sends Congratulations on the Opening of the All-African Students Conference
Author:
Date: 1960-10-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram on October 10th to congratulate the First All-African Students Conference scheduled to be held in London from October 12th to 14th. The full text of the congratulatory telegram is as follows: First All-African Students Conference, London, England:

    On the occasion of the opening of this First All-African Students Conference, I would like to extend warm congratulations to the conference on behalf of the Chinese people and the Chinese government, and in my own name. For a long time, African students and youth, together with all the African people, have carried out a heroic struggle for the liberation and freedom of Africa. The Chinese people have always deeply sympathized with and firmly supported your just cause. I wish the conference will make positive contributions to enhancing the unity of African students and youth, striving for and safeguarding national independence, opposing imperialism and colonialism, opposing racial discrimination, and defending world peace.
                      Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                                    Zhou Enlai
                          October 10, 1960, in Beijing

### 周总理电贺全非学生大会召开

1960-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺全非学生大会召开
    新华社12日讯　中华人民共和国国务院周恩来总理于10月10日打电报祝贺定于10月12日至14日在伦敦举行的第一次全非学生大会，贺电全文如下：英国伦敦第一次全非学生大会：
    在此第一次全非学生大会召开的时候，我谨代表中国人民和中国政府，并且以我个人的名义向大会表示热烈的祝贺。长期以来，非洲学生和青年同全体非洲人民一道，为争取非洲的解放和自由进行了英勇的斗争。中国人民一贯深切同情和坚决支持你们的这一正义事业。祝大会对增进非洲学生和青年的团结、争取和维护民族独立、反对帝国主义和殖民主义、反对种族歧视、保卫世界和平的正义斗争，作出积极的贡献。
  中华人民共和国国务院总理
                        周恩来
          1960年10月10日于北京
