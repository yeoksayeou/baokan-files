Title: Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe to Participate in Berlin Festival Performance
Author:
Date: 1960-10-08
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, October 6th - The Chinese Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe arrived in Berlin on the morning of October 6th for a month-long visit to the German Democratic Republic, and will participate in the annual Berlin Festival performance. The acrobatic troupe is visiting and performing according to the Sino-German cultural cooperation agreement.

### 我重庆杂技团将参加柏林节演出

1960-10-08
第5版()
专栏：

    我重庆杂技团将参加柏林节演出
    新华社柏林6日电　中国重庆杂技团6日上午抵达柏林，对德意志民主共和国进行为时一个月的访问，并将参加一年一度的柏林节的表演。杂技团是根据中德文化合作协定前来作访问演出的。
