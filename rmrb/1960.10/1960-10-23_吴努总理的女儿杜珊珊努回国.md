Title: Premier U Nu's Daughter Daw Than Than Nu Returns Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-10-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, October 22nd: Daw Than Than Nu, daughter of the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, left Kunming by plane this afternoon to return to her country.

Daw Than Than Nu accompanied Prime Minister U Nu on his visit to our country. She participated in the celebrations of the 11th anniversary of our country's founding and the signing ceremony of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty in Beijing. Subsequently, she visited factories, people's communes, schools, and children's palaces in Nanjing, Wuxi, Suzhou, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Kunming, and other cities, and toured scenic spots and historical sites in various places.

Before Daw Than Than Nu left Kunming, Liu Minghui, Vice Governor of Yunnan Province, and his wife hosted a banquet in her honor.

### 吴努总理的女儿杜珊珊努回国

1960-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    吴努总理的女儿杜珊珊努回国
    新华社昆明22日电　缅甸联邦吴努总理的女儿杜珊珊努，今天下午乘飞机离开昆明回国。
    杜珊珊努是随吴努总理来我国访问的。她曾在北京参加了我国建国十一周年庆典和庆祝中缅边界条约签字的活动，随后又先后参观访问了南京、无锡、苏州、上海、杭州、广州、昆明等城市的工厂、人民公社、学校、少年宫，并游览了各地的风景名胜。
    杜珊珊努离开昆明前，云南省副省长刘明辉和夫人曾设宴为她饯行。
