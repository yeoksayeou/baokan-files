Title: On the Eve of the Japanese General Election, Japanese Government Intensifies Suppression of Democratic Forces
Author:
Date: 1960-10-14
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 11th: On the eve of the Japanese general election, the Japanese Police Agency convened a meeting of national police chiefs today, instructing police in various regions to intensify the suppression of democratic forces.
    At the meeting, National Public Safety Commission Chairman Iwa Yamazaki instructed the police chiefs to pay "special attention" to the activities of "communist forces." Police Agency Director-General Kashiwamura clamored for "effective crackdowns" on democratic forces during the election campaign. He revealed that Japanese police authorities are plotting to continue suppressing the Japanese people's anti-American and anti-government struggles.
    Yesterday, the Japanese government also convened the seventh plenary meeting of the National Public Safety Commission Liaison Council to study the issue of strengthening the suppression of patriotic people.
    At that meeting, Iwa Yamazaki instructed the National Public Safety Commissioners to "deal severely" with the "tendency to use violence" that has recently emerged in demonstrations against the U.S.-Japan military alliance and in the struggles of the Miike coal miners.
    He also emphasized the need to increase the number of police officers and modernize police equipment.

### 在日本大选前夕  日本政府加紧镇压民主力量

1960-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

    在日本大选前夕
    日本政府加紧镇压民主力量
    新华社11日讯　东京消息：日本警察厅今天召开全国警察头目会议，布置各地警察在大选前夕加紧镇压民主力量。
    在会上，国家公安委员长山崎岩指示警察头目们要“特别注意”“共产主义势力”的活动。警察厅长官柏村叫嚣要在竞选中对民主力量进行“有效的取缔”。他透露，日本警察当局阴谋继续镇压日本人民反美、反政府的斗争。
    昨天，日本政府还召开全国公安委员联络协议会第七次全体会议，研究了加强镇压爱国人民的问题。
    在那次会上，山崎岩指令国家公安委员们要“严厉对付”最近在反对日美军事同盟的示威和三池煤矿工人的斗争中所出现的“使用暴力的倾向”。
    他并且强调，必须增加警察人数和使警察的装备现代化。
