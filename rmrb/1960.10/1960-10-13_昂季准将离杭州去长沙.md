Title: Brigadier Aung Gyi Leaves Hangzhou for Changsha
Author:
Date: 1960-10-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 12th - Brigadier Aung Gyi, Chief Representative of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee and Deputy Chief of Staff of the Armed Forces of the Union of Burma, and the representatives and advisors of the Burmese delegation, left Hangzhou by special train tonight for Changsha.

	During their stay in Hangzhou, the distinguished guests visited scenic spots and historical sites such as Jiu Xi, Longjing, Hupao, Lingyin Temple, Three Pools Mirroring the Moon, and the Flower Harbor Viewing Fish Park, and cruised on West Lake, enjoying the autumnal scenery of the lake and mountains. The distinguished guests also visited the Jianqiao People's Commune in Hangzhou, the Hangzhou Dujinsheng Silk Weaving Factory, and the Huafeng Paper Mill. On the evening of the 11th, Huo Shilian, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province, hosted a banquet to welcome the distinguished guests.

	Brigadier Aung Gyi and his party arrived in Hangzhou by special train from Shanghai on the afternoon of the 11th. Upon their arrival and departure from Hangzhou, Huo Shilian, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province, Zhang Shixiang, Vice Mayor of Hangzhou, Lieutenant General Qian Jun and Major General Li Guohou, leading officers of the Zhejiang Garrison, greeted them at the railway station.

### 昂季准将离杭州去长沙

1960-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

    昂季准将离杭州去长沙
    新华社杭州12日电　中缅边界联合委员会缅甸首席代表、缅甸联邦国防军副总参谋长昂季准将和缅甸代表团的代表、顾问，今晚乘专车离开杭州去长沙。
    贵宾们在杭州期间，游览了九溪、龙井、虎跑、灵隐寺、三潭印月、花港观鱼公园等名胜古迹，并泛舟西湖，观赏了秋天的湖山景色。贵宾们还参观了杭州市笕桥人民公社、杭州都锦生丝织厂和华丰造纸厂。11日晚上，浙江省副省长霍士廉设宴欢迎贵宾。
    昂季准将等是在11日下午由上海乘专车到达杭州的。在他们到达和离开杭州的时候，浙江省副省长霍士廉、杭州市副市长张世祥、浙江驻军首长钱钧中将、李国厚少将等，都到车站迎送。
