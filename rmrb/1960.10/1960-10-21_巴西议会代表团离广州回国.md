Title: Brazilian Parliamentary Delegation Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-10-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, October 20th: The Brazilian parliamentary delegation, consisting of eighteen members and led by Brazilian Senator and Chairman of the Senate Finance Committee, Gaspar Velloso, left Guangzhou by train today to return home. Luo Peiyuan, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Director of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Ren Bosheng, Director of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, saw them off at the station.

The guests arrived in Guangzhou on the 19th. On the evening of the 19th, Luo Peiyuan and Ren Bosheng hosted a banquet to welcome them. They also visited the South China Agricultural College in Guangzhou.

### 巴西议会代表团离广州回国

1960-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    巴西议会代表团离广州回国
    新华社广州20日电　巴西议会代表团一行十八人，在巴西参议员、参议院财政委员会主席加斯帕·贝略索率领下，今天乘火车离开广州回国。政协广州市委员会副主席、中国人民外交学会理事罗培元，中国人民外交学会理事任泊生到车站送行。
    客人们是在19日到广州的。19日晚，罗培元、任泊生曾设宴欢迎他们。他们在广州还访问了华南农学院。
