Title: Argentine Youth Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-30
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 29th: The Argentine youth delegation of five people left Beijing by plane today to return home. Zhang Chao, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, and others saw them off at the airport.

### 阿根廷青年代表团回国

1960-10-30
第4版()
专栏：

    阿根廷青年代表团回国
    据新华社29日讯　阿根廷青年代表团一行五人，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。中华全国青年联合会副主席张超等到机场欢送。
