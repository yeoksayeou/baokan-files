Title: Two Polish Guests Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 20th — Stanisław Bębnak and Józef Grobel, members of the Polish publishing delegation who came to China for a visit under the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Polish cultural cooperation agreement, arrived in Beijing today.

### 波兰两位客人到京

1960-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰两位客人到京
    新华社20日讯　根据中波文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问的波兰出版代表团成员斯塔尼斯瓦夫·本勃纳克和约瑟夫·格罗彼尔，今天到达北京。
