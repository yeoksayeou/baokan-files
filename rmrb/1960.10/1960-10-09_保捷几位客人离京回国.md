Title: Several Czechoslovakian Guests Leave Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 8th: Markov, a Bulgarian cultural relic worker who came to China for a visit according to the 1960 execution plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement; Kahitik, the manager of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic Film Import and Export Company; and Esiou, the director of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic Film Processing Factory, who were invited by the China Film Distribution Company to visit China, have concluded their visits to China and left Beijing to return to their countries in recent days.

### 保捷几位客人离京回国

1960-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

    保捷几位客人离京回国
    新华社8日讯　根据中保文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问的保加利亚文物工作者马尔科夫，应中国电影发行公司邀请来我国访问的捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国电影输出入公司经理卡赫蒂克和捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国电影洗印厂厂长埃西欧等人，结束了在我国的访问，分别在近日离开北京回国。
