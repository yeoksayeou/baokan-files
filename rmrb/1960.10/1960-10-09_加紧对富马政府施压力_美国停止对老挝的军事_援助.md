Title: Stepping Up Pressure on the Phouma Government - US Halts Military "Aid" to Laos
Author:
Date: 1960-10-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 8th: The United States government is using the cessation of military "aid" to Laos as a means of intensifying pressure on the Phouma government.

    State Department spokesman Tally confirmed this in a statement to reporters on the 7th. When asked by reporters whether the United States had stopped providing military "aid" to Laos, Tally emphasized, "So far, the U.S. government has not approved the release of local currency ("aid") funds to pay for the Laotian Kingdom's military salaries and other budget expenditures for September, pending the resolution of certain issues." He said in a threatening tone, "This issue is currently being discussed in Laos with the Laotian authorities, and it is not yet clear what arrangements may ultimately be made." Tally stated that "the United States believes that the salary payment system [of the Laotian army] must be re-examined."

### 加紧对富马政府施压力  美国停止对老挝的军事“援助”

1960-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    加紧对富马政府施压力
    美国停止对老挝的军事“援助”
    据新华社8日讯　华盛顿消息：美国政府正以停止对老挝的军事“援助”为手段，来加紧对富马政府施压力。
    国务院发言人塔利7日在对新闻记者发表谈话时证实了这一点。当记者问到美国是否停止给予老挝军事“援助”时，塔利强调说：“到目前为止，美国政府还没有批准发放当地货币的（“援助”）基金来支付9月份老挝王国的军饷和其他预算开支，以便等待一些问题的解决。”他以威胁性的口吻说：“这个问题目前正在老挝同老挝当局讨论中，还不清楚最后可能作出什么样的安排。”塔利表示，“美国认为必须对（老挝军队的）薪饷支付制度加以重新研究。”
