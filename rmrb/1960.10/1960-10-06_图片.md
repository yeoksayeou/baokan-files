    The visiting delegation and visitors from fifteen Latin American countries to China signed three documents at the Great Hall of the People on the evening of October 5: "A Letter to the Chinese People," "A Statement on the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between Cuba and the People's Republic of China and on Support for the Cuban Revolution," and "A Statement on Friendship Associations Between Latin American Countries and China." The picture shows the signing ceremony.
