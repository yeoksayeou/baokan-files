Title: Cambodian National Radio Announces Sihanouk to Visit Czechoslovakia, the Soviet Union, Mongolia and China
Author:
Date: 1960-10-19
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, October 18th. Cambodian National Radio announced this morning that Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk, at the invitation of Czechoslovakia, the Soviet Union, Mongolia and the People's Republic of China, will visit these countries.

In a letter to the Queen of Cambodia and the Cambodian people, Sihanouk said that he believes this visit is very useful for maintaining the friendship and valuable relations with these countries that have always greatly respected and extensively assisted Cambodia.

Sihanouk said that he will return to Cambodia on December 17th or 18th.

### 柬埔寨国家电台宣布  西哈努克将访问捷苏蒙中

1960-10-19
第6版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨国家电台宣布
    西哈努克将访问捷苏蒙中
    新华社金边18日电　据柬埔寨国家电台今天上午宣布，柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王应捷克斯洛伐克、苏联、蒙古和中华人民共和国的邀请，将访问这些国家。
    西哈努克在致柬埔寨王后和柬埔寨人民的信中说，他认为这次访问对于保持同这些一直非常尊重和大量援助柬埔寨的国家的友谊和十分宝贵的关系，是非常有用的。
    西哈努克说，他将在12月17日或18日回到柬埔寨。
