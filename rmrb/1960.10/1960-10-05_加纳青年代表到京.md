Title: Ghanaian Youth Representative Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Kwame Ofori Antwi Agyemang, a Ghanaian youth representative invited by the All-China Youth Federation to visit our country, arrived in Beijing on the 1st.

He was welcomed by Zhang Chao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, and others upon his arrival in Beijing.

### 加纳青年代表到京

1960-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    加纳青年代表到京
    新华社2日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国访问的加纳青年代表克瓦西·奥伏汗纳·阿克叶姆邦，在1日到达北京。
    他到达北京时，受到中华全国青年联合会副主席张超等人的欢迎。
