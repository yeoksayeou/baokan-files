Title: Our Trade Delegation Departs for Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1960-10-15
Page: 4

    Our Trade Delegation Departs for Rangoon
    Xinhua News Agency, October 14th: The government trade delegation of the People's Republic of China, invited by the government of the Union of Burma to visit Burma, left Beijing by plane today for Rangoon, led by Lei Renmin, head of the delegation and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade.
    Among those seeing the delegation off at the airport were Lu Xuzhang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and others.
    U Yawnghwe, Second Secretary of the Burmese Embassy in China, and others also saw the delegation off at the airport.

### 我贸易代表团赴仰光

1960-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

    我贸易代表团赴仰光
    新华社14日讯　应缅甸联邦政府邀请到缅甸访问的中华人民共和国政府贸易代表团，在团长、对外贸易部副部长雷任民率领下，今天乘飞机离开北京前往仰光。
    到机场送行的有对外贸易部副部长卢绪章等。
    缅甸驻中国大使馆二等秘书吴容林等也到机场送行。
