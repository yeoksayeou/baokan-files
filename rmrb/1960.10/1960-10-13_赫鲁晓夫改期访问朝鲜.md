Title: Khrushchev Postpones Visit to North Korea
Author:
Date: 1960-10-13
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th - TASS reported from Moscow on the 12th: An official announcement regarding Khrushchev's postponement of his visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea has been released here.

    The new dates for the visit will be announced later. The official announcement stated that on August 12th, an announcement was issued stating that, at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party and the government the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Comrade Khrushchev would visit in early October.

    The announcement said that, after consultation between the Central Committee of the CPSU and the Soviet government and the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party and the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, it was deemed appropriate to postpone the visit of Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPSU and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

### 赫鲁晓夫改期访问朝鲜

1960-10-13
第6版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫改期访问朝鲜
    新华社12日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科12日讯：这里公布了赫鲁晓夫改期访问朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的正式公告。
    关于访问的新日期将随后宣布。正式公告说，8月12日曾经发表公告说，应朝鲜劳动党中央委员会和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府的邀请，赫鲁晓夫同志将于10月初进行访问。
    公告说，现经苏共中央和苏联政府同朝鲜劳动党中央和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府协议，认为苏共中央第一书记、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫对朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的访问改期是适宜的。
