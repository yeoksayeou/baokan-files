Title: Algerian Art Troupe Leaves Shanghai for Nanchang
Author:
Date: 1960-10-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 19th - The Algerian Art Troupe left Shanghai by train tonight for a visit to Nanchang.

Those who came to the station to see them off included Meng Bo, Director of the Shanghai Municipal Bureau of Culture and Vice President of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries; Li Taicheng, Council Member of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association in Shanghai and Deputy Director of the Shanghai Municipal Bureau of Culture; Liu Housheng, member of the Presidium of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese Dramatists Association; and more than one hundred people from Shanghai's literary and art circles.

The Algerian Art Troupe performed two shows in Shanghai, which were warmly welcomed and praised by the people of Shanghai. During their stay in Shanghai, the artists visited the Shanghai Theatre Academy, the Opera School, the Shanghai Workers' Cultural Palace, the Children's Palace, and the Shanghai Construction Machinery Factory. The honored guests also held a get-together with students from Shanghai Jiaotong University, Tongji University, Fudan University, and the Shanghai Conservatory of Music.

### 阿尔及利亚艺术团离上海去南昌

1960-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚艺术团离上海去南昌
    新华社上海19日电　阿尔及利亚艺术团今晚乘火车离开上海去南昌访问。
    到车站欢送的，有上海市文化局局长、对外文协上海分会副会长孟波，中国非洲人民友好协会在上海理事、上海市文化局副局长李太成，中国戏剧家协会上海分会主席团委员刘厚生以及上海市文艺界人士一百多人。
    阿尔及利亚艺术团在上海共演出两场，受到上海人民热烈的欢迎和赞扬。在上海期间，艺术家们访问了上海戏剧学院、戏曲学校、上海市工人文化宫、少年宫、上海建设机器厂等。贵宾们还和上海交通大学、同济大学、复旦大学以及上海音乐学院等校学生们一起，举行了联欢会。
