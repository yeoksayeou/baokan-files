Title: Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe Leaves Hungary for Visit to Germany
Author:
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 5th. After concluding its performance tour in Hungary, the Chinese Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe left Budapest on the morning of the 5th for a visit to the German Democratic Republic.
	The Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe performed a total of thirty-three shows in Budapest, beginning on September 7th, with an audience of more than 60,000 people.

### 我重庆杂技团离匈去德国访问

1960-10-07
第6版()
专栏：

    我重庆杂技团离匈去德国访问
    新华社布达佩斯5日电　中国重庆杂技团结束了在匈牙利的访问演出后，已于5日上午离开布达佩斯前往德意志民主共和国进行访问。
    重庆杂技团从9月7日开始，在布达佩斯共演出了三十三场，观众达六万多人。
