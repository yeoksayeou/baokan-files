Title: Haitian Cultural Friendship Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The Haitian Cultural Friendship Delegation headed by Jean Laratte, and Manuel Adrianzan, a member of the Venezuelan Cultural Delegation, who visited our country at the invitation of the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association, left Beijing by plane today to return to their country.

### 海地文化友好代表团回国

1960-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    海地文化友好代表团回国
    新华社9日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请来我国访问的以让·拉脱为首的海地文化友好代表团和委内瑞拉文化代表团团员马努埃尔·阿德里安萨，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
