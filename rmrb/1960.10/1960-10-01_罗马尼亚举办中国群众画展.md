Title: Chinese Mass Art Exhibition Held in Romania
Author:
Date: 1960-10-01
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 30th - A Chinese mass art exhibition, held by the Romanian Ministry of Education and Culture and the Art Museum to celebrate the eleventh anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, opened here today. Buican, First Vice-Chairman of the Romanian Association for Foreign Cultural Liaison, and Wang Luming, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Romania, attended the opening ceremony.

　　This mass art exhibition features more than one hundred and sixty artistic works created by Chinese workers, peasants, and soldiers, including paintings, woodcuts, and paper-cuts.

### 罗马尼亚举办中国群众画展

1960-10-01
第7版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚举办中国群众画展
    新华社布加勒斯特30日电　罗马尼亚教育和文化部和艺术博物馆为庆祝中华人民共和国成立十一周年举办的中国群众画展，今天在这里开幕。罗马尼亚对外文化联络协会第一副主席布伊康和中国驻罗马尼亚临时代办王鲁明等出席了开幕式。
    这次群众画展共展出了一百六十多幅我国工农兵群众创作的绘画、木刻和剪纸等艺术作品。
