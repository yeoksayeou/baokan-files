Title: Algerian Art Troupe Goes to Changsha After Performing in Nanchang
Author:
Date: 1960-10-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, 24th -- The Algerian Art Troupe concluded its visit and performances in Nanchang today and left by train for Changsha.

The Algerian Art Troupe, led by Afa Abdul Madi, the person in charge, and Benlas Bualang, the head of the Art Troupe, arrived in Nanchang by train from Hangzhou on the 21st. The Art Troupe performed the dance drama "Towards the Light" and the three-act drama "Immortal People" in Nanchang, which were warmly welcomed and praised by people from all walks of life in the province and city. During their stay in Nanchang, the guests visited the "August 1st" Uprising Memorial Hall; Zhang Yunqiao, the Mayor of Nanchang, introduced the course of the "August 1st" Uprising to the guests. They also visited factories, toured the city, and watched the drama "August 1st Storm" performed by the Jiangxi Provincial Drama Troupe. On the evening of the 23rd, the Jiangxi Provincial People's Committee hosted a banquet for the guests.

Before visiting Nanchang, the Art Troupe visited Shanghai and Hangzhou. When the artists performed at the Shanghai People's Grand Stage on the evening of the 15th, the audience of more than 2,000 people applauded thunderously, warmly expressing their respect to the heroic Algerian people. Cao Diqiu, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, and famous figures from Shanghai's literary and art circles watched the performance, and Cao Diqiu received the person in charge of the Art Troupe during the intermission.

### 阿尔及利亚艺术团  在南昌演出后去长沙

1960-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚艺术团
    在南昌演出后去长沙
    新华社南昌24日电　阿尔及利亚艺术团今天结束了在南昌的访问演出，今天乘火车去长沙。
    阿尔及利亚艺术团由负责人阿法·阿卜杜勒·玛蒂和艺术团团长本拉斯·布阿朗率领，21日由杭州乘火车到达南昌。艺术团在南昌演出了舞剧“向光明”、三幕话剧“不朽的人”，受到省市各界人民的热烈欢迎和赞扬。客人们在南昌期间，参观了“八一”起义纪念馆；南昌市长张云樵向客人们介绍了“八一”起义的经过。他们还参观了工厂，游览了市容，观看了江西省话剧团演的话剧“八一风暴”。23日晚上，江西省人民委员会曾设宴招待客人。
    在访问南昌以前，艺术团访问了上海和杭州。艺术家们15日晚上在上海人民大舞台演出的时候，全场二千多名观众掌声雷动，热情地向英勇的阿尔及利亚人民表示敬意。上海市副市长曹荻秋以及上海市文艺界著名人士观看了演出，曹荻秋在演出休息时接见了艺术团的负责人。
