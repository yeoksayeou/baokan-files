Title: Ambassador Zhang Canming Holds Reception for Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Delegation Visiting Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1960-10-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 21st — Chinese Ambassador to Ceylon, Zhang Canming, held a reception here today for the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries delegation visiting Ceylon, headed by Ding Xilin.

The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries delegation is visiting at the invitation of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association to participate in the 11th anniversary celebrations of the founding of the People's Republic of China, which are being organized by the Ceylon-China Friendship Association.

Wilmot A. Perera, Chairman of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association, senior government officials, political leaders, and well-known figures from the cultural circles attended the reception.

The reception was held in a very friendly atmosphere.

### 为我对外文协代表团访问锡兰  张灿明大使举行招待会

1960-10-23
第6版()
专栏：

    为我对外文协代表团访问锡兰
    张灿明大使举行招待会
    新华社科伦坡21日电　中国驻锡兰大使张灿明今天在这里为以丁西林为首的中国人民对外文化协会代表团访问锡兰举行招待会。
    中国人民对外文化协会代表团是应锡中友好协会的邀请，前来参加锡中友好协会主办的中华人民共和国成立十一周年庆祝活动的。
    锡中友好协会主席威尔莫特·佩雷拉、政府高级官员、政界领袖，以及文化界著名人士出席了招待会。
    招待会是在非常友好的气氛中进行的。
