Title: Guo Moruo Hosts a Banquet in Honor of Indian Professor D.D. Kosambi
Author:
Date: 1960-10-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 26th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet tonight in honor of Professor D.D. Kosambi, an Indian mathematician and member of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, who is currently visiting China. Among those present were Liao Chengzhi, Liu Ningyi, Meng Yongqian, Du Runsheng, Qu Tangliang, Hua Luogeng, Wang Mingyuan, Zheng Senyu, Zhu Ziqi, Pan Chun, Guan Zhaozhi and others.

### 郭沫若设宴招待印度高善必教授

1960-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若设宴招待印度高善必教授
    新华社26日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席、中国科学院院长郭沫若今晚设宴招待正在我国访问的印度数学家、世界和平理事会常务理事德·德·高善必教授。出席作陪的有廖承志、刘宁一、孟用潜、杜润生、区棠亮、华罗庚、王明远、郑森禹、朱子奇、潘纯、关肇直等人。
