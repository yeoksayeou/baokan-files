Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives British Journalist
Author:
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd — Vice Premier Chen Yi received Stuart Gelder, a British freelance journalist who is currently visiting our country, today. Present at the reception were Gong Peng, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Song Zhiguang, Deputy Director of the Department of West European Affairs.

### 陈毅副总理接见英国记者

1960-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见英国记者
    新华社23日讯　陈毅副总理今天接见了正在我国访问的英国自由投稿记者斯图尔特·吉耳德。接见时在座的有外交部新闻司司长龚澎，西欧司副司长宋之光。
