Title: Female Parachutists and Aeromodelling Athletes Each Break a World Record
Author:
Date: 1960-10-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hefei, 14th October. Female parachutists from Anhui Province broke another world record during a parachuting demonstration competition held in Hefei on October 8th.

That day the weather was clear. At 2:30 PM, an "Iron Eagle" plane flew at an altitude of 1,000 meters. Female athletes Li Wenxiu, Tang Hexiu, and Du Jufang jumped out of the cabin one after another, gradually approaching the target on the ground. Finally, they landed at distances of 1.20 meters, 3.36 meters, and 3.95 meters from the target, respectively. The average distance from the target was 2.836 meters, breaking the world record of 3.39 meters for the women's daytime 1,000-meter collective precision jump, which was set in 1959 by Mei Yan, Guo Xin'e, and Zhang Jingwen, athletes from Shaanxi Province.

The 22-year-old Li Wenxiu, 21-year-old Tang Hexiu, and 20-year-old Du Jufang have each broken a world record for the second time. In July of this year, they collaborated with other female parachutists from Anhui Province to break the world record for the women's daytime 600-meter collective precision jump in Bengbu and Hefei respectively.

Xinhua News Agency, 14th October. In a recent record-breaking test conducted by the Chinese People's Aviation Club, Chinese aeromodelling athlete Gao Qinfei created a new world record of 2 hours and 5 minutes for the flight duration of a 2.5 ml engine model helicopter, breaking the world record of 54 minutes and 37 seconds set by Soviet athlete Borisov on August 18, 1959.

Gao Qinfei is 21 years old and has been involved in aeromodelling activities for six years. This test was held on September 20 in Liangxiang, a suburb of Beijing. (Attached picture)

### 女子跳伞运动员航空模型选手  各打破一项世界纪录

1960-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

    女子跳伞运动员航空模型选手
    各打破一项世界纪录
    新华社合肥14日电　安徽省女子跳伞运动员10月8日在合肥举行的一次跳伞表演赛中，又打破了一项世界纪录。
    这天天气晴朗，下午两点半钟，一架“铁鹰”在一千米高空飞行，女运动员李文秀、唐荷秀、杜菊芳三人陆续跳出机舱，逐渐向地面的靶心接近，最后她们分别降落在距靶心一米二十、三米三十六和三米九十五的地方，平均距靶心的成绩是二米八十三点六，打破了我国陕西省运动员梅严、郭新娥和张景文在1959年创造的女子日间一千米集体综合跳伞平均距靶心三米三十九的世界纪录。
    二十二岁的李文秀、二十一岁的唐荷秀和二十岁的杜菊芳，这次都是第二次打破世界纪录。今年7月间，她们三人曾同安徽省其他女子跳伞运动员合作，分别在蚌埠和合肥打破过女子日间六百米集体综合跳伞的世界纪录。
    新华社14日讯　中国人民航空俱乐部在最近举行的一次航空模型创纪录测验中，我国航空模型选手高勤飞用二点五毫升发动机模型直升飞机创造了留空时间二小时零五分的世界新纪录，打破了苏联运动员鲍利索夫在1959年8月18日创造的五十四分三十七秒的世界纪录。
    高勤飞今年二十一岁，他从事航空模型活动已经有六年的历史。这次测验是9月20日在北京郊区良乡举行的。（附图片）
