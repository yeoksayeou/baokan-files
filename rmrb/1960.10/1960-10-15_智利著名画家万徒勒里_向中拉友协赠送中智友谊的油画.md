Title: Prominent Chilean Painter Venturelli Presents Painting of Sino-Chilean Friendship to the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1960-10-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 14th dispatch: The Chilean peace activist and prominent painter, Mr. José Venturelli, today presented the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association with a large-scale color oil painting he recently created, symbolizing the friendship between the peoples of China and Chile.

The oil painting is three meters high and more than two meters wide. The scene uses the red soil mountains and pines and other trees of Chile as a background. In the foreground, a Chilean in black national costume is talking cordially with a group of farmers standing in front of the Chilean flag. Mr. Venturelli explained that the person in black represents Chilean figures who have visited China and is giving an introduction to the Chilean people about the new China.

At today's presentation, Mr. Venturelli and Mr. Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, delivered speeches, both warmly wishing for the continuous development of friendship between the Chinese people and the Chilean and Latin American peoples. Present at the presentation were Zhou Erfu, Vice President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, Wang Mingyuan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Wu Zuoren, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Artists Association, and others. Also present were Mr. Venturelli's wife and Chilean experts working in Beijing.

### 智利著名画家万徒勒里  向中拉友协赠送中智友谊的油画

1960-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

    智利著名画家万徒勒里
    向中拉友协赠送中智友谊的油画
    新华社14日讯　智利和平人士、著名画家何塞·万徒勒里先生今天把他最近创作的象征中国和智利人民友谊的巨幅彩色油画，赠给了中国拉丁美洲友好协会。
    这幅油画高达三米，宽有二米多。画面以智利的红色土壤的山和苍松等树木为背景，前面有一位身穿黑色民族服装的智利人，同站立在智利国旗前面的一群农民亲切谈话。万徒勒里先生解释说，身穿黑色服装的人代表曾经访问过中国的智利人士，他正向智利人民作着关于新中国的介绍。
   在今天赠画的时候，万徒勒里先生和中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南先后致词，都热情祝愿中国人民同智利人民和拉丁美洲人民的友谊不断发展。赠画的时候在场的，有中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副秘书长王明远，中国美术家协会副主席吴作人等。在场的还有万徒勒里先生的夫人以及在北京工作的智利专家。
