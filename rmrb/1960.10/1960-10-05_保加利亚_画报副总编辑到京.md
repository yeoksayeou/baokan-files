Title: Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "Bulgaria" Pictorial Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 4th: Piotyr Yachev, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "Bulgaria" Pictorial, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to our country according to the 1960 implementation plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### “保加利亚”画报副总编辑到京

1960-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    “保加利亚”画报副总编辑到京
    新华社4日讯　根据中保文化合作协定1960年执行计划前来我国访问的“保加利亚”画报副总编辑彼奥特·雅切夫，今天乘飞机到达北京。
