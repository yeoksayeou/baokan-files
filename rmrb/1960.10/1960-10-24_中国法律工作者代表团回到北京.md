Title: Chinese Legal Workers Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: The Chinese legal workers delegation of eight people, led by Zhang Youyu, head of the delegation, and Ke Bonian, deputy head of the delegation, who participated in the Seventh Congress of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers in Sofia, returned to Beijing by plane today.

### 中国法律工作者代表团回到北京

1960-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中国法律工作者代表团回到北京
    新华社23日讯　参加在索非亚举行的国际民主法律工作者协会第七届大会的中国法律工作者代表团一行八人，在团长张友渔、副团长柯柏年率领下，今天乘飞机回到北京。
