Title: Vice Premier Li Zhouyuan Leads Korean Government Economic Delegation to Visit Our Country; Vice Premier Li Xiannian Meets with Delegation Members
Author:
Date: 1960-10-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 4th: Li Zhouyuan, head of the government economic delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Vice Premier of the Korean Cabinet, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.

Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian welcomed the distinguished guests at the airport. Also present at the airport to welcome the Korean guests were: Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Chemical Industry Li Su, Vice Minister of the Third Ministry of Machine Building Liu Ding, Vice Minister of Posts and Telecommunications Shen Guang, President of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association Li Dequan, Vice Mayor of Beijing Jia Tingsan, and others.

Members of the Korean government economic delegation included: Vice Minister of Trade Kim Choi-sun and Ambassador to China Li Yong-ho. Vice Minister Kim Choi-sun and members of the delegation had arrived in Beijing earlier and participated in the celebrations of the People's Republic of China. They and Ambassador Li Yong-ho also went to the airport to welcome the delegation today.

After Vice Premier Li Zhouyuan got off the plane, he warmly shook hands with Vice Premier Li Xiannian and accepted flowers presented by Young Pioneers.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 4th: The government economic delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Vice Premier of the Korean Cabinet Li Zhouyuan, left Pyongyang by special plane this morning for a visit to the People's Republic of China.

Among those seeing them off at the airport were Kim Il, Hong Myung-hee, Park Jung-ae, Park Kum-chul, Kim Chang-man, Jung Il-ryong, Nam Il, Rim Hae, Jung Jun-taek and other party and government leaders, as well as Foreign Minister Park Sung-chul.

Our country's ambassador to Korea, Qiao Xiaoguang, and embassy staff also saw them off at the airport.

Xinhua News Agency, October 4th: Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian met this afternoon with Li Zhouyuan, head of the government economic delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Vice Premier of the Korean Cabinet, and members of the delegation, including Vice Minister of Trade Kim Choi-sun and Ambassador to China Li Yong-ho, and held cordial talks.

Present at the meeting were Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission Song Yangchu, and others. (Attached picture)

### 率领朝政府经济代表团访问我国  李周渊副首相到京  李先念副总理会见代表团人员

1960-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

    率领朝政府经济代表团访问我国
    李周渊副首相到京
    李先念副总理会见代表团人员
    新华社4日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府经济代表团团长、朝鲜内阁副首相李周渊，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    国务院副总理李先念到机场欢迎贵宾。到机场欢迎朝鲜贵宾的还有：对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，对外贸易部副部长李强，化学工业部副部长李苏，第三机械工业部副部长刘鼎，邮电部副部长申光，中朝友好协会会长李德全，北京市副市长贾庭三等。
    朝鲜政府经济代表团团员有：贸易省副相金最善、驻华大使李永镐。金最善副相和代表团随员已先期到达北京，并且参加了中华人民共和国庆典。他们和李永镐大使今天也到机场欢迎。
    李周渊副首相下飞机后，和李先念副总理热烈握手，并接受了少先队队员的献花。
    新华社平壤4日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府经济代表团，由朝鲜内阁副首相李周渊率领，今天早晨乘专机离平壤前往中华人民共和国进行访问。
    到机场送行的有金一、洪命熹、朴正爱、朴金喆、金昌满、郑一龙、南日、林海、郑准泽等党和政府领导人以及外务相朴成哲。
    我国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光和使馆人员也到机场送行。
    新华社4日讯　国务院副总理李先念，今天下午会见了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府经济代表团团长、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相李周渊和代表团团员贸易省副相金最善、驻华大使李永镐等，并进行了亲切会谈。
  会见时在座的，有对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，对外贸易部副部长李强，国家计划委员会副主任宋养初等。（附图片）
