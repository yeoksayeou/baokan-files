Title: Two Members of the Venezuelan Cultural Delegation Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 9th October. Dr. Raúl Serra Piñerúa, lawyer, and Dr. Diego Silva Ortiz, members of the Venezuelan Cultural Delegation who are visiting our country at the invitation of the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

### 委内瑞拉文化代表团两团员到京

1960-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    委内瑞拉文化代表团两团员到京
    新华社9日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请来我国访问的委内瑞拉文化代表团成员、律师劳尔·塞拉·皮内鲁阿博士和迭戈·席尔瓦·奥尔蒂斯博士，今天乘飞机到达北京。
