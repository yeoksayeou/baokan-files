Title: Chairman Liu Receives Iraqi Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 9th: Chairman Liu Shaoqi received the entire members of the Iraqi Military Friendship Delegation headed by Brigadier General Mahmoud Abdel Razak this evening and held friendly talks.

Present at the reception were General Luo Ruiqing, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General of the Air Force Chang Qiankun, and Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, He Ying, among others.

The Iraqi Republic's Ambassador to China, Fadil, was also present.

Chairman Liu Shaoqi shakes hands warmly with Brigadier General Mahmoud Abdel Razak, head of the Iraqi Military Friendship Delegation. Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao.

### 刘主席接见伊拉克军事代表团

1960-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见伊拉克军事代表团
    新华社9日讯　刘少奇主席今晚接见了以马哈茂德·阿卜杜勒·拉扎克准将为首的伊拉克军事友好代表团全体成员，并进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有罗瑞卿大将、萧向荣中将、常乾坤空军中将，外交部西亚非洲司司长何英等。
    伊拉克共和国驻我国大使法迪尔也在座。（附图片）
    刘少奇主席和伊拉克军事友好代表团团长马哈茂德·阿卜杜勒·拉扎克准将亲切握手　　　　　新华社记者　孟庆彪摄
