Title: Kim Il-sung Receives Our Military Friendship Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-10-25
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 24th: Premier Kim Il-sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today received all members of the People's Republic of China Military Friendship Delegation visiting Korea, led by Marshal He Long, Vice-Chairman of the National Defense Council and Vice-Premier of the State Council, and General Luo Ruiqing, Vice-Premier of the State Council and Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

    Present at the meeting on the Korean side were: Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea; Kim Il, First Vice-Premier of the Cabinet; Park Jung-ae, Park Kum-chul, Kim Chang-man and other party and government leaders who are Vice-Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea; General Kim Kwang-hyup, Vice-Premier of the Cabinet and Minister of National Defense; and senior generals of the Korean People's Army.

    Also received were Qiao Xiaoguang, Chinese Ambassador to Korea, and Senior Colonel Zhang Leting, Military Attaché.

    Premier Kim Il-sung had a cordial conversation with all members of the delegation and took a group photo with them.

    Premier Kim Il-sung also hosted a luncheon for the delegation. (Attached photo)

### 金日成接见我军事友好代表团

1960-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    金日成接见我军事友好代表团
    新华社平壤24日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成，今天接见了由中国国防委员会副主席、国务院副总理贺龙元帅，国务院副总理、中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿大将率领的中华人民共和国访朝军事友好代表团的全体人员。
    接见时朝鲜方面在座的有：朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健，内阁第一副首相金一，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、朴金喆、金昌满等党政领导人，内阁副首相兼民族保卫相金光侠大将和朝鲜人民军高级将领等。
    同时受接见的有中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光和武官张乐亭上校。
    金日成首相同代表团全体人员进行了亲切的谈话，并在一起合影。
    金日成首相还同代表团共进午餐。（附图片）
