Title: Soviet Embassy in Germany Announces Regulations for West Berlin Citizens Visiting the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin: The Soviet Embassy in the German Democratic Republic announced that new regulations regarding the issuance of visas to West Berlin citizens visiting the Soviet Union have come into effect as of September 15, 1960. The full text of the regulations is as follows:

    West Berlin citizens holding West Berlin identity cards may apply for entry permits at all Soviet consulates. Passports issued to West Berlin citizens by the West German government will not be recognized as documents for West Berlin citizens to use when applying to enter the Soviet Union.

### 苏联驻德大使馆  公布西柏林公民访苏条例

1960-10-07
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联驻德大使馆
    公布西柏林公民访苏条例
    据新华社柏林讯　苏联驻德意志民主共和国大使馆宣布，关于发给西柏林公民访问苏联的签证的新条例自1960年9月15日起生效。条例全文如下：
    持有西柏林身份证的西柏林公民可以向所有苏联领事馆申请入境许可。西德政府发给西柏林公民的护照将不被承认为是西柏林公民用来申请进入苏联的文件。
