Title: Beijing Synthetic Fiber Factory Holds Meeting to Celebrate the National Day of the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1960-10-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The Beijing Synthetic Fiber Experimental Factory held a meeting on the 6th to warmly celebrate the 11th anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.

    The Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Mr. Wang Dairen, and diplomatic officials from the embassy attended the celebration and were warmly welcomed by the workers.

    Mr. Wang Yuanfang, Secretary-General of the Sino-German Friendship Association, also attended the celebration.

    Zhang Mei, the director of the Beijing Synthetic Fiber Experimental Factory, spoke at the meeting. On behalf of all the factory workers, she warmly congratulated the German people on the festival and expressed her gratitude to the government, people, and experts of the German Democratic Republic for the assistance they provided in the construction of this factory.

    Ambassador Wang Dairen also spoke at the meeting. He introduced the great achievements made by the German people in the cause of socialist construction under the leadership of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany over the past eleven years; and condemned the US imperialist plot to revive West German militarism and actively prepare for wars of aggression. In his speech, Ambassador Wang Dairen praised the unbreakable brotherly friendship between the Chinese and German people.

    The members of the Sino-German Friendship People's Commune in Dongba, Beijing, wrote a letter to the German Ambassador to China, Mr. Wang Dairen, on the 7th to congratulate the German Democratic Republic on its National Day.

### 庆祝德意志民主共和国国庆  北京合成纤维工厂集会

1960-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝德意志民主共和国国庆
    北京合成纤维工厂集会
    新华社7日讯　北京合成纤维实验工厂6日举行集会，热烈庆祝德意志民主共和国成立十一周年。
    德意志民主共和国驻中国大使汪戴尔和使馆的外交官员参加了庆祝会，受到职工们的热烈欢迎。
    中德友好协会秘书长王元方等也参加了庆祝会。
    北京合成纤维实验工厂厂长张梅在会上讲了话。她代表全厂职工向德国人民热烈祝贺节日，并对德意志民主共和国政府、人民和专家们在这个工厂兴建过程中所给予的援助表示感谢。
    汪戴尔大使也在会上讲了话。他介绍了德国人民在德国统一社会党领导下十一年来在社会主义建设事业中所取得的巨大成就；并且谴责了美帝国主义复活西德军国主义积极准备侵略战争的阴谋活动。汪戴尔大使在讲话中赞扬了中德两国人民牢不可破的兄弟友谊。
    北京东坝中德友好人民公社的社员们，7日写信给德国驻中国大使汪戴尔祝贺德意志民主共和国国庆。
