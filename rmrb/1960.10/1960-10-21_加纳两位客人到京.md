Title: Two Guests from Ghana Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 20th. Kofi Badu, a reporter from the Ghanaian "Times" and Deputy Secretary of the Ghana Press Club, and Emmanuel William Agyaye, Assistant News Editor of the "Daily Graphic," arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association.

### 加纳两位客人到京

1960-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    加纳两位客人到京
    新华社20日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问的加纳“时报”记者、加纳新闻俱乐部副秘书科菲·巴杜和“每日写真报”助理新闻编辑伊曼纽尔·威廉·阿贾耶，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
