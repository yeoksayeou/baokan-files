Title: Japanese National Railway and Coal Mine Union Delegations Leave Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Japanese National Railway Workers' Union delegation of thirteen people, headed by Soichi Hosoi, left Beijing by train this morning to visit various places in the south.

The Japanese Coal Miners' Union delegation of ten people, headed by Reijiro Takagaki, left Beijing by train the day before yesterday evening to visit various places in the south.

### 日本国营铁路和煤矿两工会代表团离北京

1960-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

    日本国营铁路和煤矿两工会代表团离北京
    新华社18日讯　以细井宗一为首的日本国营铁路工会代表团一行十三人，今天早晨乘火车离开北京去南方各地参观访问。
    以高垣礼二郎为首的日本煤矿工会代表团一行十人，已于前天晚上乘火车离开北京到南方各地参观访问。
