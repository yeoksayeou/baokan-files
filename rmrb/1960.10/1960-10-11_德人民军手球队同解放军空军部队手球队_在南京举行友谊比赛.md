Title: East German People's Army Handball Team Plays Friendly Match with PLA Air Force Handball Team in Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1960-10-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, October 9th. The East German Democratic Republic National People's Army Handball Team defeated the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force Handball Team by a score of 20-11 in their third friendly match in China this afternoon. Fu Quejian, Major General of the Nanjing Military Region, Guan Wenwei, Vice Governor of Jiangsu Province, and other leaders watched the match and met with all members of both teams after the game.

The visiting team arrived in Nanjing by plane from Beijing this morning. On the afternoon of the 11th, they will also play their fourth friendly match in China against the Chinese People's Liberation Army Nanjing Military Region Handball Team.

### 德人民军手球队同解放军空军部队手球队  在南京举行友谊比赛

1960-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    德人民军手球队同解放军空军部队手球队
    在南京举行友谊比赛
    新华社南京9日电　德意志民主共和国国家人民军手球队在今天下午，同中国人民解放军空军部队手球队举行访华第三场友谊比赛时，以二十比十一获胜。南京部队负责人符确健少将、江苏省副省长管文蔚等观看了比赛，并在比赛结束后，接见了双方球队全体人员。
    客队于今晨由北京乘飞机到达南京。11日下午，还将同中国人民解放军南京部队手球队举行访华第四场友谊赛。
