Title: CPSU Central Committee Plenary Session to be Held in December
Author:
Date: 1960-10-31
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 29th—TASS reported: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union has decided to convene a plenary session on December 13, 1960.
　　At this plenary session, the implementation of the national plan and the socialist pledges in the production and sale of agricultural and livestock products to the state in 1960, as well as measures for the further development of agriculture, will be discussed.

### 苏共中央全会十二月举行

1960-10-31
第6版()
专栏：

    苏共中央全会十二月举行
    新华社莫斯科29日电　据塔斯社讯：苏联共产党中央委员会决定于1960年12月13日召开全会。
    在这次全会上，将讨论关于国家计划执行情况和1960年在生产和向国家交售农牧产品方面的社会主义保证执行情况、以及关于进一步发展农业的措施的问题。
