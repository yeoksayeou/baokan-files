Title: First Secretary Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-10-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 28th—Brotodiningrat, First Secretary of the Presidential Office of Indonesia, and his wife, concluded their visit to our country and left Guangzhou today via Shenzhen to return home.

　　The guests arrived in Guangzhou on the 25th from Hangzhou. On the evening of the 26th, Li Guangxiang, Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou, hosted a farewell banquet. During their stay in Guangzhou, the guests visited the China Export Commodities Fair.

### 第一秘书回国

1960-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    第一秘书回国
    新华社广州28日电　印度尼西亚总统府办公厅第一秘书布罗托迪宁格拉特和他的夫人结束了在我国的访问，今天离开广州经深圳回国。
    客人们是25日从杭州到达广州的。26日晚上，广州市副市长李广祥曾设宴欢送。客人们在广州期间，曾参观了中国出口商品陈列馆。
