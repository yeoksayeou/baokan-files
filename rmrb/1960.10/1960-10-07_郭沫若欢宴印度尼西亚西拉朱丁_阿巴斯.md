Title: Guo Moruo Hosts a Banquet for Indonesian Sila Juddin Abbas
Author:
Date: 1960-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th October: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome Sila Juddin Abbas, Chairman of the Indonesian Peace Committee, member of the Cooperative Parliament, and his wife.

Among those attending the banquet were Burhan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Li Dequan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee, Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, Da Pongsheng, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association, and Jin Zhonghua, Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association.

### 郭沫若欢宴印度尼西亚西拉朱丁·阿巴斯

1960-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若欢宴印度尼西亚西拉朱丁·阿巴斯
    新华社6日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若今晚设宴欢迎印度尼西亚和平委员会主席之 一、合作议会议员西拉朱丁·阿巴斯和夫人。
    参加宴会的还有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席包尔汉、中国亚非团结委员会副主席李德全、中国印度尼西亚友好协会副会长胡愈之、中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生、中国新闻工作者协会副主席金仲华等。
