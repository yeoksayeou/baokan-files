Title: Liu Baiyu hosts a banquet for Albanian Musaraj
Author:
Date: 1960-10-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: Liu Baiyu, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association and Vice President of the China-Albania Friendship Association, held a banquet tonight in honor of Shevqet Musaraj, Secretary-General of the Albanian Writers and Artists Association.

Misto Treska, Chairman of the Albanian Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, was invited to attend the banquet.  Also present at the banquet were Yan Wenjing, Zhang Guangnian, Guo Xiaochuan, members of the Secretariat of the Chinese Writers Association, and Yu Mengyou, Deputy Director of the Department of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 刘白羽欢宴阿尔巴尼亚穆萨莱

1960-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    刘白羽欢宴阿尔巴尼亚穆萨莱
    新华社25日讯　中国作家协会副主席、中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会副会长刘白羽今天晚上举行宴会，招待阿尔巴尼亚作家艺术家协会秘书长谢夫卡特·穆萨莱。
    阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会主席米斯托·特雷斯卡应邀出席了宴会。出席宴会作陪的，有中国作家协会书记处书记严文井、张光年、郭小川和对外文化联络委员会副司长于梦尤。
