Title: Local Armed Forces of Henan and Hubei Annihilate Over 300 Bandits
Author:
Date: 1948-01-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Front Line of Henan, Shaanxi and Hubei, 27th: Local armed forces of the Fourth and Fifth Military Sub-districts annihilated over 300 bandits this month. On the 20th, the local armed forces of the Fourth Military Sub-district jointly attacked the Kuomintang township offices of the Tangs, north and south, in the Qinglong Mountain area at the junction of Henan and Hubei, capturing over 60 bandits, seizing one 60mm mortar, two light machine guns, fifty rifles, and over 3,000 rounds of ammunition. On the 16th, the local armed forces of the Fifth Military Sub-district captured alive Chen Zhiyuan, the Kuomintang district chief, and 191 others south of Lushan city, killing two, and seizing 75 long and short guns. Another unit captured Li Wenxiang, the bandit leader, and 50 others in Chenya Village, Lushan, seizing one launcher, six submachine guns, and 24 rifles. On the 21st, another unit captured Li Shunzhen, the attached colonel of the 1st Column of the Supervisory Column of the Xi'an Pacification Headquarters, and 19 others in Wangzhuang area, Linru, seizing 21 long and short guns.

### 豫鄂地方武装歼散匪三百余

1948-01-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　豫鄂地方武装歼散匪三百余
    【新华社豫陕鄂前线二十七日电】四、五分区地方武装本月中旬共歼灭散匪三百余名。四分区地武二十日在豫鄂交界的青龙山地区，合击南北唐蒋匪乡公所，俘匪六十余名，缴六○炮一门，轻机枪二挺，步枪五十支，子弹三千余发。五分区地武十六日在鲁山城南生俘蒋匪区长陈志远以下一百九十一名，击毙二名，缴长短枪七十五支。另部又在鲁山陈崖村，俘匪首李文祥以下五十名，缴发射筒一个，冲锋枪六支，步枪二十四支。二十一日另部在临汝王庄地区俘匪西安绥署督导纵队一纵队上校支队附李舜真以下十九名，缴长短枪二十一支。
