Title: Lithographic Stone for Sale
Author:
Date: 1948-01-24
Page: 1

　　Lithographic Stone for Sale
    The lithographic stone mined in this county is of excellent quality. After experiments in various places, it is no different from foreign stone. Now, to facilitate purchases from all regions, a sales office has been established in Linqing City, Shandong Province. Those who wish to purchase are requested to contact Heji Printing House at No. 11 Yangji Yuan Street.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Finance Section of the Lin County Government, Hejian Cultural Bookstore
