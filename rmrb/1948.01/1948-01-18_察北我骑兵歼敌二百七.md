Title: Our Cavalry in Chahar North Annihilates 270 Enemies
Author:
Date: 1948-01-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Jicha Rehe Liao, 16th—A unit of the People's Liberation Army's iron cavalry in northern Chahar, on the night of the 5th, captured Chiang Kai-shek and puppet strongholds such as Bu Dianshu Temple and Ertaizi, northwest of Zhangbei, annihilating more than 50 members of the Zhangbei Security Police Force. On the morning of the 6th, another unit of our troops annihilated a part of the Third Regiment of the Fourteenth Column of the Fu bandit in the Liu Wangyingzi area, ten miles east of Ertaizi. On the same day, another unit of the People's Liberation Army fiercely attacked the Chongli puppet bandit security police force in the Dongjia Yao area, north of Ertaizi, annihilating 92 enemies. In the above three battles, a total of more than 100 enemies were killed or wounded, and one acting regimental commander of the 40th bandit column, two captains of the Zhangbei and Chongli puppet security police, one puppet police chief, and more than 170 officers and soldiers were captured. Four light machine guns, three grenade launchers, 150 rifles and horses, and more than 90 war horses were seized. More than 2,000 piculs of grain seized were distributed to the masses.

### 察北我骑兵歼敌二百七

1948-01-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　察北我骑兵歼敌二百七
    【新华社冀察热辽十六日电】察北解放军铁骑一部，五日夜攻克张北西北卜垫树庙、二台子等蒋伪据点，歼传匪张北保警队五十余名。六日晨我某部又在二台子东十里的刘旺营子一带，将傅匪十四纵队第三团一部消灭。同日，解放军另一部则在二台子以北董家窑一带，痛击崇礼伪匪保警队，歼敌九十二名。以上三次战斗共毙伤敌百余名，俘匪四十纵队代团长一名，张北、崇礼伪保警队长两名，伪公安局长一名，及官兵一百七十余名，缴获轻机枪四挺，掷弹筒三个，步马枪一百五十支，战马九十余匹，并将缴获的两千多石粮食，全部分给群众。
