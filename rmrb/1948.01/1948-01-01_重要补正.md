　　Important Correction
    In the thirty-first issue of this newspaper, on the front page, fourteenth line from the bottom of the headline news, "three municipal committee leadership organs were abolished" is a mistake, it should read "three county and municipal committee leadership organs were abolished."

### 重要补正

1948-01-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　重要补正
    三十一日本报一版头条新闻倒数十四行“有三个市委领导机关被撤销”，是“有三个县市委领导机关被撤销”之误。
