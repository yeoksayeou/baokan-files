Title: Soviet Union Grandly Commemorates Lenin; Southeast European Countries Also Hold Memorial Meetings
Author:
Date: 1948-01-30
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 27th: Reports from various sources: January 21st marked the 24th anniversary of the death of Lenin, the great teacher of the world proletariat revolution. Grand commemorative meetings were held throughout the Soviet Union. At the Moscow commemorative meeting, Stalin, Molotov, Zhdanov, and others were elected to the presidium, and Suslov delivered a report. The ceremony was solemn and grand. In Yugoslavia, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Hungary, Romania, and Bulgaria, commemorative meetings and exhibitions on Lenin's life and work were held everywhere. Newspapers published editorials in commemoration, prominently featuring Lenin's photograph and commemorative articles on the front page. Many theaters also screened the film "Lenin in October."
　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 27th: Moscow dispatch: According to TASS statistics, from the date of Lenin's death in 1924 to the 20th of this month, 24 million people from all over the world have visited Lenin's Mausoleum in Moscow to pay their respects. Furthermore, from 1917 to the beginning of this month, the Soviet publishing houses have printed 164,007,300 copies of Lenin's works, which have been translated into 77 languages.

### 苏联隆重纪念列宁　东南欧各国也举行纪念大会

1948-01-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　苏联隆重纪念列宁
  　东南欧各国也举行纪念大会
    【新华社陕北二十七日电】各方讯：二十一日为世界无产阶级革命导师列宁逝世二十四周年纪念，全苏各地均举行盛大纪念会。莫斯科纪念会上，斯大林、莫洛托夫、日丹诺夫等被选为主席团，由苏斯洛夫作报告，仪式庄严隆重。任南斯拉夫、捷克、波兰、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、保加利亚各国，亦到处举行纪念大会与列宁生平事业展览会，各报纷纷发表社论纪念，并以首页显要地位刊载列宁照片与纪念专文，并有多数戏院放映“列宁在十月”的影片。
    【新华社陕北二十七日电】莫斯科讯：据塔斯社发表统计，自一九二四年列宁逝世之日起，迄本月二十日止，世界各地人民赴莫斯科列宁陵墓瞻仰遗容者，已达二千四百万人。又自一九一七年至本月初为止，苏联书局印行之列宁著作，已达一亿六千四百万零七万三千本，并已译成七十七种文字。
