Title: Foster Discusses CPUSA Policy: Opposing Capitalism and Striving for Socialism Conforms to America's Vital Interests and Needs
Author:
Date: 1948-01-20
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, January 17th. TASS, New York, January 13th: Eugene Dennis, Chairman of the Communist Party of the United States, recently, when meeting with reporters from the New York "Daily Worker" to answer various questions concerning the Communist platform and policies, emphasized that the ambition of American monopoly capitalists to establish world hegemony threatens the peace and freedom of all nations. Foster said: The Soviet Union has today reached the highest degree of democracy in the world. He also said: American communists believe that the struggle against moribund world capitalism and for socialism is in line with America's vital interests and needs.

### 福斯特同志谈美共政策  反对资本主义争取社会主义符合美国的切身利益和需要

1948-01-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　福斯特同志谈美共政策
    反对资本主义争取社会主义符合美国的切身利益和需要
    【新华社陕北十七日电】塔斯社纽约十三日电：美国共产党主席福斯特，近在接见纽约先论坛报记者答复有关共产党纲领和政策的各种问题时，强调指出：美独占资本家要想建立世界霸权，这正威胁着一切国家的和平与自由。福斯特说：苏联今日已达到世界上最高度的民主。他又说：美国共产党人相信，反对趋于覆灭的世界资本主义和争取社会主义的斗争，是与美国的切身利益和需要符合的。
