Title: Traitor Chiang Sells Out Chinese Ports, Actually Allows US Warships Free Access

Author: 
Date: 1948-01-31
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 28th. According to an Agence France-Presse dispatch from Nanjing on the 23rd: The traitor Chiang Kai-shek's traitorous government has already allowed US warships free entry into Chinese ports "designated" by the Chiang bandit. It is alleged that the selling of this right is one of the exchange conditions for US imperialist aid in training Chiang's troops.

### 蒋贼出卖中国口岸　竟允许美军舰自由进出

1948-01-31
第2版()
专栏：

　　蒋贼出卖中国口岸
  　竟允许美军舰自由进出
    【新华社陕北二十八日电】据法新社南京二十三日消息透露：蒋介石卖国政府已允许美国军舰自由进入经蒋匪“指定”之中国口岸。据称：此项权利之出卖，为美帝国主义助蒋训练军队的交换条件之一。
