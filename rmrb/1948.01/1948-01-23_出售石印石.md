Title: Sale of Lithographic Limestone

Author:
Date: 1948-01-23
Page: 1

　　Sale of Lithographic Limestone
    The lithographic limestone mined in this county is of excellent quality. Experiments in various places have shown it to be no different from foreign products. To facilitate purchases from all regions, a sales agency has been established in Linqing City, Shandong Province. Those wishing to purchase may contact Heji Printing House at No. 11 Yangji Yuan Street.

    　　　　　　　　　　　　Finance Section of the Lin County Government
    　　　　　　　　　　　　Hejian Cultural Bookstore

### 出售石印石

1948-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　出售石印石
    本县开采之石印石，品质优良，经各地实验与外国右无异，现为便利各地购买，特在山东临清市设立代销处，如愿购者，请到养济院街门牌十一号合记印刷局接洽。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　林县县政府财政科
    　　　　　　　　　　　　合涧文化书店
