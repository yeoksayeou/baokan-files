Title: Rapid Development of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, with 1.3 Million Members
Author:
Date: 1948-01-24
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, January 22nd: Prague dispatch: The Czechoslovak Communist Party, the largest political party in Czechoslovakia, has been rapidly developing. As of January 1st, it has 1,310,444 members (excluding the Slovak Communist Party). Last year alone, 266,000 people joined the party.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, January 22nd: Prague dispatch: The Czechoslovak government recently announced that the first year (i.e., 1947) of the Two-Year Plan for industrial production has been successfully completed and exceeded. The steel industry achieved the most brilliant results, exceeding the plan by 11 percent. The main industrial production figures are: 38,570,000 tons of coal, 1,364,000 tons of iron, 1,422,000 tons of cast iron, 2,285,000 tons of steel, and 6,662,000,000 kilowatt-hours of electricity. Furthermore, in order to promote planned economy and stimulate the development of nationalized industries, the Czech government has passed a decision to centralize and specialize state-owned banks, so that each sector of the national economy has its own specialized bank. In addition, the government has also passed a law to subsidize nationalized industries.

### 捷克共产党发展得快已有党员一百三十万

1948-01-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　捷克共产党发展得快已有党员一百三十万
    【新华社陕北二十二日电】布拉格讯：捷克最大政党共产党迅速发展，截至一月一日止，已拥有党员一百三十一万零四百四十四人，（斯洛伐克共产党不计在内）去年一年入党者即达二十六万六千人。
    【新华社陕北二十二日电】布拉格讯：捷克政府日前公布二年计划的第一年（即一九四七年）工业生产计划，已顺利完成并超过，其中钢铁工业成就最为辉煌，计超过计划百分之十一。主要工业生产数字计为：煤三千八百五十七万吨、铁一百三十六万四千吨、铸铁一百四十二万二千吨、钢二百二十八万五千吨、电力六十六亿六千二百万千瓦。又讯：捷政府为促进计划经济与刺激国有化工业之发展，已通过国有银行集中化与专业化之决定，使国民经济各部门各有其专业银行。此外，政府亦通过资助国有化工业之法律。
