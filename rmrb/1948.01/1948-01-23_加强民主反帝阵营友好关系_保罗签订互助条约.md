Title: Strengthening Friendly Relations within the Democratic Anti-Imperialist Camp: Poland and Romania Sign Mutual Assistance Treaty
Author:
Date: 1948-01-23
Page: 2

　　Strengthening Friendly Relations within the Democratic Anti-Imperialist Camp
  　Poland and Romania Sign Mutual Assistance Treaty
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 21st] Bucharest dispatch: After Bulgarian Prime Minister Dimitrov arrived in Romania on the 14th with a Bulgarian delegation, he and Romanian Prime Minister Groza signed a treaty of friendship, cooperation, and mutual assistance here on the 16th. Before signing, Dimitrov delivered a speech stating: "This treaty will guarantee peace for the people of the Balkans and strengthen the friendly relations between countries in the democratic anti-imperialist camp led by the Soviet Union." Groza and Romanian Foreign Minister Mrs. Pauker both emphasized in their speeches that this treaty is of great significance to the friendship and unity of the people of both countries. When the signing ceremony was held, the people of the Romanian capital held a mass rally in "Victory Square" to warmly celebrate.

### 加强民主反帝阵营友好关系　保罗签订互助条约

1948-01-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　加强民主反帝阵营友好关系
  　保罗签订互助条约
    【新华社陕北二十一日电】布加勒斯特讯：保加利亚总理季米特洛夫，十四日率领保代表团抵罗后，已于十六日与罗马尼亚总理格罗查在此间签订友好合作互助条约。季米特洛夫在签字前发表演说称：“此项条约，将保障巴尔干人民之和平，并加强苏联为首之民主反帝阵营各国间友好关系。”格罗查与罗外长波克夫人致辞时，均强调此约对两国人民之友好团结具有重要意义。当签字仪式举行时，罗京人民曾在“胜利广场”举行群众大会，热烈庆祝。
