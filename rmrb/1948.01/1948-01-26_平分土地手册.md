　　Handbook for Equal Land Distribution
    The Central Bureau Propaganda Department has decided that all levels of Party, government, military, civilian organizations, and groups, from leaders to janitorial staff, must study the Outline Land Law of China and related documents. It also stated that every comrade must study seriously and responsibly. They must not only study themselves but also publicize to the masses and explain their questions.
    To facilitate comrades' study and work needs, this handbook is specially compiled by collecting various documents. This handbook will be published soon and will include the Outline Land Law of China, the Border Government's Supplementary Measures on the Implementation of the Outline Land Law, the Border Government's Proclamation Supporting the Land Law, the Military Region's Order to Troops for Implementing the Land Law, Letter to Party Members, Letter to Peasants, Provisional Regulations on Punishing Sabotage of Land Reform, and the Border Government's Regulations on Punishing Corruption, and other documents.

### 平分土地手册

1948-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　平分土地手册
    中央局宣传部决定：各级党政军民机关团体，自首长至杂务人员，必须学习土地法大纲，以及有关的文件。并说：每个同志，均须以严肃负责态度，认真进行学习。不仅自己进行学习，还须向群众进行宣传，给群众解释疑难问题。
    为便于同志们学习及工作上需要起见，兹特搜集各文件编印这本手册。本手册即将出版，计包括中国土地法大纲、边府关于执行土地法大纲补充办法、边府拥护土地法布告、军区给部队执行土地法命令、告党员书、告农民书、破坏土地改革治罪暂行条例、边府惩治贪污条例等文件。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　华北新华书店发行
