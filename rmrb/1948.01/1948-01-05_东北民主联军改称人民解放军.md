Title: Northeast Democratic Allied Army Renamed People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1948-01-05
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 31st: The Headquarters of the Northeast Democratic Allied Army recently issued the following notice: "In order to unify the title of the People's Liberation Army throughout the country, Commander Lin suggested to the Central Military Commission and obtained the approval of the Central Military Commission to change the Northeast Democratic Allied Army to the Northeast People's Liberation Army. From January of the 37th year of the Republic of China (1948), the title of the Northeast Democratic Allied Army will be abolished and changed to the Northeast People's Liberation Army."

### 东北民主联军改称人民解放军

1948-01-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　东北民主联军改称人民解放军
    【新华社东北三十一日电】东北民主联军总部顷发布通告如下：“为着统一全国人民解放军的称号，经林总司令向中央军委建议并得中央军委批准，改东北民主联军为东北人民解放军，从民国三十七年（一九四八年）一月起，东北民主联军的称号一律废除，改称为东北人民解放军”。
