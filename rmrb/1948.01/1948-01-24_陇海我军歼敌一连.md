Title: Our Army on the Longhai Railway Annihilates an Enemy Company
Author:
Date: 1948-01-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Su, Lu, Yu, and Wan Frontline, 21st: A delayed report: A unit of the People's Liberation Army encountered a company of Chiang Kai-shek's 51st Reorganized Division south of the Liuhe Railway Station on the Longhai Railway on the morning of the 9th. They completely annihilated the company within minutes, capturing 85 enemy soldiers including the company commander, and seizing seven machine guns and more than 30 rifles and pistols. In addition, another unit of the People's Liberation Army struck a vehicle belonging to the "People's Service Team" dispatched by Chiang Kai-shek's Ministry of National Defense News Bureau to the 55th Reorganized Division south of Minquan on the Longhai Railway on the 8th, capturing the enemy's Deputy Captain Yang Xueren (with the rank of Captain), killing one member of the team, and seizing some weapons.

Xinhua News Agency, East China, 22nd: The Chiang Kai-shek bandits entrenched in the isolated cities of Penglai and Fushan on the north coast of Jiaodong are facing difficulties with food and ammunition, and their morale is low. From the 3rd to the 18th of last month, and from the 5th to the 8th of this month, a total of 47 people have defected to the liberated areas, bringing with them two American-made light machine guns and some military supplies.

### 陇海我军歼敌一连

1948-01-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　陇海我军歼敌一连
    【新华社苏鲁豫皖前线二十一日电】迟到消息：解放军一部，九日晨在陇海路柳河车站以南地区，与蒋匪整五十一师一个连遭遇，几分钟内全歼该连，活捉连长以下八十五名，缴获机枪七挺、长短枪三十余支。又：解放军另部，八日在陇海路民权以南地区，打下蒋匪国防部新闻局派赴整五十五师的“人民服务队”汽车一辆，俘敌上尉副队长杨学仁、打死队员一名，缴获武器一部。
    【新华社华东二十二日电】龟缩胶东北海岸蓬莱、福山两孤城的蒋匪，粮弹困难，士气低落，从上月三日到十八日，和本月五日到八日先后有四十七人投奔解放区，并带来美式轻机枪二挺和军用品一部。
