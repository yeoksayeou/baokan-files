Title: Important Correction
Author:
Date: 1948-01-16
Page: 1

　　Important Correction
    In the second paragraph, 19th line, of "Letter to Party Members" on page one of this newspaper on the 14th, the phrase "更谈不到政府、农会里办事 (gèng tán bù dào zhèngfǔ, nóng huì lǐ bànshì)" [even more out of the question to work in the government and farmers' associations] was mistakenly printed as "更谈不到到政府、农会里办事(gèng tán bù dào dào zhèngfǔ, nóng huì lǐ bànshì)" [even more out of the question to to work in the government and farmers' associations]. This correction is hereby made.
