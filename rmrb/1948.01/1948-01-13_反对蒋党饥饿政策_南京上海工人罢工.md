Title: Workers in Nanjing and Shanghai Strike Against Chiang Kai-shek's Starvation Policy
Author:
Date: 1948-01-13
Page: 2

　　Against Chiang Kai-shek's Starvation Policy
  　Workers in Nanjing and Shanghai Strike
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 10th—According to various sources: The labor unrest in Beijing and Shanghai during the New Year is developing. Four thousand barbers and three hundred laundry workers in Nanjing began a strike a few days ago and were still persisting as of the 9th of this month. They demanded wage increases and protested against the Chiang gang's Social Affairs Bureau's starvation policy towards workers. Customs employees in Shanghai began a work slowdown at the end of last month. As a result of petitions by employee representatives, Chiang gang's Minister of Finance, Yu Hongjun, openly declared, "Their full demands cannot be met." As of the 6th of this month, employees driven by hunger were still resisting.

### 反对蒋党饥饿政策　南京上海工人罢工

1948-01-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　反对蒋党饥饿政策
  　南京上海工人罢工
    【新华社陕北十日电】据各方消息：京沪年关工潮正在发展中。南京理发业四千工人及洗衣业三百工人于日前开始罢工，至本月九日尚在继续坚持，他们要求增加工资，并抗议蒋匪社会局对工人的饥饿政策。上海海关职工于上月底开始怠工，职工代表请愿结果，蒋匪财政部长俞鸿钧公然宣称：“不能答应他们的全部要求”，至本月六日为饥饿逼迫的职工仍在抗争。
