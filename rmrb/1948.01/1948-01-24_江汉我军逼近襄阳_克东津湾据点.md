Title: Jianghan PLA Closes in on Xiangyang; Captures Dongjinwan Stronghold
Author:
Date: 1948-01-24
Page: 2

　　Jianghan PLA Closes in on Xiangyang
  　Captures Dongjinwan Stronghold
    [Xinhua News Agency, Hubei-Henan-Anhui Front, 22nd] A unit of the Jianghan Military Region's People's Liberation Army, on the dawn of the 20th, in one fell swoop, captured the Dongjinwan earthen stronghold east of Xiangyang city, held by local bandit Chiang Kai-shek's troops. Over 125 enemy troops, including a security battalion commander, were killed or captured. Two light machine guns and 104 rifles and pistols were seized. Chiang Kai-shek's bandit troops in Xiangyang were greatly shaken and immediately placed on high alert.

### 江汉我军逼近襄阳　克东津湾据点

1948-01-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　江汉我军逼近襄阳
  　克东津湾据点
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线二十二日电】江汉军区解放军一部二十日拂晓一举攻克襄阳城东岸之东津湾土蒋匪据点，毙俘守敌保安大队长以下一百二十五人，缴获轻机枪两挺，长短枪一○四支。襄阳蒋匪大为震动，紧急戒严。
