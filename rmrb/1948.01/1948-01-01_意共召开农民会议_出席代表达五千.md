Title: Italian Communist Party Holds Farmers' Conference; 5,000 Representatives Attend
Author:
Date: 1948-01-01
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 25th: Rome dispatch: Italian farmers and agricultural workers are strengthening their unity. On the 21st, the Italian Communist Party and the Socialist Party convened a conference of farmers and agricultural workers' associations in Bologna (northern Italy) to formulate a general program of their views on agricultural revitalization. Approximately 5,000 representatives attended, reflecting the Italian Communist Party's prestige and the magnitude of its strength among the peasantry.

### 意共召开农民会议　出席代表达五千

1948-01-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　意共召开农民会议
  　出席代表达五千
    【新华社陕北二十五日电】罗马讯：意大利农民与农业工人加强团结。二十一日，意共与社会党于波伦亚（意北）召开了农民与农业工人协会会议，以制订他们对于农业复兴意见的总纲领。出席代表人数达五千名左右，反映了意共在农民中的威信与力量的雄大。
