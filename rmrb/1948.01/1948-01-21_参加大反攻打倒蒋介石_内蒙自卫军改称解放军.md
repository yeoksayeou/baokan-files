Title: Participate in the Great Counteroffensive to Overthrow Chiang Kai-shek: Inner Mongolia Self-Defense Army Renamed People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1948-01-21
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 19th: The Inner Mongolia Communist Party Working Committee has decided that the Inner Mongolia People's Self-Defense Army will be renamed the Inner Mongolia People's Liberation Army. The decision stated: Our Inner Mongolia People's Self-Defense Army has carried out heroic and resolute struggles to protect the interests of the nation and the people. In the future, we will not only fight to protect the interests of the nation and the people, but also actively participate in the great counteroffensive, overthrow Chiang Kai-shek, and completely liberate all of Inner Mongolia. Therefore, it is decided that from January 1948, the title of Inner Mongolia People's Self-Defense Army will be abolished and renamed the Inner Mongolia People's Liberation Army.

### 参加大反攻打倒蒋介石　内蒙自卫军改称解放军

1948-01-21
第2版()
专栏：

　　参加大反攻打倒蒋介石
  　内蒙自卫军改称解放军
    【新华社东北十九日电】内蒙古共产党工作委员会决定：内蒙古人民自卫军改称内蒙古人民解放军。决定称：我们内蒙古人民自卫军为着保卫民族利益与人民利益，曾经作了英勇果敢的斗争，今后我们不仅要为保卫民族利益与人民利益奋斗，而且要积极参加大反攻，打倒蒋介石，彻底解放全内蒙，因此决定从一九四八年一月起，内蒙古人民自卫军的称号一律废除，改称为内蒙古人民解放军。
