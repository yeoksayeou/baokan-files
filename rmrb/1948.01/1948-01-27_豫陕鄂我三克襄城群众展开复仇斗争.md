Title: Henan-Shaanxi-Hubei Military Region, Our Troops Recapture Xiangcheng, Masses Launch Revenge Struggle
Author:
Date: 1948-01-27
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Henan-Shaanxi-Hubei Front, January 25th: A unit of the People's Liberation Army captured Xiangcheng for the third time on the 17th. Over thirty local Chiang bandits were captured, and one light machine gun and forty-nine rifles and pistols were seized. During our army's withdrawal from the city, landlords, despots, and local Chiang bandits carried out a reckoning against the people in the urban area, and frantically massacred seventy-two people. Now, the People's Liberation Army and the democratic county government are issuing clear orders to fully support the masses in launching a struggle against traitors and for revenge.

### 豫陕鄂我三克襄城群众展开复仇斗争

1948-01-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　豫陕鄂我三克襄城群众展开复仇斗争
    【新华社豫陕鄂前线二十五日电】解放军一部于十七日第三次攻克襄城。俘土蒋匪三十余名，缴轻机枪一挺、长短枪四十九支。在我撤离该城期间，地主恶霸及土蒋匪等曾向城关群众实行倒算，疯狂残杀群众达七十二人。现解放军与民主县政府正明令全力扶助群众展开反奸复仇斗争。
