Title: Chiang Kai-shek's Treasonous Acts Cause Serious Consequences: Trade Deficit Reaches New Record

Author:
Date: 1948-01-25
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 22nd—Chiang Kai-shek's controlled areas' foreign trade deficit reached a new record in October of last year. According to figures released by the Chiang Kai-shek customs, cited by Shanghai newspapers, the total value of goods imported into the Chiang Kai-shek controlled areas in October of last year was 1,746,766,627,000 Chiang Kai-shek currency, while the total value of exports was 530,040,000,000, less than one-third of imports. The deficit figure was as high as 1,216,725,850,000 yuan, the highest recorded deficit in the Chiang Kai-shek controlled areas in the first ten months of last year. According to the Chiang Kai-shek controlled areas' foreign trade, the trade deficit has been increasing month by month since May: the deficit in May was 110 billion yuan; June, more than 260 billion yuan; July and August, 460 to 480 billion yuan; September increased to more than 780 billion yuan.

### 蒋贼卖国造成严重恶果　贸易入超达新纪录

1948-01-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　蒋贼卖国造成严重恶果
  　贸易入超达新纪录
    【新华社陕北二十二日电】蒋管区去年十月份对外贸易入超再创新纪录。据上海报纸所引蒋匪海关发表数字：去年十月蒋区进口货总值为蒋币一万七千四百六十七亿六千六百二十七万元，出口总值为五千三百亿四千零四十二万元，还不及进口的三分之一。入超数字高达一万二千一百六十七亿二千五百八十五万元，为去年蒋区前十个月入超数字中最高之纪录。按蒋区去年对外贸易自五月份以来入超逐月增高：五月份入超为一千一百亿元；六月份为二千六百余亿元；七、八月份为四千六百至八百余亿元；九月份增至七千八百余亿元。
