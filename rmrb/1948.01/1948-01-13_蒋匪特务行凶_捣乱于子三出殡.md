Title: Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Secret Agents Commit Violence and Disrupt Yu Zisan's Funeral

Author: 
Date: 1948-01-13
Page: 2

　　Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Secret Agents Commit Violence
  　Disrupt Yu Zisan's Funeral
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 11th: Hangzhou, 4th: When the entire student body of Zhejiang University held a solemn funeral for martyr Yu Zisan, 200 Chiang Kai-shek bandit secret agents suddenly rushed into the university and beat and injured six students. When the Chiang Kai-shek secret agents committed violence, the students were filled with indignation and resisted heroically, arresting eight Chiang Kai-shek secret agents on the spot. Note: Yu Zisan was arrested on October 25th of last year along with three other students from Zhejiang University, and was murdered by the Chiang Kai-shek bandits on October 29th. The entire student body of Zhejiang University has held three strikes because of this.

### 蒋匪特务行凶　捣乱于子三出殡

1948-01-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　蒋匪特务行凶
  　捣乱于子三出殡
    【新华社陕北十一日电】杭州四日讯：浙大全校学生为于子三烈士举行庄严葬礼时，蒋匪特务二百名突然冲入该校，殴伤学生六名。蒋特行凶时学生情绪愤激，英勇反抗，当场逮捕蒋特八名。按：于子三系于去年十月二十五日与浙大其他三学生同时被捕，十月二十九日被蒋匪杀害，浙大全校学生曾为此罢课三次。
