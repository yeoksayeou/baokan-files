　　Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu Border Region Federation of Literary and Art Circles People's Fine Arts Factory
　　New Year Pictures
　　In order to meet the cultural needs of the broad masses of liberated people, we have specially printed a large number of New Year pictures, using six or seven color offset printing, which are extremely popular with the masses. All painting shops, cooperatives, and vendors are welcome to wholesale, and preferential treatment will be given to purchasers.
　　"Everyone Hates the Bandit Chiang" (200 yuan per sheet, 192 square inches)
　　"Dividing the Fruits" (200 yuan per sheet, 192 square inches)
　　"Spring Ploughing" (200 yuan per sheet, 192 square inches)
　　"Giant Portrait of Chairman Mao" (400 yuan per sheet, 576 square inches) Those who wish to purchase by mail or want samples should pay the money and postage stamps, otherwise they will not be sent. Consignment store: Cultural and Art Cooperative, No. 13 Yaqian Street, Wuan City
