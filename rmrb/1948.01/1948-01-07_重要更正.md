Title: Important Correction
Author:
Date: 1948-01-07
Page: 1

　　Important Correction
    In the report on the closing of the Central Bureau Land Conference on the first page of this newspaper on December 31st of last year, the fourteenth line from the bottom, "leadership organs of three municipal committees were revoked" should be changed to "leadership organs of three municipal and county committees were revoked." On the first page of the January 6th article "Don't Buy Printing Presses Cheaply with Guns," line fourteen: the actual price of 6 million yuan is a mistake for 60 million yuan. This correction is hereby made.

### 重要更正

1948-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　重要更正
    去年三十一日本报一版中央局土地会议闭幕消息，倒数第十四行“有三个市委领导机关被撤销”应改成“有三个市县委领导机关被撤销”。一月六日一版“不应带枪贱买印刷机”消息中第十四行：实价需六百万元系六千万元之误，特此一并更正。
