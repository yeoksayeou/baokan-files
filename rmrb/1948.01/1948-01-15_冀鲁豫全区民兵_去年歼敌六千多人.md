Title: Militias in the Entire Hebei-Shandong-Henan Region Annihilated Over 6,000 Enemies Last Year
Author:
Date: 1948-01-15
Page: 2
　　
　　Hebei-Shandong-Henan Regional Militia
  　Annihilated Over 6,000 Enemies Last Year
    [Hebei-Shandong-Henan News] The Hebei-Shandong-Henan Military Region's Armed Forces Department announced the militia's battle achievements over the past year as follows: 5,359 combat operations, killing, wounding, and capturing 6,219 enemies. Recovered 51 bunkers, destroyed 101 strongholds, and liberated 890 conscripted men. The total seizures included: 5,880 rifles, 234 pistols, 15 heavy machine guns, 169 light machine guns, 21 mortars, 60mm mortars, and hand cannons, 25 submachine guns, 295,609 rounds of rifle ammunition, 177 rounds of pistol ammunition, 542 hand grenades, 67 artillery shells, 15 vehicles (2 destroyed), 400 catties of gasoline, 24 bicycles, 5,325 catties of electric wire, 4 telephones, 41,570 catties of grain, 107,575,800 yuan in Kuomintang currency, 2,390 yuan in silver dollars, and 28,000 yuan in Hebei currency.

### 冀鲁豫全区民兵　去年歼敌六千多人

1948-01-15
第2版()
专栏：

　　冀鲁豫全区民兵
  　去年歼敌六千多人
    【冀鲁豫消息】冀鲁豫军区武装部公布一年来民兵战绩如下：作战五三五九次，毙伤俘敌六二一九名。收复碉堡五一个，摧毁据点一○一处，解放被抓壮丁八九○名。共缴获：步枪五八八○支，手枪二三四支，重机枪十五挺，轻机枪一六九挺，迫击炮、六○炮、手炮二一门，冲锋机枪二五支，步枪弹二九五六○九发，手枪弹一七七发，手榴弹五四二发，炮弹六七发，汽车十五辆（内毁二辆），汽油四百斤，自行车二四辆，电线五三二五斤，电话机四部，粮食四一五七○斤，蒋币一○七五七五八○元，现洋二三九○元，冀钞二八○○○元。
