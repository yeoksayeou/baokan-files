Title: Liaoning Local Military Group Annihilates Over a Thousand Chiang Kai-shek Bandits in Six Days
Author:
Date: 1948-01-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, 9th. The Liaoning Military Region's local forces, in six days from the 22nd to the 27th of last month, successively routed the Chiang Kai-shek bandits' 71st Army's 87th and 88th Divisions, the 53rd Army's 130th Division, and two regiments of the Provisional 30th Division, who were fleeing south from Siping, between Tieling, Kaiyuan, and Siping. A total of 1,335 enemy officers and soldiers were killed or wounded, and 288 were captured alive. The spoils included four 60mm mortars, 107 machine guns and rifles, and six trucks loaded with military supplies, while seventeen were destroyed.

### 辽宁地方兵团　六天消灭蒋匪千余

1948-01-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　辽宁地方兵团
  　六天消灭蒋匪千余
    【新华社东北前线九日电】我辽宁军区地方兵团上月二十二日至二十七日六天中，在铁岭、开原至四平间，先后击溃由四平南窜之蒋匪七一军八七、八八两个师及五三军一三○师、暂三十师两个团，共毙伤敌官兵一千三百三十五名，活捉二百八十八名。缴获六○炮四门，机步枪一○七支，满载军用品的汽车六辆，毁十七辆。
