Title: 300 Bandits Eliminated in Shouyang Area
Author:
Date: 1948-01-24
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jin察冀 (Jin-Cha-Ji) 22nd dispatch: Our local troops active in the Shouyang area of eastern Taiyuan, from December 22nd of last year to the 7th of this month, in a half-month of operations, killed, wounded, and captured over 300 enemy troops from Yan's Temporary 49th Division and the puppet Pingding and Shouyang Security Police, seizing 10 small cannons, 12 machine guns, and 116 rifles and carbines.

### 寿阳地区歼匪三百

1948-01-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　寿阳地区歼匪三百
    【新华社晋察冀二十二日电】活跃于太原东部寿阳地区我地方部队，自去年十二月二十二日至本月七日作战半月，毙伤俘阎匪暂四十九师及伪平定、寿阳保警队等三百余人，缴小炮十门，机枪十二挺，步马枪一百一十六支。
