Title: USSR Expands Foreign Trade and Belgium-Luxembourg Concludes Barter Agreement
Author:
Date: 1948-01-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 27th: Moscow dispatch: After eight weeks of talks between the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Trade and the Belgian-Luxembourg trade delegation, an agreement has been reached with the two countries to conclude a trade and payment agreement, which will be signed soon. It is said that the scale of the barter trade stipulated in the agreement is the largest between the Soviet Union and Western European countries since the war. In other news, delegations from Mongolia, Romania, Poland, Bulgaria, and Switzerland have recently gone to the Soviet capital to negotiate trade matters with the Soviet Union. The Polish delegation is led by Prime Minister Cyrankiewicz and Deputy Prime Minister Gomulka in person, and the scope of the negotiations will extend to all political and economic relations. In addition, a Soviet trade delegation has recently gone to Egypt to negotiate trade matters between the two countries.

### 苏联扩大对外贸易和比卢订换货协定

1948-01-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　苏联扩大对外贸易和比卢订换货协定
    【新华社陕北二十七日电】莫斯科讯：苏联对外贸易部与比利时、卢森堡商务代表团经八周会谈后，已于日前与该二国成立协议缔结贸易及支付协定，最近即可签字。据称该项协定规定换货规模之巨，为战后苏联与西欧国家间之首次。另讯，最近先后赴苏京拟与苏联谈判贸易事宜者，尚有蒙古、罗马尼亚、波兰、保加利亚、瑞士等国代表团。波兰代表团由总理西伦凯维兹与副总理哥穆尔卡亲自率领，谈判范围将广及一切政治经济关系。又苏联商务代表团最近已赴埃及谈判两国贸易事宜。
