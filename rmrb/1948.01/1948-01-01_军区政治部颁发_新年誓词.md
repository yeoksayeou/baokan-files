Title: Military Region Political Department Issues New Year's Oath
Author:
Date: 1948-01-01
Page: 2
　　
　　Military Region Political Department Issues
  　New Year's Oath
    [Reported by this newspaper] The Military Region Political Department issued the following New Year's Oath:
    We have victoriously passed 1947 and welcome 1948, striving for greater victories in the counter-offensive.
    We are the sons of workers and peasants, we are the People's Liberation Army, and we voluntarily join our own army. We unite as one, wholeheartedly serve the people, work as long-term laborers for the people, and fight to the end against American imperialism, to overthrow Chiang Kai-shek, to implement land reform, and to defend the nation and our land.
    We must fight resolutely, obey orders, obey command, abide by discipline, fight heroically, fear no sacrifice, and resolutely, thoroughly, and completely annihilate the traitor Chiang Kai-shek's bandit army.
    We must actively participate in the peasant's emancipation movement, thoroughly and equally distribute the land, and eliminate the feudal and semi-feudal land system: resolutely oppose all speeches and actions that oppose land reform.
    We must strictly implement the Three Main Rules of Discipline and the Eight Points of Attention, participate in mass production, protect the interests of the masses, and resolutely oppose all actions that infringe upon the interests of the masses.
    We must intensify our military and political studies, raise our class consciousness, improve our military skills, and learn how to attack fortified positions, conduct earthwork operations, and use explosives. We must help each other, learn from each other, promote class friendship, and oppose the thoughts of being old, arrogant, conceited, and unwilling to learn.
    We will resolutely implement the above items, and if we violate them, we are willing to accept the criticism of the people, the criticism of comrades, and disciplinary sanctions. We hereby solemnly swear this oath.

### 军区政治部颁发　新年誓词

1948-01-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　军区政治部颁发
  　新年誓词
    【本报讯】军区政治部颁发新年誓词原文如下：
    我们胜利的度过了一九四七年，迎接一九四八年，争取反攻中更大的胜利。
    我们是工农的儿子，我们是人民解放军，自愿参加自己的军队。我们团结一致，全心全意为人民服务，给人民当长工，为反对美帝国主义，打倒蒋介石，实行土地改革，保卫民族，保卫土地而奋斗到底。
    我们要坚决作战，服从命令，服从指挥，遵守纪律，英勇奋斗，不怕牺牲，坚决彻底全部歼灭卖国贼蒋介石匪军。
    我们要积极参加农民的翻身运动，彻底平分土地，消灭封建性和半封建性的土地制度：坚决反对一切反土地改革的言论和行为。
    我们要严格执行三大纪律八项注意，参加群众生产，保护群众利益，坚决反对一切侵犯群众利益的行为。
    我们要加紧学习军事政治，提高阶级觉悟，提高军事技术，学会攻坚、土工作业、爆破。要互相帮助，互相学习，发扬阶级友爱，反对老大骄傲，自满不爱学习的思想。
    以上各项要坚决执行，如有违犯，愿受人民指责，同志批评，纪律制裁。谨此宜誓。
