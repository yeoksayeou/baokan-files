Title: Border Region Farmers' Association Preparatory Committee Passes Supplementary Measures to the Land Law Outline
Author:
Date: 1948-01-01
Page: 2

　　Border Region Farmers' Association Preparatory Committee
  　Passes Supplementary Measures to the Land Law Outline
    [Reported by this newspaper] The draft supplementary measures to the implementation of the Land Law Outline of China, formulated by the Government of the Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu Border Region on December 28, 1947 (published on page one of this newspaper, December 31, 1947, Republic of China year 36), has been thoroughly and exhaustively discussed by the Border Region Farmers' Association Preparatory Committee, based on the specific conditions of this region and the practical demands of the farmers. Unanimous agreement was reached, and on the same day (December 28), it was submitted to the Border Region Government for official announcement and diligent implementation throughout the region. The draft has now become the formal supplementary measures to the implementation of the Land Law Outline of China in this region.

### 边区农会筹委会　通过土地法大纲补充办法

1948-01-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　边区农会筹委会
  　通过土地法大纲补充办法
    【本报讯】晋冀鲁豫边区政府于一九四七年十二月二十八日所制定之施行中国土地法大纲补充办法草案（载本报中华民国三十六年十二月三十一日一版）当经边区农会筹备会，根据本区具体情况，及农民的切实要求，反复详尽讨论终日，一致同意，并于即日（二十八日）送交边府正式布告全区切实执行，即该草案已成为本区施行中国土地法大纲正式补充办法。
