Title: Our Army in Hubei, Henan, and Anhui Continues to Capture Huangmei, Via Fuli Mountain
Author:
Date: 1948-01-07
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hubei-Henan-Anhui Front, 5th: (1) A certain unit of the Hubei-Henan-Anhui Military Region recovered the county seat of Huangmei on the 25th of last month. The defending enemy, Chiang Kai-shek's youth army and the Jiujiang County Self-Defense Force, fled towards Konglong Town. Chiang Kai-shek's bandit troops invaded and occupied our Jingfu (i.e., Xinji) County seat on the 28th of last month, but we recovered it on the 29th. (2) A certain part of the military district's armed forces recovered the county seat of Lishan on the 30th of last month, capturing over twenty members of Chiang Kai-shek's bandit security team. They also completely destroyed the telegraph lines, roads, and pillboxes in Tonghekou Town, Lishan (thirty li west of Huang'an). The Xin Zhou (a newly established county north of Huanggang) County Brigade, on the 22nd, led the district cadre team to rout three township public offices of the enemy and recovered Xin Zhou. Another unit captured over thirty local Chiang Kai-shek bandits near Luotian and Macheng in the middle of last month, seizing two light machine guns, over sixty rifles, four pistols, and four thousand rounds of ammunition. (3) A certain unit of the Liberation Army occupied Minggang Railway Station on the evening of the 30th of last month, capturing over ten Chiang Kai-shek bandits, seizing seven trucks, and a portion of grain. On the same day, another unit of the Liberation Army annihilated over one hundred and thirty Chiang Kai-shek bandits within Xincai County, seizing four light machine guns and over one hundred and twenty rifles. (4) Our army in the Western Anhui Military Region recovered Qingcaochang, more than fifty li southwest of Tongcheng, on the 22nd of last month, capturing over two hundred local Chiang Kai-shek bandits and their weapons.

### 鄂豫皖我军　续克黄梅经扶礼山

1948-01-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　鄂豫皖我军
  　续克黄梅经扶礼山
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线五日电】（一）鄂豫军区某部于上月二十五日收复黄梅县城，守敌蒋匪青年军及九江县自卫队向孔龙镇逃窜。蒋匪于上月二十八日侵占我经扶（即新集）县城，二十九日即为我收复。（二）某分区武装一部，上月三十日收复礼山县城，生俘蒋匪保安队二十余名，并将礼山通河口镇（黄安以西三十里）的电线公路及碉堡等全部破坏，新洲（在黄冈以北为新设县）县大队于二十二日率区干队将敌三个乡公所击溃，并收复新洲。另一部于上月中旬在罗田麻城附近，俘土蒋匪三十余名，获轻机枪两挺，步枪六十余支，手枪四支，子弹四千发。（三）解放军某部，于上月三十日晚占领明港车站，生俘蒋匪十余名，缴获汽车七辆，粮食一部；同日，另一部解放军在新蔡县境内，消灭蒋匪一百三十余名，缴获轻机枪四挺，步枪一百二十余支。（四）皖西军区我军于上月二十二日收复桐城西南五十余里之青草场，俘土蒋匪人枪各二百余。
