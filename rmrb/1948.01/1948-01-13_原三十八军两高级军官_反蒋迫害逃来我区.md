Title: Two Senior Officers of the Former 38th Army Fled to Our Area to Escape Chiang Kai-shek's Persecution
Author: 
Date: 1948-01-13
Page: 2

　　Former 38th Army Two Senior Officers
  　Fled to Our Area to Escape Chiang's Persecution
    [Xinhua News Agency, Front Lines of the Su, Lu, Yu, and Anhui Regions, 11th] Liu Junfu, former senior staff officer of the 38th Army under General Zhao Shoushan, and Shangguan Shude, a regimental commander, successfully escaped from Zhengzhou and Xi'an, respectively, and arrived safely in the Yu, Anhui, and Su liberated areas. Since General Zhao Shoushan came to the liberated area, both men have been wanted and prosecuted by the Chiang bandit authorities. After five months of twists and turns, they finally escaped.

### 原三十八军两高级军官　反蒋迫害逃来我区

1948-01-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　原三十八军两高级军官
  　反蒋迫害逃来我区
    【新华社苏鲁豫皖前线十一日电】赵寿山将军旧部原国民党三十八军高级参谋刘俊甫及团长上官树德两氏，分别由郑州、西安脱险安抵豫皖苏解放区。两氏自赵寿山将军来解放区后，均遭蒋匪当局通缉拿办，经五个月辗转周折，始得脱险。
