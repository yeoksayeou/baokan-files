Title: American People's Lives are Hard: Over One Hundred Chicago Poor Freeze to Death
Author:
Date: 1948-01-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 28th: New York dispatch: Prices in the United States are soaring. According to the conservative U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, retail prices in mid-December of last year had already reached their peak, an increase of 25 percent compared to June 1946, after price controls were lifted. Soaring prices, coupled with the prolonged severe cold this year, have made the lives of the vast American people even more difficult. According to a United Press dispatch on the 26th, in Chicago alone, the number of poor people who have frozen to death has increased to as many as 138.

### 美国人民生活难过　芝加哥冻死贫民一百多

1948-01-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　美国人民生活难过
  　芝加哥冻死贫民一百多
    【新华社陕北二十八日电】纽约讯：美国物价飞速高涨。据保守的美国劳工统计局称：去年十二月中旬零售物价已达最高峰，较一九四六年六月取消物价管制后上涨百分之二十五。物价飞涨加上今年长期的严寒，使美国广大人民的生活益发难过。据合众社二十六日讯：仅芝加哥一地，被冻死的贫民即已增至一百三十八人之多。
