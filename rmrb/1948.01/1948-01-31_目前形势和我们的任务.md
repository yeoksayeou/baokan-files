Title: The Current Situation and Our Tasks
Author: Mao Zedong
Date: 1948-01-31
Page: 1

　　Mao Zedong's Important Document
    The Propaganda Department of the Central Bureau has notified all regions and departments to conscientiously organize study of this important document by Chairman Mao. This report is the central focus of current study. It also calls on each of our cadres to carefully study in conjunction with their own work and to implement the spirit of the document in all work.
    To facilitate the study of this document by all cadres, a separate edition has now been published.
