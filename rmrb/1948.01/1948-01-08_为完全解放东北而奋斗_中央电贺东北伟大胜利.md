Title: "Strive for the Complete Liberation of the Northeast": Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram on the Great Victory in the Northeast
Author:
Date: 1948-01-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 6th. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China has telegraphed congratulations to the Northeast People's Liberation Army, stating:

To Comrades Lin Biao, Luo Ronghuan, Gao Gang, Chen Yun, and all commanders and fighters: Congratulations on your great victory in eliminating over 280,000 enemy troops in 1947. Continued efforts are needed to strive for the complete liberation of the Northeast.

### “为完全解放东北而奋斗”  中央电贺东北伟大胜利

1948-01-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　“为完全解放东北而奋斗”
    中央电贺东北伟大胜利
    【新华社陕北六日电】中共中央委员会顷电贺东北人民解放军称：
    林彪、罗荣桓、高岗、陈云诸同志及全体指挥员战斗员同志们：庆祝你们一九四七年消灭敌军二十八万余人的伟大胜利。尚需继续努力，为完全解放东北而奋斗。
