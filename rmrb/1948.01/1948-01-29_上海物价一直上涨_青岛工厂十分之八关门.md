Title: Shanghai Prices Continue to Rise; 80% of Qingdao Factories Close
Author:
Date: 1948-01-29
Page: 2

　　Shanghai Prices Continue to Rise
  　80% of Qingdao Factories Close
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 20th] According to foreign news agency reports: After the first economic report of the year, Shanghai newspapers, based on price surveys of major commodities in the first three weeks of this month, pointed out that the city's cost of living index rose by 40% compared to last month. Price increases were most severe in the second week. Furthermore, railway ticket prices have been announced to increase by 15% to 17% starting from the 25th, which is the second price increase since the new year.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 26th] TASS reports: In Qingdao, under the rule of the Chiang Kai-shek bandits, due to a shortage of coal and the cessation of electricity supply, 1,200 out of 1,500 factories, both large and small, have closed down, leaving tens of thousands of workers unemployed and wandering the streets.

### 上海物价一直上涨　青岛工厂十分之八关门

1948-01-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　上海物价一直上涨
  　青岛工厂十分之八关门
    【新华社陕北二十日电】据外国通讯社报到：今年第一个经济分报发生后，上海报纸根据本月最初三周主要商品价格调查指出，本月份该市生活费指数较上月上涨百分之四十，物价上涨以第二周为最剧。又，铁路票价已宣布于二十五日起增加百分之十五至十七，这是新年后的第二度加价。
    【新华社陕北二十六日电】塔斯社讯：蒋匪统治下之青岛，因燃煤缺乏，电力供应停止，致一千五百家大小工厂中，关门者达一千二百家，数万工人失业，流浪街头。
