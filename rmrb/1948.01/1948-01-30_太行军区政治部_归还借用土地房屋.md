Title: Political Department of the Taihang Military Region Returns Borrowed Land and Houses
Author:
Date: 1948-01-30
Page: 1

　　Taihang Military Region Political Department
  　Returns Borrowed Land and Houses
    Recently, the Political Department of the Taihang Military Region, in conjunction with the work of supporting and cherishing the people, inspected the discipline of the troops stationed in the area. They returned as much of the items borrowed from the masses as possible, and compensated for the damaged items at a discounted price, totaling 140,800 yuan. The 27 mu of public dry and paddy land occupied by the unit's production in the past has been returned to the masses. In addition, the stationed area was reduced, and 81 borrowed houses were vacated, repaired, and returned to the masses.　　　　　　　　　(Political Department of the Taihang Military Region)

### 太行军区政治部　归还借用土地房屋

1948-01-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　太行军区政治部
  　归还借用土地房屋
    太行军区政治部近来结合拥爱工作，检查驻地群众纪律，将借用群众的东西尽量归还群众，损坏的折价赔偿，共合十四万零八百元。过去单位生产所占的公产水旱地二十七亩，已退还群众。并紧缩驻地，腾出借用的房子八十一间，加以修补，归还群众。　　　　　　　　　（太行军区政治部）
