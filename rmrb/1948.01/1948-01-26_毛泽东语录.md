　　Quotations from Chairman Mao
    It is essential to satisfy the demands of poor peasants and farm laborers; this is the most fundamental task of land reform. It is essential to resolutely unite with the middle peasants and not harm their interests. As long as we grasp these two fundamental principles, our land reform task will certainly be completed victoriously.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　— Mao Zedong

### 毛泽东语录

　　毛泽东语录
    必须满足贫农与雇农的要求，这是土地改革最基本的任务。必须坚决团结中农，不要损害中农的利益。只要我们掌握了这两条基本原则，我们的土地改革任务就一定能够胜利地完成。
