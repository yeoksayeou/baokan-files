Title: Our Army Annihilates Bandits Everywhere, Liberating the People: 8,000 Bandits Annihilated in Tongbai in One Month
Author:
Date: 1948-01-29
Page: 2

　　Our Army Annihilates Bandits Everywhere, Liberating the People
　　Tongbai: 8,000 Bandits Annihilated in One Month
　　Xinhua News Agency, Eyuwan, 26th—Since the Eyuwan Liberation Army entered the Tongbai area, it has been annihilating Chiang bandits everywhere and liberating the local people. In the past month, it has conducted 40 battles of various sizes, killing and wounding more than 1,400 Chiang bandits, capturing 7,228 bandits alive, and successively liberating six county towns: Tongbai, Zhaoyang, Biyang, Tanghe, Xinye, and Dengxian. It seized 34 artillery pieces of various types, 101 light and heavy machine guns, 6,379 rifles and carbines, more than 700,000 rounds of ammunition, 4 cars, and a portion of other military supplies.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Eyuwan, 26th—A unit of the Liberation Army ambushed a unit of the 28th Division of Chiang bandits advancing from Xiadian to Xiangbeizui (Lishan) on the highway from Xiadian to Xiangbeizui on the 15th, and defeated them. On the same day, another unit, in coordination with local armed forces, captured Bolangdian in Xiangbeizui in one fell swoop, annihilating a part of the local Chiang Lishan Security Brigade. A total of more than 50 people were killed and wounded, more than 60 people were captured alive, 30 rifles were seized, and a portion of other military supplies were obtained.

### 我军到处歼匪解放人民　桐柏一个月歼匪八千

1948-01-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　我军到处歼匪解放人民
  　桐柏一个月歼匪八千
    【新华社鄂豫皖二十六日电】鄂豫皖解放军自进入桐柏地区后，到处歼灭蒋匪，解放当地人民。一个月来共进行大小战斗四十次，毙伤蒋匪一千四百余人，活捉匪七千二百二十八人，先后解放桐柏、枣阳、泌阳、唐河、新野、邓县等六座县城。缴获各种炮三十四门、轻重机枪一百零一挺、长短枪六千三百七十九支、子弹七十余万发、汽车四辆及其他军用品一部。
    【新华社鄂豫皖二十六日电】解放军一部十五日在夏店至向背咀（礼山）公路上，伏击由夏店向向背咀进犯之蒋匪二十八师一部，将其击溃。同日另部配合地方武装一举攻克向背咀之博郎店，歼灭土蒋礼山保安队一部。以上共计毙伤五十余人，生俘六十余人，缴步枪三十支，其他军用品一部。
