Title: A Heinous Massacre: Dutch Troops Massacre Nearly a Thousand Overseas Chinese; Thousands of Overseas Chinese Arrested and Suffering Torture
Author:
Date: 1948-01-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 22nd. A heinous massacre of Indonesian Overseas Chinese by Dutch troops has occurred in Indonesia. According to a TASS dispatch from Batavia: During the recent so-called "police action," Dutch aggressors killed more than nine hundred innocent Overseas Chinese, and thousands of Overseas Chinese were arrested and are suffering all kinds of torture. This massacre of our Overseas Chinese by Dutch imperialism occurred before and after the Chiang-Dutch aviation agreement and Chiang Kai-shek's full support for the conspiracy to split Indonesia.

### 惨绝人寰的大血案　荷兰兽军惨杀华侨近千数千侨胞被捕正受苦刑

1948-01-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　惨绝人寰的大血案
  　荷兰兽军惨杀华侨近千数千侨胞被捕正受苦刑
    【新华社陕北二十二日电】印尼发生荷兰兽军惨杀印尼华侨之惨绝人寰的大血案。据塔斯社巴城讯：荷兰侵略者在最近所谓“警卫行动”过程中，杀害了九百余名无辜华侨，并有数千华侨横遭拘捕，正蒙受种种苦刑。按荷兰帝国主义此次对我侨胞的大屠杀，发生在蒋荷航空协定及蒋匪全力支持分裂印尼的阴谋活动之前后。
