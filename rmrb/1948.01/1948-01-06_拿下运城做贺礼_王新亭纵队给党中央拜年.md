Title: Capturing Yuncheng as a New Year's Gift: Wang Xinting's Column Sends New Year Greetings to the Party Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1948-01-06
Page: 1

Celebrating the New Year

　　Capturing Yuncheng as a New Year's Gift
　　Wang Xinting's Column Sends New Year Greetings to the Party Central Committee
    [Our newspaper, Taiyue, 4th dispatch] General Wang Xinting and other generals, leading all officers and soldiers of the entire column, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu on New Year's Day, reporting the victory of capturing Yuncheng, and wishing them good health in the New Year. The original telegram is as follows: "To Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China: All officers and soldiers of our column pledge with the greatest determination and effort to capture Yuncheng before the New Year as a New Year's gift to the Party Central Committee. Under your wise leadership and the wise leadership of Comrades Liu, Deng, Xu, Teng, and Bo of the Central Bureau of the Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu Region, we have finally completed the glorious task of liberating Yuncheng and victoriously passed 1947. We hereby send a telegram of victory to congratulate you on your good health in the New Year."

### 拿下运城做贺礼　王新亭纵队给党中央拜年

1948-01-06
第1版()
专栏：庆祝新年

　　拿下运城做贺礼
  　王新亭纵队给党中央拜年
    【本报太岳四日电】王新亭等将军率领全纵队指战员，在元旦打电报给毛主席和朱总司令，报告打下运城的胜仗，并祝新年健康，原电如下：中共中央毛主席朱总司令：我纵队全体指战员誓以最大决心和努力，要在新年前打下运城，作为给党中央的新年献礼。在你们和晋冀鲁豫中央局刘、邓、徐、滕、薄各位负责同志的英明领导下，我们终于完成了解放运城的光荣任务，胜利的渡过了一九四七年。特以胜利驰电祝贺你们新年健康。
