Title: U.S. Imperialism Intensifies Aggression Against China; Plunders Central and Southern China's Industrial, Mining, and Water Conservancy Resources; Directly Supervises Chiang Kai-shek's Gang in Training Cannon Fodder

Author:

Date: 1948-01-22
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 18th: Shanghai and Tianjin newspaper reports: U.S. imperialism is intensifying its plunder of resources in South and Central China. The Ma'anshan Power Plant in Southern Anhui and the Daye Iron Works in Hubei are both being planned and built under American supervision. The American Penny Lee Company has decided to invest 200 million U.S. dollars in Guangdong and 50 million U.S. dollars in Guangxi to monopolize the industrial and mining enterprises of these two provinces. This includes the ＠Jiang Power Plant in Northern Guangdong, the Baziling Gouyadong Coal Mine, and the agricultural water conservancy of the entire Guangdong province. Mr. Perrish, manager of the American Southwest Iron Company, has visited Guangzhou to discuss the exploitation of iron ore on Hainan Island. The United States is also preparing to exploit iron ore in Fujian.

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 18th: According to foreign news agencies in Nanjing: Chiang Kai-shek recently convened an army training conference to discuss the Chiang bandit army training plan formulated by his American masters. The conference was held under the direct participation and command of personnel from the U.S. Military Advisory Group, beginning on the 13th. It concluded on the 15th, deciding to establish eight training institutions and one military academy, all under direct American control, and stipulating that only senior Chiang bandit officers who have received special American training can serve as instructors. At the same time, U.S. Southwest Pacific Fleet Commander Cooke also arrived in Nanjing from Qingdao on the 16th to discuss the Chiang bandit navy training plan with Gui Yongqing, the acting commander-in-chief of the Chiang bandit navy. U.S. Navy Rear Admiral Sapa arrived in China from the United States on the 8th to be responsible for establishing the U.S. Naval Advisory Group headquarters in Nanjing as the highest supervisory body for the Chiang bandit navy.

### 美帝加紧侵略中国　掠夺华中华南工矿水利资源直接监督蒋匪训练虾兵蟹将

1948-01-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　美帝加紧侵略中国
  　掠夺华中华南工矿水利资源直接监督蒋匪训练虾兵蟹将
    【新华社陕北十八日电】上海及天津报纸消息：美帝国主义对于华南和华中的资源掠夺，日益加紧。皖南马鞍山电厂和湖北大冶铁厂，均由美国监督筹办中。美国潘尼·李氏公司已决定在广东投资两亿美元，在广西投资五千万美元，垄断该两省工矿事业。粤北＠江发电厂、八字岭狗牙洞煤厂及广东全省农田水利，皆包括在内。美国西南辗铁公司经理柏里希，已访问过广州，接洽海南岛铁矿开采问题。福建铁矿美国亦准备开采。
    【新华社陕北十八日电】据外国通讯社南京消息：蒋介石最近召开陆军训练会议，讨论其美国主子所拟定之蒋匪陆军训练计划。该会系在美军事顾问团人员直接参与指挥下于十三日起举行。十五日结束，决定设立八个训练机构，和一个军官学校，皆归美人直接控制，并规定蒋匪高级军官只有受过美国特别训练的才能担任教官。同时，美国西南太平洋舰队司令柯克亦于十六日由青岛到达南京，与蒋匪代理海军总司令桂永清商讨蒋匪海军训练计划。美海军少将萨怕八日由美来华，负责在南京建立美海军顾问团总部，作为蒋匪海军最高监督机构。
