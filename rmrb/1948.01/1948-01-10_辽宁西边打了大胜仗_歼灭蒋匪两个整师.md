Title: A Great Victory in Western Liaoning: Two Chiang Kai-shek Divisions Annihilated
Author:
Date: 1948-01-10
Page: 2

　　Liaoning West Side Won a Great Victory
  　Annihilated Two Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Divisions
    【Xinhua News Agency, Northeast Front, Urgent Dispatch, 8th】The People's Liberation Army has won another great victory in the western Liaoning area. Yesterday (the 7th) at 8:00 PM, between Xinmin and Gongzhutun, more than 100 li northwest of Shenyang, the enemy Chiang Kai-shek's New 5th Army's direct units, and the 195th Division (originally under the 52nd Army), and the 43rd Division (originally under the 94th Army, now under the command of the New 5th Army), two entire divisions were completely annihilated, including all personnel of the division headquarters. A very large number of prisoners were captured, and are being tallied. This great victory began at 2:00 PM on the 5th and was completely resolved after 54 hours of fierce fighting. This comes only ten days after our complete annihilation of the enemy's 49th Army's 79th Division.

### 辽宁西边打了大胜仗　歼灭蒋匪两个整师

1948-01-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　辽宁西边打了大胜仗
  　歼灭蒋匪两个整师
    【新华社东北前线八日急电】解放军任辽西地区又打了大胜仗。昨天（七日）下午八点在沈阳西北一百多里的新民至公主屯间，完全歼灭蒋匪新五军军直属队及一九五师（原属五十二军），四十三师（原属九十四军，现归新五军指挥）两个整师，包括师部任内，俘获极多，正在清查中。这一大胜仗，开始在五日下午二时，经过五十四小时激战，全部解决战斗。离我全部歼灭敌四十九军七十九师才有十天。
