Title: Annihilation of 500 Enemies in the Taihu Area
Author:
Date: 1948-01-17
Page: 2

　　Annihilation of 500 Enemies in the Taihu Area
    [Xinhua News Agency, Eyuwan Front, 13th] Late news: Chiang Kai-shek's 25th Division invaded the area west of Taihu on the 5th. A certain unit of the People's Liberation Army immediately gave the enemy a head-on blow in the Lidu Village area, killing and wounding more than 500 enemy soldiers. The enemy fled back to Taihu City in a panic.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Eyuwan Front, 15th] Late news: A certain unit of the Jianghan Military Region of the People's Liberation Army liberated Sui County on the 7th, a model battle case of daytime surprise attack and annihilation of the enemy. At about 10:00 on the 6th, the People's Liberation Army suddenly appeared in Anju and Tangjiaban areas west of Sui County, encountering a part of Chiang Kai-shek's bandit troops who were invading west of Sui County from the city. The People's Liberation Army quickly launched a fierce attack. After two hours of fierce fighting, more than 100 bandit soldiers were annihilated, and the remaining bandits fled back in panic. The People's Liberation Army took advantage of the victory to pursue fiercely, directly approaching the city, and surrounded the city. A general offensive was launched on the afternoon of the 7th, and all the 1,400-plus Chiang Kai-shek bandit security 4th Corps guarding the city were completely annihilated within four hours. Since the Jianghan People's Liberation Army entered the area to fight, they have been victorious in every battle. They once created the example of a surprise attack on Zhongxiang City, annihilating more than 1,200 enemies.

### 太湖地区歼敌五百

1948-01-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　太湖地区歼敌五百
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线十三日电】迟到消息：蒋匪军二十五师于五日向太湖以西地区进犯，解放军某部当即在李杜庄地区，给该敌以迎头痛击，毙伤匪军五百余名，该敌狼狈窜回太湖城。
    【新华社鄂豫皖前线十五日电】迟到消息：江汉军区解放军某部，七日解放随县城，是白昼奔袭歼敌之模范战例。六日十时许，解放军突出现在随县城西之安居、汤家坂一带，与由该城向随西出犯之蒋匪一部遭遇，解放军迅即展开猛冲，激战两小时后，歼灭匪军百余，残匪狼狈回窜。解放军乘胜猛追，直逼城下，并将该城团团包围。至七日下午发起总攻，四小时内全歼守城蒋匪保安第四总队一千四百余名。按江汉解放军进入该区作战以来，连战皆捷，曾创奇袭钟祥城，歼敌一千二百余之范例。
