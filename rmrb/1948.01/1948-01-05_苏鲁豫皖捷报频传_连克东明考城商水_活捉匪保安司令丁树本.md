Title: Frequent Reports of Victory from Jiangsu, Shandong, Henan and Anhui: Successive Capture of Dongming, Kaocheng, and Shangshui; Bandit Security Commander Ding Shuben Captured Alive

Author:
Date: 1948-01-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Frontlines of Jiangsu, Shandong, Henan, and Anhui, 2nd: A certain unit of the People's Liberation Army launched a general offensive against the Chiang bandit puppet troops in Dongming City, the southernmost part of Hebei Province, at 3:00 PM on the 30th. After one hour of battle, the enemy's 14th Special Office of Hebei Province and the six county governments of Dongming, Puyang, Qingfeng, Daming, Nanle, and Guangping, as well as the Dongming County Security Regiment and the Special Office's Special Service Battalion, were completely annihilated. The pseudo-commissioner and Lieutenant General Security Commander Ding Shuben was captured alive. The results of the battle are still being verified.

Xinhua News Agency, Frontlines of Jiangsu, Shandong, Henan, and Anhui, 31st: On the evening of the 24th, cavalry units of the People's Liberation Army annihilated and captured more than 50 local Chiang bandits near Xiaoshang Bridge, 30 li north of Luohe, seizing 57 long and short guns.

Xinhua News Agency, Frontlines of Jiangsu, Shandong, Henan, and Anhui, 31st: On the 22nd, the People's Liberation Army of the Yu-Wan-Su Military Region recovered Shangshui City, capturing alive more than 250 pseudo-county magistrates and below. On the 25th, Zhoujiakou was recaptured. The People's Liberation Army suffered only one slightly wounded soldier, captured more than 100 Chiang bandits, and seized one mortar, ten grenade launchers, one light machine gun, eight cars, and four horse-drawn carts (Note: Both places were recovered for the second time).

Xinhua News Agency, Frontlines of Jiangsu, Shandong, Henan, and Anhui, 31st: At 10:00 PM on December 28th, the People's Liberation Army liberated Kaocheng after 45 minutes of combat, annihilating more than 1,000 enemies from the 68th Reorganized Division's Transport Battalion and four security teams.

### 苏鲁豫皖捷报频传　连克东明考城商水　活捉匪保安司令丁树本

1948-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏鲁豫皖捷报频传
    连克东明考城商水
  　活捉匪保安司令丁树本
    【新华社苏鲁豫皖前线二日电】解放军某部于三十日下午三时，对河北省最南端之东明城蒋匪伪军发起总攻，经一小时战斗，将守敌河北省十四专署及东明、濮阳、清丰、大名、南乐、广平等六个县政府及东明县保安团、专署特务营全部歼灭，活捉伪专员兼中将保安司令丁树本，战果正清查中。
    【新华社苏鲁豫皖前线三十一日电】解放军骑兵部队于二十四日晚在漯河北三十里之小商桥附近歼俘土蒋匪五十余人，缴长短枪五十七支。
    【新华社苏鲁豫皖前线三十一日电】豫皖苏军区解放军于二十二日收复商水城，生俘伪县长以下二百五十余人。二十五日再克周家口，解放军仅轻伤战士一名，俘蒋匪百余名，缴获迫击炮一门，掷弹筒十个，轻机枪一挺，汽车八辆，马车四辆（按二地均为第二次收复）。
    【新华社苏鲁豫皖前线三十一日电】解放军于十二月二十八日夜十时，经四十五分钟战斗，解放考城。歼敌整编六十八师辎重营及四个保安队共千余人。
