Title: Bohai Jinan Detachment Annihilates Over a Hundred Enemies
Author:
Date: 1948-01-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, East China, 12th. The Jinan Detachment of the Bohai Military Region repelled over 700 invading bandits from two battalions of the 54th Division of the 92nd Army of the Chiang Kai-shek forces and local reactionary corps at the Xiaowangzhuang Ferry in southern Tianjin on the 24th of last month, killing, wounding, and capturing 150 enemy personnel, including a deputy battalion commander. They also seized ten American-style machine guns, forty long and short guns, and over 10,000 rounds of ammunition. On the 22nd of last month, another sub-district's coastal defense detachment captured Xincheng Town, 25 li southwest of Tanggu, annihilating ten members of the puppet self-defense corps.

### 渤海津南支队歼敌百余

1948-01-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　渤海津南支队
  　歼敌百余
    【新华社华东十二日电】渤海区津南支队上月二十四日，在天津南小王庄渡口，打退来犯的蒋匪九十二军五十四师两个营和还乡团共七百多人，毙伤和活捉敌营副以下一百五十名，缴获美式机枪十挺，长短枪四十支，子弹一万多发。另一分区海防支队在上月二十二日打下塘沽西南二十五里的新城镇，歼灭伪自卫团十名。
