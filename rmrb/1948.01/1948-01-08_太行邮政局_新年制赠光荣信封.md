Title: Taihang Postal Bureau Creates and Donates "Glory Envelopes" for the New Year
Author:
Date: 1948-01-08
Page: 1

　　Taihang Postal Bureau
  　Creates and Donates "Glory Envelopes" for the New Year
    [This newspaper reports] The Taihang Postal Administration specially produced and printed 10,000 free "Glory Envelopes" before the New Year, and distributed them to the Political Department of the Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu Military Region and the Political Department of the Taihang Military Region, to be distributed to frontline soldiers for correspondence, and to express sincere greetings to the frontline soldiers on behalf of all employees in the region.

### 太行邮政局　新年制赠光荣信封

1948-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　太行邮政局
  　新年制赠光荣信封
    【本报讯】太行邮政管理局于年前，特制印免费光荣信封一万个，分送晋冀鲁豫军区政治部及太行军区政治部，转给前方战士通讯使用，并代表全区员工向前方战士致亲切慰问。
