Title: I Recapture Yanling

Author:

Date: 1948-01-25
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Frontline of Su, Lu, Yu, and Anhui, 23rd: Delayed news: A unit of the People's Liberation Army recaptured Yanling County town at dawn on the 13th. More than 100 local Chiang bandits were captured alive, and more than 3 light machine guns, more than 60 rifles, a car, and some ammunition were seized.

Xinhua News Agency, Frontline of Henan, Shaanxi, and Hubei, 23rd: Delayed news: The local armed forces of our Seventh Military Sub-district suppressed bandits and diehards, and on the 9th, annihilated a unit of Chiang Kai-shek's Pacification Corps at Beiwudu, 50 li north of Wuyang City, capturing 175 men, including Deputy Regiment Commander Li Yisi, seizing 170 long and short guns, more than 10,000 rounds of ammunition, 2 telephones, and 18 mules and horses.

### 我再克鄢陵

1948-01-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　我再克鄢陵
    【新华社苏鲁豫皖前线二十三日电】迟到消息：解放军一部于十三日拂晓再克鄢陵县城。活捉土蒋匪一百多人，缴获轻机枪三挺，步枪六十多支，汽车一辆，及弹药一部。
    【新华社豫陕鄂前线二十三日电】迟到消息：我七分区地方武装进剿匪顽，九日在舞阳城北五十里的北舞渡歼灭蒋匪绥靖总队一部，生俘副团长李义思以下一百七十五名，缴长短枪一百七十支，子弹万余发，电话机二部，骡马十八头。
