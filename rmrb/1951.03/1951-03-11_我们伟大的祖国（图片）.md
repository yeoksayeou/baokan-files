    Our Great Motherland
    In the vast rural areas of our country's new districts, a vigorous land reform struggle is now developing. In this struggle, the peasants not only obtained land but also learned to be the masters of the new country. This is a village peasant congress, where peasants are formulating six major disciplines for land distribution: cadres must be selfless and impartial; peasants must be united and accommodating, with fair and reasonable distribution; methods must be democratically negotiated; the goal must be conducive to production; and the results must satisfy the masses.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在我国广大的新区农村中，现在正发展着轰轰烈烈的土地改革斗争。农民们在这个斗争中不但得到了土地，也学会了作新国家的主人翁。这是一个村的农民大会，农民们在会上订出分田的六大纪律：干部大公无私；农民团结互让，分配公平合理；方法民主协商；目的有利生产；结果群众满意。
