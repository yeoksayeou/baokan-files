Title: Chinese Acrobatic Troupe Well-Received in Soviet Union and Poland
Author:
Date: 1951-03-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th - The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe, having been invited by the Soviet government to perform in the Soviet Union for four months last October, has now moved on to Poland for performances. During its four-month stay in the Soviet Union, the troupe performed 104 times in Moscow, Leningrad, Kiev, Yerevan, Baku, Tbilisi, and other places, attracting 138,000 spectators. Audiences and acrobats from all over unanimously praised the troupe's skills, considering them to be rich in national characteristics and fully demonstrating the intelligence, composure, diligence, and bravery of the Chinese people.
    
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 10th - The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe, invited by the Polish government to perform in Poland, gave its premiere performance at the Ministry of Public Security Theater in Warsaw on February 26th, attracting a packed house with no empty seats. The troupe's performances were reported in detail by various Polish newspapers with significant coverage. The People's Tribune published an article entitled "The Superior Chinese Acrobatic Troupe," highly praising the "troupe's diverse and colorful program." After its performances in Warsaw, the troupe will travel to major Polish cities such as Lodz, Katowice, and Krakow for performances.

### 我国杂技团在苏、波备受欢迎

1951-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国杂技团在苏、波备受欢迎
    【新华社莫斯科十日电】中华杂技团于去年十月应苏联政府之邀到苏联表演了四个月，业已转赴波兰表演。该团在留苏的四个月内，先后在莫斯科、列宁格勒、基辅、埃里温、巴库和第比利斯等地演出了一百零四次，观众达十三万八千人。各地的观众和杂技家对该团技艺一致赞扬，认为富有民族特性，充分表现了中国人民所具有的聪明沉着、勤劳勇敢的性格。
    【新华社布拉格十日电】应波兰政府之邀到波兰表演的中华杂技团，于二月二十六日在华沙公安部剧场首次公演，观众拥挤，座无虚席。该团所演节目，波兰各报均以显著篇幅详细报道。人民论坛报发表了题为《优越的中华杂技团》的文章，盛赞“该团节目繁多而富有色彩”。该团在华沙演出后，将赴波兰各大城市如罗兹、卡托维兹和克拉科等地表演。
