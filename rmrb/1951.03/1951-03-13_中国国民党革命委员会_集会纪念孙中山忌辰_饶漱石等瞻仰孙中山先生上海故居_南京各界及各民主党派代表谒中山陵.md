Author:
Date: 1951-03-13

　　Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang
　　Holds Meeting to Commemorate Sun Yat-sen's Death Anniversary
　　Rao Shushi and Others Visit Sun Yat-sen's Former Residence in Shanghai
　　Representatives from All Circles in Nanjing and Various Democratic Parties Pay Homage to Sun Yat-sen's Mausoleum
Xinhua News Agency Report: The 12th of March was the 26th anniversary of the death of the great pioneer of the Chinese people's revolution, Sun Yat-sen. On the afternoon of this day, the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang held a grand memorial meeting in Beijing at 7 p.m. Li Jishen, Chairman of the Central Committee of the party, Central Committee members Liu Yazi, Chen Shaoxian, Shao Lizi, Zhu Yunshan, Mei Gongbin, Wang Kunlun, Ning Wu, Li Shizhang, Zhu Xuefan, Chen Qiyuan, Li Junlong, and others, as well as Unity Committee members Zhou Binglin, Sun Yueqi, Yu Xuezhong, Wang Baozhen, and others, along with members and party members from the Beijing branch, totaling more than 300 people, attended. After the memorial ceremony, Chairman Li Jishen first reported on the glorious history of Mr. Sun's revolution and his spirit of continuous development and progress with the times in revolutionary thought. He vehemently denounced the traitor Chiang Kai-shek for seizing party power after Mr. Sun's death, violating all of Mr. Sun's revolutionary teachings, acting perversely, and bringing the entire country and nation to a state of fragmentation. In conclusion, he called on comrades of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang to remember Mr. Sun's great spirit of revolution and saving the country, to sincerely appreciate and obey the wise leadership of Chairman Mao, and to work together to achieve the complete victory of the Chinese people's revolutionary cause. After Li Jishen's report, He Xiangning, a Standing Committee member of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, reported, detailing Mr. Sun's instructions to her on his deathbed. She also highly praised the brilliant victory of the Chinese people's revolutionary cause under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, and shouted: "Followers of Mr. Sun must strive for the complete realization of New Democracy!" Subsequently, General Cai Tingkai, who had just returned from attending the World Peace Council, gave a report, and Standing Committee members Shao Lizi and Chen Qiyuan spoke. The memorial meeting lasted three hours.
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 12th: Today is the 26th anniversary of the death of Mr. Sun Yat-sen. In the morning, Rao Shushi, Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee, Pan Hannian, Deputy Mayor of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, Sheng Pihua, and people from various democratic parties and circles, including Ding Chaowu, Dai Ji, Wu Yun, Chu, Dai Baitao, Su Yanbin, Shen Baowen, and Liang Lieya, totaling more than 50 people, went to Xiangshan Road to pay their respects and visit Mr. Sun Yat-sen's former residence. The Preparatory Committee of the Shanghai Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang also held a memorial ceremony.
Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 12th: Representatives from all circles in Nanjing and various democratic parties paid homage to Sun Yat-sen's Mausoleum this morning to commemorate the 26th anniversary of the death of the great patriot Sun Yat-sen, and presented wreaths to pay their respects. Li Leping, Deputy Mayor of the Nanjing Municipal People's Government, He Jifeng, representative of the Nanjing Municipal Consultative Committee, Shao Hengqiu, head of the Nanjing Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, and others delivered speeches at the memorial meeting, unanimously calling on the people to continue to promote Mr. Sun's patriotic spirit, resolutely oppose US imperialism's rearmament of Japan, and support farmers in completing land reform from their actual work, assist the government in suppressing the activities of counter-revolutionary elements, and smash the conspiracies of US imperialism and the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang to undermine the Chinese people's revolutionary cause.

### 中国国民党革命委员会  集会纪念孙中山忌辰  饶漱石等瞻仰孙中山先生上海故居  南京各界及各民主党派代表谒中山陵

1951-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国国民党革命委员会
    集会纪念孙中山忌辰
    饶漱石等瞻仰孙中山先生上海故居
    南京各界及各民主党派代表谒中山陵
    【本报讯】三月十二日为伟大的中国人民革命先行者孙中山先生逝世二十六周年纪念日，中国国民党革命委员会特于是日下午七时在北京举行盛大纪念会。到该党中央委员会主席李济深、中央委员柳亚子、陈劭先、邵力子、朱蕴山、梅龚彬、王昆仑、宁武、李世璋、朱学范、陈其瑗、李俊龙等，团结委员周炳琳、孙越崎、于学忠、王葆真等，暨北京市分部委员、党员等共三百余人。举行纪念仪式后，首由李济深主席报告孙先生革命的光荣历史及其在革命思想上不断地发展、不断地随着时代前进的精神。他痛斥叛徒蒋介石在孙先生逝世后窃据党权，违反了孙先生的一切革命遗教，倒行逆施，把整个国家和民族弄到支离破碎之境地。他最后号召中国国民党革命委员会同志追念孙先生一生革命救国的伟大精神，一致衷心地感激和服从毛主席的英明领导，共同努力以求中国人民革命事业的彻底完成。李济深报告毕，由中国国民党革命委员会常务委员何香凝报告，她详细追述了孙先生临终时对她的嘱咐，又极力赞扬中国人民革命事业在毛主席和中国共产党领导下的辉煌胜利，并高呼：“中山先生信徒必须为新民主主义彻底实现而奋斗！”继由刚从出席世界和平理事会归来的蔡廷锴将军作报告，邵力子、陈其瑗两常务委员讲话。纪念会共历时三小时。
    【新华社上海十二日电】今日为孙中山先生逝世二十六周年纪念日。上午，华东军政委员会主席饶漱石、上海市人民政府副市长潘汉年、盛丕华及各民主党派、各界人士丁超五、戴戟、吴蕴初、戴白韬、苏延宾、申葆文、梁烈亚等共五十余人，往香山路凭吊瞻仰孙中山先生故居。中国国民党革命委员会上海市分部筹备委员会亦举行了纪念仪式。
    【新华社南京十二日电】南京市各界及各民主党派代表于今晨谒中山陵，纪念伟大爱国者孙中山先生逝世二十六周年，并献花圈致敬。南京市人民政府李乐平副市长、市协商委员会代表何基沣、中国国民党革命委员会南京分部负责人邵恒秋等人在纪念会上发表演说，一致号召人民继续发扬孙先生的爱国主义精神，坚决反对美帝国主义重新武装日本；并从实际工作中支持农民完成土地改革，协助政府镇压反革命分子的活动，粉碎美帝国主义及蒋介石匪帮破坏中国人民革命事业的阴谋。
