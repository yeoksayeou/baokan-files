Title: Berlin People Hold Protest Rally Against West German Remilitarization, European Workers' Committee Holds Inaugural Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-03-30
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 28th: TASS reports from Berlin: The working people of Berlin held a grand protest rally at the Friedrichstadt-Palast on the afternoon of the 27th, opposing the remilitarization of West Germany and the West Berlin sector. The rally was organized by the Preparatory Committee of the European Workers' Congress Against German Remilitarization. Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, attended the rally, along with various delegates to the European Workers' Congress Against German Remilitarization. Speakers at the meeting included Hannes, a representative from West Berlin; Monmousseau, a representative from the French General Confederation of Labor; Bossut, a representative from the French Confederation of Christian Workers; Szanek, a representative from Poland; Jallek, a representative from West Germany; Mováquez, a representative from Spain; Heirt, a representative from Britain; Pocski, a representative from Hungary; Pizzorno, a representative from Italy; and Fiodorov, a representative of Soviet workers. The rally was a powerful demonstration of the solidarity between the German working people and the working people of other European countries in their struggle for peace against German remilitarization.
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 29th: TASS, Berlin, March 28th: The European Workers' Committee Against German Remilitarization, elected at the recent European Workers' Congress against German Remilitarization held in Berlin, issued a communiqué announcing that the committee had held its inaugural meeting, electing the French representative Buton as chairman, the West German representative Trent as vice-chairman, and the Berlin representative Hahnienig as secretary. The committee has begun its work and, in accordance with the resolution unanimously adopted by the European Workers' Congress Against German Remilitarization, has defined its current tasks.

### 反对西德重新军国主义化  柏林人民举行抗议大会  欧洲工人委员会举行成立会议

1951-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　反对西德重新军国主义化
    柏林人民举行抗议大会   
    欧洲工人委员会举行成立会议
    【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社柏林讯：柏林劳动人民于二十七日下午在菲特烈宫举行了一个盛大的抗议大会，反对西德和柏林西区之重新军国主义化。大会是由欧洲工人反对德国重新军国主义化代表大会筹备委员会所组织的。德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克出席大会，其他到会者包括欧洲工人反对德国重新军国主义化代表大会的各个代表。会上发言的有：柏林西区代表汉尼士、法国总工会代表孟莫修、法国天主教劳工联盟代表波士、波兰代表桑克、西德代表雅列克、西班牙代表莫瓦克斯、英国代表赫尔特、匈牙利代表波茨基、意大利代表皮索尔诺与苏联工人代表佛道文。大会是德国劳动人民与欧洲其他国家劳动人民团结一致为争取和平反对德国重新军国主义化而斗争的强大示威。
    【新华社二十九日讯】塔斯社柏林二十八日讯：最近在柏林召开的反对德国重新军国主义化的欧洲工人代表大会所选出的欧洲工人反对德国重新军国主义化委员会，发表公报宣布，委员会已举行成立会议，选出了法国代表布敦为主席，西德代表特伦为副主席，柏林代表哈恩尼希为书记。委员会已开始工作，并根据反对德国重新军国主义化的欧洲工人代表大会所一致通过的决议，确定了当前的任务。
