Title: Decision of the Ministry of Labor of the Central People's Government on Promulgating the Trial Implementation of the "Draft Implementation Regulations of the Labor Insurance Regulations of the People's Republic of China"
Author:
Date: 1951-03-26
Page: 1

　　Ministry of Labor of the Central People's Government
    Decision on Promulgating the Trial Implementation of the "Draft Implementation Regulations of the Labor Insurance Regulations of the People's Republic of China"
    In order to facilitate the implementation of the Labor Insurance Regulations of the People's Republic of China, the Ministry, in conjunction with the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, has formulated the Draft Implementation Regulations of the Labor Insurance Regulations of the People's Republic of China, which is hereby promulgated for trial implementation. The trial period is tentatively set from now until the end of October 1951. During the trial period, if the enterprise administration or capital side and trade union organizations at all levels encounter any questions or difficulties in handling matters in accordance with the draft implementation regulations, they may report to the local labor administrative authorities for answers or handling at any time. Local labor administrative authorities should report the answered or handled matters to the Ministry for record, and those that cannot be answered or handled should be reported to the Ministry for handling. At the same time, during the trial period, anyone who has opinions on the draft regulations may directly send a letter to the Ministry. After collecting opinions from all parties, the draft will be studied, revised, and then officially promulgated for implementation.
                              March 25, 1951

### 中央人民政府劳动部  关于公布试行《中华人民共和国劳动保险条例实施细则草案》的决定

1951-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府劳动部
    关于公布试行《中华人民共和国劳动保险条例实施细则草案》的决定
    为了便利实施中华人民共和国劳动保险条例，本部协同中华全国总工会制定了中华人民共和国劳动保险条例实施细则草案，特予公布试行。试行期限，暂定为自即日起至一九五一年十月底止。在试行期内，凡实行劳动保险的企业行政或资方与各级工会组织，对于按照该实施细则草案办理事项发生疑问或困难时，得随时报告当地劳动行政机关解答或处理。各地劳动行政机关应将已解答或已处理事项报告本部备案，无法解答或无法处理者应报告本部处理之。同时，在试行期内，凡对该细则草案有意见者，均可直接函告本部。俟搜集各方意见，将该草案加以研究修正后，再正式公布施行。
                              一九五一年三月二十五日
