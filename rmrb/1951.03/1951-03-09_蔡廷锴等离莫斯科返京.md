Title: Cai Tingkai and Others Leave Moscow for Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1951-03-09
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th: Cai Tingkai, Peng Zemin, Xiao San, and Wu Yaozong, the representatives of our country attending the World Peace Council, left Moscow by plane for Beijing today. Edward, the representative of India attending the World Peace Council, was also traveling with them. Those who saw them off included Zeng Yongquan, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, Military Attaché Ji He, Counselor Ge Baoquan, and representatives from the Soviet Peace Protection Committee and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 蔡廷锴等离莫斯科返京

1951-03-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　蔡廷锴等离莫斯科返京
    【新华社莫斯科八日电】我国出席世界和平理事会代表蔡廷锴、彭泽民、萧三、吴耀宗，今日乘飞机离莫斯科返京。同行者有印度出席世界和平理事会代表艾德华。送行者有我国驻苏大使馆代办曾涌泉、武官吉合、参赞戈宝权和苏联保卫和平委员会、苏联对外文化协会代表。
