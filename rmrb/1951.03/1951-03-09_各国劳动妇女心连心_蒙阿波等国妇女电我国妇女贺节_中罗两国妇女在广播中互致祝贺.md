Title: Laboring Women Around the World United as One; Women from Mongolia, Albania, and Other Countries Send Greetings to Chinese Women for the Holiday; Chinese and Romanian Women Exchange Greetings via Radio

Author:

Date: 1951-03-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 8th:

Laboring Women Around the World United as One
Women from Mongolia, Albania, and Other Countries Send Greetings to Chinese Women for the Holiday
Chinese and Romanian Women Exchange Greetings via Radio
Women from the Mongolian People's Republic, the People's Republic of Albania, and other countries have sent telegrams to Chinese women, congratulating them on International Working Women's Day, March 8th.

Udvall, Chairman of the Central Council of Working Women of the Mongolian People's Republic, said in a telegram to Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation: "I wish Chinese sisters further great success in the work of prospering your great motherland and in the struggle to consolidate world peace and democracy."

The Albanian Women's Union said in a telegram to the All-China Democratic Women's Federation: "Albanian women extend to you our heartfelt greetings and hope that this holiday will strengthen our common struggle to defend world peace."

Female workers from the Polish Textile Workers' Union also sent a telegram to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, forwarding sincere greetings of friendship to Chinese female textile workers.

On the 8th, women from Romania and China also exchanged greetings through radio stations in both countries. Romanian women who gave radio speeches were Liuba Chisinievski, a member of the Presidium of the Romanian National Assembly, Vice-Chairman of the General Trade Union, and member of the Central Committee of the Democratic Women's Federation; Mania Sinca, a shock worker in the textile industry; and female poet Maria Banus. Chinese women who gave radio speeches were Cai Chang, a representative of women's circles, Chen Shaomin, a representative of trade unions, and Ding Ling, a representative of cultural circles.

### 各国劳动妇女心连心  蒙阿波等国妇女电我国妇女贺节  中罗两国妇女在广播中互致祝贺

1951-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　各国劳动妇女心连心
    蒙阿波等国妇女电我国妇女贺节
    中罗两国妇女在广播中互致祝贺
    【新华社八日讯】蒙古人民共和国、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国等国妇女纷纷致电中国妇女，祝贺“三八”国际劳动妇女节。
    蒙古人民共和国劳动妇女中央理事会主席乌德瓦尔在致中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅的电报中说：“我谨祝中国姊妹们在繁荣你们伟大祖国的工作中，在巩固全世界的和平与民主的斗争中，获得进一步的巨大成功。”
    阿尔巴尼亚妇女联合会在致中华全国民主妇女联合会的电报中说：“阿尔巴尼亚妇女谨向你们致衷心的敬礼，并希望这个节日能加强我们共同保卫世界和平的斗争。”
    波兰纺织工会女工也致电中华全国总工会转纺织女工，对中国妇女致诚挚的友谊的贺意。
    八日，罗马尼亚妇女界和中国妇女亦通过两国广播电台互致祝贺。罗马尼亚妇女界发表广播演说的是罗马尼亚国民议会主席团之一、总工会副主席、民主妇女联合会中央委员会委员流芭·吉西尼夫斯基，纺织工业突击工人玛尼亚·辛卡，女诗人玛丽亚·芭瑙丝。中国妇女界发表广播演说的是妇女界代表蔡畅、工会代表陈少敏、文化界代表丁玲。
