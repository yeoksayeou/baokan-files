Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-03-03
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    This Mongolian female rider, with her beloved Ujumchin-bred steed, participated in the horse racing meet. She, like other Mongolian male and female riders, has been skilled at riding horses since childhood. Her horse is a famous pacer from the Mongolian grasslands, known for its light build and endurance, capable of running sixty li in forty to fifty minutes.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    这位蒙古族的女骑手，带着她心爱的乌珠木沁出产的骏马，参加了赛马的大会。她和蒙古族其他男女骑手们一样，自幼就善于骑马。她的马是蒙古草原上有名的一种走马，它的躯体轻巧，能耐长跑，在四十分钟至五十分钟之内，能跑六十万里。
