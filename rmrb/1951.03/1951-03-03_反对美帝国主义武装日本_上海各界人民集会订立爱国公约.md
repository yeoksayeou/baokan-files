Title: Oppose American Imperialism Arming Japan - Shanghai People's Meeting Adopts Patriotic Covenant
Author:
Date: 1951-03-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency:

　　Oppose American Imperialism Arming Japan
    Shanghai People's Meeting Adopts Patriotic Covenant

    [This report from our paper] According to Xinhua News Agency: The Shanghai Municipal People's Representative Conference Against American Arming of Japan was held on February 27th and 28th. Attending the meeting were representatives from democratic parties, people's organizations, people from all walks of life, religious circles, ethnic minorities, returned overseas Chinese, and families of martyrs and military personnel, totaling more than 820 people. The meeting adopted a presidium of 54 people, including Ba Jin, Jin Zhonghua, Hu Wenyao, Mei Lanfang, and Xie Xuehong, and an executive chairman of 15 people, including Liu Changsheng, Xia Yan, Liu Hongsheng, and Zhu Kongjia.

    Liu Changsheng, Chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, delivered a report to the meeting entitled "Further Develop the Great Mass Movement to Resist America and Aid Korea and Oppose American Re-armament of Japan." At the meeting, 42 representatives spoke successively, angrily denouncing the heinous crimes of the Japanese invaders in Shanghai during the Anti-Japanese War, including the massacre of people, the arson and burning of industrial and commercial buildings, and the destruction of science and culture. They unanimously agreed with Liu Changsheng's report and resolved to take practical action to oppose American re-armament of Japan.

    The meeting unanimously adopted the twelve-article "Shanghai People's Common Patriotic Covenant" and fully endorsed the World Peace Council's declaration on concluding a peace covenant. The full text of the Patriotic Covenant is as follows: 1. Support Chairman Mao, support the Chinese Communist Party, support the Central People's Government, and support the Chinese People's Liberation Army. 2. The people of all circles in Shanghai unite, resolutely implement the Common Program, and strive to implement all decrees of the People's Government. 3. Continue to support the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army with various practical actions to complete the great task of resisting America and aiding Korea and defending the country. 4. Resolutely oppose the U.S. unilateral peace treaty with Japan and the rearmament of Japan, and fight to defend world peace. 5. Contribute the greatest strength of the people of Shanghai to strengthen national defense construction, consolidate national defense, and defend the people's victory. Oppose American aggression against Taiwan and fight for the liberation of Taiwan. 6. Thoroughly eliminate all aggressive forces and influences of American imperialism in Shanghai. All cultural, educational, relief agencies and religious organizations in Shanghai subsidized or operated by American imperialism must resolutely break off relations with American imperialism. 7. Assist the government in eliminating bandits and special agents, expose and monitor all counter-revolutionaries, eliminate counter-revolutionary rumors, suppress counter-revolutionary activities, and consolidate social security. 8. Implement the policy of benefiting both labor and capital, improve production and management methods, continue to give play to the spirit of self-reliance, overcome various difficulties, and carry out patriotic competition. 9. Do not owe taxes, do not evade taxes, do not leak taxes, do not engage in speculation, do not cut corners, strengthen urban and rural exchanges, stabilize the market, and complete the state's financial tasks. 10. Strengthen patriotic and internationalist study and propaganda, improve scientific and technological level, strengthen physical exercise, and do a good job in one's own work. 11. Resolutely complete land reform in the suburbs and develop agricultural production. 12. Cherish state property and keep state secrets.

### 反对美帝国主义武装日本  上海各界人民集会订立爱国公约

1951-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　反对美帝国主义武装日本
    上海各界人民集会订立爱国公约
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：上海市各界人民反对美国武装日本代表会议于二月二十七、八两日举行。出席会议的有各民主党派、各人民团体、各阶层人民、宗教界、少数民族、归国华侨及烈士家属和军人家属等代表共八百二十多人。会议通过巴金、金仲华、胡文耀、梅兰芳、谢雪红等五十四人为主席团，刘长胜、夏衍、刘鸿生、朱孔嘉等十五人为执行主席。
    中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会上海分会主席刘长胜向会议作了题为“进一步展开抗美援朝、反对美国重新武装日本的伟大群众运动”的报告。会上，有四十二位代表相继发言，他们愤怒地控诉了抗日战争时日寇在上海屠杀人民、纵火焚烧工商建筑、摧残科学和文化的万恶罪行，并一致表示同意刘长胜的报告，决以实际行动反对美国重新武装日本。
    会议全体代表一致通过十二项《上海各界人民共同爱国公约》，并完全拥护世界和平理事会关于缔结和平公约的宣言。爱国公约全文如下：一、拥护毛主席，拥护中国共产党，拥护中央人民政府，拥护中国人民解放军。二、上海各界人民团结起来，坚决贯彻共同纲领，努力实行人民政府一切法令。三、继续以各种实际行动，努力支援中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军，完成抗美援朝、保家卫国的伟大任务。四、坚决反对美国对日片面媾和及重新武装日本，为保卫世界和平而斗争。五、贡献上海人民最大力量，加强国防建设，巩固国防，保卫人民的胜利。反对美国侵略台湾，为解放台湾而斗争。六、彻底肃清美帝国主义在上海的一切侵略势力与影响，一切美帝国主义津贴或经营的上海文化教育救济机关及宗教团体，坚决与美帝国主义断绝关系。七、协助政府肃清匪特，检举和监视一切反革命分子，消灭反革命谣言，镇压反革命活动，巩固社会治安。八、贯彻劳资两利政策，改善生产与经营的方式，继续发挥自力更生的精神，克服各种困难，开展爱国主义竞赛。九、不欠税、不逃税、不漏税、不投机倒把、不偷工减料，加强城乡交流，稳定市场，完成国家财经任务。十、加强爱国主义与国际主义学习与宣传，提高科学技术水平，加强体格锻炼，做好本位工作。十一、坚决完成郊区土地改革，发展农业生产。十二、爱护国家财产，保守国家机密。
