Title: On Dispatching an Investigation Team to Malaya, Xiong Jin Ding Cables Attlee, Requesting Entry Procedures and Granting the Investigation Team the Rights They Should Enjoy
Author:
Date: 1951-03-09
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 8th: Xiong Jin Ding, Director of the Chinese People's Relief Committee for Malayan Refugees, recently sent a telegram to British Prime Minister Attlee regarding the dispatch of an investigation team to Malaya to investigate the suffering of overseas Chinese. The telegram reads as follows:

To Mr. Attlee, Prime Minister of Great Britain, London:

　　I hereby notify you that the Chinese People's Relief Committee for Malayan Refugees has organized an investigation team to investigate the suffering of overseas Chinese in Malaya.

　　The list of the investigation team is as follows:

　　Head: Pu HuarRen, Deputy Heads: Wu Zhizhi, Du Ai, Zhang Chukun.

　　Members: Wu Feng, Jin Ziming, Hu Yisheng, Chen Jingzhen, Qi Yun, Huang Zhensheng, Huang Luping, Huang Yaomian, Huang Dingchen, Zhang Zhuangfei, Yang Jingtong, Lu Xinyuan, Nie Weiqing.

　　The above seventeen people all hold People's Republic of China passports and certificates from the Chinese People's Relief Committee for Malayan Refugees. Please process their entry procedures and grant them the rights that personnel engaged in relief and welfare work should enjoy.

　　I am writing to inform you of this matter and hope you will investigate and reply by telegram.

　　Xiong Jin Ding, Director of the Chinese People's Relief Committee for Malayan Refugees
　　March 8, 1951

### 就派遣调查团赴马来亚事  熊瑾玎致电艾德礼  要求办理入境手续并给予调查团以应享之权利

1951-03-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　就派遣调查团赴马来亚事
    熊瑾玎致电艾德礼
    要求办理入境手续并给予调查团以应享之权利
    【新华社八日讯】中国人民救济马来亚难侨委员会主任熊瑾玎顷就派遣调查团赴马来亚调查华侨受难情况事致电英国首相艾德礼，电文如下：伦敦英国首相艾德礼先生：
    兹特通知阁下：中国人民救济马来亚难侨委员会已组织调查团，即将赴马来亚调查华侨受难情况。
    调查团名单如后：
    团长：浦化人、副团长伍治之、杜埃、张楚琨。
    团员：吴风、金子明、胡一声、陈经镇、戚云、黄振声、黄绿萍、黄药眠、黄鼎臣、张壮飞、杨静桐、卢心远、聂维庆。
    以上十七人均持有中华人民共和国出国护照及中国人民救济马来亚难侨委员会证件，请予办理入境手续，并请给予从事救济及福利事业人员所应享之权利。
    专此奉达，即希查照并电复为荷。
    中国人民救济马来亚难侨委员会主任熊瑾玎
                                            一九五一年三月八日
