Title: Shanghai Public and Private Enterprises Actively Apply for Registration to Implement Labor Insurance Regulations
Author:
Date: 1951-03-11
Page: 2

Shanghai public and private factories and enterprises with more than 100 employees are actively applying for registration to implement the Labor Insurance Regulations. From March 1st to March 3rd, 108 units, including the China Jingwei Jute Textile Factory, the Lun Chang Yarn Factory, the Shanghai Public Transportation Company, and the Shanghai District Company of the China People's Steamship Company, have gone to the Shanghai Municipal People's Government Labor Bureau to apply for registration.
        (Xinhua News Agency)

### 上海公私企业踊跃申请登记  实行劳动保险条例

1951-03-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　上海公私企业踊跃申请登记实行劳动保险条例
    上海市百人以上的公、私营工厂企业，踊跃申请登记实行劳动保险条例。自三月一日起，迄三日止，前往上海市人民政府劳动局申请登记者已有中华经纬麻纺织厂、纶昌纱厂、上海市公共交通公司、中国人民轮船公司上海区公司等一百零八个单位。
      （新华社）
