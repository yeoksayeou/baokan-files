Title: Cai Chang, Chairman of the National Women's Federation of China, Sends Greetings to the International Democratic Women's Federation and Women of the Soviet Union and Korea for International Women's Day; Women of the Soviet Union and Korea Also Send Greetings to Women of China

Author:

Date: 1951-03-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 6th—Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, today sent telegrams to Eugénie Cotton, President of the International Democratic Women's Federation, Popova, Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, and Pak Chong-ae, Chairman of the Korean Democratic Women's Union, respectively, to warmly celebrate International Women's Day on March 8th. The telegrams are excerpted as follows:

	To Eugénie Cotton, President of the International Democratic Women's Federation
	Dear Madame Cotton:
	On the occasion of International Women's Day on March 8th, I would like to extend my warmest respect to you on behalf of the women of China, and extend my heartfelt congratulations to the sisters all over the world who are fighting for peace! Under your leadership, Chinese women will expand the movement to resist America and aid Korea, unite with peace-loving women all over the world to realize the resolutions of the Berlin Conference of the International Democratic Women's Federation Council, oppose the American imperialists' arming of Japan and West Germany, so as to stop the threat of a new war, defend world lasting peace and safeguard the rights of women and children.
					Cai Chang, March 6th

	To Popova, Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee
	Dear Madame Popova:
	On the occasion of International Women's Day on March 8th, all Chinese women extend their warm regards to you! And sincerely congratulate the glorious Soviet sisters on the brilliant achievements they have made in the construction of communism and in the struggle to defend world peace. Your efforts and achievements have powerfully strengthened the forces of the peace camp and inspired the confidence of women all over the world to fight for peace. Your sincere friends, the Chinese women, will take you as an example, actively participate in the construction of the motherland, expand and deepen the movement to resist America and aid Korea, oppose the American imperialists' rearming of Japan and West Germany, stop the threat of a new war, and defend world lasting peace.
					Cai Chang, March 6th

	To Pak Chong-ae, Chairman of the Korean Democratic Women's Union
	Dear Madame Pak Chong-ae:
	On behalf of all the women of China, I would like to extend my warmest congratulations to you and all the heroic Korean sisters on International Women's Day on March 8th, and extend my heartfelt condolences to the Korean sisters and mothers who have been ravaged by the American invading army. All Chinese women, together with all the Chinese people, will continue to expand the movement to support your just struggle, resolutely fight alongside you to achieve the final victory in defeating American imperialism. We firmly believe that the flesh-and-blood fighting friendship and strong will of the Chinese and Korean people will be the most powerful guarantee for the final victory over our common mortal enemy—American imperialism.
					Cai Chang, March 6th

Xinhua News Agency, March 6th—The Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee and the Korean Democratic Women's Union have recently sent telegrams to the All-China Democratic Women's Federation to warmly celebrate International Women's Day on March 8th. The telegrams are as follows:

	Telegram of congratulations from the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee
	Dear Chinese sisters and women friends:
	We would like to extend to you, the glorious daughters of the great Chinese people, our warm congratulations on International Women's Day on March 8th, a day to review the strength of progressive women from all countries striving for the security and friendship of all peoples and defending world peace.
	Dear friends, we wish you further success in developing the economic and cultural prosperity of the People's Republic of China, in pursuing the happy future of your children, and in your lives.
	Long live the unbreakable friendship between the women of China and the Soviet Union!
	Long live the heroic Chinese women!
					Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, March 5th, Moscow

	Telegram of congratulations from the Korean Democratic Women's Union
	To Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation in Beijing:
	On International Women's Day, on this day when democratic women around the world who defend peace are holding demonstrations, the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Women's Union extends warm greetings to you and all the Chinese women you represent who are fighting for lasting world peace and the prosperity of their country. The Korean women, fighting for the liberation and independence of their motherland and against the American aggressors who are attempting to dominate the world, are filled with the determination to carry out our just war to victory, inspired by your sisterly support. We wish you great success in the common struggle against the new war instigators headed by the American imperialists. We firmly believe that in the struggle for lasting world peace and in the democratic construction of your country, our friendship and unity will be further consolidated.
					Pak Chong-ae, Chairman of the Korean Democratic Women's Union

### 我国民主妇联主席蔡畅  电国际妇联及苏朝妇女贺“三八”节  苏联朝鲜两国妇女亦致电我国妇女祝贺

1951-03-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国民主妇联主席蔡畅
    电国际妇联及苏朝妇女贺“三八”节
    苏联朝鲜两国妇女亦致电我国妇女祝贺
    【新华社六日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅，今日分别致电国际民主妇女联合会戈登夫人、苏联妇女反法西斯委员会波波娃主席、及朝鲜民主妇女同盟朴正爱主席热烈祝贺“三八”国际妇女节。电文分录如下：
            致国际民主妇女联合会戈登夫人电亲爱的戈登夫人：
    际此“三八”国际妇女节，谨代表中国妇女向你热烈致敬，并向全世界为和平而奋斗的姊妹们致衷心的祝贺！在你的领导下，中国妇女将扩大抗美援朝运动，与全世界爱好和平的妇女团结一致，来实现国际民主妇女联合会理事会柏林会议的决议，反对美帝国主义武装日本和西德，以制止新战争的威胁，保卫世界持久和平和维护妇女儿童的权利。
                                                 蔡畅  三月六日
    致苏联妇女反法西斯委员会波波娃主席电亲爱的波波娃主席：
    值此“三八”国际妇女节，全中国妇女向你致热烈的敬礼！并衷心祝贺光荣的苏联姊妹们在共产主义的建设，在保卫世界和平的斗争中所获得的光辉成就。你们的努力和成就有力地增强了和平阵营的力量，鼓舞着全世界妇女为和平而斗争的信心。你们诚挚的朋友——中国妇女，将以你们为榜样，积极参加祖国的建设，扩大深入抗美援朝运动，反对美帝国主义重新武装日本和西德，制止新战争的威胁，保卫世界持久和平。
                                                蔡畅  三月六日
            致朝鲜民主妇女同盟朴正爱主席电亲爱的朴正爱主席：
    我谨代表全中国妇女向你及全体英勇的朝鲜姊妹们，热烈祝贺“三八”国际妇女节，并向惨遭美国侵略军蹂躏的朝鲜姊妹和母亲们致衷心的慰问。全中国妇女与全中国人民一道，将继续扩大支援你们的正义斗争的运动，坚决和你们并肩作战，以取得击败美帝国主义的最后胜利。我们深信，中朝两国人民血肉相连的战斗友谊和坚强意志，将是最后战胜我们共同的死敌——美帝国主义的最有力的保证。
                                                蔡畅  三月六日
    【新华社六日讯】苏联妇女反法西斯委员会及朝鲜民主妇女同盟，近先后致电我中华全国民主妇女联合会，热烈祝贺“三八”国际妇女节，电文如下：
            苏联妇女反法西斯委员会贺电亲爱的中国姊妹和妇女朋友们：
    我们谨向你们，伟大中国人民的光荣女儿，热烈祝贺“三八”国际妇女节，这一天是检阅争取各族人民的安全与友好，保卫全世界和平的各国进步妇女力量的一天。
    亲爱的朋友们，我们谨祝你们在发展中华人民共和国的经济和文化繁荣，在谋取你们儿童的幸福未来的工作中以及在你们的生活中进一步成功。
    中苏两国妇女牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    英勇的中国妇女万岁！
                            苏联妇女反法西斯委员会  三月五日，莫斯科
            朝鲜民主妇女同盟贺电北京中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅：
    在国际妇女节，在保卫和平的全世界民主妇女举行示威的这一天，朝鲜民主妇女同盟中央委员会向你及你所代表的为争取全世界持久和平、繁荣自己国家而斗争的全中国妇女致热烈敬礼。争取祖国的解放和独立，反对企图独霸全世界的美国侵略者的朝鲜妇女，在你们姊妹般的支援的鼓舞下，充满了将我们的正义战争进行到胜利为止的决心。谨祝你们在反对以美帝国主义者为首的新战争挑拨者的共同斗争中，获得巨大成功。我们深信在争取全世界持久和平的斗争中，在贵国的民主建设中，我们的友谊和团结将更加巩固。
                                      朝鲜民主妇女同盟主席  朴正爱
