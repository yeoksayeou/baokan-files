Title: Indonesian Women Support the Conclusion of a Peace Pact
Author:
Date: 1951-03-13
Page: 4

　　Indonesian Women Support the Conclusion of a Peace Pact
    [Xinhua News Agency, The Hague, 8th] According to the Dutch newspaper *De Waarheid*, representatives of Indonesian women's organizations recently held a meeting in Yogyakarta. The meeting unanimously approved the declaration of the Berlin meeting of the World Peace Council regarding the demand that the five major powers conclude a peace pact.
    The resolution adopted at the meeting stated: Indonesian women's organizations must establish contact with the Women's International Democratic Federation and become its members.

### 印尼妇女拥护缔结和平公约

1951-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　印尼妇女拥护缔结和平公约
    【新华社十二日讯】据塔斯社海牙八日讯，荷兰《真理报》消息：印尼妇女团体代表最近曾在日惹举行会议。会议一致赞成世界和平理事会柏林会议关于要求五大国缔结和平公约的宣言。
    会议通过的决议指出：印尼的妇女团体必须与国际民主妇女联合会建立联系，并成为它的会员。
