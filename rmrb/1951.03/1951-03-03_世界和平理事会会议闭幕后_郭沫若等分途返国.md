Title: After the World Peace Council Meeting Concludes, Guo Moruo and Others Return Home Separately
Author:
Date: 1951-03-03
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 2nd -- After the conclusion of the First Session of the World Peace Council, the head of the Chinese delegation, Guo Moruo, and delegates Peng Zemin and Wu Yaozong, departed Berlin by plane on the 1st, heading to Moscow to return home. The Minister of National Education of the German Democratic Republic, Mr. Wandel, representatives from the German Peace Committee, and Mr. Ji Pengfei, the head of the Chinese diplomatic mission to Germany with the title of ambassador, went to the airport to see them off. The poet Xiao San and General Cai Tingkai have accepted an invitation from the Bulgarian-Soviet Friendship Association to go to Sofia to participate in a meeting of the Bulgarian-Soviet Friendship Association. Other delegates will go to Leipzig to participate in the International Spring Industrial Fair, and then travel to Prague, Warsaw, Moscow and other places for a short stay before returning home.

### 世界和平理事会会议闭幕后  郭沫若等分途返国

1951-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界和平理事会会议闭幕后
    郭沫若等分途返国
    【新华社柏林二日电】我国出席世界和平理事会第一届会议代表团团长郭沫若及代表彭泽民、吴耀宗，已于一日乘机离柏林，取道莫斯科返国。德意志民主共和国国民教育部长汪戴尔、德和平委员会的代表，我国驻德大使衔外交使团团长姬鹏飞等均往机场送行。诗人萧三及蔡廷锴将军已接受保加利亚保苏友好协会邀请，赴索非亚参加保苏友好协会会议。其他代表则将前往莱比锡，参加国际春季工业博览会，然后转往布拉格、华沙、莫斯科等地稍作逗留后返国。
