Title: Representatives of China to the Peace Council, Cai Tingkai and Others, Return to Beijing; Indian Representative Edward Arrives in Beijing on the Same Flight

Author:
Date: 1951-03-11
Page: 1

   Xinhua News Agency, 10th: The Chinese representatives to the World Peace Council, Cai Tingkai, Peng Zemin, Xiao San, and Wu Yaozong, returned to Beijing at 3:00 PM on the afternoon of the 10th. Edward, the Indian representative to the World Peace Council, arrived in Beijing on the same flight. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Zhang Xiruo, Wu Maosun, Hong Shen, Wang Zhuoru, and Mr. Gore, the Counselor of the Indian Embassy in China, and others.

### 我出席和平理事会代表  蔡廷锴等返抵北京  印度代表爱德华同机抵京

1951-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

   　我出席和平理事会代表
     蔡廷锴等返抵北京  
     印度代表爱德华同机抵京
    【新华社十日讯】中国出席世界和平理事会代表蔡廷锴、彭泽民、萧三、吴耀宗于十日下午三时返抵北京。同机抵京者有印度出席世界和平理事会代表爱德华。赴机场欢迎者有张奚若、吴茂荪、洪深、王倬如以及印度驻华大使馆参赞高尔等多人。
