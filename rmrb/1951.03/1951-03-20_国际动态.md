　　International News
    International Association of Journalists Presidium Holds Meeting in Berlin
    The Presidium of the International Association of Journalists held a meeting in Berlin on the 15th and 16th. Secretary-General Hronek of the International Association of Journalists reported on the association's activities since the third representative conference held in Helsinki. After discussing the resolutions of the Second World Peace Congress and the resolutions of the First Session of the World Peace Council, the Presidium formulated a plan for the International Association of Journalists to implement the resolutions of the World Peace Council, calling on all honest journalists to actively fight for peace and to step up the exposure of all newspapers, broadcasting stations and journalists that promote new wars. The Presidium also expressed the International Association of Journalists' support for the European Workers' Congress against the remilitarization of Germany, which will be held in Berlin on March 24.
  "Korea and China" Fine Arts Exhibition Held in Prague
    The "Korea and China" fine arts exhibition, sponsored by the Czechoslovak Committee for the Defense of Peace, opened in Prague on the 16th. Famous painter Gros gave the opening speech, saying: "This exhibition pays tribute to the heroic Korean people and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army who are fighting side by side, driving the American aggressors out of Korea, and defending world peace." More than one hundred paintings, sculptures, and cartoons by famous Czechoslovak artists are participating in the exhibition, including pencil portraits of Chairman Mao Zedong and General Kim Il-sung, as well as paintings depicting the atrocities of the US military in Korea.
  World Peace Congress Burmese Branch Holds Demonstration
    In response to all the resolutions and actions of the World Peace Congress, the Burmese branch of the World Peace Congress held a peace demonstration in Yangon on the 11th. More than a thousand people participated in the conference. The conference called on all peace-loving people in Myanmar, regardless of ethnicity, party affiliation, or religious belief, to join hands and fight against the aggressive war launched by US imperialism.
    The conference passed the following three resolutions: 1. Protest against the UN General Assembly's illegal slander of the People's Republic of China as an "aggressor"; 2. Defend permanent world peace; 3. Resolutely oppose the establishment of military bases in Myanmar by imperialist aggressors.

### 国际动态

1951-03-20
第4版()
专栏：国际动态

　　国际动态
    国际新闻记者协会主席团在柏林举行会议
    国际新闻记者协会主席团于十五、十六两日在柏林举行会议。国际记协秘书长赫朗尼克在会上报告了该会在赫尔辛基举行第三届代表大会以来的活动。主席团在讨论了第二届世界和平大会的决议及世界和平理事会第一届会议的决议之后，制订了国际记协实行世界和平理事会决议的方案，号召一切诚实的新闻记者积极为和平而斗争，并加紧揭露宣传新战争的一切报纸、广播机关和新闻记者。主席团并表示国际记协拥护并支持将于三月二十四日在柏林召开的反对德国重新军国主义化的欧洲工人代表大会。
  布拉格举行“朝鲜与中国”造型艺术展览会
    捷克斯洛伐克保卫和平委员会主办的“朝鲜与中国”造型艺术展览会于十六日在布拉格开幕。名画家格罗斯致开幕词称：“这个展览会是向正在并肩作战、把美国侵略者赶出朝鲜和保卫世界和平的英勇的朝鲜人民和中国人民志愿军致敬。”这次参加展出的有一百多件捷克斯洛伐克著名艺术家的绘画、雕刻和漫画，其中有毛泽东主席和金日成将军的铅笔画像，以及美军在朝鲜的暴行的绘画等。
  世界和平大会缅甸分会举行示威
    世界和平大会缅甸分会为响应世界和平大会的一切决议和行动，十一日在仰光举行和平示威大会。参加大会的有一千多人。大会号召全缅甸一切爱好和平的人们，应不分民族、党派、宗教信仰；携起手来，为反对美帝国主义发动侵略战争而斗争。
    大会通过下列三项决议：一、抗议联合国大会非法诬蔑中华人民共和国为“侵略者”；二、保卫世界永久和平；三、坚决反对帝国主义侵略者在缅甸建立军事基地。
