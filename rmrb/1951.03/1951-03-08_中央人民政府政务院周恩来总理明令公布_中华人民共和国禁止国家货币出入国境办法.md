Title: Premier Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council Promulgates Order: Measures of the People's Republic of China for Prohibiting National Currency from Entering and Leaving the Country

Author:
Date: 1951-03-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council promulgated the "Measures of the People's Republic of China for Prohibiting National Currency from Entering and Leaving the Country" on March 6th. The full text of the measures is as follows:

	I. These measures are formulated to consolidate the value of the national currency, stabilize finance, and protect the people's property.

	II. The national currency mentioned in these measures refers to the currency issued by the People's Bank of China and the local currency specially authorized to be issued by the Central People's Government.

	III. The People's Republic of China prohibits national currency from entering and leaving the country. Anyone who carries or smuggles national currency into or out of the country shall have it confiscated.

	IV. Those who carry or smuggle national currency into or out of the country and are suspected of undermining the national currency, or those who smuggle counterfeit or altered Renminbi into the country, the investigating authority shall send the witnesses together with the evidence to the local judicial authority for handling according to law.

	V. If national currency is found to be concealed in mail entering or leaving the country, the currency shall be confiscated. If the concealed currency is used for criminal purposes or is suspected of being counterfeit or altered, the investigating authority shall send it together with the mail to the local judicial authority for handling according to law.

	VI. These measures shall come into effect from the date of promulgation.

### 中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理明令公布  中华人民共和国禁止国家货币出入国境办法

1951-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理明令公布
    中华人民共和国禁止国家货币出入国境办法
    【新华社七日讯】中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理于三月六日明令公布“中华人民共和国禁止国家货币出入国境办法”。办法全文如下：
    一、为巩固国家货币币值，稳定金融，保障人民财产，特制定本办法。
    二、本办法所称国家货币系指中国人民银行发行之货币，及中央人民政府特许发行之地方货币。
    三、中华人民共和国禁止国家货币出入国境。凡携带或私运国家货币出入国境者，一律没收。
    四、携带或私运国家货币出入国境有破坏国家货币嫌疑者，或私运伪造、变造之人民币进入国境者，查获机关应将人证一并送当地司法机关，依法处理。
    五、出入国境之邮件，查有夹带国家货币者，没收其货币。此项夹带之货币如系供犯罪之用或有伪造、变造嫌疑者，查获机关应连同邮件送当地司法机关依法处理。
    六、本办法自公布之日施行。
