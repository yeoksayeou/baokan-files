Title: Changsha Learns from the Lessons of the Lingling County Incident: Organizing Residents to Strictly Guard Against Special Agents; 40,000 People March in Support of the Regulations on Punishing Counter-Revolutionaries
Author:
Date: 1951-03-26
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, 23rd: Since the arson incident by special agents in Lingling County, Hunan Province, the people of Changsha City have heightened their vigilance against the sabotage activities of counter-revolutionary special agents. On the 18th, 40,000 residents held a large-scale march against special agents and in support of the Regulations on Punishing Counter-Revolutionaries. In recent days, residents of various districts have also widely studied the Regulations on Punishing Counter-Revolutionaries. Generally, several households form a study group, and someone with a higher level of education is selected to explain the content of the regulations, and small-scale discussions are organized in conjunction with the Lingling incident. After the march, study, and discussions, residents of various districts have actively organized public security groups to strictly guard against various sabotage activities by special agents and bandits. Some residents have voluntarily reported lurking counter-revolutionaries to the city's public security organs.

### 长沙接受零陵县事件教训  组织居民严防特务  四万人游行拥护惩治反革命条例

1951-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　长沙接受零陵县事件教训
    组织居民严防特务       
    四万人游行拥护惩治反革命条例
    【新华社长沙二十三日电】湖南长沙市各界人民自零陵县城特务纵火事件发生后，提高了对反革命特务分子进行破坏活动的警惕性。十八日，四万居民举行了反对特务、拥护惩治反革命条例的大游行。连日来各区居民并普遍学习惩治反革命条例。一般是以若干户组成一学习小组，推选文化水平较高的人讲解条例内容，并结合零陵事件组织小型座谈。经过游行、学习、座谈后，各区居民都积极地组织治安小组，严防特务匪徒的各种破坏活动。有些居民已自动向该市公安机关检举潜伏的反革命分子。
