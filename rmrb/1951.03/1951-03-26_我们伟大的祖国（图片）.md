Our Great Motherland

    Land reform is changing everything in the countryside, including rural education. This is a scene of peasant children attending class in a rural primary school in Pingle Township, Luoyang, Henan. This primary school was established during the land reform.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    土地改革改变着农村的一切，其中也改变着农村的教育。这是在河南洛阳平乐乡一所农村小学里农民子弟正在听课的情形。这个小学就是在土地改革期间举办的。
