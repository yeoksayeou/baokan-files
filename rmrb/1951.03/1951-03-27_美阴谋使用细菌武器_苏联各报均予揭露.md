Title: U.S. Conspiracy to Use Bacteriological Weapons Exposed by All Soviet Newspapers
Author:
Date: 1951-03-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 25th: The "Truth," "News," "Labor News," "Red Star," "Komsomol Truth," and other newspapers all published on the 23rd the news regarding MacArthur Headquarters' attempt to use bacteriological weapons to poison the Korean people.

### 美阴谋使用细菌武器   苏联各报均予揭露

1951-03-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　美阴谋使用细菌武器
    苏联各报均予揭露
    【新华社莫斯科二十五日电】真理报、消息报、劳动报、红星报、共青真理报及其他各报，均于二十三日刊登了关于麦克阿瑟总部企图使用细菌武器毒害朝鲜人民的消息。
