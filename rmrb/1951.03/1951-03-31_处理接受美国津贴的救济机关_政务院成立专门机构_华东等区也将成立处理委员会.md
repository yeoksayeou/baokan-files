Title: Handling Relief Agencies Receiving US Subsidies: Government Administration Council Establishes Special Agency; East China and Other Regions Will Also Establish Handling Committees
Author:
Date: 1951-03-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 30th: In order to centrally lead the work of handling relief societies and relief agencies receiving US subsidies, relevant departments of the Central People's Government and relevant people's organizations, according to the "Circular of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government on the Establishment of the Committee for Handling Relief Agencies Receiving US Subsidies" of the 6th of this month, recently held a meeting and established the Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government for Handling Relief Agencies Receiving US Subsidies. The meeting also discussed and approved the guiding principles and implementation measures for handling relief societies and relief agencies receiving US subsidies. The committee is composed of the following twelve members: Xie Juezai, Wu Yunfu, and Pu Huairen from the China People's Relief Association; Chen Qiyuan and Chen Chuangang from the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government; Bao Jinghuan from the Ministry of Health; Fang Yuyan from the Ministry of Education; Ke Bonian from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Kang Keqing from the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Xiong Jinding and Hu Lansheng from the Red Cross Society of China; and Zhang Yuanshan, a personage from the relief and welfare sector. Xie Juezai is the chairman of the committee, and Wu Yunfu is the secretary-general. According to the aforementioned circular, large administrative regions such as East China, Central South, Northwest, and Southwest will also establish handling committees at the level of large administrative regions, and provinces and cities may establish such institutions as needed according to the circumstances. The handling committees at all levels will be dissolved after all handling work is completed.

### 处理接受美国津贴的救济机关  政务院成立专门机构  华东等区也将成立处理委员会

1951-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　处理接受美国津贴的救济机关
    政务院成立专门机构 
    华东等区也将成立处理委员会
    【新华社三十日讯】为了统一领导处理接受美国津贴的救济社团及救济机关的工作，中央人民政府有关部门和有关人民团体根据本月六日中央人民政府政务院《关于成立接受美国津贴救济机关处理委员会的通令》，于最近举行会议，成立了中央人民政府政务院处理接受美国津贴救济机关委员会。会议并讨论和通过了关于处理接受美国津贴的救济社团和救济机关的方针以及实施办法。委员会由下列十二位委员组成：中国人民救济总会谢觉哉、伍云甫、浦化人，中央人民政府内务部陈其瑗、陈传纲，卫生部鲍敬桓，教育部方与严，外交部柯柏年，全国民主妇女联合会康克清，中国红十字会熊瑾玎、胡兰生，救济福利界人士章元善。委员会由谢觉哉为主任，伍云甫为秘书长。根据上述通令，华东、中南、西北、西南等大行政区也将成立大行政区一级的处理委员会，各省、市得根据情况需要成立此项机构。各级处理委员会于全部处理工作完成后结束。
