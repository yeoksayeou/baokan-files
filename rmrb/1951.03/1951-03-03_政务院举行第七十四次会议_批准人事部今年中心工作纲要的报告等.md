Title: Government Administration Council Holds its 74th Meeting; Approves Report on the Personnel Department's Central Work Outline for this Year, etc.
Author:
Date: 1951-03-03
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, March 2nd -- The 74th Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held at 3:30 PM on the afternoon of the 2nd. At the meeting, An Ziwen, Minister of the Central People's Government Personnel Department, delivered a report on the department's central work outline for 1951. Sun Qimeng, the former Director of the Personnel Bureau of the Government Administration Council, gave a summary report on the bureau's work over the past year. Zhang Naiqi, a member of the Government Administration Council, presented a summary report on the review work of the Government Administration Council and its affiliated units' organizational structure review committee for the second half of 1950. After discussion, the meeting approved the above reports. The meeting also passed several proposals for appointments and removals, and proposals for appointments and removals to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第七十四次会议  批准人事部今年中心工作纲要的报告等

1951-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行第七十四次会议
    批准人事部今年中心工作纲要的报告等
    【新华社二日讯】中央人民政府政务院第七十四次政务会议于二日下午三时半举行。会上，中央人民政府人事部部长安子文作了该部一九五一年中心工作纲要的报告，原任政务院人事局局长孙起孟作了该局一年工作总结报告，政务委员章乃器作了政务院及所属单位机构编制审查委员会一九五○年下半年审查工作总结报告。会议经讨论后，批准了以上报告。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
