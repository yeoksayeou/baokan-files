Title: Beijing Female Medical Workers Gather to Commemorate International Women's Day

Author: 
Date: 1951-03-07
Page: 3

Beijing: Xinhua News Agency.

　　Beijing Female Medical Workers Gather to Commemorate International Women's Day
    Beijing female medical workers held a grand gathering at the auditorium of Fu Jen Catholic University at 2:00 PM on the 3rd of this month to commemorate the "March 8th" International Working Women's Day. Minister of the Central Ministry of Health, Li Dequan, Vice Minister He Cheng, and more than 1,300 female medical workers from various units attended.
    The meeting began with a report by Shen Yuanhui, Vice President of the Medical Assistant School, on the significance of the meeting. This was followed by a speech by Minister Li. She recalled the suffering of women under the double oppression of feudalism and capitalism, and the contributions of women in the liberation movement. She also encouraged everyone to give full play to their initiative under the central task of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, and to learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union to do a good job in their respective posts. Vice Minister He also encouraged everyone to strive to realize the task of protecting women and children as stated in the Common Program, and to resolutely oppose U.S. imperialism's support for the German and Japanese fascist militarists who had cruelly persecuted women. Subsequently, Zeng Zhaoyi of the Municipal Third Hospital, An Xiaolan of Peking University Medical College, and Lu Baoqi of Peking Union Medical College spoke. They unanimously expressed their willingness to serve the people's health under the leadership of the People's Government, and to respond to the government's call at any time, and to strive for the task of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea to defend our homes and country!
                                                        (Xinhua News Agency)

### 北京市女医务工作者集会纪念“三八”节

1951-03-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　北京市女医务工作者集会纪念“三八”节
    北京市女医务工作者于本月三日下午二时在辅仁大学礼堂举行了纪念“三八”国际劳动妇女节大会。到中央卫生部部长李德全、副部长贺诚及各单位女医务工作者一千三百余人。
    大会首由主席医士学校副校长沈元晖报告开会意义，继由李部长讲话。她追述了在封建主义和资本主义统治下妇女遭受双重压迫的痛苦，及妇女在解放运动中的贡献，并鼓舞大家在今日抗美援朝的中心任务下发挥积极性，学习苏联先进经验作好自己岗位上的工作。贺副部长也勉励大家努力实现共同纲领中关于保护妇女儿童的任务，坚决反对美帝国主义扶持曾残暴地迫害妇女的德、日法西斯军国主义者。继由市立第三医院曾昭懿、北京大学医学院安笑兰、协和医学院陆宝琪等讲话。她们一致表示今后愿在人民政府的领导下，努力为保卫人民的健康而服务，更要随时响应政府的号召，为抗美援朝保家卫国的任务而努力！
                                                        （新华社）
