Title: Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Discusses New Budget; Many Deputies Speak, Unanimously Supporting its Passage
Author:
Date: 1951-03-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th Dispatch: According to TASS Moscow dispatches: The Soviet Supreme Soviet’s Nationalities Council and the Union Council separately discussed the report on the draft of the 1951 Soviet National Budget by Soviet Minister of Finance Zverev on the 8th.

The Nationalities Council meeting was presided over by the Chairman of the Nationalities Council, Seleznev, and attended by Bulganin, Mikoyan, Kosygin, Shvernik, Shkiryatov, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, ministers of various ministries of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and many guests.

The Chairman of the Budget Committee of the Nationalities Council, Kokharov, emphasized in his report: about two-thirds of the total national budget of the Soviet Union is allocated for economic and cultural construction, and military expenditures only account for 21.3 percent of the budget expenditure, while the military expenditures of some capitalist countries account for more than 70 percent of the national budget. Kokharov then stated: the Budget Committee believes that the preparation of the 1951 budget draft is correct. The committee only proposes some amendments, suggesting an increase in budget revenue of 724 million rubles, and recommends that the 1951 national budget proposed by the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union with the amendments of the Budget Committee be adopted.

Those who spoke in the discussion unanimously approved the new budget proposal. The Chairman of the Central Council of Trade Unions of the Soviet Union, Kuznetsov, presented many specific figures in his speech to prove that the living standards and welfare of the Soviet working people are constantly improving.

The Union Council met on the evening of the 8th, and the meeting was presided over by the Chairman of the Union Council, Yasnov.

The Chairman of the Budget Committee of the Union Council, Kornyets, reported that the Budget Committee recommended approving the draft national budget adopted by the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and making certain amendments. During the discussion, Rumiantsev, a representative of the working people of Moscow, spoke enthusiastically in support of the new budget, stating that this year the residential building area in Moscow will reach 710,000 square meters, and a new subway line of 6.5 kilometers will be built. The total investment in the economic development and construction of Moscow this year has increased by 37 percent compared to last year.

Many deputies who spoke at the meeting unanimously agreed to adopt the draft national budget. The Union Council is scheduled to hold its next meeting on the morning of the 9th.

### 苏联最高苏维埃两院讨论新预算  许多代表发言一致表示赞成通过

1951-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　 苏联最高苏维埃两院讨论新预算
    许多代表发言一致表示赞成通过
    【新华社十日讯】综合塔斯社莫斯科消息：苏联最高苏维埃民族院与联盟院于八日分别讨论苏联财政部长兹维列夫关于一九五一年苏联国家预算草案的报告。
    民族院会议由民族院主席谢列赫密托夫主持，出席者有布尔加宁、米高扬、柯西金、什维尔尼克、施基利亚托夫、苏联最高苏维埃主席团、苏联部长会议各部部长，以及来宾多人。
    民族院预算委员会主席科哈洛夫在报告中着重指出：苏联全部国家预算约有三分之二拨充经济和文化建设之用，军事费用只占预算支出的百分之二十一点三，而若干资本主义国家的军事费用却占国家预算的百分之七十以上。科哈洛夫继称：预算委员会认为一九五一年预算草案的编制是正确的，委员会只提出一些修正，建议增加预算收入七亿二千四百万卢布，并建议将苏联部长会议提出的附有预算委员会修正案的一九五一年国家预算予以通过。
    在讨论中发言的一致赞成新预算案。全苏工会中央理事会主席库兹涅佐夫在发言中提出了许多具体数字，证明苏联劳动人民的生活福利正在不断地改进。
    联盟院于八日晚间开会，会议由联盟院主席雅斯诺夫主持。
    联盟院预算委员会主席柯尔尼叶兹报告称：预算委员会建议批准苏联部长会议所通过的国家预算草案，并作某些修正。在讨论中，莫斯科劳动人民代表鲁米扬切夫发言热烈拥护新预算称：今年莫斯科的住宅建筑面积将达七十一万平方公尺，并将建造长达六公里半的新地下铁道。本年发展莫斯科市经济及建设的投资总数，比去年增加了百分之三十七。
    在会上发言的许多代表一致赞成通过国家预算草案。联盟院定九日上午举行下一次会议。
