Title: Bulgarian Council of Ministers and Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Announce: Abolition of Industrial Goods Rationing, Price Reductions and Wage Increases
Author:
Date: 1951-03-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia: TASS reports that the Bulgarian Council of Ministers and the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party announced on the 20th a resolution on abolishing the rationing of manufactured industrial goods and on increasing workers' wages. After the abolition of rationing, various manufactured goods will be sold at uniform retail prices that are 10 to 45 percent lower than current market prices. The prices of certain food items, such as sunflower seed oil and lard, are also being prepared for reduction. The resolution stipulates an increase in workers' wages and plans to increase subsidies for workers, anti-fascist fighters, disabled veterans, and students.

### 保加利亚部长会议和保共中央宣布  取消工业品配给制   减低物价并增加工资

1951-03-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　保加利亚部长会议和保共中央宣布
    取消工业品配给制           
    减低物价并增加工资
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社索非亚讯：保加利亚部长会议和保加利亚共产党中央委员会于二十日公布关于取消工业制造品配给制及关于增加职工工资的决议。各种制成品在取消配给制后，均将按照低于现在的市场价格百分之十到四十五的统一零售价格出售。若干食品如向日葵子油和猪油亦在准备减低价格。决议规定增加职工工资，并准备增加职工、反法西斯战士、残废军人和学生的补助金。
