Title: Volunteer Army Representatives Ji Bingqian and Others Go to Zhengzhou; Received Infinite Respect from People of All Circles in Kaifeng; Families of Volunteer Soldiers Instructed to Resolutely Strike American Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1951-03-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaifeng, 18th. Volunteer Army representatives Ji Bingqian, Li Weiying, Zhang Fu and others, who arrived in Kaifeng on the 13th of this month, have left Kaifeng for Zhengzhou on the afternoon of the 17th, after reporting to the people of all circles in Kaifeng about the heroic deeds of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army in fighting the American aggressors. They will then proceed to Luoyang, Xi'an, Chengdu and other places to continue reporting. During their stay in Kaifeng, the Volunteer Army representatives gave a total of eleven reports to the people of all circles in the city, with an audience of more than 60,000 people. The people of all circles in Kaifeng expressed infinite respect for the arduous struggles of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army in Korea. People from all walks of life in the city presented the Volunteer Army representatives with a large number of gifts such as silk banners and commemorative medals. Farmers in the suburbs also brought local products to them. The city's business community also launched a donation campaign to aid the Chinese Volunteer Army with medicine.

Representatives of the families of volunteer soldiers in Kaifeng also reported to the Volunteer Army representatives about how the People's Government and the local people had helped them solve their living difficulties, hoping that the commanders and fighters of the Volunteer Army would not worry about their families and resolutely strike the American aggressors.

### 志愿军代表嵇炳前等赴郑州  在开封时受到各界人民的无限崇敬  志愿军家属嘱咐坚决打击美国强盗

1951-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　志愿军代表嵇炳前等赴郑州
    在开封时受到各界人民的无限崇敬
    志愿军家属嘱咐坚决打击美国强盗
    【新华社开封十八日电】于本月十三日抵达开封的我国人民志愿军归国代表嵇炳前、李维英、张甫等三人，在向开封各界人民报告了我国人民志愿军打击美国侵略军的英勇事迹后，已于十七日下午离开封赴郑州，并将转赴洛阳、西安、成都等地继续报告。志愿军归国代表在开封逗留期间，共向该市各界人民作了十一次报告，听众达六万余人。开封市各界人民对我国人民志愿军在朝鲜的艰苦奋战，表示无限的崇敬。该市各界人民向志愿军代表献赠锦旗、纪念章等大批礼品。郊区农民也纷纷把土产品送给他们。市工商界也发起了援助中国志愿军的捐献药物运动。
    开封市的志愿军军人家属代表，并向志愿军代表报告了人民政府和当地人民群众帮助他们解决生活困难的情形，希望志愿军的指战员们不要挂念家庭，坚决打击美国侵略者。
