Title: Qinghai Province's Xia Rong Ga Bu White Buddha Comes to the Capital to Pay Respects to Central Leaders
Author:
Date: 1951-03-20
Page: 1
　　
Xinhua News Agency, March 19th: Xia Rong Ga Bu, the White Buddha and Dharma Head of Tar Temple in Qinghai Province, who came to the capital at the end of last month to pay respects to the central leaders, presented a flag, gifts, and a message to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and Premier Zhou this afternoon. The message from Xia Rong Ga Bu White Buddha stated in brief: We resolutely support the ethnic policies in the Common Program and support the wise leadership of Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government. We Tibetan people are willing to unite with the people of all ethnic groups in the country to oppose the aggressive actions of American imperialism, continue to carry out the movement to resist America and aid Korea, and fight to the end for the liberation of Tibet and Taiwan. The gifts presented by Xia Rong Ga Bu White Buddha included hadas (regarded as extremely precious gifts by Tibetans) and precious and famous Tibetan products such as saffron, musk, lynx fur, and brocade.

### 青海省塔尔寺夏茸尕布白佛来首都向中央首长致敬

1951-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　青海省塔尔寺夏茸尕布白佛
    来首都向中央首长致敬
    【新华社十九日讯】曾于上月底由青海省前来首都向中央首长致敬的塔尔寺法台白佛夏茸尕布，今日下午向毛主席、朱总司令和周总理献旗、献礼、献词。夏茸尕布白佛的献词略称：我们坚决拥护共同纲领中的民族政策，拥护毛主席和中央人民政府的英明领导。我们藏族人民愿和全国各民族人民团结一致，反对美帝国主义的侵略行为，继续开展抗美援朝运动，并为解放西藏和台湾而奋斗到底。夏茸尕布白佛所献礼物中有哈达（藏人视为极尊贵的礼物）和藏区珍贵名产藏红花、麝香、猞猁皮、织锦缎等。
