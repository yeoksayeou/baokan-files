Title: Our Chinese People's Volunteer Army Air Defense Units Shoot Down and Damage 12 Enemy Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1951-03-08
Page: 1

　　Our Chinese People's Volunteer Army Air Defense Units
    Shoot Down and Damage 12 Enemy Aircraft
    [Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 6th] A certain anti-aircraft artillery unit of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army successively shot down one enemy Mosquito light bomber and one B-25 bomber over the Korean battlefield on February 25th and 26th, and also damaged one Mosquito light bomber. Furthermore, on the 19th, a certain anti-aircraft artillery unit shot down an enemy B-25 bomber on the east coast.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 7th] From the 1st to the 3rd of this month, the Chinese People's Volunteer Army air defense units shot down and damaged a total of eight American and British aircraft on the Korean front and in the rear. Specifically, on March 1st, one British Navy fighter plane was shot down; on the 2nd, one American F-80A and one F-84E plane were shot down, and a total of three F-80A and F-84E planes were damaged; on the 3rd, one American fighter plane was shot down and one F-84E plane was damaged.

### 我人民志愿军防空部队  击落击伤敌机十二架

1951-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　我人民志愿军防空部队
    击落击伤敌机十二架
    【新华社朝鲜前线六日电】中国人民志愿军某高射炮部队，于二月二十五、二十六两日在朝鲜战地上空先后击落敌蚊式轻轰炸机及Ｂ二十五型轰炸机各一架、另击伤蚊式轻轰炸机一架。又十九日某高射炮部队在东海岸击落敌Ｂ二十五型轰炸机一架。
    【新华社朝鲜前线七日电】中国人民志愿军防空部队从本月一日至三日，在朝鲜前线和后方击落和击伤美、英飞机共八架。计三月一日击落英海军战斗机一架，二日击落美Ｆ八○Ａ型、Ｆ八四Ｅ型机各一架，击伤Ｆ八○Ａ型、Ｆ八四Ｅ型机共三架，三日又击落美战斗机一架，击伤Ｆ八四Ｅ型机一架。
