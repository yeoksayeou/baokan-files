Title: U.S. Labor Unions and People's Joint Committee to Celebrate May Day Call for May Day Demonstrations Against Aggression and Demand Peace
Author:
Date: 1951-03-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, March 10th: The Worker's Daily reported: The Labor Union and People's Joint Committee to Celebrate May Day (including labor union workers, intellectuals, and leaders of various local organizations) issued a statement announcing that a meeting will be held in New York on March 24th to discuss organizing the annual May Day International Labor Day demonstrations. The statement was sent to the CIO, AFL, independent labor unions, and other organizations. The statement pointed out: "Peace or War?" This question is the decisive issue for us at this time. The statement called on New York workers to turn May Day into a demonstration demanding peace, demanding an end to the Korean War, demanding a ban on atomic bombs, demanding friendship with the people of the People's Republic of China and the People's Democratic countries, and demanding genuine peace negotiations with the Soviet Union.
    The committee emphasized the continuous decline in the living standards of American workers and that their economic interests are also under attack. The statement urged workers to demonstrate on May Day the solidarity of the working masses of New York on the following major issues: demand peace—oppose war, defend democratic rights—oppose a German-style secret police state, demand social progress—oppose poverty and hunger.

### 美工会与人民庆祝五一联合委员会  号召五一节大示威  反对侵略要求和平

1951-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　美工会与人民庆祝五一联合委员会
    号召五一节大示威  反对侵略要求和平
    【新华社十二日讯】据塔斯社纽约十日讯：工人日报报道：工会与人民庆祝五一联合委员会（包括工会工作者、知识分子及各种地方组织的领袖）发表文告，宣布定于三月二十四日在纽约召开会议，商讨组织一年一度的五一国际劳动节的示威游行事宜。这一文告并分致产联、劳联、独立工会以及其他组织。文告中指出：“和平呢还是战争呢？”这一问题是我们这时决定性的问题。文告号召纽约工人把五一节变成一个示威运动，要求和平，要求停止朝鲜战争，要求禁止原子弹，要求对中华人民共和国及人民民主国家的人民友好，要求与苏联进行真正的和平谈判。
    该委员会着重指出美国工人生活水平的不断降低，而且，他们的经济利益也遭到进攻。文告力促工人们在五一节表现出纽约劳动群众在下面这些主要问题上团结一致：要求和平——反对战争，保卫民主权利——反对德国式的秘密警察国家，要求社会进步——反对穷困与饥饿。
