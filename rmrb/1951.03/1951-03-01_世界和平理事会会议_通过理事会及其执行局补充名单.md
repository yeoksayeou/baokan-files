Title: World Peace Council Meeting Passes Supplementary List of Council and Executive Bureau Members
Author:
Date: 1951-03-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 27th—According to TASS, at its closing meeting on the 26th, the World Peace Council unanimously passed a supplementary list of members for the World Peace Council and its Executive Bureau.

Council member Deschamps (France) declared at the meeting that the Second World Peace Congress had instructed the World Peace Council to expand its composition. To this end, the Council recommends adding new Council members. Deschamps then announced the list of new Council members as follows:

United Kingdom: Conan, Crawford, Brown, Jones. Argentina: Mrs. Scolameri. Belgium: Brum, Cavennier. Germany: Flander, Brecht, Lino, Weirmann, Kruger. Denmark: Mrs. Aepel. Iceland: Dr. Jeffers. Iran: Homos, Boge. Italy: Varoli, Beringer. China: Mao Dun, Mei Ru'ao, Chen Hansheng. Luxembourg: Berman. Syria: Ding Iz-Chaim. United States: Kennard. Thailand: Sai Khuraft, Pradit, Chotinoochit, Palawas. France: Bernis, Dennis. Czechoslovakia: Hromadka. Japan: Hirano Yoshitaro, Takakuwa Sumio (transliteration), Yoshida Suketsugu, Kumakura Keian (transliteration), Awade Saburo, Tetsuten Masuda (transliteration), Mrs. Kushida.

All the representatives present passed this list amidst thunderous applause.

Deschamps then put forward the list of new members to be added to the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council as follows: Mao Dun (China), Friedrich (Germany), Brum (Belgium), Stuver (USA), Kawamura Kazuo, Hirano (Japan), Tikhonov (USSR), Hromatka (Czechoslovakia), Tabot (Lebanon).

All the representatives present unanimously passed this list.

The World Peace Council also unanimously decided to recommend that the following countries appoint secretaries to the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council: Britain, Belgium, France, Italy, Sweden, Argentina, and countries in the Middle East.

### 世界和平理事会会议  通过理事会及其执行局补充名单

1951-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界和平理事会会议
    通过理事会及其执行局补充名单
    【新华社二十七日讯】据塔斯社柏林讯：世界和平理事会在二十六日闭幕会议上一致通过了世界和平理事会及理事会执行局成员的补充名单。
    世界和平理事会理事德香布兰（法）在会上宣称：第二届世界和平大会曾指示世界和平理事会扩大其组成成分。为此，理事会建议增加新的理事。德香布兰随即宣布了新理事名单如下：
    英国：柯南、克劳福德、布朗、琼斯。阿根廷：斯考拉梅里夫人。比利时：布伦姆、卡文尼尔。德国：佛兰德烈、布莱契特、李诺、卫尔曼、克吕格尔。丹麦：艾派尔夫人。冰岛：杰佛斯博士。伊朗：霍摩斯、鲍盖。意大利：瓦罗利、贝林格。中国：茅盾、梅汝璈、陈翰笙。卢森堡：贝尔曼。叙利亚：丁·伊兹·柴姆。美国：肯纳德。泰国：赛库拉甫、普拉迪特、卓蒂努契特、柏拉瓦斯。法国：柏尔尼斯、丹尼士。捷克斯洛伐克：赫鲁玛德卡。日本：平野义太郎、高桑纯夫（译音）、吉田资治、熊仓敬安（译音）、淡德三郎、哲天增田（译音）、栉田夫人。
    与会全体代表在雷动的掌声中通过了这一名单。
    德香布兰继提出世界和平理事会执行局增加新的委员的名单如下：茅盾（中），腓特烈（德），布伦姆（比），史图佛（美），河村一夫、平野（日本），吉洪诺夫（苏），赫鲁玛特卡（捷克），塔伯特（黎巴嫩）。
    与会全体代表一致通过了这一名单：
    世界和平理事会并一致决定：建议由下列各国任命世界和平理事会执行局秘书：英国、比利时、法国、意大利、瑞典、阿根廷与中东各国。
