Title: Foreign Minister Zhou Enters Finland's Envoy Vahervahni – Finnish Envoy Previously Met with Heads of Our Government
Author:
Date: 1951-03-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 27th – Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, hosted a banquet for Vahervahni, the Finnish envoy to China, at 7:00 PM on February 27th. Those invited to accompany him were Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office, Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Central People's Government, Huan Xiang, Director of the Department of European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Yue, Deputy Director, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Liaison Office.
    After presenting his credentials, Finnish Envoy to China Vahervahni paid courtesy calls on Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government, Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman, Zhang Lan, Vice Chairman, Huang Yanpei, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council, Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Trade, and Wu Han, Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.

### 周外长宴芬公使瓦尔万尼  芬公使曾分别谒见我政府首长

1951-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　周外长宴芬公使瓦尔万尼
    芬公使曾分别谒见我政府首长
    【新华社二十七日讯】中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长于二月二十七日下午七时宴请芬兰驻我国公使瓦尔万尼。应邀作陪者有外交部章汉夫副部长、办公厅王炳南主任、中央人民政府典礼局余心清局长、外交部欧非司宦乡司长、张越副司长、交际处王倬如处长等。
    芬兰驻我国公使瓦尔万尼于呈递国书后，曾分别拜谒我中央人民政府朱德副主席、刘少奇副主席、张澜副主席、政务院黄炎培副总理、外交部章汉夫副部长、文化部沈雁冰部长、贸易部叶季壮部长及北京市人民政府吴晗副市长等。
