Cultural Life Updates

    Anqing City's First Group of Volunteer Medical Team Departs for Korea

    The first group of twelve members of the volunteer medical team organized by the Anqing Branch of the Red Cross Society of Northern Anhui Province in response to the call of the National Red Cross Society, led by team leader and deputy leader Zhao Yongbin and Xie Zhixuan, departed from Anqing City on the 21st of last month for the Korean front. Before their departure, various Anqing City agencies, organizations, and business people held a grand farewell meeting.

    Tianjin People's Court Holds Marriage Case Judgment Exhibition

    To commemorate "March 8th" International Women's Day, the Tianjin People's Court decided to hear marriage cases this week and allow public attendance. On the 6th, it will adjudicate the Liu Baozhong abandonment of Li Jie case, and on the 7th, it will adjudicate the Wang Jiansan abuse of Zhang Shuzhen case, both of which will invite the Women's Federation and various agencies and organizations to attend. In addition, a "Marriage Case Judgment Exhibition" will be held at the Women's Palace (inside the Workers' Cultural Palace) on the 6th, 7th, and 8th. The court will also send cadres to the venue to answer questions, and in conjunction with the judgments, large-character posters and cartoons will also be displayed.

    Central Broadcasting Station Commemorates "March 8th" Programs

    From March 5th to March 10th, the Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast special programs commemorating "March 8th" International Women's Day from 18:00 to 18:30 daily. The dates and content are as follows: March 5th, Korean folk dancer An Shengji speaks on: "How Korean sisters resist American imperialist aggression"; March 6th, Xu Huifen, a worker at the Beijing Clothing Factory, speaks on: "How we respond to the challenge of the Ma Hengchang Group"; March 7th, Zheng Yun, a teacher at the Second Affiliated Elementary School of Beijing Normal University, speaks on: "Educating our second generation well"; March 8th, Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, addresses sisters across the country in commemoration of "March 8th" International Women's Day; March 9th, Liu Yuzhen and Jing Yuzhen, conductors of the Beijing Tram Company, speak on: "Oppose American imperialist rearmament of Japan"; March 10th, Chen Zhizhang, a student at Peking University, and Wang Shujin, a housewife, speak on: "Oppose American imperialist rearmament of Japan".

    Introduction to the 10th Issue of "Current Affairs Handbook"

    The 10th issue of "Current Affairs Handbook" was published on the 5th. This issue contains Stalin's talk on the current international situation, the World Peace Council's declaration and nine important resolutions, the Regulations of the People's Republic of China on Punishment of Counterrevolutionaries, and other documents. The articles published include Lin Chao's "Defend World Peace with Action", Huang Caoliang's "The Korean War in the Past Four Months", Zheng Hui's "Exhibitions are a Good Way to Conduct Propaganda to the Masses". The material includes "The Glorious Achievements of the Chinese and Korean People's Forces in the Past Four Months". The "Knowing Our Great Motherland" column publishes Chen Sang's "The People's Democratic System Gives Our Country Great New Strength". The "Introduction to New China's Construction" column publishes Mu Qing's "The Restoration and Development of Agricultural Production in China".

### 文化生活动态

1951-03-07
第3版()
专栏：文化生活动态

　　文化生活动态
            安庆市首批志愿赴朝医疗队出发
    红十字会皖北安庆分会响应全国红十字会的号召所组织的志愿赴朝医疗队首批十二人在正、副队长赵永彬、谢之玄的率领下，已于上月二十一日自安庆市出发赴朝鲜前线。出发前，安庆市各机关、团体、工商界人士曾举行盛大的欢送会。
            天津人民法院举行婚姻案件判决展览
    天津市人民法院为纪念“三八”国际劳动妇女节，决定在本周内审理婚姻案件准许旁听。六日将判决刘宝忠遗弃李洁案，七日判决王缄三虐待张淑贞案，均将邀请妇联及各机关团体旁听。此外并将在六、七、八三日在妇女宫（工人文化宫内）举行“婚姻案件判决展览会”，该院并派有干部在会场负责解答问题，同时配合判决，还将陈列大字报和漫画等。
            中央广播电台纪念“三八”节目
    中央人民广播电台从三月五日起至三月十日止于每日十八时至十八时三十分播送纪念“三八”国际劳动妇女节特别节目，日期和内容如下：三月五日，朝鲜人民舞蹈家安圣姬讲：“朝鲜姐妹们怎样反抗美帝侵略”；三月六日，北京被服厂工人徐蕙芬讲：“我们怎样响应马恒昌小组的挑战”；三月七日，北京师范大学第二附属小学教员郑芸讲：“教好我们的第二代”；三月八日，中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅为纪念“三八”国际劳动妇女节向全国姊妹们讲话；三月九日，北京市电车公司售票员刘玉珍和景玉珍讲：“反对美帝重新武装日本”；三月十日，北京大学学生陈织章和家庭妇女王淑瑾讲：“反对美帝重新武装日本”。
            《时事手册》第十期介绍
    《时事手册》第十期已于五日出版。这一期刊载了斯大林关于目前国际形势的谈话，世界和平理事会的宣言和九项重要决议，中华人民共和国惩治反革命条例等文件。登载的文章有林朝的《以行动保卫世界和平》、黄操良的《四个月来的朝鲜战争》、郑惠的《展览会是向群众进行宣传的好方法》。资料有《四个月来中朝人民部队的光辉战绩》。《认识我们伟大的祖国》一栏登载了陈桑的《人民民主制度使我国获得伟大的新生力量》。《新中国建设介绍》一栏登载了穆青的《中国农业生产的恢复和发展》。
