Title: American Troops Invading Korea Use Poison Gas Bombs; Peking University and Normal University Students Strongly Protest
Author:
Date: 1951-03-07
Page: 1

　　American Troops Invading Korea Use Poison Gas Bombs
　　Peking University and Normal University Students Strongly Protest
　　[This newspaper dispatch] The students of Peking University and Peking Normal University have expressed their intolerable anger at the crime of American imperialism using asphyxiating gas bombs on the Korean battlefield. As of 6 p.m. on the 5th, more than 1,000 students from Peking University had signed the protest letter initiated by Class A of the first-year students of the Pre-Medical Department. Departments of Philosophy, Chinese, Politics, Economics, and History of the university have posted big-character posters, lodging serious protests against American imperialism's violation of international law and use of poison gas bombs. Many big-character posters were also posted on the democratic wall of Peking Normal University on the 5th: Serious Protest Against American Imperialism's Use of Poison Gas Bombs. The university also initiated a protest signature campaign, and by dinner time, more than 800 people had signed.

### 侵朝美军使用毒气炸弹  北大师大同学严重抗议

1951-03-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　侵朝美军使用毒气炸弹
    北大师大同学严重抗议
    【本报讯】北京大学、北京师范大学两校同学对美帝国主义在朝鲜战场上使用窒息性毒气弹的罪行，表示了不能容忍的愤怒。至五日下午六时止，北京大学已有一千余名同学在医预系一年级甲班同学发起的抗议书上签下了名字。该校哲学、中文、政治、经济、史学等系，纷纷张贴大字报，对美帝国主义破坏国际公法、使用毒气炸弹提出严重抗议。北京师范大学的民主墙上，五日亦贴了很多大字报：严重抗议美帝国主义使用毒气弹。该校亦发起抗议签名，至晚饭时，签名者已有八百多人。
