Title: Nanjing Representatives Gather, Warmly Welcome Volunteer Army Representatives Returning Home, Guarantee Support for the Volunteer Army to Eliminate the American Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1951-03-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, March 30th: More than 3,000 representatives from all walks of life in Nanjing gathered on the afternoon of the 27th to warmly welcome Chai Chuanruo, Dong Lefu, and Dou Shaoyi, representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army returning from Shanghai to Nanjing. Representatives from various mass organizations, democratic parties, and troops stationed in Nanjing spoke at the meeting, unanimously expressing their determination to vigorously support the Volunteer Army's heroic operations on the Korean front. Jiang Weiqing, Vice Chairman of the Nanjing Municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee, on behalf of the city's million people, guaranteed: We will deepen the Resist America, Aid Korea movement, and use practical actions such as strengthening production and consolidating the people's democratic dictatorship to be the Volunteer Army's strong backing and fully support the Chinese and Korean soldiers in striking against the American aggressors! Zhang Ming, a first-class combat hero of the East China Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, swore on behalf of all officers and soldiers stationed in East China: We are determined to take the heroic actions of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army as an example, learn from their combat experience, and be ready at any time to defend the border and annihilate any aggressors who dare to invade our sacred territory! Finally, Volunteer Army representatives Chai Chuanruo and Dong Lefu reported on the various atrocities of the American invading army in Korea and the many heroic deeds of the Volunteer Army fighting on the front lines. Chai Chuanruo and others guaranteed that they would definitely bring the motherland's care and love for the Volunteer Army to the officers and soldiers on the front lines, and respond to the care of their compatriots with greater victories and the annihilation of more enemies.

### 南京市各界代表集会  热烈欢迎志愿军归国代表  保证支援志愿军消灭美侵略者

1951-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　南京市各界代表集会
    热烈欢迎志愿军归国代表     
    保证支援志愿军消灭美侵略者
    【新华社南京三十日电】南京市各界代表三千余人，二十七日下午集会热烈欢迎由上海来南京的中国人民志愿军归国代表柴川若、董乐辅、窦少毅等三人。会上各群众团体、各民主党派及驻南京部队代表都讲了话，一致表示要努力支援志愿军在朝鲜前线的英勇作战。南京市人民代表会议协商委员会副主席江渭清代表全市百万人民保证：决深入抗美援朝运动，用加强生产、巩固人民民主专政等实际行动，作志愿军的后盾，全力支援中朝战士，打击美国侵略者！人民解放军华东军区一级战斗英雄张明，代表驻华东部队全体指战员宣誓：决以人民志愿军的英勇行为作榜样，学习他们的战斗经验，随时准备捍卫边疆，歼灭任何敢于进犯我神圣国土的侵略者！最后由志愿军代表柴川若、董乐辅报告了美国侵略军在朝鲜的种种暴行以及志愿军在前线奋战的许多英雄事迹。柴川若等保证一定把祖国人民对于志愿军的关怀与热爱带给前线指战员，并以更大的胜利和歼灭更多的敌人来回答祖国同胞的关怀。
