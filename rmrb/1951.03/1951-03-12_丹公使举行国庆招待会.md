Title: Danish Minister Holds National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1951-03-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 11th. Danish Minister to China, Mr. Munk, held a reception at the International Club at 5:30 this afternoon to celebrate the birthday of King Frederick IX of Denmark (Note: i.e., Danish National Day). Among those invited to attend were Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications, Lü Zhengcao, Deputy Minister of Railways, Ding Xielin, Deputy Minister of Culture, Zhang Youyu and Wu Han, Deputy Mayors of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, etc. Diplomatic envoys invited to attend included Czechoslovak Ambassador Weiskopf, Indian Ambassador Panikkar, Swedish Ambassador Amashton, Hungarian Ambassador Saffronko, Burmese Ambassador Wu Min Deng, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Swiss Minister Ren Zoli, Finnish Minister Warvani, Indonesian Embassy Chargé d'affaires Isha Marty, Polish Embassy Chargé d'affaires Dai Peirasinski, Romanian Embassy Counselor Sirad, Soviet Embassy First Secretary Jia Picai, and British, Dutch, and Norwegian government negotiation representatives.

### 丹公使举行国庆招待会

1951-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　丹公使举行国庆招待会
    【新华社十一日讯】丹麦驻我国公使穆克为庆祝丹麦国王斐特烈九世寿辰（按：即丹麦国庆日），特于今日下午五时半假国际俱乐部举行招待会。应邀出席者有中央人民政府政务院郭沫若副总理及中央人民政府外交部章汉夫副部长，邮电部朱学范部长，铁道部吕正操副部长，文化部丁燮林副部长，北京市人民政府张友渔、吴晗副市长等。外交使节应邀出席者有捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫，印度大使潘尼迦，瑞典大使阿马斯顿，匈牙利大使夏法朗柯，缅甸大使吴敏登，保加利亚大使彼得科夫，瑞士公使任佐立，芬兰公使瓦尔万尼，印度尼西亚大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂，波兰大使馆临时代办戴裴拉辛斯基，罗马尼亚大使馆参赞席拉德，苏联大使馆一等秘书贾丕才及英国、荷兰、挪威政府谈判代表等。
