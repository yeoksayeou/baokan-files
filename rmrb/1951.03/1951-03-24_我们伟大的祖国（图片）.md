    Our Great Motherland
    Rent reduction and deposit refunds are the first step in the great peasant land reform struggle. Here are Han Yuanchang (right) and Liu Zengru (left), peasants from Pixian County, Sichuan. See how happy they are to have overthrown the landlord and recovered their deposit!
