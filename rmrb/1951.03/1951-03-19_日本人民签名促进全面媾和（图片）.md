Title: Japanese People Sign Petitions to Promote Overall Peace

Author:
Date: 1951-03-19
Page: 4

The "National Representatives' Discussion Conference for Overall Peace" was held at the House of Councillors in Japan on January 15th and 16th, deciding to strive for broad public participation in the signature campaign. Workers across the country immediately responded, launching signature campaigns during their lunch breaks. The picture shows workers at the Hosaka Iron Works in Tokyo signing petitions.

### 日本人民签名促进全面媾和（图片）

1951-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    日本人民签名促进全面媾和
    一月十五、十六两日，在日本参议院会馆召开“全面媾和全国代表者恳谈会”，决定争取广大人民参加签名运动。全国工人立刻响应，利用中午时间展开签名运动。图为东京保坂铁工厂工人签名情形。
