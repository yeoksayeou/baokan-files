Title: Philippine Reactionary Government Arrests Union Leaders; All-China Federation of Trade Unions Protests
Author:
Date: 1951-03-16
Page: 4

　　Philippine Reactionary Government Arrests Union Leaders
    All-China Federation of Trade Unions Protests
    [Xinhua News Agency, 14th] The All-China Federation of Trade Unions has sent a telegram to Philippine President Quirino to protest against the arrest of Hernandez, Chairman of the Philippine Labor Organization Congress, and more than 30 other union leaders and newspaper reporters in Manila on January 26th and 27th by the Philippine reactionary government of Quirino. The telegram stated: "The Chinese working class is very indignant about this matter, and hereby lodge a serious protest with you, and hope to immediately release the arrested persons."

### 菲反动政府逮捕工会领袖  我全国总工会提出抗议

1951-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　菲反动政府逮捕工会领袖
    我全国总工会提出抗议
    【新华社十四日讯】中华全国总工会，为菲律宾季里诺反动政府于一月二十六日及二十七日在马尼拉市逮捕菲律宾劳工组织大会主席赫南德兹暨其他工会领袖与各报记者三十多人一事，顷致电菲律宾总统季里诺，提出抗议。电文称：“中国工人阶级对此事甚为愤慨，特向你提出严重抗议，并希立即释放被捕者。”
