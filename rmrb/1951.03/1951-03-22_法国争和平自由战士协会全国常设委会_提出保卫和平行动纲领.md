Title: National Standing Committee of the French Association of Fighters for Peace and Freedom Presents Action Program to Defend Peace
Author:
Date: 1951-03-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, March 19th: TASS reports: The National Standing Committee of the French Association of Fighters for Peace and Freedom held a plenary meeting on March 18th in Aubervilliers, near Paris. Representatives from 79 provinces of France participated in the meeting.

The Chairman of the Committee, Fajon, delivered a report on the French struggle for peace and its future development. Fajon said that France had collected 17 million signatures for the Stockholm Appeal. Recently, it collected 6 million signatures in just a few days to protest the rearmament of West Germany. Fajon emphasized that the scale and achievements of the peace movement demonstrate its powerful strength.

During the discussion of Fajon's report, the meeting focused on the issue of ensuring that the French people support the World Peace Council's declaration calling for a peace pact between the five major powers, as well as the issue of further expanding the achievements of the French people's signature campaign against the rearmament of West Germany.

The meeting passed a resolution expressing confidence that the cause of peace will triumph and put forward a concrete action program to defend peace. The meeting also passed a telegram of greetings to the Spanish people, praising their heroic struggle against the Franco fascist regime.

### 法国争和平自由战士协会全国常设委会  提出保卫和平行动纲领

1951-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　法国争和平自由战士协会全国常设委会
    提出保卫和平行动纲领
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社巴黎十九日讯：法国争和平自由战士协会全国常设委员会，于三月十八日在巴黎附近奥伯维尔举行全体会议，参加会议的有法国全国七十九省的代表。
    委员会主席法奇在会上作了关于法国的和平斗争及其进一步发展之前途的报告。法奇说：法国曾为斯德哥尔摩宣言征集了一千七百万人的签名。最近又在不多几天之内就征集了六百万人抗议重新武装西德的签名。法奇着重指出：和平运动的这种规模和成就，证明了这个运动有强大的力量。
    会议在讨论法奇报告时，集中研究了保证法国人民拥护世界和平理事会要求五大国缔结和平公约的宣言的问题，以及进一步扩大法国人民反对重新武装西德签名运动的成果的问题。
    会议通过决议，表示确信和平事业将取得胜利，并且提出了保卫和平的具体行动纲领。会议也通过了致西班牙人民的致敬电，赞扬他们反对佛朗哥法西斯政权的英勇斗争。
