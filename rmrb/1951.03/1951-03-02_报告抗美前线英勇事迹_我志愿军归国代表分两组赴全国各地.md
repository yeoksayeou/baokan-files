Title: Reporting Heroic Deeds from the Korean War Front: Our Volunteer Army Delegation Returns Home, Divided into Two Groups to Visit Various Parts of the Country
Author:
Date: 1951-03-02
Page: 1

　　Reporting Heroic Deeds from the Korean War Front
    Our Volunteer Army Delegation Returns Home, Divided into Two Groups to Visit Various Parts of the Country
    [Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch] The Chinese People's Volunteer Army delegation, including Chai Chuanruo and others, a group of six, departed from Beijing in two groups yesterday and today to visit major cities across the country. They will report on the heroic deeds of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army fighting alongside the Korean People's Army to resist the American invading forces.
    From February 9th to 27th, the Volunteer Army representatives gave 46 reports in Beijing to factories, schools, government organizations, democratic parties, people's organizations, religious circles, business circles, and others. The audience numbered over 100,000, and they received a warm welcome from all circles in Beijing.
    Chai Chuanruo and the other three members will first arrive in Shanghai, then proceed to Hangzhou, Nanchang, and directly to Guangzhou. Finally, they plan to return to Beijing via Hengyang, Changsha, Wuhan, Jinan, Baoding, and other cities. Ji Bingqian and his group left Beijing on the 28th, arriving in Tianjin. They will then proceed to Xi'an, Lanzhou, Kaifeng, Luoyang, Chongqing, Chengdu, Kunming, Guiyang, and other cities, and will return to Beijing after visiting Zhangjiakou, Chengde, Guisui, and other places.

### 报告抗美前线英勇事迹  我志愿军归国代表分两组赴全国各地

1951-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　报告抗美前线英勇事迹
    我志愿军归国代表分两组赴全国各地
    【新华社一日讯】中国人民志愿军归国代表柴川若等一行六人，已于昨今两日分两组离京分赴全国各大城市，报告中国人民志愿军与朝鲜人民军并肩作战，反抗美国侵略军的英勇事迹。
    自二月九日起至二十七日，志愿军代表已在京向各工厂、学校、机关、各民主党派、各人民团体、宗教界、工商界等作了四十六次报告，听众十万余人，受到北京各界热烈的欢迎。
    柴川若等一行三人，将先抵上海，然后经杭州、南昌直达广州，最后拟经由衡阳、长沙、武汉、济南、保定等城回京。稽炳前等一行已于二十八日离京抵天津，将转赴西安、兰州、开封、洛阳、重庆、成都、昆明、贵阳等城市，并在赴张家口、承德、归绥等地后返京。
