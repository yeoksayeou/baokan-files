Title: China People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea General Association, Northeast Branch, Established in Shenyang

Author:

Date: 1951-03-27
Page: 1

　　China People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea General Association
    Northeast Branch Established in Shenyang

    [Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 26th] The Northeast Branch of the China People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression was established in Shenyang on the 21st. At the meeting, 115 representatives from all strata of society, people's organizations, ethnic minorities, and different religious beliefs were elected as members of the association. Gao Chongmin was elected as chairman, and Liu Zhiming, Zhang Weizhen, Han Tianshi, and Guo Mingqiu were elected as vice-chairmen. The meeting passed the association's "Notice on Responding to the Resolution of the World Peace Council and Universally and Deeply Launching the Anti-American and Aid-Korea Movement in the Entire Northeast Region."

### 中国人民抗美援朝总会  东北总分会已在沈阳成立

1951-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民抗美援朝总会
    东北总分会已在沈阳成立
    【新华社沈阳二十六日电】中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会东北总分会已于二十一日在沈阳成立。会上选出属于各阶层、各人民团体、各少数民族及不同宗教信仰的代表一百一十五人为该会委员，并推选高崇民为主席，刘芝明、张维祯、韩天石、郭明秋为副主席。会议通过了该会《为响应世界和平理事会决议，并在东北全区普及、深入展开抗美援朝运动》的通告。
