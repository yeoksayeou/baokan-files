Workers at Shijingshan Power Plant Capture Special Agents
Lao Ding, Chen Jinyan (Artwork)

    ⑩ Due to the workers' denunciation, comrades from the security team searched Li Xiutian's home and found a handgun and five bullets.

    ? The workshop workers resented Li Xiutian's vicious actions in sabotaging production, so they held a workshop struggle session to expose his conspiracy. Everyone unanimously demanded that he be sent to the People's Court for legal punishment.

    ? The alert and responsible Chu Youcai security team protected the people's property, made great contributions, and was commended by the National Electricity Industry Association. (End)

    石景山发电厂工人捉特务

    ⑩由于工人的告发，安全小组的同志到李秀田家中搜出手枪一枝，子弹五粒。
    ?车间工人们愤恨李秀田破坏生产的毒辣行为，就开了一个车间斗争会，揭露了他的阴谋，大家一致要求送他到人民法院，依法惩办。
