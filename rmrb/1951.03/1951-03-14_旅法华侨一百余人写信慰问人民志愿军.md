Title: Over a Hundred Overseas Chinese in France Write Letters to Comfort the People's Volunteer Army
Author:
Date: 1951-03-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th: Over a hundred overseas Chinese in France, including Chen Renkuan, wrote letters to express their sympathy and solicitude to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army who are heroically fighting against the American aggressors on the Korean front. The letter reads: You bravely fought under the glorious banner of resisting America, aiding Korea, safeguarding the family, and defending the country, inflicting fatal blows to the aggressors and reversing the entire situation of the Korean War. When the news arrived, all the workers, students, and merchants among the overseas Chinese in France were deeply moved by your great righteous acts and glorious achievements. Dear soldiers! Your spirit of sacrificing blood and life to defend the motherland, and your great achievements in safeguarding world peace, will be remembered for generations. We would like to firmly assure you that we vow to be your staunch backing.

### 旅法华侨一百余人写信慰问人民志愿军

1951-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　旅法华侨一百余人写信慰问人民志愿军
    【新华社十三日讯】旅法华侨陈仁宽等一百三十二人写信慰问在朝鲜前线对美国侵略者英勇作战的中国人民志愿军。信中写道：你们英勇地在抗美援朝保家卫国的光荣旗帜下，予侵略者以致命的打击，扭转了整个朝鲜战争的局面。消息传来，旅法华侨中的全体工人、学生和商人都被你们伟大的义举和光荣的成就深深地感动了。亲爱的战士们！你们牺牲热血和生命以保卫祖国的精神，你们卫护世界和平的丰功伟业，都将流芳百世。我们愿向你们坚决保证，誓作你们的后盾。
