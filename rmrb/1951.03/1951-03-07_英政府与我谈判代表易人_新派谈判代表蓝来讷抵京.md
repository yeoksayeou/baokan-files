Title: British Government Changes Negotiating Representative; Newly Appointed Representative Lionel Lamb Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-03-07
Page: 4

　　British Government Changes Negotiating Representative
    Newly Appointed Representative Lionel Lamb Arrives in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, 6th] Mr. Hutchison, the negotiating representative dispatched by the British government to negotiate with the Central People's Government of our country on the establishment of diplomatic relations, was recalled and left Beijing for his country on March 5th. Mr. Lionel Lamb, the newly dispatched negotiating representative of the British government, arrived in Beijing by train at 7:34 pm today. Han Xu, Section Chief of the Protocol Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, went to the station to greet him.

### 英政府与我谈判代表易人  新派谈判代表蓝来讷抵京

1951-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　英政府与我谈判代表易人
    新派谈判代表蓝来讷抵京
    【新华社六日讯】英国政府为与我国中央人民政府就建立外交关系问题进行谈判而派遣之谈判代表胡阶森先生已奉调于三月五日离京返国，英国政府新派遣之谈判代表蓝来讷先生于今日下午七时三十四分搭车抵京，我外交部交际处科长韩叙赴站迎接。
