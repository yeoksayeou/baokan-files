Title: Reviewing the Achievements of the People's Film Industry: New Domestic Films Simultaneously Screened in Twenty Major Cities

Author:

Date: 1951-03-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd:

　　To review the brilliant achievements of the people's film industry in 1950, the China Film Distribution and Exhibition Corporation has scheduled a "New Film Exhibition Month of State-Owned Film Studios" to be held simultaneously in sixty cinemas in twenty major cities across the country, starting on the 8th.

　　During the Exhibition Month, each cinema will screen one new film per day. The films to be shown include "New Sons and Daughters of Heroes," "High-Spirited Advance," "Northern Shaanxi Pastoral," and "Great Unity of Chinese Nationalities," a total of twenty-six films produced by the Northeast, Beijing, and Shanghai film studios. These films praise the revolutionary heroism and patriotism of new worker, peasant, and soldier figures in their struggles against the enemy or in production and construction; and report on the leaping progress of the new China from various aspects. After the Exhibition Month, these films will be widely screened in cities, villages, factories, mines, and military units throughout the country, and will also be distributed abroad.

　　The cities holding the Exhibition Month are as follows: Beijing, Tianjin, Zhangjiakou, Shanghai, Nanjing, Jinan, Qingdao, Hangzhou, Shenyang, Harbin, Changchun, Dalian, Hankou, Changsha, Guangzhou, Guilin, Shantou, Chongqing, Xi'an, and Dihua.

### 检阅人民电影事业的成就  全国二十个大城市同时放映国产新片

1951-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　检阅人民电影事业的成就
    全国二十个大城市同时放映国产新片
    【新华社三日讯】为检阅人民电影事业一九五○年的辉煌成就，中国影片经理公司定于八日起在全国二十个大城市的六十家电影院同时举行“国营电影厂出品新片展览月”。
    展览月中，各地影院每天放映新片一部。放映的影片有东北、北京、上海三个电影制片厂摄制的《新儿女英雄传》、《高歌猛进》、《陕北牧歌》、《中国民族大团结》等二十六部。这些影片歌颂着工农兵新人物在对敌斗争或生产建设中的革命的英雄主义与爱国主义；并从各方面报道了新中国飞跃进步的情况。这些影片在展览月以后，将在全国广大的城市、乡村、工厂、矿山和部队中普遍放映，并向国外发行。
    这次举行展览月的，有下列城市：北京、天津、张家口、上海、南京、济南、青岛、杭州、沈阳、哈尔滨、长春、大连、汉口、长沙、广州、桂林、汕头、重庆、西安、迪化。
