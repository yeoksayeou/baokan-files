Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-03-21
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea, protect our homes and defend the country is gradually deepening throughout the country. The liberated peasants of Chang'an County, Shaanxi Province, have voluntarily requested to organize militia teams. They are determined to assist the troops in eliminating bandits and secret agents and defending their country.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国各地抗美援朝保家卫国的运动正在逐步深入。陕西长安县翻身的农民，自动要求组织了民兵队。他们决心协助部队肃清土匪特务，保卫自己的国家。
