Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-03-31
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    There are already forty mechanized farms in Northeast China, with a total of more than 27,000 horsepower in machinery and more than 1,500 male and female drivers. This is Liang Jun, the first female tractor driver in New China. She is the female tractor team leader of the affiliated farm of the Mengya Township Normal School in Dedu County, Heilongjiang Province.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国的机械化农场在东北已经有四十个，机器动力共二万七千余匹马力，男女驾驶员一千五百余名。这就是新中国第一名女拖拉机手——梁军。她是黑龙江省德都县萌芽乡师范学校附属农场的女拖拉机队长。
