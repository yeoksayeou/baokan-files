Title: The Korean People's Army Naval Forces Have Sunk or Damaged Sixty-Nine Enemy Ships in Eight Months

Author: 
Date: 1951-03-03
Page: 1

　　Korean People's Army Naval Forces Have Sunk or Damaged Sixty-Nine Enemy Ships in Eight Months

    [Xinhua News Agency, 2nd Dispatch] Pyongyang dispatch: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army announced a battle report on February 28th stating: From June 25, 1950, to February 22, 1951, the naval forces of the Korean People's Army have sunk and damaged a total of sixty-nine ships belonging to the American invading army and the Syngman Rhee puppet army. This includes the sinking of two cruisers, eleven destroyers, five minesweepers, four patrol boats, seventeen landing craft, six transport ships, and five other ships. Three cruisers, thirteen destroyers, and three minesweepers were damaged.

### 朝鲜人民军海军部队八个月来  击沉击伤敌舰艇六十九艘

1951-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军海军部队八个月来
    击沉击伤敌舰艇六十九艘
    【新华社二日讯】平壤讯：朝鲜人民军总司令部于二月二十八日发表战报称：朝鲜人民军海军部队自一九五○年六月二十五日到一九五一年二月二十二日为止，击沉与击伤美国侵略军和李承晚伪军舰艇共达六十九艘。内计击沉巡洋舰两艘，驱逐舰十一艘，扫雷艇五艘，巡逻艇四艘，登陆艇十七艘，运输舰六艘和其他舰只五艘。击伤巡洋舰三艘，驱逐舰十三艘和扫雷艇三艘。
