Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-03-06
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
     Various new farm tools are gradually being popularized in our country's new rural areas. This is Zhang Shun, a farmer from Dongqiaotou Village, Wugong, Shaanxi, who took the lead in using a new-style wooden-shaft five-inch plow. After plowing the field, he is carrying his beloved new farm tool, smiling, and preparing to go home.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
     各种新农具正在我国的新农村中逐步普及。这是陕西武功东桥头村带头使用新式木辕五寸步犁的农民张顺，在耕地以后，正扛起他心爱的新农具，笑逐颜开地准备回家。
