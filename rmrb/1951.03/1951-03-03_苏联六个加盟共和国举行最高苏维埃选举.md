Title: Supreme Soviet Elections Held in Six Soviet Republics
Author:
Date: 1951-03-03
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 1st. TASS reports: The Soviet Union's Belorussian, Ukrainian, Uzbek, Estonian, Armenian, and Turkmen Republics held elections for the Supreme Soviet on February 25th. Elections in all areas were conducted in a joyous festive atmosphere.

　　In Minsk, the capital of Belorussia, voters arrived at the brilliantly decorated polling stations before the appointed time. In the Stalin electoral district, where the great Stalin was a candidate for representative to the Belorussian Supreme Soviet, all registered voters had cast their ballots before 8 a.m. The vast majority of voters in all Belorussian electoral districts had voted before noon. Voters who had already voted accounted for over 90 percent of all voters in the states of Gomel, Vitebsk, Grodno, and Bobruisk.

　　Kyiv, the capital of Ukraine, was decorated with colorful flags and slogans. All registered voters in the Stalin electoral district had cast their ballots before 8 a.m. Miners from the Donbas, steelworkers from Dnipropetrovsk, and farmers from Poltava happily went to vote. In Tashkent, the capital of Uzbekistan, all registered voters had cast their ballots within the first few hours of the election. In all towns and villages of Estonia, joyous songs echoed everywhere during the election. Skaters from Kirovakan, Armenia, traveled 150 kilometers to participate in the election. Along the Turkmen Main Canal under construction, working people raced to vote, supporting candidates from the alliance of Party members and non-Party members. Polling stations were also set up on Soviet ships sailing at sea. Sailors on Soviet tankers sailing from Antarctica to Cape Town, South Africa, and on whaling ships in the Southern Ocean, cast their ballots in a very short time.

### 苏联六个加盟共和国举行最高苏维埃选举

1951-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联六个加盟共和国举行最高苏维埃选举
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社消息：苏联的白俄罗斯、乌克兰、乌兹贝克、爱沙尼亚、亚美尼亚和土库曼等六个加盟共和国，于二月二十五日举行了最高苏维埃的选举。各地选举都是在欢腾的节日气氛中进行的。
    在白俄罗斯首都明斯克，选民们都在规定时间以前赶到装饰辉煌的投票站。以伟大的斯大林为白俄罗斯最高苏维埃代表候选人的斯大林选区，全体登记的选民在上午八时以前就投完了票。白俄罗斯一切选区的绝大多数选民在正午以前已投票完毕，其中哥美尔、威得比斯克、格罗德诺、波布鲁伊斯克等州已投票的选民均占全部选民百分之九十以上。
    乌克兰的首府基辅，被旗帜和标语装饰得五彩缤纷，斯大林选区的全体选民在上午八时以前就投完了票。顿巴斯的矿工、第聂伯罗彼得罗夫斯克的炼钢工人和波尔塔发的农民，都喜气洋洋地前往投票。乌兹贝克首都塔什干全体登记的选民在选举最初数小时就已投票完毕。爱沙尼亚的一切城镇与乡村在选举期间到处荡漾着欢乐的歌声。亚美尼亚基洛瓦堪市的滑冰者赶到了一百五十公里以外的投票站参加选举。在建设中的土库曼大运河沿线的劳动人民都争先投票，拥护党与非党联盟候选人。航行在海上的苏联船只上都设立了投票站。从南极驶往南非洲开普敦港的苏联油船和南冰洋上捕鲸船上的水手，都在很短的时间内投了票。
