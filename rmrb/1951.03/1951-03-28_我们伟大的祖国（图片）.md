    Our Great Motherland
    Farmers fighting for land reform have reaped the fruits of victory. This is Mao Zhushi, a farmer from Qianqiao Township, Wuxi, Jiangsu, and her newly received land certificate. She was allotted nine fen of paddy fields.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-03-28

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为土地改革而斗争的农民已经取得了胜利的果实。这是江苏无锡钱桥乡农民毛朱氏和她刚领到的土地证。她分得了九分水田。
