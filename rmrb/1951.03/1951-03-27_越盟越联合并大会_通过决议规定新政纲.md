Title: Viet Minh-Viet Lien Merger Congress Passes Resolution Stipulating New Program

Author:

Date: 1951-03-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, March 23rd: The National Congress, convened in the first half of March to merge the two Vietnamese national united front organizations - the Viet Minh and the Viet Lien - passed a resolution, deciding on the merger of the two organizations, and stipulating the principles of the program and regulations of the merged Vietnam National United Front.

　　The resolution first expressed warm welcome to the Viet Minh Steering Committee and the Viet Lien Central Committee for their policy of merging the two organizations, and decided to merge the two organizations into the Vietnam National United Front.

　　The resolution stipulates that the new program of the Viet Lien Front will be based on the following principles:

　　(1) Unite all patriotic strata of the Vietnamese people on the basis of the alliance of the working class, peasants, and mental laborers, and carry the liberation struggle and national construction to the end.

　　(2) Armed resistance and national construction should proceed simultaneously. Armed resistance will be a long-term resistance, and national construction must take as its main goal the work necessary to achieve victory in the resistance.

　　(3) During the armed resistance, the living standards of the people should be improved simultaneously, and the principles of taking into account both public and private interests and benefiting both labor and capital should be guaranteed.

　　(4) Combine true patriotism with true internationalism, link the Vietnamese people's liberation struggle with the liberation struggles of the Lao and Cambodian people, and link it with the movement to defend world peace and democracy.

　　The resolution stipulates that the regulations of the Viet Lien Front should be drafted on the basis of the following principles: ensuring broad national unity, realizing democracy, respecting the independence of the organizations within the Viet Lien Front, and seeking progress through criticism and self-criticism.

　　The resolution also stipulates that an alliance of the people of Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia should be established on the basis of free choice, equality, and mutual assistance in order to defeat the common enemy and establish three independent, prosperous, and strong countries.

### 越盟越联合并大会   通过决议规定新政纲

1951-03-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　越盟越联合并大会
    通过决议规定新政纲
    【新华社二十六日讯】越南通讯社北越二十三日讯：为合并越南两个民族统一战线组织——越盟和越联而在三月上半月召开的全国代表大会，曾通过决议，决定了两个组织的合并，并规定合并后的越南民族联合阵线的政纲和规章的原则。
    决议首先表示热烈欢迎越盟指导委员会和越联中央委员会使这两个组织合并的政策，并决定将两个组织合并为越南民族联合阵线。
    决议规定采用以下列原则为基础的越联阵线新政纲：
    （一）以工人阶级、农民和脑力劳动者的联盟为基础，团结越南人民的一切爱国阶层，把解放斗争和国家建设进行到底。
    （二）武装抗战和国家建设同时并进。武装抗战将是长期的抗战，而国家建设必须以取得抗战胜利所必需的工作为其主要目标。
    （三）在武装抗战期间，同时提高人民的生活水平，保障公私兼顾和劳资两利的原则。
    （四）使真正的爱国主义和真正的国际主义相结合，把越南人民的解放斗争与老挝和柬埔寨人民的解放斗争联系起来，与保卫世界和平民主的运动联系起来。
    决议规定草拟以下列原则为基础的越联阵线规章：保证广泛的民族团结，实现民主，尊重越联阵线内部各组织的独立，通过批评与自我批评求得进步。
    决议并规定在自由选择、平等和互助的基础上，建立越南、老挝和柬埔寨人民的联盟，借以战胜共同敌人，建立三个独立、繁荣、强盛的国家。
