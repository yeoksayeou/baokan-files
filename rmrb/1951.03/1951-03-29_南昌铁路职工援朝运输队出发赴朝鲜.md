Title: Nanchang Railway Workers' Aid-Korea Transportation Team Departs for Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-03-29
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, 28th: The first group of volunteer aid-Korea transportation team of the Nanchang Branch of the Shanghai Railway Bureau departed from Nanchang for Korea on the 24th. People's representatives from all walks of life in Nanchang went to the station to warmly see them off.

### 南昌铁路职工援朝运输队出发赴朝鲜

1951-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　南昌铁路职工援朝运输队出发赴朝鲜
    【新华社南昌二十八日电】上海铁路局南昌分局职工第一批志愿援朝运输队已于二十四日由南昌出发赴朝鲜。南昌各界人民代表曾到车站热烈欢送。
