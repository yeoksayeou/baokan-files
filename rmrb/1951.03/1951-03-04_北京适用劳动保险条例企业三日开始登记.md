Title: Beijing Enterprises to Which Labor Insurance Regulations Apply Begin Registration on the 3rd

Author:

Date: 1951-03-04
Page: 2

Beijing Enterprises to Which Labor Insurance Regulations Apply Begin Registration on the 3rd
    [This Newspaper] According to the Beijing Municipal News Agency: The Beijing Municipal People's Government Labor Bureau issued a notice stating that the registration of enterprises in Beijing currently subject to the "Labor Insurance Regulations of the People's Republic of China" will begin on the 3rd. The notice states:
    The "Labor Insurance Regulations of the People's Republic of China" and the "Decision on Promulgating the 'Labor Insurance Regulations of the People's Republic of China' and Implementing them Periodically" have been promulgated by the State Council of the Central People's Government (see this newspaper on February 27th). All enterprises that meet the requirements of Article 2 of the regulations must, in accordance with the "Regulations on Labor Insurance Registration Procedures" promulgated by the Ministry of Labor of the Central People's Government (see this newspaper on March 1st), complete registration procedures at the Labor Bureau from the 3rd to the 15th of March.

### 北京适用劳动保险条例企业三日开始登记

1951-03-04
第2版()
专栏：

　　北京适用劳动保险条例企业三日开始登记
    【本报讯】据京市新闻处讯：北京市人民政府劳动局发布通告，自三日起开始进行北京市目前适用“中华人民共和国劳动保险条例”企业的登记工作。该通告称：
    “中华人民共和国劳动保险条例”及“关于公布《中华人民共和国劳动保险条例》并定期实行的决定”，业经中央人民政府政务院公布（见二月二十七日本报）。凡合于该条例第二条规定的企业，均须按照中央人民政府劳动部公布的“关于劳动保险登记手续的规定”（见三月一日本报），自三日起，至三月十五日止，至劳动局办理登记手续。
