Title: "Chinese Workers' Pictorial" is Very Popular in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1951-03-31
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 30th: The "Chinese Workers' Pictorial" published by the China Workers' Publishing House with Russian explanations is very popular in Moscow. The newspaper Labor published a lengthy article on the 29th titled "The Heroic Chinese Working Class in Struggle and Labor," stating: "Soviet readers are celebrating the publication of the Chinese Workers' Pictorial with great care, as it reports on the heroism of the Chinese working class and the leading role played by the Chinese Communist Party in organizing the Chinese people to achieve world-historical victories. The Chinese Workers' Pictorial will allow Soviet readers to understand the life and labor achievements of the heroic Chinese working class, which will benefit the further consolidation of the unbreakable Sino-Soviet friendship and the consolidation of the camp of peace, democracy, and socialism."

### 《中国工人画刊》在莫斯科大受欢迎

1951-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　《中国工人画刊》在莫斯科大受欢迎
    【新华社莫斯科三十日电】我国工人出版社出版的有俄文说明的“中国工人画刊”，在莫斯科大受欢迎。劳动报二十九日以“在斗争和劳动中的英雄的中国工人阶级”为题发表长文加以评介称：“苏联读者以巨大的关怀庆贺中国工人画刊的出版，它报道了中国工人阶级的英雄主义，中国共产党在组织中国人民获得世界历史性胜利中所起的领导作用。中国工人画刊将使苏联读者认识英雄的中国工人阶级的生活和劳动成就，对于牢不可破的中苏友谊的进一步巩固，对于和平、民主和社会主义阵营的巩固，均有裨益。”
