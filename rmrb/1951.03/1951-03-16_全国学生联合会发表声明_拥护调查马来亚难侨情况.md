Title: All-China Students' Federation Issues Statement Endorsing Investigation into the Situation of Malayan Refugee Overseas Chinese
Author:
Date: 1951-03-16
Page: 1

　　All-China Students' Federation Issues Statement
    Endorsing Investigation into the Situation of Malayan Refugee Overseas Chinese

    [Xinhua News Agency, 14th] The All-China Students' Federation recently issued a statement endorsing the decision of the Chinese People's Relief Committee for Malayan Refugees to send an investigation team to Malaya to investigate the suffering of overseas Chinese. The statement condemns the various atrocities committed by British imperialism against the overseas Chinese in Malaya. The statement said: We, students across the country, firmly support the just action of sending an investigation team. We are determined to work with the people of the whole country to actively support the efforts of Malayan compatriots and overseas Chinese students to defend their survival and strive for legitimate rights and interests.

### 全国学生联合会发表声明  拥护调查马来亚难侨情况

1951-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国学生联合会发表声明
    拥护调查马来亚难侨情况
    【新华社十四日讯】中华全国学生联合会顷发表声明，拥护中国人民救济马来亚难侨委员会关于派遣调查团赴马来亚调查侨胞受难情况的决定。声明谴责英帝国主义迫害马来亚华侨的各种暴行。声明称：我们全国同学坚决拥护派调查团进行调查的正义行动。我们决心和全国人民一道，积极支持马来亚侨胞和华侨同学保卫自己生存、争取正当权益的努力。
