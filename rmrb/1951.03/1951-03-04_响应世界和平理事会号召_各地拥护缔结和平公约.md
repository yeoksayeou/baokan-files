Title: Responding to the Call of the World Peace Council: Various Locations Support the Conclusion of a Peace Pact
Author:
Date: 1951-03-04
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd: People's organizations and local organizations of various democratic parties across the country unanimously support the declaration and resolutions of the World Peace Council regarding the conclusion of a peace pact and are willing to fight for their realization. Responsible persons of the Preparatory Committees of the Guangdong and Chongqing Federation of Trade Unions have made statements, expressing the working class's firm determination to support the World Peace Council's declaration and resolutions with practical actions of further carrying out patriotic production competitions. The Shandong Provincial Conference of Labor Model Representatives of Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers, which is currently being held, sent a telegram to the China Peace Committee, stating: All of our representatives are confident in completing Shandong Province's industrial and agricultural production tasks for 1951 and are determined to further promote the Resist America, Aid Korea patriotic movement, and respond to the call of the World Peace Council with practical actions. One hundred and twenty-three representatives from thirty-six secondary schools in the Central and Southern Region receiving American subsidies, who are currently in a meeting, issued a declaration saying: We will sever all ties with American imperialism from now on, as a concrete action to support the World Peace Council's declaration. Christian churches and Catholic churches in Guangzhou, Hainan Island, and other places, which are currently carrying out innovation movements, are calling on believers to strive for the realization of the World Peace Council's great declaration.

    Many people's organizations and local organizations of various democratic parties in other places such as Nanjing, Wuhan, GuiSui, Henan, Hubei, Shanxi, and Southern Jiangsu have also issued statements or talks in support. Cheng Qian, chairman of the Hunan Provincial Branch of the China Peace Committee, said in a statement: The people of Hunan Province, who are continuing to deepen the Resist America, Aid Korea patriotic movement, are determined to fight to the end for the realization of the World Peace Council's declaration and resolutions. People's organizations in Datong City, Chahar Province, have decided to immediately launch extensive propaganda activities in agencies, schools, factories, mines, and streets to support the conclusion of a peace pact.

### 响应世界和平理事会号召  各地拥护缔结和平公约

1951-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　响应世界和平理事会号召
    各地拥护缔结和平公约
    【新华社三日讯】各地人民团体及各民主党派地方组织一致拥护世界和平理事会关于缔结和平公约的宣言和各项决议，并愿为其实现而奋斗。广东省及重庆市总工会筹委会负责人均发表谈话，表示工人阶级坚决以进一步开展爱国主义生产竞赛的实际行动来拥护世界和平理事会的宣言和决议。正在举行会议的山东全省工农兵劳动模范代表会议致电中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会称：我们全体代表有信心完成山东省一九五一年的工农业生产任务，并决心更深入地开展抗美援朝爱国运动，用实际行动响应世界和平理事会的号召。正在开会的中南区接受美国津贴的三十六个中等学校一百二十三个代表发表宣言说：我们从此割断和美帝国主义的一切关系，作为我们拥护世界和平理事会宣言的具体行动。正在开展革新运动的广州市、海南岛等地基督教会、天主教会均号召教徒为实现世界和平理事会的伟大宣言而努力。
    其他如南京、武汉、归绥、河南、湖北、山西、苏南等地许多人民团体及各民主党派地方组织都发表了拥护的声明或谈话。中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会湖南省分会主席程潜发表谈话称：正在继续深入开展抗美援朝爱国运动的湖南省人民，坚决为实现世界和平理事会的宣言和决议而奋斗到底。察哈尔省大同市各人民团体决定立即在各机关、学校、工厂、矿山、街道中广泛展开拥护缔结和平公约的宣传活动。
