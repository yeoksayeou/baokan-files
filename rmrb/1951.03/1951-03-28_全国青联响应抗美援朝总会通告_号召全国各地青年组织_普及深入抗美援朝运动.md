Title: All-China Youth Federation Responds to the General Association to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea Notice, Calling on Youth Organizations Across the Country to Popularize and Deepen the Movement to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-03-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 27th: The All-China Democratic Youth Federation, at its first Standing Committee meeting of this year held on March 14th, passed a resolution to respond to the notice of the Chinese People's General Association to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, calling on democratic youth federations and their affiliated organizations across the country to make popularizing and deepening the movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea the central task of this year. The decision stated:

　　Local democratic youth federations should immediately convene youth representative conferences or forums on resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea among youth from various democratic parties, religions, ethnic minorities, unorganized youth in various districts and streets, and youth from other sectors to explain and convey issues related to resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, and mobilize the masses to consciously formulate patriotic pacts based on specific circumstances and needs to consolidate the patriotic enthusiasm of the masses. After the meeting, the democratic youth federations should organize and assist representatives in conveying the message to the broad masses of youth. In addition, local democratic youth federations should regularly hold lectures, evening parties, exhibitions, reading rooms, etc., targeting youth from all sectors and unorganized youth.

　　In late April, local democratic youth federations should launch a signature campaign among youth to support the conclusion of a peace pact and a vote on opposing the arming of Japan and concluding a comprehensive and just peace treaty with Japan.

### 全国青联响应抗美援朝总会通告  号召全国各地青年组织  普及深入抗美援朝运动

1951-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国青联响应抗美援朝总会通告
    号召全国各地青年组织
    普及深入抗美援朝运动
    【新华社二十七日讯】中华全国民主青年联合总会在三月十四日召开的本年度第一次常委会上通过了响应中国人民抗美援朝总会通告的决定，号召全国各地民主青年联合会及其所属团体，以普及和深入抗美援朝运动作为今年的中心工作。决定称：
    各地民主青年联合会应立即在各民主党派、宗教、少数民族青年，各区、各街道无组织的青年以及其他各界青年中普遍召开抗美援朝青年代表会议或座谈会，讲解及传达有关抗美援朝的问题，并根据具体情况与需要动员群众自觉地订立爱国公约，以巩固群众的爱国热情。会后，民主青年联合会应组织和帮助代表向广大青年进行传达。此外，各地民主青年联合会应经常举办以各界青年和无组织青年为对象的讲座、晚会、展览会、书报阅览等。
    四月下旬，各地民主青年联合会应发动青年举行拥护缔结和平公约的签名，和关于反对武装日本，缔结全面的公正的对日和约的投票。
