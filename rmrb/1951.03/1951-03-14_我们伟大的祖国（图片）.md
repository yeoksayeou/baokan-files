Author:

Our Great Motherland

　　This spring, the rural areas across the country are full of new scenes. The North is making early preparations for spring plowing, while the South has already started early. This is the Yi people's farmers in Sikai Damazi, Zhaojue County, Liangshan Mountain, Xikang, carrying out spring plowing. They said, "The people's government's ethnic policy has made us no longer have to fight unjustly. We must plant more land and produce more grain."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    今年春天的全国农村中都充满了新气象，北方各地正在提早准备春耕；南方各地已经提早开始了。这是西康大凉山昭觉县四开坝子的彛族农民正在进行春耕。他们说：“人民政府的民族政策，使我们再也不用打冤家了。我们一定要多种地，多打粮。”
