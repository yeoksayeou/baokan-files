Title: Korean People's Army Shoots Down Twelve Enemy Planes
Author:
Date: 1951-03-27
Page: 1

　　Korean People's Army Shoots Down Twelve Enemy Planes
    [Xinhua News Agency, 26th] Pyongyang dispatch: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army announced in war reports on the 24th and 25th that: People's Army units shot down nine enemy planes in the Seoul area, and People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down three enemy planes in the Pyongyang area.

### 朝人民军击落敌机十二架

1951-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝人民军击落敌机十二架
    【新华社二十六日讯】平壤讯：朝鲜人民军总司令部于二十四、二十五日发表战报称：人民军部队在汉城方面击落敌机九架，人民军高射炮部队在平壤地区击落敌机三架。
