Title: America Exploits Japan's Aggression in Korea, Resulting in Continuous Rise in Japanese Prices
Author:
Date: 1951-03-21
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 20th: The result of America's pushing Japan's economy increasingly onto a war footing is driving continuous price increases in Japan. According to the announcement on the 12th by the Japanese Economic Stabilization Board: since June 24th of last year (the day before the outbreak of the war of aggression), the price index in Tokyo has risen by an average of 59.9 percent, with the price of production materials rising by 66.5 percent and the price of consumer goods rising by 49.3 percent. Among consumer goods, textiles have skyrocketed by 87.1 percent. The price increase index for other items is: machinery 83.4 percent, sundries 64.3 percent, and fuel 33 percent.

### 美国利用日本侵朝结果  日本物价不断上涨

1951-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国利用日本侵朝结果
    日本物价不断上涨
    【新华社二十日讯】东京讯：美国使日本经济日益走上战争轨道的结果，促使日本物价不断上涨。据日本经济安定本部十二日宣布：自去年六月二十四日（侵略战争爆发的前一天）以来，东京的物价指数已平均上涨达百分之五十九点九，其中生产物资的价格上涨百分之六十六点五，消费物资的价格上涨百分之四十九点三。在消费物资中，纺织品暴涨百分之八十七点一。其他物品价格的上涨指数为：机器百分之八十三点四，杂货百分之六十四点三，燃料百分之三十三。
