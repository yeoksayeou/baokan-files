Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 76th Meeting Approving the Brief Summary of the Ministry of Fuel Industry's Work Last Year and the Report on This Year's Guidelines and Tasks
Author:
Date: 1951-03-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The 76th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held at 3:00 PM on the 16th. After listening to and discussing Minister Chen Yu of the Ministry of Fuel Industry's report on the brief summary of the ministry's work in 1950 and the guidelines and tasks for 1951, the meeting approved the report. The meeting also approved several appointments and removals.

### 政务院举行第七十六次会议  批准关于燃料工业部去年工作简要总结和今年方针任务的报告

1951-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行第七十六次会议
   批准关于燃料工业部去年工作简要总结和今年方针任务的报告
    【新华社十六日讯】中央人民政府政务院第七十六次政务会议于十六日下午三时举行。会议在听取和讨论了中央人民政府燃料工业部陈郁部长所作的关于该部一九五零年的工作简要总结和一九五一年的方针与任务的报告后，批准了这个报告。会议并通过任免案多起。
