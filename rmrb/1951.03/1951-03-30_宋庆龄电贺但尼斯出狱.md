Title: Soong Ching Ling Sends Congratulatory Telegram on Dennis's Release
Author:
Date: 1951-03-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Soong Ching Ling, Chairman of the China Welfare Institute, sent a telegram congratulating Eugene Dennis, General Secretary of the Communist Party of the United States, on his release from prison. The telegram reads as follows:
Dear Mr. Eugene Dennis,
We were delighted to learn of your release from prison on March 12th, and we extend our heartfelt congratulations to you.
You have dedicated all your strength to defending peace and preventing the catastrophic Third World War that would slaughter humanity. On behalf of the entire Chinese relief and welfare community, I extend my highest respects to you!

China Welfare Institute Chairman Soong Ching Ling

### 宋庆龄电贺但尼斯出狱

1951-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　宋庆龄电贺但尼斯出狱
    【新华社二十九日讯】中国人民救济总会主席宋庆龄电贺美国共产党总书记但尼斯出狱。电文如下：亲爱的尤金·但尼斯先生：
    欣悉你于三月十二日出狱，我们谨以十分欢慰的心情向你致贺。
    为了保卫和平及防止第三次世界大战屠杀人类的灾难，你曾供献出一切力量。我代表中国全体救济福利界对你致崇高的敬礼！
                            中国人民救济总会主席  宋庆龄
