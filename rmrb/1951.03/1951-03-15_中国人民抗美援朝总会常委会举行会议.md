Title: Meeting of the Standing Committee of the Chinese People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea Headquarters
Author:
Date: 1951-03-15
Page: 1

　　The meeting passed a circular on responding to the resolution of the World Peace Council and popularizing and deepening the anti-American and aid-Korea propaganda throughout the country.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 14th] The Standing Committee of the China Peace Committee Opposing American Aggression held a meeting this afternoon. Those present were Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Committee, Peng Zhen and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen, Peng Zemin, Xiao San, Xu Deheng, Zhang Xiruo, Shao Lizi, Hu Qiaomu, Liao Chengzhi, Wu Yaozong, members of the Standing Committee, and more than 20 people including heads of various departments of the Committee. The meeting discussed and passed the "Circular of the China Peace Committee Opposing American Aggression on Responding to the Resolution of the World Peace Council and Popularizing and Deepening the Anti-American and Aid-Korea Propaganda Throughout the Country." The meeting then listened to Xiao San's report on attending the Geneva meeting of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council and the meeting of the Secretariat of the World Peace Council, and listened to Wu Maosun, Deputy Secretary-General of the Committee, on the work of the Secretariat. The meeting also approved the abbreviation of the Committee's name as "Chinese People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea Headquarters".

### 中国人民抗美援朝总会常委会举行会议

1951-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民抗美援朝总会常委会举行会议
    通过为响应世界和平理事会决议并在全国普及深入抗美援朝宣传的通告
    【新华社十四日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会常务委员会于今日下午举行会议。到会者有该会主席郭沫若，副主席彭真、陈叔通，常务委员彭泽民、萧三、许德珩、张奚若、邵力子、胡乔木、廖承志、吴耀宗及该会各部门负责人等二十余人。会议讨论并通过了《中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会为响应世界和平理事会决议并在全国普及深入抗美援朝宣传的通告》。会议继听取了萧三关于出席世界和平理事会执行局日内瓦会议及世界和平理事会秘书处会议经过情况的报告，并听取了该会副秘书长吴茂荪关于秘书处工作情况的报告。会议并通过该会会名的简称为“中国人民抗美援朝总会”。
