Title: Beijing Christians Issue Declaration Supporting the Conclusion of a Peace Pact
Author:
Date: 1951-03-23
Page: 1

　　Beijing Christians Issue Declaration
    Supporting the Conclusion of a Peace Pact
    [This newspaper's dispatch] The Beijing Municipal Committee of the Christian Churches and Christian Organizations Branch of the China Peace Committee Opposing American Imperialism held a conference on the 18th, inviting Mr. Wu Yaozong to convey the resolutions of the First World Peace Council and passing the Beijing Christians' declaration supporting the conclusion of a peace pact. The declaration stated: The declaration on the conclusion of a peace pact and other resolutions adopted by the First World Peace Council clearly point out the current direction of defending world peace, and are a program of action for the peace-loving people of the world to further strive for lasting world peace and shatter the imperialist conspiracy to launch wars of aggression. We Christians in Beijing firmly support these resolutions. We will more extensively and deeply carry out the Resist America, Aid Korea movement and the movement against American imperialism's arming of Japan among Christians, enthusiastically respond to the signing of the declaration for the conclusion of a peace pact, and rapidly carry out the Three-Self Patriotic Movement of Chinese Christianity, and formulate this year's work plan for the Beijing Christian Churches and Christian Organizations to defend world peace and oppose American aggression in the shortest possible time to ensure the realization and completion of the above specific actions.

### 北京市基督教徒发表宣言   拥护缔结和平公约

1951-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京市基督教徒发表宣言
    拥护缔结和平公约
    【本报讯】北京市保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会基督教会及基督教团体分会十八日召开大会，请吴耀宗先生传达第一届世界和平理事会决议，并通过北京市基督教徒拥护缔结和平公约的宣言。宣言中称：第一届世界和平理事会所通过的关于缔结和平公约的宣言和其他决议，都明确地指出了当前保卫世界和平的方向，是世界爱好和平的人民进一步争取世界持久和平粉碎帝国主义发动侵略战争阴谋的行动纲领。我们北京市基督徒坚决拥护这些决议。我们将更广泛深入地开展在基督徒中的抗美援朝运动和反对美帝国主义武装日本的运动，热烈响应缔结和平公约宣言的签名，并迅速开展中国基督教的自治、自养、自传运动，并在最短期内制订今年北京市基督教会及基督教团体保卫世界和平反对美国侵略的工作计划，来保证以上各项具体行动的实现和完成。
