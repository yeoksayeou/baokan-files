    Our Great Motherland
    In the third century AD, China already had the world's earliest mileage-counting instrument. This is a model of the "mileage drum cart." The cart is equipped with four types of gears. As the wheels turn, for every mile traveled, the wooden figure on the cart strikes the drum to mark the distance.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在公元三世纪的时候，我国已经出现了在世界上最早的记里数的仪器。这就是“记里鼓车”的模型。车中装置四种齿轮，随着车轮的转动，每行一里 ，车上木人即击鼓为记。
