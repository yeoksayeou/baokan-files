Title: Tianjin People's Circles Hold Meeting to Welcome Representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army
Author:
Date: 1951-03-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, March 3rd: After the arrival in Tianjin of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army returning representatives Ji Bingqian, Li Weiying, and Zhang Puying, they were warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life in Tianjin. On March 2nd, they participated in four welcome rallies with attendances ranging from two thousand, five thousand to over ten thousand people. At the rally of over ten thousand people in Minyuan, various people's organizations presented them with banners, gifts, and flowers. When they mentioned the heroic deeds of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army in Korea in their reports, they repeatedly aroused prolonged applause and cheers. Their speeches were broadcast to the city by the Tianjin People's Broadcasting Station.

It is reported that they plan to stay in Tianjin for a week. Students, workers from various factories, and the people of the Tangda District will hold separate rallies to welcome them.

### 津市各界人民集会  欢迎人民志愿军代表

1951-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　津市各界人民集会
    欢迎人民志愿军代表
    【新华社天津三日电】中国人民志愿军归国代表稽炳前、李维英、张甫英三人抵津后，受到天津各界人民热烈的欢迎。三月二日一天内，他们分别参加了二千、五千到万余人的欢迎大会四起。在民园万余人的大会上，各人民团体献给了他们锦旗和礼品、鲜花。当他们在报告中提到中国人民志愿军在朝鲜的英勇事迹时，屡屡引起长久不息的掌声和欢呼声。他们的讲话都由津市人民广播电台转播给全市收听。
    据悉：他们准备在津逗留一周。全市学生、各工厂工人与塘大区人民，都将分别集会欢迎。
