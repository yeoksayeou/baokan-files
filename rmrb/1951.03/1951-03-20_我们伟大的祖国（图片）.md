    Our Great Motherland
    After gaining land ownership in the land reform struggle, peasants immediately demanded the destruction of old land deeds, which were the "legal expression" of feudal relations of production. This is a scene of peasants in the newly liberated areas publicly burning old land deeds.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    农民在土地改革斗争中取得了土地所有权之后，立即要求销毁旧的地契，也就是封建生产关系的“法律表现”。这是新区农民在当众烧毁旧地契。
