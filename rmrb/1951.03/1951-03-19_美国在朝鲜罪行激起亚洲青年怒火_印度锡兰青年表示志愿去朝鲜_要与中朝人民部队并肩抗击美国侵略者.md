Title: American Crimes in Korea Incite Asian Youth's Anger; Indian and Ceylonese Youth Express Willingness to Go to Korea to Fight Alongside Chinese and Korean People's Forces Against American Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1951-03-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 18th. Indian and Ceylonese youth have enthusiastically expressed their willingness to go to Korea to fight alongside the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers against the American aggressors. In Bombay, approximately fifty youths have expressed to the All-India Student Federation their willingness to go to Korea to fight the American aggressors. At Khalsa College in East Punjab Province, twelve students have already requested the Indian government to facilitate their travel to Korea.

Twenty-five Ceylonese youths, including veterans and students, wrote to the All-India Student Federation expressing their desire to resist America and aid Korea. The letter stated: "The imperialists treat Asia as a plaything, spreading death and wanton destruction on Korean soil, and killing Korean people. We demand an end to this massacre. However, this crime will only end after the imperialists are driven into the sea. The youth of Asia are furious and cannot tolerate it any longer. We, twenty-five Ceylonese youths, are ready to mix our blood with the blood of our Asian brothers who are fighting for their freedom and independence on the Korean battlefield." They stated with confidence: "The justice of our cause will help us overcome all obstacles in our path."

The All-India Student Federation pointed out: "These requests demonstrate the strong hatred of Indian and Asian youth for American interference in Asia; and also show their sincere desire to actively participate in resisting all acts of aggression by imperialists on our continent."

### 美国在朝鲜罪行激起亚洲青年怒火  印度锡兰青年表示志愿去朝鲜  要与中朝人民部队并肩抗击美国侵略者

1951-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国在朝鲜罪行激起亚洲青年怒火
    印度锡兰青年表示志愿去朝鲜
    要与中朝人民部队并肩抗击美国侵略者
    【新华社新德里十八日电】印度和锡兰的青年热情地表示志愿到朝鲜去，和朝鲜人民军及中国人民志愿军并肩抗击美国侵略军。在孟买，约有五十名青年向全印学生联合会表示，愿意到朝鲜去打击美国侵略者。在东旁遮普省的卡尔萨大学，有十二个学生已请求印度政府给予他们到朝鲜去的便利。
    二十五名锡兰青年，其中包括退伍军人、学生等致书全印学生联合会，表示抗美援朝的志愿。信上说：“帝国主义者视我们亚洲为玩物，在朝鲜土地上散布死亡和肆意破坏，杀害朝鲜人民。我们要求终止这种大屠杀的罪行。但是，只有在把帝国主义者赶下海去以后，这种罪行才会终止。亚洲的青年怒火中烧，忍无可忍。我们二十五个锡兰青年，已准备在朝鲜的战场上，把我们的热血与正在为他们的自由和独立而战的亚洲兄弟们的热血混合起来。”他们满怀信心地说：“我们的事业的正义性，将帮助我们克服横在我们道路上的一切障碍。”
    全印学生联合会指出：“这些要求表明印度和亚洲的青年对美国在亚洲土地上所进行的干涉的强烈痛恨；也表明了他们真诚地希望积极参加抗击帝国主义者在我们大陆上的一切侵略行为。”
