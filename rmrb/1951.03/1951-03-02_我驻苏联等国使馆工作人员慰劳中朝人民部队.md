Title: Staff of Our Embassy in the Soviet Union and Other Countries Console the Chinese and Korean People's Forces
Author:
Date: 1951-03-02
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 17th: Staff members of our embassies in the Soviet Union and Sweden enthusiastically donated money to comfort the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army. The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression recently received donations from staff members of our embassy in the Soviet Union totaling 33.2 million yuan in Renminbi equivalent, and also received donations from staff members of our embassy in Sweden totaling 10 million yuan in Renminbi equivalent.
　　Xinhua News Agency, 24th: The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression recently also received donations from staff members of our embassy in Bulgaria totaling 79 million yuan in Renminbi equivalent, donations from staff members of our embassy in Romania totaling 54 million yuan in Renminbi equivalent, and donations from staff members of our embassy in Poland totaling 5 million yuan in Renminbi equivalent. Staff members of our embassy in Romania also wrote a letter to comfort the Chinese People's Volunteers. The letter stated: The Chinese and Korean people and the people of the world are cheering and celebrating your great victory. Here, let us tell you: The Romanian people place unlimited hope and heartfelt love in you. All staff members of our embassy vow to redouble our efforts to do our job well as support for you.

### 我驻苏联等国使馆工作人员慰劳中朝人民部队

1951-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　我驻苏联等国使馆工作人员慰劳中朝人民部队
    【新华社十七日讯】我国驻苏联及瑞典大使馆工作人员，热烈捐款慰劳我国人民志愿军及朝鲜人民军。中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会最近收到我国驻苏联大使馆工作人员捐献的慰劳金计折合人民币三千三百二十万元，又收到我国驻瑞典大使馆工作人员慰劳金计折合人民币一千万元。
    【新华社二十四日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会最近又收到我国驻保加利亚大使馆工作人员捐献的慰劳金计折合人民币七千九百万元，我国驻罗马尼亚大使馆工作人员捐献的慰劳金计折合人民币五千四百万元，我国驻波兰大使馆工作人员捐献的慰劳金计折合人民币五百万元。我国驻罗马尼亚大使馆工作人员并写信慰问我国人民志愿军。信上写道：中朝人民和全世界人民都在欢呼庆祝你们的伟大胜利。在这里，让我们转告你们：罗马尼亚的人民对你们寄以无限的希望和衷心的敬爱。我们全使馆的工作人员，誓以加倍努力做好自己的工作作为对你们的支援。
