Title: New York Herald Tribune Admits: 100,000 American Troops Invading Korea Have Been Killed or Wounded
Author:
Date: 1951-03-17
Page: 1

　　New York Herald Tribune Admits:
　　100,000 American Troops Invading Korea Have Been Killed or Wounded
　　【Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 16th】The Chinese and Korean People's Army's implementation of the operational policy of annihilating the enemy's living forces has caused heavy casualties to the American troops invading Korea. According to the New York Worker's Daily of March 4th, quoting the New York Herald Tribune's estimate, the American troops participating in the invasion of Korea, including the Army, Navy, Air Force, and Marine Corps, totaled 350,000 to 400,000. The New York Herald Tribune admits that since the American army launched the invasion of Korea last year, the number of American soldiers killed or wounded evacuated from Korea has reached 100,000.

### 纽约先驱论坛报承认  侵朝美军已伤亡十万

1951-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　纽约先驱论坛报承认
    侵朝美军已伤亡十万
    【新华社布拉格十六日电】中朝人民军队执行歼灭敌人有生力量的作战方针，已使侵朝美军遭受了重大的伤亡。据三月四日纽约工人日报引纽约先驱论坛报的估计说，参加侵朝战争的美军，把陆军、海军、空军与海军陆战队计算在内，共有三十五万到四十万人。纽约先驱论坛报承认：自从去年美军发动侵朝战争以来，从朝鲜运出的美军死伤人数已有十万人。
