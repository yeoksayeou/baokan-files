Title: Peak Coal Mine Continues to Report Good News; Luo Yongjin Group Sets New Record; Pneumatic Drill Coal Production Reaches 254.4 Tons
Author:
Date: 1951-03-27
Page: 1
　　
　　Peak Coal Mine Continues to Report Good News
    Luo Yongjin Group Sets New Record
    Pneumatic Drill Coal Production Reaches 254.4 Tons
    [Our Newspaper] According to Xinhua News Agency: Workers at the state-owned Fengfeng Coal Mine have created a new national record in pneumatic drill coal mining. Following the Zhao Wenxin group's breaking of the national record of 215 tons set by the Chen Yuhua group of Huainan Coal Mine on February 25th with 243 tons in one shift on the 19th, the Luo Yongjin group created a new record of 254.4 tons on the 22nd.

### 峰峰煤矿续传捷报  罗永金组创更新纪录   风镐采煤量达二百五十四点四吨

1951-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　峰峰煤矿续传捷报
    罗永金组创更新纪录
    风镐采煤量达二百五十四点四吨
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：国营峰峰煤矿工人在创造风镐采煤新纪录中，又创造全国更新纪录。继赵文信小组于十九日夜在一班时间之内，以二百四十三吨打破了淮南煤矿二月二十五日陈玉华小组所创造的二百十五吨的全国新纪录后，二十二日罗永金小组又创造了二百五十四点四吨的更新纪录。
