Title: The 75th Meeting of the Government Administration Council Approves the People's Bank of China and the Ministry of Trade's Reports on Last Year's Work Summary and This Year's Work Plan
Author:
Date: 1951-03-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 9th—The 75th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held at 3:30 PM on the 9th. At the meeting, Nan Hanchen, President of the People's Bank of China, and Ye Jizhuang, Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Trade, respectively delivered reports summarizing the work of their respective bank and ministry in 1950 and outlining the work plan for 1951. After discussion, the meeting approved the aforementioned two reports. In addition, the meeting approved several appointment and removal cases and cases to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval of appointments and removals.

### 政务院第七十五次会议  批准人民银行、贸易部关于去年工作总结和今年工作计划的报告

1951-03-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院第七十五次会议
    批准人民银行、贸易部关于去年工作总结和今年工作计划的报告
    【新华社九日讯】中央人民政府政务院第七十五次政务会议于九日下午三时半举行。会上，中国人民银行南汉宸行长和中央人民政府贸易部叶季壮部长分别作了各该行、部一九五○年工作简要总结与一九五一年工作计划的报告。会议经讨论后，批准了上述两个报告。此外，会议通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
