Title: Hengyang Railway Workers' Volunteer Aid Korea Transportation Team Sets Off
Author:
Date: 1951-03-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, 14th—The Hengyang Railway Bureau's Volunteer Aid Korea Transportation Team departed in two groups on March 6th and 10th. When the Volunteer Aid Korea Transportation Team departed from Hengyang, they received a warm send-off from the local people. The family members of the team members all urged their loved ones to wholeheartedly support the Chinese and Korean people's soldiers and thoroughly eliminate the American aggressors.

### 衡阳铁路职工志愿援朝运输队出发

1951-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　衡阳铁路职工
    志愿援朝运输队出发
    【新华社长沙十四日电】衡阳铁路局职工志愿援朝运输队已于三月六日及十日分两批先后出发。志愿援朝运输队从衡阳出发时，受到当地人民的热烈欢送。队员的家属都叮嘱他们的亲人，要全心全意地支援中朝人民战士，彻底消灭美国侵略军。
