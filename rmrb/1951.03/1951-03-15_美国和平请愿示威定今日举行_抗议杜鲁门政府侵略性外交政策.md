Title: U.S. Peace Petition Demonstration Scheduled for Today: Protesting the Truman Government's Aggressive Foreign Policy
Author:
Date: 1951-03-15
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, March 14th: According to a Reuter's dispatch from New York: The U.S. National Pilgrimage for Peace to Washington is scheduled to take place on the 15th. Prior to the march, the American "National Pilgrimage for Peace Committee" conducted a national public opinion poll on peace. The results of the poll will be taken to Washington by the representatives participating in the march.

　　The number of representatives participating in the march will greatly exceed original expectations. The New York Labor Peace Conference and the Youth Peace Committee will each send 500 representatives to participate. The newly formed Chicago Pilgrimage for Peace Committee, composed of forty pastors, labor leaders, and other prominent figures, has pledged to send 250 representatives to participate in the march.

　　The representatives participating in the peace petition march will hold mass demonstrations in Washington to protest the Truman government's aggressive foreign policy and demand that the government take action to strive for peace.

### 美国和平请愿示威定今日举行  抗议杜鲁门政府侵略性外交政策

1951-03-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国和平请愿示威定今日举行
    抗议杜鲁门政府侵略性外交政策
    【新华社十四日讯】据电通社纽约讯：美国全国前往华盛顿的和平十字军请愿游行将于十五日举行。主持这次请愿的美国“全国和平十字军委员会”在游行前，举行了一次全国规模的和平民意测验。测验的结果将由参加请愿游行的代表们带到华盛顿去。
    参加请愿游行的代表的数目将大大地超过原来的预料。纽约劳工和平大会和青年和平委员会将各派五百名代表参加。芝加哥新成立的和平十字军委员会是由四十个牧师，劳工领袖和其他著名人士组成的，该委员会已保证派遣二百五十名代表参加请愿游行。
    和平请愿游行的代表们将在华盛顿举行群众示威运动，抗议杜鲁门政府的侵略性的外交政策，并要求政府采取行动来争取和平。
