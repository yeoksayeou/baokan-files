Title: Central People's Government Prepares to Form Central-South Visiting Delegation, Two Sub-Delegations of Southwest Visiting Delegation Return to Beijing One After Another
Author:
Date: 1951-03-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 21st: Following the Central Northwest and Southwest Nationalities Visiting Delegations, the Central People's Government is now actively preparing to organize a Central-South Nationalities Visiting Delegation. This delegation will be composed of cadres drawn from the Cultural and Educational Committee, the Financial and Economic Committee, the Political and Legal Committee, and the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council, and is scheduled to depart from Beijing in mid-April. The main tasks of the visiting delegation are to publicize the nationality policies in the Common Program to the people of all nationalities in Central-South, convey the concern of Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government for them, and understand their situation and needs, so as to promptly reflect them to the Central People's Government. The delegation plans to divide into four teams, visiting areas inhabited by or where people of various nationalities live together in Guangxi, Guangdong, Hunan, and Hainan Island. The delegation will include a cultural troupe, a medical team, and a film projection team, and will carry a large number of gifts from the Central People's Government to the people of all nationalities.

According to Xinhua News Agency: The first and third sub-delegations of the Central People's Government's Southwest Nationalities Visiting Delegation have concluded their visits. All members of these two sub-delegations, more than 80 people, have returned to Beijing one after another. The second sub-delegation remained in Yunnan Province to continue its visit and arrived in Ning'er (i.e., Pu'er) county seat in the southern part of the province on March 8th.

On their way from Kunming to Ning'er, the second sub-delegation extended cordial greetings to the people of all nationalities in Kaiyuan, Mengzi, Shiping, Yuanjiang, and Mojiang counties, and showed films for them, which were warmly welcomed by the people of all nationalities.

### 中央人民政府  筹组中南访问团  西南访问团两分团先后返京

1951-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府
    筹组中南访问团
    西南访问团两分团先后返京
    【新华社二十一日讯】继中央西北、西南各民族访问团之后，中央人民政府现正积极筹备组织中南各民族访问团。该团将由中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会、财政经济委员会、政治法律委员会、民族事务委员会等单位抽调干部组成，并准备于四月中旬由北京出发。访问团的主要任务系向中南各民族人民宣传共同纲领中的民族政策，传达毛主席和中央人民政府对他们的关怀，并了解他们的情况和需要，以便及时反映给中央人民政府。访问团计划分四个队，分赴广西、广东、湖南和海南岛等地各民族人民聚居或杂居的地区进行访问。该团设有文工队、医疗队及电影放映队等，并携带中央人民政府赠送给各民族人民的大批礼物。
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中央人民政府西南各民族访问团第一、三分团的访问工作已告结束。该两分团的全体团员八十余人已先后返抵北京。该团第二分团留在云南省继续进行访问，已于三月八日抵达该省南部的宁洱（即普洱）县城。
    二分团从昆明赴宁洱途中，曾向开远、蒙自、石屏、元江、墨江等县各民族人民进行了亲切的慰问，并为他们放映电影，受到各民族人民的热烈欢迎。
