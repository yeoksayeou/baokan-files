Author:
Date: 1951-03-27
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    After land reform, the lives of farmers are getting better day by day. Wang Shiwen, a poor peasant in Houjintun Village, Acheng County, Songjiang Province, Northeast China, used to eat chaff and wear rags for many years. Now, he not only eats well but also bought a leather coat with one stone of grain. He said to the mutual aid team leader: "This is what Chairman Mao gave me!"

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    土地改革后的农民一天天走向丰衣足食的生活。东北松江省阿城县后进屯的贫农王士文，从前长年吃糠穿破衣，现在不但吃好了，而且用一石粮食买了一件皮大衣。他对互助小组长说：“这是毛主席给我的呵！”
