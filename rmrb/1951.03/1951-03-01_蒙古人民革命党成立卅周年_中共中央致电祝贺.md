Title: Thirtieth Anniversary of the Founding of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party; CCP Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram
Author: 
Date: 1951-03-01
Page: 1

　　Thirtieth Anniversary of the Founding of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party
    CCP Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram
    【Xinhua News Agency, February 28th】March 1st is the thirtieth Anniversary of the founding of The Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party. The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party sends a congratulatory telegram. The telegram is as follows:
    To the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party:
    The Chinese Communist Party warmly celebrates the 30th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, and wishes the Chinese and Mongolian people to be forever united in the cause of defending peace.
                  The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, February 28, 1951

### 蒙古人民革命党成立卅周年  中共中央致电祝贺

1951-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒙古人民革命党成立卅周年
    中共中央致电祝贺
    【新华社二十八日讯】三月一日为蒙古人民革命党成立三十周年纪念日，中国共产党中央委员会特致电祝贺。电文如下：蒙古人民革命党中央委员会：
    中国共产党热烈地庆祝蒙古人民革命党三十周年，并祝中蒙人民在维护和平的事业中永远团结一致。
                  中国共产党中央委员会  一九五一年二月二十八日
