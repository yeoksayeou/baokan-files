Title: Xiang-Gui-Qian Railway Laibin to Nanning Section Opens to Traffic
Author:
Date: 1951-03-20
Page: 2

　　Xiang-Gui-Qian Railway Laibin to Nanning Section Opens to Traffic
    The Laibin to Nanning section of the Xiang-Gui-Qian Railway was completed on February 23rd and officially began selling passenger and freight tickets on March 10th.
                (Xinhua News Agency)

### 湘桂黔铁路来宾至南宁段通车

1951-03-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　湘桂黔铁路来宾至南宁段通车
    湘桂黔铁路来宾至南宁段已经在二月二十三日修好，从三月十日起正式出售客、货车票。
                （新华社）
