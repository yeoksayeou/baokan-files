Title: The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression Held a Grand Meeting to Send Off the Chinese Red Cross International Medical and Epidemic Prevention Service Team to Korea; The team was divided into two detachments with a total of 192 members.
Author:
Date: 1951-03-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The Chinese Red Cross International Medical and Epidemic Prevention Service Team has been established and is about to go to Korea to work. The service team is divided into two detachments, with a total of 192 members. The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression held a grand send-off meeting on the 10th. Those present included representatives of various democratic parties, representatives of people from all walks of life in Beijing, the Korean Ambassador to China, Li Zhouyuan, and all members of the two detachments of the International Medical and Epidemic Prevention Service Team, totaling more than 1,000 people.

The send-off meeting was presided over by Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, who delivered the opening speech. He said: The Chinese people's resistance against America and aid to Korea is a sacred struggle to defend world peace. The formation of the Chinese Red Cross International Medical and Epidemic Prevention Service Team is a concrete manifestation of the Chinese people's profound internationalist spirit and a great practice of the revolutionary humanitarianism of Chinese medical workers. This great action will not only encourage the patriotic enthusiasm of the entire Chinese people in resisting America and aiding Korea, but will also greatly strengthen the courage and strength of the people of our brother country's struggle.

Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, announced the list of all members of the International Medical and Epidemic Prevention Service Team at the send-off meeting, and presented the team flag to the heads of each team. In her speech presenting the flag, she encouraged all members of the medical and epidemic prevention service team to wholeheartedly treat the war wounds of the Korean people, prevent epidemics and disasters, and vigorously rescue Korean refugees after arriving in Korea, in order to strengthen the strength of the Korean people and completely crush the American imperialist aggression plan.

Li Jishen, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, said in his speech: Due to the barbaric destruction of American imperialism in Korea, millions of Korean people are in a state of suffering and displacement. The timely departure of the Chinese Red Cross International Medical and Epidemic Prevention Service Team to work is exactly what the Korean people are eagerly looking forward to.

Korean Ambassador to China, Li Zhouyuan, welcomed the Chinese Red Cross International Medical and Epidemic Prevention Service Team to work in Korea on behalf of the Korean people. He said: With the wholehearted assistance of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the entire Chinese people, as well as the support of the Soviet Union and peace-loving people all over the world, the Korean people's just war against aggression will surely achieve final victory.

Also speaking at the meeting were Zhang Bojun, Secretary-General of the Central Political Bureau of the China Democratic League, Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Fu Lianzhang, Director of the Chinese Medical Association, Shen Yuanhui, Director of the Chinese Nurses Association, and Fang Shishan, Director of the Beijing Medical and Health Community's Joint Committee for Resisting America and Aiding Korea, and others.

The Korean Embassy, Beijing Union Medical College, Beijing University Medical School, and the Trade Union of the Red Cross Society of China presented flags, flowers, or speeches to the team.

At the end of the send-off meeting, Zhou Lixin, the captain of the first detachment of the medical and epidemic prevention service team, and Feng Yanchen, the captain of the second detachment, delivered reply speeches. They unanimously assured that all members of the team would overcome all difficulties, fear no sacrifice, and resolutely complete the work tasks after arriving in Korea.

On the evening of the 10th, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression and four other units jointly hosted a banquet for all members of the International Medical and Epidemic Prevention Service Team.

### 我保卫和平反美侵略委员会举行盛会  欢送中国红十字会国际医防服务队赴朝  该队分两个大队全体队员共一百九十二人

1951-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　我保卫和平反美侵略委员会举行盛会
    欢送中国红十字会国际医防服务队赴朝
    该队分两个大队全体队员共一百九十二人
    【新华社十日讯】中国红十字会国际医防服务队已经成立，即将赴朝工作。服务队分两个大队，全体队员共一百九十二人。中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会于十日举行盛大欢送会。到会的有各民主党派代表、北京市各界人民代表、朝鲜驻华大使李周渊及国际医防服务队两个大队全体队员等共一千多人。
    欢送会由中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会陈叔通副主席主持并致开会词。他说：中国人民的抗美援朝，是保卫世界和平的神圣的斗争。中国红十字会国际医防服务队的组成，是中国人民深厚的国际主义精神的具体表现，是中国医务工作者的革命的人道主义的伟大实践。这种伟大的行动，不但将鼓舞全中国人民抗美援朝的爱国热情，并且将大大加强我们兄弟国家的人民的斗争的勇气和力量。
    中国红十字会总会李德全会长在欢送会上宣布了国际医防服务队全体人员的名单，并以队旗授予各队负责人。她在授旗致词中勉励医防服务队全体人员，到朝鲜后要全心全意为朝鲜人民医治战争创伤，预防疫疠灾害，并大力救护朝鲜难民，来增强朝鲜人民的力量，彻底粉碎美国帝国主义的侵略计划。
    中国国民党革命委员会李济深主席致词说：由于美国帝国主义在朝鲜的野蛮破坏，千百万朝鲜人民正陷于苦痛流离的境地，中国红十字会国际医防服务队的及时出国工作，正是朝鲜人民所热烈盼望的。
    朝鲜驻华大使李周渊代表朝鲜人民欢迎中国红十字会国际医防服务队到朝鲜去工作。他说：在中国人民志愿军和全中国人民的竭诚援助以及苏联和全世界爱好和平人民的声援与支持下，朝鲜人民反对侵略的正义战争，一定会得到最后的胜利。
    在会上讲话的还有中国民主同盟中央政治局秘书长章伯钧，中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅，中华医学会理事长傅连暲，中华护士学会理事长沈元晖、北京医药卫生界抗美援朝联合委员会主任委员方石珊等。
    朝鲜大使馆及北京协和医学院、北京大学医学院、中国红十字会工会的代表等，分别向该队献旗、献花或献词。
    欢送会最后由该医防服务队第一大队大队长周立新、第二大队大队长冯雁忱致答词。他们一致保证，全队人员到朝鲜后将克服一切困难、不怕任何牺牲，坚决完成工作任务。
    十日晚，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会等五个单位，并联合欢宴国际医防服务队的全体人员。
