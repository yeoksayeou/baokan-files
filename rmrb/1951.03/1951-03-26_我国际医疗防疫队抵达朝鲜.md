Title: My International Medical and Epidemic Prevention Team Arrives in Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-03-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 24th: The Chinese Red Cross International Medical and Epidemic Prevention Service Team, volunteering to aid Korea, has arrived in Korea on the 19th of this month, carrying a large quantity of medicines and medical equipment. The team was warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life in a certain area of northern Korea.

### 我国际医疗防疫队抵达朝鲜

1951-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国际医疗防疫队抵达朝鲜
    【新华社平壤二十四日电】志愿援朝的中国红十字会国际医疗防疫服务队，携带大批药品和医疗器材，已于本月十九日抵达朝鲜。该队于朝鲜北部某地受到当地各界人民的热烈欢迎。
