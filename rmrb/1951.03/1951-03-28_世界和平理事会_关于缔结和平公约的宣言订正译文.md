Title: World Peace Council Declaration on the Conclusion of a Peace Pact, Revised Translation
Author:
Date: 1951-03-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 27th: The revised translation of the declaration on the conclusion of a peace pact, issued by the Berlin meeting of the World Peace Council, is as follows:

Title: "Declaration of the World Peace Council". Subtitle: "Regarding the Conclusion of a Peace Pact." The full text is as follows:

"In order to meet the hopes of millions upon millions of people throughout the world, regardless of their views on the causes of the danger of world war, and in order to consolidate peace and ensure international security, we call for the five major powers—the United States, the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, the United Kingdom, and France—to conclude a peace pact.

We believe that if the government of any one of the five major powers refuses to hold a meeting to conclude a peace pact, it proves that it has aggressive conspiracies.

We call on all peace-loving people to support this demand for the conclusion of a peace pact, which should welcome the participation of all countries.

We have signed this declaration and call on all good people and all organizations dedicated to peace to sign this declaration. (Signatures omitted below)." (All areas printing this declaration should use this text as the standard.)

### 世界和平理事会  关于缔结和平公约的宣言订正译文

1951-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界和平理事会
    关于缔结和平公约的宣言订正译文
    【新华社二十七日讯】世界和平理事会柏林会议发表的关于缔结和平公约的宣言，订正译文如下：
    题：“世界和平理事会宣言”。副题：“关于缔结和平公约”。全文如下：
    “为了符合全世界千千万万人们的希望，不论他们对于造成世界战争危险的原因抱什么见解，并且为了巩固和平与保障国际安全，我们要求五大国——美国、苏联、中华人民共和国、英国和法国——缔结和平公约。
    我们认为：五大国任何一国的政府，如果拒绝举行会议缔结和平公约，就证明它有侵略的阴谋。
    我们号召一切爱好和平的人民支持这个缔结和平公约的要求，这个公约应当欢迎所有的国家参加。
    我们在这个宣言上签了名，并号召所有善良的人们和所有致力于和平的团体在这个宣言上签名。（下略签名）”（各地印发此项宣言均以此文为准）。
