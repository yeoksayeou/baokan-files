Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Recording of Discussion on Punishing Counter-Revolutionaries
Author:
Date: 1951-03-31
Page: 1

　　Central People's Broadcasting Station
    Broadcasting Recording of Discussion on Punishing Counter-Revolutionaries
    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] The Central People's Broadcasting Station is scheduled to broadcast a live recording of the enlarged joint conference of representatives from all circles in Beijing and its districts discussing the issue of punishing counter-revolutionary criminals from 13:00 to 16:30 on April 1st. It will also be broadcast simultaneously by the People's Broadcasting Stations in Tianjin, Shanghai, and other cities.

### 中央人民广播电台  播送讨论惩治反革命的录音

1951-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民广播电台
    播送讨论惩治反革命的录音
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台定于四月一日十三时至十六时三十分播送北京市、区各界代表扩大联席会议讨论惩治反革命罪犯问题的实况录音，并将由天津、上海等市人民广播电台联播。
