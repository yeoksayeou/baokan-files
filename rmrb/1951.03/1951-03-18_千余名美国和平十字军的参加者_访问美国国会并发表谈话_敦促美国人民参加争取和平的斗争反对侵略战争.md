Title: More Than a Thousand Participants in the American Peace Crusade Visit the U.S. Congress and Speak Out, Urging the American People to Join the Struggle for Peace and Against Wars of Aggression
Author:
Date: 1951-03-18
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Washington, March 16th: More than fifteen hundred participants in the American Peace Crusade, wearing white dove emblems and carrying banners urging people to join the struggle for peace, visited Congress. They wanted to meet with members of Congress. Congress and the White House were heavily guarded. The visit took place under the close surveillance of these guards.

　　Representatives of the Peace Crusade participants released the original text of the Peace Crusade's "Purpose" to the press. The "Purpose" stated: The participants in the Crusade expressed the desire of millions of American mothers, fathers, young people, and old people to save the nation from the disaster of a Third World War. The purpose of the Crusade is to draw the American people's attention to the danger of being drawn into a war against the rest of humanity. "Our purpose is deeply patriotic - we want to save America from war. We believe that peace is the best defense our country can have, because only in peace can our people defend their living standards, maintain and improve their civil liberties, preserve life, maintain freedom, and continue to pursue happiness."

　　The Crusade participants said, we demand that our government end the Korean War, make peace with China, and withdraw American troops from Korea. We oppose the rearmament of Germany and all rearmament. We commend the Four-Power Conference and demand that an effective agenda be drawn up immediately to eliminate militarism in Germany and Japan, and to reduce all armaments and ban the atomic bomb. We demand an end to discriminatory policies and practices against Black people. We defend civil liberties, especially the right and moral obligation to express opinions advocating peace. We oppose universal military training, oppose extending the draft law, and oppose militarizing our youth. We demand limits on profits, an end to inflation, and a peacetime economy.

　　It is reported that two thousand two hundred Peace Crusade participants have arrived in Washington.

### 千余名美国和平十字军的参加者  访问美国国会并发表谈话  敦促美国人民参加争取和平的斗争反对侵略战争

1951-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　千余名美国和平十字军的参加者
    访问美国国会并发表谈话 
    敦促美国人民参加争取和平的斗争反对侵略战争
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社华盛顿十六日讯：一千五百多名美国和平十字军的参加者自佩白鸽徽号，手执敦促人们参加争取和平的斗争的旗帜，访问了国会。他们想在国会与国会议员们会谈。国会和白宫周围警卫森严。访问是在这些卫兵的严密监视下进行的。
    和平十字军参加者的代表向报界发表和平十字军的“宗旨”的原文。该“宗旨”宣称：十字军的参加者表达了美国千百万母亲们、父亲们、年青人、年老人希望拯救国家免于三次世界大战灾难的愿望。十字军的目的是促请美国人民注意卷入反对人类其余部分的战争的危险。“我们的目的是深切的爱国主义的——我们要拯救美国免于战争。我们相信，和平是我国所能有的最好的防御，因为唯有在和平中我国人民才能保卫他们的生活水平，维持并增进他们的公民自由，保全生命、保持自由和继续追求幸福。”
    十字军的参加者说，我们要求我们政府结束朝鲜战争，与中国讲和，并自朝鲜撤退美国军队。我们反对重新武装德国及一切的重新武装。我们赞扬四大国会议，并要求立即订出有效的议程以肃清德国和日本的军国主义，并裁减一切军备和禁止原子弹。我们要求结束歧视黑人的政策与行为。我们保卫公民自由，尤其是发表意见倡导和平的权利与道义上的义务。我们反对普遍军训，反对延长征兵法，反对使我们青年军事化。我们要求限制利润，要求结束通货膨胀，并要求有一个和平时期的经济。
    据报道：二千二百名和平十字军的参加者已抵达华盛顿。
