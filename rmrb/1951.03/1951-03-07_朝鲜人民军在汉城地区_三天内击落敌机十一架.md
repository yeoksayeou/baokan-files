Title: Korean People's Army Shoots Down Eleven Enemy Planes in the Seoul Area in Three Days
Author:
Date: 1951-03-07
Page: 1

　　Korean People's Army Shoots Down Eleven Enemy Planes in the Seoul Area in Three Days
    【Xinhua News Agency, 5th】 Pyongyang News: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army announced in a war report on March 4th that the Korean People's Army units shot down eleven enemy planes in the Seoul area from March 2nd to 4th.

### 朝鲜人民军在汉城地区  三天内击落敌机十一架

1951-03-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军在汉城地区
    三天内击落敌机十一架
    【新华社五日讯】平壤讯：朝鲜人民军总司令部三月四日发表战报称：朝鲜人民军部队自三月二日到四日，在汉城地区击落敌机十一架。
