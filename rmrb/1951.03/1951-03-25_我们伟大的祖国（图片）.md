    Our Great Motherland
    Our ancestors invented the compass very early on. This is a model of the "Sinan" mentioned in the ancient books of the Zhou and Han Dynasties. A spoon made of natural lodestone is placed on a copper disc engraved with twenty-four directions. It can rotate on the disc and remain stationary in the direction of the magnetic north.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我们的祖先，在很古的时候已经发明了指南针。这是周代和汉代古书上所谓“司南”的模型。在一个刻有二十四方位的铜盘上，放一个用天然磁石琢成的勺子。它能在盘上旋转，而静止于顺极向。
