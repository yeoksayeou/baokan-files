Title: British Colonial Authorities in North Borneo Illegally Search the "Chung Hua Public News"

Author:
Date: 1951-03-11
Page: 4

　　British Colonial Authorities in North Borneo
    Illegally Search the "Chung Hua Public News"
    [Xinhua News Agency, 9th] The British colonial authorities in North Borneo have recently intensified their brutal measures to persecute overseas Chinese. British police and special agents in Sarawak, North Borneo, illegally searched the "Chung Hua Public News," a Chinese newspaper in Kuching City, on January 16th, and seized a large number of books, newspapers, letters, and photographs. This illegal measure of the British imperialists to persecute overseas Chinese newspapers has aroused great anger among the local Chinese masses.

### 北婆罗洲英国殖民当局  非法搜查中华公报

1951-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　北婆罗洲英国殖民当局
    非法搜查中华公报
    【新华社九日讯】北婆罗洲的英国殖民当局最近加紧实行迫害华侨的狂暴措施。北婆罗洲萨拉瓦克的英国警察及特务于一月十六日非法搜查了古晋市华侨报纸《中华公报》，搜去了大批书报和信件、照片等。英帝国主义这种非法迫害华侨报纸的措施，激起了当地华侨群众的极大愤怒。
