Title: Shanghai People Hold Grand Meeting to Welcome Returning Volunteer Army Representatives
Author:
Date: 1951-03-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 8th: The representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army returning to China, Chai Chuanruo, Dong Lefu, Dou Shaoqi, and others, arrived in Shanghai from Jinan on the 7th. Two thousand representatives from various circles in East China and Shanghai welcomed them at the station. In the afternoon of the same day, a welcome rally was held by people from all walks of life in the city. At the meeting, Ma Yinchu, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the East China General Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Opposition to American Aggression, and Liu Changsheng, Chairman of the Shanghai Branch, delivered welcoming speeches on behalf of 140 million people in East China and 5 million citizens in Shanghai. They said: The people of East China and Shanghai are determined to further develop the movement to resist U.S. aggression, aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend our country, oppose the United States' rearmament of Japan, and strive to support the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army to win greater victories. Amidst cheers and thunderous applause, Chai Chuanruo reported on the heroic deeds of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army fighting on the Korean front. At the meeting, representatives of various democratic parties and people's organizations presented flowers and flags to the returning representatives of the Volunteer Army.

Chai Chuanruo and others left Beijing on the 1st of this month and arrived in Jinan on the 2nd, where they were warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life. During their three-day stay in Jinan, 80,000 people from various people's organizations, democratic parties, government agencies, troops, industrial and commercial circles, medical circles, and religious circles held welcome rallies and invited them to report on the heroic deeds of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army in resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea.

### 上海人民举行大会  欢迎志愿军归国代表

1951-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海人民举行大会
    欢迎志愿军归国代表
    【新华社上海八日电】我国人民志愿军归国代表柴川若、董乐辅、窦少毅等已于七日由济南到达上海。华东和上海各界代表二千余人到车站欢迎。当日下午该市各界人民举行了欢迎大会。会上，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会华东总分会筹备委员会主席马寅初及上海分会主席刘长胜，代表华东一亿四千万人民和上海市五百万市民相继致欢迎词。他们说：全华东与全上海市人民决进一步展开抗美援朝保家卫国运动，反对美国重新武装日本，努力支援我国人民志愿军与朝鲜人民军，争取更伟大的胜利。柴川若在全场欢呼与雷动的掌声中，报告了我国人民志愿军在朝鲜前线作战的英勇事迹。会上，各民主党派和人民团体的代表纷纷向志愿军归国代表献花献旗。
    柴川若等于本月一日离京，二日抵达济南，受到该地各界人民的热烈欢迎。在他们留济的三天内，该地各人民团体、各民主党派和机关、部队、工商界、医务界、宗教界等八万人，分别举行了欢迎大会，并邀请他们报告我国人民志愿军抗美援朝的英勇事迹。
