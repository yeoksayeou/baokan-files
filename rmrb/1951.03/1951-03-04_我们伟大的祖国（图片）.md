    Our Great Motherland
    This is the famous Dujiangyan Irrigation System in Guanxian, Sichuan. This great water conservancy project was completed in 300 B.C. under the guidance of two outstanding hydraulic engineers, Li Bing and his son. It eliminated the flood threat of the Min River, established a great irrigation system, benefiting 5 million mu of farmland in 14 counties in western Sichuan to this day, and facilitated navigation.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    这是四川灌县著名的都江堰。这个伟大的水利工程，是在公元前三百年，由两位杰出的水利工程师李冰父子指导完成的。它杜绝了岷江的水患，建立了伟大的灌溉系统，使四川西部十四县五百万亩农田，至今受益，并且便利了航运。
