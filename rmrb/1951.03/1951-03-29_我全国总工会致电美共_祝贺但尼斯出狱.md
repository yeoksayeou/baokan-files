Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Cables the Communist Party USA to Congratulate Dennis on His Release from Prison
Author:
Date: 1951-03-29
Page: 4

　　All-China Federation of Trade Unions Cables the Communist Party USA
    Congratulating Dennis on His Release from Prison
    [Xinhua News Agency, 28th] The All-China Federation of Trade Unions cabled the National Committee of the Communist Party USA, congratulating the General Secretary of the Communist Party USA, Dennis, on his release from prison. The cable reads as follows:
    To the National Committee of the Communist Party USA:
    We warmly celebrate Comrade Dennis's release from prison and wish him good health in his struggle for the great cause of the American working class!
                All-China Federation of Trade Unions　　　　　　　　March 27th

### 我全国总工会致电美共  祝贺但尼斯出狱

1951-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　我全国总工会致电美共
    祝贺但尼斯出狱
    【新华社二十八日讯】中华全国总工会致电美国共产党全国委员会，祝贺美共总书记但尼斯出狱。电文如下：美国共产党全国委员会：
    热烈庆贺但尼斯同志出狱，并祝他在为美国工人阶级的伟大事业的奋斗中，身体健康！
                中华全国总工会          三月二十七日
