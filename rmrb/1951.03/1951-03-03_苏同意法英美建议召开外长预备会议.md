Title: Soviet Union Agrees to French, British and American Proposal to Hold Foreign Ministers' Preparatory Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-03-03
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 2nd: TASS, Moscow, March 2nd: On February 19th, the French, British, and American governments responded to the Soviet government's February 5th note regarding the convening of a Four-Power Foreign Ministers' meeting, proposing a preliminary meeting of representatives of the four countries be held in Paris on March 5th to draft the agenda for the Four-Power Foreign Ministers' meeting. On March 1st, the Soviet government announced its agreement to the aforementioned proposal of the three governments.

### 苏同意法英美建议召开外长预备会议

1951-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏同意法英美建议召开外长预备会议
    【新华社二日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二日讯：二月十九日，法、英、美三国政府就苏联政府二月五日关于召开四国外长会议的照会提出答复，建议于三月五日在巴黎举行四国代表的初步会议，以拟订四国外长会议的议程。苏联政府于三月一日宣布同意三国政府的上述建议。
