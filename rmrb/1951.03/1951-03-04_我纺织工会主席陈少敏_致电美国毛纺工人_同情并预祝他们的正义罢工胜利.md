Title: Textile Workers' Union Chairman Chen Shaomin Sends a Message to American Wool Textile Workers, Expressing Sympathy and Wishing Them Victory in Their Just Strike
Author:
Date: 1951-03-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd: Chen Shaomin, Chairman of the National Committee of the Textile Workers' Union of China, sent a telegram to the American wool textile workers on February 24th, wishing them victory in their just strike. The telegram is as follows: To the American wool textile workers, c/o the New York Worker's Daily:

　　All Chinese textile workers express their heartfelt sympathy for your just strike and wish you victory.

                          Chen Shaomin, Chairman, National Committee of the Textile Workers' Union of China
                                      February 24, 1951

### 我纺织工会主席陈少敏  致电美国毛纺工人  同情并预祝他们的正义罢工胜利

1951-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　我纺织工会主席陈少敏
    致电美国毛纺工人
     同情并预祝他们的正义罢工胜利
    【新华社三日讯】中国纺织工会全国委员会主席陈少敏曾于二月二十四日致电美国毛纺工人，预祝他们正义罢工的胜利。电文如下：纽约工人日报社转美国毛纺工人：
    全中国纺织工人对你们的正义罢工，表示衷心同情，祝你们胜利。
                          中国纺织工会全国委员会  主席  陈少敏
                                      一九五一年二月二十四日
