Title: The 77th Meeting of the Government Administration Council Approved the Report on the Work of the General Administration of Customs, Adopted the Provisional Customs Law of the People's Republic of China, and Approved the Report on the Work of the General Administration of Publications
Author:
Date: 1951-03-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd—The 77th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held at 3:30 p.m. on the 23rd. The meeting listened to and approved the report made by Director Kong Yuan on the work summary of the General Administration of Customs of the Central People's Government over the past year and the main points of this year's work plan, adopted the Provisional Customs Law of the People's Republic of China, and listened to and approved the report made by Director Hu Yuzhi on the work summary of the General Administration of Publications of the Central People's Government in 1950 and the main points of the work plan for 1951. The meeting also passed several motions on appointments and removals and submitted motions on appointments and removals to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院第七十七次会议  批准关于海关总署工作的报告  通过中华人民共和国试行海关法  批准关于出版总署工作的报告

1951-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院第七十七次会议
    批准关于海关总署工作的报告
    通过中华人民共和国试行海关法
    批准关于出版总署工作的报告
    【新华社二十三日讯】中央人民政府政务院第七十七次政务会议于二十三日下午三时半举行。会议听取和批准了孔原署长所作的中央人民政府海关总署一年来工作总结和本年工作计划要点的报告，通过了中华人民共和国试行海关法，听取和批准了胡愈之署长所作的中央人民政府出版总署一九五零年工作总结和一九五一年工作计划要点的报告。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
