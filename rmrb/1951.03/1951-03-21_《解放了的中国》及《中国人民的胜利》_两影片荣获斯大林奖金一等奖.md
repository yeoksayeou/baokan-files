Title: "Liberated China" and "The Victory of the Chinese People" Films Win Stalin Prizes First Class

Author:

Date: 1951-03-21
Page: 3

　　"Liberated China" and "The Victory of the Chinese People"
    Two Films Win Stalin Prizes First Class
    The feature film "Liberated China," co-produced by Chinese and Soviet filmmakers, has won a Stalin Prize First Class of 100,000 rubles, and the documentary "The Victory of the Chinese People" has won a Stalin Prize First Class of 75,000 rubles. The Soviet film industry has sent a telegram to the Chinese film industry to congratulate them. The Chinese filmmakers who won the award for "Liberated China" include literary advisor Zhou Libo, music advisor and composer He Shide, assistant director Xu Xiaoping, and Su Heqing and narrator Zhou Feng. The winners for "The Victory of the Chinese People" include: literary advisor Liu Baiyu, assistant director Wu Benli, cinematographers Hao Yusheng, Li Bingzhong, Xu Lai, Li Hua and Ye Hui, among others.
                                          (Xinhua News Agency)

### 《解放了的中国》及《中国人民的胜利》   两影片荣获斯大林奖金一等奖

1951-03-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　《解放了的中国》及《中国人民的胜利》
    两影片荣获斯大林奖金一等奖
    中苏两国电影工作者联合摄制的艺术片《解放了的中国》已荣获斯大林奖金一等奖十万卢布，文献纪录片《中国人民的胜利》荣获斯大林奖金一等奖七万五千卢布。苏联电影界为此特电中国电影界致贺。《解放了的中国》一片得奖的中国电影工作者有文学顾问周立波，音乐顾问兼作曲何士德，助理导演徐肖平及苏河清、解说者周峰。《中国人民的胜利》的得奖者包括：文学顾问刘白羽，助理导演吴本立，摄影师郝玉生、李炳中、徐来、李华和叶惠等人。
                                          （新华社）
