Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Holds Meeting; Meeting to Discuss and Pass the Law for the Protection of Peace
Author:
Date: 1951-03-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 7th - Comprehensive report from TASS, Moscow: The Soviet Supreme Soviet held a meeting in the Kremlin on the 6th. The meeting of the Council of the Union was held at 2 p.m. When Molotov, Malenkov, Beria, Voroshilov, Andreyev, Kaganovich, Khrushchev, Shvernik, Suslov, and Ponomarin appeared on the government platform, the representatives and guests responded with prolonged applause. Members of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet and government ministers were also seated on the government platform. Guests attending the meeting included foreign diplomats and journalists from Soviet and foreign newspapers. Yasnov, Chairman of the Council of the Union, delivered the opening speech. After hearing and passing the report of the Credentials Committee on the examination of the qualifications of the four newly elected deputies, the meeting adopted the agenda for the current session.

The meeting of the Council of Nationalities was opened at 4 p.m. and presided over by its Chairman, Selehmitov. Bulganin, Mikoyan, Kosygin, Shvernik, and Shkiryatov were warmly welcomed by representatives and guests when they appeared on the government platform.

The Council of the Union and the Council of Nationalities adopted the same meeting agenda as follows: 1. Approval of the Soviet Union's national budget for 1951; 2. Formulation of the Law for the Protection of Peace, according to the demands made by the Second World Peace Congress to the parliaments of all countries; 3. Election of the Supreme Court of the Soviet Union; 4. Adoption of the decrees promulgated by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. The two houses have decided to hold a joint session on the 7th to hear reports on the Soviet national budget and on the formulation of the Law for the Protection of Peace, to discuss and pass the Law for the Protection of Peace. The two houses will hold separate meetings to hear other reports from the Budget Committee, discuss the report on the budget and pass the budget, elect the Supreme Court of the Soviet Union, and approve the decrees of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet.

### 苏联最高苏维埃举行会议  会议将讨论并通过保卫和平法案

1951-03-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联最高苏维埃举行会议
    会议将讨论并通过保卫和平法案
    【新华社七日讯】综合塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃于六日在克里姆林宫举行会议。联盟院会议在下午二时举行。当莫洛托夫、马林科夫、贝利亚、伏罗希洛夫、安德列耶夫、卡冈诺维奇、赫鲁舍切夫、什维尔尼克、苏斯洛夫及波诺马林科出现在政府席上时，代表及来宾报以经久不息的掌声。最高苏维埃主席团委员及政府部长们亦在政府席上就座。参加会议的来宾包括各国外交人员及苏联和外国报纸的记者。联盟院主席雅斯诺夫致开幕词。会议在听取并通过了资格审查委员会关于审查四位补选代表的资格的报告后，通过了本届会议的议程。
    民族院的会议于下午四时由民族院主席谢列赫密托夫主持开幕。布尔加宁、米高扬、柯西金、什维尔尼克及施基利亚托夫在政府席上出现时受到代表与来宾的热烈欢迎。
    联盟院与民族院通过相同的会议议程如下：一、批准苏联一九五一年国家预算；二、根据第二届世界和平大会向各国议会所提出的要求制订保卫和平法案；三、选举苏联最高法院；四、通过苏联最高苏维埃主席团颁布的法令。两院已决定在七日举行联席会议，听取关于苏联国家预算和关于制订保卫和平法案的报告，讨论并通过保卫和平法案。两院将分别举行会议听取预算委员会的其他报告，讨论关于预算的报告并通过预算案，选举苏联最高法院和批准最高苏维埃主席团的法令。
