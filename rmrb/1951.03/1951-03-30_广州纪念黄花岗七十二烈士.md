Title: Guangzhou Commemorates the Seventy-Two Martyrs of Huanghuagang
Author:
Date: 1951-03-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 29th: Today is the anniversary of the martyrdom of the Seventy-Two Martyrs of Huanghuagang. Li Zhangda, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, Zhu Guang, Vice Mayor of the Guangzhou Municipal People's Government, and more than 200 representatives from various democratic parties, government organs, organizations, and schools in Guangzhou went to the Huanghuagang Seventy-Two Martyrs Cemetery before 9:00 a.m. to hold a public memorial ceremony and present wreaths to pay their respects.

### 广州纪念黄花岗七十二烈士

1951-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　广州纪念黄花岗七十二烈士
    【新华社广州二十九日电】今日是黄花岗七十二烈士殉难纪念日。广东省人民政府副主席李章达、广州市人民政府副市长朱光及广州市各民主党派、各机关、团体、学校代表二百余人于上午九时前赴黄花岗七十二烈士墓场举行公祭，并献花圈致敬。
