Title: Second All-India Peace Conference Preparatory Committee Decides to Collect Signatures for Peace Pact
Author:
Date: 1951-03-08
Page: 4

　　Second All-India Peace Conference Preparatory Committee Decides to Collect Signatures for Peace Pact
    Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, 7th. TASS reported from Delhi on the 6th: The meeting of the Preparatory Committee of the Second All-India Peace Conference has concluded. The Preparatory Committee decided to actively promote the resolutions of the Second World Peace Congress and collect signatures in India for the World Peace Council's declaration on the conclusion of a peace pact.
    The Preparatory Committee issued a statement saying: The movement to defend peace is currently a genuine mass movement. People with various political views who have united out of a desire for peace have all participated in this movement.

### 二届全印和平大会筹委会决定  为和平公约征集签名

1951-03-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　二届全印和平大会筹委会决定
    为和平公约征集签名
    【新华社七日讯】据塔斯社德里六日讯：第二届全印和平大会筹备委员会会议已经闭幕。筹委会决定积极地宣传第二届世界和平大会的各项决议，并在印度为世界和平理事会关于缔结和平公约的宣言征集签名。
    筹委会发表声明说：目前保卫和平的运动是一种真正的民众运动。由于热望和平而联合起来的抱着种种不同政治见解的人士都已参加了这个运动。
