Title: Bulgaria, Poland, Romania, Austria, France, Italy, Sweden, and Indian Peace Fighters Launch Signature Campaign to Endorse Peace Pact Declaration
Author:
Date: 1951-03-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 29th — Peace fighters in Bulgaria, Poland, Austria, France, Italy, Sweden, and India are enthusiastically supporting the World Peace Council's declaration on the conclusion of a peace pact among the five major powers, and have launched signature campaigns. According to TASS: The National Committee of the Bulgarian Fatherland Front and the Central Committee of the General Trade Union have issued statements respectively, calling on the people of the whole country to unanimously sign the declaration. The Central Committee of the Bulgarian General Trade Union also sent telegrams to the governments of the five countries of the Soviet Union, China, the United States, Britain, and France, requesting the immediate conclusion of a peace pact. The working people of Wrocław, Poznań, and Olsztyn Provinces in Poland held peace rallies one after another, guaranteeing to respond to the declaration of the World Peace Council with creative labor.

After the peace fighters in Austria launched a signature campaign on the 15th, they have collected more than 300,000 signatures in ten days. Residents of cities such as Nice, Cannes, and Menton in the Alpes-Maritimes region of France have held peace rallies one after another, unanimously supporting the World Peace Council's declaration. Many priests in the Province of Alessandria, Italy, actively participated in the work of publicizing the declaration on the peace pact. Priests in cities such as Montoni, Villanova, and Rasteggio called on people from all walks of life to actively sign the declaration. In Stockholm, the capital of Sweden, and many cities and villages in Norrbotten Province, the working people gathered to discuss the declaration and resolutions of the World Peace Council, and passed resolutions demanding that the five major powers immediately conclude a peace pact.

The signature campaign in India has already begun. Peace fighters in cities such as Mumbai, Madras, Coimbatore, Daroba (in East Punjab Province), and Nogaon (in Assam Province) are widely publicizing the declaration and resolutions of the World Peace Council.

Xinhua News Agency, 28th — The Romanian people are enthusiastically supporting the World Peace Council's declaration on the conclusion of a peace pact and are about to launch a signature campaign. According to TASS, Bucharest, March 27th: The working people in various parts of Romania are holding peace rallies one after another. At a rally held in the Stalin District of Bucharest, the capital, Peace Committee member Surescu declared: "The Romanian people will sign this declaration because the conclusion of a peace pact by the five major powers will enable a peaceful solution to all major international problems and will guarantee that the people of all countries can build a happy and beautiful life." In Stalin Oblast, Iași Oblast, Cluj Oblast, and Timișoara Oblast, tens of thousands of workers, staff, and peasants participated in peace rallies, unanimously expressing their enthusiastic promotion of the signature campaign. Romanian scientists also unanimously passed a resolution at a meeting of the Academy of Sciences held in Bucharest, supporting the declaration of the World Peace Council.

### 保、波、罗、奥、法、意、瑞典、印度和平战士  展开签名运动拥护和平公约宣言

1951-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　保、波、罗、奥、法、意、瑞典、印度和平战士
    展开签名运动拥护和平公约宣言
    【新华社二十九日讯】保、波、奥、法、意、瑞典和印度的和平战士，热烈拥护世界和平理事会关于缔结五大国和平公约的宣言，并展开了签名运动。据塔斯社讯：保加利亚祖国阵线全国委员会及总工会中央委员会分别发表声明，号召全国人民一致在宣言上签名。保加利亚总工会中央委员会并致电苏、中、美、英、法五国政府，要求立即缔结和平公约。波兰的罗克劳、波斯南及奥尔兹丁省的劳动人民，纷纷举行和平集会，保证以创造性的劳动来响应世界和平理事会的宣言。
    奥地利的和平战士在十五日展开签名运动后，已在十天当中征集了三十余万个签名。法国阿尔卑斯海滨省的雷斯、戛纳及蒙同等城市的居民，相继举行了和平集会，一致支持世界和平理事会的宣言。意大利亚历山得里亚省的许多神父，积极参加了宣传和平公约宣言的工作。蒙托尼、维拉诺发及拉斯提基等市的神父们，号召各阶层人民踊跃签名。瑞典首都斯德哥尔摩以及诺尔波登省的许多城市与乡村中，劳动人民集会讨论世界和平理事会的宣言与决议，并通过决定要求五大国立即缔结和平公约。
    印度的签名运动已经开始，孟买、马德拉斯、康姆巴托、达罗巴（在东旁遮普省）和诺冈（在阿萨密省）等城市的和平战士，正在广泛宣传世界和平理事会的宣言与决议。
    【新华社二十八日讯】罗马尼亚人民热烈拥护世界和平理事会关于缔结和平公约的宣言，即将展开签名运动。据塔斯社布加勒斯特二十七日讯：罗马尼亚各地的劳动人民正纷纷举行和平集会。在首都布加勒斯特斯大林区举行的集会上，和平委员会委员苏列斯古宣称：“罗马尼亚人民将在这个宣言上签名，因为五大国缔结和平公约将使一切重大的国际问题获得和平解决，并将保证各国人民能够建设幸福而美好的生活。”在斯大林州、雅西州、克罗什州、的密索拉州，成千上万的工人、职员和农民参加和平集会，一致表示要热烈推动签名运动。罗马尼亚科学家们在布加勒斯特举行的科学院会议上，也一致通过决议，拥护世界和平理事会的宣言。
