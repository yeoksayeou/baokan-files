Title: Vietnamese Labor Party to Publish "People's Weekly"
Author:
Date: 1951-03-26
Page: 4

　　Vietnam Labor Party
    Will Publish "People's Weekly"
    [Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 25th] According to the Vietnam News Agency, the Vietnam Labor Party will publish "People's Weekly." This weekly will faithfully reflect the aspirations of the people and the actual situation of the liberation war, and report on the Party's policies and plans. The Vietnam Labor Party has called on the Vietnamese people to actively assist this publication, so that it may become the voice of the free and fighting Vietnamese people in both enemy-occupied and liberated areas.

### 越南劳动党   将出版《人民周刊》

1951-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南劳动党
    将出版《人民周刊》
    【新华社二十五日讯】据越南通讯社北越讯：越南劳动党将出版《人民周刊》。这个周刊将忠实地反映人民的愿望和解放战争的实际情况，并报道党的政策和计划。越南劳动党已号召越南人民积极帮助这个刊物，使它在敌占区和解放区都成为自由而战的越南人民的喉舌。
