Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Methods for Preventing and Treating Livestock Epidemics in the Next Two Days
Author:
Date: 1951-03-21
Page: 2

Central People's Broadcasting Station
    To Broadcast Methods for Preventing and Treating Livestock Epidemics in the Next Two Days
    In order to help farmers in parts of North China, Northwest China, Southwest China, and Central South China quickly eliminate livestock epidemics and smoothly carry out spring plowing, the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government and the Central People's Broadcasting Station are jointly organizing a special program for farmers in the above-mentioned areas. The broadcast content will describe the symptoms and prevention methods for Rinderpest, Anthrax, and Foot-and-Mouth Disease. The broadcast time is from 13:00 to 14:00 on the 21st, and it will be repeated twice on the same day from 19:00 to 20:00 and on the 22nd from 13:00 to 14:00.

### 中央人民广播电台  今明两天播讲防治畜疫方法

1951-03-21
第2版()
专栏：

　　中央人民广播电台
    今明两天播讲防治畜疫方法
    中央人民政府农业部与中央人民广播电台为帮助华北、西北、西南、中南等部分地区的农民迅速扑灭畜疫，以便顺利进行春耕，特共同举办专门对上述地区农民广播的节目。广播内容为讲述牛瘟、炭疽、口蹄疫的症状和防治方法。播送时间为二十一日十三时至十四时，并将于同日晚十九时至二十时和二十二日的下午十三时至十四时重复播送两次。
