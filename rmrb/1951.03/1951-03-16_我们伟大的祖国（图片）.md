Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-03-16
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    During the land reform in the new districts, the peasants, according to the decision of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government on the division of rural class components, conducted evaluations of the class components of each class. The peasants of Matang Township, Yueyang, Hunan, adopted the method of self-reporting and public discussion, and passed and announced the list of class components through the village meeting. This is the place where the list is posted, and villagers come to check it at any time.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在新区土地改革中，农民们都按照中央人民政府政务院关于划分农村阶级成份的决定，进行对各阶级成份的评议。湖南岳阳麻塘乡农民，采取自报公议的办法，经过村民大会通过并公布阶级成份榜。这是贴榜的地方，村民们随时都来查看。
