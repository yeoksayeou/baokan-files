Title: The Philippine Quirino Puppet Government Massively Arrests Patriots
Author:
Date: 1951-03-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 10th: Manila News: The Philippine Quirino puppet government has recently been massively arresting patriots again. In the week or so up to February 16th, 500 people were arrested in Manila and the provinces of Luzon.

The chairman of the Philippine Labor Organization Congress and Manila City Councilor, Amado Hernandez, was also arrested in Manila on January 26th and 27th during an arrest. More than 30 people were arrested at the same time, most of whom were union leaders and newspaper reporters.

### 菲律宾季里诺傀儡政府  大肆逮捕爱国分子

1951-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　菲律宾季里诺傀儡政府
    大肆逮捕爱国分子
    【新华社十日讯】马尼拉消息：菲律宾季里诺傀儡政府最近又大肆逮捕爱国分子。至二月十六日为止的一个多星期中，在马尼拉和吕宋各省即有五百人被捕。
    菲律宾著名的劳工组织大会主席兼马尼拉市议员阿玛杜·赫南德兹，也在一月二十六日和二十七日的一次逮捕中在马尼拉被捕。同时被捕的有三十余人，其中大部分是工会领袖和各报记者。
