Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Voices Support for Striking Spanish Workers
Author:
Date: 1951-03-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 18th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the Spanish Democratic Trade Union on the 17th, expressing warm support for the 300,000 striking workers in Barcelona, Spain. The telegram reads as follows: To Victorio of the Italian General Confederation of Labor, forwarded to the Spanish Democratic Trade Union:

　　On behalf of the Chinese working class, please accept our heartfelt respect and warm support to the 300,000 striking worker brothers in Barcelona, and we wish you great victory in your heroic struggle against the Franco fascist rule.

　　　　All-China Federation of Trade Unions
　　　　　　March 17th

### 中华全国总工会声援西班牙罢工工人

1951-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　中华全国总工会声援西班牙罢工工人
    【新华社十八日讯】中华全国总工会于十七日致电西班牙民主工会，对西班牙巴塞罗那城三十万罢工工人表示热烈的声援。电文如下：意大利总工会维多里奥转西班牙民主工会
    谨代表中国工人阶级向巴塞罗那城三十万罢工工人兄弟们致衷心的敬意和热烈的声援，并祝你们在反对佛朗哥法西斯统治的英勇斗争中取得伟大的胜利。
          中华全国总工会
              三月十七日
