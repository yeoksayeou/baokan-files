Our Great Motherland
    Land reform in many new areas has entered the stage of land distribution. This is the situation of the land distribution committee composed of hired peasants, poor peasants, and middle peasant representatives in Chun'ao Village, Yueyang County, Hunan Province, working. They are carefully formulating a reasonable distribution plan.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    许多新区的土地改革，已经进入分配土地的阶段。这是湖南岳阳县春坳村的雇农、贫农、中农代表组成的分田委员会工作的情形。他们正在仔细地拟定合理分配的方案。
