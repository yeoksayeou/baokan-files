Title: Defend Peace, Stop Wars of Aggression! (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-03-21
Page: 5

News Bureau of the General Administration of News, All-China Democratic Women's Federation contributions

　　Defend Peace, Stop Wars of Aggression!
    The World Peace Council and the International Democratic Women's Federation Council hold meetings in Berlin.
    World Peace Council Executive Bureau Vice Chairman Nenni delivered a report on the implementation of the program of the Second World Peace Congress at the afternoon session on February 21st of the World Peace Council's First Session, and proposed five tasks for this Council meeting to consolidate peace.
    The First Session of the World Peace Council opened in Berlin on the morning of February 21, 1951. The opening meeting was presided over by World Peace Council Executive Bureau Vice Chairman Nenni. The presidium consisted of Executive Bureau Vice Chairmen Fadeyev, Guo Moruo, d'Arboussier and Executive Committee members Ehrenburg, Xiao San, Faggi, and Laffitte. The conference lasted six days, and victoriously concluded on the 26th after passing eight important resolutions.
    Guo Moruo, head of the Chinese delegation to the First Session of the World Peace Council and Vice Chairman of the World Peace Council, delivered a speech at the afternoon session on February 22nd, agreeing with and supporting the proposal to conclude a Five-Power Peace Pact.
    All the representatives attending the First Session of the World Peace Council unanimously raised their hands to pass the declaration on the conclusion of a peace pact.
    Soviet writer Ehrenburg spoke at the First Session of the World Peace Council.
    World Peace Council Executive Bureau Vice Chairman and African representative d'Arboussier described at the meeting the implementation of the Second World Peace Congress's resolution on colonies and dependencies.
    The Fourth Council Meeting of the International Democratic Women's Federation was held in the People's Parliament Building of the German Democratic Republic in Berlin from January 31st to February 5th, 1951. One hundred and three council members and representatives from thirty-four countries attended.
    German worker representatives pay their respects to the World Peace Council.
    At the Council Meeting of the International Democratic Women's Federation, International Democratic Women's Federation Secretary-General Madame Vaillant-Couturier delivered a report calling for an end to the wars of aggression in Korea and Vietnam.
    The Chinese delegation in Prague attending the welcome reception held by Mrs. Hertinova, Chairman of the Czech Peace Congress and Chairman of the Czech Democratic Women's Federation.
    When the Council Meeting of the International Democratic Women's Federation was held in Berlin, many propaganda posters showing women all over the world struggling to defend peace, and protect the lives and happiness of their children, stood on the streets of Berlin. Several children injured in World War II were wandering beside the posters, which clearly showed us: the great significance of women around the world expanding the movement to protect children.
    President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic was warmly welcomed by the representatives when he attended the Council Meeting of the International Democratic Women's Federation.

### 保卫和平，制止侵略战争！（图片）

1951-03-21
第5版()
专栏：

　　保卫和平，制止侵略战争！
    世界和平理事会国际民主妇联理事会在柏林举行会议
    新闻总署新闻摄影局、中华全国民主妇女联合会来稿
    世界和平理事会执行局副主席南尼在世界和平理事会第一届会议二月二十一日下午会议上发表了关于第二届世界和平大会纲领执行情况的报告，并提出本届理事会议巩固和平的五项任务。
    世界和平理事会第一届会议于一九五一年二月二十一日上午在柏林开幕。开幕会议由世界和平理事会执行局副主席南尼主持，主席团由执行局副主席法捷耶夫、郭沫若、达波赛和执行委员爱伦堡、萧三、法奇、拉斐德等人组成。大会共进行了六天，于二十六日通过了八项重要决议后，宣告胜利闭幕。
    我国出席世界和平理事会第一届会议代表团团长、世界和平理事会副主席郭沫若，在该会二月二十二日下午的会议上发表演说，同意并支持订立五大国和平公约的建议。
    参加世界和平理事会第一届会议的全体代表一致举手通过关于缔结和平公约的宣言。
    苏联作家爱伦堡在世界和平理事会第一届会议上发言。
    世界和平理事会执行局副主席、非洲代表达波赛在会上叙述了第二届世界和平大会关于殖民地和附属国的决议的执行情形。
    国际民主妇女联合会第四届理事会会议于一九五一年一月三十一日至二月五日在柏林德意志民主共和国人民议会大厦举行。出席三十四个国家的理事和代表一○三人。
    德国工人代表向世界和平理事会致敬。
    在国际民主妇女联合会理事会上，国际民主妇联总书记古久里夫人发表号召制止侵略朝鲜和越南的战争的报告。
    中国代表团在布拉格参加捷克和平大会主席、捷克民主妇女联合会主席贺地诺娃夫人举行的欢迎会。
    国际民主妇女联合会理事会在柏林召开时，柏林各街头矗立了许多全世界妇女为保卫和平、保卫孩子们的生命与幸福而斗争的宣传画，几个在第二次世界大战中受伤的儿童正在画旁徘徊着，这就很显明地昭示我们：全世界妇女扩大保卫儿童运动的重大意义。
    德意志民主共和国总统皮克莅临国际民主妇女联合会理事会会议时，受到代表们的热烈欢迎。
