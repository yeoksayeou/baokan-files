Title: GDR Deputy Prime Minister Heinrich Rau Reports on This Year's Economic Plan
Author:
Date: 1951-03-20
Page: 4
　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 16th: TASS reports that at the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, Deputy Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic and Chairman of the State Planning Commission, Heinrich Rau, reported on the draft of the 1951 national economic plan, pointing out that the significance of the 1951 national economic plan is more significant in many respects than the national economic plans of previous years. After the implementation of this plan, industrial output will increase by 28 percent compared to 1936, and the average labor productivity will increase by 14.7 percent compared to last year. Agricultural production will exceed pre-war levels. The task of the 1951 national economic plan is to further restore the peacetime economy. The plan stipulates the expansion of the production of consumer goods. In 1951, the volume of trade in food and general consumer manufactured goods will increase by 21.8 percent compared to last year. Heinrich Rau said: The 1951 national economic plan will further consolidate the anti-fascist democratic system of the German Democratic Republic and will greatly help the German people in their struggle for a unified, democratic, peace-loving, free, and independent Germany.

### 德意志民主共和国副总理亨利·劳报告今年经济计划

1951-03-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　德意志民主共和国副总理亨利·劳报告今年经济计划
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社柏林十六日讯：德意志民主共和国副总理、国家计划委员会主席亨利·劳在德意志民主共和国人民议会上报告一九五一年国民经济计划方案时指出：一九五一年国民经济计划的意义，在许多方面较前几年的国民经济计划更为重大。实行这一计划后，工业产量将较一九三六年增加百分之二十八，平均劳动生产率将较去年增加百分之十四点七。农业生产将超过战前水平。一九五一年国民经济计划的任务，是进一步恢复平时经济。计划规定扩充消费物品的生产。在一九五一年，食品和一般消费制造品的贸易数量将较去年增加百分之二十一点八。亨利·劳说：一九五一年的国民经济计划将要进一步巩固德意志民主共和国的反法西斯主义的民主制度，将要大大帮助德国人民为统一的、民主的、爱好和平的、自由的和独立的德国所进行的斗争。
