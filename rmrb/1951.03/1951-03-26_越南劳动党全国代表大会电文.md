Title: Telegrams from the National Congress of the Vietnam Workers' Party
Author:
Date: 1951-03-26
Page: 4

　　Telegrams from the National Congress of the Vietnam Workers' Party
    Salute to Stalin and the CPSU
    [Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 25th] According to Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, March 21st: The National Congress held to establish the Vietnam Workers' Party sent a telegram to Marshal Stalin and the Bolshevik Party of the Soviet Union to express their respect. The telegram is as follows:
    To Comrade Stalin and the Bolshevik Party of the Soviet Union: The National Congress held on February 11, 1951, to establish the Vietnam Workers' Party, respectfully extends its highest regards to the Bolshevik Party of the Soviet Union and expresses its confidence in the Bolshevik Party and Comrade Stalin, the great leader of the workers and people of the world.
    The Vietnam Workers' Party vows to advance the Vietnamese people's liberation struggle, to eliminate the French colonialists, defeat the American interventionists, and achieve complete independence; and vows to unite with the heroic people of the Soviet Union, the people of the fraternal democratic countries, and the people of other countries around the world to oppose imperialist war-mongers and safeguard world peace and democracy.
    Salute to Chairman Mao and the CCP
    [Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 25th] According to Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, March 21st: The National Congress held to establish the Vietnam Workers' Party sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong and the Chinese Communist Party to express their respect. The telegram is as follows:
    The National Congress held on February 11, 1951, to establish the Vietnam Workers' Party, respectfully extends brotherly greetings to Chairman Mao Zedong and the Chinese Communist Party.
    The Vietnam Workers' Party vows to learn from the heroic example of the Chinese Communist Party, and to learn from Mao Zedong Thought, which leads the Chinese and Asian people towards independence and democracy.
    The Vietnam Workers' Party vows to lead the Vietnamese people in their struggle to defeat the French imperialists and American interventionists, and to achieve complete independence; to unite closely with the people of China and the world, to crush the imperialists' war conspiracies, and to safeguard world peace and democracy.
    Salute to the Korean Workers' Party
    [Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 25th] According to Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, March 21st: The National Congress held to establish the Vietnam Workers' Party sent a telegram to the Korean Workers' Party, extending greetings to the Korean Workers' Party, the Korean government, the people, the People's Army, and the Chinese People's Volunteers. The telegram stated: "The Vietnam Workers' Party is determined to lead the Vietnamese people's war of liberation to complete victory, to eliminate the French imperialists, and to defeat the American interventionists who are intensifying their intervention in our country and are invading your independence and freedom. The Vietnamese people, who are currently fighting for liberation, are very excited about the victory of the Korean people. We believe that the victory of the Korean people is our own victory."
    Salute to the World Peace Council and the French Communist Party
    [Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 25th] According to Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, March 21st: The National Congress held to establish the Vietnam Workers' Party sent a telegram to the World Peace Council to express their respect. The telegram stated: We are determined to unite with the people of the world to safeguard peace. We believe that the forces of peace united around the Council will be victorious.
    The Congress also sent a telegram to the French Communist Party, French democratic groups, and the French people to express their respect. The telegram stated: The Vietnam Workers' Party warmly welcomes your struggle against the war of aggression and for the restoration of peace in Vietnam. We are determined to carry out mutually beneficial cooperation with the French people and to contribute to the maintenance of world peace and democracy.

### 越南劳动党全国代表大会电文

1951-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南劳动党全国代表大会电文
    电斯大林和联共致敬
    【新华社二十五日讯】据越南通讯社北越二十一日讯：为成立越南劳动党而举行的全国代表大会致电斯大林大元帅与苏联布尔什维克党表示敬意。电文如下：
    斯大林同志与苏联布尔什维克党：一九五一年二月十一日为成立越南劳动党而举行的全国代表大会，谨向苏联布尔什维克党致崇高的敬意，并向布尔什维克党与全世界的工人与人民的伟大领袖斯大林同志表示信赖。
    越南劳动党誓必推进越南人民的解放斗争，以消灭法国殖民者，击败美国干涉分子，并获得完全的独立；誓必与苏联的英勇人民、各兄弟民主国家的人民以及全世界的其他国家的人民团结起来，反对帝国主义战争贩子，维护世界和平与民主。
    电毛主席和中共致敬
    【新华社二十五日讯】据越南通讯社北越二十一日讯：为成立越南劳动党而举行的全国代表大会致电毛泽东主席与中国共产党表示敬意。电文如下：
    一九五一年二月十一日为成立越南劳动党而举行的全国代表大会谨向毛泽东主席与中国共产党致兄弟的敬意。
    越南劳动党誓必学习中国共产党的英雄榜样，学习领导中国及亚洲人民走向独立与民主的毛泽东思想。
    越南劳动党誓必领导越南人民进行斗争，击溃法帝国主义者与美国干涉分子，并获得完全的独立；与中国及世界的人民密切团结，粉碎帝国主义者的战争阴谋，维护世界和平与民主。
    电朝鲜劳动党致敬
    【新华社二十五日讯】据越南通讯社北越二十一日讯：为成立越南劳动党而举行的全国代表大会曾致电朝鲜劳动党，向朝鲜劳动党、朝鲜政府、人民与人民军及中国人民志愿军致敬。电文称：“越南劳动党决心领导越南人民的解放战争取得完全的胜利，决心消灭法帝国主义者，击败正加紧干涉我国的并正在侵犯你们的独立与自由的美国干涉者。目前正为解放而战斗的越南人民，对于朝鲜人民的胜利非常兴奋。我们认为：朝鲜人民的胜利，就是我们自己的胜利。”
    电和平理事会及法共致敬
    【新华社二十五日讯】据越南通讯社北越二十一日讯：为成立越南劳动党而举行的全国代表大会致电世界和平理事会表示敬意。电文称：我们誓必与全世界的人民联合起来维护和平。我们相信团结在理事会周围的和平力量是会胜利的。
    大会并致电法国共产党、法国各民主团体与法国人民表示敬意。电文称：越南劳动党热烈欢呼你们反对侵略战争与争取在越南重建和平的斗争。我们决心与法国人民实行互利的合作并对维护世界和平与民主有所贡献。
