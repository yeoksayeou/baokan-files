Title: The Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Continues to Discuss the National Budget

Author:
Date: 1951-03-12
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, March 11th: TASS reports: The Nationalities Chamber and the Union Chamber of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union held meetings on the 9th to continue discussing the report by Soviet Finance Minister Zverev on the Soviet Union's 1951 national budget. The meeting of the Nationalities Chamber was presided over by the Chairman of the Nationalities Chamber, Shekhmitov. Representatives from the Karelo-Finnish, Belorussian, Moldavian, Lithuanian, Tajik, Kyrgyz, Turkmen, and Georgian Republics spoke during the discussion. At the meeting held in the Union Chamber, representatives from the Georgian, Turkmen, Latvian, Kyrgyz Republics, and the Tatar and Bashkir Autonomous Republics delivered speeches. They reported on the great achievements in the economic and cultural construction of their respective republics and unanimously agreed to adopt the draft national budget. Soviet Minister of Construction and Road-Building Machinery Manufacturing Fomin, Minister of Fish Industry Rusakov, Minister of Food Industry Pavlov, and Minister of Agricultural Machinery Manufacturing Golemykin reported to the Nationalities Chamber and the Union Chamber, respectively, on the achievements made by their respective ministries during the first five-year plan after the war. The two chambers will continue to meet on the 10th.

### 苏联最高苏维埃继续讨论国家预算案

1951-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联最高苏维埃继续讨论国家预算案
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社消息：苏联最高苏维埃民族院与联盟院于九日分别举行会议，继续讨论苏联财政部长兹维列夫关于苏联一九五一年国家预算的报告。民族院会议由民族院主席谢赫密托夫主持。在讨论中发言的有卡累利阿——芬兰、白俄罗斯、摩尔达维亚、立陶宛、塔吉克、吉尔吉斯、土库曼、乔治亚等共和国的代表。在联盟院举行的会议上，乔治亚、土库曼、拉脱维亚、吉尔吉斯共和国及鞑靼、巴什基尔等自治共和国的代表都发表了演说。他们分别报告了各共和国经济与文化建设的巨大成就，并一致赞成通过国家预算草案。苏联建造及筑路机器制造部部长佛明、鱼品工业部部长鲁萨可夫和食品工业部部长巴甫洛夫、农业机器制造部部长高列米金分别在民族院与联盟院报告了各该部在战后第一个五年计划期间所获得的成就。两院将分别于十日继续开会。
