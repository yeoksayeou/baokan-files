Title: Zhengzhou Railway Workers' Second Volunteer Transport Team Heads to Korea

Author:

Date: 1951-03-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The second group of Zhengzhou Railway Workers' Volunteer Transportation Team to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea departed from Zhengzhou on the 15th to go to Korea. Before their departure, the railway trade union of the district and the staff of the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau held a grand farewell meeting. The first group of volunteer transport teams to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea from the district left for Korea on the 7th of last month.

### 郑州区铁路工人  第二批志愿运输队赴朝

1951-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　郑州区铁路工人
    第二批志愿运输队赴朝
    【新华社十七日讯】郑州区铁路工人第二批抗美援朝志愿运输队已于十五日由郑州出发转赴朝鲜。临行时，该区铁路工会及郑州铁路局职工曾举行盛大欢送会。该区第一批抗美援朝志愿运输队是上月七日赴朝的。
