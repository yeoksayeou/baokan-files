Title: Strive for a Peaceful Resolution of the German Question This Year; German Peace Committee Welcomes Delegates from Various Countries; Guo Moruo Delivers Speech Calling for Close Cooperation Between the Chinese and German People; Learn from the Experience of the Soviet People and Resolutely Fight Against the Enemies of Peace
Author:
Date: 1951-03-01
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 27th – The German Peace Committee held a grand gathering here at the Friedrichstadt-Palast on the morning of the 25th to welcome delegates from various countries attending the World Peace Council. More than 3,000 people attended, including representatives from the World Peace Council and representatives from all walks of life in Germany. Behind the chairman's platform, a huge peace dove was decorated on a deep blue curtain, with a golden slogan attached: "Strive for a peaceful resolution of the German question in 1951!" After Professor Friedrich, Chairman of the German Peace Committee, delivered a welcoming speech, Nenni (Italy), Vice-Chairman of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council, Guo Moruo (China), Executive Bureau Member Fajr (France), Council Member Korneichuk (Soviet Union), and representatives from the United Kingdom and West Germany delivered speeches successively. Guo Moruo, head of the Chinese delegation, paid tribute on behalf of the Chinese people to the people of Germany, the birthplace of Marx, Engels, and other great thinkers and scientists. He said: The Chinese and German people are peace-loving, labor-loving, and creative nations. Today, we have become fraternal countries, advancing side by side on the road of people's democracy. Guo Moruo called on the Chinese and German people to cooperate closely, learn from the construction experience of the great Soviet people, and unswervingly fight against the enemies of peace. Guo Moruo finally shouted: "Glory to the fraternal friendship between the Chinese and German people!" "Long live a unified and democratic Germany!" "Long live peace!" All attendees stood up and responded with thunderous applause and cheers.

### 争取今年和平解决德国问题  德和平委员会欢迎各国代表  郭沫若致词号召中德两国人民紧密合作  学习苏联人民经验对和平敌人坚决斗争
新华社
1951-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　争取今年和平解决德国问题
    德和平委员会欢迎各国代表
    郭沫若致词号召中德两国人民紧密合作
    学习苏联人民经验对和平敌人坚决斗争
    【新华社柏林二十七日电】德国和平委员会于二十五日晨在此间的佛莱德里赫宫举行了盛大的集会，欢迎出席世界和平理事会的各国代表。到会者有世界和平理事会代表及德国各界代表三千余人。在主席台后面的深蓝色布幕上装饰着巨大的和平鸽，并附有一条金色的标语：“争取在一九五一年内以和平方式解决德国问题！”德国和平委员会主席腓特烈教授致欢迎辞后，世界和平理事会执行局副主席南尼（意）、郭沫若（中），执行局委员法奇（法），理事会理事考涅楚克（苏）和英国及德国西部的代表相继发表演说。中国代表团团长郭沫若代表中国人民向曾经诞生过马克思、恩格斯和其他伟大思想家与科学家的德国的人民致敬。他说：中国人民和德国人民是爱好和平、爱好劳动和创造的民族。今天我们已成为兄弟之邦，并肩在人民民主的道路上迈进。郭沫若号召中德两国人民紧密合作，学习伟大的苏联人民的建设经验，并坚定不移地对和平敌人进行斗争。郭沫若最后高呼：“光荣归于中德人民兄弟般的友谊！”“统一、民主的德国万岁！”“和平万岁！”与会者全体起立，报以暴风雨般的掌声和欢呼。
