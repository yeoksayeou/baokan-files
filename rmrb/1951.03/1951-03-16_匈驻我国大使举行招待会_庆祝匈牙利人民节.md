Title: Hungarian Ambassador to China Holds Reception to Celebrate Hungarian People's Day
Author:
Date: 1951-03-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 15th: The Ambassador of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, Mr. Sándor Szirmai, held a reception at the Hungarian Embassy in Beijing at 5:00 PM today to celebrate the People's Day of the Hungarian People's Republic, which falls on March 15th. More than a hundred guests attended, including Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China Wu Xiuquan, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Minister of Education Ma Xulun, Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, and Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Zhu Xuefan.

The diplomatic envoys of various countries to China who were invited to attend included: Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Czechoslovak Ambassador Weisskopf, Korean Ambassador Lee Joo-yeon, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhaajav, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy Depairacinski, and Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic Grutner. The reception proceeded in a friendly and pleasant atmosphere, and a Hungarian newsreel was shown afterwards.

Note: Hungarian People's Day commemorates the festival of the Hungarian people's revolutionary movement for independence and democracy against the autocratic rule of the Austro-Hungarian Empire in 1848.

### 匈驻我国大使举行招待会  庆祝匈牙利人民节

1951-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　匈驻我国大使举行招待会
    庆祝匈牙利人民节
    【新华社十五日讯】三月十五日为匈牙利人民共和国人民节。匈牙利人民共和国驻我国大使夏法朗柯于今日下午五时在北京匈牙利大使馆举行招待会。到会来宾百余人，内有中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部伍修权副部长、文化部沈雁冰部长、教育部马叙伦部长、北京市人民政府彭真市长及中华全国总工会朱学范副主席等。
    应邀出席的各国驻华使节有：苏联大使罗申、捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫、朝鲜大使李周渊、罗马尼亚大使鲁登科、蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗、保加利亚大使彼得科夫、波兰大使馆临时代办戴裴拉辛斯基、德意志民主共和国外交使团临时代办葛鲁特纳等人。招待会在友好愉快气氛中进行，会后并放映了匈牙利新闻记录影片。
    按：匈牙利人民节是纪念一八四八年匈牙利人民为反抗奥匈帝国专制统治争取独立民主革命运动的节日。
