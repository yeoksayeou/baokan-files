Date: 1951-03-01

　　Erratum
    Regarding the article "Ancient Chinese Scientific Creations" that was reprinted in your newspaper on the 1st of last month from "China Youth" issue 57, the following errors occurred. Please correct them: 1. In the ninth line of the seventh paragraph, "Jia Lu (Jin Taiding...)" should be "Jia Lu (Yuan Taiding...)"; 2. In the eighth line of the eighth paragraph, "east of Xing'an County, Hunan" should be "east of Xing'an County, Guangxi"; 3. In the twelfth line of the tenth paragraph, "binary quadratic equation" should be "univariate quadratic equation"; 4. In the fourteenth line of the tenth paragraph, "right triangle with sides 3, 4, and 5" and the fifteenth line "geometrically proved the square of the short side plus the square of the long side" should both have "equilateral triangle" changed to "right triangle"; 5. In the first line of the fourteenth paragraph, "we probably invented gunpowder in the 12th century" should be "we probably invented gunpowder in the 10th century."
                                                      Qian Weichang

### 来函更正
钱伟长
1951-03-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　来函更正
    贵报上月一日转载《中国青年》第五十七期拙作“中国古代的科学创造”一文中，有下列错误，请予更正：一、第七段第九行“贾鲁（金泰定……）”，应为“贾鲁（元泰定……）”；二、第八段第八行“在湖南兴安县东面”，应为“在广西兴安县东面”；三、第十段第十二行“二元二次方程式”，应为“一元二次方程式”；四、第十段第十四行“正三角形的勾三股四弦五”及第十五行“用几何证明了正三角形的勾方加股方”两句中的“正三角形”，都应为“直角三角形”；五、第十四段第一行“我们大概在十二世纪内发明了火药”，应为“我们大概在十世纪内发明了火药”。
                                                      钱伟长
