Title: Government Administration Council Holds 78th Meeting; Approves Ministry of Finance's Report on Last Year's Work Summary and This Year's Work Guidelines and Tasks
Author:
Date: 1951-03-31
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, March 30th: The 78th Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 30th. The meeting heard and approved the report made by Vice Minister Rong Zihe of the Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government on the summary of work in 1950 and the guidelines and tasks for work in 1951. The meeting also passed several appointment and removal cases.

### 政务院举行第七十八次会议  批准财政部去年工作总结及今年工作的方针和任务的报告

1951-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行第七十八次会议
    批准财政部去年工作总结及今年工作的方针和任务的报告
    【新华社三十日讯】中央人民政府政务院第七十八次政务会议于三十日下午举行。会议听取和批准了戎子和副部长所作的中央人民政府财政部一九五○年工作总结及一九五一年工作的方针和任务的报告。会议并通过任免案多起。
