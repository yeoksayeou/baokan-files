Title: Tibetan Visiting Delegation Sets Off for Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 4th. The 1955 Tibetan Regional Visiting Delegation, composed of representatives from various regions and circles in Tibet, left Lhasa today via the Qinghai-Tibet Highway for Beijing. Some members of the delegation will travel from the Qamdo area to Beijing via the Sichuan-Tibet Highway.

This delegation includes representatives from the Tibetan local government, the Panchen Kampot Conference Hall Committee, and the Qamdo Regional People's Liberation Committee. Including the youth visiting group, which is part of the visiting delegation, the entire delegation has 150 members. The visiting delegation is headed by Lhalu Tsewang Dorje, former Kalon of the Tibetan local government, with Angoin Living Buddha of Tashilhunpo Monastery in Xigaze, and Renqing Dengzhu from the Qamdo region as deputy heads. The head of the youth visiting group is Sampo Tenzin Döndrup, deputy director of the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Association and Daipen of the Tibetan local government's army.

Before his departure, Lhalu Tsewang Dorje, the head of the delegation, told reporters: This visiting delegation is the largest one to date. The vast majority of the delegation members are visiting the interior of the motherland for the first time, and many Zong have sent their own representatives. Everyone hopes to see the great construction of the motherland with their own eyes, which shows that the Tibetan people's love for the motherland is increasing day by day.

Before the departure of the visiting delegation, the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held farewell banquets and soirees.

### 西藏参观团启程来北京

1955-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    西藏参观团启程来北京
    新华社拉萨四日电　由西藏各地区各界代表组成的一九五五年西藏地区参观团，今天离开拉萨取道青藏公路前往北京。一部分团员将由昌都地区取道康藏公路前往北京。
    这个参观团包括了西藏地方政府、班禅堪布会议厅委员会和昌都地区人民解放委员会三方面的代表。连同作为参观团组成部分的青年参观团在内，全团共有团员一百五十人。参观团由西藏地方政府卸任噶伦拉鲁·泽旺仁曾任团长，日喀则札什伦布寺安庆活佛和昌都地区的仁青登珠任副团长。青年参观团团长为拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会副主任委员、西藏地方政府军队代本桑颇·登增顿珠。
    拉鲁·泽旺仁曾团长临行前对记者说：这个参观团是历次参观团中人数最多的一个。参观团绝大部分团员都是第一次去祖国内地参观，许多宗都派出了自己的代表。大家都希望到内地亲自看看祖国的伟大建设，这说明西藏人民对祖国的热爱一天天增强了。
    参观团出发前，中共西藏工委和中国人民解放军西藏军区曾举行欢送宴会和晚会。
