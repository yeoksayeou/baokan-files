Title: Nan Hanchen Hosts Banquet for Japanese Business Delegation to China

Author: 
Date: 1955-09-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, hosted a banquet at 7:30 PM on September 23rd to welcome the entire delegation of the Japanese business community visiting China at their invitation.

Attending the banquet from the Chinese side were Lei Renmin, Li Zhuchen, and Ji Chaoding, Vice-Chairmen of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Lu Xuzhang, member; Wen Shizhen, Xiao Fangzhou, and Shu Ziqing, Deputy Secretaries-General; and managers of various state-owned trading companies.

Japanese figures in Beijing, including Kameda Togo, Kaneko Kentaro, and Nakamura Kanjuro, were also invited to the banquet.

### 南汉宸设宴招待日本工商界访华代表团

1955-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    南汉宸设宴招待日本工商界访华代表团
    新华社二十三日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸二十三日下午七时半设宴招待应邀来中国访问的日本工商界访华代表团全体人员。
    中国方面出席宴会的，有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民、李烛尘、冀朝鼎，委员卢绪章、副秘书长文士桢、萧方洲、舒自清和各国营贸易公司经理等。
    在北京的日本人士龟田东伍、金子健太和中村翫右卫门也应邀出席了宴会。
