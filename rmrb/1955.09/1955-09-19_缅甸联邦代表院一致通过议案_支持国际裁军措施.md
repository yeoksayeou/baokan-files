Title: The Burmese Chamber of Deputies Unanimously Passes a Resolution Supporting International Disarmament Measures
Author:
Date: 1955-09-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon: The Burmese Chamber of Deputies unanimously passed a resolution supporting international disarmament measures at a meeting held on the 7th. The resolution stated: "The agreements reached at the Geneva Four-Power Summit Conference and the ongoing work on disarmament by all countries in the United Nations are measures that contribute to the peace and happiness of mankind and promote the rapid realization of disarmament plans. The Burmese Parliament expresses its support."

### 缅甸联邦代表院一致通过议案  支持国际裁军措施

1955-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸联邦代表院一致通过议案
    支持国际裁军措施
    新华社讯　仰光消息：缅甸联邦代表院全体议员在七日举行的一次会议上一致通过了一项支持国际裁军措施的议案。这个议案说：“日内瓦四大国政府首脑会议所达成的协议以及目前在联合国继续进行中的有关各国裁军问题的工作，是有助于世界人类的和平幸福和促使裁军计划迅速实现的措施，缅甸国会表示支持。”
