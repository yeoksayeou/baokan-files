Title: China Youth Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Chinese Youth Delegation, which participated in the Fifth World Youth and Students Festival for Peace and Friendship, returned to Beijing by train on the 6th. The head of the delegation, Luo Yi, the deputy head, Qu Tangliang, and some members of the delegation, as well as some members of the youth sports team and art troupe who participated in the festival, have returned to China earlier.

### 我国青年代表团回到北京

1955-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我国青年代表团回到北京
    新华社六日讯　参加第五届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的中国青年代表团已在六日乘火车返回北京。代表团团长罗毅、副团长区棠亮和部分团员，以及参加联欢节的青年体育队和艺术团部分团员都已先期返国。
