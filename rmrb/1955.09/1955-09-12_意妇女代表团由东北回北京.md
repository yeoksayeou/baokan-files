Title: Italian Women's Delegation Returns to Beijing from Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1955-09-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th. From the 5th to the 8th, the Italian women's delegation visited Shenyang, Anshan, Fushun and other places. The delegation returned to Beijing on the 9th. In Shenyang, the delegation visited the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant, the Pneumatic Tool Plant, and the workers' village, and visited workers' families to understand the workers' living conditions. In Anshan, the delegation visited a large rolling mill, a seamless steel tube plant, and two blast furnaces.

The delegation also visited the open-pit coal mine in Fushun.

### 意妇女代表团由东北回北京

1955-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    意妇女代表团由东北回北京
    据新华社九日讯　五日到八日，意大利妇女代表团先后到沈阳、鞍山、抚顺等地进行参观访问。代表团已在九日返回北京。在沈阳，代表团参观了沈阳第一机床厂、风动工具厂和工人村，并访问了工人家庭，了解了工人的生活情况。代表团在鞍山参观了大型轧钢厂、无缝钢管厂和两个炼铁炉。
    代表团还到抚顺参观了露天煤矿。
