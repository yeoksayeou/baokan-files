Title: National Workers' Games Ball Game Competition Opens
Author: 
Date: 1955-09-17
Page: 3

The ball game competitions of the First National Workers' Games opened yesterday (16th). The opening ceremony was held at the Beijing Hotel at 3:30 PM. Attendees included Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Vice Chairman Zhu Xuefan, Secretariat Secretary Liu Ziju, Minister of Health Li Dequan, Deputy Director of the State Sports Commission Rong Gaotang, Secretariat Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Zhang Ze, and athletes from seventeen industrial units across the country. The Soviet Trade Union Sports Delegation, led by A.V. Vasiliev, Head of the Sports Department of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, also attended the opening ceremony.

The opening ceremony was presided over by Comrade Lai Ruoyu. Zhu Xuefan, Li Dequan, and Zhang Ze delivered speeches successively. In their speeches, they unanimously pointed out that the First National Workers' Games is the first major review of workers' sports since the founding of New China. They expressed hope that through this sports event, workers' sports would be more widely promoted. They hoped that all athletes would encourage more workers to participate in sports activities, strengthen their physiques, and strive to fulfill and over fulfill the First Five-Year Plan for national economic development.

The head of the Soviet Trade Union Sports Delegation, Vasiliev, also spoke at the meeting, wishing all athletes new achievements in production, study, and physical exercise.

From 8:30 AM yesterday, the ball game competitions of this sports event had already begun. Due to rain, the men's and women's basketball and volleyball matches were moved to the gymnasiums of Peking University and Tsinghua University. A total of thirty intense matches were held throughout the morning, afternoon, and evening. The football matches were postponed.

Participating in the various ball game competitions of this sports event are a total of sixty-six men's and women's basketball and volleyball teams and thirteen football teams from seventeen industrial units across the country, with a total of one thousand and ten male and female athletes. During the preliminary rounds, a total of one hundred and seventy-two matches will be held to select the winning teams for the finals.

### 全国工人体育运动大会球类竞赛揭幕

1955-09-17
第3版()
专栏：

    全国工人体育运动大会球类竞赛揭幕
    全国第一届工人体育运动大会球类竞赛昨日（十六日）揭幕。揭幕式下午三时半在北京饭店举行。参加揭幕式的有中华全国总工会主席赖若愚、副主席朱学范、书记处书记刘子久，卫生部部长李德全，体育运动委员会副主任荣高棠，青年团中央书记处书记章泽，以及全国十七个产业单位的运动员。以全苏工会中央理事会体育运动部部长阿·维·华西里耶夫为首的苏联工会体育代表团也参加了揭幕式。
    揭幕式由赖若愚同志主持，朱学范、李德全、章泽相继讲话。他们在讲话中一致指出：全国第一届工人体育运动大会是新中国建立以来工人体育运动的第一次大检阅，希望通过这次体育运动大会，把职工群众的体育运动更广泛地开展起来，各运动员应该带动职工更多地参加体育活动，增强体质，为完成和超额完成国家发展国民经济的第一个五年计划而奋斗。
    苏联工会体育代表团团长华西里耶夫也在会上讲了话，他预祝各运动员在生产中，在学习中，在体育锻炼中获得新的成就。
    从昨天上午八时半起，这次体育运动大会的球类竞赛已经开始，因天雨，男、女篮、排球比赛改在北京大学和清华大学的体育馆举行。全日上午、下午和晚间共进行了三十场紧张比赛。足球比赛则延期举行。
    参加这次体育运动大会各项球类比赛的共有全国十七个产业单位的男、女篮、排球队六十六队和足球队十三队，共有男女运动员一千零十人。他们在预赛期间共将进行一百七十二场比赛，选出优胜队再进行决赛。
