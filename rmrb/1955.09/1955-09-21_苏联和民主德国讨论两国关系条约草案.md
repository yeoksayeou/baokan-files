Title: The Soviet Union and the German Democratic Republic Discuss Draft Treaty on Bilateral Relations
Author:
Date: 1955-09-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 20th - TASS: The government delegations of the Soviet Union and the German Democratic Republic continued negotiations at the Grand Kremlin Palace in Moscow on the morning of September 19th to discuss further measures to develop and consolidate friendly relations between the two countries.

    The meeting was chaired by Prime Minister Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic.

    At the beginning of the meeting, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Bolz of the German Democratic Republic delivered a report on the draft "Treaty of Relations between the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and the German Democratic Republic," which was drafted by the joint Soviet-German expert committee established at the September 17th meeting.

    During the discussion of the draft treaty, the following representatives from the German Democratic Republic spoke: Prime Minister Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic and delegation members: First Deputy Prime Minister Ulbricht, Deputy Prime Minister Nuschke, General Secretary of the Democratic Farmers' Party Ross, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic Bolz, and Chairman of the National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany Correns.

    The following representatives from the Soviet Union spoke: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union Bulganin and Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Khrushchev.

    The next meeting is scheduled for September 20th.

### 苏联和民主德国讨论两国关系条约草案

1955-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和民主德国讨论两国关系条约草案
    新华社二十日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联和德意志民主共和国两国政府代表团九月十九日上午继续在莫斯科大克里姆林宫进行谈判，商讨关于发展和巩固两国友好关系的进一步措施的问题。
    德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥担任主席。
    会议开始时听取了德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部长博尔茨关于“苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟和德意志民主共和国关系条约”草案的报告，这个草案是由九月十七日会议所成立的苏德专家联合委员会草拟的。
    在讨论条约草案时，德意志民主共和国方面发言的有德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥和代表团团员：第一副总理乌布利希，副总理努舍克，民主农民党总书记罗斯，德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部长博尔茨，民主德国全国阵线全国委员会主席柯伦斯。
    苏联方面发言的有苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫。
    下次会议定在九月二十日举行。
