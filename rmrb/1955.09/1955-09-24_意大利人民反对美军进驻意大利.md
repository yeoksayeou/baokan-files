Title: Italian People Oppose the Stationing of U.S. Troops in Italy
Author:
Date: 1955-09-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Rome, September 22nd (TASS dispatch): "Unità" reports that residents of Vicenza, Italy, have expressed extreme dissatisfaction with the relocation of U.S. troops from Austria to Vicenza. Groups of young people in Vicenza distributed leaflets opposing the stationing of foreign troops in Italy. Police authorities arrested Andriolo, secretary of the Vicenza provincial organization of the Italian Communist Youth Federation.

According to "Unità," the arrival of U.S. troops in Vicenza was almost secret, without any formal ceremony, which proves that the Italian authorities are uneasy about the widespread protests throughout the country against the relocation of U.S. troops to northern Italy.

### 意大利人民反对美军进驻意大利

1955-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    意大利人民反对美军进驻意大利
    新华社二十三日讯　据塔斯社罗马二十二日讯：“团结报”报道，意大利威钦萨的居民对于美国军队从奥地利撤到威钦萨表示极度不满。威钦萨一批批青年人散发了反对外国军队进驻意大利的传单。警察当局逮捕了意大利共产主义青年团威钦萨省组织书记安德里奥洛。
    据“团结报”指出，美国军队几乎是秘密开到威钦萨的，并没有举行任何正式仪式，这就证明了意大利当局对于全国各地广泛开展的反对美国军队调驻意大利北部的抗议运动感到不安。
