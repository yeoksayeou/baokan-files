Title: Swedish Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-01
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, August 31st. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the five-member Swedish Cultural Delegation, led by Madame Wigforss, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 28th.

### 瑞典文化代表团到北京

1955-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞典文化代表团到北京
    新华社三十一日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的瑞典文化代表团一行五人，在团长维堡夫人率领下，二十八日下午到达北京。
