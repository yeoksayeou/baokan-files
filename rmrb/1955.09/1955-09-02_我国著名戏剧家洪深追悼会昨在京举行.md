Title: Memorial Service for Renowned Chinese Dramatist Hong Shen Held in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1955-09-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 1st. A memorial service for Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Deputy to the First National People's Congress, and renowned Chinese dramatist, was held in Beijing at 9:00 AM today.

	Wreaths were placed before and around Hong Shen's coffin and in the mourning hall. These were sent by Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Chairman of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Guo Moruo, relevant departments, organizations, and individuals, as well as many embassies of foreign countries in China and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

	At the memorial service, Ouyang Yuqian introduced Hong Shen's life story. Ouyang Yuqian said: "Comrade Hong Shen was a heroic patriot who made great contributions during the dawn of Chinese spoken drama and film. After 1930, he actively worked under the leadership of the Party and made many beneficial contributions to the education and training of the new generation in drama and film. In recent years, he has also made certain contributions to promoting international cultural exchanges."

	Attending the memorial service were Li Weihan, Xi Zhongxun, Shen Yanbing, Zhou Yang, Qian Junrui, Yu Yifu, Xia Yan, Yang Hansheng, Ouyang Yuqian, Yu Xinqing, Mei Lanfang, Lao She, Chen Zhongjing, and others, as well as responsible persons and representatives from relevant government departments, democratic parties, people's organizations, and people from the literary and art circles, Hong Shen's friends, and family members, totaling approximately 500 people.

	Many foreign embassies in China also sent representatives to attend the memorial service.

	Furthermore, Premier Zhou Enlai personally visited Comrade Hong Shen in the hospital before his death and expressed his condolences.

### 我国著名戏剧家洪深追悼会昨在京举行

1955-09-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我国著名戏剧家洪深追悼会昨在京举行
    新华社一日讯　对外文化联络局局长、第一届全国人民代表大会代表、中国著名戏剧家洪深追悼会一日上午九时在北京举行。
    在洪深的灵柩前和灵堂周围摆满了花圈。其中有国务院总理周恩来、中国文学艺术界联合会主席郭沫若以及各有关部门、团体和个人赠送的；还有许多国家驻中国的大使馆和苏联对外文化协会赠送的。
    在追悼会上，欧阳予倩介绍了洪深的生平事略。欧阳予倩说：“洪深同志是一个英勇的爱国主义者，在中国话剧和电影事业的黎明时期，作了很大的贡献。一九三○年以后，他积极地在党的领导下工作，在戏剧电影方面，对新的一代的教育和培养作了许多有益的工作。最近几年来对于促进国际文化联系，也作出一定的贡献。”
    参加追悼会的有李维汉、习仲勋、沈雁冰、周扬、钱俊瑞、于毅夫、夏衍、阳翰笙、欧阳予倩、余心清、梅兰芳、老舍、陈忠经等，还有政府有关部门、各民主党派、人民团体的负责人和代表以及文艺界人士、洪深的生前友好和家属等共约五百人。
    许多外国驻中国大使馆也派代表参加了追悼会。
    又讯：洪深同志逝世前，周恩来总理曾亲往医院探视并慰问。
