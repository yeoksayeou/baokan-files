Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives British Goodwill Delegation to China; Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet to See Off British Goodwill Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1955-09-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 12th—Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received the head of the British Goodwill Delegation to China, Maurice Orbach, and all members of the delegation on the morning of September 12th. Accompanying the reception was Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, who had invited the delegation to visit China.

The hosts and guests exchanged views on issues concerning China and Britain, as well as international matters. At the end of the meeting, Maurice Orbach expressed his gratitude for the meeting and the Chinese government's assistance in their visit to China.

Also present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Liu Guan, Secretary-General; Qiao Guanhua and Luo Longji, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Cao Mengjun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; and Ji Chaoding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

Xinhua News Agency, September 12th—Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, received Maurice Orbach, head of the British Goodwill Delegation to China, on the evening of September 12th and held a banquet to see off the British Goodwill Delegation to China.

The British Goodwill Delegation to China returned to Beijing on September 11th after visiting various places in East China and will soon leave Beijing to return home.

At the banquet, Guo Moruo said in his speech: "You have brought the friendship of the British people to the Chinese people. You have learned a lot about the new China and given us many constructive suggestions. Several scholars have also made many academic reports, for which we express our deep gratitude." Guo Moruo added that the delegation's visit to China had contributed to the strengthening of friendship between China and Britain. He expressed the hope that the delegation would bring the profound friendship of the Chinese people to the British people upon their return home, and he hoped that people from all walks of life in Britain would continue to visit China constantly in the future. He said that mutual visits and frequent contact between the people of the two countries can not only enhance mutual understanding and friendship but also promote the development of world peace.

Maurice Orbach then delivered a speech, expressing his gratitude for the sincerity of the hosts. He said: "In China, we have seen many exciting things often presented before our eyes. Whether in factories, rural areas, schools, or elsewhere, we have seen your great efforts to improve the living standards of the people." He said that the delegation was deeply moved to see the Chinese people turning water damage into water conservancy, especially the efforts to "turn the Yellow River, this river of sorrow, into a river of joy." He said that the delegation was about to end its visit to China, but its work was not over. It would also convey to the British people the situation of the hard-working and brave Chinese people striving for the industrialization of the motherland and for a better future life. He said: "China once had a long and famous history of culture and art, and now its people are creating a new history, creating more outstanding and famous culture and art." He proposed a toast to the Chinese people who are creating historical miracles.

Attending the banquet tonight were heads of various people's organizations, including Liao Chengzhi, Xu Deheng, Xu Baoju, Liu Guan, Dong Xin, Li Dequan, Qian Duansheng, Chen Kangbai, Ji Chaoding, as well as people from the political, legal, cultural, artistic, and religious circles.

### 陈毅副总理接见英国访华友好代表团  郭沫若设宴欢送英国访华友好代表团

1955-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见英国访华友好代表团
    郭沫若设宴欢送英国访华友好代表团
    新华社十二日讯　国务院副总理陈毅在十二日上午接见了英国访华友好代表团团长莫里斯·奥巴赫和代表团全体团员。陪同接见的有邀请代表团来中国访问的中国科学院院长郭沫若。
    宾主就中英两国以及国际间一些问题交换了意见。会见结束时，莫里斯·奥巴赫对这次会见以及中国政府协助他们来中国参观访问，表示感谢。
    接见时，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志、秘书长刘贯一，中国人民外交学会副会长乔冠华、罗隆基，中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，中华全国民主妇女联合会书记处书记曹孟君，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎等也在座。
    新华社十二日讯　中国科学院院长、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若在十二日晚接见了英国访华友好代表团团长莫里斯·奥巴赫，并举行宴会欢送英国访华友好代表团。
    英国访华友好代表团是在华东各地参观后于十一日返回北京，并即将离京返国。
    在宴会上，郭沫若致词说：“你们把英国人民的友谊带给了中国人民，你们在新中国了解了不少情况，给我们提了许多建设性的意见，有几位学者还作了许多学术报告，我们对此深表感谢。”郭沫若又说，代表团这次访问中国，对中英两国友谊的加强是有贡献的。他希望代表团回国后，能把中国人民对英国人民的深厚友谊带给英国人民，他并希望今后英国各界人士能继续不断地到中国来访问。他说，两国人民之间的相互访问和经常接触，不仅能增强相互间的了解和友谊，而且可以促进世界和平事业的发展。
    莫里斯·奥巴赫接着致词，对主人的诚意表示感谢。他说：“我们在中国，有许多令人非常兴奋的事情常常在我们的眼前展现。我们不管在工厂、农村、学校或者在别的什么地方，都看到你们在为提高人民生活水平而作巨大的努力。”他说，代表团见到中国人民变水害为水利，特别是“变黄河这个悲哀的河流为愉快的河流”而努力的情况，都深为感动。他说，代表团即将结束在中国的访问，但是它的工作并没有结束，它还要把勤劳而勇敢的中国人民，为祖国的工业化，为美好的未来生活而努力的情况，转告给英国人民。他说：“中国曾经有过悠久而著名的文化艺术历史，现在他的人民又在创造新的历史，创造更出色更有名的文化艺术。”他提议为创造历史奇迹的中国人民而干杯。
    出席今晚宴会的，有各人民团体负责人廖承志、许德珩、许宝驹、刘贯一、董昕、李德全、钱端升、陈康白、冀朝鼎以及政法、文艺、宗教界人士等。
