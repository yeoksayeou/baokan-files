Title: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Appoints Procurators of the Supreme People's Procuratorate
Author:
Date: 1955-09-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th. The 21st meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on September 13th passed the following list of appointments:
    Procurators of the Supreme People's Procuratorate
    Shi Ming
    Ren Chongjie
    Li Fang
    Feng Rongchang
    Zeng Ming
    Han Tiesheng
