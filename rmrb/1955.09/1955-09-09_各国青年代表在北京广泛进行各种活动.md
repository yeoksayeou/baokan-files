Title: Youth Representatives from Various Countries Conduct Extensive Activities in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 8th. Youth representatives from various countries who came to China after participating in the Fifth World Youth Festival have been in extensive contact with people from all walks of life in Beijing in recent days.

    Youth representatives from different countries and with different occupations have met with people from all aspects of Chinese society and held discussions on youth, trade unions, women, classical and folk art, music, dance, literature, religion, ethnicity, the socialist transformation of private industry and commerce, and international trade. The American youth representatives also met with Hao Jianxiu, a famous Chinese model worker. They were deeply moved when they learned that Hao Jianxiu had risen from a poor child to become a representative of the National People's Congress. The American youth representatives also met with the director of a private flour mill.

    Up to August, the heads of various departments of the State Planning Commission of the People's Republic of China had also held five symposiums for youth representatives from twenty-four countries invited to visit China, introducing them to the new China's various construction achievements and the first Five-Year Plan, and answering in detail the questions raised by the youth representatives from various countries.

    On the 7th and 8th, youth representatives from Argentina, Chile, Paraguay, Venezuela, North Korea, the United States, Mexico, and Brazil reported to university and middle school students and youth from all walks of life in Beijing on the lives of young people and other aspects of their countries.

    Many youth representatives from various countries and Chinese youths believe that this series of friendly contacts has enhanced mutual understanding.

    Youth representatives from the United States and from Latin American countries, Spain, and Portugal have held parties with students from the Western Language Department of Peking University and the Spanish Department of the Beijing Foreign Languages Institute. At a gathering of youth representatives from Egypt, Iraq, Jordan, Kuwait, Lebanon, Sudan, and Syria with students majoring in Arabic at the Department of Oriental Languages ​​of Peking University, the young friends from various Arab countries presented many Arabic books, newspapers, and magazines to the Arabic major at Peking University. At this party, youth representatives from various Arab countries sang Arab folk songs in unison, and some representatives could not help but dance.

    Catholic and Christian members of the youth representatives from various countries also went to the Catholic Church and the Congregational Church to attend Mass and worship on Sunday, September 4.

    In recent days, youth representatives from various countries have also visited and toured the Asian Student Sanatorium, the Central Institute for Nationalities, the Central Academy of Fine Arts, Peking University, Tsinghua University, the Central Institute of Physical Education, the Hui Nationality College, the Beijing Library, the Children's Hospital, kindergartens, mosques, and many factories, agricultural producers' cooperatives, and handicraft producers' cooperatives, and also toured the Forbidden City, Yonghe Temple, and various scenic spots, cultural and educational areas, and urban areas.

    Many youth representatives participated in the "Summer Music and Dance Evening" at the Labor Theater, and successively enjoyed Chinese films "The Butterfly Lovers", "The White Haired Girl", "Chicken Feather Letter", Chinese Peking Opera "The Cowherd and the Weaver Girl", "Black Whirlwind" and Chinese acrobatics performances.

### 各国青年代表在北京广泛进行各种活动

1955-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    各国青年代表在北京广泛进行各种活动
    新华社八日讯在参加第五届世界青年联欢节以后来到中国的各国青年代表，连日来在北京同中国各界人士广泛进行了接触。
    来自不同国家，有着不同职业的各国青年代表们，曾分别同中国各方面人士会见，并就青年、工会、妇女、古典和民间艺术、音乐、舞蹈、文学、宗教、民族、私营工商业社会主义改造、国际贸易等问题进行座谈。美国青年代表们还会见了中国著名的劳动模范郝建秀。当他们知道郝建秀从一个穷苦孩子成为全国人民代表大会代表时，都非常感动。美国青年代表们也曾会见了一个私营面粉厂的厂长。
    到八月为止，中华人民共和国国家计划委员会各部门负责人，还先后为邀请来中国访问的二十四个国家青年代表举行了五次座谈会，向他们介绍了新中国的各种建设成就和第一个五年计划情况，并详尽地解答了各国青年代表们提出的问题。
    在七日和八日，阿根廷、智利、巴拉圭、委内瑞拉、朝鲜、美国、墨西哥和巴西等国青年代表们，曾分别向北京的大、中学生和各界青年报告了他们本国的青年生活及其他方面的情况。
    许多国家的青年代表和中国的青年们都认为，通过这一系列的相互间的友好接触，更增进了相互的了解。
    来自美国的青年代表和来自拉丁美洲各国及西班牙、葡萄牙的青年，曾先后同北京大学西语系和北京外国语学院西班牙语系的学生们分别举行了联欢。在埃及、伊拉克、约旦、科威特、黎巴嫩、苏丹和叙利亚等国青年代表同北京大学东方语系阿拉伯语专业的学生们举行的一个联欢会上，阿拉伯各国的青年朋友们，还把许多阿拉伯文书报和杂志，赠给了北京大学阿拉伯语专业。在这个联欢会上，各个阿拉伯国家的青年代表合唱了阿拉伯民歌，有的代表还情不自禁地跳起舞来。
    各国青年代表中的天主教和基督教徒们，还曾在九月四日（星期日）分别到天主教堂和公理会作弥撒和作礼拜。
    连日来，各国青年代表并分别访问和参观了亚洲学生疗养院、中央民族学院、中央美术学院、北京大学、清华大学、中央体育学院、回民学院、北京图书馆、儿童医院、幼儿园、清真寺和许多工厂、农业生产合作社、手工业生产合作社等，还游览了故宫、雍和宫以及各风景区、文教区和市区。
    许多青年代表在劳动剧场参加了“夏季音乐舞蹈晚会”，并先后欣赏了中国电影“梁山伯与祝英台”、“白毛女”、“鸡毛信”，中国京剧“牛郎织女”、“黑旋风”和中国杂技表演等。
