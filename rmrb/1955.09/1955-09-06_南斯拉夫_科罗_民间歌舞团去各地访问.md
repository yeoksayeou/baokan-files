Title: Yugoslav "Kolo" Folk Dance Ensemble Visits Various Places
Author: 
Date: 1955-09-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 5th. After visiting and performing in Beijing, the Yugoslav "Kolo" Folk Dance Ensemble left Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 5th to visit and perform in various parts of China. Twelve young literary and art workers from Beijing accompanied the dance troupe to learn the beautiful Yugoslav folk dances. Representatives from the Ministry of Culture and the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations were at the station to see the dance troupe off as they left Beijing.
　　The dance troupe gave three wonderful song and dance performances to ten thousand spectators in Beijing. The dance troupe also visited art schools and factories in Beijing, and toured the famous historical sites and scenic spots of Beijing.

### 南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团去各地访问

1955-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团去各地访问
    新华社五日讯　南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团在北京进行访问和演出后，五日下午乘火车离北京到中国各地访问和演出。北京青年文艺工作者十二人随同歌舞团离京，向歌舞团学习优美的南斯拉夫民间舞蹈。歌舞团离开北京时，文化部和对外文化联络局的代表曾到车站欢送。
    歌舞团在北京向一万观众作了三场精采的歌舞表演。歌舞团在北京还参观了艺术学校和工厂，游览了北京的名胜古迹。
