Title: Signing of Treaty on Relations Between the Soviet Union and the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1955-09-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th: The "Treaty on Relations between the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and the German Democratic Republic" was signed on the 20th in the Grand Kremlin Palace in Moscow, according to TASS.

Representing the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, signed the treaty.

Representing the President of the German Democratic Republic, Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Grotewohl, signed the treaty.

The Soviet side participating in the signing ceremony included Kaganovich, Malenkov, Pervukhin, Saburov, Suslov, Khrushchev; Soviet Minister of Defense Zhukov, Minister of Foreign Trade Kabanov, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union Zorin, Semenov, and Soviet Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic Pushkin, and others.

The German Democratic Republic side participating in the ceremony included Deputy Prime Ministers Ulbricht, Stoph, Nuschke, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Bolz, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to the Soviet Union König, and others.

### 苏联和民主德国关系条约签字

1955-09-22
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和民主德国关系条约签字
    新华社二十一日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科二十日讯：“苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟和德意志民主共和国关系条约”二十日在莫斯科大克里姆林宫签字。
    代表苏联最高苏维埃主席团在条约上签字的是苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁。
    代表德意志民主共和国总统在条约上签字的是德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥。
    苏联方面参加签字仪式的有卡冈诺维奇，马林科夫，别尔乌辛，萨布罗夫，苏斯洛夫，赫鲁晓夫；苏联国防部长朱可夫，对外贸易部部长卡巴诺夫，苏联外交部副部长佐林、谢苗诺夫，苏联驻德意志民主共和国大使普希金等。
    德意志民主共和国方面参加仪式的有副总理乌布利希、斯多夫、努舍克，副总理兼外交部长博尔茨，德意志民主共和国驻苏联大使柯尼希等。
