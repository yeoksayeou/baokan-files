Title: World Federation of Scientific Workers Congress Opens in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1955-09-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 24th. The Fourth Congress of the World Federation of Scientific Workers opened in Berlin on the morning of the 23rd. More than eighty scientists from countries including the Soviet Union, China, the United Kingdom, France, Poland, Czechoslovakia, and Germany are participating in the congress. Chinese scientists Zhou Peiyuan, Hua Luogeng, and Tu Changwang attended the congress.

After Professor Powell, Vice President of the World Federation of Scientific Workers and Nobel Prize winner, delivered the opening speech, all the representatives stood in silent tribute to the late great scholar Professor Einstein, who devoted his life to scientific work.

Then Professor Friedrich, President of the German Academy of Sciences, delivered a speech, welcoming the scientists from various countries and expressing his hope that scientists from all countries would further cooperate on the basis of science serving peace and human happiness.

After hearing the report by Professor Bernal, Vice President of the World Federation of Scientific Workers, the conference will discuss the following three agenda items: (1) The application of atomic energy for peaceful purposes; (2) The social responsibility of scientists; (3) The World Federation of Scientific Workers' struggle to improve the social conditions of scientific workers.

### 世界科学工作者协会代表大会在柏林开幕

1955-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

    世界科学工作者协会代表大会在柏林开幕
    新华社柏林二十四日电　世界科学工作者协会第四届代表大会二十三日上午在柏林开幕。参加代表大会的有苏联、中国、英国、法国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克和德国等国的科学家八十余人。中国科学家周培源、华罗庚、凃长望出席了大会。
    世界科学工作者协会副主席、诺贝尔奖金获奖人鲍威尔教授致开幕词后，全体代表起立默哀，向终身致力于科学工作的已故的伟大学者爱因斯坦教授致敬。
    接着由德国科学院院长腓特烈教授致词，他欢迎到会的各国科学家，并且希望各国的科学家在科学为和平、为人类幸福而服务的基础上进一步进行合作。
    大会在听取世界科学工作者协会副主席贝尔纳教授的报告后，要讨论下列三项议程：（一）原子能应用于和平目的，（二）科学家的社会责任，（三）世界科学工作者协会为改善科学工作者的社会条件而斗争。
