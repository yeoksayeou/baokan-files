Title: Head of Egyptian Trade Delegation Talks About the Establishment of Trade Relations Between China and Egypt; Thai Government Agrees to Trade with Our Country
Author:
Date: 1955-09-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 3rd Dispatch from Cairo: The head of the Egyptian Republic Trade Delegation, Egyptian Minister of Commerce and Industry, Mohammed Abu Nuseir, returned to Cairo on the 2nd after visiting China and signing a trade agreement with the Chinese government.

Upon arriving in Cairo, Nuseir stated that economic relations between Egypt and People's China have been established on a solid foundation and will develop in the future. He said that Egypt has signed the largest cotton transaction in Egyptian history with China this time.

Xinhua News Agency, September 3rd Dispatch from Bangkok: The Thai Ministry of Economic Affairs recently notified the Bangkok Chinese Chamber of Commerce that the Thai government has agreed to trade with the People's Republic of China.

The notification stated that although Thailand has not yet established diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of China, conducting commercial trade between the two sides does not violate the United Nations Charter, because Thailand's trade with the People's Republic of China is normal.

The Bangkok business community is very excited about this news.

### 埃及贸易代表团团长谈中埃建立贸易关系  泰国政府同意和我国进行贸易

1955-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    埃及贸易代表团团长谈中埃建立贸易关系
    泰国政府同意和我国进行贸易
    新华社三日讯　开罗消息：埃及共和国贸易代表团团长、埃及工商部部长穆罕默德·阿卜·努赛尔在访问中国并同中国政府签订了贸易协定以后，已经在二日回到开罗。
    努赛尔在到达开罗时发表谈话说，埃及和人民中国之间的经济关系已经在巩固的基础上建立，而且今后将获得发展。他说，埃及这次和中国签订了在埃及历史上最大的棉花交易。
    新华社三日讯　曼谷消息：泰国经济部最近通知曼谷中华总商会说，泰国政府已经同意和中华人民共和国进行贸易。
    通知说，泰国虽然还没有同中华人民共和国建立邦交，但是，双方进行商业贸易并不违反联合国宪章，因为泰国同中华人民共和国的贸易是正常的。
    曼谷商界对这个消息很感兴奋。
