Title: Part of the Tunisian Delegation to China Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1955-09-24
Page: 4
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, 20th. After visiting the Northeast and East China, Tunisian friendly delegation representatives Belarouna, Shakron, and Dries returned to Beijing from Shanghai on the 19th and left Beijing for home on the morning of the 20th.
　　On the afternoon of the 19th, Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, met with Belarouna and the other two representatives.

### 突尼斯访华代表团部分人员回国

1955-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    突尼斯访华代表团部分人员回国
    新华社二十日讯　突尼斯访华友好代表团代表贝拉卢纳、沙克隆和德里斯三人在东北、华东等地参观后十九日由上海回到北京，并在二十日晨离开北京回国。
    十九日下午，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若接见了贝拉卢纳等三人。
