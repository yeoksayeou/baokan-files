Title: Egypt and Israel Accept Ceasefire Appeal
Author:
Date: 1955-09-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 5th, TASS dispatch: It is reported that the Egyptian and Israeli governments have responded affirmatively to the second appeal issued by General Burns, the Chief of Staff of the United Nations Truce Supervision Organization for Palestine, on September 3rd, regarding the cessation of armed conflicts on the Egyptian-Israeli border.

In his appeal, Burns suggested that combat operations cease before 5:00 PM on September 4th.

### 埃及和以色列接受停火呼吁

1955-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及和以色列接受停火呼吁
    新华社六日讯　塔斯社开罗五日讯：据报道，埃及和以色列政府对于联合国巴勒斯坦首席停战监察官伯恩斯将军九月三日发出的关于在埃及和以色列边界上停止武装冲突的第二次呼吁作了肯定的答复。
    伯恩斯在他的呼吁书中建议在九月四日下午五时以前停止战斗行动。
