Title: At the United Nations General Assembly Morning Session on September 23, Molotov's Speech Attracted the Attention of All Attendees
Author:
Date: 1955-09-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 23rd (TASS dispatch): At the United Nations General Assembly morning session on September 23, the heads of the Australian, Peruvian, and Soviet delegations spoke.

The Australian Minister for Foreign Affairs, Casey, pointed out that the contacts made in Geneva would become the basis for further cooperation and practical resolution of certain international problems. When discussing disarmament, he expressed his belief that a common program could be found between the proposals of U.S. President Eisenhower and the attitude of the Soviet government.

Casey believed that hastily convening a meeting to revise the United Nations Charter would be risky because the proposed amendments “may not receive the necessary votes.”

The head of the Peruvian delegation, Belaúnde, advocated reducing conventional armaments to the minimum necessary to ensure security, and he advocated a complete ban on all weapons of mass destruction under permanent and effective supervision. He supported Eisenhower’s proposal and considered it a “first step” toward disarmament.

After the Peruvian representative spoke, the President of the General Assembly invited the head of the Soviet delegation, Molotov, to speak. By the time Molotov spoke, the meeting hall, press gallery, and guest seats were already packed. All attendees listened to Molotov’s speech with great interest and attention.

### 在联合国大会九月二十三日上午会议上  莫洛托夫的发言受到全体与会者的注意

1955-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

    在联合国大会九月二十三日上午会议上
    莫洛托夫的发言受到全体与会者的注意
    新华社二十五日讯　据塔斯社纽约二十三日讯：在联合国大会九月二十三日上午会议上，澳大利亚、秘鲁和苏联代表团团长发言。
    澳大利亚外交部长凯西指出，日内瓦所进行的接触将成为实行进一步合作和实际解决某些国际问题的基础。他在谈到裁军问题时表示相信，在美国总统艾森豪威尔的建议和苏联政府的态度之间将会找到共同的纲领。
    凯西认为，匆匆召集会议来修改联合国宪章会是冒险的事，因为准备提出的修改“未必能够得到必要的票数”。
    秘鲁代表团团长贝朗德主张将常规军备裁减到保证安全所必要的最低限度，他主张在有永久性的和有效的监督的条件下完全禁止各种大规模毁灭性武器。他支持艾森豪威尔的建议，并认为这个建议是走向裁军的“第一步”。
    在秘鲁代表发言之后，大会主席请苏联代表团团长莫洛托夫发言。到莫洛托夫发言时，会议大厅、记者及来宾席已经挤得满满的了。全体到会者极感兴趣和极其注意地听取了莫洛托夫的发言。
