Title: Soviet Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-28
Page: 2

    Soviet Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    The Soviet Cultural Delegation, invited by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to attend the National Day celebrations of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing in batches on the 26th and 27th.
    The delegation is led by A.A. Surkov, Member of the Board and First Secretary of the Secretariat of the Soviet Writers' Union and Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. Members of the delegation include Mrs. Surkova, Vice Chairman of the Board of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Kalishyan, Director of the Stalingrad Oblast Agricultural Administration and Hero of Socialist Labor of the Soviet Union Sergeyev, Vice Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee Pafinova, Chairman of the Board of the Uzbek Republic Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Dzhurayev, Zoya's mother Kosmodemyanskaya, Head of the Teaching and Research Office of the Moscow Institute of Economics and Statistics Braginsky, and delegation secretary Lanskoy.
    When the Soviet Cultural Delegation arrived in Beijing, they were welcomed by Wu Yuzhang, Shen Junru, and Li Dequan, Vice Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Qian Junrui, Secretary General, Mao Dun, Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association, Lao She, Vice Chairman, Deng Yingchao, Xu Guangping, Zhang Yun, and Shi Liang, Vice Presidents of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and famous writers, scientists, and educators in Beijing.
    Charge d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, Romajin, and Qi Gangning, representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, also went to welcome them.
        (Xinhua News Agency) (Attached picture)

### 苏联文化代表团到京

1955-09-28
第2版()
专栏：

    苏联文化代表团到京
    应中苏友好协会总会的邀请前来参加中华人民共和国国庆典礼的苏联文化代表团，在二十六日和二十七日分批到达北京。
    代表团由苏联作家协会理事会理事、书记处第
  一书记、苏联最高苏维埃代表苏尔科夫率领，团员中有苏尔科夫夫人苏尔科娃、苏联对外文化协会理事会副主席卡利希扬、斯大林格勒州农业管理局局长、苏联社会主义劳动英雄谢尔盖耶夫、苏联妇女反法西斯委员会副主席巴菲诺娃、乌兹别克共和国对外文化协会理事会主席朱拉耶夫、苏联英雄卓娅的母亲科斯莫杰米扬斯卡娅、莫斯科经济统计学院教研室主任布拉金斯基和代表团秘书兰斯科伊。
    苏联文化代表团到达北京时，受到中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章、沈钧儒、李德全，秘书长钱俊瑞，中国作家协会主席茅盾、副主席老舍，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超、许广平、章蕴、史良，以及北京的著名作家、科学家、教育学家等人的欢迎。
    苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办罗迈进和苏联对外文化协会代表齐刚宁等也前往欢迎。
