Title: Vietnamese Journalists and Writers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. The Vietnamese journalists and writers delegation of eleven people, invited to visit China and led by head of delegation Deng Tai Mei, arrived in Beijing by train on the 28th. Responsible persons from the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, the All-China Journalists Association, and "People's Daily," as well as representatives from Beijing's journalistic and literary circles, were at the station to welcome them.
    Huang Wen Huan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, and embassy staff were also at the station to greet them.

### 越南记者、作家代表团到北京

1955-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    越南记者、作家代表团到北京
    新华社二十八日讯　应邀来中国访问的越南记者、作家代表团十一人，由团长邓泰梅率领，二十八日乘车到达北京。对外文化联络局、中国新闻工作者联谊会、“人民日报”的负责人员和北京新闻界、文艺界的代表都到车站欢迎。
    越南民主共和国驻华大使黄文欢和使馆人员也到车站迎接。
