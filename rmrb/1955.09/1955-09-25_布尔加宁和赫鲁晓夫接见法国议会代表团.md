Title: Bulganin and Khrushchev Receive French Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-09-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 23rd. TASS, Moscow, September 23rd: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, and the member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, received the French parliamentary delegation at the Grand Kremlin Palace on September 22nd, who came to visit the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

    The head of the delegation, Mr. Schneiter, Speaker of the French National Assembly, and members of the delegation attended the reception.

    Bulganin warmly welcomed the guests. He said that he was very happy to see them in Moscow. Afterwards, a friendly and frank conversation of about three hours took place.

    At the request of the French parliamentarians, Khrushchev and Bulganin answered the questions they raised. They expressed the Soviet Union's views on the ways and possibilities of further developing trade relations between the Soviet Union and France on the basis of mutual benefit without any discrimination, on easing international tension and consolidating peace, on the German question and the Far East situation.

    Khrushchev asked the delegation to convey the warmest congratulations to the French people, and hoped that the friendship between the French and Soviet people and the people of the world would continue to strengthen.

    Bulganin pointed out that he fully agreed with what Khrushchev had said, and on behalf of the Soviet government, he extended friendly greetings to the French people.

    Also present at the meeting between Bulganin and Khrushchev and the members of the French parliamentary delegation were: Volkov, Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Razis, Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Zorin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Joxe, French Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union, as well as Soviet and French journalists.

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫接见法国议会代表团

1955-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫接见法国议会代表团
    新华社二十三日讯　塔斯社莫斯科二十三日讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫于九月二十二日在大克里姆林宫接见了应苏联最高苏维埃的邀请前来苏联访问的法国议会代表团。
    出席招待会的有代表团团长、法国国民议会议长施奈特和代表团团员。
    布尔加宁向来宾们表示热烈的欢迎。他说，能在莫斯科看到他们，感到很愉快。随后就举行了约三小时友好、坦率的谈话。
    赫鲁晓夫和布尔加宁应法国议员们的请求，回答了他们所提出的问题。他们就苏法两国在没有任何歧视的互利基础上进一步发展贸易关系的途径和可能性的问题、缓和国际紧张局势和巩固和平的问题、德国问题和远东局势问题表示了苏联的看法。
    赫鲁晓夫请代表团向法国人民转达最热烈的祝贺，并且希望法苏人民和全世界人民之间的友谊不断加强。
    布尔加宁指出，他完全同意赫鲁晓夫所说的话，并且代表苏联政府向法国人民友好地致意。
    出席布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫同法国议会代表团人员的会谈的还有：苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席沃尔科夫、苏联最高苏维埃民族院主席拉齐斯、苏联外交部副部长佐林、法国驻苏联特命全权大使约克斯以及苏联和法国的新闻记者。
