Title: Sino-US Ambassadors Hold Twelfth Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-09-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, August 31st. The Sino-US Ambassadorial talks held their twelfth meeting at 10:00 am local time on the 31st. The meeting adjourned at 12:15 pm. The next meeting is scheduled for 10:00 am on September 6th.

    At today's meeting, the two ambassadors continued to discuss the first item on the agenda - the return of civilians to their respective countries.

### 中美两国大使举行第十二次会谈

1955-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中美两国大使举行第十二次会谈
    新华社日内瓦三十一日电　中美两国大使级会谈在三十一日本地时间十时举行第十二次会议。会议在十二时十五分休会，下次会议订于九月六日上午十时举行。
    在今天会议上，双方大使继续讨论了第一项议程——双方平民回国问题。
