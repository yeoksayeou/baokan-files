Title: 1955 Leipzig Autumn Fair Closes

Author:

Date: 1955-09-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 11th. The 1955 Leipzig Autumn Fair, which opened on September 4th, closed on the evening of the 9th. The fair aroused great interest among business people from many countries around the world. According to a communiqué issued by the Ministry of Foreign and Domestic Trade of the German Democratic Republic, the fair was visited by 265,000 people, including 13,500 residents from West Germany and West Berlin, and approximately 5,500 guests from 65 countries.
    The Leipzig Autumn Fair once again confirmed the achievements made by Eastern and Western countries in establishing and expanding trade relations.
    At the same time, the Leipzig Fair vividly demonstrated the growing strength of the German Democratic Republic in domestic and international trade. The German Democratic Republic achieved a foreign trade turnover of 706 million rubles at the fair (exports accounted for 425 million rubles), of which trade with Western countries accounted for a large proportion.

### 一九五五年来比锡秋季博览会闭幕

1955-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    一九五五年来比锡秋季博览会闭幕
    新华社柏林十一日电　九月四日开幕的一九五五年来比锡秋季博览会在九日晚间闭幕。这次博览会在全世界许多国家的贸易界人士中引起巨大的兴趣。据德意志民主共和国对外与国内贸易部发表的公报说，参观这次博览会的有二十六万五千人，其中包括来自西德和西柏林的居民一万三千五百人和六十五个国家的约五千五百个来宾。
    来比锡秋季博览会再次证实了东西方国家在建立和扩大贸易关系方面所取得的成就。
    同时，来比锡博览会生动地表明了德意志民主共和国在国内和国际贸易方面不断增长的力量。德意志民主共和国在博览会上所达成的对外贸易成交额为七亿零六百万卢布（输出占四亿二千五百万卢布），其中对西方国家的贸易额占有很大比重。
