Title: Japanese Business Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-23
Page: 4
　
    In a written statement issued upon arriving in Beijing on the 22nd, Masao Tajima, head of the Japanese Business Delegation and Vice Chairman of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, expressed his hope that this visit to China will further promote the promising trade between Japan and China and expand it even further in the future.

    Tajima Masao said that the Japanese Business Delegation came to China to reciprocate the visit of the Chinese trade delegation to Japan this spring, and also to fulfill the mission of directly and concretely promoting and expanding import and export transactions.

    He said that since the Chinese trade delegation left Japan, they immediately began preparations for the Japanese Business Delegation to visit China. It was originally planned to consist of about fifty people, but when this matter was announced to the Japanese business community, nearly two hundred people expressed their desire to participate. Finally, fifty-nine representatives were selected to form this delegation. This delegation includes traders of major commodities such as chemicals, machinery, shipbuilding, steel, agricultural products, aquatic products, and cotton yarn, as well as representatives from general small and medium-sized industrial and commercial circles, and shipping and commodity inspection experts. In addition, it also includes industrial representatives from all parts of Japan, from Hokkaido to Kyushu.

    Tajima Masao stated that they are willing to make their utmost efforts to develop trade between China and Japan in the future.

    Tajima Masao and forty members of the delegation arrived in Beijing by train from Guangzhou at 6:30 this morning. When they arrived, Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and others welcomed them at the station.

    On the afternoon of the 22nd, Masao Tajima, head of the Japanese Business Delegation, Masayoshi Noda, deputy head, Toraji Koriki, and a part of the delegation members, a total of eight people, paid a visit to Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

    Also present at the meeting were Lei Renmin, Li Zhuchen, and Ji Chao Ding, Vice Chairmen of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and others.

### 日本工商界访华代表团到达北京

1955-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    日本工商界访华代表团到达北京
    日本工商界访华代表团团长、日本国际贸易促进协会副会长田岛正雄在二十二日到达北京时发表的书面谈话中说，希望这次访问中国将进一步推动具有发展前途的日中两国间的贸易，并使它今后更加扩大。
    田岛正雄说，日本工商界访华代表团到中国来，是为了答谢今年春天中国贸易代表团访日之行，同时负有直接地具体地促进和扩大进出口交易的使命。
    他说，自从中国贸易代表团离开日本以后，他们立即开始筹组日本工商界访华代表团的工作，原定由五十人左右组成，但这件事情一向日本工商界宣布，就有近二百人希望参加。最后选出了五十九个代表，组成了这个代表团。这个代表团包括化学、机器、造船、钢铁、农产品、水产品和棉纱等主要商品的贸易商和一般中小工商业界的代表以及航运、商品检验专家，此外还包括从北海道到九州的全日本各地方的产业代表。
    田岛正雄表示，他们都愿意为今后发展中日两国间的贸易尽最大的努力。
    田岛正雄和代表团团员一行四十人是今天早晨六时三十分由广州乘火车到达北京的。当他们到达时，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民等都到车站欢迎。
    二十二日下午，日本工商界访华代表团团长田岛正雄、副团长野田雅亮、功力寅次和代表团一部分团员等共八人，拜会了中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸。
    拜会时在座的，有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民、李烛尘、冀朝鼎等多人。
