Title: Soviet Delegation and Delegation of the Federal Republic of Germany Continue Negotiations; Exchange of Views on General Issues Concluded, Foreign Ministers Tasked with Drafting Specific Recommendations
Author:
Date: 1955-09-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 11th, TASS dispatch: The delegations of the governments of the Soviet Union and the Federal Republic of Germany held their second meeting on the morning of the 10th.

At the suggestion of the head of the Soviet delegation, Bulganin, the chairmanship of the meeting rotated between the heads of the two delegations. The meeting on the 10th was chaired by Adenauer, Chancellor of the Federal Republic of Germany.

The meeting continued to discuss the establishment of direct diplomatic, trade, and cultural relations between the Soviet Union and the Federal Republic of Germany, as well as other issues of mutual interest.

Bulganin spoke at the meeting. Subsequently, Adenauer and the Foreign Minister of the Federal Republic of Germany, von Brentano, spoke successively. Then, Khrushchev, a member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and Molotov, the Soviet Foreign Minister, spoke. Subsequently, Hallstein, the Deputy Foreign Minister of the Federal Republic of Germany, spoke.

At the conclusion of the meeting, it was unanimously agreed that the exchange of views on general issues between the two delegations had concluded. The meeting tasked the foreign ministers of the two countries with drafting specific recommendations based on the results of the exchange of views.

### 苏联同德意志联邦共和国代表团继续谈判  一般性问题交换意见已经结束，责成外长草拟具体建议

1955-09-12
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联同德意志联邦共和国代表团继续谈判
    一般性问题交换意见已经结束，责成外长草拟具体建议
    新华社十一日讯　塔斯社莫斯科十日讯：苏联和德意志联邦共和国两国政府代表团谈判十日上午举行第二次会议。
    根据苏联代表团团长布尔加宁的建议，会议主席由两国代表团团长轮流担任。十日会议由德意志联邦共和国总理阿登纳担任主席。
    会议继续讨论苏联和德意志联邦共和国建立直接的外交、贸易和文化关系的问题以及双方共同感到兴趣的其他问题。
    布尔加宁在会上发言。接着，阿登纳和德意志联邦共和国外交部长勃伦塔诺相继发言。然后苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫和苏联外交部长莫洛托夫发言。随后德意志联邦共和国外交部副部长哈尔斯坦发言。
    会议在结束时一致认为，两国代表团就一般性问题交换意见已经结束。会议责成两国外交部长根据交换意见的结果草拟具体的建议。
