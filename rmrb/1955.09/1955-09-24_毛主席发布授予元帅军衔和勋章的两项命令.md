Title: Chairman Mao Issued Two Orders Conferring the Rank of Marshal and Medals

Author: 

Date: 1955-09-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, according to the decision of the 22nd Meeting of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, issued an order on September 23rd, conferring the military rank of Marshal of the People's Republic of China to Zhu De, Peng Dehuai, Lin Biao, Liu Bocheng, He Long, Chen Yi, Luo Ronghuan, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, and Ye Jianying.

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, according to the decision of the 22nd Meeting of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, issued an order on September 23rd, awarding the First Class August 1st Medal to Zhu De and 130 others, the First Class Independence and Freedom Medal to Zhu De and 116 others, and the First Class Liberation Medal to Zhu De and 569 others.

### 毛主席发布授予元帅军衔和勋章的两项命令

1955-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席发布授予元帅军衔和勋章的两项命令
    新华社二十三日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东根据中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十二次会议的决定，在九月二十三日发布命令，授予朱德、彭德怀、林彪、刘伯承、贺龙、陈毅、罗荣桓、徐向前、聂荣臻、叶剑英以中华人民共和国元帅军衔。
    新华社二十三日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东根据中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十二次会议的决定，在九月二十三日发布命令，授予朱德等一百三十一人以一级八一勋章，授予朱德等一百一十七人以一级独立自由勋章，授予朱德等五百七十人以一级解放勋章。
