Title: Order of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1955-09-01
Page: 3

    Order of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
    The "Provisional Regulations on Science Awards of the Chinese Academy of Sciences" and the "Provisional Regulations on Graduate Students of the Chinese Academy of Sciences" were adopted at the 17th Plenary Meeting of the State Council on August 5, 1955, and are hereby promulgated for implementation.

			Premier Zhou Enlai
			August 31, 1955
