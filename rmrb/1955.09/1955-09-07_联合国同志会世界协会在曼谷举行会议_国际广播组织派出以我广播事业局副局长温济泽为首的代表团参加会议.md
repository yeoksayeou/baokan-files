Title: United Nations Associations World Federation Holds Meeting in Bangkok; International Broadcasting Organization Sends Delegation Headed by Wen Jize, Deputy Director of Our Broadcasting Affairs Bureau, to Attend the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-09-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, September 6th: According to TASS, the 10th conference of the United Nations Associations World Federation opened in Bangkok on the 5th.

Attending the meeting were delegations from Australia, Austria, Belgium, Bulgaria, Cuba, Czechoslovakia, Denmark, Hungary, India, Indonesia, Iran, Israel, Italy, Japan, the Netherlands, New Zealand, Pakistan, the Philippines, Poland, Sweden, Switzerland, Thailand, the United Kingdom, the United States, Yugoslavia, Burma, and Ceylon. The Soviet Union's representative is participating in the meeting as an observer.

The opening of the conference was presided over by the Vice Chairman of the Federation's Executive Committee, Mr. Marburg.

During this conference, the Federation's various committees will discuss a number of reports, including reports on the Federation's support for the United Nations' work on the peaceful use of atomic energy, on the service of film, radio, and television to international understanding and cooperation, and on collective security, coexistence, and cooperation based on the United Nations Charter. The conference is scheduled to last until September 11th.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 6th: The Secretariat of the International Broadcasting Organization issued an announcement on the 5th stating that, at the invitation of the United Nations Associations World Federation, the International Broadcasting Organization has sent a delegation headed by Wen Jize, Deputy Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau of the People's Republic of China, to attend the Federation's 10th Plenary Conference being held in Bangkok, the capital of Thailand.

### 联合国同志会世界协会在曼谷举行会议  国际广播组织派出以我广播事业局副局长温济泽为首的代表团参加会议

1955-09-07
第4版()
专栏：

    联合国同志会世界协会在曼谷举行会议
    国际广播组织派出以我广播事业局副局长温济泽为首的代表团参加会议
    新华社六日讯　据塔斯社曼谷讯：联合国同志会世界协会第十届会议五日在曼谷开幕。
    出席会议的，有澳大利亚、奥地利、比利时、保加利亚、古巴、捷克斯洛伐克、丹麦、匈牙利、印度、印度尼西亚、伊朗、以色列、意大利、日本、荷兰、新西兰、巴基斯坦、菲律宾、波兰、瑞典、瑞士、泰国、英国、美国、南斯拉夫、缅甸、锡兰等国的代表团。苏联代表以观察员身份参加会议。
    会议由协会执行委员会副主席马尔布堡主持开幕。
    在本届会议期间，协会各委员会将讨论一些报告，其中包括关于协会支持联合国为和平利用原子能所做的工作的报告，关于电影、广播、电视为国际间的互相谅解和合作服务的报告以及关于以联合国宪章为基础的集体安全、共处和合作等问题的报告。会议要举行到九月十一日。
    新华社布拉格六日电　国际广播组织秘书处五日发表公告说，国际广播组织应联合国同志会世界协会的邀请，已派出以中华人民共和国广播事业局副局长温济泽为首的代表团，前往参加在泰国首都曼谷举行的协会第十届全体会议。
