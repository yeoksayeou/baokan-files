Title: Part of Japanese Business Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 17th—The first group of twenty-two members of the Japanese business delegation to China, invited by the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 17th, led by the delegation's Secretary-General, Minoru Shirozu.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Ji Chao-ding, Vice Chairman and Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Lu Xuzhang, member, Wen Shizhen, Deputy Secretary-General, and Japanese peace activist Togo Kameda, who is in Beijing.

Secretary-General Shirozu gave a speech in the airport lounge, talking about his desire to develop trade between Japan and China.

### 日工商界代表团部分人员到北京

1955-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    日工商界代表团部分人员到北京
    新华社十七日讯　应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请来华访问的日本工商界访华代表团，第一批二十二人在代表团秘书长白水实率领下，十七日下午到达北京。
  到机场欢迎的有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席兼秘书长冀朝鼎、委员卢绪章、副秘书长文士桢等，以及在北京的日本和平人士龟田东伍。
    代表团秘书长白水实在机场休息室发表了谈话，谈到发展日中两国贸易的愿望。
