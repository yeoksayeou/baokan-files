Title: Swedish Prime Minister to Visit Soviet Union Early Next Year
Author:
Date: 1955-09-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, September 11th: TASS Stockholm, September 11th: The Swedish Foreign Ministry issued a communique on the 9th stating that Prime Minister Erlander had accepted the Soviet government's invitation to visit the Soviet Union. He intends to visit the Soviet Union early next year. This visit will be of a non-official nature, and the Prime Minister will be accompanied by his wife.

### 瑞典首相将在明年初访苏

1955-09-12
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典首相将在明年初访苏
    新华社十一日讯　塔斯社斯德哥尔摩十一日讯：瑞典外交部在九日发表公报说，埃兰德首相接受了苏联政府的访苏邀请。他打算在明年初访问苏联。这次访问将是非官方性质的，首相将在他的夫人陪同下前往苏联访问。
