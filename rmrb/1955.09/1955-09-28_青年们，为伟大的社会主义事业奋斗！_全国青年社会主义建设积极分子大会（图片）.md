Title: Young People, Strive for the Great Socialist Cause! - National Congress of Active Young Builders of Socialism (Photos)
Author: 
Date: 1955-09-28
Page: 5

    Young People, Strive for the Great Socialist Cause!
    -- National Congress of Active Young Builders of Socialism
    The National Congress of Active Young Builders of Socialism opened on September 20. The 1,527 active young people participating in this congress are outstanding young men and women of all ethnic groups from all fronts across the country. This is the venue of the congress.
    During the congress, the active young people put forward many proposals. This is a scene of Zhao Guangrong, Wang Hengcheng, Wang Junfeng, and Li Chang, active young people on the coal mining industrial front, discussing and proposing a joint initiative to increase coal production and save pit props.
    Active young men and women from various fronts have come to Beijing. This is female pilot Luo Liefang of the "People No. 12" ship greeting other young friends enthusiastically after meeting with female aviator Wu Zhudi.
    Members of the Vietnamese youth delegation and active young people of the Korean ethnic group in China sign autographs for each other as mementos.
    The active young people view one of the gifts presented to the congress by the Soviet youth delegation – a model of an oil refinery.
    A group of active young women chat intimately.
    Active young members of the military attending the congress express their regards to Liao Yixun, a revolutionary disabled soldier and active youth who was wounded in the Korean War but still tenaciously persists in his work and studies. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Mei photo)
    The head of the Mongolian youth delegation, Zha Musurong (left), talks happily with young Mongolian people from China.
                (Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie photo)
    The Korean youth delegation presents the league flag of the Korean Democratic Youth League to the congress.
    Young people's lives are active. During breaks in the congress, they often sing and dance together.
    On the morning of September 25, all the active young people attending the congress participated in voluntary labor at Zizhuyuan in the western suburbs of Beijing. They spent only two hours eliminating weeds and potholes on more than 31,000 square meters of wasteland. (Photos on this page are by our reporters Gao Liang and Wang Zhiyuan, except where otherwise noted)

### 青年们，为伟大的社会主义事业奋斗！——全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会（图片）

1955-09-28
第5版()
专栏：

    青年们，为伟大的社会主义事业奋斗！
    ——全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会
    全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会于九月二十日开幕。参加这次大会的青年积极分子共一千五百二十七人，都是来自全国各个战线上的优秀的各族男女青年。这是大会会场。
    大会期间青年积极分子们提出了很多倡议。这是煤矿工业战线上的青年积极分子赵光荣、王恒成、王俊峰和李长等在一起研究提出增产煤炭节约坑木的联合倡议时的情形。
    各个不同战线上的男女青年积极分子都来到了北京。这是“人民十二号”轮船女驾驶员罗烈芳同女航空员伍竹迪会见后，在热烈地招呼其他的青年朋友们。
    越南青年代表团团员同我国朝鲜族青年积极分子在一起互相签名留念。
    青年积极分子们观看苏联青年代表团赠给大会的礼物之一——炼油厂模型。
    一群女青年积极分子在一起亲切地交谈。
    参加大会的部队青年积极分子慰问曾在朝鲜战争中负伤，但仍然顽强地坚持工作和学习的革命残废军人、青年积极分子廖贻训。　　	　（新华社记者章　梅摄）
    蒙古青年代表团团长札木苏荣（左）同我国蒙族青年在一起愉快地交谈。
                （新华社记者钱嗣杰摄）
    朝鲜青年代表团向大会赠送朝鲜民主青年同盟的盟旗。
    青年们的生活是活跃的，在大会休息时，他们常常在一起歌舞。
    九月二十五日上午，参加大会的全体青年积极分子都到北京西郊紫竹院参加义务劳动，他们仅仅用了两个小时就把三万一千多平方公尺荒地上的野草和土坑等消灭了。（本版照片除署名者外，均为本报记者高　粮、王志渊摄）
