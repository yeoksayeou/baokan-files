Title: Soviet Union and Federal Republic of Germany Delegations Begin Negotiations; Chancellor Adenauer Delivers Speech at the Airport Upon Arrival in Moscow
Author: 
Date: 1955-09-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 9th. TASS Moscow, September 9th: Negotiations between the Government of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and the Government Delegation of the Federal Republic of Germany began in Moscow at 11:00 AM this morning.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 8th. TASS Moscow, September 8th: Dr. Adenauer, Chancellor of the Federal Republic of Germany, who arrived in Moscow on the 8th, delivered the following speech at the airport:

    "Thank you, Mr. Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and thank you for your very kind words of welcome to me and our delegation.

    I sincerely hope that the first contact established as a result of our visit to Moscow will be the beginning of normal and good relations between the Soviet Union and Germany.

    I firmly believe that peaceful cooperation can bring many benefits to the people of the Soviet Union and the people of Germany, and that the normalization of our relations will largely determine the maintenance and consolidation of happiness and security in Europe and the world.

    We hope to dedicate all our work and talks in Moscow to the cause of peace." (Attached picture)
    
    Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Bulganin (first person on the left in the front row) shakes hands with Chancellor Adenauer upon welcoming him at the airport.
            TASS Moscow facsimile (Sent by Xinhua News Agency)

### 苏联同德意志联邦共和国代表团开始谈判  阿登纳总理到达莫斯科时在机场上发表谈话

1955-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联同德意志联邦共和国代表团开始谈判
    阿登纳总理到达莫斯科时在机场上发表谈话
    新华社九日讯　塔斯社莫斯科九日讯：苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府同德意志联邦共和国政府代表团九日上午十一时在莫斯科开始谈判。
    新华社九日讯　塔斯社莫斯科八日讯：八日到达莫斯科的德意志联邦共和国总理阿登纳博士，在机场上发表谈话如下：
    “感谢您，部长会议主席先生，感谢您在欢迎我和我们代表团时所说的非常亲切的一番话。
    我真诚希望，由于我们访问莫斯科而建立的第一次接触，将是苏德两国建立正常和良好的关系的开始。
    我坚决相信，和平合作能够给苏联人民和德国人民带来许多好处，维护和巩固欧洲以及全世界的幸福和安全在很大的程度上取决于我们关系的正常化。
    我们希望把我们在莫斯科的工作和会谈全部都贡献给和平的事业。”（附图片）
    苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁（前排左第一人）在机场欢迎阿登纳总理时和他握手
