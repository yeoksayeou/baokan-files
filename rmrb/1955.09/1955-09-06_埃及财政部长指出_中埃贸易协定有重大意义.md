Title: Egyptian Minister of Finance Points Out That Sino-Egyptian Trade Agreement Is of Great Significance
Author:
Date: 1955-09-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 5th. TASS, Cairo, September 5th: Egyptian Minister of Finance Dr. Kaissouni said at a press conference with foreign journalists: "The main purpose of the Egyptian government's economic policy is to improve the living conditions of the people." He said: "The average living standard in Egypt is very low. The national income is only 40 Egyptian pounds per person per year."
    Kaissouni said that the Egyptian government is formulating plans to develop the Egyptian economy, and the realization of these plans requires a large amount of investment.
    He emphasized that Egypt's economic situation has greatly improved in the past two or three years; the expansion of Egypt's trade links with Eastern European countries has played a very important role in this regard. Kaissouni pointed out that the trade agreement recently signed between Egypt and the People's Republic of China is of great significance, and he said that this agreement is "beneficial to Egypt."

### 埃及财政部长指出  中埃贸易协定有重大意义

1955-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    埃及财政部长指出
    中埃贸易协定有重大意义
    新华社五日讯　塔斯社开罗五日讯：埃及财政部长凯舒迪博士在外国记者招待会上说：“埃及政府经济政策的主要目的是改善人民的生活条件。”他说：“埃及的平均生活水平是十分低的。国民收入按人口计算每人一年只有四十埃镑。”
    凯舒迪说，埃及政府正在制定发展埃及经济的计划，这些计划的实现需要大量的投资。
    他着重指出，最近两三年来埃及的经济状况已经有了很大的改善；埃及同东欧国家的贸易联系的扩大在这一方面起了很重大的作用。凯舒迪指出，不久以前埃及同中华人民共和国签订的贸易协定是有重大意义的，他说，这一协定“对埃及有利”。
