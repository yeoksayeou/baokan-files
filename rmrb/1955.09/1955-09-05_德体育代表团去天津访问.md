Title: German Sports Delegation Visits Tianjin
Author: 
Date: 1955-09-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 4th. The entire delegation of the German Democratic Republic Sports Delegation, which was invited to visit China, left Beijing for Tianjin by train on the afternoon of the 4th. Thirteen Chinese male and female gymnasts accompanied them.
    During its stay in Beijing, the German Democratic Republic Sports Delegation not only gave four gymnastics performances, but also guided Chinese gymnasts in three joint practices. In addition, they visited the Temple of Heaven, the Forbidden City, and the Summer Palace; and toured the Central Institute of Physical Education, the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant, a suburban agricultural production cooperative, and the soon-to-be-completed Beijing Gymnasium.

### 德体育代表团去天津访问

1955-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    德体育代表团去天津访问
    新华社四日讯　应邀来中国访问的德意志民主共和国体育代表团全体人员，四日下午乘火车离北京去天津访问。十三名中国男、女体操运动员陪同前往。
    德意志民主共和国体育代表团在北京期间，除进行了四次体操表演外，还指导我国体操运动员进行了三次共同练习。此外，他们还游览了天坛、故宫和颐和园；参观了中央体育学院、石景山钢铁厂、郊区的农业生产合作社和即将落成的北京体育馆。
