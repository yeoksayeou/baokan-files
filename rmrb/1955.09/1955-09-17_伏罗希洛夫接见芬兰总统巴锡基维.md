Title: Voroshilov Receives Finnish President Paasikivi
Author:
Date: 1955-09-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 16th: According to TASS, on the morning of September 16th, the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Voroshilov, received the President of the Republic of Finland, Paasikivi, and his entourage in the Kremlin, Moscow. The entourage included Finnish Prime Minister U. Kekkonen, Minister of Defense E. Skog, former Foreign Minister Tuomioja, Director of the Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and State Secretary R. Seppälä, and Finnish Ambassador to the Soviet Union E. Vuori.
    Present at the reception were: Members of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Khrushchev, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR Pervukhin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR Semyonov, and Soviet Ambassador to Finland Lebedev, among others.
    During the reception, Voroshilov and Paasikivi exchanged views and decided to instruct the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR and the Finnish Prime Minister, with the participation of other representatives from both sides, to jointly consider issues concerning the further development and consolidation of the existing friendly relations between the two countries.
    The reception took place in an atmosphere of sincerity and mutual understanding.

### 伏罗希洛夫接见芬兰总统巴锡基维

1955-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫接见芬兰总统巴锡基维
    新华社十六日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科讯：九月十六日上午，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫在莫斯科克里姆林宫接见了芬兰共和国总统巴锡基维和他的随员芬兰总理乌·吉科宁、国防部长埃·斯科格、前外交部长斯汶托和外交部办公厅主任、国务秘书鲁·塞帕拉，以及芬兰驻苏联大使埃·沃里。
    接见时在座的有：苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫、苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长莫洛托夫、苏联部长会议第一副主席别尔乌辛，以及苏联外交部副部长谢苗诺夫、苏联驻芬兰大使列别杰夫等人。
    在接见时，伏罗希洛夫和巴锡基维交换了意见，决定指令苏联部长会议主席和芬兰总理在双方其他代表的参加下共同考虑关于进一步发展和巩固两国之间现有的友好关系的问题。
    接见是在诚恳和互相谅解的气氛中进行的。
