Title: Damascus International Fair Opens

Author:

Date: 1955-09-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, September 2nd: The Damascus International Fair opened on the afternoon of September 1st.

The opening ceremony was attended by Syrian President Atassi, Prime Minister Asali, cabinet ministers, representatives of participating countries, foreign diplomats and commercial counselors, and prominent figures from all walks of life.

Jiang Ming, head of the Chinese Exhibition Delegation, also attended the opening ceremony.

Syrian Minister of Economy, Kayali, said in his address at the opening ceremony that the fair is of great significance for expanding international trade relations and economic cooperation, and strengthening understanding and friendship between the people of all countries in the world. He also condemned colonialism and imperialism for hindering the development of backward countries. He said that among the factors hindering mankind from advancing towards the lofty ideal of a peaceful and stable life, most of them originate from the rule of colonialism and imperialism.

Noyrati, Director of the Fair, described the general situation of the fair. He said that a total of twenty-one countries are participating in the fair, including: Syria, Lebanon, China, Poland, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, Bulgaria, Indonesia, Pakistan, Egypt, Jordan, Japan, the Netherlands, etc.

The exhibition halls of some countries, including China, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, and Saudi Arabia, are larger than last year.

On the first day of the opening of the fair, thousands of Syrians visited the fair.

### 大马士革国际博览会举行开幕典礼

1955-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    大马士革国际博览会举行开幕典礼
    新华社大马士革二日电　大马士革国际博览会九月一日下午开幕。
    出席开幕典礼的有叙利亚总统阿塔西、总理阿萨利、内阁部长们、参加博览会的各国代表、各国外交使节和商务参赞以及各界著名人士。
    中国展览团团长江明也参加了开幕典礼。
    叙利亚经济部长卡雅利在开幕典礼上致词说，这个博览会对于扩展国际贸易关系和经济合作，加强世界各国人民之间的谅解和友谊是有巨大意义的。他还斥责了阻碍落后国家的发展的殖民主义和帝国主义。他说，在阻碍人类向和平、安定的生活这种崇高理想前进的种种因素中，大多数的因素都是导源于殖民主义和帝国主义的统治。
    博览会主任诺伊拉蒂叙述了博览会的概况。他说，参加博览会的共二十一个国家，其中包括：叙利亚、黎巴嫩、中国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利、保加利亚、印度尼西亚、巴基斯坦、埃及、约旦、日本、荷兰等。
    某些国家，其中包括中国、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利和沙特阿拉伯的展览馆都比去年为大。
    在博览会开幕的第一天有成千的叙利亚人参观了博览会。
