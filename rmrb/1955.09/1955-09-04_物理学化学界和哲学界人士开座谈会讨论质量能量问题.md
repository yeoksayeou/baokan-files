Title: Physicists, Chemists, and Philosophers Hold Symposium to Discuss Mass and Energy
Author:
Date: 1955-09-04
Page: 3

    The editorial departments of "Philosophical Research" and "Physics Bulletin" jointly held a symposium from August 26th to 28th, inviting physicists, chemists, and philosophers to discuss the issues of mass and energy. More than 30 physicists, chemists, and philosophers from the Institute of Physics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the Institute of Applied Physics, the Institute of Philosophy, Peking University, Tsinghua University, Renmin University of China, Hunan Normal College, and the Marxist-Leninist Academy participated in the symposium.

    The participating scientists explored the basic meaning of the concepts of mass and energy, and whether mass and energy can be used as measures of the quantity of matter and the quantity of motion. They also exchanged views on the relationship between the laws of conservation of mass, conservation of energy, and mass-energy equivalence in physics, and the principles of indestructibility of matter, indestructibility of motion, and the inseparability of matter and motion in philosophy. The speeches of the participants also touched upon a series of basic issues in philosophy and physics. For example, the relationship between philosophy and physics, the research objects and research methods of physics, and the relationship between moving mass and rest mass.

    At the symposium, philosophers and physicists repeatedly emphasized the necessity of cooperation between philosophers and physicists. Everyone agreed that it is necessary to explore the basic issues in physics and philosophy on the basis of the basic principles of dialectical materialism and the latest achievements of modern physics, and hoped that more such meetings could be held in the future to help everyone learn and research.

### 物理学化学界和哲学界人士开座谈会讨论质量能量问题

1955-09-04
第3版()
专栏：

    物理学化学界和哲学界人士开座谈会讨论质量能量问题
    八月二十六日到二十八日，“哲学研究”和“物理通报”的编辑部联合召开了一次座谈会，邀集物理学化学界和哲学界人士讨论质量、能量问题。参加座谈会的有中国科学院物理研究所、应用物理研究所、哲学研究所和北京大学、清华大学、中国人民大学、湖南师范学院、马克思列宁学院等单位的物理学家、化学家、哲学家三十多人。
    参加座谈会的科学家对于质量、能量概念的基本含义，质量、能量能否作为物质的量、运动的量的度量的问题进行了探讨；并且对于物理学上质量守恒、能量守恒、质能联系诸定律和哲学上物质不灭、运动不灭、物质和运动不可分割等原理之间的相互关系问题交换了意见。到会人士的发言还涉及了一系列的哲学和物理学的基本问题。例如，哲学和物理学的关系问题，物理学的研究对象和研究方法问题，运动质量和静止质量相互关系的问题等等。
    在座谈会上，哲学家、物理学家都反复强调了哲学家和物理学家共同合作的必要性，大家一致认为必须在辩证唯物主义的基本原理、现代物理学最新成就的基础之上来探讨物理学、哲学上的基本问题，并且希望在今后能多多举行这类会议来帮助大家学习和研究。
