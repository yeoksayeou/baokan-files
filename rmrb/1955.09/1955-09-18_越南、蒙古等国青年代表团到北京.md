Title: Youth Delegations from Vietnam, Mongolia, and Other Countries Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 17th: Youth delegations from Vietnam and Mongolia, invited by the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League to participate in the National Congress of Active Young Builders of Socialism, arrived in Beijing on the 17th.

    The Vietnamese youth delegation consists of five people, led by Huang Van Dinh, member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Vietnam National Salvation Youth Union, one of whom had already arrived in Beijing.

    The Mongolian youth delegation consists of five people, led by Jamtsorong, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League, three of whom arrived today.

    Luo Yi and Zhang Ze, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and youth representatives from Beijing went to welcome the two delegations.

    The Korean youth delegation, invited to participate in the National Congress of Active Young Builders of Socialism in China, consists of five people, headed by Kim Ki-soo, Vice Chairman of the Korean Democratic Youth League. They came to China after participating in the Fifth World Youth Festival.

### 越南、蒙古等国青年代表团到北京

1955-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    越南、蒙古等国青年代表团到北京
    新华社十七日讯  应中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会的邀请前来中国参加全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会的越南青年代表团和蒙古青年代表团在十七日分别到达北京。
    越南青年代表团共五人，由越南救国青年团中央常务委员会委员黄文鼎率领，其中一人已先到北京。
    蒙古青年代表团共五人，由蒙古革命青年团中央委员会书记扎木苏荣率领，今天已到三人。
    前往欢迎这两个代表团的有中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记罗毅、章泽和北京市青年代表。
    应邀前来中国参加全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会的朝鲜青年代表团共五人，团长是朝鲜民主青年同盟副委员长金基洙。他们已在参加第五届世界青年联欢节后来中国。
