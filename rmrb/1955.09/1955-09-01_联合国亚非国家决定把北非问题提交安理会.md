Title: UN Afro-Asian Nations Decide to Submit North African Issue to Security Council
Author: 
Date: 1955-09-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 31st - A meeting of representatives of the seventeen nations of the UN Afro-Asian group was held on the 30th to discuss the North African situation. According to a communique issued after the meeting, the meeting "heard statements from members (of the Afro-Asian group) expressing serious concern about the situation in North Africa." The meeting decided to submit the issue to the Security Council and unanimously agreed to establish a committee to "prepare to present the case."

### 联合国亚非国家决定把北非问题提交安理会

1955-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    联合国亚非国家决定把北非问题提交安理会
    新华社三十一日讯　纽约消息：联合国亚非集团的十七个国家的代表三十日举行会议，讨论北非局势问题。根据会后发表的公报，会议“听取了（亚非集团）成员们对于北非局势感到严重关怀的发言”。会议决定把这一问题提交安全理事会，并且一致同意建立一个委员会来“准备提出这一案件”。
