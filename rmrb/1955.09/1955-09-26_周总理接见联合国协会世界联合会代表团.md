Title: Premier Zhou Receives Delegation from the World Federation of United Nations Associations
Author:
Date: 1955-09-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th – Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, received the delegation from the World Federation of United Nations Associations visiting China at 4:30 PM on September 25th. The delegation was headed by Charles Judd (Chairman of the Executive Committee) and included member John A.F. Ennals (Secretary-General).

Present at the meeting were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Vice Presidents Hu Yuzhi and Qiao Guanhua; Secretary-General Wu Maosun; Deputy Director of the International Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Wen Jianping; and Deputy Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau, Wen Jize.

### 周总理接见联合国协会世界联合会代表团

1955-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见联合国协会世界联合会代表团
    新华社二十五日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在九月二十五日下午四时半接见了联合国协会世界联合会访华代表团团长查尔斯·贾德（执行委员会主席）、团员约翰·阿·弗·恩纳尔斯（秘书长）。
    在座的有，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，副会长胡愈之、乔冠华，秘书长吴茂荪，外交部国际司副司长温建平，广播事业局副局长温济泽。
