Title: Pakistan International Industrial Exposition Opens; Syrian President Receives Head of Chinese Delegation to Damascus International Exposition
Author:
Date: 1955-09-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, September 17th: According to the "Karachi Times," the Pakistan International Industrial Exposition opened in Karachi on the 16th.

Pakistan's Acting Governor-General Iskander Mirza stated in his opening address that the International Industrial Exposition will contribute to Pakistan's industrial development and promote cultural and friendly relations between Pakistan and other countries.

Thirteen countries, including the Soviet Union, China, India, the United States, the United Kingdom, Czechoslovakia, Yugoslavia, Japan, and France, participated in the exposition.

The Chinese exhibition hall covers an area of 52,000 square feet and is one of the largest exhibition halls in the exposition. The hall displays heavy machinery produced in New China, as well as products from large-scale industries and rural industries.

The exposition will last for one month.

Xinhua News Agency, September 19th: Damascus Dispatch: Syrian President Shukri al-Quwatli received Jiang Ming, head of the Chinese Council for the Promotion of International Trade's exhibition delegation to the Damascus International Exposition and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of China, on the 18th.

Quwatli expressed to Jiang Ming his hope that trade relations and friendly ties between Syria and China would be further developed.

### 巴基斯坦国际工业博览会开幕  叙利亚总统接见我国参加大马士革国际博览会团长

1955-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦国际工业博览会开幕
    叙利亚总统接见我国参加大马士革国际博览会团长
    新华社新德里十七日电　据“喀喇蚩时报”报道，巴基斯坦国际工业博览会十六日在喀喇蚩开幕。
    巴基斯坦代理总督伊斯坎德尔·米尔扎致开幕词说，国际工业博览会将有助于巴基斯坦的工业发展，以及促进巴基斯坦同其他国家的文化和友好关系。
    苏联、中国、印度、美国、英国、捷克斯洛伐克、南斯拉夫、日本和法国等十三个国家参加了博览会。
    中国展览馆占地五万二千平方英尺，是博览会中最大的展览馆之一。馆内陈列了新中国出产的重型机器以及大工业和乡村工业产品。
    博览会将举行一个月。
    新华社十九日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚总统舒克里·库瓦特利在十八日接见了中国国际贸易促进委员会参加大马士革国际博览会展览团团长、中国对外贸易部副部长江明。
    库瓦特利向江明表示，希望叙利亚和中国之间的贸易关系和友好联系将进一步发展。
