Title: Polish Coal Chemist Roga and Others Return Home
Author:
Date: 1955-09-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 5th. According to the 1955 execution plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Polish coal chemistry expert Professor B. Roga and Polish pianist Lei. Bakster have left Beijing on the 2nd to return to their country.

Professor Roga gave lectures and visited Beijing, Tangshan, Fushun, Anshan, and Dalian, introducing coal classification and methods of chemical processing of coal to China's coal industry.

Professor Bakster lectured at the Northeast Conservatory of Music in Shenyang.

### 波煤炭化学家罗加等回国

1955-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    波煤炭化学家罗加等回国
    新华社五日讯　根据中波文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划来中国讲学的波兰煤炭化学专家布·罗加教授和波兰钢琴家雷·巴克斯特已在二日离京返国。
    罗加教授在北京、唐山、抚顺、鞍山、大连各地进行了讲学和参观，向中国煤炭工业介绍了煤的分类和煤的化学加工的方法。
    巴克斯特教授曾在沈阳东北音乐专科学校讲学。
