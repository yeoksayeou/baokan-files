Title: The National Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front Holds Its First Meeting, Elects Leadership and Formulates Work Plan
Author: 
Date: 1955-09-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 12th: According to the Vietnam News Agency, the newly established National Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front held its first meeting in Hanoi on the morning of September 12th.

    The meeting unanimously elected Ton Duc Thang as Chairman of the Vietnam Fatherland Front. The meeting also elected 13 members of the Presidium of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, 17 members of the Standing Committee, and 8 secretaries of the Secretariat.

    After studying the documents adopted by the recent National Congress of the Vietnam National United Front, the meeting approved the work plan of the National Committee of the Fatherland Front from now until December. The plan includes the following four points: assisting various regions of Vietnam in organizing local National United Front congresses; formulating a plan to widely publicize the program and charter of the Fatherland Front among the people and assisting them in studying these documents; promoting the current political struggle in various parts of Vietnam; and mobilizing the people to complete the tasks of consolidating North Vietnam.

### 越南祖国战线全国委员会举行第一次会议  选出领导机构并制定工作计划

1955-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    越南祖国战线全国委员会举行第一次会议
    选出领导机构并制定工作计划
    新华社河内十二日电　据越南通讯社讯：新成立的越南祖国战线全国委员会十二日上午在河内举行第一次会议。
    会议一致选举孙德胜为越南祖国战线主席。会议还选出了越南祖国战线主席团的十三名委员、常务委员会的十七名委员和书记处的八名书记。
    会议在研究了最近越南民族统一战线全国大会所通过的文件以后，通过了祖国战线全国委员会从现在到十二月的工作计划。计划包括下列四点：帮助越南各地区组织当地的民族统一战线大会；制定在人民中广泛宣传祖国战线的纲领和章程并帮助他们学习这些文件的计划；推动目前越南各地的政治斗争；动员人民完成巩固北越的各项任务。
