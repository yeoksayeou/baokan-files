Title: Soong Ching Ling Hosts Banquet for Soviet Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-09-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. Soong Ching Ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, held a banquet at the Beijing Hotel on the evening of the 28th to welcome all members of the Soviet Cultural Delegation headed by Alexei Surkov, First Secretary of the Secretariat of the Soviet Writers' Union. Among those present were Wu Yuzhang, Shen Junru, Li Dequan, Ma Yinchu, and Shao Lizi, Vice Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Qian Junrui, Secretary-General; Lao She and Cao Jinghua, Vice Presidents of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Mao Dun, Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association; Yang Hansheng, Secretary-General of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; Xu Guangping and Shi Liang, Vice Presidents of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; and more than fifty well-known scholars, writers, and educators from our country. He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, Wang Kunlun, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and Romaidzin, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, also attended the banquet. At the banquet, Soong Ching Ling and Surkov delivered speeches successively. The hosts and guests repeatedly raised their glasses in a toast to the increasing cultural exchange between China and the Soviet Union and to the unbreakable friendship between the peoples of the two countries.

### 宋庆龄设宴招待苏联文化代表团

1955-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄设宴招待苏联文化代表团
    新华社二十九日讯　中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄二十八日晚在北京饭店举行宴会，欢迎以苏联作家协会书记处第一书记苏尔科夫为首的苏联文化代表团全体团员。出席作陪的有中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章、沈钧儒、李德全、马寅初、邵力子，秘书长钱俊瑞，北京市中苏友好协会副会长老舍、曹靖华，中国作家协会主席茅盾、中国文学艺术界联合会秘书长阳翰笙、中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平、史良，以及我国著名的学者、作家、教育家五十余人。外交部部长助理何伟，北京市副市长王昆仑和苏联驻华大使馆临时代办罗迈进也参加了宴会。宴会上，宋庆龄和苏尔科夫曾先后致词。宾主频频举杯，为中苏两国文化交流的日益增强，为中苏两国人民之间的牢不可破的友谊干杯。
