Title: Egypt and Israel Accept Ceasefire Proposal
Author:
Date: 1955-09-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 1st.—The Egyptian authorities announced on August 30th that the Egyptian government had accepted the ceasefire proposal put forward by the United Nations Palestine Truce Supervision Organization regarding the implementation of a ceasefire along the Egyptian-Israeli front near Gaza.
    Prior to this, military conflicts had occurred continuously for six days along the Gaza Strip.
    Meanwhile, an Israeli Foreign Ministry spokesperson announced on August 31st that the Israeli government had accepted the United Nations Palestine Truce Supervision Organization's proposal to halt the Israeli-Egyptian military conflict.

### 埃及和以色列接受停火建议

1955-09-03
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及和以色列接受停火建议
    新华社一日讯　开罗消息：埃及当局八月三十日宣布，埃及政府已经接受了联合国巴勒斯坦停战监察机构提出的沿着加沙附近埃及和以色列前线实行停火的建议。
    在这以前，沿着加沙地区曾连续六天发生军事冲突。
    同时以色列外交部发言人八月三十一日宣布，以色列政府已经接受了联合国巴勒斯坦停战监察机构提出的停止以色列—埃及军事冲突的建议。
