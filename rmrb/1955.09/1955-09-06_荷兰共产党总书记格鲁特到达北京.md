Title: Dutch Communist Party General Secretary de Groot Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 5th: Paul de Groot, General Secretary of the Communist Party of the Netherlands, arrived in Beijing by plane on September 5th with his wife to visit China.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included: Liu Shaoqi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Yang Shangkun, Deputy Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; and Zhao Yimin, Li Chuli, and Li Qixin, responsible staff members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

### 荷兰共产党总书记格鲁特到达北京

1955-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    荷兰共产党总书记格鲁特到达北京
    新华社五日讯　荷兰共产党总书记保罗·德·格鲁特同他的夫人前来访问中国，在九月五日乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有：中共中央书记处书记刘少奇，中共中央副秘书长杨尚昆，中共中央负责工作人员赵毅敏、李初梨、李启新等。
