Title: Exchange of Letters between Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister and East German Foreign Minister on Agreement on Traffic Issues between East and West Germany
Author:
Date: 1955-09-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 20th (TASS dispatch): Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Zorin and German Democratic Republic Foreign Minister Bolz exchanged letters regarding the agreement reached between the Soviet government and the German Democratic Republic government during the current negotiations on traffic issues between the German Democratic Republic and the Federal Republic of Germany.

The agreement stipulates: The German Democratic Republic is responsible for the protection and control along the borders of the German Democratic Republic, the demarcation line between the German Democratic Republic and the Federal Republic of Germany, the outer perimeter of Greater Berlin, within the city of Berlin, and the transportation routes within the German Democratic Republic between the Federal Republic of Germany and West Berlin.

The issuance and formulation of ship documents for vessels navigating the inland waterways of the German Democratic Republic fall entirely under the jurisdiction of the authorities of the German Democratic Republic.

Until a corresponding agreement is reached, the control of the movement of military personnel and goods of the French, British, and American garrison forces stationed in West Berlin between the Federal Republic of Germany and West Berlin will be temporarily carried out by the Headquarters of the Soviet Forces in Germany. In accordance with the currently valid Four-Power decisions, the aforementioned personnel and goods are permitted to pass along designated routes.

### 苏联外交部副部长和民主德国外交部长  就东西德间的交通等问题的协议互换信件

1955-09-22
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联外交部副部长和民主德国外交部长
    就东西德间的交通等问题的协议互换信件
    新华社二十一日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科二十日讯：苏联外交部副部长佐林和德意志民主共和国外交部长博尔茨就苏联政府和德意志民主共和国政府在目前的谈判中关于德意志民主共和国和德意志联邦共和国间的交通等问题取得的协议交换了信件。
    协议规定：德意志民主共和国沿着德意志民主共和国的边境、德意志民主共和国和德意志联邦共和国间的分界线、大柏林的外围、柏林市内以及德意志民主共和国境内的德意志联邦共和国同西柏林之间的交通线进行保护和管制。
    对在德意志民主共和国内河航行的船只证件的签发和制定等工作，完全属于德意志民主共和国机关的权限。
    对驻扎在西柏林的法国、英国和美国驻防军的军事人员和货物在德意志联邦共和国同西柏林间来往的管制，在达成相应协议以前暂由驻德苏军司令部进行。根据目前有效的四国决议，允许上述人员和货物沿规定的路线通行。
