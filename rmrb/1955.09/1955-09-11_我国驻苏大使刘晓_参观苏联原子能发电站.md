Title: Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Liu Xiao Visits Soviet Atomic Power Station
Author:
Date: 1955-09-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th. Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Liu Xiao, visited the Soviet Atomic Power Station on September 7th.

1955-09-11
第4版()
专栏：

    我国驻苏大使刘晓
    参观苏联原子能发电站
