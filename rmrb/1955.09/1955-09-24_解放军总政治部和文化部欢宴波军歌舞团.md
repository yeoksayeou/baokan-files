Title: PLA General Political Department and Ministry of Culture Host Banquet for Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble
Author:
Date: 1955-09-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: The General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China jointly held a banquet on the evening of the 23rd to welcome the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble, headed by Lieutenant Colonel Wolwa, Colonel Latkowski, and Colonel Ruda. Polish Ambassador to China, Kirilok, was also invited to attend the banquet.

    Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Qian Junrui, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, delivered speeches successively, warmly welcoming the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble to visit and perform in China. They expressed their belief that the song and dance ensemble's visit to China at this invitation will strengthen cultural exchanges between China and Poland and the fraternal friendship between the two peoples, and wished all comrades of the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble good health and successful performances.

    Lieutenant Colonel Wolwa, representative of the General Political Department of the Polish Army and Minister of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Polish Army, and Colonel Latkowski, head of the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble, then delivered speeches in reply, representing the song and dance ensemble in thanking them for their warm reception, and congratulating the further development of the unbreakable great friendship between the people and armies of China and Poland.

    Polish Ambassador to China, Kirilok, also delivered a speech at the banquet, wishing further strengthening of cooperation between China and Poland in the cultural and economic fields.

    Attending tonight's banquet were Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, Li Da, Deputy Minister of National Defense, Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, senior generals Peng Shaohui, Liu Yalou, Wang Hongkun, Su Zhenhua, Yang Zhicheng, Qiu Chuangcheng, Xiao Xiangrong, Liu Zhijian, Chen Yi, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs He Wei, Vice Mayor of Beijing Feng Jiping, and more than 300 well-known figures from the capital's literary and art circles.

### 解放军总政治部和文化部欢宴波军歌舞团

1955-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    解放军总政治部和文化部欢宴波军歌舞团
    新华社二十三日讯　中国人民解放军总政治部和中华人民共和国文化部在二十三日晚联合举行宴会，欢迎以沃尔瓦中校、拉特科夫斯基上校、鲁达上校为首的波兰军队歌舞团。波兰人民共和国驻华大使基里洛克也应邀出席了宴会。
    中国人民解放军总政治部副主任萧华、中华人民共和国文化部副部长钱俊瑞相继致词，热烈地欢迎波兰军队歌舞团来中国访问演出。他们表示相信歌舞团这次应邀访问我国，将会加强中波两国的文化交流和两国人民之间的兄弟友谊，并且祝波兰军队歌舞团全体同志的健康和演出成功。
    波兰军队总政治部代表，波兰军队总政治部文化部部长沃尔瓦中校、波兰军队歌舞团团长拉特科夫斯基上校接着致答词，代表歌舞团感谢对他们的亲切的招待，祝贺中波两国人民及其军队的牢不可破的伟大友谊获得进一步发展。
    波兰驻华大使基里洛克也在宴会上致词，祝中波两国文化经济方面合作的进一步加强。
    出席今晚宴会的，有国务院秘书长习仲勋，国防部副部长李达，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊、高级将领彭绍辉、刘亚楼、王宏坤、苏振华、杨志诚、邱创成、萧向荣、刘志坚、陈沂，外交部部长助理何伟，北京市副市长冯基平，首都文艺界著名人士等三百多人。
