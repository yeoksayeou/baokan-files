Title: Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League Holds Plenary Session Decides to Convene National Congress of the Youth League Suggests Changing the Name of the League to the Communist Youth League of China
Author:
Date: 1955-09-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Third Plenary Session of the Second Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League was held on the 18th.

The meeting discussed the "Resolution on Convening the Third National Congress of the Youth League and Suggesting Changing the Name of the China New Democratic Youth League to the Communist Youth League of China." The resolution was unanimously approved with warm applause from the audience. The meeting considered the change of the League's name to be a major event in the political life of the entire League and the youth of the country. The meeting called on the League organizations across the country and all League members to earnestly discuss this resolution, enhance their political awareness, study diligently, work actively, and more consciously shoulder the responsibility of building socialism.

After discussion, the meeting approved the report to be delivered by Hu Yaobang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, on behalf of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League to the National Activists' Congress for Socialist Construction of Youth.

Seventy-nine members of the Central Committee of the Youth League and thirty-one alternate members of the Central Committee attended the meeting.

### 中国新民主主义青年团中央举行全体会议  决定召开青年团全国代表大会  建议改团的名称为中国共产主义青年团

1955-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中国新民主主义青年团中央举行全体会议
    决定召开青年团全国代表大会
    建议改团的名称为中国共产主义青年团
    新华社十八日讯　中国新民主主义青年团第二届中央委员会在十八日举行了第三次全体会议。
    会议讨论了“关于召开青年团第三次全国代表大会和建议更改中国新民主主义青年团的名称为中国共产主义青年团的决议”。全场以热烈的掌声一致通过了这一决议。会议认为更改团的名称，是全团和全国青年政治生活中的一件大事。会议要求全国团的组织和全体团员认真讨论这一决议，提高自己的政治觉悟，努力学习，积极工作，更加自觉地承担起建设社会主义的责任。
    会议经过讨论，通过了将由中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦代表中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会向全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会所作的报告。
    出席这次会议的有青年团中央委员七十九人，候补中央委员三十一人。
