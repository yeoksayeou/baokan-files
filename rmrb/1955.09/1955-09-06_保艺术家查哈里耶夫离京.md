Title: Bulgarian Artist Zakhariiev Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 5th. Bulgarian ancient artist Vasil Zakhariiev, who came to China for a visit according to the 1955 execution plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, visited and conducted professional activities in Beijing from August 23rd to September 3rd. On the 4th, he left Beijing to visit other cities.

### 保艺术家查哈里耶夫离京

1955-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    保艺术家查哈里耶夫离京
    新华社五日讯　根据中保文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划来中国访问的保加利亚古代艺术家瓦西尔·查哈里耶夫，从八月二十三日到九月三日在北京进行访问和专业活动后，在四日离京赴其他城市访问。
