Title: Youth Representatives from Fifteen Countries Arrive in Beijing One After Another
Author:
Date: 1955-09-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 3rd News: On the afternoon of September 2nd, another group of youth representatives from various countries who were invited by the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth to visit China after attending the Fifth World Youth Festival arrived in Beijing. This group of 26 representatives came from seven countries: Egypt, Iraq, Jordan, Lebanon, Senegal, Sudan, and Syria.

    Responsible persons from the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, and more than one hundred young people from Beijing welcomed them at the train station.

    On the same afternoon, at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, the head of the Korean youth delegation to China after attending the Fifth World Youth Festival, Kim Ki-soo, vice chairman of the Korean Democratic Youth League, and three others also arrived in Beijing by plane. Luo Yi and Hu Keshi, secretaries of the Central Committee of the Youth League, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

    Xinhua News Agency, September 5th News: On the evening of the 5th, another group of 264 youth representatives from eight countries arrived in Beijing by special train and received a warm welcome from more than one thousand youth representatives from all walks of life in Beijing.

    This is the third and largest group of youth representatives from various countries to come to Beijing after attending the Fifth World Youth Festival. They are from North Korea, Vietnam, India, Japan, Cambodia, Laos, Ceylon, and Australia. Some of them were invited by the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth to visit China and will participate in the National Day celebrations, while others will pass through China for a short visit before returning home.

    Among the youth representatives from various countries, many are famous youth artists and youth representatives from all walks of life in their respective countries. Among the Korean representatives, there are heads of central and local youth organizations, football teams, men's and women's basketball teams, and women's volleyball teams. The Vietnamese delegation includes many well-known youth workers and outstanding artists. Among the Indian representatives, there are winners of the World Youth Festival, provincial legislators and youth representatives from various parties, dancers, folk singers, ball game players, music critics, and sports referees. Among the Japanese representatives, there are also atomic bomb victims from Hiroshima, members of the Central Chorus, and famous singers, as well as heads of the youth division of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan and the Japan Youth League Council, and local youth organizations.

    As the special train carrying the guests slowly entered the station, the band played "March of the World Democratic Youth", and the station was filled with stormy applause and cheers. As soon as the guests got off the train, they were warmly surrounded by Chinese youth and received flowers from a group of children. Youth representatives from various countries and Chinese youth representatives warmly shook hands, hugged, and shouted "Long live peace!" in different languages, like friends who had been separated for a long time. Responding to the joyful music, they happily danced and sang. Then they walked out of the station hand in hand, singing and cheering.

    Those who came to the station to welcome them tonight included responsible persons from the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, the All-China Student Federation, and the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, as well as youth representatives from all walks of life in the capital.

### 十五个国家的青年代表陆续到达北京

1955-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    十五个国家的青年代表陆续到达北京
    新华社三日讯　九月二日下午，又有一批在参加第五届世界青年联欢节以后应中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会和中华全国民主青年联合会邀请来中国访问的各国青年代表抵达北京。这批代表共二十六人，他们来自埃及、伊拉克、约旦、黎巴嫩、塞内加尔、苏丹、叙利亚七个国家。
    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国民主青年联合会的负责人和北京市一百余名青年到车站欢迎。
    同日下午，应中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会邀请，在参加第五届世界青年联欢节后来中国访问的朝鲜青年代表团团长、朝鲜民主青年同盟副委员长金基洙等四人，也乘飞机到达北京。青年团中央书记处书记罗毅、胡克实等前往机场欢迎。
    新华社五日讯　五日晚，又有一批来自八个国家的青年代表二百六十四人，乘专车到达北京，受到了北京各界青年代表一千多人的热情欢迎。
    这是在参加第五届世界青年联欢节以后第三批，也是人数最多一批的各国青年代表来到北京。他们是来自朝鲜、越南、印度、日本、高棉、寮国、锡兰和澳大利亚等国，他们中有应中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会和中华全国民主青年联合会的邀请，来中国访问并将参加国庆节观礼的，也有经过中国并作短期访问后就将返国的。
    在各国青年代表中，有许多是各国著名的青年艺术家和各界青年代表人物。在朝鲜代表中，有中央和地方的青年团体的负责人，有足球队、男女篮球队和女子排球队。越南代表包括了许多著名的青年工作者和优秀的艺术工作者。印度代表中，有世界青年联欢节的得奖者，有省议员和各党派的青年代表，有舞蹈家、民歌歌唱家、球赛选手、音乐评论员和体育裁判员。在日本代表中，还有来自广岛的原子弹受害者，有中央合唱团团员们和著名的歌唱家，也有日本“总评”青年部和日本青年团协议会以及各地方青年团体的负责人。
    当载着客人的专车徐徐进入车站时，乐队奏起了“世界民主青年进行曲”，车站上响起了暴风雨般的掌声和欢呼声。客人们一走下火车，就被中国青年们亲热地团团围住，并接受了一群少年儿童的献花。各国青年代表们和中国青年代表们像久别重逢的朋友一样，亲切地握手、拥抱、用不同的语言高呼“和平万岁”！并应和着欢乐的乐曲，愉快地跳了起来和唱了起来。然后他们手挽着手、唱着、欢呼着，一同走出车站。
    今晚到车站欢迎的，有中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国民主青年联合会、中华全国学生联合会和中国新民主主义青年团北京市委员会的负责人，以及首都各界青年代表等。
