Title: Efforts to Expand the Peace Zone (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-09-03
Page: 5

    In June 1954, Premier Zhou Enlai of China, at the invitation of Indian Prime Minister Nehru and Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, visited India and Burma successively, and held talks with the two Prime Ministers respectively. The joint statements issued by the Chinese and Indian Prime Ministers and the Chinese and Burmese Prime Ministers have had a broad impact on international relations in Asia and worldwide. The picture shows Premier Zhou Enlai in cordial conversation with Indian Prime Minister Nehru and Burmese Prime Minister U Nu during the Asian-African Conference.
    The Asian Countries Conference was held in New Delhi, the capital of India, from April 6 to 10. Representatives and observers from sixteen countries, including Burma, the People's Republic of China, India, Japan, and Mongolia, attended the meeting. The meeting adopted resolutions on political, cultural, scientific, economic and social issues. The meeting also adopted an appeal for cooperation for peace by religious believers in Asian countries. The picture shows Guo Moruo, head of the Chinese delegation, speaking at the opening session.
    The achievements of the historic Asian-African Conference expressed the common aspirations of the people of Asian and African countries to oppose colonialism, safeguard world peace, and promote friendly cooperation. The picture shows Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, speaking at the conference.

### 为扩大和平地区而努力（图片）

1955-09-03
第5版()
专栏：

    为扩大和平地区而努力
    我国周恩来总理在一九五四年六月间应印度总理尼赫鲁和缅甸总理吴努的邀请，先后访问了印度和缅甸，并和两国总理分别举行了会谈。中印两国总理、中缅两国总理发表的联合声明，在亚洲及全世界的国际关系中发生了广泛的影响。图为在亚非会议期间周恩来总理同印度总理尼赫鲁、缅甸总理吴努亲切地交谈。
    亚洲国家会议从四月六日到十日在印度首都德里召开。出席会议的有缅甸、中华人民共和国、印度、日本、蒙古等十六个国家的代表和观察员。会议通过了关于政治问题、文化问题、科学问题、经济问题和社会问题的决议。会议还通过关于亚洲国家的宗教教徒为和平而进行合作的呼吁书。图为在开幕大会上，我国代表团团长郭沫若发言。
    具有历史意义的亚非会议所取得的成果，表达了亚非国家的人民反对殖民主义、维护世界和平、增进友好合作的共同愿望。图为中华人民共和国总理兼外交部长周恩来在大会上发言。
