Title: Soviet Youth Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. At the invitation of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, the Soviet youth delegation, which came to China to participate in the National Youth Socialist Construction Activists Conference, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 18th. The delegation is headed by Zarubin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, with a total of twenty people; nineteen arrived today.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Hu Yaobang, Luo Yi, Zhang Ze, Hu Keshi, and Qu Tangliang, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, and some young activists attending the National Youth Socialist Construction Activists Conference, as well as youth representatives from Beijing.

Huang Wen Ding, head of the Vietnamese youth delegation, and Zhamusu Rong, head of the Mongolian youth delegation, as well as members of these two delegations, also went to the airport to welcome them.

When the delegation members stepped out of the plane, the welcomers swarmed forward. Hu Yaobang said in the crowd: We warmly welcome the Soviet youth delegation to China to participate in the National Youth Socialist Construction Activists Conference. Zarubin, the head of the delegation, said to the welcomers: We thank our Chinese youth friends for their warm welcome. On behalf of the members of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League and Soviet youth, I congratulate the Chinese youth on achieving greater success in socialist construction. (Attached picture)

### 苏联青年代表团到北京

1955-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联青年代表团到北京
    新华社十八日讯　应中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会的邀请前来中国参加全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会的苏联青年代表团，在十八日下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团由苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记扎鲁日尼担任团长，共二十人，今天到十九人。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦、罗毅、章泽、胡克实、区棠亮和一部分出席全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会的青年积极分子，以及北京市青年代表。
    越南青年代表团团长黄文鼎和蒙古青年代表团团长扎木苏荣，以及这两个代表团的团员也到机场欢迎。
    当代表团人员走出飞机时，欢迎的人簇拥而上。胡耀邦在人群中说：我们热烈欢迎苏联青年代表团到中国参加全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会。代表团团长扎鲁日尼向欢迎的人说：我们感谢中国青年朋友的热情欢迎，我代表苏联列宁共产主义青年团团员和苏联青年祝贺中国青年在社会主义建设中得到更大的成就。（附图片）
