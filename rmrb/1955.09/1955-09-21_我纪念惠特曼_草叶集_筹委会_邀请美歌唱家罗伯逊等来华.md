Title: The Preparatory Committee for the Commemoration of Whitman's "Leaves of Grass" Invites American Singer Robeson and Others to China
Author:
Date: 1955-09-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th. The Chinese Preparatory Committee for the Commemoration of Whitman's "Leaves of Grass" has sent a telegram inviting American singer Paul Robeson, writer Waldo Frank, novelist William Faulkner, female writer Pearl Buck, poet Carl Sandburg, and literary critic Samuel Sillen to come to China to participate in the commemorative meeting and visit China. The centennial commemoration of the first edition of American writer Whitman's "Leaves of Grass" will be held in Beijing in October this year.

### 我纪念惠特曼“草叶集”筹委会  邀请美歌唱家罗伯逊等来华

1955-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我纪念惠特曼“草叶集”筹委会
    邀请美歌唱家罗伯逊等来华
    新华社二十日讯　今年十月间将在北京举行美国作家惠特曼“草叶集”初版一百周年纪念会，中国纪念惠特曼“草叶集”筹备委员会已发电邀请美国歌唱家保罗·罗伯逊、作家华尔多·弗兰克、小说家威廉·福克纳、女作家赛珍珠、诗人卡尔·桑伯格和文学评论家塞缪尔·西伦等六人前来中国参加纪念会并在中国参观访问。
