Title: Eighth International Conference of Young Sinologists Held in the Netherlands
Author:
Date: 1955-09-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Leiden, September 2nd: The Eighth International Conference of Young Sinologists has been held in Leiden, Netherlands. About one hundred representatives from the People's Republic of China, the Soviet Union, Indonesia, the United States, the United Kingdom, the Federal Republic of Germany, the German Democratic Republic, the Netherlands, Switzerland, Ireland, and Italy participated in the conference.

The International Conference of Young Sinologists has been held annually since 1948. This conference was initiated by the Dutch Sinological Institute with the aim of exchanging views on certain aspects of Chinese history and philosophy.

The representatives have presented many reports, which have aroused enthusiastic discussions. Many papers presented at the conference proved that there is a growing desire among Western European scientists to study modern China.

The reports and question-and-answer sessions by Jian Bozan, Director of the History Department of Peking University, and Zhou Yiliang, Professor of the History Department, greatly helped the conference representatives in their study of the culture of the People's Republic of China.

### 第八届国际青年汉学家会议在荷兰举行

1955-09-05
第4版()
专栏：

    第八届国际青年汉学家会议在荷兰举行
    新华社三日讯　据塔斯社莱顿二日讯：第八届国际青年汉学家会议已经在荷兰的莱顿举行。参加会议的有来自中华人民共和国、苏联、印度尼西亚、美国、英国、德意志联邦共和国、德意志民主共和国、荷兰、瑞士、爱尔兰和意大利的约一百名代表。
    国际青年汉学家会议自一九四八年以来每年举行一次。这次会议是由荷兰汉学研究院创议召开的，目的是交换有关中国历史和哲学的某些方面的意见。
    代表们已经发表了许多报告，这些报告引起了热烈的讨论。在会上发表的许多论文证明，西欧科学家中间有一种日渐增长的研究现代中国的愿望。
    北京大学历史系主任翦伯赞和历史系教授周一良的报告以及问题解答大大帮助了大会代表们研究中华人民共和国的文化。
