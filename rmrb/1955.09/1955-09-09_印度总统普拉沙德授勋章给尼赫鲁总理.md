Title: Indian President Prasad Awards Medal to Prime Minister Nehru
Author:
Date: 1955-09-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 8th. According to the Indian Information Bureau: Indian President Prasad, on the evening of September 7th, at an award ceremony held at the Indian Presidential Palace, awarded Indian Prime Minister Nehru the highest Indian medal—the Jewel of India Medal—to commend Nehru's lifelong service to the country and his efforts for the cause of human peace.

### 印度总统普拉沙德授勋章给尼赫鲁总理

1955-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    印度总统普拉沙德授勋章给尼赫鲁总理
    新华社新德里八日电  据印度新闻处消息：印度总统普拉沙德在九月七日晚间，在印度总统府举行的授勋典礼上授予印度总理尼赫鲁以印度最高勋章——印度宝石勋章，以奖励尼赫鲁一生为国家服务，并且为人类和平事业所作的努力。
