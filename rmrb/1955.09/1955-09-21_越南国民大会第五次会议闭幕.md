Title: Fifth Session of the Vietnamese National Assembly Closes
Author: Zhan Chao
Date: 1955-09-21
Page: 4

    Fifth Session of the Vietnamese National Assembly Closes
    (Our Hanoi dispatch, September 20th) The Fifth Session of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam completed all its agenda items and closed on the 20th.
    The Fifth Session of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam mainly listened to and approved the political and economic work report made by Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong on behalf of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The meeting unanimously agreed that the guidelines and policies for the current struggle proposed by the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the people of the whole country in the spirit of the program of the Vietnam Fatherland Front are timely and in line with the general aspirations of the people of Vietnam.
    The Fifth Session of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam also stipulated the two major tasks of the Vietnamese people at present: First, to continue to correctly implement the Geneva Accords, expand and consolidate the great unity of the whole nation, and realize the national reunification of Vietnam peacefully on the basis of independence and democracy; Second, to actively restore the economy, consolidate North Vietnam, and make it a strong democratic base for consolidating peace and realizing unification. These two major tasks are inseparable and interconnected. The meeting called on the people of Vietnam to actively strive to realize the glorious tasks at present and to complete the sacred cause of peace, unification, independence, democracy, prosperity and strength of the motherland.
    The Fifth Session of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam also adopted specific plans as the basis for the Vietnamese people to realize their current struggle tasks. In order to peacefully unify the country on the basis of independence and democracy, the meeting affirmed that it is necessary to take care of the actual situation in both the North and South of Vietnam, convene consultative meetings in accordance with the Geneva Accords, and prepare to establish a "Coalition Government" including all strata, nationalities, parties, groups, religions, and even opposing groups through free general elections with universal, equal, direct, and secret voting, and allow local regulations to be formulated according to the special circumstances of the locality without violating the general laws of the country. In order to consolidate the North as a strong democratic base for the unified motherland, the meeting determined that it is necessary to actively promote the land reform movement, heal the wounds of war, strive to complete the two-year plan for restoring the national economy, raise agricultural production to the pre-war level of 1939, and at the same time, while focusing on restoring agricultural production, correspondingly restore and develop handicrafts, small industry, industry, transportation and commerce, and strive to increase income, reduce expenditure, stabilize prices, and gradually improve people's living standards.
    In order to lead the Vietnamese people to realize the glorious and arduous tasks at present, the Fifth Session of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam believes that it is necessary to adjust and strengthen the leadership structure of the government. The meeting unanimously approved the proposal of President Ho Chi Minh of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to appoint Pham Van Dong as Prime Minister of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Phan Ke Toai as Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of the Interior, and Vo Nguyen Giap as Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Defense. In order to meet the needs of economic recovery work, the meeting also unanimously approved President Ho Chi Minh's proposal to increase the original Ministry of Industry and Commerce and the Ministry of Transportation and Public Works in the government to four ministries: the Ministry of Industry, the Ministry of Commerce, the Ministry of Transportation, and the Ministry of Engineering and Water Conservancy. In addition, a number of deputy ministers and leaders of other ministries were added.
    The Fifth Session of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam firmly believes that under the correct leadership of President Ho Chi Minh, the Vietnam Workers' Party, and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Vietnamese people will bravely advance and victoriously complete the glorious tasks at present.　　　　　　　　(Zhan Chao)

### 越南国民大会第五次会议闭幕
展潮
1955-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

    越南国民大会第五次会议闭幕
    本报河内二十日电　越南民主共和国国民大会第五次会议完成了全部议程，在二十日闭幕。
    越南民主共和国国民大会第五次会议，主要听取和通过范文同副总理代表越南民主共和国政府所作的政治和经济工作报告。会议一致认为：越南民主共和国政府依据越南祖国战线纲领的精神向全国人民提出目前斗争的方针路线，是适时的，是符合越南全国人民的普遍愿望的。
    越南民主共和国国民大会第五次会议并规定了越南人民当前的两大任务：一、继续正确履行日内瓦协定，扩大和巩固全民大团结，在独立和民主的基础上通过和平方式来实现越南全国的统一；二、积极恢复经济，巩固越南北方，使其成为巩固和平实现统一的强大的民主基地。这两大任务是不可分割的、互相联系的。会议号召越南全国人民积极努力，为实现当前的光荣任务，为完成祖国的和平、统一、独立、民主和富强的神圣事业而奋斗。
    越南民主共和国国民大会第五次会议还通过各项具体方案，作为越南人民实现当前斗争任务的依据。为了在独立和民主的基础上和平统一全国，会议确认必须照顾目前越南南北两方实际情况，按照日内瓦协定召开协商会议，准备通过用广泛、平等、直接和秘密投票的自由普选，成立包括各阶层、各民族、各党派、各团体、各宗教以至各对立集团的“联合政府”，并允许在不违背国家总法律下可以依据地方的特殊情况制定地方法令。为了巩固作为统一祖国的强大的民主基地的北方，会议认定必须积极推进土地改革运动、医治战争创伤，争取完成恢复国民经济的两年计划，把农业生产提高到一九三九年战前的水平，同时在以恢复农业生产为主时相应地恢复和发展手工业、小工业、工业、交通运输业和商业，努力做到增加收入、减少开支、平稳物价；逐步改善人民生活。
    为了领导越南人民实现当前光荣而繁重的任务，越南民主共和国国民大会第五次会议认为有必要调整和加强政府的领导机构。会议一致通过越南民主共和国政府胡志明主席的提议，任命范文同为越南民主共和国政府总理、潘继遂为副总理兼内务部长、武元甲为副总理兼国防部长。为适应恢复经济工作的需要，会议又一致通过胡志明主席的提议，将政府中原有的工商部和交通公政部两个部，增设为工业部、商业部、交通运输部和工程水利部四个部，此外并增加其他一些部的副部长和领导人员。
    越南民主共和国国民大会第五次会议坚信：在胡志明主席、越南劳动党和越南民主共和国政府的正确领导下，越南人民将要英勇直前胜利地完成当前的光荣任务。　　　　　　　　（展潮）
