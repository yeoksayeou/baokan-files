Title: French Famous Writer Sartre Arrives in Beijing

Author:

Date: 1955-09-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The famous French writer Jean-Paul Sartre and Ms. Simone de Beauvoir, who were invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit our country, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 6th. Cao Yu, Yang Shuo, Ai Wu, Ren Wang and others, representatives of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the China Peace Preservation Committee and the Chinese Writers Association, went to the airport to welcome them.

### 法国著名作家萨特等到京

1955-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    法国著名作家萨特等到京
    新华社六日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的法国著名作家让·保罗·萨特和西蒙娜·德波娃女士，六日下午到达北京。中国人民对外文化协会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国作家协会的代表曹禺、杨朔、艾芜、任望等曾到机场欢迎。
