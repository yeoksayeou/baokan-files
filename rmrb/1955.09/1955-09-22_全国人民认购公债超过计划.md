Title: National Public Bond Subscription Exceeds Plan
Author:
Date: 1955-09-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 21st:

    The people of the whole country have subscribed to 600,435,000 yuan of the 1955 National Economic Construction Bonds, exceeding the original sales target (600 million yuan) by 0.73%. Among them, the subscription amount of workers and staff exceeded the original allocation by more than 43%, and the subscription amount of the troops exceeded the original allocation by more than 78%. As of September 15, bond subscribers had paid more than 470 million yuan in bond payments.

### 全国人民认购公债超过计划

1955-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民认购公债超过计划
    新华社二十一日讯　全国人民已认购一九五五年国家经济建设公债达六亿四百三十五万元，超过原定推销额（六亿元）百分之零点七三。其中职工认购数超过原定分配额百分之四十三以上，部队认购数超过原定分配额百分之七十八以上。到九月十五日，公债认购者共已缴公债款四亿七千多万元。
