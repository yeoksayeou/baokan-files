Friendly Exchanges

    Polish Physicist Infeld Arrives in Beijing
    The renowned Polish physicist, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, and Council Member of the World Federation of Scientific Workers, Professor Leopold Infeld, and his wife arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 27th.

    This Academician of the Polish Academy of Sciences, who has been engaged in scientific work for several decades, is visiting and lecturing in China according to the 1955 execution plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement and at the invitation of Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

    Yugoslav "Kolo" Folk Dance Ensemble Returns to Beijing
    The Yugoslav "Kolo" Folk Dance Ensemble returned to Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 27th after visiting and performing in Tianjin, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Shenyang, Anshan, and other places.
  
  Tunisian Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Various Places
    The Tunisian Friendly Delegation to China returned to Beijing on the 27th after visiting Shanghai, Hangzhou, Nanjing, Tianjin, and other places.

    Japanese Diet Members Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou
    The Japanese Diet Members Delegation, headed by Mr. Eikichi Kamabayashi, arrived in Guangzhou on the evening of the 27th. They were invited by Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, and Peng Zhen, Secretary-General, to participate in the National Day celebrations and visit China.

    Indian Health Minister Kaur Leaves for China for a Visit
    Indian Health Minister Rajkumari Amrit Kaur left Delhi on the 27th for a two-week visit to China at the invitation of Li Dequan, Minister of Health of the People's Republic of China. Yuan Zhongxian, Chinese Ambassador to India, saw her off at the airport.

    Youth Representatives from Ten Countries Including Indonesia Return Home
    Sixty youth representatives from ten countries, including Indonesia, India, Japan, Syria, Iraq, Brazil, Uruguay, North Korea, Australia, and the United States, who were invited to visit China after attending the Fifth World Youth Festival, left China from Beijing, Guangzhou, and other places between the 20th and 26th to return to their home countries.

    China's Science, Education, and Art Delegation Returns from Romania
    The Chinese delegation of scientific, educational, and artistic workers, which visited Romania according to the 1955 execution plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, headed by delegation leader Hou Wailu, has returned to Beijing.
                              (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 友好往来

1955-09-28
第2版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    波兰物理学家英费尔德到京
    著名的波兰物理学家、世界和平理事会副主席、世界科学工作者协会理事英费尔德同他的夫人在二十七日下午到达北京。
    这位从事科学工作已数十年的波兰科学院院士是根据中波文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划，并应中国科学院院长郭沫若的邀请来中国访问和讲学的。
    南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团返京
    南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团到天津、上海、杭州、沈阳、鞍山等地访问演出后，已在二十七日下午乘火车回到北京。
  突尼斯代表团在各地参观后返京
    突尼斯访华友好代表团在上海、杭州、南京等地和天津参观访问后，二十七日回到北京。
    日本国会议员访华团到广州
    以团长上林山荣吉为首的日本国会议员访华团在二十七日晚上到达广州。他们是应中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇和秘书长彭真的邀请来中国参加国庆观礼和访问的。
    印度卫生部长考尔启程来中国访问
    印度卫生部长拉志库马里·阿姆里特·考尔应中华人民共和国卫生部长李德全的邀请，在二十七日离开德里前往中国作为时两周的访问。中国驻印度大使袁仲贤到机场送行。
    印度尼西亚等十国青年代表回国
    在参加第五届世界青年联欢节后应邀来中国访问的印度尼西亚、印度、日本、叙利亚、伊拉克、巴西、乌拉圭、朝鲜、澳大利亚和美国等十个国家的青年代表共六十人，在二十日到二十六日分别从北京、广州等地离开中国回国。
    我国科学教育艺术代表团自罗回国
    根据中罗文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划赴罗马尼亚访问的中国科学教育艺术工作者代表团一行四人在团长侯外庐率领下，已回到北京。
                              （据新华社讯）
