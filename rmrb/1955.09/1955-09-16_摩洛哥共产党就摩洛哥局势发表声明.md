Title: Moroccan Communist Party Issues Statement on Moroccan Situation
Author:
Date: 1955-09-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, September 15th: TASS reports: The General Secretary of the Moroccan Communist Party Central Committee, Ali Yata, and the member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, A. Layashi, held a press conference in Paris on the 13th. Many French and foreign journalists attended the conference. A statement was read out, explaining the Moroccan Communist Party's position on the Moroccan situation.

The statement said that the Moroccan Communist Party advocates the formation of a Moroccan provisional government composed of representatives of all political groups demanding Moroccan national independence. The Moroccan Communist Party also demands free and universal elections to elect a National Assembly, which will then organize the Moroccan government. The Moroccan Communist Party demands the return of the deposed former Sultan Yusuf and the revocation of the decision to exile the party's General Secretary, Ali Yata, from Morocco.

### 摩洛哥共产党就摩洛哥局势发表声明

1955-09-16
第4版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥共产党就摩洛哥局势发表声明
    新华社十五日讯　塔斯社巴黎讯：摩洛哥共产党中央委员会总书记阿里·雅塔和中央委员会政治局委员阿·拉雅希十三日在巴黎举行记者招待会，法国和外国的许多记者出席了招待会。会上宣读了一项声明，说明摩洛哥共产党对摩洛哥局势所采取的立场。
    声明中说道，摩洛哥共产党主张由要求摩洛哥民族独立的所有政治团体的代表组成摩洛哥临时政府。摩洛哥共产党还要求举行自由普选，选出国民议会，然后由它组织摩洛哥政府。摩洛哥共产党要求被放逐的前苏丹优素福回国，要求撤销关于从摩洛哥放逐党的总书记阿里·雅塔的决定。
