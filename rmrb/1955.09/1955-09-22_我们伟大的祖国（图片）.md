Author:
Date: 1955-09-22
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The excavation team led by Li Chunrong of the Longfeng Mine of the Fushun Coal Mine has completed the workload for the first three years of the First Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule. In the past three months, they have saved the country more than 1,000 tons of coal by sweeping up extra loose coal every day. This is Li Chunrong at work.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    抚顺煤矿龙凤矿李春荣领导的掘进组已提前完成了第一个五年计划前三年的工作量。最近三个月来，他们又用每天多扫浮煤的办法给国家节约了一千多吨煤。这是李春荣正在工作。
