Title: Premier Zhou Holds Reception to Celebrate Conferment of Titles and Awards
Author:
Date: 1955-09-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency

Premier Zhou Holds Reception to Celebrate Conferment of Titles and Awards

On the afternoon of September 27th at 7:00 PM, Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai held a reception on the lawn of Huairentang in Beijing to celebrate the conferment of titles and awards.

The reception proceeded amidst the sound of melodious music.

Marshal Zhu De and other Marshals, Generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, meritorious personnel who were awarded medals, and their wives, all attended tonight's reception. The Marshals and Generals were all wearing navy blue dress uniforms and adorned with golden medals.

Also attending the reception were Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Liu Shaoqi and Vice Chairmen, Vice Premiers of the State Council, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and all personnel who attended the ceremony where the Chairman of the People's Republic of China conferred the rank of Marshal and awarded medals.

At the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai frequently raised his glass to the great victory of the Chinese people, to the victory of the armed struggle led by the Chinese Communist Party, to Chairman Mao, to all officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, to the Marshals, Generals, and all meritorious personnel who were awarded medals, and to their health.

After the reception, an evening performance was held, with wonderful song and dance and Peking opera programs jointly performed by the Cultural Troupe of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the China Peking Opera Theater, the Beijing Peking Opera Troupe No. 2, and the Ma Lianliang Troupe.
                (Xinhua News Agency)

### 周总理举行庆祝受衔受勋酒会

1955-09-28
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理举行庆祝受衔受勋酒会
    九月二十七日下午七时，国务院总理周恩来在北京怀仁堂的草坪上举行庆祝受衔受勋的酒会。
    酒会在悠扬悦耳的乐曲声中进行。
    朱德元帅和其他元帅们，中国人民解放军将官们，荣获勋章的有功人员以及他们的夫人们，都出席了今晚的酒会。元帅们和将官们都穿着海蓝色的礼服，佩戴着金色的勋章。
    出席酒会的，还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇和副委员长，国务院副总理，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席和参加中华人民共和国主席授予元帅军衔和授予勋章典礼的所有人员。
    在酒会上，周恩来总理频频举杯，为中国人民的伟大胜利，为中国共产党领导的武装斗争的胜利，为毛主席、为中国人民解放军全体官兵、为元帅们、将官们和所有荣获勋章的有功人员的健康而干杯。
    酒会结束后举行了晚会，由中国人民解放军总政治部文工团、中国京剧院、北京市京剧二团和马连良剧团联合演出了精彩的歌舞、京剧节目。
                （新华社）
