Title: Belgian Women's Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th. A Belgian women's delegation of eight members, invited by the All-China Democratic Women's Federation to visit China and attend the National Day celebrations, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 20th.
　　The delegation is led by Francine Reyna, President of the Belgian Women's Union for the Defense of Peace and Welfare. Other members of the delegation include parliamentarians, women, and trade union workers.
　　Zhang Yun, Vice President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 比利时妇女代表团到北京

1955-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    比利时妇女代表团到北京
    据新华社二十日讯　应中华全国民主妇女联合会邀请来我国访问并参加我国国庆节观礼的比利时妇女代表团八人，二十日下午乘机到达北京。
    代表团由比利时妇女保卫和平与福利联盟主席弗朗西妮·丽娜率领，代表团其他成员包括议员、妇女和工会工作者。
    前往机场欢迎的有中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席章蕴等。
