Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet of the Union and Nationalities Councils' Chairmen Volkov and Lacis Receive Belgian Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-09-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 8th – TASS reports: The chairmen of the Soviet Supreme Soviet's Council of the Union, Volkov, and the chairman of the Council of Nationalities, Lacis, received the Belgian parliamentary delegation led by the Speaker of the Belgian Chamber of Representatives, Huysmans, on the 6th in the Moscow Kremlin.

Welcoming the guests, Volkov and Lacis expressed their conviction that the Belgian parliamentary delegation's visit to Moscow would help consolidate the friendly relations between the two peoples.

Huysmans, on behalf of the delegation, expressed gratitude to the Soviet Supreme Soviet for inviting them to visit the Soviet Union, and thanked them for the warm reception the delegation received in Moscow.

After the meeting, the guests visited the Kremlin. They praised the outstanding architectural art and decoration of the ancient buildings of the Kremlin.

### 苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院两院主席  沃尔科夫和拉齐斯接见比利时议会代表团

1955-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院两院主席
    沃尔科夫和拉齐斯接见比利时议会代表团
    新华社八日讯据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席沃尔科夫和民族院主席拉齐斯，六日在莫斯科克里姆林宫接见了以比利时下议院议长余斯曼为首的比利时议会代表团。
    沃尔科夫和拉齐斯在欢迎来宾时表示确信，比利时议会代表团的访问莫斯科将有助于巩固两国人民的友好关系。
    余斯曼以代表团的名义表示感谢苏联最高苏维埃邀请他们访问苏联，并且感谢代表团在莫斯科所受到的热烈招待。
    接见后，客人们参观了克里姆林宫。他们对克里姆林宫古代建筑物的卓越建筑艺术和装饰表示称赞。
