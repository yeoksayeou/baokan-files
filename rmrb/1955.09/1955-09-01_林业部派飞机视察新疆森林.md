Title: Ministry of Forestry Sends Plane to Inspect Forests in Xinjiang
Author:
Date: 1955-09-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency

    The Ministry of Forestry's forest aerial survey team sent to Xinjiang began using airplanes to inspect forests in the Tianshan Mountains on the 26th. The use of airplanes to inspect forests is a first in Xinjiang.
    According to preliminary survey materials, the forest area in Xinjiang Province is more than 39 million mu. The majority of these continuous large areas of forests are valuable construction materials such as spruce, larch, and Scots pine. The aerial survey team will spend several months this year flying over these virgin forests in the Altai, Tianshan, Kunlun Mountains and the Tarim Basin to understand the distribution, area, and storage volume of the forests, in preparation for providing timber for national economic construction and the construction of the Lanzhou-Urumqi-Almaty railway.

### 林业部派飞机视察新疆森林

1955-09-01
第2版()
专栏：

    林业部派飞机视察新疆森林
    林业部派到新疆来的森林航空视察组，二十六日开始用飞机在天山视察森林。使用飞机视察森林的工作在新疆还是第一次。
    根据已往初步调查的材料，新疆省境内的森林面积有三千九百多万亩，这些绵延不断的大面积森林，绝大部分是云杉、落叶松和沙松等贵重建筑材料。航空视察组今年将用几个月的时间飞行在阿尔泰、天山、昆仑山和塔里木盆地的这些原始森林的上空，了解森林的分布、面积和蓄积量的情况，为今后国家经济建设和修建兰州—乌鲁木齐—阿拉木图铁路准备木料。　（新华社）
