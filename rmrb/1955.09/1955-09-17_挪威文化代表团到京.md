Title: Norwegian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1955-09-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th. A ten-member Norwegian cultural delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China, arrived in Beijing on September 16th, led by the head of the delegation, Kloster, and the deputy head of the delegation, Holboe.
    Representatives from the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Chinese Artists Association, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Defense, and others were at the airport to greet them.

### 挪威文化代表团到京

1955-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

    挪威文化代表团到京
    新华社十六日讯  应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的挪威文化代表团一行十人，在团长克洛斯特、副团长霍姆波率领下，在九月十六日到达北京。
    到机场迎接的有中国人民对外文化协会、中国美术家协会、中华全国总工会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的代表多人。
