Title: Premier U Nu Condemns Colonialism as Harming the Cause of Peace
Author:
Date: 1955-09-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Seventeenth of September, Rangoon Dispatch: Burmese Premier U Nu, speaking on the sixteenth of September in Rangoon at the annual meeting of Burmese Army commanders, condemned colonialism as a threat to peace.

	U Nu said that since the Geneva Four-Power Summit Conference, international tensions have noticeably eased. However, in certain dependent countries, pressure and bloodshed are growing due to the short-sighted policies of the colonial powers.

	He said: "We should not be satisfied because the Four Great Powers held a summit meeting, but should make continuous efforts to eradicate colonialism, which is the root of the disease that endangers the cause of peace."

### 吴努总理谴责殖民主义危害和平事业

1955-09-18
第4版()
专栏：

    吴努总理谴责殖民主义危害和平事业
    新华社十七日讯  仰光消息：缅甸总理吴努十六日在仰光在缅甸陆军指挥官的年会上讲话时，谴责殖民主义是对和平的威胁。
    吴努说，自从日内瓦四国政府首脑会议以来，国际紧张局势已经显著地缓和了。但是，在某些附属国内，由于殖民国家眼光短浅的政策所造成的压力和流血正在增长。
    他说：“不应当因四大国举行了最高级会谈而满足，而应当进行不断的努力来根除殖民主义，它是危害和平事业的疾病的根源。”
