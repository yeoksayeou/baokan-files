Title: The Eighth International Conference of Young Sinologists Concludes

Author:

Date: 1955-09-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, The Hague, September 5th: The Eighth International Conference of Young Sinologists, held in Oegstgeest village near Leiden, Netherlands, has concluded. Based on the suggestions of the British and French delegations, the conference unanimously passed a resolution stating that the active participation of delegations from the United States, the People's Republic of China, and the Soviet Union promoted the success of the conference and the development of scientific cooperation among the participating countries.

The head of the Soviet delegation to the conference, Kovalev, deputy director of the Institute of Oriental Studies of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, expressed his satisfaction with the results of the conference in an interview with a TASS reporter. He emphasized that an atmosphere of mutual understanding and cooperation pervaded the meeting.

The delegations from various countries also unanimously approved the suggestion put forward by the French representative: that the Ninth International Conference of Young Sinologists be held in Paris next year.

### 第八届国际青年汉学家会议闭幕

1955-09-07
第4版()
专栏：

    第八届国际青年汉学家会议闭幕
    新华社六日讯　据塔斯社海牙五日讯：在荷兰莱顿附近乌格斯特海斯特村举行的第八届国际青年汉学家会议已经闭幕。根据英国和法国代表团的建议，会议一致通过了一项决议，决议指出，美国、中华人民共和国和苏联代表团的积极参加，促进了会议的成功和与会各国之间的科学合作的发展。
    出席会议的苏联代表团团长、苏联科学院东方学研究所副所长科瓦廖夫向塔斯社记者发表谈话，对会议结果表示满意。他强调指出，会上笼罩着互相谅解和合作的气氛。
    各国代表团还一致赞成法国代表提出的建议：明年在巴黎举行第九届国际青年汉学家会议。
