Title: Overseas Chinese Visiting Group Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 18th. A group of one hundred and three overseas Chinese from Indonesia, Burma, and India, who are participating in this year's National Day celebrations, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 18th.

Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Zhuang Xiquan, Deputy Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission; Chen Qiyuan, Overseas Chinese Representative of the National People's Congress; Liu Mengchun, Deputy Secretary-General of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Chen Qiyou, member, as well as returning overseas Chinese representatives and students from Indonesia, Burma, and India in Beijing.

The overseas Chinese visiting groups from Indonesia, Burma, and India arrived in Guangzhou successively in early August, and left Guangzhou in batches in mid-August to visit Shanghai, Nanjing, and Shenyang, Anshan, Fushun, Lushun-Dalian, Tianjin and other places. They were excited about the achievements of the motherland's industrial, municipal, and other aspects of construction.

### 华侨回国观光团到北京

1955-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    华侨回国观光团到北京
    新华社十八日讯　参加今年国庆典礼的印度尼西亚、缅甸、印度华侨回国观光团一行一百零三人，十八日下午到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有：华侨事务委员会副主任庄希泉，全国人民代表大会华侨代表陈其瑗，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副秘书长刘孟纯、委员陈其尤，以及在京印度尼西亚、缅甸、印度的归国华侨代表、学生等。
    印度尼西亚、缅甸、印度华侨回国观光团在八月上旬先后到达广州，八月中旬分批离开广州，到上海、南京和沈阳、鞍山、抚顺、旅大、天津等地参观，他们对祖国工业、市政等方面建设的成就感到兴奋。
