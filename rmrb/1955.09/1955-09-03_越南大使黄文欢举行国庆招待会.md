Title: Vietnamese Ambassador Hoang Van Hoan Holds National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1955-09-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 2nd: Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Hoang Van Hoan, held a grand reception on the evening of September 2nd to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Among those attending the reception were Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China; Vice-Chairman Song Qingling; Vice-Premiers of the State Council Chen Yun and Chen Yi; Peng Zhen, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing; Dong Biwu, President of the Supreme People's Court; Zhang Dingcheng, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate; Nie Rongzhen, Cheng Qian, and Long Yun, Vice-Chairmen of the National Defense Council; Zhang Bojun, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Zhang Hanfu, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs; Su Yu, Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; as well as members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, directors and deputy directors of the offices of the State Council, heads of ministries and commissions, members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Vice-Mayor of Beijing, heads of various people's organizations, people from all walks of life in the capital, and diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries to China. More than 800 people attended the reception.

At the beginning of the reception, the band played the national anthems of Vietnam and China.

Vietnamese Ambassador Hoang Van Hoan spoke at the reception. At the end of his speech, he raised his glass to wish Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China good health.

Vice-Premier of the State Council Chen Yun then spoke. At the end of his speech, he raised his glass to wish President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam good health.

After the reception, Chinese artists performed wonderful programs such as acrobatics, Peking opera excerpts, and female solo singing.

### 越南大使黄文欢举行国庆招待会

1955-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    越南大使黄文欢举行国庆招待会
    新华社二日讯　越南民主共和国驻华大使黄文欢在二日晚举行盛大招待会，庆祝越南民主共和国成立十周年。
    出席招待会的，有中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇、副委员长宋庆龄，国务院副总理陈云、陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真，最高人民法院院长董必武，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，国防委员会副主席聂荣臻、程潜、龙云，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，国务院秘书长习仲勋，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国人民解放军总参谋长粟裕，以及全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员，国务院各办公室主任、副主任，各部、委负责人，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员，中国人民解放军高级将领，北京市副市长，各人民团体负责人，首都各界人士，各国驻华使节和外交官员等。出席招待会的共有八百多人。
    招待会开始时，乐队奏越中两国国歌。
    越南大使黄文欢在会上讲话。他在讲话结束时，举杯祝贺中华人民共和国毛泽东主席的身体健康。
    国务院副总理陈云接着讲话。他在讲话结束时，举杯祝贺越南民主共和国胡志明主席的身体健康。
    招待会结束后，中国艺术工作者表演了杂技、京剧清唱和女声独唱等精采节目。
