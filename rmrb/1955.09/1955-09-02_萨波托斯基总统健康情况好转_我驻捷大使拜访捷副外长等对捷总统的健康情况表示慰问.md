Title: President Zápotocký's Health Improves; Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Visits Czechoslovak Deputy Foreign Minister and Others to Express Concern for the Health of the Czechoslovak President
Author:
Date: 1955-09-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 1st: The Czechoslovak News Agency published a communique on August 31st regarding the health of Comrade Antonín Zápotocký, President of the Czechoslovak Republic, stating:

    Comrade Antonín Zápotocký, President of the Republic, is suffering from severe cardiac insufficiency with impaired blood circulation. After diagnosis and treatment, the heart's rhythm has improved. At 6:00 AM on August 31st, his temperature was 36.3 degrees Celsius. Blood pressure and pulse were normal. The patient spent a quiet night.

    On the evening of the same day, the Czechoslovak News Agency issued a communique on President Antonín Zápotocký's health condition at 6:00 PM on August 31st, stating that the President's overall health was good. Throughout the day, there has been further improvement in blood circulation. The ratio of blood pressure to pulse continues to be normal. President Zápotocký remains under medical supervision and treatment.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 1st: After learning of the illness of Antonín Zápotocký, President of the Czechoslovak Republic, the Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, Cao Ying, visited Czechoslovak Deputy Foreign Minister Šimovič and the Director of the Presidential Office, Novák, on the morning and afternoon of August 31st, respectively, to express concern and sympathy for President Zápotocký's health.

### 萨波托斯基总统健康情况好转  我驻捷大使拜访捷副外长等对捷总统的健康情况表示慰问

1955-09-02
第4版()
专栏：

    萨波托斯基总统健康情况好转
    我驻捷大使拜访捷副外长等对捷总统的健康情况表示慰问
    新华社布拉格一日电　八月三十一日，捷克斯洛伐克通讯社发表了关于捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统安托宁·萨波托斯基同志健康情况的公报说：
    共和国总统安托宁·萨波托斯基同志患剧烈的心脏衰弱症，并且血液循环发生障碍。经诊治后，心脏跳动情况已有改善。八月三十一日上午六时体温三十六度三。血压和脉搏正常。病人平静度过了一夜。
    同日晚间，捷克斯洛伐克通讯社发表总统安托宁·萨波托斯基八月三十一日下午六时的健康情况的公报说，总统的总的健康情况良好。在今天一天中，血液循环的情况有进一步的改善。血压和脉搏的比例继续正常。萨波托斯基总统还在医生的监护下并在治疗中。
    新华社布拉格一日电　中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛，在得悉捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基患病的消息后，于八月三十一日上午和下午分别拜会了捷外交部副部长西莫维茨和总统府办公厅主任诺瓦克，对萨波托斯基总统的健康情况表示关怀和慰问。
