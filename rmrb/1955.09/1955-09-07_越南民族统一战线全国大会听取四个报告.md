Title: The National Congress of the Vietnam Fatherland Front Hears Four Reports
Author:
Date: 1955-09-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 6th: The National Congress of the Vietnam Fatherland Front continued on the 6th.

In the morning session of the 6th, Xie Guangbao, member of the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front and member of the Preparatory Committee of the Congress, Hu Yue Sheng, and Pan Ying respectively gave reports on the implementation of the Armistice Agreement, on the land reform issue, and on financial and economic work.

In the afternoon, Yong Wenqian, member of the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front and Chairman of the South Vietnam Committee of the Vietnam National United Front, and member of the Congress Preparatory Committee, gave a report on the situation in South Vietnam.

The delegates to the congress will hold group discussions on the 7th and 8th.

### 越南民族统一战线全国大会听取四个报告

1955-09-07
第4版()
专栏：

    越南民族统一战线全国大会听取四个报告
    新华社河内六日电　越南民族统一战线全国大会六日继续举行。
    在六日上午会议上，越南国民联合战线全国委员会委员、大会筹备委员会委员谢光宝、胡曰胜和潘英分别作了关于停战协定执行情况的报告，关于土地改革问题的报告，和关于财政经济工作的报告。
    在下午，越南国民联合战线全国委员会委员、越南国民联合战线南越委员会主席、大会筹委会委员雍文谦作了关于南越情况的报告。
    大会代表们在七日和八日将分组进行讨论。
