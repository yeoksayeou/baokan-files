Title: State Council Plenary Session Approves Sino-Egyptian Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1955-09-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: The 19th plenary session of the State Council was held on the morning of the 23rd.

    The meeting heard a report from Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Ji Zhuang regarding the signing of the Sino-Egyptian trade agreement and protocol, and approved the trade agreement between The Government of the People's Republic of China and The Government of the Republic of Egypt, and the protocol on the first agreement year of the trade agreement between the Government of the Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Egypt, signed in Beijing on August 22, 1955 by representatives of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Egypt.

    The Government of the People's Republic of China notified the Government of the Republic of Egypt on September 23 that the aforementioned agreement and protocol had been approved. The Government of the People's Republic of China has also received notification that the Government of the Republic of Egypt approved the aforementioned agreement and protocol on September 14.

    The meeting also approved the decision to abolish the administrative structure of the former Gorlos Banner in Jilin Province and establish the Qian Gorlos Mongolian Autonomous County, as well as personnel appointments and removals.

### 国务院全体会议批准中埃贸易协定

1955-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院全体会议批准中埃贸易协定
    新华社二十三日讯　中华人民共和国国务院全体会议第十九次会议在二十三日上午举行。
    会议听了对外贸易部部长叶季壮关于签订中埃贸易协定和议定书的报告，批准了中华人民共和国政府代表和埃及共和国政府代表于一九五五年八月二十二日在北京签订的中华人民共和国政府和埃及共和国政府贸易协定，以及关于中华人民共和国政府和埃及共和国政府间的贸易协定第一个协定年度的议定书。
    中华人民共和国政府已于九月二十三日通知埃及共和国政府，上述协定及议定书已批准。中华人民共和国政府同时也已接到关于埃及共和国政府已在九月十四日批准上述协定和议定书的通知。
    会议还通过了关于撤销吉林省郭尔罗斯前旗的建制成立前郭尔罗斯蒙古自治县的决定以及任免事项。
