Our Great Motherland
After the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee accepted the application for joint public-private ownership, twenty-three private cotton textile factories in Shanghai have successively established preparatory organizations and formulated specific plans for joint operation. This is a scene in the fine yarn workshop of the Shanghai Shenxin No. 9 Cotton Textile Factory where inventory workers are checking assets.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    上海二十三家私营棉纺织厂经上海市人民委员会接受申请公私合营后，已先后成立了筹备组织，并分别制定了合营的具体计划。这是上海申新第九棉纺织厂细纱车间的清点工人正在查点资产的情形。
