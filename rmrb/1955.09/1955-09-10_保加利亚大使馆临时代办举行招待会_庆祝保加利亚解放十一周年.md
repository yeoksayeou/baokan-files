Title: Bulgarian Embassy Chargé d'Affaires Holds Reception to Celebrate the 11th Anniversary of Bulgaria's Liberation
Author: 
Date: 1955-09-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 9th. The Chargé d'Affaires of the People's Republic of Bulgaria in China, Mr. Takov, held a reception on the evening of the 9th to celebrate the 11th anniversary of Bulgaria's liberation.

Invited to attend the reception were Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premier of the State Council; Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council; Dong Biwu, President of the Supreme People's Court; Zhang Dingcheng, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Li Da, Vice Minister of National Defense; Xue Zizheng, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; Ministers and Vice Ministers of various ministries; Deputy Directors of various commissions; members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; heads of various people's organizations; and figures from the capital's scientific, educational, literary, artistic, and journalistic circles.

Heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials from various countries in China were also invited to attend the reception.

At the reception, Bulgarian Chargé d'Affaires Takov spoke first. At the end of his speech, he raised his glass to the health of the great leader of the Chinese people, Comrade Mao Zedong. At this time, the band played the national anthem of the People's Republic of China.

Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi then spoke. At the end of his speech, he raised his glass to the health of the leader of the Bulgarian people, Comrade Chervenkov. At this time, the band played the national anthem of the People's Republic of Bulgaria.

After the reception, Chinese literary and art workers performed brilliant programs, and a dance was held.

### 保加利亚大使馆临时代办举行招待会  庆祝保加利亚解放十一周年

1955-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚大使馆临时代办举行招待会
    庆祝保加利亚解放十一周年
    新华社九日讯　保加利亚人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办塔科夫，在九日晚举行招待会，庆祝保加利亚解放十一周年。
    应邀出席招待会的，有国务院副总理邓小平，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若，国务院副总理陈毅，最高人民法院院长董必武，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，外交部副部长章汉夫，国防部副部长李达，北京市副市长薛子正，各部部长、副部长，各委员会副主任，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员，各人民团体的负责人和首都科学、教育、文学、艺术、新闻界人士等。
    各国驻华使节和外交官员也应邀出席了招待会。
    招待会上，保加利亚临时代办塔科夫首先讲话。他在讲话结束时，举杯祝中国人民伟大的领袖毛泽东同志的健康。这时，乐队奏起中华人民共和国国歌。
    国务院副总理陈毅接着讲话。他在讲话结束时，举杯祝保加利亚人民的领袖契尔文科夫同志的健康。这时，乐队奏起保加利亚人民共和国国歌。
    招待会后，由中国文艺工作者表演了精采节目，并举行了舞会。
