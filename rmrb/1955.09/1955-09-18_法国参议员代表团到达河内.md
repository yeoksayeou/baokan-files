Title: French Senatorial Delegation Arrives in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1955-09-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 17th: According to Vietnam News Agency, the senatorial delegation of the French Republic, headed by Edmond Michelet, arrived in Hanoi by plane on the afternoon of the 16th for a visit.

### 法国参议员代表团到达河内

1955-09-18
第4版()
专栏：

    法国参议员代表团到达河内
    新华社河内十七日讯  据越南通讯社讯：以埃德蒙·密歇勒为首的法兰西共和国参议员代表团乘飞机在十六日下午到达河内访问。
