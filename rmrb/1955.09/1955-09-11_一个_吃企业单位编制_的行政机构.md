    An Administrative Institution "Living off the Payroll of Enterprise Units"

    To the Editor:

    In January 1953, the Financial and Economic Committee of the former Shandong Provincial People's Government established an organization called the "Shandong Provincial Transportation Planning Committee." Jinan, Qingdao, and other provincial-level cities, as well as Tai'an, Changwei, and other special districts, also successively established city and special district-level "Transportation Planning Committees." The task of this organization was to handle the transportation plans of local state organs, state-owned and cooperative enterprises, and joint state-private enterprises, as well as problems that could not be resolved independently by the consignor and consignee in transportation. All of its staff members were transferred from relevant enterprise units under the name of "temporary assistance." The payroll of these personnel still belonged to the original units, and wages were received from the original units. Even office expenses had to be paid monthly by the original units.

    Strangely enough, the "temporary assistance" of the staff members of this organization has now lasted for two years and eight months. After the establishment of the Provincial People's Committee, the Provincial "Transportation Planning Committee" was placed under the leadership of the Transportation Office. However, cities and special districts did not have Transportation Offices, so the "Transportation Planning Committees" in various places became organs without a superior.

    I believe that this approach to staffing is inappropriate. State-owned enterprises have already implemented an economic accounting system. It is obviously unreasonable for relevant enterprise units to be "supporting the payroll" of the "Transportation Planning Committee" for a long time and paying the salaries and office expenses of staff members. I suggest that the Shandong Provincial People's Committee quickly resolve this issue.

### 一个“吃企业单位编制”的行政机构

1955-09-11
第6版()
专栏：读者来信专页

    一个“吃企业单位编制”的行政机构编辑同志：
    前山东省人民政府财经委员会在一九五三年一月设立了一个叫做“山东省运输计划委员会”的机构；济南、青岛等省辖市和泰安、昌潍等专区也先后成立市和专区级的“运输计划委员会”。这个机构的任务是办理当地的国家机关、国营和合作社营企业、公私合营企业的运输计划，以及运输中承托双方不能自行解决的问题。它的工作人员，全部是用“暂时协助工作”的名义从各有关的企业单位抽调来的。这些人员的编制仍然属于原来单位，工资在原单位领取，甚至办公费还要原单位按月支付。
    奇怪的是，这个机构的工作人员“暂时协助工作”到现在已经两年又八个月了。省人民委员会成立以后，省“运输计划委员会”划归交通办公室领导。可是市和专区都没有交通办公室，以致各地的
  “运输计划委员会”就成为找不到上级的机关。
    我认为这样对待编制的做法是不妥当的。国营企业已经实行了经济核算制，要有关企业单位长期被“运输计划委员会”吃编制，并且支付工作人员的薪金和办公费，显然是不合理的。建议山东省人民委员会迅速解决这个问题。　一读者
