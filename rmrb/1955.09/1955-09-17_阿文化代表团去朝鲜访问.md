Title: Albanian Cultural Delegation Visits North Korea
Author: 
Date: 1955-09-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The Albanian Cultural Delegation, headed by Fadil Paçrami, has concluded its visits to Shenyang, Anshan and other places, and left Shenyang by train on the morning of the 16th to visit the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.
　　The delegation arrived in Shenyang on the morning of the 13th, and visited the No. 1 Machine Tool Factory, the Pneumatic Tool Factory, and the Gaokan Agricultural Production Cooperative in Shenyang, and toured the city of Shenyang. On the evening of the 14th, the head of the delegation, Fadil Paçrami, gave a report on the general situation of literature and art in Albania over the past ten years to more than 400 cultural and artistic workers in Shenyang. The delegation also visited Fushun and Anshan.

### 阿文化代表团去朝鲜访问

1955-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

    阿文化代表团去朝鲜访问
    据新华社十六日讯  以法迪尔·帕奇拉米为首的阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团已结束在沈阳、鞍山等地参观访问，在十六日上午乘火车离沈阳去朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访问。
    代表团在十三日上午到达沈阳，在沈阳先后参观了第一机床厂、风动工具厂、高坎农业生产合作社，并游览了沈阳市区。代表团团长法迪尔·帕奇拉米十四日晚向沈阳市四百多名文化艺术工作者作了关于阿尔巴尼亚十年来文学艺术概况的报告。代表团还去抚顺和鞍山进行了参观。
