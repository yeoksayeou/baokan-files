Title: Ding Xilin, Head of Our Art Troupe, Visits Vietnamese Party and Government Leaders
Author:
Date: 1955-09-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 2nd. Ding Xilin, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, and Wu Baihua, deputy head, visited Truong Chinh, General Secretary of the Vietnam Workers' Party, on the morning of September 1st. They also visited Nguyen Van Huyen, Minister of Education, and Nguyen Khanh Toan, Deputy Minister, at the Ministry of Education of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

That afternoon, Ding Xilin and others successively visited Hoang Quoc Viet, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front and Chairman of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, and Vo Nguyen Giap, Minister of National Defense of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnam People's Army.

At 5:00 PM on the 1st, the entire Chinese Art Troupe, led by head Ding Xilin, laid a wreath at the Vietnam Martyrs' Memorial.

### 我艺术团团长丁西林拜访越南党政领导人员

1955-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    我艺术团团长丁西林拜访越南党政领导人员
    新华社河内二日电　中国艺术团团长丁西林、副团长吴白桦在九月一日上午拜访了越南劳动党总书记长征，并且到越南民主共和国教育部访问了教育部长阮文煊、副部长阮庆全。
    当天下午，丁西林等又先后拜访了越南国民联合战线全国委员会副主席、越南总工会主席黄国越和越南民主共和国政府国防部长兼越南人民军总司令武元甲。
    一日下午五时，中国艺术团全体人员在团长丁西林率领下向越南烈士纪念碑献了花圈。
