Title: British Communist Party Demands People Support Cypriot People's Demand for Self-Determination
Author:
Date: 1955-09-20
Page: 4
　　Xinhua News Agency, London: The British Communist Party issued a statement on the 11th, demanding that the British people support the Cypriot people's demand for self-determination. The statement pointed out that the London conference of the foreign ministers of Britain, Greece, and Turkey "was doomed to failure from the start." The statement said: "The Cypriot people have no say in the discussion, but the discussion involves their lives and basic democratic rights."
    The statement condemned Britain for sending troops to Cyprus and pointed out: "The British Conservative Party is attempting to circumvent the real issue of granting self-determination to Cyprus by giving Cyprus a constitution that preserves British colonial rule, with the British government holding all decisive power."

### 英共要求人民支持塞浦路斯人民自决要求

1955-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

    英共要求人民支持塞浦路斯人民自决要求
    新华社讯　据塔斯社伦敦讯：英国共产党十一日发表声明，要求英国人民支持塞浦路斯人民的自决要求。声明指出：英国、希腊和土耳其三国外长伦敦会议“一开始就注定要失败”。声明说：“塞浦路斯人民在讨论中没有发言权，而讨论却牵涉到他们的生命和基本民主权利。”
    声明谴责英国运兵到塞浦路斯去，并且指出：“英国保守党企图通过给塞浦路斯一个保留英国殖民统治的宪法，由英国政府掌握全部决定性的权力，从而规避给予塞浦路斯以自决权的真正问题。”
