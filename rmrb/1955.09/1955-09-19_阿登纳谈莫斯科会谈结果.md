Title: Adenauer Discusses Results of Moscow Talks
Author:
Date: 1955-09-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 18th, TASS dispatch: Bonn dispatch: West German Chancellor Adenauer spoke at a press conference in Bonn on the 16th. When discussing the results of the Moscow talks, he said that all countries in the North Atlantic Alliance—of which the Federal Republic of Germany is a member—have embassies or legations in Moscow. In this respect, the decision of the government of the Federal Republic of Germany to establish diplomatic relations with the Soviet Union is not surprising. Adenauer emphasized: "Whatever other countries enjoy, we must also enjoy."

Adenauer said: I got the impression from the Moscow negotiations and talks that the Soviet Union does indeed hope to establish a security system, because the Soviet Union faces so many social, economic, and cultural problems that it needs to use all its strength to deal with these problems.

When discussing other issues, especially the issue of German borders, Adenauer emphasized: The corresponding statement by TASS was not unexpected.

### 阿登纳谈莫斯科会谈结果

1955-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

    阿登纳谈莫斯科会谈结果
    新华社十八日讯　塔斯社柏林十八日讯：波恩消息：西德总理阿登纳十六日在波恩的记者招待会上讲了话。他在谈到莫斯科谈判的结果时说，北大西洋联盟——德意志联邦共和国也是它的成员之一——的所有国家在莫斯科都有大使馆或公使馆，就这一点来说，德意志联邦共和国政府所采取的同苏联建立外交关系的决定，并没有什么使人惊奇的地方。阿登纳强调：“凡是其他国家享受到的一切，我们也必须享受到。”
    阿登纳说：我从莫斯科谈判和会谈中得到了这种印象：苏联的确希望建立一个安全体系，因为苏联面临着如此多的社会、经济和文化问题，它需要用它的全部力量来对付这些问题。
    阿登纳在谈到其他问题，特别是德国疆界问题时，强调：塔斯社相应的声明并不是出人意料的。
