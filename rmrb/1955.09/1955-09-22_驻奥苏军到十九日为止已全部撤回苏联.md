Title: Soviet Troops Stationed in Austria Completely Withdrawn to the Soviet Union by the 19th
Author:
Date: 1955-09-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, September 20th, TASS dispatch: In accordance with the decision of the Soviet government, all Soviet troops stationed in Austria had been completely withdrawn to the Soviet Union by September 19th. Therefore, the Soviet Union has fulfilled the relevant provisions of the state treaty on the reconstruction of an independent and democratic Austria ahead of schedule.

### 驻奥苏军到十九日为止已全部撤回苏联

1955-09-22
第4版()
专栏：

    驻奥苏军到十九日为止已全部撤回苏联
    新华社二十一日讯　塔斯社维也纳二十日讯：按照苏联政府的决定，驻奥地利的苏联军队到九月十九日为止已经全部撤回苏联。因此，苏联已经提前执行了关于重建独立民主的奥地利的国家条约的有关条款。
