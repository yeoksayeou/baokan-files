Title: Bulganin and Khrushchev Receive Japanese Parliamentary Delegation to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-09-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 22nd: According to TASS, Soviet Premier Bulganin and member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Khrushchev, received the Japanese parliamentary delegation to the Soviet Union, headed by Tokutaro Kitamura and Masaru Nozoe, in the Grand Kremlin Palace in Moscow on the 21st.

Bulganin delivered a brief welcome speech to the Japanese parliamentarians.

Then, Khrushchev invited the guests to frankly exchange opinions on issues related to improving Soviet-Japanese relations.

Tokutaro Kitamura and Masaru Nozoe, on behalf of the Japanese parliamentary delegation to the Soviet Union, explained the issues they particularly valued. These included the path to normalizing relations between the Soviet Union and Japan, the development of trade relations between the two countries and the significance of such relations, and the establishment of cultural ties.

Khrushchev gave detailed answers to the questions raised by the Japanese parliamentarians and clarified the Soviet point of view.

The conversation lasted for two hours. At the end of the conversation, Khrushchev asked the Japanese parliamentarians to convey the most sincere wishes of the Soviet people to the Japanese people. He emphasized that the Soviet people are eager to live in peace and friendship with the Japanese people.

Bulganin, on behalf of the Soviet government, agreed with this wish.

Details of the conversation between Bulganin and Khrushchev and the Japanese parliamentary delegation to the Soviet Union will be published in the newspapers later.

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫接见日本议员访苏团

1955-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫接见日本议员访苏团
    新华社二十二日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫二十一日在莫斯科大克里姆林宫接见了以北村德太郎和野沟胜为首的日本国会议员访苏团。
    布尔加宁向日本国会议员们致了简短的欢迎词。
    接着，赫鲁晓夫请客人们就有关改善日苏两国关系的问题开诚布公地交换意见。
    北村德太郎和野沟胜代表日本国会议员访苏团说明了他们所特别重视的那些问题。其中包括苏联和日本关系正常化的途径的问题，关于发展两国贸易关系以及这种关系的意义问题，关于建立文化联系的问题。
    赫鲁晓夫对日本议员们提出的问题做了详细的答复，并且阐明了苏联的观点。
    谈话历时两小时，在谈话结束时，赫鲁晓夫请日本国会议员们把苏联人民最真诚的愿望转达给日本人民。他强调指出，苏联人民渴望同日本人民和平和友好地相处。
    布尔加宁代表苏联政府同意这种愿望。
    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫同日本国会议员访苏团的谈话详情将在以后在报上发表。
