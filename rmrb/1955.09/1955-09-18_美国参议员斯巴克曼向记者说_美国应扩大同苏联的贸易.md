Title: U.S. Senator Sparkman Tells Reporters that the U.S. Should Expand Trade with the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-09-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 16th, TASS dispatch: According to an Associated Press reporter in Berlin, U.S. Senator Sparkman arrived in Berlin after a two-week visit to the Soviet Union and Eastern European countries. He said at a press conference that he advocates expanding trade with these countries.

He also said: "I don't think pursuing a policy of not participating in this trade will do us any good."

Sparkman pointed out that a new approach to trade policy should be adopted, which is one of the main conclusions he drew after visiting Russia, Poland and Czechoslovakia.

### 美国参议员斯巴克曼向记者说  美国应扩大同苏联的贸易

1955-09-18
第4版()
专栏：

    美国参议员斯巴克曼向记者说
    美国应扩大同苏联的贸易
    新华社十七日讯  塔斯社纽约十六日讯：据美联社驻柏林记者报道，美国参议员斯巴克曼在苏联和东欧国家访问两个星期后到达柏林。他在记者招待会上说，他主张扩大同这些国家的贸易。
    他又说：“我不认为，奉行不加入这种贸易的政策对我们自己会有什么好处。”
    斯巴克曼指出，应该采取贸易政策的新方针，这是他访问俄国、波兰和捷克斯洛伐克后得出的主要看法之一。
