Title: Xirao Jiacuo, Dao Jingban, and Others Go to Xinjiang to Attend the Founding Congress of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1955-09-19
Page: 1
　
    To Attend the Founding Congress of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region
    Xirao Jiacuo, Dao Jingban, and Others Go to Xinjiang
    Xinhua News Agency, Xining, 16th – Xirao Jiacuo (Tibetan), Vice Governor of Qinghai Province, and Ahoumaiti (Kazak), Chairman of the Alton Quk Kazak Autonomous Region, representatives of all ethnic groups in Qinghai Province to attend the founding congress of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region, departed from Xining on September 15 for Urumqi.
    According to Xinhua News Agency, Dao Jingban (Dai), Chairman of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Region of Yunnan Province, left Kunming by plane on the 14th to attend the founding congress of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region. He is attending the congress as a representative of all ethnic groups in Yunnan Province and the Xishuangbanna Dai Autonomous Prefecture, the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Region, the Honghe Hani Autonomous Region, and the Nujiang Lisu Autonomous Region.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, 17th – Wang Zaitian (Mongolian), Vice Chairman of the People's Committee of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, has left Hohhot via Beijing by plane to Urumqi to represent the people of all ethnic groups in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region at the founding congress of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region.

### 参加新疆维吾尔自治区成立大会  喜饶嘉错、刀京版等去新疆

1955-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    参加新疆维吾尔自治区成立大会
    喜饶嘉错、刀京版等去新疆
    新华社西宁十六日电　代表青海省各族人民参加新疆维吾尔自治区成立大会的青海省副省长喜饶嘉错（藏族）和阿尔顿曲克哈萨克族自治区主席阿后买提（哈萨克族），已在九月十五日从西宁出发，前往乌鲁木齐。
    据新华社讯　云南省德宏傣族景颇族自治区人民政府主席刀京版（傣族）已在十四日乘飞机离开昆明，前往乌鲁木齐参加新疆维吾尔自治区的成立大会。他是作为云南省各民族和西双版纳傣族自治州、德宏傣族景颇族自治区、红河哈尼族自治区、怒江傈僳族自治区的代表，参加这个大会。
    新华社呼和浩特十七日电　内蒙古自治区人民委员会副主席王再天（蒙族）已从呼和浩特经北京乘飞机去乌鲁木齐代表内蒙古自治区各族人民参加新疆维吾尔自治区成立大会。
