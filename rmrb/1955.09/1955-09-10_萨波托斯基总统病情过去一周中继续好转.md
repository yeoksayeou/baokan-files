Title: President Zápotocký's Condition Continues to Improve Over the Past Week
Author:
Date: 1955-09-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 9th: A communique concerning the health of the President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Antonín Zápotocký, was released here on September 8th.

    President Zápotocký's condition has continued to improve over the past week. His general health is good. The activity of the circulatory system has returned to normal. The pulse is even and strong. Blood pressure and body temperature are normal. The patient is continuing treatment.

    In view of the good subjective condition of the patient and the objective results of medical examinations, no further communiques concerning the health of President Zápotocký will be issued.

### 萨波托斯基总统病情过去一周中继续好转

1955-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

    萨波托斯基总统病情过去一周中继续好转
    新华社布拉格九日电　九月八日此间发表了关于捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基的健康情况的公报。
    共和国总统萨波托斯基的病情在过去一周中继续好转。总的健康情况良好。血液循环系统的活动已经恢复正常。脉搏均匀而有力。血压和体温正常。病人在继续治疗中。
    鉴于病人的主观情况和医学检查的客观结果均属良好，以后将不再发表关于共和国总统萨波托斯基的健康情况的公报。
