Our Great Motherland

　　The workers of the Dalian Machine Tool Plant are constantly tapping production potential to strive to complete the increased production tasks for the second half of the year. The Youth Production Group for small shafts in the plant's second workshop collectively researched and improved tools in August, and promoted the Soviet Kolesov tool grinding method, completing the monthly task three days ahead of schedule. This is a scene of the group's workers observing the tool grinding method at a technical research meeting.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    大连机床厂职工正在不断地挖掘生产潜力，争取完成下半年的增产任务。这个厂的第二车间小轴青年生产小组在八月份曾集体研究改进工具，并推广了苏联科列索夫车刀法，提前三天完成了全月任务。这是小组工人在技术研究会上观摩车刀法的情形。
