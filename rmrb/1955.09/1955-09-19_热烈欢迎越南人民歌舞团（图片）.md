Title: Warmly Welcome the Vietnamese People's Song and Dance Troupe (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-09-19
Page: 5

The Vietnamese People's Song and Dance Troupe held the opening ceremony of its performance tour in China at the Tianqiao Theater in Beijing on the 17th evening, performing twelve song and dance programs with rich national characteristics. Their performances were warmly welcomed by the Beijing audience.
Right Picture: Liu Zhonglu, head of the Vietnamese People's Song and Dance Troupe, delivering a speech at the opening ceremony of the performance.
		Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong photo

	Bamboo Grove Celebration Dance. This dance is performed by ethnic minorities in the northwestern region of Vietnam during joyful daily life. This is a scene of the Vietnamese People's Army and the people dancing together after the restoration of peace.
		Our Newspaper Wang Zhiyuan Photo

	National Instrument Ensemble. Suite: Flowing Water, Money, Spring Breeze, Dragon and Tiger.
		Our Newspaper Wang Zhiyuan Photo

	Song and Dance Performance: Spring Outing. Our Newspaper Wang Zhiyuan Photo

	Fan Dance. After the restoration of peace, the Dai girls in the northwestern region of Vietnam indulge in playing with flowers and butterflies in the fields, celebrating victory. Our Newspaper reporter Gao Liang Photo

	Chinese literary and art workers and Vietnamese artists backstage. Our Newspaper reporter Gao Liang Photo

	Male and Female Chorus: The East is Red. Our Newspaper reporter Gao Liang Photo

	Solo: Happiness. Performer: Zhu Luan. Our Newspaper reporter Na Yi Photo

	Female Vocal Ensemble: Twisting Thread Through Needle. Our Newspaper Wang Zhiyuan Photo

	Literary and art workers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army are with Vietnamese artists.
		Our Newspaper reporter Na Yi Photo

	After the performance, Zheng Zhenduo and Xia Yan, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Culture, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and others went to the stage to meet the Vietnamese artists, expressing their congratulations and condolences. Our Newspaper Wang Zhiyuan Photo

### 热烈欢迎越南人民歌舞团（图片）

1955-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    热烈欢迎越南人民歌舞团
    越南人民歌舞团十七日晚在北京天桥剧场举行在中国访问演出的开幕式，演出了十二个富有民族特色的歌舞节目。他们的表演受到北京观众的热烈欢迎。
    右图：越南人民歌舞团团长刘仲卢在演出开幕式上致词。
          新华社记者　邹健东摄
    竹丛联欢舞。越南西北地区的少数民族在欢乐日常跳此舞，这是和平恢复后，越南人民军和人民跳舞联欢的一个场面。
        本报　王志渊摄
    民族乐器合奏。组曲：流水，金钱，春风，龙虎。
  　本报　王志渊摄
    歌表演：春游。本报　王志渊摄
    扇子舞。和平恢复后，越南西北地区的傣族少女尽情地在田野里玩花扑蝶，欢庆胜利。　本报记者　高粮摄
    我国文艺工作者和越南艺术家们在后台。本报记者　高粮摄
    男女合唱：东方红。　本报记者　高粮摄
    独唱：快乐。表演者：珠鸾。本报记者　纳一摄
    女声小合唱：捻线穿针。  本报　王志渊摄
    中国人民解放军文艺工作者同越南艺术家在一起。
      本报记者　纳一摄
    演出结束后，文化部副部长郑振铎、夏衍，外交部副部长章汉夫等一同到台上会见越南艺术家们，向他们表示祝贺和慰问。本报　王志渊摄
