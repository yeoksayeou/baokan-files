Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Receives Japanese Diet Member Delegation

Author:
Date: 1955-09-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China received a Japanese Diet Member Delegation at 2:30 PM on September 29th.

    Present at the reception were Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, as well as Zhang Su, Wu Kejian, Qu Wu, and Yu Xinqing, Deputy Secretaries-General. (Photo Attached)
    Above: Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received the Japanese Diet Member Delegation on September 29th. The picture shows Chairman Liu Shaoqi shaking hands with delegation leader Eikichi Kamijoyama.
        Xinhua News Agency Reporter: Qi Guanshan

### 刘少奇委员长接见日本国会议员访华团

1955-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇委员长接见日本国会议员访华团
    新华社二十九日讯　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇在二十九日下午二时半接见了日本国会议员访华团。
    接见时在座的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、陈叔通，副秘书长张苏、吴克坚、屈武、余心清。（附图片）
    上：全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇在九月二十九日接见了日本国会议员访华团。图为刘少奇委员长和上林山荣吉团长握手。
        新华社记者　齐观山摄
