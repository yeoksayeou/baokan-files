Title: Soviet, Ukrainian, and Belorussian Governments Appoint Delegations to the Tenth Session of the UN General Assembly
Author:
Date: 1955-09-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 12th: The Soviet Council of Ministers has appointed the members of the delegation to the tenth session of the United Nations General Assembly, which opens in New York on September 20th, as follows: Head of the delegation - First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov, Deputy Head of the delegation - First Deputy Foreign Minister of the Soviet Union V.V. Kuznetsov, Representatives - Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet and President of the Lithuanian Academy of Sciences Yu.Yu. Matulis, Soviet Ambassador to the United Kingdom Ya.A. Malik, Soviet Permanent Representative to the United Nations A.Ya. Sobolev.
    Alternate Representatives - Soviet Ambassador to the United States G.N. Zarubin, Chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the Georgian Republic and President of the Georgian Republic State University V.V. Kupradze, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Kazakh Republic T.T. Tazhidayev, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union P.N. Kumykin, Deputy Minister of Health of the Russian Federation T.A. Nikolayeva, and many advisors to the delegation.
    In addition, the Government of the Ukrainian Republic has appointed a delegation to the tenth session of the United Nations General Assembly. The head of the delegation is the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Ukrainian Republic, L.F. Palamarchuk.
    The Government of the Belorussian Republic has appointed a delegation to the tenth session of the United Nations General Assembly. The head of the delegation is the Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Belorussian Republic, K.V. Kiselev.

### 苏联、乌克兰和白俄罗斯政府  任命出席联合国大会十届会议代表团人员

1955-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联、乌克兰和白俄罗斯政府
    任命出席联合国大会十届会议代表团人员
    新华社十三日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科十二日讯：苏联部长会议任命了出席九月二十日在纽约开幕的联合国大会第十届会议的代表团人员如下：代表团团长——部长会议第一副主席兼苏联外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫，代表团副团长——苏联外交部第一副部长瓦·瓦·库兹涅佐夫，代表——最高苏维埃主席团委员、立陶宛共和国科学院院长尤·尤·马图利斯，苏联驻英国大使雅·亚·马立克，苏联驻联合国常任代表阿·亚·索波列夫。
    候补代表——苏联驻美国大使格·纳·查鲁宾，格鲁吉亚共和国最高苏维埃主席兼格鲁吉亚共和国国立大学校长维·维·库普拉泽，哈萨克共和国部长会议副主席特·特·塔日达耶夫，苏联对外贸易部副部长帕·尼·库米金，俄罗斯联邦卫生部副部长塔·阿·尼古拉耶娃以及代表团顾问多人。
    又讯　乌克兰共和国政府任命了出席联合国大会第十届会议的代表团。代表团团长是乌克兰共和国外交部长勒·弗·帕拉马尔楚克。
    白俄罗斯共和国政府任命了出席联合国大会第十届会议的代表团。代表团团长是白俄罗斯共和国部长会议副主席兼外交部长库·维·基谢列夫。
