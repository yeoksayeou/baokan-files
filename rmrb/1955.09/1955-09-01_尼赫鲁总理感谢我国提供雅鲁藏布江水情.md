Title: Premier Nehru Thanks Our Country for Providing Hydrological Data on the Yarlung Zangbo River

Author:
Date: 1955-09-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 31st: According to the Press Trust of India, Indian Prime Minister Nehru expressed his gratitude at a press conference held in Dibrugarh, Assam, on the evening of August 30th to the government of the People's Republic of China for providing hydrological data on the Yarlung Zangbo River (the upper reaches of the Brahmaputra River) to India. He said: This information is helpful in arranging the protection work of Dibrugarh City.

Nehru said: The government of the People's Republic of China, at our request, made arrangements to provide us with hydrological data on the Yarlung Zangbo River on time. They established three hydrological stations and sent us daily information on the water level and flow of the Yarlung Zangbo River from there. This is very useful, and I would like to express my gratitude to the Chinese government for this valuable assistance repeatedly given.

### 尼赫鲁总理感谢我国提供雅鲁藏布江水情

1955-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁总理感谢我国提供雅鲁藏布江水情
    新华社新德里三十一日电　据印度报业托辣斯报道：印度总理尼赫鲁八月三十日晚上在阿萨姆省迪布鲁加尔城举行的记者招待会上表示感谢中华人民共和国政府给印度提供雅鲁藏布江（布拉马普特拉河上游）的水情。他说：这种情报是有助于安排迪布鲁加尔城的防护工作的。
    尼赫鲁说：中华人民共和国政府应我们的请求进行了安排，按时给我们提供雅鲁藏布江的水情。他们建立了三个水文站，并且从那里每天发出有关雅鲁藏布江水位和流量的情报。这是很有用的，我愿意对中国政府所一再给予的这种宝贵的协助表示我的感激。
