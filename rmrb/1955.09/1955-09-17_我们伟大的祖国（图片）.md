    Our Great Motherland
    The state-owned Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory officially started production on the 16th. This cotton textile factory is fully equipped with domestically-made machinery, and the operation is good after trial operation. This is the roving workshop after the start of production.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营北京第二棉纺织厂十六日正式开工生产。这个棉纺织厂是完全用国产机器装备起来的，经过试车后运转情况良好。这是开工生产后的粗纱车间。
