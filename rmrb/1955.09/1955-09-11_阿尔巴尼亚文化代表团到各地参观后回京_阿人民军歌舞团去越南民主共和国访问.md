Title: Albanian Cultural Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Various Places; Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe Visits the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1955-09-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 10th—The Albanian Cultural Delegation, headed by Fadil Paçrami, returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 10th after visiting Guangzhou, Hangzhou, Shanghai, and other places.
　　The delegation arrived in Guangzhou, a major city in southern China, on August 26th. They visited the former site of the Peasant Movement Training Institute, Sanyuanli, Huanghuagang, Yuexiu Mountain, and the Lingnan Cultural Relics Palace. They also visited Wenchong Township, a rural area in the suburbs of Guangzhou. Delegation member and writer Aleks Çaçi also visited the boat dwellers on the Pearl River, and painter Nzehmedin Zajmi visited the People's Fine Arts Publishing House and the Ivory Carving Cooperative, creating paintings featuring banana trees in South China and boats on the Pearl River.
　　On September 2nd, the delegation arrived in Hangzhou. In Hangzhou, the delegation visited the Zhejiang Museum, the East China Branch of the Central Academy of Fine Arts, the Zhejiang Linen Textile Factory, the Hangzhou Du Jinsheng Silk Weaving Factory, and the Meijiawu Tea Production Cooperative. They also toured famous scenic spots and historical sites such as West Lake, Lingyin Temple, and Yue Fei's Tomb. Writer Aleks Çaçi paid a solo visit to the famous Peking Opera actor Gai Jiaotian on the afternoon of the 3rd. Gai Jiaotian introduced Çaçi to his research on Peking Opera martial arts performance and warmly invited Çaçi to visit his collection of various folk art works. Gai Jiaotian also gave Çaçi a photo of himself in the role of Wu Song fighting the tiger as a souvenir.
　　After leaving Hangzhou, the delegation spent six days visiting Shanghai. The delegation visited the Lu Xun Memorial Hall, gaining a detailed understanding of Lu Xun's life and his representative works, and laid a wreath at Lu Xun's tomb. The delegation also visited factories, workers' cultural palaces, and workers' residential areas, and separately visited the Liberation Daily, the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese Writers Association, the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese Artists Association, and East China Normal University. They also toured the Shanghai urban area and the Huangpu River.
　　While in Guangzhou and Shanghai, the head of the delegation, Fadil Paçrami, gave a report on "An Overview of Albanian Literature and Art in the Past Ten Years" to Chinese literary and art workers, which was warmly welcomed by the audience.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, September 10th—The Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe concluded its visit and performances in Hangzhou and left Hangzhou by train on the morning of the 10th for a visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. After visiting the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the song and dance troupe will continue to perform and visit various parts of China.
　　From September 6th to 9th, the song and dance troupe braved the heat to perform three shows for the citizens and garrison troops of Hangzhou, with nearly six thousand spectators enjoying the rich and colorful performances of the Albanian artists. At each performance, many programs were performed again amidst the enthusiastic applause of the audience.
　　The song and dance troupe toured famous scenic spots and historical sites such as Yuquan and Lingyin Temple in Hangzhou. On the morning of September 9th, the members of the song and dance troupe took a boat trip on West Lake. Albanian soloists sang songs on the lake.
　　On the evening of the song and dance troupe's arrival in Hangzhou, they attended a reception held by Lin Weixian, Commander of the Zhejiang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Wu Xian, Mayor of Hangzhou, on the lawn by West Lake.

### 阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团到各地参观后回京  阿人民军歌舞团去越南民主共和国访问

1955-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团到各地参观后回京
    阿人民军歌舞团去越南民主共和国访问
    新华社十日讯　以法迪尔·帕奇拉米为首的阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团到广州、杭州、上海等地参观后，在十日下午乘飞机回到北京。
    代表团在八月二十六日到达中国南方的大城市——广州，参观游览了农民运动讲习所旧址、三元里、黄花岗、越秀山、岭南文物宫等处，并访问了广州郊区农村文冲乡。代表团团员、作家阿列克斯·恰奇还访问了珠江的水上居民，画家聂泽梅丁·查伊米参观了人民美术社、象牙雕刻合作社，并以华南的香蕉树和珠江上的船艇为题材作画。
    九月二日，代表团到达杭州。代表团在杭州参观了浙江博物馆、中央美术学院华东分院、浙江麻纺织厂、杭州都锦生丝织厂和梅家坞茶叶生产合作社，还游览了西湖、灵隐寺、岳坟等名胜古迹，作家阿列克斯·恰奇在三日下午单独访问了著名京剧演员盖叫天，盖叫天向恰奇介绍了自己研究京剧武生表演艺术的经过，并热情地招待恰奇参观了他收藏的各种民间艺术作品。盖叫天还把他的一张武松打虎的剧照赠给恰奇作为纪念。
    代表团离杭州后，在上海作了六天的参观访问。代表团曾去鲁迅纪念馆参观，对鲁迅生平事迹和他的代表著作，作了详尽的了解，并到鲁迅墓献花圈。代表团又参观了工厂、工人文化宫和工人住宅区，还分别访问了解放日报社、中国作家协会上海分会、中国美术家协会上海分会、华东师范大学等单位，游览了上海市区和黄浦江。
    代表团团长法迪尔·帕奇拉米在广州和上海时，向中国的文艺工作者作了“阿尔巴尼亚十年来文学艺术概况”的报告，受到听众的热烈欢迎。
    新华社杭州十日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团结束了在杭州的访问演出，在十日清晨乘火车离杭州到越南民主共和国进行访问。歌舞团访问越南民主共和国后，将继续到中国各地演出访问。
    九月六日到九日，歌舞团冒着酷热为杭州市民和驻军作了三场演出，近六千个观众欣赏了阿尔巴尼亚艺术家们丰富多采的表演。每次演出时，都有许多节目在观众的热烈的掌声中作了再次表演。
    歌舞团在杭州游览了玉泉、灵隐寺等名胜古迹。九日上午，歌舞团团员们乘船游览了西湖。阿尔巴尼亚的独唱家们在湖上唱了歌。
    歌舞团到达杭州的当天晚上，出席了中国人民解放军浙江军区司令员林维先和杭州市市长吴宪在西湖畔的草坪上举行的酒会。
