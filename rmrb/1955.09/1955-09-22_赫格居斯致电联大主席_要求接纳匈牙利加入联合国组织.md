Title: Hegedüs Calls on UN General Assembly President to Admit Hungary into UN Organization
Author:
Date: 1955-09-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 21st: The Hungarian News Agency reported that Hegedüs, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic, sent a telegram to the President of the United Nations General Assembly requesting the admission of Hungary into the United Nations Organization.
    In the telegram, Hegedüs said: The peace-loving Hungarian people and their government believe that the Four-Power Summit Conference, as well as the fruitful efforts made by the people and governments of all countries, including the Hungarian people and government, to establish friendly relations between countries, have eliminated all obstacles that previously prevented Hungary from joining the United Nations Organization. The Government of the Hungarian People's Republic expects that the participants of the Tenth Session of the United Nations General Assembly will admit the Hungarian People's Republic into the United Nations.

### 赫格居斯致电联大主席  要求接纳匈牙利加入联合国组织

1955-09-22
第4版()
专栏：

    赫格居斯致电联大主席
    要求接纳匈牙利加入联合国组织
    新华社布拉格二十一日讯　匈牙利通讯社报道，匈牙利人民共和国部长会议主席赫格居斯打电报给联合国大会主席，要求接纳匈牙利加入联合国组织。
    赫格居斯在电报中说：爱好和平的匈牙利人民和他们的政府认为，四国政府首脑会议，以及世界各国人民和政府、包括匈牙利人民和政府在内为建立各国之间的友好关系所作的有成效的努力，已经扫除了一切过去曾经阻止匈牙利加入联合国组织的障碍。匈牙利人民共和国政府期望，联合国大会第十届会议的参加者将接纳匈牙利人民共和国加入联合国。
