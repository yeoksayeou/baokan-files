Title: Pictures
Author: Bai Ying
Date: 1955-09-14
Page: 2

    Above picture: The Xi'an Agricultural Implement Research Institute has trial-produced a new type of Soviet-style horse-drawn hay tedder, which can ted hay more than 300 mu per day, increasing work efficiency by sixty times compared to manual hay tedding. This is the staff checking the specifications of the hay tedder.
    Below picture: The Xi'an Agricultural Implement Research Institute has successfully trial-produced a horse-drawn sprayer, which is easy to use and can flexibly adjust the nozzles and row spacing, and can be operated by only one person and one animal. Using it to kill insects, one can spray 150 mu of fields in ten hours. Xinhua News Agency reporter Bai Ying photographed.

专栏：

    上图：西安农具研究所试制成一种新型的苏联式马拉耧草机，每天能耧草三百多亩，比人工耧草的工作效率提高六十倍。这是工作人员正在检查耧草机的规格。
    下图：西安农具研究所试制成功的一种马拉喷雾器，使用方便，可以灵活调节喷头和行距，只用一人一畜就可操纵。用它来杀虫，十个小时能喷洒一百五十亩田。新华社记者　白媖摄
