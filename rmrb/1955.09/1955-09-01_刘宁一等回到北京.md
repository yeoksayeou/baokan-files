Title: Liu Ningyi and Others Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 31st—Liu Ningyi, head of the Chinese delegation to the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs held in Hiroshima, Japan, and delegation members Cheng Fangwu, Xie Bingxin, Zhao Puchu, and others returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 31st.

### 刘宁一等回到北京

1955-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    刘宁一等回到北京
    新华社三十一日讯　出席在日本广岛举行的禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的中国代表团团长刘宁一和团员成仿吾、谢冰心、赵朴初等，已在三十一日下午乘飞机回到北京。
