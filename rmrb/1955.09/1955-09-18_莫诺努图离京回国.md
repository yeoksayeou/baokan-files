Title: Mononutu Departs Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1955-09-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 17th—Mr. Mononutu, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Indonesia to our country, departed Beijing for home on the 17th. Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs and Director of the Asian Affairs Department, Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department, and Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Sino-Indonesian Friendship Association, went to the station to see him off. Staff members of the Embassy of the Republic of Indonesia in China and diplomatic envoys and staff members of various countries in China also went to see him off.

### 莫诺努图离京回国

1955-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    莫诺努图离京回国
    新华社十七日讯  印度尼西亚共和国首任驻我国特命全权大使莫诺努图已在十七日离京返国。前往车站送行的有我外交部部长助理兼亚洲司司长陈家康、礼宾司司长柯华和中国印度尼西亚友好协会副会长胡愈之。印度尼西亚共和国驻华大使馆人员和各国驻华使节和使馆人员也前往送行。
