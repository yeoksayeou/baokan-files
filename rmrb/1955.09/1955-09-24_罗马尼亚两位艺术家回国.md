Title: Two Romanian Artists Return Home
Author:
Date: 1955-09-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency dispatch:
    Two Romanian experts in decorative and applied arts, Solomon Aella and Gershoyu Maria, who came to China for a visit according to the 1955 executive plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 16th to return to their country.
    From July 10th to September 15th, Aella and Maria visited nine cities, including Beijing, Shenyang, and Shanghai.

### 罗马尼亚两位艺术家回国

1955-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚两位艺术家回国
    据新华社讯　根据中罗文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划来中国访问的罗马尼亚装饰和实用艺术专家索罗曼·艾拉和盖尔邵佑·玛丽娅在十六日晨乘飞机离北京返国。
    艾拉和玛丽娅在七月十日到九月十五日期间，曾先后访问了北京、沈阳、上海等九个城市。
