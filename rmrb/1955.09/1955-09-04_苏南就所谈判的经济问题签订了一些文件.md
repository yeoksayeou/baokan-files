Title: Soviet and Yugoslav sides sign documents on economic issues negotiated

Author:

Date: 1955-09-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd. According to TASS: The economic delegations of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia and the Soviet government signed a number of documents in the Kremlin, Moscow, on the 1st, regarding economic issues discussed by the two governments' economic delegations in Moscow from August 23rd to September 1st.

Signing on behalf of the Yugoslav side was Vukmanović-Tempo, Vice-President of the Federal Executive Council of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, and signing on behalf of the Soviet side was Mikoyan, First Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.

Those attending the signing ceremony included Kaganovich, Malenkov, Pervukhin, Saburov, Suslov, Minister of Chemical Industry Tikhomirov, Minister of Non-Ferrous Metals Industry Lomako, as well as senior officials from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Foreign Trade, the Soviet State Planning Committee, and the Soviet State Economic Committee.

On the Yugoslav side, those attending the signing ceremony included Deputy Secretary of Foreign Affairs Bulkich, National Affairs Advisor to the Secretariat of Foreign Affairs Popovich, Advisor to the Secretariat of Foreign Affairs Sajcic, Advisor to the Federal Economic Planning Bureau Salamon, Advisor to the Secretariat of Foreign Trade of the Federal Executive Council Radojevic, and the Yugoslav Ambassador to the Soviet Union Viditz, among others.

### 苏南就所谈判的经济问题签订了一些文件

1955-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    苏南就所谈判的经济问题签订了一些文件
    新华社二日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国和苏联政府经济代表团，一日在莫斯科克里姆林宫就两国政府经济代表团从八月二十三日到九月一日在莫斯科谈判的经济问题签订了一些文件。
    代表南斯拉夫方面签字的是南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国联邦执行委员会副主席伏克曼诺维奇—泰波，代表苏联方面签字的是苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬。
    参加签字仪式的有卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、苏斯洛夫、化学工业部长季霍米罗夫、有色金属工业部长洛马科以及外交部、对外贸易部、苏联国家计划委员会和苏联国家经济委员会的高级负责人。
    南斯拉夫方面参加签字仪式的有外交副秘书长布尔基奇、外交秘书处国事顾问波波维奇、外交秘书处顾问萨伊吉奇、联邦经济计划局顾问萨拉蒙、联邦执行委员会对外贸易秘书处顾问拉多耶维奇和南斯拉夫驻苏联大使维迪兹等人。
