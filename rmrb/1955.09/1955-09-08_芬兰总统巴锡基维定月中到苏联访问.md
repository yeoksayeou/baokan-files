Title: Finnish President Paasikivi to Visit Soviet Union Mid-Month
Author:
Date: 1955-09-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 7th: According to TASS, at the invitation of Kliment Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Finnish President Juho Kusti Paasikivi will visit Moscow on September 15th this year.

Accompanying President Paasikivi on his visit to Moscow will be: Finnish Prime Minister Urho Kekkonen, Minister of National Defense Emil Skog, and former Foreign Minister Gartz Svinhufvud, as well as the Director of the Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, State Secretary Seppälä, and the President's personal physician, Dr. Koskimies.

During the visit to Moscow, President Paasikivi and his entourage will hold talks with the Soviet government to discuss further development and consolidation of friendship and cooperation between the two countries.

### 芬兰总统巴锡基维定月中到苏联访问

1955-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

    芬兰总统巴锡基维定月中到苏联访问
    新华社七日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：应苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫的邀请，芬兰共和国总统巴锡基维将在今年九月十五日到莫斯科访问。
    随同巴锡基维总统访问莫斯科的将有：芬兰共和国总理吉科宁、国防部部长斯科格和前外交部长斯汶托，以及外交部办公厅主任、国务秘书塞帕拉和总统的私人医生科斯基米耶斯博士。
    在莫斯科访问期间，巴锡基维总统和他的随行人员将同苏联政府进行会谈，讨论进一步发展和巩固两国间的友谊和合作的问题。
