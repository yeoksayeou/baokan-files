Title: Central Representative Dong Biwu and Representatives of All Nationalities Arrive in Urumqi
Author:
Date: 1955-09-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, September 20th. Dong Biwu, the representative of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Central People's Government, who came to participate in the second session of the First People's Congress of Xinjiang Province to congratulate the establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region, arrived in Urumqi on the afternoon of the 19th.

Also arriving at the same time were Liu Chun, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China; Wang Zaitian (Mongolian), Vice Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region; Qin Yingji (Zhuang), Vice Governor of Guangxi Province and Chairman of the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region; Wei Chumbu (Zhuang), Deputy Director of the Department of Transportation of Guangxi Province; Lan Changfa (Yao), Vice Chairman of the First Committee of the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Member of the People's Committee of Guangxi Province; Zhu Dehai (Korean), Vice Governor of Jilin Province and Chairman of the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Region; Dao Jingban (Dai), Member of the People's Committee of Yunnan Province and Chairman of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Region of Yunnan Province; Zhang Chong (Yi), representative of the Yi people from the Greater and Lesser Liang Mountains in Sichuan Province; Ou Baichuan (Miao), Vice Governor of Guizhou Province; Han Zhao'e (Han), Vice Governor of Shaanxi Province; Ma Hongbin (Hui), Vice Governor of Gansu Province; Ce Ren Namu (Tibetan), Deputy Director of the Democratic Women's Federation of Gansu Province; Xi Rao Jia Cuo (Tibetan), Vice Governor of Qinghai Province; and A Houmaiti (Kazak), Chairman of the Alton Qukehasake Autonomous Region of Qinghai Province, among others.

People of all ethnic groups and from all walks of life in Xinjiang Province and Urumqi held a grand welcoming rally at the airport. More than 15,000 people of all ethnic groups lined up to welcome them at and outside the airport. Wang Enmao, First Secretary of the Xinjiang Sub-bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Saifuddin, Fourth Secretary, Baorhan, Chairman of the People's Government of Xinjiang Province, Tao Zhiyue and Zhang Xiqin, Deputy Commanders of the Xinjiang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zeng Di, Deputy Political Commissar, Ren Gobai, First Secretary of the Urumqi Municipal Committee of the CPC and Mayor of Urumqi, and representatives of all ethnic groups to the People's Congress of Xinjiang Province all went to the airport to welcome them.

At the welcoming rally, Baorhan, Chairman of the People's Government of Xinjiang Province, delivered a welcoming speech. On behalf of the people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang, he expressed a warm welcome and gratitude to Dong Biwu, representative of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Central People's Government, and all the guests with incomparable excitement, and expressed sincere thanks to the great leader Chairman Mao, the Party Central Committee, and the Central People's Government for their earnest care for the people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang. He said that the arrival of everyone more specifically demonstrates the great unity of the people of all ethnic groups in China. We firmly believe that after the establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region, under the correct leadership of the Party Central Committee, the Central People's Government, and Chairman Mao, we will be able to completely eliminate the de facto inequalities left over from history and, together with the people of the whole country, build our great motherland into a prosperous, happy, and prosperous socialist society.

Dong Biwu spoke at the welcoming rally. He said, "Today I have come to Urumqi to participate in the second session of the First People's Congress of Xinjiang Province on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Central People's Government. With me are representatives from all provinces of the motherland and from various ethnic autonomous regions, including representatives of the Mongolian, Hui, Tibetan, Zhuang, Miao, Yi, Korean, Dai, Yao, Kazak, and Han nationalities. Here, we will discuss together with you the issue of the establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region, and we will also celebrate the establishment of the Uyghur Autonomous Region together with the people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang. We are very excited to be able to personally participate in this historically significant event for the people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang and also for the people of all ethnic groups in the motherland. Today's meeting in Urumqi between representatives of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang and representatives of other ethnic groups within the motherland is also very meaningful. This shows that the close unity, cooperation, and mutual assistance among the ethnic groups in our great motherland's big family have been further strengthened."

As Dong Biwu and the other representatives drove through the welcoming crowds, people threw bouquets of flowers at them, and the streets were filled with cheers.

### 中央代表董必武和各民族代表到乌鲁木齐

1955-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中央代表董必武和各民族代表到乌鲁木齐
    新华社乌鲁木齐二十日电　前来参加新疆省第一届人民代表大会第二次会议，祝贺新疆维吾尔自治区成立的中国共产党中央委员会和中央人民政府代表董必武，在十九日下午到达乌鲁木齐市。
    同时前来的还有中华人民共和国民族事务委员会副主任刘春，内蒙古自治区副主席王再天（蒙古族），广西省副省长、桂西僮族自治区主席覃应机（僮族），广西省交通厅副厅长韦纯束（僮族），中国人民政协桂西僮族自治区第一届委员会副主席、广西省人民委员会委员蓝昌法（瑶族），吉林省副省长、延边朝鲜族自治区主席朱德海（朝鲜族），云南省人民委员会委员、云南省德宏傣族景颇族自治区主席刀京版（傣族），四川省大小凉山彝族代表张冲（彝族），贵州省副省长欧百川（苗族），陕西省副省长韩兆鹗（汉族），甘肃省副省长马鸿宾（回族）、甘肃省民主妇女联合会副主任策仁娜姆（藏族），青海省副省长喜饶嘉错（藏族），青海省阿尔顿曲克哈萨克族自治区主席阿后买提（哈萨克族）等。
    新疆省和乌鲁木齐各族各界人民在机场上举行了隆重的欢迎大会。机场上和机场外列队欢迎的有一万五千多各民族人民。中共中央新疆分局第一书记王恩茂、第四书记赛福鼎，新疆省人民政府主席鲍尔汉，中国人民解放军新疆军区副司令员陶峙岳、张希钦、副政治委员曾涤，中共乌鲁木齐市委员会第一书记、乌鲁木齐市市长任戈白和新疆省人民代表大会各民族代表都到机场欢迎。
    在欢迎大会上，新疆省人民政府主席鲍尔汉致欢迎词。他代表新疆各族人民以无比兴奋的心情向中国共产党中央委员会和中央人民政府代表董必武和各位来宾表示热烈欢迎和感谢，并向殷切关怀新疆各族人民的伟大领袖毛主席、党中央和中央人民政府致衷心谢意。他说，大家的到来更加具体地表明了中国各族人民的大团结。我们坚决相信，新疆维吾尔自治区成立以后，在党中央，中央人民政府和毛主席的正确领导下，一定能够完全消灭历史上遗留下来事实上的不平等，同全国人民一道将伟大祖国建设成繁荣、幸福、康乐的社会主义社会。
    董必武在欢迎会上讲话。他说，我今天来到乌鲁木齐市，是代表中国共产党中央委员会和中央人民政府来参加新疆省第一届人民代表大会第二次会议的。和我同来的还有来自祖国各省的、各个民族自治地方的，包括蒙古族、回族、藏族、僮族、苗族、彝族、朝鲜族、傣族、瑶族、哈萨克族、汉族等民族人民的代表。在这里，我们将要和大家一起共同来讨论关于成立新疆维吾尔自治区的问题，并且还要和新疆各族人民共同来庆祝维吾尔自治区的成立。我们能够亲身参加新疆各民族人民的也是祖国各民族人民的一件有历史意义的大事，感到十分兴奋。今天新疆各民族和祖国境内其他民族的代表在乌鲁木齐会面，也是很有意义的，这表现我们伟大祖国大家庭中各民族亲密团结、合作互助的关系更加增强了。
    董必武和其他代表乘车通过列队欢迎的人群时，人们把一束束鲜花投向他们，街道上一片欢呼声。
