    Our Great Motherland
    The new school year has begun. Nearly five million nine hundred thousand primary and secondary school students across the country have successively started their new school year. Two Young Pioneers from Beijing Yu Ying Primary School are accompanying new students to familiarize themselves with the school environment.

    我们伟大的祖国
