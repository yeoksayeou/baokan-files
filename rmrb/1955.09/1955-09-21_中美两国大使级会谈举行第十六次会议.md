Title: The 16th Meeting of the Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Held
Author: 
Date: 1955-09-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, September 20th—The 16th meeting of the Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks was held at the Palais des Nations at 10:00 a.m. on September 20th. In a jointly agreed-upon communiqué issued after the meeting, both sides stated that they exchanged information on the implementation of the agreed-upon statements and exchanged views on the second item on the agenda.

    The September 20th meeting adjourned at 12:00 p.m. The next meeting is scheduled to be held at 10:00 a.m. on Friday (September 23rd).

### 中美两国大使级会谈举行第十六次会议

1955-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中美两国大使级会谈举行第十六次会议
    新华社日内瓦二十日电　中美两国大使级会谈二十日上午十时在国联大厦举行了第十六次会议。双方在会后发表的一致同意的公报中说，双方就协议的声明的执行交换了情况，并且就第二项议程交换了意见。
    二十日会议在上午十二点休会。下次会议定于星期五（九月二十三日）上午十时举行。
