Title: Chinese Art Troupe Holds Grand Opening Ceremony in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1955-09-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 1st - The Chinese Art Troupe held a grand opening ceremony at the Hanoi Grand Theater at 8 p.m. on August 31st and presented its first song and dance performance in Vietnam.

    Those who attended the opening ceremony and watched the performance included: Truong Chinh, General Secretary of the Vietnam Workers' Party, and members of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party; Ton Duc Thang, Acting Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of Vietnam, and members of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly; Hoang Quoc Viet, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front, and members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee; ministers and deputy ministers of various government departments; senior officers of the Vietnamese People's Army; Sun Guangfa, representative of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association; representatives and leaders of various democratic parties and mass organizations in Vietnam; the chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee; and representatives of mass organizations in Hanoi and representatives of overseas Chinese, totaling more than 700 people.

    Also present were Luo Guibo, Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, and diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in Vietnam.

    Also present were all members of the Korean People's Delegation and the Korean Art Troupe, who had returned to Hanoi to participate in the Vietnamese National Day celebrations after visiting various parts of Vietnam.

    At the opening ceremony, To Huu, Deputy Minister of Propaganda of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, delivered the opening speech. He expressed a warm welcome on behalf of the Vietnamese government and people to the Chinese Art Troupe's visit and performance. To Huu said that he believed that the Chinese Art Troupe's visit and performance in Vietnam would further develop the friendship between the people of Vietnam and China with its glorious tradition.

    Ding Xilin, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, said in reply that the Chinese Art Troupe was very honored to have the opportunity to visit heroic Vietnam on the eve of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The arduous struggle and glorious victory of the Vietnamese people have greatly inspired the fraternal Chinese people, the people of the world, and the oppressed nations. The Chinese people are particularly grateful to the Vietnamese people for their great contribution to safeguarding peace in Asia.

    Ding Xilin said that all comrades of the Chinese Art Troupe are determined to do their best to introduce the art of the Chinese people who are building socialism to the Vietnamese people, and to learn from the Vietnamese people's experience and achievements in various fields, thereby promoting cultural exchanges between China and Vietnam and mutual understanding between the two peoples.

    Ding Xilin also expressed his deep gratitude to the government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for their warm welcome to the Chinese Art Troupe.

    After the head of the delegation, Ding Xilin, gave his reply, Vietnamese children presented flowers to Ding Xilin, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, and Wu Baihua, deputy head of the delegation, and others. At this time, warm applause resounded throughout the venue.

    Then, the song and dance performance began. The Chinese Art Troupe sang songs such as "Singing of the Vietnam Workers' Party," "Singing of Ho Chi Minh," "The East is Red," and "Dien Bien Phu Victory Song," and performed dances such as "Tea Picking and Butterfly Catching Dance," "Lotus Dance," and "Bamboo Grove Carnival Dance." They also performed the Peking Opera "Havoc in the Dragon Palace" and flute and suona solos. Every program was warmly welcomed.

    After the performance, representatives of the Vietnamese people and representatives of the Korean Art Troupe presented flowers to the Chinese Art Troupe, and a storm of applause resounded throughout the venue.

### 我国艺术团在河内举行隆重的演出开幕式

1955-09-02
第4版()
专栏：

    我国艺术团在河内举行隆重的演出开幕式
    新华社河内一日电　中国艺术团八月三十一日晚八时在河内大戏院举行隆重的演出开幕式，并举行了在越南的首次歌舞表演会。
    出席演出开幕式和观看表演的有：越南劳动党总书记长征和劳动党中央委员们，越南国民大会常务委员会代理主席孙德胜和国民大会常务委员们，越南国民联合战线全国委员会副主席黄国越和全国委员会常务委员们，政府各部部长和副部长，越南人民军的高级军官，越中友好协会代表孙光阀，越南各民主党派、各群众团体的代表和负责人，河内市行政委员会的主席以及河内各群众团体代表和华侨代表等共七百多人。
    出席的还有中国驻越南大使罗贵波、各国驻越南的使节和外交官员。
    出席的还有在越南各地参观和访问后回到河内参加越南国庆节的朝鲜人民代表团和朝鲜艺术团全体人员。
    在开幕式上，越南民主共和国政府宣传部副部长素友致开幕词。他代表越南政府和人民表示热烈欢迎中国艺术团的访问和演出。素友说，他相信中国艺术团在越南的访问演出一定能以它的光辉传统进一步发展越中两国人民的友谊。
    中国艺术团团长丁西林致答词说，中国艺术团能有机会在越南民主共和国十周年国庆的前夕访问英雄的越南，感到十分荣幸。越南人民的艰苦斗争和光辉胜利，对于兄弟的中国人民，对于全世界人民和被压迫民族，有极大的鼓舞作用。中国人民特别感谢越南人民对于保卫亚洲和平所作的巨大贡献。
    丁西林说，中国艺术团的全体同志，决心用最大的努力向越南人民介绍正在建设社会主义的中国人民的艺术，并且向越南人民学习各方面的经验和成就，从而促进中越两国之间的文化交流和两国人民之间的互相了解。
    丁西林还对越南民主共和国政府和人民对中国艺术团的热情欢迎表示深切的感谢。
    丁西林团长致答词后，越南少年儿童向中国艺术团团长丁西林、副团长吴白桦等献花。这时，会场响起了热烈的掌声。
    接着，歌舞表演开始。中国艺术团演唱了“歌唱越南劳动党”、“歌唱胡志明”、“东方红”、“奠边府胜利歌”等歌曲，表演了“采茶扑蝶舞”、“荷花舞”、“竹丛联欢舞”等舞蹈，还演出了京剧“闹龙宫”和笛子、唢呐独奏等节目。每一个节目都受到了热烈的欢迎。
    表演结束后，越南人民的代表和朝鲜艺术团的代表向中国艺术团献花，全场响起了暴风雨般的掌声。
