Title: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Holds its 22nd Meeting; Passes Resolution on Granting the Military Rank of Marshal of the People's Republic of China; Passes Resolution on Granting Medals to Personnel with Meritorious Service During the Chinese People's Revolutionary War

Author:
Date: 1955-09-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 22nd meeting on September 23rd.

The meeting, in accordance with the Regulations on the Service of Officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, reviewed the list of personnel recommended by the Premier of the State Council to be granted the military rank of Marshal of the People's Republic of China. It was decided to grant Zhu De, Peng Dehuai, Lin Biao, Liu Bocheng, He Long, Chen Yi, Luo Ronghuan, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, and Ye Jianying, senior generals who made outstanding contributions to the creation and leadership of the people's armed forces and the leadership of army corps operations, the military rank of Marshal of the People's Republic of China.

The meeting then, in accordance with the Regulations of the People's Republic of China on Granting Medals and Orders to Personnel with Meritorious Service in the Chinese People's Liberation Army during the Chinese People's Revolutionary War, reviewed the first list of personnel submitted by the Premier of the State Council for the granting of medals. It was decided to grant Zhu De, Peng Dehuai, Lin Biao, Liu Bocheng, He Long, Chen Yi, Luo Ronghuan, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Ye Jianying, Wan Yi, Wang Hongkun, Wang Enmao, Wang Xinting, Wang Zhen, Wang Zheng, Wang Shusheng, Kong Congzhou, Gan Siqi, Zhu Liangcai, Cheng Jun, Liu Yalou, Li Tianhuan, Li Kenong, Li Yanlu, Li Shouxuan, Li Da, Li Tao, Song Renqiong, Song Shilun, Yu Qiuli, Wu Faxian, Lü Zhengcao, Chen Shiju, Chen Zaidao, Chen Xianrui, Chen Bojun, Chen Qihan, Chen Geng, Chen Xilian, Zhou Shidi, Zhou Baozhong, Zhou Huan, Zhou Chunquan, Luo Ruiqing, Su Zhenhua, Hong Xuezhi, Xu Liqing, Xu Haidong, Muerguofu·Yisikagefu, Tang Liang, Wei Guoqing, Ulanhu, Zhang Lingbin, Zhang Zongxun, Zhang Xuesi, Zhang Aiping, Zhang Guohua, Zhang Yunyi, Zhang Dazhi, Zhang Jingwu, Caodanuofu·Zhayier, Guo Tianmin, Xu Shiyou, Xu Guangda, Bi Zhanyun, Cui Tianmin, Peng Shaohui, He Bingyan, Fu Qiutao, Fu Lianzhang, Fu Zhong, Cheng Shicai, Huang Kecheng, Huang Yongsheng, Zeng Sheng, Su Yu, Feng Baiju, Feng Zhongyun, Maimaitiyiming·Yiminnuofu, Yang Zhicheng, Yang Chengwu, Yang Dezhi, Ye Fei, Zhao Erlu, Zhao Shoushan, Liao Hansheng, Deng Hua, Zheng Weishan, Yan Hongyan, Yan Kuiyao, Lai Chuanzhu, Xie Fuzhi, Zhong Qiguang, Saifuding, Xiao Ke, Xiao Jingguang, Xiao Hua, Han Xianchu, Han Zhenji, Tan Zheng, Tan Guansan, and Tan Jiashu, Rao Zhengxi, with the First Class August 1st Medal, the First Class Independence Freedom Medal, and the First Class Liberation Medal, respectively, for their meritorious service during the Agrarian Revolutionary War, the War of Resistance Against Japan, and the War of Liberation.

The meeting decided to award the First Class Liberation Medal to Liu Wenhui, Liu Shanben, Li Minghao, Wu Huawen, He Jifeng, Chen Mingren, Lin Zun, Apei·Awangjinmei, Gao Shuxun, Ma Hongbin, Zhang Kexia, Zhang Zhizhong, Zhang Zhen, Tao Zhiyue, Fu Zuoyi, Cheng Qian, Zeng Zesheng, Dong Qiwu, Pei Changhui, Deng Zhaoxiang, Deng Baoshan, Deng Xihou, Pan Shoduan, Lu Han, and Han Liancheng, personnel who directly led the uprising of the original Kuomintang army during the Chinese People's Liberation War, personnel who made contributions to the People's Liberation War, and personnel who made contributions to the peaceful liberation of Tibet.

This meeting decided to award the First Class August 1st Medal to 131 people in the first batch; the First Class Independence Freedom Medal to 117 people in the first batch; and the First Class Liberation Medal to 570 people in the first batch.

Resolution of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on Granting the Military Rank of Marshal of the People's Republic of China

(Adopted at the 22nd Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on September 23, 1955)

The 22nd Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on September 23, 1955, in accordance with the Regulations on the Service of Officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, reviewed the proposal submitted by Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council and decided to grant Zhu De, Peng Dehuai, Lin Biao, Liu Bocheng, He Long, Chen Yi, Luo Ronghuan, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, and Ye Jianying the military rank of Marshal of the People's Republic of China.

Resolution of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on Granting First Class August 1st Medal, First Class Independence Freedom Medal, and First Class Liberation Medal to Personnel with Meritorious Service During the Chinese People's Revolutionary War

(Adopted at the 22nd Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on September 23, 1955)

On September 23, 1955, the 22nd Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, in accordance with the Regulations of the People's Republic of China on Granting Medals and Orders to Personnel with Meritorious Service in the Chinese People's Liberation Army during the Chinese People's Revolutionary War, reviewed the first list of personnel submitted by Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council for the granting of the First Class August 1st Medal, the First Class Independence Freedom Medal, and the First Class Liberation Medal to personnel with meritorious service during the Chinese People's Revolutionary War, and decided to grant the First Class August 1st Medal to Zhu De and 130 others, the First Class Independence Freedom Medal to Zhu De and 116 others, and the First Class Liberation Medal to Zhu De and 569 others.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行第二十二次会议  通过授予中华人民共和国元帅军衔的决议  并通过授予在中国人民革命战争时期有功人员勋章的决议

第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行第二十二次会议
    通过授予中华人民共和国元帅军衔的决议
    并通过授予在中国人民革命战争时期有功人员勋章的决议
    新华社二十三日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在九月二十三日举行了第二十二次会议。
    会议根据中国人民解放军军官服役条例审议了国务院总理建议授予中华人民共和国元帅军衔的名单，决定授予对创建和领导人民武装力量、领导战役军团作战、立有卓越功勋的高级将领朱德、彭德怀、林彪、刘伯承、贺龙、陈毅、罗荣桓、徐向前、聂荣臻、叶剑英以中华人民共和国元帅军衔。
    会议接着根据中华人民共和国授予中国人民解放军在中国人民革命战争时期有功人员的勋章奖章条例，审议了国务院总理提请授予勋章的第一批名单，决定对在中国人民革命战争工农红军时期、抗日战争时期和解放战争时期的有功人员朱德、彭德怀、林彪、刘伯承、贺龙、陈毅、罗荣桓、徐向前、聂荣臻、叶剑英、万毅、王宏坤、王恩茂、王新亭、王震、王诤、王树声、孔从周、甘泗淇、朱良才、成钧、刘亚楼、李天焕、李克农、李延禄、李寿轩、李达、李涛、宋任穷、宋时轮、余秋里、吴法宪、吕正操、陈士榘、陈再道、陈先瑞、陈伯钧、陈奇涵、陈赓、陈锡联、周士第、周保中、周桓、周纯全、罗瑞卿、苏振华、洪学智、徐立清、徐海东、马尔果甫·伊斯卡果夫、唐亮、韦国清、乌兰夫、张令彬、张宗逊、张学思、张爱萍、张国华、张云逸、张达志、张经武、曹达诺夫·扎依尔、郭天民、许世友、许光达、毕占云、崔田民、彭绍辉、贺炳炎、傅秋涛、傅连暲、傅钟、程世才、黄克诚、黄永胜、曾生、粟裕、冯白驹、冯仲云、买买提伊敏·伊敏诺夫、杨至成、杨成武、杨得志、叶飞、赵尔陆、赵寿山、廖汉生、邓华、郑维山、阎红彦、阎揆要、赖传珠、谢富治、钟期光、赛福鼎、萧克、萧劲光、萧华、韩先楚、韩振纪、谭政、谭冠三、谭家述、饶正锡等人，分别授予一级八一勋章、一级独立自由勋章、一级解放勋章。

    这次会议决定第一批授予一级八一勋章的有一百三十一人；第一批授予一级独立自由勋章的有一百一十七人；第一批授予一级解放勋章的有五百七十人。
    中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于授予中华人民共和国元帅军衔的决议
    （一九五五年九月二十三日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十二次会议通过）
    一九五五年九月二十三日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十二次会议，根据中国人民解放军军官服役条例，审议了国务院周恩来总理提出的建议，决定授予朱德、彭德怀、林彪、刘伯承、贺龙、陈毅、罗荣桓、徐向前、聂荣臻、叶剑英以中华人民共和国元帅军衔。
    中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于授予在中国人民革命战争时期有功人员一级八一勋章、一级独立自由勋章、一级解放勋章的决议
    （一九五五年九月二十三日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十二次会议通过）
