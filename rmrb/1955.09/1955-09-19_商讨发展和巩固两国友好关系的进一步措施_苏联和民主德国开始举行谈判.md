Title: Negotiations Begin Between the Soviet Union and the German Democratic Republic to Discuss Further Measures to Develop and Consolidate Friendly Relations Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1955-09-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 18th. TASS Moscow, September 17th: On September 17th, Moscow time 16:00, the government delegations of the Soviet Union and the German Democratic Republic began negotiations in the Grand Kremlin Palace in Moscow to discuss further measures for the development and consolidation of friendly relations between the Soviet Union and the German Democratic Republic.

    Participating in the negotiations on the Soviet side were: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Bulganin; Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet, Khrushchev; Minister of Foreign Trade, Kabanov; Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Semenov; Plenipotentiary Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, Pushkin; and Director of the Third European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Rabin.

    Participating in the negotiations on the German Democratic Republic side were: Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Grotewohl; Deputy Prime Ministers Ulbricht, Stoph, and Nuschke; Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Bolz; Chairman of the State Planning Commission, Rau; Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Koenig; Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the People's Chamber, Florin; Chairman of the National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany, Correns; General Secretary of the Democratic Peasants' Party of Germany, Roß; General Secretary of the Liberal Democratic Party of Germany, Gierlach; and Secretary of the delegation, Ambassador Gross.

    Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Bulganin, and Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Grotewohl, delivered speeches successively.

    After an exchange of views, both sides decided that the next meeting would be held on the morning of September 19th.

### 商讨发展和巩固两国友好关系的进一步措施  苏联和民主德国开始举行谈判

1955-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

    商讨发展和巩固两国友好关系的进一步措施
    苏联和民主德国开始举行谈判
    新华社十八日讯　塔斯社莫斯科十七日讯：九月十七日莫斯科时间十六时，苏联和德意志民主共和国两国政府代表团在莫斯科大克里姆林宫开始谈判，商讨关于发展和巩固苏联和德意志民主共和国友好关系的进一步措施。
    苏联方面参加谈判的，有苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁、最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫、对外贸易部长卡巴诺夫、外交部副部长谢苗诺夫、驻德意志民主共和国全权大使普希金、外交部第三欧洲司司长拉宾。
    德意志民主共和国方面参加谈判的，有德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥，副总理乌布利希、斯多夫、努舍克，副总理兼外交部长博尔茨，国家计划委员会主席劳施纳，驻苏联大使柯尼希，人民议院外交委员会主席弗罗林，民主德国全国阵线全国委员会主席柯伦斯，民主农民党总书记罗斯，自由民主党总书记吉尔拉赫，代表团秘书格罗斯大使。
    苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥先后发言。
    双方在交换意见以后，决定下次会议在九月十九日上午举行。
