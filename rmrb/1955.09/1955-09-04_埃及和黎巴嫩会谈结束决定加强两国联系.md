Title: Egypt and Lebanon Talks Conclude, Decide to Strengthen Bilateral Ties
Author:
Date: 1955-09-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 3rd: According to TASS, the talks between Lebanese Foreign Minister Frangieh and Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser have concluded here.

    The official communique emphasized: "Both sides attach great importance to achieving their common goals and have agreed to take effective practical measures." At the same time, both sides also agreed that the two governments will consult on all issues involving international affairs, defense, common security, and issues of common interest to Egypt and Lebanon.

    The communique stipulates the strengthening of economic, cultural, and social ties between Egypt and Lebanon.

    Egyptian newspapers emphasized the importance of the Egypt-Lebanon talks in strengthening cooperation among Arab countries. The "Republic News," close to government circles, wrote that it is hoped that after the Egypt-Lebanon talks, "the leaders of other Arab countries will hold talks to strengthen the Arab League so that it can complete the tasks before it."

### 埃及和黎巴嫩会谈结束决定加强两国联系

1955-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及和黎巴嫩会谈结束决定加强两国联系
    新华社三日讯　据塔斯社开罗三日讯：黎巴嫩外交部长弗朗吉和埃及总理纳赛尔之间的会谈已经在这里结束。
    官方公报中着重指出：“双方对实现它们的共同目标十分重视，并且同意采取有效的实际措施”。同时双方还同意两国政府将在涉及国际问题、防御、共同安全方面的一切问题以及对埃及和黎巴嫩两国有共同利害关系的问题上进行磋商。
    公报规定加强埃及和黎巴嫩两国之间的经济、文化和社会联系。
    埃及报纸着重指出埃及——黎巴嫩会谈对加强阿拉伯国家之间合作的重要性。接近政府人士的“共和国报”写道，大家希望，在埃及—黎巴嫩会谈之后，“其他阿拉伯国家的领导人将举行会谈，以加强阿拉伯国家联盟，使它能够完成摆在它面前的任务”。
