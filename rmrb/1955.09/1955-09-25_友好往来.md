Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author: 
Date: 1955-09-25
Page: 4

	Friendly Exchanges
	Korean Agricultural Delegation
	Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: The 20-member agricultural delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 23rd.
	The delegation is headed by Song Bong-uk, Chairman of the People's Committee of Hwanghae-bukto Province.
	Those who went to the station to welcome them were: Liu Ruilong, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Agriculture of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Junming, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Water Conservancy, and Du Runsheng, Secretary General of the Rural Work Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and others.
	Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, also went to the station to welcome them.
	On the evening of the 24th, Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture, hosted a banquet for all members of the delegation.
	Burmese Military Delegation
	Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 24th: The Burmese Military Delegation left Guangzhou for Beijing on the 24th. Wen Niansheng, Chen Renqi, and other leaders of the Guangzhou Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army saw them off at the station.
	On the 23rd, the delegation visited the Guangzhou Military Sports School. All staff and students of the Military Sports School warmly welcomed the distinguished guests and performed various sports activities. Shi Zhenhe, Xiao Shanzu, and Li Junwei, who have broken national weightlifting records in the light heavyweight, lightweight, and light featherweight categories respectively, performed weightlifting for the guests. The distinguished guests also watched a company's drill at the Military Sports School.
	In addition, the distinguished guests also toured scenic spots such as Yuexiu Mountain and viewed the cityscape of Guangzhou.
	Italian Cultural Delegation
	Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: The Italian Cultural Delegation, consisting of 21 members led by Piero Calamandrei, arrived in Beijing on the 24th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. Representatives from the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the China Political Science and Law Association, the Chinese Writers Association, the Chinese Medical Association, and the All-China Journalists Association welcomed them at the airport.
	Mongolian Writer Dashiin Dandamba
	Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: Dashiin Dandamba, Mongolian writer and secretary of the Mongolian Writers Association, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 24th, according to the 1955 executive plan of the Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.
	German Mathematician and Exhibition Expert
	Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: Professor Dr. Kurt Schröder, Democratic German mathematician, academician of the German Academy of Sciences, and Deputy Director of the Research Department of Humboldt University, who is coming to China to lecture according to the 1955 executive plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, and He. Tucholski, an exhibition expert who is coming to China to assist in the preparation of the "Exhibition of Fine Arts of the German Democratic Republic," arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 22nd.
	French Senators
	Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: Edmond Michelet, René Haby, Bernard Chochoy and others, Senators of the French Republic who came to China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, left Beijing by plane on the 24th. Senator Léo Hamon will remain in Beijing for further visits.

### 友好往来

1955-09-25
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    朝鲜农业考察团
    新华社二十四日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国农业考察团二十人二十三日晚乘车到达北京。
    考察团由团长、黄海北道人民委员会委员长宋凤郁率领。
    前往车站欢迎的有：中华人民共和国农业部副部长刘瑞龙、水利部副部长周骏鸣、中共中央农村工作部秘书长杜润生等。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使崔一也到车站欢迎。
    二十四日晚，农业部部长廖鲁言设宴招待考察团全体人员。
    缅甸军事代表团
    新华社广州二十四日电　缅甸联邦军事代表团二十四日离开广州前往北京，中国人民解放军广州驻军领导机关首长文年生、陈仁麒等都到车站送行。
    代表团在二十三日参观了广州的军事体育学校。军事体育学校全体工作人员和学员，热烈欢迎贵宾们的到来，并表演了各项体育活动。曾经分别打破次重级、轻量级、次轻级举重全国纪录的石振和、萧山祖、李君伟三人，向客人们表演了举重。贵宾们在军事体育学校，还观看了一个连队的操练。
    此外，贵宾们在广州还游览了越秀山等风景名胜，观看了广州市容。
    意大利文化代表团
    新华社二十四日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问的意大利文化代表团一行二十一人，在团长皮埃罗·卡拉曼德雷率领下在二十四日到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国人民对外文化协会、中国政治法律学会、中国作家协会、中华医学会和中国新闻工作者联谊会等单位的代表。
    蒙古作家达希登丹巴
    新华社二十四日讯　根据中蒙文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划来我国访问的蒙古作家、蒙古作家协会书记达希登丹巴，在二十四日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    民主德国数学家、展览专家
    新华社二十三日讯　根据中德文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划来我国讲学的民主德国数学家、德国科学院院士、洪堡大学研究部副主任库·施罗德教授博士和来华协助筹备“德意志民主共和国造型艺术展览会”的展览专家赫·图霍尔斯基已在二十二日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    法国参议员
    新华社二十四日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来中国访问的法兰西共和国参议员埃德蒙·密歇勒、雷纳·昂扎贝尔、贝纳尔·绍什瓦等人已在二十四日乘飞机离开北京。参议员利奥·阿蒙仍留北京继续参观访问。
