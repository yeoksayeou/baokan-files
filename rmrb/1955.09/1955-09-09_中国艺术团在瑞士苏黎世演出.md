Title: Chinese Art Troupe Performs in Zurich, Switzerland
Author:
Date: 1955-09-09
Page: 3
The Chinese Art Troupe Performs in Zurich, Switzerland

    The Chinese Art Troupe performed in Zurich, Switzerland on the 4th. Those who came to watch the performance included the Vice Governor of the Canton of Zurich, the Mayor of Zurich, consuls from various countries stationed in Zurich, and more than a thousand people from all walks of life.

    The programs performed by the Chinese Art Troupe were warmly welcomed, with a total of forty-five curtain calls throughout the performance. The "National Newspaper" commented on the performance, saying that the excellent level of the Chinese actors was "never seen before." The "New Zurich Newspaper" believed that the Chinese actors were "extraordinary actors" and that their performances were "a combination of music, drama, and dance performance."

    The Governor of the Canton of Zurich, Walter Ross, and the Mayor of Zurich, Larot, respectively received Zhang Zhixiang, the head of the Chinese Art Troupe, and Zhao Feng, the deputy head of the troupe, on the 5th. Feng Xuan, the Chinese Ambassador to Switzerland, accompanied them. That noon, Mayor Larot hosted a banquet for Zhang Zhixiang, Zhao Feng, the main actors of the art troupe, and Ambassador Feng Xuan, and exchanged gifts such as picture albums.

    The Chinese Art Troupe will continue its performance in Geneva on the 7th.
                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

    Zhao Feng, the deputy head of the Chinese Art Troupe, which is currently visiting and performing in Basel, Switzerland, was invited on the 2nd to visit Schmidt, the director of the Basel Art Museum, and Noller von Kulm, the director of the Music Academy. During the conversation, both sides expressed their willingness to promote contact and strengthen cultural exchanges between the two countries in the future.

    Currently, all members of the Chinese Art Troupe have visited the Basel Art Museum's valuable exhibits, and the works of the great Swiss painter Holbein have particularly impressed the members of the Chinese Art Troupe. At the same time, almost all the drama, opera, and ballet actors in the city of Basel have watched the performance of the Chinese Art Troupe, and they highly appreciate the high artistic level of Chinese classical drama. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中国艺术团在瑞士苏黎世演出

1955-09-09
第3版()
专栏：

    中国艺术团在瑞士苏黎世演出
    中国艺术团四日在瑞士苏黎世演出。前来观剧的有苏黎世州副州长、苏黎世市市长、各国驻苏黎世领事和各界人士一千多人。
    中国艺术团演出节目受到热烈的欢迎，整场谢幕达四十五次。“民族报”在评论这次演出时说，中国演员们的优秀水平是“从来没有看见过的”。“新苏黎世报”认为，中国演员们都是“非凡的演员”，他们的表演是“音乐和戏剧舞蹈表演的结合”。
    苏黎世州州长瓦特尔罗斯和苏黎世市市长拉罗特在五日分别接见了中国艺术团团长张致祥和副团长赵沨，陪同前往的还有中国驻瑞士公使冯铉。这天中午，拉罗特市长宴请了张致祥、赵沨、艺术团主要演员和冯铉公使，并且互相交换了画册等礼物。
    中国艺术团七日将在日内瓦继续演出。
                                  （新华社）
    现在正在瑞士巴塞尔作访问演出的中国艺术团副团长赵沨，二日应邀访问了巴塞尔美术博物馆馆长施密特和音乐院院长诺勒·冯·库尔姆。在谈话时，双方一致表示愿意在今后促进彼此间的联系和加强两国的文化交流。
    目前，中国艺术团全体人员已经参观了巴塞尔美术博物馆的名贵展览品，瑞士大画家赫尔拜因的作品特别给中国艺术团人员以深刻的印象。同时，巴塞尔城所有的话剧、歌剧、舞剧演员也差不多都观看了中国艺术团的演出，他们非常赞赏中国古典戏剧的高度艺术水平。（新华社）
