Title: The Eighteenth Meeting of the Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Held
Author:
Date: 1955-09-30
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, September 28th. The eighteenth meeting of the Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks was held at the Palais des Nations at 10:00 a.m. on the 28th. In a joint communiqué unanimously agreed upon by both sides after the meeting, it was stated that both sides continued to exchange information on the implementation of the agreed statement and exchanged views on the second item on the agenda.
    The meeting adjourned at 11:45 a.m. The next meeting is scheduled for 10:00 a.m. on October 5th.

### 中美两国大使级会谈举行第十八次会议

1955-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    中美两国大使级会谈举行第十八次会议
    新华社日内瓦二十八日电　中美两国大使级会谈二十八日上午十时在国联大厦举行第十八次会议。双方在会后一致同意的公报中说，双方就协议的声明的执行继续交换了情况，并且就第二项议程交换了意见。
    会议在上午十一时四十五分休会。下次会议订于十月五日上午十时举行。
