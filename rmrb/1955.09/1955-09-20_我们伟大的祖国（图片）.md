Our Great Motherland
The Tibet Engineering Survey Team, formed according to the decision of the State Council, is conducting topographic, geological, and hydrological surveys in the deep mountains and dense forests of the Bomi area to provide design data for the water conservancy construction of Zhamu, a new city on the Kangzang Highway. This is a team member conducting riverbed measurements in the Bomi area.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    根据国务院决定组成的西藏工程勘察队在波密地区的深山密林里进行地形、地质和水文勘察，给康藏公路上的新城——札木的水利建设提供设计资料。这是队员正在波密地区进行河床测量。
