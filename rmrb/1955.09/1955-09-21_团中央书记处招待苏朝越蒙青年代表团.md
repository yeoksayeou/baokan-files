Title: Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Hosts Youth Delegations from the Soviet Union, North Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1955-09-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 20th. The Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League held a banquet on the evening of the 20th to host the youth delegations from the Soviet Union, North Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia, who were invited to China to attend the National Congress of Young Socialist Construction Activists.

Attending the banquet were Hu Yaobang and Liao Chengzhi, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, as well as young activists Wang Chonglun, Hao Jianxiu, Tian Hua, and Guo Lanying, who are participating in the National Congress of Young Socialist Construction Activists.

### 团中央书记处招待苏朝越蒙青年代表团

1955-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    团中央书记处招待苏朝越蒙青年代表团
    新华社二十日讯　中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处在二十日晚举行宴会，招待应邀来我国参加全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会的苏联青年代表团、朝鲜青年代表团、越南青年代表团和蒙古青年代表团。
    出席宴会的有中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦、廖承志等人，参加全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会的青年积极分子王崇伦、郝建秀、田华、郭兰英等人也出席作陪。
