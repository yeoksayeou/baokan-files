Title: Soviet Trade Union Sports Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-16
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The Soviet Trade Union Sports Delegation, invited to China to participate in the first National Workers' Games, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 15th. The delegation is headed by A.V. Vasilyev, Director of the Department of Sports and Physical Culture of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions.
    Welcoming them at the airport were Dong Xin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Cai Shufan, Deputy Director of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, and Zhang Ze, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League.
    Personnel from the Soviet Embassy in China were also at the airport to welcome them.

### 苏联工会体育代表团到京

1955-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联工会体育代表团到京
    新华社十五日讯　应邀来我国参加全国第一届工人体育运动大会的苏联工会体育代表团一行六人，十五日下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团由全苏工会中央理事会体育运动部部长阿·维·华西里耶夫任团长。
    到机场欢迎的，有中华全国总工会书记处书记董昕，中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任蔡树藩，中国新民主主义青年团中央书记处书记章泽等。
    苏联驻我国使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
