Title: People from All Walks of Life in the Capital Hold a Rally to Celebrate the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
Author: 
Date: 1955-09-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 1st. A rally of people from all walks of life in the capital was held in Beijing on September 1st to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The celebration rally was organized by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China.

    The venue of the rally was decorated in a simple and solemn manner. On the green curtain in the center of the rostrum hung portraits of Chairman Mao Zedong and Chairman Ho Chi Minh and the national flags of China and Vietnam. Flowers and potted plants were arranged in front of the stage. A huge red banner was hung high in the hall with the words "Long Live the Unbreakable Friendship Between the Peoples of China and Vietnam!" written in both Chinese and Vietnamese. The hall was filled with workers, cadres of government departments, scientists, artists, and people from all walks of life in Beijing, heads of various departments of the Chinese government and people's organizations, as well as diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China.

    The rally was held at 8 p.m. Those seated on the rostrum included Vice Premiers of the State Council Deng Xiaoping and Chen Yi, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Weihan, Acting Minister of Culture Qian Junrui, Minister of Education Zhang Xiruo, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu. The Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Hoang Van Hoan, was also seated on the rostrum. When they entered the hall and ascended the rostrum, the audience welcomed them with warm applause.

    Acting Minister of Culture Qian Junrui presided over today's rally and delivered a brief opening speech. He announced the start of the rally, and the national anthems of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the People's Republic of China were played in the hall.

    Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi spoke first (see page 4). On behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, he extended heartfelt congratulations to the government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China Hoang Van Hoan then spoke at the meeting (see page 4), and was warmly welcomed by the attendees.

    At the celebration rally, the Vietnamese color film "Vietnam Advancing on the Road to Victory" was shown at the end.

### 庆祝越南民主共和国成立十周年  首都各界人民举行大会

1955-09-02
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝越南民主共和国成立十周年
    首都各界人民举行大会
    据新华社一日讯　九月一日，在北京举行了首都各界人民庆祝越南民主共和国建立十周年大会。庆祝大会由中华人民共和国文化部举办。
    大会会场布置得朴素庄严。主席台正中的绿色帷幕上，悬挂着毛泽东主席、胡志明主席的画像和中越两国国旗。台前排列着鲜花和盆景。会场中高悬的巨幅红布标语上面用中越两国文字写着：“中越两国人民牢不可破的友谊万岁！”在会场的大厅中，坐满了北京的工人、机关干部、科学家、艺术家和各界人士，中国政府各部门和各人民团体负责人，以及各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    大会在下午八时举行。在主席台上就座的有国务院副总理邓小平、陈毅、秘书长习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李维汉，文化部代理部长钱俊瑞，教育部部长张奚若，外交部副部长章汉夫。越南民主共和国驻华大使黄文欢也在主席台上就座。当他们步入会场登上主席台时，全场以热烈的掌声表示欢迎。
    文化部代理部长钱俊瑞主持今天大会并致简短的开会词。他宣布大会开始时，大厅中奏起了越南民主共和国和中华人民共和国的国歌。
    国务院副总理陈毅首先讲话（见第四版），他代表中华人民共和国政府和中国人民，向越南民主共和国政府和越南人民衷心地祝贺越南民主共和国建立十周年。
    越南民主共和国驻华大使黄文欢接着在会上讲话（见第四版），受到到会者的热烈欢迎。
    在庆祝大会上，最后放映了越南彩色影片“在胜利路上前进的越南”。
