Title: Hanoi Holds Ceremony to Award Medals to Twenty-Six People's Army Heroes; President Ho Chi Minh and Others Lay Wreaths at the Vietnamese Martyrs' Memorial

Author: 
Date: 1955-09-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 1st: A grand ceremony was held in Hanoi on August 31st to award medals to twenty-six Vietnamese People's Army commanders and soldiers who had been awarded the title of "Hero of the Vietnamese People's Army." At the meeting, General Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnamese People's Army, amidst warm applause and military music, pinned military medals on the chests of some of the heroes attending the conference. The decision to award them medals was recently made by President Ho Chi Minh and the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

President Ho Chi Minh attended the conference and delivered a speech. He encouraged the soldiers to work even better in the new tasks of consolidating peace and defending the national defense.

Among these Vietnamese People's Army commanders and fighters who have been awarded the title of hero, eight have gloriously sacrificed themselves in the War of Resistance, including Tran Qu, the famous Matrosov-style hero who used his body to block the enemy's gun muzzles, and Wu Mei, who rushed into the enemy ranks with a bomb and perished with a platoon of French troops.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 1st: President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, along with leading comrades of the Republic's government and the Vietnam Workers' Party, presented wreaths to the Vietnamese Martyrs' Memorial in Ba Dinh Park, Hanoi, on the afternoon of September 1st.

Amidst military music, President Ho Chi Minh placed a wreath inscribed with "The Motherland Records the Martyrs' Merits" in front of the martyrs' monument. Sun De Sheng, Chang Zheng, Vo Nguyen Giap, and Huang Guo Yue then presented wreaths.

Diplomatic missions of various countries stationed in Vietnam and representatives from all walks of life in Hanoi successively presented wreaths in front of the martyrs' monument.

### 河内举行大会授勋给二十六位人民军英雄  胡志明主席等向越南烈士纪念碑献花圈

1955-09-02
第1版()
专栏：

    河内举行大会授勋给二十六位人民军英雄
    胡志明主席等向越南烈士纪念碑献花圈
    新华社河内一日电　八月三十一日，在河内隆重举行了授勋给荣获越南人民军英雄的称号的二十六位越南人民军指挥员和战斗员的大会。会上，越南人民军总司令武元甲大将在热烈的掌声和军乐声中在一部分出席大会的英雄的胸前挂上了军功勋章。授勋给他们这件事，是胡志明主席和越南民主共和国政府在最近决定的。
    胡志明主席参加了大会，并且讲话。他勉励战士们在巩固和平和保卫国防的新任务中更好地工作。
    在这些荣获英雄称号的越南人民军指挥员和战斗员中，有八位已经在抗战中光荣牺牲，其中包括以自己身体堵塞法军枪眼、著名的马特洛索夫式英雄陈渠和抱着炸弹冲入敌群而与一个排的法军同归于尽的吴梅。
    新华社河内一日电　越南民主共和国胡志明主席以及共和国政府和越南劳动党的领导同志在九月一日下午向设于河内巴亭公园的越南烈士纪念碑呈献花圈。
    在军乐声中，胡志明主席把写有“祖国记载着烈士们的功勋”的花圈放在烈士碑前。接着献花圈的有孙德胜、长征、武元甲和黄国越。
    各国驻越南的外交使团和河内各界代表相继在烈士碑前呈献了花圈。
