Title: Japanese Diet Members Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1955-09-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 3rd - According to TASS, Moscow, September 2nd: The delegation of Japanese Diet members visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, led by Tokutaro Kitamura and Masaru Nozoe, arrived in Moscow on September 2nd.

Welcoming them at the airport were Komarov, Deputy Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, representatives of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and other individuals.

Komarov delivered a welcoming speech to the Japanese Diet members delegation. He said: "On behalf of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, I would like to extend a welcome to you upon your arrival in Moscow, the capital of the Soviet Union. I hope that this visit to the Soviet Union will promote mutual understanding, cooperation, and friendly relations between the peoples of the Soviet Union and Japan. Your arrival during the ongoing negotiations in London between the Soviet Union and Japan to restore diplomatic relations will undoubtedly contribute to the realization of this task."

Tokutaro Kitamura, the head of the Japanese Diet members delegation, then delivered a reply. He said that the visit of the Japanese Diet members delegation to Moscow is a very honorable moment in the history of the Japanese Diet. This visit to the Soviet Union at the invitation will help to further consolidate world peace.

In his reply, Tokutaro Kitamura also expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Soviet people for their warm reception of the Japanese Diet members delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, September 3rd - According to TASS, Moscow: Volkov, Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and Lacis, Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, received the Japanese Diet members delegation headed by Tokutaro Kitamura and Masaru Nozoe in the Kremlin on September 2nd.

During the meeting, the hosts and guests had a friendly and unconstrained conversation.

Volkov and Lacis expressed their conviction that this visit by the Japanese Diet members delegation would help establish friendly relations between the peoples of the Soviet Union and Japan. They also emphasized that the Soviet Union hopes to coexist peacefully with the peoples of all countries and with all nations (regardless of their national systems).

Tokutaro Kitamura said in the conversation that the Japanese people desire peace more than the people of any other country. The fact that the Japanese Diet members delegation accepted the invitation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union indicates a desire to strengthen friendly relations between Japan and the Soviet Union. The purpose and mission of the Japanese Diet members delegation's visit to the Soviet Union is to strengthen friendly relations between the peoples of Japan and the Soviet Union.

Masaru Nozoe said that the delegation members believe that the warm hospitality they received in the Soviet Union is an expression of the Soviet people's friendship towards the Japanese people. He also said that this visit by the delegation is the focus of attention of the Japanese people. The delegation members will do their best to live up to the great mission of strengthening the friendship between the two peoples.

After the conversation, the Japanese Diet members also visited the conference hall of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and many other buildings in the Kremlin.

### 日本国会议员访苏团到达莫斯科

1955-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    日本国会议员访苏团到达莫斯科
    新华社三日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科二日讯：应苏联最高苏维埃的邀请到苏联访问的日本国会议员访苏团，由北村德太郎和野沟胜率领在九月二日到达莫斯科。
    到机场欢迎的有苏联最高苏维埃民族院副主席科马罗夫、苏联最高苏维埃的代表和其他人士。
    科马罗夫向日本国会议员访苏团致欢迎词。他说：让我代表苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟最高苏维埃在你们到达苏联首都——莫斯科的时候，向你们表示欢迎。我希望你们这次来苏联访问，将促进苏联和日本两国人民之间的相互谅解、合作和友好的关系。当苏联和日本两国目前正在伦敦举行恢复两国外交关系的谈判期间，你们的到来，无疑的将有助于这个任务的实现。
    日本国会议员访苏团团长北村德太郎接着致答词。他说，日本国会议员访苏团来到莫斯科是日本国会历史上很荣幸的时刻。这次应邀访问苏联将有助于进一步巩固全世界的和平。
    北村德太郎在答词中并且衷心感谢苏联人民对日本国会议员访苏团的热情接待。
    新华社三日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席沃尔科夫和苏联最高苏维埃民族院主席拉齐斯，九月二日在克里姆林宫接见了以北村德太郎和野沟胜为首的日本国会议员访苏团。
    在接见中，宾主双方进行了友好和无拘束的谈话。
    沃尔科夫和拉齐斯在谈话中表示深信，日本国会议员访苏团的这次访问将有助于苏日两国人民建立友好的关系。他们并且着重指出，苏联希望同各国人民、同所有国家（不问它们的国家制度如何）和平共处。
    北村德太郎在谈话中说，日本人民比所有其他国家的人民更希望和平。这次日本国会议员访苏团接受苏联最高苏维埃的邀请一事，表示了要加强日苏两国友好关系的愿望。日本国会议员访苏团访问苏联的目的和任务就是要加强日苏两国人民之间的友好关系。
    野沟胜说，访苏团人员认为，他们在苏联受到的热情招待是苏联人民对日本人民具有友情的表现。他并且说，访苏团的这次访问是日本人民的注意中心。访苏团人员将尽力不辜负加强两国人民的友谊的伟大使命。
    谈话结束后，日本国会议员还参观了苏联最高苏维埃会议厅和克里姆林宫的其他许多建筑。
