Title: My Yue Opera Troupe Leaves Sverdlovsk
Author:
Date: 1955-09-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency:

    My Yue Opera Troupe Leaves Sverdlovsk
    The Chinese Yue Opera Troupe, which was invited to visit various parts of the Soviet Union, left Sverdlovsk on September 5th to continue its visit and performances in Novosibirsk.
    During its five-day visit to Sverdlovsk, the Chinese Yue Opera Troupe performed four operas: "The West Chamber," "Liang Shanbo and Zhu Yingtai," "Picking Up the Jade Bracelet," and "Beating the Golden Bough," all of which were warmly welcomed by the local audience. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 我越剧团离斯维德洛夫斯克

1955-09-09
第3版()
专栏：

    我越剧团离斯维德洛夫斯克
    应邀到苏联各地访问的中国越剧团，已经在九月五日离开斯维德洛夫斯克，去新西伯利亚继续作访问演出。
    中国越剧团在斯维德洛夫斯克访问的五天里，共演出了“西厢记”、“梁山伯与祝英台”、“拾玉镯”和“打金枝”四个剧目，得到了当地观众的热烈欢迎。（新华社）
