Title: Mayor Peng Zhen Holds Banquet to Bid Farewell to Vietnamese People's Song and Dance Troupe
Author:
Date: 1955-09-24
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd. Mayor of Beijing, Peng Zhen, held a banquet on the evening of the 23rd to bid farewell to the Vietnamese People's Song and Dance Troupe, headed by Liu Zhonglu.

    At the banquet, Peng Zhen said in his speech, "In the past few days, the people of Beijing have seen the wonderful performances of the Vietnamese People's Song and Dance Troupe. On behalf of the people of Beijing, I would like to express my heartfelt gratitude. The Vietnamese People's Song and Dance Troupe is about to leave Beijing to perform in the south of our country. I wish you greater achievements."

    The head of the Vietnamese People's Song and Dance Troupe, Liu Zhonglu, delivered a reply. He expressed his gratitude for the warm reception from the people of Beijing. During the banquet, the guests frequently raised their glasses to toast the health of the leaders of the Chinese and Vietnamese people and the friendship between the people of China and Vietnam.

    Tran Khanh Van, a young artist from the Vietnamese People's Song and Dance Troupe, sang the newly learned Chinese folk song "Riding a Horse Across the Grassland" at the banquet. Bui Chau Loan sang a newly composed song at the banquet, the content of which was: Tonight we are about to leave Beijing, we will always remember this feeling of camaraderie... Several artists also sang the Chinese song "Long Live Mao Zedong". Chinese literary and art workers also sang newly learned Vietnamese songs and Peking Opera at the meeting.

    The banquet was attended by: Xia Yan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, Liu Ren , Second Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the Beijing Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Zhang Youyu, Wu Han, Wang Kunlun, Xue Zizheng, Cheng Hongyi, Jia Tingsan and Le Songsheng, Vice Mayors of Beijing, heads of various departments and people's organizations in Beijing, and people from the capital's cultural, artistic, journalistic and other circles. Zhou Liang, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, was also invited to attend the banquet.

### 彭真市长举行宴会欢送越南人民歌舞团

1955-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    彭真市长举行宴会欢送越南人民歌舞团
    新华社二十三日讯　北京市市长彭真在二十三日晚举行宴会，欢送以刘仲卢为首的越南人民歌舞团。
    在宴会上，彭真致词说，最近几天，我们北京市人民看到了越南人民歌舞团精采的表演，我代表北京市人民表示衷心的感谢。越南人民歌舞团就要离开北京到我国南方去演出，我祝贺你们获得更大的成就。
    越南人民歌舞团团长刘仲卢致答词。他对北京市人民的热情接待表示感谢。席间，宾主们频频举杯，为中越两国人民领袖的健康和中越两国人民的友谊干杯。
    越南人民歌舞团年轻的艺术工作者陈庆云在宴会上唱了新学会的中国民歌“骑着马儿过草原”，裴珠鸾唱了在宴会上新编的一支歌曲，内容是：今天晚上我们就要离开北京，我们永远想着这种同志的感情……。几位艺术工作者还合唱了中国的歌曲“万岁毛泽东”。中国的文艺工作者也在会上演唱了新学会的越南歌曲和京剧。
    出席今天宴会的有：中华人民共和国文化部副部长夏衍，外交部部长助理陈家康，中共北京市委员会第二书记、中国人民政治协商会议北京市委员会主席刘仁，北京市副市长张友渔、吴晗、王昆仑、薛子正、程宏毅、贾庭三、乐松生，北京市各部门、各人民团体负责人，首都文化、艺术、新闻和其他各界人士。越南民主共和国驻华大使馆临时代办周亮也应邀出席了宴会。
