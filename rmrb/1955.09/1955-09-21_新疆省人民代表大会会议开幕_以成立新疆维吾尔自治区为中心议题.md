Title: Xinjiang Provincial People's Congress Meeting Opens, Centered on the Establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1955-09-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, September 20th. The second meeting of the first Xinjiang Provincial People's Congress, with the establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region as its central theme, solemnly opened in Urumqi on the 20th.

The conference was attended by 291 representatives from thirteen ethnic groups from various parts of Xinjiang and various autonomous prefectures and autonomous counties, and 89 observers.

Dong Biwu, representative of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Central People's Government, Liu Chun, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China, Wang Zaitian (Mongolian), Vice Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Qin Yingji (Zhuang), Deputy Governor of Guangxi Province and Chairman of the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region, Lan Changfa (Yao), Vice Chairman of the First Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region, Zhu Dehai (Korean), Deputy Governor of Jilin Province and Chairman of the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Region, Dao Jingban (Dai), Member of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee and Chairman of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Region of Yunnan Province, Zhang Chong (Yi), representative of the Yi people from the Greater and Lesser Liang Mountains of Sichuan Province, Ou Baichuan (Miao), Deputy Governor of Guizhou Province, Ma Hongbin (Hui), Deputy Governor of Gansu Province, Ce Ren Nam (Tibetan), Deputy Director of the Democratic Women's Federation of Gansu Province, Xi Rao Jia Cuo (Tibetan), Deputy Governor of Qinghai Province, Ahou Maiti (Kazak), Chairman of the Alton Chuk Kazakh Autonomous Region of Qinghai Province, and Han Zhao'e (Han), Deputy Governor of Shaanxi Province, all attended the conference.

Saifudin delivered the opening speech, saying that the important task of this meeting is to establish the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region, review the work of Xinjiang over the past six years, and discuss future work tasks. At the same time, the meeting will discuss and approve the first five-year plan for the development of the national economy in the Xinjiang region, the 1954 financial accounts, and the 1955 financial budget.

Then, to the sound of music, dozens of Young Pioneers from various ethnic groups and literary and art workers from the Xinjiang Military Region presented flowers to the presidium on the stage.

Dong Biwu spoke at the meeting. He extended heartfelt congratulations on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Central People's Government to the conference and the soon-to-be-established Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region, and expressed the hope that it would gloriously shoulder the great historical task of uniting the people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang to strive for socialist construction and socialist transformation.

After Dong Biwu's speech, Wang Zaitian, on behalf of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Qin Yingji, on behalf of the Guangxi Provincial People's Committee and the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region, Zhu Dehai, on behalf of the Jilin Provincial People's Committee and the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Region, and Xi Rao Jia Cuo, on behalf of the Qinghai Provincial People's Committee, successively spoke at the meeting to express their congratulations. They all congratulated the establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region with the great achievements they have achieved in their respective regions after the implementation of regional autonomy. They also expressed the hope that after the establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region, it will move forward forever under the guidance of the ethnic policy of the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao Zedong.

To the sound of military music, congratulatory banners and gifts from all over the motherland were presented to the presidium one by one. Among the gifts were Inner Mongolia's most precious gift - silver bowls, Guangxi's tropical products lychees, pineapples, grapefruit, as well as ethnic costumes of the Zhuang, Yao, and Jingpo ethnic groups. In addition, the Production and Construction Corps of the Xinjiang Military Region, agriculture, water conservancy, and various factories and mining enterprises also presented many gifts to the conference.

Finally, Bao Erhan, Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government, delivered a report to the conference on the work of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government over the past six years.

### 新疆省人民代表大会会议开幕  以成立新疆维吾尔自治区为中心议题

1955-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    新疆省人民代表大会会议开幕
    以成立新疆维吾尔自治区为中心议题
    新华社乌鲁木齐二十日电　以成立新疆维吾尔自治区为中心议题的新疆省第一届人民代表大会第二次会议，二十日在乌鲁木齐隆重开幕。
    参加大会的有新疆各地和各民族自治州、自治县的十三个民族代表二百九十一人，列席代表八十九人。
    中国共产党中央委员会和中央人民政府代表董必武、中华人民共和国民族事务委员会副主任刘春、内蒙古自治区副主席王再天（蒙古族）、广西省副省长、桂西僮族自治区主席覃应机（僮族）、中国人民政协桂西僮族自治区第一届委员会副主席蓝昌法（瑶族）、吉林省副省长、延边朝鲜族自治区主席朱德海（朝鲜族）、云南省人民委员会委员、云南省德宏傣族景颇族自治区主席刀京版（傣族）、四川省大小凉山彝族代表张冲（彝族）、贵州省副省长欧百川（苗族）、甘肃省副省长马鸿宾（回族）、甘肃省民主妇女联合会副主任策仁娜姆（藏族）、青海省副省长喜饶嘉错（藏族）、青海省阿尔顿曲克哈萨克族自治区主席阿后买提（哈萨克族）、陕西省副省长韩兆鹗（汉族）等，都出席了大会。
    赛福鼎致开幕词，他说这次会议的重要任务，就是成立新疆维吾尔自治区，并检查新疆过去六年来的工作和讨论今后的工作任务。同时讨论和批准新疆地区关于发展国民经济的第一个五年计划、一九五四年的财政决算和一九五五年的财政预算。
    接着，在乐曲声中，几十名各民族少年先锋队员和新疆军区的文艺工作者，到主席台上向主席团献花。
    董必武在大会上讲了话。他代表中国共产党中央委员会和中央人民政府向大会和即将成立的新疆维吾尔自治区致以衷心的祝贺，并希望它光荣地担负起团结新疆各民族人民为社会主义建设和社会主义改造事业而奋斗的伟大历史任务。
    董必武讲话后，王再天代表内蒙古自治区，覃应机代表广西省人民委员会和桂西僮族自治区，朱德海代表吉林省人民委员会和延边朝鲜族自治区，喜饶嘉错代表青海省人民委员会相继在大会上讲话祝贺。他们都以自己所在地方实行区域自治后所获得的各项巨大成就，祝贺新疆维吾尔自治区的成立。并希望新疆维吾尔自治区成立后，在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的民族政策指导下，永远前进。
    在军乐声中，祖国各地的贺幛、礼品一个个地送上了主席台。礼品中有内蒙古最贵重的礼物——银碗、广西热带产物荔枝、菠萝、柚子，以及僮族、瑶族、景颇族的民族服装等。此外，新疆军区生产建设部队、农业、水利和各厂矿企业等单位也给大会献了许多礼品。
    最后，新疆省人民政府主席鲍尔汉向大会作了关于新疆省人民政府六年来的工作报告。
