Title: Ambassador Luo Guibo Holds Reception for Chinese Art Troupe's Visit to Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1955-09-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 6th. On the evening of September 6th, Luo Guibo, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, held a reception for the Chinese Art Troupe's visit to Vietnam.

    Ding Xilin, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, Wu Baihua, deputy head, and all members of the art troupe attended the reception.

    The Vietnamese side was represented by Truong Chinh, General Secretary of the Vietnam Workers' Party; Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Vo Nguyen Giap, Minister of National Defense and Commander-in-Chief of the People's Army; Ton Duc Thang, Acting Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of Vietnam; ministers and deputy ministers of various ministries of the Vietnamese government; senior generals of the Vietnamese People's Army; and Tran Duy Hung, Chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee.

    Also present were Hoang Quoc Viet, Chairman of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor; Le Dinh Tham, Chairman of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace; Ton Quang Phiet, Secretary-General of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association; representatives and leaders of various democratic parties and mass organizations in Vietnam; figures from the literary and art circles and the press; and representatives of overseas Chinese.

    Also attending the reception were diplomatic envoys and personnel from various countries stationed in Hanoi.

    Chairman Desai of the International Commission of Vietnam, Mr. Bell, Acting Head of the Polish Delegation, and representatives of the French Economic and Cultural Negotiation Delegation also attended the reception.

    A total of more than 400 people attended the reception.

    The reception proceeded in a friendly and pleasant atmosphere throughout.

    Members of the Chinese Art Troupe also sang songs and performed dance programs at the reception.

### 罗贵波大使为我国艺术团访越举行招待会

1955-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    罗贵波大使为我国艺术团访越举行招待会
    新华社河内六日电　九月六日晚七时，中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国大使罗贵波为中国艺术团访问越南举行了招待会。
    中国艺术团团长丁西林、副团长吴白桦和艺术团全体人员都出席了招待会。
    越南方面出席的有越南劳动党总书记长征、越南民主共和国政府副总理兼外交部长范文同、国防部长兼人民军总司令武元甲、越南国民大会常务委员会代理主席孙德胜、越南政府各部部长和副部长、越南人民军高级将领、河内市行政委员会主席陈维兴。
    出席的还有越南总工会主席黄国越、越南保卫世界和平委员会主席黎廷探、越中友好协会总书记孙光阀，越南各民主党派、各群众团体的代表和负责人，文艺界和新闻界人士，以及华侨代表。
    出席招待会的还有驻河内的各国使节和外交人员。
    越南国际委员会主席德赛、波兰代表团代理团长贝尔，法国经济与文化谈判代表团的代表等也出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的一共有四百多人。
    招待会始终在友好愉快的空气中进行。
    中国艺术团人员并且在招待会上演唱了歌曲和舞蹈节目。
