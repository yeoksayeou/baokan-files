    Our Great Motherland
    The Arxan Forest Area Forest Railway is a newly built forest railway in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. This railway starts from Yiershi, crosses the Hala River, and extends to the forest treasure trove of deciduous pine trees – the Daheigou. This train is transporting large quantities of timber from the forest.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    阿尔山林区森林铁路是内蒙古自治区新建的一条森林铁路。这条铁路由伊尔施起，跨过哈拉哈河，一直伸展到长满落叶松的森林宝库——大黑沟。这辆列车正把大批的木材从森林里运出来。
