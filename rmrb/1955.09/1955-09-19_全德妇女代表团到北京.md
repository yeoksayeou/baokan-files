Title: All-German Women's Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 18th: The All-German Women's Delegation, invited by the All-China Democratic Women's Federation to visit China and attend the National Day celebrations, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 18th. The delegation was sent by the German Democratic Women's League.

The delegation is composed of eight women representatives from the German Democratic Republic and six women representatives from the Federal Republic of Germany. Members include women, agricultural, and educational workers. The head of the delegation, Käte Kern, is a member of the Central Committee of the German Democratic Women's League, a member of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, and the Director of the Department of Maternal and Child Health of the Ministry of Health of the German Democratic Republic.

Cao Mengjun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Yang Yunyu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Women's International Democratic Federation (China), and Wang Ziru, Vice-Chairman of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, were at the airport to welcome them.

Mr. Gruttner, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, was also at the airport to welcome them.

After the delegation members got off the plane, they accepted flowers from the welcomers and warmly shook hands and embraced each other.

### 全德妇女代表团到北京

1955-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    全德妇女代表团到北京
    新华社十八日讯　应中华全国民主妇女联合会邀请来我国访问并参加我国国庆节观礼的全德妇女代表团十八日下午乘机到达北京。代表团是由德国民主妇女联合会派遣的。
    代表团由德意志民主共和国的妇女代表八人和德意志联邦共和国的妇女代表六人组成。成员包括妇女、农业和教育工作者等。团长凯特·凯恩是德国民主妇女联合会中央委员、德意志民主共和国人民议院议员、德意志民主共和国卫生部妇幼保健司司长。
    到机场迎接的有中华全国民主妇女联合会书记处书记曹孟君，国际民主妇联书记处书记（中国）杨蕴玉和北京市民主妇女联合会副主席王子如等。
    德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆临时代办葛鲁特纳也到机场欢迎。
    代表团人员下飞机以后，接受了欢迎人们的献花，并相互热烈握手和拥抱。
