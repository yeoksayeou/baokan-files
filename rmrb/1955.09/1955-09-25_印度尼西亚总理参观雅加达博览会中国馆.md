Title: Indonesian Prime Minister Visits the Chinese Pavilion at the Jakarta Exposition
Author: 
Date: 1955-09-25
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 23rd: Indonesian Prime Minister Burhanuddin Harahap and his wife visited the Jakarta International Exposition on the 23rd. When Harahap visited the Chinese Pavilion, Huang Zhen, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Indonesia, and Ma Yimin, the head of the Chinese Exhibition Delegation, were present to welcome him.

### 印度尼西亚总理参观雅加达博览会中国馆

1955-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚总理参观雅加达博览会中国馆
    新华社雅加达二十三日电　印度尼西亚总理伯汉努丁·哈拉哈普和夫人二十三日参观了雅加达国际博览会。哈拉哈普参观中国馆时，中华人民共和国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇和中国展览团团长马一民在场欢迎。
