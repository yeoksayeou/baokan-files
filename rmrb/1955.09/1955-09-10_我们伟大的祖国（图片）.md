Date: 1955-09-10
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In order to improve silkworm rearing techniques and increase the yield and quality of silkworm cocoons to meet the needs of the textile industry, four agricultural cooperatives in Tai'an Township, Wuxi County, selected cooperative members with the best silkworm rearing techniques to form a joint silkworm rearing station to raise new varieties of autumn silkworms. The picture shows technicians from the Zhenjiang Sericulture Research Institute observing the growth of autumn silkworms with silkworm farmers at the silkworm rearing station.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    无锡县泰安乡的四个农业合作社，为了改进养蚕技术，提高蚕茧的产量和质量，供应纺织工业的需要，挑选养蚕技术最好的社员组成联合养蚕站，饲养秋蚕新品种。这是镇江蚕业研究所技术人员正同养蚕站蚕农观察秋蚕的生长情况。
