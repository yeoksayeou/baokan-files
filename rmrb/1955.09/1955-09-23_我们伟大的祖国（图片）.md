    Our Great Motherland
    Institutions of higher learning are actively cultivating talents for national construction. In the newly built seven-story building of the Beijing Institute of Iron and Steel Technology, modern experimental equipment has been mostly installed, and the future builders of the motherland have begun various experiments in the laboratory.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    高等学校正在积极为国家培养建设人材。在北京钢铁工业学院新建的七层大楼里，现代化的实验设备已经大部分安装完毕，祖国未来的建设者们已开始在实验室中进行各种实验。
