Title: National Congress of Youth Activists Concludes; Chairman Mao Zedong Attends with Central Committee and State Leaders
Author:
Date: 1955-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 28th Dispatch: The National Congress of Youth Activists for Socialist Construction victoriously concluded on the afternoon of the 28th.

The great leader of the Chinese people and their young generation, Chairman Mao Zedong, met with over 1,500 youth activists before the closing of the congress today. Members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, including Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Kang Sheng, Peng Zhen, Lin Boqu, Zhang Wentian, Deng Xiaoping, Vice Chairpersons of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, including Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, Vice Premiers of the State Council Deng Zihui, He Long, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, and Vice Chairperson of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference He Xiangning also attended today's congress.

Youth delegations from the Soviet Union, North Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia also attended today's congress.

At 3:00 PM, when Chairman Mao Zedong and the leaders of the Party Central Committee and the State ascended to the rostrum, the entire audience rose and burst into thunderous applause, which then turned into prolonged cheers. Chairman Mao cordially waved to the youth activists, and the audience shouted "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live the Communist Party of China!" At this unforgettable moment, the faces of the young people were filled with happiness, and many jumped up in excitement.

After Chairman Mao Zedong and the leaders of the Party Central Committee and the State took their seats on the rostrum, Hu Yaobang, Secretary of the Central Secretariat of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, invited Deng Xiaoping, representative of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, to give instructions to the congress.

Deng Xiaoping began his speech amid warm applause. On behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, he extended warm congratulations to the National Congress of Youth Activists for Socialist Construction and to the rising generation of youth. He expressed his belief that Chinese youth will not only be able to witness the completion of socialist society but will also further advance socialist society to communist society. He said that our Party attaches great importance to the role of youth in national life. We have no doubt that youth is our hope and our future. Deng Xiaoping concluded his speech by shouting, "Long live the youth who strive for socialism!" Almost every sentence of his speech was interrupted by applause from the entire audience. At the end of his speech, all the youth activists once again stood up and shouted, "Long live our beloved motherland!" "Long live the Communist Party of China that educates us to grow!" "Long live our mentor, Chairman Mao!"

Today, the youth activists present received the kind teachings, boundless care, and trust of the Party Central Committee with the utmost loyalty and respect. The instructions of the Party Central Committee were conveyed to the millions of young people in every corner of the country through the live recording of the Central People's Broadcasting Station.

The congress then adopted a letter of guarantee to the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao. After the letter of guarantee was read out at the meeting by representatives of the youth activists, more than 1,500 young men and women activists from various fronts and ethnic groups stood up and made a solemn oath before the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao to valiantly march towards socialism.

The congress then unanimously adopted the "Letter to the National Youth."

At 3:40 PM, Hu Yaobang announced the victorious conclusion of the congress. All the youth activists present stood up and sang the song "Ode to the Motherland."

After the closing of the congress, all the youth activists present took a group photo with their most beloved leader and mentor, Chairman Mao Zedong, and the leaders of the Party Central Committee and the State as a memento.

Xinhua News Agency, 28th Dispatch: The National Congress of Youth Activists for Socialist Construction continued to be held on the morning of the 28th.

In accordance with the "Decision on Convening the National Congress of Youth Activists for Socialist Construction" adopted by the Second Plenary Session of the Second Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, in order to commend the contributions of youth activists from various fronts to the motherland and to encourage youth activists and youth across the country to play a greater role in socialist construction, presented each youth activist attending the congress with a "Youth Activist for Socialist Construction Medal" and presented 163 advanced collective units with banners. At the meeting, the Central Committee of the Youth League also presented the "Youth Activist for Socialist Construction Medal" to all members of the youth delegations from the Soviet Union, North Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia who were invited to attend the congress. ### 全国青年积极分子大会闭幕  毛泽东主席偕同党中央和国家领导人到会

1955-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    全国青年积极分子大会闭幕
    毛泽东主席偕同党中央和国家领导人到会
    新华社二十八日讯　全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会在二十八日下午胜利闭幕。
    中国人民及其青年一代的伟大领袖毛泽东主席，今天在大会闭幕前和全体到会的一千五百多名青年积极分子见面。中国共产党中央政治局委员刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、陈云、康生、彭真、林伯渠、张闻天、邓小平，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄、李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通，国务院副总理邓子恢、贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫、李富春、李先念和中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席何香凝，也一起出席了今天的大会。
    出席今天大会的还有苏联、朝鲜、越南、蒙古等四国的青年代表团。
    下午三时，当毛泽东主席以及党中央和国家的领导人登上主席台时，全场起立，发出暴风雨般的掌声，继而转为经久不息的欢呼声。毛主席亲切地向青年积极分子们招手，全场高呼“毛主席万岁！”“中国共产党万岁！”在这个永远难忘的时刻，年轻的人们的脸上都显露出幸福的光彩，有许多人兴奋得跳跃起来。
    在毛泽东主席偕同党中央和国家的领导人在主席台上就座后，中国新民主主义青年团中央书记处书记胡耀邦请中国共产党中央委员会的代表、中共中央政治局委员邓小平向大会作指示。
    邓小平在热烈的掌声中开始讲话。他代表中国共产党中央委员会向全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会，向正在成长起来的青年一代，致热烈的祝贺。他表示相信：中国青年不但一定能够亲眼看到社会主义社会的建成，而且还要把社会主义社会进一步推向到共产主义社会。他说：我们的党十分重视青年在国家生活中的作用。我们毫不怀疑青年是我们的希望和我们的将来。邓小平在讲话的结束语中高呼：“为社会主义而奋斗的青年万岁！”他的讲话几乎每一句都为全场的掌声所打断。在他讲话结束时，全体青年积极分子再次起立，高呼：“我们亲爱的祖国万岁！”“教育我们成长的中国共产党万岁！”“我们的导师毛主席万岁！”
    今天，到会的青年积极分子怀着最大的忠诚和崇敬的心情领受党中央的亲切教导和无限的关怀和信任。党中央的指示由中央人民广播电台的实况广播录音传达给全国各个角落的千千万万的青年。
    大会接着通过了致党中央和毛主席的保证书。这个保证书由青年积极分子代表在会上宣读以后，一千五百多名来自各个战线的各民族男女青年积极分子们全体起立，在党中央和毛主席的面前，立下了向社会主义英勇进军的庄严的誓言。
    大会然后一致通过了“致全国男女青年书”。
    三时四十分，胡耀邦宣布大会胜利闭幕。到会的全体青年积极分子起立高唱起“歌唱祖国”的歌声。
    大会闭幕后，全体到会的青年积极分子同自己最敬爱的领袖和导师毛泽东主席以及党中央和国家的领导人一起摄影留念。
    据新华社二十八日讯　全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会二十八日上午继续举行。
    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会根据青年团第二届中央委员会第二次全体会议通过的“关于召开全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会的决定”，为了表彰各个战线上的青年积极分子对于祖国的贡献，鼓舞青年积极分子和全国青年在社会主义建设中发挥更大的作用，特赠予参加大会的每个青年积极分子以“青年社会主义建设积极分子章”，并赠予一百六十三个先进集体单位以锦旗。会上，青年团中央委员会还把“青年社会主义建设积极分子章”赠给应邀参加大会的苏联、朝鲜、越南、蒙古四个国家的青年代表团的全体团员。（附图片）
    毛泽东主席同党中央和国家的领导人同青年积极分子在一起摄影留念。　新华社记者　钱嗣杰摄
