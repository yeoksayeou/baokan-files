Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Burmese Cultural Delegation Head U Win and Others; Qian Junrui Hosts Banquet in Honor of Burmese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-09-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received U Win, head of the Burmese Federal Cultural Delegation, U Than Shin, deputy head of the cultural delegation, U San Maung, secretary of the delegation, U Ba Than, artistic director, U Kyaw Tun, cultural secretary, U Shin Nyo, stage manager, U Han Pa, band leader, U Thaung Tin and U Bo Wai, assistant artistic directors, at 3:00 PM on September 25th.

Present at the reception were Chen Yi, Xi Zhongxun, Zhang Hanfu, Qian Junrui, Zheng Zhenduo, Xia Yan, Chen Jiakang, and Chen Zhongjing.

U Hla Maung, Burmese Ambassador to China, was also present at the reception.

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th. Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, held a banquet on the evening of September 25th to welcome the Burmese Federal Cultural Delegation headed by U Win. Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, and embassy staff were also invited to attend the banquet.

Qian Junrui delivered a welcoming speech at the banquet. He pointed out that the friendship between the Chinese and Burmese people is not only important for the construction and happiness of China and Burma, but also an extremely important factor for peace in Asia and the world. He said that the beautiful and splendid Burmese culture and art has a long historical tradition. This visit and performance by the Burmese Federal Cultural Delegation to China is sure to be warmly loved by the broad masses of our people and will be a complete success, thereby further promoting the friendship and cultural exchange between the Chinese and Burmese people.

U Win then spoke. He expressed his great pleasure in being able to visit and perform in China. He said that our Chinese and Burmese people are not only connected by flesh and blood, but also have similarities in culture and art. This shows that the close friendship between our two countries is a natural friendship. Therefore, the Chinese and Burmese people have expressed their common desire for mutual respect and peaceful coexistence. He emphasized the important role of cultural exchange and mutual visits in enhancing friendly relations between the two countries.

Among those present at the banquet tonight were Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Culture and President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations Chen Zhongjing, Deputy Head of the former Chinese Cultural Delegation to Burma Zhou Erfu, and famous figures from the capital's literary and art circles. ### 周总理接见缅甸文化代表团团长吴温等  钱俊瑞设宴招待缅甸文化代表团

1955-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见缅甸文化代表团团长吴温等
    钱俊瑞设宴招待缅甸文化代表团
    新华社二十五日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在二十五日下午三时接见了缅甸联邦文化代表团团长吴温，文化代表团副团长吴丹盛，代表团秘书吴山貌，艺术指导吴巴丹，文化秘书吴觉吞，舞台管理吴盛纽，乐队队长吴汉帕，副艺术指导吴东阵、吴坡韦。
    接见时在座的，有陈毅、习仲勋、章汉夫、钱俊瑞、郑振铎、夏衍、陈家康、陈忠经。
    接见时，缅甸联邦驻华大使吴拉茂也在座。
    新华社二十五日讯　中华人民共和国文化部代理部长钱俊瑞在二十五日晚举行宴会，欢迎以吴温为首的缅甸联邦文化代表团。缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂和使馆人员也应邀出席了宴会。
    钱俊瑞在宴会致欢迎词，他指出，中缅两国人民的友谊不仅对于中缅两国的建设和人民的幸福是重要的，而且对于亚洲和世界和平也是一个极为重要的因素。他说，美丽、光彩的缅甸文化艺术，有着悠久的历史传统，这次缅甸联邦文化代表团应邀来中国访问演出，必将得到我国广大人民的热烈喜爱，并获得圆满的成功，从而更进一步促进中缅两国人民的友谊和文化交流的发展。
    吴温接着讲话。他对能够到中国来访问演出表示十分高兴。他说，我们中缅两国人民不仅是血肉相连，而且在文化艺术上也有相同之点，这就说明了我们两国的亲密友谊是一种自然的友谊，因此，中缅两国人民也就表现了互相尊重、和平相处的共同心情。他强调指出文化交流和相互间的访问对增进两国友好关系的重要作用。
    出席今晚宴会的，有国务院副总理陈毅、秘书长习仲勋，外交部副部长章汉夫，文化部副部长、中缅友好协会会长郑振铎、文化部副部长夏衍，对外文化联络局代局长陈忠经，前中国访问缅甸文化代表团副团长周而复和首都文艺界著名人士等。（附图片）
