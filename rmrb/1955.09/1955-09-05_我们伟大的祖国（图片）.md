    Our Great Motherland
    The construction of the Lhasa-Shigatse Highway is progressing rapidly. Since construction began in May, the highway has been open to traffic for more than 50 kilometers west of Yangbajing. Workers who once built the Kangzang Highway are participating in the Lhasa-Shigatse Highway construction project with their years of road construction experience.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    拉（萨）日（喀则）公路的修筑工程进展很快。从五月开工到现在，公路已从羊八井向西通车五十多公里。曾经修筑了康藏公路的工人，带着自己多年的筑路经验参加拉日公路的筑路工程。
