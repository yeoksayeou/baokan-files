Title: Chinese and Indonesian Artists Hold Song and Dance Soiree
Author:
Date: 1955-09-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 15th: The members of the Chinese Cultural Delegation who were invited to visit Indonesia have returned to Beijing in batches after a visit and performance tour in Indonesia lasting over a month. After performing in Jakarta, the capital of Indonesia, the delegation paid friendly visits and performed in cities such as Yogyakarta, Bali, Medan, and Surabaya, giving a total of thirty-one performances to an audience of over 400,000 people.

President Sukarno watched the performance and received the head of the delegation, Zheng Zhenduo, the deputy head, Zhou Erfu, and the principal performers.

During their visit, the delegation learned many classical and folk dances and musical programs of various ethnic groups in Indonesia from Indonesian artists.

Xinhua News Agency, September 16th: On the evening of the 16th, Chinese and Indonesian artists jointly held a song and dance soiree in Beijing.

The Chinese Cultural Delegation that recently returned from a visit to Indonesia and the Indonesian Youth Delegation that came to China after participating in the Fifth World Youth Festival performed fifteen wonderful song and dance programs at this soiree.

The soiree began with the chorus "China-Indonesia Friendship Song" by the Chinese Cultural Delegation and ended with three joint grand choruses by the Chinese Cultural Delegation and the Indonesian Youth Delegation. Chinese and Indonesian artists sang together the famous Indonesian song "Indonesia Raya (Independence Song)", "Boat Song", and the Chinese folk song "The East is Red."

At the soiree, Chinese artists performed four Indonesian dances and eight Indonesian songs from the forty-four song and dance programs they learned during their visit to Indonesia. Indonesian young artist and violin soloist Idris Sardi also played the Chinese folk song "In That Place Far Away" with piano accompaniment by Chinese artists.

Young Indonesian dancers performed three Indonesian folk and classical dances in succession, winning warm applause from the audience.

At the end of the soiree, a group of literary and art workers from the capital went on stage to present flowers to Indonesian and Chinese artists. At this time, the whole audience stood up and thunderous applause rang out.

Tonight's soiree was hosted by the China-Indonesia Friendship Association. Before the soiree began, Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, spoke at the meeting, expressing welcome and gratitude to the Indonesian Youth Delegation for visiting China and performing wonderful programs tonight; and expressed regards to the Chinese Cultural Delegation for returning from a visit to Indonesia and performing newly learned programs tonight.

Present at tonight's soiree were He Long and Chen Yi, Vice Premiers of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General, Zhang Dingcheng, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, Liu Zhiming and Xia Yan, Deputy Ministers of the Ministry of Culture, Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and other heads of departments, Zheng Zhenduo, head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation who recently returned from a visit to Indonesia, as well as heads of various people's organizations, Chinese literary and art workers, and people from all walks of life. Indonesian Ambassador to China Mononutu and embassy staff also attended tonight's soiree.

### 我国和印度尼西亚艺术工作者举行歌舞晚会

1955-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我国和印度尼西亚艺术工作者举行歌舞晚会
    新华社十五日讯  应邀赴印度尼西亚访问的中国文化代表团全体人员，在印度尼西亚作了为时一个多月的访问演出后，已分批回到北京。这个代表团在印度尼西亚首都雅加达演出后，曾到日惹、峇里、棉兰、泗水等城市进行友好访问和演出，总共演出三十一场，观众达四十多万人。
    苏加诺总统曾观看表演并接见了团长郑振铎、副团长周而复及主要演员。
    代表团在访问期间曾向印度尼西亚的艺术家们学习了很多印度尼西亚各民族古典的和民间的舞蹈、音乐节目。
    新华社十六日讯十六日晚，中国和印度尼西亚的艺术工作者们在北京联合举行了一个歌舞晚会。
    最近访问印度尼西亚归国的中国文化代表团和参加第五届世界青年联欢节后前来中国访问的印度尼西亚的青年代表团，在这个晚会上演出了十五个精彩的歌舞节目。
    晚会以中国文化代表团的合唱“中国印度尼西亚友好歌”开始，而以中国文化代表团和印度尼西亚青年代表团的三个联合大合唱告结束。中国和印度尼西亚的艺术家们一起演唱了印度尼西亚的著名歌曲“印度尼西亚独立歌”、“船歌”和中国民歌“东方红”。
    会上，中国艺术家们表演了在印度尼西亚访问时学会的四十四个歌舞节目中的四个印度尼西亚舞蹈和八个印度尼西亚歌曲。印度尼西亚青年艺术家、小提琴独奏表演者伊德里斯·萨迪也在中国艺术家的钢琴伴奏下，演奏了中国民歌“在那遥远的地方”。
    印度尼西亚年轻的舞蹈家们先后表演了印度尼西亚三个民间和古典的舞蹈，博得观众的热烈欢迎。
    在晚会结束时，一群首都文艺工作者走上台去，向印度尼西亚和中国的艺术家们献花。这时，全场起立，响起了暴风雨般的掌声。
    今天晚会由中国印度尼西亚友好协会主持。晚会开始前，中国印度尼西亚友好协会副会长胡愈之曾在会上讲话，对印度尼西亚青年代表团来中国访问，并在今晚演出精彩节目，表示欢迎和感谢；对中国文化代表团访问印度尼西亚归来，并在今晚演出了新学来的节目，表示慰问。
    出席今天晚会的，有中华人民共和国国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅，秘书长习仲勋，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，文化部副部长刘芝明、夏衍，外交部副部长章汉夫和其他各部门负责人，最近访问印度尼西亚归来的中国文化代表团团长郑振铎以及各人民团体负责人，中国文艺工作者和其他各界人士。印度尼西亚驻华大使莫诺努图和使馆人员也出席了今天晚会。
