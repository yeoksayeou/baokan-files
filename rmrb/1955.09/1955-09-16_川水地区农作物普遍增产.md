Title: Agricultural Crops in the Chuanshui Area Generally Increased in Production
Author:
Date: 1955-09-16
Page: 2

In the season of abundant harvests

    Agricultural Crops in the Chuanshui Area Generally Increased in Production
    The crops in the Chuanshui agricultural area of Qinghai Province have generally had a bumper harvest. According to reports, the unit area yield this year is much higher than the bumper harvest year of 1954. In Huzhu Tu Autonomous County, the lowest is 3 to 4 percent higher than last year, and the highest is 15 to 20 percent higher; in Guide County, it is generally 30 to 40 percent higher than last year; in Xunhua Salar Autonomous County, the area along the Yellow River has increased wheat and highland barley production by more than 1.3 million catties compared to last year. The average yield of highland barley here is more than 600 catties per mu, and the average yield of highland barley in the Unity Agricultural Production Cooperative is more than 700 catties per mu, an increase of more than 100 catties compared to last year; some yields are even as high as 800 or 900 catties per mu. The unit area yields in the Chuanshui areas of Minhe, Huangzhong, Datong and other places have also increased compared to last year.

### 川水地区农作物普遍增产

1955-09-16
第2版()
专栏：在丰收的季节里

    川水地区农作物普遍增产
    青海省农业区川水地区庄稼普遍丰收。据了解，各地今年的单位面积产量，比一九五四年丰收年又高出许多。互助土族自治县最低的较去年高出百分之三到四，最高的高出百分之十五到二十；贵德县较去年普遍高出三成到四成；循化撒拉族自治县沿黄河地区比去年增产小麦、青稞一百三十多万斤。这里的青稞平均每亩产量六百多斤，团结农业生产合作社的青稞平均每亩产量七百多斤，比去年增加一百多斤；有的每亩产量甚至高达八、九百斤。民和、湟中、大通等地川水地区单位面积产量也均比去年增加。
