Title: Poland Decides to Demobilize 47,000 Troops This Year
Author:
Date: 1955-09-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, September 4th: According to the Polish Press Agency, the government of the People's Republic of Poland passed the following resolution at a meeting on September 3rd:

    In view of the progress made by the Four Power Heads of State Geneva Conference in easing international tensions, and in the hope that Poland will make a real contribution to disarmament efforts, the government of the People's Republic of Poland has decided to reduce the number of officers and soldiers in the armed forces of the People's Republic of Poland by 47,000 before December 20, 1955.

    Personnel demobilized from the army will go to work in state-owned enterprises, factories, mines, and agriculture.

### 波兰决定今年裁军四万七千人

1955-09-05
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰决定今年裁军四万七千人
    新华社华沙四日电　据波兰通讯社报道，波兰人民共和国政府在九月三日的会议上通过了下列决定：
    波兰人民共和国政府鉴于四国政府首脑日内瓦会议在缓和国际紧张局势方面所取得的进展和希望波兰对于裁军方面的努力作出真正的贡献，决定在一九五五年十二月二十日以前把波兰人民共和国武装部队的军官和士兵裁减四万七千人。
    从军队中裁减出来的人员将到国家企业、工厂、矿山和农业中去工作。
