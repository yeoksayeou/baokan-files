Title: Telegram from Sanzo Nosaka, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, to the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1955-09-25
Page: 1

To the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party in Beijing:

    We are deeply grateful to your Party and your people for the heartfelt hospitality extended to our Central Committee members Yoshio Shiga and Tatsu Tokuda when they came to your country to receive the remains of Comrade Kyuichi Tokuda, and for the solemn memorial activities you held for Comrade Kyuichi Tokuda. Comrade Shiga and Comrade Tatsu Tokuda have now safely returned to Tokyo with the remains of Comrade Kyuichi Tokuda. Please accept our most heartfelt thanks.

		First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, Sanzo Nosaka
					September 17, 1955

### 日共中央第一书记野坂参三致中共中央电

1955-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    日共中央第一书记野坂参三致中共中央电致北京中国共产党中央委员会：
    当我们的中央委员会委员志贺义雄和德田达夫人为接运德田球一同志的遗骨来到你国时，他们受到你们党和你们人民的恳切款待，你们并为德田球一同志举行了隆重的纪念活动，对此，我们全体都十分感激。志贺同志和德田达同志现在已携回德田球一同志的遗骨安抵东京。谨致最衷心的谢意。
                  日本共产党中央委员会第一书记　野坂参三			一九五五年九月十七日
