Title: Belgian Parliamentary Delegation Visits the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-09-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 6th: According to TASS, a Belgian parliamentary delegation, headed by the Speaker of the House of Representatives, Mr. C. Huysmans, arrived in Moscow on the 5th at the invitation of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

	Welcoming them at the airport were V.V. Volkov, Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, J.E. Kalnberzins, Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities, A.F. Gorkin, Deputy Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and Walter Loridan, the Belgian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, among others.

	In his welcoming address to the guests, Kalnberzins pointed out that the visit of the Belgian parliamentary delegation to the Soviet Union would promote better mutual understanding and friendly relations between the peoples of the Soviet Union and Belgium, and would promote the consolidation of the cause of peace.

	In his reply, Mr. Huysmans, the head of the delegation, said that the Belgian people, like the people of other countries, are concerned about peace and will do their utmost to prevent war.

### 比利时议会代表团到苏联访问

1955-09-07
第4版()
专栏：

    比利时议会代表团到苏联访问
    新华社六日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：以下议院议长卡·余斯曼为首的比利时议会代表团，应苏联最高苏维埃的邀请在五日到达莫斯科。
    到机场欢迎的有苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席沃尔科夫、民族院主席拉齐斯，苏联最高苏维埃主席团副秘书戈尔金以及比利时驻苏联大使华尔特·洛里丹等人。
    拉齐斯向来宾们致欢迎词时指出，比利时议会代表团的访问苏联，将促进苏联和比利时人民之间更好的互相谅解和友好关系，将促进和平事业的巩固。
    代表团团长余斯曼致答词说，比利时人民和其他国家人民一样关心和平，并且将竭尽全力来防止战争。
