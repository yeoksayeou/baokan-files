Title: Swedish Ambassador Wistrand Recalled Home

Author:

Date: 1955-09-28
Page: 2

Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Wistrand, has been recalled and left Beijing for home on the 27th. Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was at the airport to see him off. Staff members of the Swedish Embassy in China and personnel from various foreign embassies in China were also present to see him off. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 瑞典大使魏斯特朗奉调回国

1955-09-28
第2版()
专栏：

    瑞典大使魏斯特朗奉调回国
    瑞典王国驻我国特命全权大使魏斯特朗已奉调于二十七日离京返国。前往机场送行的有我国外交部礼宾司司长柯华。瑞典王国驻华大使馆人员和各国驻华使馆人员也前往送行。　（新华社）
