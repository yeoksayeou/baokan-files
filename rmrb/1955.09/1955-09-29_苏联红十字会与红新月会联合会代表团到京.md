Title: Delegation of the Union of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies of the Soviet Union Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. At the invitation of the Red Cross Society of China, the delegation of the Union of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies of the Soviet Union arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 28th to attend the National Day celebrations. The head of the delegation is G.A. Miterev, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Union of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies of the Soviet Union. The members of the delegation are V.A. Panchinko, Member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Union of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Central Committee of the Red Cross Society of Ukraine, and P.I. Baranetsky, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Red Cross Society of Belarus.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China and Minister of Health, Fang Shishan, Secretary-General of the Chinese Medical Association, Wang Xiuying, Deputy Director of the Chinese Nursing Association, and Yan Jingqing, President of the Beijing Branch of the Red Cross Society.

Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation were Gutikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, Damilin, Acting Director of the Soviet Red Cross Hospital in Beijing, and Zhu Zhongli, Vice President, among others.

### 苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会代表团到京

1955-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会代表团到京
    新华社二十八日讯　应中国红十字会总会邀请前来中国参加国庆观礼的苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会代表团，在二十八日下午到达北京。代表团的团长是苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会执行委员会主席格·阿·米捷列夫，团员是苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会中央委员会主席团委员、乌克兰红十字会中央委员会主席瓦·阿·潘钦科，白俄罗斯红十字会中央委员会主席彼·伊·巴拉涅斯基。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国红十字会总会会长、卫生部部长李德全，中华医学会总干事方石珊，中华护士学会副理事长王琇瑛和红十字会北京市分会会长严镜清等。
    前往机场欢迎的还有苏联驻华大使馆参赞顾悌克夫和北京苏联红十字医院代理院长达米林、副院长朱仲丽等多人。
