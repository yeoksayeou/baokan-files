Title: Soviet Union and Finland Hold Talks in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1955-09-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 17th (TASS): In accordance with the agreement reached on September 16th between Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and Finnish President Paasikivi, talks on further developing and strengthening the friendly relations between the two countries continued on the 17th at the Moscow Kremlin.

    The Soviet side participating in the talks included: Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union; Khrushchev, Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union; Pervukhin, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union; Semenov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union; and Lebedev, Soviet Ambassador to Finland.

    The Finnish side participating in the talks included: Kekkonen, Prime Minister of Finland; Skog, Minister of Defense; Svento, Minister; and Seppala, State Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

    Bulganin and Kekkonen delivered speeches at the meeting.

    After the speeches, the two sides exchanged views.

    The next meeting will be held on the morning of the 19th.

### 苏联和芬兰在莫斯科举行会谈

1955-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和芬兰在莫斯科举行会谈
    新华社十八日讯　塔斯社莫斯科十七日讯：按照苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫和芬兰总统巴锡基维九月十六日达成的协议，关于进一步发展和巩固两国之间的友好关系的会谈于十七日在莫斯科克里姆林宫继续举行。
    苏联方面参加会谈的有：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁，苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫，苏联部长会议第一副主席别尔乌辛，苏联外交部副部长谢苗诺夫和苏联驻芬兰大使列别杰夫。
    芬兰方面参加会谈的有：芬兰总理吉科宁，国防部长斯科格，部长斯汶托和外交部国务秘书塞帕拉。
    布尔加宁和吉科宁在会上先后发言。
    发言以后，双方交换了意见。
    下一次会议将在十九日上午举行。
