Title: Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming Holds Reception to See Off Burmese Cultural Delegation Visiting China
Author:
Date: 1955-09-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 17th. The Burmese Cultural Delegation, led by U Win, Minister of Culture of the Union of Burma, and invited by the Government of the People's Republic of China, will depart for China on the 22nd for a visit.

    Yao Zhongming, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Burma, held a reception on the evening of the 16th to see off all members of the delegation. Minister U Win and more than sixty members of the delegation attended the reception. Ambassador Yao Zhongming delivered a speech at the reception, expressing his firm belief that the Burmese Cultural Delegation's visit and performance in China would not only be of great significance to the cultural exchange between the two countries, but would also surely enhance the friendship between the two countries.

    U Win said in his speech: The Burmese Cultural Delegation's visit to China coincides with the sixth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, and the Burmese Cultural Delegation is even more excited and encouraged to be able to participate in the National Day celebrations together with the Chinese people. He said that the Burmese Cultural Delegation's visit to China will definitely help strengthen cultural exchanges and friendship between the two countries.

    During the reception, members of the delegation performed songs and dances.

### 我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明举行招待会  欢送缅甸文化代表团来我国访问

1955-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明举行招待会
    欢送缅甸文化代表团来我国访问
    新华社十七日讯  仰光消息：应中华人民共和国政府邀请的缅甸文化代表团，在缅甸联邦文化部部长吴温率领下，将在二十二日启程去中国访问。
    中华人民共和国驻缅甸大使姚仲明在十六日晚举行招待会，欢送代表团全体人员。吴温部长和代表团团员六十多人都出席了招待会。姚仲明大使在招待会上致词，他表示深信缅甸文化代表团这次到中国访问和演出，不仅对两国文化交流有很大意义，对两国友谊也必有所增进。
    吴温致词说：缅甸文化代表团这次访问中国，恰逢中华人民共和国六周年国庆，缅甸文化代表团能和中国人民一同来参加国庆庆典，更感到兴奋和鼓舞。他说，缅甸文化代表团这次访问中国，一定会有助于加强两国之间的文化交流和友谊。
    席间，代表团团员们表演了歌曲和舞蹈。
