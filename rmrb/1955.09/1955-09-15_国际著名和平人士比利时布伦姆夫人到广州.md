Title: Prominent International Peace Activist, Madame Blum of Belgium, Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1955-09-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 14th: Madame Isabelle Blum of Belgium, a member of the Standing Committee and Secretariat of the World Peace Council, winner of the Stalin International Peace Prize for "Strengthening International Peace," and a prominent international peace activist, arrived in Guangzhou on the afternoon of the 14th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, accompanied by Zheng Senyu, representative of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, who had gone to Shenzhen to greet her.

Those who welcomed her at the station included Du Guoxiang, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Ma Hao, Secretary-General; Luo Peiyuan, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; and Zhou Wanru, Deputy Director of the Guangdong Democratic Women's Federation.

After Madame Blum got off the train, she warmly shook hands with the welcomers and accepted flowers from the children.

### 国际著名和平人士比利时布伦姆夫人到广州

1955-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

    国际著名和平人士
    比利时布伦姆夫人到广州
    新华社广州十四日电　世界和平理事会常务委员兼书记处书记、“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金获得者、国际著名和平人士比利时伊莎贝丽·布伦姆夫人，应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来中国访问。她在十四日下午，由前往深圳迎接的中国人民保卫世界和平委员会代表郑森禹陪同到达广州。
    到车站欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广东分会副主席杜国庠、秘书长马皓、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广州分会副主席罗培元，广东省民主妇女联合会副主任周婉如等。
    布伦姆夫人下车后，同欢迎者亲切握手，并接受了少年儿童的献花。
