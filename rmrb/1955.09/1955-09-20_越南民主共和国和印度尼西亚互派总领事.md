Title: The Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Indonesia Exchange Consuls-General
Author:
Date: 1955-09-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 16th. Vietnam News Agency reported on the 16th: After the government of the Republic of Indonesia de facto recognized the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the governments of the two countries conducted negotiations on the exchange of diplomatic representatives.

    Recently, the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has agreed to the appointment of Wijoyo Wirjodijo as Consul-General of the Republic of Indonesia to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. At the same time, the government of the Republic of Indonesia has agreed to the appointment of Jia Wenqing as Consul-General of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Republic of Indonesia.

    The two consuls-general are preparing to take office.

### 越南民主共和国和印度尼西亚互派总领事

1955-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国和印度尼西亚互派总领事
    新华社河内十六日电　越南通讯社十六日消息：在印度尼西亚共和国政府事实上承认越南民主共和国政府以后，两国政府进行了关于交换外交代表的谈判。
    最近，越南民主共和国政府已经同意由维约维尔多约为印度尼西亚共和国驻越南民主共和国总领事。同时，印度尼西亚共和国政府已经同意由葭文请为越南民主共和国驻印度尼西亚共和国总领事。
    这两位总领事正在准备就任。
