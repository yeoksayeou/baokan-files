Title: Burmese Military Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Premier Zhou Enlai and Marshal Peng Dehuai Receive the Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th—The Burmese Military Friendship Delegation, led by General Ne Win, Commander-in-Chief of the Burmese Armed Forces, arrived in Beijing by train on the morning of the 28th.

    As the train carrying the distinguished guests slowly entered the station, the military band played a welcoming tune.

    The distinguished guests alighted from the train and shook hands with the welcoming crowd one by one. After the military band played the national anthems of Burma and China, Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, accompanied General Ne Win in reviewing the honor guard.

    Afterward, General Ne Win delivered the following speech at the station:

    "Respected Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, and distinguished leaders:

    I feel very honored and happy to have the opportunity to lead a military friendship delegation to visit the People's Republic of China.

    As for the great leaders of the People's Republic of China, especially the military leaders, I have only heard of them from afar in the past, but now I will personally have close and friendly meetings with these leaders, and I feel extremely happy.

    There is no doubt that the existing goodwill and friendship between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Burma will be further enhanced by the mutual friendly contact and close relationship between the armed forces of the two countries.

    Long live Chairman Mao Zedong and the great leaders of the People's Republic of China!

    May the goodwill and friendship between the armed forces of our two countries last forever!"

    Among those who came to the station to welcome the delegation today were Cheng Qian and Long Yun, Vice-Chairmen of the National Defense Council; General Tan Zheng, General Wang Shusheng, and Senior General Li Da, Vice-Ministers of National Defense; General Su Yu, Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Senior General Chen Geng and Senior General Li Kenong, Deputy Chiefs of the General Staff; Senior General Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department; General Luo Ruiqing, Commander and Political Commissar of the Public Security Forces; Senior General Yang Chengwu, Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison; and generals from various military branches and arms, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and Vice-Mayor of Beijing Feng Jiping.

    U Hla Maung, Ambassador of the Union of Burma to China, military attachés of various countries stationed in China, and the Burmese Cultural Delegation also came to the station to welcome the delegation.

    As the distinguished guests passed before the welcoming crowd, the platform resounded with thunderous applause.

    The Burmese Military Friendship Delegation was accompanied from Guangzhou to Beijing by An Dong, representative of the Ministry of National Defense, Jiang Keding, Acting Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau, and Xu Yimin, Deputy Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Burma.

    The delegation consists of thirteen members, led by General Ne Win, Commander-in-Chief of the Burmese Armed Forces. The members include: Brigadier General Te Clift, Commander of the Air Force; Brigadier General Kyaw Win, Director of the Military Affairs Department of the Supreme Headquarters; Brigadier General Kyaw Soe, Commander of the Northern Burma Military Region; Brigadier General D. Blake, Commander of the Southern Burma Military Region; Colonel Chit Myaing, Commander of the 6th Infantry Brigade; Colonel Soe Myint, Commander of the Liaison Team of the Northern Burma Military Region; Colonel Khin Nyo, Commander of the 3rd Infantry Brigade; Lieutenant Commander Tin Dan Lu, Deputy Commander of the Navy; Major Lun, Officer of the General Staff Department of the Army; Major Nyunt Tun, Officer of the Burmese Army NCO School; Captain Tin Shwe, Staff Officer of the General Staff Department of the Army; and Captain Tin Kyi, Aide-de-Camp to the Chief of Staff.
    
    Xinhua News Agency, September 28th—Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China and Minister of National Defense Marshal Peng Dehuai received all members of the Burmese Military Friendship Delegation, headed by General Ne Win, Commander-in-Chief of the Burmese Armed Forces, at 11:30 a.m. on the 28th.

    Present at the reception were Vice-Premier Chen Yi, Senior General Su Yu, General Tan Zheng, General Wang Shusheng, Senior General Chen Geng, Senior General Li Kenong, Senior General Li Da, Senior General Xiao Hua, Senior General Liu Yalou of the Air Force, Senior General Wang Hongkun of the Navy, Senior General Yang Chengwu, Senior General Zhu Liangcai, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and Xu Yimin, Deputy Military Attaché of China to Burma.

    U Hla Maung, Ambassador of the Union of Burma to China, and Lieutenant Colonel Maung Maung, Military Attaché to China, were also present at the reception. (Attached picture)

### 缅甸军事友好代表团到北京  周总理彭德怀元帅接见代表团

1955-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸军事友好代表团到北京
    周总理彭德怀元帅接见代表团
    新华社二十八日讯　由缅甸联邦武装部队总司令奈温中将率领的缅甸军事友好代表团在二十八日晨乘火车到达北京。
    当载着贵宾们的列车徐徐进入车站时，军乐队奏起了欢迎曲。
    贵宾们下车后和欢迎的人们一一握手。在军乐队奏缅甸和中国两国国歌后，中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀元帅陪同奈温中将检阅了仪仗队。
    接着，奈温中将在车站上致词如下：
    “可敬的中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀元帅和各位领导者：
    我能有机会率领一个军事友好代表团前来中华人民共和国访问，感到非常荣幸和愉快。
    对于中华人民共和国的伟大领导者们，尤其是对于军事领导者们，在我说来，过去我只是在远方听说过，但现在我将亲自和这些领导者们进行亲密而友好的会晤，我感到万分高兴。
    毫无疑问，中华人民共和国和缅甸联邦现存的善意和友谊，将由于两国武装部队之间的相互的友好联系和亲密关系而更加增强。
    毛泽东主席和中华人民共和国的伟大领导者们万岁！
    愿我们两国武装部队之间的善意和友谊永存！”
    今天到车站欢迎的，有国防委员会副主席程潜、龙云，国防部副部长谭政大将、王树声大将、李达上将，中国人民解放军总参谋长粟裕大将，副总参谋长陈赓大将、李克农上将，总政治部副主任萧华上将，公安军司令员兼政治委员罗瑞卿大将，京津卫戍司令员杨成武上将，和各军种兵种的将军们，外交部副部长章汉夫，北京市副市长冯基平。
    缅甸联邦驻华大使吴拉茂和各国驻华武官，缅甸联邦文化代表团也到车站欢迎。
    贵宾们走过欢迎行列前时，月台里响起了暴风雨般的掌声。
    缅甸军事友好代表团从广州前来北京途中，由国防部代表安东、外事处代处长蒋克定和中国驻缅甸大使馆副武官徐毅民陪同。
    代表团由十三人组成，团长是缅甸联邦武装部队总司令奈温中将，团员有：空军司令特·克利夫特准将，最高统帅部军务署署长觉温准将，北缅军区司令觉素准将，南缅军区司令德·布莱克准将，第六步兵旅旅长漆棉上校，北缅军区联络队指挥苏密上校，第三步兵旅旅长庆牛上校，海军副司令丁丹卢海军少校，陆军部参谋总署官员伦少校，缅甸陆军军士学校军官尼吞少校，陆军部参谋总署人员登瑞上尉，参谋总长侍卫官丁季上尉。
    新华社二十八日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来和国防部长彭德怀元帅在二十八日上午十一时半接见了以缅甸联邦武装部队总司令奈温中将为首的缅甸军事友好代表团全体人员。
  接见时在座的，有陈毅副总理，粟裕大将、谭政大将、王树声大将、陈赓大将、李克农上将、李达上将、萧华上将、刘亚楼空军上将、王宏坤海军上将、杨成武上将、朱良才上将、萧向荣中将，外交部副部长章汉夫，和中国驻缅甸副武官徐毅民。
    接见时，缅甸联邦驻华大使吴拉茂和驻华武官茂茂中校也在座。（附图片）
