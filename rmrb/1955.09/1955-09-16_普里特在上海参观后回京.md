Title: Pritt Returns to Beijing After Visiting Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1955-09-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 15th: Pritt, President of the British Peace Committee, and his wife, accompanied by Chen Tiqiang, Director of the China Political Science and Law Society, returned to Beijing on the morning of the 15th after vacationing and visiting Lushan and Shanghai.

During their stay in Shanghai, Pritt and his wife had discussions with people from Shanghai's political and legal circles and business circles, toured the Shanghai urban area, and visited the Children's Palace, etc.

### 普里特在上海参观后回京

1955-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

    普里特在上海参观后回京
    据新华社十五日讯　英国和平委员会会长普里特和他的夫人在中国政治法律学会理事陈体强陪同下在庐山、上海休养和参观后，已在十五日晨回京。
    普里特和他的夫人在上海期间，曾和上海政法界、工商界人士进行了交谈，游览了上海市区，参观了少年儿童宫等。
