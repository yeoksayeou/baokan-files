Title: Danish Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, August 8th. A ten-member Danish cultural delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 8th, led by the head of the delegation, Hans Hansen.
    Representatives from the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the China Political Science and Law Association, the Architectural Society of China, the Chinese Medical Association, the Chinese Writers Association, and other organizations were at the airport to welcome them.

### 丹麦文化代表团到京

1955-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    丹麦文化代表团到京
    新华社八日讯  应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的丹麦文化代表团一行十人，由团长汉斯·汉森率领在八日下午到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国人民对外文化协会、中国政治法律学会、中国建筑学会、中华医学会、中国作家协会等单位的代表。
