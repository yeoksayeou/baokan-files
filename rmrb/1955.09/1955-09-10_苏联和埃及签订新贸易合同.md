Title: Soviet Union and Egypt Sign New Trade Contract
Author:
Date: 1955-09-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 8th (TASS): Following successful negotiations based on the Soviet-Egyptian trade agreement, the Soviet Union and Egypt signed contracts in Cairo on the 6th regarding the Soviet Union's purchase of 60,000 tons of rice from Egypt and the Soviet Union's export of 500,000 tons of Soviet oil to Egypt.

The signing of these contracts demonstrates the further expansion of Soviet-Egyptian trade on the basis of mutual benefit.

### 苏联和埃及签订新贸易合同

1955-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和埃及签订新贸易合同
    新华社九日讯　塔斯社开罗八日电：苏联和埃及在苏埃贸易协定的基础上进行了顺利的商谈以后，六日在开罗签订了关于苏联从埃及购买六万吨大米，并且向埃及输出五十万吨苏联石油的合同。
    这些合同的签订证明了在互利基础上苏埃贸易的进一步的扩大。
