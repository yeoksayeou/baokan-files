Title: Li Dequan Receives Miterev and Others
Author:
Date: 1955-09-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross Society, received G.A. Miterev, head of the delegation of the Soviet Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies, and members V.A. Panchnko and P.I. Baranesky at the headquarters of the Chinese Red Cross Society on the morning of September 29. Miterev, representing the Soviet Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies and the Moscow Red Cross Society, and Panchnko and Baranesky, representing the Ukrainian and Belarusian Red Cross Societies respectively, presented gifts to the Chinese Red Cross Society. Peng Zeming, Vice President of the Chinese Red Cross Society, Hu Lansheng, Vice President and Secretary-General, Chen Qiyuan, Standing Director, and Wu Yunfu also attended the reception.

### 李德全接待米捷列夫等

1955-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    李德全接待米捷列夫等
    新华社二十九日讯　中国红十字会总会会长李德全二十九日上午在中国红十字会总会接待了苏联红十字会和红新月会联合会代表团团长格·阿·米捷列夫，团员瓦·阿·潘钦科和彼·伊·巴拉涅斯基。米捷列夫代表苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会和莫斯科市红十字会，潘钦科和巴拉涅斯基分别代表乌克兰和白俄罗斯红十字会赠送了礼物给中国红十字会。参加接待的还有中国红十字会总会副会长彭泽民、副会长兼秘书长胡兰生、常务理事陈其瑗、伍云甫等。
