Title: Tibet Region Delegation and Tibet Youth Delegation Arrive in Beijing; Delegations of Various Ethnic Groups from Guizhou, Yunnan, and Sichuan Provinces Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. The Tibet Region Delegation and the Tibet Youth Delegation, a group of 202 people, led by Lhalu Tsewang Dorje, head of the Tibet Region Delegation, Vice Heads Anqin Living Buddha and Rinqen Dondrub Living Buddha, and Sangpo Tenzin Dondrub, head of the Tibet Youth Delegation, and Vice Heads Tsewang Dodan and Yexi Pingcuo, arrived in Beijing by special train on the afternoon of the 28th. Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, and others went to the station to welcome them.

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. The Guizhou Province Ethnic Groups Delegation, the Yunnan Province Ethnic Groups Delegation, and the Sichuan Province Yi Ethnic Group Delegation arrived in Beijing on the 25th, 26th, and 29th, respectively. The three delegations include twenty-three ethnic groups, totaling one hundred and sixty people.

### 西藏地区参观团和西藏青年参观团到北京  贵州云南四川三省各族参观团到北京

1955-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    西藏地区参观团和西藏青年参观团到北京
    贵州云南四川三省各族参观团到北京
    据新华社二十八日讯　西藏地区参观团和西藏青年参观团一行二百零二人，在西藏地区参观团团长拉鲁·策旺多吉、副团长安钦活佛、仁钦顿珠活佛和西藏青年参观团团长桑颇·登增顿珠、副团长才旺多登、悦希凭卓率领下，二十八日下午乘专车到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有国务院秘书长习仲勋等。
    新华社二十九日讯　贵州省各民族参观团、云南省各民族参观团、四川省彝族参观团分别在二十五日、二十六日、二十九日到达北京。三个参观团包括二十三个民族成份，共一百六十人。
