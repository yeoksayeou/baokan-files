    Our Great Motherland
    Changsha is the hub of water transportation in Hunan Province, and more than 100,000 boat people often pass through here. In order to take care of the cultural life needs of the boat people, a boat people's cultural palace has been established here, with reading rooms, game rooms, theaters, and ballrooms. This is the exterior of the Hunan Boat People's Cultural Palace.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    长沙是湖南省水上交通的中枢，全省有十多万船民常常经过这里。为了照顾船民文化生活的需要，这里建立了一座船民文化宫，设有阅览室、游艺室、剧场和球场。这是湖南船民文化宫的外貌。
