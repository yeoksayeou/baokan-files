Title: Hungary Decides to Reduce Military by 20,000 Troops This Year
Author:
Date: 1955-09-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, September 7th, TASS dispatch: According to a report by the Hungarian News Agency: The Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic, in view of the relaxation of international tension following the Geneva Four-Power Heads of Government Conference, and with the hope that the Hungarian side will effectively promote the further easing of the international situation and the restoration of trust between countries, has decided to reduce the armed forces of the Hungarian People's Republic by 20,000 troops before December 31, 1955.

The materials saved as a result of the reduction of the armed forces will be used to further develop the national economy and meet social needs. Demobilized soldiers and officers will be placed in agriculture, industry, and other sectors of the national economy and in various agencies.

### 匈牙利决定今年裁军两万人

1955-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利决定今年裁军两万人
    新华社八日讯塔斯社布达佩斯七日讯：据匈牙利通讯社报道：匈牙利人民共和国部长会议鉴于日内瓦四国政府首脑会议后国际紧张局势有了缓和，并且希望匈牙利方面有效地促进国际局势的进一步缓和和恢复国与国之间的信任，决定在一九五五年十二月三十一日以前把匈牙利人民共和国武装部队裁减两万人。
    由于裁减武装部队而省下来的物资将用在进一步发展国民经济和满足社会需要上。复员后的士兵和军官将安置在农业、工业以及国民经济其他部门和各机关中工作。
