Title: China Record Factory Replicates Vietnamese Records
Author:
Date: 1955-09-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency

    To celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the China Record Factory has replicated forty kinds of records from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, which will be released nationwide starting September 2nd.
    These records include songs such as "Ode to Ho Chi Minh," "Salute to the Vietnam Workers' Party," "Dedicated to Henri Martin," "Song of the Army and People," "Dien Bien Phu Chant," and operas such as "The North, Central and South are One Family," and "Sister Xin and Brother Tian." Among them, the song "Dedicated to Henri Martin" won an honorary award at the 4th World Youth and Students Peace and Friendship Festival held in Bucharest in 1953. Many other original songs are award-winning works from the Vietnamese Democratic Republic Association of Literary and Art Workers.

### 中国唱片厂复制越南唱片

1955-09-05
第2版()
专栏：

    中国唱片厂复制越南唱片
    中国唱片厂为庆祝越南民主共和国成立十周年，复制了四十种越南民主共和国的唱片，自九月二日起向全国各地发行。
    这些唱片包括歌曲“胡志明颂”、“向越南劳动党致敬”、“寄给亨利·马丁”、“军民之歌”、“奠边府号子”以及戏曲“中南北部是一家”、“心嫂田哥”等。其中“寄给亨利·马丁”一曲曾获得一九五三年在布加勒斯特举行的第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节荣誉奖。还有不少创作的歌曲是获得越南民主共和国文学艺术工作者协会奖励的作品。　（新华社）
