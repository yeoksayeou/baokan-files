Title: State Council Holds Eighteenth Plenary Session
Author: 
Date: 1955-09-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. The Eighteenth Plenary Session of the State Council of the People's Republic of China was held on the afternoon of the 16th.

The meeting, in accordance with the regular conscription system stipulated in the Military Service Law of the People's Republic of China, adopted an order on conscription for 1955.

The meeting discussed the issue of awarding medals to meritorious personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and approved the list of the first group of personnel to be awarded the First Class August 1st Medal, the First Class Independence Freedom Medal, and the First Class Liberation Medal, deciding to submit it to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation.

The meeting discussed and approved the new uniform styles of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the styles of the military rank shoulder boards, collar badges, and branch of service and duty symbols, deciding to implement them throughout the Chinese People's Liberation Army starting from October 1, 1955.

The meeting approved the "Organizational Regulations (Draft) of the People's Congresses and People's Committees at All Levels in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region of the People's Republic of China," deciding to submit them to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for approval.

The meeting heard the report on the visit to the Soviet Union made by Shi Liang, head of the Chinese Judicial Workers' Delegation to the Soviet Union; and approved the decision to abolish the establishment of Duan County in Guangxi Province and establish the two Yao Autonomous Counties of Duan and Bama.

Finally, the meeting approved appointment and removal matters.

### 国务院举行第十八次全体会议

1955-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第十八次全体会议
    新华社十七日讯  中华人民共和国国务院全体会议第十八次会议在十六日下午举行。
    会议根据中华人民共和国兵役法规定的定期征兵制度，通过了关于一九五五年度征兵的命令。
    会议讨论了关于授予中国人民解放军有功人员勋章问题，通过了应授予一级八一勋章、一级独立自由勋章、一级解放勋章的第一批人员的名单，决定提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议。
    会议讨论和批准了中国人民解放军新的服装制式和军衔肩章、领章和兵种、勤务符号的样式，决定从一九五五年十月一日起在中国人民解放军全军实行。
    会议通过了“中华人民共和国内蒙古自治区各级人民代表大会和各级人民委员会组织条例（草案）”，决定提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会批准。
    会议听了中国司法工作者访苏代表团团长史良所作的访问苏联的报告；通过了关于撤销广西省都安县的建制，成立都安、巴马两瑶族自治县的决定。
    会议最后通过了任免事项。
