Title: The 15th Meeting of the Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Held; China Proposes Two Issues for Discussion Under the Second Item on the Agenda
Author: 
Date: 1955-09-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 14th - The 15th meeting of the Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks was held at the Palais des Nations at 10:00 am local time on the 14th. Chinese Ambassador Wang Bingnan said at the meeting: In the same spirit of consultation and reconciliation, it should also be possible to smoothly reach an agreement on the second item on the agenda—other practical issues on which both sides have disputes. He then suggested that two issues should be discussed and resolved under the second item on the agenda: first, the embargo issue; and second, the issue of preparing for higher-level Sino-U.S. talks.

According to a spokesman for U.S. Ambassador Johnson, Ambassador Johnson spoke about the issue of "implementing the agreement" after the meeting.

The 14th meeting adjourned at 11:40 am. The next meeting will be held on September 20 (next Tuesday) at 10:00 am.

### 中美两国大使级会谈举行第十五次会议  我方提出第二项议程目前应讨论的两个问题

1955-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中美两国大使级会谈举行第十五次会议
    我方提出第二项议程目前应讨论的两个问题
    新华社日内瓦十四日电　中美两国大使级会谈于十四日本地时间十时在国联大厦举行第十五次会议。中国大使王炳南在会议上说：本着同样的协商和和解的精神，在第二项议程——双方有所争执的其他实际问题——上顺利地达成协议也应该是可能的。他接着建议在第二项议程下目前应该讨论和解决两个问题：第一是禁运问题；第二是准备举行更高级的中美会谈的问题。
    据美国大使约翰逊的发言人在会后宣布，约翰逊大使谈到“执行协议”的问题。
    十四日会议在十一时四十分休会。下次会议将在九月二十日（下星期二）上午十时举行。
