Title: Arab League Political Committee Issues Communiqué Saying It Decided to Implement Consistent Policies in Accordance with the Spirit of the Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-09-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 6th, TASS dispatch: The Arab League Political Committee held a regular meeting in Cairo from September 3rd to 5th.

The official communiqué issued after the meeting stated that the Political Committee discussed the issue of Arab countries adopting a coordinated and consistent attitude at the upcoming United Nations General Assembly meeting and passed a resolution to "implement consistent policies in the spirit of the Bandung Conference."

The communiqué pointed out that the Arab countries advocate "consolidating universal peace and supporting the demands of all countries in the world for freedom."

The Arab League Political Committee declared its support for the liberation struggles of the people of North African countries, condemned the actions of the British authorities in the Arabian Peninsula, and demanded a solution to the Palestine issue.

The communiqué stated, "Solving these problems is a necessary condition for ensuring the stability of the Middle East situation and consolidating world peace."

According to "Izvestia," the Political Committee recommended that Arab national delegations in the United Nations "support the national aspirations of the Cypriot residents for the right to self-determination."

The agenda of the Political Committee meeting included the issue of Arab countries supporting the demand for the restoration of the legitimate rights of the People's Republic of China in the United Nations. According to "Al-Ahram," the Political Committee has decided to submit this issue to the foreign ministers of the Arab countries for consideration. "Al-Ahram" wrote that a meeting of the foreign ministers of the Arab League member states is scheduled to be held on September 20th.

### 阿拉伯联盟政治委员会发表公报说  决定根据亚非会议精神执行一致的政策

1955-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯联盟政治委员会发表公报说
    决定根据亚非会议精神执行一致的政策
    新华社七日讯　塔斯社开罗六日讯：阿拉伯联盟政治委员会九月三日到五日在开罗举行了例会。
    会议结束后发表的正式公报说，政治委员会讨论了关于阿拉伯国家在即将举行的联合国大会会议上采取协同一致的态度的问题，并且通过了“本着万隆会议的精神执行一致的政策”的决议。
    公报指出，阿拉伯国家主张“巩固普遍和平，并且支持世界上所有国家获得自由的要求”。
    阿拉伯联盟政治委员会声明支持北非国家人民的解放斗争，谴责英国当局在阿拉伯半岛的行动，并且要求解决巴勒斯坦问题。
    公报说，“解决这些问题是保证中东局势稳定和巩固世界和平的必要条件。”
    据“消息报”报道，政治委员会建议阿拉伯各国代表团在联合国中“支持塞浦路斯居民要求自决权的民族愿望。”
    政治委员会会议的议程包括关于阿拉伯国家支持恢复中华人民共和国在联合国的合法权利的要求问题。据“金字塔报”报道，政治委员会已经决定把这个问题提交阿拉伯国家的外交部长们考虑。“金字塔报”写道，阿拉伯联盟成员国外交部长会议决定在九月二十日召开。
