Title: Reactions to Criticism and Suggestions
Author:
Date: 1955-09-28
Page: 6

Column: Readers' Letter Special Page

    A letter from the Northeast Branch of the Central School of Political Science and Law: The criticism of our school's violation of financial discipline and waste of state funds in the "Readers' Letter Special Page" on August 21st is correct. In addition to conducting a self-examination and turning over the surplus materials, we have also decided to strictly enforce internal financial management systems to ensure that similar errors do not occur in the future.
                ×　×　×
    A letter from the Communist Party Committee of the Qiqihar Locomotive and Rolling Stock Manufacturing Factory: The criticism of our factory's waste of materials in the "Readers' Letter Special Page" on August 7th is correct. We have already taken measures to ensure that state property is no longer wasted.
              ×　×　×
    A letter from the Hangu Salt Field Workers' Hospital: The "Readers' Letter Special Page" on August 28th criticized us for "a small hospital's great waste." We have already conducted an inspection and educated the relevant personnel; the hospital's direct superior has also given the relevant personnel the punishment they deserved.
                ×　×　×
    After the "Readers' Letter Special Page" published the letter of criticism "Failure to Listen to Mass Opinions Causes Waste" on July 10th, the Liaoning Provincial State-Owned Yantai Coal Mine, under the leadership of the mine's Party committee, organized a joint inspection team led by the Supervision Office to conduct a month-and-a-half inspection. The results proved that the readers' criticism was correct. Mine Director Li Feng has already conducted a self-criticism and is determined to learn from this lesson and improve his work.
              ×　×　×
    The "Readers' Letter Special Page" criticized the Yangquan Mining Bureau on February 20th for its poor work style in not caring about the hardships of workers, and on May 15th, it criticized the phenomenon of He Wusheng, the chairman of the No. 2 Mine Union, who also did not care about the hardships of workers, being promoted to vice chairman of the mining area union. The Yangquan Mining Bureau has already adopted methods such as organizing study sessions, convening workers' representative meetings and Party representative meetings to criticize and reverse the phenomenon of not caring about the hardships of workers. He Wusheng, the former chairman of the No. 2 Mine Union, has also been subjected to the punishment of being placed on probation within the Party for two years and being removed from his administrative post.
            ×　×　×
    The East China Coal Mining Administration wrote a letter accepting the criticism in the "Readers' Letter Special Page" on August 15th in the letter "Why Not Let the Combine be Used in Production." They reviewed the bureaucratic attitude in organizing the use of machinery and sent someone to conduct investigations and research to help relevant bureaus and mines normally utilize the existing combines.
          ×　×　×
    Qiqihar Paper Mill Director Luo Chang wrote a letter: The criticism of our factory's neglect of safe production and constant accidents in the "Readers' Letter Special Page" on August 28th is completely correct. The main responsibility for the large number of accidents in our factory lies with the factory's leadership. We have already conducted a self-criticism at the workers' congress and established a safe production responsibility system to ensure that it is conscientiously implemented in the future.
                ×　×　×
    The Beijing Municipal Social Welfare Bureau wrote a letter: The criticism of our bureau's illegal recruitment of workers on the eastern suburban construction site in the "Readers' Letter Special Page" on September 3rd is correct. We would like to thank the person who made this criticism. We have already punished those responsible for the error and mobilized the recruited farmers to return to their hometowns for production.
                  ×　×　×
    The Second Machinery Industry Administration Bureau of the First Ministry of Machine Building Industry wrote a letter: The criticism of the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Factory's lack of attention to the protection and maintenance of machine tools in the "Readers' Letter Special Page" on August 28th is correct. In order to correct this factory's erroneous behavior of only paying attention to production, neglecting equipment maintenance, and being politically negligent, we have instructed the staff of our bureau stationed in this factory to inspect and supervise the factory's equipment management and security work and propose specific improvement measures.
                  ×　×　×
    The Eighth Engineering Section of the Second Engineering Bureau of the New Railway Construction General Bureau of the Ministry of Railways wrote a letter: The "Readers' Letter Special Page" criticized us on August 3rd for "blindly purchasing and causing stockpiles." We have accepted this lesson and are determined to strengthen planned supply in future work, strictly review material usage plans, and strive to eliminate stockpiling and waste.
                ×　×　×
    The "Readers' Letter Special Page" on September 3rd criticized the Chen County Native Produce Company for receiving too much grain, resulting in the spoilage of a large batch of flour. According to a reply letter from the Hunan Provincial Native Produce Company, Zhu Leting, the manager of the Chen County Native Produce Company, has been given a demerit.

### 批评建议的反应

1955-09-28
第6版()
专栏：读者来信专页

    批评建议的反应
    中央政法干部学校东北分校来信：八月二十一日“读者来信专页”对我校违犯财政纪律、浪费国家资金的批评是正确的。我们除了进行检讨和把多余物资上缴外，并且决定严格内部的财务管理制度，保证今后不再发生类似错误。
                ×　×　×
    中共齐齐哈尔机车车辆制造工厂委员会来信：八月七日“读者来信专页”对我厂浪费器材的批评是正确的。我们已经采取措施，保证不再浪费国家资财。
              ×　×　×
    汉沽盐场职工医院来信：八月二十八日“读者来信专页”批评我们“小医院的大浪费”。我们已经做了检查，并对有关人员进行教育；我院的直属上级也已经给予有关人员以应得的处分。
                ×　×　×
    七月十日“读者来信专页”发表了“不听取群众意见造成了浪费”的批评信后，辽宁省地方国营烟台煤矿在矿党委领导下，以监察室为主组织了联合检查组，进行了一个半月的检查，结果证明读者的批评是正确的。矿长李凤已作了检讨，并决心接受这个教训，改进工作。
              ×　×　×
    “读者来信专页”在二月二十日批评了阳泉矿务局不关心工人疾苦的恶劣作风，在五月十五日又对不关心工人疾苦的二矿工会主席贺戊生被提拔为矿区工会副主席的现象提出了批评。阳泉矿务局已经通过组织学习、召开职工代表会和党代表会等办法来批判并扭转不关心工人疾苦的现象。原二矿工会主席贺戊生也已经受到党内留党察看二年、行政撤职的处分。
            ×　×　×
    华东煤矿管理局来信接受八月十五日“读者来信专页”中“为什么不让康拜因使用到生产中去”一信的批评。他们检讨了组织机械使用工作中的官僚主义态度，并且派专人进行调查研究工作，帮助有关局矿正常地利用现有的康拜因。
          ×　×　×
    齐齐哈尔造纸厂厂长罗昶来信：八月二十八日“读者来信专页”对我厂忽视安全生产、不断发生事故的批评是完全正确的。我厂事故多主要责任在于厂的领导方面。我们已经在职工大会上检讨，并建立了安全生产责任制，保证今后认真贯彻。
                ×　×　×
    北京市社会福利事业局来信：九月三日“读者来信专页”上对我局东郊工地违法招工的批评是正确的，我们特向这个批评者致谢。我们已经处分了错误的责任者，并动员这次招来的农民回乡生产。
                  ×　×　×
    第一机械工业部第二机器工业管理局来信：八月二十八日“读者来信专页”对沈阳第一机床厂不重视机床的保卫和护养工作的批评是正确的。为了改正这个工厂的只顾生产、忽视设备护养和政治上麻痹大意的错误行为，我们已责成我局派驻这个工厂的工作人员对工厂的设备管理和保卫工作进行检查督促，并提出具体的改进措施。
                  ×　×　×
    铁道部新建铁路工程总局第二工程局第八工程段来信：八月三日“读者来信专页”批评我们“盲目采购造成积压”。我们已经接受这个教训，决心在今后工作中加强计划供应，严格审查用料计划，努力消灭积压浪费现象。
                ×　×　×
    九月三日“读者来信专页”批评了郴县土产公司多领粮食霉坏大批面粉的错误。据湖南省土产公司来信答复：郴县土产公司经理祝乐亭已受到记大过的处分。
