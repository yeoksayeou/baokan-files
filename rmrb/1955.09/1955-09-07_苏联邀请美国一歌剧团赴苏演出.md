Title: Soviet Union Invites American Opera Troupe to Perform in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-09-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Washington dispatch, TASS: Soviet Chargé d'Affaires in the United States, Striganov, on the 1st, on behalf of the Soviet Ministry of Culture, invited the American "Everyman" Opera Company to tour the Soviet Union in the fourth quarter of 1955 to perform the American opera "The Taming of the Shrew and Blows". The deputy manager of this troupe, Mr. Blinn, has accepted this invitation.

### 苏联邀请美国一歌剧团赴苏演出

1955-09-07
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联邀请美国一歌剧团赴苏演出
    新华社六日讯　塔斯社华盛顿讯：苏联驻美国临时代办斯特里加诺夫一日以苏联文化部的名义邀请美国“人人”歌剧团在一九五五年第四季度到苏联去巡回演出美国歌剧“棘鬣鱼和伯斯”。这个剧团的副经理布林已接受了这一邀请。
