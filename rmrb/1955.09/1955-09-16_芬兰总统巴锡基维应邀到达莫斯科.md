Title: Finnish President Paasikivi Arrives in Moscow at Invitation

Author:
Date: 1955-09-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 15th. TASS, Moscow, September 15th: President Juho Kusti Paasikivi of the Republic of Finland arrived in Moscow on the 15th at the invitation of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

Accompanying the President were: Finnish Prime Minister Urho Kekkonen, Minister of Defense E. Skog, and former Foreign Minister Sven Tuomioja, among others.

The flags of Finland and the Soviet Union were flying at the airport. Greeting President Paasikivi were: Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, K.E. Voroshilov, V.M. Molotov, M.A. Suslov, ministers of various Soviet ministries, responsible staff members of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.

Also welcoming Finnish President Paasikivi were the Finnish Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union, E.A. Vuori, and embassy staff, diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union, as well as Soviet and foreign journalists.

### 芬兰总统巴锡基维应邀到达莫斯科

1955-09-16
第4版()
专栏：

    芬兰总统巴锡基维应邀到达莫斯科
    新华社十五日讯　塔斯社莫斯科十五日讯：芬兰共和国总统尤·库·巴锡基维应苏联最高苏维埃主席团的邀请在十五日到达了莫斯科。
    随同总统前来的有：芬兰总理乌·吉科宁，国防部长埃·斯科格和前外交部长斯汶托等人。
    飞机场上悬挂着芬兰和苏联的国旗。前来欢迎巴锡基维总统的有：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席克·叶·伏罗希洛夫，维·米·莫洛托夫，米·安·苏斯洛夫，苏联各部部长，苏联最高苏维埃主席团和苏联外交部的负责工作人员。
    欢迎芬兰巴锡基维总统的还有芬兰驻苏联特命全权大使埃·阿·沃里和大使馆工作人员，各国驻苏联外交使节以及苏联和外国的新闻记者。
