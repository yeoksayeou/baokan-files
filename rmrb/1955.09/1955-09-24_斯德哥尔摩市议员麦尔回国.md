Title: Stockholm City Councilor Mair Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1955-09-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd. Stockholm City Councilor Mair of Sweden and his wife, who were invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit China, left Beijing by plane on the 22nd to return home.
	During their nearly one-month stay in China, Mr. and Mrs. Mair visited Beijing, the Northeast, and Shanghai, and contacted responsible persons from various sectors of our country. They held discussions on issues such as economic construction, municipal administration, trade, agriculture, health, trade unions, women, and children's work.

### 斯德哥尔摩市议员麦尔回国

1955-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    斯德哥尔摩市议员麦尔回国
    新华社二十二日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来中国访问的瑞典斯德哥尔摩市议员麦尔和他的夫人，在二十二日乘飞机离开北京回国。
    麦尔夫妇在中国近一个月的时间内，参观访问了北京、东北和上海等地，并同我国各方面负责人士接触，座谈了经济建设、市政、贸易、农业、卫生、工会、妇女和儿童工作等问题。
