Title: Prominent International Peace Advocate Mrs. Blum Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 17th. Mrs. Isabelle Blum, a prominent international peace advocate from Belgium, a member of the Standing Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat of the World Peace Council, and a recipient of the Stalin International Prize "For Strengthening Peace Between Nations," arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 17th. She is visiting China at the invitation of the China Peace Committee.

Those who welcomed her at the airport included Li Yimang, member of the Standing Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat of the World Peace Council; Liu Guan-yi, Secretary-General of the China Peace Committee; Chen Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Shen Ziji, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Wu Huazhi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Kameta Togo and Vantuluri, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee; and committee member Rewi Alley.

After Mrs. Blum stepped off the plane, she warmly shook hands with the welcomers and accepted flowers.

Mrs. Blum stayed in Guangzhou and Wuhan for a total of three days on her way to Beijing.

### 国际著名和平人士  布伦姆夫人到北京

1955-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    国际著名和平人士
    布伦姆夫人到北京
    新华社十七日讯  世界和平理事会常务委员兼书记处书记、“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金获得者、国际著名和平人士比利时伊莎贝丽·布伦姆夫人在十七日下午乘飞机来到北京。她是应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请来中国访问的。
    到机场欢迎的，有世界和平理事会常务委员兼书记处书记李一氓，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一，中国人民外交学会副会长陈翰笙，中华全国民主妇女联合会书记处书记沈兹九，中国人民对外文化协会副秘书长吴化之，亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长龟田东伍、万徒勒里，委员艾黎等。
    布伦姆夫人走下飞机后，和欢迎者亲切握手，并接受了献花。
    布伦姆夫人在来京途中曾在广州、武汉共逗留了三天。
