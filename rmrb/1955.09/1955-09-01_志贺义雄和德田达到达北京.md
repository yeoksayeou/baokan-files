Title: Yoshio Shiga and Tatsu Tokuda Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 31st -- Yoshio Shiga, Central Committee member of the Japanese Communist Party, and Tatsu Tokuda, wife of the late Secretary-General of the Japanese Communist Party Kyuichi Tokuda, arrived in Beijing by plane from Guangzhou on August 31st, accompanied by Li Chuli, a responsible staff member of the CCP Central Committee. They came to Beijing to transport the remains of the late Comrade Kyuichi Tokuda, Secretary-General of the Japanese Communist Party.

Those who welcomed them at the airport were: Deng Xiaoping, member of the Political Bureau of the CCP Central Committee and Secretary-General of the CCP Central Committee; Yang Shangkun, Deputy Secretary-General of the CCP Central Committee; Zhao Yimin and Li Qixin, responsible staff members of the CCP Central Committee; Liu Ren, Second Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CCP; Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Zhang Yun, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Hu Yaobang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League; Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth; and Japanese people in Beijing such as Togo Kameda, Kenta Kaneko, Uemon Nakamura, Katsuyuki Takahashi, Juko Matsuyama, and Junji Kinoshita.

### 志贺义雄和德田达到达北京

1955-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    志贺义雄和德田达到达北京
    新华社三十一日讯　日本共产党中央委员志贺义雄和德田球一夫人德田达，由中共中央负责工作人员李初梨陪同，在八月三十一日由广州乘飞机到达北京。他们是来北京接运日本共产党已故总书记德田球一同志的遗骨的。
    到机场迎接的有：中共中央政治局委员、中共中央秘书长邓小平，中共中央副秘书长杨尚昆，中共中央负责工作人员赵毅敏、李启新，中共北京市委员会第二书记刘仁，中华全国总工会主席赖若愚，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席章蕴，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦，中华全国民主青年联合会主席廖承志，以及在北京的日本人士龟田东伍、金子健太、中村玩右卫门、高桥胜之、松山树子、木下顺二等。
