Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Stockholm City Councilor Myrdal
Author:
Date: 1955-09-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th. Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yi, received Swedish Stockholm City Councilor Myrdal and Mrs. Myrdal at 10:00 AM on the 19th. Present at the reception were Chen Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Huang Hua, Standing Director, and Wu Maosun, Secretary-General.

### 陈毅副总理接见斯德哥尔摩市议员麦尔

1955-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见斯德哥尔摩市议员麦尔
    新华社二十日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅十九日上午十时接见了瑞典斯德哥尔摩市议员麦尔和麦尔夫人。接见时在座的，有中国人民外交学会副会长陈翰笙、常务理事黄华及秘书长吴茂荪等。
