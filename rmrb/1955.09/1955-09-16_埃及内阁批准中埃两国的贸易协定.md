Title: Egyptian Cabinet Approves Trade Agreement Between China and Egypt
Author:
Date: 1955-09-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 15th: The Egyptian Cabinet approved on the 14th the trade agreement signed in Beijing on August 22, 1955, by representatives of the governments of the Egyptian Republic and the People's Republic of China.

### 埃及内阁批准中埃两国的贸易协定

1955-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

    埃及内阁批准中埃两国的贸易协定
    新华社十五日讯开罗消息：埃及内阁在十四日批准了埃及共和国和中华人民共和国两国政府代表一九五五年八月二十二日在北京签订的贸易协定。
