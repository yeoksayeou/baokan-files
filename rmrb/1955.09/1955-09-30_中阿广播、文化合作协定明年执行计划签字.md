Title: Sino-Albanian Radio and Cultural Cooperation Agreement Execution Plan for Next Year Signed
Author: 
Date: 1955-09-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: The Sino-Albanian Radio Cooperation Agreement and the 1956 Execution Plan of the Sino-Albanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement were signed in Beijing on the 28th.

Representing the Chinese side, Mei Yi, Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau, signed the Sino-Albanian Radio Cooperation Agreement, and Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Culture, signed the 1956 Execution Plan of the Sino-Albanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Representing the Albanian side, Fadil Paçrami, head of the Albanian cultural delegation and Chairman of the Albanian Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations, signed both documents.

After the signing, Qian Junrui and Paçrami delivered speeches successively, congratulating the further development of cultural exchanges between China and Albania and the consolidation and strengthening of the friendship between the two peoples.

On the Chinese side, those who attended the signing ceremony included Xia Yan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture, Ye Shengtao, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Education, He Wei, Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Zhu Kezhen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations.

On the Albanian side, those who attended the signing ceremony included Nesti Nase, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Albania to China, and members of the Albanian cultural delegation Gjimiter Lamani, Hasan Duma, Aleks Çaçi, and Nezmedin Çajmi.

### 中阿广播、文化合作协定明年执行计划签字

1955-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    中阿广播、文化合作协定明年执行计划签字
    新华社二十八日讯　中阿广播合作协定、中阿文化合作协定一九五六年执行计划二十八日在北京签字。
    代表中国方面在中阿广播合作协定上签字的是广播事业局局长梅益，在中阿文化合作协定一九五六年执行计划上签字的是文化部代理部长钱俊瑞。代表阿尔巴尼亚方面在这两项文件上签字的是阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团团长、阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会主席法迪尔·帕奇拉米。
    在签字以后，钱俊瑞和帕奇拉米相继致词，祝贺中阿两国文化交流的进一步发展和两国人民友谊的巩固和加强。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有文化部副部长夏衍，教育部副部长叶圣陶，外交部部长助理何伟，中国科学院副院长竺可桢，对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经。
    阿尔巴尼亚方面参加签字仪式的，有阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻华大使纳赛，阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团团员吉米特尔·拉玛尼、哈桑·杜玛、阿列克斯·恰奇、聂泽梅丁·查伊米。
