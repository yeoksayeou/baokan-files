Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation to Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1955-09-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 15th. At the invitation of the Federation of Trade Unions of Yugoslavia, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions has sent a trade union delegation headed by Liu Ningyi to Yugoslavia for a 20-day friendly visit. The delegation departed from Beijing by plane on the 15th.

Members of the Delegation:
Liu Ningyi (Head of Delegation), Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions
Zhang Xiuzhu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions
Shi Lei, Vice-Chairman of the China Heavy Industry Trade Union
Two Interpreters

### 我国工会代表团去南斯拉夫

1955-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我国工会代表团去南斯拉夫
    新华社十五日讯　中华全国总工会应南斯拉夫工会联合会的邀请，派遣一个以刘宁一为首的工会代表团前往南斯拉夫作二十天的友好访问。代表团在十五日乘飞机由北京启程。
    代表团组成人员
    刘宁一（团长）中华全国总工会副主席
    张修竹　中华全国总工会书记处书记
    石磊　中国重工业工会副主席
    翻译员二人
