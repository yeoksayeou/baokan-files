Title: China's Foreign Ministry Replies to British Chargé d'Affaires in China, Agreeing to the British Government Accepting the U.S. Mandate to Provide Assistance to Americans Wishing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1955-09-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th. The Chargé d'Affaires of the United Kingdom in China, Mr. Humphrey Trevelyan, notified the Chinese government in a note on September 20th that the government of the United States of America had formally entrusted the government of the United Kingdom to assume the responsibilities stipulated in the joint declaration of the Chinese and American ambassadors on September 10th regarding the return of civilians of both sides to their respective countries. The government of the United Kingdom had also notified the government of the United States of America that it had agreed to accept this responsibility. On September 22nd, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China notified the Chargé d'Affaires of the United Kingdom in China, Mr. Humphrey Trevelyan, in a reply note, that the government of the People's Republic of China agreed that the government of the United Kingdom, entrusted by the government of the United States of America, should provide the assistance stipulated in the joint declaration of the Chinese and American ambassadors for Americans in China who wish to return to the United States.

### 关于英国政府接受美国委托对愿意回国的美国人提供协助事  我国外交部复照英国驻华代办表示同意

1955-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    关于英国政府接受美国委托对愿意回国的美国人提供协助事
    我国外交部复照英国驻华代办表示同意
    新华社二十四日讯　联合王国驻中华人民共和国代办欧念儒在九月二十日以照会通知中国政府，美利坚合众国政府已正式委托联合王国政府担负中美两国大使九月十日关于双方平民回国问题的协议声明中所规定的责任，联合王国政府并已通知美利坚合众国政府，同意接受此项责任。九月二十二日，中华人民共和国外交部以复照通知联合王国代办欧念儒，中华人民共和国政府同意联合王国政府受美利坚合众国政府委托，对愿意回国的在中国的美国人返回美国，提供中美两国大使协议声明中所规定的协助。
