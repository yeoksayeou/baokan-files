Title: Japanese Communist Party Headquarters Holds Memorial Service for Tokuda Kyuichi
Author:
Date: 1955-09-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 28th - A memorial service for the late General Secretary Tokuda Kyuichi was held at the Japanese Communist Party headquarters on the 19th. Participants included Nosaka Sanzō, First Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party, Central Committee members, alternate Central Committee members, Inspection Committee members, Central Editorial Committee members, and all staff members of the headquarters.

　　After all participants observed a moment of silence, Nosaka Sanzō delivered a speech. He called on all members of the Japanese Communist Party to implement and understand the spirit of the new program of the Japanese Communist Party formulated under the leadership of Comrade Tokuda Kyuichi and the resolutions and party constitution passed by the Sixth National Congress based on the thought of Comrade Tokuda Kyuichi, and to struggle to strengthen the unity of the Party.

　　Next, Central Committee member Shiga Yoshio reported on the completion of the task of bringing back the remains of Comrade Tokuda Kyuichi.

　　The memorial service concluded with another moment of silence from all participants.

### 日本共产党总部举行吊唁德田球一仪式

1955-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    日本共产党总部举行吊唁德田球一仪式
    新华社二十八日讯　东京消息：日本共产党总部在十九日举行已故总书记德田球一的吊唁仪式。参加仪式的有日本共产党第一书记野坂参三、中央委员、候补中央委员、监察委员、中央编辑委员和总部的全体工作人员。
    全体参加仪式的人们静默致哀后，野坂参三致词。他号召日本全体共产党员贯彻和领会德田球一同志领导制定的日本共产党新纲领和第六次全国代表会议根据德田球一同志的思想而通过的决议和党章的精神，并且为加强党的团结而斗争。
    接着，中央委员志贺义雄作了完成接回德田球一同志遗骨的任务的报告。
    吊唁仪式最后在全体再度静默致哀后结束。
