Title: Guizhou Builds New Machine Lumber Mill
Author:
Date: 1955-09-06
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency

    A new machine lumber mill in the forest area at the foot of the Miao Mountains in eastern Guizhou Province was put into production in August.
    The foot of the Miao Mountains is the main forest area supplying timber for capital construction in Guizhou and neighboring provinces. After this machine lumber mill is put into production, its production efficiency will be nearly ten times higher than that of manual operation in the past.

### 贵州新建一个机器制材厂

1955-09-06
第2版()
专栏：

    贵州新建一个机器制材厂
    贵州省东部苗岭山麓的林区里，一座新建的机器制材厂已在八月份投入生产。
    苗岭山麓是供应贵州和邻省基本建设用材的主要林区。这个机器制材厂投入生产后，生产效率将比过去用人工操作提高近十倍。（新华社）
