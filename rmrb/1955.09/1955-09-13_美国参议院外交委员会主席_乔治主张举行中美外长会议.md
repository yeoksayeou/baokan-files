Title: US Senate Foreign Relations Committee Chairman George Advocates for Sino-US Foreign Ministers Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-09-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 12th—Washington News: U.S. Senator George, Chairman of the Senate Foreign Relations Committee and a Democratic Senator, told reporters on the 11th that he was very excited about the recent developments in the Sino-US ambassadorial talks. He was "pleased" that the talks had reached an agreement on the issue of the return of civilians of both sides.

George also told reporters that he advocated that China and the United States hold a foreign ministers meeting later this year to discuss the situation in the Taiwan region.

### 美国参议院外交委员会主席  乔治主张举行中美外长会议

1955-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    美国参议院外交委员会主席
    乔治主张举行中美外长会议
    新华社十二日讯　华盛顿消息：美国参议院外交委员会主席、民主党参议员乔治十一日对记者说，他对中美两国大使级会谈最近的发展感到很兴奋。会谈就双方平民回国问题达成了协议使他感到“高兴”。
    乔治还对记者说，他主张中美两国在今年晚些时候举行外长会议来讨论台湾地区局势问题。
