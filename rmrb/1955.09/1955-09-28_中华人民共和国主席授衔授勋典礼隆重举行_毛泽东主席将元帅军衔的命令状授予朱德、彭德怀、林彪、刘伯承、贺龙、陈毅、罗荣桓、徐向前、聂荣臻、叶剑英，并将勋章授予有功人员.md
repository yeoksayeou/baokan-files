Title: Grand Ceremony Held for the Conferring of Ranks and Medals by the President of the People's Republic of China. Chairman Mao Zedong Conferred the Order of Military Rank of Marshal of the People's Republic of China to Zhu De, Peng Dehuai, Lin Biao, Liu Bocheng, He Long, Chen Yi, Luo Ronghuan, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, and Ye Jianying, and Conferred Medals to Meritorious Personnel
Author: 
Date: 1955-09-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th - The grand ceremony of the President of the People's Republic of China conferring the military rank of Marshal of the People's Republic of China to officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and awarding medals to meritorious personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army during the Chinese People's Revolutionary War was solemnly held at the Huairentang Hall in Beijing at 5:00 p.m. today.

    A huge portrait of Chairman Mao Zedong was hung in the center of the rostrum, with national flags on both sides of the portrait.

    Present on the rostrum were: Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Zhu De, Vice Chairman, Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council.

    Also present on the rostrum were: Song Qingling, Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, Deng Zihui, He Long, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, Li Fuchun, and Li Xiannian, Vice Premiers of the State Council.

    Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, announced the commencement of the ceremony, and the military band played the national anthem.

    Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman and Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, read out the order of the President of the People's Republic of China conferring the military rank of Marshal of the People's Republic of China to officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Chairman Mao Zedong then personally presented the orders conferring the military rank of Marshal of the People's Republic of China to Zhu De, Peng Dehuai, Lin Biao, Liu Bocheng, He Long, Chen Yi, Luo Ronghuan, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, and Ye Jianying.

    After the ceremony of conferring the rank of Marshal, Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman and Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, read out the order of the President of the People's Republic of China awarding medals to meritorious personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army during the Chinese People's Revolutionary War.

    Chairman Mao Zedong then presented the First Class August 1st Medal, the First Class Independent Freedom Medal, and the First Class Liberation Medal to meritorious personnel who participated in the revolutionary wars during the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army period, the War of Resistance Against Japan period, and the Liberation War period, meritorious personnel who directly led the uprising of the original Kuomintang army during the Liberation War period, meritorious personnel who contributed to the People's Liberation War, and meritorious personnel who contributed to the peaceful liberation of the Tibet region.

    Those attending the ceremony today to receive medals were the personnel in Beijing from the first batch of awardees approved by the 22nd meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

    Those who won the First Class August 1st Medal, the First Class Independent Freedom Medal, and the First Class Liberation Medal were: Zhu De, Peng Dehuai, Lin Biao, Liu Bocheng, He Long, Chen Yi, Luo Ronghuan, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Ye Jianying, Wang Hongkun, Wang Jinshan, Wang Zhen, Wang Shusheng, Gan Siqi, Zhu Liangcai, Liu Zhijian, Liu Yalou, Liu Daosheng, Li Kenong, Li Da, Li Tao, Song Renqiong, Chen Geng, Zhou Shidi, Zhou Baozhong, Luo Ruiqing, Hong Xuezhi, Sun Yi, Ni Zhiliang, Zhang Zongxun, Zhang Aiping, Zhang Yunyi, Zhang Jingwu, Guo Tianmin, Xu Guangda, Mo Wenhua, Peng Shaohui, Fu Qiutao, Cheng Shicai, Huang Kecheng, Su Yu, Feng Baiju, Zhao Erlu, Yan Hongyan, Lai Chuanzhu, Xie Fuzhi, Xiao Ke, Xiao Jingguang, Xiao Hua, and Tan Zheng.

### 中华人民共和国主席授衔授勋典礼隆重举行  毛泽东主席将元帅军衔的命令状授予朱德、彭德怀、林彪、刘伯承、贺龙、陈毅、罗荣桓、徐向前、聂荣臻、叶剑英，并将勋章授予有功人员

1955-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国主席授衔授勋典礼隆重举行
    毛泽东主席将元帅军衔的命令状授予朱德、彭德怀、林彪、刘伯承、贺龙、陈毅、罗荣桓、徐向前、聂荣臻、叶剑英，并将勋章授予有功人员
    新华社二十七日讯　中华人民共和国主席授予中国人民解放军军官以中华人民共和国元帅军衔及授予中国人民解放军在中国人民革命战争时期有功人员勋章典礼，今日下午五时在北京怀仁堂隆重举行。
    主席台正面悬挂着毛泽东主席的巨幅画像，画像两侧挂着国旗。
    在主席台上的有：中华人民共和国主席毛泽东、副主席朱德，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来。
    在主席台上的还有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄、林伯渠、李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、李维汉、陈叔通，国务院副总理陈云、彭德怀、邓小平、邓子恢、贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫、李富春、李先念。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会典礼局局长余心清宣布典礼开始，军乐队奏国歌。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长兼秘书长彭真宣读了中华人民共和国主席授予中国人民解放军军官以中华人民共和国元帅军衔的命令。毛泽东主席接着将授予中华人民共和国元帅军衔的命令状，一一授予朱德、彭德怀、林彪、刘伯承、贺龙、陈毅、罗荣桓、徐向前、聂荣臻、叶剑英。
    授予元帅军衔礼成以后，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长兼秘书长彭真宣读了中华人民共和国主席授予中国人民解放军在中国人民革命战争时期有功人员勋章的命令。
    毛泽东主席接着将一级八一勋章、一级独立自由勋章、一级解放勋章，分别授予在中国工农红军时期、抗日战争时期、解放战争时期参加革命战争的有功人员，在解放战争时期直接领导原国民党军队起义的有功人员，对人民解放战争有功人员，以及对和平解放西藏地区有功人员。
