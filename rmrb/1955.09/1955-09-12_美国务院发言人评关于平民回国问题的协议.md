Title: U.S. State Department Spokesman Comments on Agreement Regarding Repatriation of Civilians
Author:
Date: 1955-09-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Washington, September 11th: U.S. State Department spokesman, Mr. Sultan, expressed "pleasure" at a press conference in Washington on the 10th regarding the agreement reached between the U.S. and Chinese ambassadors on the issue of the repatriation of civilians on both sides. Sultan said that, according to his understanding, the agreement would allow all American civilians in China to return home if they wished.

Sultan also said: "This (agreement) statement does not constitute an agreement at the governmental or other level, but rather a parallel unilateral statement.

"This statement does not in any way provide diplomatic recognition of the Chinese Communist regime, nor does it change the attitude of the United States in this regard."

### 美国务院发言人评关于平民回国问题的协议

1955-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    美国务院发言人评关于平民回国问题的协议
    新华社十一日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国务院发言人苏丹姆十日在华盛顿记者招待会上对中美两国大使在双方平民回国问题上达成协议一事表示“感到高兴”。苏丹姆说，根据他的了解，协议将使在中国的全部美国平民如果愿意的话都可回国。
    苏丹姆又说：“这项（协议的）声明并不构成一种政府级的或其他级的协议，而是一种平行的单方面声明。
    “这种声明并不在任何程度上提供对中共政权的外交上的承认，或改变美国在这方面的态度。”
