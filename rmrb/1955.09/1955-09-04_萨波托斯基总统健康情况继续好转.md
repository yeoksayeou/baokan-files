Title: President Zapotocky's Health Continues to Improve
Author:
Date: 1955-09-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 3rd: A communique regarding the health of President Antonín Zápotocký of the Republic was released here at 9:00 AM on September 2nd. The communique stated: President Zápotocký's general health condition and heart function have continued to improve over the past 24 hours. Due to the improvement in his condition, daily communiques regarding President Zápotocký's health will no longer be issued.

### 萨波托斯基总统健康情况继续好转

1955-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    萨波托斯基总统健康情况继续好转
    新华社布拉格三日电　此间九月二日上午九时发表了关于共和国总统安·萨波托斯基健康情况的公报。公报说：在过去二十四小时内，萨波托斯基总统的总的健康情况和心脏跳动情况继续好转。由于病情好转，今后将不再每天发表关于萨波托斯基总统健康情况的公报。
