Title: After the Nine-Day Meeting of the UN Disarmament Subcommittee, Soviet Representative Tells Reporters He Is Still Awaiting a US Statement
Author:
Date: 1955-09-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 10th, TASS dispatch: The United Nations Disarmament Commission's Subcommittee held a routine meeting on the 9th. The U.S. Representative, Mr. Stassen, chaired the meeting.

After the meeting, the Soviet representative, Mr. Sobolev, answered some questions from reporters. He said: "The Soviet delegation pointed out at today's Subcommittee meeting that the Soviet delegation is still awaiting the U.S. delegation's response to some of the questions raised by the Soviet delegation at the September 1st meeting. As everyone knows, these questions are about the levels of armed forces of the five major powers and some other countries after reductions; the effective date of the complete ban on atomic weapons within the overall disarmament plan; the cessation of atomic weapons testing; and the obligations of all countries not to use nuclear weapons before the agreement on the complete ban on atomic and hydrogen weapons comes into effect.

"Mr. Stassen said that the United States reserves its opinion on all previous proposals submitted to the Subcommittee, but this statement does not give a clear idea of the U.S. attitude towards the Soviet proposal of May 10. As everyone knows, this proposal contains a detailed plan for arms reduction and the prohibition of atomic weapons."

Sobolev said that the Soviet delegation also raised the following question to the U.S. delegation: does Eisenhower's proposal on the exchange of information on armed forces include information on conventional armaments and information on atomic and hydrogen weapons?

The next meeting of the Subcommittee is scheduled for the 13th.

### 在联合国裁军小组委员会九日会议结束后  苏联代表向记者说仍在期待美国表示态度

1955-09-12
第4版()
专栏：

    在联合国裁军小组委员会九日会议结束后
    苏联代表向记者说仍在期待美国表示态度
    新华社十一日讯　塔斯社纽约十日讯：联合国裁军委员会小组委员会九日举行例会。美国代表史塔生担任会议主席。
    会议结束后，苏联代表索波列夫回答了记者提出的一些问题。他说：“苏联代表团在今天小组委员会会议上指出，苏联代表团仍旧期待美国代表团回答苏联代表团在九月一日会议上所提出的一些问题。大家知道，这些问题是关于五大国和其他一些国家武装部队裁减后的水平；关于在总的裁军计划范围内完全禁止原子武器的禁令生效的时间；关于停止试验原子武器；以及关于在完全禁止原子武器和氢武器协定生效之前各国承担不使用核子武器的义务。
    “史塔生先生说，美国对小组委员会上以前所提出的一切建议保留自己的看法，可是这种说法不能使人得到一个明确的概念：美国对苏联五月十日的建议的态度究竟如何。大家知道，这一建议包含有裁减军备和禁止原子武器的详尽计划。”
    索波列夫说，苏联代表团还向美国代表团提出了这样一个问题：艾森豪威尔提出的关于交换武装部队情报的建议中是否包括常规军备的情报和原子武器和氢武器的情报。
    小组委员会下次会议定在十三日举行。
