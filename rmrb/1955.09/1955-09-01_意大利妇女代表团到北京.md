Title: Italian Women's Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 31st. At the invitation of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the nine-member Italian women's delegation arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to our country.

The delegation is led by Mrs. Rossi, Member of the Italian Parliament and President of the Italian Women's League. Other members of the delegation include parliamentarians, professors, and women and trade union workers.

Those who greeted them at the airport included Li Dequan and Zhang Yun, Vice-Chairpersons of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Luo Qiong, Cao Mengjun, and Shen Zijiu, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation, as well as responsible persons from the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defence of Children, the China Peace Committee, the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, and women representatives from all walks of life.

After the delegation disembarked, the welcomers presented them with flowers and shook hands and embraced them cordially.

During their visit to China, the Italian women's delegation will visit Beijing, Shenyang, Anshan, Fushun, Shanghai and other places.

### 意大利妇女代表团到北京

1955-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    意大利妇女代表团到北京
    新华社三十一日讯　应中华全国民主妇女联合会邀请来我国访问的意大利妇女代表团九人今日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团由意大利议会议员、意大利妇女联盟主席罗西夫人率领。代表团其他成员包括议员、教授以及妇女、工会工作者。
    到机场迎接的有中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全、章蕴，全国妇联书记处书记罗琼、曹孟君、沈兹九，以及中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、北京市民主妇女联合会等团体负责人以及各界妇女代表等。
    代表团下机后，欢迎者向她们献花，并和她们亲切地握手和拥抱。
    意大利妇女代表团访问中国期间将在北京、沈阳、鞍山、抚顺、上海等地进行参观和访问。
