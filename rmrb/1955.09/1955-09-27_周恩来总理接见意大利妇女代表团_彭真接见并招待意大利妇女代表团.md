Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Italian Women's Delegation; Peng Zhen Receives and Hosts Italian Women's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-09-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received the Italian Women's Delegation at 11:00 a.m. on the 26th.

Present at the meeting were Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premier of the State Council; Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; and Vice Chairmen Deng Yingchao, Xu Guangping, and Zhang Yun.

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman and Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, received the Italian Women's Delegation on the afternoon of the 26th.

Present at the meeting were Zhang Su, Wu Kejian, and Yu Xinqing, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Standing Committee members Xu Guangping and Deng Yingchao.

After the meeting, Peng Zhen hosted a banquet for the delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: After visiting Shanghai, Hangzhou, Wuhan, and Guangzhou for nearly two weeks, the Italian Women's Delegation returned to Beijing on the 25th.

Two members of the delegation had already returned to their country on the 18th. (Attached picture)

### 周恩来总理接见意大利妇女代表团  彭真接见并招待意大利妇女代表团

1955-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见意大利妇女代表团
    彭真接见并招待意大利妇女代表团
    新华社二十六日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在二十六日上午十一时接见了意大利妇女代表团。
    接见时在座的有：国务院副总理邓小平，外交部副部长张闻天，中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅、副主席邓颖超、许广平、章蕴等。
    新华社二十六日讯　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长兼秘书长彭真在二十六日下午接见了意大利妇女代表团。
    接见时在座的有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏、吴克坚、余心清和常务委员许广平、邓颖超等。
    接见后，彭真举行宴会招待代表团。
    据新华社二十六日讯　意大利妇女代表团在上海、杭州、武汉和广州等地参观了将近两个星期以后，已在二十五日回到北京。
    代表团团员二人已在十八日先行返国。（附图片）
