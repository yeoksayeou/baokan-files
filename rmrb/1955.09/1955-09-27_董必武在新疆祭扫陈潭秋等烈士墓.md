Title: Dong Biwu Pays Respects at the Graves of Martyrs Including Chen Tanqiu in Xinjiang
Author:
Date: 1955-09-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, September 26th: Dong Biwu, representative of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Central People's Government, who came to Urumqi to celebrate the establishment of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, paid respects on the 26th at the graves of Chen Tanqiu, one of the founders of the Communist Party of China and member of the Central Committee of the CPC, and the outstanding Communist Party members Mao Zemin and Lin Jilu, who gloriously sacrificed themselves for the cause of the liberation of the people of all nationalities in the country and Xinjiang.

Accompanying him were Liu Chun, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China, Wang Zaitian, Vice Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Qin Yingji, Vice Governor of Guangxi Province and Chairman of the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region, Wei Chunshu, Deputy Director of the Department of Transportation of Guangxi Province, Lan Changfa, Vice Chairman of the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Zhu Dehai, Vice Governor of Jilin Province and Chairman of the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Region, Dao Jingban, Member of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee and Chairman of the Dehong Dai-Jingpo Autonomous Region of Yunnan, Zhang Chong, representative of all circles of the Yi people in the Greater and Lesser Liang Mountains of Sichuan Province, Ou Baichuan, Vice Governor of Guizhou Province, Tian Feng, Director of the Office of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of Guizhou Province, Ma Hongbin, Vice Governor of Gansu Province, Ce Ren Na Mu, Deputy Director of the Gansu Provincial Women's Federation, Xi Rao Jia Cuo, Vice Governor of Qinghai Province, and Han Zhao'e, Vice Governor of Shaanxi Province.

Accompanying the tribute were Wang Enmao, First Secretary of the Xinjiang Sub-bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Saifudin, Fourth Secretary, Bao Erhan, Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government, Wu Kaizhang, Deputy Secretary of the Xinjiang Sub-bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Zeng Di, Deputy Political Commissar of the Xinjiang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Ren Gebai, First Secretary of the Urumqi Municipal Committee of the CPC and Mayor of Urumqi.

Dong Biwu and others presented wreaths in front of the martyrs' tombs and circled the tombs to pay their respects.

### 董必武在新疆祭扫陈潭秋等烈士墓

1955-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    董必武在新疆祭扫陈潭秋等烈士墓
    新华社乌鲁木齐二十六日电　前来乌鲁木齐祝贺新疆维吾尔自治区成立的中国共产党中央委员会和中央人民政府的代表董必武，二十六日祭扫了为全国和新疆各族人民解放事业光荣牺牲的中国共产党创始人之一、中共中央委员陈潭秋和中国共产党优秀党员毛泽民、林基路诸烈士的墓地。
    随同前往祭扫的还有前来乌鲁木齐祝贺新疆维吾尔自治区成立的中华人民共和国民族事务委员会副主任刘春，内蒙古自治区副主席王再天，广西省副省长、桂西僮族自治区主席覃应机，广西省交通厅副厅长韦纯束，中国人民政治协商会议桂西僮族自治区委员会副主席蓝昌法，吉林省副省长、延边朝鲜族自治区主席朱德海，云南省人民委员会委员、云南德宏傣族景颇族自治区主席刀京版，四川省大小凉山彝族各界代表张冲，贵州省副省长欧百川，贵州省民族事务委员会办公室主任田峰，甘肃省副省长马鸿宾，甘肃省民主妇女联合会副主任策仁娜姆，青海省副省长喜饶嘉错，陕西省副省长韩兆鹗。
    陪同祭扫的有中共中央新疆分局第一书记王恩茂、第四书记赛福鼎，新疆省人民政府主席鲍尔汉，中共中央新疆分局副书记武开章，中国人民解放军新疆军区副政治委员曾涤，中共乌鲁木齐市委员会第一书记、乌鲁木齐市市长任戈白。
    董必武等在烈士墓前献了花圈，并绕墓一周致敬。
