Title: Beijing to Add Over Two Thousand Hospital Beds This Year
Author:
Date: 1955-09-16
Page: 3

Beijing will add more than 2,600 hospital beds this year alone. This number will increase the total number of hospital beds in Beijing (excluding the military system) by more than one-third, equivalent to about five times the total number of hospital beds originally available in the city before liberation.

　　These newly added beds belong to nine newly built and expanded hospitals under the jurisdiction of the Ministry of Health and the Beijing Municipal Public Health Bureau. Among them, the newly completed hospitals include the largest children's hospital in the country with 600 beds, two tuberculosis hospitals, and two general hospitals.

　　According to the basic construction plan of the health department, Beijing will also start construction this year on five general hospitals with a total of 1,300 beds and an obstetrics hospital with 200 beds specializing in dystocia. This batch of medical institutions will be completed in 1956. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 北京今年将增加两千多病床

1955-09-16
第3版()
专栏：

    北京今年将增加两千多病床
    北京市今年一年内共将增加两千六百多张病床。这个数字将使北京市的病床总数（军队系统除外）增加三分之一以上，相当于解放前本市原有的病床总数的五倍左右。
    这些新增加的病床分别属于卫生部和北京市公共卫生局管辖下的九所新建和扩建的医院，其中已完成的新建医院有全国最大的、有着六百张床位的一所儿童医院、两所结核病院和两所综合医院。
    根据卫生部门的基本建设计划，北京市今年还要动工兴建共有一千三百张床位的五所综合医院和一所二百张床位的专治难产的产科医院等。这一批医疗机构将在一九五六年建成。　（新华社）
