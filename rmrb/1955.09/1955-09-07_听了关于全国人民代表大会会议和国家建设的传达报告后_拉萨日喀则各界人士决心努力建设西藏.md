Title: After Hearing Reports on the National People's Congress Meeting and National Construction, People from All Walks of Life in Lhasa and Xigaze are Determined to Work Hard to Build Tibet
Author:
Date: 1955-09-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 6th—After listening to the reports delivered by Kalon Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme and others of the leadership team of officials who accompanied the Dalai Lama to Beijing last year regarding the National People’s Congress meeting and national construction, Tibetan local government monk and lay officials, religious figures in Lhasa, and patriotic mass organization figures expressed their support. Kalon Dorje Phuntsog Rabgye of the Tibetan local government said: "After listening to the reports, we love our motherland even more. With the help of the Han people, we will work hard to build a prosperous and happy Tibet Autonomous Region." Acting Kalon Tompo Tsering Wangchuk said: "Under the brilliant light of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao’s policy of ethnic equality, the Tibetan people are realizing their right to be masters of their own affairs. In the future, the politics and religion in the Tibetan region will be even more prosperous." He also said: "Since the opening of the Kangzang and Qingzang highways to Lhasa, the daily necessities and other materials needed by the Tibetan people have been continuously transported here. Our motherland is providing great assistance to our Tibet region." Khenpo Tuba Wangpai Yexei of Sera Monastery said: "We, the representatives and Khenpos of the three major monasteries in Lhasa, will convey the main content of the report to the responsible lamas of our monasteries. For the happiness of the monks and laypeople, we must strive to do well in the work of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region." Yunkang Tubdain Nyima, Deputy Director of the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Association, said: "We must strengthen ties with the broad masses of young people, strive to learn new knowledge, and dedicate our strength to the various tasks of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region."

People from all walks of life in Xigaze also expressed their full support for the report delivered by Gyantse Jigme. Lhamin Yexei Chuchen, Director of the Panchen Kampus Assembly Hall Committee, said: "After hearing the reports on the Dalai Lama, the Panchen Erdeni, and major Tibetan officials attending the First National People’s Congress and visiting various parts of the country, our patriotic thinking has been enhanced, our confidence in building Tibet has been increased, and we have also deeply realized the correctness and greatness of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao’s ethnic policies. I sincerely thank the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao for their concern and love for the Tibetan people. In the future, we must work hard to do a good job in establishing the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, strengthen unity, and build a new Tibet." Wang Lejie, a great lama of Tashilhunpo Monastery, said: "Now, we have completely recognized that the Communist Party fully respects our religious beliefs and customs. In the future, we must follow Chairman Mao's instructions in everything and do a good job in the unity within the Tibetan ethnic group and between the Han and Tibetan ethnic groups." Nilong Gongjue, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Xigaze Patriotic Women's Federation, said: "The rapid development of various constructions in the interior of the motherland has given Tibetan women great encouragement. In the future, we must strive to study and improve our political and cultural levels, and better shoulder our glorious responsibility as women in the various tasks of building Tibet."

### 听了关于全国人民代表大会会议和国家建设的传达报告后  拉萨日喀则各界人士决心努力建设西藏

1955-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    听了关于全国人民代表大会会议和国家建设的传达报告后
    拉萨日喀则各界人士决心努力建设西藏
    新华社拉萨六日电　西藏地方政府僧俗官员、拉萨市宗教界和爱国群众团体人士，拥护去年随同达赖喇嘛去北京官员领导小组噶伦阿沛·阿旺晋美等的传达报告。西藏地方政府噶伦朵噶·彭错饶杰说：听了传达报告以后，使我们更加热爱祖国，在汉族人民的帮助下，努力建设繁荣幸福的西藏自治区。代理噶伦董波·钦绕旺曲说：在中国共产党、毛主席民族平等政策的光辉普照下，藏族人民正实现着“当家作主”的权利，今后西藏地区的政教将更加昌盛。他并说：自康藏、青藏两条公路通车拉萨以来，藏族人民需要的生活用品和其他物资源源运来，祖国对我们西藏地方正进行着大力的帮助。色拉寺堪布堆巴·汪白益希说：我们拉萨三大寺的代表和堪布将把报告中的主要内容，向本寺庙的负责喇嘛传达。为了僧俗人民的幸福，我们要努力作好西藏自治区筹备委员会的工作。拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会副主任委员云康·土登尼玛说：我们要和广大青年加强联系，努力学习新知识，为西藏自治区筹备委员会的各项工作献出力量。
    日喀则各界人士也表示完全拥护詹东·计晋美的传达报告。班禅堪布会议厅委员会主任委员拉敏·益喜楚臣说：听了达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼和西藏主要官员出席全国人民代表大会第一次会议，以及在全国各地参观情况的传达报告以后，提高了我们的爱国主义思想，增进了我们建设西藏的信心，同时也使我们深刻地认识到中国共产党、毛主席民族政策的正确伟大。我衷心感谢中国共产党、毛主席对西藏人民的关怀和爱护。今后，我们一定努力做好成立西藏自治区筹备委员会的工作，加强团结，建设新西藏。札什伦布寺大喇嘛汪乐阶说：现在，我们完全认清楚了共产党是充分尊重我们的宗教信仰和风俗习惯的，今后我们一切都要遵循毛主席的指示，作好西藏民族内部以及汉、藏民族间的团结。日喀则爱国妇女联谊会筹备委员会副主任尼隆贡觉说：祖国内地各项建设的飞跃发展，给了西藏妇女莫大的鼓舞。今后，我们要努力学习，提高政治、文化水平，在建设西藏的各项工作中，更好地担负起我们妇女的光荣责任。
