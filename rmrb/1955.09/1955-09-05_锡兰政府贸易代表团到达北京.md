Title: Ceylon Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 4th. A nine-member Ceylon government trade delegation, headed by the Minister of Trade, Commerce and Fisheries of Ceylon, Shirley Corea, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 4th. Li Zhezhi, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade, Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister, Zhang Wenjin, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Addis, Counselor of the British Charge d'Affaires Office in China, were at the airport to welcome them.

### 锡兰政府贸易代表团到达北京

1955-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    锡兰政府贸易代表团到达北京
    新华社四日讯　以锡兰贸易、商务和渔业部部长秀莱·科里亚为首的锡兰政府贸易代表团一行九人，在四日下午乘飞机到达北京。对外贸易部代理部长李哲人、副部长雷任民、外交部亚洲司副司长章文晋和英国驻华代办处参赞艾惕思曾到机场欢迎。
