Title: Czechoslovak President Zápotocký's Condition Continues to Improve
Author:
Date: 1955-09-03
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 2nd: The Czechoslovak News Agency released a bulletin regarding the health of Czechoslovak Republic President Antonín Zápotocký at 9:00 AM Prague time on September 1st.

    The bulletin stated: Republic President Antonín Zápotocký spent the night peacefully. His overall health condition remains good. The heart's activity continues to improve. His temperature on the morning of the 1st was 37.1 degrees Celsius. Antibiotics are being used for treatment.

### 捷总统萨波托斯基病情继续改善

1955-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    捷总统萨波托斯基病情继续改善
    新华社布拉格二日电　九月一日布拉格时间上午九时，捷克斯洛伐克通讯社发表了关于捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统安·萨波托斯基同志健康情况的公报。
    公报说：共和国总统安托宁·萨波托斯基平静度过了一夜。总的健康情况仍然良好。心脏跳动情况继续改善。一日上午体温三十七度一。已经采用抗生素来治疗。
