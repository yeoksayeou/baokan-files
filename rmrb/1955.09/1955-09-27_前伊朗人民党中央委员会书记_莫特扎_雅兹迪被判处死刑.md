Title: Former Secretary of the Central Committee of the Iranian People's Party, Morteza Yazdi, Sentenced to Death
Author:
Date: 1955-09-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, September 26th, TASS: According to a report from the Agence France-Presse correspondent in Tehran, Morteza Yazdi, the former Secretary of the Central Committee of the Iranian People's Party, has been sentenced to death.

### 前伊朗人民党中央委员会书记  莫特扎·雅兹迪被判处死刑

1955-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    前伊朗人民党中央委员会书记
    莫特扎·雅兹迪被判处死刑
    新华社二十六日讯　塔斯社巴黎二十六日讯：据法新社记者从德黑兰报道，前伊朗人民党中央委员会书记莫特扎·雅兹迪已被判处死刑。
