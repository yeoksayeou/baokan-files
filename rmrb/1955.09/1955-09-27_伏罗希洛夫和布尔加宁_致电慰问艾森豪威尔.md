Title: Voroshilov and Bulganin Send Telegrams of Condolence to Eisenhower
Author:
Date: 1955-09-27
Page: 4
　
    Voroshilov and Bulganin
    Send Telegrams of Condolence to Eisenhower
    Xinhua News Agency, Denver, 26th: The U.S. President's Summer White House issued a statement on the 24th, announcing that U.S. President Eisenhower suffered a heart attack in Denver on the 24th, with symptoms of coronary thrombosis.
    According to the diagnostic bulletin issued on the 25th, Eisenhower's condition is currently good and there are no complications.
    Eisenhower is currently being treated at Fitzsimons Army Hospital.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 26th: TASS reported from Moscow on the 25th: The Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Voroshilov, and the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Bulganin, sent telegrams of condolence to the ailing U.S. President Eisenhower.
    Voroshilov's telegram said: "I was very disturbed to hear the news of your illness. I sincerely wish you a speedy recovery."
    Bulganin's telegram said: "I was deeply concerned to hear the news of your illness. I wholeheartedly wish you a speedy recovery."

### 伏罗希洛夫和布尔加宁  致电慰问艾森豪威尔

1955-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫和布尔加宁
    致电慰问艾森豪威尔
    新华社二十六日讯　丹佛消息：美国总统夏季白宫在二十四日发表声明，宣布美国总统艾森豪威尔二十四日在丹佛患心脏病，症状是心脏冠状血管拴塞症。
    据二十五日发表的诊断公报说，艾森豪威尔目前的情况良好，没有并发症。
    艾森豪威尔现在正在费兹西蒙斯陆军医院治疗中。
    新华社二十六日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科二十五日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫和苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁打电报慰问卧病的美国总统艾森豪威尔。
    伏罗希洛夫的电报说：“我听到你生病的消息，非常不安。诚恳地祝你早日恢复健康。”
    布尔加宁的电报说：“我听到你生病的消息，深感不安。衷心地祝你早日恢复健康。”
