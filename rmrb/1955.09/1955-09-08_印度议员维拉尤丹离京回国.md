Title: Indian Member of Parliament Villayudan Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1955-09-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Indian Member of Parliament of the People's House, Ra. Villayudan, who was invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit China, left Beijing by plane on the 6th to return home via Guangzhou.

During his two weeks in China, Villayudan visited Beijing and Northeast China and paid a visit to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, where he was received by Vice Chairman Peng Zhen.

Villayudan issued a written statement when he left Beijing. He said that he was amazed by the way the Chinese people had improved their lives in such a short period of time. The construction of China is for the welfare of the entire people, and its progress is unprecedented. He has no doubt that, with the comardely cooperation of the Soviet Union and other socialist countries, and under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong and his talented colleagues, China will become a bastion of democracy and peace.

### 印度议员维拉尤丹离京回国

1955-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    印度议员维拉尤丹离京回国
    新华社七日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来中国访问的印度国会人民院议员拉·维拉尤丹，已在六日乘飞机离开北京，取道广州返国。
    在中国的两周期间，维拉尤丹曾参观和访问了北京和东北等地，并拜访了全国人民代表大会常务委员会，由副委员长彭真予以接见。
    维拉尤丹在离开北京的时候发表了书面谈话。他说，他为中国人民在那么短的时间内改善了生活深感惊异，中国的建设是为了整个人民的福利，其进展是史无伦比的。他毫不怀疑，在苏联和其他社会主义国家同志般的合作下，在毛泽东主席和他有才能的同僚们的领导下，中国将成为民主和和平的堡垒。
