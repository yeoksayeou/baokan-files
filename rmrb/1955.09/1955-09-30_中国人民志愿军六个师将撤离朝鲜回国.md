Title: Six Divisions of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army to Withdraw from Korea and Return to China
Author:
Date: 1955-09-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 29th. A spokesman for the Headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army announced: Six divisions of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army will withdraw from Korea and return to the motherland in October 1955.

### 中国人民志愿军六个师将撤离朝鲜回国

1955-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民志愿军六个师将撤离朝鲜回国
    新华社平壤二十九日电　中国人民志愿军总部发言人宣布：中国人民志愿军的六个师将于一九五五年的十月间撤离朝鲜返回祖国。
