Title: British Goodwill Delegation to China Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1955-09-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 13th—The British Goodwill Delegation to China left Beijing on the morning of September 13th to return home. Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Chen Kangbai, Secretary-General of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Cao Mengjun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and Wang Zizhong, a religious figure, went to the airport to see them off.

### 英国访华友好代表团回国

1955-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

    英国访华友好代表团回国
    新华社十三日讯　英国访华友好代表团在九月十三日晨离京回国。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一，中国科学院秘书长陈康白，中华全国民主妇女联合会书记处书记曹孟君和宗教界人士王梓仲等曾到机场欢送。
