Title: Fourth Congress of the World Federation of Scientific Workers Closes; Adopts Resolutions on Preparing for the Convening of an International Scientific Conference and International Scientific Cooperation, etc.
Author:
Date: 1955-09-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 26th. The Fourth Congress of the World Federation of Scientific Workers closed on the evening of the 25th. The meeting passed a resolution deciding to prepare for the convening of an international scientific conference to discuss cooperation among scientists from various countries and the opposition to the use of atomic weapons.

The meeting also passed a resolution on international scientific cooperation. The resolution stated that the congress warmly welcomes the relaxation of relations between major powers after the Geneva Conference of the heads of government of the four countries and the resulting greatly expanded possibilities for international scientific cooperation. The congress is deeply convinced that the widespread development of international cooperation, such as the International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy, is of great significance for maintaining peace, strengthening and developing the scientific traditions of different countries, and promoting the welfare of people all over the world. The association is deeply concerned with promoting this international cooperation, and it will do everything possible to seek different specific forms to realize this cooperation, and to promote the elimination of all differences that may hinder the free exchange between scientists from different countries. The congress urged the older generation of scientists to use their experience and guidance to help and support young scientists, and instructed the executive committee of the association to take all possible steps to promote understanding and friendship between young scientists from all countries.

The meeting unanimously re-elected Joliot-Curie as Chairman of the Association, and Professors Bernal (UK), Powell (UK), Li Siguang (China), Oparin (USSR) and Pauling (USA) as Vice-Chairmen of the Association.

### 世界科学工作者协会第四届代表大会闭幕  通过关于筹备召开国际科学会议和国际科学合作等决议

1955-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

    世界科学工作者协会第四届代表大会闭幕
    通过关于筹备召开国际科学会议和国际科学合作等决议
    新华社柏林二十六日电　世界科学工作者协会第四届代表大会二十五日晚闭幕。会议通过决议，决定筹备召开国际科学会议来讨论世界各国科学家间的合作和反对使用原子武器问题。
    会议并且通过关于国际科学合作的决议。决议说，大会对四国政府首脑日内瓦会议后大国之间关系的缓和以及由此产生的大大扩展国际科学合作的可能性表示热烈欢迎。大会深信，广泛开展像和平利用原子能国际会议那样的国际合作对于维护和平、加强和发展不同国家的科学传统以及促进世界各国人民的福利，都有着重大的意义。协会深切关怀促进这种国际合作，它将尽一切办法来寻求实现这种合作的不同的具体形式，促使消除可能妨碍不同国家的科学家之间的自由交流的所有分歧。大会敦促年老一代的科学家以他们的经验和指导来帮助和支持青年科学家，并且责成协会执行委员会采取一切可能的步骤来促进世界各国青年科学家之间的了解和友谊。
    会议一致选举约里奥—居里连任协会主席，贝尔纳教授（英国）、鲍威尔教授（英国）、李四光教授（中国）、奥巴林教授（苏联）和保林教授（美国）为协会副主席。
