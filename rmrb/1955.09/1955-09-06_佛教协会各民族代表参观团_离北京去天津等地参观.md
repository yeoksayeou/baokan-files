Title: Buddhist Association Delegation of Various Ethnic Groups Leaves Beijing to Visit Tianjin and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1955-09-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 4th. The Chinese Buddhist Association's delegation of representatives from various ethnic groups, consisting of twenty-two members, led by the head of the delegation, Huba Meng, and the deputy heads, Huo Ri Cang Cang and Gamma Yangsheng, left Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 4th to visit Tianjin, the Northeast, and other places. The members of the delegation are all directors and representatives who attended the Second (Enlarged) Meeting of the Council of the Chinese Buddhist Association, including Tibetan, Mongolian, Dai, and Yugur ethnic groups.

### 佛教协会各民族代表参观团  离北京去天津等地参观

1955-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    佛教协会各民族代表参观团
    离北京去天津等地参观
    新华社四日讯　中国佛教协会各民族代表参观团一行二十二人，由团长祜巴勐、副团长火日藏仓、噶玛养生率领，四日下午乘火车离北京前去天津、东北等地参观。参观团人员都是出席中国佛教协会理事会第二次（扩大）会议的理事和代表，包括藏、蒙、傣、裕固等四个民族成份。
