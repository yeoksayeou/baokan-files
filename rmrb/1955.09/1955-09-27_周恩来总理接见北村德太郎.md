Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Kitamura Tokutaro
Author:
Date: 1955-09-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: Premier Zhou Enlai received Kitamura Tokutaro, head of the first delegation of Japanese Diet members visiting the Soviet Union, on the afternoon of the 26th. Present at the meeting were Zhou Gengsheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and council members Liao Chengzhi, Chen Jiakang, and Zhao Anbo.

According to Xinhua News Agency, a group of thirteen members of the Japanese Diet members' delegation visiting the Soviet Union, headed by Kitamura Tokutaro, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 24th after concluding their visit to the Soviet Union. (Attached picture)

### 周恩来总理接见北村德太郎

1955-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见北村德太郎
    新华社二十六日讯　周恩来总理在二十六日下午接见了日本国会议员访苏团第一团长北村德太郎。接见时在座的有中国人民外交学会副会长周鲠生，理事廖承志、陈家康、赵安博。
    据新华社讯　以第一团长北村德太郎为首的日本国会议员访苏团部分人员一行十三人，结束了在苏联的访问后，二十四日下午乘飞机从莫斯科到达北京。（附图片）
