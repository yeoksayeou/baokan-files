Title: British Foreign Office Welcomes Sino-U.S. Agreement on Civilian Repatriation

Author:

Date: 1955-09-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th dispatch from London: According to a report from Agence France-Presse in London: A British Foreign Office spokesman said on the 10th that the British government was "pleased" to learn that China and the United States had reached an agreement on the repatriation of civilians by both sides.

The spokesman said that the British government is happy to be able to assist in the implementation of this agreement.

### 英外交部欢迎中美就平民回国问题达成协议

1955-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    英外交部欢迎中美就平民回国问题达成协议
    新华社十一日讯　据法新社伦敦讯：英国外交部发言人在十日说，英国政府获悉中美两国已就双方平民回国问题达成协议的消息感到“愉快”。
    发言人说，英国政府乐于能够协助执行这个协议。
