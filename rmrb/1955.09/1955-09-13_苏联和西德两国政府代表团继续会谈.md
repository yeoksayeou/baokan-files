Title: Soviet and West German Government Delegations Continue Talks
Author:
Date: 1955-09-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 12th - TASS, Moscow, September 12th: Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov and Foreign Minister von Brentano of the Federal Republic of Germany held talks at the Soviet Foreign Ministry at 10:00 AM on September 12th.

In accordance with the decision adopted during the negotiations between the government delegations of the Soviet Union and the Federal Republic of Germany on September 10th, the foreign ministers discussed the issue of submitting concrete proposals to the heads of both delegations, taking into account the opinions previously exchanged by both sides.

The foreign ministers decided to report the results of this meeting at the meeting of the two governments' delegations scheduled for 4:00 PM Moscow time on the 12th.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow - TASS, Moscow: The government delegations of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and the Federal Republic of Germany held their third meeting at 4:00 PM Moscow time on September 12th.

### 苏联和西德两国政府代表团继续会谈

1955-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和西德两国政府代表团继续会谈
    新华社十二日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科十二日讯：苏联外交部长莫洛托夫和德意志联邦共和国外交部长勃伦塔诺十二日十时在苏联外交部举行了会谈。
    按照苏联和德意志联邦共和国两国政府代表团九月十日谈判中所通过的决定，外长们讨论了关于考虑以前双方所交换的意见而向双方代表团团长提出具体建议的问题。
    外长们决定在十二日莫斯科时间十六时举行的两国政府代表团会议上报告这次会谈的结果。
    新华社十二日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：九月十二日莫斯科时间十六时，苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟和德意志联邦共和国两国政府代表团举行了第三次会议。
