Title: The Advancing Mechanical Industry of Liaoning Province (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1955-09-09
Page: 2

The mechanical industry of Liaoning Province is Advancing
Liaoning Province is one of the key construction areas during China's First Five-Year Plan. Over the past two years, with the rapid development of various sectors of the national economy, the province's mechanical industry has been advancing by leaps and bounds. Newly built, expanded, and existing machinery manufacturing plants in Liaoning Province have produced many new types of machinery and equipment to meet the needs of the Anshan Iron and Steel Complex and key projects in various regions. It has also cultivated a large number of technical talents and accumulated substantial funds for the state. During the First Five-Year Plan, the mechanical industry of Liaoning Province is undertaking arduous and glorious tasks.

    The 25-ton tower crane trial-produced by the Shenyang Bridge Factory has been largely installed. This type of crane has a complex structure and is being trial-produced for the first time in China. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Shaoqing photo

    The Shenyang Pneumatic Tool Factory manufactures various pneumatic tools for use in mining and the construction of factories and bridges. This is Ju Fuhai (right), leader of the First Youth Group in the machine assembly workshop, and Li Chuntian (left), who has not had an accident in three years, discussing ways to improve their work. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Shaoqing photo

    The Shenyang Transformer Factory has successfully trial-produced a 121,000-volt, 31,500-kilovolt-ampere large transformer. This is one of the components of this large transformer.
                      Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Shaoqing photo

    The excavators produced by the Fushun Heavy Machinery Plant are supplied to mines and basic construction sites. These excavators can lift, excavate soil, and mine coal. In one working day, it can excavate 1,000 to 1,500 cubic meters of soil, equivalent to the labor of 700 to 800 people in one day. This is the workers installing the first batch of excavators.
                      Xinhua News Agency reporter Jia Chengbin photo

    Acceptance personnel from the Jinzhou Railway Administration are inspecting the new locomotives they ordered at the Dalian Locomotive and Rolling Stock Manufacturing Plant.
                      Xinhua News Agency reporter Jia Chengbin photo

    The reconstruction project of the Shenyang Wire Factory has entered the installation phase. The eight-mold wire drawing machine in the bare wire workshop has been installed and is undergoing no-load test runs. This is a Soviet expert guiding Chinese workers in adjusting the machine. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yu Zhao photo

### 前进中的辽宁省机械工业（图片）

1955-09-09
第2版()
专栏：

    前进中的辽宁省机械工业
    辽宁省是我国第一个五年计划期间的重点建设地区之一。两年多来，随着国民经济各部门的飞跃发展，全省的机械工业正在大步前进。辽宁省新建、扩建和原有的机械制造厂，已生产了许多新型的机器设备，供应鞍山钢铁联合企业和各地重点工程的需要。还培养了不少技术人材和给国家积累了大量的资金。在第一个五年计划期间，辽宁省的机械工业担负着艰巨而光荣的任务。
    沈阳桥梁厂试制的二十五吨塔式起重机，已大体上安装完毕。这种起重机的结构复杂，在我国是第一次试制。新华社记者王少清摄
    沈阳风动工具厂制造各种风动工具，供给开凿矿山和修建厂房、桥梁等使用。这是机装车间第一青年小组组长鞠复海（右）和三年未出事故的李春田（左）正在研究改进工作的办法。新华社记者王少清摄
    沈阳变压器厂又试制成功了一种十二万一千伏特、三万一千五百千伏安的大型变压器。这是这种大型变压器的组成部分之一。
                      新华社记者王少清摄
    抚顺重型机器厂生产的挖掘机，是供给矿山和基本建设工地使用的。这种挖掘机，可以起重、挖土、挖煤。一个工作日能挖土一千到一千五百立方公尺，相当于七、八百人的一天劳动量。这是工人安装第一批挖掘机。
                      新华社记者贾承滨摄
    锦州铁路管理局的验收人员正在大连机车车辆制造厂里验收他们订购的新机车。
                      新华社记者贾承滨摄
    沈阳电线厂改建工程已进入安装阶段。裸线车间的八模拉线机已安装好了，正在空车试运转。这是苏联专家在指导我国工人调整机器。新华社记者于肇摄
