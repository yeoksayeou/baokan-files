Title: Italian Women's Delegation to the Northeast
Author:
Date: 1955-09-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 4th: The Italian women's delegation, currently visiting China, left Beijing for Northeast China on the evening of the 4th to tour the region.

On the afternoon of the 4th, the Italian women's delegation attended a report meeting at the invitation of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation. Personnel from organizations such as the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation attended the meeting.

Mrs. Rossi, the head of the delegation, and members Giuliana Nanni, Luciana Viveani, and Caterina Piccolato, respectively introduced the situation of the Italian women and female workers' movement at the meeting. These reports were warmly welcomed by the audience and received continuous applause. At the report meeting, Mrs. Rossi personally presented a letter from the young women of the Italian village of Capi and a brocade banner sewn by them to Chinese friends, expressing their friendship with the Chinese young women. The delegation members also brought gifts from the Italian Women's League and Italian female workers to their Chinese friends.

During their stay in Beijing, the delegation also visited the Baipenyao Agricultural Production Cooperative in the suburbs, the Guanting Reservoir, the Central Institute for Nationalities, the New and Old Children's Hospitals, and toured the Great Wall and the Forbidden City.

### 意大利妇女代表团去东北

1955-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    意大利妇女代表团去东北
    据新华社四日讯　在我国访问的意大利妇女代表团四日晚离北京到东北参观。
    四日下午，意大利妇女代表团人员应中华全国民主妇女联合会邀请出席了一次报告会。参加报告会的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中华全国总工会、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、北京市民主妇女联合会等单位的人员。
    代表团团长罗西夫人和团员朱利安娜·南尼、卢契安娜·维韦安尼、卡特琳娜·皮科拉托在会上分别介绍了意大利妇女、女工运动的情况。这些报告受到了听众热烈欢迎和不断的鼓掌。在报告会上，罗西夫人把意大利卡皮村女青年给中国女青年的一封信和她们亲手缝制的锦旗亲自送给中国朋友，以表达她们对中国女青年的友谊。代表团团员们又带来了意大利妇女联盟、意大利女工给中国朋友的礼品。
    代表团在京期间，还参观了郊区白盆窑农业生产合作社、官厅水库、中央民族学院、新旧儿童医院，并游览了长城和故宫。
