Title: Japanese Cooperative Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1955-09-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 4th. The Japanese Cooperative Delegation, led by Head of Delegation Takeshige Ishiguro and Deputy Head of Delegation Oumeo, who were invited by the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives to visit China, left Guangzhou this morning, September 4th, to return to their country.

### 日本合作社代表团回国

1955-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    日本合作社代表团回国
    新华社广州四日电　应中华全国供销合作总社邀请来中国访问的日本合作社代表团，由团长石黑武重、副团长奥梅尾率领，在四日早晨离广州返国。
