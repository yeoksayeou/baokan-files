Title: Head of Chinese Delegation to Damascus Fair Holds Reception; Syrian Prime Minister and Foreign Minister, and Over a Thousand Others Attend
Author:
Date: 1955-09-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 16th dispatch: Jiang Ming, head of the Chinese exhibition delegation participating in the Syrian Damascus International Fair and Vice Minister of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Trade, held a reception at the Chinese Pavilion in the fair on the evening of the 15th.

Those invited to attend included Syrian Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Said al-Ghazzi, Speaker of Parliament Nazim al-Qudsi, Interior Minister Ali Buzo, Minister of Economy Rizkallah Antaki, Minister of Education Maamoun Kuzbari and other high-ranking officials, diplomatic envoys and commercial counselors from various countries stationed in Syria, heads of delegations from various countries participating in the fair, and over a thousand prominent figures from all walks of life in Syria.

Jiang Ming introduced to the guests China's economic construction achievements since liberation and the development of China's trade with other countries. He expressed his belief that economic and cultural cooperation and friendly relations between China and Syria and other countries will continue to develop.

The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 我国参加大马士革博览会团长举行招待会  叙利亚总理兼外交部长等一千多人出席

1955-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

    我国参加大马士革博览会团长举行招待会
    叙利亚总理兼外交部长等一千多人出席
    新华社十六日讯　大马士革消息：中国参加叙利亚大马士革国际博览会展览团团长、中国对外贸易部副部长江明，十五日晚在博览会中的中国馆举行招待会。
    应邀出席的有叙利亚总理兼外交部长赛义德·加齐、议会议长奈绥姆·库德西、内政部长阿里·波佐、经济部长里兹开拉·安塔基、教育部长马茂恩·库兹巴里和其他高级官员，各国驻叙利亚外交使节和大使馆商务参赞，各国参加博览会的代表团团长，以及叙利亚各界著名人士等一千多人。
    江明向客人们介绍了解放以来中国的经济建设成就和中国同其他国家的贸易的发展情况。他表示相信，中国同叙利亚和其他国家的经济文化合作和友好关系将继续发展。
    招待会在友好的空气中进行。
