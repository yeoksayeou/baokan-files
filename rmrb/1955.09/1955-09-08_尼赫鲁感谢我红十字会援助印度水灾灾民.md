Title: Nehru Thanks Chinese Red Cross for Assisting Indian Flood Victims
Author:
Date: 1955-09-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 7th. Indian Prime Minister Nehru wrote to Yuan Zhongxian, the Chinese Ambassador to India, on September 5th, expressing his gratitude to the Chinese Red Cross for its assistance to the Indian flood victims.
    Nehru wrote in his letter: Dear Ambassador, I am grateful to you for forwarding a letter from the President of the Chinese Red Cross Society and a check for 191,570 rupees attached to the letter as relief funds for the flood-stricken areas of India. I would be grateful if you would convey my thanks to Ms. Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross Society on my behalf.

### 尼赫鲁感谢我红十字会援助印度水灾灾民

1955-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁感谢我红十字会援助印度水灾灾民
    新华社新德里七日电　印度总理尼赫鲁在九月五日写信给中国驻印度大使袁仲贤，表示感谢中国红十字会对印度水灾灾民的援助。
    尼赫鲁在信中写道：亲爱的大使，蒙你转交给我中国红十字会会长的一封信和信内所附的一张作为印度水灾地区救济金的十九万一千五百七十卢比的支票。为此我向你表示谢意。如果你愿意代我向中国红十字会会长李德全女士转致谢意的话，我将不胜感激。
