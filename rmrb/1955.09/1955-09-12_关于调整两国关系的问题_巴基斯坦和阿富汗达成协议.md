Title: Agreement Reached Between Pakistan and Afghanistan on Adjusting Bilateral Relations
Author:
Date: 1955-09-12
Page: 4

　　Concerning the Adjustment of Relations Between the Two Countries
　　Pakistan and Afghanistan Reach Agreement
　　Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, September 10th (Tass dispatch): Pakistani newspapers published a government communique stating that, following negotiations between the Pakistani ambassador to Afghanistan and the Afghan Foreign Minister, the governments of Pakistan and Afghanistan have reached an agreement on adjusting relations between the two countries. According to Agence France-Presse reports cited by the newspapers, the two countries will soon restore normal diplomatic and trade relations.

### 关于调整两国关系的问题  巴基斯坦和阿富汗达成协议

1955-09-12
第4版()
专栏：

    关于调整两国关系的问题
    巴基斯坦和阿富汗达成协议
    新华社十一日讯　塔斯社喀喇蚩十日讯：巴基斯坦各报刊载的政府公报说，在巴基斯坦驻阿富汗大使和阿富汗外交大臣举行谈判之后，巴基斯坦和阿富汗两国政府达成了关于调整两国关系的协议。各报根据法新社的消息指出，两国很快就要恢复正常的外交关系和贸易关系。
