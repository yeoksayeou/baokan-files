Title: West German Parliament Approves Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between West Germany and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-09-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bonn, September 24th (TASS dispatch): The Federal Council of the Federal Republic of Germany unanimously approved on the 23rd the proposal of the government of the Federal Republic of Germany regarding establishing diplomatic relations between the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and the Federal Republic of Germany.

1955-09-25

专栏：

    西德议院批准西德同苏联建立外交关系
