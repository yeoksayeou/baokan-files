Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram to the Congress of the Communist Party of Uruguay
Author:
Date: 1955-09-23
Page: 1

    The CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram to the Congress of the Communist Party of Uruguay
    (Our newspaper dispatch) The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram on September 20 to the Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Uruguay, congratulating them on the 16th Congress of the Communist Party of Uruguay, scheduled to be held in Montevideo on September 23. The telegram reads as follows: To the Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Uruguay and to all comrades of the 16th Congress of the Communist Party of Uruguay:
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm congratulations to the 16th Congress of the Communist Party of Uruguay!
    We sincerely wish the Communist Party of Uruguay new achievements in leading the working class of Uruguay, strengthening the worker-peasant alliance, uniting all patriotic and democratic forces, and struggling to defend the vital interests of the working people and to uphold world peace. And we wish the Communist Party of Uruguay increasingly consolidated and developed. We wish the Congress success!
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
        September 20, 1955

### 中共中央电贺乌拉圭共产党代表大会

1955-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央电贺乌拉圭共产党代表大会
    本报讯　中国共产党中央委员会九月二十日打电报给乌拉圭共产党执行委员会，祝贺定于九月二十三日在蒙得维的亚召开的乌拉圭共产党第十六次代表大会。电文如下：乌拉圭共产党执行委员会并转乌拉圭共产党第十六次代表大会全体同志们：
    中国共产党中央委员会谨向乌拉圭共产党第十六次代表大会致热烈的祝贺！
    我们谨祝乌拉圭共产党在领导乌拉圭工人阶级，加强工农联盟，团结一切爱国民主力量，为保卫劳动人民的切身利益和拥护世界和平的斗争中获得新的成就。并祝乌拉圭共产党日益巩固与发展。祝贺代表大会的成功！中国共产党中央委员会
        一九五五年九月二十日
