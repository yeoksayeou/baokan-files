Title: Marshal Peng Dehuai Hosts Banquet for Burmese Military Friendship Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-09-29
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency

    Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 28th to welcome the Burmese Military Friendship Delegation.
    The Ambassador of the Union of Burma to China, Wu La Mao, and Military Attaché Lieutenant Colonel Mao Mao, as well as the military attachés, acting military attachés, and deputy military attachés of India, Czechoslovakia, the Soviet Union, Poland, and Indonesia were also invited to attend the banquet.
    At the banquet, Marshal Peng Dehuai and Lieutenant General Ne Win, head of the Burmese Military Friendship Delegation, delivered speeches successively.
    Present at the banquet were Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council, Marshal He Long, Marshal Nie Rongzhen, Cheng Qian, Zhang Zhizhong, Long Yun, Deputy Ministers of National Defense General Huang Kecheng, General Tan Zheng, Admiral Xiao Jingguang, General Wang Shusheng, General Li Da, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Vice Mayor of Beijing Feng Jiping, and Deputy Military Attaché of China to Burma Xu Yimin.
    More than sixty senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army attended the banquet. Among them were General Su Yu, General Chen Geng, General Xu Guangda, General Zhang Zongxun, General Li Kenong, General Zhang Aiping, General Xiao Hua, General Gan Siqi, as well as generals from various departments, services, and arms.
    The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.
    After the banquet, the guests watched song and dance performances given by the Cultural Troupe of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army together.

### 彭德怀元帅欢宴缅甸军事友好代表团

1955-09-29
第2版()
专栏：

    彭德怀元帅欢宴缅甸军事友好代表团
    中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀元帅在二十八日晚举行宴会，欢迎缅甸军事友好代表团。
    缅甸联邦驻华大使吴拉茂和驻华武官茂茂中校，印度、捷克斯洛伐克、苏联、波兰、印度尼西亚等国驻华武官、代理武官和副武官也应邀出席了宴会。
    在宴会上，彭德怀元帅和缅甸军事友好代表团团长奈温中将先后讲了话。
    出席宴会的，有国防委员会副主席贺龙元帅、聂荣臻元帅、程潜、张治中、龙云，国防部副部长黄克诚大将、谭政大将、萧劲光海军大将、王树声大将、李达上将，外交部副部长章汉夫，北京市副市长冯基平，中国驻缅甸副武官徐毅民。
    中国人民解放军高级将领六十多人出席了宴会。他们之中有粟裕大将、陈赓大将、许光达大将、张宗逊上将、李克农上将、张爱萍上将、萧华上将、甘泗淇上将，以及各部、各军种、兵种的将官们。
    宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
    宴会结束后，宾主们一起观看了中国人民解放军总政治部文艺工作团演出的歌舞节目。
                                  （新华社）
