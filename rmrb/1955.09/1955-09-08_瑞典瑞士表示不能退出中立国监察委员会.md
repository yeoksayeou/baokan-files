Title: Sweden, Switzerland Say They Cannot Withdraw from Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission
Author:
Date: 1955-09-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 7th: Seoul dispatch: Syngman Rhee's ambassador to the United States, Liang Yuchan (梁祐灿), said on the 6th upon returning to Seoul that Swedish and Swiss diplomats in Washington had informed him that their governments could not withdraw their representatives from the Korean Armistice Supervisory Commission.

According to Liang Yuchan, he had attempted to "persuade" those Swedish and Swiss diplomats to request the withdrawal of their representatives.

### 瑞典瑞士表示不能退出中立国监察委员会

1955-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典瑞士表示不能退出中立国监察委员会
    新华社平壤七日电　汉城消息：李承晚驻美国大使梁祐灿六日在回到汉城时说，瑞典和瑞士的驻华盛顿外交人员已经告诉他说，他们的政府不能够把它们的代表从朝鲜停战视察小组撤出来。
    据梁祐灿说，他曾设法要“说服”那些瑞典和瑞士的外交人员去要求撤退他们的代表。
