Title: Japan-China Friendship Association Fifth National Convention Opens in Tokyo
Author:
Date: 1955-09-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, September 4th: The Japan-China Friendship Association's Fifth National Convention opened in Tokyo on September 3rd under the slogan of "All People Nationwide Jointly Promote Japan-China Friendship Relations." The convention will last for three days.

Approximately 500 delegates and guests from all over the country are participating in the convention.

The convention began with the chorus of the song "Tokyo-Beijing." Matsumoto Jiichiro, President of the Japan-China Friendship Association, delivered the opening address. He emphasized that exchanges between the people of Japan and China are increasing year by year.

At the first day's meeting, the future work policy of the Japan-China Friendship Association was put forward; representatives of the local organizations of the Japan-China Friendship Association made reports at the meeting. The representatives of the Japanese six major city delegation, which will soon visit China, were also introduced at the meeting.

Group meetings were held on the second day (September 4th). The discussions focused on Japan-China cultural exchange, economic exchanges, restoration of diplomatic relations, repatriation of overseas Chinese, improvement of the treatment of Chinese residing in Japan, and issues related to the association's own finances and organization.

### 日中友协第五届全国大会在东京开幕

1955-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    日中友协第五届全国大会在东京开幕
    新华社四日讯　东京消息：日中友好协会第五届全国大会在“全国人民共同增进日中友好关系”的口号下，九月三日在东京开幕。大会将举行三天。
    参加大会的有来自全国各地的代表和来宾共约五百人。
    大会在合唱“东京—北京”的歌声中开始。日中友好协会会长松本治一郎致开幕辞。他着重指出，日中两国人民间的往来与年俱增。
    在第一天的大会上提出了日中友好协会今后的工作方针；日中友好协会各地方组织的代表在会上作了报告。会上还介绍了不久将到中国访问的日本六大都市代表团的代表。
    第二天（四日）举行分组会。讨论日中文化交流、经济来往、恢复邦交、遣返侨民、改善旅日华侨的待遇等问题以及有关协会本身的财务和组织方面的问题。
