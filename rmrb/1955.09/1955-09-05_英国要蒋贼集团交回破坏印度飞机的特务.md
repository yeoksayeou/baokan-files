Title: Britain Demands That the Chiang Kai-shek Clique Hand Over Special Agents Who Sabotaged Indian Plane
Author:
Date: 1955-09-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 4th. According to an Associated Press report, the British Consul in Tamsui, Herman, demanded on the 3rd that Yen Chia-kan, "Chairman" of the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique in Taiwan Province, hand over Chou Tzu-ming, the Chiang Kai-shek special agent who sabotaged the "Kashmir Princess" aircraft and who is wanted by the Hong Kong government, to the Hong Kong authorities for trial.

### 英国要蒋贼集团交回破坏印度飞机的特务

1955-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    英国要蒋贼集团交回破坏印度飞机的特务
    新华社四日讯　据美联社报道，英国驻淡水领事赫尔曼在三日要求蒋介石卖国集团台湾省“主席”严家淦把香港政府宣布缉拿的破坏“克什米尔公主号”飞机的蒋贼特务周梓铭交回香港当局受审。
