    Our Great Motherland
    Sichuan Province plans to expand from the existing 28,000+ agricultural production cooperatives to 100,000 between now and the spring plowing next year. Farmers in Xinnong Township, Xinfan County, are bringing their land deeds and registering for cooperative membership in droves.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    四川省计划从现在到明年春耕前，在现有的二万八千多个农业生产合作社的基础上发展到十万个。新繁县新农乡农民带着土地证成群结队地报名入社。
