Title: Burmese Union Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th. The 70-member Burmese Union Cultural Delegation, invited to visit China, arrived in Beijing by special plane on the afternoon of the 24th.

The delegation, headed by U Win, Minister of Culture of the Burmese Union, was warmly welcomed at the airport by more than 3,000 people from all walks of life in the capital. Among those welcoming them were Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Culture, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, and head of the former Chinese Cultural Delegation to Burma; Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Wang Kunlun, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations; representatives of various Chinese literary and artistic groups; as well as Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, and embassy staff.

As the distinguished guests stepped off the plane, artists from the former Chinese Cultural Delegation to Burma in the welcoming line happily greeted friends they had met during their visit to Burma. They shook hands warmly and congratulated each other on their reunion in Beijing. In the airport lounge, members of the delegation and the welcoming party learned to say words like peace, friendship, and bouquet in Burmese and Chinese.

A grand welcoming ceremony was held at the airport. The national flags of China and Burma fluttered over the venue, and two huge banners were hung, with the words "Welcome the Burmese Union Cultural Delegation" and "Long Live Sino-Burmese Friendship" written in both Chinese and Burmese. At the meeting, Zheng Zhenduo delivered a welcome speech, and U Win delivered a reply, warmly congratulating the further development and consolidation of the friendship between the Chinese and Burmese people and the cause of cultural exchange.

As the delegation left the airport, they were greeted with cheers from the welcoming crowd, and sustained applause echoed throughout the airport.

The Burmese Union Cultural Delegation, composed of many famous Burmese artists, will perform wonderful Burmese classical and folk music, dance and other programs for the Chinese audience for the first time. (Attached picture)

### 缅甸联邦文化代表团到北京

1955-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸联邦文化代表团到北京
    新华社二十四日讯　应邀来中国访问的缅甸联邦文化代表团七十人，在二十四日下午乘专机到达北京。
    以缅甸联邦文化部部长吴温为首的代表团人员，在机场受到了首都各界三千多人的热烈欢迎。欢迎的人中有文化部副部长、中缅友好协会会长、前中国访问缅甸文化代表团团长郑振铎，文化部副部长夏衍，外交部副部长章汉夫，北京市副市长王昆仑，对外文化联络局代局长陈忠经和中国各文艺团体的代表，以及缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂和使馆人员等。
    当贵宾们走下飞机后，在欢迎行列中的前中国访问缅甸文化代表团的艺术家们高兴地招呼他们在访问缅甸时所认识的朋友们。他们热烈地握手和互相祝贺在北京的重逢。在机场上的休息室里，代表团团员们和欢迎的人们互相学习用缅甸话和中国话说着：和平、友谊、花束等词句。
    在机场上举行了盛大的欢迎会。会场上飘扬着中缅两国的国旗和悬挂着两幅巨大的标语，标语上用中、缅两国文字写着“欢迎缅甸联邦文化代表团”、“中缅友好万岁”。在会上由郑振铎致欢迎词，吴温致答词，他们热烈地祝贺中缅两国人民的友谊和文化交流事业的进一步发展和巩固。
    代表团人员在离开机场时受到了欢迎人群的夹道欢呼，机场上响起了经久不息的掌声。
    由许多著名的缅甸艺术家组成的缅甸联邦文化代表团将第一次为中国的观众演出精采的缅甸古典和民间的音乐、舞蹈等节目。（附图片）
