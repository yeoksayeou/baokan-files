Title: Yugoslav "Kolo" Folk Dance Troupe Receives Beijing Audience
Author:
Date: 1955-09-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 2nd. The Beijing audience watched the wonderful performance of the Yugoslav "Kolo" Folk Dance Troupe on the evenings of September 1st and 2nd. Among the more than 8,000 audiences in the past two days were workers, government officials, People's Liberation Army soldiers, literary and art workers, students, and housewives. The Labor Theater where the dance troupe performed was full for these two days. Hundreds of audience members stood around the theater tonight.

The performance of the dance troupe was warmly welcomed by the Beijing audience. More than half of the programs performed on the evening of the 1st were re-performed at the audience's enthusiastic request. On the evening of the 2nd, the audience watched the performance in the rain until the end, and enthusiastically applauded and requested the dance troupe to re-perform many programs.

After each performance, the Beijing audience thanked the Yugoslav artists for their outstanding performances with flowers. After the performance on the evening of the 2nd, the dance troupe members and the audience standing in the rain applauded and waved to each other, and threw flowers at the people.

### 南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团招待北京观众

1955-09-03
第4版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团招待北京观众
    据新华社二日讯　北京的观众在九月一日和二日晚间观看了南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团的精采表演。在两天来共达八千多名的观众中，有工人、机关干部、人民解放军战士、文艺工作者、学生和家庭妇女。歌舞团演出的劳动剧场这两天来都是满座，今晚在剧场周围还站满了数百名观众。
    歌舞团的表演受到北京观众的热烈欢迎。一日晚上表演的节目中，有一半以上在观众热烈要求下重演，二日晚，观众冒雨观看表演直到终场，并且热烈鼓掌要求歌舞团重演了许多节目。
    北京的观众在每一场演出结束后，都以鲜花来感谢南斯拉夫艺术家们的杰出表演。二日晚表演结束后，歌舞团人员和在雨中站立的观众一起鼓掌，招手致意，并且把一朵朵鲜花投向人们。
