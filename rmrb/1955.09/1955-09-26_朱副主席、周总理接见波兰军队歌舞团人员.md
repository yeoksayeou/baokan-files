Title: Vice Chairman Zhu and Premier Zhou Receive Members of the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble
Author:
Date: 1955-09-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: Vice Chairman Zhu De of the People's Republic of China and Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council watched a performance by the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble at Huairentang on the evening of the 25th. Before the performance, they received the leaders and performers of the ensemble.

Those received included: Lieutenant Colonel Z. Wolwa, representative of the General Political Department of the Polish Army and Minister of Culture of the General Political Department of the Polish Army; Colonel T. Ratkowski, head of the song and dance ensemble, and his wife; Colonel S. Ruda, deputy political head; A. Wiernik, music director; A. Kalinowski, dance director; M. Sawacki, choir conductor; A. Skosowski, stage supervisor; E. Onsowicz, representative of the Polish Committee for Cultural Cooperation with Foreign Countries; playwright Jan Warnaycki; and actors A. Swonicka, K. Traczewska, B. Pablocki, and B. Watysz. During the meeting, Lieutenant Colonel Wolwa and Colonel Ratkowski presented gifts to Vice Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai on behalf of the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble.

Also present at the meeting from the Chinese side were He Long, Chen Yi, Su Yu, Xiao Hua, Qian Junrui, Xia Yan, Ji Pengfei, Xiao Xiangrong, and Chen Yi.

The Ambassador of the Polish People's Republic to China, Kirilo, was also present at the reception.

The song and dance performance began at 7:30 PM. The Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble performed a rich and colorful program of songs and dances, which was warmly welcomed by the audience.

Also present at the performance tonight were Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Chen Shutong, Li Fuchun, He Xiangning, Li Kenong, members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, ministers and vice-ministers of various ministries, directors and deputy directors of various committees, and leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations.

After the performance, more than 250 Chinese military and youth literary and art workers presented flowers to the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble. The stage was filled with waving flowers, like a blooming garden. At this time, Premier Zhou Enlai, along with He Long, Xiao Hua, and others, walked onto the stage through the applauding and cheering audience to express congratulations and regards to the members of the song and dance ensemble. Members of the song and dance ensemble presented flowers to Premier Zhou Enlai. The jubilant crowd in the hall cheered "Mao Zedong" and "Berut" in unison, and the enthusiastic applause lasted for more than ten minutes. (Attached picture)

### 朱副主席、周总理接见波兰军队歌舞团人员

1955-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    朱副主席、周总理接见波兰军队歌舞团人员
    新华社二十五日讯　中华人民共和国副主席朱德、国务院总理周恩来，二十五日晚在怀仁堂观看了波兰军队歌舞团的表演，并在演出前接见了歌舞团的领导人和演员。
    被接见的有波兰军队总政治部代表、波兰军队总政治部文化部部长、兹·沃尔瓦中校，歌舞团团长特·拉特科夫斯基上校和他的夫人，政治副团长谢·鲁达上校，音乐指导阿·维尔尼克，舞蹈指导安·卡里诺夫斯基，合唱指挥马·萨瓦茨基，舞台监督阿·斯科索夫斯基，波兰对外文化合作委员会代表埃·翁索维茨，戏剧家扬·瓦尔奈茨基，演员阿·斯沃尼茨卡、克·特拉柴夫斯卡、鲍·帕布洛茨基和波·瓦蒂什。沃尔瓦中校、拉特科夫斯基上校在进见时，代表波兰军队歌舞团向朱德副主席、周恩来总理献赠了礼品。
    接见时，中国方面在座的有贺龙、陈毅、粟裕、萧华、钱俊瑞、夏衍、姬鹏飞、萧向荣、陈沂。
    接见时，波兰人民共和国驻华大使基里洛克也在座。
    歌舞表演在七时三十分开始。波兰军队歌舞团在晚会上演出了丰富多采的歌舞节目，受到了观众的热烈欢迎。
    今晚出席观看表演的，还有李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培、陈叔通、李富春、何香凝、李克农，全国人民代表大会常务委员，各部部长、副部长，各委员会主任、副主任，以及各民主党派和各人民团体负责人。
    演出结束后，中国的部队和青年文艺工作者二百五十多人向波兰军队歌舞团献花，台上鲜花挥动，宛如盛开的花园。这时，周恩来总理同贺龙、萧华等穿过鼓掌欢呼的观众走上舞台，向歌舞团人员表示祝贺和慰问。歌舞团团员向周恩来总理献赠了鲜花。大厅内欢腾的人群齐声欢呼“毛泽东”、“贝鲁特”，热烈鼓掌持续达十多分钟。（附图片）
