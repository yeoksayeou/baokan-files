Title: Soviet-Japanese Negotiations Announced Short-Term Interruption
Author: 
Date: 1955-09-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, September 23rd: TASS London, September 23rd: On September 23rd, the Soviet and Japanese delegations in London released the following joint communique regarding the Soviet-Japanese negotiations:

    “On September 21st, the Soviet and Japanese delegations exchanged views on the procedures for future negotiations. Both sides agreed that the negotiations would be suspended for a short period due to the return of the head of the Japanese delegation, Mr. Shunichi Matsumoto, to Tokyo to consult with his government. Both sides also agreed that negotiations would continue after Mr. Shunichi Matsumoto's return to London. During Mr. Shunichi Matsumoto's absence, if necessary, the negotiations will be conducted by the Soviet Plenipotentiary Representative in this regard, the Soviet Chargé d'Affaires ad interim in the United Kingdom, Belokhvostikov, and the Japanese Plenipotentiary Representative, the Chief Advisor of the Japanese Delegation, Takahashi."

### 苏日谈判宣布短期中断

1955-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

    苏日谈判宣布短期中断
    新华社二十四日讯　塔斯社伦敦二十三日讯：九月二十三日，苏日双方在伦敦公布以下关于苏日谈判的联合公报：
    “苏联和日本两国代表团九月二十一日就今后进行谈判的程序问题交换了意见。双方同意：由于日本代表团团长松本俊一先生要返回东京同本国政府商议，谈判宣布短期中断。双方还同意：在松本俊一先生返回伦敦以后继续举行谈判。在松本俊一先生回国期间，如果有必要，谈判将由苏联在这方面的全权代表、苏联驻英国临时代办别洛赫沃斯齐科夫和日本全权代表、日本代表团首席顾问高桥举行。”
