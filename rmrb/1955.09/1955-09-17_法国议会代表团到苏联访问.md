Title: French Parliamentary Delegation Visits the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-09-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 15th: According to TASS, the French parliamentary delegation, led by the Speaker of the National Assembly, Pierre Schneiter, arrived in Moscow on the 14th at the invitation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. French journalists accompanied the delegation.

Welcoming them at the airport were Volkov, Chairman of the Union Council of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Lacis, Chairman of the National Council, Gorkin, Deputy Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and Louis Joxe, the French Ambassador to the Soviet Union, among others.

The head of the delegation, Schneiter, gave TASS reporters a statement about the visit to the Soviet Union.

The statement said: We are not here to conduct negotiations or diplomatic discussions. Our wish is to visit your country and, through conversations and personal contact, to further familiarize ourselves with your leaders, social figures, and the masses of people. We hope to better understand them, and we hope they will better understand us. This sincere desire of ours will help us to understand each other better, and will help to establish closer ties between the people of the Soviet Union and France, which will not only benefit our two countries but also contribute to consolidating world peace.

### 法国议会代表团到苏联访问

1955-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

    法国议会代表团到苏联访问
    新华社十五日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科讯：应苏联最高苏维埃邀请访问苏联的法国议会代表团，由国民议会议长皮·施奈特率领已经在十四日到达莫斯科。和代表团同来的还有法国的新闻记者。
    到机场欢迎的有苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席沃尔科夫、民族院主席拉齐斯，苏联最高苏维埃主席团副秘书戈尔金，以及法国驻苏联大使路易·约克斯等人。
    代表团团长施奈特就这次访问苏联交给塔斯社记者一项声明。
    声明说：我们来到这里不是为了进行谈判和外交商谈。我们的愿望是访问一下贵国，通过谈话和个人接触，更进一步地同贵国的领导人物、社会人士和人民群众认识。我们希望能更好地了解他们，也希望他们更好地了解我们。我们这一真诚愿望将有助于我们之间的互相了解，有助于建立苏法两国人民之间的更加密切的联系，这不仅对我们两国有利，而且对巩固世界和平也有好处。
