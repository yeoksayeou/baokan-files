Title: Romanian Cooperative Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1955-09-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The Romanian Central Cooperative Alliance delegation, which visited China according to the 1955 execution plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, returned home by plane on the morning of the 16th.

The delegation arrived in Beijing on August 1st. During their one and a half months in China, they visited Shenyang, Anshan, Fushun, Shanghai, Wuxi, Hangzhou and other cities. They toured industrial enterprises such as the Anshan Seamless Steel Tube Plant, the large-scale rolling mill, the Fushun Open-pit Coal Mine, and Shanghai's textile factories, as well as supply and marketing cooperatives, consumer cooperatives, and handicraft production cooperatives in various locations. They also held forums with the National Cooperative Alliance and cooperative workers from various places to exchange work experience.

### 罗合作社代表团回国

1955-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

    罗合作社代表团回国
    新华社十六日讯  根据中罗文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划来中国访问的罗马尼亚中央合作总社代表团在十六日晨乘飞机返国。
    代表团在八月一日到北京，在中国一个半月中曾访问了沈阳、鞍山、抚顺、上海、无锡、杭州等城市，参观了鞍山无缝钢管厂、大型轧钢厂，抚顺露天煤矿、石油一厂，上海的纺织工厂等工业企业，以及各地的供销合作社、消费合作社、手工业生产合作社，并同全国合作总社和各地的合作社工作者举行了交流工作经验的座谈会。
