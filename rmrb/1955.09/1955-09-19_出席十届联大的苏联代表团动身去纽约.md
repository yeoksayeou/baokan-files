Title: Soviet Delegation to Attend 10th UN General Assembly Departs for New York
Author:
Date: 1955-09-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 18th: TASS reports that the Soviet delegation to the 10th session of the United Nations General Assembly, led by V.M. Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the USSR Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs, left Moscow for New York on the 17th.

The delegation of the Ukrainian Republic, led by the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Ukrainian Republic, P.R. Palamarchuk, and the delegation of the Byelorussian Republic, led by the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Byelorussian Republic, K.V. Kiselev, have already arrived in New York to participate in the work of the 10th session of the United Nations General Assembly.

### 出席十届联大的苏联代表团动身去纽约

1955-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

    出席十届联大的苏联代表团动身去纽约
    新华社十八日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：由苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长莫洛托夫率领的苏联出席联合国大会第十届会议的代表团十七日从莫斯科动身前往纽约。
    由乌克兰共和国外交部长帕拉马尔楚克率领的乌克兰共和国代表团和由白俄罗斯共和国外交部长基谢列夫率领的白俄罗斯共和国代表团业已到达纽约，准备参加联合国大会第十届会议的工作。
