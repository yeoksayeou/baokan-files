Title: Bulganin Sends Letter to Adenauer Regarding the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1955-09-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 26th – TASS reports: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Bulganin, wrote a letter on the 24th to the Chancellor of the Federal Republic of Germany, Adenauer.

In the letter, Bulganin said: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, based on the proposal of the Soviet government, has already approved on the 24th the agreement on the establishment of diplomatic relations between the USSR and the Federal Republic of Germany.

I am pleased to learn that the Federal Assembly of the Federal Republic of Germany has also unanimously approved the aforementioned agreement. Thus, the issue of establishing diplomatic relations between our two countries has been completely resolved.

Bulganin said: The Soviet government hereby extends its sincere congratulations to you and expresses its belief that the establishment of diplomatic relations will benefit the people of Germany and the Soviet Union, and will be conducive to the cause of world peace.

### 布尔加宁就建立外交关系问题致函阿登纳

1955-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁就建立外交关系问题致函阿登纳
    新华社二十六日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁二十四日写信给德意志联邦共和国总理阿登纳。
    布尔加宁在信里说：苏联最高苏维埃主席团根据苏联政府的建议，已经在二十四日批准了关于苏联和德意志联邦共和国之间建立外交关系的协议。
    我高兴地得知德意志联邦共和国联邦议院也一致批准了上述协议。因而，我们两国间建立外交关系的问题已经完全解决。
    布尔加宁说：苏联政府为此向你表示诚恳的祝贺，并且表示相信外交关系的建立会有利于德国和苏联人民，有利于世界和平事业。
