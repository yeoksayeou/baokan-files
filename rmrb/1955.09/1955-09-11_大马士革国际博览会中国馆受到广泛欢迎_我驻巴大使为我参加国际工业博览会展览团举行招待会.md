Title: Chinese Pavilion at Damascus International Fair Widely Welcomed; Our Ambassador to Pakistan Holds Reception for Chinese Exhibition Group Participating in International Industrial Fair
Author:
Date: 1955-09-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, September 9th: The Chinese Pavilion at the Damascus International Fair in Syria has been widely welcomed by the Syrian people and visitors from other Arab countries since its opening on September 1st.

Audiences have praised the tremendous achievements of China's economic construction as demonstrated by the Chinese exhibits. An Egyptian visitor wrote in the comments book: "I never imagined that China had reached such an advanced level in industry." An Iraqi woman wrote: "The exhibits in the Chinese Pavilion have made me realize the strength of the Chinese people." A Saudi Arabian visitor said, "Indeed, this is a great miracle created by the Chinese people." A Syrian visitor said, "Everything I have seen in this pavilion has impressed me very deeply. These products show how China has become so strong and prosperous." Several other Syrian visitors said that we are proud to see such products from an Asian country.

Visitors expressed their hope to further expand economic and cultural cooperation between Syria and other Arab countries with China. A Syrian visitor wrote in the comments book: "After visiting this pavilion, we believe that our friendship will grow stronger and more solid." Syrian writer Kayali said, "We believe that the economic and cultural relations between our two countries will be further developed, and this development will benefit the people of both our countries." A Jordanian doctor wrote: "After visiting this pavilion, my confidence in your policy of promoting friendship among all countries has become stronger."
Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, September 9th: Han Nianlong, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Pakistan, held a reception on the evening of September 6th in Karachi for the Chinese exhibition group participating in the Pakistan International Industrial Fair upon their arrival in Karachi.

Invited to the reception were Pakistani government officials, people from the business, cultural, scientific, and journalistic circles of Karachi, as well as diplomatic representatives from various countries to Pakistan, totaling about two hundred people. Personnel from the Soviet, Czechoslovak, and Yugoslav exhibition groups participating in the International Industrial Fair, as well as members of the Chinese Islamic Hajj delegation who were in Pakistan that day, also attended the reception.

People from all walks of life in Pakistan expressed a warm welcome to China's participation in the Pakistan International Industrial Fair and showed great interest in the further development of trade relations between China and Pakistan. The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 大马士革国际博览会中国馆受到广泛欢迎  我驻巴大使为我参加国际工业博览会展览团举行招待会

1955-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

    大马士革国际博览会中国馆受到广泛欢迎
    我驻巴大使为我参加国际工业博览会展览团举行招待会
    新华社九日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚大马士革国际博览会的中国馆自从九月一日开幕以来，受到叙利亚人民和来自其他阿拉伯国家的参观者广泛的欢迎。
    观众对于中国展览品所显示的中国经济建设的巨大成就表示赞扬。一个埃及参观者在意见簿上写道：“我从来没有想到过，中国在工业方面达到这样先进的程度。”一个伊拉克妇女写道：“中国馆中的展览品使我认识到了中国人民的力量。”一个沙特阿拉伯参观者说，“的确，这是中国人民所造成的一个伟大的奇迹”。一个叙利亚参观者说，“我在这个展览馆中所看到的一切给我非常深刻的印象。这些产品说明中国是如何变得这样强大和繁荣的。”另外几个叙利亚参观者说，我们因为看到来自一个亚洲国家的这种产品而感到自豪。
    参观者表示希望进一步扩大叙利亚和其他阿拉伯国家同中国之间的经济和文化合作。一个叙利亚参观者在意见簿上写道：“在参观了这个展览馆之后，我们相信，我们之间的友谊将增强并且更坚固。”叙利亚作家卡雅利说，“我们相信，我们两国之间的经济和文化关系将得到进一步发展，这种发展对于我们两国的人民都有好处。”一个约旦医生写道：“在我参观了这个展览馆之后，我对你们的促进各国间友谊的政策所怀的信心更加坚强了。”
    新华社九日讯喀喇蚩消息：中华人民共和国驻巴基斯坦大使韩念龙，为中国参加巴基斯坦国际工业博览会的展览团到达喀喇蚩在九月六日晚上举行了招待会。
    应邀参加招待会的有巴基斯坦政府官员，喀喇蚩市工商界、文化界、科学界和新闻界人士，各国驻巴基斯坦外交代表等共约二百多人。苏联、捷克斯洛伐克和南斯拉夫参加国际工业博览会的展览团人员，当天正在巴基斯坦的中国伊斯兰教朝觐团团员也参加了招待会。
    巴基斯坦各界人士对中国参加巴基斯坦国际工业博览会表示十分欢迎，并且对中国和巴基斯坦两国贸易关系的进一步发展感到很大的兴趣。酒会在友好的气氛中进行。
