Author:

Warmly Welcome the Yugoslav "Kolo" Folk Song and Dance Ensemble to Visit Our Country

The Yugoslav "Kolo" Folk Song and Dance Ensemble, led by O. Skovran, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of August 28th and held its first song and dance performance at the Tianqiao Theater in Beijing on the evening of August 31st. The song and dance programs performed by the ensemble, with their distinct national characteristics, were warmly welcomed by the Beijing audience. Some programs were applauded eight times after the performance and were performed again twice.

Left picture: At the first performance of the Yugoslav "Kolo" Folk Song and Dance Ensemble, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture of our country, delivered a welcome speech to the ensemble.
		Except for those marked, the photos on this page are by our reporter Gao Liang.

After the first performance of the ensemble in Beijing, Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, Chen Yi, and Acting Minister of Culture Qian Junrui went backstage to congratulate the Yugoslav artists on their successful performance and took a group photo together.
				Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie photographed.

The Komiti Dance
A hundred years ago, in the mountains of Macedonia, the people waged a war against Turkish rule and for the liberation of their motherland. The dance depicts the struggles and life fragments of the avenger Komiti people's guerrillas. When the head and members of the Croatian folk song and dance troupe visited the state-owned Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory, they were warmly welcomed by the workers.

Serbian Dance
Serbian folk songs and dances are rich and colorful, expressed in various steps and forms. The most popular form among the people is the "Kolo" dance.

Rogovo Suburban War Dance
This dance depicts the scene of two dancers fighting with daggers to win the "Tubanderi" drum, each hoping that the drummer will only accompany him.

The seventeen wonderful dance programs and two folk music programs performed by the ensemble at the first performance were warmly welcomed by the audience. The upper picture shows Chinese literary and art workers presenting flowers to the head of the ensemble, Skovran, before the performance. The right picture shows the audience rushing to the stage to present flowers after the performance.

Slavic and Sava River Basin Herzegovinian Folk Dance
The dance expresses the optimistic mood of these residents, and there are often pleasant and attractive dialogues during the dance.

### 热烈欢迎南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团访问我国（图片）

1955-09-02
第5版()
专栏：

    热烈欢迎南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团访问我国
    由奥·斯科夫兰率领的南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团，已于八月二十八日下午到达北京，三十一日晚在北京天桥剧场举行首次歌舞表演会。歌舞团演出的具有鲜明的民族色彩的歌舞节目，受到北京观众们的热烈欢迎，有的节目演出后谢幕八次，并重演了两次。
    左图：在南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团首次表演会上，我国文化部副部长夏衍向歌舞团致欢迎词。
                  本版照片除署名者外均为本报记者　高粮摄
    歌舞团在北京首次演出结束后，国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅和文化部代理部长钱俊瑞到后台祝贺南斯拉夫艺术家们演出成功，并在一起合影留念。
                                  新华社记者　钱嗣杰摄
    格米特舞
    一百年以前在马其顿的丛山中，人民进行了反对土耳其统治和争取祖国解放的战争，舞蹈表现了复仇者康米特人民游击队的斗争和生活片断。克罗地亚民歌歌舞团团长和团员们参观国营北京第二棉纺织厂时，受到职工们的热烈欢迎。
    塞尔维亚舞
    塞尔维亚民间歌舞是丰富多采的，是以多种多样的步法和形式来表现的，人民最喜闻乐见的形式是“科罗”舞蹈。
    罗果夫近郊战舞
    这个舞蹈表现了两个舞蹈者为了争取“图班德日”鼓用短剑角斗的场面，他们每一个人都希望鼓手只给他伴奏。
    歌舞团在首次表演会上演出的十七个精彩的舞蹈节目和两个民间音乐节目，受到观众的热烈欢迎。上图是演出开始前，我国文艺工作者向歌舞团团长斯科夫兰献花。右图是演出结束后观众纷纷上台献花。
    斯拉夫及萨瓦河流域赫尔吉亚民间舞
    舞蹈表现了这些居民生活中的乐观情绪，在舞蹈进行中经常有愉快和吸引人的对话。
