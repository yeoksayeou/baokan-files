Title: Part of British Delegation to China Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1955-09-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th. Professor Lonsdale, deputy leader of the British Friendship Delegation to China, and some members of the delegation, left Beijing in batches on August 30th and September 9th to return home after visiting Beijing, Northeast China, and East China.

On the eve of Professor Lonsdale's departure from Beijing, Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, held a farewell banquet for them.

### 英访华代表团部分团员回国

1955-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

    英访华代表团部分团员回国
    新华社九日讯　英国访华友好代表团副团长朗斯戴尔教授和部分团员在中国北京、东北和华东等地参观后，在八月三十日和九月九日分批离京返国。
    朗斯戴尔教授等离京前夕，中国科学院院长、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若为他们举行了欢送宴会。
