Title: Egyptian Deputy Prime Minister Visits New Delhi
Author:
Date: 1955-09-11
Page: 4
　
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, September 9th. Egyptian Deputy Prime Minister Gamal Salem arrived in New Delhi on the evening of the 8th from Calcutta. He is visiting India after attending Indonesia's independence anniversary and visiting Burma.
    Upon arriving in New Delhi, Gamal Salem said that India and Egypt are linked together by strong ties, and both countries are working for peace.

### 埃及副总理到新德里访问

1955-09-11
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及副总理到新德里访问
    新华社新德里九日讯　埃及副总理加麦尔·萨勒姆八日晚上从加尔各答到达新德里。他是在参加印度尼西亚独立纪念和访问缅甸以后到印度来访问的。
    加麦尔·萨勒姆到达新德里时发表谈话说，印度和埃及是被牢固的联系连结在一起的，两国都在为和平而努力。
