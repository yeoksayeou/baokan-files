Title: Chinese Art Troupe Leaves Prague for Switzerland
Author:
Date: 1955-09-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 31st: The Chinese Art Troupe left Prague by plane this morning, August 31st, for a performance tour in Switzerland. Present at the airport to see them off were Ludvík, Deputy Minister of Culture of Czechoslovakia, and Cao Ying, Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia.

On the evening of the 29th, the Czechoslovak Ministry of Culture hosted a farewell banquet for the art troupe.

### 我国艺术团离布拉格赴瑞士

1955-09-02
第4版()
专栏：

    我国艺术团离布拉格赴瑞士
    新华社布拉格三十一日电　中国艺术团八月三十一日晨乘飞机离布拉格前往瑞士作旅行演出。到机场送行的有捷克斯洛伐克文化部副部长卢德维克和中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛。
    二十九日晚，捷文化部曾设宴为艺术团饯行。
