    Our Great Motherland
    Rice is growing well and a good harvest is in sight in the Yanbian area of ​​Jilin Province. It is estimated that this year's rice output will reach or even exceed the bumper harvest of 1952. This is a picture of members of the Xinfeng Agricultural Production Cooperative in Changbai Township, Yanji County, checking the full rice grains.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    吉林省延边地区水稻生长普遍良好，丰收在望。估计今年水稻的产量将达到甚至超过丰收的一九五二年的产量。这是延吉县长白乡新丰农业生产合作社的社员正在检查颗粒饱满的水稻。
