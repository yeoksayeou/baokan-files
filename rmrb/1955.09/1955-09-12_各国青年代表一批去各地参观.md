Title: A Batch of Youth Representatives from Various Countries Visit Different Places
Author:
Date: 1955-09-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th. Eighty-four youth representatives from twenty-four countries in the Middle East, Near East, Africa, Latin America, the United States, Spain, and Portugal, who were invited to visit China, left Beijing by train on the evening of the 9th to visit the Northeast and East China regions.

On the same evening, thirty-seven youth representatives from Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia, and ninety-six youth representatives from India, left Beijing by train to return to their countries.

### 各国青年代表一批去各地参观

1955-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    各国青年代表一批去各地参观
    新华社九日讯　应邀来中国访问的来自中近东、非洲、拉丁美洲和美国、西班牙、葡萄牙等二十四个国家的青年代表八十四人，在九日晚乘火车离京去东北、华东各地参观。
    同日晚，越南、寮国、高棉青年代表三十七人，印度青年代表九十六人，乘火车离京返国。
