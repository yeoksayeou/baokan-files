Author:
Date: 1955-09-18

    Our Great Motherland
    The National Congress of Active Young Builders of Socialism will open on September 20th. More than 1,500 outstanding young representatives from all over the country have arrived in Beijing one after another. This is a scene of young representatives of various ethnic groups who have already arrived in Beijing talking together.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会将在九月二十日开幕。全国各地一千五百多个优秀青年的代表已陆续来到北京。这是已经到北京的各兄弟民族青年代表们在一起交谈的情形。
