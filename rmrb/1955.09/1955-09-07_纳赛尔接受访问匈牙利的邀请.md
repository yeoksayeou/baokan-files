Title: Nasser Accepts Invitation to Visit Hungary
Author:
Date: 1955-09-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 6th: Reports in the press say that the government of the Hungarian People's Republic has invited Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser to visit Hungary. Prime Minister Nasser has accepted this invitation.
    The reports say that the date of the visit will be decided later.

### 纳赛尔接受访问匈牙利的邀请

1955-09-07
第4版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔接受访问匈牙利的邀请
    新华社六日讯　塔斯社开罗六日讯：各报刊载消息说，匈牙利人民共和国政府邀请埃及总理纳赛尔访问匈牙利。纳赛尔总理已经接受了这个邀请。
    消息说，访问的时间将在以后决定。
