Title: Khrushchev Receives Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-09-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 22nd: The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, received the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Liu Xiao, on September 21st.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见我国驻苏大使

1955-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见我国驻苏大使
    新华社莫斯科二十二日电　苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫九月二十一日接见了中国驻苏联大使刘晓。
