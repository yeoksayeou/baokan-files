Title: Dong Biwu Inspects Factories and Enterprises in Urumqi
Author:
Date: 1955-09-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, September 24th: Dong Biwu, representative of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Central People's Government, who came to Urumqi to celebrate the establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region, inspected the state-owned Xinjiang July 1st Cotton Textile Factory on the morning of the 24th.

    Dong Biwu first listened to a brief report from the factory director, party committee secretary, and chairman of the trade union, and inquired about the party and youth league organizations, workers' living conditions, and trade union work. Then, Dong Biwu inspected the various workshops of the spinning, weaving, and printing and dyeing plants. Next to a printing and dyeing machine, the factory director introduced a type of calico with a peony pattern that was being printed. This calico was recently designed by the employees to celebrate the establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region.

    Dong Biwu also inspected the Xinjiang Petroleum Company and the Xinjiang Nonferrous Metals Company on the 22nd. The head of the Xinjiang Petroleum Company reported to Dong Biwu on their production, basic construction, learning from advanced Soviet experience, and the achievements made in training employees of various ethnic groups. They also introduced to Dong Biwu the selfless assistance provided by the Soviet Union in terms of materials, manpower, and technology during the joint operation of the two companies between our country and the Soviet Union, and after the transfer of these two companies to our country. During the briefing, Dong Biwu conveyed the central government's concern for them, encouraged them to work hard to learn from advanced Soviet experience, develop China's petroleum and metal industries, and expressed great concern for the living conditions of employees of all ethnic groups.

### 董必武在乌鲁木齐视察工厂企业

1955-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    董必武在乌鲁木齐视察工厂企业
    新华社乌鲁木齐二十四日电　前来乌鲁木齐庆贺新疆维吾尔自治区成立的中国共产党中央委员会和中央人民政府代表董必武，在二十四日上午视察了国营新疆七一棉纺织厂。
    董必武首先听取了这个厂的厂长、党委书记和工会主席的简要汇报，并询问了党、团组织，工人生活和工会工作情况。接着董必武视察了纺织、织布、印染工场的各个车间。在一台印染机旁，厂长向董必武介绍了正在印染的一种有牡丹花图案的花布。这种花布是职工们为迎接新疆维吾尔自治区成立，在最近才设计出来的。
    董必武还在二十二日视察了新疆石油公司和新疆有色金属公司。新疆石油公司负责人向董必武汇报了他们的生产、基本建设情况和学习苏联先进经验以及培养各民族职工所取得的成就。他们还向董必武介绍了这两个公司在我国和苏联合营期间以及这两个公司在移交我国后苏联在物质上、人力上、技术上给予的无私援助。董必武在听取汇报中，传达了中央对他们的关怀，勉励他们努力学习苏联先进经验，发展我国石油、金属工业，并对各族职工生活情况表示极大关怀。
