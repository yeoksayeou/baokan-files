Title: People of Shenyang and Harbin Pay Respects at Soviet Martyrs' Cemetery
Author:
Date: 1955-09-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 4th. To commemorate the victory of the Anti-Japanese War and the tenth anniversary of the Soviet Army's liberation of Northeast China, the people of Shenyang and Harbin paid respects at the Soviet Martyrs' Cemetery in Shenyang and Harbin on the morning of the 3rd, and laid wreaths in front of the tombs.

　　More than 600 people participated in the memorial ceremony in Shenyang; more than 1,500 people participated in the memorial ceremony in Harbin.

　　The responsible persons of the Party, government, and military of the provinces and cities of Shenyang and Harbin, as well as representatives of various democratic parties, people's organizations, factories, schools, and people from all walks of life, participated in the memorial ceremony.

### 沈阳和哈尔滨两市人民祭扫苏军烈士墓

1955-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    沈阳和哈尔滨两市人民祭扫苏军烈士墓
    新华社四日讯　沈阳和哈尔滨两市人民为纪念抗日战争胜利和苏军解放东北十周年，三日上午分别在沈阳市和哈尔滨市祭扫了苏军烈士墓，并在墓前献了花圈。
    在沈阳市参加祭扫仪式的，有六百多人；在哈尔滨市参加祭扫仪式的有一千五百多人。
    在沈阳市和哈尔滨两地的省、市的党、政、军负责人和各民主党派，各人民团体、工厂、学校、各界人民代表都参加了祭扫仪式。
