Title: French Medical Delegation and Belgian Medical Expert Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 2nd. The French medical delegation, led by Professor Vildmay, and the Belgian medical expert Professor Dejardin, Secretary-General of the International Society of Surgery, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 2nd at the invitation of the Chinese Medical Association.

### 法国医学代表团和比医学家到京

1955-09-03
第4版()
专栏：

    法国医学代表团和比医学家到京
    新华社二日讯　应中华医学会邀请前来中国访问的法国医学代表团维尔德麦教授等一行五人和比利时医学家、国际外科医学会秘书长德热丁教授，在二日乘飞机到达北京。
