Author:
Date: 1955-09-07
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    This year, the 2.4 million mu of intermediate rice crops on both sides of the Yangtze River in Anhui Province have generally had bumper harvests. The average yield per mu is over 500 jin, and the highest yield has reached 900 jin, an increase of 30% to 40% compared to the bumper harvest year of 1952. The more than 500 mu of intermediate rice crops in Matang Agricultural Production Cooperative in Matang Township, Wuhu County, have an average yield of over 600 jin per mu.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    安徽省长江两岸的二千四百多万亩中稻，今年普遍丰收，平均每亩产量都在五百斤以上，最高产量达到九百斤，比丰收的一九五二年还增产三、四成。芜湖县马塘乡马塘农业生产合作社的五百多亩中稻，平均每亩产量在六百斤以上。
