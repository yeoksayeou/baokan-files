Title: Chinese Youth Art Troupe Holds Closing Performance in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1955-09-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency:

    The Chinese Youth Art Troupe held its closing performance on August 30th at the Guards House in Warsaw, after visiting and performing in major cities throughout Poland. Five thousand people watched the performance.

    The First Vice Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers, Nowak, the Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Ochab, and the Vice Chairmen of the Council of Ministers, Berman, Jóźwiak-Witold, Gede, and Jaroszewicz, all attended the performance of the Chinese artists. During the intermission, they also met with the leaders of the art troupe.

    The art troupe performed songs, dances, classical plays, and acrobatics, which were warmly welcomed by the audience.

    During its stay in Poland, the art troupe split into Peking opera, song and dance, and acrobatics teams, visiting various parts of Poland for two weeks and performing a total of thirty-four shows, with a total audience of approximately 285,000 people. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中国青年艺术团在华沙举行闭幕演出

1955-09-05
第3版()
专栏：

    中国青年艺术团在华沙举行闭幕演出
    中国青年艺术团在波兰各大城市作了访问演出之后，八月三十日在华沙近卫军大厦举行闭幕演出。观看这次演出的观众有五千人。
    波兰部长会议第一副主席诺瓦克，波兰统一工人党中央委员会书记处书记奥哈布，部长会议副主席贝尔曼、约兹维亚克—韦托德、盖德和雅罗谢维奇等均出席观看中国艺术家的表演。他们并在演出休息时间，接见了艺术团的领导人。
    艺术团这次演出了歌舞、古典剧和杂技，受到观众的热烈欢迎。
    艺术团在波兰期间，曾分成京剧队、歌舞队和杂技队到波兰各地访问了两个星期，共演出了三十四场，观众共约有二十八万五千人。（新华社）
