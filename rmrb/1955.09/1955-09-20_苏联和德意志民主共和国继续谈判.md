Title: Soviet Union and German Democratic Republic Continue Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1955-09-20
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 19th (TASS): On the morning of September 19th, the government delegations of the Soviet Union and the German Democratic Republic continued negotiations at the Grand Kremlin Palace in Moscow, discussing further measures to develop and consolidate the friendly relations between the two countries.
    Prime Minister Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic presided over the meeting.

### 苏联和德意志民主共和国继续谈判

1955-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和德意志民主共和国继续谈判
    新华社十九日讯　塔斯社莫斯科十九日讯：九月十九日上午，苏联和德意志民主共和国两国政府代表团在莫斯科大克里姆林宫继续举行谈判，商讨关于发展和巩固苏联和德意志民主共和国两国友好关系的进一步措施。
    德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥担任主席。
