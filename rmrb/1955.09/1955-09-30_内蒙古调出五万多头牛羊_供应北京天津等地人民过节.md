Title: Inner Mongolia Transfers Over 50,000 Cattle and Sheep to Supply People in Beijing, Tianjin, and Other Places for the Holidays
Author:
Date: 1955-09-30
Page: 2

Since September 1st, more than 51,000 head of plump and fat cattle and sheep from the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region have been successively transported via railway lines such as Baotou and Zhangjiakou to Beijing, Tianjin, and other places to supply the people's celebrations of National Day and the Mid-Autumn Festival. This year, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region's supply of cattle and sheep to Beijing and Tianjin has increased by more than 10,000 head compared to the same period last year. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 内蒙古调出五万多头牛羊  供应北京天津等地人民过节

1955-09-30
第2版()
专栏：

    内蒙古调出五万多头牛羊  供应北京天津等地人民过节
    从九月一日起，内蒙古自治区五万一千多头膘满、体肥的牛羊，正经包头、张家口等铁路沿线陆续运往北京、天津等地供应人民欢度国庆节和中秋节。今年，内蒙古自治区供应北京、天津的牛羊比去年同时期增加了一万多头。　（新华社）
