Title: Guo Moruo and Shen Junru Host Banquet for Pritt and Madame Blum

Author: 
Date: 1955-09-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. Two internationally renowned peace activists and recipients of the Stalin International Peace Prize "For Strengthening International Peace": D.N. Pritt, Chairman of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers and President of the British Peace Committee, and Madame Blum, Permanent Member and Secretary of the Secretariat of the World Peace Council of Belgium, attended a grand welcome banquet in Beijing on the evening of the 19th.

The banquet was jointly hosted by Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and recipient of the Stalin International Peace Prize "For Strengthening International Peace," and Shen Junru, Vice Chairman of the China Political Science and Law Association.

Among those present at the banquet tonight were Chen Shutong and Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairmen of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Standing Committee members Shen Yanbing, Shao Lizi, Ma Yinchu, Xu Deheng, Xu Baoju, and Luo Longji; Zhang Zhiran and Qian Duansheng, Vice Chairmen of the China Political Science and Law Association, and council members; Shi Liang and Xu Guangping, Vice Chairwomen of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Zhou Gengsheng and Hu Yuzhi, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Zhao Yimin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Zhang Bojun, Cai Tingkai, Li Yimang, Hou Debang, and Mei Ru'ao, members of the World Peace Council; Tōgo Kameda and Vantouriere, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee; and committee member Rewi Alley.

After the banquet, the guests enjoyed a Chinese acrobatics performance together.

### 郭沫若和沈钧儒欢宴普里特和布伦姆夫人

1955-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若和沈钧儒欢宴普里特和布伦姆夫人
    新华社十九日讯　两位国际著名和平人士、“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金获得者——国际民主法律工作者协会主席、英国和平委员会会长普里特和世界和平理事会常务委员兼书记处书记、比利时布伦姆夫人，十九日晚在北京出席了一个隆重的欢迎宴会。
    这个宴会是“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金获得者、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若和中国政治法律学会副主席沈钧儒联合举行的。
    出席今晚宴会的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通、廖承志，常务委员沈雁冰、邵力子、马寅初、许德珩、许宝驹、罗隆基，中国政治法律学会副主席张志让、钱端升和理事，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席史良、许广平，中国人民外交学会副会长周鲠生、胡愈之，中国人民对外文化协会副会长赵毅敏，世界和平理事会理事章伯钧、蔡廷锴、李一氓、侯德榜、梅汝璈，亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长龟田东伍、万徒勒里，委员艾黎。
    宴会结束后，宾主一起欣赏了中国的杂技表演。
