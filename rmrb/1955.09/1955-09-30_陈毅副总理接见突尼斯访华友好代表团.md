Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Tunisian Friendship Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1955-09-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yi, received the head of the Tunisian Friendship Delegation to China, Aleya, and members of the delegation on the morning of the 29th.

    The two sides exchanged views on Sino-Tunisian friendship and the international situation. Vice Premier Chen particularly expressed the Chinese people's sympathy and support for the struggle of the Tunisian and North African people for national independence. Head of Delegation Aleya expressed his heartfelt gratitude for China's invitation to the Tunisian Friendship Delegation to visit China and for Vice Premier Chen Yi's reception.

    Those present at the reception included Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman; Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General; Yang Hansheng and Zhao Yimin, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; and Da Putsheng, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association.

### 陈毅副总理接见突尼斯访华友好代表团

1955-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见突尼斯访华友好代表团
    新华社二十九日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅在二十九日上午接见了突尼斯访华友好代表团团长阿莱亚和代表团团员。
    双方就中突友好及国际局势问题交换了意见。陈副总理特别对突尼斯及北非人民争取民族独立的斗争表示中国人民对他们的同情和支持。阿莱亚团长对中国邀请突尼斯访华友好代表团访问中国和陈毅副总理接见他们，表示衷心的感谢。
    陪同接见的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若、副主席廖承志、秘书长刘贯一，中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙、赵毅敏，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生等。
