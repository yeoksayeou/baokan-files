Title: Soviet-Japanese Negotiations Joint Communiqué
Author:
Date: 1955-09-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, London, September 7th, TASS dispatch from London, September 7th: The Soviet and Japanese negotiation parties in London released the following joint communiqué:
    At the meeting on September 6th, Soviet representative Malik and Japanese representative Shunichi Matsumoto further exchanged opinions on the issue of normalizing Soviet-Japanese relations. The next meeting is scheduled for September 13th.

### 苏日谈判联合公报

1955-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏日谈判联合公报
    新华社七日讯　塔斯社伦敦七日讯：苏日谈判双方在伦敦发表了下列联合公报：
    在九月六日的会议上，苏联代表马立克和日本代表松本俊一就苏日两国关系正常化问题进一步交换了意见。下次会议预定在九月十三日举行。
