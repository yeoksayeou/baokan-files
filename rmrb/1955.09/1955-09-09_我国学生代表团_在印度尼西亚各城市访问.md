Title: Chinese Student Delegation Visits Cities in Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1955-09-09
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency
The Chinese student delegation headed by Qian Dawei, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Students' Federation, which is currently visiting Indonesia, arrived in Yogyakarta on September 1 after visiting Jakarta, Bogor, and Bandung. They were warmly welcomed by local students and youth. They paid courtesy visits to the local administrative authorities and the president of Gadjah Mada University.

During their stay in Jakarta, the Chinese student delegation visited the University of Indonesia, museums, and other cultural sites, and also held joint gatherings with Indonesian students. They also visited the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Information, and the Parliament.

The Chinese student delegation was invited by the Indonesian National Union of University Students to attend the Indonesian Independence Day celebrations.

### 我国学生代表团  在印度尼西亚各城市访问

1955-09-09
第3版()
专栏：

    我国学生代表团
    在印度尼西亚各城市访问
    目前在印度尼西亚访问的以中华全国学生联合会副秘书长钱大卫为首的中国学生代表团，在访问了雅加达、茂物和万隆后，已在九月一日到达日惹。他们受到当地学生和青年们的热烈欢迎。他们曾对地方行政当局和加查玛达大学校长进行了礼节的访问。
    中国学生代表团在雅加达期间，曾经参观了印度尼西亚大学、博物馆和其他文化场所，并且还同印度尼西亚学生们联欢。他们还访问了印度尼西亚外交部、情报部和国会。
    中国学生代表团是应印度尼西亚全国大学生联合会的邀请来参加印度尼西亚独立日庆祝典礼的。
                                  （新华社）
