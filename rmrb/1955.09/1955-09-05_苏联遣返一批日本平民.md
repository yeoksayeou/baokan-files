Title: The Soviet Union Repatriates a Batch of Japanese Civilians
Author:
Date: 1955-09-05
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 3rd (TASS): With the assistance of the Executive Committee of the Union of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies of the USSR, a batch of Japanese civilians—thirty-six people released from prison—were repatriated from the Soviet Union on August 30th.
    The repatriated people were handed over to representatives of the Japanese Red Cross, who came to meet them at the port of Nakhodka, and then returned to Japan on the "Hokuto Maru" ship.

### 苏联遣返一批日本平民

1955-09-05
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联遣返一批日本平民
    新华社四日讯　塔斯社莫斯科三日讯：在苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会执行委员会的协助下，八月三十日有一批日本平民——从监狱释放的三十六个人从苏联遣返回国。
    被遣返的人在纳霍德卡港被移交给来接他们的日本红十字会代表们，随后乘“北斗丸”轮船返回日本。
