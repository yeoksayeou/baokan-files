Title: German Democratic Republic Council of Ministers Approves Treaty on Relations between the Soviet Union and the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1955-09-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 24th: The Prime Minister's Press Office issued a communique stating that the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic held a meeting on the 22nd to hear a report from Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers (Deputy Prime Minister) Nuschke on the Moscow talks between the Soviet Union and the German Democratic Republic. The Council of Ministers approved the Treaty on Relations between the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and the German Democratic Republic and submitted the treaty to the People's Chamber for ratification.
The People's Chamber is scheduled to meet on the 26th to discuss the treaty.

### 民主德国部长会议通过苏联和德意志民主共和国关系条约

1955-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国部长会议通过苏联和德意志民主共和国关系条约
    新华社柏林二十四日电　总理新闻处发表公报说：德意志民主共和国部长会议二十二日举行会议，听取部长会议副主席（副总理）努舍克关于苏联和德意志民主共和国莫斯科谈判的报告。部长会议通过了苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟和德意志民主共和国关系条约，并且把这个条约送交人民议院批准。
    人民议院定于二十六日开会讨论这个条约。
