Title: The State Council of the People's Republic of China Solemnly Holds Ceremony for Conferring the Rank of General

Author: 
Date: 1955-09-28
Page: 2

The State Council of the People's Republic of China solemnly held a ceremony on the afternoon of the 27th to confer the rank of general on officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

    The venue was solemn and simple. A huge portrait of Chairman Mao Zedong hung on the front of the rostrum, with the national flag hanging on both sides of the portrait.

    On the rostrum were: Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, Deng Zihui, He Long, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, and Li Xiannian; Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Commission Nie Rongzhen, Cheng Qian, Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, and Long Yun.

    Attending the ceremony were: Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, directors of various offices, ministers of various ministries, and directors of various commissions.

    At 2:30 p.m., the conferral ceremony began. The military band played the national anthem.

    Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, announced the order of the Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China conferring the rank of general on officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

    Then, Premier Zhou Enlai respectively presented the orders conferring the ranks of Grand General, General, Lieutenant General, and Major General to Su Yu and other generals in Beijing.

    The ceremony ended with the band playing the military music of the March of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. (Xinhua News Agency) (Attached pictures)

### 中华人民共和国国务院  隆重举行授予将官军衔的典礼

1955-09-28
第2版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国国务院
    隆重举行授予将官军衔的典礼
    中华人民共和国国务院在二十七日下午隆重举行授予中国人民解放军军官将官军衔典礼。
    会场庄严朴素。毛泽东主席的巨幅画像悬挂在主席台的正面，画像的两边挂着国旗。
    在主席台上的有：国务院总理周恩来，副总理陈云、彭德怀、邓小平、邓子恢、贺龙、陈毅、李富春、李先念，国防委员会副主席聂荣臻、程潜、张治中、傅作义、龙云。
    参加典礼的有：国务院秘书长习仲勋，各办公室主任，各部部长和各委员会主任。
    下午二时三十分，授衔典礼开始。军乐队奏国歌。
    国务院秘书长习仲勋宣读了中华人民共和国国务院总理授予中国人民解放军军官将官军衔的命令。
    接着，周恩来总理分别把授予大将、上将、中将、少将军衔的命令状，一一授予粟裕等在京将官。
    典礼在乐队高奏中国人民解放军进行曲的军乐声中结束。（新华社）（附图片）
