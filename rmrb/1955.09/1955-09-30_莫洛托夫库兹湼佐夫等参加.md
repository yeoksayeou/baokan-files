Title: Molotov, Kuznetsov and others attend

Author:
Date: 1955-09-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 29th. TASS News Agency: Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Foreign Affairs, attended a banquet hosted by U.S. Secretary of State Dulles on the evening of September 27 for the foreign ministers of the Soviet Union, Britain, and France.

The Soviet side attending the banquet also included: Kuznetsov, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Zarubin, Soviet Ambassador to the United States, Malik, Soviet Ambassador to the United Kingdom, and Sobolev, Permanent Representative of the Soviet Union to the United Nations.

### 莫洛托夫库兹湼佐夫等参加

1955-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫库兹湼佐夫等参加
    新华社二十九日讯　塔斯社纽约讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长莫洛托夫在九月二十七日晚参加了美国国务卿杜勒斯举行的招待苏联、英国和法国外交部长的宴会。
    苏联方面出席宴会的还有：苏联外交部第一副部长库兹湼佐夫、苏联驻美国大使查鲁宾、苏联驻英国大使马立克和苏联常驻联合国代表索波列夫。
