Title: Congratulations on the Establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region
Author: 
Date: 1955-09-23
Page: 1

Telegrams of Congratulations

    Telegram from the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress to the Second Session of the First People's Congress of Xinjiang Province:
    The establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region will further promote the development of political, economic, and cultural undertakings in the Xinjiang region, strengthen the unity of all ethnic groups, and inspire the enthusiasm of all ethnic groups to participate in the socialist construction of the motherland. We hereby send our congratulations and wish the conference success.
                            Standing Committee of the National People's Congress				September 21, 1955
    Telegram from the State Council of the People's Republic of China to the Second Session of the First People's Congress of Xinjiang Province:
    On the occasion of the establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region, we extend our warm congratulations to you and, through you, to the people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang.
    The establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region is another great victory for our country's ethnic policy and the result of the close unity and joint efforts of the people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang over the past few years. The establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region will further strengthen the unity of the Uyghur people and the people of other ethnic groups in Xinjiang, promote the development of political, economic, and cultural undertakings in the Xinjiang region, and encourage the people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang to make greater contributions to the socialist construction and socialist transformation of the great motherland.
          The State Council of the People's Republic of China
          September 20, 1955
    Telegram from the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress to the Second Session of the First People's Congress of Xinjiang Province:
    The establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region is a glorious victory for Chairman Mao's ethnic policy, a great happy event for the people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang, and a great happy event for the people of all ethnic groups in the country. We hereby send our congratulations. May the people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang unite more closely after the implementation of regional autonomy and strive to build a socialist Xinjiang!
                               Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress
                                September 17
    Telegram from the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China to the Second Session of the First People's Congress of Xinjiang Province:
    The Second Session of the First People's Congress of Xinjiang Province will discuss the establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region. This is another great victory for the ethnic policy of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, a symbol of greater unity among all ethnic groups in the great motherland, and a great happy event in the political life of the Uyghur people and the people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang. We firmly believe that the establishment of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region will surely encourage the Uyghur people and the people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang to further strengthen unity, give full play to their enthusiasm and initiative, participate in the socialist construction of the motherland, complete the tasks of the national Five-Year Plan in the Xinjiang region, and gradually build Xinjiang into a socialist Xinjiang. We hereby send our congratulations and wish the conference a successful victory and good health to all representatives.
                            Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China
                            September 19

### 祝贺新疆维吾尔自治区成立

1955-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺新疆维吾尔自治区成立
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会的贺电新疆省第一届人民代表大会第二次会议：
    新疆维吾尔自治区的成立，将进一步促进新疆地区政治、经济和文化事业的发展，加强各族人民的团结，鼓舞各族人民参加祖国社会主义建设的热忱。特此电贺，并祝大会成功。
                            全国人民代表大会常务委员会				一九五五年九月二十一日
    中华人民共和国国务院的贺电新疆省第一届人民代表大会第二次会议：
    当此新疆维吾尔自治区成立之际，特向你们，并通过你们向新疆各民族人民致以热烈的祝贺。
    新疆维吾尔自治区的成立，是我们国家的民族政策的又一伟大胜利，也是新疆各民族人民几年以来亲密团结共同努力的结果。新疆维吾尔自治区的成立，将进一步地加强维吾尔族和新疆其他各民族人民的团结，促进新疆地区的政治、经济和文化事业的发展，并鼓舞新疆各民族人民为着伟大祖国的社会主义建设和社会主义改造事业作出更大的贡献。中华人民共和国国务院
          一九五五年九月二十日
    全国人民代表大会民族委员会的贺电新疆省第一届人民代表大会第二次会议：
    新疆维吾尔自治区的成立，是毛主席民族政策的光辉胜利，是新疆各民族人民的大喜事，也是全国各民族人民的大喜事，特电祝贺。新疆各民族人民，在实行区域自治以后，更加紧密地团结起来，为建设社会主义的新疆而奋斗！全国人民代表大会民族委员会
                                九月十七日
    中华人民共和国民族事务委员会的贺电新疆省第一届人民代表大会第二次会议：
    新疆省第一届人民代表大会第二次会议将要讨论关于新疆维吾尔自治区的成立问题。这是中国共产党，毛主席的民族政策的又一伟大胜利，是伟大祖国各民族更加团结的标志，是维吾尔族人民和新疆各民族人民政治生活中的一件大喜事。我们深信，新疆维吾尔自治区的成立，一定能够鼓舞维吾尔族和新疆各民族人民进一步加强团结，发挥其积极性和主动精神，参加祖国的社会主义建设，完成国家五年计划在新疆地区的任务，逐步地把新疆建设成为社会主义的新疆。谨电致贺并祝大会胜利成功，各位代表身体健康。中华人民共和国民族事务委员会
                            九月十九日
