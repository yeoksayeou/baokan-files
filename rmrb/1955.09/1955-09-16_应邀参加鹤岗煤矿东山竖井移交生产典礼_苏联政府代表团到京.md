Title: Soviet Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Attend the Handover Ceremony of the Dongshan Shaft at the Hegang Coal Mine

Author:

Date: 1955-09-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. At the invitation of our government to attend the handover ceremony of the Dongshan Shaft at the Hegang Coal Mine, the Soviet government delegation, led by head of delegation Zabrotsky, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 15th. Xu Daben, Vice Minister of the Coal Industry Ministry of the People's Republic of China, Li Zheren, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and others went to the airport to welcome them. After attending the handover ceremony of the Dongshan Shaft at the Hegang Coal Mine, the delegation will also visit some coal mines in the Northeast. The delegation members include:

    Zabrotsky (Head of Delegation), Member of the Collegium of the USSR Ministry of Coal Industry
    Ashipov, Representative of the Soviet Foreign Economic Liaison Bureau in China (already in China)
    Kozhanov, Chief Design Engineer of the Leningrad Design Institute of the USSR Ministry of Coal Industry Enterprise Construction

### 应邀参加鹤岗煤矿东山竖井移交生产典礼  苏联政府代表团到京

1955-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加鹤岗煤矿东山竖井移交生产典礼
    苏联政府代表团到京
    新华社十五日讯　应我国政府邀请前来我国参加鹤岗煤矿东山竖井移交生产典礼的苏联政府代表团由团长扎布洛茨基率领，十五日下午乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中华人民共和国煤炭工业部副部长徐达本、对外贸易部副部长李哲人等。代表团在参加鹤岗煤矿东山竖井移交生产典礼后，并将赴东北一些煤矿参观。代表团组成人员：
    扎布洛茨基（团长）苏联采煤工业部部务委员
    阿希波夫苏联对外经济联络局驻华代表（已在我国）
    科然诺夫苏联采煤工业企业建设部列宁格勒设计院设计总工程师
