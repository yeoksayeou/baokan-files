Title: Liu Shaoqi Hosts Banquet to Welcome Shiga Yoshio and Tokuda Tatsu
Author:
Date: 1955-09-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 3rd. Liu Shaoqi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 3rd to welcome Shiga Yoshio, member of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, and Tokuda Tatsu, wife of the late General Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party, Tokuda Kyuichi, who came to Beijing to transport his remains.
    Those present at the banquet included: Chen Yun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC, Deng Xiaoping, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary-General of the CPC Central Committee, Yang Shangkun, Deputy Secretary-General of the CPC Central Committee, Zhao Yimin and Li Chuli, responsible personnel of the CPC Central Committee, and responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations, including He Xiangning, Zhang Bojun, Li Zhuchen, Liao Chengzhi, Lai Ruoyu, Hu Yaobang, and Kang Keqing.
    Japanese people in Beijing, including Kameda Togo, Kaneko Kenta, Nakamura Uemon, and Takahashi Katsuyuki were also invited to attend the banquet.

### 刘少奇设宴欢迎志贺义雄和德田达

1955-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇设宴欢迎志贺义雄和德田达
    新华社三日讯　中共中央书记处书记刘少奇在三日晚举行宴会，欢迎前来北京接运日本共产党已故总书记德田球一遗骨的日本共产党中央委员志贺义雄和德田球一夫人德田达。
    出席宴会的有：中共中央书记处书记陈云，中共中央政治局委员、中共北京市委员会书记彭真，中共中央政治局委员、中共中央秘书长邓小平，中共中央副秘书长杨尚昆，中共中央负责工作人员赵毅敏、李初梨，各民主党派和人民团体的负责人何香凝、章伯钧、李烛尘、廖承志、赖若愚、胡耀邦、康克清等。
    在北京的日本人士龟田东伍、金子健太、中村玩右卫门、高桥胜之也应邀出席了宴会。
