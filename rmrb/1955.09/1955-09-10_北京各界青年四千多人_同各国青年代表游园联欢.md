Title: More Than 4,000 Youths From All Walks of Life in Beijing Hold Garden Party With Youth Representatives From Various Countries

Author:

Date: 1955-09-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 8th. More than 4,000 youths from all walks of life in Beijing, together with youth representatives from thirty-two countries who came to China, enjoyed a wonderful early autumn evening in Zhongshan Park on the 8th.

    At the gathering in the Music Hall, Wang Zhaohua, Second Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, extended a heartfelt welcome to the foreign youth friends who had come from afar on behalf of the Beijing youth. The Literary and Art Troupe of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Central Experimental Opera House performed excellent songs and dances for the youth representatives of various countries at the meeting.

    Accompanied by Hu Yaobang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, the youth representatives from various countries came to Zhongshan Hall. The youth representatives from various countries immediately joined the students' dance circles.

    The Altar of Land and Grain, decorated with countless colored lights, also gathered the capital's youth and youth from various countries immersed in joy. Indian youth Druba Ganguly, who collected 38,000 signatures for the World Peace Council's "Appeal to the People of the World," was lifted high by the young students. Japanese youth Honpo Yukio shook hands tightly with students from Peking University, saying: The Japanese people resolutely oppose the use of atomic weapons.

    Late at night, the youth representatives from various countries and the youth from all walks of life in Beijing bid farewell to each other while singing "The March of the World Democratic Youth."

### 北京各界青年四千多人  同各国青年代表游园联欢

1955-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    北京各界青年四千多人
    同各国青年代表游园联欢
    据新华社八日讯　北京市各界青年四千多人，八日晚同到中国的三十二个国家青年代表，在中山公园欢度了一个初秋的良宵。
    在音乐堂的联欢大会上，青年团北京市委员会第二书记王照华代表北京青年向远道来访的外国青年朋友表示衷心的欢迎。中国人民解放军总政治部文艺工作团和中央实验歌剧院在会上为各国青年代表演出了精采的歌舞。
    各国青年代表在青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦等陪同下，来到了中山堂。各国青年代表立刻卷入学生们的舞圈中。
    装饰了无数彩灯的社稷坛前，也聚集了沉浸在欢乐中的首都青年和各国青年。为世界和平理事会“告全世界人民书”征集了三万八千个签名的印度青年德鲁巴·甘古利被青年学生们高高举起来。日本青年本保幸雄和北京大学的学生紧紧地握着手，他说：日本人民坚决反对使用原子武器。
    深夜，各国青年代表和北京各界青年在高唱“世界民主青年进行曲”的歌声中，互相告别了。
