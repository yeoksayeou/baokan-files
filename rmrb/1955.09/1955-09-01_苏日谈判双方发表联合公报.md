Title: Soviet-Japanese Negotiations: Joint Communiqué Issued by Both Sides
Author: 
Date: 1955-09-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, August 31st, TASS dispatch: The Soviet and Japanese negotiating parties issued the following joint communiqué in London on August 30th:

    At the meeting on August 30th, the Soviet representative Malik and the Japanese representative Shunichi Matsumoto further exchanged views on the normalization of relations between the Soviet Union and Japan. The next meeting is scheduled for September 6th.

### 苏日谈判双方发表联合公报

1955-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    苏日谈判双方发表联合公报
    新华社三十一日讯　塔斯社伦敦三十一日讯：苏日谈判双方于八月三十日在伦敦发表了下列联合公报：
    在八月三十日的会议上，苏联代表马立克和日本代表松本俊一就苏日两国关系正常化问题进一步交换了意见。下次会议预定九月六日举行。
