Title: US Holds Airlift Exercise Between Okinawa and Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1955-09-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 15th. According to a statement by the US Far East Air Force Headquarters on the 13th, the US 315th Air Transport Division's operational air transport planes recently conducted a large-scale air transport exercise. The exercise completed the task of airlifting units of the 18th Fighter-Bomber Wing from Okinawa to a "forward base" in Taiwan.

Not long before this, the United States had increased the number of the 18th Fighter-Bomber Wing’s troops stationed in Taiwan to three jet fighter squadrons, and these squadrons are conducting training under "hypothetical combat conditions."

### 美国在冲绳和台湾间举行空运演习

1955-09-18
第4版()
专栏：

    美国在冲绳和台湾间举行空运演习
    新华社十五日讯东京消息：据美国远东空军总部十三日宣布，美国第三一五空运师的作战空运飞机最近举行了一次大规模的空运演习。在演习中完成了把第十八战斗轰炸机联队的各个部队经冲绳空运到台湾的“前进基地”的任务。
    在这以前不久，美国已把第十八战斗轰炸机联队侵驻台湾的部队增加到三个喷气战斗机中队，这些中队正在“假设的战斗条件”下进行训练。
