Title: Nehru Reiterates China Should Restore its Legitimate Seat in the United Nations
Author:
Date: 1955-09-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, September 27th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru stated on the 27th, while responding to questions raised in the People's Chamber of Parliament, that "India's stated policy is to support the entry of all countries qualified to be members of the United Nations into the United Nations." He said: "India has repeatedly supported the People's Republic of China gaining its legitimate seat in the United Nations. At the current session of the United Nations General Assembly, India has supported this proposal."

### 尼赫鲁重申中国应恢复在联合国的合法地位

1955-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁重申中国应恢复在联合国的合法地位
    新华社新德里二十七日电　印度总理尼赫鲁二十七日在国会人民院答复临时提出的问题时说：“印度已经表明的政策是支持一切有资格作为联合国会员国的国家进入联合国”。他说：“印度曾一再支持中华人民共和国得到它在联合国的合法地位。在联合国大会本届会议上，印度支持了这种提案。”
