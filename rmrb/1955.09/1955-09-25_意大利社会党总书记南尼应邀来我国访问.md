Title: Italian Socialist Party Secretary-General Nenni Invited to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1955-09-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 24th. At the invitation of Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and with a letter of welcome from Premier Zhou Enlai, the Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, Chairman of the Italian National Peace Council, Secretary-General of the Italian Socialist Party, Pietro Nenni, along with his wife, daughter, and a party of five, arrived in Prague by plane on the 23rd en route to Beijing.

Welcoming them at the airport were Cao Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, and his wife, representatives from the Czechoslovak Committee for the Defense of Peace, and the Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy.

Ambassador Cao Ying and his wife hosted a banquet for Mr. and Mrs. Nenni that evening.

### 意大利社会党总书记南尼应邀来我国访问

1955-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    意大利社会党总书记南尼应邀来我国访问
    新华社布拉格二十四日电　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若的邀请，并得到周恩来总理去函欢迎前往中国访问的世界和平理事会副主席、意大利全国和平理事会主席、意大利社会党总书记南尼和他的夫人、女儿等一行五人，在前往北京途中于二十三日乘飞机到达布拉格。
    到机场欢迎的有中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛和夫人、捷克斯洛伐克保卫和平委员会的代表和苏联大使馆的临时代办。
    曹瑛大使夫妇当晚设宴招待了南尼夫妇。
