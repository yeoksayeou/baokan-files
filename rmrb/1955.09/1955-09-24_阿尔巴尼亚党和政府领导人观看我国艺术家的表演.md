Title: Albanian Party and Government Leaders Watch Performance by Chinese Artists
Author:
Date: 1955-09-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 23rd: Tirana dispatch: The Chinese Folk Music and Acrobatics Troupe performed at the People's Theater in Tirana on the evening of the 21st.

    Albanian Labor Party and government leaders Hoxha, Shehu, Kambo, Nushi, Koleka, Pilisteri, Marko, Mufti and Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly, Lleshi, attended the performance.

    Also attending the performance were the diplomatic envoys of various countries stationed in Tirana and Wang Dong, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Albania.

    The performance of Chinese folk classical music, folk songs, and acrobatics was praised by the Albanian Party and government leaders and warmly welcomed by the audience.

    At the end of the performance, Albanian artists specifically went to the stage to congratulate the Chinese artists on their successful performance and presented them with flowers.

    The Chinese artists presented a set of Chinese folk instruments for orchestra to the Albanian Conservatory of Music.

### 阿尔巴尼亚党和政府领导人观看我国艺术家的表演

1955-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚党和政府领导人观看我国艺术家的表演
    新华社布拉格二十三日电　地拉那消息：中国民间音乐杂技表演团二十一日晚间在地拉那人民剧院演出。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和政府的领导人霍查、谢胡、卡博、努什、科列加、皮里斯特里、马尔科、穆夫蒂乌和人民议会主席团主席列希都出席观看了演出。
    出席观看演出的还有驻地拉那的各国外交使节和中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王栋。
  中国民间古典音乐、民歌和杂技的演出受到阿尔巴尼亚党和政府领导人的赞扬和观众们的热烈欢迎。
    在演出结束时，阿尔巴尼亚的艺术家特地到舞台上去向中国艺术家祝贺他们演出成功，并且向他们献花。
    中国艺术家把一套供管弦乐队用的中国民间乐器赠给了阿尔巴尼亚音乐院。
