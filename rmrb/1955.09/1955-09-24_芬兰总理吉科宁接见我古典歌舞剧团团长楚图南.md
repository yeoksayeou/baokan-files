Title: Finnish Prime Minister Kekkonen Receives Chu Tunan, Head of Chinese Classical Opera and Dance Troupe
Author:
Date: 1955-09-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, September 22nd. Finnish Prime Minister Kekkonen and his wife received Chu Tunan, head of the Chinese Classical Opera and Dance Troupe, which is currently visiting and performing in Finland, on the 21st. Chen Xinren, the Chinese Ambassador to Finland, also attended the meeting.

Chu Tunan expressed his gratitude for the warm welcome and hospitality extended by the government and people of Finland to the Chinese Classical Opera and Dance Troupe in various parts of Finland.

Prime Minister Kekkonen praised the high artistic level of the Chinese classical opera and dance and expressed his satisfaction that the Finnish art community had the opportunity to see Chinese art. Both Prime Minister Kekkonen and Chu Tunan expressed their desire to continue to enhance cultural exchanges between China and Finland.

Finnish Foreign Minister Virolainen and Minister of Education Mrs. Sarasati also received Chu Tunan successively and discussed cultural and artistic issues between China and Finland.

### 芬兰总理吉科宁接见我古典歌舞剧团团长楚图南

1955-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    芬兰总理吉科宁接见我古典歌舞剧团团长楚图南
    新华社赫尔辛基二十二日电　芬兰总理吉科宁和夫人二十一日接见了现在在芬兰进行访问演出的中国古典歌舞剧团团长楚图南。中国驻芬兰大使陈辛仁也陪同受接见。
    楚图南对中国古典歌舞剧团在芬兰各地受到政府和人民的热烈欢迎和款待表示谢意。
    吉科宁总理盛赞中国古典歌舞剧的高度艺术性，并且对芬兰艺术界有机会看到中国的艺术表示十分满意。吉科宁总理和楚图南都表示要继续增进中芬两国的文化交流。
    芬兰外交部长维洛莱宁、教育部长沙拉斯蒂夫人也先后接见了楚图南，讨论了中芬两国文化艺术的问题。
