Title: Bulganin and Khrushchev Invited to Visit India This Year
Author:
Date: 1955-09-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 27th: According to TASS, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, and the member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, have been invited by the Prime Minister of the Republic of India, Nehru, to visit India this year and stay there for more than two weeks. It is understood that the visit will take place between November and December.
    Khrushchev sent a telegram to Nehru on the 26th, stating: Mr. Menon, your country's ambassador to the Soviet Union, conveyed to me your kind invitation for myself and the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, to visit India together. I am extremely pleased to accept your invitation and express my gratitude.

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫应邀在今年访问印度

1955-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫应邀在今年访问印度
    新华社二十七日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫应印度共和国总理尼赫鲁的邀请将在今年访问印度，并且在那里作两个多星期的停留。据悉，这次访问的时间在十一月和十二月之间。
    赫鲁晓夫二十六日打电报给尼赫鲁说：贵国驻苏联大使梅农先生对我转达了你邀请我和苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁一起访问印度的盛意邀请。我极其乐意地接受你的邀请，并且表示感谢。
