Title: Japanese Diet Members Delegation Leaves Beijing for the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-09-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 31st: The Japanese Diet Members Delegation, after staying in Beijing for two and a half days, left by plane this morning for a visit to the Soviet Union.

    On the afternoon of the 29th, the Japanese Diet Members Delegation visited the Soviet Embassy in China.

    During these two and a half days, the guests also separately visited the leaders of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Red Cross Society of China, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and the China Fisheries Association. They visited rural areas near Beijing, the National Youth Science and Technology and Handicraft Works Exhibition, and the Children's Hospital, and toured scenic spots in the capital such as the Summer Palace and the Forbidden City.

    The Japanese Diet Members Delegation, consisting of twelve members including Communist Party Diet member Goro Sudo and members of other parties, also went to the place where the remains of the late Secretary-General of the Japanese Communist Party, Kyuichi Tokuda, were laid to offer flowers and pay their respects.

    When the Japanese Diet Members Delegation left Beijing, Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Vice Presidents Qian Duansheng, Secretary-General Wu Maosun, Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, Vice President Hu Lansheng, Lei Renmin and Ji Chaoding, Vice Chairmen of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and Wu Yunfu, Secretary-General of the China People's Relief Administration, and others went to the airport to see them off.

    Soviet Embassy in China Counselor Gutikov, and others also went to the airport to see them off. Also at the airport to see them off were Japanese residents in Beijing Togo Kameda, Kenta Kaneko, and Uemon Nakamura.

### 日本议员访苏团离京去苏

1955-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    日本议员访苏团离京去苏
    新华社三十一日讯　日本国会议员访苏团在北京逗留了两天半以后，已在今日早晨乘飞机去苏联访问。
    二十九日下午，日本国会议员访苏团拜访了苏联驻华大使馆。
    在这两天半内，客人们还分别访问了中华全国总工会、中国红十字总会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中国渔业协会的负责人，参观了京郊农村、全国少年儿童科学技术和工艺作品展览会和儿童医院，游览了颐和园、故宫等首都名胜。
    日本国会议员访苏团中共产党议员须藤五郎和其他党派的议员共十二人，并前往已故的日本共产党总书记德田球一遗骨安放处献花致哀。
    日本国会议员访苏团在离开北京时，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、副会长钱端升、秘书长吴茂荪，中国红十字会会长李德全、副会长胡兰生，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民、冀朝鼎，以及中国人民救济总会秘书长伍云甫等都到机场欢送。
    苏联驻华大使馆参赞顾悌克夫等也到机场欢送。在机场欢送的还有在京日本人士龟田东伍、金子健太和中村玩右卫门。
