Title: Tens of Thousands of Workers in the Capital Watch Mei Lanfang's Performance
Author:
Date: 1955-09-04
Page: 3

    Tens of Thousands of Workers in the Capital Watch Mei Lanfang's Performance
    The famous performing artist Mei Lanfang gave four consecutive performances of "Cosmic Frontier" to more than 10,000 workers in the capital from August 26th to 29th. Mei Lanfang is currently rushing to film a movie about his stage life. He squeezed time out of his busy schedule to perform for the workers, expressing his condolences for their hard and arduous work in realizing the Five-Year Plan.
    Among the audience watching the show at the Labor Theater were workers from various factories and mines in the capital, as well as workers from the Shijingshan Power Plant and the state-owned Beijing No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory who came by car from the suburbs.
    Before the fourth performance, workers from the state-owned Beijing No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory, Beijing No. 1 Machine Tool Plant, and other units met Mei Lanfang backstage to thank him. At the end of the performance, worker representatives went on stage to present flowers to the people's artist. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 首都上万工人看梅兰芳的演出

1955-09-04
第3版()
专栏：

    首都上万工人看梅兰芳的演出
    著名的表演艺术家梅兰芳从八月二十六日到二十九日给首都一万多名工人连续演出四场“宇宙锋”。梅兰芳最近正在赶拍他的舞台生活电影片，他在紧张的工作中挤出时间来向工人们演出，表示他对工人们为实现五年计划紧张而辛苦地劳动的慰问。
    在劳动剧场看戏的观众中，有首都各厂矿的工人，也有乘车从郊区赶来的石景山发电厂和国营北京第一棉纺织厂的工人。
    在第四场演出前，国营北京第一棉纺织厂和北京第一机床厂等单位工人到后台会见了梅兰芳，向他致谢。表演结束时，工人代表又上台将鲜花献给这位人民艺术家。　（新华社）
