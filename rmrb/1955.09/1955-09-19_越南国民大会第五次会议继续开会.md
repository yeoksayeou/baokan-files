Title: The Fifth Session of the Vietnam National Assembly Continues

Author: 
Date: 1955-09-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 18th. The Fifth Session of the Vietnam National Assembly held group discussions on the 16th and 17th regarding Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong's report and reports on the national flag, national anthem, and national emblem. The two subcommittees also heard and discussed draft reports from the groups. The representatives provided extensive opinions to the subcommittees. On the 18th, the session held a special meeting to hear the answers of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to some of the questions raised by the representatives. President Ho Chi Minh expressed his regards to the representatives on the 17th and learned about their work.

### 越南国民大会第五次会议继续开会

1955-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

    越南国民大会第五次会议继续开会
    新华社河内十八日电　越南国民大会第五次会议在十六、十七两日分小组讨论了范文同副总理的报告和关于国旗、国歌、国徽的报告。两个小组委员会还听取和讨论了小组的报告草案。各代表向小组委员会提供了广泛的意见。十八日，会议举行特别会议，听取了越南民主共和国政府对代表们提出的一些问题的解答。胡志明主席曾在十七日向代表们表示慰问并且了解了代表们的工作情况。
