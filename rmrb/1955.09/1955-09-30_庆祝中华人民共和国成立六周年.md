Title: Celebrating the 6th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1955-09-30
Page: 4

    Ambassador Liu Xiao Holds Film Reception
    To celebrate the sixth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Liu Xiao, held a film reception in Moscow on the evening of September 27th, inviting people from all walks of life in Moscow. The reception featured the screening of four color films: "Qingdao International Children's Summer Camp," "Treasure Hunt in the Deep Mountains," "Folk Arts and Crafts," and "The Glorious Fifth Anniversary."
    Those attending the film reception included diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union, as well as staff members from various embassies and legations.
    Also present were responsible staff members from the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, as well as some Soviet and foreign journalists.
    The screened films were warmly welcomed by the audience.
    Korean People Congratulate the Volunteers on National Day
    A delegation of people from all walks of life in Korea, headed by Chen Banxiu, the Minister of Trade of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, departed from Pyongyang on the 29th to the headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army to congratulate the commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on the sixth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, and to express their regards.
    Accompanying the delegation was an art troupe composed of actors from the Korean National Classical Art Theatre.
    Romanian Foreign Cultural Association Arranges Many Activities
    To celebrate the sixth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Romanian Foreign Cultural Association has arranged a series of celebration activities in Bucharest.
    During the Chinese National Day, a Chinese film exhibition will be held, with "Reconnaissance Across the River" as the first film to be screened.
    The "Exhibition of Woodcuts from the People's Republic of China" will open in a hall of the Romanian People's Republic Art Museum. In addition, some gatherings have been arranged to celebrate the sixth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Balaceanu, Chairman of the Bucharest Association of Legal Workers and Member of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, will give a report entitled "The People's Republic of China - A World Power," and the well-known political commentator and university professor Vitner will talk about his impressions of traveling in the People's Republic of China in a report entitled "Man and Rivers."
                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 庆祝中华人民共和国成立六周年

1955-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝中华人民共和国成立六周年
    刘晓大使举行电影招待会
    为庆祝中华人民共和国成立六周年，中国驻苏联大使刘晓于九月二十七日晚在莫斯科举行电影招待会，招待莫斯科各界人士。会上放映了“青岛国际少年儿童夏令营”、“深山探宝”、“民间美术工艺”和“光辉的五周年”四部彩色影片。
    出席电影招待会的有各国驻苏联的外交使节，各国大使馆和公使馆的工作人员。
    出席观看的还有苏联外交部和苏联对外文化协会的负责工作人员以及苏联和外国的一些新闻记者。
    放映的影片受到观众的热烈欢迎。
    朝鲜人民向志愿军祝贺国庆
    以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国贸易相陈班秀为首的朝鲜各界人民代表团二十九日从平壤出发到中国人民志愿军领导机关的驻地，向中国人民志愿军的指挥员和战斗员祝贺中华人民共和国成立六周年，并且进行慰问。
    随同代表团出发的有由朝鲜国立古典艺术剧场的演员们组成的艺术队。
    罗对外文协安排了许多活动
    为了庆祝中华人民共和国成立六周年，罗马尼亚对外文化协会在布加勒斯特安排了一系列的庆祝活动。
    在中国国庆节期间将举办中国电影展览，放映的第一部影片是“渡江侦察记”。
    “中华人民共和国木刻展览会”将在罗马尼亚人民共和国艺术博物馆的一个大厅中开幕。此外，还布置了一些集会来庆祝中华人民共和国成立六周年。布加勒斯特法律工作者协会主席、罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会议员巴拉契亚努将作题为“中华人民共和国——一个世界大国”的报告，著名的政论家、大学教授维特纳将在题为“人与河流”的报告中谈他在中华人民共和国旅行的观感。
                                  （新华社）
