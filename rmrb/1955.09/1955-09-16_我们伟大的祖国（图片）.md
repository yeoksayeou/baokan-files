    Our Great Motherland
    Lhasa Primary School is the primary school with the largest number of Tibetan students and the most complete equipment. When the school was established in 1952, the number of students was nearly 300, and by May of this year it had increased to more than 1,020. This is a picture of the students practicing sketching in front of the Potala Palace.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    拉萨小学是西藏人数最多、设备比较完善的一所小学。一九五二年学校建立时学生人数近三百名，到今年五月已增加到一千零二十多名。这是学生们正在布达拉宫前练习写生。
