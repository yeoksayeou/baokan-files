Title: Wuhan Heavy Machine Tool Plant Levels Plant Site
Author:
Date: 1955-09-07
Page: 1

    Wuhan Heavy Machine Tool Plant Levels Plant Site
    The Wuhan Heavy Machine Tool Plant began leveling its plant site on the 2nd. From this point on, this large-scale machinery factory has moved from the preparatory stage to the construction preparation phase.
    According to the planned schedule, the Wuhan Heavy Machine Tool Plant will complete about 40% of the total plant site leveling work this year. Before starting the leveling work, the plant drafted three construction plans for comparison, and finally determined the most economical and reasonable one; the department responsible for the construction sent people to the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge Engineering Bureau and other units to learn advanced construction experience, thereby reducing the project cost. In this way, the Wuhan Heavy Machine Tool Plant saved the state more than 300,000 yuan in investment from this year's construction projects.
    The Wuhan Heavy Machine Tool Plant is a highly automated machinery factory. After the factory is completed, it will produce many heavy-duty machine tools with excellent performance and wide range of uses. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 武汉重型工具机厂平整厂基

1955-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    武汉重型工具机厂平整厂基
    武汉重型工具机厂已在二日开始平整厂基。从此，这座规模巨大的机械工厂就由筹建阶段进入了施工准备时期。
    按照预订计划，武汉重型工具机厂今年将要完成厂基平整工作总量的百分之四十左右。开始平整工作以前，这个厂曾拟订了三个施工方案进行比较，最后确定了其中最为经济合理的一个；担任施工的部门，曾派人到武汉长江大桥工程局等单位学习先进的施工经验，因而减低了工程造价。这样，就使武汉重型工具机厂从今年的施工项目中，为国家节省了三十多万元投资。
    武汉重型工具机厂是一座高度自动化的机械工厂。这座工厂建成后，将生产许多性能优良、用途广泛的重型机具。　（新华社）
