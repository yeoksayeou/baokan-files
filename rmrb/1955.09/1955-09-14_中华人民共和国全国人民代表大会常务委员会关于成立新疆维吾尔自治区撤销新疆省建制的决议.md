Title: Resolution of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on the Establishment of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region and the Abolition of the Xinjiang Province

Author:
Date: 1955-09-14
Page: 1

Resolution of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on the Establishment of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region and the Abolition of the Xinjiang Province

(Adopted at the 21st Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on September 13, 1955)

The 21st meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on September 13, 1955, resolved: To approve the proposal submitted by Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council to establish the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, abolish the Xinjiang Province, and designate the original administrative area of Xinjiang Province as the administrative area of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region.

### 中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于成立新疆维吾尔自治区撤销新疆省建制的决议

1955-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于成立新疆维吾尔自治区撤销新疆省建制的决议
    （一九五五年九月十三日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十一次会议通过）
    一九五五年九月十三日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十一次会议决议：批准国务院周恩来总理提出的议案，成立新疆维吾尔自治区，撤销新疆省建制，并以原新疆省的行政区域为新疆维吾尔自治区的行政区域。
