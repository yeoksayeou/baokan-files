Author:
Date: 1955-09-14
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In order to meet the needs of industrial construction and the working people, Chongqing is improving its urban transportation facilities. The Chongqing Tram Company Preparatory Office has begun installing trolleybus lines on the section from Shangqingsi to Xiaoshizi. This is a scene of the overhead engineering team installing contact wire clamps on a wire-stringing vehicle.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了满足工业建设和劳动人民的需要，重庆市正在改进城市的交通设施。重庆市电车公司筹备处已开始在上清寺至小什字一段安装无轨电车线路工程。这是架空工程队的工人在架线高车上安装触线夹板的情形。
