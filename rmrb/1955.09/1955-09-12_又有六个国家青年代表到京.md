Title: Another Six Countries' Youth Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 9th – Twenty-one Indonesian youth representatives arrived in Beijing by international train on the evening of the 9th. Some of them were visiting China at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth after participating in the Fifth World Youth Festival, while others were passing through China on their way home.

Prior to this, eight youth representatives from Egypt, Vietnam, Bolivia, Colombia, and Guatemala arrived in Beijing on the 5th, 7th, and 8th, respectively.

### 又有六个国家青年代表到京

1955-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    又有六个国家青年代表到京
    新华社九日讯　印度尼西亚青年代表二十一人，在九日晚乘国际列车到达北京。他们中有在参加第五届世界青年联欢节后，应中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会和中华全国民主青年联合会的邀请来中国访问的，也有经过中国返国的。
    在此以前，还有埃及、越南、玻利维亚、哥伦比亚和危地马拉等国青年代表八人，分别在五日、七日和八日来到北京。
