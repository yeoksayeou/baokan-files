Title: Tunisian Goodwill Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-09-07
Page: 1

    Tunisian Goodwill Delegation Leaves Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, September 6th. The Tunisian Goodwill Delegation left Beijing on the evening of September 6th to visit the Northeast and East China regions.
    During their stay in Beijing, the delegation visited the Drawnwork Production Cooperative, Peking University, the Central Institute for Nationalities, mosques, the state-owned Southern Suburbs Farm, and the Guanting Reservoir. They also held seminars with Chinese representatives from various sectors on issues related to youth, women, trade unions, politics and law, culture and education, agriculture, and finance and economics.
    After visiting the mosque and the Institute for Nationalities, delegation leader Alaya said: "Now I have personally seen that the People's China has complete freedom of religious belief. The People's Government's policy of uniting people of all ethnic groups to build China together is very correct."
    Before leaving Beijing, delegates Shakron and Belaluna, at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, gave a report on the situation in Tunisia.

### 突尼斯访华友好代表团离京

1955-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    突尼斯访华友好代表团离京
    新华社六日讯　突尼斯访华友好代表团在九月六日晚离京去东北、华东等地访问。
    代表团在京期间，参观了挑花生产合作社、北京大学、中央民族学院、清真寺、国营南郊农场和官厅水库，并同中国各界举行了有关青年、妇女、工会、政法、文教、农业和财经等问题的座谈会。
    团长阿莱亚参观清真寺和民族学院后说：“现在我亲眼看见了人民中国是完全有宗教信仰自由的。人民政府团结各民族人民共同建设中国的政策是非常正确的。”
    代表沙克隆和贝拉卢纳离京前，曾应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请，作了关于突尼斯情况的报告。
