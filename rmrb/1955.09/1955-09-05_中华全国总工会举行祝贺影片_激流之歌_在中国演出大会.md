Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Holds Celebration for the Film "Song of the Torrent" Performing in China
Author:
Date: 1955-09-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 4th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions held a celebration in Beijing on the evening of September 4th for the documentary film "Song of the Torrent" performing in China. The film "Song of the Torrent" records the Third World Trade Union Congress and the struggles of the working class and the broad masses of working people of various countries to defend peace and strive for freedom and survival. It won the "Struggle for a Better World Award" at the 8th International Film Festival.

More than 1,200 people attended the conference, including Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, representatives from central government agencies, film workers, and well-known film actors.

Representatives from the Central Council of Trade Unions of the Soviet Union, the All India Trade Union Congress, the Indonesian General Labour Organization, and the Liaison Bureau for Asia and Australia of the All-Japan Industrial Unions Conference to the World Federation of Trade Unions were invited to attend the conference.

Lai Ruoyu spoke at the meeting. He said that the film "Song of the Torrent" will enable the liberated Chinese working class to more clearly recognize the important responsibilities they bear in the struggle to defend world peace and strive for the unity and unification of the international workers' movement. Lai Ruoyu expressed the hope that through the screening and promotion of this film, the work enthusiasm and political vigilance of the broad masses of Chinese workers and laborers will be further enhanced, so as to guarantee the completion and over-completion of the first five-year plan.

Xia Yan also spoke at the meeting. He paid tribute to all the staff of the film, headed by Joris Ivens, a well-known Dutch film director and winner of the International Peace Prize, and congratulated the successful public release of the film in China on behalf of the Ministry of Culture.

After the speeches, a congratulatory telegram from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to the World Federation of Trade Unions and the film's director, Joris Ivens, and all the staff was read out. Then, the documentary film "Song of the Torrent," with Chinese narration, was screened.

### 中华全国总工会举行祝贺影片“激流之歌”在中国演出大会

1955-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国总工会举行祝贺影片“激流之歌”在中国演出大会
    新华社四日讯　中华全国总工会四日晚在北京举行祝贺纪录片“激流之歌”在中国演出大会。影片“激流之歌”纪录了世界工会第三次代表大会以及各国工人阶级和广大劳动人民为保卫和平，争取自由、生存而斗争的情况，曾在第八届国际电影节上获得“为美好世界而斗争奖”。
    参加大会的有全国总工会主席赖若愚，中华人民共和国文化部副部长夏衍以及中央各机关的代表、电影工作者和著名电影演员等共一千二百多人。
    全苏工会中央理事会、全印总工会、印度尼西亚总工会、全日本产业别工会会议驻世界工联亚澳联络局的代表应邀出席了大会。
    赖若愚在会上讲了话。他说，影片“激流之歌”将使解放了的中国工人阶级更清楚地认识到他们在保卫世界和平和争取国际工人运动的团结与统一的斗争中所担负的重大责任。赖若愚希望通过这部影片的放映和宣传，进一步提高中国广大职工和劳动人民的劳动热情和政治警惕性，以保证第一个五年计划的完成和超额完成。
    会上，夏衍也讲了话。他向以国际和平奖金获得者、荷兰著名电影导演约里斯·伊文思为首的本片全体工作人员致敬，并代表文化部祝贺这部影片在中国公映的成功。
    讲话结束后，宣读了中华全国总工会致世界工联和本片导演约里斯·伊文思及全体工作人员的贺电，接着，放映了用中文解说的纪录片“激流之歌”。
