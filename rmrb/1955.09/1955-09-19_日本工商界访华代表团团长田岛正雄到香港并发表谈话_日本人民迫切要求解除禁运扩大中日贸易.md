Title: Japanese Business Delegation Head Masao Tajima Arrives in Hong Kong and Issues Statement: Japanese People Urgently Demand Lifting of Embargo and Expansion of Sino-Japanese Trade
Author:
Date: 1955-09-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 18th – According to reports in the Hong Kong newspapers “Ta Kung Pao” and “Wen Wei Po” on the 17th, Masao Tajima, head of the Japanese business delegation to China and vice president of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, arrived in Hong Kong from Tokyo on the 16th.

    At Hong Kong airport, Masao Tajima told the press: "The embargo has adversely affected Japanese industry. The production of Japanese industries such as building steel, shipbuilding, and railway equipment has been hit by the embargo. We are doing everything we can to demand the lifting of the embargo. The Japanese people are urgently demanding the lifting of the embargo."

    Masao Tajima said: "China is a great country with a population of 600 million. We know that New China has made great progress and the purchasing power of the people has increased. We have great expectations for the large Chinese market." He said that we have the conviction that trade can be expanded in the future, and we are willing to make the greatest efforts. He also said: "Trade between China and Japan will definitely be beneficial to the people of both countries."

### 日本工商界访华代表团团长田岛正雄到香港并发表谈话  日本人民迫切要求解除禁运扩大中日贸易

1955-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    日本工商界访华代表团团长田岛正雄到香港并发表谈话
    日本人民迫切要求解除禁运扩大中日贸易
    新华社广州十八日电　据香港“大公报”和“文汇报”十七日报道，日本工商界访华代表团团长、日本国际贸易促进协会副会长田岛正雄在十六日从东京到达香港。
    田岛正雄在香港机场对报界发表谈话说：“禁运使日本工业受到不良影响，像日本的建筑用钢、造船、铁路器材等工业部门的生产，就因禁运而受打击。我们正在尽我们一切力量要求解除禁运。日本人民都迫切要求解除禁运。”
    田岛正雄说：“中国是一个拥有六亿人口的伟大国家，我们知道新中国已有了巨大的进展，人民购买力已经提高，我们对中国这个大市场的期望很大。”他说，我们怀着今后必可扩大贸易的信念，愿尽最大努力。他又说：“中日之间的贸易，必然是对两国人民都有利的。”
