Title: The Third Session of the Beijing Municipal People's Congress Closes

Author:
Date: 1955-09-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

    The Third Session of the First Beijing Municipal People's Congress closed on the 21st.
    On the afternoon of the 21st, after Mayor Peng Zhen spoke on issues such as the budget, final accounts, and grain work, the meeting unanimously adopted the resolution of the Third Session of the First Beijing Municipal People's Congress regarding the final accounts of Beijing's financial revenue and expenditure for 1954 and the budget for 1955. The meeting unanimously agreed that the execution of Beijing's financial revenue and expenditure in 1954 was basically good; Beijing's financial revenue and expenditure budget for 1955 embodies the principle of increasing production and practicing strict economy, and conforms to the state's requirements for developing the national economy and the actual situation of the city. The meeting approved Beijing's final financial revenue and expenditure accounts for 1954 and the budget for 1955, and instructed the Beijing Municipal People's Government and its subordinate units to strive to increase production, practice strict economy, oppose waste, and struggle to achieve the victorious completion of Beijing's various work tasks for 1955.
    The meeting also adopted the report of the Proposal Examination Committee on the examination of proposals.
    At the meetings on the 20th and 21st, a total of thirty-eight representatives and responsible persons from various departments of government agencies spoke. The speech by representative Jia Tingsan, Vice Mayor of Beijing, explained that Beijing's industrial production has achieved great success in implementing the policy of increasing production and practicing strict economy. The total output value of state-owned, locally state-owned, cooperative-run, and joint state-private industrial and mining enterprises in the city completed 111.6 percent of the state plan in 1954. Costs were reduced by 5.4 percent compared to 1953. In the first half of 1955, the total output value completed 105.1 percent of the state plan, completing 50.6 percent of the annual plan. Costs were reduced by 3.2 percent compared to 1954. According to preliminary statistics, the city's industrial and mining enterprises and transportation industries alone can increase production and save more than 56 million yuan for the state in addition to completing the state plan throughout the year. Representative Zhao Fan, Director of the Agriculture, Forestry and Water Conservancy Office of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee, pointed out in his speech that the number of agricultural production cooperatives in the suburbs of Beijing has increased from 412 last year to 701 this year; the number of peasant households joining the cooperatives has increased from 8 percent to 46 percent of the total number of peasant households. At present, districts in the suburbs have carried out preliminary planning or are in the process of planning for the development of agricultural production cooperatives. This winter and next spring, the number of peasant households joining the cooperatives in the suburbs will generally increase to about 60 percent of the total number of peasant households. Representative Pu Jiexun, Director of the Beijing Grain Bureau, talked about the issue of grain conservation in his speech. He said that this year, various departments of the Grain Bureau have launched a comprehensive conservation campaign, saving more than 9.8 million jin of grain from January to July. During the same period, flour mills saved more than 4.8 million jin of wheat by increasing the flour extraction rate. At present, the staff and workers of the flour mills have expressed their determination to strive to save another 2 million jin of grain on the basis of completing the original plan to save 4.4 million jin of grain, while ensuring safety and quality.

### 北京市人民代表大会第三次会议闭幕

1955-09-23
第3版()
专栏：

    北京市人民代表大会第三次会议闭幕
    北京市第一届人民代表大会第三次会议在二十一日闭幕。
    二十一日下午，市长彭真就预决算、粮食工作等问题发言后，会议最后一致通过了北京市第一届人民代表大会第三次会议关于北京市一九五四年财政收支决算和一九五五年财政收支预算的决议。会议一致认为，北京市一九五四年财政收支的执行情况基本上是良好的；北京市一九五五年财政收支预算体现了增加生产、厉行节约的方针，符合于国家发展国民经济的要求和本市的实际情况。会议批准北京市一九五四年财政收支决算和一九五五年财政收支预算，并责成北京市人民委员会和其所属各单位努力增加生产，厉行节约，反对浪费，为争取胜利完成北京市一九五五年各项工作任务而奋斗。
    会议还通过了提案审查委员会关于提案审查的报告。
    在二十、二十一两日的会议上，共有三十八位代表和政府机关各部门负责人发了言。代表、北京市副市长贾庭三的发言，说明北京市的工业生产在贯彻增加生产、厉行节约的方针中已经取得了很大成绩。全市国营、地方国营、合作社营和公私合营工矿企业的生产总值，一九五四年度完成了国家计划百分之一百一十一点六。成本比一九五三年降低了百分之五点四。一九五五年上半年生产总值完成了国家计划百分之一百零五点一，完成全年计划百分之五十点六。成本比一九五四年降低了百分之三点二。据初步统计，全市仅工矿企业、交通运输业全年在完成国家计划以外，还可为国家增产节约五千六百多万元。代表、北京市人民委员会农林水利办公室主任赵凡在发言中指出：北京市郊区农业生产合作社从去年的四百一十二个发展到今年的七百零一个；入社农户由占总农户的百分之八发展到百分之四十六。目前，郊区各区对发展农业生产合作社已进行了初步规划或正在进行规划，今冬明春郊区入社农户大体上要发展到占总农户百分之六十左右。代表、北京市粮食局长浦洁修在发言中谈到了粮食节约问题，他说今年粮食局所属各部门开展了全面节约运动，一月到七月就节约了粮食九百八十多万斤。在同一时期，各面粉厂由于提高出粉率节约小麦四百八十多万斤。目前，各面粉厂职工表示在保证安全和质量的前提下，争取在完成原节约粮食四百四十万斤计划基础上，再节约二百万斤。
                                    （新华社）
