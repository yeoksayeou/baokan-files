Title: Preparatory Office of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region Established
Author:
Date: 1955-09-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, September 22nd. The Preparatory Office of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region was formally established in Lhasa on September 20th. The office is headed by Kalon Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme (Tibetan) of the Tibetan local government, with the Deputy Directors being the Acting Chikyi Khenpo Lobsang Sandan (Tibetan), Rhenshi Danlung Tsering Paljor (Tibetan) of the Panchen Kanpo Lija Office, Ponda Dorje (Tibetan), Deputy Director of the Qamdo District People's Liberation Committee, and Chen Jingbo (Han).

On the afternoon of the same day, representatives from all circles in Lhasa held a celebration meeting for the establishment of the Preparatory Office of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region. Participants included monks and lay officials of the Tibetan local government in Lhasa, representatives from the three major monasteries of Ganden, Sera, and Drepung, as well as representatives from the Lhasa Patriotic Youth Cultural Association and the Patriotic Women's Federation. Responsible persons from various departments of the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Party of China and representatives from the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army also attended the meeting to congratulate them.

Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme reported on the establishment process of the Preparatory Office of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region at the meeting. He said: The establishment of the Preparatory Office is a necessary work before the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region. The establishment of the Preparatory Office is to make the work of establishing the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region proceed more smoothly.

Fan Ming, Deputy Secretary of the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, spoke at the meeting. He said: The establishment of the Preparatory Office is a gratifying event in Tibet's work. Fan Ming, on behalf of the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Tibet Military Region, and all personnel stationed in Tibet, extended warm congratulations on the establishment of the Preparatory Office of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and wished the Preparatory Office a smooth start in its work and strive for the early establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region.

Danlung Tsering Paljor and Ponda Dorje also spoke at the meeting, saying that the establishment of the Preparatory Office is a joyous event for the people of Tibet and expressed their willingness to do a good job and strive for the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region as soon as possible.

### 西藏自治区筹委会筹备处成立

1955-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    西藏自治区筹委会筹备处成立
    新华社拉萨二十二日电西藏自治区筹备委员会筹备处九月二十日在拉萨正式成立。筹备处由西藏地方政府噶伦阿沛·阿旺晋美（藏族）任处长，副处长分别由代理机巧堪布洛桑三旦（藏族）、班禅堪布会议厅委员会仁希丹伦·策仁班觉（藏族）、昌都地区人民解放委员会副主任邦达多吉（藏族）和陈竞波（汉族）担任。
    当天下午，拉萨市各界代表举行了西藏自治区筹备委员会筹备处成立庆祝会，参加庆祝会的有西藏地方政府在拉萨的僧俗官员，噶丹、色拉、哲蚌三大寺代表以及拉萨市爱国青年文化联谊会和爱国妇女联谊会的代表。中共西藏工作委员会各部门负责人和中国人民解放军西藏军区代表也到会庆贺。
    阿沛·阿旺晋美在会上报告了西藏自治区筹备委员会筹备处成立的经过，他说：筹备处的成立是西藏自治区筹备委员会成立前的一项必要工作。成立筹备处是为了使成立西藏自治区筹备委员会的工作更顺利地进行。
    中共西藏工委副书记、中国人民解放军西藏军区副政治委员范明在会上讲话。他说：筹备处的成立是西藏工作中一件可喜的事情。范明并代表中共西藏工委、西藏军区和全体进藏工作人员，对西藏自治区筹备委员会筹备处的成立，致以热烈祝贺，并预祝筹备处的工作顺利开展，争取西藏自治区筹备委员会早日成立。
    丹伦·策仁班觉和邦达多吉也在会上讲了话，他们都说筹备处的成立是西藏人民的喜事，表示要做好工作，争取西藏自治区筹备委员会尽快成立。
