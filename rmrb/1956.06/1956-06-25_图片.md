Title: Wheat in Beijing Suburbs Universally Begins Harvest

Author: 
Date: 1956-06-25
Page: 1

Wheat in the suburbs of Beijing has universally begun to be harvested. The Beijing Agricultural Machine Tractor Station is harvesting wheat for the Tiancun Agricultural Production Cooperative in the Shijingshan District. This cooperative has planted 2,300 mu of wheat this year, an increase of 14% over last year.
