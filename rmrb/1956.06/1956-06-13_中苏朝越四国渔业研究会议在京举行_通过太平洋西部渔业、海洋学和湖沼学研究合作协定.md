Title: China, Soviet Union, North Korea, and Vietnam Hold Fishery Research Conference in Beijing; Agreement on Cooperation in Fisheries, Oceanography, and Limnology Research in the Western Pacific Approved
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 12th. A fishery, oceanography, and limnology research conference of China, the Soviet Union, North Korea, and Vietnam was held in Beijing today. The conference discussed and approved the "Agreement on Cooperation in Fisheries, Oceanography, and Limnology Research in the Western Pacific."

Attending the meeting today were the Soviet government delegation headed by Professor Moiseev, Deputy Director of the All-Union Research Institute of Fisheries and Oceanography of the Soviet Ministry of Fisheries Industry and ichthyologist; the North Korean government delegation headed by Kim Jae-pil, Vice-Minister of the Ministry of Fisheries; the Vietnamese government delegation headed by Le Duy Trinh, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Agriculture and Forestry; and the Chinese government delegation headed by Xu Deheng, Minister of the Ministry of Aquatic Products. A total of twenty-eight delegates and experts from various countries attended the meeting.

The meeting was presided over by Xu Deheng, head of the Chinese government delegation.

Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, attended the meeting and delivered a congratulatory speech on behalf of the Chinese people and the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

The meeting started at 9:00 AM today and ended at 12:00 PM.

Since April 28th, the four delegations have held several preparatory meetings to discuss in detail the joint scientific research to be conducted by the four countries in the Western Pacific (including the Sea of Japan, Yellow Sea, East China Sea, and South China Sea) and in the bordering waters connected to the contracting countries. A draft cooperation agreement, a draft charter for the Western Pacific Fisheries Research Committee to be established to implement this agreement, a draft scientific and technological cooperation program, and the agenda for the first meeting of the committee were formulated.

Before the meeting, members of the Soviet, North Korean, and Vietnamese delegations also visited Qingdao, Lushun-Dalian, Wuhan, and other cities, where they gave several academic reports on fisheries, ichthyology, and marine biology; and visited relevant research institutions, schools, enterprises, and fishery production cooperatives.

Xinhua News Agency, June 12th. The "Agreement on Cooperation in Fisheries, Oceanography, and Limnology Research in the Western Pacific" was signed in Beijing this afternoon.

The following signed the agreement: Xu Deheng, Plenipotentiary Representative of the Government of the People's Republic of China and Minister of Aquatic Products; Moiseev, Plenipotentiary Representative of the Government of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and Deputy Director of the All-Union Research Institute of Fisheries and Oceanography; Kim Jae-pil, Plenipotentiary Representative of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Vice-Minister of the Ministry of Fisheries; and Le Duy Trinh, Plenipotentiary Representative of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Agriculture and Forestry.

The Chinese side participating in the signing ceremony included: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Zhang Wentian, Vice-Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Yan Baohang, Director of the Treaty Committee of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and all representatives and experts of the Chinese government delegation attending the meeting: Chen Kangbai, Tong Dizhou, Feng Lejin, Zhu Yuanding, He Chongben, Zeng Chengkui, and Zheng Enshou.

The Soviet side participating in the signing ceremony included: Academician Yudin, Soviet Ambassador to China; Yelemin, Commercial Representative of the Soviet Embassy in China; Sudarikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China; and all representatives and experts of the Soviet government delegation attending the meeting: Isayev, Nikolsky, Pogolov, Panin, Koren, Gracheva, Dobrovolsky, and Gulianova.

The North Korean side participating in the signing ceremony included: Kim Gwi-nam, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the North Korean Embassy in China; Kim Kuk-bo, Commercial Counselor; and the representatives and experts of the North Korean government delegation attending the meeting: Baek Sang and Jang Il-han.

The Vietnamese side participating in the signing ceremony included: Hoang Van Hoan, Vietnamese Ambassador to China; Pham Binh, First Secretary of the Vietnamese Embassy in China; and all representatives and experts of the Vietnamese government delegation attending the meeting: Tran Ngoc Thuan, Nguyen Van Nhu, Phan Huu Doi, Hoang Xuan Hai, and Dien Van Hung. ### 中苏朝越四国渔业研究会议在京举行  通过太平洋西部渔业、海洋学和湖沼学研究合作协定

1956-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏朝越四国渔业研究会议在京举行
    通过太平洋西部渔业、海洋学和湖沼学研究合作协定
    新华社12日讯　中、苏、朝、越四国渔业、海洋学和湖沼学研究会议今天在北京举行。会议讨论并且通过了“太平洋西部渔业、海洋学和湖沼学研究的合作协定”。
    今天出席会议的，有以苏联渔业工业部全苏渔业和海洋学研究所副所长、鱼类学家莫伊谢耶夫教授为首的苏联政府代表团，以朝鲜水产省副相金在弼为首的朝鲜政府代表团，以越南农林部副部长黎维贞为首的越南政府代表团，以中国水产部部长许德珩为首的中国政府代表团。出席会议的各国代表和专家共有二十八人。
    会议由中国政府代表团团长许德珩主持。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、中国科学院院长郭沫若出席了会议，他代表中国人民和中国科学院向会议致祝词。
    会议在今天上午九时开始，到十二时结束。
    自四月二十八日以来，四国代表团举行了多次预备会议，详细讨论了四国在太平洋西部（包括日本海、黄海、东海、南海）以及在缔约各国相连的边境水域中共同进行科学研究的问题，拟订了合作协定草案、为了实现这个协定而将要建立的太平洋西部渔业研究委员会的会章草案、科学技术合作项目草案以及委员会第一次会议议程。
    苏、朝、越三国代表团团员会前还访问了青岛、旅大、武汉等城市，在上述各地作了多次关于渔业、鱼类学和海洋生物学方面的学术报告；参观了有关研究机构、学校、企业和渔业生产合作社等。
    新华社12日讯　“太平洋西部渔业、海洋学和湖沼学研究的合作协定”今天下午在北京签字。
    在协定上签字的有：中华人民共和国政府全权代表水产部部长许德珩，苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府全权代表全苏渔业和海洋学研究所副所长莫伊谢耶夫，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国全权代表水产省副相金在弼，越南民主共和国政府全权代表农林部副部长黎维贞。
    中国方面参加协定签字仪式的有：中国科学院院长郭沫若，外交部副部长张闻天，外交部条约委员会主任委员阎宝航，和出席会议的中国政府代表团全体代表和专家：陈康白、童第周、冯乐进、朱元鼎、赫崇本、曾呈奎、郑恩绶。
    苏联方面参加协定签字仪式的有：苏联驻中国大使尤金院士，苏联驻中国大使馆商务代表叶烈敏、苏联驻中国大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫，和出席会议的苏联政府代表团全体代表和专家：伊萨也夫、尼柯里斯基、波戈罗夫、帕宁、科仁、格拉丘娃、多布罗沃尔斯基、古丽扬诺娃。
    朝鲜方面参加协定签字仪式的有：朝鲜驻中国大使馆临时代办金贵南，商务参赞金国辅，和出席会议的朝鲜政府代表团代表和专家：白爽、张逸韩。
    越南方面参加协定签字仪式的有：越南驻中国大使黄文欢、越南驻中国大使馆一等秘书范平，和出席会议的越南政府代表团全体代表和专家：陈玉善、阮文如、潘友对、黄春海、田文兴。（附图片）
