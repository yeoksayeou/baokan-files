Title: Weather in the Huai River Basin and Other Areas Will Improve; Heavy Rain in the Middle and Lower Reaches of the Yangtze River and Other Areas
Author:
Date: 1956-06-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a rainfall message:

    From June 6th to 11th, there has been widespread heavy rain or torrential rain in the Huai River Basin. From the 12th to the 14th, rainfall will tend to weaken, and the weather will be better.
    On the 9th and 10th, light to moderate rain fell widely in the middle and lower reaches of the Yellow River, southern Hebei, and southern Shanxi. On the 9th, there was also torrential rain in some areas of the middle reaches of the Yellow River. It is estimated that from the 11th, the weather in this area will improve quickly. In the past few days, there will be no more heavy rain in the provinces of North China.
    In addition, in the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River, northern Hunan, northern Jiangxi, southern Anhui, mountainous areas of western Zhejiang, and other areas, from the 12th to the 13th, rainfall will gradually increase to heavy rain or torrential rain.

### 淮河流域等地区天气将转好  长江中下游等地将有大雨

1956-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

    淮河流域等地区天气将转好  
    长江中下游等地将有大雨
    新华社11日讯　中央气象台发布降雨消息：
    自六月六日起到十一日，淮河流域曾经普遍下过大雨或暴雨。自十二日起到十四日这一段时间内，雨量将趋于减弱，天气较好。
    九日和十日，黄河中下游、河北南部、山西南部普遍下了小雨到中雨。九日黄河中游的局部地区并有暴雨。估计从十一日起，这一带天气将很快转好。最近几天，华北各省不致再有大雨出现。
    另外，长江中下游、湖南北部、江西北部、安徽南部、浙江西部山地等地区，从十二日起到十三日为止，雨量将逐渐加大到大雨或暴雨。
