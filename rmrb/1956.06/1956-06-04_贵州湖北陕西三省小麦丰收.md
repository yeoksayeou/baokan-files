Title: Wheat Harvests in Guizhou, Hubei, and Shaanxi Provinces
Author:
Date: 1956-06-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guiyang, 3rd June: Guizhou Province's 1.99 million mu of wheat is nearing completion of harvest. The total wheat harvest of 2,202 agricultural cooperatives in the Duyun Special District is one and a half times greater than last year, with an average yield increase of 33 jin per mu. Xingyi County harvested 10 million jin of wheat this year, equivalent to four times the total output of last year. Luo Dian, Ceheng, Lushan, Huishui and other counties inhabited by ethnic minorities have also achieved good wheat harvests not seen in decades.

This newspaper, Wuhan, 3rd June: The average yield of Hubei Province's 16 million mu of wheat will increase by 20% compared to last year, exceeding the planned output by 24.52%. Wheat in Gucheng, Xishui, Xinzhou, Xianfeng, Xianning and other counties will increase by 30% to over 60% compared to last year. Many areas will also create new records for large-scale high-yield production.

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 3rd June: Wheat harvesting work in various parts of Shaanxi Province has begun successively. At present, the southern Han River basin is entering a stage of intense harvesting, and the counties of Tongguan, Chaoyi, and Huayin, located on the right bank of the Yellow River in the central Guanzhong Plain, have also started harvesting. The total harvest of the province's 24 million mu of wheat is expected to increase by more than 40% compared to the normal harvest year of 1955, and also increase by more than 20% compared to the bumper harvest year of 1954.

### 贵州湖北陕西三省小麦丰收

1956-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

    贵州湖北陕西三省小麦丰收
    据新华社贵阳3日电　贵州省一百九十九万亩小麦快要收割完了。都匀专区2，202个农业社的小麦总收获量比去年多一倍半，每亩平均增产三十三斤。兴义县今年收获小麦一千万斤，相当于去年总产量的四倍。罗甸、册亨、炉山、惠水等少数民族聚居县的小麦，还获得几十年未有的好收成。
    本报武汉3日电　湖北省一千六百万亩小麦平均产量将比去年增产二成，可超额完成计划产量24.52％。谷城、浠水、新洲、咸丰、咸宁等县的小麦将比去年增产三成到六成以上。不少地区还将创造大面积丰产的新纪录。
    据新华社西安3日电　陕西省各地小麦收割工作陆续开始。目前南部汉水流域两岸已经到了紧张的收割阶段，中部八百里秦川靠近黄河右岸的潼关、朝邑、华阴等县也已经开镰。全省两千四百多万亩小麦的总收获量，预计比常年收成的1955年增产四成多，比丰收的1954年也增产两成以上。
