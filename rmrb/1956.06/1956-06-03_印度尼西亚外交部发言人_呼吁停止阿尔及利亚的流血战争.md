Title: Indonesian Foreign Ministry Spokesman Calls for End to Bloody War in Algeria
Author:
Date: 1956-06-03
Page: 4
　
　　Indonesian Foreign Ministry Spokesman

　　Calls for End to Bloody War in Algeria

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 2nd: An Indonesian Foreign Ministry spokesman issued a statement calling on relevant parties to immediately stop the bloody war in Algeria and resolve the Algerian issue.

　　In the statement issued on May 31, the spokesman said that the Indonesian people and government are making this appeal driven by friendly feelings towards the Algerian people and inspired by the spirit of the Bandung Asian-African Conference. He said: "The Indonesian people and government, as part of the peoples of Asia and Africa, feel the greatest sympathy for the Algerian people's struggle for freedom."

　　The spokesman said: The Indonesian government is closely monitoring the development of the national movement for independence in Algeria. The Indonesian people and government believe that "the bloody war in Algeria can be stopped as long as the Algerian people are granted independence."

### 印度尼西亚外交部发言人  呼吁停止阿尔及利亚的流血战争

1956-06-03
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚外交部发言人
    呼吁停止阿尔及利亚的流血战争
    新华社雅加达2日电　印度尼西亚外交部发言人发表谈话，呼吁有关方面立即停止阿尔及利亚的流血战争，解决阿尔及利亚问题。
    发言人在五月三十一日发表的这篇谈话说，印度尼西亚人民和政府发出这个呼吁，是由于受到对阿尔及利亚人民的友好感情的推动和万隆亚非会议的精神的鼓舞。他说：“印度尼西亚人民和政府作为亚非各国人民中的一部分，对阿尔及利亚人民争取自由的斗争感到最大的同情。”
    发言人说：印度尼西亚政府正密切注视着阿尔及利亚国内争取独立的民族运动的发展。印度尼西亚人民和政府认为，“只要给予阿尔及利亚人民以独立，阿尔及利亚的流血战争是可以停止的”。
