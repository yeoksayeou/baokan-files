Title: The Soviet Union Returns Archives Looted by the Nazis to Norway
Author:
Date: 1956-06-15
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow – The Soviet Council of Ministers has decided to return the materials from the private secretariat of Norwegian King Haakon VII to King Haakon VII himself.
　　These materials were discovered by the Soviet Army in German archives during the Second World War.

### 苏联归还挪威被纳粹劫去的档案

1956-06-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联归还挪威被纳粹劫去的档案
    据新华社莫斯科讯　苏联部长会议决定把挪威国王哈康七世私人秘书处的材料，归还哈康七世本人。
    这些材料是苏军在第二次世界大战中从德国档案库中发现的。
