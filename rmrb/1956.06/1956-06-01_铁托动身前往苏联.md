Title: Tito Departs for the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-06-01
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, May 31st: According to TASS, Yugoslav President Tito departed by train on the night of May 30th for an official visit to the Soviet Union.

    Accompanying President Tito and his wife to the Soviet Union were Yugoslav Federal Executive Council Vice President Kardelj, State Secretary for Foreign Affairs Popović, Federal Executive Council member Todorović, Chairman of the Executive Council of the People's Republic of Croatia Blažević, and others.

    Soviet Ambassador to Yugoslavia, Firyubin, accompanied President Tito to the Soviet Union.

### 铁托动身前往苏联

1956-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

    铁托动身前往苏联
    新华社31日讯　据塔斯社贝尔格莱德讯：五月三十日夜间，南斯拉夫总统铁托乘火车动身前往苏联作正式访问。
    随同铁托总统和他的夫人一起去苏联的有南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席卡德尔、外交国务秘书长波波维奇、联邦执行委员会委员托多罗维奇、克罗地亚人民共和国执行委员会主席布拉热维奇等。
    苏联驻南斯拉夫大使费留宾陪同铁托总统前往苏联。
