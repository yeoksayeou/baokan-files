Title: The People's Liberation Army on the Fujian Front Actively Supports the Agricultural Cooperative Movement in Coastal Areas

Author:

Date: 1956-06-05
Page: 1

The People's Liberation Army on the Fujian Front actively supports the agricultural cooperative movement in coastal areas. They have organized "Soldier Rural Work Teams" to assist newly established agricultural cooperatives in their production. Local staff and comrades from the work teams are studying seed selection.
