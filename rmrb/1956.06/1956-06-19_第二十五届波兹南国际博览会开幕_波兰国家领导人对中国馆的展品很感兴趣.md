Title: The 25th Poznań International Fair Opens; Polish National Leaders Express Great Interest in Exhibits at the Chinese Pavilion
Author:
Date: 1956-06-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, June 17th: The 25th Poznań International Fair officially opened this morning after being inaugurated by the Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz.

A total of thirty-five countries' trade organizations and business figures are participating in this year's fair, making it the event with the most participating nations in its history.

The fair's exhibition area covers 90,000 square meters, with the Polish pavilion occupying approximately half of the space.

This is China's second time participating in the Poznań International Fair. The Chinese pavilion has an exhibition area of 3,800 square meters, showcasing goods weighing over 3,200 tons.

The Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz, and First Deputy Chairman Nowak, accompanied by the Chinese Ambassador to Poland, Wang Bingnan, visited the Chinese pavilion at the Poznań International Fair today. After the visit, Cyrankiewicz wrote the following inscription in the guestbook:

"The Chinese Exhibition Hall has made a profound impression on us. We are particularly pleased that, in addition to seeing traditional Chinese products, we have also witnessed the achievements of China's machine industry in a series of new and complex machines. This is a sign of the rapid progress of the People's China.

"Congratulations and greetings to our Chinese friends. Thank you for exhibiting such brilliant products at this fair."

### 第二十五届波兹南国际博览会开幕  波兰国家领导人对中国馆的展品很感兴趣

1956-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

    第二十五届波兹南国际博览会开幕
    波兰国家领导人对中国馆的展品很感兴趣
    据新华社华沙17日电　第二十五届波兹南国际博览会今天上午由波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹剪彩后，正式开幕了。
    参加这一届博览会的共有三十五个国家的贸易组织和商业界人士，是历届博览会参加国家最多的一次。
    博览会的展览面积共达九万平方公尺，其中波兰馆的展览面积约占一半。
    中国是第二次参加波兹南国际博览会。中国馆的展览面积有三千八百平方公尺，展出的货品共重三千二百多吨。
    波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹和第一副主席诺瓦克，在中国驻波兰大使王炳南陪同下，今天参观了波兹南国际博览会的中国馆。在参观以后，西伦凯维兹在留言簿上题词如下：
    “中国展览馆给了我们很深刻的印象，特别使我们高兴的是，除了看到传统的中国产品以外，我们还从一系列的新式的复杂机器中，看到了中国机器工业的发展成就。这是人民中国飞速前进的标志。
    “祝贺并问候我们的中国朋友。感谢你们在这届博览会上展出如此光辉的产品。”
