Title: Cai Shufan Holds Banquet to Bid Farewell to French Labor Federation Basketball Team
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th: Cai Shufan, Deputy Director of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 25th to bid farewell to all members of the French Labor Sports Federation men's and women's basketball teams.

At the banquet, Cai Shufan expressed his gratitude to the French Labor Federation men's and women's basketball teams for their contribution to enhancing the friendship between the Chinese and French people. He said that the excellent basketball skills and good sportsmanship of the French Labor Federation men's and women's basketball teams have left a deep impression on the Chinese audience.

Rousseau, the leader of the French Labor Federation men's and women's basketball teams, spoke at the banquet. He said that their life after arriving in China was arranged as if they were at home. They received a warm welcome everywhere when they visited factories and rural areas. He said that after returning to France, they must convey the profound friendship of the Chinese people to the French people.

After the banquet, Rong Gaotang, on behalf of the All-China Sports Federation, presented gifts including news documentaries of the Labor Federation team's activities in Beijing to the French Labor Federation team. Rousseau, on behalf of the French Labor Federation team, also presented gifts to the hosts.

Xinhua News Agency, June 26th: On the morning of the 26th, Chinese athletes braved the rain at the airport to bid farewell to all members of the French Labor Sports Federation men's and women's basketball teams.

Odette Rogge, a member of the French Labor Federation women's basketball team who participated in the 1954 World Basketball Championship, told Xinhua News Agency reporters that during their stay in China, one member of the French Labor Federation women's and men's basketball teams took turns keeping a diary each day. They plan to print the diary after returning to France so that more French people can see it. They also plan to tell their friends about what they saw and heard in China in various ways to help them understand New China.

### 蔡树藩举行宴会  欢送法国劳联篮球队

1956-06-27
第3版()
专栏：

    蔡树藩举行宴会
    欢送法国劳联篮球队
    据新华社25日讯　体育运动委员会副主任蔡树藩二十五日晚上设宴欢送法国劳动体育联合会男女篮球队全体人员。
    蔡树藩在宴会上对法国劳联男女篮球队在增进中、法两国人民之间的友谊所作的贡献表示感谢。他说，法国劳联男女篮球队优秀的篮球技术和良好的体育道德作风已经给中国观众留下深刻的印象。
    法国劳联男女篮球队领队卢梭在宴会上讲了话。他说，他们到达中国后的生活被安排得像在自己家里一样。他们在工厂、农村访问的时候，到处受到热烈的欢迎。他说，他们回国以后一定要把中国人民的深厚的友谊转达给法国人民。
    宴会以后，荣高棠代表中华全国体育总会把包括劳联队在北京活动的新闻纪录片等礼物赠送给法国劳联队。卢梭代表法国劳联队也向主人们赠送了礼品。
    据新华社26日讯　二十六日早晨，中国运动员在飞机场上冒雨送别了法国劳动体育联合会男女篮球队全体人员。
    参加过1954年世界篮球锦标赛的法国劳联女子篮球队队员奥台特·罗格告诉新华社记者说，法国劳联女子篮球队和男子篮球队在中国期间，每天分别有一个人轮流记日记。他们准备回国以后把日记印出来，让更多的法国人看到它。他们还准备通过各种方式，把在中国所看到的和听到的情况告诉他们的朋友，帮助他们了解新中国。
