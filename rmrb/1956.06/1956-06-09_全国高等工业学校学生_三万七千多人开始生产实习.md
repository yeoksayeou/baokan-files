Title: More than 37,000 Students from National Higher Industrial Schools Begin Production Internships
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: More than 7,000 students from civil engineering and water conservancy related higher industrial schools across the country began their production internships on the 8th. Starting in June, a total of more than 37,700 students from various majors in higher industrial schools will begin their production internships successively.

### 全国高等工业学校学生  三万七千多人开始生产实习

1956-06-09
第3版()
专栏：

    全国高等工业学校学生
    三万七千多人开始生产实习
    新华社讯　全国七千多名土木建筑、水利类的高等工业学校学生，八日开始生产实习。 从六月份起，陆续开始生产实习的高等工业学校各类专业的学生共有三万七千七百多名。
