Title: Bulgaria Begins Construction of Atomic Energy Science Center
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, June 3rd (via TASS): Bulgaria has begun construction of an atomic energy science experimental center. This project is being carried out with Soviet assistance. An atomic reactor, a cyclotron, and a physics institute will be installed at the Bulgarian Atomic Energy Center. The Soviet Union is supplying Bulgaria with an atomic reactor and an elementary particle accelerator.

　　The Soviet Union has already handed over the necessary scientific and technical design documents to Bulgaria. Soviet experts will also assist in the installation of the equipment at the atomic energy center in practical work.

### 保开始建筑原子能科学中心

1956-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    保开始建筑原子能科学中心
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社索非亚3日讯：保加利亚已经开始建筑原子能科学实验中心。这项工程是在苏联帮助下进行的。在保加利亚原子能中心将安装一个原子反应堆、一个回旋加速器和设立一个物理研究所。苏联供给保加利亚一个原子反应堆和一个基本粒子加速器。
    苏联已经把必要的科学技术设计书交给保加利亚。苏联专家还将在实际工作中帮助安装原子能中心的设备。
