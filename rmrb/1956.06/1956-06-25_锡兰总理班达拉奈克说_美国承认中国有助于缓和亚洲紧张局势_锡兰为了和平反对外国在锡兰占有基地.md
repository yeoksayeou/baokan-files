Title: Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike Says US Recognition of China Helps Ease Asian Tensions; Ceylon Opposes Foreign Bases on its Territory for Peace
Author:
Date: 1956-06-25
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, June 24th: Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike said on June 23rd that US recognition of China would help ease tensions in Asia. He does not believe that China is "a threat to the region" and said that Ceylon would support China's entry into the United Nations.
    The Prime Minister, en route to London to attend the Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference, reiterated Ceylon's policy of "non-alignment" when speaking to reporters here. He said: "Today, to defend world peace, it is not necessary to belong to this or that group of countries."
    Bandaranaike said: "Any foreign continued occupation of bases in Ceylon is incompatible with Ceylon's status of not participating in any national bloc." He expressed the hope that the issue of British naval bases in Ceylon would be resolved during the London Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference. He reiterated that Ceylon would become a republic within the Commonwealth.

### 锡兰总理班达拉奈克说  美国承认中国有助于缓和亚洲紧张局势  锡兰为了和平反对外国在锡兰占有基地

1956-06-25
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰总理班达拉奈克说
    美国承认中国有助于缓和亚洲紧张局势
    锡兰为了和平反对外国在锡兰占有基地
    新华社喀喇蚩24日电　锡兰总理班达拉奈克六月二十三日说：美国承认中国会有助于缓和亚洲的紧张局势。他不认为中国是“对这个地区的威胁”，并且说锡兰将支持中国进入联合国。
    这位总理在前往伦敦出席英联邦总理会议的途中在这里对记者们发表谈话的时候再次强调锡兰“不参加集团”的政策。他说：“今天为了保卫世界和平，没有必要属于这一个或者那一个国家集团。”
    班达拉奈克说：“任何外国在锡兰继续占有基地都是和锡兰不参加任何国家集团的地位不相容的。”他表示希望关于英国在锡兰的海军基地问题会在伦敦英联邦总理会议期间得到解决。他重申锡兰将成为英联邦范围内的一个共和国。
