Title: The State Council Allocates Emergency Relief Funds for People in Flood-stricken Areas
Author:
Date: 1956-06-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th. In early June, Anhui and Henan experienced heavy rains, and some areas suffered waterlogging due to excessive rainfall. A county in Hebei Province also experienced flooding. The State Council is deeply concerned about this and has allocated 7.5 million yuan for emergency relief to help people in disaster areas overcome difficulties in their lives.

### 国务院拨款紧急救济水灾区人民

1956-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院拨款紧急救济水灾区人民
    新华社16日讯　六月上旬安徽、河南大雨，部分地区因雨量过大发生渍水灾害。河北省的一个县也发生水灾。国务院对此极为关怀，已经拨出七百五十万元进行紧急救济，以帮助灾区人民克服生活上的困难。
