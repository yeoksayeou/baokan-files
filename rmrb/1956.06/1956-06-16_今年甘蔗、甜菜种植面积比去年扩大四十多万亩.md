Title: This Year's Sugarcane and Sugar Beet Planting Area Expanded by More Than 400,000 Mu Compared to Last Year
Author:
Date: 1956-06-16
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 15th. This year, the national planting area of sugarcane and sugar beet has expanded by more than 400,000 mu compared to last year. The sugarcane planting area reached more than 3.4 million mu, and sugar beet reached more than 2.2 million mu. Currently, the growth of sugar beet and sugarcane in all regions is very good.

Many of the sugar beet and sugarcane planted in various regions this year are excellent varieties with high sugar content. Among them, the sugar beet varieties include the Soviet "P1537" and "Polish P," "Polish MZR," and other varieties. Among the good sugarcane varieties are "East Java No. 3016" and "Taiwan Sugar No. 134," etc.

Although the sugarcane planting area has greatly increased compared to last year, it is still more than 200,000 mu less than the original sowing plan. Currently, farmers in the sugarcane and sugar beet producing areas are actively carrying out mid-cultivation, topdressing, and further strengthening field management to increase yield per unit area and strive to complete and overfulfill the production plan.

### 今年甘蔗、甜菜种植面积比去年扩大四十多万亩

1956-06-16
第3版()
专栏：

    今年甘蔗、甜菜种植面积比去年扩大四十多万亩
    新华社15日讯　今年全国甘蔗和甜菜的种植面积都比去年扩大了四十多万亩。甘蔗的种植面积达三百四十多万亩，甜菜达二百二十多万亩。现在，各地所有的甜菜和甘蔗的生长情况都很好。
    今年各地种植的甜菜和甘蔗很多是含糖率很高的优良品种。其中甜菜有苏联的“P1537”和“波兰P”、“波兰MZR”等品种。甘蔗的良种中，有“东爪哇3016号”和“台糖134号”等。
    今年甘蔗的种植面积虽然比去年有很大增加，但比原定的播种计划数字还少二十多万亩。现在，各甘蔗、甜菜产区的农民正在积极进行中耕、追肥，并且进一步加强田间管理工作，以提高单位面积产量争取完成和超额完成生产计划。
