Title: The Fourth Interzone and Viet Bac Interzone of Vietnam Complete Fifth Phase of Land Reform
Author:
Date: 1956-06-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 6th: The Fourth Interzone and Viet Bac Interzone of Vietnam have victoriously completed the fifth phase of land reform.

	In the Fourth Interzone, the areas involved in this phase of land reform included the three provinces of Thanh Hóa, Nghệ An, and Quảng Bình, and the Vinh Linh area (belonging to Quảng Trị province), with a population of over one million. In this land reform, more than 380,000 peasants were allocated land, draught cattle, housing, and other fruits of their labor.

	Up to now, the Fourth Interzone, comprising four provinces, and the Viet Bac Interzone, comprising thirteen provinces, have completely completed land reform, with the exception of some mountainous areas and areas inhabited by ethnic minorities.

### 越南第四联区和越北联区完成第五期土地改革

1956-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    越南第四联区和越北联区完成第五期土地改革
    新华社河内6日电　越南第四联区和越北联区已经胜利完成了第五期土地改革。
    在第四联区，这期土地改革的地区包括清化、义安、广平三省和永灵地区（属于广治省），人口有一百多万人。在这次土地改革中有三十八万多农民分得了土地、耕牛和房舍等果实。
    到现在为止，拥有四个省的第四联区和拥有十三个省的越北联区除了部分山区和少数民族居住地区以外，已经全部完成了土地改革。
