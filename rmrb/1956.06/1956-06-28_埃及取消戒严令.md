Title: Egypt Lifts Martial Law
Author:
Date: 1956-06-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, June 27th: Egypt's new President, Gamal Abdel Nasser, signed his first decree on June 26th after taking office, lifting the martial law that had been in effect in Egypt for four years.

Due to national security reasons, the decree to lift martial law does not apply to the Jianghai and Sinai regions near the border between Israel and Egypt.

### 埃及取消戒严令

1956-06-28
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及取消戒严令
    新华社27日讯　开罗消息：埃及新任总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔六月二十六日签署了他就任后的第一个法令，取消在埃及实行了四年的戒严令。
    由于国家安全的原因，取消戒严令的法令不适用于靠近以色列和埃及边界的江海和西奈地区。
