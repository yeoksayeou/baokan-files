Title: Afghan Royal Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-06-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 17th: The four-member cultural delegation from the Kingdom of Afghanistan, invited by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing this afternoon, led by Dr. Popal, a geologist and head of the delegation. Greeting them at the airport were Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations; Peng Fei, Director of the Department of Education at Beijing Normal University; Cheng Yuqi, Deputy Director of the Geological Exploration Department of the Ministry of Geology; and Samad, the Afghan Ambassador to China.

The three other members of the Afghan cultural delegation are Dr. Akram, a geographer; Ansari, Director of the Information Bureau of Kata Khan Province; and Dr. Rahim, a historian.

　　At the airport, Chen Zhongjing (right), Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, warmly shakes hands with Dr. Sultan Ahmad Popal (left), head of the Afghan cultural delegation.

### 阿富汗王国文化代表团到北京

1956-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

    阿富汗王国文化代表团到北京
    新华社17日讯　应中华人民共和国文化部邀请来中国访问的阿富汗王国文化代表团一行四人，在团长、地质学家波帕尔博士率领下，今天下午到达北京。到机场迎接的有对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经、北京师范大学教育系主任彭飞、地质部地质勘探司副司长程裕琪，和阿富汗王国驻华大使萨马德。
    阿富汗文化代表团三位团员是地理学家阿克拉姆博士，卡塔汗省新闻局局长安萨里和历史学家拉希姆博士。
    在飞机场上，对外文化联络局代局长陈忠经（右）和阿富汗文化代表团团长苏丹·阿赫玛德·波帕尔博士（左）亲切地握手。　
                            新华社记者　吴化学摄
