Title: Lebanese Parliament Approves Sino-Lebanese Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-06-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, June 22nd: The Lebanese Parliament approved the Trade Agreement between the People's Republic of China and Lebanon, signed in Beirut on December 31, 1955, on June 21st.

### 黎巴嫩议会批准中黎贸易协定

1956-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩议会批准中黎贸易协定
    新华社贝鲁特22日电　黎巴嫩议会在六月二十一日批准了1955年12月31日在贝鲁特签订的中华人民共和国和黎巴嫩贸易协定。
