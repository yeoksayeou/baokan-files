Title: China National Department Store and Food Company System Advanced Workers Representative Conference Concludes; 74 Important Experiences in Production, Supply, and Sales Exchanged
Author:
Date: 1956-06-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd – At the China National Department Store and Food Company System Advanced Workers Representative Conference, representatives of advanced workers exchanged seventy-four important advanced experiences in production, supply, and sales.

This conference decided to further launch socialist competitions in the same business and trade nationwide.

During the conference, Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian addressed the attending representatives. He called on the representatives to lead all employees in diligently investigating and studying the constantly changing needs of the people, to do a good job in the supply of department store and food products, and to adapt to the demands of increased purchasing power of the people after the wage reform.

The conference concluded on the 23rd. Before the close of the conference, Minister of Commerce Zeng Shan presented awards to all the advanced workers present.

### 中国百货、食品公司系统先进工作者代表会议闭幕  交流了74项产、供、销方面重要经验

1956-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    中国百货、食品公司系统先进工作者代表会议闭幕
    交流了74项产、供、销方面重要经验
    据新华社23日讯　在中国百货、食品公司系统先进工作者代表会议上，先进工作者代表们分别交流了七十四项产、供、销等方面的重要先进经验。
    这个会议决定要在全国范围内，进一步开展同业务、同工种的社会主义竞赛。
    会议期间，国务院副总理李先念向到会的代表们讲了话。他号召代表们带动全体职工，努力调查研究人民不断变化着的需要，作好百货、食品商品的供应工作，适应工资改革后人民购买力提高的要求。
    这个会议是二十三日闭幕的。商业部部长曾山在会议闭幕前，给全体到会的先进工作者发了奖。
