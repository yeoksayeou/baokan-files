Title: Bulganin Receives Kim Il-sung
Author:
Date: 1956-06-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 6th: According to TASS, on June 5th, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, received the Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Kim Il-sung, who was passing through Moscow on his way to Berlin.

Accompanying Kim Il-sung were: the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Korea, Nam Il, and the Korean Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Li Sang-jo.

Bulganin and Kim Il-sung held friendly talks.

### 布尔加宁接见金日成

1956-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁接见金日成
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：六月五日，苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁接见了路过莫斯科前往柏林的朝鲜内阁首相金日成。
    随同金日成一起去的有：朝鲜外务相南日和朝鲜驻苏联大使李相朝。
    布尔加宁和金日成进行了友好的谈话。
