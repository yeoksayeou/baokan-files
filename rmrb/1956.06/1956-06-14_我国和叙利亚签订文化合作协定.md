Title: China and Syria Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-06-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, June 13th: The People's Republic of China and the Syrian Republic signed a cultural cooperation agreement yesterday in Damascus, the Syrian capital.

    The signing ceremony was held at the Syrian Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Representing China to sign the agreement was Bao Erhan, head of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation and Plenipotentiary Representative of the Government of the People's Republic of China. Representing Syria was Syrian Prime Minister and Foreign Minister, Ghazi.

    Bao Erhan said at the ceremony that the signing of the cultural cooperation agreement marked a new development in the friendly relations between the two governments and peoples. We firmly believe that this development of friendly relations between Syria and China is in the interests of both peoples. The Syrian and Chinese peoples share the same desire: to restore the historical relations between the two countries in all aspects. We can say with confidence that this common desire will be realized.

    Ghazi said: The signing of the cultural cooperation agreement between China and Syria not only symbolizes closer ties between the two peoples, but will also be a good start for broader and fuller cooperation between the two countries.

### 我国和叙利亚签订文化合作协定

1956-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我国和叙利亚签订文化合作协定
    新华社贝鲁特13日电　中华人民共和国和叙利亚共和国两国昨天在叙利亚首都大马士革签订了文化合作协定。
    签字仪式在叙利亚外交部举行。代表中国签字的是中国文化艺术代表团团长、中华人民共和国政府全权代表鲍尔汉，代表叙利亚签字的是叙利亚总理兼外交部长加齐。
    鲍尔汉在仪式上发表讲话说，文化合作协定的签订标志着两国政府和人民友好关系的新的发展。我们深信，叙利亚和中国之间的友好关系的这一发展是符合两国人民的利益的。叙利亚和中国人民都怀着同样的愿望：在各个方面恢复两国的历史性的关系。我们可以满怀信心地说：这个共同的愿望一定会实现。
    加齐说：中国和叙利亚的文化合作协定的签订不仅象征着两国人民的联系更加密切，而且也将成为两国间更广泛和充分的合作的一个良好的开端。
