Title: Nepalese Ambassador to China Presents Credentials to Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1956-06-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 13th: The first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Nepal to China, Lieutenant General Rana, presented his credentials to Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, on the afternoon of the 13th, accompanied by First Secretary Pant Rey and Military Attaché Colonel Basnyat.

Present were: Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China; Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Zhang Wenjin, Deputy Director of the Asian Department.

Xinhua News Agency, June 13th: The first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Nepal to China, Lieutenant General Rana, paid a visit to Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai on the morning of the 13th to discuss matters related to the presentation of credentials. Present were: Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Wenjin, Deputy Director of the Asian Department.

### 尼泊尔驻华大使向毛主席递交国书

1956-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔驻华大使向毛主席递交国书
    新华社13日讯　尼泊尔王国驻中国首任特命全权大使拉纳中将在十三日下午偕一等秘书班特瑞和武官巴斯尼亚特上校向中华人民共和国主席毛泽东递交国书。
    在场的有：中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，外交部副部长张闻天，全国人民代表大会常务委员会典礼局局长余心清，外交部礼宾司司长柯华和亚洲司副司长章文晋。
    新华社13日讯　尼泊尔王国驻中国首任特命全权大使拉纳中将在十三日上午拜会我国外交部长周恩来商谈递交国书事宜。在座的有：外交部礼宾司司长柯华和亚洲司副司长章文晋。
