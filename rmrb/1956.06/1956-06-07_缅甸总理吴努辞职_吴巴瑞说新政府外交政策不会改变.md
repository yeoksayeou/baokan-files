Title: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu Resigns, U Ba Swe Says New Government's Foreign Policy Will Not Change
Author:
Date: 1956-06-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, June 6th: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu announced yesterday that he has decided to resign from the post of Prime Minister and has recommended the current Minister of Defense, U Ba Swe, to succeed him as Prime Minister of Burma.

U Nu said at a reception attended by more than 80 local and foreign journalists that he would continue to fulfill his responsibilities as chairman of the ruling Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League. U Nu said that the new cabinet will have three deputy prime ministers, namely the current Minister of Foreign Affairs, Sao Hkun Hkio; Minister of Industry, U Kyaw Nyein; and Minister of Land Nationalization, Tekin Tin. U Ba Swe will concurrently serve as Minister of Defense and Minister of National Planning. Sao Hkun Hkio will concurrently serve as Minister of Foreign Affairs. U Nu said that the new government will pursue the policies formulated by the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League.

U Ba Swe, U Kyaw Nyein and Tekin Tin also attended the press conference. U Ba Swe told reporters: The new government's foreign policy will not change in any way, and the new government hopes to solve the domestic security problems within two years.

The list of the new cabinet was announced at the press conference. This list will be submitted to the House of Representatives for approval on June 7.

### 缅甸总理吴努辞职  吴巴瑞说新政府外交政策不会改变

1956-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸总理吴努辞职
    吴巴瑞说新政府外交政策不会改变
    新华社仰光6日电　缅甸总理吴努昨天宣布，他已经决定辞去总理的职务，并且推荐现任国防部长吴巴瑞接替他担任缅甸总理。
    吴努在一次有八十多名当地和外国记者参加的招待会上说，他将专门继续履行执政党反法西斯人民自由同盟主席的职责。吴努说，新内阁将有三位副总理，由现任外交部长藻昆卓、工业部长吴觉迎和土地国有化部长德钦阵担任。吴巴瑞将兼任国防部长和国家计划部长。藻昆卓将兼任外交部长。吴努说，新政府将奉行反法西斯人民自由同盟所制定的政策。
    吴巴瑞、吴觉迎和德钦阵也参加了这次记者招待会。吴巴瑞对记者们说：新政府的外交政策不会有任何改变，新政府希望在两年内解决国内治安问题。
    记者招待会上公布了新内阁的名单，这个名单将在六月七日提交国会代表院批准。
