Title: Beijing Circles Commemorate the Twentieth Anniversary of Gorky's Death
Author:
Date: 1956-06-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 17th - Beijing's literary and art circles and other sectors, totaling one thousand people, gathered today to commemorate the twentieth anniversary of Gorky's death.

    The commemoration was presided over by Feng Xuefeng, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association. In his opening remarks, he said that Gorky was the great founder of Soviet and world socialist realism literature, who not only pioneered the great road and brilliant example for Soviet literature but also had a profound impact on Chinese literature.
    Cao Jinghua, Vice President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Director of the Russian Language and Literature Department of Peking University, then spoke. He said that Gorky had always been concerned about China, and he was angry at the imperialist aggression against the Chinese people and rejoiced at the victory of the Chinese people. Many Chinese intellectuals were inspired, encouraged, and empowered by Gorky's works and embarked on the road of revolution.
    Soviet linguistics deputy doctor Petrovsky also spoke.
    The commemoration was jointly convened by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, the Chinese Writers Association, and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

### 北京各界纪念高尔基逝世二十周年

1956-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

    北京各界纪念高尔基逝世二十周年
    据新华社17日讯　北京文艺界及其他各界人士一千人，今天集会纪念高尔基逝世二十周年。
    纪念会由中国作家协会副主席冯雪峰主持。他在开会词中说，高尔基是苏联和世界社会主义现实主义文学的伟大奠基人，不但为苏联文学开创了伟大的道路和光辉的范例，而且对中国文学有着深远的影响。
    北京市中苏友好协会副会长、北京大学俄罗斯语言文学系主任曹靖华接着讲了话。他说，高尔基从来是很关心中国的，他为帝国主义侵略中国人民而愤怒，为中国人民的胜利而欢欣。中国许多知识分子，曾经从高尔基的作品中得到了启示、鼓舞和力量，走到革命的道路上来。
    苏联语言学副博士彼得罗夫斯基也讲了话。
    纪念会由中苏友好协会总会、中国作家协会、北京市中苏友好协会共同召开。
