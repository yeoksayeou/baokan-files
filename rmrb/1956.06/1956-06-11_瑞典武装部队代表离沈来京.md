Title: Swedish Armed Forces Representative Leaves Shenyang for Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-06-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, June 10th. Lieutenant Colonel Aris Kog, the Swedish armed forces representative who was invited to visit China, left Shenyang by train for Beijing on the evening of the 10th, after a four-day visit in Shenyang.

    After arriving in Shenyang on the 7th, Lieutenant Colonel Aris Kog was received by Lieutenant General Wu Xinquan, the leader of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in the Shenyang area, and attended a welcome banquet held by the Shenyang garrison leadership in the evening.

    While in Shenyang, Lieutenant Colonel Aris Kog visited the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant, and also visited the large rolling mill in Anshan, and the open-pit mine and the No. 1 Petroleum Plant in Fushun. He also visited the workers' retirement home.

### 瑞典武装部队代表离沈来京

1956-06-11
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞典武装部队代表离沈来京
    新华社沈阳10日电　应邀来中国访问的瑞典武装部队代表阿里斯考格中校，在沈阳进行为期四天的参观访问后，十日晚乘火车离沈阳赴北京。
    阿里斯考格中校在七日到达沈阳后，当天受到中国人民解放军驻沈阳地区领导机关吴信泉中将的接见，并且在晚间出席了沈阳驻军领导机关举行的欢迎宴会。
    阿里斯考格中校在沈阳参观了沈阳第一机床厂，并且到鞍山参观了大型轧钢厂等处，到抚顺参观了露天矿和石油一厂，访问了工人养老院。
