Title: Vice Premier He Long Receives Three Pakistani Journalists
Author:
Date: 1956-06-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 12th—Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, He Long, received the members of the Pakistani journalists' delegation to China on the afternoon of the 12th: the editor of "Anjam," Mohammad Umar Farooqi; the assistant editor of "Dawn," Akhtar Adil Rizvi; and the chief correspondent of "Civil and Military Gazette," Fazli Mahmood.
　　Present at the reception were Mei Yi, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, and Zhang Wenjin, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 贺龙副总理接见三位巴基斯坦记者

1956-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

    贺龙副总理接见三位巴基斯坦记者
    新华社12日讯　　中华人民共和国国务院副总理贺龙在十二日下午接见了巴基斯坦新闻工作者访华代表团团员：“成就报”主编穆罕默德·乌马尔·法鲁基，“黎明报”副主编阿赫塔·艾迪尔·利兹维和“军民报”首席特派员法兹利·马茂德。
    接见的时候，在座的有中国新闻工作者联谊会副会长梅益，外交部亚洲司副司长章文晋。
