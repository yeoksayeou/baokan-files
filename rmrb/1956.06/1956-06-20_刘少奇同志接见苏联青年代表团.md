Title: Comrade Liu Shaoqi Receives Soviet Youth Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: Liu Shaoqi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, received the Soviet Youth Delegation headed by A.A. Rapokhin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, this afternoon.

Present at the meeting were Deng Xiaoping, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Wang Jiaxiang, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Liu Lantao, alternate member of the Central Committee, Hu Yaobang, Hu Keshi, and Liu Xiyuan, secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, and Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation.

### 刘少奇同志接见苏联青年代表团

1956-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇同志接见苏联青年代表团
    新华社19日讯　中共中央书记处书记刘少奇，在今天下午接见了以苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记阿·阿·拉波欣为首的苏联青年代表团。
    接见的时候在座的，有中共中央政治局委员兼秘书长邓小平，中共中央委员王稼祥、候补中央委员刘澜涛，青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦、胡克实、刘西元，中华全国民主青年联合会主席廖承志，中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠。
