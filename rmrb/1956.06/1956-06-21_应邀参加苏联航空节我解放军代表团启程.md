Title: PLA Delegation Departs to Attend Soviet Aviation Day at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1956-06-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th - The Chinese People's Liberation Army delegation, invited to participate in the celebration of Soviet Aviation Day, departed from Beijing by plane at 7:00 am today for Moscow. The delegation is led by Air Force Commander General Liu Yalou, and includes six members and five attendants.

### 应邀参加苏联航空节我解放军代表团启程

1956-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加苏联航空节我解放军代表团启程
    新华社20日讯　应邀参加庆祝苏联航空节的中国人民解放军代表团，在今天上午七时由北京乘飞机去莫斯科。代表团由空军司令刘亚楼上将率领，包括团员六人，随员五人。
