Title: State Council Holds Thirty-First Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-06-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 14th: The State Council of the People's Republic of China held its thirty-first meeting on the 14th.

    After listening to Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan's explanation of the draft model charter for advanced agricultural production cooperatives, the meeting resolved to adopt this draft and submit it to the Third Session of the First National People's Congress for deliberation.

    The meeting approved the State Council's decision on dividing the Eastern United Banner of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region into the East Ujimqin Banner and the West Ujimqin Banner, and renaming the Western United Banner to Abaga Banner, as well as the State Council's decision on abolishing Huang County in Hunan Province and establishing the Xin Huang Dong Autonomous County.

    Finally, the meeting approved appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行第三十一次会议

1956-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第三十一次会议
    新华社14日讯　中华人民共和国国务院第三十一次会议在十四日举行。
    会议在听了农业部长廖鲁言关于高级农业生产合作社示范章程草案的说明后，决议通过这个草案，并将这个草案提请第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议审议。
    会议通过了国务院关于内蒙古自治区东部联合旗划分为东乌珠穆沁旗和西乌珠穆沁旗并将西部联合旗改名为阿巴嘎旗的决定和国务院关于撤销湖南省晃县成立新晃侗族自治县的决定。
    会议最后通过了任免事项。
