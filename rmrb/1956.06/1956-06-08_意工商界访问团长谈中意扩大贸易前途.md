Title: Head of Italian Business Delegation Discusses Prospects for Expanding Sino-Italian Trade
Author:
Date: 1956-06-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Rome, 7th Dispatch: The head of the Italian business delegation, Mr. Gintili, returned to Rome on the 6th after visiting China. He said that the trade contract signed in Beijing by the Italian business delegation should open the door for the growing trade between Italy and China.
    He said that due to the relaxation of the international situation and the good results of Italian traders establishing contact with China last year, the prospects for expanding trade between Italy and China have greatly developed.

### 意工商界访问团长谈中意扩大贸易前途

1956-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    意工商界访问团长谈中意扩大贸易前途
    新华社7日讯　罗马消息：意大利工商界访问团团长金蒂利在访问中国后六日回到罗马。他说，意大利工商界访问团这次在北京签订贸易合同，应该为意大利同中国之间的日益发展的贸易打开大门。
    他说，由于国际局势的缓和和意大利贸易界人士在去年同中国建立接触后的良好结果，意中两国扩大贸易的前途大大发展了。
