Title: The Third Session of the National People's Congress Will Open Today; Deputies Held Preparatory Meeting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1956-06-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 14th. All preparations for the Third Session of the First National People's Congress are complete, and it will open in Beijing on the 15th.

As of today, one thousand and thirty deputies have registered. This morning, the deputies held a preparatory meeting for the Third Session of the First National People's Congress. Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman and Secretary-General of the Standing Committee, reported on the draft agenda for the Third Session, the list of candidates for the Presidium and Secretary-General, the list of candidates for the Chairman and members of the Proposals Examination Committee, and the preparations for the Third Session, all of which were passed by the 41st meeting of the Standing Committee attended by the temporary conveners of each delegation group.

The deputies formed thirty delegation groups according to their electoral units and exchanged views on the preparations for the Third Session proposed by the Standing Committee.

### 全国人民代表大会第三次会议今天开幕  代表们昨日举行预备会议

1956-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会第三次会议今天开幕
    代表们昨日举行预备会议
    新华社14日讯　第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议的各种准备工作已经就绪，将于十五日在北京开幕。
    到今天为止，已有一千零三十位代表报到。今天上午，代表们举行了第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议预备会议。常务委员会副委员长兼秘书长彭真在会上报告了有各代表小组临时召集人参加的常务委员会第四十一次会议通过的关于第三次会议议程草案，主席团、秘书长候选名单，提案审查委员会主任委员和委员候选名单，以及关于第三次会议的准备工作情况。
    代表们按选举单位分别组成了三十个代表小组，就常务委员会提出的关于第三次会议的准备事项交换了意见。
