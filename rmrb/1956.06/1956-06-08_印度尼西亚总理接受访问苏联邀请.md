Title: Indonesian Prime Minister Accepts Invitation to Visit Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-06-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 7th: An Indonesian Foreign Ministry spokesman announced yesterday that Indonesian Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo has accepted the invitation from Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Nikolai Bulganin to visit the Soviet Union.
The spokesman said that the visit will take place within this year, but after President Sukarno's planned visit to the Soviet Union in September.

### 印度尼西亚总理接受访问苏联邀请

1956-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚总理接受访问苏联邀请
    新华社雅加达7日电　印度尼西亚外交部发言人昨天宣布，印度尼西亚总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约已经接受苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁的邀请，将前往苏联访问。
    发言人说，访问将在今年内进行，但是要在计划中的苏加诺总统九月间去苏联访问以后。
