Title: Korea Starts Demobilization According to Disarmament Decision
Author:
Date: 1956-06-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th - According to the statement by the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea regarding the demobilization of 80,000 troops, the various branches of the Korean People's Army have already begun demobilization work, and are simultaneously reducing various military equipment and materials accordingly.
    On the 23rd, units such as the Cui He-yi-led unit of the People's Army held gatherings to bid farewell to the demobilized soldiers. Many residents from the areas where the troops were stationed also went to the station to see them off as they returned to participate in peaceful construction.
    According to the decision of the Cabinet, the demobilized soldiers received living allowances and clothing from the government. They will return to their hometowns according to their wishes, or participate in the work of enterprises in various economic sectors and agricultural cooperatives.

### 朝鲜根据裁军决定开始裁军

1956-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜根据裁军决定开始裁军
    新华社平壤26日电　根据朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府关于裁军八万名的声明，朝鲜人民军各兵种部队已经开始裁军工作，同时相应地缩减各种军事装备和军用器材。
    在二十三日，人民军崔鹤一所属部队等各部队分别举行集会欢送复员军人。部队驻地的许多居民也到车站欢送他们复员参加和平建设。
    根据内阁的决定，复员军人得到了政府发给的生活补助金和衣物、被服。他们将根据自己的意愿回到家乡，或者参加各经济部门企业和农业合作社的工作。
