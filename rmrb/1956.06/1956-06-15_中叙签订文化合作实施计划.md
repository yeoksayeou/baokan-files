Title: China and Syria Sign Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-06-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, June 13th: The Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China and the Ministry of Education of the Syrian Republic, in accordance with the cultural cooperation agreement between China and Syria, signed the cultural cooperation implementation plan for 1956 to the end of 1957 in Damascus on June 12th. Ma Yuhuai, deputy head of the Chinese cultural and artistic delegation, signed on behalf of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, and Ahmed Fattal, Secretary-General of the Syrian Ministry of Education, signed on behalf of the Ministry of Education of the Syrian Republic.

### 中叙签订文化合作实施计划

1956-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中叙签订文化合作实施计划
    新华社大马士革13日电　中华人民共和国文化部和叙利亚共和国教育部按照中国和叙利亚之间的文化合作协定，在六月十二日在大马士革签订了1956年到1957年年底的文化合作实施计划。中国文化艺术代表团副团长马玉槐代表中华人民共和国文化部签字，叙利亚教育部秘书长艾哈迈德·弗塔勒代表叙利亚共和国教育部签字。
