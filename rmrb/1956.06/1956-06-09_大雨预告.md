Title: Heavy Rain Forecast
Author:
Date: 1956-06-09
Page: 1

    Heavy Rain Forecast
    Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a heavy rain forecast at noon on the 8th: In the next two to three days, the weather in North China will gradually worsen. There will be heavy rain in the middle and lower reaches of the Yellow River, southern Shanxi Province, and southern and eastern Hebei Province, with torrential rain in mountainous areas. The northern tributaries of the Huai River Basin will have heavy rain to torrential rain on the 8th.

### 大雨预告

1956-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

    大雨预告
    新华社8日讯　中央气象台八日中午发布大雨预告：在未来两三天内，华北天气逐渐变坏。黄河中下游，山西省南部，河北省南部、东部等地区将有大雨，多山地区还有暴雨。淮河流域北部的各支流，在八日有大雨到暴雨。
