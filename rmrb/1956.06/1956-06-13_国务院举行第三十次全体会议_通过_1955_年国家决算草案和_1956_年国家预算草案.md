Title: State Council Holds Thirtieth Plenary Session, Approves Draft of 1955 National Final Accounts and Draft of 1956 National Budget
Author:
Date: 1956-06-13
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 12th: The Thirtieth Plenary Session of the State Council of the People's Republic of China was held on the 12th.

    After hearing Vice Premier and Minister of Finance Li Xiannian's report on the 1955 National Final Accounts and the 1956 National Budget, the meeting approved the "Draft of the 1955 National Final Accounts and the Draft of the 1956 National Budget." The meeting resolved to submit the "Draft of the 1955 National Final Accounts and the Draft of the 1956 National Budget" to the Third Session of the First National People's Congress for review and approval, and Vice Premier and Minister of Finance Li Xiannian will present a report to the Congress on behalf of the State Council.

    After discussion, the meeting principally approved the report of the State Planning Commission and the Ministry of Finance on reducing the ex-factory prices of industrial products and adjusting budget revenues and expenditures in 1956.

    After hearing the explanation of Xue Muqiao, Director of the State Statistical Bureau, the meeting principally approved the "Communique of the State Statistical Bureau of the People's Republic of China on the Results of the Implementation of the National Economic Plan for 1955."

    After hearing the explanation of Qian Junrui, Vice Minister of Culture, the meeting ratified the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Arab Republic of Egypt, and decided to submit this agreement to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation.

    After hearing the explanation of Liu Chun, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, the meeting approved the draft organizational regulations for the People's Congress and the People's Committee of the Mengcun Hui Autonomous County, the draft organizational regulations for the People's Congress and the People's Committee of the Dachang Hui Autonomous County, and the draft organizational regulations for the People's Congress and the People's Committee of the Jianghua Yao Autonomous County, and decided to submit these three draft regulations to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation.

    Finally, the meeting also approved a number of appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行第三十次全体会议  通过1955年国家决算草案和1956年国家预算草案

1956-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第三十次全体会议
    通过1955年国家决算草案和1956年国家预算草案
    新华社12日讯　中华人民共和国国务院全体会议第三十次会议，在十二日举行。
    会议在听了李先念副总理兼财政部长关于1955年国家决算和1956年国家预算的报告后，通过了“1955年国家决算草案和1956年国家预算草案”。会议决议将“1955年国家决算草案和1956年国家预算草案”，提请第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议审查批准，并由李先念副总理兼财政部长代表国务院向大会作报告。
    会议经过讨论，原则上通过了国家计划委员会和财政部关于1956年工业产品降低出厂价格和调整预算收支的报告。
    会议在听了国家统计局局长薛暮桥的说明后，原则上批准了“中华人民共和国国家统计局关于1955年度国民经济计划执行结果的公报”。
    会议在听了文化部副部长钱俊瑞的说明后，批准了中华人民共和国政府和埃及共和国政府文化合作协定，并决定将这个协定提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议。
    会议在听了民族事务委员会副主任刘春的说明后，通过了孟村回族自治县人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例草案、大厂回族自治县人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例草案以及江华瑶族自治县人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例草案，并且决定将这三个条例草案提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议。
    会议最后还通过了任免事项多起。
