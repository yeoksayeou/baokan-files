Title: Soviet-Yugoslav Government Delegations Continue Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-06-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, via TASS News Agency – The Soviet and Yugoslav government delegations continued their talks at the Kremlin at 11:00 AM on June 9th.

    During the talks, both sides continued to exchange views extensively on the issues discussed during the first meeting. The talks proceeded in a cordial and friendly atmosphere, filled with frankness and a spirit of full understanding.
    Xinhua News Agency, June 10th – According to TASS, Moscow: Yugoslav President Tito and his entourage departed from Moscow on a special train on June 10th to visit and tour Stalingrad. Khrushchev and Mikoyan accompanied them on the journey.
    The previous morning, Tito and his party returned to Moscow from Leningrad, accompanied by the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Bulganin. During their one-day stay in Moscow, in addition to continuing talks with the Soviet government delegation, they also visited the Voroshilov Higher Military Academy and attended a performance of "Arshak II" by the Armenian National Spendiarov Opera and Ballet Theatre at the Bolshoi Theatre.

### 苏南两国政府代表团继续举行会谈

1956-06-11
第4版()
专栏：

    苏南两国政府代表团继续举行会谈
    新华社据塔斯社莫斯科讯　苏联政府代表团和南斯拉夫政府代表团六月九日上午十一时在克里姆林宫继续会谈。
    会谈中大家继续就第一次会谈所讨论的问题广泛交换意见。会谈在亲切友好的气氛中进行，会谈中充满了坦率和充分谅解的精神。
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：南斯拉夫总统铁托和他的随行人员在六月十日乘专车从莫斯科动身前往斯大林格勒参观访问。赫鲁晓夫和米高扬也陪同他们一路前往。
    前一天上午，铁托一行是由苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁陪同从列宁格勒回到了莫斯科。他们在莫斯科停留的一天中，除了和苏联政府代表团继续举行会谈外，还参观了伏罗希洛夫高等军事学院；到大剧院观看了亚美尼亚国立斯品季阿罗夫歌剧和巴蕾舞剧院剧团演出的“阿尔夏克二世”。
