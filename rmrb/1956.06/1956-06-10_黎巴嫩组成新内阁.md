Title: Lebanon Forms New Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1956-06-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, June 9th: A new Lebanese cabinet was formed on June 8th. The Prime Minister of the new cabinet is still Yafei, the former Lebanese Prime Minister who resigned on June 6th.

Rahod continues to serve as the Minister of Foreign Affairs in the new government. The position of the former cabinet's Minister of Public Works and Planning, Bustani, is taken over by Sabra.

### 黎巴嫩组成新内阁

1956-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩组成新内阁
    新华社9日讯　贝鲁特消息：黎巴嫩新内阁已经在六月八日组成，新内阁总理仍然由六月六日辞职的前黎巴嫩总理雅非充任。
    拉霍德在新政府中继续担任外交部长。前内阁的公共工程和计划部部长布斯坦尼的职位由萨布拉接替。
