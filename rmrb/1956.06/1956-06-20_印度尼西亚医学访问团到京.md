Title: Indonesian Medical Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-06-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: Beijing - The Indonesian Medical Delegation, invited by the Chinese Medical Association to visit China, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 18th. The delegation is led by Dr. Shamsuddin and consists of eight members.

### 印度尼西亚医学访问团到京

1956-06-20
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚医学访问团到京
    据新华社讯　应中华医学会邀请前来中国访问的印度尼西亚医学访问团在沙姆苏丁医生率领下，一行八人于十八日晚乘火车到达北京。
