Title: Agricultural machinery and equipment given to our country by the Czechoslovak government transported to Cang County
Author:
Date: 1956-06-14
Page: 2
    Xinhua News Agency, Baoding, 12th. The first batch of agricultural machinery and equipment, totaling fourteen train cars of heavy-duty trucks and machine parts packed in large wooden crates, donated to China by the Czechoslovak government, arrived in Cang County on the 9th.
    The relevant person in charge said that the agricultural machinery and equipment donated to China by the Czechoslovak government includes farm trailers, mobile repair vehicles, as well as various types of tractors, and various agricultural implements, totaling sixty train cars for transportation. These machines and equipment will be shipped successively this summer.

### 捷克斯洛伐克政府赠给我国的农业机器设备运到沧县

1956-06-14
第2版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克政府赠给我国的农业机器设备运到沧县
    新华社保定12日电　捷克斯洛伐克政府赠给中国的一套农业机器设备，第一批共十四个车皮的载重卡车和用大木箱包装的机器部件已经在九日运抵沧县。
    有关负责人说，捷克斯洛伐克政府赠送中国的农业机器设备，包括农场用挂车、移动式修配车，以及各式拖拉机、各种农机具等，共需六十个车皮装运。这些机器设备都将在今年夏天陆续运到。
