Title: Egypt Releases First Batch of Political Prisoners
Author:
Date: 1956-06-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, June 12th, Cairo dispatch: Egypt released 371 political prisoners on June 10th. According to official sources, the remaining 200 will be released before June 17th.
    In his speech on June 1st, Prime Minister Gamal Abdel Nasser announced that all political prisoners would be released on the day of the national referendum, June 23rd.

### 埃及释放第一批政治犯

1956-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及释放第一批政治犯
    新华社12日讯　开罗消息：埃及在六月十日释放了三百七十一名政治犯。据官方人士说，剩下的二百名将在六月十七日以前释放。
    阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔总理在六月一日的演说中曾经宣布，在本月二十三日全民投票日的时候，将释放所有政治犯。
