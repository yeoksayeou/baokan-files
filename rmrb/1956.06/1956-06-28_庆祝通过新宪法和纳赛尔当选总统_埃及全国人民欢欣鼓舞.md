Title: Nationwide Jubilation in Egypt Celebrates Passage of New Constitution and Nasser's Election as President
Author:
Date: 1956-06-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, June 27th—According to TASS, Cairo, June 26th: Newspapers report that the Egyptian people across the country are joyfully welcoming the news of the passage of Egypt's new constitution by referendum and the election of Gamal Abdel Nasser as President of the Republic.

	Mass rallies and celebrations have been held in cities and villages throughout the country. On June 25th, the working people, youth, and students of Cairo held a celebratory parade. In the evening, Cairo was brightly lit. Hundreds of thousands of people packed the squares, open spaces, and streets. Beautiful fireworks were set off at the end of the mass festivities.

	President Abdel Nasser has received many congratulatory telegrams from leaders of friendly countries, Egyptian social organizations, trade unions, and individual Egyptian citizens. Newspapers prominently published congratulatory telegrams from Kliment Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Nikolai Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, and Dmitri Shepilov, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR.

### 庆祝通过新宪法和纳赛尔当选总统  埃及全国人民欢欣鼓舞

1956-06-28
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝通过新宪法和纳赛尔当选总统 
    埃及全国人民欢欣鼓舞
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社开罗二十六日讯：报纸报道，埃及全国人民兴高彩烈地欢迎公民投票通过埃及新宪法和加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔当选为共和国总统的消息。
    全国各城市和农村都举行了群众狂欢的庆祝活动。在六月二十五日，开罗劳动人民、青年和学生们举行了庆祝游行。晚间，开罗灯火辉煌。几十万人挤满了广场、空地和街头。在群众狂欢结束的时候，还放了美丽的焰火。
    阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔总统收到了友好国家的国家领导人、埃及的社会团体和工会组织以及埃及的个别公民发来的许多贺电。报纸在显著地位登载了苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫、苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫的贺电。
