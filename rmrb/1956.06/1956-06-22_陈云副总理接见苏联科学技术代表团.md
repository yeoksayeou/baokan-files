Title: Vice Premier Chen Yun Receives Soviet Science and Technology Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 21st. Vice Premier of the State Council, Chen Yun, received the Soviet delegation this morning at 10:00 AM, which is in China to participate in the Fourth Session of the Sino-Soviet Science and Technology Cooperation Committee. The delegation was headed by N.M. Kiruyanov, Chairman of the Soviet Group of the Sino-Soviet Science and Technology Cooperation Committee and Vice Chairman of the Soviet State Economic Committee, along with N.A. Krylov, Deputy Representative of the Soviet Economic Liaison Bureau in China of the Soviet Economic Liaison General Bureau. Also present at the reception were Kong Xiangzhen, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Sino-Soviet Science and Technology Cooperation Committee and Vice Director of the State Construction Commission, and Li Qiang, Member of the Chinese Group of the Sino-Soviet Science and Technology Cooperation Committee and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade.

### 陈云副总理接见苏联科学技术代表团

1956-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    陈云副总理接见苏联科学技术代表团
    新华社21日讯　国务院副总理陈云今天上午十时接见了来中国参加中苏科学技术合作委员会第四届会议的以中苏科学技术合作委员会苏联组主席、苏联国家经济委员会副主席恩·姆·基鲁扬诺夫为首的苏联代表团和苏联经济联络总局驻华经济代表处副代表恩·阿·克雷洛夫。接见时在座的有：中苏科学技术合作委员会中国组主席、国家建设委员会副主任孔祥祯，中苏科学技术合作委员会中国组委员、对外贸易部副部长李强。
