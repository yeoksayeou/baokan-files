Title: National People's Congress Continues Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1956-06-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd: He Xiangning, Chairman of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, stated at the plenary session of the Third National People's Congress this afternoon, warmly welcoming overseas Chinese to return to China to invest in the establishment of reclamation farms.

The plenary session of the Third Session of the First National People's Congress held this afternoon began at 3:00 PM.

Wang Shoudao, Director of the Sixth Office of the State Council, described the situation of transportation and postal and telecommunications work at today's meeting.

Zhao Jianmin, Governor of Shandong Province, introduced the development of industry and agriculture in Shandong in his speech.

Wang Heshou, Director of the State Construction Commission and Minister of Metallurgical Industry, described the situation and problems in the 1956 annual plan for basic construction today.

The representatives who spoke at today's meeting also included Wang Dezhi, Zaitao, Shao Lizi, Bi Mingqi, Zhang Guofan, Hu Zi'ang, Lu Zhenfan, Li Hui, Gao Shiqi, and Huang Yan. (The full text of the representatives' speeches on this day will be published separately)

Today's meeting ended at 6:20 PM. The executive chairmen presiding over today's meeting were: Guo Moruo, Chen Jiageng, Tan Zheng, Xie Fumin, Lü Hongbin, Huang Changshui, Da Pusheng, and Yan Jici.

Tomorrow is Sunday, and the meeting will be adjourned for one day. The plenary session will continue on the 25th.

### 全国人民代表大会继续举行全体会议

1956-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会继续举行全体会议
    据新华社23日讯　华侨事务委员会主任何香凝，今天下午在全国人民代表大会第三次会议全体会议上发言时，热烈欢迎华侨回国投资建立垦殖场。
    第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议今天下午举行的全体会议，从下午三时开始。
    国务院第六办公室主任王首道，在今天的会议上叙述了运输和邮电工作的情况。
    山东省长赵健民代表，在发言中介绍了山东工农业的发展情况。
    国家建设委员会主任兼冶金工业部部长王鹤寿，今天叙述了1956年基本建设年度计划中的情况和问题。
    在今天的会议上先后发言的代表，还有王德滋、载涛、邵力子、毕鸣岐、张国藩、胡子昂、陆镇藩、李辉、高士其、黄岩。（这天的代表发言全文另发）
    今天的会议在下午六时二十分结束。主持今天会议的执行主席是：郭沫若、陈嘉庚、谭政、谢扶民、吕鸿宾、黄长水、达浦生、严济慈。
    明天是星期日，会议休会一天，二十五日继续举行全体会议。
