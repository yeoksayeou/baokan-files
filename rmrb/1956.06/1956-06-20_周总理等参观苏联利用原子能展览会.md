Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Others Visit the Soviet Union's Exhibition on the Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy
Author: Gao Liang
Date: 1956-06-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai and Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Chen Yi, and Ulanhu, along with heads of various departments, visited the Soviet Union's Exhibition on the Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy this evening.

Lieutenant General Rana, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Nepal to China, was also invited to visit the exhibition.

Accompanying them on the visit were Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, Mr. Golikov, the curator of the Soviet Union's Exhibition on the Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy, and Mr. Chelenkov, the general technical advisor.

### 周总理等参观苏联利用原子能展览会
高粮
1956-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理等参观苏联利用原子能展览会
    新华社19日讯　国务院总理周恩来和副总理陈云、陈毅、乌兰夫以及各部门负责人，今天晚间参观了苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会。
    尼泊尔王国驻中国首任特命全权大使拉纳中将等也应邀参观了这个展览会。
    陪同参观的有苏联驻中国大使尤金院士、苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会馆长戈利科夫和科学技术总指导切连科夫。
    周恩来总理等在苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会中参观。
                                本报记者　高粮摄
