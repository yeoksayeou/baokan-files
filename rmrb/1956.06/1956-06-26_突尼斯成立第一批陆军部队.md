Title: Tunisia Establishes First Army Units
Author:
Date: 1956-06-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, 24th—It was announced by the French Embassy in Tunisia that the Tunisian Prime Minister Bourguiba and the French Ambassador to Tunisia, Seydoux, have reached an agreement on the establishment of a Tunisian army.

　　1,500 Tunisian soldiers drawn from the French forces were handed over to the Secretary-General of the Tunisian Ministry of National Defense, Abdul Hamid Chergui, by the Commander-in-Chief of the French forces in Tunisia, General Beauffre, on June 21. This group of soldiers will become the first unit of the Tunisian army.

### 突尼斯成立第一批陆军部队

1956-06-26
第4版()
专栏：

    突尼斯成立第一批陆军部队
    新华社24日讯　突尼斯消息：法国驻突尼斯大使馆宣布，突尼斯首相布尔吉巴同法国驻突尼斯大使赛杜已经就建立突尼斯军队问题达成协议。
    从法国部队中抽调出来的一千五百名突尼斯士兵，已经在六月二十一日由法国驻突尼斯军队总司令拜立夫移交给突尼斯国防部秘书长阿杜勒·哈密德·谢尔比。这批士兵将成为突尼斯陆军第一批部队。
