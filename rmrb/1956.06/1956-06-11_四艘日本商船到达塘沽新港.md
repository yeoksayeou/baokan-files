Title: Four Japanese Merchant Ships Arrive at Tanggu New Port
Author:
Date: 1956-06-11
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 8th. The Japanese merchant ships "Esumaru," "Hakusan Maru," "Yubari Maru," and "Matsunami Maru" arrived at Tanggu New Port and Dagukou yesterday and today.

　　These four merchant ships brought a total of 20,000 tons of cement and 3,500 tons of fertilizer.

　　Since the beginning of this year, the number of Japanese ships entering and leaving Tanggu New Port has increased month by month. In May of this year, Japanese goods imported from Tanggu New Port increased by seven and a half times compared with the same period last year; at the same time, China's exports to Japan also increased by more than two times compared with May of last year.

### 四艘日本商船到达塘沽新港

1956-06-11
第1版()
专栏：

    四艘日本商船到达塘沽新港
    新华社天津8日电　日本商船“惠须取丸”、“博山丸”、“夕张丸”和“松南丸”，在昨天和今天先后到达了塘沽新港和大沽口。
    这四艘商船共运来水泥两万吨、肥田粉三千五百吨。
    今年以来，进出塘沽新港的日本船只逐月增加。今年五月份，从塘沽新港进口的日本货物，比去年同期增加了七倍半；同时我国向日本出口的货物，比去年五月份也增加了两倍以上。
