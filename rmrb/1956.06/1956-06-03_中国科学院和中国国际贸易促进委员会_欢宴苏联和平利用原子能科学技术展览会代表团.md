Title: The Chinese Academy of Sciences and the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade Host a Banquet for the Delegation of the Soviet Union's Exhibition of Scientific and Technological Achievements in the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy

Author:
Date: 1956-06-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The Chinese Academy of Sciences and the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade held a banquet on the afternoon of the 2nd to welcome all members of the Soviet delegation to China for the Soviet Union's Exhibition of Scientific and Technological Achievements in the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy in Beijing.

Among the guests attending the banquet were Soviet physicists and mathematicians, Dr. Cherenkov, and medical scientist Dr. Fadeyeva. On the host side were Li Siguang, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Yang Fangzhi, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council, physicist Yan Jici, nuclear physicist Zhao Zhongyao, Wen Shizhen, Deputy Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, as well as people from the scientific, engineering, educational, and medical circles in Beijing, and personnel from the Soviet Embassy in China.

### 中国科学院和中国国际贸易促进委员会  欢宴苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会代表团

1956-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中国科学院和中国国际贸易促进委员会
    欢宴苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会代表团
    新华社2日讯　中国科学院和中国国际贸易促进委员会二日下午举行宴会，欢迎为在北京举办苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会来中国的苏联代表团全体团员。
    出席宴会的客人中，有苏联物理数学家契连柯夫博士、医学家法捷也娃博士。主人方面有中国科学院副院长李四光、国务院副秘书长杨放之、物理学家严济慈、核子物理学家赵忠尧、中国国际贸易促进委员会副秘书长文士桢以及北京科学、工程、教育、医务界人士和苏联驻华大使馆人员。
