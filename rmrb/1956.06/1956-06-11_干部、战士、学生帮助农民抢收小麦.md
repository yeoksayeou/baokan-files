Title: Cadres, Soldiers, and Students Help Farmers Harvest Wheat
Author:
Date: 1956-06-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency Report:

    Cadres, Soldiers, and Students Help Farmers Harvest Wheat
    Xinhua News Agency: In recent days, continuous rainfall has occurred in various counties and cities in Henan Province. A large number of cadres, soldiers, and students have been mobilized to help farmers harvest their ripe wheat.
    In counties and cities such as Yu County, Xingyang, Zhengzhou, and Kaifeng, the number of cadres, soldiers, and students mobilized to help farmers harvest wheat has exceeded one thousand in recent days. The troops of the Henan Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Zhengzhou dispatched more than 1,800 officers and soldiers in the past few days to help nine agricultural production cooperatives in the suburbs of Zhengzhou harvest more than 630 mu of wheat. University students and secondary school students in Kaifeng, Xinxiang, and other places also took advantage of Sundays or after-school hours to help farmers near their schools harvest wheat. During the harvest, they emphasized not only fast reaping but also thorough reaping and tight binding to reduce waste.

### 干部、战士、学生帮助农民抢收小麦

1956-06-11
第3版()
专栏：

    干部、战士、学生帮助农民抢收小麦
    新华社讯　近日来阴雨连绵，河南省各县、市有大批干部、战士、学生帮助农民抢收已经成熟了的小麦。
    禹县、荥阳、郑州、开封等县市，连日来出动帮助农民抢收小麦的干部、战士、学生等都在千人以上。中国人民解放军河南军区驻郑州市的部队，这几天出动了一千八百多名军官和战士，帮助郑州市郊区的九个农业生产合作社抢收了六百三十多亩小麦。开封、新乡等地的大学生和中等学校的学生，也利用星期天或课余时间帮助学校附近的农民抢收小麦。在收割中，他们提出不仅要割的快，而且要割的净，捆得紧，以减少浪费。
