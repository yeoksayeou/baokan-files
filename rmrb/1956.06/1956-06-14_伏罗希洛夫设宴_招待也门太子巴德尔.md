Title: Voroshilov Hosts Banquet in Honor of Yemeni Crown Prince Badr
Author:
Date: 1956-06-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 13th—According to TASS, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, chaired by Voroshilov, hosted a breakfast banquet in the Grand Kremlin Palace in Moscow on the 12th to welcome Crown Prince, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Yemen, Saif al-Islam Muhammad al-Badr, who is visiting at the invitation of the Soviet government.

Leaders of the Communist Party and the Government of the Soviet Union attended the banquet.

Voroshilov and the Yemeni Crown Prince exchanged toasts at the breakfast banquet.

On the same day, the Yemeni guests and their entourage toured the Soviet capital. They visited the Moscow Subway and the historical sites within the Kremlin.

Speaking about his initial impressions of visiting Moscow, the Yemeni guest said, "What I have seen exceeds all my expectations. The simplicity and sincerity of the Soviet people, and their hospitality, have made a deep impression on me."

### 伏罗希洛夫设宴  招待也门太子巴德尔

1956-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫设宴
    招待也门太子巴德尔
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫，十二日在莫斯科大克里姆林宫设早宴，招待应邀前来访问的也门王国皇太子、副首相兼外交大臣塞弗·伊斯兰·穆罕默德·巴德尔。
    苏联共产党和政府的领导人出席了早宴。
    伏罗希洛夫和也门皇太子在早宴上相互致了词。
    在同一天，也门客人和他的随行人员游览了苏联首都。他们参观了莫斯科地下铁道和克里姆林宫内的古迹。
    在谈到参观莫斯科的初步印象的时候，也门客人说，我所看到的超过了我所期待的一切。苏联人的纯朴、诚恳和他们的殷勤招待，使我得到很深的印象。
