Title: Foreign Diplomats Stationed in China Return to Beijing After Visiting Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1956-06-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 17th: Foreign diplomats stationed in China, at the invitation of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, took a special train to Northeast China for a visit on June 10th and returned to Beijing today.

The group consisted of 51 diplomats from the embassies, legations, and commercial offices of 24 countries stationed in China, including the Soviet Union, Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, North Korea, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Mongolia, the German Democratic Republic, the Democratic Republic of Viet Nam, India, Sweden, Denmark, Burma, Indonesia, Switzerland, Finland, Pakistan, Norway, Yugoslavia, Afghanistan, the United Kingdom, the Netherlands, and Egypt.

Accompanied by Wang Yutian, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the diplomats visited the cities of Harbin, Changchun, Shenyang, Fushun, and Anshan in Northeast China. They toured modern industrial and mining enterprises, agricultural production cooperatives, the Northeast Agricultural College, the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall, and scenic spots and historical sites.

### 各国驻中国外交官参观东北后返京

1956-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

    各国驻中国外交官参观东北后返京
    新华社17日讯　各国驻中国的外交官们，应中华人民共和国外交部的邀请，曾在六月十日乘专车去东北参观，今天已经回到北京。
    他们是苏联、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、朝鲜、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、蒙古、德意志民主共和国、越南民主共和国、印度、瑞典、丹麦、缅甸、印度尼西亚、瑞士、芬兰、巴基斯坦、挪威、南斯拉夫、阿富汗、英国、荷兰、埃及等二十四个国家驻中国的大使馆、公使馆、代办处、商务代办处的外交官，共五十一人。
    外交官们由外交部礼宾司副司长王雨田陪同，在东北访问了哈尔滨、长春、沈阳、抚顺、鞍山等城市，参观了现代化的工矿企业和农业生产合作社、以及东北农学院、东北工业陈列馆，并且游览了当地的名胜古迹。
