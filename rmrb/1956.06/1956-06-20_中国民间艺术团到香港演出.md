Title: Chinese Folk Art Troupe Arrives in Hong Kong for Performance
Author:
Date: 1956-06-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, June 18th: The Chinese Folk Art Troupe, consisting of seventy members and led by Ouyang Shan, head of the South China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, arrived in Hong Kong at around 3 PM on the 18th from Guangzhou at the invitation of Hong Kong compatriots from all walks of life.
　　Responsible individuals from the "Hong Kong Chinese General Chamber of Commerce Joint Cultural Circles Reception Committee for the Chinese Folk Art Troupe's Performance in Hong Kong" and well-known figures from all walks of life greeted them at the station.

### 中国民间艺术团到香港演出

1956-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中国民间艺术团到香港演出
    据新华社香港18日电　应香港各界同胞邀请到香港演出的中国民间艺术团一行七十人，在团长、华南文学艺术界联合会主席欧阳山率领下，已经在十八日下午三时许由广州到达香港。
      “香港中华总商会联合文化界接待中国民间艺术团莅港演出委员会”负责人和各界知名人士到车站迎接。
