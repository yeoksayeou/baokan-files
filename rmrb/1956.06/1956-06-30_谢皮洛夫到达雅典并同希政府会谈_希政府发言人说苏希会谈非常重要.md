Title: Shepilov Arrives in Athens and Meets with Greek Government; Greek Government Spokesman Says Soviet-Greek Talks Are Very Important
Author:
Date: 1956-06-30
Page: 2
    Xinhua News Agency, Athens, June 29th: Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov arrived in Athens yesterday after concluding his visit to Lebanon.
    Upon his arrival at the Athens airport, Shepilov said that he hoped his visit to Greece "will help establish and develop the necessary mutual understanding, trust, and sincerity in the relations between our two countries, and will be conducive to consolidating peace and strengthening cooperation in international affairs."
    Shepilov spoke about the traditional friendly relations between the two peoples. He said that the relations between the Soviet Union and Greece are based on the well-known principles of peaceful coexistence and cooperation in affairs, mutual respect for territorial and sovereign integrity, complete equality, and non-interference in internal affairs.
    Shepilov said: "There are still many untapped possibilities for further developing good-neighborly relations between our two countries. The Soviet government advocates making extensive use of these possibilities. The further development of economic and cultural ties between the Soviet Union and Greece will benefit the people of our two countries."
    Greek government officials, diplomatic envoys from some Middle Eastern and Eastern European countries, and many Athens residents welcomed Shepilov at the airport. In the suburbs and along the roads in the city of Athens, residents gathered in large numbers to warmly welcome the Soviet Foreign Minister.
    On the afternoon of the 28th, Shepilov held the first official talks with Greek Foreign Minister Averoff. After the talks, Averoff told reporters that he was "very satisfied" with the meeting.
    Shepilov will also hold talks with Greek Prime Minister Karamanlis.
    A spokesman for the Greek government said that Shepilov's talks with the heads of the Greek government would be "very important."

### 谢皮洛夫到达雅典并同希政府会谈  希政府发言人说苏希会谈非常重要

1956-06-30
第2版()
专栏：

    谢皮洛夫到达雅典并同希政府会谈
    希政府发言人说苏希会谈非常重要
    新华社29日讯　雅典消息：苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫在结束对黎巴嫩的访问以后，昨天到达雅典。
    谢皮洛夫在雅典机场发表谈话说，他希望他这次对希腊的访问，“将有助于建立和发展我们两国关系中的必要的相互谅解、信任和诚意，将有利于巩固和平和加强国际事务合作”。
    谢皮洛夫谈到两国人民间传统的友好关系。他说，苏联同希腊的关系是建立在和平共处和事务合作、相互尊重领土和主权的完整、完全平等和互不干涉内政这些著名原则的基础上的。
    谢皮洛夫说：“在进一步发展我们两国之间的善邻关系方面，还有很多没有利用的可能性。苏联政府主张广泛利用这些可能性。苏联和希腊两国的经济和文化联系的进一步发展将有利于我们两国人民。”
    希腊政府官员，一些中东和东欧国家外交使节以及许多雅典居民在机场上欢迎谢皮洛夫。在雅典的近郊和市内的道路旁，居民们成群结队地热烈欢迎苏联外交部长。
    二十八日下午，谢皮洛夫同希腊外交大臣阿维罗夫举行了第一次的正式会谈。会谈以后，阿维罗夫对新闻记者说，他对这次会谈感到“非常满意”。
    谢皮洛夫还要同希腊首相卡拉曼利斯举行会谈。
    希腊政府的一位发言人曾经说，谢皮洛夫同希腊政府首脑们的会谈将是“非常重要的”。
