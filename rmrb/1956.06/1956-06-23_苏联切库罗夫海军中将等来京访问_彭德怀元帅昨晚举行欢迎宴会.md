Title: Soviet Vice Admiral Chekurov and Others Arrive in Beijing for Visit; Marshal Peng Dehuai Hosted Welcome Banquet Last Night
Author:
Date: 1956-06-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd: Soviet Pacific Fleet Commander Vice Admiral Chekurov and Military Commissar Rear Admiral Pochubailo arrived in Beijing by plane from Shanghai at 4:10 PM today for a short tour and visit. Commander of the East China Sea Fleet of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Rear Admiral Peng Deqing, accompanied them.
    Welcoming them at the airport were Deputy Minister of National Defense General Li Da, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Director of the General Political Department General Xiao Hua, Deputy Commander of the Navy Admiral Wang Hongkun, and senior generals Su Zhenhua Admiral, Xiao Xiangrong Lieutenant General, Yuan Ye Naval Rear Admiral, He Tingyi Major General, Zeng Mei Major General, Ji He Major General, Deputy Director of the Soviet Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Li Huichuan, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China Li Haiqiu, and military attachés of various countries in China.
    When Vice Admiral Chekurov, Rear Admiral Pochubailo, and others flew over Nanjing from Shanghai on a special plane at 8:35 AM on the 22nd, they disembarked to pay respects at the Rain Flower Terrace Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery and visit the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum. Chekurov, on behalf of all naval officers and soldiers of the Soviet Union, laid wreaths at both mausoleums to pay tribute. They all expressed deep condolences to Mr. Sun Yat-sen and the revolutionary martyrs of the Chinese people.
    Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd: Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense Marshal Peng Dehuai hosted a banquet this evening to welcome Soviet Pacific Fleet Commander Vice Admiral Chekurov and Military Commissar Rear Admiral Pochubailo, who are leading the Soviet fleet on a friendly visit to China.
    Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China Li Haiqiu and Military Attaché Major General Zudov were also invited to attend the banquet.
    Marshal Peng Dehuai said in his speech: The great friendship between the people and armies of China and the Soviet Union not only promotes the common prosperity and progress of our two countries, but is also an important factor in maintaining peace in the Far East and the world. We firmly believe that this friendly visit by the Soviet fleet will further strengthen the fraternal friendship and unity between the people of China and the Soviet Union. Marshal Peng Dehuai proposed a toast to the heroic Soviet Navy, to the unbreakable friendship between the people and armies of China and the Soviet Union, and to the health of Soviet Minister of Defense Marshal Zhukov.
    Vice Admiral Chekurov then delivered a speech, thanking them for the warm reception and kindness to the Soviet fleet. He said: We are deeply moved by the warm reception we have received everywhere. We regard these as expressions of fraternal friendship towards our Soviet people. We Soviets are proud to have such a great and loyal friend as the Chinese people. The friendship and cooperation between the Soviet Union and the socialist camp countries is an important factor in the struggle for peace and socialism. Chekurov finally proposed a toast to the Chinese government, the Chinese Communist Party, the Chinese people, and the health of Marshal Peng Dehuai.
    Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Tan Zheng, Deputy Commander of the Navy Admiral Wang Hongkun, and Deputy Political Commissar Su Zhenhua Admiral successively proposed toasts at the banquet to the friendship between the armies and navies of China and the Soviet Union, and to all the officers and soldiers of the Soviet visiting fleet.
    Before the banquet, Marshal Peng Dehuai met with Vice Admiral Chekurov and Rear Admiral Pochubailo and had a cordial conversation with them for an hour.
    Also present at the banquet were senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Chen Geng, General Xu Guangda, General Li Da, General Zhang Zongxun, General Xiao Hua, General Chen Shiqu, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Qiu Chuangcheng, Naval Rear Admiral Yuan Ye, Major General He Tingyi, Major General Zeng Mei, Major General Ji He, Naval Rear Admiral Peng Deqing, and military attachés of various countries in China. ### 苏联切库罗夫海军中将等来京访问  彭德怀元帅昨晚举行欢迎宴会

1956-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联切库罗夫海军中将等来京访问
    彭德怀元帅昨晚举行欢迎宴会
    据新华社22日讯　苏联太平洋舰队司令切库罗夫海军中将和军事委员波丘巴伊洛海军少将今天下午四点十分从上海乘飞机到达北京，进行短期的参观访问。中国人民解放军海军东海舰队司令彭德清海军少将陪同前来。
    在飞机场欢迎他们的，有国防部副部长李达上将，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊上将，总政治部副主任萧华上将，海军副司令员王宏坤海军上将和高级将领苏振华上将、萧向荣中将、袁也烈海军少将、何廷一少将、曾美少将、吉合少将，外交部苏联东欧司副司长李汇川，苏联驻华大使馆临时代办李海秋和各国驻华武官。
    切库罗夫海军中将和波丘巴伊洛海军少将等在二十二日上午八时三十五分从上海乘专机过南京的时候，曾下飞机去雨花台人民革命烈士墓凭吊，到中山陵谒陵。切库罗夫代表苏联全体海军官兵向两陵墓献花圈致敬。他们都对孙中山先生和中国人民革命烈士们表示了深切的悼念。
    新华社22日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅今天晚间举行宴会，欢迎率领苏联舰队来中国进行友好访问的苏联太平洋舰队司令切库罗夫海军中将和军事委员波丘巴伊洛海军少将。
    苏联驻华大使馆临时代办李海秋、武官朱道夫少将也应邀出席了宴会。
    彭德怀元帅致词说：中苏两国人民和军队间的伟大友谊，不仅促进了我们两国的共同繁荣和进步，而且也是维护远东和世界和平的重要因素。我们深信，通过苏联舰队这次友好访问，必将进一步加强中苏两国人民的兄弟友谊和团结。彭德怀元帅提议为英雄的苏联海军、为中苏两国人民和军队间的牢不可破的友谊、为苏联国防部长朱可夫元帅的健康干杯。
    切库罗夫海军中将接着致词感谢对苏联舰队的热情招待和好意。他说：我们到处受到的热情招待使我们深深感动。我们把这些看作是对我们苏联人民的兄弟友谊的表现。我们苏联人以有着中国人民这样伟大的忠实的朋友感到自豪。苏联和社会主义阵营各国之间的友谊和合作，是争取和平和社会主义斗争中的重要因素。切库罗夫最后提议为中国政府、中国共产党、中国人民和彭德怀元帅的健康干杯。
    中国人民解放军总政治部副主任谭政大将，海军副司令员王宏坤海军上将、副政委苏振华上将在宴会上先后提议为中苏两国军队和海军之间的友谊、为苏联访华舰队全体官兵干杯。
    在宴会以前，彭德怀元帅接见了切库罗夫海军中将和波丘巴伊洛海军少将，同他们进行了一小时的亲切谈话。
    出席宴会作陪的还有中国人民解放军高级将领陈赓大将、许光达大将、李达上将、张宗逊上将、萧华上将、陈士榘上将、萧向荣中将、丘创成中将、袁也烈海军少将、何廷一少将、曾美少将、吉合少将、彭德清海军少将，以及各国驻华武官等人。（附图片）
