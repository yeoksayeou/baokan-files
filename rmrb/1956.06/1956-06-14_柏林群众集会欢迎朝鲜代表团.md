Title: Berlin Mass Rally Welcomes Korean Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-06-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, June 13th. A mass rally was held in Berlin on June 12th by the working people of Berlin to welcome the Korean government delegation headed by Kim Il-sung.

    Prime Minister Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic spoke at the rally. He said that the German Democratic Republic and the German people are loyal friends and allies of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the entire Korean people, and are ready to do their utmost to help and support the Korean people's struggle to maintain peace and for the peaceful reunification of their country.

    Premier Kim Il-sung of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea then spoke. He emphasized the friendly and cooperative relations between the peoples of Korea and Germany, and expressed his gratitude to the German people for their assistance in Korea's recovery and development.

    He pointed out that the Korean government delegation and the German Democratic Republic government delegation had held talks on issues concerning the two governments, and that both sides had reached a consensus and signed a joint declaration, a cultural and economic cooperation agreement, etc. There is no doubt that this will further strengthen the friendly relations between the two countries and will make a great contribution to the common cause of the two peoples in maintaining peace and striving for national reunification.

### 柏林群众集会欢迎朝鲜代表团

1956-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

    柏林群众集会欢迎朝鲜代表团
    新华社柏林13日电　柏林劳动人民六月十二日举行群众大会，欢迎以金日成为首的朝鲜政府代表团。
    民主德国总理格罗提渥在大会上讲了话。他说，德意志民主共和国和德国人民是朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和全体朝鲜人民的忠实朋友和盟邦，并且准备尽力帮助和支持朝鲜人民为维护和平和为祖国的和平统一而进行的斗争。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成接着讲话。他强调指出朝鲜和德国两国人民之间的友好合作关系，并且对德国人民在朝鲜的恢复和发展方面的援助表示感谢。
    他指出，朝鲜政府代表团和民主德国政府代表团已经就有关两国政府的问题进行了会谈，双方取得了一致的看法，并且签订了联合宣言以及文化经济合作协定等。毫无疑问，这将更加加强两国之间的友好关系，并且将对两国人民为了维护和平和争取民族统一的共同事业作出巨大贡献。
