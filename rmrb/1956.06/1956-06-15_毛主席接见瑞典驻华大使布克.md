Title: Chairman Mao Receives Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Book

Author: 
Date: 1956-06-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 14th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received the Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Book, this afternoon at the Qinzheng Hall in Zhongnanhai. Also received were the embassy's archive secretary, Mr. Malmquist, and Mr. Ekston. Present at the reception were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs Huang Hua, and Director of the Bureau of Protocol of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Yu Xinqing.

### 毛主席接见瑞典驻华大使布克

1956-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见瑞典驻华大使布克
    新华社14日讯　中华人民共和国毛泽东主席，今天下午在中南海勤政殿接见了瑞典驻中国大使布克。同时受接见的还有大使馆档案秘书马尔姆奎斯特和艾克斯顿。接见时在座的，有外交部副部长张闻天、西欧非洲司司长黄华，全国人民代表大会常务委员会典礼局局长余心清。
