Title: Soviet and Yugoslav Leaders Hold Talks; Bulganin Hosts Banquet for Tito
Author:
Date: 1956-06-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 5th. TASS reports: The President of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, Tito, and the leaders of the Soviet government held their first meeting at 10:00 AM (Moscow time) on June 5th in the Soviet Council of Ministers Presidium meeting hall in the Kremlin. The talks were conducted in a sincere and friendly atmosphere.

The Yugoslav side participating in the talks included Tito, Vice President of the Federal Executive Council Kardelj, State Secretary for Foreign Affairs Popović, Member of the Federal Executive Council Todorović, Chairman of the Executive Council of the People's Republic of Croatia Blažević, and Yugoslav Ambassador to the Soviet Union Mićunović, among others.

The Soviet side participating in the talks included Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Voroshilov, Bulganin, Mikoyan, Molotov, Khrushchev, Shepilov, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union Kuznetsov, and Soviet Ambassador to Yugoslavia Filyubin, among others.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 5th. TASS reports: Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers Bulganin hosted a banquet for Yugoslav President Tito at 13:00 (Moscow time) on June 5th in the Grand Kremlin Palace.

Leaders of Yugoslavia and the Soviet Union attended the banquet.

### 苏南两国领导人会谈  布尔加宁设宴招待铁托

1956-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    苏南两国领导人会谈
    布尔加宁设宴招待铁托
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统铁托和苏联政府的领导人六月五日十时（莫斯科时间）在克里姆林宫苏联部长会议主席团会议大厅举行第一次会谈。会谈是在诚挚友好的气氛中进行的。
    南斯拉夫方面参加会谈的有铁托、联邦执行委员会副主席卡德尔、外交国务秘书长波波维奇、联邦执行委员会委员托多罗维奇、克罗地亚人民共和国执行委员会主席布拉热维奇和南斯拉夫驻苏联大使米丘诺维奇等人。
    苏联方面参加会谈的有：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫，布尔加宁、米高扬、莫洛托夫、赫鲁晓夫、谢皮洛夫，苏联外交部第一副部长库兹涅佐夫和苏联驻南斯拉夫大使费留宾等人。
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁六月五日十三时（莫斯科时间）在大克里姆林宫设宴招待南斯拉夫总统铁托。
    出席宴会的有南斯拉夫和苏联的领导人。
