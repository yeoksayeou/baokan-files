Title: All-China Women's Federation Sends Telegram to Congratulate Conference Opening
Author:
Date: 1956-06-16
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The All-China Democratic Women's Federation sent a telegram to the World Women Workers' Conference on June 14th, congratulating the opening of the conference. The telegram stated: We believe that this conference will help strengthen the unity and friendship of working women in various countries around the world, and will help strive for the rights of working women and defend the sacred cause of world peace. We sincerely wish the conference success.

### 我国妇联电贺会议开幕

1956-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    我国妇联电贺会议开幕
    新华社15日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会六月十四日致电世界女工会议，祝贺会议的开幕。电文中说：我们相信这次会议将有助于加强世界各国劳动妇女的团结和友谊，有助于争取劳动妇女的权利和保卫世界和平的神圣事业。我们衷心祝贺大会成功。
