Title: Japanese Delegation from Three Groups Goes to Fushun to Visit Imprisoned Japanese War Criminals
Author:
Date: 1956-06-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, June 25th: Representatives from three Japanese groups—the Japanese Red Cross Society, the Japan-China Friendship Association, and the Japan Peace Liaison Council—Mr. Hata Michiyoshi, Ms. Ikeda Toshiko, and Mr. Nakajima Toshishige, accompanied by Mr. Yi Chengxin, a representative from the Chinese Red Cross Society, departed from Tianjin for Fushun today. In Fushun, they will visit Japanese war criminals imprisoned for their crimes of aggression against China and Japanese war criminals sentenced to imprisonment by the Special Military Tribunal of the Supreme People's Court of the People's Republic of China.

Accompanying the Japanese delegation from the three groups to Fushun are Mr. Hamada Katsuhiro, a reporter from the Japan Broadcasting Corporation, and Ms. Yamada Yoshie, a reporter from the Central Japan Broadcasting Radio Drama Troupe.

### 日本三团体部分代表去抚顺  访问在押的日本战争犯罪分子

1956-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    日本三团体部分代表去抚顺  
    访问在押的日本战争犯罪分子
    新华社天津25日电　日本红十字会、日中友好协会、日本和平联络会三团体代表畑道好、池田利子、中岛利重今天在中国红十字会代表衣成信的陪同下，从天津去抚顺。他们在抚顺将访问在押的日本侵略中国战争中的战争犯罪分子和被中华人民共和国最高人民法院特别军事法庭判处徒刑的日本战争犯罪分子。
    随日本三团体代表到抚顺访问的还有日本广播协会记者滨田胜弘和中部日本广播电台剧团记者山田义江。
