Title: Accelerating Commodity Turnover in Central South and Northeast China: The First Cargo Ship of the Dalian-Hankou Direct Regular Freighter Service, "Peace No. 3," Arrived in Wuhan on June 17th.
Author:
Date: 1956-06-22
Page: 1

    The first cargo ship, "Peace No. 3," of the Dalian-Hankou direct regular freighter service, which accelerates commodity turnover in the Central South and Northeast regions, arrived in Wuhan on June 17th.
