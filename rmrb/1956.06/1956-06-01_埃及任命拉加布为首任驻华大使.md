Title: Egypt Appoints Ragab as First Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1956-06-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 31st. The government of the Republic of Egypt has decided to appoint Mr. Hassan Ragab as the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of China.

### 埃及任命拉加布为首任驻华大使

1956-06-01

专栏：

    埃及任命拉加布为首任驻华大使
