Title: Chairman Mao Hosts a Banquet for Nepalese Ambassador to China, Rana
Author:
Date: 1956-06-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 13th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China held a banquet tonight in the Qinzheng Hall of Zhongnanhai to welcome Lieutenant General Rana, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Nepal to China, along with First Secretary Banter and Colonel Basnyat, the military attaché.

Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, was also invited to attend the banquet.

Those attending the banquet as hosts included Vice Chairman Zhu De of the People's Republic of China, Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council, Vice Chairpersons Song Qingling, Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Peng Zhen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Vice Premiers Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, and Ulanhu of the State Council, Vice Chairman Zhang Bojun of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Vice Chairmen Cheng Qian, Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, and Long Yun of the National Defense Council, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian.

### 毛主席欢宴尼泊尔驻华大使拉纳

1956-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席欢宴尼泊尔驻华大使拉纳
    新华社13日讯　中华人民共和国毛泽东主席今晚在中南海勤政殿举行宴会，欢迎尼泊尔王国驻中国首任特命全权大使拉纳中将和一等秘书班特瑞、武官巴斯尼亚特上校等。
    印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会作陪的，有中华人民共和国朱德副主席，全国人民代表大会常务委员会刘少奇委员长，国务院周恩来总理，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄、李济深、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真，国务院副总理彭德怀、邓小平、乌兰夫，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，国防委员会副主席程潜、张治中、傅作义、龙云，对外贸易部长叶季壮，文化部长沈雁冰和外交部副部长张闻天。
