Title: Soviet Fleet Leaves Durrës to Return Home
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, via TASS reports: The Soviet fleet, which was invited to Albania for a friendly visit, left the port of Durrës on June 10th to return home.

This Soviet fleet, composed of the cruiser "Kutuzov" and two destroyers, arrived in Durrës on June 5th and was warmly welcomed by the Albanian people. Many working people and officers and soldiers of the People's Army visited the warships, exchanged gifts with the Soviet sailors, and left each other's mailing addresses. The song and dance troupe of the Soviet fleet performed wonderful programs for the residents of Tirana and Durrës.

Admiral Kasatonov, who led the Soviet fleet, held a farewell reception on the 9th. Leaders of the Albanian Labor Party and the government attended the reception.

### 苏联舰队离都拉斯回国

1956-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联舰队离都拉斯回国
    据新华社据塔斯社报道　应邀到阿尔巴尼亚作友好访问的苏联舰队，在六月十日离开都拉斯港口回国。
    这支由巡洋舰“库图佐夫号”和两艘驱逐舰组成的苏联舰队，在六月五日到达都拉斯以后，受到了阿尔巴尼亚人民的热烈欢迎。许多劳动人民和人民军的官兵曾经到军舰上参观，和苏联水兵交换了礼物，并且彼此留下了通讯地址。苏联舰队的歌舞团曾经为地拉那和都拉斯的居民演出了精彩的节目。
    率领苏联舰队的卡萨托诺夫海军上将，在九日举行了告别招待会。阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和政府的领导人出席了这次招待会。
