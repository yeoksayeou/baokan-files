Title: Premier Zhou Receives Representatives to the Four-Nation Fisheries Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-06-13
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 12th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the delegations from the Soviet Union, North Korea, Vietnam, and China who were attending the Four-Nation Fisheries Research Conference in Beijing this afternoon.

    Present at the reception were Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China; Hoang Van Hoan, the Vietnamese Ambassador to China; Kim Kwi-nam, the Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the North Korean Embassy in China; and others.

    Also present at the reception were Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Yan Baohang, Chairman of the Treaty Committee of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 周总理接见参加四国渔业会议的代表

1956-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见参加四国渔业会议的代表
    新华社12日讯　　周恩来总理今天下午在北京接见出席四国渔业研究会议的苏联、朝鲜、越南、中国四国代表团。
    接见时在座的，有苏联驻中国大使尤金院士、越南驻中国大使黄文欢、朝鲜驻中国大使馆临时代办金贵南等人。
    陪同接见的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、中国科学院院长郭沫若，国务院副总理李先念、秘书长习仲勋，外交部副部长张闻天、外交部部长助理何伟和外交部条约委员会主任委员阎宝航。
