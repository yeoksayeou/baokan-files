Title: Japanese Socialist Party Issues Statement Opposing the Establishment of US Missile Base in Japan
Author:
Date: 1956-06-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, June 26th, Tokyo Dispatch: The Japanese Socialist Party issued a statement on June 25th, firmly opposing the United States' plan to establish missile bases in Japan.

    The Socialist Party demanded that the Japanese government immediately contact the US government and request the US government to promptly cancel this plan.
    According to Japanese newspapers, a spokesman for the US Far East Naval Command said on June 25th that the United States would establish missile equipment and repair facilities near the Yokosuka naval base in fiscal year 1957. US naval authorities have requested Congressional approval to allocate $240,000 to establish this missile base.

### 日本社会党发表声明  反对美国在日本建立导弹基地

1956-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    日本社会党发表声明
    反对美国在日本建立导弹基地
    据新华社26日讯　东京消息：日本社会党六月二十五日发表声明，坚决反对美国在日本建立导弹基地的计划。
    社会党要求日本政府立即同美国政府接洽，要求美国政府迅速取消这个计划。
    据日本报纸报道，美国远东海军司令部发言人六月二十五日说，美国将在1957财政年度内在海军基地横须贺附近建立导弹设备和修理厂。美国海军当局已经要求国会同意拨款二十四万美元来建立这个导弹基地。
