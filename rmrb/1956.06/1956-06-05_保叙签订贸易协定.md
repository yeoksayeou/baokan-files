Title: Bulgaria and Syria Sign Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-06-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 4th: Syria and Bulgaria signed a trade agreement in Damascus on June 2nd. The agreement is for a term of one year, but can be extended with the consent of both parties when it expires.

According to the agreement, Syria will exchange agricultural products for Bulgarian industrial goods.

### 保叙签订贸易协定

1956-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    保叙签订贸易协定
    新华社4日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚和保加利亚六月二日在大马士革签订了一项贸易协定。协定的期限是一年，但在期满的时候，在双方的同意下，还可以延长。
    根据这项协定，叙利亚将以农产品交换保加利亚的工业品。
