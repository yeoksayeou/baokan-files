Title: The Third Session of the National People's Congress to Open Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1956-06-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 13th: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its forty-first meeting on the afternoon of June 13th.

After hearing a report by Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman and Secretary-General, on the preparations for the Third Session of the First National People's Congress, the meeting decided that the Third Session of the First National People's Congress would be held in Beijing on June 15th. It also adopted the draft agenda of the Third Session of the First National People's Congress, the list of candidates for the Presidium and Secretary-General of the Third Session of the First National People's Congress, and the list of candidates for the Chairman and members of the Proposals Examination Committee of the Third Session of the First National People's Congress, to be submitted to the Third Session of the First National People's Congress for consideration. The meeting also determined the list of temporary conveners for each delegation group of the Third Session of the First National People's Congress.

The meeting also adopted the Standing Committee's report on the handling of proposals from the Second Session of the First National People's Congress.

The meeting also heard a statement by Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai regarding the draft 1955 National Final Accounts and the draft 1956 National Budget, which were submitted to the Third Session of the First National People's Congress for review and approval. Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Finance Li Xiannian also made supplementary explanations on these drafts at the meeting.

The meeting also decided to appoint and approve several appointments and removals.

### 全国人民代表大会第三次会议明天开幕

1956-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会第三次会议明天开幕
    新华社13日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会十三日下午举行第四十一次会议。
    会议在听了副委员长兼秘书长彭真关于第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议的准备工作的报告后，决定第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议六月十五日在北京举行，并且通过了第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议议程草案、第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议主席团、秘书长候选名单、第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议提案审查委员会主任委员和委员候选名单，作为建议提交第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议。会议并且确定了第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议各代表小组临时召集人名单。
    会议还通过了常务委员会关于第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议提案办理情形的报告。
    会议还听取了国务院总理周恩来关于提请第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议审定的1955年国家决算草案和1956年国家预算草案的说明。国务院副总理兼财政部长李先念也在会上就这草案作了补充说明。
    会议还决定任命和批准任免事项多起。
