Title: GDR Expands Trade with Middle Eastern Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-06-05
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, June 4th: The Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign and Domestic Trade of the German Democratic Republic, Heinrich Rau, presented a report to the Council of Ministers regarding his visit to Sudan, Syria, Lebanon, and Egypt. He stated that during the government talks with these countries, agreements were reached on expanding trade relations, conducting scientific and technological cooperation, and expanding cultural ties. Furthermore, the following agreements were also reached: the government leaders of these countries will visit the German Democratic Republic in order to expand and strengthen direct contacts and understanding of the situation in the Republic.

### 民主德国同中东国家扩大贸易

1956-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国同中东国家扩大贸易
    新华社柏林4日电　民主德国副总理兼对外与国内贸易部部长海因里希·劳向部长会议提出了关于访问苏丹、叙利亚、黎巴嫩和埃及的报告。他说，在同这些国家的政府会谈的过程中，在扩大贸易关系、进行科学技术合作和扩大文化联系等问题上都达成了协议。此外还达成了下列协议：这些国家的政府领导人将访问德意志民主共和国，以便扩大和加强直接的联系和了解共和国的情况。
