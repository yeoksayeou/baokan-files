Title: Egyptian Revolutionary Guidance Committee Nominates Nasser as Presidential Candidate
Author:
Date: 1956-06-10
Page: 4

    Egyptian Revolutionary Guidance Committee
    Nominates Nasser as Presidential Candidate
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, June 8th: TASS reports, citing "Republic Daily," that the Egyptian Revolutionary Guidance Committee has resolved to nominate Prime Minister Gamal Abdel Nasser as a candidate for President of the Republic of Egypt.
    Egypt will hold a nationwide referendum on June 23rd to elect the President of the Republic and to adopt the Constitution of the Republic of Egypt drafted by the government.
    Currently, Egypt is actively preparing for the referendum. The voter registry has been compiled, and voter cards have been issued. Ballots have been printed and are being distributed to various election committees. Each voter will receive two ballots: one asking whether he approves of electing Gamal Abdel Nasser as President, and the other asking whether he agrees with the new Constitution.

### 埃及革命指导委员会  推举纳赛尔为总统候选人

1956-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及革命指导委员会
    推举纳赛尔为总统候选人
    据新华社9日讯　据塔斯社开罗八日讯：据“共和国报”报道，埃及革命指导委员会决议推举加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔总理为埃及共和国总统候选人。
    埃及将在六月二十三日举行全民投票选举共和国总统，并且通过政府制定的埃及共和国宪法。
    目前，埃及正积极准备全民投票。选民名册已经编好，选民证已经发出。选票已经印好并且正在分发给各选举委员会。每个选民将得到两张票：一张票要他表示是否赞成选举加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔当总统。另一张票问，他是否同意新宪法。
