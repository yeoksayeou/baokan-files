Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1956-06-11
Page: 4

    ・Friendly Exchanges・
    Niphon Saba, the Lebanese Orthodox Bishop, and his secretary, Rustom Salem Malek, arrived in Beijing on the 10th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.
    △ At the invitation of the China Political Science and Law Association, a delegation of legal workers from South American countries attending the Sixth Congress of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers has come to China for a visit. Delegations from Argentina and Brazil arrived in Beijing on the 5th and 8th, respectively.
    △ Maurice Cuvillon, manager of the French newspaper "Liberation," arrived in Beijing on the evening of the 7th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.
    △ The Chinese Women's Delegation left Hanoi by train on the morning of the 8th to return home.

### 友好往来

1956-06-11
第4版()
专栏：

    ·友好往来·
    应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来中国访问的黎巴嫩东正教主教尼冯·萨巴和他的秘书鲁斯托姆·萨勒姆·马列克，在十日下午到达北京。
    △出席国际民主法律工作者协会第六届大会的南美国家的法律工作者代表团，应中国政治法律学会邀请前来我国访问。阿根廷和巴西两国的代表团已分别于五日和八日到达北京。
    △应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来中国访问的法国“解放报”经理莫里斯·居维戎在七日晚到达北京。
    △中国妇女代表团在八日上午乘火车离开河内回国。
