Title: Soviet State Leaders Send Telegrams to U Nu and U Ba Swe, Hoping to Further Strengthen the Friendly Relations Between the Peoples of the Soviet Union and Burma

Author: 
Date: 1956-06-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 18th: Khrushchev and Bulganin sent telegrams to U Nu, Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma, and U Ba Swe, the new Prime Minister of the Union of Burma.

The telegram to U Nu stated: "Comrade Nu, as an outstanding statesman and political activist of our time, you have made many efforts to consolidate universal peace and to develop close cooperation and sincere friendship between our two peoples during your leadership of the Burmese government. Your fruitful activities are well-known and highly valued not only by the peoples of Burma and the Soviet Union but also by the peoples of all countries who yearn for peace and prosperity."

Khrushchev and Bulganin expressed their firm belief that U Nu, as Chairman of the League, will continue to strengthen and develop the friendship between the peoples of Burma and the Soviet Union, and wished U Nu the most fulfilling achievements in his activities.

In their telegram to U Ba Swe, Bulganin and Khrushchev congratulated him on his assumption of the post of Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, and expressed their conviction that the friendly relations and cooperation between the Union of Burma and the Soviet Union, established with the active assistance of U Nu, will continue to be smoothly strengthened and developed in the interests of our two peoples and the consolidation of universal peace.

The telegram concluded by congratulating Prime Minister U Ba Swe and the people of Burma on their new achievements in developing the national economy and culture, and in strengthening the independence of the Union of Burma.

### 苏联国家领导人致电吴努和吴巴瑞  希望进一步加强苏缅两国人民的友好关系

1956-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联国家领导人致电吴努和吴巴瑞
    希望进一步加强苏缅两国人民的友好关系
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科十八日讯：赫鲁晓夫和布尔加宁打电报给缅甸反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努和新任缅甸联邦总理吴巴瑞。
    致吴努的电报说：“努同志，你作为当代一位杰出的国务活动家和政治活动家，在领导缅甸政府的时候，为巩固普遍和平、为发展我们两国人民的紧密合作和真诚友谊而作了许多努力。你的有成效的活动不仅为缅甸和苏联两国人民而且也为热望和平和繁荣的各国人民所熟知，并且得到他们的很高的评价。”
    赫鲁晓夫和布尔加宁表示坚信吴努作为同盟的主席，将继续加强和发展缅甸和苏联两国人民的友谊，并且祝吴努在他的活动中获得最美满的成就。
    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫在致吴巴瑞的电报中祝贺他出任缅甸联邦总理，并且表示深信在吴努的积极协助下建立起来的缅甸联邦和苏联之间的友好关系和合作今后将为了我们两国人民和巩固普遍和平的利益而顺利加强和发展。
    电报最后祝贺吴巴瑞总理和缅甸全国人民在发展民族经济和文化、在加强缅甸联邦的独立中获得新成就。
