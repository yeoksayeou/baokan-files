Title: Yugoslav Ambassador to the Soviet Union Holds Reception for President Tito's Visit
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 19th: According to TASS, the Yugoslav Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Mićunović, held a reception on the 18th for the visit of the President of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, Tito, to the Soviet Union.
    The Yugoslav government delegation, headed by Yugoslav President Tito, attended the reception.
    The Soviet side attending the reception included leaders of the Communist Party and government, representatives from all walks of life, and responsible personnel from various ministries and administrative organs.
    Diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union, as well as Soviet and foreign journalists, also attended the reception.

### 为铁托总统访问苏联  南驻苏大使举行招待会

1956-06-20
第4版()
专栏：

    为铁托总统访问苏联
    南驻苏大使举行招待会
    新华社19日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：南斯拉夫驻苏联大使米丘诺维奇，十八日为南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统铁托的访问苏联举行招待会。
    以南斯拉夫总统铁托为首的南斯拉夫政府代表团出席了招待会。
    苏联方面出席招待会的有共产党和政府的领导人、社会各界代表、一些部和主管机关的负责工作人员。
    出席招待会的还有各国驻苏联的外交使节，以及苏联和外国的新闻记者。
