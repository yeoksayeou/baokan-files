Title: National People's Congress Deputies Watch Yunnan Opera and Peking Opera
Author:
Date: 1956-06-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. The Third Session of the First National People's Congress adjourned today.

    On this Sunday evening, the deputies watched Yunnan Opera performances by the Yunnan Provincial Yunnan Opera Troupe, including "Beating the Melon to Recruit a Bride," "Niu Gao Tears Up the Imperial Edict," and "Borrowing a Relative for a Match."
    Last night, the deputies were also invited to attend a reception hosted by the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee, where they watched Peking Opera.

### 全国人民代表大会代表观看滇剧和京剧

1956-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会代表观看滇剧和京剧
    新华社17日讯　第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议今天休会。
    在这个星期日的晚上，代表们观看了云南省滇剧团演出的“打瓜招亲”、“牛皋扯旨”、“借亲配”等滇剧。
    昨天晚上，代表们还应邀出席中共北京市委员会和北京市人民委员会的招待晚会，观看了京剧。
