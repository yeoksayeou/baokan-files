Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Two German Writers
Author:
Date: 1956-06-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th June. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received German writers Ernst Schumacher and Hans Zibulka on the afternoon of the 5th. They were invited to China to participate in the commemorative activities for the world cultural celebrity Heinrich Heine. Present at the reception were Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Gipner, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China.

### 周恩来总理接见两位德国作家

1956-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见两位德国作家
    新华社5日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，在五日下午接见了应邀来中国参加世界文化名人海涅的纪念活动的德国作家恩斯特·舒玛赫和汉斯·齐布尔卡。接见的时候，在座的有中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南和德意志民主共和国驻华大使纪普纳。
