Title: China Releases Two American Prisoners
Author:
Date: 1956-06-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. Two American prisoners, Kim Rife and Fei Youwen, had completed their sentences on June 15th. They have been released on schedule by our competent authorities.

### 我国释放两名美国犯人

1956-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    我国释放两名美国犯人
    新华社15日讯　美国犯人金理福和斐有文两人已经在六月十五日服刑期满。他们已经由我主管机关如期释放。
