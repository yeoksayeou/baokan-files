Title: Kim Il-sung to Visit the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-06-01
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, May 31st: It was announced here today that Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of North Korea, will lead a North Korean government delegation on a friendly visit to the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Soviet government.

### 金日成将访问苏联

1956-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

    金日成将访问苏联
    新华社平壤31日电　今天这里宣布：朝鲜内阁首相金日成应苏联政府的邀请，将要率领朝鲜政府代表团到苏联进行友好访问。
