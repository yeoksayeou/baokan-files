Title: Cambodian King Writes to Zawadzki, Stating Sihanouk's Visit to Poland Marks the Beginning of Cambodian-Polish Friendly Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1956-06-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, June 5th: Former Cambodian Prime Minister Sihanouk, who was invited to visit Poland, met with the Chairman of the Polish Council of State, Zawadzki, after arriving in Warsaw on the afternoon of the 3rd, and handed Zawadzki a letter from Cambodian King Norodom Suramarit.

In the letter, the Cambodian King, in his personal name and on behalf of the Cambodian people, congratulated the Polish people on their achievements. The letter expressed the belief that Sihanouk's visit to Poland would mark the beginning of a period of friendly cooperation between Cambodia and Poland.

On the evening of the 3rd, Sihanouk and his entourage visited the Palace of Culture and Science in Warsaw.

On June 4th, Sihanouk toured the city of Warsaw and visited the Zeran Automobile Factory and the National Museum in Warsaw.

### 柬埔寨国王致函萨瓦茨基表示  西哈努克访问波兰是柬波友好合作的开始

1956-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨国王致函萨瓦茨基表示
    西哈努克访问波兰是柬波友好合作的开始
    新华社华沙5日电　应邀到波兰访问的柬埔寨前首相西哈努克，在三日下午到达华沙以后，会见了波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基，把柬埔寨国王诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特的一封信交给了萨瓦茨基。
    柬埔寨国王在信中以他个人的名义并且代表柬埔寨人民祝波兰人民获得成就。信中表示相信，西哈努克的访问波兰，将是柬埔寨和波兰之间友好合作的时期的开始。
    三日晚间，西哈努克和他的随行人员参观了华沙的文化科学宫。
    六月四日，西哈努克游览了华沙市容，并且访问了华沙的泽兰汽车工厂和国家博物馆等。
