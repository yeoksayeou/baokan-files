Title: Austrian Business Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-06-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency:
    Austrian Business Delegation Arrives in Beijing

    According to Xinhua News Agency, the first group of seventeen members of the Austrian business delegation, led by Mr. Haslinger, the head of the Austrian Industrialists Association's China Committee and Vice-Chairman of the Austrian East-West Trade Association, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 28th. Representatives from the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and various specialized import and export corporations were present at the airport to welcome them.

    The Austrian business delegation is jointly organized by the Austrian Industrialists Association and the Austrian East-West Trade Association, with a total of twenty-five members. The second group of eight members will arrive in Beijing in the coming days. The delegation includes leaders from the Austrian Industrialists Association and the Austrian East-West Trade Association, as well as representatives from several Austrian state-owned factories.

    In addition, Lei Renmin, Vice-Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 29th to welcome the Austrian business delegation headed by Mr. Haslinger.

### 奥工商界代表团到京

1956-06-30
第2版()
专栏：

    奥工商界代表团到京
    据新华社讯　奥地利工商界代表团第一批十七人在团长、奥地利工业家协会中国委员会主席、奥地利东西贸易协会副主席哈斯林格率领下，二十八日下午乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中国国际贸易促进委员会和中国进出口各专业公司的负责人。
    奥地利工商界代表团是由奥地利工业家协会和奥地利东西贸易协会联合组织的，全团共二十五人，第二批八人将在日内到北京。代表团中有奥地利工业家协会和奥地利东西贸易协会的负责人和奥地利一些国营工厂的代表。
    又讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民在二十九日晚间设宴欢迎以哈斯林格为首的奥地利工商界代表团。
