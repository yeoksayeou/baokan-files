Title: Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission Recommends Temporary Withdrawal of Inspection Teams
Author:
Date: 1956-06-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 6th: According to the Korean Central News Agency on June 6th: The plenary sessions of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission held on June 4th and 5th unanimously decided that the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission recommends that the Military Armistice Commission agree to temporarily withdraw the Neutral Nations Inspection Teams stationed in the military control zones of both sides, according to the Korean Armistice Agreement. This decision was communicated to the Korean Military Armistice Commission in a letter written by the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission on June 5th. The letter stated that this temporary withdrawal would be pending the approval of the Military Armistice Commission and would be implemented immediately upon approval. The Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission believes that this withdrawal is temporary as long as both sides of the Military Armistice Commission have not reached an agreement on this point, and until an agreement is reached; the statutory status of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission remains unchanged.

### 中立国监察委员会建议暂时撤回视察小组

1956-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中立国监察委员会建议暂时撤回视察小组
    新华社平壤6日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社六日讯：中立国监察委员会六月四日和五日举行的全体会议一致决定：中立国监察委员会建议军事停战委员会同意暂时撤回根据朝鲜停战协定驻在双方军事控制区域内的中立国视察小组。这一决定是中立国监察委员会在六月五日写信通知朝鲜军事停战委员会的。信中说，这一临时性的撤回，将等到军事停战委员会的批准；批准后便立即实行。中立国监察委员会认为：只要军事停战委员会双方没有就这一点达成协议，并且到协议达成时为止，这种撤回是临时性的；中立国监察委员会的法定身份是没有改变的。
