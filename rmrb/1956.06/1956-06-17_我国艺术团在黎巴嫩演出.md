Title: China's Art Troupe Performs in Lebanon
Author:
Date: 1956-06-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, June 16th: The Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation held its premiere performance here on the evening of June 14th.

Lebanese President Camille Chamoun and Prime Minister Abdallah Yafi attended the performance by the Chinese artists. Also in attendance were ministers, high-ranking officials, senior military officers, foreign diplomats, and prominent figures from all walks of life in Lebanon, totaling more than 1,000 people. The delegation's performance was warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 我国艺术团在黎巴嫩演出

1956-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    我国艺术团在黎巴嫩演出
    新华社贝鲁特16日电　中国文化艺术代表团六月十四日晚上在这里举行了首次演出。
    黎巴嫩总统夏蒙、总理雅非出席观看了中国艺术家的表演。观看演出的还有部长、高级官员、高级军官、各国外交人员、黎巴嫩各界著名人士共一千多人。代表团的演出受到观众的热烈欢迎。
