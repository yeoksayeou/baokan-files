Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Receives Five Brazilian Congressmen
Author:
Date: 1956-06-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 14th: Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress received five Brazilian Congressmen on the evening of the 14th, who were invited to visit China: Jerônimo Dix-Huit Rosado Maia, Lycurgo Leite Filho, Estácio Gonçalves Souto Maior and his wife, Rachid Saldanha Derzi, and Getúlio Barbosa de Moura.

Present at the reception were Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, as well as Standing Committee members Wang Weizhou, Cheng Qian, Gao Chongmin, Xu Guangping, and Li Shucheng, and Deputy Secretaries-General Wu Kejian and Yu Xinqing.

Xinhua News Agency, June 14th: At the invitation of Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, a group of six Brazilian Congressmen, including Jerônimo Dix-Huit Rosado Maia, Lycurgo Leite Filho, Estácio Gonçalves Souto Maior and his wife, Rachid Saldanha Derzi, and Getúlio Barbosa de Moura, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 14th for a visit to China.

Welcoming them at the airport were Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Standing Committee members Hu Yuzhi and Xu Guangping, representatives Rong Yiren, Li Weiguang, Hu Wenyao, and Luo Shuzhang, Deputy Secretaries-General Wu Kejian and Qu Wu, Qian Duansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Wang Kunlun, Vice Mayor of Beijing. ### 刘少奇委员长接见五位巴西众议员

1956-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇委员长接见五位巴西众议员
    新华社14日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，在十四日晚间接见了应邀来中国访问的巴西众议院议员热罗尼莫·迪克祖伊特·罗扎多·马伊亚、利库尔戈·勒伊特·菲尔荷、埃斯塔西奥·贡卡尔维斯·索乌托·马约尔和他的夫人、拉希德·萨尔顿哈·德尔集、热图利奥·巴尔博扎·德毛拉。
    接见的时候，在座的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠、李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培、彭真、陈叔通，常务委员王维舟、程潜、高崇民、许广平、李书城，副秘书长吴克坚、余心清。
    新华社14日讯　应全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇的邀请，前来中国访问的巴西众议院议员热罗尼莫·迪克祖伊特·罗扎多·马伊亚、利库尔戈·勒伊特·菲尔荷、埃斯塔西奥·贡卡尔维斯·索乌托·马约尔和他的夫人、拉希德·萨尔顿哈·德尔集、热图利奥·巴尔博扎·德毛拉等一行六人，在十四日乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长陈叔通，常务委员胡愈之、许广平，代表荣毅仁、李维光、胡文耀、罗叔章，副秘书长吴克坚、屈武，中国人民外交学会副会长钱端升，中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民，中华全国总工会副主席朱学范，北京市副市长王昆仑。（附图片）
