Title: Yemen's Crown Prince Invited to Visit the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-06-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th: TASS reported from Moscow on the 8th that the Soviet government has invited His Royal Highness, the Crown Prince of Yemen, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister, Seif al-Islam al-Badr to visit the Soviet Union.

His Majesty Imam Ahmad bin Yahya bin Muhammad Hamid al-Din, the King of Yemen, agreed to this invitation and expressed his gratitude to the Soviet government for the invitation.

The Crown Prince of Yemen will visit the Soviet Union in June.

### 也门太子应邀将去苏联访问

1956-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    也门太子应邀将去苏联访问
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科八日讯：苏联政府邀请了也门太子、副首相兼外交大臣塞弗·埃尔·伊斯兰·埃尔·巴德尔殿下访问苏联。
    也门国王伊马姆·艾哈迈德·伊本·雅亚·伊本·穆罕默德·哈密德·埃丁陛下同意这个邀请，并且对苏联政府这一邀请表示感谢。
    也门太子将在六月间访问苏联。
