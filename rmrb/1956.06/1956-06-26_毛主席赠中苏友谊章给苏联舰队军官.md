Title: Chairman Mao Presents Sino-Soviet Friendship Medals to Soviet Fleet Officers
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, June 25th: This afternoon at 6:30 PM, in the reception hall on the eighth floor of the Peace Hotel on the Bund, Nanjing Road, Shanghai, Admiral Xiao Jingguang of the Navy presented "Sino-Soviet Friendship Medals" to Vice Admiral Chekurov and all the officers of the visiting fleet on behalf of Chairman Mao Zedong.

This is a golden bronze medal. Below the red enamel flags of China and the Soviet Union are the six characters "Long Live Sino-Soviet Friendship." Each medal is accompanied by a pink silk "Certificate of Presentation of Sino-Soviet Friendship Medal" bearing Chairman Mao Zedong's autograph. General Chekurov repeatedly expressed his sincere gratitude for Chairman Mao Zedong's concern and the Chinese people's friendship towards them.

Subsequently, Admiral Xiao Jingguang and Shanghai Vice Mayor Xu Jianguo, on behalf of the Chinese People's Navy and the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, presented special Chinese handicrafts to Chekurov and all the officers and soldiers of the visiting fleet.

A few hours later, at the end of the farewell banquet on the deck of the cruiser, General Chekurov, on behalf of all officers and soldiers of the Soviet Pacific Fleet's motor torpedo boat formation, presented Admiral Xiao Jingguang with a model of a torpedo boat. He also presented the Chinese People's Navy and the people of Shanghai with many valuable gifts on behalf of all the personnel of the Soviet Pacific Fleet, including books, film projectors, radios, watches, accordions, naval military items, and lighthouse models and ship models. Xiao Jingguang and Xu Jianguo asked General Chekurov to convey the gratitude of the Chinese Navy and the Shanghai citizens to all the officers and soldiers of the Soviet Pacific Fleet.

### 毛主席赠中苏友谊章给苏联舰队军官

1956-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席赠中苏友谊章给苏联舰队军官
    新华社上海25日电　今日下午六点半，在上海南京路外滩和平饭店第八楼休息厅内，萧劲光海军大将向切库罗夫海军中将和访华舰队的全体军官转送了毛泽东主席赠送给他们的“中苏友谊章”。
    这是金色铜质纪念章，在红色珐琅的中苏两国国旗的下面，有“中苏友好万岁”六字。每一枚纪念章都附有印着毛泽东主席亲笔签字的粉红丝质的“赠送中苏友谊章证明书”。切库罗夫将军对毛泽东主席对他们的关怀和中国人民对他们的友谊再三表示衷诚的感谢。
    接着，萧劲光海军大将和上海市副市长许建国，代表中国人民海军和上海市人民委员会向切库罗夫和访华舰队的全体官兵，赠送了中国的特种工艺品。
    几小时后，在巡洋舰甲板上的告别宴会结束的时候，切库罗夫将军以苏联太平洋舰队快艇编队全体官兵的名义赠给萧劲光海军大将一艘鱼雷快艇的模型。他又代表苏联太平洋舰队全体人员向中国人民海军和上海市人民，赠送了许多书籍、电影放映机、收音机、手表、手风琴、海军军用物件和灯塔模型、舰艇模型等珍贵礼品。萧劲光和许建国请切库罗夫将军转达中国海军和上海市民对苏联太平洋舰队全体官兵的谢意。
