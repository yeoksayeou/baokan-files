Title: American Federation of Scientists Demands a Ban on Nuclear Weapons Testing
Author:
Date: 1956-06-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 10th. TASS reports: The American Federation of Scientists has demanded "the reaching of an international agreement on banning any further testing of nuclear weapons," as a "preparatory step towards comprehensive and universal mandatory disarmament."

    The president of this Federation, Price, made this proposal in his speech to the Senate Subcommittee on Disarmament. He also requested that "the United States government publicly declare its support for a universal ban" on nuclear weapons and intercontinental ballistic missile testing, and "take the necessary steps to achieve the aforementioned international agreement." He said that this agreement would help ease the arms race and alleviate international tensions.

### 美国科学家联合会  要求禁止试验核子武器

1956-06-11
第4版()
专栏：

    美国科学家联合会
    要求禁止试验核子武器
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社华盛顿讯：美国科学家联合会要求“达成一项关于今后禁止对核子武器作任何试验的国际协定”，作为“全面和普遍的强制性裁军的准备步骤”。
    这个联合会的主席普赖斯在参议院裁军问题小组委员会发言中提出了这个建议。他并且要求“美国政府公开声明支持普遍地禁止”试验核子武器和洲际导弹，以及“采取必要的步骤来实现上述国际协定”。他说，这种协定将有助于缓和军备竞赛和缓和国际紧张局势。
