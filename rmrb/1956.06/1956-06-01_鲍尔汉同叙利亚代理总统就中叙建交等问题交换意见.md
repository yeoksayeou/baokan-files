Title: Burhan and Syrian Acting President Exchange Views on the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and Syria
Author:
Date: 1956-06-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, May 31st: Naim al-Qudsi, Speaker of the Syrian Parliament, who is acting as President during the President's visit to Jordan, received Burhan, the head of the Chinese cultural and artistic delegation visiting Syria, on May 28th. On the same day, Burhan also called on Ali Bouzo, the Minister of Interior and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs. They exchanged views on cultural exchange and the establishment of diplomatic relations between Syria and China.

### 鲍尔汉同叙利亚代理总统就中叙建交等问题交换意见

1956-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    鲍尔汉同叙利亚代理总统就中叙建交等问题交换意见
    新华社大马士革31日电　在总统访问约旦期间代理总统职务的叙利亚议会议长奈绥姆·库德西五月二十八日接见在叙利亚访问的中国文化艺术代表团团长鲍尔汉。同一天，鲍尔汉还拜会了内政部长兼代理外交部长阿里·波佐。他们就叙利亚和中国的文化交流和建立外交关系问题交换了意见。
