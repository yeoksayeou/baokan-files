Title: Nepal's First Ambassador to China Departs Beijing for India
Author:
Date: 1956-06-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd: Nepal's first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Lieutenant General Rana, along with First Secretary Pantri, Military Attaché Colonel Basnyat, and others, left Beijing by plane on the 23rd for India.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Jiakang and Director of the Protocol Department Ke Hua, among others.

Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries also went to see them off.

After Ambassador Rana arrived in Beijing and presented his credentials, Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council, and Vice Premier Ulanhu held banquets and receptions for him. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei invited Ambassador Rana and others to visit the Summer Palace and had dinner together in the Summer Palace. The head of the Beijing Diplomatic Corps, Ambassador Wu La Maung of the Union of Burma to China, and Indian Ambassador to China R.K. Nehru also held banquets for Ambassador Rana.

During his stay in Beijing, Ambassador Rana paid visits to Vice Chairman Zhu De of the People's Republic of China, Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council, Vice Premiers Chen Yun and Ulanhu, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian. Ambassador Rana and others also visited the Soviet Exhibition of Scientific and Technological Achievements in the Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy and the Beijing No. 2 State-owned Cotton Textile Factory, and toured the Great Wall, the Ming Tombs, the Forbidden City, the Temple of Heaven, the Yonghe Temple, and the Beijing Zoo.

### 尼泊尔首任驻华大使离北京去印度

1956-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔首任驻华大使离北京去印度
    新华社23日讯　尼泊尔王国驻中国首任特命全权大使拉纳中将偕一等秘书班特瑞、武官巴斯尼亚特上校等，在二十三日乘飞机离开北京去印度。
    前往机场欢送的有外交部部长助理陈家康和礼宾司司长柯华等。
    各国驻华使节和外交官员也前往欢送。
    拉纳大使抵京递交国书后，中华人民共和国主席毛泽东、国务院总理周恩来和副总理乌兰夫曾分别为他举行宴会和酒会。外交部副部长姬鹏飞邀请了拉纳大使等游颐和园并且在颐和园内共进晚餐。北京外交团团长、缅甸联邦驻中国大使吴拉茂和印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁也曾分别为拉纳大使举行宴会。
    拉纳大使在京期间曾分别拜会了中华人民共和国副主席朱德，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来，副总理陈云、乌兰夫，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，文化部部长沈雁冰和外交部副部长张闻天。拉纳大使等还参观了苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会和国营北京第二棉纺织厂，游览了长城、明十三陵、故宫、天坛、雍和宫以及北京动物园等。
