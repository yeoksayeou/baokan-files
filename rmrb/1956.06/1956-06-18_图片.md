Title: Picture

Author:

Date: 1956-06-18
Page: 1

    The northern section of the Baoji-Chengdu Railway, winding through the Qinling Mountains, is now essentially complete. Looking out from the railway line at the summit of the Qinling Mountains, the Sichuan-Shaanxi Highway resembles a white ribbon. What was once known as the first major obstacle from Baoji to Chengdu is now a route where trains can travel smoothly.

专栏：

    盘绕在秦岭山区中的宝成铁路北段工程已经基本上完成。从秦岭顶峰的铁路线上眺望，川陕公路像条白色飘带。从前这里被人们称为由宝鸡通向成都的第一道难关，现在火车已经能在这里顺利行驶了。
