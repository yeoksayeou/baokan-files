Title: Chinese Red Cross Representatives Hold Formal Talks with Representatives of Three Japanese Organizations; First Group of Released Japanese War Criminals Complete Departure Formalities

Author:

Date: 1956-06-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, June 27th. Representatives of the Chinese Red Cross and representatives of the three Japanese organizations—the Japanese Red Cross, the Japan-China Friendship Association, and the Japan Peace Liaison Committee—held formal talks in Tianjin this evening.
    The representatives of the three Japanese organizations returned to Tianjin today separately from Beijing and Fushun.
    After the talks, the representatives of the Chinese Red Cross hosted a banquet for all the representatives of the three Japanese organizations. Fourteen Japanese journalists also attended the banquet.

    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, June 27th. The three hundred and thirty-five released Japanese war criminals today completed all departure formalities. They will return to Japan by ship from Tianjin tomorrow.
    The more than five hundred parcels, letters, and other items brought by the Japanese Red Cross, the Japan-China Friendship Association, and the Japan Peace Liaison Committee for the Japanese war criminals have also been completely handed over to them today by the Chinese Red Cross.

### 中国红十字会代表和日本三团体代表举行正式会谈  首批被释放的日本战争犯罪分子全部办妥了出境手续

1956-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中国红十字会代表和日本三团体代表举行正式会谈
    首批被释放的日本战争犯罪分子全部办妥了出境手续
    新华社天津27日电　中国红十字会代表和日本红十字会、日中友好协会、日本和平联络会三团体代表，今天晚上在天津举行了正式会谈。
    日本三团体代表是在今天分别从北京和抚顺回到天津的。
    在会谈结束后，中国红十字会代表设宴招待日本三团体的全体代表。十四名日本新闻记者也参加了宴会。
    新华社天津27日电　被释放的三百三十五名日本战争犯罪分子今天全部办妥了出境手续。他们将在明天从天津乘船回日本。
    日本红十字会、日中友好协会、日本和平联络会三团体带给日本战争犯罪分子的五百多件包裹、信件等物品，也已经在今天由中国红十字会全部转交给他们。
