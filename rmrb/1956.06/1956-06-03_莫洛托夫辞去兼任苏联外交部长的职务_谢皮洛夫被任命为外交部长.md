Title: Molotov Relieved of Concurrent Post as Soviet Foreign Minister; Shepilov Appointed Foreign Minister

Author:
Date: 1956-06-03
Page: 1

    Moscow dispatch, June 2nd. "Pravda," "Izvestia," and "Red Star" today all published two news items: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, at the request of Comrade Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, relieved him of his concurrent post as Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union. At the same time, Comrade Shepilov was appointed Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.

### 莫洛托夫辞去兼任苏联外交部长的职务  谢皮洛夫被任命为外交部长

1956-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫辞去兼任苏联外交部长的职务   
    谢皮洛夫被任命为外交部长
    本报莫斯科2日电　“真理报”、“消息报”、“红星报”今天都刊载二则消息：苏联最高苏维埃主席团根据苏联部长会议第一副主席莫洛托夫同志的请求，解除了他兼任苏联外交部长的职务。同时任命谢皮洛夫同志为苏联外交部部长。
