Title: At the Central Institute for Nationalities (Picture)
Author: Zheng Xiaoqian
Date: 1956-06-11
Page: 3

At the Central Institute for Nationalities

The Central Institute for Nationalities currently has 1,600 students, most of whom come from various ethnic groups in the border regions of the motherland. In this big family of nationalities, students study diligently and happily, helping each other and in unity.

Below: During a break from classes, students from different ethnic groups tell stories on the lawn of the campus.

Above: Three students of the Uygur, Kazakh, and Uzbek ethnic groups (from left to right) are studying in the Han Chinese remedial class. After graduation, they can go to Chinese-language universities to study. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zheng Xiaoqian photo

### 在中央民族学院（图片）
郑小箴
1956-06-11
第3版()
专栏：

    在中央民族学院
    中央民族学院现有一千六百多学生，他们大多来自祖国边疆的各个民族。在这个民族大家庭里，学生们团结互助紧张愉快地学习着。
    下图：课余休息的时候，不同民族的学生在校园的草坪上讲故事。
    上图：在汉文补习班学习的维吾尔、哈萨克、乌孜别克族（从左至右）的三位同学正在学习。他们毕业后，便可到汉语的高等学校里去学习。    新华社记者　郑小箴摄
