Title: Syrian Government Spokesman Says Syria Will Recognize China Soon
Author:
Date: 1956-06-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, June 17th (Tass dispatch): A Syrian government spokesman told a reporter from the Syrian newspaper "Al-Khabar" that the signing of trade and cultural agreements between Syria and the People's Republic of China signifies de facto recognition of the People's Republic of China government. The Syrian government will legally recognize the People's Republic of China in the near future.

According to a report from Damascus by a reporter from the Lebanese newspaper "Al-Khabar," the new government of Sabri al-Asali in Syria has decided in principle to recognize the People's Republic of China.

### 叙政府发言人说  叙利亚将在最近承认中国

1956-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

    叙政府发言人说
    叙利亚将在最近承认中国
    新华社18日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特17日消息：叙利亚政府发言人对叙利亚“新闻报”记者说：叙利亚和人民中国之间签订贸易和文化协定意味着在事实上承认人民中国政府。叙利亚政府将在最近时期在法律上承认中华人民共和国。
    据黎巴嫩“新闻报”记者自大马士革报道，叙利亚的萨布里·阿萨利新政府在原则上已经决定承认中华人民共和国。
