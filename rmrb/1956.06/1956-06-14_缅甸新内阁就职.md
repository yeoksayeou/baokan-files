Title: Burmese New Cabinet Takes Office
Author:
Date: 1956-06-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, June 13th: Burmese Federal President Ba U, based on the nomination of the House of Representatives of the Burmese Federal Parliament, officially appointed U Ba Swe as the Prime Minister of the Burmese Federation on the afternoon of June 12th, to succeed U Nu, who recently resigned from the position of Prime Minister.

The new cabinet, headed by U Ba Swe, was sworn into office on the afternoon of the same day.

President Ba U also appointed Zaw Khun Thio, U Kyaw Nyein, and Thakin Tin as Deputy Prime Ministers of the Burmese Federation, and appointed the other twenty ministers of the new cabinet. In the new cabinet, U Ba Swe continues to serve concurrently as Minister of Defense and also as Minister of National Planning. The former Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zaw Khun Thio, also continues to serve concurrently as Minister of Foreign Affairs.

### 缅甸新内阁就职

1956-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸新内阁就职
    新华社仰光13日电　缅甸联邦总统巴宇根据缅甸联邦国会代表院的提名，在六月十二日下午正式任命吴巴瑞为缅甸联邦总理，以接任吴努最近辞去的总理职务。
    以吴巴瑞为首的新内阁已经在当天下午宣誓就职。
    巴宇总统还任命藻昆卓、吴觉迎和德钦丁为缅甸联邦副总理，并且任命了新内阁的其他二十位部长。在新内阁中，吴巴瑞仍旧兼任国防部长，并且兼国家计划部长，前任外交部长藻昆卓也仍旧兼外交部长的职务。
