Title: Grand Reception Held by Soviet Consul General in Shanghai for Visit of Soviet Fleet to Shanghai
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, June 21st: The Soviet Consul General in Shanghai, Osipov, held a grand reception tonight for the visit of the Soviet fleet to Shanghai. Ke Qingshi, Secretary of the CPC Central Committee Shanghai Bureau and First Secretary of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee, raised a glass at the reception to congratulate the naval commanders of the Soviet Pacific Fleet on the success of their visit to Shanghai.

More than 400 guests attended the reception held in the hall of the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Building, including Xiao Jingguang, Vice Minister of National Defense and Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy with the rank of Admiral, responsible persons from the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the East China Sea Fleet, and responsible persons from the Shanghai Municipal Party and Government organs. Vice Admiral Chekurov, Consul General Osipov, and Admiral Xiao Jingguang all raised their glasses at the reception to celebrate the visit of the Soviet fleet to Shanghai and the unbreakable friendship between China and the Soviet Union.

The Soviet Pacific Fleet Song and Dance Ensemble, composed of Soviet officers and soldiers, held its first performance in Shanghai after the reception. In the twenty programs performed, the performers sang the Chinese folk song "The East is Red" in Chinese, and also sang "March of the Chinese People's Liberation Army" and "Song of the Pacific", which described the glorious battle history of the Soviet Pacific Fleet, in Russian. The actors also performed several dance programs. The audience warmly welcomed the performers' performances.

### 为苏联舰队访问上海苏联驻上海总领事举行盛大酒会

1956-06-22
第3版()
专栏：

    为苏联舰队访问上海
    苏联驻上海总领事举行盛大酒会
    新华社上海21日电　苏联驻上海总领事奥西波夫今晚为苏联舰队访问上海举行盛大酒会。中共中央上海局书记、中共上海市委第一书记柯庆施在酒会上向苏联太平洋舰队的海军将领们举杯祝贺他们访问上海成功。
    这个在上海中苏友好大厦大厅里举行的酒会上有四百多位客人，其中包括国防部副部长、中国人民解放军海军司令员萧劲光海军大将，中国人民解放军上海驻军和东海舰队的负责人以及上海市党、政机关的负责人等。切库罗夫海军中将、奥西波夫总领事和萧劲光海军大将都在酒会上为苏联舰队的访问上海和中苏两国牢不可破的友谊而举杯祝贺。
    由苏军官兵组成的苏联太平洋舰队歌舞团在酒会后举行了在上海的首次演出。在演出的二十个节目中，表演者用中国语合唱了中国的民歌“东方红”，还用俄语唱了“中国人民解放军进行曲”和描述苏联太平洋舰队光荣战史的“太平洋之歌”。演员们还表演了好几个舞蹈节目。客人们的表演受到了观众的热烈欢迎。
