Title: Soviet Union Transfers a Batch of Documentary Materials to Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1956-06-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 22nd (TASS): The Soviet Council of Ministers has decided to transfer a batch of Yugoslav documentary materials to the Yugoslav government.

These documentary materials were seized by the Soviet Union from the Hitlerite gang as war booty during the Second World War.

These materials are of great value for studying Yugoslav history from 1900 to 1941.

### 苏移交南一批文献材料

1956-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    苏移交南一批文献材料
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科二十二日讯：苏联部长会议决定把一批南斯拉夫的文献材料移交给南斯拉夫政府。
    这批文献材料是苏联在第二次世界大战期间作为战利品从希特勒匪帮手中缴获的。
    这些材料对研究从1900年到1941年的南斯拉夫历史极有价值。
