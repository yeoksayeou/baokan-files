Title: National People's Congress Continues in Session with Speeches
Author:
Date: 1956-06-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 27th: The Third Session of the First National People's Congress held a plenary meeting this afternoon, starting at 3:00 PM. At today's meeting, a total of twenty people (full texts of the speeches will be published separately) including People's Representatives and heads of various departments of state organs spoke. They were: Minister of Geology Li Siguang, Governor of Hunan Province Cheng Qian, Deputy Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, Huang Shaohong, representative Ji Jinmei from Tibet, Deputy Governor of Qinghai Province Xirao Jiacuo, representative Pei Aqian from the Nujiang Lisu Autonomous Region of Yunnan, representative Huheletai from the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, representative Jin Shilong from the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture of Jilin, Governor of Shaanxi Province Zhao Shoushan, returned overseas Chinese Xu Simin from Burma, cultural and educational worker Wang Zhuchen, musician He Luting, Shandong Lü opera actress Lang Xianfen, engineering technician Hu Zhaosen, industrialists and businessmen Li Guowei, Xiong Yingdong, agricultural labor model Yin Weichen, Vice Chairman of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the CPPCC Wang Baozhen, and publishing worker Shu Xincheng.

Today's meeting ended at 7:50 PM. The executive chairmen presiding over the meeting were: Chen Shutong, Nie Rongzhen, Yang Mingxuan, Wang Weizhou, Zhou Xinfang, Long Yun, Deng Fangzhi, Zhou Shuzhao, and Xirao Jiacuo.

The meeting will continue on the 28th.

### 全国人民代表大会会议继续进行发言

1956-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会会议继续进行发言
    据新华社27日讯　第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议今天下午举行的全体会议，从午后三时开始。在今天会议上继续发言的人民代表和国家机关各部门的负责人共有二十人（发言全文另发）。他们是：地质部部长李四光，湖南省省长程潜，北京市副市长吴晗，黄绍竑，从西藏来的代表计晋美，青海省副省长喜饶嘉错，来自云南怒江傈僳族自治区的代表裴阿欠，来自内蒙古自治区的代表胡和勒泰，从吉林延边朝鲜族自治州来的代表金时龙，陕西省省长赵寿山，从缅甸回国的华侨徐四民，文教工作者王祝晨，音乐家贺绿汀，山东吕剧演员郎咸芬，工程技术人员胡兆森，工商业者李国伟，熊应栋，农业劳动模范殷维臣，政协河北省委员会副主席王葆真，出版工作者舒新城。
    今天的会议在下午七时五十分结束，主持会议的执行主席是：陈叔通、聂荣臻、杨明轩、王维舟、周信芳、龙云、邓芳芝、周叔?、喜饶嘉错。
    二十八日，会议将继续举行。
