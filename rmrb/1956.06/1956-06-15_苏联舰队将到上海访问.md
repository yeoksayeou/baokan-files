Title: Soviet Fleet to Visit Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1956-06-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 14th—At the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, a Soviet fleet led by Vice Admiral V.A. Chekurov will arrive in Shanghai on June 20 for a five-day friendly visit. The fleet consists of the cruiser "Dmitry Pozharsky" and two torpedo destroyers. After the fleet arrives in Shanghai, it will have extensive and friendly contacts with people from all walks of life in Shanghai and the naval forces of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai, and will conduct visits and celebrations.

### 苏联舰队将到上海访问

1956-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联舰队将到上海访问
    新华社14日讯　应中华人民共和国政府邀请，一支由瓦·安·切库罗夫海军中将率领的苏联舰队，将于六月二十日到达上海市作为期五天的友好访问。舰队由巡洋舰“德米特里·波日阿尔斯基”号和两艘雷击舰组成。舰队到达上海市后，将同上海市各界人民和中国人民解放军驻上海的海军部队进行广泛友好的接触，并且将进行访问和联欢。
