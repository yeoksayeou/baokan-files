Title: Xiao Jingguang Hosts Chekurov and Soviet Fleet Officers
Author:
Date: 1956-06-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, June 24th – Xiao Jingguang, Vice Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China and Commander of the Navy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, held a reception tonight at the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Building to host Vice Admiral Chekurov, Commander of the Soviet Pacific Fleet, and the officers of the visiting fleet.

　　At the reception, Admiral Xiao Jingguang said that the Soviet fleet had made a friendly visit to our country for five days, and this visit had enhanced the friendship and unity between the people and armies of China and the Soviet Union. He proposed a toast to the deep friendship between the people and armies of the two countries, and to peace in Asia and the world.

　　At the reception, Vice Admiral Chekurov expressed his gratitude for the warm reception the Soviet visiting fleet received in China.

　　At the reception, the hosts and guests gathered in small groups for cordial conversations. Many Soviet naval officers spoke freely about their impressions of visiting Shanghai.

　　Among those invited to attend today's reception were S.L. Sudarikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, Osipov, Soviet Consul General in Shanghai, Major General Zhadov, Military Attaché of the Soviet Embassy in China, and consuls from various countries in Shanghai.

　　Also present at the reception were leading officials from the Shanghai Municipal Party Committee and government, people from all walks of life, and generals and officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. After the reception, an entertainment evening was held.

### 萧劲光招待切库罗夫和苏联舰队军官

1956-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    萧劲光招待切库罗夫和苏联舰队军官
    新华社上海24日电　中华人民共和国国防部副部长、中国人民解放军海军司令员萧劲光海军大将今晚在上海中苏友好大厦大厅举行酒会，招待苏联太平洋舰队司令切库罗夫海军中将和访华舰队的军官们。
    萧劲光海军大将在酒会上说，苏联舰队在我国作了五天的友好访问，这次访问增进了中苏两国人民及其军队之间的友谊和团结。他提议，为两国人民及其军队之间的深厚友谊，为亚洲和世界和平干杯。
    切库罗夫海军中将在酒会上对苏联访华舰队在中国所受到的热情款待表示感谢。
    在酒会上，宾主围成许多小圈亲切地交谈。许多苏联海军军官畅谈了他们访问上海的感想。
    应邀参加今天酒会的有苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫、苏联驻上海总领事奥西波夫、苏联驻华大使馆武官朱道夫少将和驻上海的各国领事等。
    出席酒会的还有上海市党和政府的负责人，各界人士和中国人民解放军的将军和军官们。酒会结束后举行了文娱晚会。
