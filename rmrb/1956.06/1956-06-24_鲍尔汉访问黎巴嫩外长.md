Title: Burhan Visits Lebanese Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1956-06-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, June 23rd. Burhan, head of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, and other key members paid a courtesy visit to Lebanese Foreign Minister Lahoud yesterday morning.

During the conversation, both sides agreed that the trade agreement between China and Lebanon, already approved by the Lebanese Parliament, and the cultural exchange issues currently under discussion, will strengthen the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries.

Lahoud also expressed his great appreciation for Chinese art and expressed his pleasure and gratitude for the delegation's study of Lebanese songs and dances.

### 鲍尔汉访问黎巴嫩外长

1956-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    鲍尔汉访问黎巴嫩外长
    新华社贝鲁特23日电　中华人民共和国文化艺术代表团团长鲍尔汉和其他主要人员昨天上午向黎巴嫩外交部长拉霍德作了礼节访问。
    在交谈中，双方都认为已经由黎巴嫩议会通过的中国和黎巴嫩之间的贸易协定以及正在磋商的文化交流问题将加强两国间的友好合作关系。
    拉霍德还表示非常赞赏中国的艺术，并且对代表团学习黎巴嫩歌舞一事表示愉快和感激。
