Title: First Group of French Men's and Women's Basketball Teams Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-06-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd. The first group of twenty members of the French Labor Sports Federation men's and women's basketball teams (referred to as the French Labor Sports Federation basketball teams), led by Rousseau, the leader and chairman of the French Labor Sports Federation, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They are the first sports team from Western Europe to visit China.

The French Labor Sports Federation men's and women's basketball teams are visiting China and conducting friendly matches at the invitation of the All-China Sports Federation. The remaining members of the basketball team will be led by the deputy leader, Merlan, and will arrive in Beijing by plane in the coming days.

### 法国男女篮球队首批人员到京

1956-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    法国男女篮球队首批人员到京
    据新华社3日讯　法国劳动体育联合会男女篮球队（简称法国劳联男女篮球队）第一批人员二十人，今天下午在领队、法国劳动体育联合会主席卢梭率领下，坐飞机到达北京。他们是从西欧来中国访问的第一个体育队。
    法国劳联男女篮球队是应中华全国体育总会的邀请来中国访问和进行友谊比赛的。篮球队的其他人员将由副领队默兰率领，日内坐飞机来北京。
