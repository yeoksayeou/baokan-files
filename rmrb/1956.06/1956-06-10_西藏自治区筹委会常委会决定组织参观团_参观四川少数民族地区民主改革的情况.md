Title: The Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region Decides to Organize a Delegation to Visit the Democratic Reform in Minority Areas of Sichuan

Author: 
Date: 1956-06-10
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency News: The Standing Committee of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region held its third meeting on the 8th and decided to organize a "Tibetan Delegation" to visit minority areas of Sichuan Province.
    The Chairman of the Standing Committee, the Dalai Lama, said at the meeting on the 8th that the task of the delegation is to understand the situation of democratic reform in minority areas (mainly Tibetan areas) of Sichuan Province, and to learn from their experiences for reference in carrying out reform in Tibet.
    The Tibetan Delegation will be composed of reputable Living Buddhas and Lamas from the four major monasteries, as well as representatives of the Sakya, Kagyu, Nyingma and other sects.
    During the establishment of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region, representatives from all walks of life in Tibet requested to visit minority areas in Sichuan Province. They believe that these areas were liberated earlier, have carried out democratic reforms, and have many valuable experiences worth learning from.

### 西藏自治区筹委会常委会决定组织参观团  参观四川少数民族地区民主改革的情况

1956-06-10
第3版()
专栏：

    西藏自治区筹委会常委会决定组织参观团    
    参观四川少数民族地区民主改革的情况
    新华社讯　西藏自治区筹备委员会常务委员会八日举行第三次会议，决定组织“西藏参观团”前往四川省少数民族地区参观。
    常务委员会主任委员达赖喇嘛在八日的会议上说，参观团的任务是了解四川省少数民族地区（主要是藏族地区）实行民主改革的情况，学习他们的经验，供西藏地区进行改革的参考。
    西藏参观团将由四大寺庙有声望的活佛、喇嘛及萨迦、噶居、宁玛等教派的代表人物等组成。
    在西藏自治区筹备委员会成立期间，西藏各阶层代表就要求前往四川省少数民族地区参观。他们认为，这些地区解放比较早，进行了民主改革，有许多值得学习的宝贵经验。
