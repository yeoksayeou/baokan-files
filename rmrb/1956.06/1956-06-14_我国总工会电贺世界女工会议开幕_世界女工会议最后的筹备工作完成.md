Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Cables Congratulations on the Opening of the World Women Workers' Conference; Final Preparations Completed for the World Women Workers' Conference
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-14
Page: 1
    
    All-China Federation of Trade Unions Cables Congratulations on the Opening of the World Women Workers' Conference
    Final Preparations Completed for the World Women Workers' Conference
    Xinhua News Agency, June 13th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a cable on June 13th to congratulate the World Women Workers' Conference, scheduled to open in Budapest on June 14th. The cable said: The convening of the World Women Workers' Conference marks the awakening of women workers in various countries and symbolizes the strengthening of the desire of women workers in various countries for international solidarity. It is deeply believed that the conference will encourage and promote women workers in various countries to participate more actively in trade union activities, unite with the working class of various countries, and strive for further relaxation of the international situation, for national independence, and for the rights of trade unions and women workers.
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 13th: The World Women Workers' Conference is about to open in Budapest tomorrow. The preparatory committee for this conference held its final meeting in Prague to discuss various reports to be submitted at the conference, the conference agenda, and some organizational issues.
    A 23-member delegation from China, headed by Yang Zhihua, will participate in the World Women Workers' Conference.

### 我国总工会电贺世界女工会议开幕  世界女工会议最后的筹备工作完成

1956-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我国总工会电贺世界女工会议开幕
    世界女工会议最后的筹备工作完成
    新华社13日讯　中华全国总工会六月十三日打电报祝贺定于六月十四日在布达佩斯开幕的世界女工会议。电文说：世界女工会议的召开标志着各国女工的觉醒，象征着各国女工要求国际团结愿望的加强。深信会议将会鼓舞和推动各国女工更积极地参加工会活动，和各国工人阶级团结一起，为争取国际形势进一步缓和、争取民族独立、争取工会和女工权利而斗争。
    新华社布拉格13日电　世界女工会议明天就要在布达佩斯开幕。这个会议的筹备委员会在布拉格举行了最后一次会议，讨论准备在会议上提出的各种报告、会议日程和一些组织问题。
    中国以杨之华为首的二十三人代表团将参加世界女工会议。
