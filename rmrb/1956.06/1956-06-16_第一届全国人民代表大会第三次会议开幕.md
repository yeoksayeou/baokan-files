Title: Opening of the Third Session of the First National People's Congress
Author:
Date: 1956-06-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 15th: The Third Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China opened this afternoon at Huairen Hall in Zhongnanhai, Beijing.

Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairpersons Soong Ching Ling, Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Luo Ronghuan, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, Saifudin, Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, Deng Zihui, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, and Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng attended the opening ceremony.

When Chairman Mao Zedong and other national leaders stepped into the hall and onto the rostrum, the entire audience burst into warm applause.

The opening ceremony was presided over by Chairman Liu Shaoqi. After he announced the opening of the meeting, the orchestra played the national anthem.

The main agenda of this meeting, unanimously approved by all the representatives, included the following items:

	Review and approve the 1955 national final accounts and the 1956 national budget;
	Discuss and approve the Model Regulations for Advanced Agricultural Producers' Cooperatives;
	Discuss and approve the work report of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress;
	Elect additional members to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

Before the agenda was approved, all the representatives unanimously elected the Presidium and Secretary-General for this meeting, totaling eighty-nine people.

After the opening ceremony, the meeting adjourned for a short break. The elected Presidium held a brief meeting during this time. Subsequently, the meeting proceeded according to the approved agenda.

More than half an hour before the opening ceremony, the representatives arrived at the venue. Before the meeting started, the lounges and the hall were filled with cheerful conversations. The representatives discussed their observations during inspection tours in various regions, and talked about the victories of the socialist transformation and the thriving socialist construction cause of the motherland.

As of today, one thousand and forty-one representatives have registered for the meeting, and one thousand and twenty-five representatives attended today's meeting. Heads of various government departments attended today's meeting as observers.

Five visiting members of the Brazilian Chamber of Deputies, diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China, and some foreign guests in Beijing were invited to attend today's meeting as guests.

Xinhua News Agency, June 15th: The Third Session of the First National People's Congress listened to reports on various agenda items of this meeting this afternoon. This began at 4:00 PM, after the opening ceremony of the Congress.

The first item on the agenda of the meeting was to approve the report on the examination of the qualifications of the newly elected representatives. This report was made by Ma Mingfang, Chairman of the Credentials Committee of the First National People's Congress. The meeting unanimously approved the report made by Ma Mingfang.

Then, Vice Premier and Minister of Finance Li Xiannian, on behalf of the State Council, delivered a "Report on the 1955 National Final Accounts and the 1956 National Budget" to the meeting. His report included three parts: 1. The 1955 National Final Accounts; 2. The 1956 National Budget; 3. Striving to Achieve the 1956 National Budget. The figures and facts listed in his report not only illustrated the state of national finances in 1955 and the plan for financial revenue and expenditure in 1956, but also illustrated the state of the national economy in 1955 and the key points of the national economic development plan for 1956.

Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan gave a "Explanation on the Model Regulations (Draft) for Advanced Agricultural Producers' Cooperatives" to the meeting (full text to be published in tomorrow's newspaper). In his explanation, he pointed out that the task of agricultural collectivization has now been basically completed. The number of farm households that have joined advanced agricultural producers' cooperatives has reached 61% of the total number of farm households in the country, and advanced agricultural producers' cooperatives with a completely socialist nature have become the main organizational form of agricultural production. He pointed out: "The Model Regulations (Draft) for Advanced Agricultural Producers' Cooperatives" was drafted on the basis of the Model Regulations for Agricultural Producers' Cooperatives, which is already in general use throughout the country for primary cooperatives, addressing the new problems that have arisen after primary cooperatives have transitioned to advanced cooperatives, and based on the new experiences in the construction of agricultural producers' cooperatives.

Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman and Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, delivered a work report of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on behalf of the Standing Committee (full text to be published in tomorrow's newspaper). The report listed the various tasks carried out by the Standing Committee in the ten months from the adjournment of the Second Session of the First National People's Congress to the present, and also explained the overview of the representatives' inspection work in various regions.

The meeting also approved the list of the Chairman and members of the Proposals Examination Committee for this meeting.

The executive chairmen of today's meeting were Liu Shaoqi, Soong Ching Ling, Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Luo Ronghuan, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, and Saifudin.

The meeting adjourned at 7:00 PM. The meeting on the 16th will begin with group discussions. (Picture attached)

### 第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议开幕

1956-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

    第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议开幕
    新华社15日讯　中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议今天下午在北京中南海怀仁堂开幕。
    毛泽东主席，朱德副主席，刘少奇委员长，周恩来总理，副委员长宋庆龄、林伯渠、李济深、罗荣桓、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、李维汉、陈叔通、赛福鼎，副总理陈云、彭德怀、邓小平、邓子恢、陈毅、乌兰夫、李富春、李先念，最高人民法院院长董必武和最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，出席了会议的开幕式。
    毛泽东主席等国家领导工作人员步入会场登上主席台的时候，全场响起了热烈的掌声。
    会议的开幕式是刘少奇委员长主持的。在他宣布会议开幕以后，乐队奏起了国歌。
    经全体代表一致通过的这次会议的主要议程有下列各项：
    审查和批准1955年国家决算和1956年国家预算；
    讨论和通过高级农业生产合作社示范章程；
    讨论和通过全国人民代表大会常务委员会的工作报告；
    补选全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员。
    在通过议程以前，全体代表还一致选出了主持这次会议的主席团和秘书长，共八十九人。
    开幕式结束以后，会议宣告短时间的休息。选出的主席团在这个时间开了简短的会。随后，会议就按照通过的议程进行。
    在开幕式前半个多小时，代表们就来到了会场。在开会以前，休息室和会场上充满了愉快的谈笑声。代表们谈论着在各地视察工作的见闻，谈论着祖国社会主义改造的胜利和蒸蒸日上的社会主义建设事业。
    到今天为止，向会议报到的代表一千零四十一人，出席今天会议的代表一千零二十五人。政府各部门的负责人列席了今天的会议。
    应邀前来访问的五位巴西众议院议员、各国驻中国的使节和外交官员以及在京的一部分外宾，作了今天会议的来宾。
    新华社15日讯　第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议今天下午听取了本次会议各项议程的报告。这是在大会举行开幕式后，从四时开始进行的。
    会议的第一项议程是通过关于补选的代表资格的审查报告，这项报告是由第一届全国人民代表大会代表资格审查委员会主任委员马明方作的。会议一致通过了马明方所作的报告。
    接着国务院副总理兼财政部长李先念代表国务院向会议作了“关于1955年国家决算和1956年国家预算的报告”。他的报告包括三个部分：一、1955年国家决算；二、1956年的国家预算；三、为实现1956年的国家预算而努力。他的报告所列举的数字和事实，不但说明了1955年的国家财政状况和1956年的财政收支计划，而且说明了1955年的国民经济状况和1956年国民经济发展计划的要点。
    农业部长廖鲁言向会议作了“关于高级农业生产合作社示范章程（草案）的说明”（全文见明日本报）。他在说明中指出，现在农业生产合作化的任务已经基本完成了，单是加入高级农业生产合作社的农户，就已经达到了全国农户总数的61％，完全社会主义性质的高级农业生产合作社已经成为农业生产的主要组织形式。他指出：“高级农业生产合作社示范章程（草案）”是在已经在全国通用的供初级合作社采用的农业生产合作社示范章程的基础上，针对着初级合作社转为高级合作社以后所产生的新问题，根据农业生产合作社建设的新经验起草的。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长兼秘书长彭真代表常务委员会作了全国人民代表大会常务委员会的工作报告（全文见明日本报）。报告列举了常务委员会从第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议闭幕以后到现在的十个月来所进行的各项工作，还说明了代表们在各地视察工作的概况。
    会议还通过了本次会议的提案审查委员会的主任委员和委员的名单。
    今天会议的执行主席是刘少奇、宋庆龄、林伯渠、李济深、罗荣桓、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、李维汉、陈叔通、赛福鼎。
    会议进行到七时休会。十六日会议将开始分组讨论。（附图片）
