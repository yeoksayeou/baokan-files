Title: Leaders of Chinese Cultural Arts Troupe Visit Lebanese Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1956-06-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, June 13th: The leaders of the Chinese Cultural Arts Delegation visited Lebanese Prime Minister Abdullah Yafi.

Prime Minister Yafi welcomed the delegation's visit. He discussed cultural exchanges between China and Lebanon with the delegation leaders. He said that the delegation's visit is of great significance in strengthening the relationship between the Arab people and the Chinese people.

The two sides had a friendly conversation lasting thirty minutes.

On the same day, the delegation also held a press conference.

### 我国文化艺术团领导人访问黎巴嫩总理

1956-06-15
第4版()
专栏：

    我国文化艺术团领导人访问黎巴嫩总理
    新华社贝鲁特13日电　中国文化艺术代表团的领导人访问了黎巴嫩总理阿卜杜拉·雅非。
    雅非总理对代表团的访问表示欢迎。他同代表团的领导人谈论了中国和黎巴嫩之间的文化交流等问题。他说，代表团的访问对于加强阿拉伯人民和中国人民之间的关系具有重大的意义。
    双方在友好的气氛中进行了三十分钟的交谈。
    同一天，代表团还举行了记者招待会。
