Title: Chairman Mao Zedong and others Receive Brazilian Congressmen
Author: Qi Guanshan
Date: 1956-06-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 21st: Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai received Brazilian Congressmen Jerônimo Dix-huit Rosado Maia, Lycurgo Leite Filho, Estácio Gonçalves Souto Maior and his wife, Rachid Saldanha Derzi, and Getúlio Barbosa de Moura this evening. Also present at the reception were Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, as well as Wu Kejian and Yu Xinqing, Deputy Secretaries-General, and Zhang Wentian, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs. Chairman Mao, Chairman Liu, and Premier Zhou took a group photo with the Brazilian Congressmen. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Qi Guanshan took the photo)

### 毛泽东主席等接见巴西众议院议员
齐观山
1956-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席等接见巴西众议院议员
    新华社21日讯　毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理今天晚上接见了巴西众议院议员热罗尼莫·迪克祖伊特·罗扎多·马伊亚、利库尔戈·勒伊特·菲尔荷、埃斯塔西奥·贡卡尔维斯·索乌托·马约尔和他的夫人、拉希德·萨尔顿哈·德尔集、热图利奥·巴尔博扎·德毛拉。接见的时候，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培、彭真、陈叔通，副秘书长吴克坚、余心清，外交部副部长张闻天也在座。毛主席、刘委员长和周总理同巴西众议院议员合影。　新华社记者　齐观山摄
