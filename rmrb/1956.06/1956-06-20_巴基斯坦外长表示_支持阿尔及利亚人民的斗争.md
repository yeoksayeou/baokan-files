Title: Pakistani Foreign Minister Expresses Support for the Struggle of the Algerian People
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-20
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, June 19th: The seven-member delegation of the Pakistani All-Parties North African Muslim Countries Committee met with Pakistani Foreign Minister Chaudhry yesterday, requesting active and bold support for the independence struggle of the Algerian patriots.

    Chaudhry assured the representatives that Pakistan would take all steps within its power to enable Algeria to achieve freedom.

    Chaudhry also said that Pakistan would do its best to urge the US government and the United Nations to persuade France to cease all military actions against Algerian nationalists and to immediately begin negotiations with their representatives.

    The delegation submitted the following requests to Chaudhry for his consideration: Pakistan should support the cause of Algeria at all international conferences; Pakistan should strongly protest the use of NATO troops and weapons, especially American weapons; and demand a comprehensive economic boycott of France. Chaudhry stated that Pakistan would bring this abuse of its weapons to the attention of the United States.

### 巴基斯坦外长表示  支持阿尔及利亚人民的斗争

1956-06-20
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦外长表示 
    支持阿尔及利亚人民的斗争
    新华社喀喇蚩19日电　巴基斯坦各党派北非回教诸国委员会的七人代表团昨天会见了巴基斯坦外交部长乔德里，要求给予阿尔及利亚爱国分子的独立斗争以积极和大胆的支持。
    乔德里向代表们保证，巴基斯坦将在它的能力范围以内采取一切步骤来使阿尔及利亚得到自由。
    乔德里又说，巴基斯坦将竭力要求美国政府和联合国劝使法国停止一切反对阿尔及利亚民族主义者的军事行动，立即开始同他们的代表进行谈判。
    代表团向乔德里提出了下列要求供他考虑：巴基斯坦应该在一切国际会议上拥护阿尔及利亚的事业；巴基斯坦应该强有力地抗议使用北大西洋公约组织的军队和武器，特别是美国的武器；并且要求对法国进行全面的经济抵制。乔德里表示，巴基斯坦将提请美国注意这种滥用它的武器的情况。
