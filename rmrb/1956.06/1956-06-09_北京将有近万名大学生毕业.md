Title: Nearly Ten Thousand University Students in Beijing to Graduate
Author:
Date: 1956-06-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency News: Beijing
    Beijing's universities and colleges' graduating students have successively begun their graduation examinations in recent days.
    In the 1955-1956 academic year, Beijing's university graduates, including those graduating early, number about ten thousand, equivalent to the total number of graduates from Beijing's universities from 1950 to 1953.
    This year, there are more than 1,600 graduate students graduating, more than in any previous year.

### 北京将有近万名大学生毕业

1956-06-09
第3版()
专栏：

    北京将有近万名大学生毕业
    新华社讯　北京各高等学校应届毕业生连日来先后开始毕业考试。
    在1955—1956学年度，北京的大学毕业生包括提前毕业的在内约有一万人，相当于从1950年到1953年北京各大学毕业生人数的总和。
    今年毕业的研究生有一千六百多人，比以往任何一年都多。
