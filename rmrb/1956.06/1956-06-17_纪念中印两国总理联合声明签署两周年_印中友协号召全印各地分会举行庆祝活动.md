Title: Commemorating the Second Anniversary of the Signing of the Joint Statement by the Prime Ministers of China and India: Sino-Indian Friendship Association Calls on Branches Throughout India to Hold Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1956-06-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 16th: The National Committee of the India-China Friendship Association has called on the branches of the India-China Friendship Association throughout the country to organize commemorative activities for the second anniversary of the publication of the joint statement by the Prime Ministers of India and China.

The notice from the India-China Friendship Association stated that the historic joint statement issued on June 28, 1954, not only brought India and China closer but also elevated the long-standing relationship between India and China to a new level. The Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence put forward in the joint statement can serve as the foundation for friendship and cultural relations between all countries, especially Asian countries, as well as for international peace.

In its notice, the National Committee of the India-China Friendship Association called for commemoration in appropriate ways and for striving to involve the widest possible range of people. It also called for the promotion of the Five Principles and their importance.

The Bombay branch of the India-China Friendship Association has already arranged to hold a public gathering and cultural program on June 25th to celebrate this anniversary. It is reported that the Chief Minister of Bombay State, Desai, will deliver a speech at the meeting.

### 纪念中印两国总理联合声明签署两周年  印中友协号召全印各地分会举行庆祝活动

1956-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念中印两国总理联合声明签署两周年
    印中友协号召全印各地分会举行庆祝活动
    新华社新德里16日电　印中友好协会全国委员会号召全国各地的印中友好协会分会组织中印两国总理发表联合声明两周年的纪念活动。
    印中友好协会的通知说，1954年6月28日发表的有历史意义的联合声明不仅使印度和中国更加接近，而且使印度和中国的悠久关系提高到了新的水平。联合声明中提出的和平共处五项原则可以作为各国之间，尤其是亚洲各国之间友谊和文化关系以及国际间和平的基础。
    印中友好协会全国委员会在它的通知里号召采取适当的方式来进行纪念，并且尽可能争取最广泛的人来参加。它还号召宣传五项原则和它的重要性。
    印中友好协会孟买分会已经安排了在六月二十五日举行公众集会和文艺节目来庆祝这个纪念日。有消息说，孟买省首席部长德赛将在会上发表演说。
