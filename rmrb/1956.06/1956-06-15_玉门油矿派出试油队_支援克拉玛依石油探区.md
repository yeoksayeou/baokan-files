Title: Yumen Oilfield Sends Oil Testing Team to Support Karamay Petroleum Exploration Area
Author:
Date: 1956-06-15
Page: 2

    Yumen Oilfield Sends Oil Testing Team
    to Support Karamay Petroleum Exploration Area
    Xinhua News Agency, Yumen, 14th: The Yumen Oilfield recently dispatched an oil testing team to work in the Karamay petroleum exploration area of the Junggar Basin in the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region.
    Karamay has been confirmed as a very promising large oilfield, and the Xinjiang Petroleum Company is currently concentrating its efforts on drilling in this area. The drilled oil exploration wells need to be tested to determine what kind of oil production equipment to use. Oil testing is a complex technology, and the Karamay exploration area is in great need of such talent. Therefore, the Yumen Oilfield has sent this oil testing team to provide support.

### 玉门油矿派出试油队  支援克拉玛依石油探区

1956-06-15
第2版()
专栏：

    玉门油矿派出试油队
    支援克拉玛依石油探区
    据新华社玉门14日电　玉门油矿最近派出了一支试油队，前往新疆维吾尔自治区准噶尔盆地的克拉玛依石油探区工作。
    克拉玛依已经被证实是一个很有希望的大油田，目前新疆石油公司集中力量在这个地区钻探。钻成的石油探井，需要进行试油，才能决定采用何种采油设备进行采油。试油工作是一门复杂的技术，克拉玛依探区很需要这种人材，因此玉门油矿抽出这支试油队前去支援。
