Title: Yumin Township Agricultural Cooperative Engages in Artificial Fish Farming
Author:
Date: 1956-06-12
Page: 2

    Yumin Township Agricultural Cooperative Engages in Artificial Fish Farming
    (Our Wuhan dispatch) The Yumin Township Agricultural Cooperative in Honghu County, Hubei Province, is developing 4,000 mu of water surface for artificial fish farming in order to develop diversified operations. This year, it is expected to produce 1.2 million jin of fish, which, converted to rice, can provide the annual rations for all 2,470 people in the township. They are now building embankments for the fish ponds and sending young fishermen to Jingzhou Prefecture to learn fish farming techniques.

### 渔民乡农业合作社进行人工养鱼

1956-06-12
第2版()
专栏：

    渔民乡农业合作社进行人工养鱼
    本报武汉讯　湖北省洪湖县渔民乡农业合作社为了开展多种经营，正在开辟四千亩水面实行人工养鱼。今年预计产鱼一百二十万斤，折米可供全乡2，470人的一年口粮。现在他们正在建筑养鱼湖的围埂，同时派出青年渔民到荆州专署学习养鱼技术。
