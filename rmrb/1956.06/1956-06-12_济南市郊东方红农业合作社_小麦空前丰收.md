Title:空前收济南市郊东方红农业合作社小麦空前丰收
Author:
Date: 1956-06-12
Page: 2

    Jinan Suburban Dongfanghong Agricultural Cooperative
    Unprecedented Wheat Harvest
    Cooperation has brought an unprecedented bumper harvest to the Dongfanghong Agricultural Cooperative in the suburbs of Jinan. The average yield of the cooperative's 3,400 mu of wheat increased by about 80 jin per mu compared to last year, with 1,010 mu yielding between 200 and 600 jin of wheat per mu. (Attached pictures)
    Top image: To strive for an even greater harvest next year, cooperative members are actively selecting seeds in the fields, planning to select 6,000 jin of improved wheat varieties.
    Bottom image: The threshing ground of the Dongfanghong Agricultural Cooperative.
              Photo by Yuan Yiping (Manuscript from Dazhong Daily)

### 济南市郊东方红农业合作社  小麦空前丰收

1956-06-12
第2版()
专栏：

    济南市郊东方红农业合作社
    小麦空前丰收
    合作化给济南市郊区东方红农业合作社带来空前没有过的大丰收。全社三千四百亩小麦，平均每亩较去年增产约八十斤，每亩产麦二百斤到六百斤的达一千零十亩。（附图片）
    左图：为了争取明年更大的丰收，社员们积极进行田间选种，计划选小麦良种六千斤。
    下图：东方红农业合作社的打麦场。
              原一萍摄（大众日报社稿）
