Title: Central Delegation General Group Arrives in Xining by Plane in Batches
Author:
Date: 1956-06-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Xining, 4th. The General Group of the Central Delegation, led by Chen Yi, Head of the Delegation and Vice Premier of the State Council, and Deputy Heads Zhang Jingwu, Wang Feng, and Huang Qixiang, has arrived in Xining by plane in batches from the Lhasa suburban airport. They are the first group of passengers to arrive in Xining by plane from Lhasa after the successful trial flight of the Beijing-Lhasa air route.
    Arriving in Xining with the delegation were Zhang Guohua and Ngabo Ngawang Jigme, representatives to the Third Session of the First National People's Congress.
    Those who went to the airport to greet them were: Gao Feng, Secretary of the CPC Qinghai Provincial Committee, Zhou Renshan and Zhu Xiafu, Deputy Secretaries, Ma Fuchen (Hui nationality), Vice Governor of Qinghai Province, Major General Gao Langting, Head of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Xining, Ma Xingtai (Hui nationality), Vice Chairman of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Yang Xiyao, Chairman of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the China Democratic League, and Songbu (Tu nationality), Deputy Director of the Qinghai Provincial Nationalities Affairs Commission, etc.

### 中央代表团总团人员分批乘飞机到达西宁

1956-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中央代表团总团人员分批乘飞机到达西宁
    新华社西宁4日电　中央代表团总团由团长、国务院副总理陈毅，副团长张经武、汪锋、黄琪翔率领，已分批从拉萨市郊机场乘飞机到达西宁。他们是北京—拉萨航线试航成功后，由拉萨乘飞机抵达西宁的第一批旅客。
    和代表团一起到达西宁的还有，出席第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议的全国人民代表大会代表张国华、尧西·泽仁卓玛。
    前往机场迎接的有：中共青海省委员会书记高峰、副书记周仁山、朱侠夫，青海省副省长马辅臣（回族）中国人民解放军驻西宁部队首长高朗亭少将，中国人民政治协商会议青海省委员会副主席马兴泰（回族），中国民主同盟青海省委员会主任委员杨希尧，青海省民族事务委员会副主任松布（土族）等。
