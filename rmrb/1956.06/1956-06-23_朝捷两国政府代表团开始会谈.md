Title: Korean and Czechoslovak Government Delegations Begin Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-06-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 22nd. The government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Premier Kim Il-sung, began talks with the Czechoslovak government delegation headed by Premier Široký immediately after arriving in Prague from Budapest on the 21st.

During the talks, the two sides discussed issues related to the relations between Korea and Czechoslovakia, as well as the possibility of further expanding economic and cultural cooperation between the two countries.

On the same day, the Korean government delegation was also received by Czechoslovak President Zápotocký. The President had a long and friendly conversation with the delegation.

In the afternoon of the same day, the Korean government delegation toured the city of Prague and laid wreaths at the Gottwald Mausoleum and the Tomb of the Unknown Soldiers.

On the same evening, Široký held a reception for the distinguished Korean guests.

### 朝捷两国政府代表团开始会谈

1956-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

    朝捷两国政府代表团开始会谈
    据新华社布拉格22日电　以金日成首相为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团，二十一日自布达佩斯抵达布拉格以后，立即同以西罗基总理为首的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团开始举行会谈。
    在会谈中，双方讨论了有关朝捷两国之间的关系问题，以及进一步扩大两国之间的经济和文化合作的可能性问题。
    这一天，朝鲜政府代表团还受到了捷克斯洛伐克总统萨波托斯基的接见。总统同代表团人员进行了长时间的友好的谈话。
    当天下午，朝鲜政府代表团游览了布拉格市容，并且向哥特瓦尔德陵墓和无名英雄墓献了花圈。
    同一天晚上，西罗基为朝鲜贵宾举行了招待会。
