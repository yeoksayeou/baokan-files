Title: Converting Dry Land into Paddy Fields (Photo)
Author: Xu Bihua
Date: 1956-06-07
Page: 2

Converting Dry Land into Paddy Fields
    In the Lixiahe area of northern Jiangsu Province, more than 600,000 mu of dry land have been converted into paddy fields, and it is estimated that rice production will increase by more than 120 million jin this year. This is a windmill in the Haitong Second Agricultural Production Cooperative of Sheyang County pumping river water into the fields.
                                     Xinhua News Agency reporter Xu Bihua photographed

### 把旱地改成水田（图片）
许碧华
1956-06-07
第2版()
专栏：

    把旱地改成水田
    江苏省北部里下河地区把六十多万亩旱地改成了水田，预计今年将可增产稻谷一亿二千多万斤。这是射阳县海通第二农业生产合作社的风车把河水灌入田地。
                                     新华社记者　许碧华摄
