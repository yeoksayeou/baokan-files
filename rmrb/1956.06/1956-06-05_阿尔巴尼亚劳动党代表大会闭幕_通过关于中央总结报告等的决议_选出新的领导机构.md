Title: Albanian Labor Party Congress Concludes: Resolutions on the Central Committee's Summary Report Passed; New Leadership Elected
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana dispatch from TASS: The Third Congress of the Albanian Labor Party concluded on June 3rd.

    At the morning session on June 2nd, the chairman of the meeting announced that the committee elected by the congress to draft the resolution on the Central Committee's summary report had completed its work. The draft resolution was submitted to the congress for a vote. The delegates unanimously passed the resolution. Subsequently, the congress also passed the resolution on the draft directives for the Second Five-Year Plan proposed by the Central Committee.

    The meeting heard a report on the revision of the Constitution of the Albanian Labor Party. The report pointed out that the Albanian Labor Party is a voluntary combat alliance of communists with unified goals, composed of members of the working class, working peasants, and working intellectuals. The report also stated that it must be emphasized that every party member has the responsibility to maintain the unity and purity of the party ranks, strictly abide by party and state discipline, and carry out criticism and self-criticism. After discussion, the meeting unanimously passed a resolution approving the full text of the revised Party Constitution.

    At the closing session on June 3rd, the results of the election of the leading bodies of the Albanian Labor Party were announced. The new Central Committee consists of 43 members and 22 alternate members. In addition, a new Central Auditing Commission was elected.

    Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, delivered the closing address at the congress. He said that the Third Congress unanimously agreed with the general line of our party during the period covered by the summary report. The congress demonstrated the solidarity and unity of our party around the Central Committee. At this congress, we not only talked about achievements, but also sharply criticized our mistakes and shortcomings, and expressed our firm confidence in eliminating these mistakes and shortcomings. Unity of opinion and action is the characteristic of this congress. This is the greatest guarantee of the unshakable strength of our party. This congress has adopted the directives for the Second Five-Year Plan. The Albanian people, under the leadership of their party, will successfully accomplish these great tasks.

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表大会闭幕  通过关于中央总结报告等的决议　选出新的领导机构

1956-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表大会闭幕
    通过关于中央总结报告等的决议　选出新的领导机构
    新华社据塔斯社地拉那讯　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第三次代表大会在六月三日闭幕。
    在六月二日上午会议上，会议主席宣布说，由代表大会选举出来草拟关于中央委员会总结报告的决议草案的委员会已经完成了工作。决议草案提交大会表决。代表们一致通过了这一决议。接着，代表大会还通过了中央委员会提出的关于第二个五年计划的指示草案的决议。
    会议听取了关于修改阿尔巴尼亚劳动党党章的报告。报告指出，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党是目标一致的共产主义者的自愿战斗联盟，它由工人阶级的成员、劳动农民和劳动知识分子组成。报告中还说，必须强调指出每一个党员有责任来保持党队伍的团结和纯洁，严格遵守党和国家的纪律，展开批评和自我批评。会议经过讨论以后一致通过决议，批准了修改后的党章全文。
    在六月三日的闭幕会上宣布了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党领导机构的选举结果。新的中央委员会由委员四十三人和候补委员二十二人组成。另外，还选出了新的中央检查委员会。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩·霍查在大会上致闭幕词。他说，第三次代表大会一致同意我们党在总结报告所谈到的时期中的总路线。大会表明了我们党在中央委员会的周围的团结一致。在这次代表大会上，我们不仅讲到了成就，而且也尖锐地批评了我们的错误和缺点，并且表示了消除这些错误和缺点的坚强信心。意见和行动的一致是这次代表大会的特点。这是我们党的不可动摇的力量的最大保证。这次大会通过了关于第二个五年计划的指示。阿尔巴尼亚人民，在自己党的领导下，将成功地实现这些巨大的任务。
