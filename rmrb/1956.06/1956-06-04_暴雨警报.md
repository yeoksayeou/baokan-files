Date: 1956-06-04

    Rainstorm Alert
    At 7 p.m. on the 3rd, the Central Meteorological Observatory issued a rainstorm alert:
    It is expected that within the next 24 hours, the rainfall in eastern Hebei Province, including Tongxian Special Zone, Baoding Special Zone, Tangshan Special Zone, and Chengde Special Zone, and southern Liaoning Province will be between 30 and 50 millimeters. There will be heavy to torrential rain in eastern and northern Shandong Province, the middle and lower reaches of the Huai River, eastern Hubei Province, northern Hunan Province, northern Jiangxi Province, southern Anhui Province, and the western mountainous areas of Zhejiang Province. The wind force on the Bohai Sea and the northern Yellow Sea will be level 6 or 7, and the wind direction will be easterly. On the night of the 3rd, there will be short-term level 6 northwesterly winds on the river and lake surfaces in the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 暴雨警报

1956-06-04
第2版()
专栏：

    暴雨警报
    三日下午七时中央气象台发布暴雨警报：
    预计未来二十四小时内，河北省的东部包括通县专区、保定专区、唐山专区、承德专区等地区和辽宁省的南部，降雨量将在三十到五十公厘之间。山东省的东部和北部、淮河中下游、湖北省的东部、湖南省的北部、江西省的北部、安徽省的南部、浙江省的西部山地等地区，将有大雨到暴雨。渤海和黄海北部海面的风力有六、七级，风向偏东。三日夜里长江中下游的河面湖面上有短时间的六级西北风。（新华社）
