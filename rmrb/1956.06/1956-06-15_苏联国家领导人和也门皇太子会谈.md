Title: Talks between Soviet State Leaders and Yemeni Crown Prince
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, TASS dispatch: On June 13, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Voroshilov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers Bulganin, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Pervukhin, and Foreign Minister Shepilov held friendly and cordial talks with the Crown Prince of Yemen, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Seif al-Islam Muhammad al-Badr in the Kremlin.

On the same day, Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov received Yemeni Crown Prince Badr and held friendly talks with him.

### 苏联国家领导人和也门皇太子会谈

1956-06-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联国家领导人和也门皇太子会谈
    新华社据塔斯社莫斯科讯　六月十三日，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫、部长会议主席布尔加宁、部长会议第一副主席别尔乌辛和外交部长谢皮洛夫同也门皇太子、副首相兼外交大臣塞弗·伊斯兰·穆罕默德·巴德尔在克里姆林宫举行了友好亲切的会谈。
    同一天，苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫接见了也门皇太子巴德尔，并且同他进行了友好的交谈。
