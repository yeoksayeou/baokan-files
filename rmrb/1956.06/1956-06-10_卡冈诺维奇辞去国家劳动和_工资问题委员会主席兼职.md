Title: Kaganovich Resigns from Concurrent Post as Chairman of the State Committee on Labor and Wages

Author:
Date: 1956-06-10
Page: 4

    Moscow dispatch from our newspaper on the 9th: Today, the central newspapers here published two news items: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, at the request of L.M. Kaganovich, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, relieved him of his concurrent post as Chairman of the State Committee on Labor and Wages under the Council of Ministers of the USSR. The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR appointed A.P. Volkov as Chairman of the State Committee on Labor and Wages.

### 卡冈诺维奇辞去国家劳动和  工资问题委员会主席兼职

1956-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    卡冈诺维奇辞去国家劳动和
    工资问题委员会主席兼职
    本报莫斯科9日电　今天这里中央各报都发表了两则消息：苏联最高苏维埃主席团根据苏联部长会议第一副主席拉·莫·卡冈诺维奇的请求，解除了他兼任的直属苏联部长会议的国家劳动和工资问题委员会主席的职务。苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命了亚·彼·沃尔科夫为国家劳动和工资问题委员会主席。
