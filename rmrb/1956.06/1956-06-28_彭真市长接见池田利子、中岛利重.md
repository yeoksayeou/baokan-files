Title: Mayor Peng Zhen Receives Ikeda Toshiko and Nakajima Toshishige
Author:
Date: 1956-06-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 27th — Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen received Ikeda Toshiko, representative of the Japan-China Friendship Association, and Nakajima Toshishige, representative of the Japan Peace Liaison Association, at 8:00 p.m. tonight.
	Feng Jiping, Vice Mayor of Beijing, and Lin Shixiao, Deputy Secretary-General of the Red Cross Society of China, were present at the reception.

### 彭真市长接见池田利子、中岛利重

1956-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真市长接见池田利子、中岛利重
    新华社27日讯　北京市长彭真今晚八时接见了日中友好协会的代表池田利子、日本和平联络会的代表中岛利重。
    陪同接见的有北京市副市长冯基平，中国红十字会付秘书长林士笑。
