Title: Romania Shortens Workday for Workers in Special Occupations
Author:
Date: 1956-06-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 7th. According to a report by the Romanian News Agency on the 6th, the Romanian Council of Ministers recently stipulated in a decision to reduce the workday to less than eight hours for some workers and technicians working in difficult working conditions.

    The amount of time reduction depends on the specific working conditions. For example, workers in certain chemical factories and workers working underground where the temperature reaches 30 degrees Celsius or higher have a six-hour workday, and divers have a four- to six-hour workday.

### 罗马尼亚缩短特殊工作工人的工作日

1956-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚缩短特殊工作工人的工作日
    新华社布拉格7日电　据罗马尼亚通讯社六日报道，罗马尼亚部长会议最近在一项决定中规定，把一些在困难的工作条件下工作的工人和技术人员的工作日缩短到八小时以下。
    缩短时间的多少根据具体工作条件而定。例如某些化学工厂的工人和在温度达摄氏三十度以上的地面下工作的工人，他们的工作日是六小时，潜水员的工作日是四到六小时。
