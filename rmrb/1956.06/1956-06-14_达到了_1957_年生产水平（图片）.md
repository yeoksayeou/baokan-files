Title: Reached the 1957 Production Level (Picture)
Author: Lu Yongzhen
Date: 1956-06-14
Page: 2

    Reached the 1957 Production Level
    The state-owned Shanghai Turbine Factory had already reached the 1957 production level on May 31, one year and seven months ahead of schedule. These impeller wheels for 6,000 kW turbo-generators, which have already been bored, will undergo further precision machining. Photo by Lu Yongzhen (Draft from Labor Daily)

### 达到了1957年生产水平（图片）
陆永桢
1956-06-14
第2版()
专栏：

    达到了1957年生产水平
    国营上海汽轮机厂已经在5月31日提前一年七个月达到1957年的生产水平。这些已经磨孔完毕的六千瓩汽轮发电机的叶轮，将再进行精车加工。　陆永桢摄（劳动报社稿）
