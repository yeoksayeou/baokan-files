Title: Shen Yanbing Hosts Japanese Cultural Delegation and Film Representatives
Author:
Date: 1956-06-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 27th: Minister of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Shen Yanbing, hosted a banquet this evening to entertain the Japanese Asian Solidarity Committee Cultural Delegation and Japanese film representatives.

　　Shen Yanbing delivered a welcoming speech. He said that the friendly exchanges and cultural exchange between the people of China and Japan are increasing day by day, and the cultural exchange between the two countries has promoted mutual understanding and friendship between the people of China and Japan. He wished for the further development of Sino-Japanese cultural exchange and friendly relations.

　　The Secretary-General of the Japanese Asian Solidarity Committee, Tokusaburo Awa, delivered a speech at the banquet. He expressed his hope that a cultural delegation composed of Chinese literary and art figures could visit Japan, and raised a glass to the early realization of the visit of a Chinese cultural delegation to Japan.

　　Before the banquet, Shen Yanbing, Ding Xilin, Liu Zhiming, Xia Yan and others held talks with Japanese friends on issues such as China's writing reform.

### 沈雁冰宴日文化代表团和电影界代表

1956-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    沈雁冰宴日文化代表团和电影界代表
    据新华社27日讯　中华人民共和国文化部长沈雁冰今天晚间举行宴会，招待日本亚洲团结委员会文化代表团和日本电影界代表。
    沈雁冰致欢迎词。他说，中日两国人民的友好往来和文化交流日益增加，两国的文化交流促进了中日两国人民的相互了解和友谊。他祝中日文化交流和友好关系的进一步发展。
    日本亚洲团结委员会事务局长淡德三郎在宴会上致词。他希望有一个由中国文艺界人士组成的文化代表团能够到日本去访问，并且举杯祝中国文化代表团的访问日本能早日实现。
    在宴会以前，沈雁冰、丁西林、刘芝明、夏衍等同日本朋友就中国的文字改革等问题进行了谈话。
