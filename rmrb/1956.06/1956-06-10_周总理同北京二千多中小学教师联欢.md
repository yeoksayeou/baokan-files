Title: Premier Zhou Celebrates with More Than Two Thousand Primary and Secondary School Teachers in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-06-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 9th, Beijing—Premier Zhou Enlai spent a pleasant weekend evening with more than two thousand middle and primary school teachers in Beijing at the Central Committee Auditorium of the Communist Youth League today.
    Teachers held a celebratory evening party in the courtyard decorated with colorful electric lights.
    When Premier Zhou arrived, he was warmly surrounded by the teachers, and he met with them in the dance hall.
    Famous Chinese educators Wu Yuzhang, Xu Teli, Chen Boda, Dong Chuncai, and Lin Liru also joined the teachers tonight to enjoy many literary and artistic programs performed by teachers and students.

### 周总理同北京二千多中小学教师联欢

1956-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理同北京二千多中小学教师联欢
    新华社9日讯　周恩来总理今晚在青年团中央礼堂和北京二千多个中、小学教师在一起度过了愉快的周末。
    在布满彩色电灯的庭院里，教师们举行着联欢晚会。
    周总理来到这里的时候，被教师们热情地簇拥着，他在跳舞厅和教师们见了面。
    中国著名教育家吴玉章、徐特立、陈伯达、董纯才、林砺儒，今晚也和教师们一起欣赏了师生们演出的许多文艺节目。
