Title: Li Jishen, Shen Junru and Others Conclude Inspection in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1956-06-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, May 30th: Li Jishen and Shen Junru, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, along with representatives Qu Wu, Jiang Guangnai, Ye Duyi, Sa Kongliao, and others, a total of nine people, returned to Beijing today after concluding their inspection in Tianjin. Continuing their inspection in Tianjin are Chen Shutong, Chen Qiyou, Gao Chongmin, Cai Tingkai, Lu Han, Lü Ji, and others, a total of nineteen people.

The scope of the inspection by the People's Representatives and CPPCC members included work in industrial and agricultural production, as well as justice, street administration, overseas Chinese affairs, labor reform of criminal offenders, joint state-private enterprises, and culture and education. Vice Chairman Li Jishen inspected the Tanggu New Port, among other places, and together with other representatives, listened to reports on the socialist transformation of capitalist industry and commerce. In addition to listening to briefings, Vice Chairman Chen Shutong also visited factories and held discussions with private sector personnel. Vice Chairman Shen Junru and others visited universities and colleges to understand the situation of senior intellectuals in all aspects. After understanding the overall situation of judicial work, he also went deep among the masses for inspection.

### 李济深、沈钧儒等结束在天津的视察

1956-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    李济深、沈钧儒等结束在天津的视察
    据新华社天津30日电　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒和代表屈武、蒋光鼐、叶笃义、萨空了等九人，今天结束在天津的视察后返回北京。现在继续在天津视察的还有陈叔通、陈其尤、高崇民、蔡廷锴、卢汉、吕骥等十九人。
    人民代表和政协委员视察的范围包括工农业生产和司法、街道、侨务、刑事罪犯劳动改造、公私合营企业、文化教育等方面的工作。李济深副委员长视察了塘沽新港等，并且和其他代表一起听取了有关资本主义工商业社会主义改造工作的汇报。陈叔通副委员长除了听取情况汇报以外，还到工厂中和私方人员进行了交谈。沈钧儒副委员长等到高等学校访问高级知识分子，细致地了解了他们各方面的情况。他在了解了司法工作的全面情况后，还深入到群众中视察。
