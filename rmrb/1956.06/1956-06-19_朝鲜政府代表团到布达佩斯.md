Title: North Korean Government Delegation Arrives in Budapest
Author:
Date: 1956-06-19
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 18th: Budapest dispatch: The government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Kim Il-sung, arrived in Budapest on June 17th at the invitation of the Government of the Hungarian People's Republic from Bucharest.
    First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, Rákosi, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Hegedüs, Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, Dobi, Chairman of the National Assembly, Rónai and others greeted the distinguished North Korean guests at the airport.
    Diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union, China and other countries also welcomed them at the airport.

### 朝鲜政府代表团到布达佩斯

1956-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府代表团到布达佩斯
    据新华社布拉格18日电　布达佩斯消息：以金日成为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团，应匈牙利人民共和国政府的邀请，在六月十七日自布加勒斯特到达布达佩斯。
    匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记拉科西、部长会议主席赫格居斯、匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比、国民议会主席罗奈伊等人都到机场迎接朝鲜贵宾。
    苏联、中国和其他一些国家的外交使节也到机场欢迎。
