Title: Bountiful Wheat Harvest at Douma Agricultural Production Cooperative (Photo)
Author: Gao Liang
Date: 1956-06-17
Page: 5

    Bountiful Wheat Harvest at Douma Agricultural Production Cooperative

    The Douma Agricultural Production Cooperative in Xingping County, Shaanxi Province, achieved an unprecedented wheat harvest this year. The average yield of over 6,100 mu of wheat fields reached 500 jin per mu, with high-yield fields reaching 1,000 to 1,200 jin per mu.

    Left photo: Estimating yields and selecting seeds in the field. Bottom photo: Combine harvesters working in the wheat fields of the Douma Agricultural Production Cooperative.
                                         Our reporter Gao Liang

    The labor competition initiated by the Fourth Brigade has begun. Reporting the good news of the bumper harvest to Chairman Mao and the Third Session of the First National People's Congress.

    During the wheat harvest season, to strengthen cotton field management, female cooperative members are spreading insecticide powder to prevent red spiders.

    Grain distribution begins according to the plan to guarantee increased income for cooperative members.

    After the members' incomes increase, in addition to purchasing production tools, the women are particularly interested in buying printed fabrics.

### 豆马农业生产合作社小麦丰收（图片）
高粮
1956-06-17
第5版()
专栏：

    豆马农业生产合作社小麦丰收
    陕西省兴平县豆马农业生产合作社，今年小麦获得空前丰收。六千一百多亩麦田平均每亩收获量达五百斤，其中丰产田收获量每亩高达一千斤至一千二百斤。
    左图：田间估产和选种。　下图：康拜因在豆马农业生产合作社的麦田里作业。　                    
                                         本报记者　高粮摄
    由第四大队发起的劳动竞赛开始了。把丰收的喜讯报告给毛主席和第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议。
    在麦收季节里，为了加强棉田管理，女社员正撒杀虫粉防止红蜘蛛。
    按照保证增加社员收入的计划开始分粮。
    社员的收入增加后，除了购买生产工具以外，妇女们对购买花布特别感到兴趣。
