Title: National People's Congress Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-06-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 21st dispatch: On the afternoon of the 21st, ten people's representatives and heads of various departments of the state organs spoke at the plenary meeting of the Third Session of the First National People's Congress. They were: Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Ma Yinchu, President of Peking University; Bo Yibo, Director of the Third Office of the State Council and Director of the State Economic Commission; Fu Zuoyi, Minister of Water Resources; Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications; People's Representatives Xu Jingzhang, Yang Weiyi, Song Yunbin, Yang Hanxian, and Lin Wenbiao. (Full texts of the speeches will be issued separately.)

In his speech, Bo Yibo announced: "The long-awaited domestically produced cars will begin mass production on October 1st; the jet engine has also been successfully trial-produced." His words were greeted with warm applause.

Xu Jingzhang, director of the Douma Advanced Agricultural Production Cooperative in Shaanxi Province, entrusted by all the members of his cooperative, presented a bundle of plump and large wheat ears and a bag of newly harvested wheat grains to the Congress and Chairman Mao at today's meeting. When Chairman Mao smilingly accepted this beautiful gift and shook hands with Xu Jingzhang cordially, the hall burst into thunderous applause.

The executive chairmen presiding over today's meeting were: Shen Junru, Rong Yiren, Zhang Nanxian, Zhu Shunyu, Li Jinxi, Ji Jinmei, Wu Yaozong, and Ba Jin.

Xinhua News Agency, 21st dispatch: Most of the delegation groups of the Third Session of the First National People's Congress concluded their group discussions on the "Model Regulations (Draft) of Advanced Agricultural Production Cooperatives" this morning.

In the past few days of discussion, many representatives put forward opinions on modifying and supplementing some clauses and words in the "Model Regulations (Draft) of Advanced Agricultural Production Cooperatives." Today, some of the delegation groups discussed the work report of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

### 全国人民代表大会举行会议

1956-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会举行会议
    据新华社21日讯　二十一日下午，有十位人民代表和国家机关各部门的负责人，在第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议的全体会议上发言。他们是：全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长陈叔通，北京大学校长马寅初，国务院第三办公室主任兼国家经济委员会主任薄一波，水利部长傅作义，邮电部长朱学范，人民代表许敬章、杨惟义、宋云彬、杨汉先、林文彪。（发言全文另发）
    薄一波在发言中宣布：“盼望已久的国产汽车，将在十月一日开始大量生产；喷气式飞机发动机也已经试制成功。”他的话引起了热烈的掌声。
    陕西省豆马高级农业生产合作社主任许敬章，受他的合作社全体社员的委托，在今天的会议上把一束长得又肥又大的麦穗和一袋新收的麦粒，献给了大会和毛主席。当毛主席含笑接受这个美好的礼物并且同许敬章亲切握手的时候，会场上掌声雷动。
    主持今天会议的执行主席是：沈钧儒、荣毅仁、张难先、朱顺余、黎锦熙、计晋美、吴耀宗、巴金。
    据新华社21日讯　第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议大多数代表小组今天上午结束了对“高级农业生产合作社示范章程（草案）”的分组讨论。
    过去几天的讨论中，有许多代表对“高级农业生产合作社示范章程（草案）”的一些条文和字句提出了修改和补充的意见。今天有一部分代表小组讨论了全国人民代表大会常务委员会的工作报告。
