Title: Zhuang Youth's Sculpture Creation (Picture)
Author: Tan Zhiqiang
Date: 1956-06-17
Page: 3

    Zhuang Youth's Sculpture Creation
    Huang Jundu, a Zhuang youth and student at Nanning No. 1 Middle School, is very interested in the art of sculpture. A statue he created of a farmer gazing at the fields was exhibited at the Guangxi Provincial Youth Art Exhibition in early June and was well received by the audience. This is Huang Jundu sculpting.
      Xinhua News Agency reporter Tan Zhiqiang photo

### 僮族青年的雕塑创作（图片）
谭志强
1956-06-17
第3版()
专栏：

    僮族青年的雕塑创作
    南宁市第一中学学生、僮族青年黄君度对雕塑艺术很有兴趣。他创作的一座农民眺望田野的塑像，六月初在广西省全省青年美术作品展览会展出得到观众的好评。这是黄君度正在雕塑。
      新华社记者    谭志强摄
