Title: Chairman Mao Congratulates Nasser on Election as President
Author:
Date: 1956-06-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 26th. Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram today to Gamal Abdel Nasser, congratulating him on his election as President of the Republic of Egypt. The telegram reads as follows:
His Excellency Gamal Abdel Nasser, President of the Republic of Egypt:
    I am delighted to learn of Your Excellency's election as President of the Republic of Egypt. On behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to you and the Egyptian people. I wish you and the Egyptian people even greater achievements in the cause of building and developing the Republic of Egypt.
                                  Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                         June 26, 1956, in Beijing

### 毛主席电贺纳赛尔当选总统

1956-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电贺纳赛尔当选总统
    新华社26日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东今天打电报给加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔，祝贺他当选为埃及共和国总统。电文如下：埃及共和国加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔总统阁下：
    欣悉阁下当选为埃及共和国总统，我谨代表中国人民并且以我个人的名义向您和埃及人民表示衷心的祝贺。祝您和埃及人民在建设和发展埃及共和国的事业中获得更大的成就。
                                  中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                                         1956年6月26日于北京
