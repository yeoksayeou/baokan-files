Title: Shanghai Exceeds Public Bond Subscription Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-06-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, June 1st. As of yesterday, the people of Shanghai had subscribed to more than 72 million yuan of the 1956 National Economic Construction Bonds, exceeding the planned target. Currently, 20% of the subscribed funds have been paid into the national treasury.

### 上海推销公债超过计划

1956-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    上海推销公债超过计划
    据新华社上海1日电　到昨天止，上海人民已经认购1956年国家经济建设公债七千二百多万元，超过了预定计划。现在全市已有20％的认购债款缴入国库。
