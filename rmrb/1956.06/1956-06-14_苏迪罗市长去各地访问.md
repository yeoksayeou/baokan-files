Title: Mayor Sudiro Visits Various Places
Author:
Date: 1956-06-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 12th dispatch: The Mayor of Jakarta Special City, Indonesia, Sudiro, and his wife, Deputy Mayor Danubroto, and their entourage, left Beijing by plane this morning to visit Nanjing, Shanghai, Chongqing, and Kunming.
　　Before leaving Beijing, they accepted an invitation from the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station and delivered a radio address to the citizens of Beijing.

### 苏迪罗市长去各地访问

1956-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

    苏迪罗市长去各地访问
    据新华社12日讯　　印度尼西亚雅加达特别市市长苏迪罗和夫人，副市长达努布罗托以及随行人员，今天上午乘飞机离开北京，前往南京、上海、重庆和昆明等地参观访问。
    他们在离开北京以前，应北京人民广播电台的邀请，向北京市民发表了广播演说。
