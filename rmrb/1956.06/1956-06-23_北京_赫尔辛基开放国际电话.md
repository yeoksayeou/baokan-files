Title: Beijing-Helsinki International Telephone Service Opens
Author:
Date: 1956-06-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. In order to meet the communication needs between China and Finland, the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of our country and the telecommunications authorities of relevant countries have agreed that the Beijing-Helsinki international telephone service will be opened from June 20, 1956.

### 北京—赫尔辛基开放国际电话

1956-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

    北京—赫尔辛基开放国际电话
    新华社22日讯　为了适应中国和芬兰间的通信需要，我国邮电部和有关国家的电信主管机关已经商妥，自1956年6月20日起开放北京—赫尔辛基间的国际电话业务。
