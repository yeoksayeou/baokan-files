Title: Another New Coalfield Discovered in the Shandan Coal Mining Area, Gansu

Author:
Date: 1956-06-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency News: Another new coalfield, the Shandan No. 4 Coalfield, has been discovered in the Shandan Coal Mining Area in the Hexi Corridor of Gansu Province. According to the data obtained by the geological personnel, this coalfield can be used to design a pair of mines with an annual output of hundreds of thousands of tons.

### 甘肃山丹煤矿区又探明一个新井田

1956-06-05
第2版()
专栏：

    甘肃山丹煤矿区又探明一个新井田
    据新华社讯　甘肃省河西走廊的山丹煤矿区，又探明了一个新井田——山丹四号井田。根据地质人员们获得的资料，这个井田可供设计一对年产几十万吨的矿井。
