    Our country's first attempt to use airplanes in the early-stage aphid control work on cotton has been carried out in Anyang County, Henan Province, with a pest control efficiency of 80%-90%. Members of the Xingfu Agricultural Production Cooperative are cheering for the aphid-controlling airplane.
