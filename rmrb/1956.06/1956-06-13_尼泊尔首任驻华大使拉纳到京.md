Title: Nepal's First Ambassador to China, Rana, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-06-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 12th: Lieutenant General Rana, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Nepal to China, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 12th, accompanied by First Secretary Banterui, Military Attaché Colonel Basnyat, and others.

Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others were at the station to welcome them.

Heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials from various countries also went to greet them.

### 尼泊尔首任驻华大使拉纳到京

1956-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔首任驻华大使拉纳到京
    新华社12日讯　　尼泊尔王国驻中国首任特命全权大使拉纳中将偕一等秘书班特瑞、武官巴斯尼亚特上校等，在十二日晚乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有外交部礼宾司司长柯华等。
    各国驻华使节和外交官员也前往迎接。
