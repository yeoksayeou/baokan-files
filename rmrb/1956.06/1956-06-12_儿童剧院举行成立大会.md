Title: Children's Theatre Holds Founding Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-06-12
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 10th: The China Children's Theatre held its founding conference on the 10th.

More than 1,300 people from all walks of life in the capital, including children, celebrated the birth of the Children's Theatre at the Capital Theatre.

Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing requested at the meeting that the theatre cultivate a large number of children's drama talents, complete the task of a model children's theatre, and encouraged those engaged in children's drama to work hard to improve their political level and artistic accomplishment, and create unforgettable artistic images. He hoped that the theatre and children, and writers would make friends widely and cooperate to perform more and better children's plays.

Hu Ke Shi, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, Lin Liru, Vice Minister of Education, Shen Ziji, Secretary of the All-China Women's Federation, and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, all expressed warm congratulations on the establishment of the Children's Theatre. Representatives of the Young Pioneers of Beijing also spoke at the meeting about the children's love of drama, hoping that writers would write more children's plays.

Ren Hong, Director of the China Children's Theatre, reported on the growth of the theatre and its future tasks, saying that the theatre will perform more and better for children in the future.

After the meeting, the fairy tale play "Malan Flower" was performed.

### 儿童剧院举行成立大会

1956-06-12
第3版()
专栏：

    儿童剧院举行成立大会
    新华社10日讯　中国儿童剧院十日举行成立大会。
    首都各界人士和少年儿童一千三百多人，在首都剧场庆祝儿童剧院的诞生。
    文化部部长沈雁冰在会上要求剧院培养出大量的儿童戏剧人材，完成一个示范性的儿童剧院的任务，并且勉励从事儿童戏剧的工作者努力提高政治水平和艺术修养，创造出令人难忘的艺术形象。他希望剧院和少年儿童、作家们广泛地交朋友，共同合作演出更多更好的儿童剧。
    青年团中央委员会书记胡克实、教育部副部长林砺儒、中华全国民主妇女联合会书记沈兹九和北京市副市长吴晗都对儿童剧院的成立表示热烈祝贺。北京的少年先锋队队员代表也在会上讲述了少年儿童们热爱戏剧的心情，希望作家们多写些儿童剧。
    中国儿童剧院院长任虹报告了剧院的成长和今后的任务，表示剧院今后将更多更好地为儿童演出。
    会后演出了童话剧“马兰花”。
