Title: “Science Education Film Exhibition” to Begin in July
Author:
Date: 1956-06-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 27th: The Ministry of Culture and the China Association for Science and Technology have decided to hold a "Science Education Film Exhibition" successively in various parts of the country starting in July.

　　A total of more than twenty Soviet and Chinese science education short films will participate in the exhibition.

　　The first ten cities to hold the "Science Education Film Exhibition" will be Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Shenyang, Harbin, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chongqing, Xi'an, and Urumqi, from July 13th to July 19th.

### “科学教育影片展览”将在七月开始举行

1956-06-28
第3版()
专栏：

    “科学教育影片展览”将在七月开始举行
    据新华社27日讯　文化部、中华全国科学技术普及协会决定从七月开始，在全国各地先后举办“科学教育影片展览”。
    参加展览的共有二十多部苏联和我国的科学教育短片。
    最先举行“科学教育影片展览”的有北京、天津、上海、沈阳、哈尔滨、武汉、广州、重庆、西安、乌鲁木齐等十个城市，是从七月十三日开始到七月十九日止。
