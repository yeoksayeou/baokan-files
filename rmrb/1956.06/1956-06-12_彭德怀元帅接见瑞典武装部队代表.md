Title: Marshal Peng Dehuai Receives Representatives of the Swedish Armed Forces
Author:
Date: 1956-06-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 11th: Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, received Lieutenant Colonel Aris Kog, a representative of the Swedish Armed Forces, this afternoon.

Lieutenant Colonel Aris Kog arrived in Beijing by train this morning from Shenyang.

Present at the reception were General Chen Geng, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Li Da, Deputy Minister of National Defense, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office, and Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Book, was also present at the reception.

After the reception, Marshal Peng Dehuai had dinner with Lieutenant Colonel Aris Kog.

### 彭德怀元帅接见瑞典武装部队代表

1956-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

    彭德怀元帅接见瑞典武装部队代表
    新华社11日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅，在今天下午接见了瑞典武装部队代表阿里斯考格中校。
    阿里斯考格中校是在今天上午由沈阳乘火车到达北京的。
    接见的时候在座的，有中国人民解放军副总参谋长陈赓大将，国防部副部长李达上将、办公厅主任萧向荣中将，外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华。
    接见的时候，瑞典驻中国大使布克也在座。
    接见以后，彭德怀元帅同阿里斯考格中校共进晚餐。
