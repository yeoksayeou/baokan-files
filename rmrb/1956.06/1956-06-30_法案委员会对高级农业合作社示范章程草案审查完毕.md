Title: Legislative Committee Completes Review of Draft Model Charter for Advanced Agricultural Cooperatives
Author:
Date: 1956-06-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 29th – The Legislative Committee of the First National People's Congress today completed its review of the Draft Model Charter for Advanced Agricultural Production Cooperatives and finalized its review report on the draft charter.

After listening to Agriculture Minister Liao Luyan's explanation of the Draft Model Charter for Advanced Agricultural Production Cooperatives, the Legislative Committee held a total of four meetings to review the model charter draft.

During the deliberations at each meeting, the Legislative Committee also comprehensively studied the proposed revisions submitted by the various delegation groups. Some content of the draft was modified, and some articles that were not precise or specific enough were also modified in wording and adjusted in sequence. On the 27th, the first draft revision of the model charter was completed, and on the 29th, based on the opinions raised by the delegation groups during the discussion of the draft revision, further research and modifications were made, and the revised draft was adopted.

The Legislative Committee’s review report and the revised draft of the model charter will be submitted to the Third Session of the First National People's Congress, with the recommendation that the Third Session approve the revised draft of the model charter.

### 法案委员会对高级农业合作社示范章程草案审查完毕

1956-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    法案委员会对高级农业合作社示范章程草案审查完毕
    据新华社29日讯　第一届全国人民代表大会法案委员会今天对高级农业生产合作社示范章程（草案）审查完毕，并且完成了关于这个章程（草案）的审查报告。
    法案委员会在听了农业部长廖鲁言所作的关于高级农业生产合作社示范章程（草案）的说明以后，先后共召开了四次会议，对这个示范章程（草案）进行了审查。
    法案委员会各次会议在审议这个草案时，还综合研究了各代表小组在讨论时代表们所提出的修改意见，对草案的内容方面作了一些修改，对草案中写得不够确切或者不够具体的一些条文，也作了文字上的修改和某些次序上的调整。二十七日的会议第一次完成了关于这个示范章程的修正草案，二十九日的会议又根据各代表小组对这个修正草案讨论时所提出的意见，再次进行研究和修改，并且通过了这个修正草案。
    法案委员会的这个审查报告和这个示范章程的修正草案，都将提交第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议，并且建议第三次会议对这个示范章程修正草案予以通过。
