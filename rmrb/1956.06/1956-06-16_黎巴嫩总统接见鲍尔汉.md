Title: Lebanese President Receives Burhan
Author:
Date: 1956-06-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, June 15th: Lebanese President Camille Chamoun received Burhan, head of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, and other leaders of the delegation at noon yesterday, and had a friendly and pleasant conversation.

### 黎巴嫩总统接见鲍尔汉

1956-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩总统接见鲍尔汉
    新华社贝鲁特15日电　黎巴嫩总统夏蒙在昨天中午接见了中国文化艺术代表团团长鲍尔汉和代表团其他领导人员，进行了友好愉快的交谈。
