Title: Proposal Review Work Completed
Author:
Date: 1956-06-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th: The Proposal Review Committee of the Third Session of the First National People's Congress today completed the review of all proposals received by the session.

    The Proposal Review Committee held its first meeting on the 18th, mutually elected Zhang Naiqi and Cao Diqiu as deputy directors, and adopted the working plan of the Proposal Review Committee. According to the working plan, five specialized review groups were set up, including comprehensive (covering political and legal affairs, ethnic affairs, overseas Chinese affairs, religious affairs, etc.), industrial transportation, agriculture, forestry, water conservancy and animal husbandry, financial and trade, and cultural, educational, scientific and health. The list of committee members and the list of conveners of each group were adopted.

    Each group of the Proposal Review Committee conducted detailed and thorough investigations and studies on the situations and opinions raised in the relevant proposals, and held review meetings on the 22nd to discuss each case and put forward preliminary review opinions.

    The Proposal Review Committee held two meetings on the 23rd and 25th to review all proposals case by case and adopted the "Draft Review Opinions of the Proposal Review Committee of the Third Session of the First National People's Congress" case by case. The meeting on the 25th also discussed and adopted the Review Report of the Proposal Review Committee on the proposals.

    The Third Session of the First National People's Congress received a total of 157 proposals from more than 200 deputies and six deputy groups. After deliberation, the Proposal Review Committee believes that many opinions in these proposals are in line with the current needs of the country and also have feasible conditions. These proposals are of positive significance for completing, advancing, and overfulfilling the first five-year plan for the development of the national economy in our country.

    The draft review opinions and report of the proposals approved by the Proposal Review Committee will be submitted to the Third Session of the National People's Congress for deliberation and approval.

### 提案审查工作完成

1956-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    提案审查工作完成
    新华社25日讯　第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议提案审查委员会今天已经对这次会议收到的全部提案完成了审查工作。
    提案审查委员会在十八日举行了第一次会议，互推了章乃器、曹荻秋为副主任委员，通过了提案审查委员会工作方案；并且根据工作方案分设了综合（包括政治法律和民族事务、华侨事务、宗教事务等方面）、工业交通、农林水利畜牧、财金贸易、文教科学卫生等五个专业审查组，通过了委员分组名单和各组召集人名单。
    提案审查委员会各组对有关提案中提出的情况和意见做了详尽周密的调查研究，并且在二十二日分别召开审查会，逐案讨论，提出了初步审查意见。
    提案审查委员会在二十三日和二十五日召开了两次会议，对全部提案逐案审议，并且逐案通过了“第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议提案审查意见（草案）”。二十五日的会议上还讨论通过了提案审查委员会关于提案的审查报告。
    第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议一共收到二百多位代表和六个代表小组提出的一百五十七件提案。提案审查委员会审议后认为，在这些提案中，很多意见是符合于国家当前需要的，而且也具备着可行的条件；这些提案对于完成以及提前和超额完成我国发展国民经济第一个五年计划是具有积极作用的。
    提案审查委员会通过的提案审查意见草案和报告，都将提请全国人民代表大会第三次会议审议通过。
