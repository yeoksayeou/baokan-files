Title: Chen Yi, Vice Premier, Leads Central Delegation Back to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-06-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. After the Central Delegation, led by Vice Premier of the State Council, Chen Yi, went to Lhasa to congratulate the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, they returned to Beijing by special train this afternoon.

Those who went to the station to welcome them included Deng Xiaoping, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council; He Long and Ulanhu, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General; Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, and Saifudin, Vice Chairmen and members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; General Chen Geng, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; General Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department; Liu Geping, Chairman, and Xie Fumin, Deputy Chairman of the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress; Liu Chun and Sakongliao, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, and heads of various ministries and commissions; heads of the Dalai's Office in Beijing and the Panchen's Office in Beijing; heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations; minority nationality figures in Beijing, figures from religious circles in the capital and other walks of life, as well as cadres from government offices, totaling more than 400 people.

### 陈毅副总理率领中央代表团回到北京

1956-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理率领中央代表团回到北京
    新华社14日讯　由国务院副总理陈毅率领的中央代表团前往拉萨祝贺西藏自治区筹备委员会成立以后，在今天下午乘专车回到北京。
    前往车站欢迎的，有中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理邓小平，国务院副总理贺龙、乌兰夫，秘书长习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李维汉、陈叔通、赛福鼎和委员，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，中国人民解放军副总参谋长陈赓大将，总政治部副主任萧华上将，全国人民代表大会民族委员会主任委员刘格平、副主任委员谢扶民，民族事务委员会副主任刘春、萨空了和各部、委负责人，达赖驻京办事处和班禅驻京办事处的负责人，各民主党派和各人民团体负责人，在北京的各少数民族人士，首都宗教界和其他各界人士，以及机关干部等四百多人。
