    Extracurricular Subject Groups for Middle School Students

    In Tianjin, 16,400 students from 78 middle schools are participating in extracurricular subject groups, engaging in various interesting research activities.

    Future Silver Eagle Pilots

    The "Aviation Model" group at Tianjin No. 16 Middle School recently created a new type of free-flying model airplane. Its flight time has reached over three minutes. This group has 92 members, who often discuss their aspirations to become airplane manufacturers or pilots.

    Universal Solid Geometry Model Table

    The "Geometry Group" at Tianjin No. 18 Middle School created an ingenious "Universal Solid Geometry Model Table." Any difficult-to-draw or difficult-to-calculate solid figures can be solved with the help of this model table. The construction of this model table is very simple, using only materials such as wooden blocks, strings, and lead weights.

    Automatic Round-Trip Electric Trolley

    Dong Baoliang of the "Radio" group at Tianjin No. 3 Middle School manufactured an automatic round-trip electric trolley. After the power is turned on, this small and exquisite trolley runs from one end of the track to the other, and then automatically returns. This is an experimental product manufactured by Dong Baoliang to verify the principle of changing the direction of current.

        Weekly Flower

    First-year students at Tianjin No. 15 Middle School can see a new plant specimen every week, labeled with its name, origin, habits, and other descriptions. This is the "Michurin Biology Society" group's "Weekly Flower," done to help first-year students identify plants in real life. In the activities of beautifying the campus, this group also planted lotus flowers and raised goldfish. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 中学生的课外学科小组

1956-06-11
第3版()
专栏：

    中学生的课外学科小组
    在天津市的七十八所中等学校里，有一万六千四百多名学生参加了课外的学科小组，他们进行着各种有趣的研究活动。
    未来的银鹰驾驶者
    天津市第十六中学“航空模型”小组最近制作出一种新型的自由飞翔式模型飞机。它的飞行时间已达到三分钟以上。这个小组有九十二个成员，他们经常谈论做一个飞机制造家或者当一个飞行员的热望。
    立体几何万能模型台
    天津市第十八中学“几何小组”制作了一个精巧的“立体几何万能模型台”。任何难画的和难计算的立体图型借助于这个模型台，就能求得解决。这个模型台的构造十分简单，只用了木块、线绳、铅坠等材料。
    自动往返的小电车
    天津市第三中学“无线电”小组的董宝亮制造了一辆自动往返的小电车。这辆小巧玲珑的电车通上电以后，就从轨道的这头跑到那头，然后又自动地返回来。这是董宝亮为印证电流方向改变原理而制造的实验品。
        每周一花
    天津市第十五中学一年级的学生每周都可以看见一种新的标着名称、产地、习性等说明的植物标本。这是“米丘林生物社”小组为了帮助一年级学生在实际生活中识别植物而做的“每周一花”。这个小组在美化校园的活动中，还栽种了荷花，饲养了金鱼。　（据新华社讯）
