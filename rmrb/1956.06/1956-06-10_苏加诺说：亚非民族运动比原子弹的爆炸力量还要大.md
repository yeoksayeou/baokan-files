Title: Sukarno Says: The Afro-Asian National Movement is More Powerful Than the Explosion of an Atomic Bomb
Author:
Date: 1956-06-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: Montreal dispatch: Indonesian President Sukarno, in a speech delivered at McGill University in Montreal on the 8th, once again condemned colonialism and emphasized the significance of the national independence movements in Asian and African countries.

Sukarno said: "The national independence movements of Indonesia and other countries after World War II, in terms of their significance, are even greater than the explosive power of an atomic bomb." He said, "The world can no longer ignore Asia, and the world can no longer treat Asia merely as a market or as a source of cheap raw materials and cheap labor. Asian countries are awakening and are determined to fulfill their responsibility in international affairs in order to seek the happiness of the world."

He said that the Bandung Conference proved that Asian and African countries have reached political maturity, but these countries are facing the urgent need to improve their living standards.

Sukarno spoke about the achievements Indonesia has made in overcoming the evil legacy of colonialism after gaining independence. He said that Indonesia welcomes aid from industrialized countries. However, he said, "This aid can only be accepted if it does not restrict our independence in maintaining our actions and policies in internal and foreign affairs."

### 苏加诺说：亚非民族运动比原子弹的爆炸力量还要大

1956-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    苏加诺说：亚非民族运动比原子弹的爆炸力量还要大
    新华社9日讯　蒙特利尔消息：印度尼西亚总统苏加诺8日在蒙特利尔麦吉尔大学发表的演说中，再一次谴责殖民主义和强调亚非国家民族独立运动的意义。
    苏加诺说：“第二次世界大战后印度尼西亚和其他国家的民族独立运动从它的意义上说来，比之原子弹的爆炸力量还要巨大”，他说：“世界再也不能无视亚洲，世界再也不能把亚洲只是当作一个市场或者当作廉价原料和廉价劳动力的来源地来对待。亚洲的国家正在觉醒，而且决心为谋求世界的幸福而在国际事务中尽他们的责任”。
    他说，万隆会议证明亚洲和非洲国家已经达到政治上的成熟，但是这些国家面临着提高生活水平的迫切需要。
    苏加诺谈到印度尼西亚获得独立后在克服殖民主义留下的罪恶遗产方面所取得的成就，他说，印度尼西亚欢迎工业发达的国家的援助。但是，他说：“这种援助只有在不会限制我们在内政和外交方面保持行动和政策的独立的情况下，才可以接受。”
