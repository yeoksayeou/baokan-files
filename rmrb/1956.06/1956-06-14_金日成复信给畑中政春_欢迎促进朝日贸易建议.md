Title: Kim Il-sung Replies to Hatanaka Masaharu, Welcomes Proposal to Promote Japan-North Korea Trade
Author:
Date: 1956-06-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 13th: Premier Kim Il-sung, in a recent reply to Hatanaka Masaharu, Secretary-General of the Japan-North Korea Association, welcomed the proposal by the Japan National Conference for the Promotion of Japan-North Korea Trade and Fisheries Negotiations to send an economic delegation to North Korea to resolve trade and fisheries issues between the two countries.

The Japan National Conference for the Promotion of Japan-North Korea Trade and Fisheries Negotiations was held in Tokyo on April 28th. Hatanaka Masaharu wrote to the Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea based on the resolution passed at the conference, putting forward corresponding proposals.

### 金日成复信给畑中政春  欢迎促进朝日贸易建议

1956-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

    金日成复信给畑中政春
    欢迎促进朝日贸易建议
    据新华社平壤13日电　金日成首相在最近写给日朝协会秘书长畑中政春的复信中，欢迎日本全国促进朝日两国贸易和渔业协商大会派遣经济代表团到朝鲜解决两国间的贸易和渔业问题。
    促进朝日两国贸易和渔业协商大会是在四月二十八日在东京举行的。畑中政春根据大会通过决议写信给朝鲜民主主义人民共和国首相提出了相应的建议。
