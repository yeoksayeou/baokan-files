Title: Korean Delegation Heads to Albania
Author:
Date: 1956-06-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, June 29th: According to TASS, the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Kim Il-sung, left Sofia for Albania on June 29th. Those who went to the airport to see them off included Damyanov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly, and Yugov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, among others.

The Korean government delegation arrived in Bulgaria on June 25th for a visit. During the visit, they toured and visited industrial enterprises, construction projects, and cultural institutions in the cities of Sofia, Plovdiv, and Dimitrovgrad, as well as some agricultural production cooperatives. The Korean government delegation received a warm reception and welcome from the working people of Bulgaria everywhere they went.

### 朝鲜代表团去阿尔巴尼亚

1956-06-30
第2版()
专栏：

    朝鲜代表团去阿尔巴尼亚
    新华社29日电　据塔斯社索非亚讯：以金日成为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团，在六月二十九日离开索非亚前往阿尔巴尼亚。前往机场送行的有保加利亚国民议会主席团主席达米扬诺夫、部长会议主席于哥夫等人。
    朝鲜政府代表团是六月二十五日到保加利亚访问的。在访问期间，他们参观和访问了索非亚、普罗夫迪夫、季米特洛夫格拉德等城市的工业企业、建筑工程和文化机构以及一些农业生产合作社等。朝鲜政府代表团到处都受到了保加利亚劳动人民的亲切接待和欢迎。
