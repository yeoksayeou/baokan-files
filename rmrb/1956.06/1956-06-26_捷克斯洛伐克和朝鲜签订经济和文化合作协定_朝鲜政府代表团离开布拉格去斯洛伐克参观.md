Title: Czechoslovakia and North Korea Sign Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement; North Korean Government Delegation Leaves Prague to Visit Slovakia
Author:
Date: 1956-06-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 25th: The government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Kim Il-sung, left Prague on June 23rd.

The delegation held talks with the Czechoslovak government delegation after arriving in Prague on the 21st. On the 23rd, the two sides signed an agreement and protocol on economic and cultural cooperation between Czechoslovakia and Korea.

During the period from the 21st to the 23rd, the Korean government delegation, in addition to sightseeing and visiting in Prague, also visited a machine manufacturing factory in Pilsen.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 25th: The government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Premier Kim Il-sung, is currently visiting Slovakia. After arriving in Bratislava, the capital of Slovakia, on June 24th, they visited factories and prefabricated housing construction sites, and visited tractor stations and agricultural cooperatives.

### 捷克斯洛伐克和朝鲜签订经济和文化合作协定  朝鲜政府代表团离开布拉格去斯洛伐克参观

1956-06-26
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克和朝鲜签订经济和文化合作协定
    朝鲜政府代表团离开布拉格去斯洛伐克参观
    新华社布拉格25日电　以金日成为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团，六月二十三日离开了布拉格。
    代表团在二十一日到达布拉格以后，曾经同捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团举行了会谈。双方在二十三日签订了捷朝经济和文化合作的协定和议定书。
    在二十一日到二十三日期间，朝鲜政府代表团除在布拉格参观和访问以外，还到比尔森参观了机器制造工厂。
    新华社布拉格25日电　以金日成首相为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团目前正在斯洛伐克访问。他们在六月二十四日到达斯洛伐克首府布拉迪斯拉发后，参观了工厂和预装配房屋建筑工地，访问了拖拉机站和农业合作社。
