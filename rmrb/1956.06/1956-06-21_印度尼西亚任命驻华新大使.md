Title: Indonesia Appoints New Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1956-06-21
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 20th: Newspapers report that the Indonesian government has appointed Sukardjo Wirjopranoto as the new Indonesian Ambassador to China. Wirjopranoto is currently the Director-General of the Asian and Pacific Affairs Department of the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

    According to the Indonesian News Agency, Wirjopranoto said on June 18th that he would wait until President Sukarno returns from his recent visits abroad before departing for Beijing. Sukarno is scheduled to return to Indonesia in early July.

### 印度尼西亚任命驻华新大使

1956-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚任命驻华新大使
    新华社雅加达20日电　据报纸报道，印度尼西亚政府已经任命苏卡佐·维约普拉诺托为印度尼西亚驻中国的新大使。维约普拉诺托现任印度尼西亚外交部亚洲太平洋司司长。
    据印度尼西亚新闻社报道，维约普拉诺托六月十八日说，他要等到苏加诺总统结束最近到外国的访问回来以后才会动身去北京。苏加诺预定在七月初回国。
