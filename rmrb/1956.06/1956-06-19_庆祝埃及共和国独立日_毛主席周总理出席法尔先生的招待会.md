Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Attend Mr. Fahr's Reception Celebrating Egyptian Republic Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1956-06-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 18th: Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai attended a reception held today by Egyptian Commercial Representative to China, Mr. Fahr, to celebrate the Independence Day of the Egyptian Republic.

At the reception, Chairman Mao Zedong had a long and cordial conversation with Mr. Fahr and also went around the venue, shaking hands and conversing with foreign envoys to China and their wives.

Mr. Fahr first delivered a speech, saying that on this day of national celebration in Egypt, he was honored by the presence and joint celebration with them in their great country. He asked the Chinese and foreign guests to join him in a toast to Chairman Mao Zedong and to the prosperity of the Chinese people!

When Mr. Fahr raised his glass and walked in front of him, Chairman Mao Zedong clinked glasses with Mr. Fahr and his wife.

Mr. Fahr then proposed a toast to the health of Premier Zhou Enlai, to the health of the diplomatic corps, and to the health of all the guests present.

Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a speech at the reception, saying: Today is the anniversary of the founding of the Egyptian Republic, and also the day the Egyptian people celebrate the final withdrawal of foreign troops from Egyptian territory. Please allow me, on behalf of the Chinese government and people, to extend warm congratulations to the Egyptian government and people.

Premier Zhou Enlai proposed: To the victory of the Egyptian Republic's struggle to safeguard national independence, to the friendship between the Chinese and Egyptian peoples and the continued development of friendly relations between the two countries, to the great unity of the Asian and African peoples, to world peace, to the health of Prime Minister Nasser, and to the prosperity of the Egyptian people, let us raise our glasses!

Premier Zhou Enlai also proposed a toast to the health of the host of today's banquet, Mr. Fahr and his wife.

At the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai had extensive contact and conversations with diplomatic officials from various countries.

Among the more than 600 guests attending this first celebration of the Egyptian holiday in Beijing were Vice-Chairmen Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, Saifudin, Vice Premiers Peng Dehuai, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, and heads of various government departments. They strolled through the halls of the reception, engaging in friendly conversations with the hosts of today's reception and diplomatic officials. (Attached picture)

### 庆祝埃及共和国独立日  毛主席周总理出席法尔先生的招待会

1956-06-19
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝埃及共和国独立日
    毛主席周总理出席法尔先生的招待会
    新华社18日讯　毛泽东主席、周恩来总理今天出席了埃及驻华商务代表法尔举行的庆祝埃及共和国独立日的招待会。
    毛泽东主席在招待会上同法尔先生进行了长时间的亲切的谈话，并且绕行会场同各国驻华使节和他们的夫人握手交谈。
    法尔先生首先致词说，在埃及全国庆祝节日的今天，在你们伟大的国家里，承蒙你们光临和我们共同庆祝这个节日，感到荣幸。他请中外来宾同他一起为毛泽东主席、为中国人民的繁荣干杯！
    毛泽东主席在法尔先生举杯走到他的前面的时候，同法尔先生和他的夫人在一起干杯。
    法尔先生又提议为周恩来总理的健康干杯，为外交使团和到会的全体来宾的健康干杯。
    周恩来总理在招待会上致词说：今天是埃及共和国成立的纪念日，也是埃及人民庆祝外国军队最后从埃及领土撤退的一天，请允许我代表中国政府和人民向埃及政府和人民致热烈的祝贺。
    周恩来总理提议：为埃及共和国维护民族独立斗争的胜利，为中埃两国人民的友谊和两国友好关系的继续发展，为亚非人民大团结，为世界和平，为纳赛尔总理的健康和埃及人民的繁荣干杯。
    周恩来总理还提议为今天宴会的主人——法尔先生和他的夫人的健康干杯。
    在招待会上，周恩来总理同各国的外交官员们广泛地接触交谈。
    参加这个在北京首次举行的庆祝埃及节日招待会的六百多位客人中，有副委员长彭真、李维汉、陈叔通、赛福鼎，副总理彭德怀、陈毅、乌兰夫，最高人民法院院长董必武，外交部副部长张闻天和政府各部的负责人。他们漫步在招待会的大厅里，同今天招待会的主人及外交官员们进行友谊的交谈。（附图片）
