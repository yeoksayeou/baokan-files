Title: Retailing Newspapers and Magazines at Train Stations and on Trains
Author:
Date: 1956-06-08
Page: 2

    Retailing Newspapers and Magazines at Train Stations and on Trains
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th dispatch: Starting July 1st, passengers taking trains will be able to buy retail newspapers and magazines at train stations or on trains.
    The Ministry of Railways and the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of China jointly notified all railway administrations and post (and telecommunications) bureaus on June 5th that they should set up retail newspaper and magazine kiosks or retail cars at major train stations. The post (and telecommunications) bureaus will equip retail staff to sell newspapers and magazines in station squares, waiting rooms, and platforms, and can also sell newspapers and magazines on trains parked at the station. The post (and telecommunications) bureaus can also entrust train attendants to sell newspapers and magazines on trains. The notice stated that this measure will be implemented from July 1st.

### 在火车站和列车内零售报刊

1956-06-08
第2版()
专栏：

    在火车站和列车内零售报刊
    据新华社7日讯　从七月一日起，乘坐火车的旅客将可以在火车站或列车内买到零售报刊。
    中华人民共和国铁道部和邮电部在六月五日联合通知各铁路管理局和邮（电）局，在主要火车站设立零售报刊亭或零售车，由邮（电）局配备零售员在车站广场、候车室、站台上流动零售报刊，并且可以到停在车站上的列车内零售报刊；邮（电）局还可以委托火车列车员在列车内零售报刊。通知中说，这个办法自七月一日起实行。
