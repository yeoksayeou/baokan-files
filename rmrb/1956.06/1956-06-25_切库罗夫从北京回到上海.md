Title: Chekurov Returns to Shanghai from Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-06-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, June 24th: After a two-day visit to Beijing, Vice Admiral Chekurov, Commander of the Soviet Pacific Fleet, and Rear Admiral Pochubailo, Military Commissar, returned to Shanghai by plane this afternoon from Beijing, accompanied by Rear Admiral Peng Deqing, Deputy Commander of the East China Sea Fleet of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

　　Vice Admiral Chekurov and others visited scenic spots in Beijing such as the Temple of Heaven and the Summer Palace, and watched a performance by the Song and Dance Troupe of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 切库罗夫从北京回到上海

1956-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    切库罗夫从北京回到上海
    据新华社上海24日电　苏联太平洋舰队司令切库罗夫海军中将和军事委员波丘巴伊洛海军少将在北京作了两天的参观访问后，由中国人民解放军东海舰队副司令彭德清海军少将陪同，今天下午从北京乘飞机回到上海。
    切库罗夫海军中将等在北京游览了天坛、颐和园等名胜，并且观看了中国人民解放军歌舞团的表演。
