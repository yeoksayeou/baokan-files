Title: Voroshilov Hosts a Banquet for Tito, Tito Visits Bulganin and Khrushchev
Author:
Date: 1956-06-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow Dispatch from TASS: Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, hosted a banquet for President Tito at the Grand Kremlin Palace at 2 p.m. on June 4th.

On this day, President Tito and his wife, along with his entourage, toured the Kremlin. They first paid their respects to the rooms where Lenin lived and worked. President Tito said that he had long wanted to see this historically significant place because he had never been there before.

President Tito and his entourage also paid their respects at the Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum and laid a wreath.

Xinhua News Agency, June 4th Dispatch from TASS, Moscow: On June 4th, the President of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, Tito, visited Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR.

Accompanying Tito were Kardelj, Vice-Chairman of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia; Popović, Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs; Todorović, member of the Federal Executive Council; Blažević, Chairman of the Executive Council of the People's Republic of Croatia; and Mićunović, Yugoslav Ambassador to the Soviet Union.

Present at the meeting were: Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov and Soviet Ambassador to Yugoslavia, Firyubin.

Bulganin and Tito had a cordial conversation.

Xinhua News Agency, June 4th Dispatch from TASS, Moscow: The President of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, Tito, visited Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, at 10:30 a.m. on June 4th. Tito and Khrushchev had a cordial and friendly conversation.
  Tito Visits and Tours Moscow
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 4th: Yugoslav President Tito, along with his wife and entourage, toured Moscow on June 3rd.

    During the day, Tito and his entourage visited the Soviet Agricultural Exhibition and the Soviet Industrial Exhibition. In the evening, Tito and his wife, along with Khrushchev, strolled through some of Moscow's streets, including Gorky Street and Art Theatre Street, viewing the Pushkin and Dolgoruky monuments. They were welcomed by the people of Moscow along the way, with thousands accompanying them on their tour.

    Tito and others also visited some exhibition halls showcasing Soviet science, technology, and the peaceful use of atomic energy. Tito was very interested in the large topographic map reflecting the development plan of the Soviet unified power system.

    Tito visited the Soviet Industrial Exhibition, which is located inside the Soviet Agricultural Exhibition. He saw the latest welding machines in the "Machine Building Exhibition Hall" and said, "Welding machines are what I am most interested in; it is my old profession." A prototype of the "ZIS-111" car aroused Tito's interest. It has seven comfortable seats and can accelerate to a speed of 160 kilometers per hour. Tito said upon leaving the hall, "This is one of the most interesting exhibition halls in the exhibition."

    After visiting the exhibition, a banquet was held for the distinguished guests within the exhibition grounds. The banquet took place in a very warm and friendly atmosphere.

    The Soviet Agricultural Exhibition presented gold medals to the Yugoslav guests as a souvenir of their visit.

    Subsequently, the distinguished guests visited the nearby Botanical Garden of the Soviet Academy of Sciences.

    Accompanying President Tito and others on their visit were: Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Comrades Bulganin, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Saburov, Khrushchev, Brezhnev, and others.

### 伏罗希洛夫设宴招待铁托  铁托拜访布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫

1956-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫设宴招待铁托
    铁托拜访布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫
    新华社据塔斯社莫斯科讯　苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫于六月四日下午二时在大克里姆林宫设宴招待铁托总统。
    这一天，铁托总统和他的夫人以及他的随行人员参观了克里姆林宫。他们首先瞻仰了列宁生前工作和生活的房间。铁托总统说，他早就想看看这个有历史意义的地方，因为他过去从来没有到过这里。
    铁托总统和他的随行人员这一天还拜谒了列宁—斯大林陵墓并且献了花圈。
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：六月四日，南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统铁托拜访了苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁。
    陪同铁托的有南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席卡德尔，外交国务秘书长波波维奇，联邦执行委员会委员托多罗维奇，克罗地亚人民共和国执行委员会主席布拉热维奇和南斯拉夫驻苏联大使米丘诺维奇。
    接见时在座的有：苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫和苏联驻南斯拉夫大使费留宾。
    布尔加宁和铁托作了亲切的谈话。
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统铁托六月四日上午十点三十分拜访苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫。铁托和赫鲁晓夫进行了亲切友好的谈话。
  铁托在莫斯科参观游览
    据新华社莫斯科4日电　南斯拉夫总统铁托同他的夫人和随行人员，六月三日在莫斯科进行参观游览。
    这一天的白天，铁托和他的随行人员参观了苏联农业展览会和苏联工业展览会。晚间，铁托和他的夫人同赫鲁晓夫一道，步行游览了莫斯科一些大街，他们走过了高尔基大街和艺术剧院街等街道，观看了普希金和多尔戈鲁基纪念碑。一路上他们受到了莫斯科人的欢迎，有几千人随同他们游览。
    铁托等还参观了一些介绍苏联科学技术和和平利用原子能的展览馆。铁托对反映苏联统一动力系统发展计划的大地形图很感兴趣。
    铁托参观了苏联工业展览会。这个展览会设在苏联农业展览会里面。他在“机器制造展览馆”中看到了最新式的焊接机器，他说，焊接机器是我最感兴趣的，它是我的老本行。有一种“吉斯—111”式小汽车的试制品引起了铁托的兴趣。它有七个舒适的坐位，能够把速度加快到每小时一百六十公里。铁托在离开这个馆的时候说，这是展览会中最有趣的展览馆之一。
    在参观展览会之后，在展览会场内为贵宾们举行了宴会。宴会是在非常热烈和友好的气氛中进行的。
    苏联农业展览会把金质奖章赠给南斯拉夫客人们，作为参观展览会的纪念。
    随后，贵宾们到邻近的苏联科学院植物园去参观。
    这一天陪同铁托总统等去参观的有：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫，布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫、勃列日涅夫等同志。
