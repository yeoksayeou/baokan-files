Title: CYLC Holds Banquet to Bid Farewell to Soviet and Yugoslav Youth Delegations
Author:
Date: 1956-06-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 19th: The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League held a reception tonight to bid farewell to the Soviet Youth Delegation. More than one hundred young people from the capital happily met with the guests.

　　During the reception, Hu Yaobang, Zhang Ze, Hu Keshi, Liu Xiyuan, and other secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Youth League, raised their glasses to wish the guests a safe journey home.

　　The head of the delegation, A. A. Laposhin, said at the meeting that through this visit, the sincere friendship between the youth of China and the Soviet Union will surely be further consolidated and developed. He asked everyone to toast the unbreakable friendship between the youth of China and the Soviet Union.

　　The reception lasted for more than three hours, and the hosts and guests finally shook hands and said goodbye to each other in the song "Moscow-Beijing".

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 18th: The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League held a reception this evening to bid farewell to the Yugoslav Youth Delegation.

　　Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, and his wife were also invited to attend tonight's reception.

　　Hu Yaobang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Youth League, raised his glass at the meeting to wish the delegation a safe journey home. He hoped that the friendship between the youth of China and Yugoslavia would be further strengthened through this visit.

　　The head of the Yugoslav Youth Delegation, Milijan Neoričić, said that the Yugoslav youth have always had a deep friendship and affection for the Chinese youth. He suggested a toast to the friendship and cooperation between the youth of China and Yugoslavia.

　　Ambassador Popovic also spoke at the meeting.

### 团中央设宴欢送苏、南青年代表团

1956-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    团中央设宴欢送苏、南青年代表团
    据新华社19日讯　中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会今晚举行酒会欢送苏联青年代表团。首都一百多名青年同客人们愉快地会见。
    席间，青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦、章泽、胡克实、刘西元等纷纷举杯，祝客人们在回国途中一路平安。
    代表团团长阿·阿·拉波欣在会上说，通过这次访问，中苏两国青年间的真诚友谊一定会更加巩固和发展。他请大家为中苏两国青年牢不可破的友谊而干杯。
    酒会进行了三个多小时，宾主们最后在“莫斯科——北京”的歌声中互相握手道别。
    据新华社18日讯　中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会今天晚间举行酒会，欢送南斯拉夫青年代表团。
    南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇和他的夫人也应邀出席了今晚的酒会。
    青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦在会上举杯祝代表团在回国途中一路平安。他希望通过这次访问把中南两国青年的友谊进一步加强起来。
    南斯拉夫青年代表团团长米利扬·内奥里契奇说，南斯拉夫青年对中国青年一向有着深厚的友谊和感情，他建议为中南两国青年的友谊和合作而干杯。
    波波维奇大使也在会上讲了话。
