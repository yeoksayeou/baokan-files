Title: Pakistani Journalists Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-06-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. A delegation of Pakistani journalists, invited by the All-China Journalists Association, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a tour of China.

    The delegation consists of heads and reporters from major Pakistani newspapers and news agencies. They are: Mohsin Ali, editor of "The Morning News"; Khalilur Rahman, editor of "The Jang"; Muhammad Umar Faruqi, editor of "The Star"; Akhtar Ali Khan, editor of "The Civil and Military Gazette"; Zahur Hussain Chaudhry, editor of "Nawa-i-Waqt"; Ahmed Naim Qasmi, editor of "Today's News"; Faiz Ahmad Faiz, editor of "The Pakistan Times"; Zafar Niazi, editor of "Critic"; Mukhtar Zaman, reporter for the Associated Press of Pakistan; Qutubuddin Aziz, editor for the United Press of Pakistan; Omer Kureishi, reporter for "Karachi Daily Mail" (already arrived in Beijing); Irshad Ahmed, editor of "The River News"; Razaque Ali, deputy editor of "Pakistan Observer"; Shahadat Hussain, deputy editor of "Azad"; and Fazli Mahmood, special representative of the chairman of "Civil and Military Gazette".

    Welcoming them at the airport were Deng Tuo, Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, Vice-Chairmen Wu Lengxi, Mei Yi, Wang Yunsheng, heads of major newspapers in the capital, and Farooqi, Second Secretary of the Pakistani Embassy in China, and more than twenty others.

    Tonight, Deng Tuo, Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, hosted a welcome banquet for the Pakistani journalists. Deng Tuo and Mukhtar Zaman, reporter for the Associated Press of Pakistan, delivered speeches at the banquet, expressing their desire to further strengthen the friendly relations between the peoples of China and Pakistan.

### 巴基斯坦新闻工作者访华代表团到达北京

1956-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦新闻工作者访华代表团到达北京
    新华社2日讯　巴基斯坦新闻工作者访华代表团应中国新闻工作者联谊会的邀请，在今天下午乘飞机到达北京，他们将在中国各地进行参观访问。
    这个代表团由巴基斯坦各主要报刊和通讯社的负责人和记者组成。他们是：“晨报”主编莫辛·阿里，“战斗报”主编哈利卢尔·拉赫曼，“繁星报”主编穆罕默德·乌马尔·法鲁基，“地主报”主编阿赫塔尔·阿里汗，“新闻报”主编扎胡尔·侯赛尼·乔德里，“今天报”主编艾哈迈德·纳伊姆·卡斯米，“巴基斯坦时报”主编费兹·艾哈迈德·费兹，“批评”月刊主编扎法尔·尼阿齐，巴基斯坦联合通讯社记者穆赫塔尔·扎曼，巴基斯坦统一通讯社编辑库图布丁·阿齐兹，“喀喇蚩时报”记者欧默尔·库雷希（已先到北京），“天河报”编辑伊尔沙德·艾哈迈德，“巴基斯坦观察家报”副主编拉查克·阿里，“自由报”副主编沙哈达特·侯赛尼，“军民报”主席特派员法兹利·马茂德。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国新闻工作者联谊会会长邓拓，副会长吴冷西、梅益、王芸生，首都各主要报刊的负责人，以及巴基斯坦驻华大使馆二等秘书法鲁其等二十多人。
    今天晚间，中国新闻工作者联谊会会长邓拓为巴基斯坦的新闻同业举行了欢迎宴会。邓拓和巴基斯坦联合通讯社记者穆赫塔尔·扎曼先后在宴会上致词，表示要进一步加强中巴两国人民之间的友好关系。
