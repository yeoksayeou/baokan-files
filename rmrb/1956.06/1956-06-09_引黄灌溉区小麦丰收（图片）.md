Title: Wheat Harvest in the Yellow River Irrigation Area (Photo)
Author: Xu Zhenyan
Date: 1956-06-09
Page: 2

    Wheat Harvest in the Yellow River Irrigation Area
    The 540,000 mu of wheat in the Yellow River Irrigation Area of Henan Province has begun to be harvested. This year is the fourth year of good harvest since the Yellow River Irrigation Area began diverting water, with an output increase of about 25% compared to last year. This is a harvester from an agricultural machinery station helping the Xiaoji Agricultural Cooperative harvest wheat.
                                   Xinhua News Agency reporter Xu Zhenyan photo

### 引黄灌溉区小麦丰收（图片）
徐振炎
1956-06-09
第2版()
专栏：

    引黄灌溉区小麦丰收
    河南省引黄灌溉区五十四万亩小麦已经开始收割。今年是引黄灌溉区放水后的第四个丰收年，产量约比去年增加两成半。这是农业机器站的收割机正在帮助小冀农业合作社收割小麦。　
                                   新华社记者　徐振炎摄
