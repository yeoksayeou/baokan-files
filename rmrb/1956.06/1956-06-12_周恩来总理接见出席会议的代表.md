Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Delegates Attending the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-06-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. Premier Zhou Enlai received the heads of trade unions from various provinces and cities, and various industrial trade unions who were attending the National Trade Union Labor Insurance Living Housing Work Conference convened by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, as well as representatives from the People's Committees of various provinces and cities and the Communist Party Committees of various provinces and cities who were invited to participate in the conference, and held a long discussion.

Premier Zhou Enlai inquired in detail about issues related to workers' labor insurance, medical care, housing conditions, and other aspects of workers' lives. He was very concerned about the various difficulties in the current lives of workers, strictly criticized the phenomenon of some leading personnel not caring about the lives of workers, and gave important principled instructions on labor insurance and solving problems such as workers' housing and medical care. He asked the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to work with relevant departments to formulate specific plans to solve the current difficulties in workers' lives.

### 周恩来总理接见出席会议的代表

1956-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见出席会议的代表
    新华社11日讯　周恩来总理在今天下午四时接见了出席中华全国总工会所召开的全国工会劳动保险生活住宅工作会议的各省市工会、各产业工会的负责人，以及应邀参加这次会议的各省、市人民委员会和中共各省、市委员会的代表，并且举行了长时间的座谈。
    周恩来总理详细询问了职工劳动保险、疾病医疗、住宅状况以及其他有关职工生活方面的问题。他对当前职工生活中的各种困难问题十分关切，对于某些领导人员不关心职工生活的现象进行了严格的批评，对于劳动保险和解决职工住宅、疾病医疗等问题作了重要的原则指示。他要求中华全国总工会会同有关部门拟定解决当前职工生活困难问题的具体方案。
