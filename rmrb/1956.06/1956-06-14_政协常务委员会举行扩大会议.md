Title: CPPCC Standing Committee Holds Enlarged Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-06-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th — The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People’s Political Consultative Conference held its 24th meeting (enlarged) this morning.

After hearing a statement from Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture of the People's Republic of China, the meeting held a discussion on the "Draft Model Charter for Agricultural Production Cooperatives" for use by advanced cooperatives. Members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress in Beijing attended the meeting as observers. In the afternoon, the members divided into four groups to hold group discussions on this charter (draft).

### 政协常务委员会举行扩大会议

1956-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

    政协常务委员会举行扩大会议
    新华社11日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会今天上午举行第二十四次会议（扩大）。
    会议在听了中华人民共和国农业部部长廖鲁言说明后，就供高级合作社采用的“农业生产合作社示范章程（草案）”进行了座谈。在京的全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员列席了这次会议。下午，委员们分成四个组对这个章程（草案）进行分组座谈。
