Title: Moroccan Government Does Not Recognize American Privileges; US Air Force to Withdraw from Rabat This Summer
Author:
Date: 1956-06-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 19th, Washington Dispatch: United States Air Force authorities announced that the headquarters of the U.S. Seventeenth Air Force, stationed in Rabat, the capital of Morocco, is scheduled to withdraw from Rabat this summer and relocate to Tripoli, Libya.

The Air Force authorities announced this decision on June 15th. Sources say the U.S. government hopes to negotiate with the Moroccan government, through its embassy in Rabat, regarding the continued use of air bases in Morocco by the United States.

The United States has four large air bases in Morocco. It is one of the main strategic air bases for the United States across the Atlantic, besides Britain and Spain. The newly independent Moroccan government does not recognize the right of the United States to use these bases.

Moroccan Foreign Minister Balafrej has publicly accused the French government of secretly granting this right to the United States in the Franco-American agreement behind the backs of the Moroccan people. Balafrej said: The Moroccan government is unaware of the contents of the Franco-American agreement; the Moroccan government will independently decide its attitude towards this agreement.

### 摩洛哥政府不承认美国特权  美国空军将在今夏撤出拉巴特

1956-06-20
第4版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥政府不承认美国特权
    美国空军将在今夏撤出拉巴特
    新华社19日讯　华盛顿消息：美国空军负责当局宣布，驻扎在摩洛哥首都拉巴特的美国第十七航空队总部定在今年夏天撤出拉巴特，迁移到利比亚的的黎波里。
    空军当局是在六月十五日宣布这个决定的。有消息说，美国政府希望通过它驻拉巴特的大使馆，同摩洛哥政府谈判美国继续使用在摩洛哥的空军基地问题。
    美国在摩洛哥有四个巨大的空军基地。它是美国在大西洋彼岸除了英国、西班牙以外的主要战略空军基地之一。新近独立的摩洛哥的政府不承认美国有使用这些基地的权利。
    摩洛哥外交大臣贝拉弗里杰曾经公开指责法国政府背着摩洛哥人偷偷在法美协定中给予美国这个权利。贝拉弗里杰说：摩洛哥政府不知道法美协定内容；摩洛哥政府将自行决定对这个协定的态度。
