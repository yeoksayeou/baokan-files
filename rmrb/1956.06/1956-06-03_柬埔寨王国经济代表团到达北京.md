Title: Cambodian Royal Economic Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-03
Page: 1

    Cambodian Royal Economic Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd -- The three-member economic delegation from the Kingdom of Cambodia, led by the head of the delegation, State Secretary of the Planning Department of the Prime Minister's Office, Pho Proeung, was warmly welcomed upon their arrival here this afternoon by Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jiuzhuang, Vice Minister Lei Renmin, and Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Chen Shuliang, and others.
    This delegation is here to discuss matters concerning China's economic assistance to the Kingdom of Cambodia.
    After arriving in Beijing, the guests paid a visit to Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jiuzhuang. In the evening, Ye Jiuzhuang held a banquet to welcome all members of the delegation.

### 柬埔寨王国经济代表团到达北京

1956-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨王国经济代表团到达北京
    新华社2日讯　柬埔寨王国经济代表团一行三人，在今天下午到达这里的时候，受到了对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长雷任民和外交部亚洲司副司长陈叔亮等人的热情欢迎。
    这个代表团由团长、首相府计划部国务员蒲烈芳率领，他们是前来中国商谈有关中国给予柬埔寨王国经济援助的问题。
    客人们在到达北京以后，拜会了对外贸易部部长叶季壮。晚间，叶季壮举行了宴会，欢迎代表团全体人员。
