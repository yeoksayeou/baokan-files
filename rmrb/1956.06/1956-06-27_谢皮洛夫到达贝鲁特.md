Title: Shepilov Arrives in Beirut
Author:
Date: 1956-06-27
Page: 4
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, June 26th (TASS) - Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov and his entourage arrived in Beirut at 6:00 PM on June 25th.

    At the airport, Shepilov made a statement to reporters saying that the Soviet people have always fully understood and sympathized with the just struggle of the Lebanese people for freedom and independence. The Soviet Union highly appreciates the achievements of the Lebanese people in consolidating peace and in the common struggle of the Arab East countries against the aggressive Baghdad Pact, which brings new colonial enslavement to the peoples of all countries.

    Shepilov said that we believe that developing friendly relations with Lebanon is of great significance, and we are very pleased to have the opportunity to establish direct contact with Lebanese leaders.

### 谢皮洛夫到达贝鲁特

1956-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    谢皮洛夫到达贝鲁特
    新华社据塔斯社贝鲁特26日讯　苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫和随行人员于六月二十五日下午六时到达贝鲁特。
    谢皮洛夫在机场上向新闻记者发表谈话说，苏联人民一向充分了解和同情黎巴嫩人民争取自由和独立的正义斗争。苏联对黎巴嫩人民在巩固和平方面和在阿拉伯东方各国反对给各国人民带来新的殖民奴役的侵略性的巴格达条约的共同斗争方面的功绩有很高的评价。
    谢皮洛夫说，我们认为发展同黎巴嫩的友好关系是有很大意义的，我们很高兴有可能同黎巴嫩领导人建立直接的接触。
