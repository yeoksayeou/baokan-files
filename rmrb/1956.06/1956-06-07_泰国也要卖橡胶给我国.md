Title: Thailand Also to Sell Rubber to China
Author:
Date: 1956-06-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, 6th: Thai Minister of Finance and Acting Minister of Economic Affairs, Phra Prakas Sahakorn, said on June 5th that if Malaya sells rubber directly to China, Thailand will do the same.

He added that due to reduced demand from the United States, Thai rubber prices are falling. The average monthly export volume has decreased by approximately 3,000 tons.

### 泰国也要卖橡胶给我国

1956-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    泰国也要卖橡胶给我国
    新华社6日讯　曼谷消息：泰国财政部长兼经济部代理部长拍母里攀·裕他吉在六月五日说，如果马来亚直接卖橡胶给中国，泰国也将这样做。
    他又说，由于美国需要量减少，泰国橡胶价格正在下跌。每月平均出口量已经减少了大约三千吨。
