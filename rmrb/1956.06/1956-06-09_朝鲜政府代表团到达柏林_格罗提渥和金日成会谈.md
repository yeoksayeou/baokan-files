Title: Korean Government Delegation Arrives in Berlin, Grotewohl and Kim Il-sung Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-06-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 8th - According to the German News Agency, German Democratic Republic Premier Grotewohl received the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea headed by Cabinet Premier Kim Il-sung on the 7th.

Present at the reception on the German side were Deputy Premiers Bolz, Rau, and Nuschke, among others.

During the sincere meeting, issues related to the relations between the two countries, as well as the international situation and the situations in Germany and Korea, were discussed.

On the same day, Nam Il, Minister of Foreign Affairs of Korea and member of the Korean government delegation, met with German Democratic Republic Foreign Minister Bolz.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 8th - The Korean government delegation headed by Premier Kim Il-sung arrived in Berlin yesterday amidst the welcoming cheers of Berlin citizens waving flowers and flags.

After disembarking from the plane, Kim Il-sung shook hands with representatives of the East German government and other personnel who welcomed the delegation at the airport. He then, accompanied by Premier Grotewohl, reviewed the guard of honor.

In a brief speech at the airport, Kim Il-sung expressed gratitude to the German people for their assistance in the Korean people's struggle for national unification and independence and the construction of a socialist foundation. He stated that the Korean government and people would forever support the German people's just struggle for unification. He expressed his belief that this visit would further strengthen the friendship and economic and cultural cooperation between Korea and Germany, and would contribute to maintaining peace in Asia and Europe. ### 朝鲜政府代表团到达柏林  格罗提渥和金日成会谈

1956-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府代表团到达柏林
    格罗提渥和金日成会谈
    新华社柏林8日电　据德意志通讯社报道，德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥七日接见了以内阁首相金日成为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团。
    接见的时候德国方面在座的有副总理博尔茨、劳、努舍克等。
    在诚挚气氛的会晤中，讨论了两国关系问题，还讨论了国际局势以及德国和朝鲜的局势问题。
    同一天，朝鲜政府代表团团员、朝鲜外务相南日访晤了德意志民主共和国外交部长博尔茨。
    据新华社柏林8日电　以金日成首相为首的朝鲜政府代表团昨天在拿着鲜花和旗帜的柏林市民的欢迎声中到达柏林。
    金日成在下飞机后同在机场上欢迎代表团的民主德国政府代表和其他人员一一握手，随后在格罗提渥总理陪同下，检阅了仪仗队。
    金日成在机场上发表的简短的讲话中对德国人民在朝鲜人民争取国家统一和独立以及建设社会主义基础的斗争中所给予的援助表示感谢，并且表示朝鲜政府和人民对于德国人民要求统一的正义斗争将永远给以支持。他表示相信这次访问将进一步加强朝德两国之间的友谊和经济文化方面的合作，并且将有助于维护亚洲和欧洲的和平。（附图片）
