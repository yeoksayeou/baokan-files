Title: International Commission in Vietnam Decides to Investigate Concentration Camps in South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1956-06-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi Dispatch: According to Vietnam News Agency, the International Commission in Vietnam has decided to dispatch a mobile team to investigate concentration camps in areas such as Dai Loc in Thua Thien Province in South Vietnam, where former resistance personnel and their families are being detained by the South Vietnamese authorities.

The commission announced this decision in a letter to the liaison delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army on May 28. The International Commission also requested the French liaison delegation to comment on the incident of 4,000 members of the Hoa Hao sect being detained in concentration camps, and the forced demolition of houses and relocation of 100 families from Tuo Lu to concentration camps in Xuan Nam and Na Lin in Quang Tri Province.

### 越南国际委员会决定调查南越的集中营

1956-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    越南国际委员会决定调查南越的集中营
    新华社河内电　据越南通讯社讯：越南国际委员会已经决定派遣一个机动小组去调查越南南方当局在承天省的大禄等地拘禁前抗战人员和他们的家属的集中营。
    这个委员会在五月二十八日给越南人民军总司令部联络代表团的信中宣布了这个决定。国际委员会还要求法方联络代表团对和好教的四千个成员被拘禁在集中营和拓路的一百家人家被迫拆掉他们的房屋迁移到广治省的春南和纳林的集中营的事件表示意见。
