Title: Central Committee Leaders of the Chinese Communist Party Receive a Group of Scientists
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-15
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 14th: Chairman Mao Zedong and members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Kang Sheng, Lin Boqu, and Deng Xiaoping, received the scientists participating in the formulation of the National Long-Term Science Plan this afternoon.

### 中共中央领导人接见一批科学家

1956-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见一批科学家
    新华社14日讯　毛泽东主席和中共中央政治局委员周恩来、朱德、陈云、康生、林伯渠、邓小平，在今天下午接见了参加拟制全国长期科学规划工作的科学家们。
