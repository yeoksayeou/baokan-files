Title: China Sports Federation Hosts Banquet to Welcome French Labor Federation Men's and Women's Basketball Teams
Author:
Date: 1956-06-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 4th — The Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, Rong Gaotang, hosted a banquet in Beijing on the evening of the 4th to welcome all members of the French Labor Sports Federation's men's and women's basketball teams.
    Rong Gaotang delivered a speech at the banquet, warmly welcoming the French Labor Sports Federation's men's and women's basketball teams, headed by the President of the French Labor Sports Federation, Rousseau. He said: I hope you will feel at home during your time in China. Through friendly matches, we will learn your excellent basketball skills.
    Rousseau also spoke at the banquet. He stated that although their time in China has been short, they have deeply felt the warm welcome of the Chinese people. He thanked the All-China Sports Federation for their hospitality.
    The second group of twelve members of the French Labor Sports Federation's men's and women's basketball teams arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 4th.

### 我国体育总会设宴欢迎法国劳联男女篮球队

1956-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我国体育总会设宴欢迎法国劳联男女篮球队
    据新华社4日讯　中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠四日晚上在北京设宴欢迎法国劳动体育联合会男女篮球队全体人员。
    荣高棠在宴会上首先致词，他热烈欢迎以法国劳动体育联合会主席卢梭为首的法国劳联男女篮球队。他说：希望你们在中国期间要像在家里一样，通过友谊比赛，我们将学习你们优秀的篮球技术。
    卢梭在宴会上也讲了话。他表示来到中国的时间虽然很短，但是已经深深地感觉到中国人民对他们的热情欢迎。他感谢中华全国体育总会对他们的接待。
    法国劳联男女篮球队第二批人员十二人，已经在四日下午坐飞机到达北京。
