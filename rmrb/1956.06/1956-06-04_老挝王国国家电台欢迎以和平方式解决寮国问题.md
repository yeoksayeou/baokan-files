Title: The National Radio Station of the Kingdom of Laos Welcomes a Peaceful Resolution to the Laotian Question
Author:
Date: 1956-06-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 3rd - The National Radio Station of the Kingdom of Laos, Vientiane Radio, published a commentary on May 27, expressing its welcome to holding consultative meetings with the Laotian fighting forces to resolve the Laotian question peacefully.

Vientiane Radio said that the Laotian people want to live a peaceful and happy life and do not want their motherland to be destroyed in an atomic war. Therefore, they hope that the Laotian people can cooperate with each other, and that both the Royal Government and the Laotian fighting forces can make concessions to each other so that future consultations can achieve good results.

### 老挝王国国家电台欢迎以和平方式解决寮国问题

1956-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝王国国家电台欢迎以和平方式解决寮国问题
    新华社河内3日电　老挝王国的国家电台万象电台在五月二十七日发表评论，表示欢迎和寮国战斗部队举行协商会议，以和平方式解决寮国问题。
    万象电台说，老挝人民想过和平和幸福的生活，不愿意自己的祖国在原子战争中被摧毁。因此，他们希望老挝人民能互相合作，王国政府和寮国战斗部队双方能够互相让步，使将来的协商能够得到良好的结果。
