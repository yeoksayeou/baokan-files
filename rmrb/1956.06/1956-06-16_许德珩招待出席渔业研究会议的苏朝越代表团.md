Title: Xu Deheng Receives Soviet, Korean and Vietnamese Delegations Attending Fisheries Research Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-06-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, June 15th. Xu Deheng, Minister of Aquatic Products of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet tonight to receive the government delegations from the Soviet Union, North Korea, and Vietnam, who are attending the Sino-Soviet-Korean-Vietnamese Fisheries, Oceanography, and Limnology Research Conference.

Present at the banquet were representatives and experts from the Chinese government delegation, as well as responsible persons from the Ministry of Aquatic Products, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and the Academy of Sciences.

### 许德珩招待出席渔业研究会议的苏朝越代表团

1956-06-16
第3版()
专栏：

    许德珩招待出席渔业研究会议的苏朝越代表团
    新华社15日讯　中华人民共和国水产部长许德珩，今晚设宴招待出席中、苏、朝、越四国渔业、海洋学和湖沼学研究会议的苏联、朝鲜、越南三国政府代表团。
    出席作陪的有中国政府代表团的代表和专家，以及水产部、外交部、科学院的负责人。
