Title: Construction Preparation for Baotou Iron and Steel Complex (Photo)
Author: Lù Kē
Date: 1956-06-18
Page: 2

Baotou Iron and Steel Complex Construction Preparation

    The Baotou Iron and Steel Complex, a new construction project in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region during the First Five-Year Plan, is stepping up its preparations for plant construction. The concrete prefabrication plant serving the construction of factory buildings has been put into production and has begun manufacturing large concrete roof panels. This is the construction site of the concrete prefabrication plant.

                               Xinhua News Agency Reporter Lu Ke

### 包头钢铁联合企业的施工准备（图片）
陆轲

专栏：

    包头钢铁联合企业的施工准备 
    第一个五年计划期间在内蒙古自治区新建的包头钢铁联合企业，正在加紧进行建厂准备工作。为修筑厂房服务的混凝土预制品厂已经投入生产，开始制造混凝土大型屋面板。这是混凝土预制品厂工地。
                               新华社记者      陆轲摄
