Title: Six Students Returning from the U.S.

Author:

Date: 1956-06-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, June 19th: Six students who had been studying in the United States returned to Guangzhou on the 17th. They are: Lin Zuo Di, who obtained a doctorate from the University of Minnesota in the United States, and his wife, Xu Shui Yue, who obtained a master's degree; Niu Jingyi, a doctor of biochemistry who studied at the University of Texas and the University of California; Hou Yujun, a doctor of chemical engineering from the University of Michigan; Xie Hanjun, who studied at the Graduate School of the University of Washington; and Yin Zhiqilin, who studied at the Graduate School of the University of Tennessee.

### 留美学生六人回国

1956-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    留美学生六人回国
    据新华社广州19日电　留美学生六人在十七日回到广州。他们是：曾在美国明尼苏达大学获得博士学位的林作砥和获得硕士学位的他的夫人徐水月，曾在得克萨斯大学和加利福尼亚大学研究的生物化学博士钮经义，密执安大学化学工程博士侯虞钧，曾在华盛顿大学研究院学习的谢汉俊和曾在田纳西大学研究院学习的尹智麒。
