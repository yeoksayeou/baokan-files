Title: Chinese Ambassador to India Holds Reception for Chinese Football Team
Author:
Date: 1956-06-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 16th. Chinese Ambassador to India, Pan Zili, held a reception on the evening of June 15th to welcome the Chinese football team.

Among the 250 guests attending the reception were Deputy Minister of Education, Shrimali, and other Indian government officials.

Members of the diplomatic corps stationed in Delhi also attended the reception.

The Vice-Chairman of the Delhi Football Association, Dutta, and members of the Indian national football team, who will play a friendly exhibition match with the Chinese football team, had friendly conversations with the Chinese football team members at the reception.

### 我国驻印大使  为我足球队举行招待会

1956-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    我国驻印大使
    为我足球队举行招待会
    新华社新德里16日电　中国驻印度大使潘自力六月十五日晚上举行招待会欢迎中国足球队。
    出席招待会的二百五十位来宾中有教育部副部长什里马利和印度政府其他官员。
    驻在德里的外交团人员也出席了招待会。
    德里足球协会副主席杜塔和将要同中国足球队进行友好表演赛的印度国家足球队队员在招待会上同中国足球队队员进行了友好交谈。
