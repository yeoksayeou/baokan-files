Title: Secretary-General of the Egyptian Islamic Congress Sends Congratulations on the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-06-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Secretary-General of the China Islamic Association, Muhammad Ali Zhang Yuzhen, received a telegram today from Anwar Sadat, Secretary-General of the Egyptian Islamic Congress. The telegram stated: "On the occasion of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Egypt, I express our mutual greetings and heartfelt congratulations on behalf of all Muslims in Egypt. The establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Egypt will enable our relations to develop and prosper." Muhammad Ali Zhang Yuzhen sent a telegram to Anwar Sadat on May 25 to congratulate him on the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Egypt. Today's telegram is Anwar Sadat's reply to him.

### 埃及伊斯兰教大会秘书长来电祝贺中埃建交

1956-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

    埃及伊斯兰教大会秘书长来电祝贺中埃建交
    新华社4日讯　中国伊斯兰教协会秘书长穆罕默德·阿里·张玉珍今天收到埃及伊斯兰教大会秘书长安瓦尔·萨达特给他的一份电报。电报说：“值中埃两国建立外交关系之际，我代表埃及全体穆斯林表示我们的相互致意和衷心祝贺。中埃两国之间的建立外交关系将使我们的关系得以发展和繁荣。”穆罕默德·阿里·张玉珍曾经在五月二十五日打电报给安瓦尔·萨达特祝贺中埃建交。今天的电报是安瓦尔·萨达特给他的复电。
