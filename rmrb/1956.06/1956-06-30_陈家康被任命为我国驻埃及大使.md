Title: Chen Jiakang Appointed as China's Ambassador to Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-06-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 29th: Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has appointed Chen Jiakang as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Egypt.

### 陈家康被任命为我国驻埃及大使

1956-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    陈家康被任命为我国驻埃及大使
    新华社29日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命陈家康为中华人民共和国驻埃及共和国特命全权大使。
