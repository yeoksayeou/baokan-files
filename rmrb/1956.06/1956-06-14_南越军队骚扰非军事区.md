Title: South Vietnamese Troops Harass Demilitarized Zone
Author:
Date: 1956-06-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 13th - Vietnam News Agency reported: More than 70 South Vietnamese troops, disguised as civilians, illegally invaded the southern part of the demilitarized zone on June 3rd. They attacked four villages south of the temporary military demarcation line. According to incomplete statistics, they abducted twenty-three civilians from there.
    These South Vietnamese troops are continuing to harass the demilitarized zone.

### 南越军队骚扰非军事区

1956-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

    南越军队骚扰非军事区
    新华社河内13日电　越南通讯社讯：南越军队七十多人假装成平民在六月三日非法侵入非军事区的南部。他们袭击了临时军事分界线南面的四个村子。据不完全的统计，他们从那里架走了二十三个平民。
    这些南越军队还在继续对非军事区进行骚扰。
