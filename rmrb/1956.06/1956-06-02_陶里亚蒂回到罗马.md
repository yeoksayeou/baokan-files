Title: Togliatti Returns to Rome
Author:
Date: 1956-06-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, June 1st. TASS reported from Rome on the 1st: After visiting Yugoslavia, Italian Communist Party General Secretary Togliatti returned to Rome on May 31st.
    In a conversation with a reporter from the Ciampino news agency, Togliatti said that his talks with the leaders of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia were completely successful, thus opening a new period of mutual relations and cooperation between the two parties.

### 陶里亚蒂回到罗马

1956-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    陶里亚蒂回到罗马
    据新华社1日讯　塔斯社罗马一日讯：意大利共产党总书记陶里亚蒂在访问南斯拉夫后，五月三十一日回到罗马。
    陶里亚蒂在向查姆比诺通讯社记者发表谈话的时候说，他同南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟领导人举行的会谈取得了完全的成功，从而打开了两党相互关系和合作的新时期。
