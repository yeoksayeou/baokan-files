Title: U.S. Embassy's Interference in Ceylon's Internal Affairs Arouses Dissatisfaction Among Many Ceylonese

Author:
Date: 1956-06-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 15th: According to an Associated Press report from Colombo on June 14th, many Ceylonese are dissatisfied with the U.S. Embassy in Ceylon's interference in Ceylon's internal affairs. The car of the U.S. Ambassador Crowe was once surrounded by Ceylonese.

The news indicates that the reason for the Ceylonese dissatisfaction is that the U.S. Embassy engaged in incitement during the recent disputes in Ceylon regarding the Official Language Act.

### 美国使馆干涉锡兰内政  引起许多锡兰人的不满

1956-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    美国使馆干涉锡兰内政  
    引起许多锡兰人的不满
    新华社15日讯　据美联社六月十四日自科伦坡报道，许多锡兰人对于美国驻锡兰大使馆干涉锡兰内政表示不满。美国大使克劳的汽车曾经被锡兰人包围起来。
    消息表明，引起锡兰人不满的原因是：在锡兰最近发生的关于国语法案问题的纠纷中，美国大使馆曾经进行了煽动。
