Title: Nasser Announces in Cairo's Republic Square that Egypt is Willing to Cooperate with the Soviet Union and Other Countries to Fully Support the Independence Movements of Arab Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-06-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 20th: Cairo News: Egyptian Prime Minister Gamal Abdel Nasser, in a passionate speech yesterday, told over a hundred thousand Egyptians, "Egypt belongs to all of us, and now no flag other than the Egyptian flag flies on Egyptian soil."

The Egyptian Prime Minister addressed the crowds gathered in Cairo's Republic Square to celebrate the end of the British army's seventy-four-year occupation of the Suez Canal Zone. The square was decorated with red, white, and black revolutionary flags and the green Egyptian flag with a white crescent and three stars. His words were repeatedly interrupted by massive cheers from the crowd. He said that Britain had fulfilled its obligations under the troop withdrawal agreement. We do not dwell on the past, and we have no aggressive intentions towards it. But we will be hostile to those who are hostile to us, and peaceful to those who are peaceful to us.

Abdel Nasser emphasized, "Today is different from yesterday. The language of the past no longer affects us, because imperialism cannot stay here." He also said, "Russia has extended its hand to us. We will cooperate with it. We will also cooperate with Britain and the United States, but not by sacrificing our independence and dignity to do so."

He declared, "We will defend ourselves against foreign aggression by all means. Our policy is clear.
	"We will cooperate with anyone or any country that is willing to cooperate with us, in economic and all aspects to build our country."

Abdel Nasser emphasized Egypt's support for the national independence movements of Arab countries, saying, "What we need is for all Arab countries to gain independence. Today, representatives of independent Morocco, Crown Prince Moulay Hassan, and a representative of independent Tunisia are with us for the first time. We hope that next time we are together, we will see a representative from an independent Algeria."

He emphasized, "Our foreign policy originates from Egypt, not any foreign country."

The Prime Minister announced the end of the four-year state of martial law in Egypt. He also said, "At the same time, due to the end of martial law, there will be freedom of the press."

### 纳赛尔在开罗共和国广场宣布  埃及愿同苏联和其他国家合作  全力支持阿拉伯国家的独立运动

1956-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔在开罗共和国广场宣布
    埃及愿同苏联和其他国家合作
    全力支持阿拉伯国家的独立运动
    新华社20日讯　开罗消息：埃及总理阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔昨天在一篇充满热情的演说中对上十万埃及人说，“埃及是我们大家的，现在除了埃及的国旗以外再没有别的国家的国旗在埃及的领土上飘扬了。”
    埃及总理对聚集在开罗的共和国广场上的群众发表演说，庆祝英国军队在苏彝士运河区的七十四年的占领的结束。广场上装饰着红、白、黑革命旗帜及有一个白色新月和三颗星星的绿色的埃及国旗。他的话不时被群众巨大的欢呼声所打断。他说，英国已经完成了它根据撤军协定所负担的义务。我们不咎既往，我们对它没有侵略意图。但是我们将同那些同我们敌对的人敌对，对那些对我们和平的人和平。
    阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔强调说：“今天不同于昨天。属于过去的语言不再影响我们了，因为帝国主义不能在这里呆了。”他又说：“俄国向我们伸出手来。我们将同它合作。我们也将同英国和美国进行合作，但是不是牺牲我们的独立和尊严来这样做。”
    他声明说，“我们将用一切办法来保卫我们自己免遭外国侵略。我们的政策是明确的。
      “我们将同愿意同我们合作的任何人或任何国家进行合作，在经济方面和各个方面建设我们的国家。”
    阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔强调埃及对阿拉伯国家民族独立运动的支持，他说，“我们所需要的是所有阿拉伯国家都得到独立。今天独立的摩洛哥的代表穆莱·哈萨尼王太子和独立的突尼斯的一位代表第一次同我们在一起。我们希望下一次我们在一起看到来自一个独立的阿尔及利亚的代表。”
    他强调说，“我们的外交政策出自埃及而不是任何外国。”
    这位总理宣布结束在埃及实行了四年的戒严令。他还说，“同时由于戒严令的结束，将有新闻自由。”
