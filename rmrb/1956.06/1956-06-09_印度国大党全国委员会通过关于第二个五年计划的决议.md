Title: Indian National Congress National Committee Passes Resolution on Second Five-Year Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-06-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 3rd: The Indian National Congress National Committee meeting held in Mumbai concluded on June 3rd. In addition to passing resolutions on the international situation, the meeting also passed a resolution on the Second Five-Year Plan.
    The resolution on the Second Five-Year Plan states: "A socialist pattern of society must be built on the basis of equal opportunities for everyone and a significant reduction in economic and social inequalities." The resolution calls for vigilance against the trend of wealth concentration in rapid economic development and calls for steps to reduce wealth concentration and higher incomes, while raising lower incomes.
    The resolution agrees to significantly develop the heavy industry and power industry of the public sector. It also believes that the private sector has great opportunities for development within the scope of national goals.
    The resolution states that the agricultural production targets set in the plan are achievable and should be substantially increased, and it recommends that agricultural cooperative organizations increase agricultural production.

### 印度国大党全国委员会通过关于第二个五年计划的决议

1956-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

    印度国大党全国委员会通过关于第二个五年计划的决议
    据新华社新德里讯　在孟买举行的印度国大党全国委员会会议已经在六月三日结束。这次会议除了通过关于国际局势等项决议外，还通过了一项关于第二个五年计划的决议。
    关于第二个五年计划的决议说：“社会主义式样的社会必须建立在每个人的机会均等和大大减少经济和社会的不平等现象的基础上。”这项决议要求对在经济的迅速发展中财富集中的趋势保持警惕，并且要求采取步骤来减少财富的集中和较高的收入，同时提高较低级的收入。
    这项决议同意大大地发展公营部分的重工业和动力工业。它还认为私营部分在国家的目标范围内有着发展的巨大机会。
    决议说，计划中所规定的农业生产的目标是能够而且应该予以相当提高的，它建议农业合作社组织增加农业产量。
