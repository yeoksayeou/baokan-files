Title: Repairing Old Machines (Picture)
Author: Yang Putao
Date: 1956-06-08
Page: 2

    Repairing Old Machines
    Many joint state-private paper mills in Shanghai are repairing idle old machinery and equipment. The old machines that have been repaired can now produce approximately 12,000 tons of paper annually. The picture shows the newly repaired Fourdrinier paper machine in production at the joint state-private Jiangnan Paper Mill.
                                       Xinhua News Agency reporter Yang Putao photo

### 修复旧机器（图片）
杨溥涛
1956-06-08
第2版()
专栏：

    修复旧机器
    上海不少公私合营造纸厂，正在修复搁置的旧机器设备。目前已经修好的旧机器，全年可以造纸一万二千吨左右。这是公私合营江南造纸厂新修好的平纲抄纸机在生产。
                                       新华社记者　杨溥涛摄
