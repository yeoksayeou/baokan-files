Title: Opening of the Soviet Exhibition of Science and Technology for Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy; Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Holds Celebration Banquet
Author:
Date: 1956-06-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 15th: The Soviet Exhibition of Science and Technology for Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy officially opened in Beijing this morning.

The opening ceremony was held in the cinema of the Soviet Exhibition Center. At 9:15 AM, after the national anthems of the Soviet Union and China were played, the Soviet Ambassador to China, Yudin, delivered a speech. After describing the achievements of the Soviet Union in the peaceful use of atomic energy, he said, "In this regard, we not only unreservedly share our discoveries, but also are prepared to help other countries master these achievements in the peaceful use of atomic energy. The Soviet people are very happy to do everything possible to help our great friend, the Chinese people, and are prepared to help the Chinese people master the latest achievements in science and technology for the peaceful use of atomic energy." Yudin continued: "We believe that in the near future, China will widely develop its own atomic energy industry. We hope that this exhibition will provide Chinese scientists and engineers with more help in this regard and will further promote China's smooth socialist construction."

Zhang Wentian, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, said in his speech: "Today, when our socialist construction is developing rapidly and the whole country is advancing towards science, the opening of the Soviet Exhibition of Science and Technology for Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy will greatly inspire and help our scientific and technological work. We will take advantage of this good opportunity to organize visits and studies well." Zhang Wentian wished the exhibition success. On behalf of the Chinese government and people, he expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Soviet government and people for the help they have given us in the research and peaceful use of atomic energy science and technology.

Also speaking at the ceremony were Chelenkov, the Soviet scientific representative and chief scientific and technological advisor to the exhibition, and Yan Jici, the Chinese scientific representative and director of the Department of Technological Sciences of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. They both warmly congratulated the opening of the exhibition.

Diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries to China were also invited to attend the opening ceremony.

More than 500 people attended the opening ceremony.

At 10:10 AM, Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, cut the ribbon. The guests then visited the exhibition hall.

Xinhua News Agency, June 15th: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, held a banquet tonight to celebrate the opening of the Soviet Exhibition of Science and Technology for Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy.

Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, personnel from the Soviet Embassy in China, and Soviet experts and staff working at the exhibition were invited to attend the banquet.

President Guo Moruo and Ambassador Yudin spoke at the banquet, congratulating the opening of the exhibition.

Among those present at the banquet were the Secretary-General of the State Council, ministers and deputy ministers of relevant ministries, vice presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, heads of organizations and groups of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, China Association for Science and Technology, the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, the Vice Mayor of Beijing, and well-known figures from the capital's scientific, educational, medical, and artistic circles.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. (Attached picture)

### 苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会开幕  中国科学院院长郭沫若举行庆祝宴会

1956-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会开幕
    中国科学院院长郭沫若举行庆祝宴会
    新华社15日讯　苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会，今天上午在北京正式开幕。
    开幕典礼在苏联展览馆电影馆内举行，上午九时十五分在奏苏中两国国歌后，苏联驻中国大使尤金首先讲话。他在叙述了苏联和平利用原子能方面的成就以后说，我们在这方面不仅毫不保留我们所发现的成就，而且准备帮助其他国家掌握这种和平利用原子能的成就。苏联人民怀着十分高兴的心情准备尽自己所能帮助我们伟大的朋友——中国人民，准备帮助中国人民掌握和平利用原子能科学技术的最新成就。尤金继续说：我们相信，在不久的将来中国将广泛地发展自己的原子能工业，我们希望这个展览会将给予中国科学家和工程师们在这方面更多的帮助，将进一步促进中国顺利地进行社会主义建设。
    外交部副部长张闻天致词说：今天当我国社会主义建设正在迅速发展、全国正向科学大进军的时候，苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会的开幕，将会给我国科学技术工作以很大的启发和帮助，我们将利用这个良好的机会，很好地组织参观和学习。张闻天预祝展览会的成功，他代表中国政府和人民对于苏联政府和苏联人民在我国原子能科学技术的研究和和平利用方面所给予我们的帮助，表示衷心的感谢。
    在典礼上讲话的还有苏联科学界代表、展览会科学技术总指导切连科夫，和中国科学界代表、中国科学院技术科学部主任严济慈。他们都热烈祝贺展览会的开幕。
    各国驻华使节和外交官员也应邀参加了开幕典礼。
    参加开幕典礼的共五百多人。
    十时十分，中国科学院院长郭沫若剪彩。来宾们接着在展览会大厅参观。
    新华社15日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若今晚举行宴会，庆祝苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会开幕。
    应邀参加宴会的有苏联驻华大使尤金院士、苏联驻华大使馆人员和参加展览会工作的苏联专家和工作人员。
    郭沫若院长和尤金大使在宴会上先后讲话，祝贺展览会开幕。
    出席宴会作陪的有国务院秘书长，有关各部部长和副部长，中国科学院副院长，中苏友好协会、中国人民对外文化协会、中华全国科学技术普及协会、中国国际贸易促进委员会等机关团体负责人，北京市副市长和首都科学、教育、医务、文艺界著名人士。
    宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。（附图片）
    苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会在北京苏联展览馆文化馆开幕，由中国科学院院长郭沫若剪彩。右为苏联大使尤金。　　　　新华社记者　　钱嗣杰摄
