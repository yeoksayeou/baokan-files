Title: Ceylon Revokes Import Ban on Progressive Books
Author:
Date: 1956-06-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 4th -- Bandaranaike revoked on June 1st the ban on the import of books from socialist countries, a ban that the previous Kotelawala government had implemented in 1953.

The Prime Minister of Ceylon stipulated that any publications considered to have questionable content should be submitted to him for a decision on whether to allow their import.

The Prime Minister made this decision after the Deputy General Secretary of the Ceylon Communist Party, Keuneman, requested permission to import books and publications from China, the Soviet Union, and other People's Democratic countries.

### 锡兰撤销进步书籍进口禁令

1956-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰撤销进步书籍进口禁令
    新华社4日讯　科伦坡消息：班达拉奈克在六月一日撤消了不准社会主义国家的书籍进口的禁令，这个禁令是前科特拉瓦拉政府在1953年实施的。
    锡兰总理规定，对于被认为内容有疑问的任何书刊都应交由他来决定是否准许进口。
    总理是在锡兰共产党副总书记克尼曼要求允许从中国、苏联和其他人民民主国家进口书刊以后作出这项决定的。
