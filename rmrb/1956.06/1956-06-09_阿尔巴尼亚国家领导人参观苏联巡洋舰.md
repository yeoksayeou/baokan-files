Title: Albanian State Leaders Visit Soviet Cruiser
Author:
Date: 1956-06-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 8th: According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency, some leaders of the Albanian Labor Party and the state visited the Soviet cruiser "Kutuzov" docked in the port of Durrës on the afternoon of June 6th. The Soviet fleet arrived in Durrës from Yugoslavia on June 5th.

Chairman of the Council of Ministers Shehu and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense Lieutenant General Baluku, accompanied by Admiral Kasatonov, commander of the Soviet Black Sea Fleet, reviewed the sailors lined up on the deck. Subsequently, they visited the cruiser. A reception was held on the warship for the Albanian leaders. Kasatonov and Shehu spoke at the meeting.

The Albanian party and state leaders left the cruiser amidst a 21-gun salute from the cruiser.

### 阿尔巴尼亚国家领导人参观苏联巡洋舰

1956-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚国家领导人参观苏联巡洋舰
    新华社布拉格8日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和国家的一些领导人，在六日下午访问了停泊在都拉斯港口的苏联巡洋舰“库图佐夫号”。苏联舰队是六月五日从南斯拉夫到达都拉斯的。
    部长会议主席谢胡和部长会议第一副主席兼国防部长巴卢库中将，在苏联黑海舰队司令卡萨托诺夫海军上将陪同下，检阅了列队站在甲板上的水兵们。随后，他们参观了这艘巡洋舰。军舰上为阿尔巴尼亚的领导人举行了招待会。卡萨托诺夫和谢胡在会上讲了话。
    阿尔巴尼亚党和国家领导人在巡洋舰放礼炮二十一响声中，离开了巡洋舰。
