    Demand a Peaceful Resolution to the Algerian Question
    Anti-War Demonstrations Held in Many Places in France
    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, May 30th (TASS dispatch) – “L'Humanité” reports that as French reinforcements continue to depart for Algeria, anti-war demonstrations have occurred in recent days at train stations in cities such as Saintes, Tarbes, Nevers, and Angoulême. In Tarbes and Saintes, thousands of people participated in the demonstrations. Residents, along with young people reaching conscription age, demanded a halt to military operations in Algeria, a cessation of sending more troops to Algeria, and the commencement of peace negotiations.
  Tunisian Prime Minister Supports the Algerian People
    Xinhua News Agency, May 31st dispatch, Tunis: Tunisian Prime Minister Bourguiba stated on the 29th at the Tunisian Constituent Assembly that Tunisia stands in solidarity with the Algerian people. He said that Tunisia's attitude is not unfriendly or hostile towards France, but is hostile towards colonialism.
  Yazid Condemns US and UK Support for French Colonialists
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, May 30th – According to Antara News Agency, Mohammed Yazid, who participated in the Bandung Conference as an Algerian observer, is currently visiting Indonesia. He said that France concentrated its armed forces in Algeria after the North Atlantic Treaty Organization approved its withdrawal of these forces from the European continent, and this "could only be done with the consent and support of the United States and Great Britain." Yazid condemned French atrocities in Algeria. He pointed out that since November 1954, approximately 100,000 Algerians have been killed, but the Algerian people's struggle will make France "soon realize that the use of force cannot solve any problem."

### 要求和平解决阿尔及利亚问题

1956-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    要求和平解决阿尔及利亚问题
    法国许多地方举行反战示威
    新华社据塔斯社巴黎30日讯　“人道报”报道，当法国援军正在继续出发开到阿尔及利亚去的时候，最近几天在塞恩、塔布、内维尔、安古雷姆等城市的车站上发生了反战示威。在塔布和塞恩参加示威的有几千人。居民同到达服役年龄的青年一起要求在阿尔及利亚停止军事行动，停止往阿尔及利亚增派军队，并且开始和平谈判。
  突尼斯首相支持阿尔及利亚人民
    新华社31日讯　突尼斯消息：突尼斯首相布尔吉巴二十九日在突尼斯制宪议会上说，突尼斯是同阿尔及利亚人民团结一致的。他说，突尼斯的态度不是对法国不友好或是敌对，而是对殖民主义敌对。
  雅齐德谴责美英支持法国殖民者
    据新华社雅加达30日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，曾经以阿尔及利亚观察员身份参加万隆会议的穆罕默德·雅齐德目前正在印度尼西亚访问。他说，法国是在北大西洋集团批准它从欧洲大陆撤出它的武装部队以后，把这些部队集中在阿尔及利亚的，而这“只有在美国和英国的赞同和支持下才能够办到”。雅齐德谴责法国在阿尔及利亚的暴行。他指出，自从1954年11月以来，大约有十万阿尔及利亚人被杀，但是，阿尔及利亚人民的斗争将使得法国“不久就会知道使用武力解决不了任何问题”。
