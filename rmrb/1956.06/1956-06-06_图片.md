Title: Zhongshan County, Guangdong Province, Yongning Township Agricultural Production Cooperative Actively Develops Sideline Production

Author:
Date: 1956-06-06
Page: 1

The fish ponds of this cooperative have expanded from 2,200 mu to more than 2,900 mu, and it is estimated that 800,000 jin of various freshwater fish can be harvested throughout the year. This is a scene of the members catching fish.

专栏：

    广东省中山县永宁乡农业生产合作社积极发展副业生产。这个社的鱼塘已从二千二百亩扩大到二千九百多亩，预计全年可收各种淡水鱼八十万斤。这是社员们正在捕鱼的一个场面。
