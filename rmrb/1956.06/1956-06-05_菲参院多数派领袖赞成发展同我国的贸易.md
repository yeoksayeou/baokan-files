Title: Philippine Senate Majority Leader Advocates for Developing Trade with China
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: A Manila-based Agence France-Presse reporter reported: On May 31, Philippine Senate Majority Leader Primicias made a statement advocating for the development of trade with the People's Republic of China and calling on Philippine entrepreneurs not to overly rely on the United States.

### 菲参院多数派领袖赞成发展同我国的贸易

1956-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    菲参院多数派领袖赞成发展同我国的贸易
    据新华社讯　法新社驻马尼拉记者报道：菲律宾参议院多数派领袖普里米西亚斯在五月三十一日发表谈话，赞成发展同中华人民共和国的贸易，并且号召菲律宾的企业家不要过份信赖美国。
