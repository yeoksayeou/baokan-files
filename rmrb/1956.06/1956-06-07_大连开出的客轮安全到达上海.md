Title: Passenger Ship from Dalian Arrives Safely in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1956-06-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 5th: At 8:50 PM tonight, the first passenger ship from Dalian, the "Democracy No. 9," slowly docked at the Huangpu Wharf in Shanghai. Many passengers, upon disembarking, spoke to their relatives and friends about the comfortable life on board.

The "Democracy No. 9" sailed for four days and nights, stopping at Shidao and Qingdao along the way.

### 大连开出的客轮安全到达上海

1956-06-07
第2版()
专栏：

    大连开出的客轮安全到达上海
    据新华社上海5日电　今天晚上八时五十分，从大连开出的第一艘客轮——民主九号轮，徐徐靠上上海的黄浦码头。许多旅客一上岸就向亲友们谈论船上舒适的生活。
    民主九号轮共航行了四昼夜，途中曾在石岛、青岛两地停泊。
