Title: President Pieck Receives Delegation of the Korean Government
Author:
Date: 1956-06-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, June 9th (via TASS) – President Pieck received the delegation of the Korean government headed by Kim Il-sung yesterday.

Present at the meeting were Prime Minister Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic and Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Bolz.

Chairman of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, Dieckmann, also received the delegation on the same day.

### 皮克总统接见朝鲜政府代表团

1956-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    皮克总统接见朝鲜政府代表团
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社柏林九日讯：皮克总统昨天接见了以金日成为首的朝鲜政府代表团。
    接见时在座的有民主德国总理格罗提渥和副总理兼外交部长博尔茨。
    民主德国人民议院主席狄克曼也在同一天接见了代表团。
