Title: Pakistani Prime Minister Decides to Postpone Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1956-06-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, May 31st: According to a May 30th announcement by the Pakistani Cabinet Information Secretariat, Pakistani Prime Minister Muhammad Ali has decided to postpone his visit to China on the advice of his doctors.

According to the announcement, Muhammad Ali has been feeling unwell since the evening of May 28th. His doctors found him to have symptoms of over-fatigue and advised him to rest completely for a few weeks. Therefore, on the advice of his doctors, Prime Minister Ali has decided to postpone his visit to China.

### 巴基斯坦总理决定延期访问中国

1956-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦总理决定延期访问中国
    新华社喀喇蚩31日电　据巴基斯坦内阁新闻秘书处五月三十日宣布，巴基斯坦总理穆罕默德·阿里按照医生的建议，已经决定延期访问中国。
    据宣布，自从五月二十八日晚上以来，穆罕默德·阿里一直感到不舒服。他的医生发现他有过度疲劳的症候，并且建议他完全休养几个星期。因此，按照医生的建议，阿里总理已经决定延期访问中国。
