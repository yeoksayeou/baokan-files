Title: North Korea and Hungary Issue Joint Communiqué; Both Sides Decide to Sign Agreement on Trade and Scientific and Technological Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1956-06-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 22nd: According to a report by the Hungarian News Agency, a joint communiqué issued in Budapest by North Korea and Hungary states that, following talks with the visiting North Korean government delegation, Hungarian representatives decided that the two countries will sign a trade agreement and an agreement on scientific and technological cooperation in the near future. The communiqué states that the two sides' representatives have signed a new cultural agreement.

The communiqué points out that the talks held by the North Korean government delegation headed by Kim Il-sung and the main representatives of Hungary took place in an atmosphere of mutual friendship and sincere understanding. The two sides discussed the political, economic, and cultural relations between the two countries, issues related to the reunification of Korea, the security of Europe and Asia, and the easing of international tensions.

The North Korean government delegation paid a friendly visit to Hungary from June 17th to 20th at the invitation of the Hungarian government. During their stay in Hungary, the delegation visited industrial enterprises and cultural institutions and learned about the achievements of Hungary's construction work. The delegation was received by Dobi, Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, Rónai, Speaker of the National Assembly, Rákosi, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, and Hegedüs, Chairman of the Council of Ministers.

### 朝鲜和匈牙利发表联合公报  双方决定签订关于贸易和科学技术合作的协定

1956-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜和匈牙利发表联合公报
    双方决定签订关于贸易和科学技术合作的协定
    新华社布拉格22日电　据匈牙利通讯社报道：在布达佩斯发表的朝鲜和匈牙利联合公报说，应邀前来访问的朝鲜政府代表团，同匈牙利的代表举行会谈后决定，两国将在短时期内签订贸易协定和关于科学和技术合作的协定。公报说，双方代表已经签订了新的文化协定。
    公报指出，以金日成为首的朝鲜政府代表团同匈牙利的主要代表所举行的会谈，是在相互友好和诚挚谅解的气氛中进行的。双方曾经讨论了两国的政治、经济和文化关系，有关朝鲜的统一、欧洲和亚洲的安全问题，以及缓和国际紧张局势的问题。
    朝鲜政府代表团从六月十七日到二十日应邀到匈牙利作友好访问。在匈牙利期间，代表团参观了工业企业和文化机关，了解了匈牙利建设工作的成就。代表团受到了匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比、国民议会议长罗奈伊、匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记拉科西和部长会议主席赫格居斯等的接见。
