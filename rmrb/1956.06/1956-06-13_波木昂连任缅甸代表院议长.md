Title: Ba Swe Re-elected Speaker of Burmese Chamber of Deputies
Author:
Date: 1956-06-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, June 11th. At a meeting held today by the Chamber of Deputies, former Speaker Ba Swe was unanimously re-elected as Speaker. Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League candidate Mann Bha Sae was elected as Deputy Speaker of the Chamber of Deputies. The Chamber of Deputies meeting will elect the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma tomorrow, and the new Prime Minister will present his list of cabinet members at the meeting.

### 波木昂连任缅甸代表院议长

1956-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    波木昂连任缅甸代表院议长
    新华社仰光11日电　在代表院今天举行的会议上，前代表院议长波木昂被一致推选连任议长。反法西斯人民自由同盟候选人曼巴赛被选为代表院副议长。代表院会议将在明天选举缅甸联邦总理，新总理将在会上提出他的内阁阁员名单。
