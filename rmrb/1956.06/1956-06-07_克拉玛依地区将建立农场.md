Title: Farmland to be Established in Karamay Area
Author:
Date: 1956-06-07
Page: 1

    Karamay Area to Establish Farm
    This newspaper, Xinjiang, 5th dispatch: The Xinjiang Military Region Production and Construction Corps of the Chinese People's Liberation Army has decided to build a farm combining agriculture, animal husbandry, and vegetables in the Karamay area to provide a large amount of grain, meat, vegetables, and other foods to the people building the Karamay Oilfield nearby.
    Currently, the Production and Construction Corps has transferred a group of cadres to form a working group, which has begun preliminary surveys of hydrology and topography in the Karamay area. They will initially determine the scale of the farm, the location of the reservoir, and the electric pumping station.

### 克拉玛依地区将建立农场

1956-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    克拉玛依地区将建立农场
    本报新疆5日电　中国人民解放军新疆军区生产建设部队决定在克拉玛依地区建设一个农、牧、蔬菜相结合的农场，以便就近供应建设克拉玛依油田的人们大批粮食、肉类和蔬菜等食品。
    现在，生产建设部队已经抽调一批干部组成工作组，开始在克拉玛依地区进行水文、地形等初查工作。他们将初步确定建场的规模、水库及电动抽水站的地址。
