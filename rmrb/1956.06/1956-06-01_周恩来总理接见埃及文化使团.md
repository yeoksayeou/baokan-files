Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Egyptian Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-06-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 31st. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received Dr. Fekri, an Egyptian ancient historian, and the Egyptian cultural delegation headed by Professor Shawarby this afternoon.

Those present at the reception included Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Qian Junrui, Vice Minister of Culture; Jiang Longji, Vice President of Peking University; Da Pongsheng, Vice Chairman of the China Islamic Association; Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations; Xia Nai, Deputy Director of the Institute of Archeology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Yin Da, Deputy Director of the Second Institute of History Research; and Yao Guang, Deputy Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 周恩来总理接见埃及文化使团

1956-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见埃及文化使团
    新华社31日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，今天下午接见了埃及古代史学家费克里博士和以夏瓦比教授为首的埃及文化使团。
    接见时在座的，有中国科学院院长郭沫若，文化部副部长钱俊瑞，北京大学副校长江隆基，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生，对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经，中国科学院考古研究所副所长夏鼐，历史研究所第二所副所长尹达，外交部西欧非洲司副司长姚广。
