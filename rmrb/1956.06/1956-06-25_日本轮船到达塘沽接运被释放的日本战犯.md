Title: Japanese Ship Arrives in Tanggu to Pick Up Released Japanese War Criminals
Author:
Date: 1956-06-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 23rd—The Japanese ship "興安丸" (Kōan Maru), which came to pick up the 335 Japanese war criminals released by the Chinese government to return to their country, arrived at Tanggu New Port at 4:30 this afternoon.

Arriving on the ship were Taro Inoue, Director of the Japanese Red Cross Society's Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Jugo No, Standing Director of the Japan-China Friendship Association; Gyokuzo Abe, representative of the Japan Peace Liaison Council; and Yoshiharu Okada, the representative of the above three groups who escorted the overseas Chinese back to China. The ship also carried representatives Tao Hodo, Toshiko Ikeda, Toshishige Nakajima, and secretaries Risaburo Kiuchi, Matsuji Matsui, and Junichi Nakahara. They also brought a batch of letters and packages from the families of the Japanese war criminals to the Japanese war criminals.

They were welcomed at the wharf of Tanggu New Port by Ji Feng and Wu Shifa, representatives of the Chinese Red Cross Society, and others. Then, accompanied by Ji Feng and others, they took a car into Tianjin City.

Fourteen Japanese journalists also arrived in Tianjin on the same ship.

### 日本轮船到达塘沽接运被释放的日本战犯

1956-06-25
第3版()
专栏：

    日本轮船到达塘沽接运被释放的日本战犯
    新华社天津23日电　前来接回被中国政府释放的335名日本战争犯罪分子回国的日本轮船“兴安丸”在今天下午四时三十分到达塘沽新港。
    随船前来的有日本红十字会外交部部长井上益太郎、日中友好协会常任理事长野重右卫门、日本和平联络会代表阿部行藏，以及这次护送华侨回国的上述三团体代表冈田好治，乘船代表烟道好、池田利子、中岛利重，秘书木内利三郎、松井松次、中原淳吉。他们还带来了日本战争犯罪分子的家属给日本战争犯罪分子的一批信件和包裹。
    他们在塘沽新港的码头上受到中国红十字会代表纪锋、吴士法等人的欢迎。接着，由纪锋等陪同乘车进入天津市。
    同船到达天津的还有十四名日本新闻记者。
