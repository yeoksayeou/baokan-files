Title: Socialist Countries Continuously Expand Foreign Trade
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency: Socialist Countries Continuously Expand Foreign Trade

    Poland's Trade Relations Extend Worldwide
    Xinhua News Agency: Polish Foreign Trade Minister Dąbrowski said on the 13th that Poland currently has trade agreements with 76 countries, but in reality, it trades with almost all countries in the world.
    In recent years, 13 countries, including India, Indonesia, Burma, Pakistan, Iran, Lebanon, and Greece, which had never signed trade agreements with Poland before, have now signed trade agreements with Poland. In 1955, Poland's total foreign trade value reached 7.405 billion rubles, while in 1949 it was only around 5 billion rubles.
    At a press conference, Dąbrowski announced that in recent years, trade between Poland and socialist countries has further developed, and at the same time, the trade volume with capitalist countries has also increased significantly. In 1955, capitalist countries accounted for more than 36% of Poland's total trade value, while in 1954 it was 29.2%.
    The Foreign Trade Minister pointed out that the characteristics of Poland's foreign trade are the increasing import of industrial raw materials and the export of machinery and equipment. In 1955, industrial raw materials and semi-finished products purchased from abroad accounted for 51.7% of the total import value. This year, the export of machinery will increase to four and a half times that of 1949.
    Czechoslovakia and Lebanon Sign Trade Agreement
    Xinhua News Agency: According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency, Czechoslovakia and Lebanon signed a three-year trade agreement in Beirut on June 6.
    The report said that this is the result of the continuous increase in trade between the two countries in recent years.
    Xinhua News Agency: According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency, Czechoslovakia and Denmark have signed a one-year trade agreement and payment agreement. According to the agreement, both parties will provide commodities worth approximately 60 million crowns each.
    Czechoslovakia will supply Denmark with metallurgical industrial products, machinery, automobiles, motorcycles, tractors, and chemicals.
    Denmark will supply Czechoslovakia with butter, meat, fish, fish products, medicines, and other commodities.
  Hungary Exports Large Quantities of Industrial and Agricultural Machinery to Ceylon
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi: Colombo News: Ceylon and the Hungarian People's Republic signed a trade and payment agreement in Colombo on June 4.
    According to the agreement, Ceylon will export tea, rubber, various coconut products, graphite, and spices to Hungary. Ceylon will receive flour, sugar, industrial and agricultural machinery, and other railway vehicles from Hungary.
      Romania and Austria Sign Barter Protocol
    Xinhua News Agency: According to the Romanian News Agency, government representatives of Romania and Austria signed a barter protocol for 1956-1957 in Bucharest on June 5.
    According to this protocol, Romania will import steel, steel products, magnesite, cotton yarn, industrial equipment, and other goods from Austria, and will export industrial products, petroleum and chemical products, grain, and livestock to Austria.

### 社会主义国家对外贸易不断扩大

1956-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    社会主义国家对外贸易不断扩大
    波兰贸易关系遍及全世界
    新华社讯　波兰对外贸易部长董布罗夫斯基十三日说，波兰目前同七十六个国家签订有贸易协定，但是实际上它几乎同全世界所有的国家进行着贸易。
    近几年来，印度、印度尼西亚、缅甸、巴基斯坦、伊朗、黎巴嫩、希腊等十三个从来没有同波兰签订过贸易协定的国家，已经同波兰签订了贸易协定。1955年，波兰对外贸易总值达到七十四亿零五百万卢布，而1949年只有五十亿卢布左右。
    董布罗夫斯基在记者招待会上宣布，近几年来，波兰同社会主义国家的贸易有了进一步的发展，同时，同资本主义国家的贸易额也有了显著的增加。1955年，资本主义国家在波兰的贸易总值中占36％以上，而1954年是29.2％。
    这位对外贸易部长指出，波兰对外贸易的特点是：进口中的工业原料和出口中的机器和设备日益增加。1955年，由国外购进的工业原料和半制品，已经占进口总值的51.7％。今年出口的机器将增加到1949年的四倍半。
    捷黎、捷丹签订贸易协定
    新华社讯　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，捷克斯洛伐克和黎巴嫩六月六日在贝鲁特签订了一项为期三年的贸易协定。
    消息说，这是近年来两国之间贸易不断增加的结果。
    新华社讯　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，捷克斯洛伐克和丹麦签订了为期一年的贸易协定和支付协定。根据协定，双方各提供总值约六千万克朗的商品。
    捷克斯洛伐克将供给丹麦冶金工业制品、机器、汽车、机器脚踏车、拖拉机和化学品等商品。
    丹麦将供给捷克斯洛伐克黄油、肉、鱼、鱼制品、药品和其他商品。
  匈向锡输出大批工农业机器
    新华社新德里讯　科伦坡消息：锡兰和匈牙利人民共和国六月四日在科伦坡签订了一项贸易和支付协定。
    根据协定，锡兰将向匈牙利输出茶、橡胶、各种椰子产品、石墨和香料等。锡兰将从匈牙利得到面粉、糖、工业和农业机器和其他铁路车辆等。
      罗奥签订换货议定书
    新华社讯　据罗马尼亚通讯社报道：罗马尼亚和奥地利两国政府代表六月五日在布加勒斯特签订了两国1956—1957年换货议定书。
    根据这项议定书，罗马尼亚将从奥地利进口钢、钢材、菱镁矿、棉纱、工业装备和其他货物，将向奥地利输出工业产品、石油和化学产品、谷物和家畜等。
