Title: Premier Zhou Receives Representatives of Three Japanese Groups; Representatives of Three Japanese Groups Visit the Chinese Red Cross Society
Author:
Date: 1956-06-28
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, June 27th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received Nagano Shigemo, Abe Yukizo, Okada Yoshiji, Matsui Matsuji, Nakahara Junichi, Ikeda Toshiko, and Nakajima Toshishige, representatives of the three Japanese groups: the Japanese Red Cross Society, the Japan-China Friendship Association, and the Japan Peace Liaison Council, this afternoon.

    Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, advisor to the Chinese Red Cross Society.

    Xinhua News Agency, June 27th: Nagano Shigemo, Abe Yukizo, Okada Yoshiji, Matsui Matsuji, Nakahara Junichi and other five representatives of the three Japanese groups: the Japanese Red Cross Society, the Japan-China Friendship Association, and the Japan Peace Liaison Council, visited the Chinese Red Cross Society this morning. Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross Society, received them.

    The representatives of the three Japanese groups expressed their gratitude to the Chinese government for releasing Japanese war criminals and for the assistance given by the Chinese Red Cross Society to these released Japanese.

    During the visit, Abe Yukizo, representative of the Japan Peace Liaison Council, presented Li Dequan with a peace medal.

    Also present at the reception were twelve Japanese journalists who accompanied the representatives of the three Japanese groups to Beijing.

### 周总理接见日本三团体代表日本三团体代表访问中国红十字会

1956-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见日本三团体代表
    日本三团体代表访问中国红十字会
    新华社27日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，在今天下午接见了日本红十字会、日中友好协会、日本和平联络会三团体代表长野重右卫门、阿部行藏、冈田好治、松井松次、中原淳吉、池田利子和中岛利重。
    接见时在座的，有中国红十字会总会顾问廖承志等人。
    新华社27日讯　日本红十字会、日中友好协会、日本和平联络会三团体代表长野重右卫门、阿部行藏、冈田好治、松井松次、中原淳吉等五人，今天上午访问了中国红十字会，中国红十字会总会会长李德全接见了他们。
    日本三团体代表对中国政府释放日本战争犯罪分子和中国红十字会对这些被释放的日本人所给予的协助，表示感谢。
    在访问时，日本和平联络会代表阿部行藏向李德全赠送了和平纪念章。
    接见时在座的还有随同日本三团体代表前来北京的日本记者十二人。
