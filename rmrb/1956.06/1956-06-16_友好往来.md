Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1956-06-16
Page: 4

    △ At the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Harold Wilfred Youren, Vice-Chairman of the New Zealand National Peace Council, Australian writer Norman Randolph Freehill, and Australian female writer Dymphna Cusack arrived in Beijing on June 14.
    △ On the morning of the 13th, Chinese weightlifters saw off the entire Soviet weightlifting team who were leaving Beijing to return to their country at the airport.
    △ The Burmese film delegation headed by Wu Yang Na left Kunming by plane on the morning of the 13th to return to their country.
    △ The Burmese Buddha Tooth Relic Escort Delegation, headed by Wu Qing Mao Piao, Secretary-General of the Burmese Federal Government, left Kunming by plane on the morning of the 13th to return home.

### 友好往来

1956-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    ·友好往来·
    △应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来中国访问的新西兰全国和平理事会副主席哈罗德·韦尔弗雷德·约伦、澳大利亚作家诺曼·伦道夫·弗里希尔和澳大利亚女作家丁夫娜·库沙克在六月十四日到达北京。
    △中国的举重运动员十三日早晨在飞机场上送别了离开北京回国的苏联举重队全体人员。
    △以吴仰拿为首的缅甸电影代表团，十三日上午乘飞机离开昆明回国。
    △以缅甸联邦政府秘书长吴庆貌漂为首的缅甸佛牙护送代表团一行三人，十三日上午乘飞机离开昆明返国。
