Title: Water Level of Upper Yangtze River Recedes
Author:
Date: 1956-06-04
Page: 2

These past few days, the Yangtze River basin has enjoyed rare clear weather. The water levels of the main stream of the upper Yangtze River and the rivers of the Dongting Lake system have begun to recede. On June 1st, the Wuhan Customs water level was 23.8 meters, more than 4 decimeters lower than the level on the same day in 1954.

### 长江上游水位回落

1956-06-04
第2版()
专栏：

    长江上游水位回落
    这两天，长江流域稀有的晴朗。长江上游干流和洞庭湖水系各河流水位已经开始回落。六月一日武汉关水位二三点八公尺，比1954年同一天水位低四公寸多。
