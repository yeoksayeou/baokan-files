Title: Budget Committee Completes Examination of Last Year's National Final Accounts and This Year's National Budget
Author:
Date: 1956-06-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 28th. The Budget Committee of the National People's Congress has completed its review report on the 1955 National Final Accounts and the 1956 National Budget after reviewing them. This was announced at a meeting held today.

After listening to the "Report on the 1955 National Final Accounts and the 1956 National Budget" by Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Finance, the Budget Committee held plenary meetings and meetings of the final accounts and budget review groups to study the opinions of the National People's Congress deputies on the national final accounts and the national budget, and reviewed the national final accounts and the national budget. During the review process, the Budget Committee also consulted the government based on the opinions raised by the delegates and held discussions, raising review opinions on issues such as the 1955 final accounts revenue and 1956 budget revenue, the practice of thrift in capital construction, social, cultural, and educational expenses, farmers' burdens and rural primary school funding, and financial management. The plenary meeting held today summarized these review opinions and produced a review report on the national final accounts and the national budget.

The Budget Committee's review report believes that the situation of the 1955 National Final Accounts is basically good, and the arrangement of the 1956 National Budget revenue and expenditure is appropriate, positive, and relatively reliable. It also believes that if the situation changes during the implementation of the national budget, the State Council can make some necessary and appropriate adjustments based on the budget guidelines approved by the National People's Congress, and report to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress within a certain period. The review report also pointed out that the victorious completion of the 1956 National Budget can accelerate the speed of socialist construction in our country.

The review report completed by the Budget Committee today will be submitted to the Third Session of the First National People's Congress, and it is recommended that the Third Session approve the 1955 National Final Accounts and the 1956 National Budget.

### 预算委员会对去年国家决算和今年国家预算审查完毕

1956-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    预算委员会对去年国家决算和今年国家预算审查完毕
    新华社28日讯　全国人民代表大会预算委员会对1955年国家决算和1956年国家预算进行了审查后，在今天举行的会议上完成了关于这次国家决算和国家预算的审查报告。
    预算委员会在听了国务院副总理兼财政部长李先念“关于1955年国家决算和1956年国家预算的报告”后，曾经先后举行全体会议和决算、预算两个审查小组会议，研究了全国人民代表大会代表们对于这次国家决算和国家预算的意见，审查了这次国家决算和国家预算。在审查过程中，预算委员会还曾根据大会代表们提出的意见，向政府提出了咨询，并且进行了讨论，就关于1955年决算收入和1956年预算收入的问题、关于基本建设方面厉行节约的问题、关于社会文教费方面的问题、关于农民负担和乡村小学经费的问题和有关财政管理方面等问题都提出了审查意见。今天举行的全体会议，汇总了这些审查意见，作成关于这次国家决算和国家预算的审查报告。
    预算委员会的审查报告认为，1955年国家决算的情况基本上是良好的，1956年国家预算收支的安排也是适当的、积极的、比较可靠的。并且认为，如果在国家预算执行过程中，情况有了变化，国务院可以根据全国人民代表大会批准的预算方针作某些必要的适当的调整，并且在一定时期，向全国人民代表大会常务委员会报告。审查报告还指出，1956年国家预算的胜利完成，就可以加快我国社会主义建设的速度。
    预算委员会今天完成的这个审查报告将向第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议提出，并且建议第三次会议批准1955年国家决算和1956年国家预算。
