Title: National People's Congress Continues Meeting; Hears Reports on the Examination of State Final Accounts and Budget

Author:
Date: 1956-06-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 29th: The Third Session of the First National People's Congress continued its plenary meeting on the afternoon of the 29th.

At today's meeting, all the delegates listened to the report on the examination of the 1955 state final accounts and the 1956 state budget made by Liu Lantao, Chairman of the Budget Committee of the First National People's Congress, and the report on the examination of the draft model charter of advanced agricultural producers' cooperatives made by Wu Xinyu, Vice Chairman of the Bills Committee of the First National People's Congress.

A total of twenty-four delegates and heads of various departments of state organs spoke at today's meeting (the full text of the speeches will be published separately).

Minister of Labor Ma Wenrui spoke today on labor and wage work and improving working conditions for workers. He said: The nationwide wage reform will begin soon. After this reform, the average wage of workers across the country in 1956 will increase by 14.5% compared to 1955.

Seven well-known Chinese medical workers, Wang Shuzhen, Lin Qiaozhi, Huang Jiasi, Yu Aifeng, Zhu Futang, Wu Yingkai, and Zhu Xianyi, jointly spoke at today's meeting. Their joint speech was read by Wang Shuzhen.

Lü Hongbin, Li Chenggui, Zhang Chong, Wang Haimin, Zhao Zukang, Liu Qingyang, Wu Yuheng, Ma John, Zhang Dongmu, Li Bozhao, Zhuang Mingli, Sun Dehe, Qiu Yu Chi, Songbu, Zhang Yan, and Aimujiang also spoke at today's meeting.

The executive chairmen presiding over today's meeting were: Guo Moruo, Gao Chongmin, Zhu Kezhen, Liao Chengzhi, Luo Shifa, Deng Baoshan, Ji Jinmei, and Ba Jin.

The meeting will continue on the 30th.

### 全国人民代表大会继续举行会议  听取关于国家决算和预算等审查报告

1956-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会继续举行会议
    听取关于国家决算和预算等审查报告
    据新华社29日讯　第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议，二十九日下午继续举行全体会议。
    全体代表在今天的会议上，分别听了第一届全国人民代表大会预算委员会主任委员刘澜涛所作的关于1955年国家决算和1956年国家预算的审查报告、第一届全国人民代表大会法案委员会副主任委员武新宇所作的关于高级农业生产合作社示范章程（草案）的审查报告。
    在今天会议上发言的代表和国家机关各部门负责人共有二十四人（发言全文另发）。
    劳动部部长马文瑞今天就劳动工资工作和改善职工劳动条件等问题发言。他说：全国性的工资改革最近就要开始进行。经过这次改革，1956年全国职工的平均工资将比1955年提高14.5％。
    我国著名的医学工作者王淑贞、林巧稚、黄家驷、俞霭峰、诸福棠、吴英恺、朱宪彝等七位代表，今天联合发言。他们的联合发言由王淑贞代读。
    吕鸿宾、李呈桂、张冲、王海民、赵祖康、刘清扬、吴昱恒、马约翰、张东木、李伯钊、庄明理、孙德和、丘玉池、松布、张岩、阿衣木江在今天的会议上也发了言。
    主持今天会议的执行主席是：郭沫若、高崇民、竺可桢、廖承志、罗世发、邓宝珊、计晋美、巴金。
    三十日，会议将继续举行。
