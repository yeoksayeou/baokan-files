Title: Chairman Mao and Members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Receive Advanced Workers from the Department Store and Food Company Systems
Author:
Date: 1956-06-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd: Chairman Mao Zedong and members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Kang Sheng, Peng Zhen, Dong Biwu, Lin Boqu, Zhang Wentian, and Deng Xiaoping received all the delegates attending the Advanced Workers Representative Conference of the China Department Store and Food Company Systems at 2:30 PM today.

### 毛主席和中共中央政治局委员  接见百货、食品公司系统先进工作者

1956-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席和中共中央政治局委员
    接见百货、食品公司系统先进工作者
    新华社23日讯　毛泽东主席和中共中央政治局委员刘少奇、朱德、陈云、康生、彭真、董必武、林伯渠、张闻天、邓小平在今天下午二时三十分接见了参加中国百货、食品公司系统先进工作者代表会议的全体代表。
