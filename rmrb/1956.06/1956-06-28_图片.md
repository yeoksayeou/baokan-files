    Chairman Mao, Chairman Liu, and Premier Zhou in a group photo with members of the Finnish Parliamentary Delegation visiting China. The seventh person from the left in the front row is Head of Delegation Sukselainen, and the ninth person from the left in the front row is Finnish Ambassador Sundström. Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporters Hou Bo and Lü Houmin.
