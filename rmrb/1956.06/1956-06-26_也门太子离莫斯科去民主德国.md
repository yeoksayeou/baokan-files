Title: Yemen's Crown Prince Leaves Moscow for East Germany
Author:
Date: 1956-06-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow (TASS) - Yemen's Crown Prince, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister, Seif al-Islam Muhammad al-Badr, and his entourage, left Moscow on June 25 for the German Democratic Republic.

Badr said at the airport: "I will never forget the sincere friendly ties between myself and the Soviet people, and with their leaders. I am proud of this friendship and will do my utmost to develop and strengthen this friendship, to promote close cooperation between the two peoples, and to promote peace, happiness and prosperity."

On the eve of his departure from Moscow—on the evening of June 20—the Crown Prince of Yemen delivered a television broadcast speech on this visit. He said: "The peoples of the Soviet Union and Yemen have a long history of friendship, and this friendship is constantly strengthening. The consistency of our views and the development of international events have consolidated and deepened this friendship."

### 也门太子离莫斯科去民主德国

1956-06-26
第4版()
专栏：

    也门太子离莫斯科去民主德国
    新华社据塔斯社莫斯科讯　也门皇太子、副首相兼外交大臣塞弗·伊斯兰·穆罕默德·巴德尔和他的随行人员，六月二十五日离莫斯科前往德意志民主共和国。
    巴德尔在机场上说：“我一辈子也忘不了我同苏联人民以及同他们的领导人之间的真诚的友谊联系。我为这种友谊而感到自豪，并且将竭尽全力发展和巩固这种友谊，以促进两国人民之间的密切合作，促进和平和幸福繁荣。”
    也门皇太子在离开莫斯科的前夕——六月二十日晚上，就这次访问发表了电视广播演说。他说：“苏联和也门两国人民有着悠久的友谊，这种友谊正在不断加强。我们观点的一致和国际事件的发展，巩固和加深了这种友谊。”
