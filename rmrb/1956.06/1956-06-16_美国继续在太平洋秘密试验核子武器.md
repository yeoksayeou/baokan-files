Title: US Continues Secret Nuclear Weapons Testing in the Pacific
Author:
Date: 1956-06-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, June 14th: The United States Atomic Energy Commission announced on June 11th that the United States will continue a series of "unannounced" nuclear weapons tests in the Pacific Trust Territory.

Prior to this, Japanese scientists had already revealed the fact that the United States was secretly conducting hydrogen bomb tests. They pointed out that the United States secretly detonated a hydrogen bomb in the Pacific as early as May 28th.

### 美国继续在太平洋秘密试验核子武器

1956-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    美国继续在太平洋秘密试验核子武器
    新华社14日讯　华盛顿消息：美国原子能委员会六月十一日说，美国要继续在太平洋托管地区进行一系列“不予宣布”的核子武器试验。
    在这以前，日本科学家们早已揭露美国秘密进行氢弹试验的事实。他们指出，美国在五月二十八日就在太平洋秘密爆炸了一颗氢弹。
