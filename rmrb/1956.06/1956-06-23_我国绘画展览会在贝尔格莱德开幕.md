Title: Chinese Painting Exhibition Opens in Belgrade
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd: According to a report from the Yugoslav Telegraph Agency, a Chinese painting exhibition opened in Belgrade on the evening of the 21st.

This was organized by the Yugoslav National Commission of the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization and the Serbian Artists Association.

The exhibition features forty paintings of various sizes and twenty sculptures by forty Chinese artists. These works were created by Chinese artists from the fourth century to the early nineteenth century.

Žečević, Chairman of the Federal Chamber of the National Assembly of Yugoslavia, attended the opening ceremony of the exhibition.

### 我国绘画展览会在贝尔格莱德开幕

1956-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

    我国绘画展览会在贝尔格莱德开幕
    新华社22日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道：中国绘画展览会二十一日晚间在贝尔格莱德开幕。
    这是联合国教育、科学及文化组织南斯拉夫国家委员会和塞尔维亚艺术家协会组织的。
    展览会展出了四十位中国艺术家的四十幅各种尺寸的绘画和二十件雕刻品。这些作品是第四世纪到十九世纪初期中国艺术家们所创作的。
    南斯拉夫国民议会联邦院主席泽契维奇出席了展览会的开幕仪式。
