Title: Shenyang Promotes a Large Number of Cadres at and Above the Section Level and Technical Cadres
Author:
Date: 1956-06-14
Page: 3

Shenyang (By our newspaper) Following the inspection and criticism of right-leaning conservative thinking in the cultivation and promotion of cadres by Party organizations at all levels in Shenyang, from January to mid-May this year, 3,327 cadres at and above the section level and technical cadres were promoted, nearly twice the total number of cadres at the same level promoted throughout last year. Among them were 338 department heads of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, bureau chiefs and section chiefs of the Municipal People's Committee, and Party committee secretaries, general branch secretaries, factory directors, and managers of factories and mines. While promoting a large number of cadres, Party organizations at all levels have also attached importance to the work of cultivating and improving cadres. According to incomplete statistics, there are already 5,368 reserve cadres at and above the section chief level and technical personnel in the city.

### 沈阳提拔大批科级以上干部和技术干部

1956-06-14
第3版()
专栏：

    沈阳提拔大批科级以上干部和技术干部
    本报沈阳讯　沈阳市各级党组织检查和批判了培养和提拔干部中的右倾保守思想后，从今年一月到五月中旬，提拔了科级以上干部和技术干部3，327名，将近去年全年提拔同级干部总数的两倍。其中有中共沈阳市委的部长、市人民委员会的局长、处长、厂矿企业的党委书记、总支书记、厂长、经理338名。在大量提拔干部的同时，各级党组织也重视了培养提高干部的工作，据不完全统计，全市已经有科长级以上和技术人员的后备干部5，368名。
