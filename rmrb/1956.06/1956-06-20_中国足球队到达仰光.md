Title: Chinese Football Team Arrives in Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1956-06-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 19th. The Chinese football team led by Huang Zhong arrived in Rangoon yesterday afternoon from India. They will play two friendly matches with the Burmese football team in Rangoon.

At the airport, the Chinese football team was welcomed by Sheng Wang, Minister of Health of the Union of Burma, heads of the Burma Sports Association and other sports organizations, as well as members of the Burmese national football team who visited China last year.

### 中国足球队到达仰光

1956-06-20
第4版()
专栏：

    中国足球队到达仰光
    新华社仰光19日电　由黄中率领的中国足球队在昨天下午从印度到达仰光。他们将同缅甸足球队在仰光进行两场友谊赛。
    在机场上，中国足球队受到缅甸联邦政府卫生部长盛旺、缅甸体育协会负责人和其他体育组织的代表，以及去年访问过中国的缅甸国家足球队队员的欢迎。
