Title: Steel Materials for First Batch of Automobiles Fully Prepared
Author:
Date: 1956-06-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, June 16th: The various steel materials needed by the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant for the production of the first batch of automobiles in the third quarter have been basically fully prepared.

The steel plants in Dalian, Benxi, Fushun, Anshan, Chongqing and other places supplied the largest amount of steel to the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant, with a total of more than 260 specifications.

In the future, with the needs of the automobile industry's development, steel plants in various places will gradually increase the trial production of new products for the automobile industry, in order to achieve the goal of independent supply.
Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, June 15th: The First Automobile Manufacturing Plant's machine repair workshop has manufactured more than 600 sets of complete special equipment for the entire plant to produce automobiles.

The workers in this machine repair workshop, which has complete technical equipment, have manufactured more than 120 types of special equipment of different specifications for the entire plant. The technical conditions required for these special equipment are very high. The workers in the machine repair workshop, faced with difficulties such as a lack of materials, after repeated trials, generally managed to supply the equipment on time or ahead of schedule, ensuring the needs of equipment installation projects and production adjustments in various major workshops.

### 生产首批汽车的钢材准备齐全

1956-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

    生产首批汽车的钢材准备齐全
    新华社长春16日电　第一汽车制造厂在第三季度生产第一批汽车所需要的各种钢材已经基本准备齐全。
    大连、本溪、抚顺、鞍山、重庆等地钢厂为第一汽车制造厂供应的钢材数量最多，共有二百六十多种规格。
    今后随着汽车工业发展的需要，各地钢铁厂为汽车工业试制新产品将逐年增多，以达到独立供应的目标。
    据新华社长春15日电　第一汽车制造厂机修车间已经为全厂制造出六百多台生产汽车的成套专用设备。
    这个有着完善技术装备的机修车间的工人，为全厂制造出一百二十多种不同规格的专用设备。这些专用设备的技术条件要求很高。机修车间的工人们，在材料缺乏等困难情况下，经反复多次试制以后，一般都做到了按时和提前供应设备，保证了各个主要车间设备安装工程和调整生产的需要。
