Title: President of the Sino-Pakistan Friendship Association Hosts Banquet for Pakistani Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-06-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 26th. Liu Geping, President of the Sino-Pakistan Friendship Association, hosted a banquet tonight in honor of the Pakistani journalists delegation to China, headed by Faiz Ahmed Faiz.

    Pakistani Ambassador to China, A.H.B. Tyabji, and embassy staff were also invited to attend the banquet.

    Liu Geping delivered a speech at the banquet, welcoming the Pakistani journalists delegation and expressing his regret that the delegation would soon be leaving China. Liu Geping said that the delegation's visit to China would help further strengthen the friendly relations between China and Pakistan, and he believed that the delegation would bring the friendship of the Chinese people to the Pakistani people upon their return home.

    Delegation leader Faiz expressed his honor at the delegation's opportunity to participate in the founding meeting of the Sino-Pakistan Friendship Association and tonight's banquet. He said that contact and understanding between China and Pakistan were increasing, and that the increasing mutual understanding between the two peoples would promote friendship between the two countries, thereby helping to consolidate world peace. Faiz concluded by wishing the Sino-Pakistan Friendship Association success in its work to promote friendship and cultural exchange between China and Pakistan.

    Those present at tonight's banquet included the Vice-Presidents of the Sino-Pakistan Friendship Association, Iminov and Qian Duansheng, and its directors, as well as the heads of the All-China Journalists Association, the China Islamic Association, and the China Hui Culture Advancement Association.

### 中巴友协会长设宴招待巴基斯坦新闻工作者代表团

1956-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中巴友协会长设宴招待巴基斯坦新闻工作者代表团
    新华社26日讯　中巴友好协会会长刘格平今晚举行宴会，招待以费兹·艾哈迈德·费兹为首的巴基斯坦新闻工作者访华代表团。
    巴基斯坦驻华大使阿哈默德和使馆人员也应邀出席了宴会。
    刘格平在宴会上致词，欢迎巴基斯坦新闻工作者访华代表团，并且对代表团即将离开中国表示惜别。刘格平说，代表团的访问中国，有助于进一步加强中巴两国的友好关系，他相信代表团回国后将会把中国人民的友谊带给巴基斯坦人民。
    费兹团长表示对代表团有机会参加中巴友好协会成立大会和今晚的宴会感到荣幸。他说，中巴两国的接触和了解正在增进，两国人民相互了解的增进将促进两国人民的友谊，从而有助于巩固世界和平。费兹最后祝中巴友好协会在促进中巴两国友好和文化交流的工作中获得成功。
    出席今晚宴会的，有中巴友好协会副会长伊敏诺夫、钱端升和理事，中国新闻工作者联谊会、中国伊斯兰教协会和中国回民文化协进会的负责人。
