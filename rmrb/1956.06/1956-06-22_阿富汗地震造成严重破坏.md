Title: Afghanistan Earthquake Causes Severe Damage
Author:
Date: 1956-06-22
Page: 4

    Afghanistan Earthquake Causes Severe Damage

    Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, 19th: According to Kabul Radio, the recent earthquake in Afghanistan has caused severe damage, with more than 2,000 casualties.

    In the Kenhar River basin, entire mountains have collapsed, and it is said that all the villages at the foot of the mountains have been buried.

### 阿富汗地震造成严重破坏

1956-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

    阿富汗地震造成严重破坏
    新华社19日讯　据喀布尔电台报道，最近阿富汗发生的地震使阿富汗遭到了严重的破坏，因地震而伤亡的有两千多人。
    在肯哈尔河流域，整个山都崩溃了，据说山脚下的所有村庄都被埋没。
