Title: Czechoslovak Communist Party National Conference Opens
Author:
Date: 1956-06-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 12th. The National Conference of the Czechoslovak Communist Party opened on the afternoon of June 11th.

　　Sapotocky delivered the opening speech at the meeting, saying that the discussions and results of this conference will undoubtedly have particularly significant implications for the further development of our country's situation. When talking about the discussions conducted in various regions before the conference, he said: "This discussion has stimulated the enthusiasm of all Party organizations and the broad masses of Party members to an unprecedented extent. At the same time, this discussion has also fully confirmed that our socialist construction has made great progress, and that socialism in our country has also gradually become a recognized and indestructible system."

　　Sapotocky emphasized the correctness of the Party's general line established by the 10th Congress of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and pointed out: "This conference must and will greatly change and correct those situations that contradict and violate this general line."

　　The conference agenda adopted by the delegates included: 1. Election of various committees; 2. Report on the current situation and the tasks of the Party (reporter: Novotny); 3. Report on the draft instructions of the Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee on the development of the national economy's second five-year plan (1956-1960) (reporter: Siroky); 4. Reports from various committees.

　　Subsequently, Novotny, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, delivered a report.

### 捷克斯洛伐克共产党全国代表会议开幕

1956-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克共产党全国代表会议开幕
    新华社布拉格12日电　捷克斯洛伐克共产党全国代表会议在六月十一日下午开幕。
    萨波托斯基在会上致开幕词，他说，这次代表会议的讨论和结果，将无可争论地对于我国的情况的进一步发展具有特别重大的意义。在谈到代表会议举行以前各地所进行的讨论的时候，他说：“这种讨论在前所未有的程度上激起了全体党组织和广大党员群众的积极性。同时，这种讨论也完全证实了：我国社会主义建设已经取得了很大的进展，而且，社会主义在我国也同样逐渐变成公认的不可破坏的制度。”
    萨波托斯基强调指出了捷共第十次代表大会所确立的党的总路线的正确性，并且指出：“这次代表会议必须而且一定会大大地改变和纠正那些和这个总路线相抵触和违背这个总路线的状况。”
    代表们通过的会议议程包括：一、选举各委员会；二、关于目前的形势和党的任务的报告（报告人：诺沃提尼）；三、捷共中央关于发展国民经济第二个五年计划（1956—1960年）的指示草案的报告（报告人：西罗基）；四、各委员会的报告。
    随后，捷共中央第一书记诺沃提尼作了报告。
