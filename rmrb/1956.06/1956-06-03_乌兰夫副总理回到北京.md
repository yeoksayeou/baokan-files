Title: Vice Premier Ulanhu Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-06-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Vice Premier Ulanhu led the Chinese delegation that attended the coronation ceremony of the King of Nepal and returned to Beijing tonight.
    Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Chairman of the National People's Congress Nationalities Committee Liu Geping, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission Liu Chun, and Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Jiakang welcomed them at the railway station.

### 乌兰夫副总理回到北京

1956-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    乌兰夫副总理回到北京
    新华社2日讯　由乌兰夫副总理率领的参加尼泊尔国王加冕典礼的中国代表团，已在今晚回到北京。
    国务院副总理贺龙、秘书长习仲勋，全国人民代表大会民族委员会主任委员刘格平，民族事务委员会副主任刘春和外交部部长助理陈家康等到火车站欢迎。
