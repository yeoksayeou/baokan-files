Title: Hunan Launches Comprehensive Survey of Schistosomiasis

Author:
Date: 1956-06-04
Page: 3

Hunan comprehensively investigates schistosomiasis

Yueyang, Wangcheng, Yuanjiang and other lake areas in Hunan Province are preparing to conduct a comprehensive survey of schistosomiasis. Yiyang City and Wangcheng County have organized working groups to conduct investigations in key townships. Yueyang County has organized a skin test injection team of twenty-five people. From May 15th to 17th, it has investigated the schistosomiasis situation in six agricultural cooperatives in Liuyu Township. In early May, Yuanjiang County investigated the distribution of Oncomelania snails on 180,000 mu of lakeside wilderness.

This large-scale survey of schistosomiasis in 17 counties and cities throughout Hunan Province was a major task determined at the First Hunan Provincial Schistosomiasis Prevention and Control Work Conference not long ago. Through a comprehensive survey, reliable data can be obtained, so that the comprehensive prevention and control of schistosomiasis can be carried out more effectively and quickly in the future. (Our correspondent in Changsha)

### 湖南全面调查血吸虫病

1956-06-04
第3版()
专栏：

    湖南全面调查血吸虫病
    湖南省岳阳、望城、沅江等滨湖地区，正在准备对血吸虫病作一次全面调查。益阳市、望城县都已组织工作组，到重点乡进行调查，岳阳县组织一个由二十五人组成的皮内反应注射大队，从五月十五日到十七日，已在六垸乡调查了六个农业社有关血吸虫病的情况。沅江县在五月初在十八万亩的滨湖荒洲上，调查了钉螺的分布状况。
    这次湖南全省十七个县、市对血吸虫病的大规模调查工作，是不久前湖南省第一次血吸虫病防治工作会议上确定的主要任务。通过全面调查，可以获得可靠的资料，使今后全面的防治血吸虫病工作更有效、更迅速地进行。　（本报驻长沙记者）
