Title: Hungary Begins Construction of Atomic Reactor
Author:
Date: 1956-06-18
Page: 4

    Hungary Begins Construction of Atomic Reactor
    Xinhua News Agency, June 15th dispatch: According to a TASS report on the 15th, Hungary's first atomic reactor has begun construction on Freedom Hill near Budapest.
    This atomic reactor, built with Soviet assistance, will begin producing radioactive isotopes by the end of next year or the beginning of 1958.

### 匈开始建造原子反应堆

1956-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    匈开始建造原子反应堆
    新华社16日讯　据塔斯社十五日报道：匈牙利第一个原子反应堆已经在布达佩斯近郊的自由山上开始建造。
    这个在苏联帮助下建造的原子反应堆，将在明年年底或者1958年年初开始生产放射性同位素。
