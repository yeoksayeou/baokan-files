Title: Joint Communiqué Issued by the Governments of North Korea and Romania: Further Development of Political, Economic, and Cultural Cooperation Between the Two Countries

Author: 
Date: 1956-06-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 18th: Bucharest News: Bucharest newspapers published on June 17th the joint communiqué of the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the government of the Romanian People's Republic.

The communiqué pointed out that the North Korean government delegation exchanged views with the leaders of the Romanian Workers' Party and the government on issues related to the relations between the two countries and some international issues.

The representatives of the two governments especially pointed out that the relations between Romania and North Korea are developing on the basis of mutual understanding, mutual respect, and fraternal cooperation. The relations between the two countries are fully in line with the interests of the people of both countries and contribute to socialist construction and the consolidation of world peace.

The representatives of the two governments advocated further development of political, economic, and cultural cooperation between the two countries. To this end, both sides decided to begin negotiations on the conclusion of a long-term trade agreement and a scientific and technological cooperation agreement.

Romania will supply North Korea with equipment, machinery, and various other products needed to restore the North Korean economy.

The North Korean government delegation expressed deep gratitude to the Romanian people and the people of other brotherly countries for their current and future assistance to the North Korean people in post-war recovery and economic construction.

The representatives of the two governments noted with satisfaction the recent improvement in the international situation. They expressed their conviction that resolving disputes through negotiations and direct contact between national leaders is a way to further ease international tensions, ban weapons of mass destruction, achieve disarmament and consolidate mutual trust between countries, and to achieve collective security in Europe and Asia. The two countries advocate developing trade relations on the basis of mutual benefit, developing cultural relations, and peaceful coexistence with all countries with different social systems.

The representatives of the two governments expressed their belief that the peaceful reunification of Korea on a democratic basis will be a significant contribution to easing international tensions and consolidating peace in the Far East.

The two governments expressed satisfaction that the visit of the North Korean government delegation to Romania and the results of the exchange of views between the two sides will be a major contribution to consolidating the friendship between the Romanian and North Korean people and consolidating peace and international cooperation.

### 朝罗两国政府发表联合公报  要进一步发展两国政治、经济和文化合作

1956-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

    朝罗两国政府发表联合公报
    要进一步发展两国政治、经济和文化合作
    新华社布拉格18日电　布加勒斯特消息：布加勒斯特报纸在六月十七日刊载了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团和罗马尼亚人民共和国政府的联合公报。
    公报指出，朝鲜政府代表团同罗马尼亚工人党和政府的领导人，就两国关系问题和一些国际局势问题交换了意见。
    两国政府代表特别指出：罗马尼亚和朝鲜的关系正在互相谅解、互相尊重、兄弟合作的基础上发展着，两国关系完全符合于两国人民的利益，有助于社会主义建设和世界和平的巩固。
    两国政府代表主张进一步发展两国之间的政治、经济和文化合作。为此，双方决定着手商谈缔结一项长期贸易协定和一项科学技术合作协定。
    罗马尼亚将供给朝鲜以设备、机械和恢复朝鲜的经济所需的其他各种产品。
    朝鲜政府代表团对罗马尼亚人民和其他兄弟国家人民现在和今后对朝鲜人民在战后的恢复和经济建设方面的帮助，表示深切的感谢。
    两国政府代表满意地指出，最近国际情势有了好转。他们表示确信：通过谈判和国家领导人的直接接触来解决争执问题，是进一步缓和国际紧张局势、禁止大规模毁灭性武器、裁军和巩固国与国之间相互信任的方法，是实现欧洲和亚洲集体安全的方法。两国主张在互利的基础上发展贸易关系，主张发展文化关系、主张和不同社会制度的一切国家和平共处。
    两国政府代表表示相信，在民主基础上实现朝鲜的和平统一，将是对缓和国际紧张局势和巩固远东和平的具有重大意义的贡献。
    两国政府表示满意的是：朝鲜政府代表团的访问罗马尼亚和双方交换意见的结果，将是对巩固罗马尼亚人民和朝鲜人民的友谊、巩固和平和国际合作的重大贡献。
