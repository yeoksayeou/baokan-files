Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Issues Appeal to Working People of All Countries to Strive for Peaceful Solution of the Algerian Question
Author:
Date: 1956-06-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 10th. The World Federation of Trade Unions has issued a statement calling on the working people of all countries to strive for a peaceful solution to the Algerian question.

The statement said that more and more troops have been投入 into military operations in Algeria, thus creating a great danger to the relaxation of international tensions and the cause of peace. World public opinion advocates resolving the Algerian question peacefully and in the spirit of the United Nations Charter, avoiding bloodshed, destruction and death, and freeing the Algerian people from poverty and colonial oppression.

The statement called on all Algerian and French working people to act in unison to promote a ceasefire and negotiations in Algeria.

### 世界工联向各国劳动人民发出号召  争取和平解决阿尔及利亚问题

1956-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    世界工联向各国劳动人民发出号召
    争取和平解决阿尔及利亚问题
    新华社布拉格10日电　世界工会联合会发表声明，号召各国劳动人民争取和平解决阿尔及利亚问题。
    声明说，在阿尔及利亚有越来越多的军队投入了军事行动，因而造成了对缓和国际紧张局势和和平事业的巨大危险。世界舆论都主张用和平方式以及本着联合国宪章的精神来解决阿尔及利亚的问题，避免流血、破坏和死亡，并且使阿尔及利亚人民摆脱贫困和殖民压迫。
    声明号召全体阿尔及利亚劳动人民和法国劳动人民一致行动，来促进在阿尔及利亚实行停火，促成谈判。
