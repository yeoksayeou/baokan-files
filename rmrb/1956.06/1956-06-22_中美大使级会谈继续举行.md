Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, June 21st: The Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial talks held their 51st meeting at the Palais des Nations at 10:00 a.m. on June 21st. The meeting adjourned at 11:55 a.m. The next meeting is scheduled for June 28th.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1956-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦21日电　中美两国大使级会谈六月二十一日十时在国联大厦举行第五十一次会议，会议在十一时五十五分休会。下次会议定在六月二十八日举行。
