Title: Shepilov to Visit Egypt at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1956-06-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 9th: It was announced here that Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov, at the invitation of the Egyptian government, will travel to Egypt soon to participate in Egypt's National Day celebrations.

### 谢皮洛夫应邀将访问埃及

1956-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    谢皮洛夫应邀将访问埃及
    新华社莫斯科9日电　此间宣布，苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫应埃及政府的邀请，将在最近前往埃及参加埃及的国庆典礼。
