Title: Finnish Ambassador to China Holds Reception to Welcome Finnish Parliamentary Delegation

Author:
Date: 1956-06-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd: Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai all attended the reception held tonight by the Finnish Ambassador to China, Mr. G. J. Sundström, to welcome the Finnish Parliamentary Delegation to China.

At the reception, the head of the delegation, Väinö Johannes Sukselainen, said to Vice Chairman Zhu De: "Today, we spent this Midsummer Day in the Summer Palace of China. It was a pleasant day." Vice Chairman Chen Shutong also told the distinguished Finnish guests that he spent Midsummer Day in Finland last year and visited the Finnish Parliament.

Chairman Liu Shaoqi talked with the guests about the National People's Congress of our country and the Finnish Parliament, as well as some information about the construction of both countries. Vice Chairmen Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Peng Zhen, and Li Weihan also held talks with the guests successively.

When Premier Zhou Enlai met the distinguished guests, he said warmly: "We welcome you with warm hearts." Afterwards, he had a free discussion with the guests about China's economic construction.

Today, President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, Vice Premiers of the State Council He Long and Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian and other responsible persons from relevant ministries, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Cheng Qian, and Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun also attended this welcoming reception.

At the reception, Ambassador Sundström introduced the delegation to the diplomatic and commercial representatives of various countries in China, and to members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of our country and others.

### 芬兰驻华大使举行酒会  欢迎芬兰议会访华代表团

1956-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    芬兰驻华大使举行酒会    
    欢迎芬兰议会访华代表团
    据新华社23日讯　朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理今晚都出席了芬兰驻华大使孙士敦为欢迎芬兰议会访华代表团而举行的酒会。
    代表团团长维埃诺·约翰奈斯·苏克舍拉宁在酒会上对朱德副主席说：今天白天，我们在中国的夏宫——颐和园度过了这个仲夏节。这是一个愉快的日子。陈叔通副委员长也告诉芬兰的贵宾们，他去年在芬兰度过仲夏节，并且参观了芬兰的议会。
    刘少奇委员长同客人们交谈了我国全国人民代表大会和芬兰议会方面以及两国建设方面的一些情况。副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、彭真、李维汉也先后同客人们进行了交谈。
    周恩来总理同贵宾们见面的时候，热情地说：我们用热的心欢迎你们。随后，他同贵宾们畅谈了关于我国的经济建设情况。
    今天，最高人民法院院长董必武，国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅，外交部副部长张闻天和其他有关各部的负责人，国防委员会副主席程潜，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧也来参加了这个欢迎酒会。
    在酒会上，孙士敦大使介绍代表团同各国驻中国的外交代表和商务代表见了面，同我国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员等人见了面。
