Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Hosts Nepalese Ambassador Rana
Author:
Date: 1956-06-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 14th. Premier Zhou Enlai held a reception this afternoon to host Lieutenant General Rana, the first Nepalese Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, First Secretary Banthari, and Military Attaché Basnyat.
    At the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai introduced Ambassador Rana to leading members of Chinese state organs, heads of various departments, generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and well-known figures from all walks of life for meetings and conversations. These included Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yun, He Long, Ulanhu, and Li Xiannian, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, and Saifudin, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei.
    At the reception, Ambassador Rana and others also met and talked with diplomatic envoys, commercial representatives, and military attachés from various countries stationed in China.
    In the evening, Ambassador Rana and others attended a song and dance Peking opera performance.

### 周总理招待尼泊尔大使拉纳

1956-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理招待尼泊尔大使拉纳
    新华社14日讯　周恩来总理在今天下午举行酒会，招待尼泊尔王国驻中国首任特命全权大使拉纳中将、一等秘书班特瑞和武官巴斯尼亚特等。
    在酒会上，周恩来总理介绍拉纳大使同中国国家机关的领导人员和各部门负责人、中国人民解放军的将军们及社会各界著名人士会面交谈。其中有国务院副总理陈云、贺龙、乌兰夫、李先念，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李维汉、陈叔通、赛福鼎，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞和外交部副部长姬鹏飞。
    在酒会上，拉纳大使等还同各国驻华的使节、商务代表和武官会面交谈。
    晚间，拉纳大使等出席了歌舞京剧晚会。
