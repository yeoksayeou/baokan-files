Title: Australian Labor MP Calls on Government to Send Delegation to Visit China, Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-06-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Canberra: Australian Labor Senator Justin O'Byrne told the Senate on the 19th that other Commonwealth countries have already taken the lead over Australia in conducting new trade with communist countries.

He called on the government to send goodwill and trade delegations to visit countries such as the Soviet Union, China, and Czechoslovakia.

### 澳工党议员要求政府  派代表团访问中苏捷等国

1956-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

    澳工党议员要求政府
    派代表团访问中苏捷等国
    据新华社讯　勘培拉消息：澳大利亚工党参议员杰斯丁·奥伯尔尼19日对参议院说，英联邦其他国家在同共产党国家进行新的贸易方面已经跑在澳大利亚的前面。
    他要求政府派遣亲善代表团和贸易代表团去访问苏联、中国和捷克斯洛伐克等国家。
