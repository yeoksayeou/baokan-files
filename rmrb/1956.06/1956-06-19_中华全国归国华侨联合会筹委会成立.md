Title: Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese Established
Author:
Date: 1956-06-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese was established in Beijing.

    On the 17th, the Beijing Returned Overseas Chinese Association held a forum to discuss the establishment of the Federation's Preparatory Committee. At the meeting, Chen Jiageng was elected as the Chairman of the Preparatory Committee, and Fang Fang, You Yangzu, Wang Yuanxing, Zhuang Xiquan, Zhuang Mingli, Li Tiemin, Luo Lishi, Yi Meihou, Gao Mingxuan, Huang Changshui, Peng Zemin, and Yan Zijun were elected as the twelve Vice-Chairmen. It was also decided to reserve a Vice-Chairman position for Returned Overseas Chinese from Burma. The meeting also elected eighty members of the Preparatory Committee.
    Following this, the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese held its first meeting, electing thirty-seven standing committee members and appointing Zhuang Mingli as Secretary-General and five Deputy Secretaries-General.

### 中华全国归国华侨联合会筹委会成立

1956-06-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国归国华侨联合会筹委会成立
    据新华社17日讯　中华全国归国华侨联合会筹备委员会在北京成立。
    十七日，北京归国华侨联谊会为商讨成立联合会筹备委员会的事宜举行了座谈会。会上，推选陈嘉庚为筹备委员会主任委员，推选方方、尤扬祖、王源兴、庄希泉、庄明理、李铁民、罗理实、蚁美厚、高明轩、黄长水、彭泽民、颜子俊等十二人为副主任委员，并且决定为缅甸归国华侨保留一个副主任委员名额。会上还推选了筹备委员八十人。
    中华全国归国华侨联合会筹备委员会接着举行了第一次会议，选出了三十七位常务委员，推出庄明理为秘书长以及副秘书长五人。
