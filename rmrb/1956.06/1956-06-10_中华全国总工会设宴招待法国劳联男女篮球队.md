Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Hosts Banquet for French Labor Federation Men's and Women's Basketball Teams
Author:
Date: 1956-06-10
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 9th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions hosted a banquet tonight for all members of the French Labor Sports Federation men's and women's basketball teams.
    Zhu Xuefan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, delivered a speech at the banquet. He extended a warm welcome to the first workers' sports team from faraway France. Rousseau, the leader of the French Labor Federation men's and women's basketball teams, also spoke. He said that they were very grateful for the warm reception from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and that they were very happy to be able to be with Chinese workers' organizations.
    Also present at the banquet today were Zhang Xiuzhu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Rong Gaotang, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, and representatives of eighteen sports associations in China.

### 中华全国总工会设宴招待法国劳联男女篮球队

1956-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国总工会设宴招待法国劳联男女篮球队
    新华社9日讯　中华全国总工会今天晚上举行宴会，招待法国劳动体育联合会男女篮球队全体人员。
    中华全国总工会副主席朱学范在宴会上致词。他对来自遥远的法国的第一个工人的体育队伍表示热烈欢迎。法国劳联男女篮球队领队卢梭也讲了话。他说，他们非常感谢中华全国总工会的热情招待，他们能够跟中国工人组织在一起感到非常高兴。
    今天出席宴会的还有中华全国总工会书记处书记张修竹，中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠，以及中国的十八个体育协会的代表。
