Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Writes Letters to U Nu and U Ba Swe
Author:
Date: 1956-06-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 21st. Premier Zhou Enlai wrote letters on June 19th to U Nu, Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma, and U Ba Swe, the newly appointed Prime Minister of the Union of Burma.

The letter to U Nu said: "During your tenure as Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, you made tremendous contributions to enhancing friendly cooperation between China and Burma with your outstanding statesmanlike talents. The Chinese people will never forget all the efforts you have made as one of the initiators of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence to support the international status of New China, consolidate and develop Sino-Burmese friendly relations, promote Asian solidarity, and safeguard world peace.

I wish you greater achievements in leading the work of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League and make new and significant contributions to the friendly unity between China and Burma."

The letter to Prime Minister U Ba Swe said: "I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency on your assumption of the office of Prime Minister of the Union of Burma on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China.

The friendly and cooperative relations between China and Burma have achieved increasingly remarkable development on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. The strengthening of this friendly and cooperative relationship will make greater contributions to the cause of safeguarding peace in Asia and the world."

### 周恩来总理写信给吴努和吴巴瑞

1956-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理写信给吴努和吴巴瑞
    新华社21日讯　周恩来总理十九日写信给缅甸反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努和新任缅甸联邦总理吴巴瑞。
    给吴努的信说：“在你担任缅甸联邦政府总理的期间，你以卓越的政治家的才能，为增进中缅两国的友好合作作出了巨大的贡献。中国人民对于你作为和平共处五项原则倡议人之一，为支持新中国的国际地位、巩固和发展中缅友好关系、促进亚洲团结、维护世界和平所作的一切努力，是永远不会忘记的。
    祝你在领导反法西斯人民自由同盟的工作中获得更大的成就，并为中缅两国的友好团结作出新的重大贡献。”
    给吴巴瑞总理的信中说：“阁下荣任缅甸联邦政府总理，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向阁下致热诚的祝贺。
    中缅两国的友好合作关系已经在和平共处五项原则的基础上取得了日益显著的发展。这种友好合作关系的加强，将对维护亚洲和世界和平的事业作出更大的贡献。”
