Title: Romanian New Ambassador Chioloiu Visits Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Wentian
Author:
Date: 1956-06-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 27th: The new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Romanian People's Republic to China, Nicolae Chioloiu, paid a courtesy call to Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Wentian at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 10:00 AM on June 27th to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Deputy Director of the Protocol Department Wang Yutian was present.

### 罗马尼亚新大使乔洛尤  拜会外交部副部长张闻天

1956-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚新大使乔洛尤
    拜会外交部副部长张闻天
    新华社27日讯　罗马尼亚人民共和国新任驻华特命全权大使尼古拉·乔洛尤，在六月二十七日上午十时到外交部拜会张闻天副部长，商谈递交国书事宜。在座的有礼宾司副司长王雨田。
