Title: Asian-African Student Conference Credentials Committee Begins Work
Author:
Date: 1956-06-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bandung, June 1st. The Asian-African Student Conference did not hold a plenary session this morning.

After lengthy discussions, the Credentials Committee was finally formed by the original members of the International Preparatory Committee. The International Preparatory Committee was composed of representatives from Indonesia, China, the Philippines, Japan, and Egypt. Indonesia has two representatives on the committee, but only one vote.

The Credentials Committee began its work this morning, and as of 2:00 p.m. local time, it had approved the credentials of the delegations from China, Indonesia, the Philippines, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Egypt.

### 亚非学生会议资格审查委员会开始工作

1956-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    亚非学生会议资格审查委员会开始工作
    新华社万隆1日电　亚非学生会议今天上午没有举行全体会议。
    在长时间的讨论之后，资格审查委员会终于由原来的国际筹备委员会委员组成了。国际筹备委员会是由印度尼西亚、中国、菲律宾、日本和埃及的代表组成的。印度尼西亚在委员会中有两个代表，但是只有一票的投票权。
    资格审查委员会在今天上午开始工作，截至当地时间下午二时止，通过了对中国、印度尼西亚、菲律宾、越南民主共和国和埃及的代表团的审查。
