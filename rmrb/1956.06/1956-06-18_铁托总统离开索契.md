Title: President Tito Leaves Sochi
Author:
Date: 1956-06-18
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Sochi, June 17th: According to TASS, Yugoslav President Tito and his entourage left Sochi, the famous Crimean health resort, on the morning of June 17th.

　　Before their departure, the working people and vacationers of Sochi held a farewell meeting for President Tito. Tito and Soviet First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Mikoyan spoke at the meeting.

　　President Tito arrived in Sochi on the evening of June 13th. He rested and visited the area for a total of three days in Sochi.

### 铁托总统离开索契

1956-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    铁托总统离开索契
    据新华社17日讯　据塔斯社索契讯：南斯拉夫总统铁托和他的随行人员，在六月十七日上午离开了克里米亚的著名疗养地索契。
    在他们动身以前，索契市的劳动人民和休养者为铁托总统举行了欢送会。铁托和苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬在会上讲了话。
    铁托总统是在六月十三日晚到索契的。他在索契共休息和参观了三天。
