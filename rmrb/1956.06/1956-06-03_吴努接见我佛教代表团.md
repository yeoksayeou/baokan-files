Title: U Nu Receives Chinese Buddhist Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-06-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, June 2nd: Prime Minister U Nu received the Chinese Buddhist delegation led by Huba Meng at his residence on the afternoon of June 1st.

U Nu held a friendly 40-minute discussion with the delegation on Buddhist issues. The delegation presented gifts to U Nu and Madame Nu.

### 吴努接见我佛教代表团

1956-06-03
第4版()
专栏：

    吴努接见我佛教代表团
    据新华社仰光2日电　吴努总理六月一日下午在他的官邸接见了以祜巴猛为首的中国佛教代表团。
    吴努同代表团就佛教问题进行了四十分钟的友好会谈。代表团向吴努和努夫人赠送了礼物。
