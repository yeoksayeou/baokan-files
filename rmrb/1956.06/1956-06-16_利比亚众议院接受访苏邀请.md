Title: Libyan House of Representatives Accepts Invitation to Visit Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-06-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tripoli, June 15th (TASS dispatch): "Tarabulus al-Gharb" newspaper reports that the Libyan House of Representatives held a meeting on June 13th. The meeting discussed the invitation from the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to a delegation from the Libyan Parliament to visit the Soviet Union. The House of Representatives approved the acceptance of this invitation.

### 利比亚众议院接受访苏邀请

1956-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    利比亚众议院接受访苏邀请
    新华社15日讯　塔斯社的黎波里讯：“塔拉勃卢斯加布”报消息，利比亚国会众议院六月十三日举行会议，会议上讨论了苏联最高苏维埃主席团邀请利比亚国会代表团访苏的问题。众议院通过接受这项邀请。
