Title: A Batch of Henan Youth Reclamation Teams Arrives in the Manas River Basin
Author:
Date: 1956-06-18
Page: 2

Urumqi Dispatch: On June 10th, the Ula Usun Farm in the Manas River Basin was immersed in a joyful atmosphere of welcoming the youth volunteer reclamation team. When more than a thousand young reclamation workers from Gong County, Henan, arrived at their destination, the farm's commanders and male and female soldiers lined up to warmly welcome them. This group of young people is part of the more than 40,000 volunteer reclamation workers from Henan who came to the Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps this year.

### 河南一批青年垦荒队到达玛纳斯河流域

1956-06-18
第2版()
专栏：

    河南一批青年垦荒队到达玛纳斯河流域
    本报乌鲁木齐电　六月十日，玛纳斯河流域的乌拉乌苏农场，浸浴在迎接青年志愿垦荒队的欢乐气氛里。当一千余名从河南巩县来的青年垦荒者到达目的地的时候，农场的指挥员们、男女战士们列队热情地欢迎他们。这批青年是今年从河南来新疆生产建设部队的四万余名志愿垦荒者的一部分。
