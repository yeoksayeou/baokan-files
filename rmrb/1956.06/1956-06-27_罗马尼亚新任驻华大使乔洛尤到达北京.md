Title: New Romanian Ambassador to China, Chioaroiu, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-06-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 26th -- The new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Romanian People's Republic to China, Nicolae Chioaroiu, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 26th. Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was at the airport to greet him.

Also at the airport to welcome him were Mentianu, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Romanian Embassy in China, diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries in China.

Ke Bonian, the Chinese Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Romania, also went to welcome him.

### 罗马尼亚新任驻华大使乔洛尤到达北京

1956-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚新任驻华大使乔洛尤到达北京
    新华社26日讯　罗马尼亚人民共和国新任驻中国特命全权大使尼古拉·乔洛尤在二十六日下午到达北京。前往机场迎接的有外交部礼宾司司长柯华。
    到机场迎接的还有罗马尼亚驻华大使馆临时代办孟天努、各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    中国驻罗马尼亚特命全权大使柯柏年也前往迎接。
