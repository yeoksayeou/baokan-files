Title: Voroshilov Receives Tito
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-04
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 3rd - TASS reports: The Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Voroshilov, received the President of Yugoslavia, Tito, on the 2nd and held friendly and sincere talks with him.
　　Accompanying Tito at the reception were the Vice-Chairman of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia, Kardelj; the State Secretary for Foreign Affairs, Popović; the member of the Federal Executive Council, Todorović; the Chairman of the Executive Council of the People's Republic of Croatia, Blažević; and the Yugoslav Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Mićunović.
　　Present at the reception on the Soviet side were: the Soviet Minister of Foreign Affairs, Shepilov; the Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Begov; and the Soviet Ambassador to Yugoslavia, Filyubin, and others. (Attached picture)
　　Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Voroshilov, received the President of Yugoslavia, Tito, and his entourage on June 2nd. In the center is Voroshilov, and the first person on the left row from the right is Tito. TASS telephoto (Xinhua News Agency)

### 伏罗希洛夫接见铁托

1956-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫接见铁托
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫，在二日接见了南斯拉夫总统铁托，并且和他进行了友好而诚恳的谈话。
    陪同铁托受接见的有南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席卡德尔，外交国务秘书长波波维奇，联邦执行委员会委员托多罗维奇，克罗地亚人民共和国执行委员会主席布拉热维奇和南斯拉夫驻苏联大使米丘诺维奇。
    接见时，苏联方面在座的有：苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫，苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书别哥夫和苏联驻南斯拉夫大使费留宾等人。（附图片）
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫六月二日接见南斯拉夫总统铁托及随行人员。中是伏罗希洛夫，左排右起第一人是铁托。　塔斯社传真照片（新华社发）
