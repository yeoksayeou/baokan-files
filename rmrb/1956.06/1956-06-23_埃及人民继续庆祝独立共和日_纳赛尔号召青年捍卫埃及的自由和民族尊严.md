Title: Egyptian People Continue to Celebrate Independence Republic Day Nasser Calls on Youth to Defend Egypt's Freedom and National Dignity
Author:
Date: 1956-06-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd, TASS Cairo, June 21st: The Egyptian people continue to celebrate Independence Republic Day. Mass rallies were held in Cairo, Alexandria, Tanta, Asyut, and the Suez Canal Zone, and mass garden parties were organized.

On June 20th, a grand youth sports meet was held at the "National Sports Club" stadium in Cairo. Egyptian Prime Minister Gamal Abdel Nasser, members of the Revolutionary Guidance Committee, and ministers attended the sports meet. Students from primary and secondary schools, universities, and other schools, members of workers' sports clubs, and athletes from the National Guard performed mass sports displays in the stadium.

A sports parade followed, with more than 30,000 athletes participating. Prime Minister Gamal Abdel Nasser delivered a speech to the youth, receiving a warm welcome from those present. Countless people rhythmically chanted "Long live Gamal Abdel Nasser!" "Long live independence!" "Long live free Egypt!"

Prime Minister Gamal Abdel Nasser congratulated the youth on their achievements in sports and wished the young athletes further success in sports and labor. He said that now that we have achieved independence, the youth have a major responsibility to defend and consolidate the cause of independence. We must defend our victory, freedom, and our national dignity.

Minister of Education Kamal El-Din Hussein also spoke to those present.

### 埃及人民继续庆祝独立共和日  纳赛尔号召青年捍卫埃及的自由和民族尊严

1956-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及人民继续庆祝独立共和日
    纳赛尔号召青年捍卫埃及的自由和民族尊严
    新华社22日讯　塔斯社开罗二十一日讯：埃及人民继续庆祝独立共和日。在开罗、亚历山大港、坦塔、亚西乌特和苏彝士运河区等地都举行了群众集会，并且组织了群众游园会。
    六月二十日在开罗的“国民体育俱乐部”运动场举行了盛大的青年运动会。出席运动会的有埃及总理加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔、革命指导委员会的委员和部长们。中小学、高等学校和其他学校的学生、工人体育俱乐部会员和国家近卫军的运动员在运动场上作了群众性的体育表演。
    接着举行了体育检阅，参加检阅的有三万多名运动员。总理加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔向青年们发表了演说，他受到了到会者的热烈欢迎。无数人有节奏地高呼“加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔万岁！”“独立万岁！”“自由的埃及万岁！”
    加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔总理祝贺青年们在运动方面获得的成就，并且预祝青年运动员们在运动和劳动中获得进一步的成功。他说，在我们已经取得了独立的今天，青年们对捍卫和巩固独立的事业负有重大责任。我们应当捍卫自己的胜利、自由和自己的民族尊严。
    教育部长卡迈勒丁·侯赛尼也对到会的人讲了话。
