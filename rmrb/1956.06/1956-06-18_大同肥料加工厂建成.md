Title: Datong Fertilizer Processing Plant Completed
Author:
Date: 1956-06-18
Page: 2

    Datong Fertilizer Processing Plant Completed
    (Our report) The Datong City Fertilizer Processing Plant, which began construction in April this year, was completed and put into production in early June.
    This fertilizer processing plant uses urban feces and garbage to produce mixed fertilizer and granular fertilizer. This year, the plant will produce 1 million jin of granular fertilizer and 690,000 dan of mixed fertilizer. These two types of fertilizer can be used for more than 60,000 mu of land.

### 大同肥料加工厂建成

1956-06-18
第2版()
专栏：

    大同肥料加工厂建成
    本报讯　今年四月份动工兴建的大同市肥料加工厂，在六月初建成并投入生产。
    这个肥料加工厂，利用城市的粪便、垃圾生产杂肥和颗粒肥料。今年，这个厂将生产颗粒肥料一百万斤，杂肥六十九万担，两种肥料可以供六万多亩土地使用。
