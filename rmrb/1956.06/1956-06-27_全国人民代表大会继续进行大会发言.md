Title: National People's Congress Continues Plenary Speeches
Author:
Date: 1956-06-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 26th. The Third Session of the First National People's Congress continued its plenary meeting on the afternoon of the 26th. Seventeen People's Representatives and responsible persons from various state organs spoke at the meeting (full texts of the speeches will be published separately). They were: Minister of Food Zhang Naiqi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Huang Yanpei, Minister of Forest Industry Luo Longji, Chen Jiageng, Representative Yaoxi Zeren Zhuoma from the Tibetan Plateau, Representative Yimin Nuofu from the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, Guan Baojia from the Haixi Mongol Tibetan Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture of Qinghai Province, religious figure Chen Jianzhen, Director of the Central Meteorological Bureau Tu Changwang, Director of the Institute of Hydrobiology Wang Jiaji, Governor of Sichuan Province Li Dazhang, agricultural labor model Geng Changsuo, Bayanhu from Heilongjiang Province, educators Zhang Dexin and Ye Xichun (traditional Chinese medicine practitioner from Zhejiang), Vice Mayor of Tianjin Zhou Shutao, and industrialist and businessman Wang Shaoyan from Yunnan.
The meeting concluded at 7:30 PM today. The executive chairmen presiding over today's meeting were: Li Weihan, Shao Lizi, Gao Chongmin, Liao Chengzhi, Zhang Guohua, Zhu Dehai, and Gong Tianmin.

### 全国人民代表大会继续进行大会发言

1956-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会继续进行大会发言
    据新华社26日讯　第一届全国人民代表大会第三次会议二十六日下午继续举行全体会议，在会议上发言的人民代表和国家机关各单位的负责人共有十七人（发言全文另发）。他们是：粮食部长章乃器，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长黄炎培，森林工业部长罗隆基，陈嘉庚，来自西藏高原的代表尧西·泽仁卓玛，来自新疆维吾尔自治区的代表依敏诺夫，来自青海省海西蒙藏哈萨克自治州的代表官保加，宗教界人士陈见真，中央气象局局长凃长望，水生生物研究所所长王家楫，四川省省长李大章，农业劳动模范耿长锁，来自黑龙江省的巴彦胡，文教工作者张德馨、浙江中医叶熙春，天津市副市长周叔韬，云南工商业者王少岩。
    今天的会议在下午七时半结束。主持今天会议的执行主席是：李维汉、邵力子、高崇民、廖承志、张国华、朱德海、巩天民。
