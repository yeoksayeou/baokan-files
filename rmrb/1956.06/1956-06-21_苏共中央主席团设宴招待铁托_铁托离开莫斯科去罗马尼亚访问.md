Title: CPSU Central Committee Presidium Hosts Banquet for Tito, Tito Leaves Moscow to Visit Romania
Author:
Date: 1956-06-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 20th, TASS dispatch: The Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union held a banquet in the Grand Kremlin Palace on June 19th to entertain the leaders of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia who were visiting the Soviet Union.

    The General Secretary of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, Tito; the member of the Executive Committee of the Central Committee, and Secretary of the Central Committee, Kardelj; and members of the Central Committee Blažević, Micunovic, Popovic, Todorovic, and Humo all attended the banquet.

    Representing the Communist Party of the Soviet Union at the banquet were members of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Kirichenko, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Saburov, Khrushchev; alternate members of the Presidium Zhukov, Brezhnev, Mukhitdinov, Furtseva, Shvernik; and Secretaries of the Central Committee Aristov, Belyaev, Pospelov, etc.

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 20th: Yugoslav President Tito and his entourage left Moscow by train at 15:00 (Moscow time) on the 20th. He will travel via Kiev to the People's Republic of Romania for an official visit.

    Leaders of the Communist Party and the government of the Soviet Union, including Bulganin, Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Saburov, Khrushchev, Zhukov, and Furtseva, as well as heads of various departments of the Soviet government and diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union, were at the station to see them off.

    Tito delivered a speech at the station.

    At 3 p.m., the train left the station, and Tito waved continuously from the train.

    Before visiting Romania, President Tito and his party will visit Kiev.

### 苏共中央主席团设宴招待铁托  铁托离开莫斯科去罗马尼亚访问

1956-06-21
第4版()
专栏：

    苏共中央主席团设宴招待铁托
    铁托离开莫斯科去罗马尼亚访问
    新华社据塔斯社莫斯科20日讯　苏联共产党中央委员会主席团，六月十九日在大克里姆林宫举行宴会，招待在苏联访问的南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟的领导人。
    南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟总书记铁托，南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会执行委员会委员、中央委员会书记卡德尔，中央委员会委员布拉热维奇、米丘诺维奇、波波维奇、托多罗维奇和胡莫都出席了宴会。
    代表苏联共产党出席宴会的有苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员布尔加宁、伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、基里钦科、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫，主席团候补委员朱可夫、勃列日涅夫、穆希金诺夫、福尔采娃、什维尔尼克，中央委员会书记阿里斯托夫、别利亚耶夫、波斯伯洛夫等。
    新华社莫斯科20日电　南斯拉夫总统铁托和他的随行人员，在二十日十五时（莫斯科时间）乘火车离开莫斯科。他将取道基辅前往罗马尼亚人民共和国作正式访问。
    到车站送行的有苏联共产党和政府的领导人布尔加宁、伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫、朱可夫、福尔采娃，以及苏联政府各部门首长和各国驻苏联外交使节。
    铁托在车站上讲了话。
    下午三时，火车离开车站，铁托在车上不断招手致意。
    在去罗马尼亚访问以前，铁托总统等一行将在基辅参观。
