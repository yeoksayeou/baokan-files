Title: Singapore's New Government Takes Office; Lim Yew Hock Becomes Chief Minister
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Singapore, June 9th: Singapore's new government, headed by Lim Yew Hock, was sworn in on June 8th.

    In the new Labour Front coalition government, Lim Yew Hock, who serves as Chief Minister, also holds his original position as Minister of Labour and Welfare. Chew Swee Kee serves as Deputy Chief Minister and continues as Minister of Local Government, Lands, and Housing, a post he held previously. The responsibilities of the other ministers remain unchanged.

    Lim Yew Hock stated at a press conference held on the same day that the new government will dedicate itself to strengthening the unity of all parties and the people of Singapore to move towards independence. His new government is prepared to hold new negotiations with Britain to discuss the future of Singapore. He also stated that if his efforts in negotiations with Britain fail, he will resign as Chief Minister.

### 新加坡新政府就职  林有福担任首席部长

1956-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    新加坡新政府就职
    林有福担任首席部长
    据新华社9日讯　新加坡消息：以林有福为首的新加坡新政府在六月八日宣誓就职。
    在新的劳工阵线联合政府中，担任首席部长的林有福，还兼任他原来担任的劳工和福利部部长的职务，朱马特为副首席部长兼任他原来担任的地方政府、土地和房屋部长的职务。其他部长的职务照旧。
    林有福在当天举行的记者招待会上说，新政府将致力加强新加坡各党派和人民的团结走向独立。他的新政府准备和英国举行新的谈判，讨论新加坡的前途问题。他还表示，如果一旦他和英国举行谈判而他的努力失败了的话，他也将辞去首席部长的职务。
