Title: China's Football Team Qualifies for the 16th Olympic Games Finals
Author:
Date: 1956-06-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 2nd: The "Hindustan Standard" reported today that, according to a decision by the Fédération Internationale de Football Association (FIFA), the Chinese football team participating in the Olympic Games football preliminary round has won the right to participate in the 16th Olympic Games football finals in the preliminary round against the Philippine football team for qualification, because the latter could not come to India for the match on the date stipulated by the executive body.

This preliminary round was originally scheduled to be held in Calcutta on June 3rd and 5th. The Chinese football team arrived in Calcutta as early as May 26th. However, the Philippine football team failed to arrive.

The Chinese football team has agreed to hold an exhibition match with India's Mohun Bagan team in Calcutta on June 3rd.

Many other places in India have also requested the Chinese football team to conduct similar matches with Indian football teams.

### 我国足球代表队取得参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会决赛权

1956-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我国足球代表队取得参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会决赛权
    新华社新德里2日电　“印度斯坦旗报”今天报道，根据足球协会国际联合会的决定，参加奥林匹克运动会足球预选赛的中国足球代表队在同菲律宾足球队争取参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会足球决赛资格的预选赛中获得了优胜权，因为后者不能按照执行机关所规定的日期前来印度比赛。
    这次预选赛原定于六月三日和五日在加尔各答举行。中国足球代表队在五月二十六日就已经到了加尔各答。但是，菲律宾足球代表队却没有能到来。
    中国足球代表队已经答应在六月三日同印度的莫汉巴甘队在加尔各答举行表演赛。
    印度其他许多地方也要求中国足球代表队同印度的足球队进行类似的比赛。
