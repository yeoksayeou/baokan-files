Title: Czechoslovakia Recognizes the Independence of Morocco and Tunisia
Author:
Date: 1956-06-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 23rd: According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency on the evening of the 22nd, the Czechoslovak government has recognized Morocco and Tunisia as independent and sovereign states.

This was revealed in telegrams from Czechoslovak Premier Široký to the Prime Ministers of Morocco and Tunisia. The telegrams stated that the Czechoslovak government hopes to establish diplomatic relations with these two countries in order to contribute to the development of peaceful cooperation between all countries.

### 捷承认摩洛哥和突尼斯独立

1956-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    捷承认摩洛哥和突尼斯独立
    新华社布拉格23日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社二十二日晚间报道，捷克斯洛伐克政府已经承认摩洛哥和突尼斯是独立和主权的国家。
    这是捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基在致摩洛哥首相和突尼斯首相的电报中透露的。电报表示，捷克斯洛伐克政府希望同这两个国家建立外交关系，以便对各国之间的和平合作的发展作出贡献。
