Title: Togliatti Leaves Belgrade
Author:
Date: 1956-06-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 31st. TASS reports from Belgrade: Italian Communist Party General Secretary Togliatti left Belgrade on May 30th.

According to Belgrade Radio, Togliatti announced to the press before his departure a joint statement regarding his talks in Belgrade.

The statement said that Comrade Togliatti, General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party, accompanied by Comrade Amadei, a member of the Party's Central Committee, visited Belgrade from May 28th to 30th at the invitation of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia. During the talks with Comrade Tito, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, which were also attended by other members of the Executive Committee of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, and in other meetings, opinions were exchanged on issues of mutual concern and on future contacts to consolidate cooperation between the Italian and Yugoslav Communists. The talks were conducted in a comradely atmosphere.

### 陶里亚蒂离贝尔格莱德

1956-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

    陶里亚蒂离贝尔格莱德
    新华社31日讯　据塔斯社贝尔格莱德讯：意大利共产党总书记陶里亚蒂在五月三十日离开贝尔格莱德。
    据贝尔格莱德电台广播，陶里亚蒂在行前向报界宣布了关于他在贝尔格莱德进行的会谈的联合声明。
    声明说，意大利共产党总书记陶里亚蒂同志在党的中央委员阿马德西同志的陪同下，应南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会的邀请，在五月二十八日到三十日访问了贝尔格莱德。在同南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会总书记铁托同志所进行的、并且有南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会执行委员会其他委员参加的会谈中，以及在其他的会晤中，就双方有关问题和今后为巩固意大利和南斯拉夫共产党人之间的合作而进行接触方面交换了意见。会谈是在同志的气氛中进行的。
