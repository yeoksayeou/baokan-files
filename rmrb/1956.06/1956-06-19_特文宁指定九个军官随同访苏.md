Title: Twining Appoints Nine Officers to Accompany Him on Visit to Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-06-19
Page: 4
International Notes

    Twining Appoints Nine Officers to Accompany Him on Visit to Soviet Union
    Xinhua News Agency, June 18th—According to a United Press International dispatch from Washington: U.S. Air Force Chief of Staff Twining appointed nine officers a few days ago to accompany him on a visit to the Soviet Union.
    Twining, at the invitation of the Soviet government, will go to the Soviet Union to participate in the Soviet Aviation Day scheduled for June 24th.
    Six of the nine people appointed by Twining are generals, including three Deputy Chiefs of Staff of the Air Force.

### 特文宁指定九个军官随同访苏

1956-06-19
第4版()
专栏：国际札记

    特文宁指定九个军官随同访苏
    新华社18日讯　据合众社华盛顿消息：美国空军参谋长特文宁在几天以前指定了九名军官随同他访问苏联。
    特文宁应苏联政府的邀请，将前去苏联参加定于六月二十四日举行的苏联航空节。
    特文宁指定的九个人里面有六个人是将军，其中有三个是空军副参谋长。
