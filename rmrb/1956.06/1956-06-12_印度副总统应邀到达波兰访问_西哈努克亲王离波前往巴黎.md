Title: Indian Vice President Arrives in Poland for Visit at Invitation; Prince Sihanouk Departs Poland for Paris
Author:
Date: 1956-06-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw. The Vice President of the Republic of India, Dr. Radhakrishnan, arrived in Warsaw by plane on June 10th at the invitation of the Polish Council of State and the government.
    On the afternoon of the same day, the Indian Vice President met with the Chairman of the Polish Council of State, Zawadzki, and the Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz.
    Radhakrishnan will visit Poland for five days.
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, June 11th. Former Cambodian Prime Minister Prince Sihanouk and his entourage left Poland for Paris on June 10th.
    At the farewell at the airport, Polish First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Nowak, and Sihanouk spoke successively.
    Nowak pointed out that the friendship between the people of Cambodia and Poland is based on genuine common aspirations and common interests. Sihanouk's visit and the talks held in Poland, as well as the joint statement of Poland and Cambodia, have become an important step on the road to better mutual understanding, strengthening peace, and strengthening the friendship between Poland and Cambodia; a factor contributing to the further relaxation of international tensions.
    Sihanouk said that our talks with Polish officials and meetings with the Polish people have convinced us that the Polish people have a sincere and ardent desire for reconciliation and peace.
    Sihanouk said that our talks have led to a complete understanding between the two countries. I am deeply convinced that our unanimous recognition of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and the establishment of economic and cultural relations between our two countries will bring greater prosperity and happiness to our two peoples.

### 印度副总统应邀到达波兰访问  西哈努克亲王离波前往巴黎

1956-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    印度副总统应邀到达波兰访问
    西哈努克亲王离波前往巴黎
    据新华社华沙讯　印度共和国副总统拉德哈克里希南博士，应波兰国务委员会和政府的邀请，在六月十日乘飞机到达华沙。
    当天下午，印度副总统会见了波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基和部长会议主席西伦凯维兹。
    拉德哈克里希南将在波兰访问五天。
    新华社华沙11日电　柬埔寨前首相西哈努克亲王和他的随行人员，六月十日离开波兰前往巴黎。
    在机场分别的时候，波兰部长会议第一副主席诺瓦克和西哈努克先后讲了话。
    诺瓦克指出，柬埔寨同波兰两国人民的友谊是建立在真正的共同愿望和共同利益的基础上的。西哈努克的访问和在波兰所进行的会谈，以及波兰和柬埔寨的联合声明，已经成为在更好地相互了解、加强和平和加强波柬两国之间的友谊的道路上的一个重要步骤；成为有助于更加缓和国际紧张局势的一个因素。
    西哈努克说，我们同波兰官方人士的会谈和同波兰人民的会见，使我们相信波兰人民对和解和和平怀有真诚和殷切的愿望。
    西哈努克说，我们的会谈已经导致两国之间的完全的谅解。我深信，我们一致承认和平共处的五项原则和建立我们两国之间的经济和文化关系，将给我们两国人民带来更大的繁荣和幸福。
