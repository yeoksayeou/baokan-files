Title: Prime Minister Nehru Arrives in London
Author:
Date: 1956-06-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd, London Dispatch: Indian Prime Minister Nehru arrived in London by plane on the 22nd. He is in London to attend the Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference scheduled to open on June 27th.

During his journey, Nehru stopped in Damascus, Athens, and Rome. In Athens, he held talks with Greek Prime Minister Karamanlis and Foreign Minister Averoff. Sources say the Greek government hopes Nehru will support Greece on the Cyprus issue.

### 尼赫鲁总理到伦敦

1956-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁总理到伦敦
    新华社23日讯　伦敦消息：印度总理尼赫鲁在二十二日乘飞机到达伦敦。他到伦敦是要参加定于六月二十七日开幕的英联邦总理会议。
    尼赫鲁在旅途中曾经在大马士革，雅典和罗马停留。他在雅典同希腊首相卡拉曼利斯和外交大臣阿维罗夫举行了会谈。有消息说，希腊政府希望尼赫鲁在塞浦路斯问题上支持希腊。
