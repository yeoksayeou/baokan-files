Title: Turpan Grapes Expected to Have a Bumper Harvest
Author:
Date: 1956-06-17
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, June 16th: The vineyards in Turpan County, Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region are laden with fruit, and a bumper grape harvest is expected.

　　This year, Turpan County did not suffer from wind damage during the grape flowering period. The farmers engaged in grape cultivation also generally carried out topping, pruning, soil loosening and weeding in the grape fields. Nine grape production cooperatives in Grape Valley, Shahezi and other places have also promoted advanced technical measures such as topdressing and furrow irrigation. The grapes generally have dense ears and large grains.

### 吐鲁番葡萄可望丰收

1956-06-17
第2版()
专栏：

    吐鲁番葡萄可望丰收
    据新华社乌鲁木齐16日电　新疆维吾尔自治区吐鲁番县的葡萄园里结实累累，葡萄将获丰收。
    今年吐鲁番县在葡萄开花期间没有遭受风害，务育葡萄的农民还普遍在葡萄田里进行打尖、修枝和松土除草。葡萄沟、沙河子等地的九个葡萄生产合作社，还推广了追肥、沟灌等先进技术措施，葡萄普遍结得穗密粒大。
