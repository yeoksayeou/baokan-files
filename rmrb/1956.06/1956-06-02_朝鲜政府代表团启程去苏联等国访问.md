Title: Korean Government Delegation Departs for Visits to the Soviet Union and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-06-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 1st: The Republic delegation headed by Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, departed by special train on the afternoon of the 1st to visit the Soviet Union, the German Democratic Republic, Mongolia, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, Albania, and other People's Democratic countries.

The delegation members include: Pak Jeong-ae, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, Nam Il, Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ri Jong-ok, Chairman of the State Planning Commission, Ko Jun-taek, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the North Korean Democratic Party, Kim Byeong-je, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chondoist Chongu Party, Choe Hyeon, Vice-Minister of National Defence, Jo Geum-song, President of Kim Chaek University of Technology, Labour Hero Han Ki-chang, and Jeon Seong-bok, etc.

### 朝鲜政府代表团启程去苏联等国访问

1956-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府代表团启程去苏联等国访问
    新华社平壤1日电　以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成为首的共和国代表团，一日下午乘专车前往苏联、德意志民主共和国、蒙古、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚等人民民主国家访问。
    代表团团员有：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、外务相南日、国家计划委员会委员长李锺玉、北朝鲜民主党中央委员会副委员长高峻泽、天道教清友党中央委员会副委员长金秉济、民族保卫省副相崔贤、金策工业大学校长曹金松、劳动英雄韩基昌、全成福等。
