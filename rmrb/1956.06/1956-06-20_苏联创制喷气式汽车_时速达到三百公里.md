Title: Soviet Union Creates Jet-Powered Car with Speed of 300 Kilometers Per Hour
Author:
Date: 1956-06-20
Page: 4

    Soviet Union Creates Jet-Powered Car
    Speed Reaches 300 Kilometers Per Hour

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th: The Molotov Automobile Factory in Gorky City, Soviet Union, recently created a jet-powered racing car. It uses ordinary kerosene as fuel and can reach a speed of 300 kilometers per hour.

    This car is manufactured based on the principle of a rocket. It does not have an ordinary engine, a gearbox, or a drive shaft. The turbojet engine installed on the car is not connected to the wheels. When the kerosene, atomized in the engine and mixed with air, burns, it generates a powerful airflow that ejects from the nozzle at the rear, producing a reaction force that propels the car forward.

### 苏联创制喷气式汽车  时速达到三百公里

1956-06-20
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联创制喷气式汽车
    时速达到三百公里
    新华社莫斯科19日电　苏联高尔基城的莫洛托夫汽车工厂，最近创制了一种喷气式赛跑用汽车。它用普通煤油作燃料，时速可以达到三百公里。
    这种汽车是根据火箭的原理制造的。它没有普通的发动机，没有变速器，也没有传动轴。汽车上装置的涡轮喷气引擎，并不和车轮联在一起。当引擎中化成雾状的煤油同空气混在一起燃烧起来的时候，就产生强大的气流从车后的喷气口喷出来，发生反作用，推动汽车前进。
