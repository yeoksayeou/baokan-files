Title: Further Develop the Friendly Relations Between Czechoslovakia and India: Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan Visits Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1956-06-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 7th: Indian Vice President Dr. Radhakrishnan arrived in Prague by plane at noon on the 6th, at the invitation of the Czechoslovak government.

After disembarking from the plane, Radhakrishnan shook hands with Prime Minister Široký, Chairman of the National Assembly Fierlinger, and others who came to welcome him, and met with diplomatic envoys stationed in Prague from various countries.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 7th: On the day of his arrival in Prague, Indian Vice President Dr. Radhakrishnan met with Czechoslovak Prime Minister Široký. During a long and sincere conversation, they exchanged views on further developing the friendly relations between Czechoslovakia and India.

Subsequently, Široký hosted a luncheon in honor of the Indian Vice President, attended by leaders of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and the government, as well as diplomatic representatives of various countries stationed in Prague.

Czechoslovak President Zápotocký received the Indian Vice President in the afternoon of the same day. Chairman of the National Assembly Fierlinger and Foreign Minister David were present at the reception. President Zápotocký and his distinguished guest held friendly talks.

Many Czechoslovak newspapers published editorials on the 6th welcoming the visit of the Indian Vice President. The newspapers believe that this visit demonstrates the strengthening of friendly relations between Czechoslovakia and India.

### 进一步发展捷印两国之间的友好关系  印副总统拉德哈克里希南访捷

1956-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    进一步发展捷印两国之间的友好关系
    印副总统拉德哈克里希南访捷
    据新华社布拉格7日电　印度副总统拉德哈克里希南博士应捷克斯洛伐克政府的邀请，在六日中午乘飞机到达布拉格。
    拉德哈克里希南下飞机后，同前来欢迎的西罗基总理、国民议会主席费林格等人握了手，并且同各国驻布拉格外交使节见了面。
    新华社布拉格7日电　印度副总统拉德哈克里希南博士在到达布拉格的当天，会见了捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基。他们在长时间的诚恳的会谈中，就进一步发展捷印两国之间的友好关系交换了意见。
    随后，西罗基举行午宴招待印度副总统，捷克斯洛伐克共产党和政府的领导人以及各国驻布拉格的外交代表参加了这次午宴。　　捷克斯洛伐克总统萨波托斯基在同日下午接见了印度副总统。接见时在座的有国民议会主席费林格和外交部长戴维。萨波托斯基总统和他的贵宾举行了友好的会谈。　　捷克斯洛伐克许多报纸在六日发表了社论，欢迎印度副总统的访问。报纸认为，这次访问表明了捷、印两国友好关系的加强。
