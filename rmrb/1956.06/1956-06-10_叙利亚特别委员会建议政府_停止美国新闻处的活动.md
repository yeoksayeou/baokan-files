Title: Syrian Special Committee Recommends Government Halt Activities of U.S. Information Service
Author:
Date: 1956-06-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 9th Dispatch from Damascus: A special committee established by the Syrian government recommended on June 7th that the government halt the activities of the U.S. Information Service in Damascus. It also recommended halting the activities of other foreign cultural institutions that do not comply with Syrian law.

This special committee was established by the Syrian government on June 6th, tasked with studying methods to regulate the activities of foreign cultural institutions and submitting a report. The Syrian government established this committee due to public dissatisfaction in Syria with certain publications distributed by the U.S. Information Service.

Syrian officials said on June 7th that the report submitted by the special committee stated that the current activities of the U.S. Information Service do not comply with Syrian laws and regulations.

### 叙利亚特别委员会建议政府  停止美国新闻处的活动

1956-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    叙利亚特别委员会建议政府
    停止美国新闻处的活动
    新华社9日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚政府设立的一个特别委员会在六月七日建议政府停止设在大马士革的美国新闻处的活动。它还建议也停止其他一些不遵守叙利亚法律的外国文化机构的活动。
    这个特别委员会是叙利亚政府在六月六日成立的，它的任务是研究管制外国文化机构的活动的办法，并且提出报告。叙利亚政府成立这个委员会是由于叙利亚公众对于美国新闻处散发的一些出版物表示不满。
    叙利亚官方人士六月七日说，这个特别委员会提出的报告说，美国新闻处目前的活动没有遵守叙利亚的法律和条例。
