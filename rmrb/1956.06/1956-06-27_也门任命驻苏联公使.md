Title: Yemen Appoints Minister to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-06-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, June 25th (TASS): According to a report in the Egyptian newspaper "Akhbar El Yom," based on the agreement between the Soviet Union and Yemen on the mutual dispatch of diplomatic envoys, the Yemeni Minister to Egypt, Abdul Rahman Abu Taleb, has also been appointed as the Yemeni Minister to the Soviet Union.

### 也门任命驻苏联公使

1956-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    也门任命驻苏联公使
    新华社26日电　塔斯社开罗二十五日讯：据埃及“消息报”报道，根据苏联和也门之间达成的关于互派外交使节的协议，也门驻埃及公使阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·阿布塔莱布同时被任命为也门驻苏联公使。
