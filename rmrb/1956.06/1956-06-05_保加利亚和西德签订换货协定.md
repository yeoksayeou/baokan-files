Title: Bulgaria and West Germany Sign Barter Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-06-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 2nd. Sofia newspapers report that a one-year barter and payment agreement was signed in Bonn on May 30th between the People's Republic of Bulgaria and the Federal Republic of Germany.

The agreement stipulates that the Federal Republic of Germany will provide Bulgaria with metals, metal products, machinery and equipment, cellulose and other commodities with a total value of US$15 million.

Bulgaria will provide the Federal Republic of Germany with bricks, tiles, fruits, vegetables, tobacco, eggs and other agricultural products of equal value.

### 保加利亚和西德签订换货协定

1956-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚和西德签订换货协定
    新华社布拉格2日电　索非亚报纸报道，五月三十日在波恩签订了保加利亚人民共和国和德意志联邦共和国之间为期一年的换货和支付协定。
    协定规定德意志联邦共和国向保加利亚提供价值总共一千五百万美元的金属、金属制品、机器设备、纤维素和其他商品。
    保加利亚将向德意志联邦共和国提供同等价值的砖瓦、水果、蔬菜、烟草、鸡蛋和其他农产品。
