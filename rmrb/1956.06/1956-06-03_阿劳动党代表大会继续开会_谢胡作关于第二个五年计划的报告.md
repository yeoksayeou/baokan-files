Title: Albanian Labor Party Congress Continues; Shehu Reports on the Second Five-Year Plan
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana: The Third Congress of the Albanian Labor Party continued its sessions on May 29th, 30th, and 31st, discussing and passing the summary report of the Party Central Committee and the report of the Central Inspection Committee.

    During the meeting on the 29th, representatives from fraternal communist and workers' parties of countries such as France, the German Democratic Republic, Korea, and Mongolia delivered congratulatory speeches to the congress, receiving warm welcomes.

    On the 30th, Chairman of the Council of Ministers Shehu delivered a report on the summary of the implementation of the First Five-Year Plan and instructions on the Second Five-Year Plan (1956-1960) for the development of the economy and culture.

    The congress launched a discussion on Shehu's report. In their speeches, the representatives all agreed with the construction program stipulated in the instructions. The representatives pointed out the achievements already made in socialist construction and exposed the shortcomings existing in industry, agriculture, and mass cultural work.

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 2nd: Tirana dispatch: Chairman of the Albanian Council of Ministers, Shehu, delivered a report on the implementation of the First Five-Year Plan and instructions for the Second Five-Year Plan at the Third Congress of the Albanian Labor Party on May 30th.

    In his report, Shehu emphasized, "The First Five-Year Plan has transformed our country from a backward agricultural country into an agricultural-industrial country."

    He said that the main task of the Second Five-Year Plan is "mainly to further develop industry, especially the mining industry, based on fully utilizing existing production equipment and mobilizing internal potential; mainly to rapidly develop agriculture based on reorganizing agricultural production according to socialist principles; and, on the above basis, to continuously improve the material conditions of the Albanian people and raise their cultural level."

    According to the instructions, by 1960, the total industrial output will increase by 91% compared to 1955, of which the means of production will more than double, consumer goods will increase by 84%, oil production will increase by 56%, coal will increase by 52%, chromium ore will increase by 150%, and electricity will increase by 86%.

    In 1960, the total grain harvest will reach more than 516,000 tons, compared to 362,000 tons in 1955. Cotton harvest will increase by 87%, and meat production will increase by 53%. By the end of the Second Five-Year Plan, more than 70% of the sown area in Albania will be cultivated collectively.

    In five years, the national income will increase by 53%, and the real wages of workers and staff will increase by about 25%.

    Shehu finally emphasized that we have a strong economic foundation, and we have all the conditions to successfully implement the Second Five-Year Plan. Under the leadership of the Labor Party, the Albanian people are prepared and determined to advance heroically along the road of building socialism.

### 阿劳动党代表大会继续开会  谢胡作关于第二个五年计划的报告

1956-06-03
第4版()
专栏：

    阿劳动党代表大会继续开会
    谢胡作关于第二个五年计划的报告
    新华社地拉那消息　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第三次代表大会五月二十九日、三十日和三十一日继续开会，讨论并且通过了党中央委员会的总结报告和中央检查委员会的报告。
    在二十九日会议上，法国、德意志民主共和国、朝鲜和蒙古等国的兄弟共产党和工人党的代表向大会致贺词，受到了热烈的欢迎。
    部长会议主席谢胡三十日在会上作了关于第一个五年计划执行情况的总结和发展经济和文化的第二个五年计划（1956—1960年）的指示的报告。
    代表大会就谢胡的报告展开了讨论。代表们在发言中都赞同指示中规定的建设纲领。代表们指出了在社会主义建设方面已经取得的成就，并且揭露了在工业、农业和群众文化工作中所存在的缺点。
    新华社布拉格2日电　地拉那消息：阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席谢胡五月三十日在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第三次代表大会上作了关于第一个五年计划执行情况和对第二个五年计划的指示的报告。
    谢胡在报告中强调说：“第一个五年计划已经把我国从一个落后的农业国变成为农业—工业国。”
    他说，第二个五年计划的主要任务“主要地是在充分利用现有生产设备和动员内部潜力的基础上，进一步发展工业、特别是采矿工业；主要地是在根据社会主义原则改组农业生产的基础上，迅速发展农业；在上述的基础上来不断改善阿尔巴尼亚人民的物质状况和提高他们的文化水平。”
    根据指示，到1960年，工业总产量将比1955年增加91％，其中生产资料将增加一倍以上，消费品将增加84％，石油产量增加56％，煤增加52％，铬矿增加150％，电力增加86％。
    在1960年，谷物总收获量将达到五十一万六千多吨，而1955年为三十六万二千吨。棉花收获量将增加87％，肉类产量将增加53％。到第二个五年计划结束的时候，阿尔巴尼亚将有70％以上的播种面积实行集体耕作。
    在五年中，国民收入将增加53％，工人和职员的实际工资将增加25％左右。
    谢胡最后着重指出，我们有强大的经济基础，我们具备顺利执行第二个五年计划的一切条件。阿尔巴尼亚人民在劳动党的领导下准备并且有决心沿着建设社会主义的大道英勇地前进。
