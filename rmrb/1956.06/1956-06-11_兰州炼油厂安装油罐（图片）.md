Title: Installation of Oil Tanks at Lanzhou Refinery (Picture)
Author: Xie Shixin
Date: 1956-06-11
Page: 2

    Installation of Oil Tanks at Lanzhou Refinery
    Construction of the Lanzhou Refinery has begun. Workers are using the advanced Soviet "inverted installation method" to prefabricate a large number of oil tanks, allowing the oil tank installation work to proceed ahead of schedule. This is a picture of workers installing oil tanks.
                                           Photo by Xie Shixin (Xinhua News Agency Manuscript)

### 兰州炼油厂安装油罐（图片）
谢世辛
1956-06-11
第2版()
专栏：

    兰州炼油厂安装油罐
    兰州炼油厂的建厂工程开始了。工人们采用苏联先进的“倒装法”预制出大量油罐，使油罐安装工作提前进行。这是工人们正在安装油罐。
                                           谢世辛摄（新华社稿）
