Title: Chinese Folk Art Troupe Performs in Hong Kong
Author:
Date: 1956-06-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, June 22nd: The Chinese Folk Art Troupe held its opening performance at the Xuan Palace Theatre in Hong Kong on the evening of the 21st.
    The Xuan Palace Theatre has been completely renovated in the past two days. Outside the theatre hung huge colorful paintings, as tall as the entire floor, depicting vivid lion dances, tea-picking butterfly dances, and fan dances. Beside the paintings hung colorful flags. These magnificent patterns, symbolizing the brilliance of the motherland's culture and art, attracted countless passersby to stop and admire.
    Xu Bi Gu, Chairman of the Hong Kong Chinese General Chamber of Commerce, and Ouyang Shan, Head of the Chinese Folk Art Troupe, delivered speeches at the opening ceremony.
    Afterwards, the performance began. More than 1,300 people watched the opening performance of the Folk Art Troupe that evening.

### 中国民间艺术团在香港演出

1956-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中国民间艺术团在香港演出
    据新华社香港22日电　中国民间艺术团二十一日晚上在香港璇宫戏院举行开幕演出。
    璇宫戏院这两天装饰得焕然一新。戏院外面挂着绘有整层楼高的鲜艳夺目的狮子舞、采茶扑蝶舞和扇舞的巨幅彩画，画旁还挂着缤纷的彩旗。这些象征着祖国文化艺术的光辉的宏伟图案吸引着无数行人停下来观赏。
    香港中华总商会会长许庇谷和中国民间艺术团团长欧阳山先后在开幕式上致词。
    接着举行节目演出。这天晚上观看民间艺术团开幕演出的观众有一千三百多人。
