Title: Swedish Armed Forces Representatives Arrive in Andong on Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1956-06-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Andong, June 6th: Lieutenant Colonel Ariskog, a representative of the Swedish Armed Forces, arrived in Andong, China today by train from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, at the invitation of China. Representatives of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in the Shenyang area and representatives of the Andong Municipal People's Committee were at the station to welcome him.

### 应邀来我国访问瑞典武装部队代表到安东

1956-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国访问瑞典武装部队代表到安东
    新华社安东6日电　应邀来中国访问的瑞典武装部队代表阿里斯考格中校，今天乘火车从朝鲜民主主义人民共和国到达我国安东。中国人民解放军驻沈阳地区部队的代表和安东市人民委员会的代表曾到车站欢迎。
