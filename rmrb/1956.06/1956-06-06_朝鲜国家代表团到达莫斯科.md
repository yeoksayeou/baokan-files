Title: Korean National Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-06-06
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 5th: According to TASS, Moscow: The delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Premier of the Cabinet Kim Il-sung, arrived in Moscow by plane on June 4th.
    After Kim Il-sung and others got off the plane, they met with Mikoyan, Brezhnev, Shepilov, Furtseva, and others who came to greet them.
    The Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Soviet Union, Li Sang-jo, and many ambassadors from other countries to Moscow also went to the airport to greet them.
    Mikoyan and others warmly welcomed the Korean guests.

### 朝鲜国家代表团到达莫斯科

1956-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜国家代表团到达莫斯科
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团，在内阁首相金日成率领下，六月四日坐飞机到达莫斯科。
    金日成等下飞机以后，同前来迎接的米高扬、勃列日涅夫、谢皮洛夫、福尔采娃等见了面。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻苏联大使李相朝和许多国家驻莫斯科的大使也到机场迎接。
    米高扬等热烈地欢迎朝鲜客人们。
