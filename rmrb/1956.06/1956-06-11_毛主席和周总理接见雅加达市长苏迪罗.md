Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Receive Jakarta Mayor Sudiro
Author:
Date: 1956-06-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China and Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council received Sudiro, the Mayor of Jakarta Special City in Indonesia, and his wife, Deputy Mayor Danubroto, and five accompanying personnel this afternoon.

Present at the reception were Peng Zhen, Mayor of Beijing, Wang Kunlun, Deputy Mayor, Zhang Wenjin, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Xin Yi, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of Beijing.

Anwar Sani, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy in China, was also present at the reception. (Photo attached)
Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai receive Sudiro (second from right), Mayor of Jakarta Special City, Indonesia, his wife (first from right), and Deputy Mayor Danubroto (fourth from right), among others. Xinhua News Agency reporter Qi Guanshan photographed.

### 毛主席和周总理接见雅加达市长苏迪罗

1956-06-11
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席和周总理接见雅加达市长苏迪罗
    新华社10日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东和国务院总理周恩来，在今天下午接见了印度尼西亚雅加达特别市市长苏迪罗和夫人、副市长达努布罗托和随行人员等五人。
    接见的时候在座的，有北京市市长彭真、副市长王昆仑，外交部亚洲司副司长章文晋和北京市外事处处长辛毅。
    接见的时候，印度尼西亚驻中国大使馆临时代办安瓦·萨尼也在座。（附图片）
    毛泽东主席和周恩来总理接见印度尼西亚雅加达特别市市长苏迪罗（右起第二人）、市长夫人（右起第一人）和副市长达努布罗托（右起第四人）等。　新华社记者　齐观山摄
