Title: Our National Leaders Receive Finnish Parliamentary Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 27th. Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai received the Finnish parliamentary delegation led by the Speaker of the Finnish Parliament, Mr. Väinö Johannes Sukselainen, this evening. Present at the reception were Song Qingling, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, and Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, as well as Wu Kejian and Yu Xinqing, Deputy Secretaries-General, and Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs.
    The Finnish Ambassador to China, Mr. Sundström, was also present at the reception.
    In addition: The Finnish parliamentary delegation visited the cities of Changchun, Shenyang, and Anshan on the 25th to observe local construction progress and returned to Beijing on the 27th.

### 我国家领导人接见芬兰议会代表团

1956-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我国家领导人接见芬兰议会代表团
    新华社27日讯　毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理今天晚上接见了芬兰议会议长维埃诺·约翰奈斯·苏克舍拉宁和他所率领的芬兰议会访华代表团。接见的时候，在座的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄、李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、李维汉、陈叔通，副秘书长吴克坚、余心清，外交部副部长张闻天。
    接见的时候，芬兰驻华大使孙士敦也在座。
    又讯：　芬兰议会访华代表团二十五日去长春、沈阳、鞍山三个城市，参观当地的建设情况，二十七日回到北京。
