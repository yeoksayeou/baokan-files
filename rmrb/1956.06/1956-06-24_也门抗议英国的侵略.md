Title: Yemen Protests British Aggression
Author:
Date: 1956-06-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd, New York Dispatch: The Yemeni government announced on June 20th that if Britain does not cease its acts of aggression along the Yemen-Aden border, it will file a protest with the United Nations.

    In a communique issued to the press at United Nations headquarters, Yemen protested the British demonstration of land and air forces on the Yemeni border. The communique stated that these acts of aggression and intimidation are part of Britain's plan to forcibly establish a federation in Yemen in order to place it under British control.

### 也门抗议英国的侵略

1956-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    也门抗议英国的侵略
    新华社23日讯　纽约消息：也门政府在六月二十日宣布，如果英国不停止它沿着也门—亚丁边境的侵略行为，它将向联合国提出抗议。
    也门在联合国总部向报界发表的公报中，抗议英国在也门边境进行陆空部队示威。公报说，这种侵略行为和威吓活动，是英国企图强使也门成立一个联邦，以便把它置于英国控制之下的计划的一部分。
