Title: Establishment of the China Children's Theater

Author:

Date: 1956-06-11
Page: 1

On June 10th, the founding conference of China's first children's theater—the China Children's Theater—was held at the Capital Theater in Beijing. The picture shows children in the capital enthusiastically presenting flowers to their actors to express their congratulations.
