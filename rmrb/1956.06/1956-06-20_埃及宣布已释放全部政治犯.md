Title: Egypt Announces Release of All Political Prisoners
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, June 18th: According to an official Egyptian announcement on June 17th, all political prisoners in Egypt have been released.
    The release of political prisoners began on June 10th. Prior to that release, according to official announcements, Egypt had a total of 571 political prisoners.

### 埃及宣布已释放全部政治犯

1956-06-20
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及宣布已释放全部政治犯
    新华社18日讯　开罗消息：据埃及官方六月十七日宣布，埃及的政治犯已经全部释放。
    释放政治犯的行动是在六月十日开始的，在那次释放以前，据官方宣布，埃及共有政治犯571人。
