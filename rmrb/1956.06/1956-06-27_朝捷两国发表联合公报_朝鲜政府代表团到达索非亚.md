Title: Joint Communiqué Issued by Korea and Czechoslovakia; Korean Government Delegation Arrives in Sofia
Author: 
Date: 1956-06-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 26th: The government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Kim Il-sung, left Czechoslovak territory by plane on June 25th for Bulgaria, after a five-day visit to Czechoslovakia.

    The joint communiqué issued by Korea and Czechoslovakia before the delegation left stated that the government delegations of the two countries discussed further deepening the friendly relations between the two countries and some international issues related to both sides. Both sides noted with satisfaction that the friendly relations between the two countries have been further expanded and deepened in accordance with the needs of both sides and the interests of safeguarding world peace.

    During the talks, the two sides also discussed issues of strengthening cooperation in the economic, cultural, and scientific fields and signed a cultural cooperation agreement. The Korean government delegation expressed sincere gratitude to the people of Czechoslovakia for the economic and technical assistance given to the Korean people. The government delegations of the two countries believe that this friendly meeting has greatly promoted mutual understanding and deepened the friendly relations between the two countries.

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 26th: Sofia News: The government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Kim Il-sung, arrived in Sofia by plane on June 25th at the invitation of the Bulgarian government.

    Damyanov, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of Bulgaria, Traykov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Ganev, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, and Bulgaranov, and others welcomed the distinguished Korean guests at the airport.

    After reviewing the guard of honor at the airport, Kim Il-sung and others, accompanied by leaders of the Bulgarian Communist Party and government, traveled in open cars to the center of Sofia – "September 9th" Square – to participate in a mass welcoming rally held there. Hundreds of thousands of citizens along the way cheered and paid tribute to the Korean guests.

    At the welcoming rally, Damyanov delivered a welcoming speech, and Kim Il-sung delivered a reply.

### 朝捷两国发表联合公报  朝鲜政府代表团到达索非亚

1956-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    朝捷两国发表联合公报
    朝鲜政府代表团到达索非亚
    新华社布拉格26日电　以金日成为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团，在捷克斯洛伐克访问了五天以后，六月二十五日乘飞机离开捷克斯洛伐克国境前往保加利亚。
    在代表团离开前发表的朝鲜和捷克斯洛伐克联合公报说，两国政府代表团讨论了进一步加深两国友好关系以及同双方有关的一些国际问题。双方满意地指出，两国的友好关系已经根据双方的需要和保卫世界和平的利益，得到了继续扩大和加深。
    在会谈期间，双方还讨论了加强经济、文化和科学方面合作的问题，签订了文化合作协定。朝鲜政府代表团对于捷克斯洛伐克人民给予朝鲜人民的经济和技术援助，表示诚恳的感谢。两国政府代表团认为，这次友好的会谈大大促进了彼此的了解，加深了两国的友好关系。
    新华社布拉格26日电　索非亚消息：以金日成为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团，应保加利亚政府的邀请，在六月二十五日乘飞机到达索非亚。
    保加利亚国民议会主席团主席达米扬诺夫、部长会议第一副主席特莱科夫、保加利亚共产党中央委员会书记加涅夫和布尔加拉诺夫等到机场欢迎朝鲜贵宾。
    金日成等在飞机场上检阅了仪仗队以后，由保加利亚共产党和政府领导人陪同，坐敞篷汽车前往索非亚市中心——“九九”广场，参加在那里举行的群众欢迎大会。沿途有数十万市民向朝鲜客人欢呼致敬。
    在欢迎大会上，达米扬诺夫致了欢迎词，金日成致了答词。
