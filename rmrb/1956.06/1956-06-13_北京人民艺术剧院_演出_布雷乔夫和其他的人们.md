Title: Beijing People's Art Theatre Presents "Brechtov and Other People"
Author:
Date: 1956-06-13
Page: 3

    Beijing People's Art Theatre
    Presents "Brechtov and Other People"
    (Our report) Under the guidance of Soviet performing expert B.G. Kurinev, the Beijing People's Art Theatre has produced "Yegor Bulichov and Others" in commemoration of the 20th anniversary of the death of the great proletarian writer Gorky, and officially premiered it at the Beijing Theater yesterday. The general director of the play is Jiao Juyin, the directors are Xia Chun and Mei Qian, and the main actors include Diao Guangtan, Ye Zi, Zhu Lin, Hu Zongwen, Lü En, Yu Shizhi, Zheng Rong, Shen Mo, Dong Xingji, Li Pin, and Ying Ruocheng.
      "Yegor Bulichov and Others" is a famous work of Gorky's later years. The play is set against the historical background of the Russian bourgeois February Revolution. With a high degree of artistic generalization and concentration, the author portrays various representatives of bourgeois society before the revolution through the Bulichov family events, exposing the corrupt and ugly essence of a society where even the closest relationships can be destroyed for money.

### 北京人民艺术剧院  演出“布雷乔夫和其他的人们”

1956-06-13
第3版()
专栏：

    北京人民艺术剧院
    演出“布雷乔夫和其他的人们”
    本报讯　北京人民艺术剧院在苏联表演专家包·戈·库里涅夫指导下，为纪念伟大的无产阶级作家高尔基逝世二十周年，排演的“耶戈尔·布雷乔夫和其他的人们”，昨日起在北京剧场正式公演。这个剧的总导演是焦菊隐，导演是夏淳、梅阡，主要演员有刁光覃、叶子、朱琳、胡宗温、吕恩、于是之、郑榕、沈默、董行佶、黎频和英若诚等。
      “耶戈尔·布雷乔夫和其他的人们”，是高尔基晚年的名著。该剧是以俄国资产阶级二月革命前夕为历史背景。作者以高度的艺术概括和集中，通过布雷乔夫的家庭事件，刻划出革命前资产阶级社会形形色色的代表人物，揭露出为了金钱甚至可以破坏至亲关系的腐朽丑恶的社会本质。
