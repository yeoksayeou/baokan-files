Title: Korean Government Delegation Arrives in Romania
Author:
Date: 1956-06-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 14th: The Korean government delegation headed by Premier Kim Il-sung, after a week-long visit to the German Democratic Republic, left Berlin on June 13th and arrived in Bucharest on the same day. They are visiting at the invitation of the Romanian government.

Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, Prime Minister Stoica, and other leaders of the Party and government welcomed them at the airport. Stoica and Kim Il-sung spoke at the airport successively.

When the Korean government delegation traveled from the airport to their residence, thousands of residents welcomed them along the way. Romanian newspapers also published editorials and articles welcoming the Korean guests to Romania.

### 朝鲜政府代表团到罗马尼亚

1956-06-15
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府代表团到罗马尼亚
    据新华社布拉格14日电　以金日成首相为首的朝鲜政府代表团，在德意志民主共和国进行了一个星期的访问以后，六月十三日离开柏林，当日到达布加勒斯特，他们是应罗马尼亚政府的邀请去访问的。
    罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会第一书记乔治乌—德治，部长会议主席斯托伊卡以及党和政府的其他领导人到机场欢迎。斯托伊卡和金日成先后在机场上讲了话。
    朝鲜政府代表团从飞机场前往他们居住的地方的时候，一路上有成千上万的居民在欢迎他们。罗马尼亚的报纸也发表社论和文章，欢迎朝鲜客人到罗马尼亚访问。
