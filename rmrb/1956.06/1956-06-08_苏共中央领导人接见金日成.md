Title: Soviet Communist Party Central Committee Leaders Receive Kim Il-sung
Author:
Date: 1956-06-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th: TASS, Moscow reported: On June 6th, Khrushchev, Mikoyan, and Brezhnev received Kim Il-sung, Pak Chong-ae, and Ri Jong-ok, who were passing through Moscow on their way to Berlin, at the headquarters of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

The leaders of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Workers' Party of Korea held sincere and friendly talks.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th: TASS, Moscow reported: The delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Premier of the Cabinet Kim Il-sung, departed Moscow for Germany on the morning of June 7th, passing through Moscow on their way to Berlin.

### 苏共中央领导人接见金日成

1956-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏共中央领导人接见金日成
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：六月六日，赫鲁晓夫、米高扬和勃列日涅夫，在苏联共产党中央委员会的所在地接见了路过莫斯科前往柏林的金日成、朴正爱和李钟玉。
    苏联共产党和朝鲜劳动党的领导人进行了真诚友好的谈话。
    新华社7日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：路过莫斯科前往柏林的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团，在内阁首相金日成率领下，六月七日早晨从莫斯科动身前往德国。
