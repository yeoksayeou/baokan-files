Title: Neutral Nations Inspection Teams Stationed in North Korea Withdraw from Panmunjom Upon Instructions from Superiors
Author:
Date: 1956-06-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 11th. According to the Korean Central News Agency from Kaesong: Following instructions from the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, the Polish, Czechoslovakian, Swedish, and Swiss members of the Neutral Nations Inspection Teams stationed at the ports of Sinuiju, Manpo, and Sinanju in North Korea left their respective locations yesterday and today in special trains and are temporarily withdrawing to the headquarters in Panmunjom.

The liaison officers from the Korean-Chinese side of the Military Armistice Commission stationed at these ports held banquets to bid farewell to the local Neutral Nations Inspection Teams and presented them with gifts. The Polish, Czechoslovakian, Swedish, and Swiss members of the Neutral Nations Inspection Teams at the banquets expressed their gratitude to the liaison officers from the Korean-Chinese side of the Military Armistice Commission and the local governments for their assistance in their work and lives.

When the Neutral Nations Inspection Teams left the above three ports, liaison officers from the Korean-Chinese side of the Military Armistice Commission stationed there, representatives of local governments, and the masses went to the stations to see them off and presented them with flowers.

### 驻在朝鲜北部的中立国视察小组  奉上级指示撤回板门店

1956-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    驻在朝鲜北部的中立国视察小组
    奉上级指示撤回板门店
    新华社平壤11日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社开城讯：根据中立国监察委员会的指示，驻在朝鲜北部新义州、满浦和新安州口岸的中立国视察小组的波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、瑞典和瑞士成员已经在昨天和今天分别乘专车离开当地，暂时撤回板门店本部。
    驻在这些口岸的军事停战委员会朝中方面联络官曾分别举行宴会欢送当地的中立国视察小组，并且向他们赠送了礼物。中立国视察小组的波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、瑞典和瑞士成员在宴会上都对军事停战委员会朝中方面联络官和当地政府给与他们在工作和生活方面的帮助表示感谢。
    在上述三个口岸的中立国视察小组离开的时候，驻在当地的军事停战委员会朝中方面联络官、当地政府代表和群众都到车站欢送，并且向他们献了花。
