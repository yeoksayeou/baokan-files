Title: Lebanese Peace Committee Holds Tea Party to Welcome Chinese Art Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-06-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, June 26th: The Lebanese Peace Committee held a grand tea party at 7:00 PM on June 24th to welcome the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation.

    Saleem Dabliz, who had visited China, the famous Lebanese writer George Hanna, and the head of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, Burhan Shahidi, spoke at the meeting. They mutually expressed the friendship between the two countries and their desire for cooperation between the two countries in the cause of world peace.

    The chairman of the Lebanese Peace Committee and chairman of the Lebanese Engineers Association, Antoine Tabet, the head of the Lebanese delegation to the Bandung Conference and former Prime Minister Sami Solh, the chairman of the Lebanese Legal Workers Association Farid Kuzma, and more than 300 well-known figures from all walks of life in Lebanon attended the tea party.

### 黎巴嫩和平委员会举行茶会招待我国艺术代表团

1956-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩和平委员会举行茶会招待我国艺术代表团
    新华社贝鲁特26日电　黎巴嫩和平委员会在六月二十四日下午七点钟举行盛大茶会招待中国文化艺术代表团。
    曾经访问过中国的赛利姆·达布利兹，黎巴嫩的著名作家乔治·汉纳和中国文化艺术代表团团长鲍尔汉都在会上讲了话。他们互相表达了两国之间的友谊和他们对两国在世界和平事业中合作的愿望。
    黎巴嫩和平委员会主席、黎巴嫩工程师协会主席安托瓦·塔伯特，出席万隆会议的黎巴嫩代表团团长、前总理萨米·索勒哈，黎巴嫩法律工作者协会主席法里德·库兹马，以及黎巴嫩各界知名人士三百多人参加了这次茶会。
