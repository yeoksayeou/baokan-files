Title: Egyptian Student Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-06-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 26th—The Egyptian student delegation, invited by the All-China Students' Federation to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane today, led by Hassan Asmawi, professor of agriculture at Cairo University, Egypt.

The Egyptian student delegation is visiting China in accordance with the Sino-Egyptian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Before arriving in Beijing, they had already visited Guangzhou, Wuhan, Chongqing and other places.

### 埃及学生代表团到北京

1956-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及学生代表团到北京
    据新华社26日讯　应中华全国学生联合会邀请来中国访问的埃及学生代表团，在埃及开罗大学农学教授哈山·阿斯玛维率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    埃及学生代表团是根据中埃文化合作协定来中国访问的。来北京前，他们已经访问了广州、武汉、重庆等地。
