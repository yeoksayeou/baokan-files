Title: Heilongjiang Reclamation Station Reclaims 50,000 Hectares

Author: 
Date: 1956-06-09
Page: 2

This newspaper reports:

    Harbin Dispatch: By the end of May, the ten newly built tractor reclamation stations in Heilongjiang Province this spring had reclaimed 50,000 hectares of virgin land, exceeding the planned target by 20%. These lands have been reclaimed for immigrants from Shandong, who are now sowing seeds on these lands.

### 黑龙江垦荒站开荒五万公顷

1956-06-09
第2版()
专栏：

    黑龙江垦荒站开荒五万公顷
    本报哈尔滨讯　黑龙江省今年春季新建的十个拖拉机垦荒站，截至五月底已经开垦生荒五万公顷，超过预定计划20％。这些荒地都是为从山东搬来的移民开垦的，目前移民们正在这些土地上播种。
