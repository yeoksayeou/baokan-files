Title: Sukarno Advocates Abolishing the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization
Author:
Date: 1956-06-06
Page: 4

    Sukarno Advocates Abolishing the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization
    Xinhua News Agency, June 5th Dispatch from Ottawa: Indonesian President Sukarno left Niagara Falls, Canada on June 4th, arriving in Ottawa for a formal visit.
    According to a United Press report, before leaving Niagara Falls, Sukarno said in a statement to United Press reporters that abolishing the American-initiated Southeast Asia Treaty Organization and any other similar military treaties would be "a contribution to peaceful relations throughout the world." Sukarno said, "No military alliance is helpful in easing tensions, and expanding such alliances will only reduce, not increase, the opportunity to ease tensions."
    Sukarno revealed that the five initiating countries of the Asian-African Conference held in Bandung last year are considering holding another conference. He said he believed it would be useful to hold such a conference again.
    Sukarno said that the United Nations should allow China to participate in the next General Assembly, because continuing to "exclude hundreds of millions of people from the international conference table is indeed unrealistic."

### 苏加诺主张废除东南亚条约组织

1956-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    苏加诺主张废除东南亚条约组织
    据新华社5日讯　渥太华消息：印度尼西亚总统苏加诺六月四日离开加拿大的尼亚加拉瀑布城到达渥太华作正式访问。
    据合众社报道，苏加诺在离开尼亚加拉瀑布城之前对合众社记者发表的谈话中说，废除美国发起的东南亚条约组织和任何其他类似的军事条约，都将是“对全世界和平关系的一个贡献”。苏加诺说，“没有任何军事联盟是有助于缓和紧张局势的，扩大这类联盟的做法只会减少而不是增加缓和紧张局势的机会。”
    苏加诺透露，去年在万隆举行的亚非会议的五个发起国家正在考虑举行另一次会议。他说，他认为再举行一次这种会议是有用处的。
    苏加诺说，联合国在下届大会上应该容许中国参加，因为继续把数亿人民“排斥于国际会议桌以外的确是不现实的。”
