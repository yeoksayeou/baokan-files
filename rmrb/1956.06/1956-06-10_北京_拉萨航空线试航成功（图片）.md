Title: Beijing-Lhasa Air Route Successfully Completes Trial Flight (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1956-06-10
Page: 2

    Beijing-Lhasa Air Route Successfully Completes Trial Flight
    The Beijing-Lhasa air route officially completed its trial flight successfully on May 26th. The successful flight and landing of a Chinese aircraft on the Tibetan Plateau, known as the "Roof of the World," created an astonishing miracle in aviation history. Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie photo
    Left picture: The plane departing from Beijing is warmly welcomed by the Tibetan people as it flies over the airport near Lhasa.
    Top picture: At the airport, Taiji Geshepa Qukyi Nyima (front row, first from left), representative of the Tibetan local government, and Xiangjie Suolangduojie (back row, first from left), representative of the Panchen Kanpo Lija Council, present hadas to the aviation personnel, congratulating them on the glorious success of this trial flight.
    Right picture: Tibetan people are visiting the plane from Beijing.

### 北京——拉萨航空线试航成功（图片）

1956-06-10
第2版()
专栏：

    北京——拉萨航空线试航成功
    北京——拉萨航空线在五月二十六日正式试航成功。中国飞机在号称“世界屋脊”的西藏高原上成功地飞行和着落，创造了航空史上惊人的奇迹。　新华社记者　钱嗣杰摄
    左图：从北京出发的飞机飞临在拉萨附近机场上空时，受到藏族人民的热烈欢迎。
    上图：在机场上，西藏地方政府代表台吉喝雪巴·却吉尼玛（前排左第一人）和班禅堪布会议厅委员会的代表香吉·索南多杰（后排左第一人）向航空人员献哈达。祝贺他们这次光荣试航成功。
    右图：藏族人民在参观来自北京的飞机。
