Title: Soong Ching-ling Receives Jakarta Special Municipality Mayor Sudiro
Author:
Date: 1956-06-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd - Soong Ching-ling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received Sudiro, the Mayor of the Special Municipality of Jakarta, Indonesia, his wife, Vice-Mayor Danubroto, and their entourage this afternoon.

Present at the reception were Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Sino-Indonesian Friendship Association, and Xin Yi, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee.

### 宋庆龄接见雅加达特别市市长苏迪罗

1956-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄接见雅加达特别市市长苏迪罗
    新华社3日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄今天下午接见了印度尼西亚雅加达特别市市长苏迪罗和夫人、副市长达努布罗托以及随行人员。
    陪同接见的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏、中国印度尼西亚友好协会副会长胡愈之和北京市人民委员会外事处处长辛毅。
