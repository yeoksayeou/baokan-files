Title: Vietnamese Overturned Old Peasant Women Study Culture
Author:
Date: 1956-06-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 6th: In the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, there are now emancipated old peasants over a hundred years old who are as enthusiastic about studying culture as young people.

In Nghệ An province, there is an old peasant woman named Lê Thị Xuyến, who is one hundred years old. Although her eyesight is poor and her hearing isn't good, she studies diligently and was selected as a model student in the whole village. Recently, she received a silk short jacket from President Ho Chi Minh.

### 越南翻身老农妇学文化

1956-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    越南翻身老农妇学文化
    据新华社河内6日电　越南民主共和国出现了上百岁的翻身老农民，也像年轻人一样积极地学习文化。
    义安省有一个一百岁的老农妇黎氏钏，虽然眼睛花了，听觉也不好，但是她刻苦学习，结果被选为全乡的学习模范。最近她收到了胡志明主席送给她的一件丝的短上衣。
