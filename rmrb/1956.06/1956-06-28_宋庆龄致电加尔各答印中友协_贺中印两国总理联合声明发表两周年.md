Title: Soong Ching Ling Sends Telegram to Calcutta India-China Friendship Association to Congratulate the Second Anniversary of the Joint Statement by the Prime Ministers of China and India
Author:
Date: 1956-06-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 27th -- Soong Ching Ling sent a telegram on June 25th to Saila Kumar Mukerjee, President of the India-China Friendship Association in Calcutta, congratulating the second anniversary of the joint statement issued by the Prime Ministers of China and India.
	Soong Ching Ling's telegram stated: "As you celebrate the anniversary of the declaration issued on June 28, 1954, I extend warm congratulations to the people of India in the name of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and in the spirit of these principles."
	The telegram stated: "The past two years have indeed proven how great the historical significance of this declaration by our two Prime Ministers is. Countries representing more than half of humanity have indicated their agreement to abide by these principles. Millions of people everywhere have seen the value and practical significance of these principles and demand that the world act according to these principles. This is the path to lasting peace in the world that our two peoples have pointed out. This is the result of the deep friendship that existed between the great peoples of China and India in the past, exists today, and will exist forever in the future."

### 宋庆龄致电加尔各答印中友协  贺中印两国总理联合声明发表两周年

1956-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄致电加尔各答印中友协
    贺中印两国总理联合声明发表两周年
    新华社27日讯　宋庆龄在六月二十五日致电加尔各答印中友好协会会长沙伊洛·库马尔·慕克吉，祝贺中印两国总理联合声明发表两周年。
    宋庆龄的电报说：“当你们庆祝1954年6月28日发表的宣言周年纪念的时候，我以和平共处的五项原则的名义并且本着这些原则的精神向印度人民表示热烈的祝贺。”
    电报说：“过去的两年的确已经证明我们两国总理的这个宣言具有多么大的历史意义，拥有人类半数以上的国家已经表明它们同意遵守这些原则。各地千百万的人民已经看到这些原则的价值和它们的实际意义，并且要求全世界根据这些原则行事。这就是我们两国人民已经指出的通往世界持久安宁的道路。这就是中国和印度两国伟大人民之间以前存在，今天存在和将来永远存在的深切友谊的结果。”
