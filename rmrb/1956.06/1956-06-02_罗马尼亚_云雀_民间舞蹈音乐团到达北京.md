Title: Romanian "Skylark" Folk Dance Music Ensemble Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-06-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 1st: The Romanian "Skylark" Folk Dance Music Ensemble, a group of one hundred and twenty-six people, arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 1st to begin a two-month performance and visit in China.

This large foreign art troupe, the first to visit China this year, is composed of a dance team with a high reputation in Romania, an accompanying symphony orchestra, and a folk band. The dance team, which has been established for nearly nine years, features folk dances rich in the characteristics of various regions of Romania, dance dramas depicting ancient Romanian customs, and dances from the Soviet Union and other countries. The folk band has many excellent soloists and vocalists who will play unique Romanian wind and percussion instruments and sing Romanian folk songs. The art troupe has previously visited Hungary, Bulgaria, Poland, and Albania, and has achieved great success.

At 4:10 pm, the train carrying the "Skylark" Folk Dance Music Ensemble arrived at the Beijing Railway Station, which was decorated with the national flags of China and Romania and huge welcome banners. Members of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe, who had previously visited Romania, along with more than 600 literary and art workers from Beijing, lined up on the platform, applauding and cheering to welcome the Romanian artists and presented them with flowers. Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, General Chen Yi, Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Wang Kunlun, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and famous figures in Beijing's literary and art circles, as well as Romanian Ambassador to China Guo Zuwen and embassy staff, went to the front of the train to welcome the head of the art troupe, Yani Nicola, and others.

At the welcoming ceremony held at the station, Zheng Zhenduo delivered a welcome speech on behalf of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, and Yani Nicola, the head of the Romanian "Skylark" Folk Dance Music Ensemble, delivered a reply.

Tonight, Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, held a banquet to welcome the Romanian "Skylark" Folk Dance Music Ensemble.

### 罗马尼亚“云雀”民间舞蹈音乐团到达北京

1956-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚“云雀”民间舞蹈音乐团到达北京
    新华社1日讯　罗马尼亚“云雀”民间舞蹈音乐团一行一百二十六人在一日下午乘火车到达北京，开始在中国作为期两个月的演出和访问。
    这个在今年头一个访问中国的大型外国艺术团体，是由一个在罗马尼亚享有很高声誉的舞蹈队和伴奏的交响乐队，以及一个民间乐队组成。它的成立将近九年的舞蹈队的节目中，有富于罗马尼亚各地特色的民间舞蹈，有表现罗马尼亚古代风俗的舞剧，还有苏联和其他国家的舞蹈。民间乐队拥有许多优秀的独奏家和声乐家，将要演奏罗马尼亚特有的吹奏和打击乐器，演唱罗马尼亚民间歌曲。这个艺术团曾经先后访问过匈牙利、保加利亚、波兰和阿尔巴尼亚，获得了很大的成功。
    下午四时十分，“云雀”民间舞蹈音乐团人员乘坐的列车，驶进了装饰着中、罗两国国旗和巨幅欢迎标语的北京车站。曾经访问过罗马尼亚的中国人民解放军歌舞团的团员们，同北京的文艺工作者六百多人在月台上列队鼓掌欢呼，来欢迎罗马尼亚艺术家，并且向他们献了花。中华人民共和国文化部副部长郑振铎，中国人民解放军总政治部文化部部长陈沂将军、北京市副市长王昆仑和北京文艺界著名人士，罗马尼亚驻华大使郭佐文和使馆人员都走到列车前面，来欢迎艺术团的团长亚尼·尼古拉等人。
    在车站举行的欢迎仪式上，郑振铎代表中华人民共和国文化部致欢迎词，罗马尼亚“云雀”民间舞蹈音乐团团长亚尼·尼古拉致答词。
    今天晚间，文化部部长沈雁冰举行宴会，欢迎罗马尼亚“云雀”民间舞蹈音乐团。
