Title: Taiyuan Thermal Power Plant Installs Equipment Ahead of Schedule; Second Phase of Civil Engineering Construction Basically Completed
Author:
Date: 1956-06-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, 3rd – The civil engineering construction of the second phase of the Taiyuan Thermal Power Plant, originally scheduled to be basically completed by the end of August this year, was basically completed by the end of May.

The workers responsible for the second phase of construction of the Taiyuan Thermal Power Plant promoted a series of advanced experiences in the advanced producer movement, shortening the construction period.

Now, the equipment installation of the Taiyuan Thermal Power Plant has also started in full swing two months ahead of schedule. Workers are using new experiences to accelerate the construction progress. The workers have decided to have this phase of the project send out electricity by August 1st this year.

### 太原热电厂提前安装设备  二期土木建筑工程基本完工

1956-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

    太原热电厂提前安装设备
    二期土木建筑工程基本完工
    新华社太原3日电　原计划今年八月底基本完工的太原热电厂第二期建设工程中的土木建筑工程，在五月底就基本完工了。
    担负太原热电厂第二期建设工程的工人们在先进生产者运动中，推广了一系列的先进经验，缩短了工期。
    现在，太原热电厂的设备安装也提前两个月全面开工了。工人们正在采用新的经验，加速施工进度。工人们决定使这一期工程在今年八月一日送出电来。
