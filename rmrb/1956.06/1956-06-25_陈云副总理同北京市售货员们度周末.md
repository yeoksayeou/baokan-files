Title: Vice Premier Chen Yun Spends Weekend with Beijing Sales Clerks
Author:
Date: 1956-06-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 24th: Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yun spent a pleasant weekend last night with more than 2,500 sales clerks from Beijing.

The Beijing Sales Clerks' Weekend Party was held in a locust-shaded courtyard near Yuhe Bridge. The party started at 7:30 PM. In the locust grove, some sales clerks were playing chess, some were playing bridge, and they were enthusiastically engaging in various games. The party also featured ball games, acrobatics performances, and a dance.

Chen Yun went to the game tables, the dance floor, and the sidelines of the sports field to celebrate with the sales clerks. He also spoke with Feng Huiliang, a sales clerk from the Xidan Food Store of the Beijing Company of the China Confectionery and Pastry Company, and sales clerks from the Beijing Department Store, Food and Grocery Company, and other stores, asking them about their work, life, and studies.

Also spending the weekend with the sales clerks last night were Hu Yaobang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, Yao Yilin, Vice Minister of Commerce, and others.

### 陈云副总理同北京市售货员们度周末

1956-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    陈云副总理同北京市售货员们度周末
    新华社24日讯　国务院副总理陈云昨天晚上和北京市二千五百多个售货员一起度过了愉快的周末。
    北京市售货员周末联欢晚会设在御河桥一个槐树成荫的庭院里。晚会从七时半开始。在槐树林里，售货员们有的在下象棋，有的打桥牌，兴致勃勃地作着各种游戏。晚会上还举行了球类比赛、杂技演出和舞会。
    陈云走到游戏台前、舞池里、运动场边，和售货员们一起联欢。他并且同中国糖果糕点公司北京市公司西单食品门市部售货员冯惠良，和北京市百货公司、食品杂货公司等商店的售货员谈了话，问到他们工作、生活和学习的情况。
    昨天晚上和售货员们一块度过周末的，还有青年团中央书记处书记胡耀邦，商业部副部长姚依林等人。
