Title: Poland and Sweden Sign Aviation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-06-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, June 15th. Poland and Sweden recently held aviation talks in Warsaw, after which they signed an agreement for the flights of the two countries' aircraft on the Warsaw-Copenhagen-Stockholm route.

### 波兰瑞典签订航空协定

1956-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰瑞典签订航空协定
    新华社华沙15日电　波兰和瑞典最近在华沙举行了航空问题的谈判以后，签订了两国飞机在华沙—哥本哈根—斯德哥尔摩航线上航行的协定。
