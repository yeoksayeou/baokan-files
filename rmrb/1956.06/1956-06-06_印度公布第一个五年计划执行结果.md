Title: India Announces Results of First Five-Year Plan Implementation
Author:
Date: 1956-06-06
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 5th: According to a communiqué released by the Indian Information Bureau on June 3rd regarding the complete results of India's First Five-Year Plan, the national income increased by approximately 18%. The communiqué indicates that food production increased by 20%, cotton production increased by 45%, and the production of major oilseeds increased by 8%. More than 16 million acres of land were irrigated by large and small irrigation projects. The communiqué states that industrial production has steadily increased. Taking the provisional index of industrial production in 1946 as 100, it was 117 in 1951 and reached 161 in 1955. Electricity generation has increased from 6.575 billion kilowatt-hours in 1950-51 to 11 billion kilowatt-hours in 1955-56. Cement production is estimated to have increased from 2.7 million tons in 1950-51 to 4.3 million tons in 1955-56. The communiqué concludes by stating that preparations for the establishment of three steel plants and a heavy electrical plant have been completed, and a foundation has been laid for the greater tasks stipulated in the Second Five-Year Plan.

### 印度公布第一个五年计划执行结果

1956-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    印度公布第一个五年计划执行结果
    新华社新德里5日电　据印度新闻局六月三日发表的关于印度第一个五年计划的全部结果的公报说，国民收入增加了大约18％。公报表明，粮食生产增加了20％，棉花生产提高了45％，主要的油籽生产提高了8％。受到大小灌溉工程灌溉的土地共一千六百多万英亩。公报说，工业生产已经稳步地增加。以1946年的工业生产临时指数为一百，1951年为一百一十七，1955年达到一百六十一。发电量已经从1950—51年的六十五亿七千五百万瓩小时增加到1955—56年的一百一十亿瓩小时。水泥的生产，估计已从1950—51年的二百七十万吨增加到1955—56年的四百三十万吨。公报最后说，关于建立三个钢铁厂和重型电气工厂的准备工作已经完成，并且已经为第二个五年计划所规定的更大的任务打好了基础。
