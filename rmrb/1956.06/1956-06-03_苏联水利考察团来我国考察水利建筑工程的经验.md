Title: Soviet Water Conservancy Delegation Comes to China to Investigate the Experience of Water Conservancy Construction Projects
Author:
Date: 1956-06-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 1st. A Soviet water conservancy delegation, here to investigate water conservancy in China, will conduct a two-month inspection of irrigation and water conservancy projects in various parts of the country. The delegation arrived in Beijing on May 29th. They were warmly welcomed by the Ministry of Water Conservancy. Vice Minister of Water Conservancy Feng Zhongyun introduced them to the general situation of China's water conservancy construction.

The delegation consists of six experts, including Ananiev, Deputy Chief of the Water Conservancy General Bureau of the Soviet Ministry of Agriculture; Izmailov, Deputy Minister of the Water Conservancy Department of the Azerbaijan Soviet Socialist Republic; Prodorov, Chief Design Engineer of the Central Asian Cotton Planting Industry Water Conservancy Problem Design Institute; Smirnov, Deputy Chief of the Water Conservancy Bureau of the Soviet State Farm Ministry; Borodavchenko, Director of the Water Conservancy Bureau of the State Farm Ministry of the Russian Soviet Federated Socialist Republic; and Narozhansky, Dean of the Voroshilovgrad Water Conservancy Design Branch.

The Soviet water conservancy experts are visiting China to investigate technical experience in water conservancy construction projects in accordance with the resolution of the third meeting of the Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee.

### 苏联水利考察团来我国考察水利建筑工程的经验

1956-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联水利考察团来我国考察水利建筑工程的经验
    新华社1日讯　来中国考察水利的苏联水利考察团将在我国各地对灌溉和水利工程作两个月的考察。考察团是五月二十九日抵达北京的。他们在北京受到水利部的热烈欢迎，水利部副部长冯仲云曾向他们介绍了中国水利建设的一般情况。
    考察团由苏联农业部水利总局副局长安纳尼耶夫、阿塞拜疆加盟共和国水利部副部长依兹马依洛夫、中亚细亚植棉业水利问题设计院设计主任工程师普罗卓洛夫、苏联国营农场部水利局副局长斯米尔诺夫、俄罗斯联邦共和国国营农场部水利局局长鲍罗达夫倩柯和伏罗希洛夫斯克水利设计分院院长纳罗山维奇等六位专家组成。
    苏联水利专家是根据中苏科学技术合作委员会第三届会议的决议，来中国考察水利建筑工程方面的技术经验的。
