Title: The Friendship of Chinese and Japanese Railway Workers: Beijing Railway Workers Hold a Meeting to Welcome Japanese National Railway Workers
Author:
Date: 1956-06-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Beijing railway workers, trade union workers, and people from other circles, more than a thousand in total, held a welcoming meeting on the evening of the 5th to welcome the visiting friendship delegation of Japanese National Railway workers. They arrived in Beijing on the 4th. The head of the delegation, Suzuki Kiyoshi, said at the meeting that railway workers from both Japan and China must unite strongly and work together to promote the early normalization of relations between Japan and China.

Suzuki Kiyoshi said that we Japanese railway workers have fought for national independence, peace, democracy, and improved living standards, and have also fought for friendship between Japan and China and friendship between Japan and the Soviet Union; we will never forget the support given by the Chinese railway workers and the entire working class to our struggles; we believe that you will give us more support in the future.

At the welcoming meeting, the delegation presented the Chinese Railway Union with various gifts such as a model of the "Peace Locomotive."

At the welcoming meeting, Wang Zhijie, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Union, extended a warm welcome to the delegation on behalf of the 1.04 million railway workers in China.

Wang Zhijie said that in recent years, the friendly exchanges between the Chinese Railway Union and the Japanese National Railway Workers' Union, the Locomotive Workers' Union, and the Transportation Corporation Workers' Union have established good friendship and affection between railway workers of China and Japan, and increased the close understanding between railway workers of the two countries.

Wang Zhijie praised the heroic struggle of the Japanese railway workers and expressed the deep sympathy and brotherly support of the Chinese railway workers for their struggle.

### 中日铁路工人的友情  北京铁路工作者举行大会  欢迎日本的国营铁路工人

1956-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    中日铁路工人的友情
    北京铁路工作者举行大会
    欢迎日本的国营铁路工人
    据新华社讯　北京铁路工作者、工会工作者和其他各界人士一千多人五日晚举行欢迎大会，欢迎日本国营铁路工人访华友好代表团。他们是四日到达北京的。代表团团长铃木清在会上讲话说，日中两国铁路工人要坚强地团结起来，共同为促进日中两国关系的早日正常化而奋斗。
    铃木清说，我们日本铁路工人曾为了民族独立、和平、民主和改善生活而进行过斗争，也曾为了日中两国的友好，日苏两国的友好而进行过斗争；我们永远不能忘记中国铁路工人和整个工人阶级对我们的斗争所给予的支持；相信你们今后还会给我们更多的支持。
    在欢迎大会上，代表团向中国铁路工会赠送了“和平火车头”模型等各种各样礼品。
    在欢迎会上，中国铁路工会全国委员会主席王志杰代表中国一百零四万铁路职工向代表团表示热烈的欢迎。
    王志杰说，几年来中国铁路工会同日本国营铁路工会、机车工会、交通公社工会的友好往来，使中日两国铁路职工间建立了良好的友谊和感情，增加了两国铁路职工间的亲切了解。
    王志杰盛赞日本铁路工人的英勇斗争，并且表示中国铁路职工对于他们斗争的深切同情和兄弟般的支援。
