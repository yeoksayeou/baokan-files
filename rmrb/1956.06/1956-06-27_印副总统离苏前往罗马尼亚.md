Title: Indian Vice President Leaves Soviet Union for Romania
Author:
Date: 1956-06-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, June 26th: TASS reports that the Vice President of the Republic of India, Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan, left Kiev by plane today for Bucharest after a ten-day visit to the Soviet Union.

The Indian Vice President began his tour of the Soviet Union on June 19th from Moscow. He visited the capitals of the Kazakh, Uzbek, Azerbaijani, and Ukrainian Republics, where he was warmly welcomed by the leaders and working people of these republics.

### 印副总统离苏前往罗马尼亚

1956-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    印副总统离苏前往罗马尼亚
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社报道，印度共和国副总统拉德哈克里希南博士，在苏联作了十天的访问之后，在今天从基辅乘飞机前往布加勒斯特。
    印度副总统是六月十九日从莫斯科出发到苏联各地访问的。他访问了哈萨克、乌兹别克、阿塞拜疆和乌克兰等共和国的首都，受到了这些共和国领导人和劳动人民的热烈欢迎。
