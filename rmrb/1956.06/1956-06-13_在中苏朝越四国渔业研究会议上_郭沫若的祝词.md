Title: Guo Moruo's Congratulatory Speech at the Sino-Soviet-Korean-Vietnamese Fisheries Research Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-06-13
Page: 4

Guo Moruo's Congratulatory Speech at the Sino-Soviet-Korean-Vietnamese Fisheries Research Conference

Chairman, Representatives, Experts, Comrades:

Today, the Sino-Soviet-Korean-Vietnamese Fisheries Research Conference is officially held in Beijing. On behalf of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Chinese people, I extend my heartfelt and warm congratulations.

This is the first time that our four fraternal countries have held such a meeting to promote joint research in science. Because we have adjacent waters and jointly utilize aquatic resources, it is necessary to coordinate our scientific research work on fisheries, oceanography, and limnology, so that these scientific research works can achieve more and make the fisheries of the four countries have greater development. Therefore, this conference is of extremely important significance in further consolidating our friendship and developing our cause.

Since the arrival of friends from the Soviet, Korean, and Vietnamese delegations in Beijing, they have participated in the preparatory work for this meeting. Thanks to everyone's efforts, a draft agreement and the charter of the cooperation committee to be established in the future have been drafted, and opinions have been exchanged in advance on future technical cooperation. The valuable and diligent work of all friends has paved the way for the meeting, so that we can quickly realize the ideal of mutual assistance and common progress. On behalf of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Chinese people, I would like to express my heartfelt thanks to the Soviet delegation, the Korean delegation, and the Vietnamese delegation.

I believe that this conference will make great contributions to the development of fisheries in the four countries, the advancement of fisheries scientific research, and technical cooperation in relevant departments. I congratulate the successful conclusion of this conference and congratulate our cooperation in science and technology for further and comprehensive development. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 在中苏朝越四国渔业研究会议上  郭沫若的祝词

1956-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    在中苏朝越四国渔业研究会议上
    郭沫若的祝词主席、代表、专家、各位同志：
    今天，苏联、朝鲜、越南和中国四国渔业研究会议，在北京正式举行，我代表中国科学院和中国人民表示衷心的热烈的祝贺。
    我们四个兄弟国家举行这样的会议来促进在科学方面的共同研究，这还是第一次。由于我们有相互毗邻的水域，有共同利用的水产资源，所以有必要来协调我们关于渔业、海洋学和湖沼学的科学研究工作，使得这些科学研究工作，有更多的成就，使得四国的渔业有更大的发展。所以这一次的会议在进一步巩固我们的友谊，发展我们的事业上是有极其重大的意义的。
    苏联、朝鲜、越南代表团的各位朋友们来到北京以后，参加了这个会议的筹备工作，由于大家的努力，已经草拟出了协定的草案，草拟出了今后要成立的合作委员会的会章，并且在今后技术合作方面也预先交换了意见。各位朋友们的宝贵的辛勤的劳动，已经为会议铺平道路，使我们很快便可以实现相互协助、共同进步的理想，我代表中国科学院和中国人民向苏联代表团，朝鲜代表团和越南代表团致以衷心的感谢。
    我相信这次的会议对于四国渔业的发展，渔业科学研究的推进，以及在有关部分的技术合作方面，一定会做出很大的贡献。我祝贺这个会议的胜利成功，并祝贺我们在科学技术方面的合作获得更进一步的全面发展。（新华社）
