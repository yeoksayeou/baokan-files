Title: Premier Zhou Receives Soviet Vice Admiral Chekurov
Author:
Date: 1956-06-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd: Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China received Soviet Pacific Fleet Commander Vice Admiral Chekurov and Military Commissar Rear Admiral Pochubailo on the evening of June 23rd.

During the meeting, Vice Admiral Chekurov, on behalf of all officers and men of the Soviet Pacific Fleet, requested Premier Zhou Enlai to present the model of the cruiser "Dmitri Pozharsky" to Chairman Mao Zedong, expressing their love and admiration for the great leader of the Chinese people. Vice Admiral Chekurov also presented a model of a cruiser, a destroyer, and a torpedo boat to the Chinese People's Navy, and on behalf of Soviet Navy Commander-in-Chief Admiral Gorshkov, presented two volumes of nautical charts to Defense Minister Marshal Peng Dehuai.

Present at the reception were Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense Marshal Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier Marshal Chen Yi, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Chen Geng, Senior General Li Da, Senior General Xiao Hua, Senior General Wang Hongkun of the Navy, Senior General Su Zhenhua, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Major General Ji He, and Rear Admiral Peng Deqing of the Navy, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs He Wei and others.

Also present at the reception were Soviet Embassy Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Li Haiqiu and Military Attaché Major General Zudov.

### 周总理接见苏联切库罗夫海军中将

1956-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见苏联切库罗夫海军中将
    新华社23日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，在二十三日晚间接见了苏联太平洋舰队司令切库罗夫海军中将和军事委员波丘巴伊洛海军少将。
    切库罗夫海军中将在晋见时，代表苏联太平洋舰队全体官兵把“德米特里·波日阿尔斯基号”巡洋舰的模型请周恩来总理代为送交毛泽东主席，表示他们对中国人民的伟大领袖的爱戴。切库罗夫海军中将同时把一艘巡洋舰、一艘雷击舰和一艘鱼雷快艇的模型赠送给中国人民海军，并且代表苏联海军总司令戈尔什科夫海军上将，把两册海图赠送给国防部长彭德怀元帅。
    接见的时候，在座的有国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅，副总理陈毅元帅，中国人民解放军高级将领陈赓大将、李达上将、萧华上将、王宏坤海军上将、苏振华上将、萧向荣中将、吉合少将、彭德清海军少将，外交部部长助理何伟等人。
    接见的时候，苏联驻华大使馆临时代办李海秋、武官朱道夫少将也在座。
