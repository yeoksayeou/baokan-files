Title: Mass Blackboard
Author:
Date: 1948-02-23
Page: 1

　　Mass Blackboard
　　Our Northeast Liberation Army has won another great victory. We have recovered Anshan. Anshan is south of Shenyang, connected to Shenyang by railway. This place produces steel and machinery, ranking first in all of China. Faku County, north of Shenyang, has also been recovered by our Liberation Army. These two battles wiped out two entire divisions of Chiang Kai-shek's bandit troops.

### 大众黑板

1948-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　大众黑板
    咱们关东解放军又打了大胜仗。把鞍山收复了。鞍山在沈阳南边，有铁道和沈阳通着。这地方出产钢铁、机器，在全中国数第一。沈阳北边的法库县也叫咱们解放军收复了。这两仗消灭了蒋介石匪军两个整师。
