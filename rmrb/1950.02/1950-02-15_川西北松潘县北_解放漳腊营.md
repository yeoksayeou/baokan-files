Title: Northern Songpan County, Northwestern Sichuan, Liberates Zhang Laying Camp
Author:
Date: 1950-02-15
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, 14th: A unit of the People's Liberation Army liberated Zhang Laying Camp, north of Songpan County, on the 10th of this month. The pseudo-Songpan County Chief led more than 50 people in an attempt to flee north, but they were all annihilated by our forces en route.
    Furthermore, in the People's Liberation Army's battle to liberate Songpan, the majority of the bandit 134th Division was wiped out, capturing 843 bandits, including the division's political work director. In addition, more than 50 people surrendered under our political offensive.

### 川西北松潘县北  解放漳腊营

1950-02-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　川西北松潘县北  解放漳腊营
    【新华社成都十四日电】人民解放军某部于本月十日解放松潘县北之漳腊营。伪松潘县长率五十余人企图向北逃窜，在途中被我全部歼灭。
    又人民解放军解放松潘之战，歼匪一三四师大部，俘匪师政工主任以下八百四十三人。另在我政治攻势下投诚者五十余人。
