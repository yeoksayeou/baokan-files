Title: Tibetan Research Class Opens; Vice Chairman Zhu Attends and Speaks
Author: 
Date: 1950-02-02
Page: 3

　　Tibetan Research Class Opens
    Vice Chairman Zhu Attends and Speaks
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 1st. The Tibetan Research Class, organized by the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, opened today. At the opening ceremony, Vice Chairman Zhu De of the Central People's Government, Deputy Director Peng Zemin of the Political and Legal Affairs Committee, and Deputy Director Liu Geping of the Nationalities Affairs Commission attended and spoke, encouraging everyone to study hard the ethnic policies of the Central People's Government and to research the political, economic, military, cultural, and religious situation in Tibet.

### 藏民研究班开学  朱副主席出席讲话

1950-02-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　藏民研究班开学
    朱副主席出席讲话
    【新华社北京一日电】中央人民政府政务院民族事务委员会举办之藏民研究班今日开学。在开学典礼上，中央人民政府朱德副主席、政法委员会彭泽民副主任及民族事务委员会刘格平副主任出席讲话，勉励大家努力学习中央人民政府的民族政策，并研究西藏的政治、经济、军事、文化、宗教等情况。
