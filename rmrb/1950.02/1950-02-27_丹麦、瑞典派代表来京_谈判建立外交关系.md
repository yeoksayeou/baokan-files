Title: Danish and Swedish Representatives Arrive in Beijing to Negotiate the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1950-02-27
Page: 1

　　Danish and Swedish Representatives Arrive in Beijing
    Negotiations to Establish Diplomatic Relations
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 26th] In order to negotiate with the Central People's Government of China on the issue of establishing diplomatic relations, the Danish government has dispatched Mr. Munk as a negotiating representative, and the Swedish government has dispatched Mr. Krolus, who has been designated as Chargé d'affaires ad interim, as a negotiating representative. Mr. Munk and Mr. Krolus arrived in Beijing at 9:20 this morning. Han Xu, section chief of the Protocol Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, went to the station to greet them.

### 丹麦、瑞典派代表来京  谈判建立外交关系

1950-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　丹麦、瑞典派代表来京
    谈判建立外交关系
    【新华社北京二十六日电】为与我国中央人民政府就建立外交关系问题进行谈判，丹麦政府已派遣穆克为谈判代表，瑞典政府已派遣被指定为临时代办的高罗士为谈判代表。穆克及高罗士两氏已于今晨九时二十分抵京。我外交部派交际处科长韩叙赴站迎接。
