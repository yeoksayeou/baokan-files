Title: Vietnamese Women's Delegation Visits Beijing Municipal Democratic Women's Federation
Author:
Date: 1950-02-02
Page: 4

　　Vietnamese Women's Delegation Visits Beijing Municipal Democratic Women's Federation
    [Beijing News] The Vietnamese Women's Delegation, consisting of Nguyen Khoa Dieu Hong, Le Chan Phuong, Phan An, Hoang Boi Hoan, Nguyen Phuong Thao, and Ho Duc Minh, visited the Beijing Municipal Democratic Women's Federation yesterday morning (February 1st). Zhang Xiaomei, Chairwoman of the Beijing Municipal Democratic Women's Federation, gave them a detailed introduction to the situation and experience of women's work in Beijing. Secretary-General Hu Yizai described the experience of organizing women's production.

### 越南妇女代表团访京市民主妇联

1950-02-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南妇女代表团访京市民主妇联
    【北京讯】越南妇女代表团阮科耀红、黎真方、潘安、黄佩环、阮芳草、胡德明等于昨（二月一日）上午访问北京市民主妇女联合会。北京市民主妇女联合会主席张晓梅向她们详细介绍了北京妇女工作的情况和经验，胡一哉秘书长叙述了组织妇女生产的工作经验。
