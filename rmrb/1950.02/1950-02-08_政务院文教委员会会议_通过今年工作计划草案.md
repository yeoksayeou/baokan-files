Title: Government Administration Council's Cultural and Educational Committee Meeting Approves Draft of This Year's Work Plan
Author:
Date: 1950-02-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 7th. The Government Administration Council's Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government held its second plenary meeting on the 5th and 6th of this month, and approved a draft of the committee's work plan for 1950. This draft will be implemented after being submitted to the Government Administration Council for approval.

The meeting began at 9:00 AM on the 5th of this month. The attendees included Chairman Guo Moruo, Vice Chairmen Lu Dingyi and Shen Yanbing, and committee members Zhou Yang, Ding Xielin, Qian Junrui, Wei Que, Li Dequan, He Cheng, Su Jinguan, Tao Menghe, Zhu Kezhen, Hu Qiaomu, Hu Yuzhi, Fei Xiaotong, Wu Han, Liu Qingyang, Pan Guangdan, Fu Dingyi, Jiang Nanxiang, Shen Zijiu, Xie Bangding, Ouyang Yuqian, Tian Han, Yang Hansheng, Qian Sanqiang, Jian Bozan, Hou Wailu, Qian Duansheng, Zeng Zhaosen, Shen Tilan, Liu Yazi, Zhang Dongsun, Chen Heqin, Lei Jieqiong, and 35 others. More than 90 responsible individuals at the level of division and bureau chiefs from various ministries, academies, and administrations under the Cultural and Educational Committee attended as observers. Representatives from the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, as well as the People's Supervision Committee, the Political and Legal Committee, and the Financial and Economic Committee, also participated. Vice Premier Dong Biwu attended to provide guidance. On the first day of the meeting, Vice Chairman Shen Yanbing presided. Vice Premier Dong Biwu reported on the recent domestic military, political, and diplomatic situation. Chairman Guo Moruo reported on the main points of the Cultural and Educational Committee's work plan. The heads of various ministries, academies, and administrations each made reports on their work over the past three months. Secretary-General Hu Qiaomu reported on the draft of the Cultural and Educational Committee's 1950 annual expenditure budget. On the second day of the meeting, Vice Chairman Lu Dingyi presided. After four hours of discussion, the meeting approved the draft of the Cultural and Educational Committee's 1950 work plan and also approved the proposal by Committee Member Shen Tilan and others to establish normal universities or normal colleges in major administrative regions, deciding to submit it to the Ministry of Education for study and handling.

### 政务院文教委员会会议  通过今年工作计划草案

1950-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院文教委员会会议  通过今年工作计划草案
    【新华社北京七日电】中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会于本月五、六两日举行第二次全体会议，通过了该会一九五○年工作计划的草案。这个草案将于提请政务院通过后实施。
    会议于本月五日上午九时开始，出席主任郭沫若，副主任陆定一和沈雁冰，委员周扬、丁燮林、钱俊瑞、韦悫、李德全、贺诚、苏井观、陶孟和、竺可桢、胡乔木、胡愈之、费孝通、吴晗、刘清扬、潘光旦、符定一、蒋南翔、沈兹九、谢邦定、欧阳予倩、田汉、阳翰笙、钱三强、翦伯赞、侯外栌、钱端升、曾昭森、沈体兰、柳亚子、张东荪、陈鹤琴、雷洁琼等三十五人。列席者文教委员会所属各部、院、署的厅司局长以上负责人九十余人。政协全国委员会及人民监察、政法、财经各委均派代表参加。董必武副总理亲临指导。会议第一日由沈雁冰副主任任主席，董必武副总理报告最近国内军事、政治、外交情况，郭沫若主任报告文教委员会工作计划要点，各部院署首长分别作了三个月的工作报告，胡乔木秘书长报告文教委员会一九五○年度事业费岁出总概算草案。第二日的会议由陆定一副主任任主席。会议在四小时的讨论后通过了文教委员会一九五○年工作计划草案，并通过了沈体兰委员等关于在各大行政区设立师范大学或师范学院的提案，决定交教育部研究办理。
