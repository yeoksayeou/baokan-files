Title: Bovine Plague Basically Eliminated in Henan
Author:
Date: 1950-02-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Kaifeng, 2nd: The veterinary epidemic prevention team dispatched to Henan Province by the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government has basically eliminated bovine plague in Henan Province. After arriving in Henan Province at the end of last year, the team immediately joined the epidemic prevention team organized by the Henan Provincial Government and went to the Nanyang and Huaiyang special districts, where bovine plague was more serious, to carry out emergency treatment and prevention work. In just twenty-five days, they traveled to more than ten counties and treated and rescued more than 159,600 head of cattle.

### 河南牛瘟基本扑灭

1950-02-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　河南牛瘟基本扑灭
    【新华社开封二日电】中央人民政府农业部派遣来豫的兽疫防治队，已将河南省的牛瘟病基本扑灭。该队于去年年底抵达河南省后，立即会同河南省政府组织的防疫队，分赴牛瘟较严重的南阳、淮阳两专区，进行急救和预防工作，在短短二十五天内，走遍十余个县份，共防治和急救出耕牛十五万九千六百余头。
