Title: Sino-Soviet Alliance Inspires Japanese People's Struggle Confidence: Japanese Communist Party Akahata Editorial Hails
Author:
Date: 1950-02-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 19th: According to a United Press report from Tokyo: The Japanese Communist Party's organ, Akahata, published an editorial on the 17th, hailing the Sino-Soviet alliance as a great contribution to world peace. The editorial declared: The Japanese Communist Party fully supports the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance and will continue to fight against the elements reviving Japanese imperialism. The editorial pointed out that the treaty and agreements signed by China and the Soviet Union on the 14th will be an inspiration and encouragement to Japanese patriots and the Japanese people, and will give them confidence, while it will be a powerful deterrent to Japanese imperialists and other countries that are trying to turn Japan into a base for aggression. The editorial also condemned imperialism's attempt to use Japan as a base for aggression, pointing out that this is a violation of the Potsdam Declaration, which guarantees the democratization and demilitarization of Japan.

### 鼓舞了日本人民斗争信心  日共赤旗报社论欢呼中苏同盟

1950-02-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　鼓舞了日本人民斗争信心
    日共赤旗报社论欢呼中苏同盟
    【新华社北京十九日电】据合众社东京讯：日本共产党机关报赤旗报于十七日发表社论，欢呼中苏同盟，认为它是对世界和平的巨大贡献。社论宣称：日本共产党完全拥护中苏友好同盟互助条约，并将继续对复活日本帝国主义者的分子作斗争。社论指出，中苏两国于十四日签订的条约与协定，对于日本爱国分子和日本人民，将是一种鼓舞与鼓励，并给他们以信心，而对日本帝国主义者和其他竭力想把日本变为侵略基地的国家，则是一种强有力的喝止。社论并谴责企图将日本作为侵略基地的帝国主义，指出这是违反保证日本民主化和非军事化的波茨坦宣言的。
