Title: Breaking the Shackles of Imperialism of the Past Century: Chinese and Vietnamese People Will Unite More Closely. President of the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association Speaks on "Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Day"
Author:
Date: 1950-02-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam News: Hu Songmao, President of the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association, when discussing the relationship between China and Vietnam with a reporter from the Chinese Affairs Bureau of the Vietnamese Ministry of Internal Affairs' newspaper, *China News*, on "Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Day" (February 18th), said: "For a hundred years, capitalists and imperialists have consistently tried to isolate the people of our two countries. But today, we have broken the shackles of imperialism. Undoubtedly, the Chinese and Vietnamese people will understand each other better. Exchanges in all aspects, especially cultural exchanges, will be a natural thing. The best way to increase cultural exchange between our two countries is to develop the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association."

### 打碎百年来帝国主义桎梏  中越人民将更加团结  中越友协会长在“越苏中友好日”谈话

1950-02-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　打碎百年来帝国主义桎梏
    中越人民将更加团结     
    中越友协会长在“越苏中友好日”谈话
    【新华社北京二十一日电】据越南通讯社北越讯：中越友好协会会长胡松贸在“越、苏、中友好日”（十八日）对越南内政部中国事务局机关报《中国新闻》的记者论及中越两国的关系时，他说：“一百年来，资本家和帝国主义者们经常竭力隔离我们两国人民。而今天，我们已打碎了帝国主义的桎梏。无疑的，中国和越南人民将会更加互相了解。各方面的交流，特别是文化的交流，将是自然的事情。增加我们两国文化交流最好的办法，就是发展中越友好协会。”
