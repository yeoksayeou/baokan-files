Title: National Student Federation Executive Committee Meeting Concludes; Vice Chairman Zhu De Encourages Students Nationwide to Study Science, Love Labor, and Love the Motherland
Author: 
Date: 1950-02-24
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 23rd. The Second Enlarged Meeting of the 14th Executive Committee of the All-China Students' Federation concluded successfully on the 22nd. Before the closing of the meeting, Vice Chairman Zhu De of the Central People's Government attended and delivered a speech, which was warmly welcomed by all the executive committee members. In his speech, Vice Chairman Zhu emphasized that the basic task of Chinese students at present is to study, to study Marxism-Leninism, natural sciences, and military knowledge well, and to participate in voluntary labor in their spare time. Finally, he called on students nationwide to love labor, love the motherland, study hard, and strive to build a new democratic China. This meeting began on the 20th. Qian Junrui, Vice Minister of Education of the Central People's Government, and Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, reported on the first day of the meeting on the current educational problems in our country and the international and domestic situation. On the second day of the meeting (the 21st), the main agenda was the work report of the Presidium of the All-China Students' Federation on the work since the First Executive Committee Meeting of the 14th All-China Students' Federation, and the introduction of experiences in carrying out New Democratic studies in Harbin, Jilin, Beijing, Shanghai and other regions. On the second and third days, the meeting held group discussions on the reports of Qian Junrui and Feng Wenbin, as well as the essay "On Carrying out New Democratic Studies in Schools" recently published by Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretary of the Central Committee of the Youth League, in the 33rd issue of the Youth League Central Committee's publication "China Youth." Everyone unanimously agreed that due to the development of the revolutionary situation, the student movement has entered a new historical period. The task of Chinese students at present is to unite the vast number of students under the leadership of the People's Government and carry out New Democratic studies in order to better contribute their talents to building a new democratic China. Before the closing of the meeting, the meeting adopted a resolution on the current tasks of Chinese students. A total of sixty-four executive committee members attended the meeting. Except for Taiwan, Tibet, Hainan Island and other places that have not yet been liberated, and representatives from Xinjiang, Yunnan, Xikang and other places who have not yet arrived, representatives from all over the country participated. Compared with the 14th National Student Congress and the First Executive Committee Meeting held in March last year, the number of representatives from the newly liberated eight provinces in the Southwest, East China, Central South, and Northwest regions has increased, and the number of executive committee members has also increased by twenty-seven.

### 全国学联执委会议闭幕  朱德副主席勉励全国学生学习科学热爱劳动热爱祖国

1950-02-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　全国学联执委会议闭幕
    朱德副主席勉励全国学生学习科学热爱劳动热爱祖国
    【新华社北京二十三日电】中华全国学生联合会第十四届第二次执行委员会扩大会议于二十二日圆满闭幕。闭会前，中央人民政府朱德副主席曾到会讲话，受到全体执委的热烈欢迎。朱副主席在讲话中，着重指出目前中国学生的基本任务就是学习，要好好学习马列主义、自然科学和军事知识，并在课余参加义务劳动。最后他号召全国学生要热爱劳动，热爱祖国，好好学习，为建设新民主主义的中国而奋斗。此次会议系于二十日开始。中央人民政府教育部副部长钱俊瑞、新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬曾在第一日会议上报告：目前我国教育问题和国际国内形势。会议第二日（二十一日），主要议程为全国学联主席团关于中华全国学生联合会第十四届第一次执行委员会以来的工作报告和哈尔滨、吉林、北京、上海等地区开展新民主主义学习的经验介绍。会议在第二、三日曾分组讨论了钱俊瑞和冯文彬的报告，以及青年团中央副书记蒋南翔最近在团中央机关刊物“中国青年”第三十三期发表的“论开展学校中的新民主主义学习”的论文。大家一致认为：由于革命形势的发展，学生运动已进入一个新的历史时期。目前中国学生的任务就是在人民政府的领导下，团结广大同学，开展新民主主义学习，以便更好的贡献自己的才能，建设新民主主义的中国。会议于闭会前通过关于中国学生当前任务的决议一项。参加此次会议的执委达六十四人，除台湾、西藏、海南岛等地尚未解放，新疆、云南、西康等地代表尚未到达外，全国各地都有代表参加，比去年三月全国学生第十四届代表大会和第一次执行委员会议开会时增加了西南区和华东、中南、西北等地区新解放的八个省份的代表，执委人数也增加了二十七人。
