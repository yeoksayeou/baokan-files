Title: Beijing Postal District Postal Workers' Union to Open Member Representative Conference Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1950-02-04
Page: 4

　　Beijing Postal District Postal Workers' Union to Open Member Representative Conference Tomorrow
    [This newspaper dispatch] The inaugural Member Representative Conference of the Beijing Postal District Postal Workers' Union will be held on February 5th at the Beijing Postal Administration on Public Security Street in this city, and the Beijing Postal District Postal Workers' Union will be formally established. (Beijing Postal Workers' Union)

### 北京邮区邮政工会  明开会员代表大会

1950-02-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京邮区邮政工会  明开会员代表大会
    【本报讯】北京邮区邮政工会首届会员代表大会将于二月五日在本市公安街北京邮政管理局召开，并正式成立北京邮区邮政工会。（北京邮政工会）
