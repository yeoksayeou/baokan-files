Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Issues Notice on Convening a National Trade Union Organizational Work Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-02-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 3rd. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions issued a notice regarding the convening of a National Trade Union Organizational Work Conference. The full text is as follows:

　　(1) The conference will be held in Beijing on March 25th and will last for ten days.

　　(2) Topics for discussion at the conference:
　　1. Issues related to the work of grassroots trade union organizations: organizational forms and work content of grassroots trade unions.
　　2. Various issues related to the establishment of various industrial trade unions.
　　3. The scope and tasks of the work and organizational structure of various industrial trade unions and regional trade union organizational departments.

　　(3) The number of representatives allocated to each level is as follows:
　　1. The chairman (director) or organizational minister of the preparatory committee (or working committee) of the National Railway, Postal, Telecommunications, Textile, Coal Mining, Hardware, Electrical, Food, Shop Employees, and Transportation Industrial Trade Unions.
　　2. Each of the Northeast, North China, East China, Central South, Northwest, and Southwest Greater Administrative Regions' Federation of Trade Unions (or offices) and each industrial trade union in the region will send one chairman (director) or organizational minister.
　　3. The Beijing, Tianjin, and Shanghai Municipal Federations of Trade Unions and each of the major industrial trade unions in each city will send one chairman or organizational minister.
　　4. Each provincial federation of trade unions in the six provinces of Northeast China, Hebei, Shanxi, Pingyuan, Chahar, Suiyuan, Shandong, Northern Jiangsu, Southern Jiangsu, Northern Anhui, Southern Anhui, Zhejiang, Fujian, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Guangdong, and Guangxi will send one chairman or organizational minister.
　　The Northwest and Southwest provinces will be determined by the Northwest Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee and the Southwest Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.
　　5. Each of the following cities will send one chairman or organizational minister of the municipal federation of trade unions: Harbin, Shenyang, Changchun, Andong, Lushun-Dalian, Fushun, Anshan, Tangshan, Taiyuan, Shijiazhuang, Jinan, Qingdao, Xuzhou, Nanjing, Wuxi, Hangzhou, Fuzhou, Kaifeng, Zhengzhou, Wuhan, Nanchang, Changsha, Guangzhou, Chongqing, and Xi'an.

　　(4) In order to solve the problems of grassroots trade union organization work, the Railway, Coal Mining, and Textile Industrial Trade Unions will each select one or two trade union chairmen and organizational committee members from large mines and factories with good trade union work, one workshop or branch committee member, and one group leader.

　　(5) Each region and each industrial trade union should prepare relevant materials according to the content of the conference discussions, and at the same time, send the organizational situation of the region and each industrial trade union by March 10th. Representatives from various places should report to Beijing by March 20th.

　　(6) In order to hold the conference well, research groups will be organized for the following three issues to prepare materials and research work separately. The following places will send capable cadres to participate. The resumes of the appointed cadres should be reported before February 20th; after approval by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, they should bring the materials from their respective regions to Beijing before March 1st.
　　1. Grassroots Trade Union Group: Each of the Northeast Railway, Mining, Dalian Jinzhou Textile Factory, Tianjin Zhongfang, Shanghai Textile, and North China Coal Mine Trade Unions will send one person.
　　2. Organizational Department Work Group: Each of the Northeast and Central South Federations of Trade Unions and the Lushun-Dalian, Harbin, Tianjin, Shanghai, and Beijing Municipal Federations of Trade Unions will send one organizational minister or chairman.
　　3. Industrial Trade Union Group: Each industrial trade union in the Northeast will send one person, and Tianjin, Shanghai, Wuhan, and Nanjing will each send one person.

　　General Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, January 28, 1950

### 中华全国总工会发出通知  召开全国工会组织工作会议

1950-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　中华全国总工会发出通知
    召开全国工会组织工作会议
    【新华社北京三日电】中华全国总工会发出关于召开全国工会组织工作会议的通知。全文如下：
    （一）会议于三月二十五日在京召开，会议时间十天。
    （二）会议讨论内容：
    １、工会基层组织工作的问题：基层工会组织形式与工作内容。
    ２、有关建立各种产业工会各项问题。
    ３、各产业工会与各地区工会组织部之工作范围与任务及其机构问题。
    （三）各级出席代表名额分配如下：
    １、全国铁路、邮政、电讯、纺织、煤矿、五金、电业、食品、店员、搬运等产业总工会筹委会（或工作委员会）的主席（主任）或组织部长一人。
    ２、东北、华北、华东、中南、西北、西南各大行政区总工会（或办事处）及该区各产业总工会各派主席（主任）或组织部长一人。
    ３、北京、天津、上海市总工会及各该市主要产业工会，各派主席或组织部长一人。
    ４、东北六省，河北、山西、平原、察哈尔、绥远、山东、苏北、苏南、皖北、皖南、浙江、福建、河南、湖北、湖南、江西、广东、广西，以上各省总工会各派主席或组织部长一人。
    西北、西南所属各省，由西北总工会筹委会和中华全国总工会西南办事处决定选派。
    ５、哈尔滨、沈阳、长春、安东、旅大、抚顺、鞍山、唐山、太原、石家庄、济南、青岛、徐州、南京、无锡、杭州、福州、开封、郑州、武汉、南昌、长沙、广州、重庆、西安以上各城市各派市总工会主席或组织部长一人。
    （四）为了解决基层工会组织工作问题，由铁路、煤矿、纺织产业总工会各选派一两个大的矿厂工会工作好的工会主席和组织委员各一人，同一工厂车间或支会委员一人，小组长一人。
    （五）各地区、各产业总工会各按会议讨论内容准备有关材料，同时将该地区和各产业工会的组织情况，务必于三月十日前寄来，各地出席之代表应于三月二十日抵京报到。
    （六）为了把会议开好，组织下列三个问题的研究小组，分头准备材料研究工作，由以下各地派得力干部参加。所指派干部简历二月二十日前报来；经全国总工会认可后，携带各该地区的材料，于三月一日前到京。
    １、基层工会组：东北铁路、矿山、大连金州纺织厂、天津中纺、上海纺织、华北煤矿等工会各派一人。
    ２、组织部工作组：东北、中南总工会及旅大、哈尔滨、天津、上海、北京等市总工会组织部长或主席各一人。
    ３、产业工会组：东北各产业总工会选派一人，天津、上海、武汉、南京各一人。
    中华全国总工会办公厅  一九五○年一月二十八日
