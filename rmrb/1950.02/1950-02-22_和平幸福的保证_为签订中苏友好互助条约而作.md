Title: Guarantee of Peace and Happiness - Written for the Signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance
Author: Xiao Songyu
Date: 1950-02-22
Page: 6

　　Guarantee of Peace and Happiness
    Written for the Signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance
　　Xiao Songyu
    In the Kremlin,
    Two giants
    Hold hands;
    Face to face.
    Stalin's hand
    Mao Zedong's hand
    Steel bars forged in the furnace,
    Hands clasped tightly together;
    A castle of peace,
    Built before the people of the whole world.
    In Mao Zedong's eyes,
    The Soviet people are jumping.
    In Stalin's eyes,
    The sons and daughters of China are surging.
    Mao Zedong looks at Stalin!
    Stalin looks at Mao Zedong!
    The eyes of giants
    See there,
    There the singing surges.
    The singing becomes notes;
    The notes become words,
    The notes play the resonance of the heart;
    The words write the harmony of the mouth,
    This is not an ordinary "treaty";
    This is not an ordinary "agreement,"
    The most appropriate name—
    "Guarantee of Human Peace and Happiness"!
    Go away!
    Running dogs of imperialism!
    Go away!
    Criminals who provoke war!
    This song makes you shrug and tremble!
    This castle makes you cower and dare not step forward!
    In the Kremlin,
    Two giants
    Hot hands holding hot hands.
    In the Kremlin,
    Two giants
    Kind faces facing kind faces.
          February 17th

### 和平幸福的保证  为签订中苏友好互助条约而作
萧松煜
1950-02-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　和平幸福的保证
    为签订中苏友好互助条约而作
　　萧松煜
    克里姆林宫里，
    两个巨人
    手握着手；
    面对着面。
    斯大林的手
    毛泽东的手
    炼炉里打成的钢筋，
    双手紧紧握在一起；
    和平的城堡，
    在全世界的人们面前筑成。
    毛泽东的眼睛里，
    苏联人民在跳跃。
    斯大林的眼睛里，
    中华儿女在奔腾。
    毛泽东看着斯大林！
    斯大林看着毛泽东！
    巨人的眼睛
    看到那里，
    那里就歌声奔腾。
    歌声变成音符；
    音符变成字形，
    音符奏出心底的共鸣；
    字形写出嘴里的和声，
    这不是平常的“条约”；
    这不是平常的“协定”，
    最恰当的名称——
    “人类和平幸福的保证”！
    去吧！
    帝国主义的走狗！
    去吧！
    挑拨战争的罪犯！
    这歌声使你们耸肩发抖！
    这城堡使你们畏缩不敢上前！
    克里姆林宫里，
    两个巨人
    热手握着热手。
    克里姆林宫里，
    两个巨人
    慈颜对着慈颜。
          二，十七
