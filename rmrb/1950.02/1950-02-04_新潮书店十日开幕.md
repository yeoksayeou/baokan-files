Title: New Tide Bookstore to Open on the 10th

Author: 
Date: 1950-02-04
Page: 3

　　New Tide Bookstore to Open on the 10th
    [Report from this newspaper] The New Tide Bookstore at 107 Wangfujing Street, Beijing, is ready and will officially open on February 10th. This bookstore sells various progressive books and periodicals, as well as English and Russian foreign language books. It is reported that there will be a grand sale for ten days during the opening period, and exquisite bookmarks will be given away.

### 新潮书店十日开幕

1950-02-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　新潮书店十日开幕
    【本报讯】京市王府井大街一○七号新潮书店业已筹备就绪，定于二月十日正式开幕。该店经售各类进步书刊，以及英、俄外文版书籍。闻于开幕期间，将举行大廉价十天，并赠送精美书签。
