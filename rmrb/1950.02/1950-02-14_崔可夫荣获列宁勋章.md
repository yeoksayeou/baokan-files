Title: Chuikov Awarded the Order of Lenin
Author:
Date: 1950-02-14
Page: 4

　　Chuikov Awarded the Order of Lenin
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th—TASS reports: On the occasion of Army General Chuikov's fiftieth birthday, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, considering the merits he has rendered to the Soviet state and armed forces, issued a decree on the 11th to award Vasily Ivanovich Chuikov the Order of Lenin.
    Note: General Chuikov is a famous hero of the great Battle of Stalingrad, and is currently the chairman of the Soviet Control Commission in Germany.

### 崔可夫荣获列宁勋章

1950-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　崔可夫荣获列宁勋章
    【新华社北京十三日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：陆军上将崔可夫五十寿辰之际，苏联最高苏维埃主席团考虑到他对苏联的国家与武装部队所建的功勋，特于十一日下令授予瓦西里·伊凡诺维奇·崔可夫以列宁勋章。
    按：崔可夫将军是伟大的斯大林格勒保卫战的著名英雄，现任德境苏联管制委员会的主席。
