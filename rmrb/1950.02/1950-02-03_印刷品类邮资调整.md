Title: Postage Adjustment for Printed Matter
Author:
Date: 1950-02-03
Page: 4

　　Printed Matter Postage Adjustment
    [This City News] The postage for domestic printed matter and newsprint will be adjusted from February 1st as follows: (1) Printed matter (the same applies to documents for the blind and samples) will be 200 yuan for every 50 grams or fraction thereof, regardless of whether it is within or outside the district. (2) Ordinary newsprint will be 150 yuan for every 50 grams or fraction thereof, regardless of whether it is within or outside the district; newsprint with coupons will be 250 yuan for every 100 grams or fraction thereof; bulk newsprint will be 130 yuan per kilogram or fraction thereof. (Social Service Section of the Beijing Postal Administration)

### 印刷品类邮资调整

1950-02-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　印刷品类邮资调整
    【本市讯】国内互寄印刷品类及新闻纸类邮资自二月一日起调整如下：（一）印刷品类（瞽者文件及货样同）每重五十公分或其零数不分区内区外一律为二百元。（二）普通新闻纸每重五十公分或其零数不分区内区外一百五十元，立券新闻纸每重一百公分或其零数二百五十元，总包新闻纸每公斤或其零数一百三十元。（北京邮政管理局社会服务股）
