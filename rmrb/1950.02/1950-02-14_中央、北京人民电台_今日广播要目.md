Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
Author:
Date: 1950-02-14
Page: 4

　　Central, Beijing People's Radio
    Today's Broadcast Highlights
    [This Dispatch] Central People's Broadcasting Station today (February 14th) 17:30 Workers' Time: "How to implement the resolutions of the National Textile Workers' Union Representative Meeting and strive to restore and develop national textile industrial production." 18:30 Natural Science Lecture: General Knowledge of Electricity, Lecture 15, "The Magnetic Effect of Electric Current and Electric Bells and Telegraphs." 18:00 First Literary Program: Qinshu Storytelling "Dong Cunrui Sacrifices Himself to Blow Up the Bunker." 21:00 Second Literary Program: Yangko Opera "Milling Victory Rice." 18:45 New Songs: "Ode to the Chinese Communist Party," etc.
    [Another Dispatch] Beijing People's Radio 12:30 Workers' Program: Peng Qingzhao continues to lecture on the second lesson of the worker's political textbook, "Several Stages of Old Society's Development." 13:00 Women's Program: (1) Introducing the fourth issue of "Beijing Women," (2) An article from the fourth issue of "Beijing Women," "How Intellectual Women Should Transform Themselves." 16:30 Children's Program: (1) Current Affairs Talk, (2) Story – "The Proud Little Crab," (3) Singing. 17:00, Teaching to sing the Northeast folk song "Busy with Production." 17:45 Voice of the Municipality: Beijing Municipal Public Security Bureau's report on accepting the people's request to disband "hug-and-grab" and other hooligan organizations. 18:45 Listener Service: Talking about the use and protection of radios.

### 中央、北京人民电台  今日广播要目

1950-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台
    今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（十四日）十七点三十分职工时间：“如何贯彻全国纺织工会代表会议的各项决议，为恢复与发展全国纺织工业生产而斗争”。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：电学常识第十五讲“电流的磁效应和电铃电报”。十八点第一次文艺节目：琴书《董存瑞舍身炸碉堡》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：秧歌剧《碾胜利米》。十八点四十五分新歌曲：《中国共产党颂》等。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工节目：彭庆昭继续讲工人政治课本第二课“旧社会发展的几个阶段”。十三点妇女节目：（一）介绍《北京妇女》第四期，（二）《北京妇女》第四期上的一篇文章《知识妇女应如何改造》。十六点三十分儿童节目：（一）时事讲话，（二）故事——《骄傲的小螃蟹》，（三）唱歌。十七点，教唱东北民歌《生产忙》。十七点四十五分市政之声：北京市公安局关于接受人民要求解散“搂包”等流氓组织的报导。十八点四十五分听众服务：谈一谈收音机的使用法和保护法。
