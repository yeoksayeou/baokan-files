Title: Bank of China Shanghai Branch and Singapore Island Remittance

Author: 
Date: 1950-02-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 3rd.—To facilitate overseas Chinese remittances to the country, the Shanghai Branch of the Bank of China has begun remittance services with Singapore, Kuala Lumpur, and Penang.

### 中国银行沪分行与星岛通汇

1950-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国银行沪分行与星岛通汇
    【新华社上海三日电】中国银行上海分行为便利国外侨胞汇款回国，已开始与新加坡、吉隆坡、槟榔屿三地通汇。
