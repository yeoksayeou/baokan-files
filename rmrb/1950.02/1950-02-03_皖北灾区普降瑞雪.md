Title: Auspicious Snowfall in Disaster Areas of Northern Anhui
Author:
Date: 1950-02-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hefei, February 2nd. Since January 28th and lasting until February 1st, the disaster areas of Northern Anhui, including Suxian, Lingbi, and Sixian Counties, have experienced widespread auspicious snowfall, accumulating to about three inches. The farmers are all overjoyed.

### 皖北灾区普降瑞雪

1950-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　皖北灾区普降瑞雪
    【新华社合肥二日电】皖北宿县、灵壁、泗县等灾区，自一月二十八日起至二月一日止，普降瑞雪，积雪三寸许，农民莫不欢腾。
