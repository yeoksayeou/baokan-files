Title: Vice Foreign Minister Li Kenong Receives Hungarian Minister

Author: 
Date: 1950-02-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 11th. Hungarian People's Republic Minister to China, Száfrankó, accompanied by Second Secretaries Fekete and Simonyi, and Secretary Flórián, paid a formal visit to Vice Foreign Minister Li Kenong of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 3:30 this afternoon. Also present were Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, and Shen Ping, Deputy Director.

### 李克农副外长接见匈牙利公使

1950-02-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　李克农副外长接见匈牙利公使
    【新华社北京十一日电】匈牙利人民共和国驻我国公使夏法朗柯于今日下午三时半偕费凯尔特，希蒙尼二参事及佛洛瑞安秘书，正式拜访我外交部李克农副部长。在座者尚有外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航、交际处长王倬如、副处长沈平等。
