Title: Sunday Literary and Art Lecture: Zhou Yang Lectures on Issues of Mass Literature and Art
Author:
Date: 1950-02-25
Page: 4

　　Sunday Literary and Art Lecture
    Zhou Yang Lectures on Issues of Mass Literature and Art
    [From our newspaper] The Sunday Literary and Art Lecture series, jointly sponsored by the Mass Literature and Art Creation Research Association and the Youth Service Department, has invited Zhou Yang, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture, to be the main speaker this week. The topic of the lecture is "Issues of Mass Literature and Art." After the lecture, Guan Xuezeng and Wu Changbao will perform the Qinshu "Three Sons-in-Law Bowing for Longevity." The lecture address is at the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association, 19 Nanheyan. Time: Sunday morning at 9:30. Admission by long-term ticket or temporary ticket. Temporary tickets can be collected from the Youth Service Department on Saturday.

### 星期文艺讲演会  周扬主讲大众文艺问题

1950-02-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　星期文艺讲演会
    周扬主讲大众文艺问题
    【本报讯】大众文艺创作研究会与青年服务部联合主办的星期文艺讲演会，本周聘请中央文化部副部长周扬主讲。讲题为《大众文艺问题》。会后由关学增、吴长宝表演琴书“三婿拜寿”。讲演地址在南河沿十九号苏联对外文化协会。时间：星期日上午九时半。凭长期券、临时券入场。临时券可于星期六到青年服务部领取。
