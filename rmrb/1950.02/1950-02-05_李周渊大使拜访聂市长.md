Title: Ambassador Li Zhouyuan Visits Mayor Nie

Author:
Date: 1950-02-05
Page: 1

　　[Beijing News] Li Zhouyuan, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, paid a visit to Mayor Nie Rongzhen at 2:00 pm today. Deputy Mayor Zhang Youyu was also present.

### 李周渊大使拜访聂市长

1950-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　李周渊大使拜访聂市长
    【北京讯】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使李周渊于今日下午二时拜访聂荣臻市长，在座者有张友渔副市长。
