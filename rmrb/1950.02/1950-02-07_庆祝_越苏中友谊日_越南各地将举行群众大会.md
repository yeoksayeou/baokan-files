Title: Mass Rallies to be Held in Various Parts of Vietnam to Celebrate "Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Day"
Author:
Date: 1950-02-07
Page: 4

　　Celebrating "Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Day"
　　Mass Rallies to be Held in Various Parts of Vietnam
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 6th. Hanoi, North Vietnam, 5th (Vietnam News Agency): Vietnam is enthusiastically preparing to celebrate "Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Day," scheduled for February 18th. This celebration is hosted by the Vietnam National United Front, representing all the people of Vietnam, to congratulate Vietnam on establishing diplomatic relations with China and the Soviet Union. On that day, mass rallies will be held throughout the country to explain the historical significance of the establishment of friendly relations between China, the Soviet Union, and Vietnam, Vietnam's current position in the world, and the impact of this major event on Vietnam's national liberation struggle.
　　The national flags of the Soviet Union, New China, and Vietnam will fly throughout Vietnam, with huge banners reading: Long Live Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship! Down with French Imperialists! Down with International Reactionaries! We are fighting to defend world peace and democracy! Long Live the Soviet Union! Long Live the People's Republic of China! Long Live Marshal Stalin! Long Live Chairman Mao Zedong! Long Live Chairman Ho Chi Minh!

### 庆祝“越苏中友谊日”  越南各地将举行群众大会

1950-02-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝“越苏中友谊日”
    越南各地将举行群众大会
    【新华社北京六日电】越南通讯社北越五日讯：越南各地正热烈筹备庆祝定于二月十八日举行的“越、苏、中友谊日”。这个庆祝是由代表越南全体人民的越南民族统一战线所主持，以祝贺越南和中苏两国建立外交关系。在那一天，全国各地将举行群众集会，说明中、苏、越三国建立友好关系的历史意义，越南目前在世界上的地位，以及这一重大事件对于越南民族解放斗争的影响。
    苏联、新中国及越南的国旗，将在越南各地飘展，巨幅的旗帜上将写着：越——苏——中友谊万岁！打倒法国帝国主义者！打倒国际反动派！我们为保卫世界和平与民主而斗争！苏联万岁！中华人民共和国万岁！斯大林大元帅万岁！毛泽东主席万岁！胡志明主席万岁！
