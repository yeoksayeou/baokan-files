Title: Central, Beijing People's Broadcasting Station Today's Broadcast Schedule
Author:
Date: 1950-02-03
Page: 4
　　
    [This newspaper reported] The Central People's Broadcasting Station's recorded broadcast today (3rd) at 17:30: "Notice from the National Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Postal Workers' Union regarding the convening of the National Postal Workers' Representative Conference." At 18:30, natural science lecture: "How to view Newton's laws of motion from the perspective of dialectical materialism?" At 18:00, the first literary program: Jingyun Dagu "Ten People Bridge." At 21:00, the second literary program: Songs and instrumental music.
    [Another News] The Beijing People's Broadcasting Station at 12:30, employee program: Broadcasts the recording of Comrade Li Lisan's speech at the first Beijing Municipal Workers' Representative Conference using 670 kilocycles. Reports on the conference situation using 850 kilocycles. At 13:00, youth program: (1) Introduction to the activities of the winter vacation youth work committee, (2) Wang Yaxi, a student from the High School Attached to Beijing Normal University, speaks on "How I got rid of the baggage of individualism." At 16:30, children's program: (1) Current affairs speech, (2) Children from the August 1st Elementary School sing songs. At 17:00, singing program: New songs performed by the singing group of the Department of Pre-Medical Studies of the Faculty of Science of Peking University. At 17:45, Voice of the Municipality: Wang Mingzhi, Director of the Construction Bureau, speaks on "An Overview of the Construction Bureau's Work Over the Past Year." At 18:45, broadcast news and features from the first Beijing Municipal Workers' Representative Conference on the same day.

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-02-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（三）日十七点三十分记录广播：“中国邮政工会全国筹委会关于召开全国邮工代表大会的通知”。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：“怎样用唯物辩证法观点看牛顿运动定律？”十八点第一次文艺节目：京韵大鼓“十人桥”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：歌曲和器乐。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工节目：用六七○千周播送李立三同志在北京市首届工人代表大会上讲话的录音。用八五○千周报导大会情况。十三点青年节目：（一）介绍寒假青年工作委员会的活动，（二）师大附中王亚禧同学讲“我怎样摆脱了个人主义的包袱”。十六点三十分儿童节目：（一）时事讲话，（二）八一小学小朋友们唱歌。十七点歌咏节目：由北京大学理学院医预系歌咏团演唱新歌曲。十七点四十五分市政之声：建设局王明之局长讲“建设局一年来的工作概况”。十八点四十五分播送北京市第一届工人代表大会当天的新闻和花絮。
