Title: Youth and Students in Shanghai, Nanjing, and Other Places Gather to Oppose the Colonial System, Sending Letters to Colonial Youth to Express Condolences and Support
Author:
Date: 1950-02-24
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 23rd:

　　Youth and students in Shanghai, Nanjing, Chongqing and other cities warmly commemorated "Anti-Colonial System Struggle Day." The Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, the Shanghai Municipal Democratic Youth Federation, and the Shanghai Municipal Student Union jointly issued a letter to young friends in colonial and semi-colonial countries, expressing their condolences and support for their heroic struggle. The Shanghai Municipal Democratic Youth Federation also held a forum on February 21st for youth from all walks of life to oppose the colonial system, attended by more than 40 representatives including young soldiers, workers, students, and returned overseas Chinese. Youth representatives who had lived in various Southeast Asian colonies reported on the miserable lives of people and overseas Chinese in Vietnam, Indonesia, Malaya, the Philippines, Siam, and other places under the cruel rule of imperialism, where they were enslaved, exploited, and massacred, as well as the touching deeds of young people in various colonies in the struggle against imperialism for people's liberation. The representatives of all circles attending the meeting unanimously expressed that they would strengthen internationalist propaganda and support the struggles of colonial people. Propaganda activities were carried out in schools throughout the city, posters and commemorative paintings were put up, and commemorative meetings were held in the student community.
　　More than 3,500 college and middle school students in Nanjing held a commemorative meeting on February 21st. Guo Jiaqiang, chairman of the Nanjing Student Union, and Jiang Weiqing, representative of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, spoke at the meeting. Students in Nanjing who participated in winter vacation studies launched a "one person, one letter" campaign, writing letters to the people of various colonial countries to express their support.
　　The Chongqing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League and the preparatory committee of the Municipal Student Union separately convened youth forums on February 21st. Li Zhizhou, deputy secretary of the Chongqing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, called on Chinese youth to strengthen the struggle against imperialism and help the people of the colonies to achieve liberation in all aspects. The preparatory committee of the Municipal Student Union also broadcasted on the Chongqing People's Radio Station, expressing condolences to all those who sacrificed themselves for the cause of world peace and democracy, and extending condolences to the colonial and semi-colonial youth and students who are fighting for national liberation. In addition, youth and students in Zhangjiakou, Baoding, Wuxi, Suzhou, Changzhou and other places also gathered on February 21st to commemorate the occasion.

### 沪宁等地青年学生集会反对殖民制度  致函殖民地青年表示慰问声援

1950-02-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　沪宁等地青年学生集会反对殖民制度
    致函殖民地青年表示慰问声援
    【新华社北京二十三日电】上海、南京、重庆等市各界青年、学生热烈纪念“反对殖民制度斗争日”。青年团上海市工作委员会、上海市民主青年联合会和上海市学生联合会联合发出写给各殖民地、半殖民地国家青年朋友的信，表示慰问和支持他们英勇的斗争。沪市民主青年联合会于二十一日并举行各界青年反对殖民制度的座谈会，参加的有青年战士、工人、学生和归国华侨等代表四十余人。曾侨居东南亚各殖民地的青年代表在会上报告了越南、印尼、马来亚、菲律宾、暹罗等地人民和华侨在帝国主义残酷的统治下，被奴役、压榨、屠杀的悲惨生活，以及各殖民地青年在反对帝国主义争取人民解放斗争中的可歌可泣的事迹。与会各界代表都一致表示，要加强国际主义的宣传，支持殖民地人民的斗争。全市各学校都进行了宣传活动，张贴标语和纪念画，并举行了学生界的纪念大会。
    南京市大、中学生三千五百余人于二十一日举行纪念大会。南京市学生联合会主席郭加强、中共南京市委代表江渭清都曾在会上讲话。南京市参加寒假学习的学生们则展开了“一人一封信”运动，写信给各殖民地国家的人民，表示支援。
    青年团重庆市工作委员会和市学生联合会筹备会，于二十一日分别召开了青年座谈会。市青年团团委副书记李止舟号召中国青年加强反对帝国主义的斗争，从各方面帮助殖民地的人民获得解放。市学联筹备会并假重庆人民广播电台广播，向一切为世界和平民主斗争而牺牲的志士们致哀，向正在为民族解放事业而斗争的殖民地和半殖民地的青年学生们，表示慰问。此外，张家口、保定、无锡、苏州、常州等地的青年和学生也都在二十一日集会纪念。
