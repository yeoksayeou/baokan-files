Title: Demanding Wage Increases: French Auto Workers' Strike Expanding
Author:
Date: 1950-02-26
Page: 4

　　Demanding Wage Increases
    French Auto Workers' Strike Expanding
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 25th: Paris dispatch: The strike movement of French auto workers demanding wage increases is still continuing. Following the nationwide strike of 220,000 workers in all auto factories on the 9th of this month, according to TASS, more than 30,000 workers at the Renault auto factory went on strike again on the 21st. The 6,000 workers at the Ford auto factory in Poissy (near Paris) have also gone on strike.

### 要求增加工资  法汽车工人罢工扩展中

1950-02-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　要求增加工资
    法汽车工人罢工扩展中
    【新华社北京二十五日电】巴黎讯：法国汽车工人要求增加工资的罢工运动，仍在继续中。继本月九日全国所有汽车工厂二十二万工人大罢工之后，据塔斯社称，雷诺汽车厂三万余工人又于二十一日实行罢工。保茜（巴黎附近）的福特汽车厂的六千工人，也已罢工。
