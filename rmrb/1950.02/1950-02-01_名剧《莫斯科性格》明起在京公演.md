Title: Famous Play "Moscow Character" to Premiere in Beijing Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1950-02-01
Page: 4

　　Famous Play "Moscow Character"
　　To Premiere in Beijing Tomorrow
　　[Report from this newspaper] The Beijing People's Art Theater will premiere the famous play "Moscow Character" tomorrow. Shaffronov's famous play "Moscow Character" (a four-act, seven-scene drama), which won the first-class Stalin Prize in 1948, is a "poetic comedy, an optimistic comedy, a comedy full of confidence in the future life"; it is extremely educational. After three months of preparation and rehearsal, the Beijing People's Art Theater decided to premiere the play at the Zhenguang Cinema starting from the 2nd. In addition to the directing team being composed of Liu Yumin, Lu Su, Han Bing, Yin Zhenjia, etc., with Liu Yumin as the executive director, Ouyang Yuqian, Hong Shen, Tian Han, Jiao Juyin and others were also invited to provide detailed guidance in order to achieve perfection. The aim is to allow the people of the capital to see this play and absorb valuable Soviet experience.

### 名剧《莫斯科性格》明起在京公演

1950-02-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　名剧《莫斯科性格》
    明起在京公演
    【本报讯】北京人民艺术剧院明起公演名剧《莫斯科性格》。荣膺一九四八年度头等斯大林奖金之沙佛诺夫名剧《莫斯科性格》（四幕七场话剧），是一部“诗意的喜剧，乐观主义的喜剧，对于未来生活充满信心的喜剧”；极富教育意义。北京人民艺术剧院经过三个月的筹备、排演决于二日起，在真光影院开始公演此剧。除导演团由刘郁民、芦肃、韩冰、殷振家等担任，并以刘郁民为执行导演外，并聘请欧阳予倩、洪深、田汉、焦菊隐等加详指导，以期尽美尽善。使此剧能与首都人士见面以汲取苏联宝贵经验。
