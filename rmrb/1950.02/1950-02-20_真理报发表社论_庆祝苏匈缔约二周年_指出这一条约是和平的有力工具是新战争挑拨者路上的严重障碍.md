Title: People's Daily Publishes Editorial Celebrating the Second Anniversary of the Sino-Hungarian Treaty, Pointing Out that This Treaty Is a Powerful Tool for Peace and a Serious Obstacle in the Path of New War Provocateurs

Author:

Date: 1950-02-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 19th: *Pravda* published an editorial on the 18th, commenting on the second anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Hungarian Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance. *Pravda* said: The Sino-Hungarian Treaty fully reflects the unwavering peace-loving policy pursued by the great socialist country—a policy based on equality between countries and mutual respect for national independence and state sovereignty. This treaty is a powerful tool for peace and a serious obstacle in the path of new war provocateurs. The editorial continued: The friendship and cooperation of the Soviet Union is a strong support for the Hungarian people's efforts to further consolidate and develop an independent and democratic country and build socialism. The conspiracy of British and American imperialists to restore the old capitalist order in Hungary with the help of the Tito, Rajk, and other traitorous spy-terrorist gangs has now been completely defeated. Relying on the friendship and help of the great Soviet country, the Hungarian people have achieved considerable success in all fields of political, economic, and cultural development. The new Hungary is confidently marching along the road of economic prosperity toward socialist construction. *Pravda* concluded: The Sino-Hungarian Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance will remain a reliable weapon in the future struggle for peace and the security of all countries.

### 真理报发表社论  庆祝苏匈缔约二周年  指出这一条约是和平的有力工具是新战争挑拨者路上的严重障碍

1950-02-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　真理报发表社论  庆祝苏匈缔约二周年
    指出这一条约是和平的有力工具是新战争挑拨者路上的严重障碍
    【新华社北京十九日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：真理报于十八日发表社论，评论苏匈签订友好、合作与互助条约的第二周年。真理报说：苏匈条约充分地反映了伟大社会主义国家所奉行的坚持不渝的爱好和平政策——以国与国间的平等和互相尊重民族独立与国家主权为基础的政策。这一条约，是和平的有力工具，是新战争挑拨者道路上的严重障碍。社论继称：苏联的友谊与合作，对于匈牙利人民进一步巩固与发展独立民主国家及建设社会主义的努力，是一个有力的支持。英美帝国主义者在铁托、拉伊克与其它叛徒的间谍恐怖分子匪帮的帮助之下，梦想在匈牙利恢复旧资本主义秩序的阴谋，现在是完全惨败了。依靠着伟大苏维埃国家的友谊与帮助，匈牙利人民已在政治、经济与文化发展的一切领域中获得了相当大的成功。新的匈牙利正满怀信心地沿着经济繁荣的道路，向社会主义的建设迈进。真理报最后说：苏匈友好合作与互助条约，在今后争取和平与各国安全的斗争中，将仍然是一个可靠的武器。
