Title: Soviet Armed Forces Ministry Holds Grand Meeting to Celebrate Army Day; Congress Sends Telegram to Marshal Stalin to Extend Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1950-02-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 23rd: TASS, Moscow - The Soviet Armed Forces Ministry held a celebration meeting on February 22nd to celebrate the 32nd anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Armed Forces. Attendees included generals and officers of the Soviet Army and Navy, representatives of the working people of the capital, and representatives of Moscow's Party and public organizations.

　　The stage of the Central Red Army Theater was decorated for the festival. Portraits of Lenin and Stalin were hung on the stage, surrounded by flowers. Below the portraits were the battle flags of various famous Soviet military units.

　　The presidium included: Marshal Vasilevsky, Minister of the Soviet Armed Forces; Marshal Budyonny; Marshal Sokolovsky; Marshal Govorov; General of the Army Shtemenko; Lieutenant General Kuznetsov; and Furtseva, Secretary of the Moscow City Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

　　Marshal Vasilevsky, Minister of the Soviet Armed Forces, presided over the meeting. Amidst stormy applause, the members of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, headed by the leader of the Soviet people, Stalin, were elected as honorary presidium members.

　　Lieutenant General Fedor Kuznetsov, Director of the Political Department of the Soviet Armed Forces, delivered a report on the 32nd anniversary of the establishment of the Soviet Army. He described the combat path that the Soviet Army has taken since its establishment, and pointed out that the Soviet Armed Forces, together with all the Soviet people, are celebrating the 32nd anniversary of the establishment of the Armed Forces in an atmosphere of continuous political and economic upsurge and further increase in the Soviet Union's international prestige and influence. General Kuznetsov declared: Soviet soldiers feel every day the love of all nationalities for the Soviet Armed Forces and the unwavering care of the Bolshevik Party and Stalin himself for it.

　　The participants of the celebration meeting passed a telegram congratulating Marshal Stalin with great enthusiasm.

### 苏联武装部队部盛会庆祝建军节  大会通电向斯大林大元帅致贺

1950-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联武装部队部盛会庆祝建军节
    大会通电向斯大林大元帅致贺
    【新华社北京二十三日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联武装部队部在二月二十二日举行庆祝大会，庆祝苏联武装部队成立三十二周年。赴会者有苏联陆海军将军与校尉、首都劳动人民的代表以及莫斯科党与公共组织的代表。
    中央红军剧院的舞台作节日装饰。台上悬着列宁和斯大林的画像，画像之旁环绕着花朵。画像之下是苏军各有名部队的战旗。
    主席团人员如下：苏联武装部队部部长华西列夫斯基元帅、布琼尼元帅、索科洛夫斯基元帅、戈沃罗夫元帅、施蒂门柯陆军上将、库兹涅佐夫准上将、苏联共产党莫斯科市委会书记福尔切娃等。
    苏联武装部队部部长、苏联元帅华西列夫斯基主持开会。在暴风雨般的掌声中，以苏联人民领袖斯大林为首的联共中央委员会政治局各委员被选为名誉主席团。
    苏联武装部队政治部主任费多尔·库兹涅佐夫准上将就苏军建立三十二周年纪念作报告。他描述苏军自从建军以来所走过的战斗的道路，并指出，苏联武装部队和全体苏联人民一道，正在政治与经济上的不断高涨和苏联国际声誉与影响之进一步增长的气氛中，庆祝武装部队建立的三十二周年纪念。库兹涅佐夫将军声称：苏联的战士每天都感到各族人民对苏联武装部队的热爱和布尔什维克党及斯大林本人对它的始终不变的关怀。
    庆祝大会的与会者在巨大的热情中通过了向斯大林大元帅祝贺的电文。
