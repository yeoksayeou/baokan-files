Title: The Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress Suggests to National Parliaments: Abolish the Arms Race and Ban Atomic Bombs; Delegation to Visit Countries such as Belgium, Italy and the United States

Author:

Date: 1950-02-26
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 25th: The Polish News Agency quoted a Paris dispatch: The Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress has issued a communique to the press stating that the Secretariat of the Standing Committee held a meeting in Paris on the 22nd. At the request of several national peace organizations, and in order to immediately ease the tension in international relations, the Secretariat has decided to submit two basic issues to the parliaments of all countries around the world for consideration: 1. Abolish the arms race through measures such as immediately reducing military budgets and cutting armed forces. 2. Ban atomic bombs.

　　A delegation from the World Peace Congress will visit the parliaments of Brussels, Rome, Amsterdam, Paris, the Soviet Union, the United States, the United Kingdom, and Mexico. The Secretariat of the Standing Committee has contacted the diplomatic representatives of various countries in order to obtain entry visas for the delegation.

### 和大常设委会向各国议会建议  废除军备竞赛禁止原子弹  代表团将赴比意美等国访问

1950-02-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　和大常设委会向各国议会建议
    废除军备竞赛禁止原子弹
    代表团将赴比意美等国访问
    【新华社北京二十五日电】波兰通讯社引巴黎消息：世界拥护和平大会常设委员会顷向报界发表公报称：常设委员会秘书处于二十二日在巴黎举行会议。秘书处根据若干全国性的拥护和平组织的请求，为着立即缓和国际关系上的紧张局面，已经决定向全世界各国议会提出两个基本问题，请求予以考虑：一、经由立即减缩军事预算及裁减武装部队的措施，废除军备竞赛。二、禁止原子弹。
    和大代表团将赴布鲁塞尔、罗马、阿姆斯特丹、巴黎、苏联、美国、英国、墨西哥访问各该国议会。常设委员会秘书处已与各国外交代表取得连系，以便为代表团取得入境签证。
