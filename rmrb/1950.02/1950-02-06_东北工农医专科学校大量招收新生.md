Title: Northeast Institute of Technology, Agriculture, and Medicine Enrolls a Large Number of New Students
Author:
Date: 1950-02-06
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, February 5th: The sixteen specialized schools under the industrial, agricultural, forestry, and health departments of the Northeast People's Government will enroll over 6,800 new students this year to cultivate various talents for the construction of New China. The application period for each school began in January; since the beginning, registration has been quite enthusiastic. The three industrial specialized schools affiliated with the Ministry of Industry in Anshan, Fushun, and other locations, as well as the seven advanced industrial vocational schools in Shenyang, Benxi, Fuxin, and other places, closed their registration on January 29th. The examinations concluded on February 1st and 2nd, and a total of 4,800 students were admitted. The application deadline for the Shenyang Agricultural College under the Ministry of Agriculture and Forestry was February 4th, and the entrance examination will be held on the 7th and 8th of this month. The school will enroll 100 new students. The application deadline for the China Medical University, Harbin Medical University, Changchun Military Medical University, and Northeast Veterinary School, all under the Ministry of Health, is February 8th. The examination will be held for four days from the 10th to the 13th, and a total of 1,940 new students will be recruited. The number of students to be enrolled this time is the largest for departments related to heavy industry, such as machinery, electrical engineering, metallurgy, mining, and iron and steel metallurgy, with a planned enrollment of 2,800 new students.

### 东北工农医专科学校大量招收新生

1950-02-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　东北工农医专科学校大量招收新生
    【新华社沈阳五日电】东北人民政府工业、农林、卫生等三个部门所属十六个专门学校，本年度招收新生六千八百余人，培育新中国建设的各种人材。各校招生的报名日期自一月间开始；开始以来，报名者相当踊跃。工业部所属鞍山、抚顺等三个工业专门学校和沈阳、本溪、阜新等七个工科高级职业学校已于一月二十九日截止报名，本月一、二两日考试结束，共录取四千八百人。农林部所属沈阳农学院报名期限于本月四日截止，七、八两日举行考试，该校将录取新生一百名。卫生部所属中国医科大学、哈尔滨医科大学、长春军医大学和东北兽医学校报名期限截至二月八日止，十日到十三日四天举行考试，共将招收新生一千九百四十人。这次招生人数，以与重工业有关的科系为最多；如机械、电机、金属、采矿、钢铁冶金等九科即拟招收新生二千八百人。
