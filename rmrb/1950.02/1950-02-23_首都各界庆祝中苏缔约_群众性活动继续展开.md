Title: All Circles in the Capital Celebrate the Sino-Soviet Treaty; Mass Activities Continue to Unfold
Author:
Date: 1950-02-23
Page: 4

　　All Circles in the Capital Celebrate the Sino-Soviet Treaty
    Mass Activities Continue to Unfold

    [This newspaper reports] The mass activities celebrating the signing of the new Sino-Soviet treaty and agreements continue to unfold in the capital. Yesterday (the 22nd) at 2 PM, various women's organizations held a grand meeting at the auditorium of the New China Women's Vocational School to celebrate. More than 500 representatives from over a dozen units, including the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, the Chinese Women's Friendship Association Headquarters and its Beijing branch, the Chinese Women's Temperance Union, the Beijing YWCA, the Women Workers' Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Female Students' Department of the All-China Students' Federation, the Women Workers' Department of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, the Female Students' Department of the Beijing Students' Federation, and the New China Women's Vocational School, attended. The venue was filled with a warm and excited atmosphere. The meeting was presided over by Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation. In her speech, she pointed out the great significance and role of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance, calling on sisters to widely publicize it and celebrate it with practical actions. Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, was then invited to give a report on the matter. Xu Guangping, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, Liu Qingyang, representative of the Chinese Women's Friendship Association and its Beijing branch, Yang Yunyu, representative of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, Zhang Qin, representative of the YWCA, female worker Zhou Zhongling, female student Hu Yunru, and others spoke successively, unanimously supporting the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance, expressing their determination to celebrate with practical actions of actively engaging in production, intensifying work, and studying diligently. The National Association of Fine Arts Workers, in addition to warmly celebrating, also issued a declaration, expressing heartfelt gratitude to the Soviet Union for this great and profound friendship towards us, and paying the highest respect to Marshal Stalin and Chairman Mao. It also called on fine arts workers across the country to widely and deeply publicize the significance of Sino-Soviet friendship, as well as the spirit and specific content of this treaty and the two agreements. All employees of the Beijing Electric Power Bureau warmly supported the signing of the new Sino-Soviet treaty and specifically sent a letter of respect to Chairman Mao on the 21st, expressing their determination to guarantee the completion of production tasks with all their strength in the future. The Yenching Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, in conjunction with the Yenching University Trade Union, Women's Federation, and Student Union, held a grand celebration evening on the 20th. More than 500 people attended, including faculty, staff, workers, and students of the school, as well as nearby villagers. The evening began at 7:30 PM, starting with a speech by Lin Handa, Secretary-General of the Yenching Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. He said: The signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance is a slap in the face to all those who maliciously provoke and slander Sino-Soviet friendship! Now 700 million people around the world are united as one, and this power is unparalleled. Let the imperialists and warmongers weep and tremble! Yang Fu of the Marxist-Leninist Academy then spoke, pointing out that the signing of this treaty is not only a victory for the people of China and the Soviet Union, but also a victory for the peace-loving people of the world. The Youth League branch of Yuying Middle School is making the in-depth study of the new Sino-Soviet treaty and related documents its main task at present. In recent days, various Youth League groups and students have conducted preliminary discussions or talks together. The Beijing branch of the Democratic National Construction Association, in celebration of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance, has specifically scheduled its 58th New Knowledge Forum to be held at 6:30 PM on February 24th at the Xiehe Auditorium on East Third Street. Sun Xiaocun, Deputy Director of the Central Financial and Economic Planning Bureau, and Shi Fuliang, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Labor, have been invited to speak on "Supporting the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance." A Soviet film will also be screened after the speeches. All members and friends are welcome to attend with their notices.

### 首都各界庆祝中苏缔约  群众性活动继续展开

1950-02-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　首都各界庆祝中苏缔约
    群众性活动继续展开
    【本报讯】庆祝中苏缔结新条约新协定的群众性活动，在首都继续展开。各妇女团体昨（廿二）日下午二时假新中国妇女职业学校大礼堂举行大会庆祝。到中华全国民主妇女联合会、北京市民主妇女联合会、中国妇女联谊会总会及北京分会、中华妇女节制会、北京市女青年会、全国总工会女工部、中华全国学生联合会女学生部、北京市总工会女工部、北京市学生联合会女学生部、新中国妇女职业学校等十几个单位的代表共五百余人。会场充满了热烈兴奋的气氛。大会由全国妇联副主席李德全主持。她在致词中指出了中苏友好同盟互助条约的伟大的意义及作用，号召姊妹们广泛宣传，并以实际的行动来庆祝她。继请中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞作有关报告。全国妇联副主席许广平、中国妇女联谊会及北京分会的代表刘清扬、北京市民主妇女联合会代表杨蕴玉、女青年会代表章琴、女工周仲玲、女学生胡筠若等相继讲话，一致拥护中苏友好同盟互助条约的签订，表示决以积极生产、加紧工作、学习的实际行动来庆祝努力。全国美术工作者协会除热烈庆祝外，并发出宣言，表示衷心感谢苏联对我们的这种伟大而深厚的友谊，谨向斯大林大元帅和毛主席致最高的敬意。并号召全国美术工作者，一定要广泛的深入的宣传中苏友好的意义，以及这一条约及两个协定的精神和具体内容。北京电业局全体职工热烈拥护中苏缔结新条约，于二十一日特上书毛主席致敬，并表示今后决以一切力量，保证完成生产任务。中苏友协燕京支会联合燕大职工会、妇女会及学生会于二十日举行盛大庆祝晚会。到会五百余人，除留校师生工友外，并有附近乡民参加。晚会于七时半开始，首由中苏友协燕京支会总干事林汉达致辞，他说：中苏友好同盟互助条约的签订是给了一切恶意挑拨毁谤中苏友谊的人一个嘴巴！现在全世界有七万万人结成一条心，这力量是无比的。让帝国主义与战争贩子哭泣颤抖吧！次由马列学院杨甫讲话，他指出这条约的签订不仅是中苏两国人民的胜利，而且是全世界爱好和平人民的胜利。育英中学青年团支部把组织深入学习中苏新条约及有关文件作为目前的主要任务。连日来青年团各小组和同学一道已作了初步的漫谈或讨论。民主建国会北京市分会，为庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约的缔结，特定于二月二十四日下午六时三十分，假东单三条协和礼堂举行第五十八次新知识座谈会，敦请中央财经计划局孙晓村副局长和劳动部施复亮副部长主讲“拥护中苏友好同盟互助条约”。讲演后并放映苏联电影。凡属会员会友，均可凭通知入场。
