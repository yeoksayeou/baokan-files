Title: US Joint Chiefs of Staff "Inspect" the Far East, Conspiring to Obstruct Our Liberation of Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1950-02-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 4th: United Press and the Kuomintang Central News Agency's January 31st dispatches exposed that one of the important purposes of the US Joint Chiefs of Staff's current "inspection" of the Far East is to conspire to obstruct the Chinese People's Liberation Army from liberating Taiwan. Before the US Joint Chiefs of Staff arrived in Tokyo, MacArthur sent US Far East Air Force intelligence officer Major Van Durnpool from Tokyo to Taipei to collect first-hand intelligence to be used by the four chiefs of staff. Van Durnpool returned to Tokyo on the 31st to report to the four chiefs of staff. While in Taiwan, Van Durnpool emphasized Taiwan's strategic importance and held meetings with Kuomintang bandit chiefs Yan Xishan and Wu Guozhen to discuss the "defense details" when the Kuomintang remnants resist the Chinese People's Liberation Army's liberation of Taiwan. Van Durnpool obtained a large amount of intelligence from the US Counselor in Taiwan.

### 美联合参谋长“巡视”远东  阴谋阻扰我解放台湾

1950-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　美联合参谋长“巡视”远东  阴谋阻扰我解放台湾
    【新华社北京四日电】合众社与国民党中央社一月三十一日消息，暴露了美国联合参谋长此次“巡视”远东的重要目的之一，是阴谋阻扰中国人民解放军解放台湾。在美国联合参谋长抵达东京之前，麦克阿瑟曾派出美国远东空军情报官员范敦浦尔少校由东京飞往台北，搜集第一手的一切可能的情报，以供四参谋长使用。范敦浦尔已于三十一日返回东京，以便向四参谋长提出报告。范敦浦尔在台湾时曾强调台湾的战略重要性；并与国民党匪首阎锡山及吴国桢等举行会议，讨论对国民党残匪抵抗中国人民解放军解放台湾时的“防务细节”。范敦浦尔曾从美国驻台湾的参赞那里取得了大量情报。
