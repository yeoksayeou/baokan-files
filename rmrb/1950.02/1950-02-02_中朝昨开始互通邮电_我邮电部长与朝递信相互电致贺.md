Title: China and North Korea Begin Reciprocal Postal and Telecommunications Services Yesterday; Our Minister of Posts and Telecommunications and the DPRK Minister of Communications Exchange Congratulatory Telegrams
Author:
Date: 1950-02-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 1st: The postal and telecommunications agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, signed in Beijing last December, came into effect today. Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government of China, and Kim Jung-joo, Minister of Communications of North Korea, exchanged congratulatory telegrams as follows:

(I) Congratulatory Telegram from Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of China, to Kim Jung-joo, Minister of Communications of North Korea

To His Excellency, the Minister of Communications of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Pyongyang:

	On the occasion of the commencement of postal and telecommunications services between our two countries, I, on behalf of all the staff of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of China, extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the comrades working under your leadership. The fact that China and North Korea have reciprocal postal and telecommunications services will bring our two countries into closer contact in terms of cultural exchange and economic development, and will further consolidate the friendly and united relations between the two peoples. I would like to assure you that the Chinese postal and telecommunications staff will make their best efforts to accomplish this great task, and I wish Your Excellency and the comrades working under your leadership smooth work and good health.
	
		Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of China

(II) Congratulatory Telegram from Kim Jung-joo, Minister of Communications of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, to Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of China:

To the Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of China:

	With the start of postal and telecommunications services between China and North Korea, I believe that we will have great achievements and development in the field of communications, and that it will rapidly facilitate cultural exchange between the two countries and strengthen the friendship between the peoples of the two countries, as well as between the postal and telecommunications workers of our two countries. I wish your country's communications undertakings a bright future and extend my respects.

		Kim Jung-joo, Minister of Communications of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea

### 中朝昨开始互通邮电  我邮电部长与朝递信相互电致贺
　
1950-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　中朝昨开始互通邮电
    我邮电部长与朝递信相互电致贺
    【新华社北京一日电】中华人民共和国与朝鲜民主主义人民共和国于去年十二月在北京签订的互通邮电协定，本日起开始生效。我中央人民政府邮电部长朱学范与朝鲜递信相金廷柱，特互致贺电如下：
    （一）中华人民共和国邮电部长朱学范致朝鲜递信相金廷柱的贺电平壤朝鲜民主主义人民共和国递信相阁下：
    当兹贵我两国开始互通邮电之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国邮电部全体工作人员，向阁下及阁下领导的工作同志们，表示热烈的贺意。由于中朝互通邮电这一事实，将使贵我两国在文化交流和经济发展上愈加紧密联系起来，并使两国人民的友好和团结关系，更加巩固。我愿保证中国邮电工作人员，必以最大的努力。完成这一伟大任务，并祝阁下及阁下领导的工作同志们工作顺利和健康。
      中华人民共和国邮电部长朱学范
    （二）朝鲜民主主义人民共和国递信相金廷柱致我国邮电部长朱学范的贺电：中华人民共和国邮电部部长：
    由于中朝两国间开始通邮通电，相信能使我们在通信事业上有很大的成果与发展，并能迅速的沟通两国的文化，加强两国人民以及贵我两国邮电工作同志间的亲善。谨祝贵国通信事业前途发展，并致敬礼。
            朝鲜民主主义人民共和国递信相金廷柱
