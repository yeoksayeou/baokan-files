Title: Public Communique of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic: Remarkable Achievements in the Second Year of the Five-Year Plan; Livestock Numbers on Farms Increase by 6.5% Over the Previous Year; Grain Harvest Doubles, Progress in Industry and Transportation Achieved
Author:
Date: 1950-02-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 15th: TASS Ulaanbaatar dispatch: The State Planning Commission of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic released the following public communique on the development of the national economy and culture of the Republic in 1949 on the 14th:

　　As the main sector of the Mongolian national economy, animal husbandry has made significant progress in the second year of the Five-Year Plan. The number of livestock on various farms increased by 6.5% compared to 1948. The number of livestock in nomadic cooperatives increased by 40.8%. The livestock of state-owned farms increased by 18.8%. The number of state-owned horse and hay stations increased by 75%. The veterinary station network has also been expanded.

　　Along with the increase in grain sowing areas, the grain harvest of state-owned farms also increased by 48.3% compared to 1948.

　　The number of tractors and combine harvesters in agriculture increased by approximately 43% compared to 1948.

　　In 1949, the increase in industrial output was equal to the increase in motor transport and freight turnover rate. The most important event in the Republic in 1949 was the opening of the Naushki-Ulaanbaatar railway, built with the fraternal assistance of the Soviet Union.

　　The public communique points out the significant increase in the number of schools and students, as well as new achievements in the literacy campaign. In 1949, the National University trained another large group of experts, including doctors, veterinarians, and animal breeders. Various technical schools also trained hundreds of experts to participate in the work of various sectors of the national economy.

　　In 1949, many new and well-equipped apartment buildings, schools, hospitals, and clubs were built.

### 蒙古人民共和国部长会议公报  五年计划第二年成就显著  农场牲畜头数较上年增百分之六·五  谷物收获增半倍、工业运输均获进展

1950-02-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　蒙古人民共和国部长会议公报
    五年计划第二年成就显著
    农场牲畜头数较上年增百分之六·五  谷物收获增半倍、工业运输均获进展
    【新华社北京十五日电】塔斯社乌兰巴托尔讯：蒙古人民共和国部长会议国家计划委员会，于十四日发布关于共和国一九四九年国民经济与文化发展的公报如下：
    作为蒙古国民经济主要部门的牲畜饲养业，在五年计划的第二年中，已有显著的进展。各种农场的牲畜头数，比一九四八年增加了百分之六点五。游牧合作社的牲畜头数，增加了百分之四十点八。国营农场的牲畜，增加了百分之十八点八。国有的马匹和干草站的数目，增加了百分之七十五。兽医站网也已经扩充。
    与谷物播种地区增加的同时，国营农场谷物的收获，也比一九四八年增加了百分之四十八点三。
    农业中的拖拉机和联合机的数目，比一九四八年增加了约达百分之四十三。
    一九四九年，工业产量的增加与汽车运输、货运运转率的增加相等。在一九四九年，共和国最重要的事件，是在苏联的兄弟般的援助下建筑的纳乌施基——乌兰巴托尔铁路的通车。
    公报指出学校和学生数目的显著增加以及在识字运动上的新成就。在一九四九年，国立大学又培养了一大批专家，其中有医生、兽医和动物饲养学家。各种技术学校也训练了数以百计的专家，参加了国民经济各部门的工作。
    在一九四九年，建造了许多新的设备齐全的公寓房屋、学校、医院和俱乐部。
