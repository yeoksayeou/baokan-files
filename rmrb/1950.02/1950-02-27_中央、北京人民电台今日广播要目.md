Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Station Today's Broadcast Schedule
Author:
Date: 1950-02-27
Page: 4

　　Central, Beijing People's Radio Station Today's Broadcast Schedule
    [This newspaper reported] The Central People's Broadcasting Station's joint broadcast time today (27th) at 20:00: (1) Third News, (2) International Affairs: How the Soviet Union helps people's democratic countries restore and develop their economies, (3) Comprehensive Report: How Soviet experts help the Northeast carry out production and construction, (4) Talking about how Soviet friends help us build railways? 17:30 Women's Time: "Introducing a speech by Mishina, a Soviet Supreme Soviet representative, Stakhanov worker, and outstanding female tram driver, 'The Most Important Thing'." 18:30 Natural Science Lecture: Basic Knowledge of Electricity, Lecture 17, "Electric Motor". 18:00 First Literary Program: "Crossing the River Hero Sun Naiying" performed in the Plum Blossom Drum style. 21:00 Second Literary Program: Soviet Songs and Music. 18:45 New Songs: "Joy of Liberation", "Production Supports the Front", etc.
    [Also reported] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station's 12:30 Workers' Program: 1. Trade Union Work: Song Qi of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions' Department of Culture and Education speaks on "Introducing the Soviet Collective Contract," 2. Communication: Chen Dejun of the Water Company speaks on "We Wrote a Letter to Chairman Mao Guaranteeing to Complete and Exceed Production Tasks to Celebrate the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance." 13:00 Youth Program: Speech by Situ Meifang, student executive member of the All-China Students' Federation in Guangzhou. 16:30 Children's Program: (1) The Story of Chinese Michurin, (2) Singing. 17:00 Singing Program: Huiwen and Muzhen Middle School harmonica teams perform new songs. 17:45 Municipal Voice: The Social Services Section of the Beijing Postal Administration broadcasts "How to Remit and Cash Money Orders?" 18:45 Listener Service: (1) Answering questions such as "Where is the main receiving part of a crystal radio," (2) Announcement from the Shenyang Municipal People's Government Construction Bureau soliciting designs for the Founding Memorial Tower, (3) Announcement from the Northeast People's Government Commerce Department postponing the recruitment of professors and technical personnel and the enrollment of students.

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（二十七日）二十点联播时间：（一）第三次新闻，（二）国际时事：苏联怎样帮助人民民主国家恢复和发展经济，（三）综合报导：苏联专家怎样帮助东北进行生产建设，（四）谈谈苏联朋友是怎样帮助我们建设铁路的？十七点三十分妇女时间：“介绍苏联苏维埃最高代表、史塔哈诺夫工作者、优秀的女电车司机米申娜的一段谈话“最主要的东西”。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：电学常识第十七讲“电动机”。十八点第一次文艺节目：梅花大鼓《渡江英雄孙乃英》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：苏联歌曲和音乐。十八点四十五分新歌曲：《翻身乐》、《生产支援前线》等。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台十二点三十分职工节目：一、工会工作：市总工会文教部宋其讲“介绍苏联集体合同”，二、通讯：自来水公司陈德筠讲“我们给毛主席写信保证用完成和超过生产任务庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约”。十三点青年节目：全国学联广州市执委司徒梅芳同学讲话。十六点三十分儿童节目：（一）中国米邱林的故事，（二）歌咏。十七点歌咏节目：汇文、慕贞中学口琴队演奏新歌曲。十七点四十五分市政之声：北京邮管局社会服务股播讲“怎样汇款和兑取汇票”？十八点四十五分听众服务：（１）回答“矿石收音机主要收音的部分在那里”等问题，（２）沈阳市人民政府建设局征求开国纪念塔图样的启事，（３）东北人民政府商业部延期招聘教授、技术人员和招考学员的启事。
