Title: Representatives of Party and Government Organs in the Capital Remotely Commemorate General Yang Hucheng; Vice Chairman Zhu and Others Attend and Offer Tributes

Author: 
Date: 1950-02-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th—Representatives of Party and government organs in the capital held a remote memorial service this afternoon for General Yang Hucheng, whose remains are scheduled to be buried in Xi'an today, to comfort his loyal spirit. The ceremony was held in the auditorium of the Beijing Military Control Commission. A portrait of General Yang was hung high in the center of the auditorium. In front of the spirit seat were wreaths presented by Chairman Mao, the leader of the Chinese people. On both sides were elegiac couplets and wreaths presented from the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Central Committee of Communist Party of China, the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the China Association for Promoting Democracy and other democratic parties, as well as Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the Central People's Government, Secretary-General Lin Boqu, Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council, various organs, organizations, and individuals. From 2:00 PM, those who came to pay their respects included Vice Chairmen Zhu De and Li Jishen of the Central People's Government, Secretary-General Lin Boqu of the Central People's Government, Vice Chairmen Shen Junru and Guo Moruo of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Vice Premiers Dong Biwu and Huang Yanpei of the Government Administration Council, Director of the Beijing Military Control Commission and Mayor of Beijing Nie Rongzhen, members of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Cai Tingkai and Long Yun, Deputy Director of the Political and Legal Committee Zhang Xiruo, and more than two hundred others. In addition, several dozen of General Yang's former friends and subordinates, including Sun Weiru, Li Xingzhong, Wang Bingnan, Guo Zechen, and Shen Bochun, also came to pay their respects. Yang's second daughter, Zhengmei, stood beside the spirit seat to express gratitude. The memorial ceremony concluded at 5:00 PM.

### 首都党政机关代表  遥祭杨虎城将军  朱副主席等亲临致祭

1950-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　首都党政机关代表
    遥祭杨虎城将军
    朱副主席等亲临致祭
    【新华社北京七日电】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会以杨虎城将军遗榇定于今日在西安下葬，特于下午在首都举行遥祭，以慰忠灵。礼堂设于北京市军管会大礼堂。礼堂正中高悬杨将军遗像，灵座前，陈列中国人民领袖毛主席赠送之花圈，两旁则为中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会、中国共产党中央委员会、中国国民党革命委员会、中国民主促进会等各民主党派，以及中央人民政府刘少奇副主席、林伯渠秘书长，政务院周恩来总理等及各机关团体和个人赠送之挽联花圈。自下午二时起，陆续前往致祭者，有中央人民政府副主席朱德、李济深，中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席沈钧儒、郭沫若，政务院副总理董必武、黄炎培，北京市军管会主任兼市长聂荣臻，人民革命军事委员会委员蔡廷锴、龙云，政治法律委员会副主任张奚若等共二百余人。此外尚有杨将军生前友好、部属孙蔚如、李兴中、王炳南、郭则沉、申伯纯等数十人亦前往致奠。灵旁杨将军第二女公子拯美恭立致谢。祭奠直至下午五时奠毕。
