Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Soviet Protection Association Exchange Friendly Greetings and Encourage Each Other to Defend Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-02-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 8th:

　　Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Soviet Protection Association
    Exchange Friendly Greetings and Encourage Each Other to Defend Peace
    The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters received a New Year's letter from the Soviet Protection Association on February 4th and sent a reply on February 6th. The letters exchanged between the two parties are as follows:

To our dear friends of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association:
    The Council of the Soviet Protection Association Headquarters extends to you its New Year greetings.
    We wish you brilliant victories in the struggle for the new People's Democratic China and the great friendship between China and the Soviet Union.
    Long live the friendship of all nations in the world, headed by the heroic Soviet people!
    Long live Comrade Mao Zedong, leader of the People's Republic of China!
    Long live Marshal Stalin, the standard-bearer of world peace, democracy, and socialism!
    Council of the Soviet Protection Association Headquarters
    Chairman M. Izhakov
    Secretary-General M. Manev
    Sofia

To our dear friends of the Soviet Protection Association Headquarters:
    The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association sincerely thanks you for your New Year greetings.
    We hope you will continue to consolidate your friendship with the Soviet Union and achieve further success!
    We are willing to unite with you, under the leadership of the great Soviet Union, and work together to defend world peace and promote the friendship of all nations!
    Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters
    Chairman Liu Shaoqi
    Secretary-General Qian Junrui
    February 6th

### 中苏友协与保苏协会  互致友好祝贺  共勉保卫和平

1950-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏友协与保苏协会
    互致友好祝贺  共勉保卫和平
    【新华社北京八日电】中苏友协总会于二月四日接得保苏协会贺年信一件，并已于二月六日发出复信。双方来往信件如下：北京中苏友好协会亲爱的朋友们：
    保苏协会总会理事会向您们祝贺新年。
    我们愿望您们在为新的人民民主中国和中苏两国间的伟大友谊的斗争中，获得辉煌的胜利。
    以英勇的苏联人民为首的全世界各民族的友好万岁！
    中华人民共和国领袖毛泽东同志万岁！
    全世界和平、民主和社会主义的旗手——斯大林大元帅万岁！
    保苏协会总会理事会
  主席Ｍ·依柴夫  秘书长Ｍ·马涅夫索菲亚保苏协会总会亲爱的朋友们：
    中苏友好协会衷心感谢您们新年的祝贺。
    希望您们继续巩固与苏联的友好，并获得更进一步的成功！
    我们愿与您们团结一致，在伟大的苏联领导之下，共同为保卫世界和平与增进各民族的友好而努力！
    中苏友好协会总会
    会长刘少奇  总干事钱俊瑞  二月六日
