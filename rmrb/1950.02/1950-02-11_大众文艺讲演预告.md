Author:
Date: 1950-02-11
Page: 3

　　Mass Literature and Art Lecture Announcement
    [Beijing News] The Mass Literature and Art Creation Research Association and the Youth Service Department are jointly hosting a weekly literature and art lecture. This week, they have invited Wang Chun, the director of the Workers' Daily, to give a lecture on "Research on Water Margin." After the lecture, Cai Jinbo and Cai Liangui will perform Xihé Dagu. The lecture address is the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association on Nanheyan Street in the Eastern District. The time is 9:30 am on Sunday. Admission is by long-term or temporary ticket. Temporary tickets can be collected from the Youth Service Department on Saturday.

### 大众文艺讲演预告

1950-02-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　大众文艺讲演预告
    【北京讯】大众文艺创作研究会与青年服务部联合主办的星期文艺讲演会，本周聘请工人日报社长王春主讲，讲题是“水浒传研究”。会后由蔡金波、蔡连贵演唱西河大鼓。讲演地址在东城南河沿苏联对外文化协会，时间是星期日上午九时半。凭长期券或临时券入场，临时券可于星期六到青年服务部领取。
