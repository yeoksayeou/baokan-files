Title: Guangzhou Adorned with National Flags to Celebrate; Citizens Eagerly Buy Special Editions, Joyfully Delighted; CCP Central Committee South China Sub-bureau Sends Telegram of Congratulations

Author:
Date: 1950-02-17
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 16th. After the news of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance and the two agreements reached here, all agencies, organizations, schools, and the broad masses of people were jubilant and encouraged. The news was first broadcast by Guangzhou People's Broadcasting Station at 8:30 a.m. on the 15th, and the whole city was immediately adorned with national flags to celebrate. The Southern Daily published 100,000 copies of a special edition, which were sold around the city in two trucks, along with oral promotion to the citizens. People shopping for New Year goods on the bustling streets eagerly bought the special editions, all with joyful delight. Congratulatory telegrams forwarded by the Guangzhou branch of the New China News Agency poured in. After receiving the news, the provincial and municipal authorities immediately convened emergency cadre meetings to announce the good news of the conclusion of the Sino-Soviet treaty and agreements, and warmly celebrated. The South China Sub-bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China immediately sent a telegram to the Central People's Government to express congratulations. Li Zhangda, head of the Southern General Branch of the China Democratic League, Chen Rutang, head of the South China Provisional Working Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang, Li Boqiu, head of the Guangdong Party Affairs Consolidation Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, Chen Qiyou, Chairman of the Zhi Gong Party, and non-party democrats Zhang Luchun and Li Langru held a discussion, unanimously expressing infinite joy and support for the Sino-Soviet treaty and agreements. After receiving the news, the preparatory committee of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions immediately convened a meeting of working groups from various factories. Liao Siguang, director of the preparatory committee, pointed out at the meeting that the signing of this treaty was a serious warning to the US imperialists' conspiracy to support the resurgence of Japanese imperialism and a head-on blow to the attempts of world imperialism to provoke a new war. The preparatory committee of the Guangzhou Municipal Democratic Women's Federation sent a lion dance team to the streets to celebrate and promote. All students of Southern University went to the city to carry out propaganda in the afternoon. Professors and student representatives from Sun Yat-sen University also held a discussion. The participants enthusiastically spoke out in support of the conclusion of the treaty and agreements and jointly sent a telegram to Chairman Mao and Chairman Stalin to congratulate them, together with Southern University.

### 广州遍悬国旗志庆  市民纷纷抢购号外欣然色喜  中共中央华南分局驰电祝贺

1950-02-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　广州遍悬国旗志庆
    市民纷纷抢购号外欣然色喜  中共中央华南分局驰电祝贺
    【新华社广州十六日电】中苏友好同盟互助条约及两个协定签订的消息传到此间后，全市机关、团体、学校和广大人民都欢腾鼓舞。此项消息在十五日晨八时半首先由广州人民广播电台广播出来，全市即遍悬国旗志庆。南方日报出版号外十万份，用两辆卡车环市发售，并向市民作口头宣传。繁盛街道上采购年货的人们纷纷抢购号外，无不欣然色喜。交新华社广州分社转发的贺电，纷至沓来。省、市各机关接到消息后，均立即召开紧急干部会议，宣布中苏缔结条约和协定的喜讯，热烈庆祝。中共中央华南分局立即驰电中央人民政府致贺。民主同盟南方总支部负责人李章达、国民党革命委员会华南临时工作委员会负责人陈汝棠、农工民主党广东党务整理委员会负责人李伯球、致公党主席陈其尤、无党派民主人士张醁村、李朗如等举行了谈话会，对此次中苏缔结条约及协定一致表示无限欢欣和拥护。市总工会筹备会接到消息后，立即召集各工厂工作组开会，筹备会主任廖似光在会上指出：此项条约的签订对美国帝国主义扶植日本帝国主义死灰复燃的阴谋给予一个严重警告，对世界帝国主义挑起新战争的企图给以迎头痛击。广州市民主妇联筹委会则出动舞狮队到街头庆祝和宣传。南方大学全校学生于下午出动市区进行宣传。中山大学教授和学生代表也举行了座谈会，与会者热烈发言拥护条约和协定的缔结，并联同南方大学分电毛主席及斯大林主席庆贺。
