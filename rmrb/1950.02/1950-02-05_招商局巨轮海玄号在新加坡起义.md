Title: China Merchants Steam Navigation Company's Giant Ship "Hai Xuan" Uprising in Singapore
Author:
Date: 1950-02-05
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 4th: The China Merchants Steam Navigation Company's ocean-going giant ship "Hai Xuan" announced its uprising in Singapore on the 24th of last month. The ship has a deadweight of over 10,000 tons. It was sailing from Aden to Japan, and upon arriving in Singapore on the 24th of last month, all the sailors immediately announced an uprising, raised the five-star national flag, and telegraphed the Hong Kong branch of the China Merchants Steam Navigation Company, expressing their obedience to orders to protect state property and remain in Singapore awaiting orders.

### 招商局巨轮海玄号在新加坡起义

1950-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　招商局巨轮海玄号在新加坡起义
    【新华社广州四日电】招商局远洋巨轮海玄号于上月二十四日在新加坡宣布起义。该轮载重一万余吨，此次由亚丁港开往日本，于上月二十四日抵新加坡后，全体海员即宣布起义，改悬五星国旗，并致电招商局香港分公司，表示遵令保护国家财产，留新加坡待命。
