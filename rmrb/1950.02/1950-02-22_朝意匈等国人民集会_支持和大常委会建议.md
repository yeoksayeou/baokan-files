Title: North Korea, Italy, Hungary and other countries hold rallies to support the suggestion of the World Peace Council Standing Committee

Author:
Date: 1950-02-22
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 21st. The Korean National Committee for the Defense of World Peace held a meeting in Pyongyang on the 18th. About 1,500 representatives from all walks of life in Pyongyang City, including workers, youth, scientists, professors, writers, artists, industrialists, and businessmen, as well as prominent social figures attended the meeting. Han Xueye, member of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Defenders of Peace and Chairman of the Korean National Committee for the Defense of World Peace, delivered a report at the meeting denouncing the US and British imperialists for instigating a new war and denouncing the US aggressive policy in South Korea. He called on the Korean people to support the call of the Korean Fatherland Front and the World Congress of Defenders of Peace, to unite around the peace and democratic camp headed by the Soviet Union, and to strive for the peaceful reunification of the motherland and lasting world peace. He also emphasized that the Korean people warmly support the Soviet government's proposal to organize an international special military tribunal to try Japanese bacteriological warfare criminals such as Hirohito. The conference finally passed a resolution to defend world peace and promote the peaceful reunification of the motherland.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 21st. TASS reports: Italy's cities and towns are launching a broad peace movement, enthusiastically supporting the five peace proposals made by the World Peace Congress Standing Committee to parliaments of all countries. According to "L'Unità", about three thousand towns have passed resolutions based on the Standing Committee's proposals. When the workers of the Milan railway station met on the 18th, they unanimously passed a resolution, vowing not to transport American war materials used for imperialist wars.
　　Meanwhile, the Hungarian National Committee for the Defense of Peace has convened a meeting to discuss the recent message from the World Peace Congress Standing Committee to parliaments of all countries calling for the defense of peace. The meeting unanimously endorsed the peace proposals in the message from the World Peace Congress Standing Committee and elected a delegation to submit this peace message to the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic on the 22nd.

### 朝意匈等国人民集会  支持和大常委会建议

1950-02-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝意匈等国人民集会  支持和大常委会建议
    【新华社平壤二十一日电】朝鲜拥护世界和平全国委员会，于十八日在平壤召开会议。到会有平壤市的工人、青年、科学家、教授、作家、艺术家、工商业者等各界代表和著名社会人士一千五百余人。世界拥护和平大会常委会委员、朝鲜拥护世界和平全国委员会委员长韩雪野，在大会上作了斥责美英帝国主义者挑拨新战争与斥责美国在南朝鲜的侵略政策的报告。他呼吁朝鲜人民拥护朝鲜祖国战线和世界拥护和平大会的号召，团结在以苏联为首的和平民主阵营的周围，为争取祖国的和平统一与世界的持久和平而斗争。他又强调指出，朝鲜人民热烈支持苏联政府关于组织国际特别军事法庭、审讯裕仁等日本细菌战犯的提议。大会最后通过了拥护世界和平与促进祖国和平统一的决议。
    【新华社北京二十一日电】综合塔斯社消息：意大利各都市和乡镇正在展开广大的和平运动，热烈支持世界和平大会常设委员会给各国议会的五项和平建议。《团结报》称，约有三千个市镇已根据常设委员会的建议通过了决议。米兰火车站工人于十八日开会时，一致通过决议，宣誓不运输用于帝国主义战争的美国战争物资。
    同时，匈牙利全国拥护和平委员会已召集会议，讨论世界和平大会常设委员会最近致世界各国议会呼吁保卫和平的文告。会议一致赞同世界和平大会常设委员会文告中的和平建议，并选出代表团，将此一和平文告于二十二日提交匈牙利人民共和国主席团。
