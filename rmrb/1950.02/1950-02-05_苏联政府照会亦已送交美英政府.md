Title: Soviet Government Notes Also Delivered to US and British Governments
Author:
Date: 1950-02-05
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 4th—Simultaneously with the Soviet Embassy in China's Chargé d'affaires, Mr. Shibaev, delivering the Soviet government's note to Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Li Kenong, according to a TASS dispatch from Moscow on the 3rd, identical notes were delivered on February 1st by the Soviet Ambassadors to Washington and London to the US government and the British government, respectively.

### 苏联政府照会亦已送交美英政府

1950-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联政府照会亦已送交美英政府
    【新华社北京四日电】与苏联驻我国大使馆代办史白夫将苏联政府照会送交我国外交部李克农副部长同时，据塔斯社莫斯科三日电，同样照会已于二月一日由苏联驻华盛顿和伦敦大使分别送交美国政府和英国政府。
