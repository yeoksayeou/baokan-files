Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
Author:
Date: 1950-02-05
Page: 4

　　[This newspaper reported] Central People's Broadcasting Station today (5th) at 17:30, Staff Time: Yuan Zhihe will speak on "I Saw the Great Socialist Country - the Soviet Union." 18:30 Natural Science Lecture: "The Father of Russian Geology, Karpinsky." 18:00 First Literary Program: Xihé Dagu "Catching Demons and Exorcising Evil." 21:00 Second Literary Program: Orchestral music "Anti-Japanese War Folk Song Medley," etc. 20:30 Saturday Art: Songs celebrating the first anniversary of the liberation of Beijing. 18:45 New Songs: "Iron Tree Blossoms," etc.
    [Another dispatch] Beijing People's Radio 12:30 Staff Time: Broadcasting Xiao Ming's report "The Main Tasks of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions in the Future" (recording). 16:30 Children's Program: Sun Jingxiu tells the Soviet novel "Son of the Regiment." 17:00 Broadcasting new songs. 18:45 Reporting on the situation of the first Municipal Workers' Congress on that day.

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-02-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台
    今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（五）日十七点三十分职工时间：袁致和讲《我看到了伟大的社会主义国家——苏联》。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：“俄国地质学之父喀平斯基”。十八点第一次文艺节目：西河大鼓“捉妖拿邪”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：管弦乐“抗战民歌联奏”等。八点三十分星期文艺：庆祝北京解放一周年歌曲。十八点四十五分新歌曲：《铁树开花》等。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工时间：播送萧明的报告《市总工会今后的主要任务》的录音。十六点三十分儿童节目：孙敬修讲苏联小说《团的儿子》。十七点播送新歌曲。十八点四十五分报导本市首届工代大会当天情况。
