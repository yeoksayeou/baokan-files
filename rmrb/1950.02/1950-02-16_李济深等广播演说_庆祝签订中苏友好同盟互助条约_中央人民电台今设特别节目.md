Title: Li Jishen and Others Broadcast Speeches: Celebrating the Signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, Central People's Broadcasting Station to Present Special Program Today
Author:
Date: 1950-02-16
Page: 4

　　Li Jishen and Others Broadcast Speeches
    Celebrating the Signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance
    Central People's Broadcasting Station to Present Special Program Today
    [This newspaper's report] The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast speeches by Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Huang Yanpei, Chen Shutong, Shen Yanbing, and other figures from various democratic parties and democratic personages to the entire nation from 20:20 to 21:00 today (the 16th) regarding the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance and the two agreements. From 18:30 to 19:00, the poet Xiao San will recite his new work "Long Live Sino-Soviet Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance!" and present a musical program celebrating the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. At 17:30, during the women's hour, the person in charge of the All-China Women's Federation will deliver a written speech to celebrate the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. At 18:00, the first literary and art program: Shanxian "Liberation, Emancipation, and a Happy New Year." At 21:00, the second literary and art program: Yangko Opera "Mother Wang Goes to the Fair." At 12:45 and 19:45, Soviet music and songs will be broadcast.

### 李济深等广播演说  庆祝签订中苏友好同盟互助条约  中央人民电台今设特别节目

1950-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　李济深等广播演说
    庆祝签订中苏友好同盟互助条约 
    中央人民电台今设特别节目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（十六）日廿点二十分到二十一点由各民主党派和民主人士李济深、张澜、黄炎培、陈叔通、沈雁冰等就中苏友好同盟互助条约和两个协定的签订向全国广播演说。十八点三十分到十九点诗人萧三朗诵他的新作“中苏友好同盟互助万岁！”和庆祝签订中苏友好同盟互助条约音乐节目。十七点三十分妇女时间：全国妇联负责人为庆祝签订中苏友好同盟互助条约发表的书面谈话。十八点第一次文艺节目：单弦“解放翻身过新年”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：秧歌剧“王大娘赶集”。十二点四十五分和十九点四十五分播送苏联音乐和歌曲。
