Title: Gui Sui Convenes Workers' Representative Meeting; City Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee Established
Author:
Date: 1950-02-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Gui Sui, 12th—The first Gui Sui City Workers' Representative Meeting was held from the 3rd to the 5th of this month. Over a hundred representatives from public and private enterprises and industries across the city attended. Fu Zuoyi, Chairman of the Suiyuan Provincial Military and Political Committee, and Gao Kelin, Secretary of the Chinese Communist Party Suiyuan Provincial Committee, were present to provide guidance. At the meeting, Zhang Wenfang, head of the Workers' Representative Meeting Preparatory Committee, delivered a report on the labor movement in the city since liberation and the guiding principles for future work. He emphasized that the focus in the future should be on restoring and developing production, establishing industrial and trade unions throughout the city within three to six months; at the same time, political education for workers should be strengthened to further establish the workers' sense of ownership of the country and a new attitude towards labor. Regarding the upcoming Gui Sui City People's Representative Conference, the meeting proposed actively preparing proposals and electing delegates to attend. Finally, the meeting elected twenty-five people, including Zhang Wenfang, as members of the City Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee, formally establishing the City Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee.

### 归绥召开工人代表会议  市总工会筹委会成立

1950-02-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　归绥召开工人代表会议
    市总工会筹委会成立
    【新华社归绥十二日电】归绥市首届工人代表会议于本月三日至五日举行，到有全市公私营企业、行业职工代表百余人。绥远省军政委员会主席傅作义、中共绥远省委书记高克林等均亲临指导。会上工人代表会筹备会负责人张文芳作了关于该市解放以来的工运概况和今后工作方针的报告，他着重指出今后要以恢复和发展生产为中心，在三个月至六个月内，把全市的产业、行业工会建立起来；同时要加强工人的政治教育，进一步树立工人们的国家主人翁思想和新的劳动态度。对于不久即将召开的归绥市各界人民代表会议，会上提出应积极准备提案和推选出席的代表。最后，会议选出张文芳等二十五人为市总工会筹委会委员，正式成立市总工会筹委会。
