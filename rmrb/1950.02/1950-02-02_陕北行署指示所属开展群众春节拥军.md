Title: Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Administrative Office Issues Directive to Subordinates to Launch Mass Spring Festival Support-the-Army Campaign
Author:
Date: 1950-02-02
Page: 1

　　【This Newspaper Dispatch】The Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Administrative Office issued a directive to its subordinate local governments on the 22nd of last month, stating that they should, based on the different circumstances in various parts of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region, do the following work in this year's Spring Festival support-the-army movement:
    I. Through various gatherings and mass cultural and recreational activities, widely publicize the establishment of the Northwest Military and Political Committee of the Central People's Government and the Shaanxi Provincial People's Government, as well as the glorious achievements and immortal contributions of the People's Liberation Army, to raise the political consciousness of the masses, stimulate enthusiasm for supporting the army, and make supporting the army a warm and conscious mass movement.
    II. In an organized and planned manner, mobilize the masses to conduct warm condolences and greetings to the families of revolutionary martyrs and disabled soldiers during the New Year's celebrations. Hold forums and get-togethers by village and township, listen to their opinions, solve the difficulties in their lives and production, help them formulate production plans for the coming year, thoroughly improve future preferential treatment work, and enable them to establish their households as soon as possible to reduce the burden on the people.
    III. For the troops within the territory, it is necessary to effectively mobilize the masses to conduct New Year's condolences on a voluntary basis.
    IV. Mobilize various organizations, groups, schools, and the masses to write letters of condolence to the soldiers at the front. If there are places with Yangko teams, they can first pay New Year's greetings and celebrate achievements and victories at the homes of the families of revolutionary martyrs.

### 陕北行署指示所属开展群众春节拥军

1950-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　陕北行署指示所属开展群众春节拥军
    【本报讯】陕北行政公署，于上月二十二日指示所辖各级地方政府，应根据陕北各地不同情况，在今年春节拥军运动中做好下列工作：
    一、通过各种集会和群众性的文化娱乐活动，广泛宣传中央人民政府西北军政委员会与陕西省人民政府的成立和人民解放军辉煌的战绩与不朽的功劳，以提高群众的政治觉悟，激发拥军热忱，使拥军成为热烈而自觉的群众性运动。
    二、有组织有计划地发动群众在年节对烈军属荣誉军人等进行热烈的慰劳慰问，以村以乡为单位召开座谈会、联欢会。吸收他们的意见，解决他们生活及生产中的困难，帮助其订出来年的生产计划，彻底改进今后的优待工作，使其早日建立家务，以减少民负。
    三、对于境内之部队，须很好的发动群众在自愿原则下，进行年节慰劳。
    四、发动各机关团体、学校、群众等，给前方战士写信慰问。如组织有秧歌队的地方，可首先去烈军属家中拜年和庆功贺喜。
