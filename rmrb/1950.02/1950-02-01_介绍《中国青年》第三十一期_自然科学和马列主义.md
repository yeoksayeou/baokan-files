　　Introducing the 31st Issue of "China Youth"
    -- Natural Science and Marxism-Leninism
    Li Gongtian
    The 31st issue of "China Youth" was published on the 28th. The content of this issue focuses on the relationship between Marxism-Leninism and natural science.
    Natural science, like social science, must be studied from a Marxist-Leninist perspective. The new translation of Engels' "Dialectics of Nature Introduction," a classic Marxist-Leninist work published in the previous and current issues of "China Youth," fully and clearly illustrates this point.
    Comrade Li Ya's "Natural Science Illuminated by Marxism-Leninism" tells us how Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin ingeniously used dialectics to study natural science, guiding and supporting the development of natural science.
    Biologist Zhou Jianren, in his article "Natural Science and Marxism-Leninism, Mao Zedong Thought," introduces how the two great Soviet scientists Michurin and Lysenko used Marxism-Leninism to study natural science. He tells us that "these facts can illustrate that the study of natural science must also involve learning Marxism-Leninism and understanding the Marxist-Leninist way of thinking in order to correctly conceive and correctly research, and avoid going astray."
    Comrade Wen Jize's article "The Bourgeois Worldview in Natural Science" uses in-depth and simple scientific examples to illustrate that the bourgeois worldview, that is, the explanation of natural phenomena and the way of thinking in studying natural science, is metaphysical and idealistic. In addition, he also criticized another aspect of the bourgeois worldview in Chinese natural science: blindly worshiping the science of European and American capitalist countries, and regarding science as a means to obtain personal fame, status, and personal enjoyment.
    Professor Pei Wenzhong, in his article "Should Students of Natural Science Study Marxism-Leninism?", uses the factual process of his research on the question of "Labor Created Man Himself" to illustrate that "dialectical materialism and historical materialism can help natural scientists further their knowledge and solve problems."
    "Everything is constantly moving and changing" - this is one of the basic laws of Marxist-Leninist materialist dialectics. Then, doesn't Newton's law of motion, "A body in motion tends to stay in motion, and a body at rest tends to stay at rest," conflict with materialist dialectics? The "China Youth Q&A" column gives us an answer to this meaningful question. It not only does not conflict with dialectics, but on the contrary, it is a proof of dialectics.
    Chairman Mao told us ten years ago that the communist cultural thought led by the Chinese Communist Party has caused a great revolution in the field of social science, but "it has not yet had time to occupy the position and fight in the field of natural science. Generally, natural science is temporarily ruled by the bourgeois worldview." Ten years have passed, and China's history has undergone tremendous changes. ### 介绍《中国青年》第三十一期——自然科学和马列主义
李公天
1950-02-01
第6版()
专栏：

　　介绍《中国青年》第三十一期
    ——自然科学和马列主义
    李公天
    《中国青年》第三十一期已于二十八日出版，本期内容是以马列主义与自然科学的关系为中心。
    自然科学正和社会科学一样，必须用马列主义观点来研究。上一期和这一期《中国青年》上所刊载的马列主义经典著作——恩格斯《自然辩证法导言》的新译文，就充分而明确地说明了这一点。
    李亚同志的《在马列主义照耀下的自然科学》，告诉我们：马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林怎样天才地用辩证法研究自然科学，指导并扶植了自然科学的发展。
    生物学家周建人先生在《自然科学与马列主义、毛泽东思想》一文中，介绍了苏联的两位大科学家米邱林和李森柯怎样运用马列主义研究自然科学。他告诉我们说，“这些事实，便可以说明研究自然科学也必须学习马列主义，懂得马列主义的思想方法，方才能正确地设想，正确地研究，不致走错了路。”
    温济泽同志的《自然科学中的资产阶级宇宙观》一文，用深入浅出的科学实例说明资产阶级的宇宙观，就是对于自然现象的解释以及研究自然科学的思想方法，是形而上学的，是唯心论的。此外，他还批判了中国自然科学中资产阶级宇宙观的另一方面：盲目地崇拜欧美资本主义国家的科学，把科学看成取得个人名誉地位以及个人享受的一种手段。
    裴文中教授在《学自然科学的人是否要学马列主义》一文中，用他研究《劳动创造了人本身》这个问题的事实过程来说明“辩证唯物主义和历史唯物主义能够帮助自然科学家的学识更进一步，能够解决问题”。
    “一切东西都在不断地运动和变化”——这是马列主义唯物辩证法的基本法则之一。那么，牛顿的《动者恒动，静者恒静》这条运动定律，岂不是和唯物辩证法冲突了吗？《中国青年问答》一栏给我们解答了这个有意义的问题。它不但没有和辩证法冲突，相反的，它正是辩证法的一个证明。
    毛主席在十年前告诉我们，中国共产党所领导的共产主义的文化思想，在社会科学领域中已引起了极大的革命，但是“还没有来得及在自然科学领域占领阵地与进行战斗，一般的暂时还让自然科学为资产阶级的宇宙观所统治。”十年了，中国的历史已经有了巨大的变化，毛主席在人民政协的开幕词中已经向全世界宣布：《中国人被认为不文明的时代已经过去了，我们将以一个具有高度文化的民族出现于世界。”我们希望中国自然科学工作者在以马列主义观点探究自然科学上，作更多的努力。
