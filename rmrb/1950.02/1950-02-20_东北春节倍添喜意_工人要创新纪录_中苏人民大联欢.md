Title: Northeast Spring Festival Doubly Joyful; Workers Aim to Set New Records; Sino-Soviet People's Grand Celebration

Author:

Date: 1950-02-20
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 19th: The people of various cities in Northeast China have been jubilant and enthusiastic in supporting and celebrating the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance in recent days, adding extra joy to the annual Spring Festival.

    Harbin

　　The city of Harbin, the capital of Songjiang Province, resounded with gongs and drums all day long. High-rise archways adorned with colorful lanterns were erected in front of the municipal government, the Federation of Industry and Commerce, and other buildings, illuminating the large-character slogans celebrating the "Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship and Alliance." On the evening of the 16th, various government agencies, organizations, factories, and schools in Harbin held celebration parties. The Songjiang Provincial People's Government, the Songjiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Harbin Municipal People's Government, and the Harbin Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association also jointly held a celebration rally on the same evening, inviting Mr. Malinin, Acting Consul of the Soviet Consulate in Harbin, and Deputy Consul Koshev to participate. A get-together was held afterwards. The Harbin Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a grand meeting in Daoli and Daowai to commemorate the 30th anniversary of Soviet film art, focusing on publicizing the great significance of the new treaty and new agreements. Workers in various factories in Harbin expressed their desire to celebrate with practical actions to step up production. Workers at the railway factory have made a guarantee to exceed the February production target.

    Jilin

　　Representatives from all walks of life in Jilin City, the capital of Jilin Province, and Soviet expatriates held a celebration rally of more than a thousand people on the 16th. Liu Xiwu, Secretary of the Jilin Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, attended and spoke. On the morning of the 17th, people from all walks of life held a celebration of the Sino-Soviet treaty signing and a Spring Festival get-together, with extremely enthusiastic spirits. High-rise archways were erected in front of various government agencies and organizations in the city, with the national flags of China and the Soviet Union complementing each other.

    Qiqihar

　　People from all walks of life in Qiqihar City, the capital of Heilongjiang Province, held a grand symposium of about a hundred people on the morning of the 16th. Speakers were very enthusiastic at the meeting. Teng Xingyi, a worker at the 31st Factory, said excitedly: "The new Sino-Soviet treaty and new agreements are of great help to the industrialization of New China. We must strive to create new production records to thank the Soviet Union for its brotherly assistance in our country's construction." Zou Xiangwen, chairman of the trade union of the Qiqihar Railway Bureau Locomotive Depot, said: "The signing of the new Sino-Soviet treaty has further strengthened our confidence in building people's railways. In the future, we must work harder to learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union and produce more '884' and '631' model locomotives." On the evening of the same day, various government agencies and organizations jointly invited Soviet friends residing in Qiqihar to hold a celebration banquet. Zhang Qilong, Secretary of the Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Yu Yifu, Chairman of the Provincial People's Government, and other leaders attended. The Qiqihar Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association also hosted a grand Sino-Soviet get-together dance and film evening to celebrate enthusiastically.

    Jinzhou

　　Jinzhou City, the capital of Liaoxi Province, has also been immersed in an atmosphere of revelry in recent days. Yangko dance teams from various government agencies and organizations performed non-stop in the streets, publicizing the contents of the new Sino-Soviet treaty and agreements to the audience. The street celebration pictorials and wall newspapers published by the Liaoxi Provincial Federation of Literary and Art Circles and the Liaoxi Post and Telecommunications Administration were crowded with viewers. Many government agencies, organizations, and residences posted red couplets celebrating the new Sino-Soviet treaty on their doors. Cadres from the Party, government, and military agencies in Liaoxi Province also held a celebration rally on the 16th.

    Andong

　　In Andong City, the capital of Liaodong Province, people from all walks of life held a grand celebration meeting amidst the sound of sirens at noon on the 15th. Fireworks and drums resounded throughout the night. Chengde, the capital of Rehe Province, also held a meeting on the 16th.

### 东北春节倍添喜意  工人要创新纪录  中苏人民大联欢

1950-02-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　东北春节倍添喜意
    工人要创新纪录  中苏人民大联欢
    【新华社沈阳十九日电】东北各城市人民连日欢欣鼓舞，热烈拥护和庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约的签订，使一年一度的春节倍添喜意。
    哈尔滨
    松江省会哈尔滨市整日锣鼓喧天。市政府、工商联合会等门前都高搭彩楼，挂着彩灯，把“庆祝中苏友好同盟条约、协定”的大字标语照得通明。十六日夜晚，哈市各机关、团体、工厂、学校都召开了庆祝晚会。松江省人民政府、中共松江省委会、哈尔滨市人民政府及中苏友好协会哈市分会亦于同晚联合举行庆祝大会，并邀请苏联驻哈领事馆代理领事马里宁、副领事考谢夫等参加。会后并举行联欢。中苏友好协会哈市分会在道里、道外分别召开了庆祝苏联电影艺术三十周年纪念大会，会上着重宣传了新条约、新协定的伟大意义。哈市各工厂工人均纷纷表示要以加紧生产的实际行动来庆祝。铁路工厂工人们提出了超过二月份生产任务的保证。
    吉林
    吉林省会吉林市各界代表与苏联侨民于十六日举行了千余人的庆祝大会，中共吉林省委书记刘锡五出席讲话。十七日上午，各界人民又举行了庆祝中苏缔约和春节联欢会，情绪极为热烈。该市各机关、团体门口高扎彩楼，中苏两国国旗交相辉映。
    齐齐哈尔
    黑龙江省会齐齐哈尔市各界人士于十六日上午举行了百人左右的盛大座谈会。会上，发言者甚为踊跃。三一厂工友滕兴义兴奋地说：“中苏新条约、新协定对于新中国的工业化有很大的帮助。我们要努力创造生产新纪录来感谢苏联对于我们祖国建设的兄弟一样的援助。”齐市铁路局机务段工会主席邹相文说：“中苏新约的签订更增强了我们建设人民铁路的信心。今后我们要更加努力地学习苏联的先进经验，出产更多的‘八八四’、‘六三一’等模范机车”。是日夜晚，各机关、团体联合邀请侨齐苏联友人举行庆祝宴会，中共省委会书记张启龙和省人民政府于毅夫主席等首长均莅临参加。中苏友好协会齐齐哈尔分会并主办了盛大的中苏联欢舞会和电影晚会，热烈庆祝。
    锦州
    辽西省会锦州市连日亦浸沉于狂欢的气氛中。各机关团体的秧歌队川流不息地在街头演出，向观众宣传中苏新条约和协定的内容。辽西省文联和辽西邮电管理局出版的街头庆祝画刊和壁报等前面，围满了观众。不少机关、团体和住宅的门上张贴了庆祝中苏新约的红色对联。辽西省党、政、军各机关干部并于十六日举行庆祝大会。
    安东
    辽东省会安东市，各界人民在十五日中午气笛齐鸣声中举行了盛大的庆祝会，入夜鞭炮锣鼓声响彻云霄。热河省会承德也于十六日举行大会。
