Title: Korean Ambassador to China, Li Zhouyuan, Hosts a Banquet for Our Government Leaders; Heads of Various People's Organizations Also Invited to Attend
Author:
Date: 1950-02-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 14th: Li Zhouyuan, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 12th to entertain the leaders of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and heads of various people's organizations. Those invited to the banquet included Zhu De, Li Jishen, and Zhang Lan, Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China; Lin Boqu, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government; Dong Biwu, Guo Moruo, and Huang Yanpei, Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council; Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Li Kenong and Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs; Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications; Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture; Yao Yilin, Vice Minister of Trade; Sa Kongliao, Deputy Director of the Press Administration; Li Lisan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Deng Yingchao, Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the New Democratic Youth League of China; and Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of Beijing. From the Korean Embassy, those accompanying were Counselor Cui Yi, Cui Ying, Kim Kuk-bo, First Secretary Kang Seok-gye, and other members of the embassy. The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 朝鲜驻华大使李周渊  欢宴我政府首长  各人民团体负责人亦应邀出席

1950-02-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜驻华大使李周渊
    欢宴我政府首长
    各人民团体负责人亦应邀出席
    【新华社北京十四日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使李周渊，于十二日晚设宴招待中华人民共和国中央人民政府各首长、各人民团体负责人等。应邀赴宴者有中华人民共和国中央人民政府副主席朱德、李济深、张澜，中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠，政务院副总理董必武、郭沫若、黄炎培，中国人民革命军事委员会代理总参谋长聂荣臻，外交部副部长李克农、章汉夫，邮电部长朱学范，文化部长沈雁冰，贸易部副部长姚依林，新闻总署副署长萨空了，中华全国总工会副主席李立三，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超，中国新民主主义青年团书记冯文彬，北京市副市长张友渔等。朝鲜大使馆方面作陪者有参事崔一、崔英、金国辅，一等书记官姜锡桂，以及大使馆其他人员。宴会在充满友好的气氛中进行。
