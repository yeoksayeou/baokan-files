Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station Today's Special Program Celebrating the Sino-Soviet Treaty

Author:
Date: 1950-02-20
Page: 4

　　Central People's Broadcasting Station Today's Special Program
    Celebrating the Sino-Soviet Treaty
    [This newspaper dispatch] The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast a speech by He Xiangning, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, to overseas Chinese at 9:30 and 19:45 today (20th), entitled "A Message to Overseas Chinese on the Establishment of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance". At 20:20, a special program celebrating the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance and two agreements, including a broadcast speech to the nation by Ma Xulun, Standing Director of the China Association for Promoting Democracy. 18:00 Arts Program: (1) Workers' Chorus, (2) Folk Art "Liberation, Emancipation, and Celebrating the Spring Festival", (3) Yangko Opera "Reporting Good News". 21:00 Arts Program: (1) Opera "Entering the Tile-Roofed House", (2) "Little Tune of the Beijing-Hankou Railway".

### 中央人民电台今日广播特别节目  庆祝中苏缔约

1950-02-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央人民电台今日广播特别节目
    庆祝中苏缔约
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（二十日）九点三十分和十九点四十五分中央人民政府华侨事务委员会主任委员何香凝向海外侨胞广播演讲“为中苏友好同盟互助条约成立告侨胞”。二十点二十分庆祝签订中苏友好同盟互助条约和两个协定的特别节目：包括中国民主促进会常务理事马叙伦对全国广播演讲。十八点文艺节目：（一）工人大合唱，（二）曲艺《解放翻身过春节》，（三）秧歌剧《报喜》。二十一点文艺节目：（一）歌剧《进瓦房》，（二）《京汉路小唱》。
