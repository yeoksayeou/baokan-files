Title: Warsaw Newspapers Feature Prominently the Communiqué on the Signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1950-02-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th: Warsaw, Polish Press Agency dispatch: On the 15th, the most important foreign news item in all Polish newspapers was the signing of the Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China. All Warsaw newspapers published the TASS communiqué on the signing of the treaty in Moscow with a large headline in a prominent position on the front page. All newspapers also published portraits of Stalin, Mao Zedong, Vyshinsky, and Zhou Enlai side by side.

### 华沙各报头版刊登  中苏在莫斯科订约的公报

1950-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　华沙各报头版刊登
    中苏在莫斯科订约的公报
    【新华社北京十六日电】波兰通讯社华沙讯：十五日，波兰所有一切报纸的外国消息栏中最重要的一则消息，是苏联和中华人民共和国签订友好同盟互助条约。华沙一切报纸均以头版头条地位在大标题下刊登塔斯社关于在莫斯科订约的公报。各报均并排刊出斯大林、毛泽东、维辛斯基及周恩来四人的肖像。
