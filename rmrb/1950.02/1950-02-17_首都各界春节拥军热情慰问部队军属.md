Title: People from All Walks of Life in the Capital Enthusiastically Comfort Troops and Military Dependents During the Spring Festival

Author:

Date: 1950-02-17
Page: 4

[Beijing News] The Spring Festival Support-the-Army Week stipulated by the Beijing Municipal Government begins today (17th). The Municipal Government yesterday (16th) sent personnel with various gifts to comfort the People's Liberation Army units guarding the capital. The Civil Affairs Bureau has also allocated 440,000 catties of preferential grain, distributed to various districts, of which 70% will be used as funds for various districts to organize production for the families of revolutionary martyrs, military personnel, and cadres, and 30% has been distributed to poor families of revolutionary martyrs, military personnel, and cadres as Spring Festival subsidies. District offices in each district have, in conjunction with the district committees of the Chinese Communist Party in each district, trade union offices, youth work groups, women's work groups, public security sub-bureaus, and local garrison units, prepared to hold military-civilian get-togethers and forums for the families of revolutionary martyrs, military personnel, and cadres. In addition to performing entertainment programs, flags will be presented to the garrison troops, flowers will be presented to the families of meritorious personnel, and future production plans will be discussed. All parks and places of scenic interest in the city will also provide free admission to the families of revolutionary martyrs, military personnel, and cadres. Suburban districts will also inspect the work of substitute farming for the families of revolutionary martyrs, military personnel, and cadres, and discuss the spring production issues of the families of revolutionary martyrs, military personnel, and cadres. In addition, various democratic parties have also organized comfort teams and donated comfort money to comfort wounded soldiers and veterans.

### 首都各界春节拥军热情慰问部队军属

1950-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　首都各界春节拥军热情慰问部队军属
    【北京讯】北京市政府所规定的春节拥军周自今（十七）日起开始。市府已于昨（十六）日派员携带各种慰劳品前往慰劳警卫首都的人民解放军各部队。民政局并已拨出优待粮四十四万斤，分发各区，其中十分之七作为各区组织烈、军、干属生产的资金，十分之三已分别发给贫苦的烈、军、干属，作为春节补助金。各区区公所均已会同各该区中共区委会、工会办事处、青年工作组、妇女工作组、公安分局及当地驻军等单位，准备分别举行军民联欢会与烈、军、干属座谈会，会上除表演游艺外，并将向驻军献旗，向功臣家属献花，讨论今后的生产计划。全市各公园及名胜古迹，也一律免费招待烈、军、干属游览。郊区各区并将检查烈、军、干属代耕的工作，讨论烈、军、干属的春季生产问题。此外，各民主党派也组织慰问队、献慰问金慰问伤兵及荣誉军人。
