Title: Romanian Workers' Party Issues Organizational Resolution: Will Begin Electing Party Organs, Calls for Raising Ideological and Political Level of Party Members, and Vigilance Against Enemy Elements Infiltrating the Party.
Author: 
Date: 1950-02-11
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th: Electrommunication Agency Bucharest News: The Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party issued a resolution on organizational issues on the 9th, stating that after the current party rectification work is completed, the party will begin electing all party organs and proceed with approving candidate members as formal members.

    The resolution urges attention to the conspiracy of hostile elements attempting to infiltrate party organizations, and emphasizes prudence when absorbing candidate members. The resolution calls for strengthening vigilance and continuously improving the ideological and political level of party members.

    The Central Committee also decided that decisions jointly adopted by the Politburo, the Secretariat, and the newly established Organization Bureau will be considered decisions of the Central Committee, while the most important decisions of the Organization Bureau or the Secretariat will be submitted to the Politburo for approval.

    The resolution expresses confidence in the party's ability to complete its tasks, one of the most important of which is to rapidly carry out socialist construction.

### 罗工人党发表有关组织决议  将着手选举党内机构  号召提高党员思想与政治水平  警惕敌对分子打进党内的阴谋

1950-02-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　罗工人党发表有关组织决议
    将着手选举党内机构
    号召提高党员思想与政治水平  警惕敌对分子打进党内的阴谋
    【新华社北京十日电】电通社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会九日发表关于组织问题的决议称：目前整党工作结束后，党内将着手选举党的一切机构，行并进批准候补党员为正式党员的工作。
    决议促起注意敌对分子企图打进党组织的阴谋，在吸收候补党员时尤应审慎。决议号召加强警惕性和不断提高党员的思想与政治水平。
    中委会又决定：此后政治局、书记处及新成立的组织局所共同通过的决定，将认为是中委会的决定，而组织局或书记处的最重要的决定，则将提交政治局批准。
    决议表示确信党完成任务的能力，最重要的任务之一便是迅速进行社会主义建设。
