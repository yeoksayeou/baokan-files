Title: Executive Yuan Guidance Reception Committee - East China Work Group Returns to Beijing - Preliminary Completion of Shanghai-Nanjing-Hangzhou Takeover Matters
Author:
Date: 1950-02-03
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 2nd: The East China Work Group of the Guidance Reception Committee of the Central People's Government Executive Yuan, after more than a month of work, has preliminarily completed the takeover of the former Kuomintang government's central organs, personnel, archives, books, property, and materials in Shanghai, Nanjing, Hangzhou, and other places. The group has now returned to Beijing, led by Deputy Head Nan Hanchen. Group Head and Deputy Premier Dong Biwu had already returned to Beijing on December 16th of last year due to official business. The East China Work Group arrived in Nanjing on December 10th of last year, led by Head Dong Biwu and Deputy Head Nan Hanchen, and successively took over a total of more than 6,000 boxes of various archives, books, and instruments in Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and other places, and processed 2,047 former staff members belonging to the central system of the former Kuomintang government. In order to train and handle unassigned personnel and to sort out the archives and books remaining in Nanjing, the Executive Yuan decided to establish a resident office in Nanjing to assist the local government in handling the above-mentioned related matters. The office officially began operations on February 1st.

### 政务院指导接收委员会  华东区工作团返京  初步完成沪宁杭接管事宜

1950-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院指导接收委员会
    华东区工作团返京
    初步完成沪宁杭接管事宜
    【新华社北京二日电】中央人民政府政务院指导接收工作委员会华东区工作团，经月余的工作，已初步完成上海、南京、杭州等地前国民党政府中央各机关、人员、档案、图书、财产、物资的接管事宜，现该团已由南汉宸副团长率领返抵北京。团长董必武副总理则已于去年十二月十六日因公先返北京。华东区工作团系于去年十二月十日在董必武、南汉宸正副团长率领下抵南京，先后在宁、沪、杭等地接管各种档案、图书、仪器共达六千余箱，处理原属前国民党政府中央系统各机关的旧工作人员达二千零四十七人。政务院为训练和处理未安置之人员、清理留宁之档案、图书，决定设立驻宁办事处，协助当地政府处理上述有关事宜。该办事处已于二月一日开始办公。
