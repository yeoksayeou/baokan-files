Title: Inspection Teams Arrive in Hebei and Pingyuan Disaster Areas to Begin Work
Author:
Date: 1950-02-04
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 3rd. The Hebei Disaster Area Inspection Team, composed of Wu Xinyu, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government, and Li Zheren, Director of the Research Office of the North China Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and the Pingyuan Disaster Area Inspection Team, led by Chen Qiyuan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government, have both arrived in the disaster areas of the two provinces to conduct inspections. The Hebei Disaster Area Inspection Team arrived in Lutai, Hebei, on the 2nd of this month to inspect the disaster situation in the severely affected areas of Ninghe, Baodi, Jixian, and Yutian, and to check on the progress of emergency relief work. The Pingyuan Disaster Area Inspection Team arrived in Xinxiang City on the 21st of last month, and on the morning of the 25th, accompanied a work team led by Sun Zhaohuan, Director of the Civil Affairs Department of the Pingyuan Provincial Government, to depart for the disaster area for inspection. The members of the two teams plan to travel from Shouzhang County to Liangshan, Jiaxiang, Heze, and Dongming, and then to Yutai, Jinxiang, and Juye for inspection.

### 河北、平原两灾区视察组已抵达灾区展开工作

1950-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　河北、平原两灾区视察组已抵达灾区展开工作
    【新华社北京三日电】中央人民政府内务部副部长武新宇、中共中央华北局研究室主任李哲人等组成的河北灾区视察组，及中央人民政府内务部副部长陈其瑗所率领的平原灾区视察组，均先后到达该两省灾区，进行视察。河北灾区视察组于本月二日抵河北芦台，视察宁河、宝坻、蓟县、玉田重灾区灾情，并检查急赈工作情形。平原灾区视察组，已于上月二十一日到达新乡市，二十五日晨随同由平原省政府民政厅长孙昭寰所带领的工作组，出发灾区视察。该两组一行人员，拟由寿张县转赴梁山、嘉祥、菏泽、东明，再赴鱼台、金乡、钜野等地视察。
