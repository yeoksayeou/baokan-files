Title: Li Jishen, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang, Sends a Telegram to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou to Express Respect and Support for the New Sino-Soviet Treaty and Agreements
Author: Li Jishen
Date: 1950-02-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 16th: Li Jishen, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou, the text of which is as follows: Chairman Mao, Premier Zhou:

　　We are very excited and warmly support the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, as well as the agreements on the Chinese Changchun Railway, Lushunkou, Dalian, and the loan agreement. Because the conclusion of these treaties and agreements is entirely in line with the fundamental interests of the Chinese and Soviet peoples, it is of unparalleled significance to the construction of our People's Democratic New China and to the maintenance and consolidation of peace in the Far East and the world. All the people of China and even the world, except for the imperialists and the remnants of the Chinese reactionaries, are jubilant and sincerely support it. We send our respects for your hard work and extend our highest revolutionary salute.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Li Jishen (seal)

### 民革委员会主席李济深  电毛主席暨周总理致敬拥护中苏新条约新协定
李济深
1950-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　民革委员会主席李济深
    电毛主席暨周总理致敬拥护中苏新条约新协定
    【新华社北京十六日电】中国国民党革命委员会主席李济深致电毛主席、周总理，电文如下：毛主席、周总理：
    我们听到中苏友好同盟互助条约以及中国长春铁路，旅顺口、大连协定和贷款协定签订的消息，我们十分兴奋，热烈拥护。因为这些条约和协定之缔结，完全符合于中苏两国人民的根本利益，对于我们人民民主新中国的建设和对远东与世界和平的维持与巩固，具有无比的意义。全中国以至全世界人民，除了帝国主义和中国反动派残余外，都是欢欣鼓舞、热诚拥护的。谨电慰辛劳，并致最崇高的革命敬礼。
                            中国国民党革命委员会  李济深（铣）
