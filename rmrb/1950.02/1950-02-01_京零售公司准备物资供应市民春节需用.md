Title: Beijing Retail Company Prepares Supplies to Meet Citizens' Needs for Spring Festival

Author:
Date: 1950-02-01
Page: 4

Beijing Retail Company Prepares Supplies
    To Supply Citizens' Needs for Spring Festival
    [Beijing News] In order to meet the needs of citizens for the Spring Festival, the Beijing Retail Company has prepared 4.8 million catties of white flour and thousands of bolts of various kinds of printed cloth, which are scheduled to begin distribution today (1st).

### 京零售公司准备物资供应市民春节需用

1950-02-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　京零售公司准备物资
    供应市民春节需用
    【北京讯】北京市零售公司为应市民春节需要，已准备好白面四百八十万斤，各种花布数千匹，定于今（一）日起开始配售。
