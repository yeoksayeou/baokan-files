Title: Saifuddin Arrives in Moscow to Participate in Sino-Soviet Negotiations

Author:

Date: 1950-02-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 31st—Saifuddin, Vice Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government, leading a delegation from the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government comprising several members, arrived in Moscow on January 30th to participate in the Sino-Soviet negotiations currently being conducted by our delegation.

### 参加中苏会商工作  赛福鼎抵莫斯科

1950-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　参加中苏会商工作
    赛福鼎抵莫斯科
    【新华社莫斯科三十一日电】我新疆省人民政府副主席赛福鼎，率领新疆省人民政府代表一行多人，于一月三十日抵达莫斯科，参加我国代表团刻正进行的中苏会商工作。
