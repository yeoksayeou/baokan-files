Title: The Soviet Union often helps with economic cooperation with People's Democratic countries. The first year of Czechoslovakia's Five-Year Plan is successfully completed. Industrial production exceeds the plan, agricultural output and trade volume increase.
Author:
Date: 1950-02-24
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 23rd—TASS, Prague: The Czechoslovak State Planning Commission has recently released a communique regarding the implementation of the first year (i.e., 1949) of the Five-Year Plan for the development of the national economy.

　　Industrial production exceeded the 1949 plan by two percent. The total industrial output increased by eight percent compared to 1948 and by 11.5 percent compared to the pre-war period. The manufacturing of production tools exceeded the pre-war level by more than forty percent. Labor productivity in industry increased by 3.5 percent compared to 1948 and exceeded the pre-war level.

　　Transportation also exceeded the 1949 plan.

　　In 1949, the socialist sector in industry expanded from 91.5 percent to 97.5 percent.

　　The total output of agricultural production has also increased. The state procurement plan for agricultural products has been exceeded. In 1949, machinery and electricity were used on a large scale in agriculture. Last year, the country's agriculture received 5,500 tractors and 5,900 tractor-drawn plows and other agricultural machines.

　　In 1949, the total volume of foreign trade increased by 4.3 billion crowns compared to 1948. The value of imported raw materials and goods was 39.4 billion crowns, while the value of exported goods was 40.3 billion crowns. In 1948, the total trade volume with the Soviet Union and the People's Democratic countries accounted for 31.5 percent of Czechoslovakia's total foreign trade, while in 1949 it had increased to 45.4 percent.

　　The communique emphatically pointed out that the successful completion of the first year of the Five-Year Plan was due to the constant assistance from the Soviet Union and economic cooperation with various People's Democratic countries.

　　After the completion of the first year of the plan, the living standards of the working people have greatly improved. This is reflected in the increasing income of the working people, the increase in food consumption, and the increase in sales of manufactured goods.

　　The network of cultural institutions, as well as hospitals, nurseries, and kindergartens, has been greatly expanded. Thousands of apartments have been built.

### 苏联经常帮助和人民民主国家经济合作  捷五年计划第一年胜利完成  工业超过计划农业产量和贸易额增加

1950-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联经常帮助和人民民主国家经济合作
    捷五年计划第一年胜利完成
    工业超过计划农业产量和贸易额增加
    【新华社北京二十三日电】塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克国家计划委员会顷发表关于发展国民经济五年计划第一年（即一九四九年）执行情形的公报。
    工业超过一九四九年计划百分之二。工业总产量较一九四八年增加百分之八，较战前增加百分之十一点五。生产工具的制造超过战前水平百分之四十以上。工业中劳动生产率较一九四八年增加百分之三点五，并超过战前水平。
    运输方面也超过了一九四九年的计划。
    一九四九年，工业中的社会主义部分，已从百分之九十一点五扩大到百分之九十七点五。
    农业生产的总产量亦已增加。国家农产品缴纳计划已被超过。一九四九年，在农业方面大规模地使用了机器和电力。去年，全国农业得到了五千五百架拖拉机以及五千九百具拖拉机曳引的犁及其他农业机器。
    一九四九年，对外贸易总额较一九四八年增加了四十三亿克郎。进口原料和商品价值三百九十四亿克郎，而出口货价值为四百零三亿克郎。一九四八年对苏联和人民民主国家的贸易总额占捷克斯洛伐克全部对外贸易的百分之三十一点五，而一九四九年则已增加到百分之四十五点四。
    公报着重指出：五年计划的第一年计划之能胜利完成，是由于苏联的经常帮助和与各人民民主国家的经济合作。
    第一年计划完成后，劳动人民的生活水平已大为提高。这表现在劳动人民收入的日益增加、粮食消费的增加和制成品销售的增多上。
    文化机关网以及医院、保育院和幼稚园已大大扩大。成千座公寓已经建筑起来。
