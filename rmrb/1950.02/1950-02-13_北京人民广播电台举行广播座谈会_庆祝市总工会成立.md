Title: Beijing People's Broadcasting Station Holds Broadcast Forum to Celebrate the Establishment of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1950-02-13
Page: 4

　　Beijing People's Broadcasting Station Holds Broadcast Forum
    Celebrating the Establishment of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions
    [This Newspaper's News] The Central People's Broadcasting Station today (13th) at 17:30, Women's Time: (1) A notice from the All-China Women's Federation to the Women's Federations of major administrative regions, provinces, and major cities; (2) An introduction to the brief history of "March 8th" International Working Women's Day. 18:30 Natural Science Lecture: "The Story of Soviet Scientist Pavlov."
    [Another News Item] Beijing People's Radio 12:30 Staff Program: Broadcast Forum celebrating the establishment of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions. 13:00 Youth Program: "Talking about Criticism and Self-Criticism." 16:30 Children's Program: (1) Current Affairs Talk, (2) Songs. 17:00 Vocal Program: The Municipal Midwifery School Choir performs new songs.

### 北京人民广播电台举行广播座谈会  庆祝市总工会成立

1950-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京人民广播电台举行广播座谈会
    庆祝市总工会成立
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（十三日）十七点三十分妇女时间：（一）全国妇联给各大行政区、各省、各大城市妇联的一件通知，（二）介绍“三八”国际劳动妇女节简史。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：“苏联科学家巴夫洛夫的故事”。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工节目：庆祝市总工会成立广播座谈会。十三点青年节目：“谈谈批评与自我批评”。十六点三十分儿童节目：（一）时事讲话，（二）歌曲。十七点歌咏节目：市立助产学校歌咏团演唱新歌曲。
