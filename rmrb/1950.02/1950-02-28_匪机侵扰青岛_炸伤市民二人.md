Title: Bandit Planes Harass Qingdao, Wounding Two Citizens

Author:

Date: 1950-02-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Qingdao, 27th—At 11:40 AM today, three Kuomintang remnant bandit planes invaded the airspace of Qingdao City, indiscriminately bombing and strafing for approximately fifteen minutes, wounding two citizens. During the bandit plane raid, police officers from the Municipal Public Security Bureau and members of the residents' Winter Defense Committee's air defense team quickly mobilized to maintain traffic order and rescue the injured citizens.

### 匪机侵扰青岛  炸伤市民二人

1950-02-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　匪机侵扰青岛  炸伤市民二人
    【新华社青岛二十七日电】今日上午十一时四十分，国民党残匪飞机三架侵入青岛市空，滥肆投弹扫射约十五分钟，炸伤市民二人。匪机空袭时，市公安局员警及居民冬防委员会防空组，均迅速出动，维持交通秩序，抢救受伤市民。
