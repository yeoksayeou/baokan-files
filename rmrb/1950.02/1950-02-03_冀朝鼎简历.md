Title: Ji Chaoding's Resume
Author: 
Date: 1950-02-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 2nd: Ji Chaoding, who was appointed today by the Central People's Government as China's representative to the United Nations Economic and Social Council, is currently the Director of the Foreign Investment Bureau of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council. Ji Chaoding is a non-party democratic person and a well-known expert familiar with Chinese and foreign economic conditions.

### 冀朝鼎简历

1950-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　冀朝鼎简历
    【新华社北京二日电】中央人民政府本日任命为我国出席联合国经济暨社会理事会代表的冀朝鼎，现任政务院财政经济委员会外资企业局局长。冀朝鼎是无党派民主人士和著名的熟悉中外经济情况的专家。
