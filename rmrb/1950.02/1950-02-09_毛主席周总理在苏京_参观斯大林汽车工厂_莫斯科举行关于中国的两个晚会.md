Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou in the Soviet Capital: Visiting the Stalin Automobile Factory; Two Evenings about China Held in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1950-02-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 8th: TASS, Moscow dispatch: Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Government Administration Council of the People's Republic of China, Wang Jiaxiang, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, Li Fuchun, Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, Saifuddin, Vice Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government, and others, visited the Stalin Automobile Factory on February 7th.

The distinguished guests inspected the iron forging workshop, the foundry, the passenger car assembly department, and the central conveyor belt for two hours. Factory Director Likhachev explained the factory's history and production situation to the guests in detail.

The factory workers welcomed the distinguished guests with prolonged applause and cheers to Mao Zedong.

After touring the factory, the distinguished guests also visited the factory's Palace of Culture. They visited the library, reading room, theater, and the works of amateur art groups there.

Accompanying the distinguished guests were Soviet Ambassador to the People's Republic of China Roshchin, Secretary-General of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs Podtserob, and Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs Molochkov.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 8th: TASS, Moscow dispatch: The Central Writers' Club and the Cinema House in Moscow held evenings about China on February 7th. At the Central Writers' Club evening, Simonov, Deputy Secretary-General of the Soviet Writers' Union, film director Gerasimov, and Dubrovinina, Deputy Minister of Education of the Russian Federation, spoke about their recent impressions of their visit to the People's Republic of China, and reported on the Chinese people's efforts to establish a new and free life. A concert followed, with programs including Chinese folk songs, music by Chinese composers, and Soviet songs sung in Chinese, and a documentary about China was screened.

The Cinema House's evening was held in honor of the culture and art of the People's Republic of China, with a program featuring works by Chinese composers, and poems and songs written for democratic China by Soviet poets and musicians. Finally, the Northeast Film Studio's film "The Bridge" was screened.

### 毛主席周总理在苏京  参观斯大林汽车工厂  莫斯科举行关于中国的两个晚会

1950-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席周总理在苏京
    参观斯大林汽车工厂
    莫斯科举行关于中国的两个晚会
    【新华社北京八日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东、中华人民共和国政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来、中华人民共和国驻苏大使王稼祥、东北人民政府副主席李富春、新疆省人民政府副主席赛福鼎等，二月七日曾参观斯大林汽车工厂。
    贵宾们视察了锻铁场、翻砂间、客车装配部和中央运送带达两小时。工厂厂长利哈切夫向客人们详细解说工厂的历史及其生产情况。
    工厂工人以长时间的掌声和向毛泽东欢呼来欢迎贵宾。
    贵宾们在巡视工厂之后，并参观了工厂的文化宫。他们在那里参观了图书馆、阅览室、剧院及业余艺术团体的作品。
    陪伴贵宾们参观的有苏联驻中华人民共和国大使罗申、苏联外交部秘书长波得佐罗德及苏联外交部交际司司长莫洛赤科夫。
    【新华社北京八日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：莫斯科的作家中央俱乐部和电影大厦于二月七日分别举行关于中国的晚会。在作家中央俱乐部的晚会中，苏联作家协会副总书记西蒙诺夫、电影导演格拉西莫夫、俄罗斯联邦教育部副部长杜伯洛维娜都讲述了他们最近访问中华人民共和国所获的印象，并报告了中国人民在建立新的自由生活的情形。随后举行了音乐会，节目包括中国的民歌，中国作曲家的音乐和用华语演唱的苏联歌曲，并放映有关中国的纪录片。
    电影大厦的晚会是为了中华人民共和国的文化和艺术而举行的，节目中有中国作曲家的作品，苏联诗人及音乐家为民主中国而作的诗歌及歌曲。最后则放映东北电影制片厂的影片《桥》。
