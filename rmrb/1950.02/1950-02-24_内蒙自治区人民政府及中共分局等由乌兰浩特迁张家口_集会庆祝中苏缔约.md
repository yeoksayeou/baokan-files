Title: Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government and CCP Branch Bureau, etc. Move from Ulanhot to Zhangjiakou; Rally to Celebrate Sino-Soviet Treaty

Author:
Date: 1950-02-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 23rd: Zhangjiakou News: The People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the CCP Inner Mongolia Branch Bureau, the Inner Mongolia Military Region, and more than 500 cadres from various Inner Mongolia organs and groups in Zhangjiakou City, which recently moved from Ulanhot to Zhangjiakou, held a rally on February 16th to celebrate the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance. At the meeting, Yun Ze, Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government, elaborated in detail on the great significance of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance. He said: This kind of friendly alliance will inevitably continue permanently, and its future impact on all mankind will be extremely profound. Chairman Yun Ze called for strengthening Inner Mongolia's economic construction to welcome the new era of Sino-Soviet friendly cooperation. At present, all Inner Mongolia cadres must take the Sino-Soviet Joint Communiqué, the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance, the two agreements published at the same time, and the Xinhua News Agency editorial and other documents as the main documents for study during this period.

### 内蒙自治区人民政府及中共分局等由乌兰浩特迁张家口  集会庆祝中苏缔约

1950-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　内蒙自治区人民政府及中共分局等由乌兰浩特迁张家口
    集会庆祝中苏缔约
    【新华社北京二十三日电】张家口讯：不久前由乌兰浩特迁来张垣的内蒙古自治区人民政府、中共内蒙古分局、内蒙军区以及在张家口市的内蒙各机关团体干部五百余人，于二月十六日举行庆祝中苏缔结友好同盟互助条约大会。会上内蒙自治区人民政府云泽主席详细阐述了中苏友好同盟互助条约的伟大意义。他说：这种友好同盟必将永久继续下去，对全人类将来的影响必将极其深远。云泽主席号召加强内蒙经济建设，来迎接中苏友好合作的新时代。目前全内蒙干部必须把中苏两国政府联合公报，中苏友好同盟互助条约及同时发表的两个协定和新华社社论等文件，作为目前时期学习的主要文件。
