Title: Guangxi Provincial People's Government Established
Author:
Date: 1950-02-10
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, 9th. The Guangxi Provincial People's Government was formally established in Nanning on the 8th. Zhang Yunyi, Chairman of the Provincial Government, and Vice Chairmen Chen Manyuan, Li Renren, and Lei Jingtian were previously appointed by the fourth meeting of the Central People's Government Committee. The entire province of Guangxi was liberated as early as one month and five days after the heroic People's Liberation Army advanced. The revolutionary order of the entire province has now been initially established, and the people's lives are gradually becoming stable. On the evening of the 8th, all staff members of the Guangxi Provincial People's Government held a celebration party for the establishment of the provincial government. At the meeting, Vice Chairmen Chen Manyuan and Lei Jingtian spoke successively. Vice Chairman Chen pointed out that the main tasks in Guangxi at present are to eliminate bandits and special agents, restore and develop production, carry out rural work, and prepare for rent reduction. Vice Chairman Chen encouraged the staff to truly serve the people and strive to improve their ideological and policy level.

### 广西省人民政府成立

1950-02-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　广西省人民政府成立
    【新华社南宁九日电】广西省人民政府于八日在南宁正式成立。省政府主席张云逸、副主席陈漫远、李任仁、雷经天前经中央人民政府委员会第四次会议通过任命。广西全省在英勇的人民解放军进军一个月零五天之后，早经解放。全省革命秩序现已初步建立，人民生活亦渐臻安定。八日晚广西省人民政府全体工作人员举行庆祝省府成立晚会。会上陈漫远、雷经天两副主席先后讲话。陈副主席指出：广西当前的主要任务为肃清土匪特务，恢复和发展生产，开展农村工作，准备实行减租。陈副主席勉励工作人员要作到真正为人民服务，并努力提高思想和政策水平。
