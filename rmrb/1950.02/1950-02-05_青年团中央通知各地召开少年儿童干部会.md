Title: Youth League Central Committee Notifies All Regions to Convene Meetings of Young Pioneers Cadres
Author:
Date: 1950-02-05
Page: 3

　　Youth League Central Committee Notifies All Regions
    To Convene Meetings of Young Pioneers Cadres
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 4th: The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League issued a notice on January 30th, deciding to convene the first National Conference of Young Pioneers Cadres in Beijing on April 20th. The conference will adopt the regulations of the Chinese Young Pioneers and the nationally unified flag, song, salute, and member badge. The candidates to attend the conference will mainly be youth league cadres working with children, primary and secondary school teachers with experience in guidance work, and Young Pioneers captains. The number of attendees is estimated as follows: a total of 49 people from the major administrative regions of East China, Northeast China, North China, Central South China, Northwest China, and Southwest China. A total of 38 people from major cities such as Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang, Wuhan, Harbin, Nanjing, Guangzhou, Jinan, Shijiazhuang, Weifang, Zhangjiakou, Dalian, and Taiyuan. A total of 20 people from rural areas, factories, railways, and other units of different natures. The notice stated: Youth League Work Committees in all regions should immediately widely solicit materials for the flag, song, salute, and member badge, and prepare work reports since the establishment and transfer of teams, including statistics on the number and age of primary and secondary school students, the number of cadres and members, the activities of members, and problems in the work, materials on the guidance work of primary school teachers, typical experiences, and various materials and opinions related to the content of the conference, etc. These materials should be sent to the Youth League Central Committee's Children's Department before March 5th. Cadres attending the conference should report to Beijing on April 18th.

### 青年团中央通知各地召开少年儿童干部会

1950-02-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　青年团中央通知各地
    召开少年儿童干部会
    【新华社北京四日电】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会于一月三十日发出通知，定四月二十日在北京召开第一次全国少年儿童干部大会。大会将通过中国少年儿童队的章程和全国统一的队旗、队歌、队礼、队员标志等。参加大会的人选主要为青年团少年儿童工作干部，对辅导工作有经验的小学、中学教师和少年儿童队队长。出席人数计：华东、东北、华北、中南、西北、西南等大行政区共四十九人。北京、上海、天津、沈阳、武汉、哈尔滨、南京、广州、济南、石家庄、潍坊、张家口、大连、太原等大城市共三十八人。农村、工厂、铁路各不同性质的单位共二十人。通知称：各地青年团工作委员会要立即广泛征求队旗、队歌、队礼、队员标志等材料，并准备建队、转队以来的工作报告，包括小学生和初中学生的人数与年龄统计数字，干部人数与队员人数，队员活动情况及工作中的问题，小学教师辅导工作的材料，典型经验以及和大会内容有关的各项材料和意见等，这些材料应于三月五日以前寄达团中央少年儿童部。参加大会干部务于四月十八日到北京报到。
