Title: Democratic Korea Newspaper Publishes Editorial Endorsing Soviet Government's Note, Advocating Severe Punishment for Japanese Bacteriological Warfare Criminals
Author:
Date: 1950-02-11
Page: 4

　　Democratic Korea Newspaper Publishes Editorial
    Endorsing Soviet Government's Note, Advocating Severe Punishment for Japanese Bacteriological Warfare Criminals
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 10th] The Democratic Korea newspaper published an editorial on the 9th, endorsing the Soviet government's note and demanding severe punishment for Japanese bacteriological warfare criminals including Hirohito. The editorial emphasized that the US imperialism's support for Japan's resurgence is a threat to Korea's independence and autonomy. The Korean people urgently demand that Japanese bacteriological warfare criminals including Hirohito be brought to the International Special Military Tribunal for trial. The Workers' Party organ, "Labor News," also published an article on the 8th demanding that Japanese war criminals be brought to the international tribunal.

### 民主朝鲜报发表社论  拥护苏联政府的照会  主张严惩日细菌战犯

1950-02-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　民主朝鲜报发表社论
    拥护苏联政府的照会  主张严惩日细菌战犯
    【新华社平壤十日电】此间民主朝鲜报于九日发表社论，拥护苏联政府的照会，要求严惩裕仁等日本细菌战犯。社论着重指出：美帝国主义扶植日本再起，是对朝鲜独立自主的威胁。朝鲜人民迫切要求将裕仁等日本细菌战犯送交国际特别军事法庭审判。劳动党机关报劳动新闻，亦于八日发表一文，要求把日本战犯送交国际法庭。
