Title: New Knowledge Forum to Hold its 44th Meeting Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1950-02-02
Page: 4

　　New Knowledge Forum to Hold its 44th Meeting Tomorrow
　　[Our Dispatch] The 44th meeting of the New Knowledge Forum, sponsored by the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, is scheduled to be held at 7:00 PM on Friday, February 3rd, in the auditorium of the European and American Students Association on Nanheyan Street. Vice Minister Wang Xinyuan of the Ministry of Light Industry has been invited to report on "The 1950 National Light Industry Plan and Current Situation." The report will focus on: 1. What departments does light industry mainly include, and what is the current state of production and sales? 2. What are the key construction projects for light industry at present? How to restore its production technically and operationally? 3. How to coordinate public and private light industry? How to coordinate with heavy industry? 4. What are the existing difficulties in private light industry? How to solve them? 5. Under what conditions can private individuals invest in light industry? And in what kind of light industry should they invest?

### 新知识座谈会明开四四次会

1950-02-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　新知识座谈会明开四四次会
    【本报讯】民主建国会北京市分会主办的新知识座谈会第四十四次会，定于二月三日（星期五）下午七时在南河沿欧美同学会礼堂举行，敦请轻工业部王新元副部长报告“一九五○年全国轻工业的计划和目前概况”。报告的重点是：一、轻工业主要是包括那些部门、产销现状如何？二、当前轻工业的重点建设是什么？在技术上业务上如何恢复其生产？三、公私营的轻工业如何配合？与重工业又如何配合？四、私营轻工业现存的困难是什么？怎样解决？五、在何种条件下私人可向轻工业投资？并应向何种轻工业投资？
