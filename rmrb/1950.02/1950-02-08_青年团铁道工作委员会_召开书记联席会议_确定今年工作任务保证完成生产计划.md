Title: Communist Youth League Railway Work Committee Convenes Secretaries' Joint Meeting to Determine This Year's Work Tasks and Ensure Completion of Production Plan
Author:
Date: 1950-02-08
Page: 3

　　Youth League Railway Work Committee
    Convenes Secretaries' Joint Meeting
    Determines This Year's Work Tasks to Ensure Completion of Production Plan
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th] The National Railway Work Committee of the New Democratic Youth League held a joint meeting of secretaries of the Youth League sub-committees and above from January 20th to 25th. Teng Daiyuan, Minister of Railways of the Central People's Government, Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Youth League, and others attended the meeting and delivered speeches, giving important instructions on the work of the Railway Youth League. Lu Ping, Secretary of the Railway Work Committee of the Youth League, made a work summary report for 1949 at the meeting, and Yue Zhijian, Deputy Secretary, made a report on the work tasks for 1950.

    In his speech, Minister Teng Daiyuan pointed out that the direction of the Youth League's railway work in the future is to: unite the broad masses of young workers, implement the policy of focusing on production, carry out technical, cultural, and political studies, and consolidate and develop the League's organization in the production movement. Secretary Feng Wenbin quoted Lenin's famous saying, "Be able to grasp the central link of the chain," calling on the leading cadres of the Youth League to investigate and study the central work and tasks of the administration, the Party, and the trade union, understand the demands of the broad masses of railway employees and the existing problems, and strive to do a good job in the Youth League's work with the spirit of loving the cause, the country, and the people.

    The meeting summarized the work of 1949, reviewed the shortcomings in the past work, and based on the Ministry of Railways' 1950 work plan and the actual situation of the Youth League, decided that this year's work tasks are: on the existing basis, to further implement the work policy centered on production, lead the League members, unite the broad masses of young workers, carry out technical, cultural, and political studies, improve the technical and political level of workers, so that the League's organization can be consolidated and developed in the production movement, and strive to actively complete the 1950 production plan.

### 青年团铁道工作委员会  召开书记联席会议  确定今年工作任务保证完成生产计划

1950-02-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　青年团铁道工作委员会
    召开书记联席会议
    确定今年工作任务保证完成生产计划
    【新华社北京七日电】新民主主义青年团全国铁道工作委员会于一月二十日至二十五日举行了全国铁道青年团分团委以上书记联席会议。中央人民政府铁道部长滕代远、青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬等都到会讲话，对铁道青年团的工作作了重要的指示。青年团铁道工作委员会书记陆平在会议中作了一九四九年的工作总结报告，副书记岳志坚作了一九五零年工作任务的报告。
    滕代远部长在讲话中指出了今后青年团铁道工作的方向是：团结广大的青年工人，贯彻以生产为主的方针，开展技术、文化和政治的学习，并在生产运动中去巩固与发展团的组织。冯文彬书记则引用列宁的名言“善于抓住锁练的中心环节”，号召青年团的领导干部去调查研究行政、党和工会的中心工作与任务，了解广大铁路员工的要求和目前所存在的问题，本着爱事业、爱国家、爱人民的精神，努力做好青年团的工作。
    会议总结了一九四九年的工作，检讨过去工作中所存在的缺点，并根据铁道部一九五零年工作计划和青年团的实际情况，决定了今年的工作任务是：在现有的基础上，进一步贯彻以生产为中心的工作方针，领导团员，团结广大的青年工人，开展技术、文化和政治学习，提高工人的技术和政治水平，使团的组织在生产运动中得到巩固和发展，为积极完成一九五零年的生产计划而奋斗。
