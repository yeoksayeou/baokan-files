Title: Hungarian Minister to China Száfrankó Pays Courtesy Call on Our Government Leaders; Vice Foreign Minister Li Hosts Banquet for Hungarian Legation Staff

Author: 
Date: 1950-02-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 24th -- Following the presentation of his credentials, the Minister of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, Száfrankó, paid courtesy calls from the 22nd to the 24th on Vice Chairman Zhu De, Vice Chairman Li Jishen of the Central People's Government, Vice Premier Dong Biwu, Vice Premier Guo Moruo of the Government Administration Council, Mayor Nie Rongzhen of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Vice Mayors Zhang Youyu and Wu Han, and Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing.

Vice Foreign Minister Li Kenong of the Central People's Government's Ministry of Foreign Affairs hosted a banquet at 7:00 PM today for Hungarian Minister to China Száfrankó and all members of the legation. Invited to accompany the event were Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, Director of the General Office Wang Bingnan, and Director of the Bureau of Protocol Yu Xinqing of the General Office of the Central People's Government Committee.

### 匈公使夏法朗柯拜谒我政府首长  李副外长宴匈公使馆人员

1950-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　匈公使夏法朗柯拜谒我政府首长
    李副外长宴匈公使馆人员
    【新华社北京二十四日电】匈牙利人民共和国驻我国公使夏法朗柯于呈递国书后，自二十二日至二十四日，分别拜谒了我中央人民政府朱德副主席，李济深副主席，政务院董必武副总理，郭沫若副总理，北京市人民政府聂荣臻市长，张友渔、吴晗副市长及文化部沈雁冰部长。
    中央人民政府外交部副部长李克农于今日下午七时宴请匈牙利驻我国公使夏法朗柯及全体馆员。应邀作陪者有外交部副部长章汉夫，办公厅主任王炳南，中央人民政府委员会办公厅典礼局局长余心清等。
