Title: Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions Elects Leaders
Author:
Date: 1950-02-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 20th. The Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions held its first meeting of all committee members, alternate committee members, and all supervisory committee members on the 16th. All committee members mutually elected Liu Changsheng as Chairman of the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions, Zhang Qi, Zhu Junxin, and Shen Han as Vice-Chairmen, and Chen Gongqi, Tang Guifen, Fan Xiaofeng, Ma Xiaodi, and thirty-one others as Standing Committee members. All supervisory committee members mutually elected Shen Xiangsheng as Director of the Supervisory Committee. The meeting also approved the work plan for the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions for the next three months.

### 上海总工会选出负责人

1950-02-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　上海总工会选出负责人
    【新华社上海二十日电】上海总工会于十六日举行第一次全体委员、候补委员及全体监察委员会议。全体委员互选出刘长胜为上海总工会主席，张祺、朱俊欣、沈涵为副主席，陈公祺、汤桂芬、范小凤、马小弟等三十一人为常务委员。全体监察委员互选出沈翔声为主任监察委员。会议并通过了上海总工会三个月内的工作计划。
