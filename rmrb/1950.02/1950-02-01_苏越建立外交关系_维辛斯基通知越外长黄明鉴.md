Title: Soviet Union and Vietnam Establish Diplomatic Relations; Vyshinsky Informs Vietnamese Foreign Minister Hoang Minh Giam
Author:
Date: 1950-02-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 31st: TASS Moscow, January 31st: The Soviet Union and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam have established diplomatic relations.

On January 19th, Mr. Do Khe (transliteration), the representative of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Bangkok, acting on the instructions of his government, presented to Mr. Nemchina, the Soviet Minister to Thailand, the declaration of President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the governments of all countries of the world on January 14, 1950, regarding the establishment of diplomatic relations.

The Soviet government replied to this declaration on January 30th, informing the Vietnamese government that it agreed to establish diplomatic relations with the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and to exchange ministers.

The text of the Soviet government's reply is as follows:

To Mr. Hoang Minh Giam, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam:

The Government of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics has received President Ho Chi Minh's declaration of January 14th to all governments, proposing the establishment of diplomatic relations.

Having considered the proposal of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and taking into account that the Democratic Republic of Vietnam represents the overwhelming majority of the Vietnamese people, the Soviet Government has decided to establish diplomatic relations between the Soviet Union and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and to exchange ministers.

I am instructed by the Government of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics to notify you as above.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Vyshinsky, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　January 30, 1950

### 苏越建立外交关系  维辛斯基通知越外长黄明鉴

1950-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏越建立外交关系
    维辛斯基通知越外长黄明鉴
    【新华社北京三十一日电】塔斯社莫斯科三十一日讯：苏联与越南民主共和国建立外交关系。
    一月十九日，越南民主共和国驻曼谷代表杜凯（译音），奉本国政府命令，向苏联驻泰国公使涅姆契纳提出越南民主共和国主席胡志明一九五○年一月十四日致世界各国政府关于建立外交关系的声明。
    苏联政府在一月三十日答复这一声明，苏联政府通知越南政府，它同意和越南民主共和国建立外交关系，并互派公使。
    苏联政府的复文如下：
    越南民主共和国外交部部长黄明鉴先生：
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府业已收到胡志明主席一月十四日向各国政府建议建立外交关系的声明。
    苏联政府在考虑了越南民主共和国政府的建议之后，考虑到越南民主共和国代表越南人民的压倒多数，决定建立苏联和越南民主共和国间的外交关系，并互派公使。
    奉苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府训令通知如上。
                    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟外交部长维辛斯基
                                  一九五○年一月三十日
