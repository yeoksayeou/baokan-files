Title: Beijing Shop Employees Continue to Celebrate Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1950-02-21
Page: 4

　　[Beijing News] Shop employees from twelve industries in Beijing continued to hold a celebration rally yesterday (the 20th) at the Zhongshan Park Concert Hall to celebrate the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance and the Spring Festival. Zhang Chunjiu, director of the Shop Employees Work Committee of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, stated in his speech that the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance not only consolidated the fruits of the Chinese people's victory but also guaranteed world peace. In addition, he conveyed the spirit and resolutions of the First Beijing Municipal Workers' Congress, calling on all shop employees in the city to implement the resolutions of the congress. Immediately afterward, recreational activities began. The "Beating the City God," performed by shop employees in the catering and hotel industries, was particularly wonderful and received enthusiastic applause from the audience.

### 京市店员工人昨日继续联欢

1950-02-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　京市店员工人昨日继续联欢
    【北京讯】京市十二个行业的店员工人昨（二十）日在中山公园音乐堂继续举行庆祝中苏签订友好同盟互助条约及春节联欢大会。市总工会店员工作委员会主任张春久讲话时，首先说明了中苏两国签订友好同盟互助条约，不但巩固了中国人民胜利的果实，而且也保障了世界的和平。此外，他并传达了京市第一届工人代表大会的精神和决议，号召全市店员工人贯彻执行大会决议。随即开始游艺，其中饮食旅店业店员工人演出的“打城隍”非常精彩，受到观众热烈鼓掌。
