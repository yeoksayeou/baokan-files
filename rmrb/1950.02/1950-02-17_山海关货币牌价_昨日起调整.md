Title: Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Adjusted, Effective Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1950-02-17
Page: 4

   Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate
    Adjusted, Effective Yesterday
    [Shanhaiguan News] The Shanhaiguan Joint Office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank has adjusted the exchange rate, effective from the 16th, to be one Renminbi yuan equals ten Northeast yuan.

### 山海关货币牌价  昨日起调整

1950-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

   山海关货币牌价
    昨日起调整
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处自十六日起牌价改为人民币一元兑东北券十元。
