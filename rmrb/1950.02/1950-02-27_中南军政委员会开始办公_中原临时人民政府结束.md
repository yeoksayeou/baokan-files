Title: Central and South Military and Political Committee Begins Work; Central Plains Temporary People's Government Ends

Author:
Date: 1950-02-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, February 26th: The Central and South Military and Political Committee has begun work after its establishment on February 5th. The Central Plains Temporary People's Government has announced its end.

### 中南军政委员会开始办公  中原临时人民政府结束

1950-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　中南军政委员会开始办公
    中原临时人民政府结束
    【新华社汉口二十六日电】中南军政委员会于二月五日成立后已开始办公。中原临时人民政府已宣告结束。
