Title: Youth Art Theater to Perform for Citizens Starting Today
Author:
Date: 1950-02-27
Page: 4

　　Youth Art Theater to Perform for Citizens Starting Today
　　[Beijing News] The Youth Art Theater, originally scheduled to perform songs and dances for citizens in Tiananmen Square on the 25th, has been rescheduled due to wind. It will now perform at Dongdan Square and Tiananmen Square, starting at 9:00 AM this morning (the 27th).

### 青年艺术剧院今起表演招待市民

1950-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　青年艺术剧院今起表演招待市民
    【北京讯】青年艺术剧院原定二十五日在天安门招待市民表演歌舞，因风改于今（二十七）日上午九时起先后在东单广场和天安门两地表演。
