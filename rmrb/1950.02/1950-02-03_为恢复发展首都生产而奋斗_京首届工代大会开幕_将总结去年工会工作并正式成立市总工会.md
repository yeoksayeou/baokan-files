Author:
Date: 1950-02-03
Page: 4

　　Strive to Restore and Develop the Capital's Production
    Beijing's First Workers' Congress Opens
    Will Summarize Last Year's Trade Union Work and Officially Establish the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions
    [Beijing News] Beijing's first Workers' Congress grandly opened at 9:00 AM yesterday (the 2nd) in the Zhongshan Hall of Zhongshan Park. Li Lisan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and others attended and delivered speeches. The opening ceremony was held in the morning, and in the afternoon Xiao Ming, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, reported a summary of the trade union's work over the past year.
    The venue was surrounded by congratulatory banners from various government agencies, organizations, factories, industry trade unions, and other city federations of trade unions. A total of 429 representatives attended yesterday's congress, including workers, technicians, factory directors, and professors. Manual laborers and intellectual laborers gathered together, symbolizing the great unity of the city's working class in striving to develop production. After the congress opened, Peng Zhen, Xiao Ming, Liu Laifu, Xu Liqun, and others, totaling twenty-nine people, were elected as the presidium. Xiao Ming, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, explained the significance of the meeting, stating that the central task of the congress was to summarize the trade union's work in the past year, adopt the organizational charter of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, establish the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, determine the central tasks of the trade union work in the future, and strive to further restore and develop the capital's production.

### 为恢复发展首都生产而奋斗  京首届工代大会开幕  将总结去年工会工作并正式成立市总工会

1950-02-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　为恢复发展首都生产而奋斗
    京首届工代大会开幕
    将总结去年工会工作并正式成立市总工会
    【北京讯】北京市首届工人代表大会，昨（二）日上午九时，在中山公园中山堂隆重开幕。中华全国总工会副主席李立三、北京市人民政府副市长张友渔等，都到会讲话。大会上午举行开幕式，下午由北京市总工会筹委会主任萧明报告一年来工会工作总结。
    会场四周挂满了各机关团体，各工厂、各行业工会及其他城市总工会所送的贺幛。昨日大会共出席代表四百二十九人，其中有工人、有技师、有厂长，也有教授，体力劳动者和脑力劳动者聚集一堂，象征着全市工人阶级为发展生产而努力的大团结。大会开幕后，选出彭真、萧明、刘莱夫、许立群等二十九人为主席团。市总工会筹委会主任萧明报告开会意义时，说明大会的中心任务，是总结过去一年来的工会工作，通过市总工会组织章程，产生市总工会，决定今后工会工作的中心任务，为进一步恢复与发展首都生产而奋斗。
