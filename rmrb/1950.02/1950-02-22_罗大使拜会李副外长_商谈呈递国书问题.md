Title: Romanian Ambassador Visits Vice Foreign Minister Li to Discuss Presenting Credentials

Author: 
Date: 1950-02-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 21st. Romanian People's Republic's first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Mr. Rudenko, accompanied by Counselor Silard, Secretary Meng Tiannu, and Tao Tuolan, visited Vice Foreign Minister Li Kenong at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 4:00 PM today to discuss matters related to the presentation of credentials. Also present were Deputy Director of the General Office Yan Baohang, Director of the Protocol Department Wang Zhuoru, and Deputy Director Shen Ping, among others.

### 罗大使拜会李副外长  商谈呈递国书问题

1950-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　罗大使拜会李副外长
    商谈呈递国书问题
    【新华社北京二十一日电】罗马尼亚人民共和国驻我国首任特命全权大使鲁登科偕参事席拉德、秘书孟天努及陶托兰，于今日下午四时赴外交部拜会李克农副部长，就呈递国书问题有所商谈。在座者尚有办公厅副主任阎宝航，交际处长王倬如，副处长沈平等。
