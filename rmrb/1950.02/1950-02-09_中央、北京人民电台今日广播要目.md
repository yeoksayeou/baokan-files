Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
Author:
Date: 1950-02-09
Page: 4

　　Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
　　[This newspaper reports] The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast to overseas Chinese on the issue of overseas remittances today (9th) from 19:45 to 20:00. Women's time at 17:30: Labor hero Li Fenglian's "I Saw Soviet Women Actively Participating in Labor Production." Electrics knowledge lecture 14 at 18:30: "Magnets and Terrestrial Magnetism." First art and literature program at 18:00: Lotus Ballad "Blood Relatives" second section. Second art and literature program at 21:00: Recordings of piano, violin, and songs from the Soviet delegation's art performance team.
　　[Also reported] Beijing People's Radio workers' program at 12:30: (1) Mao Shuduo from Mentougou Coal Mine speaks on "Everyone Work Harder, the New China is Ours," (2) Li Zhicheng from the Stevedores' Union speaks on "Support the Government, Develop Production, the More Trams and Cars the Better," (3) Huang Linmao from Ci Xing Iron Factory speaks on "Welfare is Created by Us Workers Ourselves." Learning discussion at 15:00: Answering the question of whether there will still be class struggle in communist society? Will contradictions still arise between productive forces and relations of production? If not, how will future society progress? Children's program at 16:30: Continuing to teach children to sing "Learning Victory Song." New song practice at 17:00: Teaching to sing the Northeast folk song "Busy with Production." Municipal Voice at 17:45: ① Introducing the "Appraisal Committee" - business report of the Beijing People's Court, ② Notice from the Tax Bureau regarding the vehicle usage license tax for the first half of 1950. Listener service at 18:45: ① Answering the suggestion that the station establish a "Civic Political Common Sense Lecture Series," ② Answering whether recordings are the same as records, and why they can be continuous? ③ Brief introduction to the Northeast People's Government Department of Commerce's hiring of professors, technicians, and enrollment of students.

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-02-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（九）日十九点四十五分到二十点关于侨汇问题向海外侨胞广播。十七点三十分妇女时间：劳动英雄李凤莲写的《我看到了苏联妇女积极参加劳动生产》。十八点三十分电学常识第十四讲：“磁石和地磁”。十八点第一次文艺节目：莲花落“亲骨肉”第二段。二十一点第二次文艺节目：苏联代表团艺术演出队钢琴、提琴和歌曲的录音。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工节目：（一）门头沟煤矿毛树多讲“大家努力加油干，新中国是我们的”，（二）搬运工会李志诚讲“拥护政府，发展生产，电车汽车越多越好”，（三）慈型铁工厂黄林茂讲“福利是咱们工人自己创造的”。十五点学习漫谈：解答共产主义社会是否还有阶级斗争？生产力和生产关系是否还会发生矛盾？如果没有的话，将来的社会怎样进步呢？十六点三十分儿童节目：继续教小朋友们唱“学习胜利歌”。十七点新歌练习：教唱东北民歌“生产忙”。十七点四十五分市政之声：①介绍“鉴定委员会”——北京市人民法院业务报导，②税务局关于一九五○年上半年度车辆使用牌照税的通知。十八点四十五分听众服务：①回答建议该台成立“市民政治常识讲座”的问题，②回答录音是不是和唱片一样，为什么能连续不断呢？③东北人民政府商业部聘请教授、技术人员和招收学员的简章。
