Title: People's Education Centers in Beijing to Hold Spring Festival Cultural Activities
Author: Jiao Changshan
Date: 1950-02-14
Page: 4

　　People's Education Centers in Beijing
    Spring Festival Cultural Activities

    [This newspaper reports] During the Spring Festival, People's Education Centers in Beijing will hold New Year picture and photograph exhibitions, as well as cultural and recreational activities. The photograph exhibition will include introductions to the lives of Soviet people, student movements, the eight-year War of Resistance Against Japan, and the three-year War of Liberation. In addition to exhibitions held within the centers, there will also be traveling exhibitions. The People's Education Centers also plan to hold worker, shop employee, and citizen film rallies on the 20th, 21st, and 22nd, screening the Soviet film "May Day Parade." On the 22nd, 23rd, and 24th, the Qianmen Arrow Tower People's Education Center will hold entertainment evenings, performing operas such as "The Female状元 (Top Scholar)" and "Husband and Wife Buying Public Bonds." Starting from the 25th, the Liulichang Fire God Temple People's Education Center will have the Normal University perform the opera "Wang Gui and Li Xiangxiang" for three consecutive days.
                                               (Jiao Changshan)

### 京各人民教育馆  春节开展文娱活动
焦长山
1950-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　京各人民教育馆
    春节开展文娱活动
    【本报讯】京市各人民教育馆，春节将举办新年画及照片展览与文娱活动。照片展览中包括苏联人民生活介绍、学生运动、八年抗战及三年解放战争等多种。除在各该馆内举行展览外，并做外出巡回展览。各民教馆并计划于二十、二十一、二十二日分别召开工人店员市民电影大会，放映苏联影片《五一大检阅》。二十二、二十三、二十四三日，前门箭楼民教馆举行娱乐晚会，演出《女状元》、《夫妻买公债》等歌剧。二十五日起，琉璃厂火神庙民教馆，由师大连演三天《王贵与李香香》歌剧。
                                               （焦长山）
