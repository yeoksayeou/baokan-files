Title: Executive Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Decides to Launch Activities to Celebrate the Sino-Soviet Treaty; Extensive Publicity Combined with Spring Festival Cultural and Recreational Activities
Author:
Date: 1950-02-16
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 15th—After hearing the broadcast news this morning about the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, the Executive Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association immediately convened its fourth meeting to discuss the launch of publicity and celebration activities. Those present included Li Dequan, Wang Kunlun, Yan Baohang, Shen Zhiyuan, Xiao San, Hong Shen, Huan Xiang, Zhang Zhongshi, Cao Yu, Fan Hong, Ding Xiu, Wu Qingyou, Fu Xuewen, Weng Dujian, Qian Sanqiang, Ding Dian, Jin Shan, and over thirty others, chaired by Qian Junrui. The Executive Council also invited representatives from the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, the News Administration, the Publishing Administration, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and the Beijing Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. The meeting decided to establish a "Working Committee for Celebrating the Signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance" to unify efforts in carrying out the work. The meeting also decided to suggest that general branches, branches, and sub-branches in various regions cooperate with and assist local organs, groups, schools, and factories to universally and deeply conduct celebration and publicity work, actively develop friendship association members, and establish and consolidate the regular work of the friendship association. In the afternoon, the celebration working committee was formally established and held a meeting, deciding to comprehensively hold celebration and publicity activities in Beijing from February 17th to 23rd, combining them with Spring Festival cultural and recreational activities. It will conduct extensive publicity among the masses of all sectors through lectures, symposiums, films and dramas, folk arts, street publicity, radio broadcasts, publication of popular books and periodicals, articles, slide shows, slogans, New Year pictures, and other means. The working committee also suggested that various literary and art groups take this celebration and publicity as the main content of the Spring Festival cultural and recreational activities, and suggested that all organs, groups, and troops hold celebration meetings and organize the study of the content of the treaty and the Xinhua News Agency editorial.
    In addition: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association also plans to invite Soviet experts and members of the council to give lectures in various places. The fourth issue of "Sino-Soviet Friendship," originally scheduled to be published today, has decided to rush-print a special supplement to show its celebration.
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 15th—The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association today sent a telegram to the general branches and branches of the association throughout the country, suggesting that they immediately launch celebration and publicity activities for the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, and instructed on matters needing attention. (1) According to the content of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance and the two agreements, and the spirit of the Xinhua News Agency editorial, through the friendship association organizations at all levels and in connection with the specific work tasks and ideological situation at that time in the local area, use various forms to universally and deeply carry out publicity and education work on Sino-Soviet friendship. (2) Through this activity, actively develop members and consolidate and expand the organization. (3) On the basis of this activity, establish and strengthen the regular work of Sino-Soviet friendship.

### 中苏友协总会干事会讨论决定  开展庆祝中苏新约活动  结合春节文化娱乐活动进行广泛宣传

1950-02-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　中苏友协总会干事会讨论决定  开展庆祝中苏新约活动
    结合春节文化娱乐活动进行广泛宣传
    【新华社北京十五日电】中苏友好协会总会于今晨听到中苏友好同盟互助条约签订的广播消息后，当即召开第四次干事会，讨论展开宣传庆祝活动事项。出席者李德全、王昆仑、阎宝航、沈志远、萧三、洪深、宦乡、张仲实、曹禺、樊弘、丁秀、吴清友、傅学文、翁独健、钱三强、丁玷、金山等三十余人，由钱俊瑞主席。干事会并邀请中央人民政府文化部、新闻总署、出版总署及中华全国总工会、新民主主义青年团中央委员会、全国民主妇女联合会、全国文学艺术界联合会、北京市中苏友好协会分会等代表参加。会议决定成立“庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订工作委员会”，以统一力量进行工作。会议并决定建议各地总分会、分会及支会，配合和帮助各地机关、团体、学校、工厂，普遍深入地进行庆祝宣传工作，并积极发展友协会员，建立与巩固友协的经常工作。下午，庆祝工作委员会正式成立并举行会议，决定自二月十七日至二十三日，结合春节文娱活动，在北京全面举行庆祝宣传活动，通过讲演会、座谈会、电影戏剧、民间艺曲、街头宣传、电台广播、出版通俗书刊、发表文章、幻灯、标语、年画等方式，深入各界群众中去，进行广泛宣传。工作委员会又建议各文艺工作团体在春节文娱活动中以此项庆祝宣传为主要内容，建议各机关、团体、部队一律分别召开庆祝会并组织对条约内容及新华社社论的学习。
    又讯：中苏友好协会总会并拟邀请苏联专家及本会理事干事，分赴各处演讲。该会原定今日出版之《中苏友好》第四期决定赶印增刊，以示庆祝。
    【新华社北京十五日电】中苏友好协会总会今日致电该会全国各地总分会、分会，建议立即展开中苏友好同盟互助条约的庆祝宣传活动，并指示注意事项。（一）根据中苏友好同盟互助条约和两个协定的内容和新华社社论的精神，通过各级友协组织并联系当地当时的具体工作任务与思想情况，运用各种形式，普遍深入地展开中苏友好的宣传教育工作。（二）通过这一活动，积极发展会员，巩固与扩大组织。（三）以这一活动为基础，建立和加强中苏友好的经常工作。
