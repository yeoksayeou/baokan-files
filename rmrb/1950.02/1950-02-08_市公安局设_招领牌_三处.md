Title: Municipal Public Security Bureau Sets Up Three "Lost and Found Boards"
Author:
Date: 1950-02-08
Page: 4

　　Municipal Public Security Bureau
　　Sets Up Three "Lost and Found Boards"
　　[Beijing News] In order to facilitate the claiming of lost items by their owners, the Beijing Municipal People's Government Public Security Bureau has set up "Beijing Municipal People's Government Public Security Bureau Lost and Found Boards" in three locations within the city: "In front of the Qianmen Arrow Tower," "In front of the No. 22 gate on the South Main Street of Dongsi Arch," and "West Exit of the Xisi Arch T-Junction." In the future, found items, livestock, and lost children will be announced on the boards for owners to claim.

### 市公安局设“招领牌”三处

1950-02-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　市公安局
    设“招领牌”三处
    【北京讯】北京市人民政府公安局为了使拾得之物品等便于失主认领，特在本市“前门箭楼前”、“东四牌楼南大街二十二号门前”、“西四牌楼丁字街西口”等三处设置“北京市人民政府公安局招领牌”，今后随时将捡拾的物品、牲畜和迷路小孩在牌上公告招领。
