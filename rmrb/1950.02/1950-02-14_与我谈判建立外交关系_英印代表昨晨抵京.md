Title: British and Indian Representatives Arrive in Beijing Yesterday Morning for Negotiations on Establishing Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1950-02-14
Page: 1

　　Negotiations to Establish Diplomatic Relations with Us
    British and Indian Representatives Arrive in Beijing Yesterday Morning
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th] In order to conduct negotiations with the Central People's Government of our country on the issue of establishing diplomatic relations, the British government has dispatched Mr. Hutchinson, designated as Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, as the negotiation representative, and the Indian government has dispatched Mr. Panikkar, designated as Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, as the negotiation representative. Mr. Hutchinson and Mr. Panikkar arrived in Beijing at 9:40 this morning, and Han Xu, section chief of the Protocol Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, went to the station to greet them.

### 与我谈判建立外交关系  英印代表昨晨抵京
新华社
1950-02-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　与我谈判建立外交关系
    英印代表昨晨抵京
    【新华社北京十三日电】为与我国中央人民政府就建立外交关系问题进行谈判，英国政府已派遣被指定为临时代办的胡阶森先生为谈判代表，印度政府已派遣被指定为临时代办的雁谒森先生为谈判代表。胡阶森及雁谒森两先生于今晨九时四十分抵京，我外交部派交际处科长韩叙赴站迎接。
