Title: Joint Bookstore of Beijing Sends Russian-Chinese Four-Purpose Dictionary
Author:
Date: 1950-02-04
Page: 3

　　Joint Bookstore of Beijing
    Ships Russian-Chinese Four-Purpose Dictionary
    [This newspaper] The Joint Bookstore of Life, Reading, and New Knowledge in Beijing recently shipped a large quantity of the second edition of the Russian-Chinese Four-Purpose Dictionary, published by the Lüda Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. In addition to general explanations of new words, the dictionary contains detailed annotations on grammar, idioms, examples, and synonyms. (Joint Bookstore News Team)

### 京市三联书店运到俄华四用辞典

1950-02-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　京市三联书店
    运到俄华四用辞典
    【本报讯】京市生活、读书、新知三联书店，日前大批运到旅大中苏友好协会出版的第二版俄华四用辞典。辞典中对生字除一般解释外，文法、成语、举例和同义方面都有详细注释。          （三联通讯组）
