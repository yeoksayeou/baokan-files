Title: Soviet Scientists Actively Participate in Election Campaigns in Various Constituencies
Author:
Date: 1950-02-24
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 23rd (TASS Moscow Dispatch): Soviet scientists are actively participating in the work of the elections to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR. Many scientists have served as members of the central constituency and various constituency election committees. In the Lenin Constituency of Moscow, where various institutes of the Soviet Academy of Sciences are concentrated, more than twenty constituency election committees are led by prominent scientists, Doctors of Science, and Stalin Prize laureates. In Leningrad, scientists lead the district election headquarters of Ostrov District, where most of the city's scientific research institutions are located. Workers from the Academies of Sciences of Ukraine, Belarus, Georgia, Armenia, Azerbaijan, and the Soviet Baltic Republics are also actively participating in the work of the election campaign.
　　The institutes are vacating their best buildings to serve as election offices. Voters gather there every evening to listen to lectures, watch movies, or visit exhibitions on the achievements of Soviet science and technology. In the election offices, scientists hold lectures on various aspects of knowledge, explaining the Soviet election system and the Stalin Constitution. Academicians Nesmeyanov, Scriabin, and Oparin of the Academy of Sciences have given lectures on new research work in chemistry, geology, and biochemistry. Academician Palladin, President of the Academy of Sciences of the Ukrainian Republic, delivered a report at a mass meeting of voters in Kyiv on the Congress of Supporters of Peace in the Netherlands, which he had attended.
　　The Soviet Academy of Sciences has published a set of popular pamphlets on the election campaign.

### 苏科学家在各选区积极参加选举运动

1950-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏科学家在各选区积极参加选举运动
    【新华社北京二十三日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联科学家积极参加苏联最高苏维埃选举的工作。许多科学家担任了中央选区和各选区选举委员会的委员。在苏联科学院各研究所的汇集地莫斯科列宁选区，有二十多个选区选举委员会是由著名的科学家、科学博士和斯大林奖金得奖人领导的。在列宁格勒，科学家领导着该城大多数科学研究机关所在地——奥斯特罗夫区的区选举总部。乌克兰、白俄罗斯、乔治亚、阿美尼亚、阿塞拜疆和苏维埃波罗的海各共和国科学院的工作者们，也积极参加了选举运动的工作。
    研究所把最好的房屋腾出来作选举办公室。选民每晚聚集在那里听演讲、看电影或参观关于苏联科学与技术的成就的展览。在选举办公处，科学家们举办各方面知识的讲演会，讲解苏维埃选举制度和斯大林宪法。科学院院士涅斯米扬诺夫、斯克利雅宾和奥巴林，曾作了关于化学、地质学和生物化学的新研究工作的演讲。乌克兰共和国科学院院长巴拉丁，在基辅选民群众大会上发表了关于他曾经列席的荷兰和平拥护者大会的报告。
    苏联科学院已出版一套关于选举运动的通俗小册子。
