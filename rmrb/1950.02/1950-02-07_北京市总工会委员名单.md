　　List of Members of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions
    [Beijing News] The first Beijing Municipal Workers' Congress yesterday (the 6th) elected 63 members and 10 alternate members of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, as well as 9 members of the Finance Supervision Committee and 2 alternate members of the Finance Supervision Committee. The list is as follows:
