Title: Xia Fa Langke's Biography
Author:
Date: 1950-02-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 15th — Biography of Mr. Emanuel Xia Fa Langke, the first Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Hungary to our country:

　　Emanuel Xia Fa Langke was born in Moka Qimei City, Hungary in 1890. After graduating from elementary school, he became an apprentice in a flour factory. In 1908, he joined the Hungarian Social Democratic Party. In 1914, he served in the Austro-Hungarian army for four years. He participated in the 1918-1919 revolution. At that time, he served as the production supervisor of Kecskemét City and the head of the local Economic Committee.

　　In 1919, he joined the Communist Party. After the failure of the revolution, he fled to Czechoslovakia and actively participated in the local peasant movement. From 1921 to 1925, he served as the district secretary of the Red Trade Union in Uzhgorod City. In 1924, he was elected as a member of the Czech Communist Party. From 1925, he served as the district secretary of the Czech Communist Party in various regions. He was arrested several times for participating in political activities. In 1930, he was forced to live in exile in the Soviet Union. After graduating from the "Red Professor Academy," he continued to teach in middle schools and universities for many years. He returned to Hungary in 1945. In 1947, he served as the governor of Val County. In December of the same year, he was appointed as the Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Hungary to Bulgaria until 1949.

### 夏法朗柯简历

1950-02-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　夏法朗柯简历
    【新华社北京十五日电】匈牙利人民共和国首任驻我国特命全权公使夏法朗柯简历：
    爱玛奴耶利·夏法朗柯一八九○年生于匈牙利摩卡契梅城，小学毕业后，即入面粉工厂当学徒，于一九○八年加入匈牙利社会民主党，一九一四在奥匈军队中服务四年。曾参加一九一八——一九一九年革命。当时任克契克美特城生产监理及当地经济委员会的负责人。
    一九一九年参加共产党，革命失败后，逃往捷克斯拉夫，积极参加当地农民运动。一九二一年至二五年任乌什果罗德城红色职工会区书记。一九二四年被选为捷克共产党议员。一九二五年任捷共各地的区书记，因参加政治活动，曾数次被捕，至一九三○年被迫侨居苏联，读完“红色教授学院”后继在中学、大学执教多年。至一九四五年返匈。一九四七年任瓦尔省省长，同年十二月被任匈牙利人民共和国驻保加利亚特命全权公使至一九四九年。
