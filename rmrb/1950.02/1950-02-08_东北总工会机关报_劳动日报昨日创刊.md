Title: Northeast Federation of Trade Unions' Newspaper, Labor Daily, Launched Yesterday
Author: 
Date: 1950-02-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 7th. The Northeast Federation of Trade Unions' newspaper, Labor Daily, was launched in Shenyang today. The inaugural issue's editorial (acting as the inaugural message), titled "Commemorating 'February 7th' and the Tasks of the Northeast Working Class," called on trade unions in Northeast China to further implement the policy of being oriented towards production, mobilize workers to create all kinds of new records, strengthen grassroots trade union work, and strengthen trade unions' cultural and educational work. The newspaper is currently published every other day, in a four-page, broadsheet format. In the future, it will regularly report on Northeast China's industrial production, trade union work, and workers' lives.

### 东北总工会机关报  劳动日报昨日创刊

1950-02-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　东北总工会机关报
    劳动日报昨日创刊
    【新华社沈阳七日电】东北总工会机关报——劳动日报已于今日在沈阳创刊。该报创刊号在题为“纪念‘二七’与东北工人阶级的任务”的社论（代发刊词）中，号召东北各地工会进一步贯彻面向生产的方针，动员职工创造各种各样的新纪录；加强基层工会工作和加强工会的文教工作。该报现暂出隔日刊，四开四版。今后将经常报道东北的工业生产、工会工作和工人生活情况。
