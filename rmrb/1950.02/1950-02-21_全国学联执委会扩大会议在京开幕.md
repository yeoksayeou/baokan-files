Title: National Student Federation Executive Committee Enlarged Meeting Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-02-21
Page: 1

　　National Student Federation Executive Committee Enlarged Meeting Opens in Beijing
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th—The Second Enlarged Meeting of the Fourteenth Executive Committee of the All-China Students' Federation opened in Beijing at 9:00 AM today. Fifty-nine executive committee members from regions including the Northeast, Northwest, North China, East China, Central South, and major cities such as Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Nanjing, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Shenyang, and Xi'an attended the meeting. Executive committee members from the Southwest provinces and Fujian province are en route. Additionally, thirty-nine student work cadres from the Communist Youth League committees of various administrative regions, provinces, and major cities were invited as observers. Qian Junrui, Vice Minister of Education of the Central People's Government, and Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, delivered reports at today's meeting. In his report, Qian Junrui analyzed the basic situation of current Chinese education and pointed out the future direction and tasks of New Democratic education. He expressed hope that the Executive Committee of the Student Federation would call on students across the country to assist the People's Government in completing this glorious and great task of building New Democratic education. Feng Wenbin reported on the current international and domestic situation, the tasks and work of the Youth League and student unions in schools, and offered suggestions on issues such as the organizational work methods of student unions. The meeting also invited Xiao Hua, head of the China Democratic Youth Delegation to Foreign Countries, to report on his participation in the World Youth and Student Festival and the Second Congress of the World Federation of Democratic Youth.

### 全国学联执委会扩大会议在京开幕

1950-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国学联执委会扩大会议在京开幕
    【新华社北京二十日电】中华全国学生联合会第十四届第二次执行委员会扩大会议今日上午九时在京召开。到会的有东北、西北、华北、华东、中南等地区及北京、天津、上海、南京、武汉、广州、沈阳、西安等大城市的执委共五十九人，西南各省及福建省的执委正在途中。此外并有各行政区，各省及大城市的青年团工委学生工作干部三十九人受邀为列席代表。中央人民政府教育部副部长钱俊瑞，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬在今日会上作了报告。钱俊瑞在报告中分析了目前中国教育的基本情况，并指出今后新民主主义教育的方针和任务。他希望学联执委会号召全国学生来帮助人民政府完成这个光荣的伟大的新民主主义教育建设的任务。冯文彬报告了目前国际国内的形势，学校中的青年团和学生会的任务与工作，并对学生会的组织工作方式等问题，提出了意见。会议并请中国民主青年出国代表团团长萧华报告了参加世界青年与学生联欢大会，及世界民主青年联盟第二次代表大会的经过。
