Title: Nationwide Celebration of the Sino-Soviet Treaty Unfolds into In-Depth Study Movement
Author:
Date: 1950-02-23
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 22nd: A nationwide movement to promote and study the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance is underway. In Liaoxi Province, all cities and counties have designated February 15th to March 15th as the Sino-Soviet Friendship Propaganda Month. They will use various forms to explain to the people the contents and significance of the Sino-Soviet New Treaty, New Agreement, and the Xinhua News Agency editorial "A New Era of Sino-Soviet Friendship and Cooperation". The Propaganda Department of the Liaoxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party has instructed Party committees and propaganda and education departments at all levels to conscientiously organize cadres to study the above documents and to jointly formulate specific propaganda plans with various mass organizations to lead this movement in a step-by-step and organized manner. The Liaoxi branches of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will, in accordance with the instructions of the Provincial Friendship Association, mobilize members to actively participate in this movement and extensively recruit new members from the movement. Jinzhou City established a preparatory committee for the Sino-Soviet Friendship Propaganda Month on the 19th, and employees of the Railway Administration have begun to study the treaty and other documents. The Zhejiang Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association convened a meeting of the heads of Hangzhou City branches and sub-branches on the 17th and decided that each branch and sub-branch should first organize members to study and then carry out propaganda among the people. After spending the Spring Festival celebrating the Sino-Soviet New Treaty, various agencies and factories in Qingdao entered into the study of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance and related documents. The study committee organized by democratic parties and democratic figures in Guangzhou has elected Li Zhangda as the convener, and workers in many public enterprises in the city have also decided to make the Sino-Soviet New Treaty the focus of study this month. The Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Administrative Office is preparing to launch a widespread study of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance and the agreement in secondary schools. The Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Cultural Troupe has adapted the content of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance into a Yangko dance performance.

### 全国欢庆中苏缔约展开深入学习运动

1950-02-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　全国欢庆中苏缔约展开深入学习运动
    【新华社北京二十二日电】全国各地正展开深入宣传和学习中苏友好同盟互助条约的运动。辽西省各市、县规定从二月十五日到三月十五日为中苏友好宣传运动月，通过各种形式向人民讲解中苏新条约、新协定和新华社社论“中苏友好合作的新时代”等文件的内容和意义。中共辽西省委员会宣传部已指示所属各级党委、宣教部门，认真组织干部学习上述文件，并与各群众团体共同拟出具体宣传计划，有步骤、有组织地领导这一运动。该省各地中苏友好协会将根据省友好协会的指示，发动会员积极参加这个运动，并从运动中大量发展会员。锦州市已于十九日成立中苏友好宣传运动月筹委会，铁路管理局职工并已开始学习条约等文件。中苏友好协会浙江分会于十七日召开杭州市支会、支分会负责人会议，决定各支会、支分会先组织会员学习，然后再向人民群众进行宣传。青岛各机关、工厂在欢庆中苏新约中渡过春节后，即进入对中苏友好同盟互助条约以及有关文件的学习。在广州的各民主党派和民主人士组织的学习委员会已选出李章达为召集人，该市许多公营企业的工人也决定将中苏新条约作为本月学习中心。陕北行署准备发动中等学校普遍学习中苏友好同盟互助条约和协定。陕北文工团已将中苏友好同盟互助条约的内容编成秧歌戏出演。
