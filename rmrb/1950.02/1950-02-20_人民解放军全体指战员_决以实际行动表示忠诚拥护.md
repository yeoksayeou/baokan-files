Title: All Commanders and Fighters of the People's Liberation Army Determined to Show Loyal Support with Practical Actions
Author:
Date: 1950-02-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 17th: The news of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance caused great jubilation in all units of the People's Liberation Army. All commanders and fighters unanimously expressed their determination to support this treaty with various practical actions. The 19th Corps of the First Field Army and the directly subordinate units of the Shaanxi Military Region held a cadre meeting on the afternoon of the same day (February 15th), where the political commissar of the corps reported on the significance of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. Wang Hongxi, a combat hero of the corps who had been wounded 35 times and the political instructor of the first battalion of a certain regiment, said: "The Soviet Union's friendship with the Chinese people has been proven with ironclad evidence through the signing of this treaty. We must quickly and well complete the task assigned to us by our superiors of constructing the Tianshui-Baoji section of the railway, and respond to the assistance of our Soviet allies with practical actions." The Political Department of the corps and military region issued instructions to all subordinate armies and military sub-districts on February 15th to celebrate the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. It stipulates that the period from the 17th to the 23rd should be designated as a celebration and propaganda week, combined with the propaganda for the Soviet Army's Founding Day on February 23rd.

### 人民解放军全体指战员  决以实际行动表示忠诚拥护
新华社
1950-02-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　人民解放军全体指战员
    决以实际行动表示忠诚拥护
    【新华社北京十七日电】中苏签订友好同盟互助条约的消息传到人民解放军各部队后，立刻引起了巨大的欢腾。全体指战员一致表示要以各种实际行动来拥护这一条约。第一野战军十九兵团暨陕西军区直属队，在当天（十五日）下午就召开了干部大会，由兵团政治委员报告中苏友好同盟互助条约的意义。负过三十五次伤的兵团战斗英雄、某团一营教导员王鸿喜说：“苏联对中国人民的友谊，从这个条约的签订，得到了钢铁般的证明。我们要把上级交给我们修建天水、宝鸡段铁路的任务，很快很好地完成，用实际行动来回答苏联友邦的援助。”该兵团暨军区政治部，于十五日向所属各军、各军分区发出庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约的指示。规定从十七日起到二十三日止为庆祝宣传周，和二月二十三日的苏军建军节的宣传结合起来。
