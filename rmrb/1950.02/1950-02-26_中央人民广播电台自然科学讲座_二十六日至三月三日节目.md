Title: Natural Science Lectures of the Central People's Broadcasting Station, Programs from February 26th to March 3rd
Author:
Date: 1950-02-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 25th: The natural science lecture program of the Central People's Broadcasting Station from February 26th to March 3rd is as follows:

    February 26th: Soviet neurosurgery expert Batonko. February 27th: Electric Motors (Basic Knowledge of Electricity, Lecture 17) (Qin Zhuhao). February 28th: Electromagnetic Induction and Transformers (Basic Knowledge of Electricity, Lecture 18) (Guo Rusong).

    March 1st: Generators (Basic Knowledge of Electricity, Lecture 19) (Qin Zhuhao). March 2nd: Two different lives and fates of scientists before and after the Soviet October Revolution (1). March 3rd: Same as above (2).

    All programs will be broadcast at 18:30 every evening.

### 中央人民广播电台自然科学讲座  二十六日至三月三日节目

1950-02-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央人民广播电台自然科学讲座
    二十六日至三月三日节目
    【新华社北京二十五日电】中央人民广播电台从本月二十六日到下月三日自然科学讲座节目如下：
    二月二十六日苏联神经外科专家巴顿柯，二十七日电动机（电学常识第十七讲）（秦祝浩），二十八日电磁感应和变压器（电学常识第十八讲）（郭汝嵩）
    三月一日发电机（电学常识第十九讲）（秦祝浩），二日苏联十月革命前后科学家两种不同的生活和命运（一）三日同上（二）
    以上节目均在每晚十八点三十分播讲。
