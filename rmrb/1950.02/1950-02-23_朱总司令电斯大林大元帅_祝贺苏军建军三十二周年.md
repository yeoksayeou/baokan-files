Title: Commander-in-Chief Zhu Sends Telegram to Marshal Stalin Congratulating the Soviet Army on its 32nd Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1950-02-23
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 22nd: Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army has sent a telegram to Marshal Stalin and Marshal Vasilevsky, Minister of the Armed Forces of the Soviet Union, congratulating the Soviet Army on its 32nd anniversary. The telegram reads as follows:

　　To Marshal Stalin and Marshal Vasilevsky:

　　On the occasion of the 32nd anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army, I respectfully wish the Soviet Union, the bulwark of world peace and our great ally, and its heroic armed forces, unparalleled strength under the wise leadership of Marshal Stalin.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Zhu De
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　February 21, 1950

### 朱总司令电斯大林大元帅  祝贺苏军建军三十二周年

1950-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　朱总司令电斯大林大元帅  祝贺苏军建军三十二周年
    【新华社北京二十二日电】中国人民解放军朱德总司令顷致电斯大林大元帅及苏联武装部队部部长华西列夫斯基元帅，祝贺苏军建军三十二周年。电文如下：斯大林大元帅和华西列夫斯基元帅：
    值此苏军建军三十二周年纪念之际，敬祝世界和平堡垒、我们的伟大盟邦苏联及其英勇的武装部队在斯大林大元帅英明领导下强大无比。
            朱德
    一九五○年二月二十一日
