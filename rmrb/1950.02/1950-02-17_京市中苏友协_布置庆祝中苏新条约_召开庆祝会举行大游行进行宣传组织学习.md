Title: Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Arranges Celebrations for the New Sino-Soviet Treaty: Convening Celebration Meetings, Holding Grand Parades, Conducting Propaganda, and Organizing Study

Author:
Date: 1950-02-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th—The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, in order to widely and deeply publicize and celebrate the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, convened a meeting of cadres from all factories, schools, troops, government organs, and district general branches and branches at the Association's Friendship Hall this morning. Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, first explained the significance of the treaty and agreements to everyone. Subsequently, Zeng Ping of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association branch reported on the celebration methods decided upon by the Association and Beijing's various organizations through consultation, as well as the current work of the general branches and branches: 1. Convene a mass celebration meeting on the 18th (the second day of the Lunar New Year), followed by a grand parade. 2. Each general branch and branch should prepare a celebration meeting as much as possible, and use various propaganda methods to publicize to the broad masses of citizens in the streets. 3. Each general branch and branch should organize members to carefully study and discuss the content of the new Sino-Soviet Treaty and Agreements, as well as the New China News Agency's editorial. 4. Improve the work of the Friendship Association, especially in factories, continue to develop organizations and recruit new members.

### 京市中苏友协  布置庆祝中苏新条约  召开庆祝会举行大游行进行宣传组织学习

1950-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　京市中苏友协  布置庆祝中苏新条约
    召开庆祝会举行大游行进行宣传组织学习
    【新华社北京十六日电】北京中苏友好协会为普遍深入地宣传和庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约的签订，今日上午在该会友谊厅召开全市各工厂、学校、部队、机关、行政区总支会、支会的干部会议。中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞首先给大家解释了条约和协定的意义。继由北京市中苏友好协会分会曾平报告该会和京市各团体协商所决定的庆祝办法以及各总支会、支会当前的工作为：一、十八日（旧历正月初二）召开群众庆祝大会，会后举行大游行。二、各总支会和支会尽量地筹备一个庆祝会，并用各种宣传方式到街头向广大市民宣传。三、各总支会、支会要组织会员好好学习讨论中苏新条约和协定的内容和新华社的社论。四、健全友协工作，特别是各工厂要继续发展组织，吸收新会员。
