Author:
Date: 1950-02-23
Page: 3

The Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Holds Photo Exhibition

[This newspaper dispatch] The photo exhibition held by the Beijing Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries is now open to the public. The main contents include: Lenin's life; Marshal Stalin; Soviet children's lives; the reception held by the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries for the delegation of Chinese art and cultural workers to meet with journalists; the meeting of the Executive Council of the Women's International Democratic Federation, etc. Exhibition hours: 11:00 AM to 7:00 PM daily. Closed on Saturdays. A reading room is also available. The exhibition address is at the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, 19 South River Embankment.

### 苏联对外文化协会举办图片展览

1950-02-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联对外文化协会举办图片展览
    【本报讯】北京苏联对外文化协会所举办的图片展览自即日起始。其主要内容有：列宁生平；斯大林大元帅；苏联的儿童生活；中国艺术文化工作者代表团在苏联对外文化协会招待记者座谈会；国际民主妇女联合会理事会会议等。展览时间：每日自上午十一时至下午七时。星期六休息。并附设有阅览室。展览地址在南河沿十九号苏联对外文化协会。
