Title: No Time
Author: Lugong
Date: 1950-02-13
Page: 6

　　No Time
　　Lugong
　　"Learn technology!" "Learn technology!"
　　I don't know how many times you've shouted it,
　　I ask you, how's your learning going?
　　You narrow your eyes and shut them tight:
　　"Ah, alas, I really don't have the time!"
　　The clock's hands, turning non-stop,
　　A day passes, then another day,
　　One month after another goes by,
　　Spring, summer, autumn, and winter chase after each other….
　　When you think to seize the time,
　　You've already fallen far behind;
　　At that time, you old revolutionary,
　　Your set of old experiences…
　　Will find you don't fit in anywhere.
　　Time is available every day,
　　I hope you wake up,
　　Throw away your baggage and move forward,
　　Even starting from scratch, there will be a day you understand,
　　Chairman Mao calls for learning economic work in three to five years.
　　Please regard it as a top priority.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Reprinted from "People's Railway")

### 没有时间
路工
1950-02-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　没有时间
    路工
    “学技术！”“学技术！”
    听你不知叫了多少遍，
    我问你学习怎么样？
    你眼一挤关起来：
    “啊哎，实在那有时间！”
    钟上针，不停转，
    过了一天又一天，
    一月一月过去了，
    春夏秋冬跟着撵……。
    等你想起抓时间，
    早就落在大后边；
    那时你老革命，
    你一套老经验……
    你会到处吃不开。
    时间天天有，
    希望你醒过来，
    扔了包袱往前走，
    从头学起也有会的一天，
    毛主席叫三五年学会经济工作。
    请你当头等任务看待。
                                  （转载自《人民铁道》）
