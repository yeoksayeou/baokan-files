Title: Tianjin Municipality Overfulfills Public Bond Sales Target, Totaling 7.6 Million, Exceeding by 310,000
Author:
Date: 1950-02-17
Page: 4

　　Tianjin Municipality Overfulfills Public Bond Sales Target
    Totaling 7.6 Million, Exceeding by 310,000
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 16th] Tianjin Municipality has overfulfilled its first phase public bond sales target of 7.3 million shares, making it the city with the largest allocated number among those that have already completed their tasks nationwide. As of the 15th, the total number of subscriptions and actual purchases from all sectors reached 7,600,922 shares, exceeding the original allocated task by more than 300,900 shares.

### 津市公债推销超额完成  总数达七百六十万超额卅一万

1950-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　津市公债推销超额完成
    总数达七百六十万超额卅一万
    【新华社天津十六日电】天津市已超额完成七百三十万分的第一期公债推销任务，为全国各城市已经完成任务者之中配销数字最大者。截至十五日止，各界认购和实购总数共达七百六十万零九千零二十二分，超过原配销任务三十万零九千余分。
