Title: Beijing People's Bank Overtime During Spring Festival to Sell Public Bonds
Author:
Date: 1950-02-16
Page: 4

[Report from This Newspaper]

　　Beijing People's Bank Overtime During Spring Festival to Sell Public Bonds
    [This Newspaper News] To facilitate people from all walks of life to purchase People's Victory Discount Public Bonds during the Spring Festival, the Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China has stipulated that its 13 departments and offices including the Municipal Business Department will work overtime from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM every day on the 18th, 19th, and 23rd to handle the sale of public bonds. The four suburban offices in Changxindian, Haidian, Fengtai, and Mentougou will all work overtime for one day on the 20th.

### 京人民银行春节加班售公债

1950-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　京人民银行春节加班售公债
    【本报讯】中国人民银行北京分行为便利各界于春节期间购买人民胜利折实公债，特规定该行市内营业部等十三个部处，于十八、十九、二十三天每天上午十时至下午三时加班办理代售公债事宜。郊区长辛店、海甸、丰台、门头沟四办事处，一律于二十日加班一天。
