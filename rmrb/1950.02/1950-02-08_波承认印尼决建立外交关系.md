Title: Poland Recognizes Indonesia and Decides to Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author: 
Date: 1950-02-08
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th: According to the Polish News Agency in Warsaw: The Secretary-General of the Polish Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Mr. Wyblawski, notified the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the United States of Indonesia on February 3rd, announcing the Polish government's decision to recognize the United States of Indonesia and establish diplomatic relations with it. The Polish Ministry of Foreign Affairs also notified the Dutch Legation in Warsaw of its decision to recognize the United States of Indonesia.

### 波承认印尼决建立外交关系

1950-02-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　波承认印尼
    决建立外交关系
    【新华社北京七日电】据波兰通讯社华沙讯：波兰外交部秘书长威勃洛夫斯基于二月三日照会印尼合众国外交部长，宣布波兰政府决定承认印度尼西亚合众国，并与之建立外交关系。波兰外交部并将承认印尼合众国的决定，通知了荷兰驻华沙公使馆。
