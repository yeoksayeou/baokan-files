Title: Vietnam-China Friendship Association Sends Telegram to Chairman Mao to Express Respect; Chairman Mao Replies with a Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1950-02-23
Page: 1

Column: Wishing for the Development of Sino-Vietnamese Friendship

　　Vietnam-China Friendship Association Sends Telegram to Chairman Mao to Express Respect
    Chairman Mao Replies with a Congratulatory Telegram
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 22nd] On the occasion of its founding, the Provisional Executive Committee of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam sent a telegram to Chairman Mao to express its respect. Chairman Mao has replied with a telegram congratulating the association on its establishment. The Vietnam-China Friendship Association's telegram and Chairman Mao's reply are as follows:

    (I) Telegram to Chairman Mao and the Central Government of the People's Republic of China:

    The Provisional Executive Committee of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association was established on February 6th of this year. We respectfully salute Chairman Mao and the Central Government of the People's Republic of China.

    Hu Songmao, Chairman of the Provisional Executive Committee of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association

    (II) Reply to Mr. Hu Songmao:

    I am pleased to hear of the establishment of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association and extend my congratulations. I wish for the development of the friendly relations between China and Vietnam.

          Mao Zedong
    February 21, 1950

### 越华友好协会电毛主席致敬  毛主席复电致贺

1950-02-23
第1版()
专栏：祝中越友谊发展

　　越华友好协会电毛主席致敬
    毛主席复电致贺
    【新华社北京二十二日电】越南民主共和国越华友好协会临时执行委员会于其宣告成立之际电毛主席致敬。毛主席顷复电祝贺该会的成立。越华友好协会来电及毛主席复电如下：
    （一）来电毛主席并中华人民共和国中央政府：
    越华友好协会临时执行委员会已于本年二月六日成立，谨向毛主席与中华人民共和国中央政府致敬。
    越华友好协会临时执行委员会会长胡松茂
    （二）复电胡松茂先生：
    欣闻越华友好协会成立，谨致祝贺，并祝中越两国友谊关系的发展。
          毛泽东
    一九五○年二月二十一日
