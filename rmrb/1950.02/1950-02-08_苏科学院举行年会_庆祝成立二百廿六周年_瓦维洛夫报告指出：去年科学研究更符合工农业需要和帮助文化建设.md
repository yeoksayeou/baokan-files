Title: Soviet Academy of Sciences Holds Annual Meeting Celebrating its 226th Anniversary; Vavilov Reports that Last Year's Scientific Research Better Met the Needs of Industry and Agriculture and Helped Cultural Construction
Author:
Date: 1950-02-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th. TASS Moscow: The annual meeting of the Soviet Academy of Sciences was held on the 2nd of this month in the hall of the Moscow Scientists' Club, celebrating the 226th anniversary of the Academy's founding. Participants included prominent Soviet scientific and technical workers, representatives from various social and political organizations in the capital, professors from higher education institutions, and heads of various scientific research institutions. Vavilov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, delivered the opening speech, stating that a notable characteristic of the past year was that scientific research more closely aligned with the needs of industry and agriculture, and was closer to solving various problems in cultural construction. Last year, for the first time in its history, the Academy formulated a practical and extensive grand plan for research, achieving many new accomplishments in astronomy and biology. The Academy's archaeological teams conducted large-scale work in Khorezm, Tajikistan, the Southern Urals, ancient Urartu, and other places.

### 苏科学院举行年会  庆祝成立二百廿六周年  瓦维洛夫报告指出：去年科学研究更符合工农业需要和帮助文化建设

1950-02-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏科学院举行年会
    庆祝成立二百廿六周年
    瓦维洛夫报告指出：去年科学研究更符合工农业需要和帮助文化建设
    【新华社北京七日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联科学院年会于本月二日在莫斯科科学家俱乐部大厅举行，并庆祝科学院成立二百二十六周年纪念。与会者有苏联著名的科学与技术工作者、首都各社会与政治组织代表、高等教育机关的教授和各科学研究机关的首长。苏联科学院院长瓦维洛夫致开幕词称：过去一年的显著特点，就是科学研究更加符合于工农业的需要，更加接近于解决文化建设的各个问题。去年科学院在历史上第一次制订了实用的广博研究的庞大计划，并在天文学及生物学方面获得许多新成就。科学院考古队在贺列兹姆、塔吉克斯坦、在南乌拉尔、在古剌多牙及其他地方做了许多规模宏大的工作。
