Title: Indonesian Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Li Lisan and Zhu Xuefan of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Welcome Them at the Station

Author:
Date: 1950-02-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th. The 11-member delegation of the Indonesian Trade Union Congress (ITUC) to visit New China, led by its Chairman Ai Yue Nuo, arrived in Beijing at 8:40 this morning. Li Lisan and Zhu Xuefan, vice-chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions (ACFTU), and Secretary-General Xu Zhizhen, went to the station to welcome them. After the train arrived, Vice-Chairmen Li and Zhu, and Secretary-General Xu boarded the train to shake hands with each representative and express their welcome. The delegation includes Chairman Ai Yue Nuo (General Secretary of the ITUC), Secretary Su Ha Di Ao Nuo (representative of the Indonesian Petroleum Workers' Union), members Kang Duo Pu La Duo Mo (General Secretary of the Indonesian Railway Workers' Union), Su Er Ye Man (representative of the Indonesian Farmers' Association), Su Gen Duo Luo (representative of the Indonesian Railway Workers' Union), Sha Luo Lan (Chairman of the Indonesian Mining Workers' Union), Ba Su Ji (representative of the Indonesian Railway Workers' Union), Su Ni (female, Minister of the Women's Department of the ITUC), Xi Di Duo Yi Ma (female, representative of the Indonesian Leather Workers' Union), as well as Zhang Yan and Chen Wenhua.

### 印尼总工会访华团抵京  全总李立三朱学范等到站欢迎

1950-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　印尼总工会访华团抵京
    全总李立三朱学范等到站欢迎
    【新华社北京十日电】印尼总工会访问新中国代表团一行十一人，在团长艾约诺率领下，于今日上午八时四十分抵京。中华全国总工会副主席李立三、朱学范、秘书长许之桢等均往车站欢迎。列车进站后，李、朱副主席，许秘书长即登车与各代表一一握手，并致欢迎之忱。该代表团包括团长艾约诺（印尼总工会总书记）、秘书苏哈蒂奥诺（印尼石油工会代表）、团员康多普拉多摩（印尼铁路工会总书记）、苏尔耶曼（印尼农民协会代表）、苏根多罗（印尼铁路工会代表）、沙罗兰（印尼矿业工会主席）、巴苏基（印尼铁路工会代表）、苏尼（女、印尼总工会妇女部部长）、西蒂多伊玛（女、印尼皮革工会代表）及张演、陈文化。
