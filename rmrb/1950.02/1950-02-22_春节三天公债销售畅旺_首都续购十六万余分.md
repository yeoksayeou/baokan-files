Title: Spring Festival Bond Sales Booming, Capital Continues to Purchase Over 160,000 Shares
Author:
Date: 1950-02-22
Page: 4

　　Spring Festival Bond Sales Booming
　　Capital Continues to Purchase Over 160,000 Shares
    [Beijing News] During the Spring Festival period (18th, 19th, and 20th), the Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China sold a total of 160,668 shares of the People's Victory Indexed Bonds, most of which were purchased by the industrial and commercial sectors. According to preliminary statistics, the industrial and commercial sectors in the city have purchased more than 800,000 shares.

### 春节三天公债销售畅旺  首都续购十六万余分

1950-02-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　春节三天公债销售畅旺
    首都续购十六万余分
    【北京讯】春节期间（十八日、十九日、二十日），中国人民银行北京分行共售出人民胜利折实公债十六万零六百六十八分，其中绝大部分为工商界所购买。据初步统计：本市工商界已购买八十余万分。
