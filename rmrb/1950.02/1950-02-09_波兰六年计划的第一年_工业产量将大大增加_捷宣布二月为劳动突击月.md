Title: The First Year of Poland's Six-Year Plan: Industrial Output to Increase Significantly; Czechoslovakia Declares February as Labor Assault Month
Author:
Date: 1950-02-09
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 7th, TASS, Warsaw, February 5th: Polish Prime Minister Cyrankiewicz, at a routine meeting on the 4th, spoke about Poland's tasks in the first year (i.e., 1950) of the Six-Year National Economic Plan, stating that the output of all industries will increase significantly in the first year of the Six-Year Plan. State industries will produce products exceeding 16.9 percent of 1949 in value (at constant prices). Industries manufacturing means of production will expand significantly, bringing their output to over 60 percent of the total output of state industries. The capital investment plan for 1950 will increase by 38 percent compared to last year.
    Cyrankiewicz emphasized that the Soviet Union's selfless assistance is an invaluable contribution to Poland's economic development.
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 7th, TASS, Prague, February 5th: February 25th is the second anniversary of the Czechoslovak "February Events." Two years ago, under the leadership of the Communist Party, the Czech working people crushed the reactionary faction's conspiracy to subvert the People's Democratic government and restore the capitalist system in Czechoslovakia. The Czech working people are now commemorating this historically significant day by striving for new production victories. Employees from hundreds of industries and enterprises have joined the production competition. Prague, Brno, Moravska, Ostrava, Kladno, and other cities have declared February as Labor Assault Month.

### 波兰六年计划的第一年  工业产量将大大增加  捷宣布二月为劳动突击月

1950-02-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　波兰六年计划的第一年
    工业产量将大大增加
    捷宣布二月为劳动突击月
    【新华社北京七日电】塔斯社华沙五日讯：波兰总理西伦凯维兹四日在会议例会上，讲述波兰在六年国民经济计划第一年（即一九五○年）的任务称：六年计划的第一年中，一切工业的产量将大大增加。国家工业将生产在价值上（以不变价格计）超过一九四九年百分之十六点九的产品。制造生产手段的各工业，将大大扩充，使产量达到国家工业总产量的百分之六十以上。一九五○资产投资计划，将比去年增大百分之三十八。
    西伦凯维兹强调指出苏联大公无私的援助，对波兰的经济发展是一种无可估计的贡献。
    【新华社北京七日电】塔斯社布拉格五日讯：二月二十五日是捷克斯洛伐克“二月事件”的两周年纪念。两年前，捷克劳动人民在共产党领导下，粉碎了企图颠覆人民民主政府、并在捷克恢复资本主义制度的反动派的阴谋。捷克劳动人民现正以争取新的生产胜利，来纪念这个具有历史意义的日子。数以百计的各工业各企业的员工，已参加了生产竞赛。布拉格、布尔诺、摩拉夫卡、俄斯特拉发、克拉德诺及其他城市，已宣布二月为劳动突击月。
