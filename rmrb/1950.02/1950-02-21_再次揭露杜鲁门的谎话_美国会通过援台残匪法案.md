Title: Re-Exposing Truman's Lies: U.S. Congress Passes Bill to Aid Chiang Kai-shek Remnants in Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1950-02-21
Page: 4

　　Again Exposing Truman's Lies
    U.S. Congress Passes Bill to Aid Chiang Kai-shek Remnants in Taiwan
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th] According to the U.S. Information Service in Washington: The U.S. House of Representatives and Senate passed the authorization bill to aid the Chiang Kai-shek remnants in Taiwan and the Syngman Rhee government in South Korea on the 9th and 10th respectively, and the bill has been submitted to U.S. President Truman for signature. According to this bill, the deadline for using the remaining $103 million in "U.S. Aid" will be extended from February 15th to June 30th.
    The actions of the U.S. Congress once again expose the hypocrisy of Truman's statements. More than thirty days before the U.S. Senate and House of Representatives passed the aforementioned bill, on January 5th, Truman had stated: "The U.S. government does not plan to provide aid or advice to the Chinese forces in Taiwan." On February 9th, the same day the U.S. House of Representatives passed the aforementioned bill, the U.S. State Department hypocritically reiterated Truman's statement to cover up activities aiding the bandits in Taiwan.

### 再次揭露杜鲁门的谎话  美国会通过援台残匪法案

1950-02-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　再次揭露杜鲁门的谎话
    美国会通过援台残匪法案
    【新华社北京十六日电】据美国新闻处华盛顿讯：美国众议院及参议院，于九、十两日分别通过援助台湾残匪及南朝鲜李承晚政府的授权法案，并已将该法案送交美总统杜鲁门签署。依照这个法案，美国将以“美援”余款一亿三百万美元的使用期限，自二月十五日延长至六月三十日。
    美国国会的行动，又一次地揭露了杜鲁门声明的虚伪性。在美国参众两院通过上述法案的三十多天以前，即一月五日，杜鲁门曾经声明：“美国政府不拟对在台湾的中国军队供给援助或咨询。”二月九日，即在美众院通过前述法案的同一天，美国国务院又虚伪地曾重申杜鲁门的上述声明，借以掩饰援助台匪的活动。
