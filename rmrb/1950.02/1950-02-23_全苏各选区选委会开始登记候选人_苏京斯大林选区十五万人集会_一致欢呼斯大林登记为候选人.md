Title: All Electoral Committees in the Soviet Union Begin Registering Candidates; 150,000 People Rally in Stalin Electoral District of Moscow, Unanimously Cheering for Stalin's Registration as Candidate
Author:
Date: 1950-02-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 22nd. Comprehensive TASS news: The electoral committees of all electoral districts in the Soviet Union began registering candidates for representatives to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR on the 18th. A mass rally of 150,000 people was held in the Stalin electoral district of Moscow, unanimously cheering for Stalin's registration as a candidate for representative to the Supreme Soviet for the district, and passing a letter of tribute to Stalin. A rally of 100,000 people was held in the Molotov electoral district of Moscow, celebrating Molotov's registration as a candidate for representative to the district, and a letter of tribute was sent to Molotov.

　　Malenkov and Khrushchev have been registered as candidates for representatives of the Leningrad electoral district and the Kalinin electoral district in Moscow, respectively. Andreyev, Budyonny, and Suslov have been registered as candidates for representatives of the Ashgabat electoral district, the Shepetovka electoral district, and the Saratoff-Lenin electoral district, respectively. Beria, Voroshilov, and Kaganovich have been registered as candidates for representatives of the Tbilisi-Stalin electoral district, the Minsk City electoral district, and the Tashkent-Lenin electoral district, respectively. Bulganin, Mikoyan, Shvernik, and Kosygin have been registered as candidates for representatives to the Nationalities Council from the First Electoral District of Moscow, the Yerevan-Stalin Electoral District of Armenia, the Sverdlov Electoral District, and the Sixth Electoral District of Ivanovo, respectively.

　　Other representative candidates registered in Moscow's other electoral districts include: Stakhanovite worker Rossiisky, engine driver Dilazhanov, Stalin Prize winner Zhutkike, and others.

### 全苏各选区选委会开始登记候选人  苏京斯大林选区十五万人集会  一致欢呼斯大林登记为候选人

1950-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　全苏各选区选委会开始登记候选人
    苏京斯大林选区十五万人集会  一致欢呼斯大林登记为候选人
    【新华社北京二十二日电】综合塔斯社消息：全苏各选区的选举委员会，于十八日开始登记苏联最高苏维埃代表候选人。莫斯科斯大林选区举行了十五万人的群众大会，一致欢呼斯大林登记为该区最高苏维埃代表候选人，并通过给斯大林的致敬书。莫斯科莫洛托夫选区举行了十万人的大会，欢庆莫洛托夫登记为该区代表候选人，并去信向莫洛托夫致敬。
    莫斯科的列宁格勒选区和加里宁选区已分别登记马林科夫和赫鲁舍切夫为该两区的代表候选人。阿什哈巴德选区、舍赫别托夫卡选区和萨拉多夫——列宁选区分别登记安德列也夫、布琼尼和苏斯洛夫为各该区代表候选人。第比利斯——斯大林选区、明斯克市选区和塔什干——列宁选区分别登记贝利亚、伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇为代表候选人。莫斯科市第一选区、亚美尼亚的埃里温——斯大林选区、斯维德洛夫选区和伊凡诺佛第六选区分别登记了布尔加宁、米高扬、什维尔尼克和柯西金为民族院代表候选人。
    莫斯科其他选区登记的代表候选人有：斯塔哈诺夫式工人罗西斯基、引擎司机第拉然诺夫、斯大林奖金获得者朱特基克等人。
