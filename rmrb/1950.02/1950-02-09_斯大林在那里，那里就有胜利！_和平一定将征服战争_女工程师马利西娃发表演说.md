Title: Where Stalin is, there is Victory! Peace Will Surely Conquer War - Speech by Female Engineer Malysheva
Author:
Date: 1950-02-09
Page: 4

　　Where Stalin is, there is Victory!
    Peace Will Surely Conquer War
    Speech by Female Engineer Malysheva
    At the Stalingrad Tractor Factory's employee meeting, female engineer Malysheva said: "The great victory we won in Stalingrad has been engraved in gold in the history of our motherland. Now, Comrade Stalin leads the camp of hundreds of millions of peace advocates. Where Stalin is, there is Victory! Peace will surely conquer war!"
    Kuznetsov, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, Mielnikov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ukraine, Bagirov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Azerbaijan, and Charkviani, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Georgia, have all been nominated as candidates.
    The Soviet working people have nominated the most outstanding figures of the Soviet Union as representative candidates. These include: Lysenko, President of the All-Union Academy of Agricultural Sciences, aircraft designers Yakovlev and Tupolev, famous writer Fadeyev, Babaevsky, people's poet Tikhonov, Andreyanov, Secretary of the Leningrad Oblast Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, famous composer Solovyov-Sedoy, people's artist Tarasova, Zhutkik, initiator of the top-quality goods movement at the Krasnokholmsk Woolen Factory, Valegula, a famous coal miner from Donbass, Alimov, a famous cotton planter from the Kyrgyz Republic, Dulabay, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Kyrgyz Republic and former miner, and Lysenko, Chairman of the "Socialist Victory" collective farm in Kiev Oblast, among others.

### 斯大林在那里，那里就有胜利！  和平一定将征服战争  女工程师马利西娃发表演说

1950-02-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　斯大林在那里，那里就有胜利！
    和平一定将征服战争
    女工程师马利西娃发表演说
    女工程师马利西娃在斯大林格勒拖拉机工厂的员工大会上说：“我们在斯大林格勒赢得的伟大胜利，已用金字镌刻在我们祖国的历史上。现在，斯大林同志领导着亿万和平拥护者的阵营。斯大林在那里，那里就有胜利！和平一定将征服战争！”
    全苏工会中央理事会主席库兹涅佐夫，乌克兰共产党中央委员会书记米尔尼科夫，阿塞拜疆共产党中央委员会书记巴吉罗夫，乔治亚共产党中央委员会书记查尔克维安尼均已被提名为候选人。
    苏联劳动人民提出苏联最优秀的人物为代表候选人。其中包括：全苏农业科学院院长李森科，飞机设计师雅科夫列夫和杜波列夫，著名作家法捷耶夫，巴巴耶夫斯克，人民诗人吉洪诺夫，苏联共产党列宁格勒州委会书记安德列阿诺夫，名作曲家索洛维耶夫——西多爱，人民艺术家塔拉索娃，克拉斯诺贺尔姆斯克毛织工厂的头等货运动发起人朱特基克，顿巴斯著名煤矿工人瓦列古拉，吉尔吉斯共和国著名植棉人阿利莫夫，矿工出身的吉尔吉西亚最高苏维埃主席团主席杜拉拜，基辅州“社会主义胜利”集体农场主席里森克等人。
