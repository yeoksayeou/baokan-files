Title: Celebrating the Signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance: Soviet Embassy Holds Grand Reception; Heads of Our Government, Leaders of Various Parties and Organizations, and Foreign Diplomatic Envoys Attend

Author:
Date: 1950-02-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 21st. To celebrate the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance, the Chargé d'Affaires of the Soviet Embassy in China, Mr. S.P. Tevosian, held a grand cocktail reception at the embassy at 5:00 PM today. He invited the heads of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, various ministries, commissions, and administrative offices, leaders of democratic parties and people's organizations, and foreign diplomatic envoys to China.

Over 500 people attended this grand celebration banquet, including Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, and Li Jishen; Secretary-General of the Central People's Government Lin Boqu; Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council Dong Biwu, Chen Yun, Guo Moruo, and Huang Yanpei; Acting Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission Nie Rongzhen; President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru; Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Li Kenong and Zhang Hanfu; Vice Mayors of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Zhang Youyu and Wu Han; Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Li Lisan; Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Deng Yingchao; Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation Liao Chengzhi; Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation Xie Bangding; and democratic figures from all walks of life.

Among the foreign envoys present were Czech Ambassador Weisskopf, Korean Ambassador Lee Joo-yeon, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Hungarian Minister Szafranko, Polish Chargé d'Affaires Birocki, and other members of various embassies.

The reception proceeded in a friendly and warm atmosphere and ended at 7:00 PM, followed by a dance.

### 庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订  苏大使馆举行盛大酒会  我政府首长、各党派团体领袖及各国外交使节应邀参加

1950-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订
    苏大使馆举行盛大酒会
    我政府首长、各党派团体领袖及各国外交使节应邀参加
    【新华社北京二十一日电】苏联驻我国大使馆代办史白夫为庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约的签订，特于今日下午五时在大使馆举行盛大鸡尾酒会，邀宴中华人民共和国中央人民政府及各院部会行署首长，各民主党派和人民团体的领袖，以及各国驻我国外交使节。
    应邀参加这一盛大庆祝宴会的五百余人中，有中央人民政府副主席刘少奇、朱德、李济深，中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠，政务院副总理董必武、陈云、郭沫若、黄炎培，中国人民革命军事委员会代理总参谋长聂荣臻，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，外交部副部长李克农、章汉夫，北京市人民政府副市长张友渔、吴晗，中华全国总工会副主席李立三，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超，中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志，中华全国学生联合会主席谢邦定，及各界民主人士。
    各国使节方面出席的有捷克大使魏斯柯普夫，朝鲜大使李周渊，罗马尼亚大使鲁登科，匈牙利公使夏法朗科，波兰代办毕罗奇，及各使馆其他人员。
    酒会于友好、热烈的气氛中进行，于七时结束，随即举行舞会。
