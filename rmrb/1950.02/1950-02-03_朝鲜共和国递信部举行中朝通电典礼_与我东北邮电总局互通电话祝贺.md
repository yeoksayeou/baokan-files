Title: Korean Democratic People's Republic Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications Holds Inauguration Ceremony for Telecommunications with China; Congratulatory Telephone Call Exchanged with Northeast China Postal and Telecommunications Bureau

Author:
Date: 1950-02-03
Page: 1

　　Korean Democratic People's Republic Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications Holds Inauguration Ceremony for Telecommunications with China
    Congratulatory Telephone Call Exchanged with Northeast China Postal and Telecommunications Bureau
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 2nd] The agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Korean Democratic People's Republic on postal and telecommunications services officially came into effect on February 1st. On the 1st, the Korean Democratic People's Republic Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications held an inauguration ceremony for telephone and telegraph communications with China. Participants included Vice Minister of Posts and Telecommunications Park Byung-seop, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Park Dong-choo, Chief Representative of the Northeast China Commercial Delegation to China, Wen Shizhen, as well as people from all walks of life. The ceremony began with a congratulatory exchange by telephone between Cha Byeong-hyung, Director of the Foreign Communications Department of the Korean Democratic People's Republic Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, and Chen Xianzhou, Director of the Northeast China Postal and Telecommunications Administration Bureau of the People's Republic of China, followed by an exchange of congratulatory telegrams by both sides. Finally, Park Byung-seop and Wen Shizhen delivered congratulatory speeches respectively.

### 朝鲜共和国递信部举行中朝通电典礼  与我东北邮电总局互通电话祝贺

1950-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜共和国递信部举行中朝通电典礼
    与我东北邮电总局互通电话祝贺
    【新华社平壤二日电】中华人民共和国与朝鲜民主主义人民共和国间的通邮、通电等协定，已于二月一日起正式生效。一日朝鲜共和国递信部举行中朝通话通报典礼，参加者有递信部副部长朴炳燮、外交部副部长朴东楚、中国东北商业代表团首席代表文士桢，以及各界人士。典礼首由朝鲜共和国递信部国外通信部部长车秉亨与中华人民共和国东北邮电管理总局局长陈先舟，用电话互致祝辞，继由双方交换贺电，最后朴炳燮、文士桢分别发表祝贺演说。
