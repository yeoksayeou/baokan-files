Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Condolences to Shanghai Workers, Calls for Vigorous Support for the Liberation of Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1950-02-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 11th:

    All-China Federation of Trade Unions
    Sends Condolences to Shanghai Workers
    Calls for Vigorous Support for the Liberation of Taiwan
    The All-China Federation of Trade Unions has sent a telegram of condolences to the Shanghai workers who have remained steadfast at their posts despite the frenzied bombing by enemy planes. The telegram reads as follows: To the Shanghai General Labor Union, and through them to all worker brothers and sisters in Shanghai:

    We express our boundless indignation at the frenzied bombing atrocities committed against the people of Shanghai by the dying remnants of the Kuomintang bandit gang under the direct instigation and assistance of American imperialists and Japanese aggressors!

    On behalf of the entire working class of the country, we extend our grief-stricken condolences to the deceased workers! We extend our deep condolences to the families of the deceased workers! And we extend our heartfelt sympathy to you – all the workers of Shanghai!

    Let us, the workers of the whole country, together with the people of the whole country, vigorously support the People's Liberation Army, accelerate the liberation of Taiwan and the liberation of all our territory, and thoroughly eliminate American imperialism and its running dog, the remnant Kuomintang bandit gang, to avenge the dead!
                                              All-China Federation of Trade Unions
                                          February 10, 1950

### 中华全国总工会  电慰上海职工  号召奋力支援解放台湾

1950-02-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　中华全国总工会
    电慰上海职工
    号召奋力支援解放台湾
    【新华社北京十一日电】中华全国总工会电慰在匪机狂炸下坚守岗位的上海职工。电文如下：上海总工会转上海全市职工兄弟姐妹们：
    我们对于垂死的国民党残余匪帮在美帝国主义与日本侵略分子的直接指使与帮助下对上海人民的狂炸暴行，表示无限的愤慨！
    我们谨代表全国工人阶级，向死难的工友致以悲愤的哀悼！向死难工友的家属致以沉痛的吊唁！并向你们——上海全市职工致以亲切的慰问！
    让我们全国工人与全国人民奋力支援人民解放军，加速解放台湾、解放全部国土，彻底肃清美帝国主义及其走狗国民党残余匪帮，来为死难者复仇！
                                              中华全国总工会
                                          一九五○年二月十日
