Title: A Person With No Time
Author: Lu Gong
Date: 1950-02-14
Page: 6

A Person With No Time
    Lu Gong
    From morning till night,
    Busy and covered in sweat,
    What principles were you dealing with?
    You didn't think of that…
    Anyway, busy, busy, busy,
    More diligent than an ant,
    May I ask what you'll do tomorrow?
    You say, "I'll see tomorrow,
    Do one thing at a time!"
    When tomorrow comes, you're busy again,
    So busy you forget what time it is;
    You have two things to do,
    You can't be in two places at once,
    So you just run east and west,
    Your legs ache and tremble,
    Your eyes squint with sleepiness,
    If it's not finished, wait for tomorrow…
    Tomorrow, tomorrow, and tomorrow,
    Tomorrow is always out of time…
    Comrade, it's midnight again,
    Before you sleep,
    Consider: a planned tomorrow.
        (Reprinted from People's Railway)

### 没有时间的人
路工
1950-02-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　没有时间的人
    路工
    从早起到黑夜，
    忙得满头大汗，
    处理了什么原则问题？
    你没有想到这点……。
    反正忙、忙、忙，
    比蚂蚁还勤俭，
    请问明天干什么？
    你说“到明天再看，
    有一件事办一件！”
    到了明天你又忙，
    忙得忘了啥时间；
    有两件事都要你去干，
    你不能分身去两边，
    只能跑东又跑西，
    两条腿酸痛打战战，
    眼睛眯眯想瞌睡，
    办不完再等明天……。
　  明天、明天、又明天，
    明天老是没时间……。
    同志，天又半夜，
    请你没睡前，
    考虑下：过有计划的明天。
         （转载自人民铁道）
