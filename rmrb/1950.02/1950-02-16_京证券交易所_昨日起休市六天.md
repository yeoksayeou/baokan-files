Title: Beijing Stock Exchange Closed for Six Days Starting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1950-02-16
Page: 4

Beijing Stock Exchange

　　Closed for Six Days Starting Yesterday

　　[Beijing News] With the Spring Festival approaching, the Beijing Stock Exchange will be closed for six days from yesterday (the 15th) to the 20th, and will resume trading as usual on the 21st. Trading hours will also be slightly adjusted: the morning session will be from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM, and the afternoon session will be from 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM. Since the Beijing Stock Exchange opened two weeks ago, various stocks have remained stable, and trading activity has gradually increased. Between the 10th and 13th, market prices rose slightly. In the afternoon session of the 13th, Renli briefly hit the limit up of 36,000 yuan due to rumors of a capital increase, but trading volume was low.

### 京证券交易所  昨日起休市六天

1950-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　京证券交易所
    昨日起休市六天
    【北京讯】春节来临，北京证券交易所自昨（十五）日起至二十日止休市六日，二十一日起照常交易。交易时间亦将稍有变动，前市（上午）九时至十二时，后市（下午）二时至四时。北京证券交易所开业两周以来，各种股票始终平稳，交易情况，逐步活跃。十日至十三日间，行市小有上升。十三日后市仁立因传有增资消息，一度以三万六千元涨停，但成交不多。
