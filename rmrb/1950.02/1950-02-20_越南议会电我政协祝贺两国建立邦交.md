Title: Vietnamese Parliament Sends Telegram to CPPCC Congratulating on Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between the Two Countries
Author: 
Date: 1950-02-20
Page: 3

　　Vietnamese Parliament Sends Telegram to CPPCC Congratulating on Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between the Two Countries
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th] According to the Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, 12th: The Standing Committee of the Vietnamese National Assembly sent a telegram to the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the Chinese people, congratulating China and Vietnam on the establishment of diplomatic relations. The telegram reads as follows: "The Vietnamese National Assembly, representing the Vietnamese people, welcomes the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Vietnam. The Vietnamese National Assembly pays tribute to the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Central People's Government, and the Chinese people. The people of China and Vietnam are fighting shoulder to shoulder against reactionary imperialism to liberate themselves and build democratic countries. The consolidation of friendship between the people of China and Vietnam has greatly encouraged the Vietnamese people, who are currently stepping up preparations for a general counter-offensive to crush French imperialism and achieve final victory." The telegram was signed by Ton Duc Thang, Acting Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Vietnamese National Assembly.

### 越南议会电我政协祝贺两国建立邦交

1950-02-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　越南议会电我政协祝贺两国建立邦交
    【新华社北京十六日电】据越南通讯社北越十二日讯：越南国民议会常务委员会致电中国人民政治协商会议及中国人民，祝贺中越两国建立外交关系。电文如下：“越南国民议会代表越南人民欢迎中国与越南建立外交关系。越南国民议会向中国人民政治协商会议、中央人民政府和中国人民致敬。中国和越南的人民并肩地进行着对反动的帝国主义的斗争，以解放他们自己和建立民主国家。中国与越南人民之间的友谊的巩固，大大地鼓舞了越南人民，他们目前正在加紧准备总反攻，以粉碎法帝国主义并取得最后的胜利。”电文由越南国民议会常务委员会代理主席孙德胜签字。
