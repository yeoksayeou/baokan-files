Title: Beijing People's Representative Conference to Hold Second Session of the Second Conference Today
Author:
Date: 1950-02-25
Page: 4

[Beijing News] The second session of the Second Beijing People's Representative Conference is scheduled to open at 9:00 am today (the 25th) at Zhongshan Hall in Zhongshan Park. The main content of this meeting is to discuss and approve the draft work plan and financial revenue and expenditure budget of the Beijing Municipal People's Government for 1950. This meeting was originally scheduled to be held on the 22nd of this month, according to the agreement of the fourth meeting of the Beijing Second People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee, but was postponed due to unforeseen circumstances and rescheduled for today.

### 北京市各界人民代表会议今开二届二次会议

1950-02-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京市各界人民代表会议今开二届二次会议
    【北京讯】京市第二届第二次各界人民代表会议定于今（二十五）日上午九时在中山公园中山堂开幕。此次会议主要内容为讨论并通过京市人民政府一九五○年度工作计划与财政收支概算草案。此次会议经京市第二届各界代表会议协商委员会第四次会议协议，原定于本月二十二日召开，后因故延期，改在今日举行。
