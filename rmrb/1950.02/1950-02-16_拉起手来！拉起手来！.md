Title: Let's Join Hands! Let's Join Hands!
Author: Ma Fantuo
Date: 1950-02-16
Page: 6

　　Let's Join Hands! Let's Join Hands!
    Ma Fantuo
    Let's Join Hands! Let's Join Hands!
    Across the deserts, across the high mountains,
    Across the Great Wall, across the Siberian tundra,
    Across all obstacles and obstructions of the enemy...
    Let's Join Hands! Let's Join Hands!
    This is the guarantee of lasting peace in the East,
    Guaranteeing that we can eliminate any aggressor.
    In the blue sky, in the green ocean,
    Along the 12,000-kilometer coastline,
    Powerful armed forces are guarding our people!
    Let's Join Hands! Let's Join Hands!
    Across the deserts, across the high mountains,
    Across the Great Wall, across the Siberian tundra,
    Smashing all the enemy's conspiracies and sabotage...
    Let's Join Hands! Let's Join Hands!
    This is the prophecy of our happy life.
    We have warm and selfless friends and teachers,
    That is the socialist Soviet Union of Lenin and Stalin!
    Mines are opening, railways are being built, and a prosperous new China is about to appear.
    Towards brightness! Towards happiness! Forward! Forward!
    Great, victorious, invincible
    Six hundred seventy-five million people join hands!
    Peace creators, consolidators, defenders - Chinese and Soviets join hands!
    Brothers - comrades-in-arms - comrades - allies will never be separated!
            February 14th Night

### 拉起手来！拉起手来！
马凡陀
1950-02-16
第6版()
专栏：人民园地

　　拉起手来！拉起手来！
    马凡陀
    拉起手来！拉起手来！
    越过沙漠，越过高山，
    越过长城，越过西伯利亚苔原，
    越过敌人的一切阻挠障碍……
    拉起手来！拉起手来！
    这是东方永久和平的保证，
    保证我们能够消灭任何侵略者。
    在蓝色的天空，在绿色的海洋，
    在一万二千公里绵延的海岸线上，
    守望着我们人民的强大武装！
    拉起手来！拉起手来！
    越过沙漠，越过高山，
    越过长城，越过西伯利亚苔原，
    粉碎敌人的一切阴谋破坏……
    拉起手来！拉起手来！
    这是我们的幸福生活的预言。
    我们有了热情无私的朋友和先生，
    那就是列宁——斯大林的社会主义苏联！
    开起矿山，筑起铁道，富强的新中国就快出现。
    走向光明！走向幸福！向前！向前！
    伟大的，胜利的，无敌的
    六万万七千五百万人民拉起手来！
    和平的创造者，巩固者，保卫者——中国人和苏联人拉起手来！
    兄弟—战友—同志—同盟者永不分开！
            二月十四夜
