Title: General Taxation Bureau Directs Local Tax Bureaus to Strictly Prohibit Slaughtering of Draught Animals and Pregnant Livestock
Author:
Date: 1950-02-03
Page: 2

　　Taxation General Bureau Instructs Local Tax Bureaus
    Strictly Prohibit Slaughtering of Draught Animals and Pregnant Livestock
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 2nd] The Spring Festival is the season with the highest number of livestock slaughtered each year. In order to protect draught animals and prevent tax evasion, the General Taxation Bureau of the Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government has instructed local tax bureaus nationwide to strictly prohibit the slaughtering of draught animals and pregnant livestock. To prevent temporary butchers from slaughtering animals secretly and evading taxes, instructions require all localities to strengthen inspection and leadership over slaughter tax collection work, and those who violate tax regulations must be punished according to the severity of the circumstances.

### 税务总局指示各地税局  严禁屠宰耕畜胎畜

1950-02-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　税务总局指示各地税局
    严禁屠宰耕畜胎畜
    【新华社北京二日电】春节为每年屠宰牲畜最多的季节，中央人民政府财政部税务总局为保护耕畜与防止漏税，特指示全国各地税务局，严禁屠宰耕畜、胎畜。为防止临时屠户的偷宰与漏税，指示要求各地加强对屠宰税收工作的检查与领导，对违犯税则者，须分别情节轻重照章惩处。
