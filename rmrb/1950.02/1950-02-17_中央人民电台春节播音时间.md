Title: Central People's Radio Station Spring Festival Broadcast Schedule
Author:
Date: 1950-02-17
Page: 4

　　Central People's Radio Station Spring Festival Broadcast Schedule
    [Report from this newspaper] The Central People's Broadcasting Station, in accordance with the decision of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau regarding the Spring Festival holiday, will only broadcast important news and artistic programs for four days, the 17th, 18th, 19th, and 20th. All other programs will be completely suspended. All programs will resume on the 21st. The broadcast schedule for these four days is as follows: First broadcast time is from 8:30 to 9:30, artistic programs. Second broadcast time: 17:00 to 22:00. 17:15 to 17:45 artistic programs, 17:45 to 18:00 Japanese news, 18:00 to 19:00 artistic programs, 19:00 to 19:45 local language news (artistic programs on the 17th), 20:00 to 20:20 joint news broadcast by local people's radio stations. 20:20 to 21:30 artistic programs or other temporary programs. 21:30 to 22:00, English news, ending song.

### 中央人民电台春节播音时间

1950-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央人民电台春节播音时间
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台遵照广播事业局春节休假的决定，在十七、十八、十九、二十日四天，只播送重要新闻和文艺节目，其他节目完全停播。二十一日起恢复各项节目。这四天的播音节目如下：第一次播音时间八点三十分到九点三十分，文艺节目。第二次播音时间：十七点到二十二点。十七点十五分到十七点四十五分文艺节目，十七点四十五到十八点日语新闻，十八点到十九点文艺节目，十九点到十九点四十五分地方语新闻（十七日是文艺节目），二十点到二十点二十分各地人民广播电台联播新闻。二十点二十分到二十一点三十分文艺节目或其他临时节目。二十一点三十分到二十二点，英语新闻，终了曲。
