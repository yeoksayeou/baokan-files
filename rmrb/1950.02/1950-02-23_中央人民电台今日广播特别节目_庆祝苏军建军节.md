Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station Special Program Today: Celebrating Soviet Army Day
Author:
Date: 1950-02-23
Page: 4

　　Central People's Broadcasting Station Special Program Today
    Celebrating Soviet Army Day
    [This News Report] The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast a special program today (February 23rd) at 20:20 to commemorate the 32nd anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army: (1) "Talking about how the Soviet Red Army was established and grew up", (2) "Telling a few short stories about the Soviet Red Army and Chinese peasants". At 17:30, Women's Time: How the Preparatory Committee of the Xi'an Women's Federation has solved the problems of employment and study for unemployed and out-of-school women in the past six months. At 18:30, Natural Science Lecture: "The Creation of New Wheat Varieties". At 18:00, the first cultural program: Jingyun Dagu "Wu Song Fights the Tiger". At 21:00, the second cultural program: a music program commemorating the 32nd anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army: Soviet military songs and military music. At 18:45, clapper talk drama and new songs.

### 中央人民电台今日广播特别节目  庆祝苏军建军节

1950-02-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央人民电台今日广播特别节目
    庆祝苏军建军节
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（二十三日）二十点二十分纪念苏军建军三十二周年特别节目：（一）“谈谈苏联红军是怎样建立和成长起来的”，（二）“讲几个苏联红军和中国农民的小故事”。十七点三十分妇女时间：西安市妇联筹委会半年来怎样为失业失学妇女解决工作和学习问题。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：“新麦种的创造”。十八点第一次文艺节目：京韵大鼓《武松打虎》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：纪念苏军建军三十二周年音乐节目：苏联军歌和军乐。十八点四十五分快板剧和新歌曲。
