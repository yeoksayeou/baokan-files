Title: Shen Junru and Others to Broadcast Today: Celebrating the New Sino-Soviet Treaty
Author:
Date: 1950-02-17
Page: 4

　　Shen Junru and Others to Broadcast Today
　　Celebrating the New Sino-Soviet Treaty
　　[Another Dispatch] The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast speeches by leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations, including Shen Junru, Li Lisan, Deng Yingchao, Liao Chengzhi, and Xie Bangding, to the entire nation from 8:20 PM to 9:00 PM today (the 17th), continuing on the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance and the signing of the two agreements. At 8:45 PM, the cultural program will feature: "Songs of the People's Liberation Army Troops" and the Peking Opera "Three Attacks on Zhujiazhuang," the third attack. At 6:00 PM, the cultural program will feature: "Celebrating the Spring Festival Melody," etc. At 9:00 PM, the cultural program will feature: the opera "Returning Land to the Home."
　　[Another Dispatch] The Beijing People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the Spring Festival cultural program at 9:30 PM today (the 17th): (1) Taiping Lyrics "Old Man Liu Spends the Festival," (2) Northeast Drum Ballad. All other programs will rebroadcast the Central Station's programs.

### 沈钧儒等今日广播  庆祝中苏新条约

1950-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　沈钧儒等今日广播
    庆祝中苏新条约
    【又讯】中央人民广播电台今（十七）日二十点二十分到二十一点各民主党派和人民团体领袖沈钧儒、李立三、邓颖超、廖承志和谢邦定继续就中苏友好同盟互助条约和两个协定的签订向全国广播演说。八点四十五分文艺节目：《人民解放军部队歌曲》、京剧《三打祝家庄》第三打。十八点文艺节目：《庆祝春节曲》等。二十一点文艺节目：歌剧《土地还家》。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台今（十七）日二十一点三十分春节文艺节目：（一）太平歌词《刘老汉过节》，（二）东北大鼓。其他各项节目皆转播中央台的节目。
