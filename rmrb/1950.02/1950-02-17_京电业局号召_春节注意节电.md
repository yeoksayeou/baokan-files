Title: Beijing Electricity Bureau Calls for Electricity Conservation During Spring Festival
Author:
Date: 1950-02-17
Page: 4

Beijing Electricity Bureau Calls for Electricity Conservation During Spring Festival
    [This newspaper's report] The Beijing Electricity Bureau issued an official letter stating: The whole country is celebrating the 1950 Spring Festival, and the capital of the people is seeing unprecedented grandeur. However, the power supply in Beijing has recently been insufficient. The Beijing Electricity Bureau hopes that all users in the city will conserve electricity to ensure lighting throughout the city.

### 京电业局号召  春节注意节电

1950-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　京电业局号召
    春节注意节电
    【本报讯】北京电业局发出公函略称：举国欢庆一九五○年春节，人民首都，盛况空前，惟京市最近电源供应，尚感不足，北京电业局盼望本市各界用户节约用电，以保证全市照明。
