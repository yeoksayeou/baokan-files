Title: The CPC Central Committee Notifies Party Committees at All Levels to Lead All Rural Party Members to Guarantee the Completion of Production Plans
Author:
Date: 1950-02-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 27th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China issued a notice on February 27 regarding ensuring the implementation of the Ministry of Agriculture's 1950 agricultural production policy and grain and cotton production increase plan. The full text is as follows:
To all Central Bureaus, Sub-bureaus, and transferred to Party committees at all levels:
    The instructions of the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government regarding the agricultural production policy and grain and cotton production increase plan for 1950 have been approved by the Government Administration Council. The CPC Central Committee fully agrees with these instructions. We request that you assist people's governments at all levels in formulating specific agricultural production plans for this year based on the content of these instructions and the specific local conditions, and lead all rural Party members to strive to complete them, in order to ensure the realization of the national agricultural production tasks for 1950.
            The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, February 27, 1950

### 中共中央通知各级党委  领导全体农村党员  保证完成生产计划

1950-02-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央通知各级党委
    领导全体农村党员  保证完成生产计划
    【新华社北京二十七日电】中国共产党中央委员会于二月二十七日发出关于保证实施农业部一九五○年农业生产方针，及粮棉增产计划的通知，全文如下：各中央局、分局转各级党委同志们：
    中央人民政府农业部关于一九五○年农业生产方针及粮棉增产计划的指示，已经政务院批准。中共中央完全同意这个指示。要求你们协助各级人民政府按照这个指示的内容和当地具体情况，作出各地今年农业生产的具体计划，并领导全体农村党员努力地予以完成，以保证一九五○年全国农业生产任务的实现。
            中国共产党中央委员会  一九五○年二月二十七日
