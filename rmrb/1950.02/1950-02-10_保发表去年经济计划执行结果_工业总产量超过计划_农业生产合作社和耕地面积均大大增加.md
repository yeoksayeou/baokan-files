Title: Bulgaria Announces Results of Last Year's Economic Plan Execution: Total Industrial Output Exceeds Plan, Agricultural Production Cooperatives and Cultivated Land Area Greatly Increased
Author:
Date: 1950-02-10
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 9th: TASS, Sofia, February 7th: The Bulgarian State Planning Commission recently released the results of the execution of the 1949 National Economic Plan (the first year of the Five-Year Plan), stating that the 1949 industrial output exceeded the plan by 7 percent, and the total industrial output increased by 30 percent compared to 1948. The chemical, steel, machine manufacturing, construction, and electrical industries achieved the best results, greatly exceeding the original quotas. Many enterprises in light industry have also exceeded the annual plan. The freight transport plan exceeded by 7 percent, and the basic construction plan exceeded by 23 percent. Certain achievements were also obtained in agriculture. In 1949, 500 more agricultural production cooperatives were added compared to 1948, and nearly 2.5 million decares of land (equivalent to 3.8 million mu) were cultivated more than in 1948. The land area planted with various crops increased by 10 to 250 percent last year. The number of livestock also increased. The capacity of machine and tractor stations increased by more than 22,000 horsepower. The number of industrial and agricultural workers increased by 81,000 in one year. Labor productivity in various industrial sectors increased by 5 to 19 percent. In 1949, 306 educational institutions were opened, and the number of students increased by 11,400.

### 保发表去年经济计划执行结果  工业总产量超过计划  农业生产合作社和耕地面积均大大增加

1950-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　保发表去年经济计划执行结果
    工业总产量超过计划
    农业生产合作社和耕地面积均大大增加
    【新华社北京九日电】塔斯社索非亚七日讯：保加利亚国家计划委员会顷发表一九四九年国民经济计划（即五年计划的头一年）执行的成果称：一九四九年工业产量超过计划百分之七，工业总产量较一九四八年增加了百分之三十。化学、钢铁、机器制造、建筑及电气工业成绩最优，大大地超出了原来的定额。轻工业中的许多企业，也已超过了一年计划。货运计划超过了百分之七，基本建设计划超过了百分之二十三。农业方面也获得了若干成就。一九四九年较一九四八年增加了五百个农业生产合作社，较一九四八年多耕了将近二百五十万狄卡尔土地（合三百八十万市亩）。去年栽种各种作物的土地面积增加了百分之十到百分之二百五十。牲畜头数也有增加。机器与拖拉机站的能力增加了二万二千余匹马力。一年内工业与农业工人数目增加了八万一千人。工业各部门的劳动生产率提高了百分之五到百分之十九。一九四九年内开办了三百零六个教育机关，学生人数增加了一万一千四百人。
