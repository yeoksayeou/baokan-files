Title: The Beijing Municipal Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang Held a Cultural Evening to Celebrate the Sino-Soviet Treaty
Author:
Date: 1950-02-21
Page: 4

The Beijing Municipal Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang
    Held a Cultural Evening to Celebrate the Sino-Soviet Treaty
    [Beijing News] The Beijing Municipal Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang held a cultural evening at its headquarters auditorium at 7 pm on the 19th to celebrate the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance. More than 100 people attended, including Li Jishen, chairman of the committee, and central committee members Tan Pingshan, Cai Tingkai, Jiang Guangnai, Shao Lizi, Chen Shaoxian, Zhu Yunshan, Ning Wu, Wang Kunlun, Xu Baoju, Mei Gongbin, Wu Maosun, Xu Baoqiu, and Zhou Fanwen. Chairman Li Jishen spoke first, pointing out: "The Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance is a concrete manifestation of true, equal, friendly, mutually beneficial, just, peaceful, and secure relations. This is not only a great joyous event for China and the Soviet Union but also a great joyous event for the Far East and all of humanity. We must sincerely support it and carry out propaganda and study."
    Standing committee members Tan Pingshan, Cai Tingkai, Shao Lizi, and Ning Wu also spoke successively, unanimously supporting the Sino-Soviet new treaty and the other two agreements, and thanking the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao for their correct and wise decision-making and correct leadership.
    After the speeches, a cultural program was held, which did not end until 11 pm.

### 民革北京市分部  举行文娱晚会  庆祝中苏缔约

1950-02-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　民革北京市分部
    举行文娱晚会  庆祝中苏缔约
    【北京讯】国民党革命委员会北京市分部为庆祝中苏缔结友好同盟互助条约，于十九日晚七时，在总部礼堂举行文娱晚会。到会有该会主席李济深，中委谭平山、蔡廷锴、蒋光鼐、邵力子、陈劭先、朱蕴山、宁武、王昆仑、许宝驹、梅龚彬、吴茂荪、许宝骙、周范文等百余人。首由李济深主席讲话，他指出：“中苏友好同盟互助条约，是真实的、平等的、友好的、互助的、正义的、和平的、安全的具体事实表现，这不仅是中苏两国的大喜事，而且是远东的、全世界人类的大喜事，我们要竭诚的拥护它并展开宣传与学习。”
    常委谭平山、蔡廷锴、邵力子、宁武都相继讲话，一致拥护中苏新约及其他两个协定，并感谢中国共产党和毛主席的正确英明决策和正确的领导。
    讲话完毕，举行文娱节目，直至十一时始散。
