Title: The Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government Convened the North China Agricultural Technology Work Conference to Study and Promote Agricultural Technology to Ensure the Completion of the Production Increase Plan
Author:
Date: 1950-02-24
Page: 2

　　The Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government
    Convened the North China Agricultural Technology Work Conference
    Researching and Promoting Agricultural Technology to Ensure the Completion of the Production Increase Plan
    [This newspaper report] In order to study and promote agricultural technology and ensure the completion of the production increase plan, the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government specially convened the North China Agricultural Technology Work Conference in Beijing on February 24th (today). More than 120 people attended, including the technical directors of the agricultural departments of the five provinces in North China, technical cadres from various provincial experimental farms, and the North China Agricultural Science Research Institute and the North China Farm. Responsible cadres for experimental research from the provinces of Northeast China, Shaanxi, Shandong, and Henan, as well as professors from agricultural universities and experienced farmers from the suburbs of Beijing, were also invited to participate. The meeting mainly addressed the following issues: (1) The unification and division of labor of the experimental plans of the provinces in North China and the issue of leadership relations. (2) The connection between experimental and promotional work and how to complete the five-year plan for the popularization of improved varieties. (3) A review of past technical research work and the determination of the correct direction for future experimental research work. The meeting is scheduled to last ten days, and after the meeting, it is planned to discuss issues such as pest control in key fruit tree counties.

### 中央人民政府农业部  召开华北农业技术工作会议  研究推广农业技术保证增产计划完成

1950-02-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府农业部
    召开华北农业技术工作会议
    研究推广农业技术保证增产计划完成
    【本报讯】研究推广农业技术，保证增产计划完成，中央人民政府农业部特于二月二十四日（今日），在京召开华北农业技术工作会议。出席有华北五省农业厅技术主任、各省试验场技术干部及华北农业科学研究所、华北农场等一百二十余人。并邀东北、陕西、山东、河南等省试验研究负责干部及农业大学教授、京郊老农等参加。会议主要解决下列问题：（一）华北各省试验计划的统一与分工及领导关系问题。（二）试验与推广工作的联系及如何完成五年普及良种计划。（三）过去技术研究工作的检讨及确定今后试验研究工作的正确路线。会期预定十天，会后并拟讨论果树重点县虫害防治等问题。
