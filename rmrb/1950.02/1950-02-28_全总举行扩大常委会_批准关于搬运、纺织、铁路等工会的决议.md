Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Holds Enlarged Standing Committee Meeting; Approves Resolutions Regarding Transport, Textile, Railway, and Other Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1950-02-28
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 27th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions held its 22nd Enlarged Standing Committee meeting on the 25th. The meeting discussed and approved the following matters: (1) The Provisional Measures for Resolving Issues of Illness, Injury, Disability, and Death Benefits for Chinese Transport Workers from the First Congress of the Chinese Transport Workers' Union; the resolution on establishing workers' consumer cooperatives; and the labor discipline of Chinese transport workers. An Lifu and 29 others were appointed as formal members of the National Committee of the Chinese Transport Workers' Union, Gong Xinbo and 5 others as alternate members, Lu Weiyu and 4 others as review committee members, and An Lifu as Chairman of the National Committee. (2) The resolution of the National Textile Workers' Union on concluding collective contracts. (3) Teng Daiyuan, Lü Zhengcao, Li Jiebo, and 93 others were appointed as members of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Workers' Union. (4) Peng Zhen, Liu Ren, Xiao Ming, and 60 others were appointed as members of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions Committee, Liu Fugui and 9 others as alternate members, Yang Baosong and 8 others as financial supervision committee members, and Xing Yuru and 1 other as alternate financial supervision committee members. The meeting also approved the postponement of the Military Industry Workers' Union meeting.

### 全总举行扩大常委会  批准关于搬运、纺织、铁路等工会的决议

1950-02-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　全总举行扩大常委会
    批准关于搬运、纺织、铁路等工会的决议
    【新华社北京二十七日电】中华全国总工会二十五日举行第二十二次扩大常务委员会，会议讨论并批准了下列事项：（一）中国搬运工会第一届代表大会关于解决中国搬运工人疾、伤、残、亡等待遇问题的暂行办法，关于举办工人消费合作社的决议，关于中国搬运工人的劳动纪律。安力夫等三十人为中国搬运工会全国委员会正式委员，龚新波等六人为候补委员，陆维馀等五人为审查委员，安力夫为全国委员会主席。（二）全国纺织工会关于订立集体合同的决议。（三）滕代远、吕正操、李颉伯等九十六人为中国铁路工会全国委员会委员。（四）彭真、刘仁、萧明等六十三人为北京市总工会委员会委员，刘富贵等十人为候补委员，杨宝嵩等九人为经费监察委员，邢玉如等二人为候补经费监察委员。会议并通过军工工会会议延期举行。
