Title: Northeast Shipping Administration Establishes Merchant Marine College
Author:
Date: 1950-02-05
Page: 3
　　Northeast Shipping Administration
    Establishes Merchant Marine College
    [This newspaper dispatch] Northeast News: In order to meet the needs of the development of the New China's shipping industry, the Northeast Shipping Administration has specially established the Northeast Merchant Marine College to train crew members and cadres to serve the New Democratic shipping industry. The school has departments of Navigation, Marine Engineering, and Shipping Administration Management. The Navigation Department graduates in four years, learning to navigate ships. The Marine Engineering Department has the same study time, and graduates will serve as third or second assistant engineers. The Shipping Administration Management Department graduates in one year. The above three departments plan to enroll a total of 150 students this year.

### 东北航政总局创办商船专科学校

1950-02-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　东北航政总局
    创办商船专科学校
    【本报讯】东北消息：为适应新中国航运事业发展之需要，东北航政总局特创办东北商船专科学校，以培养服务于新民主主义航运事业的船员和干部。该校分设航海系、轮机系及航政管理科。航海系四年毕业，学习驾驶船舶。轮机系学习时间同前，毕业后担任三管轮或二管轮等职务。航政管理科一年毕业。上述三部分今年准备共招收学生一百五十名。
