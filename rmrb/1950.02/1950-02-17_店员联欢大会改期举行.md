Title: Shop Assistants' Joint Celebration Postponed
Author:
Date: 1950-02-17
Page: 4
　　Shop Assistants' Joint Celebration Postponed
    [Beijing News] The Beijing Shop Assistants' Joint Celebration originally scheduled for the 18th and 19th (the second and third days of the Lunar New Year) will now be held on the 19th and 20th (the third and fourth days of the Lunar New Year) due to the celebration of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance on the 18th. The location remains the Music Hall in Zhongshan Park, and the time is 10:00 AM.

### 店员联欢大会改期举行

1950-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　店员联欢大会改期举行
    【北京讯】京市店员联欢大会原定十八、十九（初二、初三）两日举行，现因十八日庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约的签订，店员联欢大会决定改于十九、二十（初三、初四）日举行。地点仍在中山公园音乐堂，时间是上午十点。
