Title: Commemorating "Day of Struggle Against Colonial System" - Guo Moruo Sends Telegram to Youth of Colonies and Semi-Colonies, Paying Tribute to Their Struggle and Expressing Firm Support
Author:
Date: 1950-02-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 5th: To commemorate the "Day of Struggle Against the Colonial System," Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, sent a telegram to the youth and students of various colonies and semi-colonies, expressing his respect. The text of the telegram is as follows:

Dear young friends,

The Chinese people pay tribute to the youth and students of all colonies and semi-colonies who are engaged in the anti-imperialist struggle for national liberation!

We hope that you will unite all patriotic youth, closely integrate with all anti-imperialist forces, and continue to participate in the national liberation movement and national liberation war, in order to finally overthrow the reactionary rule of imperialism and its running dogs.

Your struggle is just and will inevitably be victorious. The Chinese people firmly support you. Your struggle weakens the forces of imperialism, and will not only achieve the independence of your respective nations, but will also make a great contribution to lasting world peace.

Long live the united struggle of the people of the world against imperialist aggression!

Long live lasting world peace and people's democracy!

Chairman of the China Peace Committee Guo Moruo
February 5, 1950, Beijing

### 纪念“反对殖民制度斗争日”  郭沫若电殖民地半殖民地青年  对他们的斗争致敬并表示坚决支持

1950-02-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　纪念“反对殖民制度斗争日”
    郭沫若电殖民地半殖民地青年
    对他们的斗争致敬并表示坚决支持
    【新华社北京五日电】中国保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若为纪念“反对殖民制度斗争日”，致电各殖民地和半殖民地的青年和学生，表示敬意。电文如下：亲爱的青年朋友们：
    中国人民向为民族解放而进行反帝斗争的各殖民地与半殖民地的青年与学生致敬！
    希望你们团结一切爱国青年，和一切反帝力量密切结合，继续参加民族解放运动和民族解放战争，以争取最后推翻帝国主义及其走狗的反动统治。
    你们的斗争是正义的，必然胜利的，中国人民坚决支持你们。你们的斗争削弱了帝国主义的力量，不仅将获得各自民族的独立，并对世界持久和平有伟大的贡献。
    全世界反对帝国主义侵略的人民团结斗争万岁！
    世界持久和平与人民民主万岁！
      中国保卫世界和平委员会主席  郭沫若
        一九五○年二月五日北京
