Title: Pravda on Soviet Youth Preparing for Elections: Youth League to Have a Mighty Army of Ten Million

Author:
Date: 1950-02-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 30th: TASS reports: Pravda recently published an editorial discussing the role of Soviet youth in the elections to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR. The newspaper pointed out: The young generation of the Soviet Union, selflessly dedicated to the Party of Lenin and Stalin, has consistently supported the Party's wise policies in all aspects and followed the Party's leadership. Millions of patriotic youth are working conscientiously in industry, transportation, construction, and agriculture. Soviet youth are enthusiastically preparing for the elections to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR. In the upcoming elections, the Communist Youth League will appear as a mighty army of ten million communist youth builders, closely united around the Party of Lenin and Stalin.

The editorial continued: Millions of patriotic youth, along with the entire people, are preparing to celebrate the election day as a grand festival of the Soviet state. In factories and manufacturing plants, in state farms, collective farms, and machine and tractor stations, the creative activities of youth are surging. Young men and women are undertaking greater obligations in socialist competition. The editorial also discussed the tasks of the Communist Youth League in assisting Party organizations in preparing for the elections and in organizing patriotic youth to strive for the early completion of the post-war five-year plan.

### 真理报论苏联青年准备选举  青年团将出现千万大军

1950-02-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　真理报论苏联青年准备选举
    青年团将出现千万大军
    【新华社北京三十日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：真理报顷发表社论，讨论苏联青年在苏联最高苏维埃选举中所起的作用。该报指出：苏联年青的一代，忘我地献身于列宁与斯大林的党，一直是全面地支持着党的英明政策，并追随着党的领导的。数以百万计的爱国青年，以自觉的精神在工业、运输、建筑和农业等方面工作着。苏联青年正热烈地准备着苏联最高苏维埃的选举。共产主义青年团在即将到来的选举中，将以紧密团结在列宁斯大林党周围的、共产主义的青年建设者一千万之众的大军出现。
    社论继称：数以百万计的爱国青年和全体人民一道，正在准备着把选举日当作像苏维埃国家的大节日一样地来庆祝。在工厂和制造厂，在国家农场、集体农场及机器与拖拉机站，青年的创造性活动正在高涨中。男女青年们在社会主义竞赛中承担了更多的义务。社论并论及共产主义青年团在帮助党组织准备选举，以及在组织爱国青年进行争取提早完成战后五年计划方面的任务。
