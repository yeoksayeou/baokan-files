Title: Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Adjusted Starting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1950-02-12
Page: 2

　　Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Adjusted Starting Yesterday
    [Shanhaiguan News] The Shanhaiguan Joint Office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank has changed its exchange rate starting from the 11th to one yuan of Renminbi for thirteen yuan of Northeast currency.

### 山海关货币牌价  昨日起调整

1950-02-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　山海关货币牌价  昨日起调整
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处自十一日起牌价改为人民币一元兑东北券十三元。
