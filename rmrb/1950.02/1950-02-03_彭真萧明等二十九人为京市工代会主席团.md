Title: Peng Zhen, Xiao Ming and 27 Others Form Presidium of Beijing Municipal Trade Union Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-02-03
Page: 4

　　【Beijing News】The list of the Presidium of the first Beijing Municipal Workers' Congress is as follows:
　　Peng Zhen, Xiao Ming, Liu Laifu, Xu Liqun, Yang Bozheng, Mao Shuduo, Sun Yishu, Wang Wenbin, Ling Biying, Su Rui, Meng Jinsheng, Zhu Changjiang, Lu Mao, Wang Qian, Ying Yongxing, Song Fengxiang, Huang Bojin, Su Yifu, Ren Bin, Yang Yunyu, Song Ting, Cui Yingguo, Li Guanglu, Qian Duansheng, Zhao Zhenbang, Wang Guolan, Liu Keqin, Li Jingxiang, Zou Dexin, a total of 29 people.
