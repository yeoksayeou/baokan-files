Title: Saburov Awarded the Order of Lenin
Author:
Date: 1950-02-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 23rd: TASS, Moscow: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR issued the following order:

    To Comrade Maxim Zakharovich Saburov, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR and Chairman of the State Planning Committee under the Council of Ministers of the USSR, on the occasion of his fiftieth birthday, for his contribution to the economic construction of the Soviet Union, he is hereby awarded the Order of Lenin.
          N.M. Shvernik, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR
          A. Gorkin, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR
          Moscow, Kremlin, February 18, 1950

### 萨布罗夫荣获列宁勋章

1950-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　萨布罗夫荣获列宁勋章
    【新华社北京二十三日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团发布命令如下：
    值此苏联部长会议副主席兼苏联部长会议所属国家计划委员会主席萨布罗夫同志五十大寿之际，为了他对苏联经济建设的贡献，特授予马克西姆·萨哈罗维奇·萨布罗夫同志以列宁勋章。
          苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席什维尔尼克
          苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书高尔金
          一九五○年二月十八日于莫斯科克里姆林宫
