Title: People in Various Cities in East China Rejoice, Support, and Shout "Long Live!"
Author:
Date: 1950-02-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, February 18th: People in various cities in East China have been gathering to warmly celebrate the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance, as well as the other two agreements.

　　Workers, students, and various organizations in Hangzhou City were immersed in an atmosphere of joy after receiving news of the signing of the new Sino-Soviet treaty and agreements. More than 300 employees of the Hangzhou Office of the Shanghai Railway Administration gathered collectively in the auditorium early on the 15th, excitedly awaiting the oral broadcast from the Central People's Broadcasting Station. As soon as the news was broadcast over the radio, various propaganda teams appeared on the streets. The waist drum team of the Youth Cultural Troupe, wearing colorful costumes and carrying red drums, walked and drummed, shouting slogans such as "Long live Chairman Mao Zedong!" and "Long live Marshal Stalin!" together with the audience. The waist drum and dance team of Zhejiang University, with more than 300 people, also braved the snow to carry out propaganda activities at 2 pm on the 15th, with extremely enthusiastic emotions. In the evening, more than 1,000 representatives from various organizations and democratic parties in Zhejiang Province and Hangzhou City held a celebration party. At the meeting, Governor Tan Zhenlin and representatives from all walks of life spoke one after another; finally, they passed congratulatory telegrams to Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Stalin, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Foreign Minister Vyshinsky.

　　People from all walks of life in Fuzhou City held a tea party on the evening of the 15th to celebrate the signing of the new Sino-Soviet treaty and agreements. Vice Chairman Ye Fei proposed at the meeting that it is necessary to eliminate bandits in Fujian this year, eliminate the remnants of the enemy along the coast as soon as possible, and support the liberation of Taiwan to celebrate this epoch-making event. On the 16th, various agencies and organizations held separate gatherings to celebrate. The Fujian Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China convened a cadre meeting, and Chen Xinren, Minister of the Propaganda Department, called on all cadres to strengthen internationalist education, publicize the significance of the new treaty and agreements, and work hard.

　　Various agencies and people's organizations in Jinan City have been celebrating the conclusion of the new Sino-Soviet treaty for days, with extremely enthusiastic emotions. Trucks and Yangko teams continuously flowed through the streets. Red and green leaflets fluttered everywhere. On the 16th, more than 2,000 representatives from all walks of life in the province and city held a celebration meeting. Factories and troops also widely held symposiums, unanimously supporting the conclusion of the new Sino-Soviet treaty. The Provincial Federation of Trade Unions called on the entire working class in Shandong to cherish the great assistance of the Soviet Union to us, actively labor, and accelerate the recovery and development of production.

　　The people of Qingdao enthusiastically celebrated the conclusion of the new Sino-Soviet treaty and agreements. On the morning of the 15th, the workers who were attending the award ceremony for outstanding employees in the textile industry were overjoyed after hearing the news and immediately turned the conference into a celebration. The Qingdao Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association also held a celebration on the 16th, attended by more than 1,200 representatives from all walks of life. At the meeting, Xiang Ming, Director of the Military Control Commission, Peng Lin, Political Commissar of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Fan Chengchuan, Deputy Director of the Consultative Committee of the People's Representatives Conference, all gave speeches, calling on people from all walks of life to celebrate the new era of Sino-Soviet friendship and cooperation with practical actions of active learning and work.

　　More than 2,000 representatives from the party, government, and military organs and people from all walks of life in the Southern Jiangsu District and Wuxi City held a conference on the 16th to celebrate the conclusion of the Sino-Soviet brotherly alliance. Chen Pixian, Secretary of the Southern Jiangsu District Committee of the Communist Party of China, Guan Wenwei, Director of the Southern Jiangsu Administrative Office, and representatives from all walks of life spoke enthusiastically. It was unanimously believed that this was another great happy event since the founding of the People's Republic of China. Workers in various factories in Wuxi City, such as the Wuxi Agricultural Tool Factory and the Qingfeng Yarn Factory, have been holding symposiums for days to study the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance. The Wuxi City Primary and Secondary School Teachers Union also organized propaganda teams to conduct street propaganda.

　　Various agencies and organizations in the Northern Jiangsu District and Yangzhou City enthusiastically studied and publicized the new Sino-Soviet treaty and agreements during the Spring Festival holiday. The Northern Jiangsu Administrative Office also invited more than 100 representatives from all walks of life to hold a symposium. At the meeting, all representatives unanimously expressed their support. In order to meet the requirements of the people in various places, the Northern Jiangsu Daily used a speedboat that night to specially send the issue of the newspaper containing the full text of the new treaty and agreements and photos of Chairman Mao Zedong and Marshal Stalin to the main towns throughout Northern Jiangsu.

　　After receiving the news of the conclusion of the new Sino-Soviet treaty and agreements, the organs and people of the Southern Anhui District and Wuhu City were overjoyed. The Southern Anhui Administrative Office, the Southern Anhui Military Region, the Southern Anhui District Committee of the Communist Party of China, and the Wuhu City Military Control Commission and the Municipal People's Government all sent telegrams to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government to express their warm congratulations. Various agencies, organizations, and factories throughout the city held small-scale celebration meetings. Cultural troupes and the Wuhu City Student Winter Vacation Study Group also dispatched waist drum teams to conduct extensive propaganda. On the 16th, more than 1,500 representatives from all walks of life held a grand celebration meeting.

　　Various agencies, organizations, and representatives from all walks of life in the Northern Anhui District and Hefei City also held a conference on the 16th to celebrate the signing of the new Sino-Soviet treaty, and at the same time to celebrate the establishment of the East China Military and Political Committee. Song Richang, Director of the Northern Anhui Administrative Office, and Zeng Xisheng, Secretary of the Northern Anhui District Committee of the Communist Party of China, both delivered speeches, unanimously believing that the signing of the new Sino-Soviet treaty not only provides greater guarantees for world peace, but also provides great assistance to China's future economic construction. In recent days, national flags have been flying throughout Hefei City, and propaganda teams have been dispatched one after another, which is extremely lively. Workers in various factories have expressed that they will celebrate the new Sino-Soviet treaty by increasing production. After hearing the news, the workers of the Automobile Repair Factory of the Northern Anhui Highway Administration worked overtime for two hours that day to express their warm support.

### 华东各城市人民狂欢拥护高呼万岁

1950-02-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　华东各城市人民狂欢拥护高呼万岁
    【新华社上海十八日电】华东各城市人民纷纷集会，热烈庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约及其他两个协定的签订。
    杭州市工人、学生、各机关团体在获得中苏签订新条约新协定的消息后，即沉浸在欢乐的气氛中。上海区铁路管理局杭州办事处的三百多个职工，在十五日清早，即集体聚集在大礼堂里兴奋地守听中央人民广播电台的口语广播。当消息由无线电播出后，街头立刻出现了各种宣传队。青年文工团的大腰鼓队，穿着彩衣，挂着红鼓，边敲边走，和观众一齐高呼“毛泽东主席万岁！”“斯大林大元帅万岁！”等口号。浙江大学三百余人的腰鼓歌舞队，亦于十五日下午二时冒雪出动宣传，情绪极为热烈。入晚，浙江省及杭市各机关团体和各民主党派代表千余人举行了庆祝晚会。会上，省主席谭震林及各界代表相继讲话；最后并通过致毛泽东主席、斯大林主席及周恩来总理、维辛斯基外长的致敬电。
    福州市各界人民于十五日晚举行茶会，庆祝中苏新条约、新协定的签订。叶飞副主席在会上提出：要在今年内肃清福建的土匪、尽速肃清沿海残敌、支援解放台湾来庆祝这一划时代的大喜事。十六日，各机关、团体分别集会祝贺。中共福建省委召集了干部会议，宣传部部长陈辛仁号召全体干部加强国际主义教育，宣传新条约、新协定的意义，努力工作。
    济南市各机关、人民团体连日祝贺中苏缔结新约，情绪至为热烈。大街上不息地川流着卡车和秧歌队。红绿传单到处飘扬。十六日，省、市各界代表二千余人举行了庆祝大会。各工厂、部队并广泛地召开了座谈会，一致拥护中苏新约的缔订。省总工会号召全山东工人阶级要珍贵苏联对于我们的伟大援助，积极劳动，加速恢复和发展生产。
    青岛市人民热烈地欢庆中苏缔结新条约和新协定。十五日上午，正在举行授奖仪式的纺织业优秀职工大会的工人们听到这一消息后，兴奋万分，立即把大会变成了庆祝会。中苏友好协会青岛市分会亦于十六日集会祝贺，出席各界代表一千二百余人。会上，军管会主任向明、中国人民解放军军政委彭林，各界人民代表会议协商委员会副主任范澄川等均发表谈话，号召各界人民用积极学习和工作的实际行动来庆祝中苏友好合作的新纪元。
    苏南区与无锡市党、政、军各机关及各界人民代表二千余人，于十六日举行大会庆祝中苏兄弟同盟的缔结。中共苏南区党委书记陈丕显、苏南行署主任管文蔚及各界代表均热烈发言。一致认为这是中华人民共和国成立以来的又一次大喜事。无锡市各工厂如无锡农具厂、庆丰纱厂等工人，连日召开座谈会，学习中苏友好同盟互助条约。无锡市中小教联并组织宣传队，出动街头宣传。
    苏北区和扬州市各机关团体在春节假期中热烈学习和宣传中苏新条约、新协定。苏北行政公署并邀请各界代表百余人举行座谈。会上各代表一致表示拥护。苏北日报社为了满足各地人民的要求，当晚用汽艇专送刊有新条约、新协定全文和毛泽东主席、斯大林大元帅照片的该期报纸至全苏北各主要城镇。
    皖南区暨芜湖市各机人民在获得中苏缔结新条约、新协定的消息后，欢欣万状。皖南行署、皖南军区、中共皖南区委会及芜湖市军管会、市人民政府等均纷纷致电向中央人民政府毛泽东主席热烈致贺。全市各机关、团体、工厂均召开小型庆祝会。文工团和芜湖市学生寒假学习团并出动腰鼓队，广泛宣传。十六日，各界代表一千五百余人曾举行盛大庆祝会。
    皖北区暨合肥市各机关、团体及各界代表亦于十六日举行大会庆祝中苏新约的签订，同时并祝贺华东军政委员会的成立。皖北行署主任宋日昌、中共皖北区党委书记曾希圣均发表演说，一致认为：中苏新约的签订，不仅使世界和平更有保证，且给中国今后经济建设以莫大帮助。连日合肥全市国旗飞扬，宣传队纷纷出动，异常热闹。各厂工人均表示要以增加生产来祝贺中苏新约。皖北公路总局汽车修理厂工人在听到该项消息后，当天即加工两小时，表示热烈拥护。
