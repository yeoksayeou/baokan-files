Title: Municipal Government Holds 4,000-Person Rally Yesterday to Celebrate Sino-Soviet Treaty
Author:
Date: 1950-02-25
Page: 4

　　Municipal Government Holds 4,000-Person Rally Yesterday
    Celebrating the Sino-Soviet Treaty
    [Beijing News] The Beijing Municipal People's Government Sino-Soviet Friendship Association General Branch held a celebration rally for the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance at the Zhongshan Park Music Hall yesterday afternoon (February 24th). Over 4,000 members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association from various municipal government departments attended. The venue was adorned with the national flags of China and the Soviet Union, as well as portraits of Chairman Mao and Marshal Stalin. Vice Mayor Wu Han delivered a speech at the rally, analyzing the balance of power between the two camps in the world before and after the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. He explained the contents of the treaty and agreements in detail and clarified the similarities and differences between the new treaty and the old treaty of 1945. After Vice Mayor Wu’s speech, entertainment programs began.

### 市府四千人昨集会  庆祝中苏缔约

1950-02-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　市府四千人昨集会
    庆祝中苏缔约
    【北京讯】北京市人民政府中苏友好协会总支会昨（二十四）日下午在中山公园音乐堂举行庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订大会。到会有市府各单位的中苏友协会员四千余人。会场高悬中苏两国国旗及毛主席、斯大林大元帅的画像。吴晗副市长在会上作了讲话，他分析了中苏友好同盟互助条约签订前后世界两个阵营的力量对比，详细解释了条约及协定的内容，并说明了新约与一九四五年旧约的异同。吴副市长讲话后，即开始游艺节目。
