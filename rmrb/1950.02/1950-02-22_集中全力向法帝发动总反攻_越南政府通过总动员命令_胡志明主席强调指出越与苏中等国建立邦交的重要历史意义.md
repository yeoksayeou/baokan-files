Title: Concentrate All Efforts to Launch a General Counter-Offensive Against French Imperialism: Vietnamese Government Passes General Mobilization Order; President Ho Chi Minh Emphasizes the Important Historical Significance of Vietnam Establishing Diplomatic Relations with the Soviet Union, China, and Other Countries
Author: 
Date: 1950-02-22
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 21st: According to the Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam: The Government Committee of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, at a meeting earlier this month, approved the 1950 plan for waging the war of liberation and passed an order for the general mobilization of Vietnam's manpower, material resources, and financial resources to launch a general counter-offensive against the French imperialists. The meeting of the Vietnamese Government Committee was held under the chairmanship of President Ho Chi Minh. The President emphasized the important historical significance of Vietnam establishing diplomatic relations with the Soviet Union, China, and the People's Democratic countries. Foreign Minister Hoang Minh Giam gave a report on Vietnam's recent diplomatic measures. General Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnam People's Army, Vo Nguyen Giap, gave a report on the military situation in Vietnam over the past two months. He explained in detail the great victories in the Hoa Binh province area west of Hanoi, the Hanoi-Haiphong railway, Bach Mai Airport near Hanoi, Quang Binh province in central Vietnam, and southern Vietnam. The Government Committee finally approved the 1950 national activity plan, which includes military, political, diplomatic, economic, financial, social, and cultural aspects. The committee also approved the 1950 budget and the main guidelines for reforming national education.

### 集中全力向法帝发动总反攻  越南政府通过总动员命令  胡志明主席强调指出越与苏中等国建立邦交的重要历史意义

1950-02-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　集中全力向法帝发动总反攻
    越南政府通过总动员命令
    胡志明主席强调指出越与苏中等国建立邦交的重要历史意义
    【新华社北京二十一日电】据越南通讯社北越讯：越南民主共和国政府委员会在本月初的一次会议中，批准了一九五○年的进行解放战争的计划，并通过一项越南人力、物力与财力总动员，向法帝国主义者发动总反攻的命令。越南政府委员会的会议是在胡志明主席的主持下举行的。主席强调指出越南与苏联、中国和人民民主国家建立外交关系的重要历史意义。外交部长黄明鉴作了关于越南近来的外交措施的报告。越军总司令武元甲作了过去两个月的越南军事形势的报告。他详细说明了河内以西的和平省地区、河内——海防铁路、河内附近白梅机场、中越广平省以及越南南部的大胜利。政府委员会最后批准了一九五○年国家活动的计划，这个计划包括军事、政治、外交、经济、财政、社会和文化等各个方面。委员会并批准了一九五○年的预算和改革国民教育的主要方针。
