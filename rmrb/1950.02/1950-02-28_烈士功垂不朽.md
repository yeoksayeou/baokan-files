Title: The Martyrs' Achievements Will Last Forever
Author: Nie Rongzhen
Date: 1950-02-28
Page: 2

　　The heroic actions of the "February 28th" martyrs and patriots are a revolutionary flame that ignited the unyielding fighting will of the Taiwanese people. In the near future, when the liberation army crosses the sea to fight and land in front of the enemy, this buried power will erupt and burst out like irresistible volcanic lava. It is foreseeable that this internal and external collaboration will completely wipe out the last remaining remnants of the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang and repay the blood debt of the Taiwan "February 28th" incident. At that time, we will erect an immortal monument to the martyrs on the liberated land of Taiwan.

### 烈士功垂不朽
聂荣臻
1950-02-28
第2版()
专栏：纪念台湾“二·二八”三周年

　　烈士功垂不朽
　　聂荣臻
    “二·二八”烈士与志士们的英勇行动，是一把革命的烈火，它燃起了台湾人民不屈的战斗意志。在不远的未来，当解放大军跨海作战、在敌前登陆的时候，这埋藏着的力量，会像火山的熔岩不可抗拒地爆裂与喷射开来。可以预计，这种里应外合的结果，将把蒋匪帮最后残余的一堆孤魂野鬼，完全扫灭干净；使台湾“二·二八”的血债，得到偿还。那时候我们将在解放了的台湾土地上，为烈士们竖起不朽的纪念碑。
