Title: Welcoming the Supreme Soviet Elections All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions Calls on Working People to Elect Candidates from the League of Communists and Non-Party People
Author:
Date: 1950-02-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 23rd: The All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions recently issued a letter to all trade union members, workers and engineers, agricultural workers, science, literature and art workers, and staff of various agencies for the election of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, calling on all working people to unanimously vote for the candidates of the League of Communists and Non-Party People on election day. The letter pointed out that voting for the candidates of the League of Communists and Non-Party People means voting for the further prosperity of socialist industry, the further upsurge of socialist transportation, the prosperity of socialist agriculture and collective farms, a happier and more affluent life for the working people, the further prosperity of advanced socialist culture, and the victory of the forces of peace, democracy, and socialism over the instigators of new wars of imperialism.

The All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions is convinced that all trade union members and all working people will celebrate the election of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union with a new upsurge of socialist emulation and with collective labor achievements that create the welfare of the great socialist country.

### 迎接最高苏维埃选举  全苏工会号召劳动人民  选举党与非党联盟候选人

1950-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　迎接最高苏维埃选举
    全苏工会号召劳动人民
    选举党与非党联盟候选人
    【新华社北京二十六日电】塔斯社莫斯科二十三日讯：全苏工会中央理事会顷为苏联最高苏维埃选举发表告全体工会会员，工人和工程师，农业工人，科学、文学与艺术工作者，各机关职员书，号召全体劳动人民在选举日一致投票选举共产党与非党联盟的候选人，并指出投票选举党与非党联盟候选人，就是投票拥护社会主义工业的进一步繁荣、社会主义运输的进一步高涨，拥护社会主义农业和集体农场的繁荣，拥护劳动人民更幸福更丰裕的生活，拥护先进的社会主义文化的进一步繁荣，拥护和平、民主与社会主义力量对帝国主义新战争挑拨者的胜利。
    全苏工会中央理事会确信全苏工会会员，一切劳动人民，将以社会主义竞赛的新高涨，将以创造伟大社会主义国家福利的集体劳动业绩，来庆祝苏联最高苏维埃的选举。
