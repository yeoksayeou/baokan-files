Title: New American Imperialist Tricks for Invading Asia: Attempting to Implement a "Little Marshall Plan"
Author:
Date: 1950-02-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 13th: TASS, The Hague, February 11th: According to the Dutch Communist Party's newspaper, De Waarheid: The United States intends to implement a so-called "Little Marshall Plan" in Asia. According to reliable sources, prior to the United States of Indonesia receiving sovereignty, the United States government submitted a memorandum to the Dutch, French, and British governments, including clauses for the so-called Asian "Little Marshall Plan." This plan includes India, Pakistan, Ceylon, Burma, Thailand, Indochina, Indonesia, Australia, and New Zealand.

The United States demands the following massive concessions from Britain as a reward for American "aid": reducing taxes on American capital investment, granting preferential rights to American investment, and implementing free convertibility of the pound sterling, among other things.

### 美帝侵略亚洲新花样  企图实行“小马歇尔计划”

1950-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　美帝侵略亚洲新花样
    企图实行“小马歇尔计划”
    【新华社北京十三日电】塔斯社海牙十一日讯：荷共机关报真理报消息：美国打算在亚洲实行所谓“小马歇尔计划”。该报根据可靠方面消息称：美国政府在印尼合众国接收主权之前，曾向荷兰、法国和英国政府提出一项备忘录，其中包括所谓亚洲“小马歇尔计划”的条款。这一计划包括印度、巴基斯坦、锡兰、缅甸、泰国、印度支那、印尼、澳大利亚和新西兰。
    美国要求英国作如下的巨大让步，作为对美国“援助”的报答，即削减对美国资本投资的税收，美国投资应享受特惠权，英国实行英镑自由兑换等等。
