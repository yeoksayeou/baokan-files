Title: Chairman Mao Visits Irkutsk on His Return Journey
Author:
Date: 1950-02-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 25th: TASS, Moscow, February 25th: Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, Premier and Foreign Minister of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and their entourage, stopped in Irkutsk for several hours on February 24th on their way back to China from Moscow.

　　The guests toured the city, learned about the work of the Young Pioneer Palace, and visited a tea packaging factory.

　　People from all walks of life in the area warmly welcomed the guests.

### 毛主席在归途游伊尔库次克

1950-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席在归途游伊尔库次克
    【新华社北京二十五日电】塔斯社莫斯科二十五日讯：中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东、中华人民共和国政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来及随员一行，二月二十四日于自莫斯科回国途中，在伊尔库次克逗留了数小时。
    嘉宾们游览城市，了解少先宫的工作情况，并参观了茶叶包装工厂。
    该地各界人士热烈欢迎嘉宾们。
