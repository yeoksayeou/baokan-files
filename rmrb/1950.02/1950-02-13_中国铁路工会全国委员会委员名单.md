　　List of Members of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union
    [Beijing News] The list of elected members of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union is as follows: Teng Daiyuan, Lü Zhengcao, Li Jiebo, Wang Hefeng, Lu Ping, Chen Diwei, Mou Huan Kui, Xu Ziwei, Liang Yongfu, Yu Zhou, Hu Xiguang, Xiao Hongqi, Li Buying, Huang Zhixiang, Wu Jinzhu, Zhai Chunfang, Yan Yusen, Wang Ping, Mao Yisheng, Yu Guangsheng, Ma Yin, Chen Jingqian, Zhou Rong, He Jijun, Jia Xuehe, Yang Xiangrong, Du Xianyang, Luo Zhenyang, Sui Zhendong, Zhang Ende, Pu Fenglu, Wang Xiaoci, Xu Ping, Li Yong, Yang Zhiwei, Gui Peng, He Fengchi, Zhao Yadong, Wang Changgong, Zhao Jinxian, Lu Kejun, Shi Shangnong, Xie Haorong, Qin Chao, Li Yonggeng, Yang Liqi, Wang Mulin, Mu Chunong, Yue Zhijian, Zhao Yuntang, Zhang Tingrui, Song Tao, Chen Du, Zhang Yongwei, Li Mingzhe, Chen Tan, Wang Xuezeng, Fan Hui, Zhang Zhi, Yang Hua, Chen Jiaoyu, Shang Bowei, Liu Qingyuan, Qi Guizhi, Song Chengde, Jiang Haoran, Yang Gan, Huang Liang, Wang Zijie, Gao Wenlin, Dai Yuncheng, Sha Mao Shen Guofu, seventy-two people.
