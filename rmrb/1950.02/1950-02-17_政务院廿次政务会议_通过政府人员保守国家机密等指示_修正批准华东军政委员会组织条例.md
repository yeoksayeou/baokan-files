Title: The 20th Administrative Council Meeting of the Government Administration Council Passed Instructions on Government Personnel Maintaining State Secrets, etc., and Revised and Approved the Organizational Regulations of the East China Military and Political Committee
Author:
Date: 1950-02-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th – The Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government held its 20th Administrative Council meeting at 2 p.m. today. The meeting discussed and approved instructions on maintaining state secrets by government personnel at all levels and the candidates for various units of the East China Military and Political Committee, and revised and approved the organizational regulations of the East China Military and Political Committee. The meeting approved the appointment of Chu Xichun as a Counselor of the Government Administration Council, Gan Chongdou as Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government, Wan Yi as Deputy Director of the Labor Bureau of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and Zhao Zishang and Ma Yingzhong as Director and Deputy Director of the Taxation Bureau of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, respectively.

### 政务院廿次政务会议  通过政府人员保守国家机密等指示  修正批准华东军政委员会组织条例

1950-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　政务院廿次政务会议
    通过政府人员保守国家机密等指示
    修正批准华东军政委员会组织条例
    【新华社北京十六日电】中央人民政府政务院于今日下午二时举行第二十次政务会议。会议讨论并通过了关于各级政府工作人员保守国家机密的指示和华东军政委员会各单位的人选，并修正批准了华东军政委员会组织条例。会议通过任命楚溪春为政务院参事，甘重斗为中央人民政府内务部办公厅副主任，万一为北京市人民政府劳动局副局长，赵子尚、马应中为北京市人民政府税务局正副局长。
