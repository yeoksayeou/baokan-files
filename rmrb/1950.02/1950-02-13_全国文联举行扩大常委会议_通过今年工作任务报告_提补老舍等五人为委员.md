Title: All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Holds Expanded Standing Committee Meeting; Approves This Year's Work Report; Nominates and Appoints Lao She and Four Others as Committee Members
Author:
Date: 1950-02-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 12th: The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles held its fourth expanded Standing Committee meeting on the 7th. Chairman Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairmen Mao Dun, Zhou Yang, and 35 standing committee members and national committee members in Beijing attended. Chairman Mao Dun presided. The meeting resolved the following matters: (1) Approved Zhou Yang's report on the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles' work overview for the past six months and the work tasks for 1950. (2) Approved the nomination and appointment of Lao She, Shao Quanlin, Sun Fuyuan, Ai Wu, and Sha Ting as members of the National Committee. Three more positions will be filled after Taiwan and other places are liberated. (3) Regarding the award for literary and artistic works from the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art in 1946 to the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers in 1949, it was decided that the Literary and Artistic Works Award Committee, composed of the Standing Committee of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and the chairmen and vice-chairmen of its affiliated associations, would handle the matter. The committee is responsible for reviewing the list of nominated works proposed by each association after multiple discussions, determining a preliminary list, soliciting opinions from all parties, and then submitting a final plan. (4) The resignation of Zhou Weizhi, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, was approved, and Chen Qixia was appointed as his successor.

### 全国文联举行扩大常委会议  通过今年工作任务报告  提补老舍等五人为委员

1950-02-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　全国文联举行扩大常委会议
    通过今年工作任务报告  提补老舍等五人为委员
    【新华社北京十二日电】中华全国文学艺术界联合会于七日举行第四次扩大常务委员会会议，到主席郭沫若、副主席茅盾、周扬及在京常委与全国委员共三十五人，由茅盾主席。会议决议事项如下：（一）通过周扬关于全国文联半年来工作概况及一九五○年工作任务的报告。（二）通过提补老舍、邵荃麟、孙伏园、艾芜、沙汀等五人为全国委员会委员，另留三名待台湾等地解放后再补。（三）关于自一九四六年延安文艺座谈会起至一九四九年全国文学艺术工作者代表大会止这一时期文艺作品的评奖事宜，决定由全国文联常务委员会及所属各协会正副主席组成文艺作品评奖委员会办理，并责成该委员会根据各协会经过多次讨论提出的拟奖作品目录，加以复审，定出初步名单，征集各方面意见后，提出最后方案。（四）全国文联副秘书长周巍峙辞职照准，由陈企霞继任。
