　　Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
    [This newspaper dispatch] Central People's Broadcasting Station today (26th) 17:30 Workers' Time: 1. The Preparatory Committee of the China Textile Workers' Union's instructions on cultivating cadres; 2. Short commentary: "Establishing Trade Unions in Institutions." 18:30 Natural Science Lectures: "Soviet Neurosurgery Expert Battonko." 18:00 First Cultural Program: Local Opera "New Five Saints Paying Homage to the Emperor". 21:00 Second Cultural Program: Instrumental music and piano solos. 18:30 Weekly Cultural Program: Yangko opera "Great Celebration of Success."
    [Another dispatch] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station 12:30 Workers' Program: 1. Culture and Entertainment: Telecom Bureau workers' choir singing; 2. Communication: The conversation record of Wang Tongzhi, a worker at Yangquan Iron Plant in Shanxi, about self-made welding rods, "Invention Comes from Learning." 13:00 Youth Program: Guo Yongze, executive member of the All-China Students' Federation Northeast District, introduces the study and living conditions of students in Harbin. 17:00 Broadcasting "New Songs." 17:45 Peking Opera. 18:45 Soviet Music.
