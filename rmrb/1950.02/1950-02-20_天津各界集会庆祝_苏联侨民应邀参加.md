Title: Tianjin Circles Gather to Celebrate; Soviet Expatriates Invited to Attend
Author:
Date: 1950-02-20
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, February 19th: Over a thousand representatives from all circles in Tianjin held a grand meeting today to celebrate the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance. The Soviet Consul General's representative in Tianjin, Soviet expatriates, and others were invited to attend. The meeting began with the national anthems of China and the Soviet Union, followed by a speech by Mayor Huang Jing. He pointed out that the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance has formed an inviolable great force in the world, and has dealt a major blow to the revival of Japanese imperialism and other imperialist collusion with Japanese imperialism to invade China! He also pointed out that the signing of the Soviet loan agreement and other agreements, as well as Soviet technical assistance to us, has further increased the favorable conditions for China's industrialization. Then, Soviet Acting Consul General in Tianjin, Gidov, spoke on behalf of Gan Wulin. He said that the people of China and the Soviet Union, and all peace-loving people in the world, greeted and celebrated the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance with great excitement and unparalleled enthusiasm, because it not only opened a new era between the two great nations of China and the Soviet Union—an era of great friendship and fraternal cooperation between the two nations—but also a new stage in the development of international relations, which made great contributions to the consolidation of world peace and democracy. Yang Chengwu, Commander of a certain corps of the North China Liberation Army, Zhou Shutao, Deputy Mayor of Tianjin, Zhang Guofan, a university professor, Luo Yun, Director of the Women's Federation, Yang Yizhou, Manager of Tianjin China Textile Company, Bi Mingqi from the business community, Zhu Xianyi, a medical worker, and Meng Minfen, a representative of the Student Union, also spoke successively. They all praised the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance and other agreements. Every time the speakers mentioned the names of Marshal Stalin and Chairman Mao, the whole audience burst into stormy applause. Finally, the conference passed a telegram of respect to Marshal Stalin and Chairman Mao.
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, February 16th: All circles in Tianjin, with extremely joyful hearts, warmly celebrated the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance and loan agreements. Various art troupes, newspapers, radio stations, and Xinhua branch offices held a celebration evening party of 3,000 people today. At the meeting, Huang Songling, Minister of Culture and Education of the Military Control Commission, explained in detail the great significance of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance and various agreements, and various recreational programs were performed to show celebration. The Tianjin Federation of Trade Unions also held a celebration meeting for more than 1,000 trade union cadres at the same time. Many organs, factories, and schools also held celebration meetings, symposiums, and photo exhibitions of Sino-Soviet friendship and Soviet construction. On the streets of Tianjin, the sound of drums and firecrackers from Yangge teams continued all day long. Tonight, many cinemas added evening shows, and the People's Radio Station broadcasted all night long. In addition, the Political Department of a certain corps stationed in Tianjin also issued instructions, requiring the leading organs of various units to deeply convey the content and significance of the new Sino-Soviet Treaty and agreements to the commanders and fighters, carry out propaganda to the masses, and actively participate in the celebration and propaganda activities of the local party, government, and people.

### 天津各界集会庆祝  苏联侨民应邀参加

1950-02-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　天津各界集会庆祝
    苏联侨民应邀参加
    【新华社天津十九日电】津市各界代表千余人今日举行盛会，庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约的签订。苏联驻津总领事代表、苏联侨民等亦应邀与会。会议首奏中苏两国国歌，继由黄敬市长致词。他指出：由于中苏友好同盟互助条约的签订，这就在世界上结成了一个不可侵犯的伟大力量，并使得日本帝国主义的复活以及其他帝国主义勾结日本帝国主义侵略中国的阴谋遭到重大打击！他并指出：苏联对中国贷款的协定等的签订，以及苏联在技术上对我们的帮助，就更增加了中国工业化的有利条件。接着苏联驻津代理总领事吉多福代表干吾里林讲话。他说：中苏两国人民及世界上一切爱好和平的人民，都以极大的兴奋、无比的热情，来迎接与庆贺中苏友好同盟互助条约的签订，因为它不但开辟了中苏两大民族之间的新时代——两个民族伟大友谊与兄弟合作时代，同时也是国际关系发展的新阶段，它对巩固世界和平民主事业有极大的贡献。华北解放军某兵团司令员杨成武、天津副市长周叔弢、大学教授张国藩、妇联主任罗云、天津中纺公司经理杨亦周、商业界毕鸣岐、医务工作者朱宪彝、学联代表孟敏芬等亦先后讲话。他们一致盛赞中苏友好同盟互助条约及其他协定的签订。当讲演者每一提到斯大林大元帅、毛主席的名字时，全场即响起暴风雨般的掌声。大会最后通过致电斯大林大元帅、毛主席致敬。
    【新华社天津十六日电】津市各界以极端愉快的心情，热烈庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约及贷款等协定的签订。各文艺团体、报社、广播电台、新华分社，于今日举行三千人的庆祝晚会。会中军管会文教部黄松龄部长详细说明了中苏友好同盟互助条约及各协定的伟大意义，并进行了各种游艺节目，以示庆祝。天津市总工会亦于同时举行了千余工会干部的庆祝大会。许多机关、工厂、学校亦分别举行庆祝会、座谈会、中苏友好和苏联建设照片展览。津市街头，秧歌队的锣鼓声、爆竹声整日不断。今夜许多影院加演夜场，人民电台亦彻夜广播。此外，驻津某兵团政治部亦发出指示，要求各部队领导机关向指战员深入传达中苏新条约及协定的内容与意义，展开对群众宣传与积极参加当地党政民的庆祝与宣传活动。
