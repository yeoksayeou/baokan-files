Title: South Korean Patriotic Youths Actively Join Guerrilla Units
Author:
Date: 1950-02-14
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 13th: The appeal to the people of the whole country issued on January 6th by the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic United Front for the Unification of the Fatherland continues to receive enthusiastic responses from the people of South Korea, and more and more young farmers are joining the guerrilla units. According to the South Korean Laborer's News, in the week from January 15th to 21st alone, more than 500 patriotic youths from the three counties of Bonghwa, Andong, and Yeongju in northern Gyeongsangbuk-do joined the guerrilla units. On January 21st, more than 300 farmers in Wondong district, Yangsan county, eastern Gyeongsangnam-do, cooperated with the guerrilla units to annihilate more than 20 puppet "criminal police." More than 50 farmers have joined the guerrilla units. On January 29th, more than 700 farmers in Daedeok district, Damyang county, northern Jeollanam-do, held a farmers' meeting when the guerrilla units attacked the reactionary armed forces in the area, and punished more than 10 special agents and thugs who burned down villages.

### 南朝鲜爱国青年积极参加游击队

1950-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　南朝鲜爱国青年积极参加游击队
    【新华社平壤十三日电】朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会一月六日发表的告全国人民书，继续获得南朝鲜人民的热烈响应，青年农民参加游击队的事件，已愈来愈多。据南朝鲜劳力者报报道，在从一月十五日到二十一日的一周中，只在庆尚北道北部的奉化、安东、荣州三县，就有爱国青年五百余人参加了游击队。一月二十一日，庆尚南道东部梁山县院洞区的三百余农民，配合游击队歼灭了伪“刑警队”二十余名。农民五十余人，已参加了游击队。一月二十九日，全罗南道北部潭阳县大德区的农民七百余人，在游击队袭击该地反动武装时，召开农民大会，惩罚了焚毁村舍的特务暴徒十余名。
