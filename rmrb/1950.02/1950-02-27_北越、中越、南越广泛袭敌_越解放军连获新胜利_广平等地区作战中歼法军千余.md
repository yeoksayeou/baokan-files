Title: Viet Minh Widely Attacks the Enemy, Viet Minh Liberation Army Continuously Achieves New Victories, Eliminating Over 1,000 French Troops in Guangping and Other Areas
Author:
Date: 1950-02-27
Page: 4

　　North Vietnam, Central Vietnam, and South Vietnam Widely Attack the Enemy
    Viet Minh Liberation Army Continuously Achieves New Victories
    Eliminating Over 1,000 French Troops in Guangping and Other Areas
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 26th] The Vietnamese People's Liberation Army has achieved new victories in North, Central, and South Vietnam. According to Viet Nam News Agency: In January of this year, in operations east of Hanoi in Guang'an Province, North Vietnam, the Liberation Army killed or wounded 350 French troops and captured 70. In Central Vietnam, between December of last year and January of this year, regular troops and militia, in operations in the Guangping, Guangzhi, and Cheng'an areas, killed or wounded a total of 1,100 French troops, seized 500 various weapons, destroyed two enemy locomotives, 15 freight and passenger cars, and 20 trucks. The French forces in the area have now been forced into a defensive position. In the area southwest of Saigon, the Liberation Army captured eight French strongholds at the beginning of this month.

### 北越、中越、南越广泛袭敌  越解放军连获新胜利  广平等地区作战中歼法军千余

1950-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　北越、中越、南越广泛袭敌
    越解放军连获新胜利
    广平等地区作战中歼法军千余
    【新华社北京二十六日电】越南人民解放军在北越、中越和南越皆获得新的胜利。综合越南通讯社消息：解放军今年一月在北越的河内以东广安省作战中，击毙击伤法军三百五十人，俘虏七十人。在中越，正规军与民兵去年十二月和今年一月在广平、广治、承天地区作战中，共击毙击伤法军一千一百名，缴获各种武器五百个，毁敌机车两台，货客车十五节及卡车二十辆。现该地区之法军已被迫处于守势。在西贡西南地区，解放军在本月初攻克法军据点八处。
