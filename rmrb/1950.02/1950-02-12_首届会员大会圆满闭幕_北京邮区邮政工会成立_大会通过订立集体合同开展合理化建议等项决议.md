Title: First Members' Congress Successfully Concludes; Beijing Postal District Postal Workers' Union Established; Congress Passes Resolutions on Collective Contract Negotiation and Rationalization Proposals

Author:

Date: 1950-02-12
Page: 4

The First Members' Congress Successfully Concludes
Beijing Postal District Postal Workers' Union Established
Congress Passes Resolutions on Collective Contract Negotiation and Rationalization Proposals
[Beijing News] The first members' representative congress of the Beijing Postal District Postal Workers' Union successfully concluded yesterday (the 11th), formally establishing the Beijing Postal District Postal Workers' Union. The congress was convened in Beijing on the 5th of this month and lasted for seven consecutive days. A total of one hundred and sixteen representatives from various cities including Beijing, Baoding, Shijiazhuang, Zhangjiakou, Suiyuan, Baotou, Handan, and various counties in the Beijing postal district attended the congress. During the congress, Zhu Xuefan, Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government, Liu Zijiu, Director of the Culture and Education Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Li Jinghan, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the National Postal Workers' Union, Zhao Zhigang, Deputy Director of the National Postal Bureau, and Gu Chunfan, among others, attended and delivered speeches. They unanimously pointed out that the primary task of the trade union is to unite the broad masses of workers and closely cooperate with the administration to restore and develop production and complete the construction of the people's postal service. The attending representatives listened to the report by Cheng Anyu, Director of the Beijing District Postal Administration, on the work plan for the entire district in 1950, the report on the preparatory work of the Beijing District Postal Workers' Union, and the summary report on the work of the Beijing Municipal Postal Workers' Union over the past year.

After discussing the above reports, all the representatives unanimously supported the Beijing District Postal Administration's work plan for 1950 and guaranteed to implement this plan. In addition, the congress also passed resolutions on starting to negotiate collective contracts in 1950 and launching a rationalization proposal campaign. The congress received a total of 378 proposals, most of which were related to the development of business, and were properly handled after discussion by the congress.

Finally, the congress elected Huo Zhaolin, Song Fengxiang, Li Menglan, Li Ping'an, Qi Youcai, Cen Tiequan, and twenty-one others as members of the trade union executive committee; Zhao Changhe, Zhao Danlin, and seven others as members of the trade union supervisory committee, and the Beijing Postal District Postal Workers' Union was formally established.

[Another News] At the first members' representative congress of the Beijing Postal District Postal Workers' Union, Zhu Xuefan, Cheng Anyu, Song Fengxiang, and ten others were elected as representatives of the district to attend the National Postal Workers' Congress.

### 首届会员大会圆满闭幕  北京邮区邮政工会成立  大会通过订立集体合同开展合理化建议等项决议

1950-02-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　首届会员大会圆满闭幕
    北京邮区邮政工会成立     
    大会通过订立集体合同开展合理化建议等项决议
    【北京讯】北京邮区邮政工会第一届会员代表大会已于昨（十一）日圆满闭幕，正式成立了北京邮区邮政工会。大会于本月五日在北京召开，连续举行七天。出席此次大会的有北京、保定、石家庄、张家口、绥、包、邯郸等各城市及北京邮区各县代表共一百十六人。大会进行期间，中央人民政府邮电部朱学范部长、中华全国总工会文教部长刘子久、全国邮政总工会筹备会副主任李景韩、全国邮政总局副局长赵志刚、谷春帆等曾先后到会讲话，他们一致指出：工会的首要任务是团结广大职工，密切配合行政，恢复与发展生产，完成人民邮政的建设事业。出席代表听取了北京区邮政管理局成安玉局长关于一九五○年全区工作计划的报告，北京区邮政工会筹备工作报告，及北京市邮政工会一年来工作总结报告。
    全体代表于讨论上述报告后，一致拥护北京区邮政管理局一九五○年的工作计划，并保证执行这一计划。此外，大会并通过一九五○年开始订立集体合同，开展合理化建议运动等项决议。大会共收到提案三百七十八件，大部都是关于发展业务的，经大会讨论后，已分别予以适当处理。
    最后，大会选出霍兆麟、宋凤祥、李梦兰、李平安、祁有财、岑铁权等二十七人为工会执行委员；赵长和、赵丹林等九人为工会监察委员，北京邮区邮政工会遂告正式成立。
    【又讯】在北京邮区邮政工会第一届会员代表大会上，选出朱学范、成安玉、宋凤祥等十三人为该区出席全国邮工代表大会的代表。
