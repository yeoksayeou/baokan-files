Title: Snowfall in Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1950-02-01
Page: 1

　　Nanjing Snowfall
    [Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 31st] Nanjing City experienced its first auspicious snowfall today. It began at 1:30 PM and had not stopped by 8:00 PM, with the ground already covered in snow over an inch deep.

### 南京降雪

1950-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　南京降雪
    【新华社南京三十一日电】宁市今日首降瑞雪，自下午一时半降起，八时尚未停止，地面已积雪寸余。
