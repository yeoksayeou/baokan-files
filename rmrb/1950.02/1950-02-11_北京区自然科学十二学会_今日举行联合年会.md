Title: Beijing Area Natural Science Twelve Societies Hold Joint Annual Meeting Today
Author:
Date: 1950-02-11
Page: 3

Beijing Area Natural Science Twelve Societies
    Hold Joint Annual Meeting Today
    [This newspaper dispatch] The joint annual meeting of the twelve natural science societies in the Beijing area (namely, the Mathematics Society, Physics Society, Chemistry Society, Geological Society, Geographical Society, Zoology Society, Botany Society, Physiology Society, Pharmaceutical Society, Entomology Society, Oceanography and Limnology Society, and Psychology Society) will officially open at 9:30 am today (the 11th) at the Sino-French University in Donghuangchenggen. Wu Yuzhang, Guo Moruo, and Peng Zhen will be invited to report at that time. The tasks of the meeting are to exchange academic experiences, strengthen organization, and clarify tasks, so as to better serve the economic and cultural construction of the New Democracy. The meeting is scheduled for two days.

### 北京区自然科学十二学会  今日举行联合年会

1950-02-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　北京区自然科学十二学会
    今日举行联合年会
    【本报讯】北京区自然科学十二学会（即数学会、物理学会、化学会、地质学会、地学会、动物学会、植物学会、生理学会、药学会、昆虫学会、海洋湖沼学会、心理学会）联合年会将于今（十一）日上午九时半在东皇城根中法大学正式开幕。届时将请吴玉章、郭沫若、彭真出席报告。会议任务为交流学术经验，加强组织，明确任务，以便更好地为新民主主义的经济、文化建设服务。会期定为两天。
