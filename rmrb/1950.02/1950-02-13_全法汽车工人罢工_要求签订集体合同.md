Title: Nationwide Strike of French Auto Workers Demanding Collective Bargaining Agreement
Author:
Date: 1950-02-13
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th: TASS reports that 220,000 workers in all French automobile manufacturing plants, in accordance with the resolution of the National Conference of Automobile Workers, went on strike on the 9th. They demand a monthly subsidy of 3,000 francs per person from the factories, calculated from December 1, 1949, and demand discussion and signing of a collective agreement stipulating a minimum guaranteed wage of 20,000 francs, guaranteeing free trade union activities, and immediately abolishing police-managed employment measures.

### 全法汽车工人罢工  要求签订集体合同

1950-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　全法汽车工人罢工  要求签订集体合同
    【新华社北京十二日电】塔斯社讯，法国所有汽车制造厂二十二万工人，遵照汽车工人全国代表会议的决议，于九日举行罢工。他们要求自一九四九年十二月一日算起，每人由厂方月贴三千法郎，并要求讨论及签订集体合同，规定最低保证工资为二万法郎，保证工会自由活动以及立刻废止警察管理就业办法。
