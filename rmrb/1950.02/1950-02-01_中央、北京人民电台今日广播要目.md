Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Schedule
Author:
Date: 1950-02-01
Page: 4

　　Central, Beijing People's Radio
    Today's Broadcast Schedule
    [Our Newspaper] The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast a speech by Xiao Bi at 20:30 today (the 1st). Cai Tingkai will broadcast a speech to overseas Chinese at 8:30. A recording of the speech will be broadcast at 19:45. At 20:20, International Current Affairs: "The Great Achievements of the Mongolian People's Republic." At 17:30, Youth Time: "The Beijing Youth League in the Year Since Liberation." At 18:30, Natural Science Lectures: General Knowledge of Electricity, Lecture 10, "Electric Lights." At 18:00, First Literary Program: Qinshu "The Transformation of Shi Yaozong." At 21:00, Second Literary Program: Yangko Opera "The Whole Family is Busy."
    [Another News Item] Beijing People's Radio at 12:30, Workers' Program: (1) Culture and Entertainment: A recording of a joint performance by comrades from the Factory Literary Work Committee performing seven relatively excellent songs created by workers in the city. (2) Communication: Liu Hengwen, special-class labor model of the Beijing Automobile Repair Factory, speaks on "Bring out true skills and work hard to refuel." At 13:00, Youth Program: Comrade Huang Anmiao, a student of the Chinese University Russian Brigade, talks about his understanding of the land reform issue. At 18:45, Listener Services: ① Answering the question of "Whether China now has the conditions to organize collective farms," ② Answering "What is the meaning of the term 'Ren Ri'," ③ Recruitment announcement for technical personnel by the Mudanjiang Municipal People's Government and Mudanjiang Municipal Enterprise Company, ④ Enrollment announcement for the Supply School of the North China Military Region. At 13:00, a literary program celebrating the first anniversary of the liberation of Beijing for workers and peasants. At 16:30, Children's Program: Sun Jingxiu continues to tell "The Story of Lenin." At 17:00, Singing Program: The Pingmin Middle School Choir performs new songs. At 17:45, Voice of the Municipality: Weng Dujian, Director of the Bureau of Culture and Education, speaks on "The Educational Work of Beijing Municipality in the Past Year."

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-02-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台
    今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（一日）廿点三十分萧笔广播演讲。八点三十分蔡廷锴向海外华侨广播演讲。十九点四十五分播送该项讲演录音。廿点廿分国际时事：《蒙古人民共和国的伟大成就》。十七点三十分青年时间：“解放一年来的北京市青年团”。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：电学常识第十讲“电灯”。十八点第一次文艺节目：琴书《史耀宗转变》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：秧歌剧《全家忙》。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工节目：（一）文化娱乐：工厂文艺工作委员会下厂同志联合演唱本市工人创作的七个比较优秀的歌曲的录音。（二）通讯：北京汽车修配厂特等劳动模范刘恒文讲“拿出真本领，努力加油干”。十三点青年节目：华大俄文大队黄安淼同学谈谈对土改问题的认识。十八点四十五分听众服务：①回答“中国现在是不是有组织集体农场的条件的问题，②回答“人日”这名词是什么意义，③牡丹江市人民政府和牡丹江市企业公司招聘技术人员的启事，④华北军区供给学校的招生通告。十三点工人、农民同志庆祝北京解放一周年文艺节目。十六点三十分儿童节目：孙敬修继续讲《列宁的故事》。十七点歌咏节目：平民中学歌咏团演唱新歌曲。十七点四十五分市政之声：文教局翁独健局长讲演“一年来北京市的教育工作”。
