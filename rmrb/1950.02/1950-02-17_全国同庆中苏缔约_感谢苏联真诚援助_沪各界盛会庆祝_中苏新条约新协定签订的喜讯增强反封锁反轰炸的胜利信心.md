Title: The Whole Country Celebrates the Sino-Soviet Treaty, Thanks the Soviet Union for its Sincere Assistance, Shanghai Celebrates with Grand Gathering, News of the Signing of the New Sino-Soviet Treaty and Agreements Strengthens Confidence in Victory Against Blockade and Bombing
Author:
Date: 1950-02-17
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 16th - People's representatives from all circles in East China and Shanghai held a celebration meeting on the evening of the 15th to congratulate the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance and other agreements. Nearly 2,000 representatives from all walks of life attended, including Rao Shushi, Chen Yi, Liu Xiao, Liu Changsheng, Zhang Dingcheng, Chen Shaokuan, Pan Hannian, Sheng Pihua, Guo Huaruo, Chen Wangdao, Li Chang, Zhang Yun, Chen Jianchen, Shen Baowen, Su Yanbin, Xie Renbing, Hu Juewen, Rong Yiren, and Pan Zhenya. Soviet friends, including Soviet Consul General in Shanghai, Vladimir, Deputy Consul General, Virishagin, and Chairman of the Soviet Overseas Chinese Association, Tsibunovsky, and others, were also invited to participate. The meeting began with the military band playing the national anthems of China and the Soviet Union and cheers.

Rao Shushi Speaks

After Pan Hannian, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Shanghai Branch, delivered the opening speech as chairman, Rao Shushi, Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee, spoke first. He said: "Especially the people of East China and Shanghai, who are currently engaged in arduous struggles against the blockade and bombing by US imperialism and the remnants of the Chiang Kai-shek bandit forces, feel extremely excited after hearing the news of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance and other agreements.” Chairman Rao pointed out: “The Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance and other agreements have consolidated the traditional friendly historical relations between the Chinese and Soviet peoples, and have thoroughly foiled the conspiracies of imperialism and the Kuomintang reactionaries to sow discord and undermine the unity of the Chinese and Soviet peoples. The basic starting point of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance is to consolidate peace and oppose aggression. This is a fraternal alliance of friendship and mutual assistance of one-third of the world's population. The fraternal alliance between China and the Soviet Union is itself a great and invincible force."

Chen Yi Speaks

Mayor Chen Yi then spoke, first expressing his support on behalf of the people of Shanghai for these new treaties and agreements of great historical significance, and cheered for the permanent and consolidated friendship between the two great powers of China and the Soviet Union. He continued: "The new Sino-Soviet treaty and agreements have greatly encouraged the struggle of the six million people of Shanghai against blockade and bombing, and for our struggle to overcome difficulties and build a new Shanghai, greatly enhancing our confidence in winning victory."

Soviet Consul General in Shanghai Speaks

Next, the Soviet Consul General in Shanghai, Vladimir, spoke. He said: "The signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance is an event of great international significance. This treaty plays a historical role in consolidating the friendship, unity and cooperation between the two great nations of China and the Soviet Union.” Then, Liu Changsheng, Director of the East China Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Director of the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions, Su Yanbin, representative of various democratic parties, Jin Zhonghua, representative of the cultural circles, Hu Juewen, representative of the business community, and Guo Huaruo, representative of the People's Liberation Army, delivered speeches, all expressing their support for the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance and other agreements. The meeting proceeded from beginning to end in an unprecedentedly warm and joyful atmosphere. The audience constantly applauded and cheered, "Long live Sino-Soviet friendship and cooperation!" "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live Marshal Stalin!" The meeting unanimously adopted a telegram of respect to Marshal Stalin and Chairman Mao, and finally screened the famous Soviet film "The Great Turning Point."

### 全国同庆中苏缔约  感谢苏联真诚援助  沪各界盛会庆祝  中苏新条约新协定签订的喜讯增强反封锁反轰炸的胜利信心

1950-02-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国同庆中苏缔约  感谢苏联真诚援助
    沪各界盛会庆祝
    中苏新条约新协定签订的喜讯增强反封锁反轰炸的胜利信心
    【新华社上海十六日电】华东暨上海市各界人民代表，于十五日晚举行庆祝大会，祝贺中苏签订友好同盟互助条约及其他协定。出席饶漱石、陈毅、刘晓、刘长胜、张鼎丞、陈绍宽、潘汉年、盛丕华、郭化若、陈望道、李昌、章蕴、陈建晨、申葆文、苏延宾、谢仁冰、胡厥文、荣毅仁、潘震亚等各界代表近二千人。苏联驻上海总领事符拉基米洛夫、副总领事维里夏金、苏侨协会会长齐布诺夫斯基等苏联友人四十余人亦应邀参加。大会在军乐队齐奏中苏两国国歌和欢呼声中开始。
    饶漱石讲话
    主席中苏友好协会上海分会副会长潘汉年致开会词后，华东军政委员会饶漱石主席首起讲话，他说：特别是我们正在与美帝国主义及其走狗蒋匪残余势力的封锁轰炸进行艰苦斗争的华东与上海人民，在听到中苏签订友好互助同盟条约及其他协定的消息后，感到无比的兴奋。饶主席指出：“中苏友好同盟互助条约及其他协定，巩固了中国人民与苏联人民之间的传统友好的历史关系，使帝国主义和国民党反动派挑拨离间、破坏中苏人民团结的阴谋遭到彻头彻尾的失败。中苏友好同盟互助条约的基本出发点，是为了巩固和平，反对侵略。这是占全世界三分之一的人口的友好互助的兄弟同盟。中苏两国兄弟同盟，本身就是一种伟大无敌的力量。”
    陈毅讲话
    陈毅市长继起讲话，他首先代表全上海人民表示拥护这些具有伟大历史意义的新条约和新协定，并为中苏两大国的永久巩固的友谊而欢呼。他继称：“中苏的新条约，新协定，对于我们上海六百万人民反对封锁、反对轰炸的斗争，对于我们克服困难、建设新上海的斗争，具有极大的鼓励作用，大大增强了我们争取胜利的信心”。
    苏联驻沪总领事讲话
    接着，由苏联驻上海总领事符拉基米洛夫讲话。他说：“签订中苏友好同盟互助条约是具有重大国际意义的事件。这个条约对于巩固中苏两大民族之间的友好、团结和合作，起着历史的作用。”继由中华全国总工会华东办事处主任兼上海总工会主任刘长胜、各民主党派代表苏延宾、文化界代表金仲华、工商界代表胡厥文、人民解放军代表郭化若等致词，他们一致表示拥护中苏友好同盟互助条约及其他协定的签订。大会自始至终在空前热烈欢欣的气氛中进行，全场不断地鼓掌欢呼，“中苏友好合作万岁！”“毛主席万岁！”“斯大林大元帅万岁！”大会一致通过了向斯大林大元帅及毛主席致敬电，最后放映苏联著名影片“伟大的转折点”。
