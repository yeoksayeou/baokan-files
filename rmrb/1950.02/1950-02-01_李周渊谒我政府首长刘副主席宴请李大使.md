Title: Li Zhouyuan Calls on Our Government Leaders; Vice Chairman Liu Hosts Banquet for Ambassador Li
Author:
Date: 1950-02-01
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 31st. Ambassador Li Zhouyuan of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea paid respects today to Vice Chairman Zhu De, Vice Chairman Li Jishen, Vice Chairman Zhang Lan of our Central People's Government, and Vice Premier Dong Biwu of the Government Administration Council.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 31st. Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the Central People's Government hosted a banquet for Korean Ambassador to China Li Zhouyuan and all embassy staff at 7:30 this afternoon. Those invited to attend included Vice Chairman Zhu De, Vice Chairman Li Jishen, Vice Chairman Zhang Lan of the Central People's Government, Secretary-General Lin Boqu, Vice Premier Dong Biwu of the Government Administration Council, Acting Chief of General Staff Nie Rongzhen of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Vice Minister Li Kenong of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu, Director Wang Bingnan and Deputy Director Yan Baohang of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Director Yu Xinqing of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the General Office of the Central People's Government Committee, Director Wang Zhuoru and Deputy Director Shen Ping of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

### 李周渊谒我政府首长刘副主席宴请李大使

1950-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　李周渊谒我政府首长刘副主席宴请李大使
    【新华社北京三十一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使李周渊于今日分别拜谒我中央人民政府朱德副主席、李济深副主席、张澜副主席及政务院董必武副总理。
    【新华社北京三十一日电】中央人民政府刘少奇副主席于今日下午七时半宴请朝鲜驻我国大使李周渊及全体馆员。应邀作陪者有中央人民政府朱德副主席、李济深副主席、张澜副主席，林伯渠秘书长，政务院董必武副总理，人民革命军事委员会聂荣臻代总参谋长，外交部李克农副部长、章汉夫副部长，外交部办公厅王炳南主任、阎宝航副主任，中央人民政府委员会办公厅典礼局余心清局长，外交部交际处王倬如处长、沈平副处长等。
