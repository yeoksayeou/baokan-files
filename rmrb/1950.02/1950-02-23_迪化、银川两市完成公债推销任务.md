Title: Ürümqi and Yinchuan Complete Government Bond Sales Task
Author:
Date: 1950-02-23
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, February 22nd: The cities of Ürümqi and Yinchuan have both completed their government bond sales tasks. The city of Ürümqi has completed the sale of 100,000 shares of government bonds. As of the 14th, the total number of bonds purchased by people from all walks of life in Yinchuan reached 23,953 shares, exceeding the original allocated quota by more than 1,000 shares.

### 迪化、银川两市完成公债推销任务

1950-02-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　迪化、银川两市完成公债推销任务
    【新华社西安二十二日电】迪化、银川两市都已完成公债推销任务。迪化市已完成推销公债十万分。银川市至十四日止各界认购总数已达二万三千九百五十三分，超过原配销额一千余分。
