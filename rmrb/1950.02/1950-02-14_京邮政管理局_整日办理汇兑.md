Title: Beijing Postal Administration Handles Remittances All Day Long
Author:
Date: 1950-02-14
Page: 4

　　Beijing Postal Administration
    Handles Remittances All Day Long
    [Beijing News] To facilitate people from all over the country to use postal remittances, the Beijing Postal Administration has, from today onward, not only added personnel to expedite remittance processing but also eliminated the lunch break, switching to all-day service. During the Spring Festival, all post offices in the city will be closed on February 17 (i.e., the first day of the Lunar New Year). On the 18th, 19th, and 23rd, business hours will be the same as on Sundays.

### 京邮政管理局  整日办理汇兑

1950-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　京邮政管理局
    整日办理汇兑
    【北京讯】北京邮政管理局为便利各地人民利用邮局汇款，自即日起除增添人员迅速处理兑款事务外，并取消中午休息时间，改为整日办公。春节期间，本市各邮局除于二月十七日（即旧历正月初一）一律停止营业一天外，十八、十九、二十三天营业时间，均照星期日办理。
