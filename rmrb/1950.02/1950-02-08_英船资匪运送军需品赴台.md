Title: British Ships Aid Bandits by Transporting Military Supplies to Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1950-02-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 6th—According to a Reuters dispatch from Taipei on the 30th, British merchant ships are transporting military supplies to Taiwan for use by the Kuomintang remnant bandits. These items include military barbed wire and aviation gasoline. It is also reported that large quantities of grain and fodder are being shipped to Taipei via Hong Kong.

### 英船资匪运送军需品赴台

1950-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　英船资匪运送军需品赴台
    【新华社北京六日电】据路透社台北三十日电，英国商船正在运送军需物品赴台湾供给国民党残匪使用。这些物品包括军用铁丝网和航空汽油。另悉，大批粮食及秣料亦正经由香港运往台北。
