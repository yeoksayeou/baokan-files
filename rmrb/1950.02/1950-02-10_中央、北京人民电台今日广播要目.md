Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
Author:
Date: 1950-02-10
Page: 4

    [This Newspaper] Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast to overseas Chinese on the issue of remittances today (10th) at 19:45. At 17:30, Workers' Time: "All-China Federation of Trade Unions' Notice on Publishing the 'Chinese Worker' Monthly". At 18:30, Natural Science Lecture: "Airplanes Faster Than Sound". At 18:00, First Literary Program: Lotus Drop "Close Relatives" Part 3. At 21:00, Second Literary Program: "Recitation of Pushkin's Poems". At 18:45, New Songs: "Support Chairman Mao," etc.
    [Another News Item] Beijing People's Radio at 12:30 Workers' Program: Worker Wang Youshan of the Tram Company introduces the Second Hundred Vehicle Movement of the Tram Company Repair Shop, titled "There Are No Difficulties That Cannot Be Overcome". At 13:00, Youth Program: Beijing Management College of Jiaotong University Broadcasting Group (1) Talks about "Inferiority Complex," (2) New Poetry Recitation: 1. "News that Shook the World," by Li Yin; 2. "They are Looking in One Direction," by Zang Kejia; 3. "Ode to the Soviet Union," by Tian Jian. At 16:30, Children's Program: Song recording by the children of the Ninth District Central National Primary School. At 17:00, the Normal University Affiliated High School Choir sings new songs.

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

    中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（十）日十九点四十五分关于侨汇问题向海外侨胞广播。十七点三十分职工时间：“中华全国总工会关于出版‘中国工人’月报的通知”。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：“比声音还快的飞机”。十八点第一次文艺节目：莲花落“亲骨肉”第三段。二十一点第二次文艺节目：《朗诵普希金诗歌》。十八点四十五分新歌曲：《拥护毛主席》等。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工节目：电车公司工人王友山介绍电车公司修造厂二次百辆车运动，题目《没有克服不了的困难》。十三点青年节目：交大北京管理学院广播小组（一）谈谈“自卑感”，（二）新诗朗诵：１、震动世界的新闻，作者李荫；２、他们望着一个方向，作者臧克家；３、苏联赞，作者田间。十六点三十分儿童节目：九区中心国民小学小朋友们歌咏录音。十七点师大附中歌咏团演唱新歌曲。
