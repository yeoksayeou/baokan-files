Title: First Korean Ambassador to China, Li Zhouyuan, Visits Vice Premier Huang

Author:
Date: 1950-02-02
Page: 1

　　[Beijing News] Li Zhouyuan, the first Korean Ambassador to China, paid a visit to Huang Yanpei, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government, at 9:30 yesterday morning. They had a very pleasant conversation, and Ambassador Li did not leave until 10:00.

### 朝鲜驻我国首任大使  李周渊拜谒黄副总理

1950-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜驻我国首任大使
    李周渊拜谒黄副总理
    【北京讯】朝鲜驻华首任大使李周渊于昨日上午九时三十分拜谒我中央人民政府政务院黄炎培副总理，畅谈甚欢、至十时李大使方始辞去。
