Title: Long Live Sino-Soviet Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance!
Author: Xiāo Sān
Date: 1950-02-16
Page: 3

　　Long Live Sino-Soviet Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance!
　　Xiāo Sān
　　Friends, today is truly a great day,
　　Friends, today you should be extremely happy!
　　A gospel has come from Moscow,
　　An earth-shattering piece of good news.
　　Today, whether you walk on the street,
　　Or stand on the top of a high mountain,
　　Whether you sail on the sea,
　　Or fly in the clouds,
　　You will hear and see a few large characters,
　　China—Soviet Union—Friendship—Mutual Assistance—Alliance!
　　Friends, “China” and “Soviet Union” written together,
　　Already show a great, magnificent strength.
　　Now, adding “Friendship” below,
　　And adding “Alliance,”
　　And adding “Mutual Assistance”—
　　Friends, you think, on this earth,
　　Is there any sound louder than them?!
　　Is there any color more brilliant than them?!
　　This treaty radiates boundless light,
　　This treaty emits a thunderous roar.
　　This sound makes the enemy embarrassed, crying, and trembling!
　　This sound makes us rejoice, be inspired, and sing!
　　This light illuminates the entire East, the entire world!
　　This light shines on all the dead demons, ghosts, and monsters!
　　One-fourth of humanity,
　　The Chinese people have stood up!
　　One-third of humanity,
　　The people of China and the Soviet Union have joined hands!
　　The world peace camp is now invincible!
　　The security of the Far East has even more solid protection.
　　All the vermin provoking aggressive war
　　Will be destroyed and die before this camp!
　　Japanese imperialists and their allies, listen:
　　Dare you provoke the Great Authority again?!
　　Wall Street's big-bellied capitalists, listen:
　　Look at you preparing for a new war, going crazy!
　　All the stupid bugs provoking Sino-Soviet relations, listen:
　　What rumors are you still making, what tricks are you playing?
　　The Sino-Soviet treaty is like a club,
　　Giving you a fatal blow head-on!
　　All good-hearted people who do not understand the Soviet Union,
　　What doubts do you still have today?
　　The Soviet Union treats China with such warmhearted selflessness,
　　The two are like close brothers in a family.
　　How many treaties humiliating and sacrificing our sovereignty have we signed?
　　Which one can compare with this Sino-Soviet treaty?
　　The deep friendship between our two great nations, which has always existed,
　　Today has reached its peak, entering a new century.
　　Today, every stone on the ground is singing.
　　The Chinese people and all of humanity understand
　　The great morality of the Soviet Union.
　　We thank Marshal Stalin!
　　We thank Chairman Mao Zedong!
　　Long live Sino-Soviet friendship!
　　Long live the Sino-Soviet alliance!
　　Long live Sino-Soviet mutual assistance!

### 中苏友好同盟互助万岁！
萧三
1950-02-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　中苏友好同盟互助万岁！
    萧三
    朋友，今天真是个大好日子，
    朋友，今天你该万分高兴！
    从莫斯科传来了一个福音，
    一个天大的喜信。
    今天无论你走在大街之上，
    或是站在高山之巅，
    无论你航行在海面，
    或者飞行在云中，
    你将听见和看见几个大字，
    中苏——友好——互助——同盟！
    朋友，“中国”和“苏联”写在一道，
    已经显出一股伟大、雄壮的力量。
    如今下面加上“友好”
      再加上“同盟”
    再加上“互助”——
    朋友，你想，在这地面上
    还有什么声音比它们响亮？！
    还有什么色彩比它们辉煌？！
    这条约放射出万丈光芒，
    这条约发出雷霆般的巨响。
    这响声使敌人狼狈，哭泣发抖！
    这响声使我们欢欣鼓舞歌唱！
    这光芒照明了整个东方，整个世界！
    这光芒照得死妖魔鬼怪魑魅魍魉！
    占人类四分之一的
    中国人站立起来了！
    占人类三分之一的
    中苏两国人携起手来了！
    世界和平阵营从此无敌于天下！
    远东的安全更有了结实的保障。
    挑拨侵略战争的一切虫豸
    都要在这个阵营前面毁灭死亡！
    日本帝国主义及其同盟者听者：
    你们敢再在太岁头上动土？！
    华尔街的大肚皮资本家听者：
    看你们准备新的大战发狂！
    一切挑拨中苏关系的笨虫听者：
    你还造什么谣言，施什么诡计？
    中苏新约就如同一根棒子，
    迎头给你一个致命的打击！
    一切不了解苏联的好心人儿，
    今天你们还有什么怀疑？
    苏联对待中国这样热忱无私，
    两个就像一家亲密的兄弟。
    我们订过多少丧权辱国的条约？
    那一个能和这个中苏新约比拟？
    我们两大民族自来就有的深厚友谊，
    今天到达了最高峰，进入了新世纪。
    今天地面上的每个石头都唱起歌来了。
    中国人和全人类都领会
    苏联伟大的道义。
    我们感谢斯大林大元帅！
    我们感谢毛泽东主席！
    中苏友好万岁！
    中苏同盟万岁！
    中苏互助万岁！
