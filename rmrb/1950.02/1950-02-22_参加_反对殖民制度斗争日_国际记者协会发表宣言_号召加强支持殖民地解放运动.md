Title: Participating in "Day of Struggle Against Colonialism" International Association of Journalists Issues Declaration Calling for Increased Support for Colonial Liberation Movements
Author:
Date: 1950-02-22
Page: 4
　　
    Participating in "Day of Struggle Against Colonialism"
    International Association of Journalists Issues Declaration
    Calling for Increased Support for Colonial Liberation Movements
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 21st] Polish News Agency, Prague, February 20th: The Secretariat of the International Association of Journalists has issued a declaration announcing the organization's participation in commemorating "Day of Struggle Against Colonialism." The declaration calls on all progressive journalists to strengthen their support for the national liberation movements in colonial countries.

### 参加“反对殖民制度斗争日”  国际记者协会发表宣言  号召加强支持殖民地解放运动

1950-02-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　参加“反对殖民制度斗争日”
    国际记者协会发表宣言
    号召加强支持殖民地解放运动
    【新华社北京二十一日电】波兰通讯社布拉格二十日讯：国际新闻记者协会秘书处顷发表宣言，宣布该组织参加纪念“反对殖民制度斗争日”。宣言号召一切进步新闻记者加强他们对于殖民地国家民族解放运动的支持。
