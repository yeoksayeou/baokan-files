Title: US Unemployment Surges by One Million; New York Stock Prices Fall Again
Author:
Date: 1950-02-11
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th: US unemployment has surged, and New York stock prices have fallen for the second time. According to a Reuters dispatch from Washington on the 6th, US Secretary of Commerce Charles Sawyer announced that the number of unemployed in the United States reached 4.48 million in January, an increase of nearly one million compared to November of last year (the official unemployment figure announced last November was 3.49 million). New York stock prices continued to fall on the 6th and 7th, following the crash on the 12th of last month. According to a United Press dispatch on the 7th, out of the 1,123 stocks traded, 612 declined. Sloan and Wilson, the bosses of General Motors, had recently believed that the company's automobile production could be increased this year. However, it was General Motors' stock that led the decline in this stock market downturn.

### 美失业者激增百万  纽约股票价格再跌

1950-02-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　美失业者激增百万  纽约股票价格再跌
    【新华社北京十日电】美国失业人数激增，纽约股票价格第二度下跌。据路透社华盛顿六日消息，美国商务部长沙逸宣布，一月份美国失业人数达四百四十八万人，较去年十一月份增加近一百万人（按去年十一月官方宣布的失业数字为三百四十九万）。纽约股票价格继上月十二日暴跌之后，六、七两日又连续下落。据合众社七日讯，在成交的一一二三种股票中，有六一二种均告下跌。通用汽车公司的老板史隆和威尔逊，不久以前曾认为今年该公司的汽车可以增产。但在这次股票下落中领先的，正是通用汽车公司的股票。
