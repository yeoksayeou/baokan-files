Title: Indian President Prasad Cables Thanks to Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1950-02-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 5th: Indian Republic President Rajendra Prasad telegraphed a reply to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of our country to express his thanks. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To His Excellency General Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

I thank Your Excellency for the congratulatory telegram on behalf of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people on the occasion of the founding of the Republic of India. India looks forward to continuing and strengthening the friendly relations that have existed for countless centuries between India and its great neighbor, China.

Rajendra Prasad
President of the Republic of India
Sent from New Delhi at 22:30 on February 2, 1950

### 印度总统普拉沙德电谢毛主席

1950-02-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　印度总统普拉沙德
    电谢毛主席
    【新华社北京五日电】印度共和国总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德电复我国中央人民政府毛泽东主席致谢。电文如下：
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东将军阁下：
    我感谢阁下在印度共和国成立之际代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府和中国人民的贺电。印度期待继续并加强印度与它的伟大邻邦中国之间存在了无数世纪的友好关系。
            印度共和国总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德
            一九五○年二月二日二十二时三十分于新德里发
