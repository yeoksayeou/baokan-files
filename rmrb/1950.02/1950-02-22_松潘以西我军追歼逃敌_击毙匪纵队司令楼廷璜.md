Title: Our Army Pursues and Annihilates Escaping Enemies West of Songpan, Killing Bandit Column Commander Lou Tinghuang
Author:
Date: 1950-02-22
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, 20th: A certain unit of the People's Liberation Army that liberated Songpan pursued and annihilated the fleeing remnants. On the 10th of this month, in the areas of Zhongzhai, Maoniugou, and Yangjiaotang in western Songpan, they annihilated a portion of the bandit "Sui Jing" (Pacification Command) Assault Column of the Sichuan-Shaanxi-Gansu Border Region and one of its entire regiments. A total of more than 60 bandits and special agents, including the bandit chieftain and commander of the fake "Sui Jing" Assault Column of the Sichuan-Shaanxi-Gansu Border Region, Lou Tinghuang, were killed. More than 193 bandits, including the regiment commander, division chief of staff, and acting director of political work, were captured.

### 松潘以西我军追歼逃敌  击毙匪纵队司令楼廷璜

1950-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　松潘以西我军追歼逃敌  击毙匪纵队司令楼廷璜
    【新华社成都二十日电】解放松潘的人民解放军某部，追歼西逃残敌，于本月十日在松潘西部中砦、毛牛沟、羊角塘等地区歼匪川陕甘边区绥署突击纵队一部及其一个团全部，共击毙胡匪特务头子伪川陕甘边区绥署突击纵队司令楼廷璜以下六十余人，俘匪师参谋长、代理政工主任及团长以下一百九十三人。
