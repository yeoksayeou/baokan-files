Title: Domestic Postage to be Adjusted Starting Today
Author:
Date: 1950-02-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 31st: The Postal Administration of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government has been ordered to adjust domestic postage rates starting February 1st as follows:

　　1. Domestic postage for each 20 grams of ordinary letter is 800 yuan, single registration is 3,200 yuan, double registration is 5,600 yuan, ordinary express is 1,600 yuan, registered express is 4,000 yuan, postcard is 400 yuan, and news稿件 (manuscripts/copy) is 640 yuan. Postage in the Northeast regions will not be adjusted for the time being.

　　2. Fees for inquiries, re-direction, poste restante, and reissue, etc., will be adjusted according to the original proportional rate.

### 国内邮资今起调整

1950-02-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　国内邮资今起调整
    【新华社北京三十一日电】中央人民政府邮电部邮政总局顷奉令自二月一日起调整国内邮资如下：
    一、国内互寄每二十公分平信八百元，单挂号三千二百元，双挂号五千六百元，平常快递一千六百元，挂号快递四千元，明信片四百元，新闻稿件六百四十元。在东北各区交寄者暂不调整。
    二、查询、改寄、存局候领、补领手续费等均按原数比例调整。
