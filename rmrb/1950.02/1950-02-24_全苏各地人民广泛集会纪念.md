Title: Mass Rallies Held Across the Soviet Union to Commemorate

Author:

Date: 1950-02-24
Page: 1

    Mass Rallies Held Across the Soviet Union to Commemorate
    On the 22nd, celebration rallies commemorating the 32nd anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Armed Forces were also held in many enterprises, workers' clubs, and educational institutions in the capital. News of the Soviet people's widespread celebrations of the 32nd anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army has been pouring in from all over the country. Veteran soldiers of the Great Patriotic War, participants in the defense of Stalingrad, and soldiers who participated in the assault on Berlin, recounted stories of past wars at cultural palaces, election propaganda centers in various electoral districts, and worker rallies in Leningrad, Kiev, Baku, Tbilisi, and other places. In addition, exhibitions were set up to illustrate the combat path and brilliant victories of the Soviet Army. Cinemas screened the best Soviet films depicting the heroism and fortitude of the Soviet people, such as "The Battle of Stalingrad," "The Third Blow," and "The Fall of Berlin."

### 全苏各地人民广泛集会纪念

1950-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    全苏各地人民广泛集会纪念
    二十二日，在首都的许多企业和工人俱乐部以及教育机关中，也举行了苏联武装部队建立三十二周年纪念的庆祝大会。全国各地纷纷传来苏联人民广泛庆祝苏军建立三十二周年纪念的消息。伟大的爱国战争中的老战士和参加斯大林格勒保卫战及参加猛攻柏林的战士们，在文化宫、在各选区的选举宣传中心站，在列宁格勒、基辅、巴库、弟比利斯及其他各地的工人集会上讲述往日战争的故事。此外，还布置了展览会，说明苏军所走过的战斗道路和所获得的辉煌胜利。电影院都放映描写苏联人民的英勇和刚强的苏联最佳影片，如“斯大林格勒之战”、“第三次攻击”、“攻克柏林”等等。
