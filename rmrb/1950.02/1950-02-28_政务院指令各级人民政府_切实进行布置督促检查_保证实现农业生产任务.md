Title: The State Council Directs All Levels of People's Government to Conscientiously Arrange, Supervise, Inspect, and Ensure the Completion of Agricultural Production Tasks
Author:
Date: 1950-02-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 27th - The State Council of the Central People's Government, Premier Zhou Enlai, issued a directive on February 27th regarding the implementation of the Ministry of Agriculture's 1950 agricultural production policy and grain and cotton production increase plan. The full text is as follows:

　　The State Council of the Central People's Government has approved the instructions of the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government regarding the 1950 agricultural production policy and grain and cotton production increase plan. The State Council requires all levels of people's government to conscientiously arrange, supervise, and inspect this year's agricultural production based on these instructions and in accordance with local conditions, to ensure the completion of the 1950 agricultural production tasks.
          Premier Zhou Enlai, February 27, 1950

### 政务院指令各级人民政府  切实进行布置督促检查  保证实现农业生产任务

1950-02-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院指令各级人民政府
    切实进行布置督促检查  保证实现农业生产任务
    【新华社北京二十七日电】中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理二月二十七日发布关于实施农业部一九五○年农业生产方针及粮棉增产计划的指令，全文如下：
    中央人民政府政务院已经批准中央人民政府农业部关于一九五○年农业生产方针及粮棉增产计划的指示。政务院要求各级人民政府根据这一指示，并按照当地具体情况，切实地进行今年农业生产的布置、督促和检查，以保证实现一九五○年的农业生产任务。
          总理  周恩来  一九五○年二月二十七日
