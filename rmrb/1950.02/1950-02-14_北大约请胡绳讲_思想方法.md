Title: Peking University Invites Hu Sheng to Lecture on "Methods of Thinking"
Author:
Date: 1950-02-14
Page: 3

　　Peking University Invites Hu Sheng to Lecture on "Methods of Thinking"
    [This newspaper's dispatch] The Peking University Student Union, the Peking University Committee of the Communist Youth League, and the Peking University Trade Union are jointly holding a systematic winter vacation lecture series. They have specially invited Hu Sheng to lecture on methods of thinking at 2:00 PM on the 16th in the auditorium of the Third Courtyard of Peking University, and Ai Siqi to lecture on the revolutionary outlook on life at 2:00 PM on the 20th in the auditorium of the Third Courtyard.
                                              (Peking University Correspondence Group)

### 北大约请胡绳讲“思想方法”

1950-02-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　北大约请胡绳讲“思想方法”
    【本报讯】北大学生会、青年团北大团委会、北大工会联合举办的寒假系统演讲，刻约请胡绳于十六日下午二时在北大三院礼堂讲思想方法，艾思奇于二十日下午二时在三院礼堂讲革命人生观。
                                              （北大通讯组）
