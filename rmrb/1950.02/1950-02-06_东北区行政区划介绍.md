Introduction to the Administrative Divisions of the Northeast Region

　　Northeast Region—The Northeast People's Government is located in Shenyang, administering six provinces: Liaodong Province, Liaoxi Province, Jilin Province, Heilongjiang Province, Songjiang Province, and Rehe Province; four municipalities directly under the central government: Shenyang City, Fushun City, Anshan City, and Benxi City; and one administrative office: Lüda Administrative Office. The provinces and administrative office are further divided into 160 counties, 21 municipalities under the jurisdiction of provinces (or the administrative office), and seven banners. The total population of the region is 41,367,846, the area is 886,957.1 square kilometers, and the cultivated land is 15,253,081 hectares.

　　Liaodong Province

　　Liaodong Province—The Provincial People's Government is located in Andong City. It administers 28 counties and five municipalities directly under the province. The total population of the province is 8,587,666, the area is 102,992.9 square kilometers, and the cultivated land is 2,003,310 hectares.

　　28 counties—Liaoyang County, Haicheng County, Gaiping County, Fuxian County, Zhuanghe County, Xinjin County, Xiuyan County, Yingkou County, Andong County, Fengcheng County, Kuandian County, Benxi County, Fushun County, Xi'an County, Hailong County, Dongfeng County, Xifeng County, Huanren County, Xinbin County, Qingyuan County, Tonghua County, Linjiang County, Huinan County, Liuhe County, Ji'an County, Fusong County, Changbai County, Jingyu County.

　　Five municipalities directly under the province—Andong City, Yingkou City, Xi'an City, Tonghua City, Liaoyang City.

　　Liaoxi Province

　　Liaoxi Province—The Provincial People's Government is located in Jinzhou City. It administers 21 counties and four municipalities directly under the province. The total population of the province is 7,391,492, the area is 56,071.9 square kilometers, and the cultivated land is 2,566,495 hectares.

　　21 counties—Jin County, Xinmin County, Heishan County, Lishu County, Tieling County, Liaozhong County, Changtu County, Kaiyuan County, Jinxi County, Suizhong County, Fuxin County, Yixian County, Beizhen County, Faku County, Xingcheng County, Panshan County, Tai'an County, Zhangwu County, Changbei County, Kangping County, Shuangliao County.

　　Four municipalities directly under the province—Jinzhou City, Siping City, Fuxin City, Shanhaiguan City.

　　Jilin Province

　　Jilin Province—The Provincial People's Government is located in Jilin City. It administers 22 counties, two municipalities directly under the province, and one banner. The total population of the province is 6,935,671, the area is 119,496.4 square kilometers, and the cultivated land is 2,965,826 hectares.

　　22 counties—Yushu County, Yongji County, Changchun County, Huaide County, Fuyu County, Jiutai County, Nong'an County, Yanji County, Dehui County, Shulan County, Yitong County, Panshi County, Shuangyang County, Jiaohe County, Changling County, Helong County, Dunhua County, Huadian County, Wangqing County, Hunchun County, Qian'an County, Antu County.

　　Two municipalities directly under the province—Changchun City, Jilin City.

　　One banner—Guoqian Banner.

　　Heilongjiang Province

　　Heilongjiang Province—The Provincial People's Government is located in Qiqihar City. It administers 39 counties, one municipality directly under the province, and two banners. The total population of the province is 5,521,581, the area is 282,408.6 square kilometers, and the cultivated land is 3,854,900 hectares.

　　39 counties—Suihua County, Hailun County, Zhaodong County, Zhaozhou County, Longjiang County, Nehe County, Tao'an County, Wangkui County, Keshan County, Tai'an County (formerly Yi'an County), Qinggang County, Baiquan County, Qing'an County (formerly Qingcheng County), Lanxi County, Kedong County, Bei'an County, Mingshui County, Anda County, Tailai County, Zhenlai County (merger of Laibei and Zhendong counties, Laibei being a newly established county after the liberation), Taonan County, Lindian County, Fuyu County (merger with Yikeming'an Banner), Nenjiang County, Gannan County, Jingxing County, Zhanyu County, Kaitong County, Anguang County, Dalai County, Tongbei County, Tieli County, Suiling County, Dedu County, Foshan County, Aihui County, Sunwu County, Huma County (merger of Oupu, Mohe, and Huma counties), Xunke County (merger of Wuyun, Qike, and Xunhe counties).

　　One municipality directly under the province—Qiqihar City.

　　Two banners—Guohou Banner, Du'erbert Banner.

　　Songjiang Province

　　Songjiang Province—The Provincial People's Government is located in Harbin City. It administers 31 counties and four municipalities directly under the province. The total population of the province is 5,149,909, the area is 205,294.2 square kilometers, and the cultivated land is 2,241,660 hectares.

　　31 counties—Shuangcheng County, Shangzhi County, Bayan County, Bin County, Acheng County, Hulan County, Wuchang County, Hailin County, Mulan County, Yanshou County, Lalin County, Ning'an County, Boli County, Jixi County, Tangyuan County, Mishan County, Huanan County, Huachuan County, Yilan County, Fujin County, Muleng County, Tonghe County, Dongning County, Fangzheng County, Jixian County, Linkou County, Baoqing County, Hulin County, Luobei County, Raohe County, Fuyuan County.

　　Four municipalities directly under the province—Harbin City, Jiamusi City, Mudanjiang City, Hegang City.

　　Rehe Province

　　Rehe Province—The Provincial People's Government is located in Chengde City. It administers 16 counties, two municipalities directly under the province, and four banners. The total population of the province is 4,662,987, the area is 113,656.3 square kilometers, and the cultivated land is 1,243,148 hectares.

　　16 counties—Chaoyang County, Chengde County, Chifeng County, Lingyuan County, Pingquan County, Jianchang County, Jianping County, Ningcheng County, Weichang County, Qinglong County, Longhua County, Beipiao County, Wudan County, Fengning County, Luanping County, Xinglong County.

　　Two municipalities directly under the province—Chengde City, Chifeng City.

　　Four banners—Karachin Banner, Aohan Banner, Kazuo Banner, Ongniud Banner.

　　Lüda Administrative Office

　　Lüda Administrative Region—The Administrative Office is located in Dalian City. It administers three counties and three cities.

　　The total population of the region is 1,054,465, the area is 3,399.0 square kilometers, and the cultivated land is 120,180 hectares.

　　Three counties—Dalian County, Jin County, Changshan County.

　　Three cities—Dalian City, Lushun City, Jinzhou City.

　　Four Municipalities Directly Under the Central Government

　　The population of Shenyang City is 1,551,317, the area is 3,151.7 square kilometers, and the cultivated land is 246,188 hectares.

　　The population of Fushun City is 23,954, the area is 91.2 square kilometers, and the cultivated land is 3,067 hectares.

　　The population of Anshan City is 185,159, the area is 360.0 square kilometers, and the cultivated land is 6,453 hectares.

　　The population of Benxi City is 9,645, the area is 34.9 square kilometers, and the cultivated land is 1,854 hectares.
