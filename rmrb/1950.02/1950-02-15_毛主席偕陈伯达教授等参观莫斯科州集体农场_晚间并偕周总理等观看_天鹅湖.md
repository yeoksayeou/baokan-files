Title: Chairman Mao Accompanied by Professor Chen Boda and Others Visits Collective Farm in Moscow Oblast; In the Evening Accompanied by Premier Zhou and Others Attends "Swan Lake"

Author:

Date: 1950-02-15
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 14th, Dispatch from TASS, Moscow: On February 13th, Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, accompanied by Professor Chen Boda, Saifuddin, Vice Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government, and accompanying personnel, visited the "Guangming" Collective Farm in the Krasnogorsk District of Moscow Oblast.

　　The collective farmers extended a warm welcome to Mao Zedong.

　　In the evening, Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, accompanied by Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Government Administration Council of the People's Republic of China, Li Fuchun, Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, Wang Jiaxiang, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, Professor Chen Boda, Saifuddin, Vice Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government, and others, attended the ballet "Swan Lake" at the State Experimental Grand Theatre.

　　Sitting in the central box with the distinguished guests were: Molotov, Voroshilov, and Mikoyan, Vice Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union; Vyshinsky, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union; Roshen, Ambassador of the Soviet Union to the People's Republic of China; and senior officials of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.

　　When the distinguished guests appeared in the theatre, they were greeted with prolonged applause.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 14th, Dispatch from TASS, Moscow: The newspapers here published on the 14th the news that Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, accompanied by the delegation of the People's Republic of China led by him, visited the "Guangming" Collective Farm in Moscow Oblast and attended the ballet "Swan Lake" at the Grand Theatre.

### 毛主席偕陈伯达教授等参观莫斯科州集体农场  晚间并偕周总理等观看“天鹅湖”

1950-02-15
第2版()
专栏：

　　毛主席偕陈伯达教授等参观莫斯科州集体农场
    晚间并偕周总理等观看“天鹅湖”
    【新华社北京十四日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：二月十三日，中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东，偕同陈伯达教授、新疆省人民政府副主席赛福鼎及随行人员，参观莫斯科州克拉斯诺哥尔斯克区的“光”集体农场。
    集体农民们对毛泽东报以热诚的欢迎。
    晚间，中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东，偕同中华人民共和国政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来、东北人民政府副主席李富春、中华人民共和国驻苏大使王稼祥、陈伯达教授、新疆省人民政府副主席赛福鼎及其他人等在国立实验大戏院观看巴莱舞：“天鹅湖”。
    与贵宾们同坐在中央包厢中的有：苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫、伏罗希洛夫、米高扬，苏联外交部长维辛斯基，苏联驻中华人民共和国大使罗申以及苏联外交部的高级官员。
    当贵宾们出现于剧场时，受到长时间的鼓掌欢迎。
    【新华社北京十四日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：此间各报十四日刊载中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东，曾偕其所率领的中华人民共和国代表团访问莫斯科州“光”集体农场并在大剧院观巴莱舞：“天鹅湖”的新闻。
