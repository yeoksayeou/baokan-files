Title: Xinhua News Agency Important Correction
Author:
Date: 1950-02-17
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency Important Correction
    In the second paragraph of the "Speech by Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky at the Signing Ceremony" issued by this agency on the 15th, "This is what the leader of the Soviet people, Stalin, far back in 1929..." "1929" should be "1925."
    In the third paragraph of the draft "Speech by Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai at the Signing Ceremony," "Regarding the Soviet government's gratuitous transfer of property acquired in Northeast China from Japanese owners, as well as the former so-called barracks properties in Beijing and the exchange of notes with the Chinese government," "and the exchange of notes with the Chinese government" should be "to the Chinese government." (Note: Both of the above drafts were published on the third page of this newspaper on the 15th.)

### 新华社重要更正

1950-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　新华社重要更正
    本社十五日所发“苏联外交部部长维辛斯基在签字式上的演说”第二段中“这就是苏联人民的领袖斯大林远在一九二九年………”“一九二九年”应为“一九二五年”。
    “周恩来总理兼外长在签字式上的演说”稿第三段中“关于苏联政府无偿地移交在东北从日本所有者手中获得的财产以及在北京的过去所谓兵营的房产及中国政府的换文”“及中国政府的换文”应为“给中国政府的换文”。（按：以上两稿本报均载于十五日第三版。）
