Title: Romanian Ambassador to China, Rudenko, Arrives in Beijing Yesterday; Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu and Others Welcomed Him at the Station
Author:
Date: 1950-02-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th – Rudenko, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Romanian People's Republic to the People's Republic of China, accompanied by his wife and a delegation of over ten people, arrived in Beijing at 6:15 this morning. They were escorted by Zeng Yuanhui, Director of the Liaison Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in Manchuria. Those who welcomed them at the station included Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office; Yan Baohang, Deputy Director; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; Wu Han, Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; Wang Changjiang, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command; as well as Bi Luoqi, Chargé d'Affaires of the Polish Embassy; Chikhvin, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy; Lieutenant Colonel Fomink, Deputy Military Attaché; Choi Young, Counselor of the Korean Embassy; Kim Kuk-bo, Counselor; Feikert, Counselor of the Hungarian Legation; and Luo Dai, Secretary of the Czechoslovak Embassy.

### 罗马尼亚驻我国大使  鲁登科昨日抵京  章汉夫副外长等赴站欢迎

1950-02-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚驻我国大使
    鲁登科昨日抵京
    章汉夫副外长等赴站欢迎
    【新华社北京十九日电】罗马尼亚人民共和国首任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使鲁登科夫妇一行十余人于今晨六时十五分由外交部驻满洲里联络处曾远辉处长陪同抵京，到站欢迎者有我中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫，办公厅主任王炳南，副主任阎宝航，交际处处长王倬如，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，京津卫戍司令部副参谋长王长江及波兰大使馆代办毕罗奇，苏联大使馆参事齐赫文，副武官富敏克中校，朝鲜大使馆参事崔英，参事金国辅，匈牙利公使馆参事费凯尔特，捷克大使馆秘书罗戴等。
