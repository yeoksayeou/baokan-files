Title: The 19th Government Affairs Council Meeting of the Government Administration Council: Minister Xie and Others Report on Production and Disaster Relief; Decision Made to Allocate Relief Grain to the Provinces of Pingyuan and Hebei
Author:
Date: 1950-02-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th -- The Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government held its 19th Government Affairs Council meeting at 3:00 PM today. At the meeting, Ma Yinchu, Vice Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee, reported on the establishment of the committee. Zeng Shan, Vice Chairman, reported on the production and disaster relief, the collection of public grain, the promotion of the People's Victory Discount Public Bonds, and the recent bombing of Shanghai by Kuomintang bandit planes in the East China region. After listening to the report of Xie Juezai, Minister of the Interior of the Central People's Government, on the current work of production and disaster relief, and the report of Du Yuwen, member of the People's Government of Pingyuan Province, on the disaster situation in the province, the meeting made specific decisions on the allocation of emergency relief grain to Pingyuan Province and relief grain to Pingyuan and Hebei provinces. Nan Hanchen, Deputy Head of the East China Working Group of the Government Administration Council's Guidance on Reception Work Committee, reported to the meeting on the work of the group. The work of the group has been successfully completed. The meeting approved the appointment of Cai Yi as Deputy Director of the Third Department of the People's Supervision Committee of the Government Administration Council; Jiang Yankun and Zhao Mingfu as Deputy Directors of the Yellow River Water Conservancy Committee; Mo Wenhua as Mayor of Nanning City, Guangxi Province; Zheng Tianxiang as Mayor of Baotou City, Suiyuan Province, and Wang Jinquan as Deputy Mayor.

### 政务院第十九次政务会议  谢部长等报告生产救灾  决定拨发平原河北两省救济粮

1950-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院第十九次政务会议
    谢部长等报告生产救灾   
    决定拨发平原河北两省救济粮
    【新华社北京十日电】中央人民政府政务院今日下午三时举行第十九次政务会议。会上，华东军政委员会马寅初副主席作了该会成立经过的报告，曾山副主席作了关于华东区生产救灾、征收公粮、推销人民胜利折实公债和最近国民党匪机轰炸上海等情形的报告。会议在听取了中央人民政府内务部部长谢觉哉关于目前生产救灾工作的报告以及平原省人民政府委员杜毓澐关于该省灾情的报告之后，对拨发平原省急赈粮及平原、河北两省救济粮的问题作了具体决定。政务院指导接收工作委员会华东区工作团副团长南汉宸向会议作了关于该团工作的报告。该团工作任务已顺利完成。会议通过任命蔡毅为政务院人民监察委员会第三厅副厅长；江衍坤、赵明甫为黄河水利委员会副主任；莫文骅为广西省南宁市人民政府市长；郑天翔为绥远省包头市人民政府市长，王金泉为副市长。
