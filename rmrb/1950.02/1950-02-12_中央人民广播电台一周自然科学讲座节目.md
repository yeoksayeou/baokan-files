Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station Weekly Natural Science Lecture Program
Author:
Date: 1950-02-12
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 11th. The Central People's Broadcasting Station's natural science lecture program from the 12th to the 15th is as follows: 12th, The Story of Pavlov (Introduction to Soviet Scientists) (1). 13th, Same as above (2). 14th, Magnetic Effect of Electric Current and Electric Bell Telegraph (General Knowledge of Electricity, Lecture 15) (Guo Yizeng). 15th, Direct Current and Alternating Current (General Knowledge of Electricity, Lecture 16) (Xu Xurong). The broadcast time is from 18:30 to 18:45 every day.

### 中央人民广播电台一周自然科学讲座节目

1950-02-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央人民广播电台一周自然科学讲座节目
    【新华社北京十一日电】中央人民广播电台十二日到十五日的自然科学讲座节目如下：十二日，巴夫洛夫的故事（苏联科学家介绍）（一）。十三日，同上（二）。十四日，电流的磁效应和电铃电报（电学常识第十五讲）（郭沂曾）。十五日，直流电和交流电（电学常识第十六讲）（徐叙瑢）。播送时间均在每天十八点三十分到四十五分。
