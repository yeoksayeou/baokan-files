Title: Mongolian Laboring People Rally to Warmly Welcome the Sino-Soviet Treaty
Author:
Date: 1950-02-22
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 21st: According to TASS, Ulaanbaatar, February 21st: The working people of the Mongolian People's Republic are welcoming the news of the conclusion of the Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance between the Soviet Union and People’s Republic Of China and various agreements with joy. In the capital, Ulaanbaatar, many rallies have been held. Participants warmly welcomed the Sino-Soviet Treaty and various agreements, and emphasized that the treaty and various agreements are the most important contribution to consolidating the eternal and invincible friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people and strengthening world peace and democracy. Moreover, it fully guarantees the independence and autonomy of the free state of the Mongolian People’s Republic, which fully conforms to the vital interests of all Mongolian people.

### 蒙古劳动人民集会热烈欢迎中苏条约

1950-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒙古劳动人民集会热烈欢迎中苏条约
    【新华社北京二十一日电】据塔斯社乌兰巴托尔二十一日讯：蒙古人民共和国的劳动人民，以欢欣的心情迎接苏联与中华人民共和国缔结友好同盟互助条约及各项协定的消息。在首都乌兰巴托尔，曾经举行了许多次集会，与会者热烈地欢迎中苏条约及各项协定，并强调指出：条约与各项协定，对于巩固中苏两国人民永恒与不可战胜的友谊，对于加强全世界和平与民主是最重大的贡献，而且，还充分保证了蒙古人民共和国这一自由国家的独立与自主，这完全符合于全体蒙古人民的切身利益。
