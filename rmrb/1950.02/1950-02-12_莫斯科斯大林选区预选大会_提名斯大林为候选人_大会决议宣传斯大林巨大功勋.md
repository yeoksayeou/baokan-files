Title: Moscow Stalin Electoral District Preliminary Meeting Nominates Stalin as Candidate; Meeting Resolution Publicizes Stalin's Great Achievements
Author:
Date: 1950-02-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 11th: TASS, Moscow: The preliminary meeting of 26,000 workers, engineers, and staff representatives from the Stalin District and Bauman District (the two districts combined to form the Stalin Electoral District) in Moscow was held on the 7th. The meeting elected the members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party, headed by Stalin, as the honorary presidium. Alexeyev, a foreman at a machine manufacturing plant, announced that the meetings held in various enterprises in the electoral district had unanimously decided to nominate the leader of the Soviet people, Stalin, as the candidate of the Stalin Electoral District in Moscow for the Soviet Supreme Soviet Union Council. After the announcement, the entire venue stood up and applauded and cheered for Stalin for a long time. The meeting then passed a resolution nominating Stalin as a candidate representative to the Soviet Supreme Soviet Union Council. It also passed a letter to Stalin, requesting him to agree to be a candidate representative of the Stalin Electoral District to the Soviet Supreme Soviet. The meeting also passed a list of election propagandists, requiring them to publicize Stalin's life and activities, his unparalleled loyalty to Lenin's cause, his great contributions to the Soviet people in the struggle for communism, his merits to the working people of the world, and his struggle to fight for peace and against warmongers, to all voters.

### 莫斯科斯大林选区预选大会  提名斯大林为候选人  大会决议宣传斯大林巨大功勋

1950-02-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　莫斯科斯大林选区预选大会
    提名斯大林为候选人   
    大会决议宣传斯大林巨大功勋
    【新华社北京十一日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：莫斯科斯大林区与包曼区（两区合组成斯大林选区）二万六千名工人、工程师及职员的代表，七日举行预选大会。大会选出以斯大林为首的苏联共产党中央委员会政治局委员为名誉主席团。机器制造厂领班阿列克舍耶夫宣布：选区各企业中所举行的会议，已一致决定提名苏联人民领袖斯大林为莫斯科斯大林选区的苏联最高苏维埃联盟院候选人。宣布后全场起立，历久不息地为斯大林鼓掌欢呼。大会继通过提名斯大林为苏联最高苏维埃联盟院代表候选人的决议。并通过致斯大林的信，要求他同意作斯大林选区的苏联最高苏维埃代表候选人。大会又通过了选举宣传员名单，要他们把斯大林的生平与活动、他对于列宁事业无比的忠诚、在为共产主义的斗争中对苏维埃人民的巨大功勋、对于全世界劳动人民的功绩、及其争取和平反对战争贩子的斗争等，向全体选民进行宣传。
