Title: Program Adjustment of Beijing People's Radio Station
Author:
Date: 1950-02-01
Page: 4

　　Beijing People's Radio Station
    Program Adjustment
    [Our Newspaper] According to listeners' opinions, Beijing People's Broadcasting Station will adjust its programs from February 1st. The "Peking Opera" following the "Midday Program Report" from 12:00 to 12:30 noon every day will be changed to "Music." Every Thursday, from 13:00 to 13:15, a "Learning Chat" program will be added, broadcasting answers to learning questions or inviting specialists to explain relevant learning issues. The "Voice of the Municipality" time will be moved to 17:45 to 18:00 every day. The "Listener Service" program will be increased to once a day, from 18:45 to 19:00. The "Introduction to the Soviet Union and People's Democratic Countries" will be moved to Sundays from 13:00 to 13:15. In addition to the original time, the "Russian Language Lectures" will be broadcast as recordings every Tuesday and Thursday from 19:00 to 20:00.

### 北京市人民电台节目调整

1950-02-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京市人民电台
    节目调整
    【本报讯】北京市人民广播电台根据听众意见，从二月一日起调整节目。每天中午十二点到十二点三十分“报告中午节目”后的“京剧”改播“音乐”。每星期四的十三点到十三点十五分增加“学习漫谈”节目，播送关于学习问题的解答，或请专人讲解有关学习的问题。“市政之声”时间移至每天的十七点四十五分到十八点。“听众服务”节目增加为每天一次，时间在十八点四十五分到十九点。“苏联及人民民主国家介绍”移至每星期日的十三点到十三点十五分。“俄语讲座”除原有时间外，每星期二、四十九点到二十点播送录音。
