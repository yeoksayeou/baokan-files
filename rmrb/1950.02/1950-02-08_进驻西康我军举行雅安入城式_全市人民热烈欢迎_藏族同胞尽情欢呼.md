Title: Our Army Enters Xikang and Holds Entry Ceremony in Ya'an; The Entire City Warmly Welcomes Them; Tibetan Compatriots Cheer Enthusiastically

Author:

Date: 1950-02-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 7th. A unit of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Xikang solemnly held an entry ceremony into Ya'an at 12:00 noon on February 1st. 70,000 people in the city warmly welcomed the People's Liberation Army, and Tibetan compatriots also enthusiastically cheered in Tibetan in the welcoming ranks.

### 进驻西康我军举行雅安入城式  全市人民热烈欢迎·藏族同胞尽情欢呼

1950-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　进驻西康我军举行雅安入城式
    全市人民热烈欢迎·藏族同胞尽情欢呼
    【新华社重庆七日电】进驻西康的人民解放军某部，于二月一日上午十二时隆重举行进入雅安的入城式。全市七万人民热烈欢迎解放军，藏族同胞也在欢迎行列中用藏语热情欢呼。
