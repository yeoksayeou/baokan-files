Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Issues Notice Endorsing Colonial Youth Solidarity Day
Author: 
Date: 1950-02-05
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 4th: The World Federation of Trade Unions issued a notice on the 2nd of February endorsing the resolution passed by the Second World Youth Congress in September of last year, designating February 21st as Colonial Youth Solidarity Day. The notice points out that the youth of colonial countries, more than the youth anywhere else, must be the most active factor in the social struggle. The World Federation of Trade Unions strongly urges the youth of colonial countries to strengthen their unity, regardless of class, race, or nationality, and to participate more actively in the struggle for national liberation and for improving the living standards of the working people.

### 世界工联发出通知  拥护殖民地青年团结日

1950-02-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界工联发出通知
    拥护殖民地青年团结日
    【新华社北京四日电】据塔斯社巴黎讯：世界工会联合会于二日发出通知，拥护去年九月第二次世界青年代表大会通过的以二月二十一日为殖民地青年团结日的决议。通知指出：殖民地国家的青年，比其他任何地方的青年都更其必须是社会斗争中最能动的因素。世界工联力促殖民地国家的青年应不分阶级、种族或国籍，加强他们的团结，更积极地参加争取民族解放和争取改善劳动人民生活水准的斗争。
