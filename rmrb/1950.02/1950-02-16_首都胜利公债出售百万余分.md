Title: Over a Million Shares of the Capital Victory Bonds Sold
Author:
Date: 1950-02-16
Page: 4

　　The work of promoting the People's Victory Inflation-Adjusted Bonds in Beijing has largely completed its task in terms of subscriptions; as of yesterday (the 15th), a total of 1,045,203 shares have been purchased. During the Spring Festival, in order to facilitate the purchase of bonds by all sectors, the Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China and all sales outlets in the city (excluding post offices, the Bank of China, and the Bank of Communications) will remain open from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM on the 18th, 19th, and 20th to sell bonds.

### 首都胜利公债出售百万余分

1950-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　首都胜利公债出售百万余分
    【北京讯】北京市推销人民胜利折实公债的工作，在认购方面已大部分完成任务；在实际购买方面，截至昨（十五）日止共买了一百零四万五千二百零三分。春节期间，为便于各界购买公债，中国人民银行北京分行及市内各代售处（除邮局、中国银行、交通银行三处）在十八日、十九日及二十日上午十时至下午三时照常出售公债。
