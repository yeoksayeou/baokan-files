Author:
Date: 1950-02-12
Page: 4

　　Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
[This Dispatch] The Central People's Broadcasting Station today (February 12th) at 17:30 for worker's time: "Resolution on Several Issues Concerning the National Textile Workers' Union Organization." 18:30 Natural Science Lectures: "The Story of Soviet Scientist Pavlov." 18:00 First Literary Program: Xihe Dagu "The Liberation Army Crosses Little Western Heaven." 21:00 Second Literary Program: Orchestral Music. 20:30 Weekly Arts: Soviet Music and Songs. 18:45 Three new songs and music including "Singing of Chairman Mao."
[Another Dispatch] Beijing People's Radio 12:30 Worker's Program: Four dispatches: (1) Workers of Fengtai Locomotive Depot welcome locomotive No. 1939 for completing 100,000 kilometers of safe travel, (2) Cement Products Division of Fengtai Bridge Factory saved the people more than 90,000 jin of millet, (3) Workers of Qinghe Woolen Mill guarantee to complete the 1950 tasks, (4) Dispatch from North China Pottery Company: Diligent and hardworking comrades. 13:00 Introduction to the life of Russian poet Pushkin. 17:00 Vocal program: "Northeast Folk Songs." 18:45 Soviet Music.

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-02-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（十二日）十七点三十分职工时间：“关于全国纺织工会组织的几个问题的决议”。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：《苏联科学家巴夫洛夫的故事》。十八点第一次文艺节目：西河大鼓“解放军横过小西天”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：管弦乐。八点三十分星期文艺：苏联音乐和歌曲。十八点四十五分《歌唱毛主席》等三支新歌曲和音乐。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工节目：四篇通讯：（１）丰台机务段职工欢迎１９３９号机车完成十万公里安全行走，（２）丰台桥梁工厂洋灰制品所为人民节省了九万多斤小米，（３）清河制呢厂职工保证完成一九五○年任务，（４）华北窑业公司通讯：认真苦干的工友同志们。十三点介绍俄罗斯诗人普希金的生平。十七点歌咏节目：《东北民歌》。十八点四十五分苏联音乐。
