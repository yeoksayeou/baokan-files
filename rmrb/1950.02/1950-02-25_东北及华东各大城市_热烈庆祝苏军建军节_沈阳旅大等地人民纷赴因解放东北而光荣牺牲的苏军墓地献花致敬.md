Title: Major Cities in Northeast and East China Warmly Celebrate Soviet Army Day; People in Shenyang, Lüda, and Other Places Flock to Soviet Army Cemeteries to Lay Flowers and Pay Respects to the Glorious Sacrifices Made in the Liberation of Northeast China
Author: 
Date: 1950-02-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 24th: Shenyang, Lüda, Chengde, Shanghai, Nanjing, Qingdao, Xuzhou, and other places held celebrations for the 32nd anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army on the 22nd and 23rd. In addition to holding grand commemorative meetings, the people of Shenyang, Lüda, and Chengde went to the cemeteries and monuments of Soviet Army martyrs who gloriously sacrificed themselves in the liberation of Northeast China to lay flowers and pay their respects. The Victory Tower on Stalin Square in Dalian was decorated extremely solemnly and beautifully, and various organizations continuously went to lay flowers and pay their respects. In the evening, each district held a lantern parade. At the celebration held by the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, President Chen Yi called on people to learn from the Soviet Army to strengthen the quality of the People's Liberation Army, strive to liberate Taiwan as soon as possible, liberate all of China, and strengthen the defense of lasting world peace. At the meeting, a silk banner was presented to the Soviet Army, which was accepted by Deputy Consul General Viriashin of the Soviet Union in Shanghai on behalf of the Soviet Army. Representatives of various organs and groups in Nanjing, Qingdao, and Xuzhou also held celebrations.

　　Newspapers and periodicals in the above-mentioned places have published commemorative special issues. Bookstores in Lüda and Shenyang have held price reductions, and photo exhibitions of the Soviet Army have been held in Lüda and Shanghai.

### 东北及华东各大城市  热烈庆祝苏军建军节  沈阳旅大等地人民纷赴因解放东北而光荣牺牲的苏军墓地献花致敬

1950-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北及华东各大城市
    热烈庆祝苏军建军节         
    沈阳旅大等地人民纷赴因解放东北而光荣牺牲的苏军墓地献花致敬
    【新华社北京二十四日电】沈阳、旅大、承德、上海、南京、青岛、徐州等地，于二十二、二十三日纷纷举行苏联建军三十二周年纪念日的庆祝活动。沈阳、旅大、承德人民除举行了盛大的纪念会外，并赴因解放东北而光荣牺牲的苏军烈士墓地及纪念塔献花致敬。大连斯大林广场的胜利塔装饰得极为庄严美丽，各机关团体络绎不绝地前往献花致敬。晚间各区举行了提灯游行。上海中苏友协举行的庆祝会上，陈毅会长号召向苏军学习，以加强人民解放军的素质，争取早日解放台湾，解放全中国，加强保卫世界持久和平。会中并向苏军献赠锦旗，由苏联驻沪副总领事维里夏金代表接受。南京、青岛和徐州的各机关团体代表，也都分别举行了庆祝会。
    以上各地的报纸、刊物均出版了纪念专号，旅大、沈阳各书店举行减价，旅大及上海并举办了苏军照片展览会。
