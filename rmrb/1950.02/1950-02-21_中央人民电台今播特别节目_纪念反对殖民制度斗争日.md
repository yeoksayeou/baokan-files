Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Special Program Today Commemorating the Day of Struggle Against Colonialism

Author:
Date: 1950-02-21
Page: 4

　　Central People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Special Program Today
    Commemorating the Day of Struggle Against Colonialism
    [This newspaper's report] The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast a special program today (February 21st) at 20:20 to commemorate the "World Democratic Youth Day of Struggle Against Colonialism": (1) "The Origin of the Day of Struggle Against Colonialism," (2) An Open Letter to Asian Youth on the Day of Struggle Against Colonialism from the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, (3) Several Letters from Chinese Youth to Youth in Colonial Countries, (4) An Introduction to French and Dutch Youth Who are Fighting Against Colonialism. 20:35 Correspondences: (1) "The Person Sent by Marshal Stalin," (2) "Soviet People and Chinese Workers in Dalian." 17:30 Staff Time: (1) "The Great Treaty, the Great Friendship," (2) A Talk by Li Lisan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, on the Signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance and Two Other Agreements. 18:00 First Literary and Art Program: Taiping Lyrics (1) "Celebrating the New Year," (2) "Wei Bingyi Returns Home." 21:00 Second Literary and Art Program: Song Program Commemorating the World Democratic Youth Day of Struggle Against Colonialism. 18:45 New Songs: "The Iron Tree Blossoms," "Selections from the Frontline Love for the People."
    [Another report] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station, 12:30 Staff Program: Peng Qingzhao Lectures on the Third Lesson of the Workers' Political Textbook, "Communist Society." 13:00 Women's Program: Plum Blossom Drum "The Fiancée Urges Her Husband to Join the Army." 13:30 Storytelling: Ke Lan's Long Novel "The Story of the Tin Bucket." 16:30 Children's Program: (1) Current Affairs Talk, (2) Introduction to a Soviet Children's Story "The Secret of the Wooden House," (3) Singing. 17:00 New Song Practice: Teaching the Northeast Folk Song "Busy Production." 17:45 Voice of the City: Beijing Telecommunications Bureau broadcasts "How to Send a Fast, Accurate, and Money-Saving Telegram." 18:45 Yuan Hanqing lectures on "Using Exhibitions to Popularize Science."

### 中央人民电台今播特别节目  纪念反对殖民制度斗争日

1950-02-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央人民电台今播特别节目
    纪念反对殖民制度斗争日
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（二十一日）二十点二十分纪念“世界民主青年反对殖民制度斗争日”特别节目：（一）“反对殖民制度斗争日的由来”，（二）青年团中央和全国民主青联在反对殖民制度斗争日告亚洲青年书，（三）中国青年写给殖民地国家青年的几封信，（四）介绍为反对殖民制度而斗争的法国与荷兰青年。二十点三十五分通讯：（一）《斯大林大元帅派来的人》，（二）《大连的苏联人和中国工人》。十七点三十分职工时间：（一）“伟大的条约，伟大的友谊”，（二）全总副主席李立三对中苏签订友好同盟互助条约及其他两个协定的谈话。十八点第一次文艺节目：太平歌词（一）《过新年》，（二）《魏炳义回家》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：纪念世界民主青年反对殖民制度斗争日歌咏节目。十八点四十五分新歌曲：《铁树开花》、《火线爱民选曲》。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台十二点三十分职工节目：彭庆昭讲工人政治课本第三课《共产主义社会》。十三点妇女节目：梅花大鼓《未婚妻劝夫参军》。十三点三十分故事讲述：柯蓝写的长篇小说《洋铁桶的故事》。十六点三十分儿童节目：（一）时事讲话，（二）介绍一本苏联儿童故事《木板屋里的秘密》，（三）唱歌。十七点新歌练习：教唱东北民歌《生产忙》。十七点四十五分市政之声：北京电信局播讲《怎样发出一张迅速准确和省钱的电报》。十八点四十五分袁翰青讲《用展览会来普及科学》。
