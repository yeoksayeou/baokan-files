Title: Shanghai Air Defense and Public Security Committee Established
Author:
Date: 1950-02-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, February 14th: The Shanghai Air Defense and Public Security Committee was established in Shanghai on the 11th. Guo Huaruo, Commander of the Songhu Garrison, Yang Fan, Deputy Director of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government Public Security Bureau, and Cao Manzhi, Director of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government Civil Affairs Bureau, were appointed as Chairman and Vice Chairmen of the committee, respectively.

### 沪防空治安委员会成立

1950-02-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　沪防空治安委员会成立
    【新华社上海十四日电】上海市于十一日成立防空治安委员会，由淞沪警备司令部司令员郭化若、上海市人民政府公安局副局长杨帆、上海市人民政府民政局局长曹漫之分任正副主任委员。
