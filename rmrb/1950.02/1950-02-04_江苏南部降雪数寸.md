Title: Southern Jiangsu Receives Several Inches of Snow
Author:
Date: 1950-02-04
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, Wuxi, 3rd. Southern Jiangsu experienced widespread snowfall yesterday afternoon, January 31st, which continued until the morning of the 2nd. The snowfall reached approximately two to three inches, greatly benefiting the elimination of various insect pests.

### 江苏南部降雪数寸

1950-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　江苏南部降雪数寸
    【新华社无锡三日电】江苏南部各地上月三十一日下午普降瑞雪，至二日晨始停，雪量达二寸至三寸左右，对扑灭各种虫害裨益甚大。
