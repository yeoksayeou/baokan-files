Title: Vietnam Trade Union Conference Cables All-China Federation of Trade Unions to Congratulate the Victory of the Chinese People; Also Cables the French General Confederation of Labour to Salute French Workers
Author:
Date: 1950-02-11
Page: 4

　　Vietnam Trade Union Conference Cables All-China Federation of Trade Unions
    Congratulates the Victory of the Chinese People
    And Cables the French General Confederation of Labour to Salute French Workers
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 9th] Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, 8th dispatch: The First All-Vietnam Trade Union Conference, which successfully concluded in early January, sent a cable to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, warmly celebrating the historic victory of the Chinese working class and the Chinese people. The cable pointed out: "We Vietnamese workers pledge to learn from the Chinese working class and the Chinese people, strive to eliminate French imperialists, restore Vietnam's independence and unity, and make more contributions to defending world peace and democracy."
    The conference also sent a cable to the French General Confederation of Labour, hailing the relentless struggle of the French working class and people against the French reactionary government, the running dogs of American imperialism; and declared: "We Vietnamese workers pledge to cooperate closely with the French working class and the French people to strengthen our common struggle against French imperialists, defend peace, and promote democracy."

### 越工会会议电我全总  庆贺中国人民胜利  并电法总工会向法国工人致敬

1950-02-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　越工会会议电我全总
    庆贺中国人民胜利
    并电法总工会向法国工人致敬
    【新华社北京九日电】越南通讯社北越八日讯：于一月初胜利闭幕的第一届全越工会会议，曾致电中华全国总工会，热烈欢庆中国工人阶级与中国人民的历史性胜利。电文指出：“我们越南工人保证向中国工人阶级与中国人民学习，努力消灭法国帝国主义者，恢复越南的独立与统一，并对保卫世界的和平民主，作更多的供献。”
    会议同时致电法国总工会，欢呼法国的工人阶级与人民对美帝国主义的走狗法国反动政府所进行的无情斗争；并宣称：“我们越南工人保证与法国工人阶级及法国人民亲密合作，以加强我们对法国帝国主义者的共同斗争，保卫和平，促进民主。”
