Title: KMT Revolutionary Committee Central Standing Committee Passes Resolution Responding to Spring Festival Support the Army Movement; Telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu Extending Respect to the Entire Army
Author:
Date: 1950-02-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th—The Central Standing Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang passed a resolution on the 14th, responding to the Spring Festival Support the Army Movement. The resolution stipulates: (1) Send a circular to all local branches nationwide to respond to the local Spring Festival Support the Army Movement. (2) Donate funds to the Beijing branch to慰劳 (offer comfort to) the families of revolutionary martyrs and the families of soldiers and cadres. (3) Send a telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and extend respect to all commanders and fighters. The telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu and all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army stated: "As we celebrate the first Spring Festival after the founding of the People's Republic of China, the association expresses its heartfelt love and support to you with the most joyful mood, and extends the most sincere respect."

### 民革中常会通过决议  响应春节拥军运动  电朱总司令转全军致敬

1950-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　民革中常会通过决议
    响应春节拥军运动
    电朱总司令转全军致敬
    【新华社北京十六日电】中国国民党革命委员会中央常务委员会于十四日通过决议，响应春节拥军运动。决议规定：（一）通电全国各地分部响应各地春节拥军运动。（二）捐款交北京市分部，慰劳革命烈士家属和军人、干部家属分部。（三）致电中国人民解放军朱总司令并转全体指战员致敬。致朱总司令和人民解放军全体指战员的电文内称：“兹值中华人民共和国成立后的第一个春节，本会谨以最愉快的心情，向你们表示衷心的热爱和拥护，并致最崇高的敬意。”
