Title: Suzhou, Zhenjiang, Jiaxing and other cities successively complete public bond tasks
Author:
Date: 1950-02-20
Page: 4

　　Suzhou, Zhenjiang, Jiaxing and other cities
    Public bond tasks successively completed
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 17th] The public bond sales in Suzhou, Zhenjiang, Jinhua Prefecture, Jiaxing, Xuzhou, Weifang and other places in East China have been successively completed or exceeded their targets. Suzhou's 800,000 share task was fully completed on the 15th. The number of subscriptions from all sectors in Zhenjiang has reached 155,000 shares, exceeding the original quota by 15,000 shares. As of the 10th, Jinhua Prefecture's subscriptions reached 336,800 shares, exceeding the target by 52,800 shares. Jiaxing has also exceeded its 140,000 share task. As of the 14th, the total number of subscriptions in Xuzhou City was 419,976 shares, exceeding the assigned sales task of 400,000 shares. Weifang has also completed its 130,000 share task.

### 苏州镇江嘉兴等城市  公债任务相继完成

1950-02-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏州镇江嘉兴等城市
    公债任务相继完成
    【新华社北京十七日电】华东区苏州、镇江、金华专区、嘉兴、徐州、潍坊等地推销公债都已相继完成或超额完成任务。苏州八十万分任务已于十五日全部完成。镇江各界认购数字已达十五万五千分，超过原配额一万五千分。金华专区至十日止，认购数字已达三十三万六千八百分，超过任务五万二千八百分。嘉兴亦已超过十四万分的任务。徐州截至十四日止，全市认购总数为四十一万九千九百七十六分，超额完成四十万分配销任务。潍坊亦已完成十三万分任务。
