Title: Japanese Bacterial Warfare Criminals Appeal for Leniency Rejected by Soviet Supreme Court; Original Verdict of the Primorsky Military Tribunal Upheld
Author:
Date: 1950-02-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 15th: TASS, Moscow, 14th: The Military Collegium of the Supreme Court of the USSR considered the appeals filed by Japanese war criminals Yamada Otozō, Kajitsuka Ryuji, Takahashi Takaatsu, Nishi Toyohiko, Karasawa Tomio, Mitomo Katsuo, and Kikuchi Norizo, who were sentenced by the Primorsky Military Tribunal in Khabarovsk. In their appeals, the defendants confessed to the crimes for which they were convicted by the Primorsky Military Tribunal, but requested the Military Collegium of the Supreme Court of the USSR to reduce their sentences.

In view of the gravity of the defendants' crimes, and given that the punishments decided by the Primorsky Military Tribunal fully complied with the law and took into account the crimes of each individual, the Military Collegium of the Supreme Court of the USSR rejected the appeals of Yamada, Kajitsuka, Takahashi, Nishi, Karasawa, Mitomo, and Kikuchi, and upheld the original verdict of the Primorsky Military Tribunal.

### 日细菌战犯上诉要求减刑　苏最高法院已予驳回  维持滨海军区军事法庭原判

1950-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　日细菌战犯上诉要求减刑
  　苏最高法院已予驳回
    维持滨海军区军事法庭原判
    【新华社北京十五日电】塔斯社莫斯科十四日讯：苏联最高法院军事庭考虑了伯力滨海军区军事法庭所判决的日本战犯山田乙三、梶塚龙二、高桥高笃、西丰彦、唐泽富雄、三友胜雄、菊池乘三提出的上诉书。被告在他们的上诉书内对滨海军区军事法庭的判决书所判予他们的罪状表示服罪，但要求苏联最高法庭军事庭减刑。
    鉴于被告犯罪的严重性，鉴于滨海军区军事法庭决定的惩罚完全遵照了法律，并且考虑了他们每个人的罪行，苏联最高法院军事庭拒绝山田、梶塚、高桥、西丰彦、唐泽、三友和菊池的上诉书，并维持滨海军区军事法庭的原判。
