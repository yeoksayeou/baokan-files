Title: Intelligence Bureau Organ Newspaper Chinese Edition Welcomed by Readers

Author: 
Date: 1950-02-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th: The Chinese edition of the Cominform organ "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy!", published by Beijing Jiefang (Liberation) Press since October of last year, has now been published up to its 18th issue. The Chinese edition is distributed in Beijing, Tianjin, and Shenyang, with each issue selling between 40,000 and 60,000 copies. The English and Russian editions of the publication have a combined circulation of 8,000 to 10,000 copies in China. This publication is essential reading for those studying modern international politics and revolutionary movements in various countries. In China, the publication is not only welcomed by Communist Party members and members of the New Democratic Youth League, but also by a broad range of progressive individuals. In addition to publishing Cominform meeting documents, the publication regularly features articles on the struggles of working-class parties in various countries against domestic and foreign reactionaries, experiences in leading the construction of people's states, and essays criticizing and exposing the reactionary propaganda and conspiracies of world reactionary elements. The translated version of the important editorial "The Great Progress of the Liberation Movement in Colonies and Dependent Countries," published in the publication on January 27th, was prominently featured in newspapers in Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Nanjing, Jinan, Kaifeng, Hankou, Guangzhou, Xi'an, Shenyang, and other cities on the 3rd and 4th of this month. This article authoritatively pointed out the international significance of the victory of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the path of struggle for the people of Eastern countries such as India, and therefore aroused great attention from Chinese readers.

### 情报局机关报中文版受广大读者欢迎

1950-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　情报局机关报中文版受广大读者欢迎
    【新华社北京七日电】北京解放社于去年十月间开始出版的共产党与工人党情报局机关报《争取持久和平，争取人民民主！》的中文版，现已出版至第十八期。中文版分北京、天津、沈阳三地发行，每期销售四万份至六万份。该刊英文版和俄文版在中国的销售数共为八千份至一万份。该刊是研究现代国际政治和各国革命运动者的必读刊物。该刊在中国不但受到共产党员和新民主主义青年团员的欢迎，而且受到广大进步人士的欢迎。该刊除登载情报局会议文件外，经常登载关于各国工人阶级政党与国内外反动派进行斗争和领导建设人民国家的经验，以及关于批驳和揭露世界反动分子的反动宣传和阴谋活动的论文。该刊一月二十七日所发表的重要社论《殖民地与附属国解放运动的巨大进展》的译文，在本月三、四两日被北京、天津、上海、南京、济南、开封、汉口、广州、西安、沈阳等地的报纸以显著地位登载。这篇论文权威地指出了中国人民解放军战争胜利的国际意义和印度等东方国家人民的斗争道路，因而引起中国读者的极大重视。
