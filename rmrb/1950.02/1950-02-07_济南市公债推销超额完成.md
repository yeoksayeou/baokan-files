Title: Jinan City Overfulfills Public Bond Sales

Author:
Date: 1950-02-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 6th. The task of selling public bonds in Jinan City has been overfulfilled. As of the 4th, the total number of subscriptions from all sectors of Jinan reached more than 1,215,000 shares, exceeding the task by more than 5,000 shares.

### 济南市公债推销超额完成

1950-02-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　济南市公债推销超额完成
    【新华社北京六日电】济南市公债推销任务已经超额完成。截至四日止，济市各界认购总数共达一百二十一万五千余分，超过任务五千余分。
