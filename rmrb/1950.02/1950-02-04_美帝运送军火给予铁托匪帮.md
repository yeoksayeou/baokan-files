Title: US Imperialism Transports Military Supplies to the Tito Gang

Author: 
Date: 1950-02-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 31st: According to a Vienna dispatch from Telepress: The United States is transporting large quantities of weapons to Tito. An Austrian democratic newspaper published a Salzburg dispatch stating: For the past three weeks, trains loaded with military supplies have been regularly traveling from Stuttgart in the American-occupied zone of Germany, through Salzburg, to Yugoslavia. The trains are composed of huge freight cars with a loading capacity of sixty tons, containing American military trucks, light artillery, heavy anti-aircraft guns, and special artillery vehicles seized from Hitler's army.

### 美帝运送军火给予铁托匪帮

1950-02-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　美帝运送军火给予铁托匪帮
    【新华社北京三十一日电】据电通社维也纳二十七日讯：美国正运送大量武器给予铁托。奥地利民主报纸刊载萨尔斯堡消息称：过去三周中，装载军用物资的火车，按期由德境美国占领区的斯图加特驶经萨尔斯堡开赴南斯拉夫。火车由装载量达六十吨的巨大车箱组成，内装美国从希特勒军队缴获过来的军用卡车、轻炮、重防空炮和特种炮车等。
