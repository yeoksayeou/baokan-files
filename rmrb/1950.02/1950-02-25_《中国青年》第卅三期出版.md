Author:
Date: 1950-02-25
Page: 3

　　The Thirty-Third Issue of "China Youth" is Published
    [Our Newspaper] The thirty-third issue of "China Youth," the organ of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, was published on February 25th. This issue focuses on New Democratic studies in schools. It includes an article by Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretary of the Central Committee of the Youth League, entitled "On Launching New Democratic Studies in Schools." The article discusses the basic tasks of student work, as well as the implementation of ideological and political education and teacher-student cooperation. Other important articles include Fan Wenlan's "On the Reasons for the Long-Term Continuation of Chinese Feudal Society," Han Tianshi, Deputy Head of the Chinese Democratic Youth Delegation to Foreign Countries' "How the Soviet Union Youth League Carries out Communist Education," Wu Lengxi's "What is the Latest US Policy towards China?", Ai Qing's "Talking About Writing Poetry," etc.

### 《中国青年》第卅三期出版

1950-02-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　《中国青年》第卅三期出版
    【本报讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会机关刊物《中国青年》第三十三期已于本月二十五日出版。该期以学校中的新民主主义学习为中心。登有青年团中央委员会副书记蒋南翔的《论开展学校中的新民主主义学习》一文，文中对现在学生工作的基本任务以及贯彻思想政治教育、师生合作诸问题均有所论列。此外重要文章有范文澜的《论中国封建社会长期延续的原因》，中国民主青年出国代表团副团长韩天石的《苏联青年团如何进行共产主义教育》，吴冷西的《美国最近的对华政策是什么？》，艾青的《谈谈写诗》等。
