Title: American-Made Bandit Planes Invade and Ravage Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1950-02-26
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, February 25th: American-made Kuomintang bandit planes invaded Shanghai again on the 23rd, wreaking havoc. At 9:40 AM and 10:15 AM, two American-made P-51 and B-25 bandit planes each invaded the city's airspace from the east and west, respectively, indiscriminately dropping bombs, destroying many civilian houses. Preliminary statistics indicate that fifteen citizens were injured.

### 美制匪机侵沪肆虐

1950-02-26
第2版()
专栏：

　　美制匪机侵沪肆虐
    【新华社上海二十五日电】美制国民党匪帮飞机二十三日又侵袭上海施虐。该日上午九时四十分及十时十五分有美制Ｐ５１、Ｂ２５型匪机各二架分别由东西两面侵入市空滥肆投弹，炸毁民房多间，市民受伤者据初步统计有十五人。
