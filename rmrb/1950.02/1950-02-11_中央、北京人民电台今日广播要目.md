Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Schedule
Author:
Date: 1950-02-11
Page: 4

The Central People's Broadcasting Station today (11th) 17:30 Youth Time: Introducing the 32nd issue of "China Youth". 18:00 First Literary Program: Local opera "Chunyun's Divorce". 21:00 Second Literary Program: Chinese Music. 18:30: Soviet Songs. 18:45: New Songs and Instrumental Music.

In addition: Beijing People's Radio 12:30 Workers' Program: Introducing the production experience of the Shijingshan Power Plant, "The Capital of New China Will Always Be Bright". 13:00 Women's Program: Women's Health Knowledge "Uterine Fibroids". 17:45 Municipal Voice: ① "Provisional Measures for Street Cleaning Management", ② Notice from the Suburban Work Committee regarding city residents who own land in the suburbs to report their class status to the Farmers' Representatives' Meeting or the District Government in the village where the land is located. 18:45 Listener Service: ① Answering "How many miles is it from Beijing to Sichuan Province, what is the route, and how much is the travel expense?" ② Introducing the Soviet Film Exhibition's third day (12th) showings of "Gorky's Childhood" and "Genghis Khan," ③ Telegrams unclaimed at the Telecommunications Bureau. 19:00 Radio Lecture: Xiao Ming speaks on "Summary of the Beijing Municipal Trade Union's Work in the Past Year and Future Tasks."

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-02-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（十一日）十七点三十分青年时间：介绍“中国青年”第三十二期。十八点第一次文艺节目：牌子戏“春云离婚”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：国乐。十八点三十分：苏联歌曲。十八点四十五分：新歌曲和器乐。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工节目：介绍石景山发电厂的生产经验“新中国的首都永远光明”。十三点妇女节目：妇女卫生常识“子宫瘤”。十七点四十五分市政之声：①“街道清洁管理暂行办法”，②郊区工作委员会关于城内居民在郊区置有土地的到土地所在村的农民代表会或区政府自报成份的通告。十八点四十五分听众服务：①回答“由北京到四川省有多少里，怎样走法要多少路费？”②介绍苏联电影展览会第三天（十二日）上演的“高尔基幼年时代”和“成吉思汗”，③电信局招领电报。十九点广播讲演：萧明讲“北京市一年来工会工作总结和今后任务。”
