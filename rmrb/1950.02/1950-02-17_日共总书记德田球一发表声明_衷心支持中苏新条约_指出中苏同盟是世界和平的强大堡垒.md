Title: JCP General Secretary Tokuda Kyuichi Issues Statement: Heartfelt Support for the New Sino-Soviet Treaty; Points Out Sino-Soviet Alliance is a Powerful Bastion of World Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-02-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th: Tokyo dispatch: Tokuda Kyuichi, General Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party, issued a statement on the 15th, saying: The Japanese Communist Party wholeheartedly supports the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. The friendly alliance between the two great nations of China and the Soviet Union is a powerful bastion for striving for lasting world peace and encouraging the people of Asian countries to strive for national independence and peace. This alliance is a powerful blow to the Japanese reactionaries and the foreign monopolists who are frantically trying to dominate the world and prepare for new wars.

### 日共总书记德田球一发表声明  衷心支持中苏新条约  指出中苏同盟是世界和平的强大堡垒

1950-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　日共总书记德田球一发表声明
    衷心支持中苏新条约 
    指出中苏同盟是世界和平的强大堡垒
    【新华社北京十六日电】东京讯：日本共产党总书记德田球一于十五日发表声明说：日本共产党全心全意地支持中苏友好同盟互助条约。中苏两大国的友好同盟，是争取世界持久和平及鼓舞亚洲各国人民争取国家独立与和平的强大堡垒。这个同盟是对日本反动派以及疯狂地力图统治世界和准备新战争的外国垄断资本家们的有力打击。
