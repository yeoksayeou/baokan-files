Title: Xiamen Public Bonds Oversubscribed by More Than 70,000 Shares
Author:
Date: 1950-02-14
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Xiamen, 13th – Xiamen City has overfulfilled its allocation target of 300,000 shares of the Victory Public Bonds. As of the 12th, the total number of subscriptions from all sectors of the city has reached 378,200 shares, exceeding the target by more than 70,000 shares. The bond promotion committee is now striving towards the goal of exceeding the quota by 30%.

### 厦门公债推销超额七万余分

1950-02-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　厦门公债推销超额七万余分
    【新华社厦门十三日电】厦门市已超额完成胜利公债三十万分的配销任务。截至十二日止，全市各界认购总数已达三十七万八千二百分，超过任务七万余分。现劝购委员会正向超额百分之三十的目标努力中。
