　　Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
    [This Newspaper News] Central People's Broadcasting Station today (25th) at 8:30, Situ Meitang of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government will broadcast a speech to overseas Chinese. At 19:45, a recording of the speech will be broadcast. 17:30 Youth Time: "Student Movement in the Past Year". 20:20 (1) "On Soviet Loans to China". (2) Two dispatches: 1. Two hospitals in the Lushun-Dalian area, 2. Three female assistant drivers on a light oil truck. 18:00 First Literary Program: Xihe Dagu "Don't Be Superstitious". 21:00 Second Literary Program: National Music. 18:30 Soviet Music. 18:45 New Songs: "The People Have Yearned for the PLA", "Welcome the PLA", etc.
    [Further News] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station 12:30 Workers' Program: Teaching to Sing "A Thunderclap Shakes Heaven and Earth". 13:00 Women's Program: Common Knowledge of Women's Health "Uterine Cancer". 13:30 Professor Gu Zhengrong introduces the general scientific knowledge section of the Beijing Spring Festival Scientific Knowledge Exhibition. 16:30 Children's Program: The third and fourth acts of the opera "Little Red Riding Hood" performed by children from the Huabei Yucai Elementary School. 17:45 Voice of the City: ① Notice from the Beijing Air Defense Command asking citizens to strengthen air defense work, ② Report from the Beijing Public Security Bureau on the discovery of poisonous sugar in the western suburbs, asking all citizens to be vigilant. 18:45 Listener Service: ① Answering "Can planned economy be implemented in China during the New Democratic period?", ② Several unclaimed telegrams from the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau. 19:00 Broadcast Lecture: Chen Jiakang, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, speaks on "Democratic Youth in Colonies and Dependencies Unite to Oppose Imperialism".

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-02-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（二十五日）八时三十分中央人民政府华侨事务委员会司徒美堂向海外侨胞广播演讲。十九点四十五分播送该演讲的录音。十七点三十分青年时间：“一年来的学生运动”。二十点二十分（一）“关于苏联贷款给中国”。（二）两篇通讯：一、旅大地区的两个医院，二、轻油车上的三个女副司机。十八点第一次文艺节目：西河大鼓“别迷信”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：国乐。十八点三十分苏联音乐。十八点四十五分新歌曲：《老百姓盼到了解放军》、《欢迎解放军》等。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台十二点三十分职工节目：教唱歌《春雷一声天地动》。十三点妇女节目：妇女卫生常识“子宫癌”。十三点三十分顾正容教授介绍北京市春节科学知识展览会一般科学知识部分。十六点三十分儿童节目：华北育才小学小朋友演唱的歌剧“小红帽”第三幕、第四幕。十七点四十五分市政之声：①北京市防空指挥部请市民加强防空工作的通知，②北京市公安局关于西郊发现毒糖请全体市民提高警惕的报导。十八点四十五分听众服务：①回答“中国在新民主主义时期，能不能实行计划经济”，②北京电信局几件招领的电报。十九点广播讲演：青年团中央委员陈家康讲“殖民地、附属国民主青年团结起来，反对帝国主义”。
