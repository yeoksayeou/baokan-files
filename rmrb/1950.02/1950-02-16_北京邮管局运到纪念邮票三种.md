Title: Beijing Postal Administration Arrives with Three Types of Commemorative Stamps

Author:

Date: 1950-02-16
Page: 4

[This Municipality News] The Beijing Postal Administration has now received the following three types of commemorative stamps: (1) A set of "Limited to Use in Northeast China" commemorative stamps for the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (four types), with face values of 1,000 Northeast Yuan, 1,500 Northeast Yuan, 5,000 Northeast Yuan, and 20,000 Northeast Yuan, totaling 27,500 Northeast Yuan. (2) A commemorative stamp issued by the Lushun-Dalian Postal Area for the 32nd Anniversary of the Great Soviet October Revolution, with a face value of 10 Kwantung Yuan. (3) A commemorative stamp issued by the Lushun-Dalian Postal Area for the founding of the People's Republic of China (multicolored version), with a face value of 35 Kwantung Yuan. The above three types of stamps are now available for sale at the Philatelic Counter of the Beijing Postal Administration.

### 北京邮管局运到纪念邮票三种

1950-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京邮管局运到纪念邮票三种
    【本市讯】北京邮政管理局现运到下列纪念邮票三种：（一）限“东北贴用”中国人民政治协商会议纪念邮票一组（四种），面值为东北币一千元、一千五百元、五千元、二万元，共值东北币二万七千五百元。（二）旅大邮区发行伟大苏联十月革命三十二周年纪念邮票一种，面值为关东币十元。（三）旅大邮区发行中华人民共和国成立纪念邮票一种（套色版），面值为关东币三十五元。上述三种邮票自即日起在北京邮政管理局集邮抬出售。
