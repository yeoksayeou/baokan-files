Title: Beijing Municipal Youth League Work Committee Strengthens Education for League Members in Suburban Areas
Author: 
Date: 1950-02-04
Page: 3

　　Beijing Municipal Youth League Work Committee
    Strengthens Education for League Members in Suburban Areas
    [Beijing News] In order to strengthen the branch education of the Rural Youth League during the winter vacation, the Beijing Municipal Youth League Work Committee mobilized 80 Youth League members from Peking University, Tsinghua University, and Yenching University to go to villages in the suburbs where land reform has been completed on the 2nd of this month to assist the Youth League branches in carrying out education for League members.

### 京市青年团工委会加强郊区团员教育

1950-02-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　京市青年团工委会
    加强郊区团员教育
    【北京讯】青年团北京市工作委员会为利用寒假加强农村青年团的支部教育，动员了北大、清华、燕京三校青年团员八十人，于本月二日分赴郊区土地改革完成了的村庄，帮助各青年团支部进行团员教育工作。
