Title: Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Adjusted Starting on the 2nd

Author:
Date: 1950-02-04
Page: 2

Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Adjusted Starting on the 2nd
[Shanhaiguan News] The Shanhaiguan Joint Office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank has changed its exchange rate to 1 RMB yuan to 16 Northeast yuan, effective from the 2nd.

### 山海关货币牌价二日起调整

1950-02-04
第2版()
专栏：

　　山海关货币牌价二日起调整
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处自二日起牌价改为人民券一元兑东北券十六元。
