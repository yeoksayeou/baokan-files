Title: Soviet Armed Forces Minister Vasilevsky Issues Order Commemorating Army Day, Wishing All Officers and Soldiers Ever-Improving Combat Technology and Political Knowledge
Author:
Date: 1950-02-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 23rd: TASS Moscow reports: Soviet Armed Forces Minister, Soviet Marshal Vasilevsky, issued Order No. 21 in Moscow on the 23rd. The full text is as follows:

Comrades Soldiers and Sergeants of the Army and Navy!
Comrades Officers and Generals of the Army and Navy!

Today, the armed forces celebrate the thirty-second anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy.

The Soviet Army and Navy, founded by the Communist Party and its great leaders Lenin and Stalin, have proven themselves to be reliable defenders of the interests of the Soviet state in the invincible battles for the freedom and independence of the Soviet motherland.

On the occasion of the thirty-second anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy, I extend my congratulations to the soldiers, sergeants, officers, and generals of the Army and Navy.

I wish all officers and soldiers of the Army and Navy new achievements in the process of continuously improving their combat technology and political knowledge.

To commemorate this glorious day, I order:

Today—February 23rd—in our nation's capital, Moscow, in the capitals of each of the union republics, as well as in Kaliningrad, Khabarovsk, Vladivostok, and in the hero cities of Leningrad, Stalingrad, Sevastopol, and Odessa, let there be a twenty-gun salute.

Long live the Soviet Armed Forces!

Long live our powerful motherland!

Long live our glorious Bolshevik Party!

Long live our Soviet government!

Long live Comrade Stalin, the victorious inspirer and organizer of the Soviet people and their armed forces—our great leader and teacher!

                                                                                                 Soviet Armed Forces Minister, Soviet Marshal Vasilevsky (signed)

### 苏武装部队部部长华西列夫斯基  发布纪念建军节命令  祝全体指战员战斗技术政治知识益臻完善

1950-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏武装部队部部长华西列夫斯基
    发布纪念建军节命令
    祝全体指战员战斗技术政治知识益臻完善
    【新华社北京二十三日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联武装部队部部长、苏联元帅华西列夫斯基于二十三日在莫斯科发布第二十一号命令。全文如下：陆海军战士与士官同志们！陆海军校尉与将军同志们！
    今天，武装部队庆祝苏联陆海军建军的三十二周年。
    由共产党及其伟大的领袖列宁和斯大林所创立的苏联陆军和海军，已在争取苏维埃祖国的自由与独立的有我无敌的战斗中证明了它们是苏联国家利益的可靠的捍卫者。
    值此苏联陆海军建军三十二周年的纪念日，我向陆海军战士、士官、校尉、将军们致贺。
    祝陆海军全体指战员在使他们的战斗技术与政治知识益臻完善的进程中获得新的成就。
    为了纪念这个光荣的日子，我命令：
    今天——二月二十三日，在我们祖国的首都莫斯科，在各加盟共和国的首都，以及在加里宁格勒、罗夫、伯力、海参威，并在列宁格勒、斯大林格勒、悉瓦斯托波尔和敖德萨等英雄城市各鸣礼炮二十响。
    苏联武装部队万岁！
    我们强大的祖国万岁！
    我们光荣的布尔什维克党万岁！
    我们的苏维埃政府万岁！
    苏联人民及其武装部队的胜利的鼓舞者与组织者——我们伟大的领袖与导师斯大林同志万岁！
                苏联武装部队部部长、苏联元帅华西列夫斯基（签字）
