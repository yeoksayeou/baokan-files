Title: Telecom Workers' Congress to be Held in Beijing Next Month
Author:
Date: 1950-02-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 12th: The preparatory committee of the China Telecom Workers' Union has decided to convene the National Congress of Members of the China Telecom Workers' Union in Beijing on March 15th. The main agenda of the conference is to determine the working principles and tasks of the Telecom Workers' Union, discuss and adopt preliminary plans for labor insurance, welfare, and wage standards for telecom workers, and elect and formally establish the China Telecom Workers' Union.

### 电信工会全代大会下月在京召开

1950-02-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　电信工会全代大会下月在京召开
    【新华社北京十二日电】中国电信工会筹备委员会已决定于三月十五日在北京召开中国电信工会全国会员代表大会。大会主要议程为确定电信工会的工作方针及任务，讨论并通过电信职工的劳动保险、福利事业、工资标准等初步方案，选举并正式成立中国电信工会。
