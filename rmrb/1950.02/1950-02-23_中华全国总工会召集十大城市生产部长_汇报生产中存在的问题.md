Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Convenes Production Ministers from Ten Major Cities to Report on Problems Existing in Production
Author:
Date: 1950-02-23
Page: 2

　　[Beijing News] The Production Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, in order to understand the work situation of the production departments of trade unions in various regions and the problems currently existing in production, specifically convened a report meeting in Beijing on the 20th of production ministers from the general trade unions of ten major cities across the country. Approximately twenty people participated in the meeting, including the production ministers from the general trade unions of the Northeast, Lushun-Dalian, Shenyang, Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Wuhan, Tangshan, Taiyuan, Qingdao, and other places. At the meeting, each region will report on their situation, exchange work experiences, and raise issues. They will also focus on exchanging views on the democratization of factory management and the establishment of labor agreements. The meeting is scheduled to last ten days.

### 中华全国总工会召集十大城市生产部长  汇报生产中存在的问题

1950-02-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　中华全国总工会召集十大城市生产部长  汇报生产中存在的问题
    【北京讯】中华全国总工会生产部为了了解各地工会生产部的工作情况及目前生产中存在的问题，特于二十日在北京召集了全国十大城市的总工会的生产部长汇报会议。参加会议的计有：东北、旅大、沈阳、北京、天津、上海、武汉、唐山、太原、青岛等地总工会生产部长等二十人。会上将由各地汇报情况，交流工作经验、提出问题。并着重就工厂管理民主化及订立劳动公约问题交换意见。会议预定十天结束。
