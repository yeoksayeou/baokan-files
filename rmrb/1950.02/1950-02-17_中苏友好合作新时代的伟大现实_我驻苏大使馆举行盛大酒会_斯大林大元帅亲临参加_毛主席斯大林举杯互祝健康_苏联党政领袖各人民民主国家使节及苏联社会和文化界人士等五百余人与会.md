Title: The Great Reality of the New Era of Sino-Soviet Friendship and Cooperation: Our Embassy in the Soviet Union Holds a Grand Reception; Marshal Stalin Attends in Person; Chairman Mao and Stalin Toast Each Other's Health; Over 500 People Attend, Including Soviet Party and Government Leaders, Envoys from Various People's Democratic Countries, and Figures from Soviet Society and Culture

Author:
Date: 1950-02-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 15th:  Wang Jiaxiang, our Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union, and his wife, held a grand cocktail party at 9:00 PM on February 14th to celebrate Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's visit to the Soviet Union, hosting Soviet party and government leaders, envoys from various people's democratic countries to the Soviet Union, and figures from Soviet society and culture. Over 500 people attended. Among the distinguished Soviet guests were Marshal Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union; Shvernik, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union; Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union: Molotov, Malenkov, Beria, Voroshilov, Mikoyan, Kaganovich, and Bulganin; Khrushchev, Member of the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks) and Secretary of the Moscow City Committee; Chernousov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Republic; Soviet Ministers: Vyshinsky, Minister of Foreign Affairs; Zverev, Minister of Finance; Menshikov, Minister of Foreign Trade; Abakumov, Minister of State Security; Gorshenin, Minister of Justice; Kaftanov, Minister of Higher Education; Beshchev, Minister of Railways; Smirnov, Minister of Health; Bolsakov, Minister of Cinematography; Soviet Marshal Budyonny; Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs Gromyko and Zorin; Roschin, Soviet Ambassador to China; Gorkin, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union; Admiral Yumashev; Kuznetsov, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions; Mikhailov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League; and Migunov, Soviet Trade Representative to China. Among the envoys of various people's democratic countries to the Soviet Union were Rastovicka, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Czechoslovak Republic; Zhu Ninghe, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; Blagoeva, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Bulgaria; Bujic, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Romanian People's Republic; Sobek, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic; Itamgab, Minister Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic; Natanaeli, Minister Plenipotentiary of Albania; Abel, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic; Zambrovich, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Republic, as well as the heads of the commercial delegations of their respective countries visiting the Soviet Union: Grigor, Minister of Foreign Trade of Czechoslovakia; Balideanu, Minister of Foreign Trade of Romania; Ronai, Minister of Foreign Trade of Hungary; Ganev, Minister of Foreign Trade of Bulgaria; and Hanke, Minister of Foreign Trade of Germany. Among the figures from Soviet society and culture were representatives of the Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific workers delegation who visited China last year, representatives who participated in the Asian Women's Representative Conference and the Asian-Australasian Trade Union Conference, famous writers such as Fadeyev, Simonov, Ehrenburg, famous poets such as Tvardovsky and Mikhalkov, famous actors such as Lepeshinskaya, Tsarev, the Okhlopkov couple, and Derevianko, as well as TASS Director-General Bargunov, and responsible editors and famous reporters from major Soviet newspapers. Participating from our side were Chairman Mao Zedong, Premier Zhou Enlai, Professor Chen Boda, Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government Li Fuchun, Vice Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government Saifuddin, Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan, all members of our government delegation, and all staff members of our embassy in the Soviet Union. Before the cocktail party began, Chairman Mao, Premier Zhou, and Ambassador Wang Jiaxiang and his wife personally hosted the guests. When Marshal Stalin and other Soviet party and government leaders entered the venue, the entire audience applauded enthusiastically and repeatedly shouted: "Long live Stalin!" Afterwards, Chairman Mao invited Stalin and others to take their seats. During the dinner, Chairman Mao raised his glass to wish Marshal Stalin good health and to wish Sino-Soviet friendship a long life. Marshal Stalin also raised his glass several times to wish Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou good health. The cocktail party did not end until after midnight.

### 中苏友好合作新时代的伟大现实  我驻苏大使馆举行盛大酒会  斯大林大元帅亲临参加  毛主席斯大林举杯互祝健康  苏联党政领袖各人民民主国家使节及苏联社会和文化界人士等五百余人与会

1950-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏友好合作新时代的伟大现实
    我驻苏大使馆举行盛大酒会
    斯大林大元帅亲临参加
    毛主席斯大林举杯互祝健康
    苏联党政领袖各人民民主国家使节及苏联社会和文化界人士等五百余人与会
    【新华社莫斯科十五日电】我国驻苏特命全权大使王稼祥夫妇，为庆贺中央人民政府主席毛泽东及政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来访苏之行，特于二月十四日晚九时举行盛大的鸡尾酒会，招待苏联党政领袖，各人民民主国家驻苏使节及苏联社会与文化界人士。参加者五百余人。苏方贵宾中有苏联部长会议主席斯大林大元帅，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席什维尔尼克，苏联部长会议副主席：莫洛托夫，马林科夫，贝利亚，伏罗希洛夫，米高扬，卡冈诺维奇及布尔加宁，联共中央委员及莫斯科市委书记赫鲁舍切夫，俄罗斯共和国部长会议主席契尔脑索夫，苏联各部部长：外交部部长维辛斯基，财政部部长兹维列夫，对外贸易部部长孟希科夫，国家保安部部长阿巴库莫夫，司法部部长高尔谢宁，高等教育部部长卡夫坦诺夫，交通部部长别什契夫，卫生部部长斯米尔诺夫，电影事业部部长鲍尔沙利可夫，苏联元帅布琼尼，外交部副部长葛罗米柯、佐林，苏联驻华大使罗申，苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书高尔金，海军上将尤马谢夫，全苏工会中央理事会主席库兹涅佐夫，共产主义青年团中央书记米哈依洛夫及苏联驻华商务代表米古诺夫等人。各人民民主国家驻苏使节中，有捷克斯洛伐克共和国特命全权大使拉斯托维茨卡，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国特命全权大使朱宁河，保加利亚人民共和国特命全权大使布拉果耶娃，罗马尼亚人民共和国特命全权大使布吉克，匈牙利人民共和国特命全权大使索贝克，蒙古人民共和国特命全权公使伊丹甲布，阿尔巴尼亚特命全权公使纳塔奈利，德意志民主共和国外交使团团长阿贝尔，波兰共和国临时代办赞布罗维奇，以及各该国访苏之商务代表团团长：捷克斯洛伐克对外贸易部部长格里哥尔，罗马尼亚对外贸易部部长巴利迪亚努，匈牙利对外贸易部部长罗奈伊，保加利亚对外贸易部部长加涅夫，德国对外贸易部部长韩克。苏联社会及文化界人士中，有去年访华之苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团代表，参加亚洲妇女代表会议及亚澳工会会议代表，名作家有法捷耶夫，西蒙诺夫，爱伦堡，名诗人有特瓦尔多夫斯基，米哈尔柯夫，名演员有列彼辛斯卡亚，柴罗夫，奥赫洛晋柯夫妇、德列夫等人，此外尚有塔斯社社长巴尔古诺夫，及苏联各大报纸之负责编辑及名记者等。我方参加者，有毛泽东主席，周恩来总理，陈伯达教授，东北人民政府副主席李富春，新疆省人民政府副主席赛福鼎，外交部苏联东欧司司长伍修权，我政府代表团全体团员及我国驻苏大使馆全体馆员。鸡尾酒会开始前，由毛主席、周总理及王稼祥大使夫妇亲自招待。斯大林大元帅及苏联其他党政领袖步入会场时，全场热烈鼓掌，不断高呼：“斯大林万岁！”此后毛主席即请斯大林等人入席，席间毛主席曾举杯祝斯大林大元帅健康并祝中苏友好万岁。斯大林大元帅亦数度举杯，祝毛主席及周总理健康。酒会至午夜后始散。
