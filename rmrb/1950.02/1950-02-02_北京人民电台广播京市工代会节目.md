Title: Beijing People's Radio Broadcasts Programs for the Beijing Municipal Workers' Congress

Author:

Date: 1950-02-02
Page: 4

　　[This Municipality News] To welcome the opening of the First Beijing Municipal Workers' Congress, the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast a special program celebrating the Workers' Congress during the workers' time from 12:30 to 13:00 today (February 2nd). Comrade Zhang Jinbiao from the People's Printing Factory will speak on "Celebrating the First Beijing Municipal Workers' Congress" and Comrade Xu Jinsheng from the Trolleybus Company will speak on "Wishing the First Beijing Municipal Workers' Congress Success". Starting today, the news and highlights of the Beijing Municipal Workers' Congress of the day will be reported from 18:45 to 19:00 during the listener service time. From 18:00 to 18:30, Comrade Fu Qiang from Hua Da will speak on "How I Established the Concept of Serving the People."

　　[Another News] Beijing People's Radio 12:30 Staff Program: (1) Trade Union Work: Speeches by Comrades Zhang Jinbiao and Xu Jinsheng. (2) Worker Service: Answering ① What is piece-rate pay? ② What is a "cross-progressive" wage system? ③ What is the relationship between having many or few wage grades and production? 13:00 Learning Chat: Discussing "Why the leadership of the revolution must belong to the Chinese working class, even though it is small in number and has a low level of education". 16:30 Children's Program: Singing and dialogues by children from Dongbajiao Primary School in the Eighth District. 17:00 Continue teaching the song "The Sun Rises, the Sky is Red". 17:30 Voice of the Municipality: Comrade Peng Zhen's article "Celebrating the First Anniversary of the Liberation of Beijing". 18:45 Reporting news and highlights of the first day of the First Beijing Municipal Workers' Congress.

　　[This newspaper news] Central People's Broadcasting Station, today (2nd), 17:30 Women's Time: "Introducing Moscow's Second Infant Guidance Station". 18:30 Natural Science Lecture: Electrical Knowledge Lecture 11 "Electrolysis and Electroplating". 20:20 International Affairs: "Starting with the imprisonment of 100,000 children in the United States". 18:00 First Literary and Art Program: Leting Drum "Lanhqiao's Grief". 21:00 Second Literary and Art Program: "Small Songs of Sino-Soviet Friendship" and Taiping Song "Celebrating the New Year". 18:45 New Songs: "The Sun Comes Out Red All Over The Sky" etc.

### 北京人民电台广播京市工代会节目

1950-02-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京人民电台广播京市工代会节目
    【本市讯】北京市人民广播电台为了迎接北京市第一届工人代表大会的召开，特在今天（二月二日）十二点半到十三点职工时间内播送庆祝工代会特别节目。由人民印制厂张金彪同志讲“庆祝北京市首届工人代表大会”和电车公司许金声同志讲“预祝北京市第一届工人代表大会成功”。并从今天起十八点四十五分到十九点听众服务时间报导北京市第一届工人代表大会当天大会新闻和花絮。十八点到十八点三十分华大傅强同志讲“我怎样建立起为人民服务的观念”。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工节目：（一）工会工作：张金彪、许金声同志讲演。（二）工人服务：回答①按劳计酬是怎么一回事？②什么是“交叉累进”的工资制度？③工资等级分得多或分得少，对生产有什么关系？十三点学习漫谈：谈谈“中国工人阶级人数很少，文化水平又低，为什么革命的领导权必须要归于他”。十六点三十分儿童节目：八区东八角小学小朋友们唱歌、对话。十七点继续教唱《太阳一出满天红》。十七点三十分市政之声：彭真同志的文章《庆祝北京解放一周年》。十八点四十五分报导北京市第一届工人代表大会第一天的新闻和花絮。
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（二）日十七点三十分妇女时间：“介绍莫斯科第二育婴指导站”。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：电学常识第十一讲“电解与电镀”。二十点二十分国际时事：“从美国十万儿童坐牢谈起”。十八点第一次文艺节目：乐亭大鼓“蓝桥恨”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：《中苏友谊小唱》和太平歌曲《过新年》。十八点四十五分新歌曲：《太阳出来满天红》等。
