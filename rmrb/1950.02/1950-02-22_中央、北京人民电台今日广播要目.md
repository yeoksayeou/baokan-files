Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Station Today's Broadcast Schedule
Author:
Date: 1950-02-22
Page: 4

　　Central, Beijing People's Radio Station Today's Broadcast Schedule

　　[This Newspaper News] The Central People's Broadcasting Station today (the 22nd) at 17:30 Youth Time: "The Learning Activities of Harbin Students." 18:30 Natural Science Lecture: "Antimony and China's Antimony Mines." 18:00 First Literary Program: Fengtiao Daggu "People's Great Unity." 21:00 First Literary Program: Yangko Opera "Army and People as One Family." 18:45 New Songs: "Defend World Peace," "Sing of Sino-Soviet Friendship," "Long Live Sino-Soviet Friendship."

　　[Another News] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station: 12:30 Workers' Program: (1) Teach singing "A Spring Thunder Shakes the World," (2) Comprehensive report "Capital Workers Warmly Celebrate the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance." 13:00 Youth Program: Special Program for the Day Against Colonialism: (1) Two letters from all students of Tsinghua and Peking University to colonial youth, (2) Dai Hongsen, a student from Men's No. 2 Middle School, recites a poem he wrote, "Fight On, Sisters and Brothers of the Colonies!" (3) Choirs from Men's No. 2 Middle School and Women's No. 4 Middle School sing five songs. 16:30 Children's Program: Sun Jingxiu continues to tell the Soviet novel "Son of the Regiment." 17:00 Song Program: Broadcast youth songs. 17:45 Voice of the City: (1) The Beijing Public Security Bureau Fire Brigade appeals to citizens to be careful about fire prevention during the Spring Festival, (2) The Beijing Public Health Bureau informs citizens to pay attention to the prevention of smallpox, measles, and meningitis. 18:45 Listener Service: (1) Answering "Why is Beijing establishing a stock exchange, and what are its benefits to economic construction?", (2) The Construction Bureau of the Shenyang Municipal People's Government solicits designs for the Founding Monument, (3) The Industry Department of the Northeast People's Government is recruiting experts, professors, technicians, accountants, and statisticians.

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1950-02-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（二十二日）十七点三十分青年时间：“哈尔滨市同学的学习活动”。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：“锑和我国的锑矿”。十八点第一次文艺节目：奉调大鼓“人民大团结”。二十一点第一次文艺节目：秧歌剧《军民一家》。十八点四十五分新歌曲：《保卫世界和平》、《唱中苏友谊》、《中苏友好万岁》。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台：十二点三十分职工节目：（一）教唱歌《春雷一声天地动》，（二）综合报导“首都工人热烈庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约”。十三点青年节目：反对殖民制度斗争日特别节目：（１）清华和北大全体同学给殖民地青年的两封信，（２）男二中戴宏森同学朗诵他所写的一首诗《战斗吧，殖民地的姊妹兄弟们！》（３）男二中、女四中歌咏队演唱五支歌子。十六点三十分儿童节目：孙敬修继续讲苏联小说《团的儿子》。十七点歌咏节目：播送青年歌曲。十七点四十五分市政之声：（１）北京市公安局消防大队请市民在春节期间小心防火的号召，（２）北京市公共卫生局请市民注意预防天花、麻疹、脑膜炎的通知。十八点四十五分听众服务：（１）回答“北京市为什么要成立证券交易所，它对经济建设有什么好处？”，（２）沈阳市人民政府建设局征求开国纪念塔图样的启事，（３）东北人民政府工业部招聘专家、教授、技术人员、会计和统计人员的启事。
