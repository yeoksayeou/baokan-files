Title: Establishment of the Korean Central News Agency's Beijing General Branch

Author:

Date: 1950-02-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th. Following the establishment of diplomatic relations between the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and our country, the Korean Central News Agency has obtained the consent of our government to establish a general branch in Beijing, and has appointed Kim Sung-yong as the director. The general branch is now operating at 11 Nanheyan (telephone number 5-1457).

### 朝鲜中央通讯社北京总分社成立

1950-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜中央通讯社北京总分社成立
    【新华社北京十日电】在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国与我国建立外交关系后，朝鲜中央通讯社已获得我国政府同意，在北京成立总分社，并任命金圣容为社长。该总分社现已在南河沿十一号（电话五局一四五七）开始办公。
