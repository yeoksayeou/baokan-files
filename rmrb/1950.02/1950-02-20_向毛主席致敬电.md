Title: Telegram of Respect to Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1950-02-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th: Telegram of Respect to Chairman Mao from the People of All Circles in Beijing Celebrating the Signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance:

Respected Chairman Mao:

Today, the people of our capital are cheering and singing because we have an allied country like never before! The people of China and the world are cheering and singing because the lasting peace and universal security of the Far East and the world have increased guarantees!

The signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance and the agreements on the Changchun Railway, Lushun, Dalian, and loans is a great achievement. It has consolidated the victories already achieved by the Chinese people, helped the Chinese people's economic and cultural construction, and strengthened the strength of the world peace camp headed by the Soviet Union. This achievement is due to your wise decisions and Chairman Stalin's great friendship. Therefore, we sincerely thank you and Chairman Stalin!

We believe that under your leadership and the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, the Chinese people will inherit their good traditions of bravery and diligence, contribute all their strength, and with the friendly assistance of the Soviet people, will surely make the great People's Republic of China rapidly become a strong and industrialized country!

The People of All Circles in Beijing Celebrating the Signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance.
February 18, 1950

### 向毛主席致敬电

1950-02-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　向毛主席致敬电
    【新华社北京十八日电】北京市各界人民庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订大会向毛主席致敬电：敬爱的毛主席：
    今天我们首都的人民在欢呼，在歌唱；因为我们有了从来没有过的同盟国！全中国全世界的人民在欢呼，在歌唱；因为远东和世界的持久和平与普遍安全增加了保障！
    签订中苏友好同盟互助条约和关于中长路、旅大及贷款等协定，是一个伟大的成就。它巩固了中国人民已经取得的胜利，帮助了中国人民的经济文化建设，加强了以苏联为首的世界和平阵营的力量。这个成就是由于你的英明决策和斯大林主席的伟大友谊。因此我们衷心地感谢你，感谢斯大林主席！
    我们相信：中国人民在你和中国共产党的领导下，继承自己勇敢勤劳的好传统，贡献出自己的一切力量，加上苏联人民的友谊援助，必将使伟大的中华人民共和国迅速地成为强盛与工业化的国家！
    北京市各界人民庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订大会
                          一九五○年二月十八日
