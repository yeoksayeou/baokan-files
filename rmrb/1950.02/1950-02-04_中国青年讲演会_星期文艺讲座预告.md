Title: China Youth Lecture Hall, Weekly Literary Lecture Announcement
Author:
Date: 1950-02-04
Page: 3

　　China Youth Lecture Hall, Weekly Literary Lecture Announcement
    [Beijing News] The China Youth Lecture Hall is scheduled to have Miao Peishi of the Worker's Daily continue his broadcast on "Talking about Writing with Young Friends" on the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station at 7:00 pm tonight (the 4th).
    [Beijing News] The Weekly Literary Lecture, jointly organized by the Mass Literature and Art Creation Research Association and the Youth Cultural Service Society, will invite Wang Yaping, Director of the Literature and Art Division of the Municipal Bureau of Culture and Education, to lecture on "Problems in Writing Popular Poetry" this week. After the lecture, Cao Baolu and Shen Deyuan will perform "The River God Marries a Bride." The venue is the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association on Nanheyan Street, and the time is 9:30 am. Admission is by long-term and temporary tickets. Temporary tickets can be obtained from the Youth Service Department today (the 4th).

### 中国青年讲演会  星期文艺讲座预告

1950-02-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　中国青年讲演会星期文艺讲座预告
    【北京讯】中国青年讲演会定于今（四）日晚七点，请工人日报苗培时在北京市人民广播电台继续播讲“和青年朋友们谈写稿”。
    【北京讯】大众文艺创作研究会与青年文化服务社联合举办的星期文艺讲座，本周邀请市文教局文艺处处长王亚平主讲，讲题是：“通俗诗歌写作问题”。会后有曹宝禄、沈德源演唱“河伯娶妇”。地点在南河沿苏联对外文化协会，时间是上午九点半。凭长期券和临时券入场，临时券可于今（四）日到青年服务部领取。
