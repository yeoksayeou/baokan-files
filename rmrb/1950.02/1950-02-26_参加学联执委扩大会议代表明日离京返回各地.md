Title: Representatives Attending the All-China Students' Federation Executive Committee Enlarged Meeting to Return to Their Respective Regions Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1950-02-26
Page: 3

　　[Beijing News] Student representatives from various regions who came to Beijing to attend the Second Enlarged Session of the Fourteenth Executive Committee of the All-China Students' Federation will return to their respective hometowns tomorrow (the 27th). On the 23rd, all student representatives visited Yenching and Tsinghua Universities. Tsinghua students held a grand welcoming ceremony. At the meeting, student representatives from Yunnan, Fujian, Inner Mongolia and other places gave brief reports on the local student movements before and after liberation. On the 24th, all student representatives attended a reception held by the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government. In addition, they also visited the Youth Service Department and Peking Union Medical College. Before leaving, they all expressed that they must bring the spirit of the meeting back to the students in various regions.

### 参加学联执委扩大会议代表明日离京返回各地

1950-02-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　参加学联执委扩大会议代表明日离京返回各地
    【北京讯】来京参加中华全国学生联合会第十四届第二次执行委员会扩大会议的各地学生代表，将于明（二十七）日分返原地。全体学生代表于二十三日参观燕京、清华两校，清华同学举行了盛大欢迎会。会上有昆明、福建、内蒙等地学生代表就解放前后当地学运，作了简略报告；二十四日，全体学生代表出席中央人民政府教育部举行的招待会。此外，他们还参观了青年服务部和协和医学院。临行前，他们都表示一定要把会议精神带回给各地同学。
