Title: Pravda Continues to Publish Full-Page Coverage of International Commentary on the Sino-Soviet Treaty
Author:
Date: 1950-02-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 21st: TASS reported: Pravda devoted a full page on the 20th to foreign commentary on the signing of the treaty and various agreements between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China. These included commentaries from China, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Hungary, Romania, Japan, and Australia.

### 各国对中苏缔约评论真理报继续整版刊载

1950-02-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　各国对中苏缔约评论真理报继续整版刊载
    【新华社北京二十一日电】塔斯社消息：真理报于二十日以整版篇幅刊载关于苏联与中华人民共和国间签订条约与各项协定的外国评论。其中有中国、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、日本和澳大利亚的评论。
