Title: Abundant Snowfall in Various Parts of Henan
Author:
Date: 1950-02-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaifeng, 4th:  Abundant snowfall occurred in various parts of Henan Province at the end of last month. In the Xuchang, Zhengzhou, Chenliu, Huaiyang, and Shanzhou regions, the snow depth reached approximately two inches, while in the Huangchuan region, the snow depth was as much as half a foot. This heavy snowfall is greatly beneficial to winter wheat, killing pests, and eliminating the spread of epidemics.

### 河南各地普降瑞雪

1950-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　河南各地普降瑞雪
    【新华社开封四日电】河南省各地于上月底普降瑞雪。许昌、郑州、陈留、淮阳、陕州等专区雪量达二寸左右，潢川专区积雪深达半尺。此次大雪对冬麦、杀灭害虫和消除疫病蔓延裨益甚大。
