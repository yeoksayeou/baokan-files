Title: Soviet Enterprises Discuss New Collective Contracts Focusing on Expanding the Use of Internal Enterprise Potential to Increase Labor Productivity and Reduce Production Costs
Author:
Date: 1950-02-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 31st (TASS dispatch): Soviet enterprises have now begun discussing the new collective contracts for 1950, which will be signed in all factories, transportation enterprises, state farms, and machine and tractor stations in February. The working people are focusing on investigating and maximizing the use of the enterprises' internal potential to increase labor productivity and reduce production costs. This year, the collective contracts stipulate various measures and methods to develop mass invention, rationalization, and the adoption of advanced experience in production, and to train workers with Stakhanovite labor. The management departments of the enterprises are to find ways to mechanize labor-intensive production processes on a large scale. The collective contracts also include the following obligations for the management and trade union sides: to further improve working conditions in production, to provide better medical services to employees and their families, to expand the network of trade union sanatoriums and rest homes, and to build new nurseries, kindergartens, clubs, and sports facilities.

### 苏企业讨论新集体合同  集中注意扩大利用企业内部潜力提高劳动生产率和减低生产成本

1950-02-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏企业讨论新集体合同
    集中注意扩大利用企业内部潜力提高劳动生产率和减低生产成本
    【新华社北京三十一日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联各企业现已开始讨论一九五○年的新集体合同，合同将于二月在一切工厂、运输企业、国营农场和机器与拖拉机站中签订。劳动人民集中注意调查和最大限度地利用企业的内部潜力，提高劳动生产率和减低生产成本。今年，集体合同规定了种种措施和方法，以便发展群众的发明、合理化、和在生产上采用先进的经验，并以斯塔哈诺夫式的劳动来训练工人。企业的经理部门则要设法把耗费劳力的生产过程大规模机械化。集体合同并包括经理部门和工会方面的下列各项义务：进一步改进生产中的劳动条件，给予职工及其家属以更好的医药服务，扩大工会的疗养院和休息所网，建筑新保育院、托儿所、俱乐部和运动设备。
