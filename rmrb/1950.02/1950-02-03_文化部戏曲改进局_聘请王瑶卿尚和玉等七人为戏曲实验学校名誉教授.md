Title: Ministry of Culture's Opera Improvement Bureau Appoints Wang Yaoqing, Shang Heyu and Six Others as Honorary Professors of the Opera Experimental School
Author:
Date: 1950-02-03
Page: 3

　　Ministry of Culture's Opera Improvement Bureau
　　Appoints Wang Yaoqing, Shang Heyu and Six Others as Honorary Professors of the Opera Experimental School
　　[Reported by our newspaper] The Opera Improvement Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, in order to enrich the faculty of the Opera Experimental School under its jurisdiction, and to implement the People's Government's policy of uniting old artists and vigorously carrying out opera reform, has specially appointed Wang Yaoqing, Shang Heyu, Xiao Changhua, Wang Fengqing, Tan Xiaopei, Jin Zhongren, and Ma Decheng, seven senior figures in the Peking Opera world, as honorary professors of the Opera Experimental School. The school held a welcome meeting on the 26th of last month. In addition to the seven honorary professors, Lao She, Ouyang Yuqian, Hong Shen, Han Shichang, Bai Jialin, Qiu Shengrong, Zhang Yunxi, Zhang Chunhua, Xiao Bai Yushuang, Hong Qiaolan, Xin Fengxia, Yun Yanming, Xu Donglai, Zhang Menggeng, Fang Hua, and responsible comrades from the Bureau and the School, totaling more than forty people, were present. At the meeting, Director Tian Han delivered a speech, followed by speeches from Lao She, Ouyang Yuqian, He Chengxiang, Shang Heyu, Tan Xiaopei, and Bai Jialin, all of whom expressed warm support for the government's policy of uniting old artists to jointly carry out opera reform. At the meeting, Tan Xiaopei called on old actors to support the government's policy and take on the responsibility of teaching and promoting younger generations. The attendees also held a detailed discussion on the "Three Attacks on Zhujiazhuang" performed at the Popular Theater.

### 文化部戏曲改进局  聘请王瑶卿尚和玉等七人为戏曲实验学校名誉教授

1950-02-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　文化部戏曲改进局
    聘请王瑶卿尚和玉等七人为戏曲实验学校名誉教授
    【本报讯】中央人民政府文化部戏曲改进局，为充实该局所属戏曲实验学校师资，并执行人民政府团结旧艺人大力进行戏曲改革的政策，特聘请京剧界前辈王瑶卿、尚和玉、萧长华、王凤卿、谭小培、金仲仁、马德成等七人为戏曲实验学校的名誉教授。该校于上月二十六日举行欢迎会，除七位名誉教授外，并到有老舍、欧阳予倩、洪深、韩世昌、白家麟、裘盛戎、张云溪、张椿华、筱白玉霜、鸿巧兰、新凤霞、云燕铭、徐东来、张梦庚、方华及该局、校负责同志等四十余人。会上田汉局长致词后，老舍、欧阳予倩、何成湘、尚和玉、谭小培、白家麟相继发言，对于政府团结旧艺人，共同进行戏曲改革工作的政策都表示热烈的拥护。谭小培在会上号召老伶工们拥护政府政策，负起教导和提拔后进的责任。到会诸人并对大众剧场演出的“三打祝家庄”作了详尽的讨论。
