Title: Hungarian Minister to China, Mr. Száfrankó, Arrives in Beijing Yesterday; Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu and Others Welcome Him at the Station
Author:
Date: 1950-02-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th. The Hungarian People's Republic's Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of China, Mr. Száfrankó and his wife, along with a party of over ten people, arrived in Beijing this morning at 6:40 a.m., accompanied by Zeng Yuanhui, Director of the Liaison Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs stationed in Manchuria. Those who came to the station to welcome them included Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Tang Yanjie, Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command, as well as Li Zhouyuan, Ambassador of Korea to China, Counselor Cui Ying, Polish Chargé d'Affaires Bi Luoqi, Soviet Embassy Counselor Chikhvin, Deputy Military Attaché Lieutenant Colonel Fominko, Czech Embassy Secretary Luo Dai and others.

### 匈牙利驻我国公使  夏法朗柯昨抵京  章汉夫副外长等赴站欢迎

1950-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　匈牙利驻我国公使
    夏法朗柯昨抵京
    章汉夫副外长等赴站欢迎
    【新华社北京十日电】匈牙利人民共和国驻中华人民共和国特命全权公使夏法朗柯夫妇一行十余人，于今晨六时四十分由外交部驻满洲里联络处处长曾远辉陪同抵京。到站欢迎者有中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫、办公厅主任王炳南、交际处长王倬如，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗、京津卫戍司令部参谋长唐延杰，及朝鲜驻华大使李周渊、参事崔英，波兰代办毕罗奇，苏联大使馆参事齐赫文、副武官富敏科中校，捷克大使馆秘书罗戴等。
