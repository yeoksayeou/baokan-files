Title: Head of Brazilian Parliamentary Delegation to the Soviet Union Advocates Restoring Brazilian-Soviet Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-09-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, 30th - TASS reports: According to "El Popular", Vargas, head of the Brazilian parliamentary delegation that recently visited the Soviet Union and Vice-Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Brazilian Chamber of Deputies, gave a radio address in Sao Paulo praising the achievements of the Soviet people. He emphasized that Brazil must restore diplomatic and trade relations with the Soviet Union and other countries in the socialist camp.

### 巴西访苏议会代表团长  主张恢复巴苏外交关系

1956-09-01
第5版()
专栏：

    巴西访苏议会代表团长
    主张恢复巴苏外交关系
    新华社31日讯　塔斯社蒙得维的亚30日讯：据“人民报”报道，不久前访问过苏联的巴西议会代表团团长、巴西国会众议院外交委员会副主席瓦加斯在圣保罗发表广播演说，赞扬苏联人民的成就，并且强调指出，巴西必须同苏联和社会主义阵营其他国家恢复外交关系和贸易关系。
