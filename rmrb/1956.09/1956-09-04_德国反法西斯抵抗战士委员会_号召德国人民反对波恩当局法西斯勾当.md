Title: German Anti-Fascist Resistance Fighters Committee Calls on German People to Oppose the Bonn Authorities' Fascist Collusion

Author:

Date: 1956-09-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 3rd, Berlin Dispatch: The "Neues Deutschland" published on September 2nd the "Appeal to All Peace-Loving German People" by the Anti-Fascist Resistance Fighters Committee and the National Committee of the National Front of the German Democratic Republic for the upcoming International Anti-Fascist and Anti-War Day.

The appeal calls on everyone to rise up against the Bonn authorities' banning of the German Communist Party and persecution of those who oppose the new German Bundeswehr. It says: Now it is not 1933, and the despicable enemies of the German people can only carry out their dark deeds in one part of our homeland.

We still remember that people of various worldviews and religions – communists, social democrats, democrats, and Christians – united in the underground struggle against the Nazi regime to defend basic human rights and oppose the criminal war of aggression. Now we must learn from the example of these people. The time has come: all differences should be put aside so that Germans in both East and West Germany can jointly oppose the danger of fascism and militarism that threatens us all, and strive to establish freedom and democracy in West Germany.

### 德国反法西斯抵抗战士委员会  号召德国人民反对波恩当局法西斯勾当

1956-09-04
第6版()
专栏：

    德国反法西斯抵抗战士委员会
    号召德国人民反对波恩当局法西斯勾当
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社柏林讯：“新德意志报”9月2日刊载了反法西斯抵抗战士委员会和民主德国全国阵线全国委员会为即将举行国际反法西斯和反战争日而发表的告全体爱好和平的德国人民书。
    告人民书号召大家起来反对波恩当局禁止德国共产党和迫害反对新德国国防军的人。它说：现在已经不是1933年了，德国人民的可恶敌人现在只能在我们祖国的一部分干他们的黑暗勾当。
    我们还记得，当年曾经有各种不同的世界观和宗教信仰的人——共产党人、社会民主党人、民主党人和基督教人士为维护基本人权和反对罪恶的掠夺性的战争在对纳粹政权进行地下斗争的情况下团结了起来。现在必须学习这些人的榜样。这样的时候已经到了：应该把一切分歧抛在一边，使德国东西两部分的德国人共同来反对威胁我们大家的法西斯主义和军国主义的危险，争取在西德确立自由和民主。
