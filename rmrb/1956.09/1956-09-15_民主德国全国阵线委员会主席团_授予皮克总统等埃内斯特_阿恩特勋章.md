Title: Presidium of the National Front Committee of the German Democratic Republic Awards Ernst Arndt Order to President Pieck and Others
Author:
Date: 1956-09-15
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 14th: According to the German News Agency, the Presidium of the National Council of the National Front of the German Democratic Republic awarded the Ernst Arndt Order to a group of outstanding German politicians on September 13th. Some of these German politicians had signed the "Free Germany" National Committee declaration in Moscow in 1943, and some made special contributions to the patriotic people's movement after 1945.

    Among the recipients are President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic, Prime Minister Grotewohl, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany Ulbricht, Chairman of the People's Chamber Dieckmann, and two hundred others.

    Arndt was a patriotic German politician of the late eighteenth and early nineteenth centuries who actively advocated German unification.

### 民主德国全国阵线委员会主席团  授予皮克总统等埃内斯特·阿恩特勋章

1956-09-15
第6版()
专栏：

    民主德国全国阵线委员会主席团
    授予皮克总统等埃内斯特·阿恩特勋章
    新华社柏林14日电　据德意志通讯社讯：民主德国全国阵线全国委员会主席团9月13日授予一批杰出的德国政治家以埃内斯特·阿恩特勋章。这批德国政治家之中有一些曾在1943年在莫斯科签署了“自由德国”全国委员会宣言，有一些在1945年以后的爱国人民运动中有特殊贡献。
    受奖者有德意志民主共和国总统皮克、总理格罗提渥，德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希，人民议院主席狄克曼等二百人。
    阿恩特是十八世纪末叶、十九世纪上半叶德国的爱国政治家，他积极主张德国统一。
