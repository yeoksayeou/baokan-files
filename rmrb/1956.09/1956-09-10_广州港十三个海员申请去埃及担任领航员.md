Title: Thirteen Guangzhou Port Seamen Apply to Work as Pilots in Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-09-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 9th: In the past two days, thirteen seamen from Guangzhou Port have applied to the South China Maritime Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Union to work as pilots in the Suez Canal in Egypt. These seamen are all graduates of nautical academies, have ten to twenty years of sailing experience, and have all served as captains for several years.

### 广州港十三个海员申请去埃及担任领航员

1956-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    广州港十三个海员申请去埃及担任领航员
    据新华社广州9日电　广州港在最近两天内有十三个海员向中国海员工会华南海运委员会提出应聘前往埃及苏彝士运河担任领航员工作的申请。这些海员都是从航海专科学校毕业，有十多年到二十年的航海经验，并都当过好几年船长。
