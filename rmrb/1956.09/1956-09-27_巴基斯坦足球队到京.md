Title: Pakistani Football Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-27
Page: 7

    Pakistani Football Team Arrives in Beijing
    (Our dispatch) The Pakistani National Football Team, a group of nineteen people led by team leader Mu. Saeed and deputy team leader A. Mirza, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 26th. They are the first Pakistani sports team to visit our country at the invitation of the All-China Sports Federation.
    The Pakistani National Football Team has rich international competition experience and is a strong football team in Asia. Before coming to our country, they visited and competed in Ceylon and Singapore, winning five games and drawing four. After arriving in our country, the results of the two games in Guangzhou and Shanghai were tied at 1-1 and 2-2 respectively.

### 巴基斯坦足球队到京

1956-09-27
第7版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦足球队到京
    本报讯　巴基斯坦国家足球队一行十九人，由领队穆·赛义德、副领队阿·米尔札率领，在26日晚乘火车到达北京。他们是应中华全国体育总会邀请来我国访问的第一个巴基斯坦的体育队伍。
    巴基斯坦国家足球队具有丰富的国际比赛经验，是亚洲的一支足球劲旅。他们在来我国以前，曾到锡兰和新加坡等地进行访问比赛，结果胜五场，平四场；到我国以后，在广州和上海的两次比赛结果，分别以一比一和二比二踢成平局。
