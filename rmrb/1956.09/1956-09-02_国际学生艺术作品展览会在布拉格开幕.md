Title: International Student Art Exhibition Opens in Prague
Author:
Date: 1956-09-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 1st. The International Student Art Exhibition, held by the Fourth World Student Congress, opened in Prague on August 30th.

The exhibition features artwork by students from fourteen countries including Great Britain, Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, China, Denmark, Chile, North Korea, Hungary, India, Poland, Romania, the Soviet Union, Sudan, and Switzerland.

China is one of the countries with the most exhibited works at this exhibition. Among the more than sixty exhibited works, there are traditional Chinese paintings, oil paintings, watercolors, woodcuts, and handicrafts. Some of the works are familiar to Chinese youth and students, such as Zhan Jianjun's "For a Better Tomorrow," Guo Weimo's "Go to Where the Motherland Needs You," and Liu Boshu's "Lassoing Horses."

The exhibition will run until September 10th.

### 国际学生艺术作品展览会在布拉格开幕

1956-09-02
第5版()
专栏：

    国际学生艺术作品展览会在布拉格开幕
    新华社布拉格1日电　第四次世界学生代表大会举办的国际学生艺术作品展览会8月30日在布拉格开幕。
    会上展出了英国、保加利亚、捷克斯洛伐克、中国、丹麦、智利、朝鲜、匈牙利、印度、波兰、罗马尼亚、苏联、苏丹和瑞士等十四个国家的学生的艺术作品。
    中国是在这个展览会中展出作品最多的国家之一。在展出的六十多件作品中，有国画、油画、水彩画、木刻和手工艺术品。其中有些作品是中国青年和学生所熟悉的，如詹健俊的“为了一个更美好的明天”，郭维墨的“到祖国需要的地方去”和刘勃舒的“套马”等。
    展览会将举行到9月10日。
