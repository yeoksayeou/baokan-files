Title: President Nasser Says an Agreement on the Canal Issue is Possible
Author:
Date: 1956-09-21
Page: 8

    President Nasser Says
    An Agreement on the Canal Issue is Possible
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 19th, TASS dispatch: According to the Czechoslovak News Agency, Egyptian President Nasser told reporters in Cairo on September 18th that it is very possible for Egypt to reach a practical and feasible agreement with relevant countries on some issues that are very important to the canal's user countries. For example, the issue of free navigation in the canal for all countries without discrimination; the issue of developing the canal to meet future navigation needs; and the issue of fair tolls, etc.
    When meeting with reporters from the Czechoslovak News Agency, “Rude Pravo,” and “Prace,” Nasser also pointed out that the legal owner of the Suez Canal—the Egyptian people—nationalizing the canal only means that Egypt is exercising a long-deprived right. He said: First of all, we recognize this fact: the Suez Canal must be maintained in the most efficient state to meet future navigation needs. This requires that a larger portion of the tolls received must be used to develop the canal.
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 20th: The Egyptian cabinet held a four-hour meeting on the evening of September 19th.
    After the meeting, a government spokesperson said that President Nasser explained the general situation to the ministers, including the recent developments in the Suez Canal issue.

### 纳赛尔总统说  就运河问题达成协议是可能的

1956-09-21
第8版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔总统说
    就运河问题达成协议是可能的
    据新华社20日讯　塔斯社布拉格19日讯：据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，埃及总统纳赛尔9月18日在开罗对新闻记者说：埃及同有关国家就对运河使用国来说很重要的一些问题达成切实可行的协议是很可能的。例如所有国家毫无区别地在运河自由航行的问题；发展运河以满足未来航行的需要问题以及公平的通行税问题等。
    纳赛尔在接见捷克斯洛伐克通讯社、“红色权利报”和“劳动报”的记者的时候还指出：苏彝士运河的合法主人——埃及人民——把运河收归国有只意味着，埃及行使了一个长期被剥夺的权利。他说：首先，我们认识到这个事实：必须使苏彝士运河保持在最有效的状态中，以满足未来的航行需要。这就要求必须把收到的通行税的较大部分用来发展运河。
    据新华社20日讯　开罗消息：埃及内阁9月19日晚上举行了一次历时四小时的会议。
    会后一位政府发言人说，纳赛尔总统在会上向部长们说明了一般形势，包括苏彝士运河问题的最近发展情况。
