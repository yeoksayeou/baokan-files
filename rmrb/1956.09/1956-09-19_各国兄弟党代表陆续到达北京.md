Title: Representatives of Fraternal Parties from Various Countries Arrive in Beijing One After Another
Author:
Date: 1956-09-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 18th. By the 18th, delegations, representatives, and observers from fifty-five communist and workers' parties of various countries had arrived in Beijing to attend the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China. The Japanese Communist Party delegation has been unable to come to Beijing due to the obstruction of the Japanese government.

    The representatives of the Communist and Workers' Parties of various countries who have recently arrived in Beijing include:

    The head of the Syrian-Lebanese Communist Party delegation and General Secretary of the Syrian-Lebanese Communist Party, Baghdadsh, and members: Sawayya, a member of the Central Committee of the Syrian-Lebanese Communist Party, and Salibi, a member of the Central Committee.

    The head of the Moroccan Communist Party delegation and First Secretary of the Secretariat of the Moroccan Communist Party, Yata, and a member, Maisuak, a member of the Political Bureau of the Moroccan Communist Party.

    The head of the Greek Communist Party delegation and a member of the Central Committee of the Greek Communist Party, Sterlingos, and a member, Patsalidis, a member of the Central Committee.

    A member of the Mexican Communist Party delegation and a member of the Political Bureau of the Mexican Communist Party, Fuentes-Garcia.

    Representative of the Uruguayan Communist Party and Organizational Secretary of the Uruguayan Communist Party, Suarez.

    In addition, the Brazilian Communist Party delegation, the Guatemalan Labor Party delegation, the Chilean Communist Party delegation, the Costa Rican People's Vanguard Party delegation, the Bolivian Communist Party delegation, the Cuban Popular Socialist Party delegation, and the Paraguayan Communist Party delegation have also arrived in Beijing.

### 各国兄弟党代表陆续到达北京

1956-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    各国兄弟党代表陆续到达北京
    新华社18日讯　到18日止，已经有五十五个国家的共产党和工人党的代表团、代表和观察员到达北京，前来参加中国共产党第八次全国代表大会。日本共产党代表团由于日本政府的阻挠直到今天还不能前来北京。
    最近陆续到达北京的各国共产党和工人党的代表中有：
    叙利亚—黎巴嫩共产党代表团团长、叙利亚—黎巴嫩共产党总书记巴格达什，团员有：叙利亚—黎巴嫩共产党中央委员萨瓦亚，中央委员萨利比。
    摩洛哥共产党代表团团长、摩洛哥共产党书记处第一书记亚塔，团员、摩洛哥共产党政治局委员迈苏阿克。
    希腊共产党代表团团长、希腊共产党中央委员斯特林果斯，团员、中央委员帕察利迪斯。
    墨西哥共产党代表团团员、墨西哥共产党政治局委员富恩特斯—加西亚。
    乌拉圭共产党代表、乌拉圭共产党组织书记苏阿雷斯。
    另外，到达北京的还有巴西共产党代表团，危地马拉劳动党代表团，智利共产党代表团，哥斯达黎加人民先锋党代表团，玻利维亚共产党代表团，古巴社会主义人民党代表团和巴拉圭共产党代表团。
