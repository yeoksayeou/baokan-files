Title: Belgian Peace Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Belgian Peace Delegation, headed by Professor Jean Nihon, comprising fifteen members, has arrived in Beijing in batches on the 1st and 3rd.

The Belgian Peace Delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace. The delegation includes people from cultural, artistic, educational, scientific, legal, and journalistic circles.

The delegation was welcomed at the airport by responsible persons from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace upon their arrival in Beijing.

### 比和平代表团到京

1956-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    比和平代表团到京
    新华社3日讯　以让·尼昂教授为首的比利时和平代表团一行十五人，已经在1日和3日分批到达北京。
    比利时和平代表团是应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请来中国作友好访问的。代表团中包括文化、艺术、教育、科学、法律、新闻等各界人士。
    代表团到达北京时，在机场上受到中国人民保卫世界和平委员会负责人的欢迎。
