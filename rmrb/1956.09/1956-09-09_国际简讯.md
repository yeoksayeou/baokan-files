*International Briefs*
    △ Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and Mikoyan, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, received the Greek parliamentary delegation currently visiting the Soviet Union on September 7 in the Kremlin. They had a conversation.
    △ A statement issued in Belgrade on the 7th said that Indonesian President Sukarno, at the invitation of Yugoslav President Tito, will visit Yugoslavia for six days on September 12.
                  (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 国际简讯

1956-09-09
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    ·国际简讯·
    △苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫和苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬9月7日在克里姆林宫接见了现在苏联访问的希腊议会代表团。他们进行了交谈。
    △贝尔格莱德7日发表的公告说，印度尼西亚总统苏加诺应南斯拉夫总统铁托的邀请，将在9月12日到南斯拉夫作六天的访问。
                  （据新华社讯）
