Title: Mongolian-Supplied Horses Have Been Accepted by China
Author:
Date: 1956-09-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator – The 47,000 Mongolian horses supplied by the Mongolian People's Republic under this year's Sino-Mongolian trade contract have been inspected and accepted by representatives from the Chinese side. The majority of the horses have already been delivered to the Chinese side at the Sino-Mongolian border. The head of the Chinese horse inspection team to Mongolia said that after the team arrived in Mongolia in May, they formed more than 20 working groups with the cooperation of relevant Mongolian departments and went to various parts of Mongolia for inspection. They received assistance from Mongolian herdsmen in their work.

### 蒙古供应的马匹  我国已验收完毕

1956-09-30
第3版()
专栏：

    蒙古供应的马匹
    我国已验收完毕
    据新华社乌兰巴托电　蒙古人民共和国根据今年中蒙贸易合同供应中国的四万七千匹蒙古马已经由中国方面代表验收完毕，大部分马匹并且已经送到中蒙边境交给中国方面。中国赴蒙验马代表队的负责人说，验马队在5月间到达蒙古以后，在蒙古有关部门的配合下，组成了二十多个工作组，分别到蒙古各地验收。他们在工作中得到了蒙古牧民的帮助。
