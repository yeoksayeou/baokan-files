Title: Egyptian Armed Forces Commander-in-Chief Announces: Egyptian Army Mobilization Complete
Author:
Date: 1956-09-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 3rd: Egyptian Armed Forces Commander-in-Chief Hakim Amer, in a statement published in today's "Al-Ahram", announced that the organization and preparation work of the Egyptian reserve forces, National Guard, and People's Combat Units have been completed. He also said that Egyptian troops have entered strategic positions, enabling them to cope with any situation.

Amer said that the supply of armaments to Egyptian troops is satisfactory in all aspects. The army's training in the use of modern equipment has also been completed.

### 埃武装部队总司令宣布  埃及军队动员完成

1956-09-04
第6版()
专栏：

    埃武装部队总司令宣布
    埃及军队动员完成
    新华社3日讯　开罗消息：埃及武装部队总司令哈基姆·阿密尔在今天的“消息报”上刊载的一篇声明中宣布：埃及的后备部队、国民警卫队和人民战斗单位的组织和准备工作都已经完成。他并且说：埃及的部队已经进入战略阵地，这使它们能够应付任何局面。
    阿密尔说：埃及部队的军火供应在各方面都是令人满意的。陆军使用现代装备的训练也已经完成。
