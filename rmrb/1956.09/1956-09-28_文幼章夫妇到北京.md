Title: Hewlett Johnson and his wife arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-28
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: Hewlett Johnson, Chairman of the Canadian Peace Congress, Permanent Member of the World Peace Council, and Vice-Chairman of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane today. The Johnsons are visiting China at the invitation of the China Peace Committee.

### 文幼章夫妇到北京

1956-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    文幼章夫妇到北京
    据新华社27日讯　加拿大和平大会主席、世界和平理事会常务委员、亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副主席文幼章博士和他的夫人在今天乘飞机到达北京。文幼章夫妇是应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请来我国访问的。
