Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet for Delegation from the Academy of Sciences of the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1956-09-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 13th. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, held a banquet at the Beijing International Club today to welcome the delegation from the Academy of Sciences of the German Democratic Republic.

### 郭沫若欢宴民主德国科学院代表团

1956-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若欢宴民主德国科学院代表团
    新华社13日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若今天在北京国际俱乐部举行宴会欢迎德意志民主共和国科学院代表团。
