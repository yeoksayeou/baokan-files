Title: Egyptian National Schools to Add Chinese, Russian and Other Subjects
Author:
Date: 1956-09-27
Page: 8

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 26th: The Egyptian Ministry of Education announced on September 22nd that it is currently arranging to add five foreign language subjects to national schools, namely Chinese, Russian, Spanish, German, and Italian. Currently, the only foreign languages taught in schools are English and French.

### 埃及国立学校将增设中文俄文等科

1956-09-27
第8版()
专栏：

    埃及国立学校将增设中文俄文等科
    新华社26日讯　开罗消息：埃及教育部9月22日宣布，目前正在国立学校里安排增设五种外国语文科目，即中文、俄文、西班牙文、德文和意大利文。目前学校教授的外国语文只有英文和法文。
