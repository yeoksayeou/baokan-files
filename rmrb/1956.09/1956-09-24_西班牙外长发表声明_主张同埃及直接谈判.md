Title: Spanish Foreign Minister Issues Statement Advocating Direct Negotiations with Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-09-24
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, London, September 23rd: Spanish Foreign Minister Atajo issued a statement on the evening of September 21st at the conclusion of the eighteen-nation conference in London, expressing Spain's firm advocacy of direct negotiations with Egypt on the Suez Canal issue.

Atajo said, "The door for possible negotiations with the Egyptian government remains open. If these negotiations should fail, one can still resort to other mediation methods within the scope of the United Nations. The Spanish delegation resolutely suggested adopting the method of direct negotiations with the Cairo government in order to reach an agreement. Therefore, it believes that only after these methods have been exhausted should an appeal be made to the United Nations."

### 西班牙外长发表声明  主张同埃及直接谈判

1956-09-24
第7版()
专栏：

    西班牙外长发表声明
    主张同埃及直接谈判
    据新华社23日讯　伦敦消息：西班牙外交部长阿塔霍9月21日晚上在伦敦十八国会议结束的时候发表声明，表示西班牙坚决主张就苏伊士运河问题同埃及举行直接谈判。
    阿塔霍说，“同埃及政府进行可能的谈判的大门仍旧敞开着。如果这些谈判竟然失败的话，仍旧可以求助于联合国范围内的其他斡旋的办法。西班牙代表团很坚决地建议采取同开罗政府直接谈判以便取得协议的方法，因此，它认为，只有在这些方法用完以后才应当向联合国呼吁。
