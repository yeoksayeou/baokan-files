Title: Sofia Holds Military Parade and Procession to Celebrate National Day
Author:
Date: 1956-09-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 10th - According to the Bulgarian Telegraph Agency, a grand military parade and mass procession were held in Sofia on September 9th to celebrate the 12th anniversary of Bulgaria's liberation.

　　From early morning, the streets and squares, adorned for the holiday, were thronged with tens of thousands of people, raising flags, placards, and banners. The entire city of Sofia was immersed in a festive atmosphere.

　　The military parade and procession were held on "September 9th" Square. Bulgarian Communist Party and government leaders such as Damyanov, Zhivkov, and Yugov stood on the rostrum of the Dimitrov Mausoleum to review the parade.

　　Bulgarian Minister of National Defense, General Panchevski, announced the start of the military parade.

　　Neatly formed detachments of various branches of the Bulgarian armed forces first marched past the reviewing stand.

　　After the military parade began the mass procession, columns of workers, agricultural laborers, scientific and artistic workers, and athletes marched grandly across the square. Workers and farmers held up placards and charts illustrating their production achievements. The parading crowds held up banners thanking the Soviet Union and the Bulgarian Communist Party, and expressing their determination to strive for strengthening international friendship and peaceful cooperation.

　　On the same day, festive parades were also held in other cities and some large towns in Bulgaria.

### 索非亚举行庆祝国庆的阅兵和游行

1956-09-11
第6版()
专栏：

    索非亚举行庆祝国庆的阅兵和游行
    新华社10日讯　据保加利亚通讯社报道：9月9日，索非亚举行了盛大的阅兵和群众游行，庆祝保加利亚解放12周年。
    从清晨起，披上节日盛装的街道和广场上就涌现了成千上万的人群，他们高举着旗帜、标语牌和横幅标语。整个索非亚都沉浸在节日的气氛中。
    阅兵和游行是在“九九”广场上举行的。站在季米特洛夫陵墓台上检阅的有保加利亚共产党和政府领导人达米扬诺夫、日夫科夫和于哥夫等。
    保加利亚国防部长潘切夫斯基大将宣布阅兵式开始。
    保加利亚各兵种的整齐的列队首先走过检阅台。
    群众游行开始后，工人、农业劳动者、科学艺术工作者和运动员的队伍浩浩荡荡地走过广场。工人、农民高举着说明获得生产成就的标语牌和图表。游行的人群高举着感谢苏联、感谢保加利亚共产党的标语，并且表示要为争取加强国际友谊和和平合作而斗争。
    同一天，保加利亚的其他城市和一些大的村镇也举行了节日游行。
