Title: Chinese Architectural Photo Exhibition Held in Poland
Author:
Date: 1956-09-13
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 12th. The Chinese Architectural Photo Exhibition, organized by the Polish Architects Association, recently opened at the Krakow Artists' House.
    The exhibition displays 350 photos introducing ancient and modern Chinese architecture.

### 波举行中国建筑图片展览

1956-09-13
第5版()
专栏：

    波举行中国建筑图片展览
    新华社华沙12日电　波兰建筑师协会举办的中国建筑图片展览会最近在克拉科夫造型艺术工作者之家开幕。
    展览会展出了三百五十件介绍中国古代和现代建筑物的图片。
