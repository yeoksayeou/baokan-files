Title: Italian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Italian cultural delegation of eleven people, led by former Italian Prime Minister Ferruccio Parri, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation has come to China for a visit at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

Those who welcomed the Italian guests at the airport included Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Chen Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Mao Yisheng and others, members of the Chinese cultural delegation who had previously visited Italy.

### 意大利文化代表团到达北京

1956-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    意大利文化代表团到达北京
    新华社18日讯　由意大利前总理费·帕里率领的意大利文化代表团一行十一人，在今天乘飞机到达北京。代表团是应中国人民对外文化协会的邀请来中国访问的。
    到飞机场欢迎意大利客人的，有中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙，中国人民外交学会副会长陈翰笙和以前访问过意大利的中国文化代表团团员茅以升等人。
