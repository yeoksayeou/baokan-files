Title: New Achievements in World Sports
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-08
Page: 4
    New Achievements in World Sports
    Men's 3000-meter Race
    British famous long-distance runner Gordon Pirie created a new world record in the 3000-meter race in Malmö, Sweden on September 4, with a time of 7 minutes 52.8 seconds. The original world record of 7 minutes 55.6 seconds was jointly held by him and Hungarian runner Iharos.
    Men's 3200-meter Four-Man Relay
    A Belgian four-man relay team broke the previous world record for the 3200-meter four-man relay with an excellent time of 7 minutes 15.8 seconds in Brussels in August.
    The original official world record was 7 minutes 26.8 seconds, created by Soviet athletes in 1954. In May of this year, Soviet athletes had created a new record of 7 minutes 26.2 seconds.
    Long Jump
    A Polish schoolgirl, Denska-Krysińska, only sixteen years old, achieved a result of 6.35 meters in the long jump in August, breaking the world's highest female long jump record of 6.31 meters, created by the famous Soviet athlete Vinogradova in 1955.
    Denska-Krysińska achieved this result in an international competition with seventeen countries participating in Budapest.
    Women's 1500-meter Freestyle
    Dutch female swimmer Jans Koster broke the world record for the women's 1500-meter freestyle on August 21 in Utrecht with a time of 20 minutes 22.8 seconds. The original world record was 20 minutes 46.5 seconds, created by another Dutch female athlete, Leny de Nijs.
    Shot Put
    American athlete Parry O'Brien broke the world shot put record on September 3 in Oregon, USA, with a result of 19.059 meters. In June of this year, he created a world record of 18.694 meters.
    Cycling
    Soviet athlete G. Martynov completed a 100-kilometer course on a bicycle at the end of August in 2 hours 28 minutes 31 seconds. His result was 4.4 seconds faster than the world record of 2 hours 28 minutes 35.4 seconds set by Italian athlete Profita in October of last year.
    On September 5, French cyclist Vitel completed five kilometers in Milan, Italy, in 6 minutes 10.4 seconds. The original record of 6 minutes 13.2 seconds was held by Biazza of Italy.
　(Xinhua News Agency, 7th)

### 世界体育运动的新成绩

1956-09-08
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    世界体育运动的新成绩
    男子三千公尺赛跑
    英国著名长跑家戈登·皮里9月4日在瑞典的马尔摩创造了三千公尺跑的世界新成绩，时间是7分52.8秒。原来7分55.6秒的世界纪录是他和匈牙利的伊哈罗斯两人共同保持的。
    男子三千二百公尺四人接力
    比利时的一个四人接力队8月间在布鲁塞尔以7分15.8秒的优秀成绩打破了以前三千二百公尺四人接力的世界纪录。
    原来的正式世界纪录是7分26.8秒，由苏联运动员在1954年创造的。今年5月间，苏联运动员曾经创造过7分26秒2的新成绩。
    跳远
    一个才十六岁的波兰女学生登斯卡—克里辛斯卡8月间获得了跳远6.35公尺的成绩，打破了苏联著名运动员维诺格拉多娃在1955年创造的6.31公尺世界女子跳远最高成绩。
    登斯卡—克里辛斯卡是在布达佩斯的一次有十七个国家参加的国际比赛中创造这个成绩的。
    女子一千五百公尺自由泳
    荷兰女游泳家江斯·科斯特8月21日在乌得勒支以20分22.8秒的成绩打破女子一千五百公尺自由泳的世界纪录。原来的世界纪录是20分46.5秒，它的创造者是另一位荷兰女运动员勒尼·德尼斯。
    铅球
    美国运动员帕里·奥勒莱恩9月3日在美国俄勒冈打破了世界掷铅球的纪录，成绩是19.059公尺。今年6月间他创造的世界纪录是18.694公尺。
    自行车
    苏联运动员吉·马尔蒂诺夫8月底骑自行车用2小时28分31秒的时间跑完一百公里的路程。他的成绩比意大利运动员普罗菲塔去年10月所创2小时28分35.4秒的世界纪录快了4.4秒。
    9月5日法国自行车运动员维特尔在意大利的米兰用6分10.4秒的时间跑完五公里。原来的纪录6分13.2秒是意大利的比阿萨保持的。
　（新华社7日讯）
