Title: Hong Kong and Macao Athletes Arrive in Guangzhou One After Another to Participate in the Chinese Olympic Team Selection Competition
Author:
Date: 1956-09-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 29th. The Macao basketball team and swimming team, totaling twenty-three people, led by Cui Deqi, Vice Chairman of the Macao Chinese General Chamber of Commerce, and the Hong Kong basketball team of twelve, led by Qian Jinglin, former executive member of the Hong Kong Basketball Association, arrived in Guangzhou on the afternoon of the 28th. They are preparing to go to Beijing to participate in the Chinese Olympic Team Selection Competition.

The person in charge of the Guangdong Branch of the All-China Sports Federation announced today that as of the evening of the 28th, thirty-eight Hong Kong and Macao athletes have arrived in Guangzhou to report to the reception station. Many of them are renowned athletes and promising newcomers in Hong Kong and Macao. Feng Yaoshen, Bao Mazhuang, Zhou Shirong, Liang Bin, Yu Zhaomiao, and others from the Macao basketball team are all excellent athletes in the local basketball community. Wu Naxiu, Lin Jiliang, Zhong Zhenpei, Pan Weiwen, and others from the Hong Kong basketball team were once members of the Hong Kong United Basketball Team, the strongest in Hong Kong, and have represented Hong Kong in competitions in Southeast Asia. Wu Shigen, the captain of the Macao swimming team, is the champion of the 100-meter freestyle in the Macao Open in 1955 and 1956. Team member He Changming holds the Macao record for the 100-meter breaststroke in 1955 and 1956. He Zhaoxiong and Feng Runlin are the champion and runner-up of the 50-meter and 100-meter backstroke in the 1956 Macao Chinese Open. He Zhuokun is also a good freestyle swimmer in Macao.

Wang Haolin, a Hong Kong swimmer who reported earlier in the past few days, is the first place in the individual overall score in this year's Hong Kong Chinese Open. Zeng Ruolan, a young female student, is the champion of the 100-meter butterfly in Group A in the 1956 Hong Kong Chinese Open. Meng Yujun is a good female 100-meter breaststroke athlete who has achieved a time of one minute and thirty seconds in Hong Kong.

### 参加中国奥林匹克运动队选拔赛  港澳运动员陆续到达广州

1956-09-30
第3版()
专栏：

    参加中国奥林匹克运动队选拔赛
    港澳运动员陆续到达广州
    据新华社广州29日电　由澳门中华总商会副理事长崔德祺率领的澳门篮球队和游泳队共二十三人，以及由前香港篮球联合会执行委员钱景麟率领的香港篮球队一行十二人在28日下午到达广州，他们准备前往北京参加中国奥林匹克运动队选拔赛。
    据中华全国体育总会广东省分会的负责人今天宣布：截至28日晚为止，先后来到广州向接待站报到的港、澳运动员已经有三十八人。他们当中有许多是在港、澳享有声誉的运动员和新进的好手。澳门篮球队的冯耀燊、鲍马壮、周适荣、梁彬、俞兆渺等都是当地篮球界的优秀运动员。香港篮球队的吴那秀、林继良、锺振沛、潘炜文等曾经是香港实力最强的港联篮球队的队员，并且代表香港到过东南亚各地比赛。澳门游泳队的队长吴仕根是1955年和1956年全澳门公开赛的一百公尺自由泳冠军。队员何长明是澳门1955年和1956年一百公尺蛙泳纪录保持者。何兆雄和冯润霖是澳门1956年华人公开赛五十公尺和一百公尺仰泳的冠军和亚军。何焯昆也是澳门的一名自由泳好手。
    前几天个别先行报到的香港游泳运动员王浩林是今年香港华人公开赛的个人总分的第一名。年轻的女学生曾若兰是1956年香港华人公开赛甲组一百公尺蝶泳冠军。蒙余君是曾经在香港取得过一分三十秒成绩的女子一百公尺蛙泳好手。
