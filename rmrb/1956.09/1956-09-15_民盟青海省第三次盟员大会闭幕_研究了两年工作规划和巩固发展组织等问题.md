Title: The Third Congress of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the China Democratic League Concludes, Studying a Two-Year Work Plan and Issues such as Consolidating and Developing the Organization

Author:
Date: 1956-09-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Xining, September 14th: The seven-day Third Congress of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the China Democratic League concluded on the afternoon of the 12th after electing the Second Committee of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the China Democratic League.

The congress spent a considerable amount of time studying how the league's work in Qinghai Province could adapt to the current situation's development. Based on the requirements of the situation and the actual conditions of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the China Democratic League, the congress studied the work plan of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the China Democratic League for the next two years, and formulated many specific measures regarding ideological education for league members, league members' contact with the masses, and consolidation and development of the organization.

The congress affirmed the role played by the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the China Democratic League in coordinating with the central work of the Party and government and implementing the Party's policy towards intellectuals in the past two years. It pointed out the achievements made by league members engaged in education in improving teaching methods and improving teaching quality. At the same time, it promoted the spirit of democracy and examined the shortcomings of the league's work. League members attending the meeting sharply criticized the irresponsibility of certain leaders of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the China Democratic League, their lack of attention to organizational life, and their suppression of democracy.

Ji Chunguang, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Wu Ren, Minister of the Propaganda Department, attended the meeting to express their congratulations.

### 民盟青海省第三次盟员大会闭幕  研究了两年工作规划和巩固发展组织等问题

1956-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    民盟青海省第三次盟员大会闭幕
    研究了两年工作规划和巩固发展组织等问题
    据新华社西宁14日电　开了七天的中国民主同盟青海省第三次盟员大会，在12日下午选出了民盟青海省第二届委员会以后闭幕。
    大会用较长的时间研究了青海省的盟务工作如何适应当前形势的发展，并根据形势的要求和民盟青海省组织的实际情况，研究了青海省民盟组织今后两年的工作规划，拟订了关于对盟员进行思想教育工作以及盟员的联系群众工作和巩固与发展组织的许多具体措施。
    大会肯定了两年来青海省民盟组织在配合党政中心工作、贯彻党对知识分子的政策方面所起的作用，指出了从事教育工作的盟员在改进教学方法、提高教学质量上获得的成绩，同时发扬民主精神，检查了盟务工作的缺点。到会盟员对青海省民盟组织某些领导人员的不负责任、不重视组织生活和压制民主的行为提出了尖锐的批评。
    中共青海省委员会统一战线工作部部长冀春光和宣传部部长午人，都到会表示祝贺。
