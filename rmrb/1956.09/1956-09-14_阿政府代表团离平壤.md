Title: Albanian Government Delegation Leaves Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1956-09-14
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 13th - The Albanian government delegation, headed by Shehu and Hoxha, has concluded its visit to Korea and left Pyongyang by plane this morning. Many people in Pyongyang welcomed them on both sides of the street and at the airport.
The Korean state leaders, headed by Premier Kim Il-sung, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Korea went to the airport to see them off.
Shehu delivered a farewell speech at the airport, expressing his gratitude for the warm welcome and hospitality of the Korean people.

### 阿政府代表团离平壤

1956-09-14
第6版()
专栏：

    阿政府代表团离平壤
    新华社平壤13日电　以谢胡、霍查为首的阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团已经结束在朝鲜的访问，在今天上午乘飞机离开平壤。平壤市许多群众在街道两旁和飞机场上欢送。
    以内阁首相金日成为首的朝鲜国家领导人和各国驻朝鲜使节都到机场送行。
    谢胡在机场上致告别词，他对于朝鲜人民的热情欢迎和款待表示感谢。
