Title: Delegation from the Diet Members' League for Promoting Japan-China Trade Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-30
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. A delegation of thirteen members from the Japanese Diet Members' League for Promoting Japan-China Trade, led by Deputy Head Seiichi Katsumata, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They are visiting our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.
    Those who went to the airport to greet them included Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Vice Presidents Chen Hansheng, Qiao Guanhua, and Ji Chaoding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and others.
    The head of the delegation, Masanosuke Ikeda, will arrive in Beijing at the beginning of next month.

### 日议员促进日中贸易联盟代表团到京

1956-09-30
第3版()
专栏：

    日议员促进日中贸易联盟代表团到京
    新华社29日讯　日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟代表团一行十三人，由副团长胜间田清一率领，今天下午乘飞机抵北京。他们是应中国人民外交学会的邀请来我国访问的。
    前往机场迎接的有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、副会长陈翰笙、乔冠华和中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎等多人。
    代表团团长池田正之辅将在下月初到京。
