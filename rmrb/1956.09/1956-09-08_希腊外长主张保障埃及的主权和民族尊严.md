Title: Greek Foreign Minister Advocates Safeguarding Egypt's Sovereignty and National Dignity
Author:
Date: 1956-09-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Athens dispatch: Greek Foreign Minister Averoff said on September 6th that Greece advocates "safeguarding Egypt's sovereignty and national dignity." Under this premise, Greece is concerned about the freedom of navigation in the Suez Canal, the proper maintenance of the canal, and the stability of transit fees.

Averoff stated that Greece opposes the involvement of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization, of which it is a member, in the Suez Canal dispute.

The day before, a Greek Foreign Ministry spokesman also said that the Greek government had told the North Atlantic Treaty Organization that, under the current political situation, it would be a very bad choice for a British naval admiral to serve as the commander of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization's Mediterranean naval forces.

### 希腊外长主张保障埃及的主权和民族尊严

1956-09-08
第6版()
专栏：

    希腊外长主张保障埃及的主权和民族尊严
    新华社7日讯　雅典消息：希腊外交大臣阿维罗夫9月6日说，希腊主张“保障埃及的主权和民族尊严”。在这样的前提下，希腊关怀苏彝士运河的自由通航，关怀对运河的妥善保养和通行费用的稳定。
    阿维罗夫表示，希腊反对它所参加的北大西洋公约组织卷入苏彝士运河的争端中去。
    在这以前一天，希腊外交部发言人还说，希腊政府已经告诉北大西洋公约组织说，在目前政治局势下，由英国海军将领担任北大西洋公约组织地中海海军司令一职，将是一个很坏的选择。
