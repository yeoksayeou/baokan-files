Title: Typhoon and Frost Forecast
Author:
Date: 1956-09-25
Page: 6

Central Meteorological Research Institute issued typhoon and frost forecasts at 8 p.m. on the 24th:

    The typhoon that landed north of Xiamen last night has dissipated after entering northern Fujian. Another typhoon in the Southwest Pacific is currently moving northwest at a speed of 20-26 kilometers per hour. It is expected that by 2 p.m. tomorrow, the center of the typhoon will reach the sea 350 kilometers east of Taiwan, at 25 degrees north latitude and 125.5 degrees east longitude. In the next 24 hours, the wind force in the East China Sea will increase to level 6-8, and the Yellow Sea will also have northeasterly winds of about level 6 due to the influence of cold air.

    The Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Yanbei area of ​​Shanxi Province, Zhangjiakou area of ​​Hebei Province, mountainous areas of Chengde area, western mountainous areas of Liaoning Province, and western mountainous areas of Jilin Province will experience frost or frost after the wind stops and the sky clears tomorrow morning.

### 台风和霜冻预告

1956-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    台风和霜冻预告
    中央气象科学研究所24日下午八点发布台风和霜冻预告：
    昨天晚上在厦门以北登陆的台风已经在进入福建北部后消失。在西南太平洋另一个台风，目前正以每小时二十——二十六公里的速度向西北方向移动，预计明天下午两点，台风中心将达到台湾以东三百五十公里的海面上，就是北纬二十五度东经一百二十五点五度的地方。未来二十四小时内东海海面的风力将加强到六——八级，黄海因冷空气的影响也有六级左右的偏东北风。
    内蒙古自治区、山西省的雁北专区、河北省的张家口专区、承德专区的山地区、辽宁省的西部山地和吉林省西部山地区，明天早晨风停天晴后，有霜冻或霜出现。
