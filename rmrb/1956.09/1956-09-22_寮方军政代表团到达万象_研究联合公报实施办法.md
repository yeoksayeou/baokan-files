Title: Laotian Patriotic Forces Political and Military Delegation Arrives in Vientiane to Study Implementation of Joint Communiqué
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-22
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 21st: According to Vientiane Radio, the political and military delegation of the Laotian Patriotic Forces has arrived in Vientiane. This delegation will be studying with the delegation of the Royal Lao Government the implementation methods for the joint communiqué signed by both sides in early August.

### 寮方军政代表团到达万象  研究联合公报实施办法

1956-09-22
第5版()
专栏：

    寮方军政代表团到达万象
    研究联合公报实施办法
    新华社河内21日电　据万象电台广播，寮国战斗部队的政治和军事代表团已经到达万象。这个代表团即将同老挝王国政府代表团研究双方在8月初所签订的联合公报的实施办法。
