Title: Italian Peace Activist and Psychologist, Mrs. Miriam Or, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, September 2nd. Italian peace activist and psychologist, Mrs. Miriam Or, and her son, arrived in Beijing on the 2nd at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.
    The person in charge of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace went to the airport to welcome them.

### 意大利和平人士、心理学家  米丽安·奥尔夫人到京

1956-09-03
第5版()
专栏：

    意大利和平人士、心理学家
    米丽安·奥尔夫人到京
    新华社2日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请来我国访问的意大利和平人士、心理学家米丽安·奥尔夫人和他的儿子在2日抵达北京。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的负责人前往机场欢迎。
