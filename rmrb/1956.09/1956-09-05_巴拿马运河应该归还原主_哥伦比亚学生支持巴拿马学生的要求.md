Title: The Panama Canal Should Be Returned to its Original Owner: Colombian Students Support the Demands of Panamanian Students
Author:
Date: 1956-09-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Bogota dispatch: According to a report in "Peace Daily" on September 3rd, many students in the city of Medellin, Colombia, are supporting the demands of Panamanian students to nationalize the Panama Canal in the same way that Egypt nationalized the Suez Canal.

"Peace Daily" published a declaration signed by many students, stating that, "Based on the principle of student solidarity, we feel obligated to unite the strength of our intellectual circles to support the attitude taken by our Panamanian friends." The declaration believes that since the Suez Canal has been nationalized, as a logical result, the canal in the nation of Panama should also be nationalized.

### 巴拿马运河应该归还原主  哥伦比亚学生支持巴拿马学生的要求

1956-09-05
第6版()
专栏：

    巴拿马运河应该归还原主
    哥伦比亚学生支持巴拿马学生的要求
    新华社4日讯　波哥大消息：据“和平报”9月3日报道，在哥伦比亚的美得林城，有许多学生正在支持巴拿马学生提出的要求，巴拿马学生要采取埃及对苏彝士运河所采取的同样办法把巴拿马运河收归国有。
    “和平报”刊登了由许多学生签名发表的一个宣言，这个宣言说，根据学生团结的原则，我们觉得有责任联合我们知识界的力量来支持我们的巴拿马朋友所采取的态度。宣言认为，既然苏彝士运河国有化了，作为一个逻辑的结果，在巴拿马国家里的运河也应该国有化。
