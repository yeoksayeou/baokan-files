Title: Sukarno's Collection of Speeches Published in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. "A Collection of Speeches by Dr. Sukarno, President of the Republic of Indonesia" has been published by World Knowledge Press and is available in Beijing starting today.

This collection contains seventeen important speeches delivered by Dr. Sukarno from 1945 to 1956, and an appendix containing the 1930 article "Indonesia Accuses," totaling over 360,000 words. The collection reflects the heroic struggles of the Indonesian people to overthrow colonial rule, achieve national independence, defend world peace, and build a prosperous and happy society.

The first speech in the collection is "The Birth of the Five Principles of Nationhood," delivered at the meeting of the Indonesian "Preparatory Committee for Independence" in June 1945. In this speech, Dr. Sukarno elucidated his political views. The five principles he formulated based on the Indonesian situation: nationalism, humanitarianism, democracy, social prosperity, and belief in divinity, have been incorporated into the Indonesian constitution. The collection also includes Dr. Sukarno's speech "Let the New Asia and the New Africa Be Born!" at the opening session of the Asian-African Conference in 1955, as well as his speech at the joint session of both houses of the U.S. Congress and his speech "The Age of Nationalism in Asia and Africa" at the National Press Club in Washington during his visit to the United States this year.

### 苏加诺演讲集在北京出版

1956-09-29
第2版()
专栏：

    苏加诺演讲集在北京出版
    新华社28日讯　印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺博士的演讲集，已由世界知识社出版，今天开始在北京发行。
    这部演讲集，收集有苏加诺博士从1945年到1956年间发表的重要演说十七篇和附录1930年的“印度尼西亚控诉”一文，共三十六万多字。演讲集反映了印度尼西亚人民为推翻殖民统治、争取民族独立、保卫世界和平，以及为建设繁荣幸福的社会而进行的英勇斗争。
    演讲集的第一篇是1945年6月在印度尼西亚“独立准备调查会”会议上的演讲“建国五原则的诞生”。苏加诺博士在这篇演说中阐明了他的政治观点。他根据印度尼西亚的情况拟订的五项原则：民族主义、人道主义、民主、社会繁荣、信仰神道，已经载入印度尼西亚的宪法中。演讲集还收入苏加诺博士1955年在亚非会议开幕会上的演说“让新的亚洲和新的非洲诞生吧！”以及今年他访问美国时在美国国会两院联席会议上发表的演说和在华盛顿的全国新闻俱乐部的演讲“亚非两洲的民族主义时代”。
