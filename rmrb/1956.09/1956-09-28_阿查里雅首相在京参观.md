Title: Prime Minister Acharya Visits Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 27th – The Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Tanka Prasad Acharya, along with his wife and accompanying high-ranking officials, visited the State-owned Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Mill this morning. The distinguished Nepalese guests toured the mill's various workshops, workers' dining hall, and dormitories. After the visit, Prime Minister Acharya told the mill's director that he was deeply impressed by the spirit of serving the people he witnessed at the mill.

Chen Weiji, Vice Minister of the Textile Industry, Pan Zili, China's Ambassador to Nepal, and Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, accompanied the guests on their visit. This afternoon, Prime Minister Acharya's wife and some of the accompanying officials visited the Forbidden City.

### 阿查里雅首相在京参观

1956-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    阿查里雅首相在京参观
    新华社27日讯　尼泊尔王国首相坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅和他的夫人以及随行的高级官员，今天上午参观了国营北京第二棉纺织厂。尼泊尔贵宾们参观了这个厂的各个车间、工人食堂和宿舍等。阿查里雅首相在参观以后对这个厂的厂长说，他对在这个厂里所看到的为人民服务的精神印象很深。
    纺织工业部副部长陈维稷、我国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和外交部礼宾司副司长马振武等陪同客人参观。今天下午，阿查里雅首相夫人和部分随行官员参观了故宫。
