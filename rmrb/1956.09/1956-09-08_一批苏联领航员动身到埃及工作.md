Title: A Batch of Soviet Pilots Depart for Work in Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-09-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 7th -- TASS reports: Several Soviet pilots have accepted recruitment by the Egyptian Suez Canal Authority and have departed for Egypt to work as pilots. Among them, Vasilyevsky, Zhivoy, and Tabakar all have experience navigating the Suez Canal several times.

    At the same time, the Egyptian Embassy in the Soviet Union and the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs continue to receive applications from Soviet sailors hoping to work in Egypt. Some of these sailors serve in Soviet ports, while others are sailing on the Baltic Sea.
