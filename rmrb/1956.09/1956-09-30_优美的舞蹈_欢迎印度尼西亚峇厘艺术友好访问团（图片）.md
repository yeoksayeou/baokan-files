Graceful Dances
    —Welcome to the Indonesian Bali Art Friendship Delegation
    The Indonesian Bali Art Friendship Delegation, invited by the China-Indonesia Friendship Association to visit and perform in our country, began its performances in Beijing on September 23. Many of their beautiful artistic programs have been warmly welcomed by the audience.
    Top photo: The head of the Indonesian Bali Art Friendship Delegation, Saleh Umar, delivering a speech at the opening ceremony.
                Our reporter Yuan Yiping Wang Zhiyuan Cang Shi photo
    Bamboo Tube Dance Performer: Ni Gedou Garsning
    Banci Semilang Dance Performer: Ni Lu Zawan
    Ni Doya Atiani's makeup aroused great interest among young Chinese dancers Gao Shide and Wang Lijuan.
    Adalong Butterfly Dance Performers: Ni Yoman Sudani, Ni Made Wageli, Ni Made Lida, Ni Made Xinglai
    Demang Milin Dance Performer: Ni Made Tarmi
    Bee Dance Performer: Ni Guster Laga
    Daruna Dance Performers: Ni Lu Manik, Ni Gedou Garsning
    Gezhak Dance Collective Performance
    Gebia Angkada Duda Dance Performer: I Ba Oga
    Chinese dancers Dai Ailian and Zhang Jun are learning Balinese dance from Indonesian artists.
    The Indonesian Bali Art Friendship Delegation's Jiameluan Orchestra is making a recording performance for the Central People's Broadcasting Station.

### 优美的舞蹈——欢迎印度尼西亚峇厘艺术友好访问团（图片）

专栏：

    优美的舞蹈
    ——欢迎印度尼西亚峇厘艺术友好访问团
    应中国印度尼西亚友好协会邀请来我国访问演出的印度尼西亚峇厘艺术友好访问团，9月23日开始在北京演出。他们表演的许多优美的艺术节目，都受到观众的热烈欢迎。
    上图：印度尼西亚峇厘艺术友好访问团团长萨勒·乌玛尔在演出开幕式上致词。
                本报记者　袁毅平　王志渊　苍石摄
    竹筒舞　表演者：尼·格都·加斯宁
    班其·思米郎舞　表演者：尼·路·楂宛
    尼·多娅·阿特娅妮的化妆引起了我国青年舞蹈工作者高师德、王力娟很大的兴趣。
    阿达隆蝴蝶舞　表演者：尼·约曼·苏达尼、尼·玛德·娃格利、尼·玛德·利达、尼·玛德·新格莱
    德芒米林舞　表演者：尼·玛德·塔尔米
    蜜蜂舞　表演者：尼·格斯特·拉加
    达鲁纳舞　表演者：尼·鲁·玛尼克、尼·格都·加斯宁
    格楂克舞　集体演出
    格比阿·盎卡达·都达舞　表演者：伊·巴·奥加
    我国舞蹈家戴爱莲和舞蹈演员张均在向印度尼西亚艺术家学习峇厘舞蹈。
