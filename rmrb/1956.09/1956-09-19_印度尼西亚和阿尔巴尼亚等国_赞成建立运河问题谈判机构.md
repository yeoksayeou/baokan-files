Title: Indonesia, Albania, and Other Countries Support the Establishment of a Negotiating Body for the Canal Issue
Author:
Date: 1956-09-19
Page: 8
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 17th: The Indonesian government has notified Egyptian President Nasser that Indonesia supports Egypt's proposal to establish a negotiating body to prepare for a new international conference.

Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Subandrio, told Indonesian journalists that Indonesia had notified Egypt that Indonesia, in principle, agreed to hold a second international conference on the Suez issue, whether the conference was held within the scope of the United Nations or in the form of a new London conference. However, this conference must, as Nasser suggested, have a broader basis and adopt a negotiating mechanism in which all points of view can be raised.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 18th: According to the Albanian News Agency, the Albanian government, in a reply to the Egyptian government's note on the Suez Canal issue, stated that the Albanian government welcomes the Egyptian government's statement and the proposed recommendations, and expresses its willingness to participate in the work of the negotiating body proposed by Egypt.

Xinhua News Agency, September 18th: Panama City News: The Panamanian government issued a statement on September 17th announcing its support for Egypt's proposal to establish a negotiating body representing the different opinions of the Suez Canal's users.

According to the Associated Press, U.S. officials "expressed concern" about Panama's attitude.

### 印度尼西亚和阿尔巴尼亚等国  赞成建立运河问题谈判机构

1956-09-19
第8版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚和阿尔巴尼亚等国
    赞成建立运河问题谈判机构
    据新华社雅加达17日电　印度尼西亚政府已经通知埃及总统纳赛尔，印度尼西亚赞成埃及关于成立谈判机构以准备新的国际会议的建议。
    外交部秘书长苏班德里奥对印度尼西亚新闻记者说，印度尼西亚已经通知埃及，印度尼西亚在原则上赞成举行第二次关于苏彝士问题的国际会议，无论这次会议是属于联合国范围内或是以一种新的伦敦会议的形式来举行。但是，这个会议必须像纳赛尔所建议的那样，具有更广泛的基础，和采用谈判机构的方式，在这个机构里，所有的观点都可以提出来。
    据新华社布拉格18日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道，阿尔巴尼亚政府在一项回答埃及政府关于苏彝士运河问题的照会的复照中指出，阿尔巴尼亚政府欢迎埃及政府的声明和所提出的建议，并表示愿意参加埃及建议的谈判机构的工作。
    据新华社18日讯　巴拿马城消息：巴拿马政府在9月17日发表声明，宣布支持埃及提出的关于成立一个代表苏彝士运河各使用国的不同意见的谈判机构的建议。
    据美联社说，美国官方人士对巴拿马的这种态度“表示关切”。
