Title: President of the Japanese Merchandise Exhibition, Shozo Murata, Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. Shozo Murata, President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association and President of the Japanese Merchandise Exhibition, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Motozo Goto, representative of Saburo Nango, Chairman of the Japan Export and Import Association, also arrived on the same plane.

At the airport, Shozo Murata said in a statement:
  “Through the commodity exhibition, the people of the two countries will deepen mutual understanding and trust. This will not only strengthen the development of Sino-Japanese trade and make the friendly relations between the two countries closer, but will soon become the basis for the normalization of diplomatic relations.”

Those who went to the airport to welcome him included Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Vice Chairmen Lei Renmin and Ji Chaoding; Committee Member Lu Xuzhang; Deputy Secretary-General Wen Shizhen; and Cao Zhongshu and Feng Tiecheng, the head and deputy head of the Chinese Commodity Exhibition Group who went to Japan last year to preside over the Chinese Commodity Exhibition.

Eichi Shukutani, head of the Japanese Commodity Exhibition Group, deputy heads Shozo Morii and Takashi Kataoka, and Japanese personages in Beijing, including Togo Kameda, also went to the airport to welcome him.

Tonight, Shozo Murata attended a reception banquet held by Nan Hanchen.

### 日本商品展览会总裁  村田省藏到达北京

1956-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    日本商品展览会总裁
    村田省藏到达北京
    新华社28日讯　日本国际贸易促进协会会长、日本商品展览会总裁村田省藏今天下午乘飞机到达北京。同机到达的还有日本输出入协会理事长南乡三郎的代表后藤元三。
    村田省藏在机场发表谈话说：
  “两国人民通过商品展览会将加深相互了解和信赖。由此不仅加强日中贸易的发展和使两国的友好关系更加紧密，而且不久必会成为国交正常化的基础。”
    前往机场欢迎的，有中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸，副主席雷任民、冀朝鼎，委员卢绪章，副秘书长文士桢和去年曾去日本主持中国商品展览会的中国商品展览团正副团长曹中枢、冯铁城等。
      日本商品展览团团长宿谷荣一，副团长森井庄内、片冈峻和在京的日本人士龟田东伍等也到机场欢迎。
    今天晚上，村田省藏出席了南汉宸的招待宴会。
