Title: Vietnamese Government Leaders Visit Chinese Exhibition, Praising Our Country's Achievements on the Road of Socialist Industrialization
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-07
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 6th. Wang Jiujing, head of the Chinese Economic Exhibition Delegation, held a grand reception yesterday evening for the opening of the Exhibition of Achievements in China's Economic Construction.

After the banquet, Chairman Ho Chi Minh arrived at the exhibition hall. He viewed the various exhibits with pleasure.

Among the more than four hundred guests attending the banquet were Vo Nguyen Giap, Hoang Quoc Viet, Phan Anh, and many senior officials of the Vietnamese government, representatives of the Vietnamese National Assembly, representatives of various democratic parties and mass organizations, as well as foreign diplomatic envoys and experts stationed in Vietnam.

In his speech at the banquet, Delegation Head Wang Jiujing expressed his gratitude to the Vietnamese government and people for their enthusiastic assistance in the preparation of the Chinese Exhibition Hall, and hoped that this exhibition would further consolidate and develop the friendship between the peoples of the two countries and promote the development of economic cooperation and trade relations between China and Vietnam.

Vietnamese Minister of Commerce Phan Anh also spoke at the banquet. He said that the Vietnamese people who had visited the exhibition expressed their admiration for the achievements made in China's economic construction; those who had not visited were eagerly hoping for an opportunity to visit soon. Phan Anh said that the warm welcome of the Chinese Exhibition Hall by the Vietnamese people showed that the profound friendship between the Vietnamese and Chinese people was being consolidated and developed day by day.

The Exhibition of Achievements in China's Economic Construction opened in Hanoi on September 3rd. Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, General Secretary of the Vietnam Workers' Party Truong Chinh, and President of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor Hoang Quoc Viet visited the exhibition and wrote inscriptions in the exhibition's guestbook.

Pham Van Dong pointed out in his inscription that the Chinese exhibition is the image of the future Vietnam. He continued: "This exhibition shows the Vietnamese people the progress of the People's Republic of China in all aspects, the rapid momentum of progress, and the trend and reasons for this progress."

Truong Chinh's inscription said, "This exhibition clearly shows that China is making rapid progress on the road of socialist industrialization, while maintaining the fine traditions of long-established handicrafts and arts. It also skillfully applies new technologies in agriculture and handicrafts. This is an excellent experience for the Vietnamese people."

Hoang Quoc Viet, who visited China in 1951, wrote: "This exhibition shows that China has taken a big step forward in the past five years." He pointed out: "This exhibition will definitely have a great impact on the Vietnamese people, especially the workers and other working people. It will not only strengthen the friendship between the Vietnamese and Chinese people, but also strengthen the Vietnamese people's confidence in moving forward."

### 越南政府领导人参观中国展览会  盛赞我国在社会主义工业化道路上的成就

1956-09-07
第5版()
专栏：

    越南政府领导人参观中国展览会
    盛赞我国在社会主义工业化道路上的成就
    新华社河内6日电　中国经济展览团团长王久敬为中国经济建设成就展览会的开幕，在昨天晚上举行盛大的招待宴会。
    在宴会结束后，胡志明主席来到了展览馆。他以愉快的心情观看了各种展品。
    在参加宴会的四百多个宾客中，有武元甲、黄国越、潘英和越南政府许多高级官员，越南国民大会代表，各民主党派和群众团体的代表，以及各国驻越南的外交使节和专家等。
    王久敬团长在宴会上致词，他对越南政府和人民热情帮助中国展览馆的筹建工作表示感谢，并且希望通过这一次的展出，能够进一步巩固和发展两国人民间的友谊，促进中越两国的经济合作和贸易关系的发展。
    越南政府商业部部长潘英也在宴会上讲话。他说，参观过展览会的越南人民，对中国经济建设所取得的成就都表示钦佩；没有参观过的人，都殷切地希望早日得到参观的机会。潘英说，越南人民这样热烈地欢迎中国展览馆，说明了越中两国人民深厚的友谊正在日益巩固和发展。
    中国经济建设成就展览会是9月3日在河内开幕的。范文同总理、越南劳动党总书记长征和越南总工会主席黄国越都参观了这个展览会，并且在展览会上的留言簿上题了字。
    范文同在题字中指出，中国展览会是未来的越南的形象。他接着说：“这个展览会向越南人民说明中华人民共和国在各个方面的进步、推动进步的迅速动力以及这种进步的趋势和原因”。
    长征的题字说，“这个展览会清楚地说明，中国在社会主义工业化的道路上正在取得迅速的进展，而同时保持久负盛名的手工业和工艺的优良传统。它还在农业和手工业方面熟练地运用新技术。对于越南人民说来，这是极好的经验”。
    曾经在1951年访问过中国的黄国越写道：“这个展览会说明，在过去五年中，中国已经前进了一大步。”他指出：“这个展览会一定会对越南人民、特别是工人和其他阶层的劳动人民发生巨大的影响。它不仅会加强越南和中国两国人民的友谊。而且还会加强越南人民前进的信心。”
