Title: All-China Women's Federation Hosts Banquet for Mrs. Abdulgani and Others
Author:
Date: 1956-09-30
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. The All-China Democratic Women's Federation held a banquet tonight to welcome Mrs. Abdulgani, wife of the Indonesian Minister of Foreign Affairs, Mrs. Subandrio, wife of the Secretary-General of the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Mrs. Arudji Kartawinata, wife of the Second Deputy Speaker of the Indonesian Parliament, Mrs. Arifin, wife of the First Deputy Speaker of the Indonesian Parliament, Mrs. Sudarman, Chairwoman of the Indonesian Democratic Women's Association, and Mrs. Sukrisno, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of Antara News Agency.

    Deng Yingchao, Vice-President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, expressed her heartfelt and warm welcome to the guests on behalf of the National Women's Federation at the banquet. She said that your visit has brought the profound friendship of Indonesian women, which makes us feel very excited and happy. She said that women in our two countries have suffered the pain of oppression by colonialists, and we have many common experiences in the struggle for national independence, defending world peace, and defending the rights and interests of women and children. Through this visit, the understanding between the women of our two countries will be further enhanced, and the friendship between the women of our two countries will be developed. She hoped that the guests would feel at home, see whatever they wanted to see, and give guidance on any shortcomings in the work they observed. Deng Yingchao finally proposed a toast to the friendship between the women of China and Indonesia and to the health of the guests.

    Mrs. Abdulgani thanked the All-China Women's Federation for its welcome on behalf of the guests in her speech. She said that they would bring the impressions gained from this visit to all Indonesian women, and enhance the struggle of Indonesian women for peace. She proposed a toast to the health of the hosts of the banquet and the members of the All-China Women's Federation.

    Among those present at the banquet were the wife of Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council, the wife of Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, the wife of Vice Mayor of Beijing Le Songsheng, the wife of Bao Erhan, President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, the wife of Vice President Hu Yuzhi, Zeng Xianzhi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation, Luo Shuzhang, member of the Executive Committee, and heads of various departments of the All-China Women's Federation, as well as the heads of the Beijing Women's Federation. The wife of Anwar Sani, Counselor of the Indonesian Embassy, ​​and others were also invited to attend the banquet.

    The distinguished guests are visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation. They received a warm welcome when they arrived in Beijing this afternoon.

    Mrs. Sukiman, wife of Indonesian Member of Parliament Sukiman, who was also invited to visit China, is still on her way.

### 全国妇联欢宴阿卜杜加尼夫人等

1956-09-30
第2版()
专栏：

    全国妇联欢宴阿卜杜加尼夫人等
    据新华社29日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会今天晚上举行宴会，欢迎印度尼西亚外交部部长阿卜杜加尼的夫人、印度尼西亚外交部秘书长苏班德里约的夫人、印度尼西亚国会第二副议长阿鲁季·卡塔威纳塔的夫人、印度尼西亚国会第一副议长阿里芬的夫人、印度尼西亚民主妇女会主席苏达曼夫人、安塔拉通讯社副总编辑苏克里斯诺的夫人。
    中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超在宴会上代表全国妇联向朋友们表示衷心的和热烈的欢迎。她说，你们的光临带来了印度尼西亚妇女的深挚友谊，这使我们感到很兴奋和愉快。她说，我们两国妇女都曾经遭受过殖民主义者压迫的痛苦，在争取民族独立、保卫世界和平和保卫妇女儿童权益的斗争中，我们又有着许多共同的经历。通过这一次的访问，会更加增进我们两国妇女的了解和开展我们两国妇女的友谊。她希望朋友们像在自己家里一样，想看什么就看什么，对看到的工作中的缺点随时指教。邓颖超最后建议为中国和印度尼西亚两国妇女的友谊和朋友们健康干杯。
    阿卜杜加尼的夫人在讲话中代表客人们对全国妇联的欢迎表示感谢。她说，她们将把这次访问中获得的印象带给全印度尼西亚的妇女，增强印度尼西亚妇女为和平而进行的斗争。她建议为宴会的主人和全国妇联的会员们的健康干杯。
    出席宴会作陪的有国务院陈毅副总理的夫人、外交部副部长章汉夫的夫人、北京市副市长乐松生的夫人、中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长鲍尔汉的夫人、副会长胡愈之的夫人、全国妇联书记处书记曾宪植、执行委员会委员罗叔章及全国妇联各部负责人，北京市妇联的负责人等。印度尼西亚大使馆参赞安瓦·萨尼的夫人等也应邀出席了宴会。
    贵宾们是应中华全国民主妇女联合会邀请来中国访问的。她们在今天下午到达北京时受到热烈欢迎。
    应邀来中国访问的印度尼西亚国会议员苏基曼的夫人尚在来华途中。
