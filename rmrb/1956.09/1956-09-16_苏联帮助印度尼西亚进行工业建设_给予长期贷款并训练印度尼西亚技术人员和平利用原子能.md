Title: Soviet Union to Assist Indonesia in Industrial Construction, Providing Long-Term Loans and Training Indonesian Technicians in the Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1956-09-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 15th -- Indonesia and the Soviet Union today signed an agreement on Soviet technical and economic assistance to Indonesia.

According to the agreement, the Soviet Union will provide Indonesia with a credit loan of 100 million U.S. dollars at an annual interest rate of 2.5%. This loan will be repaid within twelve years, starting from the date of effective use of the loan.

The agreement states that the Soviet Union is willing to assist Indonesia next year in thoroughly exploring resources such as coal mines, non-ferrous metals, and hydroelectric power. The Soviet Union will also help Indonesia train technicians in the application of atomic energy and radioactive materials in medicine.

The agreement was signed at 1 p.m. today at the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Representing the Indonesian government was Subandrio, Acting Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and head of the Indonesian delegation; representing the Soviet government was S.A. Skachkov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union and head of the Soviet delegation.

### 苏联帮助印度尼西亚进行工业建设  给予长期贷款并训练印度尼西亚技术人员和平利用原子能

1956-09-16
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联帮助印度尼西亚进行工业建设
    给予长期贷款并训练印度尼西亚技术人员和平利用原子能
    新华社雅加达15日电　印度尼西亚和苏联今天签订了一项关于苏联给予印度尼西亚技术援助和经济援助的协定。
    根据协定，苏联将按2.5％的年息借给印度尼西亚一亿美元的信用贷款。这项贷款将从有效使用贷款之日起，在十二年内还清。
    协定说，苏联愿意在明年帮助印度尼西亚对煤矿、非铁金属、水力发电等资源进行彻底的勘查。苏联还将帮助印度尼西亚在原子能和放射性物质在医学上的应用方面训练技术人员。
    协定是今天下午一时在印度尼西亚外交部签字的。代表印度尼西亚政府签字的是外交部代理秘书长、印度尼西亚代表团团长苏班德里奥；代表苏联政府签字的是苏联对外贸易部副部长、苏联代表团团长谢米查斯特诺夫。
