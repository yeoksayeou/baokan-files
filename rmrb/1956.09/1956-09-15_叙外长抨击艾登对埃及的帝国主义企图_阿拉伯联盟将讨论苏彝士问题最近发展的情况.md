Title: Syrian Foreign Minister Condemns Eden's Imperialist Attempts on Egypt; Arab League to Discuss Recent Developments in the Suez Canal Issue
Author:
Date: 1956-09-15
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, September 14th dispatch from Damascus: Syrian Foreign Minister Salah al-Din Bitar criticized British Prime Minister Eden's speech in the House of Commons on the Suez Canal issue on September 13th. Bitar said that Eden's speech proposing the Canal Users Association plan created a "serious situation for the whole world." A Foreign Ministry spokesman said that Eden's speech revealed the "imperialist attempts by Britain and France to create world tension and endanger peace." He said that the speech was "similar to Hitler's speech threatening to conquer the world." The spokesman said, "The Canal Users Association that Eden suggested establishing is a new link in the Western Cold War chain used to prepare for aggressive actions against the Arab world."
    Xinhua News Agency, September 14th dispatch from Cairo: The Political Committee of the Arab League will meet in Cairo on September 17th to study the recent developments in the Suez Canal issue.
    It was announced on September 13th that the foreign ministers of nine Arab League member states will attend the meeting.
    An Arab League spokesman said that the main items on the agenda will be: Eden's proposal to establish a "Suez Canal Users Association," the "Five-Nation Committee's" mission to Cairo, and Nasser's proposal to establish a negotiating body to re-examine the 1888 Constantinople Convention.
    The spokesman said that Arab countries have firmly supported Egypt's position on this issue, and Egypt has also received this support from other Asian and African countries.

### 叙外长抨击艾登对埃及的帝国主义企图  阿拉伯联盟将讨论苏彝士问题最近发展的情况

1956-09-15
第5版()
专栏：

    叙外长抨击艾登对埃及的帝国主义企图
    阿拉伯联盟将讨论苏彝士问题最近发展的情况
    新华社14日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚外交部长赛拉哈丁·比塔尔9月13日抨击英国首相艾登在下院发表的关于苏彝士运河问题的演说。比塔尔说，艾登的提出运河使用国协会计划的演说，造成了“全世界的严重局势”。一位外交部发言人说，艾登的演说显露了英国和法国的“制造世界紧张局势危害和平的帝国主义企图。他说，这个演说“和希特勒扬言要征服全世界的演说相似”。这位发言人说，“艾登建议设立的运河使用国协会是用来准备对阿拉伯世界采取侵略行动的西方冷战链条中的新的一环”。
    新华社14日讯　开罗消息：阿拉伯联盟政治委员会将在9月17日在开罗开会研究苏彝士运河问题的最近的发展。
    据9月13日宣布，九个阿拉伯联盟成员国的外交部长将参加会议。
    阿拉伯联盟的一位发言人说，议程的主要项目将是：艾登提出的关于成立一个“苏彝士运河使用国协会”的建议、“五国委员会”赴开罗的使命、纳赛尔提出的关于成立一个谈判机构来重新审查1888年君士坦丁堡公约的建议。
    这位发言人说，阿拉伯国家已经坚决支持埃及在这个问题上的立场，埃及也从其他亚非国家得到这种支持。
