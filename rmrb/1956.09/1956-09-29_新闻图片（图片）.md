    News Pictures
    The Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party held a grand garden party in Zhongshan Park on September 28 to celebrate the success of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China and to welcome the representatives of fraternal parties from various countries who were invited to attend the congress. The picture on the right shows a corner of the garden party.
        Photo by Zou Jiandong
    The picture below shows the representatives of the fraternal parties being warmly welcomed by the masses as they entered the venue. Photo by Hou Bo
    Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, held a banquet on September 28 to entertain Nepalese Prime Minister Acharya. From left to right: Indian Ambassador to China R.K. Nehru, Prime Minister Acharya, Mrs. R.K. Nehru, Premier Zhou Enlai. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zheng Xiaozhen
    Nepalese Prime Minister Tanka Prasad Acharya and his entourage visited the Central Institute for Nationalities on September 28. The picture shows Prime Minister Acharya reading the Kanjur Sutra in the Tibetan Scripture Hall. Second from the left is B. Misra, Chairman of the Nepal People's Party. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Liu Dong'ao
    The inaugural meeting of the China-Nepal Friendship Association was held in Beijing on September 28. The picture shows Zhou Jianren, President of the China-Nepal Friendship Association, delivering a speech at the meeting. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zheng Xiaozhen
    The All-China Women's Federation held a tea party on September 28 to welcome Mrs. Acharya, the wife of the Nepalese Prime Minister. The picture shows Mrs. Acharya (left) and Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, at the tea party.
    Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zheng Xiaozhen
