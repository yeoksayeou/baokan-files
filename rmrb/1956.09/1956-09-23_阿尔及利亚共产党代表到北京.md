Title: Algerian Communist Party Representative Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd: The Algerian Communist Party representative, First Secretary Comrade Buhali, has arrived in Beijing recently at the invitation to attend the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China.

### 阿尔及利亚共产党代表到北京

1956-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚共产党代表到北京
    新华社22日讯　阿尔及利亚共产党由它的代表第一书记布哈利同志应邀前来参加中国共产党第八次全国代表大会，已于最近到达北京。
