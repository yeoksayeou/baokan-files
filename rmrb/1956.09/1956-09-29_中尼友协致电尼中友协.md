Title: Sino-Nepalese Friendship Association Sends Telegram to Nepal-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: The China-Nepal Friendship Association sent the following telegram to the Nepal-China Friendship Association: To Mr. Purna Bahadur, President of the Nepal-China Friendship Association, Kathmandu, Nepal:
　　The China-Nepal Friendship Association was established in Beijing on September 28, 1956.
　　The inaugural meeting was honored by the presence of Prime Minister Tanka Prasad Acharya and Premier Zhou Enlai. Now, on behalf of the Association Council and the Chinese people, we extend our heartfelt and sincere respect to the Nepal-China Friendship Association and the Nepalese people. We hope to cooperate closely with your association in the future and work together to develop friendly relations and cultural exchanges between China and Nepal. We wish the Nepalese people greater achievements in the cause of building their own country. We wish the friendship between the Chinese and Nepalese people to be continuously consolidated and developed.
　　　　　　　　　　　　President of the China-Nepal Friendship Association Zhou Jianren
　　　　　　　　　　　　Vice President Zhou Shujia

### 中尼友协致电尼中友协

1956-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中尼友协致电尼中友协
    新华社28日讯　中国尼泊尔友好协会致尼中友好协会的电文如下：尼泊尔加德满都尼中友好协会会长普尔纳·巴哈杜
  尔先生：
    中国、尼泊尔友好协会于1956年9月28日在北京成立。
    成立大会荣幸地邀请了坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅首相阁下和周恩来总理莅临参加。现在我们谨代表协会理事会和中国人民向尼中友好协会及尼泊尔人民致以衷心的诚挚的敬意。我们希望今后同贵会密切合作，为发展中国和尼泊尔两国间的友好关系和文化交流而共同努力。祝尼泊尔人民在建设自己祖国的事业中取得更大的成就。祝中尼人民间的友谊不断地巩固、发展。
          中国尼泊尔友好协会会长　周建人
                副会长　周叔迦
