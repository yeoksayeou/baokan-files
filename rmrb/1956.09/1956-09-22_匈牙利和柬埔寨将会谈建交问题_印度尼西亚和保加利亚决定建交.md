Title: Hungary and Cambodia to Hold Talks on Establishing Diplomatic Relations; Indonesia and Bulgaria Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-09-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 20th: According to TASS, Budapest, September 20th: An official announcement on the establishment of diplomatic relations between Hungary and Cambodia was published here. The announcement stated that the two governments have agreed to hold talks at a later date on the establishment of legations and the exchange of ministers.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 20th: The Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs announced today that representatives of Indonesia and Bulgaria held negotiations in New Delhi, and both sides have agreed to establish diplomatic relations.

### 匈牙利和柬埔寨将会谈建交问题  印度尼西亚和保加利亚决定建交

1956-09-22
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利和柬埔寨将会谈建交问题
    印度尼西亚和保加利亚决定建交
    新华社20日讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯20日讯：这里发表了匈牙利同柬埔寨建立外交关系的正式公告。公告说，两国政府商妥将在较晚一些时候就设立公使馆和互派公使的问题进行会谈。
    新华社雅加达20日电　印度尼西亚外交部今天宣布，印度尼西亚同保加利亚两国的代表在新德里举行了谈判，双方已经同意建立外交关系。
