Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Hosts Prime Minister Acharya

Author:
Date: 1956-09-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China hosted a banquet tonight in honor of the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Tanka Prasad Acharya, and his wife.

    Chairman Mao Zedong delivered a speech at the beginning of the banquet, warmly welcoming Prime Minister Acharya and his wife, as well as the friends who accompanied him to China. Chairman Mao Zedong said: Nepal and China have a long history of relations, and the peoples of the two countries have a deep friendship. There is a mountain between our two countries, the highest mountain in the world. This mountain not only connects China and Nepal but also connects China and India. Therefore, India, Nepal, and China are connected, and we are all close neighbors. Chairman Mao Zedong then raised his glass to wish His Majesty the King of Nepal good health, to wish His Excellency Prime Minister Acharya of the Kingdom of Nepal, his wife, and the friends who came with him to visit China good health, to wish the Indian friends present good health, to wish China, Nepal, and India solidarity, and to wish the restoration of diplomatic relations between China and Nepal and the recent signing of the agreement.

    Prime Minister Acharya then delivered a speech. He said, "I have heard about China before. Now that I can come to China to see it for myself, I will definitely bring back the impressions I gained in China to Nepal and introduce them to the Nepalese people." Prime Minister Acharya said that Chairman Mao Zedong had just mentioned the highest mountain in the world, the Himalayas, and he believed that the friendship between Nepal, China, and India would last as long as the Himalayas. He also said that the impression he gained in China made him believe that a strong nation would definitely make progress. Finally, he proposed a toast to the friendship between India, China, and Nepal, and to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong.

    Tonight, Chairman of the Nepal Praja Parishad, B. Misra, as well as Lieutenant General Rana, the Nepalese Ambassador to China, and other senior officials accompanying Prime Minister Acharya on his visit to China, were also invited to attend the banquet.

    Those present tonight to accompany the guests included Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairpersons of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, He Long, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, Li Xiannian, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Cheng Qian, Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, Long Yun, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun, Burhan Shahidi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Chinese Ambassador to Nepal Pan Zili, President of the China-Nepal Friendship Association Zhou Jianren, as well as responsible persons from various government departments, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and well-known figures from all walks of life in Beijing.

    Indian Ambassador to China R.K. Nehru and his wife were also invited to attend the banquet.

    Before the banquet began, Prime Minister Acharya presented Chairman Mao Zedong with a personally signed photograph from King Mahendra of Nepal, and also presented an embroidered portrait of Chairman Mao Zedong, an inlaid saber, a woolen blanket, and a pair of ivory tusks.

### 毛主席招待阿查里雅首相

1956-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席招待阿查里雅首相
    新华社29日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东今晚设宴招待尼泊尔王国首相坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅和夫人。
    毛泽东主席在宴会开始时致词，对阿查里雅首相和他的夫人以及同他一道来中国的朋友们表示热烈欢迎。毛泽东主席说：尼泊尔同中国有长久的历史关系，两国人民间有深厚的友谊。我们两国之间有一座山，这座山是世界上最高的山，这座山不仅连结着中国和尼泊尔，而且也连结着中国和印度。所以，印度、尼泊尔和中国是连结在一起的，我们都是近邻。毛泽东主席接着举杯祝尼泊尔国王陛下的身体健康，祝尼泊尔王国首相阿查里雅阁下和他的夫人以及同他一道来中国访问的朋友们的身体健康，祝在座的印度朋友们的身体健康，祝中国、尼泊尔和印度三国团结一致，祝尼中两国恢复外交关系和最近签订的协定。
    阿查里雅首相接着致词。他说，我以前就听到过关于中国的事情，这次我能亲自来中国看一看，我一定要把在中国所得到的印象，带回尼泊尔去，并且把它介绍给尼泊尔人民。阿查里雅首相说，刚才毛泽东主席谈到世界最高的山——喜马拉雅山，我相信尼泊尔、中国和印度三国的友谊将像喜马拉雅山一样长久。他又说，我在中国所得的印象，使我相信一个坚强的民族一定会得到进步的。最后他提议为印度、中国、尼泊尔三国的友谊，为毛泽东主席的健康而干杯。
    今晚，尼泊尔人民党主席巴·米斯腊以及尼泊尔驻中国大使拉纳中将和随同阿查里雅首相访问中国的其他高级官员，也应邀出席了宴会。
    今晚出席作陪的，有朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄、李济深、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、陈叔通，国务院副总理陈云、彭德怀、贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫、李先念，秘书长习仲勋，最高人民法院院长董必武，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，国防委员会副主席程潜、张治中、傅作义、龙云，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧、鲍尔汉，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力，中尼友好协会会长周建人，以及政府各部门负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领，北京各界著名人士。
    印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁和夫人也应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会开始前，阿查里雅首相向毛泽东主席代转了尼泊尔国王马亨德拉赠送的一幅亲笔签名的相片，并且赠送了一幅毛泽东主席的绣像、一把镶嵌佩刀、一块羊毛毯和一对象牙。
