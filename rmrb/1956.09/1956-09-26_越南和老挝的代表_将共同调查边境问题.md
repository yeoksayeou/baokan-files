Title: Vietnamese and Laotian Representatives to Jointly Investigate Border Issues
Author:
Date: 1956-09-26
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 25th: Vietnam News Agency reported that a delegation from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Nguyen Van Chi, left Hanoi by plane this morning for Vientiane.

　　This delegation will work with a delegation from the Royal Laotian Government to investigate issues related to the implementation of the joint communiqué issued by both governments on August 29th of this year, concerning the border areas between Vietnam and Laos.

### 越南和老挝的代表  将共同调查边境问题

1956-09-26
第7版()
专栏：

    越南和老挝的代表
    将共同调查边境问题
    新华社河内25日电　越南通讯社讯：一个由阮文志率领的越南民主共和国的代表团在今天早晨乘飞机离开河内前往万象。
    这个代表团将和老挝王国政府的代表团一起，对实施双方政府在今年8月29日公布的联合公报中有关越南和老挝边界地方的一些问题进行调查。
