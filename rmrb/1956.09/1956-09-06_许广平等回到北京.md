Title: Xu Guangping and Others Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 5th. Xu Guangping, head of the Chinese delegation to the Second World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs in Japan, along with delegation members Wang Yunsheng, Cao Yu, Kang Yonghe, and others, returned to Beijing yesterday.

While in Japan, the delegation attended the Second World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs held in Tokyo and Nagasaki, as well as other rallies against atomic and hydrogen bombs held in other locations. They also conveyed the friendship and condolences of the Chinese people to the Japanese people and victims of atomic weapons, donating 50,000 yuan in RMB as a token of sympathy. The delegation also had friendly and extensive contacts with people from all walks of life in Japan. Xu Guangping, as a representative of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, also participated in the Second Japanese Mothers' Congress held from August 27th to 29th.

### 许广平等回到北京

1956-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    许广平等回到北京
    新华社5日讯　参加日本第二届禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的中国代表团团长许广平，团员王芸生、曹禺、康永和等已经在昨天回到北京。
    代表团在日本期间，参加了第二届禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会在东京和长崎举行的会议以及其他地方举行的禁止原子弹氢弹集会，并且向日本人民和原子武器受害者转达了中国人民的友谊和慰问，捐赠了慰问金人民币五万元。代表团还和日本各界人士进行了友好而广泛的接触。许广平作为中华全国民主妇女联合会的代表，又参加了在8月27日到29日举行的第二届日本母亲大会。
