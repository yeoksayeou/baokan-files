Title: German Workers Prepare to Unite Against West German Conscription; East and West German Trade Union Workers Prepare to Form a Standing Committee for an All-German Workers' Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-09-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 7th. The All-German Trade Union Workers' Conference held in Leipzig on September 5th decided to establish a standing committee for an All-German Workers' Conference to handle major issues related to the All-German trade union movement. All German trade union workers who oppose West German remilitarization and conscription can participate in the work of this committee. Many West German trade union workers have already indicated that they are prepared to participate in the work of this committee. The conference also passed a resolution calling on the workers of both Germanys to work harder to defend peace and oppose West Germany's neo-fascist and militarist tendencies.

Invited to attend the conference, Walter Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, spoke at the meeting. He emphasized the importance of the united struggle of the working people of all Germany and put forward some specific proposals for establishing and expanding cooperation between the political parties and organizations of the German working class.

### 德国工人准备团结起来反对西德征兵  东西德工会工作者筹组全德工人会议常委会

1956-09-08
第5版()
专栏：

    德国工人准备团结起来反对西德征兵
    东西德工会工作者筹组全德工人会议常委会
    新华社柏林7日电　9月5日在来比锡举行的全德工会工作者会议决定成立一个全德工人会议常设委员会，处理有关全德工会运动的一些重大问题。凡是反对西德重新军国主义化和反对征兵的德国工会工作者都可以参加这个委员会的工作。西德许多工会工作者已经表示，他们准备参加这个委员会的工作。会议还通过了一个决议，号召两个德国的工人更加努力地保卫和平，反对西德的新法西斯倾向和军国主义倾向。
    被邀请出席会议的德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希曾经在会上讲话。他强调了全德国劳动人民团结斗争的重要性，并且就建立和扩大德国工人阶级的政党和组织之间的合作提出了一些具体建议。
