Title: Sino-American Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-08
Page: 5

    Sino-American Ambassadorial Talks Continue
    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, September 7th. The Sino-American ambassadorial talks held their fifty-seventh meeting at the Palais des Nations in Geneva at 10:00 a.m. on September 7th. The meeting adjourned at 11:45 a.m. The next meeting is scheduled for September 20th. Readers' Forum

### 中美继续举行大使级会谈

1956-09-08
第5版()
专栏：

    中美继续举行大使级会谈
    新华社日内瓦7日电　中美两国大使级会谈9月7日上午十时在日内瓦国联大厦举行第五十七次会议。会议在十一时四十五分休会，下次会议定在9月20日举行。读者论坛
