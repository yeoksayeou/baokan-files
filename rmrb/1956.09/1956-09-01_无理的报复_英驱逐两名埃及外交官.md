Title: Irrational Retaliation: Britain Expels Two Egyptian Diplomats
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-01
Page: 6

    Irrational Retaliation
    Britain Expels Two Egyptian Diplomats
    Xinhua News Agency, London, August 31st: The British government announced on the evening of August 30th that Egyptian embassy diplomatic officials Kaffifi and Nasser were persona non grata and ordered them to leave Britain within 72 hours. British officials offered no reason for the announcement.
    Britain's action is retaliation for Egypt's expulsion of two British embassy officials involved in the recent British spy case uncovered in Egypt.

### 无理的报复  英驱逐两名埃及外交官

1956-09-01
第6版()
专栏：

    无理的报复
    英驱逐两名埃及外交官
    据新华社31日讯　伦敦消息：英国政府在8月30日晚间宣布埃及驻英国大使馆外交官员卡非非和纳赛夫二人为不受欢迎人物，要他们在七十二小时内离开英国。英国官方在宣布时没有提出任何理由。
    英国的行动是对埃及驱逐两个涉及最近在埃及破获的英国间谍案的英国驻埃及大使馆官员的报复。
