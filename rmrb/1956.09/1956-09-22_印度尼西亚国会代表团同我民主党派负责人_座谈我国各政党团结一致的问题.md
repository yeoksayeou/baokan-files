Title: Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation Holds Talks with Leaders of China's Democratic Parties on the Issue of Solidarity Among China's Political Parties
Author:
Date: 1956-09-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 21st. The Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation held a discussion this morning with leaders of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and various democratic parties on the issue of unity among China's political parties. The discussion was chaired by Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Chairman of the Chinese Peasants and Workers Democratic Party, and Vice Chairman of the China Democratic League.

Those present at the discussion included Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC and Director of the China Islamic Association; Zhang Naiqi and Hu Ziang, Deputy Directors of the China Democratic National Construction Association; Xu Guangping, Vice Chairman of the China Association for Promoting Democracy; Xu Deheng, Chairman of the Jiusan Society; Mei Gongbin, Secretary-General of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang; and Xing Xiping, Secretary-General of the National Committee of the CPPCC.

### 印度尼西亚国会代表团同我民主党派负责人  座谈我国各政党团结一致的问题

1956-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国会代表团同我民主党派负责人
    座谈我国各政党团结一致的问题
    新华社21日讯　印度尼西亚共和国国会代表团，今天上午同中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会和各民主党派负责人就中国各政党的团结一致问题进行座谈。座谈会由中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席、中国农工民主党主席、中国民主同盟副主席章伯钧主持。
    出席座谈会的有政协全国委员会副主席、中国伊斯兰教协会主任鲍尔汉，中国民主建国会副主任委员章乃器、胡子昂，中国民主促进会副主席许广平，九三学社主席许德珩，中国国民党革命委员会秘书长梅龚彬，政协全国委员会秘书长邢西萍等。
