Title: Japan-U.S. Negotiations on Relaxing Embargo Issues
Author:
Date: 1956-09-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 15th. The Japanese Foreign Ministry stated on the 13th that the Japanese government is negotiating on the issue of relaxing the regulations of the Paris-based Coordinating Committee for Multilateral Export Controls (COCOM) on trade with China.

The Foreign Ministry said that expanding the scope of the Paris COCOM's "exception procedure" is considered necessary so that Japan can expand its trade with China within the scope of international cooperation.

The Foreign Ministry said that Japan has been negotiating with the United States, and the United States maintains the "toughest attitude" among "free countries" on the issue of Japanese trade with China.

### 日美谈判放宽禁运问题

1956-09-16
第5版()
专栏：

    日美谈判放宽禁运问题
    新华社15日讯　东京消息：日本外务省13日说，日本政府正在谈判关于放宽巴黎对华贸易管制国际委员会的规定问题。
    外务省说，扩大巴黎对华贸易管制国际委员会的“例外程序”的使用范围被认为是必要的，以便日本可以在国际合作范围内扩大同中国的贸易。
    外务省说，日本一直在同美国进行谈判，而美国对日本同中国贸易问题，在“自由国家”中保持着“最强硬的态度”。
