Title: Tsedenbal Accepts Invitation to Visit Albania
Author:
Date: 1956-09-11
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 10th: According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency, during the Albanian government delegation's visit to Mongolia, an invitation was extended to Tsedenbal, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, to visit Albania. Tsedenbal has accepted this invitation.

### 泽登巴尔接受邀请  将访问阿尔巴尼亚

1956-09-11
第6版()
专栏：

    泽登巴尔接受邀请  将访问阿尔巴尼亚
    新华社布拉格10日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社讯：阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团在蒙古访问的时候，邀请蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔访问阿尔巴尼亚。泽登巴尔已经接受这个邀请。
