Title: Delegation of the Japan Youth Organization Council Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: The delegation of the Japan Youth Organization Council was welcomed by young people from all walks of life in Beijing at the Qianmen Railway Station this evening.

The head of the delegation, Zenshu Sagae, former Vice Chairman of the Japan Youth Organization Council, constantly waved to the welcoming people, and shook hands with Liu Xiyuan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, Luo Yi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, and Sun Shuzhu, Vice Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, who came to welcome them, greeting them warmly.

The delegation, consisting of twenty-two members, is visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation for a friendly visit.

### 日本青年团协议会代表团到京

1956-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    日本青年团协议会代表团到京
    据新华社24日讯　日本青年团协议会代表团今天晚上在前门车站受到北京市各界青年的欢迎。
    代表团团长、前日本青年团协议会副会长寒河江善秋不断挥手向欢迎的人们致意，并且同前来欢迎的中华全国民主青年联合会副主席刘西元、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记罗毅、中华全国学生联合会副主席孙寿珠等一一握手问好。
    代表团共二十二人，是应中华全国民主青年联合会的邀请，前来中国进行友好访问的。
