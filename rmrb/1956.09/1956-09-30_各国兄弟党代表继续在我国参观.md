Title: Representatives of Fraternal Parties from Various Countries Continue to Visit Our Country
Author:
Date: 1956-09-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 29th: The representatives of communist and workers' parties from various countries, who were invited to attend the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China, have continued their visits and tours in recent days.

In Beijing, representatives from the communist and workers' parties of various countries visited agricultural production cooperatives and vegetable production cooperatives in the suburbs; some visited cotton textile factories and flax mills; some visited Peking University, Tsinghua University, Beijing Institute of Iron and Steel Technology, Central Institute for Nationalities, Foreign Language Institute, middle schools, primary schools, hospitals, kindergartens, libraries, and the Lu Xun Memorial Hall; some visited mosques, the Sino-African Student Sanatorium, and the Ivory Carving Production Cooperative; some visited the Guanting Reservoir; and others toured the Great Wall and the Ming Tombs and other scenic spots and historical sites.

In addition, the representatives of the Polish United Workers' Party visited Shanghai, Shenyang, Wuhan, and Guangzhou; the representatives of the Romanian Workers' Party visited Shanghai, Wuhan, and Shenyang; the representatives of the Bulgarian Communist Party visited Shanghai, Nanjing, and Jinan; the representatives of the Albanian Labor Party visited Shanghai, Nanjing, and Dalian; the representatives of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany visited Wuhan; and the representatives of the French Communist Party and the Italian Communist Party visited Shenyang, Anshan, Fushun, Dalian and other places. The representatives of the Austrian Communist Party visited Shenyang and Fushun. Representatives from the Communist and Workers' Parties of Hungary, Mongolia, Italy, France, Canada, Finland, Nepal, Sweden, Denmark, Iceland, Syria-Lebanon, Morocco, and Algeria visited Tianjin.

### 各国兄弟党代表继续在我国参观

1956-09-30
第3版()
专栏：

    各国兄弟党代表继续在我国参观
    新华社29日讯　应邀参加中国共产党第八次全国代表大会的各国共产党和工人党的代表，连日来继续进行参观和游览。
    在北京，来自各国的共产党和工人党的代表们，有的到郊区参观了农业生产合作社和蔬菜生产合作社，有的参观了棉纺厂和麻纺厂，有的参观了北京大学、清华大学、北京钢铁工业学院、中央民族学院、外国语学院和中学、小学、医院、幼儿院、图书馆、鲁迅纪念馆，有的参观了清真寺、亚非学生疗养院、象牙雕刻生产合作社，有的参观了官厅水库，还有的游览了长城和明十三陵等名胜古迹。
    另外，波兰统一工人党代表到上海、沈阳、武汉、广州进行了参观，罗马尼亚工人党代表到上海、武汉、沈阳进行了参观，保加利亚共产党代表到上海、南京、济南进行了参观，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表到上海、南京、旅大进行了参观，德国统一社会党代表到武汉进行了参观，法国共产党代表和意大利共产党代表到沈阳、鞍山、抚顺、大连等地进行了参观，奥地利共产党代表到沈阳和抚顺进行了参观。匈牙利、蒙古、意大利、法国、加拿大、芬兰、尼泊尔、瑞典、丹麦、冰岛、叙利亚—黎巴嫩、摩洛哥、阿尔及利亚等国共产党和工人党的代表们到天津市进行了参观。
