Title: More Than One Hundred Japanese Apply to Work as Pilots in Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-09-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, September 4th: An Egyptian Embassy spokesman in Japan said on September 3rd that approximately one hundred Japanese have applied to work as Suez Canal pilots.

The Egyptian Embassy spokesman said that in addition to telephone and written applications, many prospective pilots have come to the embassy to submit applications.

### 一百多名日本人申请到埃及做领航员

1956-09-05
第6版()
专栏：

    一百多名日本人申请到埃及做领航员
    据新华社4日讯　东京消息：埃及驻日本大使馆的一位发言人9月3日说，大约已经有一百名日本人申请作苏彝士运河领航员的工作。
    埃及大使馆的发言人说，除了用电话和书面申请以外，许多将来可能成为领航员的人到使馆提出了申请。
