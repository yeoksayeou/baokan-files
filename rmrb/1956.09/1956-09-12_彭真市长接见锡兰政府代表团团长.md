Title: Mayor Peng Zhen Receives Head of Ceylon Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-09-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th — Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen received K. Corea, head of the Ceylon government delegation, and his wife, and delegation member Fonseka this afternoon.

Those present at the reception included Vice Mayor of Beijing Wang Kunlun, Secretary-General of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee Chai Zemin, and Director of the Foreign Affairs Office Xin Yi.

### 彭真市长接见锡兰政府代表团团长

1956-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真市长接见锡兰政府代表团团长
    新华社11日讯　北京市市长彭真今天下午接见了锡兰政府代表团团长克·科里亚和他的夫人，团员方席卡。
    接见的时候，在座的有北京市副市长王昆仑，北京市人民委员会秘书长柴泽民、外事处处长辛毅。
