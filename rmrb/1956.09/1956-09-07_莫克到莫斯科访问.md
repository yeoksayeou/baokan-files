Title: Moc Visits Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-09-07
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 6th: According to TASS, at the invitation of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the French representative to the United Nations Disarmament Commission and member of the French National Assembly, Moc, arrived in Moscow by plane on the evening of September 4th.
    On September 5th, First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Mikoyan, received Moc. First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Gromyko, also hosted a breakfast in his honor.

### 莫克到莫斯科访问

1956-09-07
第5版()
专栏：

    莫克到莫斯科访问
    据新华社6日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：联合国裁军委员会法国代表、法国国民议会议员莫克应苏联外交部的邀请，在9月4日晚间乘飞机到达莫斯科。
    9月5日，苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬接见了莫克。苏联外交部第一副部长葛罗米柯还设早宴招待了他。
