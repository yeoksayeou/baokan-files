Title: Sukarno Visits Bulganin in Sochi
Author:
Date: 1956-09-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sochi, September 9th: According to TASS, Indonesian President Sukarno, who arrived in Sochi on the afternoon of September 7th for a visit, and his entourage, visited Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Bulganin on September 8th, who was vacationing there with his family.

Bulganin warmly received the distinguished guests. He strolled with the guests in the park outside the residence and viewed the vast sea from the cliff overlooking the coast. Afterwards, Bulganin hosted a luncheon for the guests. After the luncheon, Bulganin also invited President Sukarno and others to go boating.

### 苏加诺到索契拜访布尔加宁

1956-09-10
第5版()
专栏：

    苏加诺到索契拜访布尔加宁
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社索契9日讯：9月7日下午到达索契访问的印度尼西亚总统苏加诺和他的随行人员，9月8日拜访了正在那里同自己的家属休假的苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁。
    布尔加宁热情地接待了贵宾。他同客人们在寓所外面的公园漫步，并且从临海的悬崖上眺望了广阔的海面。随后，布尔加宁设午宴招待客人。午宴结束以后，布尔加宁还邀请苏加诺总统等划船。
