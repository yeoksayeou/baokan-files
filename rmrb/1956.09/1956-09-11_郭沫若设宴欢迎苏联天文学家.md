Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet to Welcome Soviet Astronomers
Author:
Date: 1956-09-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome Soviet astronomers Professor Gaze and Professor Shklovsky. Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Academy of Sciences, and many Chinese astronomers attended as guests.

Professor Gaze and Professor Shklovsky, who were invited to visit China, arrived in Beijing on the 9th.

### 郭沫若设宴欢迎苏联天文学家

1956-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若设宴欢迎苏联天文学家
    新华社10日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若今晚设宴欢迎苏联天文学家节依奇教授和谢格洛夫教授。科学院副院长吴有训和我国天文学家多人出席作陪。
    应邀来我国访问的节依奇教授和谢格洛夫教授是在9日到达北京的。
