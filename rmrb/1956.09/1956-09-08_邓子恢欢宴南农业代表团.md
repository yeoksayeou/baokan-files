Title: Deng Zihui Hosts Banquet for South Agricultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-09-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th September. Vice Premier of the State Council Deng Zihui hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the Yugoslav Agricultural Delegation headed by Jože Vrhovec, member of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia and member of the Executive Council of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia. The Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of Yugoslavia to China, Mr. Milicevic, and his wife were also invited to attend the banquet.
    At the banquet, Deng Zihui extended a warm welcome to the Yugoslav Agricultural Delegation for their visit to China. He said that the friendship between the people of China and Yugoslavia is continuously increasing, and the connections and cooperation in the fields of politics, economy, and culture are also continuously developing. He believes that this visit to China by the Yugoslav Agricultural Delegation will be of great significance for exchanging agricultural production experiences between the two countries and promoting friendship and understanding between the two peoples.
    The head of the delegation, Jože Vrhovec, also spoke, expressing his gratitude to the Chinese people for their fraternal care and warm reception of the delegation. He said that the Yugoslav people and the League of Communists of Yugoslavia are infinitely excited about the great achievements made by the Chinese people in socialist construction, and are deeply convinced that during their visit to China, they will personally witness the rich experience of the Chinese people in agricultural production and the great victory of social transformation.
    Among those present at the banquet were Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture, Cai Ziwei, Vice Minister, He Jili, Vice Minister of Water Conservancy, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and representatives from the Ministry of Land Reclamation.

### 邓子恢欢宴南农业代表团

1956-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    邓子恢欢宴南农业代表团
    新华社6日讯　国务院副总理邓子恢今晚设宴欢迎以南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会委员、南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国执行委员会委员约·维塞利诺夫为首的南斯拉夫农业代表团、南斯拉夫驻中国临时代办米里切维奇及夫人也应邀出席了宴会。
    邓子恢在宴会上对南斯拉夫农业代表团来中国访问表示热烈欢迎。他说，中南两国人民的友谊正在不断增进，在政治、经济和文化的联系和合作方面，也正在不断发展。他相信这次南斯拉夫农业代表团到中国来访问，对交流两国农业生产经验和促进两国人民之间的友谊和了解有很大的意义。
    代表团长约·维塞利诺夫也讲了话，他表示感谢中国人民对代表团的兄弟般的关怀和热情接待。他说，南斯拉夫人民和南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟对中国人民在社会主义建设中取得的巨大成就感到无限兴奋，并且深信，他们在中国访问期间，将亲自看到中国人民在农业生产方面的丰富经验和社会改造的伟大胜利。
    出席今晚宴会作陪的，有农业部长廖鲁言、副部长蔡子伟，水利部副部长何基澧，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，以及农垦部的代表等多人。
