Title: Returned Chiang Kai-shek Soldier Wang Jialun Reunites with Family in Hometown
Author:
Date: 1956-09-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 6th: Wang Jialun, a Chiang Kai-shek soldier who swam back from Jinmen Island, recently returned to Qingheishan Village, Daixi Township, Daishan County, Zhoushan Prefecture, Zhejiang Province, and joyfully reunited with his parents, wife, daughter, and other relatives after being separated for more than six years. Many family members whose children were abducted during the Chiang Kai-shek army's retreat came to Wang Jialun to inquire about the whereabouts of their relatives.

At the end of last year, Wang Jialun's family joined a salt agricultural production cooperative in Qingheishan Village. In the first half of this year, they received 448 yuan in cash, as well as 750 jin of rice and 200 jin of wheat, and the family is living very well. Now, Wang Jialun is also preparing to join the cooperative and return to salt agricultural production, living a happy life of peaceful labor.

### 归来的蒋军士兵王家轮  回到故乡和家人团聚

1956-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

    归来的蒋军士兵王家轮
    回到故乡和家人团聚
    新华社杭州6日电　由金门岛游水归来的蒋军士兵王家轮，最近回到了浙江省舟山专区岱山县岱西乡青黑山村，和分别了六年多的父母、妻、女等亲人欢聚一堂。许多在当年蒋军溃逃时被掳去了子弟的家属纷纷来找王家轮，探听他们的亲属的下落。
    去年年底，王家轮全家加入了青黑山村的一个盐农业生产合作社，今年上半年就分到现金四百四十八元，还有七百五十斤稻谷和二百斤麦子，一家人生活得很好。现在王家轮也准备加入合作社，重新从事盐农业生产，过和平劳动的幸福生活。
