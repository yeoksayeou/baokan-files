Title: American People Oppose Intervention in Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-09-21
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 19th: The "Wall Street Journal" published the results of an opinion poll conducted by the newspaper on September 18th, which showed that seven out of ten Americans surveyed opposed the United States taking military intervention action on the Suez Canal issue.

### 美国人民反对干涉埃及

1956-09-21
第8版()
专栏：

    美国人民反对干涉埃及
    据新华社19日讯　纽约消息：美国“华尔街日报”9月18日公布了这家报纸举行的一次民意测验的结果，它表明：受测验的每十个美国人中有七个人反对美国在苏彝士运河问题上采取军事干涉行动。
