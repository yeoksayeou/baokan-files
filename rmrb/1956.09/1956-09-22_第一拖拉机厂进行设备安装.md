Title: First Tractor Factory Commences Equipment Installation
Author:
Date: 1956-09-22
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The magnificent exterior of the First Tractor Manufacturing Plant has begun to appear in the suburbs of Luoyang.

The construction project of this factory has been underway for a full year. Currently, the external construction of ten major buildings—seven main workshops, including auxiliary, engine, stamping, forging, woodworking, non-ferrous repair casting, and fuel systems, as well as the central laboratory, gas station, and general warehouse—have been successively completed. The equipment installation project also began at the end of August, two months ahead of the state plan.

### 第一拖拉机厂进行设备安装

1956-09-22
第2版()
专栏：

    第一拖拉机厂进行设备安装
    据新华社讯　第一拖拉机制造厂的宏伟的工厂外貌，已经展现在洛阳市郊。
    这个工厂的建设工程，整整进行了一周年了。现在，这里有十个主要建筑物——辅助、发动机、冲压、锻工、木工、有色修铸、燃料系统等七个主厂房和中央实验室、煤气站、总仓库——的外型工程，已经相继完工；设备安装工程，也比国家计划提前两个月，在8月底开始进行。
