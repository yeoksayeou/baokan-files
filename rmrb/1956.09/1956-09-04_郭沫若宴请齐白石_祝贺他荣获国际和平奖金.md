Title: Guo Moruo Hosts a Banquet for Qi Baishi to Congratulate Him on Winning the International Peace Prize

Author: 
Date: 1956-09-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd — Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, hosted a banquet tonight for Qi Baishi to congratulate him on winning the 1955 World Peace Council International Peace Prize. Among those attending the banquet were Mao Dun, Standing Committee Member of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council International Peace Prize Appraisal Committee, Shao Lizi, Luo Longji, and Qian Duansheng, members of the World Peace Council, and Chinese art figures Chen Banding, Pu Xuezhai, Yu Feian, Ye Qianyu, and Ye Gongchuo, among others. Qi Baishi's family members also attended the banquet.

### 郭沫若宴请齐白石  祝贺他荣获国际和平奖金

1956-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若宴请齐白石
    祝贺他荣获国际和平奖金
    新华社3日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席、世界和平理事会副主席郭沫若今晚宴请齐白石，祝贺他荣获1955年度世界和平理事会国际和平奖金。出席宴会的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员、世界和平理事会国际和平奖金评议委员会副主席茅盾，世界和平理事会理事邵力子、罗隆基、钱端升，中国美术界人士陈半丁、溥雪斋、于非暗、叶浅予和叶恭绰等多人。齐白石的家属也出席了宴会。
