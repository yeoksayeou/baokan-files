Title: Chinese Guangxi Province Ethnic Delegation Leaves Hanoi Yesterday to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1956-09-04
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 3rd. The Chinese Guangxi Province Ethnic Delegation, invited to Vietnam to attend the founding大会 of the Viet Bac Autonomous Region, left Hanoi today to return home.

　　After attending the nine-day founding大会 of the Viet Bac Autonomous Region, the delegation visited major cities and industrial areas in Vietnam and attended the "9.2" National Day celebrations.

### 中国广西省民族代表团  昨天离开河内回国

1956-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    中国广西省民族代表团
    昨天离开河内回国
    新华社河内3日电　应邀到越南参加越北自治区成立大会的中国广西省民族代表团已经在今天离开河内回国。
    代表团在参加了为期九天的越北自治区成立大会以后，曾经到越南各主要城市和工业区去参观，并且参加了越南“9.2”国庆节观礼。
