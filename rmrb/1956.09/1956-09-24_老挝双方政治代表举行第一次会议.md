Title: First Meeting Held Between Political Representatives of Both Sides in Laos
Author:
Date: 1956-09-24
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 23rd: Vientiane Radio reported that the political delegations of the Royal Lao Government and the Lao Fighting Forces held their first meeting in Vientiane on September 22.
　　At the first meeting, both sides introduced the members of their delegations. The two sides will soon hold talks on the political provisions of the joint communiqué signed in August.

### 老挝双方政治代表举行第一次会议

1956-09-24
第7版()
专栏：

    老挝双方政治代表举行第一次会议
    新华社河内23日电　据万象电台广播，老挝王国政府和寮国战斗部队的政治代表团已经在9月22日在万象举行第一次会议。
    在第一次会议上，双方介绍了代表团的成员。双方即将就实现8月间签署的联合公报的政治条款问题进行会谈。
