Title: Yemen Announces General Mobilization to Support Egypt; Lebanese Minister of State Sternly Condemns Western Military Threats
Author:
Date: 1956-09-11
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, September 10th, Cairo dispatch: Yemen's ambassador to Egypt, Abu Taleb, said upon arriving in Cairo on the evening of September 8th that Yemen's King Ahmad had issued a general mobilization order to support Egypt's position on the Suez Canal issue.

    The ambassador said that the King of Yemen had requested that all of Yemen's strength and resources be used to fully support Egyptian President Gamal Abdel Nasser. He quoted the King as saying that Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal Company was "the first roar."

Xinhua News Agency, September 10th, TASS Cairo, September 9th: Lebanese Minister of State Salam, currently in Cairo, said in a statement to Cairo Radio reporters that the Lebanese people and government fully support Egypt's position on the Suez Canal issue.

    He said that the Suez Canal issue is related to all Arab countries, and all Arabs have openly and clearly expressed their support for Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal Company.

    Salam denounced the Western countries for adopting means of threats and intimidation in order to exert pressure on Egypt, and emphasized that these threats "cannot affect the position of the Arab countries, because the position of the Arab countries is based on justice."

### 也门宣布总动员支持埃及  黎巴嫩国务部长严斥西方武力威胁行径

1956-09-11
第5版()
专栏：

    也门宣布总动员支持埃及
    黎巴嫩国务部长严斥西方武力威胁行径
    新华社10日讯　开罗消息：也门驻埃及公使阿布塔莱布在9月8日晚到达开罗的时候说，也门国王艾哈迈德已经发布总动员令以支持埃及在运河问题上的立场。
    这位公使说，也门国王已经要求用也门的全部力量和资源来全力支持埃及总统阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔。他引用国王的话说，埃及将苏彝士运河公司收归国有的行动是“第一次怒吼”。
    新华社10日讯　塔斯社开罗9日讯：目前在开罗的黎巴嫩国务部长萨拉姆在向开罗广播电台记者发表谈话的时候说，黎巴嫩人民和政府完全支持埃及在苏彝士运河问题上的立场。
    他说，苏彝士运河问题是同所有阿拉伯国家都有关系的，所有阿拉伯人都公开明白地表示支持埃及把苏彝士运河公司收归国有。
    萨拉姆斥责西方国家为了对埃及施加压力而采取威胁和恐吓的手段，并且着重指出：这些威胁“并不能影响阿拉伯各国的立场，因为阿拉伯各国的立场是以正义为基础的”。
