Title: Chairman Mao, Chairman Liu, and Premier Zhou Receive Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-09-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 21st: Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai met this afternoon with the Indonesian Parliament Delegation, including Head of Delegation Kusnan, First Deputy Head Hassan Basri, Second Deputy Head Salan Ridwan, Third Deputy Head Yusuf Ajitorop, and all delegation members.

Present at the meeting were Vice-Chairpersons of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Song Qingling, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Deputy Secretaries-General Zhang Su, Yu Xinqing, Xin Zhichao, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen.

Also present at the meeting was the Indonesian Ambassador to China, Wiyopranoto. (Photo attached)

### 毛主席、刘委员长、周总理接见印度尼西亚国会代表团

1956-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席、刘委员长、周总理接见印度尼西亚国会代表团
    新华社21日讯　毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理，今天下午接见了印度尼西亚共和国国会代表团团长库斯南，第一副团长哈桑·巴斯里、第二副团长萨兰·里德旺、第三副团长优素福·阿吉托罗普和全体团员。
    接见的时候，在座的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄、李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、陈叔通，副秘书长张苏、余心清、辛志超，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇。
    接见的时候，印度尼西亚驻华大使维约普拉诺托也在座。（附图片）
