Title: Tito Receives Polish Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-09-14
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, September 13th. According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency, Yugoslav President Tito received the Polish parliamentary delegation headed by Deputy Speaker Kulchinsky in Belgrade on the 12th.
　　President Tito and the delegation members had a cordial conversation.

### 铁托接见波兰议会代表团

1956-09-14
第6版()
专栏：

    铁托接见波兰议会代表团
    新华社13日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，南斯拉夫总统铁托12日在贝尔格莱德接见了以副议长库尔钦斯基为首的波兰议会代表团。
    铁托总统和代表团人员进行了亲切的交谈。
