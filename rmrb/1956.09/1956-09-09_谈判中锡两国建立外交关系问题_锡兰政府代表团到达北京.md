Title: Negotiations on the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and Ceylon: Ceylonese Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-09
Page: 1

    Negotiations on the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and Ceylon
    Ceylonese Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 8th - The Ceylonese government delegation, headed by K. Corea, arrived in Beijing today from the Soviet Union on a special plane to negotiate with the government of the People's Republic of China on the establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries.
    Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and his wife, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Chen Shuliang, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department. Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin, Indian Ambassador to China R.K. Nehru and his wife, and British Chargé d'Affaires to China O'Neill also welcomed the delegation at the airport.
    Tonight, the head of the Ceylonese government delegation, Corea, and others paid a courtesy call on Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu. Zhang Hanfu hosted a banquet to welcome the Ceylonese government delegation.
    Another member of the Ceylonese government delegation, Fonseka, will arrive in Beijing on the 9th. (Attached Photo)
    K. Corea, head of the Ceylonese government delegation, shakes hands with Zhang Hanfu (left), Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of our country, who went to the airport to welcome him.

### 谈判中锡两国建立外交关系问题  锡兰政府代表团到达北京

1956-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    谈判中锡两国建立外交关系问题
    锡兰政府代表团到达北京
    新华社8日讯　以克·科里亚为首的锡兰政府代表团今天从苏联乘专机到达北京，他们是来同中华人民共和国政府谈判建立两国间的外交关系问题的。
    到机场欢迎的有中华人民共和国外交部副部长章汉夫和夫人，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，外交部亚洲司副司长陈叔亮，礼宾司副司长马振武等人。苏联驻华大使尤金，印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁和夫人，英国驻华代办欧念儒也到机场欢迎。
    今晚，锡兰政府代表团团长科里亚等人拜会了外交部副部长章汉夫。章汉夫设宴欢迎锡兰政府代表团。
    锡兰政府代表团另一团员方席卡将在9日到北京。（附图片）
