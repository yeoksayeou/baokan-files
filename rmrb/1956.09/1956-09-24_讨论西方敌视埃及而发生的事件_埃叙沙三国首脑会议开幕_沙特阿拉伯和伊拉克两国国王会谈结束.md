Title: Three-Party Summit of Egypt, Syria and Saudi Arabia Opens to Discuss Events Arising from Western Hostility towards Egypt; Saudi and Iraqi Kings' Talks Conclude
Author:
Date: 1956-09-24
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd, Cairo dispatch: The summit conference of Egypt, Syria and Saudi Arabia opened on the 22nd in Riyadh, the capital of Saudi Arabia. Participating in the conference were Egyptian President Nasser, Syrian President Kuwatly, and Saudi Arabian King Saud.

The Egyptian newspaper "Al-Ahram" reported that Nasser, Kuwatly, and Saud will discuss the events that have occurred since Egypt nationalized the Suez Canal Company, due to the Western countries' imperialist and hostile attitude towards Egypt.

According to the newspaper, the conference also intends to exchange views extensively on the most important issues in the Arab region and some issues in the current international situation.

Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd, Jeddah, Saudi Arabia dispatch: Saudi Arabian King Saud and Iraqi King Faisal concluded their historic two-day talks on September 21st and issued a joint communique.

The joint communique stated that the two kings exchanged views on issues concerning the interests of their two countries and all Arab countries, and "they agreed that their policies should serve the interests of the entire Arab world."

The King of Iraq met with the King of Saudi Arabia in Dammam, a port near the Persian Gulf, at the invitation of the King of Saudi Arabia. This meeting was the first meeting between the two kings. The oil production of these two countries accounts for more than half of the oil production in the Middle East.

According to a report from the Middle East News Agency in Cairo, the two kings also discussed the Suez Canal and Middle East oil issues, as well as the issue of the two countries' aid to Jordan.

### 讨论西方敌视埃及而发生的事件  埃叙沙三国首脑会议开幕  沙特阿拉伯和伊拉克两国国王会谈结束

1956-09-24
第7版()
专栏：

    讨论西方敌视埃及而发生的事件
    埃叙沙三国首脑会议开幕
    沙特阿拉伯和伊拉克两国国王会谈结束
    新华社23日讯　开罗消息：埃及、叙利亚和沙特阿拉伯三国首脑会议，22日在沙特阿拉伯首都利雅得开幕。参加会议的有埃及总统纳赛尔、叙利亚总统库阿特利和沙特阿拉伯国王沙特。
    埃及“金字塔报”报道说，纳赛尔、库阿特利和沙特将对埃及把苏伊士运河公司收归国有之后，由于西方国家对埃及采取帝国主义的敌对态度而发生的事件进行讨论。
    据报纸报道，会议还准备就阿拉伯地区的最重要问题和目前国际局势中的一些问题广泛地交换意见。
    新华社22日讯　沙特阿拉伯吉达消息：沙特阿拉伯国王沙特和伊拉克国王费萨尔9月21日结束了具有历史意义的为时两天的会谈，并且发表了联合公报。
    联合公报说，这两位国王就关于他们两个国家的和所有阿拉伯国家的利益的问题交换了意见，“他们对他们的政策应该为整个阿拉伯世界的利益服务这一点上的看法是一致的”。
    伊拉克国王是应沙特阿拉伯国王的邀请到沙特阿拉伯靠近波斯湾的港口丹曼会见的。这次会谈是这两位国王之间的第一次会谈。这两个国家的石油产量占中东石油产量的一半以上。
    另据中东通讯社开罗消息，这两位国王讨论了苏伊士运河和中东石油问题，也讨论了两国援助约旦的问题。
