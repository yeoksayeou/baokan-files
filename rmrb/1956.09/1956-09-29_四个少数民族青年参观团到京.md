Title: Four Youth Visiting Delegations of Minority Nationalities Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: Four youth visiting delegations of minority nationalities from the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture, and Gansu Province arrived in Beijing successively today and yesterday.

	The Inner Mongolia National Youth Visiting Delegation consists of 107 young people from all walks of life from Mongolian, Manchu, Solon, Oroqen, and Daur nationalities in Inner Mongolia. The head of the delegation is Liang Manchao. The Xinjiang National Youth Visiting Delegation consists of 57 young people from all walks of life from Uygur, Kazakh, Mongolian, and Xibe nationalities. The head of the delegation is Abudongniyazi. The Yanbian National Youth Visiting Delegation consists of 65 young people from all walks of life from Korean, Mongolian, Hui, and Manchu nationalities in the autonomous prefecture. The head of the delegation is Jin Chenglie. The Gansu National Youth Visiting Delegation consists of 96 young people from Tibetan, Mongolian, Tu, and Yugur nationalities in Gansu. The head of the delegation is Niandanzeng.

### 四个少数民族青年参观团到京

1956-09-29
第2版()
专栏：

    四个少数民族青年参观团到京
    据新华社28日讯　内蒙古自治区、新疆维吾尔自治区、延边朝鲜族自治州和甘肃省等四个少数民族青年参观团在今天和昨天先后到达北京。
    内蒙民族青年参观团由内蒙各地的蒙古、满、索伦、鄂伦春、达呼尔等民族的各界青年共一百零七人组成，团长是梁满超。新疆民族青年参观团由维吾尔、哈萨克、蒙古、锡伯等民族的各界青年共五十七人组成，团长是阿不东尼牙孜。延边民族青年参观团由自治州各地的朝鲜、蒙古、回、满等民族的各界青年共六十五人组成，团长是金承烈。甘肃民族青年参观团由甘肃各地的藏、蒙古、土、裕固等民族的青年九十六人组成，团长是年旦增。
