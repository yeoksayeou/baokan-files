Title: Talks Between Leaders of the CPSU and the Communist Party of Luxembourg
Author:
Date: 1956-09-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 5th: According to TASS, the leaders of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union held talks on September 5th with the leaders of the Communist Party of Luxembourg, who are visiting Moscow.

Participating in the talks were: Suslov, Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee, Ponomarev, member of the Central Committee, Urbany, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Luxembourg, and Useldinger and Gira, members of the Political Bureau. The talks were conducted in a comradely and friendly spirit.

### 苏共卢共领导人会谈

1956-09-07
第5版()
专栏：

    苏共卢共领导人会谈
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科5日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会领导人9月5日同访问莫斯科的卢森堡共产党领导人举行了会谈。
    参加会谈的是：苏共中央书记苏斯洛夫、中央委员波诺马烈夫，卢共中央总书记欧班尼、政治局委员乌撤尔丁格和基尔。会谈是本着同志式的友好精神进行的。
