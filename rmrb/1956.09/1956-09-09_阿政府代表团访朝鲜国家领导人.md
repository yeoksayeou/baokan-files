Title: Albanian Government Delegation Visits North Korean State Leaders
Author:
Date: 1956-09-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 8th. The Albanian government delegation, invited to visit Korea, called on the leaders of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Korean Cabinet, and the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea on the evening of the 7th.

Premier of the Korean Cabinet, Kim Il-sung, held a banquet on the same evening to entertain the Albanian government delegation.

### 阿政府代表团访朝鲜国家领导人

1956-09-09
第5版()
专栏：

    阿政府代表团访朝鲜国家领导人
    新华社平壤8日电　应邀到朝鲜访问的阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团，7日晚上访问了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会、朝鲜内阁和朝鲜劳动党中央委员会的领导人。
    朝鲜内阁首相金日成在同一天晚上举行了招待阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团的宴会。
