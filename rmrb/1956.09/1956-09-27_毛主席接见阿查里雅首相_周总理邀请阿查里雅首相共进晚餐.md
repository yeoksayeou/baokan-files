Title: Chairman Mao Receives Prime Minister Acharya; Premier Zhou Invites Prime Minister Acharya to Dinner

Author:
Date: 1956-09-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Tanka Prasad Acharya, and his wife, Lieutenant General Rana, the Nepalese Ambassador to China, Narapratap Thapa, the Foreign Affairs Secretary of the Nepalese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Bhadrakali Mishra, the Chairman of the Nepalese People's Party, and other senior officials accompanying the Prime Minister at Qin Zheng Dian, Zhongnanhai, at 8:00 PM this evening.

    Present at the meeting were Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council, Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, and Saifuddin, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, He Long, and Ulanhu, Vice Premiers of the State Council, Zhang Wentian and Zhang Hanfu, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs, and Pan Zili, Chinese Ambassador to Nepal.

    Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and his wife, invited Tanka Prasad Acharya, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, and his wife to dinner this evening.

    Also invited from the Kingdom of Nepal were Lieutenant General Rana, the Nepalese Ambassador to China, and Narapratap Thapa, the Foreign Affairs Secretary of the Nepalese Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

    Accompanying them for dinner were Ulanhu, Vice Premier of the State Council, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Pan Zili, Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, and his wife.

### 毛主席接见阿查里雅首相  周总理邀请阿查里雅首相共进晚餐

1956-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见阿查里雅首相
    周总理邀请阿查里雅首相共进晚餐
    新华社26日讯　中华人民共和国毛泽东主席，今天晚间八时在中南海勤政殿接见了尼泊尔王国首相坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅和他的夫人，尼泊尔驻华大使拉纳中将，尼泊尔外交部外事秘书纳腊普腊塔普·塔帕，尼泊尔人民党主席巴德腊卡里·米斯腊，以及首相随行的其他高级官员。
    接见的时候在座的，有中华人民共和国副主席朱德，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来，z代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、陈叔通、赛福鼎，国务院副总理陈云、彭德怀、贺龙、乌兰夫，外交部副部长张闻天、章汉夫，中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力。
    新华社26日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来及夫人，在今天晚间邀请尼泊尔王国首相坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅及夫人共进晚餐。
    尼泊尔王国方面应邀出席的，还有尼泊尔驻华大使拉纳中将，尼泊尔王国外交部外事秘书纳腊普腊塔普·塔帕。
    陪同进餐的，有国务院副总理乌兰夫，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力及夫人。
