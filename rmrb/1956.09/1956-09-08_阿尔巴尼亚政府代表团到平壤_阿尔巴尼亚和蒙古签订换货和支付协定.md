Title: Albanian Government Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang; Albania and Mongolia Sign Barter and Payment Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 7th. An Albanian government delegation headed by Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Albania, and Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, arrived in Pyongyang by plane this afternoon for a visit to Korea at the invitation of the Korean government. More than 250,000 people in Pyongyang warmly welcomed the distinguished guests from afar.

After reviewing the guard of honor, Shehu and Hoxha, together with Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of Korea, who came to greet them, rode in an open-top car to Pyongyang, where they were warmly welcomed by the masses along the way. The national flags of Korea and Albania fluttered everywhere at Pyongyang Airport and throughout Pyongyang.

In his welcoming speech at the airport, Kim Il-sung said that the visit of the Albanian government delegation to Korea would further strengthen the internationalist mutual assistance and fraternal friendship and solidarity between the two peoples, and would play a significant role in strengthening the power of the socialist camp.

In his reply, Shehu said that the mutual visits of the government delegations of Korea and Albania would promote our friendly cooperation and the cause of peace and friendship between the peoples of Asia and Europe.

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Ulaanbaatar dispatch: The Albanian government delegation headed by Shehu and Hoxha, at the invitation of the Korean government, left Ulaanbaatar by special plane this morning and flew to Pyongyang for a friendly visit.

The Albanian government delegation arrived in Ulaanbaatar on September 3. During its visit to Mongolia, it signed a barter and payment agreement with the Mongolian government.

### 阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团到平壤  阿尔巴尼亚和蒙古签订换货和支付协定

1956-09-08
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团到平壤
    阿尔巴尼亚和蒙古签订换货和支付协定
    新华社平壤7日电　应邀到朝鲜访问的以阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席谢胡、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查为首的阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团今天下午乘飞机到达平壤。平壤市二十五万多人热烈欢迎了来自远方的贵宾。
    谢胡和霍查等检阅仪仗队后，随即和前来迎接的朝鲜内阁首相金日成一同乘敞篷汽车前往平壤市，沿途受到群众的热烈欢迎。平壤飞机场和平壤全市到处飘扬着朝阿两国国旗。
    金日成在飞机场上致欢迎词说，阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团这次来朝鲜访问，将进一步加强两国人民之间的国际主义的互相援助和兄弟般的友谊团结，对加强社会主义阵营的威力将起重大作用。
    谢胡致答词说，朝阿两国政府代表团的相互访问，将促进我们的友谊合作关系和亚洲与欧洲人民的和平和友谊的事业。
    据新华社7日讯　乌兰巴托消息：以谢胡和霍查为首的阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团应朝鲜政府的邀请，已经在今天早晨乘专机离开乌兰巴托，飞往平壤进行友好访问。
    阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团是在9月3日到达乌兰巴托的，在蒙古访问期间，曾经同蒙古政府签订了一项换货和支付协定。
