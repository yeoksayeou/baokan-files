Title: Mongolian Art Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-30
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. The Mongolian art workers delegation, invited to visit China, arrived in Beijing yesterday and today in two groups, led by the head of the delegation, T. Damdinsuren.

　　The delegation arrived in Hohhot, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region in late August. For a month, they visited factories, universities and colleges, and art troupes in Hohhot and other places.

### 蒙古艺术工作者代表团到京

1956-09-30
第3版()
专栏：

    蒙古艺术工作者代表团到京
    新华社29日讯　应邀来中国访问的蒙古艺术工作者代表团一行五人，在团长策·达姆丁苏伦率领下，昨天和今天先后到达北京。
    代表团是8月下旬到达内蒙古自治区的呼和浩特的。一个月以来，他们在呼和浩特等地参观访问了工厂、高等学校和文艺团体。
