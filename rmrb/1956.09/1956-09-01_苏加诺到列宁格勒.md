Title: Sukarno Arrives in Leningrad
Author:
Date: 1956-09-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Leningrad, August 31st (TASS): The President of the Republic of Indonesia, Dr. Sukarno, and his entourage arrived in Leningrad at 10:00 a.m. on August 31st via the "Red Arrow" express train from Moscow.

The distinguished guest and his entourage were welcomed at the station by Smirnov, Chairman of the Leningrad City Soviet Executive Committee, representatives of the Supreme Soviets of the Soviet Union and the Russian Federation, numerous representatives of the working people, and journalists.

### 苏加诺到列宁格勒

1956-09-01
第5版()
专栏：

    苏加诺到列宁格勒
    据新华社31日讯　据塔斯社列宁格勒讯：印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺博士和他的随行人员从莫斯科乘“红箭”快车在8月31日上午十时到达列宁格勒。
    这位贵宾和他的随行人员在车站受到列宁格勒市苏维埃执行委员会主席斯米尔诺夫、苏联和俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃代表，许许多多劳动人民代表和记者的欢迎。
