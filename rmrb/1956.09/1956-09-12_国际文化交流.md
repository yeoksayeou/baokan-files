Date: 1956-09-12

    ·International Cultural Exchange·
    Finnish Prime Minister Hosts Reception for Chinese Art Exhibition
    Finnish Prime Minister Fagerholm hosted a reception on the evening of September 9th for the Chinese Art Exhibition that opened in Helsinki on the 7th.
    Chinese Ambassador to Finland Chen Xinren attended the reception. Tang Lan, a Chinese expert in bronzes and ancient literature, and archaeologist Li Wenxin, who arrived in Helsinki on the 8th, were also invited to attend the reception.
  My Art Troupe Meets with Brazilian Press
    At the invitation of the Brazilian Press Association, Chu Tunan, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, Zhao Feng, deputy head, Wang Li, and some members of the troupe visited the Brazilian Press Association Building on September 10th. There, Dr. Herbert Moses introduced them to the Brazilian journalists. Chu Tunan expressed his respect for the Brazilian people to the journalists present. Subsequently, Deputy Head Zhao Feng introduced them to Chinese Peking Opera, folk dance, and folk songs, and answered questions from the journalists.
    Liszt International Piano Competition Begins
    The Liszt International Piano Competition opened at the Budapest Academy of Music on September 10th.
    The competition is being held to commemorate the 70th anniversary of the death of the great Hungarian composer and pianist.
    Pianists from twenty countries including the Soviet Union, China, Britain, France, Austria, Poland, Italy, West Germany, Argentina, and Australia are participating in the competition.
  Tagore's Works in Czech Edition Exhibited in India
    An exhibition of Czechoslovak translations of Tagore's works and various letters from the poet to Czech politicians and scholars opened in Calcutta on September 8th.
    An important exhibit at the exhibition is a letter Tagore wrote to Dr. Lesny of Czechoslovakia before World War II, after Western countries sold out Czechoslovakia in Munich. In this letter, Tagore expressed his deep sympathy for the people of that country.
                (Xinhua News Agency)

### 国际文化交流

1956-09-12
第5版()
专栏：国际文化交流

    ·国际文化交流·
    芬兰总理为中国艺术展览会
        举行招待会
    芬兰总理法格霍姆9月9日晚为7日在赫尔辛基开幕的中国艺术展览会举行招待会。
    中国驻芬兰大使陈辛仁出席了招待会。8日抵达赫尔辛基的中国青铜器和古文学专家唐兰和考古学家李文信也应邀出席了招待会。
  我艺术团和巴西新闻界见面
    中国艺术团团长楚图南、副团长赵沨、王力和部分团员应巴西新闻协会邀请，在9月10日前往巴西新闻协会大厦。在那里，赫伯特·摩斯博士把他们介绍给巴西的新闻记者们。楚图南对在场的记者表示了对巴西人民的敬意。接着，副团长赵沨向他们介绍了中国的京剧、民间舞蹈和民歌，并且回答了记者们提出的问题。
    李斯特国际钢琴比赛开始
    李斯特国际钢琴比赛9月10日在布达佩斯音乐院开幕。
    这次比赛是为纪念匈牙利这位伟大的作曲家和钢琴演奏家逝世七十周年而举办的。
    参加比赛的有苏联、中国、英国、法国、奥地利、波兰、意大利、西德、阿根廷、澳大利亚等二十个国家的钢琴演奏家。
  泰戈尔作品捷文本在印展出
    泰戈尔作品的捷克斯洛伐克译本和这位诗人致捷政治家和学者的各种函件的展览会，于9月8日在加尔各答开幕。
    展览会的重要展品是：泰戈尔在第二次世界大战以前西方国家在慕尼黑出卖捷克斯洛伐克以后写给捷克斯洛伐克列斯尼博士的一封信，泰戈尔在这封信中表示了他对这个国家的人民的深刻同情。
                （新华社）
