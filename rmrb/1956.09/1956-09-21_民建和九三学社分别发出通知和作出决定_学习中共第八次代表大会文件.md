Title: China Democratic National Construction Association and Jiusan Society Issue Notices and Make Decisions to Study Documents of the Eighth National Congress of the CPC
Author:
Date: 1956-09-21
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency Report:

    China Democratic National Construction Association (CDNCA) Central Standing Committee and the Jiusan Society Central Standing Committee, respectively issued notices or made decisions, requiring subordinate local organizations and directly affiliated groups to earnestly study the documents of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China and to celebrate the convening of the congress with practical actions.
    The notice issued by the CDNCA Central Standing Committee on the 17th pointed out that the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China will play an extremely important role in the complete realization of our country's socialist transformation, the rapid development of the socialist cause, and the further consolidation and expansion of the people's democratic united front. The Central Standing Committee requested that local organizations promptly organize members to earnestly study the documents of the congress. The notice requested that through this study, the CDNCA and its members should improve both ideologically and in their work, make further achievements in cultivating and improving CDNCA members to become the backbone of the industrial and commercial sectors, giving play to the initiative in production and operation, managing the relationship between public and private partnership, and assisting the Communist Party of China and the government in completing the socialist transformation of capitalist industry and commerce.
    The Jiusan Society Central Standing Committee made a decision on congratulating the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China at its fifth meeting. It also issued a notice to its subordinate organizations on the 15th regarding the implementation of this decision. The decision pointed out: The convening of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China is a glorious milestone in our country's great socialist construction cause and has far-reaching historical and global significance. During the congress, local organizations of the Jiusan Society should promptly organize members to earnestly study the various documents of the congress in order to raise their socialist awareness and policy level. The decision stated that all members should also celebrate the convening of this congress with practical actions, and under the principle of "long-term coexistence and mutual supervision," should unite more closely around the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao, strive to advance towards science, actively promote "letting a hundred flowers bloom and a hundred schools of thought contend," so that our country's scientific and cultural undertakings can rapidly catch up with the world's advanced level, and build our country into a strong socialist industrial nation.

### 民建和九三学社分别发出通知和作出决定  学习中共第八次代表大会文件

1956-09-21
第7版()
专栏：

    民建和九三学社分别发出通知和作出决定
    学习中共第八次代表大会文件
    新华社讯　中国民主建国会中央常务委员会和九三学社中央常务委员会，分别发布通知或作出决定，要求所属各级地方组织和直属小组认真学习中国共产党第八次全国代表大会的文件和以实际行动来祝贺这次大会的召开。
    中国民主建国会中央常务委员会在17日发布的通知中指出，中国共产党第八次全国代表大会将对我国社会主义改造的完全实现，社会主义事业的迅速发展，以及对人民民主统一战线的进一步巩固与扩大，起着极为重大的作用。中央常务委员会要求各地方组织及时组织会员认真地学习代表大会的文件。通知要求中国民主建国会及其会员通过这次学习能在思想上和工作上都提高一步，在培养、提高中国民主建国会会员成为工商界的骨干分子，发挥生产经营的积极性，搞好公私共事关系，协助中国共产党和政府完成对资本主义工商业的社会主义改造方面做出进一步的成绩。
    九三学社中央常务委员会在第五次会议上作出了关于祝贺中国共产党第八次全国代表大会的决定。并且在15日向所属各级组织发布了关于执行这一决定的通知。决定指出：中国共产党第八次全国代表大会的召开，是我国伟大社会主义建设事业的一个光辉的里程碑，具有深远的历史意义和世界意义。九三学社的各级地方组织在代表大会开会期间应及时组织社员认真学习大会的各项文件，以提高社会主义觉悟和政策水平。决定说，全体社员还应以实际行动来祝贺这一次大会的召开，要在“长期共存、互相监督”的方针之下，更加紧密地团结在中国共产党和毛主席的周围，努力向科学进军，积极推进“百家争鸣”，使我国科学文化事业迅速赶上世界先进水平，把我国建设成一个强大的社会主义的工业国家。
