Title: Soviet Air Force Marshal Zhigarev Visits Britain
Author:
Date: 1956-09-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, London, September 3rd: TASS reports that a Soviet Air Force delegation led by Soviet Air Force Commander-in-Chief, Air Force Marshal Zhigarev, arrived in London on September 2nd aboard a Soviet "Tu-104" jet aircraft. The delegation is visiting Britain at the invitation of the British Royal Air Force Supreme Council. The visit will end on September 9th.

A Soviet delegation of aviation industry experts, led by Soviet Minister of Aviation Industry, Dementiev, also arrived in London on the same day at the invitation of the British Ministry of Supply. This delegation will visit British aircraft factories and other departments related to the aviation industry.

### 苏联空军主帅日加列夫访问英国

1956-09-04
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联空军主帅日加列夫访问英国
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社伦敦讯：苏联空军代表团在苏联空军总司令日加列夫空军主帅率领下，9月2日乘苏联“Ty—104”式喷气飞机抵达伦敦。代表团是应英国皇家空军最高委员会的邀请到英国访问的。访问将在9月9日结束。
    苏联航空工业部长杰明捷夫率领的苏联航空工业专家代表团应英国供应部的邀请，也在同一天抵达伦敦。这个代表团将访问英国的飞机工厂以及同航空工业有关的其他部门。
