Title: China Opens International Telephone Service with Sweden and Denmark
Author:
Date: 1956-09-17
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. In order to further facilitate communication between China and Sweden and Denmark, the Chinese telecommunications authorities have consulted with the telecommunications authorities of the relevant countries and have decided to open international telephone services between Beijing, Shanghai and other places and various places in Sweden and Denmark from August 16th and September 16th respectively.

### 我国和瑞典、丹麦  开放国际电话业务

1956-09-17
第7版()
专栏：

    我国和瑞典、丹麦
    开放国际电话业务
    新华社16日讯　为了进一步便利我国和瑞典、丹麦之间的通信联络，我国电信主管机关已经同各有关国家的电信机关商妥，分别从8月16日和9月16日起，开放北京、上海等地与瑞典、丹麦各地往来的国际电话业务。
