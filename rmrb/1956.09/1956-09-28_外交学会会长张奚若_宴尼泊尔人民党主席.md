Title: Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Hosts a Banquet for the Chairman of the Nepali People's Party
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-28
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 27th: Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet at noon today in honor of Ba. Misra, Chairman of the Nepali People's Party. Misra is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, along with the Prime Minister of Nepal. Chen Hansheng, Qiao Guanhua, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and director Zhang Wenjin attended the banquet.

### 外交学会会长张奚若  宴尼泊尔人民党主席

1956-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    外交学会会长张奚若
    宴尼泊尔人民党主席
    新华社27日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今天中午举行宴会，招待尼泊尔人民党主席巴·米斯腊。米斯腊是应中国人民外交学会邀请，和尼泊尔首相一同来华访问的。出席作陪的有中国人民外交学会副会长陈翰笙、乔冠华和理事章文晋等。
