Title: Chinese Products Extremely Popular at the Vienna International Fair; 90,000 Visitors to the Chinese Pavilion on Opening Day
Author:
Date: 1956-09-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 11th: Vienna Dispatch: The Vienna International Fair opened on September 9th. In addition to Austria, thirty-three other countries are participating in the exhibition, sixteen of which have pavilions at the fair.

    Fair Chairman Heinl said in his opening speech that this year's fair will make an important contribution to East-West trade.

    This is China's first time participating in the Vienna Fair. The Chinese Pavilion, covering an area of 1,400 square meters, has aroused great interest among visitors. The crowds visiting the Chinese Pavilion were very crowded from morning till night. On the first day alone, approximately 90,000 people visited the Chinese Pavilion. Austrian Minister of the Interior Helmer, Austrian Credit Bank General Manager Jocham, and Chairman of the Central Committee of the Austrian Communist Party, Koplenig, also visited the Chinese Pavilion.

    At the fair, machines manufactured in New China, as well as diagrams and models of water conservancy projects, the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant, and Anshan Iron and Steel, attracted many visitors. Many more lingered in front of handicrafts such as ivory carvings, lacquer carvings, carpets, and embroidery.

### 我国产品在维也纳国际博览会上极受观众欢迎  中国馆开幕第一天就有九万人参观

1956-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我国产品在维也纳国际博览会上极受观众欢迎
    中国馆开幕第一天就有九万人参观
    据新华社布拉格11日电　维也纳消息：维也纳国际博览会9月9日开幕。除了奥地利本国以外，还有三十三个国家参加了这次博览会的展出，其中有十六个国家在博览会中设有展览馆。
    博览会主席海因耳在开幕时致词说，本届博览会对东西方贸易将有重要的贡献。
    中国是第一次参加维也纳博览会。占地一千四百平方公尺的中国馆引起观众极大的兴趣，参观中国馆的人群从早到晚都非常拥挤。在第一天中就有大约九万人参观了中国馆。奥地利内政部长赫尔麦、奥地利信贷银行总经理约哈姆和奥地利共产党中央委员会主席柯普勒尼希等都参观了中国馆。
    在博览会上，新中国制造的机器以及有关水利建设工程、第一汽车制造厂和鞍钢的图解和模型吸引了许多观众。在象牙雕刻、雕漆、地毯和刺绣等手工艺品前面，许多观众更是流连不去。
