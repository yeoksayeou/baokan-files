Title: US, Britain, and France Intensify Efforts to Implement Plan to Seize the Suez Canal; So-Called Eighteen-Nation Conference of User Nations to be Held in London
Author:
Date: 1956-09-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 15th: The United States, Britain, and France are intensifying their activities to establish the "Canal Users' Association" they have proposed.

British officials announced on the 14th that the United States, Britain, and France have invited the remaining fifteen countries that supported the Dulles plan at the London Conference to jointly hold a so-called eighteen-nation conference of canal users. This conference is scheduled to be held in London on September 19th.

The White House announced on the same day that US Secretary of State Dulles would fly to London on the night of September 17th to attend the aforementioned meeting. According to senior officials in Washington, Dulles' main purpose is to seek broad support for the US-British-French "Users' Association" plan. Before the opening of the conference, Dulles will hold trilateral consultations with British and French representatives in advance to adjust strategies.

British Foreign Secretary Lloyd already arrived in Paris by plane on September 14th and is scheduled to discuss preparations for the conference with French Foreign Minister Pineau on the 15th.

Before going to Paris on the 14th, Lloyd successively received the French, Norwegian, Swedish, Danish, and Baghdad Pact member states Iran, Iraq, Turkey, and Pakistan's envoys to London.

At the same time, Britain and France are stepping up military operations and creating a tense atmosphere. In addition, the British Embassy in Egypt issued a third "warning" on September 14th to British nationals to leave Egypt.

### 美英法加紧推行夺取苏彝士运河计划  将在伦敦召开所谓使用国十八国会议

1956-09-16
第6版()
专栏：

    美英法加紧推行夺取苏彝士运河计划  将在伦敦召开所谓使用国十八国会议
    新华社15日讯　英、法、美三国正在为建立它们所提出的“运河使用国协会”进行紧张的活动。
    英国官方14日宣布，英法美三国已经邀请在伦敦会议中支持杜勒斯方案的其余十五个国家，共同举行一个所谓运河使用国的十八国会议。这个会议预定9月19日在伦敦举行。
    白宫在同一天宣布，美国国务卿杜勒斯将在9月17日夜晚飞往伦敦参加上述会议。据华盛顿高级官员说，杜勒斯的主要目的是要为英法美“使用国协会”计划争取广泛支持。在会议开幕以前，杜勒斯将预先同英、法代表举行三边协商，以便调整策略。
    英国外交大臣劳埃德已经在9月14日乘飞机到达巴黎，预定15日同法国外长比诺商讨关于这次会议的准备工作。
    在14日前往巴黎以前，劳埃德先后接见了法国、挪威、瑞典、丹麦以及参加巴格达条约的伊朗、伊拉克、土耳其和巴基斯坦驻伦敦的使节。
    同时，英法还加紧进行军事行动和制造紧张气氛。此外，英国驻埃及大使馆9月14日第三次“警告”英国侨民离开埃及。
