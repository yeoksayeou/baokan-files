Title: Indonesian Bali Dance Troupe Invited to Visit and Perform in Our Country
Author:
Date: 1956-09-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 1st – The Indonesian Bali Dance Troupe is about to visit and perform in China for three months at the invitation of the Chinese government.

　　The dance troupe is scheduled to arrive in Jakarta on September 4th and then travel to China by ship.

　　Balinese dance is world-renowned. The China-Indonesia Friendship Association has invited the Balinese Dance Troupe to visit and perform in China.

### 印度尼西亚峇里舞蹈团应邀将到我国访问演出

1956-09-02
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚峇里舞蹈团应邀将到我国访问演出
    新华社雅加达1日电　印度尼西亚峇里舞蹈团即将应邀前往中国作三个月的访问演出。
    这个舞蹈团预定在9月4日到雅加达，然后乘轮船前往中国。
    峇里岛的舞蹈是世界闻名的。中国印度尼西亚友好协会邀请了峇里舞蹈团到中国访问演出。
