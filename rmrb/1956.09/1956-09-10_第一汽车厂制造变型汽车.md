Title: First Automobile Works Manufactures Variant Vehicles
Author:
Date: 1956-09-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency

    First Automobile Works Manufactures Variant Vehicles
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The First Automobile Works is manufacturing a batch of variant vehicles. This batch of variant vehicles includes three types: water sprinklers, fire engines, and drill trucks. These three types of variant vehicles are basically manufactured according to the design of the Jiefang-brand trucks. Individual parts have been partially modified in the design to suit specific purposes. For example, while Jiefang-brand trucks are deep green, these variant vehicles use red, bean green, or gray.
    After the First Automobile Works manufactures the vehicle parts, this batch of variant vehicles will be transported to relevant factories in Dalian, Shenyang, and other places to install water spraying, fire-fighting, and drilling equipment.
    This batch of variant vehicles totals nine. They are part of the eighteen vehicles that the First Automobile Works is currently manufacturing in preparation for an overseas exhibition.

### 第一汽车厂制造变型汽车

1956-09-10
第2版()
专栏：

    第一汽车厂制造变型汽车
    新华社讯　第一汽车制造厂正在制造一批变型汽车。这批变型汽车有洒水车、消防车和车钻等三种。这三种变型汽车，基本上都是根据解放牌载重汽车的设计进行制造的。也有个别部分为了适合特殊的用途在设计上有部分的改变。如解放牌汽车采用深绿色，这批变型汽车采用的是红色、豆绿色或灰色等。
    这批变型汽车在第一汽车制造厂制造出汽车部分后，将分别运到大连、沈阳等地的有关工厂去加装洒水、消防和钻探设备。
    这批变型汽车一共九辆。是第一汽车制造厂最近正在制造的十八辆准备出国展览的汽车中的一部分。
