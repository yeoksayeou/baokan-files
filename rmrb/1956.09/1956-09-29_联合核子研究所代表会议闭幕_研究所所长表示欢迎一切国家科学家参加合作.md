Title: Joint Institute for Nuclear Research Representative Meeting Concludes; Institute Director Expresses Welcome to Scientists from All Countries to Participate in Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th: According to TASS, the meeting of plenipotentiary representatives of the member states of the Joint Institute for Nuclear Research concluded in Moscow on the 27th.

Following the conclusion of the meeting, Professor Blokhintsev, Director of the Institute, stated at a press conference that the meeting had adopted the charter of the Joint Institute for Nuclear Research. The charter stipulates the basic aims and tasks of this large-scale international scientific research institution. The charter stipulates that all members of the Joint Institute for Nuclear Research are equal, regardless of the amount of material they contribute, both in the management of the Institute and in scientific research.

The Scientific Committee and the Finance Committee of the Institute also held their first meetings recently. The Scientific Committee approved the long-term plan for scientific research work for the next five years.

Blokhintsev also said that many outstanding scientists from the member countries of the Institute are already participating in the work of the laboratories. At least fifty foreign scientists will come to the Institute to work this year. The doors of the Institute are open to all countries, and they can participate as long as they are willing to become members of this new international science center.

### 联合核子研究所代表会议闭幕  研究所所长表示欢迎一切国家科学家参加合作

1956-09-29
第8版()
专栏：

    联合核子研究所代表会议闭幕
    研究所所长表示欢迎一切国家科学家参加合作
    据新华社28日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：联合核子研究所成员国全权代表会议27日在莫斯科闭幕。
    会议结束后，研究所所长勃洛欣采夫教授在记者招待会上说，这次会议通过了联合核子研究所的章程。在章程中规定了这一规模宏大的国际科学研究机构的基本宗旨和任务。章程规定联合核子研究所的一切成员无论是在对研究所的管理工作上，或是在科学研究工作上，不问它们贡献的物质多寡，都是一律平等的。
    研究所的学术委员会和财政委员会最近也头一次举行了会议。学术委员会批准了今后五年科学研究工作的远景计划。
    勃洛欣采夫还说，研究所成员国许多卓越的科学工作者现在已经参加各实验室的工作。今年年内至少有五十个外国科学家来研究所担任工作。研究所的门为一切国家敞开着，只要它们愿意成为这一新的国际科学中心的会员，它们就可以参加。
