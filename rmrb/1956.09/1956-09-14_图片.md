    The state-owned Wuhan No. 1 Cotton Textile Mill is a modern factory with 50,000 spindles. All the equipment in the factory is made in our own country. Most of the cotton it uses is produced on both sides of the Han River. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Xinning photographed.
