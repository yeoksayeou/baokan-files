Title: Indian Parliamentary Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 28th. The Indian Parliamentary Delegation, consisting of twenty-five members, arrived in Guangzhou today from Shenzhen by special train, accompanied by Qu Wu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress. Chen Rutang and Wei Jinfei, Vice Governors of Guangdong Province, and Huang Changshui, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou, were present at the station to welcome them.
The Indian Parliamentary Delegation is visiting China at the invitation of Chairman Liu Shaoqi to attend the National Day celebrations and to conduct visits. The head of the delegation is Anantha Sayanam Ayyangar, Speaker of the People's Chamber of the Indian Republic. Members include S. V. Krishnamoorthy Rao, Deputy Speaker of the Council of States of the Indian Republic, and members of various political parties.

### 印度国会代表团到广州

1956-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    印度国会代表团到广州
    新华社广州28日电　印度共和国国会代表团一行二十五人，今天由全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长屈武陪同从深圳乘专车到达广州。广东省副省长陈汝棠、魏今非，广州市副市长黄长水等曾到车站欢迎。
    印度共和国国会代表团是应刘少奇委员长邀请来我国参加国庆节观礼和进行参观访问的。团长是印度共和国人民院议长阿南塔·萨亚纳姆·阿延加尔，团员包括有印度共和国联邦院副议长斯·符·克里希纳穆提·拉奥和其他各党派的议员。
