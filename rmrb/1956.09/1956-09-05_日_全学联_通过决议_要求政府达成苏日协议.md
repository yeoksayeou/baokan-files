Title: Japan "All-Japan Student Federation" Passes Resolution Demanding Government Reach Soviet-Japanese Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-05
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 4th—The All-Japan Federation of Student Self-Government Associations passed a resolution demanding that the Japanese government immediately reach an agreement in the Soviet-Japanese negotiations, and strongly protested Dulles's speech obstructing the Soviet-Japanese negotiations. The Federation also requested the Japanese government to convene a special session of the Diet to discuss the Soviet-Japanese negotiation issue.

At the central committee meeting held on the 2nd, 3rd, and 4th, this nationwide student union also passed resolutions demanding that the United States, Britain, and the Soviet Union immediately conclude international agreements banning atomic and hydrogen bombs and agreements on arms reduction; supporting the resolutions of the Second World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs; opposing the United States turning Okinawa Island into a nuclear weapons base; and opposing the expansion of US military bases in Japan.

The General Federation also passed a resolution calling for the protection of university autonomy and opposing thought control.

### 日“全学联”通过决议  要求政府达成苏日协议

1956-09-05
第5版()
专栏：

    日“全学联”通过决议
    要求政府达成苏日协议
    新华社4日讯　东京消息：全日本学生自治会总联合会通过决议，要求日本政府立即使苏日谈判达成协议，并且严重抗议杜勒斯阻碍苏日谈判的发言。联合会还要求日本政府召开国会临时会议，讨论苏日谈判问题。
    这个全国性学生联合组织在2、3、4日举行的中央委员会会议上，还通过决议要求美国、英国、苏联立即缔结禁止原子弹和氢弹的国际协定以及裁减军备的协定；支持第二届禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的决议；反对美国把冲绳岛变成原子武器基地；反对美军在日本扩大军事基地。
    总联合会还通过决议，呼吁保卫大学的自治权，反对言论统制。
