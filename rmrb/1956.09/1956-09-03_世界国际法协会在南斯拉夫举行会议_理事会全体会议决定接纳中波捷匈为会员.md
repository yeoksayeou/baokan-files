Title: World International Law Association Holds Meeting in Yugoslavia; Executive Council Meeting Decides to Admit China, Poland, Czechoslovakia, and Hungary as Members
Author:
Date: 1956-09-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency, the 47th meeting of the World International Law Association was held in Dubrovnik, Yugoslavia from August 27 to September 1. Approximately 500 representatives from 38 national branches of this international organization attended the meeting.

Chinese legal scholars Yang Huanan and Chen Tiqiang attended the meeting.

The meeting discussed issues such as active peaceful coexistence between countries, revision of the United Nations Charter, laws on the use of international rivers, and other international law matters. The meeting also elected Dr. Milan Bartos of Yugoslavia as the President of the World International Law Association.

At the plenary session of the council held on the first day of the meeting, decisions were made on the participation of some countries in the association. Prior to the establishment of a national branch of the association in China, the Chinese Political Science and Law Association was admitted as a member of the association. At the same time, the international law associations of Poland, Czechoslovakia, and Hungary were also admitted as members of the association.

### 世界国际法协会在南斯拉夫举行会议  理事会全体会议决定接纳中波捷匈为会员

1956-09-03
第5版()
专栏：

    世界国际法协会在南斯拉夫举行会议
    理事会全体会议决定接纳中波捷匈为会员
    新华社2日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，世界国际法协会第四十七次会议8月27日到9月1日在南斯拉夫的杜布罗夫尼克举行。参加这次会议的有这个国际组织的三十八个国家分会的代表共约五百人。
    中国法律学家杨化南和陈体强出席了这次会议。
    这次会议讨论了各国之间积极的和平共处、修改联合国宪章、国际河流使用法和其他一些国际法方面的问题。会议并且选举南斯拉夫的米兰·巴托什博士为世界国际法协会会长。
    在会议开幕头一天举行的理事会全体会议上，对一些国家参加这个协会的问题作出了决定。在中国成立这个协会的国家分会之前，接纳中国政治法律学会为这个协会的成员。同时还接纳波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利的国际法协会为这个协会的成员。
