Title: Korean Football Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 4

    Korean Football Team Arrives in Beijing
    (Our newspaper dispatch) The football team of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, which is participating in the China, Korea, and Vietnam Three-Nation Football Tournament, arrived in Beijing on the 28th.
    The leader of the football team of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea is Zheng Yihong.
    This football team of twenty-seven people, along with the football team of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, which arrived in Beijing earlier on the 26th, will attend the National Day observation ceremonies, and will hold round-robin matches with the Chinese football team on October 3rd, 6th, and 9th.

### 朝鲜足球队到北京

1956-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜足球队到北京
    本报讯　参加中国、朝鲜、越南三国足球对抗赛的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国足球队在28日到达北京。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国足球队领队是郑义鸿。
    这支由二十七人组成的足球队，和在26日先期到达北京的越南民主共和国足球队将参加国庆节观礼，并且将在10月3日、6日和9日同中国的足球队举行循环赛。
