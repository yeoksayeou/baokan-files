Author:
Date: 1956-09-05
Page: 4

    Reactions to Criticism
    A letter from the General Party Committee of the Taiyuan District Construction and Installation Enterprise: The article "Section Chief Yan's Overtime Work" published in the People's Daily on August 13, which criticized the "overtime work" of playing mahjong by several leading comrades, including Yan Chengping, chief of the personnel section of the Taiyuan Engineering Bureau, and Xu Zhanli, director of the health department, has been investigated and found to be basically in accordance with the facts. Comrades Xu and Yan have now made written self-criticisms to the Party organization.

### 对批评的反应

1956-09-05
第4版()
专栏：

    对批评的反应
    太原区建筑安装企业总党委会来信：8月13日人民日报发表的“颜科长的加班加点”一文，批评太原工程局人事科长颜承平和卫生处长徐展力等几个领导同志“加班加点”打麻将的问题，经我们调查了解，这一批评基本上是符合事实的，现在徐、颜二位同志已向党组织作了书面检查。
