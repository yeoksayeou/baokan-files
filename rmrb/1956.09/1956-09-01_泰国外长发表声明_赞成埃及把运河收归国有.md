Title: Thai Foreign Minister Issues Statement Supporting Egypt's Nationalization of the Canal
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-01
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, August 31st: Thai Foreign Minister Naradhip made a statement on August 31st stating that the Thai government supports Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal and hopes that Egypt will provide guarantees regarding the freedom of navigation on the canal that direct stakeholders might agree to.

The statement said, "If an agreement cannot be reached, this issue should be submitted to the United Nations."

Naradhip's statement also said that Thailand itself does not have much interest regarding the canal, but it hopes for a peaceful resolution in good faith.

### 泰国外长发表声明  赞成埃及把运河收归国有

1956-09-01
第6版()
专栏：

    泰国外长发表声明
    赞成埃及把运河收归国有
    据新华社31日讯　曼谷消息：泰国外交部长那拉底8月31日发表声明说，泰国政府赞成埃及把苏彝士运河收归国有，并且希望埃及提供直接有关国家可能同意的关于运河通航自由的保证。
    声明说：“如果不能达成协议，这个问题就应该提交联合国。”
    那拉底的声明还说，泰国本身没有有关这条运河的多大利益，但是它抱着善意盼望取得和平解决。
