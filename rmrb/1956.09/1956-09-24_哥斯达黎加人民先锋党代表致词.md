Title: Speech by Representative of the People's Vanguard Party of Costa Rica
Author:
Date: 1956-09-24
Page: 3

Dear Comrades:

    The People's Vanguard Party, the party of Costa Rican Communists, organizes and leads the liberation struggle of the Costa Rican people, in one of the least populated countries on the American continent.

    On behalf of the Central Committee of the People's Vanguard Party, our delegation would like to express our deep gratitude to the glorious Chinese Communist Party and its Central Committee for granting us the opportunity to participate in this historic Eighth National Congress.

    Comrades, please allow me to read the congratulatory message from the Central Committee of the People's Vanguard Party that we have brought:

    To the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China in Beijing

    Respected Comrades:

    We extend our lofty and sincere greetings to the Communist Party of China on the occasion of its Eighth National Congress.

    Undoubtedly, the Chinese revolution is another great step forward for mankind towards socialism, following the Soviet revolution. Without a brave, mature, and wise party like the Chinese Communist Party to lead the Chinese people, the Chinese revolution could not have advanced so correctly and firmly.

    Communists all over the world, especially those of us who are struggling in the heartland of American imperialism, strengthen ourselves daily with the example of the Chinese Communist Party and increase our hope under the inspiration of your great achievements.

    We wish the Eighth National Congress correct decisions.

    Long live the great and heroic Chinese people!

    Long live the glorious Chinese Communist Party!

    Long live the experienced and wise Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party!

            The Central Committee of the People's Vanguard Party of Costa Rica

### 哥斯达黎加人民先锋党代表致词

1956-09-24
第3版()
专栏：

    哥斯达黎加人民先锋党代表致词亲爱的同志们：
    哥斯达黎加共产党人的党——人民先锋党——组织和领导着美洲大陆上人口最少的国家之一的哥斯达黎加人民的解放斗争。
    对于给予我们参加这个具有历史意义的第八次代表大会的机会，我们代表团谨以人民先锋党中央委员会的名义，在这里向光荣的中国共产党及其中央委员会致以深切的谢意。
    同志们，请允许我来宣读我们带来的人民先锋党中央委员会的贺词：
    致北京中国共产党第八次全国代表大会敬爱的同志们：
    我们向召开第八次代表大会的中国共产党致以崇高和真诚的敬礼。
    无可怀疑，中国革命是继苏联革命之后，人类又向社会主义迈进一大步。如果没有像中国共产党这样勇敢、成熟和英明的党来领导中国人民，中国革命就不可能那样正确和坚定地向前迈进。
    全世界共产主义者，特别是在美帝国主义统治的心脏地带作斗争的我们，每天都以中国共产党的范例来加强我们自己，在你们伟大成就的鼓舞下增强我们的希望。
    谨祝第八次代表大会获得正确的决定。
    伟大的、英勇的中国人民万岁！
    光荣的中国共产党万岁！
    经验丰富的，英明的中国共产党中央委员会万岁！
            哥斯达黎加人民先锋党中央委员会
