Title: Nepalese Prime Minister Acharya to Visit China Soon; Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, Pan Zili, Hosts Farewell Banquet
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 18th -- Kathmandu dispatch: The Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Nepal, Pan Zili, hosted a banquet on the evening of September 16th to bid farewell to the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Acharya, and his entire entourage, who will be visiting China at the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai.
    Ministers of various ministries of the Kingdom of Nepal, the Commander-in-Chief of the Army, and diplomatic envoys of various countries to Nepal attended the banquet.
    Mishra, Chairman of the Nepali National Congress, who will also be visiting China at the invitation of Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, was also invited to attend the banquet. He will travel to China at the same time as the Prime Minister of Nepal.

### 尼泊尔首相阿查里雅即将访华  我驻尼大使潘自力设宴饯行

1956-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔首相阿查里雅即将访华
    我驻尼大使潘自力设宴饯行
    新华社新德里18日电　加德满都消息：中华人民共和国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力9月16日晚举行宴会，为即将应周恩来总理的邀请到中国访问的尼泊尔王国首相阿查里雅和他的全体随行人员饯行。
    尼泊尔王国政府各部大臣、陆军总司令和各国驻尼泊尔的外交使节出席了这个宴会。
    应中国人民外交学会会长张奚若的邀请将到中国访问的尼泊尔人民大会党主席米斯拉也应邀出席了这个宴会。他将和尼泊尔首相同时到中国。
