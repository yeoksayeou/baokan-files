Title: Syria and Egypt to Conduct Economic Cooperation Based on the Syrian-Egyptian Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-09-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, September 4th: Syria and Egypt have reached an agreement on a series of joint economic measures to be taken by the two countries.

A joint communique issued in Damascus and Cairo on September 3 stated that Egyptian Minister of Commerce Nasser and Syrian Minister of Economy Kalas agreed at a meeting held in Damascus on September 2 to encourage joint investment in various joint economic projects between Egypt and Syria. They recommended the establishment of a "Syrian-Egyptian Bank" and a "Syrian-Egyptian Foreign Trade Company."

### 叙埃协议进行经济合作

1956-09-05
第6版()
专栏：

    叙埃协议进行经济合作
    新华社4日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚和埃及已经就两国采取一系列的联合经济措施问题达成了协议。
    9月3日在大马士革和开罗发表的一项联合公报说，埃及商业部长努赛尔和叙利亚经济部长卡拉斯9月2日在大马士革举行的会谈上，协议鼓励联合投资进行埃及—叙利亚两国的各种联合经济计划。他们建议成立“叙利亚—埃及银行”和“叙利亚—埃及对外贸易公司”。
