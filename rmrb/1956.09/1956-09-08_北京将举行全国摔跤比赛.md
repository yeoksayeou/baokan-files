Title: National Wrestling Competition to be Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 7th. The 1956 National Wrestling (Chinese Wrestling) Competition, organized by the Sports Commission, will begin on September 9th at the Beihai Sports Field in Beijing.

　　More than one hundred and sixty outstanding wrestlers selected from various provinces, cities, and autonomous regions will be divided into five weight classes according to their weight for competition. These five weight classes are flyweight (under 60 kg), lightweight (60 kg to 67.5 kg), middleweight (67.5 kg to 75 kg), light heavyweight (75 kg to 82.5 kg), and heavyweight (over 82.5 kg).

　　In the 1953 National Ethnic Sports Performance and Competition Conference, a wrestling competition was held with less than forty participants. However, this is the first large-scale national wrestling competition of this kind held in China.

### 北京将举行全国摔跤比赛

1956-09-08
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    北京将举行全国摔跤比赛
    据新华社7日讯　体育运动委员会举办的1956年全国摔跤（中国摔跤）比赛，将从9月9日开始在北京市北海体育场举行。
    一百六十多名由各省、市、自治区选拔出来的优秀摔跤选手，将按体重的不同，分为五个等级分别进行比赛。这五个等级是次轻量级（六十公斤以下）、轻量级（六十公斤到六十七点五公斤）、中量级（六十七点五公斤到七十五公斤）、次重量级（七十五公斤到八十二点五公斤）和重量级（八十二点五公斤以上）。
    在1953年全国民族形式体育表演及竞赛大会中，曾经举行过不到四十人参加的摔跤比赛，但是像这次这样大规模的全国性摔跤比赛，在中国还是第一次举行。
