Title: Khrushchev Arrives in Belgrade
Author:
Date: 1956-09-20
Page: 1

    Khrushchev Arrives in Belgrade
    Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, September 19th – TASS, Belgrade, September 19th: Khrushchev arrived in Belgrade at 11:00 this morning by plane from Moscow.
    Among those who went to Zemun Airport to welcome Khrushchev were Josip Broz Tito, General Secretary of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, and Edvard Kardelj, Secretary of the Central Executive Committee of the League of Communists and General Secretary of the Socialist Alliance of Working People of Yugoslavia.

### 赫鲁晓夫到达贝尔格莱德

1956-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫到达贝尔格莱德
    新华社19日讯　塔斯社贝尔格莱德19日讯：赫鲁晓夫今天上午十一时乘飞机从莫斯科到达贝尔格莱德。
    前往泽门机场迎接赫鲁晓夫的有：南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟总书记铁托，共产主义者联盟中央执行委员会书记、南斯拉夫劳动人民社会主义联盟总书记卡德尔等。
