Title: Soviet Union Holds Exhibition Introducing the Chinese Communist Party and New China
Author:
Date: 1956-09-22
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 21st: While the Eighth National Congress of the Chinese Communist Party is in session, the Belorussian Soviet Socialist Republic and the Kirghiz Soviet Socialist Republic held exhibitions introducing the great achievements of the Chinese Communist Party and the construction of New China.

    In the exhibition held at the State Lenin Library in Minsk, the capital of the Belorussian Republic, in addition to displaying the documents already published from the Chinese Communist Party's congress, such as Mao Zedong's opening address, the reports by Liu Shaoqi, Deng Xiaoping, and Zhou Enlai, and Mikoyan's address at the congress, there were also displayed Lenin's works on the national liberation movement in China and Asian countries, Mao Zedong's works, and documents and materials related to the founding of the People's Republic of China and the First National People's Congress.

    In libraries, clubs, and cultural palaces throughout the Kirghiz Republic, exhibitions were held introducing the tremendous achievements of New China in industrial, agricultural, and cultural construction.

### 苏联举办展览会介绍中国共产党和新中国

1956-09-22
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联举办展览会介绍中国共产党和新中国
    据新华社莫斯科21日电　在中国共产党第八次全国代表大会开会期间，苏联的白俄罗斯共和国和吉尔吉斯共和国举办了展览会，介绍中国共产党和新中国建设的巨大成就。
    在白俄罗斯共和国首都明斯克国家列宁图书馆举办的展览会里，除了陈列中国共产党这次代表大会已经发表的文件，如毛泽东的开幕词，刘少奇、邓小平、周恩来的报告，以及米高扬在大会上的致词以外；还陈列了列宁关于中国和亚洲国家民族解放运动的著作，毛泽东的著作，有关中华人民共和国的成立和第一届全国人民代表大会的文件和材料。
    在吉尔吉斯共和国各地的图书馆、俱乐部、文化宫里，举办了介绍新中国在工业、农业、文化建设方面获得巨大成就的展览会。
