Title: Czechoslovak Mechanical Engineering Exhibition Opens
Author:
Date: 1956-09-10
Page: 5

    Czechoslovak Mechanical Engineering Exhibition Opens
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 9th. The second Czechoslovak Mechanical Engineering Exhibition opened in Brno on the afternoon of September 8th.
    This exhibition is even larger in scale than the first exhibition held here in September and October last year, with the exhibition area doubled to 86,000 square meters.
    More than 3,000 machines are on display at the exhibition, including complete sets of factory equipment, various machine tools, various heavy and light machines, aircraft, vehicles, agricultural machines and precision machines.
    The exhibition reflects the ever-changing achievements of the Czechoslovak mechanical industry. More than 300 new machines and equipment are on display at the exhibition alone. All new equipment or new parts on the machines were painted with eye-catching, special-colored paint, which attracted great attention from visitors.
    The exhibition will end at the end of September.

### 捷机械工业展览会开幕

1956-09-10
第5版()
专栏：

    捷机械工业展览会开幕
    新华社布拉格9日电　第二届捷克斯洛伐克机械工业展览会9月8日下午在布尔诺市开幕。
    这届展览会比去年9、10月间在这里举行的第一届展览会规模更为宏大，展览面积扩大了一倍，达到八万六千平方公尺。
    展览会上展出了三千多种机器，其中包括全套工厂设备、各种工作母机、各种重型和轻型机器、飞机、车辆、农业机器和精密机器等。
    展览会反映了捷克斯洛伐克机械工业的日新月异的成就。展览会上单是展出的新式机器和设备就有三百多件。所有机器上的新式设备或者新式部件都漆上了醒目的、有特殊颜色的油漆，受到了参观者的极大注意。
    展览会将在9月底结束。
