Title: Khrushchev Returns to the Soviet Union, Tito Makes Unofficial Visit to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 8

    Xinhua News Agency, Sevastopol Dispatch from TASS: The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, returned to the Soviet Union from Yugoslavia on the 27th at noon. Accompanying Khrushchev on an unofficial visit to the Soviet Union were Yugoslav President Tito and his wife, Vice President of the Federal Executive Council Ranković, Chairman of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Bosnia and Herzegovina, Pucăr, and their entourage.
    In the afternoon, Khrushchev and his guests went to the southern coast of Crimea where the guests will rest.

### 赫鲁晓夫回到苏联  铁托到苏联作非正式访问

1956-09-29
第8版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫回到苏联
    铁托到苏联作非正式访问
    据新华社28日讯　据塔斯社塞瓦斯托波尔讯：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫27日中午从南斯拉夫回到苏联。和赫鲁晓夫一道前来苏联作非正式访问的有：南斯拉夫总统铁托和他的夫人，联邦执行委员会副主席兰科维奇，波斯尼亚—黑塞哥维那人民共和国国民议会主席普察尔和他们的随行人员。
    下午，赫鲁晓夫和客人们前往客人们将要休息的地方克里米亚南岸。
