Title: Japanese Goods Exhibition to Open in Beijing in October; Head and Deputy Head of the Japanese Goods Exhibition Delegation Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd. The Japanese Goods Exhibition will open in early October at the Soviet Exhibition Center in Beijing, with approximately 50,000 exhibits. Most of the exhibits have already arrived and are currently being installed and arranged.

The Japanese Goods Exhibition is sponsored by the Japan International Trade Promotion Association based on the Third Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement signed in May 1955. The exhibition will showcase more than 15,000 products from over 1,000 Japanese manufacturers, including machine tools, agricultural machinery, textile machinery, printing machinery, small cars, motorcycles, medical machinery, chemical fertilizers, plastic products, hardware, and daily necessities. During the exhibition, a television transmitter and television station will be set up to broadcast the exhibition and cultural performances from some theaters in Beijing. The exhibition will also sell some Japanese goods.

The preparation work for the Japanese Goods Exhibition has received strong assistance and support from the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and other relevant parties. Staff from the Foreign Exhibitions Department of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade have fully participated in the preparation work for the exhibition and have assisted in hiring workers, engineers, and art staff from factories and enterprises in Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, and Changchun to install and arrange the exhibits.

The Japanese Goods Exhibition is scheduled to be exhibited in Beijing for three weeks and will then move to Shanghai.

Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd. The head of the Japanese Goods Exhibition Delegation, Eiichi Shukutani, and the deputy head, Shonai Morii, along with a group of three, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

Wen Shizhen, Deputy Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and Xie Qin, Director of the Foreign Exhibitions Department, were at the airport to welcome them.

Also at the airport to welcome them were Cao Zhongshu and Feng Tiecheng, the head and deputy head of the exhibition delegation that went to Tokyo and Osaka last year to host the Chinese Goods Exhibition, and Xiao Xiangqian.

Takashi Kataoka, Deputy Head of the Japanese Goods Exhibition Delegation, who arrived in Beijing earlier, also went to the airport to welcome them. (Photo attached)

The head of the Japanese Goods Exhibition Delegation, Eiichi Shukutani (first person on the right), and the deputy head, Shonai Morii (second person on the right), are seen leaving the airport accompanied by Wen Shizhen, Deputy Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade (left).

### 日本商品展览会十月在京开幕  日本商品展览团团长和副团长等到北京

1956-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    日本商品展览会十月在京开幕
    日本商品展览团团长和副团长等到北京
    新华社22日讯　日本商品展览会将于10月初在北京苏联展览馆开幕，展览品共有五万件左右。大部分展品都已运到，现在正在进行安装、布置。
    日本商品展览会是根据1955年5月签订的第三次中日贸易协定，由日本国际贸易促进协会主办的。展览会上将展出日本一千多家厂商出产的一万五千多种商品，包括机床、农业机械、纺织机械、印刷机械、小型汽车、机器脚踏车、医疗机械、化学肥料、塑胶制品、五金和日常生活用品。展览期间，将建立电视发射台和电视站，放映展览会展出情况和北京一些剧场的文艺演出。展览会还将出售一部分日本商品。
    日本商品展览会的筹备工作得到了中国国际贸易促进委员会和其他有关方面的大力协助和支持。中国国际贸易促进委员会国外来华展览部的工作人员全部参加了展出的筹备工作，并协助聘请了北京、上海、天津和长春等地工厂企业的工人、工程师和美术工作人员来安装、布置展览品。
    日本商品展览会预订在北京展出三个星期后，将转赴上海展出。
    新华社22日讯　日本商品展览团团长宿谷荣一、副团长森井庄内等一行三人已经在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    中国国际贸易促进委员会副秘书长文士桢、国外来华展览部部长谢钦等曾到机场欢迎。
    前往机场欢迎的，还有去年赴日本东京、大阪两地主持中国商品展览会的展览团正副团长曹中枢、冯铁城和萧向前。
    先期来到北京的日本商品展览团副团长片冈峻等也前往机场欢迎。（附图片）
    日本商品展览团团长宿谷荣一（右第一人），和副团长森井庄内（右第二人），在中国国际贸易促进委员会副秘书长文士桢（左）陪同下步出机场情形。
