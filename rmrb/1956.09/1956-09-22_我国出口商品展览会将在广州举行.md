Title: China's Export Commodities Fair to be Held in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1956-09-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 21st. China's first large-scale national export commodities fair will be held in Guangzhou on November 10th of this year.

    The fair is sponsored by the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade. The exhibition is scheduled to last for two months. The exhibition will set up five pavilions for industrial products, textiles, food, handicrafts, and native and special products. Here, a comprehensive introduction will be given to the varieties, specifications, and quality of China's rich export commodities; new products produced by China's industry and agriculture in recent years will also be exhibited.

    According to the person in charge of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade: During the exhibition, merchants from Asian and African countries and other countries, as well as compatriots from Hong Kong and Macao, are specially invited to visit and conduct transactions, in order to promote China's economic ties with Asian and African regions and other countries, and expand peaceful trade exchanges with various countries. At present, more than a dozen trade delegations are being organized by foreign trade corporations in various parts of China to go to Guangzhou, preparing to discuss trade and specific transactions with merchants from various countries and Hong Kong and Macao who come to visit.

    The Preparatory Committee for the China Export Commodities Fair has now been formed in Guangzhou and has begun its work.

### 我国出口商品展览会将在广州举行

1956-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我国出口商品展览会将在广州举行
    新华社21日讯　我国第一次规模宏大的全国性出口商品展览会，将于今年11月10日在广州市举行。
    这个展览会由中国国际贸易促进委员会主办。展出期间预定两个月。展览会将分别设立工业品、纺织品、食品、工艺品和土特产品等五个馆。在这里将全面地介绍我国丰富的出口商品的品种、规格和质量等情况；还将展出我国工农业近几年来生产出来的新产品。
    据中国国际贸易促进委员会负责人谈：展览会展出期间，特邀请亚非地区各国及其他各国商人和港澳同胞前来参观并进行交易，以促进我国与亚非地区和其他各国的经济联系，扩大与各国和平贸易的往来。现在我国各地对外贸易专业公司正在筹备组织十多个贸易代表团到广州，准备同各国和港澳前来参观的商人洽谈贸易和具体成交。
    中国出口商品展览会筹备委员会现已在广州组成，并已开始工作。
