Title: Zagreb International Fair: 620,000 Visitors Tour Chinese Pavilion
Author:
Date: 1956-09-24
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 23rd—The exhibits in the Chinese Pavilion at the Zagreb International Fair have been praised by business people from Yugoslavia and other countries.

　　During the entire fair, which ran from September 7th to 20th, approximately 620,000 people visited the Chinese Pavilion. The Chinese film pavilion also had over 20,000 visitors.

### 萨格勒布国际博览会  六十二万人参观中国馆

1956-09-24
第7版()
专栏：

    萨格勒布国际博览会
    六十二万人参观中国馆
    新华社布拉格23日电　萨格勒布国际博览会中国馆的展品受到了南斯拉夫和其他各国商业界人士的赞扬。
    在从9月7日到20日的整个博览会举行期间，大约有六十二万人参观了中国馆。中国电影馆的观众也在两万人以上。
