Title: Bulgarian Ballet Troupe Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1956-09-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th — The Bulgarian Ballet Troupe, led by Lyuben Tzakrov, concluded their performance tour in China and departed Beijing by plane this morning for their home country.

During their 40-plus day visit to China, the Bulgarian Ballet Troupe toured six major cities including Beijing, Tianjin, Dalian, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Guangzhou, giving 21 performances to an audience of 50,000.

On the 4th at noon, Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Culture, hosted a banquet to bid farewell to the Bulgarian artists. On the evening of the same day, the Bulgarian Embassy in China held a reception for the troupe's visit to China.

### 保加利亚芭蕾舞艺术团离京回国

1956-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚芭蕾舞艺术团离京回国
    新华社5日讯　保加利亚芭蕾舞艺术团团长柳宾·查克罗夫等一行十九人，结束了在中国的访问演出，在今天上午乘飞机离北京回国。
    保加利亚芭蕾舞艺术团在访问中国的四十多天中，旅行了中国的北京、天津、大连、上海、杭州、广州等六大城市，为五万观众演出了二十一场。
    4日中午，文化部副部长郑振铎设宴欢送保加利亚艺术家们。同日晚间，保加利亚驻华大使馆为艺术团访问中国举行了招待会。
