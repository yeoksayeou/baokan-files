Title: Learning from the Experience of Using Bamboo to Replace Steel Bars: Chinese Technicians Go to India
Author:
Date: 1956-09-03
Page: 5

    Learning from the Experience of Using Bamboo to Replace Steel Bars
    Chinese Technicians Go to India
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi. Three Chinese technicians dispatched to India to study India's experience in using bamboo to replace steel bars in building houses arrived in New Delhi on the evening of August 28th. They will stay in India for about three months.

### 学习用竹子代替钢筋的经验  我国技术人员去印度

1956-09-03
第5版()
专栏：

    学习用竹子代替钢筋的经验
    我国技术人员去印度
    新华社新德里电　中国派到印度去研究印度利用竹子代替钢筋修建房屋的经验的三个技术人员已经在8月28日晚抵达新德里。他们将在印度逗留约三个月。
