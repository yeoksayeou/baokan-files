Title: Canadian Parliamentarian Erling P. Fairbairn Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th – Erling P. Fairbairn, Liberal Member of Parliament of Canada and columnist for the Vancouver Sun, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 28th. He is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

He was welcomed at the airport by representatives from the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and the All-China Journalists Association.

### 加拿大议员艾尔英·费尔鲍特到京

1956-09-30
第3版()
专栏：

    加拿大议员艾尔英·费尔鲍特到京
    新华社29日讯　加拿大自由党众议员、温哥华太阳报专栏作家艾尔英·费尔鲍特28日下午乘飞机到达北京，他是应中国人民外交学会的邀请来中国访问的。
    他在机场上受到了中国人民外交学会和中国新闻工作者联谊会的代表的欢迎。
