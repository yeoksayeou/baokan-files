Title: International Airmail Postage Reduction
Author:
Date: 1956-09-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th — To adapt to the development of international postal communication services and simplify billing procedures, the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications has decided to reduce the rates for international airmail letters and international airmail postal forms starting from September 1st, and implement new billing methods. Airmail to all countries in the world, regardless of destination, will be charged a basic postage fee, plus an airmail fee of 30 fen per 10 grams for letters and postcards; 15 fen per 10 grams for printed matter, samples, small packages, trade documents, etc.; and 10 fen per 10 grams for newspapers and periodicals. This time, international airmail rates are generally lower than in the past, with the airmail rates for newspapers and periodicals being reduced the most.

	From the same date, airmail postal forms to all countries in the world will be fully available, with a total charge of 35 fen per item, including basic postage, regardless of destination.

### 国际航空函件邮资降价

1956-09-01
第2版()
专栏：

    国际航空函件邮资降价
    新华社30日讯　邮电部为了适应国际邮政通信业务的发展、简化计费手续，决定自9月1日起降低国际航空函件和国际航空邮简的资费，并实行新的计费办法。寄往世界各国的航空邮件不分国别除了交纳普通邮费外，信函每重十公分、明信片每件加收航空资费人民币三角；印刷品、货样、小包邮件、贸易契等每重十公分加收航空资费人民币一角五分；报纸期刊每重十公分加收航空资费人民币一角。这次国际航空资费比过去普遍降低，其中报纸期刊的航空资费降低最多。
    自同日起寄往世界各国的航空邮简全面开放，不分国别连同普通邮费在内每件共收三角五分。
