Title: Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1956-09-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 12th: The Indonesian parliamentary delegation, led by Kusnan, a member of the Indonesian Nationalist Party and Chairman of the Indonesian Parliament's Finance Committee, arrived in Guangzhou today. Xin ZhiChao, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, went to Shenzhen to meet them. The delegation was warmly welcomed at Guangzhou Railway Station by He Ximing, Deputy Governor of Guangdong Province, Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, and more than 30 representatives from all sectors of Guangdong and Guangzhou.

The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress. The delegation consists of twenty-three members of parliament and two secretaries.

### 印度尼西亚国会代表团到达广州

1956-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国会代表团到达广州
    新华社广州12日电　印度尼西亚国会访华代表团由印度尼西亚国民党议员、印度尼西亚国会财政小组主席库斯南率领，已经在今天到达广州。全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长辛志超曾往深圳迎接。代表团在广州车站上受到广东省副省长贺希明、广州市市长朱光以及广东省、广州市各界代表三十多人的热烈欢迎。
    代表团是应全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇的邀请来中国作友好访问的，代表团共有二十三位议员和两位秘书。
