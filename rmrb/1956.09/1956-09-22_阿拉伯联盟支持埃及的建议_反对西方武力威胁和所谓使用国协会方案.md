Title: Arab League Supports Egypt's Proposal, Opposes Western Threats of Force and the So-Called User Association Plan
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-22
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 19th (TASS): The Arab League Political Committee, at its meeting on September 18th, unanimously adopted a resolution stating that an attempt to seize the canal by exerting military pressure on Egypt and other Arab countries threatens the security and sovereignty of Arab nations.

    In the spirit of maintaining peace, Arab countries are prepared to take active steps to peacefully resolve this issue without violating Egypt's national sovereignty and the interests of the Arab people, and in accordance with the United Nations Charter; they resolutely defend their legitimate rights and repel aggression.

    The Political Committee resolved:

    1.  The solution proposed by the British Prime Minister regarding the establishment of an organization he calls the Suez Canal Users' Association is unacceptable because it violates the principles of the United Nations and results in undermining international peace.

    2.  Condemns the policy of threats and the use of political, economic, and military pressure employed by Britain and France to achieve their aims, the manner of which has caused anxiety among all Arab nations.

    3.  Supports Egypt's invitation to establish a negotiating body, requiring acceptance of the principle of negotiation and peaceful resolution of conflicts in accordance with the United Nations Charter.

    4.  Requests that all countries abide by the procedures stipulated in the United Nations Charter and utilize these procedures to seek a peaceful resolution.

    5.  Supports the note sent by the Egyptian government to the Security Council on September 17, 1956.

### 阿拉伯联盟支持埃及的建议  反对西方武力威胁和所谓使用国协会方案

1956-09-22
第6版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯联盟支持埃及的建议
    反对西方武力威胁和所谓使用国协会方案
    据新华社讯　塔斯社开罗19日电：阿拉伯联盟政治委员会9月18日会议一致通过一项决议说，一种想靠对埃及和其他阿拉伯国家施加军事压力来霸占运河的企图，威胁到阿拉伯国家的安全和主权。
    阿拉伯国家本着维护和平的精神准备采取积极步骤，采取在不违反埃及的国家主权和阿拉伯民族利益以及在符合联合国宪章的情况下和平解决这个问题；它们坚决维护自己的合法权利，击退侵略。
    政治委员会决议：
    一、英国首相提出的关于旨在建立一个他称为苏伊士运河使用国协会的组织的解决方案是不能接受的，因为它违背联合国的原则，而且其结果是破坏国际和平。
    二、谴责进行威胁以及采取政治、经济和军事压力的政策，因为英国和法国采取了这些措施来实现自己的目的，而它们实现目的的方式引起了所有阿拉伯国家的不安。
    三、支持埃及为了成立谈判机构所发出的邀请，要求接受谈判原则和根据联合国宪章和平解决冲突。
    四、要求各国遵守联合国宪章规定的程序，利用这些程序来求得和平解决。
    五、支持埃及政府在1956年9月17日送给安全理事会的照会。
