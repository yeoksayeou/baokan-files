Title: Britain and France Strengthen Military Preparations, French Troops Continue to Arrive in Cyprus
Author:
Date: 1956-09-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 5th: Britain and France are continuing intensive activities to strengthen their military preparations in the Eastern Mediterranean region. French Mediterranean Fleet Commander Vice Admiral Poël Barjot arrived in London on the 4th to meet with leaders of the British Admiralty.

French troops are steadily being dispatched to the island of Cyprus. According to an Associated Press report, unofficial estimates suggest that France is preparing to deploy approximately 5,000 troops to Cyprus, the majority of whom are paratroopers and air force personnel.

### 英法加强军事准备  法军继续开往塞浦路斯

1956-09-06
第6版()
专栏：

    英法加强军事准备
    法军继续开往塞浦路斯
    据新华社5日讯　英法两国继续进行紧张的活动，以加强东地中海地区的军事准备。法国地中海舰队司令波埃尔·巴尔若海军中将4日到达伦敦和英国海军部领袖们会谈。
    法国部队正在源源不断地开到塞浦路斯岛。美联社消息说，根据非官方估计，法国准备派驻塞浦路斯的部队人数大约为五千人，其中大多数是伞兵部队和空军人员。
