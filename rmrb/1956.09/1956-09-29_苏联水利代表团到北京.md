Title: Soviet Water Conservancy Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. The Soviet Water Conservancy Delegation, headed by Academician Askochensky, Deputy Minister of Agriculture of the Soviet Union and Director of the General Administration of Water Conservancy, has arrived in Beijing by plane between the 25th and 28th, except for one member who has not yet arrived.

This delegation is here to participate in the Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Exchange Conference on the Construction and Reconstruction of Irrigation Systems, which will be held in our country soon. Before the conference, they will also participate in the National Day celebrations and visit various places in our country.

When the delegation arrived in Beijing, Minister of Water Conservancy Fu Zuoyi and Vice Minister He Jifeng went to the airport to welcome them.

### 苏联水利代表团到北京

1956-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联水利代表团到北京
    新华社28日讯　以苏联农业部副部长兼水利总局局长阿斯科钦斯基院士为首的苏联水利代表团，除团员一人尚未到京外，其余十一人已在25日到28日先后乘飞机到达北京。
    这个代表团是来参加即将在我国举行的中苏灌溉系统新建改建科学技术交流会议的。在会议举行前，他们还将参加我国国庆节观礼并到各地参观。
    代表团到达北京的时候，水利部长傅作义、副部长何基灃等曾前往机场欢迎。
