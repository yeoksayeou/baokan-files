Title: CPPCC National Committee Hosts Banquet for Overseas Chinese Visiting Groups and Hong Kong and Macao Compatriot Visiting Groups
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-27
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th—The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held a banquet tonight to entertain Overseas Chinese visiting groups and Hong Kong and Macao compatriots visiting groups.

    Overseas Chinese who returned to China for sightseeing from Indonesia, Malaya, Vietnam, Burma, India, Europe, South Africa, South America, and compatriots from Hong Kong and Macao who came to Beijing for the National Day celebrations and sightseeing were all invited to attend the banquet. More than a thousand people attended the banquet tonight.

    Dong Biwu, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, extended a warm welcome at the banquet to the Overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macao compatriots who had traveled a long way to visit Beijing. In his speech, Dong Biwu also expressed his hope that after everyone returns, they will introduce the situation of the motherland and the cordial friendship of the compatriots of the motherland to the broad masses of Overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macao compatriots abroad, so as to further strengthen the great patriotic unity of Overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macao compatriots, and enhance the friendship between Overseas Chinese and the people of their countries of residence.

    Ye Cunxu, the head of the Indonesian Overseas Chinese visiting group, and Li Song, the representative of the Hong Kong and Macao compatriots visiting group, also spoke at the meeting one after another. They unanimously praised the great achievements made by the motherland in its construction endeavors and expressed their gratitude to the motherland for its care for Overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macao compatriots.

    Among those present at the banquet tonight were Shen Junru, He Xiangning, Li Weihan, Zhang Bojun, Bao Erhan, Vice Chairmen of the CPPCC National Committee, Xing Xiping, Secretary-General, responsible persons of various democratic parties, and people from all walks of life in the capital.

### 政协全国委员会举行宴会  招待华侨回国观光团和港澳同胞观光团

1956-09-27
第7版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会举行宴会
    招待华侨回国观光团和港澳同胞观光团
    新华社26日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会今晚举行宴会，招待华侨回国观光团和港澳同胞观光团。
    印度尼西亚、马来亚、越南、缅甸、印度、欧洲、南非、南美等处归国观光的华侨和港澳来北京观礼、观光的同胞都应邀出席了宴会。参加今晚宴会的共有一千余人。
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席董必武在宴会上对远道来到北京观光的归国华侨和港澳同胞表示热烈欢迎。董必武在讲话中还希望大家回去之后，把祖国的情况和祖国同胞的亲切情谊介绍给国外的广大侨胞和港澳同胞，以进一步加强国外侨胞以及港澳同胞的爱国大团结，并且增进国外侨胞和侨居国人民之间的友谊。
    印度尼西亚华侨回国观光团团长叶存胥、港澳同胞观光团代表李崧等都先后在会上讲了话。他们一致称赞祖国在建设事业方面所获得的伟大成就，并对祖国对海外侨胞和港澳同胞的关怀表示感谢。
    出席今晚宴会的有政协全国委员会副主席沈钧儒、何香凝、李维汉、章伯钧、鲍尔汉，秘书长邢西萍，各民主党派负责人以及首都各界人士等。
