Title: Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation Departs for Visit to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1956-09-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 8th. An Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation left Jakarta this morning for a twenty-five-day visit to the People's Republic of China. The delegation, led by Kusnan, is composed of twenty-three members of parliament.

Gan Yetao, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of our Embassy in Indonesia, held a banquet on the evening of September 7 to entertain all members of the delegation. The Speaker of the Indonesian Parliament, Sardono, and the Deputy Speaker, Achmad, also attended the banquet.

In their speeches, Gan Yetao and Sardono both said that the visit of the Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation to China will strengthen the friendly ties and understanding between the people of Indonesia and the people of China.

### 印度尼西亚国会代表团启程来我国访问

1956-09-09
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国会代表团启程来我国访问
    据新华社雅加达8日电　印度尼西亚国会代表团今天上午离开雅加达到中华人民共和国进行二十五天的访问。由库斯南率领的这个代表团是由二十三名议员组成的。
    我国驻印度尼西亚大使馆临时代办甘野陶在9月7日晚上举行了一次宴会招待代表团的全体团员。印度尼西亚国会议长沙多诺和副议长艾哈迈德也出席了宴会。
    甘野陶和沙多诺在他们的演说中都说，印度尼西亚国会代表团的访问中国将加强印度尼西亚人民和中国人民之间的友好联系和谅解。
