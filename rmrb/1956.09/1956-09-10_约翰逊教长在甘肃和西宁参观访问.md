Title: Archbishop Johnson Visits Gansu and Xining
Author:
Date: 1956-09-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Archbishop Johnson of Canterbury, along with his wife and two daughters, left Lanzhou by plane on the morning of the 7th. Those who went to the airport to see them off included Wu Hongbin, Chairman of the Lanzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Zhang Shiye, Director of the Reception Office of the Gansu Provincial People's Committee, and religious figures from Lanzhou. Archbishop Johnson's old friend, New Zealand peace activist Rewi Alley, also came to the airport to see them off.
Archbishop Johnson of Canterbury, accompanied by his wife and daughters, arrived in Gansu for a visit on August 25th by plane from Urumqi. During his stay in Gansu, Archbishop Johnson visited the famous Dunhuang Thousand Buddha Grottoes and the Jiayuguan Pass of the Great Wall of China.
Archbishop Johnson, along with his wife and daughters, arrived in Xining, Qinghai Province on the afternoon of September 3rd for a visit. After their visit in Xining, they left Xining on the 6th and returned to Lanzhou. In Xining, they visited the Chaoyang Agricultural Production Cooperative in the suburbs, Ta'er Monastery, the Great Mosque, and other places.

### 约翰逊教长在甘肃和西宁参观访问

1956-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    约翰逊教长在甘肃和西宁参观访问
    据新华社讯　英国坎特伯雷教长约翰逊和他的夫人及两个女儿7日晨乘飞机离开兰州。前往机场欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会兰州市分会主席吴鸿宾、甘肃省人民委员会交际处处长张士业和兰州市宗教界人士等，约翰逊教长的老朋友、新西兰和平人士艾黎也到机场欢送。
    坎特伯雷教长约翰逊偕夫人和女儿是在8月25日乘飞机离开乌鲁木齐到甘肃进行参观访问的。约翰逊教长在甘肃期间，参观了著名的敦煌千佛洞和中国万里长城的嘉峪关等。
    约翰逊教长偕夫人和女儿在9月3日下午到青海省西宁参观访问，他们在西宁参观访问以后，在6日离开西宁回到兰州。他们在西宁市参观了市郊的朝阳农业生产合作社、塔尔寺、清真大寺等。
