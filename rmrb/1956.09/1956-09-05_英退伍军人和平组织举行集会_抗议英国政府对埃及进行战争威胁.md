Title: British Veterans' Peace Organization Holds Rally Protesting British Government's War Threat Against Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-09-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, September 4th: According to TASS, the British Veterans for Peace Movement Association held a rally in Hyde Park on September 2nd to protest the government's war preparations regarding the Suez issue.

    Gordon Schaffer, Chairman of the British Peace Committee, spoke at the meeting. He sharply condemned the British government's actions. He said, "Anyone who has read the documents and speeches of the London Conference on the Suez Canal issue will see that these documents and speeches all emphasize the need for negotiations." Schaffer emphasized that the use of force against Egypt would meet with resolute opposition from world public opinion.

    Hawkes, representing the British Veterans for Peace Movement Association, said, "If the shareholders of the Canal Company want to protect their shares through military action, then let them go to war themselves."

    The conference passed a resolution demanding that Parliament immediately reconvene to discuss the Suez issue. Supporters of the conference marched through the West End of London, holding banners that read: "No War over Suez!"

### 英退伍军人和平组织举行集会  抗议英国政府对埃及进行战争威胁

1956-09-05
第6版()
专栏：

    英退伍军人和平组织举行集会
    抗议英国政府对埃及进行战争威胁
    据新华社4日讯　据塔斯社伦敦讯：英国退伍军人保卫和平运动协会9月2日在海德公园举行集会，抗议政府在苏彝士问题上所进行的战争准备。
    英国和平委员会主席戈登·夏菲尔在会上讲了话。他尖锐地谴责英国政府的这种作法。他说：“凡是读过关于苏彝士运河问题的伦敦会议的文件和演说的人，都会看到，这些文件和演说全都强调进行谈判的必要性。”夏菲尔强调说，如果对埃及使用武力，就会遇到世界公众的坚决反对。
    霍克斯代表英国退伍军人保卫和平运动协会讲话，他说：“如果运河公司的股东们想以军事行动来保护他们的股份，那就让他们自己去打仗吧。”
    大会通过一项决议，要求议会立即复会来讨论苏彝士问题。大会的支持者举着旗帜游行通过伦敦西区，旗帜上写着：“不许在苏彝士问题上发动战争！”
