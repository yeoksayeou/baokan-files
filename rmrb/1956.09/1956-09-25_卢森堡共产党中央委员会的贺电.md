Title: Telegram of Greetings from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Luxembourg

Author:

Date: 1956-09-25
Page: 3

To the Congress of the Communist Party of China, Esteemed Comrades:

    We sincerely thank you for inviting us to attend your Party Congress. We deeply regret that we cannot send a delegation because we must concentrate all our efforts on fighting the reactionaries' conspiracy to undermine the constitution and national sovereignty.

    We wholeheartedly care about your important meeting and congratulate you on achieving complete victory in your work.

    The great Chinese people have victoriously freed themselves from imperialist oppression and are freely and independently building a happy socialist future. The working people of Luxembourg are closely united with you.

    The working people of our country deeply feel the brotherly solidarity with the Communist Party of China, which is a shining example to everyone, having achieved such great success in ensuring the cause of peace and socialism.

    We believe that your congress will lay the foundation for new and greater achievements. Your victory decisively consolidates the development of peace and freedom throughout the world.

    Please accept the warmest respect from all progressive people in Luxembourg.

                Central Committee of the Communist Party of Luxembourg
                    General Secretary: Dominique Urbany

### 卢森堡共产党中央委员会的贺电

1956-09-25
第3版()
专栏：

    卢森堡共产党中央委员会的贺电致中国共产党代表大会敬爱的同志们：
    我们衷心地感谢你们邀请我们出席你们的党代表大会。我们十分遗憾，不能派遣一个代表团，因为我们要集中全部力量向反动派破坏宪法和国家主权的阴谋进行斗争。
    我们全心全意地在关怀你们这样重要的大会，并祝贺你们在工作中取得完全的胜利。
    伟大的中国人民已胜利地摆脱了帝国主义的压迫，并正在自由、独立地建设幸福的社会主义将来，卢森堡的劳动人民和你们紧密地团结在一起。
    我国的劳动人民深感和中国共产党兄弟般地团结一致，中国共产党对大家是一个光辉榜样，它在保证和平与社会主义事业上已经获得了这样巨大的成就。
    我们相信，你们的大会将为新的、更大的成就奠定基础。你们的胜利决定性地巩固着全世界的和平自由的发展。
    请接受卢森堡全体进步人士最热忱的敬意。
                卢森堡共产党中央委员会
                    总书记　多·欧班尼
