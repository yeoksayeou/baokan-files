Title: China Turns Over Bodies of Two U.S. Military Personnel to British Representative in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1956-09-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, September 12th: The Foreign Affairs Office of the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee today handed over two bodies of U.S. military personnel and their belongings to officials of the British Representative Office in China for forwarding to the United States.

The two bodies were discovered by relevant departments in Zhejiang Province on the beaches of two islands in the Zhoushan Archipelago on August 31st and September 4th, respectively. Based on the records on the cards in the pockets of the deceased's military uniforms, it was determined that they were the bodies of U.S. military personnel. Their names were William F. Hoskins and Jack A. Curtis.

After learning of the discovery of the above-mentioned U.S. military bodies, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China notified the British Representative in China on September 3rd and 11th, asking him to inform the U.S. government.

### 我国将两美军人员尸体交英代办处在上海官员

1956-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我国将两美军人员尸体交英代办处在上海官员
    新华社上海十二日电　上海市人民委员会外事处今天将美军人员尸体两具和遗物移交给英国驻华代办处在上海的官员转交美国方面。
    这两具尸体是8月31日和9月4日由浙江有关部门先后在舟山群岛中两个岛屿的海滩上发现的。根据死者军服口袋的卡片的记载，判定是美军人员尸体。他们的姓名是威兼·弗·赫斯金斯和杰克·阿·克提斯。
    中华人民共和国外交部在知悉发现上述美军尸体后，就在9月3日和11日把有关情况通知英国驻华代办，请他转告美国政府。
