Title: Indonesia Strengthens Economic Independence: Dutch-Operated Deli Railway Company to Be Nationalized
Author:
Date: 1956-09-28
Page: 7

Indonesia Strengthens Economic Independence
Dutch-Operated Deli Railway Company to Be Nationalized
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 27th: Indonesia will nationalize the Dutch-owned Deli Railway Company in North Sumatra.

Minister of Transportation Tedjasukmana said on September 25th while giving a speech in Padang that the Ministry of Transportation is planning to establish a supervisory committee to manage the 600-kilometer-long railway before nationalization.

Tedjasukmana said that the amount of compensation for nationalization has not yet been decided.

He also announced that, according to the Five-Year Plan, the Ministry of Transportation has allocated 600 million rupiahs (approximately equivalent to 55 million US dollars) for railway repairs and the purchase of new vehicles.

### 印度尼西亚加强经济独立  将把荷兰经营的日里铁路公司收归国有

1956-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚加强经济独立
    将把荷兰经营的日里铁路公司收归国有
    新华社雅加达27日电　印度尼西亚将把荷兰所有的北苏门答腊日里铁路公司收归国有。
    交通部长特贾苏克马纳9月25日在巴东发表演说的时候说，交通部正在计划成立一个监督委员会，以便在把这条全长六百公里的铁路收归国有以前进行管理。
    特贾苏克马纳说，收归国有的补偿费用的数额目前还没有决定。
    他还宣布，根据五年计划，交通部已经拨出六亿盾（大约折合五千五百万美元）的款项来供修复铁路和购买新的车辆之用。
