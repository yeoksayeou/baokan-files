Title: Boyd-Orr to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, London, September 26th. Lord Boyd-Orr, Chairman of the British Council for the Promotion of International Trade, left London by plane for Beijing on September 25th. Lord Boyd-Orr told reporters that he intends to discuss the prospects for expanding British-Chinese trade during his visit to China.

### 波伊德—奥尔来京

1956-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    波伊德—奥尔来京
    新华社伦敦26日电　英国国际贸易促进委员会主席波伊德—奥尔勋爵9月25日乘飞机离伦敦前往北京。波伊德—奥尔勋爵对记者说，他打算在访问中国期间商讨扩大英中贸易的远景问题。
