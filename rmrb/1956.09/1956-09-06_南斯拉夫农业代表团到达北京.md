Title: Yugoslav Agricultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 5th. The Yugoslav agricultural delegation, invited to visit China and led by Jože Vrhovec, member of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia and member of the Executive Council of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

    Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Deng Zihui, Vice Premier of the State Council, Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture, Cai Ziwei, Vice Minister, He Jifeng, Vice Minister of Water Resources, and representatives from the Ministry of Land Reclamation. The Yugoslav Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Mirićević, and others also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 南斯拉夫农业代表团到达北京

1956-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫农业代表团到达北京
    据新华社5日讯　应邀来我国访问的南斯拉夫农业代表团，由团长、南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会委员、南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国执行委员会委员约·维塞利诺夫率领，在今天乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的，有国务院副总理邓子恢、农业部长廖鲁言、副部长蔡子伟和水利部副部长何基沣以及农垦部的代表等多人。南斯拉夫驻中国临时代办米里切维奇等也前往机场欢迎。
