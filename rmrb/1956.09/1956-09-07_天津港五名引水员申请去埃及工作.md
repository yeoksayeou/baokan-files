Title: Five Tianjin Port Pilots Apply to Work in Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-09-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, September 6th. In recent days, five pilots from Tianjin Port have applied to work in the Suez Canal in Egypt.

These pilots all have more than 20 years of rich maritime experience. They have been working as pilots in the narrow and winding Tianjin Port channel, and have always ensured the safe navigation of ships for several years. Among them, Cai Yannan once served as a captain for four years and a pilot for eight years. Shi Xueliang has 22 years of maritime experience. Because of his excellent piloting skills and conscientiousness, he was elected as a model worker in Tianjin. When applying to go to Egypt, he said: "I am applying to go to Egypt to support the just struggle of the Egyptian people."

### 天津港五名引水员申请去埃及工作

1956-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    天津港五名引水员申请去埃及工作
    新华社天津6日电　最近几天，天津港有五名引水员申请前往埃及苏彝士运河工作。
    这些引水员都有二十多年丰富的航海经验，他们在狭窄、曲折的天津港航道担任领航工作，几年来始终保证了船只的安全航行，其中蔡燕南曾经担任过四年船长，八年引水员。施学良有二十二年航海经验，因为他领航技术优良，认真负责，曾当选为天津市的劳动模范。他在申请去埃及时说：“我申请去埃及是为了支援埃及人民的正义斗争。”
