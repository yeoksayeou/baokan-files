Title: Pakistan's New Prime Minister Discusses Domestic and Foreign Policy, Expressing Hope to Maintain Friendly Relations with All Countries
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, September 13th: Pakistan's new Prime Minister, Huseyn Shaheed Suhrawardy, announced the basic principles of his foreign and domestic policies in a radio address on Radio Pakistan on the evening of September 12th.

Suhrawardy said: "We earnestly hope to maintain friendly relations with all countries, especially with our neighbors and our Muslim brothers. I will take friendliness towards all countries and ill-will towards none as the principle of our foreign policy."

He said: "I will seek the cooperation of all countries, and we will make a full contribution to maintaining peace. We must abide by our promises and agreements."

Suhrawardy said: "I do not want to be made a pawn on the international political stage."

The new Prime Minister said that, however, to be respected by other countries, we must rectify our internal affairs.

Suhrawardy said: "We must have a clean government and clean politics." He also said, "General elections must be held as soon as possible on the basis of universal adult suffrage, and the general elections must be absolutely free and fair."

Referring to the Kashmir issue, Suhrawardy said: "This is such a sore spot, and it has damaged our relations with India to such an extent that we cannot use all our resources to promote the happiness of our two peoples. How wonderful it would be if this problem could be resolved and we and India could cooperate amicably and move forward together."

### 巴基斯坦新总理谈内外政策  表示希望同一切国家保持友好关系

1956-09-14
第5版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦新总理谈内外政策
    表示希望同一切国家保持友好关系
    据新华社喀喇蚩13日电　巴基斯坦新总理苏拉瓦底9月12日晚上在巴基斯坦电台发表的广播演说中宣布了他的外交和内政政策方面的基本原则。
    苏拉瓦底说：“我们热切希望同一切国家，主要是同我们的邻国以及我们的穆斯林兄弟保持友好关系。我将把对所有国家都友好和不对任何国家存有恶意作为我们的外交政策的原则。”
    他说：“我将寻求一切国家的合作，我们将对维护和平作出充分的贡献。我们必须遵守我们的诺言和协议。”
    苏拉瓦底说：“我不愿被人弄成国际政治舞台上的一名走卒。”
    这位新总理说，但是想受到其他国家的尊敬，我们必须整顿我们的内部。
    苏拉瓦底说：“我们必须有一个廉洁的政府和廉洁的政治。”他又说，“必须尽快地在成年人都有投票权的基础上举行普选，普选必须绝对自由和光明正大。”
    在谈到克什米尔问题时，苏拉瓦底说：“这是这样一个痛处，并且这使我们同印度的关系坏到这样的程度，以致我们不能把我们所有的资源用来促进我们两国人民的幸福。如果这个问题能够得到解决而我们和印度能够进行友好合作和一同前进，那是多么好的事。”
