Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Postponed
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 19th - The Chinese and American ambassadors have agreed to postpone the talks originally scheduled for September 20th to September 22nd.

### 中美大使会谈延期举行

1956-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使会谈延期举行
    新华社日内瓦19日电　中美两国大使已经同意把原定在9月20日举行的会谈，延期到9月22日举行。
