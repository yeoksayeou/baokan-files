Title: Khrushchev to Visit Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1956-09-19
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 18th. TASS reports: It is understood that Khrushchev will visit Yugoslavia in a private capacity on September 19th.

	Khrushchev will spend a few days there on vacation.

### 赫鲁晓夫将访问南斯拉夫

1956-09-19
第8版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫将访问南斯拉夫
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：据悉，赫鲁晓夫将在9月19日以私人身份到南斯拉夫访问。
    赫鲁晓夫将在那里度假休养几天。
