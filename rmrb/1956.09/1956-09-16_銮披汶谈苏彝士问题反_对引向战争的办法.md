Title: Phibunsongkhram on the Suez Issue Opposes Methods Leading to War
Author:
Date: 1956-09-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, September 15th: Thai Prime Minister Phibunsongkhram said on September 14th that Thailand opposes any solution to the Suez Canal crisis that might lead to war.

### 銮披汶谈苏彝士问题反  对引向战争的办法

1956-09-16
第6版()
专栏：

    銮披汶谈苏彝士问题反
    对引向战争的办法
    据新华社15日讯　曼谷消息：泰国总理銮披汶9月14日说，泰国反对任何可能引向战争的关于苏彝士运河危机的解决办法。
