Title: Chairman Mao Appoints Yu Qiuli as Minister of Finance of the People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1956-09-01
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, according to the decision of the 44th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, appointed Yu Qiuli as Minister of Finance of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on the 29th.

### 毛主席任命余秋里为解放军财务部部长

1956-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    毛主席任命余秋里为解放军财务部部长
    新华社29日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第四十四次会议的决定，在29日任命余秋里为中国人民解放军财务部部长。
