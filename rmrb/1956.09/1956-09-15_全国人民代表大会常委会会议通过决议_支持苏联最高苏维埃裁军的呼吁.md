Title: Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Passes Resolution Supporting the Soviet Supreme Soviet's Call for Disarmament
Author:
Date: 1956-09-15
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 14th. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its forty-fifth meeting this morning.

    The meeting discussed the Soviet Supreme Soviet's appeal to parliaments of all countries on the issue of disarmament and passed a resolution supporting the Soviet Supreme Soviet's appeal to parliaments of all countries on disarmament.

    The meeting heard and approved the summary report on the visit to Tibet by Chen Yi, head of the Central Delegation and Vice Premier of the State Council.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会会议通过决议  支持苏联最高苏维埃裁军的呼吁

1956-09-15
第2版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会会议通过决议
    支持苏联最高苏维埃裁军的呼吁
    新华社14日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会今天上午举行第四十五次会议。
    会议讨论了苏联最高苏维埃向各国议会呼吁裁军的问题，通过了关于支持苏联最高苏维埃向各国议会呼吁裁军的决议。
    会议听取并且批准了中央代表团团长、国务院副总理陈毅关于访问西藏的总结报告。
