Title: Spanish Communist Party Central Committee Plenary Session Elects New Political Bureau
Author:
Date: 1956-09-03
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, September 2nd. "L'Humanité" published a communiqué from the Central Committee of the Spanish Communist Party on September 1st. The communiqué stated that the Central Committee held a plenary session at the end of August, during which it discussed the party's policy of promoting harmony among Spaniards and elected a new Political Bureau.

The plenary session, based on the report by the party's General Secretary, Ibárruri, passed a resolution expressing its approval of the report and the Central Committee's political line. Following Ibárruri's suggestion, the plenary session decided to write a letter to the leadership committee of the Spanish Socialist Workers' Party.

The plenary session also passed a resolution on the issue of opposing the cult of personality and on other issues of ideologically and organizationally consolidating the party.

The plenary session elected a new Political Bureau, its members are as follows: Álvarez, Carrillo, Claudin, Delicado, Gallego, Ibárruri, Líster, Mije, Santis, Sánchez, and Uribe.

Elected as alternate members of the Political Bureau were Homes, Marín, and Sapiain (i.e., Sergio).

### 西共中央全会选出新的政治局

1956-09-03
第6版()
专栏：

    西共中央全会选出新的政治局
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社巴黎讯：“人道报”在9月1日刊载了西班牙共产党中央委员会的公报。公报说，中央委员会在8月底举行了全体会议，会上讨论了党促进西班牙人之间和睦的政策，并且选出了新的政治局。
    全体会议根据党的总书记伊巴露丽的报告通过决议，表示赞同这个报告和中央委员会的政治路线。根据伊巴露丽的建议，全体会议决定写信给西班牙社会工人党领导委员会。
    全体会议还就反对个人崇拜的问题以及在思想上和组织上巩固党的其他问题通过了一项决议。
    全体会议选出了新的政治局，它的成员如下：阿尔瓦雷斯、卡里洛、克劳丁、德利卡多、加尔埃果、伊巴露丽、利斯特尔、米赫、圣桑、桑切斯和乌里贝。
    当选为政治局候补委员的有霍姆斯、马林和萨皮腊恩（即赛尔吉奥）。
