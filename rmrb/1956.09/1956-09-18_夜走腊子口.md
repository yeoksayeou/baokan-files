Title: Night Walk Through Lazikou
Author: Xiao Yingjun
Date: 1956-09-18
Page: 8

    Night Walk Through Lazikou
  　Xiao Yingjun
    Ancient pines cover the stone path,
    Cliffs obscure the night sky.
    Stars twinkle in the pine branches,
    Night mist is hazy on the mountainside.
    I embrace these lush ancient pines,
    They once tethered the Red Army's warhorses.
    I caress these rugged, grotesque rocks,
    They once sheltered the Red Army's charge.
    Our predecessors used steadfast steps,
    To traverse the fiery, bloody road.
    I tread in the footsteps of my predecessors,
    Following behind with the stride of a soldier.
    The night wind blows fiercely through the pine needles,
    Like ten thousand horses galloping.
    Far away on the high mountains,
    The exploration team's red light shines.
        1955.11, in Luoda, Gannan

### 夜走腊子口
萧英俊
1956-09-18
第8版()
专栏：

    夜走腊子口
  　萧英俊
    古松复盖着石道，
    悬崖遮住了夜空。
    松枝里闪烁着星点，
    夜雾在山腰一片朦胧。
    我拥抱这苍郁的古松，
    它曾拴过红军的战马。
    我抚摸这嶙峋的怪石，
    它曾掩蔽过红军冲锋。
    前辈用坚韧的步伐，
    走过了血火的道路。
    我踏着前辈的脚印，
    用战士的步伐跟在后头。
    夜风猛吹松梢，
    仿佛万马奔腾。
    远处高高山上，
    亮着勘探队的红灯。
        1955、11，于甘南罗达
