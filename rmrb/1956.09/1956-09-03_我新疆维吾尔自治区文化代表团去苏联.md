Title: Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Cultural Delegation to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-09-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, September 2nd. The cultural delegation of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region of the People's Republic of China, headed by Ziya Samet, left Urumqi by plane today for a visit to the Soviet Union. The delegation is visiting the Soviet Union in accordance with the 1956 implementation plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement signed in Moscow on July 13 this year.

    The delegation includes the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Song and Dance Troupe, writers, poets, painters, and cultural and artistic workers of various ethnic groups. The delegation consists of fifty people, who will visit the Kazakh, Kyrgyz, and Uzbek Republics in the Soviet Union for two months, and will extensively exchange experiences in culture and art. The song and dance troupe has also prepared more than fifty folk song and dance programs from various ethnic groups in Xinjiang.

### 我新疆维吾尔自治区文化代表团去苏联

1956-09-03
第5版()
专栏：

    我新疆维吾尔自治区文化代表团去苏联
    新华社乌鲁木齐2日电　以孜牙·赛买提为首的中华人民共和国新疆维吾尔自治区访苏文化代表团，今天从乌鲁木齐乘飞机前往苏联访问。代表团是根据今年7月13日在莫斯科签订的中苏文化合作协定1956年执行计划去苏联访问的。
    这个代表团包括新疆维吾尔自治区歌舞团，各民族的作家、诗人、画家和文化艺术工作者。代表团共五十人，他们将在苏联哈萨克、吉尔吉斯和乌兹别克等共和国进行为期两个月的访问活动，并将在文化艺术方面广泛地交流经验。歌舞团还准备了五十多个新疆各民族的民间歌舞节目。
