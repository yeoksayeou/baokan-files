Title: Sihanouk Re-Declares Cambodia Will Strictly Adhere to a Neutral Policy
Author:
Date: 1956-09-24
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 22nd – According to a report from the Vietnam News Agency: Cambodian Prime Minister Sihanouk, when the National Assembly approved the new cabinet, addressed the Assembly and reaffirmed Cambodia's strict adherence to neutrality and the implementation of a policy of peaceful coexistence based on the Five Principles proposed by China and India.

He said: "The new government will issue a statement clearly clarifying and formally announcing the policy of neutrality, and will require Cambodian people at home and abroad to abide by this policy, and take a firm attitude towards those who undermine neutrality."

On domestic affairs, Sihanouk proposed some specific policies aimed at unifying government agencies, promoting the implementation of national construction plans, uniting all levels of Cambodian people and political parties, and safeguarding the policy of neutrality.

### 西哈努克再一次宣布  柬埔寨将严格遵守中立政策

1956-09-24
第7版()
专栏：

    西哈努克再一次宣布
    柬埔寨将严格遵守中立政策
    新华社河内22日电　据越南通讯社讯：柬埔寨新内阁首相西哈努克在国民议会批准新内阁的时候向议会发表演说，重新肯定了柬埔寨的严格遵守中立以及实行以中国和印度提出的五项原则为基础的和平共处政策。
    他说：“新政府将发表公告，明确阐明和正式宣布中立政策，并且要求国内外的柬埔寨人民遵守这个政策，对中立的破坏者采取坚决的态度。”
    在内政上，西哈努克提出了一些具体的政策，其目的在于统一政府各机构，促进国家建设计划的实现，团结柬埔寨各阶层人民和政党，以及维护中立政策。
