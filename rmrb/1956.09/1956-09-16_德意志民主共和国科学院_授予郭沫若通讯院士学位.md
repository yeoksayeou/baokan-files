Title: Academy of Sciences of the German Democratic Republic Awards Guo Moruo the Title of Corresponding Academician
Author:
Date: 1956-09-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The Academy of Sciences of the German Democratic Republic has awarded Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the title of Corresponding Academician of the Academy of Sciences of the German Democratic Republic.

    The awarding ceremony was held this morning at the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China. Steinmitz, Vice President of the Academy of Sciences of the German Democratic Republic, personally presented the certificate of Corresponding Academician to Guo Moruo.

    Steinmitz and Guo Moruo delivered speeches successively. Steinmitz said that awarding the outstanding Chinese scientist Guo Moruo as a Corresponding Academician of the Academy of Sciences of the German Democratic Republic is the unanimous wish of German scientists. He believes that this is of great significance to the scientists of Germany and China and the people of both countries. In his speech, Guo Moruo said that it was a great honor for him to be awarded the title of Corresponding Academician of the Academy of Sciences of the German Democratic Republic. He expressed his willingness to do his best to bring Chinese and German scientists into closer cooperation and jointly contribute to a better cause.

    Among those who attended today's ceremony were Kippner, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, and all the representatives of the delegation of the Academy of Sciences of the German Democratic Republic; from the Chinese side were Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Yan Jici, Director of the Department of Technical Sciences, Pan Zinian, Director of the Institute of Philosophy, Zhang Wenyu, Deputy Director of the Institute of Geology, and Lü Shuxiang, Deputy Director of the Institute of Linguistics.

### 德意志民主共和国科学院  授予郭沫若通讯院士学位

1956-09-16
第3版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国科学院  授予郭沫若通讯院士学位
    新华社15日讯　德意志民主共和国科学院授予中国科学院院长郭沫若以德意志民主共和国科学院通讯院士学位。
    授予仪式是今天上午在德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆举行的。德意志民主共和国科学院副院长施太尼次把通讯院士证书亲手交给郭沫若。
    施太尼次和郭沫若相继致词。施太尼次说，授予杰出的中国科学家郭沫若为德意志民主共和国科学院通讯院士，是德国科学家们一致的愿望。他相信这对德中两国的科学家和两国人民都有很大的意义。郭沫若在致词时说，授予他为德意志民主共和国科学院通讯院士是他极大的荣誉。他表示愿意尽最大的努力来使中德两国科学家更加密切合作，共同为美好的事业而贡献力量。
    参加今天仪式的，德国方面有德意志民主共和国驻华大使纪普纳和德意志民主共和国科学院代表团全体代表；中国方面有中国科学院副院长吴有训，技术科学部主任严济慈，哲学研究所所长潘梓年，地质研究所副所长张文佑和语言研究所副所长吕叔湘等。
