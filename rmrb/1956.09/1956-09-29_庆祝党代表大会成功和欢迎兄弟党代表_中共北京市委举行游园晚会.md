Title: Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China Holds Garden Party to Celebrate the Success of the Party Congress and Welcome Representatives of Fraternal Parties
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: The Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China held a garden party this evening to celebrate the success of the Party's Eighth National Congress and to welcome representatives of the Communist and Workers' Parties of various countries who attended the Congress.

More than 10,000 people attended the garden party, including representatives of the Communist and Workers' Parties from more than 50 countries, representatives of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China, members and alternate members of the Eighth Central Committee, representatives of China's democratic parties and non-party democrats, all staff members of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China, and Communists from Beijing.

At 7:30 p.m., the guests arrived one after another at Zhongshan Park, which was full of ancient cypress trees, beautiful scenery, and brilliant lights. At this time, people constantly applauded warmly to express their welcome.

Chinese Communist Party leaders Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun and other comrades came here to celebrate the first evening after the successful conclusion of the Party's Eighth National Congress with everyone.

At the beginning of the garden party, Peng Zhen, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, delivered a welcome speech to the guests. He said that all the Communist Party members and all the people of Beijing, like all the members of the Communist Party of China and all the people of China, saw not only the great prospects of socialist construction in our motherland, and the solid unity and great strength of the Communist Party of China, but also the solid unity and great strength of the international working class from the Party's Eighth National Congress. We express our warm congratulations on these.

He said, comrades, representatives of fraternal parties from various countries! You have come from afar to attend our Party Congress despite your hard work, which is a great support for our Party and our people. We would like to express our deep gratitude and high respect to you. We will respond to your support by intensifying our work and achieving greater victories. Peng Zhen finally extended a warm welcome and high respect to all comrades and friends who came to the garden party, and wished them good health.

The garden party began. The leaders of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, together with the representatives of the Communist and Workers' Parties from various countries, first appreciated the lion dance and acrobatics performed by the China Acrobatic Troupe at the Altar of Land and Grain, which was illuminated by thousands of lights.

After that, the representatives of the Communist and Workers' Parties from various countries went to various parts of the park according to their own preferences to enjoy various artistic performances. Some of them were enjoying opera programs sung by famous opera actors, some gathered outside the Tanximen to watch the song and dance performances by the Central Experimental Opera House and the Beijing Dance School, some were enjoying the folk instrumental ensemble of the Central People's Broadcasting Station Orchestra under the West Tree Forest, some were watching the wonderful performances of the Central National Song and Dance Ensemble at Tannamen, some were watching the Peking Opera performed by the Beijing Opera School at Lai Jin Yu Xuan, shadow puppet enthusiasts were watching the performance of the Deshun Shadow Puppet Troupe, and many guests walked into the crowded game stations to play various interesting games.

In Zhongshan Park, which was full of friendship, unity, joy and pleasure, there were beautiful songs everywhere, and there were friendly gatherings everywhere. Many representatives of the Communist and Workers' Parties from various countries danced to the melodious music together with the Communist Party members from various agencies, factories, and schools in Beijing; they sang the "Internationale" and songs from China and other countries in various languages.

The night was deep, but people still showed no sign of fatigue. It was not until half past eleven that the guests happily bid farewell to their hosts.

### 庆祝党代表大会成功和欢迎兄弟党代表  中共北京市委举行游园晚会

1956-09-29
第2版()
专栏：

    庆祝党代表大会成功和欢迎兄弟党代表
    中共北京市委举行游园晚会
    新华社28日讯　中国共产党北京市委员会，今晚举行游园晚会，庆祝党的第八次全国代表大会的成功和欢迎参加这次大会的各国共产党和工人党的代表。
    参加游园晚会的有五十多个国家的共产党和工人党的代表，中国共产党第八次全国代表大会的代表和八届中央委员会委员、候补中央委员，我国各民主党派和无党派民主人士的代表，中国共产党第八次全国代表大会全体工作人员，以及北京市的共产党员等共一万多人。
    七时半，客人们陆续来到古柏参天、景色秀丽、灯火辉煌的中山公园。这时，人们不断地热烈鼓掌向他们表示欢迎。
    中国共产党的领导人毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、陈云等同志来到这里和大家一起欢度党的第八次全国代表大会胜利闭幕后的第一个晚上。
    游园会开始的时候，中共北京市委书记彭真向客人们致欢迎词。他说，我们北京市的全体共产党员和全体人民，同中国共产党全体党员和全中国人民一样，从党的第八次全国代表大会上，不但看到了我们祖国的社会主义建设的伟大远景，看到了中国共产党的坚固的团结和伟大的力量，而且看到了国际工人阶级的坚固的团结和伟大的力量。对于这些，我们表示热烈的庆祝。
    他说，各国兄弟党的代表同志们！你们不辞劳苦，远道来参加我们党的代表大会，是对于我们党和我国人民的极大的支持。我们谨向你们表示深深的感谢和崇高的敬意。我们将用加紧工作、取得更大的胜利，来回答你们的支持。彭真最后向来游园的所有同志们、朋友们表示热烈的欢迎和崇高的敬意。并祝他们的身体健康。
    游园晚会开始了。中共中央领导人同来自各国的共产党和工人党的代表，首先在万盏灯火的社稷坛欣赏了中国杂技团表演的狮子舞和杂技。
    接着，来自各国的共产党和工人党的代表们按照自己的爱好，分别到公园的各处，欣赏各种艺术表演。他们有的在欣赏著名戏曲演员演唱的戏曲节目，有的聚集在坛西门外观看中央实验歌剧院和北京舞蹈学校演出的歌舞，有的在西树林下欣赏中央人民广播电台管弦乐团的民间乐器合奏，有的在坛南门观看中央民族歌舞团的精采演出，有的在来今雨轩看北京戏曲学校表演的京剧，皮影戏的爱好者在观看德顺皮影剧社的表演，还有许多客人走进人群拥挤的游戏站玩着各种有趣的游戏。
    在充满着友谊、团结、欢乐和愉快的中山公园里，到处有优美的歌声，到处是友谊的联欢，各国共产党和工人党的许多代表同北京各机关、工厂、学校的共产党员们在一起随着悠扬的乐曲尽情地跳舞；他们用各种不同的语言唱“国际歌”和中国及其他国家的歌曲。
    夜已深沉，人们仍毫无倦容。直到十一时半，客人才与主人尽欢告别。
