Title: Grand Gathering in Beijing Welcomes Prime Minister Acharya
Author:
Date: 1956-09-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th -- Prime Minister Tanka Prasad Acharya of the Kingdom of Nepal and the distinguished Nepalese guests accompanying him on their visit to China, attended a welcoming rally held this afternoon by 8,000 people from all walks of life in Beijing.

Prime Minister Acharya and his wife, along with other Nepalese guests, were accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, Mayor Peng Zhen and his wife, as they entered the venue and ascended the rostrum at 2 p.m. The band played a welcoming tune, and the entire audience stood up and applauded warmly for a long time to welcome the distinguished guests. A group of female students presented them with flowers.

Mayor Peng Zhen delivered a welcome speech, saying that since the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Nepal and the determination to use the famous Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence as the basic principles guiding the relations between the two countries, the friendly relations between the two countries have been redeveloped on a new basis. Through this visit by the distinguished Nepalese guests, the friendly relations between our two countries will be further developed.

Prime Minister Acharya declared to the welcoming masses at today's meeting that the Chinese people will forever receive the sympathy of the Nepalese people and himself in all aspects. His enthusiastic words aroused a storm of applause in the hall.

In his speech, Prime Minister Acharya expressed that upon returning to his country, he would tell the Nepalese people that all their work for the benefit of the motherland in political, economic, social and cultural aspects would receive the sympathy and encouragement of the Chinese people. He said that when the Nepalese people know that the great Chinese people are sympathetically paying attention to their sincere efforts, they will feel infinitely encouraged. The Prime Minister's speech was warmly welcomed.

Mayor Peng Zhen also presented a silk banner on behalf of Beijing Municipality to Prime Minister Acharya and presented gifts to the Prime Minister. During the presentation of the banner and gifts, the hall was filled with joy and applause.

Today's meeting was held in the competition hall of the Beijing Gymnasium. The national flags of Nepal and China fluttered in the wind in front of the venue, and slogans written in gold characters on a red background were hung high in the hall, reading: "Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Nepalese people" and "Long live Asian-African and world peace." Vice Premiers He Long, Chen Yi, and Ulanhu, Vice Chairman Chen Shutong, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Chinese Ambassador to Nepal Pan Zili and his wife, President of the China-Nepal Friendship Association Zhou Jianren, and heads of various departments and people's organizations of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee attended the meeting.

At the welcoming rally, Chinese artists performed music, dance and acrobatics. After the meeting, Prime Minister Acharya and others visited the gymnasium's training hall and swimming pool.

### 北京各界盛会欢迎阿查里雅首相

1956-09-30
第2版()
专栏：

    北京各界盛会欢迎阿查里雅首相
    据新华社29日讯　尼泊尔王国首相坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅和同他一起来中国访问的尼泊尔贵宾们，今天下午出席了北京市各界人民八千人举行的欢迎大会。
    阿查里雅首相和夫人及其他的尼泊尔贵宾们由周恩来总理、彭真市长和夫人陪同，在下午2时进入会场登上主席台。乐队奏起了欢迎曲，场内全体起立，长久地热烈鼓掌来欢迎贵宾们。一群女学生向他们献了花。
    彭真市长首先致欢迎词，他说，中尼两国自从建立外交关系并且确定以著名的和平共处五项原则作为指导两国关系的基本原则以来，两国的友好关系在新的基础上重新得到了发展。通过尼泊尔贵宾们这次访问，我们两国的友好关系将会获得进一步的发展。
    阿查里雅首相在今天的大会上向欢迎群众宣称，中国人民一定会在各方面永远地得到尼泊尔人民和他本人的同情。他的热情洋溢的话语在会场内激起了暴风雨般的掌声。
    阿查里雅首相在他讲话中表示他在回国后将要告诉尼泊尔人民，他们在政治、经济、社会和文化各方面为祖国利益所作的一切工作，都会得到中国人民的同情和鼓励。他说，当尼泊尔人民知道伟大的中国人民正在同情地关注着他们的真诚努力的时候，他们一定会感到无限欢欣鼓舞。首相的讲话受到了热烈的欢迎。
    彭真市长在会上还代表北京市向阿查里雅首相赠送了一面锦旗，并且向首相赠送了礼品。献旗献礼时，会场内一片欢腾，掌声经久不息。
    今天的大会在北京体育馆比赛馆内举行。尼泊尔和中国的国旗在会场前迎风飘扬，会场内高悬着红底金字的标语，上面写着：“中尼两国人民的友谊万岁”、“亚非和世界和平万岁”。副总理贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫、副委员长陈叔通，外交部副部长张闻天，中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和夫人，中国尼泊尔友好协会会长周建人，北京市人民委员会各部门和各人民团体的负责人参加了大会。
    在欢迎大会上，中国的文艺工作者表演了音乐、舞蹈和杂技等节目，在大会以后，阿查里雅首相等参观了体育馆的练习馆和游泳馆。
