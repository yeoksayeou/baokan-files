Title: New Oil Refinery in Albania Goes Into Production
Author:
Date: 1956-09-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 21st: According to a report by the Albanian Telegraph Agency on September 20th, a new and large-scale oil refinery in Albania has begun production.

	This oil refinery is located on the Cerrik plain in central Albania, near the city of Elbasan. In recent days, the oil pipelines from the Patos oil field have been working intensively to transport the first batch of crude oil to the refinery. The refinery can refine 150,000 tons of oil per year.

	With the construction of this new factory, Albania will be able to refine all of its domestically produced crude oil by 1957. By 1960, the amount of domestically refined crude oil will increase by 276% compared to 1955.

### 阿尔巴尼亚一新型炼油厂投入生产

1956-09-22
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚一新型炼油厂投入生产
    新华社布拉格21日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社20日报道，阿尔巴尼亚的一所新型的巨大的炼油厂已经开始生产。
    这所炼油厂位于阿尔巴尼亚中部柴立克平原上，靠近爱尔巴桑城。近几天来，巴托斯油田的油管正在紧张地为炼油厂输送第一批原油。炼油厂一年可以提炼十五万吨石油。
    由于建成了这一座新工厂，到1957年，阿尔巴尼亚就可以提炼国内出产的全部原油。1960年国内提炼的原油将比1955年增加276％。
