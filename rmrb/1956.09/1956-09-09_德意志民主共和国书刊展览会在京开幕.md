Title: German Democratic Republic Book and Publication Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-09
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency
The "German Democratic Republic Book and Publication Exhibition" opened on the morning of the 7th at the waterside pavilion in Zhongshan Park, Beijing.

Over 1,400 exquisitely printed and bound publications are on display, arranged in four exhibition rooms. These include the classic works of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin published by the German Democratic Republic; the Selected Works of Mao Zedong published in German; world literature masterpieces, social science, and natural science books; illustrated reading materials for children, and political economy textbooks for the blind; as well as famous paintings reproduced using collotype and offset printing. Chinese publishing workers who came to visit expressed praise and admiration for the development and brilliant achievements of the German Democratic Republic's publishing industry.

The exhibition is sponsored by the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the People's Republic of China and will be on display until September 28.

### 德意志民主共和国书刊展览会在京开幕

1956-09-09
第7版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国书刊展览会在京开幕
    新华社讯　“德意志民主共和国书刊展览会”7日上午在北京中山公园水榭开幕。
    会上展出的一千四百多种印刷和装帧精美的出版物，分别陈列在四个展览室中。这里有德意志民主共和国出版的马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林的经典著作，有用德文出版的毛泽东选集，有世界文学名著和社会科学、自然科学书刊，有供儿童阅读的附有插图的读物和供盲人学习的政治经济学教科书，还有用珂罗版、胶版翻印的名画。前来参观的中国出版工作者都对德意志民主共和国出版事业的发达和光辉成就表示赞扬和钦佩。
    展览会是由中华人民共和国对外文化联络局主办的，将要展出到9月28日。
