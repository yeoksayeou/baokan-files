Title: Lebanese Prime Minister Receives Chinese Business Representatives
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, September 9th: Lebanese Prime Minister Abdullah Yafi received Wang Liewang, the newly arrived Chinese business representative to Lebanon, and Deputy Representative Dai Peichen.

During the meeting, Prime Minister Yafi welcomed their arrival and expressed the hope that trade relations between the two countries would be developed and strengthened for the benefit of both. Wang Liewang presented Prime Minister Yafi with a letter of greetings from Premier Zhou Enlai.

### 黎巴嫩总理接见我国商务代表

1956-09-10
第5版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩总理接见我国商务代表
    新华社9日讯　贝鲁特消息：黎巴嫩总理阿卜杜拉·雅非接见了新近到达的中国驻黎巴嫩商务代表王烈望和副代表戴佩宸。
    在接见时，雅非总理对他们的到达表示欢迎，并且希望为了两国的利益，发展和加强两国之间的贸易关系。王烈望把周恩来总理的一封问候信递交给雅非总理。
