Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Banquet for Egyptian Ambassador Ragab
Author:
Date: 1956-09-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th -- "The Chinese government and the Chinese people will do everything possible to support the heroic struggle of the Egyptian people to safeguard the sovereignty of the Suez Canal." This was stated by Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai at the banquet tonight to welcome the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Egypt to China, General Hassan Ragab.

Premier Zhou Enlai said: "You are to our west, at the forefront of the anti-colonialist struggle. Your victory is our victory." He raised his glass to wish the Egyptian people victory in their struggle for Egypt's sovereignty, independence, and dignity under the outstanding leadership of President Nasser.

Egyptian Ambassador Hassan Ragab said that the Egyptian people are very grateful to the Chinese government and people for standing on the side of the Egyptian people on the Suez Canal issue. He said that Egypt is able to be so firm in the face of imperialism, which possesses all kinds of weapons, because we have the support of the people of China and other countries. He said that if we can win this struggle, the end of imperialism will come. He firmly believes that the struggle of the people all over the world for freedom and independence will be victorious.

Hassan Ragab said that we are pleased that the Chinese people have freed themselves from the shackles of colonialism. Under the leadership of President Nasser, the Egyptian people are following the path of China, freeing themselves from the oppression of imperialism. The Egyptian people are inspired and strengthened by China's good example.

Also present at the banquet tonight were Egyptian Commercial Representative Mr. and Mrs. Far, Deputy Representative Mr. and Mrs. Meng Dawei, and others.

Invited to attend were Vice Premier of the State Council Marshal He Long, Marshal Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Minister of Communications Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, Head of the Chinese Islamic Pilgrimage Delegation, and Head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation to Egypt Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Fu Zuoyi, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian and Zhang Hanfu, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and heads of various departments of state organs and people's organizations.

### 周总理欢宴埃及大使拉加卜

1956-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理欢宴埃及大使拉加卜
    新华社19日讯“中国政府和中国人民将尽一切可能支持埃及人民维护苏彝士运河主权的英勇斗争。”这是国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来在今晚欢迎埃及共和国首任驻华特命全权大使哈桑·拉加卜将军的宴会上说的。
    周恩来总理说：“你们在我们的西面，在反殖民主义斗争的最前线。你们的胜利就是我们的胜利。”他举杯祝埃及人民在纳赛尔总统卓越的领导下，为埃及的主权、独立、尊严的斗争取得胜利。
    埃及大使哈桑·拉加卜说，埃及人民非常感谢中国政府和人民在苏彝士运河问题上站在埃及人民的一方面。他说，埃及能够在拥有各式各样武器的帝国主义面前表现得非常坚决，这是因为我们有中国和其他国家人民的支持。他说，如果我们能够取得这个斗争的胜利的话，帝国主义的末日就到了。他深信，全世界人民为争取自由、独立的斗争一定会取得胜利。
    哈桑·拉加卜说，我们对于中国人民已经摆脱了殖民主义的束缚感到高兴，埃及人民在纳赛尔总统的领导下，正在追随着中国的道路，摆脱帝国主义的压迫，埃及人民从中国良好的榜样中得到鼓舞和力量。
    出席今晚宴会的客人还有埃及商务代表法尔夫妇、副代表孟大维夫妇等。
    应邀出席作陪的，有国务院副总理贺龙元帅、陈毅元帅，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席、交通部部长章伯钧，政协全国委员会副主席、中国伊斯兰教朝觐团团长、中国赴埃及文化代表团团长鲍尔汉，国防委员会副主席傅作义，外交部副部长张闻天、章汉夫，中国人民解放军高级将领，国家机关各部门和人民团体的负责人。
