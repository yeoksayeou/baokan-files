Title: Aeromodelling Sport Widely Developed in Many City Middle Schools
Author:
Date: 1956-09-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Following the 1956 Aeromodelling Competition, the Central National Defense Sports Club convened the Second Professional Conference on Aeromodelling Sports for 1956 in thirteen regions, including Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Nanjing, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chengdu, Chongqing, Shenyang, Changchun, Harbin, Xi'an, and Inner Mongolia, from August 27th to September 1st in Beijing. Representatives from the Ministry of Education and the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League also attended.

    At the meeting, various regions reported that since the first professional conference in March 1956, the number of middle schools with aeromodelling activities in the thirteen regions had increased from 254 to 472. Cities such as Nanjing, Chengdu, Changchun, and Harbin had launched aeromodelling activities in 100% of their middle schools. The number of participants had increased from 10,682 to 21,672. The number of group instructors had increased from 1,054 to 2,830.

    The professional conference also studied the organizational charter of aeromodelling clubs and specific measures for implementing the pilot athlete ranking system. It also discussed revisions to the activity outlines for aeromodelling groups at all levels. The conference also decided to establish aeromodelling clubs in thirteen cities in the second half of the year.

### 航空模型运动在许多城市中学中广泛开展

1956-09-02
第4版()
专栏：

    航空模型运动在许多城市中学中广泛开展
    新华社1日讯　中央国防体育俱乐部继1956年航空模型竞赛大会之后，于8月27日至9月1日在北京召开了北京、上海、天津、南京、武汉、广州、成都、重庆、沈阳、长春、哈尔滨、西安和内蒙等十三个地区1956年的航空模型运动第二次专业会议。列席参加的还有教育部和团中央等单位。
    会上各地汇报从1956年3月份第一次专业会议后，十三个地区已由二百五十四个中学发展到四百七十二个中学有航空模型活动；南京、成都、长春、哈尔滨等市已在全市100％的中学中开展了航空模型活动。参加活动的人数由一万零六百八十二人发展到二万一千六百七十二人。小组辅导员由一千零五十四人发展到二千八百三十人。
    专业会议上还研究了航空模型俱乐部的组织章程、试行运动员等级制的具体措施；并讨论了航空模型各级小组活动提纲的修改意见。会议还决定了下半年在十三个城市建立航空模型俱乐部。
