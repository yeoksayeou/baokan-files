Title: Chairman Mao Receives Indonesian Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1956-09-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received the Indonesian Ambassador to China, Wirjopranoto, on the afternoon of the 18th.

Present at the reception were: Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; and Zhang Wenjin, Deputy Director of the Asian Department. Huang Zhen, the Chinese Ambassador to the Republic of Indonesia, was also present.

### 毛主席接见印度尼西亚大使

1956-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见印度尼西亚大使
    新华社18日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东在18日下午接见了印度尼西亚共和国驻我国大使维约普拉诺托。
    接见时在座的有：外交部副部长章汉夫，礼宾司司长王倬如和亚洲司副司长章文晋。在座的还有我国驻印度尼西亚共和国大使黄镇。
