Title: ACFU Holds Banquet for Icelandic Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-09-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 13th. The Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries (ACFU) hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Icelandic Cultural Delegation.

    Ding Xilin, Vice President of the ACFU, delivered a speech at the banquet, welcoming the Icelandic Cultural Delegation's visit to China and expressing his belief that this visit would play a positive role in cultural exchange between China and Iceland.

    Jakob Benediktsson, head of the Icelandic Cultural Delegation, also spoke at the banquet, expressing his hope that this visit would enhance the friendly relations between Iceland and China.

    Those present at the banquet included Qu Wu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Chen Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and representatives from the All-China Federation of Cooperatives and cultural circles in Beijing.

### 对外文协欢宴冰岛文化代表团

1956-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

    对外文协欢宴冰岛文化代表团
    新华社13日讯　中国人民对外文化协会今晚设宴欢迎冰岛文化代表团。
    中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林在宴会上致词，他对冰岛文化代表团的访问中国表示欢迎，并且相信这一次访问对中、冰两国的文化交流将起很好的作用。
    冰岛文化代表团团长雅可布·贝纳狄克生也在宴会上讲话，他希望这次访问能够增加冰、中两国的友好关系。
    今晚出席作陪的有，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长屈武，中国人民外交学会副会长陈翰笙以及中华全国合作总社和北京文化界的代表。
