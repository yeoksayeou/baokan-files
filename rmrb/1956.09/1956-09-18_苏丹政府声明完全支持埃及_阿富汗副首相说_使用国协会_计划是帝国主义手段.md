Title: Sudanese Government Statement Fully Supports Egypt; Afghan Deputy Prime Minister Says "Users' Association" Plan is an Imperialist Means
Author:
Date: 1956-09-18
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 17th, dispatch from Cairo via TASS: According to the Middle East News Agency, the Sudanese government issued a formal statement saying: "The Sudanese government and people fully support Egypt, sympathize with its determination to safeguard the sovereignty of all its territory, and agree with Egypt's statement regarding its willingness to guarantee freedom of navigation in the Suez Canal."

The statement said that Sudan condemns the use of threats of force as a means of resolving international conflicts and advocates resolving the issue through peaceful means.

Xinhua News Agency, September 17th, dispatch from Kabul: TASS quoted a statement from the Afghan Ministry of Foreign Affairs citing the Afghan Eastern News Agency saying that the Afghan government agrees with the Egyptian government's proposal for resolving the Suez issue. The Afghan government supports the Egyptian government's proposal to convene a conference of representatives of countries using the Suez Canal.

Xinhua News Agency, September 17th, dispatch from Kabul: Afghan Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Ali Mohammed Naim Khan told reporters on the 16th that the Western countries' plan for a "Suez Canal Users' Association" completely violates Egypt's national interests.

The Foreign Minister declared that this plan is an attack on Egypt's sovereignty. He added that the Suez Canal issue cannot be resolved by such "imperialist means."

### 苏丹政府声明完全支持埃及  阿富汗副首相说“使用国协会”计划是帝国主义手段

1956-09-18
第7版()
专栏：

    苏丹政府声明完全支持埃及
    阿富汗副首相说“使用国协会”计划是帝国主义手段
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社开罗17日讯：据中东通讯社报道，苏丹政府发表了一个正式声明，声明说：“苏丹政府和人民完全支持埃及，同情它维护本国全部领土的主权的决心，并且赞同埃及关于愿意保障苏彝士运河通航自由的声明”。
    声明说，苏丹谴责使用武力威胁作为解决国际冲突的办法，并且主张通过和平方式解决这个问题。
    新华社17日讯　塔斯社喀布尔16日讯：据阿富汗东方通讯社引证阿富汗外交部的声明说，阿富汗政府对埃及政府提出的关于解决苏彝士问题的建议表示同意。阿富汗政府支持埃及政府提出召开一个使用苏彝士运河各国的代表会议的建议。
    新华社17日讯　喀布尔消息：阿富汗副首相兼外交大臣阿里·穆罕默德·纳伊姆汗16日对记者说，西方国家关于“苏彝士运河使用国协会”的计划完全违反了埃及的民族利益。
    这位外交大臣宣称，这个计划是对埃及主权的一种打击。他又说，苏彝士运河问题不能够用这样的“帝国主义手段”来加以解决。
