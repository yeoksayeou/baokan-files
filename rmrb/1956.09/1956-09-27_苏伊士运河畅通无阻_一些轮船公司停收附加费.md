Title: Suez Canal Unobstructed; Some Shipping Companies Stop Collecting Surcharges
Author:
Date: 1956-09-27
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: London dispatch: In view of the unimpeded passage through the Suez Canal, some European shipping companies operating routes from the United Kingdom, Scandinavia, and North European ports to the Far East have announced that they will cease collecting freight surcharges today. This surcharge was added last week in anticipation of possible delays in Suez Canal navigation.

### 苏伊士运河畅通无阻  一些轮船公司停收附加费

1956-09-27
第8版()
专栏：

    苏伊士运河畅通无阻
    一些轮船公司停收附加费
    据新华社26日讯　伦敦消息：鉴于苏伊士运河畅通无阻，欧洲一些经营从英国、斯堪的纳维亚和北欧港口到远东航线的轮船公司宣布今天停止征收货物附加运费。这项附加运费是上星期为预防在苏伊士运河通航方面的可能拖延而增加的。
