Title: Sukarno Leaves Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-09-13
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 12th (TASS): Indonesian President Sukarno and his entourage left Moscow by plane for Yugoslavia on the morning of September 12th.
    Soviet state leaders went to the airport to see off the distinguished guests.
    President Sukarno and his entourage arrived in Moscow on August 28th. They stayed in the Soviet Union for two weeks.

### 苏加诺离开莫斯科

1956-09-13
第5版()
专栏：

    苏加诺离开莫斯科
    据新华社12日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：印度尼西亚总统苏加诺和他的随行人员在12日上午乘飞机离开莫斯科前往南斯拉夫。
    苏联国家领导人到机场欢送贵宾。
    苏加诺总统和他的随行人员是8月28日到达莫斯科的。他们在苏联共逗留了两个星期。
