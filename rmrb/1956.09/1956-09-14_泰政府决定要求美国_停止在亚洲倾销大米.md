Title: Thai Government Decides to Demand the United States Stop Dumping Rice in Asia
Author:
Date: 1956-09-14
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, September 12th: According to newspaper reports, the Thai government has decided to demand that the United States stop dumping rice in Asian markets.
　　The newspaper said that if the United States continues to dump rice in Asian markets, Thailand's economy will suffer a serious blow. It said the Thai government attaches great importance to the export of Thai rice and is concerned about the United States continuing to dump surplus rice in Asian markets next year.
　　The newspaper said that the Thai government believes that the US policy of dumping surplus rice abroad contradicts the agreement on rice exports previously signed between Thailand and the United States.

### 泰政府决定要求美国  停止在亚洲倾销大米

1956-09-14
第5版()
专栏：

    泰政府决定要求美国  停止在亚洲倾销大米
    据新华社12日讯　曼谷消息：据报纸报道，泰国政府已经决定要求美国停止在亚洲市场倾销大米。
    报纸说，泰国政府认为，如果美国继续在亚洲市场倾销大米，泰国的经济将遭受严重打击。它说，泰国政府非常重视泰国大米的输出问题，并且对美国明年将继续在亚洲市场倾销剩余大米表示担忧。
    这家报纸说，泰国政府认为，美国这种将剩余大米向外倾销的政策是同泰国和美国以前所签订的有关米粮外销的协定相抵触的。
