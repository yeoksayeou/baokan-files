Title: Indian Military Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. The Indian military goodwill delegation to China, headed by Lieutenant General Thorat Nath Chaudhuri of the Indian Army, arrived in Beijing this afternoon by special plane from Guangzhou.

Those who greeted them at the airport included General Li Da, Deputy Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, General Li Kenong, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense, Vice Admiral Luo Shunchu of the Navy, Lieutenant General Chang Qiankun of the Air Force, Lieutenant General Zheng Weishan, Deputy Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison, and Senior Colonel Zhu Kaiyin, Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense, and others.

Indian Ambassador to China R.K. Nehru, Military Attaché Malik, and military attachés from various countries in China also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

On its way to Beijing, the delegation stopped at Wuhan Airport for about an hour and was warmly received by representatives of the local garrison.

### 印度军事代表团到北京

1956-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    印度军事代表团到北京
    新华社28日讯　以印度共和国陆军中将卓荣铎·纳特·乔杜里为首的印度军事友好访华代表团，今天下午乘专机由广州到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中华人民共和国国防部副部长李达上将，中国人民解放军副总参谋长李克农上将，总政治部副主任甘泗淇上将，国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将，海军副司令员罗舜初海军中将，空军副司令员常乾坤空军中将，京津卫戍司令部副司令员郑维山中将和国防部外事处长朱开印大校等人。
    印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁和武官马立克以及各国驻华武官也到机场欢迎。
    代表团在来北京途中，曾在武汉机场停留一小时许，受到当地驻军代表的热烈接待。
