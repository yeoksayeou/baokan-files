Title: Tokyo University, Japan Organizes Academic Survey Team to Iraq and Iran
Author: Ran
Date: 1956-09-01
Page: 7

Academic Trends

    Tokyo University, Japan Organizes Academic Survey Team to Iraq and Iran

    Recently, Tokyo University's Institute of Oriental Culture, with the consent of the Iranian government, organized an "Academic Survey Team to Iraq and Iran." From September 29th to November 26th of this year, archaeological excavations will be conducted in Mesopotamia and the area northwest of Mosul. The survey team consists of twelve experts in archaeology, anthropology, art history, and architectural history, including Professor Namio Egami of Tokyo University. Currently, in preparation for this work, a "Tokyo University Iraq and Iran Archaeological Excavation Committee" has been established within Tokyo University, with Tokyo University President Tadaharu Yanaibara serving as the chairman. In addition, a specialized committee composed of eighteen experts has been established as an advisory body.
　(Ran)

### 日本东京大学组织伊拉克、伊朗学术调查团

1956-09-01
第7版()
专栏：学术动态

    日本东京大学组织伊拉克、伊朗学术调查团
    最近，日本东京大学东洋文化研究所，在伊朗政府同意下，组织了一个“伊拉克、伊朗学术调查团”。从本年9月29日起到11月26日止，将在美索不达米亚和莫索尔西北一带举行考古发掘。这个调查团是由东京大学教授江上波夫等十二名考古学、人类学、美术史、建筑史专家组成的。目前，为了准备这一工作，已在东京大学内设立了一个“东京大学伊拉克、伊朗遗迹发掘委员会”，由东京大学校长矢内原忠雄任委员长。另外还设立了一个由十八个专家组成的专门委员会，作为咨询机构。
　（然）
