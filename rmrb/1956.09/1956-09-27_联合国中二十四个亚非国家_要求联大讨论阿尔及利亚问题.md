Title: Twenty-Four Asian-African Nations in the UN Request General Assembly Discussion of the Algerian Question
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-27
Page: 8
Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 26th: Twenty-four Asian-African nations participating in the United Nations decided on September 25th to once again raise the Algerian question before the United Nations General Assembly.

	Arthur Lall, the chairman for September of the group of twenty-four Asian-African nations, announced after a meeting of the group on the 25th: "A letter to the (United Nations) Secretary-General requesting that an item called "The Algerian Question" be placed on the agenda of the General Assembly has been signed today by some countries in this group and will soon be delivered to the Secretary-General."

	Last year, Asian-African countries requested the United Nations General Assembly to discuss the Algerian question. However, under the coercion of France and the manipulation of some other Western countries, the General Assembly voted to temporarily postpone the discussion of this issue.

### 联合国中二十四个亚非国家  要求联大讨论阿尔及利亚问题

1956-09-27
第8版()
专栏：

    联合国中二十四个亚非国家
    要求联大讨论阿尔及利亚问题
    新华社26日讯　纽约消息：参加联合国的亚非国家9月25日决定再次向联合国大会提出阿尔及利亚问题。
    二十四个亚非国家集团的9月份主席阿瑟·拉尔在这个集团25日的一次会议后宣布：“一封给（联合国）秘书长的要求把名为‘阿尔及利亚问题’的项目列入大会议程的信件，今天已由这个集团的一些国家签字，不久将交给秘书长。”
    去年亚非国家曾经要求联合国大会讨论阿尔及利亚问题。但是在法国的要挟和其他一些西方国家的操纵下，大会表决暂不讨论这个问题。
