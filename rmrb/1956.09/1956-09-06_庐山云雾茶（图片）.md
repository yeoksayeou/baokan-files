Title: Lushan Cloud and Mist Tea (Picture)
Author: Yan Zhennan
Date: 1956-09-06
Page: 3

Lushan Cloud and Mist Tea
    Lushan is shrouded in clouds and mist, and the climate is cool. The tea grown here, due to the sunshine and mist, has an exceptionally fragrant taste that lasts for a long time. It is a famous special tea in our country. This is a worker from the Lushan Workstation of the Institute of Botany, Chinese Academy of Sciences, pruning and cultivating in the tea garden.
                           Xinhua News Agency reporter Yan Zhennan photographed

### 庐山云雾茶（图片）
严震南
1956-09-06
第3版()
专栏：

    庐山云雾茶
    庐山云雾弥漫，气候凉爽，这里所种的茶叶，因日照雾熏，其味清香异常，经久不散，是我国有名的特种茶。这是中国科学院植物研究所庐山工作站的工人在茶园里进行整枝培育工作。
                           新华社记者　严震南摄
