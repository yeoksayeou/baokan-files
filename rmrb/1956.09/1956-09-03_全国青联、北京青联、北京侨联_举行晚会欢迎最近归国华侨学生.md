Title: All-China Youth Federation, Beijing Youth Federation, Beijing Overseas Chinese Federation Hold Soiree to Welcome Recently Returned Overseas Chinese Students
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 1st. The All-China Youth Federation, the Beijing Youth Federation, and the Beijing Overseas Chinese Federation held a get-together tonight to welcome the more than 500 overseas Chinese students who recently returned to the country.

    As dusk fell on early autumn Beijing, the courtyard of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League was exceptionally beautiful, illuminated by countless colored lights. Under the locust trees, overseas Chinese students enthusiastically danced group dances. Some people played cards and various games under the trees, some people watched the plays "Family Problems," "Mother and Daughter," and "The White-Clothed Murderer" with rapt attention in the auditorium, and some people watched the movie "Spring Returns to the Earth" in the open air. Some people danced ballroom dances in the hall. The entire courtyard and hall were enveloped in an atmosphere of joy.

    Those attending the soiree included Zhang Xiruo, Minister of Education of the People's Republic of China, Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation and Deputy Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, and Luo Yi, Hu Keshi, and Liu Xiyuan, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, as well as more than 1,000 young people from all walks of life in Beijing and overseas Chinese students studying in Beijing.

    The entire staff of the Singapore Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation and the Federation of Malaya Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation were also invited to attend the soiree.

### 全国青联、北京青联、北京侨联  举行晚会欢迎最近归国华侨学生

1956-09-03
第4版()
专栏：

    全国青联、北京青联、北京侨联
    举行晚会欢迎最近归国华侨学生
    新华社1日讯　中华全国民主青年联合会、北京市民主青年联合会、北京市归国华侨联合会今晚为欢迎最近归国的五百多名华侨学生举行联欢晚会。
    当夜幕降临在初秋的北京时，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会的一座庭院被无数彩色的电灯照耀得分外美丽。在槐树林下，华侨学生热情地跳着集体舞。一些人在树下打扑克、做各种游戏，一些人在礼堂聚精会神地观看话剧“家庭问题”、
  “母女俩”、“白衣凶手”，一些人在露天看电影“春回大地”。一些人在大厅里跳交谊舞。整个庭院、大厅被欢乐的气氛笼罩着。
    出席晚会的，有中华人民共和国教育部部长张奚若，中华全国民主青年联合会主席、华侨事务委员会副主任廖承志和中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记罗毅、胡克实、刘西元，以及北京市各界青年和在北京学习的华侨学生一千多人。
    新加坡工商业贸易考察团、马来亚联合邦工商业贸易考察团全体人员也应邀出席了晚会。
