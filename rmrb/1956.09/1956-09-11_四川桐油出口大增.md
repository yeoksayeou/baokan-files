Title: Sichuan Tung Oil Exports Greatly Increased
Author:
Date: 1956-09-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency Report:

    Sichuan tung oil, which enjoys an international reputation, exported 21,600 tons from January to the end of August this year, which is more than 8,000 tons more than the total export volume last year.
    Sichuan Province is the province with the largest output of tung trees in my country. Last year, the province produced approximately 420 million catties of tung seeds, reaching the highest level in history.

### 四川桐油出口大增

1956-09-11
第2版()
专栏：

    四川桐油出口大增
    据新华社讯　享有国际声誉的四川桐油，今年1月到8月底共出口了两万一千六百吨，比去年全年出口量还多八千多吨。
    四川省是我国出产桐子最多的省份。去年全省出产的桐子约四亿两千多万斤，达到了历史上的最高水平。
