Title: The Navy Sends Five Landing Ships to Participate in Yangtze River Shipping Work
Author:
Date: 1956-09-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. To support the rush transport of materials and passengers en route to Sichuan, the Headquarters and Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy issued an order on the 5th for the East China Sea Fleet to immediately dispatch five large and medium-sized landing ships to the Shanghai Branch of the Yangtze River Navigation Administration for assignment, and to send a responsible officer to participate in the work of the unified transportation command in Hankou. The order required all crew members participating in the emergency transportation work to work actively, overcome difficulties, and ensure transportation safety.

### 海军派五艘登陆舰  参加长江航运工作

1956-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    海军派五艘登陆舰
    参加长江航运工作
    新华社6日讯　为支援抢运入川途中的物资和旅客，中国人民解放军海军司令部、政治部于5日下令东海舰队立即派大、中型登陆舰五艘前往长江航务局上海分局听候分配，并派一负责军官参加在汉口的运输统一指挥部的工作。命令中要求参加紧急运输工作的全体舰员积极工作，克服困难，保证运输安全。
