Title: He Long, Chen Yi, and Ulanhu Host Banquet to Welcome Ethnic Minority Visiting Groups
Author:
Date: 1956-09-30
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. Vice Premiers of the State Council He Long, Chen Yi, and Ulanhu held a grand banquet at the Beijing Hotel this noon to welcome fifteen ethnic minority visiting groups from all parts of the motherland, who are here to visit the capital and participate in the National Day celebrations.

One thousand three hundred members of ethnic minority visiting groups from Tibet, Xinjiang, Gansu, Qinghai, Shaanxi, Sichuan, Yunnan, Inner Mongolia, Guangdong, Yanbian, and other parts of the country attended today's banquet.

In his speech, Vice Premier He Long, on behalf of the State Council, warmly welcomed the ethnic minority visiting groups to visit the capital—Beijing—and participate in the National Day celebrations on the eve of the seventh anniversary of the founding of the nation. He said that in the past seven years, with the fundamental changes taking place in all aspects of our motherland, the appearance of the brotherly ethnic minority regions has undergone great changes, and the unity among all ethnic groups in our country has also been strengthened. The gathering here today of more than 1,300 people from more than 30 ethnic groups precisely demonstrates the new atmosphere of ethnic unity in our country.

Vice Premier He Long concluded by saying, "Let us people of all ethnic groups further unite and redouble our efforts to build our great socialist motherland."

Vice Premiers Chen Yi and Ulanhu also rose to propose toasts at the banquet, congratulating the great unity of all ethnic groups in the motherland's big family.

During their speeches and toasts, warm applause constantly resounded throughout the banquet, and people toasted to the great unity of all ethnic groups and the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of all ethnic groups!

Also present were: Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairmen of the Nationalities Affairs Commission Wang Feng, Liu Chun, Sa Kongliao, Yang Jingren, Chairman of the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress Liu Geping, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Bao Erhan, Director of the Office of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China Zhang Jingwu, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region Zhang Guohua, Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Tan Guansan, Deputy Director of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee Xing Xiping, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Communist Youth League Central Committee Hu Yaobang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation Liu Xiyuan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Xu Guangping, Director of the Dalai Lama's Beijing Office Dunwang·Jianzan Zhaba, Acting Director of the Panchen's Beijing Office Lian Huizhu, President of the Buddhist Association of China Xirao Jiacuo, Vice Chairman of the China Islamic Association Da Putsheng, and responsible persons from various government departments.

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. The fifteen brotherly ethnic minority visiting groups who came to the capital of the motherland, Beijing, to visit and participate in the National Day celebrations have arrived in Beijing one after another.

These visiting groups include nearly thirty brotherly ethnic groups from the big family of ethnic groups in the motherland, such as Mongolian, Tibetan, Uygur, Hui, Miao, Li, and Hani, totaling more than 1,300 people. Many of them are upper-class figures from various ethnic groups, monastic and secular officials, Living Buddhas, chieftains, and imams. Some are ethnic minority cadres.

These visiting groups are: the Tibet Visiting Group, the Tibet Women's Visiting Group, the Sichuan, Gansu, and Qinghai Border Ethnic Minority Visiting Group, the Yunnan Province Ethnic Minority Visiting Group, the Sichuan Province Ethnic Minority Visiting Group, the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Visiting Group, the Gansu Province Pastoral Area Ethnic Minority Visiting Group, the Guangdong Province Ethnic Minority Visiting Group, the Shaanxi Province Ethnic Minority Visiting Group, and the Qinghai Province Pastoral Area Ethnic Minority Visiting Group. In addition, there are four ethnic minority youth visiting groups and an ethnic minority cadre visiting group from the Xinjiang Non-Ferrous Metals Industry.

### 贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫设宴欢迎各民族参观团

1956-09-30
第2版()
专栏：

    贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫设宴欢迎各民族参观团
    新华社29日讯　国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫今天中午在北京饭店举行盛大宴会，欢迎十五个从祖国各地来到首都参观和参加国庆观礼的民族参观团。
    来自祖国西藏、新疆、甘肃、青海、陕西、四川、云南、内蒙古、广东、延边等地的一千三百名各民族参观团团员出席了今天的宴会。
    贺龙副总理在致词中代表国务院热烈欢迎各民族参观团在建国七周年的前夕来到首都——北京参观和参加国庆节观礼。他说，在过去七年期间，随着我们祖国各方面发生的根本变化，各兄弟民族地区的面貌已经有了很大的变化，我国各民族间的团结也有了加强。今天在这里欢聚一堂的三十多个民族的一千三百多人，正好显示了我国民族团结的新气象。
    贺龙副总理最后说，让我们各民族人民进一步团结起来，为建设我们伟大的社会主义祖国而加倍努力。
    陈毅和乌兰夫副总理也分别在宴会上起立祝酒，祝贺祖国大家庭中各民族的大团结。
    在他们致词和祝酒的时候，宴会上不断响起热烈的掌声，并且为各民族的大团结和各民族人民的伟大领袖毛泽东主席的健康而干杯！
    出席作陪的有：国务院秘书长习仲勋，民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、刘春、萨空了、杨静仁、全国人民代表大会民族委员会主任刘格平委员，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席鲍尔汉，中华人民共和国主席办公厅主任张经武，西藏自治区筹备委员会副主任张国华委员，中国人民解放军西藏军区政治委员谭冠三，中共中央统一战线工作部副部长邢西萍，青年团中央书记处书记胡耀邦，中华全国民主青年联合会副主席刘西元，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平，达赖喇嘛驻京办事处处长顿旺·坚赞扎巴，班禅驻京办事处代处长联慧珠，中国佛教协会会长喜饶嘉错，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生和政府各部门负责人等。
    据新华社29日讯　到祖国首都北京来参观和参加国庆观礼的十五个兄弟民族参观团先后到达北京。
    在这些参观团中，包括了祖国各民族大家庭中的蒙古、藏、维吾尔、回、苗、黎、哈尼等近三十个兄弟民族，共有一千三百多人。他们中许多人是各民族的上层人物、僧俗官员、活佛、头人、阿訇，有些是民族干部。
    这些参观团是：西藏参观团，西藏妇女参观团，川、甘、青边境各民族参观团，云南省各民族参观团，四川省各民族参观团，新疆维吾尔自治区参观团，甘肃省牧区各民族参观团，广东省各民族参观团，陕西省各民族参观团，青海省牧区各民族参观团。另外还有四个民族青年参观团和一个新疆有色金属工业的民族干部参观团。
