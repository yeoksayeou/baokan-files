Title: Lao Premier Cables Thanks to China and Vietnam for Reception
Author:
Date: 1956-09-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 4th. Vientiane Radio broadcast today: After returning home from visits to China and Vietnam, Lao Kingdom Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma sent telegrams to Chairman Mao Zedong, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen, thanking the Chinese people and Chinese leaders for the grand reception given to the Lao Kingdom government delegation. He also praised the achievements made by the Chinese people in the past and wished the friendship between the two countries would be further consolidated.

In his telegram to Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Premier Pham Van Dong, Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma said: "For the happiness of the Lao people and the Vietnamese people, the Lao government guarantees the implementation of the joint communiqué of August 28."

### 老挝首相电谢中越两国的接待

1956-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    老挝首相电谢中越两国的接待
    新华社河内4日电　万象电台今天广播：老挝王国首相梭发那·富马在访问了中国和越南回国以后，打电报给毛泽东主席、周恩来总理和北京市彭真市长，感谢中国人民和中国领袖们对老挝王国政府代表团的隆重接待，同时赞扬中国人民在过去期间所获得的成就，并且祝两国的友谊日益巩固。
    梭发那·富马首相在给胡志明主席和范文同总理的电文中说：“为了老挝人民和越南人民的幸福，老挝政府保证执行8月28日的联合公报。”
