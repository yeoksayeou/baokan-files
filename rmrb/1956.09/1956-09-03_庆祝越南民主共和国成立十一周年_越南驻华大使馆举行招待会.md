Title: Celebrating the 11th Anniversary of the Founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Vietnamese Embassy in China Holds a Reception
Author:
Date: 1956-09-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Vice Chairman Zhu De attended the reception held this evening by Zhou Liang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China. The reception was held to celebrate the 11th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

At the reception, Zhou Liang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, and Vice Chairman Zhu De successively raised their glasses to congratulate the development and consolidation of the unbreakable friendship between the people of China and Vietnam. They also separately proposed toasts to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong and Chairman Ho Chi Minh.

This evening, Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, and Li Xiannian, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Shen Junru, and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Cheng Qian and Zhang Zhizhong, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, all extended festive greetings to the host of the banquet.

The reception was held at the Beijing Hotel, and more than 700 Chinese and foreign guests were invited to attend. Among them were heads of various departments of state organs, generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, people from all walks of life in Beijing, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China.

After the reception, Chinese and foreign guests watched the Vietnamese color documentary films "Vietnamese Dance Excerpts" and "Vietnamese Bamboo."

### 庆祝越南民主共和国成立十一周年  越南驻华大使馆举行招待会

1956-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝越南民主共和国成立十一周年
    越南驻华大使馆举行招待会
    新华社2日讯　朱德副主席今晚出席了越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办周亮举行的招待会。这个招待会是庆祝越南民主共和国成立十一周年的。
    招待会上，越南驻华大使馆临时代办周亮和朱德副主席相继举杯祝贺中越两国人民间牢不可破的友谊的发展和巩固。他们并分别提议为毛泽东主席和为胡志明主席的健康干杯。
    今晚，副总理陈云、陈毅、乌兰夫、李先念，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、陈叔通，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，国防委员会副主席程潜、张治中和外交部副部长张闻天，都向宴会的主人作节日的祝贺。
    招待会在北京饭店举行，有七百多位中外人士应邀出席。其中有国家机关各部门的负责人，中国人民解放军的将军们，北京社会各界人士和各国驻中国的外交官员。
    招待会后，中外客人一起观看了越南彩色纪录影片“越南舞蹈剪辑”和“越南竹子”。
