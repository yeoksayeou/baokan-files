Title: Tito and Sukarno Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-09-15
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, September 14th: According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency, Indonesian and Yugoslav politicians held talks in Belgrade on the morning of the 13th.

The two sides exchanged views on the international situation and the relations between Yugoslavia and Indonesia in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

The Indonesian side participating in the talks included President Sukarno, Foreign Minister Abdulgani, and Indonesian Ambassador to Yugoslavia Sudarsono, among others.

The Yugoslav side participating in the talks included President Tito, Vice Presidents of the Federal Executive Council Kardelj, Ranković, and Vukmanović, and Deputy Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs Prica, among others.

### 铁托和苏加诺会谈

1956-09-15
第6版()
专栏：

    铁托和苏加诺会谈
    新华社14日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，印度尼西亚和南斯拉夫的政治家13日上午在贝尔格莱德举行了会谈。
    双方在融洽和友好的气氛中就国际局势和南斯拉夫同印度尼西亚之间的关系交换了意见。
    印度尼西亚方面参加会谈的有苏加诺总统，外交部长阿卜杜加尼和印度尼西亚驻南斯拉夫大使苏达索诺等人。
    南斯拉夫方面参加会谈的有铁托总统，联邦执行委员会副主席卡德尔、兰科维奇和伏克曼诺维奇，外交国务副秘书长普里察等人。
