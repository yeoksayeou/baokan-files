Title: Hungarian Photography Art Exhibition Concludes in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, September 26th: The Hungarian Photography Art Exhibition concluded in Shanghai on the 25th. The Shanghai People's Fine Arts Publishing House selected over 130 works from the exhibits and decided to reproduce them as a picture album. The exhibition displayed 263 works by 51 photography artists. During the seventeen days of the exhibition, more than 55,000 visitors attended.

### 匈摄影艺术展览在沪结束

1956-09-29
第2版()
专栏：

    匈摄影艺术展览在沪结束
    据新华社上海26日电　匈牙利摄影艺术展览25日在上海结束。上海人民美术出版社从展品中选出一百三十多幅作品，决定复制印成画册。展览会展出五十一个摄影艺术家的二百六十三幅作品。在展出的十七天中，有五万五千多观众前往参观。
