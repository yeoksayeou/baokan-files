Title: Congratulatory Messages Exchanged Between China and Nepal on the Signing of the Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-09-26
Page: 1

    Congratulatory Messages Exchanged Between China and Nepal on the Signing of the Agreement
    King Mahendra Cables Beijing:
    To His Excellency Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:
    On this joyous occasion of the signing of the agreement between our two governments, I extend, in my own name and on behalf of my government and people, warm congratulations to Your Excellency, your government, and your people. I hope and believe that this new relationship established today will usher in a new era of happiness and prosperity for our two peoples.
          His Majesty King Mahendra of the Kingdom of Nepal, September 23, 1956
          Chairman Mao Zedong Replies to Kathmandu, King Mahendra of Nepal:
    On the occasion of the signing of the Sino-Nepalese agreement, I thank you for your congratulations. On behalf of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, and in my own name, I extend my heartfelt gratitude to Your Majesty, the Government of the Kingdom of Nepal, and the Nepalese people. I wish that the traditional friendship between the Chinese and Nepalese peoples will be increasingly consolidated and developed on the basis of this agreement signed in accordance with the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. I wish Your Majesty good health.
             Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, September 25, 1956

### 互贺中尼两国签订协定

1956-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    互贺中尼两国签订协定
    马亨德拉国王来电北京　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东阁下：
    值此我们两国政府签订协定的愉快的时际，我以我个人的名义，并代表我的政府和人民向阁下、您的政府和人民致热烈的祝贺。我希望并且相信今天建立的这种新的关系将开创我们两国人民的幸福和繁荣的新纪元。
          尼泊尔王国国王陛下　马亨德拉　1956年9月23日
          毛泽东主席复电加德满都尼泊尔国王马亨德拉陛下：
    值此中尼协定签订之际，承致祝贺，我谨代表中华人民共和国和中国人民，并且以我个人的名义向陛下、尼泊尔王国政府和人民表示衷心的感谢。祝中尼两国人民的传统友谊，在这一根据和平共处五项原则签订的协定的基础上日益巩固和发展。祝国王陛下健康。
             中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东　1956年9月25日
