Title: Abdugani Goes to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-09-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 2nd. Indonesian Foreign Minister Abdugani left Jakarta by plane this morning, via Rome and Warsaw, to meet President Sukarno, who is currently visiting the Soviet Union, in Moscow.

Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo and other officials saw him off at the airport.

### 阿卜杜加尼去莫斯科

1956-09-03
第5版()
专栏：

    阿卜杜加尼去莫斯科
    据新华社雅加达2日电　印度尼西亚外交部长阿卜杜加尼今天早上乘飞机离开雅加达，取道罗马和华沙去莫斯科同正在苏联访问的苏加诺总统会合。
    在机场送行的有沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理和其他官员。
