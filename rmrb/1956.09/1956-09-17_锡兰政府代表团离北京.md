Title: Ceylon Government Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-17
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The Ceylon Government Delegation, headed by Claude Corea, left Beijing by plane this morning. They will visit Shanghai and then return home via Guangzhou.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and his wife, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Chen Shuliang, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Commissioner Bi Shuowang.

U Hla Maung, the Burmese Ambassador to China, Popovic, the Yugoslav Ambassador to China, and his wife, R.K. Nehru, the Indian Ambassador to China, and O'Neill, the British Chargé d'Affaires in China, also saw them off at the airport.

During their stay in Beijing, the head of the delegation, Claude Corea, and members T.B. Subasinghe and T.G. Abeyasinghe, visited factories in Shenyang and Anshan in Northeast China, and toured the scenic spots and historical sites of Shenyang. Some members of the delegation visited factories, schools, hospitals, and suburban agricultural producers' cooperatives in Beijing, and toured the Great Wall, the Ming Tombs, etc. They also watched famous Chinese Peking operas and enjoyed Chinese folk music and dance.

### 锡兰政府代表团离北京

1956-09-17
第7版()
专栏：

    锡兰政府代表团离北京
    新华社16日讯　以克劳德·科里亚为首的锡兰政府代表团，今天早晨乘飞机离开北京。他们将在上海参观后经广州回国。
    到机场欢送的有中华人民共和国外交部副部长章汉夫和夫人，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，外交部亚洲司副司长陈叔亮、专员毕朔望等。
    缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂，南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇和夫人，印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁，英国驻华代办欧念儒也到机场欢送。
    在京期间，团长克劳德·科里亚和团员特·布·苏巴辛格、蒂·格·阿比亚辛格，曾去东北参观了沈阳、鞍山的工厂，游览了沈阳的名胜古迹。部分团员分别参观了北京的工厂、学校、医院和郊区农业生产合作社，游览了长城、十三陵等。他们还观看了中国著名的京剧，欣赏了中国的民间音乐歌舞。
