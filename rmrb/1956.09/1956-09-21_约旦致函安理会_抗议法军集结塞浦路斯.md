Title: Jordan Sends Letter to Security Council Protesting French Troop Buildup in Cyprus
Author:
Date: 1956-09-21
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 19th. Following Syria and Lebanon, Jordan sent a letter to the United Nations on the 18th protesting France's troop buildup in the British colony of Cyprus during the Suez Canal crisis.

### 约旦致函安理会  抗议法军集结塞浦路斯

1956-09-21
第8版()
专栏：

    约旦致函安理会
    抗议法军集结塞浦路斯
    据新华社19日讯　纽约消息：约旦继叙利亚和黎巴嫩之后在18日致函联合国抗议法国在苏彝士运河危机中在英国殖民地塞浦路斯集结军队。
