Title: Former Indonesian Defense Minister Kusumasmantri Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 28th—At the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, former Indonesian Defense Minister Iwa Kusumasmantri and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to our country.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Senior Colonel Chai Chengwen, representative of the Ministry of National Defense, and Chen Shuliang, Deputy Director of the Asian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

### 印度尼西亚前国防部长  库素玛素曼特里到京

1956-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚前国防部长
    库素玛素曼特里到京
    新华社28日讯　应中国人民外交学会的邀请前来我国访问的印度尼西亚前国防部长伊哇·库素玛素曼特里及夫人今天下午乘飞机抵达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之，国防部代表柴成文大校和外交部亚洲司副司长陈叔亮等多人。
