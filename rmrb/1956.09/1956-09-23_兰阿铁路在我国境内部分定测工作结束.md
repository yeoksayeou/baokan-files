Title: Land and Aerial Railway in Our Country Section of Detailed Survey Work Ends
Author:
Date: 1956-09-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi. The detailed survey work of the Lanzhou-Urumqi-Aktogay Railway in our country has been completed as of the 14th. Now, the technicians are returning to Urumqi from the field one after another and starting the design of this railway that runs across Xinjiang for more than 1,200 kilometers.
The railway from Hami to Urumqi is the most geologically complex section of the Lanzhou-Urumqi-Aktogay Railway in Xinjiang, with saline-alkali land, gypsum land, swamps, and more than 100 kilometers of uninhabited and water-scarce Gobi Desert. In particular, the geological complexity and tunnel length of the seven-kilometer line through the Tianshan Mountains exceed those of the famous Wuqiaoling Mountains.

### 兰阿铁路在我国境内部分定测工作结束

1956-09-23
第6版()
专栏：

    兰阿铁路在我国境内部分定测工作结束
    据新华社乌鲁木齐讯　兰州——乌鲁木齐——阿克斗卡铁路在我国境内的部分，到14日为止，全线定测工作已经结束。现在，技术人员陆续从野外返回乌鲁木齐，开始进行横贯新疆境内的这一千二百多公里铁路的设计。
    从哈密到乌鲁木齐的铁路，是新疆境内兰州——乌鲁木齐——阿克斗卡铁路地质情况最复杂的一段，有盐碱地、石膏地、沼泽和一百公里以上的无人少水的戈壁；特别是通过天山的七公里线路，地质情况的复杂和隧道长度都超过了著名的乌鞘岭。
