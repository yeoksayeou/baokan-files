Title: Cambodian King Receives Chinese Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 8
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 28th: Phnom Penh dispatch: The economic delegation of the People's Republic of China to Phnom Penh was received by King Suramarit and the Queen of Cambodia at the Royal Palace in Cambodia at 10:00 a.m. on September 26.

Present at the reception were Prince Sihanouk and other senior officials.

During the reception, the King and Queen wished the delegation a pleasant stay, and raised their glasses to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai, and to the prosperity and happiness of the Chinese people.

Cao Guisheng, the deputy head of the Chinese delegation, thanked the King and Queen and said that he was very happy to be in Cambodia. He said that the delegation felt extremely honored to be here working for the happiness and prosperity of the Cambodian people and for the friendship between China and Cambodia.

The reception lasted about half an hour. After the King and Queen had a cordial and frank conversation with the delegation, photographers and cinematographers took several shots.

When the Chinese economic delegation arrived at and left the Royal Palace, a guard of honor paid tribute to them.

### 柬埔寨国王接见我经济代表团

1956-09-29
第8版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨国王接见我经济代表团
    新华社河内28日电　金边消息：中华人民共和国驻金边经济代表团9月26日上午十时在柬埔寨王宫大殿中受到柬埔寨国王苏拉玛里特和王后的接见。
    接见时在场的有西哈努克亲王和其他高级官员。
    在接见时，国王和王后祝代表团生活愉快，并且举杯祝毛泽东主席和周恩来总理健康以及祝中国人民繁荣幸福。
    中国代表团副团长曹桂生感谢国王和王后，并且表示来到柬埔寨深感愉快。他说，代表团能够在这里为柬埔寨全国人民的幸福和繁荣以及为中柬两国的友谊而工作，感到极大的荣幸。
    接见的时间约半小时。在国王和王后同代表团融洽坦率地交谈之后，摄影记者和电影摄影师拍摄了几个镜头。
    中国经济代表团抵达和离开王宫时，都有一支仪仗队向他们致敬。
