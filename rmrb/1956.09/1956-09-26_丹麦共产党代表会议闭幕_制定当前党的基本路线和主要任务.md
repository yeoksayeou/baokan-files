Title: Danish Communist Party Congress Concludes: Establishes the Party's Basic Line and Main Tasks for the Present
Author:
Date: 1956-09-26
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, September 25th: TASS Copenhagen, September 24th—The Danish Communist Party Congress concluded on the 23rd. The delegates attending the meeting unanimously adopted several resolutions. One of these resolutions made a general assessment of the discussions within the party on the resolutions of the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. Another resolution stipulated the basic line and main tasks of the Danish Communist Party in the immediate period.

"Land and People" wrote that the delegates demonstrated high enthusiasm at this Party Congress. The most important issues in the work of the Danish Communist Party were mentioned and discussed in the delegates' speeches.

Danish Communist Party Chairman Larsen emphasized at the close of the Congress that the meeting proved the party's unity and vitality.

### 丹麦共产党代表会议闭幕  制定当前党的基本路线和主要任务

1956-09-26
第7版()
专栏：

    丹麦共产党代表会议闭幕
    制定当前党的基本路线和主要任务
    新华社25日讯　塔斯社哥本哈根24日讯：丹麦共产党代表会议23日闭幕。参加会议的代表们一致通过了几项决议。其中的一项决议对党内所进行的对苏联共产党第二十次代表大会的决议所作的讨论作出了总的评价。在另一项决议中规定了丹麦共产党在最近时期内的基本路线和主要任务。
    “国土与人民报”写道，在这次党代表会议上，代表们表现了很高的积极性。在代表的发言中都谈到了并且讨论了丹麦共产党工作上的一些最重要的问题。
    丹麦共产党主席拉森在代表会议闭幕时着重指出，这次会议证明了党的团结一致和党的生命力。
