Title: Chinese Economic Delegation Arrives in Phnom Penh
Author:
Date: 1956-09-26
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 24th: Phnom Penh Dispatch: The Chinese Economic Delegation to Phnom Penh, led by delegation head Ye Jinghao, arrived in Phnom Penh, the capital of the Kingdom of Cambodia, on September 21st. Cambodian senior officials and several thousand overseas Chinese welcomed them. This economic delegation was sent by China to the Kingdom of Cambodia in accordance with the Agreement on Economic Aid between China and Cambodia signed in Beijing on June 21 this year and the Protocol on the implementation of the Agreement on Economic Aid.

### 我国经济代表团到达金边

1956-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我国经济代表团到达金边
    新华社河内24日电　金边消息：中国驻金边经济代表团已经在9月21日在团长叶景灏率领下到达柬埔寨王国首都金边。欢迎他们的有柬埔寨的高级官员和几千名华侨。这个经济代表团是根据今年6月21日在北京签订的中柬两国关于经济援助的协定和实施经济援助协定的议定书的规定，由中国派到柬埔寨王国去的。
