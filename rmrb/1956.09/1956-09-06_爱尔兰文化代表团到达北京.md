Title: Irish Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 5th. An eight-member Irish Cultural Delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of September 4th. Representatives from the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Chinese Writers Association, and other organizations welcomed them at the airport.

### 爱尔兰文化代表团到达北京

1956-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    爱尔兰文化代表团到达北京
    新华社5日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问的爱尔兰文化代表团一行八人，在9月4日下午到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国人民对外文化协会、中国作家协会等单位的代表。
