Historical City – Zunyi

Zunyi City, Guizhou Province, is the location where the Chinese Communist Party held the enlarged meeting of the Central Political Bureau in January 1935. To commemorate this historically significant meeting, the people of Zunyi established the Zunyi Conference Memorial Hall at the original site, for people to admire and visit.

After several years of construction, this ancient historical city has become more prosperous and magnificent.

    历史名城—遵义
    贵州省遵义市是中国共产党在1935年1月召开的中央政治局扩大会议的所在地。遵义人民为纪念这次有历史意义的会议，在原会址建立了遵义会议纪念馆，供人们瞻仰和参观。
    这座古老的历史名城，经过几年来的建设，市面日见繁荣，显得更壮丽了。
