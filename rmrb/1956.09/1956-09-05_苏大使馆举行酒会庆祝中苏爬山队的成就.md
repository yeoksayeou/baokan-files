Title: Soviet Embassy Holds Reception to Celebrate Achievements of Sino-Soviet Mountaineering Team
Author:
Date: 1956-09-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 4th. The Soviet Embassy in China's Chargé d'affaires ad interim, Li Haiqiu, held a reception tonight to celebrate the victory of the Sino-Soviet mixed mountaineering team in climbing Mount Muztagh Ata.

    All members and staff of the mountaineering team were invited to attend the reception.

    Li Haiqiu said in his speech at the reception that the Sino-Soviet mixed mountaineering team climbed Mount Muztagh Ata, which had never been conquered by mankind, and achieved a victory of great significance. This victory of the young Chinese mountaineers on the eve of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China is the best gift to the Congress.

    Berezhnoi, the 48-year-old captain of the Sino-Soviet mixed mountaineering team, said at the reception that the minor achievements made by the Sino-Soviet mountaineering team this time have been highly praised by the people. The Sino-Soviet mountaineers will not be dizzy with success by this victory. Berezhnoi thanked the governments and people of China and the Soviet Union for their support and care.

    Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Deputy Directors of the Sports Commission Cai Tingkai, Cai Shufan, Huang Qixiang, Rong Gaotang, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Dong Xin, Zhang Xiuzhu and others were also invited to attend the reception.

    After the reception, the mountaineering team members also attended a joint soiree held for them by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 苏大使馆举行酒会庆祝中苏爬山队的成就

1956-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    苏大使馆举行酒会庆祝中苏爬山队的成就
    新华社4日讯　苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办李海秋今晚举行酒会，庆祝中、苏混合爬山队攀登慕士塔格山的胜利。
    爬山队全体队员和工作人员应邀出席了酒会。
    李海秋在酒会上致词说，中、苏混合爬山队登上了从来没有被人类征服过的慕士塔格山，取得了有巨大意义的胜利。年轻的中国爬山运动员在中国共产党第八次全国代表大会前夕取得的这个胜利，正是给大会最好的献礼。
    中、苏混合爬山队队长、四十八岁的别列次基在酒会上说，中、苏爬山队这次取得的微小成就，已经得到人们很高的评价，中、苏爬山队员不会为这次胜利冲昏头脑。别列次基感谢中、苏两国政府和人民对他们的支持和关怀。
    外交部副部长姬鹏飞，体育运动委员会副主任蔡廷锴、蔡树藩、黄琪翔、荣高棠，中华全国总工会书记处书记董昕、张修竹等人也应邀出席了酒会。
    酒会以后，爬山队队员们还出席了中苏友好协会和中华全国总工会为他们举办的联欢晚会。
