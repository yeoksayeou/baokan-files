Title: India and Vietnam Conclude Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-09-24
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, September 23rd: India and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam concluded an agreement on the development of trade in New Delhi on September 22nd.

According to this three-year agreement, India will import cement, livestock, timber, fruit, limestone, and kaolin from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; the Democratic Republic of Vietnam will import various machines, electrical equipment, agricultural implements, jute products, fabrics, clothing, and rubber from India.

### 印度和越南缔结贸易协定

1956-09-24
第7版()
专栏：

    印度和越南缔结贸易协定
    新华社新德里23日电　印度和越南民主共和国9月22日在新德里缔结了关于发展贸易的协定。
    根据这个为期三年的协定，印度将从越南民主共和国输入水泥、牲畜、木材、水果、石灰石和白瓷土等；越南民主共和国将从印度输入各种机器、电气器材、农具、黄麻制品、布匹、服装和橡胶等。
