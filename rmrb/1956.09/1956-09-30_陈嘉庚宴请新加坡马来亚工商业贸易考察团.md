Title: Tan Kah Kee Hosts Banquet for Singapore-Malaya Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-09-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: Tan Kah Kee hosted a banquet tonight to entertain all members of the Singapore and Federation of Malaya Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation.

Tan Kah Kee said in his speech at the beginning of the banquet that the visit of the Singapore and Federation of Malaya Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation to China will surely enable the normal trade and friendly exchanges between China and Singapore and Malaya to achieve significant development on a new basis.

The head of the Singapore Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation, Ko Teck Kin, and the head of the Federation of Malaya Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation, Lee Yan Lian, both said in their speeches that the trade work they have conducted in China has already achieved considerable results, and they are also very pleased to see the construction of the new China.

Those invited to accompany them tonight included He Xiangning, Chairman of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, Tao Zhu, Governor of Guangdong Province, Ye Fei, Governor of Fujian Province, Chen Qiyou, Chairman of the China Zhi Gong Party, and many other prominent figures.

### 陈嘉庚宴请新加坡马来亚工商业贸易考察团

1956-09-30
第2版()
专栏：

    陈嘉庚宴请新加坡马来亚工商业贸易考察团
    据新华社29日讯　陈嘉庚今晚设宴招待新加坡和马来亚联合邦工商业贸易考察团全体人员。
    陈嘉庚在宴会开始时致词说，这次新加坡和马来亚联合邦工商业贸易考察团的到中国访问，必将使中国同新加坡和马来亚的正常贸易和人民的友好往来在新的基础上获得重大发展。
    新加坡工商业贸易考察团团长高德根和马来亚联合邦工商业贸易考察团团长李延年在致词时都说，他们这次在中国所进行的贸易工作已经取得相当的成果，并且还很高兴地看到了新中国建设的情况。
    今晚被邀作陪的有华侨事务委员会主任何香凝，广东省省长陶铸，福建省省长叶飞，中国致公党主席陈其尤以及其他许多著名人士。
