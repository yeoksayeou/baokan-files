Title: President of the British International Trade Promotion Association, Boyd-Orr, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: At the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, the President of the British International Trade Promotion Committee, Boyd-Orr, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to China.  Chairman Nan Han Chen of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and his wife, and Vice Chairman Ji Chao Ding and his wife, were at the airport to greet them.

That evening, Nan Han Chen hosted a banquet to welcome Mr. and Mrs. Boyd-Orr.

### 英国际贸易促进会主席波伊德—奥尔到京

1956-09-30
第3版()
专栏：

    英国际贸易促进会主席波伊德—奥尔到京
    新华社29日讯　应中国国际贸易促进委员会的邀请前来中国访问的英国国际贸易促进委员会主席波伊德—奥尔今天下午乘飞机到达北京。前往机场迎接的有中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸及夫人和副主席冀朝鼎及夫人等。
    当晚，南汉宸设宴欢迎波伊德—奥尔夫妇。
