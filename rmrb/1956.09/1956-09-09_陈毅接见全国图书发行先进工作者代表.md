Title: Chen Yi Receives Representatives of National Advanced Workers in Book Distribution
Author:
Date: 1956-09-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. Vice Premier of the State Council, Chen Yi, received all the representatives attending the National Conference of Advanced Workers in Book Distribution today. He encouraged the representatives and all book distribution workers to further improve their work, strive to advance towards science, and work hard to do a good job in book distribution in order to implement the policy of "Let a hundred flowers bloom, let a hundred schools of thought contend."

### 陈毅接见全国图书发行先进工作者代表

1956-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅接见全国图书发行先进工作者代表
    新华社8日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天接见了出席全国图书发行先进工作者代表会议的全体代表。他鼓励代表们和全体图书发行工作者进一步改进工作，为向科学进军，为贯彻“百花齐放，百家争鸣”的方针，努力做好图书发行工作。
