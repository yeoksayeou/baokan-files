Title: Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation Holds Talks with Responsible Persons from Relevant Government Departments
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. The Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation today held group discussions with responsible persons from relevant Chinese government departments on issues such as the transformation of capitalist industry and commerce in China. The topics of the group discussions also included national defense, education, finance, banking, social undertakings, and social welfare.

The Indonesian Parliamentary Delegation returned to Beijing yesterday from Shanghai. Since September 22nd, the delegation has visited the three cities of Nanjing, Hangzhou, and Shanghai. During their time in Shanghai, they visited factories and held discussions with people from industrial and commercial circles.

### 印度尼西亚国会代表团  同我政府各有关部门负责人座谈

1956-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国会代表团
    同我政府各有关部门负责人座谈
    新华社28日讯　印度尼西亚共和国国会代表团在今天分组就中国资本主义工商业改造等问题同中国政府各有关部门负责人座谈。分组座谈的问题还有国防、教育、财政、金融、社会事业和社会福利问题。
    印度尼西亚国会代表团是在昨天从上海回到北京的。从9月22日以来，代表团访问了南京、杭州、上海三个城市。他们在上海时曾经参观了工厂，并且同工商界人士举行了座谈。
