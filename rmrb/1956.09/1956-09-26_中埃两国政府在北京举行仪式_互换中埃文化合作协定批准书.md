Title: Sino-Egyptian Governments Hold Ceremony in Beijing to Exchange Instruments of Ratification for Sino-Egyptian Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-09-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: The governments of China and Egypt exchanged instruments of ratification for the Sino-Egyptian Cultural Cooperation Agreement in Beijing on September 25th. Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and Hassan Ragab, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Egypt to the People's Republic of China, signed the protocol on the exchange of instruments of ratification on behalf of their respective governments at the ceremony held this morning.

On the Chinese side, those who attended the ceremony included: Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture; Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture; Liu Zizai, Vice Minister of Higher Education; Burhan, head of the former Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation that visited Egypt; Ma Yuhuai, deputy head; Ke Hua, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Yan Baohang, Chairman of the Treaty Committee; and Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations.

On the Egyptian side, those who attended the ceremony included: Far, Commercial Representative of the Embassy of the Republic of Egypt to the People's Republic of China, and Meng Dawei, Deputy Commercial Representative.

The Sino-Egyptian Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed in Cairo on April 15, 1956, and was approved by Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, on June 14, 1956, and by Gamal Abdel Nasser, President of the Republic of Egypt, on May 24, 1956, respectively.

### 中埃两国政府在北京举行仪式  互换中埃文化合作协定批准书

1956-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中埃两国政府在北京举行仪式
    互换中埃文化合作协定批准书
    新华社25日讯　中埃两国政府25日在北京互换中埃文化合作协定批准书。中华人民共和国外交部副部长章汉夫和埃及共和国驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使哈桑·拉加卜在今天上午举行的仪式上，分别代表中埃两国政府签署了关于互换批准书的议定书。
    中国方面参加仪式的有：文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长夏衍，高等教育部副部长刘子载，曾经访问埃及的前中国文化艺术代表团团长鲍尔汉、副团长马玉槐，外交部西亚非洲司司长柯华，条约委员会主任委员阎宝航，对外文化联络局代局长陈忠经。
    埃及方面参加仪式的有：埃及共和国驻中华人民共和国大使馆商务代表法尔和商务副代表孟大维。
    中埃文化合作协定于1956年4月15日在开罗签订，并且先后在1956年6月14日和1956年5月24日分别经中华人民共和国主席毛泽东和埃及共和国总统加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔批准。
