Title: Anshan Iron and Steel Cadres Support Wuhan Iron and Steel

Author:

Date: 1956-09-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, 4th. Another 150-plus production and technical leading cadres from Anshan Iron and Steel went to the Wuhan Iron and Steel Base on the 1st to participate in construction work. They are all engineers, section chiefs, workshop directors, etc. with practical work experience.

So far, Anshan Iron and Steel has transferred more than 350 leading cadres at all levels to Baotou Iron and Steel Company and Wuhan Iron and Steel Company this year.

### 鞍钢干部支援武钢

1956-09-06
第2版()
专栏：

    鞍钢干部支援武钢
    据新华社鞍山4日电　又有一百五十多名鞍钢的生产技术领导干部，在1日去武汉钢铁基地参加建设工作。他们都是有实际工作经验的工程师、科长、车间主任等。
    至此，鞍钢今年抽调给包头钢铁公司、武汉钢铁公司的各级领导干部，已经有三百五十多人。
