Title: Chairman Mao Receives Delegation of Former Japanese Soldiers

Author:
Date: 1956-09-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received the entire delegation of former Japanese soldiers visiting China at the Qin Zheng Hall in Zhongnanhai this evening.

Present at the meeting were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Vice President Qiao Guanhua, and Council Members Liao Chengzhi and Zhao Anbo. (Attached picture)

Chairman Mao with the entire delegation of former Japanese soldiers visiting China. Xinhua News Agency reporter Hou Bo, Lü Houmin.

### 毛主席接见日本前军人代表团

1956-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见日本前军人代表团
    新华社4日讯　中华人民共和国毛泽东主席，今天晚间在中南海勤政殿接见了日本前军人访华代表团全体人员。
    接见的时候在座的，有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、副会长乔冠华和理事廖承志、赵安博。（附图片）
    毛主席同日本前军人访华代表团全体人员合影。　新华社记者　侯波、吕厚民摄
