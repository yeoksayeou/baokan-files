Title: Bulganin Sends Congratulations to the Conference on the Statute of the International Atomic Energy Agency, Hoping the Conference Can Promote International Cooperation in the Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1956-09-25
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: According to TASS, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, sent a telegram congratulating the opening of the conference discussing and approving the Statute of the International Atomic Energy Agency.

In his congratulatory telegram, Bulganin said: "The extensive use of atomic energy for peaceful purposes can create great possibilities for the happy life of people of all countries, especially for the people of economically backward countries. Of course, the prohibition of atomic and hydrogen weapons can create the most favorable conditions for the peaceful use of atomic energy. The Soviet Union will continue to work towards this goal."

Bulganin wished the conference success in promoting international cooperation in the peaceful use of atomic energy among countries based on the principles of equality and respect for national sovereignty.

According to a report by the "Christian Science Monitor" correspondent at the United Nations headquarters, United Nations officials were satisfied with Bulganin's telegram, believing that it proved the Soviet Union's policy was to cooperate with the Atomic Energy Agency.

According to this reporter, at the same time, Western diplomatic circles were uneasy about the increasing recognition of the Soviet Union's policy in this regard.

Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 22nd (TASS): The conference discussing and approving the Statute of the International Atomic Energy Agency held a plenary meeting on the 21st and adopted the report of the Coordination Committee.

The next meeting is scheduled for the morning of September 24th.

### 布尔加宁电贺国际原子能机构章程会议  希望会议能促进和平利用原子能的国际合作

1956-09-25
第7版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁电贺国际原子能机构章程会议
    希望会议能促进和平利用原子能的国际合作
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社消息：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁打电报祝贺讨论和批准国际原子能机构章程会议的开幕。
    布尔加宁在贺电里说：“广泛地把原子能用于和平目的，可以为各国人民、特别是为经济落后国家的人民的幸福生活创造很大的可能性。当然，禁止原子武器和氢武器能为和平使用原子能创造最有利的条件。苏联仍然将为达到这一目的而继续努力。”
    布尔加宁预祝会议取得成就，以促进各国根据平等和尊重民族主权的原则在和平利用原子能方面进行的国际合作。
    据“基督教科学箴言报”驻联合国总部记者报道，联合国人士对布尔加宁的电报感到满意，认为这证明了苏联的政策是要同原子能机构合作。
    据这位记者报道，同时，西方外交界人士对苏联在这一方面的政策得到日益众多的承认一事感到不安。
    新华社23日讯　塔斯社纽约22日讯：讨论和批准国际原子能机构章程的会议21日举行了全体会议，通过了协作委员会的报告。
    下次会议定9月24日上午举行。
