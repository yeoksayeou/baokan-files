Title: Soviet Union Transfers "Manchuria" Archives to China; Signing Ceremony Held in Moscow
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-21
Page: 7

    Soviet Union Transfers "Manchuria" Archives to China
    Signing Ceremony Held in Moscow

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th – A signing ceremony for the Soviet government to transfer a set of so-called Manchurian archives to the Chinese government was held in Moscow on September 18th.
    Representing the Soviet side was Belov, Director of the Main Archives Administration of the Soviet Union, and representing the Chinese side was Wen Ning, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union.
    This set of "Manchuria" archives includes documents from various government offices in China's Heilongjiang and Jilin provinces from 1675 to 1900. It was taken from Qiqihar by the Tsarist Russian army in 1901. The Soviet Council of Ministers decided to transfer it to China on August 21st of this year.

### 苏联移交“满洲”档案给我国  签字仪式在莫斯科举行

1956-09-21
第7版()
专栏：

    苏联移交“满洲”档案给我国
    签字仪式在莫斯科举行
    新华社莫斯科19日电　苏联政府把一套所谓满洲的档案移交给中国政府的签字仪式，9月18日在莫斯科举行。
    代表苏联方面签字的是苏联档案总管理局局长别洛夫，代表中国方面签字的是中国驻苏联大使馆参赞温宁。
    这套“满洲”档案包括中国黑龙江和吉林两省各衙署从1675年到1900年的文献资料。它是沙俄军队1901年从齐齐哈尔运走的。今年8月21日苏联部长会议决定把它移交给中国。
