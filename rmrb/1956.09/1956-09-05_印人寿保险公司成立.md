Title: Establishment of Indian Life Insurance Corporation
Author:
Date: 1956-09-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, September 3rd: The Life Insurance Corporation of India was established on September 1st.

This company was established in accordance with the recently passed Life Insurance Corporation Act. It will take over all Indian life insurance companies and foreign life insurance companies operating in India. These companies have been placed under the management and control of the Indian government according to an order issued by the President of India on January 19th of this year.

H.M. Patel, Secretary of the Ministry of Finance, has been appointed as the manager of this company.

Former Indian Finance Minister Deshmukh once believed that the nationalization of life insurance enterprises was a milestone on India's road to a socialist-style society.

### 印人寿保险公司成立

1956-09-05
第6版()
专栏：

    印人寿保险公司成立
    新华社新德里3日电　印度全国人寿保险公司已经在9月1日成立。
    这个公司是根据最近通过的人寿保险公司法成立的。它将接管所有的印度人寿保险公司以及在印度营业的外国人寿保险公司。根据印度总统今年1月19日颁布的法令，这些公司都已经置于印度政府的管理和控制之下。
    财政部秘书赫·姆·巴特尔已经被任命为这个公司的经理。
    印度前财政部长德希穆克曾经认为人寿保险企业的国有化是印度走向社会主义式样社会的道路上的一个里程碑。
