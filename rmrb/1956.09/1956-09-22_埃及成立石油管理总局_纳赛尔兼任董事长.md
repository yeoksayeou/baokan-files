Title: Egypt Establishes General Petroleum Administration; Nasser Concurrently Serves as Chairman
Author:
Date: 1956-09-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 21st: The Egyptian government announced on September 20th a decree establishing a General Petroleum Administration. This General Petroleum Administration, under the Ministry of Industry, will handle all issues related to Egypt's petroleum production, refining, and distribution.

Agence France-Presse reports that Western sources in Cairo believe this law may be preparing the ground for the nationalization of the petroleum industry.

According to the newly promulgated decree, this new petroleum agency will be responsible for managing the government-owned Suez refinery and organizing all public organizations related to petroleum or petroleum products. It can also cooperate with other companies in Egypt or abroad that are related to the petroleum industry.

Article 1 of the decree states that the General Petroleum Administration may purchase, merge with, or join companies.

Egyptian President Nasser has been appointed as the Chairman of the Board of Directors of the General Petroleum Administration.

### 埃及成立石油管理总局  纳赛尔兼任董事长

1956-09-22
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及成立石油管理总局
    纳赛尔兼任董事长
    新华社21日讯　开罗消息：埃及政府9月20日公布了一项设立一个石油管理总局的法令。这个属于工业部管理的石油管理总局将处理一切有关埃及石油生产、制炼和分配的问题。
    法新社说，开罗的西方人士认为这条法律可能是为石油工业国有化作好准备。
    按照新颁布的法令，这个新石油机构将负责管理属于政府的苏伊士炼油厂和组织一切和石油或石油产品有关的公共组织。它还可以和埃及的或国外的其他同石油工业有关的公司合作。
    法令的第一条说，石油管理总局可以收买、合并或者加入公司。
    埃及总统纳赛尔被任命为石油管理总局董事会董事长。
