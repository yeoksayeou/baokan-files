Title: Lebanese President and Libyan Prime Minister Protest French Troop Deployment to Cyprus
Author:
Date: 1956-09-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 6th: TASS Beirut reported: According to the "Beirut Daily" news, Lebanese President Camille Chamoun summoned the French Ambassador to Beirut on September 4th and requested him to explain the French military's actions in assembling in Cyprus. The President reiterated that all Arab countries support Egypt on the issue of Egypt nationalizing the Suez Canal Company.

Xinhua News Agency, September 6th: TASS Beirut, September 6th: Representatives of various Lebanese political parties and organizations held a meeting on September 4th in the resort city of Bhamdoun to study the situation caused by the landing of French troops in Cyprus and the concentration of Western warships in the Mediterranean.

	"Telegraph" said that the meeting decided to request the government to prohibit Western warships from entering the port of Beirut. The meeting called on all Lebanese citizens to use their full strength to defend national sovereignty.

Xinhua News Agency, September 5th: Tripoli, Libya: Libya today lodged a protest with France regarding the French troop deployment to Cyprus.

	Libyan Prime Minister Mustafa Ben Halim summoned the French Minister to Libya and handed him the letter of protest.

	Western news agencies quoted Halim as saying that while President Gamal Abdel Nasser and Menzies' "Five-Nation Committee" were negotiating the future of the Suez Canal, the French military's deployment to Cyprus was a threat to Egypt.

	Halim also summoned the U.S. Ambassador, expressing Libya's concern about the consequences that this French military movement might cause.

### 黎巴嫩总统和利比亚首相  抗议法国派兵到塞浦路斯

1956-09-07
第6版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩总统和利比亚首相
    抗议法国派兵到塞浦路斯
    新华社6日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特讯：“贝鲁特报”消息，据报道，黎巴嫩总统米加耶·夏蒙在9月4日召见了法国驻贝鲁特大使，要求他解释法国军队在塞浦路斯集结的行动。总统重申，所有阿拉伯国家在埃及把苏彝士运河公司收归国有这个问题上都支持埃及。
    新华社6日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特6日讯：黎巴嫩各政党和团体的代表9月4日在疗养城市巴哈姆顿举行了一次会议，研究法国军队在塞浦路斯登陆和西方国家军舰在地中海集中所引起的局势。
      “电讯报”说，会议决定要求政府禁止西方国家的军舰驶入贝鲁特港。会议呼吁黎巴嫩全体公民用全力来保卫国家主权。
    新华社5日讯　利比亚的黎波里消息：利比亚今天就法国调遣军队到塞浦路斯一事向法国提出抗议。
    利比亚首相穆斯塔法·本·哈利姆召见法国驻利比亚公使，把抗议书交给他。
    西方通讯社引述哈利姆的话说，在阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔总统和孟席斯的“五国委员会”正在就苏彝士运河前途问题进行谈判的时候，法军到塞浦路斯是对埃及的一个威胁。
    哈利姆还召见了美国大使，表示利比亚关心法国军队这一调动可能引起的后果。
