    Aphorisms
    Victor Hugo (1802-1885)
    When a person is in deep thought, he is not idle. There is visible labor, and there is invisible labor.
            ＊
    Nothing resembles the human mind more closely than a bee. It flies from this flower to that flower, just as the mind flies from this planet to that planet. The bee gathers nectar as it flies, just as the mind gathers light as it flies.
    Henrik Ibsen (1828-1906)
    Society is like a ship, everyone should be prepared to steer.
          ＊
    The spirit of adhering to truth and loving freedom – that is the pillar of society.
                  ＊
    I believe that justice belongs to those who are most closely connected with the future.
              (Compiled and translated by Zhu Fu)

    雨果（1802——1885）
    当一个人在深思的时候，他并不是在闲着。有看得见的劳动，也有看不见的劳动。
            ＊
    最酷似人类心灵的，莫如蜜蜂了。它从这朵花飞到那朵花，就像心灵从这个星球飞到那个星球。蜜蜂随飞随搜集蜜汁，正像心灵随飞随搜集光明。
    易卜生（1828——1906）
    社会有如一条船，每个人都应该有掌舵的准备。
          ＊
    坚持真理和热爱自由的精神——这就是社会的栋梁。
                  ＊
    我认为正义是属于那些跟未来结合得最紧密的人们的。
              （柱父辑译）
