Title: Chinese Communist Party Leaders Host Reception for Delegates from Fraternal Parties

Author:
Date: 1956-09-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 14th. Chinese Communist Party leaders Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Kang Sheng, Peng Zhen, Dong Biwu, Lin Boqu, Zhang Wentian, Peng Dehuai, and Deng Xiaoping hosted a reception this evening to welcome delegates from communist and workers' parties of various countries who were invited to attend the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China.

	At 6:30 PM, delegates from communist and workers' parties of various countries arrived at the CPPCC Auditorium. Comrades Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, and others greeted the guests in the hall and shook hands with them one by one.

	At the reception, Comrade Liu Shaoqi, on behalf of the CPC Central Committee, extended a warm welcome to the delegates of fraternal parties who were invited to attend the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China, expressed gratitude to the communist and workers' parties of various countries for sending them to participate in this congress, and conveyed high respect to the fraternal parties and peoples of various countries through them.

	Wang Jiaxiang, member of the CPC Central Committee and responsible staff member of the CPC Central Committee, introduced the agenda and other relevant matters of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China to the guests on behalf of the CPC Central Committee.

	Then, Comrade Mao Zedong extended a warm welcome to the delegates of fraternal parties and raised a glass to wish them good health.

	At the reception, the CPC Central leaders and Chinese personnel present, accompanying the guests, engaged in cordial conversations with the delegates of fraternal parties.

	The delegations from the communist and workers' parties of forty-six countries attending the reception this evening included: the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Polish United Workers' Party, the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, the Romanian Workers' Party, the French Communist Party, the Italian Communist Party, the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, the Albanian Party of Labour, the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, the Bulgarian Communist Party, the Hungarian Working People's Party, the Communist Party of Indonesia, the Communist Party of India, the Workers' Party of Korea, the Vietnam Workers’ Party, the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, the Communist Party of Great Britain, the Communist Party of Spain, the German Communist Party, the Communist Party of Argentina, the Communist Party of Belgium, the Labor-Progressive Party of Canada, the Communist Party of Finland, the Communist Party of Australia, the People’s Party of Iran, the Communist Party of Turkey, the Communist Party of Norway, the Ceylon Communist Party, the Communist Party of Nepal, the Communist Party of Sweden, the Communist Party of Mexico, the Communist Party of Austria, the Swiss Party of Labour, the Communist Party of Denmark, the Communist Party of New Zealand, the Communist Party of Ecuador, the Uruguayan Communist Party, the Israeli Communist Party, the Icelandic United Socialist Party and observers such as the Communist Party of the United States.

	Those present at the reception to accompany the guests included members of the CPC Central Committee: Li Fuchun, He Long, Chen Yi, Lu Dingyi, Li Xiannian, Nie Rongzhen, Ye Jianying, Cai Chang, Deng Yingchao, Liao Chengzhi, Wang Jiaxiang, Chen Boda, and others.

### 中共领导人设酒会欢迎各国兄弟党代表

1956-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中共领导人设酒会欢迎各国兄弟党代表
    新华社14日讯　中国共产党的领导人毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、陈云、康生、彭真、董必武、林伯渠、张闻天、彭德怀、邓小平，在今天晚间，设酒会欢迎应邀参加中国共产党第八次全国代表大会的各国共产党和工人党的代表。
    六时三十分，各国共产党和工人党的代表来到了政协礼堂，毛泽东同志、刘少奇同志、周恩来同志、朱德同志和陈云同志等都在大厅里迎接客人们，并且同客人们一一握手。
    在酒会上，刘少奇同志代表中共中央委员会向应邀前来参加中国共产党第八次全国代表大会的各兄弟党的代表们表示热烈的欢迎，对各国共产党和工人党派他们来参加这次代表大会表示感谢，并通过他们向各国兄弟党和各国人民表示崇高的敬意。
    中共中央委员、中共中央负责工作人员王稼祥代表中共中央委员会把中国共产党第八次全国代表大会的日程和其他有关事项向客人们作了介绍。
    接着，毛泽东同志对各国兄弟党的代表们表示热烈的欢迎，并且举杯祝各国兄弟党代表们的健康。
    酒会上，中共中央领导人和中国方面出席作陪的人员，同各兄弟党的代表们互相进行亲切的交谈。
    今天晚间出席酒会的四十六个国家的共产党和工人党的代表团中有：苏联共产党代表团，波兰统一工人党代表团，德国统一社会党代表团，罗马尼亚工人党代表团，法国共产党代表团，意大利共产党代表团，捷克斯洛伐克共产党代表团，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表团，蒙古人民革命党代表团，保加利亚共产党代表团，匈牙利劳动人民党代表团，印度尼西亚共产党代表团，印度共产党代表团，朝鲜劳动党代表团，越南劳动党代表团，南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟代表团，英国共产党代表团，西班牙共产党代表团，德国共产党代表团，阿根廷共产党代表团，比利时共产党代表团，加拿大劳工进步党代表团，芬兰共产党代表团，澳大利亚共产党代表团，伊朗人民党代表团，土耳其共产党代表，挪威共产党代表团，锡兰共产党代表团，尼泊尔共产党代表团，瑞典共产党代表团，墨西哥共产党代表团，奥地利共产党代表团，瑞士劳动党代表团，丹麦共产党代表团，新西兰共产党代表团，厄瓜多尔共产党代表团，乌拉圭共产党代表，以色列共产党代表团，冰岛统一社会党代表团和美国共产党列席观察员等。
    出席酒会作陪的，有中共中央委员李富春、贺龙、陈毅、陆定一、李先念、聂荣臻、叶剑英、蔡畅、邓颖超、廖承志、王稼祥、陈伯达等。
