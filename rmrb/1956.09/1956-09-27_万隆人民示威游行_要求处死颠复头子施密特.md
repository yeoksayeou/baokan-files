Title: Bandung People Demonstrate, Demanding the Execution of Subversion Leader Schmidt
Author:
Date: 1956-09-27
Page: 8
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 25th: Antara News Agency reported that tens of thousands of people from all walks of life in Bandung held an anti-subversion demonstration on September 23rd. The resolution passed by the mass meeting demanded that Schmidt, the Dutch subversion ringleader currently on trial, be sentenced to death. The conference also called on the government to take firm steps to deal with colonialists.
The delegation of the anti-subversion demonstration met with Supreme Judge Wirjono Prodjodikoro and Attorney General Suprapto on September 24th and handed them the resolution. The Supreme Judge promised to convey the wishes of the masses to the judges presiding over Schmidt's trial.

### 万隆人民示威游行  要求处死颠复头子施密特

1956-09-27
第8版()
专栏：

    万隆人民示威游行
    要求处死颠复头子施密特
    据新华社雅加达25日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，万隆各界成千上万的人在9月23日举行反颠复示威游行。群众大会通过的决议要求把现正在受审的荷兰颠复头子施密特处以死刑。大会还要求政府采取坚定的步骤来对付殖民主义分子。
    反颠复示威游行的代表团在9月24日会见了最高法官维尔约诺·普罗佐迪科罗和检察总长苏普拉普托，把决议交给他们。最高法官答应把群众的愿望转达给主持审讯施密特的法官。
