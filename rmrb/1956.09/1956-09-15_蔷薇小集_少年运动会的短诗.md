Title: Rose Collection – Short Poems from the Youth Games
Author: Sha Ou
Date: 1956-09-15
Page: 8

    Rose Collection
    —Short Poems from the Youth Games
    Sha Ou
    Fan Dance
    This flock of butterflies,
    Flies in, carrying the fragrance of Beijing's flowers.
    Gently landing on the flower bushes,
    Facing the wind, they fly to the grass again...
    Flying in music like a mountain spring,
    Flying in people's smiles.
     Archery
    This arrow still carries the breath of the prairie,
    Shot out from between the girl's fingers.
    Naughty sea breeze,
    Aren't you afraid of being blown away!
    With a sound, praise rushes in like the tide,
    The girl's smiling face is like a hibiscus flower.
    The competition field is like paratroopers just touching the ground,
    Like white snowball flowers just blooming.
    Beside the parachute,
    Eagles are tumbling;
    Beside the flowers,
    Swallows are flying.
    I don't know where to look,
    Everywhere is a painting—
    With the most vibrant colors,
    The most beautiful lines...
      August 1956, in Qingdao

### 蔷薇小集——少年运动会的短诗
沙鸥
1956-09-15
第8版()
专栏：

    蔷薇小集
    ——少年运动会的短诗
    沙鸥
    扇子操
    这一群蝴蝶，
    带着北京的花香飞来。
    轻轻地落在花丛上，
    迎着风又向草地飞去……
    飞在山泉一样的音乐里，
    飞在人们的微笑中。
     射箭
    这支箭还带着草原的气息，
    从姑娘的指缝间射出去。
    调皮的海风，
    不怕你吹呵！
    一声响，赞美像海潮袭来，
    姑娘的笑脸是一朵芙蓉。
    竞赛场上像伞兵刚刚挨着地面，
    像白绣球花刚刚开放。
    在伞的旁边，
    鹰在翻腾；
    在花的旁边，
    燕子在飞舞。
    我不知道看哪里好，
    哪里都是一幅画——
    有最鲜艳的色彩，
    最美的线条……
      1956年8月于青岛
