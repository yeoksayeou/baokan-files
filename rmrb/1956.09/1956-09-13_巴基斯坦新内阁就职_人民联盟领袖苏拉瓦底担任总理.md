Title: Pakistan's New Cabinet Takes Office; Awami League Leader Suhrawardy Becomes Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1956-09-13
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, September 12th: The new Pakistani cabinet, headed by Awami League leader Huseyn Shaheed Suhrawardy, was sworn in this morning in Karachi.

The new cabinet currently has nine members, including the Prime Minister. Five are members of the Republican Party and four are members of the Awami League.

The Republican Party members are: Malik Firoz Noon, Syed Amjad Ali, Ghulam Ali Talpur, Sardar Amir Azam Khan, and Mian Jafar Shah.

The Awami League members are Suhrawardy (Prime Minister), Abul Mansur Ahmad, Abdul Khaliq, and Dildar Ahmed.

### 巴基斯坦新内阁就职  人民联盟领袖苏拉瓦底担任总理

1956-09-13
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦新内阁就职
    人民联盟领袖苏拉瓦底担任总理
    据新华社喀喇蚩12日电　以人民联盟领袖侯赛尼·夏希德·苏拉瓦底为首的巴基斯坦新内阁今天上午在喀喇蚩宣誓就职。
    新内阁目前连总理在内共有九个阁员。其中五个是共和党人，四个是人民联盟盟员。
    共和党阁员是：马利克·菲罗兹·努恩、赛义德·阿姆贾德·阿里、吴拉姆·阿里·塔尔普、萨达尔·阿米尔·阿扎姆汗和米安·贾法尔·沙阿。
    人民联盟阁员是苏拉瓦底（总理）、阿布勒·曼苏尔·艾哈迈德、阿卜杜勒·哈利克和迪勒达尔·艾哈迈德。
