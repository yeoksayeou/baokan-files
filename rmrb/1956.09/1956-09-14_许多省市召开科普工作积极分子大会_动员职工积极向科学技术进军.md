Many Provinces and Cities Convene Conferences of Activists in Popular Science, Mobilizing Workers to Actively Advance Towards Science and Technology

    This newspaper reports Recently, many provinces and cities have convened or are actively preparing to convene conferences of activists in popular science and technology work among workers.

    Among the thirty-one provinces and cities that have decided to convene such conferences, nine units, including Xinjiang, Anshan, Lüda, Wuxi, Chongqing, and Wuhan, had already convened their conferences by the end of August. They summarized the achievements of popular science and technology work among workers over the past few years, exchanged experiences in learning and organizing popular science and technology work, commended activists and advanced units, and selected representatives to attend the First National Conference of Activists in Popular Science and Technology Work among Workers. These activists include workers, scientific workers, engineering and technical personnel, professors, trade union club staff, workshop directors, as well as doctors and nurses. The activists attending the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region's Conference of Activists in Popular Science and Technology Work among Workers include representatives from seven ethnic groups, including Han, Uygur, Hui, and Tatar.

    Through the convening of the activists' conferences, the enthusiasm of the masses of workers for learning scientific and technological knowledge has greatly increased, and workers in various places have expressed their response to the call of the conference and actively advancing towards science and technology.

### 许多省市召开科普工作积极分子大会  动员职工积极向科学技术进军

1956-09-14
第7版()
专栏：

    许多省市召开科普工作积极分子大会
    动员职工积极向科学技术进军
    本报讯　最近一个时期，许多省市召开了或正在积极筹备召开职工科学技术普及工作积极分子大会。
    在决定召开大会的三十一个省市中，到8月底止，已经召开了大会的计有新疆、鞍山、旅大、无锡、重庆、武汉等九个单位。他们总结了几年以来职工科学技术普及工作的成就，交流了学习和组织科学技术普及工作的经验，表扬了积极分子和先进单位，并选出了出席全国第一次职工科学技术普及工作积极分子大会的代表。在这些积极分子中，有工人、科学工作人员、工程技术人员、教授、工会俱乐部工作人员、车间主任，还有医师和护士。出席新疆维吾尔自治区职工科学技术普及工作积极分子大会的积极分子中，包括汉族、维吾尔族、回族、塔塔尔族等七个民族的代表。
    通过积极分子大会的召开，职工群众学习科学技术知识的劲头更大起来，各地职工群众都表示响应大会号召，积极向科学技术进军。
