Title: Yugoslav Military Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 15th. A Yugoslav military delegation led by General Kosta Nadj, Commander of the Belgrade Military Region of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 15th. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included General Xiao Ke, Vice Minister of National Defense; General Li Kenong, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; General Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department; Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense; Vice Admiral Luo Shunchu, Deputy Commander of the Navy; Lieutenant General Chang Qiankun, Deputy Commander of the Air Force; Lieutenant General Zheng Weishan, Deputy Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command; and Senior Colonel Zhu Kaiyin, Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense, and others.

Yugoslav Ambassador to China Popovic and military attachés of various countries in China also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

The Yugoslav military delegation consists of six people. The members are Colonel Mirko Bulović, Colonel Milan Joka, Yugoslav Military Attaché to China Colonel Bruno Vuletić, Lieutenant Colonel Milivoje Stanković, and Deputy Military Attaché to China Lieutenant Stane Brovet. (Attached picture)

General Kosta Nadj (right), head of the military delegation of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, and General Xiao Ke (left), Vice Minister of National Defense, who came to welcome him, at the airport.

### 南斯拉夫军事代表团到北京

1956-09-16
第3版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫军事代表团到北京
    新华社15日讯　由南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国贝尔格莱德军区司令科斯塔·纳季上将率领的南斯拉夫军事代表团，在15日下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团是应中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀元帅邀请来我国进行友好访问的。
    到机场欢迎的，有国防部副部长萧克上将，中国人民解放军副总参谋长李克农上将，总政治部副主任萧华上将，国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将，海军副司令员罗舜初海军中将，空军副司令员常乾坤空军中将，京津卫戌司令部副司令员郑维山中将和国防部外事处长朱开印大校等人。
    南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇以及各国驻华武官也到机场欢迎。
    南斯拉夫军事代表团由六人组成。团员有米尔科·布洛维奇上校，米蓝·约卡上校，南斯拉夫驻华武官布鲁诺·武累提奇上校，米里沃耶·斯坦科维奇中校，驻华副武官斯塔内·布罗维特中尉。（附图片）
    南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国军事代表团团长科斯塔·纳季上将（右）和前往欢迎的国防部副部长萧克上将（左）在机场上。
                新华社记者　刘东鳌摄
