Title: Britain and France Take a Big Step Towards War; Ceylon Prime Minister Condemns Western "Users' Association" Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-09-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, September 14th: Colombo Dispatch: Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike said on September 13th that "Britain and France have taken a big step towards war."

He told reporters, "Establishing a Suez Canal Users' Association effectively means that Britain, France, and the United States will manage the Suez Canal. President Nasser and the Egyptian government certainly cannot but resist with force."

Bandaranaike also said that Britain did not consult with Ceylon, as a member of the Commonwealth, regarding Britain's recent actions.

### 英法朝着战争迈了一大步  锡兰总理斥责西方“使用国协会”计划

1956-09-15
第5版()
专栏：

    英法朝着战争迈了一大步
    锡兰总理斥责西方“使用国协会”计划
    新华社新德里14日电　科伦坡消息：锡兰总理班达拉奈克9月13日说，“英国和法国已经朝着战争迈了一大步”。
    他对新闻记者发表的谈话说，“建立（苏彝士）运河使用国协会事实上意味着英国、法国和美国将管理苏彝士运河。纳赛尔总统和埃及政府当然不能不以武力反抗。”
    班达拉奈克还说，英国并没有把锡兰作为英联邦成员国之一来和它就英国最近的行动进行磋商。
