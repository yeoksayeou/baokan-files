Title: Singapore People's Action Party Advocates Forming Alliance of Anti-Colonial Parties to Prepare for Negotiations with Britain Next Year for Singapore's Independence
Author:
Date: 1956-09-01
Page: 6

    Singapore People's Action Party Advocates
    Forming Alliance of Anti-Colonial Parties
    To Prepare for Negotiations with Britain Next Year for Singapore's Independence
    Xinhua News Agency, 31st - Singapore News: The Singapore People's Action Party issued a statement on August 28th, on the eve of the opening of the Legislative Assembly, stating that all anti-colonial parties in Singapore should form an alliance to prepare for negotiations with Britain before April of next year in order to strive for Singapore's independence.
    In this lengthy statement, the People's Action Party analyzed in detail the current political situation developments in Singapore and Malaya. The statement said that Singapore and Malaya are still under colonial control, and the dangerous trend in the Federation of Malaya is the implementation of a "follow-the-leader policy."
    The statement advocated stopping the shooting war in Malaya.

### 新加坡人民行动党主张  各反殖民主义政党结成联盟  准备明年同英国谈判争取新加坡独立

1956-09-01
第6版()
专栏：

    新加坡人民行动党主张
    各反殖民主义政党结成联盟
    准备明年同英国谈判争取新加坡独立
    新华社31日讯　新加坡消息：新加坡人民行动党8月28日在立法议会开幕前夕发表文告说，目前全新加坡反殖民主义的政党应该结成联盟，准备在明年4月前同英国谈判，以争取新加坡的独立。
    人民行动党在这篇长篇文告中详细地分析了新加坡和马来亚目前政治局势的发展，文告说，新加坡和马来亚仍受殖民主义控制，马来亚联合邦的危险趋势在于实行“追随政策”。
    文告主张停止在马来亚的射击战争。
