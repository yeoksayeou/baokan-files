Title: Guangzhou Gymnasium Begins Construction
Author:
Date: 1956-09-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 7th. Construction began on the Guangzhou Gymnasium on the 6th. The gymnasium will be second in size only to the Beijing Gymnasium. The entire project is scheduled to be completed in January next year.

This gymnasium is being built near Liuhua Bridge, next to Yuexiu Mountain in Guangzhou. Upon completion, the gymnasium will be 120 meters long and 85 meters wide, with a construction area of over 16,000 square meters. The competition hall, under the central arched roof, can accommodate more than 5,000 spectators. The competition area is 23 meters wide and 36 meters long, and can be used for basketball, volleyball, tennis, badminton, gymnastics and other competitions.

The gymnasium will also have table tennis rooms, billiards rooms, boxing rooms, weightlifting rooms, gymnastics corridors, and training halls.

### 广州市体育馆动工兴建

1956-09-08
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    广州市体育馆动工兴建
    新华社广州7日电　规模仅次于北京体育馆的广州市体育馆6日动工兴建。体育馆的全部工程预定在明年1月完成。
    这座体育馆建筑在广州市越秀山旁流花桥附近。体育馆建成后全长一百二十公尺，宽八十五公尺，建筑面积达一万六千多平方公尺。体育馆中央拱形屋顶的比赛馆，可以容纳五千多观众；比赛活动的场地宽二十三公尺，长三十六公尺，能作篮球、排球、网球、羽毛球和体操等项比赛活动。
    体育馆还将设有乒乓球室、弹子（台球）室、拳击室、举重室、体操廊、练习馆等。
