Title: Kim Ung-gi Presents Medals to Shehu and Hoxha
Author:
Date: 1956-09-13
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 12th -- Kim Ung-gi, Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, today presented Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Albania, and Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, who are visiting Korea, with the Order of the National Flag, First Class, and the Order of Freedom and Independence, each one.
　　Kim Ung-gi also presented medals to other members of the Albanian government delegation.

### 金应基赠勋章给谢胡和霍查

1956-09-13
第5版()
专栏：

    金应基赠勋章给谢胡和霍查
    新华社平壤12日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会副委员长金应基今天赠给来朝鲜访问的阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席谢胡和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查每人以一级国旗勋章和自由独立勋章各一枚。
    金应基并且赠给阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团的其他成员以勋章。
