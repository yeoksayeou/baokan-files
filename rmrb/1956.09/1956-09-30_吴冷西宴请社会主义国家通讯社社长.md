Title: Wu Lengxi Hosts Banquet for Heads of News Agencies from Socialist Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-09-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th – Wu Lengxi, Director of the Xinhua News Agency, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 29th to welcome the heads of news agencies from socialist countries who are visiting China at the invitation of the Xinhua News Agency.  Attending the banquet were members of the Beijing press and Beijing-based reporters from news agencies of socialist countries.

As of the 29th, the heads of news agencies who had arrived in Beijing included: P. F. Pargunov, Director of the Soviet TASS News Agency; Nano, Director of the Albanian Telegraph Agency; Gavrilova, Director of the Bulgarian Telegraph Agency; Balogh, Director of the Hungarian Telegraph Agency; Hoang Tuấn, Director of the Vietnam News Agency; Weiland, Director of the German News Agency; Park Mu, Director of the Korean Central News Agency; Dumitrescu, Director of the Romanian News Agency; Mechel, Deputy Director of the Czechoslovak News Agency; Hoffmann, Director of the International Department of the Polish Press Agency; and Kwieciński, Director of the Foreign Department. Stajduhar, Deputy Director of the Yugoslav News Agency, is scheduled to arrive in Beijing on the 30th.

At the welcoming banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly raised their glasses to warmly congratulate the prosperity of the news agencies and the press in all countries.

### 吴冷西宴请社会主义国家通讯社社长

1956-09-30
第2版()
专栏：

    吴冷西宴请社会主义国家通讯社社长
    新华社29日讯　新华通讯社社长吴冷西29日晚设宴欢迎应新华通讯社邀请来我国访问的社会主义国家通讯社社长。出席宴会的有北京新闻界人士和社会主义国家通讯社驻北京记者多人。
    截至29日止，各国通讯社社长到达北京的已有苏联塔斯社社长帕尔古诺夫、阿尔巴尼亚通讯社社长纳诺、保加利亚通讯社社长加夫里洛娃、匈牙利通讯社社长巴尔奇、越南通讯社社长黄俊、德意志通讯社社长韦兰德、朝鲜中央通讯社社长朴茂、罗马尼亚通讯社社长杜明特内斯库、捷克斯洛伐克通讯社副社长梅切尔、波兰通讯社国际部主任霍夫曼和对外部主任克维琴斯基。南斯拉夫通讯社副社长斯台杜哈定于30日到京。
    在欢迎宴会上，宾主一再举杯，热烈祝贺各国通讯社事业和新闻事业的繁荣。
