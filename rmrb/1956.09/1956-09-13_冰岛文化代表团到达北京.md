Title: Icelandic Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-13
Page: 1

    Icelandic Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, September 12th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the nine-member Icelandic Cultural Delegation arrived in Beijing on the 11th.
    Representatives from the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives, among others, were at the airport to welcome them.

### 冰岛文化代表团到达北京

1956-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    冰岛文化代表团到达北京
    新华社12日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的冰岛文化代表团一行九人已在11日到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国人民对外文化协会、中国人民外交学会以及中华全国供销合作总社的代表等。
