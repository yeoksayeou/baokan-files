Title: The New Look of the Ancient City of Luoyang (Picture)
Author: Tang Maolin
Date: 1956-09-07
Page: 2

The ancient city of Luoyang has taken on a completely new look. In the past few years, more than a thousand new houses, covering 680,000 square meters, have been built in the suburbs of Luoyang. With the prosperity and development of the urban area, new buildings are constantly increasing. This is a corner of the workers' dormitory area in the suburbs of Luoyang. Xinhua News Agency reporter Tang Maolin.

### 古都洛阳的新面貌（图片）
唐茂林
1956-09-07
第2版()
专栏：

    古都洛阳的新面貌
    古城洛阳的面貌已经焕然一新。几年来在洛阳市郊建筑了上千幢达六十八万平方公尺的新屋。随着市区的繁荣和发展，新的建筑物还在不断增加中。这是洛阳市郊的职工宿舍区一角。　　新华社记者　唐茂林摄
