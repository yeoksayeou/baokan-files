Title: Indonesian Bali Dance Troupe Departs for Visit to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1956-09-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 4th: An eighty-member Balinese dance troupe departed from Jakarta by ship this afternoon en route to Beijing. This largest dance troupe ever sent abroad from Bali is visiting China for three months at the invitation of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association. The dance troupe is composed of dancers from northern and southern Bali with different performance styles. Some of them have previously visited Europe and the Americas. The head of the dance troupe is Umar, Vice-Chairman of the Indonesia-China Friendship Association and a member of parliament.

### 印度尼西亚峇里舞蹈团启程来我国访问

1956-09-05
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚峇里舞蹈团启程来我国访问
    据新华社雅加达4日电　由八十人组成的一队峇里岛舞蹈团今日下午在雅加达搭船动身前往北京。这一个由峇里派往国外的最大舞蹈团，是应中国—印度尼西亚友好协会的邀请，去中国作三个月的访问的。这一舞蹈团是由有不同的表演风格的峇里北部与南部的舞蹈家们组成的。其中有几位在以前曾经访问过欧洲和美洲。舞蹈团团长是印度尼西亚—中国友好协会副主席、国会议员乌马尔。
