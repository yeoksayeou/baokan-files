Title: Czech Musicians Delegation Performs in Shenyang for the First Time
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, September 4th: The Czechoslovakian Musicians Delegation held its first performance in China in Shenyang on the evening of the 3rd. More than 1,300 audience members enjoyed the artists' wonderful musical program. Before the performance, Che Xiangchen, Vice Governor of Liaoning Province, and Shao Kai, Vice Mayor of Shenyang, received the delegation leader Zdanek Mikula and all the members of the delegation.

    At the concert, pianist Mi Kalin, flute soloist Hai Kashurik, cello soloist F. Smetana, oboe soloist F. Hantak, and other musicians successively performed many famous classical and modern musical pieces. The extraordinary artistry of the Czechoslovakian musicians transported the Shenyang audience to a poetic and wonderful realm. After each musician's performance, new programs were added at the enthusiastic request of the audience.

    The Czechoslovakian Musicians Delegation, a group of twelve led by delegation leader Zdanek Mikula, arrived in Shenyang by train on August 31st.

### 捷音乐家代表团在沈阳首次演出

1956-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    捷音乐家代表团在沈阳首次演出
    据新华社沈阳4日电　捷克斯洛伐克音乐家代表团于3日晚在沈阳举行访问中国的首次演奏会。有一千三百多名观众欣赏了艺术家们的美妙音乐节目。演奏前，辽宁省副省长车向忱、沈阳市副市长邵凯接见了代表团团长日丹涅克·米库拉和全体团员。
    演奏会上，钢琴家米·卡林、笛子独奏家海·卡许里克、大提琴独奏家佛·斯美坦纳、双簧管独奏家佛·汗塔克和其他音乐家们都先后演奏了许多著名的古典和现代乐曲。捷克斯洛伐克音乐家们的卓越的艺术，把沈阳的听众带入了诗一样的美妙景界中。每个音乐家演奏后，都在听众的热情请求下，加奏了新的节目。
    捷克斯洛伐克音乐家代表团一行十二人，是由团长日丹涅克·米库拉率领，在8月31日乘火车到达沈阳的。
