Title: Sukarno Arrives in Our Country Via the Soviet Union for a Visit; Will Visit the Mongolian People's Republic Soon

Author:

Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: According to TASS, President Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia and his entourage arrived in Sochi, a famous resort city in Crimea, Soviet Union, on the 26th while en route from Prague to visit China. President Sukarno and others will have a short rest there.

On September 27th, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, and Foreign Minister Shepilov, who were vacationing in Sochi, met with Sukarno and Indonesian Foreign Minister Abdulgani.

Xinhua News Agency, Sochi, September 28th: According to TASS, President Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia and his entourage departed from Sochi by plane to Kuibyshev on the 28th, preparing to travel from there to visit the People's Republic of China.

Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Gorkin, General Kazakov, Director of the Southeast Asia Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Volkov, and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Lopatin, will accompany Dr. Sukarno and his entourage all the way to the Sino-Soviet border.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, September 28th: It was announced here that Indonesian President Sukarno will visit the Mongolian People's Republic soon at the invitation of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of Mongolia.

### 苏加诺经过苏联前来我国访问  将在最近访蒙古人民共和国

1956-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    苏加诺经过苏联前来我国访问
    将在最近访蒙古人民共和国
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社消息：印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺和他的随行人员在从布拉格前往中国访问的途中，26日到达苏联的克里米亚著名休养城索契。苏加诺总统等将在那里作短期的休息。
    9月27日，正在索契休假的苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和外交部长谢皮洛夫会见了苏加诺和印度尼西亚外交部长阿卜杜加尼。
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社索契讯：印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺博士和他的随行人员28日从索契乘飞机前往古比雪夫，准备从那里前往中华人民共和国访问。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书戈尔金、卡扎科夫大将、苏联外交部东南亚司司长沃尔科夫、苏联外交部礼宾司副司长洛帕京科将送苏加诺博士和他的随行人员一直到苏中国境。
    新华社乌兰巴托28日电　此间宣布，印度尼西亚总统苏加诺应蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团的邀请，将在最近访问蒙古人民共和国。
