Title: Excellent Films Since the "May Fourth" Movement Will Be Re-printed and Publicly Screened Again
Author:
Date: 1956-09-05
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 4th: The excellent film "The Spring River Flows East" will be re-released in Beijing and other locations starting in late September. This film was made by a group of progressive filmmakers before liberation and was once loved by a broad audience.

Representative excellent films since the "May Fourth" Movement will be printed with new copies and meet the audience one after another. The famous film "Song at Midnight" has been confirmed for public release within this year; films such as "Street Angel," "Song of the Fishermen," "The Highway," "New Women," "Morning in the Metropolis," "Crossroads," "The Torment of the Young," "Children of the Storm," "The Lost Lamb," "Song of Everlasting Sorrow," "My Life," "Eight Thousand Li of Cloud and Moon," "Night Shop," "Hope in the World," and "Crows and Sparrows" will be released successively.

### “五四”以来的优秀影片都将重新印制和公映

1956-09-05
第7版()
专栏：

    “五四”以来的优秀影片都将重新印制和公映
    新华社4日讯　优秀影片“一江春水向东流”，9月下旬起将在北京和各地重新公映。这部影片是一群进步的电影工作者在解放前拍摄的，曾经得到广大观众的喜爱。
      “五四”以来有代表性的优秀影片都将印制新拷贝，先后和观众见面。名片“夜半歌声”已确定在今年年内公映；“马路天使”、“渔光曲”、“大路”、“新女性”、“都会的早晨”、“十字街头”、“桃李劫”、“风云儿女”、“迷途的羔羊”、“长恨歌”、“我这一辈子”、“八千里路云和月”、“夜店”、“希望在人间”和“乌鸦与麻雀”等影片都将陆续上映。
