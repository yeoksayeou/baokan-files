Title: Strive for a Bumper Harvest and Overcome Natural Disasters (Picture)
Author: Ding Jun
Date: 1956-09-14
Page: 3

    ·Strive for a Bumper Harvest and Overcome Natural Disasters·
    The "March 8th" Agricultural Cooperative in Su County, Anhui Province, has suffered continuous natural disasters such as waterlogging and typhoons since the beginning of summer this year. In order to make up for the losses caused by the disasters, the members have rush-planted more than 8,000 mu of late autumn crops such as sweet potatoes, buckwheat, and vegetables.
                                  Xinhua News Agency reporter Ding Jun photo
    Members are planting Chinese cabbage. Members have broken the long-standing convention of "hanging up the hoe after the Autumn Equinox" and are strengthening the field management of late autumn crops. This is the members weeding in the sweet potato field.

### 力争多收  战胜灾荒（图片）
丁峻
1956-09-14
第3版()
专栏：

    ·力争多收  战胜灾荒·
    安徽省宿县“三八”农业社，今年入夏以来，连续遭受到内涝、台风等自然灾害。为了弥补灾害所带来的损失，社员们抢种了八千多亩甘薯、荞麦、蔬菜等晚秋作物。
                                  新华社记者  丁峻摄
    社员们正在栽种大白菜。社员们打破了历年来“立了秋、挂锄钩”的惯例，加强晚秋作物的田间管理。这是社员们在甘薯地里锄草。
