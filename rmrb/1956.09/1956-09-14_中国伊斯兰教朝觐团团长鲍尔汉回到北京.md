Title: Leader of the Chinese Islamic Hajj Delegation, Burhan, Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th. Burhan, the head of the Chinese Islamic Hajj Delegation and Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 13th after leading Muslims of all ethnic groups in China to complete the Hajj and visiting countries such as Egypt.

Burhan went abroad this year in February as the head of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation to visit and perform in Arab countries. After the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation completed its visit and performance tasks and returned to China, Burhan led the Chinese Islamic Hajj Delegation to Saudi Arabia for the Hajj and also led the Hajj Delegation to visit countries such as Egypt, Syria, Jordan, and Lebanon.

After the return of the head of the Chinese Islamic Hajj Delegation, Burhan, the delegation is currently led by Acting Head Li Shu to continue visiting other Arab countries.

### 中国伊斯兰教朝觐团团长鲍尔汉回到北京

1956-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中国伊斯兰教朝觐团团长鲍尔汉回到北京
    新华社13日讯　中国伊斯兰教朝觐团团长、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席鲍尔汉，在领导中国各族伊斯兰教徒完成朝觐任务和访问埃及等国以后，在13日下午乘飞机回到北京。
    鲍尔汉是在今年2月率领中国文化艺术代表团赴阿拉伯各国访问演出时出国的。在中国文化艺术代表团完成访问演出任务回国后，鲍尔汉又率领中国伊斯兰教朝觐团到沙特阿拉伯朝觐，并且率领朝觐团访问了埃及、叙利亚、约旦、黎巴嫩等国。
    中国伊斯兰教朝觐团在鲍尔汉团长回国后，正由代理团长李恕率领继续访问其他阿拉伯国家。
