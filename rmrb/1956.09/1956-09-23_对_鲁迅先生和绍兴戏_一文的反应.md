Title: Reaction to "Mr. Lu Xun and Shaoxing Opera"
Author: Huang Yuan
Date: 1956-09-23
Page: 8

    Reaction to "Mr. Lu Xun and Shaoxing Opera"
    We are very interested in Comrade Xu Gan's article "Mr. Lu Xun and Shaoxing Opera" published in the People's Daily (September 5, 6, 7, page 8).
    At the first Lu Xun Commemoration Meeting after the liberation of Shanghai in 1949, we performed "Male Hanging Ghost and Female Hanging Ghost" (Shaoxing Grand Opera). This year, we originally planned to invite the Shaoxing Opera Troupe to perform some programs to participate in the Lu Xun Commemoration Meeting, but we had not fully considered the repertoire. Comrade Xu Gan's article provides us with a lot of information. We plan to convene seven Shaoxing Opera Troupes in Hangzhou soon to discuss performance matters. Comrade Xu Gan's suggestions (a group of four plays) will be used as a preliminary plan for discussion at the meeting.
          Zhejiang Provincial Bureau of Culture　Huang Yuan

### 对“鲁迅先生和绍兴戏”一文的反应
黄源
1956-09-23
第8版()
专栏：读者、作者、编者

    对“鲁迅先生和绍兴戏”一文的反应
    徐淦同志在人民日报（9月5日、6日、7日八版）上发表的“鲁迅先生和绍兴戏”一文，我们很感兴趣。
    在1949年上海解放后的第一次鲁迅纪念会上，我们曾演出过男吊与女吊（绍兴大班的）。今年我们本来也准备请绍剧团演出一些节目参加鲁迅纪念会，但关于剧目却还没有全盘考虑过。徐淦同志的文章，给我们提供了很多情况。我们准备最近即召集七个绍剧团来杭开会，筹划演出事宜，徐淦同志的建议（一组四个剧目），将作为会上讨论的初步方案。
          浙江省文化局　黄源
