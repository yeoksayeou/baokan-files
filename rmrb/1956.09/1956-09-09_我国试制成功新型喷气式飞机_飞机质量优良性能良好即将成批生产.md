Title: China Successfully Trial-Produces New Jet Aircraft; Aircraft of Excellent Quality and Performance to be Mass-Produced Soon
Author:
Date: 1956-09-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 8th Dispatch: China has successfully trial-produced a new type of jet aircraft.

　　At 4:00 PM today, the state acceptance signing ceremony for the successful manufacturing of the jet aircraft was held at a certain location.

　　This type of jet aircraft, after static and dynamic testing—flight tests—has proven to be of excellent quality and performance. All the employees of the factory manufacturing this jet aircraft, with the assistance of Soviet experts, completed the trial production task of the aircraft one year and five months ahead of schedule. This aircraft will soon be mass-produced.

### 我国试制成功新型喷气式飞机  飞机质量优良性能良好即将成批生产

1956-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我国试制成功新型喷气式飞机
    飞机质量优良性能良好即将成批生产
    新华社8日讯　我国已试制成功新型的喷气式飞机。
    今天下午四时，在某地举行了喷气式飞机制造成功的国家验收签字仪式。
  这种喷气式飞机，经过静力试验和动力试验——试飞，证明质量优良，性能良好。　制造这种喷气式飞机的工厂的全体职工在苏联专家的帮助下，提前一年零五个月完成了飞机的试制任务。这种飞机即将成批生产。
