Title: Preliminary Results from China and Singapore-Malaya Trade Talks: Trade Volume Exceeds £3.2 Million
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 12th:

    Following trade talks which began on August 30th in Beijing between the Singapore Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation and the Malaya Federation Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation with various Chinese import and export corporations, preliminary statistics as of September 11th indicate that the total value of import and export transactions concluded and agreed upon has exceeded £3.2 million.
    The commodities that have been traded and are still under negotiation include:
    Exports from Singapore and the Malaya Federation include rubber, coconut oil, copra, timber, tin, betel nuts, and other spices.
    Exports from China include rice, soybeans, various canned goods, food, fruits, vegetables, sugar, cotton cloth, silk, glassware, steel, cement, traditional Chinese medicine, handicrafts, and other light industrial products.

### 我国和新加坡马来亚贸易会谈有初步结果交易额已超过三百二十万英镑

1956-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我国和新加坡马来亚贸易会谈有初步结果
    交易额已超过三百二十万英镑
    新华社12日讯　新加坡工商业贸易考察团和马来亚联合邦工商业贸易考察团同中国各进出口公司，自8月30日在北京开始进行贸易会谈后，截至9月11日止，据初步统计，进出口成交和达成协议的总额已超过三百二十万英镑。
    已经达成交易的和还在继续商谈的商品包括：
    在新加坡和马来亚联合邦方面出口的，有橡胶、椰子油、椰干、木材、锡、槟榔及其他香料。
    在中国方面出口的，有大米、大豆、各种罐头、食品、水果、蔬菜、糖、棉布、丝绸、玻璃制品、钢材、水泥、中药、手工艺品及其他轻工业品。
