Title: German Government Delegation Returns to Berlin
Author:
Date: 1956-09-14
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 13th: According to the German News Agency, the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic returned to Berlin on September 12th after a five-day visit to Czechoslovakia.
    President Pieck received the delegation on the same day and listened to the report of Prime Minister Grotewohl, the head of the delegation.

### 德政府代表团回到柏林

1956-09-14
第6版()
专栏：

    德政府代表团回到柏林
    新华社柏林13日电　据德意志通讯社讯：德意志民主共和国政府代表团在捷克斯洛伐克进行五天访问以后，在9月12日回到柏林。
    皮克总统在当天接见代表团，听取了代表团团长格罗提渥总理的报告。
