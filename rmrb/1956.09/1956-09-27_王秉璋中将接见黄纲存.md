Title: Lieutenant General Wang Bingzhang Receives Huang Gangcun
Author:
Date: 1956-09-27
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 26th -- Lieutenant General Wang Bingzhang, Deputy Commander of the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, received Huang Gangcun, a major in the Air Force of the Chiang army who revolted by flying an airplane back to the mainland, at the Air Force Headquarters this afternoon. Present at the reception were Senior Colonel Liu Shanben, Deputy Director of the Training Department of the PLA Air Force, and others.

### 王秉璋中将接见黄纲存

1956-09-27
第7版()
专栏：

    王秉璋中将接见黄纲存
    新华社26日讯　中国人民解放军空军副司令员王秉璋中将，今天下午在空军总部接见了由台湾架驶飞机起义飞回大陆的蒋军空军少校黄纲存。接见时在座的有解放军空军训练部副部长刘善本大校等人。
