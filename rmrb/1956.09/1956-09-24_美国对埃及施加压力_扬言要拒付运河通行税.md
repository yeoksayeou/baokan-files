Title: U.S. Pressures Egypt, Threatens to Refuse Payment of Canal Tolls
Author:
Date: 1956-09-24
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, September 22nd: The Associated Press reported from Washington that U.S. officials said on September 21st that the U.S. Treasury Department will soon stop U.S. ships from directly paying Suez Canal tolls to the Egyptian government. Up to now, the United States has frozen Egyptian assets in the United States but is still paying tolls to Egypt.

### 美国对埃及施加压力  扬言要拒付运河通行税

1956-09-24
第7版()
专栏：

    美国对埃及施加压力
    扬言要拒付运河通行税
    据新华社22日讯　美联社华盛顿消息：美国官员9月21日说，美国财政部不久就要制止美国船只直接向埃及政府交纳苏伊士运河通行税。到现在为止，美国冻结了埃及在美国的财产，但仍然向埃及付通行税。
