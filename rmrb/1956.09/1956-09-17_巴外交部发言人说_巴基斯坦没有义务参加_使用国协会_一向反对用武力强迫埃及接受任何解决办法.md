Title: Pakistani Foreign Ministry Spokesman Says Pakistan is Under No Obligation to Join "Users' Association" and Has Always Opposed Forcing Egypt to Accept Any Solution by Force

Author: 
Date: 1956-09-17
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, September 16th. A Pakistani Foreign Ministry spokesman said on September 15th that Pakistan has "no obligation" to "accept this proposal regarding the (canal) Users' Association" merely because it once agreed to the Dulles Plan.

Responding to questions from reporters, he said, "If the proposition to organize a Users' Association is to force passage through the Suez Canal with guns, then this proposition is certainly fraught with danger, and we certainly cannot participate in the use of force."

He said Pakistan cannot decide whether to attend the upcoming eighteen-nation Users' meeting until it receives more accurate and detailed materials regarding the meeting.

The spokesman said that Pakistan has received an invitation to attend the meeting convened by Egypt. He said, "Pakistan expresses its approval of the Egyptian government's desire to seek a solution to the dispute through peaceful means and consultation." He added that if the majority of the invited countries agree to participate, "then Pakistan is willing and will gladly participate in this meeting."

The spokesman stated: "We have always opposed any attempt to impose any solution on Egypt by force. We believe that the problem can still be resolved through negotiation. The hope for a peaceful solution must not be abandoned simply because the Egyptian government refuses to accept the resolution passed by the eighteen nations as the basis for discussion."

### 巴外交部发言人说  巴基斯坦没有义务参加“使用国协会”  一向反对用武力强迫埃及接受任何解决办法

1956-09-17
第8版()
专栏：

    巴外交部发言人说
    巴基斯坦没有义务参加“使用国协会”
    一向反对用武力强迫埃及接受任何解决办法
    新华社喀喇蚩16日电　巴基斯坦外交部发言人9月15日说，巴基斯坦“没有任何义务”仅仅因为它曾经同意杜勒斯方案就“必须接受这个关于（运河）使用国协会的建议”。
    他在回答新闻记者的问题的时候说：“如果组织使用国协会的主张是要用枪炮强行通过苏彝士运河的话，那么这一主张肯定是充满着危险的，我们当然不能参加使用武力。”
    他说，在得到关于这次使用国的十八国会议的更加确切的详细材料之前，巴基斯坦不可能决定是否参加这次会议。
    这位发言人说，巴基斯坦已经接到请它参加埃及召集的会议的邀请。他说，“巴基斯坦对于埃及政府愿意通过和平方法经过协商求得纠纷的解决这种愿望表示赞同”。他又说，如果大多数被邀请国都同意参加，“那么巴基斯坦也愿意并且将欣然参加这次会议。”
    发言人声明：“我们一直反对用武力把任何解决办法强加到埃及身上的任何企图。我们认为问题仍旧可以通过谈判的手段取得解决。决不能因为埃及政府拒绝接受十八国通过的决议作为讨论的基础就放弃求得和平解决的希望。”
