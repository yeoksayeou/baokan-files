Title: Indian Vice President to Visit Our Country at Invitation
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 21st – At the invitation of Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, the Vice President of the Republic of India, Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan, will visit China in mid-October.

### 印度副总统应邀将来我国访问

1956-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    印度副总统应邀将来我国访问
    新华社21日讯　印度共和国副总统沙·拉德哈克里希南博士应中华人民共和国主席毛泽东的邀请，将于10月中旬来中国访问。
