Title: Delegation from the Academy of Sciences of the German Democratic Republic Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-13
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 12th. The delegation from the Academy of Sciences of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Dr. Steinmetz, Vice President of the Academy of Sciences of the German Democratic Republic, arrived in Beijing today.

    Tao Menghe, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and heads of relevant research institutes of the Academy of Sciences, welcomed the delegation at the airport.

    The Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Gipner, and First Secretary Deikes also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 德意志民主共和国科学院代表团到北京

1956-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国科学院代表团到北京
    新华社12日讯　以德意志民主共和国科学院副院长施太尼次博士为首的德意志民主共和国科学院代表团一行七人，今日到达北京。
    中国科学院副院长陶孟和和科学院有关研究所负责人都到飞机场欢迎。
    德意志民主共和国驻华大使纪普纳和一等秘书戴克斯也前往飞机场欢迎。
