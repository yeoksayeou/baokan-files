    Chen Zhaoting (right), director of the spinning workshop of the state-owned Shanghai No. 3 Wool Textile Factory, and foreman Zhou Rongqing (center), and others, studied the advanced experience of the Soviet Union and changed the multi-doubling and multi-drafting to less doubling and less drafting, and changed the original nine processes of machines to six processes, which can save the country about 20,000 yuan per year. This is the scene of them studying the yarn spinning. Photo by Bi Pinfu, Xinhua News Agency.

    国营上海第三毛纺织厂纺部车间主任陈肇亭（右）、工长周荣庆（中）等学习了苏联先进经验，把多并条、多牵伸，改为少并条、少牵伸，把原来九道车改为六道车，每年可为国家节约约两万元。这是他们在研究纺线的情形。　　　　毕品富摄　新华社稿
