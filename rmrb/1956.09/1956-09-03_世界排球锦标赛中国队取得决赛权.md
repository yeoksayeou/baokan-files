Title: Chinese Team Secures Finals Spot in World Volleyball Championship
Author:
Date: 1956-09-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 2nd -- Paris Dispatch: The Chinese men's and women's volleyball teams participating in the World Volleyball Championships defeated the Indian team and the West German team by 3-1 and 3-0 respectively on September 1st, securing spots in the finals.

    The men's volleyball match between China and India was very exciting, with both sides demonstrating high athletic skill and thrilling moves. The Indian team displayed skills in the third game that they had not shown before. Both teams were cheered loudly by the audience. The results were 15-5; 15-9; 10-15; 15-7.

    The result of the match between the Chinese women's team and the West German team was 15-1; 15-3; 15-2.

    Now, all the teams that have qualified for the finals of this championship have been selected. The ten countries that have qualified for the men's volleyball finals are China, the Soviet Union, the United States, Yugoslavia, Romania, France, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, and Hungary.

    The teams that have qualified for the women's volleyball finals are China, Poland, Czechoslovakia, the United States, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Romania, the Netherlands, the Soviet Union, Bulgaria, and East Germany.

### 世界排球锦标赛中国队取得决赛权

1956-09-03
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    世界排球锦标赛中国队取得决赛权
    新华社布拉格2日电　巴黎消息：参加世界排球锦标赛的中国男、女排球队9月1日分别以三比一和三比零战胜印度队和西德队，都取得了决赛权。
    中国和印度的男子排球赛十分精彩，双方表现出很高的运动水平和惊险动作。印度队在第三场比赛中显示出了平时所没有的技巧。两队都受到观众的大声喝采。比赛结果是十五比五；十五比九；十比十五；十五比七。
    中国女子队和西德队比赛结果是十五比一；十五比三；十五比二。
    现在，这次锦标赛所有取得决赛权的球队都已经选拔出来。取得男子排球决赛权的十个国家是中国、苏联、美国、南斯拉夫、罗马尼亚、法国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、保加利亚和匈牙利。
    取得女子排球队决赛权的是中国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、美国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、罗马尼亚、荷兰、苏联、保加利亚和民主德国。
