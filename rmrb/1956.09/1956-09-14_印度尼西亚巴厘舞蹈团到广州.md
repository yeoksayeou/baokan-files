Title: Indonesian Bali Dance Troupe Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1956-09-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 13th. The Indonesian Bali Dance Troupe, consisting of 81 members, arrived in Guangzhou by special train from Shenzhen this afternoon, invited by the Chinese-Indonesian Friendship Association to visit and perform in China. Members of the dance troupe were warmly welcomed by people from Guangzhou's literary and art circles.
    Dai Ailian,理事of the Chinese-Indonesian Friendship Association and Vice Chairman of the Chinese Dance Art Research Association, went to Shenzhen to greet the dance troupe.
    The dance troupe is led by Saleh Umar, the Third Vice Chairman of the Indonesian-Chinese Friendship Association and a member of the National Assembly. Many members of the dance troupe are famous Indonesian dancers.
    All members of the dance troupe attended a welcome banquet hosted by Guo Qiaoran, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou, this evening.

### 印度尼西亚巴厘舞蹈团到广州

1956-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚巴厘舞蹈团到广州
    新华社广州13日电　应中国印度尼西亚友好协会邀请来中国访问演出的印度尼西亚巴厘舞蹈团一行八十一人，今天下午自深圳乘专车到达广州。舞蹈团人员受到广州市文艺界人士的热烈欢迎。
    中国印度尼西亚友好协会理事、中国舞蹈艺术研究会副主席戴爱莲曾到深圳迎接舞蹈团。
    舞蹈团由印度尼西亚—中国友好协会第三副主席、国会议员萨勒·乌马尔任团长。舞蹈团的成员中有不少是印度尼西亚的著名舞蹈家。
    舞蹈团全体人员在今天晚上出席了广州市副市长郭翘然举行的欢迎宴会。
