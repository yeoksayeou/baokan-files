Title: Representatives of Fraternal Parties from Various Countries Visit Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1956-09-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 15th—Representatives of communist and workers' parties from various countries, invited to attend the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China, have been sightseeing in Beijing for the past few days since their arrival.

    Representatives of the French and Italian Communist Parties visited the Urban Planning Exhibition.

    Representatives of the Indian and British Communist Parties visited the Beijing Institute of Iron and Steel Technology, Tsinghua University, and the Central Institute for Nationalities, respectively.

    Representatives of the Labor-Progressive Party of Canada, the People's Party of Iran, and the Communist Party of Turkey visited the state-owned Southern Suburb Farm and the Chenguang Agricultural Production Cooperative in Luyuan Township, respectively.

    In addition, many representatives of communist and workers' parties from various countries toured Beijing's scenic spots and viewed the city's appearance.

### 各国兄弟党代表在京参观

1956-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

    各国兄弟党代表在京参观
    新华社15日讯　应邀参加中国共产党第八次全国代表大会的各国共产党和工人党的代表到达北京以后，连日来在北京参观游览。
    法国、意大利共产党的代表曾参观了城市规划展览会。
    印度、英国共产党的代表分别参观了北京钢铁学院、清华大学和中央民族学院。
    加拿大劳工进步党代表、伊朗人民党代表和土耳其共产党代表分别参观了国营南郊农场和鹿园乡晨光农业生产合作社。
    另外，有许多国家共产党和工人党的代表游览了北京的名胜，观看了北京市容。
