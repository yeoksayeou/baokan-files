Title: Voroshilov Holds Talks with Sukarno Discussing Further Development of Cooperation Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-09-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 11th – Soviet politicians held talks with Indonesian politicians in the Kremlin on September 10th.

The Soviet side present at the talks included: Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR; Mikoyan and Pervukhin, First Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the USSR; Kabanov, Minister of Foreign Trade; Gromyko, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Fedrin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Koval, Director of the Central Bureau of Economic Relations; Zhukov, Soviet Ambassador to Indonesia, and others.

The Indonesian side present at the talks included: President Sukarno; Foreign Minister Abdulgani; Indonesian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Bala, and others.

The talks were conducted in a friendly atmosphere of mutual understanding. The two sides discussed issues concerning the further development of cooperation between the two countries, and also discussed certain aspects of the current international situation that are relevant to both countries.

The two sides agreed to conclude the talks on September 11th.

According to Xinhua News Agency, President Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia, who is currently visiting the Soviet Union, held a luncheon in Moscow on September 10th. Leaders of the Soviet government and the Communist Party were invited to attend the luncheon.

### 伏罗希洛夫同苏加诺举行会谈  讨论有关进一步发展两国合作的问题

1956-09-12
第5版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫同苏加诺举行会谈
    讨论有关进一步发展两国合作的问题
    新华社莫斯科11日电　苏联政治家同印度尼西亚政治家9月10日在克里姆林宫举行了会谈。
    苏联方面出席会谈的有：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫，苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬、别尔乌辛，对外贸易部长卡巴洛夫、外交部第一副部长葛罗米柯、外交部副部长费德林、中央经济关系局长科瓦尔、苏联驻印度尼西亚大使茹科夫等。
    印度尼西亚方面出席会谈的有：苏加诺总统，外交部长阿卜杜加尼，印度尼西亚驻苏联大使巴拉等。
    会谈是在友好和互相谅解的气氛中进行的。双方讨论了有关进一步发展两国之间的合作问题，并且讨论了目前国际局势对两国有关的某些方面。
    双方同意在9月11日结束会谈。
    据新华社讯　正在苏联访问的印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺9月10日在莫斯科举行午宴。苏联政府和共产党的领导人应邀出席了午宴。
