Title: International Buddhist Monks Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. An international Buddhist monks delegation composed of high monks from seven countries, India, Ceylon, Nepal, Laos, Cambodia, Thailand, and Vietnam, arrived in Beijing by plane from Kunming at 5:35 pm today. The delegation was welcomed at the Beijing airport by figures from the Chinese Buddhist community and representatives from relevant departments of the Chinese government and people's organizations.

This delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Buddhist Association of China. The delegation is headed by Indian Bhikkhu Badanta Ananda Kausalya Yana, member of the management department of the Indian Maha Bodhi Society, and the deputy head is Ceylonese Bhikkhu Niluwe Jinara Tana, director and joint secretary of the Indian Maha Bodhi Society. Most of the nine members are engaged in research at the Nalanda University in India, where the eminent monk Xuanzang of the Tang Dynasty once studied and which has recently been re-established. After disembarking from the plane, the members of the delegation were introduced by Zhao Puchu, vice president of the Buddhist Association of China, to Zhang Ce, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council, He Chengxiang, Director of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council, Wang Feng, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, and Venerable Neng Hai, Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China, who came to welcome them. The hosts and guests greeted each other with their palms together. More than 500 Chinese Buddhist monks and youths of various ethnic groups gathered at the airport, singing "Ode to the Sangha" and applauding warmly to express their welcome.

Badanta Ananda Kausalya Yana spoke at the airport. On behalf of the eleven monks from seven countries, he thanked the Chinese Buddhist community for their welcome and expressed his hope to have the opportunity to exchange views with the Buddhist Association of China on issues related to Buddhism.

The delegation presented three statues of Sakyamuni Buddha brought from India and six bodhi tree seedlings planted in golden basins to the Buddhist Association of China.

### 国际佛教僧侣代表团到京

1956-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    国际佛教僧侣代表团到京
    新华社8日讯　由印度、锡兰、尼泊尔、老挝、柬埔寨、泰国、越南七国的高僧组成的国际佛教僧侣代表团，今天下午5时35分从昆明乘飞机到达北京。代表团在北京机场受到中国佛教界人士和中国政府有关部门、人民团体的代表的欢迎。
    这个代表团是应中国佛教协会的邀请，前来中国进行访问的。印度比丘、印度摩呵菩提会管理部委员巴丹达·阿难陀·柯萨尔雅雅那担任代表团团长，锡兰比丘、印度摩呵菩提会理事兼联合秘书尼鲁威·吉那拉塔那任副团长，团员九人大多在中国唐代高僧玄奘法师曾经留学过、最近又重新建立的印度那灿陀学院从事研究工作。代表团人员在下飞机后，由中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初居士介绍同前来欢迎他们的国务院副秘书长张策、国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘、民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、中印友好协会会长丁西林、中国佛教协会副会长能海法师等见面。宾主们合十互相致意。聚集在机场上的中国佛教僧侣和各民族青年五百多人，唱起“僧室赞”和热烈鼓掌表示欢迎。
    巴丹达·阿难陀·柯萨尔雅雅那在机场上讲话。他代表来自七个国家的十一位僧侣感谢中国佛教界对他们的欢迎，并且表示希望有机会同中国佛教协会就有关佛教的问题交换意见。
    代表团把从印度携来的释迦牟尼像三尊，种在金盆中的菩提树苗六株赠送给中国佛教协会。
