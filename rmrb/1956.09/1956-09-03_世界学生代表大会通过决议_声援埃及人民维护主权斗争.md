Title: World Student Congress Passes Resolution in Support of the Egyptian People's Struggle to Safeguard Sovereignty
Author:
Date: 1956-09-03
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 2nd - The Fourth World Student Congress today passed a resolution on the nationalization of the Suez Canal Company, stating that the Fourth World Student Congress recognizes the right of the Egyptian people to exercise their sovereignty and to nationalize the Suez Canal Company.

The World Student Congress believes that the mobilization of armed forces, the implementation of economic pressure, and the use of military threats against Egypt by certain countries endanger world peace and undermine the principles of the United Nations Charter regarding the peaceful resolution of international disputes.

The Congress expresses its solidarity with the Egyptian students and people and hopes that this issue will be resolved peacefully.

### 世界学生代表大会通过决议  声援埃及人民维护主权斗争

1956-09-03
第6版()
专栏：

    世界学生代表大会通过决议
    声援埃及人民维护主权斗争
    新华社布拉格2日电　第四次世界学生代表大会今天通过的关于苏彝士运河公司国有化的决议说，第四次世界学生代表大会承认埃及人民行使它的主权、把苏彝士运河公司收归国有的权利。
    世界学生代表大会认为某些国家动员武装部队、施行经济压力和对埃及使用军事威胁危及世界和平并且破坏联合国宪章关于和平解决国际争端的原则。
    代表大会表示声援埃及学生和人民，并且希望这个问题得到和平解决。
