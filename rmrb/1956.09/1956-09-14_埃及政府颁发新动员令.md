Title: Egyptian Government Issues New Mobilization Order
Author:
Date: 1956-09-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 13th. The Egyptian government issued a new decree this morning authorizing the Minister of Defense to mobilize officials and employees of any public utility, or employees of private companies, to serve in the armed forces or in munitions and aircraft factories.

The only restriction stipulated in this decree is that those mobilized must be no younger than twenty-five years of age or older than thirty-five years of age.

### 埃及政府颁发新动员令

1956-09-14
第5版()
专栏：

    埃及政府颁发新动员令
    新华社13日讯　开罗消息：埃及政府今天早晨颁布了一条新的法令，授权国防部长可以动员任何公用事业的官员和职员，或者私营公司的职员到武装部队或军火工厂和飞机工厂去服务。
    这项法令所规定的唯一限制是被动员的人的年龄不得小于二十五岁或大于三十五岁。
