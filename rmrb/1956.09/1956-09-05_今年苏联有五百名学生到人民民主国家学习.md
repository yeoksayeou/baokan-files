Title: This Year, the Soviet Union Sends 500 Students to Study in People's Democratic Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-09-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 3rd: This year, the Soviet Union is sending 500 male and female students to study at institutions of higher education in the People's Democratic countries. These students are currently leaving the Soviet Union for these countries in succession.

The students will mainly study the humanities of these countries. Some will study the development history of the languages and literatures of Albania, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Poland, and Romania, respectively. Students going to Peking University in China will study Chinese, Chinese literature, history, geography, and economics. Many will also study the achievements of the People's Democratic countries in science, technology, and agriculture.

### 今年苏联有五百名学生到人民民主国家学习

1956-09-05
第5版()
专栏：

    今年苏联有五百名学生到人民民主国家学习
    据新华社莫斯科3日电　今年苏联有五百名男女学生到人民民主国家的高等学校学习。目前这些学生正在陆续离开苏联前往这些国家。
    留学生们将主要研究这些国家的人文科学。有的将分别研究阿尔巴尼亚、匈牙利、捷克斯洛伐克、保加利亚、波兰和罗马尼亚的语言和文学的发展史。到中国北京大学学习的留学生将研究汉语、中国文学、历史、地理和经济。也有很多人将研究人民民主国家在科学、技术和农业方面的成就。
