Title: Egyptian Intelligence Chief Issues Stern Statement, Denouncing Western Conspiracy to Establish "Users' Association," Reaffirming Egypt's Willingness to Guarantee Freedom of Navigation in the Canal According to the 1888 Convention
Author:
Date: 1956-09-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 13th, Cairo Dispatch: Cairo Radio broadcast a statement by the Director of Egyptian Intelligence, Hatem, on the evening of September 12th, saying that Egypt considers the announcement by the three Western powers to establish a "Suez Canal Users' Association" as "a clear provocation aimed at leading to war."

Hatem said that the establishment of the "Users' Association" means that certain countries are conspiring to infringe upon Egypt's rights and sovereignty by interfering in the management of the Suez Canal. At the same time, this will cause serious disruption to the free navigation of the canal. Hatem said, "Egypt is willing to provide various conveniences to canal users in accordance with the 1888 Convention to ensure freedom of passage, but Egypt cannot accept uncompromising conditions that infringe upon its legitimate rights and sovereignty."

Egypt's Middle East News Agency reported on the evening of September 12th that Egypt cannot accept the Western plan to establish a "Suez Canal Users' Association" to manage the Suez Canal. Eden's description in the British House of Commons of the Western three-power plan constitutes a provocation aimed at finding an excuse for armed aggression against Egypt.

### 埃及情报司长发表严正声明  斥责西方想搞“使用国协会”的阴谋  重申埃及愿意根据1888年公约保证运河航行自由

1956-09-14
第5版()
专栏：

    埃及情报司长发表严正声明
    斥责西方想搞“使用国协会”的阴谋
    重申埃及愿意根据1888年公约保证运河航行自由
    新华社13日讯　开罗消息：开罗电台9月12日晚上广播埃及情报司司长哈特姆的声明说，埃及认为西方三国宣布的准备成立一个“苏彝士运河使用国协会”的做法是“目的在引向战争的一种明白的挑衅”。
    哈特姆说，“使用国协会”的成立意味着某些国家阴谋用干涉苏彝士运河管理的方法来侵犯埃及的权利和它的主权。同时，这将会在运河的自由通航方面造成严重的紊乱。哈特姆说，“埃及愿意根据1888年的公约给予运河使用者各种方便以保证通行自由，但是埃及不能接受侵犯它的合法权利和主权的不妥协条件。”
    埃及中东通讯社9月12日晚说，埃及不能接受关于设立“苏彝士运河使用国协会”来管理苏彝士运河的西方计划。艾登在英国下院叙述的西方三国计划构成了目的在于为武装攻击埃及找借口的一种挑衅。
