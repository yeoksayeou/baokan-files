Title: Lhasa Representatives Gather to Celebrate the Establishment of the Lhasa Prefecture-Level Office

Author:

Date: 1956-09-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, August 31st: Over five thousand representatives from all walks of life in Lhasa gathered at the People's Square today to celebrate the official establishment of the Lhasa Prefecture-Level Office of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region.

The Lhasa Prefecture-Level Office is one of the eight representative organs at the prefecture level established by the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region throughout Tibet. Today, the streets of Lhasa were plastered with colorful slogans, and colorful flags fluttered over the People's Square. Representatives from the twenty-eight Dzongs in the Lhasa area and the citizens of Lhasa together celebrated this great happy event in the construction of the Tibetan regime.

The Working Committee of the Chinese Communist Party in Tibet, the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, the People's Liberation Army Tibet Military Region, and the Tibetan Local Government all sent representatives to the meeting to offer congratulations. Also present to offer congratulations were representatives from the three major monasteries in Lhasa, representatives from Wei Ji and Xue Lie Kong (the Tibetan local government's offices in the Lhasa area), and representatives from patriotic mass organizations in Lhasa. They all presented congratulatory banners to the inaugural meeting of the Lhasa Prefecture Office.

At today's meeting, Lhamin Yeshe Tsultrim, on behalf of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, presented the official seal to the Lhasa Prefecture Office. Sangpo Tenzin Wangyal, the director of the Lhasa Prefecture Office, received the seal.

Sangpo Tenzin Wangyal said in his speech at the meeting that the establishment of the Lhasa Prefecture Office marks a further development in the political life of the Tibetan people following the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, making all monks and laypeople more united and progressive, thereby accelerating the realization of a unified Tibet Autonomous Region.

Fan Ming, Deputy Secretary of the Working Committee of the Chinese Communist Party in Tibet, delivered a congratulatory speech on behalf of the Working Committee of the Chinese Communist Party in Tibet and the Tibet Military Region. He said that the establishment of the Lhasa Prefecture-Level Office and the upcoming establishment of other Prefecture-Level Offices will greatly enhance the connection between the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region and the broad masses of the people, strengthen unity between various regions, accelerate the development of various construction undertakings in the Lhasa area, and contribute to the realization of a unified Tibet Autonomous Region.

Lhamin Yeshe Tsultrim, Deputy Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, said in his congratulatory speech that he hoped that under leadership of the Working Committee of the Chinese Communist Party in Tibet and the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Automous Region, the Lhasa Prefecture-Level Office, adhering to Chairman Mao's guiding principle of "unity, progress and greater development," will unite patriotic people from all walks of life in the Lhasa area, work and study actively, serve the people wholeheartedly, and strive to develop Tibet's politics, economy and culture, and build a prosperous, happy and theocratic socialist new Tibet.

### 拉萨各界代表集会  庆祝拉萨基巧级办事处成立

1956-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    拉萨各界代表集会
    庆祝拉萨基巧级办事处成立
    新华社拉萨31日电　拉萨市各界代表五千多人今天在人民广场集会，庆祝西藏自治区筹备委员会拉萨基巧级（相当专区）办事处正式成立。
    拉萨基巧级办事处是西藏自治区筹备委员会设在全藏的八个基巧级代表机关之一。今天，拉萨沿街贴满了彩色标语，人民广场上飘着彩旗，拉萨地区二十八个宗的代表们和拉萨市民一起，庆祝西藏政权建设中的这一件大喜事。
    中共西藏工作委员会、西藏自治区筹备委员会、中国人民解放军西藏军区、西藏地方政府都派了代表到会祝贺。前来祝贺的还有拉萨三大寺院的代表，卫基和雪列空（西藏地方政府在拉萨地区的办事机构）的代表以及拉萨市爱国群众团体的代表。他们都向拉萨基巧办事处成立大会献赠了贺幛。
    在今天大会上，拉敏·益喜楚臣代表西藏自治区筹备委员会向拉萨基巧办事处授印。拉萨基巧办事处由主任桑颇·才旺仁增受印。
    桑颇·才旺仁增在大会上致词说，拉萨基巧办事处的成立，标志着西藏人民的政治生活继西藏自治区筹备委员会成立后又得到了进一步的发展，使全体僧俗人民更加团结和进步，从而使实现统一的西藏自治区加快了速度。
    中共西藏工委副书记范明代表中共西藏工委和西藏军区向成立大会致祝词。他说，拉萨基巧级办事处的成立和其他的基巧级办事处的即将成立，对加强西藏自治区筹委会和广大人民群众之间的联系，加强各地区之间的团结，加速拉萨地区各项建设事业的发展和对实现统一的西藏自治区，会有很大作用。
    西藏自治区筹备委员会副秘书长拉敏·益喜楚臣在祝词中，希望拉萨基巧级办事处在中共西藏工委和西藏自治区筹备委员会的领导下，本着毛主席指示的“团结进步更加发展”的方针，团结拉萨地区各阶层爱国人士，积极工作和学习，全心全意为人民服务，为发展西藏的政治、经济和文化，建成繁荣幸福和政教昌盛的社会主义的新西藏而奋斗。
