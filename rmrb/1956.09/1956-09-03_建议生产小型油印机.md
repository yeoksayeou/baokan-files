Title: Suggesting the Production of Small Offset Printing Machines
Author: Jin Kerchen
Date: 1956-09-03
Page: 2

    Suggestion to Produce Small Offset Printing Machines
    The mimeograph machines we currently use are generally suitable for folio (8-K) sized paper. According to the unified document specifications, most documents are printed on sixteenmo (16-K) sized paper, except for some special forms. Large mimeograph machines easily damage stencils, stain documents, damage gauze, damage ink rollers, and are inconvenient to move. I suggest that relevant factories produce more small, practical mimeograph machines in a certain proportion.
        Karaqin Left Banner Newspaper Office, Jin Kerchen

### 建议生产小型油印机
靳国宸
1956-09-03
第2版()
专栏：

    建议生产小型油印机
    我们现在使用的油印机，一般都是适合八开纸的。按文书统一规格来说，除了印一些专用表格等以外，大多数都是印十六开纸的。油印机大，容易坏腊纸，沾污文件，坏纱布，坏油磙，搬运起来也不方便。我建议有关工厂按一定比例，多生产一些小型的实用的油印机。
        喀喇沁左旗报社　靳国宸
