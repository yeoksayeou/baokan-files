Title: Two Thousand Athletes to Participate in Olympic Team Selection Trials; Guangdong Sports Branch Announces Measures for Hong Kong and Macau Athletes to Participate in Selection Trials
Author:
Date: 1956-09-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 14th: The Preparatory Committee for China's Participation in the 16th Olympic Games announced today that it is expected that nearly two thousand male and female athletes will participate in the selection trials for the Chinese Olympic team, to be held in Beijing from October 7th to 16th. Among them, one thousand two hundred athletes will participate in track and field events.

The selection criteria have been initially determined: The qualifying condition will be based on achieving results that would have earned a place in the finals at the previous Olympic Games; the athletes' usual performance will also be considered during the selection process.

At its fourth meeting held today, the Preparatory Committee for China's Participation in the 16th Olympic Games established a selection committee, including: Director Rong Gaotang, Deputy Directors Ma John, Wu Yunrui, Dong Shouyi, Huang Zhong, Zhang Huilan (female), and Han Fudong, and twenty-three members.

The meeting heard a report from Huang Zhong, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Olympic Committee, who had just returned from Melbourne, Australia, on the preparations for the 16th Olympic Games. He said that the Chinese Olympic Committee delegation's ten days of work in Melbourne had proceeded smoothly, and the delegation was satisfied with the preparations made by the Melbourne 16th Olympic Games Organizing Committee.

Huang Zhong said that the delegation received a warm welcome from the Melbourne 16th Olympic Games Organizing Committee and met with Sir Frank Selleck, the Mayor of Melbourne. The delegation visited all the venues that will be used for this Olympic Games, including the Melbourne Cricket Ground, which can accommodate 120,000 spectators.

Today's meeting also discussed the style of clothing for the Chinese delegation participating in the 16th Olympic Games and other related matters.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 14th: The Guangdong Branch of the All-China Sports Federation today announced the registration and reception procedures for Hong Kong and Macau athletes to participate in the Chinese Olympic team selection trials.

The procedures stipulate that: Any Chinese sports organizations and Chinese athletes in Hong Kong and Macau who meet the qualifications stipulated in the notice from the Preparatory Committee for China's Participation in the 16th Olympic Games and are willing to participate in the selection trials for various events within the specified categories, are required to send their registration forms to the Guangdong Branch of the All-China Sports Federation in Guangzhou before September 25th; Chinese sports organizations and Chinese athletes in Hong Kong and Macau who register to participate in the selection trials should report to the Guangzhou Sports Club between September 27th and 29th after receiving notification from the Guangdong Sports Branch.

The procedures also stipulate that all travel and accommodation expenses for sports teams or athletes who are notified by the Guangdong Sports Branch to participate in the selection trials will be fully covered by the Sports Branch.

In addition, to receive sports teams and athletes from Taiwan who are going to Beijing to participate in the selection trials, the Guangdong Sports Branch, entrusted by the Preparatory Committee for China's Participation in the 16th Olympic Games, has decided to set up a reception station at the Guangzhou Sports Club from September 27th.

### 两千名运动员将参加奥林匹克运动队选拔赛  广东体育分会公布港澳运动员参加选拔赛的办法

1956-09-15
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    两千名运动员将参加奥林匹克运动队选拔赛
    广东体育分会公布港澳运动员参加选拔赛的办法
    新华社14日讯  中国参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会筹备委员会今天宣布，预计将有近两千名男、女运动员参加10月7日到16日在北京举行的中国奥林匹克运动队的选拔赛。其中有一千二百名运动员将参加田径比赛。
    选拔赛的入选标准已经初步确定为：以能在上届奥林匹克运动会获得决赛权的成绩为入选条件；在选拔运动员的时候，并将考虑到他们平时的成绩。
    中国参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会筹备委员会在今天召开的第四次会议上，成立了一个选拔委员会，成员包括：主任荣高棠，副主任马约翰、吴蕴瑞、董守义、黄中、张汇兰（女）和韩复东，以及二十三名委员。
    会议听了刚从澳大利亚墨尔本回来的中国奥林匹克委员会副秘书长黄中关于第十六届奥林匹克运动会筹备情况的报告。他说，中国奥林匹克委员会代表团在墨尔本十天的工作进行得很顺利，代表团对墨尔本第十六届奥林匹克运动会组织委员会所作的准备工作表示满意。
    黄中说，代表团受到了墨尔本第十六届奥林匹克运动会组织委员会的热烈欢迎，并且会见了墨尔本市市长斯莱克爵士。代表团参观了本届奥林匹克运动会将使用的所有的场地，其中包括能容纳十二万观众的墨尔本中央体育场。
    今天的会议还讨论了参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会中国代表团的服装式样等问题。
    新华社广州14日电　中华全国体育总会广东省分会今天公布关于香港、澳门运动员参加中国奥林匹克运动队选拔赛的报名和接待办法。
    这个办法规定：凡是合乎中华人民共和国参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会筹委会通知中所规定的资格，和愿意参加规定项目中各种项目选拔赛的在香港和澳门的中国人的体育组织和中国运动员，要求在9月25日前将报名单寄到在广州市的中华全国体育总会广东省分会；报名参加选拔赛的香港、澳门的中国人的体育组织和中国运动员，在接到广东省体育分会的通知后，在9月27日到29日到广州体育俱乐部报到。
    这个办法还规定，凡接到广东省体育分会通知参加选拔赛的运动队或运动员的旅费、食宿费都全部由体育分会负责供给。
    此外，为了接待前往北京参加选拔赛的台湾的运动队和运动员，广东省体育分会接受中国参加第十六届奥林匹克运动会筹备委员会的委托，已决定从9月27日起在广州体育俱乐部设立招待站。
