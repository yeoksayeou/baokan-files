Title: Tianjin Steel Plant Rolls Out More Steel
Author:
Date: 1956-09-07
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, September 5th: The Soviet advanced experience of rolling more steel from the same amount of steel ingots is being promoted in the Tianjin Steel Plant. When the plant rolled four specifications of round steel in August, it adopted this advanced experience and rolled an additional 61,900 meters of round steel.

The advanced experience is: when rolling steel according to national standards, control the negative tolerance of the cross-sectional diameter of the steel. For example, when rolling a type of round steel with a cross-sectional diameter of 10 mm, the national standard stipulates that the thickest should not exceed 10.4 mm, and the thinnest should not be lower than 9.5 mm. Now, the cross-sectional diameter of this specification of round steel is controlled between 9.5 mm and 10 mm. In this way, each steel ingot can roll an additional 6.4 meters of round steel.

According to preliminary calculations by the factory, without increasing the number of steel ingots, the plant can roll an additional 231,000 meters of round steel of only 10 and 12 mm specifications from September to December.

### 天津钢厂轧出更多的钢材

1956-09-07
第2版()
专栏：

    天津钢厂轧出更多的钢材
    新华社天津5日电　用同样数量的钢锭能轧制出较多钢材的苏联先进经验，正在天津钢厂推广。这个厂在8月份轧制四种规格的元钢的时候，采用了这种先进经验，多轧制出元钢六万一千九百公尺。
    这项先进经验是：按照国家规定的标准，轧制钢材的时候掌握钢材断面直径的负允许公差。例如轧制一种断面直径为十公厘的元钢，国家规定最粗不得超过十点四公厘，最细不得低于九点五公厘。现在把这种规格元钢的断面直径都掌握在九点五公厘至十公厘之间。这样，每颗钢锭可以多轧制出六点四公尺元钢。
    据工厂初步计算，在不增加钢锭的情况下，这个厂在9月到12月份以内，仅十、十二公厘两种规格的元钢，就能多轧出二十三万一千公尺。
