Title: Indonesian Foreign Minister Issues Statement: Suez Issue Must Be Resolved Through Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1956-09-18
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 17th -- Indonesian Foreign Minister Abdulgani issued a statement on September 16th, saying, "The Suez dispute can be resolved either by force or by negotiation. Force is neither useful nor recommendable, but negotiations must be conducted."
	The Indonesian Foreign Minister, who is accompanying President Sukarno on a visit to Yugoslavia, emphasized, "Indonesia opposes all attempts to resolve this issue by force."
	He said: The London conference discussing the Suez issue failed to produce any resolution, and the eighteen-nation proposal is unacceptable to Egypt because it infringes upon Egypt's sovereignty.
	Abdulgani said: Indonesia recognizes Egypt's right to nationalize the Suez Canal as part of its territory. He believes that Egypt can guarantee the freedom of navigation of the canal.

### 印度尼西亚外长发表声明  苏彝士问题必须用谈判解决

1956-09-18
第7版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚外长发表声明
    苏彝士问题必须用谈判解决
    新华社17日讯　贝尔格莱德消息：印度尼西亚外交部长阿卜杜加尼9月16日发表声明说：“苏彝士争端不是用武力就是用谈判来解决。武力既无用处也不值得推荐，但是谈判却必须进行。”
    正伴随苏加诺总统在南斯拉夫访问的印度尼西亚外长强调说：“印度尼西亚反对以武力解决这个问题的一切企图。”
    他说：讨论苏彝士问题的伦敦会议未能作出任何决议，而十八国的建议对埃及说来是不能接受的，因为它侵犯了埃及的主权。
    阿卜杜加尼说：印度尼西亚承认埃及有权把作为它的领土一部分的苏彝士运河收归国有。他相信埃及能够保证运河的自由通航。
