Title: Hanoi People from All Walks of Life Gather to Commemorate the Eleventh Anniversary of the Anti-War Resistance in Southern Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1956-09-25
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 24th. People from all walks of life in Hanoi held a rally on the evening of the 23rd to commemorate the eleventh anniversary of the Anti-War Resistance in Southern Vietnam. The rally was convened by the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front.

Pham Ngoc Thach, Deputy Minister of Medical Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and former Chairman of the Saigon-Cholon Anti-War Resistance Administrative Committee, spoke at the meeting, describing the struggle of the southern people for the peaceful reunification, independence, and democracy of the motherland.

The assembly concluded by passing a telegram to the people of the South.

Those present at the rally last night included Ton Duc Thang, Phan Ke Toai, Truong Chinh, Ton Quang Phiet, as well as representatives of various democratic parties, mass organizations, and cadres and people from the South who had gathered in the North.

On the evening of the 22nd, more than 10,000 cadres and soldiers from the South who had gathered in the North, as well as people from Hanoi, also held a rally to commemorate the eleventh anniversary of the Anti-War Resistance in Southern Vietnam.

### 河内各阶层人民集会  纪念越南南部抗战十一周年

1956-09-25
第7版()
专栏：

    河内各阶层人民集会
    纪念越南南部抗战十一周年
    新华社河内24日电　河内市各阶层人民23日晚上举行大会，纪念越南南部抗战十一周年，大会是由越南祖国战线中央委员会召集的。
    越南民主共和国医济部副部长、前西贡、堤岸抗战行政委员会主席范玉石在大会上讲话，叙述了南部人民为祖国的和平统一和独立民主所进行的斗争。
    大会最后通过了给南方人民的电文。
    出席昨天晚上大会的有孙德胜、潘继遂、长征、孙光阀以及各民主党派、群众团体和集结到北方来的南方干部和人民的代表等。
    在22日晚上，从南部集结到北方来的干部、部队和河内人民一万多人也曾举行集会，纪念越南南部抗战十一周年。
