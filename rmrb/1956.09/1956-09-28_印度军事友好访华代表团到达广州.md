Title: Indian Military Friendship Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1956-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 27th. The Indian military friendship delegation (formerly translated as the Indian Military Goodwill Delegation to China) led by Lieutenant General Thorat Nath Choudhuri of the Indian Army, arrived in Guangzhou by special plane from Hanoi this afternoon. The delegation is visiting China at the friendly invitation of Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China.

Welcoming the delegation at the airport today were Lieutenant General Liang Xingchu, commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou, and heads of various services and arms.

The Indian military friendship delegation consists of eight members. The members are Major General Hanmantrao Matandrao Mohite of the Army, Commodore Adhar Kumar Chatterji of the Navy, Air Commodore Pratap Chandra Lal, Brigadier David Datt, Brigadier Mohinder Singh Fuddi, Air Force Colonel Mohan Swarupa Chaturvendi, and Navy Lieutenant Commander Triloki Nath Kochar.

### 印度军事友好访华代表团到达广州

1956-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    印度军事友好访华代表团到达广州
    新华社广州27日电　由印度共和国陆军中将卓荣铎·纳特·乔杜里率领的印度军事友好访华代表团（前译印度军事访华亲善代表团），在今天下午从河内乘专机到达广州。代表团是应中华人民共和国国防部部长彭德怀元帅邀请来我国进行友好访问的。
    今天到机场欢迎的，有中国人民解放军广州驻军首长梁兴初中将和各军种兵种负责人。
    印度军事友好访华代表团由八人组成。团员有陆军少将汉曼特劳·马坦德劳·莫赫特、海军准将艾德巴·古玛尔·查特奇、空军准将普拉塔帕·昌德拉·拉尔、陆军准将戴维·德特、陆军准将穆欣德勒·辛格·弗迪、空军上校穆罕·斯华鲁帕·查托凡迪、海军中校特里洛基·纳特·柯恰尔。
