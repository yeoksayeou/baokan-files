Title: DPRK Supreme People's Assembly Delegation Visits Soviet Union at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1956-09-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 15th: A delegation from the DPRK Supreme People's Assembly left for Moscow by plane today. The delegation is visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

### 朝最高人民会议代表团  应邀去苏联访问

1956-09-16
第5版()
专栏：

    朝最高人民会议代表团
    应邀去苏联访问
    据新华社平壤15日电　朝鲜最高人民会议代表团今天乘飞机前往莫斯科。代表团是应苏联最高苏维埃的邀请到苏联去访问的。
