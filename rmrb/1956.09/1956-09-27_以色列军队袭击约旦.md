Title: Israeli Troops Attack Jordan
Author:
Date: 1956-09-27
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th, Amman Dispatch: The Jordanian Army Headquarters announced today that Israeli troops launched an unprecedented large-scale attack on Jordan on the evening of September 25th.

The communique stated that an estimated 5,000-strong Israeli infantry brigade, under artillery cover, attacked the villages of Wadi Fukin and Hassan, southwest of Bethlehem, at 9 p.m. yesterday. Jordanian forces fought the Israeli forces for several hours. The Israeli forces did not withdraw until 1:30 a.m. on September 26th.

Jordan has filed a complaint with the Mixed Armistice Commission regarding the Israeli aggression.

Israel's "People's Voice" pointed out today, when discussing the incident of Israeli troops invading Jordanian territory on the night of September 25th, that it was primarily Britain that hoped for tension on the Jordan-Israeli armistice line.

### 以色列军队袭击约旦

1956-09-27
第8版()
专栏：

    以色列军队袭击约旦
    据新华社26日讯　安曼消息：约旦陆军总部今天发表公报说，以色列军队在9月25日晚上对约旦进行了一次空前大规模的袭击。
    公报说，估计有五千人的一个以色列步兵旅在炮兵掩护下，在昨天晚上9时袭击了伯利恒西南的瓦底·夫金村和哈桑村。约旦部队同以色列部队战斗了几小时。以色列部队一直到9月26日晨1时半才撤退。
    约旦已经向停战混合委员会控诉以色列的侵袭。
    以色列“人民呼声报”今天在谈到9月25日夜间发生的以色列军队侵入约旦领土的事件时指出，首先是英国希望约旦—以色列停战线上局势紧张。
