Title: Today's Yao Mountains
Author: Zhang Luo
Date: 1956-09-05
Page: 4

    Today's Yao Mountains
    Photo by Zhang Luo
    In the undulating mountains of Liannan Yao Autonomous County, Guangdong Province, there is a new atmosphere everywhere. The Yao people here, like other ethnic groups, are enthusiastically building their new life.
    The right picture shows two Yao girls embroidering. Picturesque Sanpai Forest in Liannan Yao Autonomous County.
    Farmers in Liannan Yao Autonomous County have also organized agricultural production cooperatives. This is a member of the Guangming Agricultural and Forestry Production Cooperative in Jinkeng Township estimating yields in the rice fields.
      Liannan Yao Autonomous County has established health centers, and health clinics have been established in various districts, basically eliminating endemic diseases in the mountains. This is a medical staff member of the health center diagnosing Yao compatriots. Yao women purchase daily necessities at the Liannan Yao Autonomous County National Trade Company. Liannan Yao Autonomous County has established fifty-one elementary schools and one middle school. This is an elementary school student reviewing their lessons. (All of the above are Xinhua News Agency drafts)

### 今日瑶山
张洛
1956-09-05
第4版()
专栏：

    今日瑶山
    张洛摄
    在广东省峰峦起伏的连南瑶族自治县，到处是一片新气象。这里的瑶族人民和其他各族人民一样，在热情地建设着自己的新生活。
    右图是两个瑶族姑娘在刺绣。风景秀丽的连南瑶族自治县的三排林。
    连南瑶族自治县的农民，也组织了农业生产合作社。这是金坑乡光明农林生产合作社的社员在稻田里估计产量。
      连南瑶族自治县建立了卫生院，各区建立了卫生所，基本上消灭了山区的流行病。这是卫生院的医务人员在给瑶胞诊病。瑶族妇女在连南瑶族自治县民族贸易公司购买日用品。连南瑶族自治县已建立了五十一所小学和一所中学。这是一个小学的学生在温习功课。　（以上都是新华社稿）
