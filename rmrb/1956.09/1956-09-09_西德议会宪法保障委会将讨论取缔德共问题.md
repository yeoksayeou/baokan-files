Title: West German Parliamentary Committee on Constitutional Protection to Discuss Banning the German Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1956-09-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 8th: Bonn dispatch: The West German Federal Parliament's Committee on Constitutional Protection will discuss the West German authorities' measures to ban the German Communist Party within the next week.

### 西德议会宪法保障委会将讨论取缔德共问题

1956-09-09
第6版()
专栏：

    西德议会宪法保障委会将讨论取缔德共问题
    据新华社柏林8日电　波恩消息：西德联邦议院宪法保障委员会将在下个星期内就西德当局禁止德国共产党的措施进行讨论。
