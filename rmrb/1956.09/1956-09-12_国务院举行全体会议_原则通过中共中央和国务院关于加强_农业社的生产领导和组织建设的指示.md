Title: State Council Holds Plenary Session: In Principle, Approved the Directives of the CPC and the State Council on Strengthening Production Leadership and Organizational Construction of Agricultural Cooperatives
Author:
Date: 1956-09-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th: The 37th Plenary Session of the State Council was held on the afternoon of the 11th.
    After listening to the explanation by Deng Zihui, Minister of the Rural Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, the meeting approved in principle the directives of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the State Council on strengthening the production leadership and organizational construction of agricultural production cooperatives.
    The meeting approved the State Council's decision on the establishment of Huludao City in Liaoning Province, the State Council's decision on abolishing Sandu County and Songtao County and establishing the Sandu Shui Autonomous Prefecture and Songtao Miao Autonomous County, the State Council's decision on adjusting the administrative division of banners and counties under the jurisdiction of Chahar League in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the State Council's decision on the establishment of Linxia Hui Autonomous Prefecture, and the State Council's decision on the establishment of Diqing Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture, Ninglang Yi Autonomous County, and the abolition of Ninglang County.
    Finally, the meeting approved personnel appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行全体会议  原则通过中共中央和国务院关于加强  农业社的生产领导和组织建设的指示

1956-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行全体会议
    原则通过中共中央和国务院关于加强  农业社的生产领导和组织建设的指示
    新华社11日讯　国务院全体会议第三十七次会议，在11日下午举行。
    会议听了中共中央农村工作部部长、国务院副总理邓子恢的说明后，原则通过了中国共产党中央委员会、国务院关于加强农业生产合作社的生产领导和组织建设的指示。
    会议通过了国务院关于设置辽宁省葫芦岛市的决定，国务院关于撤销三都县、松桃县和设置三都水家族自治州、松桃苗族自治县的决定，国务院关于调整内蒙古自治区察哈尔盟所属旗县行政区划的决定，国务院关于设置临夏回族自治州的决定，国务院关于设置迪庆藏族自治州、宁蒗彝族自治县及撤销宁蒗县的决定。
    会议最后通过了任免事项。
