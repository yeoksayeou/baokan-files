Title: Public Bond Principal and Interest Payment Notice
Author:
Date: 1956-09-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th — Starting from September 29th this year, local People's Banks will cash the second interest payment of the 1954 National Economic Construction Bonds and the first interest payment of the 1955 National Economic Construction Bonds. Holders of bonds selected for principal repayment in the lottery can also simultaneously collect their principal.

The Ministry of Finance issued a notice on September 5th stating that the second principal and interest payment for the 1954 National Economic Construction Bonds and the first principal and interest payment for the 1955 National Economic Construction Bonds should commence on September 30th this year, according to regulations. However, this day falls on a Sunday. To accommodate the interests of bondholders and facilitate timely collection of principal and interest, the principal and interest payment work will be specially brought forward to commence on September 29th this year.

### 公债还本付息通知

1956-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    公债还本付息通知
    新华社7日讯　从今年9月29日开始，各地人民银行兑付1954年国家经济建设公债的第二次利息和1955年国家经济建设公债的第一次利息。抽签还本中签债券的持有人，还可以同时兑取本金。
    财政部在9月5日发出通知说，1954年国家经济建设公债第二次和1955年国家经济建设公债第一次还本付息，按规定应在今年9月30日开始兑付，但这一天正是星期日，为了照顾持券人的利益，及时兑取本息，今年特提前在9月29日开始办理本息兑付工作。
