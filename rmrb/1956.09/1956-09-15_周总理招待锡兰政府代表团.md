Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Ceylon Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-09-15
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 14th — Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, held a reception this afternoon to host the entire Ceylon Government Delegation headed by Claude Corea.
    At the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai raised his glass, wishing Ceylon prosperity and its people happiness.
    Also attending the reception were Vice Premiers of the State Council He Long and Chen Yi, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Cheng Qian and Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, and representatives from various democratic parties and social circles.
    Diplomatic officials from various countries also attended the reception.

### 周总理招待锡兰政府代表团

1956-09-15
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理招待锡兰政府代表团
    新华社14日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来今天下午举行酒会，招待以克劳德·科里亚为首的锡兰政府代表团全体人员。
    周恩来总理在酒会上举杯，祝锡兰繁荣、人民幸福。
    出席酒会的，还有国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、郭沫若、陈叔通，国防委员会副主席程潜、张治中，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，文化部部长沈雁冰以及各民主党派和社会各界人士等。
    各国外交官员们也出席了酒会。
