Title: Highway from Shua Jing Si to Ma'erkang Opens to Traffic
Author:
Date: 1956-09-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, 31st - Sichuan Province's Aba Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture's second highway - the Shua (Jing Si) Ma (Erkang) Highway was fully opened to traffic on the 28th.

This is a simple highway that winds entirely through mountains at altitudes of two to three thousand meters above sea level, with a total length of seventy-two kilometers. It connects to the Cheng-A Highway, and through post roads, connects the three counties of Da Jin, Xiao Jin, and Zhuosijia, which have good agricultural conditions, abundant local products, but are separated by high mountains.

### 刷经寺到马尔康公路通车

1956-09-02
第2版()
专栏：

    刷经寺到马尔康公路通车
    新华社成都31日电　四川省阿坝藏族自治州的第二条公路——刷（经寺）马（尔康）公路28日全线通车。
    这是一条全部盘旋在拔海二千到三千多公尺高山中的简易公路，全长七十二公里。它和成阿公路相连接，并通过驿道连着农业条件好、土特产丰富、但被高山阻隔的大金、小金、绰斯甲三县。
