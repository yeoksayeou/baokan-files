Title: Two Egyptian Painters Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-28
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: According to the Sino-Egyptian Cultural Agreement, Mohamed Ezzat Mustafa, Deputy Director of the Art Department of the Egyptian Ministry of Education and painter, and Abdel Salam El-Sherif, Egyptian painter, arrived in Beijing this afternoon to visit China and hold an Egyptian art exhibition.

### 埃及两画家到北京

1956-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    埃及两画家到北京
    新华社27日讯　根据中埃文化协定前来中国访问和举办埃及艺术展览的埃及教育部艺术司副司长、画家穆罕默德·埃扎特·穆斯塔法和埃及画家阿卜杜勒·萨拉姆·舍里夫在今天下午到达北京。
