Title: All-India Peace Council Calls for "Suez Month"
Author:
Date: 1956-09-30
Page: 4

    All-India Peace Council
    Calls for "Suez Month"
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, September 28th: The All-India Peace Council on September 27th called on the Indian people to hold a "Suez Month" in October to express the Indian people's support for "the Egyptian people defending their national sovereignty and preventing the forceful restoration of colonial control over their country," and to express the Indian people's "firm opposition to all threats of war currently being carried out by certain countries."
    Kitchlew, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, called on all parties and groups that support the Indian government's efforts to peacefully resolve the Suez issue to take the lead in educating public opinion on this issue, thereby making the Suez Month a success.
    The All-India Peace Council called on all peace committees across the country and other groups that hope for a peaceful resolution of the Suez Canal issue to hold meetings and demonstrations, collect signatures, and carry out other activities during "Suez Month."

### 全印和平理事会  号召举行“苏伊士月”

1956-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    全印和平理事会
    号召举行“苏伊士月”
    据新华社新德里28日电　全印和平理事会9月27日号召印度人民在10月份举行“苏伊士月”，表示印度人民支援“埃及人民保卫他们的国家主权并且不准对他们的国家强行恢复殖民主义控制”，同时表示印度人民“坚决反对某些国家目前正在进行的一切战争威胁”。
    全印和平理事会主席克其鲁号召那些支持印度政府为了和平解决苏伊士问题而进行努力的一切党派和团体，带头就这一问题对舆论进行教育，从而使苏伊士月获得成功。
    全印和平理事会号召全国一切和平委员会和希望苏伊士运河问题和平解决的其他团体，在“苏伊士月”中举行大会和示威、征集签名并且进行其他活动。
