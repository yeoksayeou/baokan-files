Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Hosts Banquet for Chinese and Soviet Mountaineering Teams
Author:
Date: 1956-09-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 1st. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions hosted a banquet tonight to welcome all members and staff of the Sino-Soviet joint mountaineering team.

    Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Bereczky, captain of the Sino-Soviet joint mountaineering team, both spoke at the banquet.

    Zhao Lingyun, Deputy Director of the Sports Commission of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, and Yang Kexian, a reporter from the Xinhua News Agency who participated in the mountaineering activity, were invited to attend tonight's banquet.

    Representatives from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, secretaries of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and representatives from various industrial trade unions also attended tonight's banquet.

### 全国总工会欢宴中苏爬山队员

1956-09-02
第1版()
专栏：

    全国总工会欢宴中苏爬山队员
    新华社1日讯　中华全国总工会今天晚上设宴欢迎中、苏混合爬山队全体队员和工作人员。
    中华全国总工会主席赖若愚和中、苏混合爬山队队长别列次基在宴会上都讲了话。
    陪同爬山队来北京的新疆维吾尔自治区的代表，自治区体育运动委员会副主任赵凌云，以及参加这次爬山活动报道的新华社记者杨克现应邀出席了今晚的宴会。
    出席今晚宴会作陪的有中苏友好协会的代表，中华全国总工会的书记们和各产业工会的代表等。
