Title: Burma Supports Egypt's Recovery of the Canal, Opposes the "Users' Association" Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-09-21
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, September 20th: The "Yangon Daily" quoted a Burmese government spokesman as saying that the Western countries' plan to establish a so-called "Suez Canal Users' Association" is a step hindering the resolution of the Suez issue.

The spokesman pointed out that the Burmese government believes that the Egyptian government has the right to nationalize the Suez Canal.

### 缅甸支持埃及收回运河  反对“使用国协会”计划

1956-09-21
第8版()
专栏：

    缅甸支持埃及收回运河
    反对“使用国协会”计划
    据新华社仰光20日电　“仰光日报”引述缅甸政府发言人的话说，西方国家建立所谓“苏彝士运河使用国协会”的计划是一个阻碍苏彝士问题获得解决的步骤。
    这位发言人指出，缅甸政府认为，埃及政府有权把苏彝士运河收归国有。
