Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation Arrives in Damascus for Visit
Author:
Date: 1956-09-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, September 5th: A delegation from the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, led by Shenin, Chairman of the Budget Committee of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet, arrived in Damascus on September 4th at the invitation of the Syrian Parliament.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the Damascus airport included Syrian Parliament Speaker Nazim al-Qudsi, Deputy Speaker of the Parliament Rafik Bashur, and Foreign Minister Salah al-Din Bitar.

Many Damascus citizens gathered in the square next to the airport. Everywhere were banners with the words: "Long live the Soviet Union, loyal friend of the Arabs!" "Long live Soviet-Arab friendship!"

### 苏联最高苏维埃代表团到达大马士革访问

1956-09-07
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃代表团到达大马士革访问
    据新华社5日讯　塔斯社大马士革5日讯，苏联最高苏维埃代表团，应叙利亚议会的邀请，在苏联最高苏维埃联盟院预算委员会主席谢宁率领下，于9月4日到达大马士革。
    到大马士革机场欢迎的有叙利亚议会议长奈绥姆·库德西、议会副议长拉菲克·巴舒尔、外交部长萨拉赫丁·比塔尔。
  许多大马士革公民聚集在机场旁的广场上。到处都是横旗，上面写着：“阿拉伯人的忠实朋友，苏联万岁！”“苏联—阿拉伯友好万岁！”
