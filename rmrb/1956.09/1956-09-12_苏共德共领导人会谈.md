Title: Meeting Between Leaders of the CPSU and the KPD
Author:
Date: 1956-09-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 11th: According to TASS, leaders of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (CPSU) held talks with a delegation from the Communist Party of Germany (KPD) on September 10th.

Participating in the meeting from the CPSU side were Suslov, Secretary of the Central Committee, and Ponomarev, a member of the Central Committee. From the KPD side were Reimann, First Secretary of the Central Committee, and Herzner, a member of the Central Committee.

### 苏共德共领导人会谈

1956-09-12
第5版()
专栏：

    苏共德共领导人会谈
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会领导人9月10日同德国共产党代表团举行了会谈。
    参加会谈的，苏联共产党方面是中央委员会书记苏斯洛夫和中央委员波诺马烈夫，德国共产党方面是中央委员会第一书记雷曼和中央委员赫茨纳。
