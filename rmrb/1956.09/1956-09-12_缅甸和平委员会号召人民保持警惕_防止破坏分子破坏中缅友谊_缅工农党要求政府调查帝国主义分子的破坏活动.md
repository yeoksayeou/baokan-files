Title: Burmese Peace Committee Calls on People to Be Vigilant, Preventing Saboteurs from Undermining Sino-Burmese Friendship; Burmese Workers and Peasants Party Demands Government Investigation of Imperialist Sabotage

Author:

Date: 1956-09-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, September 11th: The Burmese Peace Preservation Committee issued a statement on September 10th pointing out that certain groups, both inside and outside Burma, are now using the Sino-Burmese border issue to undermine the friendship between the two countries, attempting to drag Burma into the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization.

The statement called on the Burmese people to be vigilant against the activities of saboteurs and to strengthen the friendship between Burma and China.

The statement said that the friendship between Burma and China has been consolidated and developed. Economic and cultural exchanges are closer than ever before. This friendly relationship has made a significant contribution to peace in Asia and the world.

The statement called on the governments of Burma and China to resolve the border issue based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, September 10th: According to Rangoon newspapers today, the Second All-Burma Conference of the Burmese Workers and Peasants Party, in a resolution, called on the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League, the Burmese National United Front, the Burmese Peace Preservation Committee, and other organizations and parties to hold mass rallies throughout the country to support the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence proposed by Zhou Enlai and U Nu.

The conference also passed a resolution calling on the Burmese government to investigate the activities instigated by imperialist elements to undermine the friendship between Burma and China, and demanding an end to the civil war.

The Second All-Burma Conference of the Burmese Workers and Peasants Party began on September 5th and concluded on September 7th.

### 缅甸和平委员会号召人民保持警惕  防止破坏分子破坏中缅友谊  缅工农党要求政府调查帝国主义分子的破坏活动

1956-09-12
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸和平委员会号召人民保持警惕
    防止破坏分子破坏中缅友谊
    缅工农党要求政府调查帝国主义分子的破坏活动
    新华社仰光11日电　缅甸保卫世界和平委员会9月10日发表声明指出：缅甸国内和国外的某些集团现在正利用中缅边境问题来破坏这两个国家之间的友谊，企图把缅甸拖进东南亚条约组织。
    声明要求缅甸人民对破坏者的活动保持警惕，并且加强缅甸和中国之间的友谊。
    声明说，缅甸和中国之间的友谊已经得到巩固和发展。经济和文化的交流比以前任何时候都更密切。这种友好关系对亚洲和世界和平作出了巨大的贡献。
    声明要求缅中两国政府根据和平共处五项原则来解决边境问题。
    新华社仰光10日电　据仰光报纸今天报道说，缅甸工农党第二届全缅会议在一项决议中，要求反法西斯人民自由同盟、缅甸民族团结阵线、缅甸保卫世界和平委员会和其他组织、党派在全国各地召开群众大会，来支持周恩来和吴努提出的和平共处五项原则。
    会议还通过决议，要求缅甸政府调查帝国主义分子为了破坏缅甸和中国之间的友谊所煽动的活动，并且要求停止内战。
    缅甸工农党第二届全缅会议是在9月5日开始举行的，会议已经在9月7日结束。
