Title: US Exerts Pressure on Yugoslavia, Refuses to Provide Wheat Aid
Author:
Date: 1956-09-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 2nd - According to United Press International, quoting well-informed sources, the US State Department has refused Yugoslavia's request for wheat aid, using the excuse that Congress has not yet made a decision. Yugoslavia submitted this request to the US in early summer, estimating a shortage of 300,000 tons of wheat this winter.

Well-informed sources say that the US State Department told Yugoslavia about ten days ago that the United States could not agree to supply Yugoslavia with wheat because the special study of aid to Yugoslavia stipulated by Congress is still underway.

Last month, the US International Cooperation Administration announced on the same grounds that the US had stopped delivering an alleged 380 jet aircraft to Yugoslavia.

Since the normalization of relations between Yugoslavia and the Soviet Union, the United States has been putting pressure on Yugoslavia in an attempt to prevent the consolidation and development of Soviet-Yugoslav relations. Prior to this, the US Congress stipulated in this year's foreign aid appropriation bill that no military aid should be given to Yugoslavia from this fiscal year's appropriations.

### 美国对南施加压力  拒绝给予小麦援助

1956-09-03
第6版()
专栏：

    美国对南施加压力
    拒绝给予小麦援助
    据新华社2日讯　华盛顿消息：据合众社引消息灵通人士的话报道，美国国务院已经以国会还没有作出决定为借口，拒绝了南斯拉夫向美国提出的给它以小麦援助的请求。南斯拉夫这项要求是在今年夏初向美国提出的，它估计今年冬天缺少三十万吨小麦。
    消息灵通人士说，美国国务院在大约十天以前告诉南斯拉夫，美国不能同意供给南斯拉夫小麦，因为国会规定的对南斯拉夫的援助所作的特别研究仍然在进行。
    美国国际合作署在上个月以同样的理由宣布，美国已经停止向南斯拉夫交给据说为数三百八十架的喷气式飞机。
    自从南斯拉夫同苏联关系正常化以来，美国一直在对南斯拉夫施加压力，力图阻挠南苏关系巩固和发展。在这以前，美国国会在今年的援外拨款法案中规定不得用本财政年度的拨款给南斯拉夫以军事援助。
