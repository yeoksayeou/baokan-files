Title: Afghan Circles Vie to See Chinese Song and Dance; Afghan Acting Foreign Minister Receives Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-09-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, 6th – The Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation, currently visiting here, gave a special performance on the evening of September 4th for officers of the Afghan armed forces.

A total of 1,500 Afghan generals and officers of all ranks watched the performance. Among them were General Nik Mod, commander of the Kabul Army First Division, General Mod Issa, president of the Military Academy, General Lekreia Tazi, commander of the Ordnance Corps, General Mod Ali, director of the Arsenal, and other senior generals.

They greatly enjoyed the Chinese songs and dances. They were particularly interested in programs such as the "Plate Dance", "Uproar in the Dragon Palace" and "Crossroads Inn". When the performers first performed the Afghan dance "Grape Picking Dance" that they had just learned, thunderous applause erupted from the audience.

After the performance, General Mod Ali, director of the Arsenal, walked onto the stage, thanked Ma Hanbing, head of the Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation, and presented a silver cup as a souvenir.

The Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation's several performances have been warmly welcomed by the Kabul audience. On the evening of September 3rd, when the delegation held its thirteenth performance in Kabul, more than a thousand people sat in the theater an hour before the official start of the show to wait for the performance to begin. More than 600 people climbed onto the roofs around the theater to watch the performance. More than 2,000 people watched on the bridge outside the theater, some riding camels, and others standing on carriages.

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, 6th – Afghan Acting Foreign Minister Mohammad Wal received Ma Hanbing, head of the Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation, and Song Xiao, deputy head, on the morning of September 5th.

Also present at the meeting was Ding Guoyu, Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan. During the talks, both sides expressed their hope to strengthen cultural exchanges between the two countries and agreed to continue further discussions on this issue.

### 阿富汗各界争看中国歌舞  阿代理外交大臣接见我文化艺术代表团

1956-09-07
第5版()
专栏：

    阿富汗各界争看中国歌舞
    阿代理外交大臣接见我文化艺术代表团
    新华社喀布尔6日电　正在这里访问的中国文化艺术代表团9月4日晚上特别为阿富汗武装部队的军官们举行演出。
    观看这次表演的有阿富汗的将军和各级军官共一千五百人。其中有喀布尔陆军第一师师长尼克·莫德将军、军事学院院长莫德·伊萨将军、辎重兵司令列克雷雅·塔齐将军、兵工署署长莫德·阿里将军和其他高级将领。
    他们很喜爱中国的歌曲和舞蹈。特别使他们感到兴趣的是“盘子舞”、“闹龙宫”和“三岔口”等节目。当演员们第一次演出他们刚刚学会的阿富汗舞蹈“采葡萄舞”的时候，台下响起了雷鸣一样的掌声。
    表演结束以后，兵工署署长莫德·阿里将军走上舞台，向中国文化艺术代表团团长马寒冰表示感谢，并且赠送银杯一只作为纪念。
    中国文化艺术代表团的几次演出都受到喀布尔观众的热烈欢迎。9月3日晚上代表团举行在喀布尔的第十三次演出的时候，有一千多人在正式开演之前一小时就坐在剧场里等待开演。有六百多人爬到剧场周围的屋顶上观看表演。还有两千多人在剧场外面的桥上观看，有人骑在骆驼上，也有人站在马车上。
    新华社喀布尔6日电　阿富汗代理外交大臣穆罕默德·瓦尔在9月5日上午接见了中国文化艺术代表团团长马寒冰和副团长宋肖。
    接见时在座的还有中国驻阿富汗大使丁国钰。双方在交谈中都表示希望加强两国之间的文化交流，并且同意就这个问题继续作进一步的商谈。
