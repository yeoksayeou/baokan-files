Title: Japanese Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, September 15th: A twenty-five member Japanese Private Railway Workers' Union Confederation delegation, invited by the All-China Railway Workers' Union to visit China, arrived in Beijing on the evening of September 15th. The head of the delegation is Yasutsune Ryoichi, Secretary-General of the Japanese Private Railway Workers' Union Confederation Headquarters. The deputy heads are Itakawa Masago, Executive Committee Chairman of the Tobu Transportation Workers' Union, and Kitamura Tomizo, Executive Committee Chairman of the Kinki Nippon Railway Workers' Union.

### 日本—工会考察团到京

1956-09-16
第3版()
专栏：

    日本—工会考察团到京
    据新华社15日讯　应中国铁路工会全国委员会的邀请来中国访问的日本私营铁道工会总联合会中国考察团一行二十五人，在9月15日晚间到达北京。考察团团长是日本私营铁道工会总联合会本部书记长安恒良一，副团长是东武交通工会执行委员长板川正五和近畿日本铁道工会执行委员长北村富藏。
