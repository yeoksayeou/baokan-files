Title: World Student Congress Issues Appeal to Students Worldwide, Demanding an End to the Arms Race and the Opening of a Path to Lasting Peace
Author:
Date: 1956-09-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 4th: The Fourth World Student Congress concluded in Prague on the morning of September 3rd, after holding eight days of meetings.

The Congress adopted an appeal to students worldwide, stating: Students all over the world sincerely hope for peace. They absolutely do not want to meet on the battlefield. The appeal expressed the students' wish for the conclusion of an agreement ending the arms race and opening a path to lasting peace. The appeal pointed out that maintaining peace depends to a large extent on the joint efforts of students cooperating with people all over the world. Students are determined to do their utmost to ensure peace.

The Congress revised the charter of the International Student Union in accordance with the current situation of the world student movement, in order to promote cooperation and unity among students worldwide.

The Congress elected the president, vice-presidents, and new executive committee and secretariat of the International Student Union.

Jiri Pelikan was re-elected as President of the International Student Union.

### 世界学生代表大会发表告世界学生书  要求结束军备竞赛打开建立持久和平的道路

1956-09-05
第5版()
专栏：

    世界学生代表大会发表告世界学生书
    要求结束军备竞赛打开建立持久和平的道路
    新华社布拉格4日电　第四次世界学生代表大会在举行了8天的会议以后，9月3日上午在布拉格闭幕。
    大会通过了告全世界学生书。其中说：全世界的学生都衷心地希望和平。他们绝不希望在战场上相见。书中表示了学生们的愿望，这就是缔结一项结束军备竞赛和打开建立持久和平的道路的协议。告全世界学生书指出，维护和平在很大程度上要依靠同全世界人民合作的学生们的共同努力。学生们决心要竭尽他们的力量以保证和平。
    大会按照目前世界学生运动的形势修改了国际学生联合会的章程以促进世界学生的合作和团结。
    大会选举了国际学生联合会的主席、副主席和新的执行委员会和书记。
    尤里·贝利康再度当选为国际学联主席。
