Title: Party Spirit and Humanity
Author: Xiao Luo
Date: 1956-09-05
Page: 8

    Party Spirit and Humanity
    Xiao Luo
    After a certain advanced agricultural production cooperative held a "cock-crow meeting" (an all-night meeting that lasts until the rooster crows, as it is called in the countryside), a cooperative cadre timidly approached the cooperative director.

    Cadre: (Pointing to his bloodshot eyes) Director, my eyes hurt a lot. I'd like to take a day off tomorrow to rest.

    Director: (Casually) How many eyes are red?

    Cadre: (Blankly) One.

    Director: (Smiling slyly) Oh, one. Then cover up that red eye and use the other good eye to work. Comrade, work is busy, many of our cooperative cadres have bloodshot eyes. This is precisely the time to test our Party spirit.

    Author's aside: Director, this is also the time to test your humanity.

### 党性和人性
小螺
1956-09-05
第8版()
专栏：

    党性和人性
    小螺
    某高级农业生产合作社开了“鸡鸣会”（彻夜开会，直至鸡鸣方罢，乡间称为“鸡鸣会”）以后，有个社干怯生生地走到社主任的面前。
    社干：（指着自己熬红了的眼睛）主任，我的眼睛痛得很，我想明天请个假休息一天。
    主任：（若无其事地）几个眼睛红了？
    社干：（茫茫然）一个。
    主任：（狡猾地笑了）呵，一个。那就把这个红眼捂起来，用另一个好眼做工作。同志，工作忙，我们社干熬红了眼的有的是，这正是考验我们党性的时候。
    作者旁白：主任，这也是考验你的人性的时候。
