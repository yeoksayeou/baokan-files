Title: Czechoslovakia to Supply Equipment Again to Help India Build a Power Plant
Author:
Date: 1956-09-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 14th: According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency, representatives of the Czechoslovak Foreign Trade Corporation, after recent negotiations with relevant Indian parties, have decided to supply all the equipment needed for India's new Shahpur Power Plant.

    This new factory will be put into operation in 1958, and the total power generation of the factory is 10,000 kilowatts.

    This is the second time Czechoslovakia has provided equipment to help India build a power plant. The equipment provided for the Surat power plant was shipped this spring.

### 捷又一次供应装备帮助印度建立发电厂

1956-09-15
第6版()
专栏：

    捷又一次供应装备帮助印度建立发电厂
    新华社布拉格14日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道：捷克斯洛伐克对外贸易公司的代表最近和印度有关方面进行谈判以后，决定供给印度新建的沙普尔发电厂所需的全部装备。
    这个新工厂将在1958年投入生产，工厂的总发电量是一万瓩。
    捷克斯洛伐克提供装备帮助印度建立发电厂，这算是第二次。前一次提供给苏拉特发电厂的装备已经在今年春天运去。
