Title: Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway Workers Use Mass Blasting to Clear the Black Mountain Gorge
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jiuquan, 8th. On September 5th, Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway construction workers used 230 tons of explosives to blast away over 80,000 cubic meters of rock in the Black Mountain Gorge, overcoming the final key obstacle in the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway's passage from Gansu into Xinjiang.

The Black Mountain Gorge is located in the western part of the Hexi Corridor in Gansu Province, only eighty-odd kilometers from the Xinjiang border. The terrain here is undulating and the rock is hard. Using large-scale blasting to clear the Black Mountain Gorge not only saved a great deal of labor but also greatly shortened the construction period.

### 兰新路职工用大爆破法打通了黑山峡

1956-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    兰新路职工用大爆破法打通了黑山峡
    新华社酒泉8日电　9月5日，兰新铁路筑路职工用二百三十吨炸药在黑山峡炸除了八万多立方公尺的石方，突破了兰新铁路从甘肃进入新疆的最后一个关键工程的障碍。
    黑山峡在甘肃省河西走廊的西部，离新疆边境只有八十多公里。这里山势起伏，石质坚硬，用大规模爆破的方法来打通黑山峡，不仅节省了大量的劳动力，而且大大缩短了工期。
