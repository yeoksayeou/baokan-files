Title: He Long, Chen Yi, and Ulanhu Host Overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macao Compatriots Visiting Groups
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 28th: Vice Premiers of the State Council, He Long, Chen Yi, and Ulanhu, hosted a banquet at the Beijing Hotel today at noon for the Overseas Chinese Visiting Group and the Hong Kong and Macao Compatriots Visiting Group.

At the banquet, Vice Premier He Long proposed a toast to the great unity of the people of the motherland, overseas Chinese, and Hong Kong and Macao compatriots, and to the advancement of the great motherland's unification and the victory of socialism. In his speech, Vice Premier Chen Yi said that today's banquet is a banquet for the big family of the motherland. Chen Jiageng, a member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, also spoke at the banquet. He said: As the saying goes, "Seeing once is better than hearing a hundred times." Today, everyone is touring various parts of the motherland and can naturally see very clearly whether the motherland is good or bad. He Xiangning, Chairman of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, expressed hope at the banquet that the visiting groups would offer more criticism of the shortcomings in various aspects of the motherland's work, and help the government to correct the shortcomings and jointly improve the motherland's various construction undertakings.

At the banquet, representatives of the Overseas Chinese Visiting Groups from India, Indonesia, Myanmar, and the Hong Kong Chinese Reform Association successively presented flags to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou, which were accepted on their behalf by Vice Premiers He Long, Chen Yi, and Ulanhu, respectively. Basiji Daji, a Tibetan compatriot from India, also presented a Hada to Chairman Mao at the meeting, which was accepted on his behalf by Vice Premier He Long.

More than 1,000 people attended today's banquet, including: Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun, heads of various departments of the government and democratic parties, and people from all walks of life in the capital.

### 贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫招待华侨和港澳同胞两观光团

1956-09-29
第2版()
专栏：

    贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫招待华侨和港澳同胞两观光团
    新华社28日讯　国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫今天中午在北京饭店设宴招待华侨回国观光团和港澳同胞观光团。
    贺龙副总理在宴会上提议为祖国人民和海外华侨、港澳同胞的大团结，为伟大祖国的统一和社会主义的胜利前进而干杯。陈毅副总理在讲话中说，今天的宴会是一次祖国大家庭的宴会。全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员陈嘉庚也在宴会上讲了话。他说：俗语讲得好，“百闻不如一见”，今天大家在祖国各地观光，自然可以很清楚地看到祖国是好是坏。华侨事务委员会主任何香凝在宴会上希望观光团对祖国各方面工作的缺点多提出批评，并且帮助政府改正缺点，共同来搞好祖国的各种建设事业。
    在宴会上，印度、印度尼西亚、缅甸等国华侨回国观光团和香港华人革新协会的代表先后向毛主席、周总理献旗，由副总理贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫分别代为接受。印度藏族侨胞巴西大吉也在会上向毛主席献了哈达，由贺龙副总理代为接受。
    出席今天宴会的有：国务院秘书长习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，政府各部门和各民主党派负责人以及首都各界人士等一千多人。
