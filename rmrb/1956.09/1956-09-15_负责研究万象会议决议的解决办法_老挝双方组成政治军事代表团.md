Title: Lao Sides Form Political and Military Delegations to Study Solutions to Vientiane Conference Resolutions
Author:
Date: 1956-09-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 14th -- According to Vietnam News Agency reports: Lao newspapers report that the Lao Royal Government and the Lao Fighting Forces have formed their political and military delegations. They will be responsible for studying the solutions to various issues raised in the joint communique recently signed in Vientiane.

    The Royal Government's political delegation is led by Kong Sananikone, and the military delegation is led by Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Buazi.

    The Lao Fighting Forces' political delegation is led by Phoumi Vongvichit, and the military delegation consists of Colonel Phung Sipaseut and others.

    The Lao Fighting Forces delegation is scheduled to arrive in Vientiane in mid-September, and negotiations are about to begin.

    In a letter to the Lao Royal Government introducing the members of the Lao Fighting Forces delegation, Prince Souphanouvong expressed his hope that the negotiations will achieve complete success, just like the recent Vientiane talks.

### 负责研究万象会议决议的解决办法  老挝双方组成政治军事代表团

1956-09-15
第6版()
专栏：

    负责研究万象会议决议的解决办法
    老挝双方组成政治军事代表团
    新华社河内14日电　据越南通讯社讯：老挝的报纸报道，老挝王国政府和寮国战斗部队已经组成了他们的政治和军事代表团。它们将负责研究最近在万象签订的联合公报中所提出的各个问题的解决办法。
    王国政府政治代表团由贡·萨纳尼空率领，军事代表团由外交部副大臣布阿齐率领。
    寮国战斗部队政治代表团由富米·冯维希率领，军事代表团由冯·西帕克苏特上校等人组成。
    寮国战斗部队代表团预定在9月中旬到达万象，谈判即将开始。
    苏发努冯亲王在他的一封致老挝王国政府的介绍寮国战斗部队代表团成员的信里表示，他希望谈判将获得完满的结果，像最近的万象会谈一样。
