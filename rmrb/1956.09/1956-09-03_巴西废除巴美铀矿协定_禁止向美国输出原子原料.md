Title: Brazil Abolishes Brazil-U.S. Uranium Mining Agreement; Bans Export of Atomic Materials to the United States
Author:
Date: 1956-09-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 1st (TASS): According to a "New York Times" correspondent in Rio de Janeiro, the Brazilian government has banned the export of atomic materials to the United States and announced the abolition of the agreement with the United States on joint exploration of uranium deposits in Brazil.

The reporter said: "This action was taken at a time when a broad anti-American movement is rising in Brazil."

### 巴西废除巴美铀矿协定  禁止向美国输出原子原料

1956-09-03
第6版()
专栏：

    巴西废除巴美铀矿协定
    禁止向美国输出原子原料
    据新华社2日讯　塔斯社纽约1日讯：据“纽约时报”驻里约热内卢记者报道，巴西政府已经禁止向美国输出原子原料，并且宣布废除同美国缔结的关于联合勘察巴西的铀矿产地的协定。
    这个记者说：“这种行动是在巴西的广泛的反美运动日益高涨的时候采取的。”
