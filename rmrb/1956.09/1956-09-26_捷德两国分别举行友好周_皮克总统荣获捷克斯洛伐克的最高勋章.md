Title: Czechoslovakia and East Germany hold Friendship Weeks; President Pieck Receives Czechoslovakia's Highest Order

Author:

Date: 1956-09-26
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 25th: Czechoslovakia is holding a Czech-German Friendship Week starting from September 24th.

This year's Friendship Week includes extensive celebrations. Public gatherings with delegations of East German working people participating will be held in all state capitals. Several sports competitions will also be held to commemorate the Friendship Week between the Czech and German peoples.

A 21-member delegation from the Czechoslovak National Front has left Prague for Berlin to participate in the activities commemorating this Friendship Week. The delegation is led by Minister of Justice Škoda.

A 20-member delegation from the National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany, led by the Chairman of the National Council, Professor Correns, arrived in Prague on the 23rd.

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: TASS, Berlin, September 24th: The German-Czechoslovak Friendship Week, jointly initiated by the National Council of the National Front of Democratic Germany and the Association for Foreign Cultural Relations, opened grandly in Berlin on the 24th. Prime Minister Grotewohl of Democratic Germany and other leaders of the Republic attended the rally held at the Friedrichstadt-Palast. Chairman Dieckmann of the Presidium of the People's Chamber of Democratic Germany and the head of the Czechoslovak delegation attending the German-Czech Friendship Week, Minister of Justice Škoda, both spoke at the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 25th: According to a report by the German News Agency: Czechoslovak Ambassador Klika, on behalf of Czechoslovak President Zápotocký, presented the highest Czechoslovak order, the Order of the White Lion First Class, to Democratic German President Pieck in Berlin on the 24th, praising his contribution to strengthening the friendship between the Czech and German peoples.

### 捷德两国分别举行友好周  皮克总统荣获捷克斯洛伐克的最高勋章

1956-09-26
第7版()
专栏：

    捷德两国分别举行友好周
    皮克总统荣获捷克斯洛伐克的最高勋章
    据新华社布拉格25日电　捷克斯洛伐克从9月24日起举行捷德友好周。
    今年的友好周有广泛的庆祝活动。在所有的州中心里，都要举行有德国劳动人民的代表团参加的公众集会。为了纪念捷德两国人民的友好周，还将举行若干运动比赛。
    捷克斯洛伐克民族阵线的二十一人代表团已经离开布拉格前往柏林参加纪念这个友好周的活动。代表团是由司法部长什科达率领的。
    民主德国全国阵线全国委员会的二十人代表团在全国委员会主席柯伦斯教授率领下已经在23日到达布拉格。
    据新华社25日讯　塔斯社柏林24日讯：民主德国全国阵线全国委员会和对外文化协会联合发起的德国—捷克斯洛伐克友好周24日在柏林举行了隆重的开幕式。民主德国总理格罗提渥和共和国其他领导人出席了在腓特烈宫举行的集会。民主德国人民议院主席团主席狄克曼和前来参加德捷友好周的捷克斯洛伐克代表团团长、司法部长什科达都在会上讲了话。
    新华社柏林25日电　据德意志通讯社报道：捷克斯洛伐克大使克利卡代表捷克斯洛伐克总统萨波托斯基，24日在柏林把捷克斯洛伐克的最高勋章一级白狮勋章授给民主德国总统皮克，表扬他在加强捷德两国人民的友谊的工作中所作的贡献。
