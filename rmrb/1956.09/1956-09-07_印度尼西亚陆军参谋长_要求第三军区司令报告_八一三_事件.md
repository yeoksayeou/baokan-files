Title: Indonesian Army Chief of Staff Demands Report on the "August 13th" Incident from Third Military Region Commander
Author:
Date: 1956-09-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 5th. Indonesia News Agency today quoted sources close to the army as saying that Army Chief of Staff Nasution has requested that Colonel Kawilarang, the former commander of the Third Military Region, report on the "August 13th" incident, in which he ordered the arrest of Foreign Minister Abdulgani, before leaving to take up his new post.

These sources said that the Army Chief of Staff has asked Kawilarang to explain on what grounds and evidence he took such action on August 13th, during his tenure as commander of the Third Military Region. These sources said that Kawilarang will submit a report on this incident before he leaves for Washington on September 7th.

When reporters asked Army Spokesman Piengapi about this matter, he only replied, "This is not impossible."

### 印度尼西亚陆军参谋长  要求第三军区司令报告“八一三”事件

1956-09-07
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚陆军参谋长
    要求第三军区司令报告“八一三”事件
    新华社雅加达5日电　印度尼西亚新闻社今天援引接近陆军的人士的话说，陆军参谋长纳苏蒂安已经要求前第三军区司令卡维拉朗上校在动身去就任新职之前报告下令拘捕外交部长阿卜杜加尼的“八一三”事件的情况。
    这些人士说，陆军参谋长已经要求卡维拉朗说明，8月13日在第三军区司令的任内，他是根据什么原因和证据来采取那种步骤的。这些人士说，卡维拉朗将在他9月7日动身前往华盛顿之前提出关于这个事件的报告。
    当新闻记者向陆军发言人皮恩加比询问这个问题的时候，他只回答说：“这并不是不可能的。”
