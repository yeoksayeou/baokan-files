Title: Bulgarian Embassy Holds Reception Celebrating the National Day of the People's Republic of Bulgaria
Author:
Date: 1956-09-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th — The Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of Bulgaria in China, Mr. Etropolski, held a reception this evening to celebrate the National Day of the People's Republic of Bulgaria.

    Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China; Li Jishen and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Chen Yi and Ulanhu, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; and Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, attended the reception.

    At the beginning of the reception, the Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Bulgarian Embassy, Mr. Etropolski, invited his more than four hundred Chinese and foreign guests to raise their glasses to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China.

    Vice Chairman Zhu De then proposed a toast to the unbreakable friendship between the peoples of China and Bulgaria, and to the health of the leaders of the Party and government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria.

    The reception was held in the hall of the Beijing Hotel. Also present at the reception this evening were responsible persons from various departments of Chinese state organs, generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and people from all walks of life in Beijing.

    Diplomats from various countries to China also attended the reception.

### 庆祝保加利亚人民共和国国庆节  保加利亚大使馆举行招待会

1956-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝保加利亚人民共和国国庆节
    保加利亚大使馆举行招待会
    新华社9日讯　保加利亚人民共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办埃特罗波尔斯基，今晚举行招待会，庆祝保加利亚人民共和国国庆节。
    中华人民共和国副主席朱德，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、陈叔通，国务院副总理陈毅、乌兰夫，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，外交部副部长姬鹏飞等都出席了招待会。
    在招待会开始的时候，保加利亚大使馆临时代办埃特罗波尔斯基请他的四百多位中外客人举杯为中华人民共和国毛泽东主席的健康干杯。
    朱德副主席接着提议为中保两国人民之间牢不可破的友谊、为保加利亚人民共和国的党和政府的领导人的健康干杯。
    招待会是在北京饭店的大厅里举行的。今晚出席招待会的还有中国国家机关各部门的负责人，中国人民解放军的将军们和北京社会各界人士。
    各国驻中国的外交官们也出席了招待会。
