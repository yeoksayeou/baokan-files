Title: Prime Minister Acharya Calls on Vice-Chairman Zhu De
Author:
Date: 1956-09-30
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. The Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Tanka Prasad Acharya, and his wife paid a visit to the Vice-Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Zhu De, and his wife this afternoon.

During the visit, the hosts and guests had a cordial conversation.

This morning, the Foreign Affairs Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Nepal, Narapratap Thapa, and the Secretary of the Ministry of Planning and Development and the Ministry of Industry and Commerce, Saidar Bhim Bahadur Pande, paid a visit to the Minister of Foreign Trade, Ye Jizhuang. The Secretary of the Ministry of Planning and Development and the Ministry of Industry and Commerce of the Kingdom of Nepal, Saidar Bhim Bahadur Pande, also paid a visit to the Minister of Communications, Zhang Bojun.

### 阿查里雅首相拜会朱德副主席

1956-09-30
第2版()
专栏：

    阿查里雅首相拜会朱德副主席
    新华社29日讯　尼泊尔王国首相坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅和夫人今天下午拜会了中华人民共和国副主席朱德和夫人。
    拜会时宾主间进行了亲切的谈话。
    今天上午，尼泊尔王国外交部外事秘书纳腊普腊塔普·塔帕和计划发展部及工商部秘书赛达尔·比姆·巴哈杜尔·潘德，前往拜会了对外贸易部部长叶季壮。尼泊尔王国计划发展部及工商部秘书赛达尔·比姆·巴哈杜尔·潘德并且前往拜会了交通部部长章伯钧。
