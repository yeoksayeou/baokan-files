Title: Hungary and Cambodia Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 28th. According to the Vietnam News Agency: the Cambodian News Agency reports that the Hungarian People's Republic and the Kingdom of Cambodia have decided to establish diplomatic relations based on the principles of peaceful coexistence.

    The purpose of this decision is to promote political, economic, and cultural relations between the two countries.
    The issue of exchanging diplomatic representatives at the level of ministers will be discussed in New Delhi.

### 匈柬决定建交

1956-09-29
第8版()
专栏：

    匈柬决定建交
    新华社河内28日电　据越南通讯社讯：柬埔寨通讯社报道，匈牙利人民共和国和柬埔寨王国根据和平共处的原则已经决定建立外交关系。
    这个决定的目的是要促进两国间的政治、经济和文化关系。
    互派公使级外交代表的问题将在新德里讨论。
