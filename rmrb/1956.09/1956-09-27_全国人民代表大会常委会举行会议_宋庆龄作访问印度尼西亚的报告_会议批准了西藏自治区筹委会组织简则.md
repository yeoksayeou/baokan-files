Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Meeting; Soong Ching-ling Reports on Visit to Indonesia; Meeting Approves Organizational Regulations for the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region
Author:
Date: 1956-09-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 26th — The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 47th meeting today.
    Vice Chairman Soong Ching-ling delivered a report at the meeting on her visit to Indonesia (full text on page 6). The meeting discussed the report and unanimously agreed that Vice Chairman Soong Ching-ling's visit to Indonesia further strengthened and developed the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Indonesia, and played a significant role in promoting the cause of peace and promoting more friendly relations between overseas Chinese in Indonesia and the Indonesian people.
    After listening to the explanation by Ulanhu, Vice Premier of the State Council and Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, the meeting discussed and approved the organizational regulations for the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议  宋庆龄作访问印度尼西亚的报告  会议批准了西藏自治区筹委会组织简则

1956-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议
    宋庆龄作访问印度尼西亚的报告
    会议批准了西藏自治区筹委会组织简则
    新华社26日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会今天举行第四十七次会议。
    宋庆龄副委员长在会上作了访问印度尼西亚的报告（全文见第六版）。会议就这个报告进行了讨论，一致认为，宋庆龄副委员长这次访问印度尼西亚，进一步加强和发展了中国和印度尼西亚的友好合作关系，并且对促进和平事业和促进在印度尼西亚的华侨同印度尼西亚人民更加友好相处方面都起了很大作用。
    会议在听取了国务院副总理兼民族事务委员会主任乌兰夫的说明后，讨论并且批准了西藏自治区筹备委员会组织简则。
