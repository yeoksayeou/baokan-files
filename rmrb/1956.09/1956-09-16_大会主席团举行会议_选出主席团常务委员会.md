Title: Presidium of the Congress Holds Meeting; Elects Standing Committee of the Presidium
Author:
Date: 1956-09-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th -- The Presidium of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China held a meeting tonight and elected Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Kang Sheng, Peng Zhen, Dong Biwu, Lin Boqu, Zhang Wentian, Peng Dehuai, Lin Biao, Deng Xiaoping and other thirteen people to form the Standing Committee of the Congress Presidium.

### 大会主席团举行会议  选出主席团常务委员会

1956-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

    大会主席团举行会议
    选出主席团常务委员会
    新华社15日讯　中国共产党第八次全国代表大会主席团今晚举行会议，推选了毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、陈云、康生、彭真、董必武、林伯渠、张闻天、彭德怀、林彪、邓小平等十三人组成大会主席团的常务委员会。
