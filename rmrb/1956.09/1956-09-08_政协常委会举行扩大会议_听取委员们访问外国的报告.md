Title: CPPCC Standing Committee Holds Enlarged Meeting to Hear Reports from Committee Members on Visits to Foreign Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-09-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th - The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held its 28th meeting (enlarged) today.

    At the meeting, Standing Committee member Shi Liang reported on her visit to Czechoslovakia from late June to mid-July this year, when she was invited to participate in the National Congress of the Czechoslovak Women's Committee. Standing Committee member Qian Duansheng reported on his trip to Canada in August this year at the invitation of Eaton of the United States to participate in an international affairs discussion. National Committee member Liu Wang Liming also reported at the meeting on her trip to West Germany to attend the 20th Congress of the World's Woman's Christian Temperance Union.

    After listening to the explanation of Mei Gongbin, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee, the meeting discussed and adopted the handling opinions on the proposals submitted by the Second Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC to the Standing Committee for consultation with relevant departments for handling and reviewing.

### 政协常委会举行扩大会议  听取委员们访问外国的报告

1956-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    政协常委会举行扩大会议
    听取委员们访问外国的报告
    新华社7日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会今天举行第二十八次会议（扩大）。
    在会上，常务委员史良报告了今年6月下旬至7月中旬她应邀参加捷克斯洛伐克妇女委员会全国代表大会时访问捷克斯洛伐克的情况，常务委员钱端升报告了他应美国伊顿的邀请在今年8月间前往加拿大参加国际问题讨论会的情况。全国委员会委员刘王立明也在会上报告她到西德参加世界基督教妇女节制会第二十届代表大会的情况。
    会议在听了常务委员会副秘书长梅龚彬的说明后，讨论并通过了关于中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第二次全体会议交常务委员会负责同有关部门协商处理和审核办理的提案的处理意见。
