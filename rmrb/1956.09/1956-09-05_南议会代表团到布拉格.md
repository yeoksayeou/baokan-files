Title: South Parliament Delegation Arrives in Prague
Author:
Date: 1956-09-05
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 4th: A delegation from the South (Yugoslavian) Parliament arrived in Prague by plane on the evening of September 3rd for a visit to Czechoslovakia at the invitation of the Czech government. The head of the delegation is Marinko, member of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia and Chairman of the Slovenian National Assembly.

### 南议会代表团到布拉格

1956-09-05
第5版()
专栏：

    南议会代表团到布拉格
    据新华社布拉格4日电　应邀到捷克斯洛伐克访问的南斯拉夫议会代表团9月3日晚间乘飞机到达布拉格。代表团团长是南斯拉夫联邦国民议会外交委员会委员、斯洛文尼亚国民议会主席马林科。
