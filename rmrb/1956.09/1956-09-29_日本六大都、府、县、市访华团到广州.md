Title: Japanese Delegation from Six Major Metropolises, Prefectures, Counties, and Cities Visits Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 28th. The Japanese delegation from six major metropolises, prefectures, counties, and cities, consisting of twenty-three members, arrived in Guangzhou today by train from Shenzhen. They are visiting China at the invitation of Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen to attend the National Day celebrations and conduct visits. The head of the delegation is Shoichiro Ando, a current member of the Tokyo Metropolitan Assembly. The members include assembly members from Tokyo, Nagoya, Yokohama, Kyoto, Osaka, Kobe, and other prefectures, counties, and cities.

### 日本六大都、府、县、市访华团到广州

1956-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    日本六大都、府、县、市访华团到广州
    新华社广州28日电　日本六大都、府、县、市访华代表团一行二十三人，今天从深圳乘火车到达广州。他们是应北京市市长彭真邀请前来我国参加国庆观礼和进行参观访问的。代表团团长是现任东京都议会议员安藤章一郎，团员包括有东京、名古屋、横滨、京都、大阪、神户以及其他府、县、市的议员。
