Title: Siroky Receives Ceylon Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-09-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 14th: Czechoslovak Premier Viliam Široký received the Ceylon Government Delegation visiting Czechoslovakia on September 13th. The delegation held a reception on the same day, inviting Czechoslovak government leaders, representatives from foreign trade organizations, and representatives from some machine manufacturing plants in Prague.

### 西罗基接见锡政府代表团

1956-09-15
第6版()
专栏：

    西罗基接见锡政府代表团
    新华社布拉格14日电　捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基9月13日接见了正在捷克斯洛伐克访问的锡兰政府代表团。代表团在同一天举行了招待会，招待捷克斯洛伐克政府领导人、捷对外贸易机关和布拉格一些机器制造厂的代表。
