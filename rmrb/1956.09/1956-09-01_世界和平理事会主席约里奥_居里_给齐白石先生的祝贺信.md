Title: World Peace Council Chairman Joliot-Curie's Letter of Congratulations to Mr. Qi Baishi
Author:
Date: 1956-09-01
Page: 7

World Peace Council Chairman Joliot-Curie
Letter of Congratulations to Mr. Qi Baishi

Master Qi Baishi, dear friend:
I recently learned that you will soon be awarded the International Peace Prize.

Over the years, I have received some of your works. One of the paintings hangs in my studio. I often look at it, and through it, I am accustomed to feeling the image of your people and the cause of cultural figures vividly.

Therefore, I am particularly pleased that the International Peace Prize Jury has decided to award you this lofty honor this year. I would like to express my congratulations to you and my admiration for your majestic, vigorous, youthful works and your enthusiastic activities in striving for peace.

Please accept my deep friendship.
　Most sincerely, Frédéric Joliot-Curie
　1956 Paris

### 世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里  给齐白石先生的祝贺信

1956-09-01
第7版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里
    给齐白石先生的祝贺信
    齐白石先生大师，亲爱的朋友：
    最近获悉您将于近日接受国际和平奖金。
    几年来，曾收到您的一些作品。其中一幅画挂在我的工作室里，我经常看到它，并习惯于通过它感到贵国人民的形象及文化人士的事业历历在目。
    因此，国际和平奖金评议委员会今年决定给予您这个崇高的荣誉，我感到特别高兴。我谨向您表示祝贺，并对您的雄伟劲健而富有青春气息的作品以及对您热烈争取和平的活动，表示钦佩。
    请接受我深切的友谊。　最诚挚的　腓特烈·约里奥—居里
    1956年　巴黎
