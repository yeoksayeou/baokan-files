Title: Soviet, Czech and Other Countries Participate in Thessaloniki International Fair
Author:
Date: 1956-09-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Athens, September 3rd: The Thessaloniki International Fair in Greece officially opened on September 1st, presided over by Greek Prime Minister Karamanlis. The Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Democratic Germany, Hungary, Romania, and Poland, along with other countries, participated in the fair.

At the opening ceremony, Greek Minister of Commerce and Trade, Babaligouras, specifically mentioned that Greece's connections with the Soviet Union and the People's Democratic countries have been expanded. The chairman of the exhibition committee, Dimitrakopoulos, and others also emphasized the important significance of this fair for developing economic relations between countries.

### 苏捷等国参加萨罗尼加国际博览会

1956-09-04
第5版()
专栏：

    苏捷等国参加萨罗尼加国际博览会
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社雅典讯：在希腊萨罗尼加举行的国际博览会9月1日在希腊首相卡拉曼利斯主持下正式开幕。苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、民主德国、匈牙利、罗马尼亚和波兰同其他国家一起参加了这次博览会。
    在开幕式上，希腊商业与贸易大臣巴巴利古拉斯特别提到了希腊同苏联及各人民民主国家的联系已经扩大。展览委员会主席迪米特腊科普洛斯等人也着重指出了这次博览会对发展各国经济关系的重要意义。
