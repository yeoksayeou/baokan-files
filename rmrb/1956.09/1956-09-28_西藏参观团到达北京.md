Title: Tibetan Visiting Group Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: The Tibetan visiting group arrived in Beijing by special train this morning. The visiting group consists of more than 240 members.

At the train station, Honorary Head of the Visiting Group, Pabala Living Buddha, Head of the Group, Sampo Tsesong Ritang, and Deputy Head of the Group, Sharzur Living Buddha, presented hadas and warmly shook hands with Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Zhang Jingwu, Director of the Office of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China; Liu Geping, Chairman of the National People's Congress Nationalities Committee; Wang Feng, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission; and Xue Zizheng, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, who came to welcome them.

Also present at the station to welcome the group were Xie Fumin, Sangji Yexi, Liu Chun, Yang Jingren, Zhang Guohua, Tan Guansan, He Chengxiang, and Zhao Puchu.

Dunwang Jianzan Zhaba, Director of the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing, and Lian Huizhu, Acting Director of the Panchen's Office in Beijing, also came to the station to welcome the group. (Attached pictures)

### 西藏参观团到达北京

1956-09-28
第2版()
专栏：

    西藏参观团到达北京
    据新华社27日讯　西藏参观团在今天上午乘专车到达北京。参观团共有团员二百四十多人。
    在车站上，参观团名誉团长帕巴拉活佛、团长桑颇·扎萨·才旺仁增和副团长夏孜活佛等向前来欢迎的国务院秘书长习仲勋，中华人民共和国主席办公厅主任张经武，全国人民代表大会民族委员会主任委员刘格平，民族事务委员会副主任汪锋和北京市副市长薛子正献哈达，并且一一亲切地握手。
    今天到车站欢迎的，还有谢扶民、桑吉悦希、刘春、杨静仁、张国华、谭冠三、何成湘、赵朴初等。
    达赖喇嘛驻京办事处处长顿旺·坚赞札巴、班禅驻京办事处代处长联慧珠也到车站欢迎。（附图片）
