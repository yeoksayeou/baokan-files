Title: Soviet Union Renames Award for Outstanding Peace Fighters as the "International Lenin Prize for Strengthening Peace Among Peoples"
Author:
Date: 1956-09-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th. The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union issued a decree on September 6th, announcing that the Stalin International Prize would be renamed the "International Lenin Prize for Strengthening Peace Among Peoples."

The decree, signed by Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet, and Gorkin, Secretary, stated that the decision to rename the Stalin International Prize as the "International Lenin Prize for Strengthening Peace Among Peoples" was made in accordance with the wishes of the Stalin International Prize Committee.

The decree stipulates that recipients of the Lenin International Prize will receive: (1) A certificate of award for the Lenin International Prize; (2) A gold badge engraved with Lenin's image; and (3) A prize of 100,000 rubles.

Those who received the International Stalin Prize for "Strengthening Peace Among Peoples" between 1950 and 1955 will receive the first and second items mentioned above.

The "International Lenin Prize for Strengthening Peace Among Peoples" will be awarded annually by the Lenin International Prize Committee, with a quota of ten recipients.

The methods for nominating candidates for the "International Lenin Prize for Strengthening Peace Among Peoples" and the conditions for awarding the prize will be formulated and announced by the Lenin International Prize Committee.

### 苏联把给予世界杰出的和平战士的奖金  改称为“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金

1956-09-09
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联把给予世界杰出的和平战士的奖金
    改称为“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金
    新华社莫斯科8日电　苏联最高苏维埃主席团9月6日颁布了一项命令，宣布把斯大林国际奖金改称为“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金。
    由苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫和秘书戈尔金签署的这项命令说，把斯大林国际奖金改称为“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金的决定，是根据斯大林国际奖金委员会的愿望作出的。
    命令规定，获得列宁国际奖金的人将要得到：（一）列宁国际奖金得奖人奖状；（二）刻有列宁像的金质胸章；（三）十万卢布的奖金。
    在1950到1955年时期内“加强国际和平”国际奖金的得奖人，将要重新得到上述得奖项目中的第一、二两项。
    “加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金由列宁国际奖金委员会每年颁发一次，名额为十人。
    推选“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金得奖人人选的办法和授予这种奖金的条件，由列宁国际奖金委员会拟定公布。
