Title: Telegram of Greetings from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Netherlands
Author:
Date: 1956-09-23
Page: 4

Dear Comrades,
    On behalf of the Communist Party of the Netherlands, we extend fraternal greetings to your Congress.
    We are very sorry that we are unable to send a delegation to your Congress because our party will hold its 18th Congress in early October. We hope to strengthen the ties between our two parties in the future.
    The Communists of the Netherlands are closely watching the experience of the Communist Party of China in winning national independence, building socialism and striving for peace. The new methods adopted by your party in building socialism have attracted the attention of the international labor movement.
    The rise of New China is a milestone in the current era of the collapse of the colonial system. The changes in the world situation caused by this development are particularly evident in the Bandung Conference and the struggle of the new independent countries for peaceful coexistence.
    Comrades, we wish your Congress a fruitful outcome.
    We are convinced that the resolutions you make will advance the cause of peace, democracy and socialism. The unity of the communist parties will greatly strengthen our strength in this common struggle.
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Netherlands
    Amsterdam, August 27, 1956

### 荷兰共产党中央委员会的贺电

1956-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    荷兰共产党中央委员会的贺电亲爱的同志们：
    我们代表荷兰共产党向你们的代表大会致兄弟的祝贺。
    我们感到非常抱歉，由于我们党将在十月初举行第十八次代表大会，我们不能够派代表团参加你们的代表大会。我们希望能在日后加强我们两党的联系。
    荷兰共产党人密切注意中国共产党在赢得民族独立、建设社会主义和争取和平的斗争中的经验。你们党在建设社会主义中所采取的新方法引起国际劳工运动的重视。
    新中国的兴起是目前殖民制度崩溃时代的里程碑。这一发展所引起的世界局势的变化，在万隆会议和新的独立国家争取和平共处的斗争中表现得特别清楚。
    同志们，我们祝你们的大会取得丰富的成果。
    我们深信，你们作出的决议将推进和平、民主和社会主义的事业。共产主义政党的团结一致将大大加强我们在这一共同斗争中的力量。
    荷兰共产党中央委员会
    一九五六年八月二十七日于阿姆斯特丹
