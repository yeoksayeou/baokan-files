Title: DPRK Supreme People's Assembly Standing Committee Appoints Jung Il-ryong as Vice Premier
Author:
Date: 1956-09-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 6th - According to the Korean Central News Agency on the 5th: The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued an order on September 1st, removing Choi Chang-ik from his position as Vice Premier, Park Chang-ok from his position as Vice Premier and Minister of the Machine Industry, and Yun Kong-heum from his position as Minister of Commerce.

Jung Il-ryong has been appointed as Vice Premier. The Ministry of Trade and the Ministry of Commerce have been merged, and Chen Ban-soo has been appointed as Minister of Commerce.

### 朝鲜最高人民会议常委会任命郑一龙为副首相

1956-09-07
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜最高人民会议常委会任命郑一龙为副首相
    新华社平壤6日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社5日讯：朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会9月1日发布命令，免去崔昌益副首相、朴昌玉副首相兼机械工业相以及尹公钦商业相的职务。
    郑一龙已经被任命为副首相。贸易省同商业省合并，陈班秀被任命为商业相。
