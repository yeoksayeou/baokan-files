Title: Ho Chi Minh Hosts National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1956-09-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 2nd: President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held a grand National Day reception at the Presidential Palace tonight, hosting diplomatic envoys, delegations, and experts stationed in Hanoi. High-ranking leaders of the Vietnamese government and military, as well as heads of the National Assembly, various parties, people's organizations, and institutions, also attended the reception.

### 胡志明举行国庆招待会

1956-09-03
第5版()
专栏：

    胡志明举行国庆招待会
    新华社河内2日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明今天晚上在主席府举行了盛大的国庆招待会，招待各国驻河内的使节、代表团和专家等；出席招待会的还有越南政府和军队的高级首长，以及国民大会、各党派、各人民团体和机关的负责人。
