Title: Seshu's Death Anniversary Exhibition Closes

Author: 
Date: 1956-09-10
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 9th: The exhibition commemorating the 450th anniversary of the death of Seshu, a world cultural figure, which opened in Beijing on August 23rd, closed today. Over ten thousand people visited the exhibition during its run.
    The audience highly praised the works of Seshu, this outstanding Japanese painter.

### 雪舟逝世纪念展览会闭幕

1956-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    雪舟逝世纪念展览会闭幕
    据新华社9日讯　8月23日起在北京举行的世界文化名人雪舟逝世四百五十周年纪念展览会今日闭幕。展览会举行期间，共有一万多人前去参观。
    观众们对这位日本杰出的画家——雪舟的作品推崇备至。
