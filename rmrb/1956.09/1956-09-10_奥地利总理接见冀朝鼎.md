Title: Austrian Chancellor Receives Ji Chao Ding
Author:
Date: 1956-09-10
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 9th: Vienna dispatch: Austrian Chancellor Raab received Ji Chao Ding, head of the Chinese delegation to the Vienna International Exposition and Vice-Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, in his office on the morning of September 8th, and held cordial talks.

### 奥地利总理接见冀朝鼎

1956-09-10
第5版()
专栏：

    奥地利总理接见冀朝鼎
    新华社布拉格9日电　维也纳消息：奥地利总理拉布9月8日上午在总理办公室接见了中国参加维也纳国际博览会代表团团长、中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎，并且进行了融洽的交谈。
