Title: Hammarskjöld Draws Security Council's Attention to Border Incidents Between Jordan and Israel
Author:
Date: 1956-09-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 27th: According to TASS, United Nations Secretary-General Dag Hammarskjöld handed a letter to the President of the Security Council, Núñez-Portuondo, on September 26th, drawing the Security Council's attention to "recent incidents on the armistice demarcation line between Jordan and Israel."

Hammarskjöld said that, as entrusted by the Security Council, he would soon submit not only a report on the situation in Jordan and Israel, but also a report on the border situation between Israel and Egypt.

Before sending the letter, Hammarskjöld had met with representatives of Jordan and Israel, and had consulted with the representatives of the United States, Britain, and France to the United Nations.

### 哈马舍尔德提请安理会  注意约以两国边界事件

1956-09-28
第7版()
专栏：

    哈马舍尔德提请安理会
    注意约以两国边界事件
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社纽约27日讯：联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德9月26日交给安全理事会主席努奈斯—波图翁多一封信，提请安全理事会注意“在约旦和以色列的停战分界线上最近发生的事件”。
    哈马舍尔德说，根据安全理事会的委托，他不仅很快就要提出关于约旦和以色列情势的报告，而且还要提出关于以色列和埃及的边界情况的报告。
    哈马舍尔德在发出这封信以前，曾经会见约旦和以色列的代表，并且同美、英、法三国驻联合国的代表磋商过。
