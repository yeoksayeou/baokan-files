Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Holds Talks with Prime Minister Acharya
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 27th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China held talks with Prime Minister Tanka Prasad Acharya of the Kingdom of Nepal at 11:30 this morning.

    The Chinese side participating in the talks included Vice Premier of the State Council Ulanhu and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu.

    The Nepalese side participating in the talks included Narapratap Thapa, Foreign Affairs Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Nepal.

### 周总理同阿查里雅首相会谈

1956-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理同阿查里雅首相会谈
    新华社27日讯　中华人民共和国国务院周恩来总理同尼泊尔王国坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅首相在今天上午十一时三十分举行了会谈。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有国务院副总理乌兰夫和外交部副部长章汉夫。
    尼泊尔王国方面参加会谈的，有尼泊尔王国外交部外事秘书纳腊普腊塔普·塔帕。
