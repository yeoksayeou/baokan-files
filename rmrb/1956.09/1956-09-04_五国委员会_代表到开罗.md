Title: "Five-Nation Committee" Representatives Arrive in Cairo
Author:
Date: 1956-09-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 3rd: Representatives of the so-called "Five-Nation Suez Canal Committee," composed of Australia, Ethiopia, Iran, Sweden, and the United States, arrived in Cairo by plane on September 2nd. The "Five-Nation Committee's" talks with Egyptian President Nasser are scheduled to begin on September 3rd.

The task of the "Five-Nation Committee," orchestrated by the West, is to contact Egypt regarding the "international control" plan for the Suez Canal proposed by Dulles.

According to Western news agencies, before boarding his plane to Cairo, the chairman of the "Five-Nation Committee," Menzies, told reporters that the representatives of the "Five-Nation Committee" were not "merely messengers conveying a document, because there are many things in this document that must be explained, and we hope to discuss this document." Menzies then added: "However, we have no authority to abandon any point of the principle of this main proposal."

The representatives of the "Five-Nation Committee" are: Australian Prime Minister Robert Menzies, Ethiopian Foreign Minister Aklilu Habte-Wold, Iranian Foreign Minister Ali Amini, Swedish Foreign Minister Östen Undén, and U.S. Deputy Assistant Secretary of State Loy Henderson.

### “五国委员会”代表到开罗

1956-09-04
第6版()
专栏：

    “五国委员会”代表到开罗
    据新华社3日讯　开罗消息：由澳大利亚、埃塞俄比亚、伊朗、瑞典和美国组成的所谓“五国苏彝士运河委员会”的代表已经在9月2日先后乘飞机到达开罗。“五国委员会”同埃及纳赛尔总统的会谈定在9月3日开始。
    西方策划的“五国委员会”的任务，是负责就杜勒斯所提出的“国际管制”苏彝士运河计划同埃及接触。
    据西方通讯社报道，“五国委员会”的主席孟席斯在登机离开伦敦去开罗之前对记者发表谈话说，“五国委员会”的代表并不“仅仅是传达一纸公文的传信人，因为这项文件里有许多东西必须加以解释，并且我们希望就这项文件进行讨论。”孟席斯紧接着说：“但是，我们没有权力放弃这个主要建议的原则的任何一点。”
    “五国委员会”的代表是：澳大利亚总理罗伯特·孟席斯，埃塞俄比亚外交大臣阿克利路·哈普特沃耳德，伊朗外交大臣阿里·阿达兰，瑞典外交大臣厄斯顿·恩顿，美国副国务卿帮办洛埃·汉德逊。
