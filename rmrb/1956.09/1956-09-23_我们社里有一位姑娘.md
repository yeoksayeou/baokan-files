Title: There is a Girl in Our Cooperative
Author: Ji Xuepei
Date: 1956-09-23
Page: 8

    There is a girl in our cooperative,
    Young and pretty,
    If she just glances at someone!
    That person's heart flusters.
    The young men are not convinced,
    Putting on airs,
    They deliberately hum and say:
    "Never really put her in my heart."

    In the season of harvesting wheat in May,
    The girl comes to the fields,
    The young men are flustered and fumble,
    The sickle almost cuts their own foot.
    The south wind blows gently,
    The sickle rustles;
    Sneak a peek when there's a chance,
    Could she be looking at me?

    The girl smiles and smiles!
    Thinking secretly:
    "You must not exhaust yourselves!
    I have my own ideas."

### 我们社里有一位姑娘
吉学霈
1956-09-23
第8版()
专栏：

    我们社里有一位姑娘
  　吉学霈
    我们社里有一位姑娘，
    年轻又漂亮，
    她要向谁看一眼呀！
    谁的心里就发慌。
    小伙子们可不服气，
    装模又作样，
    他们故意哼着鼻子说：
    “从来就没把她放在心上。”
    在五月收麦的季节里，
    姑娘来到地头上，
    小伙子们慌手又慌脚，
    镰刀差一点砍在自己的脚上。
    南风轻轻的吹，
    镰刀沙沙的响；
    瞅个空儿瞟一眼呀，
    她可在向我望？
    姑娘笑呀笑呀！
    心里暗暗想：
    “你们千万别累坏了哟！
    我自有主张。”
