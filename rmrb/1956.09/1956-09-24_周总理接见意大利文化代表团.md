Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Italian Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-09-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd. Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai today received all members of the Italian Cultural Delegation headed by former Italian Prime Minister Ferruccio Parri.
    Present at the reception were Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Ma Yinchu, President of Peking University, and Huang Hua, Director of the Department of Western European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 周总理接见意大利文化代表团

1956-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见意大利文化代表团
    新华社23日讯　国务院总理周恩来今天接见了以意大利前总理费·帕里为首的意大利文化代表团全体人员。
    接见的时候，在座的有中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙，高等教育部部长杨秀峰，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，北京大学校长马寅初和外交部西欧司司长黄华。
