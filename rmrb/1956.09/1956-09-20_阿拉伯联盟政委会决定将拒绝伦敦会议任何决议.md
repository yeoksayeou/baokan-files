Title: Arab League Political Committee Decides to Reject Any Resolutions of the London Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-09-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 19th: Cairo News: The Political Committee of the Arab League, at its meeting on September 18th, passed a resolution to preemptively reject any resolutions made at the meeting opening in London on September 19th to discuss the "Suez Canal Users Association" plan.
    The meeting adopted a total of five resolutions. The other four resolutions are: condemning the economic and military pressure that Britain and France have already exerted in order to achieve their goals; agreeing to Egypt's decision to convene an international conference aimed at resolving the Suez Canal issue by peaceful means; calling on all countries to abide by the principles embodied in the United Nations Charter; and agreeing to Egypt's note to the United Nations Security Council urging attention to the actions of Britain and France regarding the Suez Canal issue.
    In a communique, the committee said that it would continue to meet.

### 阿拉伯联盟政委会决定将拒绝伦敦会议任何决议

1956-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯联盟政委会决定将拒绝伦敦会议任何决议
    据新华社19日讯　开罗消息：阿拉伯联盟政治委员会在9月18日的会议上通过决议，预先拒绝9月19日在伦敦开幕的讨论“苏彝士运河使用国协会”计划的会议所作出的任何决议。
    会议共通过了五项决议，其他四项决议是：谴责英法为了达到它们的目的而已经行使的经济的和军事的压力；同意埃及关于召开目的在于以和平方法解决苏彝士运河问题的国际会议的决定；号召各国遵守联合国宪章所体现的各项原则；同意埃及向联合国安全理事会提出的促请注意英法关于苏彝士运河问题的行动的照会。
    委员会在一项公报中说，委员会将继续开会。
