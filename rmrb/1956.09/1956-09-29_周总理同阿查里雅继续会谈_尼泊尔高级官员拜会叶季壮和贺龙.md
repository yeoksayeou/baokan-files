Title: Premier Zhou continues talks with Acharya; High-ranking Nepalese officials visit Ye Jizhuang and He Long
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th – Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China continued talks this morning with Prime Minister Tanka Prasad Acharya of the Kingdom of Nepal.

    On the Chinese side participating in the talks were Vice Premier Ulanhu and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu.

    On the side of the Kingdom of Nepal participating in the talks was Narapratap Thapa, Foreign Affairs Secretary of the Nepalese Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th – Sedar Bhim Bahadur Pande, Secretary of the Ministry of Planning and Development and the Ministry of Commerce and Industry of the Kingdom of Nepal, and Kumar Das Shresta, Deputy Secretary of the Ministry of Planning and Development, paid a visit to Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, this morning.

    In the afternoon of the same day, Lieutenant General Nir Shumshere Rana, Chief of Staff of the Nepalese Army, and Lieutenant General Arjun Shumshere, Honorary Aide-de-Camp to the King, paid a visit to Marshal He Long, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council.

### 周总理同阿查里雅继续会谈  尼泊尔高级官员拜会叶季壮和贺龙

1956-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理同阿查里雅继续会谈
    尼泊尔高级官员拜会叶季壮和贺龙
    新华社28日讯　中华人民共和国国务院周恩来总理同尼泊尔王国坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅首相今天上午继续举行了会谈。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有国务院副总理乌兰夫和外交部副部长章汉夫。
    尼泊尔王国方面参加会谈的，有尼泊尔外交部外事秘书纳腊普腊塔普·塔帕。
    新华社28日讯　尼泊尔王国计划发展部及工商部秘书塞达尔·比姆·巴哈杜尔·潘德和计划发展部副秘书库马尔·达斯·希雷斯塔，在今天上午拜会了中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮。
    同一天下午，尼泊尔陆军参谋长尼尔·舒姆谢尔·拉纳中将和国王名誉副官阿尔琼·舒姆谢尔中将拜会了国防委员会副主席贺龙元帅。
