Title: The Congress Approves the List of the Presidium and Other Personnel, and the Meeting Agenda; Liu Shaoqi Delivers a Political Report on Behalf of the Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1956-09-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 15th -- The Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China opened today at the auditorium of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beijing. 1,021 delegates attended the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China, representing 10,730,000 party members. At 2:00 PM, Chinese Communist Party leaders Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Kang Sheng, Peng Zhen, Dong Biwu, Lin Boqu, Zhang Wentian, Peng Dehuai, Lin Biao, and Deng Xiaoping, representatives of communist and workers' parties from various countries, leaders of various democratic parties in China, and non-party democratic figures ascended the podium. At this moment, all the delegates welcomed them with a storm of applause.

At 2:05 PM, Comrade Mao Zedong delivered the opening speech (the full text of the opening speech will be issued separately). He announced the official opening of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China. At this moment, the entire audience stood up and applauded warmly, and the band played the solemn Internationale.

Comrade Mao Zedong then said that in the past eleven years, many comrades and friends in China and around the world have given their lives for the cause of communism and human liberation. We should always remember them. At this moment, the entire audience stood up and observed a moment of silence for the martyrs. In his opening speech, Mao Zedong pointed out: The task of this National Congress is to: summarize the experience since the Seventh Congress, unite the entire party, unite all forces that can be united at home and abroad, and strive to build a great socialist China.

Mao Zedong's opening speech was interrupted by enthusiastic applause from time to time.

Before concluding his opening speech, Mao Zedong extended a warm welcome to the representatives of the Communist Party, Workers' Party, Labor Party, and People's Revolutionary Party of over fifty countries present, and to the representatives of various democratic parties and non-party democratic figures in China present, for their participation in the congress. At this moment, the entire audience repeatedly stood up, and sustained applause resounded.

After Mao Zedong delivered his speech, the entire audience stood up and applauded enthusiastically for a long time.

Subsequently, the congress approved the three lists submitted by the Central Committee: the Congress Presidium, the Congress Secretariat, and the Delegate Credentials Examination Committee, and also approved the Congress's agenda and rules of procedure.

        List of the Congress Presidium
    The Congress Presidium is composed of 63 people, and the list is as follows: Xi Zhongxun, Mao Zedong, Wang Jiaxiang, Deng Xiaoping, Deng Zihui, Deng Yingchao, Ye Jianying, Shuai Mengqi, Liu Shaoqi, Liu Bocheng, Liu Geping, Liu Lantao, Zhu De, Li Jingquan, Li Xiannian, Li Xuefeng, Li Fuchun, Song Renqiong, Wu Yuzhang, Chen Yun, Chen Shaomin, Chen Boda, Chen Yu, Chen Yi, Lu Dingyi, Zhou Enlai, Zhou Yang, Luo Ronghuan, Luo Ruiqing, Lin Boqu, Lin Biao, Lin Feng, Lin Tie, Ouyang Qin, Zheng Weisan, Hu Yaobang, Ke Qingshi, Xu Xiangqian, Xu Teli, Tao Zhu, Ma Mingfang, Ulanhu, Zhang Yunyi, Zhang Wentian, Zhang Dingcheng, Zhang Desheng, Kang Sheng, Huang Kecheng, Huang Jing, Peng Zhen, Peng Dehuai, He Long, Yang Xiufeng, Jia Tuofu, Dong Biwu, Liao Chengzhi, Qian Ying, Cai Chang, Lai Ruoyu, Bo Yibo, Nie Rongzhen, Tan Zheng, Tan Zhenlin.
        List of the Congress Secretariat
    The Congress Secretariat is composed of 13 people, and the list is as follows: Wang Jiaxiang, Deng Xiaoping, Liu Ningyi, Liu Lantao, Li Xuefeng, Song Renqiong, Lin Feng, Hu Qiaomu, Ma Mingfang, Zhang Jichun, Yang Shangkun, Tan Zheng, Tan Zhenlin. Secretary-General: Deng Xiaoping.
    List of the Congress Delegate Credentials Examination Committee
    The Congress Delegate Credentials Examination Committee is composed of 29 people, and the list is as follows: Dong Biwu (Director), Tan Zhenlin (Deputy Director), Liu Lantao (Deputy Director), Wang Enmao, Deng Yingchao, An Ziwen, Li Jingquan, Li Xuefeng, Song Renqiong, Wu Zhipu, Lin Feng, Lin Tie, Ouyang Qin, Ke Qingshi, Xu Xiangqian, Tao Zhu, Ma Mingfang, Ulanhu, Zhang Desheng, Zhang Dingcheng, Huang Kecheng, Huang Oudong, He Long, Shu Tong, Yang Shangkun, Cai Chang, Xie Fuzhi, Tan Zheng, Gong Zirong.
        Main Agenda of the Congress
    The main agenda of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China approved by the Congress is as follows:
    (1) The Political Report of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to the Eighth National Congress - Reporter: Liu Shaoqi.
    (2) Report on the Revision of the Constitution of the Communist Party of China - Reporter: Deng Xiaoping.
    (3) Report on the Proposal for the Second Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy - Reporter: Zhou Enlai.
    (4) Election of the Central Committee of the Party.

    Following this, the Congress entered the first item on the agenda, with Comrade Liu Shaoqi delivering a political report to the Congress on behalf of the Central Committee.

    Representatives, delegates, and observers from the Communist and Workers' Parties of more than fifty countries were invited to attend this Congress.

    Leaders of various democratic parties and non-party democratic figures, and working comrades from departments directly under the CPC Central Committee, central state organs, the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and people's organizations, totaling more than 400 people, were also invited to attend the Congress.

### 大会通过主席团等名单和会议日程  刘少奇代表中央作政治报告

1956-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

    大会通过主席团等名单和会议日程
    刘少奇代表中央作政治报告
    新华社15日讯　中国共产党第八次全国代表大会今天在北京中国人民政治协商会议礼堂开幕。出席第八次全国代表大会的代表有一千零二十一人，他们代表着一千零七十三万党员。下午二时，中国共产党领导人毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、陈云、康生、彭真、董必武、林伯渠、张闻天、彭德怀、林彪、邓小平，各国共产党和工人党的代表们以及中国各民主党派的领导人和无党派民主人士登上主席台。这时，全体代表用暴风雨般的掌声来欢迎他们。
    二时零五分，毛泽东同志致开幕词（开幕词全文另发）。他宣布中国共产党第八次全国代表大会正式开幕，这时全场起立，热烈鼓掌，乐队奏起了庄严的国际歌。
  毛泽东同志接着说，在过去的十一年间，在全中国和全世界有很多同志和朋友为了共产主义和人类解放事业曾付出了自己的生命。我们应当永远纪念他们。这时，全场起立，为先烈们默哀。毛泽东在开幕词中指出：这次全国代表大会的任务是：总结从七次大会以来的经验，团结全党，团结国内外一切可能团结的力量，为了建设一个伟大的社会主义的中国而奋斗。
    毛泽东的开幕词不时为热烈的掌声所打断。
    毛泽东在结束他的开幕词之前，向在座的五十几个国家的共产党、工人党、劳动党和人民革命党的代表，和在座的国内各民主党派和无党派民主人士的代表，对他们的参加大会表示热烈的欢迎。这时，全场一再起立，响起了经久不息的掌声。
    毛泽东致词结束后，全场起立，长时间的热烈鼓掌。
    接着，大会通过了中央委员会提出的大会主席团、大会秘书处、代表资格审查委员会三个名单，并且通过了大会的会议日程和会议规则。
