Title: All-China Youth Federation Hosts Banquet for Delegation from Japan Youth Organization Council
Author:
Date: 1956-09-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 25th. The All-China Youth Federation hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the delegation from the Japan Youth Organization Council.

    Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, delivered a speech at the banquet, extending a warm welcome on behalf of the All-China Youth Federation and the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League to the Japanese youth friends who had come from afar. He said that China and Japan have had thousands of years of friendly relations with each other. The people of both countries do not want war and demand peace. The people of both countries are willing to live in peace and friendship. Therefore, the friendship between the youth of China and Japan has a broad path for development. He hoped that the visit of the delegation would make the contacts between the youth of China and Japan more frequent and closer in the future.

    Head of the delegation, Yoshiaki Sagae, then delivered a speech, expressing his gratitude for the warm reception from the hosts. He said that the Japanese people and youth, who suffered from the atomic bomb disaster in Hiroshima ten years ago, have a stronger desire for peace. He said that the purpose of the delegation's visit to China this time is to correctly understand the situation of China's socialist construction, strengthen the sincere friendship between the youth of China and Japan, and thus contribute to world peace. He hoped to meet Chinese youth in Tokyo in the near future.

    Among those present at the banquet tonight were Hu Yaobang, Luo Yi, and Hu Keshi, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Youth League, Sun Shouzhu, Vice Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, and Du Qian, Secretary-General of the All-China Youth Federation.

### 全国青联宴日本青年团协议会代表团

1956-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    全国青联宴日本青年团协议会代表团
    新华社25日讯　中华全国民主青年联合会今天晚间举行宴会，欢迎日本青年团协议会代表团。
    全国青联主席廖承志在会上首先致词，他代表全国青联和中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会向远道而来的日本青年朋友们表示热情的欢迎。他说，中国和日本有着好几千年的相互友好关系，两国人民都是不要战争要求和平，两国人民都愿意能和平友好相处，因此中日两国青年的友谊就有着宽阔发展的道路。他希望通过代表团的访问使中日两国青年今后的接触更加频繁和密切起来。
    代表团团长寒河江善秋接着致词，他向主人们的热情招待表示感谢。他说，十年前遭受过广岛原子弹灾害的日本人民和青年要求和平的愿望更强烈，他说代表团这次来中国访问的目的就是要正确了解中国社会主义建设的情况，加强中日两国青年的真诚友好，从而对世界和平有所贡献。他希望不久将来能在东京和中国青年见面。
    出席今晚宴会的有青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦、罗毅、胡克实，全国学联副主席孙寿珠，全国青联秘书长杜前等。
