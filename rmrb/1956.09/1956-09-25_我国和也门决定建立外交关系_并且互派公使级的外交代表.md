Title: China and Yemen Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations and Exchange Ministers
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: The Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Kingdom of Yemen have issued a joint communiqué on the establishment of diplomatic relations and the exchange of diplomatic envoys at the ministerial level: "The Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Kingdom of Yemen, considering the mutual wishes of the two countries, have decided to establish diplomatic relations and exchange diplomatic representatives at the ministerial level."

### 我国和也门决定建立外交关系  并且互派公使级的外交代表

1956-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我国和也门决定建立外交关系
    并且互派公使级的外交代表
    新华社24日讯　中华人民共和国政府和也门王国政府就两国建立外交关系和互派外交使节事发表联合公报如下：“中华人民共和国政府和也门王国政府考虑到两国的相互愿望，已经决定建立外交关系，并且互派公使级的外交代表。”
