Title: Commemorating the 20th Anniversary of Lu Xun's Death: National Federation of Literary and Art Circles Holds Report Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-09-16
Page: 7

    In order to help young literary and art workers, as well as workers, students, and cadres who love literature and art, understand Lu Xun's literary and artistic thoughts, his creations, and his life, the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles has decided to hold a Lu Xun 20th Anniversary Memorial Report Meeting every Sunday morning at the Zhongshan Park Concert Hall in Beijing, starting from September 16th. The report meeting will be divided into four lectures: the first lecture will be given by Huang Yaomian on "The Development of Lu Xun's Literary and Artistic Thoughts," the second lecture will be given by Ba Ren on "Lu Xun's Novels," the third lecture will be given by Chen Yong on "Lu Xun's Essays and Prose," and the fourth lecture will be given by Xu Guangping on "Lu Xun's Creative Life." (Wang)

### 念鲁迅逝世二十周年  全国文联举行报告会

1956-09-16
第7版()
专栏：

    念鲁迅逝世二十周年  全国文联举行报告会
    本报讯　中国文学艺术界联合会为了帮助青年文艺工作者以及爱好文艺的工人、学生、干部了解鲁迅的文艺思想、他的创作和生活，决定从9月16日起，每星期日上午在北京中山公园音乐堂举办鲁迅逝世二十周年纪念报告会。报告会内容共分四讲：第一讲由黄药眠主讲“鲁迅文艺思想的发展”，第二讲由巴人主讲“鲁迅的小说”，第三讲由陈涌主讲“鲁迅的杂文及散文”，第四讲由许广平主讲“鲁迅的创作生活”。（汪）
