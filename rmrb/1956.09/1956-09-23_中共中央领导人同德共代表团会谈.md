Title: CCP Central Leaders Hold Talks with German Communist Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-09-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd—Central leaders of the Chinese Communist Party and the German Communist Party delegation held talks on the 22nd. Representing the Chinese Communist Party were Mao Zedong, Zhou Enlai, and Zhu De. Representing the German Communist Party delegation were Reimann and Herzner.

### 中共中央领导人同德共代表团会谈

1956-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人同德共代表团会谈
    新华社22日讯　中国共产党中央领导人和德国共产党代表团在22日举行了会谈，中国共产党方面出席的有毛泽东、周恩来、朱德，德国共产党代表团方面出席的有雷曼和赫茨纳。
