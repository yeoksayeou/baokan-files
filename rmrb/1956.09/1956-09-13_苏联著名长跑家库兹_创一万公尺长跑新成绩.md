Title: Soviet Famous Long-Distance Runner Kuz Sets New Record in 10,000-Meter Run
Author:
Date: 1956-09-13
Page: 4
Sports News

    Soviet Famous Long-Distance Runner Kuz
    Sets New Record in 10,000-Meter Run
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 12th. Soviet famous long-distance runner Kuz created a new world record in the 10,000-meter race with a time of 28 minutes and 30.4 seconds at a track and field meet held in Moscow on September 11th.
    The official world record for the 10,000-meter race (28 minutes 54.2 seconds) was set by the world-renowned Czechoslovakian athlete Zatopek in June 1954. In mid-July of this year, Hungarian athlete Iharos ran 28 minutes and 42.8 seconds, beating Zatopek's record by 11.4 seconds. Now Kuz's time has shortened Iharos' time by another 12.4 seconds.

### 苏联著名长跑家库兹  创一万公尺长跑新成绩

1956-09-13
第4版()
专栏：体育消息

    苏联著名长跑家库兹
    创一万公尺长跑新成绩
    新华社莫斯科12日电　苏联著名长跑家库兹9月11日在莫斯科举行的一次田径赛中，以28分30.4秒的成绩创造了一万公尺赛跑的世界新成绩。
    一万公尺赛跑的世界正式纪录（28分54.2秒）是世界闻名的捷克斯洛伐克运动家查托皮克在1954年6月创造的。今年7月中旬，匈牙利运动员伊哈罗斯曾经以28分42.8秒的成绩比查托皮克的纪录快了11.4秒。现在库兹的成绩又比伊哈罗斯的成绩缩短了12.4秒。
