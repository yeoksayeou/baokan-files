Title: Soviet Jet Airliner Begins Formal Scheduled Flights
Author:
Date: 1956-09-15
Page: 6

Moscow, September 14th (This newspaper dispatch) After a long period of test flights, the Soviet "TU-104" jet airliner has officially begun scheduled flights. The first route, beginning September 15th, is the Moscow to Irkutsk line. Regular routes to be opened soon include Moscow-Tbilisi, Moscow-Tashkent, Moscow-Khabarovsk, and Prague-Moscow-Beijing. The entire flight from Prague to Beijing via Moscow on the "TU-104" jet airliner takes only fourteen and a half hours. The ordinary "IL-12" airliner requires forty-two hours and twenty-five minutes.

　　On September 13th, a special ticket office for "TU-104" jet airliners opened in Moscow.

　　The "TU-104" jet airliner began test flights in May 1955. It has visited London, Copenhagen, Budapest, Warsaw, Bucharest, Beijing, Prague, and other places. During the test flights, many excellent jet airliner pilots and navigators were trained.

### 苏联喷气客机正式定期开航

1956-09-15
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联喷气客机正式定期开航
    本报莫斯科14日电　经过长时间试飞后，苏联“TY—104”式喷气客机正式定期开航。定9月15日开始的第一条航线是莫斯科到伊尔库茨克线。不久将开辟的定期航线有莫斯科—第比利斯线、莫斯科—塔什干线、莫斯科—伯力线、布拉格—莫斯科—北京线。“TY—104”式喷气客机由布拉格起飞经过莫斯科到达北京全程飞行只要十四小时三十分。而普通的“ИЛ—12”式客机飞行则需要四十二小时二十五分。
    9月13日，在莫斯科开设了“TY—104”式喷气客机专门售票处。
    “TY—104”式喷气客机试航于1955年5月。它先后访问过伦敦、哥本哈根、布达佩斯、华沙、布加勒斯特、北京、布拉格等地。在试飞中培养出很多优秀的喷气客机驾驶员与领航员。
