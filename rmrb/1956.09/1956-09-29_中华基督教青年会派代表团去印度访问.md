Title: Chinese YMCA Sends Delegation to Visit India
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 4

The Chinese YMCA has sent a delegation to visit India at the invitation of the YMCA Federation of India and Ceylon. The delegation, led by Li Shoupao, Deputy General Secretary of the National Association of Chinese YMCA, and including members Wang Shenyin, Xiong Zhenpei, Zhu Peien, Luo Guanzong, and Situ Tong, a group of six people, left Beijing on September 25 for a friendly visit to India. Among those who saw them off at the airport were Lu Qichen, General Secretary of the Beijing YMCA, and other church figures.

### 中华基督教青年会派代表团去印度访问

1956-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    中华基督教青年会派代表团去印度访问
    本报28日讯　中华基督教青年会应印度、锡兰基督教青年会联合会的邀请派代表团去印度访问。代表团团长、中华基督教青年会全国协会副总干事李寿葆，团员王神荫、熊真沛、诸培恩、罗冠宗、司徒桐等一行六人于9月25日离京赴印度进行友好访问。到机场欢送的有北京基督教青年会总干事陆琪臣等教会人士。
