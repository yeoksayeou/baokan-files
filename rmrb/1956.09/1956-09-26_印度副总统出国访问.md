Title: Indian Vice President Visits Foreign Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-09-26
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, September 25th - Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan left New Delhi on the 25th for a four-week goodwill visit to Singapore, Indonesia, Japan and China.

At the airport, Radhakrishnan told reporters that he was visiting these countries with great interest. He said that he was visiting various types of countries and could learn a lot. He expressed hope that this trip would promote the cause of peace.

### 印度副总统出国访问

1956-09-26
第7版()
专栏：

    印度副总统出国访问
    新华社新德里25日电　印度副总统拉德哈克里希南25日离开新德里前往新加坡、印度尼西亚、日本和中国作为期四个星期的友好访问。
    拉德哈克里希南在机场告诉记者们说，他以很大的兴趣去访问这些国家。他说，他去访问的是各种不同类型的国家，可以学到很多东西。他表示希望这次旅行将对和平事业有所促进。
