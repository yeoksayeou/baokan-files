Title: Greek Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Greek delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Those who welcomed them at the airport included Chen Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Zhu Boshen, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and representatives of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

### 希腊访华代表团到达北京

1956-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    希腊访华代表团到达北京
    新华社6日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的希腊访华代表团一行十人，在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的，有中国人民外交学会副会长陈翰笙、中国人民对外文化协会副秘书长朱伯深和中国国际贸易促进委员会的代表等。
