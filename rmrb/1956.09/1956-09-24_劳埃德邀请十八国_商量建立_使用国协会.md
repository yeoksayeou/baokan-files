Title: Lloyd Invites Eighteen Countries to Discuss Establishing "Users' Association"
Author:
Date: 1956-09-24
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, London, September 23rd - The British Foreign Office announced on the 22nd that Lloyd, chairman of the London conference of eighteen countries discussing the Suez Canal issue, has sent invitations to the governments of these countries, inviting them to participate in an "ambassador-level meeting" to establish a "Suez Canal Users' Association" and to establish the two bodies of this association - a council and an "executive group." This meeting will be held in London on October 1st.

### 劳埃德邀请十八国  商量建立“使用国协会”

1956-09-24
第7版()
专栏：

    劳埃德邀请十八国
    商量建立“使用国协会”
    新华社伦敦23日电　英国外交部22日宣布，十八国讨论苏伊士运河问题的伦敦会议主席劳埃德已经向这些国家的政府发出邀请书，邀请它们参加“大使级会议”，来建立“苏伊士运河使用国协会”和建立这个协会的两个机构——理事会和“执行小组”。这次会议将在10月1日在伦敦举行。
