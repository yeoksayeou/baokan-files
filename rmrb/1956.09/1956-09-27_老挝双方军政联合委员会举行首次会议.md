Title: Lao Joint Military-Political Committees Hold First Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-09-27
Page: 8
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 25th: According to a Vientiane radio broadcast, the political and military joint committees between the Lao Royal Government delegation and the Lao Fighting Forces delegation held their first meeting at the Prime Minister's Office of the Lao Royal Government at 10:00 AM today.

    The head of the Lao Royal Government delegation, Sananikone, delivered the opening remarks. He formally introduced the members of the two delegations of the Lao Royal Government to the two delegations of the Lao Fighting Forces. At the same time, he expressed his hope that, in the spirit of unity, tolerance, and mutual understanding, this negotiation would achieve good results.

### 老挝双方军政联合委员会举行首次会议

1956-09-27
第8版()
专栏：

    老挝双方军政联合委员会举行首次会议
    新华社河内25日电　据万象电台广播，老挝王国政府代表团和寮国战斗部队代表团之间的政治和军事两个联合委员会，今天上午十时在老挝王国政府首相府举行第一次会议。
    老挝王国政府代表团团长萨纳尼空致开幕词。他正式向寮国战斗部队的两个代表团介绍老挝王国政府的两个代表团人员，同时他还表示，希望在团结、忍让和互相了解的精神下，使这一次的谈判获得良好的结果。
