Title: Soviet Mountaineers Return to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-09-09
Page: 4
    Sports News

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th - The Soviet mountaineering team that climbed Muztagh Ata returned to Moscow by plane from Beijing on September 7th. They were welcomed at the airport by representatives from the All-Union Committee for Physical Culture and Sports, the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, and the Moscow sports community.

### 苏联爬山队员回到莫斯科

1956-09-09
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    苏联爬山队员回到莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科8日电　攀登慕士塔格山的苏联爬山队员于9月7日乘飞机从北京到达莫斯科。他们在机场受到全苏体育运动委员会、全苏工会中央理事会和莫斯科体育界的代表的欢迎。
