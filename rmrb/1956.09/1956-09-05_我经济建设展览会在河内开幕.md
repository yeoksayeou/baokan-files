Title: My Economic Construction Exhibition Opens in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1956-09-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 3rd: The Chinese Economic Construction Achievements Exhibition officially opened in Hanoi today after Premier Pham Van Dong cut the ribbon.

　　The opening ceremony began at 3 p.m. in front of the huge exhibition hall with yellow walls and a green roof. In front of the exhibition hall stood the national flags of China and Vietnam, symbolizing the unbreakable brotherly friendship between the people of China and Vietnam.

　　Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam Luo Guibo said in his opening speech that we hope this exhibition can enhance mutual understanding between the people of China and Vietnam and help promote mutual economic and trade exchanges.

　　Vietnamese Minister of Commerce Phan Anh said that the holding of the exhibition shows that the brotherly friendship between Vietnam and China is developing day by day. The economic relationship between Vietnam and China is built on the basis of equality and cooperation. This exhibition will further strengthen the economic relationship between Vietnam and China.

　　After Premier Pham Van Dong cut the ribbon amidst warm applause, more than 900 guests who attended the ceremony, accompanied by Ambassador Luo Guibo and Wang Jiujing, head of the Chinese Economic Exhibition Delegation, visited the exhibition.

### 我经济建设展览会在河内开幕

1956-09-05
第5版()
专栏：

    我经济建设展览会在河内开幕
    据新华社河内3日电　中国经济建设成就展览会今天由范文同总理剪彩以后，在河内正式开幕。
    开幕典礼下午三时在黄墙绿顶的巨大的展览馆前开始举行。展览馆的门前矗立着中越两国国旗，象征着中越两国人民牢不可破的兄弟般的友谊。
    中国驻越南大使罗贵波在开幕词中说，我们希望这次的展出能够增进中越两国人民间的相互了解，有助于相互间的经济贸易往来。
    越南商业部部长潘英说，展览会的举行表明越中两国兄弟般的友谊日益发展。越南和中国之间的经济关系，是建立在平等和合作的基础上。这次的展览将使越南和中国之间的经济关系更进一步密切起来。
    范文同总理在热烈的掌声中剪彩以后，参加典礼的九百多名来宾在罗贵波大使和中国经济展览团团长王久敬的陪同下，参观了展览会。
