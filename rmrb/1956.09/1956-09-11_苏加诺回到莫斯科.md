Title: Sukarno Returns to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-09-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 10th. According to TASS: After traveling more than 10,000 kilometers throughout the Soviet Union, the President of the Republic of Indonesia, Sukarno, and his entourage returned to Moscow by plane from Stalingrad on the afternoon of September 9th.

　　Stalingrad was the last city Sukarno visited on his long journey throughout the Soviet Union. He arrived in this city from Sochi on the morning of September 9th. The Chairman of the Stalingrad City Soviet, Dinkin, held a banquet for Sukarno and presented him with a small box containing soil from the graves of the defenders of Stalingrad.

### 苏加诺回到莫斯科

1956-09-11
第6版()
专栏：

    苏加诺回到莫斯科
    据新华社10日讯　据塔斯社报道：印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺和他的随行人员在苏联各地作了一万多公里的访问旅行以后，在9月9日下午乘飞机从斯大林格勒回到莫斯科。
    斯大林格勒是苏加诺这次在苏联各地作长途旅行中所到的最后一个城市。他在9日上午由索契到达这个城市。斯大林格勒市苏维埃主席丁金为苏加诺举行了宴会，并且把一只盛装着斯大林格勒保卫者的坟墓上的泥土的小匣赠送给他。
