Title: Jiangxi Establishes Forestry Science Institute
Author:
Date: 1956-09-09
Page: 3

    Jiangxi Establishes Forestry Science Institute
    (Our report) Jiangxi is one of the major forest producing areas in southern China. The province will begin in 1956 to cultivate 50 million mu of various forests, nurture 257,754 mu of forest seedlings, and close off 20 million mu for afforestation within ten years. In order to meet the needs of forestry development, the Jiangxi Provincial Forestry Science Institute, the Forest Pest Control Institute, and the Forest Seed Inspection Station were established in August this year. These institutions are responsible for systematically studying and disseminating forestry science knowledge, and guiding farmers in mountainous areas to carry out forest pest control and forest seed technology inspection.

### 江西成立林业科学研究所

1956-09-09
第3版()
专栏：

    江西成立林业科学研究所
    本报讯　江西是我国南方主要林产区之一，全省将从1956年开始，在十年内营造各种森林五千万亩，培育森林苗木二十五万七千七百五十四亩，封山育林二千万亩。为了适应林业工作发展的需要，今年8月份建立了江西省林业科学研究所、森林病虫害防治所、林木种籽检验站等机构，这些机构负责系统地研究和传播林业科学知识，指导山区农民开展森林病虫害的防治、林木种籽技术检验等工作。
