Title: Jordan Rejects Britain's Unreasonable Demands: Does Not Allow British Troops to Land

Author:

Date: 1956-09-06
Page: 6

    Jordan Rejects Britain's Unreasonable Demands
    Does Not Allow British Troops to Land
    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, September 4th: Newspapers report that Britain is putting pressure on Jordan, attempting to get Jordanian authorities to allow British troops to land in Aqaba and to allow reinforcements for the air force units based at Mafraq airport in northern Jordan.
    According to reports, the Jordanian government has rejected Britain's demands.

### 拒绝英国无理要求  约旦不准英军登陆

1956-09-06
第6版()
专栏：

    拒绝英国无理要求
    约旦不准英军登陆
    据新华社5日讯　塔斯社大马士革4日讯：报纸消息说，英国正在对约旦施以压力，企图使约旦当局准许英国军队在亚喀巴登陆和准许增援以约旦北部的麦法拉克机场为基地的空军部队。
    据报道，约旦政府已经拒绝了英国的要求。
