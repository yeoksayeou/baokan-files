Title: Abundant Grape Harvest on Changbai Mountain
Author:
Date: 1956-09-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency Report:

    This year, the wild grapes on Changbai Mountain in southeastern Jilin Province have had a rare bumper harvest, estimated to be 20% higher than last year. The wild grapes are now ripe, and farmers are gathering them in groups on the mountain.
    Wild grapes are the raw material for wine. Originally a wild plant, agricultural cooperatives in various places have now begun transplanting wild grapes into their home gardens. Linjiang County alone has transplanted 100,000 plants, and by the autumn of 1958, it is expected to harvest 6 million jin of wild grapes.

### 长白山上葡萄丰收

1956-09-04
第3版()
专栏：

    长白山上葡萄丰收
    据新华社讯　今年吉林省东南部的长白山上的山葡萄获得几年来少有的丰收，估计要比去年多收二成。现在山葡萄已经成熟，农民成群上山采集。
    山葡萄是葡萄酒的原料，原是野生植物，今年各地农业社已开始把野生山葡萄移植在家园里。仅临江县已移植了十万株，到1958年秋天就可收六百万斤山葡萄。
