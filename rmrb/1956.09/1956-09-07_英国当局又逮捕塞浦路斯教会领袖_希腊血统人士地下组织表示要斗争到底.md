Title: British Authorities Arrest Cypriot Church Leader Again; Greek Cypriot Underground Organization Vows to Fight to the End
Author:
Date: 1956-09-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Nicosia, 6th: British authorities in Cyprus arrested Nicos Kranidiotis, Director-General of the Cypriot Church Committee, on September 5th on the grounds that he was connected with local armed anti-British activities.

This is the second persecution by the British authorities in Cyprus against this leading Cypriot organization in a week; on August 29th, the British authorities placed Bishop Anthimos, another leader of the Church Committee, under house arrest on the same pretext.

According to United Press, the news of Kranidiotis's arrest "spread like wildfire among church personnel." The Church Committee office issued a statement that evening protesting the British authorities' action. The statement pointed out: "This action is a new example of the repressive policy implemented by the British authorities in Cyprus."

Meanwhile, a newly formed underground organization of Greek Cypriots, the "Political Committee for the Struggle for Cyprus," distributed leaflets saying: "The British must always remember: all Greek Cypriots are members of the 'Struggle for Cyprus' organization and participate in the struggle against enslavement and for freedom. Harding (British Governor of Cyprus) has not yet understood that if he intends to arrest and imprison members of the 'Struggle for Cyprus' organization, then he must imprison all Cypriots."

The leaflet said, "As long as one person is alive in our ranks, as long as we have one weapon, or even if only our hands and teeth remain, our struggle will continue. British: what are you doing on this Greek island?"

### 英国当局又逮捕塞浦路斯教会领袖  希腊血统人士地下组织表示要斗争到底

1956-09-07
第6版()
专栏：

    英国当局又逮捕塞浦路斯教会领袖
    希腊血统人士地下组织表示要斗争到底
    新华社6日讯　尼科西亚消息：塞浦路斯英国当局9月5日逮捕了塞浦路斯教会委员会总干事尼科斯·克拉尼迪奥蒂斯，罪名是，说他同当地的武装反英活动有关连。
    这是在一个星期内英国当局对塞浦路斯人的这个领导机构的第二次迫害；8月29日，英国当局曾经以同样的借口软禁了教会委员会的另一个领袖安戴莫斯主教。
    合众社消息说，逮捕克拉尼迪奥蒂斯的消息“像野火一样传遍了教会人士”。教会委员会办事处在当天晚上就发表声明，抗议英国当局的这一措施。声明指出：“这个行动是塞浦路斯英国当局所执行的镇压政策的新的例证”。
    同时，希腊血统的塞浦路斯人新近成立的一个地下组织“为塞浦路斯而斗争政治委员会”散发传单说：“英国人必须永远记住：全体希腊血统的塞浦路斯人都是“为塞浦路斯而斗争”组织的成员，并且参加反对奴役、争取自由的斗争。哈定（英国驻塞浦路斯总督）还没有了解，假如他打算逮捕和监禁“为塞浦路斯而斗争”组织的成员，那么他就必须监禁全体塞浦路斯人。”
    这份传单说，“只要我们的阵地上还有一个人活着，只要我们还有一件武器，或者还仅仅剩下了我们的手和牙齿，我们的斗争就仍然要继续下去。英国人：你们在这个希腊海岛上作什么呢？”
