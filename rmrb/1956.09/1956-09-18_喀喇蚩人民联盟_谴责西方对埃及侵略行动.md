Title: Karachi People's League Condemns Western Aggression Against Egypt
Author:
Date: 1956-09-18
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, September 17th. The Working Committee of the Karachi People's League passed a resolution on September 16th condemning the Western countries' orchestration of the "Suez Canal Users' Association" as an act of aggression interfering in Egypt's internal affairs.

    The Working Committee requested the Pakistani government to support Egypt in the event of an attack by Western countries.

    The Working Committee's resolution also expressed the hope that the government would adopt an independent foreign policy and support the national movements of Asian and African countries.

### 喀喇蚩人民联盟  谴责西方对埃及侵略行动

1956-09-18
第7版()
专栏：

    喀喇蚩人民联盟
    谴责西方对埃及侵略行动
    新华社喀喇蚩17日电　喀喇蚩人民联盟工作委员会9月16日通过决议，谴责西方国家策划成立“运河使用国协会”是干涉埃及内政的侵略行动。
    工作委员会要求巴基斯坦政府在埃及一旦受到西方国家进攻的时候支持埃及。
    工作委员会的决议还希望政府采取独立的外交政策，并且支持亚非国家的民族运动。
