Title: International Conference of Sinologists Held in Paris
Author:
Date: 1956-09-09
Page: 7
　
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 6th Tass: The Ninth International Conference of Sinologists, discussing the periodization of Chinese history, was held in Paris. More than 150 representatives from 21 countries attended the conference.

　　Jian Bozan, professor at Peking University, Kovalev, Deputy Director of the Institute of Oriental Studies of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and Sinologists from other countries delivered reports at the meeting.

### 国际汉学家代表会议在巴黎举行

1956-09-09
第7版()
专栏：

    国际汉学家代表会议在巴黎举行
    新华社讯　塔斯社巴黎6日讯：讨论中国历史分期问题的第九次国际汉学家代表会议在巴黎举行。参加这次会议的有来自二十一个国家的一百五十多个代表。
    北京大学教授翦伯赞、苏联科学院东方学研究所副所长科瓦利夫以及其他国家的汉学家在会上作了报告。
