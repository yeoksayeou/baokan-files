Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, September 22nd - The 58th meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks was held at the Palais des Nations here at 10:00 am on September 22nd. The meeting adjourned at 12:05 pm and the next meeting is scheduled for October 4th.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1956-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦22日电　中美两国大使级会谈第五十八次会议9月22日上午十时在这里国联大厦举行。会议在十二时零五分休会，下次会议定10月4日举行。
