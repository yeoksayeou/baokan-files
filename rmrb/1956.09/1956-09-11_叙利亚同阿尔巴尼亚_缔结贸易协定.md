Title: Syria and Albania Conclude Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-09-11
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 10th. Syria and Albania concluded a one-year trade agreement on September 9th.

    The joint communique of the two countries announced that both sides guaranteed to promote trade between the two countries and stipulated that Syria would export agricultural products to Albania in exchange for Albanian coal and asphalt.

### 叙利亚同阿尔巴尼亚  缔结贸易协定

1956-09-11
第6版()
专栏：

    叙利亚同阿尔巴尼亚
    缔结贸易协定
    新华社10日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚同阿尔巴尼亚在9月9日缔结了一项为期一年的贸易协定。
    两国的联合公报宣布，双方保证促进两国之间的贸易，并且规定叙利亚向阿尔巴尼亚输出农产品以交换阿尔巴尼亚的煤和沥青。
