Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet for Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-09-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet at the International Club today at noon to welcome the delegation of the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences, headed by Academician Jaroslav Prušek.

Among those present as hosts were many Chinese scientists, as well as Václav, Counselor of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, and Šimerka, First Secretary, and Handel, Second Secretary.

### 郭沫若欢宴捷克斯洛伐克科学院代表团

1956-09-30
第2版()
专栏：

    郭沫若欢宴捷克斯洛伐克科学院代表团
    新华社29日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若今天中午在国际俱乐部举行宴会，欢迎以亚·普实克院士为首的捷克斯洛伐克科学院代表团。
    出席作陪的有中国科学家多人和捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆参赞瓦塞克，一等秘书谢默拉和二等秘书汉德尔。
