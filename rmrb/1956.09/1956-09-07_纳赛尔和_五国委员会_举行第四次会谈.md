Title: Nasser and "Five-Nation Committee" Hold Fourth Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-09-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 6th: The "Five-Nation Committee" delegation, headed by Australian Prime Minister Menzies, held its fourth meeting with Egyptian President Nasser in Cairo on the afternoon of September 5th.

After the meeting, the "Five-Nation Committee" spokesman said that the meeting lasted for one hour and forty-five minutes, during which a general round-table discussion was held on "all aspects of the problem." He emphasized that, up to now, the entire meeting has been conducted in a good atmosphere. There was no "excitement or hostility" during the meeting.

The spokesman said that Menzies hopes to conclude his committee's mission in Egypt before this Saturday. Menzies will go to London after the conclusion of the talks to personally report to London Conference Chairman Lloyd.

### 纳赛尔和“五国委员会”举行第四次会谈

1956-09-07
第6版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔和“五国委员会”举行第四次会谈
    据新华社6日讯　开罗消息：以澳大利亚总理孟席斯为首的“五国委员会”代表团9月5日下午在开罗同埃及总统纳赛尔举行了第四次会谈。
    会后，“五国委员会”发言人说，会谈进行了一小时四十五分，会上就“问题的各方面”进行了一般的圆桌讨论。他强调说，到现在为止，整个会谈是在良好气氛中进行的。会谈中没有“激动或敌对的情绪”。
    发言人说，孟席斯希望在本星期六以前结束他的委员会在埃及的任务。孟席斯将在结束会谈以后去伦敦，亲自向伦敦会议主席劳埃德提出报告。
