Title: Syrian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 28th: The Syrian Cultural Delegation, invited by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China to visit China, arrived in Beijing this afternoon. The delegation was led by En. Nader. Nabulsi, Dean of the Faculty of Science of the University of Syria.

	Those who welcomed them at the airport included Xia Yan, Deputy Minister of Culture, as well as representatives from the Ministry of Higher Education, Peking University, and the Beijing Municipal Education Bureau.

### 叙利亚文化代表团到北京

1956-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    叙利亚文化代表团到北京
    新华社28日讯　应中华人民共和国文化部邀请来中国访问的叙利亚文化代表团一行十人，在团长、叙利亚大学理学院教授恩·纳德尔·纳布勒西率领下今天下午到达北京。
    到飞机场欢迎的有文化部副部长夏衍，以及高等教育部、北京大学、北京市教育局等单位的代表。
