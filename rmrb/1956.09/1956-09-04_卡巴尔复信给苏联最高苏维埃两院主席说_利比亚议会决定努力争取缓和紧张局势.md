Title: Kabar Replies to the Chairmen of the Two Chambers of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Saying the Libyan Parliament Decided to Strive to Ease Tensions
Author:
Date: 1956-09-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tripoli, 3rd Dispatch (TASS): The Libyan Parliament's House of Representatives, in reply to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR's appeal to all countries' parliaments on the disarmament issue, said, "The Libyan Parliament's House of Representatives has taken note of this issue and will do everything possible to realize the noble task of easing international tensions."

The reply, signed by Majid Kabar, Chairman of the Libyan Parliament's House of Representatives, was addressed to the Chairmen of the Soviet of the Union and the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR. The Soviet Embassy in Libya received this reply on August 30.

### 卡巴尔复信给苏联最高苏维埃两院主席说  利比亚议会决定努力争取缓和紧张局势

1956-09-04
第5版()
专栏：

    卡巴尔复信给苏联最高苏维埃两院主席说
    利比亚议会决定努力争取缓和紧张局势
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社的黎波里讯：利比亚议会众议院答复苏联最高苏维埃关于裁军问题致各国议会的呼吁书说，“利比亚议会众议院注意到这个问题，将尽一切努力来实现缓和国际紧张局势这一崇高的任务”。
    这封由利比亚议会众议院主席麦吉德·卡巴尔签署的复信是给苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席和民族院主席的。苏联驻利比亚大使馆在8月30日接到了这封复信。
