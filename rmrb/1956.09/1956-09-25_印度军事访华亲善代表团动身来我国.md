Title: Indian Military Goodwill Delegation to China Departs for Our Country
Author:
Date: 1956-09-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, September 24th. The Indian military goodwill delegation to China, led by Lieutenant General K.S. Thimayya, left Delhi for China this morning on an Indian Air Force aircraft.
    Those who saw them off at the airport included Rear Admiral A. Chakravarti, Acting Chief of Naval Staff of India, other officers of the Indian Defence Forces, and Senior Colonel Zhang Tong, Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in India.

### 印度军事访华亲善代表团动身来我国

1956-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    印度军事访华亲善代表团动身来我国
    新华社新德里24日电　由季·恩·乔杜里中将率领的印度军事访华亲善代表团今天上午乘印度空军飞机离开德里前往中国。
    到机场欢送的有印度代理海军参谋长阿·查克拉瓦蒂海军准将和印度防务部队的其他军官，以及中国驻印度大使馆武官张彤大校。
