Title: All-China Women's Federation Holds Tea Party to Entertain Mrs. Acharya
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. The All-China Democratic Women's Federation held a tea party this afternoon to entertain the wife of the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Tanka Prasad Acharya.

    Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, presided over the tea party. On behalf of the All-China Women's Federation and Chinese women, she extended her greetings to Prime Minister Acharya's wife and, through her, to all Nepalese sisters. She said that the Prime Minister's wife's visit to China shows that the road of friendship between the sisters of the two countries is becoming wider and wider. She hoped that the ties between the women of the two countries could be further strengthened in the future to enhance mutual friendship and understanding.

    Prime Minister Acharya's wife spoke amidst warm applause. She said she was pleased to have the opportunity to meet with Chinese women friends. She said that the people of Nepal and China are not only old friends but also close neighbors. She wished Chinese sisters and children health and happiness.

    The guests and hosts had a warm and friendly conversation at the tea party.

    Many women representatives from all walks of life, heads of various departments of the All-China Women's Federation, and heads of the Beijing Women's Federation attended the tea party today.

### 全国妇联举行茶会招待阿查里雅夫人

1956-09-29
第2版()
专栏：

    全国妇联举行茶会招待阿查里雅夫人
    新华社28日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会今天下午举行茶会，招待尼泊尔王国首相坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅的夫人。
    全国妇联副主席李德全主持这次茶会，她代表全国妇联和中国妇女向阿查里雅首相夫人，并通过她向全体尼泊尔姊妹们致敬。她说，首相夫人这次访问中国，说明两国姊妹们友谊的道路越来越宽广。她希望今后能更进一步加强两国妇女间的联系，以增进彼此间的友谊和了解。
    阿查里雅首相夫人在热烈掌声中讲话。她对有机会同中国妇女朋友见面感到高兴，她说，尼泊尔和中国两国人民不但是老朋友，而且是最近的邻居。她祝中国姊妹和孩子们健康和幸福。
    宾主在茶会上进行了热烈友好的交谈。
    今天出席茶会作陪的有各界妇女代表、全国妇联各部门负责人和北京市妇联负责人等多人。
