Title: Vietnamese Football Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-27
Page: 7

    Vietnamese Football Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    (Our Newspaper) The Vietnamese football delegation, consisting of twenty-eight members led by Song Viet Xuan, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of September 25th, invited to participate in the friendly football matches between China, North Korea, and Vietnam. Upon arrival, the guests were warmly welcomed by Zhu Debao, Deputy Director of the Competition Department of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, and Chinese athletes.

### 越南足球代表队到京

1956-09-27
第7版()
专栏：

    越南足球代表队到京
    本报讯　应邀来我国参加中、朝、越三国足球友谊赛的越南足球代表队一行二十八人，在领队宋曰春的率领下，在9月25日晚乘火车到达北京。客人们下车后，受到了我国体育运动委员会竞赛司副司长朱德宝和我国运动员的热烈欢迎。
