Title: International Democratic Women's Federation Issues Statement Demanding Peaceful Resolution of Suez Canal Issue
Author:
Date: 1956-09-19
Page: 8

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 17th. TASS reports: The International Democratic Women's Federation has issued a statement criticizing the Western countries' policies on the Suez Canal issue.

The organization demands "the use of all peaceful means to seek a solution that both safeguards Egypt's rights and takes into account the interests of the countries using the Suez Canal."

### 国际民主妇联发表声明  要求和平解决苏彝士运河问题

1956-09-19
第8版()
专栏：

    国际民主妇联发表声明
    要求和平解决苏彝士运河问题
    新华社18日讯　塔斯社柏林17日讯，国际民主妇女联合会发表声明，指责西方国家在苏彝士运河问题上的政策。
    这个组织要求“运用一切和平手段以求得既保障埃及权利又照顾到苏彝士运河使用国利益的解决办法”。
