Title: Polish Parliament Delegation Visits Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1956-09-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 4th: The Polish Parliament delegation, invited to visit Yugoslavia by the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia, arrived in Belgrade by plane on September 3rd. The delegation will visit Yugoslavia for more than two weeks.

### 波议会代表团赴南访问

1956-09-05
第5版()
专栏：

    波议会代表团赴南访问
    据新华社4日讯　应南斯拉夫联邦国民议会的邀请到南斯拉夫访问的波兰议会代表团在9月3日乘飞机到达贝尔格莱德。代表团将在南斯拉夫访问两个多星期。
