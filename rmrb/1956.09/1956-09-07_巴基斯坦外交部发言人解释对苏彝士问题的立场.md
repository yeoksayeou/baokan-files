Title: Pakistani Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Explains Position on Suez Issue
Author:
Date: 1956-09-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. According to a Reuters dispatch from Karachi, a spokesperson for the Pakistani Foreign Ministry explained Pakistan's position on the Suez Canal issue on September 5th, stating that Pakistan recognizes Egypt's right to nationalize the canal but supports the implementation of international control over the canal within the scope of Egyptian sovereignty.

He made these remarks in response to some reports reaching here that President Nasser said that Pakistani Foreign Minister Chaudhri had violated his promise to support Egypt on the Suez Canal issue. This promise was reportedly made when Chaudhri visited Cairo last month on his way to attend the London Conference.

The spokesperson said that Egypt had not lodged a formal protest through diplomatic channels regarding Pakistan's position on the Suez issue.

He also denied reports that Chaudhri had been notified not to pass through Cairo when returning to Karachi after the London Conference ended.

### 巴基斯坦外交部发言人解释对苏彝士问题的立场

1956-09-07
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦外交部发言人解释对苏彝士问题的立场
    新华社6日讯　据路透社从喀喇蚩发出的消息说，巴基斯坦外交部一位发言人9月5日解释巴基斯坦在苏彝士运河问题上的立场说，巴基斯坦承认埃及把运河收归国有的权利，但赞成在埃及主权范围内对运河实行国际管制。
    他是针对传到这里的一些消息说这番话的。这些消息说，纳赛尔总统说巴基斯坦外交部长乔德里违背了在苏彝士运河问题上支持埃及的诺言。据说这个诺言是在乔德里上个月去参加伦敦会议途中访问开罗的时候许下的。
    这位发言人说，没有收到埃及通过外交途径对巴基斯坦在苏彝士问题立场上提出的正式抗议。
    他同时否认下列消息：有人通知乔德里在伦敦会议结束返回喀喇蚩的时候不要经过开罗。
