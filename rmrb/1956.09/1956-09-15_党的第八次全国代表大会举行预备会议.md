Title: Preparatory Meeting Held for the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1956-09-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 14th — One thousand and eighteen delegates (with eight others yet to arrive) and eighty-six alternate delegates to the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China arrived in Beijing from all over the country on August 28. From August 29 to September 13, a preparatory meeting for the National Congress was held. All the delegates and alternate delegates present formed delegate groups according to their elected regions and units, and conducted detailed discussions on the various reports and documents that the Central Committee prepared to submit to the National Congress, and put forward opinions for revision. The delegates also fully deliberated and prepared for the election of the Eighth Central Committee.

### 党的第八次全国代表大会举行预备会议

1956-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

    党的第八次全国代表大会举行预备会议
    新华社14日讯　中国共产党第八次全国代表大会代表一千零一十八人（另有八人尚未到达）候补代表八十六人，于8月28日由全国各地到达北京，从8月29日到9月13日，举行全国代表大会的预备会议。到会的全体代表和候补代表，按照选举的地区和单位，分别组成了代表小组，对于中央委员会准备提向代表大会的各项报告和文件，进行了详细的讨论，并且提出了修改的意见。代表们还对于第八届中央委员会的选举工作，进行了充分的酝酿和准备。
