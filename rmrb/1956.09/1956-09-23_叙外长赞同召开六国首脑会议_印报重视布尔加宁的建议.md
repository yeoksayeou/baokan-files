Title: Syrian Foreign Minister Agrees to Six-Nation Summit Meeting; Indian Newspapers Value Bulganin's Suggestion
Author:
Date: 1956-09-23
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 21st: Cairo Radio reported on September 21st that Syrian Foreign Minister Bitar, in a statement in Cairo, expressed his support for the Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Bulganin’s proposal to convene a six-nation summit to peacefully resolve the Suez Canal issue. Bitar said that this suggestion could lead to a peaceful resolution of the Suez Canal issue. He made these remarks before departing from Cairo to return to Syria.

Xinhua News Agency, September 21st: TASS, Delhi, September 20th: The majority of Indian newspapers today placed particular emphasis on the replies of Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Bulganin to questions posed by Kingsbury Smith, Vice President of the International News Service of the United States.

The "Indian Express" published the news about Bulganin's replies under the large headline: "Bulganin Proposes Six-Nation Summit on Suez Issue, Calls for Seeking a Broad Basis for Agreement."

The headline in the "Times of India" emphasized that the Soviet Union advocates a six-nation meeting on the Suez issue in order to seek a just solution to the problem.

### 叙外长赞同召开六国首脑会议  印报重视布尔加宁的建议

1956-09-23
第7版()
专栏：

    叙外长赞同召开六国首脑会议
    印报重视布尔加宁的建议
    新华社讯　开罗电台9月21日报道，叙利亚外交部长比塔尔在开罗发表谈话，表示赞成苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁提出的为和平解决苏伊士运河问题而召开六国政府首脑会议的建议。比塔尔说，这个建议可以导致苏伊士运河问题的和平解决。他是从开罗启程回叙利亚之前发表这项谈话的。
    新华社21日讯　塔斯社德里20日讯：今天印度大多数报纸特别重视苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁对美国国际新闻社副社长金斯伯里·史密斯所提的问题的答复。
    “印度快报”用下面的大标题登载了关于布尔加宁的答复的消息：“布尔加宁建议就苏伊士问题举行六国政府首脑会谈，呼吁为取得协议寻求广泛的基础。”
    “印度时报”的标题着重说，苏联主张就苏伊士问题举行六国会谈，以便为这个问题谋求公正的解决。
