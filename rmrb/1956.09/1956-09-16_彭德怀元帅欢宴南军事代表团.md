Title: Marshal Peng Dehuai Hosts Banquet for Yugoslav Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-09-16
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 15th.

    Marshal Peng Dehuai Hosts Banquet for Yugoslav Military Delegation

    Xinhua News Agency, 15th. Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, Marshal Peng Dehuai, held a banquet tonight to welcome the Yugoslav Military Delegation headed by General Kosta Nadj.
    Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, and military attaches of various countries in China were also invited to the banquet.
    In his speech at the banquet welcoming the Yugoslav Military Delegation, Marshal Peng Dehuai praised the heroic fighting history of the Yugoslav people and army, and congratulated Yugoslavia on the victories it has achieved in the cause of building socialism. He pointed out that the people and army of Yugoslavia waged a heroic struggle for the independence and freedom of their nation during the period of fighting against German fascism, which was admired by the Chinese people and the people of the world. Marshal Peng Dehuai then said that there is a deep friendship and cordial feelings between the Chinese and Yugoslav peoples in the struggle for the independence and liberation of their motherlands. Now, through the visit of the Yugoslav Military Delegation, this fraternal friendship between the two countries will surely be further promoted.
    General Kosta Nadj, head of the Yugoslav Military Delegation, then spoke, thanking them for the friendly reception of the Yugoslav Military Delegation. He said that the Yugoslav people understand the past hard struggles of the Chinese people and care about the great cause of the Chinese people. The Yugoslav people know that during the difficult period when they were surrounded by enemies, in addition to the Soviet army, there was also the Chinese People's Liberation Army fighting side by side with them. General Kosta Nadj pointed out that there are many common points in the struggles of the Chinese and Yugoslav peoples, and the people's armies of the two countries draw strength from the people. He hoped that the people's armies of China and Yugoslavia will exchange experiences with each other.
    At the end of the speeches by Marshal Peng Dehuai and General Kosta Nadj, the guests all stood up and toasted the fraternal friendship between the peoples and armies of China and Yugoslavia, and the health of the leaders of the two peoples.
    Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, also spoke at the banquet.
    Attending the banquet as hosts were senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including General Huang Kecheng, General Chen Geng, General Tan Zheng, General Wang Shusheng, General Xiao Jingguang, General Xu Guangda, Senior General Xiao Ke, Senior General Li Da, Senior General Li Kenong, Senior General Xiao Hua, Air Force Senior General Liu Yalou, Senior General Yang Chengwu, Senior General Chen Shiju, and Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong.
    Before the banquet, Marshal Peng Dehuai received all members of the Yugoslav Military Delegation.

### 彭德怀元帅欢宴南军事代表团

1956-09-16
第3版()
专栏：

    彭德怀元帅欢宴南军事代表团
    新华社15日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅今晚举行宴会，欢迎以科斯塔·纳季上将为首的南斯拉夫军事代表团。
    南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇和各国驻华武官也应邀出席了宴会。
    彭德怀元帅在宴会上致词欢迎南斯拉夫军事代表团时，赞扬南斯拉夫人民和军队的英勇斗争历史，并且祝贺南斯拉夫在建设社会主义的事业中获得的胜利。他指出南斯拉夫的人民和军队在反对德国法西斯的时期，为了自己民族的独立和自由进行了英勇的斗争，受到了中国人民和全世界人民的钦佩。彭德怀元帅接着说，中、南两国人民在争取自己祖国独立和解放的斗争中，就存在着深厚的友谊，有着亲切的感情。现在通过南斯拉夫军事代表团的访问，必将进一步促进两国之间的这种兄弟友谊。
    南斯拉夫军事代表团团长科斯塔·纳季上将接着讲话，感谢对南斯拉夫军事代表团的友好的招待。他说，南斯拉夫人民了解中国人民过去的艰苦的斗争，关怀着中国人民的伟大事业。南斯拉夫人民知道当他们在敌人的包围下的艰苦的时期，除了苏联军队之外，还有中国人民解放军是在同他们并肩地进行着战斗。科斯塔·纳季将军指出，中、南两国人民的斗争有着很多的共同点，两国的人民军队都是从人民中得到力量的，他希望在中、南两国人民军队之间互相交流经验。
    彭德怀元帅和科斯塔·纳季将军讲话结束的时候，宾主们都起立为中、南两国人民和军队的兄弟友谊，为两国人民领袖的健康干杯。
    南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇也在宴会上讲了话。
    出席宴会作陪的，有中国人民解放军高级将领黄克诚大将、陈赓大将、谭政大将、王树声大将、萧劲光大将、许光达大将、萧克上将、李达上将、李克农上将、萧华上将、刘亚楼空军上将、杨成武上将、陈士矩上将、萧向荣中将等人。
    在宴会举行以前，彭德怀元帅接见了南斯拉夫军事代表团的全体人员。
