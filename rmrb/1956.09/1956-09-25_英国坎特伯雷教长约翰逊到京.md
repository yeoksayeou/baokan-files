Title: Archbishop of Canterbury Johnson Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: The Archbishop of Canterbury, Dr. Johnson of England, his wife, and two daughters arrived in Beijing by train from Hankou this evening. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for the Defense of World Peace, welcomed them at the station.

Those who also welcomed them at the station included Luo Longji, Xu Deheng, and Xu Baoju, Standing Committee members of the Chinese Committee for the Defense of World Peace; He Chengxiang, Director of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council; Le Songsheng, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; Reverend Chen Chonggui; Reverend Wang Zizhong; and figures from the religious and literary circles of the capital. New Zealand peace activist Rewi Alley also welcomed them at the station.

When interviewed by reporters, Archbishop Johnson said that his recent travels in northwestern and southwestern China had given him a very good impression. He said he was deeply moved by the large-scale industrial construction currently underway in these regions and the Chinese government's correct ethnic policies.

Archbishop Johnson is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese Committee for the Defense of World Peace. Over the past month, he and his family have visited and toured Urumqi, Xining, Lanzhou, the famous Dunhuang Thousand Buddha Caves, Xi'an, Kunming, Chongqing, and Wuhan.

### 英国坎特伯雷教长约翰逊到京

1956-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    英国坎特伯雷教长约翰逊到京
    新华社24日讯　英国坎特伯雷教长约翰逊博士和他的夫人及两个女儿今晚乘火车由汉口抵达北京。中国保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若到车站欢迎。
    到车站欢迎的还有中国保卫世界和平委员会常务委员罗隆基、许德珩和许宝驹，国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘，北京市副市长乐松生，陈崇桂牧师，王梓仲牧师和首都宗教、文艺界人士。新西兰和平人士艾黎也到车站欢迎。
    约翰逊教长在接见记者时说，他最近在中国西北和西南部的旅行给他以极为良好的印象。他说，目前正在这些地区进行的规模巨大的工业建设和中国政府的正确的民族政策使他深受感动。
    约翰逊教长是应中国保卫世界和平委员会的邀请来访问中国的。在过去的一个多月中，他和他的家属在乌鲁木齐、西宁、兰州、著名的敦煌千佛洞、西安、昆明、重庆和武汉等地进行了参观访问。
