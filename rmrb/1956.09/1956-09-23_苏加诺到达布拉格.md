Title: Sukarno Arrives in Prague
Author:
Date: 1956-09-23
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 22nd. Indonesian President Sukarno and his entourage arrived in Prague today.
    Czechoslovak state and Communist leaders Sapotocky, Siroky, Novotny, and Fierlinger were at the airport to welcome the distinguished guests.
    From the airport to Prague Castle, the guests were warmly welcomed by the citizens of Prague.

### 苏加诺到达布拉格

1956-09-23
第7版()
专栏：

    苏加诺到达布拉格
    新华社布拉格22日电　印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺和他的随行人员今天到达布拉格。
    捷克斯洛伐克国家和共产党的领导人萨波托斯基、西罗基、诺沃提尼和费林格等到机场欢迎贵宾。
    从机场到布拉格宫的路上，客人们受到了布拉格市民的热烈欢迎。
