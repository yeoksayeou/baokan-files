Title: Our Swimmers Win Six First Places and Break Two National Records in Competition with Hungarian and German Athletes

Author: 
Date: 1956-09-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 1st: Budapest dispatch: The Chinese swimming team, currently visiting Hungary, held friendly matches with the swimming team of the German Democratic Republic's national reserve team and the Hungarian national reserve team in Bécs on August 29th and 30th.

    Chinese athletes won championships in six events and broke two national records.

    In the men's 200-meter breaststroke, Mu Xiangxiong won first place with a time of 2 minutes 40.8 seconds, and Qi Lieyun won second place with a time of 2 minutes 41.5 seconds. Both of them broke the national official record of 2 minutes 42 seconds held by Xu Zhixiang. Another Chinese athlete, Yan Shang, took fourth place.

    Chinese athletes took the top four places in the men's 100-meter breaststroke. Mu Xiangxiong won first place (1 minute 13.6 seconds). Yan Shang took second place, Zeng Yingyi took third place, and Xu Zhixiang took fourth place.

    In the men's 100-meter freestyle competition, Lin Jinzhu took first place with a time of 59.2 seconds. Mu Ruilong took second place.

    Lin Jinzhu also won first place in the men's 200-meter freestyle with a time of 2 minutes 14 seconds.

    Chinese athlete Li Xiqing won first place in the men's 800 meters with a time of 10 minutes 18.3 seconds.

    Lin Jinzhu, Mu Xiangxiong, Wang Qiangli, and Mu Ruilong won first place in the men's 400-meter medley relay. The time was 4 minutes 32.1 seconds, which was 2.9 seconds faster than the original official national record.

    In addition, Chinese athletes also won second place in the men's 200-meter butterfly and the women's 400-meter freestyle relay; and third place in the men's 100-meter butterfly and the women's 200-meter breaststroke.

### 我游泳运动员同匈、德运动员比赛中  获六项第一破两项全国纪录

1956-09-02
第4版()
专栏：

    我游泳运动员同匈、德运动员比赛中
    获六项第一破两项全国纪录
    新华社布拉格1日电　布达佩斯消息：现在匈牙利访问的中国游泳队8月29日、30日在贝奇城同德意志民主共和国国家候补游泳选手队和匈牙利国家候补选手队举行了友谊比赛。
    中国运动员获得了六个项目的冠军，并且打破了两项全国纪录。
    在男子二百公尺蛙泳中，穆祥雄以2分40秒8的成绩获得第一名，戚烈云以2分41秒5的成绩获得第二名，他们都打破了徐致祥所保持的2分42秒的全国正式纪录。另一名中国运动员严尚获得了第四名。
    中国运动员在男子一百公尺蛙泳中获得前四名。穆祥雄得第一名（1分13秒6）。严尚得第二名，曾英逸得第三名，徐致祥得第四名。
    在男子一百公尺自由泳比赛中，林锦珠得第一名，成绩是59秒2。穆瑞龙得第二名。
    林锦珠还以2分14秒的成绩获得男子二百公尺自由泳的第一名。
    中国运动员李喜庆以10分18秒3的成绩获得男子八百公尺第一名。
    林锦珠、穆祥雄、王强立、穆瑞龙获得了男子四百公尺混合式接力赛的第一名。成绩是4分32秒1，比原来的正式全国纪录快了2秒9。
    此外，中国运动员还获得了男子二百公尺蝶泳、女子四百公尺自由式接力第二名；男子一百公尺蝶泳、女子二百公尺蛙泳第三名。
