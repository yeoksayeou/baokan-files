Title: Egypt Does Not Recognize Any Other Canal Authority; Egyptian Intelligence Department Declares Vessels Passing Through the Canal Must Comply with All Regulations
Author:
Date: 1956-09-23
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo dispatch: According to a United Press International report from Cairo: The Egyptian Intelligence Department issued the following statement on September 19:

    "Some news agencies reported that Mahmoud Younis (General Manager of the Suez Canal Authority) had made the following statement: The Egyptian canal authorities will allow ships of the 'Users' Association' to pass through the canal if these ships have Egyptian pilots.

    "In fact, Younis said: The Egyptian government does not recognize any authority or organization other than the current Egyptian authority responsible for canal affairs. Since nationalizing the canal fifty-four days ago, the Egyptian authority has been managing canal affairs in an orderly manner and maintaining freedom of navigation on the waterway, despite the departure of foreign pilots and encountering various obstacles.

    "Younis actually said in his statement yesterday: Any ship wishing to pass through the canal should comply with all regulations and rules of the Egyptian Suez Canal Authority."

A United Press International dispatch from Port Said on September 18 reported: "When asked if he would allow ships of the proposed Users' Association to pass through the canal, Younis replied: 'We will let them pass, but such ships must comply with the regulations, which are: the ships must have our pilots on board.'"

### 埃及不承认任何其他运河机构  埃情报司声明通过运河船只必须遵守一切规定

1956-09-23
第7版()
专栏：

    埃及不承认任何其他运河机构
    埃情报司声明通过运河船只必须遵守一切规定
    新华社讯　据合众社开罗消息：埃及情报司9月19日发表声明如下：
    “某些通讯社报道说，马哈茂德·尤尼斯（苏伊士运河管理局总经理）曾经发表了这样的谈话：埃及的运河当局将准许‘使用国协会’的船只通过运河，如果这些船上有埃及领航员的话。
    “事实上，尤尼斯说：埃及政府除了目前负责运河事宜的埃及当局以外，不承认任何其他当局或组织。埃及当局自从五十四天前把运河收归国有以来，尽管外国领航员离职，并且碰到种种阻碍，一直把运河事务指挥得秩序井然，并且保持着水道上的航行自由。
    “尤尼斯实际上在他昨天的声明中这样说：任何希望通过运河的船只都应该遵守埃及苏伊士运河管理当局的一切条例和规定。”
    合众社在9月18日从塞得港发出的一则消息中曾经这样报道：“有人问尤尼斯说，他是否将准许拟议中的使用国协会的船只通过运河。他回答说：‘我们将让它通过，但是这样的船只必须遵守规章，规章是：船只上必须有我们的领航员。’”
