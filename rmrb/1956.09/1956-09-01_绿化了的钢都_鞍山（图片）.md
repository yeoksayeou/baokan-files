Anshan, the Greened Steel Capital

In recent years, the Anshan Urban Construction Bureau has vigorously carried out mass greening work, planting a total of five million trees throughout the city. Now, around the Anshan Iron and Steel Company's factory area, workers' dormitories, streets, and squares, trees have grown into forests, providing lush greenery everywhere, making this famous steel city appear particularly beautiful. This is the greened Victory Square in Anshan. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yang Limen photographed it.

### 绿化了的钢都——鞍山（图片）

1956-09-01
第2版()
专栏：

    绿化了的钢都——鞍山
    几年来，鞍山城市建设局大力开展群众性的绿化工作，全市共栽植了五百万棵树木。现在，在鞍山钢铁公司的厂区外围、职工宿舍、街道和广场，到处都是树木成林，一片绿荫，使这个著名的钢铁城市显得格外优美。这是绿化了的鞍山胜利广场。　新华社记者　杨礼门摄
