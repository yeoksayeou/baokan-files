Title: Chairman Mao Receives Belgian Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-09-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. Chairman Mao Zedong received the entire Belgian Kingdom Parliamentary Delegation, headed by Camille Huysmans, this evening.

Present at the reception were Vice-Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice-Premiers of the State Council Chen Yun, He Long, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, and Li Xiannian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Yu Xinqing, and Deputy Director of the Bills Committee Qian Duansheng.

### 毛主席接见比利时国会代表团

1956-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见比利时国会代表团
    新华社29日讯　毛泽东主席今天晚上接见了以卡米勒·胡斯曼为首的比利时王国国会代表团全体人员。
    接见的时候，在座的有朱德副主席，周恩来总理和国务院副总理陈云、贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫、李先念，外交部副部长章汉夫，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长余心清、法案委员会副主任委员钱端升。
