Title: Poland, Czechoslovakia, Romania Support Egyptian Government's Proposal; All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions Expresses Solidarity with Egypt's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1956-09-22
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 19th - A Polish government statement released here on the 19th stated that the Polish government believes the Suez Canal issue should be resolved through peaceful negotiations.
    In this statement, the Polish government reiterated its proposal to convene a conference of all countries concerned with the Suez Canal to peacefully resolve this issue, and at the same time, requested that all countries concerned with the Suez Canal refrain from using force or other forms of pressure.
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 20th - According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency on the night of the 18th: Czechoslovak Foreign Minister David, in response to questions raised by the Egyptian Ambassador to Czechoslovakia during a visit, stated that the Czechoslovak government supports Egypt's proposal to establish a negotiating body and is convinced that "this step can promote a just, peaceful, and rapid solution to the Suez Canal issue."
    Xinhua News Agency, 19th - In a reply to the Egyptian government's note on September 17th, the Romanian government expressed its full support for the Egyptian government's proposal to establish a negotiating body sufficient to represent the various opinions of the users of the Suez Canal.
    In view of the new situation created by the nationalization of the Suez Canal Company, the Romanian government expressed its readiness to participate in a conference to review the 1888 Convention.
    Xinhua News Agency, 19th - TASS, Moscow, 18th - The All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, representing its 45 million trade union members, announced that it supports and stands in solidarity with the just struggle of the Egyptian working people to win their national independence.

### 波捷罗等国支持埃及政府建议  全苏工会中央理事会声援埃及的斗争

1956-09-22
第6版()
专栏：

    波捷罗等国支持埃及政府建议
    全苏工会中央理事会声援埃及的斗争
    新华社华沙19日电　19日在这里公布的波兰政府的一项声明说，波兰政府认为，苏伊士运河问题应该以和平谈判方式求得解决。
    波兰政府在这个声明中重申它的关于召开一切同苏伊士运河有关的国家的会议来和平解决这个问题的主张，同时要求一切同苏伊士运河有关的国家都拒绝使用武力或其它压力。
    据新华社布拉格20日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社18日夜间报道：捷克斯洛伐克外交部长戴维在答复拜访他的埃及驻捷克斯洛伐克大使所提的问题时说，捷克斯洛伐克政府支持埃及的成立谈判机构的建议，并且深信：“这个步骤能够促使苏伊士运河问题得到公正的、和平的、迅速的解决。”
    据新华社19日讯　罗马尼亚政府9月17日在一份答复埃及政府照会的复照中，表示完全支持埃及政府关于成立一个足以代表苏伊士运河使用国的各种意见的谈判机构的建议。
    鉴于苏伊士运河公司国有化所造成的新局面，罗马尼亚政府表示准备参加审查1888年公约的会议。
    据新华社19日讯　塔斯社莫斯科18日讯：全苏工会中央理事会代表它的四千五百万工会会员宣布，它声援并且支持埃及劳动人民为了争取自己的民族独立而进行的正义斗争。
