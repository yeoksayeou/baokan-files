Title: Li Dequan and Others Donate Funds to the Nepal Anti-Tuberculosis Association
Author:
Date: 1956-09-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, September 22nd: Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross, and Huang Dingchen, Director of the Chinese Anti-Tuberculosis Association, presented 20,000 yuan (RMB) (equivalent to 38,314 Indian Rupees) to Tanka Prasad Acharya, Prime Minister of Nepal and President of the Nepal Anti-Tuberculosis Association, as a donation to the Nepal Anti-Tuberculosis Association. This donation was handed over to Tanka Prasad Acharya's secretary in Kathmandu on September 20th by Ma Muming, First Secretary of the Chinese Embassy in Nepal, on behalf of Ambassador Pan Zili.

### 李德全等向尼泊尔防痨协会赠款

1956-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    李德全等向尼泊尔防痨协会赠款
    新华社新德里22日电　中国红十字会会长李德全和中国防痨协会理事长黄鼎臣送交尼泊尔首相兼尼泊尔防痨协会主席坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅人民币二万元（合三万八千三百十四印度卢比），作为对尼泊尔防痨协会的捐赠。这笔赠款已经在9月20日由中国驻尼泊尔大使馆一等秘书马牧鸣代表潘自力大使在加德满都交给坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅的秘书。
