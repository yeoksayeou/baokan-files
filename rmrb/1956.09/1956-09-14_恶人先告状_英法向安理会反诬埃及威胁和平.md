Title: The Villain Sues First: Britain and France Counter-Accuse Egypt in the Security Council, Falsely Claiming Egypt Threatens Peace
Author:
Date: 1956-09-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 13th—United Nations news: The British and French representatives to the United Nations submitted a letter to the President of the Security Council, Nunes-Portuondo, on September 12, claiming that Egypt's rejection of Western proposals for the "international control" of the Suez Canal has led to an "aggravation" of the situation.

The letter said that the British and French governments believed that Egypt's rejection "indicates an aggravation of the situation; this situation, if allowed to continue, will constitute a clear threat to peace and security."

Both governments requested that the content of the letter be "brought to the attention of the members of the Security Council." However, the two governments did not request a meeting of the Security Council to consider the Suez Canal issue.

According to UN diplomats, the step taken by Britain and France to notify the Security Council is a preliminary measure, preparing to place the "crime of threatening world peace" on Egypt if Nasser refuses to accept Eden's proposal for the "Suez Canal Users Association" to take over the canal, thereby "accusing" Egypt in the Security Council.

On the same day, the Egyptian representative to the United Nations, Loutfi, met with UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld to discuss the Suez situation. Loutfi also gave a copy of the Egyptian government's statement issued on September 10 to the President of the Security Council, Nunes-Portuondo. In this statement, the Egyptian government suggested organizing a negotiating body of the canal's user countries to resolve the Suez Canal issue.

### 恶人先告状  英法向安理会反诬埃及威胁和平

1956-09-14
第5版()
专栏：

    恶人先告状
    英法向安理会反诬埃及威胁和平
    新华社13日讯　纽约联合国消息：英国和法国驻联合国代表9月12日交给安全理事会主席努奈斯—波图翁多一封信，硬说埃及拒绝西方“国际管制”苏彝士运河的建议造成了局势的“恶化”。
    这封信说，英法两国政府认为，埃及的拒绝“表示局势是恶化了；这种情况如果任其继续，将会构成对和平和安全的明显威胁”。
    两国政府要求把这封信的内容“提请安全理事会理事国注意”。但是两国政府并没有要求召开安全理事会会议来考虑苏彝士运河问题。
    据联合国外交家认为，英法两国采取这一通知安理会的步骤是一个预备性的措施，准备在纳赛尔拒绝接受艾登提出的由“运河使用国协会”来接管运河的建议时，反过来把“威胁世界和平的罪名”强加在埃及身上，借此在安理会“控告”埃及。
    在同一天，埃及驻联合国代表劳特菲会见了联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德，和他讨论了苏彝士局势问题。劳特菲还把埃及政府在9月10日发表的声明的副本交给安理会主席努奈斯—波图翁多。埃及政府在这个声明中建议组织运河各使用国的谈判机构来解决苏彝士运河问题。
