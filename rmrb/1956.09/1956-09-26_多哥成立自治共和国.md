Title: Togo Established as Autonomous Republic
Author:
Date: 1956-09-26
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Lome, September 25th: Togo (in West Africa), a French-mandated territory, celebrated the establishment of Togo as an autonomous republic on September 21st. The Togolese flag was raised for the first time on the roof of the Legislative Assembly building, accompanied by enthusiastic cheers.

### 多哥成立自治共和国

1956-09-26
第7版()
专栏：

    多哥成立自治共和国
    新华社25日讯　多哥洛美消息：法国托管地多哥（在非洲西部）9月21日庆祝多哥成为自治共和国。多哥国旗第一次在立法议会屋顶上升起，升旗的时候，人们给以热烈的欢呼。
