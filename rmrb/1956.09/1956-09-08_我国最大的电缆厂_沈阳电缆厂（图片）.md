Title: China's Largest Cable Factory - Shenyang Cable Factory (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1956-09-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zhu Ying:
    The annual output of the Shenyang Cable Factory will account for two-thirds of the total number of cable and wire products in the country, and it can produce various industrial and civil cables and wires. Cables and wires are the blood vessels of industrial power. This factory is about to be formally put into production.
    The bare wire workshop is one of the main workshops of the cable factory. In addition to producing steel-cored aluminum stranded wire, it also produces the raw materials for cables and wires - bare copper wire, to supply other workshops. This is a corner of the bare wire workshop.
    The cable workshop is producing a thick power cable for power transmission. This is the finished cable.
    The new-style insulation pulping machine is used to produce insulation pulp. This pulp is wrapped around the outer layer of the cable, which can insulate the cable and ensure safe power transmission. The complete equipment of this machine is equivalent to the equipment of a small paper mill.
    The new continuous vulcanizer in the rubber wire workshop can vulcanize and insulate at the same time, changing the backward production method of separating insulation and vulcanization into two processes, reducing product mobility and ensuring product quality.

### 我国最大的电缆厂——沈阳电缆厂（图片）

1956-09-08
第2版()
专栏：

    我国最大的电缆厂——沈阳电缆厂
    沈阳电缆厂的年产量将占全国电缆、电线产品总数的三分之二，可以生产各种工业用和民用的电缆、电线。电缆、电线是工业动力的血管。这个厂即将正式投入生产。　新华社记者　朱　瑛摄
    裸线车间是电缆厂的主要车间之一，除生产钢芯铝绞线外，还生产电缆和电线的原料——裸铜线，供应其他车间。这是裸线车间的一角。
    电缆车间在生产一种粗大的输电用的电力电缆，这是已经制成的电缆。
    新式的绝缘纸浆机是生产绝缘纸浆的。这种纸浆包在电缆外层，能使电缆绝缘，保证输电安全。这台机器的全部设备相当于一个小型造纸厂的设备。
    橡皮线车间新型的连续硫化机能够边硫化、边绝缘，改变了过去把绝缘和硫化分成二个工序的落后生产方式，减少产品的流动性，保证了产品质量。
