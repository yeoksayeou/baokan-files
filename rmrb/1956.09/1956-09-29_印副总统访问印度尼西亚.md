Title: Indian Vice President Visits Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 8

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 28th: Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan arrived in Jakarta on September 26th at the invitation of the Indonesian government. On the 27th, he flew from Jakarta to visit Yogyakarta and Bali. He is scheduled to return to the Indonesian capital on September 29th and leave Indonesia on September 30th for visits to Japan and China.

    During his stay in Jakarta, Radhakrishnan visited Indonesian Vice President Hatta, Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo, and Parliament Speaker Sardjono.

### 印副总统访问印度尼西亚

1956-09-29
第8版()
专栏：

    印副总统访问印度尼西亚
    据新华社雅加达28日电　印度副总统拉德哈克里希南9月26日应邀到达雅加达，27日乘飞机离开雅加达前往日惹和峇厘访问。他预定在9月29日回到印度尼西亚首都，并且在9月30日离开印度尼西亚前往日本和中国访问。
    拉德哈克里希南在雅加达期间，曾经拜访了印度尼西亚副总统哈达、总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约和议会议长沙多诺。
