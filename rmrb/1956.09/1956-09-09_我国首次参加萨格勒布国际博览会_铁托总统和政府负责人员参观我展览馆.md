Title: China Participates in Zagreb International Fair for the First Time; President Tito and Government Officials Visit Chinese Pavilion
Author:
Date: 1956-09-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 8th: Zagreb dispatch: The International Fair in Zagreb, Yugoslavia, opened at 11:00 AM on September 7th. Trade groups and companies from twenty-six countries in Europe, Asia, Africa, and the Americas are participating in the fair.

President Tito of Yugoslavia attended the opening ceremony of the fair and visited the pavilions of various countries.

President Tito and his wife, along with other responsible officials of the Yugoslav government, visited the Chinese Pavilion at 12:30 PM. Accompanying President Tito and others were Wu Xiuquan, the Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia, Wu Xuezhi, the head of the Chinese government's fair visiting delegation, and Xiao Fangzhou, the head of the Chinese exhibition delegation.

This is the first time China has participated in the Zagreb Fair. The Chinese Pavilion covers an area of approximately four thousand square meters and exhibits over two thousand five hundred items. The various machines, silks, and handicrafts inside the Chinese Pavilion aroused great interest among the guests.

After the visit, President Tito wrote in the guestbook: "This beautiful Chinese industrial pavilion has given me a deep impression, and it demonstrates the great creative talents of the Chinese working people."

### 我国首次参加萨格勒布国际博览会  铁托总统和政府负责人员参观我展览馆

1956-09-09
第5版()
专栏：

    我国首次参加萨格勒布国际博览会
    铁托总统和政府负责人员参观我展览馆
    新华社布拉格8日电　萨格勒布消息：在南斯拉夫萨格勒布举行的国际博览会9月7日上午十一时开幕。参加这次博览会的有欧洲、亚洲、非洲、美洲的二十六个国家的贸易团体和公司。
    南斯拉夫总统铁托参加了博览会的开幕式，并且参观了各个国家的展览馆。
    铁托总统和他的夫人以及南斯拉夫政府其他负责官员在十二时半参观了中国馆。陪同铁托总统等参观的，有中国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权、中国政府博览会参观团团长吴雪之和中国展览团团长萧方洲。
    中国是第一次参加萨格勒布博览会。中国馆的面积约有四千平方公尺，展出物品有二千五百种以上。中国馆里面的各种机器、丝绸和手工艺品引起了来宾们很大兴趣。
    铁托总统参观后在留言簿上题词说：“这个美丽的中国工业馆给予我很深的印象，它表现了中国劳动人民巨大的创造性的才能”。
