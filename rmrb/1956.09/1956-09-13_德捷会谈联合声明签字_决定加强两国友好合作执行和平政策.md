Title: Signing of the Joint Statement of the German-Czech Talks: Decision to Strengthen Friendly Cooperation and Implement a Policy of Peace
Author:
Date: 1956-09-13
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 12th: According to a report from the Czechoslovak News Agency: The talks between the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic and the Government Delegation of Czechoslovakia have concluded, and the signing ceremony for a joint statement was held on the 11th. The protocol on economic cooperation between the two countries, the treaty on legal assistance, and the agreement on cooperation in social policy were also signed simultaneously.

At the signing ceremony, Czechoslovak Premier Široký said that these documents affirmed the desire and efforts of the two governments to continuously strengthen and expand friendly cooperation between the two countries, and demonstrated their determination to continue implementing a policy of peaceful coexistence.

Premier Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic said that the results of the talks had opened a new stage in relations between the two countries. The two countries had completely identical views on all issues of foreign policy. The specialization of economic cooperation and the coordination of production between the two countries, as well as the coordination of the five-year plans of socialist countries, had opened up new and vast prospects for the development of the two countries and the entire socialist camp.

At the signing ceremony, Premier Grotewohl, on behalf of President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic, also awarded President Zápotocký of Czechoslovakia the Karl Marx Medal in recognition of his great contributions to the international workers' movement.

President Zápotocký, on the recommendation of the Czechoslovak government, awarded President Pieck the Czechoslovak Order of the White Lion, First Class, in recognition of his outstanding contributions to the development of friendly relations between Czechoslovakia and the German Democratic Republic.

### 德捷会谈联合声明签字  决定加强两国友好合作执行和平政策

1956-09-13
第5版()
专栏：

    德捷会谈联合声明签字
    决定加强两国友好合作执行和平政策
    新华社布拉格12日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社消息：德意志民主共和国政府代表团和捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团的会谈已经结束，11日举行了联合声明的签字仪式。两国间关于经济合作的议定书、关于法律援助的条约和关于社会政策方面合作的协定也同时签了字。
    捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基在签字仪式上说，这些文件肯定了两国政府为不断加强和扩大两国间友好合作的愿望和努力，并表现了它们继续执行和平共处的政策的决心。
    德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥说，会谈的结果打开了两国关系中新的阶段。两国在外交政策的一切问题上意见完全一致。两国经济合作的专业化和生产的配合以及社会主义国家五年计划的配合，已经为两国以及整个社会主义阵营的发展开辟了新的巨大的前景。
    在签字仪式上，格罗提渥总理还代表德意志民主共和国总统皮克授给捷克斯洛伐克总统萨波托斯基一枚卡尔·马克思勋章，以表彰他在国际工人运动中的巨大贡献；
    萨波托斯基总统根据捷克斯洛伐克政府的建议，授给皮克总统一枚捷克斯洛伐克一等白狮勋章，以表彰他在发展捷克斯洛伐克和民主德国之间友好关系方面的卓越贡献。
