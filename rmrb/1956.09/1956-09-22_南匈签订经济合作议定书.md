Title: South Yugoslavia and Hungary Sign Economic Cooperation Protocol
Author:
Date: 1956-09-22
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, September 20th: The Yugoslav News Agency reports that Yugoslavia and Hungary signed a protocol in Belgrade on the 19th. This protocol stipulates the scope and means for further development of South Yugoslavia and Hungary in trade and payments, industrial cooperation, infrastructure construction, transportation, water utilization, and travel.

    Special groups of experts from South Yugoslavia and Hungary have studied specific issues in various aspects of cooperation. Agreements have been reached on many issues of economic cooperation. In order to implement this protocol, negotiations on some other issues will also be held.

### 南匈签订经济合作议定书

1956-09-22
第5版()
专栏：

    南匈签订经济合作议定书
    据新华社20日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，南斯拉夫和匈牙利19日在贝尔格莱德签订一项议定书。这个议定书规定了南、匈两国在贸易和付款、工业合作、基本建设、运输、水力利用和旅行等方面进一步发展的范围和途径。
    南、匈两国专家的特别小组研究了进行合作的各个方面的具体问题。在许多经济合作的问题上已经取得了协议。为实行这个议定书，还将举行关于一些其他问题的谈判。
