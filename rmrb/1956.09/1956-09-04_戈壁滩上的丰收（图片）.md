Title: Harvest on the Gobi Desert (Picture)
Author: Huo Yan, Wu Qing
Date: 1956-09-04
Page: 4

    Harvest on the Gobi Desert
    In the autumn of 1955, a unit of the Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps of the People's Liberation Army reclaimed another 100,000 mu of wasteland in a Gobi area called "Xia Ye Di" in the Manas River Basin. In winter, the soldiers planted wheat on this reclaimed virgin land. Now, they have produced more than 31,110,000 jin of grain for the country. Left picture: Combine harvesters harvesting wheat on the Gobi Desert. Right picture: The joy of harvest.
              Photo by Huo Yan and Wu Qing (Manuscript from the PLA Pictorial)

### 戈壁滩上的丰收（图片）
火焰  武卿
1956-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    戈壁滩上的丰收
    人民解放军新疆生产部队某部于1955年秋天，在玛纳斯河流域一片叫“下野地”的戈壁滩上，又开垦了十万亩荒地。冬天，战士们在这片被开垦的处女地上种下了小麦。现在，他们为国家生产了三千一百一十多万斤粮食。左图：联合收割机在戈壁滩上收割小麦。右图：丰收的喜悦。
              火焰、武卿摄（解放军画报社稿）
