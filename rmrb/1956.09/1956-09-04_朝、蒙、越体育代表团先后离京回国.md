Title: Korean, Mongolian, and Vietnamese Sports Delegations Leave Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1956-09-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 3rd: The sports delegations from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the Mongolian People's Republic, who participated in the quadripartite sports conference of Korea, Mongolia, Vietnam, and China, left Beijing for home on August 30th, September 2nd, and September 3rd respectively, after completing their sightseeing tours in various parts of China.

Vice Premier of the State Council and Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, He Long, received the members of the sports delegations from Korea, Mongolia, and Vietnam on August 30th.

### 朝、蒙、越体育代表团先后离京回国

1956-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    朝、蒙、越体育代表团先后离京回国
    新华社3日讯　参加朝、蒙、越、中四国体育会议的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、越南民主共和国和蒙古人民共和国体育代表团，结束了在中国各地的参观游览活动以后，先后在8月30日、9月2日和9月3日离开北京回国。
    国务院副总理、体育运动委员会主任贺龙8月30日曾经接见了朝、蒙、越三国体育代表团人员。
