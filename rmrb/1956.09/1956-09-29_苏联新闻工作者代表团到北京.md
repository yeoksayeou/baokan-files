Title: Soviet Journalists Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. The 14-member Soviet Journalists Delegation, invited by the "People's Daily" to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The head of the delegation, Satyukov, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "Pravda" and member of the Auditing Commission of the CPSU Central Committee, had already arrived in Beijing to attend the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China.

At the airport, the delegation was warmly welcomed by Deng Tuo, Editor-in-Chief of the "People's Daily", and heads of various newspapers and news agencies in Beijing.

Representatives from the Soviet Embassy and Soviet journalists stationed in Beijing also went to the airport to welcome them.

This evening, Deng Tuo, Editor-in-Chief of the "People's Daily," hosted a banquet to welcome all members of the Soviet Journalists Delegation. In his speech, Deng Tuo said that we are very happy, cordial, and delighted to welcome the arrival of the Soviet Journalists Delegation after the successful conclusion of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China and on the eve of our National Day.

Satyukov, head of the delegation, said that with the development of the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people, the friendship and cooperation between our two countries' journalists has become closer and more consolidated. He said that Soviet newspapers regard reporting and introducing the situation and achievements of the Chinese people as their internationalist duty.

At today's banquet, members of the Soviet Journalists Delegation met extensively with Chinese journalists.

After the banquet, members of the delegation also participated in a garden party held by the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC.

### 苏联新闻工作者代表团到北京

1956-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联新闻工作者代表团到北京
    新华社28日讯　应“人民日报”的邀请来中国访问的苏联新闻工作者代表团团员十四人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团团长、“真理报”副总编辑、苏共中央检查委员会委员萨丘科夫因参加中国共产党第八次全国代表大会已经先到北京。
    在飞机场上，代表团人员受到“人民日报”总编辑邓拓和北京各报刊和通讯社的负责人的热烈欢迎。
    苏联大使馆的代表和驻北京的苏联记者也到机场欢迎。
    今天晚间，“人民日报”总编辑邓拓设宴欢迎苏联新闻工作者代表团全体人员。邓拓在致词中说，在中国共产党第八次全国代表大会刚刚闭幕以后和我国国庆前夕，迎接苏联新闻工作者代表团的到来，我们感到非常高兴、亲切和愉快。
    代表团团长萨丘科夫说，随着中苏两国人民的友谊的发展，我们两国新闻工作者的友谊和合作也更加密切和巩固了。他说，苏联报刊把报道和介绍中国人民的情况和成就作为自己国际主义的职责。
    在今天的宴会上，苏联新闻工作者代表团人员同中国新闻工作者广泛地见了面。
    宴会以后，代表团人员又参加中共北京市市委举行的游园晚会。
