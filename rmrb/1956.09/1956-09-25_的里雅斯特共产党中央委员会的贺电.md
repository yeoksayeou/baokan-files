    Telegram of Congratulations from the Central Committee of the Trieste Communist Party

    To the Communist Party of China, Beijing:

    The Trieste Communist Party wishes your Party Congress success, as it is a factor in new victories for the cause of peace and socialism.

          Secretary: Vida
      September 14, 1956
              Trieste
