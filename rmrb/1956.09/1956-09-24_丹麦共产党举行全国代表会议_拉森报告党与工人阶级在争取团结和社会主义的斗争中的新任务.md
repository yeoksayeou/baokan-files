Title: Danish Communist Party Holds National Congress, Larsen Reports on the Party's and the Working Class's New Tasks in the Struggle for Unity and Socialism
Author:
Date: 1956-09-24
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd dispatch from Copenhagen: The Danish Communist Party held its National Congress in Copenhagen, attended by 250 delegates and guests from Sweden, Norway, and Finland.

According to "Land and People," the organ of the Danish Communist Party, Danish Communist Party Chairman Larsen said in his opening remarks that the purpose of convening this meeting was to discuss the major issues in the international workers' movement mentioned at the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and the Danish Communist Party should adopt a positive stance on these issues. The upsurge in the domestic workers' movement has presented us with special tasks and expanded the possibilities for our work. Larsen said that the congress would discuss the opinions on the resolutions of the 20th Congress of the CPSU within the Communist Party in recent months and draw conclusions on these opinions.

The congress heard Larsen's report: "The Party's and the Working Class's New Tasks in the Struggle for Unity and Socialism."

When talking about the Danish workers' movement, Larsen especially mentioned the issue of striving for the unity of the working class, the path to the victory of socialism in Denmark, the democratic centralism of the Danish Communist Party, and the party's leading role in the workers' movement.

Discussions were held at the meeting on Larsen's report.

### 丹麦共产党举行全国代表会议  拉森报告党与工人阶级在争取团结和社会主义的斗争中的新任务

1956-09-24
第7版()
专栏：

    丹麦共产党举行全国代表会议
    拉森报告党与工人阶级在争取团结和社会主义的斗争中的新任务
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社哥本哈根22日讯：丹麦共产党全国代表会议在哥本哈根举行，出席会议的有二百五十名代表以及瑞典、挪威和芬兰的来宾。
    据丹麦共产党机关报“国土与人民报”报道，丹麦共产党主席拉森在会议开幕词中说，召开这次会议是为了讨论苏联共产党第二十次代表大会上所提到的国际工人运动中的重大问题，丹麦共产党应该对这些问题采取肯定的立场。国内工人运动的高涨向我们提出了一些特别的任务，扩大了我们工作的可能性。拉森说，代表会议将讨论共产党内最近几个月来对苏共第二十次代表大会的决议的意见，并对这些意见作出结论。
    代表会议听取了拉森的报告：“党与工人阶级在争取团结和社会主义的斗争中的新任务”。
    拉森在谈到丹麦工人运动问题的时候，特别提到争取工人阶级团结的斗争的问题，社会主义在丹麦胜利的道路的问题，丹麦共产党的民主集中制和党在工人运动中的领导地位的问题。
    会上就拉森的报告展开了讨论。
