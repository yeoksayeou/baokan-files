Title: Hungarian Minister of Foreign Trade Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-27
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th—Hungarian Minister of Foreign Trade, Bagner, and his two accompanying staff members, who are visiting China, arrived in Beijing today.

### 匈对外贸易部长到京

1956-09-27
第7版()
专栏：

    匈对外贸易部长到京
    据新华社26日讯　前来中国访问的匈牙利对外贸易部部长包格纳尔及其随员二人已于今天抵达北京。
