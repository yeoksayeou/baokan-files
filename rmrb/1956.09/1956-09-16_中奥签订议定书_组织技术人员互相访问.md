Title: China and Austria Sign Protocol to Organize Mutual Visits of Technical Personnel
Author:
Date: 1956-09-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 15th: Vienna dispatch: The Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Ji Chao Ding, and the Chairman of the Austrian Committee for East-West Trade, Dubreutzberger, representing the aforementioned two organizations respectively, signed a protocol in Vienna on the evening of September 15th regarding the organization of mutual visits by Chinese and Austrian technical personnel.

According to this protocol, the aforementioned two organizations will also assist economists, industrialists, and business people from both countries in conducting frequent mutual visits and longer-term inspections in each other's countries.

### 中奥签订议定书  组织技术人员互相访问

1956-09-16
第5版()
专栏：

    中奥签订议定书
    组织技术人员互相访问
    新华社布拉格15日电　维也纳消息：中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎和奥地利东西方贸易委员会主席杜布瑞兹柏格分别代表上述两个机构，在9月15日晚间在维也纳签定了关于组织中国和奥地利两国技术人员互相访问的议定书。
    根据这个议定书，上述两个机构并且将帮助两国经济学家和工商业者经常地进行相互访问和在对方的国家中作较长时间的考察。
