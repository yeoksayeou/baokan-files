Title: All-India Peace Council Executive Committee Meeting Passes Resolution Demanding Peaceful Resolution of the Suez Canal Issue
Author:
Date: 1956-09-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, September 3rd: The National Executive Committee meeting of the All-India Peace Council, which closed on September 2nd, passed a resolution on the Suez Canal issue, demanding that the dispute over the Suez Canal be resolved through peaceful negotiations on the basis of respecting Egyptian sovereignty. The resolution stated that the committee firmly opposes belligerent gestures and economic retaliation against Egypt, as this seriously threatens peace. The committee appealed to all relevant countries to resolve this issue in the spirit of the Bandung Conference and the Geneva Conference.

The resolution expressed disapproval of the Dulles Plan, and pointed out that the Dulles Plan infringes upon Egyptian sovereignty and therefore cannot be the basis for peaceful negotiations.

The resolution praised the efforts made by India, the Soviet Union, Indonesia, and Ceylon to seek a correct solution to this problem, and expressed agreement with India's proposal.

The September 2nd meeting was chaired by Ambarvi, a highly experienced leader of the Punjab Congress Party. At this meeting, a resolution calling for unity among Asian and African countries was also adopted. The resolution emphasized the importance of the tremendous force generated by the unity of Asian and African countries in advocating peaceful coexistence, opposing colonialism, and supporting national self-determination in eliminating the threat of war.

### 全印和平理事会执委会会议通过决议  要求和平解决苏彝士运河问题

1956-09-04
第6版()
专栏：

    全印和平理事会执委会会议通过决议
    要求和平解决苏彝士运河问题
    据新华社新德里3日电　9月2日下午闭幕的全印和平理事会全国执行委员会会议通过了关于苏彝士运河问题的决议，要求在尊重埃及主权的基础上用和平谈判的方式来解决关于苏彝士运河的争端。决议说，委员会坚决反对针对埃及的好战姿态和经济报复措施，因为这严重地威胁了和平。委员会吁请各有关国家按照万隆会议和日内瓦会议的精神来解决这个问题。
    决议表示不赞成杜勒斯计划，并且指出，杜勒斯计划是侵犯埃及主权的，因此，它不能成为和平谈判的基础。
    这个决议赞扬印度、苏联、印度尼西亚和锡兰为这个问题寻求正确的解决办法所作的努力，并且表示赞同印度的建议。
    9月2日的会议是由旁遮普国大党资历很高的领袖阿姆巴尔维主持的。在这次会议上还通过了一项吁请亚非国家团结的决议。决议强调指出亚非国家团结所产生的主张和平共处、反对殖民主义、支持民族自决的巨大力量在消除战争威胁方面的重要性。
