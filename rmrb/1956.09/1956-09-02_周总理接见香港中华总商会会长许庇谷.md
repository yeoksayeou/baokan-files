Title: Premier Zhou Receives Xu Bigu, President of the Hong Kong Chinese General Chamber of Commerce
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-02
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai received Xu Bigu, President of the Hong Kong Chinese General Chamber of Commerce, at noon today. Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Tao Zhu, and Xu Bing.

### 周总理接见香港中华总商会会长许庇谷

1956-09-02
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见香港中华总商会会长许庇谷
    新华社1日讯　国务院总理周恩来今天中午接见了香港中华总商会会长许庇谷。接见时在座的，有廖承志、陶铸、徐冰。
