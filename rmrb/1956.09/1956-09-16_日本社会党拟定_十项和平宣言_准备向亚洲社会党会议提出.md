Title: Japanese Socialist Party Drafts "Ten-Point Peace Declaration" - To Be Submitted to Asian Socialist Conference
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-16
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 14th: The Japanese Socialist Party decided today to submit a "Ten-Point Peace Declaration" to the Asian Socialist Conference to be held in Bombay in early November. The contents of this declaration are as follows:

	I. Respect the Universal Declaration of Human Rights and the purposes and principles of the United Nations Charter;
	II. Respect the right to self-determination and national independence of all nations, and the admission of all independent countries to the United Nations;
	III. Respect the sovereignty and territorial integrity of all countries;
	IV. Recognize the equality of all races and the equality of all countries;
	V. Non-interference in the internal affairs of other countries;
	VI. Non-participation in any military alliances and withdrawal of foreign troops;
	VII. Elimination of threats of aggression against other countries and prohibition of the use of force (especially atomic weapons);
	VIII. Peaceful settlement of international disputes;
	IX. Establishment of a comprehensive integrated planned economy for Asian countries to promote economic development, thereby raising the living standards of the people of Asian countries;
	X. Respect for justice and international obligations.

This declaration was decided by a joint meeting of the Socialist Party's International Bureau and the Policy Deliberation Council held today.

### 日本社会党拟定“十项和平宣言”  准备向亚洲社会党会议提出

1956-09-16
第5版()
专栏：

    日本社会党拟定“十项和平宣言”
    准备向亚洲社会党会议提出
    新华社14日讯　东京消息：日本社会党在今天决定向11月初在孟买举行的亚洲社会党会议提出一个“十项和平宣言”。这个宣言的内容如下：
    一、尊重世界人权宣言和联合国宪章的目的和原则；
    二、尊重一切民族的自决权和国家独立，一切独立国家加入联合国；
    三、尊重一切国家的主权和领土；
    四、承认一切种族平等和一切国家平等；
    五、不干涉别国内政；
    六、不参加任何军事同盟和撤退外国军队；
    七、消除对别国的侵略威胁和禁止使用武力（特别是原子武器）；
    八、和平解决国际争端；
    九、建立亚洲各国的全面的综合计划经济，促进经济开发，以此来提高亚洲各国人民的生活水平；
    十、尊重正义和国际义务。
    这个宣言是由社会党国际局和政策审议会今天举行的联席会议决定的。
