Title: The Chinese Communist Party Holds the Seventh Plenary Session of the Seventh Central Committee, Adopts Political Report and Draft Amendments to the Party Constitution, Draft Proposal for the Second Five-Year Plan, and Two Related Reports
Author:
Date: 1956-09-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 14th: The Seventh Plenary Session of the Seventh Central Committee of the Communist Party of China was held successively on August 22nd, September 8th, and September 13th. Forty-four members of the Central Committee and twenty-three alternate members of the Central Committee were present. Responsible comrades from departments directly under the CPC Central Committee, central state organs, and the first secretaries of the Party committees of provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions attended the meeting as observers. The plenary session conducted a detailed discussion on various preparatory tasks for the upcoming Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China. The meeting reviewed and adopted the political report to be submitted to the Eighth National Congress by the Seventh Central Committee, the draft amendments to the Party Constitution and the report on amending the Party Constitution, and the draft proposal for the Second Five-Year Plan for National Economic Development and the report on the proposal for the Second Five-Year Plan for National Economic Development. The plenary session also adopted the draft list of the Presidium of the Congress, the list of the Secretariat, and the list of the Credentials Committee of the Congress, which are to be submitted to the Congress.

### 中国共产党举行七届七中全会  通过政治报告和党章修改草案、第二个五年计划建议草案以及有关的两个报告

1956-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中国共产党举行七届七中全会
    通过政治报告和党章修改草案、第二个五年计划建议草案以及有关的两个报告
    新华社14日讯　中国共产党第七届中央委员会第七次全体会议，在8月22日、9月8日、9月13日连续举行会议，到会的有中央委员四十四人，候补中央委员二十三人。列席会议的有中共中央直属机关、中央国家机关各部门的负责同志和各省市、自治区党委第一书记。全会为即将召开的中国共产党第八次全国代表大会的各项准备工作，进行了详细的讨论。会议审查和通过了第七届中央委员会准备向第八次全国代表大会提出的政治报告、党的章程的修改草案和关于修改党的章程的报告、关于发展国民经济的第二个五年计划的建议草案和关于发展国民经济第二个五年计划的建议的报告。全会还通过了准备向代表大会提出的大会主席团名单、秘书处名单和大会代表资格审查委员会名单的草案。
