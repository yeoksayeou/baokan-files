Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives the Ceylon Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-09-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th -- Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, received the Ceylon Government Delegation headed by C. Corea on the afternoon of the 10th.

Those present at the reception included Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Chen Shuliang, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department. (Photo attached)

A group photo of Premier Zhou Enlai and the Ceylon Government Delegation. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Liu Qingrui.

### 周总理接见锡兰政府代表团

1956-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见锡兰政府代表团
    新华社10日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来10日下午接见了以克·科里亚为首的锡兰政府代表团。
    接见的时候，在座的有外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，外交部亚洲司副司长陈叔亮，礼宾司副司长马振武。（附图片）
    周恩来总理和锡兰政府代表团合影。　新华社记者　刘庆瑞摄
