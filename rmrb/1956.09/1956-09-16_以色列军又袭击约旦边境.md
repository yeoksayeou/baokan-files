Title: Israeli Troops Again Attack Jordanian Border
Author:
Date: 1956-09-16
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Amman, September 15th: A Jordanian military spokesman said on September 14th that on the evening of September 13th, an Israeli force of about 1,000 men, equipped with heavy weapons and supported by three military aircraft, launched a large-scale attack on the Jordanian outpost of Ghardandal in southern Jordan.

This is the second large-scale attack by Israel on Jordanian border outposts in two days. On September 11th, the Jordanian outpost of Rawah, nearly one mile inside Jordanian territory, was attacked by Israeli forces.

Jordan immediately filed an urgent complaint with the United Nations Truce Organization, and United Nations truce observers have flown to the scene of the attack.

### 以色列军又袭击约旦边境

1956-09-16
第6版()
专栏：

    以色列军又袭击约旦边境
    据新华社15日讯　安曼消息：约旦军方发言人9月14日说，一支人数在一千左右的以色列部队装备着重型武器并有三架军用机支援，在9月13日晚上对约旦南部的加兰达尔据点发动了大规模的袭击。
    这是以色列在两天内对约旦边境据点发动的第二次大规模的袭击。9月11日，在约旦境内将近一英里的拉瓦的约旦据点受到以色列军队的攻击。
    约旦立刻向联合国停战组织提出了紧急控诉，联合国停战观察员们已经飞往发动攻击的地点。
