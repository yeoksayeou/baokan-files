Title: Adenauer Regime Increasingly Isolated, Free Democratic Party Chairman Refuses to Rejoin Government Coalition, Bavarian Party Chairman Criticizes Rearmament Policy
Author:
Date: 1956-09-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 21st September: Bonn Dispatch: The Free Democratic Party, which withdrew from the West German government coalition in February of this year, stated again on September 20th that it is unwilling to return to the government coalition led by Adenauer's Christian Democratic Union.

The "Free Democratic Party Press Release" announced that the party's chairman, Dehler, had informed West German Chancellor Adenauer by letter, refusing to hold a second meeting with him on September 20th.

Dehler had held a meeting with Adenauer on September 14th. It is reported that during this meeting, Adenauer explained to Dehler the difficulties he was encountering within the Christian Democratic Union and expressed his willingness to give the Free Democratic Party four ministerial seats in the West German government, on the condition that the Free Democratic Party rejoin the government coalition and reorganize the North Rhine-Westphalia state government and the Bavarian state government, both of which have Social Democrats as their premiers. However, the Free Democratic Party rejected Adenauer's proposal.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 21st September: The Vice-Premier of the State of Bavaria, West Germany, and Chairman of the Bavarian Party, Baumgartner, said in a radio address in Munich on September 19th: "The Bavarian Party opposes a federal government that returns our youth to the SS officers of the Nazi Party."

Baumgartner pointed out that the history of Germany shows that "it is dangerous to hand over all decision-making power to an arbitrary party in the entire rearmament and reconstruction of the Bundeswehr issue."

Baumgartner also expressed opposition to Adenauer's attempt to use the "restriction clause" of the election law to stifle small parties.

### 阿登纳政权越来越孤立  自由民主党主席拒绝重返政府联盟  巴伐利亚党主席指责重新武装政策

1956-09-22
第6版()
专栏：

    阿登纳政权越来越孤立
    自由民主党主席拒绝重返政府联盟
    巴伐利亚党主席指责重新武装政策
    据新华社柏林21日电　波恩消息：今年2月间退出了西德政府联盟的自由民主党在9月20日再次表示，它不愿意回到阿登纳的基督教民主联盟领导的政府联盟中。
    “自由民主党新闻公报”宣布，这个党的主席戴勒已经用书信通知西德总理阿登纳，拒绝同他在9月20日举行第二次会晤。
    戴勒在9月14日曾经同阿登纳举行了一次会谈。据悉，在这次会谈中，阿登纳向戴勒说明了他自己在基督教民主联盟内部所遇到的困难，并且表示愿意把西德政府中的四个部长席位给予自由民主党，条件是自由民主党重新参加政府联盟以及改组由社会民主党人担任总理的北莱因—威斯特伐利亚州政府和巴伐利亚州政府。但是，自由民主党拒绝了阿登纳的建议。
    据新华社柏林21日电　西德巴伐利亚州副总理、巴伐利亚党主席鲍姆加特纳9月19日在慕尼黑发表广播演说的时候说：“巴伐利亚党反对一个把我们的青年重新交给纳粹党卫军军官们的联邦政府。”
    鲍姆加特纳指出，德国过去的历史表明，“在整个的重新武装和重建国防军的问题上把一切决定权都交给一个独断独行的党”是危险的。
    鲍姆加特纳还表示反对阿登纳企图利用选举法的“限制条款”来扼杀各个小党派的作法。
