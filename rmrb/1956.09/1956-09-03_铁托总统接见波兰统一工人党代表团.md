Title: President Tito Receives Delegation of Polish United Workers' Party
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-03
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency on the 1st, the President of Yugoslavia and General Secretary of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, Tito, received the delegation of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party on the island of Brioni. Tito held talks with the delegation and hosted them for lunch.

### 铁托总统接见波兰统一工人党代表团

1956-09-03
第5版()
专栏：

    铁托总统接见波兰统一工人党代表团
    新华社2日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社1日报道，南斯拉夫总统、南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟总书记铁托在布里俄尼岛接见了波兰统一工人党中央委员会代表团。铁托同代表团人员进行了谈话，并且招待他们共进午餐。
