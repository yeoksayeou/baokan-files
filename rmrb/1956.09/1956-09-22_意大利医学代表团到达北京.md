Title: Italian Medical Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 21st. An eight-member Italian medical delegation composed of prominent Italian medical experts arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Representatives from the Chinese Medical Association and Chinese medical experts warmly welcomed the delegation at the airport.

The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese Medical Association. Professor Motura, Dean of the Institute of Physiology and Organic Pathology at the University of Turin, is the head of the delegation.

### 意大利医学代表团到达北京

1956-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    意大利医学代表团到达北京
    新华社21日讯　由意大利著名医学家组成的意大利医学代表团一行八人，今天下午坐飞机到达北京。代表团人员在飞机场上受到中华医学会的代表和中国医学专家们的热烈欢迎。
    代表团是应中华医学会邀请来中国访问的，都灵大学生理学和有机病理学院院长莫土腊教授担任代表团团长。
