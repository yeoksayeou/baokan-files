Title: Ma Hanbing Visits Kabul Mayor
Author:
Date: 1956-09-05
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, September 4th. Ma Hanbing, head of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, and some members of the delegation paid a courtesy call on Kabul Mayor Mohammed Arif Sohar. Ma Hanbing expressed his gratitude to the mayor for the warm welcome the delegation received from the municipal government and the citizens of Kabul.

### 马寒冰拜访喀布尔市长

1956-09-05
第5版()
专栏：

    马寒冰拜访喀布尔市长
    据新华社喀布尔4日电　中国文化艺术代表团团长马寒冰和代表团的部分团员对喀布尔市长穆罕默德·阿里夫·苏哈耳作了礼节上的拜访。马寒冰对于代表团在喀布尔受到市政府和市民的热情欢迎向市长表示感谢。
