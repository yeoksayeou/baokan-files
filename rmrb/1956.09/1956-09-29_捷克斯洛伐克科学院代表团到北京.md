Title: Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. The delegation from the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences, which is visiting China under the Sino-Czechoslovak Cultural Cooperation Agreement, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The head of the delegation is Academician A. Prusek, Member of the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences and Director of the Oriental Institute of the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences.

The delegation was welcomed at the airport by Pan Zinian, Deputy Director of the Department of Philosophy and Social Sciences of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Chinese scientists. Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Grigor, also came to welcome the delegation.

### 捷克斯洛伐克科学院代表团到北京

1956-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克科学院代表团到北京
    新华社28日讯　根据中捷文化合作协定来中国访问的捷克斯洛伐克科学院代表团一行五人今天下午坐飞机到达北京。代表团团长是捷克斯洛伐克科学院院士、捷克斯洛伐克科学院东方研究所所长亚·普实克。
    代表团人员在飞机场上受到中国科学院哲学社会科学部副主任潘梓年和中国科学家们的欢迎。捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使格里哥尔也来欢迎代表团。
