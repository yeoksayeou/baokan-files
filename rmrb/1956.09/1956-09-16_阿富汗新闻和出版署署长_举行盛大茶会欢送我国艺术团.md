Title: Afghan Director of News and Publications Hosts Grand Tea Party to Bid Farewell to Chinese Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1956-09-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, September 15th: The Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation concluded its friendly visit to Afghanistan on September 14th and departed from Kabul by plane. On the eve of their departure, Qasim Rishtia, the Director of the Afghan News and Publications Department, held a grand tea party to bid farewell to the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation and the Soviet Art Delegation.

Rishtia presented gifts on behalf of Afghan King Zahir Shah to Ma Hanbing, the head of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation. He also presented gifts on behalf of former Prime Minister Marshal Shah Mahmud to Ma Hanbing and Wang Hanmin, the head of the Chinese Exhibition Delegation.

On September 12th, Ma Hanbing visited Ali Ahmad Popal, the acting Minister of Education of Afghanistan, Qasim Rishtia, the Director of the News and Publications Department, and Abdul Sattar Shalizai, the Deputy Director, to bid them farewell. Ma Hanbing presented gifts to the News and Publications Department on behalf of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation. The gifts included Chinese national musical instruments, books, sheet music, and records.

### 阿富汗新闻和出版署署长  举行盛大茶会欢送我国艺术团

1956-09-16
第5版()
专栏：

    阿富汗新闻和出版署署长
    举行盛大茶会欢送我国艺术团
    据新华社喀布尔15日电　中国文化艺术代表团9月14日结束了在阿富汗的友好访问，乘飞机离开喀布尔。在动身前夕，阿富汗新闻和出版署署长卡西姆·里什提亚曾经举行盛大茶会欢送中国文化艺术代表团和苏联艺术代表团。
    里什提亚代表阿富汗国王查希尔·沙阿向中国文化艺术代表团团长马寒冰赠送了礼物。他还代表前首相沙阿·马茂德元帅向马寒冰和中国展览团团长王汉民赠送礼物。
    马寒冰在9月12日访问了阿富汗教育部代理大臣阿里·艾哈迈德·波帕尔、新闻和出版署署长卡西姆·里什提亚和副署长阿卜杜勒·萨塔尔·沙利扎伊，向他们告别。马寒冰代表中国文化艺术代表团向新闻和出版署赠送了礼物。礼物中包括中国的民族乐器、书籍、乐谱和唱片。
