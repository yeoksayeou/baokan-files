Title: World Federation of United Nations Associations Meets to Discuss Activities of the Past Decade
Author: 
Date: 1956-09-06
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, September 3rd: The 11th Plenary Assembly of the World Federation of United Nations Associations opened on the evening of September 2nd at the Palais des Nations in Geneva. The meeting will discuss issues related to the activities of the Federation and the United Nations over the past decade.
    Representatives from more than fifty countries are participating in the meeting.
    At the invitation of the World Federation of United Nations Associations, Zhou Gengsheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, and Wen Jianping, Deputy Secretary-General, are attending the meeting as observers.

### 联合国协会世界联合会开会  讨论近十年来的活动

1956-09-06
第5版()
专栏：

    联合国协会世界联合会开会
    讨论近十年来的活动
    新华社3日讯　日内瓦消息：联合国协会世界联合会第十一次会议9月2日晚在日内瓦国联大厦开幕。会议将讨论有关联合会和联合国最近十年来的活动的一些问题。
    参加会议的有五十多个国家的代表。
    中国人民外交协会副会长周鲠生、秘书长吴茂荪和副秘书长温建平应联合国协会世界联合会邀请，以观察员身份参加了会议。
