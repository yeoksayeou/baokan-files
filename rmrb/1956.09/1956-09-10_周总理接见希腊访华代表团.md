Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Greek Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1956-09-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, received the head of the Greek delegation to China, Kitsikis, and all members of the delegation at 2:30 pm today.

Present at the meeting were Zhao Yimin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. (Photo attached)

A group photo of Premier Zhou Enlai and the Greek delegation to China. The fourth person from the right is the head of the delegation, Kitsikis. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zheng Xiaozhen

### 周总理接见希腊访华代表团

1956-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见希腊访华代表团
    新华社9日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，今天下午2时30分接见了希腊访华代表团团长基特西基斯和全体团员。
    接见的时候，在座的有中国人民对外文化协会副会长赵毅敏，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华。（附图片）
    周恩来总理和希腊访华代表团的合影。右起第四人是代表团团长基特西基斯。　新华社记者　郑小箴摄
