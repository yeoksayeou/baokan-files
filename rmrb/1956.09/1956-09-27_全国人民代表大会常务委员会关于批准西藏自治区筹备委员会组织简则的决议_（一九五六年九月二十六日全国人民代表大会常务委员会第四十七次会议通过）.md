Title: Resolution of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on Approving the Organizational Regulations of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region (Passed at the 47th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on September 26, 1956)
Author:
Date: 1956-09-27
Page: 1

The 47th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress resolved on September 26, 1956: To approve the Organizational Regulations of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region formulated by the First Meeting of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region.

第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于批准西藏自治区筹备委员会组织简则的决议
    （一九五六年九月二十六日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第四十七次会议通过）
