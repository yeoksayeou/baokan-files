Title: China and Czechoslovakia Sign Scientific and Technological Cooperation Protocol
Author:
Date: 1956-09-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 6th: The Sino-Czechoslovak Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation held its fourth meeting in Prague from September 3rd to 5th, and signed the Sino-Czechoslovak Scientific and Technological Cooperation Protocol. Based on the common requirements of both parties, a resolution was passed to establish direct connections between the corresponding ministries, design institutions, and scientific research institutions of the two countries to promote scientific and technological cooperation.
    According to the Sino-Czechoslovak Scientific and Technological Cooperation Protocol, the People's Republic of China will provide Czechoslovakia with technical data related to coal mining, paper making, and other light industries. Czechoslovakia will provide China with technical data related to machine manufacturing, power industry, chemical industry, and other sectors, and will dispatch a large number of hydropower station construction experts to China to assist in construction work.
    Vice Minister of the Ministry of Electric Power Industry of the People's Republic of China, Wang Lin, and Minister of Power Industry of Czechoslovakia, Dr. Flašař, signed the protocol on behalf of their respective governments. After the signing of the protocol, Dr. Flašař held a banquet to entertain the Chinese delegation.
    The Chinese delegation will leave Prague on the afternoon of the 6th to return home.

### 中捷签订科学技术合作议定书

1956-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中捷签订科学技术合作议定书
    新华社布拉格6日电　中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会9月3日到5日在布拉格举行第四届会议，签订了中捷科学技术合作议定书，并且根据双方的共同要求通过一项决议，决定建立两国相应的部、设计机构及科学研究机构之间的直接联系，以促进两国的科学技术合作。
    根据中捷科学技术合作议定书的规定，中华人民共和国将供给捷克斯洛伐克有关煤矿工业、造纸工业和其它轻工业等方面的技术资料，捷克斯洛伐克将供给中国有关机器制造工业、动力工业、化学工业等部门的技术资料，并且派遣大批水电站建设专家到中国帮助建设工作。
    代表双方政府在这项议定书上签字的是中华人民共和国电力工业部副部长王林和捷克斯洛伐克动力工业部部长弗拉沙克博士。议定书签字以后弗拉沙克博士举行了宴会，招待中国代表团。
    中国代表团将于6日下午离布拉格回国。
