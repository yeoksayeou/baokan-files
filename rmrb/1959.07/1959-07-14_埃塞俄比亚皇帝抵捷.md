Title: Ethiopian Emperor Arrives in Czechoslovakia
Author: 
Date: 1959-07-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 13th: Ethiopian Emperor Haile Selassie I and his entourage flew to Prague this morning for a five-day state visit at the invitation of the President of Czechoslovakia.

Those who greeted him at the airport included: Czechoslovak President Novotny, First Deputy Prime Minister Ďuranský, and Chairman of the Czechoslovak National Assembly Zdeněk Fierlinger, among others.

After arriving, Emperor Selassie I visited President Novotny and attended a luncheon hosted by the President.

Xinhua News Agency, July 13th: According to TASS, Moscow: Ethiopian Emperor Haile Selassie I left here this morning aboard a "Tu-104" special plane for Prague.

Those who went to the airport to see him off included: Voroshilov, Ignatov, Kuusinen, Mikoyan, Khrushchev and other Soviet Party and government leaders, as well as ministers of various Soviet departments and representatives of the Supreme Soviet.

Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Voroshilov, delivered a farewell speech at the airport. Selassie I also spoke at the airport.

### 埃塞俄比亚皇帝抵捷

1959-07-14
第4版()
专栏：

    埃塞俄比亚皇帝抵捷
    新华社布拉格13日电　埃塞俄比亚皇帝海尔·塞拉西一世及其随员于今天上午飞抵布拉格，他应捷克斯洛伐克总统的邀请，将在这里进行为时五天的国事访问。
    到机场迎接的有：捷克斯洛伐克总统诺沃提尼，第一副总理杜兰斯基、捷克斯洛伐克国民会议主席兹德尼克·费林格等。
    塞拉西一世皇帝在到达后拜访了诺沃提尼总统，并且出席了总统招待午宴。
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：埃塞俄比亚皇帝海尔·塞拉西一世13日晨乘“图—一○四”专机离开此地，前往布拉格。
    前往机场欢送的有：伏罗希洛夫、伊格纳托夫、库西宁、米高扬、赫鲁晓夫等苏联党政领导人以及苏联各部部长、最高苏维埃代表等。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫在机场上致了欢送词。塞拉西一世也在机场上讲了话。
