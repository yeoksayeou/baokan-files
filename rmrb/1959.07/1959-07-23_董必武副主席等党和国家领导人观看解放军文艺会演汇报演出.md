Title: Vice Chairman Dong Biwu and Other Party and State Leaders Watch the PLA Art Performance Reporting Performance
Author:
Date: 1959-07-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd: The Second Literary and Art Performance Conference of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held a reporting performance tonight at Huairen Hall in Zhongnanhai.

Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairmen Li Jishen and Guo Moruo, and other party and state leaders attended and watched tonight's performance. Also attending tonight's performance were responsible persons from various central departments and senior generals from various services and arms of the People's Liberation Army.

The programs performed tonight were excellent programs from professional art troupes and amateur performance teams during the second stage of this literary and art performance. Among them were the dance "Morning on the East Sea," depicting the People's Liberation Army protecting the people's production and military life, the cross-talk "Ten Years on the Island," the sculpture performance "The Good Eighth Company on Nanjing Road," the instrumental performance "Battlefield Music" showing the heroic spirit and high revolutionary optimism of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, the poem recitation "Under the Old Pear Tree" praising the profound friendship between the Chinese and Korean people, solo performances by female and male voices, as well as folk songs, mountain songs, dances, and a banhu solo. These programs were warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 董必武副主席等党和国家领导人观看解放军文艺会演汇报演出

1959-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    董必武副主席等党和国家领导人观看解放军文艺会演汇报演出
    新华社22日讯　中国人民解放军第二届文艺会演大会，今晚在中南海怀仁堂举行汇报演出。
    董必武副主席、陈毅副总理、李济深、郭沫若副委员长等党和国家领导人，出席观看了今晚的演出。出席观看今晚演出的还有中央各部门负责人和人民解放军各军种、各兵种部队的高级将领。
    今晚演出的节目，是这届文艺会演第二阶段演出中的专业文工团和业余演出队的优秀节目。其中有描写人民解放军保卫人民生产和部队生活的舞蹈“东海的早晨”、对口词“进岛十年”、塑像表演“南京路上好八连”，有表现志愿军英雄气概和高度的革命乐观主义精神的器乐演奏“战地音乐”，歌颂中朝人民深厚友谊的诗朗诵“老梨树下”，有女声、男声独唱，还有民歌、山歌、舞蹈、板胡独奏等。这些节目都受到观众的热烈欢迎。
