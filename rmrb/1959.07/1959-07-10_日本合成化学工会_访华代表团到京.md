Title: Japan Synthetic Chemical Workers' Union Delegation Visits Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-07-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, July 9th: The Japanese Synthetic Chemical Workers' Union Delegation, a group of six led by the head of the delegation, Kaoru Saruwatari, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the National Committee of the China Heavy Industry Trade Union.

### 日本合成化学工会  访华代表团到京

1959-07-10
第4版()
专栏：

    日本合成化学工会
    访华代表团到京
    新华社9日讯　日本合成化学工会访华代表团一行六人，在团长猿渡馨率领下今天乘飞机到达北京。代表团是应中国重工业工会全国委员会邀请来我国访问的。
