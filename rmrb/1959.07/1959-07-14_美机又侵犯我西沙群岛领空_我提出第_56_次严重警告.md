Title: U.S. Plane Again Intrudes into Airspace Over Xisha Islands; We Issue 56th Serious Warning
Author: 
Date: 1959-07-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 13th: At 10:35 on July 13th, a U.S. Navy patrol plane intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island and North Island of the Xisha Islands in Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was ordered to issue the 56th serious warning.

### 美机又侵犯我西沙群岛领空  我提出第56次严重警告

1959-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

    美机又侵犯我西沙群岛领空
    我提出第56次严重警告
    新华社13日讯　7月13日10时35分，美国海军巡逻机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、北岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第五十六次严重警告。
