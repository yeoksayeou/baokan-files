Title: Jia Chengbin, Miao Ming
Author: Jia Chengbin, Miao Ming
Date: 1959-07-22
Page: 7

    Dalian City has launched a mass medicinal herb collecting campaign. In the first battle, they collected more than 40,000 catties of over 70 kinds of medicinal materials, including Scutellaria baicalensis, Bupleurum, Glehnia littoralis, Trichosanthes kirilowii, and white peony. These are Yi Yangchu (right) and Chen Wenxian (left), who actively participated in the herb collecting campaign, sorting out the various precious medicinal materials they collected. Xinhua News Agency reporter Jia Chengbin and Miao Ming photographed.
