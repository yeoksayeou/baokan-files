Title: President Sukarno Accepts Resignation of Djuanda Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1959-07-07
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 6th: The Indonesian Djuanda Cabinet decided to resign after holding its last meeting on the evening of the 5th. President Sukarno accepted the resignation of the Djuanda Cabinet on the morning of the 6th. Sukarno said that the new cabinet will be formed as soon as possible. In a brief speech, Sukarno expressed his gratitude to Djuanda and his cabinet. He said that the resignation of the Djuanda Cabinet was due to the restoration of the 1945 Constitution, as his cabinet was formed under the Provisional Constitution. He requested the resigned Djuanda Cabinet to continue its work until the new cabinet is formed.

### 苏加诺总统接受朱安达内阁辞职

1959-07-07
第5版()
专栏：

    苏加诺总统接受朱安达内阁辞职
    据新华社雅加达6日电　印度尼西亚朱安达内阁在5日晚上举行了最后一次会议后，决定提出辞职。苏加诺总统6日上午接受了朱安达内阁的辞呈。苏加诺说，新内阁将尽快成立。苏加诺在简短的讲话中对朱安达和他的内阁表示感谢。他说，朱安达内阁的辞职是由于恢复了1945年宪法，因为他的内阁是根据临时宪法成立的。他要求辞职了的朱安达内阁在新内阁尚未成立以前继续担任工作。
