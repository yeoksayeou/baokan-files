Title: Table Tennis Tournament Concludes: Chinese Athletes Win Four Championships
Author:
Date: 1959-07-30
Page: 4

The Seventh World Youth Festival Table Tennis Tournament concluded on the evening of the 28th, and the awards ceremony was held.

Chinese athletes achieved great success in this competition. Hu Keming won the Women's Singles Championship, Zhuang Zedong and Li Furong won the Men's Doubles Championship, Zhuang Zedong and Hu Keming won the Mixed Doubles Championship, and Hu Keming and the fourteen-year-old Czechoslovakian athlete Bosa won the Women's Doubles Championship. Li Furong won the Men's Singles Silver Medal. The Men's Singles Championship was won by Czechoslovakian athlete Miko. Hu Keming played bravely and excellently, winning three gold medals. Her powerful smash was highly praised by the audience. The famous Czechoslovakian long-distance runner Zatopek watched today's competition and participated in the awards ceremony. His appearance was welcomed by the entire audience.

### 乒乓球赛结束  我国选手获四项冠军

1959-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

    乒乓球赛结束
    我国选手获四项冠军
    第七届世界青年联欢节乒乓球赛28日晚上已经结束，并且举行了授奖仪式。
    中国运动员在这次比赛中取得很大胜利，胡克明获女子单打冠军，庄则栋、李富荣获男子双打冠军，庄则栋、胡克明获男女混合双打冠军，胡克明和捷克斯洛伐克十四岁的运动员波莎获女子双打冠军，李富荣获男子单打亚军。男子单打冠军为捷克斯洛伐克运动员米科获得。胡克明打得勇猛出色，获得了三枚金质奖章，她的大力扣杀很受观众称赞。捷克斯洛伐克著名长跑运动员查托皮克观看了今天的比赛，并参加了授奖仪式，他的出现受到全场观众的欢迎。
