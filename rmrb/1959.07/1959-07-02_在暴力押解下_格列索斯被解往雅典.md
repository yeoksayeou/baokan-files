Title: Under Violent Escort, Glezos is Sent to Athens
Author:
Date: 1959-07-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Athens: According to Greek newspapers, Greek national hero Manolis Glezos was violently escorted from Crete prison to Piraeus on June 30th and will be transferred from there to Athens prison. "Independent News" pointed out that Glezos will be handed over to a military tribunal for trial, which will be held before July 15th at the Athens Military Tribunal. (Attached picture)
    The picture shows Glezos righteously and sternly denouncing the enemy in court in 1954.

### 在暴力押解下  格列索斯被解往雅典

1959-07-02
第5版()
专栏：

    在暴力押解下
    格列索斯被解往雅典
    新华社1日讯　据塔斯社雅典讯：据希腊报纸报道，希腊民族英雄格列索斯6月30日在暴力押解下被从克里特监狱解往比里犹斯，并将从那里再解往雅典监狱。“独立新闻报”指出，格列索斯将被交付军事法庭审讯，这个审讯将在7月15日以前在雅典军事法庭进行。（附图片）
    图为1954年格列索斯在法庭上义正词严地痛斥敌人
