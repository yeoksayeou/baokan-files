Title: Nursery Rhyme
Author: Xue Bing
Date: 1959-07-09
Page: 8
　　
　　·Little Bamboo Pole·
　　Little bamboo pole, three feet eight,
　　Two colored ribbons hang on the pole.
　　Going to the fields to look at the crops, it is my horse;
　　Uncle comes to the threshing ground, taking it to chase chickens and ducks.
　　My little bamboo pole, ah, can carry a pot of tea on one end.
　　Delivering cool tea to the fields, uncles and aunts praise me.

  ·小竹竿·小竹竿，三尺八，两条彩绸竿上挂。下地看庄稼，它是我的马；叔叔来晒场，拿它撵鸡鸭。我的小竹竿呵，一头能挑一壶茶。凉茶送到地头上，叔叔姨姨把我夸。
