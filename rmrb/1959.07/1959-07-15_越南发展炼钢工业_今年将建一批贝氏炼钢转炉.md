Title: Vietnam Develops Steel Industry: A Batch of Bessemer Converters to be Built This Year
Author:
Date: 1959-07-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 14th: According to a report by the Vietnam News Agency today, all industrial enterprises in Vietnam will each build a Bessemer converter before the end of this year. This is a decision made by the Vietnamese Ministry of Industry to meet the growing need for steel from the developing state-owned and local industries.

The Vietnamese State Science Committee recently held a meeting to exchange experiences in steelmaking using Bessemer converters.

An enterprise manufacturing machines under the General Logistics Department of the Vietnamese People's Army gave a report at the meeting. The report stated that the workers of this enterprise successfully manufactured such a furnace using local materials, which can produce 150 to 200 kilograms of steel in twelve to fifteen minutes.

### 越南发展炼钢工业  今年将建一批贝氏炼钢转炉

1959-07-15
第4版()
专栏：

    越南发展炼钢工业
    今年将建一批贝氏炼钢转炉
    新华社河内14日电　据越南通讯社今天报道：越南的所有工业企业在今年年底以前将各建一座贝氏炼钢转炉。这是越南工业部为了满足正在发展中的国营工业和地方工业对钢的不断增长的需要，作出的决定。
    越南国家科学委员会在最近召开的一次会议上交流了用贝氏转炉炼钢的经验。
    越南人民军总后勤部一个制造机器的企业在会上作了报告。报告说，这个企业的工人成功地用当地的材料制成了一座这样的炉子，在十二到十五分钟内可以生产一百五十到二百公斤的钢。
