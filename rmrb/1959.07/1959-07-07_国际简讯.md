International Briefs

    Tokyo Overseas Chinese Association Holds General Meeting
    Tokyo Dispatch: The Tokyo Overseas Chinese Association held its 1959 annual general meeting on the 5th. More than two thousand overseas Chinese residing in Tokyo and neighboring cities and towns participated in the meeting.
    Singapore Bans Films Promoting Colonialism
    Singapore Dispatch: The Singapore government issued a directive on the 4th, completely banning the screening in Singapore of films "whose main intention is to glorify colonialism and defend colonialism." The directive stated that the ban also applies to films whose purpose is "to generally subject Asians and so-called colored people to contempt, ridicule, and disgust."

### 国际简讯

专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    东京华侨总会举行会员大会
    东京讯　东京华侨总会于5日举行1959年度会员大会。旅居东京和邻近城乡的华侨两千多人参加了大会。
　　新加坡禁映宣扬殖民主义影片
    新加坡讯　新加坡政府在4日发布一项指令，完全禁止在新加坡放映“主要意图是歌颂殖民主义并且为殖民主义进行辩护的”影片。指令中说，禁令还适用于那些目的
  “在于使亚洲人和所谓有色人普遍地受到轻视、愚弄和嫌恶”的影片。
