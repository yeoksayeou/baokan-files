Title: Chen Yi Receives Haitian and Brazilian Writers
Author:
Date: 1959-07-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 7th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received Haitian writer Alexis and Brazilian writer Moraes, who are currently visiting China, this afternoon. Chu Tunan and Wang Yafan were present.

### 陈毅副总理接见海地和巴西作家

1959-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见海地和巴西作家
    新华社7日讯　陈毅副总理在今天下午接见了正在我国访问的海地作家阿列克西斯和巴西女作家莫拉依斯。在座的有楚图南和王亚凡。
