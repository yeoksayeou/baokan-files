Title: Further Developing Soviet-Ethiopian Friendly Relations: Ethiopian Emperor Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1959-07-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 29th - Emperor Haile Selassie I of the Kingdom of Ethiopia and his entourage arrived in Moscow this afternoon aboard a "Tu-104" special plane for an official visit.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Soviet Party and state leaders Voroshilov, Khrushchev, Mikoyan, etc., as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in the Soviet Union and representatives from all walks of life in Moscow.

After Emperor Haile Selassie I disembarked from the plane, he accepted flowers presented by Young Pioneers and shook hands with the Soviet leaders who had come to greet him. Accompanied by Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Selassie I reviewed the guard of honor. Voroshilov spoke at the welcoming ceremony held at the airport. On behalf of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and the Soviet government, he extended a heartfelt welcome to Selassie I for his visit to the Soviet Union. He said that there are good relations between the Soviet Union and the Kingdom of Ethiopia, and Selassie I's visit to the Soviet Union will surely promote the further development of Soviet-Ethiopian friendly relations.

Selassie I replied that we are very honored to be visiting the Soviet Union for the first time. We hope to learn about the progress of the Soviet Union in all aspects through this visit, and we believe that this visit will contribute to the development of friendship between Ethiopia and the Soviet Union.

### 进一步发展苏埃友好关系  埃塞俄比亚皇帝到莫斯科

1959-07-01
第4版()
专栏：

    进一步发展苏埃友好关系
    埃塞俄比亚皇帝到莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科29日电　埃塞俄比亚王国皇帝海尔·塞拉西一世及其随行官员今日下午乘“图—104”专机到达莫斯科作正式的访问。
    前往机场欢迎的有苏联党和国家领导人伏罗希洛夫、赫鲁晓夫、米高扬等以及各国驻苏联的外交使节和莫斯科各界代表。
    海尔·塞拉西一世下飞机后接受了少先队员的献花并和前往迎接的苏联领导人一一握手。在苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫的陪同下，塞拉西一世检阅了仪仗队。伏罗希洛夫在机场上举行的欢迎会上讲了话。他代表苏联最高苏维埃和苏联政府衷心欢迎塞拉西一世到苏联访问。他说，苏联和埃塞俄比亚王国间有着良好的关系，塞拉西一世访问苏联一定会促进苏埃友好关系的进一步发展。
    塞拉西一世致答词说，我们第一次到苏联来进行访问，感到非常荣幸。我们希望通过这次访问了解苏联各方面的进步，并且相信我们这次访问将为发展埃苏两国友谊作出贡献。
