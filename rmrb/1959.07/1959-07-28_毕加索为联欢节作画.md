Title: Picasso Paints for the Festival
Author: 
Date: 1959-07-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, July 27th: The famous French painter Picasso has created a painting as a gift for the 7th World Youth and Student Festival for Peace and Friendship. The painting depicts a group of young men and women in festive attire, hand-in-hand, joyfully dancing around the Earth. This giant painting has already been erected in the center of Vienna's Heldenplatz (Heroes' Square), where young people will hold a grand parade for peace and friendship on August 1st.

### 毕加索为联欢节作画

1959-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    毕加索为联欢节作画
    据新华社维也纳27日电　著名的法国画家毕加索作画赠给第七届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节，画的是一群身着节日盛装的青年男女，手搀着手，欢乐地围绕着地球跳舞。这幅巨画已经竖立在维也纳英雄广场的中心，青年们将在8月1日在这个广场举行和平和友谊的盛大游行。
