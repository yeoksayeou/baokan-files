Title: Comrades Peng Zhen, Luo Ronghuan, Chen Yi, and Others Receive Representatives of the PLA Literary and Art Performance Conference

Author:
Date: 1959-07-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: Comrades Peng Zhen, Luo Ronghuan, Chen Yi, Bo Yibo, Wang Jiaxiang, Huang Kecheng, Liu Lantao, Zhang Zongxun, Xiao Hua, Chen Shiju, and others received the second group of representatives attending the Second Literary and Art Performance Conference of the Chinese People's Liberation Army this morning.

### 彭真罗荣桓陈毅等同志  接见解放军文艺会演大会代表

1959-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真罗荣桓陈毅等同志
    接见解放军文艺会演大会代表
    新华社16日讯　彭真、罗荣桓、陈毅、薄一波、王稼祥、黄克诚、刘澜涛、张宗逊、萧华、陈士榘等同志，在今天上午接见了参加中国人民解放军第二届文艺会演大会的第二批代表。
