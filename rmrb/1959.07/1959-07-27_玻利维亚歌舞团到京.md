Title: Bolivian Song and Dance Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-07-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 26th: The Bolivian "Fundacion" Song and Dance Troupe, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to China. The troupe consists of thirty-two members and is led by troupe leader Ascarrunz and artistic director Mrs. Ascarrunz.

### 玻利维亚歌舞团到京

1959-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    玻利维亚歌舞团到京
    据新华社26日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的玻利维亚“芳达西亚”歌舞团一行三十二人，在团长阿斯卡伦斯和艺术指导阿斯卡伦斯夫人的率领下，于今日下午乘飞机到达北京。
