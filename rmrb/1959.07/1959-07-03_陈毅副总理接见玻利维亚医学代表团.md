Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Bolivian Medical Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1959-07-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 2nd: Vice Premier Chen Yi received all the distinguished members of the Bolivian medical delegation, led by Dr. Javier Torres Goitia, this morning and held a friendly conversation. Among those present at the reception was Fu Lianzhang, President of the Chinese Medical Association. (Attached photo)
    Vice Premier Chen Yi receives the Bolivian medical delegation. The fourth person from the right in the front row is the head of the delegation, Javier Torres Goitia.

### 陈毅副总理接见玻利维亚医学代表团

1959-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见玻利维亚医学代表团
    新华社2日讯　陈毅副总理在今天上午接见了由哈维尔·托雷斯戈伊蒂亚博士率领的玻利维亚医学代表团的全体贵宾，进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有中华医学会会长傅连暲等人。（附图片）
    陈毅副总理接见玻利维亚医学代表团。前右第四人为哈维尔·托雷斯戈伊蒂亚团长。
  　新华社记者　唐理奎摄
