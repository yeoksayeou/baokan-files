Title: Specializing in Insecticides
Author:
Date: 1959-07-09
Page: 2
    Specializing in Insecticides
    The first phase of construction of the new Shanghai Pesticide Factory has basically been completed, and some equipment has already been put into production. This factory specializes in the manufacture of emulsifiable insecticide concentrates, with an annual production capacity of more than 10,000 tons of various emulsifiable pesticides. The largest output is the widely used DDT emulsion.

### 专产杀虫药

1959-07-09
第2版()
专栏：

    专产杀虫药
    新建上海农药厂第一期工程基本结束，部分设备已经投入生产。这个厂是一家专门制造乳剂杀虫药的工厂，每年可以生产各种乳剂农药一万多吨。其中产量最大的是用途较广的滴滴涕乳剂。
