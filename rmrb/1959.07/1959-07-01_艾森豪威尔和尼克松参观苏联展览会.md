Title: Eisenhower and Nixon Visit Soviet Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1959-07-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 30th dispatch: According to TASS news, U.S. President Eisenhower and Vice President Nixon visited the Soviet scientific, technological and cultural achievements exhibition on the afternoon of the 29th, before its official opening. They met with Soviet First Deputy Premier Kozlov and Soviet Ambassador to the United States Menshikov, and toured the exhibition with them.

### 艾森豪威尔和尼克松参观苏联展览会

1959-07-01
第4版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔和尼克松参观苏联展览会
    新华社30日讯　据塔斯社纽约消息：美国总统艾森豪威尔和副总统尼克松29日下午在苏联科学、技术、文化成就展览会正式开幕前参观了这个展览会。他们会见了苏联部长会议第一副主席科兹洛夫和苏联驻美国大使缅希科夫，并且在他们的陪同下参观。
