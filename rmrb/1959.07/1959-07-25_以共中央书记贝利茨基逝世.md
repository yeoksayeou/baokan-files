Title: Israeli Communist Party Central Committee Secretary Beilitzki Dies
Author:
Date: 1959-07-25
Page: 5
　
Xinhua News Agency, Jerusalem, Israeli Region, July 24th: Dr. Mordechai Beilitzki, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Israeli Communist Party, died on the 21st due to a heart condition.

Beilitzki was elected as a member of the Central Committee of the Party at the Eighth Congress of the Israeli Communist Party in 1944 and was elected as Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee in 1955.

On the 23rd, communists and the public held a funeral for Beilitzki in Tel Aviv. Shmuel Mikunis, General Secretary of the Israeli Communist Party, praised Beilitzki's spirit of dedication to the ideals of communism in his eulogy.

### 以共中央书记贝利茨基逝世

1959-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    以共中央书记贝利茨基逝世
    据新华社24日讯　耶路撒冷以色列区消息：以色列共产党中央委员会书记莫德恰伊·贝利茨基博士因患心脏病在21日逝世。
    贝利茨基在1944年以色列共产党第八次代表大会上被选为党的中央委员会委员，1955年，被选为中央委员会书记处书记。
    23日，共产党人和公众在特拉维夫为贝利茨基送葬。以色列共产党总书记舍穆勒·米库尼斯在致悼词时赞扬贝利茨基献身于共产主义的理想的精神。
