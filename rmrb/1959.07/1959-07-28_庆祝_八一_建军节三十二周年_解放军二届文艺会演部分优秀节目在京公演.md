Title: Celebrating the 32nd Anniversary of the "August 1st" Army Day: Performances of Selected Excellent Programs from the Second PLA Arts Festival in Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1959-07-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th. To celebrate the 32nd anniversary of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's "August 1st" Army Day, performances of selected excellent programs from the Second PLA Arts Festival began in the capital today.

This festival is sponsored by the Propaganda Department of the General Political Department of the PLA. The programs being performed include the dance drama "Five Red Clouds" from the Guangzhou Military Region, the opera "Red Eagle" from the Lanzhou Military Region, and the spoken dramas "Locust Tree Village," "Eastward March Overture," "General as Soldier," and "Young Eagles" from the Beijing, Nanjing, Jinan Military Regions and the Air Force. Also included are music, dance, folk art, and acrobatics from the General Political Department's Cultural Troupe. These programs reflect the heroic struggle life of the PLA in various revolutionary periods and since the Great Leap Forward last year from various perspectives, possessing a strong spirit of the times, distinct military characteristics, and national style.

In addition, the "August 1st" Film Studio will also present a large-scale documentary "On Guarding the Motherland" and a color documentary "The Second Sports Meet of the Chinese People's Liberation Army" to the general audience around "August 1st".

### 庆祝“八一”建军节三十二周年  解放军二届文艺会演部分优秀节目在京公演

1959-07-28
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝“八一”建军节三十二周年
    解放军二届文艺会演部分优秀节目在京公演
    新华社27日讯　为庆祝中国人民解放军“八一”建军节三十二周年，参加全军第二届文艺会演的部分优秀节目今天起在首都举行公演。
    这次会演是解放军总政治部宣传部主办的。公演的节目有广州部队的舞剧“五朵红云”，兰州部队的歌剧“红鹰”，北京、南京、济南部队和空军部队的话剧“槐树庄”、“东进序曲”、“将军当兵”、“年青的鹰”，总政文工团的音乐、舞蹈、曲艺和杂技等。这些节目从各个不同角度，反映了各个革命时期和去年大跃进以来解放军的英勇斗争生活，具有强烈的时代精神、鲜明的部队特色和民族风格。
    此外，“八一”电影制片厂也将在“八一”前后为广大观众献映大型纪录片“在保卫祖国的岗位上”、彩色纪录片“中国人民解放军第二届体育大会”。
