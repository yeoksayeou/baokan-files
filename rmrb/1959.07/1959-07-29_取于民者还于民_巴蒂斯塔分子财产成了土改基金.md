Title: What is taken from the people is returned to the people: Batista faction's property becomes land reform fund
Author:
Date: 1959-07-29
Page: 5

    What is taken from the people is returned to the people
    Batista faction's property becomes land reform fund
    Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 28th - Perez, the Minister of the Recovery of Stolen Property of Cuba, handed over a portion of the confiscated property of the Batista faction to the National Institute of Agrarian Reform of Cuba at a mass rally held in the capital on the 26th.
    Prime Minister Castro, in his capacity as Chairman of the National Institute of Agrarian Reform, accepted a check for 20 million US dollars and land deeds worth 51 million US dollars for use in the land reform cause.
  Prior to this, the Ministry of Recovery of Stolen Property had already transferred 132 estates taken over in Havana Province to the National Institute of Agrarian Reform for disposal.

### 取于民者还于民  巴蒂斯塔分子财产成了土改基金

1959-07-29
第5版()
专栏：

    取于民者还于民
    巴蒂斯塔分子财产成了土改基金
    新华社28日讯　哈瓦那消息：古巴恢复失窃财产部部长佩雷兹26日在首都举行的群众大会上把没收巴蒂斯塔分子的一部分财产交给了古巴土地改革全国委员会。
    总理卡斯特罗以土地改革全国委员会主席的身份接受了一张二千万美元的支票和价值五千一百万美元的地契，以便用在土地改革事业上。
  在这以前，恢复失窃财产部已经把在哈瓦那省接管的一百三十二个庄园移交给了土地改革全国委员会去处理。
