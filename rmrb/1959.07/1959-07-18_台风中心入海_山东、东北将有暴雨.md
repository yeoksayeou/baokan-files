Title: Typhoon Center Enters the Sea; Shandong and Northeast China to Experience Heavy Rain
Author:
Date: 1959-07-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th: The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a typhoon report at 12:00 noon today:

At 8:00 AM today, the center of the typhoon has entered the sea north of the Yangtze River estuary and will move in a north-northeast direction. In the next 24 hours, the Yellow Sea will experience winds of force 8 to 10, the Shandong Peninsula will experience winds of force 7 to 9, the Liaodong Peninsula and the eastern Bohai Sea will experience winds of force 6 to 8, and the Shandong Peninsula and southeastern Northeast China will experience heavy rain.

In the next 24 to 48 hours, the wind force in the Yellow Sea area will weaken, and eastern Northeast China will experience heavy rain.

### 台风中心入海  山东、东北将有暴雨

1959-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    台风中心入海
    山东、东北将有暴雨
    新华社17日讯　今日中午十二时，中央气象科学研究所发布台风消息：
    今天上午八时，台风中心已在长江口以北入海，将向北偏东方向移动。未来二十四小时内，黄海有八级到十级大风，山东半岛有七级到九级大风，辽东半岛和渤海东部有六级到八级大风，山东半岛和东北的东南部将有暴雨。
    未来二十四小时到四十八小时，黄海一带风力将减弱，东北东部将有暴雨。
