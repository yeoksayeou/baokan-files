Title: Albania Embarks on Extensive Construction: This Year's Construction Volume is Thirty Times That of 1938
Author:
Date: 1959-07-31
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, July 29th: Albania's construction volume this year is thirty times that of 1938. Currently, Albania can complete in one day the work that would have taken a month in 1938.
    According to statistics, in the fifteen years since liberation, the total area of construction in Albania could accommodate a population as large as that of the capital, Tirana.
    This year, Albania's total investment in construction projects amounts to over 5.88 billion lek, an increase of 28% compared to 1958. This year, more than 200 large and medium-sized construction projects are being newly built or expanded nationwide, including the Tirana United Food Factory, the Kurbnesh Copper Mine Processing Plant, the Cerrik Oil Refinery, and the Vlora Cement Plant.
    The Albanian government is actively building housing to improve people's living conditions. In the three years from 1956 to 1958, the state built more than 26,000 houses; this year, the area of new housing will be another 220,000 square meters.
    The ranks of construction engineering cadres are also growing year by year. Currently, Albania has nine times as many engineers and architects as in 1945, and nineteen times as many technicians.

### 阿尔巴尼亚大兴土木  今年建筑工程量为1938年的三十倍

1959-07-31
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚大兴土木
    今年建筑工程量为1938年的三十倍
    新华社地拉那29日电　阿尔巴尼亚今年的建筑工程量为1938年的三十倍。目前，阿尔巴尼亚只要用一天的时间就可以完成1938年需要花一个月的时间才能完成的工作。
    据统计，在解放十五年来，阿尔巴尼亚建筑的面积的总和已经可以容纳像首都地拉那那样多的人口。
    今年，阿尔巴尼亚在建筑工程方面的总投资额达五十八亿八千多万里克，比1958年增加28％。今年全国新建和扩建的大、中型建筑工程项目达二百多项，其中包括地拉那联合食品工厂、库尔布奈什铜矿加工厂和柴立克炼油厂、发罗拉水泥厂等。
    阿尔巴尼亚政府积极兴建住宅，以改善人民的居住条件。在1956—1958年的三年间，国家新建的住宅就达二万六千多幢；今年新建住宅的面积又将有二十二万平方公尺。
    建筑工程干部也在逐年成长壮大。目前，阿尔巴尼亚的工程师和建筑师为1945年的九倍，技术人员增为十九倍。
