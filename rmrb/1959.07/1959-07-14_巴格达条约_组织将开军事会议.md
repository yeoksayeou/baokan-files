Title: "Baghdad Pact" Organization to Hold Military Meeting
Author:
Date: 1959-07-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ankara, July 13th: A spokesman for the "Baghdad Pact" organization announced that military representatives from the United States and this aggressive military bloc will hold a meeting in Ankara on July 14th, the first anniversary of the Iraqi revolution.

### “巴格达条约”组织将开军事会议

1959-07-14
第5版()
专栏：

    “巴格达条约”组织将开军事会议
    据新华社13日讯　安卡拉消息：“巴格达条约”组织的一位发言人宣布，美国以及这个侵略性军事集团的军事代表将在7月14日伊拉克革命胜利一周年的时候，在安卡拉举行会议。
