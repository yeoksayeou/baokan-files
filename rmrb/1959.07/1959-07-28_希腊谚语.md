Greek Proverbs

Only in difficult places can heroes be born. The name of virtue is freedom, the name of evil is slavery. A person who lives in justice is safe wherever he goes. Time is always appropriate for justice. If there were no injustice, courage would not be a virtue. In a just cause, the weak can also overcome the strong.
          (Translated by Cai Shixiong)

### 希腊谚语
蔡师雄
1959-07-28
第8版()
专栏：

    希腊谚语艰苦的地方，才能出英雄。美德的名字是自由，罪恶的名字是奴役。生活在正义当中的人，到哪儿去都是安全的。时间对于正义来说，不论何时都相适宜。如果没有非正义，勇气不成为美德。在正义事业中，弱者也能胜强者。
          （蔡师雄译）
