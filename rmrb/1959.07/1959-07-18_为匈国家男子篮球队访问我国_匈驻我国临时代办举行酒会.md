Title: Hungarian Chargé d'Affaires in China Holds Reception for Visit of Hungarian National Men's Basketball Team to China
Author:
Date: 1959-07-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th. Hungarian Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Mr. Szegti, held a reception tonight to welcome the Hungarian National Men's Basketball Team's visit to China.

　　The entire Hungarian National Men's Basketball Team attended the reception.

　　Also invited to the reception were Rong Gaotang and Huang Zhong, Vice Ministers of the Chinese State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, as well as members of the Chinese National Men's Basketball Team, the Beijing Men's Basketball Team, and the "August 1st" Men's Basketball Team.

### 为匈国家男子篮球队访问我国  匈驻我国临时代办举行酒会

1959-07-18
第4版()
专栏：

    为匈国家男子篮球队访问我国
    匈驻我国临时代办举行酒会
    新华社17日讯　匈牙利驻华大使馆临时代办西格蒂，今晚为欢迎匈牙利国家男子篮球队来中国访问举行酒会。
  匈牙利国家男子篮球队全体人员应邀出席了酒会。
    应邀出席酒会的还有我国国家体委副主任荣高棠、黄中，以及我国国家男子篮球队、北京男子篮球队和“八一”男子篮球队的队员们。
