International Briefs

Italian cities celebrate the Italian Communist Party Press Month
    Rome News: Many Italian cities began activities on the 26th to celebrate the "Unità" festival organized for the "Communist Party Press Month". Many working people in the provinces of Florence, Mantua, Livorno and Novara participated in the provincial festival activities.
    In the provinces of Milan, Bologna and Modena, "Unità" festival activities were also held in many cities.

Burma National United Front supports Thakin Tin's speech
    Rangoon News: The Presidium of the Burma National United Front, at a meeting held on July 25, studied the speech delivered by Thakin Tin, Vice-Chairman of the Clean AFPFL, on Martyrs' Day, July 19.
    In his speech on Martyrs' Day, Thakin Tin emphasized the need to strictly implement Burma's neutral foreign policy, defend national independence, safeguard the people's democratic rights and uphold national unity.
    According to Rangoon newspapers on the 27th, the Presidium of the Burma National United Front decided to cooperate with the Clean AFPFL in implementing certain common programs, and instructed the Burma National United Front organizations at all levels in various regions to support Thakin Tin's speech.

### 国际简讯

1959-07-29
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    意各地庆祝意共报刊月
    罗马讯　意大利许多城市在26日开始举行为庆祝“共产党报刊月”而组织的“团结报”节日的活动。佛罗伦萨、孟都亚、里窝那和诺瓦拉等省的许多劳动人民都参加了本省的节日活动。
    在米兰省、波伦亚省和摩德纳省，许多城市里也都举行了“团结报”节日的活动。
    缅甸民族团结阵线支持德钦丁的演说
    仰光讯　缅甸民族团结阵线主席团在7月25日举行的会议上，研究了廉洁的反法西斯人民自由同盟副主席德钦丁在7月19日烈士日发表的演说。
    德钦丁在烈士日发表的演说中强调说，必须严格执行缅甸的中立外交政策，必须保卫国家的独立，必须保障人民的民主权利和坚持国家的团结。
    据仰光报纸27日报道，缅甸民族团结阵线主席团决定在实现某些共同的纲领方面同廉洁的反法西斯人民自由同盟进行合作，并且指示缅甸民族团结阵线在各地区的各级组织支持德钦丁的演说。
