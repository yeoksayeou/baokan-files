Title: Supporting the Vietnamese People's Struggle for Peaceful Reunification: Various Chinese Organizations Prepare for "Vietnam Day" Activities
Author:
Date: 1959-07-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. In enthusiastic response to the call from the Asian-African People's Solidarity Council to hold "Vietnam Day", the Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China, and the All-China Youth Federation have held preparatory meetings to organize "Vietnam Day" activities. The meeting decided to hold a "Capital People's Commemoration of the Fifth Anniversary of the Geneva Accords and Support for Vietnamese Peaceful Reunification Conference" on July 20th. The meeting also decided that on the 19th and 20th, mass rallies would also be held in Shanghai, Nanning, Kunming, and other cities, and it was decided to launch related propaganda activities from now on.

This year, July 20th marks the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Geneva Accords. The Asian-African People's Solidarity Council has designated this day as "Vietnam Day", calling on the people of Asian and African countries and the people of the world to support the Vietnamese people's struggle for peaceful reunification.

### 支持越南人民争取和平统一  我各团体筹备“越南日”活动

1959-07-17
第5版()
专栏：

    支持越南人民争取和平统一
    我各团体筹备“越南日”活动
    新华社16日讯　为了热烈响应亚非人民团结理事会关于举行“越南日”的号召，中国亚非团结委员会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国越南友好协会、中华全国总工会、中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会、中华全国青年联合会等团体已经为组织“越南日”活动举行了筹备会议。会议决定在7月20日举行“首都各界人民纪念日内瓦协议五周年、支持越南和平统一大会”，会议还决定在19日和20日，上海、南宁、昆明等城市也分别举行群众集会，并决定从即日起展开有关宣传活动。
    今年7月20日是日内瓦协议签订五周年，亚非人民团结理事会规定这一天为“越南日”，号召亚非各国人民和世界人民支持越南人民和平统一的斗争。
