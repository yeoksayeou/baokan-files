Title: U.S. Warship Again Intrudes into Our Territorial Waters for Provocation; We Issue the 59th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1959-07-21
Page: 1

    U.S. Warship Again Intrudes into Our Territorial Waters for Provocation
    We Issue the 59th Serious Warning
    Xinhua News Agency, 20th – From 1:28 AM to 3:15 AM on July 20th, a U.S. warship intruded into the sea area of ​​Pingtan and Baiquan areas of Fujian Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, a spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 59th serious warning.

### 美舰又侵入我领海挑衅  我提出第59次严重警告

1959-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

    美舰又侵入我领海挑衅
    我提出第59次严重警告
    新华社20日讯　7月20日一时二十八分到三时十五分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省平潭、白犬地区海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第五十九次严重警告。
