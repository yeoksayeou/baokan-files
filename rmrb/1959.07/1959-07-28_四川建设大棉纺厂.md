Title: Sichuan Building Large Cotton Textile Factory
Author:
Date: 1959-07-28
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, July 27th: The largest cotton textile factory in Sichuan Province, the Sichuan No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory, is under accelerated construction.

This factory is being built in Chengdu. Currently, the foundation work for the factory buildings has been completed, and the prefabrication of the roof trusses, beams, and columns of the main factory building has fully begun.

Once fully completed, the Sichuan No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory will have 100,000 spindles, more than 4,000 looms, and a variety of printing and dyeing equipment. Its spinning capacity will be equivalent to two-fifths of the province's existing mechanical spinning capacity, and its weaving capacity will be equivalent to twice the province's existing mechanical weaving capacity. The annual cotton cloth production, calculated at two zhang per person, can supply more than 20 million people.

### 四川建设大棉纺厂

1959-07-28
第2版()
专栏：

    四川建设大棉纺厂
    新华社成都27日电　四川省最大的棉纺织厂——四川第一棉纺厂正在加速施工。
    这座工厂建设在成都市。目前，厂房基础工程已经全部建成，主厂房的屋架、梁、柱的预制工作全面开始。
    四川第一棉纺厂全部建成后将拥有十万纱锭、四千多台织布机和许多印染设备。它的纺纱能力相当于全省现有机械纺纱能力的五分之二，织布能力相当于全省现有机械织布能力的两倍。每年生产的棉布按每人二丈计算，能供应两千多万人使用。
