Title: Asian and African Countries Launch "Vietnam Day" Activities to Support the Struggle of the Vietnamese People and Advocate the Implementation of the Geneva Accords
Author:
Date: 1959-07-21
Page: 4

　　Asian and African Countries Launch "Vietnam Day" Activities
　　Supporting the Struggle of the Vietnamese People and Advocating the Implementation of the Geneva Accords

　　This newspaper combines reports from Xinhua News Agency: On July 20th, "Vietnam Day", people's organizations in Asian and African countries and many other countries carried out activities to support the struggle of the Vietnamese people for the unification of their motherland. Newspapers in many countries published commentaries calling for the implementation of the Geneva Accords and condemning the interference and aggression of American imperialism in South Vietnam.
　　People from all walks of life in Ulan Bator responded to the call of the Secretariat of the Asian-African People's Solidarity Council to hold a "Vietnam Day" rally on the afternoon of the 20th. The meeting unanimously adopted a telegram to the Asian-African People's Solidarity Committee of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and other groups, expressing the Mongolian people's full support for the Vietnamese people's struggle to unify their motherland and stop the atrocities of American imperialism and the Ngo Dinh Diem clique, and wishing the Vietnamese people further victories in this struggle.
　　In a statement issued on "Vietnam Day," the Indian Asian-African Solidarity Committee called for the full implementation of the Geneva Accords in Vietnam, the withdrawal of foreign military advisors, the removal of foreign bases, and demanded that South Vietnam cooperate with the International Commission and stop suppressing the people. The statement also called for the implementation of the Geneva Accords regarding Laos.
　　The Indonesian Peace Committee issued a statement on "Vietnam Day" saying that the Indonesian people wholeheartedly support the efforts of the Vietnamese people to reunify their motherland.

### 亚非各国开展“越南日”活动   声援越南人民的斗争  主张贯彻日内瓦协议

1959-07-21
第4版()
专栏：

    亚非各国开展“越南日”活动
    声援越南人民的斗争  主张贯彻日内瓦协议
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：在7月20日“越南日”这一天，亚非国家和其他许多国家的人民团体开展声援越南人民争取祖国统一斗争的活动，许多国家的报纸发表评论，要求贯彻执行日内瓦协议，并谴责美帝国主义在南越的干涉和侵略活动。
    乌兰巴托市各界人民响应亚非人民团结理事会书记处举行“越南日”的号召，20日下午举行了集会。会议一致通过致越南民主共和国亚非人民团结委员会等团体的一封电报，表示蒙古人民完全支持越南人民统一祖国、制止美帝国主义和吴庭艳集团暴行的斗争，并祝越南人民在这一斗争中进一步取得胜利。
    印度亚非团结委员会在一项就“越南日”发表的声明中，要求在越南完全实现日内瓦协议，撤出外国军事顾问，取消外国基地，要求南越同国际委员会合作，并且停止镇压人民。声明还要求贯彻日内瓦协议关于老挝的条款。
    印度尼西亚和平委员会就“越南日”发表声明说，印度尼西亚人民全心全意支持越南人民为重新统一祖国所作的努力。
    印度尼西亚“人民日报”20日发表社论指出，印度尼西亚和越南两国在苏加诺总统访问越南民主共和国期间所发表的联合公报表明，苏加诺总统支持越南人民为重新统一祖国所作的努力，两国首脑都认为，必须根据日内瓦协议来解决重新统一越南的问题。这家报纸表示希望，印度尼西亚人民将贯彻这个公报，并且支持越南人民的斗争。
    在“越南日”这一天，由日本亚非团结委员会、日本和平委员会、日本工会总评议会、日苏协会和社会党等十六个组织组成的委员会派遣代表到美、英、法、苏等十三个国家的驻日使馆递交一份要求书，要求这些国家为完全履行日内瓦协议和实现越南的和平统一而努力，并且表示反对岸信介政府单方面给南越战争赔偿。
