Title: Mongolia's Council of Ministers Hosts Experts from Fraternal Countries to Celebrate the 38th Anniversary of the Victory of the Mongolian People's Revolution
Author:
Date: 1959-07-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, July 9th. To celebrate the 38th anniversary of the victory of the Mongolian People's Revolution, the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic held a grand reception this evening for experts from fraternal countries such as the Soviet Union, China, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, the German Democratic Republic, and Korea working in Mongolia.

Mongolian party and government leaders Tsedenbal, Sambuu, Tsende, Dugersuren, Tumur-Ochir and others, as well as diplomatic envoys from various fraternal countries to Mongolia, attended the reception.

Tsedenbal, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, spoke at the meeting. On behalf of the Party and the government, he expressed his gratitude to the experts from various fraternal countries. He said that inspired by the great achievements of the people of socialist countries, and with the fraternal assistance of the Soviet Union and other socialist countries, the Mongolian people have continuously achieved brilliant successes in the cause of socialist construction.

Tsedenbal said that the labor and contributions of Soviet comrades for the happy life of the Mongolian people have written a glorious page in the history of friendship between the Mongolian and Soviet peoples. He said that this year a large number of Soviet experts came to help Mongolia reclaim wasteland, enabling the wasteland reclamation plan to be overfulfilled. Chinese experts and technicians are carrying out fruitful work in various economic sectors of Mongolia. Experts from other socialist countries have also provided great help. This assistance is of great significance for accelerating Mongolia's development. Soviet Ambassador to Mongolia, Molotov, spoke on behalf of the envoys of various fraternal countries and the experts present. In his speech, he thanked the Mongolian Party and government for the reception and wished the Mongolian people greater achievements in the cause of socialist construction.

### 庆祝蒙古人民革命胜利三十八周年  蒙部长会议招待兄弟国家专家

1959-07-11
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝蒙古人民革命胜利三十八周年
    蒙部长会议招待兄弟国家专家
    新华社乌兰巴托9日电　蒙古人民共和国部长会议为庆祝蒙古人民革命胜利三十八周年，今天晚间举行了盛大的招待会，招待在蒙古的苏联、中国、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、德意志民主共和国和朝鲜等兄弟国家的专家。
    蒙古党政领导人泽登巴尔、桑布、曾德、杜格苏伦、图穆尔—奥奇尔等人以及各兄弟国家驻蒙古的外交使节出席了招待会。
    蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记、部长会议主席泽登巴尔在会上讲了话。他代表党和政府向各兄弟国家的专家表示感谢。他说，蒙古人民在社会主义各国人民的伟大成就的鼓舞下，在苏联和其他社会主义国家兄弟般的援助下，在社会主义建设事业中不断取得辉煌成就。
    泽登巴尔说，苏联同志为蒙古人民的幸福生活所进行的劳动和贡献在蒙苏两国人民的友谊历史上写下了光辉的一页。他说，今年有大批苏联专家来帮助蒙古开垦荒地，使开荒计划超额完成。中国专家和技术工人在蒙古各经济部门进行着卓有成效的劳动。其他社会主义国家的专家也给予了很大帮助。这些帮助对加速蒙古的发展有着重大意义。苏联驻蒙古大使莫洛托夫代表各兄弟国家使节和在场的专家们讲了话。他在讲话中感谢蒙古党和政府的招待，祝蒙古人民在社会主义建设事业中取得更大的成就。
