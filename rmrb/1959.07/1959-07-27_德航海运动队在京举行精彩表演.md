Title: German Maritime Sports Team Gives Wonderful Performance in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-07-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 26th: The four model ship athletes of the German Democratic Republic maritime sports team, who are visiting our country, were warmly welcomed by Chinese model ship enthusiasts and holidaymakers when they gave a model ship sailing performance in Beihai Park, Beijing this morning.

Today's performance took place on the water around the Five Dragon Pavilions in Beihai Park, and thousands of tourists were attracted to this interesting performance. They stood on the long bank and watched the performance for about two hours.

Chinese model ship athletes from Qingdao, Qinhuangdao, and Beijing also participated in the performance. After the performance, Li Da, Director of the China National Defense Sports Association, Vice Admiral Liu Daosheng, and Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, met with the guest team athletes and took a group photo with them. The ten-member maritime sports team of the German Democratic Republic arrived in Beijing on July 6.

### 德航海运动队在京举行精彩表演

1959-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    德航海运动队在京举行精彩表演
    据新华社26日讯　应邀来我国访问的德意志民主共和国航海运动队的四名舰船模型选手，今天上午在北京北海公园举行舰船模型航行表演时，受到我国舰船模型爱好者和欢度星期假日的游人们热烈的欢迎。
    今天的表演是在北海公园五龙亭一带的水面上进行的，几千名游人都被这种有趣的表演吸引住了。他们站在长长的岸边观看了约两个钟头的表演。
    我国来自青岛、秦皇岛和北京的舰船模型选手，也参加了表演。表演结束后，中国人民国防体育协会主任李达，刘道生海军中将和对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥等人接见了客队选手，并同他们在一起合影。由十人组成的德意志民主共和国航海运动队是7月6日到达北京的。
