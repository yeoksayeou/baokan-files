Title: Nepalese Kingdom Buddhist Delegation Arrives in Kunming
Author:
Date: 1959-07-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 23rd - The Nepalese Kingdom Buddhist Delegation, consisting of six members, arrived in Kunming by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Buddhist Association of China.

The delegation is led by Ganlu Xi, President of the Nepalese Kingdom Buddhist Revival Association and Vice President of the World Fellowship of Buddhists. Members of the delegation include Anuruddha, Vice President of the Nepalese Kingdom Buddhist Revival Association; Asala Sisaka, Secretary-General; Dayapeer Singh, Standing Committee Member; Satyamohan Yoshi, Standing Committee Member; and Vimala Devi, daughter of delegation leader Ganlu Xi.

Welcoming them at the airport were Wu Gula (Dai nationality), Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China, Song Mou (Tibetan nationality), Director of the Buddhist Association of China, and representatives from religious circles in Yunnan Province and Kunming City.

### 尼泊尔王国佛教代表团到昆明

1959-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔王国佛教代表团到昆明
    新华社昆明23日电　应中国佛教协会邀请来我国访问的尼泊尔王国佛教代表团一行六人，今天下午乘飞机到达昆明。
    代表团由尼泊尔王国佛教复兴会会长、世界佛教徒联谊会副主席甘露喜率领。代表团团员有尼泊尔王国佛教复兴会副会长阿努罗陀、秘书长阿萨拉姊·释迦、常务委员达雅皮尔·辛格、常务委员萨蒂雅摩汗·约西和甘露喜团长的女儿维梅拉·德维。
    到机场欢迎的有中国佛教协会副会长伍古腊（傣族），中国佛教协会理事松谋（藏族）以及云南省和昆明市宗教界的代表。
