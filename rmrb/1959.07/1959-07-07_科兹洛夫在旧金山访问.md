Title: Kozlov Visits San Francisco
Author:
Date: 1959-07-07
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, San Francisco, June 6th: According to TASS, Soviet First Deputy Premier Kozlov and his entourage traveled by car to Sonoma Valley, north of San Francisco, on the 5th. He visited the Bohemian Grove Club, which has about a thousand members representing industrialists, financiers, and intellectuals of California. Bohemian Grove Club President Wheland hosted a banquet for Kozlov. The banquet was held in an unrestrained and friendly atmosphere. Kozlov said, "Our meeting today once again shows that we and you can get along well and coexist peacefully and friendly. You gave me redwood seeds grown near your club as a souvenir. After we return home, we will plant these seeds and make them grow and mature, making them a symbol of our friendship."
    Kozlov and members of the Soviet delegation watched an ice ballet performance in the evening.
    On the afternoon of the 4th, Kozlov took a yacht along San Francisco Bay to visit the piers of one of the largest ports in the United States. Then, Port Director Magagnin invited the Soviet guests to his country villa, where a grand banquet was held. More than one hundred prominent industrialists and financiers from California attended the banquet.

### 科兹洛夫在旧金山访问

1959-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

    科兹洛夫在旧金山访问
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社旧金山讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席科兹洛夫和随行人员5日乘汽车前往旧金山北面的索诺玛谷。他访问了波希米亚丛林俱乐部，这一俱乐部约有一千会员，他们是加利福尼亚州的工业家、金融家和知识分子代表。波希米亚丛林俱乐部主席韦伦德设宴招待科兹洛夫。宴会是在无拘束的友好气氛中举行的。科兹洛夫说，“我们今天的会晤再一次表明，我们和您们能够很好的相处，和平和友好的共处。你们把你们俱乐部附近生长的红木的种子赠给我作为纪念。我们回国后，将种植这些种子，使它们长大成熟，使它们成为我们友谊的象征。”
    科兹洛夫和苏联代表团人员晚间观看了冰上的芭蕾舞表演。
    4日下午，科兹洛夫乘游艇沿着旧金山海湾游览了美国最大的港口之一的码头。然后，港务局长马格宁邀请苏联客人到自己的城外别墅去，在那里举行了盛大的宴会。一百多名加利福尼亚州著名的工业家和金融家出席了宴会。
