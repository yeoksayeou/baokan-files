International Briefs

    Nine African Nations Discuss Algerian Issue
    New York dispatch: Representatives of nine independent African nations at the United Nations formally announced on the 7th that their governments will hold a ministerial meeting in Monrovia, Liberia, from August 4th to 8th to discuss the Algerian issue.
    These nine countries are: Ethiopia, Ghana, Guinea, Liberia, Libya, Morocco, Sudan, Tunisia, and the United Arab Republic.
    Mexico Abolishes Privileges of a U.S. Company
    Mexico City dispatch: According to the Mexican newspaper "News," the Mexican Ministry of National Resources has abolished the lease rights of the U.S. "Texas International Sulphur Company" branch to exploit sulfur in 52,000 hectares of land.
    British Queen Invites De Gaulle to Visit Britain Next Year
    London dispatch: London officially announced on the 8th that British Queen Elizabeth has invited French President De Gaulle to pay a state visit to Britain in 1960. It is reported that De Gaulle has accepted the invitation.
    Burma to Accept a U.S. "Aid" Grant
    Rangoon dispatch: The Burmese government announced on the 6th that Burma will accept an economic "aid" grant from the United States totaling $37 million. This will be the first U.S. "aid" that Burma has accepted since 1953, when Burma stopped accepting U.S. "aid" due to the threat posed by the remnants of the Chiang Kai-shek clique's armed forces, which were supported by the United States.

### 国际简讯

1959-07-10
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    非洲九国讨论阿尔及利亚问题
    纽约讯　九个非洲独立国家驻联合国的代表在7日正式宣布，他们的政府将在8月4日到8日在利比里亚的蒙罗维亚举行部长级会议，讨论阿尔及利亚问题。
    这九个国家是：埃塞俄比亚、加纳、几内亚、利比里亚、利比亚、摩洛哥、苏丹、突尼斯和阿联。墨西哥废除美国一公司的特权
    墨西哥城讯　据墨西哥“新闻报”报道，墨西哥国家资源部废除了美国“得克萨斯国际硫磺公司”分公司在五万二千公顷土地上开采硫磺的租借权。
    英女王邀请戴高乐明年访英
    伦敦讯　伦敦方面8日正式宣布，英国女王伊丽莎白邀请法国总统戴高乐在1960年到英国作国事访问。据报道，戴高乐已经接受邀请。
    缅甸将接受一笔美“援”
    仰光讯　缅甸政府6日宣布，缅甸将接受美国提供的一笔总数达三千七百万美元的经济“援助”款项。自从1953年缅甸由于美国支持蒋介石集团的残余武装力量威胁缅甸而停止接受美“援”以来，这将是缅甸接受的第一笔美“援”。
