Title: United Arab Republic Military Attaché ad interim Holds Reception to Celebrate Anniversary of Armed Forces Day

Author:

Date: 1959-07-24
Page: 4

    Celebrating the Anniversary of the Founding of the Armed Forces of the United Arab Republic
    UAR Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Holds Reception
    Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd. Lieutenant Colonel Wu Bo, Acting Military Attaché of the United Arab Republic in China, held a reception this evening to celebrate the anniversary of the founding of the UAR Armed Forces and the anniversary of the Revolution.
    Senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including General Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff, and General Li Juikui, were invited to attend the reception. He Gongkai, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, as well as diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries to China, were also invited to attend the reception. General Zhang Zongxun and Lieutenant Colonel Wu Bo spoke at the meeting, wishing that the friendship between the two peoples and armies would develop and consolidate day by day.

### 庆祝阿联武装部队建军节  阿联驻华代理武官举行招待会

1959-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝阿联武装部队建军节
    阿联驻华代理武官举行招待会
    新华社23日讯　阿拉伯联合共和国驻我国代理武官吴勃中校今晚举行招待会，庆祝阿联武装部队建军节及革命纪念日。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊上将和李聚奎上将等高级将领应邀出席招待会。应邀出席招待会的还有我国外交部西亚非洲司副司长何功楷以及各国驻华使节和武官。张宗逊上将和吴勃中校在会上讲话，祝两国人民和军队之间的友谊，日益发展和巩固。
