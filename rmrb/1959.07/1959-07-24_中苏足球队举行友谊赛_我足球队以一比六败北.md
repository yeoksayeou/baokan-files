Title: Chinese-Soviet Football Teams Hold Friendly Match; Our Football Team Loses 1-6
Author:
Date: 1959-07-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: According to TASS news agency, the Chinese national football team played a friendly match against the Moscow "Lokomotiv" team on the "Dynamo" stadium in Moscow on the 22nd, and lost with a score of one to six.

The "Lokomotiv" team is one of the Soviet Class A teams.

### 中苏足球队举行友谊赛  我足球队以一比六败北

1959-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中苏足球队举行友谊赛
    我足球队以一比六败北
    新华社讯　据塔斯社消息：中国国家足球队22日在莫斯科“狄纳莫”运动场上同莫斯科“火车头”队进行了一场友谊赛，结果以一比六失利。
    “火车头”队是苏联甲级球队之一。
