Title: Rice Husks Replace Gasoline
Author:
Date: 1959-07-21
Page: 2
Column:

    Rice Husks Replace Gasoline
    The Grain, Cotton and Oil Processing Plant of the Zhongxiang County Grain Bureau in Hubei Province uses gas produced by burning rice husks to replace gasoline to drive its vehicles. On a stove that is 3.27 meters in diameter and 2.42 meters high, burning 1,400 jin of rice husks per day can generate enough gas to drive all the machines in the processing plant for 18 hours, saving 220 jin of gasoline per day.

### 谷壳代汽油

1959-07-21
第2版()
专栏：

    谷壳代汽油
    湖北省钟祥县粮食局粮棉油加工厂，用谷壳燃烧后产生的煤气代汽油开车。在直径三点二七米、高二点四二米的一座炉上，每天烧谷壳一千四百斤，产生的煤气就可以带动加工厂的全部机器开十八个小时，每天能节约汽油二百二十斤。
