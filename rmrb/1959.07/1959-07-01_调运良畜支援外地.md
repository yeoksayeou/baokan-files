Title: Transferring Fine Livestock to Support Other Regions
Author: Xià Gé'ěr
Date: 1959-07-01
Page: 3

    Transferring Fine Livestock to Support Other Regions
    (Our newspaper, Urumqi, 30th dispatch) The Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region is transferring nearly 30,000 head of fine livestock to support more than twenty provinces, cities and autonomous regions including Beijing, Shanghai, Hunan, and Hubei, to develop agricultural production and improve livestock breeds. The livestock transferred out include the nationally renowned Xinjiang fine-wool sheep, Kuqa improved sheep, and Ili draft and riding horses. (Xia Ge'er)

### 调运良畜支援外地
夏格尔
1959-07-01
第3版()
专栏：

    调运良畜支援外地
    本报乌鲁木齐30日电　新疆维吾尔自治区调运将近三万多头优良牲畜，支援北京、上海、湖南、湖北等二十多个省市和自治区，发展农业生产和改良牲畜品种。外调牲畜包括驰名全国的新疆细毛羊、库车改良羊和伊犁种马与耕马。（夏格尔）
