Title: Chilean Writer Yañez Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-07-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 28th. Chilean writer Lautaro Yañez and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 智利作家杨加斯到京

1959-07-29
第4版()
专栏：

    智利作家杨加斯到京
    新华社28日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的智利作家拉乌达罗·杨加斯和他的夫人，在今天乘飞机到达北京。
