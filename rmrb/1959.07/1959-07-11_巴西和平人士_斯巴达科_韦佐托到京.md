Title: Brazilian Peace Activist Spartaco Vezzoto Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-07-11
Page: 4

    Brazilian Peace Activist
    Spartaco Vezzoto Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, July 10th—Spartaco Vezzoto, Vice-Chairman of the Sao Paulo State Peace Committee of Brazil and expert in psychiatry, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.

### 巴西和平人士  斯巴达科·韦佐托到京

1959-07-11
第4版()
专栏：

    巴西和平人士
    斯巴达科·韦佐托到京
    新华社10日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请前来我国访问的巴西圣保罗州和平委员会副主席、精神病学专家斯巴达科·韦佐托，今天乘飞机到达北京。
