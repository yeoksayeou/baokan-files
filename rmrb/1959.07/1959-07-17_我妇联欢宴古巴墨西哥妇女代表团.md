Title: All-China Women's Federation Hosts Banquet for Cuban and Mexican Women's Delegations
Author:
Date: 1959-07-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th - The All-China Women's Federation hosted a banquet at the New Qiao Hotel tonight to welcome the Cuban and Mexican women's delegations visiting China.

	Cai Chang, President of the All-China Women's Federation, extended a warm welcome on behalf of the All-China Women's Federation to the sisters from Latin America.

	The banquet hall was filled with friendly laughter and cordial conversation. The hosts and guests repeatedly raised their glasses in toasts to the friendship between China and Cuba, China and Mexico, to world peace, and to safeguarding the rights and interests of women and children!

### 我妇联欢宴古巴墨西哥妇女代表团

1959-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

    我妇联欢宴古巴墨西哥妇女代表团
    新华社16日讯　全国妇联今晚在新侨饭店举行宴会，欢迎来我国访问的古巴妇女代表团和墨西哥妇女代表团。
    全国妇联主席蔡畅代表全国妇联向来自拉丁美洲的姊妹们表示热烈欢迎。
    宴会厅里，充满了友谊的欢笑和亲切的交谈声。宾主不断举杯，为中国同古巴、中国同墨西哥的友谊，为世界和平，为保卫妇女和儿童的权益干杯！
