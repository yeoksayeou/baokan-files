Title: Chinese Government Delegation Meets with Leaders of the Iraqi-Chinese Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1959-07-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, July 20th: The Chinese government delegation led by Luo Guibo, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Li Dequan, the Chinese women's representative, and leaders of the Chinese Song and Dance Troupe met amicably this morning with leaders of the Iraqi-Chinese Friendship Association.

Deputy Minister Luo Guibo told the Chairman of the Association, Abed Zarzara, that the Chinese government and people attach great importance to Iraq's National Day. Therefore, at the invitation of the Iraqi government, they sent a government delegation to participate in the celebrations. Luo Guibo said, "We believe that the victory of the July 14th Revolution is of great significance not only to the Iraqi people but also to world peace."

Luo Guibo said that the delegation was deeply impressed by the warm friendship of the Iraqi people towards the Chinese people. The Iraqi people love China and understand the Chinese people. Luo Guibo praised the Iraqi-Chinese Friendship Association for the positive role it has played in promoting and strengthening friendly relations between the two countries.

Zarzara welcomed the Chinese delegation's visit to Iraq. He said that the Soviet Union, China, and other peace-loving democratic countries have consistently opposed imperialism and strived for world peace. They have always supported the struggles of people of all countries for national liberation. Iraq's July 14th Revolution and the Cuban people's revolution have both received their support. Zarzara expressed his gratitude for the Chinese people's support for Iraq, Algeria, and other Arab countries striving for national liberation. He said that the Iraqi-Chinese Friendship Association will work to further strengthen the understanding and friendship between the Iraqi and Chinese people.

Chinese Ambassador Chen Zhifang also attended the meeting.

### 我国政府代表团同伊中友协领导人会见

1959-07-22
第4版()
专栏：

    我国政府代表团同伊中友协领导人会见
    新华社巴格达20日电　由中华人民共和国外交部副部长罗贵波率领的中国政府代表团、中国妇女代表李德全和中国歌舞团领导人今天上午同伊拉克—中国友好协会领导人作了友好会见。
    罗贵波副部长对协会主席阿布德·扎尔扎拉说，中国政府和人民非常重视伊拉克国庆，因此应伊拉克政府的邀请，派遣了政府代表团参加庆祝。罗贵波说：“我们认为，7月14日革命的胜利不仅对于伊拉克人民，而且对于世界和平都有非常重大的意义。”
    罗贵波说，代表团对于伊拉克人民对中国人民的热烈友情有深刻印象。伊拉克人民爱中国，并且了解中国人民。罗贵波赞扬伊拉克—中国友好协会在促进和加强两国友好关系方面所起的良好作用。
    扎尔扎拉欢迎中国代表来伊拉克访问。他说，苏联、中国和其他和平民主国家一贯反对帝国主义，争取世界和平，它们始终支持各国人民争取民族解放的斗争，伊拉克7月14日革命和古巴人民革命都得到过它们的支持。扎尔扎拉表示感谢中国人民对伊拉克、阿尔及利亚和其它争取民族解放的阿拉伯国家的支持。他说，伊拉克—中国友好协会将为进一步加强伊拉克人民和中国人民的谅解和友谊而努力。
    中国大使陈志方也参加了这次会见。
