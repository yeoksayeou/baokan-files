Title: Jinan Adds a New Blast Furnace
Author:
Date: 1959-07-27
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 26th July: Jinan Shengjian Steel Plant's No. 4 100-cubic-meter blast furnace has been completed and officially put into production. After appraisal, the equipment of each system of the blast furnace is operating well, and the quality of iron has reached the specified standard for special-grade foundry pig iron.
	The designed capacity of this blast furnace is 140 tons of iron per day.

### 济南又添一座新高炉

1959-07-27
第2版()
专栏：

    济南又添一座新高炉
    新华社济南26日电　济南生建钢铁厂四号一百立方米高炉建成，并正式投入生产。经过鉴定，高炉各系统的设备运转情况良好，铁的质量达到规定的特级铸造生铁的标准。
    这座高炉的设计能力为日产铁一百四十吨。
