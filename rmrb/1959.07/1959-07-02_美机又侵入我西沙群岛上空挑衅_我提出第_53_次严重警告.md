Title: U.S. Aircraft Provokes Again by Intruding into Airspace Over Xisha Islands; We Issue 53rd Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1959-07-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. At 11:45 on June 30, a U.S. Navy patrol plane intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, a spokesman for the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 53rd serious warning.

### 美机又侵入我西沙群岛上空挑衅  我提出第53次严重警告

1959-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

    美机又侵入我西沙群岛上空挑衅
    我提出第53次严重警告
    新华社1日讯　6月30日十一时四十五分，美国海军巡逻机一架，侵入我国广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第五十三次严重警告。
