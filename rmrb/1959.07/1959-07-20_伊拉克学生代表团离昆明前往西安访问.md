Title: Iraqi Student Delegation Leaves Kunming for Xi'an for Visit
Author: 
Date: 1959-07-20
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 19th. The Iraqi Republic student delegation, invited by the All-China Students' Federation to visit our country, left Kunming by plane today to visit Xi'an.

### 伊拉克学生代表团离昆明前往西安访问

1959-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克学生代表团离昆明前往西安访问
    新华社昆明19日电　应中华全国学生联合会邀请来我国访问的伊拉克共和国学生代表团，今天乘飞机离开昆明前往西安访问。
