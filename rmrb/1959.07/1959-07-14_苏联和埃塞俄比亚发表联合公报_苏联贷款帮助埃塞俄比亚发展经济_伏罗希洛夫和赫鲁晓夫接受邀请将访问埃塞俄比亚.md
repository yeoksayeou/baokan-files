Title: USSR and Ethiopia Issue Joint Communiqué; Soviet Loans to Help Ethiopia Develop its Economy; Voroshilov and Khrushchev Accept Invitation to Visit Ethiopia
Author:
Date: 1959-07-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 13th -- TASS reported on July 13th that the Soviet-Ethiopian joint communiqué was published.

The communiqué stated that His Majesty Emperor Haile Selassie I of Ethiopia, accompanied by senior officials of the government of the Kingdom of Ethiopia, arrived in Moscow on June 29, 1959, for a fourteen-day state visit to the Soviet Union. During their visit, they toured Sverdlovsk and the Ural industrial region, Stalingrad, the Lenin Volga Hydroelectric Station, the Volga-Don Canal, the Caucasian and Crimean resorts on the Black Sea coast, and Leningrad, among other places. His Majesty the Emperor showed great interest in the industrial enterprises and agricultural engineering projects under construction in the Soviet Union.

The Emperor and his entourage were warmly and sincerely received by the residents in various places, who expressed deep sympathy for the distinguished guests and the friendly Ethiopian people.

The communiqué stated that during Emperor Selassie I's visit, Marshal Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, awarded him the Order of Empress Savskaya on a golden chain. The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union awarded him the Order of Suvorov, First Class, the highest Soviet military order.

Emperor Selassie I held several beneficial talks with Chairman Khrushchev of the Soviet Council of Ministers on cooperation between the two countries in maintaining world peace and striving for the freedom and independence of the African people. The leaders of the two countries expressed their support for the principles of collective security, non-interference, and coexistence as necessary conditions for developing peaceful relations between countries.

The two sides also held negotiations on expanding trade between the Soviet Union and Ethiopia and signed a trade and economic agreement between the two countries. As a result of the negotiations, the Soviet Union agreed to provide Ethiopia with a long-term loan of 400 million rubles at low interest to develop industry and agriculture. The two sides also reached an agreement on further developing cultural ties between the two countries.

The leaders of the two countries agreed to support the principles formulated at the Bandung Conference and the principles included in the resolutions of the Accra Conference of Independent African States. The leaders of the two countries expressed their pleasure at having the opportunity to personally discuss and exchange views on issues of mutual interest. They were confident that after their negotiations and friendly contacts, the relationship between the two countries and between the two peoples would be further consolidated and developed.

The communiqué concluded by saying that Emperor Haile Selassie I invited Marshal Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and Chairman Khrushchev of the Soviet Council of Ministers to visit Ethiopia. This invitation has been gratefully accepted. The date of the visit will be determined by mutual consultation.

Xinhua News Agency, July 13th -- TASS Moscow reported: Soviet Minister of Foreign Trade Patolichev and Ethiopian Foreign Minister Yilma Deressa signed a trade agreement between the Soviet Union and Ethiopia in Moscow on the 11th. At the same time, Skachkov, Chairman of the State Committee of the Soviet Council of Ministers for Foreign Economic Relations, and Deressa reached an agreement on economic and technical cooperation between the two countries in the form of an exchange of letters.

According to the trade agreement, the Soviet Union will export various machines and equipment, steel, medicines, petroleum products, etc., to Ethiopia. Ethiopia will export leather raw materials, peanuts, olive seeds, etc., to the Soviet Union.

According to the agreement on economic and technical cooperation, the Soviet government, in order to fulfill the wishes of the Ethiopian government, expressed its willingness to assist Ethiopia in developing its national economy. To this end, the Soviet Union guarantees to provide Ethiopia with economic and technical assistance in the construction of industrial and other projects. The expenses required for the implementation of design and exploration work, the provision of materials necessary for the construction industry and other projects from the Soviet Union to Ethiopia, and other technical assistance will be paid from the long-term loans granted by the Soviet government to the government of the Kingdom of Ethiopia on preferential terms.

Xinhua News Agency, July 13th -- TASS Moscow reported: In commemoration of the visit of Ethiopian Emperor Haile Selassie I to the Soviet Union, the Soviet government decided to establish and equip a middle school in Addis Ababa that can accommodate 1,000 students and has laboratories, a gymnasium, and a canteen, as a gift to the government of the Kingdom of Ethiopia; and decided to send a set of medical equipment to the Prince Harald Hospital in Addis Ababa, which is under construction.

The Soviet government will also give Emperor Selassie I a specially equipped "IL-14" aircraft as a personal gift.

### 苏联和埃塞俄比亚发表联合公报  苏联贷款帮助埃塞俄比亚发展经济  伏罗希洛夫和赫鲁晓夫接受邀请将访问埃塞俄比亚

1959-07-14
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和埃塞俄比亚发表联合公报
    苏联贷款帮助埃塞俄比亚发展经济
    伏罗希洛夫和赫鲁晓夫接受邀请将访问埃塞俄比亚
    新华社13日讯　塔斯社13日发表了苏联—埃塞俄比亚联合公报。
    公报说，埃塞俄比亚皇帝海尔·塞拉西一世在埃塞俄比亚王国政府高级官员的陪同下于1959年6月29日到达莫斯科，在苏联作了十四天的国事访问。他们在访问期间，参观了斯维德洛夫斯克和乌拉尔工业区、斯大林格勒，列宁伏尔加水电站、伏尔加河—顿河运河、濒临黑海的高加索和克里米亚的避暑地以及列宁格勒等地。皇帝陛下对苏联正在建设的工业企业和农业工程项目感到很大兴趣。
    皇帝及其随行人员在各地受到居民的热情和真诚的接待，各地居民对贵宾和友好的埃塞俄比亚人民表示深切的同情。
    公报说，在塞拉西一世皇帝访问期间，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫元帅授予他一枚金链的萨夫斯卡娅皇后勋章。苏联最高苏维埃主席团授与他一枚苏联最高军事勋章——苏沃洛夫一级勋章。
    塞拉西一世皇帝同苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫就两国为维护世界和平以及争取非洲人民自由和独立的合作问题进行了多次有益的会谈。两国领导人表示支持作为发展国与国之间和平关系必要条件的集体安全、不干涉和共处的原则。
    双方还举行了关于扩大苏联和埃塞俄比亚之间贸易的谈判，签订了两国的贸易和经济协定。谈判结果，苏联同意以低息给予埃塞俄比亚四亿卢布的长期贷款来发展工农业。双方还就进一步发展两国文化联系达成了协议。
    两国领导人同意支持万隆会议所制订的原则以及非洲独立国家阿克拉会议决议中所包括的原则。两国领导人对有机会亲自就互相感兴趣的问题进行讨论和交换意见表示高兴。他们确信，在他们这次谈判和友好的接触后，两国之间和两国人民之间的关系将更加巩固和发展。
    公报最后说，海尔·塞拉西一世皇帝邀请苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫元帅和苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫访问埃塞俄比亚。这个邀请已被感激地接受。访问日期将由双方磋商决定。
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联对外贸易部长帕托利切夫和埃塞俄比亚外交大臣伊勒马·德雷萨11日在莫斯科签订了苏联同埃塞俄比亚两国之间的贸易协定。和这同时，苏联部长会议国家对外经济关系委员会主席斯卡奇科夫和德雷萨以交换信件的形式就两国经济和技术合作问题达成了协议。
    根据贸易协定，苏联将向埃塞俄比亚输出各种机器和设备、钢材、药品、石油制品等。埃塞俄比亚方面将向苏联输出皮革原料、花生、橄榄籽等。
    根据关于经济和技术合作协议，苏联政府为满足埃塞俄比亚政府的愿望，表示愿意协助埃塞俄比亚发展民族经济，为此目的苏联保证在建设工业和其他项目方面给予埃塞俄比亚以经济技术援助。在执行设计勘探工作、苏联向埃塞俄比亚提供建筑工业和其他项目所必须的资料以及其他技术援助方面所需的开支，由苏联政府在优惠条件下给予埃塞俄比亚王国政府的长期贷款中支付。
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联政府为了纪念埃塞俄比亚皇帝海尔·塞拉西一世访问苏联，决定在亚的斯亚贝巴建立和装备一所能容纳一千学生、设有实验室、体育馆和食堂的中学，作为对埃塞俄比亚王国政府的赠礼；并且决定送一套医疗设备给正在建设中的亚的斯亚贝巴哈拉尔王子医院。
    苏联政府还将把一架专门装设的“伊尔—十四式”飞机送给塞拉西一世皇帝作为私人礼品。
