Title: Soviet Peace Committee Issues Statement Demanding End to Cold War and Convening of Summit Conference
Author:
Date: 1959-07-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th. TASS reports: The Soviet Peace Committee issued a statement on the 18th, demanding an end to the "Cold War" as soon as possible, and demanding that a conference of heads of government of various countries be held to resolve urgent international issues, primarily the German question.

The statement pointed out that the Soviet people firmly advocate the conclusion of a German peace treaty and the creation of conditions for ensuring European security. The Soviet people demand a universal and permanent ban on nuclear weapons testing, the establishment of nuclear-weapon-free zones, and the separation of armed forces in those areas that pose a threat to peace.

The statement pointed out that working people throughout the Soviet Union have sent resolutions, telegrams and letters to the foreign ministers participating in the Geneva Conference, expressing their conviction that for the safety of all peoples, it is necessary to take the first concrete steps on the road to reaching agreement, and it is necessary to help the upcoming summit conference of heads of government to completely resolve disputed issues.

### 苏联保卫和平委员会发表声明  要求停止冷战召开首脑会议

1959-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联保卫和平委员会发表声明
    要求停止冷战召开首脑会议
    新华社19日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联保卫和平委员会18日发表声明，要求尽快停止“冷战”，要求举行各国政府首脑会议，解决迫切的国际问题，首先是德国问题。
    声明指出，苏联人坚决主张缔结对德和约并为保障欧洲安全创造条件。苏联人民要求普遍地和永久地禁止核武器试验，要求建立无原子武器地区，要求把对和平存在着威胁的那些地区里的武装部队隔离开来。
    声明指出，苏联各地劳动人民纷纷向参加日内瓦会议的各国外交部长们发出决议、电报和信件，他们表示确信，为了各国人民的安全，必须在达成协议的道路上采取头几个具体步骤，必须帮助即将举行的政府首脑会议彻底解决争执的问题。
