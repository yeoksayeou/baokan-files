Title: Hubei's Hefeng Builds Copper Factory
Author:
Date: 1959-07-09
Page: 2

The Meiziping Copper Factory in Hefeng County, Hubei Province, was recently completed and put into production.

The factory currently has ten copper smelting furnaces, and plans to produce 250 tons of copper this year.

### 鹤峰建铜厂

1959-07-09
第2版()
专栏：

    鹤峰建铜厂
    湖北省鹤峰县梅子坪铜厂已在最近建成投入生产。
    这个厂现有十座炼铜高炉，今年计划产铜二百五十吨。
