Title: The Iraqi and Korean People Will Always Stand Together: Premier Kassem Receives Korean Government Delegation, Both Sides Emphasize Developing Economic and Cultural Cooperation

Author:
Date: 1959-07-19
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, July 17th: Iraqi Prime Minister Kassem received the Korean government delegation headed by Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Nam Il at midnight on the 16th.

Prime Minister Kassem and Vice Premier Nam Il held a lengthy discussion on issues concerning the interests of the people of both countries. Both sides emphasized the necessity of developing economic and cultural relations between the two countries.

Prime Minister Kassem praised the patriotism displayed by the Korean people and army in the war against American imperialist aggression. He assured the Korean guests that the Iraqi people will always stand with the Korean people.

Vice Premier Nam Il conveyed the congratulations of Premier Kim Il Sung, the Korean government, and the Korean people to Prime Minister Kassem, the Iraqi government, and the Iraqi people. He presented Prime Minister Kassem with a silk banner from the Korean people to the Iraqi people. Vice Premier Nam Il told Prime Minister Kassem that the delegation had personally witnessed the Iraqi people resolutely defending their revolutionary achievements. He believes that the Iraqi people's fervent revolutionary spirit and unity are the guarantee of their revolutionary victory. He assured that the Korean people will stand with the Iraqi people.

### 伊朝人民永远站在一起  卡塞姆总理接见朝鲜政府代表团  双方强调发展两国经济文化合作

1959-07-19
第3版()
专栏：

    伊朝人民永远站在一起
    卡塞姆总理接见朝鲜政府代表团
    双方强调发展两国经济文化合作
    新华社巴格达17日电　伊拉克总理卡塞姆在16日午夜，接见了以朝鲜副首相兼外务相南日为首的朝鲜政府代表团。
    卡塞姆总理和南日副首相就有关两国人民利益的问题进行了长时间的会谈。双方强调了发展经济和两国文化关系的必要性。
    卡塞姆总理赞扬了朝鲜人民和军队在反击美帝国主义侵略战争中所表现出来的爱国主义精神。他向朝鲜贵宾保证，伊拉克人民将永远同朝鲜人民站在一起。
    南日副首相向卡塞姆总理、伊拉克政府和人民转达了金日成首相、朝鲜政府和人民的祝贺。他把朝鲜人民送给伊拉克人民的锦旗交给了卡塞姆总理。南日副首相对卡塞姆总理说，代表团亲眼看到伊拉克人民在坚决保卫他们的革命成果。他认为，伊拉克人民热烈的革命精神和团结是他们取得革命胜利的保证。他保证，朝鲜人民将同伊拉克人民站在一起。
