Title: Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association and All-China Federation of Trade Unions Send Cables for "Vietnam Day" Supporting the Struggle of the Vietnamese People and Condemning the Crimes of the U.S.-Diem Clique
Author:
Date: 1959-07-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 20th - The Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association sent a telegram to the Viet-China Friendship Association on the 19th, stating that the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association and the Chinese people warmly respond to the just call of the Asian-African People's Solidarity Council to hold "Vietnam Day," resolutely supporting the Vietnamese people's opposition to imperialism and the Ngo Dinh Diem clique's continuous sabotage of the Geneva Accords, military expansion and war preparations, creation of tension, and all kinds of crimes of massacring patriotic and innocent Vietnamese people.

The telegram said that the people in various parts of China have recently held various mass rallies, enthusiastically supporting the just struggle of the Vietnamese people and angrily protesting against the reactionary crimes of U.S. imperialism and the Ngo Dinh Diem clique.

The telegram concluded by expressing firm belief that the heroic struggle of the Vietnamese people, with the support of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union and the peace-loving people of the world, will surely realize the aspiration of peaceful reunification of the motherland.

Xinhua News Agency - Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, wrote to Hoang Quoc Viet, Chairman of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, on the 17th, on the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Geneva Accords and the upcoming "Vietnam Day," warmly supporting the just struggle of the Vietnamese people for the peaceful reunification of Vietnam.

The letter said: "The Chinese people and all workers, as a signatory to the Geneva Accords, fully support the just struggle of the Vietnamese people, condemn the crimes of the Ngo Dinh Diem clique in undermining the Geneva Accords, and demand that U.S. imperialism get out of South Vietnam. We believe that the heroic Vietnamese people, with the sympathy and support of the peace-loving forces of the world, will surely smash the reactionary conspiracy of U.S. imperialism and its running dog, the Ngo Dinh Diem clique, and win the complete victory of the cause of unification."

### 中越友协和全国总工会就“越南日”发出函电  支持越南人民斗争　谴责美吴集团罪行

1959-07-21
第4版()
专栏：

    中越友协和全国总工会就“越南日”发出函电
    支持越南人民斗争　谴责美吴集团罪行
    新华社20日讯　中越友好协会19日打电报给越中友好协会，表示中越友好协会和中国人民热烈响应亚非人民团结理事会举行“越南日”的正义号召，坚决支持越南人民反对帝国主义及吴庭艳集团不断破坏日内瓦协议，扩军备战，制造紧张局势，以及屠杀越南爱国无辜人民的种种罪行。
    电报说，中国各地的人民连日纷纷举行各种群众集会，热烈地支持越南人民的这一正义斗争，愤怒地抗议美帝国主义及吴庭艳集团的反动罪行。
    电报最后表示坚信，越南人民的英勇斗争，在以苏联为首的社会主义阵营和世界和平人民的支持下，一定能够实现和平统一祖国的愿望。
    新华社讯　中华全国总工会主席刘宁一在日内瓦协议签订五周年和“越南日”即将到来的时候，17日写信给越南总工会主席黄国越，热烈支持越南人民争取越南和平统一的正义斗争。
    信中说：“作为日内瓦协议签字国的中国人民和全体职工完全支持越南人民的正义斗争，谴责吴庭艳集团破坏日内瓦协议的罪行，要求美帝国主义滚出南越去。我们相信，英勇的越南人民在全世界和平力量的同情和支持下，必将粉碎美帝国主义和它的走卒吴庭艳集团的反动阴谋，而赢得统一事业的彻底胜利。”
