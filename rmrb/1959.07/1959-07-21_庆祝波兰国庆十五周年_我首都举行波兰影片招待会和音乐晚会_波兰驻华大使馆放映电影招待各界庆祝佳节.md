Title: Celebrating the 15th Anniversary of Polish National Day: Polish Film Reception and Music Evening Held in Our Capital; Polish Embassy in China Screens Film to Entertain Various Circles in Celebration of the Festival
Author:
Date: 1959-07-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 20th. To celebrate the upcoming 15th anniversary of the National Day of the People's Republic of Poland, the Ministry of Culture, the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and the Sino-Polish Friendship Association jointly held the premiere reception of the Polish film "The Mermaid of Warsaw" this afternoon; the Sino-Polish Friendship Association and the Chinese Musicians Association held a concert in the evening to celebrate Polish National Day.

Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan and Polish Ambassador to China Kirylok attended the premiere reception of "The Mermaid of Warsaw" and delivered speeches. Also present at the reception were Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zeng Yongquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, He Chengxiang, Vice President of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association, and diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China.

In his speech, Vice Minister Xia Yan said that Polish films are generally welcomed and loved by the broad masses of the Chinese people. Since 1952, more than 30 Polish films have been screened in China, with more than 60 million viewers. The screening of these films has enhanced the fraternal friendship and cultural cooperation between the Chinese and Polish people. He warmly congratulated the further strengthening of cultural cooperation and friendship between the two peoples.

In his speech, Ambassador Kirylok introduced the vigorous development of the Polish film industry after liberation and expressed his hope that the screening of Polish films in China would enhance understanding and friendship between the two peoples. He said, "Let Polish films serve the people of the two countries by allowing them to better understand each other, and serve the two peoples who are fighting together for peace and socialism through the screening of Polish films in China and Chinese films in Poland."

At the evening concert, the Central Philharmonic Orchestra, the Central Experimental Opera House, and the Central Conservatory of Music performed works by the great Polish musician Chopin and the famous composer Moniuszko. Commercial Counselor of the Polish Embassy in China, Szymanski, and embassy officials attended today's concert.

Xinhua News Agency, July 20th. The Polish Embassy in China held a film reception at the Beijing Hotel tonight to celebrate the upcoming 15th anniversary of the National Day of the People's Republic of Poland. Heads of various departments of our government, diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China attended the reception.

The Polish feature film "Eagle", produced in 1958, was screened at the reception. It depicted the true story of how a Polish submarine heroically fought against the Hitler bandit army during World War II.

### 庆祝波兰国庆十五周年  我首都举行波兰影片招待会和音乐晚会  波兰驻华大使馆放映电影招待各界庆祝佳节

1959-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝波兰国庆十五周年
    我首都举行波兰影片招待会和音乐晚会
    波兰驻华大使馆放映电影招待各界庆祝佳节
    新华社20日讯　为庆祝即将到来的波兰人民共和国国庆十五周年，文化部、对外文化联络委员会和中国波兰友好协会今天下午联合举办波兰影片“华沙美人鱼”首次放映招待会；中波友好协会和中国音乐家协会在晚间举行了庆祝波兰国庆音乐会。
    文化部副部长夏衍和波兰驻中国大使基里洛克出席“华沙美人鱼”首映招待会并讲了话。出席招待会的，还有对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武，外交部副部长曾涌泉，中波友好协会副会长何成湘和各国驻华使节与外交官员。
    夏衍副部长在讲话中说，波兰影片普遍地受到了中国广大人民的欢迎和喜爱。从1952年以来，中国上映的波兰影片有三十多部，观众六千多万人次，这些影片的放映增进了中波两国人民的兄弟友谊和文化合作。他热烈祝贺两国人民的文化合作和友谊的进一步加强。
    基里洛克大使在讲话中介绍了解放后波兰电影事业蓬勃发展的情况，并且希望波兰影片在中国放映能增进两国人民的了解和友谊。他说，让波兰影片在中国和中国影片在波兰，为两国人民更好地了解服务，为共同为和平和社会主义而斗争的两国人民服务。
    在晚间的音乐会上，中央乐团、中央试验歌剧院和中央音乐学院表演了波兰伟大音乐家萧邦和著名作曲家孟纽什柯等人的作品。波兰驻中国大使馆商务参赞席曼斯基和大使馆官员参加了今天的音乐会。
    新华社20日讯　波兰驻中国大使馆今天晚间在北京饭店举行电影招待会，庆祝即将到来的波兰人民共和国国庆十五周年。我国政府各部门负责人、各国驻华使节与外交官员，出席了招待会。
    招待会上放映了1958年摄制的波兰故事片“雄鹰”，它描写了一艘波兰潜水艇在第二次世界大战期间如何同希特勒匪军进行英勇战斗的真实故事。
