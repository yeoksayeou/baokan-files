Title: Cambodian National Men's Basketball Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-07-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd: The Cambodian National Men's Basketball Team, a delegation of fifteen people led by team leader Sophat, arrived in Beijing by train this morning at the invitation of our country.

The visiting team will visit Beijing, Tianjin, Hangzhou, Shanghai, and Guangzhou for more than three weeks, and will hold eight friendly matches with our country's men's basketball teams. Their schedule for the three friendly matches in Beijing is set as follows: July 24th against the Chinese National Team, July 26th against the Beijing Team, and July 27th against the "August 1st" Team.

### 柬国家男子篮球队到京

1959-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

    柬国家男子篮球队到京
    据新华社22日讯　应邀来我国访问的柬埔寨王国国家男子篮球队一行十五人，由领队索帕特率领，今天清晨乘火车到达北京。
    客队这次将在北京、天津、杭州、上海、广州访问三个多星期，并同我国的男子篮球队举行八场友谊比赛。他们在北京的三场友谊比赛日程确定为：24日对中国国家队，26日对北京队，27日对“八一”队。
