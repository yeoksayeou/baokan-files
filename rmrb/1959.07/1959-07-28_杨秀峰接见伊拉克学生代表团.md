Title: Yang Xiufeng Receives Iraqi Student Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-07-28
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, July 27th. Minister of Education Yang Xiufeng received the Iraqi Republic student delegation this afternoon. Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, was present at the reception.

### 杨秀峰接见伊拉克学生代表团

1959-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    杨秀峰接见伊拉克学生代表团
    新华社27日讯　教育部长杨秀峰今天下午接见了伊拉克共和国学生代表团。接见时在座的有中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立。
