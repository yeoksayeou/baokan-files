Title: America's Budget Deficit is Alarmingly Huge; National Debt Reaches 285 Billion Dollars
Author:
Date: 1959-07-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington dispatch: The U.S. Treasury Department announced on the 20th that for the 1959 fiscal year, which ended on June 30th, the U.S. government's budget deficit was 12.5 billion dollars, an unprecedented record for a U.S. budget deficit in peacetime.

The Treasury Department's report indicates that budget expenditures for this fiscal year reached a peacetime high, totaling over 80.6 billion dollars. This figure is more than 6 billion dollars higher than the peak expenditure level during the Korean War and more than any year during World War II (except for two years). However, income was only over 68.1 billion dollars, resulting in a shortfall.

The U.S. government's huge budget deficit is mainly due to the frenzied increase in military spending. Last year, the U.S. government spent as much as 40.5 billion dollars on weapons production and expanding its military.

At the beginning of last year, U.S. President Eisenhower boasted in his budget message that the U.S. government would have a surplus of 500 million dollars at the end of the 1959 fiscal year. Now, the Treasury Department's final accounts prove that there is a huge deficit, which is tantamount to a slap in Eisenhower's face.

The July 13th issue of "U.S. News & World Report" magazine, in an article titled "Endless Deficits? What's the Outlook After a Bad Budget Year?", stated, "In the past twenty-eight years, (the U.S. government budget) has had deficits in twenty-three years and surpluses in only five years," "All indications are that the new fiscal year beginning July 1 will once again see a deficit."

The magazine also said, "Since 1931, when the first deficit appeared, the U.S. government's debt has increased from 16.2 billion dollars to nearly 285 billion dollars. At Eisenhower's urgent request, Congress has temporarily raised the national debt ceiling to 295 billion dollars. The 'permanent' ceiling is 285 billion dollars. However, this ceiling is often raised when deficits occur." (Attached chart)

### 美国预算赤字惊人庞大  国债达到二千八百五十亿美元

1959-07-29
第5版()
专栏：

    美国预算赤字惊人庞大
    国债达到二千八百五十亿美元
    据新华社讯　华盛顿消息：美国财政部20日宣布，在今年6月30日结束的1959财政年度，美国政府的预算赤字是一百二十五亿美元，这是和平时期美国预算赤字的空前纪录。
    财政部的报告表明，这一财政年度的预算开支达到和平时期支出的最高峰，达八百零六亿余美元。这个数字比进行侵略朝鲜战争时期的最高开支水平还多六十多亿美元，比第二次世界大战期间任何一年（有两年除外）的开支都要多。但是，收入只有六百八十一亿余美元，因而入不敷出。
    美国政府庞大的预算赤字主要是由于疯狂地增加军事开支造成的。在去年，美国政府用在武器生产和从事扩军活动的开支就高达四百零五亿美元。
    在去年年初的时候，美国总统艾森豪威尔在提出预算咨文时曾经吹嘘说，在1959财政年度结束时，美国政府预算将有五亿美元的盈余。现在财政部的决算却证明有庞大的赤字，这无异是给艾森豪威尔的一记耳光。
    7月13日一期的“美国新闻与世界报道”杂志在一篇题为“没有完的赤字？在预算很糟的一年后前景如何”的文章中说，“在过去二十八年中，（美国政府预算）二十三年出现赤字，只有五年有结余”，“目前一切迹象表明，7月1日开始的新财政年度还将再度出现赤字。”
    杂志还说，“自从第一次出现赤字的1931年以来，美国政府的债务已经从一百六十二亿美元增加到将近二千八百五十亿美元。根据艾森豪威尔的紧急要求，国会已经把国债暂时最高限额提高到二千九百五十亿美元。‘永久的’最高限额为二千八百五十亿美元。但是在由于产生赤字而需要时，这个最高限额经常提高。”（附图表）
