Title: Swedish and Other Countries' Women Representatives Return Home After Concluding Visit to China
Author: 
Date: 1959-07-03
Page: 5

    Swedish and Other Countries' Women Representatives Return Home After Concluding Visit to China
    Xinhua News Agency, July 2nd. The Swedish women representatives, who were invited by the All-China Women's Federation to visit China, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.
    Prior to this, the Chilean women's delegation had returned home on June 30th, and the Iraqi women's delegation had left for a visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on June 30th.

### 结束在我国访问  瑞典等国妇女代表回国

1959-07-03
第5版()
专栏：

    结束在我国访问
    瑞典等国妇女代表回国
    新华社2日讯　应全国妇女联合会邀请来我国访问的瑞典妇女代表今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    在这以前，智利妇女代表团已在6月30日回国，伊拉克妇女代表团已在6月30日去越南民主共和国访问。
