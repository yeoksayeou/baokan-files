Title: New Thermal Power Plant Built in Wenzhou
Author:
Date: 1959-07-25
Page: 2

A new thermal power plant in Wenzhou, Zhejiang Province, has been officially completed and put into production. The new power plant can generate 15 million kilowatt-hours of electricity annually.

### 温州新建一座火电厂

1959-07-25
第2版()
专栏：

    温州新建一座火电厂
    浙江省温州市一座新的火力发电厂正式建成并投入生产。新电厂每年可发出一千五百万度电。
