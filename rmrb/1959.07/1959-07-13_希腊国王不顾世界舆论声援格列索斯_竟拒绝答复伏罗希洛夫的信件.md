Title: The Greek King Disregards World Public Opinion in Support of Glezos and Refuses to Reply to Voroshilov's Letter
Author:
Date: 1959-07-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Athens, 12th—News: Greek King Paul I unreasonably refused on the 10th the request from Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, for the release of the Greek national hero Glezos.

Voroshilov wrote to Paul I on the 9th, expressing that Soviet public opinion was deeply disturbed by the threats to Glezos's life and freedom, and requested Paul I to take measures to release Glezos. Greek Deputy Foreign Minister Skouze on the 10th informed the Soviet Ambassador to Athens that Paul I could not reply to Voroshilov's letter, using the excuse that the King "does not interfere in judicial affairs."

### 希腊国王不顾世界舆论声援格列索斯  竟拒绝答复伏罗希洛夫的信件

1959-07-13
第5版()
专栏：

    希腊国王不顾世界舆论声援格列索斯
    竟拒绝答复伏罗希洛夫的信件
    新华社12日讯　雅典消息：希腊国王保罗一世10日竟然无理拒绝了苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫关于释放希腊民族英雄格列索斯的要求。
    伏罗希洛夫9日曾经写信给保罗一世，表示苏联社会舆论对格列索斯的生命和自由遭受威胁深感不安，并要求保罗一世采取措施释放格列索斯。希腊副外交大臣斯克弗里斯10日通知苏联驻雅典大使说，保罗一世不能答复伏罗希洛夫的信件，拒绝的借口是：国王“不干预司法事务”。
