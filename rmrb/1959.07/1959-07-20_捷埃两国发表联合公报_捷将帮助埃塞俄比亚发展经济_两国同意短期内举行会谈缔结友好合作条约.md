Title: Joint Communiqué Issued by Czechoslovakia and Ethiopia: Czechoslovakia to Help Ethiopia Develop its Economy; Both Countries Agree to Hold Talks in the Near Future to Conclude a Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1959-07-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 17th: A joint Czechoslovak-Ethiopian communiqué was released here on the afternoon of the 17th.

The communiqué stated that Ethiopian Emperor Haile Selassie I and Czechoslovak President Novotný discussed issues of mutual concern and issues related to safeguarding world peace and security during their talks.

The representatives of both sides reaffirmed that they would uphold the principles of collective security, non-interference, and peaceful coexistence.

Regarding economic and trade relations between Czechoslovakia and Ethiopia, the communiqué stated that the Czechoslovak government expressed its willingness to assist Ethiopia's economic development with infrastructure materials. In this regard, it has been decided that talks will be held soon to discuss the conclusion of an agreement on economic and technical cooperation between the two countries.

The communiqué said that the heads of state of the two countries agreed to hold talks in the near future on the conclusion of a treaty of friendship and cooperation and a cultural agreement.

The communiqué said that the diplomatic missions of the two countries would be upgraded to embassy level.

The communiqué also stated that President Novotný accepted the invitation from Emperor Selassie I to visit Ethiopia.

The communiqué concluded by saying that the heads of state of both countries expressed great satisfaction with the results of the Ethiopian Emperor's visit.

### 捷埃两国发表联合公报  捷将帮助埃塞俄比亚发展经济  两国同意短期内举行会谈缔结友好合作条约

1959-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    捷埃两国发表联合公报
    捷将帮助埃塞俄比亚发展经济
    两国同意短期内举行会谈缔结友好合作条约
    新华社布拉格17日电　17日下午在这里发表了捷克斯洛伐克—埃塞俄比亚联合公报。
    公报说，埃塞俄比亚皇帝海尔·塞拉西一世和捷克斯洛伐克总统诺沃提尼会谈时讨论了两国共同关心的问题以及有关维护世界和平与安全的问题。
    双方代表再一次确认，他们将拥护集体安全的原则、不干涉与和平共处的原则。
    公报在谈到捷克斯洛伐克和埃塞俄比亚之间的经济和贸易关系时说，捷克斯洛伐克政府表示愿意以基本建设的物资来帮助埃塞俄比亚的经济发展。在这方面已经决定，不久即将举行会谈来讨论缔结两国间进行经济和技术合作的协定。
    公报说，两国首脑同意在短期内举行缔结友好和合作条约以及文化协定的会谈。
    公报说，两国的外交代表机关将提升到大使馆级。
    公报又说，诺沃提尼总统接受了塞拉西一世皇帝提出的访问埃塞俄比亚的邀请。
    公报最后说，两国首脑对于埃塞俄比亚皇帝访问的结果表示非常满意。
