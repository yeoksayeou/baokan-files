Title: Soviet Consumer Cooperative Delegation Arrives in China for a Visit at Invitation

Author:

Date: 1959-07-11
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The Soviet Consumer Cooperative Delegation, headed by Kabonov, Vice Chairman of the Central Union of Soviet Consumer Cooperatives, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives.

### 苏联消费合作社代表团应邀到达我国访问

1959-07-11
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联消费合作社代表团应邀到达我国访问
    新华社10日讯　以苏联消费合作社中央联合会副主席卡巴诺夫为首的苏联消费合作社代表团一行二人，今天乘飞机到达北京。代表团是应中华全国供销合作总社的邀请来我国访问的。
