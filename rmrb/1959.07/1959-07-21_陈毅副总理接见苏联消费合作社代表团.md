Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Delegation from the Soviet Union's Consumer Cooperatives
Author:
Date: 1959-07-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 20th – Vice Premier Chen Yi received all the distinguished guests of the Soviet Union's Consumer Cooperatives delegation, headed by Kabanov, today and held a friendly conversation. Present at the meeting were Yan Guhang, Deputy Director of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives, Chen Qixiang, Director of the Commodity Exchange Bureau of the Ministry of Commerce, and others.

Sudarikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, was also present at the reception.

### 陈毅副总理接见苏联消费合作社代表团

1959-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见苏联消费合作社代表团
    新华社20日讯　陈毅副总理在今天接见了以卡巴诺夫为首的苏联消费合作社代表团全体贵宾，进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有中华全国供销合作总社副主任阎顾行、商业部商品交换局局长陈其襄等。
    接见时，苏联驻中国大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫也在座。
