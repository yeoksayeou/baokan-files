Title: Hungarian National Men's Basketball Team Defeats Shanghai Men's Basketball Team
Author:
Date: 1959-07-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai. The Hungarian National Men's Basketball Team defeated the Shanghai Men's Basketball Team, which ranked seventh in last year's National Basketball League A-League, with a score of 61 to 53 in Shanghai on the evening of the 22nd. Six thousand spectators watched this exciting game.
    The Hungarian National Men's Basketball Team arrived in Shanghai on the 21st. They will also hold a friendly match with the Sichuan Provincial Men's Basketball Team in Shanghai on the 23rd.

### 匈国家男子篮球队胜上海男子篮球队

1959-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    匈国家男子篮球队胜上海男子篮球队
    新华社上海讯　匈牙利国家男子篮球队22日晚在上海以六十一比五十三赢了去年全国篮球甲级队联赛第七名——上海市男子篮球队。有六千名观众看了这场精彩的比赛。
    匈牙利国家男子篮球队是21日到上海的。23日还将在上海同四川省男子篮球队举行一场友谊比赛。
