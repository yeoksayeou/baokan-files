Title: Soviet and Polish Leaders Hold Talks
Author: 
Date: 1959-07-22
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 21st: The Soviet Party and government delegation and the leaders of the Polish United Workers' Party held talks in Warsaw today.

    The Soviet side participating in the talks included: the head of the Soviet Party and government delegation, First Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee and Chairman of the USSR Council of Ministers, Khrushchev, and members of the delegation Firyubin, Andropov, Abrassimov and others.

    The Polish side participating in the talks included: First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party Gomulka, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party Jedrychowski, Zawadzki, Zambrowski, Morawski, Rapacki, Spychalski, Cyrankiewicz and others.

    The talks were conducted in a very sincere and friendly atmosphere.

### 苏波两国领导人举行会谈

1959-07-22
第6版()
专栏：

    苏波两国领导人举行会谈
    新华社华沙21日电　苏联党政代表团和波兰统一工人党的领导人，今天在华沙举行了会谈。
    苏联方面参加会谈的有：苏联党政代表团团长、苏共中央第一书记、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫，代表团团员费留宾、安特罗波夫、阿布拉西莫夫等人。
    波兰方面参加会谈的有：波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记哥穆尔卡，波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员英德里霍夫斯基、萨瓦茨基、萨姆布罗夫斯基、莫拉夫斯基、腊帕茨基、斯彼哈尔斯基、西伦凯维兹等人。
    会谈是在非常诚恳、友好的气氛中进行的。
