Title: Iraqi General Federation of Trade Unions Congress Closes, Calling for the Defense of the Republic and Democratic System
Author:
Date: 1959-07-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, July 11th: The first Congress of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Iraq closed here today. The congress passed several important resolutions and elected the Central Council of the Federation.

The political report presented and adopted at the congress considered that the characteristics of the current situation are the struggle of the masses to defend the republic and its democratic system, and the unity of all nationalist forces to fight against the imperialist reactionaries' conspiracy to separate the republic from the cause of democracy.

In an appeal to the workers of Iraq, the congress called on all workers to unite and support their federation; strengthen the worker-peasant alliance; safeguard the country's political and economic independence; oppose world imperialism; strive to improve working conditions; and defend world peace.

The economic report presented at the congress mentioned the consequences of British rule on the Iraqi economy.

In a resolution on foreign aid, the congress "supports the government's measures to abolish economic agreements signed with imperialist countries," and emphasized that Iraq "needs the sincere help of socialist countries, and the agreement concluded between Iraq and the Soviet Union is a vivid example of the benefits of trading with socialist countries."

The congress's resolution on participating in the National United Front stated that the Iraqi working class is part of the Iraqi people, and their interests are inseparable. The resolution stated, "We fought in the past to destroy the old regime, and we must continue to fight to defend the democratic system of the republic and the revolutionary achievements of all the people." The resolution pointed out, "A united national united front will block the path of imperialist conspiracies against our republic and maintain the unity of the people, their government, and the army."

The congress expressed its support to Prime Minister Qassem in a letter addressed to him.

The congress announced that the General Federation of Trade Unions of Iraq would join the World Federation of Trade Unions.

In an appeal to the Arab workers, the congress called on them to unite against imperialism and reactionaries. The appeal called on workers' organizations in Arab countries to establish close cooperation in this struggle.

The congress paid tribute to the heroic Cuban people and welcomed their struggle against imperialism.

Another resolution of the congress expressed support for the Chinese people's struggle to liberate Taiwan and demanded the restoration of the People's Republic of China's legitimate seat in the United Nations.

The congress sent a telegram to the Geneva Foreign Ministers' Conference, calling for a peaceful resolution of the German question, a halt to nuclear testing, and the abolition of military aggression blocs.

The congress elected the Central Council of the General Federation of Trade Unions, consisting mostly of leaders of various Iraqi trade unions.

### 伊拉克工会总联合会代表大会闭幕  号召保卫共和国和民主制度

1959-07-13
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克工会总联合会代表大会闭幕
    号召保卫共和国和民主制度
    新华社巴格达11日电　伊拉克工会总联合会第一次代表大会今天在这里闭幕。大会通过了若干重要的决议，并且选出了联合会的中央理事会。
    代表大会上发表的并通过的政治报告认为目前的局势的特点是人民群众为保卫共和国和它的民主制度而斗争，团结一切民族主义力量为反对帝国主义反动派想使共和国脱离民主事业的阴谋而斗争。
    代表大会在告伊拉克工人的呼吁书中号召全体工人团结起来支持他们的联合会；加强工农联盟；维护国家在政治上和经济上的独立；反对世界帝国主义；争取改善工作条件以及保卫世界和平。
    代表大会上所发表的经济报告提到了英国的统治对伊拉克经济造成的后果。
    在关于外援问题的决议中，代表大会“支持政府所采取的废除同帝国主义国家所签订的经济协定的措施”，并且强调指出，伊拉克“需要社会主义国家的真诚帮助，伊拉克和苏联缔结的协定是一个生动的例子，说明同社会主义国家进行贸易的好处”。
    代表大会关于参加民族统一阵线的决议说，伊拉克工人阶级是伊拉克人民的一部分，他们的利益是不可分的。决议说，“我们在过去为摧毁旧政权而斗争，我们必须继续进行斗争来保卫共和国的民主制度，保卫全体人民所取得的革命的成果。”决议指出，“联合的民族统一阵线将堵塞帝国主义者对我们共和国进行的阴谋的道路，并且保持人民和他们的政府和军队的团结。”
    代表大会在致卡塞姆总理的信中对他表示支持。
    代表大会宣布，伊拉克工会总联合会参加世界工联。
    代表大会在告阿拉伯工人的呼吁书中号召他们联合起来对付帝国主义和反动派。呼吁书号召阿拉伯国家的工人组织在这个斗争中建立紧密的合作。
    代表大会向英勇的古巴人民致敬，并且欢迎他们所进行的反对帝国主义的斗争。
    代表大会另一个决议表示支持中国人民解放台湾的斗争，并且要求恢复中华人民共和国在联合国中的合法席位。
    代表大会致电日内瓦外长会议，要求和平解决德国问题、停止核试验以及废除军事侵略集团。
    代表大会选出了工会总联合会的中央理事会，理事中大多数是伊拉克各个工会的领导人。
