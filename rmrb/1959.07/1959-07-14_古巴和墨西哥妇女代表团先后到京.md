Title: Cuban and Mexican Women's Delegations Arrive in Beijing Successively
Author:
Date: 1959-07-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, July 13th – At the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation, the Cuban women's delegation of six people headed by Violeta Casal and the Mexican women's delegation of four people headed by Marianela Mondragón Núñez arrived in Beijing today and on the 10th respectively for a visit to our country.
    The two women's delegations from Latin America were warmly welcomed by responsible personnel from the All-China Women's Federation at the airport.

### 古巴和墨西哥妇女代表团先后到京

1959-07-14
第4版()
专栏：

    古巴和墨西哥妇女代表团先后到京
    新华社13日讯　应全国妇女联合会邀请前来我国访问的以比奥莱塔·卡萨耳为首的古巴妇女代表团一行六人和以玛里亚内拉·蒙德拉贡·努涅斯为首的墨西哥妇女代表团一行四人已于今天和十日分别到达北京。
    来自拉丁美洲的这两个妇女代表团在机场受到了全国妇联负责人员的热烈欢迎。
