Title: Polish Ambassador to China Hosts Reception for Iraqi Special Representative
Author:
Date: 1959-07-19
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Polish Ambassador to China, Jerzy Knothe, hosted a reception this afternoon in honor of the Special Representative of the Republic of Iraq, Aziz Sharif. Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of our country, attended the reception. Also present at the reception were diplomatic envoys from various countries to China.

### 波兰驻华大使举行酒会  招待伊拉克特别代表

1959-07-19
第3版()
专栏：

    波兰驻华大使举行酒会
    招待伊拉克特别代表
    新华社18日讯　波兰驻华大使基里洛克今天下午举行酒会，招待伊拉克共和国特别代表阿齐兹·谢里夫。我国外交部副部长姬鹏飞出席了酒会。出席酒会的还有各国驻华使节。
