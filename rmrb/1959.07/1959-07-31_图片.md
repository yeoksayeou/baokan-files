      The "Five-Good" movement has promoted an upsurge in military training to strive for "specialization in one area with competence in multiple skills, and one soldier with multiple uses." This is a picture of a pilot from a certain air force unit learning aircraft maintenance work and the structural principles of special equipment from a mechanic.
