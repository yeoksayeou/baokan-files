Title: Kozlov Meets with U.S. President, Discusses Peace Issues and Geneva Conference
Author:
Date: 1959-07-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, June 2nd -- TASS reports: Kozlov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, visited U.S. President Eisenhower at the White House on July 1st and held talks. He was accompanied by the Soviet Ambassador to the United States, Menshikov, and the Director of the American Department of the Soviet Foreign Ministry, Soldatov. The U.S. side participating in the talks included U.S. Secretary of State Herter.
    The talks lasted for an hour and a half. Kozlov told reporters after the talks that he and Eisenhower discussed various issues. He said,
    "Among the various issues we discussed are important peace issues, and I believe that world peace will be guaranteed, and here we are referring to a lasting and reliable peace."
    Prior to this, Kozlov visited U.S. Secretary of State Herter at the State Department on the morning of July 1st. Kozlov told reporters after the meeting: "During my meeting with Mr. Herter, we exchanged views on our country's attitude towards the issues discussed at the Geneva Conference."

### 科兹洛夫同美国总统会谈  讨论和平问题和日内瓦会议等问题

1959-07-03
第5版()
专栏：

    科兹洛夫同美国总统会谈
    讨论和平问题和日内瓦会议等问题
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社纽约讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席科兹洛夫1日在白宫访问了美国总统艾森豪威尔并举行了会谈。陪同他前往的有苏联驻美国大使缅希科夫，苏联外交部美国司司长索尔达托夫。美国方面参加会谈的有美国国务卿赫脱。
    会谈进行了一个半小时。科兹洛夫在会谈后告诉记者说，他和艾森豪威尔讨论了各种问题。他说，
  “在我们讨论的各种问题中有重要的和平问题，我认为，世界上的和平将得到保证，这里所指的是一种持久的和可靠的和平。”
    在这以前，科兹洛夫1日早上在国务院访问了美国国务卿赫脱。科兹洛夫在会谈后告诉记者说：“在我同赫脱先生会谈的时候，我们就我们国家对日内瓦会议所讨论的问题的态度交换了意见。”
