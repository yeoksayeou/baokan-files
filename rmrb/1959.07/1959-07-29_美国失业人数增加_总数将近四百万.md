Title: US Unemployment Rises, Totaling Nearly Four Million

Author: 
Date: 1959-07-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington dispatch: According to a report from the U.S. Department of Labor, the number of employed people in the United States has increased by 1,326,000 since May, bringing the total number of employed people to 67,342,000.

However, the Labor Department's report also pointed out that due to a large number of students graduating, the number of unemployed people in the United States increased by 593,000 in June.

Currently, there are 3,982,000 unemployed people in the United States, accounting for 4.9% of the civilian labor force. (Attached picture)

“Your numbers are up, but I'm still unemployed.”
(Originally published in the American "People's World")

### 美国失业人数增加  总数将近四百万

1959-07-29
第5版()
专栏：

    美国失业人数增加
    总数将近四百万
    新华社讯华盛顿消息：据美国劳工部报告说，美国5月份以来就业人数增加了一百三十二万六千人，就业人数总数为六千七百三十四万二千人。
    但是劳工部的报告又指出，由于大批学生毕业，6月份美国失业人数增加了五十九万三千人。
    目前美国全国有三百九十八万二千人失业，占平民劳动力的4.9％。（附图片）
    “你的数字是上升了，可是我还在失业”。
    （原载美国“人民世界报”）
