Title: Chiang Kai-shek Troops entrenched on Matsu Islands Shell Our Fishing Boats; Our Artillery Retaliated as a Warning

Author:

Date: 1959-07-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, July 27th: At 06:40 on July 27th, Chiang Kai-shek troops entrenched on the Matsu Islands shelled fishing boats operating in Huangqi Bay, firing over ten rounds. At 07:00, the People's Liberation Army artillery units stationed on the Huangqi Peninsula retaliated appropriately against the Matsu-based Chiang Kai-shek artillery that shelled our fishing boats, as a warning.

### 盘据马祖蒋军炮击我渔船  我炮兵给予还击以示警告

1959-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

    盘据马祖蒋军炮击我渔船
    我炮兵给予还击以示警告
    新华社福建前线27日电　27日六时四十分，盘据在马祖列岛的蒋军，向在黄歧湾打鱼的渔船炮击十余发；我驻防黄歧半岛的解放军炮兵部队于七时对炮击我渔船的马祖蒋军炮兵，给予了适当的还击，以示警告。
