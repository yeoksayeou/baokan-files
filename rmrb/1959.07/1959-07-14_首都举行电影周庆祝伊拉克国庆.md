Title: Capital Holds Film Week to Celebrate Iraqi National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-07-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 13th: Today, on the eve of Iraqi National Day, the opening ceremony of Iraqi Film Week was held in Beijing. In accordance with the Sino-Iraqi Cultural Agreement, the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China and the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, in order to celebrate the first anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Iraq, will hold Iraqi Film Week in the four major cities of Beijing, Shanghai, Shenyang, and Wuhan from July 14th to 20th, screening the feature film "Neighbor," which reflects the life of the Iraqi people before the revolution, and four documentaries: "In the Name of the People," "River of Time," "The North is Paradise," and "Orange Picking Song."

    At today's opening ceremony, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, delivered the opening speech. Aziz Sharif, Special Representative of the Republic of Iraq, and Hadi, manager of the Iraqi Art Federation Film Company and actor and producer of the film "Neighbor," also spoke.

    In his speech, Xia Yan warmly congratulated the great achievements made by the Republic of Iraq in the past year and congratulated the success of the Iraqi Film Week. He said: "The Iraqi Film Week" is of great significance at this time, it shows the increasing friendship and cultural exchanges between the peoples of China and Iraq. Soon, a Chinese Film Week will also be held in Iraq. The exchange of films between China and Iraq will promote the further development of cultural relations and friendly unity between the two peoples.

    Aziz Sharif said: The cultural cooperation between our two peoples has a long history. Our two peoples have made contributions to restoring their freedom and to world peace. This "Iraqi Film Week" held in China is the beginning of broader and deeper cultural cooperation between China and Iraq, and will further strengthen cooperation between the two countries in culture and other aspects, contributing to world peace.

    Also attending the opening ceremony of the "Iraqi Film Week" were Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Ding Xilin, Deputy Director, Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Liu Ningyi, President of the China-Iraq Friendship Association, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China. After the meeting, the films "Neighbor" and "In the Name of the People" were screened.

### 首都举行电影周庆祝伊拉克国庆

1959-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

    首都举行电影周庆祝伊拉克国庆
    据新华社13日讯　今天，在伊拉克国庆节前夕，首都北京举行了伊拉克电影周的开幕式。中华人民共和国文化部和对外文化联络委员会根据中伊文化协定，为了庆祝伊拉克共和国诞生一周年，自7月14日至20日在北京、上海、沈阳、武汉四大城市举办伊拉克共和国电影周，将上映反映革命前伊拉克人民生活情景的故事片“邻居”和四部纪录片“在人民的名义下”、“时代的河流”、“北部是天堂”、“采橙歌”。
    在今天的开幕式上，文化部副部长夏衍致开幕词，伊拉克共和国特别代表阿齐兹·谢里夫和伊拉克艺联影片公司经理、影片“邻居”的演员和制片人哈迪都讲了话。
    夏衍在致词中热烈庆贺伊拉克共和国一年来所取得的伟大成就，庆贺伊拉克电影周的成功。他说：“伊拉克共和国电影周”在这个时候举行是具有重要意义的，它显示了中、伊两国人民间的友谊和文化交流的日益增长。不久在伊拉克也将举行中国的电影周，中、伊电影的交流，将会促使两国人民间的文化关系和友好团结的进一步发展。
    阿齐兹·谢里夫讲话说：我们两国人民之间的文化合作，有着悠久的历史。我们两国人民都为恢复自己的自由，为世界和平做出了贡献。这次在中国举行的“伊拉克共和国电影周”，是中、伊两国更广泛更深入的文化合作的开始，今后将进一步加强两国在文化及各方面的合作。为世界和平贡献自己的力量。
    参加“伊拉克共和国电影周”开幕式的还有对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若、副主任丁西林、政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉、外交部副部长姬鹏飞、中国伊拉克友好协会会长刘宁一和各国驻华使节等。会后放映了影片“邻居”和“在人民的名义下”。
