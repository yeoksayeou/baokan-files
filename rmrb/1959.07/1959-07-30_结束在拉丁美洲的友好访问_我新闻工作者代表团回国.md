Title: Our Journalists Delegation Returns Home After Concluding Friendly Visit to Latin America
Author:
Date: 1959-07-30
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 28th — The Chinese journalists delegation led by Yao Zhen concluded its friendly visits to Cuba and other Latin American countries and flew back home today.
    The delegation paid a courtesy call yesterday on Juan Marinello, Chairman of the Cuban People's Socialist Party, who recently visited China. Earlier, the delegation attended a reception held by the Latin American News Agency for foreign journalists.
    The delegation visited Brazil, Chile, Uruguay, and Cuba in Latin America. Upon leaving Cuba, they sent letters to the news associations of these countries, thanking them for their warm hospitality.

### 结束在拉丁美洲的友好访问  我新闻工作者代表团回国

1959-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

    结束在拉丁美洲的友好访问
    我新闻工作者代表团回国
    新华社哈瓦那28日电　以姚溱为首的中国新闻工作者代表团结束了在古巴和拉丁美洲其他国家的友好访问，今天乘飞机回国。
    代表团昨天拜会了最近曾访问中国的古巴人民社会党主席胡安·马里内略。在早些时候，这个代表团参加了拉丁美洲通讯社为外国记者举行的招待会。
    代表团在拉丁美洲访问了巴西、智利、乌拉圭和古巴等国。他们在离开古巴时，曾分别致函这些国家的新闻协会，感谢对他们的热情招待。
