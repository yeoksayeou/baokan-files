Title: Nepalese Buddhist Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-07-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 24th. The Buddhist delegation from the Kingdom of Nepal, invited by the Buddhist Association of China to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon from Kunming. The head of the delegation is Ganlushi, President of the Buddhist Revival Association of the Kingdom of Nepal and Vice President of the World Fellowship of Buddhists.

The delegation was welcomed at the Capital Airport by Zhao Puchu, Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China, Zhou Shujia, Vice President of the China-Nepal Friendship Association, Juzan, Vice President of the Chinese Buddhist Academy, Yu Zheng, Secretary-General of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council, as well as more than one hundred monks, lamas, and lay Buddhists.

### 尼泊尔佛教代表团到京

1959-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔佛教代表团到京
    新华社24日讯　应中国佛教协会邀请来中国访问的尼泊尔王国佛教代表团一行六人，今天下午乘飞机从昆明到达北京。代表团团长是尼泊尔王国佛教复兴会会长、世界佛教徒联谊会副主席甘露喜。
    代表团在首都机场受到了中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初、中国尼泊尔友好协会副会长周叔迦、中国佛学院副院长巨赞、国务院宗教事务局秘书长余峥，以及僧众、喇嘛、居士等一百多人的欢迎。
