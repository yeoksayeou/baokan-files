Readers, Authors, Editors

    Photographs Lacking Ideological Content
    The People's Daily published twelve photographs of universities and colleges on June 11th, page six. Only one of them depicted the students' learning life. The remaining photos almost exclusively showcased magnificent buildings and beautiful campus scenery. We feel that these photos occupied a lot of space but lacked ideological content. They also lack educational and guiding significance for students applying to universities and colleges.

     缺乏思想性的照片
     6月11日人民日报第六版登载了十二幅高等学校的照片。其中只有一幅是介绍学生的学习生活的。其余照片所展示出的几乎都是一些巍峨的建筑物和美丽的校景。我们觉得这些照片占用了很大篇幅，但缺乏思想性。对于投考高等学校的学生来说，也缺乏教育和指导意义。　李杰毅　徐寿椿　何世昌
