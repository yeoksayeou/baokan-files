Title: Fuzhou Light Bulb Factory Construction Completed
Author:
Date: 1959-07-28
Page: 2

Fuzhou Light Bulb Factory Construction Completed

(Our dispatch) The construction of the Fuzhou Light Bulb Factory has recently been completed.

The Fuzhou Light Bulb Factory is a modern glass factory. Once fully operational, it will be able to produce 1,100 tons of glass blanks, 11 million light bulbs, 300 tons of chemical instruments, and 300 tons of various glassware annually.

### 福州灯泡厂土建完成

1959-07-28
第2版()
专栏：

    福州灯泡厂土建完成
    本报讯　福州灯泡厂的土建工程，已在最近完成。
    福州灯泡厂是一座现代化的玻璃工厂，全面投入生产后，每年可生产玻璃坯一千一百吨、灯泡一千一百万只、化学仪器三百吨和各种玻璃器皿三百吨。
