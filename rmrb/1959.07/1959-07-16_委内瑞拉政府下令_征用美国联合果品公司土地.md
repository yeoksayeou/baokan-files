Title: Venezuelan Government Orders Expropriation of United Fruit Company Land
Author:
Date: 1959-07-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, July 14th: According to a Latin American News Agency report, the Venezuelan government yesterday ordered the expropriation of 10,000 hectares of idle land in Zulia state belonging to the American United Fruit Company. The report said that Carlos Andrés Pérez and Arístides Béa Húm, members of the House of Representatives' Agricultural Committee, will go to Zulia state today to conduct a land survey and draft a report on the expropriation of land for public use.

### 委内瑞拉政府下令  征用美国联合果品公司土地

1959-07-16
第5版()
专栏：

    委内瑞拉政府下令
    征用美国联合果品公司土地
    新华社哈瓦那14日电　据拉丁美洲通讯社报道：委内瑞拉政府昨天下令征用美国联合果品公司所占有的在苏里亚州的一万公顷闲置土地。消息说，众议院农业委员会的委员卡洛斯·安德烈斯·佩雷兹和阿里斯蒂德斯·贝亚珲今日将赴苏里亚州去进行土地调查，并草拟一份因公使用而实行征用土地的报告。
