Title: People of Various Countries Voice Support for the Struggle of the Vietnamese People; Soviet Asian-African Solidarity Committee Issues Statement; Grotewohl Sends Telegram to Premier Pham Van Dong; Rallies Held in New Delhi and Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1959-07-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, July 21st -- TASS reports: The Soviet Asian-African Solidarity Committee issued a statement on the 20th, expressing support for the just struggle of the Vietnamese people against the policy of perpetual division of Vietnam pursued by Ngo Dinh Diem and his ruling clique.

The statement demanded that the American aggressive forces immediately cease interfering in the affairs of Indochina.

The statement pointed out that the Soviet people, together with the peoples of Asian and African countries, demand the restoration of Vietnamese unity and the implementation of the Geneva Accords.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 20th -- The Premier of the German Democratic Republic, Grotewohl, sent a telegram today to the Premier of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Pham Van Dong, supporting the struggle of the Vietnamese people.

The telegram said that the government and people of the German Democratic Republic firmly condemn American imperialism and the Ngo Dinh Diem clique for violating the Geneva Accords, and condemn their plans to turn South Vietnam into a military base for attacking the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and for carrying out atomic armament.

The telegram said that the Democratic Republic of Vietnam will always receive the fraternal support of the German Democratic Republic in its struggle.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 20th -- A rally celebrating the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Geneva Accords was held here today. The rally was hosted by the Delhi State Peace Council. The rally passed a resolution demanding the implementation of the Geneva Accords, demanding the withdrawal of all foreign military advisors from Indochina, and the elimination of foreign bases there.

The rally paid warm tribute to the Vietnamese people who are striving to implement the Geneva Accords, peacefully unify their country, and defend Indochina and world peace.

Those attending the rally included Sundarlal, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, and representatives of peace activists.

The resolution expressed regret "at the attitude of the South Vietnamese government in persistently refusing to discuss reunification and election issues over the past five years."

The resolution believes that the International Commissions for Laos and Vietnam should convene immediately to take measures to implement the Geneva Accords, and demands that the South Vietnamese government and the Lao government fully cooperate with the International Commissions.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, July 20th -- Representatives of the Burmese World Peace Council, as well as representatives of Burmese workers', peasants', women's organizations, and various political parties, unanimously passed a resolution at a general meeting this evening, demanding that American military personnel withdraw from South Vietnam, implement the Geneva Accords, and achieve the reunification of Vietnam through free and fair elections.

### 各国人民声援越南人民的斗争  苏联亚非团结委员会发表声明　格罗提渥  致电范文同总理　新德里和仰光举行集会

1959-07-22
第6版()
专栏：

    各国人民声援越南人民的斗争
    苏联亚非团结委员会发表声明　格罗提渥
    致电范文同总理　新德里和仰光举行集会
    新华社21日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联亚非团结委员会20日发表声明，声援越南人民为反对吴庭艳及其统治集团所实行的永远分裂越南的政策而进行的正义斗争。
    声明中要求美国侵略势力立即停止干涉印度支那事务。
    声明指出，苏联人民同亚非各国人民一同要求恢复越南统一和履行日内瓦协议。
    新华社柏林20电　德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥今天打电报给越南民主共和国总理范文同，支持越南人民的斗争。
    电报说，德意志民主共和国政府和人民坚决谴责美帝国主义和吴庭艳集团破坏日内瓦协议，谴责他们把南越变为进攻越南民主共和国的军事基地和进行原子武装的计划。
    电报说，越南民主共和国在斗争中，将永远得到德意志民主共和国和兄弟般的支持。
    新华社新德里20日电　今天这里举行了一个庆祝日内瓦协议签订五周年的集会。集会是由德里邦和平理事会主持的。集会通过决议，要求贯彻日内瓦协议，要求所有外国军事顾问撤离印度支那和取消那里的外国基地。
    集会向正在争取贯彻日内瓦协议，和平统一他们的国家和保卫印度支那及世界和平的越南人民致以热烈的敬意。
    出席集会的有全印和平理事会主席森德拉尔及和平人士的代表。
    决议“对南越政府在过去五年坚持拒绝讨论重新统一和选举问题的态度”表示遗憾。
    决议认为，老挝和越南国际委员会应该立即举行会议采取措施来贯彻日内瓦协议，并且要求南越政府和老挝政府同国际委员会充分合作。
    新华社仰光20日电　缅甸世界和平理事会代表以及缅甸各工农组织、妇女组织和各政党的代表今天晚上在一次大会上一致通过决议，要求美国军事人员撤出南越，履行日内瓦协议，并且通过自由和公平的大选来取得越南的重新统一。
