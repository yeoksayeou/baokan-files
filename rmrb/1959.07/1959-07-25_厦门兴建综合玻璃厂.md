Title: Xiamen to Build Comprehensive Glass Factory
Author:
Date: 1959-07-25
Page: 2

    Xiamen to Build Comprehensive Glass Factory
    Construction has begun on the large-scale Xiamen Comprehensive Glass Factory.
    Once fully operational, the factory will produce 1 million cases of plate glass, 2,000 tons of fiberglass, 1,000 tons of fiberglass reinforced plastic, 15,000 tons of glass bottles, and 20,000 tons of glass tubes annually.

### 厦门兴建综合玻璃厂

1959-07-25
第2版()
专栏：

    厦门兴建综合玻璃厂
    规模较大的厦门综合玻璃厂已经破土动工。
    这个厂全面投入生产后，将年产平板玻璃一百万箱、玻璃纤维二千吨、玻璃钢一千吨、玻璃瓶一万五千吨、玻璃管两万吨。
