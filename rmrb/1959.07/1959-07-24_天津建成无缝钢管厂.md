Title: Tianjin Builds Seamless Steel Tube Plant
Author:
Date: 1959-07-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, July 23rd: Tianjin's first seamless steel tube plant has been completed ahead of schedule, and some equipment has already been put into production.

The designed capacity of the Tianjin Seamless Steel Tube Plant is 15,000 to 20,000 tons of seamless steel tubes per year. Its first phase of construction was completed last year. The second phase of construction began in June this year and was originally scheduled for completion at the end of July; it was finally completed fifteen days ahead of schedule.

### 天津建成无缝钢管厂

1959-07-24
第2版()
专栏：

    天津建成无缝钢管厂
    据新华社天津23日电　天津市第一座无缝钢管厂提前建成，部分设备已经投入生产。
    天津市无缝钢管厂的设计能力，是年产无缝钢管一万五千吨到二万吨。它的第一期工程是去年完工的。第二期工程在今年6月份开工，原定在7月底完工；最后提前十五天就完成了施工任务。
