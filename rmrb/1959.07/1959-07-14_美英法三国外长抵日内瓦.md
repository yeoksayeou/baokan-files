Title: US, British and French Foreign Ministers Arrive in Geneva
Author:
Date: 1959-07-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 13th: U.S. Secretary of State Herter, British Foreign Secretary Lloyd, and French Foreign Minister Couve de Murville arrived in Geneva on the 12th to attend the foreign ministers' conference reconvening today.

	Upon arriving in Geneva, Herter said at the airport that the discussions among the foreign ministers before the recess had already shown factors that could lead to an agreement on specific arrangements for West Berlin. However, he also said that he did not come to Geneva with high hopes.

	British Foreign Secretary Lloyd said he was pleased to be back in Geneva for the work of the foreign ministers' conference.

	He said, "I have always believed that it is possible to reach an agreement on certain issues. This is what I still believe."

	That evening, the Western foreign ministers held a meeting at the French delegation's residence to coordinate their positions. In addition to the U.S., British, and French foreign ministers, West German Foreign Minister Brentano and Italian Foreign Minister Pella also attended the meeting.

### 美英法三国外长抵日内瓦

1959-07-14
第4版()
专栏：

    美英法三国外长抵日内瓦
    新华社13日讯　日内瓦消息：美国国务卿赫脱、英国外交大臣劳埃德和法国外交部长德姆维尔在12日分别抵达日内瓦，参加今天复会的外长会议。
    赫脱在到达日内瓦时在机场发表谈话说，外长们在休会前的讨论中已经显示出有可能就西柏林的具体安排达成协议的因素。但是他又说，他到日内瓦来并没有怀着很高的希望。
    英国外交大臣劳埃德对能够重新回到日内瓦进行外长会议的工作表示高兴。
    他说，“我始终相信就某些问题达成协议是可能的。这是我仍然相信的事情”。
    当天晚间，西方外长们在法国代表团住所举行了协调他们的阵线的会谈。参加会谈的除美、英、法外长外，还有西德外交部长勃伦塔诺和意大利外交部长贝拉。
