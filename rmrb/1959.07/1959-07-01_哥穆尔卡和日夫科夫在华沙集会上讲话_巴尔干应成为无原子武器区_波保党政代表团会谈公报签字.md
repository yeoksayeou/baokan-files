Title: Gomulka and Zhivkov Speak at Warsaw Rally; Balkans Should Become a Nuclear-Free Zone; Signing of Communiqué of Talks Between Polish and Bulgarian Party and Government Delegations
Author:
Date: 1959-07-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 29th - The working people of Warsaw held a meeting this afternoon at the Palace of Culture and Science with the Bulgarian Party and government delegation. Participating in the meeting were Polish Party and state leaders Gomulka, Zawadzki, Cyrankiewicz, and others, as well as three to four thousand people from all walks of life in Warsaw.
    Gomulka spoke at the meeting. In his speech, he emphasized the great achievements of the Polish and Bulgarian peoples in socialist construction and the great significance of the friendly cooperation between the Polish and Bulgarian peoples and the entire socialist camp.
    Speaking about the international situation, Gomulka strongly condemned Adenauer's war policy. He said that we fully support the Soviet Union's proposal to conclude a peace treaty with Germany.
    He continued: We support all the efforts and proposals of the Soviet Union and other socialist countries against the establishment of nuclear weapons and rocket bases in the Balkans, and we support the proposal of the Romanian government to hold a Balkan conference for the common security of this region.
    Zhivkov, head of the Bulgarian Party and government delegation, also spoke at the meeting. He said that the socialist camp is the decisive factor in defending peace and stopping warmongers and aggressors, and it is the force capable of crushing the war attempts of the imperialist aggressive bloc.
    Speaking about the international situation, Zhivkov expressed full support for the Soviet Union's realistic policy of eliminating the danger of war and rationally resolving the Berlin and German questions, and support for the constructive proposals put forward by the Soviet Union at the Geneva Foreign Ministers' Conference. He said that the Balkans should become a zone of peace, and we fully support the Soviet Union's proposal that the Balkan and Adriatic Sea region become a nuclear-free zone.
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 29th - The communiqué of the talks between the Party and government delegations of the Polish People's Republic and the Bulgarian People's Republic was signed today at the Belweder Palace in Warsaw. The communiqué was signed by Gomulka, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic, and Zhivkov, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, and Yugov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Bulgarian People's Republic.
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 30th - The Bulgarian Party and government delegation headed by Zhivkov, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and member the Presidum of the Bulgarian National Assembly, left Warsaw by special train at noon today to return home after a six-day friendly visit to Poland.

### 哥穆尔卡和日夫科夫在华沙集会上讲话  巴尔干应成为无原子武器区  波保党政代表团会谈公报签字

1959-07-01
第4版()
专栏：

    哥穆尔卡和日夫科夫在华沙集会上讲话
    巴尔干应成为无原子武器区
    波保党政代表团会谈公报签字
    新华社华沙29日电　华沙劳动人民今天下午在科学文化宫和保加利亚党政代表团举行了会见大会。参加会见的有波兰党和国家领导人哥穆尔卡、萨瓦茨基、西伦凯维兹等及华沙各界人民三四千人。
    哥穆尔卡在会上讲了话。他在讲话中强调指出了波兰和保加利亚两国人民在社会主义建设中所获得的巨大成就和波保两国人民以及整个社会主义阵营友好合作的巨大意义。
    在谈到国际局势时，哥穆尔卡严厉地谴责了阿登纳的战争政策。他说，我们完全支持苏联关于缔结对德和约的建议。
    他接着说：我们支持苏联和其它反对在巴尔干建立原子武器和火箭基地的社会主义国家的一切努力和建议，我们支持罗马尼亚政府所提出的为了这一地区的共同安全召开巴尔干会议的建议。
    保加利亚党政代表团团长日夫科夫也在会上讲了话。他说，社会主义阵营是保卫和平制止战争挑拨者和侵略者的决定性因素，是能够粉碎帝国主义侵略集团战争企图的力量。
    在谈到国际局势时，日夫科夫表示完全支持苏联关于消除战争危险和合理解决柏林和德国问题的现实性的政策，支持苏联在日内瓦外长会议上提出的建设性的建议。他说，巴尔干应当成为和平地区，我们完全支持苏联关于巴尔干和亚得里亚海地区成为无原子区的建议。
    新华社华沙29日电　波兰人民共和国和保加利亚人民共和国党政代表团会谈公报今天在华沙贝尔维德宫签字。在公报上签字的有波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记哥穆尔卡，波兰人民共和国部长会议主席西伦凯维兹和保加利亚共产党中央委员会第一书记日夫科夫，保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席于哥夫。
    新华社华沙30日电　以保加利亚共产党中央委员会第一书记、保加利亚国民议会主席团委员日夫科夫为首的保加利亚党政代表团在波兰进行了为期六天的友好访问后，今天中午乘专车离开华沙返国。
