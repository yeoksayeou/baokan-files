Title: Hungarian National Men's Basketball Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-07-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The Hungarian National Men's Basketball Team, consisting of nineteen members and led by team leader Kovacs, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The team includes two tall players measuring two meters and two meters five centimeters in height, and they were warmly welcomed by Chinese male basketball players.
    This is a return visit by the Hungarian National Men's Basketball Team following the Chinese Men's Basketball Team's visit to Hungary in April this year. Their first friendly match with the Chinese Men's Basketball Team is scheduled to be held in Beijing on the 13th.

### 匈国家男子篮球队到京

1959-07-11
第4版()
专栏：

    匈国家男子篮球队到京
    新华社10日讯　拥有身高二米和二米零五两名长人的匈牙利国家男子篮球队一行十九人，今天由领队科瓦茨率领乘飞机到达北京时，受到了我国男子篮球运动员们的热烈欢迎。
    这是中国男子篮球队今年4月间访问匈牙利后，匈牙利国家男子篮球队的答拜访问。这次，他们同中国男子篮球队的第一场友谊比赛，预定13日在北京举行。
