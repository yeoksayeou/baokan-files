Title: Khrushchev Receives Polish Agricultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-07-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 9th: According to TASS, Soviet Premier and First Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee, Khrushchev, received the Polish agricultural delegation led by Ignar, Vice-Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers and Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Polish United Peasants' Party, who are currently visiting the Soviet Union, on the 8th. Khrushchev also met with Farkas, Chairman of the Szolnok Agricultural Production Cooperative of Hungary, and Moros, head of a Hungarian machine tractor station.

Khrushchev invited the guests to lunch. In a warm and friendly conversation, Khrushchev spoke about some of the results of implementing the resolutions of the 21st Congress of the CPSU, and also shared his own views on agricultural development with the guests.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见波农业代表团

1959-07-11
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见波农业代表团
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席、苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫八日接见了正在苏联访问的、以波兰部长会议副主席、波兰统一农民党中央委员会主席团主席伊格纳尔为首的波兰农业代表团。赫鲁晓夫同时还接见了匈牙利肖兰农业生产合作社主席法尔卡什和匈牙利一个机器拖拉机站站长莫罗什。
    赫鲁晓夫邀请客人们共进午餐。在热情和友好的交谈中，赫鲁晓夫讲述了执行苏共第二十一次代表大会决议的一些结果，并且向客人们谈到他自己对农业发展的看法。
