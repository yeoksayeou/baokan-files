Title: My First Ambassador to Sudan Arrives in Khartoum
Author:
Date: 1959-07-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 16th—Wang Yutian, China's first ambassador to Sudan, arrived in Khartoum, the capital of Sudan, on the evening of the 15th. The Director and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Sudanese Ministry of Foreign Affairs went to the airport to welcome him. Diplomatic envoys from socialist countries to Sudan also went to the airport to welcome him.

### 我首任驻苏丹大使到喀土穆

1959-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

    我首任驻苏丹大使到喀土穆
    新华社开罗16日电　中国驻苏丹首任大使王雨田15日晚间到达苏丹首都喀土穆。苏丹外交部礼宾司司长和副司长曾前往机场欢迎。前往机场欢迎的还有社会主义国家驻苏丹的外交使节。
