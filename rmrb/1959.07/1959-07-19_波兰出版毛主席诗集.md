Title: Polish Publication of Mao Zedong's Poetry Collection
Author:
Date: 1959-07-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 17th: The Polish National Publishing House recently published a collection of Mao Zedong's poems. The collection includes sixteen of Mao Zedong's poems. The translators of the poems are the famous Polish poets and sinologists, Jabłoński and Wojtasiewicz, among others. The poetry collection includes illustrations.

### 波兰出版毛主席诗集

1959-07-19
第3版()
专栏：

    波兰出版毛主席诗集
    新华社华沙17日电　波兰国家出版社最近出版了毛泽东诗集。诗集共收入了毛泽东的十六篇诗作。诗作的译者是波兰著名诗人和汉学家雅布翁斯基和沃伊塔希维奇等人。诗集中附有插图。
