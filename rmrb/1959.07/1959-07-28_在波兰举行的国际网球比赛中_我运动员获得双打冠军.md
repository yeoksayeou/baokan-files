Title: Our Athletes Win Doubles Championship in International Tennis Tournament in Poland
Author:
Date: 1959-07-28
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 26th: Chinese tennis players Mei Fuji and Zhu Zhenhua won the men's doubles championship at the international tennis tournament held in Sopot, Poland. This international competition started on the 22nd and ended on the 26th. Seventy athletes from eight countries including China, Poland, Hungary, Bulgaria, Romania, the German Democratic Republic, Australia and Yugoslavia participated in the competition. The competition included five events: men's and women's singles, men's and women's doubles, and mixed doubles.

### 在波兰举行的国际网球比赛中  我运动员获得双打冠军

1959-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    在波兰举行的国际网球比赛中
    我运动员获得双打冠军
    新华社华沙26日电　中国网球运动员梅福基和朱振华在波兰索波特举行的国际网球比赛中获得男子双打冠军。这次国际比赛是从22日开始的，26日结束。参加比赛的有中国、波兰、匈牙利、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、德意志民主共和国、澳大利亚和南斯拉夫等八个国家的七十名选手。比赛项目有男、女单打，男、女双打，混合双打五项。
