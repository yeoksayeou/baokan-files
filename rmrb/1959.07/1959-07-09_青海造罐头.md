Title: Qinghai Produces Canned Goods
Author:
Date: 1959-07-09
Page: 2

    Qinghai Province recently produced its first batch of canned goods. These are products of the Qinghai Provincial Commercial Department's Xining Refrigeration Plant's affiliated canning experimental factory, which has just been put into production. The factory can produce one ton of steamed fish, oil-soaked fish, and braised fish per day.

### 青海造罐头

1959-07-09
第2版()
专栏：

    青海造罐头
    青海高原最近生产出第一批罐头。这是刚投入生产的青海省商业厅西宁冷藏库附属罐头试验厂的产品。这个厂每天能生产清蒸鱼、油浸鱼和红烧鱼等一吨。
