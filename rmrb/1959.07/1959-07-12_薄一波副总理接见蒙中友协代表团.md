Title: Vice Premier Bo Yibo Receives Delegation from the Mongolia-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1959-07-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 11th - Vice Premier Bo Yibo received the delegation from the Mongolia-China Friendship Association headed by Professor Nachukdorj, Vice Chairman of the Mongolia-China Friendship Association, this afternoon, who are currently visiting our country, and held cordial talks.
    Those present at the reception included Zhang Zhixiang, President of the China-Mongolia Friendship Association, and others. Sharav, the Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, was also present.

### 薄一波副总理接见蒙中友协代表团

1959-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

    薄一波副总理接见蒙中友协代表团
    新华社11日讯　薄一波副总理今天下午接见了正在我国访问的以蒙中友好协会副主席那楚克道尔吉教授为首的蒙中友协代表团，亲切地进行了交谈。
    接见时在座的有中蒙友好协会会长张致祥等。蒙古人民共和国驻我国大使沙拉布也在座。
