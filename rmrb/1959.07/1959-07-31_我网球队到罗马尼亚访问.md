Title: Our Tennis Team Visits Romania
Author:
Date: 1959-07-31
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, July 29th. The Chinese tennis team, led by Guo Kunhe, arrived here by train from Warsaw on the 29th.

The Chinese tennis team will visit Romania for about half a month. During the visit, it will hold friendly matches with the Romanian national tennis team and some local teams.

### 我网球队到罗马尼亚访问

1959-07-31
第4版()
专栏：

    我网球队到罗马尼亚访问
    新华社布加勒斯特29日电　中国网球队在领队郭坤和的率领下29日从华沙乘火车到达这里。
    中国网球队将在罗马尼亚访问半个月左右。访问期间，它将同罗马尼亚国家网球队和一些地方球队进行友谊比赛。
