Title: Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission Celebrates Sixth Anniversary: Senior Member of the Korean-Chinese Side Sends Congratulatory Letter
Author:
Date: 1959-07-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 29th: According to a report from the Korean Central News Agency in Kaesong, Major General Zhu Changjun, Senior Member of the Korean-Chinese side of the Korean Military Armistice Commission, sent a letter today to the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, congratulating it on its sixth anniversary. He also expressed his gratitude to the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission for its contributions to consolidating the Korean armistice and safeguarding peace over the past six years.

The letter pointed out that the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission will surely make greater contributions to consolidating the Korean armistice and safeguarding peace in its future work.

### 中立国监察委员会成立六周年  朝中方面首席委员致函祝贺

1959-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

    中立国监察委员会成立六周年
    朝中方面首席委员致函祝贺
    新华社平壤29电　据朝鲜中央通讯社开城讯：朝鲜军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员朱昌骏少将今天写信给中立国监察委员会，祝贺中立国监察委员会成立六周年。并且对中立国监察委员会在过去的六年来对巩固朝鲜停战和维护和平所作出的贡献，表示感谢。
    信中指出，中立国监察委员会在今后的工作中一定会对巩固朝鲜停战和维护和平，作出更大的贡献。
