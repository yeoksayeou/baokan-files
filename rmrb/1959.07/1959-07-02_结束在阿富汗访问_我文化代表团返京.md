Title: My Cultural Delegation Returns to Beijing After Concluding Visit to Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1959-07-02
Page: 4

    My Cultural Delegation Returns to Beijing After Concluding Visit to Afghanistan
    Xinhua News Agency, 1st - The Chinese cultural delegation of six people, led by Qu Wu, head of the delegation and Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, returned to Beijing by plane today after a friendly visit to the Kingdom of Afghanistan at the invitation of the Afghan government.

### 结束在阿富汗访问  我文化代表团返京

1959-07-02
第4版()
专栏：

    结束在阿富汗访问
    我文化代表团返京
    新华社1日讯　应邀到阿富汗王国进行友好访问的中国文化代表团一行六人，在团长、对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武率领下，今天乘飞机回到北京。
