Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and other Units Hold Evening Party to Commemorate the 100th Anniversary of the Performance of "The Thunderstorm"
Author:
Date: 1959-07-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 9th. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Chinese Dramatists Association, and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association jointly held an evening party tonight to commemorate the 100th anniversary of the performance of the famous "The Thunderstorm" by the great Russian playwright Ostrovsky. The Experimental Theater of the Central Academy of Drama performed "The Thunderstorm" at the party.

Before the performance began, Ouyang Yuqian, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Dramatists Association, spoke at the party. He said that "The Thunderstorm" has been performed for a hundred years since its first performance in 1859. Today, the 100th anniversary commemorative performance of "The Thunderstorm" on the stage of the capital of China will further enrich the cultural exchange activities between China and the Soviet Union and further strengthen the friendship between the peoples of the two countries.

Invited to attend the party were all the diplomatic officials of the Soviet Embassy in China, some experts from the Soviet Union and other fraternal countries in Beijing, and Soviet travelers in Beijing. On the Chinese side, the attendees included the Vice Presidents and Directors of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the heads of the Chinese Dramatists Association, and more than 1,000 people from literary and art circles and all walks of life in the capital.

The Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association specially sent telegrams to congratulate the success of tonight's commemorative performance and the increasing development of cultural cooperation between the Soviet Union and China.

### 中苏友协等单位举行晚会  纪念“大雷雨”演出百周年

1959-07-10
第4版()
专栏：

    中苏友协等单位举行晚会
    纪念“大雷雨”演出百周年
    新华社9日讯　中苏友好协会总会、中国戏剧家协会和北京市中苏友好协会今晚联合举行晚会，纪念伟大俄罗斯剧作家奥斯特洛夫斯基名著“大雷雨”演出一百周年。中央戏剧学院实验话剧院在晚会上演出了“大雷雨”。
    演出开始以前，中国戏剧家协会副主席欧阳予倩在晚会上讲话，他说，“大雷雨”自从1859年首次演出到现在，已经一百年了。今天在中国首都舞台上举行“大雷雨”一百周年纪念演出，将进一步丰富中苏两国的文化交流活动，进一步加强两国人民的友谊。
    应邀出席晚会的，有苏联驻华大使馆全体外交官员、苏联和其他兄弟国家在北京的部分专家，苏联在北京的旅行家。中国方面出席的有中苏友协总会和北京市中苏友协的副会长和理事、中国戏剧家协会的负责人，以及首都文艺界和各界人士共一千多人。
    苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会、苏中友好协会特地拍来电报祝贺今晚纪念演出的成功和苏中两国文化合作的日益发展。
