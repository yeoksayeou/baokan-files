	Extracting Fragrant Oil from China Fir Sawdust

	Workers at the Jiangxi Province Deshengguan Reclamation Farm have extracted a valuable fragrant oil from China fir sawdust. This oil contains cedrol, an important chemical raw material.

### 杉木屑提芳香油

1959-07-21
第2版()
专栏：

    杉木屑提芳香油
    江西省德胜关垦殖场职工从杉木屑中提炼出一种价值高贵的芬香油。这种油含有杉木脑，是一种重要的化工原料。
