Title: South Wind Blows, Early Rice Fragrant
Author: Lian Sun
Date: 1959-07-27
Page: 3

    South Wind Blows, Early Rice Fragrant
  The south wind blows, bringing the fragrance of early rice,
  Golden rice ears gleam all over the slopes,
  The people's commune is truly good,
  This year, golden rice will fill the granaries;
  Fill the granaries,
  Songs of a bumper harvest echo in all directions.
  Busy in the fields, busy on the threshing ground,
  Male and female commune members are all on edge,
  Seizing the season, not letting up,
  Reaping early rice while transplanting seedlings;
  Transplanting seedlings,
  Winning one victory after another.

### 南风吹来早稻香
连孙
1959-07-27
第3版()
专栏：

    南风吹来早稻香
  南风吹来早稻香，
  满坡稻穗耀眼黄，
  人民公社真正好，
  今年黄稻装满仓；
  装满仓，
  丰收山歌响四方。
  田里忙来场上忙，
  男女社员都紧张，
  抓紧季节不放松，
  边割早稻边插秧；
  边插秧，
  打胜一仗又一仗。
            （连孙）
