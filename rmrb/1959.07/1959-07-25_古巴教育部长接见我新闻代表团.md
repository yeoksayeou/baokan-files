Title: Cuban Minister of Education Receives Chinese News Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-07-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 23rd - Cuban Minister of Education, Armando Hart, received the visiting Chinese news delegation this afternoon and held cordial talks. Hart expressed the hope of developing cultural exchanges between Cuba and China. Accompanying Armando Hart in receiving the Chinese news delegation was Armando Hart's wife, the Cuban women's leader, Santa Marina.

### 古巴教育部长接见我新闻代表团

1959-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴教育部长接见我新闻代表团
    据新华社哈瓦那23电　古巴教育部部长阿曼多·阿特今天下午接见了在古巴访问的中国新闻工作者代表团，并进行了亲切的交谈。阿特表示希望开展古巴和中国之间的文化交流。陪同阿特接见中国新闻工作者代表团的有阿曼多·阿特的夫人——古巴妇女领袖圣·马林纳。
