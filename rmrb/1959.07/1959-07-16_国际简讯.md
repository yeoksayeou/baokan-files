International Briefs

    A Revolutionary Education School in Cuba Opens
    Havana Dispatch: A newly established school in Cuba for revolutionary theory education related to the July 26th Movement opened on the 13th. Raúl Castro, Commander-in-Chief of the Cuban Armed Forces, and leaders of local organizations of the "July 26th Movement" attended the opening ceremony.
    The school will teach land reform and other revolutionary theories and currently has more than fifty students.
    The Chinese journalists delegation led by Yao Zhen also attended the opening ceremony on the 13th at the invitation. After the meeting, the students and members of the "July 26th Movement" asked the Chinese delegation many questions about China's land reform and agricultural cooperatives.

    Burmese President Announces National Assembly Meeting in August
    Rangoon Dispatch: Burmese President U Win Maung announced in an order on the 14th that the session of the House of Representatives of the Burmese Parliament would open on August 13, and the session of the House of Nationalities would open on August 24.

    Floods in West Pakistan
    Karachi Dispatch: According to news in local newspapers on the 12th and 13th, seven hundred and fifty villages in the Sialkot, Multan, and Muzaffarnagar districts of West Pakistan have already been affected by floods. In Multan district alone, approximately 100,000 residents have been affected.
    It is reported that the water level of the Indus River, the largest river in West Pakistan, as well as the Ravi, Chenab, and Jhelum Rivers, continues to rise.

### 国际简讯

专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    古巴一所革命教育学校开学
    哈瓦那讯　古巴新设的一所进行有关7月26日运动革命理论教育的学校13日开学。古巴武装部队总司令劳尔·卡斯特罗和“7月26日运动”地方组织的领导人参加了开学式。
    这所学校将讲授土地改革和其他革命理论，目前有五十多个学员。
    由姚溱率领的中国新闻工作者代表团13日也应邀参加了开学式。学员们和“7月26日运动”的成员在会后向中国代表团询问了好些有关中国土地改革和农业合作社的问题。缅甸总统宣布八月开国会会议
    仰光讯　缅甸总统吴温貌14日在一项命令中宣布，缅甸国会代表院会议在8月13日开幕，民族院会议在8月24日开幕。
    西巴基斯坦洪水成灾
    卡拉奇讯　据12、13日这里的报纸消息，仅仅在西巴基斯坦的锡尔科特、穆尔坦和穆札法纳加尔等县，就已经有七百五十个村遭到洪水的侵害。在穆尔坦一个县，遇灾的居民大约有十万人。
    据报道，西巴基斯坦最大的一条河流印度河以及拉维河、契纳布河和杰隆河的河水都在继续上涨。
