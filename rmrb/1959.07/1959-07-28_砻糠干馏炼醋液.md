Title: Rice Husk Pyrolysis to Refine Acetic Liquor
Author:
Date: 1959-07-28
Page: 2

    Rice Husk Pyrolysis to Refine Acetic Liquor
    The Shanghai Shuguang Chemical Plant uses rice husk pyrolysis to extract a kind of acetic liquor, which is a raw material for manufacturing the chemical raw materials acetone and propanone. In addition to acetic liquor, the plant can also obtain wood tar by pyrolyzing rice husks.

### 砻糠干馏炼醋液

1959-07-28
第2版()
专栏：

    砻糠干馏炼醋液
    上海曙光化工厂利用砻糠干馏，提炼出一种醋液，它是制造化工原料丙醋和丙酮的原料。这个厂用砻糠经过干馏的方法，除了可得到醋液外，还可得到木焦油。
