Title: The Friendship Between the Chinese and Polish People Has a Long History; Liqiao Commune Renamed China-Poland Friendship Commune; Polish Ambassador Presents Gifts to the Commune on Behalf of the Government
Author:
Date: 1959-07-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 16th - The Liqiao People's Commune in Shunyi District, a suburb of Beijing, was today renamed the China-Poland Friendship People's Commune.

The China-Poland Friendship People's Commune was filled with joy today. Many colorful slogans were posted along the village streets. Early in the morning, over a thousand commune members gathered at the edge of the village, beating drums and gongs, and setting off firecrackers to welcome Polish Ambassador to China, Kiryluk, and embassy staff who came to attend the naming ceremony.

Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, spoke at the beginning of the naming ceremony. He said that the people of China and Poland have a deep friendship. Let us unite more closely in the socialist family headed by the Soviet Union and strive together for socialist construction and the maintenance of world peace.

Ambassador Kiryluk delivered a friendly speech at the naming ceremony. He praised the great achievements made by Chinese farmers under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the government in their efforts to develop agricultural production and improve living standards after the realization of the people's communes.

At the naming ceremony, Ambassador Kiryluk, on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of Poland, presented the China-Poland Friendship People's Commune with gifts such as agricultural machinery, radios, cameras, and Polish books and periodicals. After the meeting, Ambassador Kiryluk and embassy staff visited the commune's machinery repair shop, grain processing factory, and kindergarten.

Also attending the naming ceremony were Cai Ziwei, Vice Minister of Agriculture; Yu Zhan, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; He Chengxiang, Vice President of the China-Poland Friendship Association; and Xin Yi, Deputy Secretary-General and Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee.

### 中波两国人民的友谊源远流长  李桥公社命名为中波友好公社  波兰大使代表政府向公社赠送礼物

1959-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

    中波两国人民的友谊源远流长
    李桥公社命名为中波友好公社
    波兰大使代表政府向公社赠送礼物
    新华社16日讯　北京市郊顺义区李桥人民公社今天被命名为中波友好人民公社。
    中波友好人民公社今天喜气洋洋，村里街道两旁贴着许多彩色标语。上千名社员清早齐集村边，敲锣打鼓，燃放鞭炮，迎接前来参加命名大会的波兰驻我国大使基里洛克和使馆人员。
    北京市副市长吴晗在命名大会开始时讲话。他说，中波两国人民有着深厚的友谊，让我们更加紧密地团结在以苏联为首的社会主义大家庭里，为社会主义建设事业，为维护世界和平共同奋斗。
    基里洛克大使在命名大会上发表了充满友情的讲话。他赞扬中国农民在中国共产党和政府的领导下，实现人民公社化以后在农业生产和提高生活水平的努力中所取得的伟大成就。
    在命名大会上，基里洛克大使以波兰人民共和国政府的名义，赠给中波友好人民公社农业机械、收音机、照像机和波兰书刊等礼品。会后，基里洛克大使和使馆人员参观了公社的机械修配厂、粮食加工厂和幼儿园。
    参加命名大会的，还有农业部副部长蔡子伟、外交部苏联东欧司副司长余湛、中波友好协会副会长何成湘和北京市人民委员会副秘书长兼外事处处长辛毅。
