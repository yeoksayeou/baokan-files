Title: Soviet Bolshoi Theatre Ballet Company to Visit China for Performances in September
Author:
Date: 1959-07-05
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, July 3rd: According to TASS, the Bolshoi Theatre Ballet Company of the Soviet Union, which has just returned with honors from a tour of the United States and Canada, will visit China in September.

	For this visit to China, the Bolshoi Theatre has specially rehearsed "Path of Thunder," a new work by Azerbaijani composer Kara Karayev, which will premiere in China. In addition, the ballet company will perform the ballet "The Stone Flower" and two concerts for the Chinese audience. The famous ballets "Swan Lake" and "Romeo and Juliet" are also scheduled to be performed.

	The ballet company will tour Beijing and major cities across the country for about a month and a half.

### 苏联大剧院芭蕾舞蹈团  九月将来我国访问演出

1959-07-05
第3版()
专栏：匈牙利通讯

    苏联大剧院芭蕾舞蹈团
    九月将来我国访问演出
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社消息：刚从美国和加拿大巡回演出载誉归国的苏联大剧院芭蕾舞蹈团将于九月来我国访问。
    为了这次来中国访问演出，大剧院特别排练了阿塞拜疆作曲家卡拉耶夫的新作“雷声中的小路”，这个舞剧将在中国作首次演出。另外，芭蕾舞蹈团还要给中国观众演出舞剧“宝石花”和两个音乐会。著名舞剧“天鹅湖”和“罗米欧与朱丽叶”预定也将上演。
    芭蕾舞蹈团将在北京和全国各大城市巡回演出一个半月左右。
