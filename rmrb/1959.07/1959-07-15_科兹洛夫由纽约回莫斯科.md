Title: Kozlov Returns to Moscow from New York
Author:
Date: 1959-07-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 13th. Soviet Council of Ministers First Deputy Chairman Kozlov returned to Moscow tonight from New York on the "Tu-114" special plane he took to visit New York.

　　Kozlov went to the United States on June 28 to attend the opening ceremony of the Soviet Exhibition, and then made a two-week visit in the United States.

　　Tonight, the "Tu-114" special plane carrying Kozlov flew non-stop from New York to Moscow in nine hours and forty-eight minutes, which was more than an hour shorter than the trip to New York.

### 科兹洛夫由纽约回莫斯科

1959-07-15
第4版()
专栏：

    科兹洛夫由纽约回莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科13日电　苏联部长会议第一副主席科兹洛夫今晚乘他去纽约访问的“图—114”专机由纽约返回莫斯科。
    科兹洛夫是6月28日前往美国参加苏联展览会开幕式的，之后在美国作了两星期的访问。
    今晚科兹洛夫所乘的“图—114”专机从纽约到莫斯科不着陆飞行了九小时四十八分钟，这比去纽约时缩短了一小时多。
