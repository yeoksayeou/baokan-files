International Briefs

    Iraq Agrees to Hold Arab Foreign Ministers Meeting in Baghdad
    Baghdad Dispatch: An Iraqi Foreign Ministry spokesperson announced on the afternoon of the 29th that the Iraqi government has agreed to hold a meeting of Arab foreign ministers to discuss the Palestinian issue. This meeting will be held in Baghdad.
    It is reported that, so far, eight Arab countries have agreed to participate in the meeting. They are the UAR, Lebanon, Saudi Arabia, Jordan, Yemen, Sudan, Libya, and Iraq. Tunisia and Morocco have not yet responded.

    Indonesian Army Officers in Charge Hold Meeting
    Jakarta Dispatch: Indonesian Army officers in charge began a meeting on the 29th to discuss economic issues and the restoration of the 1945 Constitution.
    According to the Army Information Service, Army Chief of Staff Lieutenant General Nasution gave a report on the overall situation at the meeting on the 29th; Minister of State for Economic Stabilization Suprayogi gave a report on economic and financial issues.
    Prime Minister and Minister of Defense Juanda, officers from Army Headquarters, central and local wartime power holders, and provincial governors attended the meeting.

    US to Establish Embassy in Nepalese Capital
    Washington Dispatch: The US State Department announced on the 29th that the US will establish an embassy in Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal. Currently, the US ambassador to Nepal is concurrently held by the ambassador to India.

### 国际简讯

1959-07-01
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    伊拉克同意在巴格达举行阿拉伯外长会议
    巴格达讯　伊拉克外交部发言人29日下午宣布，伊拉克政府已经同意举行阿拉伯国家外交部长会议讨论巴勒斯坦问题。这个会议将在巴格达举行。
    据报道，到现在为止，已经有八个阿拉伯国家同意参加会议，它们是阿联、黎巴嫩、沙特阿拉伯、约旦、也门、苏丹、利比亚和伊拉克。突尼斯和摩洛哥还没有答复。
    印度尼西亚陆军负责军官举行会议
    雅加达讯　印度尼西亚陆军负责军官29日开始举行会议，讨论经济问题和恢复1945年宪法问题。
    据陆军新闻处说，陆军参谋长纳苏蒂安中将向29日的会议作了关于总的形势的报告；负责经济稳定事务的国务部长苏普腊约基就经济和财政问题作了报告。
    总理兼国防部长朱安达、陆军总部的军官、中央和地方战时掌权者和各省省长出席了会议。
    美将在尼泊尔首都建立大使馆
    华盛顿讯　美国国务院29日宣布，美国将在尼泊尔首都加德满都建立大使馆。目前美国驻尼泊尔的大使是由驻印度的大使兼任。
