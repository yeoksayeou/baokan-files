    International Briefs

    Kishi Leaves Brazil for Argentina
    Rio de Janeiro dispatch: Japanese Prime Minister Nobusuke Kishi has concluded his visit to Brazil and left Brazil on the 28th for Argentina.
    Brazil was the main target of Kishi's visit to South America. He attempted to pave the way for the expansion of Japanese monopoly capital into Brazil and to solve the problem of continued Japanese immigration to Brazil.

    UAR and the United States Sign Agreement on Importing American Wheat and Flour
    Cairo dispatch: The UAR and the United States signed an agreement here on the 29th. According to this agreement, the Egyptian region of the UAR will import wheat and flour from the United States worth $57.7 million.
    UAR Central Minister of Economy Kaissouni and Norbert Anschutz, Chargé d'Affaires of the U.S. Embassy in Cairo, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments.

### 国际简讯

1959-07-31
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    岸信介离巴西去阿根廷
    里约热内卢讯　日本首相岸信介已经结束了对巴西的访问，在28日离开巴西前往阿根廷。
    巴西是岸信介访问南美的主要对象，他企图为日本垄断资本向巴西扩张铺平道路，并且解决日本继续向巴西移民的问题。
    阿联和美国签订进口美国小麦和面粉协定
    开罗讯　阿联和美国29日在这里签订了一项协定。根据这项协定，阿联埃及地区将从美国进口价值五千七百七十万美元的小麦和面粉。
    阿联中央经济部长凯舒尼和美国驻开罗大使馆临时代办诺伯特·安舒茨代表两国政府在协定上签了字。
