Title: Our Youth Delegation Sets Off to Participate in the 7th World Youth Festival

Author: 
Date: 1959-07-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 12th:

    Our Youth Delegation Sets Off to Participate in the 7th World Youth Festival
    
The Chinese youth delegation participating in the 7th World Youth and Students for Peace and Friendship Festival left Beijing by train this morning, heading to Vienna via the Soviet Union.

The delegation consists of over 400 people, including an art troupe of more than 200 people and a sports team of 21. The delegation includes advanced producers who have emerged in the Great Leap Forward in industrial and agricultural production, young scientists, teachers, doctors, literary and art workers, students, representatives from religious circles and the business community, representatives of minority nationalities, and youth workers. Among them are well-known national model workers such as Huang Baomei, a female worker from Shanghai State-owned Cotton Mill No. 17; Wang Chengliang, a youth leader of the solidarity furnace at Anshan No. 2 Steel Plant; and Yin A'mei, a female tractor driver from Wusong People's Commune in Baoshan County, Shanghai.

The art troupe of the Chinese youth delegation includes Peking Opera, song and dance, and acrobatics groups, which will perform nearly 100 programs for young men and women from all over the world at the festival. The sports team includes excellent young male and female table tennis players and a young men's basketball team, who will participate in the festival's sports meet. In addition, the delegation's artists will also participate in international art competitions such as national musical instrument, piano, stringed instrument, and wind instrument solo performances, vocal solos, and dance. In addition, a batch of carefully selected Chinese paintings, prints, sculptures, handicrafts, photographic works, children's paintings, and stamps created by young artists will be exhibited at various exhibitions of the festival. The delegation is also bringing three color films to participate in the international film competition.

The delegation is headed by Liu Xiyuan, Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, with Zhang Chao and Wang Guangying, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Youth Federation, Gao Tianhui, Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, and Zhu Yujin, President of China Youth Publishing House, as deputy leaders.

### 参加第七届世界青年联欢节  我国青年代表团启程

1959-07-13
第4版()
专栏：

    参加第七届世界青年联欢节
    我国青年代表团启程
    新华社12日讯　参加第七届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的中国青年代表团，已于今天早晨乘火车离开北京，取道苏联前往维也纳。
    代表团共有四百多人，其中包括一个二百多人的艺术团和由二十一人组成的体育队。代表团中包括工农业生产大跃进中涌现出来的先进生产者、青年科学家、教师、医生、文艺工作者、学生、宗教界、工商界的代表、少数民族代表和青年工作者，其中有著名的全国劳动模范——上海国棉十七厂女工黄宝妹、鞍山第二炼钢厂青年团结炉炉长王成良、上海宝山县吴淞人民公社女拖拉机手尹阿妹等。
    中国青年代表团的艺术团包括京剧、歌舞、杂技三个团，他们将在联欢节上为世界各国男、女青年演出近一百个节目。体育队包括优秀青年男、女乒乓球选手和青年男子篮球队，他们将参加联欢节的运动会。此外，代表团的艺术家们还将参加民族乐器、钢琴、弦乐器和管乐器独奏、独唱以及舞蹈等国际艺术比赛项目。另外还有一批精选出来的由青年美术家创作的中国画、版画、雕塑、工艺品、摄影作品、儿童画和邮票将在联欢节的各种展览会上展出。代表团还带着三部彩色影片，准备参加国际电影比赛。
    代表团由中华全国青年联合会主席刘西元任团长，中华全国青年联合会副主席张超和王光英、中国共产主义青年团中央委员会常务委员高天辉和中国青年出版社社长朱语今等四人任副团长。
