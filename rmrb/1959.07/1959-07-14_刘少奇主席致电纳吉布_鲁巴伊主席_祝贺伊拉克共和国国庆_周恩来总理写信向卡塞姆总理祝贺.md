Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Sends Telegram to Chairman Najib al-Ruba'i to Congratulate the National Day of the Republic of Iraq; Premier Zhou Enlai Writes Letter to Premier Qassem to Congratulate
Author:
Date: 1959-07-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 13th -- Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram on the 10th to Najib al-Ruba'i, Chairman of the Supreme Authority Committee of the Republic of Iraq, to congratulate the first anniversary of the National Day of the Republic of Iraq. The telegram reads as follows:
To His Excellency Najib al-Ruba'i, Chairman of the Supreme Authority Committee of the Republic of Iraq:

On the occasion of the first anniversary of the National Day of the Republic of Iraq, I extend warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Iraq on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish the people of Iraq greater achievements in their struggle to safeguard national independence. I wish the friendship between China and Iraq will develop day by day. I wish the Republic of Iraq prosperity and strength.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　Chairman of the People's Republic of China　Liu Shaoqi
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　July 10, 1959

Xinhua News Agency, July 13th -- Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, wrote a letter on the 10th to Abdul Karim Qassem, Premier of the Republic of Iraq, congratulating the first anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Iraq. The full text of the letter is as follows:

To His Excellency Abdul Karim Qassem, Premier of the Government of the Republic of Iraq:

On the occasion of the first anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Iraq, I would like to express my heartfelt and warm congratulations to Your Excellency, the Iraqi government, and the people of Iraq through the Chinese government delegation headed by Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, who is invited to attend your country's National Day celebrations.

In the past year, under Your Excellency's leadership, the Iraqi government and people have achieved significant victories in safeguarding national independence, thwarting foreign subversive conspiracies, and developing the national economy. The victory of the Iraqi people has been greatly admired and praised by the Chinese people.

Under the guidance of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and the Bandung Spirit, the friendly relations between China and Iraq have developed rapidly in the past year, which is entirely in the interests of the people of both countries. I believe that with the development of cultural and economic relations between the two countries in the future, the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries will inevitably develop and strengthen day by day.

I wish Your Excellency good health, and I wish the great Iraqi people and the Republic of Iraq progress and prosperity.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China　Zhou Enlai
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　July 10, 1959

### 刘少奇主席致电纳吉布·鲁巴伊主席  祝贺伊拉克共和国国庆  周恩来总理写信向卡塞姆总理祝贺

1959-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席致电纳吉布·鲁巴伊主席
    祝贺伊拉克共和国国庆
    周恩来总理写信向卡塞姆总理祝贺
    新华社13日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇在10日打电报给伊拉克共和国最高权力委员会主席纳吉布·鲁巴伊，祝贺伊拉克共和国国庆一周年。电文如下：伊拉克共和国最高权力委员会纳吉布·鲁巴伊主席阁下：
    当伊拉克共和国国庆一周年的时候，我谨代表中国人民并且以我个人的名义，向阁下和伊拉克人民致热烈的祝贺。祝伊拉克人民在维护民族独立的斗争中获得更大的成就。祝中国和伊拉克两国间的友谊日益发展。祝伊拉克共和国繁荣富强。
                  中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                                  1959年7月10日
  新华社13日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在10日写信给伊拉克共和国总理阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·卡塞姆，祝贺伊拉克共和国成立一周年。信件全文如下：伊拉克共和国政府阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·卡塞姆总理阁下：
    当伊拉克共和国成立一周年纪念的时候，我愿意通过应邀参加贵国国庆大典的、以外交部副部长罗贵波为首的中国政府代表团，向阁下、向伊拉克政府和人民表示衷心的、热烈的祝贺。
    一年来，伊拉克政府和人民在阁下领导下，在维护民族独立、粉碎外来的颠覆阴谋以及在发展民族经济等方面都取得了重大胜利。伊拉克人民的胜利得到了中国人民极大的钦佩和赞扬。
    中、伊两国的友好关系在和平共处五项原则和万隆精神的指导下，一年来有了迅速的发展，这是完全符合两国人民利益的。我相信，今后随着两国文化、经济关系的发展，两国之间的友好合作关系必将日益发展和加强。
    祝阁下身体健康，祝伟大的伊拉克人民和伊拉克共和国进步和繁荣。
            中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                1959年7月10日
