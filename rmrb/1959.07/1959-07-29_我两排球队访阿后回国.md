Title: Our Two Volleyball Teams Return Home After Visiting Albania
Author:
Date: 1959-07-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, July 27th: The Chinese Youth Women's Volleyball Team and the Guangdong Men's Volleyball Team, which were invited by the Albanian Sports Commission to visit, returned home by plane on the 27th after visiting here for half a month.
    The Chinese Women's Volleyball Team played three matches with the Albanian Women's Volleyball Team in Tirana and Korçë, winning all three matches with a score of 3-0.
    The Guangdong Men's Volleyball Team played four matches with the Albanian Volleyball Team, winning two and losing two.
    During the visit, the负责人 of Tirana and the Albanian Sports Commission jointly met with all members of the two volleyball teams.

### 我两排球队访阿后回国

1959-07-29
第4版()
专栏：

    我两排球队访阿后回国
    据新华社地拉那27日电　应阿尔巴尼亚体育运动委员会邀请前来访问的中国青年女子排球队和广东省男子排球队在这里访问了半个月后，于27日乘飞机返国。
    中国女子排球队在地拉那和科尔察两地同阿尔巴尼亚女子排球队共比赛了三场，结果都以三比○获胜。
    广东省男子排球队同阿尔巴尼亚排球队共进行了四场比赛，两胜两负。
    在访问期间，地拉那市和阿尔巴尼亚体育运动委员会负责人曾联合接见这两个排球队的全体人员。
