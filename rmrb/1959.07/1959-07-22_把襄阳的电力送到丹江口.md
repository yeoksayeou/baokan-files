Title: Bringing Xiangyang's Electricity to Danjiangkou
Author:
Date: 1959-07-22
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, July 18th. The construction of the high-voltage transmission line from Xiangyang to Danjiangkou has recently commenced. This line is 93.5 kilometers long, and all the poles and towers are made of reinforced concrete structures.

After the completion of this high-voltage transmission line, it will be able to transmit electricity from Xiangyang to Danjiangkou, providing power and lighting for the Han River-Danjiangkou Water Conservancy Hub project.

### 把襄阳的电力送到丹江口

1959-07-22
第2版()
专栏：

    把襄阳的电力送到丹江口
    新华社武汉18日电　襄阳到丹江口的高压输电线路最近开工兴建。这条线路全长九十三点五公里，杆塔全部是钢筋混凝土结构。
    这条高压输电线路完工以后，能把襄阳的电力输送到丹江口，为汉水—丹江口水利枢纽工程提供动力和照明用电。
