Title: U.S. Warships Intruded into Waters off Pingtan and Baiquan Islands; I Issue the 54th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1959-07-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th July: From 3:44 to 4:09 and from 19:00 to 20:00 on July 4th, a U.S. warship twice intruded into the waters off Pingtan and Baiquan Islands in Fujian Province. From 3:00 to 4:40 on July 5th, another U.S. warship intruded into the waters off Pingtan and Baiquan Islands. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. warships, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 54th serious warning.

### 美舰侵入我平潭、白犬地区海域  我提出第54次严重警告

1959-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    美舰侵入我平潭、白犬地区海域
    我提出第54次严重警告
    新华社5日讯　7月4日三时四十四分至四时零九分，及十九时至二十时，美国军舰一艘两次侵入我福建省平潭、白犬地区海域。7月5日三时至四时四十分又有美国军舰一艘侵入我平潭、白犬地区海域。对于美国军舰这种军事挑衅行为，我外交部发言人奉命提出第五十四次严重警告。
