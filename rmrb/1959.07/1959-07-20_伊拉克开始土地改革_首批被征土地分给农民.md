Title: Iraq Begins Land Reform; First Batch of Expropriated Land Distributed to Farmers
Author:
Date: 1959-07-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, July 17th: Iraqi Prime Minister Qassem distributed land certificates to a group of farmers in the model village of Latifiyah, south of Baghdad, this morning, marking the beginning of the distribution of the first batch of land confiscated from landowners to farmers under the land reform law.

### 伊拉克开始土地改革  首批被征土地分给农民

1959-07-20
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克开始土地改革
    首批被征土地分给农民
    新华社巴格达17日电　伊拉克总理卡塞姆今天上午在巴格达以南的拉堤菲亚赫的模范村把土地证明书发给了一批农民，这是伊拉克根据土地改革法把第一批从土地主那里征收的土地分配给农民的开始。
