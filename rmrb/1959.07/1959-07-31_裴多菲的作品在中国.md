Author: Huang Peixi
Date: 1959-07-31

On Today's Calendar

    Petőfi's Works in China
    Today marks the 110th anniversary of the death of the great Hungarian poet and revolutionary democrat Sándor Petőfi (1823-1849). Petőfi and his works were introduced to China starting half a century ago. The first to introduce Petőfi was Mr. Lu Xun. In the last section of "On the Power of Mara Poetry," written in 1907, Lu Xun penned a concise and vivid critical biography of Petőfi. A few months later, Mr. Lu Xun's translation of "Petőfi's Poetic Theory" was also published in "Henan" magazine. Subsequently, the "Fiction Monthly" of January 1923 published Mao Dun's "Centennial Memorial of the Hungarian Patriotic Poet Petőfi."
    The first person in our country to translate Petőfi's poems was also Lu Xun. The earliest Chinese translation of Petőfi's work is Petőfi's "Song of Hope" quoted in the seventh piece of "Wild Grass": "Hope" (see "Complete Works of Lu Xun," Volume II, page 171). In January 1925, "Yu Si" Weekly published Lu Xun's translation of five poems by Petőfi, among which "My Father's and My Craft" is the most famous. Following this, at Lu Xun's suggestion, Bai Mang translated nine short poems by Petőfi. "Life is dear, and love is dearer; but both can be sacrificed for the sake of freedom," this famous poem comes from Yin Fu's translation.
    In 1931, Sun Yong translated Petőfi's long poem "Brave John" based on the Esperanto version. Mr. Lu Xun devoted much effort to proofreading the translation and introducing its publication. In 1951, Sun Yong also translated "Forty Poems by Petőfi," and in 1954, the Writers Publishing House published "Selected Poems of Petőfi." (Huang Peixi)

### 裴多菲的作品在中国
黄培熙
1959-07-31
第8版()
专栏：在今天的日历上

    裴多菲的作品在中国
    今天是匈牙利伟大诗人、革命民主主义者裴多菲·山陀尔（1823—1849）逝世110周年纪念日。裴多菲和他的作品被介绍到中国来，是从半个世纪以前开始的。第一个介绍裴多菲的是鲁迅先生。鲁迅在1907年写成的“摩罗诗力说”的最后一节，以精辟生动的文笔，为裴多菲写了评传。几个月后，鲁迅先生翻译的“裴彖飞诗论”，也在“河南”杂志上发表。随后，又有1923年1月的“小说月报”发表了茅盾的“匈牙利爱国诗人裴都菲的百年纪念”。
    我国第一个翻译裴多菲诗篇的也是鲁迅，在“野草”的第七篇：“希望”里引用的裴多菲的“希望”之歌（见“鲁迅全集”第二卷，第一百七十一页），就是裴多菲作品最早的中文译文。1925年1月间“语丝”周刊发表了鲁迅翻译的裴多菲诗五首，其中“我的父亲的和我的手艺”一首，最为著名。接着，由于鲁迅的提议，白莽又译了九首裴多菲的短诗。“生命诚可贵，爱情价更高；若为自由故，两者皆可抛”，这首名诗出自殷夫的译笔。
    1931年，孙用根据世界语译本迻译了裴多菲的长诗“勇敢的约翰”，鲁迅先生用很多的精力为这一译本校定字句，并介绍出版。1951年，孙用又译出了“裴多菲诗四十首”，1954年，作家出版社出版了“裴多菲诗选”。　（黄培熙）
