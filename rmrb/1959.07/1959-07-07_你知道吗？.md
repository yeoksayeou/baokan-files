Title: Did You Know?
Author: Yu Wen
Date: 1959-07-07
Page: 8

    Did You Know?
    △ If all the railways in the world were laid end to end in a straight line, their length would be equivalent to two and a half times the distance from the Earth to the Moon.
    △ A total of 16 million square kilometers of the Earth are covered by ice all year round. This area accounts for 11% of the Earth's entire landmass.
    △ Since 1920, when people began to discover the widespread warming phenomenon in the Arctic, navigation conditions have gradually improved. (Yu Wen)

### 你知道吗？
宇文
1959-07-07
第8版()
专栏：

    你知道吗？
    △如果把全世界上的铁路排成一条直线，它的长度就相当于两个半从地球到月球的距离。
    △地球上共有一千六百万平方公里终年复盖着冰层。这一面积占地球上整个陆地的11％。
    △从1920年人们开始发现北极有普遍变暖现象，因此航海条件逐渐变好。　（宇文）
