Title: Grand Parade and Procession Held in Warsaw to Celebrate the 15th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of Poland
Author:
Date: 1959-07-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 22nd: Today, a grand military parade and procession were held in Warsaw, the capital of Poland, to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of Poland.

　　Early in the morning, as the sun just began to shine on the gilded spire of the Palace of Culture and Science, the entire city of Warsaw was already seething with enthusiasm. Songs were heard everywhere, and red flags fluttered in the breeze. Hundreds of thousands of citizens poured from every corner of the city towards Marshal Koszewski Avenue, where the troops to be reviewed and the procession were scheduled to pass.

　　The Palace of Culture and Science Square was newly decorated today, with hundreds of Polish flags and huge portraits of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and prominent Polish labor movement figures hanging around it.

　　At exactly ten o'clock, Polish party and government leaders Gomulka, Zawadzki, Cyrankiewicz, and others, along with members of the Soviet party and government delegation headed by Khrushchev, ascended the reviewing stand on the Palace of Culture and Science Square. Those standing on the viewing platform applauded them warmly.

　　Polish Minister of National Defense Spychalski reviewed the troops lined up on the square to be inspected. He congratulated the soldiers on the festival and called on them to unite with the armies of socialist countries and fight to defend world peace.

　　Before the military review began, a group of young people the same age as their country walked into the square and presented flowers to the party and state leaders.

　　Then, the parade began.

　　Trainees from various military schools, infantry, naval soldiers, artillery, tanks, and other branches of the armed forces marched in formation past the reviewing stand. More than two hundred fighter jets and bombers flew over the reviewing square in various formations to be inspected. Supersonic aircraft also streaked across the sky.

　　The military review lasted for more than an hour, demonstrating Poland's powerful national defense capabilities. The people of Warsaw, who had suffered the hardships of war, saw in their country's powerful armed forces a guarantee of national security and peace.

　　Subsequently, a procession of 15,000 athletes marched in neat steps past the reviewing stand, cheering for the party and state leaders and the Soviet guests. The athletes also performed graceful gymnastics and other programs in front of the reviewing stand.

　　The military parade and procession ended at one o'clock in the afternoon.

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 21st: This afternoon at four o'clock, people from all walks of life in Warsaw held a grand rally at the Palace of Culture and Science to solemnly celebrate the 15th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of Poland.

　　More than 4,000 people attended the celebration, including Polish party and government leaders, representatives from all walks of life, prominent social activists, foreign delegations visiting Poland, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Poland.

　　The conference hall of the Palace of Culture and Science was decorated solemnly and solemnly today. When Polish party and government leaders Gomulka, Zawadzki, Cyrankiewicz, and others, along with the Soviet party and government delegation headed by Khrushchev, stepped onto the rostrum, the entire audience stood up and applauded thunderously.

　　Polish State Council Chairman Zawadzki announced the opening of the meeting, and he expressed a warm welcome to the Soviet party and government delegation headed by Khrushchev. At this time, the entire audience stood up again and shouted "Long live!" in unison.

　　Then, Gomulka and Khrushchev spoke at the meeting one after another. Their speeches were frequently interrupted by applause.

　　The atmosphere in the venue was high, constantly cheering for the great achievements of the Polish people and the friendship between Poland and the Soviet Union.

　　After the celebration meeting, a grand cultural and artistic performance was held.

### 庆祝波兰人民共和国国庆十五周年  华沙举行盛大阅兵和游行

1959-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝波兰人民共和国国庆十五周年
    华沙举行盛大阅兵和游行
    新华社华沙22日电　今天，波兰首都华沙举行了盛大的阅兵和游行，庆祝波兰人民共和国国庆十五周年。
    一清早，当阳光刚刚照射到文化科学宫镀金的尖顶上的时候，全华沙市到处就已热情沸腾起来。到处歌声飘扬，红旗招展。几十万市民从城市的各个角落，涌向受检阅的部队和游行队伍预定要通过的马绍科夫斯基大街。
    文化科学宫广场今天布置一新，四周悬挂起成百幅波兰国旗和马克思、恩格斯、列宁以及波兰著名的工人运动家们的巨幅画像。
    十时正，波兰党政领导人哥穆尔卡、萨瓦茨基、西伦凯维兹等人和以赫鲁晓夫为首的苏联党政代表团人员登上了文化科学宫广场上的检阅台。站在观礼台上的人向他们热烈鼓掌。
    波兰国防部长斯彼哈尔斯基检阅了排列在广场上的接受检阅的部队。他向士兵们祝贺节日，并且号召士兵们与社会主义国家的军队团结一致，为保卫世界和平而斗争。
    军事检阅开始前，一群同自己的祖国同岁的少年走进广场，向党和国家领导人献花。
    接着，阅兵分列式开始。
    各军事学校的学员、步兵、海军士兵、炮兵、坦克和其他各兵种的部队相继列队走过检阅台前面。二百多架歼击机、轰炸机排成各式队形，飞过检阅广场上空，接受检阅。超音速飞机也掠空而过。
    军事检阅共进行了一个多小时，显示出了波兰强大的国防力量。饱经战争苦难的华沙人民从自己国家强大的武装部队身上看出了祖国安全和和平的保障。
    随后，由一万五千名运动员组成的游行队伍，迈着整齐的步伐走过检阅台，向党和国家领导人以及苏联贵宾欢呼。运动员们在检阅台前还表演了优美的体操以及其他节目。
    阅兵和游行在下午一时结束。
    新华社华沙21日电　华沙各界人民今天下午四时在文化科学宫举行盛大集会，隆重庆祝波兰人民共和国国庆十五周年。
    出席庆祝大会的有波兰党政领导人、各界人民代表、著名社会活动家、在波兰访问的外国代表团以及各国驻波兰的外交使节等共四千多人。
    文化科学宫会议大厅，今天布置得肃穆庄严。当波兰党政领导人哥穆尔卡、萨瓦茨基、西伦凯维兹等人和以赫鲁晓夫为首的苏联党政代表团走上主席台时，全场起立，掌声雷鸣。
    波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基宣布开会，他向以赫鲁晓夫为首的，苏联党政代表团表示热烈的欢迎。这时全场再次起立，齐呼“万岁！”
    接着，哥穆尔卡和赫鲁晓夫先后在会上讲了话。他们的讲话经常为掌声所打断。
    会场上情绪高涨，不断为波兰人民的伟大成就和波苏友谊欢呼。
    庆祝大会结束后，举行了盛大的文娱表演。
