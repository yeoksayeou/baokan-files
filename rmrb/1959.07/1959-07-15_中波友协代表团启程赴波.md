Title: Delegation of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association Departs for Poland
Author:
Date: 1959-07-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 14th: The delegation of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association, consisting of Wu Qiang (理事 Li Shi of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association), Li Qianfeng (理事 Li Shi of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association), and Wang Yi (理事兼秘书长 Li Shi and Secretary-General of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association), left Beijing by plane this morning. They are visiting Poland and participating in the celebrations of the 15th anniversary of Poland's National Day at the invitation of the General Association of the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association. Wang Weizhou (理事 Li Shi, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress), the head of the delegation, is already abroad.

### 中波友协代表团启程赴波

1959-07-15
第4版()
专栏：

    中波友协代表团启程赴波
    新华社14日讯　中波友好协会代表团团员吴强（中波友协理事）、李千峰（中波友协理事）、王仪（中波友协理事兼秘书长）等今天早晨乘飞机离开北京。他们是应波中友好协会总会的邀请，赴波兰访问并参加波兰国庆十五周年庆祝活动的。代表团团长王维舟（中波友协理事、中共中央委员、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员）已在国外。
