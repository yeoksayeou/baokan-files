Title: Luxembourg-China Friendship Association Secretary Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-07-25
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 24th. Huang Shunjing, secretary of the Luxembourg-China Friendship Association, and her husband, Margue, conductor of the Luxembourg Broadcasting Orchestra, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 卢中友协秘书到京

1959-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    卢中友协秘书到京
    新华社24日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的卢森堡卢中友好协会秘书黄顺经及她的丈夫卢森堡广播电台乐队指挥马尔格，今天乘飞机到达北京。
