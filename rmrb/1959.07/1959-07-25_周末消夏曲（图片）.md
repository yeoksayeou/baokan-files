Summer Serenade on Weekends

Since the beginning of summer, weekends in Beijing have taken on a new radiance. In places like Zhongshan Park, Beihai Park, the Summer Palace, and the Working People's Cultural Palace, there are often colorful garden parties in the evening, receiving thousands upon thousands of visitors. In the open spaces among the ancient trees of these parks, open-air stages are set up, and many famous professional troupes and amateur artists perform dramas, folk arts, acrobatics, or show open-air movies. Sports enthusiasts also hold various sports performances here. Everyone can spend a happy and fulfilling evening in these places according to their own preferences. (1) A family enjoying the evening together (2) The evening performance is about to begin (3) Young men and women dance gracefully in the park's open-air dance floor (4) In the ancient cypress forest, acrobat Zhou Fengxian performs a red silk dance on a tightrope (5) A ball game is in full swing

Xinhua News Agency reporters Fu Jun, Yu Huiru, and Na Yi photographed.

### 周末消夏曲（图片）
傅军　喻惠如　纳一
1959-07-25
第4版()
专栏：

    周末消夏曲
    入夏以来，北京的周末增添了新的光彩。在中山公园、北海公园、颐和园和劳动人民文化宫等地方，经常举行丰富多彩的游园晚会，接待千千万万的游人。在这些公园的古树丛中的空地上，搭着露天舞台，许多著名的专业剧团和业余文艺工作者，分别在这里演出戏剧、曲艺、杂技或放映露天电影。体育爱好者，还在这里举行各种体育表演。每个人都可以按着自己的喜好，在这些地方度过一个欢乐、幸福的夜晚。（1）晚会场上的一家老幼（2）晚会演出就要开始了（3）青年男女们在公园的露天舞池翩翩起舞（4）在古老的柏树林中，杂技演员周凤仙在钢丝上表演红绸舞（5）球赛在激烈进行中
    新华社记者　傅军　喻惠如　纳一摄
