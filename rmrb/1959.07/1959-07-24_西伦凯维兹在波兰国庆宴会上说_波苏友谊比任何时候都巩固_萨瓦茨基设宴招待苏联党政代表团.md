Title: Cyrankiewicz Says at Polish National Day Banquet That Polish-Soviet Friendship is More Consolidated Than Ever; Zawadzki Hosts a Banquet for the Soviet Party and Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-07-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 22nd: Polish Premier Cyrankiewicz held a grand banquet tonight to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Polish People's Republic.

Attending the banquet were Polish party and government leaders Gomulka, Jedrychowski, Loga-Sowiński, Morawski, Rapacki, Spychalski, Zawadzki, and others, as well as members of the Polish Council of State, government ministers, social activists, generals and officers, representatives of industrial enterprises, figures from the cultural, scientific, and artistic circles, and diplomatic envoys from various countries.

The Soviet Party and Government Delegation headed by Khrushchev also attended the banquet at the invitation.

Cyrankiewicz pointed out at the banquet that the entire Polish people are celebrating their historical achievements of the past fifteen years today, and at the same time, are full of confidence in the future. He said that we are delighted to have the Soviet Party and Government Delegation with us as we celebrate this festival. Every achievement of the Polish people is inseparable from the alliance and cooperation with the Soviet Union, and the friendship between Poland and the Soviet Union is now more consolidated than ever.

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 22nd: Polish Council of State Chairman Zawadzki hosted a banquet today at a villa on the outskirts of Warsaw to entertain the Soviet Party and Government Delegation headed by Khrushchev.

First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party Gomulka, Premier Cyrankiewicz, and other leaders of the Party and government attended the banquet.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 西伦凯维兹在波兰国庆宴会上说  波苏友谊比任何时候都巩固  萨瓦茨基设宴招待苏联党政代表团

1959-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    西伦凯维兹在波兰国庆宴会上说
    波苏友谊比任何时候都巩固
    萨瓦茨基设宴招待苏联党政代表团
    新华社华沙22日电　波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹今晚举行盛大宴会，庆祝波兰人民共和国国庆十五周年。
    出席宴会的有：波兰党政领导人哥穆尔卡、英德里霍夫斯基、洛加—索文斯基、莫拉夫斯基、腊帕茨基、斯彼哈尔斯基、萨瓦茨基等人，还有波兰国务委员会委员，政府的部长，社会活动家，将军和军官们，工业企业的代表，文化、科学、艺术界人士以及各国的外交使节。
    以赫鲁晓夫为首的苏联党政代表团也应邀出席了宴会。
    西伦凯维兹在宴会上指出，波兰全国人民今天都在庆祝他们十五年来取得的历史成就，同时对未来充满了信心。他说，在我们欢度这一节日的时候，有苏联党政代表团和我们在一起，使我们感到高兴。波兰人民的每一项成就都是和苏联的同盟和合作分不开的，波苏之间的友谊现在比任何时候都更加巩固。
    新华社华沙22日电　波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基今天在华沙市郊别墅设宴，招待以赫鲁晓夫为首的苏联党政代表团。
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记哥穆尔卡、部长会议主席西伦凯维兹以及党和政府的其他领导人出席了宴会。
    宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
