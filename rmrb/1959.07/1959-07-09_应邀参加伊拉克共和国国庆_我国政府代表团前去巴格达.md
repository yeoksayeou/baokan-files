Title: Chinese Government Delegation to Baghdad to Attend Iraqi Republic National Day Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1959-07-09
Page: 1
　
Xinhua News Agency, July 7th—At the invitation of the government of the Republic of Iraq, a Chinese government delegation headed by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo departed this morning for Baghdad to attend the first anniversary celebrations of the founding of the Republic of Iraq.

### 应邀参加伊拉克共和国国庆  我国政府代表团前去巴格达

1959-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加伊拉克共和国国庆
    我国政府代表团前去巴格达
    新华社7日讯　以外交部副部长罗贵波为首的我国政府代表团，应伊拉克共和国政府邀请，今天早晨起程赴巴格达参加伊拉克共和国国庆一周年典礼。
