Title: Delegations from Various Parts of Lebanon Meet with Government Leaders Demanding Measures to Secure the Release of Helou
Author:
Date: 1959-07-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, July 9th: According to reports in the local press, numerous delegations from across Lebanon have met with Lebanese Prime Minister Karami, Deputy Prime Minister Gemayel, and Interior Minister Eddeh, demanding that the Lebanese government intervene to secure the release of Lebanese citizen and Lebanese Communist Party leader Helou. Helou was arrested by the UAR authorities in Damascus on June 25th.

It is reported that Prime Minister Karami told a delegation from northern Lebanon that he would take the necessary measures regarding this issue. According to newspaper reports, Interior Minister Eddeh told journalists and visiting delegations yesterday that the Lebanese authorities have decided to contact the UAR authorities in the Syrian region to inquire about the reasons for Helou's arrest.

### 黎巴嫩各地代表团会见政府领导人  要求采取措施使赫卢获释

1959-07-11
第5版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩各地代表团会见政府领导人
    要求采取措施使赫卢获释
    新华社贝鲁特9日电　据此间报纸报道，来自黎巴嫩全国各地的许多代表团会见了黎巴嫩总理卡拉米、副总理杰马耶勒和内政部长埃迪，要求黎巴嫩政府进行干预，使黎巴嫩公民、黎巴嫩共产党领袖赫卢获得释放。赫卢是6月25日在大马士革被阿联当局逮捕的。
    据报道，卡拉米总理对来自黎巴嫩北部的代表团说，他将就这个问题采取必要的措施。据报纸报道，内政部长埃迪昨天对这里的新闻记者和前往会见他的代表团说，黎巴嫩当局决定同叙利亚地区的阿联当局进行接触，询问逮捕赫卢的原因。
