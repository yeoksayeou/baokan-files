Title: Soviet Party and Government Delegation Visits Poznan
Author:
Date: 1959-07-20
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Poznan, 19th: According to TASS, the Soviet Party and Government Delegation, headed by Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, arrived in Poznan on the 18th for a visit.

    After arriving in Poznan, Khrushchev went directly from the station to the countryside to understand the agricultural situation in Poznan.

    Accompanied by Polish Party and government officials, Khrushchev and Gomulka visited the "Pławce" agricultural production cooperative in Szreem County.

    Khrushchev and Gomulka participated in a mass meeting held by the cooperative farmers and spoke at the meeting. In his speech, Khrushchev introduced the achievements of Soviet agriculture in realizing the magnificent program of building communism in the Soviet Union. He said that the Soviet Union will definitely be able to catch up with and surpass the United States in terms of per capita product output. Khrushchev emphasized that the socialist road of rural development is the only way to prosperity and improving the welfare of the working people.

    In his speech, Gomulka talked about the development of Polish agriculture. He pointed out that Polish farmers will sooner or later be convinced of the great superiority of collective agricultural management.

### 苏党政代表团访问波兹南

1959-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    苏党政代表团访问波兹南
    新华社19日电　据塔斯社波兹南讯：以苏共中央第一书记、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫为首的苏联党政代表团，18日到达波兹南访问。
    到波兹南后，赫鲁晓夫从车站上直接到农村去了解波兹南的农业情况。
    赫鲁晓夫和哥穆尔卡在波兰党政负责人员的陪同下访问了施罗德县“普拉夫采”农业生产合作社。
    赫鲁晓夫和哥穆尔卡参加了合作社农民举行的群众大会，并且在会上讲了话。赫鲁晓夫在讲话时介绍了苏联农业在实现苏联建设共产主义的宏伟纲领方面所取得的成绩。他说，苏联在按人口平均计算的产品产量方面一定能够赶上和超过美国。赫鲁晓夫着重指出，农村发展的社会主义道路，是富裕和提高劳动人民福利的唯一道路。
    哥穆尔卡在讲话中谈到了发展波兰农业问题。他指出，波兰农民迟早将确信集体经营农业的巨大优越性。
