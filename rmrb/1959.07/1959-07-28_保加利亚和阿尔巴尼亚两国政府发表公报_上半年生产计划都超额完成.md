Title: Bulgaria and Albania Announce Joint Communiqué: Production Plans Exceeded in First Half of the Year
Author:
Date: 1959-07-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, July 25th: Bulgarian industrial and agricultural production has seen significant development this year. In the first half of the year, total industrial output value increased by 23.4% compared to the same period last year. According to estimates from the Ministry of Agriculture and Forestry, a bumper harvest is expected this year for wheat, sunflowers, corn, beets, tobacco, and other crops.

According to a communiqué released today by the Central Statistical Bureau of the Bulgarian Council of Ministers regarding the implementation of the national economic development plan for the first half of this year, the total industrial output value plan was fulfilled by 101.4%. Many industrial sectors saw substantial growth compared to the first half of 1958. For example, electricity and heat energy increased by 26.2%, the fuel industry by 16.4%, ferrous metal mining and smelting by 24.3%, non-ferrous metal mining and smelting by 32.5%, machinery manufacturing and metal processing by 44%, the chemical industry by 32.2%, building materials by 36.8%, rubber by 32.7%, the textile industry by 21%, and the food industry by 14.3%.

In the first half of this year, Bulgaria's electricity production reached 1.759 billion kilowatt-hours, 25% more than the same period last year; coal reached 7,398,000 tons, 19% more; cement reached 687,000 tons, 54% more; cotton fabrics reached 99,739,000 meters, 22% more; canned fruits reached 30,148 tons, 61% more; and canned vegetables reached 12,427 tons, 51% more.

In the first half of this year, the labor productivity of workers increased by 7.1% compared to the same period last year, and freight volume increased by 27.8%.

During the first half of the year, domestic trade increased by 16.5% compared to the first half of last year, and foreign trade increased by 35%.

The communiqué pointed out that in the first half of this year, cooperative members, state farm workers, and machine tractor station workers made tremendous efforts to achieve the task of leaping forward in agricultural production. Compared with the first half of 1958, the sown area of sunflowers increased by 18%, cotton by 14.5%, and tomatoes by 48.4%.

In the first half of this year, the workload of machine tractor stations increased by 46.4% compared to the first half of last year. The national irrigated area increased by more than 150,000 hectares in six months.

As of July 1, 1959, the number of cattle in cooperatives and state farms increased by 16% compared to the same period last year, dairy cows by 28%, sheep by 17%, pigs by 57%, and poultry by more than 150%. In the first half of this year, the amount of poultry meat sold by cooperatives to the state was more than seven and a half times that of the first half of last year.

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, July 26th: The Statistical Bureau of the Albanian State Planning Commission released a communiqué on July 26th on the completion of the production plan for the first half of this year.

The communiqué stated that the total industrial output value plan for the first half of the year was fulfilled by 103.3%, an increase of 26.1% compared to the same period last year.

Compared with the first half of last year, electricity production increased by 18.8%, coal mining increased by 17.9%, chromite by 22.5%, copper by 45.1%, iron by 20.6%, and asphalt by 35.1%.

Labor productivity in several important industrial and mining production sectors increased by 18% compared to the same period last year.

When discussing the agricultural achievements in the first half of the year, the communiqué stated that the spring sowing area increased by 11.8% compared to the same period last year, and the number of tractors and other agricultural machinery increased by 355.

The communiqué stated that capital construction in the first half of the year increased by 32% compared to the same period last year, the motor transport plan was fulfilled by 114.6%, and the commodity circulation plan was fulfilled by 103.9%.

The communiqué pointed out that a price reduction in April this year benefited the people by 1.5 billion leks annually, and people's savings increased by 75% compared to the same period last year.

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, July 27th: According to the Albanian News Agency, in the first half of this year, Albania's cotton cloth production every five days was equal to the total cotton cloth production of the entire year of 1938.

This year, Albania will produce 23.3 million meters of cotton fabrics, an increase of 1.3 million meters compared to last year, which is about 65 times the production of 1938. This year, the cotton fabrics obtained by the working people will increase by 13% compared to last year.

Albania will also build a new large textile factory. This year's cotton production will increase by 34% compared to last year, and more than double compared to 1950.

### 保加利亚和阿尔巴尼亚两国政府发表公报  上半年生产计划都超额完成

1959-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚和阿尔巴尼亚两国政府发表公报
    上半年生产计划都超额完成
    据新华社索非亚25日电　今年以来保加利亚工农业生产获得很大的发展。半年中工业总产值比去年同期增长了23.4％。据农林部估计，今年小麦、向日葵、玉蜀黍、甜菜、烟草和其他作物将获得大丰收。
    根据今天发表的保加利亚部长会议中央统计局关于今年上半年发展国民经济计划执行情况的公报，今年上半年工业总产值计划完成了101.4％，许多工业部门的生产比1958年上半年有了巨大的增长，例如，电能和热能增长了26.2％，燃料工业——16.4％，黑色金属开采和冶炼——24.3％，有色金属开采和冶炼——32.5％，机器制造业和金属加工业——44％，化学工业——32.2％，建筑材料——36.8％，橡胶——32.7％，纺织工业——21％，食品工业——14.3％。
    今年上半年，保加利亚电力产量达十七亿五千九百万度；比去年同期多25％；煤——七百三十九万八千吨，多19％；水泥——六十八万七千吨，多54％；棉织品——九千九百七十三万九千米，多22％；水果罐头——三万零一百四十八吨，多61％；蔬菜罐头——一万二千四百二十七吨，多51％。
    今年上半年，工人的劳动生产率比去年同期提高了7.1％，货运量增加了27.8％。
    在上半年中，国内贸易比去年上半年增加了16.5％，对外贸易增加了35％。
    公报指出，今年上半年，合作社社员、国营农场和机器拖拉机站的工人为实现农业生产跃进任务进行了巨大工作。今年上半年同1958年上半年相比，向日葵播种面积增加了18％，棉花——14.5％，番茄——48.4％。
    今年上半年机器拖拉机站的工作量比去年上半年增加46.4％。半年中全国灌溉面积增加了十五万公顷以上。
    截至1959年7月1日，合作社和国营农场的牛头数比去年同期增加了16％，乳牛——28％，羊——17％，猪——57％，家禽——一倍半以上。今年上半年，合作社向国家交售的家禽肉类比去年上半年多七倍半。
    新华社地拉那26日电　阿尔巴尼亚国家计划委员会统计局26日发表了今年完成上半年生产计划公报。
    公报说，上半年的工业总产值计划完成了103.3％，比去年同期增加了26.1％。
  与去年上半年相比电力生产增加了18.8％，煤的开采增加了17.9％，铬铁—22.5％，铜—45.1％，铁—20.6％，沥青—35.1％。
    劳动生产率在几项重要的工矿业生产中均比去年同期提高了18％。
    公报在谈到上半年农业成就时说，春播面积比去年同期增加了11.8％，拖拉机和其他农业机器增加了三百五十五台。
    公报说，上半年基本建设比去年同期增长了32％，汽车运输计划完成了114.6％，商品流通计划完成了103.9％。
    公报指出，今年4月份的一次降低物价使人民一年获益十五亿列克，人民储蓄比去年同期增加了75％。
    新华社地拉那27日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道，在今年上半年，阿尔巴尼亚每五天的棉布产量就等于1938年全年的棉布产量。
    今年阿尔巴尼亚将生产两千三百三十万米的棉织品，比去年增加一百三十万米，约为1938年产量的六十五倍。今年劳动人民获得的棉织品将比去年增加13％。
    阿尔巴尼亚还将建筑一个新的大型纺织厂。今年的棉花产量将比去年增加34％，比1950年增加一倍以上。
