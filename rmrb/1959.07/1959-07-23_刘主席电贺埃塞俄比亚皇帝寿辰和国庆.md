Title: Chairman Liu Sends Telegram to Congratulate Ethiopian Emperor on Birthday and National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-07-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd: Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to Ethiopian Emperor Haile Selassie I, congratulating the Emperor on his birthday and Ethiopia's National Day. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Majesty Haile Selassie I, Addis Ababa, Ethiopia:

On the occasion of Your Majesty's birthday and your country's National Day, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to you and the people of your country on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness.

                                 Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                 July 21, 1959

### 刘主席电贺埃塞俄比亚皇帝寿辰和国庆

1959-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席电贺埃塞俄比亚皇帝寿辰和国庆
    新华社22日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇致电埃塞俄比亚皇帝海尔·塞拉西一世，祝贺皇帝寿辰和埃塞俄比亚国庆。电文如下：埃塞俄比亚　亚的斯亚贝巴海尔·塞拉西一世陛下：
    值此陛下寿辰和贵国国庆之际，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人名义向你和贵国人民致热诚的祝贺。祝贵国繁荣，人民幸福。
                  中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                                  1959年7月21日
