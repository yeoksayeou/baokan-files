Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Iraqi Student Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-07-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 26th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received Ali Abdu Qadar, head of the Iraqi Republic Student Delegation, and all members of the delegation this morning. Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, was present at the meeting.

### 陈毅副总理接见伊拉克学生代表团

1959-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见伊拉克学生代表团
    新华社26日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了伊拉克共和国学生代表团团长阿里·阿卜杜·卡达尔和代表团全体人员。接见时在座的有中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立。
