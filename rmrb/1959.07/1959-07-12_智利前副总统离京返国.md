Title: Former Chilean Vice President Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1959-07-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - Former Chilean Vice President Ibáñez Betrejal and his wife concluded their visit to our country and left Beijing by plane today to return home.

### 智利前副总统离京返国

1959-07-12
第3版()
专栏：

    智利前副总统离京返国
    新华社11日讯　智利前副总统贝特雷加尔和他的夫人结束了在我国的访问后，已于今日乘飞机离开北京回国。
