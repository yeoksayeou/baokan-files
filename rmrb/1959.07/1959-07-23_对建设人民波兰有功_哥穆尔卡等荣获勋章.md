Title: Gomulka and Others Awarded Medals for Contributions to the Construction of People's Poland
Author:
Date: 1959-07-23
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 22nd. On the eve of the fifteenth anniversary of Poland's National Day, the Polish State Council awarded medals on the 21st to Gomulka and other individuals who have made contributions to the construction of the country.
    The awardees also included the Speaker of the Polish Parliament, Wiechech, and the President of the Academy of Sciences, Kotarbinski, as well as thirteen other outstanding national builders, all of whom were awarded the "Builder of People's Poland" Medal.
    In his speech at the award ceremony, the Chairman of the State Council, Zawadzki, pointed out that Gomulka is an outstanding activist in the struggle for the liberation of the Polish working people and in the cause of building People's Poland.
    Polish party and government leaders attended the award ceremony.

### 对建设人民波兰有功  哥穆尔卡等荣获勋章

1959-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

    对建设人民波兰有功
    哥穆尔卡等荣获勋章
    新华社华沙22日电　在波兰国庆十五周年前夕，波兰国务委员会21日授奖给哥穆尔卡和其他一批对国家建设有功的人员。
    获奖人还包括波兰议会议长维策赫和科学院院长科塔宾斯基以及其他十一名优秀的国家建设者，他们都被授予“人民波兰建设者”勋章。
    国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基在授奖仪式的讲话中指出：哥穆尔卡是争取波兰劳动人民解放斗争中和人民波兰建设事业中的杰出的活动家。
    波兰党政领导人出席了授奖大会。
