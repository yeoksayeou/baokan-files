Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into Airspace Over Xisha Islands to Provoke, We Issue 60th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1959-07-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 25th. At 9:49 a.m. to 10:01 a.m. on July 25th, a U.S. Navy patrol plane intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island and Shidao Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was ordered to issue the 60th serious warning.

### 美机侵入我西沙群岛领空挑衅  我提出第60次严重警告

1959-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

    美机侵入我西沙群岛领空挑衅
    我提出第60次严重警告
    新华社25日讯　7月25日九时四十九分到十时零一分，美国海军巡逻机一架，侵入我国广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、石岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第六十次严重警告。
