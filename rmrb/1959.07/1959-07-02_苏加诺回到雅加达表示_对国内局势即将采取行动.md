Title: Sukarno Returns to Jakarta, States He Will Take Action Regarding the Domestic Situation

Author:

Date: 1959-07-02
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 29th - Indonesian President Sukarno, upon returning today from a foreign visit, delivered a brief speech to tens of thousands of people gathered in front of the Presidential Palace.

    He said that imperialist newspapers claim that the Indonesian nation and people are falling apart. He refuted this, saying that the Indonesian people are united and the nation is not falling apart.

    Speaking about the restoration of the 1945 Constitution, Sukarno said that he would take wise actions and decisions in a few days. He said, "I do not want to be a dictator. I am a democrat. I will make decisions based on the wishes of the people."

### 苏加诺回到雅加达表示  对国内局势即将采取行动

1959-07-02
第5版()
专栏：

    苏加诺回到雅加达表示
    对国内局势即将采取行动
    新华社雅加达29日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺今天出国访问回来，向聚集在总统府前数以万计的人们发表了简短的讲话。
    他说，帝国主义的报纸称印度尼西亚国家和人民是分崩离析的。他驳斥说，印度尼西亚人民是团结一致的，国家也并不是分崩离析的。
    谈到恢复1945年宪法时，苏加诺说，他将在几天内采取明智的行动和决定。他说，“我不愿做独裁者。我是个民主主义者。我将根据人民的愿望采取决定。”
