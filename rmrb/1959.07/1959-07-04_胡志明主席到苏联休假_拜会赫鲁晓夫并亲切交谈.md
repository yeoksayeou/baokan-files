Title: President Ho Chi Minh Vacations in the Soviet Union, Meets with Khrushchev and Engages in Cordial Conversation
Author:
Date: 1959-07-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, July 3rd, Moscow dispatch by TASS: President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Chairman of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, Ho Chi Minh, arrived in Moscow on July 2nd aboard a "Tu-104" aircraft. Greeting him at the airport were Voroshilov, Mukhitdinov, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Georgadze, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, Semenov, and other leaders of the Soviet Party and state. Also present to welcome him were the Soviet Ambassador to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Sokolov, and the Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union, Nguyen Van Kinh. President Ho Chi Minh is visiting the Soviet Union for a vacation, during which he plans to visit several republics of the Soviet Union. On the same day, President Ho Chi Minh met with Voroshilov at the Kremlin and had a friendly and cordial conversation.

Xinhua News Agency, July 3rd, Moscow dispatch by TASS: President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Ho Chi Minh, paid a visit to Khrushchev at the Kremlin on July 3rd. Ho Chi Minh and Khrushchev had a cordial conversation. On the same day, a banquet was held at the Kremlin in honor of Ho Chi Minh. Soviet Party and government leaders such as Voroshilov, Ignatov, Mikoyan, Mukhitdinov, and Khrushchev attended the banquet.

### 胡志明主席到苏联休假  拜会赫鲁晓夫并亲切交谈

1959-07-04
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席到苏联休假
    拜会赫鲁晓夫并亲切交谈
    新华社3日讯　塔斯社莫斯科2日讯：越南民主共和国主席、越南劳动党中央委员会主席胡志明2日乘“图——一○四”飞机到达莫斯科。前往机场迎接的有：伏罗希洛夫、穆希金诺夫、苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书格奥尔加泽、苏联外交部副部长谢苗诺夫以及苏联党和国家其他领导人。苏联驻越南民主共和国大使索科洛夫和越南民主共和国驻苏联大使阮文镜也在场欢迎。胡志明主席这次来苏是来休假的，休假期间他准备访问苏联的几个共和国。同日胡志明主席在克里姆林宫会见了伏罗希洛夫并进行了友好而亲切的谈话。
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：越南民主共和国主席胡志明3日在克里姆林宫拜会了赫鲁晓夫。胡志明同赫鲁晓夫进行了亲切的交谈。同一天，在克里姆林宫为胡志明举行了宴会。苏联党和政府的领导人伏罗希洛夫、伊格纳托夫、米高扬、穆希金诺夫和赫鲁晓夫等出席了宴会。
