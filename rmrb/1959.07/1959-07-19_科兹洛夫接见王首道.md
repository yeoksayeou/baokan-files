Title: Kozlov Receives Wang Shoudao
Author:
Date: 1959-07-19
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 17th: Kozlov, First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, received Wang Shoudao, the Chinese Minister of Communications who is visiting the Soviet Union at invitation, and the members of the Chinese Water Transport Delegation he led, this morning.
    During the meeting, they had a cordial and friendly conversation.
    The Chinese Water Transport Delegation arrived in Moscow from Beijing on the 11th.

### 科兹洛夫接见王首道

1959-07-19
第3版()
专栏：

    科兹洛夫接见王首道
    新华社莫斯科17日电　苏联部长会议第一副主席科兹洛夫今天上午接见了应邀前来访问的中国交通部长王首道和由他率领的中国水运代表团团员。
    接见时双方进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    中国水运代表团是11日从北京前来莫斯科的。
