Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into Waters off Pingtan and Baiquan Islands; We Issue 55th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1959-07-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 6th: One U.S. warship intruded into the waters off Pingtan and Baiquan Islands, Fujian Province, from 22:38 on July 5th to 9:05 on July 6th. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, a spokesman for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 55th serious warning.

### 美舰侵入我平潭、白犬地区海域  我提出第55次严重警告

1959-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

    美舰侵入我平潭、白犬地区海域
    我提出第55次严重警告
    新华社6日讯　7月5日二十二时三十八分至6日九时零五分，美国军舰一艘侵入我福建省平潭、白犬地区海域。对于美国军舰这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第五十五次严重警告。
