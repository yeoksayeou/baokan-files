Title: Bolivian Song and Dance Troupe Performs in Beijing; Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Leaders of the Troupe
Author:
Date: 1959-07-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, July 28th: The Bolivian "Fundacion" Song and Dance Troupe gave its premiere performance at the Tianqiao Theater tonight, receiving a warm welcome from the audience in the capital.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, Vice Minister of Culture Qian Junrui, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Zhixiang, and prominent figures from the capital's literary and art circles watched tonight's performance.

The first Bolivian artistic group from Latin America, in its more than two hours of performance tonight, presented more than twenty song, dance, and music programs rich in strong national characteristics and folk art features, showcasing the life and struggle of the Bolivian people to the audience in the capital in many aspects.

During the intermission, Vice Premier Chen Yi received the head of the song and dance troupe, Ascarrunz, and the artistic director, Mrs. Ascarrunz. Vice Premier Chen Yi praised their art for vividly depicting the life of the Bolivian people and warmly congratulated them on the success of their performance. Head of the Troupe Ascarrunz said: We brought art and friendship, and the hearts of the Bolivian people are with the Chinese people.

After the performance, the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries presented the song and dance troupe with a flower basket. Amidst the long applause of the audience warmly congratulating the Bolivian artists on the success of their performance, Vice Premier Chen Yi and others went on stage, warmly shook hands with all the performers, and took a group photo.

The song and dance troupe will visit and perform in Beijing, Jinan, Shanghai, Suzhou, Tianjin, Changchun, Harbin, and other places for a month and a half. (Attached picture)

After the performance, Vice Premier Chen Yi and others took a group photo with the actors. (Photo by Lu Xiangyou, our reporter)

### 玻利维亚歌舞团在京演出  陈毅副总理接见歌舞团领导人

1959-07-29
第4版()
专栏：

    玻利维亚歌舞团在京演出
    陈毅副总理接见歌舞团领导人
    据新华社28日讯　玻利维亚“芳达西亚”歌舞团今晚在天桥剧场首次演出，受到了首都观众的热烈欢迎。
    陈毅副总理，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，文化部副部长钱俊瑞，外交部副部长曾涌泉，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥和首都文艺界著名人士，观看了今晚的演出。
    来自拉丁美洲的第一个玻利维亚艺术团体，今晚在两个多小时的演出中，表演了二十多个富于浓厚民族色彩和民间艺术特色的歌舞音乐节目，多方面地向首都观众展现了玻利维亚人民的生活和斗争。
    演出休息时，陈毅副总理接见了歌舞团团长阿斯卡伦斯和艺术指导阿斯卡伦斯夫人。陈毅副总理称赞他们的艺术很好地表现了玻利维亚人民的生活，热烈祝贺他们演出的成功。阿斯卡伦斯团长说：我们带来了艺术和友谊，玻利维亚人民同中国人民的心是在一起的。
    演出结束后，对外文协向歌舞团赠送花篮。在全场热烈祝贺玻利维亚艺术家演出成功的长时间掌声中，陈毅副总理等登上舞台，同全体演员热烈握手，合影留念。
    歌舞团将在北京、济南、上海、苏州、天津、长春、哈尔滨等地访问演出一个半月。（附图片）
