Title: Vice-President of the All-India Peace Council, General Sok Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-07-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, July 4th. General Sok, member of the World Peace Council, Vice-President of the All-India Peace Council, and renowned bacteriologist, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.

General Sok is visiting China at the invitation of Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace, after attending the special meeting of the World Peace Council held in Stockholm. Those who welcomed him at the airport today included Huang Jiasi, President of the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences, and Li Deqi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Medical Association.

### 全印和平理事会副主席索克少将到京

1959-07-05
第3版()
专栏：

    全印和平理事会副主席索克少将到京
    新华社4日讯　世界和平理事会理事、全印和平理事会副主席、著名细菌学家索克少将，在今天早晨乘飞机抵达北京。
    索克少将是在出席了斯德哥尔摩举行的世界和平理事会特别会议后，应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若的邀请，来我国访问的。今天到机场欢迎的有中国医学科学院院长黄家驷、中华医学会副秘书长李得奇等人。
