Title: Gromyko Meets with Lloyd
Author:
Date: 1959-07-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 16th: TASS, Geneva, July 16th: Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko met with British Foreign Secretary Lloyd at the Soviet delegation's residence on the 16th. During the meeting, they exchanged views on the work of the Geneva conference.

### 葛罗米柯会见劳埃德

1959-07-18
第4版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯会见劳埃德
    新华社17日讯　塔斯社日内瓦16日讯：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯16日在苏联代表团寓所会见了英国外交大臣劳埃德。在会见时交换了关于日内瓦会议工作问题的意见。
