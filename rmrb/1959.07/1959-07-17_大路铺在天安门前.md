Title: The Road Paved Before Tiananmen
Author: Wu Guan
Date: 1959-07-17
Page: 8

    The Road Paved Before Tiananmen
    Wu Guan
    The road is paved before Tiananmen,
    A solid roadbed, a smooth surface,
    Stretching straight to the heavens at both ends,
    The sun slowly departs westward from here,
    The east wind urges him to gallop along.
    The road is paved before Tiananmen,
    Green trees provide shade, red flowers are bright,
    Chairman Mao meets us here,
    Warm gestures, kind words,
    Happy memories over and over again.
    The road is paved before Tiananmen,
    Tiananmen is the crown of the motherland,
    The road, like giant arms, branches out on both sides,
    Embracing the earth in a warm embrace,
    Nurturing humanity's eternal spring.
    The road is paved before Tiananmen,
    Farewell, I will travel far,
    To the ends of the earth, to desolate wilderness,
    Wherever there is a road,
    It will be forever connected to you.

### 大路铺在天安门前
吴灌
1959-07-17
第8版()
专栏：

    大路铺在天安门前
    吴灌
    大路铺在天安门前，
    坚实的路基，光滑的路面，
    东西两头直通上青天，
    太阳从这里缓步西去，
    东风催他一同快马加鞭。
    大路铺在天安门前，
    绿树成荫，红花鲜艳，
    毛主席和我们在这里相见，
    热情的手势，亲切的语言，
    幸福的回忆千遍万遍。
    大路铺在天安门前，
    天安门是祖国的冠冕，
    路，像巨臂分向两边，
    把大地拥入温暖的怀抱，
    孕育人类永恒的春天。
    大路铺在天安门前，
    告别你，我将走得很远，
    天涯海角，漠野荒原，
    只要那里有路
    那里便会和你永远相连。
