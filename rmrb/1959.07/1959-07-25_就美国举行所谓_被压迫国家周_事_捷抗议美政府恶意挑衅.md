Title: Czechoslovakia Protests US Government's Malicious Provocation Regarding the US Holding of the So-Called "Captive Nations Week"
Author: 
Date: 1959-07-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 23 - Czechoslovak Deputy Foreign Minister Hajek, on behalf of the Czechoslovak government, lodged a strong protest on July 23 with U.S. Charge d'Affaires Crawford, protesting the U.S. government's resolution to hold a so-called "Captive Nations Week" targeting socialist countries. In this resolution, Czechoslovakia was subjected to obvious attacks.

Hajek pointed out that this was a new provocative action by the United States. He asked the U.S. Charge d'Affaires to remind U.S. President Eisenhower that Czechoslovakia is an independent country and its system can only be decided by its own people.

### 就美国举行所谓“被压迫国家周”事  捷抗议美政府恶意挑衅

1959-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    就美国举行所谓“被压迫国家周”事
    捷抗议美政府恶意挑衅
    据新华社布拉格23日电　捷克斯洛伐克外交部副部长哈耶克23日代表捷克斯洛伐克政府向美国临时代办克劳福德提出了一项强烈抗议，抗议美国政府关于针对社会主义国家举行所谓“被压迫国家周”的决议。在这项决议中，捷克斯洛伐克受到了明显的攻击。
    哈耶克指出，这是美国的一项新的挑衅行动。他要求美国代办提醒美国总统艾森豪威尔注意：捷克斯洛伐克是一个独立的国家，它的制度只有它自己的人民才能决定。
