Title: Good News Continues from Production Fronts in Bulgaria, Hungary, etc.: Industrial Production Plans Exceeded in the First Half of the Year; Significant Achievements in Production by Various Industrial and Mining Enterprises in Albania in the First Half of the Year
Author:
Date: 1959-07-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, July 10th: Bulgaria's industrial production has exceeded its production plan for the first six months by 1.4%, with the total industrial output value increasing by 23.4% compared to the same period last year. According to the plan, this year's production should increase by 27.8% compared to last year. Preliminary statistical materials show that various industrial sectors have achieved significant results in the first half of this year: Electricity production plan was completed by 100.9% in the first half of the year, an increase of 26.2% compared to the same period last year; the coal production plan was exceeded by 6%, an increase of 16.9% compared to the same period last year; non-ferrous metal industry and non-ferrous metal mining industry exceeded the plan by 3.7%, an increase of 32.5% compared to the same period last year; the iron and steel industry and iron ore mining industry exceeded the plan by 6.9%, an increase of 24.3% compared to the same period last year; the machinery manufacturing and metal processing industry exceeded the plan by 6%, an increase of 44% compared to the same period last year; the chemical industry exceeded the plan by 5.1%, an increase of 32.2% compared to the same period last year; the textile industry exceeded the plan by 4.5%, an increase of 21.1% compared to the same period last year.

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, July 12th: In the first half of this year, Hungary's industrial production increased by approximately 9% compared to the same period last year, exceeding the original semi-annual production plan by 3%. The average output per worker increased by 3% compared to the same period last year.
	This was announced by the Hungarian Central Statistical Office in a communique issued today regarding the implementation of the national economic plan for the first half of the year.
	The communique stated that in the first six months, the production of heavy industrial enterprises increased by 13% compared to the same period last year, with the production growth of machinery manufacturing, electrical machinery industry, and building materials industry exceeding the average development level of general heavy industrial enterprises. The production of agricultural machinery increased by one-third compared to the same period last year. The production of light industrial and food industrial enterprises increased by 3%.
	In the first half of this year, coal production reached 12.5 million tons, an increase of 4% compared to the same period last year; electricity production reached 3.366 billion kilowatt-hours, an increase of 9% compared to the same period last year; steel production was 856,000 tons, an increase of 4% compared to the first half of last year; oil production increased by 15% compared to the first half of last year; tractors increased by 76%; television sets increased by 114%; nitrogen fertilizer increased by 127%.
	Regarding agriculture, by the end of March this year, there were a total of 4,517 agricultural production cooperatives nationwide, an increase of over 1,000 compared to the end of last year. Their cultivated land area accounted for 36.1% of the total cultivated land area in the country. Currently, the cultivated land area of state farms and agricultural production cooperatives accounts for 50.3% of the total cultivated land area in the country.
	The degree of agricultural mechanization has also significantly improved. In terms of foreign trade, imports increased by 17% compared to the same period last year, and exports increased by 14%. The communique pointed out that residents' income increased by 8% compared to the same period last year.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 12th: The total industrial output value of the German Democratic Republic in the first half of this year increased by 12.4% compared to the same period last year.
	The Press Office of the State Planning Commission of the German Democratic Republic issued a communique on the production situation in the first half of this year on the 11th. The communique stated that in the first half of the year, labor productivity in socialist industrial enterprises increased by 9.6%, and in centrally managed enterprises, labor productivity increased by 10.7%.
	Basic construction investment increased by 24% compared to the same period last year. The workload of the construction industry increased by 26% compared to the same period last year.
	The volume of foreign trade increased by 13.9% compared to the same period last year.
	The communique stated that due to the development of industrial production, the increase in foreign trade volume, and the increase in various livestock products, the supply to residents has greatly increased in the first half of this year. In addition, wages and pensions were increased and prices were lowered in the first half of this year. Therefore, the domestic retail sales volume in the first half of this year increased by 12.6% compared to the same period last year.

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, July 9th: According to reports in local newspapers: Albania's industrial and mining enterprises have achieved significant results in production in the first half of this year.
	The Stalin Textile Factory, one of the largest enterprises in the country, exceeded its plan for the first half of the year, with production increasing by 15% compared to the same period last year. The oil workers of the Stalin Oil Union Factory exceeded their semi-annual plan by 14.1%, and the Zhelik refinery also exceeded its plan by 6.7%. The Enver Hoxha Machine Factory produced over 4 million lek worth of products above the plan in the first half of the year. Good news also came frequently from mining enterprises in various regions. The semi-annual production of chromium ore, which occupies an important position in Albania's exports, exceeded the original plan by 4,000 tons. The Korça Coal Mine also exceeded its semi-annual plan. Various transportation enterprises also exceeded their semi-annual plans.

### 保匈等国生产战线捷报频传  上半年工业生产计划超额完成  阿尔巴尼亚各工矿企业上半年生产获显著成就

1959-07-13
第4版()
专栏：

    保匈等国生产战线捷报频传
    上半年工业生产计划超额完成
    阿尔巴尼亚各工矿企业上半年生产获显著成就
    新华社索非亚10日电　保加利亚的工业生产已经超额1.4％完成了头六个月的生产计划，工业总产值比去年同期增长了23.4％。按照计划，今年要比去年增产27.8％。初步统计材料表明，今年上半年各个工业部门取得了巨大成绩：上半年电力生产计划完成了100.9％，比去年同期增长了26.2％；超额6％完成了煤炭生产计划，比去年同期增长了16.9％；有色金属工业和有色金属开采工业超额3.7％完成了计划，比去年同期增长了32.5％；钢铁工业和铁矿开采工业超额6.9％完成了计划，比去年同期增长了24.3％；机器制造业和金属加工业超额6％完成了计划，比去年同期增长了44％；化学工业超额5.1％完成了计划，比去年同期增长了32.2％；纺织工业超额4.5％完成了计划，比去年同期增长了21.1％。
    新华社布达佩斯12日电　今年上半年内匈牙利的工业生产比去年同时期增长了大约9％，超额3％完成了原订的半年生产计划。每个工人的平均生产量比去年同时期增加了3％。
    这是匈牙利中央统计局在今天公布的关于上半年国民经济计划执行情况的公报中宣布的。
    公报说，头六个月内重工业企业的生产比去年同时期增长了13％，其中机器制造业、电机工业和建筑器材工业生产的增长超过了一般重工业企业发展的平均水平。农业机器的生产比去年同时期提高了三分之一。轻工业和食品工业企业的生产增长了3％。
    今年上半年煤产量达一千二百五十万吨，比去年同时期增加了4％；电产量达三十三亿六千六百万度，比去年同时期增加了9％；钢的产量是八十五万六千吨，比去年上半年增加了4％；石油的产量比去年上半年增加了15％；拖拉机增加了76％；电视机增加了114％；氮肥增加了127％。
    关于农业方面，到今年3月底为止，全国共有四千五百一十七个农业生产合作社，比去年年底增加了一千多个。它们的耕地面积占全国耕地总面积的36.1％。目前国营农场和农业生产合作社的耕地面积已占全国耕地面积的50.3％。
    农业的机械化程度也有了显著的提高。在对外贸易方面，进口额比去年同时期提高了17％，出口额提高了14％。公报指出，居民的收入比去年同时期提高了8％。
    新华社柏林12日电　德意志民主共和国今年上半年的工业生产总产值比去年同期增长了12.4％。
    德意志民主共和国国家计划委员会新闻处11日发表了今年上半年的生产情况公报。公报说，在前半年，社会主义工业企业中的劳动生产率提高了9.6％，中央直接管理的企业中，劳动生产率提高了10.7％。
    基本建设投资比去年同期增长了24％。建筑业工作量比去年同期增长了26％。
    对外贸易额比去年同期增长了13.9％。
    公报说，由于工业生产的发展，对外贸易额的增长和各种畜产品的增多，今年前半年对居民的供应大大增加，此外，今年上半年提高了工资和养老金，又降低了物价，因此，今年前半年国内商品零售额比去年同期增加了12.6％。
    新华社地拉那9日电　据此间报纸报道：阿尔巴尼亚各工矿企业在今年上半年的生产中获得了显著成就。
    全国最大的企业之一斯大林纺织工厂超额完成了上半年计划，生产量比去年同期增加了15％。斯大林石油联合工厂的采油工人超额14.1％完成了半年计划，柴立克炼油厂也超额6.7％完成了计划。恩维尔·霍查机器厂在上半年超计划生产了价值四百多万里克的产品。各地的采矿企业也捷报频传，在阿尔巴尼亚出口中占有重要位置的铬矿石的上半年产量超过了原订计划四千吨。科尔察煤矿也超额完成了半年计划。超额完成半年计划的还有各运输企业等。
