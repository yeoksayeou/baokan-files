Title: Khrushchev Receives a Group of Visiting American Governors
Author:
Date: 1959-07-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 7th: TASS reports that on July 7th, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, received a group of American governors who were concluding their visit to the Soviet Union at the Kremlin and held talks with them.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见一批访苏美国州长

1959-07-09
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见一批访苏美国州长
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科7日讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫7日在克里姆林宫接见了一批结束在苏联访问的美国州长，并同他们进行了谈话。
