Title: Poem on the "Yan'an Bridge" (Picture)
Author: Dong Biwu
Date: 1959-07-01
Page: 8

    Poem on the "Yan'an Bridge"
    Dong Biwu
    Autumn waters fill the river, no trace of floods,
    The ford blocked, hindering travelers.
    A bridge is built, connecting east and west banks,
    Before the Pagoda Hill, no longer asking for a crossing.
        (Painting by Zhang Yanqing)

### 题“延安大桥”（图片）
董必武
1959-07-01
第8版()
专栏：

    题“延安大桥”
    董必武
    秋水盈川没涨痕，
    步头无渡阻行人。
    一桥架合东西岸，
    宝塔山前不问津。
        （张彦青　画）
