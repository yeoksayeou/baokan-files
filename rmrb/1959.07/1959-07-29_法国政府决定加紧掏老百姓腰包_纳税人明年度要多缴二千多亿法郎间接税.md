Title: French Government Decides to Tighten the Squeeze on the People: Taxpayers to Pay Over 200 Billion More Francs in Indirect Taxes Next Year
Author:
Date: 1959-07-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Paris: French taxpayers will pay 220 billion more francs in indirect taxes in the 1960 budget year.

French Finance Minister Pinay revealed this in a recent cabinet meeting report on the preparation of the 1960 budget. Pinay's planned 1960 budget revenue is 5.8 trillion francs, and expenditure is 6.4 trillion francs.

The 1960 budget revenue will be 220 billion francs more than the 1959 budget. This money will come from taxpayers paying more indirect taxes. Therefore, Pinay refuted the so-called "tax reform," that is, reducing taxes, with the facts. The government has openly admitted that the 1960 defense budget will reach an unprecedented record of at least 1.8 trillion francs. This defense budget will swallow up the entire increased tax revenue of 220 billion francs.

### 法国政府决定加紧掏老百姓腰包  纳税人明年度要多缴二千多亿法郎间接税

1959-07-29
第5版()
专栏：

    法国政府决定加紧掏老百姓腰包
    纳税人明年度要多缴二千多亿法郎间接税
    据新华社讯　巴黎消息：法国纳税人将在1960年预算年度多缴纳二千二百亿法郎间接税。
    法国财政部长比内在最近一次内阁会议报告1960年度预算准备工作时透露了这一点。比内计划中的1960年度预算收入为五万八千亿法郎，支出为六万四千亿法郎。
    1960年度预算的收入要比1959年度多二千二百亿法郎，这笔钱将从纳税人多缴纳间接税取得。因此比内就用事实否认了所谓将进行“捐税改革”、即降低捐税的说法。政府方面已经公然承认，1960年度国防预算至少将达一万八千亿法郎的空前纪录。这笔国防预算将把整个增加的二千二百亿法郎税收吞噬掉。
