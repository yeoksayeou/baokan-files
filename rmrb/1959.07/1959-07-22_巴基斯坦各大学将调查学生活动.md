Title: Pakistani Universities to Investigate Student Activities
Author: 
Date: 1959-07-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, July 20th: According to a recent issue of the Karachi English-language weekly "Sunday Post", Karachi university authorities plan to conduct a thorough investigation into the behavior and activities of students at Karachi University and other institutions of higher learning.

This investigation, which the magazine calls "vetting", aims to eliminate "saboteurs" from student groups. This vetting will also not spare female students.

### 巴基斯坦各大学将调查学生活动

1959-07-22
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦各大学将调查学生活动
    新华社卡拉奇20日电　据最近一期卡拉奇英文周刊“星期日邮报”消息，卡拉奇各大学当局打算对卡拉奇大学和其他高等学校的学生的行为和活动进行一次彻底的调查。
    被这家杂志称为“甄别”的这次调查的目的，是要清除学生团体中的“破坏分子”。这次甄别也将不放过女学生。
