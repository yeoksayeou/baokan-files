Title: Wuhan Iron and Steel No. 2 Blast Furnace Begins Baking

Author:
Date: 1959-07-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, July 9th. The Wuhan Iron and Steel No. 2 Blast Furnace has begun its production prelude. This morning, the hot blast stove of the blast furnace sent powerful wind into the furnace. No. 2 Blast Furnace has officially started baking.

Currently, the No. 2 Blast Furnace project is basically completed. The blast furnace has been connected to water, electricity, ventilation, gas, steam, and compressed air. The hot blast stove has been baked. After the furnace bricks are dried, No. 2 Blast Furnace can be charged for production.

Before the baking of No. 2 Blast Furnace, the Wuhan Iron and Steel Party Committee put forward the slogan of "Strict Inspection, More Testing, Be Prepared" to all producers and builders of the blast furnace. Now, the workers of No. 2 Blast Furnace are striving to complete the baking task ahead of schedule with great energy and enthusiasm, to ensure that the blast furnace is put into production as soon as possible, and to fight hard in the third quarter to produce more iron and good iron to welcome the 10th anniversary of the National Day.

### 武钢二号高炉开始烘炉

1959-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    武钢二号高炉开始烘炉
    新华社武汉9日电　武钢二号高炉揭开了生产的序幕。今天早晨，高炉的热风炉把强大的风力送进了高炉。二号高炉正式烘炉了。
    目前，二号高炉工程已基本竣工，高炉已经通水，通电，通风，通煤气、蒸气和压缩空气。热风炉已经烘好。等炉砖烘干以后，二号高炉就可以上料生产了。
    二号高炉在烘炉前，武钢党委向高炉全体生产者、建设者提出了“严查、多试、有备”的口号。现在二号高炉职工正以极大的干劲和热情，力争提前完成烘炉任务，保证让高炉尽快地投入生产，并且要大战第三季度，多出铁、出好铁，来迎接国庆十周年。
