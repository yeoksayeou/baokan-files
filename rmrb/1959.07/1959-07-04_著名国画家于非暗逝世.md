Title: Famous Chinese Painter Yu Feian Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1959-07-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 3rd. The famous Chinese painter, Vice President of the Beijing Chinese Painting Academy, and Vice Chairman of the Beijing Chinese Painting Research Association, Yu Feian, passed away at Beijing Hospital today after failing to respond to medical treatment.
    Yu Feian had profound research on painting, calligraphy, and seal carving during his lifetime, and was particularly good at meticulous flower and bird paintings. His works such as peonies, dahlias, cannas, doves of peace, red apricots, and bamboo partridges were well received in the Chinese painting circle.
    The organization for his funeral arrangements has been formed by Guo Moruo and others.

### 著名国画家于非暗逝世

1959-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

    著名国画家于非暗逝世
    新华社3日讯　著名国画家、北京中国画院副院长、北京中国画研究会副会长于非暗，因病医治无效，今天在北京医院逝世。
    于非暗生前对绘画、书法和篆刻很有研究，特别擅长工笔花鸟画。他画的牡丹、大理花、美人蕉、和平鸽和红杏、山鹧等作品，在国画界中受到好评。
    为他治丧的组织已经由郭沫若等人组成。
