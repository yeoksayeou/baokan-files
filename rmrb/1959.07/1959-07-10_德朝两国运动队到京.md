Title: German and Korean Sports Teams Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-07-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, July 9th. The German Democratic Republic Sailing Team and the Korean Gliding Team, invited to visit our country, arrived in Beijing on July 6th and 9th respectively.

### 德朝两国运动队到京

1959-07-10
第4版()
专栏：

    德朝两国运动队到京
    新华社9日讯　应邀来我国访问的德意志民主共和国航海运动队和朝鲜滑翔运动队，已经先后在7月6日和9日到达北京。
