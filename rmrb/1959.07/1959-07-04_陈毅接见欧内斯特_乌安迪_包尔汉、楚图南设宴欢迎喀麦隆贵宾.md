Title: Chen Yi Receives Ernest Ouandié, Burhan Shahidi and Chu Tunan Host Banquet Welcoming Cameroonian Guests
Author:
Date: 1959-07-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 3rd: Vice Premier Chen Yi received Ernest Ouandié, Vice President of the Cameroonian People's Union, at noon today and had a friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, Zhu Ziqi, Secretary-General, and Gong Dafei, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Xinhua News Agency, July 3rd: Burhan Shahidi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, and Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome Ernest Ouandié, Vice President of the Cameroonian People's Union. (Photo attached)

Right: Vice Premier Chen Yi and Ernest Ouandié shake hands warmly.
              Xinhua News Agency Reporter Liu Dong’ao

### 陈毅接见欧内斯特·乌安迪  包尔汉、楚图南设宴欢迎喀麦隆贵宾

1959-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅接见欧内斯特·乌安迪
    包尔汉、楚图南设宴欢迎喀麦隆贵宾
    新华社3日讯　陈毅副总理在今天中午接见了喀麦隆人民联盟副主席欧内斯特·乌安迪，进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志、秘书长朱子奇，外交部西亚非洲司副司长宫达非。
    新华社3日讯　中国亚非团结委员会副主席包尔汉和对外文化协会会长楚图南，今晚设宴欢迎喀麦隆人民联盟副主席欧内斯特·乌安迪。（附图片）
    右图：陈毅副总理和欧内斯特·乌安迪亲切握手。
              新华社记者　刘东鳌摄
