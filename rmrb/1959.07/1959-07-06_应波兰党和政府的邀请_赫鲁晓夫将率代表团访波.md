Title: At the invitation of the Polish Party and Government, Khrushchev will lead a delegation to visit Poland
Author:
Date: 1959-07-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 5th: TASS reports that the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union have decided to send a Party and government delegation headed by N.S. Khrushchev, First Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, to Poland for a friendly visit.

The delegation is visiting Poland at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and the Polish government. The delegation will depart on the 14th.

### 应波兰党和政府的邀请  赫鲁晓夫将率代表团访波

1959-07-06
第4版()
专栏：

    应波兰党和政府的邀请
    赫鲁晓夫将率代表团访波
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议决定委派以苏共中央第一书记、部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫为首的党政代表团到波兰进行友好访问。
    代表团是应波兰统一工人党中央委员会和波兰政府的邀请前往波兰的。代表团将在14日启程。
