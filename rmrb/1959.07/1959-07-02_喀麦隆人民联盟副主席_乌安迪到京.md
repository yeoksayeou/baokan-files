Title: Cameroonian People's Union Vice-Chairman Ouandie Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-07-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 1st. Ernest Ouandie, Vice-Chairman of the Cameroonian People's Union, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to China at the invitation of the Chinese Committee for Asian-African Solidarity.

### 喀麦隆人民联盟副主席  乌安迪到京

1959-07-02
第4版()
专栏：

    喀麦隆人民联盟副主席
    乌安迪到京
    新华社1日讯　喀麦隆人民联盟副主席欧内斯特·乌安迪，应中国亚非团结委员会邀请来我国访问，在今天乘飞机到达北京。
