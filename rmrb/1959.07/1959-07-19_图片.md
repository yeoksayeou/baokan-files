Title: Picture
Author: Jin Chuanzhen
Date: 1959-07-19
Page: 7

    You feed the silkworms, I gather mulberry leaves,
    The silkworms spin cocoons and send them to the factory.
    Weave silk into clothes,
    Wear them on New Year's Day to celebrate the bumper harvest.
     Jin Chuanzhen, poem and paper-cut
