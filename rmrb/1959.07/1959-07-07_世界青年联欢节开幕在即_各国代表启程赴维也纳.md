Title: World Youth Festival is About to Open, Representatives from Various Countries Set Off for Vienna
Author:
Date: 1959-07-07
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 6th - The 7th World Youth Festival is about to open, and delegations from many countries have already set off for Vienna.

　　Currently, in addition to Australia and Indonesia, which had already departed, delegations from the United States, Costa Rica, Brazil, Nicaragua, Chile, Honduras, and other countries farther from Vienna have also set off one after another.

　　The Vietnamese youth delegation, including 106 members, set off on June 29th.

　　A Cambodian delegation consisting of fourteen people also departed from Phnom Penh on July 5th. The delegation will also conduct short visits while passing through China and the Soviet Union. Delegations from Japan, India, and other Southeast Asian countries will also depart one after another soon.

　　Xinhua News Agency, 6th - A Vietnamese youth delegation consisting of seventy-one people, participating in the 7th World Youth and Students Festival for Peace and Friendship, left Beijing by international train today via the Soviet Union for Vienna. The Vietnamese youth delegation arrived in Beijing on July 2nd.

### 世界青年联欢节开幕在即  各国代表启程赴维也纳

1959-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

    世界青年联欢节开幕在即
    各国代表启程赴维也纳
    新华社6日讯　第七届世界青年联欢节开幕在即，不少国家的代表团已启程前往维也纳。
    目前，除澳大利亚、印度尼西亚早已出发外，美国、哥斯达黎加、巴西、尼加拉瓜、智利、洪都拉斯和其他一些距维也纳较远国家的代表团都已先后启程。
    包括一百零六名团员的越南青年代表团已经在6月29日启程。
    一个由十四人组成的柬埔寨代表团也于7月5日从金边出发。代表团在经过中国和苏联时还将进行短期访问。日本、印度和其他一些东南亚国家的代表团也将在最近陆续动身。
    新华社6日讯　参加第七届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的越南青年代表团一行七十一人，今天从北京乘国际列车经苏联去维也纳。越南青年代表团是在7月2日到达北京的。
