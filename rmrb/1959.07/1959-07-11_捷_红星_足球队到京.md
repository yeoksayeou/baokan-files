Title: Czechoslovak "Red Star" Football Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-07-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 10th — The 25-member "Red Star" football team from Brno, Czechoslovakia, led by team leader Svoboda, arrived in Beijing by plane today, invited to visit and play friendly matches in our country.

The first friendly match of the visiting team in our country is scheduled to be held in Beijing on the 12th, and the opponent will be the Beijing football team.

### 捷“红星”足球队到京

1959-07-11
第4版()
专栏：

    捷“红星”足球队到京
    新华社10日讯　应邀来我国访问和进行友谊比赛的捷克斯洛伐克布尔诺市“红星”足球队一行二十五人，今天由领队斯沃博率领乘飞机到达北京。
    客队这次来我国的第一场友谊比赛，预定12日在北京举行，对手是北京足球队。
