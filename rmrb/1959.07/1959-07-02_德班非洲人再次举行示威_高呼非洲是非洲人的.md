Title: Durban Africans Demonstrate Again, Chanting "Africa is for Africans"
Author:
Date: 1959-07-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Durban, 30th -- Hundreds of Africans staged a demonstration in front of a municipal beer hall in the Cato Manor African residential area of Durban port on the 29th, where large-scale demonstrations against racial discrimination had recently erupted.

The municipal beer halls in this area have been closed since the demonstrations against racial discrimination in mid-June. On the 29th, three of them reopened to test the reaction of the Africans. The Africans therefore held demonstrations to protest.

Armed police attacked the demonstrators and arrested more than 40 of them on so-called "public disturbance" charges. When a demonstrator wearing an African National Congress badge was arrested, he shouted "Mayibuye" (meaning "Africa is for Africans"), and other Africans responded by shouting the same slogan.

### 德班非洲人再次举行示威  高呼非洲是非洲人的

1959-07-02
第5版()
专栏：

    德班非洲人再次举行示威
    高呼非洲是非洲人的
    新华社30日讯　德班消息：在不久前爆发大规模反抗种族歧视示威的德班港卡托马诺尔非洲人居住区，29日有好几百名非洲人在一家市营啤酒店门前举行示威。
    这个地区的市营啤酒店从6月中旬发生反抗种族歧视的示威以后就停业了。29日有三家重新开业试探非洲人的反应。非洲人因此举行示威表示抗议。
    武装警察攻击示威者，并且以所谓“公共骚乱罪”逮捕了四十多名示威者。当一名带有非洲人国民大会肩章的示威者被捕时，他高呼“马伊布伊”（意思是“非洲是非洲人的”），其他非洲人也高呼同样的口号响应他。
