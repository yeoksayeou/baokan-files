Title: Lebanese Government Sends Personnel to Syria to Investigate Arrest of Lebanese Communist Leader
Author:
Date: 1959-07-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, 19th - According to a report from the Eastern News Agency, the Lebanese government has dispatched an official from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs to Damascus to investigate the arrest of Lebanese patriot Farajallah Helou.

The leader of the Lebanese Communist Party was arrested by the authorities of the United Arab Republic in Damascus on June 25.

According to "Al Nada" newspaper, due to the widespread protest movement demanding Helou's release, the UAR authorities have transferred Helou from the Mezzeh prison in Damascus to an unknown location. The newspaper also stated that the life of this Lebanese patriot is under serious threat.

### 黎巴嫩政府派员赴叙利亚  调查黎共领袖被捕情况

1959-07-20
第4版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩政府派员赴叙利亚
    调查黎共领袖被捕情况
    新华社贝鲁特19日电　据东方通讯社报道，黎巴嫩政府已经派出一名外交部官员到大马士革去调查黎巴嫩爱国者法拉贾拉·赫卢的被捕情况。
    这位黎巴嫩共产党领袖是6月25日在大马士革被阿联当局逮捕的。
    据“呼声报”报道，由于展开了广泛的抗议运动，要求释放赫卢，阿联当局已把赫卢从大马士革的马泽监狱转移到没有人知道的地方去。这家报纸又说，这位黎巴嫩爱国者的生命正受到严重的威胁。
