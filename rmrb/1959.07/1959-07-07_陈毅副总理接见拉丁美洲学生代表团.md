Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Latin American Student Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-07-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Vice Premier Chen Yi received a student delegation today from ten Latin American countries: Argentina, Brazil, Chile, Cuba, Colombia, Uruguay, Peru, Venezuela, Bolivia, and Ecuador.
    Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, was present at the meeting. The student delegation from ten Latin American countries will leave Beijing by plane on the morning of the 7th to return to their home countries.

### 陈毅副总理接见拉丁美洲学生代表团

1959-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见拉丁美洲学生代表团
    新华社6日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了阿根廷、巴西、智利、古巴、哥伦比亚、乌拉圭、秘鲁、委内瑞拉、玻利维亚、厄瓜多尔等十个拉丁美洲国家的学生代表团。
    接见时在座的有中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立等。拉丁美洲十国学生代表团将于7日晨乘飞机离开北京回国。
