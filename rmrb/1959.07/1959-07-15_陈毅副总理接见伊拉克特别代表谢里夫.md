Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Iraqi Special Representative Sharif
Author:
Date: 1959-07-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th
Vice Premier Chen Yi received Aziz Sharif, Special Representative of the Republic of Iraq, this morning. The host and guest held friendly talks. Present at the reception were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei and Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Luo Jun. Also present were Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Burhan Shahidi, and Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity Liao Chengzhi. After the reception, Vice Premier Chen Yi hosted a banquet for Aziz Sharif.

### 陈毅副总理接见伊拉克特别代表谢里夫

1959-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见伊拉克特别代表谢里夫
    新华社14日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见伊拉克共和国特别代表阿齐兹·谢里夫，宾主进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的有外交部副部长姬鹏飞和对外文委副主任罗俊。在座的还有政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志等。接见以后，陈毅副总理设宴招待阿齐兹·谢里夫。
