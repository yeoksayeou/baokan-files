Title: Interior Minister of the German Democratic Republic Points Out Escalating Espionage Activities in West Berlin
Author:
Date: 1959-07-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 12th. The newspapers in the democratic sector of Berlin published a conversation between Karl Maron, the Interior Minister of the German Democratic Republic, and a reporter from the Soviet "Izvestia." He stated that dismantling the espionage organizations in West Berlin is essential for safeguarding peace in Europe. Maron pointed out that there are over 400 espionage organizations from West Germany, the United States, and other countries in West Germany and West Berlin, employing various methods to attempt to destroy the social system of the German Democratic Republic.

### 德意志民主共和国内政部长指出  西柏林特务活动日益猖獗

1959-07-18
第4版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国内政部长指出
    西柏林特务活动日益猖獗
    新华社柏林讯　12日柏林民主区各报刊登了德意志民主共和国内政部长卡尔·马隆对苏联“消息报”记者的谈话，他说，取消西柏林的特务组织，就保卫欧洲和平来说，是十分必要的。马隆指出，在西德和西柏林有四百多个西德、美国和其它国家的间谍组织，它们用各种各样的手段企图摧毁德意志民主共和国的社会制度。
