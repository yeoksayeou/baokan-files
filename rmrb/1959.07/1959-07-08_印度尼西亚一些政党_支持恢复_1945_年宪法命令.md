Title: Some Indonesian Parties Support Order to Restore 1945 Constitution
Author:
Date: 1959-07-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, July 6th: Some Indonesian political parties have expressed their support for President Sukarno's order to restore the 1945 Constitution and have expressed their opinions on the issue of organizing a new cabinet.

    Indonesian Communist Party General Secretary Aidit, in a telegram to all Indonesian Communist Party branch committees today, called on party members and the Indonesian people to resolutely support President Sukarno's order together with the armed forces, patriotic political parties, and mass organizations. The telegram also stated that Indonesian Communist Party members and the Indonesian people unanimously support President Sukarno's efforts to organize a new cabinet. Aidit expressed his hope that national unity and fairness would serve as the foundation for organizing the new cabinet, the Supreme Advisory Council, and the People's Consultative Assembly. He added that when organizing these institutions, the will, ideas, and sentiments of the people should be taken into consideration, especially to resolve economic difficulties. He said that the members of the new cabinet should possess the spirit of the Declaration of Independence and the comprehensive constructive spirit to realize a just and prosperous society.

    The Central Committee of the Catholic Party issued a statement supporting Sukarno's order, expressing hope that the new cabinet should be composed of capable, upright individuals who have the support of the parliament, the armed forces, and the majority of the people.

    Indonesian Nationalist Party Chairman Suwirjo yesterday expressed his happiness with President Sukarno's decision and called on all Indonesian Nationalist Party members to support the president's order and help implement it.

    The Presidium of the National Committee of the All-Indonesia Central Trade Union Federation, in a telegram to President Sukarno yesterday, welcomed the order to restore the 1945 Constitution and expressed the hope that the future program of struggle should focus on financial and economic aspects.

    The Islamic Teachers' Association and the Masyumi Party have not yet expressed their opinions on the President's order. It is reported that the central committees of the two parties will soon hold separate meetings to discuss this order.

### 印度尼西亚一些政党  支持恢复1945年宪法命令

1959-07-08
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚一些政党
    支持恢复1945年宪法命令
    新华社雅加达6日电　印度尼西亚一些政党表示支持苏加诺总统关于恢复1945年宪法的命令，并且就组织新内阁的问题表示了意见。
    印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地今天在给所有印度尼西亚共产党支部委员会的电报中，号召党员和印度尼西亚人民同武装部队、爱国的政党和群众团体一道坚决支持苏加诺总统的命令。电报还说，印度尼西亚共产党党员和印度尼西亚人民一致支持苏加诺总统组织新内阁的努力。艾地表示希望，以民族团结和公平作为组织新内阁、最高谘询委员会和人民协商会议的基础。他又说，当组织这些机构的时候，应当考虑人民的意愿、想法和情绪，特别是为了要解决经济困难。他说，新内阁的成员应当具有独立宣言精神和实现公平繁荣社会的全面的建设性精神。
    天主教党中央委员会发表声明，支持苏加诺的命令，声明表示希望，新内阁应当由有能力、正直和得到议会、武装部队以及大多数人民支持的人组成。
    印度尼西亚国民党总主席苏维约昨天表示对苏加诺总统的决定感到高兴，并且号召印度尼西亚国民党全体党员支持总统的命令，并帮助执行命令。
    全印度尼西亚中央职工会全国委员会主席团在昨天给苏加诺总统的电报中，欢迎恢复1945年宪法的命令，并且希望今后的斗争纲领应该着重在财政和经济方面。
    伊斯兰教师联合会和马斯友美党还没有表示他们对总统命令的意见。据报道，两党的中央委员会即将分别举行会议来讨论这个命令。
