Title: Good Rain in Heilongjiang
Author:
Date: 1959-07-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, July 1st. Heilongjiang Province experienced widespread welcome rainfall from June 24th to 29th. Rainfall in general areas was around 20 millimeters, while Heihe, Tailai, Hailun and other areas received more than 50 millimeters of rainfall. This has greatly helped to alleviate the drought situation throughout the province. After the rain, many areas are seizing the opportunity to replant seedlings and sow seeds.

### 黑龙江喜雨

1959-07-02
第3版()
专栏：

    黑龙江喜雨
    新华社哈尔滨1日电　黑龙江省在6月24日至29日普降喜雨。一般地区降雨二十公厘左右，黑河、泰来、海伦等地区降雨五十公厘以上。这对缓和全省旱象作用很大。雨后，许多地区正抓紧时机补苗补种。
