Title: Indian "Blitz" Weekly Reveals: Indian Monopoly Capitalists Plotting an Anti-Nehru Organization
Author:
Date: 1959-07-22
Page: 6

    Indian "Blitz" Weekly Reveals
    Indian Monopoly Capitalists Plotting an Anti-Nehru Organization
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, July 20th: The latest issue of the "Blitz" weekly published a secret document exposing a political plan drafted as early as 1956 by the "Forum of Free Enterprise," an organization of Indian monopoly capital groups, to oppose Prime Minister Nehru, and its connection with the newly established conservative party, the "Swatantra Party."
    The weekly said that the document, proposed by Masani, a key figure in the "Forum of Free Enterprise," was "published by the Democratic Research Association of Bombay on June 19th." The "Democratic Research Association" is a pro-American organization.
    The weekly said that the document put forward a "strategic blueprint for building a major anti-Nehru group" in India. It said that although the document was written as early as 1956, it had already predicted the emergence of a conservative party through the "Forum of Free Enterprise."
    The secret document strongly urged the establishment of an organization that "should have sufficient strength, appeal, and a broad base" to oppose communism. This organization should have its political ideology, economic plans, universities, propaganda organs, research groups, leading members, and cadres.
    The document mentioned "encouraging responses at home and abroad;" and said, "In today's international situation, we must expect moral support from the United States and the democratic forces of the world."
    The drafter of the document shortlisted the notorious former socialist leader Narayan as a potential leader for this organization. It also called for winning over Congress Party leaders, civil administration officials, and military leaders, while avoiding the recruitment of "younger generation cadres nurtured by Nehru's ideas – if not something worse."

### 印度“闪电”周报揭露  印垄断资本家要搞反尼赫鲁组织

1959-07-22
第6版()
专栏：

    印度“闪电”周报揭露
    印垄断资本家要搞反尼赫鲁组织
    新华社新德里20日电　最近一期的“闪电”周报刊载了一个秘密文件，揭露印度垄断资本集团的组织“自由企业论坛”早在1956年所拟订的一份反对尼赫鲁总理的政治计划和它同新近成立的保守政党
  “自由党”的联系。
    这家周报说，由“自由企业论坛”的首要人物马萨尼提出的这个文件是“由孟买的民主研究会在6月19日发表的”。“民主研究会”是一个亲美组织。
    这家周报说，这个文件提出了在印度搞起一个“主要的反尼赫鲁集团的战略蓝图”。它说，虽然这个文件是早在1956年写的，但是那时它已经预言将通过“自由企业论坛”出现一个保守党。
    这个秘密文件力促建立一个组织，这个组织“要有足够的力量、吸引力和广泛的基础”来反对共产主义。这个组织要有它的政治思想、经济计划、大学、宣传机构、研究团体、领导成员和干部。
    这个文件提到“国内和国外的令人鼓舞的反应”；并且说，“在今天这种国际形势下，我们必须期望美国和世界上的民主力量来提供道义上的支持”。
    这个文件的起草人为这个组织物色的领袖人选中包括臭名远扬的前社会党领袖纳拉扬。并且还要求争取国大党领袖、民政机构官员和军事领袖，而不要吸收“受尼赫鲁思想——如果不是更坏的东西的话——培养的年青一代的干部”。
