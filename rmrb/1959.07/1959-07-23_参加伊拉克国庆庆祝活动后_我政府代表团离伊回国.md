Title: Chinese Government Delegation Returns Home After Attending Iraqi National Day Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1959-07-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, July 22nd: The Chinese government delegation, led by Vice Foreign Minister Luo Guibo, which attended the Iraqi National Day celebrations, left Baghdad this afternoon to return home.
    The Bulgarian government delegation left Baghdad on the same plane to return home.
    Present at the airport to see them off were Iraqi Foreign Minister Hashim Jawad, the ambassadors and diplomatic officials of China, Bulgaria, and other socialist countries to Iraq, and the chairman of the Iraq-China Friendship Association, among others.
    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, July 21st: Li Dequan, the Chinese women's representative, left here by plane this morning to return home.
    She paid a nine-day visit to Iraq and participated in the celebrations of the first anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Iraq.
    Iraqi Minister of Health Mohammed Shawwaf, representatives of the Iraqi Women's Rights Defense League, and Chinese Ambassador to Iraq Chen Zhifang, among others, went to the airport to see her off.
    The Chinese trade union delegation, headed by Liu Changsheng, returned home on the same plane. During its visit to Iraq, the Chinese trade union delegation participated in the first congress of the Iraqi General Federation of Trade Unions and the Iraqi National Day celebrations.
    The Korean women's delegation also left Baghdad on the same plane.

### 参加伊拉克国庆庆祝活动后  我政府代表团离伊回国

1959-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

    参加伊拉克国庆庆祝活动后
    我政府代表团离伊回国
    新华社巴格达22日电　由外交部副部长罗贵波率领的参加伊拉克国庆庆祝活动的中国政府代表团，今天下午离开巴格达回国。
    保加利亚政府代表团同机离开巴格达回国。
    到机场欢送的有伊拉克外交部长哈希姆·贾瓦德，中国、保加利亚以及其它社会主义国家驻伊拉克的大使和外交官员，伊拉克—中国友好协会的主席等。
    新华社巴格达21日电　中国妇女代表李德全今天上午离开这里乘飞机回国。
    她在伊拉克进行了历时九天的访问，并且参加了伊拉克共和国成立一周年的庆祝活动。
    前往机场送行的有伊拉克卫生部长穆罕默德·沙瓦夫、伊拉克维护女权联盟的代表以及中国驻伊拉克大使陈志方等。
    以刘长胜为首的中国工会代表团同机回国。中国工会代表团在伊拉克访问期间，参加了伊拉克工会总联合会第一次代表大会和伊拉克的国庆活动。
    同机离开巴格达的还有朝鲜妇女代表团。
