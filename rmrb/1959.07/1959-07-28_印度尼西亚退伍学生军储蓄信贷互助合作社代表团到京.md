Title: Indonesian Veterans Student Army Savings and Loan Cooperative Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-07-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th—The Indonesian Veterans Student Army Savings and Loan Cooperative Delegation, invited by the Chinese-Indonesian Friendship Association to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation consisted of five members and was led by Widaarbo.

### 印度尼西亚退伍学生军储蓄信贷互助合作社代表团到京

1959-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚退伍学生军储蓄信贷互助合作社代表团到京
    新华社27日讯　应中国印度尼西亚友好协会邀请来中国访问的印度尼西亚退伍学生军储蓄信贷互助合作社代表团一行五人在团长维达尔波率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
