Title: Soviet People Warmly Celebrate Iraqi National Day; All Circles in Moscow Gather to Decide on Establishing the Soviet-Iraqi Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1959-07-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, July 15th: The Soviet people are warmly celebrating the first anniversary of the Iraqi revolution victory, according to a TASS report.

　　On the occasion of the festival, Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and Gromyko, Minister of Foreign Affairs, sent congratulatory messages to Rubai, Chairman of the Supreme Power Committee of the Republic of Iraq, Prime Minister Kassem, and Foreign Minister Jawad respectively.

　　In his congratulatory message to Prime Minister Kassem, Khrushchev pointed out that on July 14, 1958, "the freedom-loving people of Iraq established a free republic that steadfastly advances along the path of progress and consolidation of national independence, thereby resolutely cutting off the shackles of colonial oppression and severely striking at the aggressive Baghdad Pact and the imperialist plans of aggression against the Near and Middle East."

　　Khrushchev emphasized that the victory of the Iraqi national revolution opened a new page in Soviet-Iraqi relations, which are developing on the lofty principles of equality, mutual respect, and beneficial cooperation.

　　People from all walks of life in Moscow held a grand meeting on the 14th to celebrate the first anniversary of the Iraqi revolution.

　　Kaftanov, Chairman of the Soviet Association for Friendship and Cultural Relations with the Arab Eastern Countries, delivered a report at the meeting. He spoke about the significance of the July 14th Iraqi revolution.

　　The participants discussed the initiative proposed by some Soviet social organizations regarding the establishment of the Soviet-Iraqi Friendship Association. The meeting finally passed a resolution to establish this association.

　　Abdul Wahab Mahmoud, Ambassador of the Republic of Iraq to the Soviet Union, was invited to attend the meeting and spoke at the meeting. He emphasized that friendship is a solid foundation for consolidating world peace. He said, I welcome and thank all my friends for speaking here and proposing the establishment of the Soviet-Iraqi Friendship Association. There is no doubt that the establishment of this association will open the door to further development of friendly relations between the peoples of the two countries.

　　When talking about the Iraqi people's struggle for freedom and independence and against imperialism and colonialism, the ambassador pointed out that the great October Revolution inspired the Iraqi people in this struggle.

### 苏联人民热烈庆祝伊拉克国庆  莫斯科各界集会决定成立苏伊友协

1959-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联人民热烈庆祝伊拉克国庆
    莫斯科各界集会决定成立苏伊友协
    据新华社15日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联人民热烈庆祝伊拉克革命胜利一周年。
    在节日之际，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫和外交部长葛罗米柯，分别致电伊拉克共和国最高权力委员会主席鲁巴伊、总理卡塞姆和外交部长贾瓦德表示祝贺。
    赫鲁晓夫在致卡塞姆总理的贺电中指出，1958年7月14日，“爱好自由的伊拉克人民建立了坚定不移地沿着进步与巩固民族独立的道路前进的自由共和国，从而坚决地斩断了殖民主义压迫的枷锁，沉重地打击了侵略性的巴格达条约和帝国主义对中近东的侵略计划”。
    赫鲁晓夫着重指出，伊拉克民族革命的胜利揭开了苏伊关系的新的一页，这一关系正在平等、互相尊重和有益合作的高度原则的基础上发展。
    莫斯科各界人士14日举行集会，隆重庆祝伊拉克革命一周年。
    苏联同阿拉伯东方国家友好和文化联络协会主席卡弗坦诺夫在会上做了报告。他讲述了7月14日伊拉克革命的意义。
    与会者讨论了苏联一些社会团体提出的关于成立苏联—伊拉克友好协会的倡议。会议最后通过了成立这个协会的决议。
    伊拉克共和国驻苏联大使阿卜杜勒·瓦哈卜·马哈茂德应邀出席了会议，并且在会上讲了话。他强调指出，友谊是巩固世界和平的牢固基础。他说，我欢迎并感谢各位朋友在这里发言提议成立苏联—伊拉克友好协会。毫无疑问，这一协会的成立将打开进一步发展两国人民之间的友好关系的大门。
    大使在谈到伊拉克人民争取自由和独立、反对帝国主义和殖民主义的斗争时指出，在这一斗争中，伟大的十月革命鼓舞了伊拉克人民。
