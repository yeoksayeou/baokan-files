Title: Government Delegations from Soviet Union, China and Other Countries Arrive in Baghdad to Participate in Iraqi National Day

Author:
Date: 1959-07-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, July 12th: Government delegations from the Soviet Union, China, North Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia arrived in Baghdad this morning. These delegations were invited by the Iraqi government to attend the first anniversary celebration of Iraqi National Day.

The head of the Soviet government delegation is Kuznetsov, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; the head of the Chinese government delegation is Luo Guibo, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; the head of the North Korean government delegation is Nam Il, Deputy Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs; the head of the Vietnamese government delegation is Nguyen Duc Duong, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; and the head of the Mongolian government delegation is Lhamsuren, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers.

Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China, also arrived in Baghdad today at the invitation of the Iraqi League for the Defense of Women's Rights to participate in the celebration.

Also arriving here today were Kim Young-soo, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Women's Union, and a Soviet music and dance troupe.

Iraqi Foreign Minister Hashim Jawad and Agriculture Minister Haji Humud greeted the guests at the airport this morning. Also at the airport to greet them were the President of the Iraqi-Soviet Friendship Association, the President of the Iraqi-Chinese Friendship Association, representatives of the Iraqi League for the Defense of Women's Rights, and diplomatic envoys from socialist countries.

In a speech to Baghdad Radio, Luo Guibo, head of the Chinese government delegation, extended warm congratulations and high respect from the government and people of the People's Republic of China to the government and heroic people of Iraq. He wished the heroic Iraqi people, under the leadership of Prime Minister Qasim, even more brilliant achievements in their great cause of safeguarding national independence, opposing imperialism, and building their motherland.

The heads of other delegations also delivered congratulatory speeches.

Government delegations from Bulgaria, Canada, Ethiopia, Japan, Lebanon, Romania, Tunisia, and the United States have also arrived in Baghdad to participate in the Iraqi National Day celebrations.

### 参加伊拉克国庆  苏中等国政府代表团抵巴格达

1959-07-14
第5版()
专栏：

    参加伊拉克国庆
    苏中等国政府代表团抵巴格达
    新华社巴格达12日电　苏联、中国、朝鲜、越南、蒙古的政府代表团在今天上午到达巴格达。这些代表团是应伊拉克政府邀请来参加伊拉克国庆一周年典礼的。
    苏联政府代表团团长是外交部第一副部长库兹涅佐夫，中国政府代表团团长是外交部副部长罗贵波，朝鲜政府代表团团长是副首相兼外务相南日，越南政府代表团团长是外交部副部长阮德扬，蒙古政府代表团团长是部长会议副主席拉姆苏伦。
    应伊拉克维护女权联盟的邀请前来参加庆典的中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会副主席李德全也在今天到达巴格达。
    今天到达这里的还有朝鲜民主妇女同盟中央委员会副委员长金英秀和苏联音乐舞蹈团。
  伊拉克外交部长哈希姆·贾瓦德和农业部长哈吉·胡穆德今天上午在机场上迎接客人。到机场迎接的还有伊苏友好协会会长、伊中友好协会会长、伊拉克维护女权联盟的代表和社会主义国家的外交使节。
    中国政府代表团团长罗贵波在对巴格达电台发表谈话时，代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向伊拉克政府和英勇的伊拉克人民致热烈的祝贺和崇高的敬意。他祝英勇的伊拉克人民在卡塞姆总理的领导下，在他们维护民族独立、反对帝国主义和建设他们的祖国的伟大事业中，取得更加辉煌的成就。
    其他代表团团长也发表了祝贺的讲话。
    已经到达巴格达来参加伊拉克国庆典礼的还有保加利亚、加拿大、埃塞俄比亚、日本、黎巴嫩、罗马尼亚、突尼斯和美国的政府代表团。
