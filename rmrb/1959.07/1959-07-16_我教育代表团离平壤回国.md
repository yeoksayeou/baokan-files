Title: Chinese Education Delegation Leaves Pyongyang for Home

Author:

Date: 1959-07-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 15th: The Chinese Education Delegation, led by Shi Weisan, Deputy Director of the General Education Department of the Ministry of Education, which came to Korea for a visit under the 1959 Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Plan, concluded its visit to Korea and left Pyongyang by train today for home.

Li Chang-su, Vice Minister of Education and Culture of Korea, Korean educators, and Mao Dafeng, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Korea, were at the station to see the delegation off.

During its one-month visit to Korea, the delegation visited schools and educational institutions in Pyongyang, Kaesong, Chongjin, Hamhung, and other places.

### 我教育代表团离平壤回国

1959-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

    我教育代表团离平壤回国
    新华社平壤15日电　根据1959年中朝文化合作计划来朝鲜访问的中国教育代表团由教育部普通教育司副司长师唯三率领，结束了在朝鲜的访问，今天乘火车离平壤回国。
    到车站欢送代表团的有朝鲜教育文化省副相李昌洙、朝鲜教育界人士和中国驻朝鲜大使馆文化专员毛大风等。
    代表团在朝鲜访问的一个月期间，先后访问了平壤、开城、清津、咸兴等地的学校和教育机关。
