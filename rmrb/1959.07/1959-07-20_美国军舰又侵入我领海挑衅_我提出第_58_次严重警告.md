Title: U.S. Warship Again Intrudes into Our Territorial Waters to Provoke: We Issue the 58th Serious Warning
Author: 
Date: 1959-07-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th July: From 13:58 to 16:37 on July 19th, a U.S. warship intruded into the sea area of Pingtan and Baiquan areas of Fujian Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, a spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 58th serious warning.

### 美国军舰又侵入我领海挑衅  我提出第58次严重警告

1959-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军舰又侵入我领海挑衅
    我提出第58次严重警告
    新华社19日讯　7月19日十三时五十八分至十六时三十七分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省平潭、白犬地区海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第五十八次严重警告。
