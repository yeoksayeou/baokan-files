Title: Sino-Polish Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Warsaw to Attend Polish 15th National Day Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1959-07-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 16th. The delegation of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association, led by Wang Weizhou, member of the council of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association, member of the CPC Central Committee, and member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, arrived in Warsaw on the 16th. The delegation is visiting at the invitation of the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association to attend the 15th National Day celebrations of the Polish People's Republic.

Welcoming the delegation at the airport were Windhe, Vice President of the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association and Vice Chairman of the Polish Democratic Party, Stavinski, Minister of Light Industry of Poland, Lesh, Minister of Internal Trade, Goranski, Minister of Higher Education, and Bulkin, Secretary-General of the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association, and others.

### 应邀参加波兰十五周年国庆典礼  中波友协代表团抵华沙

1959-07-18
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀参加波兰十五周年国庆典礼
    中波友协代表团抵华沙
    新华社华沙16日电　以中波友好协会理事、中共中央委员、中国人民代表大会常务委员会委员王维舟为首的中波友好协会代表团，于16日抵达华沙。代表团是应波中友好协会的邀请前来参加波兰人民共和国十五周年国庆典礼的。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有波中友好协会副会长、波兰民主党副主席温德，波兰轻工业部长斯塔文斯基，对内贸易部长莱什，高等教育部长戈兰斯基和波中友好协会总书记布尔金等人。
