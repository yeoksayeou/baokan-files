Title: Cuban Women's Delegation Departs for Visit to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1959-07-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, July 5th. A Cuban women's delegation departed for China by plane on the 5th. This delegation is visiting China at the friendly invitation of the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China, and includes six members.

### 古巴妇女代表团启程来我国访问

1959-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

    古巴妇女代表团启程来我国访问
    新华社哈瓦那5日电　古巴妇女代表团在5日乘飞机前往中国。这个代表团是应中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会的邀请前往中国作友好访问的，代表团包括六个团员。
