Title: One Million Workers in Italy on Strike, Production Halted in Many Steel, Machine, and Shipbuilding Factories
Author:
Date: 1959-07-13
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Rome, July 11th: As scheduled, 100,000 Italian steelworkers began a four-day strike at midnight this morning, demanding wage increases and improved working conditions.

    Combined with the one million metal and machine manufacturing workers who began striking yesterday, the number of strikers in the two important production sectors of metallurgy and machine manufacturing in Italy has now reached 1.1 million. This strike is one of the largest in Italy since the war. The strike has brought production to a standstill in many steel mills, machine factories, and shipyards throughout Italy.

### 意百万工人大罢工  许多炼钢、机器和造船工厂生产停止

1959-07-13
第5版()
专栏：

    意百万工人大罢工
    许多炼钢、机器和造船工厂生产停止
    新华社11日讯　罗马消息：意大利十万名炼钢工人按照原订计划在今晨零时开始为时四天的罢工，要求增加工资和改善劳动条件。
    连同在昨天开始罢工的一百万名五金和机器制造工人，目前在意大利冶金和机器制造工业这两个重要的生产部门中，罢工的人数已经达到一百一十万人。这次罢工是战后以来意大利规模巨大的罢工之一。罢工使得意大利各地的许多炼钢厂、机器制造厂和造船厂的生产都停止了。
