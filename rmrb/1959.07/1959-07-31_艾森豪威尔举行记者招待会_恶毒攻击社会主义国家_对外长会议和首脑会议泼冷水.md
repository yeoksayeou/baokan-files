Title: Eisenhower Holds Press Conference, Viciously Attacks Socialist Countries, Douses Cold Water on Foreign Ministers' Meeting and Summit Conference
Author:
Date: 1959-07-31
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 30th -- US President Eisenhower yesterday, at a press conference, once again defended the vicious slander campaign launched by the United States – the so-called "Captive Nations Week," and openly stated that the United States will adhere to its aggressive policy of hostility toward socialist countries.

When a reporter asked him why he announced this so-called "Captive Nations Week" precisely when Nixon was visiting the Soviet Union, Eisenhower evaded answering the question, but he then repeated his anti-Soviet and anti-communist clichés, nonsensically claiming that socialist countries are "enslaved," making it impossible to establish "true peace." He said that when he first ran for president in 1952, he said, "The United States will not believe, nor agree, that true peace can be established until each nation has the right to express its own opinion on its rights and destiny."

Regarding the recent Western proposals on the West Berlin issue, Eisenhower indicated that the United States still adheres to the unreasonable position of indefinitely occupying West Berlin.

Eisenhower once again poured cold water on the Foreign Ministers' meeting and the summit conference. He said, "The Foreign Ministers have not yet made progress that would justify convening a meeting of heads of government."

### 艾森豪威尔举行记者招待会  恶毒攻击社会主义国家  对外长会议和首脑会议泼冷水

1959-07-31
第5版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔举行记者招待会
    恶毒攻击社会主义国家
    对外长会议和首脑会议泼冷水
    新华社30日讯　华盛顿消息：美国总统艾森豪威尔昨天在记者招待会上再次为美国所发动的恶毒诽谤运动——所谓“被奴役国家周”辩解，并且公开表示美国将坚持敌视社会主义国家的侵略政策。
    当记者问他为什么恰好在尼克松访问苏联的时候宣布举行这个所谓“被奴役国家周”时，艾森豪威尔回避答复这个问题，但是，他接着重弹他反苏反共的滥调，胡说什么社会主义国家“被奴役”，使得“真正的和平”不能建立起来。他说，1952年他第一次竞选总统的时候曾说，“美国不会相信，也不同意，在各国有权对它的权利和命运表示自己的意见以前能够建立真正的和平。”
    在谈到西方最近对西柏林问题的建议时，艾森豪威尔表示美国仍然坚持无限期占领西柏林这一无理的立场。
    艾森豪威尔又一次对外长会议和首脑会议泼冷水。他说，“外长们还没有取得将说明有理由召开政府首脑会议的进展。”
