Title: Nixon Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1959-07-24
Page: 4

    Nixon Arrives in Moscow
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 23rd: According to TASS, U.S. Vice President Nixon arrived in Moscow by plane on the afternoon of the 23rd. Accompanying Nixon on his visit to the Soviet Union were his wife, President Eisenhower's brother Milton Eisenhower, U.S. intelligence chief Allen Dulles, Vice Admiral Hyman G. Rickover, and others.
    Greeting him at the airport were First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers Kozlov, Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet Lobanov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Kuznetsov, and others.
    Kozlov delivered a welcome speech at the airport. He expressed his hope that the American guests' visit to the Soviet Union would help eliminate the mutual suspicion and distrust that had formed in Soviet-American relations in recent years. In his speech, Nixon stated that during his visit to the Soviet Union, his discussions with his entourage at all levels, "from the Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers to factory workers, will help both sides better understand and appreciate each other."

### 尼克松到达莫斯科

1959-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    尼克松到达莫斯科
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：美国副总统尼克松23日下午乘飞机到达莫斯科。陪同尼克松到苏联访问的有他的夫人以及美国总统的弟弟密·艾森豪威尔、美国情报局领导人艾伦·杜勒斯、海军中将里科维尔等人。
    前往机场欢迎的有苏联部长会议第一副主席科兹洛夫、苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席洛巴诺夫、苏联外交部副部长库兹?佐夫等人。
    科兹洛夫在机场上致欢迎词。他表示希望美国客人的访问苏联，应该有助于消除最近一些年来苏美两国关系中形成的相互猜疑和不信任。尼克松在讲话时表示，在苏联访问期间，他和他的随行人员同“上至苏联部长会议主席、下至工厂的工人进行的各级水平上的讨论，将有助于双方更好的了解和谅解”。
