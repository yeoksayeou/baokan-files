Title: Sino-US Ambassadors Continue Talks
Author:
Date: 1959-07-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 28th: The ninety-first meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks was held in Warsaw at 2 p.m. today. The meeting lasted for one hour and forty-five minutes.

After the meeting, both sides announced that the next meeting will be held on August 25th.

### 中美大使继续会谈

1959-07-29
第4版()
专栏：

    中美大使继续会谈
    新华社华沙28日电　中美大使级会谈第九十一次会议，于今天下午二时在华沙举行。会议历时一小时四十五分。
    会后双方宣布，下次会议将在8月25日举行。
