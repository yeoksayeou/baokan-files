Title: Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Leaves Hohhot for Baotou

Author: 
Date: 1959-07-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, July 4th. The three-member delegation of the Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Association, headed by Professor Nachukdorji, Vice Chairman of the Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Association, concluded its visit to Hohhot and left for Baotou by car on the afternoon of the 3rd.

### 蒙中友协代表团  离呼和浩特去包头访问

1959-07-05
第3版()
专栏：

    蒙中友协代表团
    离呼和浩特去包头访问
    新华社呼和浩特4日电　以蒙古蒙中友好协会副主席那楚克道尔吉教授为首的蒙中友协代表团一行三人，结束了在呼和浩特的访问，3日下午乘车前往包头访问。
