Title: First Group of Iraqi Army Football Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-07-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th: The first group of sixteen members of the Iraqi Army Football Team, the first Iraqi Republic sports team to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane today, led by coach Lieutenant Colonel Aziz Hajiya. The second group of nine members of this football team, led by team leader Colonel Ismail Razouqi, will arrive in Beijing on the 30th. The Iraqi Army Football Team will visit Beijing, Shanghai, Shenyang, Tianjin and other places this time. Their first friendly match in our country is scheduled to be held in Beijing on August 2, against the "August 1st" Football Team.

### 伊拉克军队足球队首批人员到达北京

1959-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克军队足球队首批人员到达北京
    据新华社27日讯　应邀来我国访问的第一个伊拉克共和国体育运动队伍——伊拉克军队足球队的首批人员十六人，今天由教练阿齐兹·哈吉雅中校率领乘飞机到达北京。这个足球队的第二批人员九人，将由领队伊斯梅尔·拉佐基上校率领，在30日到达北京。伊拉克军队足球队这次将访问北京、上海、沈阳、天津等地。他们在我国的第一场友谊比赛，预定8月2日在北京举行，对手是“八一”足球队。
