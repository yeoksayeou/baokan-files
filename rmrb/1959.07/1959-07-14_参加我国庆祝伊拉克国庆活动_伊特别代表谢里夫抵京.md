Title: Iraqi Special Representative Sharif Arrives in Beijing to Attend China's Celebration of Iraqi National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-07-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 13th -- Aziz Sharif, the special representative of the Republic of Iraq, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. He is here to participate in the celebration activities related to the first anniversary of the Iraqi revolution.
    Welcoming him at the airport were Burhan Shahidi, Vice Chairman of The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Gong Dafei, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Shijie, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, and Luo Jun, Vice President of the China-Iraq Friendship Association. This afternoon, Aziz Sharif also met with Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs.

### 参加我国庆祝伊拉克国庆活动  伊特别代表谢里夫抵京

1959-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

    参加我国庆祝伊拉克国庆活动
    伊特别代表谢里夫抵京
    新华社13日讯　伊拉克共和国特别代表阿齐兹·谢里夫已于今天下午乘飞机到达北京。他是来我国参加有关伊拉克革命一周年庆祝活动的。
    到机场欢迎的有政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉、外交部西亚非洲司副司长宫达非、礼宾司副司长张世杰、中国伊拉克友好协会副会长罗俊等。今天下午，阿齐兹·谢里夫还会见了外交部副部长姬鹏飞。
