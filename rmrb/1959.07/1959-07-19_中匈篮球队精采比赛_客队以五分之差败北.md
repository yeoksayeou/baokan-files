Title: Exciting Match Between Chinese and Hungarian Basketball Teams: Visiting Team Loses by Five Points
Author:
Date: 1959-07-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: The Chinese National Men's Basketball Team today defeated the Hungarian National Men's Basketball Team for the second time with a score of 65 to 60. In the first half, the Chinese team led with a score of 32 to 28. This was the fourth and final exciting friendly match held in Beijing by the visiting team.

The visiting team is about to leave Beijing for a visit to Shanghai, where they are scheduled to hold friendly matches with the Shanghai and Sichuan men's basketball teams next week.

### 中匈篮球队精采比赛  客队以五分之差败北

1959-07-19
第3版()
专栏：

    中匈篮球队精采比赛
    客队以五分之差败北
    据新华社18日讯　中国国家男子篮球队今晚以六十五比六十第二次战胜了匈牙利国家男子篮球队。上半场中国队以三十二比二十八领先。这是客队在北京举行的第四场也是最后一场精采的友谊比赛。
    客队即将离北京去上海访问，预定下星期在那里先后同上海、四川男子篮球队举行友谊比赛。
