Title: Shouzhang Taiqian Commune Overcomes Various Natural Disasters: Per-Mu Yield on Over 4,000 Mu More Than Doubles the Past
Author:
Date: 1959-07-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 4th: Commune members of the Taiqian People's Commune's Taiqian Management District in Shouzhang County, Shandong Province, worked hard throughout the winter and spring, successively overcoming drought, pests and diseases, frost, and lodging, and won a comprehensive bumper wheat harvest. The 4,500 mu of wheat in the entire management district, after actual harvesting and threshing, and verified by weighing, averaged 524 jin and 9 liang per mu, an increase of 222% compared to last year.

### 寿张台前公社战胜各种自然灾害  四千多亩亩产比过去提高二倍多

1959-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

    寿张台前公社战胜各种自然灾害
    四千多亩亩产比过去提高二倍多
    据新华社济南4日电　山东寿张县台前人民公社台前管理区的社员，苦干一冬一春，先后战胜干旱、病虫害、霜冻和倒伏，赢得了小麦全面大丰收。全管理区的四千五百亩小麦，经实收实打，过秤核实，平均每亩产量比去年提高222％，达到五百二十四斤九两。
