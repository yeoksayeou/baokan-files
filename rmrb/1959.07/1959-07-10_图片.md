Our country's self-made 2000-horsepower electric transmission "Xianxing" (Pioneer) diesel locomotive is being tested on the Shanghai-Nanjing line. The entire operation of this diesel locomotive is electrically automated, with rapid start-up and acceleration, smooth running, and good test results. Photo by Yang Rongliang (Xinhua News Agency稿)
