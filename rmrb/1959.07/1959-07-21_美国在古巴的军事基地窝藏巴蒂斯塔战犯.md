Title: U.S. Military Base in Cuba Harboring Batista War Criminals
Author:
Date: 1959-07-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, July 19th: Cuban newspapers have revealed that the United States is harboring war criminals at the U.S. Naval Base in Guantanamo, Cuba.

According to "Revolución" newspaper, Cuban customs inspector Miguel Gonzalez reported to the Cuban authorities that Batista war criminal and former Lieutenant Juan Horacio Moralejo, sentenced to twenty years in prison, is hiding in the U.S. Naval Base in Guantanamo, located on Cuban territory, after escaping Cuban sanctions. His wife and others are also under U.S. protection.

"Hoy" newspaper protested this action by the United States, calling on the Cuban people to demand that the United States hand over this despicable fugitive. The newspaper wrote, "What will happen if, after the first one, dozens and hundreds of war criminals take refuge on Cuba's own soil, arm themselves, and plan and prepare attacks on Cuba?" The newspaper pointed out that this disgraceful behavior, coupled with the Díaz Lanz affair, the newspaper propaganda campaign, the plan to allow Batista to take refuge in the United States, and the obvious activities of imperialism after the Urrutia conspiracy, all indicate that we are suffering a series of carefully planned provocations and attacks.

### 美国在古巴的军事基地窝藏巴蒂斯塔战犯

1959-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    美国在古巴的军事基地窝藏巴蒂斯塔战犯
    新华社哈瓦那19日电　古巴报纸揭露美国把战犯窝藏在古巴的关塔那摩美国海军基地。
    据“至上报”报道，古巴海关检查员米格尔·冈萨雷斯向古巴当局报告，被判处二十年徒刑的巴蒂斯塔战犯、前中尉胡安·奥拉西奥·莫拉荣斯，在逃出古巴的制裁之后，隐藏在古巴领土上的关塔那摩美国海军基地。他的妻子和其他人都受到美国的保护。
    “今日报”抗议美国的这种作法，号召古巴人民要求美国交出这个卑鄙的逃犯。报纸写道，“如果在第一个之后，接着有几十个和几百个战争罪犯都在古巴自己的土地上避难，武装起来，策划和准备攻击古巴的计划，那将发生什么情况呢？”这家报纸指出，这种不光彩的行为，再加上迪亚斯·兰斯事件、报纸的宣传运动、计划让巴蒂斯塔到美国避难，以及在乌鲁蒂亚的阴谋之后帝国主义的明显活动，都表明了我们正遭受一系列经过周密计划的挑衅和攻击。
