Title: Lebanese Communist Party Calls for Saving Lebanese Communist Leader Helou
Author: 
Date: 1959-07-08
Page: 5
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, July 5th: The Central Committee of the Lebanese Communist Party has issued a call, urging all patriots and democrats in Lebanon and all Arab countries, as well as world public opinion, to loudly demand that the United Arab Republic authorities release Lebanese Communist leader Helou, whom they arrested, and to request that Lebanese officials intervene to save him. The call, published in today's "Al Nidaa" and "Al Usbu' al Massarawi", states that for thirty years, the Lebanese people have known Helou, the Secretary of the Central Committee of the Lebanese Communist Party, and consider him a "patriot who has waged an unremitting struggle against imperialism."

### 黎巴嫩共产党呼吁  拯救黎共领袖赫卢

1959-07-08
第5版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩共产党呼吁
    拯救黎共领袖赫卢
    新华社贝鲁特5日电　黎巴嫩共产党中央委员会发出呼吁书要求黎巴嫩和所有阿拉伯国家的所有爱国者和民主人士以及世界舆论大声疾呼，要求阿联当局释放它逮捕的黎巴嫩共产党领袖赫卢，并且要求黎巴嫩负责人士进行干预，来拯救他。刊载在今天的“呼声报”和“消息周报”上的这项呼吁说，三十年来，黎巴嫩人民很了解黎巴嫩共产党中央委员会书记赫卢，认为他是“对帝国主义进行不懈斗争的爱国者”。
