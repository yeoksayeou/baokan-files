Title: Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Uganda National Congress, Kiwanuka, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-07-14
Page: 4

    Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Uganda National Congress
    Kiwanuka Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, July 13th: Kiwanuka, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Uganda National Congress, who was invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.
    Greeting him at the airport were Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General, and Zhu Ziqi, Secretary-General of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, among others.

### 乌干达国民大会执委会主席  基瓦努卡抵京

1959-07-14
第4版()
专栏：

    乌干达国民大会执委会主席
    基瓦努卡抵京
    新华社13日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请前来我国访问的乌干达国民大会执行委员会主席基瓦努卡已于今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有外交学会秘书长吴茂荪、副秘书长吴晓达，中国亚非团结委员会秘书长朱子奇等。
