Title: Iraqi Student Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-07-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd: The Iraqi Republic student delegation, invited by the All-China Students' Federation to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon from Xi'an, accompanied by Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation. Sun Shuzhu, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, and student representatives from the capital went to the airport to welcome them. Before arriving in Beijing, the delegation made short visits to Kunming and Xi'an.

### 伊拉克学生代表团到京

1959-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克学生代表团到京
    新华社22日讯　应中华全国学生联合会邀请来我国访问的伊拉克共和国学生代表团一行十人，在全国学联主席胡启立陪同下，今天下午自西安乘飞机到达北京的全国学联副主席孙寿珠和首都学生代表曾到机场欢迎，代表团在来京前，曾在昆明、西安作了短期访问。
