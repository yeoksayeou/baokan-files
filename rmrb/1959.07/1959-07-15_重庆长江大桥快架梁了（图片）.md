Title: Chongqing Yangtze River Bridge Will Soon Have Beams Erected (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1959-07-15
Page: 2

    Chongqing Yangtze River Bridge Will Soon Have Beams Erected
    The pier construction of the Chongqing Yangtze River Bridge has been fully completed and beams are about to be erected.
    Left picture: The bridge scene on the eve of beam erection.
    Top picture: All twelve precast concrete girders and four steel truss girders needed for the Chongqing Yangtze River Bridge have been shipped to the construction site from the Shanhaiguan Bridge Factory. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Shilin photo
