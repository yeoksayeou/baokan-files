Title: Czechoslovak Cultural Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-07-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd. The Czechoslovak Cultural Delegation, headed by Delegation Leader Zdzislav Brival, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home after visiting Beijing, Shanghai, Nanjing, Hangzhou and other cities in our country.

### 捷文化代表团离京

1959-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

    捷文化代表团离京
    新华社22日讯　以兹吉斯拉夫·布瑞瓦尔团长为首的捷克斯洛伐克文化代表团，在我国访问了北京、上海、南京、杭州等城市以后，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
