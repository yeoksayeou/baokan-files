Title: Chinese Muslim Pilgrimage Delegation Arrives in Casablanca
Author:
Date: 1959-07-20
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Casablanca, July 18th: The Chinese Muslim pilgrimage delegation headed by Ma Zhuozhou arrived in Casablanca this afternoon. The pilgrimage delegation had visited Cairo and Khartoum after their pilgrimage to Mecca.

### 我穆斯林朝觐团到达卡萨布兰卡

1959-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

    我穆斯林朝觐团到达卡萨布兰卡
    新华社卡萨布兰卡18日电　以马卓洲为首的中国穆斯林朝觐团今天下午到达卡萨布兰卡。朝觐团到麦加朝觐后曾在开罗、喀土穆访问。
