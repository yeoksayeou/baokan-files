Title: Inner Mongolia Builds Mirabilite Factory
Author:
Date: 1959-07-23
Page: 2

    Inner Mongolia Builds Mirabilite Factory
    Xinhua News Agency report: Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region has started construction of an anhydrous mirabilite factory near Erlian. Once completed, the factory will be able to produce 50,000 tons of anhydrous mirabilite annually.
    Anhydrous mirabilite is an important chemical raw material and is indispensable to industries such as alkali production, papermaking, leather, glass, and rayon. The Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region has abundant natural mirabilite reserves. The newly built Erlian Anhydrous Mirabilite Factory will use natural weathering methods to process natural mirabilite into anhydrous mirabilite.

### 内蒙古兴建芒硝厂

1959-07-23
第2版()
专栏：

    内蒙古兴建芒硝厂
    新华社讯  内蒙古自治区在二连附近动工兴建一座无水芒硝厂。这座工厂建成后，每年可以生产无水芒硝五万吨。
    无水芒硝是重要的化工原料，制碱、造纸、皮革、玻璃、人造纤维等工业部门都离不开它。内蒙古自治区天然芒硝蕴藏丰富，新建的二连无水芒硝厂将用自然风化的方法，将天然芒硝加工成无水芒硝。
