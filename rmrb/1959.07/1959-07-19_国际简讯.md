International Briefs

Austrian New Government Announces Neutrality Policy
    Vienna dispatch: Austrian New Government Prime Minister Raab announced to the parliament on the 17th that his government will continue to pursue a policy of neutrality. The Austrian New Government, jointly formed by Raab's People's Party and the Socialist Party, was established on the 16th after experiencing nearly two months of government crisis. The People's Party and the Socialist Party each occupy six ministerial seats in this coalition government.

Israeli Former Prime Minister Ben-Gurion Forms New Cabinet
    Jerusalem (Israel area) dispatch: Israeli former Prime Minister Ben-Gurion accepted the request of Israeli President Yitzhak Ben-Zvi on the 15th to form a new cabinet.

### 国际简讯

1959-07-19
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    奥新政府宣布奉行中立政策
    维也纳讯　奥地利新政府总理拉布17日向议会宣布，他的政府将继续奉行中立政策。由拉布的人民党和社会党联合组成的奥地利新政府是在经历了将近两个月的政府危机之后在16日成立的。人民党和社会党在这个联合政府内各占六个部长席位。
    以色列前总理本—古里安组新阁
    耶路撒冷（以色列区）讯　以色列前总理本—古里安15日接受了以色列总统伊萨克·本·兹维的要求，组织新的内阁。
