Title: Tianjin Makes Movie Color Developer
Author: Ou Minwei
Date: 1959-07-31
Page: 2

    Tianjin Makes Movie Color Developer
    (Our newspaper dispatch) The Tianjin Light Industry Daily Chemical Technology Research Institute and other units recently trial-produced a batch of color developers, and the Tianjin Photographic Equipment Factory successfully used them to coat movie film. The Central Film Studio used it to replicate a segment of the film "Swan Lake." After inspection, it was found that the quality basically met the requirements.

### 天津制成电影成色剂
藕民伟
1959-07-31
第2版()
专栏：

    天津制成电影成色剂
    本报讯　天津市轻工业日用化学技术研究所等单位，最近试制出一批成色剂，并由天津市摄影器材厂用来涂布电影胶片成功。中央电影制片厂拿它复制影片“天鹅湖”片断，检定后，认为质量基本达到要求。　（藕民伟）
