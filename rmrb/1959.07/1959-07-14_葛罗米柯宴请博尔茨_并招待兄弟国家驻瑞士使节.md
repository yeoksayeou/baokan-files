Title: Gromyko Hosts a Banquet for Bolz and Receives Envoys from Brotherly Countries to Switzerland
Author:
Date: 1959-07-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, July 13th: According to TASS, Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko hosted a banquet on July 12th at the residence of the Soviet delegation attending the Geneva Foreign Ministers' Conference to entertain the delegation from the German Democratic Republic, diplomatic representatives of socialist countries to Switzerland, and representatives to the European Office of the United Nations.

    In addition to the delegation from the German Democratic Republic, headed by Minister Bolz, those present at the banquet included the Chinese and Polish Ambassadors to Switzerland, the Czechoslovak, Bulgarian, and Romanian Ministers to Switzerland, and the Hungarian Chargé d'Affaires.

    On the Soviet side, Zorin, Malik, Kolyukin, Chistyakov, and Ilyichev attended the banquet.

    The banquet was held in a sincere atmosphere.

### 葛罗米柯宴请博尔茨  并招待兄弟国家驻瑞士使节

1959-07-14
第4版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯宴请博尔茨
    并招待兄弟国家驻瑞士使节
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社日内瓦12日讯：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯12日在出席日内瓦外长会议的苏联代表团的住所设宴招待德意志民主共和国的代表团、各社会主义国家驻瑞士的外交代表和驻联合国欧洲办事处的代表。
    出席宴会的除了以博尔茨部长为首的德意志民主共和国代表团外，还有中国和波兰驻瑞士的大使，捷克斯洛伐克、保加利亚和罗马尼亚驻瑞士的公使，以及匈牙利的代办。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有：佐林、马立克、科柳金、契斯季亚科夫和伊利切夫。
    宴会是在真诚的气氛中进行的。
