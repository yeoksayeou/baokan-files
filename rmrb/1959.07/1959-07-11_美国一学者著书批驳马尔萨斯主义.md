Title: American Scholar Publishes Book Criticizing Malthusianism
Author:
Date: 1959-07-11
Page: 7

Academic and Cultural Trends

    American Scholar Publishes Book Criticizing
    Malthusianism
    The East German "Journal of Economics" published an article in its first issue this year by G. Sisskin, introducing the book "Must Mankind Starve?" by the American Jacob Oser. The article's author says that this book is an excellent weapon against hunger and Malthusianism. Oser states that the so-called "in overpopulated China, hunger is a necessary and welcome tool for regulating the overall economic situation" and "doubts whether rescuing malaria patients from death is an act of friendship towards the patients" are pseudo-science serving reactionary, militarized state monopoly capitalism. He uses convincing statistical material to prove the absurdity of the Malthusian theory of population. He points out that the poorest and most famine-stricken areas in the world, such as Africa and Latin America, are not overpopulated, but rather sparsely populated. This can be seen from the following table:
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
    　　　　People per Square Mile　　　　Annual Income per Person (USD)
    　　　　Bolivia　　　　　　　　3　　　　　　　　　　　　　　35
    　　　　Saudi Arabia　　　　　4　　　　　　　　　　　　　　40
    　　　　Belgian Congo　　　　5　　　　　　　　　　　　　　71
    　　　　Belgium　　　　　　　288　　　　　　　　　　　　　716
    　　　　United Kingdom　　　　208　　　　　　　　　　　　　809

    Oser further points out that the high birth rate in backward countries is the result of extreme poverty, not its cause. He says that underdeveloped countries can escape poverty and hunger through industrialization; they can improve their material living standards and cultural levels; and after industrialization, they can both maintain a large population and implement birth control, which is a double benefit. Because ignorant and impoverished people cannot reduce the birth rate. He also believes that war is the cause of poverty, not the result of poverty. He says, when in modern history has a poor country been seen to invade a wealthy country? Oser says that extreme poverty in the world today is the product of imperialism. Imperialism deliberately keeps underdeveloped countries backward through methods such as dumping and foreign investment. He believes that the fragmentation of agricultural land use is an important cause of low productivity and poverty. He says that mechanization, increasing grain yield per unit area, land consolidation, and the production of artificial food are all ways to increase grain production.

### 美国一学者著书批驳马尔萨斯主义

1959-07-11
第7版()
专栏：学术文化动态

    美国一学者著书批驳
    马尔萨斯主义
    德意志民主共和国“经济学杂志”今年第一期发表了格·西斯金写的，介绍美国雅各勃·奥色尔的“人类必然会饿死吗”一书的文章。文章作者说这书是反对饥饿、反对马尔萨斯主义的出色武器，奥色尔说，所谓“在人口过多的中国，饥饿是调节总的经济情况的必需的和值得欢迎的工具”、“怀疑把疟疾病人从死亡中救出来是不是对病人的友爱举动”等等，都是为反动的军国主义化的国家垄断资本主义服务的伪科学。他用令人信服的统计材料证明马尔萨斯人口论的荒谬性。他指出，全世界最贫穷、遭饥饿的地区，如非洲和拉丁美洲，都不是人口过多，而是人口很少的。从下表就可看出：
    每平方英里人数　每年每人收入（美元）
    玻利维亚　3　35
    沙特阿拉伯　4　40
    比属刚果　5　71
    比利时　288　716
    英国　208　809
    奥色尔并进一步指出，在落后国家中出生率高，是极度贫困的结果，而不是其原因。他说不发达国家可以用工业化摆脱贫困和饥饿，它们可以提高物质生活水平和文化水平；它们在工业化后既可以维持大量人口，也可以实行出生率控制，这是双重利益。因为无知和赤贫的人们是无法减低出生率的，他并认为，战争是贫穷的原因，而不是贫穷的结果。他说，现代史上几时见到一个贫穷的国家去侵略一个富裕国家？奥色尔说，目前世界中极度贫穷是帝国主义的产物。帝国主义利用倾销、国外投资等方法，故意使不发达国家保持落后状态。他认为，农业土地利用面积的零散是生产力低和贫穷的一个重要原因，他说，机械化、提高粮食单位面积产量、整理土地、生产人造食品等等，都是增产粮食的办法。
