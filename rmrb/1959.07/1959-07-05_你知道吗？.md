Title: Did you know?
Author:
Date: 1959-07-05
Page: 8

    Did you know?
    △The place where thunder can be heard is no more than twenty-five kilometers away from where the thunder originates.
    △If the entire Earth were covered with ice from the South Pole, the ice layer would be eighty-five meters thick.
    △The ancestors of elephants were smaller than present-day pigs.
    △The venom sprayed by African cobras can reach a distance of three and a half meters.

### 你知道吗？

1959-07-05
第8版()
专栏：

    你知道吗？
    △能听到雷响的地方离发出雷声处不会超过二十五公里。
    △如果用南极的冰来铺盖整个地球，那么冰层厚达八十五公尺。
    △象的祖先比现在的猪还小。
    △非洲的眼镜蛇喷出的毒素能达三点五公尺远。
