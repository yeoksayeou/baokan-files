Title: I Love Coal Miners
Author: Li Dezhen
Date: 1959-07-18
Page: 2

    I Love Coal Miners
    Beijing Mentougou Coal Mine Li Dezhen
    I love coal miners,
    The sound of the pneumatic drill in the coal mine,
    Is like a machine gun firing.
    I love coal miners,
    Picking up the pneumatic drill and electric drill,
    They never relax when they start working.
    I love coal miners,
    The lights in the underground tunnels are twinkling,
    Like the stars in the sky.
    I love coal miners,
    They work like Wu Song,
    Singing while walking after ascending from the mine,
    Happy and content after completing the task.
    (Attached picture)
    Zhao Zhifang illustration

### 我爱煤矿工
李德珍
1959-07-18
第2版()
专栏：

    我爱煤矿工
    北京门头沟煤矿  李德珍我爱煤矿工，煤矿风锤打眼，像机枪扫射声。我爱煤矿工，拿起风锤电钻，干起活来不放松。我爱煤矿工，井下巷道灯闪闪，好像天上的明星。我爱煤矿工，干起活来赛武松，升井后一边走一边唱，完成任务喜洋洋。（附图片）
