Title: Japanese-Soviet Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-07-06
Page: 4

    Japanese-Soviet Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, July 5th - A Japanese-Soviet Friendship Delegation of six people, led by head of the delegation Kiyome Ito, arrived in Beijing by train this morning. They are passing through Beijing on their way back to their country after visiting the Soviet Union and will be making a short visit in Beijing.

### 日苏亲善使节团到京

1959-07-06
第4版()
专栏：

    日苏亲善使节团到京
    新华社5日讯　日苏亲善使节团一行六人，在团长伊藤清眼率领下，今天上午乘火车到达北京。他们是在访问苏联后回国途中路过北京的，将在北京作短期访问。
