Title: Eisenhower's Prayer
Author: Migu, Chi Bei'ou
Date: 1959-07-31
Page: 5

Eisenhower's Prayer
	Migu Painting, Chi Bei'ou Poem
	Lord, please bring disaster upon those ten billion proud madmen, who only believe in themselves and do not rely on God, who dare to build paradise on earth, who dare to hang man-made stars in the sky.
	Lord, please punish those ten billion slaves who have stood up, who actually refuse to obey my will, who, when we strike their left cheek, retaliate against our right cheek.
	Lord, please grant me strength, I need more weapons of murder, to liberate those people from freedom, and use chains to adorn their unyielding necks.
	Lord, please grant me courage, I am truly flustered and panic-stricken, I hope you can restrain that swift easterly wind, and save our declining capitalism. Amen!

	News report: As a prelude to the so-called "Captive Nations Week" farce, Eisenhower actually used the religious ceremony of Christians praying for peace and happiness to carry out anti-communist propaganda, cursing the people of socialist countries.

### 艾森豪威尔的祈祷文
米谷　池北偶
1959-07-31
第5版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔的祈祷文
    米谷画　池北偶诗主啊，请降祸给那十亿骄傲的狂人，他们只相信自己而不指望神，他们竟敢在人间建造天堂，竟敢在天上挂起手造的星辰。主啊，请处罚那十亿站了起来的奴隶，他们居然不肯顺从我的意旨，我们要是打他们的左脸，他们就向我们的右脸还击。主啊，请赐给我实力，我需要有更多杀人的武器，好把那些人们从自由中解放出来，用锁链去装饰他们不屈的脖子，主啊，请赐给我勇气，我实在心慌意乱、魂不附体，但愿你能喝住那疾劲的东风，打救我们日趋末落的资本主义。阿门！
    报载：作为所谓“被奴役国家周”丑剧的序幕，艾森豪威尔竟利用基督教徒祈求和平幸福的宗教仪式来进行反共宣传，咒骂社会主义国家的人民。
