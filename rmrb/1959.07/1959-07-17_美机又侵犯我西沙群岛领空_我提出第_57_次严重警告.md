Title: U.S. Aircraft Again Invades Our Xisha Islands' Airspace; We Issue the 57th Serious Warning
Author: 
Date: 1959-07-17
Page: 1

    U.S. Aircraft Again Invades Our Xisha Islands' Airspace
    We Issue the 57th Serious Warning
    Xinhua News Agency, 16th - At 9:45 AM to 10:08 AM on July 16th, a U.S. Navy patrol plane intruded into the airspace above Yongxing Island, Dong Island, Nan Island, and Bei Island of our Guangdong Province's Xisha Islands. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, a spokesperson for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 57th serious warning.

### 美机又侵犯我西沙群岛领空  我提出第57次严重警告

1959-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    美机又侵犯我西沙群岛领空
    我提出第57次严重警告
    新华社16日讯　7月16日九时四十五分至十时零八分，美国海军巡逻机一架，侵入我国广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、东岛、南岛、北岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第五十七次严重警告。
