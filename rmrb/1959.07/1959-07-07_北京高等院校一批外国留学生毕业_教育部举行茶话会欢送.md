Title: A Group of Foreign Students Graduated from Beijing Higher Education Institutions; Ministry of Education Holds Tea Party to Bid Farewell

Author: 
Date: 1959-07-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th — The Ministry of Education held a tea party today to bid farewell to foreign students from twelve countries, including the Soviet Union, who are graduating from Beijing higher education institutions this year. Over 140 foreign students gathered at the tea party to bid each other farewell. Representatives of the foreign students expressed their gratitude to the Chinese government and schools for their assistance and cultivation.
    Liu Zizai, Vice Minister of Education, who presided over the tea party, congratulated the foreign students on their successful completion of their studies in China and expressed the hope that after returning to their home countries, the students would achieve new accomplishments in promoting cultural exchange and friendly cooperation between their countries and China, and in safeguarding the cause of world peace.
    This year, a total of 291 foreign students from twelve countries, including the Soviet Union, Vietnam, North Korea, the German Democratic Republic, Romania, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Bulgaria, Hungary, India, Indonesia, and Yugoslavia, will graduate from higher and secondary specialized schools in our country. Those in Beijing are a part of this group.

### 北京高等院校一批外国留学生毕业  教育部举行茶话会欢送

1959-07-07
第4版()
专栏：

    北京高等院校一批外国留学生毕业
    教育部举行茶话会欢送
    新华社6日讯　教育部今天举行茶话会，欢送在北京高等院校今年毕业的苏联等十二个国家留学生。在茶话会上，一百四十余名外国留学生欢聚一堂，依依话别。留学生代表对中国政府和学校的帮助、培养表示感谢。
    主持这个茶话会的教育部副部长刘子载祝贺留学生们在中国胜利完成了学业，希望留学生们回国后，在促进本国与中国的文化交流和友好合作、维护世界和平事业等方面，取得新的成就。
    在我国高等及中等专业学校学习的外国留学生，今年将有苏联、越南、朝鲜、德意志民主共和国、罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、保加利亚、匈牙利、印度、印度尼西亚、南斯拉夫等十二个国家的二百九十一名留学生毕业，在北京的是其中一部分。
