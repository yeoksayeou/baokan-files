Title: Iraqi Kurdistan Unified Democratic Party Statement: Strengthen Unity to Thwart Enemy Conspiracies
Author:
Date: 1959-07-21
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, July 20: The Iraqi Kurdistan Unified Democratic Party, in a statement issued on July 14th to commemorate the first anniversary of the revolution, called for “strengthening the unity of the democratic forces of all nationalities in Iraq, demanding greater vigilance to stop and thwart all conspiracies of the enemies of the republic, striving for more victories and creating a better future for all the people, and demanding that the Kurds enjoy the national rights granted by the July 14th Revolution.”

The statement, published today by "Voice of Free Men," emphasized that the Kurdistan Unified Democratic Party has never abandoned the weapons of revolutionary struggle, and has never withdrawn from the ranks of fierce struggle against imperialism, reactionaries, and feudalism before and after the revolution.

The statement guarantees that the Kurdistan Unified Democratic Party will continue to fight loyally and earnestly, and will carry out the glorious task of uniting the ranks and forces of the national united front, in order to defend the republic and strengthen democratic practices, and to defend the achievements of the revolution under the leadership of Qasim. The statement expressed the Kurdistan Unified Democratic Party's support for the democratic policies implemented by the government in the domestic sphere.

### 伊拉克库尔德斯坦统一民主党声明  加强团结挫败敌人阴谋

1959-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克库尔德斯坦统一民主党声明
    加强团结挫败敌人阴谋
    新华社巴格达20日电　伊拉克库尔德斯坦统一民主党在7月14日为纪念革命一周年而发表的一项声明中，要求“加强伊拉克的所有民族的民主力量的团结，要求更加提高警惕来制止和挫败共和国敌人的一切阴谋，要求争取更多的胜利和为全体人民创造更美好的前途，并且要求库尔德人享受7月14日的革命所赋予的民族权利。”
      “自由人之声报”今天刊载的这项声明着重指出，库尔德斯坦统一民主党从没有放弃过革命斗争的武器，并且在革命以前和革命以后从没有退出对帝国主义、反动派和封建主义进行激烈斗争的行列。
    声明保证，库尔德斯坦统一民主党将继续忠诚地和认真地进行斗争，并且执行团结民族统一阵线的队伍和力量的光荣任务，以便保卫共和国和加强民主实践，并且在卡塞姆的领导下保卫革命成果。声明表示，库尔德斯坦统一民主党支持政府执行的国内方面的民主政策。
