Title: Commemorating the 32nd Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army: Pyongyang Exhibits Friendship Pictures of the Korean and Chinese People's Armies
Author:
Date: 1959-07-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 29th. An exhibition of pictures held by the Korean Ministry of National Defence and the Korea-China Friendship Association to commemorate the 32nd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army opened this afternoon at the Korean Fatherland Liberation War Memorial in Pyongyang. The exhibition displays more than two hundred pictures recording the history of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the friendship between the peoples' armies of China and Korea.

Ha Ang-cheon, Chairman of the Korea-China Friendship Association, cut the ribbon for the opening of the exhibition. Seo Cheol, Director of the General Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army and Vice Chairman of the Korea-China Friendship Association, delivered a speech at the opening ceremony.

Cao Ke-qiang, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy, and Colonel Zhang Le-ting, military attache, were also invited to attend the opening ceremony of the exhibition.

### 纪念我人民解放军建军32周年  平壤展出朝中人民军队友谊图片

1959-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念我人民解放军建军32周年
    平壤展出朝中人民军队友谊图片
    新华社平壤29日电　朝鲜民族保卫省和朝中友好协会为中国人民解放军建军三十二周年举办的图片展览会今天下午在平壤市朝鲜祖国解放战争纪念馆内开幕。展览会展出了纪录着中国人民解放军历史和中朝两国人民军队的友谊的图片二百多幅。
    朝中友好协会会长河仰天为展览会剪彩。朝鲜人民军总政治局局长、朝中友好协会副会长徐哲将中在开幕式上致了词。
    中国大使馆临时代办曹克强和武官张乐亭上校，各国驻朝使馆人员也应邀参加了展览会的开幕式。
