Title: Czechoslovak Journalists Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-07-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 20th: A Czechoslovakian journalists' delegation, consisting of six members, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon, according to the 1959 execution plan of the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

　　The delegation is led by Comrade Oldřich Švestka, editor-in-chief of "Rudé Právo" and alternate member of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party. The delegation members include: Andrej Krotak, editor-in-chief of the Slovak Communist Party Central Committee's newspaper "Pravda"; Oldřich Jarušev, editor-in-chief of the Brno Province "Rovnost"; Jaroslav Černý, editor of "People's Defence"; Karel Hájek, photojournalist of "World Pictorial"; and He Shima, correspondent of the Czechoslovak News Agency stationed in Beijing.

　　Welcoming them at the airport were Hu Jiwei, deputy editor-in-chief of "People's Daily," Kang Maozhao, deputy director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and representatives of the capital's press circles.

### 捷新闻工作者代表团到京

1959-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    捷新闻工作者代表团到京
    新华社20日讯　根据中捷文化合作协定1959年执行计划前来我国访问的捷克斯洛伐克新闻工作者代表团一行六人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团由“红色权利报”总编辑、捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央候补委员奥德里赫·什维斯特卡同志率领。代表团团员有：斯洛伐克共产党中央机关报“真理报”总编辑安德烈以·克洛托奇、布尔诺州
  “平等报”总编辑奥德里赫·亚鲁谢夫、“人民防御报”编辑雅鲁斯拉夫·切尔内、“世界画报”摄影记者卡雷尔·哈耶克、捷克斯洛伐克通讯社驻北京记者何诗玛。
    到机场欢迎的有‘人民日报’副总编辑胡绩伟、外交部新闻司副司长康茅召及首都新闻界代表。
