Title: Zhengzhou Makes Light Bulbs
Author:
Date: 1959-07-09
Page: 2

Zhengzhou Light Bulb Factory in Henan Province, which has an annual output of more than 4 million light bulbs, is under intensive construction and is scheduled to be completed in the fourth quarter. In order to meet market demand, the first set of machines recently installed has been put into production. The optician of Zhengzhou Light Bulb Factory is learning the technology of inspecting filaments from the technician (standing) of Shanghai Ya'pu'er Light Bulb Factory.

Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Qingyun photo

### 郑州制灯泡

1959-07-09
第2版()
专栏：

    郑州制灯泡
    年产灯泡四百多万只的河南省郑州灯泡制造厂正在加紧建设，计划到第四季度全部建成。为了供应市场需要，最近安装好的第一套车已经投入生产。郑州灯泡厂的验光员在向上海亚浦耳灯泡厂的技术员（立者）学习检验灯丝的技术。
        新华社记者　张青云摄
