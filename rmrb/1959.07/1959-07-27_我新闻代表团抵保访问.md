Title: Chinese News Delegation Arrives in Bulgaria for Visit
Author:
Date: 1959-07-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, July 26th. A Chinese news delegation, headed by Yu Guanxi, editor-in-chief of the "Zhejiang Daily," arrived in Sofia on the 25th from Warsaw.

　　The delegation is visiting here according to the executive plan of the Sino-Bulgarian cultural cooperation agreement.

### 我新闻代表团抵保访问

1959-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    我新闻代表团抵保访问
    新华社索非亚26日电　以“浙江日报”总编辑于冠西为首的中国新闻工作者代表团，25日从华沙抵达索非亚。
    代表团是根据中保文化合作协定执行计划来这里访问的。
