Title: New Mongolian Ambassador to China Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-07-03
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, July 2nd—The new Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, Sharav, arrived in Beijing today.
　　This afternoon, Ambassador Sharav met with Vice Foreign Minister Ji Pengfei to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials.

### 蒙古新任驻华大使到京

1959-07-03
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古新任驻华大使到京
    新华社2日讯　蒙古人民共和国新任驻华大使沙拉布今天到达北京。
    今天下午，沙拉布大使会见了我外交部副部长姬鹏飞，商谈递交国书事宜。
