Title: The Fun of Miao Children is Endless (Picture)
Author: Yuan Ying, Gu Cun
Date: 1959-07-30
Page: 6

    The Fun of Miao Children is Endless
                  Poem by Yuan Ying, Photography by Gu Cun
    Clear wind and cool water,
    The fun of summer vacation life is endless.
    The tinkling zither and the gentle song,
    The joy of Miao children can't be sung enough.

### 苗家孩子的乐趣说不完（图片）
袁鹰　谷村
1959-07-30
第6版()
专栏：

    苗家孩子的乐趣说不完
                  袁鹰诗　谷村摄影
    清清的风呀凉凉的水，
    暑期生活的乐趣说不完哩。
    琤琤的琴呀缓缓的歌，
    苗家孩子的欢乐唱不完哩。
