Title: Cambodian Basketball Team Plays Against "August 1st" Team
Author:
Date: 1959-07-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th: The Cambodian National Men's Basketball Team won warm applause and praise from 6,000 Chinese spectators tonight in Beijing during a friendly match with the Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" Men's Basketball Team, with their skilled dribbling techniques and good sportsmanship.

Although the visiting team lost to the "August 1st" team with a score of 48 to 120 at the end of the game, they had tied the score with the "August 1st" team, which is known for its fast breaks, at 32 to 48 at the end of the first half.

The visiting team has concluded its three friendly matches in Beijing and will go to Tianjin, where it is scheduled to hold a friendly match with the Hebei Provincial Men's Basketball Team on the 30th.

### 柬埔寨篮球队同“八一”队比赛

1959-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨篮球队同“八一”队比赛
    据新华社27日讯　柬埔寨王国国家男子篮球队今晚在北京同中国人民解放军“八一”男子篮球队举行友谊比赛时，以他们熟练的运球技术和良好的体育运动道德作风，赢得了六千名中国观众的热烈掌声和赞扬。
    尽管客队在全场比赛结束时以四十八比一百二十的比分败给“八一”队，但上半场结束时，他们曾同以擅长快速进攻闻名的“八一”队打成了三十二比四十八的局面。
    客队已经结束了在北京的三场友谊比赛，即将到天津去，预定30日同河北省男子篮球队举行友谊比赛。
