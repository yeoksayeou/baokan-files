Title: Vietnam-Mongolia Sign Trade Protocol
Author: 
Date: 1959-07-19
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi Dispatch: The Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Mongolian People's Republic signed a barter and payment protocol for 1960 and a protocol on Mongolia providing economic assistance to Vietnam on the 14th in Hanoi.
    The first protocol stipulates that the volume of barter between the two countries in 1960 will increase by 35% compared to 1959.
    Dang Viet Chau, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Shagdarjalyn Baturga, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of Mongolia, signed the protocols.

### 越蒙签订贸易议定书

1959-07-19
第3版()
专栏：

    越蒙签订贸易议定书
    新华社讯　越南通讯社河内讯：越南民主共和国和蒙古人民共和国14日在河内签订了一项1960年的易货和支付议定书以及一项蒙古给越南以经济援助的议定书。
    第一项议定书规定：两国之间在1960年的易货数量将比1959年增加35％。
    越南民主共和国对外贸易部副部长邓曰州和蒙古对外贸易部副部长夏格达里·巴图尔加在议定书上签了字。
