Title: Our Youth Table Tennis Team Arrives in Prague
Author:
Date: 1959-07-16
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 14th: The Chinese Youth Table Tennis Team, a group of ten led by Yan Zongpo, arrived here on the afternoon of the 14th. The team members include well-known athletes Sun Meiying, Xu Yinsheng, and Chen Xiezhong.

　　The Chinese Youth Table Tennis Team will visit Czechoslovakia for a week, during which they will play several friendly matches with Czechoslovak athletes. Afterwards, they will also visit Romania.

### 我青年乒乓球队到达布拉格

1959-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

    我青年乒乓球队到达布拉格
    新华社布拉格14日电　中国青年乒乓球队一行十人，在领队阎宗坡率领下，14日下午抵达这里。队员中包括著名运动员孙梅英、徐寅生、陈协中等人。
    中国青年乒乓球队将在捷克斯洛伐克访问一周，访问期间将同捷克斯洛伐克运动员进行数场友谊比赛。此后，还将前往罗马尼亚访问。
