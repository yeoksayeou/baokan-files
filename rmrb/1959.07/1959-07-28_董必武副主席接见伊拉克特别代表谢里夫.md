Title: Vice Chairman Dong Biwu Receives Iraqi Special Representative Sharif
Author:
Date: 1959-07-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 27th: Vice Chairman Dong Biwu received Aziz Sharif, the Special Representative of the Republic of Iraq, at Qin Zheng Hall in Zhongnanhai this morning. The host and guest engaged in friendly talks.

Present at the reception were Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity Liao Chengzhi, and others. (Picture attached)

From left to right: Ji Pengfei, Chen Yi, Aziz Sharif, Dong Biwu, Guo Moruo, Liao Chengzhi. Photographed by Xinhua News Agency reporters Zhang Bin and Zou Jiandong.

### 董必武副主席接见伊拉克特别代表谢里夫

1959-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

    董必武副主席接见伊拉克特别代表谢里夫
    新华社27日讯　董必武副主席今天上午在中南海勤政殿接见伊拉克共和国特别代表阿齐兹·谢里夫，宾主进行了友好谈话。
    接见时在座的，有国务院副总理陈毅，人大副委员长郭沫若，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志等。（附图片）
