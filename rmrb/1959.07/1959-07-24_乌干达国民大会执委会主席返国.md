Title: Uganda National Congress Executive Chairman Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-07-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd: Kiwanuka, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Uganda National Congress, concluded his visit to our country and left Beijing by plane today to return home.

### 乌干达国民大会执委会主席返国

1959-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    乌干达国民大会执委会主席返国
    新华社23日讯　乌干达国民大会执行委员会主席基瓦努卡结束了在我国的访问，已经在今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
