Title: "August 1st" Men's Basketball Team Defeats Hungarian National Team
Author:
Date: 1959-07-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" Men's Basketball Team defeated the Hungarian National Men's Basketball Team tonight with a fast-paced offense. The score was 73 to 59. The "August 1st" team led in the first half with a score of 31 to 27.

### “八一”男子篮球队胜匈牙利国家队

1959-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

    “八一”男子篮球队胜匈牙利国家队
    新华社16日讯　中国人民解放军“八一”男子篮球队今晚以快速的攻势，战胜了匈牙利国家男子篮球队，比分是七十三比五十九。上半场是三十一比二十七，“八一”队领先。
