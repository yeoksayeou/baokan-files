Title: China-Cambodia Men's Basketball Friendly Match: Beijing Team Wins 128-44
Author:
Date: 1959-07-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 26th. The Beijing Men's Basketball Team held a friendly match with the Cambodian National Men's Basketball Team at the Beijing Gymnasium today. As a result, they won with a score of 128-44. The Beijing team led 66-20 at the end of the first half. Approximately six thousand spectators watched the match.

### 中柬男子篮球友谊赛  北京队以128比44获胜

1959-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    中柬男子篮球友谊赛
    北京队以128比44获胜
    据新华社26日讯　北京市男子篮球队今晚在北京体育馆比赛馆同柬埔寨王国国家男子篮球队举行友谊比赛，结果以一百二十八比四十四获胜。北京队在上半场结束时以六十六比二十领先。约有六千名观众观看了这场比赛。
