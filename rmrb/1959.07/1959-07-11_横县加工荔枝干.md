Title: Heng County Processes Dried Lychees
Author:
Date: 1959-07-11
Page: 2

Heng County Processes Dried Lychees

A large quantity of Nangxiang lychees in Heng County, Guangxi, have already entered the market.

In order to guarantee the supply to cities and support national exports, the state-owned Nangxiang Store is vigorously organizing the processing of dried lychees, and has already established three processing plants and thirty-five processing stoves.

### 横县加工荔枝干

1959-07-11
第2版()
专栏：

    横县加工荔枝干
    广西横县南乡荔枝已经大量上市。
    为了保证城市供应和支援国家出口，国营南乡商店大力组织加工荔枝干，已经建立了三个加工场和三十五个加工炉。
