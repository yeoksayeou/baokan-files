Title: New Mongolian Ambassador to China, Sharab, Presents Credentials to Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling
Author:
Date: 1959-07-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 3rd: The new Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, Sharab, accompanied by all the diplomatic officials of the embassy, presented his credentials to Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling today. Present were: Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Chen Shuliang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department.

    When presenting his credentials, Ambassador Sharab delivered a eulogy, and Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling delivered a reply.

    In his eulogy, Ambassador Sharab said: Please allow me to take this opportunity to represent the Presidium of the Great People's Hural, the government, and the Mongolian people in extending our most heartfelt congratulations to you, and through you, to our great neighbor—the fraternal Chinese people and the Government of the People's Republic of China.

    The Ambassador said: The fraternal friendship and close cooperation between the Mongolian and Chinese peoples, based on the principles of mutual assistance, sincere cooperation, and equality, are being consolidated and developed day by day for the happiness of our two peoples. The Mongolian people express their deep gratitude to the Chinese people for the comprehensive and generous assistance they have provided to our socialist construction.

    The Ambassador said: I am honored to assure you that I will do everything in my power to further strengthen and develop the friendly relations between our two peoples.

    In her reply, Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling said: The Chinese and Mongolian peoples have a traditional friendship. Our two countries share a border, and our two peoples are as close as brothers. Over the past decade, with the rapid development of economic and cultural cooperation between our two countries, the friendly relations between our two peoples have been further strengthened and consolidated. Our mutual assistance and cooperation in the socialist family headed by the Soviet Union has not only powerfully promoted our respective socialist construction endeavors but also opened up broad prospects for the good life of our two peoples. Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling said: Comrade Ambassador, I warmly welcome you to take office as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to the People's Republic of China. In your work to consolidate and develop the friendship and mutual assistance and cooperation between China and Mongolia, you will surely receive my full support and that from the Government of the People's Republic of China. I wish you complete success in your work.

### 蒙古新任驻华大使沙拉布  向宋庆龄副主席递交国书

1959-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古新任驻华大使沙拉布
    向宋庆龄副主席递交国书
    新华社3日讯　蒙古人民共和国新任驻中国大使沙拉布偕同大使馆全体外交官员今天向宋庆龄副主席递交国书。在场的有：国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮、礼宾司司长俞沛文。
    递交国书时，沙拉布大使致颂词，宋庆龄副主席致答词。
    沙拉布大使在颂词中说：请允许我借此机会代表大人民呼拉尔主席团、政府和蒙古人民向您并通过您向我们伟大的邻邦——兄弟的中国人民、中华人民共和国政府致以最衷心的祝贺。
    大使说：建立在互助、真诚的合作和平等原则上的蒙中两国人民的兄弟友谊和亲密合作，正在为着我们两国人民的幸福而日益巩固和发展。蒙古人民对中国人民在我国社会主义建设中给予的全面的慷慨的援助表示深深的谢意。
    大使说：我荣幸地向您保证，我将为进一步加强和发展我们两国人民的友好关系，尽自己的一切力量。
    宋庆龄副主席在答词中说：中蒙两国人民有着传统的友谊。我们两国国境毗连，我们两国人民亲如兄弟。十年来，随着两国经济、文化合作的迅速发展，我们两国人民的友好关系也得到了进一步的加强和巩固。我们两国在以苏联为首的社会主义大家庭中的互助合作，不仅有力地推进了我们各自的社会主义建设事业，而且也为我们两国人民的美好生活开辟了广阔的前景。宋庆龄副主席说：大使同志，我热烈地欢迎你出任蒙古人民共和国驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使。在你为巩固和发展中蒙两国友谊和互助合作的工作中，一定能够得到我和中华人民共和国政府的全力支持。我预祝你在工作中获得完全的成功。
