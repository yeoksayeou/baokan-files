Title: Our Muslim Hajj Delegation Departs for Casablanca
Author:
Date: 1959-07-27
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, July 25th: The Chinese Muslim Hajj Delegation, headed by Ma Zhuozhou, concluded its seven-day visit and left here this morning for Casablanca.

### 我穆斯林朝觐团去卡萨布兰卡

1959-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

    我穆斯林朝觐团去卡萨布兰卡
    新华社拉巴特25日电　以马卓洲为首的中国穆斯林朝觐团结束了为时七天的访问，今天上午离开这里前往卡萨布兰卡。
