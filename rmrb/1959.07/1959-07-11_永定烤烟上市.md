Title: Yongding Cured Tobacco Goes on the Market
Author:
Date: 1959-07-11
Page: 2

    Yongding cured tobacco, from Fujian, which is fragrant and of excellent quality, is now available in large quantities. This year's Yongding cured tobacco production increased by about 20% compared to last year. It is currently the peak season for marketing, with the highest daily amount reaching over 64,000 jin. This year, the state's purchase volume is expected to reach approximately 4 million jin, an increase of 1.7 million jin compared to last year. Currently, this high-quality tobacco is being shipped to Shanghai, Tianjin, Guangzhou and other places.

### 永定烤烟上市

1959-07-11
第2版()
专栏：

    永定烤烟上市
    气味芳香、品质优良的福建永定烤烟已经大量上市。今年永定烤烟产量比去年约增加20％。目前正是上市旺季，最高日上市量达六万四千多斤，今年国家收购量约可达四百万斤，比去年可增一百七十万斤。现在，这种品质优良的烟叶正源源运往上海、天津、广州等地。
