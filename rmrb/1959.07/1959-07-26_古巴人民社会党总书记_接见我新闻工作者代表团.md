Title: General Secretary of the Cuban People's Socialist Party Receives Chinese Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-07-26
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Havana, July 24th:  The General Secretary of the Cuban People's Socialist Party, Roca, received the Chinese journalists delegation visiting Cuba yesterday. Members of the Executive Bureau of the National Committee of the People's Socialist Party were present at the meeting.

The Chinese journalists delegation is in Cuba for a friendly visit at the invitation of the Cuban Press Association. Prior to this, they had been received by the Cuban government and leaders of the "July 26th" Movement.

### 古巴人民社会党总书记  接见我新闻工作者代表团

1959-07-26
第3版()
专栏：

    古巴人民社会党总书记  接见我新闻工作者代表团
    新华社哈瓦那24日电　古巴人民社会党总书记罗加昨天接见了正在古巴访问的中国新闻工作者代表团。接见时在座的还有人民社会党全国委员会执行局的委员。
    中国新闻工作者代表团是应古巴报业联盟的邀请到古巴进行友好访问的。在此以前，已受到古巴政府和“7月26日”运动领导人的接见。
