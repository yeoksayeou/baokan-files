Title: China and Afghanistan Sign 1968 Barter Agreement
Author:
Date: 1968-01-15
Edition: 6

	The 1968 barter agreement between China and Afghanistan was signed in Kabul on the 10th.

	The representatives who signed the agreement were: Ma Yiji, the Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Afghanistan, and Ali Nawaz, the Director of the Commercial Department of the Afghan Ministry of Commerce.

	Afghan Deputy Minister of Commerce Akbar Omar and relevant officials, as well as staff from the Chinese Embassy, attended the signing ceremony. After the ceremony, Afghan Minister of Commerce Nur Ali met with both parties and had a friendly conversation with the Chinese side. Both sides expressed satisfaction with the further development of trade between the two countries.

### 中国阿富汗签订今年换货议定书

1968-01-15
第6版()
专栏：

    中国阿富汗签订今年换货议定书
    新华社喀布尔十二日电　中国和阿富汗之间的一九六八年度换货议定书于十日在喀布尔签字。
    代表双方签字的是：中华人民共和国驻阿富汗大使馆临时代办姚吉年，阿富汗商业部商业司长阿里·纳瓦兹。
    阿富汗商业副大臣阿克巴尔·奥马尔和有关官员，以及中国大使馆人员出席了签字仪式。签字仪式后，阿富汗商业大臣努尔·阿里接见了双方人员，并同中国方面的人员进行了友好的谈话。双方都对两国贸易的进一步发展表示满意。
