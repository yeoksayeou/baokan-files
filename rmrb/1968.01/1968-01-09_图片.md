Title: Workers and Soldiers Study New Year's Editorial and Resolve to Raise High the Great Red Banner of Mao Zedong Thought
Author:
Date: 1968-01-09
Edition: 1

Workers from the Beijing Power Supply Bureau's emergency repair team and soldiers carrying out the task of supporting the left, after studying the New Year's editorial, have resolved to raise higher the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought and strive for a comprehensive victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.
