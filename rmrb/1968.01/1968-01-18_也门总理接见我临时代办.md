Title: Yemen's Prime Minister Meets Chinese Chargé d'Affaires
Author:
Date: 1968-01-18
Edition: 6

Yemen's Prime Minister and Commander-in-Chief of the Yemeni Armed Forces, Hassan Al-Amri, received the Chinese Chargé d'Affaires to Yemen, Zheng Kangping, on the evening of the 14th. They had a very friendly conversation.

### 也门总理接见我临时代办

1968-01-18
第6版()
专栏：

    也门总理接见我临时代办
    新华社萨那十四日电　也门总理兼也门武装部队总司令哈桑·阿姆里十四日晚上接见了中国驻也门大使馆临时代办郑康平。他们进行了十分友好的谈话。
