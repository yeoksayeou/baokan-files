Title: Benxi Coal Mine Fires the First Shot of Production for This Year
Author:
Date: 1968-01-07
Edition: 3

The revolutionary workers and the People's Liberation Army soldiers stationed at Benxi Coal Mine, inspired by the New Year's editorials in the People's Daily, Red Flag Magazine, and the Liberation Army Daily, have fired the first shot of the 1968 campaign to seize the revolution and promote production. On New Year's Day, the coal production across the entire mine exceeded the plan by 9.1 percent, and the excavation advance exceeded the plan by 11.2 percent.

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 本溪煤矿打响了今年生产第一炮

1968-01-07
第3版()
专栏：

    本溪煤矿打响了今年生产第一炮
    本溪煤矿的广大革命职工和驻矿人民解放军指战员，在《人民日报》、《红旗》杂志、《解放军报》元旦社论的鼓舞下，打响了一九六八年抓革命、促生产的第一炮。元旦这天，全矿区煤炭产量比计划提高百分之九点一，掘进进尺比计划提高百分之十一点二。
              （据新华社讯）
