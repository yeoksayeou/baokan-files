Title: Soldiers of the 4618 Unit Bring Chairman Mao's Latest Instructions to the Countryside
Author: Wei Junjie Wang Xuandong
Date: 1968-01-25
Edition: 3

Soldiers and officers of the 4618 Unit of the People's Liberation Army have gone deep into the countryside, delivering Chairman Mao's latest instructions to the hearts of the poor and lower-middle peasants. The image shows soldiers assisting in agricultural work studying Chairman Mao's works together with old poor peasant Li Desheng.
