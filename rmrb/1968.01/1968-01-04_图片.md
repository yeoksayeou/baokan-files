Title: Mao Zedong Thought Study Classes Have Been Established Throughout Jiangxi
Author:
Date: 1968-01-04
Edition: 3

Mao Zedong Thought study classes have been widely established in various parts of Jiangxi. This is a photo of the Mao Zedong Thought study class for revolutionary cadres and Red Guards organized by the Jiangxi Provincial Revolutionary Committee. The class is holding a session to speak bitterness, criticize revisionism, and expose one's own faults. Photo by our reporter.
