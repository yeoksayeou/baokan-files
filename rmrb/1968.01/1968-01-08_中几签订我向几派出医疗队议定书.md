Title: China and Guinea Sign Agreement on Sending Medical Team
Author:
Date: 1968-01-08
Edition: 5

According to Xinhua News Agency, Konakry. The Chinese Ambassador to Guinea, Lin Bingnan, and Guinea's Minister of Health and Social Affairs, Diallo Alpha Amadou, signed an agreement on December 30 last year on behalf of their respective governments. The agreement pertains to the dispatch of a Chinese medical team to Guinea. This agreement was signed at the request of the Guinean government.

### 中几签订我向几派出医疗队议定书

1968-01-08
第5版()
专栏：

    中几签订我向几派出医疗队议定书
    据新华社科纳克里电　中国驻几内亚大使馆临时代办林秉南和几内亚卫生和社会事务部长迪亚洛·阿尔法·阿马杜，去年十二月三十日在这里分别代表两国政府签订了一项关于中国向几内亚派出一个医疗队的议定书。这项议定书是应几内亚政府的要求签订的。
