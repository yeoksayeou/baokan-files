Title: Bursting with the Combat Friendship Between Chinese and Vietnamese Peoples
Author:
Date: 1968-01-16
Edition: 6

	Bursting with the combat friendship between Chinese and Vietnamese peoples, the delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, led by combat hero Nguyen Van Dan, returned to South Vietnam after participating in China's National Day celebrations and conducting a friendly visit.

	The delegation reported to the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam and the Command of the People's Liberation Armed Forces of South Vietnam about the successful results of their friendly visit to China.

	Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam and other leaders, as well as leaders of the Command of the People's Liberation Armed Forces, warmly received the delegation and highly praised the delegation's friendly visit. The delegation will also hold a report meeting to introduce their visit to China to the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front and the various departments under the Command of the People's Liberation Armed Forces, as well as to the military and people of the liberated areas.

### 满载中越人民之间的战斗友谊  越南南方民族解放阵线代表团回到南越

1968-01-16
第6版()
专栏：

    满载中越人民之间的战斗友谊
    越南南方民族解放阵线代表团回到南越
    新华社河内十五日电 据越南南方解放通讯社报道，由越南南方战斗英雄黄文旦率领的越南南方民族解放阵线代表团在参加中国国庆活动和进行友好访问之后，满载中越两国人民之间的战斗友谊，胜利地回到了越南南方解放区。
    代表团向越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会和南方人民解放武装力量指挥部，报告了代表团对中国的友好访问所取得的美满成果。
    越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团主席阮友寿和其他领导人，以及人民解放武装力量指挥部的领导人亲切地接见了代表团，并对代表团的这次友好访问给予高度的评价。代表团还将举行报告会，向民族解放阵线中央委员会和人民解放武装力量指挥部所属各机关，以及解放区军民介绍他们访问中国的情况。
