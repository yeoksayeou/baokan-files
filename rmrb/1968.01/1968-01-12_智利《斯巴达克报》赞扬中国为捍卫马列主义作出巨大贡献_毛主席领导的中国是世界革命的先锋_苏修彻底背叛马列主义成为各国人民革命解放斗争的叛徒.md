Title: Chilean "Spartacus" Praises China's Great Contribution to Defending Marxism-Leninism
Author:
Date: 1968-01-12
Edition: 6

The Chilean revolutionary communist party's organ, "Spartacus," recently published an editorial and articles praising China under the leadership of Chairman Mao as a strong bastion for promoting world revolution and condemning the Soviet revisionist leadership's betrayal of the October Revolution.

The newspaper, in an editorial, pointed out that the Chinese revolution, which triumphed in 1949, and the October Socialist Revolution in the Soviet Union "are the two most important events in contemporary history with the greatest significance for humanity. These two revolutions are the victories of the creative application of scientific socialism by the two most outstanding disciples of Marx and Engels—Lenin and Mao Zedong."

The editorial said: "However, the Soviet Union and China have now taken fundamentally different directions."

The editorial pointed out: "In the Soviet Union, a group of representatives of the privileged stratum that usurped power have betrayed the fundamental principles of Marxism-Leninism, accelerating the restoration of capitalism in this country and promoting the infiltration of imperialism. While in China, under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong, the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is being carried out to eliminate a handful of counter-revolutionary revisionists and to train a new generation of people marching towards communism."

The editorial said: "For the revolutionary future of the peoples of the various countries in Latin America, the opposite directions taken by the Soviet Union and China are not insignificant." It pointed out: "The traitors who usurped power in the great country of Lenin are using the high platform of authority established by the Russian proletariat through struggle to preach betrayal and cooperation with imperialism, to spread the illusion of a false 'peaceful' road to seizing power, and in general, to betray the teachings of Marxism-Leninism and suppress the revolutionary liberation struggles of the peoples of various countries."

The editorial pointed out that under these circumstances, the People's Republic of China, led by Chairman Mao Zedong, has become the vanguard and bastion of the world revolution. The editorial, on behalf of the oppressed people of Chile, "extends warm congratulations to the people and the Communist Party of China, who are guided by the thoughts of the greatest Marxist-Leninist of our time, Comrade Mao Zedong."

The newspaper, in another article, pointed out: "The socialist revolution in China, under the guidance of Mao Zedong and the great Marxism-Leninism-Mao Zedong Thought and the Chinese Communist Party, now marks a new era in the development of world Marxism-Leninism."

The article praised China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and the vanguard role played by the Chinese Communist Party in the struggle against modern revisionism centered on the Soviet revisionist leadership. The article said that China, standing on the genuine Marxist-Leninist position of proletarian internationalism, "points the way forward for the other socialist countries and the revolutionary peoples of the world."

The newspaper, in another article in the same issue, condemned the Soviet revisionist leadership's comprehensive betrayal of Marxism-Leninism in domestic and international policies.

The newspaper expressed the belief: "The Soviet working class will certainly rise up against the usurpers of the Soviet power and re-establish proletarian dictatorship in their own country."

### 智利《斯巴达克报》赞扬中国为捍卫马列主义作出巨大贡献  毛主席领导的中国是世界革命的先锋   苏修彻底背叛马列主义成为各国人民革命解放斗争的叛徒

1968-01-12
第6版()
专栏：

    智利《斯巴达克报》赞扬中国为捍卫马列主义作出巨大贡献
    毛主席领导的中国是世界革命的先锋
    苏修彻底背叛马列主义成为各国人民革命解放斗争的叛徒
    新华社讯　圣地亚哥消息：智利革命共产党机关报《斯巴达克报》在最近发表社论和文章，赞扬毛主席领导下的中国已经成为促进世界革命的坚强堡垒，并且谴责苏修领导集团对十月革命的背叛。
    这家报纸在一篇社论中指出，在一九四九年取得胜利的中国革命和苏联十月社会主义革命“都是当代发生的对人类具有最重要意义的大事。这两个革命是马克思和恩格斯的两位最杰出的学生——列宁和毛泽东创造性地运用科学社会主义的胜利”。
    社论说：但是，苏联和中国现在“却走向了根本不同的方向”。
    社论指出：在苏联，窃据政权的一批特权阶层的代表人物背叛了马克思列宁主义的根本原则，在这个国家加速复辟资本主义和促进帝国主义的渗透。而中国，却在毛泽东主席的领导下，正在通过无产阶级文化大革命，清除一小撮反革命修正主义分子，并锻炼走向共产主义的一代新人。
    社论说：对于拉丁美洲各国人民的革命前途说来，苏联和中国“所走的这种相反的方向不是无关紧要的”。它指出：“在伟大列宁的祖国篡夺政权的叛徒们，利用俄国无产阶级通过斗争树立起威信的这个崇高讲台，鼓吹变节和同帝国主义合作，散布虚伪的和平掌握政权的合法道路的幻想，总之，背叛马克思列宁主义的教导，遏制各国人民的革命解放斗争。”
    社论指出，在这种情况下，毛泽东主席领导下的中华人民共和国已经成为世界革命的先锋队和堡垒。社论以智利被剥削人民的名义，“向在当代最伟大的马克思列宁主义者毛泽东同志的思想指导下的中国人民和中国共产党，表示热烈的祝贺”。
    这家报纸在另一篇文章中指出：“中国的社会主义革命在毛泽东和伟大的马克思列宁主义——毛泽东思想以及中国共产党的指引下，现在标志着世界马克思列宁主义发展的一个新时代。”
    文章赞扬了中国的无产阶级文化大革命和中国共产党在反对以苏修领导集团为中心的现代修正主义的斗争中所起的先锋作用。文章说，中国站在无产阶级国际主义的真正马列主义立场上，
  “给其它社会主义国家和全世界的革命人民指出了前进的道路”。
    这家报纸在同期的另一篇文章中谴责了苏修领导集团在国内、国际政策方面对马克思列宁主义的全面背叛。
    这家报纸表示确信：“苏联劳动阶级一定会站起来反对苏联政权的篡夺者，在自己的国家重新建立无产阶级专政。”
