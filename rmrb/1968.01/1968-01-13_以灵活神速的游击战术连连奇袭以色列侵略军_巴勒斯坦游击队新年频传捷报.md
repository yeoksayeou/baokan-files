Title: Palestinian Guerrillas Frequently Report Victories in New Year with Flexible and Swift Guerrilla Tactics to Repeatedly Ambush Israeli Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1968-01-13
Edition: 5

Palestinian guerrillas have intensified their attacks on Israeli aggressors as the new year begins. Using flexible and swift guerrilla tactics, they have repeatedly ambushed Israeli military camps, achieving significant victories.

According to a battle report issued by the Palestinian underground nationalist organization "Fatah" on January 5, on the night of January 2, a Palestinian guerrilla unit used mortars to shell the Israeli military camp at Kfar Ruppin. The shells directly hit the enemy command center, ammunition depot, power station, and three military barracks. Simultaneously, a Palestinian "Storm" commando unit attacked the Israeli aggressors' headquarters in the Malakh area north of the Besan Valley, annihilating the entire guard unit, including a lieutenant.

On the same day, another "Storm" commando unit attacked the Israeli aggressors' camp at Ein Yahav in the Negev, killing and wounding fifteen enemy soldiers and causing severe damage to the enemy camp. This commando unit safely retreated after achieving victory.

At the end of last year, Palestinian guerrillas had repeatedly launched surprise attacks on Israeli aggressors, striking fear into the hearts of the enemy. According to "Fatah's" battle report, a "Storm" commando unit successfully blew up the bridge connecting Beersheba and Beit Jibrin on the night of December 18. The next night, they planted two landmines on the road leading to the enemy's Handiya camp in the Beersheba area, destroying two enemy vehicles and killing and wounding many enemy soldiers.

On the night of December 31, a Palestinian guerrilla patrol unit attacked an enemy armored convoy north of Jericho and engaged in fierce combat with enemy reinforcements. Another guerrilla unit annihilated two enemy ambush units north of Besan and seized their weapons.

According to a report from Cairo, Palestinian guerrillas reported combat victories at the beginning of the new year. They attacked Israeli aggressors in the sparsely populated southern half of the region—Negev Desert and the port of Eilat on the Red Sea's Gulf of Aqaba—and achieved new victories.

According to Cairo newspapers, on January 3, Arab guerrillas launched two attacks on Israeli occupying forces in the Negev region, killing two and wounding one.

On January 4, Palestinian guerrillas blew up part of the irrigation facilities in a farm in the Jordan Valley and detonated two landmines near the Yarmouk River in the border area between Jordan and Israel.

On January 5, the guerrillas completely destroyed a water pumping station in the Tabariyas region near the border with Lebanon. On the same night, the guerrillas appeared for the first time near the port of Eilat and fired rockets at a power plant three kilometers away from the port.

According to a battle report issued by the Palestinian underground nationalist organization "Fatah" on January 8, Palestinian guerrillas have successfully attacked Israeli aggressors in the occupied areas in recent days, achieving new victories.

The report stated that the guerrilla mortar units shelled the Israeli aggressors' camp at Mozahim in the Besan Valley on the night of January 5, causing severe damage to the camp. A fuel station caught fire.

On the same night, a guerrilla unit blew up a major pumping station and reservoir near the Margaliot camp in the Manara area.

Another guerrilla unit blew up a water pump and other water facilities near the Ashdod-Yaakov camp in the Samak area with explosives and destroyed an armored vehicle carrying Israeli aggressors with a landmine.

On the same night, a guerrilla unit shelled the power station in the city of Eilat, cutting off the electricity supply to this camp.

### 以灵活神速的游击战术连连奇袭以色列侵略军  巴勒斯坦游击队新年频传捷报

1968-01-13
第5版()
专栏：

    以灵活神速的游击战术连连奇袭以色列侵略军
    巴勒斯坦游击队新年频传捷报
    据新华社大马士革电　巴勒斯坦游击队在新的一年开始的时候，加强了对以色列侵略者的袭击。他们以灵活神速的游击战术，连连奇袭以色列侵略军的营地，取得了很大的战果。
    据巴勒斯坦地下民族主义组织“法塔赫”五日发表的战报说，一月二日晚上，一支巴勒斯坦游击队用迫击炮轰击了克法鲁平的以色列军营地，炮弹直接命中了敌军指挥部、弹药库、发电站和三幢军人宿舍。同时，一支巴勒斯坦“暴风”突击队袭击了贝桑山谷以北的马利赫区的以色列侵略者总部，歼灭了全部警卫部队，其中包括一名中尉军官。
    同一天，另一支“暴风”突击队袭击了以色列侵略军在内格夫的艾因亚希夫营地，打死打伤敌军十五名，并使敌人营地遭受严重损失。这支突击队在取得胜利后，安全撤退。
    去年年底，巴勒斯坦游击队曾多次对以色列侵略军进行出其不意的袭击，打得敌人惊恐万状。据“法塔赫”的战报说，一支“暴风”突击队在去年十二月十八日晚上成功地爆炸了贯通贝尔塞巴和贝特吉布林的大桥之后，第二天晚上又在贝尔塞巴地区通向敌人的汉迪亚营地的公路上埋置了两颗地雷，炸毁了两辆敌人车辆，炸死炸伤车上许多名敌军士兵。
    去年十二月三十一日夜间，一支巴勒斯坦游击队的巡逻队在杰里科以北袭击了敌人的一支装甲车队，并且同敌人的增援部队展开激战。另一支游击队在贝桑以北歼灭了两支敌人的伏击部队，并缴获了他们的武器。
    据新华社开罗电　巴勒斯坦游击队在新年开始的时候，首传战斗喜讯。他们在被以色列侵占的人烟稀少的南半部地区——内格夫沙漠以及红海亚喀巴湾上的埃拉特港，袭击以色列侵略者，获得了新的战绩。
    据开罗报纸报道：一月三日，阿拉伯游击队对内格夫地区的以色列占领军发动了两次攻击，打死以色列占领军两名，打伤一名。
    四日，巴勒斯坦游击队炸毁约旦河谷农庄的一部分水利设施，并在约旦同以色列交界的雅穆克河附近地区爆炸了两颗地雷。
    五日，游击队彻底摧毁了以色列同黎巴嫩交界附近的太比里亚斯地区的一个扬水站。同一天夜里，游击队首次出现在埃拉特港附近，并向离这个港口三公里的发电厂发射了火箭炮。
    新华社大马士革电　据巴勒斯坦地下民族主义组织“法塔赫”八日发表的一项战报说，巴勒斯坦游击队在最近几天成功地袭击了以色列占领区的侵略者，取得了新的胜利。
    战报说，游击队的迫击炮部队在五日晚上炮击了以色列侵略者在贝桑山谷的毛兹海姆营地，使这个营地遭到严重破坏。一个燃料站着了火。
    在同一天晚上，一支游击队炸毁了马纳拉地区马尔加利约特营地附近的一个主要抽水机站和水库。
    另一支游击队在六日晚上用炸药炸毁了萨马克地区靠近阿什杜德—雅各布营地的抽水机及其他水利设施，还用地雷炸毁了载着以色列侵略者的一辆装甲车。
    同一天晚上，一支游击队炮击了伊拉特城的发电站，中断了以色列对这个营地的电力供应。
