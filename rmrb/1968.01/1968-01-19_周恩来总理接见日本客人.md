Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1968-01-19
Edition: 4

Premier Zhou Enlai today received Japanese guests visiting Beijing, including Japanese Diet members Shiyeniu Jiuman and Zhizhucun Yaozuo, a friendship delegation from Chiba Prefecture led by Fuyuan Fengcailang, and a study delegation of secretaries of Japanese Socialist Party Diet members led by Songben Kangxing. He had a friendly talk with them.

Present at the reception were Zhang Qiyue, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and other relevant officials.

Xinhua News Agency, January 16th: A delegation of six, led by Songben Kangxing, arrived in Beijing on the 16th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

Below is a photo taken on January 18th, showing Premier Zhou Enlai receiving Japanese Socialist Party Diet members Shiyeniu Jiuman and Zhizhucun Yaozuo, a friendship delegation from Chiba Prefecture led by Fuyuan Fengcailang, and a study delegation of secretaries of Japanese Socialist Party Diet members led by Songben Kangxing. The photo was taken by a Xinhua News Agency reporter.

### 周恩来总理接见日本客人

1968-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见日本客人
    新华社十八日讯　周恩来总理今天接见了正在北京访问的日本社会党国会议员石野久男、枝村要作，以藤原丰次郎为首的日本千叶县友好人士代表团，以松本康行为首的日本社会党国会议员秘书学习团，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若以及其他有关方面的负责人。
    新华社十六日讯　由松本康行率领的日本社会党国会议员秘书学习团一行六人，应中国人民外交学会邀请，于十六日到达北京。（附图片）
