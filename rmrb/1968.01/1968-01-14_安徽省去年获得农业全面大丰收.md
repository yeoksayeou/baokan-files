Title: Anhui Province Achieved a Full-Fledged Bumper Harvest in Agriculture Last Year
Author:
Date: 1968-01-14
Edition: 3

Anhui Province overcame a severe drought and achieved a full-fledged bumper harvest last year. The total grain and cotton production in the province increased on the basis of the previous year's bumper harvest, and the total oilseed production also reached the level of the previous year's bumper harvest. Tea, flue-cured tobacco, silkworm cocoons, large livestock, and pigs all saw significant increases compared to the previous year. In particular, the Huabei region achieved an unprecedented bumper harvest in the 18 years since liberation.

### 安徽省去年获得农业全面大丰收

1968-01-14
第3版()
专栏：

     安徽省去年获得农业全面大丰收
    安徽战胜了严重干旱，夺得去年的全面大丰收。全省粮棉总产量在前年丰收的基础上继续增产，油料总产量也达到前年丰收水平，茶叶、烤烟、蚕茧、大家畜、生猪都比前年有较大幅度增长。特别是淮北地区获得了解放十八年来空前未有的大丰收。
