Author: Chen Shixin
Date: 1968-01-01
Edition: 5

	Relying on Mao Zedong Thought to Make the Revolution
	Chen Shixin

	"The voyage of a great ship relies on the helmsman, making the revolution relies on Mao Zedong Thought." Vice Commander-in-Chief Lin wrote these words, and joy resounds through the three rivers and five ridges. Every front reports victories, and workers, peasants, soldiers, and students are full of spirit. The revolutionary situation is excellent, and seven hundred million people are enjoying a good spring!

	"The voyage of a great ship relies on the helmsman, making the revolution relies on Mao Zedong Thought." Vice Commander-in-Chief Lin wrote these words, and the response echoes across the five continents and four seas: This is an unshakable great truth, this is the action guideline for the proletariat, this is the summary of the history of world revolution, this is the direction of human social progress!

	"The voyage of a great ship relies on the helmsman, making the revolution relies on Mao Zedong Thought." Navigating through rapids, crossing dangerous shoals, and avoiding reefs all depend on Chairman Mao steering the ship! Piercing the fog, facing the headwinds, and splitting the waves, the most brilliant helmsman points the way. Fearless ships advance forward, always heading towards victory!

	"The voyage of a great ship relies on the helmsman, making the revolution relies on Mao Zedong Thought." Hundreds of millions of people cheer together, Vice Commander-in-Chief Lin is our model for learning! Step by step, we follow the helmsman Chairman Mao, riding the wind and breaking the waves without deviating from the course! Forever loyal to Mao Zedong Thought, singing the song of victory loudly!
