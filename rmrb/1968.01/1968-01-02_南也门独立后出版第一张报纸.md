Title: South Yemen Publishes First Newspaper After Independence
Author:
Date: 1968-01-02
Edition: 6

South Yemen's People's Republic published its first Arabic-language newspaper, the weekly "Revolutionary," on December 28, 1967, following its independence. The South Yemen government announced on January 8 that it had revoked the licenses of all newspapers operated by British colonialists and stipulated that no newspaper could resume publication without government approval.

### 南也门独立后出版第一张报纸

1968-01-02
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

    南也门独立后出版第一张报纸
    南也门人民共和国在一九六七年十二月二十八日出版了独立后的第一张阿拉伯文报纸《革命者》周刊。南也门政府在八日曾宣布吊销所有英国殖民者经营的报纸的执照，并规定任何一家报纸，未经政府允许，不得恢复出版。
