Title: Lao Patriotic Military and Civilians Achieve Major Victory in Brave Combat
Author:
Date: 1968-01-12
Edition: 5

Lao patriotic military and civilians have achieved major victories in their brave combat. In Xiengkhuang and Sam Neua, patriotic military and civilians annihilated over a hundred enemy troops. In Savannakhet and other provinces, patriotic military and civilians shot down eleven American planes.

	New Xinhua News Agency, Khamkeut, January 10th – According to a report from the Pathet Lao News Agency, on January 4th, the Lao People's Liberation Army attacked a rightist military camp in the Phalane region of Sam Neua Province. Preliminary reports indicate that seventy-three enemy troops were killed or captured. Additionally, a platoon of twenty-five soldiers surrendered to the patriotic forces.

	At the end of last December, the American puppet troops in the Phalane region were defeated by the patriotic military and civilians. The enemy, unwilling to accept defeat, sent a camp to the southern slopes of the Three Pagodas, ten kilometers south of Phalane, attempting a counterattack. The Lao patriotic military and civilians, taking advantage of the enemy's lack of footing, launched a surprise attack, resulting in another heavy defeat for the enemy.

	New Xinhua News Agency, Khamkeut, January 8th – According to a broadcast from the Pathet Lao Radio: On January 4th, patriotic military and civilians in Xiengkhuang Province achieved significant victories in their counterattack against the enemy's "sweeping" operations, annihilating seventy-eight enemy troops, including forty-two killed.

	The broadcast stated that on January 4th, the American imperialists and their puppets launched a fierce attack on the southern Nan Nern region of Xiengkhuang Province with a camp of troops. They immediately faced the brave counterattack of the patriotic military and civilians of Xiengkhuang Province. Under the heavy blows of the patriotic military and civilians of Xiengkhuang Province, the enemy suffered heavy casualties, and the remaining enemy troops fled in all directions.

	New Xinhua News Agency, Khamkeut, January 8th – According to a broadcast from the Pathet Lao Radio: Recently, patriotic military and civilians in Savannakhet Province shot down seven more American aggressor planes.

	New Xinhua News Agency, Khamkeut, January 11th – According to a broadcast from the Pathet Lao Radio: In November, the patriotic military and civilians of Xiengkhuang Province bravely counterattacked the invading American air pirates on the 4th, 5th, and 20th, shooting down three enemy planes.

	The military and civilians of Vientiane also shot down one American aggressor plane on November 1st last year.

### 老挝爱国军民英勇战斗取得重大战果  桑怒川圹爱国军民歼敌百余　沙湾拿吉等省爱国军民击落美机十一架

1968-01-12
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国军民英勇战斗取得重大战果
    桑怒川圹爱国军民歼敌百余　沙湾拿吉等省爱国军民击落美机十一架
    新华社康开十日电 据巴特寮通讯社报道，老挝人民解放军四日在桑怒省法隆地区袭击右派军队一个营。据初步消息，敌军有七十三人被打死和被俘虏。此外，一个包括二十五人的排向爱国军队投诚。
    去年十二月底，老挝的美国走狗军队在法隆地区被爱国军民打得一败涂地。不甘心失败的敌人又派遣了这个营到法隆以南十公里的三凯山麓，企图进行反扑。老挝爱国军民趁着敌军尚未站稳脚跟的时候，就给以突然袭击，使他们又一次遭到了惨重失败。
    新华社康开八日电据巴特寮电台广播：老挝川圹省爱国军民四日在反击敌人“扫荡”的战斗中，取得重大战果，共消灭敌人七十八名，其中打死四十二名。
    广播说，美帝国主义及其走狗四日出动一个营的兵力，向川圹省南部南嫩地区发动疯狂进攻，当即遭到川圹省爱国军民的英勇反击。在川圹省爱国军民的沉重打击下，敌人伤亡重大，残存敌人纷纷四处逃窜。
    据新华社康开电　据巴特寮电台八日广播：老挝沙湾拿吉省爱国军民最近又击落美国强盗飞机七架。
    新华社康开十一日电据巴特寮电台广播：老挝东方省爱国军民，在去年十一月四日、五日和二十日三天中，英勇反击入侵的美国空中强盗，击落敌机三架。
    万象省军民也在去年十一月一日击落美国强盗飞机一架。
