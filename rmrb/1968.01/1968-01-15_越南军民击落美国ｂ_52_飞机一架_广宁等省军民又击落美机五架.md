Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down One U.S. B-52 Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-01-15
Edition: 6

Vietnamese military and civilians shoot down one U.S. B-52 aircraft.

Military and civilians in Quang Ninh and other provinces shoot down five more U.S. aircraft.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 14 — It was announced here that the military and civilians of the Vinh Linh district in North Vietnam shot down one invading U.S. B-52 type aircraft on January 11.

	This is the sixth U.S. B-52 type aircraft shot down by the military and civilians of the Vinh Linh district since September 17 last year.

	The military and civilians of Quang Ninh province also shot down one U.S. aircraft on January 10.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 11 — It was announced here that the military and civilians of Ha Tinh province in North Vietnam shot down one U.S. aggressor aircraft on January 10, and the military and civilians of Nam Ha province shot down one U.S. aircraft on January 8. It was also announced that in addition to the two enemy aircraft already reported, the military and civilians of Nghe An province shot down two more enemy aircraft on January 1 and 2.

### 越南军民击落美国Ｂ—52飞机一架  广宁等省军民又击落美机五架

1968-01-15
第6版()
专栏：

    越南军民击落美国Ｂ—52飞机一架
    广宁等省军民又击落美机五架
    新华社河内十四日电　据这里公布，越南北方永灵区军民十一日击落入侵的美国Ｂ—52型飞机一架。
    这是永灵区军民自去年九月十七日以来击落的第六架美国Ｂ—52型飞机。
    广宁省军民十日也击落美机一架。
    新华社河内十一日电　据这里公布，越南北方河西省军民十日击落美国强盗飞机一架，南河省军民八日击落美机一架。据补充公布，义安省军民一日和二日除已报道的击落敌机两架外，还击落敌机两架。
