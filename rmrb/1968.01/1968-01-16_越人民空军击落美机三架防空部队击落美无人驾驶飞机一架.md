Title: Vietnamese People's Air Force Shoots Down Three U.S. Aircraft, Air Defense Forces Shoot Down One U.S. Unmanned Drone
Author:
Date: 1968-01-16
Edition: 6

	Vietnamese People's Air Force shoots down three U.S. aircraft, air defense forces shoot down one U.S. unmanned drone

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 15 — It was announced here that on the morning of the 15th, a U.S. unmanned reconnaissance aircraft invaded Hanoi's airspace and was immediately shot down by the local air defense forces.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 15 — It was announced here that on the 14th, the Vietnamese People's Air Force, with the support of ground fire, shot down three U.S. aggressor aircraft that had invaded the airspace of North Vietnam.

	On the 13th, eight elderly militia members from Lishui County in Quang Binh Province shot down one U.S. aircraft. On the same day, the military and people of Nghe An Province also shot down one U.S. aircraft.

### 越人民空军击落美机三架防空部队击落美无人驾驶飞机一架

1968-01-16
第6版()
专栏：

    越人民空军击落美机三架防空部队击落美无人驾驶飞机一架
    新华社河内十五日电　据这里公布，十五日早晨，一架美国无人驾驶侦察机侵入河内上空，当即被这里的防空部队击落。
    新华社河内十五日电　据这里公布，越南人民空军在地面火力配合下，十四日击落侵入越南北方领空的美国强盗飞机三架。
    广平省丽水县的八个老年民兵十三日打下美国飞机一架。同一天，义安省军民也击落美机一架。
