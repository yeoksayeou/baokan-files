Title: The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Holds an Evening Gala
Author:
Date: 1968-01-08
Edition: 5

To enthusiastically celebrate the great victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, personally initiated and led by our great leader Chairman Mao, and to welcome the year 1968, a year of struggle, the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries held an evening gala on the 2nd and 3rd to entertain foreign experts working in Beijing, and foreign trainees and students studying in Beijing.

President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Chu Tu-nan, and Vice President Chou Yi-ping, attended the gala.

During the gala, revolutionary artists from the China Acrobatic Troupe performed acrobatic acts they created during the Cultural Revolution, filled with revolutionary spirit, which were warmly received by the foreign friends.

### 我对外友协举行晚会  招待外国专家和实习生及留学生

1968-01-08
第5版()
专栏：

    我对外友协举行晚会
    招待外国专家和实习生及留学生
    新华社讯　为热烈欢呼我们伟大领袖毛主席亲自发动和领导的无产阶级文化大革命的伟大胜利，迎接战斗的一九六八年，对外友协在二日和三日举行晚会，招待在北京工作的外国专家和在北京的外国实习生及留学生。
    对外友协会长楚图南、副会长周一萍等，出席了晚会。
    晚会上，中国杂技团的革命文艺战士，演出了他们在文化大革命中创作的充满革命气息的杂技节目，受到外国朋友的热烈欢迎。
