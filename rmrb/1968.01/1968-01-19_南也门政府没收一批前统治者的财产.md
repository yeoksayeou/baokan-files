Title: The Government of South Yemen Seizes Assets of Former Rulers
Author:
Date: 1968-01-19
Edition: 5

According to a report from the New China News Agency, the government of the People's Democratic Republic of Yemen announced on December 26 last year a list of the first batch of assets seized from 123 former rulers of South Yemen.

It was announced that these assets were public property illegally seized during the colonial rule period, including land, palaces, houses, furniture, vehicles, horses, jewelry, and bank deposits.

A government spokesman said that the assets of these individuals were seized in accordance with a decree issued on December 11.

### 南也门政府没收一批前统治者的财产

1968-01-19
第5版()
专栏：

    南也门政府没收一批前统治者的财产
    据新华社讯　亚丁消息：南也门人民共和国政府去年十二月二十六日宣布没收第一批南也门前统治者一百二十三人的财产的名单。
    据宣布，这些财产是属于在殖民统治时期非法掠夺来的公共财产，包括土地、宫殿、房屋、家具、车辆、马匹、珠宝、银行存款等。
    政府发言人说，这些人的财产是根据十二月十一日的一项法令没收的。
