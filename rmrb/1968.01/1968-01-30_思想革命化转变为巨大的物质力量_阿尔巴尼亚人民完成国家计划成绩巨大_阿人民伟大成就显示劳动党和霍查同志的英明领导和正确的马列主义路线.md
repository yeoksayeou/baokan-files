Title: Ideological Revolution Transforms into a Great Material Force
Author:
Date: 1968-01-30
Edition: 6

Albania's people have achieved great success in completing the national plan. The great achievements of the Albanian people show the wise leadership of the Labor Party and Comrade Enver Hoxha and the correct Marxist-Leninist line.

	NEW YORK, January 27 — A report on the completion of the 1967 national plan, issued by the State Planning Commission's Statistics Bureau of Albania on January 25, pointed out that Albania's various economic sectors achieved excellent results in completing the national plan last year.

	The report said that 1967 was a year of great victories for the Albanian working class, laboring peasants, and intellectuals under the leadership of the Party. The historically significant Fifth Congress of the Party of Labor of Albania, Comrade Enver Hoxha's speech on February 6, 1967, the announcement by the Central Committee of the Party of Labor and the Council of Ministers on April 29, 1967, Comrade Enver Hoxha's historically significant report at the Fourth Congress of the Democratic Front, and other documents recently issued by the Party and the government have aroused unprecedented labor enthusiasm among the broad masses of the people, promoted the ideological revolutionization of all working people, and achieved excellent results in completing the national plan in various economic sectors.

	The report discussed the annual plan completion situation for major industrial products: crude oil 104 percent, fuel 101 percent, asphalt 107 percent, coal 101 percent, chrome ore 105 percent, copper ingots 103 percent, copper wire 101 percent, iron-nickel ore 101 percent, sulfuric acid 129 percent, caustic soda 167 percent, cement 103 percent, textiles 103 percent.

	The report said that the total industrial output value plan was completed at 103.5 percent, an increase of 12.8 percent compared to 1966. Compared to 1966, the power industry increased by 34 percent, the petroleum industry by 12 percent, the coal industry by 12 percent, asphalt by 7 percent, chrome by 6 percent, copper by 10 percent, iron-nickel by 34 percent, chemical products by 1.8 times, building materials by 29 percent, and the machinery industry by 23 percent.

	The report said that the broad masses of peasants also achieved great successes in 1967. As early as the first quarter of 1967, the mountain peasants of Albania responded enthusiastically to the call of the Fifth Congress of the Party of Labor and voluntarily established agricultural cooperatives, thus realizing agricultural collectivization.

	In 1967, the reclamation plan was overfulfilled by 21 percent. The area of crops sown increased by 6 percent compared to the previous year. Due to the establishment of the domestic chemical fertilizer industry, the amount of chemical fertilizers used in agriculture increased by approximately four times compared to 1966. Irrigation capacity completed the plan by 102 percent, an increase of 5 percent compared to 1966.

	The report said that in 1967, many construction projects were completed, including the Ferri nitrogen fertilizer plant, the Laci superphosphate plant, the Lushnj copper smelting plant, the Fier caustic soda plant, the Fushë-Krujë cement plant, and the Tirana grape processing plant.

	The report said that transportation, retail trade, exports, and other sectors all overfulfilled the plan.

	The report pointed out that the completion of the 1967 national plan is a solid foundation for the smooth implementation of the 1968 production plan (the third year of the Fourth Five-Year Plan). These achievements once again demonstrate the selfless labor of our working people, the iron-like unity of the people with the Party, and the wise leadership of the Party of Labor of Albania and the Central Committee led by Comrade Enver Hoxha, and the correct Marxist-Leninist line.

### 思想革命化转变为巨大的物质力量  阿尔巴尼亚人民完成国家计划成绩巨大  阿人民伟大成就显示劳动党和霍查同志的英明领导和正确的马列主义路线

1968-01-30
第6版()
专栏：

    思想革命化转变为巨大的物质力量
    阿尔巴尼亚人民完成国家计划成绩巨大
    阿人民伟大成就显示劳动党和霍查同志的英明领导和正确的马列主义路线
    新华社地拉那二十七日电　阿尔巴尼亚国家计划委员会统计局二十五日发表的一九六七年国家计划完成情况的公报指出，阿尔巴尼亚各个经济部门去年在完成国家计划方面取得了很好的成绩。
    公报说，一九六七年是阿尔巴尼亚工人阶级、劳动农民和人民的知识分子在党的领导下取得伟大胜利的一年。具有历史意义的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次代表大会，恩维尔·霍查同志一九六七年二月六日的讲话，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会和部长会议一九六七年四月二十九日发表的公告，恩维尔·霍查同志在民主阵线第四次代表大会上所作的具有历史意义的报告，以及党和政府最近发表的其他文件，在广大人民群众中激起了空前未有的劳动热情，促进了全体劳动人民的思想革命化，使各经济部门在完成国家计划方面取得了很好的成绩。
    公报谈到了主要工业产品的全年计划完成情况：原油百分之一百零四，燃料百分之一百零一，沥青百分之一百零七，煤炭百分之一百零一，铬矿百分之一百零五，泡铜百分之一百零三，铜线百分之一百零一，铁镍矿百分之一百零一，硫酸百分之一百二十九，烧碱百分之一百六十七，水泥百分之一百零三，纺织品百分之一百零三。
    公报说，工业生产总值计划完成了百分之一百零三点五，与一九六六年相比，增长了百分之十二点八。同一九六六年相比，电力工业增长了百分之三十四，石油工业百分之十二，煤炭工业百分之十二，沥青百分之七，铬百分之六，铜百分之十，铁镍百分之三十四，化学制品一点八倍，建筑材料百分之二十九，机械工业百分之二十三。
    公报说，广大农民在一九六七年也取得了巨大成就。一九六七年第一季度刚开始，阿尔巴尼亚山区农民就热烈响应劳动党第五次代表大会的号召，自愿建立了农业合作社，从而实现了农业集体化。
    一九六七年，垦荒计划超额完成了百分之二十一。农作物的播种面积比去年扩大了百分之六。由于本国化学肥料工业的建立，农用化肥比一九六六年增加了约四倍。灌溉能力完成计划的一百零二，比一九六六年增加百分之五。
    公报说，在一九六七年，不少建设工程已经完工，其中包括费里氮肥厂，拉奇过磷酸盐厂，卢比克炼铜厂，发罗那烧碱厂，富什—克鲁亚水泥厂，地拉那葡萄加工厂等。
    公报说，交通运输、商品零售、出口等方面，都超额完成了计划。
    公报指出，完成一九六七年的国家计划是顺利地执行一九六八年（第四个五年计划的第三年）生产计划的牢固基础。这些成果再一次显示了我国劳动人民的忘我劳动，人民同党的钢铁般的团结，显示了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和以霍查同志为首的党中央委员会的英明领导和正确的马克思列宁主义路线。
