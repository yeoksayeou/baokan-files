Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Four More U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-01-22
Edition: 6

Vietnamese military and civilians in the provinces of Ninh Binh and Nghe An shot down two invading U.S. aircraft on January 19. On January 18, military and civilians in the province of Quang Binh also shot down two U.S. aircraft.

### 越南军民又击落美机四架

1968-01-22
第6版()
专栏：

    越南军民又击落美机四架
    新华社河内二十一日电　越南北方宁平、永福两省军民，十九日击落入侵的美国飞机两架。河静省军民，十八日也曾击落美机两架。
