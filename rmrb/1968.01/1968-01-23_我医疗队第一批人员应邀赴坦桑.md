Title: The First Batch of Our Medical Team Invited to Tanzania
Author:
Date: 1968-01-23
Edition: 6

At the invitation of the United Republic of Tanzania, the first batch of personnel from our medical team left Beijing on the 18th to go to Tanzania to engage in medical work. Relevant officials from our department, as well as Tanzania's Ambassador to China, Mwaluko, were at the airport to see them off.

### 我医疗队第一批人员应邀赴坦桑

1968-01-23
第6版()
专栏：

    我医疗队第一批人员应邀赴坦桑
    新华社十九日讯　应坦桑尼亚联合共和国的邀请，我国医疗队第一批人员在十八日离开北京，前往坦桑尼亚进行医疗工作。我有关部门的负责人，以及坦桑尼亚驻华大使姆瓦卢科等到机场欢送。
