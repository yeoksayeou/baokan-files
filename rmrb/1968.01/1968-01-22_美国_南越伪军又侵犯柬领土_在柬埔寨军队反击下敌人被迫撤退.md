U.S.—South Vietnamese Puppet Forces Again Invade Cambodian Territory

In the face of Cambodian military counterattacks, the enemy was forced to retreat

	NEW DELHI, Jan. 21 (Xinhua) — U.S.—South Vietnamese puppet forces on Jan. 18 again invaded and savagely bombed Cambodian territory, attacking a sentry post in the Peyomontaya area of the province of Pursat, killing three Cambodians and wounding one. Under the counterattack of the Cambodian army, the enemy was forced to retreat. This incident once again exposed the aggressive nature of U.S. imperialism.

	The Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Ministry of Information of the Kingdom of Cambodia on Jan. 20 invited diplomats from various countries stationed in Cambodia, Cambodian journalists, and foreign journalists stationed in Cambodia to the scene to view the evidence of the U.S.—South Vietnamese puppet forces' aggression.

	According to the Cambodian military authorities, on the morning of Jan. 18, U.S.—South Vietnamese puppet forces invaded Cambodian territory and attacked the aforementioned sentry post. Under the counterattack of the Cambodian army, the enemy was forced to retreat and used aircraft to bomb and strafe Cambodian territory to cover the enemy's escape. The enemy, while retreating, dragged away about 15 enemy corpses, including two Americans, and several wounded soldiers.

	In this battle, the Cambodian army captured two enemy radio sets and two carbines.

### 美国—南越伪军又侵犯柬领土  在柬埔寨军队反击下敌人被迫撤退

1968-01-22
第6版()
专栏：

    美国—南越伪军又侵犯柬领土
    在柬埔寨军队反击下敌人被迫撤退
    新华社金边二十一日电　美国—南越伪军，十八日又侵入和野蛮轰炸柬埔寨领土，袭击波罗勉省的佩昂蒙特亚哨所，使柬埔寨方面三人死亡，一人受伤。在柬埔寨军队反击下，敌人被迫撤退。这个事件又一次暴露了美帝国主义的侵略本性。
    柬埔寨王国政府外交大臣和新闻大臣，二十日就此事件邀请各国驻柬埔寨的外交使节、柬埔寨新闻界和驻柬埔寨的外国记者到现场观看了美国—南越伪军的侵略罪证。
    据柬埔寨军事当局揭露，在十八日上午五时，美国—南越伪军侵入了柬埔寨领土，袭击了上述哨所。在柬埔寨军队的反击下，敌人被迫撤退，并出动飞机轰炸和扫射柬埔寨领土，以掩护敌军逃跑。敌人在逃跑时拖走了包括两名美国人在内的约十五具敌军尸体和几名受伤者。
    柬埔寨军队在这次战斗中，缴获了敌人两部无线电机和两支卡宾枪。
