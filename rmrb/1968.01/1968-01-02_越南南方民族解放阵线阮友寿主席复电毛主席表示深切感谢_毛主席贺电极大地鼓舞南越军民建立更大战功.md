Title: Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam Expresses Heartfelt Thanks to Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1968-01-02
Edition: 2

Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam has sent a telegram to Chairman Mao expressing heartfelt thanks. Chairman Mao's congratulatory message has greatly inspired the military and people of South Vietnam to achieve greater victories.

Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho sent a telegram to Premier Zhou expressing gratitude for the enthusiastic celebration by the Chinese people of the seventh anniversary of the founding of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam.

	New China News Agency, January 1st: Our beloved great leader, Chairman Mao Zedong, has received a telegram from Nguyen Huu Tho, Chairman of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, expressing thanks for Chairman Mao's congratulatory message on the seventh anniversary of the founding of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam. The full text of Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho's telegram is as follows:

	To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chairman Mao Zedong,
	Respected Chairman:

	Your congratulatory message on the seventh anniversary of the founding of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam has greatly inspired the people and armed forces of South Vietnam to advance and achieve new and greater victories.

	Under the leadership of the National Liberation Front, the military and people of South Vietnam deeply understand that "the 700 million people of China are the steadfast rear guard of the Vietnamese people, and the vast territory of China is the reliable rear base of the Vietnamese people." We deeply understand that our victories are inseparable from the great and effective sincere support and assistance of the fraternal People's Republic of China.

	On behalf of the people, armed forces, and the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, and on my own behalf, I express my heartfelt and deep gratitude to you, the Communist Party of China, the Chinese government, and the fraternal Chinese people for your special care and valuable support for our just war.

	I wish you good health forever.

	Chairman of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam
	Nguyen Huu Tho
	December 26, 1967, in South Vietnam

	New China News Agency, January 1st: Premier Zhou Enlai has received a telegram from Nguyen Huu Tho, Chairman of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, expressing thanks for the enthusiastic celebration by the Chinese people of the seventh anniversary of the founding of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam.

	The telegram states: "When our Central Committee learned that various places in China had organized grand and extensive celebrations for the seventh anniversary of the founding of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, and that the delegation of the people of South Vietnam led by Nguyen Van Luong had the honor of being received by Chairman Mao, we were greatly encouraged. These precious supports once again eloquently demonstrate the steadfast and resolute position of the fraternal 700 million people of China in firmly supporting the people of South Vietnam to thoroughly defeat the U.S. aggressors and their lackeys."

	The telegram states: "On behalf of the people of South Vietnam, the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front, and myself, I express sincere thanks to Your Excellency and, through you, to the great Communist Party of China led by the beloved Chairman Mao, the Chinese government, and the Chinese people, for the precious assistance they have given to the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam and the people of South Vietnam who are waging a sacred struggle against American aggression for national salvation."

### 越南南方民族解放阵线阮友寿主席复电毛主席表示深切感谢  毛主席贺电极大地鼓舞南越军民建立更大战功

1968-01-02
第2版()
专栏：

    越南南方民族解放阵线阮友寿主席复电毛主席表示深切感谢
    毛主席贺电极大地鼓舞南越军民建立更大战功
    阮友寿主席致电周总理感谢中国人民热烈庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立七周年
    新华社一日讯　我们敬爱的伟大领袖毛泽东主席，收到了越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团主席阮友寿为感谢毛主席电贺越南南方民族解放阵线成立七周年发来的电报。阮友寿主席电报的全文如下：北京中国共产党中央委员会毛泽东主席敬爱的主席：
    主席祝贺越南南方民族解放阵线成立七周年的贺电，已经和正在极大地鼓舞着越南南方人民和人民解放武装力量乘胜前进，去建立新的更加巨大的战功。
    越南南方军民在民族解放阵线的领导下，深刻地认识到：“七亿中国人民是越南人民的坚强后盾，辽阔的中国领土是越南人民的可靠后方。”深刻地认识到我们的胜利是同兄弟的中华人民共和国的巨大而有效的真诚支持和援助分不开的。
    我谨代表越南南方人民、人民解放武装力量、民族解放阵线中央委员会和我本人，衷心地和深切地感谢主席、中国共产党、中国政府和兄弟的中国人民，对我们的正义战争所给予的特别关怀和宝贵支援。
    祝阁下身体永远永远健康。
    越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团主席
                                    阮友寿律师
          一九六七年十二月二十六日　于越南南方
    新华社一日讯　周恩来总理收到了越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团主席阮友寿为感谢中国人民热烈庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立七周年发来的电报。
    电报说：“我们民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团当获悉中国各地隆重和广泛地组织越南南方民族解放阵线成立七周年庆祝活动，以及以阮春龙为首的越南南方人民代表团光荣地得到毛主席接见等消息，感到非常振奋。”“这些珍贵的支持，再次雄辩地表明了兄弟的七亿中国人民坚决大力支援越南南方人民彻底打败美国侵略军及其走狗的始终如一的立场。”
    电报说：“我谨代表越南南方人民、民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团和我本人对总理阁下表示真诚的感谢，并通过阁下感谢以敬爱的毛主席为首的伟大的中国共产党、中国政府和中国人民，对正在进行抗美救国神圣抗战的越南南方民族解放阵线和越南南方人民所给予的珍贵的援助。”
