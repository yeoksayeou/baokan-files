Title: March Forward to Achieve Complete Victory
Author:
Date: 1968-01-05
Edition: 5

In the Vietnam South National Liberation Front's seventh anniversary, the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front decided to award the Fatherland Medal and various levels of Bronze Wall Medals to many units and regions that have made great contributions in the war against American aggression for national salvation.

The Central Committee of the National Liberation Front decided to award the Fatherland Medal to various people's liberation armed forces in Vietnam South.

The following regions and their people's armed forces were awarded the first-class Bronze Wall Medal: Quang Ngai, Quang Nam, and Quang Tri provinces in the Central Region; Phu Yen province in the South Central Region; Long An, My Tho, and Ben Tre provinces in the Southwestern Region; Tay Ninh and Chau Doc provinces in the Mekong Delta; Quang Tri-Thu Thien region; Phu Yen province in the Southeastern Region; Gia Dinh province and District 1 in the Saigon-Cholon region.

The following regions and their people's armed forces were awarded the second-class Bronze Wall Medal: Thua Thien, Quang Nam, Kontum, and Pleiku provinces in the Central Region; Ninh Thuan province in the South Central Region; Binh Dinh province in the Southwestern Region; An Giang and Kien Giang provinces in the Mekong Delta; Binh Duong and Long An provinces in the Southeastern Region.

The following regions and their people's armed forces were awarded the third-class Bronze Wall Medal: Quang Nam and Da Nang provinces in the Central Region; Lam Dong, Phu Yen, and Phu Quoc provinces in the South Central Region; An Giang and Kien Giang provinces in the Southwestern Region; Binh Duong and Long An provinces in the Southeastern Region.

The Central Committee of the National Liberation Front also decided to award the first-class, second-class, and third-class Bronze Wall Medals and the first-class and third-class Liberation Medals to the people of Vietnam South who have made outstanding contributions in people's guerrilla warfare, political struggles, military supplies, maintaining production, providing manpower and materials to the front, and in cultural and educational movements.

The editorial of the "Liberation Army News," the official organ of the Vietnam South People's Liberation Armed Forces, recently stated that the National Liberation Front's decision to award the Fatherland Medal to the people's liberation armed forces is the highest praise for the iron will of the entire army and people to dare to fight and determine to defeat the brutal imperialist leader, the American aggressors. The editorial concluded by calling on all kinds of people's liberation armed forces to march forward with iron will and extraordinary efforts, to overwhelm the enemy, and to achieve complete victory.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 3, the Vietnam "People's Army Newspaper" published an editorial on January 2, enthusiastically welcoming the recent awards of the Fatherland Medal and Bronze Wall Medal by the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front to the people's liberation armed forces and the military and people of various regions in Vietnam South.

The editorial concluded by saying that the anti-American national salvation cause of the entire Vietnamese people is entering a fierce period. The obstinate American aggressors are desperately struggling to their death. The military and people of the entire country are firmly grasping the advantage of victory. Chairman Ho Chi Minh's battle call at the beginning of the year has inspired the Vietnamese people. The military and people of the North are determined to stand shoulder to shoulder with the military and people of the South to push the anti-American national salvation cause to complete victory.

### 勇猛前进夺取彻底胜利  越南南方民族解放阵线授予在抗美救国战争中立功单位和地区勋章

1968-01-05
第5版()
专栏：

    勇猛前进夺取彻底胜利
    越南南方民族解放阵线授予在抗美救国战争中立功单位和地区勋章
    据新华社河内二日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：在越南南方民族解放阵线成立七周年的时候，民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团决定把祖国勋章和各级铜墙勋章授予在抗美救国战争中立下巨大功勋的很多单位和地区。民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团决定：授予越南南方各种人民解放武装力量以祖国勋章。
    授予以下地区军民一级铜墙勋章：中部中区广信省、广义省和广南省；中部南区平顺省；南部中区隆安省、美萩省和槟椥省；南部西区茶荣省和芹苴省；广治—承天地区；南部东区平阳省；西贡—嘉定地区的嘉定省和D一区。
    授予以下地区军民二级铜墙勋章：中部中区富安省、平定省、昆嵩省和嘉莱省；中部南区宁顺省；南部中区建丰省；南部西区金瓯省、朔庄省、永隆省和迪石省；南部东区西宁省和福隆省。
    授予以下地区军民三级铜墙勋章：中部中区庆和省和多乐省；中部南区林同省、宣德省和平龙省；南部中区安江省和建祥省；南部东区巴地省和边和省。
    越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团还决定对在人民游击战争、政治斗争、兵运工作、坚持生产和保卫生产、为前线提供人力物力以及在文化教育等运动中立下战功的越南南方人民，分别授予一级、二级和三级铜墙勋章以及一级、三级解放勋章。
    越南南方人民解放武装力量指挥部机关报《解放军报》，最近就越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团决定授予人民解放武装力量以祖国勋章发表社论说，民族解放阵线授予人民解放武装力量以祖国勋章，是对敢于战斗和决心战胜残暴的帝国主义头子美国强盗的全军全民的钢铁般的意志的最热烈的表扬。社论最后号召各种人民解放武装力量，以钢铁般的决心和非凡的努力，以压倒敌人的气势，勇敢前进，夺取彻底的胜利。
    据新华社河内三日电　越南《人民军队报》二日发表社论，热烈欢呼越南南方人民解放武装力量和越南南方各地军民最近荣获南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团授予的祖国勋章和铜墙勋章。
    社论最后说，越南全国人民的抗美救国事业正进入激烈的时期，顽固的美国强盗正在疯狂地进行垂死的挣扎。越南全国军民正牢牢地掌握胜利的优势。胡主席在年初发出的战斗号召鼓舞了越南人民。越南北方军民决心同南方军民并肩一起，把抗美救国事业推向彻底胜利。
