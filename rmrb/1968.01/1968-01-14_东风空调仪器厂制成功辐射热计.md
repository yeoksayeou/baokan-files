Title: Dongfeng Air Conditioning Instrument Factory Successfully Produces Radiation Thermometer
Author:
Date: 1968-01-14
Edition: 3

The workers of the Dongfeng Air Conditioning Instrument Factory in Tianjin, under the guidance of the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, have successfully manufactured a radiation thermometer using simple equipment. The product is now being mass-produced. The thermometer has been evaluated for stability and sensitivity, both of which have reached a high level, and it is also cost-effective.

### 东风空调仪器厂制成功辐射热计

1968-01-14
第3版()
专栏：

    东风空调仪器厂制成功辐射热计
    天津市东风空调仪器厂革命职工高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，用简陋的设备制造成功辐射热计，并成批投入生产。经鉴定，产品稳定性、灵敏度都达到较高水平，而且成本低。
                                （据新华社）
