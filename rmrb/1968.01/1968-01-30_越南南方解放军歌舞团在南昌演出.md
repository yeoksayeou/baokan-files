Title: The South Vietnamese Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe Performs in Nanchang
Author:
Date: 1968-01-30
Edition: 6

The South Vietnamese Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe, hailing from the frontlines of the anti-American struggle, held their first performance in Nanchang this evening. Their outstanding performance was met with enthusiastic applause.

Tonight, the theater was filled with the fervent atmosphere of the fighting unity between the peoples of China and Vietnam.

The South Vietnamese comrades sang the song "The East is Red," praising the great leader of the world's people, Chairman Mao, in Chinese with immense enthusiasm. They also sang and played "Long Live Chairman Mao," "Sailing the Seas Depends on the Helmsman," and songs composed with quotations from Chairman Mao and his poems, fully expressing the boundless love of the South Vietnamese people for the great leader Mao Zedong and the great Mao Zedong Thought. The South Vietnamese cultural warriors also sang songs praising the great leader of the Vietnamese people, President Ho Chi Minh, extolling the fighting friendship between the peoples of China and Vietnam, and reflecting the heroic deeds of the South Vietnamese military and civilians in their struggle against American imperialism and for national salvation. The performance of the song and dance troupe was highly praised by the audience.

Leaders of the Jiangxi Revolutionary Committee and the Jiangxi Military Region, including Cheng Shiqing, Yang Dongliang, and Wen Daohong, attended the performance. After the performance, Cheng Shiqing, on behalf of the Jiangxi Revolutionary Committee and the Jiangxi Military Region, presented a banner to the troupe that read, "Victory will surely belong to the heroic Vietnamese people."

Nguyen Van Quang, head of the Permanent Representative Office of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China, arrived in Nanchang by plane from Shanghai today and attended the performance in the evening.

According to Xinhua News Agency in Nanchang on January 27, the South Vietnamese Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe, led by Director Nguyen Thuan Hung, arrived in Nanchang, the hero city, this morning from Shanghai for a visit and performance. They were warmly welcomed by the soldiers and officers of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Jiangxi Province, the Red Guards, and revolutionary cultural workers from the province and city.

Leaders of the Jiangxi Revolutionary Committee, including Wen Daohong, the deputy political commissar of the Jiangxi Military Region, went to the station to welcome them warmly.

### 越南南方解放军歌舞团在南昌演出

1968-01-30
第6版()
专栏：

    越南南方解放军歌舞团在南昌演出
    据新华社南昌二十八日电　来自反美斗争最前线的英雄的越南南方解放军歌舞团，今晚在南昌举行首次演出。他们的精采演出受到热烈欢迎。
    今晚，剧场里洋溢着中越两国人民战斗团结的热烈气氛。
    越南南方战友们用中国话，以极大的热情演唱了歌颂世界人民伟大领袖毛主席的歌曲《东方红》，歌唱和演奏了《万岁！毛主席》、《大海航行靠舵手》，以及用毛主席语录和诗词谱写的歌曲，充分表达越南南方人民对伟大领袖毛主席和伟大的毛泽东思想的无限热爱。越南南方的文艺战士们还演唱了歌颂越南人民的伟大领袖胡志明主席，颂扬中越两国人民战斗友谊的歌曲，以及反映南方军民反美救国斗争英勇事迹的歌舞。歌舞团的演出受到了观众的热烈赞扬。
    江西省革命委员会、江西省军区负责人程世清、杨栋梁、文道宏等观看了演出。演出结束后，程世清代表江西省革命委员会、江西省军区向歌舞团赠送了“胜利一定属于英雄的越南人民”的锦旗。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长阮文广，今天由上海乘飞机到达南昌，晚上陪同观看了演出。
    据新华社南昌二十七日电　来自反美斗争最前线的越南南方解放军歌舞团，在团长阮春鸿率领下，今天上午由上海到达英雄城市南昌，进行访问演出，受到驻江西省人民解放军指战员、红卫兵革命小将和省、市革命文艺工作者的热烈欢迎。
    江西省革命委员会常委、江西省军区副政治委员文道宏等前往车站热烈欢迎。
