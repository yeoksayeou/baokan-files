Title: Celebrating the Ninth Anniversary of Cuba's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1968-01-03
Edition: 5

The Cuban chargé d'affaires at the Cuban Embassy in China, Garcia, held a reception tonight to celebrate the ninth anniversary of Cuba's liberation. Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman Liu Ningyi, and relevant officials attended the reception upon invitation. Garcia and Vice Premier Chen Yi proposed a toast at the reception, toasting to the friendship between the peoples of China and Cuba.

Envoys from various countries to China also attended the reception.

### 庆祝古巴解放九周年  古巴驻华临时代办举行招待会

1968-01-03
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝古巴解放九周年
    古巴驻华临时代办举行招待会
    新华社二日讯　古巴驻中国大使馆临时代办加西亚今晚举行招待会，庆祝古巴解放九周年。陈毅副总理、刘宁一副委员长和有关方面的负责人，应邀出席了招待会。加西亚临时代办和陈毅副总理在招待会上祝酒，提议为中古两国人民的友谊干杯。
    各国驻中国的使节出席了招待会。
