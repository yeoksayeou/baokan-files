Title: Zambian President Receives Chinese Railway and Road Survey Teams
Author:
Date: 1968-01-21
Edition: 6

Lusaka, January 20 (Xinhua) — Zambian President Kenneth Kaunda received the entire delegation of the Chinese railway and road survey teams, who are currently conducting surveys in Zambia, on January 19. President Kaunda had a friendly and cordial conversation with the Chinese experts.

President Kaunda first extended greetings to Chairman Mao, the great leader of the Chinese people. President Kaunda also expressed his gratitude to Chairman Mao, Premier Zhou, and the great Chinese people for the warm reception he received during his visit to China. He went on to say that the people of Asia and Africa have all experienced the oppression of imperialism and colonialism, and this shared experience has brought Zambia and China together.

Present at the reception were Zambian Foreign Ministry Protocol Department Director Nkumbula and Acting Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Zambia, Li Zhenhe.

### 赞比亚总统接见我铁路和公路考察组

1968-01-21
第6版()
专栏：

    赞比亚总统接见我铁路和公路考察组
    新华社二十日讯　卢萨卡消息：赞比亚总统卡翁达十九日接见了正在赞比亚进行考察工作的中国铁路考察组和公路考察组全体成员。赞比亚总统同中国专家们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    卡翁达总统首先向中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席表示问候。卡翁达总统还对他在访问中国期间受到的热情接待，向毛主席和周总理以及伟大的中国人民表示感谢。他接着说，亚非人民都经历帝国主义和殖民主义的压迫，这种共同的经历使赞比亚和中国联系在一起。
    接见时在座的有赞比亚外交部礼宾司长尼伦达和中国驻赞比亚大使馆临时代办李振河。
