Title: The People's Revolution Under the Guidance of Mao Zedong Thought is the Locomotive of Historical Progress
Author:
Date: 1968-01-07
Edition: 3

The people's revolution under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought is the locomotive of historical progress. In the unprecedented Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, hundreds of millions of people have further grasped Mao Zedong Thought, generating immense material force to transform heaven and earth, and powerfully promoting the rapid development of socialist undertakings. This is a corner of Nanxingqiao Station of Hangzhou Railway Bureau, which has excellently completed its freight and passenger transport tasks, presenting a bustling scene of revolution and production.

    毛泽东思想指引下的人民革命是历史前进的火车头。在史无前例的无产阶级文化大革命中，亿万人民群众进一步掌握了毛泽东思想，产生了改天换地的强大物质力量，有力地促进了社会主义事业迅猛发展。这是出色完成货运、客运任务的杭州铁路分局南星桥车站一角，呈现着一片热气腾腾的革命和生产景象。
