Title: Ethiopian Students Hold Anti-American Protest
Author:
Date: 1968-01-09
Edition: 6

Hundreds of Ethiopian students held an anti-American protest on the 6th, opposing the activities of U.S. Vice President Hubert Humphrey in Ethiopia.

Humphrey is currently promoting U.S. aggressive policies across various parts of Africa. On the afternoon of the 5th, he arrived in Ethiopia from Zambia for a "visit." According to the plan, he was to give a speech at Haile Selassie I University on the 6th. Upon learning this news, students at the university immediately organized an anti-American protest to "welcome" this war-mongering demon.

Due to the students' strong anti-American protest, Humphrey was too frightened to go to the university.

### 埃塞俄比亚大学生举行反美示威

1968-01-09
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

    埃塞俄比亚大学生举行反美示威
    数百名埃塞俄比亚大学生六日举行反美示威，抗议美国副总统汉弗莱到埃塞俄比亚活动。
    汉弗莱正在非洲各地推销美国的侵略政策。五日下午他从赞比亚到达埃塞俄比亚“访问”。根据计划，六日这天他要到海尔·塞拉西一世大学作演讲。这个大学的学生得知这个消息后，立即举行反美示威，以“迎接”这个战争瘟神。
    由于学生的强烈的反美示威，汉弗莱被吓得不敢去这个大学。
