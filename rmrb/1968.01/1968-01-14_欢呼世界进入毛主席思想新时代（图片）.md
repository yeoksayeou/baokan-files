Title: Hail the World Entering the New Era of Chairman Mao's Thought
Author:
Date: 1968-01-14
Edition: 6

Today, the world has entered a new revolutionary era under the great banner of Mao Zedong Thought. Chairman Mao's precious works have spread throughout China, and the brilliance of Mao Zedong Thought illuminates the world! Revolutionary cultural workers from Albania, during their visit to China, joyfully received the red-covered treasure—the Albanian-language version of "Quotations from Chairman Mao." They excitedly shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live Mao Zedong Thought!" "Long live the fighting friendship between the peoples of Albania and China!"

	New China News Agency Photographer

The heroic Vietnamese people, in their struggle against American aggression and for national salvation, have deeply experienced that Mao Zedong Thought is the guide for the Chinese revolution, the Vietnamese revolution, and the revolutionary struggles of the peoples of Asia, Africa, and Latin America. The revolutionary peoples of the world can only finally defeat American imperialism and all its lackeys by mastering the powerful Mao Zedong Thought. The picture shows Vietnamese women studying "Quotations from Chairman Mao" seriously.

	New China News Agency Photographer

The great leader Chairman Mao's brilliant thought that "political power grows out of the barrel of a gun" is the guide for the revolutionary struggles of all the peoples of the world. It leads all the oppressed and exploited peoples to the true path of liberation. The flames of people's armed struggle are blazing in Asia, Africa, and Latin America.

	New China News Agency Photographer

Friends from Guinea love Chairman Mao and Mao Zedong Thought. They say, "The invincible Mao Zedong Thought is the most powerful weapon guiding us in our struggle against imperialism." The picture shows friends from Guinea studying "Quotations from Chairman Mao."

	New China News Agency Photographer

Chairman Mao's works are like food and weapons; only by relying on Mao Zedong Thought can we win the final victory. Soldiers of the Lao People's Liberation Army study Chairman Mao's works seriously and are determined to follow the revolutionary direction indicated by Chairman Mao, carrying on the struggle against American aggression and for national salvation to the end!

	New China News Agency Photographer

The storm of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution has swept across China and shaken the world. Beijing has become the center of the world revolution. Mao Zedong is the Lenin of our times! Revolutionary peoples of the world love the great teacher Chairman Mao and the invincible Mao Zedong Thought. This is a picture of children from Yemen and Brazil, with hearts full of boundless love for Chairman Mao, flipping through a picture album of Chairman Mao inspecting the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution troops.

	Our Reporter

Revolutionary cultural workers from Japan, with hearts full of boundless respect for Chairman Mao, visited the revolutionary holy land of Yan'an. This is a picture of them at the place where Chairman Mao and the American journalist Anna Louise Strong had their talk, studying Chairman Mao's brilliant teachings on how imperialism and all reactionaries are paper tigers.

	Our Reporter

### 欢呼世界进入毛主席思想新时代（图片）

1968-01-14
第6版()
专栏：

    欢呼世界进入毛主席思想新时代
    今天，世界已经进入以毛泽东思想为伟大旗帜的革命新时代。毛主席的宝书发遍全中国，毛泽东思想的光辉照亮全世界！阿尔巴尼亚的革命文艺战士在访问中国期间，幸福地得到了红彤彤的宝书——阿尔巴尼亚文版《毛主席语录》。他们激动地高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛泽东思想万岁！”“阿中两国人民的战斗友谊万岁！”
                          新华社记者摄
    英雄的越南人民在抗美救国斗争中深刻体验到：毛泽东思想是中国革命、越南革命以及亚、非、拉各国革命斗争的指南。世界革命人民只有掌握了威力无穷的毛泽东思想，才能最后消灭美帝国主义及其一切走狗。图是越南妇女在认真学习《毛主席语录》。
            　新华社记者摄
    伟大领袖毛主席关于“枪杆子里面出政权”的光辉思想，是全世界人民革命斗争的指南。它引导着一切被压迫和被剥削的人民走上真正解放的道路。人民武装斗争的烈火已在亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲熊熊燃烧。
                  新华社记者摄
    几内亚朋友热爱毛主席，热爱毛泽东思想。他们说：“战无不胜的毛泽东思想，是指导我们同帝国主义进行斗争的最有力武器”。图为几内亚朋友在学习《毛主席语录》。
          新华社记者摄
    毛主席著作好比粮食和武器，只有依靠毛泽东思想才能赢得最后胜利。老挝人民解放军战士认真学习毛主席著作，决心沿着毛主席指引的革命方向，把抗美救国斗争进行到底！
              新华社记者摄
    无产阶级文化大革命的风暴席卷全中国，震撼全世界。北京，已成为世界革命的中心。毛泽东，就是当代的列宁！全世界革命人民热爱伟大导师毛主席，热爱战无不胜的毛泽东思想。这是也门和巴西的儿童怀着对毛主席无限热爱的心情，翻阅毛主席检阅无产阶级文化革命大军的画报。
                                本报记者摄
    日本的革命文艺战士，怀着对毛主席无限崇敬的心情，访问了革命圣地延安。这是他们在当年毛主席和美国记者安娜·路易斯·斯特朗谈话的地方，学习毛主席关于帝国主义和一切反动派都是纸老虎的英明教导。
                                        本报记者摄
