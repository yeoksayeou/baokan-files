Title: China and Romania Sign the 1968 Barter Payment Agreement
Author:
Date: 1968-01-01
Edition: 6

	China and Romania Sign the 1968 Barter Payment Agreement
	New China News Agency, January 31st

	The 1968 exchange of goods and payment agreement between China and Romania was signed in Beijing on the 30th.

	Vice Premier Li Xiannian attended the signing ceremony.

	Li Qiang, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and Valentin Striobor, Vice Minister of the Romanian Ministry of Foreign Trade and head of the Romanian government trade delegation, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments.

	The Romanian government trade delegation arrived in China recently to discuss 1968 trade. The Chinese government trade delegation and the Romanian government trade delegation held talks in a friendly atmosphere.

	The agreement signed yesterday stipulates that the trade volume between China and Romania in 1968 will further increase compared to 1967.

	Before the signing ceremony, Vice Premier Li Xiannian met with the head and members of the Romanian government trade delegation and had a friendly conversation with them.

	Those who attended the signing ceremony from the Chinese side included Lin Haiyun, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and others. Those who attended the signing ceremony from the Romanian side included Dumitru, the Romanian Ambassador to China, and members of the Romanian government trade delegation.

### 中罗签订本年度换货付款协定

1968-01-01
第6版()
专栏：

    中罗签订本年度换货付款协定
    新华社三十一日讯  中国和罗马尼亚一九六八年度交换货物和付款协定，三十日在北京签订。
    国务院副总理李先念参加了签字仪式。
    对外贸易部副部长李强和罗马尼亚政府贸易代表团团长、罗马尼亚对外贸易部副部长瓦伦丁·斯特里奥博尔，分别代表两国政府在协定书上签字。
    罗马尼亚政府贸易代表团是不久前来我国商谈一九六八年贸易的。我国政府贸易代表团和罗马尼亚政府贸易代表团在友好的气氛中举行了会谈。
    昨天签订的协定规定，中国和罗马尼亚一九六八年度贸易额将比一九六七年度有进一步的增长。
    李先念副总理在举行签字仪式前，接见了罗马尼亚政府贸易代表团团长和团员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，有对外贸易部代理部长林海云等。罗马尼亚方面参加签字仪式的，有罗马尼亚驻中国大使杜马和罗马尼亚政府贸易代表团成员。
