Title: The Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea Elects the Standing Committee and Forms a New Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1968-01-02
Edition: 6

The Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea held its fourth session on December 16th of last year, electing the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly and forming a new cabinet for the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

The session elected Choi Yong-gon as the Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, and Hong Myong-hui, Pak Chong-ae, Kang Ryang-hu, and Ri Yong-ho as Vice-Chairmen.

During the session, a new cabinet was formed with Kim Il-sung as the Premier. The First Vice-Premier is Kim Il, and the Vice-Premiers are Kim Kwang-su, Pak Song-chol, Kim Chang-bong, Ri Chu-yon, Nam Il, Ri Chong-yol, Choe Yong-jin, and Choe Chun-chae.

### 朝鲜最高人民会议  选出常任委员会和组成新内阁

1968-01-02
第6版()
专栏：

    朝鲜最高人民会议
    选出常任委员会和组成新内阁
    新华社平壤电　据朝鲜中央通讯社报道，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议第四届第一次会议在去年十二月十六日的会议上，选出了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会和组成朝鲜民主主义人民共和国新内阁。
    会议选举崔庸健为最高人民会议常任委员会委员长，洪命熹、朴正爱、康良煜、李永镐为副委员长。
    在会议上组成了以金日成为首相的新内阁，内阁第一副首相为金一，内阁副首相有金光侠、朴成哲、金昌凤、李周渊、南日、李钟玉、崔勇进和郑准泽。
