Title: Mao Zedong Thought Shines Everywhere
Author: Wáng Pèiqiú
Date: 1968-01-01
Edition: 5

A: Mao Zedong Thought's red flag flies high,
	The heroes of today are truly great.
B: Mao Zedong Thought shines everywhere,
	Millions of workers and peasants leap forward.
A: Mao Zedong Thought is boundless,
	It dares to change the heavens and the earth.
B: Mao Zedong Thought shines with golden light,
	The world is turned upside down, full of passion.
Both: (Speaking) Chairman Mao said: "The situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is excellent, not just a little good. The overall situation is better than ever before."

A: Look up, comrade,
	The whole country is red.
B: From the cities to the countryside,
	Criticism echoes everywhere.
A: From schools to factories,
	We denounce and write to eliminate the wolves.
B: Soldiers and civilians unite in criticism,
	The country's appearance has greatly changed.
	Look at—
	The storm in Shanghai spreads nationwide,
	The dawn in the Northeast shines over the mountains and rivers.
	The thunder in the Southwest resounds through the sky,
	The victory song in Shandong stretches across the rainbow.
	The sun in Beijing redden the sky,
	The victory reports from Taihang shake the mountains and rivers.
	The war drums beat on the Northwest plateau,
	The red flags dance on the Inner Mongolia grasslands.
	The banks of the Haihe River are bathed in morning light,
	The east wind blows across the country.

A: The east wind blows, the red flags unfurl,
	Every household studies "Selected Works of Mao Zedong."
	Look at—
	"Speak Bitterness" meetings are held in the fields,
	"Criticize Self" meetings are held in the workshops,
	Criticism meetings are held in the classrooms,
	Debate meetings are held in the homes.
	Big meetings, small meetings,
	All are guided by Mao Zedong Thought.

B: (Speaking) Yes, indeed, Mao Zedong Thought is widely spread.
	Mao Zedong Thought is a treasure,
	The revolutionary masses are fully mobilized.
	Everyone cares about national affairs,
	Men, women, old, and young all participate.
	Look at—
	The elderly put up big-character posters,
	The little children shout slogans:
	"Resolutely overthrow the capitalist roaders,
	The iron mountain will be red for ten thousand generations!
	We will defend Chairman Mao to the death,
	Our resolve will never change even if the sea dries up and the stones crumble!"
	The Chinese Khrushchev has fallen,
	The capitalist reactionary line is buried.
A: Drumming and gonging to celebrate victory,
	The revolutionary masses are so spirited.
	Look at—
	Everyone fights self and criticizes revisionism with one heart.
	Revolutionary big-unity red flags flutter in the wind,
	Revolutionary "three-in-one combination" signs shine brightly.
	Revolutionary big-criticism war drums shake the heavens and earth,
	Victory reports cover the walls.
	Teams of joyous messengers march past,
	A surge of victorious joy wells up in the chest.
B: The revolutionary situation is so great,
	The whole country is singing.
A: (Speaking) What are they singing?
B: They sing, "Long live Chairman Mao!"
	They sing of the new victories of the Cultural Revolution.
	They sing of the widespread Mao Zedong Thought,
	They sing of revolutionary, productive miracles.
	They sing of grain and cotton yields increasing together,
	They sing of surging waves of steel and iron.
	They sing of the new developments in educational revolution,
	They sing of nuclear explosions frightening the imperialists, revisionists, and reactionaries.
	They sing, "The vast sea relies on the helmsman, all things grow relying on the sun..."

A: (Speaking) Hey... you're singing too?
B: The revolutionary situation is so good,
	All thanks to Chairman Mao's excellent leadership.
	Chairman Mao leads us to victory,
	Shouldn't we sing?
A: (Speaking) Yes, we should sing.
	The vast sea relies on the helmsman,
	I swear to follow Chairman Mao forever.
B: Revolution relies on Mao Zedong Thought,
	I will forever follow Chairman Mao to brave the storms.
A: Chairman Mao, say a word,
	I dare to descend into the fires of hell.
B: Chairman Mao, give the order,
	I will die for the revolution.
A: I love Chairman Mao, I love him,
B: I support Chairman Mao, I support him,
A: I follow Chairman Mao's instructions,
B: I march forward with Chairman Mao's wave.
Both: We will raise high the great red flag of Mao Zedong Thought,
	Step by step, we follow Chairman Mao.
	We will turn the whole country into a vast school of Mao Zedong Thought,
	Let the brilliance of Mao Zedong Thought shine forever and ever!

1968-01-01

    祖国面貌大改变。

    海枯石烂志不移！”
    中国的赫鲁晓夫垮了
      台，
    资产阶级反动路线被
      葬埋。甲：敲锣打鼓庆胜利，
    革命群众好神气。
    你看那——
    一个个斗私批修同心
      干。
    一面面革命大联合红
      旗迎风扬，
    一块块革命“三结合”
      牌子放光芒。
    一阵阵革命大批判战
      鼓震天地，
    一张张增产的捷报满
      墙壁。
    一队队报喜的队伍走

    一股股胜利的喜悦涌

    全国人民都在唱。甲：（白）都是唱的什

      展，
    唱的是核爆吓坏帝修
      反。

      行靠舵手，万物生
      长靠太阳……”甲：（白）哎……你怎么
    也唱上啦？乙：革命形势这样好，
    全靠毛主席的好领
      导。
