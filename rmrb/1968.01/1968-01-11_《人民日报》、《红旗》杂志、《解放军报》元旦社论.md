Title: To Ensure That Chairman Mao's Latest Instructions Are Fully Implemented
Author:
Date: 1968-01-11
Edition: 1

We must ensure that Chairman Mao's latest instructions are fully implemented, comprehensively implemented. We must make Chairman Mao's brilliant thoughts and specific policies directly accessible to the broad masses of the people, transforming them into the conscious revolutionary actions of hundreds of millions of revolutionary people.
