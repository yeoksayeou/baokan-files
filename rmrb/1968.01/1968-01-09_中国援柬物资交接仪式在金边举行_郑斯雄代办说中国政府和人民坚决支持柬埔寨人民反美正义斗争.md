Title: Ceremony for the Handing Over of Chinese Aid to Cambodia Held in Phnom Penh
Author:
Date: 1968-01-09
Edition: 6

The ceremony for the handover of Chinese aid to Cambodia was held in Phnom Penh. The Chinese chargé d'affaires, Zheng Sixiong, stated that the Chinese government and people resolutely support the Cambodian people's just struggle against American imperialism.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, January 8th: The high representative of the Head of State of Cambodia, Prince Norodom Sihanouk, and the Prime Minister of the Kingdom, Lon Nol, presided over the handover ceremony of Chinese aid on the morning of the 4th. The President of the National Assembly, Hou Yuon, the Minister of Defense, Lon Nol, the Chief of Staff of the Royal Cambodian Air Force, Nou Houy, and other military and political officials of Cambodia also attended the ceremony. The Chinese chargé d'affaires in Cambodia, Zheng Sixiong, and other diplomatic officials from the Chinese Embassy also participated in the ceremony.

Lon Nol said in his speech at the ceremony: "We indeed believe that the new colonial policy of the United States is a great danger to world peace and stability."

He pointed out: "Under the current situation, the important military aid from friendly China is of great significance. In fact, we see that the threat of U.S. interference with our country's independence, neutrality, and territorial integrity is increasing, and we cannot rule out the possibility that in the coming weeks or months, we will have to mobilize all our forces to resist the invaders."

He continued: "I am happy to take this opportunity to express our sincere gratitude to the People's Republic of China for its recent declaration of the most resolute support for our country, which is under threat, to the Chinese government and the great people of China." Lon Nol concluded by shouting: "Long live Chairman Mao Zedong! Long live the People's Republic of China! Long live Cambodian-Chinese friendship!"

Zheng Sixiong said in his speech: "The Chinese government and people resolutely support Cambodia's just struggle against American imperialism, to defend national sovereignty, independence, neutrality, and territorial integrity. Our great leader Chairman Mao pointed out: 'Our common enemy is American imperialism, and we are all on the same front, we all need to unite and support each other.' We will follow Chairman Mao's great teachings, stand on the side of the Cambodian people, and fight to the end, defeating our common enemy—American imperialism."

He expressed his belief that the friendly relations between China and Cambodia will continue to develop and strengthen in the common struggle against American imperialism and on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.

Zheng Sixiong concluded by wishing Queen Sihanouk good health, wishing the Head of State of Cambodia, Prince Norodom Sihanouk, good health, and wishing the Kingdom of Cambodia prosperity and prosperity! He shouted: "Long live the fighting unity and friendship between China and Cambodia!"

### 中国援柬物资交接仪式在金边举行  郑斯雄代办说中国政府和人民坚决支持柬埔寨人民反美正义斗争

1968-01-09
第6版()
专栏：

    中国援柬物资交接仪式在金边举行
    郑斯雄代办说中国政府和人民坚决支持柬埔寨人民反美正义斗争
    据新华社金边八日电  柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王的高级代表、王国政府首相宋双，四日上午主持了中国援助物资的交接仪式。王国议会议长高基兴，国防大臣杨森安，柬埔寨王家空军参谋长努呼，以及其他柬埔寨军、政官员参加了仪式。中国驻柬埔寨大使馆临时代办郑斯雄和中国大使馆的外交官员也参加了仪式。
    宋双在仪式上讲话说：“我们的确认为，美国新殖民主义政策对世界和平与稳定是极大的危险。”
    他指出：“在当前的形势下，友好的中国给予我们的重要军事援助具有重大意义。事实上，我们看到，美国对我国的独立、中立和领土完整进行干涉的威胁不断增长，而且不能排除这样的可能性：在以后的几星期、几个月中，我们将不得不动员我们的全部力量来抗击侵略者。”
    他接着说：“我高兴地借此机会，对中华人民共和国最近为最坚决地支持受到威胁的我国而发表的声明，向中国政府和伟大的中国人民，表示我们的真挚的感激之情。”宋双最后高呼：“毛泽东主席阁下万岁！中华人民共和国万岁！柬中友谊万岁！”
    郑斯雄在讲话中说：“中国政府和人民坚决支持柬埔寨为反对美帝国主义，为保卫国家主权、独立、中立和领土完整而进行的正义斗争。我们的伟大领袖毛主席指出：‘我们共同的敌人是美帝国主义，我们大家都是站在一条战线上，大家需要互相团结互相支持。’我们将遵循毛主席的伟大教导，站在柬埔寨人民一边，把斗争进行到底，打败共同的敌人——美帝国主义。”
    他表示相信，中柬两国的友好关系，将在反对美帝国主义的共同斗争中和在和平共处五项原则的基础上继续发展和加强。
    郑斯雄最后祝王后陛下身体健康！祝柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王身体健康！祝柬埔寨王国繁荣昌盛！他高呼：“中柬两国战斗团结友谊万岁！”
