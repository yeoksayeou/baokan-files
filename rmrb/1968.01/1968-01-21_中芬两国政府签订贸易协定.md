Title: The Governments of China and Finland Sign a Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1968-01-21
Edition: 6

The trade agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Finland for 1968 was signed in Beijing today after friendly negotiations.

The agreement stipulates that the bilateral trade volume will further increase compared to 1967.

### 中芬两国政府签订贸易协定

1968-01-21
第6版()
专栏：

    中芬两国政府签订贸易协定
    新华社二十日讯　中华人民共和国和芬兰共和国政府一九六八年贸易协定，经过友好商谈，今天在北京签订。
    协定规定双方进出口贸易额比一九六七年有进一步增长。
