Title: To Ensure the Implementation of Chairman Mao's Latest Instructions
Author:
Date: 1968-01-20
Edition: 2

To ensure that Chairman Mao's latest instructions are fully implemented, we must make sure that Chairman Mao's brilliant thoughts and all specific policies are directly grasped by the broad masses of the people and transformed into the conscious revolutionary actions of hundreds of millions of revolutionary people.
