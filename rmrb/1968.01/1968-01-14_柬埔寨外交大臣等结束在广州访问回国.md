Title: Cambodian Foreign Minister and Delegation Conclude Visit to Guangzhou and Return Home
Author:
Date: 1968-01-14
Edition: 5

Cambodian Foreign Minister and Delegation Conclude Visit to Guangzhou and Return Home

According to Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, January 13th: The Kingdom of Cambodia's Foreign Minister Norodom Phurissara, along with the Cambodian Ambassador to Vietnam, Sim Vath, and the Director of the Foreign Minister's Office, Yul Lanvato, have concluded their visit to Guangzhou and departed on the 11th to return home.

Those who saw off the Cambodian guests included Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong, and leaders of the Guangdong Provincial Revolutionary Committee Preparatory Group, Qiu Guoguang, among others. The Vietnamese Consul General in Guangzhou, Du Wenmin, also came to see them off.

The Cambodian guests, after visiting Vietnam, stopped in Guangzhou on their way back home at the invitation of the Chinese side on the 8th.

### 柬埔寨外交大臣等结束在广州访问回国

1968-01-14
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨外交大臣等结束在广州访问回国
    据新华社广州十三日电　柬埔寨王国外交大臣诺罗敦·富里萨拉以及随行的柬埔寨驻越南大使沈力速，柬埔寨外交大臣办公厅主任育兰瓦托，结束了在广州的访问，已于十一日离开广州回国。
    欢送柬埔寨客人的有外交部副部长韩念龙，广东省革命委员会筹备小组领导成员邱国光等。越南驻广州总领事杜文敏也前往欢送。
    柬埔寨客人是在访问越南后回国途中，应邀于八日来到广州访问的。
