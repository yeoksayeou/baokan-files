Title: In the New Year, Strive for Comprehensive Victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1968-01-04
Edition: 1

In the new year, the entire Party, the entire army, the revolutionary masses of the proletariat nationwide, and all the people of the nation must hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought. We must take Chairman Mao’s latest instructions as the guideline, muster our strength, strive for the forefront, and press on with redoubled efforts. We must seize comprehensive victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in terms of ideology, politics, economy, and organization.
