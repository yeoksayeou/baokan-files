Title: Commemorating the 500th Anniversary of the Death of Albanian National Hero Skanderbeg
Author:
Date: 1968-01-20
Edition: 6

Albanian Ambassador to China, Natanaeli, hosted a film screening and reception tonight to commemorate the 500th anniversary of the death of Albanian national hero George Kastrioti Skanderbeg.

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, relevant officials Qiao Guanhua, Zhou Yiping, Ding Xilin, and revolutionary mass representatives from the capital were invited to attend the film screening and reception.

During the reception, comrades from both China and Albania frequently raised their glasses, toasting to the eternal friendship and unity between the peoples of China and Albania, and wishing long life to the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao, and the great leader of the Albanian people, Comrade Enver Hoxha.

After the reception, a film about Skanderbeg's struggle was screened.

### 纪念阿民族英雄斯坎德培逝世五百周年  阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使举行电影酒会

1968-01-20
第6版()
专栏：

    纪念阿民族英雄斯坎德培逝世五百周年
    阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使举行电影酒会
    新华社十九日讯　阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使纳塔奈利今晚举行电影酒会，纪念阿尔巴尼亚民族英雄乔治·康斯坦丁·斯坎德培逝世五百周年。
    人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，有关方面负责人乔冠华、周一萍、丁西林以及首都革命群众代表，应邀出席了电影酒会。
    酒会上，中阿两国战友频频举杯共祝中阿两国人民的战斗友谊和团结万古长青，祝中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席和阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖恩维尔·霍查同志万寿无疆。
    酒会以后放映了介绍斯坎德培斗争事迹的阿尔巴尼亚影片。
