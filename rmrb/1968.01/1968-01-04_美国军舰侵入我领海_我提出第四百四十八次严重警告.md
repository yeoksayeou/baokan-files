Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into Our Territorial Waters
Author:
Date: 1968-01-04
Edition: 6

On January 2, from 6:18 AM to 4:17 PM, a U.S. warship intruded into our territorial waters south of Hainan Island, Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 10:10 AM to 1:20 PM, another U.S. warship intruded into our territorial waters east of Huangqi Peninsula, Fujian Province. In response to this military provocation by U.S. warships, the spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the ministry, issued the 448th serious warning.

### 美国军舰侵入我领海  我提出第四百四十八次严重警告

1968-01-04
第6版()
专栏：

    美国军舰侵入我领海
    我提出第四百四十八次严重警告
    新华社二日讯　一月二日六时十八分至十六时十七分美国军舰一艘，侵入我广东省海南岛以南地区领海。同日十时十分至十三时二十分美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省黄岐半岛以东地区领海。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百四十八次严重警告。
