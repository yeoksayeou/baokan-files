Title: West Germany's Unemployment Surges to 520,000
Author:
Date: 1968-01-09
Edition: 6

West Germany's unemployment figures have surged to 526,200, according to official announcements. In December 1967, the number of unemployed in West Germany reached 526,200, an increase of 154,600 compared to the same period last year, and an increase of 348,000 compared to the same period in 1965.

### 西德失业人数猛增到五十二万人

1968-01-09
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

    西德失业人数猛增到五十二万人
    据西德官方宣布：西德一九六七年十二月份失业人数猛增到五十二万六千二百人，比前年同期增加十五万四千六百人，比一九六五年同期增加了三十四万八千人。（据新华社讯）
