	Chairman Mao's Quotations

	The question of study style is a question of the method of thinking for the leading organs, all the cadres, and all the Party members. It is a question of our attitude towards Marxism-Leninism. It is a question of the work style of all comrades in the Party. Since this is the case, the question of study style is an extremely important question, it is the first important question.

	*Rectifying the Party's Work Style*
