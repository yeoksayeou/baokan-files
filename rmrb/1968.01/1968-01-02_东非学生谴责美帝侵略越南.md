Title: East African Students Condemn U.S. Imperialism's Aggression Against Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1968-01-02
Edition: 6

East African students have strongly condemned the U.S. imperialists' savage aggression against Vietnam and expressed resolute support for the heroic Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation. This was stated at a recent meeting held in Kampala by the East African Students' Union.

### 东非学生谴责美帝侵略越南

1968-01-02
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

    东非学生谴责美帝侵略越南
    东非大学生联合会最近在坎帕拉举行的一次讨论会上强烈谴责美帝国主义对越南的野蛮侵略，并表示坚决支持英雄的越南人民的抗美救国斗争。
