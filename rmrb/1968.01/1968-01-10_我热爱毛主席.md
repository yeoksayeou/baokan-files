Title: I Love Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1968-01-10
Edition: 5

I Love Chairman Mao

An Italian doctor working in Algeria once brought his child to meet a Chinese doctor working there. The child, not yet twelve years old, was very friendly upon seeing the Chinese doctor. He snuggled up close and said, "This is my first time seeing a Chinese person. The Little Red Guards in China are truly amazing. I love Chairman Mao. I hope you can give me a Chairman Mao badge."

### 我热爱毛主席

1968-01-10
第5版()
专栏：

    我热爱毛主席
    一位在阿尔及利亚的意大利医生，有一次特地带了自己的孩子，去见见在阿尔及利亚工作的中国医生。这个孩子还不到十二岁，一看到中国医生便显得十分亲热。他紧紧地挨着中国医生说：“我第一次看到中国人。中国红卫兵小朋友本事真大。我热爱毛主席，我希望你们能送给我毛主席像章。”
                                          （新华社）
