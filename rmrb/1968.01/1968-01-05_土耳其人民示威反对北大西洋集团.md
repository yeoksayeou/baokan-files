Title: Turkish People Protest Against NATO
Author:
Date: 1968-01-05
Edition: 6

Turkish people have recently been actively engaged in a struggle, strongly demanding that Turkey withdraw from the North Atlantic Treaty Organization, which is controlled by American imperialism. In the Turkish capital, Ankara, approximately one thousand students recently held a demonstration. They distributed pamphlets, expressing their firm opposition to the North Atlantic Treaty Organization.

Students at the Economic and Commercial Science Academy in the western Turkish city of Izmir are continuing their movement against the North Atlantic aggression pact, demanding that Turkey withdraw from this organization. Many buildings in this city are adorned with slogans opposing the North Atlantic Treaty Organization.

### 土耳其人民示威反对北大西洋集团

1968-01-05
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

    土耳其人民示威反对北大西洋集团
    土耳其人民最近积极展开斗争，强烈要求土耳其退出美帝国主义把持的北大西洋侵略集团——北大西洋公约组织。在土耳其首都安卡拉，大约有一千名学生最近举行示威。他们散发小册子，表示坚决反对北大西洋集团。
    土耳其西部城市伊斯密尔的经济商业科学学院的学生正在继续开展反对北大西洋侵略集团的运动，要求土耳其退出这个集团。在这个城市的许多建筑上都挂着反对北大西洋公约组织的标语。
