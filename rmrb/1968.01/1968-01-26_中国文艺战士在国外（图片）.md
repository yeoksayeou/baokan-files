Title: Chinese Cultural Warriors Abroad
Author:
Date: 1968-01-26
Edition: 5

	Chinese Cultural Warriors Abroad

	The Chinese Red East Acrobatic Troupe received a warm welcome in Japan. The picture shows members of the Red East Acrobatic Troupe studying "Quotations from Chairman Mao" with Japanese youth. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency)

	The Chinese Dawn Song and Dance Troupe is a propaganda team for Mao Zedong Thought. They went deep into the masses of Guinea, extensively promoting the boundless Mao Zedong Thought, and forged a deep friendship with the broad masses of the people of Guinea. The picture shows Chinese revolutionary cultural warriors helping a peasant family pound rice. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter)

	The people of Palestine warmly welcomed the arrival of the Chinese Song and Dance Troupe and sincerely thanked Chairman Mao and the Chinese people for their staunch support and strong backing in the Palestinian people's struggle for national liberation. The picture shows members of the Chinese Song and Dance Troupe singing "The East Is Red" together with Palestinian youth who had visited China. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter)

### 中国文艺战士在国外（图片）

1968-01-26
第5版()
专栏：

    中国文艺战士在国外
    中国东方红杂技团在日本受到热烈欢迎。图为东方红杂技团的演员们和日本青年一起学习《毛主席语录》。新华社发
    中国曙光歌舞团是一支毛泽东思想宣传队。他们深入到几内亚群众中，广泛宣传光焰无际的毛泽东思想，同几内亚广大人民结下了深厚的友谊。图是中国革命文艺战士帮助一家农民舂米。新华社记者摄
    巴勒斯坦人民热烈欢迎中国歌舞团的到来，并衷心感谢毛主席和中国人民对巴勒斯坦人民争取民族解放的战斗声援和大力支持。图为中国歌舞团演员和曾访问过中国的巴勒斯坦青年同声高唱《东方红》。			新华社记者摄
