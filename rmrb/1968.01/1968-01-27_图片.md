Title: Japanese Patriots Protest Against U.S. Nuclear Aircraft Carrier in Sasebo
Author:
Date: 1968-01-27
Edition: 7

Japanese patriotic workers and students held a demonstration in Sasebo to protest against the U.S. nuclear aircraft carrier "Enterprise" entering Sasebo. The photo shows patriotic students participating in the demonstration in front of the U.S. military base in Sasebo, bravely fighting against the reactionary police.
