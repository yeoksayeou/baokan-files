Title: Celebrating the Revolutionary Holiday of the People of Zanzibar
Author:
Date: 1968-01-14
Edition: 5

Tens of thousands of Tanzanian people of Zanzibar gathered in Zanzibar City on the morning of the 12th to hold a military review and mass rally, enthusiastically celebrating the fourth anniversary of the Zanzibar armed revolution.

Participants in the parade sang revolutionary songs and chanted slogans. They held placards with slogans such as "Definitely liberate Africa" and "Down with imperialism and its running dogs."

The fully armed Tanzanian People's Defense Force of Zanzibar and young volunteer armed with long guns marched past the reviewing stand, displaying the determination of the people of Zanzibar to defend the fruits of the revolution.

President Nyerere and First Vice President Karume reviewed the parade and delivered speeches.

In his speech, President Nyerere called on the Tanzanian people of Zanzibar to carry forward the revolutionary spirit, overcome difficulties on the road ahead, and achieve progress.

More than twenty Asian and African countries and some other countries sent guests to Zanzibar to participate in the celebration activities. Among them were representatives of the Chinese government and the acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Tanzania, Zhou Boqing. Representatives from the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam and various national liberation movements in Africa also participated in the celebration.

In the evening, First Vice President Karume hosted a state banquet at the State House in Zanzibar to entertain the guests from various countries who participated in the celebration.

In the afternoon of the 12th, in the capital Dar es Salaam, thousands of Tanzanians from various sectors also held a parade and a mass rally. President Nyerere and Second Vice President Kawawa attended the rally and delivered speeches.

### 欢庆桑给巴尔人民的革命节日  坦桑尼亚举行军事检阅和集会游行

1968-01-14
第5版()
专栏：

    欢庆桑给巴尔人民的革命节日
    坦桑尼亚举行军事检阅和集会游行
    据新华社桑给巴尔十二日电　成千上万的坦桑尼亚桑给巴尔人民十二日上午在桑给巴尔市举行军事检阅和群众集会，热烈庆祝桑给巴尔武装革命四周年。
    参加游行的群众高唱革命歌曲，高呼口号。他们举着标语牌，上面写着“一定要解放非洲”“打倒帝国主义及其走狗”等口号。
    桑给巴尔全副武装的坦桑尼亚人民国防军和手持长枪的青年志愿人员，雄赳赳气昂昂地经过检阅台，表现了桑给巴尔人民捍卫革命果实的决心。
    尼雷尔总统和第一副总统卡鲁姆检阅了游行队伍并发表了讲话。
    尼雷尔总统在讲话中要求坦桑尼亚桑给巴尔人民发扬革命精神，克服前进道路上的困难，取得进步。
    二十多个亚非国家和一些其他国家的国宾到桑给巴尔参加了庆祝活动，其中有中国政府代表、中国驻坦桑尼亚大使馆临时代办周伯萍。越南南方民族解放阵线和非洲各民族解放运动的代表也参加了庆祝活动。
    当晚，卡鲁姆第一副总统在桑给巴尔的国家大厦举行国宴，招待参加这次庆祝活动的各国客人。
    十二日下午，在首都达累斯萨拉姆，数千名坦桑尼亚各界人士也举行了游行，并举行了群众大会。尼雷尔总统和第二副总统卡瓦瓦出席了大会，并讲了话。
