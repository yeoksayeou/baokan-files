    Brief News
    North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Four U.S. Aircraft

    According to Hanoi, the military and civilians of North Vietnam's Hai Phong City, Bac Ninh Province, and Quang Ninh Province shot down one U.S. aggressor aircraft each on the 5th.

    According to additional reports, the military and civilians of Nghe An Province shot down one U.S. aircraft on December 31st of last year.

    Kenyan People Protest Humphrey's Activities in Kenya

    On the evening of the 7th, when the U.S. imperialist leader Humphrey arrived in Nairobi, the capital of Kenya, a large number of angry Kenyan masses, despite various restrictions imposed by the police, held a protest demonstration at the airport with placards, strongly condemning U.S. imperialism's attempt to turn Kenya into a base for its economic and political aggression in Africa.

    The police suppressed the demonstrators and arrested some of them before Humphrey was able to slip out of the airport.

    U.S. Imperialists Hold Secret Talks with Their Lackey Israel

    The U.S. imperialist leader Johnson held two days of secret talks with Israeli Prime Minister Eshkol on the 7th and 8th in the United States. A statement issued after the talks indicated that U.S. imperialism "agreed to actively and sympathetically study Israel's military defensive capabilities" and promised to provide Israel with weapons and equipment to enable it to launch further aggression against the peoples of the Arab countries.

    In the statement from the talks between Johnson and Eshkol, they openly pressured Arab countries, forcing them to accept a series of humiliating conditions and to fully surrender to the U.S.-Israeli aggressors.

### 简明新闻

专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    越南北方军民击落美机四架
    据河内公布，越南北方海防市、河北省、广宁省军民五日各击落美国强盗飞机一架。
    据补充报道，义安省军民去年十二月三十一日也击落美机一架。
    肯尼亚人民抗议汉弗莱到肯活动
    七日晚上，当美帝国主义头目汉弗莱到达肯尼亚首都内罗毕活动时，大批愤怒的肯尼亚群众不顾警察当局的种种限制，手持标语牌，在机场举行抗议示威，强烈谴责美帝国主义企图把肯尼亚变为它在非洲进行经济和政治侵略的据点。
    警察对示威者进行了镇压，并抓走了一些示威群众后，汉弗莱才得以溜出机场。
    美帝同其走狗以色列秘密会谈
    美帝头子约翰逊同以色列总理艾希科尔七日和八日在美国接连举行两天秘密会谈。会谈后发表的声明表明，美帝国主义“同意对以色列军事防御能力进行积极的、同情的研究”，并保证向以色列提供武器装备，以便它向阿拉伯各国人民发动进一步侵略。
    约翰逊同艾希科尔在会谈声明里，公然对阿拉伯国家施加压力，逼迫阿拉伯国家接受一系列丧权辱国的条件，向美以侵略者全面投降。
                          （据新华社讯）
