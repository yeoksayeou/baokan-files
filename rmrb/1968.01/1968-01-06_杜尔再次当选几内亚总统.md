Title: Duval Re-elected as President of Guinea
Author:
Date: 1968-01-06
Edition: 6

Guinea Republic held a nationwide presidential election on January 1st, and Sekou Toure, the President, was re-elected as the President of the Guinea Republic.

On the same day, Guinea also held elections for members of the National Assembly, electing seventy-five members.

According to the constitution of the Guinea Republic, presidential elections are held every seven years. The first presidential election was held in January 1961.

### 杜尔再次当选几内亚总统

1968-01-06
第6版()
专栏：

    杜尔再次当选几内亚总统
    新华社科纳克里三日电　几内亚共和国一日在全国举行总统选举，塞古·杜尔总统再次当选为几内亚共和国总统。
    同一天，几内亚全国还举行了国民议会议员的选举，选出了七十五名国民议会议员。
    根据几内亚共和国宪法，总统选举每隔七年举行一次，第一次总统选举是在一九六一年一月举行的。
