Title: Guinea Government Reshuffled
Author:
Date: 1968-01-25
Edition: 6

Guinea Republic President Sekou Toure announced on the evening of the 19th in Conakry Radio that the Guinea government had undergone a reshuffle on January 19th.

The new government, headed by President Toure, consists of ten ministers and several state secretaries. Among them are the Minister of State for Finance Diallo Seforaie, Foreign Minister Lansana Beavogui, Minister of Economic Development Ismail Toure, Minister of Trade Keta Enfamara, Minister of Social Affairs Toure Mamouna, and Minister of People's Army and Defense of the Revolution Dianke Lansana. These ministers are all members of the Political Bureau of the Guinea Democratic Party.

### 几内亚政府改组

1968-01-25
第6版()
专栏：

    几内亚政府改组
    新华社科纳克里电　几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔十九日晚在科纳克里电台宣布，几内亚政府在一月十九日进行了改组。
    以杜尔总统为首的新政府由十名部长和十几名国务秘书组成，其中有负责财政的国务部长迪亚洛·塞福拉耶、外交部长兰萨纳·贝阿沃吉、经济发展部长伊斯梅尔·杜尔、贸易部长凯塔·恩法马拉、社会事务部长杜尔·马穆纳和人民军和保卫革命部部长迪亚奈·兰萨纳。这些部长都是几内亚民主党全国政治局委员。
