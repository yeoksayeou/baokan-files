Title: Under the Warm Care of the Party of Labour, the Albanian People Overcome the Earthquake Disaster and Demonstrate Heroic Spirit in the Face of Severe Trials
Author:
Date: 1968-01-01
Edition: 6

The people of Albania's Librazhd and Dibra districts, who were struck by the earthquake on November 30, have, under the care of the Party of Labour Central Committee, the government, and the people of the whole country, through arduous and intense labor, successfully completed the task of eliminating the earthquake disaster ahead of schedule on December 24 and 28 respectively.

The laboring masses of Librazhd and Dibra districts have sent letters and telegrams to Comrade Enver Hoxha, the great leader of the Albanian people, and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Mehmet Shehu, reporting this victory.

In their letters and telegrams, they pointed out that this great victory was achieved thanks to the warm care of the Party of Labour, the great help from the government and the laboring masses of the whole country. This earthquake was a severe test for the Albanian people. In this test, the great power of the Party and the people was fully manifested, as was the firm unity and solidarity between the Party and the people, and the revolutionary heroic spirit of the Albanian people.

The laboring masses of Librazhd district built 2,177 houses and other buildings in 24 days. The laboring masses of Dibra district, in less than a month, rapidly built 436 new houses, repaired 527 houses that were severely damaged, and 2,755 houses that were lightly damaged. In addition, they built 16 new school buildings and repaired 137 economic, social, and cultural buildings.

### 在劳动党的亲切关怀下  阿人民战胜地震灾害  经受住严重考验表现出英雄气概

1968-01-01
第6版()
专栏：

    在劳动党的亲切关怀下
    阿人民战胜地震灾害
    经受住严重考验表现出英雄气概
    新华社地拉那三十日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道：十一月三十日受到地震灾害的阿尔巴尼亚利布拉什德区和迪勃拉区的广大劳动人民，在劳动党中央、政府和全国人民的关怀下，经过紧张和艰苦的劳动，已先后于二十四日和二十八日提前胜利地完成了消除地震灾害的工作。
    利布拉什德和迪勃拉两区的劳动人民分别写信和打电报给阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖恩维尔·霍查同志和部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡同志，报告了这一胜利消息。
    他们在信和电报中指出，这一巨大胜利，是由于劳动党的亲切关怀，由于政府和全国各地劳动人民的极大援助而取得的。这次地震对阿尔巴尼亚人民是一次严重的考验，在这次考验中充分地体现了党和人民的巨大力量，体现了党和人民的坚强团结和一致，体现了阿尔巴尼亚人民的革命英雄主义气概。
    利布拉什德区的劳动人民在二十四天内共修建了两千一百七十七所住房和其他建筑。迪勃拉区的劳动人民在不到一个月的时间内，迅速地建造了四百三十六所新房屋，修好了五百二十七所受到严重破坏的房屋以及两千七百五十五所受到较轻破坏的房屋。此外，还建造了十六所新校舍，修好一百三十七座经济、社会和文化用途的建筑。
