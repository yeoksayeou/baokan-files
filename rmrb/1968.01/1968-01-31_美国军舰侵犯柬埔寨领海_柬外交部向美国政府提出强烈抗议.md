Title: U.S. Warship Violates Cambodian Territorial Waters
Author:
Date: 1968-01-31
Edition: 5

U.S. Warship Violates Cambodian Territorial Waters
Cambodian Foreign Ministry Lodges Strong Protest with U.S. Government

BEIJING, Jan. 30 (Xinhua) -- According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency today, the Royal Government of the Kingdom of Cambodia's Ministry of Foreign Affairs lodged a strong protest with the U.S. government on January 27 over the U.S. warship's pirate-like behavior of violating Cambodian territorial waters.

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Government pointed out that on January 5, a U.S. Navy warship audaciously anchored in Cambodian territorial waters, about one and a half nautical miles from the coast. Despite warnings from the Royal Cambodian Navy's patrol boats, the warship dared to remain in Cambodian waters until nearly 10:30 a.m. on January 6 before leaving.

The Royal Government strongly protests against the U.S. Navy warship's violation of Cambodian territorial waters.

### 美国军舰侵犯柬埔寨领海  柬外交部向美国政府提出强烈抗议

1968-01-31
第5版()
专栏：

    美国军舰侵犯柬埔寨领海
    柬外交部向美国政府提出强烈抗议
    新华社金边三十日电　据柬埔寨新闻社今天报道，柬埔寨王国政府外交部一月二十七日就美国军舰侵犯柬埔寨领海的强盗行为，向美国政府提出强烈抗议。
    王国政府外交部指出，一月五日，美国海军的一艘军舰，竟然在柬埔寨领海内约一海里半的海面上停泊。尽管柬埔寨王家海军的护卫舰提出了警告，但是，这艘军舰竟敢在柬埔寨海面上一直停泊到一月六日近十时三十分才离去。
    王国政府强烈抗议美国海军军舰侵犯柬埔寨领海的行为。
