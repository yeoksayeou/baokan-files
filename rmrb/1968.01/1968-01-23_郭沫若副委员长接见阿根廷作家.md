Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets Argentinean Writer
Author:
Date: 1968-01-23
Edition: 6

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress received Argentinean writer Bernardo Kordon and his wife, who are currently visiting Beijing, this afternoon. He had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见阿根廷作家

1968-01-23
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见阿根廷作家
    新华社二十二日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天下午接见了正在北京访问的阿根廷作家贝尔纳多·克尔顿和他的夫人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
