Title: The Swiss Federal Government Announces a Reshuffle Due to Internal Conflicts
Author:
Date: 1968-01-02
Edition: 6

The Swiss Federal Government announced a reshuffle on December 30, 1967, amid internal conflicts and worsening economic conditions. Monopolistic capitalists have been demanding the replacement of the finance minister. Military Minister Nello Celio will take over from Roger Bonvin as the new finance minister. Roger Bonvin will be reassigned to the Ministry of Transportation, Communications, and Electricity. The current head of that ministry, Rudolf Gnägi, will take over as the new military minister. The reshuffle will take effect in July 1968.

### 瑞士联邦政府因内部矛盾宣布改组

1968-01-02
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

    瑞士联邦政府因内部矛盾宣布改组
    在瑞士经济情况恶化，垄断资本家之间的矛盾加剧并纷纷要求更换财政部长的情况下，瑞士联邦政府一九六七年十二月三十日宣布改组：军事部长内洛·塞利奥接替罗歇·邦万担任财政部长；罗歇·邦万改任交通、运输和电力部长；原交通、运输和电力部长鲁道夫·格纳埃吉改任军事部长。改组将于一九六八年七月起生效。
                            （据新华社讯）
