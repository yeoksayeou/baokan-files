Title: Finnish Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-01-07
Edition: 5

A trade delegation from the Finnish government, led by Deputy Director Usiwilda of the Commercial Department of the Finnish Ministry of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.

### 芬兰政府贸易代表团到京

1968-01-07
第5版()
专栏：

    芬兰政府贸易代表团到京
    新华社六日讯　由芬兰外交部商务司副司长乌西维尔达率领的芬兰政府贸易代表团今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
