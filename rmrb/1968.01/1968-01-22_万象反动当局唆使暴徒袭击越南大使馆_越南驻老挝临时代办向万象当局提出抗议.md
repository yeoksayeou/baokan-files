Title: The Reactionary Government in Vientiane Incites Mob to Attack the Vietnamese Embassy
Author:
Date: 1968-01-22
Edition: 6

Vietnamese Chargé d'Affaires in Laos Protests to Vientiane Authorities

	HANOI, Jan. 21 (Xinhua) - According to the Vietnam News Agency, the Chargé d'Affaires of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Kingdom of Laos, Vu Tien, on January 18, protested to the Vientiane authorities over the incident where reactionary elements, instigated by the Vientiane authorities, threw a hand grenade at the Vietnamese Embassy on January 17.

	Vu Tien pointed out that this incident is a serious provocation against the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The Vientiane authorities must bear full responsibility for this.

	Vu Tien demanded that the Vientiane authorities take necessary measures to immediately stop hostile actions against the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and ensure the absolute safety of the Vietnamese Embassy staff in Laos.

	HANOI, Jan. 19 (Xinhua) - The Vietnam People's Army Daily on January 19 published a commentary strongly condemning the Vientiane reactionary authorities for inciting mobs to attack the Vietnamese Embassy in Vientiane.

	The commentary said that this incident is part of the conspiracy by U.S. imperialism and the Vientiane authorities to oppose Vietnam and sow discord between the Vietnamese and Lao peoples.

	The commentary said that this incident clearly shows that U.S. imperialism and its Lao lackeys are further intensifying their activities against the Vietnamese people. This is a blatant provocation and a crime against the Vietnamese people. The Vientiane authorities must bear full responsibility for this.

	The commentary demanded that the Vientiane authorities immediately stop the destruction and provocation against the Vietnamese Embassy in Vientiane and compensate for all damages.

	HANOI, Jan. 19 (Xinhua) - According to the Vietnam News Agency, on January 17, 1968, at 8:00 PM, the Vientiane authorities in Laos allowed reactionary elements to throw a hand grenade at the Vietnamese Embassy in Vientiane, causing property damage to the embassy.

	This is a violation of international law and a blatant provocation against the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 万象反动当局唆使暴徒袭击越南大使馆  越南驻老挝临时代办向万象当局提出抗议

1968-01-22
第6版()
专栏：

    万象反动当局唆使暴徒袭击越南大使馆
    越南驻老挝临时代办向万象当局提出抗议
    新华社河内二十一日电　据越南通讯社报道：越南民主共和国驻老挝王国大使馆临时代办武进，一月十八日就万象当局纵容反动分子在一月十七日向越南民主共和国大使馆投掷手榴弹一事，向万象当局提出抗议。
    武进指出，这一事件是对越南民主共和国的严重挑衅。万象当局必须对此承担全部责任。
    武进要求万象当局采取必要措施，立即制止对越南民主共和国的敌对行动，并确保越南民主共和国驻老挝大使馆人员的绝对安全。
    新华社河内十九日电越南《人民报》十九日发表评论，强烈谴责万象反动当局唆使暴徒袭击越南民主共和国驻万象大使馆的罪行。
    评论说，这个事件是美帝国主义伙同万象当局反对越南和离间越老两国人民的阴谋的一个组成部分。
    评论说，从这个事件中可以清楚地看出，美帝国主义及其老挝走狗正在进一步加紧进行反对越南人民的活动。这是一种明目张胆的挑衅行动，是对越南人民犯下的罪行。万象当局必须对此负全部责任。
    评论要求万象当局立即停止对越南驻万象大使馆的破坏和挑衅，并且赔偿一切损失。
    新华社河内十九日电据越南通讯社报道，一九六八年一月十七日二十时，老挝万象当局纵容反动分子向越南民主共和国驻万象大使馆投掷手榴弹，给大使馆造成财产方面的损失。
    这是违反国际法，对越南民主共和国进行极其明目张胆的挑衅行动。
