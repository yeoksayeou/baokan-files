Title: In the New Year, Let Us Hold High the Great Red Banner of Mao Zedong Thought
Author:
Date: 1968-01-03
Edition: 1

In the new year, the entire Party, the entire army, the revolutionary masses of the proletariat nationwide, and all the people of the nation must hold even higher the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought. We must take Chairman Mao's latest directives as our guideline, muster our strength, strive to excel, and press on with renewed vigor. We must strive for victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in all aspects—ideological, political, economic, and organizational.
