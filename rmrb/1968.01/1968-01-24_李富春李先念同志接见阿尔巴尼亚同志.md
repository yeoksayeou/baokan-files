Title: Comrade Li Fuchun and Li Xiannian Receive Albanian Comrades
Author:
Date: 1968-01-24
Edition: 4

Comrade Li Fuchun, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, and Comrade Li Xiannian, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, received tonight the delegation of Albanian transport workers led by Blonb Kaushi, the delegation of Albanian pesticide experts led by Isuf Dajti Lazi, and Albanian scientist Din Mela. The two comrades had a very cordial and friendly conversation with the Albanian comrades.

Ambassador Natani of Albania to China was present at the reception. (Photo attached)

The photo shows Comrade Li Fuchun and Comrade Li Xiannian receiving the Albanian comrades.

### 李富春李先念同志接见阿尔巴尼亚同志

1968-01-24
第4版()
专栏：

    李富春李先念同志接见阿尔巴尼亚同志
    新华社二十三日讯　中共中央政治局常委、国务院副总理李富春，中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理李先念，今晚接见了正在北京访问的以布隆布·克劳西为首的阿尔巴尼亚交通工作者考察团，以介弗戴特·拉泽为首的阿尔巴尼亚农药考察团，以及阿尔巴尼亚科学工作者迪奈·迈勒亚。
    李富春同志和李先念同志，同阿尔巴尼亚的同志们进行了十分亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使纳塔奈利在座。（附图片）
    图为李富春、李先念同志接见阿尔巴尼亚同志时的合影。		　　新华社记者摄
