Title: Amidst the unprecedentedly favorable situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, various types of Mao Zedong Thought study classes have sprouted up like bamboo shoots after a spring rain, covering the entire country.

Author:
Date: 1968-01-19
Edition: 4

    Under the unprecedentedly favorable situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, various types of Mao Zedong Thought study classes have sprouted up like bamboo shoots after a spring rain, covering the entire country.

    This is a scene from the Longgang Brigade of the Longgang People's Commune in Xingguo County, Jiangxi Province. Revolutionary masses in the Tangbei Production Team are holding a Mao Zedong Thought study class in the fields, seriously studying the brilliant works of Chairman Mao.  Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
