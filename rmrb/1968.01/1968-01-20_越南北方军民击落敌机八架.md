Title: Vietnamese People in the North Shoot Down Eight Enemy Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-01-20
Edition: 5

The military and civilians of North Vietnam shot down eight enemy aircraft.

According to a report from here, the military and civilians of Ha Bac Province in North Vietnam shot down two invading American aggressor aircraft on the 18th and captured a group of American air pirates.

The military and civilians of Ha Tinh Province and Quang Binh Province shot down three enemy aircraft on the 17th.

The military and civilians of Ha Tinh Province shot down one enemy aircraft on the 16th. The military and civilians of Quang Binh Province also shot down one enemy aircraft on the 14th.

According to a supplementary report, the military and civilians of Lao Cai Province shot down one enemy aircraft on the 6th.

### 越南北方军民击落敌机八架

1968-01-20
第5版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落敌机八架
    新华社河内十九日电　据这里公布，越南北方河北省军民十八日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机两架，并活捉一批美国飞贼。
    河静省和广平省军民十七日击落敌机三架。
    河静省军民十六日击落敌机一架。广平省军民十四日也击落敌机一架。
    据补充报道，谅山省军民六日击落敌机一架。
