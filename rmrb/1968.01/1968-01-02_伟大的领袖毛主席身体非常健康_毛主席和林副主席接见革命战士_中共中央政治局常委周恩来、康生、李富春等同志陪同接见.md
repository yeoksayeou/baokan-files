Title: The Great Leader Chairman Mao is in Excellent Health Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin Receive Revolutionary Fighters
Author:
Date: 1968-01-02
Edition: 2

The great leader Chairman Mao is in excellent health. Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin received revolutionary fighters. Members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, and others accompanied the reception.

A red sun rises. The great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms, Vice Chairman Lin Biao, walked onto the stage of the Great Hall of the People with great joy, warmly meeting with over 20,000 revolutionary fighters. Instantly, the entire venue erupted in excitement. Every revolutionary fighter's heart pounded fiercely in their chest, and every happy face greeted the red sun in their hearts, Chairman Mao. Revolutionary fighters from all over the country waved their red "Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong," shouting: "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Long live Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line! Long live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution! Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought! We wish Chairman Mao eternal life!" They expressed their determination to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, carry forward the thorough revolutionary spirit of the proletariat, create new achievements for the country and the people, and seize the comprehensive victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!

New China News Agency, January 1. Our most beloved great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms, Vice Chairman Lin Biao, along with members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, and Li Fuchun, members of the Central Cultural Revolution Group, Chen Boda and Yao Wenyuan, on December 31, 1967, received PLA cadres, cadres from various regions, and representatives of revolutionary masses who were in Beijing attending Mao Zedong Thought study classes. They also received representatives attending the conference of active participants in studying Chairman Mao's works in the PLA Air Force direct-affiliated organs and troops, representatives attending the conference on the publication of Chairman Mao's works, and other professional conferences, as well as representatives from some provincial and autonomous region mass organizations, PLA cadres, and local cadres who came to Beijing for meetings. They also received attendees of the meeting held by the National Defense Science and Technology Commission.

Chairman Mao, Chairman Mao, you are the greatest Marxist-Leninist of our time, the reddest red sun in our hearts. Under your brilliant leadership, the unprecedented Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution has achieved decisive victories. Under your series of latest instructions, the hundreds of millions of military and civilian people across the country are striving forward to seize the comprehensive victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. At this moment, our great teacher, great leader, great supreme commander, and great helmsman, Chairman Mao, and his close comrade-in-arms, Vice Chairman Lin Biao, receiving revolutionary fighters, is the greatest care and encouragement for the military and civilian people across the country, and the greatest happiness.

"A ship on the vast ocean needs a helmsman, revolution needs Mao Zedong Thought." Before the reception, over 20,000 revolutionary fighters from all over the country gathered in the Great Hall of the People, with boundless love, faith, admiration, and loyalty to our great leader Chairman Mao, holding the revolutionary treasure "Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong," reading Chairman Mao's teachings over and over, singing "A Ship on the Vast Ocean Needs a Helmsman," "Father and Mother Are Not as Dear as Chairman Mao," and "Wishing Chairman Mao Eternal Life," awaiting the most blessed moment.

A red sun rises. The great leader Chairman Mao, in excellent health and full of vigor, and Vice Chairman Lin Biao, along with Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, and others, walked onto the stage of the Great Hall of the People with great joy, warmly meeting with revolutionary fighters.

Instantly, the entire venue erupted in excitement. Every revolutionary fighter's heart pounded fiercely in their chest, and every happy face greeted the red sun in their hearts, Chairman Mao. Everyone waved their red "Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong," shouting: "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Long live Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line! Long live the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution! Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought! We wish Chairman Mao eternal life!"

Chairman Mao, with a beaming smile, frequently waved and clapped to the entire audience, extending warm greetings to everyone.

The reception ended. Revolutionary fighters, with hearts full of excitement, wrote down the blessed moment of seeing Chairman Mao on the cover page of their "Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong" and on the back of their portraits of Chairman Mao. They expressed their determination to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, carry forward the thorough revolutionary spirit of the proletariat, create new achievements for the country and the people, and seize the comprehensive victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

Those who participated in the reception also included Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Ye Jianying, Xie Fuzhi, Yang Chengwu, Su Yu, Wu Faxian, Wang Dongxing, Ye Qun, Liu Ningyi, Yu Quli, Li Tianyou, Wang Xinting, Wen Yucheng, Qiu Huizuo, Zhang Chuming, Wang Shusheng, Luo Xunzhi, Liu Huaqing, Cai Shungui, Zhang Zhenhuan, Chen Huatang, Xiao Jinguang, Li Zuopeng, Wang Hongkun, Zhang Xiuquan, Yu Ligang, Liu Jiping, Chen Renqiu, Wu Lie, Huang Zhiyong, Chen Shizhang, Tan Furen, Luo Huasheng, Jiang Wen, Huang Wenming, Li Zhen, Liu Zhong, Song Weizhi, Chen Xilian, Song Renqiong, Chen Xianrui, Liu Geping, Zhang Riquing, Yang Dezhi, Wang Xiaoyu, Du Ping, Li Desheng, Han Xianchu, Liu Peishan, Cheng Shicai, Chen Kang, Luo Yuanfa, Kang Jianmin, Liu Xianquan, Wang Enmao, Saifuding, Guo Peng, Fu Chongbi, Huang Zuozhen, and others.

### 伟大的领袖毛主席身体非常健康  毛主席和林副主席接见革命战士  中共中央政治局常委周恩来、康生、李富春等同志陪同接见

1968-01-02
第2版()
专栏：

    伟大的领袖毛主席身体非常健康  毛主席和林副主席接见革命战士
    中共中央政治局常委周恩来、康生、李富春等同志陪同接见
    一轮红日升起。伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，十分高兴地走上人民大会堂主席台，同两万多名革命战士亲切见面。顿时，全场一片欢腾。一颗颗红心在每个革命战士的胸中激烈跳动，一张张幸福的笑脸迎着心中的红太阳毛主席。来自各地的革命战士，挥动着红彤彤的《毛主席语录》，欢呼：毛主席万岁！万万岁！高呼：毛主席的无产阶级革命路线胜利万岁！无产阶级文化大革命胜利万岁！战无不胜的毛泽东思想万岁！敬祝毛主席万寿无疆！他们表示：一定要更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，发扬无产阶级的彻底革命精神，为祖国、为人民创建新的功勋，夺取无产阶级文化大革命的全面胜利！
    新华社一日讯　我们最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，以及中共中央政治局常委周恩来、康生、李富春同志，中央文化革命小组戚本禹、姚文元同志，于一九六七年十二月三十一日，接见了正在北京参加毛泽东思想学习班学习的中国人民解放军干部、各地党政干部和革命群众的代表，出席中国人民解放军空军直属机关、部队学习毛主席著作积极分子代表大会的代表，出席毛主席著作出版会议和其他一些专业会议的人员，以及来北京开会的一些省、自治区群众组织的代表、解放军干部和地方干部，并且接见了出席国防科委举行的会议的人员。
    毛主席啊，毛主席，您是当代最伟大的马克思列宁主义者，是我们心中最红的红太阳。在您的天才领导下，史无前例的无产阶级文化大革命已经取得了决定性的胜利。在您一系列最新指示的指引下，全国亿万军民正在为夺取无产阶级文化大革命全面胜利而奋勇前进。在这个时候，我们的伟大导师、伟大领袖、伟大统帅、伟大舵手毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，接见革命战士，这是对全国军民的最大关怀、最大鼓舞，是全国军民最大的幸福。
    “大海航行靠舵手，干革命靠毛泽东思想”。在接见之前，来自各地的两万多革命战士，聚集在人民大会堂，怀着对伟大领袖毛主席无限热爱、无限信仰、无限崇拜、无限忠诚的心情，手捧革命宝书《毛主席语录》，一遍一遍地朗读毛主席的教导，高唱《大海航行靠舵手》、《爹亲娘亲不如毛主席亲》、《祝福毛主席万寿无疆》，等待最幸福的时刻的到来。
    一轮红日升起。伟大领袖毛主席，身体非常健康，神采奕奕，和林彪副主席以及周恩来、康生、李富春等同志，十分高兴地走上人民大会堂主席台，同革命战士亲切见面。
    顿时，全场一片欢腾。一颗颗红心在每个革命战士的胸中激烈跳动，一张张幸福的笑脸迎着心中的红太阳毛主席。大家挥动着红彤彤的《毛主席语录》，欢呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万万岁！”高呼：“毛主席的无产阶级革命路线胜利万岁！”“无产阶级文化大革命胜利万岁！”“战无不胜的毛泽东思想万岁！”“敬祝毛主席万寿无疆 ！”
    毛主席满面笑容，频频向全场同志招手、鼓掌，向大家亲切致意。
    接见结束了，革命战士们怀着无比激动的心情，把见到毛主席的幸福时刻，写在《毛主席语录》的扉页上，写在随身带着的毛主席像片的背面。他们表示，一定要更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，发扬无产阶级的彻底革命精神，为祖国、为人民创建新的功勋，夺取无产阶级文化大革命的全面胜利。
    参加接见的，还有陈毅、李先念、徐向前、聂荣臻、叶剑英、谢富治、杨成武、粟裕、吴法宪、汪东兴、叶群、刘宁一、余秋里、李天佑、王新亭、温玉成、邱会作、张池明、王树声、罗舜初、刘华清、蔡顺礼、张震寰、陈华堂、萧劲光、李作鹏、王宏坤、张秀川、余立金、刘锦平、陈仁麒、吴烈、黄志勇、陈士榘、谭甫仁、罗华生、江文、黄文明、李真、刘忠、宋维栻、陈锡联、宋任穷、陈先瑞、刘格平、张日清、杨得志、王效禹、杜平、李德生、韩先楚、刘培善、程世清、陈康、罗元发、康健民、刘贤权、王恩茂、赛福鼎、郭鹏、傅崇碧、黄作珍等同志。
