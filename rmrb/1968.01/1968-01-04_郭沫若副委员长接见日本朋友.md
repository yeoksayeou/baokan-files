Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets with Japanese Friends
Author:
Date: 1968-01-04
Edition: 6

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo this morning received a delegation from the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, led by Shirakawa Gofu. He had a friendly talk with them.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本朋友

1968-01-04
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本朋友
    新华社二日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，今天上午接见了由白土吾夫率领的日本中国文化交流协会活动家代表团，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
