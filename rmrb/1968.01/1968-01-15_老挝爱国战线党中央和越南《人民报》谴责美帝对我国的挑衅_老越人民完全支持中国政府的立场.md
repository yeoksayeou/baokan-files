Title: The Lao Patriotic Front Central Committee and the Vietnamese People's Daily Condemn U.S. Imperialist Provocations Against Our Country
Author:
Date: 1968-01-15
Edition: 6

The Lao Patriotic Front Central Committee and the Vietnamese People's Daily condemn U.S. imperialist provocations against our country

The Lao and Vietnamese peoples fully support the Chinese government's stance

KANGPHANG, Jan. 13 (Xinhua) — According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio station, the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front issued a statement on Jan. 10 strongly condemning the U.S. imperialists' despicable act of sending pirate planes to bomb the Chinese cargo ship Hongqi 158 docked at the port of Cam Pha in Vietnam.

The statement said that the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front and the entire Lao people enthusiastically support the statement issued by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China on Jan. 7, 1968.

The statement pointed out that the above-mentioned actions by U.S. imperialism indicate that it is making every effort to provoke war against the People's Republic of China.

The statement warned that U.S. imperialism must immediately stop all provocations and acts of war against the People's Republic of China, otherwise it will be fully responsible for all consequences arising from its mad actions.

HANOI, Jan. 14 (Xinhua) — The Vietnamese People's Daily published a commentary on Jan. 14 strongly condemning the serious war provocation by U.S. imperialist and right-wing Lao pirate planes that bombed the Miao village area in Yunnan Province, China, on Jan. 7.

The commentary pointed out that the bombing of the Miao village area in Yunnan Province by U.S. and right-wing Lao planes is a premeditated action by U.S. imperialism and is part of its plan to provoke war against the People's Republic of China. This act of war provocation is linked to the U.S. plan to escalate its aggression in Indochina.

The commentary emphasized that the Vietnamese people fully support the stance elucidated in the statement issued by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China on Jan. 12, 1968.

The commentary pointed out that the recent mad provocations by U.S. imperialism and its lackeys against the Vietnamese people, the Cambodian people, the Lao people, and China further expose the extremely stubborn warlike nature of U.S. imperialism. These mad actions only indicate that it has fallen into a desperate plight.

### 老挝爱国战线党中央和越南《人民报》谴责美帝对我国的挑衅  老越人民完全支持中国政府的立场

1968-01-15
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国战线党中央和越南《人民报》谴责美帝对我国的挑衅
    老越人民完全支持中国政府的立场
    新华社康开十三日电 据巴特寮电台广播：老挝爱国战线党中央委员会发言人十日发表声明，强烈谴责美国派遣强盗飞机轰炸中国停泊在越南锦普港口的货船红旗一五八号的滔天罪行。
    声明说，老挝爱国战线党中央委员会和全体老挝人民热烈支持中华人民共和国外交部一九六八年一月七日的声明。
    声明指出，美帝国主义的上述行动表明，它正在千方百计地对中华人民共和国进行战争挑衅。
    声明警告美帝国主义必须立即停止对中华人民共和国的一切挑衅和战争行动，否则，它将对由它的疯狂行动所引起的一切后果承担全部责任。
    新华社河内十四日电越南《人民报》十四日发表评论，强烈谴责美帝国主义和老挝右派的强盗飞机七日轰炸中国云南省苗寨地区的严重战争挑衅行动。
    评论指出，美帝和老挝右派的飞机轰炸中国云南省苗寨地区的事件，是美帝国主义的一种预谋的行动，是它对中华人民共和国进行战争挑衅计划的一个组成部分。这次战争挑衅是同美国在印度支那扩大侵略战争的“升级”计划连在一起的。
    评论强调说，越南人民完全支持中华人民共和国外交部在一九六八年一月十二日的声明中所阐明的立场。
    评论指出，美帝国主义及其走狗最近对越南人民、柬埔寨人民和老挝人民以及对中国的疯狂挑衅活动，进一步暴露了美帝国主义极其顽固的好战本性。这些疯狂的行动，只能说明它已陷入绝望的困境。
