Title: South Vietnamese Military and Civilians Annihilated 360,000 Enemy Troops Last Year
Author:
Date: 1968-01-03
Edition: 6

The heroic military and civilian population of South Vietnam, harnessing the immense power of people's war, continuously and bravely struck at the enemy throughout 1967, achieving tremendous victories. According to incomplete statistics, they annihilated nearly 365,000 enemy troops, including over 170,000 U.S. aggressor troops and puppet soldiers.

Among the annihilated enemy forces were: 44 infantry battalions (including 17 U.S. battalions and 1 puppet battalion), 6 artillery battalions, 3 engineer battalions, 20 armored battalions, 378 infantry companies (including 101 U.S. companies), and 680 platoons (including 164 U.S. platoons).

During this year, the South Vietnamese military and civilians shot down and destroyed 3,200 enemy aircraft, destroyed 8,500 military vehicles (including over 4,000 armored vehicles), destroyed 730 cannons, over 200 ships and boats, 38 locomotives, 101 train cars, blew up over 560 bridges, and numerous ammunition and fuel depots. They also seized a large amount of enemy weapons and military supplies.

Compared to 1965, the total number of enemy troops annihilated by the South Vietnamese military and civilians in 1967 increased by more than onefold, the number of U.S. and puppet troops annihilated increased by nearly eightfold, the number of armored vehicles destroyed increased by nearly ninefold, and the number of enemy aircraft shot down and destroyed increased by one and a half times. Compared to 1966, the total number of enemy troops annihilated in 1967 increased by one-third, the number of U.S. and puppet troops annihilated increased by about 50%, the number of armored vehicles destroyed increased by two and a half times, the number of enemy aircraft shot down and destroyed increased by more than 50%, and the number of ships and boats destroyed increased by onefold.

### 南越军民去年歼敌三十六万  歼灭美军和帮凶军十七万　击落击毁敌机三千二百架

1968-01-03
第6版()
专栏：

    南越军民去年歼敌三十六万
    歼灭美军和帮凶军十七万　击落击毁敌机三千二百架
    新华社河内一日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，英雄的越南南方军民，发挥人民战争的无比威力，在一九六七年一年中连续地、英勇地打击敌人，取得了极其巨大的胜利。据不完全统计，共歼灭敌人近三十六万五千名，其中包括美国侵略军和帮凶军十七万多名。
    在被歼灭的敌军中包括：全歼四十四个步兵营（其中美军十七个营和帮凶军一个营），六个炮兵营，三个工兵营，二十个装甲营，三百七十八个步兵连（其中美军一百零一个连），六百八十个排（其中美军一百六十四个排）。
    在这一年中，越南南方军民击落击毁敌机三千二百架，击毁各种军车八千五百辆（其中装甲车四千多辆），击毁大炮七百三十门，船艇二百多艘，火车头三十八台，车厢一百零一节，炸毁桥梁五百六十多座和许多弹药和燃料库，同时还缴获敌人的大批武器和军用物资。
    同一九六五年相比，一九六七年越南南方军民歼敌总人数增加一倍多，歼灭的美军和帮凶军人数增加近八倍，击毁的装甲车增加近九倍，击落击毁的敌机增加一倍半。同一九六六年相比，一九六七年的歼敌总数增加了三分之一，歼灭的美军和帮凶军人数增加约百分之五十，击毁的装甲车增加二倍半，击落击毁的敌机增加百分之五十以上，击毁的船艇增加一倍。
