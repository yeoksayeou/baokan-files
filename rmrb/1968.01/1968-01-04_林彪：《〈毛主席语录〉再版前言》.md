Title: Lin Biao: Preface to the Reprint of "Quotations from Chairman Mao"
Author: Lin Biao
Date: 1968-01-04
Edition: 1

	Permanently hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, arm the minds of the people of the entire country with Mao Zedong Thought, and insist on using Mao Zedong Thought to lead in all work. This is the most fundamental task of our Party's political and ideological work.
