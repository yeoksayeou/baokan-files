Title: Chinese Ambassador Meets Egyptian State Secretary of Foreign Affairs Expressing China's Support for Arab People's Anti-Aggression Struggle
Author:
Date: 1967-05-25
Edition: 6

Chinese Ambassador Huang Hua expressed on May 21 in Cairo that the Chinese government and people have consistently supported the Arab people's struggle against the aggression of U.S. imperialism and its tool, Israel.

Huang Hua, in a meeting with Ahmed Hassan Feki, the State Secretary of the Egyptian Foreign Ministry, said that the Chinese government and people support the Egyptian government's recent just stance against the provocations of U.S. imperialism and its tool, Israel, as well as Egypt's legitimate demand for the withdrawal of the United Nations Emergency Force from the Gaza Strip and the Sinai Peninsula to defend its sovereignty and oppose Israel's aggression against Arab countries.

Feki briefed Huang Hua on the current situation and the steps taken by the Egyptian government to support Syria against Israel's armed threats. He expressed gratitude for the friendly attitude of the Chinese government and people.

### 我大使会见阿联外交部国务秘书表示  中国政府和人民一贯支持阿拉伯人民的反侵略斗争

1967-05-25
第6版()
专栏：

    我大使会见阿联外交部国务秘书表示
    中国政府和人民一贯支持阿拉伯人民的反侵略斗争
    新华社开罗二十二日电　中国驻阿联大使黄华二十一日在这里表示，中国政府和人民一贯支持阿拉伯人民为反对美帝国主义及其工具以色列的侵略而进行的斗争。
    黄华大使在会见阿联外交部国务秘书艾哈迈德·哈桑·费基时说，中国政府和人民支持阿联政府最近对美帝国主义及其工具以色列的挑衅所采取的正义立场，以及阿联政府为保卫自己的主权和反对以色列对阿拉伯国家的侵略而提出的联合国紧急部队撤出加沙地区和西奈地区的正当要求。
    费基向黄华大使说明了当前局势以及阿联政府为支持叙利亚反对以色列武装威胁所采取的步骤。他表示感谢中国政府和人民的友好态度。
