Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Seven American Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1967-05-23
Edition: 5

The heroic military and civilians of Hanoi City and Ha Bac Province, with high morale and increasing experience, shot down seven American aggressor aircraft and damaged several more on May 21. They also annihilated and captured many American air pirates.

On May 21, the sky over the Hanoi region was clear. At 2 PM, the American aggressors dispatched multiple waves of aircraft to attack densely populated residential areas in the city center and suburbs.

The military and civilians of Hanoi, fueled by deep hatred for the enemy, immediately launched a fierce counterattack. Various armed forces coordinated closely, striking the enemy aircraft from different directions, achieving the glorious victory of shooting down five American aggressor aircraft and damaging several more.

On the same day, the military and civilians of Ha Bac Province also shot down two American aggressor aircraft.

### 越南北方军民又击落美机七架

1967-05-23
第5版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民又击落美机七架
    新华社河内二十一日电　英雄的河内市和河北省军民，斗志昂扬，越战越有经验，二十一日又以猛烈的火力击落美国强盗飞机七架，击伤多架，并且消灭和活捉了许多美国飞贼。
    二十一日，河内地区天空晴朗。下午两点钟，美国强盗派遣多批飞机，两次袭击市中心和郊区的许多居民稠密区。
    河内市军民怀着对敌人的高度仇恨，立即给敌人以猛烈的反击。各种武装力量紧密配合，从不同的方向猛击敌机，取得了击落美国强盗飞机五架、击伤多架的辉煌胜利。
    同一天，河北省军民也击落美国强盗飞机二架。
