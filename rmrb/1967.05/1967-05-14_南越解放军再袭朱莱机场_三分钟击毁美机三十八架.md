Title: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Strikes Jule Airbase Again
Author:
Date: 1967-05-14
Edition: 5

The South Vietnamese Liberation Army once again attacked Jule Airbase. In just three minutes, they destroyed 38 American aircraft.

	Hanoi, May 12 — According to a report from the Liberation Communications Agency of South Vietnam, the liberation armed forces of Quang Nam Province in South Vietnam launched a fierce artillery attack on Jule American military airbase on the night of April 11. They destroyed 38 enemy aircraft and killed 475 American invaders.

	The report stated that the liberation armed forces' mortar shells accurately hit the center of the airbase and an American brigade command post. In just three minutes of battle, they achieved the above-mentioned great victory. Most of the destroyed enemy aircraft were jet planes, and many of the killed and wounded American invaders were pilots and technical personnel. Additionally, the liberation armed forces also destroyed three bunkers and one artillery position.

	This was the sixth attack by the liberation armed forces of Quang Nam Province on Jule Airbase since October 1965.

	According to statistics, in the six attacks, the liberation armed forces have destroyed a total of 220 enemy aircraft and killed more than 1,500 American invaders.

### 南越解放军再袭朱莱机场  三分钟击毁美机三十八架

1967-05-14
第5版()
专栏：

    南越解放军再袭朱莱机场
    三分钟击毁美机三十八架
    新华社河内十二日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方广南省解放武装力量，四月十一日深夜猛烈炮轰朱莱美军机场，击毁敌机三十八架，消灭美国侵略军四百七十五名。
    报道说，解放武装力量的迫击炮弹准确地击中机场中心区和美军的一个旅指挥所，仅用三分钟的战斗就取得了上述巨大战果。被击毁的敌机大部分是喷气式飞机，被打死打伤的美国侵略军中许多是飞行员和技术人员。另外，解放武装力量还摧毁碉堡三座和炮台一座。
    这是广南省解放武装力量从一九六五年十月以来对朱莱机场进行的第六次袭击。
    据统计，在六次袭击中，解放武装力量共击毁敌机二百二十架，消灭美国侵略军一千五百多名。
