Title: U.S.-Soviet Military Collusion Targets China
	Conspiring to Establish an "International Anti-Missile System"
Author:
Date: 1967-05-16
Edition: 6

	Washington (New China News Agency) — Within the scope of the U.S.-Soviet global deal, the Johnson administration is conspiring with the Soviet revisionist leadership to engage in a new military collusion — establishing a so-called "international anti-missile system" aimed at China.

	On May 9, U.S. Secretary of State Dean Rusk revealed the Johnson administration's plan to the Senate's "Disarmament" Subcommittee. He said that the U.S. is considering establishing an "international anti-missile system" to provide "some security guarantees" for the U.S. and the Soviet Union. It is known that U.S. imperialism is the world's largest aggressor, and the Soviet revisionist leadership is the chief accomplice of U.S. imperialism. The U.S.'s new conspiracy and the "security guarantees" it advocates are actually a plot by these two nuclear powers to tighten their collusion, oppress the revolutionary peoples of the world, and specifically target China. U.S. Deputy Secretary of Defense Cyrus Vance explicitly admitted in the subcommittee that the U.S. plan to establish an "international anti-missile system" is aimed at "dealing with China."

	Meanwhile, U.S. Senator Albert Gore proposed that the U.S. could invite the Soviet Union to cooperate with the U.S. to deploy a "sparse anti-ballistic missile line" from Greenland (northeast of Canada) to Kamchatka (the easternmost part of the Soviet Union), and place it under the supervision of an international organization, preferably the United Nations. This "defense line" would cover all fixed missile launch bases of the U.S. and the Soviet Union.

	U.S. newspapers had recently advocated that the U.S. would deploy an "anti-missile missile barrier" in the Pacific to counter China, and the Soviet Union should also "do some minimal things" to counter China. Rusk's recent revelation of the U.S. government's new conspiracy precisely confirms these statements by U.S. newspapers.

### 美苏军事勾结矛头针对中国  阴谋建立“国际反导弹系统”

1967-05-16
第6版()
专栏：

    美苏军事勾结矛头针对中国
    阴谋建立“国际反导弹系统”
    新华社讯　华盛顿消息：在美苏全球大交易的范围内，美国约翰逊政府正阴谋同苏联修正主义领导集团在军事方面进行一项新的勾结——建立一个矛头针对中国的所谓“国际反导弹系统”。
    美国国务卿腊斯克九日在美国参议院“裁军”小组委员会透露了约翰逊政府的这个打算。他说，美国正考虑建立一个“国际反导弹系统”，以便给美苏两国“提供某种安全保障”。人们知道，美帝国主义是世界上最大的侵略者，而苏联修正主义领导集团是美帝国主义的头号帮凶，美国的新阴谋和它所鼓吹的“安全保障”，实际上就是这两个核霸王加紧勾结，对付世界革命人民，特别是把矛头针对中国。美国国防部副部长万斯在这个小组委员会上就明白地承认，美国关于建立“国际反导弹系统”的计划是为了“对付中国”的。
    与此同时，美国参议员戈尔还提出，美国可以邀请苏联同美国合作，从格陵兰（在加拿大东北方）到堪察加（苏联最东部）部署一条“稀疏的反弹道导弹线”，并且把它置于一个国际机构，“最好是联合国”的监督下。这条“防线”将分管美苏两国所有的固定导弹发射基地。
    美国报纸不久前就曾经鼓吹美国将在太平洋部署对付中国的“反导弹导弹屏障”，苏联也要为对付中国而“做一些起码的事”。腊斯克现在透露的美国政府的新阴谋，恰恰证实了美国报纸的这些说法。
