Title: Nasser Warns U.S. Not to Interfere with UAR Sovereignty
Author:
Date: 1967-05-30
Edition: 4

Nasser Warns U.S. Not to Interfere with UAR Sovereignty
Middle East Tensions Due to U.S. Support for Israeli Provocations
UAR Government Orders "UN Emergency Force" to Leave Within 48 Hours

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 28th - UAR President Nasser warned American imperialism on the 28th: "If the United States interferes with our sovereignty, we will resist this interference with all our strength."

At a press conference he held, Nasser said: "The Strait of Tiran is Egyptian territorial waters, and we have actually exercised sovereignty over the Strait of Tiran." He pointed out: "No force in the world, no matter how powerful, can destroy our sovereignty over this strait or ignore our sovereignty over this strait."

President Nasser strongly condemned the U.S. for concocting and supporting Israel, and inciting Israel to provoke Syria and other Arab countries. He pointed out that this is the root cause of the tensions in the Middle East.

Nasser pointed out that the Arab people will never give up the rights of the Palestinian people. He said: "We do not accept any way of coexistence with Israel."

President Nasser said: "The Arab people should recognize their friends and enemies. Those who support Israel are our enemies; those who stand on our side are our friends."

Nasser said: "If Israel launches any aggression against any Arab country, then we will not consider it a limited aggression, but a full-scale war." "If the United States invades the UAR or violates its sovereignty, then the UAR will defend its sovereignty and resist this aggression."

Nasser also said that the issue of withdrawing the "UN Emergency Force" is not up for discussion, because the "UN Emergency Force" entered the UAR with the consent of the UAR. Now the UAR has withdrawn its consent. Therefore, the "UN Emergency Force" must withdraw unconditionally.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 28th - According to the UAR's *Al-Ahram* newspaper, the UAR formally demanded on the 27th that the Canadian contingent of the "UN Emergency Force" in the UAR must withdraw from UAR territory within 48 hours.

UAR Armed Forces Chief of Staff Fawzi had already notified the so-called commander of the "UN Emergency Force," Indian General Rikhye, of the UAR's decision on the 27th.

The newspaper said that the UAR took this decision because the Canadian government had taken a suspicious, unreasonable, and warlike attitude towards the United Arab Republic, and because the Canadian government had taken a stance supporting Israel.

On May 16th, at the order of UAR Armed Forces Deputy Commander Amer, Chief of Staff Fawzi had already demanded the withdrawal of the "UN Emergency Force" to enable all UAR armed forces to "be prepared to take immediate action against Israel if it invades any Arab country."

On the 18th, UAR Foreign Minister Riad announced that the UAR had decided not to allow the "UN Emergency Force" to continue to stay in UAR territory and the Gaza Strip.

After this, the Canadian government actually opposed the withdrawal of the "UN Emergency Force" and proposed that the United Nations intervene in this matter.

This so-called "UN Emergency Force" was established by the United Nations General Assembly in November 1956, when the UAR nationalized the Suez Canal Company, and Britain, France, and Israel launched a military invasion of Egypt. Since the "UN Emergency Force" entered the Gaza Strip and the Strait of Tiran's Sharm el-Sheikh, it has always acted as a tool of U.S. imperialism, supporting Israel against the Arab people.

### 纳赛尔总统警告美国不得干涉阿联主权  中东局势紧张是由于美国支持以色列挑衅  阿联政府限令“联合国紧急部队”在四十八小时内滚出去

1967-05-30
第4版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔总统警告美国不得干涉阿联主权
    中东局势紧张是由于美国支持以色列挑衅
    阿联政府限令“联合国紧急部队”在四十八小时内滚出去
    新华社开罗二十八日电　阿联总统纳赛尔二十八日警告美帝国主义说：“如果美国干涉我国主权，我们将以全部力量抵抗这种干涉。”
    纳赛尔在他举行的记者招待会上说：“蒂朗海峡是埃及的领海，我们实际上已经对蒂朗海峡行使了主权。”他指出：“世界上任何力量，不管它多么强大，都不可能破坏我们对这个海峡的主权或者漠视我们对这个海峡的主权。”
    纳赛尔总统强烈谴责美国炮制和支持以色列，并唆使以色列对叙利亚和其他阿拉伯国家进行挑衅。他指出，这是中东局势紧张的根源。
    纳赛尔指出，阿拉伯人民决不会放弃巴勒斯坦人民的权利。他说：“我们不接受同以色列共处的任何办法。”
    纳赛尔总统说：“阿拉伯人民应当认清他们的朋友和敌人。凡是支持以色列的，就是我们的敌人；凡是站在我们一边的，就是我们的朋友。”
    纳赛尔说：“如果以色列对任何阿拉伯国家发动任何侵略，那么我们将不认为它是有限的侵略，而认为它是全面的战争。”“如果美国侵略阿联或者侵犯它的主权，那么阿联将保卫它的主权，反对这种侵略。”
    纳赛尔还说，撤退“联合国紧急部队”的问题是不容讨论的，因为“联合国紧急部队”进驻阿联是以阿联的同意为条件的。现在阿联已经撤销了它的同意。因此，“联合国紧急部队”必须无条件地撤出。
    新华社开罗二十八日电　据阿联《金字塔报》报道，阿联二十七日正式要求在阿联的“联合国紧急部队”的加拿大部队必须在四十八小时之内撤离阿联领土。
    阿联武装部队参谋长法齐二十七日已把阿联的这个决定通知了“联合国紧急部队”的所谓司令官印度人里克耶。
    报纸说，阿联采取这一决定是由于“加拿大政府对阿拉伯联合共和国采取了怀疑的无理的和好战的态度”，“是由于加拿大政府采取了支持以色列的态度”。
    阿联武装部队参谋长法齐奉阿联武装部队副总司令阿密尔的命令，在五月十六日就要求“联合国紧急部队”撤退，以便使所有阿联武装部队“做好准备，在以色列侵略任何一个阿拉伯国家时立即对以色列采取行动”。
    十八日，阿联外交部长里亚德又宣布，阿联决定不让“联合国紧急部队”继续驻在阿联领土和加沙地区。
    在这以后，加拿大政府竟反对撤退“联合国紧急部队”，并提出要联合国在这方面进行干预。
    这支所谓“联合国紧急部队”是在一九五六年阿联把苏伊士运河公司收归国有，英、法、以色列三国对埃及发动武装侵略时，由联合国大会在同年十一月决定建立的。“联合国紧急部队”进驻加沙地带和蒂朗海峡的沙姆沙伊赫等地以来，一直充当美帝国主义的工具，支持以色列反对阿拉伯人民。
