Title: Chairman Mao and Comrade Lin Biao Receive Red Diplomats Returning from Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1967-05-02
Edition: 2

Our most, most beloved great leader Chairman Mao and his intimate comrade-in-arms Comrade Lin Biao this evening received on Tiananmen Gate red diplomats, the acting charge d'affaires of our embassy in Indonesia, Comrade Yao Denshan, and the consul general in Jakarta, Comrade Xu Ren, who were expelled from Indonesia by the Indonesian reactionary government.

Comrade Yao Denshan said to Chairman Mao, "We represent all patriotic overseas Chinese in Indonesia and all staff of the Chinese embassy and consulates in Indonesia to greet Chairman Mao and wish Chairman Mao eternal life!"

Comrade Yao Denshan also said that the Indonesian people love Chairman Mao dearly.

Chairman Mao had a cordial conversation with them. Those who accompanied the reception were Comrades Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, and Jiang Qing.

### 毛主席林彪同志接见从印尼回国的红色外交战士  姚登山同志代表印尼全体爱国华侨和驻印尼使领馆同志祝毛主席万寿无疆！

1967-05-02
第2版()
专栏：

    毛主席林彪同志接见从印尼回国的红色外交战士
    姚登山同志代表印尼全体爱国华侨和驻印尼使领馆同志祝毛主席万寿无疆！
    新华社一日讯　我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪同志，今晚在天安门城楼上接见了红色外交战士、被印尼反动政府无理勒令离境的我国驻印尼大使馆临时代办姚登山同志和驻雅加达总领事徐仁同志。
    姚登山同志向毛主席说：“我们代表印尼全体爱国华侨，代表驻印尼使领馆全体同志，向毛主席问好，祝毛主席万寿无疆！”姚登山同志还说，印尼人民无限热爱毛主席。
    毛主席同他们进行了亲切的谈话。陪同接见的，有周恩来、康生、江青同志。
