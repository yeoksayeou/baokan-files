Title: Chairman Shehu Receives Three Chinese Delegations
Author:
Date: 1967-05-07
Edition: 6

Tirana, May 6 (Xinhua) — Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Albania, Shehu, received on May 5 the Chinese Cultural Delegation led by Chinese Ambassador to Albania Liu Xiao, the Chinese Trade Union Delegation led by Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Zhao Guoqing, and the Chinese Photography Delegation led by Shang Jin.

Chairman Shehu had a very cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Albanian Foreign Minister Nasi, Secretary-General of the Central Council of Trade Unions Jakova, Chairman of the Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations Treska, and General Manager of the Albanian News Agency Simidiou.

### 谢胡主席接见我三个代表团

1967-05-07
第6版()
专栏：

    谢胡主席接见我三个代表团
    新华社地拉那六日电　阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席谢胡，五日接见了由中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使刘晓率领的中国文化代表团、由中华全国总工会书记处书记赵国强率领的中国工会代表团和由尚进率领的中国摄影代表团。
    谢胡主席同他们进行了极为亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有，阿尔巴尼亚外交部部长纳赛，工会中央理事会总书记雅科瓦，对外文委主席特雷斯卡和阿尔巴尼亚通讯社社长西米季乌。
