Title: Celebrating the Third Anniversary of the Geneva "Know China Society"
Author:
Date: 1967-05-01
Edition: 2

The Geneva "Know China Society" in Switzerland, chaired by Leon Buffet, held a report meeting today to celebrate its third anniversary. Buffet enthusiastically praised China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, saying that he had seen the breadth and depth of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution guided by Mao Zedong Thought in China. The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is a herald of a new civilization. At present, revolutionary people all over the world are looking to China.

The President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, Chu Tu-nan, and representatives of the revolutionary masses of Beijing attended the report meeting. Chu Tu-nan spoke at the meeting, expressing gratitude to the Swiss friends of the Geneva "Know China Society" for their efforts in promoting Mao Zedong Thought and the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China, and for promoting friendship and mutual understanding between the peoples of China and Switzerland.

After the report meeting, the documentary film "Chairman Mao is the Red Sun in Our Hearts" was screened.

### 庆祝日内瓦“认识中国社”成立三周年  我对外友协举行报告会

1967-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    庆祝日内瓦“认识中国社”成立三周年
    我对外友协举行报告会
    据新华社三十日讯　瑞士日内瓦“认识中国社”主席利昂·布法，今天在对外友协为庆祝日内瓦“认识中国社”成立三周年的报告会上热烈赞扬我国无产阶级文化大革命。他说，他在中国看到了在毛泽东思想指导下的无产阶级文化大革命的广度和深度。无产阶级文化大革命是新文明的预告。目前，全世界革命人民都眼望着中国。
    对外友协主任楚图南和首都无产阶级革命派代表出席了报告会。楚图南在报告会上讲话，感谢日内瓦“认识中国社”的瑞士朋友为宣传毛泽东思想和中国无产阶级文化大革命，为促进中国瑞士两国人民的友谊和相互了解所作的努力。
    报告会后，放映了大型彩色文献纪录片《毛主席是我们心中的红太阳》。
