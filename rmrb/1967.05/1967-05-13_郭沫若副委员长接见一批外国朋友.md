Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets a Group of Foreign Friends
Author:
Date: 1967-05-13
Edition: 6

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress met in the afternoon of May 10 with Japanese writers Yuxiang Zhi and Yingshan Sanlang. Vice Chairman Guo Moruo briefed them on the situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress met in the afternoon of May 11 with the entire delegation of the 14th visit to China by activists from the General Headquarters of the Japan-China Friendship Association (orthodox), led by Zhongcun Jiuyilang, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress met in the afternoon of May 12 with the entire delegation of the Azania (South Africa) Pan-Africanist Congress Women's Delegation led by Mrs. Jie Saigejie, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present were Shen Ziqiu, member of the Standing Committee of the All-China Women's Federation.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见一批外国朋友

1967-05-13
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见一批外国朋友
    新华社十一日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若十日下午接见了日本作家由起重子和影山三郎。郭沫若副委员长向他们介绍了我国无产阶级文化大革命的情况，并同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    新华社十一日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天下午接见了由中村九一郎率领的日中友协（正统）总部第十四次活动家访华代表团全体成员，并同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    新华社十二日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天下午接见杰·赛格杰夫人率领的阿扎尼亚（南非）泛非主义者大会妇女代表团全体成员，和她们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    在座的有全国妇联主席团委员沈兹九。
