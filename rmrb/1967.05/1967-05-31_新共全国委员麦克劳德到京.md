Title: New Zealand Communist Party National Committee Member McLeod Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-05-31
Edition: 4

At the invitation of the People's Daily, Hugh McLeod, a member of the New Zealand Communist Party National Committee and chairman of the editorial board of The People's Voice, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening. Central Committee alternate member Zhao Yimin and responsible person of the People's Daily, Tang Pingzhu, and other comrades went to the airport to give him a warm welcome.

### 新共全国委员麦克劳德到京

1967-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

    新共全国委员麦克劳德到京
    新华社三十日讯　应《人民日报》邀请前来我国访问的新西兰共产党全国委员会委员、《人民之声》编辑委员会主席休·麦克劳德同志，今晚乘飞机到达北京。中共中央候补委员赵毅敏、《人民日报》负责人唐平铸等同志前往机场热烈欢迎。
