Author:
Date: 1967-05-17
Edition: 6

Premier Zhou Meets with Pakistani Guests

Xinhua News Agency, May 16th — Premier Zhou Enlai today met with Pakistani National Assembly member Gohar Ayub Khan and his wife, engaging in friendly discussions.

Present at the meeting were Vice Premier Chen Yi and Deputy Foreign Minister Qian Hanrong.

Pakistan's Chargé d'Affaires at the Chinese Embassy, Chaman, was also present.

After the meeting, Premier Zhou hosted a banquet in honor of the Pakistani guests.

Xinhua News Agency, May 16th — Pakistani National Assembly member Gohar Ayub Khan and his wife, who were invited to visit China, departed Beijing by plane this afternoon to visit southern China.

Vice Premier Chen Yi and Deputy Foreign Minister Qian Hanrong saw them off at the airport. Pakistan's Chargé d'Affaires at the Chinese Embassy, Chaman, also saw them off.

### 周总理接见巴基斯坦客人

1967-05-17
第6版()
专栏：

    周总理接见巴基斯坦客人
    新华社十六日讯　周恩来总理今天接见了巴基斯坦国民议会议员古哈·阿尤布·汗和夫人，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有，陈毅副总理，外交部副部长韩念龙。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆临时代办查曼也在座。
    接见后，周总理设宴招待巴基斯坦客人。
    新华社十六日讯　应邀来我国访问的巴基斯坦国民议会议员古哈·阿尤布·汗和夫人，今天下午乘飞机离开北京到我国南方访问。
    陈毅副总理和外交部副部长韩念龙到机场送行。巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆临时代办查曼也到机场送行。
