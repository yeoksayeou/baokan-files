Title: Wilcock Praises Chairman Mao's Leadership in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-05-07
Edition: 5

New Zealand Communist Party General Secretary Wilcock recently wrote an article enthusiastically praising China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. He used his personal experiences from his visit to China to strongly refute the slanders against the Cultural Revolution by bourgeois newspapers.

In an article published on April 5 in the New Zealand Communist Party's organ, the weekly newspaper "People's Voice," Wilcock pointed out that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, initiated and led by Chairman Mao, will ensure the victory of Marxism-Leninism throughout the world.

He pointed out that the Chinese people are united and responding to Chairman Mao's call. "China is advancing along the revolutionary socialist road with the enthusiastic support of the masses," he said. "Those who want China to return to the capitalist road are doomed to failure."

Wilcock highly praised the new creation of the proletarian revolutionary faction in the Cultural Revolution—the revolutionary committee—as "the most powerful and politically most advanced organ of proletarian dictatorship." He said, "What I saw was the true development of the dictatorship of the proletariat under the comprehensive leadership of Chairman Mao and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China."

He also described the enthusiastic support and backing of the broad masses of the Chinese people for the revolutionary committees.

Throughout the article, Wilcock enthusiastically described what he saw and heard during the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China. He pointed out that the distorted reports by Western bourgeois newspapers about the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution are all nonsense.

He believed that, "At this time, visiting China was a great encouragement for me."

### 威尔科克斯热情称颂毛主席亲自领导的中国文化大革命  中国正在走革命的社会主义道路  中国无产阶级革命派在文化大革命中的新创造——革命委员会是最强有力的、政治上最先进的无产阶级专政机构

1967-05-07
第5版()
专栏：

    威尔科克斯热情称颂毛主席亲自领导的中国文化大革命
    中国正在走革命的社会主义道路
    中国无产阶级革命派在文化大革命中的新创造——革命委员会是最强有力的、政治上最先进的无产阶级专政机构
    新华社讯　新西兰共产党总书记威尔科克斯不久前写文章热情赞扬中国无产阶级文化大革命，并以他访问中国时的亲身感受，严词驳斥资产阶级报刊对文化大革命的污蔑。
    威尔科克斯这篇发表在四月五日的新西兰共产党机关报《人民之声》周报上的文章中指出，由毛主席亲自发动和领导的无产阶级文化大革命，将保证马克思列宁主义在全世界取得胜利。
    他指出，中国人民团结一致，响应毛主席的号召。“中国正在群众的热情支持下走革命的社会主义道路”，“那些想要中国退回到资本主义道路上去的人是没有希望的”。
    威尔科克斯高度评价中国无产阶级革命派在文化大革命中的新创造——革命委员会是“最强有力的，政治上最先进的无产阶级专政机构”。他说：“我看到的是在毛主席和中国共产党中央委员会全面领导下真正的工人阶级专政的发展。”
    他还描述了中国广大群众对革命委员会的热情拥护和支持。
    在整篇文章中，威尔科克斯热情洋溢地描述了他在中国无产阶级文化大革命中的所见所闻，并指出西方资产阶级报刊对中国无产阶级文化大革命的歪曲报道都是胡说。
    他认为，对他来说，“在这个时候访问中国是一个极大的鼓舞。”
