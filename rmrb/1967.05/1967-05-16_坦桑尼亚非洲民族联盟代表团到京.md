Title: Delegation of the Tanganyika African National Union Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-05-16
Edition: 5

A delegation from the Tanganyika African National Union, consisting of Rutakiamirwa, the chief political education officer of the Tanganyika National Service Corps, and Kidda, a member of the Tanganyika National Assembly and assistant general secretary of the Tanganyika Federation of Labour, arrived in Beijing today by plane. The delegation was invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit China for a friendly visit.

### 坦桑尼亚非洲民族联盟代表团到京

1967-05-16
第5版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚非洲民族联盟代表团到京
    新华社十五日讯　由坦桑尼亚国民服务队首席政治教育官鲁塔基亚米尔瓦和坦桑尼亚国民议会议员、坦桑尼亚全国工人联合会助理总书记基达组成的坦桑尼亚非洲民族联盟代表团，应中国人民外交学会邀请前来我国进行友好访问，今天乘飞机到达北京。
