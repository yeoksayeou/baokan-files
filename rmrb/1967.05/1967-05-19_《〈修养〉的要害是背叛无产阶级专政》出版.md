Title: Publication of "The Essence of 'Cultivation' is Betraying the Dictatorship of the Proletariat"
Author:
Date: 1967-05-19
Edition: 3

The important article "The Essence of 'Cultivation' is Betraying the Dictatorship of the Proletariat" by the editorial departments of Red Flag Magazine and People's Daily has been published as a standalone book by the People's Publishing House. Starting from the 19th, it will be distributed in Beijing and will be sequentially distributed nationwide.

### 《〈修养〉的要害是背叛无产阶级专政》出版

1967-05-19
第3版()
专栏：

    《〈修养〉的要害是背叛无产阶级专政》出版
    新华社十八日讯　红旗杂志编辑部和人民日报编辑部重要文章《〈修养〉的要害是背叛无产阶级专政》，已由人民出版社出版单行本，从十九日起在北京发行，并将在全国陆续发行。
