Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-05-30
Edition: 4

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Guo Moruo received and hosted a dinner for a delegation of Japanese social scientists led by Professor Inoue Tsuyoshi, a renowned historian from Kyoto University, and the Director of the General Affairs Office of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, Shiraki Go. The hosts and guests engaged in cordial and friendly conversations.

	In attendance were Japanese peace advocates in Beijing, including Nishizonoji Koichi.

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo today received the entire delegation of the first group of former Japanese residents in China, dispatched by the Headquarters of the Japan-China Friendship Association (Orthodox), led by Hamasaki Isao, who are currently visiting our country. He had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1967-05-30
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社二十九日讯  人大常委会副委员长、中国科学院院长郭沫若，今晚接见并宴请以日本京都大学教授、著名历史学家井上清为首的日本社会科学家代表团，以及日中文化交流协会事务局长白土吾夫。宾主进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    在座的，有在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一。
    新华社二十七日讯  郭沫若副委员长今天接见正在我国访问的由浜崎功率领的日中友协（正统）总部派遣的原在华日侨第一次代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
