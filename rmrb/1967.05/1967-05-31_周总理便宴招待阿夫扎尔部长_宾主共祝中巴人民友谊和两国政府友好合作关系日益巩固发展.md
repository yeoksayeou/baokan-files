Title: Premier Zhou Hosts a Banquet for Minister Afzal
Author:
Date: 1967-05-31
Edition: 2

Premier Zhou hosted a banquet today for Pakistani Defense Minister Afzal Rahman Khan, Admiral, and Pakistani Foreign Affairs Secretary Yusuf and others.

During the banquet, the host and guests had a cordial conversation, toasting to the ever-strengthening friendship between the peoples of China and Pakistan and the ever-deepening friendly cooperation between the two governments.

Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan Muhammad Khan, along with Air Attaché I.H. Khan, Colonel, and Military Attaché Kayum, Colonel, were invited to attend.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Ye Jianying, Deputy Defense Ministers Xiao Jinguang and Wang Shusheng, and Vice Foreign Minister Qian Pengnong attended to accompany the guests.

### 周总理便宴招待阿夫扎尔部长  宾主共祝中巴人民友谊和两国政府友好合作关系日益巩固发展

1967-05-31
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理便宴招待阿夫扎尔部长
    宾主共祝中巴人民友谊和两国政府友好合作关系日益巩固发展
    新华社三十日讯　周恩来总理今天中午设便宴，招待巴基斯坦国防部长阿夫扎尔·拉赫曼·汗海军中将和巴基斯坦外事秘书优素福等。
    席间，宾主亲切交谈，共祝中巴两国人民的友谊和两国政府的友好合作关系日益巩固和发展。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗和大使馆空军武官伊·哈·汗空军上校、武官卡尤姆上校应邀出席。
    国务院副总理陈毅，国防委员会副主席叶剑英，国防部副部长萧劲光、王树声，外交部副部长韩念龙等出席作陪。
