Title: The China-Nepal Friendship Highway Completed and Soon to Open to Traffic
Author:
Date: 1967-05-27
Edition: 7

The China-Nepal Friendship Highway, built with Chinese assistance, has been completed and will soon open to traffic. King Mahendra of Nepal personally cut the ribbon at the grand completion ceremony held on the morning of the 26th.

The ribbon-cutting ceremony for the Kathmandu-Kodari Highway was held at the starting point of the highway in the city of Bhaktapur. Approximately three thousand people from various sectors of Nepal participated in the celebration.

In his speech, Nepal's Minister of Transportation, Vyathit, praised the friendly relations between Nepal and China. He thanked the Chinese experts for their hard work and commended the friendly cooperation between the engineering and technical personnel of the two countries in building this highway.

Lin Haiyun, the head of the Chinese government delegation, praised the friendship between the peoples of China and Nepal in his speech. He also introduced the excellent situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, personally initiated and led by our most, most beloved great leader Chairman Mao. His speech was warmly received.

Those who attended the completion ceremony today included Nepal's Queen Ratna, Prime Minister Suriya Bahadur Thapa, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs N. T. Bista, various ministers, and generals.

Today, the Nepalese people celebrated the completion of the Kathmandu-Kodari Highway as if it were a festival. From Kathmandu to the "Friendship Bridge" on the Sino-Nepalese border, there were decorated arches along the way, and the national flags of China and Nepal fluttered in the wind. Every town and village along the route gathered many people heading to celebrate the highway's opening. Many farmers came from dozens of miles away to towns and villages along the highway to participate in the celebrations.

After the completion ceremony, Prince Basundhara and various ministers, along with four hundred people from various sectors, traveled by car from Kathmandu to the "Friendship Bridge" on the Sino-Nepalese border and entered Chinese territory. They were warmly welcomed by Yang Dongsheng, Vice Chairman of the Tibet Autonomous Region, and other party, government, and military leaders.

Today, the "Friendship Bridge" was adorned with red flags. Many Nepalese people told Xinhua News Agency reporters, "Great Leader Chairman Mao is the brightest sun in the hearts of all the people of the world," and "Long live Chairman Mao forever."

### 中尼友谊公路建成即将通车  尼泊尔各界三千人参加庆典  马亨德拉国王亲自剪彩  尼交通大臣赞扬中尼友好合作和中国专家的辛勤劳动

1967-05-27
第7版()
专栏：

    中尼友谊公路建成即将通车
    尼泊尔各界三千人参加庆典  马亨德拉国王亲自剪彩
    尼交通大臣赞扬中尼友好合作和中国专家的辛勤劳动
    据新华社加德满都二十六日电　中国帮助尼泊尔建设的加德满都—科达里公路已经建成，即将通车。尼泊尔国王马亨德拉在二十六日上午举行的盛大的完工典礼上亲自剪彩。
    加德满都—科达里公路的剪彩庆祝仪式是在这条公路的起点巴德冈市举行的。尼泊尔各界人士共约三千人参加了庆祝仪式。
    尼泊尔交通大臣维亚蒂特在讲话中赞扬尼中两国的友好关系。他感谢中国专家们的辛勤劳动，赞扬修建这条公路的两国工程技术人员的友好合作。
    中国政府代表团团长林海云在讲话中赞扬中尼两国人民的友谊，并介绍最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席亲自发动和领导的中国无产阶级文化大革命的大好形势。他的讲话受到热烈的欢迎。
    参加今天完工仪式的还有，尼泊尔王后拉特纳，首相苏里亚·巴哈杜尔·塔帕，副首相兼外交大臣尼·梯·比什塔，各位大臣和将军们。
    今天，尼泊尔人民象过佳节一样来庆祝加德满都—科达里公路的完工。从加德满都到中尼边界“友谊桥”沿途搭上了彩楼，中尼两国国旗迎风飘扬。沿途每个城镇、村庄都聚集了许多前往庆祝公路通车的人群，许多农民从几十里路远的地方赶到公路沿线的城镇、村庄参加庆祝活动。
    公路完工庆祝仪式举行后，巴松德拉亲王和各大臣们以及各界人士四百多人乘车从加德满都到中尼边界的“友谊桥”，并到了中国境内。他们受到了中国西藏自治区副主席杨东生等党、政、军负责人的热烈欢迎。
    今天，“友谊桥”上红旗飘扬。许多尼泊尔人民对新华社记者表示，“伟大的领袖毛主席是全世界人民心中最红的太阳”，“敬祝毛主席万寿无疆”。
