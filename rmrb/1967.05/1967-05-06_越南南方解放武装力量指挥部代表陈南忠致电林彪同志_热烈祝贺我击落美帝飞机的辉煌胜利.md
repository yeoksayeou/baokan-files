Title: Representative Chen Nanzhong of the South Vietnam Liberation Armed Forces Command Cables Comrade Lin Biao
Author:
Date: 1967-05-06
Edition: 1

Representative Chen Nanzhong of the South Vietnam Liberation Armed Forces Command cabled Comrade Lin Biao on May 2, enthusiastically congratulating the glorious victory of our heroic air force in shooting down five American aggressor planes in the skies over a certain location in Guangxi over the past eight days. The text of the cable is as follows:

To Comrade Lin Biao,
Minister of National Defense,
Beijing, People's Republic of China

We have learned with great excitement and joy that the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force shot down five American aggressor planes invading the skies over a certain location in Guangxi over the past eight days.

This is a stern warning to the American aggressors and a powerful encouragement to the South Vietnam Liberation Armed Forces.

We extend our warmest congratulations to the brilliant achievements of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force and the participating units.

Representative of the South Vietnam Liberation Armed Forces Command
Chen Nanzhong

May 2, 1967, South Vietnam

### 越南南方解放武装力量指挥部代表陈南忠致电林彪同志  热烈祝贺我击落美帝飞机的辉煌胜利

1967-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    越南南方解放武装力量指挥部代表陈南忠致电林彪同志
    热烈祝贺我击落美帝飞机的辉煌胜利
    新华社五日讯　越南南方解放武装力量指挥部代表陈南忠五月二日打电报给国防部长林彪同志，热烈祝贺我英雄空军部队最近在广西某地上空击落美国强盗飞机五架的辉煌胜利。电文如下：北京中华人民共和国
    国防部长林彪阁下
    获悉中国人民解放军空军在过去八天中击落侵犯广西某地上空的美国强盗飞机五架的消息，越南南方解放武装力量感到无比兴奋和喜悦。
    这是对美国强盗的严厉警告，也是对越南南方解放武装力量的一个强有力的鼓舞。
    我们谨向中国人民解放军空军及参战部队的光辉战绩表示热烈的祝贺。
    越南南方解放武装力量指挥部代表
                                               陈南忠
                                一九六七年五月二日于越南南方
