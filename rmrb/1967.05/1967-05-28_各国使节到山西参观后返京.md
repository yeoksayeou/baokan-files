Title: Envoys from Various Countries Return to Beijing After Visiting Shanxi
Author:
Date: 1967-05-28
Edition: 3

Envoys from various countries and their spouses recently visited Shanxi and have returned to Beijing on the morning of May 26. From May 18 to 26, they visited factories, enterprises, and schools in Taiyuan and its surrounding areas. They also visited the tomb of Martyr Liu Huilan in Wenxian County and the Dazhai Brigade of Xixian County's Dazhai Commune.

During their visit, the envoys from various countries and their spouses were warmly received by the Shanxi Provincial Revolutionary Committee, the proletarian revolutionary factions, and the broad revolutionary masses. Leaders of the Shanxi Provincial Revolutionary Committee and representatives from relevant revolutionary factions introduced the great situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in Shanxi Province and the revolutionary masses' new spirit to the envoys. Many envoys highly praised and evaluated the significance and achievements of China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Xu Yixin and representatives of the proletarian revolutionary faction of the Foreign Ministry accompanied the visit.

### 各国使节到山西参观后返京

1967-05-28
第3版()
专栏：

    各国使节到山西参观后返京
    新华社二十六日讯　各国驻华使节和夫人最近到山西参观访问，已于二十六日上午回到北京。他们从五月十八日至二十六日参观访问了太原及其附近地区的工厂、企业、学校，访问了文水县的刘胡兰烈士陵园，以及昔阳县大寨公社大寨大队等。
    各国驻华使节和夫人在参观访问过程中，受到了山西省革命委员会、无产阶级革命派和广大革命群众热情友好的接待。山西省革命委员会的负责人和有关单位革命派代表向各国使节介绍了山西省无产阶级文化大革命的大好形势和革命群众崭新的精神面貌。很多驻华使节对我国伟大的无产阶级文化大革命的意义和所取得的成果给予高度的赞扬和评价。外交部副部长徐以新和外交部无产阶级革命派代表陪同参观。
