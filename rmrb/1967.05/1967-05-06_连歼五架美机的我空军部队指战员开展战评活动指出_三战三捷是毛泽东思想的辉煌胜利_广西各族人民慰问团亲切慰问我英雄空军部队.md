Title: Air Force Unit That Shot Down Five U.S. Aircraft Conducts Battle Review, Stating That Three Victories in Three Battles Are a Glorious Triumph of Mao Zedong Thought
Author:
Date: 1967-05-06
Edition: 2

In just eight days, our People's Air Force hero unit shot down five invading U.S. aggressor aircraft, achieving a brilliant record of three victories in three battles. Recently, the unit has conducted a mass battle review using Mao Zedong Thought. The officers and soldiers unanimously pointed out that this victory is a great victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, a great victory of Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line, and a great victory of the boundless Mao Zedong Thought. It fully demonstrates that the People's Liberation Army, tempered by the Cultural Revolution, has raised the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought even higher and has greatly enhanced its revolutionary fighting spirit. It further proves that the People's Liberation Army is a loyal defender of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and a strong pillar of the dictatorship of the proletariat.

Chairman Mao teaches us, "The decisive factor is people, not things," and "All reactionaries are paper tigers." The officers and soldiers of the combat unit said in their review of the three victories in three battles, "Why were we able to achieve consecutive victories? The fundamental reason is that we resolutely responded to the call of Vice Chairman Lin Biao, held high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, studied and applied Chairman Mao's works actively, emphasized proletarian politics, and carried out a thorough revolution in people's ideology. Especially since the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, they have studied the 'Three Articles' with new problems arising from class struggle, eliminated selfishness and established public spirit, and reformed their worldview, building a 'public' spirit deep in their souls. Only by being selfless can one be fearless, and only by being fearless can one be invincible. This is the fundamental guarantee for their courage and tenacity in air combat and their consecutive victories over the enemy."

In the mass battle review, the broad officers and soldiers always measured themselves against the standard of "completely" and "thoroughly" serving the people. They followed Chairman Mao's teaching, "Modesty helps one to progress; conceit makes one lag behind," and conducted activities to find faults after winning battles, guarding against arrogance and impatience, striving for greater victories. The officers and soldiers unanimously expressed that they would hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, emphasize proletarian politics, carry out a thorough revolution in people's ideology, shoot down enemy aircraft when they come, and study "Self-Cultivation" when they do not come. They will continue to uphold the spirit of daring to fight, fearing no sacrifice, fearing no fatigue, and continuous combat, making new contributions to defending the country's airspace and defending the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, May 5th: Carrying the deep concern of the revolutionary people of all ethnic groups in Guangxi for the heroic air force soldiers, a delegation from various sectors of Guangxi recently formed a delegation to climb mountains and cross rivers, reaching the heroic air force unit that achieved three victories in three battles, and conducted a warm visit.

### 连歼五架美机的我空军部队指战员开展战评活动指出  三战三捷是毛泽东思想的辉煌胜利  广西各族人民慰问团亲切慰问我英雄空军部队

1967-05-06
第2版()
专栏：

    连歼五架美机的我空军部队指战员开展战评活动指出
    三战三捷是毛泽东思想的辉煌胜利
    广西各族人民慰问团亲切慰问我英雄空军部队
    据新华社南宁五日电  八天内连歼五架入侵我国领空的美国强盗飞机，取得三战三捷辉煌战绩的我人民空军英雄部队，最近用毛泽东思想开展群众性的战评活动。指战员一致指出，这一胜利，是无产阶级文化大革命的伟大胜利，是毛主席无产阶级革命路线的伟大胜利，是光焰无际的毛泽东思想的伟大胜利。它充分显示了：经过文化大革命锤炼的人民解放军，更高地举起了毛泽东思想伟大红旗，百倍增强了革命战斗精神。它更加证明了：人民解放军是无产阶级文化大革命的忠实保卫者，是无产阶级专政的坚强柱石。
    毛主席教导我们：“决定的因素是人不是物”，“一切反动派都是纸老虎”。参战部队的指战员们，在评讲三战三捷的胜利时说，我们为什么能连战连捷呢？最根本的原因，就是坚决响应林彪副主席的号召，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，活学活用毛主席著作，突出无产阶级政治，大搞人的思想革命化的结果。特别是在无产阶级文化大革命运动以来，他们带着阶级斗争中的新问题活学活用“老三篇”，破私立公，改造世界观，在灵魂深处建立起一个“公”字。无私才能无畏，无畏才能无敌。这是他们在空战中英勇顽强、接连战胜敌人的根本保证。
    在群众性的战评活动中，广大指战员始终用“完全”、“彻底”为人民服务的标准衡量自己。他们遵循毛主席“虚心使人进步，骄傲使人落后”的教导，纷纷开展打了胜仗找缺点的活动，戒骄戒躁，再接再厉，争取更大的胜利。指战员们一致表示，要更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，大力突出无产阶级政治，大搞人的思想革命化，敌机来了打敌机，敌机不来，批《修养》，继续发扬勇敢战斗，不怕牺牲，不怕疲劳和连续作战的作风，为保卫祖国领空，为保卫无产阶级文化大革命作出新贡献。
    据新华社南宁五日电带着广西各族革命群众对英雄空军健儿们的深切关怀，广西各界人民最近组成慰问团，爬山涉水，深入到三战三捷的英雄空军部队，进行亲切慰问。
