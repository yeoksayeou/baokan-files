Title: China and the UAR Sign the 1967 Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1967-05-27
Edition: 7

The trade agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the United Arab Republic for the year 1967 has been successfully negotiated through friendly consultations and signed in Beijing on May 25. Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Liu Xihuan and Muhammad Mazhar Farid, head of the UAR trade delegation, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments.

Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, Deputy Director of the West Asia and North Africa Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Zhao Yuan, and UAR Ambassador to China Imam attended the signing ceremony.

The UAR trade delegation departed Beijing by plane this morning to visit southern China and then return to their country via Guangzhou.

### 我国和阿联签订今年贸易议定书

1967-05-27
第7版()
专栏：

    我国和阿联签订今年贸易议定书
    新华社二十六日讯　中华人民共和国政府和阿拉伯联合共和国政府一九六七年的贸易议定书，经过双方友好商谈，已圆满地达成协议，并于五月二十五日在北京签字。对外贸易部部长助理刘希文和阿联政府贸易代表团团长穆罕默德·迈扎特·法尔分别代表各自的政府在议定书上签字。
    对外贸易部副部长李强、外交部西亚北非司副司长赵源和阿联驻华大使伊马姆参加了签字仪式。
    阿联政府贸易代表团已于今天上午乘飞机离开北京去我国南方参观访问，然后经广州回国。
