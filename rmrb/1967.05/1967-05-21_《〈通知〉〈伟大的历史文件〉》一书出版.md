Title: Publication of the Book "The Notice" and "The Great Historical Document"
Author:
Date: 1967-05-21
Edition: 1

The book "The Notice" and "The Great Historical Document" has been published. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China's "Notice" (May 16, 1966) and the article "The Great Historical Document" by the editorial departments of the "Red Flag" magazine and the "People's Daily" have been compiled into a book by the People's Publishing House. Starting from May 22, it will be distributed in Beijing and will gradually be distributed nationwide.

### 《〈通知〉〈伟大的历史文件〉》一书出版

1967-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

    《〈通知〉〈伟大的历史文件〉》一书出版
    新华社二十日讯　中国共产党中央委员会《通知》（一九六六年五月十六日）和《红旗》杂志编辑部、《人民日报》编辑部文章《伟大的历史文件》，已由人民出版社编辑成书出版，从二十二日起在北京发行，并将在全国陆续发行。
