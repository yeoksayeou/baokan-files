Title: Chairman Mao and Comrade Lin Biao Receive Comrades from the Revolutionary Committees of Beijing, Shanghai, and Shanxi
Author:
Date: 1967-05-02
Edition: 2

Our most beloved and great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Comrade Lin Biao this evening received in the Tiananmen Gate Tower all the members of the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee and representatives from the Shanghai Municipal and Shanxi Provincial Revolutionary Committees, and had a cordial talk with them. Accompanying the reception were central and other leading comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Xie Fuzhi, Xiao Hua, Yang Chengwu, Jiang Qing, Wang Li, Guan Feng, Chen Boda, and Ye Qun.

### 毛主席林彪同志接见北京上海山西革命委员会同志  周恩来陈伯达康生等同志陪同接见

1967-05-02
第2版()
专栏：

    毛主席林彪同志接见北京上海山西革命委员会同志
    周恩来陈伯达康生等同志陪同接见
    新华社一日讯　我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪同志，今晚在天安门城楼接见了北京市革命委员会全体委员，以及上海市和山西省革命委员会代表团的同志，同他们进行了亲切的谈话。陪同接见的有中央和其他方面的负责同志周恩来、陈伯达、康生、谢富治、萧华、杨成武、江青、王力、关锋、戚本禹、叶群等。
