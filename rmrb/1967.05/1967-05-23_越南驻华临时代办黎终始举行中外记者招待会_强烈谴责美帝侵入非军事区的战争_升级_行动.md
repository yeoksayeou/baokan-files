Title: Vietnamese Chargé d'Affaires Ly Cong Han Holds Press Conference
Author:
Date: 1967-05-23
Edition: 5

Vietnamese Chargé d'Affaires Ly Cong Han held a press conference for Chinese and foreign journalists today, strongly condemning the recent aggressive actions of U.S. imperialist forces, which brazenly invaded the demilitarized zone in southern Vietnam. This is a highly serious escalation of the war.

Ly Cong Han pointed out that the U.S. imperialists' mad actions show that they are waging a full-scale war against the people of the demilitarized zone and pose a direct threat to the ground forces of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

He said that while the U.S. imperialists are madly bombing Hanoi and Haiphong, they have also invaded the demilitarized zone. This further exposes the fraudulent nature of the so-called "peace talks" peddled by the Johnson clique.

Ly Cong Han concluded by saying that no matter how much the U.S. imperialists escalate the war, they will never shake the ironclad resolve of the 31 million Vietnamese people to liberate the South, defend the North, and reunify the country. All the U.S. imperialists' adventurous actions will meet with their deserved punishment.

The press conference also distributed a statement issued by the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on May 21st, condemning the invasion of the demilitarized zone by U.S.-puppet forces.

### 越南驻华临时代办黎终始举行中外记者招待会  强烈谴责美帝侵入非军事区的战争“升级”行动

1967-05-23
第5版()
专栏：

    越南驻华临时代办黎终始举行中外记者招待会
    强烈谴责美帝侵入非军事区的战争“升级”行动
    新华社二十二日讯  越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黎终始今天举行中外记者招待会，强烈谴责美帝国主义侵略军最近悍然侵入越南非军事区南部这一极其严重的战争“升级”行动。
    黎终始指出，美帝国主义的这种疯狂行径，说明它对非军事区人民进行着一场全面的战争，是在地面上对越南民主共和国的直接威胁。
    他说，美帝国主义在疯狂轰炸河内、海防的同时侵入了非军事区，这就由它们自己进一步揭穿了约翰逊一伙向来所兜售的所谓“和谈”的骗局。
    黎终始最后说，不管美帝国主义把战争扩大到什么地步，也绝不能动摇三千一百万越南人民解放南方、保卫北方和统一祖国的钢铁般的决心。美帝国主义的一切冒险行动必将遭到应有的惩罚。
    记者招待会上还散发了越南民主共和国政府二十一日就美伪军侵入非军事区所发表的声明。
