Title: Egyptian President Denounces US Support for Israel and Reiterates Egypt's Readiness to Counter US Economic Pressure
Author:
Date: 1967-05-09
Edition: 6

Egyptian President Nasser strongly denounced U.S. imperialism as the "world's counter-revolutionary leader" during a speech at a mass rally in the industrial center of Shubra al-Kheima near Cairo to celebrate May Day on the evening of May 2.

Nasser said, "We maintain our independence and freedom, which are far more important than anything else."

He continued, "We are prepared to face U.S. economic pressure. If anyone tries to put pressure on us, we will not repay the debts we owe them."

Nasser vehemently condemned U.S. support and backing for Israel, and expressed support for the Palestinian people's struggle against imperialism and Israel. He said, "Our struggle against imperialism and Israel will continue steadfastly until we achieve final victory."

In his speech, Nasser also denounced U.S. provocations against the Yemen Arab Republic and the "Islamic Pact" plotted by U.S. and British imperialism and their tools in the Arab world.

### 阿联总统谴责美帝扶植以色列  重申阿联准备对付美帝经济压力

1967-05-09
第6版()
专栏：

    阿联总统谴责美帝扶植以色列 
    重申阿联准备对付美帝经济压力
    据新华社开罗电　阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔二日晚上在开罗附近的工业中心舒卜拉赫马庆祝“五一”国际劳动节的群众大会上讲话时，谴责美帝国主义是“世界反革命头子”。
    纳赛尔说，我们保持我们的独立和自由，它比其他任何东西都重要得多。
    他接着说，我们准备对付美国的经济压力。他说：“如果任何人企图对我们施加压力，我们就不偿还欠他们的债务。”
    纳赛尔强烈谴责美帝扶植和支持以色列，并表示支持巴勒斯坦人民反对帝国主义和以色列的斗争。他说：“我们同帝国主义和以色列的战斗将顽强地继续下去，直到取得最后胜利为止。”
    在讲话中，纳赛尔还谴责美国及其走狗对也门共和国的挑衅以及美、英帝国主义及其在阿拉伯世界的工具所策划的“伊斯兰条约”。
