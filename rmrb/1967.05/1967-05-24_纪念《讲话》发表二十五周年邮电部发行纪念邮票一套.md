Title: Post and Telecommunications Department Issues a Set of Commemorative Stamps for the 25th Anniversary of the "Talk"
Author:
Date: 1967-05-24
Edition: 4

To commemorate the 25th anniversary of Chairman Mao's "Talks at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art," the Post and Telecommunications Department will issue a set of commemorative stamps starting from the 23rd. The set consists of three stamps with the following designs:

	First Stamp: An excerpt from "Talks at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art": "Our literature and art are for the people's masses, first of all for the workers, peasants, and soldiers. We create for the workers, peasants, and soldiers and we create for them to use."

	Second Stamp: A propaganda team for Mao Zedong Thought in the realm of literature and art.

	Third Stamp: An excerpt from "Talks at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art": "We must make literature and art become a good component of the entire revolutionary machine, as a powerful weapon for uniting the people, educating the people, attacking the enemy, and annihilating the enemy, to help the people unite as one and fight against the enemy."

### 纪念《讲话》发表二十五周年邮电部发行纪念邮票一套

1967-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念《讲话》发表二十五周年邮电部发行纪念邮票一套
    新华社二十三日讯　为了纪念毛主席的《在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话》这一光辉著作发表二十五周年，邮电部从二十三日起发行纪念邮票一套，共三种，图案是：
    第一图：摘自《在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话》一文：我们的文学艺术都是为人民大众的，首先是为工农兵的，为工农兵而创作，为工农兵所利用的。
    第二图：毛泽东思想文艺宣传队。
    第三图：摘自《在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话》一文：要使文艺很好地成为整个革命机器的一个组成部分，作为团结人民、教育人民、打击敌人、消灭敌人的有力的武器，帮助人民同心同德地和敌人作斗争。
