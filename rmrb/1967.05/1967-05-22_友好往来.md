	Friendly Exchanges

	New Zealand Communist Party Delegation Returns Home

	The delegation from the New Zealand Communist Party, led by Ralph Hegeman, a member of the Political Committee of the National Committee of the New Zealand Communist Party, came to China at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to participate in the celebrations of the May Day International Labor Day and to visit various parts of the country. The delegation left Guangzhou for home on May 21.

	Albanian Transport Ministry Delegation Arrives in Beijing

	The delegation from the Albanian Transport Ministry, which came to China to attend the sixth session of the Board of Directors of the Sino-Albanian Shipping Joint Stock Company, is led by Marko Karoli, Deputy Minister of the Albanian Transport Ministry and Chairman of the Albanian side of the Board of Directors of the Sino-Albanian Shipping Joint Stock Company. The delegation arrived in Beijing in the afternoon of May 21.

	Guo Moruo Meets with Japanese Friends

	Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, met with two prominent figures of the Japanese Socialist Party, Tanaka Jin'o and another, on the morning of May 19, and had a friendly conversation with them.

	Secretary of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association Arrives in Beijing

	Ahmad Gora Ibrahim, the Secretary of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association from Azania (South Africa), arrived in Beijing by plane on the evening of May 20.

### 友好往来

1967-05-22
第6版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    新共代表团回国
    由新西兰共产党全国委员会政治委员会委员拉尔夫·赫格曼率领的新西兰共产党代表团，应中共中央邀请前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动，并在我国各地访问后，于二十一日离广州回国。
    阿交通部代表团到京
    前来中国参加中阿轮船股份公司管理委员会第六次会议的阿尔巴尼亚交通部代表团，由阿尔巴尼亚交通部副部长、中阿轮船股份公司管理委员会阿方主任委员马尔科·卡罗利率领，于二十一日下午抵京。
    郭沫若接见日本朋友
    人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，十九日上午接见日本社会党著名人士田中稔男等二人，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    亚非记协一书记到京
    亚非新闻工作者协会书记处阿扎尼亚（南非）驻会书记艾哈迈德·高拉·易卜拉欣二十日晚上乘飞机到达北京。　（据新华社讯）
