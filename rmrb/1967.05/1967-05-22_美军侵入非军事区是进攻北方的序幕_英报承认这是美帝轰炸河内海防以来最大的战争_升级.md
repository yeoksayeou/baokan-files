Title: U.S. Military Invasion of Demilitarized Zone is Prelude to Attack on the North
Author:
Date: 1967-05-22
Edition: 6

A large number of U.S. aggressor troops brazenly invaded the southern part of the Vietnamese demilitarized zone on May 18. This is a premeditated action by U.S. imperialism to escalate the war in Vietnam. It is a more serious "escalation" of the U.S. war of aggression in Vietnam and a prelude to expanding the war to the entire Indochina Peninsula.

The British newspapers *Daily Telegraph* and *Daily Mail* pointed out that the U.S. military's invasion of the southern part of the demilitarized zone is "the largest escalation of the war since the bombing of Hanoi and Haiphong." It may be the "prelude to an invasion of North Vietnamese territory."

However, on May 19, U.S. State Department spokesman Robert McCloskey issued a statement defending the U.S. war escalation, claiming that the invasion of the demilitarized zone was "a purely defensive measure." This is sheer bandit logic. McCloskey's remarks essentially admit that U.S. aggressor troops can use any pretext to invade the demilitarized zone and launch an attack on the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 美军侵入非军事区是进攻北方的序幕  英报承认这是美帝轰炸河内海防以来最大的战争“升级”

1967-05-22
第6版()
专栏：

    美军侵入非军事区是进攻北方的序幕
    英报承认这是美帝轰炸河内海防以来最大的战争“升级”
    据新华社二十一日讯  大批美国侵略军十八日悍然侵入越南非军事区南部，这是美帝国主义扩大侵越战争的一个蓄谋的行动，是美国侵越战争的一次更严重的“升级”，是它要把战争扩大到整个印度支那的一个先声。
    英国《每日电讯报》和《每日邮报》指出，美军对非军事区南部的入侵，“是自从轰炸河内和海防以来最大的战争升级”，它可能是“入侵北越领土的序幕”。
    但是，美国国务院发言人麦克洛斯基十九日发表声明却为美国这一扩大战争的罪行进行诡辩，胡说什么入侵非军事区是“一种纯粹的防御性措施”。这是十足的强盗逻辑。麦克洛斯基的这番话，实际上是供认了美国侵略军可以随便用一个什么借口入侵非军事区，并进而对越南民主共和国发动进攻。
