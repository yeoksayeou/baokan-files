Title: Vietnamese Charge d'Affaires Strongly Condemns U.S. Imperialism's New "Escalation" of War
Author:
Date: 1967-05-21
Edition: 5

Vietnamese Charge d'Affaires Le Cong Binh today held a press conference for Chinese and foreign journalists, severely condemning the crime of U.S. imperialism's bombing and strafing of Hanoi on May 19. Le Cong Binh said at the press conference that U.S. imperialist planes twice invaded the airspace of our capital, Hanoi, on May 19, and brazenly dropped bombs and fired rockets at residential areas in the city and suburbs. This is the eighth time in the past three weeks that U.S. imperialism has bombed and strafed Hanoi, and it is a new "escalation" of the war by U.S. imperialism. He pointed out that U.S. imperialism's "escalation" of the war can only meet with more severe punishment from the Vietnamese people and will end in failure.

Le Cong Binh said, the Vietnamese people are resolutely carrying out the teachings of Chairman Ho Chi Minh, and under any conditions, are determined to fight to the end with U.S. imperialism until final victory is achieved. The bombs of U.S. imperialism cannot shake the iron will of our people to resist the Americans and save the country.

### 黎终始临时代办痛斥美帝新的战争“升级”罪行  美帝炸弹动摇不了越南人民抗战决心

1967-05-21
第5版()
专栏：

    黎终始临时代办痛斥美帝新的战争“升级”罪行
    美帝炸弹动摇不了越南人民抗战决心
    据新华社二十日讯　越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黎终始，今天举行中外记者招待会，严厉谴责美帝国主义五月十九日轰炸和扫射越南河内的罪行。黎终始在招待会上说，美帝国主义飞机五月十九日两次侵入我国首都河内的领空，猖狂地向市内和郊区一些居民区投下了炸弹和发射火箭，这是美帝国主义最近三周内第八次对河内进行轰炸和扫射，是美帝国主义新的“战争升级”。他指出，美帝国主义的“战争升级”，只能遭到越南人民的更严厉的惩罚，并以失败告终。
    黎终始说，越南人民坚决贯彻胡志明主席的教导，不论在任何条件下，决心同美帝国主义打到底，直至取得最后的胜利。美帝国主义的炸弹，决不能动摇我国人民抗美救国的钢铁般的决心。
