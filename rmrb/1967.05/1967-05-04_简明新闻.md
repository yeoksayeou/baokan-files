A Pakistani weekly demands the dismantling of the U.S. imperialist spy agency.

The recent issue of the Pakistani newspaper *Sunday Mail* published an article demanding the smashing of U.S. imperialist spy and subversive activities in Pakistan. The article stated, "It has been confirmed," that organizations such as the Ford Foundation and the American Friends of the Middle East, along with similar other American organizations, "have close ties with the (U.S.) Central Intelligence Agency's spy network." The article emphasized, "Pakistan does not need such organizations," and "they should pack up and go back to their own country."

East Africa to stop circulation of British-issued currency

The Bank of Tanzania recently announced that paper currency issued by the East African Currency Board will cease to circulate in the three East African countries starting September 14, 1967.

The East African currency was first issued in 1921 by the then British colonial authorities in East Africa. For a long time, British imperialism has used the issuance of East African currency to carry out ruthless economic plunder and control over the three East African countries—Tanzania, Uganda, and Kenya.

Explosion at U.S. military base in Thailand destroys 11 U.S. planes

According to Thai newspapers, on April 28, an F-4C fighter-bomber loaded with ammunition and preparing to take off to massacre the Vietnamese people suddenly exploded at the U.S. military base in Ubon, northeastern Thailand. The explosion destroyed another 10 planes on the base, damaged a runway, and destroyed several military buildings. One U.S. aggressor was seriously injured.

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 简明新闻

1967-05-04
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    巴一周报要求取缔美帝间谍机构
    巴基斯坦《星期日邮报》最近一期发表一篇文章，要求粉碎美帝国主义在巴基斯坦的间谍和颠覆活动。文章说，“已经查明”，象福特基金会和美国中东之友协会这些组织和类似的其他美国组织，“同（美国）中央情报局的间谍网有紧密的联系。”文章强调说，“巴基斯坦不需要这种组织”，“要它们卷铺盖回国。”
    东非将停止流通英国发行的货币
    坦桑尼亚银行最近宣布，东非货币局发行的纸币将从一九六七年九月十四日起在东非三国停止流通。
    东非货币是在一九二一年由当时的东非英国殖民当局首次发行的。长期以来，英帝国主义利用发行东非货币，对东非三国——坦桑尼亚、乌干达和肯尼亚进行了残酷的经济掠夺和经济控制。
    泰国美军基地爆炸毁美机十一架
    据泰国报纸报道，四月二十八日，泰国东北部乌汶美军基地一架装满弹药正准备起飞去屠杀越南人民的F4C型战斗轰炸机，突然发生爆炸，使基地上的另外十架飞机也被炸毁，基地内的一条跑道和不少军事建筑物被炸毁，一个美国侵略军受重伤。
                          （据新华社讯）
