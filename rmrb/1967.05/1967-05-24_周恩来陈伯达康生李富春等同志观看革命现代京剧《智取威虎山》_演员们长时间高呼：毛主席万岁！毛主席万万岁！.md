Title: Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun and Others Watch the Revolutionary Modern Peking Opera "Taking Tiger Mountain by Strategy"
Author:
Date: 1967-05-24
Edition: 4

Central and relevant department leaders Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Dong Biwu, Li Xiannian, Xie Fuzhi, Jiang Qing, Xiao Hua, Yang Chengwu, Deng Yingchao, Guo Moruo, Wang Li, Guan Feng, Chen Boda, Ye Qing, and Wang Dongxing, among others, watched the Shanghai Peking Opera Troupe's performance of the excellent model opera, the revolutionary modern Peking opera "Taking Tiger Mountain by Strategy," at the Great Hall of the People this evening.

After the performance, the actors waved red copies of "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and shouted for a long time: "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Chairman Mao is our great savior!" Zhou Enlai and the others walked onto the stage and warmly shook hands with the actors, congratulating them on their successful performance.

To commemorate the 25th anniversary of Chairman Mao's brilliant work "Talks at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art," various theaters in the capital also staged excellent model operas, including the revolutionary modern Peking operas "On the Docks," "The Red Lantern," "Shajiabang," and "The Battle of White Tiger Regiment," the revolutionary modern ballet "The Red Detachment of Women," and the revolutionary modern symphony "Shajiabang."

After the performance, Zhou Enlai and the others walked onto the stage, shook hands with the actors, and took a group photo.

### 周恩来陈伯达康生李富春等同志观看革命现代京剧《智取威虎山》  演员们长时间高呼：毛主席万岁！毛主席万万岁！

1967-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    周恩来陈伯达康生李富春等同志观看革命现代京剧《智取威虎山》
    演员们长时间高呼：毛主席万岁！毛主席万万岁！
    新华社二十三日讯　中央和有关方面负责同志周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春、董必武、李先念、谢富治、江青、萧华、杨成武、邓颖超、郭沫若、王力、关锋、戚本禹、叶群、汪东兴等，今晚观看了上海京剧院在人民大会堂演出的优秀样板戏革命现代京剧《智取威虎山》。
    演出结束以后，演员们挥动红彤彤的《毛主席语录》，长时间地高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万万岁！”周恩来等同志走上舞台，和演员们亲切握手，祝贺他们演出的成功。
    为纪念毛主席的光辉著作《在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话》发表二十五周年，今晚在首都各剧场，还分别演出了优秀样板戏革命现代京剧《海港》、《红灯记》、《沙家浜》、《奇袭白虎团》，革命现代芭蕾舞剧《红色娘子军》、《白毛女》，革命现代交响音乐《沙家浜》。
