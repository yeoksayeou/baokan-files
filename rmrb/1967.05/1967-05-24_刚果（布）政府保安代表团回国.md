Title: Delegation of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Government Security Team Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1967-05-24
Edition: 8

According to Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, May 23rd: The delegation of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Government Security Team, led by Michel Bindi, the head of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Republic's security department, departed Shanghai by plane today to return home. During their stay in Shanghai, Zhang Chunqiao, a responsible person of the Shanghai Revolutionary Committee, received the Congolese (Brazzaville) guests and had lunch with them. The guests also visited the site of the First National Congress of the Communist Party of China and several factories in Shanghai.

### 刚果（布）政府保安代表团回国

1967-05-24
第8版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）政府保安代表团回国
    据新华社上海二十三日电　由刚果（布）保安部门负责人米歇尔·宾迪率领的刚果共和国（布）政府保安代表团，今天乘飞机离开上海回国。在上海逗留期间，上海市革命委员会负责人张春桥接见了刚果（布）客人，并同他们共进午餐。客人们在上海还参观了中国共产党第一次代表大会会址和工厂等。
