Title: Chairman Mao Receives Guests from Congo (Brazzaville)
Author:
Date: 1967-05-17
Edition: 1

Chairman Mao, our most, most beloved great leader, received Michel Bindi, the head of the security department of Congo (Brazzaville), and members of the Congolese (Brazzaville) government security delegation led by him, this afternoon, and had a friendly and cordial conversation with them. Those who participated in the reception included Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier and Minister of Public Security Xie Fuzhi, and others. Ambassador Bazanga of Congo (Brazzaville) to China was also present at the reception.

Our most, most beloved great leader Chairman Mao received Michel Bindi, the head of the security department of Congo (Brazzaville), and members of the Congolese (Brazzaville) government security delegation led by him, on May 16.

### 毛主席接见刚果（布）客人

1967-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见刚果（布）客人
    新华社十六日讯　我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席，今天中午接见刚果（布）保安部门负责人米歇尔·宾迪，以及由他率领的刚果共和国（布）政府保安代表团的成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。参加接见的，有国务院总理周恩来，副总理兼公安部长谢富治等。接见时，刚果（布）驻中国大使巴赞加也在座。
    我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席，五月十六日接见刚果（布）保安部门负责人米歇尔·宾迪，以及由他率领的刚果共和国（布）政府保安代表团的成员。
