Yemen President Denounces U.S. Imperialism's Subversion

Yemen President Abdullah al-Sallal said on May 8 that the crisis in Yemen's relations with the United States is the result of subversive activities carried out by the U.S. "Agency for International Development" according to the plans of the U.S. Central Intelligence Agency.

Sallal made these remarks in response to a question from a U.S. journalist. He said that these subversive activities include: planting mines on Yemeni roads to kill Yemeni people, attacking the presidential palace, and bombing the Technical School in Taiz.

Iraq President Forms New Cabinet

Iraq formed a new cabinet on May 10, with President Abdul Rahman Arif also serving as Prime Minister.

The previous Prime Minister, Naji Talib, recently resigned.

The new cabinet includes four deputy prime ministers: Tahir Yahya, Abdul Gani al-Rawi, Ismail Mustafa, and Fadhil al-Jamali.

West Germans Protest U.S. Aggression in Munich

Residents of Munich, West Germany, held a protest demonstration on May 11 during a gathering organized by the Bavarian state government for the so-called "West German-American Friendship Week." They strongly condemned U.S. aggression in Vietnam. This was the third anti-U.S. protest held by West Germans during the five-day "West German-American Friendship Week."

U.S. Imperialism Intensifies Drafting for Vietnam War

In the context of U.S. imperialism's escalation of the war in Vietnam, the U.S. Department of Defense announced on May 10 that 19,900 people will be drafted into the army in July. This is the highest number of draftees in a single month so far this year. To intensify the drafting of cannon fodder, the U.S. Senate Armed Services Committee even proposed amending the draft law.

### 简明新闻

1967-05-14
第5版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    也门总统谴责美帝搞颠覆
    也门总统萨拉勒八日说，也门同美国的关系发生危机是美国“国际开发署”根据美国中央情报局的计划进行颠覆活动的结果。
    萨拉勒是在回答美国记者向他提出的问题时这样说的。他说，这种颠覆活动包括：在也门公路上埋置地雷来杀害也门人民，袭击总统的官邸，和炸塔伊兹技术学校。
    伊拉克总统组成新的内阁
    伊拉克十日组成新内阁，由总统阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·阿里夫兼任总理。
    前任总理纳吉·塔列布是在最近辞职的。
    新内阁里有四名副总理：塔赫尔·叶海亚、阿卜杜勒·加尼·拉维、伊斯梅尔·穆斯塔法、法德·阿里夫。
    西德人民又举行反美示威
    西德慕尼黑市居民在巴伐利亚州当局十一日为所谓“（西）德—美友好周”举行的集会上，举行抗议示威，强烈谴责美国侵略越南。这是西德人民在西德当局举行的“（西）德—美友好周”期间五天之内举行的第三次反美示威。
    美帝加紧搜罗侵越炮灰
    在美帝国主义加紧扩大侵越战争的情况下，美国国防部十日宣布，今年七月份将征召一万九千九百人入陆军服役。这是今年以来美国征兵人数最高的一个月。为了加紧搜罗炮灰，美国参议院军事委员会甚至提出要修改征兵法。　（据新华社讯）
