Title: Comrade Kang Sheng Meets and Hosts a Banquet for Canadian Comrades
Author:
Date: 1967-05-14
Edition: 5

Comrade Kang Sheng, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, today met and hosted a banquet for Comrade Jack Scott, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Progressive Workers Movement of Canada, and Comrade John Wood, member of the Central Committee, who are visiting China. They had a cordial and friendly conversation.

Present at the meeting were Comrade Zhao Yimin, alternate member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and others.

### 康生同志会见并宴请加拿大同志

1967-05-14
第5版()
专栏：

    康生同志会见并宴请加拿大同志
    新华社十三日讯　中共中央政治局常委康生同志，今天会见并宴请了来我国访问的加拿大进步工人运动中央委员会主席杰克·斯科特同志和中央委员约翰·伍德同志，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    在座的有中共中央候补委员赵毅敏等同志。
