Title: Celebrating International Labor Day: "Long Live Chairman Mao" Stamp Set to Be Issued Today
Author:
Date: 1967-05-01
Edition: 3

To celebrate International Labor Day, the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of China will issue a set of commemorative stamps titled "Long Live Our Great Teacher, Great Leader, Great Supreme Commander, and Great Helmsman Chairman Mao" on May 1, 1967. The set consists of eight stamps, with the following designs:

	First stamp (4 fen): A quotation from the communiqué of the Eleventh Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China: "Comrade Mao Zedong is the greatest Marxist-Leninist of the contemporary era. Comrade Mao Zedong has brilliantly, creatively, and comprehensively inherited, defended, and developed Marxism-Leninism, elevating it to a new and higher stage. Mao Zedong Thought is Marxism-Leninism in the era when imperialism is heading towards comprehensive collapse and socialism is marching towards worldwide victory. Mao Zedong Thought is the guiding principle for all the work of the entire Party and the entire nation."

	Second stamp (8 fen): "The seas churn with waves of anger, and the continents tremble with thunderous roars."

	Third stamp (8 fen): "Chairman Mao is the red sun in the hearts of the people of the world."

	Fourth stamp (8 fen): "Chairman Mao with the Red Guards."

	Fifth stamp (8 fen): "Chairman Mao with Vice Chairman Lin."

	Sixth stamp (8 fen): "Chairman Mao receiving the Red Guards at Tiananmen."

	Seventh stamp (8 fen): "Chairman Mao receiving the Red Guards at Tiananmen."

	Eighth stamp (10 fen): "Chairman Mao with Vice Chairman Lin."

This set of stamps was jointly designed by the Beijing Stamp Factory and the Stamp Issuing Bureau.

### 庆祝“五一”国际劳动节  “毛主席万岁”邮票一套今发行

1967-05-01
第3版()
专栏：

    庆祝“五一”国际劳动节
    “毛主席万岁”邮票一套今发行
    新华社三十日讯　为庆祝“五一”国际劳动节，中华人民共和国邮电部订于一九六七年五月一日发行“我们伟大的导师、伟大的领袖、伟大的统帅、伟大的舵手毛主席万岁”纪念邮票一套，共八枚，图案是：
    第一图（4分）：中国共产党第八届中央委员会第十一次全体会议公报中的一段话：毛泽东同志是当代最伟大的马克思列宁主义者。毛泽东同志天才地、创造性地、全面地继承、捍卫和发展了马克思列宁主义，把马克思列宁主义提高到一个崭新的阶段。毛泽东思想是在帝国主义走向全面崩溃，社会主义走向全世界胜利的时代的马克思列宁主义。毛泽东思想是全党全国一切工作的指导方针。
    第二图（8分）：四海翻腾云水怒，五洲震荡风雷激。
    第三图（8分）：毛主席是世界人民心中的红太阳。
    第四图（8分）：毛主席和红卫兵在一起。
    第五图（8分）：毛主席和林副统帅在一起。
    第六图（8分）：毛主席在天安门上接见红卫兵。
    第七图（8分）：毛主席在天安门上接见红卫兵。
    第八图（10分）：毛主席和林副统帅在一起。
    这套邮票是由北京邮票厂、邮票发行局共同设计的。
