Title: Vice Premier Li Xiannian Meets with Pakistani Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-05-03
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Li Xiannian today afternoon met with a Pakistani trade delegation led by Chairman Hasni of the Pakistan Federation of Chambers of Commerce and Industry. They had a friendly conversation.

Present at the meeting were Hou Tong, Liu Xiven, and others.

### 李先念副总理接见巴基斯坦客人

1967-05-03
第6版()
专栏：

    李先念副总理接见巴基斯坦客人
    新华社一日讯　李先念副总理今天下午接见以巴基斯坦工商联合会主席哈斯尼为首的巴基斯坦贸易代表团，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有侯桐、刘希文等。
