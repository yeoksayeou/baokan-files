Title: Arab Friends Point Out That the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is the Most Profound Revolution in History
Author:
Date: 1967-05-09
Edition: 5

Arab revolutionary people warmly praise China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution as a new development in the Marxist-Leninist theory of the dictatorship of the proletariat.

A progressive from Syria wrote: "The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China is the most profound class struggle in history. It shakes the foundations of the old society, touches the depths of people's souls, and opens a new era in the history of the socialist movement. It is a brilliant new beginning for proletarian revolutionary theory, a new development in the Marxist-Leninist theory of the dictatorship of the proletariat, and a banner for the world revolution to realize its ideals."

A young person from Iraq said: "The Cultural Revolution in China will accelerate the world's progress towards communism. Therefore, its victory belongs not only to the Chinese people but also to the workers, peasants, and other progressive forces around the world."

A progressive from Syria wrote an article refuting the slanders of imperialism, modern revisionism, and reactionaries from various countries against China's Cultural Revolution. He said in the article, "The Cultural Revolution is to prevent the emergence of a revisionist, degenerate, bourgeois generation as happened in the Soviet Union. Therefore, the American and British imperialists and the traitorous Khrushchev revisionists are frantically slandering and launching hysterical attacks on the Cultural Revolution." Another progressive from Syria wrote in an article that the Soviet revisionist leadership attacks the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China because they "fear that the Soviet people will rise up against them, revolt against revisionism, and stop the restoration of capitalism."

A Palestinian student studying in the United Arab Republic said: "Chairman Mao teaches us to fight against the enemy, but the Soviet revisionists want us to surrender." He mentioned that once, a Soviet revisionist, upon hearing that he wanted to be a fighter for the liberation of his country after graduation, actually said, "You are really foolish. Israel is supported by the Americans, if you go to fight against Israel, you will die in vain." I angrily replied, "I would rather die standing than live kneeling. Even if I die in armed struggle, our country will definitely be liberated, and our national dignity will definitely be maintained." The student said that since then, he has more clearly seen through the traitorous face of the Soviet revisionists.

### 阿拉伯朋友指出我文化大革命是历史上最深刻的革命  文化大革命将加速世界走向共产主义  苏修攻击文化大革命是害怕苏联人民起来造他们的反

1967-05-09
第5版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯朋友指出我文化大革命是历史上最深刻的革命 
    文化大革命将加速世界走向共产主义
    苏修攻击文化大革命是害怕苏联人民起来造他们的反
    据新华社讯　阿拉伯革命人民热情赞扬中国无产阶级文化大革命是马克思列宁主义关于无产阶级专政理论的新发展。
    叙利亚一位进步人士著文说：“中国无产阶级文化大革命是历史上的一场最深刻的阶级斗争。它动摇着旧社会的基础，触及着人们的灵魂深处，开辟了社会主义运动史的新纪元。它是无产阶级革命理论光辉的新开端，是马克思列宁主义关于无产阶级专政理论的新发展，是世界革命实现其理想的旗帜。”
    伊拉克一位青年说：中国的文化大革命“将加速世界走向共产主义的进程。因此，它的胜利不仅属于中国人民，而且属于世界工农和其他进步力量。”
    叙利亚一位进步人士写文章驳斥帝国主义、现代修正主义和各国反动派对中国文化大革命的诬蔑。他在文章中说，中国文化大革命“是为了避免象在苏联所发生的那样的修正主义的、堕落的、资产阶级的一代的出现。正因为如此，美英帝国主义者和叛徒赫鲁晓夫修正主义者发疯似地对文化大革命进行造谣诬蔑和发动歇斯底里的攻击。”另一位叙利亚进步人士在文章中说，苏联修正主义领导集团攻击中国无产阶级文化大革命，是因为“害怕苏联人民起来反对他们，造修正主义的反，制止资本主义复辟”。
    一位在阿联学习的巴勒斯坦学生说：“毛主席教导我们要同敌人进行斗争，而苏联修正主义者却要我们投降。”他谈到，有一次，有个苏修分子在听说他毕业后想当一名为祖国的解放而斗争的战士时，竟然说什么：“你真傻。以色列是得到美国人支持的，如果你去同以色列打仗，你就会白白送死。”我当时气愤地回答说：“我宁肯站着死，也不愿跪着生。即使我在武装斗争中死去，但是我们的祖国一定会得到解放，我们的民族尊严一定会得到维护。”这位学生说，从那以后，他更加清楚地认清了苏修的叛徒嘴脸。
