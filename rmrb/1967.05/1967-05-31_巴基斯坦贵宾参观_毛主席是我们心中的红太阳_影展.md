Title: Pakistani Guests Visit the "Chairman Mao is the Red Sun in Our Hearts" Photo Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1967-05-31
Edition: 4

Pakistani Defense Minister Admiral Afzal Rahman Khan and Pakistani Foreign Secretary Yusuf, among others, visited the "Chairman Mao is the Red Sun in Our Hearts" photo exhibition this morning, accompanied by Vice Minister of National Defense Wang Shusheng and others.

The distinguished guests viewed the radiant image of our most beloved and great leader Chairman Mao in the exhibition hall.

When Admiral Afzal Rahman Khan saw the historic moment of Chairman Mao sitting on the ground with the Red Guards watching fireworks to celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, he said, "Chairman Mao is very happy and in good health!"

The distinguished guests repeatedly praised the large-scale photo exhibition as excellent, saying it left a deep impression on them.

### 巴基斯坦贵宾参观“毛主席是我们心中的红太阳”影展

1967-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦贵宾参观“毛主席是我们心中的红太阳”影展
    据新华社三十日讯　巴基斯坦国防部长阿夫扎尔·拉赫曼·汗海军中将，巴基斯坦外事秘书优素福等，今天上午，在我国防部副部长王树声等陪同下，参观了“毛主席是我们心中的红太阳”摄影展览。
    贵宾们在展览大厅里，瞻仰了我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席的光辉形象。
    阿夫扎尔·拉赫曼·汗部长在看到庆祝中华人民共和国成立十七周年，毛主席和红卫兵小将在一起席地而坐观看焰火的伟大历史镜头时说：“毛主席很高兴，身体很健康！”
    贵宾们一再赞扬这个大型摄影展览很好，给他们留下了深刻的印象。
