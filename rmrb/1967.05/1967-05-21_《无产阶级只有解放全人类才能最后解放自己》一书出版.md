Title: The Book "The Proletariat Can Only Finally Liberate Itself by Liberating All of Mankind" Published
Author:
Date: 1967-05-21
Edition: 1

The book "The Proletariat Can Only Finally Liberate Itself by Liberating All of Mankind" has been published by the People's Publishing House and is now available in Beijing, with nationwide distribution to follow soon.

The book features a photograph of the most beloved leader Chairman Mao, a group photo of Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Comrade Lin Biao attending the May Day celebration with the revolutionary masses of the capital, and Comrade Lin Biao's personal inscription: "Long live the great teacher, great leader, great supreme commander, and great helmsman Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand, ten thousand years!"

The content of the book includes three editorials published by the People's Daily, the PLA Daily, and the Beijing Daily respectively to celebrate the 1967 May Day International Labor Day: "The Proletariat Can Only Finally Liberate Itself by Liberating All of Mankind," "Long Live the Great Alliance of the Revolutionary Left," and "Long Live the Great Unity of the Revolution."

### 《无产阶级只有解放全人类才能最后解放自己》一书出版

1967-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

    《无产阶级只有解放全人类才能最后解放自己》一书出版
    新华社二十日讯　《无产阶级只有解放全人类才能最后解放自己》一书，已由人民出版社出版，并已在北京发行，即将在全国陆续发行。
    这本书前面印有最最敬爱的领袖毛主席的照片、毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪同志参加首都革命群众的“五一”游园会时的合影，以及林彪同志亲笔题词“伟大的导师、伟大的领袖、伟大的统帅、伟大的舵手毛主席万岁！万岁！万万岁！”书的内容包括：《人民日报》、《解放军报》和《北京日报》为庆祝一九六七年“五一”国际劳动节而分别发表的题为《无产阶级只有解放全人类才能最后解放自己》、《无产阶级革命派大联合万岁！》、《革命的大联合万岁》等三篇社论。
