Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets Tanzanian Friends
Author:
Date: 1967-05-18
Edition: 5

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo received Tanzanian friends this afternoon. He met with members of the Tanganyika African National Union delegation, Rutakiamirwa and Kida, and had a warm and friendly conversation with them. Present at the meeting were Zhang Sirou, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Tanzanian Ambassador to China, Mwaluko.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见坦桑尼亚朋友

1967-05-18
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见坦桑尼亚朋友
    新华社十七日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，今天下午接见了坦桑尼亚坦噶尼喀非洲民族联盟代表团成员鲁塔基亚米尔瓦和基达，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若。坦桑尼亚驻中国大使姆瓦卢科也在座。
