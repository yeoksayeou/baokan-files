Title: U.S. Imperialist Aircraft and Warships Intrude into Our Airspace and Territorial Waters
Author:
Date: 1967-05-28
Edition: 6

U.S. imperialist aircraft and warships intrude into our airspace and territorial waters
We issue the 434th serious warning

New China News Agency, May 25th

On May 24th from 11:00 to 11:43, two batches of two U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Dong Island and Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. On the same day from 11:17 to 23:56, one U.S. warship intruded into the territorial waters southeast of Pinghai in Fujian Province. On May 25th from 11:20 to 11:29, one U.S. military aircraft again intruded into the airspace over Dong Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. Regarding the military provocation by U.S. military aircraft and warships, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the Ministry, issues the 434th serious warning.

### 美帝飞机军舰侵入我领空领海  我提出第四百三十四次严重警告

1967-05-28
第6版()
专栏：

    美帝飞机军舰侵入我领空领海
    我提出第四百三十四次严重警告
    新华社二十五日讯　五月二十四日十一时至十一时四十三分，美国军用飞机两批两架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的东岛、永兴岛地区领空。同日十一时十七分至二十三时五十六分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省平海东南地区领海。五月二十五日十一时二十分至十一时二十九分，美国军用飞机一架，又侵入我广东省西沙群岛的东岛地区领空。对于美国军用飞机和军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百三十四次严重警告。
