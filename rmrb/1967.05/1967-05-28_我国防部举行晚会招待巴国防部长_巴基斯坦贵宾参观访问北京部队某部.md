Title: China's Ministry of National Defense Holds Evening Gala to Welcome Pakistani Defense Minister
Author:
Date: 1967-05-28
Edition: 3

The Ministry of National Defense held an evening gala tonight to welcome Pakistan's Defense Minister Admiral A.F. Rahman Khan and Pakistan's Secretary for Foreign Affairs Yusuf, who are visiting China. The guests were accompanied by China's Deputy Ministers of National Defense Xiao Jinguang and Wang Shusheng, as well as senior officials from various branches of the People's Liberation Army. They watched the revolutionary modern ballet "White-Haired Girl." The Pakistani Ambassador to China, Sultan M. Khan, and the military attaché from the Pakistani Embassy were also invited to attend the gala.

Throughout the performance, the audience erupted in enthusiastic applause. After the performance, Admiral A.F. Rahman Khan and his entourage, accompanied by Xiao Jinguang and Wang Shusheng, went on stage to shake hands with the performers and congratulate them on the successful show.

This morning, Admiral A.F. Rahman Khan and Yusuf, along with other Pakistani guests, visited a unit of the Beijing Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, accompanied by Wang Shusheng and others.

### 我国防部举行晚会招待巴国防部长  巴基斯坦贵宾参观访问北京部队某部

1967-05-28
第3版()
专栏：

    我国防部举行晚会招待巴国防部长
    巴基斯坦贵宾参观访问北京部队某部
    据新华社二十七日讯  国防部今晚举行文艺晚会，欢迎巴基斯坦国防部长阿夫扎尔·拉赫曼·汗海军中将和陪同前来访问的巴基斯坦外事秘书优素福。贵宾们由我国国防部副部长萧劲光、王树声以及中国人民解放军各军种负责人陪同，观看了革命现代芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》。应邀出席晚会的有巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗和大使馆武官。
    演出过程中，剧场内不断发出一阵又一阵的热烈掌声。演出结束时，阿夫扎尔·拉赫曼·汗等在萧劲光、王树声等陪同下，走上舞台同演员们握手，祝贺演出成功。
    今天上午，阿夫扎尔·拉赫曼·汗和优素福等巴基斯坦贵宾，在王树声等陪同下，参观访问了中国人民解放军北京部队某部。
