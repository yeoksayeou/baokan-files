Title: Delegation of the (Orthodox) Japan-China Friendship Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-05-31
Edition: 4

At the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation, a delegation of 24 members from the fifth visit to China by the General Headquarters of the (Orthodox) Japan-China Friendship Association, led by Chairman Yohei Enomoto, arrived in Beijing this evening. They were warmly welcomed by representatives of the revolutionary masses of the proletariat and Red Guards in the capital, as well as by responsible officials from relevant departments.

### 日中友协（正统）代表团到京

1967-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

    日中友协（正统）代表团到京
    据新华社三十日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请前来我国进行友好访问的日中友好协会（正统）总部第五次访华代表团一行二十四人，在团长榎本庸夫的率领下，于今晚到达北京，受到首都无产阶级革命派、红卫兵小将代表和有关方面负责人的热烈欢迎。
