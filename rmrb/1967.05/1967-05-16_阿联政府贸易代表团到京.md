Title: Trade Delegation from the United Arab Republic Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-05-16
Edition: 5

A trade delegation from the United Arab Republic, led by Muhammad Mazhar Far, Director of the Commercial Representative Office of the UAR Ministry of Economic Affairs and Foreign Trade, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 阿联政府贸易代表团到京

1967-05-16
第5版()
专栏：

    阿联政府贸易代表团到京
    新华社十四日讯　由阿联经济外贸部商务代表司司长穆罕默德·迈扎特·法尔率领的阿联政府贸易代表团，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
