Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets Swiss Friends
Author:
Date: 1967-05-03
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with Swiss friends today. The New China News Agency reported that Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council met with the chairman of the "Know China Society" in Geneva, Switzerland, Leon Buffat and his wife, and the vice chairman, Fernand Olivier and his wife, this afternoon. Vice Premier Chen Yi had a warm and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the meeting were Chu Tunan, president of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries.

### 陈毅副总理接见瑞士朋友

1967-05-03
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见瑞士朋友
    新华社二日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天下午接见了瑞士日内瓦“认识中国社”主席利昂·布法夫妇、副主席费尔南·奥利维尔夫妇。陈毅副总理同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有对外友协会长楚图南。
