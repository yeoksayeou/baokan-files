Title: China's Friendship Association Hosts Banquet for Sri Lankan Friends
Author:
Date: 1967-05-04
Edition: 5

In the afternoon of May 4, the China's Friendship Association held a banquet to warmly welcome the "Sanghasara Manjari" cultural troupe from Sri Lanka, led by team leader Guruchchi Kema Dasa.

In his speech at the banquet, Chu Tu-nan, president of the China's Friendship Association, said that the Chinese people firmly support the anti-imperialist revolutionary struggle of the Sri Lankan people. Team leader Kema Dasa, on behalf of the Sri Lankan people, wished for eternal friendship between China and Sri Lanka and long live Chairman Mao!

The Sri Lankan Ambassador to China, Gunawardena, was also invited to attend the banquet.

The "Sanghasara Manjari" cultural troupe arrived in Beijing by plane this morning and was warmly welcomed by representatives from the capital's cultural and arts circles and the Red Guards.

### 我对外友协欢宴锡兰朋友

1967-05-04
第5版()
专栏：

    我对外友协欢宴锡兰朋友
    新华社讯　对外友协一日下午举行宴会，热烈欢迎由古鲁奇·凯马达萨团长率领的锡兰“桑几萨·曼雅理”文工团。
    对外友协会长楚图南在宴会上致词时说，中国人民坚决支持锡兰人民的反帝革命斗争。凯马达萨团长代表锡兰人民祝中锡友谊万岁！祝毛主席万岁！
    锡兰驻中国大使古纳瓦德纳应邀出席了宴会。
    “桑几萨·曼雅理”文工团今天上午乘飞机到达北京时，受到首都文艺界和红卫兵代表的热烈欢迎。
