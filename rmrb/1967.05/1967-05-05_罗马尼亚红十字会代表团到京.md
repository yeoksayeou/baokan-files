Title: Romanian Red Cross Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-05-05
Edition: 6

A delegation from the Romanian Red Cross, headed by Vice Chairman I. Turai, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation consists of two people.

They are in China at the invitation of the Chinese Red Cross for a friendly visit.

### 罗马尼亚红十字会代表团到京

1967-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚红十字会代表团到京
    新华社三日讯　以罗马尼亚红十字会副主席依·图拉依为首的罗马尼亚红十字会代表团一行二人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    他们是应中国红十字会邀请来我国进行友好访问的。
