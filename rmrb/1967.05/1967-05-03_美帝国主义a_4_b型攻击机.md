The U.S. Imperialist A-4B Attack Aircraft

	New China News Agency, May 2nd

	Data: The U.S. Imperialist A-4B Attack Aircraft

	On May 1st, our heroic People's Liberation Army Air Force shot down two more U.S. imperialist A-4B attack aircraft. This type of aircraft is piloted by one person, has a maximum speed of over 1,090 kilometers per hour, a maximum altitude of 13,700 meters, and a maximum range of 1,850 kilometers without auxiliary fuel tanks, and 3,200 kilometers with auxiliary fuel tanks. Its operational radius is over 980 kilometers. It is equipped with two 20mm machine guns and one to three "Sidewinder" missiles.

	The fact that the U.S. imperialist A-4B attack aircraft were shot down once again demonstrates that our heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force, nurtured by Mao Zedong Thought, has developed formidable skills. Against U.S. imperialist military aircraft that invade our airspace to provoke war, whether they are manned or unmanned, whether they are fighter jets or attack aircraft, we will ensure that they come in but do not return.

### 美帝国主义A—4B型攻击机

1967-05-03
第2版()
专栏：资料

    美帝国主义A—4B型攻击机
    新华社二日讯　资料：美帝国主义A—4B型攻击机
    五月一日，我英雄的人民解放军空军部队又击落了两架美帝国主义A—4B型攻击机。这种飞机由一名飞行员驾驶，最大时速一千零九十多公里，最大飞行高度一万三千七百米，最大航程不带副油箱时，为一千八百五十公里，带副油箱时，为三千二百公里，活动半径为九百八十多公里。武器装备有二十毫米机关炮二门，“响尾蛇”导弹一至三枚。
    击落美帝国主义A—4B型攻击机的事实再一次说明了我英雄的中国人民解放军空军部队，在毛泽东思想哺育下，练就了一身硬本领，对于侵入我国领空进行战争挑衅的美帝国主义的军用飞机，不管是有人驾驶也好，无人驾驶也好，是战斗机也好，是攻击机也好，统统都要叫它有来无回。
