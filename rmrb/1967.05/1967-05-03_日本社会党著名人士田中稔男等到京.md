Title: Prominent Figures from the Japanese Socialist Party Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-05-03
Edition: 6

Prominent figures from the Japanese Socialist Party, Mr. Tanaka Jin'ou and another individual, arrived in Beijing today. They were invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to participate in the celebrations for International Labor Day on May 1st and to conduct a visit and tour of our country. They arrived in Beijing by airplane this afternoon.

### 日本社会党著名人士田中稔男等到京

1967-05-03
第6版()
专栏：

    日本社会党著名人士田中稔男等到京
    新华社一日讯　日本社会党著名人士田中稔男等二人，由中国人民外交学会邀请，前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节的庆祝活动和参观访问，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
