Title: Romanian Shooting Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-05-05
Edition: 6

At the invitation of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, a 14-member shooting team from the "Star" Club of the Romanian Armed Forces, led by team leader Colonel Lezeresku, arrived in Beijing yesterday afternoon by plane.

### 罗马尼亚一射击代表队到京

1967-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚一射击代表队到京
    新华社四日讯　应中国人民解放军总参谋部邀请前来我国访问的罗马尼亚武装部队“星”俱乐部射击代表队一行十四人，在领队勒泽列斯库中校率领下，昨天下午乘飞机到达北京。
