Title: Prince Sihanouk Forms Special Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1967-05-06
Edition: 5

Prince Sihanouk, Head of State of Cambodia, accepted the full powers granted by the National Assembly and formed a special cabinet under his own leadership at a meeting held on May 1. In the newly formed cabinet, Bin Sihanouk, the Head of State's personal advisor, serves as government advisor. Son Sann, another personal advisor to the Head of State, serves as the chief minister and representative at the Prime Minister's Office. Prince Norodom Sihanouk serves as the Minister of Foreign Affairs.

According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on May 1, former Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, General Lon Nol, submitted a letter of resignation to Prince Sihanouk on April 29. Lon Nol had taken office as Prime Minister on October 22 of the previous year.

### 西哈努克亲王组成特别内阁

1967-05-06
第5版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王组成特别内阁
    新华社金边五日电　据柬埔寨新闻社报道，柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王接受国民议会授予的全权，在一日举行的会议上组成了他自己领导的特别内阁。在新组成的内阁中，国家元首私人顾问宾努任政府顾问，国家元首私人顾问宋双任首席大臣兼驻首相府代表，富里萨拉亲王任外交大臣。
    据柬埔寨新闻社一日报道，柬埔寨王国政府前首相朗诺中将在四月二十九日写信给西哈努克亲王，要求辞职。朗诺是在去年十月二十二日就任首相的。
