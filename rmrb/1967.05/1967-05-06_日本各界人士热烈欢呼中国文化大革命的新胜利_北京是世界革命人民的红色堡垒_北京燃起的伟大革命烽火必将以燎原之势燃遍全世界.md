Title: Japanese Figures from Various Circles Warmly Applaud New Victories of China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-05-06
Edition: 5

Japanese Figures from Various Circles Warmly Applaud New Victories of China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution

Beijing is the Red Citadel of the World's Revolutionary People

The Magnificent Revolutionary Beacon Lit in Beijing Will Surely Spread with the Momentum of a Prairie Fire Across the Entire World

New China News Agency, Tokyo: In recent days, people from various circles in Japan have warmly applauded the establishment of the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee.

Highashi Takashi, a leader of the Japanese trade union movement, said: The establishment of the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee has extremely profound significance. He emphasized that the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee is established in Beijing, the heart of the world's revolutionary base. It is a new red citadel for the world's revolutionary people. The establishment of the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee has great historical significance.

Tanaka Toshio, a member of the Socialist Party, said: The establishment of the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee is a major event for people all over the world. The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China, led by Chairman Mao and advancing with the momentum of a storm, shows that the fundamental issue of revolution is the issue of political power.

An employee of a trading company said: "The establishment of the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee is a great creative victory achieved by 700 million Chinese people under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought." He said: Its establishment has shattered the delusions of U.S. imperialism, Soviet revisionism, and their old and new followers and reactionaries in various countries who attempted to make the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China fail. He said: "I am inspired by this victory. I am determined to fight against U.S. imperialism and strengthen the combat friendship between the peoples of China and Japan."

A worker in the publishing industry said: "The establishment of the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee is a great victory of Mao Zedong Thought. Mao Zedong Thought is the pinnacle of contemporary Marxism-Leninism. The brilliance of Mao Zedong Thought brings light to all those who fight against the rule of U.S. imperialism and strive for national liberation."

A university student said: "The magnificent revolutionary beacon lit in the world's revolutionary base, Beijing, will surely spread with the momentum of a prairie fire across the entire world, dealing a decisive blow to U.S. imperialism and reactionaries."

New China News Agency, According to a report from the East Asia News Agency: The 14th delegation of active members from the headquarters of the Japan-China Friendship Association (Genuine) sent a telegram on April 25 congratulating the establishment of the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee, saying: The birth of the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee is a "brilliant victory of Mao Zedong Thought."

The telegram of congratulations said: "We, the headquarters of the Japan-China Friendship Association (Genuine), who are about to send the 14th delegation of active members to visit China, are filled with joy upon hearing the news of the establishment of the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee and extend our warm congratulations."

The telegram of congratulations said: "In the great city of Beijing, the birthplace of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, under the intimate care and leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao, the revolutionary 'three-in-one combination' Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee has been born. This is a brilliant victory of Mao Zedong Thought."

The telegram of congratulations said: "Under the inspiration of the invincible Mao Zedong Thought, the Japanese people will surely be able to complete the cause of liberating Japan."

The telegram of congratulations concluded with the slogans: "Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought!"

"Long live the combat friendship between the peoples of China and Japan!"

### 日本各界人士热烈欢呼中国文化大革命的新胜利  北京是世界革命人民的红色堡垒  北京燃起的伟大革命烽火必将以燎原之势燃遍全世界

1967-05-06
第5版()
专栏：

    日本各界人士热烈欢呼中国文化大革命的新胜利
    北京是世界革命人民的红色堡垒
    北京燃起的伟大革命烽火必将以燎原之势燃遍全世界
    新华社讯　东京消息：日本各界人士连日来热烈欢呼北京市革命委员会的成立。
    日本工会运动领导人高野实说：北京市革命委员会的成立，具有极其深远的意义。他强调指出，北京市革命委员会是在世界革命根据地的心脏北京成立的，它是世界革命人民的新的红色堡垒。北京市革命委员会的成立，具有重大的历史意义。
    社会党人士田中稔男说：北京市革命委员会的成立是全世界人民的大事。由毛主席领导的、以暴风骤雨之势向前推进的中国无产阶级文化大革命表明：革命的根本问题是政权问题。
    一位贸易公司的职员说：“北京市革命委员会的成立，是七亿中国人民在毛泽东思想的指导下取得的创造性的伟大胜利。”他说：它的成立粉碎了美帝国主义、苏联修正主义及其新旧追随者和各国反动派妄图使中国无产阶级文化大革命失败的痴心妄想。他说：“我被这个胜利所鼓舞。我决心为反对美帝国主义、加强日中两国人民的战斗友谊而奋斗。”
    一位出版界的工人说：“北京市革命委员会的成立是毛泽东思想的伟大胜利。毛泽东思想是当代马克思列宁主义的顶峰，毛泽东思想的光辉给所有为反对美帝国主义的统治、争取民族解放而斗争的人们带来了光明。”
    一位大学生说：“在世界革命的根据地北京燃烧起来的伟大的革命的烽火，必将以燎原之势燃遍全世界，给美帝国主义和反动派以决定性的打击。”
    新华社讯　据东方通讯社报道：日中友好协会（正统）总部第十四次积极分子访华代表团，在四月二十五日发出的祝贺北京市革命委员会成立的电报中说：北京市革命委员会的诞生，“是毛泽东思想的辉煌胜利。”
    贺电说：“我们日中友好协会（正统）总部即将派出的第十四次积极分子访华代表团，在听到北京市革命委员会成立的消息时，以满怀喜悦的心情，表示热烈的祝贺。”
    贺电说：“在伟大的无产阶级文化大革命的发源地北京，在英明的领袖毛主席的亲切的关怀和领导下，诞生了革命的‘三结合’的北京市革命委员会。这是毛泽东思想的辉煌胜利。”
    贺电说：“日本人民在战无不胜的毛泽东思想的胜利的鼓舞下，也一定能够完成日本的解放事业。”
    贺电最后高呼：“战无不胜的毛泽东思想万岁！”
    “日中两国人民的战斗友谊万岁！”
