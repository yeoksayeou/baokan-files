Title: Delegation of the Sudan-China Friendship Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-05-01
Edition: 2

A delegation of the Sudan-China Friendship Association, led by its chairman, Abdel Wahab Zain Al-Abdin, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation consists of seven members. They are visiting China at the invitation of the China-Africa Friendship Association.

### 苏丹—中国友好协会代表团到京

1967-05-01
第2版()
专栏：

    苏丹—中国友好协会代表团到京
    新华社三十日讯　由苏丹—中国友好协会主席阿卜杜勒·瓦哈卜·宰因·阿卜丁率领的苏丹—中国友好协会代表团一行七人，今天乘飞机到达北京。代表团是应中非友协邀请来我国访问的。
