Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-05-23
Edition: 1

Revolutionary culture is a powerful weapon for the masses of the people. Before the revolution, revolutionary culture serves as the ideological preparation for the revolution; during the revolution, it is an essential and important front in the general revolutionary line.

	_New Democracy_

### 毛主席语录

    毛主席语录
    革命文化，对于人民大众，是革命的有力武器。革命文化，在革命前，是革命的思想准备；在革命中，是革命总战线中的一条必要和重要的战线。
