Lao PDR Charge d'Affaires Holds National Day Reception

BEIJING, May 11 (Xinhua) — The Charge d'Affaires of the Lao PDR Embassy in China, Phonseya, held a reception today to celebrate the National Day of the Lao PDR.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Han Nianlong, and relevant officials attended the reception at the invitation.

Ambassadors and diplomats from various countries stationed in China also attended the reception.

### 老挝驻华临时代办举行国庆招待会

1967-05-13
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝驻华临时代办举行国庆招待会
    新华社十一日讯　老挝王国驻中国大使馆临时代办丰舍雅今天举行招待会，庆祝老挝王国国庆。
    外交部副部长韩念龙和有关方面负责人应邀出席了招待会。
    各国驻中国使节和外交官员出席了招待会。
