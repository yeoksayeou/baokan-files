Title: Italian "Red Guards" Newspaper Says China's Cultural Revolution Gains Support from Revolutionaries Worldwide
Author:
Date: 1967-05-18
Edition: 6

	Italian "Red Guards" Newspaper Says China's Cultural Revolution Gains Support from Revolutionaries Worldwide
	Red Guards Make Great Contributions to World Revolution

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Rome: The official newspaper of the Italian Communist Youth League (Marxist-Leninist), "Red Guards," recently published an editorial enthusiastically praising Chairman Mao and the Chinese proletarian cultural revolution. It wrote: "Today, it is Mao Zedong—the contemporary Lenin—who leads us, who leads all revolutionaries. He is the helmsman of a great revolutionary army."

	The editorial severely refuted the malicious slanders of imperialism and modern revisionism against the Chinese proletarian cultural revolution. It pointed out that since last August, the "proletarian cultural revolution in China has made rapid and vigorous progress. It has foiled the plots of imperialists and revisionists, won the full support of all revolutionaries in the world, and pointed out new great directions for the proletariat to consolidate socialist struggles, oppose revisionism, and implement broad revolutionary democracy under the dictatorship of the proletariat."

	In discussing the Chinese Red Guards, the editorial enthusiastically wrote: "The Chinese Red Guards are educating the youth of the whole world in discipline and revolutionary spirit. The task of the Red Guards to smash the old world and become the successors of the communist cause is their most effective contribution to the world revolution."

	In discussing the guiding significance of Mao Zedong Thought to the revolutionary struggle of Italian youth, the editorial pointed out: "If we can correctly apply Mao Zedong Thought in our daily struggles, the Italian Communist Youth League (Marxist-Leninist) will fulfill its vanguard role and the role of a communist school."

### 意大利《红卫兵》报说中国文化大革命赢得世界革命者支持  红卫兵为世界革命作出伟大贡献

1967-05-18
第6版()
专栏：

    意大利《红卫兵》报说中国文化大革命赢得世界革命者支持
    红卫兵为世界革命作出伟大贡献
    据新华社讯　罗马消息：意大利共产主义青年联盟（马克思列宁主义）机关报《红卫兵》最近在一篇社论中热烈赞扬毛主席和中国无产阶级文化大革命时写道：“今天领导我们、领导一切革命者的是毛泽东——当代的列宁，他是一支伟大的革命军队的舵手。”
    社论严厉地驳斥了帝国主义和现代修正主义对中国无产阶级文化大革命的恶毒诽谤，并且指出，自去年八月以来，中国的“无产阶级文化大革命突飞猛进地向前发展。它挫败了帝国主义者和修正主义者的阴谋，赢得了世界上所有革命者的充分支持，为无产阶级巩固社会主义的斗争、反对修正主义的斗争和实行无产阶级专政下的广泛的革命的民主指出了新的伟大的方向。”
    社论在谈到中国的红卫兵时热情地写道：“中国的红卫兵在向全世界青年进行纪律和革命干劲的教育。红卫兵所担负的砸烂旧世界、做共产主义事业接班人的任务，是他们为世界革命作出的最有效的贡献。”
    社论在谈到毛泽东思想对意大利青年的革命斗争的指导意义时指出：“如果我们在青年日常的斗争中能够善于正确地运用毛泽东思想，意大利共产主义青年联盟（马克思列宁主义）就会起到它的先锋作用、共产主义学校的作用。”
