Title: Marching Forward Victoriously Along Chairman Mao's Revolutionary Literary and Artistic Line!
Author:
Date: 1967-05-15
Edition: 6

Marching Forward Victoriously Along Chairman Mao's Revolutionary Literary and Artistic Line!
—In Commemoration of the 25th Anniversary of Chairman Mao's "Talks at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art"

Long Live Chairman Mao's Revolutionary Literary and Artistic Line!

We must make literature and art a powerful weapon for uniting and educating the people, for attacking and annihilating the enemy, and for helping the people to fight the enemy with one heart and one mind. Mao Zedong

In the present-day world, all culture or literature and art belong to a definite class and a definite political line. Art for art's sake, art above classes, and art that is parallel to or independent of politics do not exist in fact. Mao Zedong

Chinese revolutionary writers and artists, writers and artists with a future, must go among the masses, must go among the workers, peasants, and soldiers for a long period, unconditionally and wholeheartedly, to the fiery struggles, to the only source which is both the broadest and the richest. They must observe, experience, study, and analyze all people, all classes, all groups, all living forms of struggle and all original materials for literature and art. Only then will it be possible to enter the creative process. Mao Zedong

### 沿着毛主席的革命文艺路线胜利前进！——纪念毛主席《在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话》发表二十五周年（图片）

1967-05-15
第6版()
专栏：

    沿着毛主席的革命文艺路线胜利前进！
    ——纪念毛主席《在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话》发表二十五周年
    毛主席的革命文艺路线胜利万岁！
    要使文艺很好地成为整个革命机器的一个组成部分，作为团结人民、教育人民、打击敌人、消灭敌人的有力的武器，帮助人民同心同德地和敌人作斗争。  毛泽东
    在现在世界上，一切文化或文学艺术都是属于一定的阶级，属于一定的政治路线的。为艺术的艺术，超阶级的艺术，和政治并行或互相独立的艺术，实际上是不存在的。  毛泽东
    中国的革命的文学家艺术家，有出息的文学家艺术家，必须到群众中去，必须长期地无条件地全心全意地到工农兵群众中去，到火热的斗争中去，到唯一的最广大最丰富的源泉中去，观察、体验、研究、分析一切人，一切阶级，一切群众，一切生动的生活形式和斗争形式，一切文学和艺术的原始材料，然后才有可能进入创作过程。
    毛泽东
