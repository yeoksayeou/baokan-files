Title: Vice Premier Xie Receives Anti-Revisionist Fighters Expelled by Mongolian Revisionists
Author:
Date: 1967-05-25
Edition: 3

Vice Premier Xie Richi today afternoon received Wang Zhiyun, Chen Lu, and Zuo Guiyi, three teachers from the Ulan Bator Chinese School who were expelled from Mongolia by the Mongolian revisionist authorities. He had a cordial talk with them.

Vice Premier Xie Richi extended a warm welcome to the three anti-revisionist fighters from among the overseas Chinese and encouraged them to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, to study and apply Chairman Mao's works, and to actively participate in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

Present at the reception were Wu De, vice chairman of the Beijing Municipal Revolutionary Committee, and responsible persons from the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, the Ministry of Education, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and other relevant departments, as well as representatives of the revolutionary masses.

### 谢副总理接见被蒙修无理驱逐的我反修战士  勉励他们高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，积极参加无产阶级文化大革命

1967-05-25
第3版()
专栏：

    谢副总理接见被蒙修无理驱逐的我反修战士
    勉励他们高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，积极参加无产阶级文化大革命
    新华社二十四日讯　谢富治副总理今天下午接见被蒙古修正主义当局无理驱逐出境回国的乌兰巴托华侨小学教师王志云、陈禄、左贵义，同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    谢富治副总理向王志云等三位华侨反修战士表示热烈欢迎，勉励他们高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，活学活用毛主席著作，积极参加无产阶级文化大革命。
    接见时，北京市革命委员会副主任委员吴德、华侨事务委员会、教育部、外交部等有关方面负责人以及无产阶级革命派代表在座。
