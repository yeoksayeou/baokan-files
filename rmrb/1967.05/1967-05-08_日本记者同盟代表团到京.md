Title: Delegation of the Japanese Federation of Newspaper Workers Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-05-08
Edition: 5

A delegation of the Japanese Federation of Newspaper Workers, consisting of five members, arrived in Beijing today by airplane. The delegation, led by Chairman Kojiro Kobayashi, is visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association.

The delegation was warmly welcomed at the airport by Mu Xin and Lü Bin, responsible persons of the capital's news units, Wang Xiaoyun, deputy secretary-general of the China-Japan Friendship Association, and representatives of the proletarian revolutionary faction of the news industry. Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation were General Secretary Charoen of the Afro-Asian Journalists Association, members of the Secretariat Shigenori Sugiyama and Yang Yi, and Indonesian representatives Supeno and Saeed.

Japanese friend Iizuka Junichiro was also at the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 日本记者同盟代表团到京

1967-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

    日本记者同盟代表团到京
    新华社七日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来华访问的日本记者同盟代表团一行五人，在团长、日本记者同盟主席小林雄一率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。他们在机场受到首都新闻单位的负责人穆欣、吕彬，中日友协副秘书长王晓云和新闻界无产阶级革命派代表的热烈欢迎。到机场迎接的还有亚非记协总书记查禾多、书记处成员杉山市平、杨翊和印度尼西亚代表苏佩诺、赛义德。
    日本朋友井出润一郎也到机场欢迎。
