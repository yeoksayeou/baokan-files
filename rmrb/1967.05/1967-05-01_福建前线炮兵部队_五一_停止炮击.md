Title: Fuzhou Front Artillery Units Cease Fire on May Day
Author:
Date: 1967-05-01
Edition: 3

The spokesperson for the headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's Fujian Front announced today that May 1st is International Labor Day, a glorious day for all laborers around the world. To allow our compatriots and Nationalist soldiers on islands such as Da Jinmen, Xiao Jinmen, Da Dang, and Er Dang to celebrate the holiday, the Fujian Front artillery units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army have been ordered to cease fire for one day on May 1st, as a gesture of concern.

### 福建前线炮兵部队“五一”停止炮击

1967-05-01
第3版()
专栏：

    福建前线炮兵部队“五一”停止炮击
    新华社福建前线三十日电　中国人民解放军福建前线部队司令部发言人今天宣布，五月一日是国际劳动节，是全世界劳动人民的光辉节日。为了让大金门、小金门、大担、二担等岛屿上的祖国同胞和国民党军官兵欢度节日，中国人民解放军福建前线炮兵部队奉命在五月一日停止炮击一天，以示关怀。
