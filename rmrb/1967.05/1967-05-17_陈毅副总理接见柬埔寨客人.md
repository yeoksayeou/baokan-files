Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Cambodian Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-05-17
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with Cambodian guests today evening. He held a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Yap Ben Tong, Secretary-General of the Cambodian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Beijing on the 15th.

Cambodian Ambassador to China, Zhang Gang, was present at the meeting.

### 陈毅副总理接见柬埔寨客人

1967-05-17
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见柬埔寨客人
    新华社十六日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天晚上接见了来我国进行友好访问的柬埔寨外交部秘书长雅本塘，同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    雅本塘是十五日抵达北京的。
    接见时柬埔寨驻中国大使张岗在座。
