Title: New Finnish Ambassador Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-05-26
Edition: 7

Finnish Republic's new ambassador to the People's Republic of China, Vanri Heileiniu, arrived in Beijing by airplane this evening.

### 芬兰新任驻华大使到京

1967-05-26
第7版()
专栏：

    芬兰新任驻华大使到京
    新华社二十五日讯　芬兰共和国新任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使凡利·海莱尼乌斯今晚乘飞机到达北京。
