Title: The So-Called "Mass Culture"
Author:
Date: 1967-05-20
Edition: 6

Khrushchev invented the so-called "mass party" and "mass state." His successors, through the organs they control, have now put forward the fallacy of "mass culture."

Soon after the new leadership of the CPSU took office, the magazine *Communist* published an article stating: "The consolidation of unity in society, economy, politics, and ideological consciousness has created the real basis for the emergence of a fundamentally new type of culture—a mass socialist culture." This "mass culture" is said to appear as the true democratic culture of the contemporary era, embodying a society of people who are spiritually united and free from class antagonism and exploitation.

The article further claims: "The mass nature of socialist culture determines the unified aesthetic foundation of our art and the socialist realist method." It goes on to say: "The mass culture of socialist society is filled with humanitarian ideals and profound social content, serving as a powerful tool for the ideological and artistic education of people."

Everyone can clearly see that in today's Soviet Union, where is there any "mass culture"? What exists is the decadent bourgeois culture serving the reactionary rule of the Soviet revisionists.

### 所谓“全民文化”

1967-05-20
第6版()
专栏：看苏修怎样在国内复辟资本主义

    所谓“全民文化”
    赫鲁晓夫发明了所谓“全民党”、“全民国家”，赫鲁晓夫的继承者又通过他们所控制的机关刊物，提出了“全民文化”的谬论。
    《共产党人》杂志在苏共新领导上台后不久，发表文章说：“社会与经济、政治和思想意识上的统一的巩固，给原则上是新型的文化——全民的社会主义文化的产生，创造了实在的基础。”这种“全民文化是作为当代真正民主的文化而出现的，它把没有阶级对抗而表现不受剥削的人民在精神上的一致的社会，体现了出来”。
  “社会主义文化的全民性质，规定了我们的艺术的美学基础、社会主义现实主义方法的统一。”这篇文章还胡说什么：“社会主义社会的全民文化，充满着人道主义理想和深刻的社会内容，是对人进行思想教育和艺术教育的强大工具”。
    谁都看得很清楚，在今天的苏联，哪里有什么“全民文化”？有的是为苏修集团反动统治服务的腐朽的资产阶级文化。
