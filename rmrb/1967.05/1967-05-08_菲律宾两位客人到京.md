Title: Two Guests from the Philippines Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-05-08
Edition: 5

The general secretary of the "Promote Nationalism Movement" of the Philippines, Jose Sison, and the head of the Western Luzon region of the "Promote Nationalism Movement" of the Philippines, Hilario Lin, arrived in Beijing today by plane at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to conduct a friendly visit to China.

    菲律宾两位客人到京
