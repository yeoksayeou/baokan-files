Title: Pakistani Defense Minister Arrives in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1967-05-26
Edition: 7

Pakistani Defense Minister Arrives in Shanghai

New China News Agency, Shanghai, May 25th - At the invitation of the Chinese government, Admiral Afzal Rahman Khan, Minister of Defense of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, arrived in Shanghai by plane this afternoon en route to Beijing for a friendly visit. Accompanying him on the visit were Pakistan's Foreign Secretary Yusuf and others.

Senior officials from the Shanghai garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including Chen Shifa, Xie Zhenghao, and Ji Shitang, as well as officials from the Shanghai Revolutionary Committee's foreign affairs department, went to the airport to welcome them.

Pan Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense, also came to the airport to welcome the delegation specially.

Air Commodore I.H. Khan of the Pakistan Embassy in China also came to Shanghai to welcome the delegation.

### 巴基斯坦国防部长到达上海

1967-05-26
第7版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦国防部长到达上海
    新华社上海二十五日电　应我国政府邀请前来我国进行友好访问的巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国国防部长阿夫扎尔·拉赫曼·汗海军中将，在赴北京途中今天下午乘飞机到达上海。陪同前来访问的有巴基斯坦外事秘书优素福等。
    中国人民解放军驻上海部队负责人陈士法、谢正浩、吉世堂以及上海市革命委员会外事部门负责人等前往机场欢迎。
    专程来上海迎接的国防部办公厅副主任潘振武也到机场欢迎。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆空军武官伊·哈·汗空军上校也专程来沪迎接。
