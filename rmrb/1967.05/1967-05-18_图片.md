Title: Cambodian Revolutionary Youths' Love for Chairman Mao and Praise for China's Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-05-18
Edition: 6

Cambodian revolutionary youths have an unlimited love for Chairman Mao. They enthusiastically praise China's Cultural Revolution. When they received a photograph of the great leader Chairman Mao reviewing the Cultural Revolution army, they immediately gathered around to carefully examine it.
