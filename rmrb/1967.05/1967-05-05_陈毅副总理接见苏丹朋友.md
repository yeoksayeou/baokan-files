Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Sudanese Friends
Author:
Date: 1967-05-05
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the Sudanese-Chinese Friendship Association Chairman Abdel Wahab Zein Abdelin, who is leading a delegation from the Sudanese-Chinese Friendship Association, this afternoon. Vice Premier Chen Yi had a warm and friendly conversation with the Sudanese friends.

Present at the meeting were relevant officials such as Ding Xilin.

### 陈毅副总理接见苏丹朋友

1967-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见苏丹朋友
    新华社四日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了苏丹—中国友好协会主席阿卜杜勒·瓦哈卜·宰因·阿卜丁率领的苏丹—中国友好协会代表团。陈毅副总理和苏丹朋友进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时丁西林等有关方面负责人在座。
