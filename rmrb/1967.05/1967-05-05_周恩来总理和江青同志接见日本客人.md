Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Comrade Jiang Qing Receive Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-05-05
Edition: 1

Premier Zhou Enlai and the first deputy director of the Central Cultural Revolution Group, Comrade Jiang Qing, received the delegation of the Japan-China Friendship Association (orthodox) headquarters women's delegation visiting China, led by Nakajima Chiyo, this afternoon. They had a cordial and friendly conversation with the delegation members.

Present at the reception were Yang Yunyu, secretary of the All-China Women's Federation Secretariat.

### 周恩来总理和江青同志接见日本客人

1967-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理和江青同志接见日本客人
    新华社四日讯　周恩来总理和中央文革小组第一副组长江青同志，今天下午接见了由中岛千代率领的日中友协（正统）总部妇女访华代表团全体成员，同她们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有全国妇联书记处书记杨蕴玉。
