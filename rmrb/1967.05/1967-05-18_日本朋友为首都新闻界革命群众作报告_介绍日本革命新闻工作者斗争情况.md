Title: Japanese Friends Report to Revolutionary Journalists in the Capital on the Struggle of Japanese Revolutionary Journalists
Author:
Date: 1967-05-18
Edition: 5

A delegation led by Kobayashi Yuichi of the Japanese Journalists' Alliance today reported to revolutionary journalists in the capital on the struggle of Japanese revolutionary journalists. Kobayashi Yuichi said that Mao Zedong Thought has shown the direction of struggle for Japanese revolutionary journalists. He declared that Japanese revolutionary journalists are determined to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought and resolutely struggle against the revisionists of the Japanese Communist Party.

Finally, he shouted, "Long live Mao Zedong Thought! Long live the great leader of all the people of the world, Chairman Mao!"

Members of the Japanese Journalists' Alliance delegation, Nagamin Yoshiki and Kakuya Eichi, also spoke at the meeting. Lu Bin, the first secretary of the All-China Journalists' Association, welcomed the anti-imperialist and anti-revisionist friends from Japan on behalf of the journalists in the capital.

### 日本朋友为首都新闻界革命群众作报告  介绍日本革命新闻工作者斗争情况

1967-05-18
第5版()
专栏：

    日本朋友为首都新闻界革命群众作报告
    介绍日本革命新闻工作者斗争情况
    据新华社十七日讯  由小林雄一率领的日本记者同盟代表团，今天向首都新闻界革命群众报告了日本革命新闻工作者的斗争情况。小林雄一说，毛泽东思想为日本革命的新闻工作者指明了斗争方向。他表示日本革命的新闻工作者决心高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，坚决和日共修正主义分子进行斗争。
    最后他高呼：“毛泽东思想万岁！”“全世界人民伟大导师毛主席万岁！”
    日本记者同盟代表团成员长南芳树、角田荣一在报告会上也讲了话。中华全国新闻工作者协会第一书记吕彬代表首都新闻界向来自日本的反帝反修战友表示热烈欢迎。
