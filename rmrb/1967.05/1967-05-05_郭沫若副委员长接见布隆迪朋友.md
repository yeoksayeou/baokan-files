Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Receives Friends from Burundi
Author:
Date: 1967-05-05
Edition: 6

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress received today the delegation from the Burundian Revolutionary Youth Organization of Ruwa-Gasoro, headed by François Kisamare, Minister of National Education and Culture of Burundi, and had a friendly conversation with them.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见布隆迪朋友

1967-05-05
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见布隆迪朋友
    新华社四日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天接见了以布隆迪国民教育和文化部长弗朗索瓦·基萨马雷为首的布隆迪鲁瓦加索尔革命青年组织代表团，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
