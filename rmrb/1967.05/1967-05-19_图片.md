Title: The People's Joyous Response to the Central Committee's Notice
Author: Liú Zhènxiáng
Date: 1967-05-19
Edition: 4

After the publication of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China's notice, the revolutionary masses of the capital, with hearts brimming with boundless excitement, flocked in large groups to the Central Masses Reception Station to share their joy. They enthusiastically celebrated the great victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.
