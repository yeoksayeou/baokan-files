Title: Comrade Xu Xing Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1967-05-12
Edition: 3

National People's Congress Representative and former Director of the Safety Bureau of the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry, Comrade Xu Xing, passed away at 9 PM on May 6, 1967, at the Anti-Imperialist Hospital in Beijing due to myocardial infarction. He was 62 years old. Despite medical treatment, the condition proved fatal.

### 许兴同志逝世

1967-05-12
第3版()
专栏：

    许兴同志逝世
    新华社十日讯　全国人民代表大会代表、冶金工业部原安全局局长许兴同志，因患心肌梗死症，经医治无效，于一九六七年五月六日下午九时在北京反帝医院逝世，年六十二岁。
