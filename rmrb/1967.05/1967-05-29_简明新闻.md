North Vietnam Shoots Down Eight American Aggressor Planes

On the afternoon of the 27th, American aggressor planes attacked residential areas in the northern Vietnamese province of Bac Ninh. The military and civilians of Bac Ninh responded with a fierce counterattack, immediately shooting down two American planes and damaging several others, capturing a batch of American air pirates. Additionally, it was reported that military and civilians in Nghe An and other areas also shot down six American aggressor planes.

Lao Patriotic Military and Civilians Kill Over 700 Enemies in April

In April, the patriotic military and civilians of Laos fought bravely on various battlefields, repelling the enemy's repeated infiltrations. According to incomplete statistics, the Lao patriotic military and civilians killed a total of 701 enemy soldiers in these battles, including 420 killed, 182 wounded, and 99 captured.

People of Australia and New Zealand Continue to Hold Anti-American Demonstrations

More than 2,000 Australians braved heavy rain on the 23rd to hold two demonstrations in Sydney, protesting against American aggression in Vietnam and opposing the Australian government's role as an accomplice to the U.S. in the Vietnam War.

In recent days, similar demonstrations were held in Wellington, Auckland, and Christchurch in New Zealand, protesting against American imperialism's aggression in Vietnam and the presence of American warships in New Zealand ports.

Brazilian Students Demonstrate Against American Cultural Aggression

On the 24th, university students in Rio de Janeiro held a demonstration and march, protesting against the agreement signed by the U.S. and the Brazilian government to "reform" Brazil's educational system. The U.S. signed this agreement to make Brazilian culture a colony of the U.S. According to reports, students in at least nine states held demonstrations, and students in six states went on strike.

### 简明新闻

1967-05-29

专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    越南北方击落美国强盗飞机八架
    美国强盗飞机二十七日下午袭击越南北方河北省的一些居民区。河北省军民给以迎头痛击，当场击落美国飞机两架，击伤多架，活捉美国飞贼一批。另据补充公布，义安等地的军民还打下美国强盗飞机六架。
    老挝爱国军民四月份歼敌七百多
    老挝爱国军民四月间在各个战场上英勇作战，击溃了敌人的多次蚕食进攻。据不完全统计，老挝爱国军民在这些战斗中共消灭敌人七百零一名，其中打死四百二十名，打伤一百八十二名，俘虏九十九名。
    澳新两国人民不断举行反美示威
    两千多澳大利亚人民不顾下着大雨，二十三日在悉尼举行了两次示威，抗议美国侵略越南和反对澳大利亚政府充当美国侵越帮凶。
    在新西兰的惠灵顿、奥克兰和克赖斯特彻奇，最近几天也举行了群众示威，抗议美帝国主义侵略越南和美国军舰在新西兰港口停留。
    巴西学生示威抗议美帝文化侵略
    巴西里约热内卢的大学生二十四日举行示威游行，抗议美帝同巴西政府签订所谓“改革”巴西教育制度的协定。美国签订这项协定是为了使巴西文化殖民主义化。据报道，至少有九个州学生举行了示威，六个州学生举行了罢课。
                            （据新华社讯）
