Title: Hong Kong Workers Arrested by British Authorities Brave Violence and Fight Back
Author:
Date: 1967-05-15
Edition: 2

Hong Kong workers who were unjustly arrested by the British authorities bravely defied violence and fought back. Even while handcuffed inside a police car, they held high the "Quotations from Chairman Mao," angrily protesting against the fascist atrocities committed by the British authorities.
