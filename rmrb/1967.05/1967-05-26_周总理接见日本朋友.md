Title: Premier Zhou Receives Japanese Friends
Author:
Date: 1967-05-26
Edition: 3

Premier Zhou received the Japanese Journalists' League delegation visiting China, led by team leader Xiao Lin Yongyi and deputy team leader Xiao Quan Xianwu, and had a cordial conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Vice Chairman Mu Xin of the All-China Journalists Association, First Secretary Lü Bin of the All-China Journalists Association, and Director Qin Jialin of the News Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, among others.

### 周总理接见日本朋友

1967-05-26
第3版()
专栏：

    周总理接见日本朋友
    新华社二十五日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见由团长小林雄一、副团长小泉省吾率领的日本记者同盟访华代表团的全体成员，同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会副主任穆欣，中华全国新闻工作者协会第一书记吕彬，外交部新闻司司长秦加林等。
