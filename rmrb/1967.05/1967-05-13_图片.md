Title: Propaganda Team of Shanghai Municipal Bureau of Transportation Performs for Workers
Author: Gù Sōngnián
Date: 1967-05-13
Edition: 1

The propaganda team of the Shanghai Municipal Bureau of Transportation, guided by Mao Zedong Thought, performed at the Shanghai Automobile Transportation Fourth Depot. They presented a cultural program praising the "Three Articles" for the workers.
