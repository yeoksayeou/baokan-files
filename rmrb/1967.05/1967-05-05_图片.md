Title: Premier Zhou and Comrade Jiang Qing Meet with the Japan-China Friendship Association (Genuine) Women's Delegation
Author: Zhang Guiyu
Date: 1967-05-05
Edition: 6

    On May 4, Premier Zhou and Comrade Jiang Qing received a delegation from the Japan-China Friendship Association (Genuine) headquarters, led by Nakajima Chiyo (front row, second from the left). The delegation was a women's visiting group to China. The photo was taken by New China News Agency photographer Zhang Guiyu.
