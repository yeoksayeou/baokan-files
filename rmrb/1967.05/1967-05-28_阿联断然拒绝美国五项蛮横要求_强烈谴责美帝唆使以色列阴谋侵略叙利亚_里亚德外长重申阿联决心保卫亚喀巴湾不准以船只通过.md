Title: Egypt Rejects Five American Demands, Condemns US for Inciting Israeli Aggression Against Syria
Author:
Date: 1967-05-28
Edition: 6

Egypt firmly rejects five arrogant American demands and strongly condemns US imperialism for inciting Israel to invade Syria. Foreign Minister Riad reaffirms Egypt's determination to defend the Gulf of Aqaba and prevent Israeli ships from passing through.

CAIRO, May 26 (Xinhua) — According to a report by the Cairo newspaper "Al-Ahram" on May 26, the Egyptian government has firmly rejected five arrogant demands made by the American government. It also strongly condemned US imperialism for inciting Israel to invade Syria.

The newly appointed American ambassador to Egypt presented five ultimatum-style demands to Egyptian Foreign Minister Riad on May 23. These demands are: before the United Nations General Assembly makes a decision, the "UN Emergency Force" should continue to stay in the Gaza Strip and Sharm el-Sheikh (southern tip of the Sinai Peninsula); before the Egyptian government formally declares that the shipping in the Strait of Tiran will not be restricted, Egyptian troops should not enter Sharm el-Sheikh; Egyptian troops should not enter the Gaza Strip; before the issue is resolved, the United Nations and its affiliated organizations should continue to administer the Gaza Strip; Egyptian troops stationed in Sinai and Israeli troops stationed in the Negev should return to their original positions.

Egyptian Foreign Minister Riad rejected all these arrogant demands from US imperialism. At the same time, he condemned US imperialism for creating Israel, supporting, and inciting Israel to invade. He emphasized that this is something Egypt cannot tolerate. "If Israel invades any Arab territory, the United States is an accomplice to this invasion."

Riad said: The Egyptian army's deployment in Sinai and Egypt's decision to prevent Israeli ships from entering and exiting the Gulf of Aqaba are all exercises of Egypt's sovereignty and a response to Israeli aggression.

"Al-Ahram" reported that the United States is doing its utmost to defend, protect, and encourage Israel's aggression. As time goes by, due to the United States' attitude, this confrontation has almost become a confrontation between Arab countries and the United States, rather than between Arab countries and Israel.

On May 25, Egyptian Foreign Minister Riad reiterated that Egypt will not allow Israeli ships to pass through the Gulf of Aqaba and is determined to defend the Gulf of Aqaba. He said: "We believe that any Israeli ship entering our territorial waters is an act of aggression, and we need to take all necessary measures to defend our territory, territorial waters, and the security of our armed forces."

Riad said: "Any attempt by any country to use our territorial waters to transport strategic materials to Israel is an unfriendly act, aiding Israel in its war against Egypt and all Arab countries." He said that Egypt will continue to allow ships other than Israeli ships to freely enter and exit the Strait of Tiran.

### 阿联断然拒绝美国五项蛮横要求  强烈谴责美帝唆使以色列阴谋侵略叙利亚  里亚德外长重申阿联决心保卫亚喀巴湾不准以船只通过

1967-05-28
第6版()
专栏：

    阿联断然拒绝美国五项蛮横要求
    强烈谴责美帝唆使以色列阴谋侵略叙利亚
    里亚德外长重申阿联决心保卫亚喀巴湾不准以船只通过
    新华社开罗二十六日电　据开罗报纸《金字塔报》二十六日报道，阿联政府断然拒绝美国政府提出的五项狂妄要求，并强烈谴责美帝国主义唆使以色列对叙利亚进行侵略。
    新任的美国驻阿联大使二十三日在同阿联外交部长里亚德会谈时，无理提出美国政府对阿联的五项最后通牒式的要求。这五项要求是：在联合国大会作出决定之前，“联合国紧急部队”将继续留在加沙地带和沙姆沙伊赫（西奈半岛南端）；在阿联政府正式宣布保证蒂朗海峡的船运不受限制之前，阿联军队不得进入沙姆沙伊赫；阿联军队不得进入加沙地带；在问题解决之前，联合国及其所属机构继续负责加沙地带的行政管理；集结在西奈的阿联军队和集结在内格夫的以色列军队应该回到他们的原驻地。
    阿联外交部长拒绝了美帝国主义所有这些蛮横要求，同时谴责美帝国主义制造以色列，支持、唆使以色列进行侵略。他强调指出，这是阿联所不能容忍的。“如果以色列在阿拉伯领土上进行任何侵略，美国就是这种侵略的帮凶。”
    里亚德说：阿联军队在西奈集结，阿联决定不准以色列船只出入亚喀巴湾，这一切完全是阿联行使它的主权和回击以色列侵略的任务。
    《金字塔报》在报道这个消息时说，美国正在竭尽全力来捍卫、保护和鼓励以色列的侵略。随着时日的进展，由于美国的态度，这种对抗，几乎已成为阿拉伯国家和美国之间的对抗，而不是阿拉伯国家和以色列之间的对抗。
    二十五日，阿联外长里亚德重申，阿联决不让以色列船只通过亚喀巴湾，并决心保卫亚喀巴湾，他说：“我们认为，任何以色列船只进入我们的领水是一个侵略行动，我们需要为了保卫我们领土、领水和武装部队的安全而采取一切必要的措施。”
    里亚德说：“任何国家利用我们的领水向以色列运送战略物资的任何企图都是不友好的行动，是帮助以色列对付阿联和所有阿拉伯国家的战争活动。”他说，阿联将继续允许除以色列以外的其他船只自由出入蒂朗海峡。
