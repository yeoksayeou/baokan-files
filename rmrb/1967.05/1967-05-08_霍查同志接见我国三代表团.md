Title: Comrade Enver Hoxha Receives Three Chinese Delegations
Author:
Date: 1967-05-08
Edition: 5

Comrade Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania, received on May 6 the Chinese cultural delegation led by Ambassador Liu Xiao of the People's Republic of China to Albania, the Chinese trade union delegation led by Zhao Guoqiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and the Chinese photography delegation led by Shang Jin, who are visiting Albania.

During the reception, Comrade Enver Hoxha had a very cordial and friendly talk with the Chinese comrades.

Present at the reception were Aliya, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania, Yakova, General Secretary of the Central Council of Trade Unions, Treshka, Chairman of the Committee for Cultural and Educational Relations with Foreign Countries, and Simitiu, Director of the Albanian Telegraphic Agency.

### 霍查同志接见我国三代表团

1967-05-08
第5版()
专栏：

    霍查同志接见我国三代表团
    新华社地拉那七日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查同志，六日接见了正在阿尔巴尼亚访问的由中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使刘晓率领的中国文化代表团、由中华全国总工会书记处书记赵国强率领的中国工会代表团和由尚进率领的中国摄影代表团。
    接见时，霍查同志同中国同志进行了十分亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、书记处书记阿利雅，工会中央理事会总书记雅科瓦，对外文委主席特雷斯卡和阿尔巴尼亚通讯社社长西米季乌。
