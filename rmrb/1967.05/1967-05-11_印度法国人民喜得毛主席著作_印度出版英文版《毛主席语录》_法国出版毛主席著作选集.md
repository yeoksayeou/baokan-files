Indian and French People Delighted to Receive Chairman Mao's Works

    Indian and French People Delighted to Receive Chairman Mao's Works
    India Publishes English Version of Chairman Mao's Quotations; France Publishes a Selection of Chairman Mao's Works

    Xinhua News Agency, May 9th: New Delhi reports that the English version of the precious book for the revolutionary people of the world, "Chairman Mao's Quotations," has recently been reprinted and published by the "Thought Publishing House" in the Northern Province of India. This is a great joy for the revolutionary people of India.

    The reprinted "Chairman Mao's Quotations" includes a portrait of Chairman Mao and a preface written by Comrade Lin Biao on December 16, 1966, for the reprint.

    According to Xinhua News Agency, May 6th: New Delhi reports that two brilliant works by Chairman Mao have been translated into Hindi and published by the "Thought Publishing House" in New Delhi. These two articles are "Opposing Liberalism" and "Reform Our Study." After the publication of these two articles, they were warmly welcomed by the broad masses of Indian laborers. The publishing house also received many letters from Mumbai, Hyderabad, Allahabad, Varanasi, and many other places in India.

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, May 9th: The French "Maspero Publishing House" has recently published a pocket edition of a selection of Chairman Mao's works.

    This collection is divided into three volumes, including 21 brilliant works by Chairman Mao such as "Analysis of the Classes in Chinese Society," "On Correcting Mistaken Ideas in the Party," "Problems of Strategy in China's Revolutionary War," "On Practice," "On Contradiction," "On New Democracy," "Talks at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art," and "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People," as well as 18 poems.

    The large-scale publication of Chairman Mao's works in France reflects the widespread enthusiasm of the French people for studying Chairman Mao's works.

### 印度法国人民喜得毛主席著作  印度出版英文版《毛主席语录》　法国出版毛主席著作选集

1967-05-11
第6版()
专栏：

    印度法国人民喜得毛主席著作
    印度出版英文版《毛主席语录》　法国出版毛主席著作选集
    新华社九日讯　新德里消息：全世界革命人民的宝书《毛主席语录》英文版，最近由印度北方邦的“思想出版社”再版发行。这是印度革命人民的一件大喜事。
    再版的《毛主席语录》刊印了一幅毛主席的像片和林彪同志在一九六六年十二月十六日为《毛主席语录》写的再版前言。
    据新华社六日讯  新德里消息：毛主席的两篇光辉著作已经译成印地文，由新德里的“思想出版社”出版。这两篇文章是：《反对自由主义》和《改造我们的学习》。这两篇文章出版后，受到印度广大劳动人民的极大欢迎。出版社还从孟买、海得拉巴、阿拉哈巴德、瓦拉纳西和印度其他许多地方收到很多来信。
    新华社巴黎九日电  法国“马斯佩罗出版社”最近出版了一套袖珍本的毛主席著作选集。
    这一套选集分成三册，共包括毛主席的《中国社会各阶级的分析》、《关于纠正党内的错误思想》、《中国革命战争的战略问题》、《实践论》、《矛盾论》、《新民主主义论》、《在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话》、《关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题》等二十一篇光辉著作和十八首诗词。
    毛主席著作在法国的大量出版，反映了法国人民学习毛主席著作的普遍热情。
