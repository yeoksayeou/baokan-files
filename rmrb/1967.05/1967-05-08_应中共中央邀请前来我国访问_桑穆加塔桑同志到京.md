Title: N. Samarasinghe Comes to Beijing at the Invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1967-05-08
Edition: 2

N. Samarasinghe, a member of the Political Bureau and the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ceylon, arrived in Beijing this noon by plane at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China for a visit and medical treatment, accompanied by his wife and daughter.

Liu Ningyi, a member of the Central Committee and the Secretariat of the Communist Party of China, and Zhao Yimin, an alternate member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, went to the airport to extend a warm welcome.

### 应中共中央邀请前来我国访问  桑穆加塔桑同志到京

1967-05-08
第2版()
专栏：

    应中共中央邀请前来我国访问
    桑穆加塔桑同志到京
    新华社七日讯　应中共中央邀请前来我国访问和治病的锡兰共产党中央政治局委员、书记处书记纳·桑穆加塔桑同志偕夫人和女儿，今天中午乘飞机到达北京。
    中共中央委员、中共中央书记处书记刘宁一同志，中共中央候补委员赵毅敏同志前往机场热烈欢迎。
