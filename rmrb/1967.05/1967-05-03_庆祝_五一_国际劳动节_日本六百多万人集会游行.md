Title: Celebrating International Labor Day
Author:
Date: 1967-05-03
Edition: 6

On May 1, 500,000 revolutionary workers, citizens, and students in Tokyo participated in the central rally and parade to celebrate International Labor Day. Among the rally and parade participants, people carried giant portraits of the great leader Chairman Mao Zedong, who is deeply respected by the revolutionary people of the world.

On this day, a total of 6.6 million people across Japan took part in the celebrations for International Labor Day.

The revolutionary workers, citizens, and students who participated in the central rally and parade held high banners reading "Long live the great victory of the Chinese proletarian cultural revolution!" "Long live, long live, and ten thousand years to the great leader Chairman Mao!" "Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought!" and sang the "Internationale," "The East Is Red," and songs based on Chairman Mao's quotations. They also held banners reading "Down with American imperialism! Down with modern revisionism!" "Smash the anti-China policies of the revisionist elements in the Japanese Communist Party!" "Long live the fighting friendship between the peoples of China and Japan!" and chanted slogans such as "Oppose American imperialism's aggression in Vietnam!" "Smash the U.S.-Japan 'Security Treaty'!" "Drive American imperialism out of Japan and Asia!"

On the same day, revolutionary masses in Yamaguchi Prefecture participated in rallies and parades in Yamaguchi City and Shimonoseki City.

In Shimonoseki, revolutionary workers carried banners reading "Firmly support the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. aggression for national salvation!" "Strive for the realization of the second Sino-Japanese Youth Friendship Festival!" "Smash the anti-China policies of the revisionist elements in the Japanese Communist Party!" and participated in the rally and demonstration to celebrate International Labor Day.

Tokyo's revolutionary workers, citizens, and students, 500,000 in total, participated in the central rally and parade to celebrate International Labor Day. They carried giant portraits of the great leader Chairman Mao Zedong and marched proudly down the streets. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency)

### 庆祝“五一”国际劳动节  日本六百多万人集会游行

1967-05-03
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝“五一”国际劳动节
    日本六百多万人集会游行
    新华社一日讯　据东方通讯社报道：东京的革命工人、市民和学生五十万人，参加了庆祝“五一”国际劳动节的中央集会和游行。在集会和游行队伍中，人们抬着世界革命人民最敬爱的伟大领袖毛泽东主席的巨幅画像。
    这一天，日本全国共有六百六十万人参加了庆祝“五一”国际劳动节的活动。
    参加中央集会和游行的革命工人、市民和学生，高举着“中国无产阶级文化大革命胜利万岁！”、“伟大的领袖毛主席万岁！万岁！万万岁！”、“战无不胜的毛泽东思想万岁！”等标语牌，高声歌唱《国际歌》、《大海航行靠舵手》和毛主席语录歌。他们还高举着“打倒美帝国主义！打倒现代修正主义！”、“粉碎日共修正主义分子的反华政策！”、“日中两国人民的战斗友谊万岁！”等横幅标语，高呼“反对美帝国主义侵略越南！”“粉碎日美‘安全条约’！”“把美帝国主义从日本和亚洲赶出去！”等口号。
    山口县的革命群众这一天参加了山口市和下关市的集会和游行。
    在下关市，革命的工人高举着“坚决支持越南人民的抗美救国斗争！”“争取实现第二次日中青年友好大联欢！”“粉碎日共修正主义分子的反华政策！”等标语，参加了庆祝“五一”国际劳动节的集会和示威。（附图片）
    东京的革命工人、市民和学生五十万人，参加了庆祝“五一”国际劳动节的中央集会和游行。他们抬着世界革命人民最敬爱的伟大领袖毛泽东主席的巨幅画像，阔步走在大街上。　传真照片（新华社发）
