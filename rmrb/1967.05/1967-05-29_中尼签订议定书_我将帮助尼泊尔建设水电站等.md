Title: China and Nepal Sign Agreement
Author:
Date: 1967-05-29
Edition: 7

The governments of the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Nepal signed an agreement in Kathmandu on the 25th regarding the Chinese government's assistance to the Nepalese government in the construction of the Sun Koshi hydroelectric power station and the installation of a power transmission line.

The Chinese government delegation, led by Lin Haiyun, and Nepal's Minister of Communications, Viyati, signed the agreement. Both sides spoke highly of the deepening friendship between China and Nepal.

Those present at the signing ceremony included: Nepal's Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Kirtinidhi Bista, Minister of Defense, Giri Prasad Budhathoki, Minister of Industry and Commerce, Nakindra Prasad Rijal, Minister of Health, Food, Agriculture, and Land Reform, Tribhuvan Prasad Pandey, and senior officials. China's Ambassador to Nepal, Yang Gongsu, also attended the signing ceremony.

### 中尼签订议定书  我将帮助尼泊尔建设水电站等

1967-05-29
第7版()
专栏：

    中尼签订议定书
    我将帮助尼泊尔建设水电站等
    据新华社加德满都电　中华人民共和国政府和尼泊尔王国政府二十五日在加德满都签署了一项关于中国政府帮助尼泊尔政府建设逊科西水电站和架设一条输电线的议定书。
    中国政府代表团团长林海云和尼泊尔交通大臣维亚蒂特分别在议定书上签字，并先后讲话，赞扬中尼友谊日益增进。
    出席签字仪式的有：尼泊尔大臣会议副主席兼外交大臣基尔提·尼迪·比斯塔，国防大臣吉里·普拉萨德·布达托基，工商大臣纳金德拉·普拉萨德·里贾尔，卫生、粮食、农业和土地改革大臣特里贝尼·普拉萨德·普拉丹和高级官员。中国驻尼泊尔大使杨公素也出席了签字仪式。
