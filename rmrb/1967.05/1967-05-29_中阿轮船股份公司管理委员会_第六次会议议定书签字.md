Title: Signing of the Minutes of the Sixth Meeting of the Management Committee of the Sino-Albanian Shipping Company
Author:
Date: 1967-05-29
Edition: 7

The Management Committee of the Sino-Albanian Shipping Company held its sixth meeting and signed the minutes today. The New China News Agency reported on May 28 that the Chinese delegation of the Ministry of Communications and the Albanian delegation of the Ministry of Communications held the signing ceremony of the minutes of the sixth meeting of the Management Committee of the Sino-Albanian Shipping Company this morning. The Deputy Minister of Communications of China and the Chairman of the Chinese side of the Management Committee of the Sino-Albanian Shipping Company, Peng Deqing, and the Deputy Minister of Communications of Albania and the Chairman of the Albanian side of the Management Committee of the Sino-Albanian Shipping Company, Marko Karoli, signed the minutes on behalf of both sides.

### 中阿轮船股份公司管理委员会  第六次会议议定书签字

1967-05-29
第7版()
专栏：

    中阿轮船股份公司管理委员会
    第六次会议议定书签字
    新华社二十八日讯　中国交通部代表团和阿尔巴尼亚交通部代表团今天上午举行了中阿轮船股份公司管理委员会第六次会议议定书签字仪式。中国交通部副部长、中阿轮船股份公司管理委员会中方主任委员彭德清和阿尔巴尼亚交通部副部长、中阿轮船股份公司阿方主任委员马尔科·卡罗利分别代表双方在议定书上签字。
