Title: Coal Conservation Exhibition Opens
Author: 
Date: 1957-09-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 1st. The Coal Conservation Exhibition opened today at the Soviet Exhibition Center in Beijing. The Coal Conservation Exhibition is sponsored by the China Coal Construction Company and is scheduled to last for one month.

### 节约用煤展览会开幕

1957-09-03
第5版()
专栏：

    节约用煤展览会开幕
    据新华社1日讯  节约用煤展览会今天在北京苏联展览馆开幕。节约用煤展览会是中国煤建公司主办的，展览会预定展出一个月。
