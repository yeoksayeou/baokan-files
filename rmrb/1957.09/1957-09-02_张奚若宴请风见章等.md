Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts a Banquet for Kazami Akira and Others
Author: 
Date: 1957-09-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet tonight for Kazami Akira, advisor of the Japanese Socialist Party, director of the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations, and Japanese Senator Abe Kimiko.

Among those present at the banquet were Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China; Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee; Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; and Qiao Guanhua, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 张奚若宴请风见章等

1957-09-02
第3版()
专栏：

    张奚若宴请风见章等
    新华社1日讯  中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今晚宴请了日本社会党顾问、日本恢复日中邦交国民会议理事长风见章和日本参议院议员安部君子。
    出席宴会的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长陈叔通，中国红十字会会长李德全，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民和中国人民外交学会副会长乔冠华等。
