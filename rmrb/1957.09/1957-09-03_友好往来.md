Friendly Exchanges

    Japanese Yamanashi Prefecture Industry and Culture Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, September 2nd. The Japanese Yamanashi Prefecture Industry and Culture Delegation, consisting of nine members led by the head of the delegation, Ono Nagahide, arrived in Beijing by train on August 31st at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.
    Mao Xiding Leaves Beijing for Home
    Xinhua News Agency, September 2nd. Khaled Mohammed Mao Xiding, a member of the Egyptian National Assembly and editor-in-chief of "Al-Messa" newspaper, left Beijing by plane this morning and will return home via Guangzhou.
    Those who went to the airport to see him off included Chen Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, responsible persons from the Sino-Egyptian Friendship Association, people from all walks of life, and the Egyptian Ambassador to China, Ragab, etc.
    Vietnamese, Indonesian, and Thai Youth Representatives Arrive in Beijing
    According to Xinhua News Agency, September 2nd. Seven Vietnamese youth representatives, two Indonesian youth representatives, and three Thai youth representatives arrived in Beijing by plane from Moscow today. They are the third group of visitors to China at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation, after participating in the Sixth World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival.

### 友好往来

1957-09-03
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    日本山梨县产业、
    文化访华团到京
    新华社2日讯  应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的日本山梨县产业、文化访华代表团一行九人，在团长小野永雄率领下在8月31日乘火车到达北京。
    毛希丁离京回国
    新华社2日讯  埃及国民议会议员、“晚报”总编辑哈立德·穆罕默德·毛希丁今天早晨乘飞机离开北京，将经广州回国。
    到机场欢送的，有中国人民外交学会副会长陈翰笙和中埃友好协会负责人，各界人士，以及埃及驻中国大使拉加卜等。
    越南、印度尼西亚、
    泰国青年代表到京
    据新华社2日讯  越南青年代表七人、印度尼西亚青年代表二人和泰国青年代表三人，今天乘飞机从莫斯科到达北京。他们是在参加第六届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节后，应中华全国民主青年联合会的邀请，第三批来中国进行访问的。
