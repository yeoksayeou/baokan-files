Title: Vukmanović Arrives in Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1957-09-16
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, September 15th: At the invitation of the Mongolian government, the Vice-President of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia, Vukmanović, arrived in Ulaanbaatar by plane at noon today for a friendly visit.
    Leaders of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the government, Tsedenbal, Damdin, Molomjamts, Jagvaral, as well as the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia He Ying, the Soviet Ambassador Molotov, and diplomatic envoys from various countries went to the airport to welcome him.

### 伏克曼诺维奇到乌兰巴托

1957-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　伏克曼诺维奇到乌兰巴托
    据新华社乌兰巴托15日电  南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席伏克曼诺维奇应蒙古政府的邀请，在今天中午坐飞机到达乌兰巴托进行友好访问。
    蒙古人民革命党和政府的领导人曾德、达木丁、莫洛木扎姆茨、扎格瓦尔拉以及中国驻蒙古大使何英、苏联大使莫洛托夫和各国外交使节曾经前往机场欢迎。
