Title: Yugoslav Parliamentary Delegation Visits Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1957-09-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, September 24th: The Yugoslav Federal National Assembly Delegation left Lüda by special plane at 8:00 this morning for Hangzhou, via Tianjin and Nanjing. Among those who saw them off at the airport were Song Li, Mayor of Lüda, Yu Guying, Deputy Mayor, and Zhang Zhicheng, Vice Chairman of the Lüda Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. The Soviet Deputy Consul in Lüda, Preditbailo, also came to the airport to see them off.
    Yesterday, the distinguished guests visited the Lushun Museum and the Lushun Military History Museum in Lushun, and also visited the naval forces of the People's Liberation Army. The distinguished guests visited the naval fleet and took photos with the naval officers and soldiers on the warship. Chairman Stambolic also spoke to the naval officers and soldiers.
    Before visiting Lushun yesterday morning, the distinguished guests visited the Dalian Shipyard in Dalian.

### 南议会代表团去杭州访问

1957-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

    南议会代表团去杭州访问
    新华社旅大24日电　南斯拉夫联邦国民议会代表团今天上午八时乘专机离开旅大，经天津、南京去杭州访问。到机场欢送的有旅大市市长宋黎、副市长于谷莺，政协旅大市委员会副主席张志诚等人。苏联驻旅大副领事普利第巴依洛也到机场欢送。
    昨天，贵宾们到旅顺参观了旅顺博物馆和旅顺军事历史博物馆，并访问了人民解放军海军部队。贵宾们参观了海军编队舰艇，在军舰上同海军官兵一起照了像，斯坦鲍利奇主席并向海军官兵讲了话。
    贵宾们昨天上午去旅顺访问前，曾在大连参观了大连造船厂。
