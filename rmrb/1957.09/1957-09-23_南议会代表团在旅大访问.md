Title: Yugoslav Parliament Delegation Visits Lüda
Author:
Date: 1957-09-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, September 22nd: The Yugoslav Federal National Assembly Delegation, led by Chairman Stanbolić, concluded its visit to Shenyang and left Shenyang by special plane at 8:30 this morning to visit Lüda. Wu Xinyu, Deputy Director of the Bills Committee of the National People's Congress, accompanied them. The Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popović, also accompanied them.

The distinguished guests visited the Dalian Seaport and toured the city. In the afternoon, the distinguished guests visited the Dalian Locomotive and Rolling Stock Plant and visited the Jin County Friendship Orchard Collective Farm.

In the evening, the distinguished guests were invited to attend a banquet held by Song Li, Mayor of Lüda.

### 南议会代表团在旅大访问

1957-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    南议会代表团在旅大访问
    据新华社沈阳22日电　以斯坦鲍利奇主席为首的南斯拉夫联邦国民议会代表团结束在沈阳的访问，今天上午八时三十分乘专机离开沈阳去旅大访问。全国人民代表大会法案委员会副主任委员武新宇陪同前往。南斯拉夫驻中国大使波波维奇也随同前往。
    贵宾们参观了大连海港并游览了市容。午后，贵宾们参观了大连机车车辆厂，访问了金县友谊果园集体农庄。
    晚间，贵宾们应邀出席了旅大市市长宋黎举行的宴会。
