Title: Indian Vice President Leaves Shanghai for Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1957-09-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, September 26th: The Vice President of the Republic of India, Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan, accompanied by Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Guo Moruo, and the Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru and his wife, left Shanghai by special plane this morning for Hangzhou.

At the airport, Vice President Radhakrishnan received flowers presented by the Young Pioneers. He put his palms together to bid farewell to Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Soong Ching Ling, Vice Mayors of Shanghai, Cao Diqiu, Liu Jiping, Jin Zhonghua, Rong Yiren, and people from all walks of life who came to see him off, and took group photos with the well-wishers.

Also at the airport to see him off today were consuls of various countries stationed in Shanghai and Indian nationals residing in Shanghai.

### 印度副总统离沪去杭州

1957-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    印度副总统离沪去杭州
    新华社上海26日电　印度共和国副总统拉达克里希南由郭沫若副委员长、印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁夫妇陪同，今天上午乘专机离上海去杭州。
    拉达克里希南副总统在机场上接受了少先队员的献花。他合掌向前来欢送的宋庆龄副委员长，上海市副市长曹荻秋、刘季平、金仲华、荣毅仁和各界人士道别，并和欢送的人合影留念。
    今天到机场送行的还有各国驻上海领事及印度在上海的侨民。
