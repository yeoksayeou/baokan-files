    Brief News
    
    Socialist People's Front of Malaya Established
    Kuala Lumpur dispatch: The Malayan People's Party and the Labour Party decided to establish a Socialist People's Front on August 31.
    A spokesman for the organization said that the goals of the Socialist People's Front are: to establish a "democratic socialist state" in Malaya; to cooperate with all foreign socialist organizations; to promote the political, social, and economic liberation of the people; and to strive for the merger of Singapore and Malaya.

    French Ships Resume Carrying Egyptian Goods
    Cairo dispatch: French merchant ships have resumed carrying Egyptian goods. The French merchant ship "Cosser" left Port Said on September 3 carrying Egyptian onions to Singapore. Another French ship left Alexandria two days earlier carrying Egyptian cotton to France.

    Finnish Government Reorganized
    Helsinki dispatch: Finnish President Urho Kekkonen accepted the resignations of six ministers on September 2 and appointed new government members to replace them. The Prime Minister of the reorganized Finnish government is still V.J. Sukselainen (Agrarian League). The Deputy Prime Minister and Director of the Office of the Council of State is A. Simonen (opposition faction within the Social Democratic Party). The ministers include members of the Agrarian League, the Finnish People's Party, and the opposition faction within the Social Democratic Party.

    简讯
    马来亚社会主义人民阵线成立
    吉隆坡消息：马来亚人民党和劳工党在8月31日决定成立一个社会主义人民阵线。
    这个组织的发言人说，社会主义人民阵线的目标是：在马来亚建立一个“民主的社会主义国家”；同外国的一切社会主义团体合作；促进人民在政治、社会和经济方面的解放；争取新加坡和马来亚合并。
    法国船恢复载运埃及货
    开罗消息：法国商船已经恢复载运埃及的货物。9月3日离开塞得港的法国商船“科塞尔号”载着埃及洋葱驶往新加坡。两天前离开亚历山大港的另一艘法国船载着埃及棉花驶往法国。
    芬兰政府改组
    赫尔辛基讯：芬兰共和国总统吉科宁9月2日接受了政府六个部长的辞职，并且任命了新的政府委员代替他们。改组的芬兰政府总理仍为维·苏克西莱宁（农民联盟）。副总理兼国务院办公厅主任是阿·西莫宁（社会民主党内反对派）。部长中包括农民联盟、芬兰人民党、社会民主党内反对派的人。
