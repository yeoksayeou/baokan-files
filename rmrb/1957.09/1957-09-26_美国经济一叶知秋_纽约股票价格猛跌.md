Title: American Economy: One Leaf Signals the Coming of Autumn; New York Stock Prices Plunge
Author:
Date: 1957-09-26
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 24th: New York stock market prices plummeted on the 23rd, with stock prices falling to their lowest point in two years.

Stockbrokers said that the stock price decline caused by the strong selling pressure "clearly reflects Wall Street's general pessimism (about the economic future)."

The decline began last week, with stocks in almost all major economic groups being hit. Caterpillar Tractor Company's stock fell by more than four dollars per share. Other large monopolies such as Firestone Tire & Rubber Company, Douglas Aircraft Company, DuPont, Baltimore & Ohio Railroad Company, and Gulf Oil Corporation were not spared.

The decline resulted in a total loss of $4.46 billion in the value of stocks listed on the New York Stock Exchange.

### 美国经济一叶知秋  纽约股票价格猛跌

1957-09-26
第7版()
专栏：

    美国经济一叶知秋
    纽约股票价格猛跌
    据新华社24日讯　纽约消息：纽约股票市场价格23日出现猛跌，股票价格下跌到两年以来的最低点。
    股票经纪人说，这次由于强烈的抛风造成的股票跌价“显然反映了华尔街（对经济前途）的普遍的悲观情绪”。
    这次下跌是在上星期开始的，几乎所有重要的经济集团的股票都受到打击，有名的履带拖拉机公司的股票每股跌了四美元多，其他的大垄断公司如费尔斯通轮胎橡胶公司、道格拉斯飞机公司、杜邦公司、巴尔的摩·俄亥俄铁路公司和海湾石油公司等都没有幸免。
    这次下跌使纽约股票交易所挂牌的股票总值一共损失了四十四亿六千万美元。
