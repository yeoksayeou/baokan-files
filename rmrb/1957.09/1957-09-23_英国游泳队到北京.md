Title: British Swimming Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-23
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd: The British swimming team, a group of thirteen people, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the All-China Sports Federation to visit our country.

### 英国游泳队到北京

1957-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    英国游泳队到北京
    据新华社22日讯　应中华全国体育总会邀请来我国访问的英国游泳队一行十三人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
