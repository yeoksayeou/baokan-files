Title: Hungarian Government Delegation Departs for China, Premier Kádár Heads the Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-09-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, September 24th: At the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, the Hungarian Government Delegation departed for China on the 24th for a visit.

The head of the delegation is Comrade János Kádár, Premier of the Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government. The members are Comrade György Marosán, Minister of State, and Comrade Sándor Nógrádi, Hungarian Ambassador to the People's Republic of China.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 24th: The Hungarian Government Delegation, headed by Premier Kádár, which is on its way to visit China, arrived in Moscow by plane at 1:30 PM today. Soviet leaders such as Aristov, Mikoyan, and Suslov were at the airport to welcome them.

The Hungarian Government Delegation will stay here for two days.

### 匈政府代表团启程来华  卡达尔总理担任团长

1957-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    匈政府代表团启程来华
    卡达尔总理担任团长
    新华社布达佩斯24日电　应中华人民共和国政府的邀请，匈牙利政府代表团在24日启程赴中国访问。
    代表团团长是工农革命政府总理卡达尔·亚诺什同志。团员是国务部长马罗山·久尔吉同志和匈牙利人民共和国驻华大使诺格拉第·山道尔同志。
    新华社莫斯科24日电　以卡达尔总理为首的前往中国访问的匈牙利政府代表团今日下午一时半乘飞机到达莫斯科。阿里斯托夫、米高扬、苏斯洛夫等苏联领导人前往机场欢迎。
    匈牙利政府代表团将在这里停留两日。
