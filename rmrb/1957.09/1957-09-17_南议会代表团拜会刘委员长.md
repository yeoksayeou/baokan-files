Title: Yugoslav Parliamentary Delegation Pays a Visit to Chairman Liu

Author:
Date: 1957-09-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th — The President of the Federal People's Assembly of Yugoslavia, Petar Stambolić, and the Yugoslav Federal People's Assembly delegation he led, paid a visit to Chairman Liu Shaoqi at 7 p.m. today. Present at the meeting were Vice-Chairmen Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, and Liu Geping, Zhang Su, Ji Pengfei, Wu Kejian, Yu Xinqing, Liu Guanyi, and Wu Xinyu. The Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popović, was also present at the meeting.

### 南议会代表团拜会刘委员长

1957-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　南议会代表团拜会刘委员长
    新华社16日讯　南斯拉夫联邦国民议会主席彼得·斯坦鲍利奇和他率领的南斯拉夫联邦国民议会代表团，今天下午七时拜会了刘少奇委员长。拜会时在座的有林伯渠、李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培、彭真、李维汉、陈叔通副委员长和刘格平、张苏、姬鹏飞、吴克坚、余心清、刘贯一、武新宇。拜会时，南斯拉夫驻中国大使波波维奇也在座。
