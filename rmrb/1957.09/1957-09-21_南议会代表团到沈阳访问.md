Title: Yugoslav Parliamentary Delegation Visits Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1957-09-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, September 20th: The Yugoslav Federal National Assembly Delegation, led by Chairman Stambolic, arrived in Shenyang by special plane at 10:40 this morning for a visit. Wu Xinyu, Deputy Director of the Legislative Affairs Committee of the National People's Congress, accompanied them. Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, also came along.

The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by Zhang Xuexuan, Deputy Governor of Liaoning Province, Xu Zhi, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, He Qingji, Major General of the Liaoning Garrison, and responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations.

In the afternoon, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Deputy Mayor Xu Zhi, visited the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant. Before the visit, Chairman Stambolic spoke at a welcome rally held by the factory's employees.

The distinguished guests also visited the production situation of the Shenyang Cable Factory. In the evening, the distinguished guests attended a banquet hosted by Zhang Xuexuan, Deputy Governor of Liaoning Province.

### 南议会代表团到沈阳访问

1957-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

    南议会代表团到沈阳访问
    据新华社沈阳20日电　以斯坦鲍利奇主席为首的南斯拉夫联邦国民议会代表团，今天上午十时四十分乘专机来到沈阳访问。全国人民代表大会法案委员会副主任武新宇陪同前来。南斯拉夫驻中国大使波波维奇也随同前来。
    贵宾们在机场上受到辽宁省副省长张雪轩、沈阳市副市长徐志、辽宁驻军首长贺庆绩少将和各民主党派、人民团体负责人等的热烈欢迎。
    下午，贵宾们在徐志副市长陪同下参观了沈阳第一机床厂。参观前，斯坦鲍利奇主席在这个工厂举行的职工欢迎大会上讲了话。
    贵宾们还参观了沈阳电缆厂的生产情形。晚上，贵宾们出席了辽宁省副省长张雪轩举行的宴会。
