Title: Indian Vice President Watches Peking Opera Performance
Author:
Date: 1957-09-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd:

    Indian Republic Vice President Radhakrishnan, accompanied by Vice Chairman Zhu De and Vice Premier He Long, watched a Peking Opera performance tonight.
    The Peking Opera evening was held by the Ministry of Culture to welcome Vice President Radhakrishnan. At the evening, Peking Opera actors Li Hezeng, Yuan Shihai, Du Jinfang, Jing Rongqing, Zhang Chunhua, and Huang Yuhua performed "Generals and Ministers Reconcile," "Farewell My Concubine," and "Autumn River" respectively.
    Vice President Radhakrishnan's entourage and Indian Ambassador to China, R. K. Nehru, also attended the evening.
    Also accompanying the viewing were Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, among others.
    After watching the performance, Vice President Radhakrishnan presented flower baskets to the actors and went on stage to meet them.

### 印度副总统观看京剧表演

1957-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    印度副总统观看京剧表演
    新华社23日讯　印度共和国副总统拉达克里希南今晚由朱德副主席和贺龙副总理陪同观看了京剧。
    京剧晚会是文化部为欢迎拉达克里希南副总统而举行的。晚会上，京剧演员李和曾、袁世海、杜近芳、景荣庆、张春华、黄玉华分别演出了“将相和”、“霸王别姬”和“秋江”。
    拉达克里希南副总统的随行人员、印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁也出席了晚会。
    陪同观剧的还有文化部部长沈雁冰和外交部副部长姬鹏飞等人。
    拉达克里希南副总统在看了表演以后，向演员们赠送了花篮，并登台同演员们会见。
