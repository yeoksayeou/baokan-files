Title: Khrushchev Receives the Bevans

Author: 
Date: 1957-09-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, September 18th: The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, received Bevan, a leader of the British Labour Party and a member of the British Parliament, and his wife, Jennie Lee, a member of the British Parliament, on the 17th.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见比万夫妇

1957-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见比万夫妇
    据新华社18日讯　苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫17日接见了英国工党领导人之一、英国议会议员比万和他的夫人、英国议会议员珍尼·李。
