Title: Soviet Fleet to Conduct Exercises in Northern Seas
Author:
Date: 1957-09-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 3rd Telegraph: The Soviet Ministry of Defense announced:

    In accordance with the combat training plan, the Soviet Northern Fleet will conduct exercises in the Barents Sea and the Kara Sea in September and October this year, with the participation of warships and the air force. Various modern weapons will be used in actual combat during the exercises.
    Therefore, the following areas are declared dangerous areas for navigation of Soviet and foreign ships and flight of aircraft from September 10 to October 15 this year: the western boundary is 42 degrees 0 minutes east longitude, the northern boundary is 77 degrees 30 minutes north latitude, the southern boundary is 70 degrees 30 minutes north latitude, and the eastern boundary is a line connecting the following points: 70 degrees 30 minutes north latitude, 57 degrees 30 minutes east longitude, 72 degrees 20 minutes north latitude, 65 degrees 0 minutes east longitude, 77 degrees 30 minutes north latitude, 74 degrees 0 minutes east longitude.
    The Soviet Ministry of Defense warns all owners of Soviet and foreign ships, warships, and aircraft that the Soviet Ministry of Defense will not be responsible for any material losses suffered if ships, warships, and aircraft enter the above-mentioned dangerous areas.

### 苏舰队将在北部海上演习

1957-09-04
第6版()
专栏：

    苏舰队将在北部海上演习
    新华社3日讯　塔斯社莫斯科3日电：苏联国防部宣布：
    按照战斗训练计划，苏联北方舰队将于今年9、10月间在巴伦支海和喀拉海进行有军舰和空军参加的演习，演习时将实际使用各种现代武器。
    因此宣布下列地区在今年9月10日到10月15日期间为苏联和外国船只航行及飞机飞行的危险地区：西界为东经四十二度零分，北界为北纬七十七度三十分，南界为北纬七十度三十分，东界为连结下列诸点之线：北纬七十度三十分，东经五十七度三十分，北纬七十二度二十分，东经六十五度零分，北纬七十七度三十分，东经七十四度零分。
    苏联国防部警告所有苏联和外国船只、军舰和飞机的所有者，如果船只、军舰和飞机进入上述危险地区因而遭到任何物质损失，苏联国防部将不负责任。
