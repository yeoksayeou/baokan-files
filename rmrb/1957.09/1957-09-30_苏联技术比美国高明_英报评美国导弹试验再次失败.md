Title: Soviet Technology Superior to US; British Newspaper Comments on Another Failed US Missile Test
Author:
Date: 1957-09-30
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, London, September 28th - According to TASS, the "England Daily" commented on the second failed test of the American "Atlas" ballistic missile, saying that currently, the Soviet Union is definitely technologically ahead of the United States.

	The newspaper said: "This week is ominous for the prestige of the United States. Closely following the moral failure due to the Little Rock incident is a technical failure - the second failed test of the 'Atlas' missile."

	The newspaper said, "When the Soviet Union declared last month that it had successfully tested a missile capable of reaching any region in the world, the United States panicked. Despite official statements that the United States is on par with the Soviet Union in this important sector, Congress has shown clear signs of unease…". The newspaper continued: "The apparent Soviet advantage once again demonstrates the contrast in the technological strength of the two countries."

### 苏联技术比美国高明  英报评美国导弹试验再次失败

1957-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联技术比美国高明
    英报评美国导弹试验再次失败
    据新华社29日讯　据塔斯社伦敦28日讯：“英格兰人报”评论美国放射“阿特拉斯”式弹道导弹的第二次试验失败说，目前，苏联在技术方面肯定的比美国领先。
    这家报纸说：“这个星期对美国的威望是不吉利的。紧跟着由于小石城事件而在道义上的失败而来的是技术上的失败——即‘阿特拉斯’式导弹第二次试验失败”。
    报纸说，“当苏联在上月声明已经试验成功了能达到世界上任何地区的导弹时，美国惊惶失措。尽管官方声明说美国在这个重要部门同苏联并驾齐驱，但是国会却表现了明显的不安迹象……”。报纸接着说：“苏联显明的优势再一次表明了这两个国家的技术力量的对比”。
