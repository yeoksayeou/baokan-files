Title: Masses in Various Parts of Syria Hold Rallies to Condemn U.S. Aggression Plot
Author:
Date: 1957-09-29
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 28th. It is learned here that mass rallies have been continuously held in Damascus and Latakia for the past two days to condemn the U.S. aggression intentions against Syria. It is also learned that a preparatory committee composed of representatives from the Communist Party, the Arab Ba'ath Socialist Party, the Nationalist Party, and trade unions has been established in Homs, preparing to hold a similar rally in Homs next Monday.

### 叙各地群众纷纷集会  谴责美国的侵略阴谋

1957-09-29
第7版()
专栏：

    叙各地群众纷纷集会
    谴责美国的侵略阴谋
    新华社大马士革28日电　据这里获悉，两天来大马士革和拉塔基亚不断举行群众大会，谴责美国对叙利亚的侵略意图。还获悉，霍姆斯成立了由共产党、阿拉伯复兴社会党、国民党和工会的代表组成的筹备委员会，准备在下星期一在霍姆斯举行类似的大会。
