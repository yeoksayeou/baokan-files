Title: Chairman Yugov Visits Northeast China, Tours Geological Institute and Film Studio in Changchun
Author:
Date: 1957-09-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 17th: Chairman Yugov Visits Northeast China
Tours Geological Institute and Film Studio in Changchun

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 17th: The Government Delegation of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, headed by Chairman Yugov, left Beijing by special plane this morning to visit various places in Northeast China. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria Zhou Zhuan, and Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building Cao Xiangren accompanied the delegation to the Northeast.
　　Those who saw them off at the airport today included Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, and Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China Li Yonghao.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, September 17th: The Bulgarian government delegation, headed by Chairman Yugov, arrived in Changchun by special plane at 11:50 this morning for a visit.
　　The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by Governor of Jilin Province Li Youwen, First Secretary of the Jilin Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party Wu De, Commander of the Jilin Provincial Garrison Major General Shu Xing, heads of various departments of the Jilin Province and Changchun Municipal Government, representatives of democratic parties, people's organizations, and more than a thousand people from all walks of life.
　　Consul Li Seung-an of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in Changchun also came to the airport to welcome them.
　　After arriving in Changchun, the distinguished guests first visited the Changchun Geological Exploration College. In this college, the distinguished guests carefully visited the Mineral Geology Exhibition Hall, which houses 3,800 various mineral specimens, as well as various laboratories and equipment.
　　The distinguished guests also visited the Changchun Film Studio today to see the film production and translation. The delegation accepted a gift from the film studio, presented by Director Yama on behalf of all the staff.
　　In the evening, the distinguished guests attended a banquet hosted by Li Youwen, Governor of Jilin Province.

### 于哥夫主席去东北访问  在长春参观了地质学院和制片厂

1957-09-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　于哥夫主席去东北访问
　　在长春参观了地质学院和制片厂
    新华社17日讯　以于哥夫主席为首的保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团今天上午乘专机离开北京去东北各地参观。外交部副部长曾涌泉、中国驻保加利亚大使周竹安、第一机械工业部副部长曹祥仁等陪同代表团前往东北。
    今天到机场送行的，有国务院副总理李先念、外交部副部长张闻天和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使李永镐等。
    据新华社长春17日电　以于哥夫主席为首的保加利亚政府代表团，在今天上午十一时五十分乘专机来到长春访问。
    贵宾们在机场上受到吉林省省长栗又文、中共吉林省委第一书记吴德、吉林省驻军首长舒行少将、吉林省和长春市政府各部门、各民主党派、人民团体的负责人以及各界人民一千多人的热烈欢迎。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻长春领事李承安也到机场欢迎。
    贵宾们到长春后，首先参观了长春地质勘探学院。贵宾们在这个学院中仔细地参观了收藏着三千八百多块各种矿石标本的矿产地质陈列馆和各实验室、仪器设备等。
    贵宾们今天还到长春电影制片厂参观了影片摄制和译制情形。代表团接受了制片厂厂长亚马代表全厂职工赠送的礼品。
    晚上，贵宾们出席了吉林省省长栗又文举行的宴会。
