Title: Syrian Reactionary Politicians Hiding in Turkey to Plot Treason
Author:
Date: 1957-09-14
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 13th dispatch from London: The Istanbul correspondent of the British "News Chronicle" reported that a group of Syrian reactionary politicians who have fled to Turkey, under the protection of the United States, Turkish, and Iraqi governments, are plotting in Istanbul to establish an organization like a "Syrian government in exile" and are drafting conspiracy plans to overthrow the current Syrian government.

The report says that these reactionary politicians include former Syrian Prime Minister Husni al-Barazi, former Syrian Foreign Minister Riyan, and former Syrian military attache in Rome, Husni, among others. This group of fugitives has settled in Istanbul.

### 叙利亚反动政客  藏在土耳其策划叛国

1957-09-14
第7版()
专栏：

　　叙利亚反动政客
　　藏在土耳其策划叛国
    据新华社13日讯　伦敦消息：英国“新闻纪事报”驻伊斯坦布尔记者报道，一批逃亡在土耳其的叙利亚反动政客，正在美国、土耳其和伊拉克政府的庇护下，在伊斯坦布尔策划成立一个“叙利亚流亡政府”之类的组织，并且拟制推翻叙利亚现政府的阴谋计划。
    消息说，这批反动政客包括前叙利亚总理侯赛尼·巴拉齐、前叙利亚外交部长利安和前叙利亚驻罗马武官侯赛尼等人，这一批亡命之徒定居在伊斯坦布尔。
