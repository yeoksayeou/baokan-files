Title: Award Evaluation Meeting
Author: Su Qu
Date: 1957-09-26
Page: 8

        Award Evaluation Meeting Office: "Listen to my statement first,
                I really don't know the details,
                Whether the deeds are good or bad,
                Everyone can democratically evaluate."
        ×× Section: "Please listen to me first,
                We have many achievements,
                Let's lay out the facts,
                We are not speaking carelessly."
        ×× Section: "Please listen to me,
                We have great achievements,
                Let's present the facts,
                We are not making empty talk."
        Labor Union: "Who is good, after all?
              I really don't know,
              I'll remain silent,
              To avoid saying something wrong."
        Manager: "This matter is really difficult,
              I'm secretly calculating,
              This one is a hero,
              That one is a model,
              If we evaluate him and not him,
              There will definitely be disagreements,
              Better not evaluate anyone at all,
              And the meeting will finally adjourn."
      (Beijing Petroleum Company, Su Qu)

### 评奖会议

1957-09-26
第8版()
专栏：街谈巷议

    评奖会议办公室：“听我先声明，
        实在不知情，
        事迹好与坏，
        大家民主评。”××科：“请听我先说，
        我们成绩多，
        事情摆一摆，
        不是信口说。”××科：“请听我的吧，
        我们成绩大，
        事情摆出来，
        不是说空话。”工会：“到底是谁好，
      我也不知晓，
      哑口不发言，
      免得说错了。”经理：“这事真难办，
      心中暗盘算，
      这个是英雄，
      那个是模范，
      评他不评他，
      一定闹意见，
      干脆全不评，
      会议这才散。”
      （北京市石油公司　苏屈）
