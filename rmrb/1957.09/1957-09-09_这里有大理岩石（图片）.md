Title: Here are Marble Rocks
Author: Chen Jie
Date: 1957-09-09
Page: 4

    Here are Marble Rocks
    The Qinling Bio-Geographic Expedition Team of the Northwest Branch of the Chinese Academy of Sciences in Xi'an, mainly composed of university teachers, recently conducted a 35-day expedition to the West Taibai Mountain in Shaanxi Province. They investigated the natural conditions of plants, geography, geology, and meteorology on the main peak of the Qinling Mountains, which is more than 3,000 meters above sea level. This expedition provided extremely rich and reliable data for future scientific research. This is a photograph taken by Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Jie of geological scientists investigating rocks on West Taibai Mountain, more than 2,000 meters above sea level. They discovered marble rocks in this area.

### 这里有大理岩石（图片）
陈捷
1957-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    这里有大理岩石
    中国科学院西北分院西安办事处组织的一支主要由大学教师组成的秦岭生物地理考察队，不久以前到陕西省西太白山考察了三十五天。他们在拔海三千多公尺的秦岭主峰上考察了植物、地理、地质、气象等自然情况。这一次的考察，给今后的科学研究工作提供了极为丰富的可靠的资料。这是地质科学工作者在拔海二千多公尺的西太白山上考察岩石，他们在这个地区发现大理岩石。　新华社记者　陈捷摄
