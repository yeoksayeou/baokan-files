Title: Goa People's Party Calls on Patriots to Liberate Portuguese-Occupied Territory
Author:
Date: 1957-09-06
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, September 4th: The Goa People's Party has concluded a three-day regional representative conference in Bombay. The conference called on all Goan patriots to form a united front against the Portuguese colonialists and liberate the Indian territory they have occupied.
　　Participants in the conference passed a resolution calling on all domestic and international organizations to urge the Portuguese government to release the imprisoned Goan Indians.

### 果阿人民党号召爱国者解放葡萄牙侵占的领土

1957-09-06
第6版()
专栏：

    果阿人民党号召爱国者解放葡萄牙侵占的领土
    新华社讯　塔斯社德里4日讯：果阿人民党在孟买举行为时三天的地区代表会议已闭幕。会议号召果阿一切爱国者结成统一战线反对葡萄牙殖民主义者，解放被它侵占的印度领土。
    会议参加者通过决议，呼吁一切国内的和国际的组织促使葡萄牙政府释放被囚禁的果阿印度人。
