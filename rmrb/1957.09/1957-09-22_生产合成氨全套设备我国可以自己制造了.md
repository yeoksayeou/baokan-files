Title: China Can Now Manufacture Complete Sets of Equipment for Synthetic Ammonia Production
Author:
Date: 1957-09-22
Page: 5

China's machinery industry has now successfully mastered the technology for producing complete sets of equipment for synthetic ammonia production.

These equipments include: ammonia synthesis towers, high-pressure gas compressors with a capacity of 16,000 cubic meters per hour, carbon dioxide scrubbing equipment, complete sets of water gas generators, high-pressure circulating compressors, complete sets of air separation equipment, and hydrogen separation equipment. Mass production arrangements have been made for these equipments. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 生产合成氨全套设备我国可以自己制造了

1957-09-22
第5版()
专栏：

    生产合成氨全套设备
    我国可以自己制造了
    我国机械工业现在已经成功地掌握了生产制造合成氨的全套设备的技术。
    这些设备包括：氨合成筒，每小时达一万六千立方公尺的高压气体压缩机，二氧化碳洗涤设备，成套的水煤气发生炉，高压循环压缩机，全套空气分离设备及氢气分离设备等。这些设备已经作了大量生产的安排。　（据新华社讯）
