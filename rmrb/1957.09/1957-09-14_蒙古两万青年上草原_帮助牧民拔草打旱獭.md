Title: 20,000 Mongolian Youths Go to the Grasslands to Help Herdsmen Pull Weeds and Hunt Marmots
Author:
Date: 1957-09-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, September 13th: Erdun Chao Ke Tu, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League, said that 20,000 Mongolian youths are now on the grasslands helping herdsmen pull weeds, and thousands of youths are hunting marmots.

Erdun Chao Ke Tu told reporters that the Youth League organizations, on behalf of the Mongolian youth, have accepted 50% of this year's weeding task. They responded to the call of the Central Committee of the League, launched a socialist production competition, and will do a good job in completing this important task of protecting livestock through the winter.

### 蒙古两万青年上草原　帮助牧民拔草打旱獭

1957-09-14
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙古两万青年上草原
    帮助牧民拔草打旱獭
    据新华社乌兰巴托13日电  蒙古革命青年团中央委员会书记额尔敦朝克图说，现在有两万名蒙古青年在草原上帮助牧民拔草，数千名青年在猎取旱獭。
    额尔敦朝克图向记者说，青年团组织代表蒙古青年们接受了今年拔草任务的50％。他们响应团中央的号召，开展社会主义生产竞赛，要很好地完成这项保畜过冬的重要任务。
