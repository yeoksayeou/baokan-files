Title: Guo Moruo Receives Hungarian Medical Scientists
Author:
Date: 1957-09-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, received all members of the Hungarian medical scientist delegation headed by Academician Rusznyák, President of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences, on the afternoon of the 7th.

### 郭沫若接见匈医学家

1957-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见匈医学家
    新华社7日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若7日下午接见了以匈牙利科学院院长鲁斯尼雅克院士为首的匈牙利医学家代表团全体团员。
