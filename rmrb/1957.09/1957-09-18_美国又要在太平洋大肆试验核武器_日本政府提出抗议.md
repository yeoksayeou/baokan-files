Title: The United States is Again Conducting Large-Scale Nuclear Weapons Tests in the Pacific; The Japanese Government Protests
Author:
Date: 1957-09-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency dispatches, September 17th: Washington news: The U.S. Department of Defense and the Atomic Energy Commission issued a joint statement on the 15th, announcing that the United States will begin a new series of nuclear weapons tests at the Eniwetok Proving Ground in the Pacific in April next year.
	New York News: The United States conducted another nuclear weapons explosion test at the Nevada Test Site on the 16th. This is the twentieth nuclear weapons test conducted by the United States this year.
	Tokyo News: The Japanese government lodged a formal protest with the U.S. government on the 17th, resolutely opposing the United States' plan to conduct nuclear weapons tests again in the Pacific next spring.
	The protest stated that although the Japanese people are eagerly hoping for a rapid cessation of all nuclear tests, the U.S. government is currently still conducting nuclear tests in Nevada, which the Japanese government considers extremely regrettable.
	In the protest, the Japanese government also expressed regret over the U.S. plan to conduct nuclear tests on Eniwetok Island in the Pacific next spring. It stated that because this area is close to areas where Japanese merchant ships and fishing boats navigate and fish, the Japanese government cannot but express serious concern. The Japanese government demands that the United States stop this nuclear test and stop all nuclear tests.

### 美国又要在太平洋大肆试验核武器  日本政府提出抗议

1957-09-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　美国又要在太平洋大肆试验核武器
　　日本政府提出抗议
    本报综合报道　据新华社17日讯：华盛顿消息：美国国防部和原子能委员会15日发表联合声明，宣布美国将在明年4月在太平洋的恩纳威托克试验场开始新的一系列的核武器试验。
    纽约消息：美国16日在内华达州试验场又进行了一次核武器爆炸试验。这是今年以来美国所举行的第二十次核武器试验。
    东京消息：日本政府17日向美国政府提出正式抗议，坚决反对美国计划于明年春季在太平洋再次进行核武器试验。
    抗议书说，尽管日本国民迫切希望迅速停止一切核试验，美国政府目前仍然在内华达州进行核试验，这是日本政府认为极其遗憾的事情。
    日本政府在抗议书中对于美国明年春季在太平洋恩纳威托克岛进行核试验一事也表示遗憾。它说，由于这个地区接近日本商船和渔船等航行和捕鱼的区域，日本政府不能不表示严重的关心。日本政府要求美国停止这次核试验，并且停止一切核试验。
