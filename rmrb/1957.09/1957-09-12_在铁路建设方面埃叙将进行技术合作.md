Title: Egypt and Syria to Conduct Technical Cooperation in Railway Construction
Author: 
Date: 1957-09-12
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 10th - It was announced today that Egypt and Syria will conduct technical cooperation in railway construction. According to arrangements made under the recent economic union agreement signed by the two countries, Egypt will send engineers and technicians to work in Syria, and train Syrian technicians in Egypt. Egypt will supply railway vehicles and other equipment to Syria, and participate in the construction of new railways in Syria.

### 在铁路建设方面埃叙将进行技术合作

1957-09-12
第7版()
专栏：

    在铁路建设方面埃叙将进行技术合作
    新华社开罗10日电　今天宣布，埃及和叙利亚将在铁路建设方面进行技术合作。根据两国最近签订的经济联盟协定所作出的安排，埃及将派遣工程师和技术人员前往叙利亚工作，以及在埃及训练叙利亚技术人员。埃及将向叙利亚供应铁路车辆和其他设备，并且参加叙利亚建设新铁路的工程。
