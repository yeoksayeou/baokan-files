　　Friendly Exchanges
　　Vietnamese Architectural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
　　Xinhua News Agency, September 15th: The architectural delegation from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Bui Quang Tao, Deputy Minister of Water Conservancy and Construction, arrived in Beijing yesterday evening.
　　The delegation will undertake a three-month visit and inspection in our country.
　　George Hanna Arrives in Beijing
　　Xinhua News Agency, September 16th: George Hanna, member of the Lebanese Peace Committee and member of the World Peace Council, arrived in Beijing today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.
　　Two Mongolian Writers Return Home
　　Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, September 13th: The writers Tarva and Pulebdaoerji from the Mongolian People's Republic, who visited our country for more than a month under the Sino-Mongolian Cultural Agreement, left Hohhot yesterday to return home.
　　Our Film Delegation
　　Goes to Cairo to Participate in the Chinese Film Festival
　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 14th: The Chinese film delegation, invited by the Egyptian government to participate in the Chinese Film Festival, arrived in Cairo by plane this morning.
　　The head of the Chinese delegation is Situ Huimin, Deputy Director of the Film Bureau, and the members include Peng Hourong, Deputy Director of the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio, as well as film actors Bai Yang and Qin Yi.
　　The Chinese Film Festival is sponsored by the Egyptian National Guidance Department. The Chinese Film Festival will be held at the Cairo Cinema from September 16th to 27th.
　　The films to be screened include six feature films—"New Year Sacrifice," "Guerrillas on the Plain," "Ode to the Desert," "Hasan and Gamila," "The Fairy Couple," and "Reconnaissance Across the River"—as well as several short films and newsreels.

### 友好往来

1957-09-17
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

　　友好往来
    越南建筑代表团到京
    新华社15日讯　越南民主共和国建筑代表团在越南水利和建筑部副部长裴光造率领下，已经在昨天晚上到达北京。
    代表团将在我国作为期三个月的参观考察。
    乔治·哈纳到京
    新华社16日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请前来中国参观访问的黎巴嫩和平委员会委员、世界和平理事会理事乔治·哈纳，在今天到达北京。 两位蒙古作家返国
    据新华社呼和浩特13日电　根据中蒙文化协定来我国进行了一个多月访问的蒙古人民共和国作家塔尔瓦和普列布道尔吉，昨天离呼和浩特回国。
    我电影代表团
    赴开罗参加中国电影节
    新华社开罗14日电　应埃及政府邀请前来参加中国电影节的中国电影代表团今天上午乘飞机到达开罗。
    中国代表团团长是电影事业管理局副局长司徒慧敏，团员有中央新闻纪录电影制片厂副厂长彭厚嵘以及电影演员白杨和秦怡。
    中国电影节是由埃及国家指导部主办的。中国电影节将要从9月16日到27日在开罗电影院举行。
    上映的影片中有六部故事片——“祝福”、“平原游击队”、“沙漠的赞歌”、“哈森与加米拉”、“天仙配”和“渡江侦察记”——以及若干部短片和新闻纪录片。
