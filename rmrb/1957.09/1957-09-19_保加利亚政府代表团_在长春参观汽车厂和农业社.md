Title: Bulgarian Government Delegation Visits Automobile Factory and Agricultural Cooperative in Changchun
Author:
Date: 1957-09-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, September 18th: The Bulgarian government delegation, headed by Chairman Yugov, accompanied by Li Youwen, Governor of Jilin Province, today visited the Changchun First Automobile Manufacturing Plant and the Yinma River Agricultural Production Cooperative in Jiutai County.

### 保加利亚政府代表团  在长春参观汽车厂和农业社

1957-09-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　保加利亚政府代表团
　　在长春参观汽车厂和农业社
    据新华社长春18日电　以于哥夫主席为首的保加利亚政府代表团，今天由吉林省省长栗又文陪同参观了长春第一汽车制造厂和九台县饮马河农业生产合作社。
