Title: American Troops Flex Muscles at Middle Eastern Countries; Gang Up With Aggression Bloc Armies in Large-Scale Exercises; US and Turkey Intensify Plotting to Invade Syria
Author:
Date: 1957-09-26
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: Washington Dispatch: The United States Navy and Marine Corps densely massed at the entrance of the Dardanelles Strait on September 24th to participate in a North Atlantic Treaty Organization military exercise aimed at intimidating Middle Eastern countries. This is the largest gathering of the US Navy and Marine Corps in the Mediterranean since the war.

The centerpiece of this exercise is an amphibious landing on the Thracian Peninsula, 600 miles from the Syrian border, by an 8,000-strong US Marine Corps force.

United Press International, in reporting on this exercise, said: This largest concentration of American warships in the Mediterranean, "is an open expression of American concern for the oil-rich Middle East."

In addition to the US Army, Navy, and Air Force, the land, sea, and air forces of Britain, Turkey, Greece, and Italy are also participating in the exercise.

This exercise, named "Sea War," is commanded by the Southern Command of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization. The exercise area extends from northern Italy to Turkey, and the dates are from September 24th to September 28th.

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: Washington Dispatch: The United States continues to woo certain Middle Eastern countries in seeking to interfere in the Syrian political situation.

On the 24th, Secretary of Defense Wilson discussed Turkey's "defense situation" and how the United States can assist Turkey in opposing Syria with Turkish Finance Minister Polatkan.

Polatkan also handed Secretary of State Dulles a note from Turkish Prime Minister Menderes regarding the situation in Syria.

Sources say that in Turkey, American military delegations are consulting with Turkish military leaders on the Syrian situation.

According to an Ankara dispatch: Turkish Prime Minister Menderes stated in a statement to the Anatolian News Agency on September 24th that the situation in Syria is threatening the security of the Middle East, and that "Turkey is anxiously watching developments." Menderes's words echo Dulles's recent statement that the Syrian situation is causing unease among its neighbors.

Menderes also falsely accused the Soviet Union of creating the current "dangerous situation" in Syria. He said that Turkey is "in a dangerous encirclement." In this regard, he did not mention the activities of US Deputy Assistant Secretary of State Henderson in Turkey recently plotting aggression against Syria. (Attached picture)

### 美军向中东国家张牙舞爪  伙同侵略集团军队举行大演习  美土两国加紧密谋侵略叙利亚

1957-09-26
第7版()
专栏：

    美军向中东国家张牙舞爪
    伙同侵略集团军队举行大演习
    美土两国加紧密谋侵略叙利亚
    新华社25日讯　华盛顿消息：美国的海军和海军陆战队9月24日密集在达达尼尔海峡的进口处，参加目的在于威吓中东国家北大西洋集团军事演习。这是战后以来美国海军和海军陆战队在地中海上最大的一次集结。
    这次演习的中心是由八千人组成的美国海军陆战队在去距离叙利亚边界六百英里的色雷斯半岛进行两栖登陆。
    合众社在报道这次演习的消息时说：美国军舰这次在地中海的最大的一次集中，“是美国关心盛产石油的中东的公开表现”。
    参加演习的除了美国的陆海空军外，还有英国、土耳其、希腊、意大利的陆海空军。
    这次名为“海战”的演习是由北大西洋公约组织的南方司令部指挥的。演习的活动范围从意大利北部到土耳其，日期从9月24日到9月28日。
    据新华社25日讯　华盛顿消息：美国继续拉拢某些中东国家谋求干涉叙利亚政局。
    24日，国防部长威尔逊同土耳其财政部长波拉特干讨论了土耳其的“防务形势”以及美国怎样援助土耳其来反对叙利亚的问题。
    波拉特干还把土耳其总理曼德列斯的一份关于叙利亚局势的照会交给了国务卿杜勒斯。
    有消息说，在土耳其，美国的军事代表团正就叙利亚形势同土耳其军事领袖进行磋商。
    另据安卡拉消息：土耳其总理曼德列斯9月24日在对安那托里亚通讯社发表的声明中硬说，叙利亚的局势正在威胁着中东的安全，并且说，“土耳其正焦虑地注意着事态发展”。曼德列斯这些话和杜勒斯最近所谓叙利亚局势引起了它的邻国不安的说法如出一辙。
    曼德列斯还诬蔑说，叙利亚目前的“危险局势”是由苏联造成的。他说，土耳其“处于危险的包围中”。在这一方面，他没有提到美国副国务卿帮办汉德逊最近在土耳其布置对叙利亚的侵略阴谋的活动。（附图片）
