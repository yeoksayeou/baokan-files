Title: Soviet Expert Goltzki and Others Arrive in Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1957-09-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: Soviet experts on highland crops, Goltzki and Tupitsyn, arrived in Beijing by plane at noon on the 24th. They will depart for various locations in the Northwest around the 28th for an inspection tour lasting approximately two months.

### 苏专家高尔茨基等到京

1957-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

    苏专家高尔茨基等到京
    新华社25日讯　苏联来华考察高原作物的专家高尔茨基和图皮岑两人，24日中午乘飞机到北京。他们将在28日出发到西北各地考察，为期约两个多月。
