Title: Syrian Journalists Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd: A six-member Syrian journalists delegation, led by Tofik Yazgi, Director of the News Department of the Syrian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They are visiting China according to the 1957 executive plan of the Sino-Syrian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

That evening, Deng Tuo, Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, hosted a banquet to welcome them.

### 叙利亚新闻工作者代表团到达北京

1957-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    叙利亚新闻工作者代表团到达北京
    新华社22日讯　叙利亚新闻工作者代表团一行六人，在叙利亚外交部新闻司司长托菲克·雅兹率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。他们是根据中叙文化合作协定1957年执行计划来中国访问的。
    当天晚上，中华全国新闻工作者协会会长邓拓设宴欢迎。
