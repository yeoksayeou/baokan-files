Title: "August First" Basketball Team Achieves Five Wins in Five Games in Czechoslovakia

Author:

Date: 1957-09-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 10th: The Chinese People's Liberation Army "August First" basketball team defeated the Czechoslovak "National Youth" team by a score of 61 to 55 in Hradec Králové on September 9th, achieving a record of five wins in five games during its visit to Czechoslovakia.

Another dispatch: On September 7th, the "August First" team played its fourth match in Czechoslovakia in Přerov, Moravia, against the city's "Spartak" team. The "August First" team won with a score of 78 to 41.

### 我“八一”篮球队在捷五战五胜

1957-09-11
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    我“八一”篮球队
    在捷五战五胜
    据新华社布拉格10日电　中国人民解放军“八一”篮球队9日在赫拉岱茨克拉罗维市以六十一比五十五战胜了捷克斯洛伐克“国家青年”队，取得了在访问捷克斯洛伐克的比赛中五战五捷的成绩。
    又讯　9月7日，“八一”队在摩拉维亚州的普累罗夫城同这个城的“斯巴达克”队进行了到捷克斯洛伐克以来的第四场比赛，“八一”队以七十八比四十一获胜。
