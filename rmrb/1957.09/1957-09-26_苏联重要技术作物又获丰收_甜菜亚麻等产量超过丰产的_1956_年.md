Title: Soviet Union Reaps Another Bumper Harvest of Important Industrial Crops; Yields of Sugar Beets, Flax, etc., Exceed the Bumper Harvest of 1956
Author:
Date: 1957-09-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 25th. The harvest of crops in the Soviet Union this year is nearing completion. According to preliminary estimates based on harvest conditions in various regions, the Soviet Union has achieved another bumper harvest of important industrial crops such as sugar beets, flax, sunflowers, tobacco, as well as vegetables and potatoes. Their yields will exceed the bumper harvest of 1956.

The Soviet Union is the world's largest producer of sugar beets. This year, the sown area of this crop has been expanded to over two million hectares, and the sugar beets are growing well in various regions. The important oil crop, sunflowers, is also growing well, and the amount of sunflowers harvested in various regions is already several times higher than the same period last year. This year, the national potato planting area has increased by nearly 500,000 hectares compared to last year. Many collective farms and state farms in the central states of the Russian Federation, Belarus, the Baltic republics, and the northern regions of Ukraine have harvested 200 quintals or even more potatoes per hectare. The production of vegetables and melon crops has also increased compared to last year. By mid-September, the national purchase of tomatoes was already more than double that of the same period last year, and the amount of watermelons, cantaloupes, and zucchini harvested in Ukraine, Moldova, and Georgia was several times higher than last year.

### 苏联重要技术作物又获丰收  甜菜亚麻等产量超过丰产的1956年

1957-09-26
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联重要技术作物又获丰收
    甜菜亚麻等产量超过丰产的1956年
    据新华社莫斯科25日电　苏联今年的农作物收获工作即将结束。根据各地收获情况初步估计，今年苏联的重要技术作物甜菜、亚麻、向日葵、烟草以及蔬菜和马铃薯等又获得了丰收，它们的产量将超过丰产的1956年。
    苏联是世界上生产甜菜最多的国家。今年，这种作物的播种面积已经扩大到二百万公顷以上，各地的甜菜长得很好。重要的油料作物向日葵也长得很好，各地现在收获的向日葵已经比去年同时期多好几倍。今年全国马铃薯的播种面积比去年差不多增加了五十万公顷，俄罗斯联邦中部各州、白俄罗斯、波罗的海沿岸各加盟共和国和乌克兰北部地区的许多集体农庄和国营农场，从每公顷土地上收获了二百公担甚至更多的马铃薯。蔬菜和瓜类作物的产量也比去年增加了。到9月中旬，全国采购的西红柿已经比去年同期多一倍以上，乌克兰、摩尔达维亚、格鲁吉亚收获的西瓜、香瓜和西葫芦比去年多好几倍。
