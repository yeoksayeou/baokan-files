Title: Hungarian Folk Song and Dance Troupe Performs in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Hungarian Debrecen Folk Song and Dance Troupe gave its first performance in China tonight at the Beijing People's Theater. Each of their lively and cheerful programs tonight repeatedly won warm applause from the audience.

Vice Premier of the State Council Li Fuchun and Bo Yibo watched the performance tonight. During the intermission, accompanied by the Hungarian Ambassador to China, Nogrády Sándor, they met with the head of the Debrecen Folk Song and Dance Troupe, Maróti Gyula, and other leaders of the troupe. Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Minister Zhang Zhixiang, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei were also present at the meeting. After the performance, Vice Premiers Li Fuchun and Bo Yibo, among others, went on stage to shake hands with the performers and take pictures together.

The Debrecen Folk Song and Dance Troupe is one of Hungary's most famous folk song and dance troupes. Tonight, they performed thirteen musical, vocal, dance, and dance drama programs. The musical program performed by the seven-member folk band was particularly well-received by the audience. Five members of this band are brothers. The song and dance troupe will perform six more times in Beijing.

### 匈牙利民间歌舞团在京演出

1957-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利民间歌舞团在京演出
    新华社6日讯　匈牙利得布勒森民间歌舞团今晚在北京人民剧场举行来中国后的第一次演出。他们今晚演出的每一个轻快活泼的节目，都多次博得了观众的热烈掌声。
    国务院副总理李富春、薄一波观看了今晚的演出，并且在演出休息时，由匈牙利驻中国大使诺格拉第·山道尔陪同，接见了得布勒森民间歌舞团团长马罗皮·久洛和歌舞团的其他负责人。接见的时候，文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长张致祥和外交部副部长姬鹏飞等人也在座。在演出结束以后，李富春、薄一波副总理等人，还到台上同歌舞团的演员们一一握手和在一起照了像。
    得布勒森民间歌舞团是匈牙利最有名的民间歌舞团之一，他们今晚共演出了十三个音乐、歌唱、舞蹈和舞剧节目。其中由七人组成的民间乐队演出的音乐节目，特别受到观众欢迎。这个乐队中有五人是五兄弟。歌舞团还将在北京演出六次。
