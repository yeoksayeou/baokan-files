Title: Premier Zhou Receives Delegation from the Indian Asian Solidarity Committee
Author:
Date: 1957-09-11
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Premier Zhou Enlai received the delegation from the Indian Asian Solidarity Committee, headed by Mrs. R. Nehru, this afternoon. Guo Moruo, Li Dequan, Cai Tingkai, Liu Ningyi, Liao Chengzhi, Zhao Puchu, and Zhu Ziqi were also present at the meeting.

### 周总理接见印度亚洲团结委员会代表团

1957-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见印度亚洲团结委员会代表团
    新华社10日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见以拉·尼赫鲁夫人为首的印度亚洲团结委员会代表团。接见时，郭沫若、李德全、蔡廷锴、刘宁一、廖承志、赵朴初、朱子奇也在座。
