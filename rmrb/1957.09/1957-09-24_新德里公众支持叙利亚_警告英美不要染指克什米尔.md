Title: New Delhi Public Supports Syria, Warns Britain and America Not to Interfere in Kashmir
Author:
Date: 1957-09-24
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, September 23rd. Two public rallies were held in New Delhi on the evening of September 22nd, sharply criticizing the aggressive and bellicose intentions of American imperialism.

    The "No Interference in Syria" rally initiated by the Delhi Committee of the Communist Party of India unanimously passed a resolution. The resolution stated that the current actions of American imperialists in the Middle East "are a challenge to the freedom and unity of Asian and African countries, to the policy of peaceful coexistence, and to Panchsheel." The meeting pledged full support for the Syrian people's struggle to defend their independence and considered the Soviet Union's warning to Turkey an "action to defend peace."
    At the "Kashmir Day" commemoration, Siddiq, a prominent figure in Delhi and a Muslim leader, said that the United States is truly interested in gaining a foothold in this strategic region. The meeting passed a resolution warning British and American imperialists not to take any action aimed at aggression against Kashmir.

### 新德里公众支持叙利亚  警告英美不要染指克什米尔

1957-09-24
第7版()
专栏：

    新德里公众支持叙利亚
    警告英美不要染指克什米尔
    新华社新德里23日电　9月22日晚上新德里举行了两个公众集会，尖锐指责美国帝国主义侵略和好战的意图。
    印度共产党德里委员会发起的“不许干涉叙利亚”的集会一致通过了一项决议。决议说，美帝国主义者目前在中东的行动“是在对亚非国家的自由和团结、对共处政策和潘查希拉进行挑战”。会议保证全力支持叙利亚人民保卫自己独立的斗争，并且认为苏联对土耳其的警告是“保卫和平的行动”。
    在“克什米尔日”纪念会上，德里著名人士、穆斯林领袖西迪克说，美国真正感兴趣的是在这个战略地区得到立足点。会议通过决议警告英美帝国主义者不要采取任何旨在侵略克什米尔的行动。
