Title: Hungarian, Indonesian, Burmese, and Cambodian Guests Watched Chinese Classical Drama Last Night
Author:
Date: 1957-09-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 29th - Hungarian, Indonesian, Burmese, and Cambodian guests gathered at the CPPCC Auditorium in Beijing tonight to enjoy Chinese classical dramas - Kunqu Opera and Peking Opera.

They were: the Hungarian People's Republic Government Delegation headed by Kádár, Dr. Hatta and his wife and entourage, the Burmese Friendship Delegation headed by U Min Thein, and Cambodian Princess Phoumi Devy, Prince Norodom Chakrapong, and Fang Bingzhen, head of the Cambodian Cultural and Artistic Delegation.

At 8 p.m., when the distinguished guests, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers He Long and Chen Yi, President Dong Biwu, CPPCC Vice Chairman Burhan, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, entered the hall, the audience stood up and applauded warmly.

This evening party was held by the Ministry of Culture to welcome the distinguished guests. At the party, famous Chinese Kunqu Opera and Peking Opera actors performed "Zhao Jun's Departure from the Frontier," "Fisherman's Revenge," and "Farewell My Concubine."

After the performance, the distinguished guests from the four countries presented flower baskets to the actors. The distinguished guests also went on stage to meet with the actors. (Attached picture)

### 匈、印度尼西亚、缅、柬四国贵宾  昨晚观看我国古典戏剧

1957-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    匈、印度尼西亚、缅、柬四国贵宾
    昨晚观看我国古典戏剧
    新华社29日讯　匈牙利、印度尼西亚、缅甸和柬埔寨四国贵宾，今晚欢聚在北京政协礼堂欣赏了我国的古典戏剧——昆剧和京剧。
    他们是：以卡达尔为首的匈牙利人民共和国政府代表团，哈达博士和夫人及随行人员，以吴敏登为首的缅甸友好代表团，以及柬埔寨拍花·黛维公主、夏卡朋王子和柬埔寨文化艺术代表团团长方秉祯。
    晚上八时，当贵宾们由周恩来总理，贺龙、陈毅副总理，董必武院长，政协副主席包尔汉，文化部部长沈雁冰和外交部副部长张闻天等陪同步入会场的时候，全场起立热烈鼓掌欢迎。
    这个晚会是文化部为欢迎贵宾们而举行的。在晚会上，中国著名的昆剧演员和京剧演员分别表演了“昭君出塞”、“打渔杀家”和“霸王别姬”。
    演出结束以后，四个国家的贵宾分别向演员们赠送了花篮。贵宾们并登台同演员们会见。（附图片）
