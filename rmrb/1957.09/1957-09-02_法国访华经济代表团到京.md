Title: French Economic Delegation to China Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-02
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 1st. The French Economic Delegation to China, consisting of sixteen members, led by Senator Henri Rochereau, Chairman of the Senate Committee on Economic Affairs, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade for a visit to our country. Another two members of the delegation will arrive in Beijing on September 4.
    Greeting them at the airport were Ji Chao-ding, Vice-Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and responsible persons from various import and export corporations.

### 法国访华经济代表团到京

1957-09-02
第3版()
专栏：

    法国访华经济代表团到京
    新华社1日讯  应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请前来我国访问的法国访华经济代表团一行十六人，在参议员、参议院经济事务委员会主席亨利·罗谢罗率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团另有团员二人将在9月4日来京。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎和各进出口专业公司的负责人。
