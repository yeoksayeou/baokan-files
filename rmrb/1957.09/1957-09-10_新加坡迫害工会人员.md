Title: Singapore Persecutes Trade Unionists
Author:
Date: 1957-09-10
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Singapore, 8th: It is announced that the Singaporean authorities have decided to invoke the "Public Security Ordinance" to detain thirty-seven trade union workers arrested in late August for two years.

Many of those detained are members of the Singapore People's Action Party, including those recently elected to the Central Executive Committee. The Singaporean authorities have indicated in a white paper that this crackdown on trade union workers is directed against the People's Action Party.

### 新加坡迫害工会人员

1957-09-10
第7版()
专栏：

    新加坡迫害工会人员
    据新华社8日讯　新加坡消息：据宣布，新加坡当局已经决定援引“公共安全法令”把8月下旬逮捕的工会工作者三十七人拘留两年。
    被拘留的人中许多是新加坡人民行动党的党员，包括最近当选为中央执行委员的人在内。新加坡当局曾经在一项白皮书中表明，这次镇压工会工作者的行动是针对着人民行动党。
