Title: To Greet the Opening of the Guangzhou-Beijing Railway: Six Comfortable Passenger Trains Stand Ready
Author:
Date: 1957-09-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 27th: As the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge is completed, Guangzhou railway workers are working intensely to prepare for the opening of the Guangzhou-Beijing line.

　　The six passenger trains newly allocated to Guangzhou by the Ministry of Railways are now parked at Guangzhou Station; each train has a green, new-style soft-berth carriage with very comfortable seats. It will be the first to run directly from Guangzhou to Beijing.

　　Currently, the tickets for the Guangzhou-Beijing line train are ready. More than 230 train service personnel are also being assigned.

### 迎接广州—北京通车  六列舒适的客车整装待发

1957-09-28
第5版()
专栏：

    迎接广州—北京通车
    六列舒适的客车整装待发
    新华社广州27日电　随着武汉长江大桥的完工，广州铁路职工迎接广州—北京线通车的准备工作正在紧张进行。
    铁道部新拨给广州的六列客车现停在广州车站；每列车都有一辆绿色的、座位非常舒适的新式软席卧车。它将首先从广州直接开往北京。
    现在，广州—北京线火车的票据已经准备好。二百三十多名列车服务人员也正在调配中。
