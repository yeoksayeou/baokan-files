Title: First Song Upon Arriving in the Countryside
Author: Hou Dian
Date: 1957-09-30
Page: 8

First Song Upon Arriving in the Countryside
　　Hou Dian
　　The moment we stepped out of school, we smelled the fragrance of life,
　　We soared like fledglings leaving the nest.
　　In the clear autumn sky,
　　Echoed everywhere is a harmonious chorus—
　　"Who wants to be a flower in a greenhouse?
　　Who fears the wind and sun, rain, snow, and frost?
　　We will turn barren land into fertile soil,
　　And repay our Party with labor.

　　"The new life is vast and boundless,
　　Storms are just right for strengthening our wings,
　　Why choose the most arduous path?
　　Because we are the newborn strength of the motherland!"

　　Goodbye! Prosperous cities, colorful campuses,
　　Ships carry us to the land of fish and rice,
　　Along the way, soldiers are assigned to the countryside,
　　The train puffs light smoke, sending us to the frontier.

　　The best and smartest young men,
　　Stride swiftly, carrying knapsacks,
　　The destination is the resource-rich mountainous area,
　　The first leg is to cross that towering mountain ridge.

　　We settle down here,
　　As if returning to our own hometown,
　　The local customs are not unfamiliar at all,
　　Smiling faces of rice and wheat dispel our last trace of melancholy.

### 下乡初唱
侯甸
1957-09-30
第8版()
专栏：

    下乡初唱
    侯甸
    一出校门就闻到生活的芳香，
    我们像离窠的雏鹰振翼高翔。
    在初秋的晴空里，
    处处回荡着和谐的合唱——
    “谁愿做温室里的花朵？
    谁怕风吹日晒，雨雪冰霜？
    我们要把贫瘠变膏腴，
    用劳动去报答我们的党。
    “新的生活海阔天空，
    风暴正好锻炼我们的翅膀，
    为什么挑选最艰辛的道路？
    因为我们是祖国新生的力量！”
    再见！繁荣的都市，缤纷的校园，
    轮船载我们来到鱼米之乡，
    沿途向农村分派入伍的战士，
    列车吐着轻烟把我们送上边疆。
    最棒的也最聪明的小伙子，
    健步如飞，背着行囊，
    目的地是蕴藏富饶的山区，
    第一程先翻过那耸入云霄的山梁。
    我们落脚到这里，
    如同回到自己的家乡，
    风土人情一点也不陌生，
    笑语迎人的稻麦消去了我们最后一丝惆怅。
