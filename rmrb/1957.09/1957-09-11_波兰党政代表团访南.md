Title: Polish Party and Government Delegation Visits Yugoslavia
Author: 
Date: 1957-09-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, September 10th: The Polish government and party delegation, headed by Gomulka and Cyrankiewicz, arrived in Belgrade this morning.

The delegation was welcomed by Yugoslav party and government leaders headed by Tito.

### 波兰党政代表团访南

1957-09-11
第6版()
专栏：

　　波兰党政代表团访南
    新华社贝尔格莱德10日电　以哥穆尔卡和西伦凯维兹为首的波兰政府和党的代表团，今天早晨到达贝尔格莱德。
    代表团受到了以铁托为首的南斯拉夫党政领导人的欢迎。
