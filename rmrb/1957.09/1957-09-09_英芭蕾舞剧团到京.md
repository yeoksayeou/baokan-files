Title: English Ballet Company Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 8th: The English Ballet Rambert, led by Director Marie Rambert, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Ballet Rambert will perform in China for two months, presenting a total of six ballets: Coppélia, Giselle, Winter Night, Gala Performance, Les Sylphides, and Pas des Déesses. (Image Attached)
The English Ballet Rambert photographed with well-wishers at the airport upon their arrival in Beijing on September 8th.
Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Mei

### 英芭蕾舞剧团到京

1957-09-09
第3版()
专栏：

    英芭蕾舞剧团到京
    据新华社8日讯　英国兰伯特芭蕾舞剧团在团长玛丽·兰伯特率领下，今日乘飞机到达北京。
    兰伯特芭蕾舞剧团应中国人民对外文化协会邀请，将在中国作两个月的访问演出，一共演出六个芭蕾舞剧：葛蓓利娅、吉赛尔、冬夜、节日的表演、仙女们和仙女的步伐。（附图片）
    英国兰伯特芭蕾舞剧团9月8日到达北京时和欢迎者在机场上合影。
                            新华社记者  章梅摄
