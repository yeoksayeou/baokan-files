　　Dinosaur Fossil
    The Fourth Petroleum Exploration Team of Sichuan Province discovered a "dinosaur" fossil in Fuxing Township, Taihe District, Hechuan County. The Sichuan Provincial Museum and the Chongqing Municipal Museum successively sent cadres to organize it. They believe that this is a relatively large "dinosaur" from the Paleozoic Era about 50 to 60 million years ago, which was deposited after its death. The dinosaur fossil is 18.8 meters long from head to tail. Except for the middle part which has been damaged, the neck, head, and tail are intact, and the vertebrae can be clearly distinguished. (Zhu Dakang)

### 恐龙化石
朱大康
1957-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　恐龙化石
    四川省石油勘探第四队在合川县太和区复兴乡发现“恐龙”化石，四川省博物馆和重庆市博物馆先后派干部前往整理。他们认为这是距今约五、六千万年前古生代的一种较大的“恐龙”，死后被沉积下来。恐龙化石首尾全长十八点八公尺，除中间部分遭受破坏外，颈头、尾部完好，脊椎骨能清楚分辨。（朱大康）
