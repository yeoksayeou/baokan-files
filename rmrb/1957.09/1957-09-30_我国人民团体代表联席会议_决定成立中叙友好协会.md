Title: China's People's Organizations Joint Conference Decides to Establish the China-Syria Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1957-09-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 29th. A joint conference of representatives from sixteen Chinese people's organizations was held in Beijing this afternoon. The conference decided to establish the China-Syria Friendship Association and unanimously approved Da Puchen, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association, as the President of the China-Syria Friendship Association, and Yang Jingren, Zhang Tiesheng, and Zhang Jie as Vice Presidents.
    Today's meeting was presided over by Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. The meeting adopted the charter of the China-Syria Friendship Association, which defined the association's purpose as developing the brotherly friendship between China and Syria, promoting cultural exchanges between the two countries, and enhancing mutual understanding between the two peoples. The association's work mainly involves promoting cultural exchanges between China and Syria and supporting the just struggle of the Syrian people to defend their independence and oppose imperialist aggression.
    The list of the President, Vice Presidents, and Council Members of the China-Syria Friendship Association unanimously approved by the joint conference is as follows:
    President: Da Puchen.
    Vice Presidents: Yang Jingren, Zhang Tiesheng, Zhang Jie.
    Forty Council Members: Ding Yimin, Yu Zhenying, Feng Zikai, Wang Shouren, Wang Yinpu, Bao Erhan, Zuohala, Jiang Ming, Liu Jinzhong, Liu Linrui, Yan Jici, Da Puchen, Du Bo, Li Shu, Shang Lizhai, Wu Xiaobang, Abudureheman, Hu Ziang, Hu Lansheng, Hu Chuankui, Fan Jin, Zha Fuxi, Gong Dafei, Ma Mingji, Ma Tengai, Gao Ge, Zhang Jie, Zhang Zhengwu, Zhang Tiesheng, Wen Jize, Min Ganghou, Cheng Ximeng, Zhong Shitong, Yang Jingren, Yang Shuo, Yang Yunyu, Yang Huilin, Dong Xin, Jiang Tianzuo, Ge Yihong.
    The people's organizations that initiated the establishment of the China-Syria Friendship Association are: the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the China Islamic Association ,the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace, the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, the All-China Women's Federation, the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Red Cross Society of China, the Chinese Medical Association, the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, the China Political Science and Law Society, the China Journalists Association, the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, and the China Hui Nationality Culture Advancement Association.
    This afternoon, the China-Syria Friendship Association held its first council meeting and decided to hold a grand inaugural meeting of the China-Syria Friendship Association in Beijing on the 30th.

### 我国人民团体代表联席会议  决定成立中叙友好协会

1957-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我国人民团体代表联席会议
    决定成立中叙友好协会
    新华社29日讯　我国十六个人民团体的代表今天下午在北京举行联席会议，会议决定成立中国叙利亚友好协会，并一致通过中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生为中叙友好协会会长，杨静仁、张铁生和张杰为副会长。
    今天的会议由中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南主持。会议通过了中叙友好协会的章程，确定协会的宗旨是发展中叙两国兄弟般的友好关系，促进中叙两国文化交流，增进两国人民的相互了解。协会的工作主要是，促进中叙两国的文化来往，支持叙利亚人民保卫独立和反对帝国主义侵略的正义斗争。
    联席会议一致通过的中叙友好协会会长、副会长、理事会理事的名单如下：
    会长：达浦生。
    副会长：杨静仁、张铁生、张杰。
    理事四十人：丁毅民、于振瀛、丰子恺、王守仁、王荫圃、包尔汉、左哈拉、江明、刘进中、刘麟瑞、严济慈、达浦生、杜波、李恕、尚立斋、吴晓邦、阿不都热合满、胡子昂、胡兰生、胡传揆、范瑾、查阜西、宫达非、马明基、马腾霭、高戈、张杰、张正武、张铁生、温济泽、闵刚侯、程希孟、钟师统、杨静仁、杨朔、杨蕴玉、杨慧琳、董昕、蒋天佐、葛一虹。
    发起成立中叙友好协会的各人民团体有中国人民对外文化协会、中国伊斯兰教协会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国人民外交学会、中国文学艺术界联合会、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、中华人民共和国妇女联合会、中华全国民主青年联合会、中华全国总工会、中国红十字会总会、中华医学会、中国国际贸易促进委员会、中国政治法律学会、中国新闻工作者协会、中华全国工商业联合会和中国回民文化协进会。
    今天下午中叙友好协会召开第一次理事会，决定30日在北京隆重举行中叙友好协会成立大会。
