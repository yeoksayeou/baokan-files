Friendly Exchanges

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 3rd: The military delegation of the German Democratic Republic, led by Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Defense General Stoph, left Berlin by plane today for a visit to China.

The delegation is visiting at the invitation of Chinese Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Defense Marshal Peng Dehuai.

Chinese Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic Wang Guoquan saw the delegation off at the airport.

Australian Building Workers' Union Delegation to Anshan

Xinhua News Agency, September 2nd: The Australian Building Workers' Union delegation of four people, headed by Hancock, left Beijing by train this afternoon for Anshan; accompanying them was Zhang Jin, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Building Workers' Union.

Famous Danish Writer Arrives in Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, September 3rd: Johanna Andersen Nexø, wife of the famous Danish writer Martin Andersen Nexø, who was invited to China for a visit by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

Hungarian Debrecen Folk Dance Troupe Arrives in China for a Performance Tour

Xinhua News Agency, September 2nd: The Hungarian Debrecen Folk Dance Troupe, a group of thirty people, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

The dance troupe is led by the head of the troupe, Maróti Gyula. They are visiting and performing in China in accordance with the provisions of the 1957 Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement plan.

Two other members of the dance troupe will arrive in Beijing in the coming days.

### 友好往来

1957-09-04
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    副总理斯多夫上将率领
    民主德国军事代表团启程来华
    新华社柏林3日电　德意志民主共和国副总理兼国防部长斯多夫上将所率领的军事代表团今天乘飞机离开柏林前往中国访问。
    代表团是应中国副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅的邀请进行这次访问的。
    中国驻民主德国大使王国权到机场给代表团送行。
    澳大利亚建筑业工会代表团去鞍山
    据新华社2日讯　以汉柯克为首的澳大利亚建筑业工会代表团一行四人今天下午乘火车离北京前往鞍山；陪同前往的是中国建筑工会全国委员会副主席张进。
    丹麦著名作家到京
    新华社3日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的丹麦著名作家马丁·安德森·尼克索的夫人约翰娜·安德生·尼克索，在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    匈牙利得布勒森民间舞蹈团来我国访问演出
    新华社2日讯　匈牙利得布勒森民间舞蹈团一行三十人，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    舞蹈团由团长马罗皮·久洛率领。他们是执行1957年中匈文化合作协定计划的规定，来中国访问演出的。
    舞蹈团其他两人将在日内到北京。
