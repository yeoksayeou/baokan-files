Title: Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Mission to the Soviet Union Leaves Beijing for the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-09-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th. The Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Mission to the Soviet Union, headed by Tetsu Katayama, left Beijing by plane this morning for a visit to the Soviet Union.
    Qiao Guanhua, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, and Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, were at the airport to see them off.

### 日社会党访苏亲善使节团离京去苏联

1957-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

    日社会党访苏亲善使节团离京去苏联
    新华社20日讯　以片山哲为首的日本社会党访苏亲善使节团今天上午乘飞机离开北京去苏联访问。
    前往机场送行的有中国人民外交学会副会长乔冠华、秘书长吴茂荪，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志。
