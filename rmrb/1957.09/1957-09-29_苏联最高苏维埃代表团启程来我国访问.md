Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation Departs for Visit to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1957-09-29
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th. The delegation of the Soviet Supreme Soviet, invited by the National People's Congress of China to visit China, left Moscow for China at 10:00 AM today aboard a "Tu-104" jet airliner. The delegation is led by Aristov, member of the Legislative Proposals Committee of the Soviet Supreme Soviet Union Council and member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.
    The delegation consists of twenty-five people, including politicians, social activists, scientists, writers, engineers, and workers.
    Among those who saw the delegation off at the airport were representatives of the Soviet Supreme Soviet, ministers of the Soviet government, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union. (Image attached)
    Comrade Aristov

### 苏联最高苏维埃代表团启程来我国访问

1957-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃代表团启程来我国访问
    新华社莫斯科28日电　应中国全国人民代表大会的邀请前往中国访问的苏联最高苏维埃代表团，今天上午十时乘“图——一○四”喷气客机离莫斯科前往中国。代表团由苏联最高苏维埃联盟院法案委员会委员、苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员阿里斯托夫率领。
    代表团由二十五人组成。其中有政治家、社会活动家、科学家、作家、工程师和工人等。
    在机场欢送代表团的有苏联最高苏维埃代表，苏联政府的部长及各国驻苏联外交使节等。（附图片）
    阿里斯托夫同志
