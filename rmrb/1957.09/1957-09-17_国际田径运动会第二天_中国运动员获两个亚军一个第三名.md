Title: International Track and Field Games Day Two: Chinese Athletes Win Two Silvers and One Bronze
Author:
Date: 1957-09-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 16th. On the second day (15th) of the Romanian International Track and Field Championships, Chinese athletes Jiang Yumin and Liu Yuying won the silver medals in the women's 200 meters and women's 80-meter hurdles respectively, and Liu Zheng won the bronze medal in the women's 80-meter hurdles. Their results all broke the Chinese national records and met the standards for Master of Sport.

　　Chinese first-class athlete Jiang Yumin won the silver medal in the women's 200 meters with an excellent time of 24.8 seconds. The champion in the 200 meters was the Dutch athlete Blomhoff. Her time was 24.4 seconds.

　　In the women's 80-meter hurdles preliminary round, Chinese athletes Liu Yuying and Liu Zheng both clocked 11.2 seconds, winning the silver and bronze medals respectively. Their new achievements have both broken the national record of 11.3 seconds for the women's 80-meter hurdles, and are even better than the fifth-place time at the Olympics.

　　The champion in the women's 80-meter hurdles was Romania's Anna Sherban, whose time was 11.1 seconds.

### 国际田径运动会第二天  中国运动员获两个亚军一个第三名

1957-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际田径运动会第二天
　　中国运动员获两个亚军一个第三名
    据新华社布加勒斯特16日电　在罗马尼亚国际田径锦标赛的第二天（15日），中国运动员姜玉民、刘玉英分别获得女子二百公尺和女子八十公尺低栏亚军，刘正获女子八十公尺低栏第三名。她们的成绩都突破了中国全国最高成绩和运动健将标准。
    中国一级运动员姜玉民以二十四秒八的优异成绩获得女子二百公尺亚军。二百公尺的冠军是荷兰的运动员勃洛姆霍夫。她的成绩是二十四秒四。
    在女子八十公尺低栏预赛中，中国选手刘玉英和刘正同以十一秒二的成绩分别获得了亚军和第三名。她们的新成就，都打破了十一秒三的女子八十公尺低栏全国最高成绩，比奥林匹克第五名的成绩还好。
    女子八十公尺低栏冠军是罗马尼亚的安娜·谢尔班，她的成绩是十一秒一。
