Title: Xiangxi Tujia and Miao Autonomous Prefecture Officially Established
Author:
Date: 1957-09-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jishou, September 20th: The first session of the First People's Congress of the Xiangxi Tujia and Miao Autonomous Prefecture concluded on the 20th, officially proclaiming the establishment of the Xiangxi Tujia and Miao Autonomous Prefecture.

    The meeting lasted for six days. Delegates heard a report by Zhang Mengxu, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Xiangxi Tujia and Miao Autonomous Prefecture, on the preparatory work of the prefecture; a report by Shi Bangzhi, the former Governor of the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture and Secretary of the CPC Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture Committee, on the achievements of the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture over the past five years and the future work tasks of the Xiangxi Tujia and Miao Autonomous Prefecture; and a report by Zhao Dexin, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture Committee, on thoroughly carrying out the socialist education movement. The delegates earnestly discussed the aforementioned reports in groups, with fifty-six delegates speaking at the meeting and fourteen delegates submitting written statements, unanimously endorsing and approving these reports.

  In accordance with the State Council's decision to abolish the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture and establish the Xiangxi Tujia and Miao Autonomous Prefecture, the meeting today elected the members of the Autonomous Prefecture People's Committee and the President of the Autonomous Prefecture Intermediate People's Court by secret ballot. Shi Bangzhi (Miao nationality) was elected as Governor, Peng Zugui (Tujia nationality), Zhao Dexin (Han nationality), Chen Yanbin (Han nationality), Long Zaiyu (Miao nationality), Tian Jingui (Tujia nationality), Shi Yuanji (Miao nationality), and Huang Muru (Tujia nationality) were elected as Deputy Governors. Tian Kaisheng (Tujia nationality), Shi Baying (female, Miao nationality), Qi Shouliang (Han nationality), and thirty-four others were elected as members of the Prefecture People's Committee. Dong Qianxiang was elected as President of the Xiangxi Tujia and Miao Autonomous Prefecture Intermediate People's Court. Governor Shi Bangzhi, on behalf of the elected officials, said at the meeting: We thank the people of all ethnic groups in the autonomous prefecture for their trust in us. We will strive to unite closely with everyone under the leadership of the Communist Party and diligently serve the people of all ethnic groups in the autonomous prefecture.

    Finally, the meeting adopted congratulatory telegrams to the CPC Central Committee and Chairman Mao, as well as congratulatory telegrams to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and other telegrams and resolutions.

### 湘西土家族苗族自治州正式成立

1957-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    湘西土家族苗族自治州正式成立
    新华社吉首20日电　湘西土家族苗族自治州首届人民代表大会第一次会议已于20日闭幕，湘西土家族苗族自治州正式宣告成立。
    这次会议共召开了六天，听取了湘西土家族苗族自治州筹备委员会主任张孟旭关于湘西土家族苗族自治州筹备工作的报告，原湘西苗族自治州州长、中共湘西苗族自治州地委书记石邦智关于湘西苗族自治州五年来的工作成就及湘西土家族苗族自治州今后工作任务的报告和中共湘西苗族自治州地委副书记赵德新关于深入开展社会主义教育运动的报告。代表们对上述报告分小组认真进行了讨论，有五十六个代表在大会上发言，十四个代表发表书面发言，一致拥护和通过这些报告。
  会议根据国务院关于撤销湘西苗族自治州建立湘西土家族苗族自治州的决定，采用无记名投票方式在今天选举了自治州人民委员会组成人员和自治州中级人民法院院长。选出石邦智（苗族）为州长，彭祖贵（土家族）、赵德新（汉族）陈彦滨（汉族）、龙再宇（苗族）、田荆贵（土家族）、石元机（苗族）、黄穆如（土家族）为副州长，田开胜（土家族）、石拔英（女、苗族）、齐寿良（汉族）等三十七人当选为州人民委员会委员，董千祥当选为湘西土家族苗族自治州中级人民法院院长。州长石邦智代表当选人员在会上说：我们感谢自治州各族人民对我们的信任，我们将努力在共产党的领导下，紧密地和大家团结在一起，勤勤恳恳地为自治州各族人民服务。
    最后，会议通过了向中共中央和毛主席的致敬电，以及向全国人民代表大会常委会的致敬电等电文和各项决议。
