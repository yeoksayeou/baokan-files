Title: Sichuan Cotton Harvest (Picture)
Author: Li Xuan
Date: 1957-09-08
Page: 5

Sichuan Cotton Harvest

This year, agricultural cooperatives in various parts of Sichuan Province have once again achieved a bumper cotton harvest. The 2,630 mu of cotton fields in the Jiefang Agricultural Cooperative of Shehong Township, Jianyang County alone are expected to yield over 170,000 jin, an average increase of 15 to 20 jin per mu compared to the bumper harvest year of 1955. The picture shows members of the fourth cultivation zone of the Jiefang Agricultural Cooperative in Jianyang County, Sichuan picking cotton.

Li Xuan, photo (Sichuan Daily Press Release)

### 四川棉花丰收（图片）
李煊
1957-09-08
第5版()
专栏：

    四川棉花丰收
    今年，四川省各地农业社的棉花又获得了丰收，仅简阳县射洪乡解放农业社的二千六百三十亩棉田，就可收获十七万斤以上，平均每亩比1955年大丰收年还多收十五到二十斤。图为四川简阳县解放农业社第四耕作区的社员们在摘棉花。
            李煊摄（四川日报社稿）
