Title: Italian Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 28th: The Italian Friendship Delegation, consisting of nine members, arrived in Beijing yesterday and today.
    The delegation is composed of Italian senators, prominent lawyers, writers, and journalists.
    The delegation members were warmly welcomed at the airport by Zhao Yimin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, as well as representatives from the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, the All-China Journalists Association, and the Chinese Writers Association.

### 意友好访华代表团到京

1957-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    意友好访华代表团到京
    新华社28日讯　意大利友好访华代表团一行九人在昨天和今天先后到达北京。
    代表团由意大利的上议员、著名律师、作家、记者等组成。
    代表团人员在飞机场上受到中国人民对外文化协会副会长赵毅敏以及中国人民外交学会、中国新闻工作者联谊会、中国作家协会的代表们的热烈欢迎。
