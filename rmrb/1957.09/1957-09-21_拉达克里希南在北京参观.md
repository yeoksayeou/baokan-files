Title: Radhakrishnan Visits Beijing

Author:
Date: 1957-09-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th -- Dr. Radhakrishnan, Vice President of the Republic of India, visited the famous Peking University this morning in Beijing, toured the Wuta Temple and the Summer Palace, and had lunch with Vice Premier Chen Yi and others at the Summer Palace at noon. At 9:30 this morning, Dr. Radhakrishnan, accompanied by Vice Chairman and President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Guo Moruo, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and Indian Ambassador to China R.K. Nehru, first drove to visit the Wuta Temple in the western suburbs of Beijing. This is an ancient temple built in imitation of the Buddha Gaya Vihara in India.

The teachers and students of Peking University lined up at the school gate to welcome the distinguished guests. Dr. Radhakrishnan visited the Philosophy Department and the Oriental Languages ​​Department teaching building of Peking University with great interest. In the library of the Department of Oriental Languages, he saw that there were many of his works collected there. Today, he decided to donate some of his works to the Peking University Library.

### 拉达克里希南在北京参观

1957-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

    拉达克里希南在北京参观
    据新华社20日讯　印度共和国副总统拉达克里希南博士今天上午在北京参观了著名的北京大学，游览了五塔寺和颐和园，中午同陈毅副总理等在颐和园共进午餐。上午九时半，拉达克里希南博士由副委员长、中国科学院院长郭沫若、外交部副部长章汉夫和印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁等陪同，首先驱车游览了北京西郊的五塔寺。这是模仿印度佛陀伽耶精舍建成的一座古寺。
    北京大学的师生们列队在校门口欢迎贵宾。拉达克里希南博士很有兴致地参观了北京大学的哲学系和东方语系教学大楼。在东方语系的图书室里，他看到那里收藏着很多他的著作。今天，他又决定把他的一些著作赠给北京大学图书馆。
