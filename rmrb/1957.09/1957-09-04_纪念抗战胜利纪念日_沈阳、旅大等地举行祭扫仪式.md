Title: Commemorating the Anniversary of Victory in the War of Resistance Against Japan, Shenyang, Lüda and Other Places Hold Memorial Ceremonies
Author:
Date: 1957-09-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, September 3rd: Today morning, representatives from all sectors of Liaoning Province and Shenyang City successively paid respects at the Cemetery of Soviet Martyrs and the Monument to Soviet Martyrs, commemorating the "September 3rd" Anniversary of the Victory Against Japanese Aggression and the 12th Anniversary of the Liberation of Northeast China by the Soviet Army.
    A memorial ceremony was held in front of the Cemetery of Soviet Martyrs. Zhang Like, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang City, spoke at the memorial ceremony.
    In addition, this morning, leading members of the Party and government of Lüda City, along with more than 500 representatives from all sectors of society, laid wreaths at the Tower of Soviet Martyrs in Stalin Square in the city, commemorating the Soviet martyrs who heroically sacrificed their lives twelve years ago to liberate Northeast China and defeat the Japanese invaders.

### 纪念抗战胜利纪念日  沈阳、旅大等地举行祭扫仪式

1957-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念抗战胜利纪念日
    沈阳、旅大等地举行祭扫仪式
    据新华社沈阳3日电　今天上午，辽宁省、沈阳市各界代表先后祭扫了苏军烈士墓和苏军阵亡烈士纪念碑，纪念“九三”抗日胜利暨苏联军队解放东北十二周年。
    在苏军烈士墓前举行了祭扫仪式。沈阳市副市长张力克在祭扫仪式上讲了话。
    又讯　今天上午，旅大市党和政府负责人和各界代表五百多人在市内斯大林广场上的苏军烈士塔前敬献花圈，纪念十二年前为解放东北、击败日本侵略者而英勇牺牲的苏军烈士。
