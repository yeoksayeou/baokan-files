Title: Czechoslovakia Announces Candidacy for Security Council Seat
Author:
Date: 1957-09-01
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, New York, August 31st: The Czechoslovakian delegation to the United Nations issued a statement on August 31st, announcing that Czechoslovakia will run for a non-permanent seat on the Security Council for 1958 and 1959, which originally belonged to an Eastern European country.

### 捷宣布竞选安理会席位

1957-09-01
第6版()
专栏：

    捷宣布竞选安理会席位
    据新华社31日讯  纽约消息：捷克斯洛伐克驻联合国代表团8月31日发表声明，宣布捷克斯洛伐克将竞选1958和1959年安全理事会中原来属于东欧国家的一个非常任理事的席位。
