Title: Arab League Makes Decision to Support the Struggles of Oman and Algeria
Author:
Date: 1957-09-06
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 4th: The Arab League Council decided today that Arab countries will continue to support the legitimate struggle of the Imamate of Oman, and instructed the representatives of these countries to the United Nations to take steps to ensure victory when the Oman issue is brought before the United Nations General Assembly.

On the Algerian issue, the Council called on all Arab countries to support Algeria in the United Nations and to provide all possible assistance to the nationalists.

The Arab League Council met after the meeting of the League's Political Affairs Committee. The Council approved all the decisions made by the Political Affairs Committee in the past two days.

At its final meeting today, the Political Affairs Committee decided to re-elect Abdul Khaleq Hassouna as Secretary-General of the Arab League for a term of five years.

Xinhua News Agency, September 4th: Damascus dispatch: Syrian Prime Minister al-Asali said on September 3rd: "Syria will do everything possible to provide all kinds of material and other assistance to help the Yemeni and Omani people stop British aggression."

Al-Asali made these remarks to reporters after receiving telegrams from the King of Yemen and the Imam of Oman. The King of Yemen sent a telegram to Syrian President Kuwatli expressing his determination to resist new British aggression. The Imam of Oman requested financial, political and military support in his telegram.

### 阿拉伯联盟作出决定  支持阿曼和阿尔及利亚斗争

1957-09-06
第6版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯联盟作出决定
    支持阿曼和阿尔及利亚斗争
    新华社开罗4日电　阿拉伯联盟理事会今天决定阿拉伯国家将继续支持阿曼教长国的合法斗争，并且指示这些国家驻联合国的代表当阿曼问题在联合国大会上提出来的时候采取步骤以保证获得胜利。
    在阿尔及利亚问题上，理事会号召各阿拉伯国家在联合国支持阿尔及利亚，并且对民族主义者给予一切可能的援助。
    阿拉伯联盟理事会是在联盟政治事务委员会会议结束后举行会议的。理事会批准了政治事务委员会在过去两天内所作出的一切决定。
    政治事务委员会在今天举行的最后一次会议上决定，阿卜杜勒—哈勒克·哈苏纳连任阿拉伯联盟秘书长五年。
    新华社4日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚总理阿萨利9月3日说：“叙利亚将尽一切力量提供各种物质的和其它的援助来帮助也门人和阿曼人制止英国的侵略。”
    阿萨利是在接到也门国王和阿曼教长的电报之后对记者这样说的。也门国王发电报给叙利亚总统库阿特利表示决心抗击英国新的侵略。阿曼教长在电报中要求叙利亚给予财政、政治和军事支持。
