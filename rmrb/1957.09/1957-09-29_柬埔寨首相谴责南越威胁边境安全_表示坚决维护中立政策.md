Title: Cambodian Prime Minister Condemns South Vietnam for Threatening Border Security; Expresses Determination to Uphold Neutral Policy
Author:
Date: 1957-09-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, September 28th: The Cambodian "People's Socialist Community Newspaper" reported on the 27th that Cambodian Prime Minister Sim Var, in an interview with the newspaper regarding the issue of South Vietnam concentrating troops on the Cambodian border, condemned the South Vietnamese authorities' attempts to undermine Cambodia's neutral policy. Sim Var said that the Cambodian government will wholeheartedly uphold the neutral policy it is pursuing.

### 柬埔寨首相谴责南越威胁边境安全  表示坚决维护中立政策

1957-09-29
第6版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨首相谴责南越威胁边境安全
    表示坚决维护中立政策
    据新华社金边28日电　柬埔寨“人民社会同盟报”27日报道，柬埔寨首相沈法在就南越在柬埔寨边境集结军队问题对这家报纸记者发表谈话时，谴责南越当局企图破坏柬埔寨的中立政策。沈法说，柬埔寨政府将全心全意地维护它所执行的中立政策。
