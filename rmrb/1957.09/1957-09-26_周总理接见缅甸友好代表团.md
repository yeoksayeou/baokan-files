Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Burmese Friendship Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-09-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th. Premier Zhou Enlai received the Burmese Friendship Delegation headed by U Min Thein this evening. Present at the reception were Vice Premier He Long, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, our Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming, and Director of the Asian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wenjin. After the reception, Premier Zhou hosted a buffet dinner for all members of the delegation. The delegation arrived in Beijing this afternoon.

### 周总理接见缅甸友好代表团

1957-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见缅甸友好代表团
    据新华社25日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见了以吴敏登为首的缅甸友好代表团。接见时在座的有副总理贺龙，外交部副部长章汉夫，我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和外交部亚洲司司长章文晋。接见后，周总理举行便宴招待代表团全体成员。这个代表团是今天下午到达北京的。
