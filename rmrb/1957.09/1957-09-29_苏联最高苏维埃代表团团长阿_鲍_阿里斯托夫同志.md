Title: Comrade A.B. Aristov, Head of the Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation

Author:
Date: 1957-09-29
Page: 6

Comrade Averky Borisovich Aristov, head of the Soviet Supreme Soviet delegation visiting our country at the invitation of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, is an outstanding Soviet statesman and one of the leaders of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

Comrade Aristov was born in 1903 into a fisherman's family in the town of Krasny Yar, Astrakhan Oblast. From the age of twelve, he worked in the fisheries of the northern Caspian Sea.

In 1921, Comrade Aristov joined the Communist Party. He served in the Red Army. He first studied at a workers' technical school and later transferred to the Leningrad Industrial Institute, graduating with a degree in metallurgical engineering. After graduation, he worked as a foreman and engineer in a factory in Leningrad, and later taught at the Leningrad Industrial Institute and the Sverdlov Industrial Institute. He has held the academic title of associate professor and the degree of associate doctor of engineering.

From 1939 to 1955, Comrade Aristov worked in the Party, serving as Party secretary in many regions. He served as the Party secretary of the following regions: Sverdlovsk Oblast, Kemerovo Oblast, Krasnoyarsk Krai, Chelyabinsk Oblast, and Khabarovsk Krai.

Comrade Aristov has been a member of the CPSU Central Committee since 1952. He is currently a member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee, a secretary of the Secretariat of the CPSU Central Committee, and a member of the Bureau of the CPSU Central Committee for the Russian Federation.

Comrade Aristov is a deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and a member of the Legislative Bills Committee of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

### 苏联最高苏维埃代表团团长阿·鲍·阿里斯托夫同志

1957-09-29
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃代表团团长阿·鲍·阿里斯托夫同志
    应我国全国人民代表大会常务委员会邀请，率领苏联最高苏维埃代表团前来我国访问的阿维尔基·鲍里索维奇·阿里斯托夫同志，是苏联杰出的政治家，是苏共中央领导人之一。
    阿里斯托夫同志于1903年生在阿斯特拉罕州红谷城的一个渔民家庭里。他从十二岁起就在里海北部的渔场里作工。
    1921年阿里斯托夫同志加入共产党。他曾经在红军里服务过。他先在工人专科学校学习，后来转入列宁格勒工业学院学习，毕业时得到冶金工程师学位。毕业后在列宁格勒一个工厂里担任工长和工程师，以后在列宁格勒工业学院和斯维德洛夫工学院作教学工作。曾经获得大学副教授学衔和工学副博士学位。
    1939年到1955年，阿里斯托夫同志一直作党的工作，在很多地区担任党的书记。他先后担任过：斯维德洛夫斯克州、基米罗伏州、克拉斯诺雅尔斯克边疆区、车里雅宾斯克州和伯力边疆区等地区的党委书记。
    阿里斯托夫同志从1952年起就被选为苏共中央委员，他现在是苏共中央主席团委员、苏共中央书记处书记和苏共中央俄罗斯联邦局委员。
    阿里斯托夫同志是苏联最高苏维埃代表和苏联最高苏维埃联盟院法案委员会委员。
