Title: Indian Vice President Returns to Beijing After Visiting Mongolia, Bids Farewell to Chinese Leaders
Author:
Date: 1957-09-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan, along with his entourage, paid farewell visits to Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai on the evening of the 23rd, the day before his departure from Beijing. Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, also accompanied him.

During the farewell, the guests and hosts engaged in friendly talks. Vice President Radhakrishnan also presented gifts to Chairman Mao, Vice Chairman Zhu, Chairman Liu, and Premier Zhou.

At the end of the farewell, Chairman Mao and Vice President Radhakrishnan walked out of the gate hand in hand. Before getting into the car, Vice President Radhakrishnan warmly embraced Chairman Mao. The Vice President also embraced Vice Chairman Zhu, Chairman Liu, and Premier Zhou.

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: After visiting the Mongolian People's Republic, Indian Vice President Dr. Radhakrishnan and his entourage returned to Beijing by special plane this afternoon. Ambassador R.K. Nehru also arrived in Beijing on the same plane.

When Vice President Radhakrishnan left Ulaanbaatar today, Chairman Sambuu of the Presidium of the Mongolian Great People's Khural, along with Zedenbal, Damba, Surenjab, Tsende, and other national leaders, saw him off at the airport.

### 印度副总统访问蒙古后回京  向我国家领导人辞行

1957-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    印度副总统访问蒙古后回京
    向我国家领导人辞行
    新华社23日讯　拉达克里希南副总统在离开北京前一天——23日的晚间偕随行人员向毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理辞行。印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁也随同前往。
    在辞行的时候，宾主进行了友好的谈话。拉达克里希南副总统还向毛主席、朱副主席、刘委员长和周总理赠送了礼品。
  辞行结束后，毛主席和拉达克里希南副总统手挽着手走出大门口。在登车以前，拉达克里希南副总统同毛主席热烈拥抱。副总统还同朱副主席、刘委员长和周总理拥抱。
    据新华社23日讯　印度副总统拉达克里希南博士和他的随行人员在访问了蒙古人民共和国以后，今天下午乘专机回到北京。拉·库·尼赫鲁大使也同机到京。
    拉达克里希南副总统今日离开乌兰巴托的时候，蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布和泽登巴尔、达姆巴、苏伦扎布、曾德等国家领导人到机场送行。
