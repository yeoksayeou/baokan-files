Date: 1957-09-15

Letters to the Editor

Concerning my letter "Why Take the Long Route Instead of the Short Route" published on page 5 of your newspaper on August 18th, please correct two points:

	① In the second paragraph, third line, please add the word "water transport" before "...per ton kilometer." That is, "the freight rate for water transport per ton kilometer..."

	② In the third paragraph, sixth line, please add "kilometers" after "two hundred and ten million tons..." so it reads "will lose two hundred and ten million ton-kilometers..."

	Tan Zhaomo
