Title: Attending the Funeral of King Haakon VII of Norway Mao Zedong Appoints Wang Youping as Special Envoy
Author:
Date: 1957-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 28th: Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has appointed Wang Youping, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Norway, as Special Envoy of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China, to attend the funeral of the late King Haakon VII of Norway.

### 参加挪威国王哈康七世的丧礼  毛主席任命王幼平为主席特使

1957-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    参加挪威国王哈康七世的丧礼
    毛主席任命王幼平为主席特使
    新华社28日讯　中华人民共和国毛泽东主席任命中华人民共和国驻挪威大使王幼平为中华人民共和国主席特使，参加挪威已故国王哈康七世的丧礼。
