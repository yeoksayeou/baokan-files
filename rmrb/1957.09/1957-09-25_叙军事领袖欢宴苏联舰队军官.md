Title: Syrian Military Leaders Host Banquet for Soviet Fleet Officers
Author:
Date: 1957-09-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, September 23rd: Lieutenant Colonel Amir, commander of the Syrian coastal region, hosted a banquet on September 22nd to entertain Vice Admiral Kotov, who led the Soviet fleet to visit the Syrian port of Latakia, and other Soviet officers. Attending the banquet were Nifouri, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Syrian Army, and many Syrian officers.

In his speech at the banquet, Nifouri emphasized that the friendship between Syria and the Soviet Union is conducive to universal peace, and that this friendship is based on the principle of complete equality. In his reply, Kotov said that the Soviet warships' visit to Syria was a visit between friends, which proves the good relations between the two countries.

After the banquet, the Soviet guests visited the scenic spots and historical sites of Latakia.

Syrian newspapers have reported in detail on the visit of Soviet warships to the port of Latakia.

### 叙军事领袖欢宴苏联舰队军官

1957-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    叙军事领袖欢宴苏联舰队军官
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社大马士革23日讯：叙利亚海岸地区司令阿密尔中校9月22日设宴招待率领苏联舰队访问叙利亚拉塔基亚港的科托夫海军中将和其他苏联军官。出席宴会的有叙利亚陆军副参谋长尼福里和许多叙利亚军官。
    尼福里在宴会上讲话时着重指出，叙利亚和苏联两国的友谊有利于普遍和平，这种友谊是以完全平等的原则为基础的。科托夫在致答辞时说，苏联军舰访问叙利亚是朋友之间的访问，这证明两国间的关系良好。
    宴会后，苏联客人参观了拉塔基亚的名胜古迹。
    叙利亚各报都详细报道了苏联军舰访问拉塔基亚港的消息。
