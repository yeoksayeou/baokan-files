Title: Japanese Biologists Declare: Never Research Nuclear Weapons
Author:
Date: 1957-09-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, September 8th: The plenary session of the Democratic Scientists Association Biology Group, held in Kyoto on September 7th, passed a resolution containing a declaration against atomic and hydrogen bombs. In the declaration, these scientists expressed their opposition to atomic and hydrogen bombs and their determination never to engage in atomic weapons research. The declaration was signed by 120 people, including Professor Denzaburo Miyaji of Kyoto University and Professor Jiro Honjo of Osaka University.

### 日本生物学家表示  决不研究核武器

1957-09-10
第6版()
专栏：

    日本生物学家表示
    决不研究核武器
    据新华社8日讯　东京消息：9月7日在京都举行的民主主义科学者协会生物会全会通过一项反对原子弹和氢弹声明的决议。这些科学家在声明中表明了他们反对原子弹、氢弹的立场和决不从事原子武器研究工作的决心。在声明上签字的有京都大学教授宫地传三郎、大阪大学教授本城市次郎等一百二十人。
