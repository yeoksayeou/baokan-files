Title: Clear Away This Pile of Stinking Garbage
Author: Chao Hai
Date: 1957-09-20
Page: 8

Clear Away This Pile of Stinking Garbage
　　Chao Hai

　　The Wu "small family" announced its establishment,
　　A rightist banner was unfurled in front of the door;
　　"Eldest brother" and "eldest sister" sit in the central hall,
　　A group of underlings call each other brothers;
　　There are no secrets within the family (1),
　　But one must be vigilant against the Party at all times...
　　The family's members are from all walks of life,
　　Literature, drama, music, and editing...
　　You are "China's Pushkin,"
　　I am "China's Belinsky";
　　The Party better not lead literature and art anymore,
　　The "organ of power" is in the hands of the Wu family (2).
　　Attacking leaders, slandering the organization,
　　Is called "displaying the family's power";
　　Eating and drinking lavishly, playing with women,
　　Is called "the family's cherished secret";
　　Attacking the Party is a "combination of hobbies";
　　Flattery and co-opting are "pure friendship."
　　The family's interests are above all else,
　　"Comrade" is the most hateful term;
　　Socialism is a dark corner,
　　Literature and art are not as good as before;
　　Taking advantage of the Communist Party's rectification,
　　Is a good time to launch an attack...
　　No! Rightist gentlemen,
　　You have miscalculated!
　　In the garden of socialism,
　　Don't even think about spreading bacteria and poison gas!
　　Everyone pick up iron brooms,
　　And clear away this pile of stinking garbage!

Note (1) "There are no secrets within the family" is one of the "house rules" of the "small family."
(2) Members of Wu Zuguang's rightist group once called their "small family" the "highest organ of power in literary and art circles."

### 清除这堆腥臭的垃圾
超海
1957-09-20
第8版()
专栏：

　　清除这堆腥臭的垃圾
　　超海
　　吴姓“小家族”宣布成立，
　　门前扯起了右字大旗；
　　“大哥”和“大嫂”坐镇中堂，
　　一群小喽啰称兄道弟；
　　在家族之内没有秘密（一），
　　对党可必须随时警惕……
　　家族的成员各界都有，
　　文学、戏剧、音乐和编辑……
　　你就是“中国的普希金”，
　　我是“中国的别林斯基”；
　　党趁早别再领导文艺，
　　“权力机关”在吴家手里（二）。
　　打击领导、诬蔑组织，
　　叫做“显示家族的威力”；
　　大吃大喝、玩弄女性，
　　叫做“家族眷恋的秘密”；
　　向党进攻是“爱好的结合”；
　　吹捧拉拢是“纯洁的友谊”。
　　家族的利益高于一切，
　　“同志”是最可恨的名词；
　　社会主义是黑暗角落，
　　文学艺术是今不如昔；
　　趁着共产党开始整风，
　　是发动进攻的好时机……
　　不！右派先生，
　　你们打错了主意！
　　在社会主义的花园里，
　　休想散布细菌和毒气！
　　大家拿起钢铁的扫帚，
　　清除这堆腥臭的垃圾！
    注（一）“家族之内没有秘密”是“小家族”的“家规”之一。
      （二）吴祖光右派集团的成员曾把他们的“小家族”称为“文艺界的最高权力机关”。
