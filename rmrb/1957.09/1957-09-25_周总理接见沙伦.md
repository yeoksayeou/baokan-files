Title: Zhou Enlai Receives Salon

Author: 
Date: 1957-09-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 24th — Premier Zhou Enlai today received Sim Var, Chairman of the Commissions of the Parliament of the Kingdom of Cambodia, including the Defense Commission, the Internal Affairs and Foreign Affairs Commission, and the Finance Commission.

Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Vice President Qiao Guanhua, and Secretary-General Wu Maosun were also present at the reception.

### 周总理接见沙伦

1957-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见沙伦
    新华社24日讯　周恩来总理今天接见了柬埔寨王国议会委员会及王国议会国防委员会、内政外交委员会、财政委员会主席深·沙伦。
    接见时，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、副会长乔冠华、秘书长吴茂荪也在座。
