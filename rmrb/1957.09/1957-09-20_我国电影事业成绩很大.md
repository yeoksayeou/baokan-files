Title: Our Country's Film Industry Has Made Great Achievements
Author:
Date: 1957-09-20
Page: 3

Digital Facts and Figures

　　Our country's film industry is one area where right-wing elements have launched vicious attacks. Right-wing elements slanderously claim that our film industry is worse than before, that domestically produced films are unpopular, and that attendance rates are low. Here are some figures that clearly show the development of our film industry over the past few years.

　　Film Screening Units: By the end of 1956, there were 8,662 film screening units nationwide, nearly tripling the 2,282 units in 1952. Among them, more than 2,300 film projection teams were developed in 1956 alone, equivalent to the total number of film projection teams developed in the past few years.
    There are 952 cinemas nationwide, an increase of 1.5 times compared to the highest number of 382 before liberation in 1936.

　　Film Audiences: In 1956, there were more than 1.397 billion film attendees. If calculated based on 70% of the country's population of 600 million (excluding infants), each person watched films more than three times a year on average. In 1949, there were 50 million film attendees. The number of film attendees in 1956 increased 27 times compared to 1949 and 41% compared to 1955.

　　Film Production: The following table shows the development of film production in the years after liberation (1950 and 1951 are not included) (the number of films produced does not include newsreels):

　　　　　　1949　1952　1953　1954　1955　1956
　　Films Produced　22　46　53　80　112　176
　　Dubbed Films　3　85　95　109　136　238

　　The number of dubbed films in 1956 increased 78.3 times compared to 1949.

　　The films produced in 1956 included 40 art films (including 8 color art films), an increase of 5.6 times compared to 1949.

    In addition, 89 news documentaries were produced in 1956, an increase of 13.8 times compared to 1949. (Domestic Data Group of this Newspaper)

### 我国电影事业成绩很大

1957-09-20
第3版()
专栏：数字与事实

　　我国电影事业成绩很大
    我国的电影事业，是右派分子进行恶毒攻击的一个方面。右派分子污蔑说我们的电影事业今不如昔，说国产片不受欢迎，上座率低等等。这里举出下列一些数字，大家可以看清楚我国电影事业几年来的发展状况。
　　电影放映单位　1956年底，全国有电影放映单位八千六百六十二个，比1952年的二千二百八十二个增加近三倍。其中1956年一年中发展的电影放映队有二千三百多个，等于过去几年来发展的电影放映队总数。
    全国有电影院九百五十二座，比解放前最高的1936年的三百八十二座增加一点五倍。
　　电影观众　1956年电影观众为十三亿九千七百多万人次，如果按全国六亿人口的70％（除去幼儿）计算，平均每人每年能看三次以上电影。1949年电影观众为五千万人次，1956年的电影观众人次比1949年增加二十七倍，比1955年增41％。
　　影片生产　从下表可以知道解放后几年来（1950、1951年未列入）影片生产的发展（摄制影片数字内不包括新闻片）
　　　　　　1949年　1952年　1953年　1954年　1955年　1956年
　　摄制影片　22　    46　    53　    80　   112　   176
　　译制影片　 3　    85　    95　   109　   136　   238
　　1956年译制影片，比1949年增加七十八点三倍。
　　1956年摄制的影片中包括艺术影片四十部（其中彩色艺术片八部），比1949年增加五点六倍。
    此外，1956年还生产了新闻纪录片八十九部，比1949年增加十三点八倍。　（本报国内资料组）
