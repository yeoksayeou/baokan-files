Title: Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling Hosts a Banquet in Honor of Vice President Radhakrishnan
Author:
Date: 1957-09-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, September 25th. Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress hosted a banquet at her residence today in honor of the Vice President of the Republic of India, Radhakrishnan. Vice Chairman Guo Moruo attended as an accompaniment.

    Vice Chairman Soong received the distinguished guest in her quiet living room. Vice President Radhakrishnan thanked Vice Chairman Soong for braving the rain to greet him at the airport yesterday.

    He and Vice Chairman Soong and others had a very harmonious conversation at the banquet. He also spoke about his initial meeting with Gandhi in 1913 and Gandhi's character. Vice Chairman Soong spoke of her pleasant memories of visiting India the year before and the continuously increasing friendship between China and India.

    Before parting, Vice Chairman Soong and Vice President Radhakrishnan, and Mrs. R.K. Nehru, the Indian Ambassador to China, took a group photo as a memento. Vice President Radhakrishnan presented gifts he brought from India to Vice Chairman Soong.

    Also invited to attend today were Shanghai Deputy Mayors Cao Diqiu and Jin Zhonghua, the Indian Ambassador to China, Mr. and Mrs. R.K. Nehru, and the Indian Consul General in Shanghai, Charlie.

### 宋庆龄副委员长欢宴拉达克里希南副总统

1957-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄副委员长欢宴拉达克里希南副总统
    新华社上海25日电　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄今天在她的寓所设宴欢迎印度共和国副总统拉达克里希南，郭沫若副委员长出席作陪。
    宋副委员长在她幽静的客厅里接待贵宾。拉达克里希南副总统感谢宋副委员长昨天冒雨到机场去迎接他。
    他和宋副委员长等在宴会上谈得非常融洽。他还谈到1913年他最初会见甘地时的情况和甘地的为人。宋副委员长谈到她前年访问印度的愉快回忆和中印友谊不断增进的情况。
    临别时，宋副委员长和拉达克里希南副总统、印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁夫人合影留念。拉达克里希南副总统把他从印度带来的礼品赠送给宋副委员长。
    今天应邀出席作陪的还有上海市副长市曹荻秋、金仲华，印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁夫妇、印度驻上海总领事查理。
