Title: Premier Zhou Receives Czechoslovak Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th – Premier Zhou Enlai received this afternoon the Czechoslovak National Assembly delegation headed by Chairman Fierlinger and the Prague Municipal Central People's Committee delegation headed by Mayor Svoboda.

    Present at the meeting were Vice Premier Li Xiannian, and Li Xuefeng, Ji Pengfei, Liu Guanyi, and Feng Jiping.

    Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Bušiňák, was also present.

### 周总理接见捷议会代表团

1957-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见捷议会代表团
    新华社28日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了以费林格主席为首的捷克斯洛伐克国民议会代表团和以斯沃波达市长为首的布拉格市中央人民委员会代表团。
    接见时在座的，有李先念副总理和李雪峰、姬鹏飞、刘贯一、冯基平。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使布希尼亚克也在座。
