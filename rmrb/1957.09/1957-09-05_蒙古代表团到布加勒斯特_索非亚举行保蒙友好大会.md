Title: Mongolian Delegation Arrives in Bucharest, Bulgarian-Mongolian Friendship Rally Held in Sofia
Author:
Date: 1957-09-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic departed from Sofia by plane on the morning of the 4th and arrived in Bucharest on the same day.

Before the departure of the government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic from Sofia, the Sofia City Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, together with the Sofia City Committee of the Bulgarian Patriotic Front and the Sofia City People's Council, held a Bulgarian-Mongolian friendship rally for the Mongolian government delegation's visit to Bulgaria at the September 9th Square in Sofia on the afternoon of the 3rd. Bulgarian Chairman of the Council of Ministers Yugov spoke at the rally. Yugov spoke about the achievements of the Bulgarian and Mongolian people in socialist construction. The Bulgarian and Mongolian people belong to the friendly big family of socialist camp countries headed by the Soviet Union. The friendly visit of the Mongolian government delegation will help develop and strengthen the fraternal ties between the Bulgarian and Mongolian people and will help strengthen the unity of the socialist camp countries and world peace.

The head of the Mongolian government delegation and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic Tsedenbal also spoke at the rally. On behalf of the Mongolian government delegation, he warmly thanked the Bulgarian people for the warm hospitality they received during their visit to Bulgaria. He said that our two peoples have a common goal—building socialism, and unbreakable friendly ties based on the unwavering principles of proletarian internationalism. Our eternal friendship and loyalty to the Soviet Union bring our two peoples closer together and make us sincere friends marching shoulder to shoulder toward a bright and happy future—socialism.

### 蒙古代表团到布加勒斯特  索非亚举行保蒙友好大会

1957-09-05
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙古代表团到布加勒斯特
    索非亚举行保蒙友好大会
    本报综合报道　据新华社消息：蒙古人民共和国政府代表团4日早晨乘飞机离索非亚，于当日到达布加勒斯特。
    在蒙古人民共和国政府代表团离开索非亚前夕，保加利亚共产党索非亚市委员会会同保加利亚爱国阵线索非亚市委员会和索非亚市人民会议，3日下午在索非亚九九广场为蒙古政府代表团的访问保加利亚举行保蒙友好大会。保加利亚部长会议主席于哥夫在会上讲了话。于哥夫谈到保蒙两国人民在社会主义建设方面所获得的成就。保加利亚和蒙古两国人民属于以苏联为首的社会主义阵营国家的友好大家庭。蒙古政府代表团的友好访问，将有助于发展和加强保蒙两国人民的兄弟联系，将有助于加强社会主义阵营国家的团结和世界和平。
    蒙古政府代表团团长、蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔也在会上讲了话。他代表蒙古政府代表团热烈感谢在保加利亚访问时所受到的热情招待。他说，我们两国人民有共同的目标——建设社会主义，有以无产阶级国际主义不可动摇的原则为基础的牢不可破的友好联系。我们的永恒友谊和对苏联的忠诚，使我们两国人民更加接近，使我们成为向着光辉幸福的未来——社会主义并肩前进的真挚朋友。
