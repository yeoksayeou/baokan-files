Title: New Antibiotic "Malucidin"
Author:
Date: 1957-09-16
Page: 7

	New Antibiotic "Malucidin"

	American Yale University microbiologist, Baffenjiefu, has recently isolated an antibiotic protein from yeast, called "Malucidin."

	The new antibiotic Malucidin can kill all bacteria and fungi, or inhibit their growth and reproduction. According to experiments by bacteriologists, veterinarians, and microbiologists, small doses of Malucidin can protect mice from infection, and can also effectively treat fungal skin diseases in dogs.

	According to experiments, in addition to its antibiotic effects, Malucidin can also remove animal and human allergies to protein components, such as removing the allergies of mice and children to pertussis vaccines.

	Scientists will continue to study the application of Malucidin in the human body.

### 新抗生素“马六西丁”

1957-09-16
第7版()
专栏：世界科学技术新成就

    新抗生素“马六西丁”
    美国耶鲁大学微生物学家巴芬捷夫新近从酵母中分离出了一种具有抗生作用的蛋白，叫做“Malucidin”（“马六西丁”）。
    新抗生素Malucidin可以杀死一切细菌和真菌，或抑制其生长、繁殖。根据细菌学家、兽医学家和微生物学家们的试验，小剂量的Malucidin即可使小鼠免于感染疾病，还可以有效地治疗犬类真菌性皮肤病。
    根据试验，Malucidin除具抗生作用外，还可脱除动物和人体对蛋白质组成物的过敏性，例如脱除小鼠和儿童对百日咳菌苗的过敏性。
    科学家将继续研究Malucidin在人体的应用。
