Title: Road Surface of Yangtze River Bridge Highway Bridge Completed
Author:
Date: 1957-09-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, September 15th: The road workers completed the paving of the road surface of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge highway bridge yesterday afternoon. Now, the highway bridge of the Yangtze River Bridge is completely connected to the highway network of Wuhan.

### 长江大桥公路桥路面铺好

1957-09-18
第5版()
专栏：

    长江大桥公路桥路面铺好
    新华社武汉15日电　筑路工人们已经在昨天下午铺完了武汉长江大桥的公路桥路面。现在，长江大桥的公路桥已经同武汉市的公路网完全联接起来。
