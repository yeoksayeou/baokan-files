Title: Sino-Hungarian Premiers Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1957-09-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 28th: Hungarian Premier János Kádár held talks with Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai today.

    On the Hungarian side, participants in the talks included members of the Hungarian government delegation: State Minister Marosan, Hungarian Ambassador to China Nogradi, and delegation staff: Director of the Intelligence Bureau Szirmai, Deputy Director of the Planning Bureau Vaj, and Director of the Asian-African Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Kósa.

    On the Chinese side, participants in the talks included Vice Premier He Long, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Chinese Ambassador to Hungary Hao Deqing, and Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Li Huichuan.

### 中匈两国总理举行会谈

1957-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中匈两国总理举行会谈
    新华社28日讯　匈牙利人民共和国卡达尔总理今天同我国周恩来总理进行了会谈。
    匈牙利方面参加会谈的，有匈牙利政府代表团团员：国务部部长马罗山，匈牙利驻中国大使诺格拉第，和代表团随行人员：情报局局长西尔莫伊，计划局副局长瓦伊，外交部亚非处处长科什。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有副总理贺龙，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长张闻天，中国驻匈牙利大使郝德青，和外交部苏联东欧司副司长李汇川。
