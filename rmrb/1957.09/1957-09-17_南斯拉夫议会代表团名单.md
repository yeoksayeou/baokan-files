Title: List of Yugoslav Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-09-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 16th. The list of the delegation of the Federal People's Assembly of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia is as follows:

Head of Delegation:
    Petar Stambolić
    President of the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia, member of the Central Executive Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, member of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Serbia.

Members:
    Filip Bajković
    Member of the Federal Council of the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia, member of the Federal Executive Council, Chairman of the Executive Council of the People's Republic of Montenegro, member of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Montenegro.

    Marijan Brecelj
    Member of the Federal Council of the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia, Secretary-General of the Federal State Secretariat for Commodity Circulation, member of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Slovenia.

    Štefan Doronjski
    Member of the Federal Council of the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia, member of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, member of the Federal Council of the Socialist Alliance of Working People of Yugoslavia, Chairman of the People's Assembly of the Autonomous Province of Vojvodina.

    Josip Cazi
    Chairman of the Economic Organization Committee of the Producers' Council of the Federal National Assembly, member of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, Chairman of the Producers' Council of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Croatia, Chairman of the Trade Union of the People's Republic of Croatia.

    Grujo Novaković
    Member of the Federal Council of the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia, member of the Central Supervisory Commission of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, member of the Executive Council of the People's Republic of Bosnia and Herzegovina.

    Franjo Gaži
    Member of the Federal Council of the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia, member of the Executive Council of the People's Republic of Croatia.

    Mileva Planojević (female)
    Member of the Federal Council of the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia, member of the Central Committee of the Association of Veterans of the People's Liberation War, member of the Central Committee of the Socialist Alliance of Working People of Serbia.

    Krsto Markovski
    Member of the Federal Council of the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia, member of the Central Executive Committee of the League of Communists of Macedonia.

    Sinan Hasani
    Member of the Federal Council of the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia, member of the Executive Council of the Autonomous Region of Kosovo-Metohija, member of the Committee of the Autonomous Region of Kosovo-Metohija of the League of Communists of Serbia.

### 南斯拉夫议会代表团名单

1957-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　南斯拉夫议会代表团名单
    新华社16日讯　南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国联邦国民议会代表团名单如下：

    彼得·斯坦鲍利奇
    南斯拉夫联邦国民议会主席，南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央执行委员会委员，塞尔维亚共产主义者联盟中央委员。
    团员：
    菲利普·拜科维奇
    南斯拉夫联邦国民议会联邦院议员，联邦执行委员会委员。门的内哥罗人民共和国执行委员会主席，门的内哥罗人民共和国共产主义者联盟中央委员。
    马里扬·布雷策利
    南斯拉夫联邦国民议会联邦院议员，联邦商品流通国务秘书处秘书长，斯洛文尼亚共产主义者联盟中央委员。

    联邦国民议会生产者院经济组织委员会主席，南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员，克罗地亚人民共和国国民议会生产者院主席，克罗地亚人民共和国工会主席。

    联邦国民议会联邦院议员，马其顿共产主义者联盟中央执行委员会委员。

    联邦国民议会联邦院议员，戈索伏—密托希亚自治区执行委员会委员，塞尔维亚共产主义者联盟戈索伏—密托希亚自治区委员会委员。
