Title: East German Delegation Visits Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1957-09-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, September 23rd: A three-member delegation from East Germany, led by Deputy Prime Minister of East Germany, Mr. Oelssner, arrived in Wuhan by plane this morning. They were accompanied by Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Luo Chengde and others. Hubei Province Governor Zhang Tixue, Vice Governor Wang Haishan, and Wuhan Vice Mayor Wang Kewen welcomed them at the airport.
    In the afternoon, the entire delegation, accompanied by Vice Governor Wang Haishan and others, visited the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge. Deputy Prime Minister Oelssner spoke with Wuhan municipal cadres who were participating in voluntary labor on the bridge, and after the visit, he inscribed a message for the bridge.

### 民主德国代表团到武汉参观

1957-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国代表团到武汉参观
    新华社武汉23日电　民主德国代表团一行三人，在团长、民主德国副总理厄斯纳率领下，今天上午乘飞机到达武汉。陪同前来的有对外贸易部副部长罗成德等。湖北省省长张体学、副省长王海山，武汉市副市长王克文等到机场欢迎。
    下午，代表团全体人员在副省长王海山等人陪同下参观了武汉长江大桥，厄斯纳副总理同正在大桥参加义务劳动的武汉市机关干部谈了话，参观后并为大桥题了词。
