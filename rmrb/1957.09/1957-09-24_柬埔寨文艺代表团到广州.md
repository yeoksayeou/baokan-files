Title: Cambodian Art Troupe Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1957-09-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 22nd: The Cambodian Cultural and Art Delegation, a group of fifty-six people, arrived in Guangzhou this afternoon by special train.

The art troupe, led by Fang Bingshun, Deputy Secretary of State of the Cambodian Ministry of Education, is visiting China at the invitation of our Ministry of Culture. Members of the art troupe include Cambodian Princess Norodom Phakadevi and Prince Norodom Chakrapong. Cao Guisheng, Deputy Head of the Economic Delegation of our country to the Kingdom of Cambodia, accompanied them.

### 柬埔寨文艺代表团到广州

1957-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨文艺代表团到广州
    据新华社广州22日电　柬埔寨文化艺术代表团一行五十六人，今天下午乘专车到达广州。
    由柬埔寨教育部副国务秘书方炳顺率领的这个艺术团，是应我文化部邀请来我国访问的。艺术团的成员中有柬埔寨王室公主诺罗敦·拍花黛维和亲王诺罗敦·夏卡朋。我国驻柬埔寨王国经济代表团副团长曹桂生陪同到达。
