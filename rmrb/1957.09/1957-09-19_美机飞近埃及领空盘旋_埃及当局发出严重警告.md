Title: American Planes Fly Near Egyptian Airspace and Circle; Egyptian Authorities Issue Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1957-09-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 18th. "Al-Ahram" reported on the 17th that Egyptian authorities have issued a warning: Any foreign aircraft must not approach the Egyptian coast or fly over Egyptian territory without Egyptian permission, otherwise Egypt will not be responsible for the consequences.

This warning was issued after a recent incident. The incident involved several aircraft from the US Sixth Fleet flying near Alexandria in Egyptian territorial waters, circling there for a while before flying away.

### 美机飞近埃及领空盘旋  埃及当局发出严重警告

1957-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

    美机飞近埃及领空盘旋
　　埃及当局发出严重警告
    新华社开罗18日电　“金字塔报”17日报道，埃及当局已经发出警告：任何外国飞机没有得到埃及准许不得飞近埃及海岸和飞越埃及的领土，否则埃及将对后果不负责任。
    这个警告是在最近发生的一次事件以后发出的。这次事件是，美国第六舰队的一些飞机在埃及领海附近飞近亚历山大港，并且在那里盘旋了一会才飞走。
