Title: Publication of "Propaganda Outline" as a Separate Booklet in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-26
Page: 4

Publication of Books and Periodicals Commemorating the 40th Anniversary of the October Revolution

    The "Propaganda Outline for Commemorating the 40th Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution (1917-1957)" will be published as a separate booklet with 140,000 copies, starting tomorrow in Beijing.

    This propaganda outline was jointly drafted by the Propaganda and Agitation Department of the CPSU Central Committee and the Institute of Marxism-Leninism of the CPSU Central Committee. It systematically and comprehensively describes the brilliant achievements of the Soviet Union's various constructions over the past forty years, describes the continuous development and growth of the socialist camp, points out the increasing disintegration of the imperialist colonial system, and criticizes the decadent ideas of "national communism" and "revisionism."

    The 140,000 copies to be released in Beijing are published by the People's Publishing House. At the end of this month, Chongqing will also publish and distribute 20,000 copies of this booklet. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 宣传提纲单行本在京发行

1957-09-26
第4版()
专栏：出版书刊纪念十月革命四十周年

    宣传提纲单行本在京发行
    “纪念伟大十月社会主义革命四十周年（1917——1957）”宣传提纲的十四万册单行本，明天起开始在北京发行。
    这个宣传提纲是苏共中央宣传与鼓动部和苏共中央马克思列宁主义研究院共同拟订的。它系统而详尽地叙述了四十年来苏联各项建设的辉煌成就，叙述了社会主义阵营的不断发展和壮大，指出了帝国主义殖民体系的日益土崩瓦解，并批判了“民族共产主义”和“修正主义”的腐朽观念。
    北京发行的十四万册单行本，是由人民出版社出版。本月底，重庆还将出版发行两万册这种单行本。　（新华社讯）
