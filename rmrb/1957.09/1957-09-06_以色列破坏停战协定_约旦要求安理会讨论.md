Title: Israel Violates Armistice Agreement, Jordan Requests Security Council Discussion
Author:
Date: 1957-09-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 5th, New York Dispatch: Jordan's representative to the United Nations requested on the 4th that the Security Council hold an emergency meeting to study the issue of Israel's violation of the Israel-Jordan armistice agreement.

Jordanian representative, Hekal, wrote a letter to the President of the Security Council accusing Israelis of entering the no-man's land south of Jerusalem under the protection of Israeli security forces, thereby violating the armistice agreement. It is reported that the Security Council may hold a meeting on the 6th to discuss Jordan's complaint against Israel.

### 以色列破坏停战协定  约旦要求安理会讨论

1957-09-06
第6版()
专栏：

    以色列破坏停战协定
    约旦要求安理会讨论
    新华社5日讯　纽约消息：约旦驻联合国代表4日要求安全理事会举行紧急会议，研究以色列破坏以—约停战协定的问题。
    约旦代表赫卡尔写给安全理事会主席一封信，指责以色列人在以色列保安部队的保护下进入了耶路撒冷南部的无人区，从而破坏了停战协定。有消息说，安全理事会可能在6日举行会议，讨论约旦对以色列的控诉。
