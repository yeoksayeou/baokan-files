Title: Women Representatives from Various Countries Leave Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1957-09-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. Women representatives from various countries who were invited to attend the Third National Women's Congress of China have been leaving China successively. Today, representatives from Vietnam, Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, the Soviet Union, and North Korea left Beijing for home. Before today, representatives from the Women's International Democratic Federation and representatives from Albania, East Germany, Yugoslavia, and Romania had left China. Representatives from Mongolia and Poland will also be leaving China soon.

### 各国妇女代表离京回国

1957-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    各国妇女代表离京回国
    新华社23日讯　应邀出席中国妇女第三次全国代表大会的各国妇女代表陆续离开中国。今天离京回国的有越南、保加利亚、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、苏联、朝鲜六个国家的代表。在今天以前离开中国的有国际民主妇联的代表和阿尔巴尼亚、民主德国、南斯拉夫、罗马尼亚四个国家的代表。蒙古、波兰两国的代表也将于最近离开中国。
