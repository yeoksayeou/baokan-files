Title: China-Myanmar Friendship Association Holds Reception to Welcome Wu Min Deng; Zhou Enlai, He Long, Dong Biwu and Others Attend
Author:
Date: 1957-09-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 28th. The China-Myanmar Friendship Association held a reception this evening to welcome the Myanmar friendship delegation headed by Wu Min Deng, Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of the Union of Burma.
    Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu attended the reception.
    Professor Jian Bozan, Director of the China-Myanmar Friendship Association, and Wu Min Deng exchanged speeches and toasts, unanimously congratulating the further development and consolidation of the kinship-like friendship between China and Myanmar. Wu Min Deng said that when he came to China, this friendly relative country, his stay in Beijing was just like still living in Yangon.
    The Myanmar Youth Delegation, Women's Delegation, Medical Delegation, and other Myanmar friends in Beijing were also invited to attend the reception tonight.
    During the reception, Premier Zhou and prominent figures from all walks of life in our country who had visited Myanmar toasted every Myanmar guest, and the guests and hosts chatted cordially.

### 中缅友协举行酒会欢迎吴敏登  周恩来、贺龙、董必武等出席了酒会

1957-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    中缅友协举行酒会欢迎吴敏登
    周恩来、贺龙、董必武等出席了酒会
    据新华社28日讯　中缅友好协会今晚举行酒会，欢迎以缅甸联邦最高法院首席法官吴敏登为首的缅甸友好代表团。
    周恩来总理、贺龙副总理和最高人民法院董必武院长出席了酒会。
    中缅友好协会理事翦伯赞教授和吴敏登互相致词祝酒，他们一致祝贺中缅两国亲戚般的友谊进一步发展和巩固。吴敏登说，当他来到中国这个友好的亲戚国家时，他停留在北京就好像仍然住在仰光一样。
    今晚，缅甸青年代表团、妇女代表团、医学代表团和其他在京的缅甸朋友们也应邀出席了酒会。
    席间，周总理和曾经到缅甸访问过的我国各界著名人士，向每一个缅甸客人都敬了酒，宾主亲切地交谈着。
