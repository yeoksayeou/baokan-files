Title: Guo Moruo Receives Honorary Academician Title from Romanian Academy of Sciences
Author:
Date: 1957-09-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd September. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, has been elected as an honorary academician of the Romanian Academy of Sciences. Romanian Ambassador to China, Chivu Stoica, presented the certificate of honorary academician to Guo Moruo today on behalf of the Romanian Academy of Sciences.

After receiving the certificate of honor, Guo Moruo said that his receiving the title and certificate of honorary academician from the Romanian Academy of Sciences was not only an honor for him personally, but also an honor for all Chinese scientists. He also asked Ambassador Chivu Stoica to convey his gratitude to the Romanian Academy of Sciences and all Romanian scientists.

### 郭沫若荣获罗科学院荣誉院士称号

1957-09-03
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若荣获罗科学院荣誉院士称号
    新华社2日讯  中国科学院院长郭沫若被罗马尼亚科学院选为荣誉院士。罗马尼亚驻中国大使乔洛尤今天代表罗马尼亚科学院把荣誉院士的证书授予郭沫若。
    郭沫若在接受了荣誉证书以后说，他能够得到罗马尼亚科学院荣誉院士的称号和证书，不仅仅是他个人的光荣，而且也是全体中国科学家的光荣。他并请乔洛尤大使向罗马尼亚科学院和全体罗马尼亚科学家转达他的谢意。
