Title: International Shooting Championships Open in Romania; Zhang Hong and Liu Jiaqiu Win Second Place Respectively
Author:
Date: 1957-09-24
Page: 4

    Sports News
    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, September 23rd: The Fourth International Shooting Championships opened in Bucharest on the 22nd. In the first day of competition, Chinese athletes Zhang Hong and Liu Jiaqiu won second place in the slow-fire small-bore pistol and prone large-bore rifle events, respectively.

    The events held on this day included: slow-fire small-bore pistol, prone large-bore rifle, and skeet shooting. Chinese athletes participated in the first two events.

    In the slow-fire small-bore pistol competition, Zhang Hong achieved second place with a score of 553 rings. The champion was Romanian athlete Iuliu.

    In the prone large-bore rifle competition, Liu Jiaqiu achieved second place with a score of 388 rings. The champion was Poland's Taduuli, whose score was 390 rings.

    Shooters from China, Romania, Bulgaria, Poland, Hungary, Yugoslavia, and Italy are participating in the Fourth International Shooting Championships. The competition will continue for five days.

### 国际射击锦标赛在罗开幕  张鈜、刘佳秋分别获得第二名

1957-09-24
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    国际射击锦标赛在罗开幕
    张鈜、刘佳秋分别获得第二名
    据新华社布加勒斯特23日电第四届国际射击锦标赛22日在布加勒斯特开幕。中国选手张鈜和刘佳秋，在第一天的比赛中分别获得了小口径手枪慢射和大口径步枪卧射比赛的第二名。
    这一天举行的比赛项目有：小口径手枪慢射、大口径步枪卧射和飞碟射击三项。中国选手参加了前两项比赛。
    在小口径手枪慢射比赛中，张鈜以五百五十三环的成绩取得了第二名。冠军是罗马尼亚选手尤利乌。
    在大口径步枪卧射比赛中，刘佳秋以三百八十八环的成绩获得第二名。冠军是波兰的塔杜乌利，他的成绩是三百九十环。
    参加第四届国际射击锦标赛的有中国、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、波兰、匈牙利、南斯拉夫和意大利七个国家的射击选手。比赛将连续举行五天。
