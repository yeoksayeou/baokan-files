Title: Typhoon to Land in the Xiamen Area
Author:
Date: 1957-09-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 14th: The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued an emergency typhoon warning at 7:00 PM today:

    The typhoon in the Pacific Ocean has entered the northeastern part of the South China Sea. At 2:00 PM today, the typhoon center was located approximately 150 kilometers southwest of Hengchun, Taiwan, at 21.6 degrees north latitude and 119.5 degrees east longitude. The maximum wind speed near the center is 50 meters per second, equivalent to level 12 or higher. Currently, the typhoon center is moving northwest at a speed of 8-10 knots per hour. It is expected that around noon on the 15th, the typhoon center will make landfall in the area between Xiamen and Shantou. After landfall, the typhoon's intensity will quickly weaken. The northeastern part of the South China Sea, the Taiwan Strait, and the land along the coast from Xiamen to Shantou will experience strong winds of level 10-12. The land along the coast from Fuzhou to Xiamen will experience strong winds of level 8-10. The coast from Shantou to the Pearl River Estuary and the central South China Sea will experience strong winds of level 6-8. Southern Fujian and eastern Guangdong will also experience heavy rain.

### 台风将在厦门地区登陆

1957-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    台风将在厦门地区登陆
    新华社14日讯  今天下午七时，中央气象科学研究所发布台风紧急警报如下：
    太平洋上的台风已经进入南海东北部。今天下午二时，台风中心位置在台湾恒春西南方约一百五十公里的海面上，就是北纬二十一点六度东经一百一十九点五度的地方。中心附近最大风速为每秒五十公尺，相当于十二级以上。目前台风中心正以每小时八—十浬的速度向西北方向移动。预计15日中午前后，台风中心将在厦门到汕头之间的地区登陆。登陆以后，台风强度将很快减弱，南海东北部、台湾海峡、厦门到汕头沿岸的陆地上将有十—十二级的大风，福州到厦门之间沿岸陆地上将有八—十级的大风，汕头到珠江口的沿岸和南海中部海面将有六—八级的大风，福建南部和广东东部并将有暴雨。
