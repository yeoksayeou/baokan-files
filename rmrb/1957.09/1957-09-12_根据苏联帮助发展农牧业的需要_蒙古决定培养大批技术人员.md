Title: To Meet the Needs of Soviet Assistance in Developing Agriculture and Animal Husbandry, Mongolia Decides to Train a Large Number of Technical Personnel
Author:
Date: 1957-09-12
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 11th - The Mongolian Council of Ministers and the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party recently adopted a resolution deciding to train 2,405 various technical personnel this year and next year, according to the needs of the Soviet Union in assisting Mongolia to develop agriculture and animal husbandry in the next three years.
	The resolution instructs the Ministry of Agriculture and Animal Husbandry to hold various training courses to train 1,127 people, including tractor drivers and agricultural machinery repair workers; instructs the Railway Administration to train 84 technical workers for power plants; and also requires the Ministry of Military Affairs and Public Security to train 600 automobile drivers from demobilized soldiers.
	The resolution instructs relevant departments to build school buildings in a timely manner, prepare all equipment, and supply materials.
	The training courses will hire agricultural and animal husbandry machinery engineers from the Soviet Union to teach, and purchase textbooks from the Soviet Union.

### 根据苏联帮助发展农牧业的需要  蒙古决定培养大批技术人员

1957-09-12
第6版()
专栏：

    根据苏联帮助发展农牧业的需要
    蒙古决定培养大批技术人员
    新华社乌兰巴托11日电　蒙古部长会议和蒙古人民革命党中央委员会最近通过决议，决定根据未来三年内苏联帮助蒙古发展农牧业的需要，在今年和明年内培养二千四百零五名各种技术人员。
    决议责成农牧业部举办各种训练班，培养拖拉机手和农业机器修理工人等一千一百二十七人；责成铁路管理局培养发电站技术工人八十四名；同时还要求军事和公安部从复员军人中培养六百名汽车司机。
    决议责成有关部门及时修建校舍，准备一切设备和供应物资。
    训练班将从苏联聘请农牧业机械工程师教课，并且从苏联购置教科书。
