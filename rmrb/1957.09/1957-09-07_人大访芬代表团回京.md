Title: NPC Delegation Returns to Beijing after Visiting Finland
Author:
Date: 1957-09-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th — The National People's Congress delegation returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon after concluding its visit to Finland.

　　Vice Chairman Chen Shutong, responsible persons from relevant units and people's organizations, and people from all walks of life, as well as the Finnish Ambassador to China, Sun Shidun, and others, welcomed them at the airport.

### 人大访芬代表团回京

1957-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    人大访芬代表团回京
    新华社6日讯　全国人民代表大会代表团结束了在芬兰的访问后，于今天下午乘飞机返回北京。
    陈叔通副委员长，各有关单位和各人民团体负责人以及各界人士，芬兰驻中国大使孙士敦等，都到机场欢迎。
