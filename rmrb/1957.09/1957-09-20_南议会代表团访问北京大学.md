Title: Yugoslav Parliamentary Delegation Visits Peking University
Author:
Date: 1957-09-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 19th: The Yugoslav Federal People's Assembly Delegation, headed by Chairman Stevan Stambolić, visited Peking University this afternoon.

　　When the distinguished guests arrived, accompanied by Vice-Chairman Li Weihan and others, Principal Ma Yinchu and more than a thousand teachers and students lined the streets in front of the school gate to welcome them.

　　Chairman Stambolić delivered a speech at the welcoming meeting of Peking University teachers and students. He first congratulated the young generation of the Chinese revolution, then conveyed the fraternal and warm greetings of Yugoslav university students to the Chinese youth, and introduced the life and struggle of Yugoslav youth.

　　After summarizing the history of Yugoslavia and the great achievements since the victory of the revolution, Stambolić said that Yugoslavia has always pursued a peaceful foreign policy and is willing to conduct friendly cooperation with all countries that are prepared to cooperate with it. He also said that Yugoslavia sincerely supports and hopes for closer cooperation with all socialist countries, which will help to defend world peace and promote positive international cooperation.

　　Chairman Stambolić's speech repeatedly won enthusiastic applause from the audience. At the beginning of the welcoming meeting, Principal Ma Yinchu delivered a speech on behalf of all teachers and students to welcome the distinguished guests.

　　This morning, the delegation was invited to attend the opening ceremony of the Indian Exhibition and visited the exhibition.

　　At noon, the distinguished guests toured the Temple of Heaven.

### 南议会代表团访问北京大学

1957-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　南议会代表团访问北京大学
　　据新华社19日讯　以斯坦鲍利奇主席为首的南斯拉夫联邦国民议会代表团今天下午访问北京大学。
　　当贵宾们由李维汉副委员长等陪同到达时，马寅初校长和一千多师生在校门前夹道欢迎。
　　斯坦鲍利奇团长在北京大学师生的欢迎会上发表了演说。他首先向中国革命的青年一代表示祝贺，接着转达了南斯拉夫大学生对中国青年的兄弟般的热烈问候，并介绍了南斯拉夫青年生活和斗争情况。
　　在概括地叙述了南斯拉夫的历史和革命胜利以来所获得的巨大成果后，斯坦鲍利奇说，南斯拉夫一贯奉行和平外交政策，愿意同所有准备与它合作的国家进行友好合作。他又说，南斯拉夫真诚支持和希望与所有社会主义国家更加紧密合作，这种合作必将有助于保卫世界和平和促进积极的国际合作。
　　斯坦鲍利奇团长的演说不断博得全场热烈的掌声。在欢迎会开始时，马寅初校长代表全体师生致词欢迎贵宾。
　　今天上午，代表团应邀参加了印度展览会的开幕典礼，并参观了展览会。
　　中午，贵宾们游览了天坛。
