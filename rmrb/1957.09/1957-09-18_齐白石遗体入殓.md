Title: Qi Baishi's Body Placed in Coffin

Author: 
Date: 1957-09-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: Mr. Qi Baishi's body was placed in a coffin at the Beijing Hospital at 2:00 PM today, after which the remains were moved to Jiaxing Temple. Present at the encoffining were Qi Yanming, Xia Yan, Tian Han, Yang Hansheng, Chen Banding, Wu Zuoren, Ye Qianyu, Liu Kaiqu, Cai Ruohong, Xu Beihong's widow Liao Jingwen, as well as Mr. Qi Baishi's family and many friends from his lifetime.

The Qi Baishi Funeral Committee held a meeting on the afternoon of the 17th, and decided that the time for all circles to pay their respects would be from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM on the 21st. The funeral procession will depart on the 22nd, and the burial will take place at the Hunan Cemetery in the western suburbs.

### 齐白石遗体入殓

1957-09-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　齐白石遗体入殓
    新华社17日讯　齐白石先生遗体今天下午二时在北京医院入殓，入殓后移灵嘉兴寺。入殓时在场的有齐燕铭、夏衍、田汉、阳翰笙、陈半丁、吴作人、叶浅予、刘开渠、蔡若虹和徐悲鸿夫人廖静文以及齐白石先生家属和生前友好多人。
    齐白石先生治丧委员会17日下午开会，定于21日上午八时至下午六时为各界祭奠时间，22日起灵，安葬于西郊湖南公墓。
