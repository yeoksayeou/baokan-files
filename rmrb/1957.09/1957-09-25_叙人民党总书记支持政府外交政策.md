Title: Syrian People's Party Secretary-General Supports Government's Foreign Policy
Author:
Date: 1957-09-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, September 24th: The Secretary-General of the Syrian People's Party, Dawalibi, said on the 23rd that the Syrian People's Party supports the Syrian government's current foreign policy of active neutrality.

In a written statement to the press, he said that the Syrian People's Party does not participate in the current government, not because it disagrees with the current government's foreign policy.

Dawalibi's statement was a response to US Secretary of State Dulles' speech at the United Nations General Assembly on September 19, in which Dulles attempted to sow discord among various political parties in Syria.

Dawalibi pointed out that the Syrian National Covenant already stipulates that Syria should adopt a policy of active neutrality. The People's Party signed this covenant.

### 叙人民党总书记支持政府外交政策

1957-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    叙人民党总书记支持政府外交政策
    新华社24日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚人民党总书记达瓦利比23日说，叙利亚人民党支持叙利亚现政府奉行的积极中立的外交政策。
    他在给报界的一个书面声明中说，叙利亚人民党不参加现政府，并不是由于不同意现政府奉行的外交政策。
    达瓦利比的声明是对美国国务卿杜勒斯9月19日在联合国大会上所作的发言的一个回击，杜勒斯在他的发言中企图挑拨叙利亚国内各党派的关系。
    达瓦利比指出，叙利亚国民公约中已经规定叙利亚要采取积极中立政策。人民党是在这个公约上签了字的。
