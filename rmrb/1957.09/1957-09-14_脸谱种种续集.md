Title: More Portraits of Faces
Author: Sha Ou, Fang Cheng
Date: 1957-09-14
Page: 8

　　More Portraits of Faces
　　Sha Ou (poetry) Fang Cheng (drawing)
　　Feng Xuefeng
　　Evidence of Anti-Party Crime
　　Like bronze cast
　　All denials are in vain
　　He concealed himself
　　Using "individual farming"
　　Dug a trench
　　Hurriedly using "ideological self-criticism"
　　As a floating white smokescreen
　　To block our vision
　　History is speaking
　　More than twenty years ago
　　He drew his sword to declare war on the Party…
　　And today
　　That Ding-Chen clique, like a wolf's den
　　Has a seat for him
　　Listening to his directions
　　Where is the "individual farming"?
　　Be honest
　　Don't hide in the trenches
　　Pretending to gently brush off dust
　　In this final moment
　　Before the Party
　　Ai Qing
　　No matter to whom
　　No matter the place
　　He uses grievances and indignation
　　To decorate his language
　　"Within the Party, the division of labor is clear
　　A group of people rectify others
　　A group of people are rectified..."
　　Followed by a long sigh
　　Could it be that our Party
　　Is a patched-up sectarian clique?
　　The black and white of life are so reversed
　　The Party's care
　　He calls it a cruel blow
　　The Party's warmth
　　He treats it as snowy and icy weather
　　Without a common language
　　He demands the Party to hold him high
　　Like a Bodhisattva, untouchable
　　He is obsessed with "international reputation"
　　Regardless of how sordid the soul is
　　He wants to wear an eternal laurel
　　The great Party in his eyes
　　Is just a pair of leather shoes
　　Or an antique
　　But he, towards ambitious individuals
　　Smiles gleefully
　　So intimate, so sweet
　　He runs between
　　Several anti-Party cliques
　　Like a telephone switchboard
　　Connecting this line to that line
　　Li Youran
　　When the rightists attack us
　　When the dark clouds cover the night
　　His eyes are also full of joy
　　Thinking that the sky is about to change
　　He has long hated the Party
　　Although he is still a Party member
　　He uses carefully selected words
　　To cover that tumor-like heart
　　Dreaming of becoming a "big shot"
　　Loyal to the Ding-Chen anti-Party clique
　　He shouts: I want to kill people
　　Without concealing his ferocious face
　　This servant of capital
　　Actually recalls affectionately
　　In France, in Shanghai
　　The Trotskyist ringleaders
　　Brought him spring
　　His estimates were too optimistic
　　The great wave of anti-rightism swept him away
　　Look at him facing the microphone
　　His face red, trembling
　　In our enraged storm
　　Decades of reactionary history
　　Were all exposed…
　　Chen Ming
　　You collected intelligence for her
　　You painstakingly offered advice and drafted plans
　　For her anti-Party conspiracy
　　You contacted people for her
　　Making phone calls with the voice of a young girl
　　Shamelessly calling "Uncle"
　　You made a pact of mutual defense for her
　　Squatting under roadside bushes
　　Sneaking around like a little spy
　　You for her retreat
　　Like a fake medicine vendor
　　Lying at the Party group meeting
　　You for her—
　　Your Empress and wife
　　Betrayed the Party, sold out the Party
　　(Attached image)

### 脸谱种种续集
沙鸥  方成
1957-09-14
第8版()
专栏：

　　脸谱种种续集
　　沙鸥诗  方成画
　　冯雪峰
　　反党的罪证
　　像是青铜铸成
　　一切推脱都是徒然
　　他隐蔽自己
　　用“单干户”
　　挖了一道壕堑
　　急忙忙以“检查思想”
　　当飘浮的白色烟幕
　　来封锁我们的视线
　　历史却在发言
　　二十多年前
　　他提刀向党宣战……
　　而今天
　　那像狼窟似的丁陈集团
　　有他坐的交椅
　　听他的指点
　　哪里是“单干”
　　老老实实吧
　　不要躲在壕堑里
　　装着轻轻拍灰尘
　　在这最后的时刻
　　在党的面前
　　艾青
　　不论对谁
　　不管什么地点
　　他都用委屈和愤慨
　　装饰他的语言
　　“党内，分工明确
　　一批人整人
　　一批人挨整……”
　　接着是一声长叹
　　难道我们的党
　　是拼凑的宗派集团
　　生活的黑白被如此颠倒
　　党的爱护
　　他说是残酷打击
　　党的温暖
　　他当成雪地冰天
　　没有共同语言
　　竟要求党高高捧着他
　　像一尊菩萨不能侵犯
　　他迷恋“国际声誉”
　　不管灵魂多么龌龊
　　却想戴永恒的桂冠  
　　伟大的党在他眼中
　　不过是皮鞋一双
　　或古玩一件
　　而他，对野心家
　　却眉开眼笑
　　那么亲、那么甜
　　他奔跑在
　　几个反党集团之间
　　像一架电话总机
　　把这根线接上那根线
　　李又然
　　当右派进攻我们
　　当阴云密布的夜晚
　　他眼里也充满快乐
　　以为就要变天
　　党，他早就讨厌
　　虽然他还是一个党员
　　他用挑选的字句
　　盖着那颗恶瘤一样的心
　　幻想成为“大人物”
　　效忠丁陈反党集团
　　他喊着：我要杀人
　　毫不掩饰凶横的嘴脸
　　这位资本的奴仆
　　竟含情默默地回忆
　　在法国、在上海
　　托派的头子们
　　给他带来的春天
　　估计过于乐观了
　　反右派的大浪把他席卷
　　你看他对着麦克风
　　面红耳赤，抖抖颤颤
　　在我们激怒的风暴中
　　几十年反动的老底
　　全被揭穿……
　　陈明
　　你为她收集情报
　　你为她的反党阴谋
　　苦心地献策，起草
　　你为她联络人
　　打电话装小姑娘的声音
　　不知羞耻地叫“叔叔”
　　你为她订攻守同盟
　　蹲在街头的矮树下
　　鬼鬼祟祟像个小特务
　　你为她的退却
　　像一个卖假药的商人
　　在党组会上说谎
　　你为她——
　　你的女皇和妻子
　　背叛党，出卖党
　　（附图片）
