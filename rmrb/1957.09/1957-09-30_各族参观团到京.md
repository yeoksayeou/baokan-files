Title: Visiting Delegations from Various Ethnic Groups Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: The visiting delegations of Tibet, the visiting delegations of various ethnic groups in Qinghai Province, the She ethnic group visiting delegation, the Manchu ethnic group visiting delegation, the visiting delegations of various ethnic groups in Yunnan Province, the visiting delegations of various ethnic groups in Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, the visiting delegations of various ethnic groups in Sichuan Province, the Tibetan cadre visiting delegation, the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region industrial visiting delegation, the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region education visiting delegation, the Yunnan Province minority Christian visiting delegation, the Tibetan youth visiting delegation, and the Guizhou Province youth visiting delegation of various ethnic groups, totaling more than 700 people, arrived in Beijing separately before September 29. When the delegations arrived in Beijing, they were welcomed by the Nationalities Affairs Commission, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, the Bureau of Religious Affairs under the State Council, and relevant units.

The delegation members include Mongolian, Hui, Tibetan, Uygur, Miao, Yi, Manchu, Dai, Hani, Kirgiz, Tu, Lisu, Russian, Uzbek, Tajik, Tatar, Xibe, Nu, She, and other ethnic groups, totaling more than twenty.

### 各族参观团到京

1957-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    各族参观团到京
    据新华社29日讯　来京参加国庆节观礼的西藏参观团、青海省各民族参观团、畬族参观团、满族参观团、云南省各民族参观团、新疆维吾尔自治区各民族参观团、四川省各民族参观团、西藏干部参观团、新疆维吾尔自治区工业参观团、新疆维吾尔自治区教育参观团、云南省少数民族基督教参观团、西藏青年参观团和贵州省各民族青年参观团，共七百余人，已于29日前分别到达北京。各团到京时，受到民族事务委员会、共青团中央委员会、国务院宗教事务局和有关单位的欢迎。
    参观团团员包括蒙、回、藏、维吾尔、苗、彝、满、傣、哈尼、柯尔克孜、土、傈僳、俄罗斯、乌孜别克、塔吉克、塔塔尔、锡伯、怒、畬等二十多个民族成份。
