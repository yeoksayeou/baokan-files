Title: Vietnam and Laos Reach Agreement: Both Sides Withdraw Troops from Border Areas
Author:
Date: 1957-09-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 22nd: According to the Vietnam News Agency, the delegations of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Kingdom of Laos have agreed that both sides will withdraw their armed forces from the Muong Xu and Nam Khan areas on their common border.

The commander of the Vietnam People's Army garrison in Hanoi held a banquet on the evening of the 21st to entertain the delegation from the Kingdom of Laos. In his speech at the banquet, the commander expressed the wish that the friendship between Vietnam and Laos, two neighboring countries, would be increasingly strengthened, and hoped that the current border issue would achieve further success.

Thong Savath, chief representative of the delegation from the Kingdom of Laos, said that this first achievement of the current negotiations is a goal of Laos.

The delegation from the Kingdom of Laos, led by Thong Savath, arrived in Hanoi on September 14th for negotiations to determine the boundaries of the Muong Xu and Nam Khan areas between the two sides.

### 越南和老挝达成协议  双方从边界地区撤军

1957-09-23
第6版()
专栏：

    越南和老挝达成协议
    双方从边界地区撤军
    新华社河内22日电　据越南通讯社报道，越南民主共和国和老挝王国两国代表团已经同意双方从在两国边界的芒苏和南堪地区撤退武装部队。
    越南人民军驻河内部队的司令官21日晚举行宴会招待老挝王国代表团。司令官在宴会上致词表示越南和老挝两个邻国的友谊将日益加强的愿望，并且希望目前进行的边界问题将获得进一步的成就。
    老挝王国代表团首席代表通沙瓦说，目前举行的谈判所取得的这个第一个成果，是老挝的一个目标。
    通沙瓦率领的老挝王国代表团是为了进行确定芒苏和南堪地区的双方边界问题的谈判在9月14日到达河内的。
