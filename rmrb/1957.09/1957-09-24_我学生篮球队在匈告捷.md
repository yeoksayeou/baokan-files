Title: Our Student Basketball Team Achieves Victory in Hungary
Author:
Date: 1957-09-24
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 23rd - The Chinese student men's basketball team played a friendly match with the representative team of Miskolc, Hungary (one of Hungary's excellent teams) on the 21st, and the Chinese team won with a score of 79 to 50.

　　The Chinese student men's basketball team came to Hungary on September 19th for a friendly visit after participating in the sports meeting in Paris celebrating the 50th anniversary of the founding of the French National Student Union. They will stay in Hungary for a week.

### 我学生篮球队在匈告捷

1957-09-24
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    我学生篮球队在匈告捷
    新华社布达佩斯23日电　中国学生男子篮球队，21日在匈牙利密斯科尔兹和这个市的代表队（匈牙利的优秀队之一）进行了一场友谊赛，结果中国队以七十九比五十获胜。
    中国学生男子篮球队是在巴黎参加庆祝法国全国学生联合会成立五十周年运动会后，应邀于9月19日来匈牙利进行访问比赛的。他们将在匈牙利逗留一周。
