Title: Dr. Hatta Visits Premier Zhou, Premier Zhou Hosts a Banquet for the Distinguished Guest
Author:
Date: 1957-09-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd: Former Indonesian Vice President Dr. Hatta, accompanied by his wife and entourage, paid a visit to Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China at 6:00 PM today.

Present during the meeting were Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, Vice Premier Li Fuchun, President of the Chinese-Indonesian Friendship Association Burhan Shahidi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Qu Wu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Director of the Asian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wenjin, and Acting Director of the Protocol Department Wang Yutian.

Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd: Premier Zhou Enlai and his wife hosted an informal dinner tonight in honor of former Indonesian Vice President Dr. Hatta and his wife.

Also invited to attend were Director of the American Affairs Department of the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs Mononutu, and Indonesian Ambassador to China Wilopo and his wife.

Present to accompany them were Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu.

### 哈达博士拜会周总理  周总理设便宴招待贵宾

1957-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    哈达博士拜会周总理
    周总理设便宴招待贵宾
    新华社22日讯　印度尼西亚前副总统哈达博士偕夫人和随行人员，在今天下午六时拜会了中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来。
    拜会时在座的，有陈毅副总理和夫人，李富春副总理，中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长包尔汉，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长屈武，外交部副部长章汉夫、亚洲司司长章文晋和礼宾司代司长王雨田。
    新华社22日讯　周恩来总理和夫人今晚举行便宴，招待印度尼西亚前副总统哈达博士和夫人。
    应邀出席的，还有印度尼西亚外交部美洲司司长莫诺努图，印度尼西亚驻华大使维约普拉诺托和夫人。
    出席作陪的，有陈毅副总理和夫人，外交部副部长章汉夫。
