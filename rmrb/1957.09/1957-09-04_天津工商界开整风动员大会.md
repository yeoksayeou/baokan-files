Title: Tianjin Industrial and Commercial Circles Hold Rectification Mobilization Meeting
Author:
Date: 1957-09-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, September 3rd: The rectification movement in Tianjin's industrial and commercial circles has officially begun. Today, the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association and the Tianjin Federation of Industry and Commerce jointly held a mobilization meeting. More than 2,000 key members of the city's industrial and commercial circles attended.

At the meeting, Wang Guangying, Vice Chairman of the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association and the Tianjin Federation of Industry and Commerce, gave a report.

Currently, key members of the industrial and commercial circles in six districts of the city have begun a rectification movement that initially focuses on the struggle against rightists. They are currently engaged in reasoned struggle against rightists Liu Bangzhou, Wang Lühe, Shang Yushan, Rong Zizheng, and 9 others. Following today's meeting, leading cadres of the industrial and commercial circles in all districts of the city will universally launch this movement.

### 天津工商界开整风动员大会

1957-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    天津工商界开整风动员大会
    据新华社天津3日电　天津市工商界的整风运动已经正式开始。今天，民建天津市委会和天津市工商联联合举行动员大会。到会的有全市工商界骨干分子二千多人。
    会上，民建天津市委会和市工商联副主任委员王光英作了报告。
  目前，全市已有六个区的工商界骨干分子，开始了先以反对右派斗争为内容的整风运动，他们正在对右派分子刘邦周、王履和、尚玉山、荣子正等十三人进行说理斗争。在今天的会议以后，全市各区工商界领导骨干都将普遍开展这一运动。
