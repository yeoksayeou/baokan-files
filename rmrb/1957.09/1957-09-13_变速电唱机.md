Title: Variable-Speed Record Player
Author:
Date: 1957-09-13
Page: 5

　　Variable-Speed Record Player
    The variable-speed record player, trial-produced by the jointly owned Rong Sheng Electrical Appliance Cooperative, has begun small-scale production. It can play ordinary records with 78 revolutions per minute, as well as slow-speed microgroove records with 45 or 33 revolutions per minute.

### 变速电唱机

1957-09-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　变速电唱机
    公私合营荣生电工社试制成的变速电唱机已开始小批生产。它可以放每分钟七十八转的普通唱片，也可以放每分钟四十五转或三十三转的慢转密纹唱片。
