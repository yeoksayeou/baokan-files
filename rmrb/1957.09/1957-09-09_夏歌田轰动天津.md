Title: Xia Getian Sensation in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1957-09-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 8th. The deeds of the handcraft worker Xia Getian, who caused a sensation in Tianjin, are on display in the handicraft hall of the Increase Production and Practice Economy Exhibition, which opened today. Xia Getian broke the conservative tailoring method of "seven-foot pants, eight-foot jacket" and used fourteen feet of blue wool cloth to tailor two pairs of Western-style pants and a uniform jacket.

### 夏歌田轰动天津

1957-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    夏歌田轰动天津
    据新华社天津8日电　在今天开幕的增产节约展览会的手工业馆里，展出了轰动天津的手工业工人夏歌田的事迹。夏歌田打破了“七尺裤子、八尺袄”的保守裁剪法，用十四尺毛蓝布裁了两条西服裤和一件制服上衣。
