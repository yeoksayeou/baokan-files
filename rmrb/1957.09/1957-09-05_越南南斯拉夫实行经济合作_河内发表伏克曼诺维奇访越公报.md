Title: Vietnam and Yugoslavia Implement Economic Cooperation; Hanoi Issues Communiqué on Vukmanović's Visit to Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1957-09-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 4th September: The Prime Minister's Office of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam today released a communiqué regarding the visit of Yugoslav Federal Executive Council Vice President Vukmanović to Vietnam.

The communiqué states that during his stay in Vietnam, Comrade Vukmanović and his entourage were received by President Ho Chi Minh, participated in the National Day celebrations of Vietnam, and held talks with Prime Minister Pham Van Dong and other members of the Vietnamese government.

The communiqué states that Vice President Vukmanović and his entourage emphasized that the people and government of Yugoslavia are very pleased to see that the people and government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam respect and correctly implement the Geneva Accords. The people and government of Yugoslavia hope that the Vietnamese people's struggle for the peaceful and democratic reunification of the country will be victorious.

The communiqué states that on September 3, 1957, government officials of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held talks in Hanoi with Vice President Vukmanović and his entourage on issues of economic and technical cooperation. The two sides exchanged views on the economic situations of the two countries and expressed satisfaction with the achievements of the two countries in building and developing agriculture, industry, and trade.

The two sides agreed that, for the benefit of developing friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries, economic cooperation between Vietnam and Yugoslavia must be implemented. Both sides will send commissioners to jointly consult on these issues. The communiqué concludes by saying that the friendly visit of Vice President Vukmanović and his entourage to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has contributed to the development of fraternal cooperation between the peoples of the two countries and to the consolidation of peace in Asia and the world.

Vukmanović left Hanoi for Tokyo on the 4th and will visit our country in mid-September.

### 越南南斯拉夫实行经济合作  河内发表伏克曼诺维奇访越公报

1957-09-05
第6版()
专栏：

    越南南斯拉夫实行经济合作
    河内发表伏克曼诺维奇访越公报
    新华社河内4日电　越南民主共和国总理府今天发表了关于南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席伏克曼诺维奇在越南访问的公报。
    公报说，在越南逗留的期间，伏克曼诺维奇同志和他的随行人员受到胡志明主席的接见，参加了越南国庆节典礼，并且同范文同总理和越南政府其他人员进行了会谈。
    公报说，伏克曼诺维奇副主席和随行人员强调说，南斯拉夫人民和政府非常满意地看到，越南民主共和国人民和政府尊重并且正确履行了日内瓦协议。南斯拉夫人民和政府希望越南人民以和平、民主方式统一国家的斗争获得胜利。
    公报说，1957年9月3日，越南民主共和国政府人员同伏克曼诺维奇副主席以及随行人员曾经在河内就经济和技术合作问题进行会谈。双方已经就两国的经济情况交换了意见，并且对两国建设和发展农业、工业和贸易的成就表示满意。
    双方一致认为，为了发展两国之间的友好合作关系的利益，必须实行越南和南斯拉夫之间的经济合作。双方将派遣专员，以便就这些问题共同进行协商。公报最后说，伏克曼诺维奇副主席和随行人员到越南民主共和国的友好访问，已经为发展两国人民之间的兄弟般的合作关系作出了贡献，为巩固亚洲和世界和平作出了贡献。
    伏克曼诺维奇4日离开河内赴东京，本月中旬将来我国访问。
