Title: East German Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Vice Premier Bo Yibo Invites Distinguished Guests to Dinner
Author:
Date: 1957-09-21
Page: 1

    East German Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Vice Premier Bo Yibo Invites Distinguished Guests to Dinner
    Xinhua News Agency, September 20th News: The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, consisting of four people and led by Deputy Prime Minister Ölsner of the German Democratic Republic, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of our government.
    Those who welcomed them at the airport included Vice Premier of the State Council Bo Yibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building Wang Daohan, and Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, among others.
    The delegation will participate in our National Day celebration ceremonies.
    Xinhua News Agency, September 20th News: Vice Premier Bo Yibo received all members of the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Deputy Prime Minister Ölsner, this afternoon.
    Minister of the Second Ministry of Machine Building Zhao Erlu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Luo Chengde were also present at the reception.
    That evening, Vice Premier Bo Yibo invited Deputy Prime Minister Ölsner and other members of the delegation to dinner.

### 民主德国政府代表团到京  薄一波副总理邀请贵宾共进晚餐

1957-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    民主德国政府代表团到京
    薄一波副总理邀请贵宾共进晚餐
    新华社20日讯　应我国政府邀请来华访问的德意志民主共和国政府代表团一行四人，在德意志民主共和国副总理厄斯纳率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有国务院副总理薄一波，外交部副部长姬鹏飞、第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵和对外贸易部部长叶季壮等人。
    代表团将参加我国国庆节庆祝典礼。
    新华社20日讯　薄一波副总理今天下午接见了以厄斯纳副总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团全体人员。
    接见时，第二机械工业部部长赵尔陆、外交部副部长姬鹏飞、对外贸易部副部长罗成德等也在座。
    当日晚，薄一波副总理邀请厄斯纳副总理及代表团其他人员共进晚餐。
