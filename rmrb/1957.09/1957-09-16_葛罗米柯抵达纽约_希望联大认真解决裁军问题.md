Title: Gromyko Arrives in New York Hoping the UN General Assembly Will Seriously Address Disarmament Issues
Author:
Date: 1957-09-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York Dispatch via TASS: Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko arrived at McGair Airport on September 14 aboard a "Tu-104" jet airliner. He is attending the 12th session of the United Nations General Assembly as the head of the Soviet delegation.

Gromyko said in a statement at the airport: "The 12th session of the United Nations General Assembly will consider a number of international issues of great significance for maintaining and strengthening peace and developing international cooperation. The correct resolution of these issues will enhance the prestige of the United Nations."

Gromyko said: "The first issue is disarmament."

"The Soviet delegation will strive to work for disarmament and the resolution of other international issues. We will put forward our own proposals on these issues. We hope that everyone will make efforts to earnestly try to solve these problems."

Also arriving on the same plane were the deputy head of the Soviet delegation to this session of the General Assembly, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Kuznetsov, the deputy representatives of the delegation – Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Uzbek Republic Gani, Deputy Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Moscow City Soviet Mironova, and member of the Collegium of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs Podtserob.

The delegations of the Ukrainian Republic and the Belorussian Republic to the 12th session of the United Nations General Assembly, headed by the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Ukrainian Republic, Palamarchuk, and the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Belorussian Republic, Kiselyov, also arrived on the same plane.

### 葛罗米柯抵达纽约  希望联大认真解决裁军问题

1957-09-16
第6版()
专栏：

　　葛罗米柯抵达纽约
　　希望联大认真解决裁军问题
    据新华社15日讯  据塔斯社纽约讯：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯9月14日乘“图——一○四”喷气客机到达麦盖尔机场。他是作为苏联代表团团长来参加联合国大会第十二届会议的。
    葛罗米柯在机场发表谈话说：“联合国大会第十二届会议将考虑对保持与加强和平和发展国际合作具有重要意义的一些国际问题。这些问题的正确解决将会提高联合国的威望”。
    葛罗米柯说：“头一个问题就是裁军。”
    “苏联代表团将竭力为裁军和解决其他国际问题而工作。关于这些问题，我们将提出我们自己的建议。我们希望大家都努力认真地设法解决这些问题”。
    同机到达的还有出席这一届大会的苏联代表团副团长、外交部第一副部长库兹涅佐夫，代表团副代表——乌兹别克共和国外交部长甘尼、莫斯科市执行委员会副主席米罗诺娃和苏联外交部部务委员会委员波德采罗布。
    以乌克兰共和国外交部长帕拉马尔楚克和白俄罗斯共和国外交部长基谢寥夫为首的出席联合国大会第十二届会议的乌克兰共和国代表团和白俄罗斯共和国代表团也同机到达。
