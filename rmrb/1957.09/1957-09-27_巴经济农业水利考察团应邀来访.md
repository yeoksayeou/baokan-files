Title: Pakistani Economic, Agricultural, and Water Conservancy Delegation Arrives for Visit
Author:
Date: 1957-09-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 26th: A six-member Pakistani Economic, Agricultural, and Water Conservancy Delegation arrived in Guangzhou this afternoon.

　　The delegation is visiting at the invitation of the Ministry of Agriculture of our country. The head of the delegation is Syed Hassan, Deputy Chairman of the Pakistan Planning Commission.

　　Tonight, Jia Bolin, Deputy Director of the Guangdong Provincial Department of Agriculture, hosted a banquet for the guests.

　　The delegation is scheduled to leave for Beijing tomorrow morning.

### 巴经济农业水利考察团应邀来访

1957-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    巴经济农业水利考察团应邀来访
    新华社广州26日电　巴基斯坦经济农业水利考察团一行六人，今天下午到达广州。
    考察团是应我国农业部的邀请前来进行访问的，考察团的团长是巴基斯坦计划委员会副主席赛义德·哈桑。
    今晚，广东省农业厅副厅长贾柏林设宴招待客人。
    考察团定明晨赴北京。
