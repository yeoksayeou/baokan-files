Title: Joint Statement of the Bulgarian and Mongolian Governments' Talks: Both Sides Will Further Develop Comprehensive Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1957-09-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 5th: The Bulgarian newspapers published on August 4th the joint statement of the delegations of the Bulgarian and Mongolian governments’ talks.

The joint statement pointed out that during the visit of the Mongolian government delegation to Bulgaria, the Bulgarian and Mongolian government delegations held talks in a sincere and friendly atmosphere.

The talks showed that the two governments' estimations of the issues discussed were completely consistent. During the talks, the two sides discussed issues related to further developing the friendship and cooperation between Bulgaria and Mongolia. Both sides noted with satisfaction that for the benefit of the two peoples, the fraternal friendship and close cooperation between the two countries will be strengthened and developed day by day. Bulgaria and Mongolia will continue to develop and strengthen the economic and cultural ties between the two countries and provide each other with necessary assistance in peaceful construction.

For this purpose, the two sides decided to conclude a goods exchange agreement between Bulgaria and Mongolia for the period of 1958-1960 in the near future.

As a result of the talks, the government delegations of the two countries also signed an agreement on cultural cooperation between Bulgaria and Mongolia.

Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Mongolia, Tsedenbal, on behalf of the government of the Mongolian People's Republic, invited Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria, Yugov, along with other Bulgarian leaders, to visit Mongolia.

Yugov has accepted this invitation.

### 保蒙两国政府会谈联合声明  双方将进一步发展全面合作

1957-09-06
第6版()
专栏：

    保蒙两国政府会谈联合声明
    双方将进一步发展全面合作
    据新华社索非亚5日电　保加利亚报纸8月4日刊载了保加利亚和蒙古两国政府代表团会谈的联合声明。
    联合声明指出，在蒙古政府代表团访问保加利亚期间，保蒙两国政府代表团在真诚友好的气氛中举行了会谈。
    会谈表明，两国政府对所讨论的问题的估计是完全一致的。会谈期间，双方讨论了有关进一步发展保蒙两国之间的友谊和合作的问题。双方满意地指出，为了两国人民的利益，两国的兄弟友谊和紧密合作将日益加强和发展。保蒙两国将继续发展和加强两国之间的经济和文化联系，并且在和平建设方面，彼此给予必要的帮助。
    为了这个目的，双方决定在最近的将来缔结关于1958—1960年期间保蒙两国的换货协定。
    会谈结果，两国政府代表团还签订了关于保蒙两国的文化合作协定
    蒙古部长会议主席泽登巴尔代表蒙古人民共和国政府邀请保加利亚部长会议主席于哥夫同保加利亚其他领导人一道访问蒙古。
    于哥夫已经接受了这个邀请。
