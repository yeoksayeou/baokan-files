Title: Premier Zhou Receives Yugoslav Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-09-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the delegation of the Federal National Assembly of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, headed by the President of the Yugoslav Federal National Assembly, Petar Stambolić, this evening. Present at the reception were Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Wu Xinyu, Deputy Director of the Legislative Affairs Commission of the National People's Congress. The Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popović, was also present at the reception.

### 周总理接见南议会代表团

1957-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见南议会代表团
    新华社17日讯　周恩来总理今天晚上接见了以南斯拉夫联邦国民议会主席彼得·斯坦鲍利奇为首的南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国联邦国民议会代表团。接见时在座的，有国务院秘书长习仲勋、外交部副部长张闻天和全国人民代表大会法案委员会副主任委员武新宇。接见时，南斯拉夫驻中国大使波波维奇也在座。
