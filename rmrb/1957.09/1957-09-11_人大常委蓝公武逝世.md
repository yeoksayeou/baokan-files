Title: NPC Standing Committee Member Lan Gongwu Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1957-09-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Lan Gongwu, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, passed away at 8 p.m. on the 9th in Beijing.

The relevant departments have formed the Funeral Committee for Comrade Lan Gongwu, with the following members:

Lin Boqu, Xi Zhongxun, Shi Liang, Rong Zihe, Chi Long, Liu Renshu, Liu Lantao, Wu Gaizhi, Li Liuru, Li Shuzhao, Shen Junru, Xing Xiping, Chen Shutong, Lin Zaiping, Lin Tie, Luo Ronghuan, Luo Ruiqing, Nan Hanchen, Duan Suquan, Qin Linshu, Gao Kelin, Zhang Zhirang, Zhang Su, Tao Xijin, Peng Zhen, Huang Yanpei, Yang Xiufeng, Dong Biwu, Lei Renmin, Bo Yibo, Xie Juezai, Tan Biao.

### 人大常委蓝公武逝世

1957-09-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　人大常委蓝公武逝世
    新华社讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员蓝公武于9日下午八时在北京逝世。
    有关方面已经组成蓝公武委员治丧委员会，名单如下：
    林伯渠、习仲勋、史良、戎子和、池龙、刘仁树、刘澜涛、吴溉之、李六如、李树藻、沈钧儒、邢西萍、陈叔通、林宰平、林铁、罗荣桓、罗瑞卿、南汉宸、段苏权、秦林舒、高克林、张志让、张苏、陶希晋、彭真、黄炎培、杨秀峰、董必武、雷任民、薄一波、谢觉哉、谭彪。
