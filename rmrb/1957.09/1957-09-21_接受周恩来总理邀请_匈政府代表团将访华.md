Title: Hungarian Government Delegation to Visit China at Premier Zhou Enlai's Invitation
Author:
Date: 1957-09-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th – The State Council Premier of the People's Republic of China, Comrade Zhou Enlai, on behalf of the Chinese government, invited a Hungarian government delegation to visit China. The Hungarian government has informed the Chinese government that it has gladly accepted this invitation.

### 接受周恩来总理邀请  匈政府代表团将访华

1957-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    接受周恩来总理邀请
    匈政府代表团将访华
    新华社20日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志代表中国政府邀请了匈牙利政府代表团到中国访问。匈牙利政府已经通知中国政府，它愉快地接受了这项邀请。
