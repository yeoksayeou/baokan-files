Title: Soviet Fleet Visits Latakia
Author: Jin Yinhua
Date: 1957-09-25
Page: 8

    Soviet Fleet Visits Latakia
    Jin Yinhua
    Giants sail towards Latakia,
    Snow-white doves fly from the floral wreath towards the shore,
    Like shimmering silver stars
    Radiating above the crowds, breaking through the dark clouds in their hearts.
    Giants sail towards Latakia,
    A huge wave of cheers rises on the sea;
    Heroic Syria, at the forefront of defending independence,
    Welcomes its friends, welcomes peace.
    Giants sail towards Latakia,
    American war provocateurs tremble with fear,
    Retreating is impossible, advancing is not an option,
    Embarrassed and flustered, only because the Soviet Union is for the people.
    Welcome, giants sailing towards Latakia!
    Your beautiful fleet, red banners,
    You serenely brave the wind and waves,
    Accepting the entrustment of the whole world, comforting all peace-loving hearts.

### 苏联舰队访问拉塔基亚
金银花
1957-09-25
第8版()
专栏：

    苏联舰队访问拉塔基亚
    金银花
    巨人向拉塔基亚航行，
    雪白的乳鸽从花冠上飞向海岸，
    像闪耀的银星
    放射在人群头上，冲破人们心上的乌云。
    巨人向拉塔基亚航行，
    海上卷起了万众欢呼的巨浪；
    英雄的叙利亚在保卫独立的最前线
    迎接自己的朋友，迎接和平。
    巨人向拉塔基亚航行，
    美国战争挑衅者胆战心惊，
    他后退不肯，前进又不能，
    狼狈万状，只因苏联是为了人民。
    欢迎啊，巨人向拉塔基亚航行！
    你美丽的舰队，红色的旌旗，
    你安详地冲风破浪，
    接受全世界的委托，抚慰一切爱和平的心。
