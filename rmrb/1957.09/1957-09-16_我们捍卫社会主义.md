Title: We Defend Socialism
Author: Han Yiping
Date: 1957-09-16
Page: 5

We Defend Socialism
Beijing No. 1 State Cotton Factory Fitter Han Yiping
　　…………
　　We workers cannot tolerate it
　　Although some rightists have already raised the white flag
　　We must pursue them relentlessly
　　To thoroughly defeat them
　　We can no longer return to
　　That era of enslavement
　　We can no longer allow dockworkers to endure whippings
　　We can no longer allow coal miners to be buried alive in the pits
　　We can no longer allow the sisters of textile factories to be humiliated
　　We can no longer allow the land of China
　　To be branded with the mark of the USA
　　Cannot! A hundred cannots!…………
　　Dear Party
　　The electrical workers erecting high-voltage power lines on iron towers
　　Are looking at you
　　The seafarers heading out to sea in the wind
　　Are looking at you
　　The street cleaners stepping onto the streets in the first rays of sunlight
　　Are looking at you
　　The printing workers waiting before the printing presses for dawn
　　Are looking at you
　　The weaving women accelerating their pace
　　Patrolling beside the machines
　　Are looking at you
　　The iron smelters wearing iron helmets, busily working in the smoke and fire
　　Are looking at you
　　The drilling workers manipulating and swinging the drill handles on the high mountains
　　Are looking at you
　　The pneumatic drill operators with faces blackened beneath the earth's surface
　　Are looking at you with bright eyes…
　　All of us workers are looking at you
　　We must defend socialism—
　　We must bury the shameless conspiracies in the grave.

### 我们捍卫社会主义
韩忆萍
1957-09-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　我们捍卫社会主义
　　北京国棉一厂钳工  韩忆萍
　　…………
　　我们工人不能容忍
　　虽然有几个右派已举起白旗
　　但我们还要穷追猛打下去
　　要彻底击溃他们
　　我们不能再回到
    那被奴役的时代
　　不能再叫码头工人忍受鞭笞
　　不能再叫挖煤工人活埋在井底
　　不能再叫纺织厂的姐妹被污辱
　　不能再叫中国的土地
    烙上USA的标记
　　不能！一百个不能！…………
　　亲爱的党
　　那在铁塔上架设高压电线的
    电气工人望着您
　　那迎风出海的
    海员工人望着您
　　那披着第一缕阳光走上街头的
    清路工人望着您
　　那在印报机前守候着黎明的
    印刷工人望着您
　　那加快着脚步
    巡回在机器身边的
    织布女工望着您
　　那头戴铁盔奔忙在烟火里的
    化铁工人望着您
　　那在高山上操纵挥动的钻把的
    钻探工人望着您
　　地层下满脸漆黑的风镐手
    用雪亮的眼睛望着您……
　　我们所有的工人都望着您
　　我们要捍卫社会主义——
    就要把无耻的阴谋打入坟墓。
