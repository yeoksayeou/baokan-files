Title: Lebanese Film Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-02
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, September 1st: The Lebanese film delegation arrived in Beijing today. To date, film delegations from fourteen of the sixteen countries participating in "Asian Film Week" have arrived.
    The Lebanese film delegation consists of two people, including Georges Nasser, the director of the Lebanese film "To Where?"

### 黎巴嫩电影代表团到京

1957-09-02
第3版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩电影代表团到京
    据新华社1日讯  黎巴嫩电影代表团今天到达北京。至此，参加“亚洲电影周”的十六个国家中，有十四个国家的电影代表团来到了。
    黎巴嫩电影代表团共两人，其中有黎巴嫩影片“向何处去”的导演乔治·纳赛尔。
