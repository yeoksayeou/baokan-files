Title: Six Cabinet Members in Thailand Resign; Sixty-One Members of Parliament Withdraw from Ruling Party

Author:

Date: 1957-09-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, 12th: It was reported from Bangkok that on September 11th, another fifteen members of parliament from Thailand's Manangkasila Party collectively withdrew from this ruling party. With the addition of the forty-six members, including the Commander-in-Chief of the Army, Nai Sarit, who resigned as Minister of Defense in August, who had previously left the Manangkasila Party, the total number of parliamentarians who have withdrawn from the ruling party has reached sixty-one.

Army Marshal Nai Pinit resigned from his position as Minister of Agriculture on the 11th. Including Nai Sarit, a total of six cabinet members have resigned from their cabinet positions.

Most of the members of parliament who withdrew from the Manangkasila Party are army generals, with some also being navy and air force officers. They are all second-category members of parliament appointed by the King.

A spokesperson for the Manangkasila Party said that the members of parliament who recently withdrew had expressed their intention to remain neutral in parliament. The ruling party currently holds one hundred and forty-two seats in the two hundred and sixty-eight-seat parliament.

### 泰国已有六个阁员辞职  六十一名议员先后退出执政党

1957-09-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　泰国已有六个阁员辞职
　　六十一名议员先后退出执政党
    据新华社12日讯　曼谷消息：9月11日又有十五名泰国马兰卡西拉自由党的议会议员集体退出这个执政党。至此，加上前一天包括在8月间辞去国防部长职务的陆军总司令乃沙立等四十六名退出马兰卡西拉自由党的议员，退出执政党的议员共达六十一人。
    陆军元帅乃屏在11日辞去了农业部长的职务。包括乃沙立在内，辞去了内阁职务的共有六名阁员。
    退出马兰卡西拉自由党的议员大多数是陆军将领，也有一些是海军和空军的军官。他们都是由国王任命的第二类议员。
    马兰卡西拉自由党的一个发言人说，最近退党的议员曾经表示要在议会中保持中立。执政党目前在二百六十八席的议会中保持着一百四十二席。
