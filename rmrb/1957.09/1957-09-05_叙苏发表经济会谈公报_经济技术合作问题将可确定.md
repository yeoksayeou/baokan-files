Title: Syria and the Soviet Union Issue Joint Communique on Economic Talks; Economic and Technical Cooperation Issues to be Determined
Author:
Date: 1957-09-05
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 4th. TASS reports that Moscow released a communique on September 3rd regarding talks between the Syrian economic delegation and the State Committee for Foreign Economic Relations of the USSR Council of Ministers.

The communique pointed out that, during the talks, the two sides preliminarily discussed several issues related to the communique issued by the two countries on August 6th of this year. This concerns cooperation between the two sides in areas such as railway and highway construction, farmland irrigation, construction of power stations and other industrial equipment, and geological exploration.

The communique stated that the two sides agreed that a final meeting to determine the above-mentioned issues will be held in Syria, and a corresponding economic and technical cooperation agreement will be signed. To this end, the Soviet Union will soon send an economic delegation to visit Syria. The delegation will be led by N.S. Nikitin, Deputy Chairman of the State Committee for Foreign Economic Relations of the USSR Council of Ministers. The delegation will include experts from various economic sectors who reached an agreement in the negotiations.

The Syrian economic delegation arrived in Moscow on August 28th.

### 叙苏发表经济会谈公报  经济技术合作问题将可确定

1957-09-05
第7版()
专栏：

    叙苏发表经济会谈公报
    经济技术合作问题将可确定
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯，莫斯科在9月3日发表了叙利亚经济代表团和苏联部长会议国家对外经济关系委员会会谈的公报。
    公报指出，在会谈中，双方初步讨论了同两国今年8月6日发表的公报有关的若干问题。这就是双方在铁路和公路建设、农田灌溉、发电站和其他工业设备的建设以及地质勘探等方面进行合作的问题。
    公报说，双方协议：最后确定上述问题的会议将在叙利亚举行，并且将签订一项相应的经济技术合作协定。为了这一目的，苏联不久将派遣一个经济代表团访问叙利亚。代表团将由苏联部长会议国家对外关系委员会副主席尼基京率领。代表团人员将包括在这次谈判中达成协议的各个经济部门的专家。
    叙利亚经济代表团是在8月28日到莫斯科的。
