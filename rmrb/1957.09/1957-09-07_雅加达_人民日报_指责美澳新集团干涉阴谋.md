Title: Jakarta "People's Daily" Accuses ANZUS Group of Conspiracy to Interfere
Author:
Date: 1957-09-07
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 6th: According to a report in today's Jakarta "People's Daily," the upcoming fifth annual meeting of the ANZUS Council, which includes "the expansion of communist forces in Indonesia" on its agenda, is an interference containing a "Dulles odor."

The newspaper said that Indonesia has encountered this kind of interference more than once. The Manila Treaty aggressor group also included the Indonesian issue on its agenda, but it was strongly protested by Indonesia and other countries.

### 雅加达“人民日报”  指责美澳新集团干涉阴谋

1957-09-07
第7版()
专栏：

    雅加达“人民日报”
    指责美澳新集团干涉阴谋
    据新华社雅加达6日电　据雅加达“人民日报”今天报道，即将举行的美澳新理事会第五次年会把“共产主义势力在印度尼西亚的扩张”列为议程之一，是一种含有“杜勒斯气味”的干涉。
    这家报纸说，印度尼西亚碰到这种干涉已经不止一次。马尼拉条约侵略集团也曾经把印度尼西亚问题列入议程，但是遭到了印度尼西亚和其他国家的强烈抗议。
