Title: Yugoslav Parliamentary Delegation Visits Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1957-09-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, September 27th. The Yugoslav Parliamentary Delegation, headed by Stambolic, arrived in Shanghai by special plane this morning from Hangzhou for a visit.

Xu Jianguo, Vice Mayor of Shanghai, Song Richang, Rong Yiren, and more than 300 people from all walks of life welcomed the guests at the airport.

Accompanying the guests to Shanghai were Wu Xinyu, Deputy Director of the Legislative Affairs Commission of the National People's Congress, and Popovic, the Yugoslav Ambassador to China.

In the morning, the distinguished guests toured the city. In the afternoon, the delegation visited Shenxin No. 9 Factory, our country's largest joint state-private enterprise. The distinguished guests held a discussion with Rong Yiren on the socialist transformation of private enterprises with great interest. The distinguished guests also visited the workshops.

The workers of this factory held a mass meeting to welcome the delegation. Stambolic spoke amidst thunderous applause.

In the evening, Vice Mayor Xu Jianguo hosted a banquet to welcome Stambolic and all the guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, September 27th. The Yugoslav Federal National Assembly Delegation, headed by Peter Stambolic, Chairman of the Yugoslav Federal National Assembly, after arriving in Hangzhou on the 24th, toured scenic spots such as Three Pools Mirroring the Moon, Lingyin Temple, Yuquan, and Liuhe Pagoda, and visited the famous Dujinsheng Silk Weaving Factory, the Pingfengshan Sanatorium, and the Hangzhou Tea Factory. Chairman Stambolic praised the exquisite craftsmanship and skills of the workers at the Dujinsheng Silk Weaving Factory during his visit.

The distinguished guests also watched Zhejiang's folk songs, dances, and acrobatics in Hangzhou.

### 南议会代表团到上海参观

1957-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

    南议会代表团到上海参观
    据新华社上海27日电　以斯坦鲍利奇为首的南斯拉夫议会代表团，今天上午从杭州乘专机来上海访问。
    上海市副市长许建国、宋日昌、荣毅仁和各界人士三百多人在机场上欢迎客人。
    陪同客人来上海的是全国人民代表大会法案委员会副主任委员武新宇和南斯拉夫驻中国大使波波维奇。
    上午，贵宾们游览了市区。午后，代表团访问了我国最大的公私合营工厂——申新九厂。贵宾们以极大兴趣同荣毅仁就私营企业的社会主义改造问题进行了座谈。贵宾们还到车间参观。
    这个厂的工人们举行了群众大会来欢迎代表团。斯坦鲍利奇在雷动的掌声中讲了话。
    晚上，许建国副市长设宴欢迎斯坦鲍利奇和全体客人。
    据新华社杭州27日电　以南斯拉夫联邦国民议会主席彼得·斯坦鲍利奇为首的南斯拉夫联邦国民议会代表团，24日到达杭州后，游览了三潭印月、灵隐寺、玉泉、六和塔等名胜古迹，参观了著名的都锦生丝织厂、屏风山疗养院和杭州茶厂。斯坦鲍利奇主席在参观都锦生丝织厂时，对这个厂工人的绝妙手艺和技巧倍加赞扬。
    贵宾们在杭州还观看了浙江的民间歌舞杂技。
