Title: Ho Chi Minh Reports to the Vietnamese National Assembly: The Great Strength of the Unity of 900 Million People
Author:
Date: 1957-09-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 10th -- President Ho Chi Minh told cheering deputies at the opening session of the Seventh Congress of the Vietnamese National Assembly today that after his recent visits abroad, he more deeply saw the great power of Marxism-Leninism and more deeply felt the strength of the socialist camp.
    He said that the unity of 900 million people in the socialist countries is a tremendous force. With the support of this force, the Vietnamese people will surely be able to overcome difficulties, unify their country, and gradually move towards socialism.
    President Ho Chi Minh said that during this visit, he personally witnessed the great assistance of the Soviet Union to the fraternal countries and saw the great sacrifices the Soviet Union made in helping to liberate the fraternal countries.
    He said that the fraternal countries warmly welcomed the victories China had achieved in the socialist revolution and their creative application of Marxism-Leninism.

### 胡志明在越南国会报告  九亿人民团结力量大

1957-09-11
第6版()
专栏：

　　胡志明在越南国会报告
　　九亿人民团结力量大
    新华社河内10日电　胡志明主席今天在越南国会第七次会议的开幕式上向欢呼的议员们说，经过他最近的出国访问，他更加深切地看到马克思、列宁主义的伟大力量，更加深切地体会到社会主义阵营的强大。
    他说，社会主义国家九亿人民的团结是雄厚的力量，有了这种力量的支持，越南人民就一定能够克服困难，一定能够统一他们的国家和逐步走向社会主义。
    胡志明主席说，在这次访问中，他亲眼看到苏联对各兄弟国家的伟大援助，看到苏联在帮助解放各兄弟国家中付出了很大的牺牲。
    他说，各兄弟国家热烈欢迎中国在社会主义革命中取得的胜利和他们创造性地运用了马列主义。
