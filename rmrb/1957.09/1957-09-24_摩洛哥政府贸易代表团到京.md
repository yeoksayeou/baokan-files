Title: Moroccan Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-24
Page: 4

    Moroccan Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 23rd -- The Moroccan Government Trade Delegation, led by Mr. Benani, Head of the Economic Treaty Department of the Moroccan Ministry of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 摩洛哥政府贸易代表团到京

1957-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥政府贸易代表团到京
    新华社23日讯　摩洛哥政府贸易代表团在摩洛哥外交部经济条约司司长培拿尼率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
