Title: Austrian Communist Party Delegation Visits Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1957-09-14
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 12th: An Austrian Communist Party delegation arrived in Prague on September 10th at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party.

　　The delegation will learn about Czechoslovakia's socialist construction and the people's living conditions.

### 奥共代表团到捷访问

1957-09-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　奥共代表团到捷访问
    新华社布拉格12日电  奥地利共产党代表团应捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会的邀请，9月10日抵达布拉格。
    代表团将了解捷克斯洛伐克社会主义建设的问题和人民的生活情况。
