Tianjin Holds Workers' Bicycle Race

(Our dispatch) The Tianjin Sixth Workers' Sports Meet bicycle competition was held on the 14th and 15th along the Jincheng Highway in the Zhanzhuangzi area. More than 250 male and female athletes from 26 units under various industrial sports associations and district workers' sports associations participated in the competition. The men's route was ten kilometers, and the women's route was five kilometers. They all rode ordinary bicycles.

Gao Jukun, a contestant from the Nankai District Workers' Sports Association, took the lead in the first group of men's competition, completing the entire course in sixteen minutes and forty and six tenths of a second. A total of twelve outstanding women athletes participated in the women's group. Yang Jiarong, a Zhong Sheng Sports Association athlete and sportswoman, won first place with a time of ten minutes and eleven seconds.

### 天津举行工人自行车赛

1957-09-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　天津举行工人自行车赛
    本报讯　天津市第六届工人体育运动大会自行车比赛，14、15日在津塘公路詹庄子一带举行。各产业体育协会和各区职工体育协会二十六个单位的二百五十多名男女运动员参加了比赛。男子组的路程是十公里，女子组是五公里，他们骑的都是普通自行车。
    南开区职工体协选手高聚坤，在男子第一组比赛中，一马当先，用十六分四十秒六的时间跑完全程。女子组共有十二名健儿参加，钟声体协选手、运动健将杨家荣以十分十一秒的成绩获得了第一名。
