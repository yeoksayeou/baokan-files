Title: Cachin Awarded the Order of Lenin
Author:
Date: 1957-09-21
Page: 6

    Cachin Awarded the Order of Lenin
    Xinhua News Agency, September 20th – TASS reported on the 20th that the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has awarded the Order of Lenin to Marcel Cachin, a leader of the French Communist Party, in recognition of his many years of unremitting efforts in consolidating the friendship between the French and Soviet peoples.
    Cachin is a member of the Political Bureau of the French Communist Party Central Committee, and today is his eighty-eighth birthday.

### 加香荣获列宁勋章

1957-09-21
第6版()
专栏：

    加香荣获列宁勋章
    新华社20日讯　据塔斯社20日报道：苏联最高苏维埃主席团授予法国共产党领导人之—马赛尔·加香列宁勋章，以表彰他多年来不懈地从事巩固法苏人民友谊活动的功绩。
    加香是法共中央政治局委员，今天是他的八十八岁生日。
