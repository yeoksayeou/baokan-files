Title: Dr. Hatta Returns to Beijing After Visiting the Northeast; Visits Museum and Dalian Shipyard in Lushun-Dalian
Author:
Date: 1957-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 28th — Former Indonesian Vice President Dr. Hatta and his entourage returned to Beijing by special plane this afternoon from Lushun-Dalian after visiting various places in Northeast China.

Vice Premier Chen Yi and Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu, among others, were at the airport to greet them.

This morning, Mrs. Hatta and some Indonesian guests visited the No. 2 State Cotton Factory and the Beijing No. 1 Enamel Production Cooperative, and visited the home of Beijing Deputy Mayor Le Songsheng.

This evening, General Su Yu, Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, hosted a banquet for Lieutenant Colonel Suwido and Lieutenant Colonel Suyatmo, who accompanied Dr. Hatta on his visit to China. Those present included General Li Kenong, General Gan Siqi, and General Fu Qiutao.

Xinhua News Agency, Lushun-Dalian, 28th — This morning, former Indonesian Vice President Dr. Hatta and some senior officials accompanying him on his visit, accompanied by Mayor Song Li and others, visited the Lushun Museum and the Dalian Shipyard.

### 哈达博士在东北参观后回京  在旅大参观了博物馆和大连造船厂

1957-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    哈达博士在东北参观后回京
    在旅大参观了博物馆和大连造船厂
    新华社28日讯　印度尼西亚前副总统哈达博士和他的随行人员在我国东北各地参观后，今天下午由旅大乘专机回到北京。
    到机场迎接的，有陈毅副总理和章汉夫副部长等多人。
    今天上午，哈达夫人和一部分印度尼西亚客人在北京参观了国棉二厂和北京市第一珐琅生产合作社，并且访问了北京市副市长乐松生的家庭。
    今天晚间，中国人民解放军总参谋长粟裕大将宴请随同哈达博士来我国访问的苏维多中校和苏亚特莫中校。出席作陪的，有李克农上将、甘泗淇上将和傅秋涛上将。
    据新华社旅大28日电　今天上午，印度尼西亚前副总统哈达博士和随他来访问的部分高级官员，由宋黎市长等陪同参观了旅顺博物馆和大连造船厂。
