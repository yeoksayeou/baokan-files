Title: Beijing Ping Pong Team Defeats Macau Team
Author:
Date: 1957-09-16
Page: 3
　　Sports News

　　Beijing Ping Pong Team Defeats Macau Team
    (Reported by this newspaper) Yesterday, the Beijing and Macau men's team table tennis match was held in the Beijing Gymnasium. The Beijing team won 5-0. Among them, Luo Jinshun, the 1956 Macau singles champion, lost to Wang Chuanyao and Cen Huaiguang of the Beijing team, who had been out of play for a long time due to illness.
    Zhuang Jiafu of the Beijing team defeated Kuang Quan 2-1.
    Wang Chuanyao defeated Liu Zhuying 2-0, and Cen Huaiguang defeated Kuang Quan 2-0.
    Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi and President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu attended the game.

### 北京乒乓球队胜澳门队

1957-09-16
第3版()
专栏：体育新闻

　　北京乒乓球队胜澳门队
    本报讯  昨天，在北京体育馆举行了北京和澳门男子团体乒乓球比赛，北京队以5比0获胜。其中，1956年澳门单打冠军罗锦燊分别败于北京队的王传耀和因病久未上场的岑淮光。
    北京队庄家富以2比1战胜邝权。
    王传耀以2比0胜刘铸英，岑淮光以2比0胜邝权。
    国务院副总理陈毅、最高人民法院院长董必武参观了球赛。
