    New World Geographic Discovery
    American scientists recently discovered an undersea mountain range about 1.6 kilometers high in the Arctic region. Its length and width have not yet been measured.
    American scientists discovered this mountain range while measuring the depth of the seawater with echo sounders at the Arctic Ice Floe Scientific Station. Its location is between the Arctic and Point Barrow, Alaska, about 640 kilometers from the North Pole.
    According to American scientists, this mountain range appears to be parallel to the large Lomonosov Ridge in the center of the Arctic Ocean. The Lomonosov Ridge stretches from Greenland across the Arctic Ocean to the New Siberian Islands.
          (Xinhua News Agency)

### 世界地理新发现

1957-09-03
第6版()
专栏：

    世界地理新发现
    美国科学家最近在北极地区发现了一座约一点六公里高的海底山脉。它的长度和宽度还没有测量出来。
    美国科学家是在北极浮冰科学站上用回声探测器测量海水深浅的时候发现这座山脉的。它的位置在北极同阿拉斯加的巴罗角之间，距北极约六百四十公里。
    据美国科学家说，这座山脉似乎同北极圈中央的大洛蒙诺索夫海底山脊是平行的。大洛蒙诺索夫海底山脊的走向是从格陵兰起穿过北极海一直到新西伯利亚群岛。
          （新华社）
