Title: China and Japan Reach a Deal: Supplying Japan with 400,000 Tons of Kailuan Coal
Author:
Date: 1957-09-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th. Representatives from seven well-known Japanese steel factories, including Yawata, Fuji, and Nippon Kokan, as well as six gas companies, including Tokyo Gas, who came to negotiate the import of coal from China, have reached an agreement with the China Minerals Company for China to supply 400,000 tons of Kailuan coal. The agreement for this transaction was signed this afternoon by Mr. Fumio Okazaki, the Japanese representative, and Mr. Shang Guangwen, the manager of the China Minerals Company.

### 中日达成一项交易  供应日本四十万吨开滦煤

1957-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　中日达成一项交易
　　供应日本四十万吨开滦煤
    新华社13日讯  日本八幡、富士和日本钢管等七家著名钢铁厂以及东京瓦斯等六家煤气公司派来谈判进口我国煤炭的代表，已与中国矿产公司达成一项由中国供应四十万吨开滦煤的协议，这一项交易的协定书，已经在今天下午由日方代表人冈崎文勋和中国矿产公司经理商广文签字。
