Title: Polish Military Delegation Arrives in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1957-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, September 28th. The Polish People's Republic Defense Minister, General Marian Spychalski, leading the Polish military delegation, arrived in Shanghai by train from Nanjing at 9:15 PM on the 28th, receiving a warm welcome. Among those welcoming them were: Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng, Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Wei Wenbo, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Song Richang, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, and many generals and officers from the troops stationed in Shanghai.

The Consul General of the Polish People's Republic in Shanghai, Biernacki, was also at the station to welcome them.

### 波军事代表团抵沪

1957-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    波军事代表团抵沪
    新华社上海28日电　波兰人民共和国国防部长马里安·斯彼哈尔斯基上将率领的波兰军事代表团于28日下午九时十五分乘车由南京到达上海，受到热烈欢迎。前往欢迎的有：中国人民解放军上海警备区司令员王必成中将，中共上海市委书记处书记魏文伯，上海市副市长宋日昌及驻上海各部队将、校军官多人。
    波兰人民共和国驻上海总领事别尔纳茨基也到车站欢迎。
