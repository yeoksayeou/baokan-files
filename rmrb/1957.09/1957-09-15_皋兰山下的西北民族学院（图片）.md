Northwest Nationalities Institute Under Gannan Mountain

The Northwest is one of the regions in our country where ethnic minorities are concentrated. In order to vigorously cultivate ethnic minority intellectuals and cadres, the Party and the government have established a large-scale Northwest Nationalities Institute under Gannan Mountain in Lanzhou. Currently, there are more than 2,200 students of sixteen ethnic groups, including Uyghur, Tibetan, and Hui, studying at the institute. This is an exterior view of the main building of the Northwest Nationalities Institute.

### 皋兰山下的西北民族学院（图片）
刘奇
1957-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　皋兰山下的西北民族学院
    西北是我国少数民族聚居地区之一。党和政府为了大力培养少数民族知识分子和干部，在兰州皋兰山下建立了一座规模宏大的西北民族学院。目前在该院学习的，有维吾尔、藏、回等十六个民族的二千二百多名学生。这是西北民族学院主要建筑的外景。              
    （刘奇摄）
