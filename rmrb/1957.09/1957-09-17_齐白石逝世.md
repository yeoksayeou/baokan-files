Title: Qi Baishi Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1957-09-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Qi Baishi, representative of the National People's Congress, Chairman of the Chinese Artists Association, Honorary President of the Beijing Chinese Painting Academy, Honorary Professor of the Central Academy of Fine Arts, winner of the 1955 International Peace Prize, and People's Artist, passed away at Beijing Hospital at 6:40 PM on September 16, 1957, at the age of ninety-seven.

The list of the Qi Baishi Funeral Committee is as follows:

Chairman: Guo Moruo

Members (in order of stroke count of surnames): Yu Feian, Fang Rending, Ye Qianyu, Tian Han, Liu Kaiqu, Qi Yanming, Lao She, Yang Hansheng, Shen Yinmo, Guan Shanyue, He Xiangning, Wu Zuoren, Li Jishen, Zhou Enlai, Zhou Yang, Shen Yanbing, Chen Banding, Chen Shutong, Zheng Zhenduo, Xia Yan, Mei Lanfang, Li Jinxi, Cai Ruohong, Qian Junrui.

### 齐白石逝世
