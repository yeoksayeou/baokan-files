Title: Czechoslovak Parliament Delegation to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1957-09-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 1st. It was announced here yesterday that a delegation from the Czechoslovak National Assembly, led by National Assembly Chairman Fierlinger, will visit China at the end of September at the invitation of Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China.

The delegation is also invited to visit Mongolia and North Korea.

### 捷议会代表团将访华

1957-09-02
第1版()
专栏：

    捷议会代表团将访华
    新华社布拉格1日电  据这里昨天宣布，由国民议会主席费林格率领的捷克斯洛伐克国民议会代表团，应中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会刘少奇委员长的邀请，将在9月底到中国访问。
    代表团应邀还将访问蒙古和朝鲜。
