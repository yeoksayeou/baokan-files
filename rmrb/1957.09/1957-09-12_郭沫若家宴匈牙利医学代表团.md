Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Hungarian Medical Delegation at Home
Author:
Date: 1957-09-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet at his home on the evening of the 10th in honor of the Hungarian medical delegation led by Academician Rusznyák, President of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences. Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Academy of Sciences, and others were also present.

### 郭沫若家宴匈牙利医学代表团

1957-09-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　郭沫若家宴匈牙利医学代表团
    新华社11日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若10日晚在家中欢宴以匈牙利科学院院长鲁斯尼亚克院士为首的匈牙利医学代表团。作陪的有科学院副院长吴有训等。
