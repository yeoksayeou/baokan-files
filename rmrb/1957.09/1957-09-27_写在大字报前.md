Title: Before Writing a Big-Character Poster
Author: Li Xue'ao
Date: 1957-09-27
Page: 8

    Before Writing a Big-Character Poster
    Before Writing a Big-Character Poster
    Like a peacock spreading its tail,
    Like a hundred flowers blooming,
    Red and green big-character posters,
    Cover the walls along the road.
    Concise language,
    Clear ideas,
    I watch and walk before the big-character posters,
    Like swimming in an ocean of wisdom.
    To Comrades
    Rectification is a clear mirror,
    Embedded in the gate of socialism.
    Whether you can pass through,
    Please check and measure yourselves before the mirror.
    Dust on your face must be washed away,
    Do not condone or conceal it!
    Simple Person
    Wearing cloth shoes,
    Dressed in blue,
    In the crowd,
    He is as simple as an old worker.
    Workers love to tell him their innermost thoughts,
    He loves to read the workers' articles even more.
    When he leaves the factory,
    People realize he is a leader from the Central Committee.

### 写在大字报前
李学鳌
1957-09-27
第8版()
专栏：

    写在大字报前
    李学鳌
    写在大字报前
    像孔雀展屏，
    像百花齐放，
    红红绿绿的大字报啊，
    贴满路旁的墙。
    精粹的语言，
    明确的思想，
    我在大字报前观看、行走，
    像游泳在智慧的海洋。
    给同志
    整风是一面明镜，
    嵌在社会主义的关上。
    能否过得关去，
    请在镜前检查、衡量。
    脸上有灰尘必须洗掉，
    切莫姑息、掩藏！
    淳朴的人
    脚穿布便鞋，
    身穿蓝衣裳，
    在人群里，
    他淳朴得像老师傅一样。
    工人们爱向他说心里话，
    他从心里更爱读工人们的文章。
    当他离开工厂的时候，
    人们才知他是中央来的首长。
