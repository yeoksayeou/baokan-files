Title: Dr. Hatta Visits and Tours Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd—Former Indonesian Vice President Dr. Hatta and his entourage, accompanied by Burhan Shahidi, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People’s Political Consultative Conference and President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, visited the Beijing Women's Drawnwork Production Cooperative and the Beijing Electron Tube Factory this morning and afternoon, and toured the Forbidden City. (Attached picture)
    The picture shows Dr. Hatta (rear right) and his wife (front row, third from the right) visiting the clock and watch exhibition room in the Palace Museum.

### 哈达博士在京参观游览

1957-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    哈达博士在京参观游览
    据新华社23日讯　印度尼西亚前副总统哈达博士及其随行人员，由政协全国委员会副主席、中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长包尔汉等人陪同，在今天上下午分别参观了北京市妇女挑花生产合作社和北京电子管厂，游览了故宫。（附图片）
    图为哈达博士（右后）及其夫人（前排右第三人）等在故宫博物院钟表陈列室参观。
