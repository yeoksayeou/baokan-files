Title: First Visit to China by Yugoslav Federal Leaders: Vukmanović Arrives in Wuhan via Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1957-09-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 11th: The Vice President of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia, S. Vukmanović, arrived in Guangzhou by special train from Shenzhen at 12:30 noon today. This is the first visit to China by a leader of the Yugoslav Federation. Accompanying Vice President Vukmanović on his visit to China are the Deputy Secretary-General of the Federal Secretariat of State for Foreign Affairs of Yugoslavia, Velebit, and the President of the National Bank, Gucina, among others.

Welcoming them at the station were Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province; Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou; Lieutenant General Wen Niansheng, Head of the Guangzhou Garrison; leading members of the Guangdong Provincial and Guangzhou Municipal CPPCC, various democratic parties, and people's organizations, as well as more than 1,000 people from all walks of life.

Also at the station to welcome them were Soviet Consul in Guangzhou, Orlov; Acting Consul General of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Guangzhou, Le Chung Thuy; and Czechoslovak friends in Guangzhou. Counselor Krulic of the Yugoslav Embassy in China came from Beijing to greet them.

On behalf of the people of Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, Chen Yu welcomed the distinguished Yugoslav guests from afar and wished them a smooth and successful visit to China.

In his reply, Vice President Vukmanović said: Although our two countries are geographically far apart, for many years we have been paying close attention to and expressing great sympathy for the heroic struggle of the Chinese people against reactionary forces. The people of Yugoslavia have also experienced equally arduous struggles, and this links our two peoples together. Today, we can assure you that in your cause, you can fully count on the sympathy of the Yugoslav people. We wish you success in your construction endeavors.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, September 11th: The Vice President of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia, Vukmanović, and others, who are visiting our country at our invitation, arrived in Wuhan by special plane from Guangzhou today on their way to Beijing.

After disembarking from the plane at 5:30 this afternoon, Vice President Vukmanović and others, accompanied by Zhang Tixue, Governor of Hubei Province, who came to welcome them, visited the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge and received a commemorative album of "The Construction of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge Project" presented by Yang Zaitian, Deputy Director of the Bridge Engineering Bureau. Afterwards, they took a drive to tour the city of Wuhan.

### 南斯拉夫联邦领导人首次访华  伏克曼诺维奇经广州到武汉

1957-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　南斯拉夫联邦领导人首次访华
　　伏克曼诺维奇经广州到武汉
    新华社广州11日电　南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席斯·伏克曼诺维奇今天中午十二时三十分自深圳乘专车到达广州。这是南斯拉夫联邦领导人第一次来我国访问。随同伏克曼诺维奇副主席一起来我国访问的，有南斯拉夫联邦外交国务秘书处副秘书长维莱比特、国家银行行长古齐纳等人。
    到车站欢迎的有广东省长陈郁、广州市长朱光、广州驻军首长文年生中将、广东省、广州市政协、各民主党派、人民团体的负责人以及各界人民一千多人。
    到车站欢迎的还有苏联驻广州领事鄂洛夫、越南民主共和国驻广州代理总领事黎终始、在广州的捷克斯洛伐克友人。南斯拉夫驻我国大使馆参赞克鲁尼奇，自北京前来迎接。
    陈郁代表广东省和广州市人民，向远道而来的南斯拉夫贵宾表示欢迎，并预祝他们在中国的访问顺利和成功。
    伏克曼诺维奇副主席在答词中说：尽管我们两国在地理上相距很远，但是，多少年来，我们是以极大的注意和高度的同情关怀着中国人民对反动势力的英勇斗争的。南斯拉夫人民也经历过同样艰苦的斗争，这把我们两国人民联系起来。今天，我们可以向你们保证：在你们的事业中，你们完全可以得到南斯拉夫人民的同情。祝你们建设事业成功。
    据新华社武汉11日电　应邀来我国访问的南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席伏克曼诺维奇等在去北京途中，今天乘专机由广州来到武汉。
    伏克曼诺维奇副主席等在今天下午五点半下飞机后，就在前来欢迎的湖北省省长张体学陪同下，参观了武汉长江大桥，并接受了大桥工程局副局长杨在田赠送的“武汉长江大桥工程建设”纪念册。接着驱车游览了武汉市容。
