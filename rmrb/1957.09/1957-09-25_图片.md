This year, Xuanhua area has a bumper harvest of grapes, especially the Milk grapes which are plumper than in previous years. This is Cai Qichun, a member of the Chunguang Agricultural Production Cooperative in Xuanhua Town and a junior high school graduate who is participating in agricultural production this year, selecting high-quality Milk grapes on the grape trellis.
