Title: Yugoslavia and Mongolia Decide to Develop Cooperation: Tito Accepts Tsedenbal's Invitation to Visit Mongolia
Author: 
Date: 1957-09-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, August 31st: Yugoslav President Tito and Mongolian Chairman of the Council of Ministers Tsedenbal signed a joint statement of the two governments' delegations' talks in Belgrade on the evening of August 30th.

The statement said that the two delegations noted with satisfaction that their views on the fundamental issues of the current international situation are very close. The two governments are convinced that safeguarding world peace is not only necessary but also possible. They believe that all countries, whether large or small, must work together for this purpose and develop their cooperation on the basis of peace and active coexistence, according to the principles of respect for independence, sovereignty, equality, non-interference in internal affairs and mutual respect.

The statement pointed out that the talks were conducted in a friendly and sincere atmosphere in the spirit of mutual understanding and mutual respect. In addition to exchanging views on some current international relations issues, the two sides also exchanged views on further developing and strengthening relations between the two countries.

The two sides unanimously expressed their willingness to cooperate economically by developing trade and decided that the relevant institutions of the two countries should hold negotiations on the modalities and scope of such cooperation.

The two sides expressed their hope to develop cultural and scientific and technological cooperation between the two countries, and both sides agreed to conclude a cultural cooperation agreement.

The two sides unanimously decided to continue to directly exchange opinions and develop personal contacts.

The statement concluded by pointing out that President Tito had gladly accepted Chairman Tsedenbal's invitation to visit Mongolia.

### 南蒙两国决发展合作  铁托接受泽登巴尔邀请将访问蒙古

1957-09-01
第6版()
专栏：

    南蒙两国决发展合作
    铁托接受泽登巴尔邀请将访问蒙古
    据新华社贝尔格莱德31日电  南斯拉夫总统铁托和蒙古部长会议主席泽登巴尔，8月30日晚在贝尔格莱德签署了两国政府代表团的会谈联合声明。
    声明说，两国代表团满意地指出，它们在估计目前国际局势的基本问题方面的看法是非常接近的。两国政府深信保卫世界和平不仅必要而且可能。它们认为，必须使所有国家，不论大国或者小国，一致为这个目的而努力，并且根据尊重独立、主权、平等、互不干涉内政和互相尊重的原则，在和平和积极共处的基础上发展它们的合作。
    声明指出，这次会谈是在友好和诚恳的气氛中本着互相谅解、互相尊重的精神进行的。双方除了就目前的一些国际关系问题交换了意见以外，还就进一步发展和加强两国间的关系问题交换了意见。
    双方一致表示愿意通过发展贸易的方法在经济方面进行合作，并且决定由两国有关机构就这种合作的方式和范围举行谈判。
    双方表示希望发展两国间的文化和科学技术合作，双方一致同意缔结一个文化合作协定。
    双方一致决定继续直接交换意见和发展个人接触。
    声明最后指出，铁托总统已经高兴地接受了泽登巴尔主席提出的请他访问蒙古的邀请。
