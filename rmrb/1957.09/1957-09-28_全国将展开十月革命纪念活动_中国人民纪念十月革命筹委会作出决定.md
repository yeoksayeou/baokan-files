Title: National Commemoration Activities for the October Revolution to be Launched; Decision Made by the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese People for Commemorating the October Revolution
Author:
Date: 1957-09-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 27th: The Preparatory Committee of the Chinese People for Celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution held its first meeting on the 26th to discuss the preparatory work for celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.
    The meeting passed the following resolution:
    Resolution of the First Meeting of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese People for Celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution:
    (1) The commemoration activities for the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution will begin throughout the country after National Day. The various regions will dedicate roughly one month to focus on publicity work and various commemorative activities. The political consultative conferences and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations of various provinces, cities, and autonomous regions should immediately join with other people's organizations to handle the preparatory work.
    (2) The capital and the municipalities directly under the central government, the provincial capitals, and the medium-sized cities should hold celebration rallies with the participation of representatives from all walks of life on the festival day or on the eve of the festival.
    (3) Various regions should hold as many reports, exhibitions, and cultural and artistic activities commemorating the October Revolution as possible, and conduct extensive and in-depth publicity to the masses. Factories, enterprises, government organs, troops, middle schools and above, agricultural producers' cooperatives, and handicraft producers' cooperatives should, whenever possible, hold commemorative meetings, reports, or forums before and after the festival, under the condition that production is not affected.
    (4) Newspapers, periodicals, and radio stations should plan well the work of commemorating the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution. The various cultural departments, drama, music, fine arts, film, etc., should hold exhibitions and performances of Soviet cultural and artistic achievements and commemorations of the October Revolution.
    (5) It is suggested that the central organs of the various democratic parties and people's organizations, in accordance with the spirit of the resolution of the joint meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and with reference to this resolution, issue instructions to their subordinate organizations on the specific methods for celebrating the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. The relevant propaganda organs should draw up publicity plans and implement them.

### 全国将展开十月革命纪念活动  中国人民纪念十月革命筹委会作出决定

1957-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    全国将展开十月革命纪念活动
    中国人民纪念十月革命筹委会作出决定
    新华社27日讯　中国人民庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年筹备委员会26日举行第一次会议，讨论庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年的筹备工作。
    会议通过决议如下：
    中国人民庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年筹备委员会第一次会议决议：
    （一）全国各地纪念十月革命四十周年的活动，从国庆节以后即行开始。各地大体上以一个月时间，比较集中地进行宣传工作和各项纪念活动。各省、市、自治区的政治协商会议和中苏友好协会应即联合其他人民团体共同办理筹备工作。
    （二）首都和各直辖市、各省会以及各中等城市在节日或节日前夕举行有各界代表参加的庆祝大会。
    （三）各地应当尽可能举办各种纪念十月革命的报告会、展览会和文化艺术活动，广泛和深入地向人民群众进行宣传。各工厂、企业、机关、部队、中等以上学校、农业生产合作社、手工业生产合作社在节日前后应当尽可能在不妨碍生产的条件下举行纪念会、报告会或座谈会。
    （四）报纸、刊物、广播电台应当有计划地做好纪念十月革命四十周年的工作。各文化部门，戏剧、音乐、美术、电影等方面，应当举办苏联文化艺术成就和纪念十月革命的展览和演出。
    （五）建议各民主党派和各人民团体的中央机关根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会和中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会联席会议的决议的精神，并且参照本决议，向所属组织发出关于庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年具体办法的指示。各有关宣传机关应当订出宣传计划并且贯彻执行。
