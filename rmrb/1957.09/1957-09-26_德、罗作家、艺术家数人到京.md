Title: Several German and Romanian Writers and Artists Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 25th. German Academy of Arts academician and photographic artist John Heartfield and his wife arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 13th. Artists Stotz and Matzek arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 14th. Female writer Annemarie Reinharz and Libor-Brockdorff and her husband arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 24th.
　　They are all visiting China according to the 1957 executive plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement.
　　Romanian visual artists Sabo Bella and Pop Arina Maria, who are visiting China according to the 1957 executive plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 24th. Andip Constantin, editor-in-chief of the Romanian "Defense of the Motherland" newspaper, also arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 德、罗作家、艺术家数人到京

1957-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

    德、罗作家、艺术家数人到京
    新华社25日讯　德国艺术院院士、摄影艺术家约翰·赫尔特菲尔德及其夫人在13日下午到京。美术家史托茨、麦兹克在14日下午到京。女作家安妮玛丽·雷因哈特、利波尔——布罗克道夫夫人在24日下午到京。
    他们都是根据中德文化合作协定1957年执行计划来我国访问的。
    根据中罗文化合作协定1957年执行计划来中国访问的罗马尼亚造型艺术家萨波·贝拉、波普·阿琳娜·玛丽亚在24日下午到京。罗马尼亚“保卫祖国”报总编辑安狄普·康斯坦丁也在今日下午乘飞机到达北京。
