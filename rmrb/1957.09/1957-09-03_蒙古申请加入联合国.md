Title: Mongolia Applies to Join the United Nations
Author: 
Date: 1957-09-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, September 2nd: Mongolian Foreign Minister Avarzed sent a telegram to the President of the United Nations Security Council on September 1st, reiterating that Mongolia should enjoy the right to become a member of the United Nations and requesting that the current Security Council discuss Mongolia's application. The telegram stated that Mongolia has the desire to consolidate international security, the cause of peace, and cooperate with the United Nations. As in previous statements since 1949, the People's Republic of Mongolia is willing to undertake and fulfill all obligations stipulated in the United Nations Charter.

### 蒙古申请加入联合国

1957-09-03
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙古申请加入联合国
    新华社乌兰巴托2日电  蒙古外交部长阿维尔齐德9月1日打电报给联合国安全理事会主席，重申蒙古应该享有成为联合国会员国的权利，并且要求本届安全理事会讨论蒙古的申请。电报说，蒙古抱有为巩固国际安全、和平事业以及同联合国合作的愿望，蒙古人民共和国正如1949年以来的历次声明一样，愿意承担和履行联合国宪章所规定的一切义务。
