Title: Picture
Author: Wang Weiling
Date: 1957-09-15
Page: 5

    On the shore of Dianchi Lake, the largest automated thermal power plant in Kunming is under construction. The equipment for this power plant is supplied by Czechoslovakia, and its generating capacity is 24,000 kilowatts. It is expected that 12,000 kilowatts of generating equipment will be put into production by the end of this year. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Weiling photographed.
