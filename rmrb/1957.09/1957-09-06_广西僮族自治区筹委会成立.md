Title: Preparatory Committee for the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Established
Author:
Date: 1957-09-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, 5th. The Preparatory Committee for the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region was announced yesterday afternoon at the closing ceremony of the Fifth Session of the First People's Congress of Guangxi Province. The congress also approved the appointment of Wei Guoqing (Zhuang) as the chairman of the preparatory committee, and Li Renren (Han), Jin Baosheng (Yao), Qin Zhenwu (Dong), Yang Wengui (Miao), and forty others as members of the preparatory committee.

The Preparatory Committee for the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region will be responsible for drafting various regulations and continuing to conduct in-depth publicity work related to ethnic policies, preparing for the formal establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region in the spring of next year.

### 广西僮族自治区筹委会成立

1957-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    广西僮族自治区筹委会成立
    新华社南宁5日电　广西僮族自治区筹备委员会，昨天下午在广西省第一届人民代表大会第五次会议的闭幕式上宣布成立。大会并通过由韦国清（僮）任筹备委员会主任委员、李任仁（汉）、金宝生（瑶）、秦振武（侗）、杨文贵（苗）等四十人为筹备委员会委员。
    广西僮族自治区筹备委员会将负责草拟各种条例，并继续深入开展有关民族政策的宣传工作，准备在明年春季正式成立广西僮族自治区。
