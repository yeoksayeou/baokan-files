Author:
Date: 1957-09-07
Page: 4

    Sports News

    "August 1st" Basketball Team Wins Again in Czechoslovakia
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 5th. On the afternoon of September 3rd, the Chinese "August 1st" Men's Basketball Team played a match against the representative team of Prešov city in eastern Czechoslovakia. The result was a 79-52 victory over the opponent. This is the second victory for the "August 1st" basketball team in Czechoslovakia during this competition.

### “八一”篮球队在捷再次获胜

1957-09-07
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    “八一”篮球队在捷再次获胜
    新华社布拉格5日电　3日下午，中国“八一”男子篮球队在捷克斯洛伐克东部的普雷索夫城同这个城市的代表队举行了比赛，结果以七十九比五十二战胜对方。这是“八一”篮球队这次在捷克斯洛伐克举行比赛以来所获得的第二次胜利。
