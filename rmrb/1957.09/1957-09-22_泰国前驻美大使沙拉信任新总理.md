Title: Former Thai Ambassador to the US, Sarasin, Trusts New Prime Minister
Author: 
Date: 1957-09-22
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, September 21st: A Thai army spokesman announced today that the interim parliament, composed of 123 members appointed by the King of Thailand, decided at a meeting held today to appoint Nai Pote Sarasin, former Thai Ambassador to the United States, as the new Prime Minister. He will organize a "caretaker cabinet" until parliamentary elections are held again in three months. Nai Pote Sarasin is currently the Secretary-General of the Manila Treaty Organization. Yesterday, the US Ambassador to Thailand, Bishop, visited him. According to Thai newspapers, Bishop "asked Nai Pote Sarasin to accept the position of Prime Minister."

### 泰国前驻美大使沙拉信任新总理

1957-09-22
第7版()
专栏：

    泰国前驻美大使沙拉信任新总理
    据新华社21日讯　曼谷消息：泰国陆军发言人今天宣布，由泰国国王指定的一百二十三名议员组成的临时议会在今天举行的会议上，决定由前泰国驻美国大使乃朴·沙拉信担任新总理，并且由他组织一个“看守内阁”，直到三个月后重新举行议会选举为止。乃朴·沙拉信目前担任马尼拉条约组织的秘书长。昨天，美国驻泰国大使毕晓普拜访过他。据泰国报纸报道，毕晓普曾经“要求乃朴·沙拉信接受总理的职位”。
