Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Delegation from the Japanese Socialist Party to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-09-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 19th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the Goodwill Mission of the Japanese Socialist Party to the Soviet Union, headed by Tetsu Katayama, this afternoon.

Present at the meeting were Qiao Guanhua, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, and Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.

### 周总理接见日本社会党访苏使节团

1957-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见日本社会党访苏使节团
    新华社19日讯　周恩来总理今日下午接见了以片山哲为首的日本社会党访苏亲善使节团。
    接见时在座的有中国人民外交学会副会长乔冠华、秘书长吴茂荪和中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志等。
