Title: Polish Military Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Marshal Peng Dehuai Receives and Hosts Banquet

Author:
Date: 1957-09-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 20th – At the invitation of Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, the military delegation of the Polish People's Republic, led by General Speichalski, Minister of National Defense, arrived in Beijing this afternoon for a friendly visit to our country.

　　After General Speichalski stepped off the plane, he reviewed the guard of honor accompanied by Marshal Peng Dehuai. Afterwards, a welcoming ceremony was held at the airport.

　　Among those present at the airport to welcome the delegation today were senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including Grand General Su Yu, Grand General Tan Zheng, Grand General Wang Shusheng, and Grand General Xu Guangda, as well as Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei.

　　The Ambassador of the Polish People's Republic to China, Kirilok, and embassy staff, as well as military attachés from various countries in China, also came to the airport to welcome the delegation.

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 20th – Minister of National Defense Marshal Peng Dehuai held a banquet this evening to welcome the military delegation of the Polish People's Republic headed by General Speichalski.

　　Marshal Peng Dehuai said in his speech at the banquet that the Polish People's Army is an army of the working people born in the flames of the anti-fascist struggle and is a loyal defender of the interests of the Polish people. The Chinese People's Liberation Army is greatly delighted and admires the heroic spirit and great victories achieved by the Polish people in the anti-fascist war.

　　General Speichalski said in his speech that the great Chinese soldiers, Soviet soldiers, Polish soldiers, and soldiers of other socialist countries, educated with a profound spirit of patriotism and internationalism, vigilantly safeguard the peaceful labor of their people, and they do not threaten the security of any country in the world.

　　Accompanying Marshal Peng Dehuai at the banquet today were senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including Grand General Su Yu, Grand General Tan Zheng, Grand General Wang Shusheng, and Grand General Xu Guangda, as well as Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei.

　　The Ambassador of the Polish People's Republic to China, Kirilok, and military attachés from various countries in China were also invited to attend the banquet.

　　Before the banquet, Marshal Peng Dehuai met with all members of the military delegation of the Polish People's Republic.

### 波兰军事代表团到京  彭德怀元帅接见并设宴欢迎

1957-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰军事代表团到京
    彭德怀元帅接见并设宴欢迎
    据新华社20日讯　应中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀元帅邀请来我国作友好访问的波兰人民共和国军事代表团，在国防部长斯彼哈尔斯基上将率领下，今天下午到达北京。
    斯彼哈尔斯基上将走下飞机后，在彭德怀元帅陪同下检阅了仪仗队。接着，在机场上举行了欢迎仪式。
    今天到机场欢迎的，有粟裕大将、谭政大将、王树声大将、许光达大将等中国人民解放军高级将领，还有外交部副部长姬鹏飞。
    波兰人民共和国驻中国大使基里洛克和使馆人员，以及各国驻华武官也到机场欢迎。
    据新华社20日讯　国防部长彭德怀元帅今晚举行宴会欢迎以斯彼哈尔斯基上将为首的波兰人民共和国军事代表团。
    彭德怀元帅在宴会上致辞说，波兰人民军是在反法西斯斗争的烽火中诞生的劳动人民的军队，是波兰人民利益的忠实的保卫者。中国人民解放军对波兰人民在反法西斯战争中所表现的英勇精神和所取得的巨大胜利感到极大的欢欣和钦佩。
    斯彼哈尔斯基上将致辞说，以深刻的爱国主义、国际主义精神教育出来的伟大人民中国战士、苏联战士、波兰战士以及其他社会主义国家的战士，警惕地保卫着自己人民的和平劳动，他们不威胁世界上任何国家的安全。
    陪同出席今天宴会的，有粟裕大将、谭政大将、王树声大将、许光达大将等中国人民解放军高级将领以及外交部副部长姬鹏飞。
    波兰人民共和国驻中国大使基里洛克以及各国驻华武官也应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会前，彭德怀元帅接见了波兰人民共和国军事代表团全体人员。
