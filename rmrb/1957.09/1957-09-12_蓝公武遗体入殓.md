Title: Blue Gongwu's Remains Placed in Coffin
Author:
Date: 1957-09-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: The remains of Lan Gongwu, a member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, were placed in a coffin at Beijing Hospital at 9:00 am today.

Those who personally witnessed the placement in the coffin included Shen Junru and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the National People's Congress Standing Committee; Bo Yibo, Vice Premier of the State Council; Dong Biwu, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; heads of various departments of national leading organs; and friends of Lan Gongwu during his lifetime.

When the remains were placed in the coffin, the wife and children of Committee Member Lan Gongwu kept vigil by the side.

Committee Member Lan Gongwu was from Wujiang County, Jiangsu Province, and passed away at the age of seventy-one.

### 蓝公武遗体入殓

1957-09-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　蓝公武遗体入殓
    新华社11日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员蓝公武遗体今天上午九时在北京医院入殓。
    亲视入殓的，有全国人民代表大会副委员长沈钧儒、陈叔通，国务院副总理薄一波，政协全国委员会副主席董必武，国家领导机关各部门负责人和蓝公武生前友好等。
    入殓的时候，蓝公武委员的夫人和子女们在旁守灵。
    蓝公武委员是江苏省吴江县人，享年七十一岁。
