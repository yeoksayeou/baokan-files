Title: The Third National Women's Congress of China Opens Today; Cai Chang Hosts a Banquet for Women Representatives from Thirteen Countries Attending the Congress
Author:
Date: 1957-09-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The Third National Women's Congress of China is scheduled to open in Beijing on the 9th. As of the 8th, 1,241 delegates have registered. With the exception of 114 specially invited delegates, all other delegates were elected by local women's congresses and various representative women's meetings.

From September 5th to 7th, the delegates held preparatory meetings to discuss the work report of the Second Executive Committee of the All-China Women's Federation to the Congress, and discussed the Constitution of the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China. The preparatory meeting also discussed and approved the list of the presidium of the Congress consisting of 93 people, and approved the list of the Proposals Examination Committee, the Delegate Credentials Examination Committee, the Election Committee, the Secretary-General and Deputy Secretary-Generals of the Congress, as well as the agenda of the Congress.

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. Women representatives from thirteen countries invited to attend the Third National Women's Congress of China have arrived in Beijing one after another.

Tonight, the All-China Women's Federation hosted a banquet to entertain the representatives of various countries. Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, extended a warm welcome to the guests at the banquet.

Representing the Women's International Democratic Federation at this Congress is Pushpa Moie Bose, Vice-Chairman of the Women's International Democratic Federation, who arrived in Beijing yesterday afternoon. Miss Bose is the Chairman of the All India Women's Federation and a well-known Bengali writer.

The representatives of the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries invited to China to attend this Congress are: the Hungarian women's delegation, Bodoni Anna et al. (2 people), the Democratic German women's delegation, Ruth Schirmer et al. (2 people), the Mongolian women's delegation, Gombin Dolgosurong et al. (2 people), the Polish women's delegation, Ewa Schierburg-Zarembina et al. (2 people), the Bulgarian women's delegation, Rada Ilieva Bulgarova et al. (2 people), the Romanian women's delegation, Elena Vircoci et al. (2 people), the Albanian women's delegation, Eleni Trezi et al. (2 people), the Yugoslav women's delegation, Bosa Cvetić et al. (2 people), the Czechoslovak women's delegation, Tania Tumova et al. (2 people), the Soviet women's delegation, Ekaterina Ivanovna Leontieva et al. (3 people), the Korean women's delegation, Kim Young-soo et al. (2 people), and the Vietnamese women's delegation, Nguyen Thi Thap et al. (3 people).

Mrs. Rameshvari Nehru, Chairman of the Asian Solidarity Committee of India, and two others who are visiting Beijing, have also accepted the invitation to attend the Third National Women's Congress of China.

### 中国妇女第三次代表大会今日开幕  蔡畅宴请出席大会的十三个国家妇女代表

1957-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中国妇女第三次代表大会今日开幕
    蔡畅宴请出席大会的十三个国家妇女代表
    据新华社8日讯　中国妇女第三次全国代表大会定9日在北京开幕。到8日为止，报到的代表已有一千二百四十一人，除了一百十四名代表是特邀的以外，其余所有代表都是由各地妇女代表大会、各种妇女代表性会议选举产生的。
    9月5日到7日，代表们举行了预备会，讨论了全国妇联二届执行委员会向大会的工作报告，讨论了中华人民共和国妇女联合会章程。预备会议还讨论并通过了由九十三人组成的大会主席团名单，通过了提案审查委员会、代表资格审查委员会、选举委员会、大会秘书长、副秘书长的名单，以及大会的议程等。
    据新华社8日讯　应邀出席中国妇女第三次全国代表大会的十三个国家的妇女代表，已陆续到京。
    今天晚间，全国妇联设宴招待各国代表。全国妇联主席蔡畅在宴会上向客人们表示热烈欢迎。
    代表国际民主妇联参加这次大会的是国际民主妇联副主席普什帕莫耶·鲍斯，她在昨天下午到达北京。鲍斯小姐是印度全国妇女联合会主席，是著名的孟加拉文作家。
    应邀来中国参加这次大会的苏联及各人民民主国家的代表有：匈牙利妇女代表团博多尼·安娜等二人，民主德国妇女代表团鲁特·席尔默等二人，蒙古妇女代表团贡宾·多尔戈苏荣等二人，波兰妇女代表团爱娃·歇尔布格·扎雷比娜等二人，保加利亚妇女代表团拉达·伊利耶娃·保加罗娃等二人，罗马尼亚妇女代表团埃伦娜·维耳科奇等二人，阿尔巴尼亚妇女代表团埃莱尼·特雷齐等二人，南斯拉夫妇女代表团鲍萨·茨维蒂奇等二人，捷克斯洛伐克妇女代表团坦尼亚·图莫娃等二人，苏联妇女代表团叶卡杰林娜·伊凡诺芙娜·列昂节娃等三人，朝鲜妇女代表团金英秀等二人和越南妇女代表团阮氏十等三人。
    正在北京访问的印度亚洲团结委员会主席拉米希瓦里·尼赫鲁夫人等三人也接受了邀请，将出席中国妇女第三次全国代表大会。
