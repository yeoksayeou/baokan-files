Title: Poem Leaflet
Author: Jiang Wei, Sha Liu, Gui Xiao
Date: 1957-09-03
Page: 8

    Poem Leaflet
    Portrait of the Author of “Grass and Trees”
    Jiang Wei
    He is irreconcilable with the motherland,
    "Rather go to a capitalist country to be
    a free impoverished son...";
    He is at daggers drawn with the people,
    "Going to breed sons and daughters in the desert...".
    A slavish face,
    A flattering image;
    A face full of橫肉,
    A body of bayonets.
    False Appearance
        Sha Liu
    He doesn't confess himself,
    But criticizes others,
    Attempting to create a false appearance:
    I am an anti-rightist vanguard,
    Simply wanting to deceive people's eyes!
    Delusion
        Gui Xiao
    A cunning fox,
    Deluding to deceive the bright crescent moon;
    A hoarse crow,
    Deluding to cover half the world,
    A venomous snake's stinky egg,
    Deluding to break the rock;
    An ugly toad,
    Deluding to take down the elephant's teeth.
    (Originally published in "Jiangxi Daily")

### 诗传单
蒋维  沙柳  桂笑
1957-09-03
第8版()
专栏：诗传单

    诗传单
    为“草木篇”作者造像
    蒋维
    他与祖国势不两立，
    “宁愿去资本主义国家作
    个自由的贫困儿……”；
    他与人民不共戴天，
    “要去沙漠里繁殖着儿女…”。
    一副奴颜，
    一副媚像；
    一脸横肉，
    一身刺刀。
    假象
        沙柳
    他不交代自己，
    尽来批判旁人，
    企图制造假象：
    我是反右尖兵，
    无非是想迷惑人们的眼睛！
    妄想        桂笑
    狡猾的狐狸，
    妄想骗过皎洁的月牙；
    嘶哑的乌鸦，
    妄想遮住半个天下，
    毒蛇的臭蛋，
    妄想把岩石碰垮；
    丑恶的癞蛤蟆，
    妄想取下大象的牙。
    （原载“江西日报”）
