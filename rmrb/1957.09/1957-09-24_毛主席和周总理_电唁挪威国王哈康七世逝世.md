Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Telegrams of Condolence on the Death of King Haakon VII of Norway
Author:
Date: 1957-09-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd. Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram to King Olav of the Kingdom of Norway on September 22nd, expressing condolences on the death of King Haakon VII of the Kingdom of Norway. The telegram reads: “To His Majesty King Olav of the Kingdom of Norway:
    I have learned with sorrow of the unfortunate death of His Majesty King Haakon VII of the Kingdom of Norway. Please accept my condolences.
            Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, September 22, 1957, Beijing”

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd. Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Foreign Minister Lange of the Government of the Kingdom of Norway on September 22nd, expressing condolences on the death of King Haakon VII of the Kingdom of Norway. The telegram reads: “To His Excellency Mr. Lange, Foreign Minister of the Government of the Kingdom of Norway:
    I have learned with sorrow of the unfortunate death of His Majesty King Haakon VII of the Kingdom of Norway. I hereby send my condolences.
        Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, September 22, 1957, Beijing”

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd. Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department Zhang Shijie visited the Norwegian Embassy in China this afternoon to express their condolences to Ambassador Klok-Hensen on the death of King Haakon VII of the Kingdom of Norway.

### 毛主席和周总理  电唁挪威国王哈康七世逝世

1957-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席和周总理
    电唁挪威国王哈康七世逝世
    新华社23日讯　毛泽东主席22日致电挪威王国国王奥拉夫，吊唁挪威王国国王哈康七世逝世。电文如下：“挪威王国国王奥拉夫陛下：
    顷悉挪威王国国王哈康七世陛下不幸逝世，谨致哀悼。
            中华人民共和国主席毛泽东　1957年9月22日北京”
    新华社23日讯　周恩来外长22日致电挪威王国政府外交大臣兰格，吊唁挪威王国国王哈康七世逝世。电文如下。“挪威王国政府外交大臣兰格先生阁下：
    顷悉挪威王国国王哈康七世陛下不幸逝世，特此电唁。
        中华人民共和国外交部部长周恩来　1957年9月22日北京”
    新华社23日讯　外交部副部长章汉夫和礼宾司副司长张世杰今天下午到挪威驻华大使馆，就挪威王国国王哈康七世逝世向克洛格—亨生大使表示哀悼。
