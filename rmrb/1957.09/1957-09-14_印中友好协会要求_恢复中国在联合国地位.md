Title: India-China Friendship Association Demands Restoration of China's Status in the United Nations
Author:
Date: 1957-09-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 13th—The National Executive Committee of the India-China Friendship Association passed a resolution at a meeting held here demanding that "steps be taken immediately to grant the People's Republic of China the position it deserves in the United Nations without further delay."

Mrs. Uma Nehru, Acting President of the India-China Friendship Association, said in a telegram to the Secretary-General of the United Nations on September 11, "This issue has remained unresolved. In order to meet the wishes of the vast people of the world and to seek satisfactory solutions to many issues closely related to world peace, this issue must be properly resolved."

### 印中友好协会要求  恢复中国在联合国地位

1957-09-14
第6版()
专栏：

    印中友好协会要求
　　恢复中国在联合国地位
    新华社新德里13日电  印中友好协会全国执行委员会在这里举行的会议上通过一项决议，要求“立即采取步骤，不再拖延给予中华人民共和国在联合国中应得的地位。”
    印中友好协会代理主席乌玛·尼赫鲁夫人在9月11日致联合国秘书长的电文中说，“这个问题一直未获解决。为了满足世界广大人民的愿望，以及为了求得与世界和平密切有关的许多问题的圆满解决，必须把这个问题适当解决。”
