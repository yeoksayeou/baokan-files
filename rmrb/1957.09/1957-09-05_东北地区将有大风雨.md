Title: Strong Winds and Rain Expected in Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1957-09-05
Page: 4
Meteorological Observatory

    Strong Winds and Rain Expected in Northeast China

    (Reported by this newspaper) The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a gale warning at 7:00 PM on the 4th: Affected by a low-pressure system moving from the Mongolian People's Republic, in the next 24 hours, eastern Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Heilongjiang Province, northern Liaoning Province, and northern Jilin Province will experience successively level 6-8 gales from west to east, with the wind direction turning from southerly to northerly. Southern Liaoning Province and southern Jilin Province will also experience level 5-7 gales. Central and western Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region will continue to experience level 6-8 westerly winds. Central Heilongjiang and the middle and upper reaches of the Nenjiang River and other areas will have heavy rain.

    In addition, at 2:00 PM today, the center of the typhoon in the Pacific Ocean was located near 28 degrees north latitude and 132.3 degrees east longitude, with a maximum wind force near the center of 40 meters per second (above level 12). Within 600 kilometers of the typhoon center, there are level 6 or higher gales on the sea. Currently, the typhoon center is moving in a west-northwest direction at a speed of 5-7 knots per hour. In the next 24 hours, the East China Sea and the southeastern Yellow Sea will experience level 6-9 northeasterly winds.

### 东北地区将有大风雨

1957-09-05
第4版()
专栏：气象台

    东北地区将有大风雨
    本报讯　4日下午七时，中央气象科学研究所发布大风消息：受由蒙古人民共和国移来的低气压影响，未来二十四小时内，内蒙古自治区的东部、黑龙江省、辽宁省北部和吉林省北部自西向东将先后有6—8级的大风，风向偏南转偏北。辽宁省南部和吉林省南部也将有5—7级的大风。内蒙古自治区的中部和西部仍将有6—8级的偏西风。黑龙江中部和嫩江中、上游等地区将有大雨。
    另外，今天下午二时，太平洋上的台风中心位置在北纬28度，东经132.3度附近，中心附近最大风力为每秒四十米（十二级以上）。距台风中心六百公里以内的海面上，有六级以上的大风。目前台风中心正以每小时5—7浬的速度向偏西方向移动。未来二十四小时东海面上和黄海东南部将有6—9级的东北风。
