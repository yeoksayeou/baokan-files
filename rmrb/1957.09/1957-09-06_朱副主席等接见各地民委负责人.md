Title: Zhu Vice Chairman and Others Receive Heads of Local Ethnic Affairs Commissions
Author:
Date: 1957-09-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 5th: Vice Chairman Zhu De and Vice Chairmen Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, and Chen Shutong received this afternoon the representatives attending the conference of directors of ethnic affairs commissions from various provinces and cities held in Beijing, as well as the ethnic minority visiting delegation from Jilin Province.

### 朱副主席等接见各地民委负责人

1957-09-06
第4版()
专栏：

    朱副主席等接见各地民委负责人
    新华社5日讯　朱德副主席和李济深、黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通副委员长，今天下午接见了参加在北京举行的各省市民族事务委员会主任会议的代表和吉林省各民族参观团。
