Title: Equal and Reciprocal Entry of Chinese and American Journalists for Interviews; Chinese Delegation Holds Press Conference After Sino-US Talks
Author:
Date: 1957-09-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 12th—Following the seventieth meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks, the Chinese delegation held a press conference at the Chinese Consulate General in Geneva at 2 p.m. today, announcing the suggestion it made to the US side at this morning's meeting regarding the mutual permission for journalists from both China and the United States to enter each other's countries for news coverage on the basis of equality and reciprocity.

Approximately twenty foreign journalists attended the press conference, including representatives from TASS, United Press, Associated Press, Reuters, Agence France-Presse, ANSA News Agency, The Times of London, The New York Times, and several broadcasting companies.

### 中美记者入境采访要平等互惠  中国代表团在中美会谈后举行记者招待会

1957-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　中美记者入境采访要平等互惠
　　中国代表团在中美会谈后举行记者招待会
    新华社日内瓦12日电  在中美大使级会谈第七十次会议后，中国代表团今天下午两点在日内瓦中国总领事馆举行记者招待会，发表了它在今天上午会议上向美方提出的关于中美双方在平等互惠基础上允许对方记者入境采访新闻的建议。
    大约二十名外国记者参加了记者招待会，其中包括塔斯社、合众社、美联社、路透社、法新社、安莎通讯社、伦敦泰晤士报、纽约时报的代表和几家广播公司的记者。
