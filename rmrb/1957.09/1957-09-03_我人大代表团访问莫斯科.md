Title: Chinese NPC Delegation Visits Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-09-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 2nd: According to a TASS report: The Chinese National People's Congress delegation headed by Saifudin arrived in Moscow on September 2nd from Leningrad after visiting Finland. The delegation will visit the Soviet capital for a few days.

### 我人大代表团访问莫斯科

1957-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我人大代表团访问莫斯科
    据新华社2日讯  据塔斯社报道：以赛福鼎为首的中国全国人民代表大会代表团在访问芬兰后，于9月2日从列宁格勒到达莫斯科。代表团将在苏联首都访问几天。
