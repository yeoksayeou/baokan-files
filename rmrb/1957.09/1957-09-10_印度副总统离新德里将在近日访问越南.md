Title: Indian Vice President Leaves New Delhi, to Visit Vietnam Soon
Author:
Date: 1957-09-10
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, September 9th: The Vietnamese President's Office issued a communique today stating: At the invitation of Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Vice President of the Republic of India, Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan, will officially visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on September 12, 1957.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, September 8th: The Vice President of the Republic of India, Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan, left New Delhi by plane this morning to visit several Asian countries. He will visit Cambodia, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Laos, China, Mongolia, and South Vietnam. Indian Prime Minister Nehru, Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili, and diplomatic envoys from other countries were at the airport to see him off.

### 印度副总统离新德里将在近日访问越南

1957-09-10
第7版()
专栏：

    印度副总统离新德里将在近日访问越南
    新华社河内9日电　越南主席府今天发表公报说：应越南民主共和国胡志明主席的邀请，印度共和国副总统拉达克里希南将在1957年9月12日正式访问越南民主共和国。
    新华社新德里8日电印度共和国副总统拉达克里希南今天上午乘飞机离开新德里前往若干亚洲国家访问。他将要访问柬埔寨、越南民主共和国、老挝、中国、蒙古、南越。印度总理尼赫鲁、中国驻印度大使潘自力和其他国家外交使节曾到机场送行。
