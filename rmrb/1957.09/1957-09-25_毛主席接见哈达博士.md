Title: Chairman Mao Receives Dr. Hatta

Author:

Date: 1957-09-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th – Chairman Mao Zedong received former Indonesian Vice President Dr. Hatta and his wife this evening, along with the Indonesian dignitaries accompanying him on his visit to China.

    Present at the meeting were Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman Peng Zhen, and Vice Minister Zhang Wentian.

    Also present at the meeting was the Indonesian Ambassador to China, Wiyopranoto.

    During the meeting, Dr. Hatta presented gifts to Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Vice Chairman Peng Zhen. (Picture attached)

    Below: Chairman Mao receives Dr. Hatta. Front row, from left to right: Zhang Wentian, Iskandar, Zhou Enlai, Wiyopranoto, Zhu De, Hatta's daughter, Mrs. Hatta, Mao Zedong, Hatta, Liu Shaoqi, Mrs. Wiyopranoto, Mononutu, Peng Zhen, Sumodiwiryo.
        Xinhua News Agency reporter Hou Bo, Zou Jiandong, photographed.

### 毛主席接见哈达博士

1957-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见哈达博士
    新华社24日讯　毛泽东主席在今天晚间接见了印度尼西亚前副总统哈达博士和夫人以及随同他来中国访问的印度尼西亚贵宾。
    接见时在座的有朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理、彭真副委员长和张闻天副部长。
    接见的时候，印度尼西亚驻中国大使维约普拉诺托也在座。
    哈达博士在接见时向毛泽东主席、朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理和彭真副委员长赠送了礼品。（附图片）
