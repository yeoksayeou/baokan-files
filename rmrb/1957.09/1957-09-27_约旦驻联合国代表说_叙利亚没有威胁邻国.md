Title: Jordanian Representative to the UN Says Syria Does Not Threaten Neighboring Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-09-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 26th - Jordanian delegation leader to the UN General Assembly, Heikal, issued a statement to the press on the 25th saying: "Arab countries do not believe that Syria poses a military threat to any other country."

### 约旦驻联合国代表说  叙利亚没有威胁邻国

1957-09-27
第6版()
专栏：

    约旦驻联合国代表说
    叙利亚没有威胁邻国
    据新华社26日讯　纽约消息：约旦出席联合国大会代表团团长赫卡尔25日向新闻界发表声明说：“阿拉伯国家并不认为叙利亚构成对任何别的国家的军事威胁。”
