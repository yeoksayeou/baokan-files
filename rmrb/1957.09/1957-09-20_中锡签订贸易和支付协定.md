Title: China and Ceylon Sign Trade and Payment Agreement
Author:
Date: 1957-09-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th - China and Ceylon governments today in Beijing signed the Sino-Ceylon Five-Year Trade and Payment Agreement (1958 to 1962). The first Sino-Ceylon Five-Year Trade Agreement will expire at the end of this year.
    Premier Zhou Enlai attended the signing ceremony.
    The Sino-Ceylon 1958 barter protocol and economic assistance agreement were also signed simultaneously today.
    Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jiuzhuang signed the above three agreements on behalf of the Chinese government. The Ambassador of Ceylon to China, Mr. Pereira, signed on behalf of the Ceylon government.
    After the signing ceremony, Ambassador Pereira said in his speech: "The agreement we signed today will undoubtedly further strengthen the friendship between the two countries."
    Minister Ye Jiuzhuang said in his speech: "In the past nearly five years, China and Ceylon have carried out satisfactory cooperation on the basis of mutual exchange of needed goods. In this negotiation, both sides, in the spirit of the Bandung Conference, conducted sincere discussions based on the principles of equality and mutual benefit, mutual assistance and mutual support. The negotiation proceeded very harmoniously and smoothly."
    Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin also attended today's signing ceremony.
    Mr. T.B. Subasinghe, co-leader of the Ceylon government trade delegation, and Mr. Ranganberg, Permanent Secretary of the Ceylon Ministry of Commerce and Trade, also attended the signing ceremony.
    Also present were the negotiators from both sides who participated in this trade negotiation.
    Mr. Wijesinghe, the other co-leader of the Ceylon government trade delegation, has already left Beijing for home.

### 中锡签订贸易和支付协定

1957-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　中锡签订贸易和支付协定
    新华社19日讯　中国和锡兰政府今天在北京签订了中锡五年贸易和支付协定（1958年到1962年）。第一个中锡五年贸易协定将在今年年底满期。
    周恩来总理参加了签字仪式。
    在今天同时签字的还有中锡两国1958年换货议定书和经济援助协定。
    对外贸易部部长叶季壮代表中国政府在上述三个协定上签字。代表锡兰政府签字的是锡兰驻中国大使佩雷拉。
    在签字仪式后，佩雷拉大使致词说：“今天我们签订的协定无疑的将会进一步加强两国间的友谊。”
    叶季壮部长在致词中说：“在过去将近五年的时期里，中锡两国在互通有无的基础上，进行了满意的合作。在这次谈判中，双方本着万隆会议的精神，根据平等互利、互相帮助、互相支持的原则进行了诚恳的商谈，谈判的进行是很融洽和顺利的。”
    参加今天签字仪式的还有外交部副部长章汉夫、对外贸易部副部长雷任民。
    锡兰政府贸易代表团联合团长特·勃·苏巴辛格和锡兰商业贸易部常任秘书兰根贝格也出席了签字仪式。
    出席的还有双方参加这次贸易的谈判人员。
    锡兰政府贸易代表团另一联合团长维杰辛格已先期离开北京回国。
