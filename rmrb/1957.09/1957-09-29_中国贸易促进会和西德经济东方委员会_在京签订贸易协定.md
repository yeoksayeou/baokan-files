Title: China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and West German Eastern Economic Committee Sign Trade Agreement in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th. The China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and the delegation of the German Eastern Economic Committee of the Federal Republic of Germany, who were invited to visit our country, signed the trade agreement between the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and the German Eastern Economic Committee in Beijing this afternoon after friendly talks based on the common desire to develop trade between the People's Republic of China and the Federal Republic of Germany. Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and Otto Wolff, Chairman of the German Eastern Economic Committee, signed the agreement.

Attending the signing ceremony were Lei Renmin, Li Zhuchen, and Ji Chaoding, Vice-Chairmen of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Lu Xuzhang, a member of the Council, Zhan Wu, Deputy General Manager of the Bank of China, and all members of the delegation of the German Eastern Economic Committee of the Federal Republic of Germany.

### 中国贸易促进会和西德经济东方委员会  在京签订贸易协定

1957-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    中国贸易促进会和西德经济东方委员会
    在京签订贸易协定
    新华社27日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会和应邀来我国访问的德意志联邦共和国德国经济东方委员会代表团，基于发展中华人民共和国和德意志联邦共和国两国贸易的共同愿望，经过友好的洽谈，今天下午在北京签订了中国国际贸易促进委员会和德意志经济东方委员会贸易协定。在协定书上签字的是中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸和德意志经济东方委员会主席奥托·沃尔夫。
    出席签字仪式的有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民、李烛尘、冀朝鼎，委员卢绪章，中国银行副总经理詹武和德意志联邦共和国德国经济东方委员会代表团的全体人员。
