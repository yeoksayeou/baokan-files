Title: Premier Zhou Enlai's Welcome Speech at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1957-09-28
Page: 4

Premier Zhou Enlai's Welcome Speech at the Airport

Dear Comrade János Kádár, Prime Minister of the Hungarian People's Republic's Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government, dear comrades of the Government Delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic, comrades, friends:

Today, we are here with particularly joyous and cordial feelings to welcome the representatives of the heroic Hungarian people – the Government Delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic headed by Prime Minister Kádár – who have come to our country for a friendly visit at the invitation of the Chinese government. First, please allow me, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, to extend a warm welcome and our highest respect to our distinguished guests who have come from afar.

Dear Comrade Prime Minister and comrades of the delegation, I sincerely thank you for bringing the precious friendship of the fraternal Hungarian people to the Chinese people. During your visit to our country, you will surely feel everywhere the friendship and love of the Chinese people for the fraternal Hungarian people. The friendship between our two peoples, built on the basis of proletarian internationalism, is unbreakable.

Comrade Prime Minister, this visit to our country by the Government Delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic, headed by you, will surely further develop the friendly and cooperative relations between our two countries, and at the same time will make a significant contribution to strengthening the unity of socialist countries and safeguarding the noble cause of world peace. Please allow me to once again express my welcome and gratitude to you.

Long live the unbreakable fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Hungarian people!

Long live the unity of the socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union!

Long live world peace!

### 在机场上周恩来总理致欢迎词

1957-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

    在机场上周恩来总理致欢迎词亲爱的匈牙利人民共和国工农革命政府总理卡达尔同志，亲爱的匈牙利人民共和国政府代表团同志们，同志们，朋友们：
    今天，我们怀着特别愉快和亲切的心情来迎接应中国政府的邀请前来我国进行友好访问的英勇的匈牙利人民的代表——以卡达尔总理为首的匈牙利人民共和国政府代表团。首先，请允许我代表中华人民共和国政府和中国人民向我们远道而来的贵宾们表示热烈的欢迎和崇高的敬意。
    亲爱的总理同志和代表团的同志们，我衷心地感谢你们带来了兄弟的匈牙利人民对中国人民的珍贵友谊。你们在我国访问期间一定会到处感到中国人民对兄弟的匈牙利人民的友情和热爱。建筑在无产阶级国际主义基础上的我们两国人民之间的友谊是牢不可破的。
    总理同志，以你为首的匈牙利人民共和国政府代表团这次来我国访问，一定会进一步发展我们两国间的友好合作关系，同时对于加强社会主义各国的团结和维护世界和平的崇高事业也一定会作出重大的贡献。请允许我再一次向你们表示欢迎和感谢。
    中匈两国人民牢不可破的兄弟友谊万岁！
    以苏联为首的社会主义各国的团结万岁！
    世界和平万岁！（附图片）
    卡达尔总理（前左第二人）和费林格主席（前左第三人）在机场上受到群众的热烈欢迎。　新华社记者　齐观山摄
