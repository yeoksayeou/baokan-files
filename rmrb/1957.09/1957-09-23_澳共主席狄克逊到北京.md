Title: Australian Communist Party Chairman Dixon Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-23
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, 22nd: The Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Australia, Dixon, member of the Political Bureau, Campbell, and member of the Central Committee, Chang De Le, who are here to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.
    Greeting them at the airport were Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee, among others.

### 澳共主席狄克逊到北京

1957-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    澳共主席狄克逊到北京
    新华社22日讯　前来中国访问的澳大利亚共产党中央委员会主席狄克逊、政治局委员坎贝尔和中央委员张德勒今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中共中央总书记邓小平、中共中央书记处书记王稼祥和中央委员刘宁一等。
