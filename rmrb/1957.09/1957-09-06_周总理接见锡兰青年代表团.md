Title: Premier Zhou Receives Ceylon Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-09-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 5th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the Ceylon Youth Delegation this afternoon, headed by Piyasena Tennakoon, Deputy Speaker of the Ceylon Senate. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation after participating in the World Youth and Students Peace and Friendship Festival.

    Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Liu Xiyuan, Wu Xueqian, Zhang Wenjin and others.

    Ceylon's Ambassador to China, W. A. Perera, was also present at the reception.

### 周总理接见锡兰青年代表团

1957-09-06
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理接见锡兰青年代表团
    新华社5日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了以锡兰参议院副议长皮亚塞纳·膝纳昆为首的锡兰青年代表团。代表团是在参加了世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节后，应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国访问的。
    接见时在座的有廖承志、刘西元、吴学谦、章文晋等人。
    接见时，锡兰驻华大使威·阿·佩雷拉也在座。
