Title: "August 1st" Basketball Team Achieves Six Wins in Seven Matches in Czechoslovakia; Loses Final Game to Czechoslovakian National Team
Author:
Date: 1957-09-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 15th: The Chinese "August 1st" men's basketball team lost to the Czechoslovakian National Team 44-52 in a fierce battle in the rain on the evening of the 13th. With this, the "August 1st" team's visit to Czechoslovakia has come to an end, achieving an excellent record of six wins in seven matches.
During its month-long visit to Romania and Czechoslovakia, the "August 1st" team played a total of thirteen games, achieving twelve victories. They will depart for home today.

### “八一”篮球队在捷七战六胜  最后一场比赛败于捷国家代表队

1957-09-16
第3版()
专栏：体育新闻

　　“八一”篮球队在捷七战六胜
    最后一场比赛败于捷国家代表队
    据新华社布拉格15日电  中国“八一”男子篮球队13日晚在一场冒雨激战中以四十四比五十二败于捷克斯洛伐克国家代表队。至此“八一”队在捷克斯洛伐克的访问比赛全部结束，取得了七战六胜的优异成绩。
    “八一”队在访问罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克两国的一个月期间，共比赛了十三场，取得了十二次胜利。他们将于今日启程回国。
