Title: Sino-Bulgarian Talks Held
Author:
Date: 1957-09-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 15th: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, Yugov, and Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, held talks this afternoon.

　　On the Bulgarian side, the members of the delegation who participated in the talks were: First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Traikov; Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the National Assembly, Ganev; Minister of Trade, Taskov; Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lukanov; Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Negalkov; and advisors to the delegation.

　　On the Chinese side, those who participated in the talks were: Vice Premier of the State Council, Li Xiannian; Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zeng Yongquan; Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Li Zheren; and Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria, Zhou Zhuan.

### 中保双方举行会谈

1957-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　中保双方举行会谈
    新华社15日讯  保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席于哥夫和中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在今天下午举行了会谈。
    保加利亚方面参加会谈的，有代表团团员：部长会议第一副主席特莱科夫、国民议会外交委员会主席加涅夫、贸易部长塔斯科夫、外交部长卢卡诺夫、保加利亚驻中国大使涅加尔科夫，以及代表团的顾问。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有国务院副总理李先念，外交部副部长曾涌泉，对外贸易部副部长李哲人和中国驻保加利亚大使周竹安。
