Title: Nan Hanchen Hosts Banquet for West German Economic Eastern Committee Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1957-09-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th Dispatch: Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the German Economic Eastern Committee delegation from the Federal Republic of Germany, headed by Otto Wolff. The delegation paid a courtesy call on Nan Hanchen this morning.

### 南汉宸宴请西德经济东方委员会代表团

1957-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    南汉宸宴请西德经济东方委员会代表团
    据新华社9日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸今日晚设宴欢迎以奥托沃尔夫为首的德意志联邦共和国德国经济东方委员会代表团。这个代表团在今天上午拜会了南汉宸。
