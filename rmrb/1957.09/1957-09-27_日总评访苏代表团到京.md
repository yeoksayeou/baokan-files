Title: Japanese General Council of Trade Unions (Sohyo) Delegation to China Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1957-09-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: The Japanese General Council of Trade Unions (Sohyo) delegation, after visiting the Soviet Union, arrived in Beijing yesterday by plane at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.  Dong Xin, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and responsible persons from the postal and telecommunications, heavy industry, food, education, coal mining, and highway transportation trade unions went to the airport to welcome them.

The head of the delegation, Iwai Akira, after visiting the Soviet Union, will travel to Asian and African countries, and is scheduled to visit China on October 7th. The delegation will participate in the National Day celebrations in Beijing and make friendly visits to various places.

### 日总评访苏代表团到京

1957-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    日总评访苏代表团到京
    新华社26日讯　日本工会总评议会访苏代表团在访问苏联后，应中华全国总工会邀请在昨天乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会书记处书记董昕以及邮电、重工业、食品、教育、煤矿、公路运输等产业工会负责人。
    代表团团长岩井章在访苏后，转往亚非各国访问，预定10月7日来我国访问。代表团将在北京参加我国国庆节观礼。并到各地作友好访问。
