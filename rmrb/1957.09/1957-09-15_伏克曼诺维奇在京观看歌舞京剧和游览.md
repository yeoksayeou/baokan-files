Title: Vukmanović Watches Song and Dance Peking Opera and Sightsees in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th: The Vice-President of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia, Svetozar Vukmanović, and his entourage attended a song and dance Peking opera performance held by the Ministry of Culture at Huairentang tonight.

The Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popović, was also invited to attend the evening performance.

Accompanying them to watch the performance were Vice Premier Chen Yun, Vice Premier Bo Yibo, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, among others.

This afternoon, Vice President Vukmanović and others, accompanied by Vice Premier Bo Yibo, visited the Summer Palace in the western suburbs. The distinguished guests boated on Kunming Lake and climbed the Tower of Buddhist Incense to overlook the scenery of the garden. After returning to the city, Vice President Vukmanović and others also visited the Forbidden City and the Temple of Heaven.

### 伏克曼诺维奇在京观看歌舞京剧和游览

1957-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　伏克曼诺维奇在京观看歌舞京剧和游览
    新华社14日讯  南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席斯·伏克曼诺维奇和他的随行人员，今晚出席了文化部在怀仁堂举行的歌舞京剧晚会。
    南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇也应邀出席了晚会。
    陪同观看表演的，有陈云副总理、薄一波副总理和文化部部长沈雁冰，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长曾涌泉等人。
    今天下午，伏克曼诺维奇副主席等由薄一波副总理陪同游览了西郊的颐和园。贵宾们在昆明湖上泛舟，又登上佛香阁眺望了园内景色。在返回市区以后，伏克曼诺维奇副主席等又游览了故宫和天坛。
