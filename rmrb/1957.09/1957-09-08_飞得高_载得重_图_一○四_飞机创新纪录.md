Title: Flying High, Carrying Heavy Loads: “Tu-104” Aircraft Sets New Records
Author:
Date: 1957-09-08
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 7th: According to TASS, the Soviet "Tu-104" aircraft set two world records in altitude and load capacity on the 6th: carrying a load of 21,000 kilograms, it ascended to a height of 11,800 meters.

The previous world records belonged to a French pilot who, flying a seaplane, carried a load of 18,000 kilograms to a height of 2,000 meters, and to an American pilot who, flying a land-based "B-29" "Flying Fortress," carried a load of 15,166 kilograms to the same height.

### 飞得高  载得重  “图—一○四”飞机创新纪录

1957-09-08
第7版()
专栏：

    飞得高  载得重
    “图—一○四”飞机创新纪录
    据新华社7日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联“图—一○四”飞机6日在高度和载重方面创造了两项世界纪录：载重二万一千公斤，升至一万一千八百公尺的高空。
    以前的世界纪录属于法国的飞行员，他乘水上飞机载重一万八千公斤上升至二千公尺的高空，和美国的飞行员，他乘陆用飞机“B—二九”“飞行堡垒”载重一万五千一百六十六公斤升至同样高度。
