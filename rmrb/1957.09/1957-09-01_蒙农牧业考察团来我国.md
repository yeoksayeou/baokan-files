Title: Mongolian Agricultural and Animal Husbandry Delegation Arrives in China
Author:
Date: 1957-09-01
Page: 4

    Mongolian Agricultural and Animal Husbandry Delegation Arrives in China
    Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, August 31st. The Mongolian Agricultural and Animal Husbandry Delegation, visiting China under the 1957 Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, arrived in Hohhot this morning.

### 蒙农牧业考察团来我国

1957-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙农牧业考察团来我国
    据新华社呼和浩特31日电  根据1957年中蒙文化合作协定来我国访问的蒙古农牧业考察团，今天上午到达呼和浩特市。
