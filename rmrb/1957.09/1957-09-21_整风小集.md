Title: Rectification Snippets
Author: Fan Yiben
Date: 1957-09-21
Page: 8

    Rectification Snippets
    Fan Yiben, Beijing State-owned Cotton Mill No. 1
    Spring Thunder
    The rectification movement is like spring thunder shaking the world,
    We immediately step forward to chase the thunder,
    The words we want to say are like spring rain,
    Washing away the dust from the Party!
    Bulletin Board
    Who is wielding the iron hammer under the moonlight,
    Erecting rows of wooden posts,
    Nailing bamboo boards onto the pillars,
    Ah! It is our Party committee building a bulletin board!
    Colored Clouds
    Big-character posters cover the mountain-like bulletin board,
    Like thousands of colored clouds floating in the sky!
    But these clouds do not come from the sky,
    They come from the heart of every worker!
    Night Rain
    Who is he? Carrying a large roll of fine matting on his shoulder,
    Dashing through the storm and heavy rain,
    To prevent the big-character posters from being blown away by the wind,
    The Party Secretary gets up in the middle of the night to protect them!

### 整风小集
范以本
1957-09-21
第8版()
专栏：

    整风小集
    北京国棉一厂　范以本
    春雷
    整风运动像春雷响彻天下，
    我们立刻迈步把雷声追赶，
    我们要说的话是春天的雨水，
    冲洗掉党身上的灰尘！
    报棚
    是谁披着月光舞起铁锤，
    把一排排木头竖起，
    把一块块竹板钉上木柱，
    啊！那是我们的党委在搭报棚！
    彩云
    大字报贴满了山峰似的报棚，
    像天空飘游着千万朵彩云！
    但这云彩不是来自天际，
    是来自每个工人的心！
    夜雨
    他是谁？肩上扛着一大卷细席，
    冒着暴风急雨飞奔，
    为了不使大字报叫风卷走，
    党委书记半夜起来把它保卫！
