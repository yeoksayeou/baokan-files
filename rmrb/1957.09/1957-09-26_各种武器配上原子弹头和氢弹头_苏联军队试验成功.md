Title: Various Weapons Equipped with Atomic and Hydrogen Warheads Successfully Tested by Soviet Army
Author:
Date: 1957-09-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 25th, TASS dispatch: In accordance with the Soviet Army and Navy military training plan, the Soviet Union conducted explosions of atomic and hydrogen warheads on various weapons.

The military exercises were very successful. For the safety of residents, these explosions were carried out in uninhabited areas and at high altitudes.

### 各种武器配上原子弹头和氢弹头  苏联军队试验成功

1957-09-26
第6版()
专栏：

    各种武器配上原子弹头和氢弹头
    苏联军队试验成功
    新华社25日讯　塔斯社莫斯科25日讯：根据苏联陆军和海军军事训练计划，苏联进行了各种武器上的原子弹头和氢弹头的爆炸。
    军队的演习进行得很成功。为了居民的安全，这种爆炸是在无人地区和高空进行的。
