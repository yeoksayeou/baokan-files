Title: Taiyuan-Zhongwei Railway Begins Surveying
Author:
Date: 1957-09-24
Page: 5

Gleanings

    Taiyuan-Zhongwei Railway Begins Surveying
    The surveying of the railway from Taiyuan, Shanxi to Zhongwei, Gansu has begun.
    According to preliminary selections by surveyors, this railway will cross the Yellow River westward from Taiyuan, pass north of Yan'an, traverse the Loess Plateau in northern Shaanxi, and then enter Gansu Province, with a total length of over 900 kilometers.
    This railway will be an economic artery between North and Northwest China, and it is also of great significance for the prosperity of the old revolutionary base areas in northern Shaanxi.

### 太原—中卫铁路开始勘测

1957-09-24
第5版()
专栏：东鳞西爪

    太原—中卫铁路开始勘测
    从山西太原到甘肃中卫的铁路已经开始勘测。
    据踏勘人员初步选择，这条铁路从太原向西跨过黄河，经过延安的北面，穿行在陕北黄土高原上，然后进入甘肃省，全长九百多公里。
    这条铁路，将是华北、西北间的经济大动脉，这条铁路对繁荣陕北老区也有很大的意义。
