Date: 1957-09-17
Page: 5

　　Important Correction
　　The third point in the CCP Central Committee’s instruction on implementing the policy of mutual benefit within agricultural cooperatives, published on page four of this newspaper on September 16th, read: “Certain special means of production, such as fish ponds, reed marshes, orchards ...” The words “reed marshes” should be deleted.

### 重要更正

1957-09-17
第5版()
专栏：

　　重要更正
　　本报9月16日四版刊登中共中央关于在农业合作社内部贯彻执行互利政策的指示中的第三点：“某些特殊的生产资料，如鱼塘、苇地、果园 ……”其中“苇地”两字应删去。
