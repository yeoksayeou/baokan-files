Title: Vice Chairman Zhu and Premier Zhou Receive German Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-09-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 8th. Vice Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai received the entire delegation of the German Democratic Republic military delegation headed by General Willi Stoph this evening. The hosts and guests had a cordial conversation for more than an hour. Present at the reception were: Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Marshal Peng Dehuai, Deputy Minister of National Defense General Tan Zheng, General Li Da, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, and our country's military attaché to Germany Colonel Song Denghua. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan and Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the German Democratic Republic to China, Bielbach, were also present at the reception.
After the reception, the German Democratic Republic military delegation presented gifts to Vice Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai, and took a group photo as a souvenir. (Photo attached)

### 朱副主席和周总理接见德国军事代表团

1957-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    朱副主席和周总理接见德国军事代表团
    新华社8日讯　朱德副主席、周恩来总理今晚接见了以维利·斯多夫上将为首的德意志民主共和国军事代表团全体人员。宾主双方亲切地交谈了一个多小时。接见时在座的有：副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅，国防部副部长谭政大将、李达上将，国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将和我国驻德武官宋登华上校。接见时，外交部副部长曾涌泉和德意志民主共和国驻华临时代办比尔巴哈也在座。
    接见后，德意志民主共和国军事代表团向朱德副主席、周恩来总理赠送了礼品，并且合影留念。（附图片）
