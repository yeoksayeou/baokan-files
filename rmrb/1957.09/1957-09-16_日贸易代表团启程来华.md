Title: Japanese Trade Delegation Departs for China
Author:
Date: 1957-09-16
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 15th: The Japanese trade delegation to China, led by the head of the delegation, Masanosuke Ikeda, left Tokyo for China this morning. The delegation, composed of representatives from the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, the Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade, and the Japan-China Import and Export Association, will negotiate with relevant Chinese parties on the issue of concluding the fourth Japan-China trade agreement.

### 日贸易代表团启程来华

1957-09-16
第6版()
专栏：

    日贸易代表团启程来华
    据新华社15日讯  东京消息：日本访华贸易代表团在团长池田正之辅率领下，今天清晨离开东京前往中国。这个由日本国际贸易促进协会、日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟和日中输出入协会三个团体的代表组成的代表团，将同中国有关方面就缔结第四次日中贸易协定问题进行谈判。
