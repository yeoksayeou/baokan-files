Title: Monkey Picking Watermelons (Picture)
Author: Zhang Lu
Date: 1957-09-18
Page: 8

　　Monkey Picking Watermelons (Woodcut)
　　Zhang Lu
　　I. The monkey wanted to pick the biggest watermelon in the field.
　　II. "This one is small, that one is big."
　　III. "This one is small, that one is even bigger."
　　IV. "This must be the biggest!"
    V. "Oh! I didn't expect the watermelon floating in the river to be the biggest."
    VI. The biggest watermelon is still on the bridge, but the monkey has jumped into the water.

### 猴子拣西瓜（图片）
张路
1957-09-18
第8版()
专栏：

　　猴子拣西瓜（木刻）
　　张路
　　一、猴子在田里想拣个最大的西瓜。
　　二、“这个小，那个大。”
　　三、“这个小，那个更大。”
　　四、“这该是最大的了！”
    五、“咦！想不到河里飘着的西瓜，才是最大的。”
    六、最大的西瓜还在桥上，可是猴子呢，跳下水里去了。
