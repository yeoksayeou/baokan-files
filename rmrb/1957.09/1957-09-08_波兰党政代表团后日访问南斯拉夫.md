Title: Polish Party and Government Delegation to Visit Yugoslavia the Day After Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1957-09-08
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 7th - According to the Polish Press Agency, a delegation from the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and the Polish government will pay a seven-day official visit to Yugoslavia starting on September 10th, at the invitation of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, and of the Yugoslav government. The delegation will be led by First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Gomulka, and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz.

Members of the delegation include: Polish Foreign Minister Rapacki, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Minister of Agriculture Ochab, and Polish Ambassador to Yugoslavia Grochulski.

### 波兰党政代表团后日访问南斯拉夫

1957-09-08
第7版()
专栏：

    波兰党政代表团后日访问南斯拉夫
    新华社华沙7日电　据波兰通讯社报道，波兰统一工人党中央委员会和波兰政府代表团，应南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会和南斯拉夫政府的邀请，将在9月10日前往南斯拉夫作为期七天的正式访问。代表团由波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记哥穆尔卡和部长会议主席西伦凯维兹率领。
    代表团成员包括：波兰外交部长拉帕茨基，波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员、农业部长奥哈布和波兰驻南斯拉夫大使格罗胡耳斯基。
