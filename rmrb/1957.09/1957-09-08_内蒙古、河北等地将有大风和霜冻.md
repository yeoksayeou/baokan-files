Title: Strong Winds and Frost Expected in Inner Mongolia, Hebei, and Other Regions
Author:
Date: 1957-09-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 7th - The Central Meteorological Research Institute has issued a forecast for strong winds and frost:

    Affected by the southward movement of cold air, it is predicted that in the next 24 hours, there will be northerly winds of force 6 or 7 in central Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the Bohai Sea, the Yellow Sea, the southern part of the Liaodong Peninsula, and the eastern part of the Shandong Peninsula.

    It is predicted that on the morning of the 8th, after the wind stops and the sky clears in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and the Zhangjiakou Special Zone and the northern part of the Chengde Special Zone of Hebei Province, the minimum temperature will drop to 5 degrees Celsius or below. There will be frost and frost damage in the above areas. In the Ulanqab League, Hetao, Pingdiquan, Chahar League, and Zhaowuda League of Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the minimum temperature will drop to 0 degrees Celsius to -2 degrees Celsius.

### 内蒙古、河北等地将有大风和霜冻

1957-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

    内蒙古、河北等地将有大风和霜冻
    据新华社7日讯　中央气象科学研究所发布大风和霜冻预告：
    受冷空气南下的影响，预计未来二十四小时内，内蒙古自治区中部、渤海海面、黄海海面、辽东半岛南部、山东半岛东部将有六、七级偏北风。
    预计8日晨，内蒙古自治区和河北省的张家口专区，承德专区北部，风停天晴后，最低气温将下降到摄氏五度或五度以下，以上地区将有霜和霜冻。内蒙古自治区的乌兰察布盟、河套、平地泉、察哈尔盟和昭乌达盟最低气温将下降到摄氏零度到零下二度。
