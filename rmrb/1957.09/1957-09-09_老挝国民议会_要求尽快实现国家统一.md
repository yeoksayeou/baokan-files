Title: Lao National Assembly Demands Swift National Unification
Author:
Date: 1957-09-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 8th: Vientiane dispatch: The Lao National Assembly passed a motion on the 7th demanding that the government fulfill Prime Minister Phouma's promise in his authorization speech to incorporate the Lao Fighting Forces into the national family, so as to realize the unification of the kingdom as soon as possible.

    The motion was passed with twenty votes in favor and four abstentions.

### 老挝国民议会  要求尽快实现国家统一

1957-09-09
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝国民议会
    要求尽快实现国家统一
    新华社河内8日电　万象消息：老挝国民议会7日通过了一项动议，要求政府实现富马首相在授权演说中所作的诺言，把寮国战斗部队并入民族大家庭，以便尽速实现王国的统一。
    这项动议是以二十票赞成、四票弃权通过的。
