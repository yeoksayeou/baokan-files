Title: Prominent Figures in West Germany Jointly Issue Declaration Demanding Negotiations with East Germany
Author:
Date: 1957-09-15
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 14th: According to a report by the West German German News Agency, Count Karol Westphalen, one of the founders and chairman of the "Germans 1954 Club," and 948 other prominent figures in West Germany have jointly issued a declaration demanding that the West German government hold negotiations with the East German government and establish diplomatic and trade relations with Poland and other relevant socialist countries.
    The declaration also calls for a change in the one-sidedness of West German foreign policy, demanding that Germany maintain conservative neutrality and hold active political negotiations with the Soviet Union.
    Signatories to the declaration include: Professor Ludwig Bergsträsser, Father Fresenius, Professor Franz Lauhau, former Speaker of the Danzig Senate Rauschning, and journalism expert Professor Hagmann.
    The "Germans 1954 Club" was founded in Cologne in February 1955. The purpose of this club is to discuss international and German political issues.

### 西德著名人士联名发表宣言  要求同民主德国谈判

1957-09-15
第6版()
专栏：

　　西德著名人士联名发表宣言
　　要求同民主德国谈判
    据新华社14日讯  据西德德意志新闻社报道，“德国人1954年俱乐部”创办人之一和这个俱乐部的主席卡罗尔·威斯特伐伦伯爵和西德的九百四十八名其他著名人士联名发表了一项宣言，要求西德政府同民主德国政府举行谈判，并且和波兰以及其他有关的社会主义国家建立外交和贸易关系。
    宣言还要求改变西德外交政策中的一面倒，要求德国保守中立，以及和苏联举行积极的政治谈判。
    在宣言上签名的有：路德维希·贝格斯特拉斯教授、弗雷斯尼厄斯神父、弗朗茨·劳豪教授、革但斯克参议院前议长劳什宁和报业专家哈格曼教授。
    “德国人1954年俱乐部”是1955年2月在科隆成立的。这个俱乐部的宗旨是讨论国际和德国的政治问题。
