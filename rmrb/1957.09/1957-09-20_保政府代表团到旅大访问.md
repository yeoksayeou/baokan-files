Title: Bulgarian Government Delegation Visits Lüda
Author:
Date: 1957-09-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, September 19th: The Bulgarian government delegation, headed by Chairman Yugov, arrived in Lüda by plane this morning from Changchun, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building Cao Xiangren, and our Ambassador to Bulgaria Zhou Zhuan. They were welcomed by Lüda Mayor Song Li, CCP Lüda Municipal Committee Secretaries Bai Qingjiang and Hu Zhonghai, as well as leaders from various organizations, parties, groups, and the military. Soviet Deputy Consul in Lüda, Pridibaillo, also welcomed them at the airport.

　　In the afternoon, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Mayor Song Li, visited the Dalian Shipyard and the Lüda State-owned First Farm.

### 保政府代表团到旅大访问

1957-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　保政府代表团到旅大访问
    据新华社旅大19日电　以于哥夫主席为首的保加利亚政府代表团，今天上午由我国外交部副部长曾涌泉、第一机械工业部副部长曹祥仁和我国驻保加利亚大使周竹安等陪同，由长春乘飞机到达旅大，受到旅大市市长宋黎、中共旅大市委书记白清江、胡忠海和各机关、党派、团体以及部队的领导人的欢迎。苏联驻旅大副领事普利第巴依洛也到机场欢迎。
    当天下午，贵宾们由宋黎市长陪同参观了大连造船厂和旅大国营第一农场。
