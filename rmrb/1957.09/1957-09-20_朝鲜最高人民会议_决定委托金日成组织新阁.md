Title: Korean Supreme People's Assembly Decides to Entrust Kim Il-sung with Organizing New Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1957-09-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 19th - The first session of the Second Supreme People's Assembly of Korea today passed the following decision: 1. The meeting accepted the statement of Premier Kim Il-sung regarding returning his authority to the Supreme People's Assembly and simultaneously acknowledged that the current cabinet has satisfactorily completed its tasks. 2. The meeting entrusted Kim Il-sung to propose the organization of a new cabinet. 3. The current cabinet will continue to perform its duties until the new cabinet is approved by this meeting.

At the meeting of the First Session of the Second Supreme People's Assembly of Korea held this morning, Premier Kim Il-sung of the Korean Cabinet issued a statement returning his authority to the current Supreme People's Assembly. Deputy Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Park Jung-ai, proposed on behalf of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party that Kim Il-sung be entrusted with organizing a new cabinet. All deputies enthusiastically supported her proposal. At today's meeting, Li Hyo-soon, Chairman of the Credentials Committee, reported on the results of the review of deputies' qualifications. The meeting passed a resolution recognizing that all deputies possess the qualifications of deputies.

### 朝鲜最高人民会议  决定委托金日成组织新阁

1957-09-20
第6版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜最高人民会议
　　决定委托金日成组织新阁
    据新华社平壤19日电　朝鲜第二届最高人民会议第一次会议今天通过如下的决定：一、会议接受内阁首相金日成关于把他的权限交还给最高人民会议的声明，同时认为现任内阁圆满地完成了自己的任务。二、会议委托金日成提出组织新内阁的提案。三、在这次会议批准新内阁以前，由现任内阁继续执行任务。
    在朝鲜第二届最高人民会议第一次会议在今天上午举行的会议上，朝鲜内阁首相金日成发表了声明，把他的权限交还给本届最高人民会议。议员、朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱在会上代表朝鲜劳动党中央委员会提出建议，委托金日成组织新内阁。全体议员热烈支持她的建议。在今天的会议上，议员资格审查委员会委员长李孝淳作了关于议员资格审查结果的报告。会议通过决定，认为全部议员都具有议员的资格。
