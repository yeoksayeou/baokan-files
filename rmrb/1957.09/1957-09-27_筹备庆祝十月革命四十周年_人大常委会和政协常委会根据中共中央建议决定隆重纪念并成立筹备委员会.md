Title: Preparing to Celebrate the Fortieth Anniversary of the October Revolution: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the Standing Committee of the CPPCC Decide to Commemorate the Occasion Grandly and Establish a Preparatory Committee Based on the Suggestions of the CPC Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1957-09-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th: The 79th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the 45th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held a joint meeting today, resolving to establish the Chinese People's Preparatory Committee for Celebrating the Fortieth Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

The joint meeting's resolution was made based on the suggestion of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party. The joint meeting believes that commemorating the fortieth anniversary of the October Revolution is of extremely significant importance. The joint meeting called on people from all regions and all walks of life across the country to solemnly and grandly celebrate the fortieth anniversary of the October Revolution.

The meeting decided that the Chinese People's Preparatory Committee for Celebrating the Fortieth Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution would be composed of forty-four members. Liu Shaoqi is the Chairman, Song Qingling, Guo Moruo, and Wu Yuzhang are the Vice-Chairmen, and Qian Junrui is the Secretary-General.

The joint meeting decided that the preparatory committee should begin work immediately.

The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress also adopted at this meeting a reply to the Supreme People's Court regarding how to implement the resolution on death penalty cases being judged or approved by the Supreme People's Court. The meeting also approved appointments and removals.

### 筹备庆祝十月革命四十周年  人大常委会和政协常委会根据中共中央建议决定隆重纪念并成立筹备委员会

1957-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    筹备庆祝十月革命四十周年
    人大常委会和政协常委会根据中共中央建议决定隆重纪念并成立筹备委员会
    据新华社26日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会第七十九次会议和政协全国委员会常务委员会第四十五次会议今天举行联席会议，决议成立中国人民庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年筹备委员会。
    联席会议的这项决议是根据中共中央委员会的建议作出的。联席会议认为，纪念十月革命四十周年，具有极其重大的意义。联席会议号召全国各地和各界人民，庄严和隆重地庆祝十月革命四十周年。
    会议决定中国人民庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年筹备委员会由四十四位委员组成。刘少奇为主任委员，宋庆龄、郭沫若、吴玉章为副主任委员，钱俊瑞为秘书长。
    联席会议决定，筹备委员会即开始工作。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会这次会议还通过了关于死刑案件由最高人民法院判决或者核准的决议如何执行问题给最高人民法院的批复。会议还通过了任免事项。
