Title: Indonesian Art and Writers Delegation Invited to Visit

Author:

Date: 1957-09-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 20th. The Indonesian Maluku Art Delegation, a group of forty led by head Sain Nasution, set sail for China today for a performance tour.

The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association.

The Indonesian Writers Delegation will also fly to Beijing on September 23rd. This delegation is led by Sumarjo, Secretary-General of the Indonesian People's Cultural Association, and includes seven male and female writers.

### 印度尼西亚艺术和作家代表团应邀来访

1957-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚艺术和作家代表团应邀来访
    据新华社雅加达20日电　印度尼西亚马鲁古艺术代表团一行四十人在团长赛因·纳苏蒂安率领下今天坐船到中国去进行访问演出。
    代表团是应中国—印度尼西亚友好协会的邀请去中国访问的。
    印度尼西亚作家访华团也将在9月23日坐飞机到北京去。这个代表团由印度尼西亚人民文化协会秘书长苏马尔若率领，全团包括男女作家七人。
