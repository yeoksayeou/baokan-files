Title: Turkey Amasses Troops on Syrian Border; Syrian Premier Says Ready to Respond

Author:

Date: 1957-09-19
Page: 5

　　Turkey Amasses Troops on Syrian Border
　　Syrian Premier Says Ready to Respond

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 18th: Syrian Premier al-Assali said on the 17th that the Syrian government, people, and army are united and have preparations to cope with any eventuality.

Al-Assali made these remarks to reporters after a cabinet meeting. The meeting was presided over by President Quwatli and attended by General Bizri, Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces. The meeting heard a report from Foreign Minister Bitar on the latest developments in the situation.

Al-Assali pointed out that the cabinet meeting studied the current situation from all aspects, so as not to be caught off guard by the imperialists.

On the same day, Foreign Minister Bitar visited the Egyptian Embassy and held discussions with Egyptian diplomatic representatives on the current situation.

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, 18th: TASS reports that according to the "Telegraph," Turkey has amassed a large number of troops on the Syrian border. According to Damascus sources, nine divisions of Turkish troops have been deployed to the Syrian border area.

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 17th: "Victory Daily" today continued to publish an editorial entitled "The Chinese People Stand by Our Side," welcoming Ambassador Chen Zhifang's remarks on September 15, representing Premier Zhou Enlai and the Chinese government, expressing China's support for the Syrian people's struggle. "Victory Daily" pointed out: "This powerful support strengthens Syria's confidence in victory and helps Syria to persevere in its cause of freedom."

### 土耳其陈兵叙利亚边境  叙总理表示准备应变

1957-09-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　土耳其陈兵叙利亚边境
　　叙总理表示准备应变
    新华社18日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚总理阿萨利17日说，叙利亚政府、人民和军队团结无间，而且已经有应付任何事变的准备。
    阿萨利是在一次内阁会议后向新闻记者说这番话的。这次会议由库阿特利总统主持，并且有武装部队总司令比兹里将军参加。会议听取了外交部长比塔尔关于局势最近发展的报告。
    阿萨利指出，内阁会议从各方面研究了目前的形势，以免被帝国主义者弄得措手不及。
    同一天，外交部长比塔尔访问了埃及大使馆，同埃及外交代表就当前局势作了商谈。
    新华社18日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特18日讯：据“电讯报”报道，土耳其在叙利亚边境集结了大批军队。据大马士革消息，土耳其军队有九个师已开到叙利亚边境地区。
    据新华社大马士革17日电“胜利报”今天以“中国人民站在我们一边”为题，继续发表社论，欢迎陈志方大使在9月15日代表周恩来总理和中国政府发表的表示中国支持叙利亚人民斗争的谈话。“胜利报”指出：“这种有力的支持加强了叙利亚必胜的信心，并且帮助叙利亚坚持它的自由事业”。
