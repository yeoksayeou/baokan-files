Title: Berlin Rally of Ten Thousand Celebrates "Peace Day"
Author:
Date: 1957-09-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 3rd – According to a report by the German News Agency: Major cities in the German Democratic Republic held mass rallies on September 1st to celebrate "Peace Day." Guests from the Soviet Union, the United Kingdom, Egypt, and Algeria also participated in the rallies.

    Chairman Havermann of the Berlin Peace Fighters Committee spoke at a mass rally of over ten thousand people in Berlin, emphasizing the importance of peaceful coexistence among all nations. He called on the citizens of Berlin to fight for the prohibition of atomic and hydrogen weapons.

    On the eve of "Peace Day," residents of the German Democratic Republic also held demonstrations and torchlight processions along the Oder-Neisse River German-Polish peace border and in some places along the border between East and West Germany, expressing their determination to safeguard peace.

### 柏林万余人集会  庆祝“和平节”

1957-09-04
第6版()
专栏：

    柏林万余人集会
    庆祝“和平节”
    新华社柏林3日电　据德意志通讯社报道：民主德国各大城市都在9月1日举行群众集会，庆祝“和平节”。参加集会的还有苏联、英国、埃及和阿尔及利亚等国的来宾。
    柏林市和平战士委员会主席哈佛曼在柏林一万多人的群众大会上讲话，他着重指出各国人民和平共处的重要意义。他号召柏林市民为禁止原子武器和氢武器而斗争。
    在“和平节”的前夕，民主德国的居民在沿奥得—尼斯河德波和平疆界以及东西德交界线的一些地方还举行了示威和火炬游行，表示了他们维护和平的决心。
