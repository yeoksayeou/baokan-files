Title: Our Student Basketball Team Defeats French Team
Author: 
Date: 1957-09-08
Page: 4

    Our Student Basketball Team Defeats French Team
    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, September 7th. The Chinese student men's basketball team, participating in the games celebrating the 50th anniversary of the founding of the French National Student Union, defeated the French team on September 6th with a score of 60 to 50.
    At the end of the first half, the score was 32 to 21, with the Chinese team in the lead.

### 我学生篮球队胜法国队

1957-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

    我学生篮球队胜法国队
    新华社巴黎7日电　参加庆祝法国全国学生联合会成立五十周年运动会的中国学生男子篮球队，9月6日以六十比五十胜法国队。
    上半场结束的时候，比数是三十二比二十一，中国队占先。
