Title: All-India Federation of Educational Associations Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: The All-India Federation of Educational Associations delegation, consisting of six members led by the association's vice-president, Mrs. Hiralkar, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation is visiting at the invitation of the National Committee of the Educational Workers' Trade Union of China.

### 全印教育协会联合会代表团抵京

1957-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    全印教育协会联合会代表团抵京
    新华社29日讯　全印教育协会联合会代表团一行六人，在这个协会的副主席希尔拉卡尔夫人率领下，今天下午乘飞机抵北京。这个代表团是应中国教育工会全国委员会的邀请来访问的。
