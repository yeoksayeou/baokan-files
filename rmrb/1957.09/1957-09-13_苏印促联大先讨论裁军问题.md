Title: USSR and India Urge UN General Assembly to Discuss Disarmament First
Author:
Date: 1957-09-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 12th: The Soviet delegation to the United Nations General Assembly wrote to the Chairman of the United Nations Disarmament Commission on September 11th, requesting an early meeting of the Disarmament Commission to study the subcommittee's report, thereby preparing a report of the Disarmament Commission to be submitted to the United Nations General Assembly.

The letter pointed out that this was to enable the Twelfth Session of the United Nations General Assembly to list the Disarmament Commission's report as the first item on the agenda for discussion.

Prior to this, the Indian delegation had also requested the United Nations General Assembly Political Committee to give priority to the discussion of the disarmament issue.

### 苏印促联大先讨论裁军问题

1957-09-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　苏印促联大先讨论裁军问题
    新华社12日讯　纽约消息：苏联出席联合国大会代表团9月11日写信给联合国裁军委员会主席，要求早日召开裁军委员会会议来研究小组委员会的报告，从而准备一个提交联合国大会的裁军委员会的报告。
    这封信指出，这样做是为了使联合国大会第十二届会议能把裁军委员会的报告列为第一项议程来讨论。
    在这以前，印度代表团也曾经要求联合国大会政治委员会优先讨论裁军问题。
