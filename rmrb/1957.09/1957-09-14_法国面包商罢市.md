Title: French Bakers' Strike
Author:
Date: 1957-09-14
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, September 13th:

　　French national bakers began a two-day strike on September 11th to protest the government's price control policies. According to official sources, 85% of bakers throughout France participated in the strike.

　　The French government has issued orders authorizing police to force bakeries to open "when necessary."

### 法国面包商罢市

1957-09-14
第7版()
专栏：

　　法国面包商罢市
    据新华社13日讯  巴黎消息：
    法国全国面包商9月11日开始罢市两天，抗议政府的物价管制政策。据官方消息：法国全境85％的面包商都参加了罢市。
    法国政府已发布命令，准许警察“在必要时”强迫面包房开业。
