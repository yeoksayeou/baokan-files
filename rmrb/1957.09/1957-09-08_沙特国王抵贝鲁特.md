Title: Saudi King Arrives in Beirut
Author:
Date: 1957-09-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Saudi Arabian King Saud arrived in Beirut by plane from Riyadh this morning. Lebanese President Camille Chamoun, Prime Minister Sami as-Solh, Foreign Minister Charles Malik, and other senior Lebanese officials greeted him at the airport. The official purpose of King Saud's sudden visit was not announced. According to Western news agencies, he is there to discuss the Syrian situation with the Lebanese authorities.

### 沙特国王抵贝鲁特

1957-09-08
第6版()
专栏：

    沙特国王抵贝鲁特
    新华社7日讯　沙特阿拉伯国王沙特在今天上午从利雅得乘飞机到达贝鲁特。黎巴嫩总统夏蒙、总理索勒哈、外交部长马利克等黎巴嫩高级官员都到机场迎接。官方没有宣布沙特国王的突然访问的目的。据西方通讯社报道，他是去同黎巴嫩当局商谈叙利亚局势问题的。
