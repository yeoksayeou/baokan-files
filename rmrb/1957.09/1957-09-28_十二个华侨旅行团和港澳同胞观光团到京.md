Title: Twelve Overseas Chinese Tourist Groups and Hong Kong and Macao Compatriot Sightseeing Groups Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 27th: By this evening, twelve Overseas Chinese tourist groups and Hong Kong and Macao compatriot sightseeing groups have successively arrived in Beijing. In addition to sightseeing in Beijing, some of them will be invited to attend the National Day observation ceremony.

Before arriving in Beijing, they had visited the hometowns of Overseas Chinese in Guangdong and Fujian, as well as Central China and Northeast China.

According to the person in charge of the Beijing Overseas Chinese Travel Service, more than 900 Overseas Chinese who are touring the country at their own expense and Hong Kong and Macao compatriots who have returned to the mainland for sightseeing. Among them, the Overseas Chinese come from more than ten countries on the five continents of Europe, Asia, Africa, America, and Australia.

### 十二个华侨旅行团和港澳同胞观光团到京

1957-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

    十二个华侨旅行团和港澳同胞观光团到京
    新华社27日讯　到今天晚上为止，已经有十二个华侨旅行团和港澳同胞观光团陆续到达北京。他们除在北京参观游览外，有一部分人将应邀参加第八届国庆观礼。
    他们到达北京以前，曾经分别在广东、福建侨乡和华中、东北等地参观游览。
    据北京华侨旅行服务社负责人说，这次成批或零星自费在国内观光的华侨和回内地游览的港澳同胞共九百多人。其中华侨来自欧、亚、非、美、澳五大洲的十多个侨居国。
