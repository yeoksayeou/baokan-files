Title: All-China Women's Federation Hosts Banquet for Singaporean Women's Tourist Group
Author:
Date: 1957-09-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: The All-China Women's Federation hosted a banquet this evening at Fangshan Restaurant in Beihai Park to welcome the Singaporean Women's Tourist Group headed by Zhang Rihao. This tourist group, consisting of twenty people, arrived in Beijing on the 23rd.

Yang Zhihua, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, delivered a speech at the banquet, extending a warm welcome to the tourist group.

### 全国妇联欢宴新加坡妇女观光团

1957-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

    全国妇联欢宴新加坡妇女观光团
    据新华社27日讯　全国妇联今晚在北海公园仿膳举行宴会欢迎以张日好为首的新加坡妇女观光团。这个观光团一行二十人，是在23日到达北京的。
    全国妇联副主席杨之华在宴会上致词对观光团表示热烈欢迎。
