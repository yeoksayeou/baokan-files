Title: Saudi Arabian Prime Minister Refutes Imperialist Slanders: Syria Does Not Threaten the Security of Neighboring Countries; The United States and the Baghdad Pact are Plotting New Anti-Syrian Conspiracies

Author:
Date: 1957-09-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 24th: Saudi Arabian Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Faisal refuted on the 23rd the imperialist-spread claim that Syria is a threat to its neighbors. He said, "There is no threat. No Arab country would threaten the security of any other Arab country."

Faisal also refuted the slanderous assertion that "Syria is ruled by Moscow." He said, why doubt Syria's independence and freedom? Syria is an autonomous country exercising its own powers.

The Prime Minister said that the Saudi Arabian government is "concerned" about the development of the situation in Syria, but not "anxious."

Faisal held talks with U.S. President Eisenhower on the Middle East situation on the 23rd. According to the White House press secretary, the meeting was a "general discussion." The above remarks were made by Faisal to reporters after the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, London, 24th: The United States and five Baghdad Pact member states began meeting in London on the 23rd to plot new conspiracies against Syria.

The report said that this Baghdad Pact's "Anti-Subversion Committee" meeting will continue for a week. It will then submit a report to the Baghdad Pact Council with new recommendations for interfering in the development of the situation in Syria.

At the same time, the U.S. Department of Defense in Washington announced that from September 24th to 28th, approximately 8,000 U.S. Marines will conduct a military exercise with Turkish forces about 600 miles from the Syrian border.

### 沙特阿拉伯首相驳斥帝国主义谰言  叙利亚并没有威胁邻国的安全  美国和巴格达集团策划反叙新阴谋

1957-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    沙特阿拉伯首相驳斥帝国主义谰言
    叙利亚并没有威胁邻国的安全
    美国和巴格达集团策划反叙新阴谋
    新华社24日讯　华盛顿消息：沙特阿拉伯首相兼外交大臣费萨尔23日驳斥了帝国主义散布的所谓叙利亚是对它的邻国的一种威胁的说法。他说：“没有任何威胁。没有任何一个阿拉伯国家会威胁任何其他阿拉伯国家的安全。”
    费萨尔还驳斥了“叙利亚受莫斯科统治”的诬蔑论调。他说，为什么要怀疑叙利亚的独立和自由呢？叙利亚是一个行使自己权力的自主的国家。
    这位首相说，沙特阿拉伯政府对叙利亚局势的发展感到“关怀”，但不是“焦虑”。
    费萨尔在23日同美国总统艾森豪威尔就中东局势举行了会谈。据白宫新闻秘书说，这次会谈是一个“一般性的讨论会”。上面这些谈话就是费萨尔在会谈以后向新闻记者们发表的。
    新华社24日讯　伦敦消息：美国和五个巴格达条约参加国23日起在伦敦开会策划反对叙利亚的新阴谋。
    消息说，这个巴格达条约的“反颠覆委员会”会议将要连续举行一星期。然后它将向巴格达条约理事会提出干涉叙利亚局势发展的新的建议的报告。
    在这同时，华盛顿的美国国防部宣布，从9月24日到28日，大约有八千名美国海军陆战队人员将和土耳其部队一起在离叙利亚边界六百英里处举行一次军事演习。
