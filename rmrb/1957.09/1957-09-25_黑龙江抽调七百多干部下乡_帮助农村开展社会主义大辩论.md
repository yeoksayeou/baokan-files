Title: Heilongjiang Dispatches Over 700 Cadres to the Countryside to Help Rural Areas Conduct Socialist Great Debate
Author:
Date: 1957-09-25
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, September 24th. The Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party has dispatched over 700 cadres from various departments directly under the province to form five work groups. Starting September 24th, they will go to five special districts and directly subordinate counties, including Nenjiang, Hejiang, Suihua, Heihe, and Mudanjiang, to help carry out the socialist great debate in rural areas. The task and focus of the work groups is to assist rural areas in carrying out the socialist great debate centered on the grain issue, and to coordinate the work of autumn harvest and grain purchase.

### 黑龙江抽调七百多干部下乡  帮助农村开展社会主义大辩论

1957-09-25
第2版()
专栏：

    黑龙江抽调七百多干部下乡
    帮助农村开展社会主义大辩论
    新华社哈尔滨24日电　中共黑龙江省委在省直属各机关抽调了七百多名干部组成五个工作组，于9月24日起分赴嫩江、合江、绥化、黑河、牡丹江等五个专区和直属县，帮助开展农村社会主义大辩论。工作组的任务和工作重点是帮助农村开展以粮食问题为中心的社会主义大辩论，结合搞好秋收和粮食征购工作。
