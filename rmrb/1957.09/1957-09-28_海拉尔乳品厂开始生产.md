Title: Hailar Dairy Plant Begins Production
Author:
Date: 1957-09-28
Page: 5
Eastern Scales and Western Claws

    Hailar Dairy Plant Begins Production
    A newly built modern dairy plant officially began production in Hailar on the 25th.

    This dairy plant, which is large in scale and design capacity, began construction last July. It can process 100 tons of milk per day, producing 4,200 kilograms of butter, 4,000 kilograms of lactose, and more than 3,000 kilograms of casein.
                      (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 海拉尔乳品厂开始生产

1957-09-28
第5版()
专栏：东鳞西爪

    海拉尔乳品厂开始生产
    一座新建的现代化乳品厂25日在海拉尔正式投入生产。
    这座规模和设计能力都很大的乳品厂，是去年7月开始兴建的。它每天能处理牛奶一百吨，生产奶油四千二百公斤，乳糖四千公斤和酪胶三千多公斤。
                      （据新华社）
