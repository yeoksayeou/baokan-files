Title: How is the Football League A going?
Author:
Date: 1957-09-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, September 22nd: The September matches of the second round of the 1957 National Football League A, held in the Northeast competition zone, concluded today.

The win-loss records of each team are as follows: Shenyang Military Region team won two matches and lost one; Changchun team and First Machine Sports Association team both won one, drew one, and lost one; Shenyang Institute of Physical Education team won one match and lost two. These four football teams will also play sixteen matches in the second round of October, starting on October 6th, against Shanghai Institute of Physical Education team, Nanjing Military Region team of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, China Red Flag Sports Association team, and Wuhan Institute of Physical Education team in Shenyang, Changchun, and Dalian respectively.

The other four League A football teams participating in this league—Beijing Youth team, Tianjin team, "August 1st" team of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and China Railway Sports Association team—will also start their second round of October on October 6th, holding six matches in the Beijing and Tianjin competition zones.

### 足球甲级队联赛情况如何？

1957-09-24
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    足球甲级队联赛情况如何？
    据新华社沈阳22日电　在东北竞赛区举行的1957年全国足球甲级队联赛第二循环9月份的比赛，今天已经全部结束。
    各队胜负情况是：沈阳部队队胜二场、败一场；长春队和一机体协队都是胜一场平一场败一场；沈阳体育学院队胜一场败二场。这四个足球队从10月6日开始，还将分别同上海体育学院队、中国人民解放军驻南京部队队、中国红旗体育协会队、武汉体育学院队在沈阳、长春、旅大三地举行第二循环10月份的十六场比赛。
    参加这次联赛的其他四个甲级足球队——北京青年队、天津队、中国人民解放军“八一”队、中国火车头体育协会队也将从10月6日开始在北京、天津竞赛区举行第二循环10月份的六场比赛。
