Title: Chu Tunan Hosts Banquet for Japan-China Friendship Association Delegation

Author:
Date: 1957-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th—Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet in Beijing this evening to welcome the delegation of the Japan-China Friendship Association headed by Matsumoto Jiichiro.

    Chu Tunan and Matsumoto Jiichiro spoke at the banquet successively.

    Among those present at the banquet were Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and prominent figures from society, including Zhao Puchu, Zhao Anbo, Xie Nanguang, and Ma Shaobo.

    Before the banquet, Chu Tunan met with all members of the visiting delegation. Matsumoto Jiichiro, on behalf of the delegation, presented souvenirs to the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 楚图南欢宴日中友协访华代表团

1957-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    楚图南欢宴日中友协访华代表团
    据新华社28日讯　中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南今天晚上在北京设宴欢迎以松本治一郎为首的日中友协访华代表团。
    楚图南和松本治一郎在宴会上先后讲了话。
    今晚出席作陪的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志，中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙，以及社会著名人士赵朴初、赵安博、谢南光、马少波等多人。
    在宴会以前，楚图南接见了访华团全体人员，松本治一郎代表访华团向中国人民对外文化协会赠送了纪念品。
