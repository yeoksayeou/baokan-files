Title: Former Indonesian Vice President Hatta Departs for Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1957-09-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 19th: At the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai, former Indonesian Vice President Dr. Hatta and his wife, along with their entourage, departed by plane for a visit to China on the morning of the 19th.

Before his departure, Hatta made a brief statement to reporters from Radio Indonesia, saying that he believed this visit to China would be beneficial to him.

Those who came to the airport to see him off included Indonesian Prime Minister Djuanda, First and Third Deputy Prime Ministers, and many ministers, Vice Chairman of the National Council Abdoelgani, the Chief and Deputy Chief of Staff of the Army, the Chief of Staff of the Navy, and other military and political leaders, as well as Vice Secretary-General of the Indonesian Communist Party Lukman, former Commander of the 7th Military District Soemual, and others.

Chinese Ambassador Huang Zhen also went to the airport to see him off.

### 印度尼西亚前副总统  哈达启程来我国访问

1957-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚前副总统
　　哈达启程来我国访问
    新华社雅加达19日电　应周恩来总理的邀请，印度尼西亚前副总统哈达博士夫妇和随行人员在19日早晨乘飞机前往中国访问。
    哈达在临行前曾经对印度尼西亚广播电台的记者发表简短的谈话说，他相信这次去中国参观访问对他来说是有益处的。
    到机场欢送的有印度尼西亚总理朱安达，第一、第三副总理和许多部长、民族委员会副主席阿卜杜加尼、陆军正副参谋长和海军参谋长等军政领导人以及印度尼西亚共产党副总书记鲁克曼、前第七军区司令苏穆阿尔等人。
    中国大使黄镇也到机场欢送。
