Title: Italy and Iran Issue Joint Communiqué, Will Regularly Discuss Middle East Issues
Author:
Date: 1957-09-13
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, September 12th: Italy's President Gronchi and Iran's King Pahlavi issued a joint communiqué on September 11th stating that Italy and Iran will hold regular meetings to discuss Middle East issues.

　　Gronchi arrived in Iran on September 8th for a state visit.

　　The joint communiqué stated that they believe that Western countries must adopt a common policy towards the Middle East based on "sincere cooperation and mutual respect." However, the communiqué also said: "Iran and Italy believe that remaining loyal to the defensive alliance systems to which they belong is a fundamental factor in their policies."

　　The joint communiqué also stated that the two countries will develop their economic relations and will exchange technical delegations. "The formation of the Italo-Iranian Oil Company is an example illustrating the possibility of cooperation between the two countries."

### 意大利和伊朗发表会谈公报  将定期讨论中东问题

1957-09-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　意大利和伊朗发表会谈公报
    将定期讨论中东问题
    据新华社12日讯  德黑兰消息：意大利总统格隆基和伊朗国王巴列维9月11日发表会谈公报说，意大利和伊朗将定期举行会议来讨论中东问题。
    格隆基是在9月8日到达伊朗进行国事访问的。
    联合公报说，他们认为，西方国家必须对中东采取一种以“真诚合作和互相尊重”为基础的共同政策。但是，公报又说：“伊朗和意大利认为，忠于它们所参加的防御性联盟体系，是它们政策中的基本因素。”
    联合公报还说，这两国将发展它们的经济关系，并且将互派技术代表团。“意伊石油公司的组成是说明这两国间进行合作的可能性的例证。”
