Title: Ulaanbaatar Publishes Communiqué on South Delegation's Visit: Mongolia and Yugoslavia Decide to Develop Cooperative Relations
Author:
Date: 1957-09-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, September 17th: A joint communiqué on the visit of Yugoslav Federal Executive Council Vice President Vukmanović and the Yugoslav delegation he led to Mongolia was published here today. The communiqué stated that the visit of the Mongolian government delegation to Yugoslavia and the visit of the Yugoslav delegation to Mongolia this time are of great significance for the continued consolidation and development of friendship and cooperation between the two peoples.

The communiqué pointed out that the responsible persons of the two governments introduced each other to the economic and cultural development of their respective countries in the talks, and exchanged views on certain issues and conditions for developing economic and cultural cooperation between the two countries based on the joint statement issued in Belgrade. The communiqué said that both sides are satisfied that, for the purpose of building socialism and maintaining world peace, the two governments sincerely desire such cooperation and believe that it is entirely possible.

The communiqué stated that in order to further understand the economic development of the two countries, both sides will send economic delegations to visit each other.

### 乌兰巴托发表南代表团访问公报  蒙南两国决定发展合作关系

1957-09-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　乌兰巴托发表南代表团访问公报
　　蒙南两国决定发展合作关系
    新华社乌兰巴托17日电　今天这里发表了南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席伏克曼诺维奇和他率领的南斯拉夫代表团访问蒙古的联合公报。公报说，蒙古政府代表团的访问南斯拉夫和南斯拉夫代表团这次来蒙古访问，对继续巩固和发展两国人民的友谊和合作有很大意义。
    公报指出，两国政府负责人在谈话中相互介绍了两国经济及文化的发展情况，并且根据在贝尔格莱德发表的联合声明就发展两国经济、文化合作方面的某些问题及条件交换了意见。公报说，双方满意地认为，为了建设社会主义和维护世界和平，两国政府衷心愿望这种合作，并且认为完全有可能。
    公报说，为了继续了解两国经济的发展情况，双方将派遣经济代表团相互访问。
