Title: He Xiangning Hosts Banquet for Overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macao Compatriots
Author:
Date: 1957-09-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: He Xiangning, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, held a welcome banquet at noon today to entertain overseas Chinese who have returned to the country to participate in National Day celebrations and sightseeing, as well as compatriots from Hong Kong and Macao who have returned to Beijing to participate in National Day celebrations and sightseeing.

At the beginning of the banquet, He Xiangning delivered a welcoming speech. On behalf of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, she welcomed overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macao compatriots back to participate in National Day celebrations and sightseeing in various places. She said that in the past seven or eight years, under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong, the motherland's construction cause has achieved great achievements, and its international status has been unprecedentedly improved. These are all things that can be gratifying to all overseas Chinese. She also said that overseas Chinese love the motherland, and the future of the motherland is infinitely bright. In ten or eight years, the motherland's construction cause will develop more vigorously than it is now. She hopes that all overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macao compatriots will contribute their strength to building a prosperous and strong socialist motherland.

Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi attended the banquet and delivered a speech.

A total of more than 900 people attended the banquet.

### 何香凝欢宴侨胞和港澳同胞

1957-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    何香凝欢宴侨胞和港澳同胞
    据新华社29日讯　华侨事务委员会主任何香凝今天中午举行欢迎宴会，招待回国参加国庆观礼和观光的侨胞以及回到北京参加国庆观礼和观光的港澳同胞。
    宴会开始时，何香凝致欢迎词。她代表华侨事务委员会欢迎侨胞、港澳同胞回来参加国庆观礼和到各地观光。她说，七八年来，在毛泽东主席的英明领导下，祖国的建设事业已取得了伟大的成绩，国际地位已空前提高。这些都是可以告慰各位侨胞的。她又说，海外侨胞是热爱祖国的，祖国的前途是无限远大的，十年八年以后，祖国的建设事业将会比现在更加蓬勃地发展起来。她希望各位侨胞和港澳同胞，共同为建设繁荣、富强的社会主义祖国而贡献出自己的力量。
    国务院副总理陈毅出席了宴会，并讲了话。
    参加宴会的共九百多人。
