Title: Inner Mongolian Frozen Beef Shipped to Egypt
Author:
Date: 1957-09-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hailar, 19th – Yesterday, three refrigerated carriages carrying sixty-five tons of frozen beef departed from the Hailar Meat Combined Processing Factory in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. This frozen beef will be exported to Egypt.

From October of last year to the present, this factory has shipped over 1,840 tons of frozen meat to Egypt.

In accordance with the living habits of the Islamic people of Egypt, the healthy cattle and sheep processed by this factory are slaughtered by an Ahong from the mosque, and the cleaning and packaging work is carried out by Hui workers. Before loading, all the frozen meat is wrapped in white cloth. Before departure, the Ahong inspects and signs before the train departs.

### 内蒙冻牛肉运往埃及

1957-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

    内蒙冻牛肉运往埃及
    新华社海拉尔19日电　昨天有三节冷藏车厢装载着六十五吨冻牛肉，从内蒙古自治区海拉尔肉类联合加工厂运出。这些冻牛肉将出口运往埃及。
    这个工厂从去年10月到现在，运往埃及的冻肉已经有一千八百四十多吨。
    根据信奉伊斯兰教的埃及人民的生活习惯，这个工厂加工的牛羊健畜，在屠宰的时候都由清真寺的阿訇下刀，由回族工人进行清洁包装工作。在装车前，全部冻肉都用白布包装。启运时由阿訇检验后签字发车。
