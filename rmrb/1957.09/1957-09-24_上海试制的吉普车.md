Title: Shanghai-Made Jeep

Author: 
Date: 1957-09-24
Page: 5

    Shanghai-Made Jeep
    Our country's first jeep, made entirely of domestic materials and parts, demonstrated excellent performance during a long-distance test drive of over 300 kilometers to and from Shanghai to Suzhou on the 20th.
    More than 6,000 parts of this jeep were supplied and specially made by dozens of electromechanical factories and auto parts factories in Shanghai, Nanjing, and other places. This jeep did not experience any accidents during its more than 300 kilometers of long-distance travel.
                (According to Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 上海试制的吉普车

1957-09-24
第5版()
专栏：

    上海试制的吉普车
    我国第一辆全部用国产材料和零件制造的小吉普车，20日在上海到苏州等地来回三百多公里的长途试车中，表现了优良的性能。
    这辆吉普车有六千多种零件都是上海、南京等地几十家机电工厂和汽车配件厂供应和特制的。这辆吉普车在三百多公里的长途奔驰中，没有发生任何事故。
                （据新华社讯）
