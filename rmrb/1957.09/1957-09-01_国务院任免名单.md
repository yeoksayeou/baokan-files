Title: State Council Appointment and Removal List
Author:
Date: 1957-09-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 29th: The State Council appointed and removed a number of personnel from state organs, embassies abroad, and universities on July 26. The list is as follows:

    Appointed Chen Mu and Li Shaoyu as Assistant Governors of the People's Bank of China;
    Li Jusheng as Consul General of the People's Republic of China in Jakarta, Republic of Indonesia;
    He Jilin as President of Changchun Institute of Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Medicine, and Cao Yinhuai and Sayin as Vice Presidents;
    Zhu Xianyi as President of Tianjin Medical College, and Li Zimu and Cai Gongqi as Vice Presidents;
    Su Kun as Vice President of Shanghai Theatre Academy;
    Li Zhenxun as President of Dalian Medical College, and Zhang Yi as Vice President;
    Bi Zhonghan as Vice President of Xi'an Power Institute;
    Zeng Zhenwu as President of Xi'an Institute of Physical Education;
    Guo Ruozhen and Wen Zhizhong as Vice Presidents of Qinghai Nationalities Institute;
    Pu Jingquan as President of Fujian Medical College.

    Removed Zhao Zhongshi from the post of Consul General of the People's Republic of China in Jakarta, Republic of Indonesia;
    Deng Shuqun from the post of Vice President of Shenyang Agricultural College;
    Li Zhenxun from the post of Vice President of Dalian Medical College;
    Zhu Xianmin from the post of Vice President of Hefei Mining Institute;
    Huang Kaiyun from the post of President of Fujian Medical College, and Pu Jingquan from the post of Vice President.

### 国务院任免名单

1957-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    国务院任免名单
    新华社29日讯  国务院7月26日任免一批国家机关、驻外使馆和高等学校的工作人员。名单如下：
    任命陈穆、李绍禹为中国人民银行行长助理；
    李菊生为中华人民共和国驻印度尼西亚共和国雅加达总领事；
    何济林为长春畜牧兽医大学校长，曹荫槐、萨音为副校长；
    朱宪彝为天津医学院院长，李自牧、蔡公琪为副院长；
    苏坤为上海戏剧学院副院长；
    李震勋为大连医学院院长，张毅为副院长；
    毕仲翰为西安动力学院副院长；
    曾震五为西安体育学院院长；
    郭若珍、温志忠为青海民族学院副院长；
    浦敬铨为福建医学院院长。
    免去赵仲时的中华人民共和国驻印度尼西亚共和国雅加达总领事职务；
    邓叔群的沈阳农学院副院长职务；
    李震勋的大连医学院副院长职务；
    朱献民的合肥矿业学院副院长职务；
    黄开云的福建医学院院长职务，浦敬铨的副院长职务。
