Title: Afro-Asian People's Conference to be Held in Cairo at the End of November
Author: 
Date: 1957-09-24
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 23rd: It has been learned here that the Afro-Asian People's Conference will be held in Cairo at the end of November this year. Thirty countries will participate in the conference, including all countries that participated in the Bandung Conference, as well as Ghana, Tunisia, Morocco, Malaya, and Somaliland.

### 亚非人民会议十一月底在开罗举行

1957-09-24
第7版()
专栏：

    亚非人民会议
    十一月底在开罗举行
    据新华社开罗23日电　这里获悉，亚非人民会议将在今年11月底在开罗举行。参加会议的将有三十个国家，包括参加万隆会议的所有国家以及加纳、突尼斯、摩洛哥、马来亚和索马里兰。
