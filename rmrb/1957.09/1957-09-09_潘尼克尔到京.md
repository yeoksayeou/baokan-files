Title: Panikkar Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-09
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, September 8th: Mr. Panikkar, head of the Indian Exhibition Delegation to China, arrived in Beijing by plane at 3 p.m. today. Those who went to the airport to greet him were the responsible persons of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.
    The Indian Exhibition at the Soviet Exhibition Hall in Beijing is scheduled to open on September 18th.

### 潘尼克尔到京

1957-09-09
第3版()
专栏：

    潘尼克尔到京
    据新华社8日讯　印度来华展览会代表团团长潘尼克尔今天下午三时乘飞机到达北京，前往机场迎接的有中国国际贸易促进委员会负责人。
    在北京苏联展览馆举行的印度展览会，已定于9月18日开幕。
