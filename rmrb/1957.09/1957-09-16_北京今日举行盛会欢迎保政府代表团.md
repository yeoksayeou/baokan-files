Title: Beijing to Hold Grand Gathering Today to Welcome Bulgarian Government Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1957-09-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 15th: Beijing will hold a grand gathering tomorrow (16th) afternoon with people from all walks of life to welcome the Bulgarian People's Republic government delegation led by Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Bulgarian People's Republic, Yugov. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the live proceedings of this meeting on its first program from 4:20 PM.

### 北京今日举行盛会欢迎保政府代表团

1957-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

    北京今日举行盛会欢迎保政府代表团
    新华社15日讯  明天（16日）下午，北京市各界人民将举行盛会，欢迎以保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席于哥夫为首的保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团。中央人民广播电台将从十六点二十分起在第一种节目转播这次大会的实况。
