Title: Refuting the "Party's World"

Author: 
Date: 1957-09-13
Page: 3

　　Refuting the "Party's World"

　　Rightist Chu Anping once said: "Throughout the country, in every unit, large or small, even in a section or a group, a Party member must be arranged to be the leader." It is true that all of our country's work must be led by the Communist Party, as solemnly stipulated in the Constitution. However, our Party has never allowed Party members to act arbitrarily in any work, but has always advocated cooperation and joint efforts with non-Party individuals to jointly build socialism.

　　Readers can see the absurdity of the so-called "Party's World" from the following statistics.

　　Highest National Power Organ

　　The Constitution of the People's Republic of China stipulates that the National People's Congress is the highest organ of state power and the sole organ exercising state legislative power. Among the National People's Congress deputies, the proportion of non-Party members is not small. For example:

　　First National People's Congress Representatives: 1,226 in total
　　　　CPC: 668 people, accounting for 54.48%
　　　　Democratic Parties: 274 people, accounting for 22.36%
　　　　Non-Party Individuals: 284 people, accounting for 23.16%

　　Standing Committee of the National People's Congress: 79 in total
　　　　CPC: 40 people, accounting for 50.63%
　　　　Democratic Parties: 25 people, accounting for 31.64%
　　　　Non-Party Individuals: 14 people, accounting for 17.73%

　　Highest State Administrative Organ

　　The State Council is the executive organ of the highest state power organ and the highest state administrative organ. According to recent statistics:

　　State Council Ministers (Directors): 38 in total
　　　　CPC: 22 people, accounting for 57.9%
　　　　Non-Party Individuals: 16 people, accounting for 42.1%

　　Vice Ministers (Directors): 211 in total
　　　　CPC: 182 people, accounting for 86.3%
　　　　Non-Party Individuals: 29 people, accounting for 13.7%

　　Local State Administrative Organs

　　Governors and Vice Governors of various provinces: 192 in total
　　　　CPC: 130 people, accounting for 67.7%
　　　　Non-Party Individuals: 62 people, accounting for 32.3%

　　(Domestic Data Group of this Newspaper)

### 斥“党天下”

1957-09-13
第3版()
专栏：数字与事实

　　斥“党天下”
    右派分子储安平曾说：“在全国范围内，不论大小单位，甚至一个科一个组，都要安排一个党员做头儿。”本来我们国家的各项工作都必须有共产党的领导，庄严的宪法早有规定。但是，我们党在任何工作中从不允许党员独断专行，而是一贯主张与党外人士合作共事，共同建设社会主义。
    读者从下列统计就可看出所谓“党天下”的荒谬。
　　最高国家权力机关
    中华人民共和国宪法规定，全国人民代表大会是最高国家权力机关，是行使国家立法权的唯一机关。在全国人民代表当中，非党人士占的比例是不小的。例如：
    第一届全国人民代表大会代表共1，226人
　　中共　668人　占54.48％
　　各民主党派　274人　占22.36％
　　无党派人士　284人　占23.16％
    人民代表大会常务委员共79人
　　中共　40人　占50.63％
　　各民主党派　25人　占31.64％
　　无党派人士　14人　占17.73％
　　最高国家行政机关
    国务院是最高国家权力机关的执行机关和最高国家行政机关。据最近的统计材料：
　　国务院部长（主任）共38人
　　中共　22人　占57.9％
　　非党人士　16人　占42.1％
    副部长（主任）共211人
　　中共　182人　占86.3％
　　非党人士　29人　占13.7％
    地方国家行政机关
    各省省长副省长共192人
　　中共　130人　占67.7％
　　非党人士　62人　占32.3％
　　（本报国内资料组）
