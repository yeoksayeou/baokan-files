Title: Syria Inquires of Neighboring Countries Jordan, Iraq, and Lebanon About Their Views on the U.S. Anti-Syria Statement
Author:
Date: 1957-09-13
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, September 12th: According to a report in "Al-Ra'i" on September 11th, Syrian Foreign Minister Bitar sent notes to the governments of Jordan, Iraq, and Lebanon on the evening of September 10th, inquiring about the U.S. President Eisenhower's statement regarding Syria.

Eisenhower said in a recent statement that Syria's neighbors were deeply disturbed by developments in Syria.

The Syrian note asked the three governments whether the words in Eisenhower's statement were consistent with what they discussed with U.S. special envoy Henderson during his visit to the Middle East.

### 叙利亚照会约伊黎三邻国  探询对美国反叙声明的看法

1957-09-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　叙利亚照会约伊黎三邻国
　　探询对美国反叙声明的看法
    新华社大马士革12日电　据“舆论报”9月11日报道，叙利亚外交部长比塔尔在9月10日晚间向约旦、伊拉克和黎巴嫩三国的政府分别发出照会，询问关于美国总统艾森豪威尔关于叙利亚的声明的问题。
    艾森豪威尔在最近发表的一个声明中曾经说，叙利亚的邻国对叙利亚局势的发展深为不安。
    叙利亚的照会询问三国政府说，艾森豪威尔声明中所说的话，同它们在美国特使汉德逊访问中东期间和他所会谈的内容是否相符。
