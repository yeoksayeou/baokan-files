Title: Yugoslav Parliamentary Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: The Yugoslav Federal National Assembly Delegation, headed by Chairman Stanboulić, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation previously visited Shenyang, Anshan, Lüda, Hangzhou, Shanghai and other places.

### 南议会代表团返京

1957-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    南议会代表团返京
    新华社29日讯　以斯坦鲍利奇主席为首的南斯拉夫联邦国民议会代表团今天下午乘飞机返回北京。代表团曾先后访问了沈阳、鞍山、旅大、杭州、上海等地。
