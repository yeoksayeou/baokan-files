Title: Sudanese Foreign Minister Reiterates Non-Acceptance of the Eisenhower Doctrine
Author:
Date: 1957-09-01
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Khartoum, August 31st: Sudanese Foreign Minister Mahgoub said on August 30th that it is not in Sudan's interest to accept the Eisenhower Doctrine at present.

In a conversation with a Middle East News Agency reporter, Mahgoub said that Sudan is pursuing an independent policy and is prepared to cooperate equally with Eastern and Western countries.

He said: "We will accept any financial assistance, as long as such assistance does not come with conditions that impede our independence."

### 苏丹外长重申不接受艾森豪威尔主义

1957-09-01
第7版()
专栏：

    苏丹外长重申不接受艾森豪威尔主义
    新华社31日讯  喀土穆消息：苏丹外交部长马哈古卜8月30日说，在目前接受艾森豪威尔主义是不符合苏丹利益的。
    马哈古卜在对中东通讯社记者发表的谈话中说，苏丹正在奉行独立的政策，它准备同东方国家和西方国家进行同样的合作。
    他说：“我们将接受任何财政援助，只要这种援助不附带妨碍我们独立的条件。”
