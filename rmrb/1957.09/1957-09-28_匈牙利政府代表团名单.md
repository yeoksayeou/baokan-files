Title: List of the Hungarian Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-09-28
Page: 4

    List of the Hungarian Government Delegation
    Head of Delegation: János Kádár – Prime Minister of the Hungarian Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, Member of the Political Bureau.
    Members:
    György Marosán – Minister of State of the Hungarian Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government, First Secretary of the Budapest Municipal Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, Member of the Political Bureau;
    Sándor Nográdi – Ambassador of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, Member of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party.
      Retinue:
    István Szirmay – Director of the Information Bureau of the Hungarian Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government, Member of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, Minister of the Central Propaganda and Agitation Department of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party;
    Péter Vályi – Deputy Director of the Planning Bureau;
    Péter Kós – Director of the Asian-African Department of the Hungarian Ministry of Foreign Affairs;
    Mrs. Dezső Blott – Director of the Far East Department of the Hungarian Ministry of Foreign Trade.
