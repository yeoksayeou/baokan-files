Eastern Scales and Western Claws

　　Another Group of Workers Enters University
　　In the new academic year, one hundred and twenty industrial workers and cadres drawn from industry and agriculture have entered six higher engineering colleges in Beijing: Tsinghua, Steel, Mining, Petroleum, Railway, and Geological Exploration.

　　Collected Papers on the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge Project to be Published
　　Chinese and Soviet engineering technicians are systematically summarizing the construction experience of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge. A "Collected Papers on the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge Project" with 80,000 characters and approximately 200 illustrations has been compiled, is currently being typeset, and is expected to be published by the end of September or early October.
　　The construction experience of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge is planned to be compiled into three books for publication. The other two are: "Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge (Project Introduction)" and "Compilation of Construction Experience of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge".

　　Xinjiang Explores Gold Mines
　　On September 17, the Industry Department of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region dispatched a gold mine survey team to Hotan and Yutian areas to investigate the situation of gold producing areas, providing data for the restoration of old gold mines and the organization of mass gold production.
　　Xinjiang is one of China's famous gold producing areas. There are dozens of known gold producing areas throughout the region, especially the Altai Mountains, which produce gold of good quality and high yield. There is a local saying: "Seventy-two gullies in the Altai Mountains, every gully has gold." (Xinhua News Agency)

### 东鳞西爪

1957-09-22
第5版()
专栏：东鳞西爪

    东鳞西爪
    又一批工人上大学
    新学年中，有一百二十多名产业工人以及从工农业中抽调的干部进入了北京清华、钢铁、矿业、石油、铁道、地质勘探等六所高等工科院校去学习。
    武汉长江大桥工程论文集将出版
    中苏工程技术人员正在对武汉长江大桥的施工经验进行系统的总结。一本有八万字和约二百张插图的“武汉长江大桥工程论文集”已经编辑就绪，正在排印，预计在9月底或10月初即可出版。
    武汉长江大桥的建设经验，计划编成三本书出版。另外两本是：“武汉长江大桥（工程介绍）”和“武汉长江大桥建设经验汇编”。
    新疆勘查金矿
    新疆维吾尔自治区工业厅17日派出了一个金矿调查组，前往和阗、于阗一带勘查黄金产地情况，为恢复旧金矿和组织群众性的黄金生产提供资料。
    新疆是我国著名的黄金产地之一。全疆已知黄金产地有数十处之多，尤以阿尔泰山区所产黄金质地好，产量多。当地有“阿尔泰山七十二道沟、沟沟有黄金”的谚语。　（据新华社讯）
