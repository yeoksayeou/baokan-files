Title: French Troops Again Invade Tunisian Border
Author:
Date: 1957-09-10
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 7th: The Tunisian Embassy in Cairo announced today that French troops have launched a new invasion of the Tunisian border.
    The embassy's statement said that French armored forces crossed the Tunisian border on the 6th and opened fire on Tunisians, killing two and wounding many. Tunisia has established a commission to investigate the invasion.

### 法军又侵入突尼斯边境

1957-09-10
第7版()
专栏：

    法军又侵入突尼斯边境
    据新华社开罗7日电　突尼斯驻开罗大使馆今天宣布，法国部队对突尼斯边界发动了新的侵犯。
    大使馆的声明说，法国的装甲部队6日越过了突尼斯边界，并且向突尼斯人开火，击毙两人，击伤许多人。突尼斯已经成立了一个委员会来调查这次侵犯。
