Brief News

	Chinese Cultural Delegation Arrives in Sofia
	Sofia Dispatch: The Chinese Cultural Delegation, led by Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Culture, arrived in Sofia by plane on the 6th. The delegation will learn about Bulgaria's cultural life and participate in the fifth anniversary celebration of the signing of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

	Romania and the Soviet Union Sign Dual Nationality Agreement
	Bucharest Dispatch: The newspapers here published on the 6th the agreement between Romania and the Soviet Union on adjusting dual nationality and consular issues. The agreement stipulates that citizens with Romanian-Soviet dual nationality can freely decide their nationality within one year after the agreement comes into effect and submit an application to the diplomatic representation of the selected country. (Xinhua News Agency)

    简讯
    我文化代表团抵索非亚
    索非亚讯：由文化部副部长郑振铎率领的中国文化代表团6日乘飞机到达索非亚。代表团将了解保加利亚的文化生活，参加中保文化合作协定签订五周年庆祝典礼。
    罗苏签订双重国籍协定
    布加勒斯特讯：这里的报纸在6日发表了罗马尼亚和苏联关于调整双重国籍和领事问题的协定。协定规定，具有罗苏双重国籍的公民，可以在协定生效后的一年内自由决定他们的国籍，并且向所选定的国家的外交代表机构提出申请。（新华社）
