Title: Grain Procurement Increases in Shache County, Xinjiang
Author:
Date: 1957-09-08
Page: 5

    Grain Procurement Increases in Shache County, Xinjiang

    (Our report) Shache County, located on the western edge of the Tarim Desert in Xinjiang, launched a major debate in rural areas centered on the grain issue, increasing the county's daily grain storage from 150,000 jin to 500,000 jin.

    During the great debate, due to the implementation of socialist propaganda and education, the criticism of capitalism, individualism, and departmentalism in rural areas, and the suppression of the destructive words and deeds of landlords and rich peasants, socialist ideology gained absolute dominance.
              (Xinjiang Daily Editorial Department)

### 新疆莎车县入库公粮增加

1957-09-08
第5版()
专栏：

    新疆莎车县入库公粮增加
    本报讯　新疆塔里木沙漠西部边缘的莎车县，在农村中开展了以粮食问题为中心的大辩论，使全县每天粮食入仓量由以前的十五万斤增加到五十万斤。
    在大辩论中，由于进行了社会主义的宣传教育、批判了农村中的资本主义、个人主义和本位主义思想，打击了地主富农的破坏言行，使社会主义思想占了绝对优势。
              （新疆日报编辑部）
