Title: Henan Farmers Advance Autumn Grain Sales

Author: 
Date: 1957-09-19
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency: Farmers in many areas of Henan Province are pre-selling autumn grain after harvesting some of their autumn crops.

　　In the Xinyang Special District, where there is a bumper grain harvest this year, farmers have pre-paid public grain and pre-sold surplus grain totaling 35 million jin since the autumn crops came onto the market. According to incomplete statistics, more than 70 million jin of autumn grain has been pre-paid and pre-sold throughout the province.

### 河南农民预先交售秋粮

1957-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　河南农民预先交售秋粮
    据新华社讯　河南省不少地区的农民在一部分秋季作物收割完毕后，提前预交预售秋粮。
    今年粮食丰收的信阳专区，自秋季作物登场到目前为止，农民已预交公粮和预售余粮三千五百万斤。据不完全统计，全省已预交预售秋粮七千多万斤。
