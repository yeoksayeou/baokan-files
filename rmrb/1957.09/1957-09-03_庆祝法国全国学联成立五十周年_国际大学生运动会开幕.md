Title: Celebrating the 50th Anniversary of the French National Union of Students: International University Games Open
Author:
Date: 1957-09-03
Page: 6

    Celebrating the 50th Anniversary of the French National Union of Students
    International University Games Open
    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, September 2nd. The University Games, organized by the World University Games Committee to celebrate the 50th anniversary of the French National Union of Students, officially opened on the evening of August 31st at the Charlety Stadium in Paris. Chinese athletes are also participating in the games.
    The stadium was packed with spectators. After the band played the Marseillaise, 1,600 athletes from 28 countries, including France, China, the Soviet Union, and the United States, entered the stadium in a procession led by their team leaders carrying their national flags, in alphabetical order of their country names. When the Chinese basketball team appeared in the stadium, the audience burst into thunderous applause. Many Chinese expatriates warmly welcomed the athletes from their motherland participating for the first time in the University Games held in Paris.
    The games will continue until September 8th. The competitions held during the games include: track and field, swimming, volleyball, basketball, tennis, and fencing.

### 庆祝法国全国学联成立五十周年  国际大学生运动会开幕

1957-09-03
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝法国全国学联成立五十周年
    国际大学生运动会开幕
    新华社巴黎2日电  为庆祝法国全国学生联合会成立五十周年由世界学生运动会委员会组织的大学生运动会，8月31日晚上在巴黎夏尔蒂运动场正式开幕。中国运动员也参加了这次运动会。
    运动场上挤满了观众。乐队奏马赛曲后，参加运动会的法国、中国、苏联、美国等二十八国一千六百名运动员由领队打着国旗，按照国家名称第一个字母的顺序列队进入运动场。当中国篮球队出现在运动场上的时候，观众掌声雷动。许多中国侨民热烈欢迎祖国的运动员首次参加在巴黎举行的大学生运动会。
    这次运动会将进行到9月8日。在运动会中举行的比赛项目有：田径、游泳、排球、篮球、网球和击剑等。
