Title: Thailand's New Cabinet Formed
Author:
Date: 1957-09-25
Page: 6
　
Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, September 24th: Thai radio announced on September 24th that Nai Pote Sarasin had formed his cabinet on the 23rd. He will serve as Prime Minister and Minister of Finance. The cabinet list has been approved by the King of Thailand and will be submitted to the interim parliament for approval today.

Prince Naradhip (i.e., Prince Wan Waithayakon) will remain as Foreign Minister. He is the only member of the overthrown Plaek Phibunsongkhram cabinet among the twenty-six new cabinet members.

The leader of the coup, Bangkok Military Commander Nai Sarit, did not hold a cabinet position.

### 泰国新内阁组成

1957-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    泰国新内阁组成
    据新华社24日讯　曼谷消息：泰国电台9月24日宣布，乃朴·沙拉信已经在23日组成他的内阁，他任总理兼财政部长。内阁名单已经由泰国国王批准，并且将在今天提交临时议会通过。
    那拉底（即旺·威泰耶康亲王）留任外交部长。在二十六名新阁员中，他是唯一的被推翻的銮披汶内阁的成员。
    政变首领、曼谷军事总监乃沙立没有担任内阁职务。
