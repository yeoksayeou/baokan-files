Title: Level 10 Typhoon Hits Japan
Author:
Date: 1957-09-08
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, September 7th: A powerful typhoon that recently appeared in the Pacific Ocean struck the Kyushu region of southern Japan last night and today hit the Shikoku region. This strong typhoon has caused enormous loss of life and property in these two regions.
    According to the latest figures released by the Japanese police authorities, a total of more than 15,000 houses in these two regions were destroyed or blown away by the typhoon; four people died, ten people are missing, and more than 20,000 people are homeless.

### 十级强台风袭击日本

1957-09-08
第6版()
专栏：

    十级强台风袭击日本
    据新华社7日讯　东京消息：最近在太平洋上出现的强大台风从昨天夜里袭击日本南部的九州地区后，今天又袭击了四国地区。这次强台风给这两个地区造成了巨大的生命财产的损失。
    据日本警察当局发表的最新数字，这两个地区共有一万五千多所房屋被台风吹毁或者刮走；四人死亡，十人失踪，两万多人无家可归。
