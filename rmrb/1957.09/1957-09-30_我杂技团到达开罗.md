Title: Our Acrobatic Troupe Arrives in Cairo
Author:
Date: 1957-09-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th - The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe, led by head Zhang Tiesheng, arrived in Cairo in two groups by plane from Damascus on the 28th and 29th.

The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe received a warm welcome during its nearly month-long performances in Syria.

### 我杂技团到达开罗

1957-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

    我杂技团到达开罗
    据新华社29日讯　中国杂技艺术团在团长张铁生率领下在28日和29日分两批从大马士革乘飞机到达开罗。
    中国杂技艺术团在叙利亚将近一月的演出中，受到了热烈欢迎。
