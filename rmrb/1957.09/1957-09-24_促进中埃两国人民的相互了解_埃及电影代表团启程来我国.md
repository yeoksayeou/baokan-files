Title: Promoting Mutual Understanding Between the Chinese and Egyptian People: Egyptian Film Delegation Departs for China
Author:
Date: 1957-09-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, September 23rd: A five-member Egyptian film delegation left here on the morning of September 22 to attend the Egyptian Film Week opening in Beijing on September 28.

The delegation is led by Yehia Haqqi, Director of the Arts Department of the Egyptian National Guidance Department. The members are: Faten Hamama, a famous Egyptian film actress; Hamama's husband, film actor Omar Sharif; Mohamed Madkour, Chief of the Film Section of the Arts Department; and Mrs. Haqqi.

Chen Jiakang, the Chinese Ambassador to Egypt, hosted a farewell banquet for the delegation on the evening of September 21. Radwan, the Egyptian Minister of National Guidance, also attended the banquet.

In a brief speech, Chen Jiakang welcomed the delegation to visit China on behalf of the Chinese people. He said that China and Egypt are building their own industries, agriculture, and new cultural lives, so holding film festivals and exchanging film delegations will help promote mutual understanding and enrich the cultural lives of the two peoples. Radwan said that the exchange of film delegations will promote the cultural life and friendship between the two countries. These representatives will inspire people to love peace, prevent war, and build their new lives, which is indeed everyone's responsibility.

Chinese film actresses Bai Yang and Qin Yi, who are currently in Cairo attending the Chinese Film Festival, also attended the banquet.

### 促进中埃两国人民的相互了解  埃及电影代表团启程来我国

1957-09-24
第6版()
专栏：

    促进中埃两国人民的相互了解
    埃及电影代表团启程来我国
    新华社开罗23日电　由五人组成的埃及电影代表团在9月22日上午离开这里前往中国出席9月28日在北京开幕的埃及电影周。
    代表团由埃及国家指导部文艺处处长耶雅·哈基率领，团员是：埃及著名电影女演员法登·哈马玛；哈马玛的丈夫、电影演员奥马·薛里夫；文艺处电影科科长穆罕默德·马德库和哈基夫人。
    中国驻埃及大使陈家康在9月21日晚间设宴为代表团饯行。埃及国家指导部部长拉德温也出席了宴会。
    陈家康在简短的致辞中代表中国人民欢迎代表团前去中国访问。他说，中国和埃及正在建立自己的工业、农业和新的文化生活，因此举行电影节和互派电影代表团会有助于促进相互的了解，并且丰富两国人民的文化生活。拉德温说，互派电影代表团将促进两国的文化生活和友谊。这些代表都将鼓舞人民热爱和平、防止战争和建立他们的新生活，而这的确是每一个人的责任。
    目前在开罗参加中国电影节的中国电影女演员白杨和秦怡也出席了宴会。
