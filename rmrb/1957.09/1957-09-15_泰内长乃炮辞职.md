Title: Thai Interior Minister Nai Pote Resigns
Author:
Date: 1957-09-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, September 13th: Thai Prime Minister Pibul Songgram announced on September 12th that General Nai Pote Sarasin had resigned from his position as Minister of the Interior, but he will continue to serve as the Director-General of Police.

Pibul Songgram said that he will concurrently hold the positions of Minister of Defense and Minister of Cooperative Affairs, and the positions of the three deputy ministers will remain vacant.

### 泰内长乃炮辞职

1957-09-15
第6版()
专栏：

    泰内长乃炮辞职
    据新华社13日讯  曼谷消息：泰国总理銮披汶9月12日宣布，乃炮·是耶暖将军已经辞去了内政部长的职务，但是，他将继续担任警察总监的职务。
    銮披汶说，他将兼任国防部长和合作事务部长的职务，三个副部长的职位将空着。
