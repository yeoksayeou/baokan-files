Title: Indian Vice President Arrives in Beijing Today
Author:
Date: 1957-09-18
Page: 1

　　Indian Vice President Arrives in Beijing Today
    Xinhua News Agency, 17th. The Vice President of the Republic of India, Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan, was invited by Chairman Mao Zedong to visit our country. Originally scheduled to arrive in Beijing by plane on the 17th, the arrival has been postponed to the 18th due to weather conditions.

### 印度副总统今日抵京

1957-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　印度副总统今日抵京
    新华社17日讯　印度共和国副总统萨瓦帕利·拉达克里希南应毛泽东主席邀请访问我国，原定在17日乘飞机到达北京，因气候关系延期于18日到京。
