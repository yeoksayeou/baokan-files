Title: American Financial Groups Intensify Plunder of Foreign Countries: Private Overseas Investment Increases Greatly
Author:
Date: 1957-09-03
Page: 7

　　Comprehensive report from our newspaper: According to materials on U.S. foreign investment in 1956 recently released by the U.S. Department of Commerce, private U.S. investment abroad increased by approximately $4 billion last year (exceeding previous records), reaching a total of $33 billion. Combined with government foreign investment, these private investments amount to about $49.5 billion.

　　Of the private investment increase in 1956, $1.4 billion was invested in the oil industries of various countries. U.S. private capital is most active in Latin American countries, particularly Venezuela, and in the Near East. To pursue excess profits, U.S. companies have also increased large amounts of capital to develop oil resources in Canada, Australia, Indonesia, and other countries.

　　U.S. capital still occupies the most consolidated position in the economies of Canada and Latin American countries. In this year, U.S. capital increased by $1 billion in Canada, $800 million in Latin American countries, and $500 million in Western Europe.

　　In 1956, U.S. private foreign investment earned a total profit of $3.438 billion, an increase of $370 million compared to 1955, with an average profit rate of over 10% (by the end of last year, the total U.S. private foreign investment was $33 billion).

　　Classified by region, Latin America is the region most severely exploited by U.S. monopoly capital, with profits earned from this region reaching $1.096 billion. Classified by industry, the most profit was obtained from the oil industry, totaling $1.406 billion.

### 美国财阀加强对外国掠夺  私人海外投资大量增加

1957-09-03
第7版()
专栏：

    美国财阀加强对外国掠夺
    私人海外投资大量增加
    本报综合报道  据新华社讯：根据美国商务部最近发表的1956年美国国外投资的材料，美国国外的私人投资在去年大约增加了四十亿美元（这个数字超过已往纪录），达到三百三十亿美元的总数。这些私人投资同政府的国外投资加在一起，约达四百九十五亿美元。
    在1956年增加的私人投资中，有十四亿美元是投在各国的石油工业中。美国私人资本在拉丁美洲国家、首先是在委内瑞拉以及在近东最为活跃。美国公司为了追逐超额利润，还增加了大批资金来开发加拿大、澳大利亚、印度尼西亚和其他国家的石油资源。
    美国资本仍然在加拿大和拉丁美洲国家的经济中占据最巩固的地位。在这一年，美国资本在加拿大增加了十亿美元，在拉丁美洲各国增加了八亿美元，对西欧的投资增加了五亿美元。
    美国的私人对外投资在1956年共赚到三十四亿三千八百万美元的利润，这个数字比1955年增加了三亿七千万美元，其平均利润率达到10％以上（到去年年底美国私人对外投资总数为三百三十亿美元）。
    按照地区来分类，拉丁美洲是受美国垄断资本剥削得最凶的一个地区，从这个地区赚得的利润达到十亿九千六百万美元。按照投资的行业分类，从石油业中获得的利润最多，共计十四亿零六百万美元。
