Title: Soviet Scientists Discuss Future Transportation: Passengers to Travel by Multi-Stage Ballistic Rocket, Half an Hour from Moscow to New York
Author:
Date: 1957-09-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 25th. Soviet scientists recently said in a Moscow broadcast that the peaceful use of ballistic rockets as a means of transportation on Earth is entirely possible.

The well-known Soviet expert in interstellar transportation, Sternfeld, said that the Soviet Union now possesses a multi-stage ballistic rocket that can transport people and goods from Moscow to New York in half an hour. If the rocket makes a stop in London for passengers to disembark, it would still only take forty-five minutes from Moscow to New York.

### 苏联科学家谈未来的交通工具  旅客乘坐多级弹道火箭  半小时从莫斯科到纽约

1957-09-26
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联科学家谈未来的交通工具
    旅客乘坐多级弹道火箭  半小时从莫斯科到纽约
    新华社莫斯科25日电　苏联科学家最近在莫斯科的广播中说，和平利用弹道火箭，把它作为地球上的交通工具是完全可能的。
    这位苏联著名的星际交通专家斯登菲耳德说，苏联现在拥有一种多级弹道火箭，它可以在半小时内，把人和货物从莫斯科送到纽约。如果火箭途经伦敦时暂停，以便乘客下来的话，它从莫斯科到纽约也只要四十五分钟。
