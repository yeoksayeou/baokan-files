Title: Kazami Akira Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 31st. Akira Kazami, advisor to the Japanese Socialist Party and chairman of the Japan National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations, and his secretary Hisashi Kawada arrived in Beijing by plane from Ulaanbaatar this afternoon. Kazami Akira is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs after participating in the 6th World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival as a specially invited representative.

Also arriving in Beijing on the same plane was Japanese Senator Kimiko Abe, who is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

Greeting them at the airport were Chen Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and prominent figures from all walks of life, including Liao Chengzhi.

### 风见章到北京

1957-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    风见章到北京
    新华社31日讯  日本社会党顾问、日本恢复日中邦交国民会议理事长风见章和他的秘书川田寿今天下午乘飞机从乌兰巴托到达北京。风见章是在以特邀代表身份参加第六届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节后，应中国人民外交学会的邀请来中国访问的。
    同机到达北京的，还有应邀来中国访问的日本参议员安部君子。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国人民外交学会副会长陈翰笙和各界著名人士廖承志等。
