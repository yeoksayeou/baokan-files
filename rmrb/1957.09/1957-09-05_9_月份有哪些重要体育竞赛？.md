Author: Guó'ān
Date: 1957-09-05
Page: 4

What are the Important Sports Competitions in September?

(Our report) In the clear and refreshing month of September, many exciting international and domestic sports competitions will be held across the country.

The Albanian Tirana football and volleyball teams, after their friendly matches in Shenyang, Changchun, Wuhan, Tianjin, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and other places, will return to Beijing around the 10th. The Tirana football team will play a friendly match against the combined team A on the 15th, and the volleyball team will play three friendly matches against strong Beijing volleyball teams on the 12th, 14th, and 15th.

The Czechoslovakian tennis team, invited to visit our country, will arrive in Beijing in late September. After holding friendly matches in Beijing, they will visit Shanghai, Guangzhou, Wuhan, and other places.

The first British sports delegation to visit our country—the British swimming team, consisting of thirteen members—will arrive in Beijing in late September, compete in one match in Beijing, and then visit and compete in Shanghai, Guangzhou, and other places.

The first stage of the second round of the National Football League (Group A), featuring China's top football teams, will be held in Wuhan, Shenyang, Changchun, and other places in mid-to-late September. From the 8th to the 28th of this month, the National Basketball League (Group B), with 24 teams and nearly 400 athletes participating, will be held in Xi'an.

The largest national gymnastics competition since liberation—the 1957 National Gymnastics Championships—will be held in Shanghai on the 22nd of this month. Six hundred athletes from various provinces and cities across the country will participate in this competition, and the national gymnastics team will participate in exhibition matches. (Guoan)

### 9月份有哪些重要体育竞赛？
国安
1957-09-05
第4版()
专栏：

    9月份有哪些重要体育竞赛？
    本报讯　在秋高气爽的9月份，全国各地将举行不少精彩的国际、国内的体育竞赛活动。
    阿尔巴尼亚地拉那足球、排球队在沈阳、长春、武汉、天津、上海、广州等地访问比赛后，将于10日前后回到北京。地拉那足球队将在15日对联队一队进行一场友谊比赛，排球队将在12、14、15三天对在京的排球劲旅进行三场友谊比赛。
    应邀来我国访问的捷克斯洛伐克网球队将于本月下旬到达北京，在北京举行友谊比赛后，去上海、广州、武汉等地访问。
    第一个来我国的英国体育代表团体——英国游泳队一行十三人将于本月下旬来北京，在京比赛一场后再去上海、广州等地访问比赛。
    有我国第一流足球强队参加的全国足球甲级队联赛第二循环第一阶段将于本月中、下旬在武汉、沈阳、长春等地进行联赛。本月8日至28日，在西安将举行有二十四个队、近四百名运动员参加的全国篮球乙级队联赛。
    解放以来规模最大的一次全国性的体操比赛——1957年全国体操锦标赛将于本月22日在上海举行。参加这次比赛的有全国各省、市的六百名运动员，国家体操队将参加表演比赛。（国安）
