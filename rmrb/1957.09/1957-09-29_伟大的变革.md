    Great Transformation
    During the period of the First Five-Year Plan, along with the rapid development of China's industrial construction, brilliant achievements were also made on the agricultural front. Over the past five years, under the correct leadership of the Party, agricultural cooperation has achieved a great victory. Now, 97% of farmers across the country have joined cooperatives. From now on, half a billion farmers have been freed from the shackles of the individual economy, eradicated the roots of poverty, and embarked on the socialist road that guarantees common prosperity.
    The upsurge of cooperation has brought about an upsurge in production, and the enthusiasm of farmers across the country has soared to unprecedented heights. In developing diversified economies, changing cultivation systems, overcoming natural disasters, and rationally arranging labor, cooperatives have demonstrated their unparalleled superiority. 1956 was a year when China's rural areas suffered relatively severe natural disasters, but nationwide, all major crops still achieved overall increases in production, with yields even higher than the bumper year of 1955.
    On the basis of agricultural cooperation, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China has put forward the first grand plan for developing agricultural productive forces and transforming China's nature in the agricultural field – the "National Agricultural Development Program (Draft) from 1956 to 1967." Now, farmers across the country are steadily advancing in accordance with this plan. (Attached pictures)

### 伟大的变革

1957-09-29
第5版()
专栏：

    伟大的变革
    在第一个五年计划期间，和我国工业建设迅速发展的同时，在农业战线上也取得了辉煌的成就。五年来，在党的正确领导下，农业合作化取得了伟大的胜利，现在，全国已有97％的农户参加了合作社。五亿农民从此摆脱了个体经济的束缚，挖掉穷根，走上了保证共同富裕的社会主义的大道。
    合作化高潮带来了生产高潮，全国农民生产积极性空前高涨。在发展多种经济、改变耕作制度、战胜自然灾害、合理安排劳动力等方面，合作社显示了它无比的优越性。1956年是我国农村遭受自然灾害相当严重的一年，但是在全国范围内，各种主要农作物仍然获得全面增产，产量比丰收的1955年还有所增加。
    在农业合作化的基础上，中共中央又提出了为发展农业生产力和农业方面改造我国自然的第一个宏伟的计划——“1956年到1967年全国农业发展纲要”（草案）。现在全国农民正脚踏实地地按照这个规划迈进。（附图片）
