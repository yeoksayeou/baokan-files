Title: Guiana Government Inaugurated
Author:
Date: 1957-09-07
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Georgetown, September 6th: The government organized by Cheddi Jagan, leader of the People's Progressive Party of British Guiana, was sworn in on September 5th. Jagan serves as Chief Minister and Minister of Trade and Industry. In the newly formed government, the People's Progressive Party holds five government seats, with the remaining three seats being designated by the British government.

### 圭亚那政府就职

1957-09-07
第7版()
专栏：

    圭亚那政府就职
    据新华社6日讯　乔治城消息：英属圭亚那人民进步党领袖契迪·贾根组织的政府在9月5日宣誓就职。贾根任首席部长兼工商部长。在新组成的政府中，人民进步党人拥有五个政府席位，其余三席是由英国政府指定的。
