Title: Supporting Harbin
Author:
Date: 1957-09-07
Page: 4

Liaoning Transports Large Quantities of Flood Control Materials
\tXinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 6th – Liaoning Province has transferred another 400,000 grass sacks to Harbin.
\tIncluding the materials transported in the previous few days, Liaoning Province's support for Harbin's flood control materials totals 314 train cars, including 1.4 million grass sacks, more than 44,100 catties of rope, and more than 85,300 small carrying poles.

Longjiang and Other Counties Dispatch 90,000 Laborers
\tXinhua News Agency, Harbin, 6th – Heilongjiang Province's non-flood season areas are actively supporting areas threatened by floods along the Songhua River with manpower and materials.
\tSince late August, more than 40 counties and cities in non-flood areas such as Longjiang, Gannan, Wuchang, and Boli have dispatched 80,000 to 90,000 laborers and transported large quantities of flood control materials such as grass sacks to support the flood control efforts in Harbin, Zhaoyuan, Zhaodong, Zhaozhou, Tangyuan, and Fujin cities and counties. The Yichun Forestry Area transported more than 174,000 pieces of timber for flood control in the seven days up to August 30th.

### 支援哈尔滨

1957-09-07
第4版()
专栏：

    支援哈尔滨
    辽宁运去大批防汛器材
    新华社沈阳6日电　辽宁省又抽调了四十万个草袋，运往哈尔滨。
    包括前几天运送的物资在内，辽宁省支援哈尔滨的防汛器材共达三百一十四辆车皮，其中有草袋一百四十万个、绳子四万四千一百多斤、小扁担八万五千三百多根。
    龙江等县派出九万民工
    新华社哈尔滨6日电　黑龙江省非汛区积极以人力和物力支援松花江沿岸受洪水威胁的地区。
    8月下旬以来，非汛区的龙江、甘南、五常、勃利等四十多个县、市已派出八、九万名民工，并运出大批草袋等防汛物资，支援哈尔滨、肇源、肇东、肇州、汤原、富锦等市、县的防汛斗争。伊春林区到8月30日为止的七天内，运出防汛用的木材达十七万四千多根。
