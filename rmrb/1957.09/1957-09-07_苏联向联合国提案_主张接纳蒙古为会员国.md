Title: The Soviet Union Proposes to the United Nations Advocating Admission of Mongolia as a Member State
Author:
Date: 1957-09-07
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, New York, 6th (TASS): The Permanent Representative of the Soviet Union to the United Nations, Sobolev, wrote to the Secretary-General of the United Nations regarding the application of the Mongolian People's Republic to join the United Nations, and submitted a draft resolution of the Soviet delegation recommending the admission of the Mongolian People's Republic as a member of the United Nations. In the letter, Sobolev requested that the aforementioned draft resolution be distributed as a document of the Security Council and submitted to the upcoming meeting of the Security Council to study the resolution of the United Nations General Assembly regarding the admission of new member states for discussion.

### 苏联向联合国提案  主张接纳蒙古为会员国

1957-09-07
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联向联合国提案
    主张接纳蒙古为会员国
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社纽约5日讯：苏联驻联合国常驻代表索波列夫就蒙古人民共和国申请加入联合国问题写信给联合国秘书长，并且提出了苏联代表团建议接纳蒙古人民共和国为联合国会员国的决议草案。索波列夫在信里要求把上述决议草案作为安全理事会的文件加以散发，并提交即将研究联合国大会有关接纳新会员国问题决议的安全理事会会议进行讨论。
