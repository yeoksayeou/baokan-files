Title: Vukmanović Visits Shanghai; Met with Mayor Huang Huoqing and Others in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1957-09-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, September 19th: Yugoslav Federal Executive Council Vice President Vukmanović and his entourage arrived in Shanghai by special plane from Shenyang today for a visit. They were welcomed by Shanghai Vice Mayors Xu Jianguo, Song Jiwen, Jin Zhonghua, and people from all walks of life. Personnel from various countries' consulates in Shanghai and those handling overseas Chinese affairs also greeted them at the airport.

　　This afternoon, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Vice Mayor Xu Jianguo, toured the urban area.

　　In the evening, Vice President Vukmanović and his entourage were invited to attend a welcome banquet hosted by Vice Mayor Xu Jianguo.

　　On their way from Shenyang to Shanghai today, the distinguished guests stopped at Tianjin Airport for more than forty minutes and met with Tianjin Mayor Huang Huoqing, Vice Mayors Li Gengtao, Li Huasheng, and others.

### 伏克曼诺维奇到上海访问  路过天津时曾会见黄火青市长等人

1957-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　伏克曼诺维奇到上海访问
　　路过天津时曾会见黄火青市长等人
    据新华社上海19日电　南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席伏克曼诺维奇和随行人员，今天由沈阳乘专机来上海访问，受到上海市副市长许建国、宋季文、金仲华以及各界人士的欢迎。各国驻上海领事馆人员和办理侨务的人员也到机场迎接。
    今天下午，贵宾们由许建国副市长陪同游览了市区。
    晚间，伏克曼诺维奇副主席及随行人员应邀出席了许建国副市长举行的欢迎宴会。
    贵宾们今天由沈阳来上海途中，曾在天津机场停留了四十多分钟，同天津市市长黄火青、副市长李耕涛、李华生等见了面。
