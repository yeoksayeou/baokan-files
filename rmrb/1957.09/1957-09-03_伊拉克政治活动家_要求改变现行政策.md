Title: Iraqi Political Activists Demand Changes to Current Policies
Author:
Date: 1957-09-03
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, September 1st (TASS) – “Victory Newspaper” reports that hundreds of Iraqi lawyers, including leaders and members of parliament from many Iraqi political parties, have sent a petition to the Iraqi Prime Minister demanding that swift measures be taken to eliminate the abnormal situation created in Iraq during the Said government's rule.

The petition calls on the government to grant basic democratic freedoms, release political prisoners, implement an Arab liberation policy, and condemn the US's aggressive policy towards the Near East.

### 伊拉克政治活动家  要求改变现行政策

1957-09-03
第7版()
专栏：

    伊拉克政治活动家
    要求改变现行政策
    据新华社2日讯  塔斯社大马士革1日讯：“胜利报”报道，数百名伊拉克律师，包括伊拉克许多政党领袖和议员送给伊拉克首相一份请愿书，要求迅速采取措施消除伊拉克在赛义德政府执政时期所造成的不正常情况。
    请愿书要求政府给予基本的民主自由，释放政治犯，执行阿拉伯解放政策和谴责美国对中近东的侵略政策。
