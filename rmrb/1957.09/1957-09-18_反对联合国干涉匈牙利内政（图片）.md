Title: Oppose the United Nations' Interference in Hungary's Internal Affairs (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1957-09-18
Page: 6

The Hungarian Patriotic People's Front and the National Peace Council jointly held a mass rally to protest the so-called "Hungarian Question" report submitted by the United Nations "Committee of Five" and oppose the discussion of this report. The picture shows the venue of the conference.

　　Hungarian miners, 21,000 strong, signed a protest against the so-called "Hungarian Question" report submitted by the United Nations "Committee of Five" and opposed the United Nations' discussion of the report. The picture shows a miners' representative presenting the letter of protest to Prime Minister Kadar at the mass rally held during the Seventh Totobanio Miners' Festival in Hungary.

Hungarian News Agency (Xinhua News Agency)

### 反对联合国干涉匈牙利内政（图片）

1957-09-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　反对联合国干涉匈牙利内政
    匈牙利矿工二万一千人签名抗议联合国“五人委员会”提出的所谓“匈牙利问题”的报告并反对联合国讨论这个报告。图为在匈牙利第七届托托巴尼奥矿工节举行的群众大会上，矿工代表将这个抗议书呈交给卡达尔总理。
    匈牙利爱国人民阵线和全国和平理事会联合召开群众大会，抗议联合国“五人委员会”提出的所谓“匈牙利问题”的报告和反对讨论这个报告。图为大会会场。　匈牙利通讯社稿（新华社发）
