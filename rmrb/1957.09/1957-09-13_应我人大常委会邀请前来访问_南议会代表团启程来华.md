Title: South Parliament Delegation Departs for China at the Invitation of the NPC Standing Committee

Author:

Date: 1957-09-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 12th - The delegation of the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia departed from here by plane today for China.

　　The delegation is visiting China for a friendly visit of three weeks at the invitation of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China.

　　The delegation consists of ten people, led by President of the Federal National Assembly, Stambolic.

　　Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included Vice-President of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia, Djurakovic, Vice-Presidents of the Federal National Assembly, Simic and Shencurlz, and Deputy Secretary-General of the State Secretariat for Foreign Affairs, Ivkovic.

　　The Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia, Wu Xiuquan, also saw the delegation off at the airport.

　　On the 11th, Ambassador Wu Xiuquan hosted a banquet for all members of the delegation.

### 应我人大常委会邀请前来访问  南议会代表团启程来华

1957-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　应我人大常委会邀请前来访问
　　南议会代表团启程来华
    新华社贝尔格莱德12日电　南斯拉夫联邦国民议会代表团今天从这里乘飞机前往中国。
    代表团是应中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会的邀请、到中国作为时三周的友好访问的。
    代表团由十人组成，团长是联邦国民议会主席斯坦鲍利奇。
    到机场送行的有南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席乔拉科维奇、联邦国民议会副主席西米奇和申丘尔茨、外交国务秘书处副秘书长伊维科维奇等。
    中国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权也到机场送行。
    11日，伍修权大使曾经设宴招待了代表团的全体人员。
