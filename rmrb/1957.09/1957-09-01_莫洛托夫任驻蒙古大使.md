Title: Molotov Appointed Ambassador to Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1957-09-01
Page: 6

    Molotov Appointed Ambassador to Mongolia
    Moscow Dispatch from Our Newspaper, August 31st: Today, the "Pravda" published a message stating: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has appointed Vyacheslav Mikhailovich Molotov as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the Mongolian People's Republic. Former Ambassador Vasily Ilyich Pisarev has been reassigned as Counselor with the rank of Minister to the Embassy of the Soviet Union in the Mongolian People's Republic.

### 莫洛托夫任驻蒙古大使

1957-09-01
第6版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫任驻蒙古大使
    本报莫斯科31日电  真理报今天刊载一则消息说：苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命维亚切斯拉夫·米哈伊洛维奇·莫洛托夫为苏联驻蒙古人民共和国特命全权大使，前任大使瓦西里·伊利奇·皮萨列夫改任为驻蒙古人民共和国大使馆公使衔参赞。
