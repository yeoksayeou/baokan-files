Title: Jilin Increases Deer Antler Production
Author:
Date: 1957-09-09
Page: 5

Jilin's deer farms expect to produce 14,500 taels of deer antler this year, exceeding the original plan by 8.3% and increasing by 38% compared to last year. Moreover, the antlers are thick, robust, and of high quality, with 80% being first-class or higher.

In terms of breeding fawns, Jilin's deer farms have also overfulfilled this year's plan. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 吉林鹿茸增产

1957-09-09
第5版()
专栏：

    吉林鹿茸增产
    吉林省鹿场今年生产的鹿茸，预计可以达到一万四千五百两，超过原计划8.3％，比去年增加38％。而且，鹿茸，粗壮肥大，质量在一等以上的达80％。
    在繁殖仔鹿方面，吉林省鹿场也超额完成了今年的计划。（新华社）
