Title: Resolution of the Joint Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (Adopted on September 26, 1957)
Author:
Date: 1957-09-27
Page: 1

    The joint conference of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference unanimously agreed with the proposal of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on preparing for the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, and decided to establish the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese People for Celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution to uniformly arrange various related tasks. It was also decided that Wang Jiaxiang, Deng Yingchao, Bao Erhan, Shi Liang, Zhu De, Zhu Xuefan, Lao She, Liu Shaoqi, Liu Ningyi, Liu Geping, He Xiangning, Wu Yuzhang, Soong Ching Ling, Li Siguang, Li Jishen, Li Dequan, Li Zhucheng, Shen Junru, Chen Shutong, Lu Dingyi, Zhou Enlai, Zhou Yang, Lin Boqu, Zhu Kezhen, Hu Yaobang, Mao Dun, Ulanhu, Ma Xulun, Ma Yinchu, Zhang Xiruo, Zhang Wentian, Cao Jinghua, Mei Lanfang, Xu Deheng, Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, Peng Dehuai, Huang Yanpei, Dong Biwu, Liao Chengzhi, Cai Chang, Lai Ruoyu, Qian Junrui, and Saifudin would be members, with Liu Shaoqi as the chairman, Soong Ching Ling, Guo Moruo, and Wu Yuzhang as vice-chairmen, and Qian Junrui as the secretary-general, to form the preparatory committee and begin work immediately.

    The joint conference believes that commemorating the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution is of extremely significant importance. The great October Revolution opened a new era in the entire history of mankind, illuminated the path to complete liberation for the working people of the world, and has immeasurable greatness and far-reaching influence on the revolutionary cause of the people of the world and the Chinese people. The Chinese people have always regarded the Chinese revolution as a continuation of the great October Revolution. Over the past forty years, under the leadership of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Soviet people have defended and developed the achievements of the October Revolution, achieved great victories in socialist construction, and made great contributions to safeguarding world peace and the cause of human progress. The history of the Soviet Union fully demonstrates the unparalleled superiority of the socialist system and the infinite vitality of Marxism-Leninism. In our country's socialist construction, the advanced experience of the Soviet Union and the spirit of hard work and thrift in national construction of the Soviet people are examples that our people must strive to learn from.

    The joint conference calls on the people of all regions and sectors of the country to solemnly and grandly celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, continue to learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union, continue to carry out socialist propaganda and education universally and deeply, and, together with the Soviet Union and the working people of the world, hold high the banner of the October Revolution, further consolidate and strengthen the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, maintain and strengthen the internationalist unity of the world proletariat, and strive for new victories in the cause of human progress and the defense of world peace along the victorious road of the October Revolution.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会联席会议的决议（1957年9月26日通过）

1957-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常务委员会、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会联席会议的决议
    （1957年9月26日通过）
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会和中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会联席会议一致同意中国共产党中央委员会提出的关于筹备庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年的建议，决定成立中国人民庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十周年筹备委员会，统一部署各项有关工作，并且决定以王稼祥、邓颖超、包尔汉、史良、朱德、朱学范、老舍、刘少奇、刘宁一、刘格平、何香凝、吴玉章、宋庆龄、李四光、李济深、李德全、李烛尘、沈钧儒、陈叔通、陆定一、周恩来、周扬、林伯渠、竺可桢、胡耀邦、茅盾、乌兰夫、马叙伦、马寅初、张奚若、张闻天、曹靖华、梅兰芳、许德珩、郭沫若、彭真、彭德怀、黄炎培、董必武、廖承志、蔡畅、赖若愚、钱俊瑞、赛福鼎为委员，以刘少奇为主任委员，以宋庆龄、郭沫若、吴玉章为副主任委员，以钱俊瑞为秘书长，组成筹备委员会，即开始工作。
    联席会议认为：纪念十月革命四十周年，具有极其重大的意义。伟大的十月革命开辟了整个人类历史的新时代，照亮了全世界劳动人民彻底解放的道路，对于全世界人民和中国人民的革命事业具有无可估量的伟大和深远的影响。中国人民历来把中国革命看成是伟大的十月革命的继续。四十年来，苏联人民在苏联共产党的领导下，保卫和发展了十月革命的成果，在社会主义建设方面获得了伟大的胜利，并且对于保卫世界和平和人类进步事业作出了伟大的贡献。苏联的历史充分显示了社会主义制度的无比的优越性和马克思列宁主义的无限的生命力。在我国社会主义建设中，苏联的先进经验和苏联人民艰苦奋斗、勤俭建国的精神，是我国人民必须努力学习的榜样。
    联席会议号召全国各地和各界人民，庄严和隆重地庆祝十月革命四十周年纪念，继续学习苏联的先进经验，继续普遍深入地进行社会主义的宣传教育，同苏联和全世界劳动人民一起，高举十月革命的旗帜，更进一步地巩固和加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结，维护和加强全世界无产阶级的国际主义团结，并且沿着十月革命的胜利道路，为争取人类进步事业和保卫世界和平的新的胜利而奋斗。
