Title: Nehru Hosts Banquet for Indian Vice President; Chairman Mao, Vice Chairman Zhu, Chairman Liu, and Premier Zhou Attend the Banquet
Author:
Date: 1957-09-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 20th. Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Vice President of the Republic of India, Radhakrishnan, on his visit to China.

Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai attended tonight's banquet.

Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan delivered a speech at the banquet, saying that the Chinese people have now "become a strong, dignified, and self-respecting nation" and "have achieved political stability." He also said that some of the facts he had seen in China in the past few days had convinced him that China "indeed deserves our congratulations."

Vice President Radhakrishnan proposed a toast at the banquet to the health, longevity, and happiness of Chairman Mao Zedong, and to the prosperity of the Chinese people.

Chairman Mao said during his toast at the banquet, "Thank you, Your Excellency the Vice President, for your good wishes to the Chinese people just now. The people of our two countries will always stand together. I wish Your Excellency the Vice President good health and longevity. I wish President Prasad good health."

Also invited to attend tonight's banquet were Vice Premiers He Long, Chen Yi, and Li Xiannian; Vice Chairmen Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, and Li Weihan; President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu; Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng; Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) Bao Erhan; Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu; and well-known figures from all aspects of our country.

Also attending tonight's banquet were envoys from various countries to China.

After the banquet, the Indian film "Kabuliwala" was shown. (Picture attached)

### 尼赫鲁大使欢宴印度副总统  毛主席朱副主席刘委员长周总理出席宴会

1957-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁大使欢宴印度副总统
    毛主席朱副主席刘委员长周总理出席宴会
  新华社20日讯　印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁今晚举行宴会，欢迎印度共和国副总统拉达克里希南来中国访问。
    毛泽东主席，朱德副主席，刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理出席了今晚的宴会。
    印度副总统拉达克里希南在宴会上首先致词说，中国人民现在已经“变成了一个刚强而尊严的有自尊心的民族”，“并且实现了政治上的稳定”。他还说，他这几天在中国看到的一些事实，已使得他确信中国“的确不愧受到我们的祝贺”。
    拉达克里希南副总统在宴会上提议大家为毛泽东主席的健康、长寿和幸福，为中国人民的繁荣而干杯。
    毛主席在宴会上祝酒的时候说，感谢副总统阁下刚才对中国人民的很好的祝愿。我们两国人民将永远站在一起。祝副总统阁下身体健康，永远长寿。祝普拉沙德总统身体健康。
    被邀出席今晚宴会的，还有副总理贺龙、陈毅、李先念，副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、彭真、李维汉，最高人民法院院长董必武，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，外交部副部长章汉夫，以及我国各方面的著名人士。
    出席今晚宴会的还有各国驻中国的使节。
    宴会后放映了印度电影“来自喀布尔的一个人”。（附图片）
