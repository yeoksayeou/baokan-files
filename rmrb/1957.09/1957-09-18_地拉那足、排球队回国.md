Title: Tirana Football and Volleyball Teams Return Home
Author:
Date: 1957-09-18
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The entire Albanian Tirana men's volleyball team and football team returned home by plane from Beijing this morning. They have conducted a visit and competition in China for more than a month.

### 地拉那足、排球队回国

1957-09-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　地拉那足、排球队回国
    新华社17日讯　阿尔巴尼亚地拉那男子排球队和足球队全体人员，今天早晨由北京乘飞机回国。他们在中国共进行了一个多月的访问比赛。
