Title: Beijing-Guangzhou Route Night Flight Test Successful
Author:
Date: 1957-09-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 18th. China's Civil Aviation Administration has successfully completed its first night flight test on the Beijing-Guangzhou route.

　　The aircraft undertaking the test flight took off from Beijing at 7:25 PM on the 17th and safely arrived in Guangzhou at 5:20 AM on the 18th.

　　The aircraft landed in Zhengzhou, Wuhan, and Changsha along the way. The weather was good throughout the journey, and the flight was smooth and comfortable.

### 京穗线夜航试航成功

1957-09-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　京穗线夜航试航成功
    新华社广州18日电　中国民用航空局在北京——广州航线上首次夜间飞行试航成功。
    担任试航的飞机在17日下午七点二十五分从北京起飞，18日晨五点二十分安全抵达广州。
    这架飞机沿途曾经在郑州、武汉和长沙等地降落，一路天气良好，飞行平稳、舒适。
