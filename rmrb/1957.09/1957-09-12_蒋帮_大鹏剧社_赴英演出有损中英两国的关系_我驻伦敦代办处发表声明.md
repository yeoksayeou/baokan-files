Title: Chiang Gang's "Dapeng Drama Troupe" Performance in Britain Harms Sino-British Relations; Our Charge d'Affaires Office in London Issues Statement
Author:
Date: 1957-09-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, 10th. Qin Jialin, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the People's Republic of China's Office in London, today issued a statement to London newspapers regarding the so-called "Taiwan Chinese Classical Drama Troupe's" performance in Britain in mid-September.

The statement said that the so-called "Taiwan Chinese Classical Drama Troupe" is actually the "Dapeng Drama Troupe" belonging to the Air Force of the Chiang Kai-shek clique entrenched in Taiwan, Chinese territory. Its purpose in coming to Britain is obviously to attempt to engage in activities that slander the Chinese government and undermine the friendly relations between the Chinese and British people under the guise of cultural activities.

The statement went on to point out that there are also people in Britain using this matter to carry out propaganda to create two Chinas. This cannot but harm Sino-British relations and cannot but be resolutely opposed by the Chinese government and people.

### 蒋帮“大鹏剧社”赴英演出有损中英两国的关系  我驻伦敦代办处发表声明

1957-09-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　蒋帮“大鹏剧社”赴英演出有损中英两国的关系
　　我驻伦敦代办处发表声明
    新华社伦敦10日电　中华人民共和国驻伦敦代办处临时代办秦加林今日就所谓“台湾中国古典剧团”9月中旬到英国举行演出事向伦敦各报发表声明。
    声明说，所谓“台湾中国古典剧团”实际上是盘据在中国领土台湾的蒋介石集团空军部队所属的“大鹏剧社”。它来英国的目的显然是企图在文化活动的名义下进行诬蔑中国政府和破坏中英两国人民友好关系的活动。
    声明接着指出，在英国国内也有人利用这件事来进行制造两个中国的宣传。这就不能不有损中英两国的关系，不能不受到中国政府和人民的坚决反对。
