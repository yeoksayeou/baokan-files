Title: Vukmanović Leaves Beijing for Visit to Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1957-09-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 15th: Yugoslav Federal Executive Council Vice President Svetozar Vukmanović and his entourage left Beijing by special plane this morning to visit the Mongolian People's Republic. After visiting the Mongolian People's Republic, Vice President Vukmanović will return to northeastern China and other places to continue his sightseeing.

Those who saw him off at the airport today included Vice Premiers Chen Yun and Bo Yibo, Vice Chairmen Li Jishen and Chen Shutong, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian and Wang Jiaxiang, heads of government departments, democratic parties, and people's organizations, as well as diplomatic envoys from Yugoslavia, Mongolia, and other countries stationed in China.

Vice Premier Chen Yun delivered a farewell speech, saying that although our distinguished Yugoslav guests stayed in Beijing for a short time, their various friendly activities in Beijing have left us with a deep impression and have made beneficial contributions to enhancing the friendship between the Chinese and Yugoslav peoples and strengthening the friendly relations between our two countries. He thanked Vice President Vukmanović for bringing the deep friendship of the Yugoslav people to the Chinese people and hoped that he would also convey the sincere friendly wishes of the Chinese people to the people of all ethnic groups in Yugoslavia.

Vice President Vukmanović then spoke, expressing his great pleasure that he had the opportunity to meet with the highest leaders of the People's Republic of China, to exchange information on the existing achievements and further development plans of China and Yugoslavia, and to exchange opinions frankly and amicably on issues of common concern. He expressed his belief that the contact between the leaders of China and Yugoslavia has helped to enhance mutual understanding and will help to develop constructive cooperation between the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia and the People's Republic of China. This will also help to accelerate the development of socialist relations and strengthen world peace, which is the common goal of our two peoples.

Vice President Vukmanović, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yun, reviewed the honor guard and shook hands with the well-wishers one by one to say goodbye. After delivering his speech, Vice President Vukmanović and others boarded the special plane amidst music and applause from the well-wishers and departed.

### 伏克曼诺维奇离京去蒙古访问

1957-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　伏克曼诺维奇离京去蒙古访问
    新华社15日讯  南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席斯·伏克曼诺维奇和随行人员今天早晨乘专机离北京前往蒙古人民共和国进行访问。伏克曼诺维奇副主席将在访问蒙古人民共和国以后回到中国的东北等地继续进行参观。
    今天到机场欢送的，有陈云、薄一波副总理，李济深、陈叔通副委员长，外交部副部长张闻天、王稼祥，政府各部门、各民主党派和各人民团体的负责人，以及南斯拉夫、蒙古和各国驻中国的使节。
    陈云副总理致欢送词说，我们的南斯拉夫贵宾在北京逗留的时间虽然很短，可是他们在北京进行的各种友好活动给我们留下了深刻的印象，并且已经为增进中南两国人民的友谊和加强我们两国间的友好关系做了有益的贡献。他感谢伏克曼诺维奇副主席带来了南斯拉夫人民对于中国人民的深厚友谊，并且希望他把中国人民对于南斯拉夫人民的诚挚的友好愿望也带给南斯拉夫的各族人民。
    伏克曼诺维奇副主席接着讲话，对他能够有机会同中华人民共和国的最高领导人员会晤，就中南两国现有的成就和进一步的发展计划互相介绍情况并且对双方共同关心的问题坦白而友好地交换意见，感到十分高兴。他表示相信，中南两国领导人的接触已经有助于增进双方的相互了解并且将有助于发展南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国和中华人民共和国之间的建设性的合作。这同时也有助于加速社会主义关系的发展和加强世界和平，而这是我们两国人民的共同目标。
    伏克曼诺维奇副主席由陈云副总理陪同检阅了仪仗队，并且同欢送者一一握手告别。在发表了讲话以后，伏克曼诺维奇副主席等在音乐声和欢送者的掌声中登上专机离去。
