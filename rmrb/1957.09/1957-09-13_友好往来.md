　　Friendly Exchanges
　　Japanese Agricultural and Peasant Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou
　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 12th. The Japanese Agricultural and Peasant Delegation, led by Ishida Yuzen, Vice Chairman of the Japan Farmers' Association, a group of twenty-three people, arrived in Guangzhou this afternoon.
　　The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese Agronomy Society and is preparing to investigate the situation of agricultural cooperation in our country.
　　In the evening, Xie Huanting, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Branch of the Chinese Agronomy Society, hosted a banquet to welcome the guests.
　　The delegation will travel to Beijing by train tomorrow morning.
　　Japanese Buddhist Goodwill Mission to China Arrives in Guangzhou
　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 12th. The Japanese Buddhist Goodwill Mission to China, led by Takakai Ryusen, leader of the Soto Zen sect of Japanese Buddhism, a group of sixteen people, arrived in Guangzhou this afternoon. They are visiting our country at the invitation of the Buddhist Association of China. Reverend Ji Guang, Director of the Buddhist Association of China, traveled from Beijing to Shenzhen to greet the guests.
　　The eighty-two-year-old Reverend Takakai Ryusen immediately went to the Liurong Temple to pay homage after arriving in Guangzhou.
　　The mission will take a train to Beijing tomorrow morning.
　　Brazilian Ophthalmologist Dr. Silva and Wife Arrive in Beijing
　　Xinhua News Agency, September 10th. Brazilian ophthalmologist Dr. Silva and his wife, who are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese Medical Association, arrived in Beijing by plane today.
　　Indian Asian Solidarity Committee Delegation Returns Home
　　Xinhua News Agency, September 12th. The delegation of the Indian Asian Solidarity Committee, led by Mrs. R. Nehru, left Beijing by plane today to return to their country after visiting Beijing, Shenyang, Nanjing, Shanghai, Xi'an and other places.
　　Li Dequan, Liu Ningyi, Liao Chengzhi, Zhao Puchu, Chen Hansheng and other heads of relevant people's organizations went to the airport to see them off.

### 友好往来

1957-09-13
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

　　友好往来
    日农业农民代表团到广州
    新华社广州12日电　由日本农民协会副会长石田宥全率领的日本农业农民代表团一行二十三人，今天下午到达广州。
    代表团应中国农学会邀请前来我国访问，准备考察我国农业合作化的情况。
    晚上，中国农学会广东分会副理事长谢焕庭设宴欢迎客人。
    代表团将在明天早上乘车去北京。
　　日佛教访华亲善使节团到广州
    新华社广州12日电　以日本佛教曹洞禅宗领袖高阶珑仙为首的日本佛教访华亲善使节团一行十六人，今天下午到达广州。他们是应中国佛教协会邀请前来访问我国的。中国佛教协会理事济广法师从北京赶到深圳迎接客人。
    八十二岁的高阶珑仙法师到广州后立即前往六榕寺参拜。
    使节团将在明晨乘火车赴北京。
　　巴西眼科专家西尔瓦夫妇到京
    新华社10日讯　应中华医学会邀请来华访问的巴西眼科专家西尔瓦夫妇，在今日乘飞机到达北京。
    印度亚洲团结委员会代表团回国
    新华社12日讯　以拉·尼赫鲁夫人为首的印度亚洲团结委员会访华代表团全体团员在北京、沈阳、南京、上海、西安等地参观访问后于今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    前往机场欢送的有各有关人民团体负责人李德全、刘宁一、廖承志、赵朴初、陈翰笙等。
