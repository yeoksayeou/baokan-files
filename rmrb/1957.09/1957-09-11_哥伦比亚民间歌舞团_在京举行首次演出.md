Title: Colombian Folk Dance Troupe Holds First Performance in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-11
Page: 4

　　Colombian Folk Dance Troupe
　　Holds First Performance in Beijing
Xinhua News Agency: The Colombian Delia Zapata Olivella Folk Dance Troupe, invited to visit China, gave its opening performance at the Beijing People's Theater on the evening of the 9th. Each of the troupe's ethnically-flavored programs was welcomed by more than a thousand spectators.
    Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi and Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing watched the performance and met with all the members of the dance troupe during the intermission.
    At the opening ceremony held before the performance, Zhao Yimin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, welcomed the first art troupe from South America.
    In the eighteen song and dance programs performed by the dance troupe, they demonstrated the traditional folk art of the Colombian people and their confidence in striving for freedom and independence.
    The dance troupe will perform three more times in Beijing.

### 哥伦比亚民间歌舞团  在京举行首次演出

1957-09-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　哥伦比亚民间歌舞团
　　在京举行首次演出
    据新华社讯　应邀来中国访问的哥伦比亚黛丽雅·萨巴达·奥丽维娅民间歌舞团，9日晚在北京人民剧场举行开幕式演出。歌舞团表演的每一个有民族特色的节目，都受到了一千多观众的欢迎。
    国务院副总理陈毅和文化部部长沈雁冰观看了演出，并且在演出休息时接见了歌舞团的全体人员。
    在演出以前举行的开幕式上，中国人民对外文化协会副会长赵毅敏在讲话中对来自南美洲的第一个艺术团，表示欢迎。
    歌舞团在演出的十八个歌舞节目中，体现了哥伦比亚人民民间的传统艺术和为争取自由和独立的信心。
    歌舞团还将在北京演出三次。
