Title: The Syrian-Egyptian Economic Union Agreement is a Prelude to Political Union. Syrian Premier Says Two Countries Will Establish Complete Union
Author:
Date: 1957-09-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, September 5th: Syrian Premier al-Assali said on September 4th that the economic union agreement signed between Syria and Egypt is “a prelude to the political union of the two countries.” He said that Syria will soon hold talks with Egypt to establish a complete union between the two countries.

Egyptian Minister of Finance Qaisuni said on the same day before leaving Damascus for Greece, that the economic agreement signed by Syria and Egypt on September 3rd "paves the way for the close unity not only of our two countries, but of all Arab countries."

He said that thanks to the instructions of Egyptian President Nasser and Syrian President Kuwatly, the negotiations were quickly and successfully completed. He said that the fraternal relations between Egypt and Syria will be further strengthened by this agreement.

### 叙埃经济联盟协定是政治联盟的序曲  叙总理说两国将确立完全联盟

1957-09-06
第6版()
专栏：

    叙埃经济联盟协定是政治联盟的序曲
    叙总理说两国将确立完全联盟
    新华社5日讯大马士革消息：叙利亚总理阿萨利9月4日说，叙利亚和埃及签订的经济联盟协定是“两国政治联盟的序曲”。他说，叙利亚不久就要和埃及举行会谈以确立两国的完全联盟。
    埃及财政部长凯舒尼同一天在离开大马士革前往希腊以前说，叙利亚和埃及在9月3日签订的经济协定“不仅为我们两国，并且为所有的阿拉伯国家的紧密团结铺平了道路”。
    他说，由于埃及总统纳赛尔和叙利亚总统库阿特利的指示，谈判很快就胜利完成了。他说，叙埃两国的兄弟关系将由于这个协定而得到进一步的加强。
