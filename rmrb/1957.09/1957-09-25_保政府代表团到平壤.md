Title: Bulgarian Government Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1957-09-25
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 24th: The Bulgarian government delegation, led by Bulgarian Council of Ministers Chairman Yugov, arrived in Pyongyang by plane at 11:00 a.m. today. The delegation is visiting Korea at the invitation of the Korean government, and they were warmly welcomed by over 200,000 people.
    Korean Cabinet Premier Kim Il-sung, Supreme People's Assembly Standing Committee Chairman Choi Yong-kun, and other state leaders, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Korea, were at the airport to welcome them.
    Kim Il-sung held a grand banquet to welcome the Bulgarian government delegation headed by Yugov.
    Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, Vice Premiers of the Cabinet Hong Myung-hee, Nam Il, and Park Ui-wan, leaders of the Party and government, heads of various democratic parties and social organizations, and senior generals of the People's Army attended the banquet. Diplomatic envoys from various countries also attended the banquet.

### 保政府代表团到平壤

1957-09-25
第6版()
专栏：

    保政府代表团到平壤
    据新华社平壤24日电　由保加利亚部长会议主席于哥夫率领的保加利亚政府代表团，今天上午十一时乘飞机到达平壤。代表团是应朝鲜政府的邀请到朝鲜访问的，他们受到二十多万群众的热烈欢迎。
    朝鲜内阁首相金日成，最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健等国家领导人和各国驻朝鲜外交使节曾经到机场欢迎。
    金日成举行盛大宴会欢迎以于哥夫为首的保加利亚政府代表团。
    朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健，内阁副首相洪命熹、南日、朴义琓等党和政府领导人，内阁各相，各民主党派、社会团体负责人，人民军高级将领等出席了宴会。出席宴会的还有各国外交使节。
