Title: Harbin River Water Level Rises
Author:
Date: 1957-09-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 2nd – The water level of the Harbin river rose again to 120.02 meters at 6:00 PM today, a three-centimeter increase compared to the same time yesterday. Over the past two days, Ouyang Qin, First Secretary of the Heilongjiang Provincial Party Committee and Governor, and the heads of the Harbin municipal Party and government have taken boats to the various dikes to express their regards to the flood control personnel.

Today, 170,000 people in Harbin participated in the second phase of the dike reinforcement project. The rescue team also salvaged seven dangerous situations.

### 哈尔滨江面水位上升

1957-09-03
第4版()
专栏：

    哈尔滨江面水位上升
    新华社哈尔滨2日电  哈尔滨江面水位今天下午六时又回升到一二○·○二公尺，比昨天同期上涨三公分。两天来，中共黑龙江省委第一书记、省长欧阳钦和哈尔滨市党政负责人分别乘船到各堤坝，慰问防汛人员。
    今天，哈尔滨市有十七万人参加抢修第二期堤坝加固工程。抢险队员还抢救了七处险情。
