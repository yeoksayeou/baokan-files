Title: Under Socialism, Man Will Conquer Nature: Harbin Overcomes Flood Threat
Author:
Date: 1957-09-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 17th—The Harbin Flood Control Headquarters announced today that the city of Harbin has been relieved of the threat of a massive flood. At 10:15 AM, the river's water level had dropped to a safe level of 119 meters, and the water continues to recede. Thus, the people of Harbin, under the leadership of the Party and the government, have overcome the largest flood since hydrological records began.

Today, Harbin is filled with joy. Several hundred firefighters and horticultural workers are cleaning up Stalin Park along the riverbank, preparing to reopen it to visitors the day after tomorrow. In Zhaolin Park, workers are stepping up preparations for a city-wide flood control celebration rally to be held here on the 21st. The army, cadres, workers, and students who participated in the flood control efforts have all resumed their work and studies after a short rest.

### 社会主义制度下人定胜天  哈尔滨战胜洪水威胁

1957-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　社会主义制度下人定胜天
　　哈尔滨战胜洪水威胁
    新华社哈尔滨17日电　哈尔滨市防汛指挥部今天宣布：哈尔滨市已经解除了特大洪水的威胁。上午十点十五分江面水位已经下降到安全水位一百一十九公尺，水势仍在继续下落。至此，哈尔滨人民在党和政府领导下，已经战胜了有水文记载以来的最大洪水。
    今天，哈尔滨市区到处喜气洋洋，几百个消防队员和园艺工人正在打扫江边的斯大林公园，准备在后天重新开放，迎接游人。在兆麟公园里，工人们正在加紧布置，全市防汛庆祝大会将于21日在这里召开。参加防汛工作的军队、干部、工人和学生经过短期休息以后，都已恢复工作和学习。
