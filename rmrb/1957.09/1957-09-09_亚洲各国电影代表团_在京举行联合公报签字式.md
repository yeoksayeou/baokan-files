Title: Asian Countries' Film Delegations Hold Joint Communiqué Signing Ceremony in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-09
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 8th -- The joint communiqué of the film delegations from fourteen countries—Burma, Cambodia, China, India, Indonesia, Japan, Korea, Lebanon, Mongolia, Pakistan, Syria, Tajikistan, Thailand, and Vietnam—participating in the Asian Film Week, was signed in Beijing this afternoon.

After the signing ceremony, the delegations from the fourteen countries toasted each other in celebration and took photos together, hand in hand.

Wang Lanxi, Assistant Minister of Culture and Director of the Film Bureau, attended the signing ceremony.

The film delegations from the various countries will travel to Nanjing by train tonight. The Pakistani delegation will leave Beijing in the next few days and return home via Guangzhou.

### 亚洲各国电影代表团  在京举行联合公报签字式

1957-09-09
第3版()
专栏：

    亚洲各国电影代表团
    在京举行联合公报签字式
    新华社8日讯　参加亚洲电影周的缅甸、柬埔寨、中国、印度、印度尼西亚、日本、朝鲜、黎巴嫩、蒙古、巴基斯坦、叙利亚、塔吉克斯坦、泰国和越南等十四个国家电影代表团的联合公报，今天下午在北京举行签字仪式。
    在签字仪式结束后，十四个国家的代表团互相举杯祝贺，并手携着手在一起照了相。
    文化部部长助理、电影事业管理局局长王阑西出席了签字仪式。
    各国电影代表团今晚乘火车前往南京。巴基斯坦代表团将在日内离京取道广州回国。
