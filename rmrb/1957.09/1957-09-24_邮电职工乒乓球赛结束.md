Title: Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Table Tennis Tournament Concludes
Author:
Date: 1957-09-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 22nd. The National Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Table Tennis Tournament held in Nanjing concluded today. Liu Guozhang from the Shanghai team and Yan Aijin from the Fujian team won the men's and women's championships, respectively. Lin Muxin and Huang Huiyi from the Guangdong team won the men's and women's runner-up titles.

　　The National Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Table Tennis Tournament began on September 15th. Seventy-nine outstanding athletes from the postal and telecommunications systems of twenty-seven provinces, cities, and autonomous regions participated in the competition.

　　Table tennis for postal and telecommunications workers has been widely developed since liberation. According to statistics, more than 1,400 table tennis teams have been established in grassroots units of the national postal and telecommunications system.

### 邮电职工乒乓球赛结束

1957-09-24
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    邮电职工乒乓球赛结束
    据新华社南京22日电　在南京举行的全国邮电职工乒乓球比赛今日结束。上海队刘国璋、福建队严爱金分别获得男女冠军，广东队林木欣、黄慧怡获得男女亚军。
    全国邮电职工乒乓球赛是9月15日开始的。参加比赛的有二十七个省、市和自治区邮电系统的七十九名优秀运动员。
    全国邮电职工的乒乓球运动解放后有了广泛的开展，据统计，目前全国邮电系统基层单位一共建立了一千四百多个乒乓球队。
