Long Live the Friendship of the People of Asian Countries
——Welcome to the Film Delegations of Various Countries Participating in the "Asian Film Week"

The "Asian Film Week," with the participation of sixteen countries, grandly opened in Beijing on August 31. Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, Chairman Guo Moruo, and Mrs. Rameshwari Nehru, Chairperson of the Indian Committee for Asian Solidarity, all attended. Starting from September 1st, many films from various countries have been screened in ten major cities, including Beijing, Shanghai, and Guangzhou, and have been warmly welcomed by the vast audience in our country. This is a grand gathering of Asian film art and a grand meeting of film friends from various countries filled with harmony and friendship. The upper picture is of the opening ceremony of the "Asian Film Week." (Our reporters Cao Guijiang and Wang Zhiyuan photographed)

    亚洲各国人民友好万岁
    ——欢迎参加“亚洲电影周”的各国电影代表团
    有十六个国家参加的“亚洲电影周”已于8月31日在北京隆重开幕。周恩来总理、贺龙副总理、和大郭沫若主席及印度争取亚洲团结委员会主席拉米希瓦里·尼赫鲁夫人均出席参加。从9月1日起，在北京、上海、广州等十个大城市分别上映许多部各国的影片，受到我国广大观众的热烈欢迎。这是一次亚洲各国电影艺术的大荟萃，是一次各国电影朋友的充满和睦友谊感情的大会见。上图是“亚洲电影周”的开幕式。（本报记者曹桂江、王志渊摄）
