Title: Turning the Ideal of Progress into Reality: Chairman Zhou Enlai Thanks Dr. Hatta for His Speech
Author:
Date: 1957-09-25
Page: 4

To His Excellency Dr. Hatta:

On behalf of all the members of the Enlarged Meeting of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference today, I thank you for your speech.

The Chinese people inherited the revolutionary spirit of Mr. Sun Yat-sen, accepted the influence of the October Revolution, and, as you said, combined Marxism-Leninism with the revolutionary practice of China, finally overthrowing the reactionary rule in 1949, enabling the Chinese people to achieve true liberation and establishing our new People's Republic of China.

In 1950, the second year after the establishment of New China, when Dr. Hatta was serving as Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Republic of Indonesia, our two new countries—the Republic of Indonesia and the People's Republic of China—recognized each other and established new diplomatic relations.

Since that time, the people of our two new countries have restored old friendships and developed new ones on this new foundation. Last year, President Sukarno visited China on the eve of our National Day. This year, former Indonesian Vice President Dr. Hatta is visiting China again on the eve of our National Day. This is evidence of the development of the new friendship between our two peoples.

We very much agree with Dr. Hatta's suggestion just now that the Soviet Union and the United States reach a twenty-five-year peace agreement. If such a proposal can be realized, it will give the hope of world peace a more lasting foundation and enable the ideal of human progress to become a reality.

Let the people of our two countries strive against colonialism, for world peace, for friendship among all nations, and for the progress and construction of our respective countries!

The Chinese people and the Indonesian people stand together.

Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Indonesian peoples!

Long live world peace!

Let us once again thank Dr. Hatta for his speech.

### 使进步的理想变为现实  周恩来主席感谢哈达博士的讲话

1957-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

    使进步的理想变为现实
    周恩来主席感谢哈达博士的讲话哈达博士阁下：
    我代表我们今天中国人民政治协商会议扩大会议的全体同人，感谢你的演说。
    中国人民继承了孙中山先生革命的精神，接受了十月革命的影响，并且正如同你所说的，将马克思列宁主义和中国的革命实践结合起来，终于在1949年推翻了反动统治，使中国人民得到了真正的解放，建立了我们新的中华人民共和国。
    在新中国建立的第二年，1950年，正当哈达博士担任印度尼西亚共和国的总理兼外长的时候，我们两个新的国家——印度尼西亚共和国和中华人民共和国就彼此承认，建立了新的邦交。
    从那个时候起，我们两个新的国家的人民，就在这个新的基础上，恢复了旧的友谊，发展了新的友谊。去年苏加诺总统正在我们国庆的前夕来访问了中国。今年，印度尼西亚前副总统哈达博士又在我们的国庆前夕来访问中国。这就是我们两国人民新的友谊在发展的证据。
    我们很同意刚才哈达博士所提出的由苏联和美国达成二十五年和平协议的建议。这样的建议，如果能够实现，将使世界和平有更加持久的希望，使人类进步的理想能够变为现实。
    让我们两国人民为反对殖民主义、为世界和平、为各国友好、为各自国家的进步和建设而奋斗吧！
    中国人民和印度尼西亚人民是站在一起的。
    中国和印度尼西亚两国人民友好万岁！
    世界和平万岁！
    让我们再一次感谢哈达博士的讲话。
