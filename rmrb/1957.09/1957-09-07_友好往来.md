    Friendly Exchanges
    Colombian Folk Dance Troupe Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, September 6th: The first Colombian dance troupe to visit China, the Colombian Delia Zapata Folk Dance Troupe, a group of thirteen people, arrived in Beijing by train today. The dance troupe is scheduled to hold its first performance in China in Beijing on the 9th.
    The dance troupe, led by Manuel Zapada, came to China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries after participating in the Sixth World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival in Moscow.
    Japanese Ibaraki Prefecture Peace Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, September 5th: The Japanese Ibaraki Prefecture Peace Delegation, a group of twelve people, arrived in Beijing today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.
    Youth Delegations Return to Beijing One After Another
    Xinhua News Agency, September 6th: Youth representatives from Syria, Lebanon, Morocco, and West Africa, who participated in the Sixth World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival and visited China, returned to Beijing by train this morning after visiting Nanjing and Shanghai.
    Xinhua News Agency, September 5th: Thirteen youth representatives from Burma, India, Nepal, and Ceylon, who participated in the Sixth World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival and visited China, returned to Beijing by train today from Shenyang.
    Five West German youth representatives also returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon after visiting Shanghai and Hangzhou.

### 友好往来

1957-09-07
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    哥伦比亚一民间歌舞团到京
    新华社6日讯　第一个来中国访问的哥伦比亚的歌舞团——哥伦比亚黛丽雅萨巴达民间歌舞团一行十三人，今天坐火车到达北京。歌舞团预定9日在北京举行访华的首次演出。
    这个由麦努埃尔·萨巴达率领的歌舞团，是在莫斯科参加了第六届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节以后，应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的。
    日本茨城县和平代表团到京
    新华社5日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来中国参观访问的日本茨城县和平代表团一行十二人，在今天到达北京。
    青年代表团陆续回京
    据新华社6日讯　参加第六届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节来中国访问的叙利亚、黎巴嫩、摩洛哥、西非等四个国家的青年代表，访问南京和上海以后，今天上午乘火车回到北京。
    据新华社5日讯　参加第六届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节后来中国访问的缅甸、印度、尼泊尔和锡兰青年代表一行十三人自沈阳乘火车今天回到北京。
    西德青年代表五人也在上海和杭州参观后，于今天下午乘飞机回到北京。
