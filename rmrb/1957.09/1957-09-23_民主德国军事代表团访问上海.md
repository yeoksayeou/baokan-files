Title: East German Military Delegation Visits Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1957-09-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, September 22nd. The military delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by General Willi Stoph and accompanied by General Li Da, Deputy Minister of National Defense, and Colonel Song Denghua, our military attaché in the German Democratic Republic, arrived in Shanghai by special plane on the morning of the 22nd for a visit, after leaving Nanjing.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng, Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Xu Jianguo, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, and Vice Admiral Tao Yong, Commander of the East China Sea Fleet of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and others.

In the evening, Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng hosted a banquet to welcome all members of the military delegation.

### 民主德国军事代表团访问上海

1957-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国军事代表团访问上海
    新华社上海22日电　以维利·斯多夫上将为首的德意志民主共和国军事代表团，在国防部副部长李达上将及我国驻德意志民主共和国武官宋登华上校陪同下，于22日上午由南京乘专机飞抵上海访问。
    到机场欢迎的有中国人民解放军上海警备区司令员王必成中将，中共上海市委书记、上海市副市长许建国以及中国人民解放军东海舰队司令员陶勇海军中将等人。
    晚间，王必成中将设宴欢迎军事代表团全体人员。
