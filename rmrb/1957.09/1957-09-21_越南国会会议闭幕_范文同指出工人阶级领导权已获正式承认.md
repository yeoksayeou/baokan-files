Title: Vietnamese National Assembly Meeting Concludes: Pham Van Dong Points Out Leadership of Working Class Has Been Formally Recognized
Author:
Date: 1957-09-21
Page: 6

Comprehensive report from our newspaper: According to Xinhua News Agency, the seventh session of the Vietnamese National Assembly, which opened on the 10th of this month, lasted nine days and concluded on the 19th.

On the first day of the congress, President Ho Chi Minh delivered a report on his recent visits to fraternal countries. Ton Quang Phiet, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly, delivered a report on the work of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly, and Tran Huy Lieu, member of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly, delivered a report on the work of revising the constitution.

On the 11th, the National Assembly heard reports from the government on issues such as the national budget, the 1957 national plan, the struggle for national unification, the Meilin Water Gate incident, the draft Trade Union Law, and the rectification of errors in land reform and organizational consolidation.

After thorough discussion, the meeting unanimously adopted the government's final national accounts for 1956 and the national budget for 1957 on the 18th.

On the 19th, the meeting was declared closed. Premier Pham Van Dong spoke at the closing session, saying that the passage of the Vietnam Trade Union Law in this National Assembly is a matter of great significance for Vietnam at present. The National Assembly has formally recognized the leadership of the working class in the revolutionary cause of Vietnam and its leadership over the country.

Pham Van Dong said that if we want to continue to progress and build a socialist country, we must constantly use the weapon of criticism and self-criticism to improve our work.

### 越南国会会议闭幕   范文同指出工人阶级领导权已获正式承认

1957-09-21
第6版()
专栏：

    越南国会会议闭幕
    范文同指出工人阶级领导权已获正式承认
    本报综合报道  据新华社消息：本月10日开幕的越南国会第七次会议，历时九天，已于19日闭幕。
    大会的第一天，胡志明主席作了关于最近访问各兄弟国家的报告，国会常务委员会副委员长孙光阀作了关于国会常务委员会的工作报告，国会常务委员会委员陈辉燎作了关于修改宪法工作的报告。
    11日，国会听取了政府所作的关于国家预算、1957年国家计划、争取国家统一的斗争、梅林水闸事件、工会法草案、土地改革和整顿组织的纠偏工作情况等问题的报告。
    会议经过充分讨论以后，18日一致通过政府关于1956年国家决算和1957年国家预算。
    19日，会议宣告闭幕。范文同总理在闭幕会上讲了话，他说，越南工会法在这次国会得到通过，是当前越南具有重大意义的事情。国会已经正式承认了工人阶级在越南革命事业中的领导权和对国家的领导权。
    范文同说，我们要不断进步，要建设社会主义国家，我们就必须不断地运用批评和自我批评的武器来改进我们的工作。
