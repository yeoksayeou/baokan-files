Title: Recording the Operation of Artificial Satellites: The Soviet Union Builds the First Observation Station
Author: 
Date: 1957-09-12
Page: 6

    Recording the Operation of Artificial Satellites
    The Soviet Union Builds the First Observation Station
    Xinhua News Agency, 11th—According to TASS: The artificial satellite observation station belonging to the Ulyanov-Lenin Kazan State University Observatory in the Soviet Union has recently been completed. This is one of the many artificial satellite observation stations being built in the Soviet Union. According to Associate Professor Yevdokimov, the head of the station, the station is equipped with "ATE-1" optical instruments specially designed and manufactured for observing the operation of artificial satellites. Any artificial satellite launched from any location in the Soviet Union, as long as it enters the observation range of this station, can have its operation accurately recorded by this station.

### 纪录人造卫星运行情况  苏联建成第一座观测站

1957-09-12
第6版()
专栏：

    纪录人造卫星运行情况
    苏联建成第一座观测站
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社讯：苏联国立乌里扬诺夫—列宁喀山大学天文台所属的人造卫星观测站最近已经建成，这是苏联正在建成的许多人造卫星观测站之一。据这个站的站长叶夫多基莫夫副教授指出，这个站装备了一些为观测人造卫星的运行而专门设计和制造的“阿特一一号”光学仪器。在苏联任何地点发射出来的人造卫星，只要进入了这个站的观测范围，这个站就可以把它的运行情况准确地纪录下来。
