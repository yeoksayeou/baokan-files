    Erratum
    Regarding the article "Lanxi White Lotus Production Increases Sixfold" on the fifth page of the August 25th issue, the author has submitted the following corrections:
    ① The total output of 1,270,000 jin should be changed to 1,270 dan.
    ② The export of 100,000 jin should be changed to 100 dan.
    ③ The shipment of 700,000 jin to Hangzhou, Shanghai and other places should be changed to 700 dan. 　Jiang Tao

### 来函更正

专栏：

    来函更正
    8月25日第五版“兰溪白莲增产六倍”一文，作者来函作如下更正：
    ①总产量一百二十七万斤应改为一千二百七十担。
    ②将出口十万斤应改为一百担。
    ③运销杭州、上海等地七十万斤应改为七百担。　姜　韬
