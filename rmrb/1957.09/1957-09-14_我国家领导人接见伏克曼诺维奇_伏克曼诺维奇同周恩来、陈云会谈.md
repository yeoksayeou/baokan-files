Title: Our National Leaders Receive Vukmanović, Vukmanović Holds Talks with Zhou Enlai and Chen Yun
Author:
Date: 1957-09-14
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 13th: Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai received Vukmanović, Vice President of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia, and his entourage at Qin Zheng Dian in Zhongnanhai this evening.
　　Present at the reception were Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yun and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Peng Zhen, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, and Yugoslav Ambassador to China Popović.
　　Xinhua News Agency, September 13th: Vukmanović, Vice President of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia, held talks with Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yun this morning and afternoon, respectively.
　　Participating in the talks from the Yugoslav side were Velebit, Deputy Secretary-General of the Federal State Secretariat of Yugoslavia, Gucina, Governor of the National Bank, and Popović, Yugoslav Ambassador to China, among others.
　　Participating in the talks from the Chinese side were Zhang Wentian, Ye Jizhuang, Zeng Yongquan, and Cao Juru.

### 我国家领导人接见伏克曼诺维奇　伏克曼诺维奇同周恩来、陈云会谈

1957-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国家领导人接见伏克曼诺维奇
　　伏克曼诺维奇同周恩来、陈云会谈
    新华社13日讯  朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理，今晚在中南海勤政殿接见了南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席伏克曼诺维奇和他的随行人员。
    接见时在座的有：国务院副总理陈云、邓小平，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真，外交部副部长曾涌泉以及南斯拉夫驻中国大使波波维奇。
    新华社13日讯  南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席伏克曼诺维奇，今天上午和下午分别同周恩来总理和陈云副总理会谈。
    南斯拉夫方面参加会谈的，有南斯拉夫联邦国务秘书处副秘书长维莱比特，国家银行行长古齐纳和南斯拉夫驻中国大使波波维奇等人。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有张闻天、叶季壮、曾涌泉和曹菊如。
