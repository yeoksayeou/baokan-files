Title: Japanese Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1957-09-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 17th. The Japanese Trade Delegation to China, composed of the League of Diet Members Promoting Japan-China Trade, the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, and the Japan-China Export-Import Association, led by the head of the delegation, Masanosuke Ikeda, arrived in Beijing today. Those who greeted them at the airport included Lei Renmin and Li Zhuchen, Vice-Chairmen of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, as well as managers from the Bank of China and various professional companies.

### 日访华通商使节团到京

1957-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    日访华通商使节团到京
    新华社17日讯　由日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟、日本国际贸易促进协会、日中输出入组合三个团体组成的日本访华通商使节团，在团长池田正之辅率领下，在今天到达北京。到机场迎接的有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民、李烛尘及中国银行和各专业公司的经理等。
