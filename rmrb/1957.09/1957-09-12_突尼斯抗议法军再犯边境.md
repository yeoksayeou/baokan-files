Title: Tunisia Protests French Military Intrusion Across Border Again
Author:
Date: 1957-09-12
Page: 7
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, 11th: The Tunisian Foreign Ministry delivered a note to the French Chargé d'Affaires in Tunisia, Bernard, on the evening of the 10th, strongly protesting the French army's renewed intrusion into the Tunisian border.

According to a communiqué issued by the Tunisian Information Department, on the morning of the 10th, a batch of French troops intruded into Henchir Zeddab in the Kasserine region, eighteen miles inside Tunisia. They illegally searched residents' homes and kidnapped four Tunisian citizens to Algeria.

This is the third time this month that French troops have illegally intruded into the Tunisian border.

The communiqué pointed out that Tunisian government officials reminded Bernard that Tunisia is determined to exercise its legitimate right to defend its territorial integrity.

### 突尼斯抗议法军再犯边境

1957-09-12
第7版()
专栏：

　　突尼斯抗议法军再犯边境
    新华社11日讯　突尼斯消息：突尼斯外交部10日晚间交给法国驻突尼斯代办贝纳尔一份致法国政府的照会，强烈抗议法军再度侵入突尼斯边境。
    据突尼斯情报部发表的公报说，10日早晨，法国部队一批侵入了突尼斯境内十八英里卡塞林地区的亨希尔泽达布。他们非法地搜查了居民的住宅，并且把四个突尼斯公民绑架到阿尔及利亚去。
    法军非法侵入突尼斯边境在本月以来这是第三次。
    公报指出，突尼斯政府官员提醒贝纳尔说，突尼斯决心使用保卫自己领土完整的合法权利。
