Title: British Chargé d'Affaires Ad Interim Mr. Youens Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Mr. Youens, the new British Chargé d'Affaires Ad Interim to China, arrived in Beijing this afternoon. Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, greeted him at the station.
