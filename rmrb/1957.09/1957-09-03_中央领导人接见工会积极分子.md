Title: Central Leaders Receive Labor Union Activists
Author:
Date: 1957-09-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd — Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, and Zhu De, Vice Chairmen of the CCP Central Committee, Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary, Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, Peng Zhen, Li Fuchun, and He Long, members of the Central Political Bureau, and Zhang Wentian, Lu Dingyi, Kang Sheng, and Bo Yibo, alternate members, this afternoon received all the representatives attending the National Conference of Labor Union Activists. Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Vice Chairmen Liu Changsheng and Zhu Xuefan were also present at the reception.

### 中央领导人接见工会积极分子

1957-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中央领导人接见工会积极分子
    新华社2日讯  中共中央副主席刘少奇、周恩来、朱德，总书记邓小平，中央政治局委员林伯渠、董必武、彭真、李富春、贺龙，候补委员张闻天、陆定一、康生、薄一波，今天下午接见了出席全国工会积极分子代表会议的全体代表。接见时，中华全国总工会主席赖若愚，副主席刘长胜、朱学范也在座。
