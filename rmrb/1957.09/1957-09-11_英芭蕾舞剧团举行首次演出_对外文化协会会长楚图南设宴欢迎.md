Title: British Ballet Rambert Holds First Performance; President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Chu Tunan, Hosts Banquet to Welcome the Troupe
Author:
Date: 1957-09-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 10th: The British Ballet Rambert held its first performance in China tonight at the Tianqiao Theater in Beijing.

Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Vice Ministers of Culture Xia Yan and Zhang Zhixiang, and Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian and Zhang Hanfu, among others, attended tonight's performance. During the intermission, Chen Yi met with the director of the ballet troupe, Marie Rambert, and some of the principal dancers. After the performance, Chen Yi, accompanied by the British Chargé d'Affaires to China, Mr. 文郁生 (Wen Yusheng), went on stage to meet with all the members of the ballet troupe and took photos with them.

Xinhua News Agency: The President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Chu Tunan, hosted a banquet in Beijing on the evening of September 9th to welcome the British Ballet Rambert.

The British Chargé d'Affaires to China, Mr. Wen Yusheng, was invited to attend the banquet tonight. Also attending the banquet tonight were Vice Minister of Culture Zhang Zhixiang, and our country's Chargé d'Affaires to the United Kingdom, Huan Xiang, among others.

### 英芭蕾舞剧团举行首次演出  对外文化协会会长楚图南设宴欢迎

1957-09-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　英芭蕾舞剧团举行首次演出
    对外文化协会会长楚图南设宴欢迎
    据新华社10日讯　英国蓝伯特芭蕾舞剧团今晚在北京天桥剧场举行访华的首次演出。
    国务院副总理陈毅，文化部副部长夏衍、张致祥和外交部副部长张闻天、章汉夫等人出席观看了今晚的演出。在演出休息时，陈毅接见了芭蕾舞剧团团长玛丽·蓝伯特和一些主要演员。在演出结束以后，陈毅还由英国驻中国代办文郁生陪同，到台上同芭蕾舞剧团全体人员见面，并在一起照了像。
    据新华社讯　中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，9日晚在北京设宴欢迎英国蓝伯特芭蕾舞剧团。
    英国驻中国代办文郁生应邀出席了今晚的宴会。出席今晚宴会的还有文化部副部长张致祥和我国驻英国代办宦乡等人。
