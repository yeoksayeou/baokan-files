Title: Sarit Thanarat Seizes Thai Military Power, Appointed Supreme Commander of the Armed Forces
Author:
Date: 1957-09-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th, Bangkok: A Bangkok dispatch reports that Thai radio broadcast on the evening of September 27th that the King of Thailand had appointed Marshal Sarit Thanarat as the Supreme Commander of the Armed Forces. Thai radio also broadcast a royal announcement stating that Sarit Thanarat had resigned from his position as Military Governor of Bangkok.

Martial law is still in effect in Bangkok, but the situation is calm.

### 乃沙立独揽泰国军权受任武装部队最高司令

1957-09-30
第6版()
专栏：

    乃沙立独揽泰国军权
    受任武装部队最高司令
    据新华社28日讯　曼谷消息：泰国电台9月27日晚广播，泰国国王已经任命乃沙立元帅为武装部队最高司令。泰国电台还广播国王的一项公告说，乃沙立已经辞去了曼谷军事总督的职务。
    目前曼谷仍在戒严中，但是局面平静。
