Title: Hammarskjöld Re-elected as UN Secretary-General
Author:
Date: 1957-09-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 27th: The United Nations General Assembly on the 26th, by a vote of eighty to zero, appointed Dag Hammarskjöld to serve another five-year term as Secretary-General of the United Nations. Prior to this, the Security Council had unanimously recommended him to the General Assembly as Secretary-General.

### 哈马舍尔德连任联合国秘书长

1957-09-28
第6版()
专栏：

    哈马舍尔德连任联合国秘书长
    新华社27日讯　　纽约消息：联合国大会26日以八十票对零票任命哈马舍尔德再做任期五年的联合国秘书长。在这以前，安全理事会已经全体一致地向大会推荐他作秘书长。
