Title: GDR Delegation to Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1957-09-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, September 26th: The delegation from the German Democratic Republic, led by Deputy Prime Minister Oelsner, concluded its visit here and departed for Shanghai by special plane at 11:50 this morning.

The GDR delegation arrived in Wuhan on the 23rd. During their stay in Wuhan, the distinguished guests visited the Yangtze River Bridge, the construction site of the Wuhan Iron and Steel Works, the construction site of the Heavy Machinery Plant, and other places. They also had extensive contact with people from all walks of life.

### 民主德国代表团赴上海

1957-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国代表团赴上海
    据新华社武汉26日电　由厄斯纳副总理率领的民主德国代表团，在此间结束了访问活动后，今日上午十一时五十分乘专机前往上海。
    民主德国代表团是23日到达武汉的。在武汉期间，贵宾们参观了长江大桥和正在施工的武钢工地、重型机床厂工地等处。并同各界人士进行了广泛的接触。
