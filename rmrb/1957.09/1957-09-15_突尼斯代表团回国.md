Title: Tunisian Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-09-15
Page: 4

    Tunisian Delegation Returns Home
    (Xinhua News Agency, 14th) The Tunisian cultural delegation of eight people left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.
    The delegation visited China in mid-August at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 突尼斯代表团回国

1957-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    突尼斯代表团回国
    据新华社14日讯  突尼斯文化代表团一行八人在今日早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。
    代表团是应中国人民对外文化协会的邀请，在8月中旬到我国访问的。
