Title: National Women's Congress to be Held in Beijing on the 9th

Author:

Date: 1957-09-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 3rd. The Fourth Plenary Meeting of the Second Executive Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation was held in Beijing from the 2nd to the 3rd.

The meeting decided that the Third National Congress of Chinese Women will be held in Beijing on September 9th. The expected duration is eleven days.

The meeting discussed in detail the various preparatory works for the Women's Congress. The meeting reviewed and approved the work report that the Second Executive Committee prepared to make to the Congress, the new constitution of the Women's Federation submitted to the Congress for discussion, and the report on amending the constitution. The meeting also discussed and approved the organizational structure of the Women's Congress and the agenda of the Congress.

### 全国妇代大会九日在京举行

1957-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    全国妇代大会九日在京举行
    新华社3日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会第二届执行委员会第四次扩大会议2日到3日在北京举行。
    会议决定中国妇女第三次全国代表大会9月9日在北京举行。预定会期为十一天。
    会议详细讨论了有关妇代大会的各项准备工作。会议审议并通过了第二届执行委员会准备向大会所作的工作报告和提交大会讨论的妇女联合会的新章程以及修改章程的报告。会议还讨论并通过了妇代大会的组织机构及大会的日程等问题。
