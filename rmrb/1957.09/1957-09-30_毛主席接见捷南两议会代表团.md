Title: Chairman Mao Receives Czechoslovak and Yugoslav Parliamentary Delegations
Author:
Date: 1957-09-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: Chairman Mao Zedong received the Czechoslovak National Assembly delegation headed by Fierlinger and the Prague Central People's Committee delegation headed by Svoboda tonight. Present at the reception were Peng Zhen, Li Xuefeng, Zeng Yongquan, Liu Guanyi, Yu Xinqing, and Feng Jiping. Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Bušiňák, and his wife were also present at the reception.
    Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: Chairman Mao Zedong received the Yugoslav Federal National Assembly delegation headed by Stambolic tonight. Present at the reception were Peng Zhen, Zeng Yongquan, Wu Xiuquan, Liu Geping, Wu Xinyu, and Yu Xinqing. Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popović, was also present at the reception. (Attached pictures)
    Chairman Mao Receives the Czechoslovak Parliamentary Delegation and the Prague Central People's Committee Delegation. Fifth from the left in the front row is Fierlinger, seventh is Svoboda, and second is Bušiňák.
    Chairman Mao Receives the Yugoslav Parliamentary Delegation. Sixth from the left in the front row is Stambolic, and eighth is Popović. (Photographs by Xinhua News Agency reporters Hou Bo and Zou Jiandong)

### 毛主席接见捷南两议会代表团

1957-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见捷南两议会代表团
    新华社29日讯　毛泽东主席今天夜里接见了以费林格为首的捷克斯洛伐克国民议会代表团和以斯沃波达为首的布拉格市中央人民委员会代表团。接见时在座的，有彭真、李雪峰、曾涌泉、刘贯一、余心清、冯基平。接见时，捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使布希尼亚克和夫人也在座。
    新华社29日讯　毛泽东主席今天晚上接见了以斯坦鲍利奇为首的南斯拉夫联邦国民议会代表团。接见时在座的，有彭真、曾涌泉、伍修权、刘格平、武新宇、余心清。接见时，南斯拉夫驻中国大使波波维奇也在座。（附图片）

    毛主席接见南议会代表团。前排左起第六人是斯坦鲍利奇、第八人是波波维奇　新华社记者　侯波、邹健东摄
