Title: Renewed Fierce Fighting in Algeria
Author:
Date: 1957-09-01
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Algiers, August 31st: Fierce fighting broke out again between Algerian National Liberation Army troops and French forces on the evening of August 29th in the area north of Tlemcen. The French army deployed air force support.
    In the border area close to Tunisia, several French army outposts have been surrounded by the Algerian National Liberation Army in recent days. In addition, near Collo, a French train derailed after hitting a landmine on August 29th. Many people were injured. A French military vehicle was also destroyed by a landmine ten kilometers south of Fort de l'eau. The day before, a French "Broussard" reconnaissance aircraft was shot down in the Saint-Denis-du-Sig region, and the flight personnel were killed on the spot.

### 阿尔及利亚激战再起

1957-09-01
第7版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚激战再起
    新华社31日讯  阿尔及尔消息：阿尔及利亚民族解放军部队与法军之间8月29日晚上在德兰仙以北地区又发生激战。法军出动了空军助战。
    在接近突尼斯交界的边境地区，近几天来有几个法军岗哨被阿尔及利亚民族解放军包围。此外，在科洛附近8月29日有一列法国火车中地雷翻车。有不少人受伤。在福姆图布以南十公里的地方也有一辆法国军用汽车被地雷炸毁。在这前一天，在圣但尼杜西格地区还有一架法国“布鲁沙尔”型侦察机被击落，飞行人员当场毙命。
