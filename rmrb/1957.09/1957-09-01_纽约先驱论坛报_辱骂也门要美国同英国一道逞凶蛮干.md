Title: "New York Herald Tribune" Abuses Yemen, Wants the US to Join Britain in Acting Fiercely and Arbitrarily

Author:
Date: 1957-09-01
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, August 31st, New York: The "New York Herald Tribune" on August 30th demanded "a decisive and forceful course of action" to "suppress" Yemen.

The newspaper abused Yemen as "a chaotic land," blaming it for being "close" to Egypt and the Soviet Union. It demanded that the United States, along with Britain, Saudi Arabia, Iraq, and Iran, immediately "form an alliance and adopt a common policy" to deal with Yemen, without "delay."

### “纽约先驱论坛报”辱骂也门要美国同英国一道逞凶蛮干

1957-09-01
第7版()
专栏：

    “纽约先驱论坛报”辱骂也门要美国同英国一道逞凶蛮干
    新华社31日讯  纽约消息：“纽约先驱论坛报”8月30日要求“采取干脆有力的行动方针”来“压制”也门。
    这家报纸辱骂也门是“一块乱七八糟的土地”，责备它“靠近”埃及和苏联。它要求美国同英国以及沙特阿拉伯、伊拉克和伊朗等国立即“结成联盟，采取共同的政策”，去对付也门，而不要“拖延”。
