Title: Delegation of the Japan Teachers' Union Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: A fifteen-member delegation from the Japan Teachers' Union, headed by Hirahara Miyoshi, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

The delegation is visiting China to exchange the "Temporary Agreement on Strengthening Friendship and Unity between Chinese and Japanese Teachers" with the National Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China. In February this year, the Educational Study Group of the Japan Teachers' Union, which visited our country, signed this agreement with the National Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China.

### 日教职员工会代表团到京

1957-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

    日教职员工会代表团到京
    据新华社23日讯　以平垣美代司为首的日本教职员工会代表团一行十五人，今天下午乘飞机到北京。
    代表团是来中国同中国教育工会全国委员会交换“加强中日两国教职员的友好与团结问题的临时协定”的。今年2月间，访问我国的日本教职员工会教育考察团同中国教育工会全国委员会签订了这项协定。
