Title: The Heibergs Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Norwegian Writers' Association Chairman Heiberg and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane on the morning of the 16th for a visit to China.

### 海贝尔格夫妇到京

1957-09-19
第3版()
专栏：

    海贝尔格夫妇到京
    新华社18日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的挪威作家协会主席海贝尔格及其夫人在16日上午乘飞机到达北京。
