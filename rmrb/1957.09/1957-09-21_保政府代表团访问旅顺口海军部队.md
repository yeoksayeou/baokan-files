Title: Bulgarian Government Delegation Visits Lushunkou Naval Forces

Author: 
Date: 1957-09-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, September 30th: The Bulgarian government delegation, led by Chairman Yugov, visited the Chinese People's Liberation Army naval forces in Lushunkou today, accompanied by Song Li, the mayor of Lüda City, and others. Chairman Yugov inspected the naval honor guard on the coast. Then, the distinguished guests toured the fleet of ships. On the warships, Chairman Yugov addressed the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy.

The distinguished guests took group photos with the naval officers and soldiers on the warships. Near noon, the distinguished guests watched a performance by the People's Liberation Army Navy fleet from the warships off the coast of Lushunkou.

### 保政府代表团访问旅顺口海军部队

1957-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

    保政府代表团
    访问旅顺口海军部队
    据新华社旅大30日电　以于哥夫主席为首的保加利亚政府代表团，今天由旅大市市长宋黎等陪同访问了旅顺口的中国人民解放军海军部队。于哥夫主席在海岸上检阅了海军仪仗队。然后，贵宾们参观了编队的舰艇。在军舰上，于哥夫主席向中国人民解放军海军官兵讲了话。
    贵宾们在军舰上同海军官兵合影留念。快中午时，贵宾们在旅顺口外从军舰上观看了人民解放军海军舰队的表演。
