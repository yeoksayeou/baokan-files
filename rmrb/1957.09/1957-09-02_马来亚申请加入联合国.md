Title: Malaya Applies to Join the United Nations
Author:
Date: 1957-09-02
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 1st: Malayan Prime Minister Rahman telegraphed United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld, requesting him to submit Malaya's application to join the United Nations for discussion at the next Security Council meeting.

### 马来亚申请加入联合国

1957-09-02
第6版()
专栏：

    马来亚申请加入联合国
    据新华社1日讯  纽约消息：马来亚总理拉赫曼打电报给联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德，请他将马来亚要求加入联合国的申请提交下次的安全理事会会议讨论。
