Title: Bulgarian Government Delegation Visits North Korea
Author:
Date: 1957-09-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 24th. The Bulgarian government delegation, headed by Chairman Yugov, left Shenyang by special plane at 8:40 this morning to visit the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of Korea, Li Dong-geon, who came to Shenyang to welcome the Bulgarian government delegation, accompanied them.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the distinguished guests today included Qiu Youwen, Deputy Governor of Liaoning Province, Zhang Like, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, and people from all walks of life. Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of China, Cao Xiangren, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building Industry, and Zhou Zhuan'an, Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria, also went to the airport to see them off.

Kim Yong-bin, the Korean Consul General in Shenyang, also went to the airport to see them off.

After arriving in Shenyang on the 21st, the Bulgarian government delegation visited factories, the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall, and toured the workers' village and the Beiling and Nanhu scenic spots.

### 保政府代表团去朝鲜访问

1957-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

    保政府代表团去朝鲜访问
    新华社沈阳24日电　以于哥夫主席为首的保加利亚政府代表团今天上午八时四十分乘专机离开沈阳去朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访问。特来沈阳迎接保加利亚政府代表团的朝鲜外务省副相李东健陪同前往。
    今天到机场欢送贵宾的，有辽宁省副省长仇友文，沈阳市副市长张力克和各界人士。我国外交部副部长曾涌泉，第一机械工业部副部长曹祥仁和我国驻保加利亚大使周竹安也都到机场欢送。
    朝鲜驻沈阳总领事金龙彬也到机场欢送。
    保加利亚政府代表团21日到达沈阳后，曾参观了工厂、东北工业陈列馆并游览了工人村和北陵、南湖风景区。
