Title: Vice-Chairman Vukmanović Continues Visit to Northeast China from Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1957-09-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, September 17th: After concluding his visit to Mongolia, Yugoslav Federal Executive Council Vice-Chairman Vukmanović and his entourage left here by plane this morning for Northeast China to continue their visit to China.

At the airport, Vukmanović said that his two-day visit to Mongolia felt like being at home. He saw that Mongolia had ample conditions for development.

Mongolian First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Tsend gave a farewell speech, wishing the friendship between the Mongolian and Yugoslav peoples would be further consolidated and developed.

Accompanied by Tsend, Vukmanović shook hands with Mongolian Party and government leaders who came to see him off, including Sürenjav, Molomjamts, Jagvaral, as well as Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia He Ying, Soviet Ambassador Molotov, and diplomatic envoys from various countries, and waved goodbye to the crowd seeing him off.

### 伏克曼诺维奇副主席  由蒙古回东北继续参观

1957-09-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　伏克曼诺维奇副主席
　　由蒙古回东北继续参观
    新华社乌兰巴托17日电　南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席伏克曼诺维奇和他的随行人员结束了在蒙古的访问以后，今天上午从这里坐飞机前往中国东北，继续他们在中国的访问。
    伏克曼诺维奇在飞机场上讲话说，他在蒙古的两天访问就像在自己的家里一样。他看到蒙古有着充分的发展条件。
    蒙古部长会议第一副主席曾德致欢送词，祝蒙南两国人民的友谊更加巩固和发展。
    伏克曼诺维奇由曾德陪同和前来欢送他的蒙古党政负责人苏伦扎布、莫洛木扎姆茨、扎格瓦尔拉以及中国驻蒙古大使何英、苏联大使莫洛托夫和各国使节握手告别，并且向欢送他的人群挥手告别。
