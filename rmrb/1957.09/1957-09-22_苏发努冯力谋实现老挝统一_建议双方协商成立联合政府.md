Title: Souphanouvong Strives to Achieve Lao Unity, Suggests Negotiations for a Coalition Government

Author:

Date: 1957-09-22
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 20th – Vientiane dispatch: Prince Souphanouvong, leader of the Lao Fighting Units, issued a statement on the 11th, suggesting that a coalition government with the participation of the Lao Fighting Units be established through negotiations between the Royal Lao Government and the Lao Fighting Units.

In his statement, Souphanouvong suggested that the Royal Lao Government and the Lao Fighting Units jointly seek effective measures to achieve national unity on the basis of the agreements already concluded. Other measures proposed by Souphanouvong in his statement include: incorporating the administrative organs and troops of the Lao Fighting Units in the provinces of Sam Neua and Phong Saly into the coalition government; guaranteeing the legal existence and activities of the Lao Patriotic Front, the Lao Fighting Units, and other political groups; accepting a certain number of cadres from the Lao Fighting Units to participate in the work of the Royal administrative organs and economic departments; promulgating laws that do not discriminate against or retaliate against the Lao Fighting Units and former resistance personnel; and implementing supplementary elections with the participation of the Lao Fighting Units. Political delegations from both sides in Laos resumed negotiations on September 16. Prior to this, both sides had reached an agreement on December 28 of last year to establish a coalition government to achieve national unity.

### 苏发努冯力谋实现老挝统一  建议双方协商成立联合政府

1957-09-22
第7版()
专栏：

    苏发努冯力谋实现老挝统一
    建议双方协商成立联合政府
    据新华社河内20日电　万象消息：寮国战斗部队领袖苏发努冯11日发表声明，建议通过老挝王国政府同寮国战斗部队之间的谈判，成立有寮国战斗部队参加的联合政府。
    苏发努冯在声明中，建议老挝王国政府同寮国战斗部队双方在已经缔结的协议的基础上共同寻求实现民族统一的有效措施。苏发努冯在声明中提出的其他各项措施是：把桑怒和丰沙里两省的寮国战斗部队行政机关和部队并入联合政府；保障老挝爱国战线和寮国战斗部队及其他政治团体的合法存在和活动；接受一定数量的寮国战斗部队的干部参加王国行政机关和经济部门工作；颁布不歧视和不报复寮国战斗部队和前抗战人员的法律；实行有寮国战斗部队参加的补充选举。老挝双方的政治代表团已经在9月16日恢复谈判。在此以前，双方曾经在去年12月28日达成关于成立联合政府以实现国家统一的协议。
