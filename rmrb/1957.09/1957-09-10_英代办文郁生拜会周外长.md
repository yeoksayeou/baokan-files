Title: British Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Mr. Wen Yuesheng calls on Foreign Minister Zhou

Author:
Date: 1957-09-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th. Mr. Wen Yuesheng, the newly appointed British Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, called on Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, this afternoon. Present at the meeting were Huang Hua, Director of the Department of Western European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

### 英代办文郁生拜会周外长

1957-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    英代办文郁生拜会周外长
    新华社9日讯　英国新任驻我国代办文郁生先生今天下午拜会中华人民共和国外交部部长周恩来。在座的有外交部西欧司司长黄华、礼宾司副司长马振武。
