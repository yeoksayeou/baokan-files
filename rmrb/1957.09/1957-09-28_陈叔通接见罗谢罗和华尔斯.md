Title: Chen Shutong Receives Rochereau and Walls
Author:
Date: 1957-09-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 27th: Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received French Senator Henri Rochereau and Deputy Walls this morning.

Present at the reception were Nan Hanchen, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Wu Kejian, Deputy Secretary-General, and others.

### 陈叔通接见罗谢罗和华尔斯

1957-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

    陈叔通接见罗谢罗和华尔斯
    新华社27日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长陈叔通今天上午接见了法国议会参议员亨利·罗谢罗和众议员华尔斯。
    接见时在座的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员南汉宸、副秘书长吴克坚等人。
