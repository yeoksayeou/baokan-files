Title: Syrian Newspapers Prominently Publish Soviet Note

Author:

Date: 1957-09-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, September 5th. All newspapers here today published news of the Soviet note to the governments of the United States, Britain, and France on the Middle East situation on the front page with headlines spanning the entire page.

In addition to publishing the Soviet note, "Al-Nour," "Al-Raya," and "Al-Yaum" also published excerpts from the editorial of the Beijing "People's Daily" on September 4th, under the headlines: "Chinese Strongly Warn the United States." "Immediately stop the United States from carrying out aggression against Syria, it will suffer the same fate as Britain and France in Egypt."

### 叙报显著刊载苏联照会

1957-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    叙报显著刊载苏联照会
    新华社大马士革5日电　这里的所有各报今天在第一版以横贯全页的大标题刊登了苏联就中东局势致美国、英国和法国政府的照会的消息。
    “光明报”、“旗帜报”和“今天报”除了刊载苏联的照会外，还刊登了9月4日北京“人民日报”的社论摘要，它们的标题是：“中国人强有力地警告美国。”“立即制止美国对叙利亚进行侵略，它将遭到英国和法国在埃及所遭到的同样的命运。”
