Title: Mrs. Rameshwari Nehru Holds Reception; Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet to Bid Farewell to Entire Delegation

Author:

Date: 1957-09-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: Mrs. Rameshwari Nehru, head of the Indian Asian Solidarity Committee delegation, and all members of the delegation, who are about to leave Beijing, held a reception tonight to say goodbye to people from all walks of life in China.

Among those present at the reception were Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese Asian Solidarity Committee, Vice-Chairmen Mao Dun, Li Dequan, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairwomen of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Deng Yingchao, Shi Liang, Zhang Yun, and prominent figures from all walks of life in China.

Indian Ambassador to China R.K. Nehru and his wife also attended the reception.

Mrs. Rameshwari Nehru, the chairwoman of the Asian Solidarity Committee, said at the meeting that our countries have suffered from the hardships inflicted by imperialism, and we will never allow colonialism and imperialism to make a comeback. We must be vigilant and not allow the conspiracies of those hidden new colonialist and imperialist countries to be realized in any of our Asian and African countries. We must unite and help each other, understand each other's problems, and provide support to countries that are still suffering under colonial rule. She also hoped that the work of the Asian Solidarity Committee could continue to expand, in order to consolidate and develop its achievements, enrich its content, and make it more dynamic.

Guo Moruo said in his speech that the delegation's visit to China not only brought a message of peace, but also brought the fraternal friendship of the Indian people to the Chinese people. He expressed his gratitude for this. Under the leadership of Mrs. Rameshwari Nehru, the Asian Solidarity Committee has promoted greater unity among the people of Asian countries. He hoped that the committee would further unite the people of Asian countries, make greater achievements, and expand this unity to African and Latin American countries, support the people of all countries in their struggle for and defense of national independence, defend world peace, and eliminate war.

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese Asian Solidarity Committee, and his wife hosted a banquet today to bid farewell to all members of the Indian Asian Solidarity Committee delegation to China, headed by Mrs. Rameshwari Nehru.

Also invited to the banquet were Indian Ambassador to China R.K. Nehru and his wife, and Miss P. Bose, Vice-President of the Women's International Democratic Federation.

### 拉·尼赫鲁夫人举行招待会  郭沫若设宴欢送代表团全体人员

1957-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　拉·尼赫鲁夫人举行招待会
　　郭沫若设宴欢送代表团全体人员
    新华社11日讯　行将离开北京的印度亚洲团结委员会代表团团长拉·尼赫鲁夫人和全体团员今晚举行招待会，向中国各界人士辞行。
    出席招待会的，有中国亚洲团结委员会主席郭沫若，副主席茅盾、李德全、廖承志、刘宁一，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超、史良、章蕴和中国各界著名人士。
    印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁和夫人也出席了招待会。
    拉·尼赫鲁夫人，这位亚洲团结委员会的主席在会上说，我们的国家都曾受过帝国主义所给予的苦难，我们决不允许殖民主义、帝国主义卷土重来。我们必须警惕，不许那种隐蔽的新的殖民主义、帝国主义国家的阴谋在我们任何亚非国家实现。我们必须团结互助，了解彼此存在的问题，并且对尚在殖民统治的苦难中的国家给以支援。她还希望亚洲团结委员会的工作能继续扩大，以巩固和发展它的成就，丰富它的内容，使它更富有生命力。
    郭沫若在讲话时说，代表团这次来中国访问，不仅带来了和平的信息，而且带来了印度人民对中国人民兄弟的友情。对此他表示感谢。亚洲团结委员会在拉·尼赫鲁夫人领导下，已促使亚洲各国人民更加团结。他希望委员会进一步团结亚洲各国人民，做出更大成就，并把这种团结扩大到非洲和拉丁美洲各国，支持各国人民争取和保卫民族独立，保卫世界和平，消灭战争。
    据新华社11日讯　中国亚洲团结委员会主席郭沫若和他的夫人今天设宴欢送以拉·尼赫鲁夫人为首的印度亚洲团结委员会访华代表团全体人员。
    应邀出席宴会的还有印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁和夫人以及国际民主妇联副主席普·鲍斯小姐。
