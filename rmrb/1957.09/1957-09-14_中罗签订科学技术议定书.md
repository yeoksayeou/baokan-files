Title: China and Romania Sign Scientific and Technological Protocol
Author:
Date: 1957-09-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, September 11th: The fifth session of the Sino-Romanian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee issued a communique on September 10th, stating that the committee held a meeting in Bucharest from August 28th to September 10th. At the meeting, a series of resolutions aimed at further developing scientific and technological cooperation between the two countries were adopted.

The protocol, signed on September 10th, stipulates that China will provide Romania with technical data in the fields of petroleum, textiles, ceramics, handicrafts, afforestation, medicinal materials, and parasitology, and will accept Romanian technicians for professional internships in printing, ceramics, cotton spinning, silk, and handicrafts production.

Romania will provide China with technical data and samples related to the petroleum industry, petroleum equipment, seeds of various crops, and microbiology, accept Chinese experts to investigate issues such as nitrogen fertilizer, methane gas chemical processing, and canned food production, and send a Romanian expert to China to solve problems related to crude oil electrical desalting units.

The committee also decided to strengthen scientific and technological cooperation between scientific research institutions in the two countries.

### 中罗签订科学技术议定书

1957-09-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　中罗签订科学技术议定书
    据新华社布加勒斯特11日电  中国和罗马尼亚科学和技术合作委员会第五届会议9月10日发表公报说，委员会在8月28日到9月10日在布加勒斯特举行了会议。在会议上通过了一系列的旨在进一步发展两国科学技术合作的决议。
    在9月10日签订的议定书中规定：中国将供给罗马尼亚石油、纺织、陶瓷业、手工艺品、造林、药材和寄生虫学等方面的技术资料，接受罗马尼亚的技术人员在印刷、陶瓷、棉纺、丝绸和手工艺品等生产方面进行专业实习。
    罗马尼亚将供给中国有关石油工业、石油设备、各种农作物的种子和微生物学等技术资料和样品，接受中国的专家考察氮肥、甲烷气化学加工以及罐头生产等问题，并且派遣一名罗马尼亚专家到中国解决与原油电力脱乳装置有关的问题。
    委员会还决定加强两国科学研究机构之间的科学技术合作。
