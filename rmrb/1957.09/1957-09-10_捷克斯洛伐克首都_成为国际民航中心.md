Title: Czechoslovak Capital Becomes an International Civil Aviation Center
Author:
Date: 1957-09-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 9th: The Czechoslovak capital, Prague, has become one of the most important international civil aviation centers in Europe. Its level of international civil aviation transport work can be compared with Zurich and Stockholm, well-known air transport centers in Europe.

At Ruzyne Civil Airport on the outskirts of Prague, 115 airplanes take off and land every day, with an average of one airplane taking off or landing approximately every twelve minutes.

### 捷克斯洛伐克首都  成为国际民航中心

1957-09-10
第6版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克首都
    成为国际民航中心
    据新华社布拉格9日电　捷克斯洛伐克首都布拉格已经成为欧洲最重要的国际民航中心之一，它的国际民航运输工作的水平已经可以和欧洲著名空运中心苏黎世和斯德哥尔摩并列。
    布拉格市郊的鲁兹尼民用机场每天有一百一十五架飞机起飞和降落，平均约每十二分钟就有一架飞机起落。
