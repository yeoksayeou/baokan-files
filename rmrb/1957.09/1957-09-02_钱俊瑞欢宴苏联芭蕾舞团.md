Title: Qian Junrui Hosts Banquet for Soviet Ballet Troupe
Author:
Date: 1957-09-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, September 1st. Qian Junrui, Vice Minister of Culture and Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, held a welcoming banquet in Shanghai on the evening of the 31st to welcome the Novosibirsk Opera and Ballet Theatre of the Soviet Union, which came to our country for a visiting performance.

　　Qian Junrui said in his welcoming speech at the banquet: Soviet ballet, with its long history and tradition, enjoys extremely high honor in our country.

　　We must learn from your outstanding artistic achievements and your fine style of serving the working people.

　　The head of the dance troupe, Ligachev, and the deputy head, Zelimanov, both spoke at the banquet, thanking the hosts for their warm hospitality and support for the dance troupe.

### 钱俊瑞欢宴苏联芭蕾舞团

1957-09-02
第3版()
专栏：

    钱俊瑞欢宴苏联芭蕾舞团
    据新华社上海1日电  文化部副部长、中苏友好协会总会秘书长钱俊瑞，31日晚上在上海举行欢迎宴会，为来我国作访问演出的苏联新西伯利亚歌舞院芭蕾舞团洗尘。
    钱俊瑞在宴会上致欢迎词说：有着悠久历史传统的苏联芭蕾舞，在我国享有极高的荣誉。
    我们要学习你们卓越的艺术成就，学习你们优良地为劳动人民服务的作风。
    舞蹈团的团长利加乔夫和副团长泽利马诺夫都在宴会上讲了话，感谢主人的盛情招待和给予舞蹈团的支持。
