In September, schools in the southern states of the United States opened. The racists who controlled state power firmly obstructed the abolition of the segregation system in public schools. Orval Faubus, the Governor of Arkansas, dispatched hundreds of fully armed National Guard troops to stand guard in front of Little Rock Central High School to prevent nine black students from attending school.

专栏：

    9月间，美国南部各州中小学校开学，掌握州权的种族主义者坚决阻挠公立学校取消种族隔离制度。阿肯色州长奥·法布斯派遣了数百名全付武装的国民警卫队，在小石城中央中学门前布岗，阻止九名黑人学生上学。
