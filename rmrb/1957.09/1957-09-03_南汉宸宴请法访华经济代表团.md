Title: Nan Hanchen Hosts Banquet for French Economic Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1957-09-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd September. Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the French Economic Delegation to China.

Among those present were Lei Renmin and Ji Chao Ding, Vice-Chairmen of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Xue Muqiao, Lu Xuzhang, and other responsible persons from China's trade, banking and economic circles.

### 南汉宸宴请法访华经济代表团

1957-09-03
第4版()
专栏：

    南汉宸宴请法访华经济代表团
    新华社2日讯  中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸今天晚上设宴欢迎法国访华经济代表团。
    出席作陪的有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民、冀朝鼎，委员薛暮桥、卢绪章及我国贸易、银行和经济界的负责人等。
