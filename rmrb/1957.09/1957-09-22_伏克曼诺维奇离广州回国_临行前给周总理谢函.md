Title: Vukmanović Leaves Guangzhou for Home, Sends Thank-You Letter to Premier Zhou Before Departure
Author:
Date: 1957-09-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 21st: S. Vukmanović, Vice-President of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia, and his entourage concluded their visit to our country and left Guangzhou for Hong Kong by train at 6:30 this morning.

The distinguished guests visited our country for a total of eight days. Before his departure, Vice-President Vukmanović delivered a farewell speech to the welcoming crowd gathered in Guangzhou Railway Station Square. He said that although the visit was short, he learned many novel and very interesting things. He expressed his gratitude to the leading comrades of the various provinces and cities he visited, because they enabled him to understand many situations and allowed him to compare these achievements with Yugoslavia. He said that this kind of comradely, candid exchange of opinions and experiences made him very happy.

Vice-President Vukmanović concluded by wishing China continued success in its great cause of socialist construction, and wishing the cooperation between China and Yugoslavia to be consolidated and developed.

Before Vice-President Vukmanović spoke, Chen Rutang, Vice-Governor of Guangdong Province, delivered a farewell speech.

Vice-President Vukmanović arrived in Guangzhou from Shanghai by special plane yesterday afternoon.

Xinhua News Agency, September 21st: Upon leaving our country, S. Vukmanović, Vice-President of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia, sent thank-you letters to Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice-Premier Chen Yun.

In his letter to Premier Zhou, Vice-President Vukmanović said: "As I depart your great country, I would like to express my sincere gratitude for the friendship and concern we received during our stay in the People's Republic of China. I believe that our frank exchange of information and opinions will help us to better understand each other's problems and the future development paths towards our common goal of socialism, and will contribute to the establishment of lasting world peace.

Please accept my best wishes for the prosperity and happiness of your country and for your personal happiness."

### 伏克曼诺维奇离广州回国  临行前给周总理谢函

1957-09-22
第4版()
专栏：

    伏克曼诺维奇离广州回国
    临行前给周总理谢函
    据新华社广州21日电　南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席斯·伏克曼诺维奇和随行人员结束在我国的访问，今晨六时三十分乘火车离开广州去香港。
    贵宾们在我国共访问了八天。伏克曼诺维奇副主席行前对聚集在广州车站广场上的欢送群众发表了告别讲话。他说，这次访问时间虽短，但是他了解到了许多新奇和十分有趣的事。他向他所访问过的各个省市的领导同志表示感谢，因为他们使他了解到许多情况，使他能够拿这些成就同南斯拉夫作一个比较。他说，这种同志般的开诚布公的交换意见、交换经验，使他非常高兴。
    伏克曼诺维奇副主席最后祝中国继续顺利地进行伟大的社会主义建设事业，祝中国和南斯拉夫的合作得到巩固和发展。
    在伏克曼诺维奇副主席讲话之前，广东省副省长陈汝棠致了欢送词。
    伏克曼诺维奇副主席是昨天下午乘专机自上海来广州的。
    新华社21日讯　南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席斯·伏克曼诺维奇在离开我国时，曾给周恩来总理和陈云副总理各一封谢函。
    伏克曼诺维奇副主席在给周总理的信中说：“在离别你们伟大的国家时，我愿对我们在中华人民共和国停留期间所得到的友谊和关切表示诚挚的谢意。我相信，我们坦率地交换情况和意见将有助于我们更好地了解彼此的问题和我们走向社会主义共同目标的未来发展道路，并且有助于建立世界持久和平。
    请接受我最好的祝愿，祝贵国繁荣幸福，祝你本人幸福。”
