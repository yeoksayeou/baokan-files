Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet for Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Delegation to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-09-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Delegation to the Soviet Union, headed by Tetsu Katayama, arrived in Beijing this afternoon.  Those who went to the airport to greet them included Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, and Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

G.A. Gutikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, also went to the airport to welcome them.

Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, held a banquet tonight to welcome all members of the delegation.

### 张奚若宴请日社会党访苏亲善使节团

1957-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　张奚若宴请日社会党访苏亲善使节团
    新华社17日讯　以片山哲为首的日本社会党访苏亲善使节团在今天下午到达北京。前往机场迎接的有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志、中国红十字会会长李德全和中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民等。
    苏联驻中国大使馆参赞顾悌克夫也到机场欢迎。
    中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今晚举行宴会欢迎使节团全体人员。
