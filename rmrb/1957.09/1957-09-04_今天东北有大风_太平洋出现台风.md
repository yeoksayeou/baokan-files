Title: Strong Winds in Northeast China Today, Typhoon Appears in the Pacific
Author:
Date: 1957-09-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 3rd. The Central Meteorological Research Institute released a strong wind advisory at 7 PM on September 3rd:

    Due to the influence of a low-pressure system moving from central Inner Mongolia, starting on the morning of the 4th, central and eastern Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Heilongjiang Province, western Jilin Province, and northern Liaoning Province will experience level 5-7 southwest winds successively from west to east. Starting on the night of the 4th, the winds will gradually turn to level 5-6 northwest winds from west to east. In the next 48 hours, heavy rain is expected in northern Heilongjiang Province, and moderate rain is expected in southern Heilongjiang Province, western Jilin Province, most of Liaoning Province, and central and eastern Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.

    In addition, a typhoon has appeared in the Pacific Ocean. At 2 PM today, the center of the typhoon was located near 28.2 degrees north latitude and 134.7 degrees east longitude. The maximum wind force near the center is above level 12, and there are winds of level 6 or above within 700 kilometers of the center. Currently, the typhoon is moving in a northwest-west direction at a speed of 4-6 knots per hour. In the next 24 hours, there will be level 6-9 northerly winds in the southeastern Yellow Sea and eastern East China Sea. There will be level 6-7 northeasterly winds in the Taiwan Strait.

### 今天东北有大风  太平洋出现台风

1957-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    今天东北有大风
    太平洋出现台风
    新华社3日讯中央气象科学研究所3日下午七时发布大风消息：
    受内蒙古中部移来的低气压影响，4日上午起，内蒙古自治区的中部和东部，黑龙江省、吉林省西部，辽宁省北部，自西向东先后有五——七级西南风。4日夜间起，由西向东逐渐转为五、六级的西北风。未来四十八小时内，黑龙江省北部将有大雨，黑龙江省南部，吉林省西部，辽宁省大部和内蒙古自治区的中部和东部，将有中雨。
    此外，太平洋上有台风出现。今天下午两点钟，台风中心位置在北纬二八·二度东经一三四·七度附近。中心附近最大风力在十二级以上，距中心七百公里以内的海面上，有六级以上的大风。目前台风正以每小时四——六浬的速度向西北偏西的方向移动。未来二十四小时内，黄海东南部，东海东部，有六——九级的偏北风。台湾海峡有六、七级的东北风。
