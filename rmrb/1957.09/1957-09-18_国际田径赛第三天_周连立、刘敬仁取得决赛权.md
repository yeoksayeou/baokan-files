Title: International Track and Field Meet Day 3: Zhou Lianli and Liu Jingren Secure Spots in Finals
Author:
Date: 1957-09-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, September 17th: The Romanian International Track and Field Championships continued at 4:00 PM on the 16th, but the competition was interrupted by heavy rain five minutes after it began. By 5:00 PM, the competitions were suspended due to thunderstorms. During the competition, Chinese athletes Zhou Lianli and Liu Jingren secured their spots in the finals.

    In the 110-meter hurdles preliminary, Zhou Lianli broke his own national record of 14.9 seconds with a time of 14.8 seconds.

    In the 200-meter preliminary, Liu Jingren secured a spot in the finals with a time of 22.5 seconds.

### 国际田径赛第三天  周连立、刘敬仁取得决赛权

1957-09-18
第4版()
专栏：

    国际田径赛第三天
    周连立、刘敬仁取得决赛权
    据新华社布加勒斯特17日电　16日下午四时，罗马尼亚国际田径锦标赛继续举行，但是比赛开始五分钟后即遇大雨，进行到五点钟雷雨交加，各项比赛暂停。在比赛中，中国运动员周连立和刘敬仁已经取得决赛权。
    周连立在一百一十公尺高栏赛跑预赛中，以十四秒八的成绩打破了他自己创造的十四秒九的中国全国最高纪录。
    刘敬仁在二百公尺赛跑预赛中，以二十二秒五的成绩取得了决赛权。
