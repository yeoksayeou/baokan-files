Title: Chairman of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association and Former Indian Medical Team Members Who Aided China Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th. Mrs. Gunawardena, Chairman of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association, and her husband and assistants, a group of four, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

A group of six people, including members of the Indian Medical Team that aided China during our War of Resistance Against Japan and their families, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association.

### 锡中友协主席等人前印度援华医疗队员到京

1957-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

    锡中友协主席等人前印度援华医疗队员到京
    新华社25日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的锡中友好协会主席古纳瓦地尼夫人和她的丈夫、助手等一行四人，在今日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    在我国抗日战争中曾支援我国人民的印度援华医疗队队员和他们的家属等一行六人，应中印友好协会邀请访问中国，在今日下午乘飞机到达北京。
