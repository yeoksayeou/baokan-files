Title: Happiness
Author: Wu Guangming
Date: 1957-09-25
Page: 8

    Happiness
    Is a happy day,
    Is an unforgettable day,
    Our factory director and Party Secretary,
    Representing us
    Had the honor of seeing Chairman Mao.
    Shanghai Boiler Factory Worker Wu Guangming
