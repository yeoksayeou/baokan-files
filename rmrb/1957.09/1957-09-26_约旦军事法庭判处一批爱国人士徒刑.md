Title: Jordanian Military Court Sentences a Group of Patriots to Imprisonment
Author:
Date: 1957-09-26
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Amman, September 25th: The Jordanian military court today sentenced a group of patriots to prison terms ranging from ten to fifteen years.

Five people were sentenced to fifteen years of imprisonment, including the two former Jordanian Army Chiefs of Staff, Nowar and Hayari, as well as the former Minister of State for Foreign Affairs, Rimawi, who have already fled the country. Twelve people were sentenced to ten years of imprisonment.

### 约旦军事法庭判处一批爱国人士徒刑

1957-09-26
第7版()
专栏：

    约旦军事法庭
    判处一批爱国人士徒刑
    据新华社25日讯　安曼消息：约旦军事法庭今天对一批爱国人士判处十年到十五年的徒刑。
    被判处十五年徒刑的有五人，其中包括已经逃到国外的约旦两个前陆军参谋长诺瓦尔和哈亚里以及前外交国务大臣里马维。被判处十年徒刑的有十二人。
