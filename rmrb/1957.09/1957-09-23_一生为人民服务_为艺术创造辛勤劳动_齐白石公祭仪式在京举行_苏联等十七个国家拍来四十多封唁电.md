Title: Serving the People Throughout His Life, Diligently Working for Artistic Creation: Qi Baishi's Public Memorial Ceremony Held in Beijing; Over Forty Telegrams of Condolences Sent from Seventeen Countries Including the Soviet Union

Author:

Date: 1957-09-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. The public memorial ceremony for Qi Baishi, representative of the National People's Congress, Chairman of the Chinese Artists Association, and People's Artist, was held this morning at Jiaxing Temple in Beijing. The mourning hall was decorated solemnly and respectfully. Wreaths and elegiac couplets presented by Premier Zhou Enlai, relevant departments, organizations, and international friends were displayed and hung around the mourning hall.

Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Lin Boqu and Chen Shutong, President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, Head of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee Li Weihan, Deputy Head of the Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee Zhou Yang, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, representatives of relevant units and people's organizations, as well as friends of Qi Baishi and his students, totaling about 400 people, attended the public memorial. Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Shen Junru, also came to pay his respects before the public memorial. Badrang, cultural attache of the Egyptian Embassy in China, and many other foreign guests also attended the public memorial.

Guo Moruo spoke at the beginning of the public memorial. He said that Mr. Qi Baishi was a great Chinese painter and also a great painter most admired by peace-loving people all over the world. He served the Chinese people throughout his life and served to enhance Chinese culture. It can be said that he fulfilled his due responsibility in life. Guo Moruo said that Qi Baishi came from the working people, and his life was imbued with a noble spirit of self-sacrificing labor. The main reason he was able to achieve so much and so brilliantly was because he had a dedicated spirit of labor to serve the people. This is something worthy of our learning and admiration by everyone and by future generations.

Cai Ruohong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Artists Association, introduced Qi Baishi's life course of nearly a century, pointing out that his life was one of diligent labor and also one of infinitely rich artistic creation. Even in the last year of Qi Baishi's life, he created approximately four hundred works.

Zhong Ling then, on behalf of the Qi Baishi Funeral Committee, announced that the committee had received more than forty telegrams of condolences from many units, organizations, and individuals from the World Peace Council and seventeen countries including the Soviet Union, as well as many telegrams of condolences from all over the country. He also read out some of the telegrams.

After Qi Baishi's family bowed to the attendees to express their thanks, the public memorial ended. After the public memorial, the coffin was moved to the Hunan Cemetery in the western suburbs for burial. Deputy Head of the Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee Zhou Yang, Vice Ministers of Culture Qian Junrui and Xia Yan, as well as Qi Baishi's friends and family, attended the burial ceremony at the cemetery.

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. The Qi Baishi Funeral Committee has received more than forty telegrams of condolences in the past few days from the World Peace Council and seventeen countries, mourning the People's Artist and winner of the 1955 International Peace Prize, Qi Baishi.

The units sending telegrams of condolences include the World Peace Council, the Soviet Asian Solidarity Committee and the Soviet Peace Protection Committee, the Japan-China Friendship Association, the Britain-China Friendship Association, as well as the Ministries of Culture and Artists Associations of the Soviet Union, the German Democratic Republic, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Albania, Yugoslavia, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Mongolian People's Republic, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Indonesia, Italy, Britain, Japan, Burma, and other cultural and artistic institutions and organizations.

Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Jorge Zalamea Borda, Secretary of the International Peace Prize Judging Committee of the World Peace Council, Tetsuji Katayama, President of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, and Pari, Chairman of the Italian Research Center for Developing Economic and Cultural Relations with China, all sent telegrams of condolences to Qi Baishi. Chilean painter Venturini also sent a letter of condolence.

The embassies of the Soviet Union, the German Democratic Republic, Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, Burma, and Norway in China, the cultural attache of the Egyptian Embassy in China, and Biswas, the representative of the Indian People's Theater Association, all presented wreaths separately to express their condolences.

### 一生为人民服务　为艺术创造辛勤劳动  齐白石公祭仪式在京举行  苏联等十七个国家拍来四十多封唁电

1957-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    一生为人民服务　为艺术创造辛勤劳动
    齐白石公祭仪式在京举行
    苏联等十七个国家拍来四十多封唁电
    新华社22日讯　全国人民代表大会代表、中国美术家协会主席、人民艺术家齐白石的公祭仪式今天上午在北京嘉兴寺举行。灵堂布置得庄严肃穆，周恩来总理以及各有关部门、团体和国际友人等赠送的花圈、挽联陈列和悬挂在灵堂的四周。
    国务院总理周恩来、副总理陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠、陈叔通，最高人民法院院长董必武，中共中央统战部部长李维汉，中共中央宣传部副部长周扬，文化部部长沈雁冰以及各有关单位、人民团体的代表和齐白石的生前友好和他的学生共约四百人参加了公祭。全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒在公祭前也来吊唁。前来参加公祭的还有埃及驻华大使馆文化专员巴德朗以及外宾多人。
    郭沫若在公祭开始时讲了话。他说，齐白石先生是中国伟大的画家，同时也是全世界爱好和平的人士们最敬仰的伟大的画家。他一生为中国人民服务，为提高中国的文化服务，可以说他是尽了他应尽的人生责任。郭沫若说，齐白石出身于劳动人民，他的一生贯串了自我牺牲的崇高的劳动精神，他所以能有这么多和这么光辉的成就，主要是因为他有为人民服务的献身的劳动精神，这一点值得我们大家和后代们学习景仰。
    中国美术家协会副主席蔡若虹介绍了齐白石将近一个世纪的生活历程，指出他的一生是辛勤劳动的一生，也是无穷丰富的艺术创造的一生，就是在齐白石的生命的最后一年中，他还创作了约四百幅作品。
    钟灵接着代表齐白石治丧委员会宣布，委员会收到了世界和平理事会以及苏联等十七个国家的许多单位、团体、个人拍来的四十多封唁电和国内各地拍来的许多唁电，他并且宣读了一部分电文。
    在齐白石的家属向与祭者行礼致谢后，公祭结束。公祭后即移灵赴西郊湖南公墓安葬。中共中央宣传部副部长周扬，文化部副部长钱俊瑞、夏衍，以及齐白石的生前友好和家属等都到墓地参加了安葬仪式。
    新华社22日讯　齐白石先生治丧委员会这几天收到了来自世界和平理事会以及苏联等十七个国家的四十多封唁电，悼念人民艺术家和1955年国际和平奖金获得者齐白石。
    来电吊唁的单位有世界和平理事会，苏联亚洲团结委员会和苏联保卫和平委员会，日中友好协会，英中友好协会，以及苏联、德意志民主共和国、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、阿尔巴尼亚、南斯拉夫、越南民主共和国、蒙古人民共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、印度尼西亚、意大利、英国、日本、缅甸等国的文化部、美术家协会等文化艺术机关、团体。
    越南民主共和国主席胡志明，世界和平理事会国际和平奖金评议委员会书记豪尔赫·萨拉米亚—波尔达，日中文化交流协会会长片山哲，意大利争取与中国发展经济文化关系研究中心主席帕里等人，都来电吊唁齐白石。智利画家万徒勒里也来信吊唁。
    苏联、德意志民主共和国、保加利亚、捷克斯洛伐克、缅甸、挪威等国的驻华大使馆、埃及驻华使馆文化专员，以及印度人民戏剧协会的代表比斯瓦斯等都分别赠献花圈，表示哀悼。
