International Briefs

    Chinese Youth Song and Dance Troupe Returns Home from Soviet Union
    Chita dispatch: The Chinese Youth Song and Dance Troupe performed its last show in the Soviet Union on the 20th and left Chita to return home. After the Sixth World Youth Festival, the troupe toured various parts of the Soviet Union according to the 1957 executive plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement. It performed in seven cities including Tula, Kaluga, and Chita for forty days, giving a total of twenty-five performances to an audience of 24,000 people. The Soviet people were deeply interested in Chinese art and expressed a warm welcome.

    Hungarian Agricultural Cooperatives and Mutual Aid Groups Increase
    Budapest dispatch: Hungary now has 3,507 agricultural production cooperatives and production mutual aid groups, with 134,000 farming households and 1.42 million holds of land (each hold is equivalent to 8.55 Chinese mu). Mátyás Antal, Director of the Production Cooperation Department of the Hungarian Ministry of Agriculture, recently said at the opening ceremony of the Agricultural Leading Cadre School in Szambek City, Pest County, that since the October incident last year, approximately 1,200 previously disbanded production cooperatives have been restored.

    Soviet Athletes to Visit China
    Beijing dispatch: The famous Soviet male high jumper Shtegin and long jumper Ter-Ovanesyan will both come to China in early October to visit the 1957 National Track and Field Championships and hold friendly competitions with Chinese track and field athletes.
    Shtegin and Ter-Ovanesyan recently participated in the 10th International Track and Field Championships held in Romania, and won the championships in men's high jump and long jump respectively.

    Bevan Leaves Soviet Union to Return Home
    TASS reports: British Labour Party MP Bevan and his wife, who visited the Soviet Union at the invitation of some representatives of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, left Moscow by plane on the 20th to return home.
    (Xinhua News Agency)

### 国际简讯

1957-09-22
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    中国青年歌舞团离苏回国
    赤塔讯：中国青年歌舞团20日在苏联演出了最后一场，离开赤塔回国。这个歌舞团是在第六届世界青年联欢节结束后，按照中苏文化合作协定1957年度执行计划在苏联各地巡回演出的。它在图拉、卡卢加、赤塔等七个城市演出了四十天，一共演出了二十五场，观众达两万四千人。苏联人民对中国艺术充满了深厚的兴趣，并且表示热烈的欢迎。
    匈牙利农业社和互助组增多
    布达佩斯讯：匈牙利现在有三千五百零七个农业生产合作社和生产互助组，拥有十三万四千个农户和一百四十二万霍尔德土地（每霍尔德相当于八点五五市亩）。匈牙利农业部生产合作司司长马奇什·安托尔最近在佩斯州桑比克市农业领导干部学校开学典礼上说，在去年十月事件以后，到现在已经恢复了大约一千二百个以前被解散的生产合作社。
    苏联运动健将将访我国
    北京讯：著名的苏联男子跳高选手斯特金和跳远选手捷尔—奥瓦涅西安，10月初都将来我国参观1957年全国田径锦标赛，并且同我国田径选手举行友谊比赛。
    斯特金和捷尔—奥瓦涅西安最近都参加了在罗马尼亚举行的第十届国际田径锦标赛，并且分别得到了男子跳高和跳远的冠军。
    比万离苏回国
    据塔斯社报道：应苏联最高苏维埃的一些代表邀请到苏联访问的英国工党议员比万和他的夫人，已经在20日乘飞机离莫斯科回国。　
    （新华社）
