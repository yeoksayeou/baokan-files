Title: Yugoslav Ambassador to China Popović Holds Reception to Welcome Vice President Vukmanović's Visit to China; Vukmanović Hosts Banquet for Chinese National Leaders
Author:
Date: 1957-09-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 13th. Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popović, held a reception this evening to welcome Vice President Vukmanović and his entourage to China.

	Vice President Vukmanović delivered a speech at the reception. He wished the People's Republic of China prosperity and that China would develop its national economy in a very short period of time and become an industrially developed country, and wished Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai good health.

	Premier Zhou Enlai then delivered a speech thanking Vice President Vukmanović for his friendly congratulations. He said: We, the Chinese people, with the same sentiment, wish Yugoslavia success in socialist industrialization and the cause of international peace. Premier Zhou also proposed a toast to the friendship between the people of China and Yugoslavia, to the unity of socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union, to the friendship of the people of the world and world peace, to the good health of Yugoslav leader President Tito, and to the good health of Vice President Vukmanović, Ambassador Popović, and Yugoslav comrades.

	Among the ambassador's more than three hundred guests were Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Chen Yi, and Bo Yibo, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, and other prominent figures from all walks of life in China, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to China.

	At the reception, the guests gathered together to drink and talk. Vice President Vukmanović and Premier Zhou Enlai also spoke with members of the diplomatic corps and took group photos with young people of various ethnic minorities in China who attended the reception.

	This reception, filled with friendship, lasted for two hours.

Xinhua News Agency, 13th. Yugoslav Federal Executive Council Vice President Vukmanović hosted a banquet at noon today to entertain Chinese national leaders.

	Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Chen Yun, He Long, Chen Yi, Bo Yibo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Peng Zhen, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee Deng Xiaoping, and responsible persons from relevant government departments Zhang Wentian, Wang Jiaxiang, Ye Jizhuang, Shen Yanbing, Zeng Yongquan, Cao Juru, Wang Yutian, etc., attended the banquet.

	Yugoslav comrades accompanying Vice President Vukmanović on his visit to China, and Yugoslav Ambassador to China Popović also attended the banquet.

### 南驻华大使波波维奇举行招待会　欢迎伏克曼诺维奇副主席访华　伏克曼诺维奇宴请我国家领导人

1957-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　南驻华大使波波维奇举行招待会
　　欢迎伏克曼诺维奇副主席访华
　　伏克曼诺维奇宴请我国家领导人
    新华社13日讯  南斯拉夫驻中国大使波波维奇今晚举行招待会，欢迎伏克曼诺维奇副主席和他的随行人员访问中国。
    伏克曼诺维奇副主席在招待会上首先致词。他祝中华人民共和国的繁荣，祝中国在很短时期内发展国民经济变成工业发达的国家，并祝毛泽东主席和周恩来总理身体健康。
    周恩来总理接着致词感谢伏克曼诺维奇副主席的友谊的祝贺。他说：我们中国人民以同样的心情祝南斯拉夫在社会主义工业化和国际和平事业中所作出的成就。周总理还提议为中国和南斯拉夫两国人民的友好，为以苏联为首的社会主义各国的团结，为世界人民的友好和世界和平，为南斯拉夫人民的领袖铁托总统身体健康和为伏克曼诺维奇副主席、波波维奇大使和南斯拉夫同志们身体健康干杯。
    在大使的三百多位客人中，有副总理陈云、陈毅、薄一波，副委员长李济深、郭沫若、彭真、陈叔通，国防委员会副主席张治中，外交部副部长张闻天等和中国各方面的著名人士，以及各国驻中国的使节。
    在酒会上，宾主欢聚在一起饮酒交谈。伏克曼诺维奇副主席和周恩来总理还同外交使团的人员们谈了话，并且和参加酒会的中国各少数民族的青年在一起合影留念。
    这个洋溢着友谊的招待会共进行了两个小时。
    新华社13日讯  南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席伏克曼诺维奇今天中午举行宴会，招待我国国家领导人员。
    国务院总理周恩来，副总理陈云、贺龙、陈毅、薄一波，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真，中共中央总书记邓小平，以及政府有关部门的负责人张闻天、王稼祥、叶季壮、沈雁冰、曾涌泉、曹菊如、王雨田等出席了宴会。
    随同伏克曼诺维奇副主席访问中国的南斯拉夫同志们，和南斯拉夫驻中国大使波波维奇也出席了宴会。
