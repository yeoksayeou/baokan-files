Title: U.S. Military Interferes in West German Election
Author:
Date: 1957-09-16
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 13th (Tass dispatch): U.S. military personnel stationed in West Germany are crudely interfering in the West German election campaign. According to "Deutsche Presse-Agentur," near the U.S. military base in Nabornbach, American officers and soldiers actually prevented residents from distributing leaflets calling for votes for the German Social Democratic Party candidate on September 11, when the election campaign entered its climax.

### 美军干涉西德竞选

1957-09-16
第6版()
专栏：

    美军干涉西德竞选
    据新华社讯  塔斯社柏林13日讯：驻在西德的美国军人粗暴地干涉西德的竞选运动。据“德意志新闻社”报道，在美军基地纳博伦巴赫附近，美国军官和士兵9月11日在竞选进入高潮的时候竟然阻止居民散发传单号召投票选举德国社会民主党候选人。
