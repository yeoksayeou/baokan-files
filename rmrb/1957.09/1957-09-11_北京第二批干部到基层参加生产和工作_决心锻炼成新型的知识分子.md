Title: Beijing's Second Group of Cadres Go to the Grassroots to Participate in Production and Work, Determined to Temper Themselves into a New Type of Intellectual
Author:
Date: 1957-09-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th. "Temper ourselves into a working-class intellectual cadre." Today, more than 1,300 cadres from Beijing went to participate in labor production and grassroots work with this aspiration. This is the second group of cadres from Beijing who have been sent to grassroots units and to participate in labor production; they were drawn from twenty-eight units. This morning, a send-off rally with more than 4,000 people participating was held at the Zhongshan Park Music Hall. More than one thousand of these cadres will participate in agricultural production, while more than two hundred will participate in handicrafts production, street work, and work in middle schools. Among these staff members, there are more than five hundred Communist Party members and Communist Youth League members.

### 北京第二批干部到基层参加生产和工作  决心锻炼成新型的知识分子

1957-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京第二批干部到基层参加生产和工作
    决心锻炼成新型的知识分子
    据新华社9日讯　“把自己锻炼成为一个工人阶级的知识分子干部”，今天北京市一千三百多名干部带着这个心愿去参加劳动生产和基层工作。这是北京市下放到基层单位和参加劳动生产的第二批干部，他们是在二十八个单位里抽调出来的。今天上午在中山公园音乐堂举行了有四千多人参加的欢送大会。这批干部中有一千零几十个人去参加农业生产，另有二百多人分别参加手工业生产、街道工作和中等学校工作。在这批工作人员中，共产党员和共青团员有五百多人。
