Title: Premier Zhou, Deng Xiaoping, He Long, and Vice Premier Li Fuchun Attend Richter Couple Concert
Author:
Date: 1957-09-09
Page: 3
    Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The Soviet Ambassador to China's Chargé d'affaires Abrasimov and his wife, held a concert tonight for the famous Soviet pianist Sviatoslav Richter and his wife, soprano Nina Dorliak, who are visiting China to perform. At the concert, Richter and Dorliak performed piano solos and vocal solos.
    Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Deng Xiaoping, He Long, and Li Fuchun, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian and others were invited to attend tonight's concert.
    Many diplomatic envoys from various countries to China were also invited to attend tonight's concert.
    Soviet People's Artist Richter performed works by Schubert, Schumann, Mussorgsky, and Liszt tonight. Soviet Honored Artist Dorliak also sang songs by Tchaikovsky, Liszt, Weckerlin, and Prokofiev.

### 周总理、邓小平、贺龙、李富春副总理  出席李赫特尔夫妇音乐会

1957-09-09
第3版()
专栏：

    周总理、邓小平、贺龙、李富春副总理
    出席李赫特尔夫妇音乐会
    新华社8日讯　苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办阿布拉西莫夫和他的夫人，今晚为著名的苏联钢琴家斯·李赫特尔和他的夫人、高音歌唱家尼·多尔利阿克访华演出，举行音乐会。会上由李赫特尔和多尔利阿克表演了钢琴独奏和独唱。
    周恩来总理，邓小平、贺龙、李富春副总理，文化部部长沈雁冰和外交部副部长张闻天等人应邀出席了今晚的音乐会。
    许多国家驻中国的外交使节也应邀出席了今晚的音乐会。
    苏联人民演员李赫特尔今晚演奏了舒伯特、舒曼、莫索尔斯基和李斯特等人的作品。苏联功勋演员多尔利阿克也独唱了柴可夫斯基、李斯特、魏克尔林和普洛克菲也夫等人的歌曲。
