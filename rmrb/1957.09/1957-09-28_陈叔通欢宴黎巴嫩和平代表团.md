Title: Chen Shutong Hosts Banquet for Lebanese Peace Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-09-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 27th — Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee, hosted a banquet today to welcome the Lebanese Peace Delegation.

Attending as guests were leading members of relevant Chinese people's organizations, including Liu Ningyi, Da Punsheng, Chu Tunan, Zhang Zhiran, and Le Songsheng.

### 陈叔通欢宴黎巴嫩和平代表团

1957-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

    陈叔通欢宴黎巴嫩和平代表团
    新华社27日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通今天设宴欢迎黎巴嫩和平代表团。
    出席作陪的有我国有关人民团体负责人刘宁一、达浦生、楚图南、张志让、乐松生等。
