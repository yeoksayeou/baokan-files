Title: Resolutely Support the Syrian People's Anti-Colonialist Struggle: More Than 5,000 Beijing University Students Gather to Welcome Syrian Student Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-09-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd: More than 5,000 university students from various universities in Beijing held a grand gathering at Peking University tonight to welcome the Syrian student delegation and express their resolute support for the Syrian people's heroic struggle against colonialism.

    Zhao Zhenjiang, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Students' Federation and Chairman of the Peking University Student Union, delivered a speech at the meeting, extending a warm welcome to the guests on behalf of the students of Beijing and Peking University.

    Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, said in his speech that the recent military provocations and subversive activities planned by U.S. imperialism in Syria have aroused the anger of all Chinese students. All Chinese students, together with all Chinese people and peace-loving people all over the world, resolutely support the Syrian people's struggle against aggression. In this struggle, we will always be your most loyal friends and brothers.

    The head of the delegation, Khalil Khaled Abdul Nour, spoke and expressed his gratitude to the Chinese people for their support of the Syrian people's anti-colonialist struggle. He said that we will certainly defeat the imperialist plot. The people of Arab countries are determined to carry out the struggle against imperialism and will never let imperialism step over our corpses. He also introduced the achievements made by Syria in economic and cultural construction since its independence.

    Michel As'ad, a member of the delegation, also spoke at the meeting. He said that as long as we are with the Chinese people and all peace-loving people in the world, we will surely defeat imperialism.

    The delegation, which consists of six members and was invited to visit China by the All-China Students' Federation, arrived in Beijing at 3 p.m. today. They are the first group of Syrian student representatives to visit China under the Sino-Syrian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 坚决支持叙利亚人民反殖民主义斗争  北京五千多大学生集会欢迎叙学生代表团

1957-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    坚决支持叙利亚人民反殖民主义斗争
    北京五千多大学生集会欢迎叙学生代表团
    据新华社22日讯　北京市各高等学校五千多位学生今晚在北京大学举行盛大集会，欢迎叙利亚学生代表团，表示坚决支持叙利亚人民反殖民主义的英勇斗争。
    北京市学生联合会副主席、北京大学学生会主席赵震江在会上致词，代表北京市学生和北大学生向客人表示热烈欢迎。
    中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立讲话，他说，最近美帝国主义在叙利亚阴谋策划的军事挑衅和颠覆活动，引起了我们全中国学生的愤怒。我们全中国学生和全中国人民一起，和全世界爱好和平的人民一起，坚决支持叙利亚人民的反侵略斗争。在这一斗争中，我们将永远是你们的最忠实的朋友和弟兄。
    代表团团长哈利勒·哈立德·阿卜杜勒·努尔讲话，向中国人民对叙利亚人民的反殖民主义斗争的支持表示感谢。他说，我们一定能击败帝国主义的阴谋。阿拉伯各国人民坚决进行反对帝国主义的斗争，永远不会让帝国主义在我们尸体上跨过去。他还介绍了叙利亚独立后在经济、文化建设等方面所取得的成就。
    代表团团员米歇尔·阿斯阿特也在会上讲了话。他说，我们只要同中国人民和全世界所有爱好和平人民在一起，我们一定能战胜帝国主义。
    这个应中华全国学生联合会邀请来中国访问的代表团共六人，是在今天下午三时到达北京的。他们是根据中叙文化合作协定来中国访问的第一批叙利亚学生代表。
