Title: Indian Writer Ghosh and Wife Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. At the invitation of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, Indian writer and mineralogist Ghosh and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon to visit our country.

### 印度作家高士夫妇到京

1957-09-09
第3版()
专栏：

    印度作家高士夫妇到京
    新华社6日讯　应中印友好协会邀请来我国访问的印度作家及矿物学家高士及其夫人，在今日下午乘飞机到达北京。
