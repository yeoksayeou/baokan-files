Title: Premier Zhou Receives French Economic Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1957-09-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 26th. Premier Zhou Enlai received the French economic delegation headed by Mr. Rochereau this evening.

    Present at the reception were Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Lei Renmin and Li Zhucheng, Vice-Chairmen; Lu Xuzhang, Member; Huang Jing, Director of the State Technological Commission; and Huang Hua, Director of the West European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 周总理接见法国经济代表团

1957-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理接见法国经济代表团
    新华社26日讯　周恩来总理今天晚间接见以罗谢罗为首的法国访华经济代表团。
    接见时在座的，有中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸，副主席雷任民、李烛尘和委员卢绪章，国家技术委员会主任黄敬，外交部西欧司司长黄华。
