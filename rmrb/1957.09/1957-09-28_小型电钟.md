Title: Small Electric Clock
Author:
Date: 1957-09-28
Page: 5

    Small Electric Clock
    Recently, Shanghai Changming Clock Factory successfully trial-produced a novel small electric clock. This electric clock only needs one ordinary battery to run accurately for two months. The clock case is made of plastic, with a beautiful and sturdy design.
                    (Xinhua News Agency)

### 小型电钟

1957-09-28
第5版()
专栏：

    小型电钟
    上海昌明制钟厂最近试制成功一种新颖的小型电钟。这种电钟只需安上一节普通电池就可以很准确的行走二个月。电钟的外壳是用塑料制成的，式样美观、坚固。
                    （新华社）
