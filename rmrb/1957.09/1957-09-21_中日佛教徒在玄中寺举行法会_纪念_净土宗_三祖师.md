Title: Chinese and Japanese Buddhists Hold Dharma Assembly at Xuanzhong Temple to Commemorate the Three Patriarchs of the "Pure Land School"
Author:
Date: 1957-09-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, September 20th. Japanese Buddhist goodwill delegation to China and Chinese Buddhist monks held a consecration ceremony at Xuanzhong Temple in Jiaocheng County, Shanxi Province, the sacred site of the Buddhist "Pure Land School," yesterday, and commemorated the three great patriarchs of the "Pure Land School," Tanluan, Daochuo, and Shandao.

Takagami Senryu, head of the Japanese Buddhist goodwill delegation to China, and Zhao Puchu, vice president of the Chinese Buddhist Association, both spoke at the Dharma Assembly.

### 中日佛教徒在玄中寺举行法会  纪念“净土宗”三祖师

1957-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

    中日佛教徒在玄中寺举行法会
    纪念“净土宗”三祖师
    据新华社太原20日电　日本佛教访华亲善使节团和中国佛教僧众昨天在佛教“净土宗”的圣地——山西省交城县玄中寺举行开光法会，并且纪念“净土宗”的三大祖师昙鸾、道绰、善导。
    日本佛教访华亲善使节团团长高阶仙珑和中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初都在法会上讲了话。
