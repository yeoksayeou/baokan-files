Title: German Military Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-09-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 17th. The military delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by General Willi Stoph, returned to Beijing by special plane this morning, accompanied by Li Da, Deputy Minister of National Defense, after visiting Shenyang, Anshan, Lüda and other places.
    After a short rest in Beijing, the delegation will continue to visit Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou and Guangzhou.

### 德军事代表团回京

1957-09-18
第4版()
专栏：

    德军事代表团回京
    新华社17日讯　以维利·斯多夫上将为首的德意志民主共和国军事代表团在沈阳、鞍山和旅大等地参观访问以后，今天上午在国防部副部长李达上将陪同下，乘专机返回北京。
    代表团在北京稍事休息以后，还将去南京、上海、杭州和广州等地继续参观访问。
