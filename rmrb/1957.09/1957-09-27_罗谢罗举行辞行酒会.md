Title: Roche Held a Farewell Cocktail Party
Author:
Date: 1957-09-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: The French economic delegation to China, headed by Henri Rochereau, held a cocktail party in Beijing tonight to bid farewell to people from China's economic, trade, and industrial circles.

### 罗谢罗举行辞行酒会

1957-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

    罗谢罗举行辞行酒会
    据新华社26日讯　以亨利·罗谢罗为首的法国访华经济代表团今天晚上在北京举行酒会，向我国经济、贸易和工业界人士辞行。
