Title: Premier Zhou Receives Delegation from Democratic Germany; Vice Premier Bo Yibo Hosts Banquet for Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1957-09-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 21st: Premier Zhou Enlai received this afternoon the delegation from Democratic Germany, headed by Deputy Prime Minister Ölsner of Democratic Germany, and including members Minister Apel of Heavy Machinery Industry, Democratic Germany's Ambassador to China Gipner, Commercial Counsellor Norbert, and others.

Xinhua News Agency, September 21st: Vice Premier of the State Council Bo Yibo held a banquet this evening to welcome all members of the delegation from Democratic Germany, headed by Deputy Prime Minister Ölsner.

### 周总理接见民主德国代表团  薄一波副总理欢宴贵宾

1957-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见民主德国代表团
    薄一波副总理欢宴贵宾
    据新华社21日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了民主德国代表团团长、民主德国副总理厄斯纳及团员重型机械工业部部长阿佩尔，民主德国驻华大使纪普纳，商务参赞诺伯特等。
    据新华社21日讯　国务院副总理薄一波今晚举行宴会，欢迎以厄斯纳副总理为首的民主德国代表团的全体人员。
