Author:
Date: 1957-09-29
Page: 4
Weather Forecast

    Strong Winds Expected in North China and Other Regions

    (Our report) The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a strong wind warning and frost forecast at 7:00 PM on the 28th.

    The cold air mass originally over the Mongolian People's Republic has now moved to the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. The cold air will continue to move southeastward. It is expected that within the next 24 hours, under the influence of the cold air, central and southern Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and northwestern Hebei will still have northwesterly winds of about force 6. Shanxi, northern Shaanxi, and the Hebei Plain will still have northwesterly winds of force 5-6. The Northeast Plain will have strong winds of about force 6. The Bohai Sea, Shandong Peninsula, Liaodong Peninsula, and northern Yellow Sea will have strong winds of force 6-8. The southern Yellow Sea will have winds of force 5-7. Western Shandong will have winds of force 5-6, and the wind direction will be southerly turning northerly. Starting tomorrow afternoon, the northern Jiangsu coast will also have northwesterly winds of force 5-6.

    Tomorrow morning and the morning after, after the wind stops and the sky clears, the minimum temperature in western Heilongjiang Province and areas of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region other than the Ik Zhao League will drop below zero degrees Celsius, and there will be frost. The morning after tomorrow, the minimum temperature in the Shanxi Plateau, northern Shaanxi, northern Hebei, the Ik Zhao League of Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, western Liaoning, and northwestern Jilin will drop below five degrees Celsius, and there will be frost or frost damage.

### 华北等地区将有大风

1957-09-29
第4版()
专栏：气象台

    华北等地区将有大风
    本报讯　28日下午七时中央气象科学研究所发布大风警报和霜冻预告。
    原在蒙古人民共和国的冷空气，目前已移到内蒙古自治区。以后冷空气仍将向东南方向移动。预计未来二十四小时内，受冷空气影响，内蒙古自治区的中部、南部和河北西北部，仍将有六级左右的西北风，山西、陕西北部，河北平原仍将有五、六级的西北风。东北平原将有六级左右的大风。渤海海面、山东半岛、辽东半岛和黄海北部海面，将有六—八级的大风。黄海南部海面将有五—七级的大风。山东西部将有五—六级的大风，风向均为偏南转偏北。明天下午起，江苏北部沿海也将有五—六级的西北风。
    明天早晨和后天早晨，风停天晴以后，黑龙江省西部和内蒙古自治区除伊克昭盟以外的地区，最低气温将下降到零度以下，并将有冰冻出现，后天早晨，山西高原、陕西北部、河北北部，内蒙古自治区的伊克昭盟、辽宁西部和吉林西北部，最低气温都将下降到五度以下，并将有霜或霜冻。
