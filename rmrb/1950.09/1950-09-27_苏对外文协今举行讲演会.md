Title: Soviet Foreign Cultural Association to Hold Lecture Today
Author: Chen Yingnan
Date: 1950-09-27
Page: 10

The Soviet Foreign Cultural Association to Hold Lecture Today

　　Today, in 1920 (September 27th), the Soviet government issued its second declaration to China (note: the first declaration was on July 25th, 1919), reiterating its renunciation of Tsarist Russia's privileges in China. To commemorate the 30th anniversary of this glorious day, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association will hold a lecture at 7:30 this afternoon at 19 Nanheyan Street, with Liao Gailong speaking on: "The Unity of 700 Million People." All those holding admission tickets or "Introduction to the Soviet Union" long-term listening passes are welcome to attend. (Chen Yingnan)

### 苏对外文协今举行讲演会
陈应楠
1950-09-27
第10版()
专栏：

　　苏对外文协今举行讲演会
    一九二○年的今天（二十七日）苏联政府发表第二次对华宣言（按：第一次宣言在一九一九年七月二十五日），重申放弃帝俄在华特权。北京市中苏友好协会与苏联对外文化协会为纪念这个光辉的日子的三十周年，定于今日下午七时半在南河沿十九号举行讲演会，请廖盖隆讲：《七亿人民的团结》。凡持有入场券或“苏联介绍”长期听讲证者，届时均可前往听讲。（陈应楠）
