Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation Visits Peking University Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1950-09-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: The World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation visited Peking University yesterday afternoon. The delegation visited the Li Dazhao Memorial Room, Chairman Mao's Studio, Democracy Square, the library, and the Geology Museum. The delegation's visit was warmly welcomed by all teachers and students of the university.

### 世界青联代表团昨参观北京大学

1950-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界青联代表团昨参观北京大学
    【新华社二十四日讯】世界青联代表团二十四日下午参观北京大学。代表团参观了李大钊纪念室、毛主席工作室、民主广场、图书馆及地质馆。代表团的访问受到了该校全体师生的热烈欢迎。
