Title: Consolidate the Peace Camp and Prevent West German Remilitarization; Joint Declaration Issued by Bulgaria and Germany; Romania and Germany Separately Signed Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreements
Author:
Date: 1950-09-28
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 27th dispatch from Sofia (TASS): The negotiations between the delegation of the Government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria and the delegation of the Provisional Government of the German Democratic Republic, held in Sofia on the 23rd, concluded on the 25th. The two governments issued a joint declaration on the same day, stating that the primary task of the two governments is to comprehensively consolidate the peace camp, led by the Soviet Union, in its struggle against the imperialist aggressors; to prevent West German remilitarization; and to establish a unified and peaceful German Democratic Republic. The declaration also strongly condemns the armed intervention of US imperialism in Korea; calls on all peace-loving nations to jointly stop aggression and demands the withdrawal of US troops from Korea and a peaceful solution to the Korean issue. The declaration was signed by Bulgarian Prime Minister Chervenkov and German Deputy Prime Minister Ulbricht on behalf of their respective governments, who also led the two delegations participating in the negotiations. In addition to the joint declaration, the two countries also signed a cultural cooperation agreement and a protocol on non-trade payments during the negotiations.
　　Xinhua News Agency, September 25th dispatch from Bucharest (TASS): The government delegations of the Romanian People's Republic and the German Democratic Republic signed an agreement in Bucharest on the 23rd regarding economic, financial, cultural, scientific, and technological cooperation between the two countries on the basis of equality and mutual benefit. The government delegations of the two countries also issued a joint statement, declaring that the two governments will spare no effort to strengthen cooperation between themselves and with other peace-loving countries in order to consolidate the peace camp and thwart the plundering plans of the imperialists. The two governments will resolutely defend the independence, freedom, and culture of the two peoples, and oppose the imperialist policy of intervention.

### 巩固和平阵营防止西德重新军国主义化  保德两国发表联合宣言  罗德、保德分别签订经济文化合作协定

1950-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　巩固和平阵营防止西德重新军国主义化
    保德两国发表联合宣言
    罗德、保德分别签订经济文化合作协定
    【新华社二十七日讯】据塔斯社索非亚讯：保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团与德意志民主共和国临时政府代表团，于二十三日在索非亚举行的谈判，已于二十五日结束。两国政府于同日发表联合宣言，声明：两国政府的首要任务是全面巩固在苏联领导之下向帝国主义侵略者进行斗争的和平阵营；防止西德重新军国主义化；建立统一的、和平的德意志民主共和国。宣言并坚决谴责美帝国主义对朝鲜的武装干涉；号召爱好和平的各民族共同制止侵略，要求美军撤出朝鲜与和平解决朝鲜问题。宣言系由保国总理契尔文科夫与德国副总理乌布利希代表两国政府签字，参加此次谈判的两国代表团即由他们领导。两国此次谈判除发表联合宣言外，并签订了文化合作协定与关于非贸易性付款的议定书。
    【新华社二十五日讯】据塔斯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚人民共和国与德意志民主共和国政府代表团，二十三日在罗京签订了关于两国在平等互惠的基础上进行经济、财政、文化与科学技术合作的协定。两国政府代表团并发表了联合声明，宣称两国政府为了巩固和平阵营和挫败帝国主义者的掠夺计划，将不遗余力地加强彼此间及与其他爱好和平国家间的合作。两国政府将坚决保卫两国人民的独立、自由和文化，反对帝国主义的干涉政策。
