Title: The Jingcheng District Real Estate Exchange Adds Two Branch Offices
Author:
Date: 1950-09-21
Page: 8

The Jingcheng District Real Estate Exchange, in order to facilitate real estate transactions for citizens and to handle real estate transfer registration and deed tax matters, has established two additional branch offices on September 20th, in addition to the existing first and second branch offices. The locations are as follows:

　　Third District Real Estate Transaction Branch: 11 Zhushida Street, telephone number Four Bureau 0140.
　　Fourth District Real Estate Transaction Branch: 6 Fongsheng Hutong, Xisi, telephone number Two Bureau 2246.

　　In addition, the Jingcheng District Real Estate Exchange Headquarters has moved to 11 Hougongyongku, Xinjiekou, for office work.
　　(Beijing News Agency)

### 京城区房地产交易所增设两个分所

1950-09-21
第8版()
专栏：

　　京城区房地产交易所增设两个分所
    京市城区房地产交易所为便利市民房地交易，并代办房地产转移登记及税契工作，除已成立第一第二两分所外，又于九月二十日成立第三、第四两分所，地点如下：
    第三城区房地产交易分所：猪市大街十一号，电话四局○一四○号
    第四城区房地产交易分所：西四丰盛胡同六号，电话二局二二四六号
    又，京市城区房地产交易总所已迁至新街口后公用库十一号办公。
            （京市新闻处）
