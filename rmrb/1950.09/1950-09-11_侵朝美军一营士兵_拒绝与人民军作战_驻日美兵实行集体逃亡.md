Title: US Aggressor Troops in Korea: One Battalion Refuses to Fight the People's Army; US Troops Stationed in Japan Stage Mass Desertions
Author:
Date: 1950-09-11
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Eighth Dispatch: A TASS dispatch from Tokyo on the 7th reports: Approximately two hundred American soldiers who refused to go to Korea deserted in Kokura (Kyushu Island) in July. These soldiers engaged in street fighting with the American troops dispatched to arrest the deserters. Several American officers and soldiers, as well as six Japanese people, were killed or wounded in the fighting.
    According to a report in the Soviet newspaper Pravda on August 22nd: A battalion of the 24th Division of the U.S. Eighth Army refused to fight the Korean People's Army and abandoned their positions. At that time, chaos broke out among the officers and soldiers of this battalion, and they killed each other, resulting in numerous deaths and injuries. MacArthur ordered twenty-eight of the American soldiers to be executed for "treason."

### 侵朝美军一营士兵  拒绝与人民军作战  驻日美兵实行集体逃亡

1950-09-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　侵朝美军一营士兵
    拒绝与人民军作战
    驻日美兵实行集体逃亡
    【新华社八日讯】塔斯社七日电引东京消息：约两百名拒绝赴朝鲜的美国兵，于七月间在小仓（九州岛）逃跑。这些士兵与被派往逮捕逃兵的美军之间，曾发生巷战，数名美国官兵及六名日本人在战斗中被击毙或击伤。
    另据苏联真理报八月二十二日消息：美第八军第二十四师的一营士兵，曾拒绝和朝鲜人民军作战并放弃他们的阵地，当时这一营的官兵之间发生混乱，自相残杀，结果死伤多人。麦克阿瑟下令以“反叛罪”将其中二十八名美兵枪决。
