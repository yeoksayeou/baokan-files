Title: Major Cities Across the Country are Enthusiastically Preparing to Celebrate National Day; Preparing to Hold Grand Parades and Demonstrations
Author:
Date: 1950-09-21
Page: 1

　　Major Cities Across the Country
    Enthusiastically Preparing to Celebrate National Day
    Preparing to Hold Grand Parades and Demonstrations
    [Xinhua News Agency, 20th] Major cities across the country are enthusiastically preparing to celebrate the first National Day since the founding of New China. Shanghai, Nanjing, Tianjin, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chongqing, Chengdu, and Xi'an have all established National Day Celebration Preparatory Committees, with local military and government leaders serving as directors of the committees. Nanjing has decided to hold a city-wide representative celebration meeting on the eve of National Day; district mass rallies will be held on National Day, and a lantern parade will be held in the evening; a city-wide model worker conference will be held on the 2nd. In addition, factories, schools, and organizations throughout the city will organize symposiums to discuss the city's construction achievements since the founding of New China. Tianjin will hold a parade of 300,000 people on National Day and widely展开开展 mass cultural and recreational activities. Wuhan has begun to carry out publicity activities in factories, schools, government agencies, troops, and the vast number of farmers in the suburbs, and has decided to hold a military parade and mass demonstration on National Day. On National Day, Guangzhou's major streets will be decorated with arches, and lanterns will be hung everywhere in the evening. On October 1st, celebration meetings and parades of 100,000 to 150,000 people will be held in Hebei and Henan, respectively, and a three-day flower boat entertainment party will be held on the Pearl River starting on October 1st. Chongqing and Chengdu will both hold mass rallies to celebrate National Day. More than 20,000 farmers' self-defense forces from seven counties near Chengdu will also participate in the review. In addition to holding a grand military parade on National Day, Xi'an will also hold various celebration parties, mass gatherings, and other celebration activities in the evening by various government agencies, factories, schools, and people's organizations.

### 全国各大城市  热烈筹备庆祝国庆节  准备举行盛大游行示威

1950-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国各大城市
    热烈筹备庆祝国庆节
    准备举行盛大游行示威
    【新华社二十日讯】全国各大城市热烈筹备庆祝新中国成立后的第一个国庆节。上海、南京、天津、武汉、广州、重庆、成都和西安等地均先后成立了庆祝国庆筹备委员会，由当地军政首长担任筹委会主任。南京市决定于国庆日前夕举行全市代表庆祝大会；国庆日分区召开群众大会，晚间举行提灯大游行；二日召开全市劳模大会，此外全市各工厂、学校、机关团体将组织座谈会座谈新中国成立后该市建设成绩。天津市将于国庆日举行三十万人大游行，并广泛展开群众性的文化娱乐活动。武汉市已开始在工厂、学校、机关、部队和郊区的广大农民中展开宣传活动，并决定在国庆日举行阅兵和群众示威游行。广州各大街上将于国庆日遍搭彩楼，晚上普挂花灯，十月一日则分别在河北、河南两地举行十万至十五万人的庆祝大会和游行，另于十月一日起在珠江河面举行三天花艇娱乐晚会。重庆和成都两地都将召开群众大会庆祝国庆。成都附近七个县的两万余农民自卫队并将参加检阅。西安市除于国庆日举行盛大的阅兵典礼外，晚间各机关、工厂、学校、人民团体将分别召开各种庆祝晚会，群众集会和其他的庆祝活动。
