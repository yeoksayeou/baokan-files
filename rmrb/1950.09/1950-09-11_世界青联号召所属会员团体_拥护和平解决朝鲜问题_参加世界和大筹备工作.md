Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Calls on Member Organizations to Support Peaceful Resolution of the Korean Issue and Participate in Preparatory Work for the World Congress for Peace

Author:

Date: 1950-09-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, September 10th: The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth has issued a circular calling on all member organizations of the WFDY to fully support the proposals adopted at the Prague meeting of the Executive Bureau of the Standing Committee of the World Congress for Peace regarding the convocation of the Second World Congress for Peace, namely: to demand the prohibition of atomic weapons, universal disarmament and arms control; to oppose all local acts of aggression; to condemn armed intervention in the internal affairs of various peoples and to stop such armed intervention; to demand a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue; to condemn repeated bombing of peaceful residents; and to demand the prohibition of all forms of war propaganda.

The circular points out that in order to thwart the increasingly intensified war preparations of the imperialists, it is necessary to mobilize forces more intensively and more rapidly. The circular urges member organizations to actively participate in the work of the Peace Committees, to participate in the preparation and holding of local, district and national representative conferences, and to discuss at these conferences the proposals of the Prague meeting of the Executive Bureau of the World Congress for Peace.

The circular emphasizes the great victory of the signature campaign for the Peace Declaration, calling on all youth organizations to intensify the campaign to get all young people to sign the Peace Declaration, and calling on youth organizations to actively participate in the fundraising for the International Peace Fund. The circular concludes by calling on all young people who have already signed the Peace Declaration and are preparing for the Second World Peace Congress, regardless of political or religious beliefs, to unite and intensify the struggle to defend world peace.

### 世界青联号召所属会员团体  拥护和平解决朝鲜问题  参加世界和大筹备工作

1950-09-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界青联号召所属会员团体
    拥护和平解决朝鲜问题
    参加世界和大筹备工作
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社巴黎九日讯：世界民主青年联盟秘书处顷发出通告，号召世界青联所属一切会员团体，以全力支持世界拥护和平大会常设委员会执行局布拉格会议通过的关于召开第二届世界拥护和平大会决议中的各项建议，即：要求禁止原子武器、普遍裁减及管制军备；反对一切地方发生的侵略行为；谴责武装干涉各国人民的内政，并且制止此种武装干涉；要求和平解决朝鲜问题；斥责一再轰炸和平居民；要求禁止各种鼓吹战争的宣传。
    通告指出：为了挫败帝国主义者日益加紧的战争准备，必须更紧张地、更迅速地把力量动员起来。通告敦促各会员团体积极参加和平委员会的工作，参加筹备和举行地方的、州区的和全国性的代表会议，并在这些会议上讨论和大常委会执行局布拉格会议的各项建议。
    通告强调指出为和平宣言征集签名运动的伟大胜利，号召一切青年组织加紧展开争取全体青年都在和平宣言上签名的运动，并且号召各青年组织积极参加国际和平基金的募捐。通告最后号召一切已在和平宣言上签了名和正在筹备第二届世界和平大会的工作的青年，不分政治与宗教信仰，一致团结起来，加紧为保卫世界和平而斗争。
