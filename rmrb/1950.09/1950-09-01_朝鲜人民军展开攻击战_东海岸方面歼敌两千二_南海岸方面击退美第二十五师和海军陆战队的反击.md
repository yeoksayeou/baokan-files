Title: Korean People's Army Launches Offensive: 2,200 Enemies Annihilated on the East Coast; Counterattacks by the US 25th Division and Marines Repelled on the South Coast
Author:
Date: 1950-09-01
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 31st: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war bulletin this morning that: The People's Army units continue to fight fiercely with the enemy on all fronts. The People's Army units repelled the counterattacks of the US military and the remaining puppet troops of Syngman Rhee, supported by aircraft, tanks, and motorized units, and launched offensive operations. The enemy abandoned many corpses and equipment and retreated.

　　US aircraft have continued to barbarically bomb and strafe cities and villages in areas north of the 38th parallel and in the liberated areas of South Korea for days. On August 27th, the People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down three US fighter planes that invaded the Wonsan area and captured an American pilot who landed in the nearby sea.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 31st: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war bulletin this evening that: The People's Army units continue to fight fiercely on all fronts with the US military and the remaining puppet troops of Syngman Rhee, who are launching counterattacks. On the east coast, the People's Army units inflicted heavy blows on the enemy, who continued to stubbornly resist relying on solid defensive positions, and are continuing to advance.

　　In the fighting on the east coast, the People's Army units killed or wounded over 2,000 enemies and captured over 200 prisoners; and seized six rocket launchers, over ten vehicles, radio stations, hand grenades, and a large quantity of various ammunition and other war trophies.

　　On the south coast, the People's Army units repelled the counterattacks of the US 25th Division and the Marines, and launched a fierce offensive.

　　The People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down three US aircraft that invaded the Nampo (South Pyongan Province) area in the two days of August 26th and 27th.

### 朝鲜人民军展开攻击战  东海岸方面歼敌两千二  南海岸方面击退美第二十五师和海军陆战队的反击

1950-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军展开攻击战
    东海岸方面歼敌两千二     
    南海岸方面击退美第二十五师和海军陆战队的反击
    【新华社平壤三十一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部三十日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上与敌人继续猛烈的战斗。人民军部队击退了在飞机、坦克及摩托化部队掩护下的美军和李承晚残余伪军的反攻，并且展开攻击战。敌人遗弃了很多的尸体和装备而退却。
    美国飞机连日继续野蛮地轰炸和扫射三八线以北地区和南朝鲜解放区的城市和农村。八月二十七日，人民军高射炮部队击落侵袭元山地区的美战斗机三架，活捉了降落在附近海上的美国驾驶员一名。
    【新华社平壤三十一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部三十日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上，与进行反击的美军和李承晚残余伪军继续猛烈的战斗。人民军部队在东海岸方面，给予依靠坚固的防御阵地而继续顽抗的敌人以沉重打击，并在继续前进中。
    人民军部队在东海岸方面战斗中，毙伤敌二千余名，俘敌二百余名：并缴获火箭炮六门，汽车十余辆，电台、手榴弹及各种弹药等战利品甚多。
    人民军部队在南海岸方面，击退美军第二十五师和海军陆战队的反击，并展开猛烈的攻击战。
    人民军高射炮部队，在八月二十六日至二十七日两日间，击落侵袭南浦（平安南道）地区的美机三架。
