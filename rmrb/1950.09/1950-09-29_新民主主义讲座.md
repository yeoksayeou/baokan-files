Lectures on New Democracy

　　The 101st lecture in the New Democracy lecture series sponsored by the Beijing YMCA is scheduled for 7:00 PM on September 30th. Professor Chen Tiqiang of Tsinghua University will speak on: "The Struggle Between the Peace and Democratic Camp and the Warmongering Clique in the Security Council." All are welcome to attend.

### 新民主主义讲座

1950-09-29
第12版()
专栏：

　　新民主主义讲座
    京市青年会主办之新民主主义讲座第一○一讲，定九月三十日下午七时请清华大学教授陈体强先生在该会讲：《安理会内和平民主阵营与战贩集团的斗争”。欢迎各界前往听讲。
