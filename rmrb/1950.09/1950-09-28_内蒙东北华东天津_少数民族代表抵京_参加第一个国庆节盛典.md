Title: Representatives of Ethnic Minorities from Inner Mongolia, Northeast China, East China, and Tianjin Arrive in Beijing to Participate in the First National Day Celebration

Author: 
Date: 1950-09-28
Page: 3

　　Inner Mongolia, Northeast China, East China, Tianjin
    Representatives of Ethnic Minorities Arrive in Beijing
    To Participate in the First National Day Celebration
    [Xinhua News Agency, September 27th] The Inner Mongolia delegation, Korean ethnic representatives from the Northeast region, Gaoshan and Hui ethnic representatives from the East China region, and Hui ethnic representatives from Tianjin, who are here in the capital to celebrate the first National Day of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing successively on the 27th. Lin Boqu, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government; Li Weihan, Secretary-General of the State Council of the Central People's Government and Director of the Ethnic Affairs Commission; Ulanhu, Deputy Director of the Ethnic Affairs Commission; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; Yu Xinqing, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Central People's Government; Zhu Zaoguan, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Liu Chun and Kui Bi, members of the Ethnic Affairs Commission; Yang Jingren, Director of the General Office of the Ethnic Affairs Commission; Wang Baiping, Deputy Director of the Secretariat of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Zhou Zijian, Deputy Director of the General Office of the State Council, and more than 240 welcoming personnel from the Ethnic Affairs Commission and other institutions and the Mongolian and Tibetan schools went to the station to welcome them.
    The Inner Mongolia delegation is headed by Erqin Batu (League Leader of Inner Mongolia Hulunbuir League); Yide Gasulong (Secretary of the United Front Work Department of the Inner Mongolia Sub-bureau of the Chinese Communist Party) and Wang Qing (Deputy League Leader of Inner Mongolia Xilin Gol League) serve as the first and second deputy heads of the delegation, respectively. The delegation has a total of eighteen official representatives (two of whom have not yet arrived), including twelve Mongolian representatives, two Daur representatives, and one representative each from the Buryat Mongolian, Solon, Tungusic, and Oroqen ethnic groups. Including the Inner Mongolia Art Troupe and accompanying personnel, there are a total of ninety-four people. The Northeast Korean ethnic representatives Lin Renxi and Jin Xinshu, as well as the Korean Art Troupe and accompanying personnel, total forty-three people. The East China Gaoshan ethnic representative Lin Sanliang and Hui ethnic representative Wu Xiufu and accompanying personnel total three people. The Tianjin Hui ethnic representative is Wang Xiaoxi, one person.

### 内蒙东北华东天津  少数民族代表抵京  参加第一个国庆节盛典

1950-09-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　内蒙东北华东天津
    少数民族代表抵京
    参加第一个国庆节盛典
    【新华社二十七日讯】为庆祝中华人民共和国第一个国庆节而来首都的内蒙代表团、东北区朝鲜族代表及华东区高山族和回族的代表、天津市回民代表，于二十七日先后到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠、中央人民政府政务院秘书长、民族事务委员会主任李维汉、民族事务委员会副主任乌兰夫、北京市副市长吴晗、中央人民政府办公厅副主任余心清、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会办公厅副主任朱早观、民族事务委员会委员刘春、奎壁、民族事务委员会办公厅主任杨静仁、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会秘书处副处长王百评、政务院秘书厅副厅长周子健，及民族事务委员会等机关和蒙藏学校的欢迎人员共二百四十余人。
    内蒙代表团由额尔钦巴图（内蒙呼纳盟盟长）任团长；义得嘎苏隆（中国共产党内蒙分局统战部秘书）和旺庆（内蒙锡林郭勒盟副盟长）分任第一、第二副团长。该代表团共有正式代表十八人（内有二人尚未到），其中蒙古族代表十二人、达呼尔族代表两人、布里雅特蒙古族、索伦族、通古斯族和鄂伦春族代表各一人。连同内蒙文艺工作团与随从人员在内，共计九十四人。东北区朝鲜族代表林人锡、金信淑及朝鲜文艺工作团和随员共四十三人。华东区高山族代表林三良和回族代表吴修福及随员共三人。天津市回民代表王筱溪一人。
