Title: Stockholm Esperantists Call: Serve Peace with Esperanto, Expose the Masks of Warmongers
Author:
Date: 1950-09-20
Page: 4
　
　　Stockholm Esperantists Call:
    Serve Peace with Esperanto
    Expose the Masks of Warmongers
    [Xinhua News Agency, 19th] The preparatory committee of the Chinese Esperanto Association today received a declaration from the Stockholm Esperantists Peace Committee regarding the defense of world peace. The declaration expresses firm support for the Stockholm Resolution of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress and issues the following call to Esperantists:

    We ask Esperantists to consciously strive to work to realize Zamenhof's beautiful ideal of lasting world peace, to respond to the call of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress. …

    Translating appropriate materials from progressive international Esperanto magazines and publishing them in national language newspapers and magazines is also one of our tasks. … We should strive, with the help of Esperanto, to end the "Cold War" that is extremely detrimental to world progress. We must disseminate the truth about progressive countries. At the same time, we must not forget to expose the masks of warmongers. …

    Great work awaits us! Let us carry out the work of defending world peace!

    We shout: Serve peace with Esperanto!

    (The address of the Stockholm Esperantists Peace Committee is: K.V.Ahlberg BjOrngardsgatan 7, Stockholm SO, Svedio)

    Note: The Chinese Esperanto international propaganda publication "People's China Reports" called on Esperantists around the world in June to respond to the call of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress with signatures and publicity. The preparatory committee of the All-China Esperanto Association also issued a declaration in August, calling on Esperantists around the world to protest against American imperialist aggression in Korea and to respond to the peace signature campaign.

### 斯德哥尔摩世界语者号召：用世界语为和平服务  揭穿战争贩子们的假面具

1950-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　斯德哥尔摩世界语者号召：
    用世界语为和平服务
    揭穿战争贩子们的假面具
    【新华社十九日讯】中国世界语协会筹委会本日接到斯德哥尔摩世界语者和平委员会的关于保卫世界和平的宣言。宣言表示坚决支持和大常委会斯德哥尔摩决议，并向世界语者发出如下号召：
    我们要求世界语者们自觉地努力工作，以实现柴门霍夫世界持久和平的美丽的理想，来响应保卫世界和平大会常委会的号召。……
    从国际间进步的世界语杂志中翻译适当的材料，在民族语报杂志上发表，也是我们的任务之一。……我们应该努力，借世界语的帮助，来结束极其妨害世界进步的“冷战”。我们要传播进步国家的真实情况。同时我们不要忘记揭穿战争贩子们的假面具。……
    伟大的工作在等着我们！来进行保卫世界和平的工作吧！
    我们高呼：用世界语为和平服务！
    （斯德哥尔摩世界语者和平委员会的通讯处是：K.V.Ahlberg BjOrngardsgatan 7,Stockholm SO, Svedio）
    按：中国世界语国际宣传刊“人民中国报道”曾于六月间号召全世界的世界语者用签名和宣传响应世界保卫和平大会常委会号召。而中华全国世界语协会筹备委员会也于八月间发表宣言，号召全世界世界语者抗议美帝侵略朝鲜，响应和平签名运动。
