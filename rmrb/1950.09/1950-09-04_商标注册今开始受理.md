Title: Trademark Registration Accepted Starting Today
Author:
Date: 1950-09-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 4th: The Central Private Enterprise Bureau will begin accepting trademark registrations today. The State Council of the Central People's Government has decided that the Private Enterprise Bureau of the Financial and Economic Commission will be responsible for handling trademark registration work in accordance with the law. The "Provisional Regulations on Trademark Registration," the "Detailed Rules for the Implementation of the Provisional Regulations on Trademark Registration," the "Measures for Replacing Trademark Registration Certificates by Local People's Governments," and the "Measures for Handling Trademarks Registered by the Trademark Bureau of the Former Kuomintang Reactionary Government" have also been promulgated by the State Council of the Central People's Government successively. The Central Private Enterprise Bureau has recently decided to begin accepting applications for trademark registration cases starting from September 4th.

### 商标注册今开始受理

1950-09-04
第2版()
专栏：

　　商标注册今开始受理
    【新华社四日讯】中央私营企业局今日开始受理商标注册。中央人民政府政务院已经决定由财政经济委员会私营企业局负责依法办理商标注册工作。商标注册暂行条例，商标注册暂行条例施行细则，各地方人民政府注册商标证更换办法，及前国民党反动政府商标局注册商标处理办法等，也已经中央人民政府政务院先后公布。中央私营企业局顷决定自九月四日起开始受理申请商标注册案件。
