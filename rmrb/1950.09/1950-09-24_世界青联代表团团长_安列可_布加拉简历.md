Title: Biography of Enrico Berlinguer, Head of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1950-09-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd: Biography of Enrico Berlinguer, Head of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation and Secretary-General of the World Federation of Democratic Youth:

　　Enrico Berlinguer was born in Italy in 1920.

　　From 1940 to 1945, he engaged in underground struggles against the German and Italian fascists in Tunisia, North Africa, and with Spano, now a member of the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party. After the end of World War II, he returned to Italy and was responsible for the work of young Communist Party members in the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party. In 1948, he served as the secretary of the National Committee of the Italian Youth League. In 1949, he was elected Secretary-General of the World Federation of Democratic Youth at the Second Congress of the World Federation of Democratic Youth.

### 世界青联代表团团长  安列可·布加拉简历

1950-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界青联代表团团长
    安列可·布加拉简历
    【新华社二十三日讯】世界民主青年联盟代表团团长、世界民主青年联盟总书记安列可·布加拉简历：
    安列可·布加拉一九二零年生于意大利。
    一九四零年到一九四五年在北非突尼斯和现在的意大利共产党中央委员斯巴诺在一起进行反抗德、意法西斯的地下斗争。二次世界大战结束后回到意大利，负责意共中央青年党员的工作。一九四八年担任意大利青年同盟全国委员会书记。一九四九年在世界民主青年联盟第二次代表大会上被选为世界青年联盟总书记。
