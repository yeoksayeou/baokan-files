Title: Lectures on New Democracy
Author:
Date: 1950-09-01
Page: 8

The New Democracy Lecture Series, sponsored by the Beijing YMCA, will feature Dr. Qiu Zuyuan of the Beijing First Health Clinic at 7:00 PM on Saturday, September 2nd. Dr. Qiu will lecture on "The Eighth National Medical Conference and the National Health Plan." A health education film will be shown after the lecture. All are welcome to attend.

### 新民主主义讲座

1950-09-01
第8版()
专栏：

　　新民主主义讲座
    京市青年会主办之新民主主义讲座定九月二日（星期六）下午七时特请北京市第一卫生所裘祖源大夫在该会讲：“第八届中华医学会与全国卫生计划”。会后并放映有关卫生教育电影，欢迎各界前往听讲。
