Title: Sunday Literary Lecture
Author:
Date: 1950-09-16
Page: 8

The Sunday Literary Lecture, jointly organized by the Mass Literature and Art Creation Research Association and the Youth Service Department, will feature Yan Chen as the speaker this week. The topic is: "An Informal Talk on Folk Songs." After the lecture, Li Shaohua and Feng Dalong will perform the Henan Zhui Zi ballad "The Liberation Army Crosses Xiaoxi Tian." The address is the Friendship Hall on the second floor of the Youth Palace. The time is 9:30 a.m. on Sunday. Admission is by long-term or temporary ticket. Temporary tickets can be obtained from the Youth Service Department on Saturday.

### 星期文艺讲座

1950-09-16
第8版()
专栏：

　　星期文艺讲座
    大众文艺创研会与青年服务部合办的星期文艺讲座，本周请严辰主讲，讲题是：《民歌漫谈》，会后由李少华、冯达龙演唱河南坠子《解放军横过小西天》。地址在青年宫二楼交谊厅，时间在星期日上午九时半，凭长期券临时券入场，临时券可于星期六到青年服务部领取。
