Title: Xinjiang's Peaceful Liberation Anniversary: Dihua Holds Celebration Rally, Telegram to Chairman Mao Expressing Respect and Reporting Work Achievements
Author:
Date: 1950-09-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Dihua, 26th:  September 25th marked the anniversary of Xinjiang's peaceful liberation. Leading figures from the Provincial People's Government and other agencies, as well as representatives from various people's organizations, held a celebration rally on that day. Shen Junru, head of the Central People's Government Northwest Nationalities Visiting Delegation, his deputy, Sa Kongliao, and other members of the delegation attended. The rally was extremely grand and enthusiastic. Provincial People's Government Chairman, Bao Erhan; Central People's Government Northwest Nationalities Visiting Delegation head, Shen Junru; CCP Central Committee Xinjiang Branch Secretary and Acting Commander of the People's Liberation Army Xinjiang Military Region, General Wang Zhen; and 22nd Army Commander, General Tao Zhiyue, all gave speeches. After recounting the uprising, Chairman Bao Erhan stated: To commemorate the peaceful liberation of Xinjiang, we must consolidate the people's democratic dictatorship and the people's organizations of all fraternal nationalities, and strive for the complete liberation of the people of all nationalities under the banner of Mao Zedong. General Wang Zhen pointed out in his speech: Only when the people of all nationalities are closely united can Xinjiang be built well. The meeting passed a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao, Chairman Peng Dehuai, and Vice Chairmen Xi Zhongxun and Zhang Zhizhong of the Northwest Military and Political Committee. The telegram to Chairman Mao reported the work achievements of Xinjiang's peaceful liberation over the past year. The telegram also stated: This is only the first step on the long road to building a new Xinjiang for the people. The people of all nationalities in Xinjiang will unite even more closely under the banner of Chairman Mao, eliminate all obstacles on the road ahead, and strive towards the goal of building a free, happy, and industrially developed new Xinjiang!

### 新疆和平解放一周年  迪化举行庆祝大会  电毛主席致敬报告工作成绩

1950-09-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　新疆和平解放一周年
    迪化举行庆祝大会
    电毛主席致敬报告工作成绩
    【新华社迪化二十六日电】本月二十五日为新疆和平解放周年纪念，省人民政府等机关首长和各人民团体代表等，曾于当日举行庆祝大会。中央人民政府西北各民族访问团团长沈钧儒、副团长萨空了及访问团团员均曾出席参加。大会极为隆重热烈。省人民政府鲍尔汉主席、中央人民政府西北各民族访问团沈钧儒团长、中共中央新疆分局书记、人民解放军新疆军区代司令员王震将军、第二十二兵团司令员陶峙岳将军等先后讲话。鲍尔汉主席于叙述起义经过后称：纪念新疆和平解放要巩固人民民主专政、各兄弟民族人民团体，在毛泽东的旗帜下，为争取各民族人民彻底解放而奋斗。王震将军在讲话中指出：只有各民族人民紧密地团结，才能把新疆建设好。大会通过了向毛主席及西北军政委员会彭德怀主席、习仲勋和张治中副主席的致敬电。致毛主席电报告了新疆和平解放一年来的工作成绩。该电并称：这不过是长期建设人民新新疆途程中的第一步，新疆各民族人民将更加紧密地团结在毛主席的旗帜下，排除前进道路上的一切障碍，向着建设自由幸福、工业发达的新新疆的目标奋斗！
