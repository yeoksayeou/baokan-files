Title: Compatriots from All Circles in Hong Kong and Macau Actively Prepare to Celebrate National Day
Author:
Date: 1950-09-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 21st – Compatriots from all circles in Hong Kong and Macau are actively preparing to warmly celebrate the National Day of the new People's China. The Hong Kong and Kowloon Federation of Trade Unions has designated the Naval Dockyard Workers' Union, the Dairy Workers' Union, and the Telephone Workers' Union to be responsible for all preparatory work. The Chinese General Chamber of Commerce, the Chinese Manufacturers' Association, and figures from the industrial and commercial sectors have organized a "Preparatory Committee for the Hong Kong and Kowloon Industrial and Commercial Circles' National Day Celebration Rally," planning to hold a grand celebration rally on October 1st. The Macau Federation of Trade Unions, the Federation of Students, the Women's Federation, the Chinese Overseas Association, and over forty other organizations have also formed a "Preparatory Committee for the Celebration of the National Day of the People's Republic of China," deciding that all circles in Macau will have a day off on October 1st, raise flags throughout Macau in the morning, fire cannons at noon, and hold a celebration rally and recreational evening party. Decorated archways will be erected on major streets, and every household will be decorated with lanterns and colored streamers.

### 香港澳门各界同胞积极筹备庆祝国庆

1950-09-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　香港澳门各界同胞积极筹备庆祝国庆
    【新华社广州二十一日电】香港、澳门各界同胞正在积极筹备热烈庆祝人民新中国的国庆节。港九工会联合会已推定由洋务工会、牛奶工会和电话工会三个单位负责一切筹备工作。华商总会、中华厂商联合会及工商界人士组织了“港九工商界庆祝国庆大会筹备会”、准备在十月一日举行隆重的庆祝大会。澳门工联、学联、妇联、华侨协会等四十余单位也组织了“庆祝中华人民共和国国庆大会筹备会”，决定十月一日全澳各界放假一日，早晨全澳升旗，中午鸣炮，并举行庆祝大会及游艺晚会，各大街将搭彩花牌楼，家家户户张灯结彩。
