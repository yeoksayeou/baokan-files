Title: Romania Holds Peace Conference; Sends Telegram to the Security Council Demanding the Withdrawal of US Troops from Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-09-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 16th: Bucharest Dispatch: The Romanian National Congress for the Defense of Peace was held in Bucharest, the capital of Romania, on the 9th. The conference lasted for three days and fully demonstrated the Romanian people's firm will and determination to defend world peace.

　　A total of 2,500 delegates elected by the people from all over the country attended the conference, including advanced workers and peasants, outstanding scientists and cultural workers, social leaders, and representatives of patriotic clergy. Representatives of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace, D'Arboussier and Amado, a Soviet delegation composed of well-known Soviet scientists such as Oparin, and delegations from Germany, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Hungary, Vietnam, and France, Italy, Austria and other countries also attended the conference.

　　D'Arboussier delivered a speech at the conference, praising the great contribution of this conference to the world movement for the defense of peace, and urged the expansion of the struggle to defend peace. Misis Nicu, a member of the Romanian Standing Committee for the Defense of Peace, reported on the progress of the Romanian movement for the defense of peace, and pointed out: A total of 17,024 peace committees have been established in Romania, with a total of 237,635 members, including 55,000 workers and 112,537 laboring peasants. She said: After more than 10.04 million people in Romania signed the peace declaration, they have achieved great success in the struggle to complete the production plan.

　　The conference unanimously adopted a resolution to send a telegram to the United Nations Security Council, strongly protesting the US aircraft's indiscriminate bombing of peaceful cities and villages in Korea, demanding the withdrawal of US troops from Korea, demanding the prohibition of atomic weapons, condemning war propaganda and implementing disarmament.

　　The conference elected a delegation including representatives from various well-known figures to attend the Second World Congress of Supporters of Peace to be held in London in November. The conference also elected the Standing Committee for the Defense of Peace, and elected Academician Sadoveanu of the Academy of Sciences as chairman.

　　On the 12th, the working people of Bucharest held a grand rally to celebrate the successful conclusion of the Romanian National Congress for the Defense of Peace.

### 罗马尼亚举行和平大会  致电安理会要求美军撤出朝鲜

1950-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚举行和平大会
    致电安理会要求美军撤出朝鲜
    【新华社十六日讯】布加勒斯特消息：罗马尼亚全国拥护和平大会，于九日在罗国首都布加勒斯特举行。大会历时三日，充分显示了罗马尼亚人民保卫世界和平的坚决意志与决心。
    出席此次大会的共有各地人民选出的二千五百名代表，包括先进的工人与农民，杰出的科学家与文化工作者，社会领袖和爱国教士的代表等。世界拥护和平大会常设委员会代表达波赛及阿玛多，由苏联著名科学家奥巴林等人组成的苏联代表团，德国、捷克、保加利亚、匈牙利、越南以及法、意、奥地利等国代表团，亦出席了大会。
    达波赛在大会上发表演说，盛赞这次大会对世界保卫和平运动的巨大贡献，并敦促扩大保卫和平的斗争。罗马尼亚保卫和平常设委员会委员米辛西斯库，报告了罗马尼亚保卫和平运动的进展情况，并指出：罗马尼亚总计已成立了一万七千零二十四个和平委员会，共有二十三万七千六百三十五名委员，包括工人五万五千名，劳动农民十一万二千五百三十七名等。她说：罗马尼亚一千零四万以上人民在和平宣言上签名后，已在为完成生产计划的斗争中获得了巨大的成就。
    大会一致决议致电联合国安理会，坚决抗议美国飞机滥炸朝鲜和平的城市与乡村，要求美军撤出朝鲜，要求禁止原子武器，谴责战争宣传和实行裁减军备。
    大会选出了包括各方面著名人士的代表团，前往出席十一月间在伦敦举行的第二届世界拥护和平大会，大会又选出了保卫和平常设委员会，选举科学院院士沙杜维亚努连任主席。
    十二日，布加勒斯特劳动人民举行了盛大集会，庆祝罗马尼亚全国拥护和平大会的胜利闭幕。
