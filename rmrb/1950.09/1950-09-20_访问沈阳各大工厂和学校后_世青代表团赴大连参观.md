Title: World Youth Delegation Visits Dalian After Visiting Factories and Schools in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1950-09-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, September 19th: After completing their visit to Shenyang, the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation, accompanied by Han Tianshi, Secretary of the Northeast Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, and Zhang Ze, Deputy Secretary, left for Dalian at 11:00 PM on the 17th for a visit. Among those who went to the station to see them off were Zhang Xiushan, Director of the Organization Department of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Che Xiangchen, Minister of Education of the Northeast People's Government, Zhu Qiwen, Mayor of Shenyang, Shen Zhilan, Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, and more than 500 others.

　　During their three-day visit to Shenyang, the delegation, in addition to attending two welcome rallies, also visited major factories and schools in separate groups.

　　On the afternoon of the 15th, the delegates visited China Medical University. In order to express their support for the people of Korea, Vietnam, Spain, and Greece who are engaged in the anti-aggression struggle, the university presented the representatives of the above countries with four volumes of "Modern War Surgery" and various surgical instruments, and a portrait of Chairman Mao to the delegation.

　　On the 16th, the delegates visited the Shenyang Smelting Plant, the No. 6 Electrical Engineering Plant, the No. 1 Rubber Factory, the No. 5 Machinery Factory, the Railway Factory, and the Northeast Institute of Technology in separate groups. The delegates were warmly welcomed by young workers and students everywhere they went. Chen Lian'gao and Zhao Qiming, the creators of the magnesium brick furnace at the Shenyang Smelting Plant, specially presented a banner with the words "Good Teacher and Helpful Friend" to Soviet youth representative Semi Chasni in gratitude for the help they had received from Soviet expert Brudalov. At the No. 5 Machine Factory, the delegates observed in detail the first universal milling machine mother machine produced by the factory and the Ma Hengchang Group that was working, and congratulated the group on its achievements. At the welcome meeting held at the factory, the delegates had close talks with Zhu Jinqing, a member of the Ma Hengchang Group, Tong Junshan, the creator of twelve new records, and Chen Yucai, the director of the model workshop. They received a large number of gifts from the youth during their visits to these factories and schools. The delegates also exchanged commemorative albums with the welcoming youth in the factories and schools. The youth representative from Austria also left an address, hoping that the students of the Northeast Institute of Technology would correspond with the youth of Austria.

　　On the afternoons of the 15th and 17th, the delegation participated in two symposiums. At the symposiums, Zhang Xiushan, Director of the Organization Department of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Jiao Ruoyu, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, respectively introduced the restoration and development of the Northeast's economy and culture, as well as the achievements of Shenyang's restoration and construction since its liberation. Han Tianshi, Secretary of the Northeast Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, also introduced the youth movement and the work of the Youth League in the Northeast to the delegation at the meeting.

### 访问沈阳各大工厂和学校后  世青代表团赴大连参观

1950-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　访问沈阳各大工厂和学校后
    世青代表团赴大连参观
    【新华社沈阳十九日电】世界民主青年联盟代表团完成在沈阳的访问后，已于十七日晚十一时由中国新民主主义青年团东北委员会书记韩天石、副书记章泽陪同，前往大连参观。赴车站欢送者有中共中央东北局组织部长张秀山、东北人民政府教育部长车向忱、沈阳市长朱其文、中国新民主主义青年团沈阳市委员会书记申之澜等五百余人。
    该团在访问沈阳的三天内，除出席两次欢迎大会外，并曾分赴各大工厂、学校参观。
    十五日下午代表团各代表参观了中国医科大学。该校为了表示对正在进行反侵略斗争的朝鲜、越南、西班牙和希腊人民的支援，以“现代战争外科学”四本及外科医疗器具多件，赠与上述各国代表；并以毛主席画像赠给代表团。
    十六日代表团各代表分组参观了沈阳冶炼厂、电工六厂、橡胶一厂、第五机械厂、铁路工厂及东北工学院。代表们每到一处都受到青年工人和学生的热烈欢迎。沈阳冶炼厂镁砖炉创造者陈连高、赵启明为了感谢苏联专家布鲁达洛夫曾经给予他们的帮助，特亲向苏联青年代表赛米恰斯尼献赠“良师益友”锦旗一面。在机器五厂，代表们详细观察了该厂出产的第一部万能铣床母机和正在工作中的马恒昌小组，并向该组获得的成就祝贺。在该厂举行的欢迎会上，代表们和马恒昌小组组员祝晋庆、十二次新纪录创造者佟俊山、模范车间主任陈毓材等亲密倾谈。他们在参观和访问这些工厂和学校中曾受到青年们赠送大批礼物。代表们在工厂和学校中还与欢迎的青年交换了纪念册。奥地利的青年代表并留下了地址，希望东北工学院的同学和奥地利的青年通信。
    代表团在十五日和十七日下午先后参加了两次座谈会。在座谈会上中共中央东北局组织部长张秀山和沈阳市副市长焦若愚分别介绍东北经济文化的恢复与发展情况以及沈阳市解放以来恢复建设的成绩。新民主主义青年团东北委员会书记韩天石也在会上向代表团介绍了东北青年运动和青年团的工作情况。
