Title: Xi'an and Shaanxi People from All Walks of Life Telegraph Support for Foreign Minister Zhou's Protest Against the U.S.

Author:

Date: 1950-09-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, August 31st: People from all walks of life in Xi'an and Shaanxi Province have telegraphed their support for Foreign Minister Zhou's stern protest to the U.S. government. The Xi'an branch of the China Democratic League, the preparatory committee of the Xi'an branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, the Xi'an branch of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace, the Xi'an branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and people's organizations such as workers, women, youth, and students, have expressed great anger at the blatant criminal actions of American aircraft. In their telegrams, they expressed their firm confidence and vowed to be the backing of the Central People's Government and to smash the aggressive actions of U.S. imperialism.

The Shaanxi Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, the Farmers' Association, the Preparatory Committee of the Democratic Women's Federation, the Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and the Shaanxi Working Committee of the New Democratic Youth League also jointly issued a telegram on the 29th. It stated: We were extremely indignant upon hearing the news of the armed provocations by American aircraft in the Northeast. Now, two million people in the province have signed the peace declaration. We will further dispatch cadres to go deep into the countryside to carry out propaganda, expand the peace signature campaign, and call on the people of the province to defeat the US imperialist conspiracy of aggression.

### 西安市及陕西省各界人民  通电拥护周外长对美抗议

1950-09-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　西安市及陕西省各界人民
    通电拥护周外长对美抗议
    【新华社西安三十一日电】西安市和陕西省的各界人民纷纷通电拥护周外长向美国政府提出的严正抗议。中国民主同盟西安市支部、民主建国会西安分会筹备委员会、中国保卫世界和平大会委员会西安分会、中苏友好协会西安分会以及工人、妇女、青年、学生等人民团体，均对美国飞机明目张胆的犯罪行动表示极大的愤怒。他们在电文里，表示坚决的信心，誓为中央人民政府的后盾，粉碎美帝国主义的侵略行动。
    陕西省总工会、农民协会、民主妇女联合会筹委会、文学艺术界联合会、新民主主义青年团陕西工作委员会等亦于二十九日联合发表通电。内称：我们听到美国飞机在东北的武装挑衅消息后，极为愤慨。现在全省已发动了二百万人在和平宣言上签了名。我们将更进一步地派遣干部，深入农村进行宣传，扩大和平签名运动，号召全省人民打垮美帝侵略阴谋。
