Title: Chinese Sports Delegation Departs for the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1950-09-01
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, 30th.

　　The Chinese Sports Delegation, consisting of twelve members led by Xu Yingchao, the head of the delegation, and Wang Ke, the deputy head, departed for the Soviet Union on the evening of the 28th. The delegation was organized by the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Sports Federation at the invitation of the Sports Committee of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union. The main task of the delegation is to learn from the advanced and rich experience of Soviet sports, and to strengthen the friendship between Chinese and Soviet sports workers, athletes, and people.
　　Those who went to the station to see them off included Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, Rong Gaotang, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Sports Federation, Li Fenglou, Deputy Director of the Beijing Sports Association, Duan Bingchen, and representatives of sports workers and teachers from major universities, middle and primary schools in Beijing.
　　The list of the Chinese Sports Delegation is as follows:
　　Head of Delegation: Xu Yingchao (Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Sports Federation); Deputy Head of Delegation: Wang Ke (Representative of the Chinese People's Liberation Army); Secretary-General: Li Yiqing; Deputy Secretary-General: Ke Li; Members: Yang Zhongxiu, Chen Zhenhua, Hu Lihe, Huang Shi, Yang Lie (female), Song Junfu, Wu Gongjun; Translator: Wei Yunfeng.

### 中国体育访问团启程赴苏

1950-09-01
第6版()
专栏：

　　中国体育访问团启程赴苏
    【新华社三十日讯】中国体育访问团一行十二人由团长徐英超、副团长王克率领，已于二十八日晚启程前往苏联。该访问团系中华全国体育总会筹委会应苏联部长会议体育委员会之邀请而组织的。该团的主要任务为学习苏联体育运动的先进、丰富的经验，加强中苏两国体育工作者、运动员及人民的友谊。
    前往车站欢送的有青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬、中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志、中华全国体育总会筹委会副主任荣高棠、北京市体育分会副主任李凤楼、段炳辰及中华全国学生联合会、北京市各大、中、小学校的体育工作者、教师代表等多人。
    中国体育访问团名单如下：
    团长徐英超（中华全国体育总会筹委会副主任），副团长王克（中国人民解放军代表），秘书长李一青，副秘书长柯犁，团员杨钟秀、陈镇华、胡理和、黄狮、杨烈（女）、宋君复、吴功俊，翻译魏云峰。
