Title: Workers Continue to Protest American Aircraft Atrocities; Determined to Strike at Aggressors with Practical Actions
Author:
Date: 1950-09-06
Page: 4

　　Workers Continue to Protest American Aircraft Atrocities
　　Determined to Strike at Aggressors with Practical Actions
　　[This newspaper's dispatch] Workers in various locations continue to express their angry protests against the atrocities of American aircraft invading our airspace and killing and wounding our people.
　　70th Ordnance Factory
　　The staff and workers pointed out: "Recently, American aircraft have continuously intruded into our Northeast airspace, killing and wounding our compatriots. This is clearly a planned act of provocation by American imperialism." They expressed their firm opposition to this crazy atrocity by American imperialism, calling on the people of the whole country to unite as one, heighten their vigilance, and stop the aggressive actions of American imperialism. They also support Foreign Minister Zhou's serious protest to the American government, and will strike at the war criminals with practical actions.
　　Fengfeng Mining Bureau Repair Plant
　　Upon hearing the news of American aircraft invading our airspace, the staff and workers were filled with indignation and unanimously expressed their determination to fight for revenge for the compatriots who died in the Northeast!
　　Tianjin Steel Fittings Factory No. 1
　　All the staff and workers unanimously responded to the call of the trade union, supporting Foreign Minister Zhou's statement with practical actions, guaranteeing the early completion of the September production plan, improving product quality, and eliminating accidents in work, in order to oppose the aggressive behavior of American imperialism with practical actions!
　　Tangshan Power Plant
　　All the members of the Youth League expressed that, in addition to supporting Foreign Minister Zhou's statement, demanding that the US imperialists immediately punish the air force personnel involved in the incident and compensate for all our losses, they are also determined, under the leadership of the Party, to actively work and study hard in the factory, thoroughly eliminate the occurrence of accidents, ensure safe power transmission, and support the soldiers at the front in liberating Taiwan and Tibet quickly.

### 工人继续抗议美机暴行  决以实际行动打击侵略者

1950-09-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　工人继续抗议美机暴行
    决以实际行动打击侵略者
    【本报讯】对美机侵犯我领空、杀伤我人民的暴行，各地工人继续表示愤怒的抗议。
    七十兵工厂
    职工指出：“最近美机不断侵入我东北领空，杀伤我国同胞，显然是美帝有计划的挑衅行为”，表示坚决反对美帝这种疯狂的暴行，呼吁全国人民团结一致，提高警惕，制止美帝扩大侵略的行动；并拥护周外长对美国政府所提出的严重抗议，以实际行动打击战争犯子。
    峰峰矿务局修理厂
    职工闻悉美机侵犯我领空消息后，群情激愤，一致表示坚决为东北遇难同胞复仇而斗争！
    天津钢铁配件一厂
    全体职工一致响应工会号召，以实际行动拥护周外长声明，保证提前完成九月份生产计划，提高产品质量，消灭工作中事故，以实际行动反对美帝国主义的侵略行为！
    唐山发电厂
    全体青年团员表示除拥护周外长声明，要美帝立即惩办肇事空军人员、赔偿我一切损失，并且决心在党的领导下，在工厂中积极工作努力学习，彻底消灭事故的发生，保证安全发送用电，支援前方战士迅速解放台湾和西藏。
