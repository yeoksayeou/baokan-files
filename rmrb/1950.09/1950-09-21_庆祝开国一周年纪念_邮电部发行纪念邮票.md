Title: Celebrating the First Anniversary of the Founding of the Nation: Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications Issues Commemorative Stamps
Author:
Date: 1950-09-21
Page: 1

　　Celebrating the First Anniversary of the Founding of the Nation
　　Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications Issues Commemorative Stamps
　　[Xinhua News Agency, 20th] To celebrate the first anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government is issuing a set of three-color, finely printed "Commemorative Stamps for the First Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China." Five types of these stamps will be issued in both the customs area and the Northeast. Post offices in major cities across the country are scheduled to begin selling them on October 1st of this year. Post offices in remote areas will sell them upon receipt.
　　At the same time, the General Postal Administration of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications has also notified postal administrations (postal and telecommunications administrations in the Northeast) and their subordinate offices throughout the country to use the National Day commemorative date stamp on October 1st. The design of this date stamp is such that the upper crescent reads "Commemoration of the First Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China," and the lower crescent reads the name of the post office.

### 庆祝开国一周年纪念  邮电部发行纪念邮票

1950-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝开国一周年纪念
    邮电部发行纪念邮票
    【新华社二十日讯】中央人民政府邮电部为庆祝开国一周年纪念，特发行《中华人民共和国开国一周年纪念》三色精印邮票。此项邮票关内和东北各发五种。全国各大城市邮局，定于本年十月一日起开始发售。边远邮区则于收到后发售。
    与此同时，邮电部邮政总局并通知全国各地邮政管理局（东北为邮电管理局）及所属各局于十月一日使用国庆纪念日戳。此项日戳式样，上月牙为“中华人民共和国开国周年纪念”，下月牙为局名。
