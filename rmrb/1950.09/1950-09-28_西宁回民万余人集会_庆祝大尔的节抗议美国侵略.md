Title: Xining Hui People Hold Rally of Over Ten Thousand to Celebrate Eid al-Adha and Protest American Aggression
Author:
Date: 1950-09-28
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 26th: Over ten thousand Hui people in Xining City, Qinghai Province, held a rally at the Grand Mosque on the 23rd to celebrate Eid al-Adha (also known as the Festival of Sacrifice) and to protest American imperialist aggression in Korea and Taiwan. Zhang Zhongliang, Secretary of the CPC Provincial Committee, Zhao Shoushan, Chairman of the Provincial People's Government, and Xirao Gyatso, Vice Chairman, were invited to speak. Ma Fuchen, Deputy Minister of the Industry Department of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, conveyed to the assembly the details of the Northwest minority nationalities delegation's visit to Beijing to meet Chairman Mao, and conveyed the Central People's Government and Chairman Mao's care and concern for all fraternal nationalities. During the prayer ceremony, over ten thousand Hui people unanimously opposed American imperialist aggression against Taiwan and Korea, and prayed for lasting world peace. 12,714 people signed the petition supporting peace at the prayer meeting.

### 西宁回民万余人集会  庆祝大尔的节抗议美国侵略

1950-09-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　西宁回民万余人集会
    庆祝大尔的节抗议美国侵略
    【新华社西安二十六日电】青海省会西宁市回族人民万余人，于二十三日在清真大寺举行西宁市回族人民庆祝大尔的节（亦称忠孝节）暨反对美帝侵略朝鲜台湾大会。中共省委张仲良书记，省人民政府赵寿山主席，喜饶嘉错副主席等均被邀讲话。西北军政委员会工业部马辅臣副部长向大会传达了西北各少数民族参观团到达北京谒见毛主席的经过，并传达了中央人民政府及毛主席对各兄弟民族爱护关怀之意。万余回族人民在举行祈祷仪式中，一致反对美帝侵略台湾朝鲜，祈祷世界永久和平。祈祷会上签名拥护和平者达一万二千七百十四人。
