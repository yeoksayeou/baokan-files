Title: Northwest Military Region and First Field Army Model Hero Representatives to Depart for Beijing Soon
Author:
Date: 1950-09-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 15th. The Northwest Military Region and the First Field Army of the People's Liberation Army have selected 64 hero and model representatives (still lacking one combat hero from the Fifth Army and ten militia heroes from Shaanxi and Gansu), who will soon depart for Beijing. Among the elected representatives are the famous Special-Class Combat Hero Liu Sihu, the model soldier-loving and Special Merit recipient Lü Shunbao, the excellent commander and combat hero Hu Qingshan who bravely smashed the more than 40-day siege by Usman bandits, the Special-Class Combat Hero Liu Jiyao who cleverly captured the North Peak of Mount Hua in Shaanxi, the combat hero Du Lihai who was the first to plant the victorious red flag on the summit of Huti Mountain during the counter-attack in the Huanglong Mountain area of Shaanxi, the famous sharpshooter Bai Bingwu, and the outstanding combat hero Zhang Baoying in the Lanzhou Campaign. The representatives are also carrying precious gifts from the officers and soldiers of various provincial military districts to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and the conference, as well as more than 1,200 charts, photographs, specimens, etc., prepared for exhibition in Beijing. The Political Department of the Northwest Military Region and the First Field Army held a grand evening party on the 11th to bid farewell to the hero and model representatives going to Beijing.

### 西北军区和第一野战军  英模代表即启程来京

1950-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　西北军区和第一野战军
    英模代表即启程来京
    【新华社西安十五日电】人民解放军西北军区和第一野战军已选出英雄、模范代表六十四名（尚缺第五军战斗英雄一名，陕西、甘肃民兵英雄十名），即将启程赴京。当选的代表中包括著名的特等战斗英雄刘四虎，爱兵模范、特功功臣吕顺保，英勇粉碎乌斯满土匪四十余天围攻的优秀指挥员战斗英雄胡青山，奇取陕西华山北峰的特等战斗英雄刘吉尧，在陕西黄龙山反击战役中第一个把胜利红旗插上壶梯山顶的战斗英雄杜立海，著名的神炮手白炳武，兰州战役中出色的战斗英雄张保英等人。代表们并携带有各省军区指战员献给毛主席、朱总司令及大会的珍贵礼物，和准备在京展览的图表、照片、标本等一千二百余件。西北军区和第一野战军政治部曾于十一日举行盛大晚会，欢送赴京的英雄模范代表。
