Title: Studying the Revolutionary Qualities of Heroes and Role Models: All Students of Hebei Institute of Technology in Tianjin Send a Congratulatory Letter
Author:
Date: 1950-09-30
Page: 3

　　Learning the Revolutionary Qualities of Heroes and Role Models
    All Students of Hebei Institute of Technology in Tianjin Send a Congratulatory Letter
    [Our Newspaper's Dispatch] All students of Hebei Institute of Technology in Tianjin sent a congratulatory letter to the National Congress of Combat Heroes and the National Congress of Labor Models of Workers, Peasants and Soldiers on September 27th. The original text of the letter is as follows: Representatives of the National Congress of Combat Heroes and the National Congress of Labor Models of Workers, Peasants and Soldiers:
    As we welcome the grand celebration of the founding of the nation, you are holding these two historically significant meetings in the capital, which centrally demonstrates the glorious achievements of uniting the nation's progressive forces under the leadership of the Communist Party and the Central People's Government. You, on the military front, have demonstrated the great courage and wisdom of the Chinese working people, and on the production and construction front, have displayed a high degree of initiative and creativity. You are the banners of progress for the nation's working people and the backbone of the new China's combat and production construction. All of us students extend our highest respect to you with utmost sincerity! And we will learn from your excellent revolutionary qualities of loving the motherland and loving labor, and learn from your brilliant examples in production and combat. Your heroic deeds will be immortal and will be the direction of our efforts as students. We are willing to improve our studies to create conditions for the construction of the new China, and prepare to fight for world peace and democracy, and for the fundamental improvement of the nation's financial and economic situation. Finally, we wish the representative meetings a complete success and the representatives good health!
                All Students of Hebei Institute of Technology in Tianjin
                September 27, 1950.

### 学习英雄模范的革命品质  天津河北工学院全体同学发出贺信

1950-09-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　学习英雄模范的革命品质
    天津河北工学院全体同学发出贺信
    【本报讯】天津河北工学院全体同学于九月二十七日向全国战斗英雄代表会议和全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议发出贺信。该信原文如下：全国战斗英雄代表会议和全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的代表们：
    正值迎接开国盛典之际，你们在首都举行这两个富有历史意义的会议，这正集中地表现了在共产党和中央人民政府领导之下团结全国前进力量的光辉成就，你们，在军事战线上表现了中国劳动人民极大的勇敢和智慧，在生产建设战线上发挥了高度的积极性和创造性，你们是全国劳动人民进步的旗子，是新中国战斗和生产建设上的骨干，我们全体同学谨以万分的热诚向你们致以崇高的敬礼！并学习你们爱祖国、爱劳动的优秀的革命品质，学习你们在生产和战斗中光辉的榜样。你们的英雄事业将永垂不朽，将是我们全体同学努力的方向，我们愿意以搞好学习为新中国的建设事业创造条件，准备着为争取全世界和平民主，和祖国的财政经济基本好转而斗争。最后，谨祝代表会议的胜利成功和代表们身体健康！
                天津河北工学院全体同学
                一九五○、九、二十七。
