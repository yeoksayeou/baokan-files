Title: British National Peace Committee Statement Calls for Continued Signature Collection, Welcomes First Million Peace Signatories
Author:
Date: 1950-09-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, September 17th: TASS London, September 15th - The Supreme Committee of the British National Peace Committee issued a statement welcoming the first million signatures on the Peace Declaration and welcoming the nearly two million votes at the Trade Union Congress in favor of holding international negotiations to ban the atomic bomb. The statement pointed out that the Labor government's new expansion plan and the extension of military service to two years have already caused widespread anxiety in Britain. The statement declared that the Supreme Committee is proud of the honor given to British peace supporters by the decision to hold the Second World Peace Congress in Britain. The committee called on all peace-loving people in Britain to participate in the activities and continue to collect millions of signatures on the Peace Declaration to serve as unashamed hosts for this grand gathering.

The statement concluded with the following slogans: "Force the limitation of all armaments! Oppose all war propaganda! Condemn all aggression, condemn military intervention in the affairs of other countries! Support all measures for a peaceful solution to the Korean question! Unite in the cause of peace! Participate in the world peace movement to save humanity!"

### 英全国和平委员会声明  号召继续征集签名  欢迎首批一百万和平签名者

1950-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　英全国和平委员会声明
    号召继续征集签名
    欢迎首批一百万和平签名者
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社伦敦十五日讯：英国全国和平委员会最高委员会发表声明，欢迎首批一百万人在和平宣言上签名，欢迎将近两百万人在职工大会上投票赞成举行禁止原子弹的国际谈判。声明指出：工党政府的新的扩军计划和延长兵役到两年的措施已经在英国引起广泛的不安。声明宣称：最高委员会因第二次世界拥护和平大会将在英国举行的决定所给予英国和平拥护者的荣幸而感到骄傲。该会并号召英国所有爱好和平的人民参加活动，继续征集数百万人在和平宣言上签名，以充当这次盛大集会的、毫无愧色的主人。
    声明最后提出下列口号：“强迫限制一切军备！反对一切战争宣传！谴责一切侵略，谴责对他国事务的军事干涉！支持为和平解决朝鲜问题的一切措施！在和平的事业中团结起来！参加拯救人类的世界拥护和平运动！”
