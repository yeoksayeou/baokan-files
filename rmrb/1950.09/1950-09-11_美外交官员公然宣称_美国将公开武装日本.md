Title: U.S. Diplomatic Officials Publicly Declare that the United States Will Openly Arm Japan
Author:
Date: 1950-09-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. U.S. diplomatic officials admit that the United States will openly rearm Japan. According to a Washington dispatch from the United Press: U.S. diplomatic officials declared on the 2nd that, regarding the rearmament of Japan, the U.S. position is: "In favor of unrestricted rearmament (of Japan) after the conclusion of a peace treaty." The U.S. attitude is: "The peace conference should not include provisions requiring Japan to remain disarmed." The dispatch reveals that the United States is using "the Korean War and all the issues involved" as a pretext to force its puppet states to accept the U.S. proposition of rearming Japan.

### 美外交官员公然宣称  美国将公开武装日本

1950-09-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　美外交官员公然宣称
    美国将公开武装日本
    【新华社六日讯】美国外交官员承认美国将公开重新武装日本。据合众社华盛顿讯：美国外交界官员二日宣称：关于重新武装日本，美国的主张是：“赞成在和约缔结后，对（日本）重整军备，不加限制”，美国的态度是：对日“和会中不应该有日本必须保持无武装状态的规定”。该电透露，美国正以“朝鲜战争及其涉及的一切问题”为借口，强迫其仆从国家接受美国重新武装日本的主张。
