Title: Dai Guowen, Representative of Chinese Leather Workers, Travels to Romania to Attend International Conference of the Leather and Fur Workers Union
Author:
Date: 1950-09-10
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 9th. Dai Guowen, representative of Chinese leather workers and Director of the Leather Work Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, left Beijing on the 5th of this month for Bucharest, the capital of Romania, to attend the Second Meeting of the International Administrative Committee of the World Federation of Trade Unions of Leather and Fur Workers, which will be held from September 11th to 13th. This meeting will review the work of the International Leather and Fur Workers Union over the past year, focusing on how to strengthen the unity of leather workers worldwide and defend world peace. Dai Guowen is the director of the Shenyang No. 1 Equipment Factory. He was elected as an executive member at the founding meeting of the International Leather and Fur Workers Union of the World Federation of Trade Unions on June 20th last year.

### 出席皮革皮毛工人工会国际会议  我皮革工人代表戴国文赴罗

1950-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　出席皮革皮毛工人工会国际会议
    我皮革工人代表戴国文赴罗
    【新华社九日讯】我国皮革工人代表、中华全国总工会皮革工作委员会主任戴国文，已于本月五日离北京前往罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特，以便出席将于九月十一日至十三日召开的世界工联皮革皮毛工人工会国际行政委员会第二次会议。这次会议将检讨过去一年来皮革皮毛工人工会国际的工作，着重讨论如何加强全世界皮革工人的团结及保卫世界和平等问题。按戴国文系沈阳第一装具厂厂长，去年六月二十日在世界工联皮革皮毛工人工会国际成立大会上被选为行政委员。
