Title: US and France Eager to Transform Thailand into a Base for Aggression Against Vietnam; Thai Government Has Agreed to Allow French Troops Passage for Military Transport
Author:
Date: 1950-09-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, 30th: Reports from Thai newspapers indicate that American and French imperialists are transforming Thailand into a base for intensifying their war of aggression against the Vietnamese people.

	The *Hekong Saen Daily*, which has ties to the government, reported that the French government intends to transport troops through Thailand to Indochina. The newspaper claimed that the French embassy had requested the Thai Ministry of Foreign Affairs to allow French aircraft en route to Indochina to fly over Thai airspace.

	The *Thai Pula Shi Daily* stated that the Thai government had given a positive response to the French note.

### 美法亟谋变泰为侵越基地  泰政府已应允法军假道运兵

1950-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　美法亟谋变泰为侵越基地
    泰政府已应允法军假道运兵
    【新华社三十日讯】塔斯社德里讯：泰国报纸的消息显示，美国和法国帝国主义者，正在把泰国变成加紧反对越南人民的侵略战争基地。
    与政府方面有关系的《赫康萨恩报》报道：法国政府打算经过泰国运军队到印度支那·该报声称：法国大使馆已要求泰国外交部准许前往印度支那的法机飞过泰国领空。
    《泰普拉施报》声称，泰国政府对法国的照会已给予肯定的回答。
