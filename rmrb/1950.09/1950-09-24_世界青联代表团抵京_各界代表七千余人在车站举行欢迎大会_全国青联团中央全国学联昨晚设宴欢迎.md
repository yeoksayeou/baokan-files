Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Over 7,000 Representatives from All Circles Hold Welcome Rally at the Train Station; All-China Youth Federation, Communist Youth League Central Committee, and All-China Students' Federation Host Welcome Banquet Last Night
Author:
Date: 1950-09-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: The World Federation of Democratic Youth (WFDY) delegation, after visiting major cities in Northeast China and Tianjin, arrived in Beijing, the capital of China, this morning.

Those who went to welcome them included Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, Vice-Chairmen Qian Junrui, Sha Qianli, and Qian Sanqiang, Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, Vice-Secretary Jiang Nanxiang, Committee Member Chen Jiakang, Xie Bangding, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, Xu Zhizhen, Secretary-General of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Xu Guangping, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Zhao Shuli, Standing Committee Member of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Workers, Xiao San, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Ding Zan, Deputy Secretary-General of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace, Wu Maosun of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Central Military Commission of the People's Revolutionary Military Council, Yang Bozhen and Zhang Dazhong, Deputy Secretaries of the Beijing Municipal Working Committee of the Youth League, Li Bozhao, Vice-Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Literary and Art Workers, and more than 7,000 representatives from various agencies, organizations, schools, factories, and military units.

Combat heroes Tian Guangwen, Li Guangzheng, and Liu Zilin, who attended the National Congress of Combat Heroes, and labor models Zhao Guoyou, Liu Guilan, Tian Guiying, Liang Jun, Pan Changyou, Wang Weibin, Jin Yusheng, Yan Jinfang, and Liu Xingzhen, who attended the National Congress of Labor Models of Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers, also went to the station to welcome them.

Upon disembarking, the delegation was greeted with flowers from Young Pioneers, warm handshakes and cordial greetings from representatives from all walks of life, and welcoming applause and cheers from the general public.

The national flags of China and other countries shone brightly against each other at the station. The masses held aloft giant portraits of leaders of major people's democratic parties, headed by Stalin and Chairman Mao. The applause, slogans, and military music blended into one.

Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, delivered a welcome speech on behalf of all Chinese youth to the delegation. Delegation leader Bugar addressed in reply. Semychasny, one of the secretaries of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, paid tribute to the Chinese youth on behalf of the Soviet youth. He said that the Soviet youth regarded the Chinese youth as the best brothers with common aspirations in the joint struggle for peace and democracy. He emphasized that the two largest forces of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, the Soviet youth and the Chinese youth, would more resolutely dedicate all their strength to the cause of consolidating the camp of peace and democracy.

After a short rest at the guesthouse, the delegation held a press conference and in the evening attended a welcome banquet hosted by the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, the and the All-China Students' Federation and participated in a musical soiree.

We salute the youth of the capitalist countries who are fighting for peace and democracy!

Long live the World Federation of Democratic Youth!

Long live the solidarity of democratic youth throughout the world!

### 世界青联代表团抵京  各界代表七千余人在车站举行欢迎大会  全国青联团中央全国学联昨晚设宴欢迎

1950-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界青联代表团抵京
    各界代表七千余人在车站举行欢迎大会
    全国青联团中央全国学联昨晚设宴欢迎
    【新华社二十三日讯】世界民主青年联盟代表团，于访问了中国东北各大城市及天津后，今日上午到达中国首都北京。
    前往欢迎者有中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志，副主席钱俊瑞、沙千里、钱三强，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬，副书记蒋南翔，委员陈家康，中华全国学生联合会主席谢邦定，中华全国总工会秘书长许之桢，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平，中华全国文学艺术工作者联合会常委赵树理，中苏友好协会副总干事萧三，中国保卫世界和平大会委员会副秘书长丁瓒，中国人民外交协会吴茂荪，外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华，青年团北京市工作委员会副书记杨伯箴、张大中，北京市文艺工作者联合会副主席李伯钊及各机关、团体、学校、工厂、部队等代表七千余人。
    出席全国战斗英雄代表会议的战斗英雄田广文、李广正、刘子林及出席全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的劳动模范赵国有、刘桂兰、田桂英、梁军、潘长有、王维本、金玉声、阎金芳、刘行珍等也前赴车站欢迎。
    代表团下车后，即受到少年儿童队队员的献花、各界代表的热烈的握手与亲切的问候，以及广大市民群众的欢迎鼓掌与欢呼。
    车站上中国国旗与各国国旗互相辉映。与会群众高擎着以斯大林、毛主席为首的各国主要人民民主政党领袖的巨像。群众的掌声、口号声和军乐声响成一片。
    中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志，代表全中国青年向代表团致欢迎词。代表团团长布加拉致答词。苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记之一赛米恰斯尼代表苏联青年向中国青年致敬。他说：苏联青年把中国青年看作是争取和平与民主的共同斗争中的志同道合的最好的弟兄。他着重地指出：世界民主青年联盟的两个最大队伍：苏联青年与中国青年，将更加坚决地把所有力量都贡献给巩固和平与民主阵营的事业。
    代表团回到招待所稍事休息后，即举行记者招待会；晚上出席了中华全国民主青年联合总会、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会和中华全国学生联合会所举行的欢迎宴会，并参加音乐晚会。
    为争取和平民主而斗争的各资本主义国家的青年们，我们向你们致敬！
    世界民主青年联盟万岁！
    全世界民主青年团结万岁！
