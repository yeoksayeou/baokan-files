Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation Arrives in Soviet Union, Briefly Stays in Moscow Before Coming to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1950-09-01
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 31st: According to TASS, the World Federation of Democratic Youth (WFDY) delegation arrived in Moscow by air from Czechoslovakia on the 30th. The delegation, led by WFDY General Secretary Bukkara, is visiting New China at the invitation of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth.

　　Representatives of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League (VLKSM), the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, the Moscow Committee of the VLKSM, and various Moscow youth organizations welcomed the delegation at the airport. Kozimazov, Chairman of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, and Sizov, Secretary of the Moscow Committee of the VLKSM, delivered welcoming speeches to the delegation. Bukkara delivered a reply on behalf of the WFDY delegation. After a short stay in Moscow, the delegation will proceed to New China.

### 世界民主青联代表团抵苏  在莫斯科小事勾留即来我国

1950-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界民主青联代表团抵苏
    在莫斯科小事勾留即来我国
    【新华社三十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：世界民主青年联盟代表团于三十日由捷克斯洛伐克飞抵莫斯科。代表团系应中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会及中华全国民主青年联合总会之邀，由世界民主青年联盟总书记布加拉率领，前往新中国参观访问。
    苏联共产主义青年团中央委员会、苏联青年反法西斯委员会、苏联共产主义青年团莫斯科委员会及莫斯科各青年团体的代表均往机场欢迎。苏联青年反法西斯委员会主席科齐马索夫、和苏联共产主义青年团莫斯科委员会书记西佐夫，向代表团致欢迎辞。布加拉代表世界青联代表团致答辞。代表团在莫斯科小事勾留后，即转赴新中国。
