Title: Fiftieth Meeting of the Government Administration Council: Discussed and Approved Reports from the National Cooperative Workers' Representative Conference, Rubber Conference, and Match Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-09-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th: The 50th Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 15th. The meeting listened to a comprehensive report by Meng Yongqian, Director of the Central Cooperative Enterprise Administration, on the first National Cooperative Workers' Representative Conference, and a comprehensive report by Huang Yanpei, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council and Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Light Industry, and Vice Minister Wang Xinyuan, on the National Rubber Conference and the National Match Conference. After discussion, the meeting approved the above reports. In addition, the meeting passed several cases of appointments and removals and cases of appointments to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院第五十次会议  讨论并批准了全国合作社工作者代表会、橡胶及火柴会议的报告

1950-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院第五十次会议
    讨论并批准了全国合作社工作者代表会、橡胶及火柴会议的报告
    【新华社十五日讯】中央人民政府政务院第五十次政务会议于十五日下午举行。会议听取了中央合作事业管理局局长孟用潜所作的全国第一届合作社工作者代表会议的综合报告，以及政务院副总理兼中央人民政府轻工业部部长黄炎培、副部长王新元所作的全国橡胶会议和全国火柴会议的综合报告。会议经讨论后，批准了以上各项报告。此外，会议通过任免及通过提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
