Title: Heroes Gather in Beijing
Author: Lu Gong
Date: 1950-09-27
Page: 3

　　Heroes Gather in Beijing
    Lu Gong
    The people's country holds a heroes' meeting,
    Heroes gather in Beijing;
    Too many heroes to count,
    Heroes are like stars in the sky.
　
    Stars follow the sun,
    Heroes follow Mao Zedong;
    The sun shines brightly, the stars are bright,
    Heroes emerge under Mao Zedong's banner!
　
    Heroes' names shine brightly,
    Heroes' contributions are endless;
    Heroes' faces are full of smiles,
    Wanting to see the leader Mao Zedong.
　
    Hundreds of millions of people raise their heads,
    Their eyes looking towards Beijing;
    Wholeheartedly hoping for the heroes:
    "Learn your skills and teach us……."

### 英雄会师北京城
路工
1950-09-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　英雄会师北京城
    路工
    人民国家召开英雄会，
    英雄会师北京城；
    数数英雄数不清，
    英雄好比满天星。
　
    星星跟着太阳行，
    英雄跟着毛泽东；
    太阳发光星星明，
    毛泽东旗下出英雄！
　
    英雄名字闪闪明，
    英雄功劳说不尽；
    英雄满脸露笑容，
    要见领袖毛泽东。
　
    亿万人民抬着头，
    眼睛了望北京城；
    一心盼着英雄们：
    “学来本领教咱们……。”
