Title: Bulgarian Ambassador to China Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-09-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: The Ambassador of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China, Peterko and Counselor Tsar Vranov arrived in Beijing by plane at 4:30 pm on the 27th. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office; Wu Xiuquan, Director of the Soviet and European Affairs Department; Xu Yixin, Deputy Director; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; Wang Changjiang, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command; Luo Shen, Soviet Ambassador to China; Weisskopf, Czechoslovak Ambassador to China; Li Zhouyuan, Korean Ambassador to China; Counselor Cui Ying; Rudenko, Romanian Ambassador to China; Koenig, Head of the German Diplomatic Mission to China; Counselor Gruttner; Jalkhasaihan, Mongolian Ambassador to China; Xiafaranko, Hungarian Ambassador to China; Mook, Danish Minister to China; Steiner, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Swiss Legation in China; Gosto, Commercial Counselor of the Polish Embassy in China; Kolos, First Secretary of the Swedish Embassy in China; Kumar, Third Secretary of the Indian Embassy in China; and U Zaw Win, Third Secretary of the Burmese Embassy in China.

### 保加利亚驻华大使抵京

1950-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　保加利亚驻华大使抵京
    【新华社二十七日讯】保加利亚人民共和国驻华大使彼得科夫及参赞察尔伏拉诺夫于二十七日下午四时半乘机抵京。赴机场欢迎者有我外交部副部长章汉夫，办公厅副主任阎宝航，苏欧司司长伍修权，副司长徐以新，交际处处长王倬如，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，京津卫戍司令部副参谋长王长江，苏联驻华大使罗申，捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使魏斯柯普夫，朝鲜驻华大使李周渊，参赞崔英，罗马尼亚驻华大使鲁登科，德意志驻华外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希，参赞葛鲁特纳，蒙古驻华大使贾尔卡赛汗，匈牙利驻华大使夏法朗柯，丹麦驻华公使穆克，瑞士驻华公使馆临时代办施禔纳，波兰驻华大使馆商务专员顾士多，瑞典驻华大使馆一等秘书高罗士，印度驻华大使馆三等秘书古玛尔，缅甸驻华大使馆三等秘书吴藻文等。
