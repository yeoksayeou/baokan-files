Title: On the Front Lines of Defending Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-09-15
Page: 4
　　Xinhua News Agency, September 11th: On the front lines of defending peace.
    As of September 8th, a total of 340,424 people in the Netherlands had signed the Peace Declaration. Many Dutch soldiers also signed the Peace Declaration, demanding a ban on atomic weapons and an end to American aggression in Korea.
    The Peace Committee of the Free Territory of Trieste recently announced that 85,448 people in the port of Trieste and its surrounding villages have signed the Peace Declaration to date, with 8,000 signatures collected in the last two weeks. The committee pledged to collect 200,000 signatures before the opening of the Second World Congress of Supporters of Peace.
    The Central Council of the Association of Victims of German Nazism has issued a declaration to all German citizens, calling on them to participate in the Conference for the Defense of Peace and Against the Revival of West German Fascism to be held on September 10th. The declaration states: All those who support war are enemies of our people and all mankind. Engaging in war propaganda is a serious crime.
    The Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace recently continued to receive Peace Declaration signatures from Franco's Spain. Catalonia alone sent dozens of letters. Signatories include a Catalan priest and two scientists from Madrid.
    In the Yalta district of Finnmark Province, Norway, 90% of the residents have signed the Peace Declaration. Arendal has established a peace committee to begin collecting signatures. Prominent writer Berstad and famous actor Grepp have signed the Peace Declaration.
    In view of some Norwegian writers expressing opposition to the Peace Declaration, the great Danish proletarian writer Martin Andersen Nexø recently sent a telegram to the Norwegian Writers' Association, refusing to continue as an honorary member of the association.

### 在保卫和平的阵线上

1950-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　在保卫和平的阵线上
    【新华社十一日讯】在保卫和平的阵线上。
    荷兰到九月八日为止，总计已有三十四万零四百二十四人在和平宣言上签了名。许多荷兰士兵也在和平宣言上签了名，他们要求禁止原子武器和制止美国侵略朝鲜。
    的里雅斯特自由区和平委员会最近宣称：的港及其附近村落迄今已有八万五千四百四十八人在和平宣言上签了名，其中有八千人的签名是在最近两周内征集的。该委员会保证要在第二届世界拥护和平大会开幕前征集二十万人的签名。
    德国纳粹主义受害者协会中央理事会，顷向全德国公民发表宣言，号召他们参加将于九月十日举行的保卫和平与反对西德法西斯主义复活大会。宣言指出：一切支持战争的人都是我们人民及全体人类的公敌。从事战争的宣传乃是严重的罪行。
    世界拥护和平大会常设委员会最近继续收到来自佛朗哥西班牙的和平宣言签名信。仅加泰隆尼亚一地就达数十封。签名者包括一个加泰隆尼亚的教士，两个马德里的科学家。
    挪威的芬玛克省亚尔塔区，已有百分之九十的居民在和平宣言上签了名。阿伦达尔已成立了和平委员会开始征集签名。著名作家柏斯塔德及著名演员格雷普等都在和平宣言上签了名。
    丹麦伟大的无产阶级作家马丁·安德森·涅克索，鉴于一些挪威作家表示反对和平宣言，已于最近致电挪威作家协会，拒绝继续作该协会的名誉会员。
