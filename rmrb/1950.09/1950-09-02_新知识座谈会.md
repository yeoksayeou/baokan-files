Title: New Knowledge Forum
Author: Zhen
Date: 1950-09-02
Page: 8

　　New Knowledge Forum
    The Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association will hold its 85th New Knowledge Forum at the Xiehe Auditorium at 7:30 PM on September 2nd. Mr. Zhang Naiqi, Member of the Government Administration Council, is invited to report on "The Korean People's War Against Aggression." Mr. Zhang has just returned from Korea, witnessing the heroic struggle of the Korean people and the heinous crimes of American imperialist planes bombing peaceful Korean residents. Anyone wishing to attend can obtain an admission ticket in advance through introductions from their organizations, groups, or member associates at No. 24 Wuliang Daren Hutong.
   (Zhen)

### 新知识座谈会
桢
1950-09-02
第8版()
专栏：

　　新知识座谈会
    民主建国会北京市分会九月二日下午七点半，在协和礼堂举行第八十五次新知识座谈会，敦请政务院政务委员章乃器先生报告“朝鲜反侵略的战争”，章先生是刚从朝鲜回来的，目睹了朝鲜人民英勇斗争的事实和美帝飞机轰炸朝鲜和平居民的罪恶暴行。凡愿听讲的，可事前由机关、团体或会员会友介绍，到无量大人胡同二十四号领取入场券。   
   （桢）
