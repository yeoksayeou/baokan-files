Title: Korean People's Army Heroically Defends Seoul; Invading American Troops Suffer Over 4,000 Casualties

Author:

Date: 1950-09-28
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 27th: TASS, Pyongyang: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the morning of the 26th that the American invading forces, fiercely attacking Seoul, mobilized all their troops in an attempt to break through the defensive lines of the People's Army. The People's Army launched a powerful counterattack, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy.

　　The People's Army troops fighting in the Seoul area are engaged in hand-to-hand combat with the American troops striving to break into the city center. In several days of fighting in the Seoul area, the People's Army troops annihilated and wounded over 2,500 enemy officers and soldiers and destroyed a large number of enemy weapons.

　　Residents in and around Seoul have risen up to defend Seoul with unparalleled patriotism and selflessness.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 27th: TASS, Pyongyang: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the evening of the 26th that the People's Army troops were engaged in fierce fighting with the American troops on all fronts, inflicting heavy blows on the enemy.

　　On the 25th, the American Marines attempted to rush into Seoul from several directions, and the People's Army troops are resolutely resisting. In these battles, the People's Army troops killed and wounded over 1,500 American officers and soldiers. A large amount of enemy equipment was destroyed, including five tanks and more than ten artillery pieces of various calibers.

　　Along the Nakdong River line, the People's Army troops are fighting fiercely, repelling the enemy's counter-offensive.

### 朝鲜人民军英勇保卫汉城  进犯美军已被歼四千余人

1950-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军英勇保卫汉城  进犯美军已被歼四千余人
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十六日晨发表战报称：猛攻汉城的美国侵略军动员了他们所有的军队，企图突破人民军部队的防线。人民军部队发动有力的反击，使敌军遭受了重大损失。
    在汉城地区作战的人民军部队与竭力突入市中心的美军正在进行肉搏战。在汉城地区的数天战斗中，人民军部队歼灭和击伤敌军官兵二千五百余人，并击毁敌军武器甚多。
    汉城市内与郊区的居民已以无比的爱国主义与忘我的精神起而保卫汉城。
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十六日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线与美军进行激战，予敌重创。
    二十五日，美海军陆战队企图自数方面冲入汉城，人民军部队正坚决抵抗中。在这些战斗中，人民军部队毙、伤美军官兵一千五百余人。敌军装备大量被毁，其中包括坦克五辆、各种口径炮十余门。
    在洛东江一线，人民军部队迎击敌军的反攻，正在进行激战。
