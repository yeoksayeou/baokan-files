Title: List of Customs Superintendents and Deputy Superintendents of Various Customs (Offices) under the General Administration of Customs Approved by the Government Administration Council

Author:
Date: 1950-09-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th: The Government Administration Council recently approved the list of Customs Superintendents and Deputy Superintendents of various Customs (Offices) under the General Administration of Customs of the Central People's Government.

　　Manzhouli Customs:
        Superintendent: Yu Houxuan, formerly Director of Yantai Customs.
        Deputy Superintendent: Ding Rongsheng, formerly Deputy Superintendent of Manzhouli Customs.

　　Suifenhe Customs:
        Superintendent: Zhang Xia, formerly Superintendent of Suifenhe Customs.
        Deputy Superintendent: Chen Jiaqi, formerly Deputy Superintendent of Suifenhe Customs.

　　Tumen Customs:
        Superintendent: Lian Shili, formerly Acting Superintendent of Tumen Customs.
        Deputy Superintendent: Liu Baozhu, formerly Deputy Superintendent of Tumen Customs.

　　Jian Customs:
        Superintendent: Hou Bingxin, formerly Superintendent of Jian Customs.

　　Andong Customs:
        Superintendent: Xu Maosong, formerly Deputy Superintendent of Yingkou Customs.
        Deputy Superintendent: Zhang Dexian, formerly Director of the Secretariat of Andong Customs.

　　Yingkou Customs:
        Superintendent: Lin Zhanxian, formerly Superintendent of Wafangdian Customs.

　　Qingdao Customs:
        Superintendent: Bi Kejing, formerly Chief of the Customs Section of the Foreign Trade Bureau of the Shandong Provincial People's Government's Department of Industry and Commerce.
        Deputy Superintendent: Zhang Xueli, formerly Acting Deputy Superintendent of Qingdao Customs.
                 Shang Zhiyi, formerly Acting Deputy Superintendent of Qingdao Customs.

　　Fuzhou Customs:
        Superintendent: Zhang Chao, current Takeover Commissioner of Fuzhou Customs.
        Deputy Superintendent: Lin Shuyong, formerly Acting Deputy Superintendent of Fuzhou Customs.

　　Xiamen Customs:
        Superintendent: Wu Kejing, current Deputy Military Representative of Xiamen Customs.

　　Wuhan Customs:
        Superintendent: Chen Ce, formerly Superintendent of Hankou Customs.
        Deputy Superintendent: Cui Ren, formerly Deputy Superintendent of Hankou Customs.

　　Shantou Customs:
        Superintendent: Zhu Weisong, formerly Acting Superintendent of Shantou Customs.

　　Guangzhou Customs:
        Superintendent: Chen Yingzhong, current Deputy Director of the Customs Division of the South China Financial and Economic Committee of the Central South Military and Political Committee.
        Deputy Superintendent: Sun Chuanlu, formerly Acting Superintendent of Guangzhou Customs.
                 Lü Shaoxi, formerly Deputy Tax Commissioner of Guangzhou Customs.

　　Kowloon Customs:
        Superintendent: Zhuang Zhongren, formerly Acting Superintendent of Kowloon Customs.
        Deputy Superintendent: Lai Tian, formerly Acting Deputy Superintendent of Kowloon Customs.
                 Zhang Zhongwei, formerly Acting Deputy Superintendent of Kowloon Customs.

　　Zhanjiang Customs:
        Superintendent: Zhong Wenhua, formerly Acting Superintendent of Zhanjiang Customs.

　　Dihua Customs:
        Superintendent: Zhu Zhaoshen, formerly Acting Superintendent of Dihua Customs.

### 政务院批准任命海关总署各海关（关）关长、副关长名单
