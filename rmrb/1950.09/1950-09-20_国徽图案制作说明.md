Title: National Emblem Pattern Production Instructions
Author:
Date: 1950-09-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th: Instructions for the Production of the National Emblem Pattern of the People's Republic of China

　　1. Two ears of wheat and rice form a perfect circular ring. A gear is placed at the intersection of the wheat and rice stalks at the bottom. The center of the gear is tied with a red ribbon, which is knotted to the left and right, holding the wheat and rice and hanging down. The gear is divided into upper and lower parts.

　　2. Draw a vertical straight line from the center of the pattern; its left and right parts are completely symmetrical.

　　3. The position and size of each part of the pattern can be enlarged or reduced according to the proportion of the grid ink line diagram.

　　4. If making a relief sculpture, the height of each part can be enlarged or reduced according to the proportion of the cross-sectional view.

　　5. The colors of the national emblem are gold and red: the wheat and rice, five stars, Tiananmen, and gear are gold, and the background inside the circular ring and the hanging ribbons are red; the red is true red (the same as the national flag), and the gold is bright red gold (light-colored and lustrous gold).

### 国徽图案制作说明

1950-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　国徽图案制作说明
    【新华社二十日讯】中华人民共和国国徽图案制作说明
    １、两把麦稻组成正圆形的环。齿轮安在下方麦稻秆的交叉点上。齿轮的中心交结着红绶向左右绾住麦稻而下垂。把齿轮分成上下两部。
    ２、从图案正中垂直画一直线，其左右两部分，完全对称。
    ３、图案各部分之地位、尺寸，可根据方格墨线图之比例，放大或缩小。
    ４、如制作浮雕，其各部位之高低，可根据断面图之比例放大或缩小。
    ５、国徽之涂色为金红二色：麦稻、五星、天安门、齿轮为金色，圆环内之底子及垂绶为红色；红为正红（同于国旗），金为大赤金（淡色而有光泽之金）。
