Title: Our Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, Tan Xilin, Arrives in Soviet Capital

Author:
Date: 1950-09-02
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th—General Tan Xilin, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of our country to the Czechoslovak Republic, arrived in Moscow on the 28th and will soon proceed to Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia, to assume his post.

### 我驻捷大使谭希林抵苏京

1950-09-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　我驻捷大使谭希林抵苏京
    【新华社莫斯科三十日电】我国驻捷克斯洛伐克共和国首任特命全权大使谭希林将军，已于二十八日抵莫斯科，即将转赴捷京布拉格莅任。
