Title: Beijing Preparatory Committee for National Day Celebrations Established; Grand Military Parade to be Held on National Day; Guizhou Minority Representatives Depart for Beijing to Participate in National Day

Author:
Date: 1950-09-10
Page: 1

　　Beijing Preparatory Committee for National Day Celebrations Established
    Grand Military Parade to be Held on National Day
    Guizhou Minority Representatives Depart for Beijing to Participate in National Day
    [Xinhua News Agency, 9th] Various organizations, democratic parties, people's organizations, and people from all walks of life in Beijing are enthusiastically preparing to celebrate National Day. Representatives from the Central People's Government and various Beijing organizations, democratic parties, and people's organizations, totaling fifty-one people, held a preparatory meeting for celebrating the National Day of the People's Republic of China at 9:00 AM on the 9th in the office of the Beijing People's Representative Consultative Committee in Zhongshan Park. The meeting decided to hold a grand military parade and celebratory procession in Tiananmen Square on National Day, and elected Peng Zhen, Nie Rongzhen, Li Lisan, Li Dequan, Feng Wenbin, Luo Ruiqing, Hu Qiaomu, Xiao Hua, Liu Ren, Yu Xinqing, Tang Yanjie, Yang Chengwu, Zhang Zhixiang, Xue Zizheng and fourteen others as preparatory committee members, forming the Beijing Preparatory Committee for Celebrating the National Day of the People's Republic of China, responsible for leading and carrying out the various preparatory work for celebrating National Day.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Guiyang, 9th] A delegation of twenty-four representatives and artists from the Miao, Yi, and Hui ethnic minorities in Guizhou Province set out from Guiyang on the 6th to participate in National Day celebrations in Beijing. The ethnic representatives are carrying brocade banners to be presented to the National Day celebration and ceremonial attire for Chairman Mao. Additionally, there are more than 150 kinds of Miao and Yi clothing, embroidery, musical instruments, and photographs that will be exhibited in the capital. The performing arts troupe plans to perform Miao and Yi songs and dances in Beijing in order to exchange culture and learn from each other with fraternal ethnic groups.

### 北京庆祝国庆筹委会组成  国庆日将举行盛大阅兵典礼  贵州少数民族代表启程来京参加国庆节

1950-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京庆祝国庆筹委会组成
    国庆日将举行盛大阅兵典礼
    贵州少数民族代表启程来京参加国庆节
    【新华社九日讯】北京各机关、各民主党派、各人民团体和各界人民热烈筹备庆祝国庆节。中央人民政府和北京市各机关、各民主党派、各人民团体代表五十一人于九日上午九时在中山公园内北京市各界人民代表协商委员会办公厅举行庆祝中华人民共和国国庆节筹备会。会议决定于国庆日在天安门前广场举行盛大的阅兵典礼和庆祝游行，并推选彭真、聂荣臻、李立三、李德全、冯文彬、罗瑞卿、胡乔木、萧华、刘仁、余心清、唐延杰、杨成武、张致祥、薛子正等十四人为筹备委员，组成北京庆祝中华人民共和国国庆节筹备委员会，负责领导进行各项庆祝国庆节的筹备工作。
    【新华社贵阳九日电】贵州省苗族、彝族、回族各少数民族的代表暨文艺工作队一行二十四人，于六日自贵阳启程来京参加国庆节。各族代表携有献给庆祝国庆日大会的锦旗和献给毛主席的礼服。另有苗族、彝族的衣服、刺绣、乐器及照片等一百五十余种，将在首都举行展览。文工队并拟在京演出苗族、彝族的歌舞，以与各兄弟民族交流文化，互相学习。
