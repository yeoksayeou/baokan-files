Title: Korean Youth Representative Jeon Jong-hwan Denounces U.S. Military Atrocities in Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-09-10
Page: 4

　　Korean Youth Representative Jeon Jong-hwan
    Denounces U.S. Military Atrocities in Korea
    [This newspaper reports] (Xinhua News Agency, September 9th): Jeon Jong-hwan, one of the Korean representatives of the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation (two other Korean representatives are yet to depart), stated at a reception for Chinese and foreign journalists in Beijing today that the U.S. invading army's brutal acts of slaughtering Korean people have only further aroused the determination of the vast Korean people to rise up and kill the enemy, and they are determined to drive the U.S. invaders out of Korea. In answering reporters' questions, Jeon Jong-hwan listed the facts of the U.S. invaders' inhuman atrocities, such as killing Korean people, indiscriminately bombing civilian houses, hospitals, and schools, and strafing women and children.

### 朝鲜青年代表全宗焕  斥美军在朝鲜暴行

1950-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜青年代表全宗焕
    斥美军在朝鲜暴行
    【本报讯】据新华社九日讯：世界民主青年联盟代表团朝鲜代表之一全宗焕（朝鲜还有两位代表尚待启程）今日在北京招待中外记者会上力称：美国侵略军队屠杀朝鲜人民的残暴行为，只是更加激起了朝鲜广大人民奋起杀敌的决心，他们誓将美国侵略者赶出朝鲜去。全宗焕在答复记者的询问时列举了美国侵略者杀害朝鲜人民，滥炸民房、医院、学校，扫射妇女儿童等灭绝人性暴行的事实。
