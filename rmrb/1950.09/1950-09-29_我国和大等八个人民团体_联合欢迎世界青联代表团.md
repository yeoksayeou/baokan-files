Title: Our Country's Great Alliance and Eight Other People's Organizations Jointly Welcome the World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1950-09-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 27th: The China Peace Committee, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, the Joint Office of China's Social Science Research Societies, and the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs held a tea party tonight to welcome the World Federation of Democratic Youth (WFDY) delegation. The WFDY delegation was accompanied by Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth. Guo Moruo, Cai Chang, Xu Zhizhen, Shen Yanbing, Kang Keqing, Xiao San, Hu Yuzhi, Zhao Shuli, Shao Lizi, Luo Longji, Zeng Zhaolun, and Zhang Zhirang were all in attendance.

At the meeting, Chairman Guo Moruo delivered a welcoming speech, saying: The Chinese people, like people all over the world, have always loved peace. Today, as American imperialism provokes war and invades Korea and Chinese territory, the Chinese people are even more determined to defend their motherland and world peace.

Subsequently, Shen Yanbing and Cai Chang respectively reported on the progress of the Chinese peace signature campaign and the active participation of women in New China in national construction and the defense of world peace.

French representative Kébir, representing the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation, delivered a reply. He emphasized that the victory of the Chinese people has greatly strengthened the confidence of oppressed peoples around the world in their struggle for national independence. He stated that the representatives would, upon returning to their countries, inform the people of their respective nations of the great contributions made by the Chinese people and youth in striving for peace, and called on democratic youth of all countries to further strengthen their unity and oppose aggressive war. At the meeting, representatives from Republican Spain and Finland presented gifts to the All-China Women's Federation. The China Peace Committee, the All-China Women's Federation, and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association also presented gifts such as photo collections of the peace signature campaign and commemorative badges from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to the representatives. ### 我国和大等八个人民团体  联合欢迎世界青联代表团

1950-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国和大等八个人民团体
    联合欢迎世界青联代表团
    【新华社二十七日讯】中国保卫世界和平大会委员会、中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中苏友好协会总会、中华全国文学艺术界联合会、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、中国社会科学各研究会联合办事处、中国人民外交学会等八个人民团体联合于今晚举行欢迎世界民主青年联盟代表团茶会。世界民主青年联盟代表团由中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志陪同出席。郭沫若、蔡畅、许之桢、沈雁冰、康克清、萧三、胡愈之、赵树理、邵力子、罗隆基、曾昭抡、张志让等均出席。
    会上首由主席郭沫若致欢迎词，他说：中国人民和全世界人民一样，向来爱好和平，在美国帝国主义挑动战争、侵略朝鲜及中国领土的今天，中国人民更加坚决保卫祖国，保卫世界和平。
    随后由沈雁冰、蔡畅分别报告中国和平签名运动开展情况及新中国妇女积极参加祖国建设与保卫世界和平的情况。
    世界青联代表团法国代表基培尔代表代表团致答辞。他着重指出：中国人民的胜利大大加强了全世界各被压迫民族为民族独立而斗争的信心。他表示代表们在回国后一定向各国人民宣传中国人民和青年对于争取和平所作的伟大贡献，并号召各国民主青年进一步加强团结，反对侵略战争。会上，共和西班牙代表与芬兰代表向中国妇联献礼。中国保卫世界和平大会委员会，中国妇联和中苏友好协会等团体也以和平签名运动相片集、中苏友协纪念章等礼物分赠各代表。（附图片）
