Title: MacArthur Instructs the Yoshida Government to Illegally Shut Down "The Overseas Chinese People's Daily"
Author:
Date: 1950-09-30
Page: 4

　　MacArthur Instructs the Yoshida Government
    Illegally Shut Down "The Overseas Chinese People's Daily"
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 29th dispatch] According to the International News Service in Tokyo: Under the instruction of MacArthur, the reactionary Yoshida government of Japan, through its Ministry of Justice, unreasonably shut down "The Overseas Chinese People's Daily" (a weekly newspaper, the organ of the Democratic Promotion Association of Overseas Chinese in Japan), which is published in Tokyo by our overseas Chinese compatriots. Japanese police also illegally searched the editorial department and printing factory of "The Overseas Chinese People's Daily," and sealed off the newspaper's machinery and office equipment. The International News Service revealed that the measure to shut down "The Overseas Chinese People's Daily" "was approved by MacArthur's headquarters."
    Another dispatch: The Special Investigation Bureau of the Yoshida government's Ministry of Justice ordered the "Democratic Youth News," published by the Japan Youth Fatherland Front, to cease publication on September 13.

### 麦克阿瑟指使吉田政府  非法封闭《华侨民报》

1950-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　麦克阿瑟指使吉田政府
    非法封闭《华侨民报》
    【新华社二十九日讯】据国际新闻社东京讯：在麦克阿瑟的指使下，日本吉田反动政府法务府于二十八日无理封闭我国侨胞在东京出版的《华侨民报》（周刊，旅日华侨民主促进会机关报）。日本警察还非法搜查《华侨民报》的编辑部及印刷厂，并查封该报的机器与办公用具。国际新闻社透露，封闭华侨民报的措施，“是经麦克阿瑟总部批准的”。
    又讯：吉田政府法务府特别审查局于九月十三日勒令日本青年祖国战线出版的《民主青年新闻》停刊。
