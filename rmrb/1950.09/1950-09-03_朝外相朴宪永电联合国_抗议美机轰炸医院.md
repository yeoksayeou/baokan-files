Title: 朝 Foreign Minister Park Hun-young Sends Telegram to the United Nations Protesting the US Bombing of a Hospital
Author:
Date: 1950-09-03
Page: 4

　　朝鲜 Foreign Minister Park Hun-young Sends Telegram to the United Nations
    Protesting the US Bombing of a Hospital
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 2nd] TASS, Pyongyang, 1st: Park Hun-young, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, telegraphed Malik, the rotating chairman of the Security Council for August, and UN Secretary-General Trygve Lie on August 29, informing them that on August 29, multiple US aircraft bombed a Korean hospital 25 kilometers from Pyongyang. The planes dropped six bombs, one of which hit the hospital. Three people were killed, one building was destroyed, and other buildings in the hospital were severely damaged. Red Cross markings were clearly visible on the hospital roof and near the hospital buildings. It is thus clear that these bombings of the hospital were completely intentional. On behalf of the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Park Hun-young lodged a serious protest against this new crime committed by the US interventionists.

### 朝外相朴宪永电联合国  抗议美机轰炸医院

1950-09-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝外相朴宪永电联合国
    抗议美机轰炸医院
    【新华社二日讯】塔斯社平壤一日电：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相朴宪永八月二十九日致电安理会八月份轮值的主席马立克和联合国秘书长赖伊，通知他们说：八月二十九日，美国飞机多架轰炸了离平壤二十五公里的一所朝鲜医院。飞机投弹六枚，其中一枚炸中该医院。有三人被炸死，房屋一幢炸毁，医院其他房屋受到重大的毁坏。红十字标记在医院屋顶与医院房屋附近均清晰可见。由此可见这些轰炸医院是完全故意的。朴宪永代表朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府对美国干涉者这次新的罪行提出严重抗议。
