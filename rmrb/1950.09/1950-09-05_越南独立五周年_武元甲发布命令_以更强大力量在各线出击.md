Title: Fifth Anniversary of Vietnamese Independence: Vo Nguyen Giap Issues Order to Attack on All Fronts with Greater Strength
Author:
Date: 1950-09-05
Page: 4

　　Vietnam Independence Five Anniversary
    Vo Nguyen Giap Issues Order
    Attack on All Fronts with Greater Strength

    [Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 4th] Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam dispatch: General Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnamese People's Liberation Army, issued an order to the officers and soldiers of the Vietnamese People's Liberation Army on September 2nd, the fifth anniversary of Vietnamese Independence, declaring: In the near future, there will be more significant and fiercer battles on all fronts in Vietnam. The Vietnamese People's Liberation Army should be prepared militarily and ideologically to win major battles in the autumn and winter of this year.

    General Vo Nguyen Giap then ordered the officers and soldiers of the Vietnamese People's Liberation Army and local forces to attack on all fronts with greater strength, to crush the enemy's autumn and winter offensives; to develop guerrilla warfare, especially in the enemy's rear; to protect the lives of the people, and to defeat the enemy's attempts to destroy crops; to carefully study military techniques and tactics, and to universally launch criticism and self-criticism among personnel at all levels.

### 越南独立五周年  武元甲发布命令  以更强大力量在各线出击

1950-09-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南独立五周年
    武元甲发布命令
    以更强大力量在各线出击
    【新华社四日讯】越南通讯社北越讯：越南人民解放军总司令武元甲将军，于二日越南独立五周年纪念日，向越南人民解放军指战员发布命令宣称：在不久的将来，越南各战线上将有更重大的、更激烈的战事。越南人民解放军应在军事上与思想上作好准备，在今年秋冬期内赢得重大的战役。
    武元甲将军继即命令越南人民解放军与地方军的指战员们，以更强大的力量在各战线上出击，粉碎敌军的秋季、冬季攻势；发展游击战争，特别是在敌人后方；保护人民的生命，击溃敌人破坏农作物的企图；仔细研究军事技术与战术，在各级人员中普遍展开批评与自我批评。
