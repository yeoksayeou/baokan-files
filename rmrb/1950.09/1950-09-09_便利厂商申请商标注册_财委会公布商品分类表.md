To Facilitate Trademark Registration for Manufacturers
Finance Committee Announces Commodity Classification Table

[This newspaper report] After the Government Administration Council's Finance and Economics Committee of the Central People's Government promulgated the Provisional Regulations for Trademark Registration and various measures, the Central Private Enterprise Bureau has already begun accepting trademark registrations. To facilitate applications from various manufacturers, the Finance and Economics Committee of the Government Administration Council has specifically announced the Commodity Classification Table. As for trademark printing plates that need to be produced on behalf of the Central Private Enterprise Bureau, the temporary fee is 25,000 yuan per plate, which will be adjusted at any time in the future according to market prices.
