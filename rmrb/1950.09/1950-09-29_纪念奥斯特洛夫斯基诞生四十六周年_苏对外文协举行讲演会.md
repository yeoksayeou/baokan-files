Title: Commemorating the Forty-Sixth Anniversary of Ostrovsky's Birth: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Lecture

Author: Chen Yingnan
Date: 1950-09-29
Page: 12

Commemorating the Forty-Sixth Anniversary of Ostrovsky's Birth
	Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Lecture

　　Today (September 29th) is the forty-sixth anniversary of the birth of the famous Soviet writer, author of "How the Steel Was Tempered," Ostrovsky. The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association, to commemorate this outstanding and talented writer, are scheduled to hold a lecture at 7:30 this evening at No. 19 Nanheyan, with a lecture by Maksimova entitled: "The Life and Creation of Ostrovsky." All those holding admission tickets and long-term lecture passes for "Introduction to the Soviet Union" are welcome to attend at that time.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Chen Yingnan)

### 纪念奥斯特洛夫斯基诞生四十六周年  苏对外文协举行讲演会
陈应楠
1950-09-29
第12版()
专栏：

　　纪念奥斯特洛夫斯基诞生四十六周年
    苏对外文协举行讲演会
    今天（九月二十九日）是苏联名作家，《钢铁是怎样炼成的》作者奥斯特洛夫斯基诞生四十六周年纪念日。北京市中苏友好协会与苏联对外文化协会为纪念这位杰出的天才作家，定于今日下午七时半在南河沿十九号举行讲演会，请马克西姆娃讲：《奥斯特洛夫斯基的生活与创造》。凡持有入场券与《苏联介绍》长期听讲证者，届时均可前往听讲。
                （陈应楠）
