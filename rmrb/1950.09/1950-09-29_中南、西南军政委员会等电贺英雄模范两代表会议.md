Title: Central-South, Southwest Military and Political Committees Send Congratulatory Telegrams to Hero and Model Representatives Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-09-29
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency: The leading organs of the Central-South Administrative Region sent telegrams to the National Combat Heroes Representatives Conference and the National Worker-Peasant-Soldier Labor Model Representatives Conference, warmly congratulating the opening of the conference.
　　The congratulatory telegram from the Central-South Military and Political Committee stated: "Labor models are the vital force on the production front of the people's country. Under the inspiration of the great Chairman Mao, the working class will pool their wisdom and efforts to strive for a fundamental improvement in the country's financial and economic situation, and will surely achieve glorious victory." The congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party's Central-South Bureau expressed the hope that combat heroes and labor models will summarize valuable experiences at this conference, widely disseminate them, and in the future, implement and promote the spirit of patriotism and excellent qualities, study diligently, improve their work, and unite more working people and commanders and fighters to strive for the establishment of a prosperous and strong, independent New China.
　　In addition, the Jiangxi Provincial People's Government, the Jiangxi Provincial People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee, the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Ministry of Water Resources of the Central-South Military and Political Committee, and all commanders and fighters of the 15th Corps of the Guangdong Military Region also sent telegrams to the two conferences to express their congratulations.
　　Xinhua News Agency, 27th: The Southwest Military and Political Committee sent a telegram congratulating the opening of the National Combat Heroes Representatives Conference and the National Worker-Peasant-Soldier Labor Model Representatives Conference. The congratulatory telegram briefly stated: Under the correct leadership of Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, heroes and models have played and will continue to play a backbone leading role in the Chinese people's liberation cause. At present, American imperialism is frantically invading Korea and constantly making unreasonable provocations against the Chinese people. We must strengthen domestic construction in all aspects, especially the construction of national defense forces and economic construction, so as to more effectively consolidate the Chinese people's hard-won victory and consolidate lasting peace in the Far East and the world. The telegram concluded by wishing the conference success.

### 中南、西南军政委员会等电贺英雄模范两代表会议

1950-09-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　中南、西南军政委员会等电贺英雄模范两代表会议
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中南行政区各领导机关致电全国战斗英雄代表会议和全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议，热烈祝贺会议的开幕。
    中南军政委员会贺电称：“劳动模范是人民国家生产战线上的生力军，工人阶级在伟大的毛主席感召下，群策群力，为争取国家财政经济情况的基本好转，定能获得光辉的胜利。”中国共产党中共中南局贺电中表示希望战斗英雄和劳动模范们在本届大会中总结出宝贵的经验，广泛加以传播，并在今后贯彻发扬爱国主义精神与优秀品质，努力学习，提高工作，团结更多的劳动人民和指战员，为建立富强、独立的新中国而奋斗。
    此外，江西省人民政府、江西省各界人民代表会议协商委员会、中国共产党广州市委员会、中南军政委员会水利部、广东军区十五兵团全体指战员等均分别驰电两个大会致以祝贺。
    【新华社二十七日讯】西南军政委员会致电祝贺全国战斗英雄代表会议和全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议开幕。贺电略称：英雄模范们在毛主席和中国共产党的正确领导下，对中国人民解放事业曾经起了而且将继续起着骨干带头作用。目前美国帝国主义正在疯狂侵略朝鲜，并不断向中国人民进行无理挑衅，我们必须加强国内各种建设，特别是国防军建设和经济建设，以便更有效地巩固中国人民既得的胜利成果，巩固远东的和世界的持久和平。电文最后预祝大会的成功。
