Title: World Youth Delegation Visits the Forbidden City Yesterday, Praising the Great Creativity of the Chinese People
Author:
Date: 1950-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th—The World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation visited the Forbidden City today. The Forbidden City's magnificent and splendid architecture, exquisite and precious art, and craft works aroused great attention and strong interest from the delegates. They highly praised the outstanding wisdom and great creativity of the Chinese working people.

After visiting the Forbidden City, the delegates attended district lectures held by factories, troops, government agencies, and schools in Beijing. Today's lectures were held in twenty-one districts, with over 40,000 young people from more than seventy units participating. The General Secretary of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, Buga'ra, and the Chairman of the Central Committee of the North Korean Democratic Youth League, Hyun Jung-min, who arrived in Beijing today, went to Renmin University to give lectures. One of the Secretaries of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union, Semi Chastny, went to the Central Youth League School to give a lecture. The youth masses of each unit warmly welcomed the delegates' reports.

### 世青代表团昨游览故宫  赞扬我国人民伟大的创造力

1950-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　世青代表团昨游览故宫
    赞扬我国人民伟大的创造力
    【新华社二十八日讯】世界民主青年联盟代表团今日前往故宫游览。故宫雄伟瑰丽的建筑、精致珍贵的美术、工艺作品引起代表们极大的注意和浓厚的兴趣。他们对中国劳动人民卓越的智慧和伟大的创造力备致赞扬。
    代表们游览故宫后，分别出席北京市工厂、部队、机关、学校所举办的分区讲演会。今天的讲演会分二十一个地区举行，共有七十余单位的四万多青年参加。世界青联总书记布加拉和今日抵京的北朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会委员长玄正民赴人民大学讲演。苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记之一赛米恰斯尼赴中央团校讲演。各单位青年群众热烈欢迎代表们的报告。
