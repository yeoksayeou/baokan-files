Long Live the Solidarity of Democratic Youth of the World!

The World Federation of Democratic Youth (WFDY) delegation, led by WFDY Secretary-General Bugała, arrived at Manzhouli, our border city, at 2:00 AM on September 11th. The delegation was invited by the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, departing from Prague, Czechoslovakia, to attend our first National Day celebration and visit various locations.

The delegation is composed of 38 representatives from 30 countries, representing 70 million organized democratic youth worldwide. Upon the delegation's arrival, those who went to Manzhouli Station to welcome them included Wu Han, Secretary-General of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth; Qu Tangliang, Director of the International Liaison Department of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League; Zeng Yuanhui, Director of the Liaison Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government in Manzhouli; and over 200 representatives from the Manzhouli Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Municipal People's Government, the Municipal Committee of the Youth League, and various sectors and ethnic groups.

On the morning of September 12th, the WFDY delegation arrived in Qiqihar, Heilongjiang Province, where over 50,000 young people gathered to welcome them.

The delegation arrived in Harbin at 8:00 AM on the 13th on a special train driven by Wang Fengming, a renowned model driver of the Northeast Railway. Over 20,000 young people and citizens of Harbin went to the station to welcome them. After visiting Harbin, the delegation arrived in Shenyang on the 15th. Four youth representatives from the Mongolian People's Republic and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, who arrived in Shenyang from Beijing on the 14th, also joined the delegation here. At this point, the WFDY delegation consisted of 42 representatives from 32 countries. After disembarking, the delegation participated in a welcome rally of nearly 20,000 people in Zhongshan Square in the city center. (See attached photos)

### 全世界民主青年团结万岁！

1950-09-24
第8版()
专栏：

　　全世界民主青年团结万岁！
    以世界民主青年联盟总书记布加拉为首的世界民主青年联盟代表团于九月十一日上午二时抵达我国的边境城市满洲里。代表团是应中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会与中华全国民主青年联合总会的邀请，由捷京布拉格出发前来参加我国首届国庆节，并赴各地访问。
    代表团由三十个国家的三十八个代表组成，代表了世界各国七千万有组织的民主青年。代表团到达时，前往满洲里车站欢迎者有中华全国民主青年联合总会秘书长吴晗，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会国际联络部部长区棠亮，中央人民政府外交部驻满洲里联络处长曾远辉，及满洲里中共市委、市人民政府、青年团市工委及各界各民族代表等二百余人。
    九月十二日上午世界民主青年联盟代表团抵黑龙江省齐齐哈尔，齐齐哈尔五万余青年集会，欢迎世界民主青年联盟代表团。
    代表团乘坐东北铁路著名模范司机王凤鸣驾驶的专车于十三日上午八时抵哈尔滨。哈尔滨市青年和市民两万余人前往车站欢迎。参观哈尔滨市后，于十五日抵达沈阳，十四日由京抵沈的蒙古人民共和国与朝鲜民主主义人民共和国青年代表共四人亦在此会合。至此，世界民主青年联盟代表团共拥有三十二个国家的四十二位代表。代表团代表下车后，在市中心区中山广场，参加了近两万人的欢迎大会。（附图片）
