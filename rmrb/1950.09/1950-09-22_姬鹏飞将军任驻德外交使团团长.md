Title: General Ji Pengfei Appointed Head of Diplomatic Mission to Germany

Author: 
Date: 1950-09-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 21st—The Central People's Government has appointed General Ji Pengfei as the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary and head of the diplomatic mission to the German Democratic Republic.

### 姬鹏飞将军任驻德外交使团团长

1950-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　姬鹏飞将军任驻德外交使团团长
    【新华社二十一日讯】中央人民政府已任命姬鹏飞将军为我国驻德意志民主共和国首任特命全权大使衔外交使团团长。
