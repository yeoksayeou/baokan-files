Title: Northern Part of Korea to Have a Bountiful Harvest This Autumn
Author:
Date: 1950-09-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 17th: The northern part of Korea will have a bountiful harvest this autumn. The harvest of late-season crops will not only exceed last year's, but will also exceed this year's plan. Preliminary surveys have been completed in some areas. Survey results show that millet production will exceed the plan by 11.1 percent, yellow millet by 10.4 percent, sorghum by 7 percent, and corn by 20 percent. Surveys of rice, cotton, potatoes, and other harvests are underway.

### 朝鲜北半部今秋将丰收

1950-09-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜北半部今秋将丰收
    【新华社平壤十七日电】朝鲜北半部各地今秋将获丰收。晚期农作物的收获量不仅将超过去年，且将超过今年度的计划。秋收前的一部分调查工作已告完成。调查结果表明，谷子的产量将超过计划百分之十一点一，黄米将超过百分之十点四，高粱将超过百分之七，玉蜀黍将超过百分之二十。稻子、棉花、马铃薯等收获量的调查工作，正在进行中。
