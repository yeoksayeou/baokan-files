Title: The 52nd Meeting of the Government Administration Council Discusses and Approves the Provisional Regulations on the Registration of Social Organizations
Author:
Date: 1950-09-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 29th—The 52nd meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held at 3:30 PM on the 29th. After listening to the explanation of the Provisional Regulations on the Registration of Social Organizations made by Chen Qiyuan, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government, the meeting discussed and approved the regulations. The meeting also approved several cases of appointments and removals and submitted appointment proposals to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院五十二次会议  讨论通过社会团体登记暂行办法

1950-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院五十二次会议
    讨论通过社会团体登记暂行办法
    【新华社二十九日讯】中央人民政府政务院第五十二次政务会议于二十九日下午三时半举行。会议在听取了中央人民政府内务部陈其瑗副部长所作的关于社会团体登记暂行办法的说明后，讨论和通过了该办法。会议并通过任免和提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
