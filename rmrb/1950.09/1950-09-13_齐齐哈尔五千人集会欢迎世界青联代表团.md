Title: Qiqihar Rally of 5,000 People Welcomes World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1950-09-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Qiqihar, 12th. The World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation, accompanied by Wu Han, Secretary-General of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, Qu Tangliang, Director of the International Liaison Department of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, and Zhang Ze, Deputy Secretary of the Northeast Working Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, arrived in Qiqihar at 9:30 PM on September 11th. Representatives from various organizations in Heilongjiang Province and Qiqihar City, along with more than 5,000 workers, soldiers, students, and members of the public, held a grand welcome rally at the station. Yu Yifu, Chairman of the Heilongjiang Provincial People's Government, Vice-Chairmen Yang Yingjie and Wang Zimu, Zhao Dezun, Secretary of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Li Jianbai, Secretary of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Youth League, Wang Jinru, Mayor of Qiqihar City, and Zhang Pujia, Secretary of the Qiqihar Municipal Committee of the Youth League, all went to the station to welcome the delegation. Liang Jun, the famous female tractor driver, Xing Zhaokai, a representative attending the National Congress of Labor Models of Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers, and Guo Yulan, a Heilongjiang Provincial Committee member and female peasant labor model, also rushed to participate in the welcoming ceremony. Various organizations presented flowers to the delegation.

### 齐齐哈尔五千人集会欢迎世界青联代表团

1950-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　齐齐哈尔五千人集会欢迎世界青联代表团
    【新华社齐齐哈尔十二日电】世界民主青年联盟代表团，由中华全国民主青年联合总会秘书长吴晗、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会国际联络部部长区棠亮、中国新民主主义青年团东北工作委员会副书记章泽等陪同，于九月十一日下午九时半抵达齐齐哈尔。黑龙江省、齐齐哈尔市各机关团体的代表和工人、军队、学生、群众五千余人，在车站举行了盛大的欢迎会。黑龙江省人民政府主席于毅夫、副主席杨英杰、王梓木、中共黑龙江省委书记赵德尊、青年团黑龙江省团委书记李剑白、齐齐哈尔市人民政府市长王劲如、齐齐哈尔市青年团市委书记张蒲家等均赴车站欢迎。著名女拖拉机手梁军、出席全国工农兵劳模大会代表邢兆开、黑龙江省府委员妇女农民劳动模范郭玉兰等亦赶来参加欢迎会。各机关团体纷纷向代表团献花。
