Popular Science Lecture

The Popular Science Lecture, co-sponsored by the Youth Service Department and the Central People's Science Museum, will hold its third lecture in September, "Soybeans," at 7:00 p.m. on September 16th at the Labor People's Cultural Palace Stadium. Those who have received monthly tickets may attend the lecture on time. After the lecture, a film will be shown in conjunction with the topic.
