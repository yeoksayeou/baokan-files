Title: The Second Stage of Striving for Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-09-21
Page: 6

Column: "Peace Advocate" Editorial

　　The Second Stage of Striving for Peace
    The Enlarged Meeting of the Executive Bureau held in Prague released three documents:
    1. A notice convening the Second World Peace Congress;
    2. A resolution addressed to the United Nations Security Council and governments of all countries;
    3. Several resolutions concerning the preparatory work for the Congress and plans to expand the movement.
    Among these documents, the most important is the notice convening the Second World Peace Congress.
    This notice outlines the resolutions made in Prague and clarifies the policies and foundations of our movement. Therefore, the goal of our efforts will be to bring the movement to defend world peace to a new stage, which is the Second World Peace Congress to be held in the United Kingdom from November 13th to 19th.
    The Second World Peace Congress will not only be the culmination of the Stockholm signature campaign but also the beginning of a new stage. This new stage will, in addition to continuing to insist that atomic weapons are illegal, also extend to include all other matters related to peace.
    The Stockholm signature campaign has already gained the support of millions of men and women. This great movement remains focused on a specific goal: banning atomic weapons. This remains one of the themes of our movement. Because, if the movement is successful, it can open the door for negotiations and peace agreements between governments, and can remove the threat of mass slaughter currently facing humanity. But our movement is not limited to banning atomic weapons; its main purpose, as stated in the Congress declaration, is to seek true and lasting peace among all nations.
    The recent outbreak of new wars—potentially the center of world conflict; the frantic intensification of arms preparation; and the unprecedented war clamor indicate the serious deterioration of the international situation, and give us reason to be more concerned than ever before about the possible outbreak of another world war. At the same time, these circumstances also create new possibilities for the unity of peace-loving people, and give rise to the new urgent tasks mentioned in the Prague Declaration.
    The call of the World Peace Congress Executive Bureau calls for continued efforts in the Stockholm Declaration signature campaign, and also puts forward three other bases for action that can be used to gain wider support from all those who seek peace:
    1. Universal disarmament under international control;
    2. Prohibit any country from using any means to promote war;
    3. Expose aggression wherever it occurs, and condemn foreign armed intervention in the internal affairs of any country.
    This last point prompted the World Peace Congress Executive Bureau to support the already proposed peace initiative to stop the Korean War, and will send a suggestion to the Security Council and governments of all countries as the Executive Bureau's contribution to a peaceful resolution, which will be supported by the same resolutions of the world's peace movements.
    In summary, the Prague resolutions represent our basic policies. Around these resolutions, countries should organize open gatherings without delay, and discussions should be held around these resolutions before representatives to the Second World Peace Congress are elected.
    Therefore, it is very important that the call of the World Peace Congress Executive Bureau should be disseminated using all possible tools around the world, such as radio, newspapers, magazines, leaflets, posters, public gatherings, etc.
    The Prague call, and the discussions it triggers, will help us to again gain millions of signatures on the peace declaration, while opening up new possibilities for expanding the peace movement. This new expansion will certainly be achieved in the process of preparing for the Second World Peace Congress, which serves as a new step in the struggle for peace.

    Editor's Note: The editorial in the World Peace Congress Committee's official publication, "Peace Advocate," points out: The movement to defend world peace has entered a new stage. In order to stop the US imperialists' conspiracy to launch a new world war, those who have signed the Stockholm Declaration demanding the banning of atomic weapons must also take three other actions as a basis, namely, to demand (1) universal disarmament under international control; (2) prohibit any country from using any means to promote war; (3) expose aggression wherever it occurs, and condemn foreign armed intervention in the internal affairs of any country. On this basis, the current central link is: to demand an end to armed intervention in Korea, and to demand the immediate elimination of the center of this war crisis created by US imperialism. ### 争取和平的第二阶段

1950-09-21
第6版()
专栏：“和平拥护者”社论

　　争取和平的第二阶段
    在布拉格举行的执行局扩大会议公布了三个文件：
    １、召开第二次世界保卫和平大会的通告；
    ２、致送联合国安全理事会及各国政府的一项决议；
    ３、有关大会筹备工作及计划扩大和运的几项决议。
    这几项文件中，最重要的就是召开第二次世界保卫和平大会的通告。
    此项通告概述了布拉格所作的决议，并明确了我们运动的政策及其基础。因此我们努力的目标，将是要使保卫世界和平运动达到一个新的阶段，这就是将由十一月十三日到十九日在英国举行的第二次世界保卫和平大会。
    第二次世界保卫和平大会将不仅是斯德哥尔摩签名运动的高潮，而且将是一个新阶段的开始。这一新阶段除继续坚持原子武器为非法外，还要扩展到包括所有有关和平问题的其他事项。
    斯德哥尔摩签名运动，已得到了千百万男女的拥护。这一伟大运动，仍然集中于一项具体目标：禁止原子武器。这仍然是我们运动的主题之一。因为，假若运动是成功的，它就能打开各国政府间商谈与和平协议之门，可以解除目前人类所遭遇的大屠杀的威胁。但是我们的运动，不仅仅限于禁用原子武器上，它的主要目的，正如大会宣言所述，是在求得各民族间真正而持久的和平。
    最近新的战争爆发——可能是世界冲突的中心；疯狂的加紧备战；空前的战争叫嚣，指明了国际形势的严重恶化，并使我们有理由较以前更忧虑另一次世界大战可能爆发。同时，这些情况也创造了爱好和平人民间团结的新的可能性，引起了布拉格宣言中所述的新的紧急任务。
    世界和大执行局的号召要求继续努力于斯德哥尔摩宣言签名运动，并且还提出了另外三点行动的基础，可以用来获得所有要求和平者更广泛的支持：
    １、在国际管制下，普遍裁减军备；
    ２、禁止任何国家采用各种方式宣传战争；
    ３、揭露任何地方发生的侵略，并谴责对任何国家内部事务的外国武装干涉。
    这最后一点，促使世界和大执行局支持业已提出的停止朝鲜战争的和平创议，并将建议一件，致送安理会及各国政府，作为执行局对于和平解决的贡献，这将为世界各国和平运动的同样决议所支持。
    总括起来说，布拉格决议代表我们的基本政策，围绕这些决议，各国应毫不延迟地组织公开的集会，在参加第二次世界和大的代表选出以前，应围绕这些决议展开讨论。
    因此，很重要的，世界和大执行局的号召，应在世界各地使用各种可能的工具加以宣传，如无线电、报纸、杂志、传单、招贴、公开集会等。
    布拉格的号召，及由此展开的讨论，将帮助我们再获得千百万人在和平宣言上签名，同时展开扩大和平运动的新的可能。这一新的扩展，在作为争取和平的新步骤的第二次世界和平大会筹备的过程中，一定能够达到。
    编者按：世界保卫和平大会委员会机关刊物《和平拥护者》的社论中指出：保卫世界和平运动进入了新阶段。为了制止美帝国主义者发动新的世界战争的阴谋，凡在斯德哥尔摩宣言上签名要求禁用原子武器的人们还须以另外三点行动作基础，即要求（一）在国际管制下普遍裁减军备；（二）禁止任何国家采用各种方式宣传战争；（三）揭露任何地方发生的侵略，并谴责对任何国家内部事务的外国武装干涉。在这一基础上，目前的中心环节就是：要求制止对朝鲜的武装干涉，要求立刻消除由美帝造成的这个战争危机的中心。（附图片）
