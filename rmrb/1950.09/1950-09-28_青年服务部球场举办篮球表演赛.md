Title: Basketball Exhibition Games Held at Youth Service Department Stadium
Author:
Date: 1950-09-28
Page: 12

　　Basketball Exhibition Games Held at Youth Service Department Stadium
    The Youth Service Department Stadium will hold two basketball exhibition games at 7:30 PM on September 28th: (1) Beidou vs. Peking University Sports Group; (2) People's Bank of China vs. Headquarters. Location: Opposite the Beijing Hotel. Ticket price: 1,000 yuan. Ticket office: The stadium of the department.

### 青年服务部球场举办篮球表演赛

1950-09-28
第12版()
专栏：

　　青年服务部球场举办篮球表演赛
    青年服务部球场定于九月二十八日下午七时半，在该场举办篮球表演赛两场：（一）北斗——北大体群；（二）人民银行——纠总。地点在北京饭店对面。票价一千元。售票处：该部球场。
