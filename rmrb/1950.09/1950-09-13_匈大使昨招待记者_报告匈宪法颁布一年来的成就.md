Title: Hungarian Ambassador Hosts Journalists, Reports on Achievements Since Promulgation of Hungarian Constitution One Year Ago
Author:
Date: 1950-09-13
Page: 4

　　Hungarian Ambassador Hosts Journalists
    Reports on Achievements Since Promulgation of Hungarian Constitution One Year Ago
    [Xinhua News Agency, September 12th] The Hungarian Ambassador to China, Xia Falan Ke, held a reception for journalists in Beijing on September 12th to commemorate the first anniversary of the promulgation of the Hungarian Constitution. Many Chinese and foreign journalists from the capital attended. Ambassador Xia Falan Ke spoke at length about the brilliant achievements of the Hungarian People's Democratic Constitution in the past year, and then said: The Hungarian people firmly stand on the internationalist position, within the peace camp headed by the Soviet Union, and are fighting to build socialism. The Hungarian people are supporting the Korean people's war against American imperialism. The Hungarian people fully support the Chinese people's movement against American imperialist aggression against Taiwan and Northeast China, and the movement against American aggression is unfolding throughout Hungary. Ambassador Xia Falan Ke finally said: The Hungarian people, who have become masters of the country, are fighting with firm confidence and high enthusiasm to build a happy future.

### 匈大使昨招待记者  报告匈宪法颁布一年来的成就

1950-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　匈大使昨招待记者
    报告匈宪法颁布一年来的成就
    【新华社十二日讯】匈牙利人民共和国驻华大使夏法朗柯，为纪念匈牙利宪法颁布一周年，特于十二日在京举行招待记者会。到会者有首都中外记者多人。夏法朗柯大使畅谈匈牙利人民民主宪法实施一年来的辉煌成就后称：匈牙利人民坚定地站在国际主义的立场，站在以苏联为首的和平阵营内，为建设社会主义而斗争。匈牙利人民正支援朝鲜人民的反对美帝国主义的战争。匈牙利人民完全支持中国人民反对美国帝国主义侵略台湾和东北，反对美国侵略的运动正在全匈牙利展开。夏法朗柯大使最后称：已经成为国家主人的匈牙利人民，正以坚定的信心和高涨的热情，为建设幸福的将来而进行着斗争。
