Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Directly Selects Labor Model Conference Representatives; Zhao Zhankui and Six Others Designated
Author:
Date: 1950-09-09
Page: 1

　　All-China Federation of Trade Unions Directly Selects Labor Model Conference Representatives
　　Zhao Zhankui and Six Others Designated
    [Xinhua News Agency, 8th] The All-China Federation of Trade Unions (ACFTU) directly selected representatives to attend the National Congress of Labor Models of Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers. The 48th Standing Committee expanded meeting of the ACFTU designated Zhao Zhankui, Li Fenglian, Zhen Rongdian, Liu Yingyuan, Li Yong, Yu Songru, and Yang Fuchun as the seven representatives. These seven labor model representatives include workers, technicians, factory managers, and trade union workers. Zhao Zhankui was formerly a furnace tender at the Yan'an Farm Implement Factory. In 1943, he was selected as a special-class labor hero of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region. During the War of Resistance Against Japan and the Liberation War, he became a direction of struggle for workers in the liberated areas. He is currently a member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) and Deputy Director of the Labor Department of the Northwest Military and Political Committee. Li Fenglian previously worked in printing factories, clothing factories, and nurseries in Yan'an. She was a special-class female labor hero of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region. She is currently a member of the National Committee of the CPPCC and is studying at the Renmin University of China. Zhen Rongdian is an ordnance worker and a famous "Shell King." In 1943, he was selected as a labor hero of the Shanxi-Hebei-Shandong-Henan Border Region and an industrial labor banner of the Taihang Region. He is currently studying at the North China Ordnance School in Taiyuan. Model factory director Liu Yingyuan is an electrician by origin. He was a labor hero of the Northeast Liberated Area and is now the director of the Shijingshan Power Plant in Beijing of the North China Electric Power Bureau. Li Yong is an old worker who has worked on the railways for more than thirty years and is the driver of the "Mao Zedong" locomotive, a national locomotive banner. Yu Songru is a mechanical and electrical engineer at Tianjin Zhongfang No. 1 Factory with twenty-five years of service. Because he led the workers in studying technology and improving boiler equipment and combustion technology, he saved 2,600 tons of coal from January to July this year, equivalent to 600,000 jin of millet. He manufactured turbine blades and nozzles that have never been manufactured in China before, saving the state foreign exchange equivalent to 300,000 jin of millet. Yang Fuchun is a tinsmith and is currently the chairman of the trade union of the Linkou Locomotive Depot of the Harbin Railway Bureau of the Northeast Railway. Under his leadership, all eighteen locomotives in the depot have become members of the "Iron Ox Team," and the entire depot has traveled safely for more than one million kilometers.

### 全总直接评选劳模会议代表  评定为赵占魁等七名

1950-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　全总直接评选劳模会议代表
    评定为赵占魁等七名
    【新华社八日讯】由中华全国总工会直接评选参加全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的代表，经全国总工会第四十八次常委会扩大会议评定为赵占魁、李凤莲、甄荣典、刘英源、李永、于松如和杨富春等七人。这七名劳动模范代表包括有工人、技术人员、工厂管理人员和工会工作者。赵占魁过去是延安农具厂化铁炉看炉工人，一九四三年即被选为陕甘宁边区的特等劳动英雄、在抗日战争和解放战争中成为解放区工人奋斗的方向，现为中国人民政协全国委员会委员，西北军政委员会劳动部副部长。李凤莲过去在延安参加过印刷厂、被服厂和托儿所的工作，是陕甘宁边区的女工特等劳动英雄，现为人民政协全国委员会委员，在中国人民大学学习。甄荣典为兵工工人，是著名的炮弹大王，一九四三年被选为晋冀鲁豫边区劳动英雄和太行区工业劳动旗手，现在太原华北兵工学校学习。模范厂长刘英源是电气工人出身，过去是东北解放区的劳动英雄，现在是华北电业局北京石景山发电厂的厂长。李永是一个在铁路工作了三十几年的老工人，为全国机车旗帜——毛泽东号机车的司机长。于松如是天津中纺一厂的机电工程师，有二十五年工龄，由于他带动工人钻研技术，改进了锅炉设备和燃烧技术，今年一月至七月节省燃煤二千六百吨，折合小米六十万斤；他制造了国内向来没有制造过的透平机的汽叶和汽口，为国家节省的外汇折合小米三十万斤。杨富春是白铁工人，现为东北铁路哈尔滨路局林口机务段的工会主席，在他领导下，全段十八台机车都成为铁牛队队员，全段安全行走超过百万公里。
