Title: List of Appointments and Removals Approved at the Fifty-First Government Affairs Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1950-09-26
Page: 8
Xinhua News Agency, 24th. The following are the lists of appointments and removals approved by the 51st Government Affairs Council Meeting of the Central People's Government:

    I. Supplementary List of Staff for the Central People's Government Ministry of Heavy Industry
        Director of the Nonferrous Metals Industry Bureau: Wang Yiqun, formerly Director of the Political Department of the Central and Southern Military and Political University.
        Deputy Director of the Accounting Department: Zou Jiayou, formerly Director of the Business Office of the Northeast Military Region Military Industry Department of the People's Liberation Army.
    II. Supplementary List of Staff for the Central People's Government Ministry of Railways
        Director of the Zhengzhou Railway Administration: Liu Jianzhang, currently Deputy Director of the Tianjin Railway Administration of the Central People's Government Ministry of Railways.
    (Removed Wu Shi'en from his current position as Director of the Zhengzhou Railway Administration of the Central People's Government Ministry of Railways).
    (Removed Liu Jianzhang from his current position as Deputy Director of the Tianjin Railway Administration of the Central People's Government Ministry of Railways.)
    (Removed Xu Ziwei from his current position as Political Commissar of the Zhengzhou Railway Administration of the Central People's Government Ministry of Railways.)
        Deputy Director: Guo Wenxue, currently Director of the Political Department of the Zhengzhou Railway Administration of the Central People's Government Ministry of Railways.
        Deputy Director of the Taiyuan Railway Administration: Peng Bozhou, currently Principal of the Staff School of the Central People's Government Ministry of Railways.
        Deputy Director of the Shanghai Railway Administration: Lin Yan, currently Director of the Political Department of the Shanghai Railway Administration of the Central People's Government Ministry of Railways.
    III. Supplementary List of Staff for the Chinese Academy of Sciences
        Deputy Director of the Planning Bureau: Cao Richang, currently Deputy Director of the Preparatory Office of the Institute of Psychology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.
    IV. Supplementary List of Staff for the Hunan Provincial People's Government
        Director of the Finance and Economics Committee: Huang Kecheng (concurrent)
    (Removed Wang Shoudao from his concurrent position as Director of the Finance and Economics Committee of the Hunan Provincial People's Government.)
        Deputy Director: Wang Shoudao (concurrent)
    V. List of Deputy Directors of the Ganxinan Administrative Office of Jiangxi Province
        Deputy Director of the Ganxinan Administrative Office: Zhong Min, formerly Deputy Director of the Ganxinan Administrative Office.
    VI. Supplementary List of Vice Presidents of the Nationalities Institute
        Vice President: Liu Chun, currently a member of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government.

### 第五十一次政务会议任免的各项名单

1950-09-26
第8版()
专栏：

　　第五十一次政务会议任免的各项名单
    【新华社二十四日讯】中央人民政府政务院第五十一次政务会议通过任免的各项名单如下：
