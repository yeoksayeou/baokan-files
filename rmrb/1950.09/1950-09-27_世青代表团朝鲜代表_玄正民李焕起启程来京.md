Title: World Youth Delegation Korean Representatives Hyun Jung-min and Lee Hwan-ki Depart for Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-09-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th. Hyun Jung-min, Chairman of the Central Committee of the North Korean Democratic Youth League, and Lee Hwan-ki, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the South Korean Democratic Youth League, left Pyongyang on the 25th for Beijing to join the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation to visit China; and will represent all Korean youth in attending the celebration大会 of the National Day of the People's Republic of China on October 1st.

### 世青代表团朝鲜代表  玄正民李焕起启程来京

1950-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　世青代表团朝鲜代表
    玄正民李焕起启程来京
    【新华社平壤二十六日电】北朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会委员长玄正民与南朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会副委员长李焕起，于二十五日离平壤赴北京，参加世界民主青年联盟代表团，访问中国；并将代表全朝鲜青年，参加十月一日中华人民共和国国庆日庆祝大会。
