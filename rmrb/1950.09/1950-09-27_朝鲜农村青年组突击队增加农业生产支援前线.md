Title: Korean Rural Youth League Shock Brigades Increase Agricultural Production to Support the Front Lines
Author:
Date: 1950-09-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 25th: In order to increase agricultural production and support the front lines, rural youth throughout the northern half of Korea have widely organized front-line youth shock brigades. They have played a key role in weeding, fertilizing, increasing production, and paying in-kind taxes. In Gangwon Province, rural areas have organized 2,238 front-line youth shock brigades, actively assisting military families with labor shortages in weeding, harvesting, and transporting crops, ensuring the fulfillment of wartime crop production plans. The wartime shock brigades of rural youth near the 38th parallel in Hwanghae Province and Gangwon Province, in addition to actively participating in the repair of roads and bridges destroyed by enemy aircraft, are also reclaiming land left barren due to the invasion of the US-Syngman Rhee bandit armies, restoring the cultivation of farmland.

### 朝鲜农村青年组突击队增加农业生产支援前线

1950-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜农村青年组突击队增加农业生产支援前线
    【新华社平壤二十五日电】朝鲜北半部各地农村青年为了增加农业生产和支援前线，广泛地组织了前线青年突击队。他们在锄草、施肥、增加生产及缴纳现物税等工作中，起了核心作用。江原道各农村组织了二千二百三十八个前线青年突击队，积极协助劳力不足的军属进行锄草、秋收与搬运等工作，保证了战时农作物的增产计划。黄海道与江原道三八线附近的农村青年的战时突击队，除了积极参加修复被敌机炸毁的道路及桥梁之外，并且开垦由于美、李匪军侵犯而荒芜了的土地，恢复农田的耕种。
