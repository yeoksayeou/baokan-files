Title: Overseas Chinese in Indonesia and other places warmly welcome the National Day; Overseas Chinese in North Korea will hold a photo exhibition of our country

Author:

Date: 1950-09-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 28th: Overseas Chinese in Indonesia are actively preparing to celebrate the first National Day of the motherland. According to a report in the *South China Daily* on the 15th: Ninety-five overseas Chinese organizations in Jakarta held a representative meeting on the 10th to discuss celebration matters. The meeting decided to hold a mass rally on the morning of October 1st, followed by a parade in the city, and a garden party on October 2nd and 3rd. The meeting decided to strengthen the peace signature campaign around the National Day. The representative meeting passed a telegram to Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai, expressing anger at the US imperialism's aggression against China's territory Taiwan, and the intrusion of US planes into the motherland's airspace. At the same time, the meeting sent a telegram to the Security Council, protesting against the US imperialism's aggression against Taiwan, and the US aircraft's attack on Northeast China and the bombing of the Korean people.

Aneta News Agency, Bandung: Overseas Chinese in Bandung have also established a committee and are actively preparing to celebrate the National Day. Overseas Chinese in Bandung are scheduled to hold a parade on September 30th and a mass rally on the morning of October 1st.

(Reported by this newspaper) According to Xinhua News Agency: The Overseas Chinese Students Federation of Java has issued instructions calling on all members to actively participate in and promote the expansion of National Day celebrations in various regions. The Singapore Overseas Chinese Teachers' Association has also held a meeting to discuss ways to commemorate the National Day.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 28th: To celebrate the first National Day of the People's Republic of China, the Federation of Overseas Chinese in North Korea is scheduled to hold a "Mobile Photo Exhibition" introducing the situation of the motherland around the National Day. The exhibition will include: the four-year People's Liberation War, land reform, production and construction, the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, overseas Chinese support for the motherland's Liberation War and support for the Korean Democratic Government, etc. The propaganda vehicle is scheduled to depart from Pyongyang on the 29th for public exhibition in various cities and counties. The duration is set for one month.

### 印尼等地侨胞热烈迎接国庆  北朝鲜华侨将举办我国图片展览

1950-09-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　印尼等地侨胞热烈迎接国庆
    北朝鲜华侨将举办我国图片展览
    【新华社二十八日讯】印尼华侨热烈筹备庆祝祖国的第一个国庆节。据《南侨日报》十五日消息：雅加达九十五个华侨团体于十日召开代表大会，商讨庆祝事宜。会议决定于十月一日上午举行群众大会，散会后在市内游行，并于十月二、三两日举行游园会。会议决定在国庆前后加强和平签名运动。代表大会通过致电周恩来外交部长，对美帝国主义侵略中国的领土台湾、及美机侵入祖国领空表示愤怒。大会同时致电安理会，抗议美帝国主义侵略台湾，及美国飞机侵袭东北及滥炸朝鲜人民的暴行。
    另据安尼塔社万隆讯：万隆华侨也已成立委员会，热烈筹备庆祝国庆节。万隆华侨定于九月三十日举行游行，十月一日上午举行群众大会。
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：爪哇华侨学生联合会已发出指示，号召全体会员积极参加和推动各地区扩大庆祝国庆节。新加坡华侨教师公会亦已召开会议，讨论纪念国庆的办法。
    【新华社平壤二十八日电】北朝鲜华侨联合总会为庆祝中华人民共和国第一个国庆日，定于国庆日前后举办介绍祖国情况的“流动照片展览会”。展览内容包括：四年人民解放战争、土地改革、生产建设、人民政协、华侨支援祖国解放战争与拥护朝鲜民主政府等。宣传车定二十九日自平壤出发赴各市县公开展览。时间定为一个月。
