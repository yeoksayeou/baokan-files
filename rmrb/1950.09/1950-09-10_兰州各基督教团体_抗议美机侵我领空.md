Title: Lanzhou Christian Groups Protest US Aircraft Intrusion into Our Airspace
Author:
Date: 1950-09-10
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, 1st. Christian groups in Lanzhou, including the Chinese Christian Church, the Seventh-day Adventist Church, the Lanzhou Church of the Gansu Provincial Chinese Christian Church, the True Jesus Church, the Chinese Christian Church, the Park Road Meeting Place, and the Lanzhou YMCA, representing over three thousand Christians, jointly issued a circular telegram on September 1st, protesting against the US aircraft’s continuous intrusion into our airspace and the atrocities of killing and wounding our people. The telegram stated: We support Foreign Minister Zhou's serious protest to the US government and the request to the UN Security Council to sanction the American aggressors. At the same time, we hope that Christians all over the world will sympathize with and respond to our appeal to stop the imperialist acts of aggression by the United States, to maintain international justice, and to ensure lasting world peace.

### 兰州各基督教团体  抗议美机侵我领空

1950-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　兰州各基督教团体
    抗议美机侵我领空
    【新华社兰州一日电】兰州市中国基督教会、安息日会、甘肃省中华基督教会兰州教会、真耶稣教会、中华基督教会、公园路聚会处、兰州基督教青年会等宗教团体，代表三千多基督教徒于九月一日联合发出通电，抗议美机连续侵我领空，并杀伤我国人民的兽行。电文称：我们拥护周外长向美国政府所提出的严重抗议和向联合国安理会提出的制裁美国侵略者的要求。同时，我们希望全世界的基督教徒同情并响应我们的呼吁，制止美帝国主义的侵略暴行，以维国际正义，确保世界永久的和平。
