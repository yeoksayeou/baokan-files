Title: National Beijing Library Exhibits Soviet Drama Art Pictures
Author: Chen Zhongchi
Date: 1950-09-05
Page: 6

　　National Beijing Library Exhibits Soviet Drama Art Pictures
　　The National Beijing Library is exhibiting pictures of Soviet drama art from September 1st. The content includes about 270 pictures of the Soviet National Little Theatre and the Moscow Grand Theatre's affiliated dance school, the Moscow Film Studio, worker actors, and the Uzbek Theatre. Among them, the National Little Theatre has a history of 125 years, and many famous plays and actors have performed here. The director of the Little Theatre, Prof. Sadovsky, and the general director, Zubov, have both been awarded the Stalin Prize. From these pictures, one can gain a lot of knowledge about Soviet drama art.
                                                        (Chen Zhongchi)

### 国立北京图书馆展览苏联戏剧艺术画片
陈仲篪
1950-09-05
第6版()
专栏：

　　国立北京图书馆展览苏联戏剧艺术画片
    国立北京图书馆自九月一日起展览苏联戏剧艺术的图片，内容有苏联国立小剧院和莫斯科大戏院附设舞蹈学校，莫斯科电影制片厂，工人演员，乌兹别克剧场等概况画片约二百七十余幅，其中国立小剧院，它已有一百二十五年的历史，许多著名的剧本和演员，都在这里演出过。小剧院的指导者普洛夫·萨多夫斯基，总导演祖伯夫等，都荣膺了斯大林奖金。从这些图片中，对苏联的戏剧艺术可以得到很多的认识。
                                                        （陈仲篪）
