Title: Central Cooperative Administration Bureau Dispatches Cadres to Various Regions for Inspection
Author: Feng Yun
Date: 1950-09-02
Page: 5

Central Cooperative Administration Bureau
　　Dispatches Cadres to Various Regions for Inspection

　　[This Newspaper's Report] After the conclusion of the First National Congress of Cooperative Workers, in order to systematically inspect the work of consumer, supply and marketing, and production cooperatives, and to systematically collect materials for the purpose of rectifying the cooperative work in various regions, the Central Cooperative Administration Bureau specially dispatched forty cadres (including eleven section chiefs) into nine groups, to various regions to inspect work with a focus and collect materials.
　　The areas determined for inspection this time are: Jiaohe and Yushu in Northeast China (in addition, the Northeast Cooperative General Office selected two other locations); Tongxian, Handan, Dingxian, Cangxian, Yangquan in the North China region; Laiyang, Weifang, Shanghai and Beijing, Tianjin and other fourteen places in the East China region. Each inspection group departed on August 20th and is expected to return to the Bureau on September 10th.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Feng Yun)

### 中央合作事业管理局  抽调干部赴各地检查
冯云
1950-09-02
第5版()
专栏：

　　中央合作事业管理局
    抽调干部赴各地检查
    【本报讯】全国合作社工作者第一届代表大会闭幕后，为了有系统地检查消费、供销和生产合作的工作，并有系统地搜集材料，以便整顿各区合作社工作，中央合作事业管理局特抽调四十个干部（处长十一人）分成九组，分赴各地有重点地检查工作，搜集材料。
    此次确定检查的地区为：东北的蛟河、榆树（另有东北合作总社自选两处；华北区的通县、邯郸、定县、沧县、阳泉；华东区的莱阳、潍坊、上海及北京、天津等十四处。各检查组已于八月二十日出发，预计九月十日返局。
                                                    （冯云）
