Title: Chairman Mao Hosts Banquet for All Model Heroes on Mid-Autumn Festival Night
Author:
Date: 1950-09-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government and the Central People's Revolutionary Military Commission hosted a banquet last night to entertain all representatives attending the National Conference of Combat Heroes and the National Conference of Model Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers. Commander-in-Chief Zhu De, Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman Li Jishen, Secretary-General Lin Boqu, Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Director of the General Political Department of the Central People's Revolutionary Military Commission Luo Ronghuan, heads of all ministries, commissions, institutes, administrations, and banks of the Central People's Government, heads of all departments of the Central People's Revolutionary Military Commission, and all representatives of combat heroes and labor models, totaling more than 1,100 people, attended the banquet.

　　After the banquet began, Commander-in-Chief Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai delivered short banquet speeches, congratulating the heroes and models on their achievements in combat and production work, and proposed a toast to the heroes and models for their greater efforts in building a strong national defense force and a strong economic power.

　　Hero and heroine representatives from the People's Liberation Army and militia organizations, and male and female labor model representatives from all regions and ethnic groups across the country, took the stage one after another, team after team, to deliver speeches continuously. They warmly thanked Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De for their wise leadership in liberating the people of the whole country, and expressed their willingness to redouble their efforts to strengthen the national defense force and restore and develop industrial and agricultural production. The attendees enthusiastically shouted "Long live Chairman Mao! Long live Commander-in-Chief Zhu!" and toasted to the health of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De. In this way, the model figures of the Chinese nation joyfully spent the Mid-Autumn Festival night of 1950 with the leaders of the motherland.

### 毛主席中秋夜设宴招待全体英模代表

1950-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席中秋夜设宴招待全体英模代表
    【新华社二十七日讯】中央人民政府和中央人民革命军事委员会毛泽东主席于昨晚设宴招待出席全国战斗英雄代表会议和全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的全体代表。出席宴会的有朱总司令、刘少奇副主席、李济深副主席、林伯渠秘书长、政务院周恩来总理、中央人民革命军事委员会总政治部罗荣桓主任、中央人民政府各部、会、院、署、行和中央人民革命军事委员会各部首长以及战斗英雄、劳动模范全体代表共一千一百余人。
    宴会开始后，朱总司令、周恩来总理先后致简短宴词，祝贺英雄模范们在战斗和生产工作中的成就，并为预祝英雄模范们在建立强大的国防军和建立强大的经济力量中更加努力而干杯。
    来自人民解放军和民兵组织中的男女英雄代表们和来自全国各地区、各民族人民中的男女劳动模范代表们一个接着一个，一队接着一队连续不断地走上宴台致词。他们热烈感谢毛主席、朱总司令的英明领导，使全国人民翻了身，并愿为加强国防军及为恢复和发展工业农业生产而加倍努力。与宴者热烈高呼毛主席万岁、朱总司令万岁！并为毛主席、朱总司令的健康而干杯。中华民族的模范人物们就是这样欢乐地和祖国的领导者们度过了一九五零年中秋的夜晚。
