Title: After Attending the World Federation of Trade Unions Educational Workers' Congress, Representatives of the Chinese Educational Trade Union Have Returned to Beijing; Delegation Attending the World Student Congress Departs for Home

Author:
Date: 1950-09-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 6th:

　　After Attending the World Federation of Trade Unions Educational Workers' Congress
	Representatives of the Chinese Educational Trade Union Have Returned to Beijing
	Delegation Attending the World Student Congress Departs for Home
    [Xinhua News Agency, 6th] Fang Ming and Sheng Jin (female), representatives of the Chinese Educational Trade Union who attended the Fifth International Congress of the Trade Union International of Educational Workers, held in Vienna, the capital of Austria, from August 6th to 10th, returned to Beijing on September 2nd. According to the returning representatives, the conference was attended by representatives of educational trade unions from seventeen countries, symbolizing the great unity of more than three million educators around the world. The representatives of the Chinese Educational Trade Union were warmly welcomed by representatives from various countries and were elected as vice-chairmen. The meeting heard a report by Comrade Delano, General Secretary of the Trade Union International of Educational Workers, on the summary of work in the past year and the guiding principles and tasks for future work. When Comrade Grifgaff, head of the Soviet delegation, made a speech on the international activities of the educational workers' trade union, the entire audience chanted "Long live Stalin!" for a long time. This meeting called on educators around the world to unite and strive to defend world peace, implement the democratization of education, and guarantee and improve the material lives of teachers. All the representatives attending the meeting signed the Declaration for the Defense of Peace.
    The representatives of the Chinese Educational Trade Union were invited to report on the situation of the Chinese people's movement to defend peace at a mass meeting of 150,000 people, which was warmly welcomed by the audience.

    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 6th] The delegation of our country attending the Second World Student Congress departed from Prague, Czech Republic on the 5th via the Soviet Union to return home. Those who went to the station to see them off included Comrade Hrzal, Chairman of the Czech Youth League, and more than 50 representatives of the Czech Student Union. The ten members of the Chinese basketball team who went to Romania for a friendly match will also depart from Romania for Moscow on the 7th to return home with the delegation.

### 出席世界工联教育工作者代表大会后  我国教育工会代表已返京  出席世界学代会的代表团启程返国

1950-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　出席世界工联教育工作者代表大会后
    我国教育工会代表已返京
    出席世界学代会的代表团启程返国
    【新华社六日讯】出席八月六日至十日在奥地利首都维也纳召开的世界工联教育工作者工会国际第五次代表大会的中国教育工会代表方明、盛瑾（女）等，已于九月二日返京。据归国代表称：此次大会有十七个国家的教育工会代表出席，象征了世界各国三百万以上教育工作者的大团结。中国教育工会的代表受到各国代表的热烈欢迎，并被选为副主席。会议听取了教育工会国际总书记戴拉诺关于过去一年工作的总结与今后工作的方针任务的报告。当苏联代表团团长格里夫高夫在作了关于教育工作者工会国际活动的发言以后，全场高呼斯大林万岁经久不息。这次会议召号全世界教育工作者团结起来，为保卫世界和平，实行教育民主化，保证并提高教师物质生活而奋斗。出席会议的全体代表都在保卫和平宣言书上签了名。
    中国教育工会的代表曾被邀在一次有十五万人的群众大会报告中国人民保卫和平运动的情况，受到听众的热烈欢迎。
    【新华社布拉格六日电】我国出席第二届世界学生代表大会的代表团，于五日由捷京布拉格启行经苏联返国。赴车站欢送者有捷克青年联盟主席赫兹拉尔及捷克学联代表等五十余人。前赴罗马尼亚作友谊比赛的我国篮球队十人，亦将于七日自罗马尼亚赴莫斯科随代表团返国。
