Title: The People of Southern China and Guangzhou Express Limitless Anger at US Aircraft Intrusion
Author:
Date: 1950-09-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 31st - The people of Southern China and Guangzhou are infinitely angered by the incident of US imperialist aircraft intruding into our Northeast airspace and shooting our compatriots. The Preparatory Committee of the Guangdong Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, the Guangdong Provincial Farmers Association, the Southern China Working Committee of the Communist Youth League, the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Youth League, the Preparatory Committee of the Guangdong Provincial Democratic Women's Federation, and the Guangdong Provincial Student Federation issued a joint declaration, denouncing the US imperialist atrocities and resolutely supporting Foreign Minister Zhou's request to the UN Security Council and the solemn protest to the US government. They also called on workers, farmers, youth, women, and students throughout Southern China to unite under the leadership of the Central People's Government and Chairman Mao, fully prepare our strength, and launch a widespread peace signature campaign, penetrating into small and medium-sized cities and villages, to make the broad masses of people more deeply aware of the face of US imperialism, unite, and fight to smash any act of aggression by US imperialism! The Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Municipal Democratic Women's Federation, the Guangzhou Municipal Student Federation, and the industrial and commercial circles of Guangzhou have all sent telegrams to Malik, Chairman of the UN Security Council, and Secretary-General Lie, demanding sanctions against the US imperialist aggression and atrocities, and ordering the US government to completely withdraw its troops invading Korea. Local organizations of various democratic parties: the South China Provisional Working Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the Southern General Branch and Guangdong Branch of the China Democratic League, and the Guangdong Party Affairs Consolidation Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party have also successively issued statements or talks. Three hundred and fifty-seven university teachers held a forum and jointly issued a declaration against US imperialist aggression.

### 华南和广州市各界人民  对美机侵犯表示无限愤怒

1950-09-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　华南和广州市各界人民
    对美机侵犯表示无限愤怒
    【新华社广州三十一日电】华南和广州市各界人民为美帝飞机侵入我东北领空并射杀我同胞事件，激起了无限愤慨。广东省总工会筹委会、广东省农民协会、青年团华南工作委员会、青年团广东工作委员会、广东省民主妇联筹委会、广东省学生联合会等六大团体联合发表宣言，痛斥美帝暴行，坚决拥护周外长致联合国安理会的要求和向美国政府所提出的严正抗议。并号召全华南的工人、农民、青年、妇女、学生在中央人民政府和毛主席领导下，团结一致，充分准备力量，并广泛展开和平签名运动，深入中小城市和乡村，使广大人民更深刻地认清美帝国主义的面目，一致起来，为粉碎美帝国主义任何侵略的行为而斗争！广州市总工会筹委会、广州市民主妇女联合会筹委会、广州市学生联合会、广州市工商业界都分别致电联合国安理会主席马立克和秘书长赖伊，要求制裁美帝国主义侵略暴行，并令美国政府把侵略朝鲜的军队完全撤退。各民主党派的地方组织：中国国民党革命委员会华南临时工作委员会、中国民主同盟南方总支部暨广东省支部、中国农工民主党广东党务整理委员会等亦相继发表了声明或谈话。大学教师三百五十七人举行了座谈会，并联名发表反对美帝侵略的宣言。
