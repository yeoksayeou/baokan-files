Title: Korean People Enthusiastically Support the Front, Donating Large Amounts of Funds for National Defense Expenses
Author:
Date: 1950-09-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 27th dispatch: TASS, 25th dispatch: Pyongyang Radio broadcast that a campaign to donate funds to support the front is underway throughout the country. The people of South Hamgyong Province have already donated 89,700,040 won in cash, 15,638 bags of grain (60 kilograms per bag), 770 cattle, 250 pigs, and other items. Women in Bukcheong County donated 2,292 gold ornaments, and women in Sinheung County donated 2,306 gold ornaments for national defense expenses.

### 朝鲜人民热烈支前  捐献大批基金充作国防费用

1950-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民热烈支前
    捐献大批基金充作国防费用
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社二十五日讯：平壤电台广播称：全国各地正展开捐献支援前线基金的运动。咸镜南道的人民已捐献现款八千九百七十万零四千元，谷物一万五千六百三十八包（一包合六十公斤），牛七百七十头、猪二百五十头，及其他等物。北青县的妇女捐献了二千二百九十二件金首饰，新兴县的妇女捐献了二千三百零六件金首饰，作为国防费用。
