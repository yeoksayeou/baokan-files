Title: Korean People's Army Continues Heroic Advance, Annihilating Over Two Thousand Enemy Troops on All Fronts
Author:
Date: 1950-09-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 5th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the morning of the 4th that the People's Army units on all fronts, having broken through the defensive lines of the US military and the remnants of the Li Chengwan (Syngman Rhee) puppet army, who were resisting stubbornly with all their forces, continued to launch fierce offensive battles.

The People's Army units advancing on the southern coast inflicted a powerful blow on the US military units and continued to advance. In the battle, the People's Army units killed or wounded more than 500 enemy soldiers, captured many prisoners, and seized six tanks, ten vehicles, eleven heavy machine guns, and a large number of other war trophies.

The People's Army units advancing eastward on the east bank of the Luodong River (Naktong River) crushed the enemy's stubborn resistance and continued to advance heroically. In the battle on this front, the People's Army units killed or wounded more than 600 US soldiers, captured more than 100 prisoners, and seized fifteen artillery pieces of various types, more than twenty vehicles, and a large number of other weapons and ammunition. The enemy army abandoned a large number of corpses and destroyed military supplies, retreating in disorder.

The People's Army Air Force bravely fought the numerically superior US aircraft on all fronts, bombing concentrated enemy forces and enemy rear transport lines, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy, and covering the advance of the People's Army ground forces.

On September 1st, the People's Army units on the east coast countered US military aircraft invading that area, shooting down one enemy bomber and one fighter.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 5th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the evening of the 4th that the People's Army units on all fronts, having broken through the defensive lines of the stubbornly resisting US military units and the remnants of the Li Chengwan (Syngman Rhee) puppet army, continued to launch courageous offensive battles.

The People's Army units advancing after liberating Waguan (Waegwan) repelled the enemy's fierce counterattacks, inflicting great losses on the enemy, and continued to attack fiercely. In the battle on this front, the People's Army units killed or wounded more than 1,500 enemy soldiers, captured more than 100 prisoners, and seized a large number of war trophies.

The People's Army units advancing south after liberating Junwei (Gunwi) fiercely attacked the enemy, who were stubbornly resisting from solid defensive positions, killing and wounding many enemies, capturing more than 100 prisoners, seizing more than 200 rifles, and seizing mortars, light and heavy machine guns, and many other kinds of weapons and ammunition.

The People's Army units advancing on the east coast and the south coast repelled the enemy's counterattacks and continued to launch fierce offensive battles.

### 朝鲜人民军继续英勇进击  各线又歼灭敌军两千余名

1950-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军继续英勇进击
    各线又歼灭敌军两千余名
    【新华社平壤五日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部四日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上，突破了动员全部兵力进行顽抗的美军和李承晚残余伪军的防线，继续展开猛烈的攻击战。
    在南海岸方面举行进攻的人民军部队，予美军部队以强有力的打击，继续挺进中。人民军部队在战斗中，毙、伤敌军五百余名，俘敌甚多，缴获坦克六辆、汽车十辆、重机枪十一挺及其它大量战利品。
    在洛东江东岸向前进攻的人民军部队，击溃敌人的顽抗，继续英勇进击。在该方面的战斗中，人民军部队毙、伤美军六百余名，俘虏一百余名，缴获各种炮十五门、汽车二十余辆及其他武器弹药等战利品甚多。敌军遗弃大批尸体和破坏了的军火，无秩序地退却。
    人民军空军在各战线上与数量上占优势的美军飞机勇敢地作战，轰炸集结的敌军和敌后运输线，使敌人遭受重大损失，并掩护人民军地面部队的进攻。
    九月一日，东海岸方面的人民军部队迎击侵袭该方面的美军飞机，击落敌轰炸机和战斗机各一架。
    【新华社平壤五日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部四日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上，突破顽抗的美军部队和李承晚残余伪军的防线，继续展开勇敢的攻击战。
    解放倭馆后挺进中的人民军部队，击退敌人猛烈的反攻，予敌人以莫大损失，继续猛烈地进攻。在该方面的战斗中，人民军部队毙、伤敌军一千五百余名，俘敌一百余名，并缴获大量战利品。
    解放军威后向南挺进中的人民军部队，猛攻依据坚固防御阵地进行顽抗的敌人，毙、伤许多敌人，俘虏一百余名，缴获步枪二百余支，并缴获迫击炮、轻重机枪、及其他各种武器弹药等军火甚多。
    在东海岸和南海岸方面挺进中的人民军部队，击退了敌军的反攻，继续展开猛烈的进攻战。
