Title: Celebrating the International Organization of Journalists Congress: Helsinki Masses Rally and March, Passing Resolution Condemning US Aggression in Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-09-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, 18th: According to TASS, the people of the Finnish capital held a mass rally and torchlight procession on the evening of the 16th to celebrate the opening of the Third Congress of the International Organization of Journalists and the upcoming Second Finnish Peace Congress.

    Kilpi, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Finnish People's Democratic League Executive Committee, pointed out in his opening speech that nearly 800,000 people in Finland have signed the peace declaration. Chekashov, member of the Soviet Peace Committee and People's Artist of the Soviet Union, Stern, representative of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress, and Saslavsky, representative of Soviet journalists, also delivered speeches at the meeting. When Qi Shifu, the Korean journalist representative, addressed the meeting, the audience responded with a storm of cheers. The conference unanimously passed a resolution firmly condemning the US aggression against Korea, demanding an immediate halt to the barbaric bombing by the US Air Force, and the immediate withdrawal of all US troops from Korea. The resolution also sternly condemned the reactionary propaganda of the Finnish anti-peace movement's reactionary news agencies and radio stations.

### 庆祝国际记协代表大会  芬京群众集会游行  通过决议谴责美国侵略朝鲜

1950-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝国际记协代表大会
    芬京群众集会游行
    通过决议谴责美国侵略朝鲜
    【新华社十八日讯】据塔斯社赫尔辛基消息：芬兰首都的人民为庆祝国际新闻记者协会第三届代表大会的开幕，和即将召开的芬兰第二届和平大会，于十六日晚举行了群众大会及火炬游行。
    芬兰人民民主联盟执行委员会常务委员会副主席基尔比在开幕词中指出：芬兰已有将近八十万人在和平宣言上签了名。苏联拥护和平委员会委员、苏联人民艺术家却卡邵夫，世界拥护和平大会常设委员会的代表斯特恩，苏联新闻记者代表萨斯拉夫斯基均在会上发表讲演。当朝鲜的新闻记者代表奇石福向大会致词时，会众报以暴风雨般的欢呼。大会一致通过决议，坚决谴责美国侵略朝鲜，要求立即制止美国空军的野蛮轰炸，立即从朝鲜撤退所有美军。决议并严正谴责了芬兰反对和平运动的反动新闻机构与广播电台的反动宣传。
