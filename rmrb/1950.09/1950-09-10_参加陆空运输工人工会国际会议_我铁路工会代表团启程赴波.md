Title: Our Railway Workers' Delegation Departs for Poland to Attend International Conference of the Land, Air and Water Transport Workers' Union
Author:
Date: 1950-09-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th:

　　Our Railway Workers' Delegation Departs for Poland to Attend International Conference of the Land, Air and Water Transport Workers' Union

The Chinese Railway Workers' Union delegation departed today via the Soviet Union for Poland to attend the administrative committee meeting of the International Land, Air and Water Transport Workers' Union (one of the industrial departments of the World Federation of Trade Unions), which will be held in Warsaw, the capital of Poland, starting on the 18th of this month. The conference will discuss how to organize the strength of land, air, and water transport workers around the world to defend world peace. The delegation will also visit the Soviet Railway Workers' Union. The delegation consists of eleven members, led by Li Jiebo, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Workers' Union, and Li Mingzhe, Chairman of the Northeast Region Railway Workers' Union Committee, as the deputy leader. Members include Mu Chunong, Minister of the Organization Department of the Chinese Railway Workers' Union, Zhang Xueke, Director of the Political Department of the China Changchun Railway, Gao Gewu, Chief of the Machinery Department of the General Supervision Office of the Ministry of Railways and Inspector, Guo Shude, special-class labor hero and driver of the Mao Zedong locomotive, and Xing Baoyin, Chairman of the Trade Union of the Xinyang Locomotive Depot. This morning, when the delegation left Beijing, Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Wu Jingtian, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Railways, Lu Ping, Director of the Political Department of the Ministry of Railways and Chairman of the National Railway Work Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, and more than forty others went to the airport to see them off. Representatives from the Polish Embassy also went to the airport to see them off.

### 参加陆空运输工人工会国际会议  我铁路工会代表团启程赴波

1950-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　参加陆空运输工人工会国际会议
    我铁路工会代表团启程赴波
    【新华社八日讯】中国铁路工会代表团于本日启程经苏联飞赴波兰，参加陆空运输工人工会国际（世界工联产业部之一）于本月十八日起在波兰首都华沙举行的行政委员会会议。此次会议将讨论如何组织全世界陆空运输工人的力量保卫世界和平问题。会议代表团并将访问苏联铁路工会。代表团一行十一人，由中国铁路工会全国委员会主席李颉伯任团长，东北地区铁路工会委员会主席李明哲任副团长。团员为：中国铁路工会组织部部长慕纯农、中长铁路政治部主任张学轲、铁道部总监察室机务主任监察高戈伍、毛泽东号机车司机特等劳动英雄郭树德、信阳机务段工会主席邢保寅等。本日晨代表团离开北京时，中华全国总工会刘宁一副主席、铁道部武竞天副部长、铁道部政治部主任兼新民主主义青年团全国铁道工作委员会主席陆平等四十余人前往机场欢送。波兰大使馆代表也到机场欢送。
