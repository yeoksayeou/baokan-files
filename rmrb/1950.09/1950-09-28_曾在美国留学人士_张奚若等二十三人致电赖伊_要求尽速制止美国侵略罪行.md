Title: Former U.S. Students, Zhang Xiruo and 23 Others, Telegraph Lie, Demanding the Swift Cessation of U.S. Acts of Aggression
Author:
Date: 1950-09-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 27th: Zhang Xiruo, Ma Yinchu, Qian Duansheng, Luo Longji, Zhao Zichen, and twenty-three other individuals who had previously studied in the United States sent a telegram to the United Nations on September 27, opposing American aggression. The original text is as follows: To Mr. Trygve Lie, Secretary-General of the United Nations:

We, who have studied in the United States and are currently engaged in various works in New China, hereby appeal to the United Nations to halt the American acts of aggression against China, Korea, and other regions of Asia. Such acts of aggression endanger world peace and the existence of the United Nations.

The United States uses its armed forces to invade China's Taiwan and attack peaceful villages in the Liaodong Province of Northeast China. It attacks and ravages Korea and widely expands its acts of aggression in various ways to other regions of Asia. None of these acts is not a blatant violation of the United Nations Charter, yet none of these acts is not carried out under the guise of the United Nations.

All these American acts of aggression not only constitute a violation of law and morality and a threat to world peace, but also a sad betrayal of the excellent American traditions represented by Jefferson, Lincoln, and Roosevelt.

We, along with the angry Asian people, unanimously demand that the United Nations immediately take the following decisive actions:

(1) Stop the United States from expanding its acts of aggression under the cover of the United Nations.

(2) Order the United States armed forces to immediately withdraw from Taiwan and cease all military provocations that infringe upon Chinese sovereignty;

(3) Compel the United States to immediately compensate for all losses of life and property caused by the unwarranted and illegal attacks on Chinese territory in Liaodong Province on August 27 and 29, and September 22, 1950, and fully guarantee that such barbaric acts will not be repeated in the future.

(4) Order the immediate withdrawal of the American invading forces from Korea and take effective steps to prevent further interference and aggression by the United States in other regions of Asia.

We solemnly request the United Nations to take these steps as soon as possible. The actions of the United States in Asia are plunging the reputation of the United Nations into the mire of international injustice and war. The United Nations must act for peace and justice.

Signatories: Zhang Xiruo (Professor, Tsinghua University), Ma Yinchu (President, Zhejiang University), Qian Duansheng (Dean, Law School, Peking University), Luo Longji (Member of the Central Political Bureau, China Democratic League), Zhao Zichen (Dean, School of Religion, Yenching University), Wu Yifang (female) (President, Ginling College), Chen Yuguang (President, Nanjing University), Chen Xujing (President, Lingnan University of Guangzhou), Zhu Kezhen (Vice President, Chinese Academy of Sciences), Hou Debang (Scientist, General Manager of Yongli Chemical Industries), Lao She (Writer), Zeng Zhaolun (Provost, Peking University), Zhou Peiyuan (Provost, Tsinghua University), Liang Sicheng (Architectural Engineer), Jin Yuelin (Professor, Tsinghua University), Feng Youlan (Professor, Tsinghua University), Wu Yaozong (Representative of the Christian Political Consultative Conference), Tu Yuqing (General Secretary of the National Christian Council), Liu Wang Liming (female) (President of the Chinese Women's Temperance Society), Jin Baoshan (Public Health Expert), Shen Kefei (Doctor), Zhang Yuanshan (Former General Director of the China International Relief Committee), Ling Qijun (Manager of Renli Industrial Company)

### 曾在美国留学人士  张奚若等二十三人致电赖伊  要求尽速制止美国侵略罪行

1950-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　曾在美国留学人士
    张奚若等二十三人致电赖伊
    要求尽速制止美国侵略罪行
    【新华社二十七日讯】曾在美国留学人士张奚若、马寅初、钱端升、罗隆基、赵紫宸等二十三人于九月二十七日致电联合国，反对美国侵略，原文如下：联合国秘书长赖伊先生：
    我们，曾经在美国留过学，目前正在新中国从事各种工作，谨向联合国呼吁制止美国对于中国、朝鲜和亚洲其他地区的侵略行为。这类侵略行为危害了世界和平与联合国的生存。
    美国使用其武装部队侵略中国的台湾，并袭击中国东北辽东省的和平村庄。它进攻和蹂躏朝鲜，并以各种方式广泛扩张其侵略行动于亚洲其他地区。这类行为无一不是对于联合国宪章的明显的违犯，然而这类行为却无一不是在盗用联合国名义之下进行的。
    所有这些美国侵略行为不但构成对于法纪道德的破坏和世界和平的威胁，即对于以杰斐逊、林肯和罗斯福所代表的美国优秀传统，也是一种可悲的背叛。
    我们和愤怒的亚洲人民，一致要求联合国立即采取下列断然行动：
    （一）制止美国在联合国掩护下扩展其侵略行动。
    （二）命令美国武装部队立即从台湾撤退，并停止一切侵害中国主权的军事挑衅行动；
    （三）促使美国立即赔偿一九五○年八月二十七、二十九两日与九月二十二日在辽东省对中国领土无故非法袭击所造成的一切生命与财产的损失，并充分保证今后此类野蛮行动不致重演。
    （四）命令美国侵略部队立即从朝鲜撤退，并采取有效步骤，防止美国对亚洲其他地区再有干涉和侵略行动。
    我们郑重要求联合国尽速采行这些步骤。美国在亚洲的行动正使联合国的声望陷于国际不义与战争的污泥中。联合国必须为和平与正义而行动。
    签名者：张奚若（清华大学教授）马寅初（浙江大学校长）钱端升（北京大学法学院长）罗隆基（中国民主同盟中央政治局委员）赵紫宸（燕京大学宗教学院院长）吴贻芳（女）（金陵女子大学校长）陈裕光（南京金陵大学校长）陈序经（广州岭南大学校长）竺可桢（中国科学院副院长）侯德榜（科学家，永利化学工业公司总经理）老舍（作家）曾昭抡（北京大学教务长）周培源（清华大学教务长）梁思成（建筑工程师）金岳霖（清华大学教授）冯友兰（清华大学教授）吴耀宗（基督教政协代表）涂羽卿（全国基督教总干事）刘王立明（女）（中国妇女节制会会长）金宝善（公共卫生专家）沈克非（医生）章元善（前华洋义赈会总干事）凌其峻（仁立实业公司经理）
