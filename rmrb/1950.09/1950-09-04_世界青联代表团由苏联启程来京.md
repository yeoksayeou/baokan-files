Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation Departs from Soviet Union for Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-09-04
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 3rd: The World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation, en route to visit New China via the Soviet Union, departed by train today for Beijing. The delegation consists of thirty-nine representatives from thirty countries, led by the General Secretary of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, Bugała. Wu Xueqian, the representative of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth to the World Federation of Democratic Youth, accompanied the delegation.

### 世界青联代表团由苏联启程来京

1950-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界青联代表团由苏联启程来京
    【新华社莫斯科三日电】道经苏联前往新中国访问的世界民主青年联盟代表团，已于今日乘火车离此前赴北京。代表团由三十个国家的三十九位代表组成，由世界青联总书记布加拉率领。中华全国民主青年联合总会驻世界民主青联代表吴学谦陪同代表团前往。
