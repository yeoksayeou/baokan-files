Title: After a Short Tour of the Soviet Union on the Way Home, Our Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union Delegation Sets Off for Home
Author:
Date: 1950-09-24
Page: 4

　　After a Short Tour of the Soviet Union on the Way Home
    Our Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union Delegation Sets Off for Home
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st] The Chinese Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union delegation, headed by Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, participated in the Postal, Telegraph and Telephone International Executive Committee held in Prague, Czech Republic, in August, and the 3rd Congress of the Free German Trade Union held in Berlin. On the way home, they made a short tour of the Soviet Union. The delegation left Moscow by train on the 20th to return to Beijing.

### 返国途中在苏联短期观光后  我邮电工会代表团起程返国

1950-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　返国途中在苏联短期观光后
    我邮电工会代表团起程返国
    【新华社莫斯科二十一日电】以中华全国总工会副主席朱学范为首的中国邮电工会代表团，于八月间参加了在捷京布拉格举行的邮电工人工会国际执行委员会，和在柏林召开的自由德国工会第三届代表大会后，在返国途中曾在苏联作短期观光。该代表团已于二十日乘火车离莫斯科返北京。
