Title: The Central People's Government Appoints General Cao Xiangren as Ambassador to Bulgaria
Author:
Date: 1950-09-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The Central People's Government has appointed General Cao Xiangren as the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of our country to the People's Republic of Bulgaria.

### 中央人民政府任命曹祥仁将军为驻保大使

1950-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府任命曹祥仁将军为驻保大使
    【新华社二日讯】中央人民政府已任命曹祥仁将军为我国驻保加利亚人民共和国首任特命全权大使。
