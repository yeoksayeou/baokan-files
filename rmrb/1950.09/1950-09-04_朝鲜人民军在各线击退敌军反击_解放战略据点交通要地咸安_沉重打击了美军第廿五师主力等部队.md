Title: Korean People's Army Repels Enemy Counterattacks on All Fronts; Liberates Strategic Stronghold and Key Transportation Hub Ham-an; Inflicts Heavy Damage on Main Forces of U.S. 25th Division and Other Units
Author:
Date: 1950-09-04
Page: 1

　　[Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 3rd] The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the morning of the 2nd that the People's Army units on all fronts repelled the desperate counterattacks of the U.S. military and the remnants of the Lee Seung-man puppet army, inflicting heavy blows and continuing their fierce offensive operations.
    On August 25th, about a company (i.e., a platoon) of the Lee Seung-man puppet army, on steamships near Haiti Village (phonetic, South Jeolla Province), attempted to land in a surprise attack. The People's Army repelled them with concentrated artillery fire, killing or wounding over fifty enemy soldiers. The enemy abandoned three heavy artillery pieces, twenty carbines, six radio stations, and a large number of rifles, pistols, ammunition, etc., and fled in panic from the sea.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 3rd] The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the evening of the 2nd that the People's Army units continued to launch fierce offensive operations on all fronts, striking at the stubborn U.S. military and the remnants of the Lee Seung-man puppet army.
    On the southern coast, the People's Army units advancing gave a powerful blow to the U.S. military that forcibly crossed the river in an attempt to stop our People's Army's offensive, killing or wounding over two hundred enemy soldiers, seizing four enemy ships intended for crossing the river, and destroying three enemy tanks. The remaining enemy forces collapsed. Our People's Army units crushed the enemy's attempt to cross the river and continue to harass the enemy.
    The People's Army units on the southern coast broke through the solid defensive positions built by the U.S. military based on favorable geographical conditions, continuously striking the enemy's defense line. On September 1st, the People's Army liberated Ham-an, an important strategic stronghold and key transportation hub for the enemy, and is continuing to advance fiercely. In the battle to liberate Ham-an, the People's Army inflicted heavy damage on the main force of the U.S. 25th Division, the 19th Battalion of the 24th Division, and the 5th Combat Unit of the Marine Corps, seizing many spoils of war, which are currently being inventoried.

### 朝鲜人民军在各线击退敌军反击  解放战略据点交通要地咸安  沉重打击了美军第廿五师主力等部队

1950-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军在各线击退敌军反击
    解放战略据点交通要地咸安
    沉重打击了美军第廿五师主力等部队
    【新华社平壤三日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上，击退垂死挣扎进行反击的美军部队和李承晚残余伪军，并给予沉重打击，继续进行猛烈的攻击战。
    八月二十五日，李承晚伪军约一个中队（即一个连），分乘汽船在海地村（译音，全罗南道）附近，企图登陆偷袭。人民军的密集的炮火予以击退，共毙伤敌人五十余名。敌人遗弃重炮三门、卡宾枪二○挺、电台六部、及大批步枪、手枪、弹药等，由海上仓惶逃跑。
    【新华社平壤三日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二日晚发表战报称：人民军部队继续在各战线上展开猛烈的攻击战，打击顽抗的美军和李承晚残余伪军。
    在南海岸方面挺进中的人民军部队，对强行渡江以图阻止我人民军进攻的美军给予有力的打击，毙伤敌人二百余名，缴获敌人企图用来渡江的船舶四艘，击毁了敌军坦克三辆。残敌溃退。我人民军部队粉碎了敌人渡江进犯的企图，并继续困扰敌人。
    南海岸方面的人民军部队，突破了美军依据有利的地理条件所构筑的坚固防御阵地，不断打击敌人防御线。九月一日，人民军解放了敌军重要战略据点与交通上要地咸安，正继续猛烈挺进中。在解放咸安的战斗中，人民军沉重地打击了美军第二十五师主力、第二十四师第十九营海军陆战队第五战斗部队，缴获战利品甚多，正在清查中。
