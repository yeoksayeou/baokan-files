Title: World Youth Delegation Korean Representatives Hyun Jung-min and Lee Hwan-ki Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-09-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 28th—Hyun Jung-min, a member of the Central Committee of the North Korean Democratic Youth League, and Lee Hwan-ki, Vice-Chairman of the South Korean Democratic Youth League, arrived in Beijing this morning. They will participate in the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation that recently came to Beijing to celebrate the National Day of the People's Republic of China and to visit Chinese youth. This morning, Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, Vice-Chairman Qian Sanqiang, Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, Vice-Secretary Jiang Nanxiang, Xie Bangding, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and representatives from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and various people's organizations in Beijing, totaling approximately 400 people, went to the station to welcome them. Abel, a representative of the International Student Union, Jeon Jong-hwan, a Korean youth representative, and Goralski, a Polish youth representative, all members of the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation who arrived in Beijing a few days ago, also went to welcome them on behalf of the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation.

The Korean representatives received a warm welcome from the welcomers at the station. Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretary of the Central Committee of the Youth League, delivered a welcome speech. He first extended his highest respects on behalf of the Chinese youth to the Korean youth who are fighting against the aggression of American imperialism, and pointed out that the youth of China and North Korea had fought side by side against Japanese imperialism and have always had a deep friendship forged in battle. Today, the leaders of the Korean youth movement have come to visit China, which will further strengthen and consolidate the unity of the youth of China and North Korea. Hyun Jung-min, Chairman of the Central Committee of the North Korean Democratic Youth League, delivered a reply speech. He expressed his gratitude on behalf of all Korean youth to the Chinese youth and people for their active support of the Korean people's just war, and stated that the Korean people and youth are determined to fight to the end and have full confidence in defeating the American aggressors. ### 世青代表团朝鲜代表  玄正民李焕起抵京

1950-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　世青代表团朝鲜代表
    玄正民李焕起抵京
    【新华社二十八日讯】北朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会委员会玄正民和南朝鲜民主青年同盟副委员长李焕起于今晨抵京。即将参加日前来京的世界民主青年联盟代表团，庆祝中华人民共和国国庆，并访问中国青年。今晨前往车站欢迎的有中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志、副主席钱三强，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬、副书记蒋南翔，中华全国学生联合会主席谢邦定，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，以及中央人民政府外交部、中国人民革命军事委员会总政治部、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中苏友好协会、及北京市各人民团体代表约四百余人。日前到达北京的世界民主青年联盟代表团团员国际学联代表艾贝尔斯、朝鲜青年代表全宗焕、波兰青年代表戈拉尔斯基亦代表世青代表团前往欢迎。
    朝鲜代表在车站上接受了欢迎者的热烈欢迎。青年团中央副书记蒋南翔致欢迎词，他首先代表中国青年向为反抗美国帝国主义侵略而斗争的朝鲜青年致崇高的敬礼，并指出中朝两国青年曾经并肩作战反抗过日本帝国主义，一向有着深厚的战斗的友谊，今天朝鲜青年运动的领袖来访问中国，将更进一步加强与巩固中朝两国青年的团结。北朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会委员长玄正民致答词，他代表全朝鲜青年对中国青年与人民积极声援朝鲜人民的正义战争表示感谢，并表示朝鲜人民与青年决心战斗到底，并且有充分信心击败美国侵略者。（附图片）
