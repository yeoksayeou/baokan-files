Title: People of All Ethnic Groups and Circles in Northwest China Protest Against American Aircraft Bombing of Andong
Author:
Date: 1950-09-28
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 25th. People from all walks of life and ethnic groups in Northwest China are protesting with immense anger against the aggressive and brutal act of the American invading air force bombing my city of Andong again. Ethnic representatives participating in the first Northwest Literary and Art Workers Representative Conference, Aiminna (female, Kazakh), Shaditov (Uyghur), Guo Jinlan (Xibe), Dorje (Mongolian), Cai Huajia (Tibetan), Zhu Zhonglu and others, all made speeches, expressing their full support against this new act of aggression by US imperialism. Du Yanqing, Deputy Director of the Northwest Federation of Trade Unions, called on Northwest workers to increase the strength of the motherland through practical actions of production competition, oppose the aggressive crimes of US imperialism, and defend world peace. Luo Yi, Secretary of the Northwest Working Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, issued a written statement saying: "US imperialism is conspiring to expand the war of aggression, but we have full determination and confidence to defend our motherland and world peace. The United Nations should give the American aggressors the punishment they deserve." Troops gathered in Xi'an to participate in the National Day parade unanimously stated that they are ready at any time to smash all aggressive actions of the American aggressors and resolutely defend the motherland.

### 西北各族各界人民抗议美机轰炸安东

1950-09-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　西北各族各界人民抗议美机轰炸安东
    【新华社西安二十五日电】西北各界人民以万分的愤怒抗议美国侵朝空军再次轰炸我安东市的侵略暴行。正在参加首届西北文学艺术工作者代表大会的各族代表艾明娜（女，哈族）、沙的托夫（维吾尔族）、郭金兰（锡伯族）、多尔杰（蒙族）、才化甲（藏族）、朱仲禄等均发表谈话，表示以全力反对美帝国主义这一新的侵略行为。西北总工会杜延庆副主任号召西北工人以生产竞赛的实际行动，增加祖国的力量，反对美帝侵略罪行，保卫世界和平。中国新民主主义青年团西北工作委员会书记罗毅发表书面谈话称：“美帝国主义阴谋扩大侵略战争，但我们有充分的决心与信心保卫祖国与世界和平。联合国应予美国侵略者以应得的惩处”。齐集西安参加国庆阅兵的部队一致表示，随时准备粉碎美国侵略者一切侵略行动，坚决保卫祖国。
