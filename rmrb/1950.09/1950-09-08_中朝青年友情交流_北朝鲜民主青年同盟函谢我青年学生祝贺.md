Title: China-North Korea Youth Friendship Exchange: North Korean Democratic Youth League Sends Letter Thanking Chinese Youth and Students for Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1950-09-08
Page: 1

　　China-North Korea Youth Friendship Exchange
    North Korean Democratic Youth League Sends Letter Thanking Chinese Youth and Students for Congratulations
    [Xinhua News Agency, dated 6th] The North Korean Democratic Youth League, representing the youth and students of Korea, sent a letter to the All-China Students' Federation on August 21st, thanking Chinese youth and students for their congratulations on the great victories achieved by Korean youth in their heroic struggle against the armed aggression of American imperialism and the treasonous crimes of its lackey, Syngman Rhee. The letter also stated that the peace camp headed by the great Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China, including hundreds of millions of people around the world, has given us enthusiastic support, which has strengthened our confidence in victory. The victory of the heroic Chinese youth in the great Chinese Liberation War has further encouraged Korean youth to move towards victory.

### 中朝青年友情交流　北朝鲜民主青年同盟函谢我青年学生祝贺

1950-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　中朝青年友情交流
    北朝鲜民主青年同盟函谢我青年学生祝贺
    【新华社六日讯】北朝鲜民主青年同盟代表朝鲜的青年和学生于八月二十一日致函中华全国学生联合会，答谢我国青年学生对朝鲜青年在反对美帝国主义的武装侵略及其走狗李承晚的卖国罪行进行英勇斗争获得巨大胜利的祝贺。来信并说，伟大的苏维埃联盟和中华人民共和国为首的和平阵营，包括全世界数亿人民，给了我们热烈的支援，更加强了我们对胜利的信心。英勇的中国青年在伟大的中国解放战争中的胜利，更加鼓舞着朝鲜青年走向胜利。
