Title: New Soviet Representative to Allied Council for Japan, Major General Kislenko, Arrives in Tokyo
Author:
Date: 1950-09-05
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 31st: TASS, Tokyo, August 31st: Major General Kislenko, appointed by the Soviet Council of Ministers as the Soviet representative to the Allied Council for Japan, arrived in Tokyo from Vladivostok on August 30th.
    Lieutenant General Derevyanko has been ordered to take up a new post and has therefore been relieved of his duties as a member of the Allied Council for Japan.

### 苏新任盟国对日委员会代表  基斯连科少将抵东京

1950-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏新任盟国对日委员会代表
    基斯连科少将抵东京
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社东京八月三十一日讯：苏联部长会议委任为苏联驻盟国对日委员会代表的基斯连科少将，于八月三十日从海参崴抵达东京。
    德勒维扬哥中将已奉命出任新职，所以解除了他的盟国对日委员会委员的职务。
