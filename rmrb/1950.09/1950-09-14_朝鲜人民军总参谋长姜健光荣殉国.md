Title: Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, Kang Kon, Gloriously Sacrifices His Life

Author:

Date: 1950-09-14
Page: 4

　　TASS, Pyongyang, September 12th: The Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Central Committee of the Workers' Party, the Supreme Command of the People's Army, the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and the Democratic Front for the Unification of the Fatherland of Korea announced that Kang Kon, Deputy Minister of National Defense and Chief of General Staff of the Korean People's Army, heroically sacrificed his life on the front lines on September 8, 1950.

　　Kang Kon had been fighting against the Japanese aggressors since his youth. He was one of the organizers of the heroic Korean People's Army and Kim Il-sung's closest comrade-in-arms. Kang Kon was a member of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party and a representative of the Supreme People's Assembly. Kang Kon was loved and respected by all the Korean people. He gave his life in the just war for the freedom and independence of the fatherland and against the American imperialists and the Syngman Rhee bandit gang.

　　The Cabinet has established a committee to handle Kang Kon's funeral arrangements.

### 朝鲜人民军总参谋长姜健光荣殉国

1950-09-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军总参谋长姜健光荣殉国
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁、劳动党中央委员会、人民军总司令部、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会及朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线宣布：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫副相兼朝鲜人民军总参谋长姜健，于一九五零年九月八日在前线作战时牺牲。
    姜健自青年时起即对日本侵略者进行斗争，他是英雄的朝鲜人民军组织者之一，金日成的最亲密的战友。姜健是劳动党中央委员会委员，最高人民会议代表。姜健受到全朝鲜人民的爱戴与尊敬。他在为了祖国的自由、独立和反对美国帝国主义者及李承晚匪帮而进行的正义战争中，献出了他的生命。
    内阁已成立委员会办理姜健的治丧事宜。
