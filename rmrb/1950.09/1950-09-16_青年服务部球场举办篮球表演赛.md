　　Basketball Exhibition Games Held at the Youth Service Department Stadium

　　The Youth Service Department Stadium is scheduled to hold two basketball exhibition games at 7:30 pm today (September 16th) at the stadium: (1) Trade Union Federation - General Correction Department; (2) People's Bank of China - Student Federation. The location is across from the Beijing Hotel. Tickets are priced at one thousand yuan. The Youth Service Department information desk and stadium have already started selling tickets.

### 青年服务部球场举办篮球表演赛

1950-09-16
第8版()
专栏：

　　青年服务部球场举办篮球表演赛
    青年服务部球场定于今日（九月十六日）下午七时半在该场举办篮球表演赛两场：（一）工联——纠总；（二）人民银行——学联。地点在北京饭店对面。票价一千元。青年服务部问答台及球场均已开始售票。
