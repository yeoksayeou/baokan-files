Title: List of Delegates of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1950-09-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd: List of delegates of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation:

　　Bugala: Head of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Democratic Youth.
　　Aebeles: Executive Committee Member of the International Union of Students, representative of the Australian Democratic Youth.
　　Ekbetani: Representative of the International Union of Students, representative of the Iranian Democratic Youth.
　　Semichastny: One of the Secretaries of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union.
　　Irina (female): Minister of the Children's Department of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union.
　　Fudowen: Executive Editor of the Russian edition of the World Federation of Democratic Youth's publication, "World Youth."
　　Quan Zonghuan: Representative of the Korean Democratic Youth, recipient of the First Class National Flag Order of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.
　　(Two more representatives from Korea have not yet arrived)
　　Wu Chunrong: Representative of the Vietnamese Youth.
　　Golalski: Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish Youth Union.
　　Svoboda: Editor-in-Chief of the Czechoslovak Youth Union's newspaper, "Youth Front."
　　Halmert: Member of the Central Committee of the Free German Youth League.
　　Stephannesc: Member of the Central Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Romanian Working Youth Union.
　　Raducanu: Editor-in-Chief of the Romanian Working Youth Union's newspaper, "Youth Spark."
　　Pearson: Representative of the Hungarian Working Youth Union.
　　Mannafov: Editor-in-Chief of the Bulgarian Dimitrov People's Youth Union's newspaper, "People's Youth."
　　Buchari: Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Working Youth Union.
　　Nazhuodaozhi: Member of the Central Committee of the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League.
　　Sandage: Same as above.
　　Ishkhan (female): Doctor of Qiao Ba Shan University, Mongolia.
　　Gohar: Minister of the Anti-Colonial System Fighting Youth Department of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, representative of the Indian Democratic Youth.
　　Slamet: Representative of the Indonesian Democratic Youth.
　　Tara: Representative of the Burmese Democratic Youth.
　　Kipell: Member of the Executive Bureau of the National Committee of the French Republican Youth League.
　　Mayaleri (female): Member of the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Youth Federation.
　　Dutieno: Editor of the Italian Communist Youth Federation's newspaper, "New Youth."
　　Weiss (female): Minister of the Student Department of the American Labor Youth League.
　　Mark An: Chairman of the Executive Committee of the American Progressive Youth Association.
　　Niterdun: Minister of the Organization Department of the Central Committee of the British Communist Youth League.
　　Mais: Representative of the Unified Socialist Youth League of Republican Spain.
　　Yang Dulu: Same as above.
　　Kiporos: Representative of the Greek Democratic Youth.
　　Mansuri Kazan: Representative of the Iranian Democratic Youth.
　　Moharami: Representative of the Syrian People's Youth League.
　　Bravo: Chairman of the Cuban Communist Youth League.
　　Grom: Representative of the Free Austrian Youth League.
　　Claba: Secretary-General of the Algerian Democratic Youth League.
　　Voight: Chief Writer for the Danish Communist Youth League's Central Organ, "Forward Newspaper."
　　Hamsley: Representative of the Canadian Democratic Youth.
　　Ming Dinning: Representative of the Finnish Democratic Youth.
　　Wolf: Member of the Central Committee of the Dutch Youth League.
　　Days: Representative of the Brazilian Democratic Youth.
　　Momoni: Representative of the African Democratic Youth Congress.
