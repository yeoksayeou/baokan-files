Title: Chairman Mao Sends Telegram to Congratulate the 30th Anniversary of the Founding of the Communist Party of Great Britain
Author:
Date: 1950-09-24
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: The Communist Party of Great Britain will hold a celebration in London on September 24th to commemorate the 30th anniversary of its founding. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram of congratulations. The telegram reads as follows:
Dear Comrade Pollitt and all comrades of the Communist Party of Great Britain:

    The Communist Part of China warmly congratulates the Communist Party of Great Britain on its 30th anniversary. The Communist Party of Great Britain's unyielding struggle against capitalism and imperialism for the past thirty years demonstrates that only the Communist Party of Great Britain is the hope for the British working class to obtain freedom and peace. I wish the Communist Party of Great Britain and all British fighters who are fighting for social justice and international peace final victory.
               Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China

### 毛主席电贺英共成立三十周年

1950-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席电贺英共成立三十周年
    【新华社二十四日讯】英国共产党为纪念其成立三十周年，定九月二十四日在伦敦举行庆祝。中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东特致电祝贺。电文如下：亲爱的波立特同志和英国共产党全体同志们：
    中国共产党热烈地祝贺英国共产党成立三十周年。英国共产党在三十年来反对资本主义和帝国主义的不屈不挠的奋斗，表明了只有英国共产党才是英国工人阶级获得自由与和平的希望。祝英国共产党人和英国一切为社会正义与国际和平而战的战士们得到最后胜利。
              中国共产党中央委员会主席  毛泽东
