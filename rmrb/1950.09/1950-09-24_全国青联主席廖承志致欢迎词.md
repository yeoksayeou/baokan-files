Title: Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, Delivers Welcome Speech
Author: 
Date: 1950-09-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd:

　　Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, delivered a welcome speech at the Beijing Railway Station to welcome the delegation of the World Federation of Democratic Youth. Dear brothers and sisters:
    I am here today with the most excited and happy mood to welcome the world's young warriors who defend peace to Beijing. We warmly welcome the World Federation of Democratic Youth, the banner of unity for democratic youth around the world, to send a delegation to visit our country.
    On behalf of the youth of all China, I extend my highest revolutionary salute to you and the democratic youth around the world you represent. Your heroic battles and diligent work have played a huge role in the cause of defending world peace, and have always inspired us, the Chinese youth, to fight with you for world peace.
    We salute the Soviet Lenin Communist Youth League and the Soviet youth, who stand at the forefront of the world's democratic youth!
    We salute the Korean youth who have won brilliant victories in their heroic resistance against the American aggressors!
    We salute the youth of the people's democratic countries who are working hard to build their motherland and defend world peace!
    We salute the youth of all oppressed nations around the world who are fighting for national liberation, and the Vietnamese youth who are heroically resisting French imperialism! (Attached picture)
    Bucara, Liao Chengzhi, Semi-Chastny, and Feng Wenbin (from left to right) walk side by side out of the platform.

### 全国青联主席廖承志致欢迎词

1950-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国青联主席廖承志致欢迎词
    【新华社二十三日讯】中华全国青年联合总会主席廖承志在北京车站欢迎世界青年联盟代表团时致欢迎词。亲爱的兄弟姊妹们：
    我今天以最兴奋愉快的心情来欢迎全世界保卫和平的青年战士来到北京。我们热烈欢迎全世界民主青年团结的旗帜——世界民主青年联盟派遣代表团来访问我国。
    我代表全中国的青年向你们及你们所代表的全世界的民主青年，致以崇高的革命敬礼。你们英勇的战斗，勤劳的工作对保卫世界和平的事业起着巨大的作用，就一直鼓舞着我们中国青年和你们一起为争取世界和平而战斗的决心。
    站在全世界民主青年最前列的苏联列宁共产主义青年团和苏联青年们，我们向你们致敬！
    英勇地反抗美国侵略者而取得了辉煌胜利的朝鲜青年们，我们向你们致敬！
    为建设祖国，保卫世界和平而努力的人民民主国家的青年们，我们向你们致敬！
    为争取民族解放而战斗的全世界一切被压迫民族的青年们，英勇地抗击着法帝国主义的越南青年们，我们向你们致敬！（附图片）
    布加拉、廖承志、赛米恰斯尼、冯文彬（自左向右）并肩步出月台
                新闻摄影局陈正青摄
