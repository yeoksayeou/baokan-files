Title: ACFTU Delegation to Ceylon to Attend the 50th Anniversary Celebration of the Ceylon Federation of Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1950-09-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th:

　　An All-China Federation of Trade Unions (ACFTU) delegation, including Jin Ziming and Jin Mingyuan, departed from Beijing on September 1st for Hong Kong, en route through India to Colombo, the capital of Ceylon, to attend the 50th Anniversary Celebration of the Ceylon Federation of Trade Unions, which will be held from September 28th to October 1st.

### 全总代表团赴锡兰  参加锡兰工会联合会成立五十周年庆祝会

1950-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　全总代表团赴锡兰
    参加锡兰工会联合会成立五十周年庆祝会
    【新华社九日讯】中华全国总工会代表团金子明、金明远等一行已于本月一日离京赴香港，取道印度前往锡兰首都科伦坡，参加将于本月二十八日至十月一日举行的锡兰工会联合会成立五十周年庆祝大会。
