Title: Beijing Federation of Trade Unions Launches Workers' Radio Singing Contest to Celebrate National Day
Author:
Date: 1950-09-05
Page: 6

　　To celebrate the National Day, the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions and the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station are launching a Beijing Workers' Radio Art Creation and Singing Contest to celebrate National Day. Workers are welcome to participate enthusiastically. Forms include radio dramas (plays, operas, Peking operas, etc.), clapper talks, ballads, poetry, La Yangpian (crank film shows), novels, songs, etc. The content should preferably combine the specific circumstances of production, study, and life with the celebration of National Day. Winners of the competition will be awarded prizes. Workers who wish to participate in the competition must bring their creations or performances to the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station at No. 3 West Chang'an Street before the 15th of this month to register (telephone number is (3) 6003).

### 庆祝国庆节京总工会发起工人广播演唱竞赛

1950-09-05
第6版()
专栏：

　　庆祝国庆节京总工会发起工人广播演唱竞赛
    北京市总工会和北京市人民广播电台为庆祝中华人民共和国国庆节，特发起庆祝国庆节北京工人广播文艺创作演唱大竞赛，欢迎工人同志踊跃参加，形式有广播剧（话剧、歌剧、京剧等）、快板、曲艺、诗歌、拉洋片、小说、歌曲等，内容最好以生产、学习、生活各方面的具体情况与庆祝国庆节结合。竞赛优胜者，发给优胜奖品。凡愿参加竞赛的工人同志得在本月十五号以前携带创作或演唱的节目到西长安街三号北京市人民广播电台报名（电话是（三）局六○○三）。
