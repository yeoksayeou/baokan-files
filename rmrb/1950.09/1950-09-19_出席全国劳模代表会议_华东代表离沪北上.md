Title: Attending the National Model Worker Representative Conference: East China Delegates Leave Shanghai for the North

Author:

Date: 1950-09-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, September 18th: Forty-one labor model representatives from Shanghai, Nanjing, Southern Jiangsu, Zhejiang, and Fujian in the East China region, who are attending the National Congress of Labor Model Representatives of Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers, left Shanghai for the north on the afternoon of the 18th. Ma Yinchu, Vice Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee, Wu Kejian, Secretary-General, Zhu Junxin and Shen Han, Vice Chairmen of the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions, and representatives from Shanghai's industrial trade unions and people's organizations, totaling more than 1,700 people, went to the station to see them off. The representatives also carried a set of exquisitely crafted new-style briquette car models created by Shanghai labor model Zhang Deqing, with the words "Dedicated to the great leader of the Chinese people - Chairman Mao Zedong" written on it; In addition, there were several gifts such as seamless copper pipes, rice thermometers, and fixed-amount postage machines, which were given to the National Congress of Labor Model Representatives. On the evening of the 17th, Rao Shushi, Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee, hosted a banquet to bid farewell to the labor models.

### 出席全国劳模代表会议  华东代表离沪北上

1950-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　出席全国劳模代表会议
    华东代表离沪北上
    【新华社上海十八日电】华东区出席全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的上海、南京、苏南、浙江、福建等地工、农业劳动模范代表四十一人，已于十八日下午离沪北上。华东军政委员会马寅初副主席、吴克坚秘书长，上海总工会朱俊欣、沈涵两副主席及上海各产业工会、人民团体等代表共一千七百余人前往车站欢送。代表们并携有一套精制的上海劳动模范张德庆创造的新式白煤车模型，上面写着“献给中国人民伟大领袖——毛泽东主席”；另有无缝铜管、米温计、定额邮资机等礼物多件，送给全国劳动模范代表会议。十七日晚，华东军政委员会饶漱石主席曾设宴为劳动模范们饯行。
