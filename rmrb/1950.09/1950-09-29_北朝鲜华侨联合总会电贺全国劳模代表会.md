Title: North Korean Chinese Overseas Association Sends Congratulations to National Model Worker Representative Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-09-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 27th: The North Korean Chinese Overseas Association sent a telegram to the Presidium of the National Congress of Labor, Peasant and Soldier Model Workers, congratulating them on their meeting. The full text is as follows:

To the Presidium of the National Congress of Labor, Peasant and Soldier Model Workers of the People's Republic of China: We are delighted to hear that you, the model people's heroes, are holding the National Congress of Labor, Peasant and Soldier Model Workers in Beijing, the people's capital. Our North Korean Chinese Overseas Association, on behalf of all overseas Chinese in North Korea, warmly congratulates the Congress and calls on all overseas Chinese to learn from your great spirit and achievements in building the people's motherland.

　　　　　　　North Korean Chinese Overseas Association
　　　　　　　　September 26, 1950, in Pyongyang

### 北朝鲜华侨联合总会电贺全国劳模代表会

1950-09-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　北朝鲜华侨联合总会电贺全国劳模代表会
    【新华社平壤二十七日电】北朝鲜华侨联合总会致电全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议祝贺，全文如下：
    中华人民共和国全国工农兵劳动模范代表大会主席团：欣闻各位人民功臣模范在人民首都北京举行全国工农兵劳动模范代表大会，我们北朝鲜华侨联合总会代表北朝鲜全体华侨热烈地向大会祝贺，并号召全体侨胞向各位功臣模范学习建设人民祖国的伟大精神和成就。
                北朝鲜华侨联合总会
        一九五零年九月二十六日于平壤
