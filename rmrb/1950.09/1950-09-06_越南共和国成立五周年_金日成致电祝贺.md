Title: Fifth Anniversary of the Founding of the Republic of Vietnam: Kim Il-sung Sends Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1950-09-06
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 5th: Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, sent a telegram to Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, on September 2nd, congratulating him on the fifth anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The telegram reads as follows:

　　On the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the Vietnamese people overthrowing the Hanoi puppet government protected by Japanese imperialism and declaring the establishment of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, I, on behalf of the government and all the people of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and in my own name, extend warm congratulations to Your Excellency and, through you, to all the Vietnamese people.

　　The great achievements made by the friendly Vietnamese people in the liberation war against the French and American imperialist aggressors have inspired the Korean people, who are waging a just war for the national independence and honor of their motherland and against the armed aggression of the Syngman Rhee gang and American imperialism.

　　I wish the Vietnamese people even greater success in their future liberation struggle and in the struggle for peace and democracy.

### 越南共和国成立五周年　金日成致电祝贺

1950-09-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南共和国成立五周年
    金日成致电祝贺
    【新华社平壤五日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国金日成首相，于九月二日致电越南民主共和国胡志明主席，祝贺越南民主共和国诞生五周年。电文如下：
    欣逢越南人民打倒日本帝国主义所庇护的河内傀儡政府，宣布越南民主共和国成立五周年纪念日，我以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和全体人民以及我个人的名义，向阁下并通过阁下向全体越南人民致以热烈的祝贺。
    友好的越南人民在反对法美帝国主义侵略者的解放战争中取得的巨大成果，鼓舞了正在为自己祖国的民族独立与荣誉、反对李承晚匪帮和美帝国主义的武装侵略而进行着正义战争的朝鲜人民。
    谨祝越南人民在今后的解放斗争与为和平民主的斗争中，获得更大的成果。
