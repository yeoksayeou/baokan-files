　　A Peace Appeal from Lidice

　　The women of the village of Lidice in Czechoslovakia, held a gathering in June of this year, commemorating the eighth anniversary of the destruction of their home by fascism and war, and issued the following appeal to women and mothers throughout the world who love peace and love children:

　　We, the women and children of Lidice, today—on the eighth anniversary of the day our village was razed to the ground by German fascist bandits—standing on this desolate field where our homes once stood; we stand beside the graves of our husbands, fathers, sons, and brothers, under the crosses covered with thorn wreaths, and issue this appeal to you, the women and mothers of the world:

　　Never allow another Lidice to happen!
　　Stop the war!

　　Today, in our souls, we still hear the desperate cries of our beloved children: "Mommy! Don't let them take me away!" We held our children in our arms, but they were snatched from our hands. Only sixteen poor, sick children were returned to us. The other eighty-two children, aged from fourteen months to fourteen years, were all brutally murdered in gas chambers; fifty-seven Lidice mothers died in concentration camps after suffering brutal torture.

　　We will never forget these sacrifices of our village, never forget Oradour-sur-Glane in France, and the hundreds of thousands of villages and towns in the Soviet Union, Poland, and other places that were destroyed like Lidice. Remembering the seventy-five million victims of the last war, we cannot help but associate this with the fact that in today's world, some people are once again preparing for mass slaughter for profit. But we are building a world of peace.

　　Right next to this graveyard where the thorn-wreathed crosses stand, a new Lidice village grows as a symbol of life. The new Czechoslovak Republic has given a new, beautiful, and complete life to the survivors of Lidice and all its citizens after the war.

　　Let us unite in the name of life, and we can avoid new slaughters. This is a demand on each of you, because only by uniting our strength today can we prevent the recurrence of the tragedy of Lidice and the terrible catastrophe of war.

　　Join our unflinching ranks, you mothers and women (we are counting on you to avoid the suffering of war), please remember us, and please recognize that a new war will also strike you. Hundreds of millions of people around the world are fighting, and hundreds of millions have united and signed a resolution for peace:

　　"We demand an unconditional ban on atomic weapons, this weapon of terror and mass destruction of humanity.

　　We demand the establishment of strict international controls to ensure the implementation of this resolution.

　　We believe that any government that first uses atomic weapons against any country has committed a crime against humanity and should be treated as a war criminal.

　　You cannot stand idly by! In the name of our tortured and murdered children, in the name of the future of mankind, we ask you to join the worldwide movement to defend peace.

　　Fight for peace!
　　Stop new wars!"

　　(Translated by Di Yuancang from the June 1950 issue of Czech Life Pictorial) (Attached image)

　　On the eighth anniversary of the destruction of Lidice village by the Hitler commando, the women of Lidice held a memorial ceremony for the victims and signed the peace declaration.

### 来自利台斯的和平号召

1950-09-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　来自利台斯的和平号召
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国的利台斯村的妇女们，在本年六月举行集会，纪念她们的家园被法西斯主义和战争摧毁的八周年纪念，并向全世界爱好和平、爱好孩子的妇女们和母亲们发出下列的呼吁：
    我们，利台斯村的妇女和儿童，今天——在我们的村庄被德国法西斯匪徒铲平的八周年纪念日——站在曾经存在过我们的家园的这荒凉的原野上；我们站在我们的丈夫、父亲、儿子们和兄弟们的坟墓旁边覆有荆圈的十字架下，向你们、全世界的妇女们和母亲们，发出这一个呼吁：
    决不允许再发生新的利台斯事件！
    阻止战争！
    今天，我们在自己的灵魂里，还听见我们亲爱的孩子们的绝望的哭叫声：“妈咪！不要让他们带我走！”我们是把孩子们抱在手中的，但他们从我们手中抢走了孩子们。只有十六个可怜的，病着的孩子还给了我们。另外八十二个从十四个月到十四岁的孩子，全在煤气室中惨遭了屠杀；五十七个利台斯的母亲们在集中营中受尽了酷刑死去。
    我们永远忘不了我们本村的这些牺牲，永远忘不了法兰西的奥拉多尔，以及苏联、波兰和其他各地成千成万的村镇像利台斯一样的被摧毁。想起了上次战争中间七千五百万个牺牲者，我们不得不联想到在今日的世界中，还有一些人们为了利润，又复正在准备大规模屠杀。然而我们却建造着和平的世界。
    就在这覆有荆圈的十字架竖立着的坟场近旁，一个新的利台斯村作为生命的象征生长起来。新的捷克斯洛伐克共和国把一个新的、美丽的和完全的生命赋予利台斯村的战后余生者和所有的公民。
    让我们在生命的名义下团结起来，我们就可以避免新的屠杀了。这是对你们每一个人的要求，因为今天只有把我们的力量联合起来，才能阻止利台斯的惨剧和可怕的战争灾难的再来。
    请参加我们绝不畏缩的队伍吧，你们，母亲们和妇女们（我们是指望你们避免战争的苦难的），请记住我们，并且请认清：新的战争也会打击到你们的。全世界千百万人正在作战，亿万人已经团结起来，签了和平决议：
    “我们要求无条件地禁止原子武器，这个恐怖的和大量毁灭人类的武器。
    我们要求建立严格的国际管制，来保证这个决议的执行。
    我们认为，那一个政府，它首先使用原子武器去反对任何国家的，就是犯了反对人类的罪过，就应该当作战犯看待。
    你们不能置身事外！用了我们受酷刑死去的孩子们的名义，用了人类的将来的名义，我们要求你们参加全世界的保卫和平运动。
    为和平而斗争！
    阻止新战争！
    （狄源沧译自一九五○年六月号捷克生活画报）（附图片）
