Title: The First Team of the Central Northwest Visiting Delegation Arrives in Yining, Xinjiang's Uyghur, Hui, Kazakh, and Other Ethnic Groups Celebrate the New Year

Author:

Date: 1950-09-26
Page: 3

　　【Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 24th】The first team of the Central People's Government Northwest Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation, consisting of more than 30 people, led by Head of Delegation Shen Junru and Deputy Heads of Delegation Sa Kongliao and Pengsike, flew to Yining from Dihua on the 17th. Leading comrades from Party, government, military, and civilian organizations in the Yining area, as well as representatives from troops and schools, went to the airport to warmly welcome them.
    【Xinhua News Agency, Dihua, 24th】September 23rd is the New Year festival – the Eid al-Adha (Sacrifice Feast) – for the Uyghur, Hui, Kazakh, Uzbek, Tajik, and other ethnic groups who account for more than 90 percent of Xinjiang's total population and believe in Islam. People of all ethnic groups are celebrating the first New Year festival since the liberation of Xinjiang with great joy. Chairman Bao Erhan of the Provincial People's Government called on all ethnic groups who believe in Islam to celebrate the festival with the spirit of opposing American imperialism and Kuomintang remnant saboteurs, and strengthening the united front and ethnic unity.

### 中央西北访问团第一队飞抵伊宁  新疆维回哈等族欢度年节

1950-09-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　中央西北访问团第一队飞抵伊宁
    新疆维回哈等族欢度年节
    【新华社西安二十四日电】中央人民政府西北各民族访问团第一队一行三十余人，由沈钧儒团长、萨空了、朋斯克副团长率领，已于十七日自迪化飞抵伊宁。伊宁区党、政、军、民各机关首长及部队、学校等代表均前往机场热烈欢迎。
    【新华社迪化二十四日电】九月二十三日是占全新疆人口百分之九十以上，信奉伊斯兰教的维吾尔族、回族、哈萨克族、乌孜别克族、塔吉克族等民族的年节——库尔班节（即牺牲节）。各民族人民都以极愉快的心情来庆贺新疆解放后的第一个年节，该省人民政府鲍尔汉主席号召信仰伊斯兰教的各民族，以反对美帝国主义及国民党残余破坏分子，加强统一战线和民族团结的精神来庆贺佳节。
