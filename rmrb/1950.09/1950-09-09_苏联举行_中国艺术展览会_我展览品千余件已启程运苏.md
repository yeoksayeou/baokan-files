Title: Soviet Union to Hold "Chinese Art Exhibition"; Over 1,000 of Our Exhibits Have Set Off for the Soviet Union

Author:

Date: 1950-09-09
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Exhibits from our country participating in the Soviet Union's "Chinese Art Exhibition" have set off for the Soviet Union. Since the Soviet Union proposed holding a large-scale "Chinese Art Exhibition" in Moscow this spring to promote the Soviet people's understanding of Chinese art, the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government began preparations. The Bureau of Cultural Relics of the Ministry was responsible for collecting classical artworks, while the Bureau of Arts was responsible for collecting modern artworks. By the end of July, most of the collection had been completed, with a total of more than 1,000 artworks. A preview was held in Beijing from July 29 to August 5. The artworks set off for the Soviet Union on August 26. Wang Yejiu and four others are responsible for transportation and exhibition arrangement.

### 苏联举行“中国艺术展览会”  我展览品千余件已启程运苏

1950-09-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联举行“中国艺术展览会”
    我展览品千余件已启程运苏
    【新华社六日讯】我国参加苏联“中国艺术展览会”的展览品已启程运苏。自今春苏联提出在莫斯科举办一个大规模的“中国艺术展览会”以促进苏联人民对中国艺术的认识后，中央人民政府文化部即开始筹办，由该部的文物局负责征集古典艺术品，另由艺术局征集现代艺术品，至七月底已大部征集就绪，共得艺术品约一千余件，曾于七月二十九日至八月五日在京举行预展。现该项艺术品已于八月二十六日启程运苏。负责运送和展览会布置的有王冶秋等五人。
