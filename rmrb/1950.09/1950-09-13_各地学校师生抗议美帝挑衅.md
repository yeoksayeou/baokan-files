Title: School Teachers and Students Across the Country Protest U.S. Imperialist Provocations
Author:
Date: 1950-09-13
Page: 4

　　[Report from this newspaper] School teachers, students and staff all over the country continue to voice their opinions, protesting the provocative actions of U.S. imperialism against our country.
    The presidium of the student representatives of the Capital's North China People's Revolutionary University, the entire faculty, students and staff of Beijing Normal University, the Hui People's College, the Jinghua Academy of Fine Arts, the No. 1 Primary School for Railway Workers' Children, the faculty and staff family study group of Chongde Girls' High School, the entire faculty and students of Anguo Middle School in Hebei Province, the Zhongran Village Elementary School in Qingyuan District 5, all cadres and students of the Northeast Military and Political School, students of the No. 2 Complete Primary School in Siping City, and librarians of the Shanghai Young People's Spare Time Library, respectively held gatherings, forums or issued protest letters, unanimously expressing their firm support for Foreign Minister Zhou's solemn statement, strengthening their studies, and working hard to respond to the aggression of U.S. imperialism. All the students of the No. 2 Complete Primary School in Siping City unanimously believe: The Chinese people have the confidence and certainty to defeat any U.S. aggression against our territory and are determined to liberate Taiwan and defend the new China that has been won with blood over the past twenty years.

### 各地学校师生抗议美帝挑衅

1950-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　各地学校师生抗议美帝挑衅
    【本报讯】各地学校师生员工继续发表意见，抗议美帝国主义对我国的挑衅行为。
    首都华北人民革命大学学生代表会主席联席会议、北京师范大学、回民学院、京华美术学院、铁路职工第一子弟小学等校全体师生员工，崇德女子中学教职员工家属学习会，河北省安国中学、清苑五区中冉村初级小学等校全体师生，东北军政学校全体干部学员，四平市第二完全小学学生，上海中青课余图书馆员分别集会，举行座谈会或发抗议书，一致表示坚决支持周外长的严正声明，加强学习，努力工作，回答美帝国主义的侵略。四平市第二完全小学全体学生一致认为：中国人民有信心，有把握打垮美国对我国领土的任何侵略，坚决要解放台湾，保卫二十余年来用血换来的新中国。
