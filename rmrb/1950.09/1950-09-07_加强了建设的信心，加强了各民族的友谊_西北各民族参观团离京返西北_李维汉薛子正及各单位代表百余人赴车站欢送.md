Title: Enhanced Confidence in Construction, Strengthened Friendship Among Nationalities: Northwest Nationalities Visiting Delegation Returns to Northwest After Departing Beijing; Li Weihan, Xue Zizheng, and Over a Hundred Representatives from Various Units Go to the Station to See Them Off
Author:
Date: 1950-09-07
Page: 1

　　Strengthened Confidence in Construction, Strengthened Friendship Among Nationalities
    Northwest Nationalities Visiting Delegation Returns to Northwest After Departing Beijing
    Li Weihan, Xue Zizheng, and Over a Hundred Representatives from Various Units Go to the Station to See Them Off
    [Xinhua News Agency, 6th] The Northwest Nationalities Visiting Delegation departed Beijing for the Northwest on the morning of the 6th. Those who went to the station to see them off included Li Weihan, Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, Xue Zizheng, Secretary-General of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, representatives from units such as the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government, the Mongolian and Tibetan School, and all members of the Northwest Nationalities Youth Visiting Delegation, totaling over a hundred people. The Northwest Nationalities Visiting Delegation arrived in Beijing on August 8th and visited the construction in Beijing and Tianjin, receiving a warm welcome and hospitality from various organs, organizations, and people from all walks of life in the two cities. They visited industrial constructions in the capital such as the Shijingshan Power Plant, the Steel Plant, the suburban Beijing Farm Implement Factory, and in Tianjin, the state-owned No. 1 Textile Factory of China, the privately owned Dongya and Renli Woolen Mills, the Tianjin Paper Mill, the North China Tannery, and cultural and educational institutions such as Tsinghua University, Peking University, the Revolutionary University, the Mongolian and Tibetan School, the National Beijing Library, as well as the Tanggu New Port project, etc., and participated in a symposium hosted by the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government. On August 20th, all members of the visiting delegation met Chairman Mao with infinite joy and excitement and presented flags and gifts to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and Premier Zhou to pay their respects. Before leaving Beijing, they published their written impressions of this visit to Beijing and Tianjin, stating that they had learned a lot of new knowledge during this visit. They expressed their willingness to bring the insights from this visit back to various parts of the Northwest and widely share them with the broad masses of people of all nationalities. Finally, they stated: We, the members of the delegation, are even more willing to contribute all our strength to build an equal, mutually supportive, and friendly national family, and to build our new Northwest and new China.

### 加强了建设的信心，加强了各民族的友谊  西北各民族参观团离京返西北  李维汉薛子正及各单位代表百余人赴车站欢送

1950-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　加强了建设的信心，加强了各民族的友谊
    西北各民族参观团离京返西北
    李维汉薛子正及各单位代表百余人赴车站欢送
    【新华社六日讯】西北各民族参观团已于六日晨离京返西北。前往车站欢送的有中央人民政府民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉、北京市人民政府秘书长薛子正及中央人民政府文化教育委员会、蒙藏学校等单位代表以及西北各族青年参观团全体团员等共百余人。西北各民族参观团于八月八日到京，参观了京、津两地的建设，受到两地各机关、团体及各界人民的热烈欢迎和招待。他们参观了首都的石景山发电厂、钢铁厂、京郊农具制造厂和天津的国营中纺一厂、私营东亚、仁立毛织厂、天津造纸厂、华北制革厂等工业建设，清华、北大、革大、蒙藏学校、国立北京图书馆等文教机关以及塘沽新港工程等，并参加了中央人民政府民族事务委员会所主持的座谈会。八月二十日参观团全体人员以无限愉快和兴奋的心情谒见了毛主席，并向毛主席、朱总司令、周总理献旗、献礼致敬。离京前，他们发表此次参观京、津的书面观感，表示他们在这次参观中学习了不少的新鲜知识。愿意把这次参观的心得，带回西北各地去，普遍地告诉给各民族的广大人民。最后他们表示：我们团员本身更愿贡献出全部力量，为建设平等、互助、友爱的民族大家庭，为建设我们的新西北、新中国而奋斗。
