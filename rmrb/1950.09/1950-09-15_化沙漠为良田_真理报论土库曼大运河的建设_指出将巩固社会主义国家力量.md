Title: Transforming Desert into Fertile Land: *Pravda* on the Construction of the Turkmen Main Canal – Strengthening the Power of Socialist Countries
Author:
Date: 1950-09-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 14th: According to TASS, Moscow newspapers on the 12th prominently featured the decision of the Soviet Council of Ministers regarding the construction of the Turkmen Main Canal. *Pravda*'s editorial stated: "Shortly after the world's progressive people heard the exciting news of the construction of the large Kuibyshev and Stalingrad hydroelectric power stations in the Soviet Union, they heard another inspiring decision on the construction of the Turkmen Main Canal. People all over the world regard these massive communist construction projects as vivid manifestations of the Soviet Union's peaceful policy and its invincible strength. The Turkmen Main Canal will be 1,100 kilometers long and will irrigate 1.3 million hectares of newly reclaimed cotton fields and 7 million hectares of desert area. This great project is part of the glorious Stalin's plan for transforming nature, which will consolidate the strength of the socialist motherland and further enhance the happiness of the Soviet people."

The editorial continued: "For many years, the fraternal nationalities of the Soviet East have dreamed of crystal-clear rivers and orchards laden with fruit, longing for lush forests and happy oases in the desert. Now, in just a few years, the wilderness will be transformed into a fertile golden valley, and the land covered with scorching sand will become blossoming orchards and pastures. In the vast desert where even the campfires of nomads were rarely seen, modern electric lights, water pipes, artificial seas, and canals will appear. This historic decision by the Soviet government once again proves its unwavering commitment to implementing Lenin-Stalin's national policy, to further consolidate the unbreakable fraternal friendship among the nationalities of the Soviet Union, and to enhance the glory and strength of the Soviet Union."

### 化沙漠为良田  真理报论土库曼大运河的建设  指出将巩固社会主义国家力量

1950-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　化沙漠为良田
    真理报论土库曼大运河的建设  指出将巩固社会主义国家力量
    【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：十二日，莫斯科各报均以首要地位刊载苏联部长会议关于开凿土库曼大运河的决定。真理报发表社论称：全世界进步人类在欣闻苏联建造古比雪夫和斯大林格勒两大水力发电站的消息之后不久，又听到了开凿土库曼大运河的令人感奋的决定。全世界的人民都把这些共产主义的巨大建设工程，看作苏联和平政策及其不可战胜力量的生动表现。土库曼大运河全长将达一千一百公里，它将灌溉一百三十万公顷新开垦的棉田和七百万公顷的沙漠地带。这个伟大工程是辉煌的斯大林改造自然计划的一部分，它将巩固社会主义祖国的力量，并将进一步增进苏联人民的幸福。
    社论继称：许多年以来，苏联东方各兄弟民族都憧憬着晶莹澄碧的河流与佳果累累的林园，热望着沙漠中的茂林与幸福的仙岛。现在，只要在几年之后，荒漠便将变成丰饶的黄金之谷，遍布灼热流沙的土地，便将变成百花怒放的果园与牧场了。在以前连游牧者的野火都少见到的大沙漠里，将出现近代化的电灯、水管、人造海洋和河渠。苏联政府这一具有历史意义的决定，再度证明了它始终不渝地执行着列宁——斯大林的民族政策，以进一步巩固苏联各民族间牢不可破的兄弟情谊，和增进苏联的光荣与力量。
