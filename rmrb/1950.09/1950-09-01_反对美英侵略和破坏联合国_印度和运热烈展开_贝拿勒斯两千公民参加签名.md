Title: Opposition to US-British Aggression and Sabotage of the United Nations: India Carries Out Campaign Vigorously; 2,000 Citizens in Benares Participate in Signing

Author: 
Date: 1950-09-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, 28th: According to TASS, Delhi, 23rd: The *Crossroads* newspaper recently published letters from many citizens of India and Pakistan under the title "Why I Signed the Stockholm Appeal." Rehana Hatton. Muslaim, a mother of three children living in the United Provinces, said: "No mother wants war. Only exploiters and warmongers dream of new wars." The people's poet Romanna Leibdi wrote: "I want peace. In peacetime, people can write and create; they can build, think, and sing of happiness." Barahadas Geupta of Madya Prodesh pointed out: "Today, mankind stands at the crossroads of life and death—the atomic bomb and peace. The Soviet Union defends the principles of the United Nations; however, the United States and Britain are systematically violating the Charter of the United Nations and undermining the work of the United Nations." He continued: "Today, when the clouds of war loom over Asia, we must sign the Stockholm Appeal."

Kazmi of Karachi, Pakistan, wrote in a letter to *Crossroads*: "We are closely watching those Americans who intend to use atomic weapons, those who recognize nothing but the exploitation of the poor, those who extract blood and sweat from the people."

Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, 29th: According to TASS, Delhi, 29th: The *Hindustan Times* reported: More than 2,000 citizens of Benares have signed the Stockholm Appeal, including philosophy professor Bavandas and Supreme Court Justice Fuzali. Narain, the leader of the Socialist Party in Benares, refused to sign the peace declaration.

### 反对美英侵略和破坏联合国  印度和运热烈展开  贝拿勒斯两千公民参加签名

1950-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　反对美英侵略和破坏联合国
    印度和运热烈展开     
    贝拿勒斯两千公民参加签名
    【新华社二十八日讯】据塔斯社德里二十三日讯：《十字路报》顷在“我为什么在斯德哥尔摩宣言上签名？”的标题下，发表印度和巴基斯坦许多公民们的来信。居住在联合省的三个孩子的母亲雷亨娜·汉顿。穆斯拉姆说：“没有一个做母亲的人希望战争。只有剥削者和战争贩子们才梦想新的战争。”人民诗人罗曼纳·莱布迪写道：“我要和平。在和平期间，人们可以写作和创造；可以建设、思想和歌唱幸福。”马德亚·普洛德斯的巴拉哈达斯·格普塔指出：“在今天，人类站在生和死——原子弹与和平的十字路口。苏联保卫联合国的原则；然而美英两国却在有计划地违犯联合国的宪章、并且破坏联合国的工作。”他继称：“在今天战云笼罩亚洲的时候，我们必须签名于斯德哥尔摩宣言。”
    巴基斯坦喀喇蚩的卡兹密在写给十字路报的信中称：“我们密切地注视着打算使用原子武器的那些美国人，那些除了剥削穷人以外不承认任何事情的人，那些从人民身上榨取血汗的人。”
    【新华社三十日讯】据塔斯社德里二十九日讯：印度斯坦时报报道：贝拿勒斯两千多公民，已在斯德哥尔摩宣言上签名，其中包括哲学教授巴汶达斯和最高法院推事弗兹阿里等。刻在贝拿勒斯的社会党领袖纳拉茵，拒绝在和平宣言上签字。
