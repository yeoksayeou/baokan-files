Title: Korean People's Army on All Fronts Continuously Repulses Enemy Counterattacks; Over 1,600 Enemies Annihilated on the Southern Coast; People's Army Coastal Artillery Repulses Enemy Ships
Author:
Date: 1950-09-02
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 1st – The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report this morning, the 31st, that People's Army units on all fronts repulsed counterattacking enemies and launched daring offensive operations.

Enemy air force and ground forces attempted to hinder the People's Army's advance, mobilizing all their forces and firepower, and relying on fortified defensive positions to conduct frantic defensive and counterattacking warfare. The People's Army units that liberated cities such as Waegwan, Gunwi, and Uiseong and advanced southward, as well as the People's Army forces advancing in the Southern Sea and East Coast areas, closely coordinated various arms of the military in their offensive, repulsing or crushing the defenses and counterattacks of the U.S. military and the remaining troops of the Lee Seung-man puppet army, dealing powerful blows to the enemy, and continuing to advance.

People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down two U.S. fighter planes that attacked the Wonsan and Heukgyo (Hwanghae Province) areas on August 28th, and shot down one U.S. fighter plane that attacked the Pyongyang area on August 29th.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 1st – The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the evening of August 31st that People's Army units continued to engage in fierce battles with the enemy on all fronts. The People's Army units advancing on the southern coast repulsed the counterattacks of the U.S. 25th Division, part of the 24th Division, and the Marine Corps, who were stubbornly resisting with the support of aircraft and tanks, taking advantage of favorable terrain, and launched daring offensive operations. From August 18th to 22nd, People's Army units, in operations on the southern coast, killed or wounded over 1,500 enemy soldiers, captured over 100 enemy soldiers, shot down two enemy planes, captured four tanks, five 105mm artillery pieces, ten mortars, six anti-aircraft guns, fifteen vehicles, and a large quantity of heavy machine guns, light machine guns, rifles, carbines, pistols, and various ammunition.

People's Army coastal artillery units on the East and West Coasts bravely repulsed enemy warships shelling our cities, villages, and forest areas.

### 各线朝鲜人民军连续打退敌人反击  南海岸方面歼敌一千六百余  人民军海岸炮兵击退敌舰

1950-09-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　各线朝鲜人民军连续打退敌人反击
    南海岸方面歼敌一千六百余
    人民军海岸炮兵击退敌舰
    【新华社平壤一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部三十一日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上击退反击的敌人，展开果敢的攻击战。
    敌空军部队和地面部队企图阻止我人民军的进攻，动员全部兵力和火力，并依据坚固的防御阵地，疯狂地进行防御战和反击。解放倭馆、军威、义兴等城市后向南挺进的人民军部队和在南海及东海岸方面前进中的人民军军队，在其进攻道路上，以各兵种部队的密切协同动作，击退或粉碎美军和李承晚残余伪军的防御和反击，并予敌以强有力的打击，继续前进中。
    人民军高射炮部队在八月二十八日击落侵袭元山和黑桥（黄海道）方面的美战斗机二架，并在八月二十九日，击落侵袭平壤地区的美战斗机一架。
    【新华社平壤一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部八月三十一日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上与敌人继续着激烈的战斗。在南海岸方面挺进中的人民军部队，击退在飞机和坦克掩护下利用有利地形顽抗的美军第二十五师、第二十四师一部和海军陆战队的反击，展开着果敢的攻击战。八月十八日至二十二日，人民军部队在南海岸方面作战中，毙伤敌一千五百余名，俘敌一百余名，击落敌机二架，缴获坦克四辆，一○五公厘炮五门，迫击炮十门，机关炮六门，汽车十五辆及重机枪、轻机枪、步枪、卡宾枪、手枪以及各种弹药等甚多。
    人民军海岸炮兵部队在东海岸及西海岸方面，勇敢地击退炮击我城市和乡村以及森林地区的敌军兵舰。
