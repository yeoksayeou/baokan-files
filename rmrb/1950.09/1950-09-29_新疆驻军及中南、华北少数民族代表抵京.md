Title: Garrison Troops in Xinjiang and Minority Representatives from Central South and North China Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-09-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 28th. The ethnic minority representatives from the Northwest delegation participating in the National Day celebrations who are garrisoned in Xinjiang, and the ethnic minority representatives from the Central South Region and the Chahar and Shanxi provinces of the North China Region, arrived in Beijing on the 28th one after another. Lin Boqu, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government, Li Weihan, Secretary-General of the State Council of the Central People's Government and Director of the Ethnic Affairs Commission, Ulanhu, Deputy Director of the Ethnic Affairs Commission, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, Yu Xinqing, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Central People's Government, Zhu Zaoguan, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Central People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Liu Chun and Kui Bi, members of the Ethnic Affairs Commission, Yang Jingren, Director of the General Office of the Ethnic Affairs Commission, and representatives from the National Muslim College, the Nationalities Institute, and other units, totaling more than 400 people, went to the airport and train station to welcome them.

　　The Xinjiang garrison troops' ethnic minority delegation totals eleven people. Among them are four Uygurs, three Russians, three Kazakhs, and one each from the Hui, Tatar, Uzbek, and Sibe ethnic groups. Including Lieshken, commander of a certain army of the People's Liberation Army (formerly the Xinjiang National Army), Taibofu, deputy chief of staff of the Xinjiang Military Region, and Cao Danuofu, deputy political commissar of the army. They departed from Yining by plane on the 25th, passing through Dihua, Lanzhou, Xi'an and other places. The military and political leaders of each place personally went to the airport to greet them.

　　The Central South Region's ethnic minority delegation totals thirteen people, including five Miao representatives, three Yao representatives, and one representative each from the Zhuang, Dong, Hui, Li, and Yi ethnic groups.

　　The ethnic minority delegation from Chahar Province in North China totals five people, including three Hui representatives and two Mongolian representatives. Wu Tieshan, a Hui representative from Shanxi Province, and Zhao Luozengnima, a Tibetan representative, and their entourage, total three people.

### 新疆驻军及中南、华北少数民族代表抵京

1950-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　新疆驻军及中南、华北少数民族代表抵京
    【新华社二十八日讯】来京参加国庆大典的西北少数民族代表团中新疆驻军的各民族代表、中南区和华北区察哈尔省及山西省的各民族代表，均于二十八日先后抵达北京。分赴机场和车站欢迎的有中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠，中央人民政府政务院秘书长、民族事务委员会主任李维汉，民族事务委员会副主任乌兰夫，北京市副市长吴晗，中央人民政府办公厅副主任余心清，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会办公厅副主任朱早观，民族事务委员会委员刘春、奎璧，民族事务委员会办公厅主任杨静仁以及国立回民学院、民族学院等单位代表共四百余人。
    新疆驻军的各民族代表共十一人。其中维吾尔族四人、俄罗斯族三人、哈萨克族三人、回族、塔塔尔族、乌孜别克族和锡伯族各一人。包括人民解放军某军（前新疆民族军）军长列士肯、新疆军区副参谋长塔依波夫、军副政治委员曹达诺夫等。他们于二十五日由伊宁乘机启程，途经迪化、兰州、西安等地，各地的军政首长均亲赴机场迎送。
    中南区少数民族代表共十三人，其中苗族代表五人、徭族代表三人、僮族、侗族、回族、黎族、彝族的代表各一人。
    华北区察哈尔省的少数民族代表共五人，其中回族代表三人、蒙族代表两人。山西省的回族代表乌铁山和藏族代表赵罗增呢嘛及随员共三人。
