Title: Sports Meet Registration Ends Today
Author:
Date: 1950-09-28
Page: 12

　　Sports Meet Registration Ends Today
　　Registration for track and field events, group performances, tug-of-war, and other events of the People's Sports Meet has been open since the 25th. As of the 27th, 72 organizations have registered, with approximately 2,600 athletes. Today (the 28th) is the last day for registration. Those who wish to participate in performances and competitions must register at the General Assembly Preparatory Committee inside Xinhua Middle School on Xidan North Street between 9:00 AM and 12:00 PM, or 2:00 PM and 5:00 PM today.
　　　　　　　　（Beijing News Agency）

### 体育大会报名今为最后一日

1950-09-28
第12版()
专栏：

　　体育大会报名今为最后一日
    本届人民体育大会田径赛、集体表演、拔河比赛等项目，已自二十五日开始报名。截至二十七日止，报名者有七十二个单位，运动员约计二千六百人。今（二十八）日为报名最后一日，希欲参加表演及竞赛者，务于今日上午九时至十二时，下午二时至五时前往西单北大街新化中学内大会筹委会报名。
                （京市新闻处）
