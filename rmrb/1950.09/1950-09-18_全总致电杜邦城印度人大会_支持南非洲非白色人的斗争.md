Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends a Telegram to the Durban Indian Congress, Supporting the Struggle of Non-White People in South Africa
Author:
Date: 1950-09-18
Page: 1
　　
　　All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends a Telegram to the Durban Indian Congress
    Supporting the Struggle of Non-White People in South Africa
    [Xinhua News Agency, 16th] The All-China Federation of Trade Unions today sent a telegram to the Durban Indian Congress in South Africa, expressing full support for the just struggle of the non-white people of South Africa. The text of the telegram is as follows:

    Durban, South Africa
    Joint Secretaries A.I. Meer and J.N. Singh, South African Indian Congress:

    We, representing the 12.8 million workers of the People's Republic of China, express our full support for the South African Indian Congress's meeting to oppose the South African Federal Government's discrimination and oppression of the non-white people of South Africa. We wish the meeting success.

          All-China Federation of Trade Unions

### 全总致电杜邦城印度人大会  支持南非洲非白色人的斗争

1950-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　全总致电杜邦城印度人大会
    支持南非洲非白色人的斗争
    【新华社十六日讯】我全国总工会本日致电南非杜邦城印度人大会，表示完全支持南非洲非白色人民的正义斗争。电文如下：
    南非联邦杜邦城
    南非印度人大会会议联合书记Ａ·Ｉ梅尔Ｊ·Ｎ兴赫先生：
    对南非印度人大会集会反对南非联邦政府歧视压迫南非非白色人民一事，我们代表中华人民共和国一千二百八十余万工人表示完全支持。祝会议的成功。
          中华全国总工会
