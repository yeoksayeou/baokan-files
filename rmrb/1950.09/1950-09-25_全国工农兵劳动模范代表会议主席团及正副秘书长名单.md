Title: List of Chairman, Vice-Secretaries, and Secretaries-General of the Presidium of the National Conference of Labor Models of Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers

Author:
Date: 1950-09-25
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: The Presidium of the National Conference of Labor Models of Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers consists of fifty-six members, with three Secretaries-General and Vice-Secretaries-General. The list is as follows:

　　Presidium: Chen Yun, Huang Yanpei, Xiao Hua, Teng Daiyuan, Chen Yu, Li Lisan, Liu Lantao, Zhang Bojun, Li Shucheng, Fu Zuoyi, Mao Qihua, Zhao Zhankui, Li Fenglian (female), Zhen Rongdian, Liu Yingyuan, Li Yong, Yu Songru, Yang Fuchun, Zhao Guoyou, Zhang Zifu, Zhao Guilan (female), Meng Tai, Zhao Lan, Wang Zhentang, Han En, Rong Guanxiu (female), Zhang Shuyi, Ma Liuhaizi, Liu Jiuxue, Li Shunda, Shen Wenhan, Pan Changyou, Liu Dezhen, Zhong Dui (Mongolian), Zhang Deqing, Pan Ayyao, Luo Shufang (female), Zeng Yicheng, Wang Daqiao, Wang Jintang, Zhang Fugui, Liu Qining, Wang Changying, Gao Yumei (female), Zhou Wenbin, Liu Jianzhang, Liu Yuhou, Re Wushi (Kazak), Deng Nanshan, He Guoxiu (female, Moxi), Du Xianyang, Wang Juqian, Ding Zhihui (female), Jiang Weiping, Ma Deyou, Yang Liandi.

　　Secretary-General: Mao Qihua

　　Vice-Secretaries-General: Li Fanwu, Dong Xin
