Title: Chinese People's Delegation in Korea (Photos)
Author: Yu Liqun Deng Qingxian
Date: 1950-09-02
Page: 6

　　Chinese People's Delegation in Korea
    The Presidium of the Korean meeting celebrating the fifth anniversary of the "August 15th" Liberation.
    The entire delegation expresses condolences to the peaceful residents of Pyongyang who were bombed.
    The Chinese People's Delegation presents a flag to General Kim Il-sung.
    Korean trade union representatives present gifts to the delegation.
    The delegation and Korean literary and art workers hold a discussion.
    Korean Vice Premier Hong Myong-hui presents farewell gifts to the delegation.
    The delegation visits a group of American prisoners of war.
    The Korean Women's Alliance holds a discussion with the delegation.

### 中国人民代表团在朝鲜（图片）
于立群  邓清仙
1950-09-02
第6版()
专栏：

　　中国人民代表团在朝鲜
    朝鲜庆祝“八一五”解放五周年大会主席台。
    代表团全体慰问平壤被炸之和平居民。
    中国人民代表团向金日成将军献旗。
    朝鲜工会代表向代表团献礼。
    代表团和朝鲜文艺工作者座谈。
    朝鲜副首相洪命熹向代表团作临别赠礼。
    代表团参观美军俘虏之一部。
    朝鲜女性同盟与代表团座谈。
