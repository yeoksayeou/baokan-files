Title: Soviet Foreign Cultural Association to Hold Lecture Today to Celebrate Soviet Tank Day
Author: Chen Yingnan
Date: 1950-09-10
Page: 6

　　Soviet Foreign Cultural Association to Hold Lecture Today
    Celebrating Soviet Tank Day

    Today (the 10th) is Soviet Tank Day. In order to celebrate this glorious festival, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association are scheduled to hold a lecture at 7:30 PM today at 19 Nanheyan Street. Mr. Anisimov will speak on "Soviet Tank Operators". All those holding admission tickets or long-term Soviet introduction lecture passes are welcome to attend.
    (Chen Yingnan)

### 苏对外文协今日举行讲演会  庆祝苏联坦克节
陈应楠
1950-09-10
第6版()
专栏：

　　苏对外文协今日举行讲演会
    庆祝苏联坦克节
    今天（十日）是苏联坦克节，北京市中苏友好协会与苏联对外文化协会为了庆祝这个光辉的节日，定于今日下午七时半在南河沿十九号举行讲演会，请阿尼西莫夫讲“苏联坦克车手”。凡持有入场券或苏联介绍长期听讲证者，届时均可前往听讲。
    （陈应楠）
