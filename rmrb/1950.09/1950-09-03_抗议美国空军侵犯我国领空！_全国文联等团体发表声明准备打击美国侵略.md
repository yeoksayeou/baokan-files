Title: Protest Against the US Air Force's Intrusion into Our Country's Airspace! National Federation of Literary and Art Circles and Other Organizations Issue Statements Preparing to Strike Against American Aggression
Author:
Date: 1950-09-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency report: The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the All-China Federation of Natural Science Specialized Societies, the All-China Association for the Popularization of Science and Technology Knowledge, the Chinese Transport Workers' Union, and other organizations have issued written statements protesting the provocative actions of American planes intruding into our country's airspace and killing our compatriots. They unanimously demand that the United Nations Security Council take effective measures to sanction this aggressive atrocity by the United States.

　　　　National Federation of Literary and Art Circles

The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, in its statement, called on all literary and art workers to immediately mobilize and publicize the criminal acts of American imperialism in undermining world peace to the broad masses of people.

　　　　Science Federation, etc.

The All-China Federation of Natural Science Specialized Societies and the China Association for the Popularization of Science and Technology Knowledge unanimously stated in their statements that natural science workers throughout China are determined to fight to the end to resist American imperialist aggression and to defend world peace.

　　　　Transport Workers' Union

The National Transport Workers' Union, in its statement, called on transport workers across the country to strengthen transport operations, improve work efficiency, and strive to liberate Taiwan and smash the aggressive actions of American imperialism.

　　　　People's Relief Association Chinese Red Cross

[Xinhua News Agency, 1st] The Chinese People's Relief Association and the Chinese Red Cross recently jointly issued a written statement protesting the atrocities of American planes intruding into our Northeast airspace and killing and wounding our compatriots. The statement said: The continuous attacks on us by the American aggressive air force in Korea prove that the American imperialists are deliberately expanding their aggressive actions and undermining world peace. The Chinese people cannot tolerate this barbaric atrocity by American imperialism. We firmly support Foreign Minister Zhou's serious protest to the US government and the accusation to the UN Security Council, demanding that the Security Council immediately sanction the crimes of American imperialism in invading our country's territory and endangering world peace.

　　　　China Vocational Education Society

[This newspaper] The China Vocational Education Society recently sent a telegram protesting the intrusion of American planes into our country's airspace and the shooting and wounding of our compatriots. The telegram stated briefly:

　　We express our boundless anger and resolute protest against the brutal, illegal, and aggressive actions of American imperialists in using aircraft to intrude into our airspace, strafing our peaceful constructions, and slaughtering our peaceful residents! This despicable and shameless provocative crime by American imperialists only more truthfully exposes their fascist face and their deliberate attitude of being enemies with the Chinese people. This absolutely cannot intimidate us, the Chinese people who have already stood up. Under the leadership of Chairman Mao of the Central People's Government, our people across the country are always ready to fight to the end against any enemy who dares to invade, for the independence and integrity of our country's territory and sovereignty.

### 抗议美国空军侵犯我国领空！  全国文联等团体发表声明准备打击美国侵略

1950-09-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　抗议美国空军侵犯我国领空！
    全国文联等团体发表声明准备打击美国侵略
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中华全国文学艺术界联合会、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、中华全国科学技术知识普及协会、中国搬运工人工会等团体分别发表书面声明，抗议美国飞机侵入我国领空、残杀我同胞的挑衅行为，一致要求联合国安理会采取有效措施，对美国这种侵略暴行，进行制裁。
            全国文联
    中华全国文学艺术界联合会在声明中号召全国文艺工作者立即动员起来，向广大人民宣传美帝破坏世界和平的罪恶行为。
            科联等
    中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会和中国科学技术知识普及协会在声明中一致表示全中国的自然科学工作者决意为抵抗美帝国主义的侵略，为保卫世界和平而奋斗到底。
            搬运工会
    全国搬运工人工会在声明中号召全国搬运工人，加强搬运业务，提高工作效率，为解放台湾，粉碎美帝的侵略行动而奋斗。
            人民救济总会  中国红十字会
    【新华社一日讯】中国人民救济总会和中国红十字会顷联合发表书面声明，抗议美机侵入我东北领空、杀伤我同胞的暴行。声明称：美国侵略朝鲜空军连续不断地对我进行袭击，证明美帝国主义者正蓄意扩大其侵略行动，破坏世界和平。美帝的这种野蛮暴行是中国人民不能容忍的。我们坚决拥护周外长向美国政府提出的严重抗议和向联合国安理会提出的控诉，要求安理会立即制裁美帝侵犯我国领土和危害世界和平的罪行。
            中华职教社
    【本报讯】中华职业教育社顷通电抗议美机侵入我国领空并射死和射伤我国同胞，电文略称：
    我们对于美帝国主义者用飞机侵入我领空，扫射我和平建筑，屠杀我和平居民的残暴的违法的侵略行为表示无比的愤怒和坚决的抗议！美帝国主义者这种卑鄙无耻的挑衅罪行，只是更真实地暴露它法西斯的面目，蓄意与中国人民为敌的态度，这绝对不能吓倒我们已经站立起来的中国人民。我们全国人民在中央人民政府毛主席的领导下，时刻准备着为祖国的领土与主权的独立完整而向任何敢来进犯的敌人斗争到底。
