Title: Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Pays Courtesy Call on Czech Prime Minister
Author: 
Date: 1950-09-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 16th. General Tan Xilin, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Czechoslovak Republic, after presenting his credentials on the 13th, paid courtesy calls on Czech Prime Minister Zápotocký, Czech National Assembly Chairman John, and the doyen of the diplomatic corps in Prague, Soviet Ambassador to the Czech Republic, Silin, on the 15th. (In the manuscript "My Ambassador to the Czech Republic, Tan Xilin, Presents Credentials to the Czech President" issued by this agency on the 15th, the last sentence of the first paragraph "Among those who attended the ceremony was Czech Vice President Fillinger..." should be Vice President = Vice Premier.)

### 我驻捷大使拜会捷总理

1950-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　我驻捷大使拜会捷总理
    【新华社布拉格十六日电】我国驻捷克斯洛伐克共和国特命全权大使谭希林将军，于十三日呈递国书后，复于十五日分别拜会捷总理萨波托斯基，捷国民议会主席伊昂，及驻布拉格外交团领袖、苏联驻捷克大使西林。（本社十五日所发《我驻捷大使谭希林向捷总统呈递国书》一稿中第一段末句“参加典礼的尚有捷副总统费林格……”，副总统应为副总理。）
