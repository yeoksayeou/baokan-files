Title: Northwest Military and Political Committee Welcomes Central Delegation; Central Southwest Nationalities Visiting Delegation's Third Sub-Delegation Warmly Welcomed by People of All Nationalities in Guiyang
Author:
Date: 1950-09-08
Page: 3

　　Northwest Military and Political Committee
    Welcomes Central Delegation
    Central Southwest Nationalities Visiting Delegation's Third Sub-Delegation Warmly Welcomed by People of All Nationalities in Guiyang

　　Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 2nd -- The Northwest Military and Political Committee held an evening party on the evening of the 1st to welcome the Central People's Government Visiting Delegation to Northwest Nationalities, which arrived in Xi'an on August 31st. Yang Mingxuan, Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, delivered a welcoming speech at the meeting, after which Shen Junru, the head of the Central Delegation, delivered a speech. He said: Under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, the people of all nationalities in the Northwest live in a large family of unity, mutual assistance, and friendship. However, we should also overcome the influence of Han chauvinism and narrow nationalism, earnestly implement the ethnic policies stipulated in the Common Program, and strive for the democracy, unity, and prosperity of the entire country.
    The Masses Daily published a commentary on September 1st, warmly welcoming the arrival of the Central Delegation.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guiyang, August 30th -- The Third Sub-Delegation of the Central People's Government Southwest Nationalities Visiting Delegation, led by Deputy Head Fei Xiaotong, arrived in Guiyang on August 14th. The sub-delegation was warmly welcomed by the people of Miao, Yi, Hui and other nationalities in Guiyang. Half a month before the arrival of the delegation, the Miao and Yi compatriots in Mengguan Township, a suburb of Guiyang City, organized a welcoming team to welcome the Central Delegation. Miao compatriots living in Baiyun Township, Guizhu County, and Yi compatriots in Fenghuangshao also came from the countryside to participate in the welcoming meeting held in Guiyang City to welcome the Central Delegation. The Miao compatriots also presented the delegation with a red flag embroidered with the words "World Peace" in gold in the style of the Miao people.
    The sub-delegation held three discussion meetings and four consolation evenings with representatives and cadres of various ethnic minorities in Guiyang City and held a picture exhibition. The delegation also distributed clothes, fabrics, salt, needles, and thread, as well as commemorative badges with Chairman Mao's image, to the fraternal nationalities.

### 西北军政委员会  欢迎中央访问团  中央西南各民族访问团三分团在贵阳受到各族人民热烈欢迎

1950-09-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　西北军政委员会
    欢迎中央访问团
    中央西南各民族访问团三分团在贵阳受到各族人民热烈欢迎
    【新华社西安二日电】西北军政委员会一日晚特举行晚会欢迎八月三十一日抵达西安的访问西北各民族的中央人民政府访问团。西北军政委员会文化教育委员会主任杨明轩在会上致欢迎辞后，中央访问团沈钧儒团长发表讲话。他说：西北各民族的人民在中国共产党和毛主席的领导下，生活在团结互助友爱的大家庭中。但我们还应克服大民族主义和狭隘民族主义思想的影响，切实执行共同纲领中规定的民族政策，为整个国家的民主、统一和富强而奋斗。
    群众日报于九月一日发表评论，对中央访问团的到达表示热烈欢迎。
    【新华社贵阳八月三十日电】中央人民政府西南各民族访问团第三分团，由费孝通副团长率领，已于八月十四日抵达贵阳。该分团在贵阳曾受到苗、彝、回等民族人民的热烈欢迎。在该访问团到达的半个月前，贵阳市郊孟关乡苗族、彝族的同胞，即组织了欢迎中央访问团的欢迎队。住在贵筑县百云乡苗族的同胞和凤凰哨彝族的同胞，也从乡下赶来参加贵阳市举行的欢迎中央访问团的欢迎会。苗族同胞并向访问团献出用苗族人民的风格绣有“世界和平”金字的红旗一幅。
    该分团在贵阳市曾与各少数民族的代表和干部，举行了三次座谈会，四次慰问晚会，举办了画片展览会。该团并将携带的衣服、布匹、食盐、针线及制有毛主席像的纪念章，分赠各兄弟民族。
