Title: Northwest Ethnic Youth Delegation Departs Beijing Yesterday to Return to Northwest

Author:
Date: 1950-09-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Northwest Ethnic Youth Delegation departed Beijing on the morning of the 11th to return to the Northwest. Representatives from the Central People's Government Nationalities Affairs Commission, the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the All-China Democratic Youth Federation, the All-China Students' Federation, the Beijing Working Committee of the Youth League, the Beijing Women's Federation, and the Beijing Student Federation were present to see them off. After arriving in Beijing on August 19th, the Northwest Ethnic Youth Delegation was welcomed and received by various agencies, organizations, and the broad masses of youth in the capital, and they also met with Chairman Mao. In Beijing and Tianjin, they successively visited the Zhongfang Factory, the North China Paper Mill, the Renmin University of China, Tsinghua University, the National Mongolian Tibetan School, the Guomin College, and the Tractor School, and participated in symposiums held for them by various units.

### 西北各族青年参观团昨日离京返西北

1950-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　西北各族青年参观团昨日离京返西北
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：西北各族青年参观团于十一日晨离京返西北。前往欢送的有中央人民政府民族事务委员会、新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中华全国民主青年联合总会、中华全国学生联合会、青年团北京市工作委员会、北京市妇联、北京市学联等单位的代表。西北各族青年参观团自八月十九日抵京后，受到首都各机关、团体及广大青年的欢迎和接待，并谒见了毛主席。他们在北京天津先后参观了中纺工厂、华北造纸厂、中国人民大学、清华大学、国立蒙藏学校、国民学院、拖拉机学校等，参加了各单位为他们举行的座谈会。
