Title: Picture

Author:

Date: 1950-09-25
Page: 9

    This is a poster of the Korean people opposing the American aggressors, which reads:
    "Compatriots! Brothers and sisters! The beastly American robbers are strafing you and your children. Arise! Eliminate these heinous American aggressors! Blood debts must be paid in blood!"

### 图片

1950-09-25
第9版()
专栏：

    这是一幅朝鲜人民反对美国侵略者的招贴画，上面写着：
    “同胞们！兄弟姐妹们！野兽般的美国强盗向你和你的儿女身上扫射了。起来！消灭这些万恶不赦的美国侵略者！血债要用血来还！”
