Title: US Imperialism Actively Supports Japanese Rearmament: "Police Reserve Force" Soon to be Fully Recruited
Author:
Date: 1950-09-18
Page: 4

　　US Imperialism Actively Supports Japanese Rearmament
    "Police Reserve Force" Soon to be Fully Recruited
    [Xinhua News Agency, 17th dispatch] TASS, 15th dispatch from Tokyo: The recruitment of personnel for the Japanese "Police Reserve Force" is about to end. Currently, 45,265 people have been accepted into the force, and the remaining privates will be recruited before September 25th.
    All personnel cards of those willing to join the force have been retained. Fingerprints are currently being taken. It is evident that all those willing to join the force have in effect been registered on the mobilization roster.

### 美帝积极扶植日本武装  “警察后备队”即将募齐

1950-09-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　美帝积极扶植日本武装
    “警察后备队”即将募齐
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社十五日电引东京消息：日本“警察后备队”招募人员的工作即将结束。目前已有四万五千二百六十五人被录取参加该队，兵卒级的余额将在九月二十五日以前募齐。
    所有愿意参加该队的人的人事卡片，均被保留。目前正在按捺指纹印。由此可见，所有愿意参加该队的人，实际上已被登记在动员名册内。
