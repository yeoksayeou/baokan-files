Title: All-China Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters to Regularly Convene National Work Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-09-22
Page: 1

　　All-China Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters
    Regularly Convene National Work Conference
    [Xinhua News Agency, 21st] The All-China Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters is scheduled to convene a national work conference in Beijing on October 5th. This conference will mainly address the following issues: (1) The guidelines, tasks, and organization of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; (2) Summarizing the work of local Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations in the past year, especially regarding the experience of strengthening Sino-Soviet friendship and internationalist propaganda and education; (3) Solving some urgent and solvable problems, such as strengthening Sino-Soviet friendship publications. Representatives from various regions have arrived in Beijing one after another.

### 中苏友好协会总会  定期召开全国工作会议

1950-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏友好协会总会
    定期召开全国工作会议
    【新华社二十一日讯】中苏友好协会总会定于十月五日在北京召开全国工作会议。此次会议主要将解决下列各问题：（一）中苏友好协会的方针、任务、组织等问题；（二）总结近一年来各地中苏友好协会的工作，特别是关于加强中苏友好和国际主义的宣传教育经验；（三）解决一些迫切而又可能解决的问题，如加强中苏友好刊物等问题。现各地代表已陆续来京。
