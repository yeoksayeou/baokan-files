Title: The 51st Governmental Affairs Council Meeting Discusses and Approves the Comprehensive Report from the Ministry of Textile Industry and the Report from the Ministry of Finance on the National Salt Affairs Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-09-23
Page: 1

　　[Xinhua News Agency, 22nd] The 51st session of the Central People's Government's Governmental Affairs Council was held at 3:30 pm on the 22nd. The meeting listened to the comprehensive report on the five professional conferences of the Ministry of Textile Industry by Qian Zhiguang, Deputy Minister of the Ministry, and the report on the National Salt Affairs Conference by Wang Shao'ao, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Finance. After discussion, the meeting approved the above two reports. In addition, the meeting approved a number of appointments and removals.

### 第五十一次政务会议  讨论批准纺织工业部的综合报告和财政部关于全国盐务会议报告

1950-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　第五十一次政务会议
    讨论批准纺织工业部的综合报告和财政部关于全国盐务会议报告
    【新华社二十二日讯】中央人民政府政务院第五十一次政务会议于二十二日下午三时半举行。会议听取了中央人民政府纺织工业部钱之光副部长所作的关于该部五个专业会议的综合报告和中央人民政府财政部王绍鏊副部长所作的关于全国盐务会议的报告。会议经讨论后批准了以上两个报告。此外，会议通过了任免案多起。
