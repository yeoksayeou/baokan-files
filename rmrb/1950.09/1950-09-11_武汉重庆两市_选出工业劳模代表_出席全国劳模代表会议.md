Title: Wuhan and Chongqing Select Model Industrial Workers to Attend National Model Worker Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-09-11
Page: 1

　　Wuhan and Chongqing
    Select Model Industrial Workers
    to Attend National Model Worker Conference
    [Xinhua News Agency, 9th] Wuhan and Chongqing have selected their industrial representatives to attend the National Conference of Model Workers, Peasants and Soldiers. The four representatives elected by Wuhan are He Shigu, an advanced ordnance worker; Liu Qiyu, a model technician; Zhu Jiu, a model female textile worker; and Li Dexin, a model seaman who participated in the Hainan Island campaign. The five representatives elected by Chongqing are Wu Kunshan, Meng Baoyu, Yue Songshan, Yang Rukun, and Deng Nanshan. They are model figures in various aspects such as protecting factories, improving production, and economizing on materials.

### 武汉重庆两市  选出工业劳模代表  出席全国劳模代表会议

1950-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　武汉重庆两市
    选出工业劳模代表
    出席全国劳模代表会议
    【新华社九日讯】武汉、重庆两市出席全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的工业代表均已选出。武汉市当选的四名代表为先进的兵工工人贺世古，模范技师刘启宇，模范纺织女工朱玖和参加海南岛战役的模范参战海员李德心。重庆市当选的五名代表为吴坤山、孟宝玉、岳松山、杨如坤和邓南山。他们是护厂、改进生产、约节物资等各方面的模范人物。
