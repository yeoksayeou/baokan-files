Title: Korean People's Army Launches Heroic Counteroffensive: Heavily Inflicts Casualties on Enemy in Seoul Street Battles; Nine US Aircraft Shot Down in the Imjin River Area
Author:
Date: 1950-09-30
Page: 4

　　Korean People's Army Launches Heroic Counteroffensive
    Heavily Inflicts Casualties on Enemy in Seoul Street Battles
    Nine US Aircraft Shot Down in the Imjin River Area
    [Xinhua News Agency, September 29th] TASS, Pyongyang dispatch: The General Headquarters of the Korean Democratic People's Republic People's Army announced in a war report on the evening of the 28th that: People's Army units were engaged in fierce battles with enemy troops on all fronts.

    On September 27th, the People's Army defending Seoul launched several fierce counterattacks against the dispersed US troops who had broken through the city's defenses. People's Army units engaged in hand-to-hand combat with the enemy. The street fighting caused heavy losses in personnel and equipment to the enemy.

    On the east coast and the Nakdong River area, due to strategic considerations, the People's Army Command has ordered the People's Army units to systematically withdraw to new battle lines.
    [Xinhua News Agency, September 29th] TASS, Pyongyang dispatch: The General Headquarters of the Korean Democratic People's Republic People's Army announced in a war report on the morning of the 28th that: On September 27th, People's Army units fighting in the Seoul area continued to engage in fierce battles with US troops.

    The US military launched a fierce new offensive against Seoul. In fierce fighting, the People's Army units stopped the enemy's advance.

    The People's Army has inflicted heavy blows on the enemy in recent days, causing the US forces attempting to capture Seoul to suffer significant losses in personnel and equipment.

    The People's Army's anti-aircraft artillery units and ground forces have inflicted heavy losses on American aircraft every day. From September 23rd to 27th, nine enemy aircraft were shot down in the Imjin River area and the cities of Nampo and Sariwon.

### 朝鲜人民军英勇反攻  汉城巷战中重创敌军  临津江地区击落美机九架

1950-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军英勇反攻
    汉城巷战中重创敌军
    临津江地区击落美机九架
    【新华社二十九日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十八日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上与敌军进行激战。
    九月二十七日，保卫汉城的人民军对突破该城防线的美军分散的队伍数度发动猛烈反攻，人民军部队与敌人进行肉搏战。巷战使敌军在人员、装备损失惨重。
    东海岸及洛东江地区，由于战略考虑，人民军司令部已命令人民军部队有计划地撤退到新战线。
    【新华社二十九日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十八日晨发表战报称：九月二十七日，在汉城地区作战的人民军部队继续与美军激战。
    美军对汉城发动了猛烈的新攻势。在激烈的战斗中，人民军部队阻止了敌军的前进。
    人民军数日来予敌以惨重打击，使力图攻占汉城的美军部队在人员与装备上遭受重大损失。
    人民军的高射炮部队和地面部队每日都使美国飞机遭受重大损失。九月二十三日至二十七日，在临津江地区和南浦及沙里院两城击落敌机九架。
