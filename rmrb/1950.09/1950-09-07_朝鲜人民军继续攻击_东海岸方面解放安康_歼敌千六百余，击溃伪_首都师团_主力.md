Title: Korean People's Army Continues to Attack; East Coast Front Liberates Ankang; Over 1,600 Enemies Annihilated; Main Force of the Puppet "Capital Division" Smashed
Author:
Date: 1950-09-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 6th - The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war bulletin this morning, the 5th, that: on all fronts, the People's Army units are vigorously striking the American troops and the remaining remnants of the Li Seung-man puppet army, who are attempting to counterattack and hold their defensive positions under the cover of airplanes and tanks, launching fierce offensive battles.

The People's Army units attacking on the east coast are bravely attacking the enemy, who are desperately counterattacking using favorable geographical conditions and solid positions, inflicting heavy losses on them.

In the battles on the east coast, the People's Army units killed and wounded over 1,500 enemy soldiers, captured over 150, and seized 5 rocket launchers, 15 mortars, 25 heavy machine guns, 21 light machine guns, 280 rifles, 140 carbines, 14 vehicles, 5 radios, and a large amount of ammunition.

On other fronts, the People's Army units are routing the stubborn enemy and continuing their offensive.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 6th - The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war bulletin last night, the 5th, that: on all fronts, the People's Army units are continuing fierce battles with the American troops and the remaining remnants of the Li Seung-man puppet army.

The People's Army units attacking on the east coast frustrated the desperate counterattack of the so-called "Capital Division" of the Li Seung-man puppet army, which had been replenished and reorganized, inflicting serious blows on the enemy troops, and completely liberated Ankang, an important defensive stronghold and transportation hub of the enemy (the headquarters of the "Capital Division" of the Li Seung-man puppet army was located here) on September 4th.

In the battle to liberate Ankang, the People's Army units smashed the main force of the "Capital Division" of the Li Seung-man puppet army and seized a large amount of war materiel. One platoon of 57 puppet soldiers, including the platoon leader, surrendered to the People's Army.

There were no major changes on other fronts.

### 朝鲜人民军继续攻击  东海岸方面解放安康  歼敌千六百余，击溃伪“首都师团”主力

1950-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军继续攻击
    东海岸方面解放安康
    歼敌千六百余，击溃伪“首都师团”主力
    【新华社平壤六日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部五日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上，强有力地打击在飞机和坦克掩护下试图反击以守住自己防御阵地的美军部队和李承晚残余伪军，展开着猛烈的攻击战。
    在东海岸方面进攻的人民军部队，勇敢地攻击利用有利地理条件和坚固的阵地拼死进行反攻的敌人，使他们遭受重大损失。
    人民军部队在东海岸方面战斗中，毙、伤敌军一千五百余名，俘虏一百五十余名，缴获火箭炮五门、迫击炮十五门、重机枪二十五挺、轻机枪二十一挺、步枪二百八十支、卡宾枪一百四十支、汽车十四辆、电台五部及大量弹药。
    在其他战线，人民军部队击溃顽抗的敌军，继续进攻中。
    【新华社平壤六日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部五日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上与美军部队与李承晚残余伪军，继续进行激战。
    在东海岸方面进攻的人民军部队，挫败经过补充和整编的李承晚伪军所谓“首都师团”的拼死反攻，予敌军以严重的打击，于九月四日完全解放了敌人的重要防御据点与交通要地安康（李承晚伪军“首都师团”曾在此设立司令部）。
    人民军部队在解放安康的战斗中，击溃李承晚傀儡军“首都师团”的主力，并缴获大批战利品。伪军一个排五十七名连同排长在内，向人民军投诚。
    其他战线无重大变化。
