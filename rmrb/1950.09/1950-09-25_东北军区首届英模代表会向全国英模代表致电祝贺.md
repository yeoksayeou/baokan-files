Title: Northeast Military Region's First Conference of Heroic and Model Representatives Sends Congratulatory Telegram to National Conference of Heroic and Model Representatives
Author:
Date: 1950-09-25
Page: 2

　　Northeast Military Region's First Conference of Heroic and Model Representatives Sends Congratulatory Telegram to National Conference of Heroic and Model Representatives
    [This Dispatch from Shenyang] The Political Department of the Northeast Military Region convened the first Conference of Heroic and Model Representatives of the entire military region from the 15th to the 18th of this month. In addition to electing 18 representatives from the Northeast Military Region to attend the National Conference of Combat Heroes, Labor Models of Workers, Peasants and Soldiers, the conference also passed a telegram in the name of the conference "Protesting the American Imperialist Aggression" and a congratulatory telegram "To the Beijing National Conference of Combat Heroes Representatives and the National Conference of Labor Model Representatives of Workers, Peasants and Soldiers." The full text of the congratulatory telegram is as follows:
To all representatives of the National Conference of Combat Heroes and Labor Models of Workers, Peasants and Soldiers:
    We extend our sincere congratulations on the success of the conference with boundless excitement and joy, and pay our highest respects to all representatives. You have created brilliant achievements in the People's Liberation War and in production and construction, and have made great contributions to the cause of the people's liberation. You are the best examples for the people of the whole country and the clear banners on the road forward. We will humbly learn from you, further improve our military skills and production labor, resolutely defend the territory of our motherland from imperialist aggression, and strive to complete the great cause of building a new China.
          The First Conference of Heroic and Model Representatives of the Northeast Military Region
                September 18th

### 东北军区首届英模代表会向全国英模代表致电祝贺

1950-09-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　东北军区首届英模代表会向全国英模代表致电祝贺
    【本报沈阳讯】东北军区政治部于本月十五日至十八日召开的全军区首届英模代表会议，除选出了东北军区出席全国战斗英雄及工农兵劳动模范代表会议的代表十八名外，同时会中全体代表通过以大会名义发出“抗议美帝侵略暴行”的通电及“致北京全国战斗英雄代表会议、全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议”的贺电。贺电全文如后：全国战斗英雄与工农兵劳动模范代表会议全体代表：
    我们谨以无限振奋、无限欢愉的心情预祝大会的成功，并向全体代表致以崇高的敬礼。你们在人民解放战争和生产建设中，创造了光辉的成就，对人民解放事业有了伟大的贡献。你们是全国人民的最好榜样，和前进路上的鲜明旗帜，我们将虚心地向你们学习，把军事技术与生产劳动提高一步，坚决保卫祖国的疆土不受帝国主义侵犯，为完成建设新中国的伟大事业而奋斗。
          东北军区第一届英模代表会议
                九月十八日
