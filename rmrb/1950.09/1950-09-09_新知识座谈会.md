　　New Knowledge Forum

    The New Knowledge Forum, organized by the Beijing branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, has been rescheduled to Sunday (the 10th of this month) at 7:30 PM because the Xiehe Auditorium is holding another gathering this Saturday (the 9th) evening. The location remains the Xiehe Auditorium. Vice Minister Wang Xinyuan of the Central Ministry of Light Industry will be conveying "the spirit and content of relevant professional meetings on light industry." Business and industrial circles are welcome to attend the lecture. Those who have not received a notification can pick up admission tickets at the association.
    (Zhen)

### 新知识座谈会

1950-09-09
第8版()
专栏：新知识座谈会

　　新知识座谈会
    民建京分会主办的新知识座谈会，这次因为协和礼堂在本星期六（九日）夜晚另有集会，特改期到星期天（本月十日）下午七时半集会。地点仍在协和礼堂。请中央轻工业部王新元副部长传达“有关轻工业各专业会议的精神和内容”，欢迎工商界参加听讲。没有接到通知的，可到该会领取入场券。
    （桢）
