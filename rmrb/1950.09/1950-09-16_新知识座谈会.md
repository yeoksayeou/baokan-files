Title: New Knowledge Symposium

Author:
Date: 1950-09-16
Page: 8

The New Knowledge Symposium, sponsored by the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, will hold its 87th gathering this evening (Saturday) at 7:00 PM in the Xiehe Auditorium on Dongdan Santiao. Yao Yilin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Trade, has been invited to speak on "The Current Trade Situation." Those from all walks of life who wish to attend the lecture can obtain admission tickets by first presenting a letter of introduction to the Association.
          (Zhen)

### 新知识座谈会

1950-09-16
第8版()
专栏：

　　新知识座谈会
    民建京分会主办的新知识座谈会，定于今（十六）日（星期六）下午七时，在东单三条协和礼堂举行第八十七次集会，敦请了中央贸易部姚依林副部长主讲“目前贸易情况”。各界愿往听讲的，可持介绍信先到该会洽领入场券。
          （桢）
