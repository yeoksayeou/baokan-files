Title: Capital Rivers Release Water on the Eve of National Day
Author:
Date: 1950-09-29
Page: 2

[Beijing News Agency Report] In order to welcome National Day and clarify the water in the Jade Belt River in front of Tiananmen Square, the Tongzi River in the Forbidden City, and the Three Seas River, the Beijing Municipal People's Government's Sanitation Engineering Bureau has been releasing water into the city from the Tieling Gate west of Deshengmen since the afternoon of the 24th. It is expected that the Jade Belt River, Tongzi River, and Three Seas can be replaced with clean water today (29th). The water level of the Jade Belt River in front of Tiananmen Square will also be raised on National Day to maintain a water depth of one meter.

### 国庆前夕首都各河放水

1950-09-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　国庆前夕首都各河放水
    【京市新闻处讯】为迎接国庆，使天安门前玉带河、紫禁城筒子河及三海河水澄清，京市人民政府卫生工程局已自二十四日下午起在德胜门迤西铁棂闸提闸向城内放水，预计今（二十九）日玉带河、筒子河、三海即可更换为净水。天安门前的玉带河水位，并于国庆日提高，保持水深一公尺。
