Title: Sublime Manifestation of Internationalism! Hungarian Medical Team Works Selflessly in Korea, Curing Over a Hundred Wounded Korean People's Army Soldiers

Author:
Date: 1950-09-12
Page: 4

Sublime Manifestation of Internationalism!
Hungarian Medical Team Works Selflessly in Korea
Has Already Cured Over a Hundred Wounded Korean People's Army Soldiers
(Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 9th) The Hungarian People's Medical Team, which came to Korea to rescue the valiant soldiers of the Korean People's Army and the Korean people wounded in the national liberation war, established a field hospital after arriving in Korea in late July. With the cooperation of Korean doctors, the team members, displaying a lofty spirit of internationalism, worked tirelessly, allowing many seriously wounded patients to recover ahead of schedule. As of the 5th of this month, more than one hundred wounded and sick soldiers of the Korean People's Army have been cured by the hospital and returned to the front lines. The friendly assistance of the Hungarian doctors and nurses has greatly encouraged the soldiers, who have all expressed their determination to more bravely eliminate the American aggressors in order to repay the lofty assistance of their international friends.

### 国际主义精神的崇高表现！  匈医疗队在朝鲜忘我工作  已治愈朝鲜人民军伤员百余人

1950-09-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际主义精神的崇高表现！
    匈医疗队在朝鲜忘我工作
    已治愈朝鲜人民军伤员百余人
    【新华社平壤九日电】为了救护在民族解放战争中负伤的朝鲜人民军勇士和朝鲜人民而来朝鲜的匈牙利人民医疗队，自七月下旬抵达朝鲜后即成立了战地医院。该队队员们在朝鲜医师的合作下，发挥着崇高的国际主义精神，废寝忘食地进行医疗工作，使许多重伤病号得以提前痊愈。截至本月五日止，朝鲜人民军伤病员已有一百余人经该院治愈重赴前线。匈牙利医师和护士们的友谊帮助，使战士们获得极大鼓舞，他们都表示要更加勇敢地消灭美国侵略者，来报答国际友人崇高的帮助。
