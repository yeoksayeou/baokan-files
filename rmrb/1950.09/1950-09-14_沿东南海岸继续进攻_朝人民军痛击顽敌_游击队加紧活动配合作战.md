Title: Continuing the Offensive Along the Southeast Coast, Korean People's Army Vigorously Strikes the Stubborn Enemy, Guerrillas Intensify Activities to Coordinate Operations
Author:
Date: 1950-09-14
Page: 4

　　[Xinhua News Agency, September 13th, dispatch from Pyongyang, TASS]: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in its war report on the evening of the 12th that People's Army units advancing along the southern coast engaged in battle with enemy forces putting up stubborn resistance. People's Army units that inflicted devastating blows on enemy forces south of Pohang and in the Angang-ri area, and units currently advancing in the Yongcheon area, are continuing their offensive, shattering the stubborn resistance of the U.S. military and the Lee Seung-man puppet army. In this offensive, the People's Army units inflicted enormous losses on the enemy's manpower and equipment.
    [Xinhua News Agency, September 13th, dispatch from Pyongyang, TASS]: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in its war report on the morning of the 12th:
    People's Army units have shattered the enemy's fierce resistance and are continuing to conduct courageous offensive operations on all fronts.
    In the east coast, north and northwest of Daegu, and in the Yongcheon area, U.S. military and Lee Seung-man puppet troops are resisting the advancing People's Army units.
    People's Army units operating on the east bank of the Nakdong River have broken through the enemy's stubborn resistance, breached the enemy's defenses in many locations, and are continuing their offensive.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 12th, via Korean Central News Agency]: People's guerrillas in Gyeongsangbuk-do and Gyeongsangnam-do are fiercely launching activities in coordination with the People's Army's offensive. People's guerrillas in the Daegu area are constantly attacking enemy forces in the Yongcheon and Gyeongsan areas; at the same time, they are sabotaging railroads, highways, and telegraph and telephone lines between Daegu and Busan, and attacking enemy transportation and supply lines.
    People's guerrillas active near Masan, in coordination with the People's Army advancing towards Masan, are intensifying activities in the Gimhae and Changnyeong areas, seriously threatening the enemy's rear.

### 沿东南海岸继续进攻  朝人民军痛击顽敌  游击队加紧活动配合作战

1950-09-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　沿东南海岸继续进攻
    朝人民军痛击顽敌
    游击队加紧活动配合作战
    【新华社十三日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十二日晚发表战报称：沿南海岸前进的人民军部队与进行顽抗的敌军展开战斗。在浦项以南和安康里地区予敌军以毁灭性打击的人民军部队和目前在永川地区前进的部队，击溃美军和李承晚伪军的顽强抵抗，继续进攻中。在这次进攻中，人民军部队使敌军的有生力量和装备遭受巨大损失。
    【新华社十三日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十二日晨发表战报称：
    人民军部队击溃了敌军的激烈抵抗，继续在各战线进行勇敢的攻击战。
    在东海岸、大邱以北和西北及永川地区，美军和李承晚伪军对向前推进的人民军部队正在进行抵抗。
    在洛东江东岸作战的人民军部队击破敌军的顽抗，在许多地点突破敌军防御，并在继续进攻中。
    【新华社平壤十二日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：庆尚北道和庆尚南道的人民游击队，配合人民军的进攻，猛烈地展开活动。大邱方面的人民游击队在永川、庆山一带，不断地袭击敌军；同时破坏大邱、釜山间的铁路、公路及电报、电话线，袭击敌军的运输和供应。
    活跃于马山附近的人民游击队，配合向马山进攻的人民军，在金海、昌宁地区加紧活动，严重地威胁着敌军后方。
