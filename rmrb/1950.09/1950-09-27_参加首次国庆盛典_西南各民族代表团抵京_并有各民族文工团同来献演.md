Title: Southwest Ethnic Groups' Delegations Arrive in Beijing to Participate in First National Day Celebration; Ethnic Performing Arts Troupes Arrive to Perform
Author:
Date: 1950-09-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: The Southwest Ethnic Groups' Delegations, totaling twenty-seven representatives, along with the various ethnic performing arts troupes and accompanying personnel, totaling one hundred and twelve people, arrived at Beijing East Station by train at 10:00 AM on the 26th to participate in the National Day celebration in Beijing.

Those who went to welcome them included Li Weihan, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council and Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission; Ulanhu, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; Yu Xinqing, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Central People's Government; Zhu Zaoguan, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Yang Hansheng, Deputy Secretary-General of the Central People's Government's Cultural and Educational Committee; Liu Chun and Kui Bi, members of the Nationalities Affairs Commission; Yang Jingren, Director of the General Office of the Nationalities Affairs Commission; and representatives from the Central Nationalities Affairs Commission and other organizations, the Central Institute for Nationalities, the Central Academy of Drama, and schools and groups in Beijing, totaling over four hundred people.

The twenty-seven representatives of the Southwest Ethnic Groups' Delegations represent the people of twelve different fraternal ethnic groups in the Southwest region, coming to their capital to participate in the National Day celebration. Among them are four representatives of the Tibetan ethnic group, four representatives of the Miao ethnic group, three representatives of the Hui ethnic group, one representative of the Yi ethnic group, four representatives of the Thai ethnic group, two representatives of the Zhongjia ethnic group, two representatives of the Minjia ethnic group, two representatives of the Mosuo ethnic group, one representative of the Axi ethnic group, two representatives of the Sani ethnic group, one representative of the Ailao ethnic group, and one representative of the Lisu ethnic group.

The Southwest Ethnic Groups' Performing Arts Troupes, totaling seventy-three people, also came to Beijing with the delegations. They have prepared various programs and will perform in Beijing during the National Day celebration.

Before the 15th of this month, the representatives of the delegations arrived in Chongqing successively from Yunnan, Guizhou, and Kangding, and arrived in Hankou by ship on the 22nd, where they were warmly welcomed along the way. When the delegation arrived in Wuhan, Deng Zihui and Cheng Qian, Vice-Chairmen of the Central South Military and Political Committee, personally went to the dock to greet them.

After the Southwest Ethnic Groups' Delegations, led by Wang Weizhou (Vice-Chairman of the Southwest Military and Political Committee and Director of the Southwest Nationalities Affairs Commission), Deputy Leaders Banda Dorje (Tibetan, Chief Representative of Xikang Province), Li Ruyun (Miao, Chief Representative of Guizhou Province), and Ma Bo'an (Hui, Chief Representative of Yunnan Province) arrived in Beijing, Li Weihan, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council, and Ulanhu, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, personally welcomed them to Changguanlou in the Western Suburbs Park for rest.

### 参加首次国庆盛典  西南各民族代表团抵京  并有各民族文工团同来献演

1950-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　参加首次国庆盛典
    西南各民族代表团抵京
    并有各民族文工团同来献演
    【新华社二十六日讯】来京参加国庆盛典西南各民族代表团二十七位代表、暨各民族文艺工作队及随行人员共一百一十二人，于二十六日上午十时乘火车抵达北京东站。
    前往欢迎者，计有中央人民政府政务院秘书长、民族事务委员会主任李维汉，民族事务委员会副主任乌兰夫，北京市副市长吴晗，中央人民政府办公厅副主任余心清，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会办公厅副主任朱早观，中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会副秘书长阳翰笙，民族事务委员会委员刘春、奎壁，民族事务委员会办公厅主任杨静仁等及中央民族事务委员会等机关、中央民族学院、中央戏剧学院及北京市各学校团体代表四百余人。
    西南各民族代表团二十七位代表，共代表西南区十二个不同的兄弟民族人民，前来自己的首都参加国庆大典。其中藏族代表四人、苗族代表四人、回族代表三人、彝族代表一人、泰族代表四人、仲家族代表二人、民家族代表二人、摩些族代表二人、阿西族代表一人、撒尼族代表二人、爱黎族代表一人、傈僳族代表一人。
    与代表团同来北京的西南各民族文工队共七十三人。他们已准备好各种节目，国庆节时将在北京献演。
    代表团各代表于本月十五日前，先后自滇、黔、康抵达重庆，二十二日乘轮船抵达汉口，沿途备受欢迎。代表团到达武汉时，中南军政委员会邓子恢副主席、程潜副主席等并亲赴码头迎接。
    西南各民族代表团在团长王维舟（西南军政委员会副主席、西南民族事务委员会主任）、副团长邦达多吉（藏族，西康省首席代表），李儒云（苗族，贵州省首席代表）、马伯安（回族，云南省首席代表）率领抵京后，中央人民政府政务院李维汉秘书长，民族事务委员会乌兰夫副主任即亲迎至西郊公园畅观楼休憩。
