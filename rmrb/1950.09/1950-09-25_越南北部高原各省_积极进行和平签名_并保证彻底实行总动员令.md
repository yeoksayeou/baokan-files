Title: The Provinces of Northern Vietnam Plateau Actively Conduct Peace Signatures and Guarantee the Thorough Implementation of the General Mobilization Order
Author:
Date: 1950-09-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam Dispatch: The campaign to collect signatures for the Stockholm Peace Appeal has been underway in the plateau provinces of northern Vietnam for the past few weeks. In Ha Giang and Lang Son provinces, southwest and southeast of the Chinese border, an increasing number of minority peoples such as the So and Meng gather to sign the Peace Appeal. They also guarantee the thorough implementation of the Vietnamese government's general mobilization order. At a meeting in early September, 7,000 minority people in Lang Son province signed the Peace Appeal, including 1,700 from French-occupied areas.

In Thanh Hoa province in central Vietnam, many Catholic parishioners in various counties have signed the Peace Appeal. In early September, Catholic parishioners in the Phuc Dan (transliteration) parish near the capital of Thanh Hoa province held a grand meeting. The local deputy pastor, on behalf of the Catholic parishioners in the parish, said, "We Catholics long for the independence of our country and world peace. The people of the world now hate war, and they will severely condemn and punish the criminals who attempt to slaughter other people with atomic bombs."

### 越南北部高原各省  积极进行和平签名  并保证彻底实行总动员令

1950-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

    越南北部高原各省
    积极进行和平签名
    并保证彻底实行总动员令
    【新华社二十四日讯】越南通讯社北越讯：征集斯德哥尔摩和平宣言签名运动，过去数周间在越南北部的高原各省展开。在中国边境西南和东南的河江、凉山省，日益增多的索族、孟族等少数民族人民举行集会，在和平宣言上签名。他们并保证彻底实行越南政府的总动员令。在九月初的一次集会上，凉山省有七千少数民族人民在和平宣言上签名，其中有一千七百名是来自法国占领区。
    在越南中部清化省，许多县内的天主教教民已在和平宣言上签名。九月初，清化省会附近复丹（译音）教区教民举行盛大集会。当地副牧师代表该教区的天主教教民说：“我们天主教徒渴望着我们国家的独立与世界的和平。全世界的人民现在痛恨战争，他们将严厉地斥责和惩罚企图用原子弹屠杀其他人民的罪犯。”
