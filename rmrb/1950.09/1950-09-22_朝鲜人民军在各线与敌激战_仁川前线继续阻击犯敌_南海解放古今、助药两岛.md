Title: Korean People's Army Fiercely Fights the Enemy on All Fronts; Inchon Front Continues to Block the Invading Enemy; South Sea Liberates Gujin and Joyak Islands
Author:
Date: 1950-09-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 21st dispatch: TASS, Pyongyang, September 20th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced: In the Inchon area, the People's Army units are fiercely fighting the enemy forces attempting to break into Seoul and cross the Han River.
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 21st dispatch: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a battle report on the morning of the 20th, stating: People's Army units are engaged in fierce battles with enemy forces on all fronts.

　　The People's Army units in the Inchon area have blockaded the enemy landing forces and are continuing to launch fierce counterattacks to annihilate the enemy.

　　The People's Army units that liberated major islands such as Wando in the South Sea liberated Gujin Island and Joyak Island, major islands south of Jeolla Namdo, on September 15th.

　　In the battles to liberate these islands, the People's Army units killed and wounded more than 200 remnant puppet soldiers and puppet police of the Syngman Rhee regime, captured more than 50 people, and seized three rocket launchers, nine light and heavy machine guns, and more than 200 submachine guns, carbines, rifles, two boats, and a large number of other war spoils.
    
　　Xinhua News Agency, September 21st dispatch: TASS, Pyongyang dispatch: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a battle report on the evening of the 20th, stating: People's Army units are engaged in fierce battles with enemy forces on all fronts.

　　In the Daegu and Masan areas, the People's Army units steadily maintained the positions previously occupied. The enemy forces launched repeated counterattacks under the cover of aircraft and tanks, but were repelled by the People's Army units, and the People's Army inflicted effective blows on the enemy.

　　On other fronts, the People's Army units successfully repelled the enemy's fierce counterattacks.

　　On September 20th, American aircraft bombed Pyongyang, indiscriminately bombing the city. Many residences, schools, hospitals, and cultural and educational institutions were destroyed, and a number of citizens were injured or killed.

### 朝鲜人民军在各线与敌激战  仁川前线继续阻击犯敌  南海解放古今、助药两岛

1950-09-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军在各线与敌激战
    仁川前线继续阻击犯敌
    南海解放古今、助药两岛
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社平壤二十日讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部宣称：在仁川地区，人民军部队正与企图突入汉城和渡过汉江的敌军激战。
    【新华社平壤二十一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上与敌军展开激烈的战斗。
    在仁川方面的人民军部队，封锁了敌军登陆部队，为消灭敌军，继续展开猛烈的反击战。
    解放南海上的莞岛等主要岛屿的人民军部队，于九月十五日解放了全罗南道以南的主要岛屿古今岛和助药岛。
    在解放这些岛屿的战斗中，人民军部队毙、伤李承晚残余伪军与伪警察二百余人，俘获五十余人，缴获火箭炮三门、轻重机枪九挺、冲锋枪、卡宾枪、步枪等二百余支，船艇二艘及其他大量战利品。
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线与敌军进行激战。
    在大邱和马山地区，人民军部队稳定地保住了以前占领的阵地。敌军在飞机和坦克掩护下，一再发动反攻，但均被人民军部队击退，人民军予敌人以有效的打击。
    在其他战线上，人民军部队胜利地击退了敌军激烈的反攻。
    九月二十日，美国飞机空袭平壤，滥炸该市。许多住宅、学校、医院、文化教育机关被毁、市民有若干伤亡。
