Notice of the Second World Peace Congress
    Passed by the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Congress Committee at the Prague Conference on August 18, 1950

    Hundreds of millions of men and women have united and will continue to unite around the Stockholm Peace Appeal. In the name of these men and women, the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Congress calls for the Second Congress to be held in the United Kingdom, scheduled for November 13-19, 1950.

    The recent deterioration of the international situation directly endangers world peace and places new and more urgent responsibilities on all peace-loving people.

    The defenders of peace are determined to carry out the movement to ban atomic weapons and reiterate their support for the universal reduction of all armaments under supervision, the increase of which sharpens the war crisis and burdens the people with heavy sacrifices.

    The defenders of peace declare themselves against aggression, wherever it occurs, and condemn any country's use of force to interfere in the internal affairs of other countries. They call for an end to such interference, wherever it has already occurred.

    The defenders of peace welcome and support all peaceful steps taken to stop the war in Korea, a dangerous area where a full-scale conflict could erupt. They strongly condemn the massive bombing that endangers civilians.

    The defenders of peace demand that the Security Council address this issue promptly and peacefully, which must include the presence of legitimate representatives of the five major powers on the Security Council and hearing the views of all parties concerned.

    The defenders of peace demand a ban on all propaganda promoting war in any form, in any country.

    We urge all political, cultural, social, religious groups, trade unions, women's organizations, youth organizations, and every man and woman who is passionate about safeguarding world peace to jointly undertake the implementation of these tasks, regardless of their political, philosophical, or religious beliefs.

    We know the enormous power represented by the vast masses united around the Stockholm Appeal, and these masses must become more extensive and more numerous. We have been able to measure the effect of our struggle. On the path, the first step has been taken, and what will follow is: to be able to and must obtain for the people of all countries the lasting peace they expect.

    Such a peace cannot be achieved by tyranny. It will be won by the joint action of men and women of goodwill throughout the world, determined to achieve the victory of public reason and justice.

### 召开第二次世界保卫和平大会通告  世界保卫和平大会委员会执行局一九五零年八月十八日在布拉格会议通过

1950-09-21
第6版()
专栏：

　　召开第二次世界保卫和平大会通告
    世界保卫和平大会委员会执行局一九五零年八月十八日在布拉格会议通过
    亿万男女已经团结，并将继续团结在斯德哥尔摩和平宣言的周围。世界保卫和平大会执行局，以这些男女的名义，召集第二届大会于英国，定期一九五○年十一月十三至十九日。
    国际形势的最近的恶化直接危害着世界和平，把新的更紧急的责任加于一切爱好和平的人士之身。
    和平的保卫者坚决进行禁止原子武器的运动，并再度宣布他们拥护任何一种军备在监督下的普遍裁减，这些军备的增加使战争危机更形尖锐，使人民负担了重大牺牲。
    和平的保卫者宣布他们自己反对侵略，不管它发生在什么地方，他们谴责任何国家用武力干涉其他国家的内部事务，他们号召，要停止这种干涉，不管它已经发生在什么地方。
    和平的保卫者欢迎，并拥护所有为停止朝鲜战事而采用的和平步骤，而朝鲜是可能发生全面冲突的危险地区。他们极力痛斥那种使平民遭殃的大规模轰炸。
    和平的保卫者要求安全理事会及早以和平解决的方式处理这一问题，而这样做必须有五强的合法代表出席安理会，并须听取有关双方意见。
    和平的保卫者要求禁止以任何形式，在任何国家内所进行的一切赞成战争的宣传。
    我们敦促一切政治、文化、社会、宗教团体、职工会、妇女组织、青年组织以及每一个热心保全世界和平的男女，共同担当这些任务的执行，不管他们的政治、哲学或宗教的主张怎样。
    我们知道团结在斯德哥尔摩宣言周围的大量群众所代表的巨大力量，这些群众必须更加广泛，更加众多。我们已经能够衡量我们的斗争的效果。道途上，第一步已经跨出，跟着将要做的是：能够而且必须为各国人民获得他们所期待的持久和平。
    这样的一种和平，非淫威所能为力。它将由决心获取公理与正义的胜利的，全世界善意的男女以他们的共同行动所赢得。
