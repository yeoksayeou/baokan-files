　　Popular Science Lecture
    The Popular Science Lecture co-sponsored by the Youth Service Department and the Central People's Science Museum will hold its second lecture for September, "Coal," at 7:00 PM on Saturday, September 9th, at the Laboring People's Cultural Palace Theater. Comrade Wang Longfu from the Industrial Experiment Institute of the Ministry of Heavy Industry will be the speaker. A film related to the lecture topic will be shown afterwards. Ticket holders are requested to attend the lecture on time.

### 大众科学讲座

1950-09-08
第6版()
专栏：大众科学讲座

　　大众科学讲座
    青年服务部、中央人民科学馆合办的大众科学讲座，九月九日（星期六）下午七时在劳动人民文化宫剧场举行九月份第二讲《煤》，由重工业部工业试验所王隆甫同志主讲。会后配合讲题放映电影，希持有月票听众准时出席听讲。
