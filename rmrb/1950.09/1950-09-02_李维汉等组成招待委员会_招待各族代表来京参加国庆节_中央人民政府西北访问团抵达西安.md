Title: Li Weihan and Others Form Reception Committee to Host Representatives of All Ethnic Groups Coming to Beijing for National Day; Central People's Government Northwest Visiting Group Arrives in Xi'an
Author:
Date: 1950-09-02
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 1st: To host representatives of all ethnic groups coming to Beijing to participate in the first National Day of the People's Republic of China, the State Council of the Central People's Government specifically appointed Li Weihan, Ulanhu, Zhu Zaoguan, Yu Xinqing, Tao Xijin, Yang Hansheng, Liu Chun, Kui Bi, Yang Jingren, Weng Dujian, Zhu Dehai, Zheng Zhenduo, Su Jingguan, Yao Yilin, Xue Zizheng, Ye Lan, Xu Zirong, Zhou Weizhi, Zhou Zijian, Wang Baiping, Wang Tuo and other twenty-one people to form a reception committee on September 1st, and appointed Li Weihan as the director of the committee, Ulanhu, Zhu Zaoguan, Yu Xinqing, Tao Xijin, and Yang Hansheng as deputy directors, Yu Xinqing as the secretary-general, and Yang Jingren as the deputy secretary-general.
    Xinhua News Agency, September 1st: The Central People's Government Northwest Ethnic Groups Visiting Group, a party of more than 50 people, along with the accompanying Peking Opera troupe and film projection team, arrived in Xi'an at 11:00 am on August 31st. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Ma Wenrui, Director of the Organization Department of the Northwest Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Yang Mingxuan, Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, Cheng Bairen, Minister of Propaganda of the Northwest General Branch of the China Democratic League, Ma Xiwu, President of the Northwest Branch of the Supreme People's Court, Ma Mingfang, Chairman of the Shaanxi Provincial People's Government, Zhang Fengbo, Deputy Mayor of the Xi'an Municipal People's Government, and representatives of workers', youth, women's, and students' organizations, totaling more than 70 people. The group will stay in Xi'an for six or seven days before splitting up to visit and comfort the minority ethnic groups in Gansu, Ningxia, Qinghai, Xinjiang, and other provinces.

### 李维汉等组成招待委员会  招待各族代表来京参加国庆节  中央人民政府西北访问团抵达西安

1950-09-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　李维汉等组成招待委员会
    招待各族代表来京参加国庆节
    中央人民政府西北访问团抵达西安
    【新华社一日讯】为招待各民族代表来京参加中华人民共和国的第一个国庆节，中央人民政府政务院特于九月一日指定李维汉、乌兰夫、朱早观、余心清、陶希晋、阳翰笙、刘春、奎璧、杨静仁、翁独健、朱德海、郑振铎、苏井观、姚依林、薛子正、叶澜、徐子荣、周巍峙、周子健、王百评、王拓等二十一人组成招待委员会，并指定李维汉为该会主任，乌兰夫、朱早观、余心清、陶希晋、阳翰笙为副主任，余心清为秘书长，杨静仁为副秘书长。
    【新华社一日讯】中央人民政府西北各民族访问团一行五十余人及随行的京剧队、电影放映队等，已于八月三十一日上午十一时抵达西安。前往车站欢迎的有中共中央西北局组织部长马文瑞、西北军政委员会文化教育委员会主任杨明轩、中国民主同盟西北总支部宣传部长成柏仁、最高人民法院西北分院院长马锡五、陕西省人民政府主席马明方、西安市人民政府副市长张锋伯及工人、青年、妇女、学生等机关团体代表七十余人。该团将在西安逗留六、七日，即分赴甘肃、宁夏、青海、新疆等省慰问各少数民族。
