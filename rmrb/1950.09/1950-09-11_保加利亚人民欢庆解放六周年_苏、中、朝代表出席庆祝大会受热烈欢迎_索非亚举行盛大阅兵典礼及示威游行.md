Title: Bulgarian People Celebrate the Sixth Anniversary of Liberation Soviet, Chinese, and Korean Representatives Attended Celebration Meeting and Received Warm Welcome Grand Military Parade and Demonstration Held in Sofia
Author:
Date: 1950-09-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 10th dispatch: A comprehensive report from TASS in Sofia: The Bulgarian people joyfully celebrated the sixth anniversary of the liberation of Bulgaria on September 9th.

　　On the eve of the festival, the people of Sofia held a celebration meeting at the Sofia People's Theater. Foreign diplomatic envoys stationed in Bulgaria attended. When Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria, Chervenkov, and Bulgarian government and Communist Party leaders, the Soviet government delegation headed by Soviet Marshal Budyonny, and representatives from China and Korea appeared at the meeting, the venue resounded with thunderous applause. Politburo member of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Pop Tomov, reported on the situation of the Bulgarian people's uprising on September 9, 1944. Marshal Budyonny, head of the Soviet government delegation, delivered a speech, warmly congratulating the Bulgarian people on the great achievements they have made in construction. Budyonny's speech was frequently interrupted by enthusiastic applause and cheers of "Stalin, Stalin!" The meeting passed a telegram of respect to Marshal Stalin with high enthusiasm.
　　On the 9th, a grand military parade and demonstration with the participation of more than 300,000 people were held in Sofia. Bulgarian government and Communist Party leaders, the Soviet government delegation, and diplomatic envoys from various people's democratic countries attended the review on the platform of Dimitrov's Mausoleum. Bulgarian Minister of National Defense Panchovsky delivered a congratulatory speech to all commanders and fighters of the Bulgarian Defense Forces, followed by the military parade and demonstration. The parade lineup was extremely spectacular and moving. The procession was led by winners of the Dimitrov Prize and outstanding workers. The working people marched forward holding high portraits of Stalin, Dimitrov, Kolarov, Chervenkov, and leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and the Bulgarian Communist Party, as well as portraits of government leaders and leaders of Communist and Workers' Parties of fraternal countries. Thousands of colorful flags fluttered in the parade, and countless slogans called on the people to consolidate the peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union. Countless charts showed Bulgaria's construction achievements, vividly demonstrating the Bulgarian people's high degree of spiritual and political unity, and their love for the great Soviet Union and Stalin.
　　On this day, the people of Sofia spent the festival in a joyful atmosphere.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 10th dispatch: TASS Sofia: The Bulgarian Council of Ministers held a grand reception on the occasion of the sixth anniversary of the liberation of Bulgaria on the 9th. The attendees included the Soviet government delegation composed of Soviet Marshal Budyonny, Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Bogomolov, and Soviet Ambassador to Bulgaria Podrov, delegations and diplomatic envoys from various people's democratic countries, Dimitrov Prize winners in Bulgaria, well-known scientists, artists, and representatives of various people's organizations, and many others. Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria, Chervenkov, members of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, and government leaders warmly received the guests. The reception proceeded in a warm atmosphere, and the guests and hosts enjoyed themselves before departing.

### 保加利亚人民欢庆解放六周年  苏、中、朝代表出席庆祝大会受热烈欢迎  索非亚举行盛大阅兵典礼及示威游行

1950-09-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　保加利亚人民欢庆解放六周年
    苏、中、朝代表出席庆祝大会受热烈欢迎
    索非亚举行盛大阅兵典礼及示威游行
    【新华社十日讯】综合塔斯社索非亚消息：保加利亚人民欢腾鼓舞地庆祝了九月九日保加利亚解放六周年纪念日。
    在节日前夕，索非亚人民在索非亚人民剧院举行了庆祝大会。各国驻保的外交使节均往参加。当保加利亚部长会议主席契尔文科夫和保加利亚政府与共产党领袖，以苏联元帅布琼尼为首的苏联政府代表团，和中国、朝鲜的代表在会上出现时，会场上响彻了雷鸣般的掌声。保共中央委员会政治局委员、部长会议副主席波普托莫夫在会上报告了一九四四年九月九日保加利亚人民起义的情形。苏联政府代表团团长布琼尼元帅发表演说，热烈祝贺保加利亚人民在建设中获得的伟大成就。布琼尼的演说不时为热烈的掌声和“斯大林、斯大林！”的欢呼声所打断。大会以高涨的热情通过致电斯大林大元帅致敬。
    九日，索非亚举行了三十余万人参加的盛大阅兵典礼及示威游行。保加利亚政府与共产党的领袖，苏联政府代表团及各人民民主国家的外交使节均在季米特洛夫的陵墓台上参加检阅。保加利亚国防部长潘琪夫斯基向保加利亚国防军的全体指战员发表了祝贺演说后，即举行了阅兵及游行。游行的阵容极为壮观动人。行列以季米特洛夫奖金获得者及优秀的劳动者为先导。劳动人民高举着斯大林、季米特洛夫、科拉罗夫、契尔文科夫和苏联共产党领袖及保共领袖的画像，高举着各兄弟国家政府领袖及共产党与工人党领袖的画像前进。成千成万的彩色旗帜在游行队伍中飘扬，无数的标语牌号召人民巩固以苏联为首的和平民主阵营，无数的图表显示了保加利亚的建设成绩，动人地说明了保加利亚人民精神上与政治上高度的团结，和他们对于伟大的苏联和斯大林的热爱。
    在这一天，索非亚人民在欢乐气氛中度过了佳节。
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社索非亚讯：保加利亚部长会议于九日保加利亚解放六周年纪念日举行盛大招待会。出席招待会的有以苏联元帅布琼尼、苏联外交部副部长波格莫洛夫及苏联驻保加利亚大使保德罗夫组成的苏联政府代表团，各人民民主国家的代表团与外交使节，保加利亚的季米特洛夫奖金获得者、著名的科学家、艺术家和各人民团体的代表等多人。保加利亚部长会议主席契尔文科夫、保加利亚共产党中央委员会各委员及政府领袖殷勤招待了来宾。招待会在热烈的气氛中进行，宾主尽欢始散。
