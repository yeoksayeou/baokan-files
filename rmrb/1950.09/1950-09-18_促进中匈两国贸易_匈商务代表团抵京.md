Title: Promoting Trade Between China and Hungary: Hungarian Business Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-09-18
Page: 1
　　
    To Promote Trade Between China and Hungary
    Hungarian Business Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, 17th] To promote trade between China and the People's Republic of Hungary, with the consent of our Central People's Government, Hungary has dispatched a business delegation led by Dr. Sarusi-Szűts to China for trade talks. The delegation arrived in Beijing by train this morning, the 17th. Those who went to welcome them included Jia Shi, Deputy Director of the Foreign Trade Department of our Ministry of Trade, Han Xu, Chief of the Reception Section of the Reception Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Fekete, Counselor of the Hungarian Embassy in China, and László, Commercial Counselor.

### 促进中匈两国贸易  匈商务代表团抵京

1950-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　促进中匈两国贸易
    匈商务代表团抵京
    【新华社十七日讯】为了促进中匈两国贸易，匈牙利人民共和国于取得我中央人民政府同意后，已派遣由沙鲁辛斯基博士率领之商务代表团来华商谈。该团已于十七日晨乘车抵京。前往欢迎者有我国贸易部国外贸易司副司长贾石，外交部交际处交际科科长韩叙，匈牙利驻华大使馆参赞费凯尔特，商务参赞拉兹罗等。
