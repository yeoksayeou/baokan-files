Title: Welcoming World Peace Day, Central Academy of Drama to Stage "Peace Dove"
Author:
Date: 1950-09-24
Page: 10

　　Welcoming World Peace Day
    Central Academy of Drama to Stage "Peace Dove"
    In order to cooperate with the enormous development of the World Peace Movement, strengthen the publicity of the Peace Movement, and welcome the great World Day of Struggle for Peace, the Central Academy of Drama will stage the large-scale dance drama "Peace Dove." This dance drama uses doves to symbolize peace, showing the progressive people of the world uniting to wage a resolute struggle against the warmongers in order to safeguard lasting world peace and people's democracy. The editor is Ouyang Yuqian. The entire dance troupe will perform, starring Dai Ailian. Those responsible for directing individual scenes are Dai Ailian, Zhao Yunge, Chen Jinqing, Ding Ning, Wang Ping, Gao Dian, etc. Ouyang Yuqian is the general director. In terms of music, Zhang Yan, Ma Ke, Liu Shixin, Zhang Dinghe, Chen Zi, Liu Chi, Bian Jun, Du Yu, Liu Hanzhang, Yang Bihai and others are collectively composing. The rehearsals are now initially ready, and performances will begin on October 2nd, the International Day of Struggle for Peace, in Beijing and Tianjin.
            (Central Academy of Drama Communications Team)

### 迎接世界和平日  中央戏剧学院将上演“和平鸽”

1950-09-24
第10版()
专栏：

　　迎接世界和平日
    中央戏剧学院将上演“和平鸽”
    为了配合世界和平运动的巨大开展，加强和平运动的宣传，迎接伟大的世界和平斗争日，中央戏剧学院将上演大型舞剧“和平鸽”，该舞剧以鸽子象征和平，表现世界进步的人民，团结一致，向战争贩子作坚决的斗争，以保卫世界持久和平与人民民主。编者欧阳予倩，由舞蹈团全体演出，戴爱莲主演，分场负责导演者为戴爱莲、赵郓哥、陈锦清、丁宁、王萍、高第安等，欧阳予倩为总导演，音乐方面，由章彦、马可、刘式昕、张定和、陈紫、刘炽、边军、杜宇、刘汉章、杨碧海等集体作曲，现排演已初步就绪，将于十月二日国际和平斗争日起，在京津演出。
            （中央戏剧学院通讯组）
