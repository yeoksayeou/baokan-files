New Democracy Lecture

The New Democracy Lecture series, sponsored by the Beijing YMCA, will hold its 99th lecture on September 17th (Sunday) at 4:00 PM. We have invited Mr. Ma Jian, an expert on ethnic issues and professor at Peking University, to speak on "The Religious Beliefs and Customs of the Hui People." All are welcome to attend.

### 新民主主义讲座

1950-09-16
第8版()
专栏：

　　新民主主义讲座
    京市青年会主办之新民主主义讲座，第九十九讲定九月十七日（星期日）下午四时举行。请民族问题专家、北大教授马坚先生讲：“回民的宗教信仰和风俗习惯”。欢迎各界前往听讲。
