Title: German Peace Movement Thrives: Over 20 Million Signatures, 50,000 Peace Committees Established Nationwide
Author:
Date: 1950-09-19
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 18th: TASS reports: The German Peace Committee met on the 15th to discuss the German Peace Congress, scheduled to be held in Berlin from November 4th to 5th. The congress will elect the German delegation to attend the Second World Peace Congress and create a new All-German Peace Committee.

　　The General Secretary of the German Peace Committee, Willmann, reported to the committee that, to date, 20.5 million people throughout Germany have signed the Stockholm Appeal. In West Germany, despite police persecution, over 2 million people have signed the Stockholm Appeal. West German peace advocates are planning to collect at least another 500,000 signatures by the opening of the German Peace Congress.

　　The Chairman of the Free German Trade Union Federation, Herbert Warnke, declared that nearly 50,000 peace committees have been established in Germany.

### 德国和平运动蓬勃展开  二千余万人已签名  全国已建立五万个和平委员会

1950-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　德国和平运动蓬勃展开
    二千余万人已签名
    全国已建立五万个和平委员会
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社柏林讯：德国和平委员会曾于十五日开会，讨论定于十一月四日至五日在柏林召开德国和平大会事宜。大会将选出出席第二届世界和平大会的德国代表团，并产生新的全德和平委员会。
    德国和平委员会总书记威尔曼向委员会报告：德国全境截至目前止，已有二千零五十万人在斯德哥尔摩宣言上签名。在西德，不顾警察的迫害，在斯德哥尔摩宣言上签名的已经超过二百万人。西德和平拥护者们正计划在此次德国和平大会揭幕的时候，至少再征集五十万人签名。
    自由德国工会主席赫伯特·瓦恩克宣称：在德国已经建立将近五万个和平委员会。
