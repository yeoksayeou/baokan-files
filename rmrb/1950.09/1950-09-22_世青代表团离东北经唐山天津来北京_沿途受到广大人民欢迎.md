Title: World Youth Delegation Leaves Northeast China, Passes Through Tangshan and Tianjin on Way to Beijing, Receives Warm Welcome from the People Along the Way
Author:
Date: 1950-09-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 21st – The World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation, headed by Bugala, arrived in Xiongyue Town, Gaiping County from Dalian at 7:00 AM on the 20th. After visiting the Liaodong Provincial Agricultural Experiment Station, they took a train that evening via Shenyang to Beijing.

When the delegation left Dalian, Han Guang, Director of the Lüda People's Administrative Office, Qiao Chuanjue, Deputy Director, Ouyang Qin, Secretary of the CPC Lüda District Committee, and Liu Nansheng, Secretary of the Lüda District Committee of the Communist Youth League, were all at the station to see them off. After Liu Nansheng delivered a farewell speech, Golarzski, one of the secretaries of the Central Committee of the Polish Youth Union, responded, expressing sincere thanks for the warm reception from the people of Dalian. Golarzski said: Although the people of Dalian were among the first to be oppressed and enslaved by Japanese imperialism, after liberation they took on the task of building an advanced region of the new China. He hoped that the people of Lüda would create even greater achievements in peaceful and democratic construction.

The delegation's special train received a warm welcome from the local people when it passed through Wafangdian.

When the delegation arrived at Xiongyue Station, it was welcomed by more than 2,000 people. Jin Zhaoye, Director of the Liaodong Provincial People's Government, Liu Cikai, member of the CPC Liaodong Provincial Committee, and Yang Haibo, Secretary of the Liaodong Provincial Committee of the Communist Youth League, all rushed from Andong to welcome them. Yang Haibo first paid tribute to the delegation on behalf of the youth of Liaodong, and denounced the atrocities of American planes repeatedly and unreasonably strafing the peaceful people of Liaodong. The Iranian representative pointed out in his speech that although American imperialism is trying to expand its aggression, the world's peace and democracy forces, led by the Soviet Union, are growing stronger, and we will surely achieve final victory. Grome, the representative of Free Austria, said that after returning to his country, he must inform the Austrian people about the enthusiastic work and study of the Chinese people for their motherland.

The representatives visited the agricultural experiment station, which covers an area of 1,600 mu, in Xiongyue Town. They praised the apples in the orchard and the lush rice and cotton crops in the nursery. The delegation left Xiongyue Town by train at 9:30 PM that evening via Shenyang to Beijing.

[This newspaper, Tangshan news] The World Federation of Youth Delegation arrived in Tangshan at 5:40 PM on the 21st. More than 8,000 people, including heads of various agencies in Tangshan and young workers, held a grand welcome meeting at the railway station square. Zhang Wenqi, Deputy Mayor of Tangshan, and Shi Yi, Secretary of the Tangshan Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, delivered speeches, warmly welcoming the delegation. Sandag, the Mongolian youth representative, and Winkbetani, the Iranian youth representative, then delivered speeches. They all paid tribute to the Chinese people and the people of Tangshan with great enthusiasm. They stated in their speeches that young people all over the world are firmly united under the banner of the Soviet Union, the world's powerful fortress of peace, to oppose the aggressive camp headed by American imperialism, and have the confidence and strength to eliminate war and strive for lasting world peace. At 6:25, amid enthusiastic cheers and slogans, the delegation boarded the train and left Tangshan for Tianjin.

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 21st – The World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation, consisting of more than 40 people, arrived in Tianjin by train from Dalian at 9:00 PM on the 21st after visiting major cities in Northeast China. Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Huang Jing, Mayor of Tianjin, Zhou Shutao, Deputy Mayor, Huang Huoqing, Chairman of the Tianjin Federation of Trade Unions, He Qijun, Chairman of the Tianjin Democratic Youth Federation, as well as young workers, students, and soldiers, totaling more than 1,000 people. Tang Pingke, Acting Consul General of the Soviet Consulate General in Tianjin, and Levin, a representative of Soviet youth living in Tianjin, also went to welcome them.

The representatives got off the train amid shouts of "Long Live the Solidarity of World Youth" and "Long Live World Peace" and warm applause. At the welcome meeting held in front of the station, Mayor Huang Jing and He Qijun, Chairman of the Municipal Youth Federation, delivered welcome speeches. Goha, the Indian youth representative of the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation, and Mannavov, the Bulgarian representative, delivered reply speeches.

### 世青代表团离东北经唐山天津来北京  沿途受到广大人民欢迎

1950-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　世青代表团离东北经唐山天津来北京
    沿途受到广大人民欢迎
    【新华社沈阳二十一日电】以布加拉为首的世界民主青年联盟代表团于二十日上午七时自大连抵盖平县熊岳镇，参观辽东省立农事试验场后，当晚乘车经沈阳转道赴北京。
    代表团离大连时，旅大人民行署主任韩光、副主任乔传珏、中共旅大区党委书记欧阳钦、青年团旅大区团委书记刘南生等都到车站欢送。刘南生致欢送词后，波兰青年联盟中央委员会书记之一戈拉尔斯基代表致答词，对大连人民的热诚款待表示亲切的谢意。戈拉尔斯基说：大连人民虽然最早受到日本帝国主义的压迫和奴役，但解放后他们即担负起了建设新中国先进地区的工作。他希望旅大人民在和平民主建设中创造更大成就。
    代表团专车行经瓦房店时受到当地人民的热烈欢迎。
    代表团抵达熊岳站时，受到二千余群众的欢迎。辽东省人民政府金肇野厅长、中共辽东省委委员刘慈凯、青年团辽东省委书记杨海波等都从安东赶来欢迎。杨海波首先代表辽东青年向代表团致敬，并控诉美机一再无理扫射辽东和平人民的暴行。伊朗代表在致词中指出：美帝国主义虽力图扩大侵略，但是以苏联为首的世界和平民主力量壮大起来了，我们一定能取得最后胜利。自由奥地利代表葛罗姆表示，他回国后一定要把中国人民正在热情地为祖国工作和学习的情形，告知奥国人民。
    代表们在熊岳镇参观了占地一千六百亩的农事试验场。他们对果园里的苹果和苗圃里茂壮的水稻、棉花等作物，都表示赞扬。代表团于当晚九时半乘车离熊岳镇经沈阳赴北京。
    【本报唐山讯】世界青联代表团于二十一日午后五时四十分抵达唐山。唐山市各机关负责人和青年劳动人民八千余人，在车站广场举行盛大欢迎会。唐山市副市长张文奇、青年团唐山市委会书记史一，先后致辞，对代表团表示热烈欢迎。外蒙古青年代表桑达格和伊朗青年代表文克贝塔尼相继发表谈话。他们都以极大的热情向中国人民及唐山市人民致敬。他们在讲话中表示，全世界青年坚决团结在世界强大的和平堡垒苏联的旗帜下，反对以美帝国主义为首的侵略阵营，而且有信心、有力量来消灭战争，争取世界持久和平。六点二十五分，在热烈的欢呼口号中，代表团登车离唐去津。
    【新华社天津二十一日电】世界民主青年联盟代表团一行四十余人，于访问我国东北各大城市后，已于二十一日晚九时由大连乘车抵津。赴站欢迎者有：天津市市长黄敬、副市长周叔韬、市总工会主席黄火青、天津市民主青年联合会主任何启君及青年工人、学生、战士等一千余人。苏联驻津总领事馆代理总领事唐平克、驻津苏侨青年代表列文等亦前往欢迎。
    在“世界青年团结万岁”、“世界和平万岁”的口号声及热烈的掌声中，代表们下车。在站前举行的欢迎会上，黄敬市长及市青联主任何启君致欢迎词，世界民主青年联盟代表团印度青年代表戈哈、保加利亚代表曼那伏夫致答词。
