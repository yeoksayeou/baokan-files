Title: Before the Opening of the United Nations General Assembly, Vyshinsky Issues Statement: This Assembly Must Discuss and Resolve Several Issues of Major International Significance
Author:
Date: 1950-09-21
Page: 1
　　
　　Before the Opening of the United Nations General Assembly
    Vyshinsky Issues Statement
    This Assembly Must Discuss and Resolve Several Issues of Major International Significance
    [Xinhua News Agency, New York, 20th] TASS, New York, 20th: The Soviet delegation, headed by Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky, the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic delegation, headed by Baranovksy, and the Belorussian Soviet Socialist Republic delegation, headed by Kiselev, arrived in New York on September 19th aboard the "Queen Elizabeth" liner to attend the Fifth United Nations General Assembly.
    Before arriving in New York, Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky made the following statement to newspaper reporters and radio station representatives on the ship: "The Fifth United Nations General Assembly must this time discuss and resolve several issues of major international significance. All peace-loving people are extremely concerned about the successful resolution of these issues facing the Assembly. To achieve success, it is first necessary to have the goodwill and determination to act in the interests of universal peace and the security of all countries. In the work of the United Nations General Assembly session opening today, the Soviet delegation will steadfastly adhere to the principles of the Soviet foreign policy aimed at consolidating peace and consolidating cooperation and friendship among nations. I would like to take this opportunity to express my respect to the people of the United States of America."

### 在联合国大会开幕之前  维辛斯基发表声明  这次大会必须讨论解决若干具有重大国际意义的问题

1950-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　在联合国大会开幕之前
    维辛斯基发表声明       
    这次大会必须讨论解决若干具有重大国际意义的问题
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社纽约二十日讯：以苏联外交部长维辛斯基为首的苏联代表团，以巴拉诺夫斯基为首的乌克兰苏维埃社会主义共和国代表团，以及以基塞列夫为首的白俄罗斯苏维埃社会主义共和国代表团，于九月十九日乘“伊丽莎白皇后”号轮船抵达纽约，出席联合国第五届大会。
    苏联外交部长维辛斯基于抵达纽约之前在船上向各报记者及各广播公司代表发表如下声明：“第五届联合国大会这一次必须讨论而且解决若干具有重大国际意义的问题。所有爱好和平的人民都对大会所面临的这些问题之顺利解决极为关心。要获得成功，首先就必须具有为普遍和平与各国安全的利益而行动的善意与决心。在今天开幕的联合国大会会议的工作中，苏联代表团将坚定不移地遵守苏联旨在巩固和平、巩固各国间的合作与友好的外交政策原则。借此机会，我谨向美利坚合众国的人民表示敬意。”
