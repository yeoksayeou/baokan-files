Title: East Coast and East Bank of the Nakdong River Fronts: Korean People's Army Continues Offensive, Annihilating Over Five Thousand American Soldiers Earlier This Month
Author:
Date: 1950-09-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 10th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a battle report on the evening of the 9th that the People's Army units advancing south after liberating Ankang are striking at the stubbornly resisting enemy and continuing their offensive.

The enemy south of Ankang repeatedly launched frantic counterattacks under the cover of aircraft and tanks. The People's Army repelled the enemy's counterattacks and inflicted heavy losses on the enemy.

From September 1st to 6th, in the battles on this front, the People's Army units killed or wounded more than 2,400 enemy soldiers, captured more than 200, seized 2 tanks, 5 60mm rocket launchers, 16 artillery pieces of various types, 20 heavy machine guns, more than 2,300 rifles, 7 radio stations, and many other weapons and ammunition, and shot down 1 American aircraft.

The People's Army units on the east coast shot down an American bomber that attacked the area south of Pohang.

The People's Army units operating on the south coast and the east bank of the Nakdong River continued their offensive and expanded their gains.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 10th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a battle report on the morning of the 9th that the People's Army units attacking Gyeongju after liberating Ankang bravely broke through the enemy's solid defensive positions built using the mountainous terrain, inflicting heavy blows on the enemy, and are now continuing to advance.

In the battles on this front, the People's Army units destroyed more than 40 enemy bunkers and killed or wounded more than 1,000 enemy soldiers.

The People's Army units attacking on the east bank of the Nakdong River fiercely attacked the remnants of the US 24th Regiment, the Marine Corps, and the main force of the 2nd Division, which were launching counterattacks under the cover of tanks, shattering the enemy's counterattack attempts and inflicting significant losses in personnel and equipment on the enemy.

From September 1st to 4th, in the battles on this front, the People's Army units killed or wounded more than 2,600 American soldiers, captured more than 250 American soldiers, destroyed 13 tanks, 38 armored vehicles, and many other supplies, and captured 14 tanks, 6 105mm guns, 15 107mm mortars, 8 81mm mortars, 5 60mm mortars, 34 60mm rocket launchers, 14 60mm guns, 6 heavy machine guns, more than 60 carbines, more than 200 rifles, more than 50 vehicles, and a large number of other pistols, radio stations, and other war spoils.

### 东海岸与洛东江东岸各战线  朝鲜人民军继续进攻  本月初歼灭美军逾五千名

1950-09-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　东海岸与洛东江东岸各战线
    朝鲜人民军继续进攻
    本月初歼灭美军逾五千名
    【新华社平壤十日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部九日晚发表战报称：解放安康后向南挺进的人民军部队，打击顽强抵抗的敌军，并继续进攻中。
    安康以南的敌军在飞机和坦克的掩护下，一再疯狂反攻。人民军击退敌军的反攻，并使敌军遭受严重损失。
    自九月一日至六日，人民军部队在该方面的战斗中，毙、伤敌二千四百余名，俘虏二百余名，缴获坦克二辆、六零公厘火箭炮五门、各种炮十六门、重机枪二十挺、步枪二千三百余支、电台七部及其他武器、弹药甚多，另击落美军飞机一架。
    东海岸的人民军部队击落侵袭浦项以南地区的美军轰炸机一架。
    在南海岸和洛东江东岸行动的人民军部队，继续进攻，扩大战果。
    【新华社平壤十日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部九日晨发表战报称：解放安康后向庆州进攻的人民军部队，勇敢地突破敌军利用山地构筑的坚固防御阵地，予敌以沉重打击，现正继续挺进中。
    在该方面的战斗中，人民军部队摧毁敌军碉堡四十余个，毙、伤敌一千余名。
    在洛东江东岸进攻的人民军部队，猛烈攻击在坦克掩护下重行反攻的美军第二十四团残部、海军陆战队和第二师的主力，粉碎了敌军的反攻企图，予敌军人员与装备以重大的损失。
    自九月一日至四日，人民军部队在该方面的战斗中，毙、伤美军二千六百余名，俘虏美军二百五十余名，击毁坦克十三辆、装甲车三十八辆及其他物资甚多，缴获坦克十四辆、一零五公厘炮六门、一零七迫击炮十五门、八一迫击炮八门、六零迫击炮五门、六零火箭炮三十四门、六零炮十四门、重机枪六挺、卡宾枪六十余支、步枪二百余支、汽车五十余辆，及其他手枪、电台等大量战利品。
