Title: Relief for Disaster Victims in Northern Anhui, Northern Jiangsu, Hebei, and Henan: Cold Weather Clothing Donation Association Established
Author:
Date: 1950-09-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: In view of the approaching winter and the urgent need for cold-weather clothing for disaster victims in some disaster-stricken areas of northern Anhui, northern Jiangsu, Hebei, and Henan, the China People's Relief Association and the China Red Cross Society Headquarters have specially invited various people's organizations, relevant agencies, democratic parties, and enthusiastic relief workers to establish the Cold Weather Clothing Donation Association for Disaster Victims in Northern Anhui, Northern Jiangsu, Hebei, and Henan on the 18th of this month. Those present included Dong Biwu, Huang Yanpei, Zhang Zhizhong, Chen Shutong, Cai Tingkai, Zhang Bojun, Feng Youlan, Shu Sheyu, Peng Zemin, Zhu Yunshan, Wu Yunfu, Xiong Jinding, Cao Mengjun, Hu Lansheng, Ni Feijun, and others, more than fifty people in all. Dong Biwu was elected as Chairman, Zhang Zhizhong, Xu Guangping, and Yang Lisan as Vice-Chairmen, and Xiong Jinding as Secretary-General, with Chen Chuangang, Hu Lansheng, and Ni Feijun as Deputy Secretaries-General. The donation work will begin immediately.

### 救助皖北苏北河北河南灾民  寒衣劝募总会成立

1950-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　救助皖北苏北河北河南灾民
    寒衣劝募总会成立
    【新华社十八日讯】中国人民救济总会及中国红十字会总会等，鉴于冬令将到，皖北、苏北、河北、河南等地部分受灾地区灾民寒衣需要迫切，特邀集各人民团体、各有关机关、各民主党派及热心救济人士，于本月十八日成立皖北苏北河北河南灾民寒衣劝募总会。到会者有董必武、黄炎培、张治中、陈叔通、蔡廷锴、章伯钧、冯友兰、舒舍予、彭泽民、朱蕴山、伍云甫、熊瑾玎、曹孟君、胡兰生、倪斐君等五十余人。当推选董必武为主任委员，张治中、许广平、杨立三为副主任委员，熊瑾玎为秘书长，陈传钢、胡兰生、倪斐君为副秘书长，即日开始劝募工作。
