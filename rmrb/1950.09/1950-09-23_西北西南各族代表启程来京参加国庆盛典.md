Title: Representatives of Various Ethnic Groups from Northwest and Southwest China Set Off for Beijing to Participate in National Day Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1950-09-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, 22nd. Twenty-nine representatives of seventeen ethnic groups from Gansu, Ningxia, Qinghai, and Xinjiang provinces, including Uyghur, Hui, Mongol, Tibetan, Tu, Salar, Kazakh, Kyrgyz, Uzbek, Russian, Tatar, Xibe, Solon, Tajik, Manchu, Han, and Dongxiang, have departed from Lanzhou on the 22nd to participate in the National Day celebrations in Beijing. The Nationalities Affairs Committee of the Northwest Military and Political Committee hosted a banquet in Lanzhou on the 20th to bid them farewell.
    Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 22nd. The representatives of various fraternal ethnic groups from Yunnan, Guizhou, and Xikang provinces who are going to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations have arrived in Chongqing and have jointly formed the Southwest Nationalities Celebration Delegation, which left Chongqing for Beijing on the 20th. The delegation is headed by Wang Weidan, Vice Chairman of the Southwest Military and Political Committee and Director of the Nationalities Affairs Committee, with Bangda Duoji (Tibetan), the chief representative of Xikang, Ma Bo'an (Hui), the chief representative of Yunnan, and Li Ruyun (Miao), the chief representative of Guizhou, as deputy heads. The delegation includes twenty-five representatives from the Yi, Miao, Zhongjia, Tibetan, Bai Yi, Minjia, Mosuo, Woni, Lisu, Sani, and Hui ethnic groups; in addition, there are seventy-nine members of the performing arts troupe, jointly formed by the fraternal ethnic groups' performing arts teams from the three provinces of Yunnan, Guizhou, and Xikang.
    During the delegation's stay in Chongqing, women representatives held a gathering with staff members of the Southwest Women's Work Committee; the performing arts troupe performed three recreational evenings of ethnic song and dance; and the delegation also held a two-day exhibition of ethnic cultural relics, which was warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life in Chongqing.

### 西北西南各族代表启程来京参加国庆盛典

1950-09-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　西北西南各族代表启程来京参加国庆盛典
    【新华社兰州二十二日电】甘肃、宁夏、青海、新疆四省赴京参加国庆盛典的维吾尔族、回族、蒙族、藏族、土族、撒拉族、哈萨克族、柯尔克斯族、乌孜别克族、俄罗斯族、塔塔尔族、锡伯族、索伦族、塔吉克族、满族、汉族、东乡族等十七个民族的代表共二十九人，已于二十二日由兰州启程赴京。西北军政委员会民族事务委员会曾于二十日在兰州设宴欢送。
    【新华社重庆二十二日电】赴京参加国庆的云南、贵州、西康各兄弟民族代表抵渝后，已联合组成西南各民族庆祝国庆代表团，二十日离渝赴京。该团由西南军政委员会副主席兼民族事务委员会主任委员王维丹为团长，西康首席代表邦达多吉（藏族）、云南首席代表马伯安（回族）、贵州首席代表李儒云（苗族）三人为副团长。代表团包括彝族、苗族、仲家族、藏族、摆彝族、民家族、麽些族、窝尼族、傈僳族、撒尼族、回族代表共二十五人；另有文工团团员七十九人，由滇黔康三省兄弟民族文工队联合组成。
    在代表团留渝期间，妇女代表曾和西南妇女工作委员会工作人员举行联欢；文工团出演了三次各民族歌舞的游艺晚会；代表团并举办了两天各民族文物展览会，受到重庆各界人民的热烈欢迎。
