Title: Xinhua News Agency Important Correction
Author:
Date: 1950-09-22
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency Important Correction
    In item (1) of the "Production Instructions for the National Emblem of the People's Republic of China" in our telegram of the 20th, "The center of the gear is intertwined with a red ribbon, which is fastened to the left and right of the wheat and rice and hangs down, dividing the gear into upper and lower parts" should be corrected to "The center of the gear is intertwined with a red ribbon. The red ribbon is fastened to the left and right of the wheat and rice and hangs down, dividing the gear into upper and lower parts." (This description was published on the first page of this newspaper on September 20th.)

### 新华社重要更正

1950-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    新华社重要更正
    本社二十日电“中华人民共和国国徽图案制作说明”（一）项内“齿轮的中心交结着红绶向左右绾住麦稻而下垂。把齿轮分成上下两部。”应更正为“齿轮的中心交结着红绶。红绶向左右绾住麦稻而下垂，把齿轮分成上下两部。”（此项说明本报登在九月二十日第一版）
