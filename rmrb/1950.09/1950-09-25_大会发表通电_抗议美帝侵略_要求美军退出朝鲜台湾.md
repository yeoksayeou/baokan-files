Title: Conference Issues Circular Telegram Protesting American Imperialist Aggression and Demanding the Withdrawal of US Troops from Korea and Taiwan
Author: 
Date: 1950-09-25
Page: 4

　　Conference Issues Circular Telegram
    Protesting American Imperialist Aggression
    Demanding the Withdrawal of US Troops from Korea and Taiwan
    [Xinhua News Agency, September 24th] The full text of the circular telegram from the Beijing Youth Mass Meeting Welcoming the World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation is as follows:
    American imperialists have interfered in the Korean civil war, dispatching US naval, land, and air forces to invade Korean territory, bombing and massacring peaceful Korean residents. American aggressors have also invaded China's territory – Taiwan.
    We firmly oppose the American imperialists' acts of aggression and their conspiracy to launch a world war. We demand that the American imperialist warships, airplanes, and troops immediately withdraw from Korea and China's Taiwan. We call on the youth of the world to unite, support Korea and all the people and youth of the world in their struggle against aggression, and defend lasting world peace.

### 大会发表通电  抗议美帝侵略  要求美军退出朝鲜台湾

1950-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　大会发表通电
    抗议美帝侵略
    要求美军退出朝鲜台湾
    【新华社二十四日讯】北京青年欢迎世界民主青年联盟代表团群众大会的通电全文如下：
    美国帝国主义者干涉朝鲜内战，派遣了美国的海军、陆军、空军侵入朝鲜境内，对朝鲜的和平居民大肆轰炸和屠杀。美国侵略者同时侵略了中国的领土——台湾。
    我们坚决反对美帝国主义者的侵略行为和发动世界战争的阴谋。我们要求美帝国主义的军舰、飞机、军队，立即退出朝鲜以及中国的台湾。我们号召全世界青年团结起来，支援朝鲜和全世界一切人民和青年反对侵略的斗争，保卫世界持久和平。
