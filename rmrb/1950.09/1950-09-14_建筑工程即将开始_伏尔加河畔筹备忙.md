Title: Construction Project About to Begin: Preparations Busily Underway on the Banks of the Volga

Author:

Date: 1950-09-14
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 13th: A comprehensive TASS dispatch reports that preparations for the construction of the Kuibyshev and Stalingrad hydroelectric power stations on the banks of the Volga River in the Soviet Union are intensifying. At the construction site of the Kuibyshev power station, a large number of engineers, technicians, and workers arrive from all over the country every day. In a scenic area on the banks of the Volga River, construction has begun on an industrial city for construction workers. This city will have high-rise buildings, parks, sports fields, schools, hospitals, clubs, and cinemas. Railway branch lines are being built on both sides of the Volga River, and the construction of motor roads is also being prepared. Various machines and equipment are being shipped from Moscow, Leningrad, Stalingrad, the Urals, and other places in a steady stream, and loading and unloading operations are being carried out day and night.

　　Industrial enterprises in many cities are manufacturing orders for the construction sites of the two power stations. Automobile factories in Moscow and Gorky have already sent trucks to Kuibyshev. Personnel from the Ochniki Iron and Steel Plant in Yenakiyevo have started manufacturing the equipment needed for the Volga hydroelectric power station in Donbass.

### 建筑工程即将开始  伏尔加河畔筹备忙

1950-09-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　建筑工程即将开始
    伏尔加河畔筹备忙
    【新华社十三日讯】综合塔斯社消息：苏联在伏尔加河畔建造古比雪夫及斯大林格勒水力发电站的准备工作，日渐紧张。古比雪夫发电站的建筑现场上，每天都有大批工程师、技师和工人们从全国各地到来。伏尔加河岸上的一处风景区，已经着手为建筑工人建设一个工业城市，这个城市将拥有高楼大厦、公园、运动场、学校、医院、俱乐部及电影院。伏尔加河两岸正在建筑铁路支线，汽车路的建筑工程也在准备中。各种机器与器材正从莫斯科、列宁格勒、斯大林格勒、乌拉尔及其他各地源源运到，起卸工作在昼夜进行中。
    许多城市的工业企业正在制造两个发电站建设处的定货。莫斯科与高尔基两地的汽车制造厂已经把卡车送往古比雪夫。叶纳耶伏的奥强尼基柴冶金厂的人员，已在顿巴斯首先开始制造伏尔加水力发电站所需的设备。
