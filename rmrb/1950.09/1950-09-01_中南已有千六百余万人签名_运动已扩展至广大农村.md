Title: Over 16.6 Million People in Central and Southern China Have Signed; The Movement Has Expanded to the Vast Rural Areas
Author: 
Date: 1950-09-01
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, 28th. The number of people signing the peace petition in the Central and Southern China region continues to increase. As of recently, according to incomplete statistics: 16,610,602 people in the entire region have signed the Peace Declaration. Among them, 1,037,452 people in Wuhan City, accounting for 93.8 percent of the city's population. 2,373,363 people in Hubei Province, accounting for approximately 10 percent of the province's population. 4,035,835 people in Henan Province, accounting for 12.6 percent of the province's population. Over 2,451,200 people in Guangdong Province, including over 795,000 in Guangzhou City and over 51,200 in Haikou City. 3,000,000 people in Jiangxi Province, where the number of signatures in Nanchang and Ganzhou cities accounts for over 90 percent of the city's population. Over 2,501,300 people in Hunan Province. In response to the call by the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace to continue expanding the peace signature campaign, the China Peace Committees and branches in Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi and other places, as well as people's organizations in Guangxi Province, have issued notices to various regions, calling on people from all walks of life to sign enthusiastically. At present, the peace signature movement has expanded to small and medium-sized cities and the vast rural areas.

### 中南已有千六百余万人签名  运动已扩展至广大农村

1950-09-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　中南已有千六百余万人签名
    运动已扩展至广大农村
    【新华社二十八日讯】中南区和平签名人数继续增加。截至最近为止，据不完全统计：全区已有一千六百六十一万零六百零二人在和平宣言上签名。其中武汉市一百零三万七千四百五十二人，占全市人口百分之九十三点八。湖北省二百三十七万三千三百六十三人，约占全省人口百分之十。河南省四百零三万五千八百三十五人，占全省人口百分之十二点六。广东省二百四十五万一千二百余人，其中广州市为七十九万五千余人，海口市为五万一千二百余人。江西省三百万人，其中南昌、赣州两市的签名人数已占全市人口百分之九十以上。湖南省为二百五十万零一千三百多人。为响应中国保卫世界和平大会委员会继续扩大和平签名运动的号召，湖北、湖南、江西等地的中国和大分会及广西省各人民团体等均分别向各地发出通知，号召各界人民踊跃签名。目前各地的和平签名运动已扩展至中小城市和广大农村。
