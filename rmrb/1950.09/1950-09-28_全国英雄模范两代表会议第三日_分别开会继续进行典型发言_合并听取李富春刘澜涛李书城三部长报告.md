Title: The Third Day of the National Congress of Heroes and Model Workers: Separate Meetings Continue with Typical Speeches; Joint Hearing of Reports from Ministers Li Fuchun, Liu Lantao, and Li Shucheng

Author:

Date: 1950-09-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: The third day of the National Congress of Combat Heroes and the National Congress of Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers' Labor Models saw separate plenary sessions in the morning to continue making typical speeches. In the afternoon, a joint session was held to hear reports from leading comrades.

　　At this morning's National Congress of Combat Heroes, the representatives who made typical speeches included: Tai Xide (Mongolian), Deputy Chief of Staff of a regiment of a certain unit of the North China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and cavalry hero; Jiang Xinliang, Deputy Company Commander and combat hero of a certain unit of the Northeast Military Region; Wang Hongzhi, hero of the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Zhao Xiaoan, hero of the Navy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; and Liu Zilin, Deputy Battalion Commander and combat hero of a certain unit of the Second Field Army. At the meeting, the Navy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army presented flowers, and the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions presented a flag and delivered a congratulatory speech. Finally, He Cheng, Executive Chairman of the Presidium of the Congress, announced that eight more congratulatory telegrams had been received.

　　At this morning's National Congress of Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers' Labor Models, the labor model representatives who made typical reports were: Ma Hengchang, leader of the production team of the Northeast Shenyang Machine Tool Factory No. 5, the banner of national production teams; Liang Jun, the first female tractor driver in Northeast China and captain of the female tractor team of the Nanqu Farm in Dedu County, Heilongjiang; Li Jinghua, passenger train conductor of the "Masses" train of the Northeast Jilin Train Section; Zhao Lan, model factory director and director of the Northeast Shenyang Smelter; Hou Deyuan, model engineer of the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau; Wei Zhenhua, labor model of the Tianjin China Vegetable Oil Factory for transforming the oil press; Wang Zhendong, agricultural labor model and special-class model village cadre of Heilongjiang Province; Wang Qingheng, forestry labor model of Northeast China; and Han En, agricultural labor model and labor mutual aid model of Jilin Province. The nine labor model representatives vividly reported their model deeds and experiences. They all believed that only in the new society could their enthusiasm have the opportunity to be brought into play. They unanimously expressed that in the future, they must continue to work more actively in industrial, agricultural, forestry, and other positions to support the liberation of Tibet and Taiwan. The representatives who made the reports lodged a solemn protest against the aggressive act of American imperialism bombing An Dong. At the meeting, congratulatory telegrams from the Third Field Army, the Fourth Field Army, and various democratic parties in Shanghai were read.

　　At 3 p.m., the National Congress of Combat Heroes and the National Congress of Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers' Labor Models were held jointly to hear reports from Li Fuchun, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government and Minister of Heavy Industry, Liu Lantao, Minister of the North China Affairs Department of the Central People's Government, and Li Shucheng, Minister of Agriculture of the Central People's Government.

　　In his speech, Minister Li Fuchun pointed out that in the future, industrial production should implement democratic management, establish and improve the economic accounting system, learn and master and improve technology, and strengthen the unity of the working class, especially the unity of workers and technicians, to achieve the goals of improving business management, improving quality, and reducing costs.

　　Minister Liu Lantao said: Agricultural production in North China is advancing by leaps and bounds. The production plan for 1950 has been successfully completed after overcoming various difficulties. In order to raise agricultural production in North China a step further on the existing basis, we should: (1) further organize and strive to increase production; (2) improve technology and overcome disasters; and (3) develop the work of cooperatives, especially supply and marketing cooperatives.

　　Li Shucheng spoke on behalf of the three ministries of agriculture, forestry and land reclamation, and water conservancy of the Central People's Government. He gave a brief report on the work of the three ministries, the achievements they had made, and the future work guidelines. He said that the general task of the three ministries is to guarantee the interests of farmers and to strive to develop China's agricultural production.

### 全国英雄模范两代表会议第三日  分别开会继续进行典型发言  合并听取李富春刘澜涛李书城三部长报告

1950-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国英雄模范两代表会议第三日
    分别开会继续进行典型发言
    合并听取李富春刘澜涛李书城三部长报告
    【新华社二十七日讯】全国战斗英雄代表会议和全国工农兵劳模代表会议第三日，上午分别举行大会，继续进行典型发言，下午合并举行，听取首长报告。
    在今日上午全国战斗英雄代表会议上，作典型发言的代表有中国人民解放军华北军区某部团副参谋长、骑兵英雄邰喜德（蒙族），东北军区某部副连长、战斗英雄姜新良，中国人民解放军空军英雄王洪智，中国人民解放军海军英雄赵孝庵，第二野战军某部副营长战斗英雄刘子林。会上，有中国人民解放军海军部队献花，北京市总工会献旗并致祝词。最后由大会主席团执行主席贺诚宣读继续收到贺电八件。
    在今日上午全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议上，作典型报告的劳模代表有：全国生产小组的旗帜、东北沈阳机器五厂生产小组组长马恒昌，东北第一个女拖拉机手、黑龙江德都县南区农场女拖拉机队队长梁军，东北吉林列车段“群众号”列车、客运车长李静华，模范厂长、东北沈阳冶炼厂厂长赵岚，北京电信局模范工程师侯德原，天津中国植物油厂改造榨油机的劳动模范魏振华，农业劳动模范、黑龙江省特等模范村干部王振堂，东北林业劳动模范王清恒，农业劳动模范、吉林省劳动互助模范韩恩。九位劳动模范代表生动地报告了他们的模范事迹和经验。他们都认为：只有在新社会中，他们的这种积极性才有发挥的机会。他们一致表示：今后一定要继续在工业、农业、林业等工作岗位上，更加积极地努力生产、支援西藏和台湾的解放。作报告的代表们，对美国帝国主义轰炸安东的侵略行为，提出严正的抗议。会上并宣读了第三野战军、第四野战军及上海市各民主党派给大会的贺电。
    下午三时，全国战斗英雄代表会议和全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议合并举行，听取了中央人民政府财经委员会副主任兼重工业部部长李富春、中央人民政府华北事务部部长刘澜涛、中央人民政府农业部部长李书城的报告。
    李富春部长在讲话时指出，今后工业生产应贯彻管理民主化，建立与健全经济核算制度，学习掌握与提高技术，加强工人阶级的团结，特别要加强工人与技术人员的团结，以达到改善经营管理，提高质量，减低成本的目的。
    刘澜涛部长说：华北的农业生产正在大踏步前进。一九五○年的生产计划在克服了各种困难之后，已顺利完成。为了使华北的农业生产在现有的基础上提高一步，今后应该：（一）进一步组织起来，努力增产；（二）改良技术，战胜灾害；（三）开展合作社特别是供销合作社的工作。
    李书城代表中央人民政府农业、林垦、水利三部致词。他大略报告了三部的工作情况和获得的成绩及今后工作方针。他说三部总的任务是保证农民的利益，为了发展中国的农业生产而努力。
