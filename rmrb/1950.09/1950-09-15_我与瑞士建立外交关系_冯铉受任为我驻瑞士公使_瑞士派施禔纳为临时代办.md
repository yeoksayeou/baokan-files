Author:
Date: 1950-09-15
Page: 1

　　China Establishes Diplomatic Relations with Switzerland
    Feng Xuan Appointed as China's Envoy to Switzerland; Switzerland Sends Walter Dr. Stöcklin as Chargé d'Affaires ad interim
    [Xinhua News Agency, 14th] The Swiss Federal Government announced on January 17th and February 6th of this year its willingness to establish diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of China and appointed Mr. Walter Dr. Stöcklin as Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to come to Beijing to contact the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. Li Kenong, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, replied to Mr. Petitpierre, President of the Swiss Federal Council, on February 10th, expressing the same wish and accepting Mr. Walter Dr. Stöcklin as the representative sent by the Swiss Federal Government to Beijing to negotiate the preliminary and procedural issues of establishing diplomatic relations between China and Switzerland. Representative Walter Dr. Stöcklin arrived in Beijing on May 16th and immediately began these negotiations, which have now successfully concluded. China and Switzerland have agreed that the Swiss Federal Government will temporarily appoint Mr. Walter Dr. Stöcklin as Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Swiss Federal Republic to the People's Republic of China before officially appointing its envoy to the People's Republic of China; the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China will dispatch Mr. Feng Xuan as Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Swiss Federal Republic.

### 我与瑞士建立外交关系  冯铉受任为我驻瑞士公使  瑞士派施禔纳为临时代办

1950-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　我与瑞士建立外交关系
    冯铉受任为我驻瑞士公使  瑞士派施禔纳为临时代办
    【新华社十四日讯】瑞士联邦政府于本年一月十七日及二月六日宣布愿与中华人民共和国建立外交关系，并任命施禔纳先生为临时代办前来北京与中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部取得联系。我外交部李克农副部长当于二月十日电复瑞士联邦委员会主席彼蒂彼爱，表示同样愿望，并接受施禔纳先生为瑞士联邦政府派来北京，就中瑞两国建立外交关系的初步与程序问题进行谈判的代表。施禔纳代表于五月十六日到达北京，随即开始进行此项谈判，现谈判已顺利结束。中瑞两国并已同意：瑞士联邦政府在未正式派定该国驻中华人民共和国公使之前，暂派施禔纳先生为瑞士联邦共和国驻中华人民共和国临时代办；中华人民共和国中央人民政府派遣冯铉先生为中华人民共和国驻瑞士联邦共和国特命全权公使。
