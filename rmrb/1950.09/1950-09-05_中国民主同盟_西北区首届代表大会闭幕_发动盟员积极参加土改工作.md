Title: China Democratic League Northwest District First Congress Concludes: Mobilizing League Members to Actively Participate in Land Reform Work
Author:
Date: 1950-09-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an: The first Congress of the China Democratic League Northwest District, lasting ten days, concluded on September 2nd. One hundred and three League member representatives attended, with eighty people attending as observers. Peng Dehuai, Chairman of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, and Ma Mingfang, Third Secretary of the Northwest Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, attended and offered congratulations. The congress approved the "Work Report of the Northwest General Branch Provisional Work Committee" by Yang Mingxuan, Chairman of the Provisional Work Committee of the League's Northwest General Branch, and the report on "The New Historical Stage and New Political Tasks" by Cheng Boren, Minister of the Propaganda Department. The congress determined that the future task is to "strengthen and expand the people's democratic united front," enhance organizational work, unite and educate intellectuals, industrialists, and businessmen, as well as the broad middle strata, and under the leadership of the working class and its political party—the Chinese Communist Party—strive to realize the Common Program and achieve historical tasks.
　　The congress mobilized all League members to respond to the League Central Committee's call and actively participate in land reform work.
　　Under the supervision of Sa Kongliao, the League Central Committee's elected representative, the congress elected Yang Mingxuan, Han Zhao'e, Cheng Boren, Yang Zilian, and thirty-two others as members of the Northwest General Branch Committee, as well as eleven alternate members.
　　On the closing day of the congress, Shen Junru, Vice Chairman of the League Central Committee, and Sa Kongliao, the elected representative, attended and spoke, calling on everyone to clearly understand the new era, cooperate with various democratic parties, and strive for the New Democratic Revolution under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party.

### 中国民主同盟  西北区首届代表大会闭幕  发动盟员积极参加土改工作

1950-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国民主同盟
    西北区首届代表大会闭幕
    发动盟员积极参加土改工作
    【本报讯】据新华社西安讯：中国民主同盟西北区第一次盟员代表大会，历时十天，已于九月二日闭幕。到会盟员代表一百零三人，列席八十人。西北军政委员会主席彭德怀，中共中央西北局第三书记马明方等，均曾赴会致贺。大会通过了该盟西北总支部临时工作委员会主任委员杨明轩关于“西北总支部临时工作委员会工作报告”和宣传部部长成柏仁关于“新的历史阶段和新的政治任务”的报告。大会确定今后的任务是在“加强和扩大人民民主统一战线”的总方针下，增进组织工作，团结和教育知识分子、工商业者以及广大的中间阶层，在工人阶级及其政党——中国共产党领导之下，为实现共同纲领、达成历史任务而奋斗。
    大会发动全体盟员响应该盟中央号召，积极参加土地改革工作。
    大会在该盟中央监选代表萨空了监选下选出了杨明轩、韩兆鹗、成柏仁、杨子廉等三十六人为西北总支部委员及十一名候补委员。
    大会闭幕日，该盟中央副主席沈钧儒、监选代表萨空了莅会讲话，号召大家明确认识新的时代，配合各民主党派，在中国共产党领导下，为新民主主义革命而努力。
