Title: Zhou Foreign Minister Cables UN Secretary-General, etc., Accusing U.S. Warships of Attacking Our Merchant Ships, Demanding This Be Included in the UN General Assembly Agenda and Inviting Our Attendance for Discussion
Author:
Date: 1950-09-28
Page: 1
　
Xinhua News Agency, September 27th:

Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai, regarding the incident of U.S. invading Korean warships shelling and illegally searching our merchant ship "Anhai No. 21" near Chengshan Cape, sent a cable on September 27th to UN Secretary-General Trygve Lie, and forwarded it to UN Fifth General Assembly President Entezam and Security Council President Jebb, accusing the U.S. government of a new act of aggression that undermined our country's navigation rights. He demanded that the UN General Assembly immediately include this case, together with our country's accusation of U.S. aircraft infringing upon our airspace, on the agenda, accept our representative to attend, present opinions, and participate in discussions, and demanded that the UN sanction the U.S. aggression and promptly withdraw all U.S. forces invading Korea, including the air force and navy. The text of the cable is as follows:

To Mr. Trygve Lie, Secretary-General of the United Nations, and forwarded to Mr. Entezam, President of the Fifth United Nations General Assembly, and Mr. Jebb, President of the Security Council:

According to a report from the Northeast People's Government of our country: The "Anhai No. 21" merchant ship, belonging to the Andong City Port Authority of our country, departed from Shidao, the southeast corner of Jiaodong Peninsula, on September 20th, carrying passengers and goods back to Andong. At 22:45 Beijing time on the 21st, while sailing near Chengshan Cape, at 38 degrees 11 minutes 30 seconds north latitude and 123 degrees 6 minutes east longitude, it was suddenly shelled for 25 minutes by a U.S. destroyer, number D29, from the southwest rear direction, forcing it to stop sailing. At 23:10, the ship approached our ship, used searchlights to illuminate it, and forcibly conducted a search. It also ordered all passengers to line up on the deck and interrogated them for about 20 minutes before leaving. When the U.S. ship fired at our ship, a U.S. aircraft was circling overhead.

This is another provocative act by the U.S. government against the People's Republic of China, following its previous crimes of invading Chinese airspace with military aircraft of its troops invading Korea, indiscriminately strafing and bombing, killing and injuring Chinese people, and damaging Chinese property. In its aggression against the Korean War, the U.S. government, in order to expand the flames of war, disregarded everything and used its invading Korean navy to forcibly search merchant ships of the People's Republic of China in international waters, hindering the navigation rights of the People's Republic of China.

I now, on behalf of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, accuse the U.S. government of this new act of aggression in undermining our country's navigation rights, and demand that the UN General Assembly immediately include the case of U.S. invading Korean warships shelling and illegally searching merchant ships of the People's Republic of China, together with the proposal of the People's Republic of China accusing U.S. invading Korean troops of using military aircraft to invade our country's airspace, indiscriminately strafing and bombing, killing and injuring our people, and damaging our property, on the agenda of the General Assembly, and accept the representative of the People's Republic of China to attend, present opinions, and participate in discussions. The UN General Assembly should immediately recommend that the UN Security Council take effective measures to sanction U.S. aggression and promptly withdraw all U.S. forces invading Korea, including the air force and navy, to restore peace in the Far East and the world.

　　　　　　　　　　　　Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　September 27, 1950, in Beijing

### 周外长致电联合国秘书长等  控诉美军舰袭击我商轮  要求将此列入联大议程并邀我出席讨论

1950-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　周外长致电联合国秘书长等
    控诉美军舰袭击我商轮
    要求将此列入联大议程并邀我出席讨论
    【新华社二十七日讯】外交部周恩来部长为美国侵略朝鲜军舰在成山角附近炮轰并非法盘查我国商船“安海二十一号”事于二十七日致电联合国秘书长赖伊，并转联合国第五届大会主席安迪让及安全理事会主席杰伯，控诉美国政府破坏我国航行权利的新的侵略暴行，要求联合国大会立即将此案与我国控诉美机侵犯领空案一并列入议程，接纳我国代表出席陈述意见和参加讨论，并要求联合国制裁美国侵略罪行，从速撤退美国侵略朝鲜的一切武力，包括空军海军在内。电文如下：联合国秘书长赖伊先生并转联合国第五届大会主席安迪让先生及安全理事会主席杰伯先生：
    据我东北人民政府报告：我安东市港湾管理局所属“安海二十一号”商船，于九月二十日由我胶东半岛东南角石岛，运载旅客、货物开返安东。二十一日北京时间二十二时四十五分，行至成山角附近，北纬三十八度十一分三十秒，东经一百二十三度六分之处，突有番号Ｄ２９之美国驱逐舰一艘，由西南后方向我“安海二十一号”商船发炮达二十五分钟，迫令停航。二十三时十分，该舰迫近我船，用探照灯指照，强迫进行盘查。又令全部旅客排列舱面，加以盘问约二十余分钟，始行离去。当美舰向我船发炮时，有美机一架在上空盘旋。
    这是美国政府在以其侵朝军队的军用飞机侵入中国领空、肆行扫射轰炸、杀伤中国人民、损坏中国财产造成历次罪行之后，又一次对中华人民共和国的挑衅行为。美国政府在其进行侵略朝鲜战争中，为了扩大战争火焰，竟不顾一切以其侵朝海军在公海之内强制盘查我中华人民共和国的商船，妨害我中华人民共和国的航行权利。
    我现在代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府，向联合国控诉美国政府破坏我国航行权利这一新的侵略暴行，并要求联合国大会立即将美国侵朝军舰炮击和非法盘查我中华人民共和国商船一案，与我中华人民共和国控诉美国侵朝军队军用飞机侵入我国领空、肆行扫射轰炸、杀伤我国人民并损害我国财产的提案，一并列入大会议事日程，并接纳中华人民共和国的代表出席陈述意见和参加讨论。联合国大会应立即建议联合国安全理事会采取有效办法，制裁美国侵略罪行，并从速撤退美国侵略朝鲜的一切武力，包括空军海军在内，以恢复远东和世界的和平。
      中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来
        一九五○年九月二十七日于北京
