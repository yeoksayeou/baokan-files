Title: Qu Zheng and Hong Shilu Go to Czechoslovakia to Attend the Czechoslovak Microbiological Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-09-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The Czechoslovak Microbiological Society decided to convene the Sixth Czechoslovak Microbiological Congress in Prague on September 24th. The Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government of China, at the invitation of the Czechoslovak Ministry of Health, has sent Qu Zheng, Deputy Minister of Health of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, and Hong Shilu, Director of the Zhejiang Provincial Health Experimental Institute, as two representatives to depart for Prague on the morning of the 15th to attend the conference.

### 出席捷微生物学大会  曲正洪式闾赴捷

1950-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　出席捷微生物学大会
    曲正洪式闾赴捷
    【新华社十五日讯】捷克微生物学会决定于九月二十四日在布拉格召开捷克第六届微生物学大会，我中央人民政府卫生部应捷克卫生部的邀请，已派西北军政委员会卫生部副部长曲正，与浙江省立卫生实验院院长洪式闾两代表于十五日晨起程前往布拉格参加该会。
