Title: Celebrating National Day: China National Department Store Offers Three-Day Discount
Author: Liu Zhixin
Date: 1950-09-27
Page: 10

　　Celebrating National Day
    China National Department Store Offers Three-Day Discount
    To celebrate the first anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the China National Department Store has decided that all levels of companies nationwide will offer a three-day discount on September 28, 29, and 30. All retail goods in stores will be given a 5% discount. A telegram was sent to all regional companies on the 21st of this month to instruct their subordinate companies to comply.
    (Liu Zhixin)

### 庆祝国庆节  中国百货公司减价三天优待
刘之信
1950-09-27
第10版()
专栏：

　　庆祝国庆节
    中国百货公司减价三天优待
    中国百货公司为庆祝中华人民共和国开国一周年纪念，特决定全国各级公司于九月二十八、二十九、三十日减价三天，门市零售商品均予九五折优待。已于本月二十一日电各区公司转饬所属公司遵照执行。
    （刘之信）
