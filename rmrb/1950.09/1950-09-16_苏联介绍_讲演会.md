Title: "Introduction to the Soviet Union" Lecture

Author: Chen Yingnan
Date: 1950-09-16
Page: 8

    "Introduction to the Soviet Union" Lecture
    The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries are scheduled to hold an "Introduction to the Soviet Union" lecture today (the 16th) at 19 Nanheyan Street. Mr. Semirikh will speak on "The Soviet Socialist State." All those holding admission tickets or long-term listening passes for the Introduction to the Soviet Union are welcome to attend the lecture. (Chen Yingnan)

### “苏联介绍”讲演会
陈应楠
1950-09-16
第8版()
专栏：

    “苏联介绍”讲演会
    北京市中苏友好协会与苏联对外文化协会定于今日（十六日）在南河沿十九号举行“苏联介绍”讲演会，请谢米里赫讲“苏维埃社会主义国家”。凡持有入场券或苏联介绍长期听讲证者，届时均可前往听讲。（陈应楠）
