Title: Korean People's Army Continues to Annihilate Over Four Thousand Enemies; Total of 16,000 U.S. Troops Killed or Captured in Two Months
Author:
Date: 1950-09-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency

　　The Korean People's Army Continues to Annihilate Over Four Thousand Enemies
    Total of 16,000 U.S. Troops Killed or Captured in Two Months
    [News from this newspaper] According to a war report issued by the Supreme Command of the Korean People's Army relayed by Xinhua News Agency from Pyongyang: On the east coast, the Korean People's Army continued to advance south, killing or wounding over 1,700 enemies and capturing over 700 enemies. In the Nakdong River area, the People's Army crushed several counterattacks by enemy forces and continued to attack, killing or wounding over 1,300 enemies. After liberating Uiheung, the advancing People's Army units broke through the defensive positions of the Rhee Syngman army's 8th Division, killing or wounding over 300 enemies and capturing over 150 enemies. In the above battles, a large number of weapons such as eight tanks, thirty-six vehicles, and various artillery pieces and guns were captured.
    [Xinhua News Agency, dated 8th] Pyongyang broadcasted on the 6th: The Supreme Command of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced the communique of the losses suffered by the American invading army in Korea, stating: In July and August, the Korean People's Army killed 15,176 officers and soldiers of the U.S. Army, wounded 45,000, and captured 1,736.
    The communique listed in detail the dates, locations, unit numbers, and numbers of U.S. troops annihilated in each battle, among which the important ones are: In the battle of Chungju on July 18, 1,318 bodies of the U.S. 24th Infantry Division's 34th Regiment were left behind, and 13 were captured. In the battle of Daejeon from July 20 to 24, 1,444 bodies of the U.S. 24th Infantry Division were left behind, and 134 were captured. In the battle of Hwanggan on July 29, the U.S. 1st Cavalry Division and 25th Infantry Division left behind as many as 1,219 bodies. In the battle of Jindong-ri from August 1 to 12, the U.S. 25th Infantry Division's 35th Regiment and the 1st Marine Division left behind 2,808 bodies, and as many as 770 were captured.

### 朝鲜人民军续歼敌四千余  两月共毙俘美军一万六千

1950-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军续歼敌四千余
    两月共毙俘美军一万六千
    【本报讯】据新华社平壤电转述的朝鲜共和国人民军总司令部所发战报称：朝鲜人民军在东海岸方面继续向南推进，毙、伤敌一千七百余名，俘敌七百余名。在洛东江一带人民军粉碎了敌军数次反攻，继续进攻，毙伤敌一千三百余名。解放义兴后继续前进的人民军部队突破李承晚军第八师的防御阵地，毙伤敌三百余，俘敌一百五十余名。以上各次战斗共缴获坦克八辆、汽车三十六辆及各种炮、枪等武器甚多。
    【新华社八日讯】据平壤六日广播：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部发表美国侵略军在朝鲜所受损失的公报称：在七月和八月两个月内，朝鲜人民军击毙美陆军部队官兵一万五千一百七十六名，击伤四万五千名，俘掳一千七百三十六名。
    公报详细列举了美军历次被歼的日期，地点，番号和人数，其中重要者有：七月十八日在忠州战斗中，美军第二十四步兵师第三十四团遗尸一千三百一十八具，被俘十三名。七月二十日至二十四日在大田战斗中，美军第二十四步兵师遗尸一千四百四十四具，被俘一百三十四名。七月二十九日在黄涧战斗中，美军第一骑兵师与第二十五步兵师遗尸达一千二百十九具。八月一日至十二日在镇东里战斗中，美军第二十五步兵师第三十五团与海军陆战队第一师遗尸二千八百○八具，被俘者达七百七十名。
