Title: Welcoming the Polish Congress for Peace: Polish Workers Launch Production Competition; Donations to Support the Korean People Approach 200 Million Polish Zloty

Author:
Date: 1950-09-11
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 31st - According to the Polish Press Agency, Polish workers are enthusiastically launching production competitions to welcome the Polish Congress for Peace. Among members of the Polish Youth League in Wrocław, on the construction site of the "Nowa Huta" metallurgical factory near Kraków, and among the female workers of the International Factory in the Olkusz district of Kraków Province, the movement of peace work teams is expanding daily. Warsaw has launched a campaign to strive for model construction sites. The printing workers of the "Prus" Workers' Publishing Cooperative in Grünberg have already completed the production guarantees undertaken in celebration of the Polish Congress for Peace.

　　At the same time, the Polish working people have raised 182 million Polish Zloty (approximately 14.65 billion RMB) in donations to support the Korean people and to relieve the Korean victims of American air raids.

### 迎接波兰拥护和平大会  波劳动人民展开生产竞赛  声援朝鲜人民捐款已近二亿波币

1950-09-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　迎接波兰拥护和平大会
    波劳动人民展开生产竞赛
    声援朝鲜人民捐款已近二亿波币
    【新华社三十一日讯】据波兰通讯社华沙讯：波兰劳动人民为迎接波兰拥护和平大会，正热烈地开展生产竞赛。在罗克劳波兰青年联盟的盟员中间，在克拉科附近的“诺瓦·胡塔”冶金工厂的建筑场上，在克拉科省俄尔库兹区国际工厂的女工中间，和平工作班的运动已日趋扩大。华沙已展开争取模范建筑场的运动。格伦堡“普鲁斯”工人出版合作社的印刷工人，已经完成了为庆祝波兰拥护和平大会而承担的生产保证。
    与此同时，波兰劳动人民为声援朝鲜人民和救济美国飞机轰炸下的朝鲜受难者，已募集捐款波币一亿八千二百万兹罗提（约合人民币一百四十六亿五千余万元）。
