Title: World Youth Delegation Leader Bugala's Reply

Author:

Date: 1950-09-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd: World Youth Delegation Leader
Bugala's Reply

The head of the World Democratic Youth League delegation, and Secretary-General of the World Democratic Youth League, Bugala, delivered the following speech at the welcome meeting held by representatives from all walks of life in Beijing today: Dear comrades, dear friends:

After arriving in China for more than ten days, we have come to the capital of New China. During our visit of more than ten days, the people and youth of Northeast China warmly welcomed us. Their unparalleled enthusiasm, deep friendship, and spirit of internationalist unity deeply moved us.

Today, the youth of Beijing are welcoming us with the same enthusiasm. Please allow me, on behalf of the World Democratic Youth League delegation, to express our heartfelt gratitude to you and to extend warm revolutionary greetings on behalf of the millions of young people around the world who are fighting for peace and freedom.

The young peace fighters of the world will be with you to celebrate the great October 1st – the first anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

From now on, October 1st will not only be a festival for the Chinese people and youth, but also a festival for the people and youth of the world. They will celebrate the anniversary of the birth of this powerful new democratic country in the peace camp, which stands firm in its fighting position.

We have come to cheer your victory and to cheer your great achievements in building a new China.

We have come to pay tribute to the Chinese people and youth, and to pay tribute to Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people.

We have come to pay tribute to the heroes who sacrificed themselves for the cause of a free and democratic New China and for the cause of progressive mankind throughout the world.

After we return home, the young peace fighters of the world will know that, in the arduous struggle against the warmongers, they are fighting side by side with the Chinese youth, who are resolutely opposed to imperialist aggression and defending world peace.

The young peace fighters of the world will know that, thanks to your victory, the forces of peace greatly outweigh the forces of war, and the ideals of truth and freedom that we uphold will surely triumph over lies and oppression. As long as we unite closely, we will achieve our beautiful future, and we will be able to build a happy and free new world. The dawn of such a world has already shone brightly in the great and beautiful China.

Long live the Chinese youth and its vanguard – the Chinese New Democratic Youth League!

Long live the solidarity of the world's youth in the struggle for peace!

Long live the World Democratic Youth League!

Long live Chairman Mao Zedong, the beloved leader of the Chinese people and youth!

Long live Marshal Stalin, the teacher and best friend of young peace fighters around the world! (Attached pictures)

Beijing youth and children warmly welcome the World Youth Federation delegation at the train station square.

### 世青代表团团长  布加拉致答词

1950-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　世青代表团团长
    布加拉致答词
    【新华社二十三日讯】世界民主青年联盟代表团团长、世界民主青年联盟总书记布加拉本日在北京各界代表欢迎会上致词如下：亲爱的同志们，亲爱的朋友们：
    在到达了中国十多天以后，我们来到了新中国的首都。在我们这十多天的访问中，东北的人民和青年热烈地欢迎了我们，他们无比的热情，深厚的友谊，和国际主义的团结精神，使我们深深地感动。
    今天北京的青年以同样的热情来欢迎我们。请允许我以世界民主青年联盟代表团的名义，向你们致以衷心的谢意，并代表正在为争取和平与自由的全世界千百万青年致以热烈的革命敬礼。
    全世界的青年和平战士将与你们在一起，共同庆祝伟大的十月一日——中华人民共和国成立一周年的纪念日。
    从今以后，十月一日将不仅仅是中国人民和青年的节日，而且是全世界人民和青年的节日。他们将庆祝这个和平阵营中坚持斗争岗位的强大的新的民主国家的诞生纪念日。
    我们是来欢呼你们的胜利的，是来欢呼你们在建设新中国中的伟大成就的。
    我们是来向中国人民和青年致敬的，是来向中国人民伟大的领袖毛泽东主席致敬的。
    我们是来向为争取一个自由民主的新中国，为全世界进步人类的事业而牺牲的英雄们致敬的。
    在我们回国以后，全世界的青年和平战士们将会知道：在反对战争贩子的艰苦斗争中，与他们并肩作战的有着坚决反对帝国主义侵略和保卫世界和平的中国青年。
    全世界的青年和平战士们将会知道，由于你们的胜利，和平力量大大地超过了战争力量，我们所坚持的真理和自由的理想一定会战胜谎言和压迫。只要我们紧密地团结起来，我们将获得我们美好的未来，我们将能建设一个幸福的自由的新世界。这样的世界的曙光已经在伟大的美丽的中国发出了光辉。
    中国青年和它的先锋队——中国新民主主义青年团万岁！
    保卫和平斗争中的全世界青年团结万岁！
    世界民主青年联盟万岁！
    中国的人民和青年热爱的领袖毛泽东主席万岁！
    全世界青年和平战士的导师和最好的朋友斯大林大元帅万岁！（附图片）
