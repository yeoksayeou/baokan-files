Title: Weekly Literary and Art Lecture
Author:
Date: 1950-09-09
Page: 8

The Weekly Literary and Art Lecture, co-organized by the Mass Literature and Art Creation and Research Association and the Youth Service Department, has invited Comrade Lu Su this week to give a lecture titled: "Literature and Music." After the lecture, Mr. Guan Xuezeng and Mr. Wu Changbao will perform a Qinshu (a form of Chinese storytelling accompanied by stringed instruments): "Ode to the Heroes of the Taizi River." Venue: Friendship Hall of the Youth Palace. Time: Sunday morning at 9:30 AM. Admission by long-term or temporary ticket. Temporary tickets can be obtained from the Youth Service Department from Saturday.

### 星期文艺讲座

1950-09-09
第8版()
专栏：

　　星期文艺讲座
    大众文艺创研会与青年服务部合办的星期文艺讲座，本周聘请卢肃同志主讲，讲题：《文学与音乐》。会后由关学增、吴长宝先生演唱琴书：《太子河英雄赞》。会址：青年宫交谊厅。时间：礼拜日上午九时半。凭长期券临时券入场。临时券可于星期六至青年服务部领取。
