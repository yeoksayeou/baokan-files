Title: Local Elections Successfully Completed in 19 Counties of South Jeolla Province, Korea; 95.32% of Voters Cast Affirmative Votes
Author:
Date: 1950-09-21
Page: 4
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 20th: According to the Korean Central News Agency, the Central Guidance Department for Local Elections in the Southern Half of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced on the 11th that the election work in 19 counties, 202 districts, and 2,374 villages in South Jeolla Province had been successfully completed. A total of 702,533 voters participated in the village people's committee and district representative election meetings. They elected village people's committees and district representatives, then the district representative meetings elected district people's committees and county representatives, and finally, the county representatives elected county people's committees. Among the 677 elected county people's committee members, there were 70 workers, 509 farmers, 60 staff members, and 16 intellectuals. Women accounted for 93 members. Those who voted in favor accounted for 95.32% of all voters.

### 朝鲜全罗南道十九个县  地方选举胜利完成  百分之九五·三二选民投赞成票

1950-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜全罗南道十九个县
    地方选举胜利完成
    百分之九五·三二选民投赞成票
    【新华社平壤二十日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国南半部地方选举中央指导部十一日公布：全罗南道十九个县二百零二个区二千三百七十四个村的选举工作，业已胜利完成。参加村人民委员与区代表选举大会的选民，共计七十万二千五百三十三人。他们选出了村人民委员会和区代表，然后由区代表会议再选出区人民委员会和县代表，最后由县代表选出县人民委员会。当选的六百七十七名县人民委员中有：工人七十名、农民五百零九名、职员六十名、知识分子十六名。妇女占九十三名。投赞成票者，占全体选民的百分之九十五点三二。
