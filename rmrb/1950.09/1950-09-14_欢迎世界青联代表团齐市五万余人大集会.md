Title: Qi City Holds 50,000-Strong Rally to Welcome World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1950-09-14
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Qiqihar, 13th. Over 50,000 people gathered in Qiqihar on the morning of the 12th to welcome the delegation of the World Federation of Democratic Youth. Li Jianbai, Secretary of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Youth League, Zhao Dezun, Secretary of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Yu Yifu, Chairman of the Heilongjiang Provincial People's Government, and Wu Han, Secretary-General of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, delivered speeches successively, all expressing a warm welcome to the delegation of the World Federation of Democratic Youth for their visit to China. Boukella, head of the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation, Soviet representative Semi Chastny, Iranian representative Mansuri Khasan, Polish representative Goralewski, and African representative Momoni also delivered speeches one after another. In their speeches, they all expressed that the democratic youth of the world will forever stand with the Chinese youth and fight to the end against new conspiracies of war and to defend world peace. Boukella said in his speech, "We have the same hatred for the imperialist oppressors and the same love for peace. We will unite even broader youth to fight for peace and democracy." The conference presented the delegation with a banner that read: "Youth of the world, unite and fight for lasting peace and people's democracy."
    The delegation left Qiqihar for Harbin on the evening of the 12th. More than 2,000 people from various government organizations and people's representatives from all walks of life in Qiqihar went to the station to see them off. In addition to attending the welcome meeting in Qiqihar, the delegation visited the Chahayang Mechanical Farm, the largest state-run farm in Northeast China.

### 欢迎世界青联代表团齐市五万余人大集会

1950-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　欢迎世界青联代表团齐市五万余人大集会
    【新华社齐齐哈尔十三日电】齐齐哈尔于十二日上午有五万余人集会，欢迎世界民主青年联盟代表团。青年团黑龙江省团委书记李剑白、中共黑龙江省委书记赵德尊、黑龙江省人民政府主席于毅夫以及中华全国民主青年联合总会秘书长吴晗相继致词，一致对世界民主青年联盟代表团的莅临我国表示热烈欢迎。世界民主青年联盟代表团团长布加拉、苏联代表赛米恰斯尼、伊朗代表满苏里喀山、波兰代表戈拉尔斯基、非洲代表摩摩尼等亦先后发表演说。他们在演说中一致表示全世界民主青年将和中国青年永远站在一起，为反对新的阴谋战争、保卫世界和平而奋斗到底。布加拉演说称，我们对帝国主义压迫者有同样的憎恨，对和平也有同样的爱好。我们必将团结更广大的青年，为和平民主而斗争。大会赠给代表团“全世界青年团结起来，为争取持久和平与人民民主而斗争”的锦旗一面。
    代表团于十二日晚间即离齐齐哈尔赴哈尔滨。齐齐哈尔各机关团体和各界人民代表二千余人赴车站送行。代表团在齐齐哈尔除出席欢迎会外，曾参观东北最大的黑龙江省营查哈阳机械农场。
