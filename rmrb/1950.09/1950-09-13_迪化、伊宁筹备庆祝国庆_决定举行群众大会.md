Title: Dihua, Yining Preparing to Celebrate National Day: Decision to Hold Mass Rally
Author:
Date: 1950-09-13
Page: 2

　　Dihua, Yining Preparing to Celebrate National Day
    Decision to Hold Mass Rally
    [Xinhua News Agency, Dihua, 12th] All organizations and groups in Dihua City, the capital of Xinjiang Province, formed a preparatory committee for celebrating National Day and the first anniversary of the peaceful liberation of Xinjiang on the 9th. Bao Erhan, Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government, serves as the director of the preparatory committee, and General Wang Zhen, General Tao Zhiyue, Deputy Chairman Gao Jinchun of the Provincial People's Government, and Deputy Chairman Saifuddin, among others, totaling forty-five people, are members of the preparatory committee. The preparatory committee initially decided to hold a National Day propaganda campaign week from September 25th to October 1st, and simultaneously hold a production exhibition. On October 1st, a mass rally and sports meet will be held, and a cadre evening party and torch parade will be held that evening.
    Furthermore, Yining City, an important town in northern Xinjiang, formed a National Day preparatory committee on the 8th, deciding to hold a mass rally, sports meet, and military parade on National Day. In addition, a political education study week will be organized, stipulating the study of the Common Program and other documents to strengthen the patriotic education of cadres of all ethnic groups.

### 迪化、伊宁筹备庆祝国庆  决定举行群众大会

1950-09-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　迪化、伊宁筹备庆祝国庆
    决定举行群众大会
    【新华社迪化十二日电】新疆省会迪化市各机关、团体于九日组成庆祝国庆节与新疆和平解放一周年的筹备委员会。新疆省人民政府鲍尔汉主席任筹备会主任，王震将军、陶峙岳将军、省人民政府高锦纯副主席、赛福鼎副主席等四十五人为筹备委员会委员。筹委会初步决定于九月二十五日至十月一日举行国庆节宣传运动周，并同时举行生产展览会；十月一日举行群众大会及运动大会，当晚并举行干部晚会与火炬游行。
    又讯：北疆重镇伊宁市，已于八日组成国庆节筹备会，决定于国庆日举行群众大会、运动会与阅兵式，此外并将组织政治教育学习周，规定学习共同纲领等文件，以加强对各民族干部的爱祖国教育。
