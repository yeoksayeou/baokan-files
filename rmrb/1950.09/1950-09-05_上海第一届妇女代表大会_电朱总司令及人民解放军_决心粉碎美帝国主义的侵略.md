Title: Shanghai First Women's Congress Sends Telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu and the People's Liberation Army: Determined to Crush the Aggression of American Imperialism
Author: 
Date: 1950-09-05
Page: 3

　　Shanghai First Women's Congress
    Telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu and the People's Liberation Army
    Determined to Crush the Aggression of American Imperialism
    [Our Newspaper's Dispatch] The Shanghai First Women's Congress sent a telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu and all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, thanking the heroic People's Liberation Army for defeating the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang and achieving the basic victory of the revolution. The delegates of the congress, representing all women in Shanghai, stated: they will, with the greatest determination and effort, support and assist our People's Liberation Army in liberating Taiwan, eliminating the remnant forces of Chiang Kai-shek, crushing the aggression of American imperialism, and building a new China for the people! The congress concluded on August 17th, and the Shanghai Democratic Women's Federation has been formally established.

### 上海第一届妇女代表大会  电朱总司令及人民解放军  决心粉碎美帝国主义的侵略

1950-09-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　上海第一届妇女代表大会
    电朱总司令及人民解放军           
    决心粉碎美帝国主义的侵略
    【本报讯】上海市第一届妇女代表大会致电朱总司令及中国人民解放军全体指战员，感谢英勇的人民解放军打垮了蒋匪帮，取得了革命的基本胜利。大会代表全上海的妇女表示：将以最大的决心和努力，拥护并支持我人民解放军解放台湾，消灭蒋匪残余势力，粉碎美帝国主义的侵略，建设人民的新中国！大会已于八月十七日闭幕，上海市民主妇女联合会已正式成立。
